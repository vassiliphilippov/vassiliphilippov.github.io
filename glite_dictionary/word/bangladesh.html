Bangladesh

1. PROPN

/ bæŋɡləˈdɛʃ / (AmE), / baŋɡlaˈdɛʃ / (BrE)
A country in southern Asia, bordered by India and the Bay of Bengal, which became independent from Pakistan in 1971.
country in southern Asia

Examples

The garment industry is a major part of Bangladesh's economy.
Many rivers flow through Bangladesh, giving it fertile land for agriculture.
In 1971, Bangladesh won its independence after a war with Pakistan.

Etymology

From Bengali বাংলাদেশ (Bānlādeś), meaning 'Country of Bengal'.

2. PROPN

/ ˌbæŋɡləˈdɛʃ / (AmE), / ˌbaŋɡləˈdɛʃ / (BrE)
a country in southern Asia, bordered by India and Myanmar, with the Bay of Bengal to the south; it became independent from Pakistan in 1971.
country in South Asia, independent since 1971

Examples

The capital city of Bangladesh is Dhaka, which is also its largest city.
Since its independence in 1971, Bangladesh has made significant progress in development.
Monsoon rains can cause severe flooding in parts of Bangladesh each year.

Etymology

From Bengali 'Bangladesh', meaning 'country of Bengal'.
