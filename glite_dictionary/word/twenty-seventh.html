twenty-seventh

1. ADJ

/ ˌtwɛntiˈsɛvənθ / (AmE), / ˌtwɛntiˈsɛvənθ / (BrE)
next after the twenty-sixth in a sequence; being number 27 in order or position.
ordinal number for 27th

Examples

Her birthday falls on the twenty-seventh of September.
He finished twenty-seventh in the marathon after a strong run.
This is the twenty-seventh edition of the annual conference.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-sixth - ordinal number for 26th
  • Degree Higher: twenty-eighth - ordinal number for 28th
