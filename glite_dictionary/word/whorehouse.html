whorehouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈhɔːˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A building where people engage in sexual activity with prostitutes for payment; a brothel.
building for prostitution; brothel
vulgar

Examples

One of the city's oldest buildings used to be a whorehouse in the nineteenth century.
The police raided the illegal whorehouse after receiving several complaints from neighbors.
He discovered that the establishment was operating as a whorehouse behind its innocent facade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brothel - a house of prostitution

Etymology

From 'whore' (Old English 'hōre') + 'house'.
