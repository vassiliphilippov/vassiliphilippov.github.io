Nippon

1. PROPN

/ nɪˈpɑn / (AmE), / nɪˈpɒn / (BrE)
the official Japanese name for Japan, used especially in formal contexts
the official Japanese name for Japan

Examples

The coins bore the word Nippon alongside English inscriptions.
During his speech, the ambassador referred to his country as Nippon.
Many stamps issued in Nippon feature traditional Japanese art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Japan - an island country in East Asia, also known as Nippon

Etymology

From Japanese 日本 (Nippon), meaning 'Japan'.
