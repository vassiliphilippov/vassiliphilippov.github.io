salving

1. ADJ

/ ˈsævɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɑːlvɪŋ / (BrE)
having a softening or soothing effect, especially on the skin
having a soothing or softening effect

Examples

The lotion had a salving effect on her sunburnt skin.
His words were as salving as a gentle balm after a rough day.
A salving ointment can help relieve dry or damaged skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soothing - having a calming or relieving effect
  • Antonym: irritating - having a stinging or aggravating effect

Etymology

From the verb 'salve', ultimately from Old English 'sealf' (ointment, salve).
