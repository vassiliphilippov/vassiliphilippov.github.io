single-humped

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɪŋɡlˈhʌmpt / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋɡlˈhʌmpt / (BrE)
having a single hump, especially referring to camels or similar animals.
having one hump (esp. animal)

Examples

The dromedary is a single-humped camel native to the Middle East and Africa.
A single-humped appearance distinguishes the dromedary from the Bactrian camel.
We saw a single-humped camel grazing in the desert.

Synset Examples

a one-humped camel

Related Terms

  • Antonym: double-humped - having two humps, especially referring to camels
