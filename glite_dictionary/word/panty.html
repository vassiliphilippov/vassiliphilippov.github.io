panty

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæn.ti / (AmE), / ˈpæn.ti / (BrE)
a pair of short close-fitting underpants, typically worn by women or children
short underpants for women or children

Examples

She bought a new silk panty set for the trip.
The little girl wore a pink panty under her dress.
That store sells every color and style of panty imaginable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: panties - short underpants for women or girls (plural form)
  • Synonym: briefs - short, close-fitting underpants for men or women

Etymology

From French *pantalon* (trousers), with diminutive form used in English for undergarments.
