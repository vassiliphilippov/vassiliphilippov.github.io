unhurt

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhɝt / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɜːt / (BrE)
Not injured or harmed; remaining physically unharmed after an incident.
not injured or harmed

Examples

Miraculously, the driver walked away from the accident unhurt.
Although the house was damaged by fire, everyone inside was unhurt.
She was relieved to see her son return home unhurt after the storm.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: injured - harmed or wounded

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'hurt'.
