rattle-top

1. NOUN

/ ˈrætəl tɑp / (AmE), / ˈrætəl tɒp / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant of the buttercup family (genus Cimicifuga), found from Maine and Ontario to Wisconsin and south to Georgia; also known as bugbane.
a North American plant, also called bugbane

Examples

Wild rattle-top can be seen growing along the forest edge in late summer.
Gardeners sometimes plant rattle-top to help repel insects naturally.
The leaves of rattle-top are deeply cut and attractive even when not in bloom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bugbane - another name for the same plant genus (Cimicifuga)
