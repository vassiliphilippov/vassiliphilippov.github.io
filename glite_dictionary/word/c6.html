C6

1. NOUN

/ si sɪks / (AmE), / si sɪks / (BrE)
an envelope size measuring 114 mm x 162 mm, commonly used for enclosing A6 cards or folded A4 sheets
specific standard envelope size (114 x 162 mm)

Examples

We need to purchase more C6 envelopes for our invitations.
The letter fits perfectly inside a C6 envelope when the A4 sheet is folded twice.
Our stationery supplier offers a discount on boxes of C6 envelopes.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: A6 - a paper size measuring 105 x 148 mm
  • Degree Higher: C5 - an envelope size measuring 162 x 229 mm

Etymology

Designation from the ISO 269 standard for envelope sizes, derived from the ISO 216 paper size system.
