shackled

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃækəld / (AmE), / ˈʃækəld / (BrE)
bound by chains fastened around the ankles, often as a form of restraint
bound with chains around the ankles

Examples

The prisoners were shackled together as they were led to the courtyard.
She felt shackled by tradition, longing for freedom.
His legs remained shackled throughout the entire journey.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unshackled - not bound by shackles; freed from restraints or chains
  • Synonym: chained - bound or fastened with chains
