flesh-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈflɛʃ ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈflɛʃ ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having a pale pink or light beige color resembling that of human skin
of a color like human skin

Examples

She wore a flesh-colored dress that blended with her skin tone.
The makeup artist chose a flesh-colored foundation for a natural look.
Some bandages are designed to be flesh-colored so they are less noticeable on the skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skin-colored - of a color similar to that of human skin
  • Degree Higher: rosy - having a pink or reddish color
