suicidal

1. ADJ

/ suːɪˈsaɪdəl / (AmE), / suːɪˈsaɪdəl / (BrE)
(of an action, plan, or behavior) extremely dangerous to oneself or one's interests; likely to cause serious harm or ruin.
dangerous to oneself or one's interests

Examples

The company's suicidal expansion put all its investments at risk.
Ignoring the warning signs was a suicidal decision for the hikers.
He pursued a suicidal course of action despite everyone's concerns.

Synset Examples

suicidal impulses
a suicidal corporate takeover strategy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reckless - showing a lack of concern for danger or consequences
  • Antonym: prudent - acting with care and thought for the future

Etymology

From suicide + -al, probably first appearing in the 19th century.
