arterial

1. ADJ

/ ɑːrˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving the arteries, the blood vessels that carry blood away from the heart.
relating to arteries
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that the patient's arterial pressure was abnormally high.
An arterial blockage can lead to serious health complications if left untreated.
Oxygen-rich arterial blood is circulated throughout the body from the heart.

Synset Examples

arterial disease
the arterial system
arterial blood

Related Terms

  • Antonym: venous - relating to veins

Etymology

From Latin 'arterialis', from 'arteria' (artery).
