dropkicker

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɑpˌkɪkər / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpˌkɪkə / (BrE)
A player in football who kicks the ball by dropping it and kicking it just as it touches the ground.
football player who dropkicks the ball

Examples

The team relied on their skilled dropkicker to score points from difficult angles.
He became famous as a dropkicker during the early years of professional football.
Most modern football teams no longer use a dedicated dropkicker in their lineup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dropkick - a kick in which the ball is dropped and kicked as it touches the ground

Etymology

From 'dropkick' (football kick where the ball is dropped and kicked as it touches the ground) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
