immunochemical

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪm.juː.noʊˈkɛm.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌɪm.juː.nəʊˈkɛm.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving immunochemistry, especially the chemical aspects of immune responses or the use of antibodies in laboratory analysis.
relating to immunochemistry

Examples

The laboratory performed an immunochemical assay to detect the presence of specific antibodies.
Researchers use immunochemical techniques to study immune system proteins.
The diagnosis was confirmed through immunochemical analysis of the patient's blood sample.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immunology - the branch of science dealing with immunity and the immune system

Etymology

From 'immuno-' (relating to immunity) + 'chemical'.
