flexile

1. ADJ

/ ˈflɛk.saɪl / (AmE), / ˈflɛks.aɪl / (BrE)
easily bent or capable of being flexed; flexible.
easily bent; flexible

Examples

The willow branches are exceptionally flexile and used in basket weaving.
Her movements were so flexile that she easily performed the most difficult yoga poses.
This flexile material is ideal for making durable, bendable phone cases.

Synset Examples

slim flexible birches

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flexible - able to bend easily without breaking

Etymology

From Latin 'flexilis', from 'flectere' meaning 'to bend'.
