non-paying

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈpeɪɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˈpeɪɪŋ / (BrE)
not required to pay or not having paid; given or received without payment.
not paying or not paid for

Examples

The company hosted several non-paying interns during the summer.
They were frustrated with non-paying customers who cancelled at the last minute.
The event offered seats for both non-paying guests and those who purchased tickets.

Synset Examples

non-paying guests
non-paying bidders on eBay

Related Terms

  • Antonym: paying - giving money in exchange for something; opposite of non-paying

Etymology

Formed by the prefix 'non-' (not) + 'paying' (present participle of 'pay').
