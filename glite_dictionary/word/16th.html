16th

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪksˌtiːnθ / (AmE), / ˈsɪksˌtiːnθ / (BrE)
coming next after the fifteenth in position; being number sixteen in a sequence
ordinal number: in sixteenth position

Examples

She finished the race in 16th place.
This is the 16th time I've asked you to turn off the lights.
Our meeting will be held on the 16th of July.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fifteenth - coming next after the fourteenth in position
  • Degree Higher: seventeenth - coming next after the sixteenth in position
