2. NOUN

/ ˈswitˌhɑrt / (AmE), / ˈswiːthɑːt / (BrE)
A person who is loved, especially in a romantic way; used as a term of endearment.
a loved person or term of endearment

Examples

She greeted her sweetheart at the airport with a warm embrace.
Happy anniversary, my sweetheart! I love you more each day.
You have always been my sweetheart since high school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: darling - a person dearly loved or regarded with affection
  • Synonym: beloved - a much loved person
  • Antonym: enemy - a person who is actively opposed or hostile to someone

Etymology
