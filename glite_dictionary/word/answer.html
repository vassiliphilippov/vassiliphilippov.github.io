answer

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.sɚ / (AmE), / ˈɑːn.sə / (BrE)
a spoken or written reply to a question, request, or situation

Examples

The teacher waited patiently for the student's answer.
He searched for the right answer but couldn't find it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: response - something said or done as a reaction to something
  • Antonym: question - a sentence or phrase used to find out information
