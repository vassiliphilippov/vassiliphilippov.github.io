uninfected

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪnˈfɛktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈfɛktɪd / (BrE)
not affected or contaminated by infection; free from disease-causing organisms.
not affected by infection

Examples

The doctor was relieved to see that the wound remained uninfected after several days.
It is important to keep food uninfected during preparation and storage.
Only uninfected patients are allowed into the surgical ward.

Synset Examples

a clean (or uninfected) wound

Related Terms

  • Antonym: infected - affected with disease-causing organisms

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (not) to 'infected'.
