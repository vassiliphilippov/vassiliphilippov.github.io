Sir Thomas Raffles

1. PROPN

/ sɚ ˈtɑməs ˈræfəlz / (AmE), / sɜː ˈtɒməs ˈræfəlz / (BrE)
(1781–1826) A British colonial administrator known for founding modern Singapore.
British colonial founder of Singapore

Examples

Sir Thomas Raffles is widely recognized as the founder of modern Singapore.
The legacy of Sir Thomas Raffles remains evident in the city's vibrant history.
