weensy

1. ADJ

/ ˈwiːnsi / (AmE), / ˈwiːnsi / (BrE)
Very small; tiny (informal).
very small; tiny (informal)
informal

Examples

The kitten looked so weensy curled up in the basket.
Could I have just a weensy bit more of that cake?
She wore a pair of weensy earrings to the party.

Synset Examples

a wee tot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiny - very small in size
  • Synonym: wee - very small; little (especially Scottish or informal English)

Etymology

Blend of 'wee' (small) and the diminutive suffix '-sy.'
