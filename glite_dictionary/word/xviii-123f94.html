xviii

1. NUM

/ ɛks viː ˈaɪ aɪ ˈaɪ / (AmE), / ɛks viː ˈaɪ aɪ ˈaɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for the number eighteen (18)
Roman numeral for 18

Examples

The king issued an edict in the year xviii of his reign.
The inscription on the building reads 'Anno Domini xviii'.
Chapter xviii of the book explores key philosophical concepts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eighteen - the cardinal number 18

Etymology

From Latin numerals; 'x' for ten (10) and 'viii' for eight (8).
