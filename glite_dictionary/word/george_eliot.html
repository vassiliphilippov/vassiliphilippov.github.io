George Eliot

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɔrdʒ ˈɛliət / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒ ˈɛlɪət / (BrE)
the pen name of Mary Ann Evans (1819–1880), a British novelist known for her psychologically insightful and realistic novels of Victorian society.
British novelist; pen name of Mary Ann Evans

Examples

Many consider George Eliot one of the greatest novelists of the Victorian era.
Her novel 'Middlemarch,' written as George Eliot, is still widely read today.
Students analyze the themes and character development in works by George Eliot.

Etymology

Pen name adopted by Mary Ann Evans, British novelist, in the mid-19th century.
