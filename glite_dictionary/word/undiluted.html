undiluted

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.daɪˈluː.t̬ɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.daɪˈljuː.tɪd / (BrE)
not made weaker or thinner by the addition of another substance; pure or complete.
not diluted; pure

Examples

This beverage is made with undiluted juice, giving it a strong flavor.
Her undiluted enthusiasm inspired everyone around her.
The scientist studied undiluted samples to ensure accurate results.

Synset Examples

undiluted milk
an undiluted racial strain

Related Terms

  • Antonym: diluted - made thinner or weaker by adding another substance

Etymology

From un- (not) + diluted, past participle of dilute, from Latin diluere (to wash away, dissolve).
