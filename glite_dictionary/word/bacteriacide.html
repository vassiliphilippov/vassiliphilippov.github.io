bacteriacide

1. NOUN

/ bækˈtɪəriəˌsaɪd / (AmE), / bækˈtɪəriəˌsaɪd / (BrE)
A chemical substance that destroys bacteria.
substance that kills bacteria
medical

Examples

The hospital uses a powerful bacteriacide to disinfect surgical instruments.
Adding a bacteriacide to the water supply helps prevent the spread of disease.
Always handle any bacteriacide with care and wear protective gloves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bactericide - substance that destroys bacteria

Etymology

From 'bacteria' + Latin '-cide' (killer).
