oval-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊ.vəl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.vəl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the rounded, elongated form of an oval, similar to the shape of an egg.
having the shape of an oval

Examples

She admired the oval-shaped mirror hanging in the hallway.
The bird laid several oval-shaped eggs in the nest.
His face was oval-shaped, with gentle features and bright eyes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oval - having the rounded shape of an egg or ellipse
