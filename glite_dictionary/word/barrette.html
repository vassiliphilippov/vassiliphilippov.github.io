barrette

1. NOUN

/ bəˈrɛt / (AmE), / bəˈrɛt / (BrE)
a small decorative clasp or pin used to hold hair, especially women's or girls', in place
decorative hair clasp or pin

Examples

She wore a sparkling barrette to keep her hair out of her face.
After losing her favorite barrette, she bought a new set at the store.
The little girl's red barrette matched her dress perfectly.

Etymology

From French 'barrette,' diminutive of 'barre' (bar).
