Asplenium nigripes

1. NOUN

/ æˈspliːniəm ˈnaɪɡrɪps / (AmE), / æsˈpliːniəm ˈnaɪɡrɪps / (BrE)
A rare fern species of the genus Schaffneria, known for its delicate fronds.
rare fern species of Schaffneria

Examples

The Asplenium nigripes thrives in damp, shaded regions of Central America.
Botanists documented the unique characteristics of Asplenium nigripes during their expedition.
Efforts are underway to protect Asplenium nigripes due to its rarity and ecological importance.

Etymology

From Latin: 'Asplenium' (a fern genus), 'nigripes' meaning 'black-footed' or 'with black stalks'.
