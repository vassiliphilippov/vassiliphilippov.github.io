diversely

1. ADV

/ daɪˈvɜrsli / (AmE), / daɪˈvɜːsli / (BrE)
in various or different ways; in a manner showing diversity
in diverse ways; differently

Examples

People from around the world think diversely about important issues.
The committee is composed of members who are diversely skilled.
Plants in the rainforest grow diversely, making the ecosystem very rich.

Synset Examples

the alternatives that are variously represented by the participants
the speakers treated the subject most diversely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: variously - in several different ways

Etymology

Formed from 'diverse' and adverbial suffix '-ly'. 'Diverse' from Latin 'diversus' (turned different ways, various).
