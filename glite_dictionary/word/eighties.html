eighties

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ.tiz / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.tiz / (BrE)
the decade from 1880 to 1889; the period between 1880 and 1889, often referred to as 'the eighties.'

Examples

He is writing a book about European politics in the eighties.
