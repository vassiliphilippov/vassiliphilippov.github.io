NE

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛnˈi / (AmE), / ˌɛnˈi / (BrE)
The compass point that is midway between north and east, at 45 degrees.
midway compass point between north and east

Examples

The storm was moving rapidly towards the NE of the city.
They set their course to the NE and sailed overnight.
Our cabin is located just NE of the main downtown area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: northeast - the compass point between north and east
  • Antonym: NW - the compass point between north and west

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'northeast', from Old English 'norþ' (north) and 'east'.
