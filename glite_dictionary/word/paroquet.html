paroquet

1. NOUN

/ ˌpæroʊˈkɛt / (AmE), / ˌparəˈkɛt / (BrE)
A small, slender parrot with a long tail; an alternative spelling of 'parakeet'.
small long-tailed parrot

Examples

A brilliant green paroquet fluttered noisily among the branches.
In the 19th century, paroquet was a common term for these birds.
She keeps a pair of paroquets in her sunroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parakeet - a small, long-tailed parrot

Etymology

Variant of 'parakeet', from French 'paroquet', possibly by influence of Italian 'parrocchetto'.
