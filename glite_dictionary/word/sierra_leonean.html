Sierra Leonean

1. NOUN

/ siˌɛrə liˈoʊniən / (AmE), / siˌɛərə liˈəʊniən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Sierra Leone
person from Sierra Leone

Examples

She is a proud Sierra Leonean who loves sharing her culture with others.
Many Sierra Leoneans live and work in neighboring countries.
As a Sierra Leonean, he enjoys traditional dishes from his homeland.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sierra Leone - a country in West Africa

2. ADJ

/ siˌɛrə liˈoʊniən / (AmE), / sɪˌɛərə liˈəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to Sierra Leone or its people or culture
of or relating to Sierra Leone

Examples

The new Sierra Leonean restaurant opened downtown last week.
She specializes in Sierra Leonean literature and history.
Many Sierra Leonean traditions are celebrated during national holidays.

Synset Examples

Sierra Leonean politics

Etymology

From Sierra Leone (country in West Africa) + -an (adjectival suffix).
