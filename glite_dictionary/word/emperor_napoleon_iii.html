Emperor Napoleon III

1. PROPN

/ ɛmˈpɪrər nəˈpoʊliən ðə θɜrd / (AmE), / ɛmˈpɪərə nəˈpəʊliən ðə θɜːd / (BrE)
(1808–1873) Nephew of Napoleon I, he was emperor of the French from 1852 to 1871.
French emperor (1852–1871), nephew of Napoleon I

Examples

Emperor Napoleon III was instrumental in the rebuilding of Paris during his reign.
Historians often debate the legacy of Emperor Napoleon III on European politics.
The defeat of Emperor Napoleon III in the Franco-Prussian War marked the end of his rule.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Napoleon I - French military leader and first emperor of France
