appearance

1. NOUN

/ əˈpɪrəns / (AmE), / əˈpɪərəns / (BrE)
the way that someone or something looks to other people, especially as imagined or described rather than as actually seen
the way someone or something looks (especially mentally represented)

Examples

The witness described the suspect's appearance as she remembered it from the scene.
Can you give me a detailed appearance of the missing painting?
She struggled to recall the appearance of her childhood home.

Synset Examples

I tried to describe his appearance to the police

Related Terms

  • Synonym: semblance - an outward or apparent form of something

Etymology

From Old French *aparance* (appearance), from Late Latin *apparentia* (semblance).

Examples

Related Terms
