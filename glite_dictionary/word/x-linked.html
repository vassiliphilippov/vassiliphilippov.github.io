X-linked

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛks lɪŋkt / (AmE), / ˈɛks lɪŋkt / (BrE)
(of a gene, characteristic, or condition) carried on or associated with the X chromosome.
carried on the X chromosome
medical

Examples

Hemophilia is an X-linked disorder that primarily affects males.
The doctor explained that color blindness can be caused by an X-linked gene.
Research on X-linked conditions has advanced rapidly in recent years.

Synset Examples

an X-linked mutation

Etymology

From X (denoting the X chromosome) + linked (associated or connected).
