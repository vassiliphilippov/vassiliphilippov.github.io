XIV

1. NUM

/ ɛks aɪ ˈvi / (AmE), / ɛks aɪ ˈviː / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for the number fourteen
Roman numeral for fourteen

Examples

The chapter was labeled XIV at the top of the page.
He wore a jersey with the number XIV on the back during the finals.
Super Bowl XIV was played in 1980.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 14 - the number fourteen

Etymology

From Latin numerals: X (10) + IV (4, where I before V means one less than five).

2. NUM

/ ɛks aɪ ˈvi / (AmE), / ɛks aɪ ˈvi / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for the number fourteen (14)
Roman numeral for 14

Examples

The book was published in Chapter XIV of the series.
The monument was built in the year XIV of the king's reign.
In Roman numerals, fourteen is written as XIV.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 14 - the cardinal number fourteen
  • Degree Higher: XV - the Roman numeral for fifteen (15)
  • Degree Lower: XIII - the Roman numeral for thirteen (13)

Etymology

From Latin numerals: X (10) + IV (4).
