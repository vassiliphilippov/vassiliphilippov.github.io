repairer

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈpɛrər / (AmE), / rɪˈpeərə / (BrE)
a person who fixes or restores something that is broken or damaged, especially as a profession
person who repairs things

Examples

The repairer finished fixing the washing machine within an hour.
If your watch stops working, take it to a skilled repairer for inspection.
The company hired an experienced repairer to maintain their fleet of vehicles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fixer - a person who fixes things; someone who repairs

Etymology

From the verb 'repair' + the agent suffix '-er'.
