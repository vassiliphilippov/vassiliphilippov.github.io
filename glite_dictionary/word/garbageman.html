garbageman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrbɪdʒˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒˌman / (BrE)
a man whose job is to collect and dispose of refuse from homes or businesses
a man who collects and disposes of refuse

Examples

The garbageman comes early every Monday morning to collect the trash.
When the garbageman arrived, the bins were already full and waiting on the curb.
As a child, he was fascinated by the big trucks driven by the garbageman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garbage collector - a person employed to collect and dispose of refuse
  • Synonym: sanitation worker - a worker responsible for collecting and disposing of waste

Etymology

From 'garbage' + 'man', patterned after similar occupational terms.
