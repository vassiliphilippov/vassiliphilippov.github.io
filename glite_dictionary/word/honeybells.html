honeybells

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌniˌbɛlz / (AmE), / ˈhʌniˌbɛlz / (BrE)
An African shrub with creeping stems and slender, yellow, honey-scented flowers, sometimes produced singly or in pairs.
African shrub with yellow, honey-scented flowers

Examples

The vibrant honeybells filled the garden with a sweet fragrance.
She planted honeybells along the walkway for their beautiful yellow blooms.
Botanists were fascinated by the delicate flowers of the honeybells shrub.
