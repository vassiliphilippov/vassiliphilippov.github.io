Fr

1. NOUN

/ ɛf ɑr / (AmE), / ɛf ɑː / (BrE)
The chemical symbol for francium, a highly radioactive element of the alkali-metal group.
chemical symbol for francium (element)

Examples

Fr is the chemical symbol for francium, one of the rarest elements on Earth.
The atomic number of Fr is 87.
Scientists usually detect Fr as a product in the decay chains of other radioactive elements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: francium - a highly radioactive chemical element of the alkali metal group

Etymology

Abbreviation from the Latin 'Francium', named after France where it was discovered.
