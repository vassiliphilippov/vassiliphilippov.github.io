oculus

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑː.kjə.ləs / (AmE), / ˈɒk.jʊ.ləs / (BrE)
the eye; the organ of sight
the eye; organ of sight

Examples

The physician examined the patient's oculus for any signs of infection.
Artists often focus on the oculus to convey emotion in their portraits.
The left oculus was injured in the accident and required immediate treatment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eye - the organ of sight; the spherical structure in the head that detects light

Etymology

From Latin *oculus* (eye).
