Cannes

1. PROPN

/ kænz / (AmE), / kan / (BrE)
A port and resort city on the French Riviera, famous for its annual international film festival.
city in France, site of famous film festival

Examples

Every spring, film stars from around the world gather in Cannes for its renowned film festival.
Cannes attracts tourists with its golden beaches and luxury hotels.
She has always dreamed of visiting Cannes during the festival season.

Etymology

From the French name 'Cannes', possibly from Latin 'Cannæ'.
