camion

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæmiˌɑn / (AmE), / ˈkæmiɒn / (BrE)
a large truck designed for carrying heavy loads, often without sides
large truck for heavy loads

Examples

The military convoy included a camion loaded with supplies for the troops.
Workers carefully secured the crates on the open camion before departure.
He drove an old camion across mountain passes to deliver construction materials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: truck - a motor vehicle for carrying goods

Etymology

From French *camion* (heavy vehicle), ultimately from Italian *camione*.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkæmi.ɒn / (AmE), / ˈkæm.i.ɒn / (BrE)
a low, heavy cart without sides, pulled by horses and used for transporting heavy loads
a low heavy horse cart for haulage

Examples

The workers loaded the stones onto the camion for transport to the construction site.
In the old photograph, a horse-drawn camion waits outside the warehouse.
The museum exhibit displayed a restored camion used during World War I.

Etymology

From French 'camion' meaning 'cart, truck'.
