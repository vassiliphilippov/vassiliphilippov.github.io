Exacum

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˈzækəm / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzækəm / (BrE)
A genus of tropical Asiatic and African plants, especially known for the Persian violet.
genus of tropical flowering plants

Examples

The Exacum genus includes the well-known Persian violet, prized for its vibrant flowers.
Gardeners often recommend Exacum as an ornamental plant for bright indoor spaces.
Some species in the Exacum genus are native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

Etymology

From Latin *Exacum*, the genus name, of uncertain origin.
