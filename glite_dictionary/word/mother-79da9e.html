Mother

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌðɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌðə / (BrE)
(in certain Christian religious orders) a title used to refer to a mother superior.
title for a mother superior

Examples

The sisters greeted their Mother with respect as she entered the room.
During the ceremony, Mother addressed the congregation in a calm voice.
When in doubt, the novices always sought guidance from Mother.
