presidentship

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛzɪdəntˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈprɛzɪdən(t)ʃɪp / (BrE)
the position, office, or term of a president; the responsibilities and duties associated with being a president
the office or role of president

Examples

She achieved the presidentship of the organization after years of dedicated service.
His presidentship was marked by significant political reforms and economic growth.
The presidentship of the club changed hands every two years to encourage new leadership.

Synset Examples

Andrew Jackson expanded the power of the presidency beyond what was customary before his time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: presidency - the office or tenure of a president

Etymology

Formed by combining 'president' and the suffix '-ship', denoting state or condition.
