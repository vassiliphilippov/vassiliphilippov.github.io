nup

1. INTJ

/ nʌp / (AmE), / nʌp / (BrE)
An informal way of saying 'no.'
informal 'no'
informal

Examples

"Are you coming to the party tonight?" "Nup, I have to study."
He asked if I wanted dessert and I just replied, "nup."
Whenever someone offers her coffee, she always says "nup."

Synset Examples

When asked if he'd help, he simply replied 'nup.'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no - word used to indicate a negative response or denial
