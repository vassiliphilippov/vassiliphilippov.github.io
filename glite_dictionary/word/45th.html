45th

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrti ˈfɪfθ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːti ˈfɪfθ / (BrE)
coming after the forty-fourth in a sequence; being number forty-five in order.
ordinal number for forty-five

Examples

She finished in 45th place out of hundreds of competitors.
The 45th anniversary will be celebrated with a special ceremony.
He was the 45th president of the organization.
