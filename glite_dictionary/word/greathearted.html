greathearted

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪtˌhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtˌhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
Having a noble and generous spirit; magnanimous.
noble and generous in spirit

Examples

She was known as a greathearted leader who always put her team first.
His greathearted actions inspired many people to volunteer for the cause.
Despite the criticism, he remained greathearted and forgave his opponents.

Synset Examples

a greathearted general
a magnanimous conqueror

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magnanimous - generous and forgiving, especially toward a rival
  • Antonym: mean-spirited - having a petty, unkind, or selfish disposition

Etymology

Formed from 'great' + 'hearted', meaning 'having a great heart.'
