strong-minded

1. ADJ

/ strɔŋ ˈmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / strɒŋ ˈmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
having a determined and resolute will; not easily influenced by others
having a determined will

Examples

She is a strong-minded leader who never backs down in the face of adversity.
His strong-minded approach helped the team overcome many obstacles.
Though young, she proved to be remarkably strong-minded during the negotiations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: determined - having made a firm decision and being resolved not to change it
  • Antonym: weak-willed - lacking determination or resolve

2. ADJ

/ strɔŋ ˈmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / strɒŋ ˈmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
having or showing vigorous independence of thought and judgment; determined and resolute.
having firm, independent opinions

Examples

Despite criticism from her peers, she remained strong-minded and stood by her beliefs.
He admired her strong-minded approach to solving difficult problems.
In debates, a strong-minded individual rarely backs down from their position.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: determined - having made a firm decision and being resolved not to change it
  • Antonym: weak-minded - lacking firmness of mind or will
