alphabetisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌæl.fə.bə.tɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌæl.fə.bə.taɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of arranging items in alphabetical order
arranging in alphabetical order

Examples

The alphabetisation of the files made it easier to find important documents.
Correct alphabetisation is crucial in library catalogues and directories.
Teachers often teach alphabetisation to help children organise words effectively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alphabetization - the act of arranging alphabetically (American spelling)

Etymology

From French 'alphabétisation', from 'alphabet'.
