B.O.

1. NOUN

/ bi oʊ / (AmE), / biː əʊ / (BrE)
an unpleasant smell from the human body caused by perspiration, especially when not washed regularly; body odor
unpleasant body odor from sweating
informal

Examples

After a long day without a shower, he was embarrassed by his B.O..
Some deodorants are effective at masking B.O. even after exercise.
She worried that her B.O. might be noticeable during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: body odor - unpleasant smell from the body, especially when not washed

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'body odor', first used in American advertising in the 20th century.
