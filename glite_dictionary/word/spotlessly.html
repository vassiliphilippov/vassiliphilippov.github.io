spotlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈspɑːt.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈspɒt.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a perfectly clean or immaculate manner; without any spot or stain.
in a perfectly clean manner

Examples

The kitchen was spotlessly clean before the guests arrived.
She always dresses spotlessly, paying attention to every detail.
The hotel rooms are maintained spotlessly for every new guest.

Synset Examples

spotlessly clean

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immaculately - in an extremely clean or tidy way

Etymology

From 'spotless' + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
