ameer

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɪr / (AmE), / əˈmɪə / (BrE)
An independent ruler or chieftain, especially in Africa or Arabia.
independent ruler or chieftain

Examples

The ameer governed his people with wisdom and justice.
Several tribes swore allegiance to the new ameer of the region.
The British negotiated with the local ameer to ensure safe passage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emir - a Muslim (especially Arab) ruler or chieftain

Etymology

From Arabic 'amīr' (commander, ruler).
