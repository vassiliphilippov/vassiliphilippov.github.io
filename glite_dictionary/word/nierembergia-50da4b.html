nierembergia

1. NOUN

/ niːˌɛrəmˈbɜːrdʒiə / (AmE), / nɪərɛmˈbɜːrdʒiə / (BrE)
Any of several plants of the genus Nierembergia, typically grown for their upturned bell-shaped flowers.
a plant of the genus Nierembergia

Examples

The garden was filled with nierembergia, adding soft color to the borders.
Many gardeners appreciate nierembergia for its delicate, bell-shaped blooms.
She chose to plant nierembergia near the walkway because of its neat, low-growing habit.

Etymology

Named after Spanish Jesuit and naturalist Juan Eusebio Nieremberg (1595–1658).
