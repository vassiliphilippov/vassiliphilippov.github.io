appreciated

1. ADJ

/ əˈpriʃiˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / əˈpriːʃieɪtɪd / (BrE)
fully understood or grasped; recognized in its true nature or extent.
fully understood or recognized

Examples

The subtle differences between the two theories were not appreciated by the general public.
The full significance of his discovery was only appreciated years later.
Many risks in the plan were not appreciated until problems began to arise.

Synset Examples

dangers not yet appreciated
these apprehended truths
a thing comprehended is a thing known as fully as it can be known

Related Terms

  • Synonym: understood - perceived or grasped the meaning of something
  • Synonym: recognized - acknowledged or identified as being true or valid
  • Antonym: misunderstood - failed to understand the meaning or significance of something

Etymology

Past participle of 'appreciate,' from Latin 'appretiare' (to estimate, to value).
