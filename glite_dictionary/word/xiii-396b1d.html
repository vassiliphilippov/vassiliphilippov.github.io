xiii

1. NUM

/ ˌθɜrˈtiːn / (AmE), / ˌθɜːˈtiːn / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for thirteen
Roman numeral for thirteen

Examples

The king was crowned in the year XIII of his reign.
An apartment on the XIII floor is available for rent.
The volume labeled XIII contains the final chapters of the series.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thirteen - the number 13; one more than twelve

Etymology

From Latin, using the Roman numeral system: X (10) + III (3) = XIII (13).
