capacious

1. ADJ

/ kəˈpeɪʃəs / (AmE), / kəˈpeɪʃəs / (BrE)
having a lot of space inside; roomy or able to contain a large amount
roomy or spacious

Examples

She bought a capacious suitcase for her long trip overseas.
The library contains a capacious reading room with tall windows.
Despite its compact exterior, the car has a surprisingly capacious interior.

Synset Examples

she carried a capacious bag

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spacious - having ample space
  • Antonym: cramped - uncomfortably small or restricted

Etymology

From Latin capax (capable of holding much), from capere (to take, hold).
