older

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈəʊldə / (BrE)
having gained skill or experience through long service or practice; more experienced.
more experienced through long practice

Examples

After years of working in the courtroom, the older lawyers were respected for their wisdom.
The older technicians quickly solved the problem that baffled their younger colleagues.
She listened to the older members of the team, knowing their advice came from experience.

Synset Examples

an old offender
the older soldiers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: veteran - a person with long experience in a particular field
  • Antonym: novice - someone with little or no experience

2. ADJ

/ ˈoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈəʊldə / (BrE)
having lived for more years; advanced in age; not young.
more advanced in age

Examples

Her older brother just graduated from college this year.
The older residents of the village gather every morning in the park.
As he grew older, his interests changed and matured.

Synset Examples

aged members of the society
elderly residents could remember the construction of the first skyscraper
senior citizen

Related Terms

  • Antonym: younger - having lived fewer years; less advanced in age
  • Synonym: elderly - old or advanced in age, especially about people
  • Synonym: aged - having lived a long life; of advanced age

3. ADJ

/ ˈoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈəʊldə / (BrE)
(postnominal) Used after the name of the elder of two people with the same name, especially to distinguish a father from his son.
postnominal to denote elder of two with same name

Examples

Many people referred to him as Bill Adams, older, to distinguish him from his son.
On official documents, he is listed as Frank Turner, older.
The business was started by John Smith, older, in the early 1900s.

Synset Examples

Bill Adams, Sr.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: younger - used after the name of the younger of two people with the same name, especially a son
  • Synonym: senior - used after a name to denote the elder of two people, typically a father
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02107311-s
Synset POS: s
