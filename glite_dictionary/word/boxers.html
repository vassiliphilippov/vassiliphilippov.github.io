boxers

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑksərz / (AmE), / ˈbɒksəz / (BrE)
A type of men's underpants that are loose-fitting and similar in style to shorts.
loose-fitting men's underpants

Examples

He prefers wearing boxers instead of briefs for comfort.
The store sells a variety of boxers with colorful patterns.
After doing laundry, he realized he was out of clean boxers.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: briefs - close-fitting men's underpants

Etymology

Named for their similarity to shorts worn by boxers in the sport of boxing.
