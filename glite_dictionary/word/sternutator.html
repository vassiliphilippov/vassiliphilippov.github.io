sternutator

1. NOUN

/ stɜrˈnuːteɪtər / (AmE), / stɜːˈnjuːtətə / (BrE)
a chemical substance that causes sneezing, coughing, and tears, often used as a riot control agent
chemical causing sneezing and tears

Examples

The police released a sternutator into the crowd to disperse the protesters.
Exposure to the sternutator made everyone's eyes water uncontrollably.
A sternutator can cause significant discomfort by inducing sneezing and coughing.

Synset Examples

police used a sternutatory to subdue the mob

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tear gas - gas that causes severe eye irritation and tearing, used in riot control

Etymology

From Latin 'sternutatorius', from 'sternutatio' meaning 'a sneeze'.
