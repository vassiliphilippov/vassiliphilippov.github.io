dyspnoeal

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈnoʊ.i.əl / (AmE), / dɪsˈnəʊ.i.əl / (BrE)
Experiencing difficulty in breathing or shortness of breath.
having difficulty breathing
medical

Examples

The patient became dyspnoeal after climbing one flight of stairs.
During the asthma attack, she was visibly dyspnoeal and required immediate assistance.
Doctors described the man as dyspnoeal due to his severe lung infection.

Synset Examples

breathless at thought of what I had done
breathless from running
followed the match with breathless interest

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breathless - unable to breathe easily; short of breath
  • Synonym: dyspnoea - difficult or labored breathing (noun)

Etymology

From Greek 'dys-' (bad, difficult) + 'pnoia' (breathing).
