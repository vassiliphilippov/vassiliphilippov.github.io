imbecilic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪmbəˈsɪlɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪmbəˈsɪlɪk / (BrE)
extremely stupid or foolish; showing a lack of good judgment.
very stupid or foolish

Examples

His imbecilic remarks embarrassed everyone at the meeting.
It would be imbecilic to leave your car unlocked in this neighborhood.
I can't believe you made such an imbecilic mistake on the exam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiotic - extremely foolish or senseless
  • Antonym: sensible - showing good sense or judgment

Etymology

From Latin 'imbecillus' (weak, feeble), later used in psychology to denote intellectual disability (now outdated and offensive).
