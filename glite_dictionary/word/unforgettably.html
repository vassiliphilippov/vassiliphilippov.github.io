unforgettably

1. ADV

/ ʌn.fərˈɡɛ.tə.bli / (AmE), / ʌn.fəˈɡɛ.tə.bli / (BrE)
In a way that is very memorable and difficult to forget.
in a memorable manner

Examples

The concert ended unforgettably with an encore that brought the audience to its feet.
She spoke unforgettably about her experiences during the ceremony.
The sunset over the mountains was unforgettably beautiful.

Synset Examples

Horowitz could play Chopin memorably

Related Terms

  • Synonym: memorably - in a way that is memorable
