to owe

1. VERB

/ oʊ / (AmE), / əʊ / (BrE)
to have something, such as success, knowledge, or an idea, because of another person or thing; to be indebted to in an abstract sense
be indebted for ideas or influence

Examples

Modern physics owes a great deal to Einstein's theories.
She owes much of her success to her mentor's guidance and support.
The company owes its reputation to years of dedicated service.

Synset Examples

This new theory owes much to Einstein's Relativity Theory

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indebted - owing gratitude, recognition, or influence to someone or something

Etymology

From Old English āgan (to own, possess), related to German 'eigen' (own).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02737689-v
Synset POS: v

2. VERB

/ oʊ / (AmE), / əʊ / (BrE)
to have an obligation to pay or repay money or to return a favor to someone
be obliged to pay or repay

Examples

You still owe me fifty dollars from last week.
The team owes its success to careful planning and hard work.
She owed both her parents an apology for her behavior.

Synset Examples

She owes me $200
I still owe for the car
The thesis owes much to his adviser

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to repay - to pay back money that is owed
  • Antonym: to lend - to give someone money or something temporarily with the expectation of return

Etymology

From Old English *āgan* (to own, possess) via Middle English *owen*.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02258764-v
Synset POS: v

3. VERB

/ oʊ / (AmE), / əʊ / (BrE)
to be under an obligation to pay or repay money or a favor to someone
be obliged to pay or repay

Examples

I still owe him fifty dollars from last week.
She owes her success to her supportive family.
They owed a large sum of money to the bank.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to repay - to pay back money that you have borrowed or received
  • Antonym: to borrow - to take and use something belonging to someone else with the intention of returning it

Etymology

From Old English āgan (to possess, own), related to ownership and obligation.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02258963-v
