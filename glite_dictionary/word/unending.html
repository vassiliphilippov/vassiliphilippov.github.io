unending

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
having or seeming to have no end; continuing forever or indefinitely
having no end; endless

Examples

Their struggle for justice seemed unending as new obstacles kept appearing.
We sat through what felt like an unending lecture on ancient history.
The unending noise from the construction site made it hard to concentrate.

Synset Examples

the ageless themes of love and revenge
eternal truths
life everlasting
hell's perpetual fires
the unending bliss of heaven

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endless - having or seeming to have no end
  • Synonym: eternal - lasting forever; without end
  • Antonym: finite - having an end or limit

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'ending'.
