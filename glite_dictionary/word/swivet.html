swivet

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪvət / (AmE), / ˈswɪvɪt / (BrE)
A state of agitation, panic, or extreme confusion.
state of agitation or panic
informal

Examples

She was in such a swivet before her big presentation that she almost forgot her notes.
The unexpected call from her boss sent him into a sudden swivet.
Don't get yourself into a swivet over small problems; take a deep breath and relax.

Synset Examples

it threw her into a swivet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: panic - A sudden overwhelming fear or anxiety
  • Antonym: calm - A state of emotional tranquility
