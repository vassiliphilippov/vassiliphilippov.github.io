Kyrgyzstani

1. ADJ

/ kɪrˌɡɪˈstɑːni / (AmE), / kɪəˌɡɪˈstɑːni / (BrE)
relating to Kyrgyzstan, its people, or its culture.
relating to Kyrgyzstan or its people

Examples

The Kyrgyzstani government announced new trade policies this week.
She enjoyed tasting Kyrgyzstani cuisine during her trip to Bishkek.
A Kyrgyzstani artist won an international photography competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kyrgyz - relating to the people, language, or culture of Kyrgyzstan, especially the ethnic group

Etymology

From Kyrgyzstan + -i (forming adjectives for nationality or relation).
