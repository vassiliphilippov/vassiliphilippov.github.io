mingle-mangle

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪŋɡəlˌmæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈmɪŋɡl̩ˌmaŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
a confused mixture or jumble of different things; a motley assortment
a confused mixture or jumble
archaic

Examples

The attic was a mingle-mangle of old toys, books, and forgotten clothes.
Her thoughts became a mingle-mangle after the hectic day.
The artist's studio was cluttered with a mingle-mangle of paints, brushes, and half-finished canvases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hotchpotch - a confused mixture
  • Synonym: medley - a varied mixture of things

Etymology

Reduplication of 'mingle', imitative of confused mingling.
