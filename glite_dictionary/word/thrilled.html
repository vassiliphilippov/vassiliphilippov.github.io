thrilled

1. ADJ

/ θɹɪld / (AmE), / θɹɪld / (BrE)
experiencing a strong feeling of excitement and pleasure
feeling strong excitement and pleasure

Examples

She was thrilled to receive the invitation to the award ceremony.
I'm absolutely thrilled with the results of our project.
The children were thrilled when they saw the puppies in the backyard.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: disappointed - feeling sad because something did not happen as expected
