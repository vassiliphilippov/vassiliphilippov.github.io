Elagatis

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlæɡətɪs / (AmE), / ɪˈlæɡətɪs / (BrE)
A genus of tropical marine fish in the family Carangidae; includes the rainbow runner.
genus of tropical fish (rainbow runner)

Examples

The Elagatis genus contains only one species, known as the rainbow runner.
Marine biologists study Elagatis to learn more about tropical pelagic fish habits.
Specimens of Elagatis have been found in warm oceans around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rainbow runner - a tropical pelagic fish, Elagatis bipinnulata, belonging to the genus Elagatis
