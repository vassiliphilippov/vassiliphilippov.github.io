fossiliferous

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɑːsəˈlɪfərəs / (AmE), / ˌfɒsɪˈlɪfərəs / (BrE)
(of rock or sediment) containing or bearing fossils.
containing fossils (esp. rocks or strata)

Examples

The museum displayed samples of fossiliferous rock containing well-preserved trilobites.
Geologists identified several fossiliferous strata within the canyon walls.
The discovery of fossiliferous deposits shed new light on the region's prehistoric life.

Synset Examples

fossiliferous strata

Etymology

From Latin 'fossilis' (dug up) + '-ferous' (bearing, carrying).
