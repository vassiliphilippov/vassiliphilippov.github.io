grammatically

1. ADV

/ ɡrəˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ɡrəˈmatɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that conforms to the rules of grammar.
in accordance with grammar rules

Examples

He explained the sentence grammatically to help his students understand its structure.
Her paper was well written and grammatically correct throughout.
Although he spoke fluently, he did not always speak grammatically.

Synset Examples

this child already speaks grammatically

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ungrammatically - in a manner not conforming to grammar rules

Etymology

From grammatical + -ly; grammatical from Greek grammatike (art of letters).
