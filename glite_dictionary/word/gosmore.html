gosmore

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑzˌmɔr / (AmE), / ˈɡɒzˌmɔː / (BrE)
A European weed with yellow flowers and leaves that resemble a cat's ears, often naturalized in North America.
a yellow-flowered European weed

Examples

The field was dotted with gosmore among the taller grasses.
Botanists found that gosmore had spread rapidly in the region's meadows.
She recognized the distinctive leaves of the gosmore from her gardening books.
