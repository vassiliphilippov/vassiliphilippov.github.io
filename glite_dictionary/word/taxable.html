taxable

1. ADJ

/ ˈtæksəbəl / (AmE), / ˈtæksəbəl / (BrE)
(of income, goods, or property) subject to taxation by law.
subject to taxation

Examples

All of her taxable income must be reported to the government.
Gifts under a certain value are not considered taxable by law.
Their accountant explained which items would be taxable on their next return.

Synset Examples

taxable income
nonexempt property

Related Terms

  • Antonym: nontaxable - not subject to taxation

Etymology

From tax + -able, following the pattern of Latin-derived adjectives meaning 'capable of being acted upon'.
