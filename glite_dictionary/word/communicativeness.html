communicativeness

1. NOUN

/ kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪtɪvnəs / (AmE), / kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪtɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being willing or able to communicate easily with others
willingness to communicate

Examples

Her communicativeness made her an excellent team leader who always kept everyone informed.
A certain level of communicativeness is essential for success in customer service roles.
Despite his communicativeness at work, he was rather quiet at home.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: reticence - the quality of being unwilling to communicate or reveal thoughts
  • Synonym: talkativeness - the quality of talking a lot

Etymology

From 'communicative' + '-ness', indicating the state or quality of being communicative.
