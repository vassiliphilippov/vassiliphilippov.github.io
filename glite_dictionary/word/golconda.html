Golconda

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlˈkɑndə / (AmE), / ɡɒlˈkɒndə / (BrE)
a source of great wealth, especially one that produces abundant riches
a source of great wealth

Examples

The discovery of oil turned the small town into a veritable Golconda.
The museum's art collection proved to be a Golconda for investors.
He hoped his invention would become his personal Golconda, securing his fortune.

Etymology

From Golconda, the name of a ruined city in southern India, once known for its diamond mines.
