gumptious

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡʌmpʃəs / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmpʃəs / (BrE)
Energetic, enterprising, and resourceful, especially in promoting an undertaking or enterprise.
energetic and enterprising

Examples

The new manager was exceptionally gumptious, quickly turning the failing business around.
Her gumptious attitude made her a standout in the competitive marketing department.
Despite limited resources, the gumptious team launched their project ahead of schedule.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enterprising - having or showing initiative and resourcefulness
  • Synonym: energetic - showing or involving great activity or vitality
  • Antonym: uninspired - lacking in originality or initiative

Etymology

Formed from 'gumption' + '-ous', originally Scottish in origin.
