punchayet

1. NOUN

/ pənˈtʃɑː.jɛt / (AmE), / pʌnˈtʃaɪ.ɪt / (BrE)
(in India and southern Pakistan) a local village council that administers community affairs and resolves disputes.
village council in India or Pakistan

Examples

The elders gathered at the punchayet to resolve the villagers' dispute.
Many decisions affecting local governance are made by the punchayet.
The tradition of the punchayet dates back several centuries in rural India.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: panchayat - alternative form, village council in India or Pakistan

Etymology

From Hindi 'pañcāyat', from Sanskrit 'pañcāyat', meaning group of five (originally referring to five elders).
