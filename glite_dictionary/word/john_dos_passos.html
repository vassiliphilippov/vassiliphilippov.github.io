John Dos Passos

1. PROPN

/ dʒɑn doʊs pəˈsoʊs / (AmE), / dʒɒn dɒs ˈpæsɒs / (BrE)
(1896–1970) American novelist known for his works depicting life in the United States, particularly the 'U.S.A.' trilogy.
American novelist (1896–1970)

Examples

Many critics consider John Dos Passos one of the major novelists of the early twentieth century.
