cacodemonic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkækoʊdɪˈmɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌkækəʊdɪˈmɒnɪk / (BrE)
Relating to evil spirits or demons; demonic or possessed by evil influences.
relating to evil spirits

Examples

The ancient text described a cacodemonic presence haunting the village after dark.
Some horror films rely on cacodemonic imagery to create an unsettling atmosphere.
The rituals were believed to summon cacodemonic forces into the mortal world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cacodemon - an evil spirit or demon
  • Synonym: demonic - relating to demons or evil spirits
  • Antonym: angelic - resembling an angel; innocent or good

Etymology

From Greek kako- (bad, evil) + daimon (spirit, demon), modeled on 'cacodemon'.
