ecdysiast

1. NOUN

/ ɛkˈdɪziˌæst / (AmE), / ɛkˈdɪziːəst / (BrE)
a performer who provides erotic entertainment by slowly undressing, typically to music; a striptease artist
erotic performer who undresses; striptease artist

Examples

The famous dancer began her career as an ecdysiast in the cabarets of Paris.
An ecdysiast performs with skill and artistry, captivating audiences with each act.
She was acclaimed as the leading ecdysiast of her generation, known for her elegance and wit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stripper - a person who removes their clothes as entertainment

Etymology

Coined by H. L. Mencken (1940) from Greek ecdysis (shedding) + -ist (one who practices).
