DPH

1. NOUN

/ di pi eɪtʃ / (AmE), / diː piː eɪtʃ / (BrE)
Doctor of Public Health; a doctoral degree in preventive medicine and public health.
doctoral degree in public health

Examples

She earned her DPH from a prestigious university to further her career in community health.
Many global health leaders hold a DPH as their highest academic qualification.
The candidate’s DPH focused on improving preventive healthcare systems in rural areas.

Etymology

Abbreviation of Doctor of Public Health.
