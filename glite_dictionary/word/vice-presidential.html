vice-presidential

1. ADJ

/ ˌvaɪsˌprɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌvaɪsˌprɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl / (BrE)
relating to a vice president or vice-presidency.
relating to a vice president

Examples

The vice-presidential debate attracted millions of viewers across the country.
She was chosen as the vice-presidential candidate for the upcoming election.
His vice-presidential duties included representing the president at official events.

Synset Examples

Vice-presidential debates

Related Terms

  • Antonym: presidential - relating to a president or the presidency

Etymology

vice- (from Latin vice, meaning 'in place of') + presidential.
