anemoclinometer

1. NOUN

/ ˌænɪmoʊklɪˈnɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / ˌanɪməʊklɪˈnɒmɪtə / (BrE)
An instrument used to measure the angle or inclination of the wind relative to the horizontal plane.
instrument for measuring wind inclination

Examples

The meteorologist adjusted the anemoclinometer before recording the wind's inclination.
A well-calibrated anemoclinometer is crucial for accurate atmospheric measurements.
During flight tests, the anemoclinometer provided vital data on crosswind angles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anemometer - instrument for measuring wind speed

Etymology

From Greek 'anemos' (wind) + 'klinein' (to lean) + '-meter' (measuring device).
