moronity

1. NOUN

/ mɔˈrɑnɪti / (AmE), / mɒˈrɒnɪti / (BrE)
(dated, offensive) Mild mental retardation; the state or condition of being a moron.
dated term for mild mental retardation
archaic offensive medical

Examples

The outdated concept of moronity once appeared in early psychological literature.
He studied the historical classifications of mental deficiency, including moronity and imbecility.
Attitudes toward moronity in the past reflected a lack of understanding about intellectual disabilities.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: idiocy - profound mental retardation; state of being an idiot
  • Degree Higher: imbecility - moderate mental retardation; state of being an imbecile

Etymology

From 'moron' + '-ity'. 'Moron' from Greek mōros (foolish, dull).
