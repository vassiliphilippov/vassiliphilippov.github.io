to deodourise

1. VERB

/ diˈoʊdəraɪz / (AmE), / diˈəʊdəraɪz / (BrE)
to remove or neutralize an unpleasant smell from something
eliminate odor from something
british

Examples

You can deodourise your shoes by sprinkling baking soda inside overnight.
I always deodourise the kitchen bin to keep it smelling fresh.
The cleaning staff deodourised all the rooms before the guests arrived.

Synset Examples

This stick will deodorize your armpits

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to deodorize - remove or neutralize odor (main American spelling)

Etymology

From de- (removal) + odour (Latin *odor*, smell) + -ise (British verb-forming suffix).
