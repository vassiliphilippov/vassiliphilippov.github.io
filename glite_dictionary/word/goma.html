Goma

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡoʊmə / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊmə / (BrE)
a city in eastern Democratic Republic of the Congo, situated at the northern end of Lake Kivu near the border with Rwanda.
city in eastern Democratic Republic of the Congo

Examples

During their trip to Africa, they visited Goma to see Lake Kivu and the surrounding volcanoes.
Many humanitarian organizations operate in Goma due to the region's ongoing conflicts.
The border crossing between Goma and Rwanda is one of the busiest in Central Africa.
