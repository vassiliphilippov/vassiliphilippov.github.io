traveling

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrævəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of going from one place to another, especially over a distance
the act of journeying from place to place

Examples

Her job as a consultant involves a lot of traveling throughout the year.
After months of traveling, he was happy to finally be home.
They saved money for traveling around Europe during the summer.

Synset Examples

he enjoyed selling but he hated the travel

Related Terms

  • Synonym: journey - an act of traveling from one place to another
  • Synonym: commuting - regular travel between home and work or school
  • Antonym: stationary - not moving or not intended to be moved

Etymology

From Middle English *travailen* (to journey), from Old French *travailler* (to toil, journey).
