shiv

1. NOUN

/ ʃɪv / (AmE), / ʃɪv / (BrE)
an improvised knife, especially one used as a weapon in prison or by criminals
improvised knife, especially in prison
informal slang

Examples

The inmate was caught hiding a shiv in his mattress during the inspection.
He made a crude shiv from a sharpened toothbrush handle.
Police found a shiv tucked inside the suspect's boot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knife - a cutting instrument with a blade and handle
  • Synonym: dagger - a short knife with a pointed and edged blade, used as a weapon

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from the Romani word 'chiv' meaning 'blade' or 'knife'.
