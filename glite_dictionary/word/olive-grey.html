olive-grey

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːlɪv ɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv ɡreɪ / (BrE)
a shade of grey tinged with olive green
grey with an olive green tint

Examples

She chose an olive-grey couch to complement her living room decor.
The sky turned an olive-grey as the storm approached.
His uniform was a distinct olive-grey, making him stand out among the soldiers.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: olive-green - a dark yellowish-green color, like that of olives
  • Degree Lower: grey - a color between black and white
