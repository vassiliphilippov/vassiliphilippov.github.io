upcurved

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌpˌkɝvd / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌkɜːvd / (BrE)
having a shape that curves or bends upward
curving upward

Examples

The bird's beak was distinctively upcurved, helping it reach nectar in deep flowers.
She admired the elegant, upcurved lines of the antique chair.
Many tropical fish have fins that are slightly upcurved for better movement.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: downcurved - having a shape that curves or bends downward
