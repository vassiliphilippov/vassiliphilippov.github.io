Clinton

1. PROPN

/ ˈklɪn.tən / (AmE), / ˈklɪn.tən / (BrE)
The surname of Bill Clinton, the 42nd President of the United States (1993–2001), known for his domestic and international policies.
42nd President of the United States (Bill Clinton)

Examples

President Clinton introduced several key economic reforms during his administration.
Many historians debate the legacy of Clinton's foreign policy decisions.
Clinton's presidency was marked by a period of economic growth and political controversy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hillary Clinton - American politician and former Secretary of State, wife of Bill Clinton

Etymology

Of English origin, from a place name meaning 'settlement on the River Glyme'.
