Vanessa atalanta

1. NOUN

/ vəˈnɛsə ˌætəˈlæntə / (AmE), / vəˈnɛsə ˌatəˈlantə / (BrE)
A species of butterfly, commonly known as the red admiral, found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia, with black wings featuring red and white markings.
species of butterfly (red admiral)

Examples

The Vanessa atalanta is easily recognized by the bold red bands on its wings.
Many gardeners enjoy seeing the Vanessa atalanta fluttering among the summer flowers.
The migration of Vanessa atalanta is closely studied by entomologists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red admiral - common name of Vanessa atalanta butterfly

Etymology

From Latin, where 'Vanessa' is the genus name, and 'atalanta' references the Greek mythological heroine Atalanta.
