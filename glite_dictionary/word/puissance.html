puissance

1. NOUN

/ ˈpwɪsəns / (AmE), / ˈpjuːɪsəns / (BrE)
great power, influence, or might; the ability to control or command
great power or influence

Examples

The puissance of the king was apparent in every law he enacted.
Political puissance is often measured by the ability to influence public opinion.
She admired the puissance of his words during the debate.

Synset Examples

the puissance of the labor vote

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power - the ability or capacity to do something or act in a particular way
  • Synonym: might - great and impressive power or strength

Etymology

From Old French 'puissance', from 'puissant' (powerful), from Latin 'posse' (to be able).
