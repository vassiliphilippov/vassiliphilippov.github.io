errorless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛr.ɚ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈɛr.ə.ləs / (BrE)
Not containing any mistakes; completely free from error.
without error; flawless

Examples

The team played an errorless game, impressing both fans and critics.
She submitted an errorless report after carefully reviewing her work several times.
Their performance on the test was nearly errorless, with only one minor mistake.

Synset Examples

an errorless baseball game

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flawless - without any imperfections or faults
  • Synonym: perfect - having no mistakes or defects; complete
  • Antonym: imperfect - having faults or defects; not perfect

Etymology

Formed from 'error' + '-less', meaning 'without error'.
