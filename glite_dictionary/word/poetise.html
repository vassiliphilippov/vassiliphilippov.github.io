to poetise

1. VERB

/ ˈpoʊ.ɪˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpəʊ.ɪ.taɪz / (BrE)
to compose poetry or to express or treat in verse form
to compose poetry or write in verse

Examples

After reading the story, she decided to poetise it for her creative writing class.
He often tries to poetise even the most mundane experiences in life.
The ancient legends were poetised by writers throughout the centuries.

Synset Examples

He versified the ancient saga

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to versify - to turn into verse; to compose poetry

Etymology

From French 'poétiser', from Greek 'poiētēs' (poet).
