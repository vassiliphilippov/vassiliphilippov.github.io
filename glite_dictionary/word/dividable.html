dividable

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈvaɪdəbl̩ / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪdəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be divided, especially without leaving a remainder
able to be divided (esp. evenly)

Examples

The number 20 is easily dividable by five and four.
The cake was dividable into equal slices for the guests.
Is twelve dividable by three without a remainder?

Synset Examples

15 is dividable by 3

Related Terms

  • Synonym: divisible - able to be divided without leaving a remainder

Etymology

From 'divide' + '-able' suffix, following standard English adjective formation.
