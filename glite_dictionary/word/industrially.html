industrially

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈdʌstriəli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəli / (BrE)
by means of industry or industrial methods; in a way relating to industry or manufacturing
by industrial means; relating to industry

Examples

Many goods are now industrially produced to meet global demand.
The region is industrially developed, with numerous factories and processing plants.
Cheese made industrially often tastes different from traditional farmhouse varieties.

Synset Examples

industrially produced

Related Terms

  • Antonym: manually - by hand rather than by industrial or automated means

Etymology

From 'industrial' + '-ly', with 'industrial' from French 'industriel', from Latin 'industria' (diligence)
