unabused

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈbjuːzd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈbjuːzd / (BrE)
not physically abused or harmed; treated properly and with care
not abused; treated properly

Examples

The rescued dogs were finally safe and unabused in their new homes.
She was grateful to have grown up unabused and loved by her family.
The unabused property stood in sharp contrast to those that had been vandalized.
