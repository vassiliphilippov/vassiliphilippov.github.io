outlined

1. ADJ

/ ˈaʊtˌlaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌlaɪnd / (BrE)
having a shape, edge, or boundary that is clearly shown or defined against a background.
clearly defined in shape or boundary

Examples

The mountain was outlined against the bright morning sky.
Her silhouette was outlined by the light coming through the doorway.
The artist drew an outlined sketch of the building before adding any details.

Synset Examples

hills defined against the evening sky
the setting sun showed the outlined figure of a man standing on the hill

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defined - having distinct or clear outlines or boundaries
