ordinariness

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔr.dəˈner.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˌɔː.dɪˈneə.ri.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being ordinary or unremarkable
the state of being ordinary

Examples

She was uncomfortable with the ordinariness of her daily routine.
The film's strength lies in its honest portrayal of ordinariness.
He never aspired to fame, content instead with the ordinariness of a simple life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mediocrity - the state of being average or unremarkable
  • Antonym: extraordinariness - the quality of being extraordinary

Etymology

From 'ordinary' + '-ness', with 'ordinary' from Latin 'ordinarius' (orderly, regular).
