suspensor

1. NOUN

/ səˈspɛnsər / (AmE), / səˈspɛnsə / (BrE)
a support garment worn by men to protect or support the genitals during strenuous physical activity
support garment for the genitals

Examples

He wore a suspensor to protect himself while playing rugby.
The doctor recommended a suspensor for added support after the injury.
Athletes often use a suspensor during intense training sessions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jockstrap - an elasticized men's undergarment for supporting the genitals during sports

Etymology

From Late Latin 'suspensor' (a supporter), from Latin 'suspendere' (to hang up).
