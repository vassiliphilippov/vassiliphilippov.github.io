Guadalajara

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡwɑdəlaˈhɑrə / (AmE), / ˌɡwɑːdələˈhɑːrə / (BrE)
a city in southwestern Mexico, known for its colonial architecture and importance as a cultural and economic center
city in southwestern Mexico

Examples

We visited Guadalajara to explore its famous colonial architecture and vibrant markets.
The international book fair in Guadalajara attracts authors and publishers from around the world.
After living in Guadalajara for a year, she became fluent in Spanish and deeply appreciated the local culture.

Etymology

From Spanish Guadalajara, named after the city of Guadalajara in Spain, from Arabic wadi al-hijarah (valley of stones).
