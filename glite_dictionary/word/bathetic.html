bathetic

1. ADJ

/ bəˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / bəˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
(of language, art, or emotion) excessively or insincerely sentimental or emotional, especially in a way that is intended to be touching but instead seems trivial or ridiculous.
excessively sentimental or emotional

Examples

The film's ending was so bathetic that it made the audience laugh instead of cry.
Her bathetic speech failed to move anyone because it felt forced and insincere.
Critics dismissed the novel as bathetic and overly sentimental.

Synset Examples

a bathetic novel
maudlin expressions of sympathy
mushy effusiveness
a schmaltzy song
sentimental soap operas
slushy poetry

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maudlin - tearfully or weakly emotional; overly sentimental
  • Synonym: sentimental - having or showing excessive or superficial emotion
  • Antonym: poignant - evoking a keen sense of sadness or regret

Etymology

From 'bathos', itself from Greek βάθος (bathos) meaning 'depth', influenced by analogy with 'pathetic'.
