astounded

1. ADJ

/ əˈstaʊndɪd / (AmE), / əˈstaʊndɪd / (BrE)
filled with overwhelming surprise or amazement; greatly astonished
overwhelmed by surprise or shock

Examples

The guests were astounded by the breathtaking view from the mountaintop.
She looked astounded when she heard the unexpected news.
We were astounded at how quickly the team finished the huge project.

Synset Examples

an amazed audience gave the magician a standing ovation
I stood enthralled, astonished by the vastness and majesty of the cathedral
astounded viewers wept at the pictures from the Oklahoma City bombing
stood in stunned silence
stunned scientists found not one but at least three viruses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amazed - filled with great surprise or wonder
  • Synonym: astonished - very surprised; shocked
  • Antonym: unimpressed - not feeling admiration, interest, or surprise

Etymology

Past participle of 'astound', from Middle English astoned (stunned, astonished).
