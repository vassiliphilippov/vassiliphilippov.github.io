haemic

1. ADJ

/ ˈhiːmɪk / (AmE), / ˈhiːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to, containing, or affecting blood.
relating to blood
medical

Examples

The doctor suspected a haemic disorder after reviewing the blood tests.
This medication is used to treat haemic deficiencies in patients with anemia.
Researchers are studying the haemic effects of the new drug on red blood cell production.

Synset Examples

a hematic cyst
a hematic crisis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hematic - relating to or affecting the blood; another term for 'haemic'

Etymology

From Greek 'haima' meaning 'blood'.
