perfervid

1. ADJ

/ pərˈfɝː.vɪd / (AmE), / pəˈfɜː.vɪd / (BrE)
Characterized by intense and passionate feeling; extremely fervent or ardent.
intensely passionate or fervent

Examples

Her perfervid speech moved the audience to tears.
The debate was marked by perfervid exchanges between the candidates.
He attacked the injustice with perfervid zeal.

Synset Examples

ardent love
an ardent lover
a fervent desire to change society
a fervent admirer
fiery oratory
an impassioned appeal
a torrid love affair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fervent - having or displaying passionate intensity
  • Synonym: ardent - very enthusiastic or passionate
  • Antonym: impassive - not feeling or showing emotion

Etymology

From Latin 'perfervidus', meaning 'very hot' or 'fiery'.
