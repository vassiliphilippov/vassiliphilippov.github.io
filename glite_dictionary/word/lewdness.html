lewdness

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːdnəs / (AmE), / ˈluːdnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being obscene or indecent, especially in a sexual way
obscene or indecent behavior

Examples

The judge warned that lewdness would not be tolerated in the courtroom.
He was arrested for public lewdness after behaving inappropriately at the festival.
The film was criticized for scenes of excessive lewdness that some viewers found offensive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obscenity - offensive or indecent behavior or expression
  • Antonym: modesty - the quality of being modest and avoiding indecency

Etymology

From Middle English 'lewdnesse', from 'lewd' + '-ness', originally meaning 'ignorance', later 'indecency'.
