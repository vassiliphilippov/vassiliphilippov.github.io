openhanded

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊ.pənˈhæn.dɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊ.pənˈhæn.dɪd / (BrE)
generous in giving; willing to give or share freely with others.
generous; freely giving

Examples

Her openhanded nature made her popular among friends and family.
The company is known for its openhanded support of local charities.
Despite his wealth, he remained openhanded with his time and advice.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: generous - willing to give more than expected or necessary
  • Antonym: stingy - unwilling to give or spend; ungenerous

Etymology

From open + handed, referring to the image of a hand open to give or share.
