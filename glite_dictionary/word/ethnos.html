ethnos

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛθ.nɒs / (AmE), / ˈɛθ.nɒs / (BrE)
a group of people of the same race or nationality who share a distinctive culture
cultural or ethnic group

Examples

Anthropologists study how an ethnos maintains its traditions across generations.
The festival celebrated the heritage of every ethnos within the city.
Language plays a key role in the identity of an ethnos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ethnic group - a community with shared cultural, racial, or national background

Etymology

From Greek ἔθνος (ethnos), meaning 'nation' or 'people'.
