unmanageably

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈmænɪdʒəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈmænɪdʒəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is difficult or impossible to control or manage.
in an unmanageable manner

Examples

The river was flowing unmanageably fast after the heavy rains.
His workload became unmanageably heavy, forcing him to delegate tasks.
The weeds in the garden spread unmanageably over the summer.

Synset Examples

‘This house is unmanageably large,’ she complained

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unmanageable - difficult or impossible to manage or control
