meshuga

1. ADJ

/ məˈʃʊɡə / (AmE), / məˈʃʊɡə / (BrE)
senseless or crazy; behaving in an irrational or foolish manner
senseless; crazy (from Yiddish)

Examples

Anyone who tries to swim across that river must be meshuga.
My grandmother always said my fashion choices were a little meshuga.
He came up with a meshuga idea that no one took seriously.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged; extremely foolish or irrational
  • Antonym: sane - having a healthy mind; reasonable and sensible

Etymology

From Yiddish משוגע‎ (meshugge), from Hebrew מְשֻׁגָּע‎ (meshuggá‘), meaning 'crazy'.
