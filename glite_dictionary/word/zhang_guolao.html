Zhang Guolao

1. PROPN

/ dʒɑŋ ˈɡwɔːˌlaʊ / (AmE), / dʒæŋ ˈɡwɔːˌlaʊ / (BrE)
In Chinese mythology, one of the Eight Immortals of Taoism, often depicted riding a donkey backwards and known for wisdom and magical abilities.
a Taoist immortal from Chinese mythology

Examples

In ancient tales, Zhang Guolao is famous for riding his donkey backwards.
Many paintings of the Eight Immortals include Zhang Guolao with his magical bamboo drum.
The legend of Zhang Guolao reflects themes of longevity and enlightenment in Taoism.

Etymology

From Mandarin 张果老 (Zhāng Guǒlǎo), a legendary figure from Taoist folklore.
