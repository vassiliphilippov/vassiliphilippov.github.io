hidrotic

1. ADJ

/ hɪˈdrɑtɪk / (AmE), / hɪˈdrɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affecting sweat or the secretion of sweat
relating to sweat or sweating
medical

Examples

The doctor noted a hidrotic response during the patient's skin test.
Excessive hidrotic activity can be a symptom of certain medical conditions.
Some medications may have hidrotic effects, increasing perspiration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sudorific - causing or promoting sweating
  • Antonym: anhidrotic - preventing or reducing sweat production

Etymology

From Greek 'hidrōs' (sweat) + '-ic' (relating to).
