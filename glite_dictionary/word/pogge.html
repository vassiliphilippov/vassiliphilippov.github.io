pogge

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɒɡ.i / (AmE), / ˈpɒɡ.i / (BrE)
A small sea fish (Agonus cataphractus) found in the northern Atlantic, noted for its armored body and also known as the sea poacher.
small northern Atlantic fish; sea poacher

Examples

The fisherman pulled a pogge from his net and examined its bony plates.
Divers sometimes spot a pogge resting on the seafloor among the rocks.
Despite its unusual appearance, the pogge is harmless to humans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea poacher - another name for the pogge, an armored northern Atlantic fish
