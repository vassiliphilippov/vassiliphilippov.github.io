notepad

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊtˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtˌpad / (BrE)
a small pad of blank or lined paper for writing notes
pad for writing notes

Examples

She always carried a notepad to jot down ideas during meetings.
The detective flipped open his notepad and began asking questions.
Please write your grocery list on a notepad so you don’t forget anything.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: notebook - a book of blank pages for notes (usually bound)

Etymology

Compound of 'note' and 'pad'.
