parachuting

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛɹəˌʃuːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpærəˌʃuːtɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity or sport of descending to the ground from an aircraft using a parachute.
the sport of descending by parachute

Examples

After months of training, she finally tried parachuting for the first time.
Many people consider parachuting to be an extreme sport that requires courage.
The group spent the weekend parachuting over the scenic countryside.

Synset Examples

he had done a lot of parachuting in the army

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skydiving - the sport of jumping from an aircraft and performing acrobatics before deploying the parachute

Etymology

Formed from 'parachute' (from French 'parachute') + '-ing'.
