uvula

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuːvjələ / (AmE), / ˈjuːvjʊlə / (BrE)
a small, fleshy lobe that hangs down from the back of the soft palate in the mouth
fleshy lobe at back of mouth
medical

Examples

The doctor examined her throat and noticed her uvula was swollen.
Snoring can sometimes be caused by a large uvula.
During the surgery, the surgeon carefully avoided touching the uvula.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'uvula', diminutive of Latin 'uva' (grape).
