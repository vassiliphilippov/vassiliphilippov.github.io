Qom

1. PROPN

/ koʊm / (AmE), / kɒm / (BrE)
A city in northwestern Iran that is an important pilgrimage site for Shiite Muslims.
city in northwestern Iran; Shiite pilgrimage site

Examples

Many devotees travel to Qom every year to visit its holy shrines.
The seminaries in Qom are famous for their religious scholarship.
After Tehran, Qom is one of Iran’s most significant cities in Shiite Islam.
