Lady Emma Hamilton

1. PROPN

/ ˈleɪdi ˈɛmə ˈhæmɪltən / (AmE), / ˈleɪdi ˈemə ˈhæmɪltən / (BrE)
(1765–1815) An English socialite and artist's muse, best known as the mistress of Admiral Horatio Nelson.
English socialite, Nelson's mistress

Examples

The life of Lady Emma Hamilton has fascinated historians for centuries.
Lady Emma Hamilton is often depicted in portraits by the artist George Romney.
The dramatic relationship between Admiral Nelson and Lady Emma Hamilton is still widely discussed today.
