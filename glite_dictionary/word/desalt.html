to desalt

1. VERB

/ diˈsɔlt / (AmE), / ˌdiːˈsɔːlt / (BrE)
to remove salt from something, especially from water
remove salt from a substance

Examples

The new facility was built to desalt seawater for drinking purposes.
Engineers are working to desalt contaminated groundwater in the region.
The company uses advanced technology to desalt crude oil during processing.

Synset Examples

desalinate water

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to desalinate - to remove salt and other minerals from water

Etymology

From de- (removal) + salt, modeled after similar formations like 'desaltinate'.
