to screenshot

1. VERB

/ ˈskriːnˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈskriːnˌʃɒt / (BrE)
to capture an image of what is displayed on a computer or device screen
capture an image of a screen

Examples

Can you screenshot that error message and send it to me?
I screenshot important conversations for my records.
He was screenshotting the webpage before it was taken down.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: screen capture - an act of capturing what is displayed on a screen

Etymology

Formed from 'screen' + 'shot', modeled after 'snapshot'.
