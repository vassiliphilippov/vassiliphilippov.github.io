gib

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪb / (AmE), / ɡɪb / (BrE)
a male cat that has been castrated
castrated male cat

Examples

After the gib was neutered, he became much calmer around the house.
The farmer kept a few gibs in the barn to control the mice population.
In old English tales, a gib was often a familiar companion to witches.

Etymology

Probably from Middle English Gib, a pet name for Gilbert, often used for male cats.
