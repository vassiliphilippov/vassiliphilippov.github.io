entertainment

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛn.tɚˈteɪn.mənt / (AmE), / ˌɛn.təˈteɪn.mənt / (BrE)
an activity or performance intended to amuse, interest, or hold the attention of an audience
activity that amuses or holds attention

Examples

The hotel offers live entertainment every evening for its guests.
She considers reading to be her favorite form of entertainment.
Family entertainment has changed dramatically over the past decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amusement - something that entertains or amuses
  • Antonym: boredom - the state of being bored, a lack of interest or excitement

Etymology

From Old French 'entretenement', based on 'entretenir' (to hold together, maintain).
