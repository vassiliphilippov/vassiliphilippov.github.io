doorcase

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔrˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈdɔːkeɪs / (BrE)
the frame of a doorway that supports and surrounds a door
frame around a doorway

Examples

The original oak doorcase was carefully restored during the renovation of the cottage.
A decorative arch was added above the doorcase to enhance the entrance.
The carpenter measured the doorcase before fitting the new door.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doorframe - the supporting frame for a door
