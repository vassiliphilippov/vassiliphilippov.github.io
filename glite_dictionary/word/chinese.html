Chinese

1. ADJ

/ tʃaɪˈniz / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈniːz / (BrE)
relating to China, its people, language, or culture
of or relating to China or its people

Examples

She loves to cook Chinese dishes for her family.
The new museum features exhibits on Chinese history and art.
Many students are learning Chinese culture as part of their curriculum.

Synset Examples

Chinese food

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chinese - a person from China
  • Synonym: Chinese - the language spoken in China

Etymology

From Persian *Chīn* via Portuguese *Chinês* or French *Chinois*, ultimately from Sanskrit *Cīna* (China).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02976327-a
Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ tʃaɪˈniz / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈniːz / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of China, including both the People's Republic of China and, historically, the Republic of China (Taiwan)
a person from China

Examples

Many Chinese living abroad celebrate Lunar New Year with family traditions.
The new exchange student is a Chinese from Beijing.
Chinese have greatly influenced international cuisine and culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chinese people - the people of China collectively

Etymology

From Persian *Chīnī* (‘Chinese’), from *Chīn* (‘China’), ultimately from Sanskrit *Cīnaḥ*.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09717504-n
Synset POS: n

3. NOUN

/ tʃaɪˈniz / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈniːz / (BrE)
any of the languages belonging to the Sino-Tibetan family spoken in China, considered dialects but often mutually unintelligible, sharing a common writing system
group of Sino-Tibetan languages of China

Examples

I am learning Chinese to communicate with my relatives in Beijing.
Several different forms of Chinese are spoken across the country, including Mandarin and Cantonese.
She translated the document from Chinese into English.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mandarin - the official language of China, a principal form of Chinese
  • Synonym: Cantonese - a major Chinese language spoken in Guangdong and Hong Kong

Etymology

From Persian *Chīnī* (of China), from Middle Chinese 蚩 (Chī), referring to the people or language of China.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06942519-n
Synset POS: n
