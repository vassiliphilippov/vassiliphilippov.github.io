bedevilment

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈdɛvəlmənt / (AmE), / bɪˈdɛvɪlmənt / (BrE)
persistent harassment or torment; the act of continually annoying or troubling someone
persistent harassment or torment

Examples

She finally left her job because of the constant bedevilment from her supervisor.
The team's progress was hampered by the bedevilment of unforeseen technical problems.
Many children have faced the bedevilment of schoolyard bullies at some point.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harassment - the act of persistently annoying or attacking someone

Etymology

From the verb 'bedevil', itself from 'be-' (intensive prefix) + 'devil'.
