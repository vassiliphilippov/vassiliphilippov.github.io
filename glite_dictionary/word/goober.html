goober

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːbər / (AmE), / ˈɡuːbə / (BrE)
a pod of the peanut plant containing usually two seeds; a peanut
peanut pod; peanut
american

Examples

He cracked open a goober and popped the peanuts into his mouth.
At the baseball game, vendors sold bags of roasted goobers to the crowd.
She picked up a handful of goobers from the bowl during the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: groundnut - British term for peanut or its pod
  • Synonym: monkey nut - British term for peanut in the shell
  • Synonym: peanut - the seed or pod of the peanut plant

Etymology

From Kongo *nguba* and related Bantu languages, meaning 'peanut'.
