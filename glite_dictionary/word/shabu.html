shabu

1. NOUN

/ ʃəˈbu / (AmE), / ʃəˈbu / (BrE)
a potent, crystalline form of methamphetamine used as an illegal stimulant drug
crystalline methamphetamine used illicitly

Examples

The police seized several packets of shabu during the raid.
Authorities are concerned about the rising use of shabu among teenagers.
He was arrested for selling shabu in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: methamphetamine - a powerful, addictive stimulant drug affecting the central nervous system

Etymology

Borrowed from Philippine and Japanese slang, possibly from Japanese シャブ (shabu), slang for this drug.
