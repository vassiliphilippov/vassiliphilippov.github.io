SCPO

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛs ˌsi ˈpi ˌoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɛs ˌsiː ˈpiː ˌəʊ / (BrE)
abbreviation for Senior Chief Petty Officer, a senior noncommissioned officer rank in the US Navy or Coast Guard, equivalent to a master sergeant in the Army
senior Navy or Coast Guard NCO rank (US)
american

Examples

After twenty years of service, he was promoted to SCPO in the Navy.
The SCPO supervised junior sailors during the fleet inspection.
At the ceremony, the commanding officer congratulated the newly appointed SCPO.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Senior Chief Petty Officer - full title for SCPO; senior NCO in Navy/Coast Guard

Etymology

Abbreviation of Senior Chief Petty Officer.
