Albania

1. PROPN

/ ælˈbeɪniə / (AmE), / ælˈbeɪniə / (BrE)
A country in southeastern Europe on the Adriatic coast of the Balkan Peninsula.
country in southeastern Europe

Examples

She traveled through Albania during her summer vacation.
The capital city of Albania is Tirana.
Many people in Albania speak both Albanian and other regional languages.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'Albania', from Greek 'Albanía', from native name 'Shqipëria'.
