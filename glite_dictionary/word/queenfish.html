queenfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwiːnˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkwiːnˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a silvery and bluish drumfish found in shallow coastal waters of California
a silvery drumfish of California coast

Examples

The angler was thrilled to catch a large queenfish near the pier.
Schools of queenfish are often seen glimmering just beneath the surface of the water.
Local restaurants occasionally serve grilled queenfish as a seasonal delicacy.
