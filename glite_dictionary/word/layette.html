layette

1. NOUN

/ leɪˈɛt / (AmE), / leɪˈɛt / (BrE)
a set of clothing and other essentials prepared for a newborn baby
newborn baby's outfit set

Examples

We received a beautiful layette as a gift before our baby was born.
She carefully packed the layette with soft blankets, tiny hats, and onesies.
The baby's grandmother knitted a special sweater for his first layette.

Etymology

From French 'layette' (a small box or chest), later referring to a baby's set of clothes.
