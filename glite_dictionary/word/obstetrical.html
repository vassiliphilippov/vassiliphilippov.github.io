obstetrical

1. ADJ

/ əbˈstɛtrɪkəl / (AmE), / ɒbˈstɛtrɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to childbirth and the care of women during and after pregnancy, especially within the field of obstetrics.
relating to obstetrics or childbirth
medical

Examples

She was admitted to an obstetrical unit for specialized care during her pregnancy.
The hospital has advanced obstetrical equipment for monitoring fetal health.
Dr. Harris is an expert in obstetrical procedures and high-risk deliveries.

Synset Examples

obstetric hospital

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obstetric - relating to childbirth and obstetrics

Etymology

From Latin 'obstetricius', from 'obstetrix' meaning 'midwife'.
