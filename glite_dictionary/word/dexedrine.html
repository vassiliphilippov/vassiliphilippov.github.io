Dexedrine

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛksəˌdrin / (AmE), / ˈdɛksɪdriːn / (BrE)
A brand name for dextroamphetamine, a central nervous system stimulant used to treat ADHD and narcolepsy.
brand name for dextroamphetamine drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Dexedrine to help manage her symptoms of ADHD.
Patients sometimes misuse Dexedrine because of its stimulant effects.
My pharmacy was out of Dexedrine, so I had to wait for a new shipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amphetamine - a synthetic stimulant used as a drug
