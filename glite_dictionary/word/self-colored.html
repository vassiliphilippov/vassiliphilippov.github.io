self-colored

1. ADJ

/ sɛlf ˈkʌlərd / (AmE), / sɛlf ˈkʌləd / (BrE)
Having the same color throughout; not patterned or variegated.
of uniform color throughout

Examples

The designer chose a self-colored fabric for a minimalist look.
I prefer self-colored curtains to those with patterns.
The pottery was notable for its self-colored glaze.

Synset Examples

solid color

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solid-color - having a single color throughout

Etymology

Formed from 'self-' meaning 'of itself' + 'colored'.
