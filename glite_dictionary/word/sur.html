Sur

1. PROPN

/ sʊr / (AmE), / sʊə / (BrE)
A port city in southern Lebanon on the Mediterranean Sea, formerly a major Phoenician seaport renowned for its silk production.
port city in southern Lebanon

Examples

The ancient city of Sur attracts many tourists interested in Phoenician history.
Many artifacts from Sur have been discovered in archaeological excavations.
The port of Sur played a crucial role in maritime trade during antiquity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tyre - ancient name for Sur, a major Phoenician city

Etymology

From Arabic صور (Ṣūr), historically known as Tyre in classical antiquity.
