Ionesco

1. PROPN

/ joʊˈnɛskoʊ / (AmE), / jɒˈnɛskəʊ / (BrE)
Eugène Ionesco (1912–1994), a French dramatist of Romanian origin, known as a leading figure in the theater of the absurd.
French dramatist, leader in theater of the absurd

Examples

Ionesco's plays often explore the meaninglessness of human existence.
A production of Ionesco's 'The Bald Soprano' was staged at the local theatre.
Many critics consider Ionesco a master of absurdist drama.

Etymology

From the Romanian surname Ionescu, adapted to French spelling.
