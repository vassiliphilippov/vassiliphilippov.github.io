postwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to deliver letters and parcels to people's homes and businesses
female mail carrier

Examples

The postwoman delivered a package to my door this morning.
Everyone on the street greets the friendly postwoman by name.
The postwoman braved the rain to make sure our letters arrived on time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: postman - a man who delivers the mail
  • Synonym: mail carrier - a person who delivers mail (gender neutral)

Etymology

Formed by analogy to 'postman', combining 'post' (mail) and 'woman'.
