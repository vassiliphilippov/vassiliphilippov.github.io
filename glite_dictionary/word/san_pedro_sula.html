San Pedro Sula

1. PROPN

/ sæn ˈpeɪdroʊ ˈsuːlə / (AmE), / san ˈpeɪdrəʊ ˈsuːlə / (BrE)
an industrial city in northwestern Honduras, known as the country's economic center
industrial city in Honduras

Examples

The manufacturing industry in San Pedro Sula plays a vital role in the Honduran economy.
Many travelers fly into San Pedro Sula before visiting other parts of Honduras.
San Pedro Sula has experienced rapid urban growth in recent decades.

Etymology

From Spanish, meaning 'Saint Peter Valley'.
