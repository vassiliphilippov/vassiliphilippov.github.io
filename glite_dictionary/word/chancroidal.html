chancroidal

1. ADJ

/ ʃæŋˈkrɔɪd.əl / (AmE), / ʃæŋˈkrɔɪd.əl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of chancroids, a type of infectious genital ulcer.
relating to chancroids (a genital ulcer disease)
medical

Examples

The patient presented with chancroidal ulcers that required immediate treatment.
He wrote a scientific article describing the characteristics of chancroidal lesions.
Chancroidal infections are less common in developed countries but still occur in certain regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chancroid - a bacterial infection causing genital ulceration

Etymology

From chancroid (from French *chancre*) + -al (adjective suffix).
