amorously

1. ADV

/ ˈæm.ɚ.əs.li / (AmE), / ˈæm.ər.əs.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows romantic or sexual love or interest.
in a romantic or loving way

Examples

He gazed amorously into her eyes, hoping she would notice his affection.
She smiled amorously at him from across the room, making her intentions clear.
The couple strolled amorously through the park, holding hands and laughing.

Synset Examples

he looked at her amorously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lovingly - in a loving manner

Etymology

From Latin 'amorosus' (loving), from 'amor' (love).
