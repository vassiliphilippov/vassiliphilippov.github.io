bloodless

/ ˈblʌd.ləs / (AmE), / ˈblʌd.ləs / (BrE)
Lacking vitality, enthusiasm, or energy; without vigor or zest.

Examples

The meeting was a rather bloodless affair, with no enthusiasm from the participants.
She gave a bloodless response, showing no excitement or interest.
