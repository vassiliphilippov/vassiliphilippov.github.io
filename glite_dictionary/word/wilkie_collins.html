Wilkie Collins

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪlki ˈkɑlɪnz / (AmE), / ˈwɪlki ˈkɒlɪnz / (BrE)
(1824–1889) English writer known for his pioneering detective novels, including 'The Woman in White' and 'The Moonstone'.
English novelist (1824–1889)

Examples

Wilkie Collins is often credited with writing one of the first English detective novels.
Many literature courses include Wilkie Collins alongside other Victorian novelists.
I've just finished reading Wilkie Collins's 'The Moonstone,' and found it fascinating.
