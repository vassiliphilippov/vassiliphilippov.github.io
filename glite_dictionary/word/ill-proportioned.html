ill-proportioned

1. ADJ

/ ɪl pɹəˈpɔɹʃənd / (AmE), / ɪl pɹəˈpɔːʃənd / (BrE)
lacking harmonious or pleasing proportions; not well proportioned
not having pleasing proportions

Examples

The sculpture was criticized for its ill-proportioned features that disrupted its visual balance.
Her dress appeared ill-proportioned, making her look shorter than she really was.
The architect believed that an ill-proportioned building could ruin the character of the street.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: well-proportioned - having harmonious or pleasing proportions
