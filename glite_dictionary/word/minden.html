Minden

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɪndən / (AmE), / ˈmɪndən / (BrE)
a battle fought in 1759 during the Seven Years' War in which English forces and their allies defeated the French.
1759 battle in the Seven Years' War

Examples

The Battle of Minden marked a turning point in the Seven Years' War.
Many British regiments still commemorate Minden Day each year.
Historians often highlight Minden as a decisive victory for the Anglo-German forces.

Etymology

From the name of the town of Minden in Germany, near which the battle was fought.
