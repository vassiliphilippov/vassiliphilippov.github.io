aristocratically

1. ADV

/ əˌrɪstəˈkrætɪkli / (AmE), / əˌrɪstəˈkrætɪkəli / (BrE)
In a manner characteristic of or befitting an aristocrat; with elegance, dignity, or exclusivity associated with the aristocracy.
in an aristocratic manner

Examples

She walked aristocratically into the room, her chin held high and her posture impeccable.
Despite his humble origins, he dined aristocratically, using each piece of cutlery with precision.
The host smiled aristocratically, barely acknowledging the newcomers.

Synset Examples

they behaved aristocratically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aristocratic - characteristic of the aristocracy

Etymology

From 'aristocratic' + '-ally', ultimately from Greek 'aristokratikos'.
