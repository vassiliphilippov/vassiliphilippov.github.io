high-level

1. ADJ

/ ˈhaɪ ˌlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪ ˈlɛv(ə)l / (BrE)
(of an action or event) occurring at or from a relatively high altitude, especially in aviation or military contexts.
at or from a high altitude

Examples

The pilot executed a high-level bombing run over the enemy base.
Satellite images are usually taken from a high-level perspective to cover vast areas of land.
The migration paths of geese were studied using high-level surveillance flights.
