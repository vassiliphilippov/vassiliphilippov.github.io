apparency

1. NOUN

/ əˈpɛrənsi / (AmE), / əˈparənsi / (BrE)
the state or quality of being apparent; obviousness or visibility
the state of being apparent

Examples

His guilt had a certain apparency that made the jury suspicious.
The apparency of danger caused everyone to evacuate the building quickly.
Despite the apparency of her sadness, she insisted she was fine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appearance - the way that someone or something looks
  • Synonym: obviousness - the quality of being obvious; easily seen or understood
  • Antonym: concealment - the action of hiding or preventing something from being known

Etymology

From Latin 'apparentia' (appearance).
