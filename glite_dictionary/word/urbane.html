urbane

1. ADJ

/ ɝːˈbeɪn / (AmE), / ɜːˈbeɪn / (BrE)
(of a person, especially a man) showing refinement, sophistication, and social polish; courteous and confident in manner.
refined and polished in manner

Examples

She was an urbane host, making everyone feel comfortable at the elegant dinner party.
His urbane wit and charm impressed everyone he met on his travels.
The ambassador spoke in an urbane manner, displaying both tact and confidence.

Synset Examples

his polished manner
maintained an urbane tone in his letters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sophisticated - having a refined knowledge or worldly experience
  • Antonym: crude - lacking refinement or sophistication

Etymology

From Latin urbanus (belonging to a city, refined), from urbs (city).
