Pterospermum

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛrəˈspɜrməm / (AmE), / ˌtɛrəˈspɜːməm / (BrE)
A genus of tropical Asian trees and shrubs with fragrant flowers, belonging to the family Malvaceae.
genus of tropical Asian trees and shrubs

Examples

The leaves of Pterospermum are often used in traditional medicine in some Asian countries.
Some species of Pterospermum produce large, fragrant flowers that attract pollinators.
Botanists discovered a new Pterospermum species in the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

Etymology

From Greek 'pteron' (wing) and 'sperma' (seed), referring to the winged seeds characteristic of the genus.
