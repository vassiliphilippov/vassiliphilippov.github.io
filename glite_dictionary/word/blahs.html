blahs

1. NOUN

/ blɑz / (AmE), / blɑːz / (BrE)
A general feeling of boredom, mild depression, or lack of enthusiasm.
a feeling of boredom or mild depression
informal

Examples

I've had the blahs all week and can't seem to get motivated.
After the holidays, many people experience the midwinter blahs.
A new hobby might help you shake off the blahs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blues - a feeling of sadness or depression
  • Degree Higher: depression - a state of severe despondency and dejection
