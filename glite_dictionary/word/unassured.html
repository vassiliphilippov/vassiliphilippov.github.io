unassured

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈʃʊrd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈʃʊəd / (BrE)
not confident or lacking in boldness; uncertain in manner or action
not confident; lacking boldness

Examples

She gave an unassured smile, betraying her nervousness about the presentation.
His unassured manner made it clear that he was not comfortable speaking in public.
The actor’s performance was surprisingly unassured, lacking the usual confidence he displayed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: confident - feeling or showing certainty or boldness
  • Antonym: sure - having confidence or certainty
  • Synonym: uncertain - not sure or confident

Etymology

From un- (not) + assured (confident, certain).
