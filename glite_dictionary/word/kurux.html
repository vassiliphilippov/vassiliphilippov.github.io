Kurux

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːruːʃ / (AmE), / ˈkuːrʊks / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken by the Kurukh people in eastern India, especially in Jharkhand, Chhattisgarh, and Odisha
a Dravidian language of eastern India

Examples

The Kurux language is listed as vulnerable by UNESCO.
Many villagers in eastern India communicate daily in Kurux.
He is conducting research on the grammar of Kurux.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kurukh - an alternative name for the Kurux language and people

Etymology

Name derived from the self-designation of the Kurukh people.
