sacramental

1. ADJ

/ ˌsækrəˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌsakrəˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving a sacrament, especially in a Christian religious context
relating to a religious sacrament

Examples

The priest performed the sacramental rituals with great reverence.
Baptism and communion are two sacramental practices in many Christian churches.
He spoke about the sacramental aspects of marriage in the Catholic faith.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sacrament - a religious ceremony regarded as imparting divine grace

Etymology

From Late Latin sacramentalis, from sacramentum (sacrament).
