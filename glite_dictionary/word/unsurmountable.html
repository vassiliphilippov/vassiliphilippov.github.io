unsurmountable

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.sɚˈmaʊn.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌn.səˈmaʊn.tə.bəl / (BrE)
(of a difficulty or obstacle) not capable of being overcome or surmounted.
impossible to overcome

Examples

The team faced unsurmountable odds after losing their star player to injury.
Some challenges may seem unsurmountable at first, but perseverance often makes a difference.
The company declared bankruptcy due to unsurmountable financial difficulties.

Synset Examples

insurmountable disadvantages

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insurmountable - (of a difficulty or obstacle) impossible to overcome

Etymology

From un- (not) + surmount (to overcome) + -able.

2. ADJ

impossible to overcome, climb, or surpass
impossible to overcome or climb

Examples

The team faced what seemed like an unsurmountable obstacle, but refused to give up.
Climbing the sheer, icy cliff appeared unsurmountable to even the most experienced mountaineers.
She showed determination when overcoming problems that others found unsurmountable.
