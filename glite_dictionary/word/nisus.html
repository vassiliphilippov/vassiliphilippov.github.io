nisus

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪ.səs / (AmE), / ˈnaɪ.səs / (BrE)
a sustained or strenuous effort towards achieving a particular aim or goal
a determined effort or striving

Examples

Her nisus to complete the project was evident in her long hours and unwavering dedication.
The philosophical nisus towards knowledge has shaped centuries of inquiry.
Despite repeated failures, his nisus to win the championship never faltered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: effort - a vigorous or determined attempt
  • Synonym: striving - making great efforts to achieve or obtain something
  • Antonym: indolence - avoidance of activity or exertion; laziness

Etymology

From Latin nisus (effort, striving), from niti (to strive).
