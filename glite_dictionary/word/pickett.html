Pickett

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɪkɪt / (BrE)
(1825–1875) An American Confederate general best known for leading Pickett's Charge during the Battle of Gettysburg in the American Civil War.
Confederate general, led Pickett's Charge at Gettysburg

Examples

History books often discuss Pickett's leadership at Gettysburg.
Many believe Pickett's Charge was a turning point in the Civil War.
The bravery and tragedy of Pickett are still debated by historians.
