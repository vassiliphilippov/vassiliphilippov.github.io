shindig

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪnˌdɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪn.dɪɡ / (BrE)
an informal party, often large and lively, with music, dancing, or celebration
lively party or celebration
informal

Examples

We hosted a big shindig to celebrate their anniversary last weekend.
Their annual holiday shindig is the highlight of the year for the whole family.
The mayor threw a fancy shindig for the city council.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party - a social gathering of invited guests

Etymology

Origin unknown; possibly an alteration of shindy (uproar, commotion).
