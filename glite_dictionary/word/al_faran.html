Al Faran

1. PROPN

/ æl fəˈrɑːn / (AmE), / æl fəˈrɑːn / (BrE)
an Islamic militant group originally operating in Pakistan and Afghanistan, later designated as a terrorist organization active mainly in Kashmir seeking the region's accession to Pakistan
Islamic militant group in Kashmir

Examples

The Indian government blamed Al Faran for several kidnappings in Kashmir during the 1990s.
Reports indicate that Al Faran continued its activities along the border after the war ended.
International agencies have labeled Al Faran as a terrorist organization.
