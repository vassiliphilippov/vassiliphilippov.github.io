one-hundred-thousandth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn hʌndrəd ˈθaʊzəndθ / (AmE), / wʌn hʌndrəd ˈθaʊzəndθ / (BrE)
Each of one hundred thousand equal parts into which something can be divided; also, the ordinal number corresponding to one hundred thousand in a sequence.
one part in a hundred thousand; ordinal number

Examples

The scientist measured the concentration down to the one-hundred-thousandth of a gram.
Only a one-hundred-thousandth of the total amount was found to be contaminated.
Winning by a one-hundred-thousandth of a second is rare in any sport.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: millionth - one part in a million equal parts
  • Degree Lower: ten-thousandth - one part in ten thousand equal parts
  • Degree Higher: ten-millionth - one part in ten million equal parts
