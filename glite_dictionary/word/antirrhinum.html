Antirrhinum

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.tɪˈraɪ.nəm / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪˈraɪ.nəm / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the family Scrophulariaceae, known for their brightly colored irregular flowers; commonly called snapdragons.
genus of snapdragon plants

Examples

The garden was full of Antirrhinum in various vibrant colors.
She specializes in cultivating rare species of Antirrhinum for botanical research.
Antirrhinum thrives in well-drained soil and sunny locations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snapdragon - common name for Antirrhinum plants

Etymology

From Greek anti- (like) + rhis, rhin- (nose), referring to the flower's shape.
