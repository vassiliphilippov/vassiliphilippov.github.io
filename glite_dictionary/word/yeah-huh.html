yeah-huh

1. INTJ

/ ˈjɛ hə / (AmE), / ˈjɛ hə / (BrE)
used informally to indicate agreement or acknowledgment in response to a statement or question
informal agreement or acknowledgment
informal

Examples

When I asked if she wanted more coffee, she just muttered yeah-huh and looked away.
He gave a distracted yeah-huh when I reminded him about the meeting.
I explained the instructions, and all I got was a bored yeah-huh in response.

Synset Examples

She just nodded and said, yeah-huh, without paying attention.
When I asked if he understood, he replied, yeah-huh.
I pointed out the mistake, and he just mumbled, yeah-huh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uh-huh - an informal expression showing agreement or affirmation
  • Synonym: yeah - used to express assent, agreement, or affirmation
  • Antonym: nope - an informal way of saying no
