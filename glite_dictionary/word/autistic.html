autistic

1. ADJ

/ ɔˈtɪstɪk / (AmE), / ɔːˈtɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or affected by autism; having characteristics of autism.
relating to autism
medical

Examples

The teacher developed new techniques to support her autistic students in the classroom.
He advocates for greater awareness and inclusion of autistic people in society.
Some autistic individuals have heightened sensitivity to lights and noises.

Synset Examples

autistic behavior
autistic children

Related Terms

  • Synonym: autism - a developmental disorder characterized by difficulties with social interaction and communication

Etymology

From autism + -ic, denoting relating to or characterized by.
