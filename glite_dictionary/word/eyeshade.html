eyeshade

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪˌʃeɪd / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌʃeɪd / (BrE)
a brim or visor that projects forward to shade the eyes from sunlight or bright light
a projected brim that shades the eyes

Examples

He wore a green eyeshade while working at his desk.
The baseball cap's eyeshade protected her from the afternoon sun.
She pushed the eyeshade down to keep the glare out of her eyes.

Synset Examples

he pulled down the bill of his cap and trudged ahead

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visor - a projecting front brim of a cap that shades the eyes
