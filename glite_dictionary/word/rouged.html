rouged

1. ADJ

/ ruːʤd / (AmE), / ruːʒd / (BrE)
(of a person's face or lips) colored with red makeup, especially rouge.
colored with red makeup

Examples

She wore a vintage dress and her rouged cheeks gave her a glamorous look.
The actress's rouged lips stood out on the red carpet.
He noticed her carefully rouged face as she greeted him at the door.

Synset Examples

freshly rouged lips
rouged cheeks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blushed - having a natural or artificial pink or red color in the cheeks

Etymology

From French "rouge" (red), meaning red makeup; past participle of the verb "to rouge".
