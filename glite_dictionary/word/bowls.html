bowls

1. NOUN

/ boʊlz / (AmE), / bəʊlz / (BrE)
A lawn game in which players roll biased wooden balls so that they stop as close as possible to a smaller ball called a 'jack.'
lawn game using biased wooden balls

Examples

Every Saturday afternoon, she enjoys playing bowls at the local club.
The green was perfectly maintained for the annual bowls tournament.
He learned the rules of bowls from his grandfather.
