spicery

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪsəri / (AmE), / ˈspaɪsəri / (BrE)
the quality or property of being flavored with spices; the state of being spicy or aromatic from spices.
quality of being flavored with spices

Examples

The chef added plenty of cloves and cardamom, giving the dessert an intense spicery.
The spicery of the curry completely transformed the simple rice dish.
Her cakes are renowned for their delicate spicery and balanced sweetness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiciness - the quality of being hot or spicy in flavor

Etymology

From Old French *espicerie*, from *espice* (spice), from Latin *species* (kind, spice).
