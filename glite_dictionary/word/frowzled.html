frowzled

1. ADJ

/ ˈfraʊzəld / (AmE), / ˈfraʊzəld / (BrE)
In a state of disarray or extreme untidiness; messy or tousled.
messy; in disarray

Examples

After tossing and turning all night, her hair looked completely frowzled in the morning.
He arrived at work in a frowzled suit, clearly having overslept.
The wind left his notes frowzled on the park bench, pages fluttering everywhere.
