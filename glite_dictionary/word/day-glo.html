Day-Glo

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪ ɡloʊ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ ɡləʊ / (BrE)
a bright fluorescent color or paint that is highly visible, often used for safety or decoration
bright fluorescent color or paint

Examples

The dancers wore Day-Glo costumes that glowed under the black lights.
He painted his bicycle with Day-Glo green to make it more visible at night.
Her poster was highlighted with Day-Glo colors to attract attention.

Synset Examples

His Day-Glo shirt stood out in the crowd.
She painted her room in Day-Glo colors.
The signs were made with Day-Glo paint for visibility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fluorescent - vividly bright due to fluorescence

Etymology

From the trademark 'Day-Glo', originally a brand name for fluorescent pigments.
