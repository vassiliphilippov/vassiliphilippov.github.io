Byron

1. PROPN

/ ˈbaɪrən / (AmE), / ˈbaɪrən / (BrE)
George Gordon Byron, known as Lord Byron (1788–1824), English Romantic poet famous for his passionate verse and unconventional lifestyle.
English Romantic poet (1788–1824)
british
