unharmed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhɑrmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɑːmd / (BrE)
not injured, damaged, or hurt; remaining safe and without harm
not injured or harmed

Examples

Despite the accident, all the passengers remained unharmed.
She managed to escape the fire unharmed, thanks to the quick response of firefighters.
The precious artwork was found unharmed in the ruins after the earthquake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uninjured - not physically hurt or injured
  • Synonym: unscathed - not hurt or damaged; without injury
  • Antonym: injured - hurt or physically harmed
  • Antonym: harmed - physically or mentally hurt or injured

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + harmed (past participle of harm).
