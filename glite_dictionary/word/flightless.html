flightless

1. ADJ

/ ˈflaɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈflaɪtləs / (BrE)
(of birds or insects) unable to fly.
unable to fly (esp. birds or insects)

Examples

The ostrich is a flightless bird native to Africa.
Many islands have unique species of flightless insects due to the absence of predators.
She was fascinated by the penguin's flightless nature and graceful swimming.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: flying - capable of flight; able to fly

Etymology

From 'flight' (Middle English, from Old English 'flyht') + '-less' (meaning 'without').
