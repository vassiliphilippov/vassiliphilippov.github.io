straw-coloured

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrɔːˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈstrɔːˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having a pale yellow color similar to that of dry straw
pale yellow, like dry straw
british

Examples

The old barn had a straw-coloured roof that glowed in the sunlight.
She wore a straw-coloured dress that matched her summery mood.
A straw-coloured dog ran across the field, blending in with the tall grass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: straw-colored - having a pale yellow color like straw (American spelling)
