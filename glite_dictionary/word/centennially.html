centennially

1. ADV

/ sɛnˈtɛniəli / (AmE), / sɛnˈtɛniəli / (BrE)
Once every hundred years; at intervals of a century.
every hundred years

Examples

The parade is held centennially to mark the city's historic founding.
The ancient ritual is performed centennially by local villagers.
The archives are opened centennially for public viewing during special ceremonies.

Synset Examples

the birthday of this city is being celebrated centennially

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: annually - once a year
  • Degree Lower: biennially - once every two years
  • Degree Higher: millennially - every thousand years

Etymology

From 'centennial' (relating to a hundred years) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
