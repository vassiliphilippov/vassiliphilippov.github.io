zoophilia

1. NOUN

/ zoʊˈɒfɪliə / (AmE), / zəʊˈɒfɪliə / (BrE)
a sexual attraction to animals, considered a psychological disorder
sexual attraction to animals
medical

Examples

The psychiatrist specialized in treating rare conditions such as zoophilia.
Laws against zoophilia exist in many countries due to animal welfare concerns.
Zoophilia is classified as a paraphilia in psychiatric manuals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bestiality - sexual relations between a human and an animal

Etymology

From Greek zōion (animal) + -philia (love, affection).
