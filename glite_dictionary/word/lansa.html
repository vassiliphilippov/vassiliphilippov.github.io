lansa

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæn.sə / (AmE), / ˈlan.sə / (BrE)
An East Indian fruit with tart, yellow, berrylike characteristics, commonly grown for its edible qualities.
East Indian tart yellow fruit

Examples

The ripe lansa is prized for its sweet-tart flavor in many Southeast Asian dishes.
We tasted the unique, tangy taste of lansa during our trip to Indonesia.
She bought a basket of fresh lansas from the local market.

Etymology

From Malay 'lansat', the common name for the fruit in Southeast Asia.
