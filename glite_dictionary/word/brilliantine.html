brilliantine

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪljəntiːn / (AmE), / ˈbrɪljəntiːn / (BrE)
a scented oil or pomade used to make the hair shiny and manageable
a hair oil or pomade

Examples

He slicked his hair back with brilliantine before heading out for the evening.
The old-fashioned barbershop still stocks jars of brilliantine on its shelves.
A subtle fragrance wafted from his hair, thanks to the brilliantine he used daily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pomade - a scented ointment for the hair

Etymology

From French 'brillantine', from 'brillant' (shining).
