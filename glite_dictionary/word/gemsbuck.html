gemsbuck

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɛmzˌbʌk / (AmE), / ˈɡɛmzbʌk / (BrE)
a large South African antelope (oryx) with a distinctive broad black band along its flanks
large South African antelope (oryx)

Examples

The tourists watched as a solitary gemsbuck grazed in the savanna.
The gemsbuck is admired for its striking markings and long, straight horns.
Conservation efforts have helped increase the gemsbuck population in southern Africa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oryx - a genus of large antelope with long straight horns

Etymology

From Afrikaans 'gemsbok', from Dutch 'gemsbok' (chamois-buck).
