Radyera

1. NOUN

/ ˌreɪdiˈɛrə / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiˈɛːrə / (BrE)
A small genus of shrubs native to the Southern Hemisphere, commonly known as bush hibiscus.
genus of Southern Hemisphere shrubs

Examples

The Radyera genus includes several shrubs found in arid regions of Australia.
Botanists are studying the unique adaptations of Radyera to dry climates.
Gardeners value Radyera for its attractive bush hibiscus flowers.
