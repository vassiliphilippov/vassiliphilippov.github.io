sensitised

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛnsɪˌtaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsɪˌtaɪzd / (BrE)
having developed an allergy or unusually strong reaction to a particular substance or factor
having developed an allergy or abnormal sensitivity
medical british

Examples

After years of exposure, Emily became sensitised to chemicals in cleaning products.
Some people are sensitised to pollen and suffer intense allergic reactions during spring.
The doctor warned that repeat contact could leave you sensitised to latex gloves.

Synset Examples

allergic children
hypersensitive to pollen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensitized - having developed an allergy or abnormal sensitivity (American spelling)
  • Antonym: desensitised - having reduced sensitivity or allergy

Etymology

From 'sensitise', based on 'sensitive' and the suffix '-ise/-ize'.
