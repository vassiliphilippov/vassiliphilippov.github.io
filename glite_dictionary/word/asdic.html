asdic

1. NOUN

/ ˈæz.dɪk / (AmE), / ˈæz.dɪk / (BrE)
a device for detecting underwater objects by sending out sound pulses and measuring the echoes, chiefly used in submarine detection
submarine detection device using sound pulses

Examples

The destroyer scanned the ocean with its asdic to search for enemy submarines.
Early versions of asdic played a crucial role in naval warfare during World War II.
The ship's asdic detected an unusual echo, prompting the crew to investigate further.

Synset Examples

sonar is an acronym for sound navigation ranging
asdic is an acronym for antisubmarine detection investigation committee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sonar - a system for detecting objects under water using sound waves

Etymology

Acronym from Anti-Submarine Detection Investigation Committee, which developed the technology in Britain during World War I.
