Yellowknife

1. PROPN

/ ˈjɛloʊˌnaɪf / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊˌnaɪf / (BrE)
the capital city of the Northwest Territories in northern Canada, located on the north shore of Great Slave Lake
city in Northwest Territories, Canada

Examples

We flew into Yellowknife for the start of our Arctic adventure.
Yellowknife is known for its beautiful views of the northern lights.
The conference will be held in Yellowknife this summer.

Etymology

Named after the Yellowknives Dene, an Indigenous people of the area.
