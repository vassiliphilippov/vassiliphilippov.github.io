butterfingered

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʌt̬ərˌfɪŋɡɚd / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌfɪŋɡəd / (BrE)
clumsy or awkward in handling objects, especially with the hands; prone to dropping things
clumsy, especially with the hands

Examples

Because he was so butterfingered, he dropped the vase as soon as he picked it up.
The butterfingered chef spilled flour all over the kitchen while trying to bake a cake.
Even the most butterfingered players can improve with practice and attention to detail.

Synset Examples

a bumbling mechanic
a bungling performance
ham-handed governmental interference
could scarcely empty a scuttle of ashes, so handless was the poor creature

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or handling things
  • Antonym: deft - demonstrating skill and ease in movement

Etymology

From the noun 'butterfingers,' originally meaning someone likely to drop things as if their fingers were slippery with butter.
