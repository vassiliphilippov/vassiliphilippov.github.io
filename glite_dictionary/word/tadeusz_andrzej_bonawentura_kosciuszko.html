Tadeusz Andrzej Bonawentura Kosciuszko

1. PROPN

/ təˈdeɪ.ʊʃ ˌɑːnˈdrɛʒ bɒ.nəˈvɛn.tjʊ.rə kɒʃˈtʃuːs.koʊ / (AmE), / taˈdɛ.ʊʃ ˌanˈdrɛʃ bɒ.nəˈvɛn.tjʊ.rə kɒʃˈtʃuːs.kəʊ / (BrE)
(1746–1817) Polish military leader and patriot who fought in the American Revolution and led a national uprising in Poland.
Polish patriot and soldier, 1746–1817

Examples

Tadeusz Andrzej Bonawentura Kosciuszko played a significant role in both the American and Polish struggles for independence.
The story of Tadeusz Andrzej Bonawentura Kosciuszko is taught in history classes around the world.
Many monuments commemorate Tadeusz Andrzej Bonawentura Kosciuszko for his dedication to freedom.

Etymology

Polish form of the name; 'Tadeusz' from Thaddeus, 'Kosciuszko' is a Polish surname.
