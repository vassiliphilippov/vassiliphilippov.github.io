facilitator

1. NOUN

/ fəˈsɪl.əˌteɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / fəˈsɪl.ɪ.teɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person who makes a process or task easier or helps a group to work more effectively together
someone who helps make progress easier

Examples

The facilitator guided the team through the brainstorming session to ensure everyone participated.
As a facilitator, her job is to help the meeting run smoothly and keep discussions on track.
Good facilitators encourage open communication and resolve conflicts effectively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moderator - a person who presides over a discussion
  • Antonym: obstructor - a person who makes progress more difficult

Etymology

From Latin 'facilitare' (to make easy), from 'facilis' (easy).
