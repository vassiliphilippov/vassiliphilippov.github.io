salting

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔltɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɔːltɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of adding salt to food, typically to enhance flavor or aid in preservation.
adding salt to food

Examples

The salting of the meat ensures it remains edible for months.
He was careful with the salting to avoid making the soup too salty.
Traditional salting methods are still used in some rural villages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seasoning - the act of adding herbs, spices, or salt to food for flavor

Etymology

From the verb 'salt' + '-ing', denoting the process or action.
