star-struck

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɑrˌstrʌk / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌstrʌk / (BrE)
Fascinated or greatly impressed by a famous or well-known person.
fascinated by a famous person
informal

Examples

She was completely star-struck when she met her favorite actor backstage.
Even seasoned journalists can feel star-struck around celebrities at award shows.
He tried not to appear star-struck during the interview with the famous director.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awestruck - filled with awe; very impressed
  • Antonym: indifferent - lacking interest or enthusiasm; not caring

Etymology

Formed from 'star' (celebrity) and 'struck' (affected, as in 'thunderstruck').
