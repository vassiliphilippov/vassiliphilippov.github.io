analytically

1. ADV

/ ˌæn.əˈlɪt.ɪk.li / (AmE), / ˌæn.əˈlɪt.ɪk.li / (BrE)
in a manner that involves analysis or logical reasoning
in an analytical manner

Examples

She approaches problems analytically, carefully breaking them down into their basic components.
The data was examined analytically to find underlying patterns and correlations.
To succeed in this role, you must think analytically and solve complex issues logically.

Synset Examples

assuming that the distinction is maintained, one may ask which is to be analytically prior?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: systematically - in a methodical or organized manner
  • Antonym: creatively - in an imaginative or inventive manner

Etymology

From 'analytical' + -ly; 'analytical' from Greek analutikos (able to be loosened up, analytical).
