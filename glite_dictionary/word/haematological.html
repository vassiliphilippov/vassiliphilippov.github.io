haematological

1. ADJ

/ ˌhiːməˌtɑːˈlɑːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˌtɒlˈɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of blood, its diseases, or conditions involving blood
relating to blood or its diseases
medical british

Examples

The patient was referred to a haematological specialist for further tests.
She presented with multiple haematological abnormalities indicating a possible blood disorder.
A wide range of haematological conditions can affect both children and adults.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hematological - relating to the study of blood (American spelling)

Etymology

From Greek 'haima' (blood) + English '-logical', relating to 'haematology'.
