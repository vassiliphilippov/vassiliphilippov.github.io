to orientalise

1. VERB

/ ɔːrˈjɛntəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˌɔːrɪˈɛntəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make or give something the character, style, or qualities associated with the Orient (Eastern Asia)
give Eastern/Asian character
british

Examples

Many Western artists tried to orientalise their paintings in the nineteenth century.
The architect proposed to orientalise the public garden with exotic plants and pagodas.
Efforts to orientalise the decor made the restaurant feel like it belonged in East Asia.

Synset Examples

orientalize your garden

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to orientalize - make oriental in character (American spelling)

Etymology

From 'oriental' + '-ise', modeled on the American spelling 'orientalize'.
