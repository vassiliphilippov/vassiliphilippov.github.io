tawdrily

1. ADV

/ ˈtɔːdɹɪli / (AmE), / ˈtɔːdɹɪli / (BrE)
In a tasteless or showy manner that is cheap, garish, or lacking in good taste.
in a tastelessly showy manner

Examples

The lobby was tawdrily decorated with glitter and neon lights.
She dressed tawdrily for the party, drawing shocked glances from the guests.
Billboards lined the street tawdrily advertising flashy sales and cheap jewelry.

Synset Examples

the temple was garishly decorated with bright plastic flowers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garishly - in an overly bright or showy way

Etymology

From 'tawdry,' itself from 'tawdry lace,' a corruption of 'Saint Audrey.'
