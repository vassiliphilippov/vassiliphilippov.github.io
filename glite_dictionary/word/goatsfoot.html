goatsfoot

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊtsˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊtsˌfʊt / (BrE)
a short-stemmed South African plant with bluish flowers, known for its hoof-shaped leaves
South African plant with bluish flowers

Examples

The goatsfoot thrives in dry, sandy soils throughout South Africa.
Botanists studied the distinctive bluish flowers of the goatsfoot during their field research.
Gardeners sometimes cultivate goatsfoot for its unique appearance and drought resistance.

Etymology

Refers to the shape of the plant's leaves, resembling a goat's hoof.
