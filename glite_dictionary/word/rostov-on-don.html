Rostov-on-Don

1. PROPN

/ ˌrɑːstɑːv ən ˈdɑːn / (AmE), / ˌrɒstɒf ɒn ˈdɒn / (BrE)
a city and seaport on the Don River near the Sea of Azov, in the European part of Russia.
city in southwestern Russia

Examples

Rostov-on-Don is an important transport hub in southern Russia.
She traveled from Moscow to Rostov-on-Don by train last summer.
Many international companies have offices in Rostov-on-Don.

Etymology

From Russian 'Ростов-на-Дону' (Rostov-na-Donu), meaning 'Rostov on the Don [River]'.
