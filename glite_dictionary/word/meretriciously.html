meretriciously

1. ADV

/ ˌmɛrəˈtrɪʃəsli / (AmE), / ˌmɛrəˈtrɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a way that is attractive in a flashy, vulgar, or falsely showy manner, but lacking real value or integrity.
in a showy, insincere manner

Examples

The hallway was meretriciously decorated with cheap glitter and gaudy ornaments.
He tried to impress his guests by dressing meretriciously for the party.
The film was criticized for being meretriciously sentimental, relying on flashy effects rather than real emotion.

Synset Examples

the boat is meretriciously decorated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ostentatiously - in a way that is showy or designed to impress

Etymology

From meretricious + -ly; 'meretricious' from Latin meretricius (of or pertaining to prostitutes), from meretrix (prostitute).
