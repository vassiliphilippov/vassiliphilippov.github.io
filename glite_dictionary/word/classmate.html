classmate

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæsˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈklɑːs.meɪt / (BrE)
a person who is in the same class as you at school, college, or university
a person in your class at school or college

Examples

She often studied with her classmate before exams.
Each classmate signed the yearbook with a special message.
Tom's best friend is also his classmate in science class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schoolmate - a person who attends the same school as you

Etymology

From 'class' + 'mate', with 'mate' meaning companion or associate.
