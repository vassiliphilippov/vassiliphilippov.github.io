Dexone

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛkˌsoʊn / (AmE), / ˈdɛksəʊn / (BrE)
A trademarked drug name for dexamethasone, a corticosteroid used to treat allergies and inflammation.
corticosteroid drug for allergies/inflammation
medical

Examples

After his allergic reaction, the doctor prescribed Dexone to reduce inflammation.
Some patients respond well to Dexone when other treatments are ineffective.
The pharmacist explained that Dexone is also known by its generic name, dexamethasone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dexamethasone - a corticosteroid medication used to treat inflammatory conditions
