busyness

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪzinəs / (AmE), / ˈbɪzinɪs / (BrE)
the state of being busy or actively engaged in activities
state of being busy

Examples

Her constant busyness made it hard to find time for friends.
Despite his apparent busyness, he always found time to help others.
The busyness of the restaurant was overwhelming during the lunch rush.

Synset Examples

they manifested all the busyness of a pack of beavers
there is a constant hum of military preparation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: business - the practice of making one's living by engaging in commerce; also a confusion source with 'busyness' in pronunciation and spelling

Etymology

From 'busy' + '-ness', formed in Middle English.
