white-haired

1. ADJ

/ ˈwaɪt hɛrd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪt heəd / (BrE)
Having hair that is white, typically as a result of aging.
having white hair, usually from age

Examples

The white-haired woman smiled kindly at her grandchildren.
A white-haired professor stood at the front of the lecture hall, preparing his notes.
His once-black curls are now completely white-haired after years of stress.

Synset Examples

whose beard with age is hoar
nodded his hoary head

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gray-haired - having gray hair, especially due to age
  • Antonym: bald - having no hair on the scalp
