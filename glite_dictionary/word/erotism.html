erotism

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrəˌtɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈɛrətɪzəm / (BrE)
a state or quality of heightened sexual excitement or anticipation
sexual anticipation or excitement

Examples

There was a palpable erotism in the air during the intimate dinner.
The novel is filled with moments of subtle erotism that heighten the tension between the characters.
Critics noted the film's artistic use of erotism rather than explicit scenes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eroticism - sexual desire or excitement

Etymology

From French érotisme, based on Greek eros (love, desire).
