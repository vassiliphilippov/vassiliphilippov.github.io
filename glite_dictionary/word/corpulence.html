corpulence

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrpjələns / (AmE), / ˈkɔːpjʊləns / (BrE)
the state of being excessively fat or obese; excessive fatness
excessive fatness; obesity

Examples

The doctor's notes mentioned the patient's corpulence as a factor in his treatment plan.
In the portrait, the monarch's corpulence is depicted with delicate brush strokes.
His increasing corpulence worried his family and prompted lifestyle changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obesity - the state of being obesity; excessive fatness
  • Antonym: leanness - the state of being lean; thinness or a lack of excess fat

Etymology

From Latin 'corpus' (body) via French 'corpulence'.
