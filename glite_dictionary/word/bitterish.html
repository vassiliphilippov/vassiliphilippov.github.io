bitterish

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɪtərɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɪtərɪʃ / (BrE)
Somewhat bitter in taste, feeling, or character.
somewhat bitter

Examples

The tea had a bitterish aftertaste that lingered on my tongue.
His bitterish remarks made everyone in the room uncomfortable.
She prefers a bitterish flavor in her dark chocolate.
