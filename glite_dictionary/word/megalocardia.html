megalocardia

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛgəloʊˈkɑrdiə / (AmE), / ˌmɛgələʊˈkɑːdiə / (BrE)
an abnormal enlargement of the heart
abnormal heart enlargement (medical)
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed the patient with megalocardia after reviewing the chest X-ray.
Athletes rarely develop megalocardia, but it can occur in certain heart conditions.
Symptoms of megalocardia may include shortness of breath and chest discomfort.

Synset Examples

mild cardiomegaly is common in athletes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cardiomegaly - enlargement of the heart

Etymology

From Greek 'megalo-' (large) + 'kardia' (heart).
