stroking

1. NOUN

/ ˈstroʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstrəʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of gently passing the hand over something, typically in a calm or comforting way
gentle act of running a hand over something

Examples

The gentle stroking of her hair helped calm her down.
He found the stroking of the cat soothing after a long day.
The artist's stroking of the canvas created beautiful, soft textures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caressing - the act of touching in a gentle and loving way

Etymology

From 'stroke' (verb) + -ing, forming a noun referring to the action.
