Lolo

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.ləʊ / (BrE)
a Loloish language spoken by the Yi or Lolo people in China, Vietnam, and Thailand.
a language spoken by the Yi or Lolo people

Examples

The Lolo language is still spoken by many communities in southern China.
Researchers have documented hundreds of stories in Lolo.
Bilingual education programs in Yunnan include classes in both Mandarin and Lolo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Yi - a Tibeto-Burman language related to Lolo
