Bay Stater

1. NOUN

/ beɪ ˈsteɪtər / (AmE), / beɪ ˈsteɪtə / (BrE)
a native or resident of Massachusetts
native or resident of Massachusetts

Examples

As a proud Bay Stater, she loved cheering for the Red Sox.
Many Bay Staters flock to Cape Cod during the summer months.
He introduced himself as a lifelong Bay Stater from Boston.

Etymology

Formed from 'Bay State,' a nickname for Massachusetts, and the agent noun suffix '-er'.
