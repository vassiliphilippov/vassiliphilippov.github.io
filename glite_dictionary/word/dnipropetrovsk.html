Dnipropetrovsk

1. PROPN

/ dniprəˈpɛtroʊvsk / (AmE), / dnɪprəˈpɛtrɒvsk / (BrE)
A city in east central Ukraine on the Dnieper River, known as a center of the metallurgical industry.
city in east central Ukraine

Examples

Many factories in Dnipropetrovsk contribute to the region's strong metallurgical industry.
She traveled to Dnipropetrovsk to visit her relatives along the Dnieper River.
The university in Dnipropetrovsk is renowned for its engineering programs.

Etymology

From Ukrainian "Дніпропетровськ" (Dnipropetrovsk), derived from the Dnieper River and the name of Soviet leader Grigory Petrovsky.
