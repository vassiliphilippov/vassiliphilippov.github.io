dustrag

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌs.træɡ / (AmE), / ˈdʌs.træɡ / (BrE)
A piece of cloth, often an old or worn-out one, used for cleaning dust from surfaces.
piece of cloth for dusting

Examples

She grabbed a dustrag to wipe the shelves before putting away the books.
After cleaning the table with a dustrag, the surface looked spotless.
Please wash the dustrag after you finish dusting the living room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rag - a piece of old cloth, especially one used for cleaning
