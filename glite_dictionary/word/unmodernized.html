unmodernized

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmɑːdərˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɒdənaɪzd / (BrE)
Not brought up to date by modern methods, technology, or ideas; lacking modernization.
not brought up to date

Examples

The factory remained unmodernized for decades, using outdated equipment and processes.
Much of the old city remains unmodernized, giving visitors a glimpse of the past.
Many criticized the unmodernized curriculum for failing to meet current educational standards.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: modernized - brought up to date with modern methods or ideas

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'modernized' from 'modern.'
