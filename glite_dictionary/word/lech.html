lech

1. NOUN

/ lɛtʃ / (AmE), / lɛtʃ / (BrE)
a man who behaves in a lecherous or sexually predatory way
sexually predatory man
informal

Examples

He was considered a lech by his colleagues because of his constant inappropriate remarks.
That creepy old lech made everyone uncomfortable at the party.
She's tired of fending off the attention of every lech in the bar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lecher - a man with excessive sexual desire
  • Antonym: prude - a person who is excessively proper or modest, especially regarding sexual matters

Etymology

Shortened from 'lecher', Middle English from Old French 'lecheor' (licker, debauchee).
