peekaboo

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːk.əˌbu / (AmE), / ˈpiː.kəˌbuː / (BrE)
a game played with young children in which one briefly hides the face and then suddenly reveals it, saying 'boo!'
game for young children involving hiding and revealing the face

Examples

Whenever she plays peekaboo with her baby, he bursts into laughter.
The classic game of peekaboo never fails to entertain toddlers.
Grandparents love playing peekaboo with their grandchildren during family gatherings.

Etymology

Reduplicative play with 'peek' (look quickly), imitating nursery and childish speech.
