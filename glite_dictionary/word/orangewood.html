orangewood

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɪndʒˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒˌwʊd / (BrE)
fine-grained wood from the orange tree, valued for use in fine woodwork and crafts
wood from the orange tree, used in crafts

Examples

The violin maker preferred using orangewood for its smooth texture and pale color.
Artists often select orangewood sticks for detail work in sculpture and cleaning.
High-quality orangewood is prized by craftsmen making delicate wooden instruments.
