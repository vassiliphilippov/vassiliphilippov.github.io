petfood

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛtˌfuːd / (AmE), / ˈpɛtˌfuːd / (BrE)
food that is specially prepared and manufactured for pets, especially cats and dogs, to provide their nutritional needs.
food prepared for pets

Examples

The supermarket has a large section devoted to petfood for cats and dogs.
She always reads the labels carefully before buying petfood for her puppy.
Many veterinarians recommend a specific type of petfood for pets with allergies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pet - domesticated animal kept for companionship
  • Synonym: pet food - alternative spelling of petfood; food for pets
