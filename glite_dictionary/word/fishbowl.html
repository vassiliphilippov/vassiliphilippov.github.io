fishbowl

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃˌboʊl / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌbəʊl / (BrE)
a situation in which a person or group has no privacy and is subject to constant observation
state of no privacy

Examples

Being a celebrity can feel like living in a fishbowl, with paparazzi following your every move.
The small town was such a fishbowl that everyone knew each other's business.
She found it difficult to adjust to the fishbowl environment of her new high-profile job.

Synset Examples

the president lives in a goldfish bowl

2. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃˌboʊl / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃbəʊl / (BrE)
a glass bowl, typically round and transparent, used for keeping small fish as pets
glass bowl for keeping pet fish

Examples

She kept her goldfish in a large fishbowl on the living room table.
The fishbowl needed to be cleaned after a week of use.
He tapped on the fishbowl and watched the fish dart around.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aquarium - a tank, typically larger than a fishbowl, for keeping aquatic animals and plants
