stein

1. NOUN

/ staɪn / (AmE), / staɪn / (BrE)
a large mug, typically made of ceramic, used for drinking beer
beer mug

Examples

He raised his stein and toasted the newlyweds.
The Bavarian festival featured traditional music, food, and overflowing steins of beer.
She collected vintage steins from breweries all over Germany.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tankard - a large drinking cup with a single handle, often made of silver or pewter

Etymology

From German 'Stein' (stone, beer mug).
