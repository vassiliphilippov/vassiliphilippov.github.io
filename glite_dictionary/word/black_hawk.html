Black Hawk

1. PROPN

/ blæk hɑk / (AmE), / blæk hɔːk / (BrE)
(1767–1838) A Sauk leader who led Sauk and Fox warriors against the United States in the Black Hawk War of 1832.
Sauk leader, led 1832 war against U.S.

Examples

Black Hawk is remembered for his leadership during the resistance against U.S. expansion.
Many historians study the legacy of Black Hawk and the Black Hawk War.
The autobiography of Black Hawk provides insight into Native American history.
