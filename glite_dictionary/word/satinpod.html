satinpod

1. NOUN

/ ˈsætɪnˌpɑd / (AmE), / ˈsætɪnˌpɒd / (BrE)
a southeastern European plant (Lunaria annua) cultivated for its fragrant purplish flowers and round, flat, silvery-white seedpods used in indoor decoration
ornamental plant with silvery seedpods

Examples

The dried satinpod seedpods shimmer in the sunlight on her windowsill.
Gardeners often plant satinpod to enjoy its beautiful spring flowers and unique seedpods.
She arranged a bouquet of satinpod stems as a striking table centerpiece.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honesty - another common name for Lunaria annua, the satinpod plant
