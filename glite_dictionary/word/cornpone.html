cornpone

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrnˌpoʊn / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌpəʊn / (BrE)
A simple cornbread, usually made without milk or eggs, and often baked or fried, especially in the southern United States.
simple southern cornbread

Examples

She made a fresh skillet of cornpone for breakfast every morning.
The smell of cornpone and beans filled the kitchen.
His grandmother's recipe for cornpone did not call for milk or eggs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cornbread - bread made from cornmeal, especially in the US

Etymology

From 'corn' + 'pone'; 'pone' derives from Virginia Algonquian *apan*, meaning 'baked'.
