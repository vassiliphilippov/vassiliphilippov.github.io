saree

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːri / (AmE), / ˈsɑːri / (BrE)
A traditional garment worn by women, especially in South Asia, consisting of a long length of fabric wrapped around the body and draped over one shoulder.
traditional South Asian women's garment

Examples

She wore a vibrant red saree to the wedding ceremony.
The museum showcased a collection of antique sarees from different regions of India.
Learning to drape a saree can be challenging for first timers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sari - alternate spelling of 'saree' for the same garment

Etymology

From Hindi 'sāṛī', from Sanskrit 'śāṭī' (strip of cloth).
