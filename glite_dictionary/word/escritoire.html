escritoire

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛskrəˈtwɑr / (AmE), / ˌɛskrɪˈtwɑː / (BrE)
A writing desk, especially one with drawers and compartments.
writing desk with compartments

Examples

She restored the antique escritoire she inherited from her grandmother.
The letters were neatly stored in the drawers of the escritoire.
He sat at his escritoire every evening to write in his journal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desk - a piece of furniture with a flat surface used for working at

Etymology

From French 'écritoire' (writing desk), from 'écrire' (to write).
