3rd

1. ADJ

/ θɝːd / (AmE), / θɜːd / (BrE)
coming next after the second and just before the fourth in position; ordinal form of three
ordinal number: third

Examples

She finished in 3rd place in the race.
His office is on the 3rd floor of the building.
This is the 3rd time he has visited this week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: third - coming next after the second; ordinal number
  • Degree Lower: 2nd - coming next after the first; ordinal number
  • Degree Higher: 4th - coming next after the third; ordinal number
