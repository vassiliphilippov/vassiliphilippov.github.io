bizonal

1. ADJ

/ baɪˈzoʊnəl / (AmE), / bʌɪˈzəʊnəl / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with the combined affairs of two administrative zones, especially in reference to post-World War II Germany.
relating to two combined administrative zones

Examples

After the war, a bizonal administration managed several aspects of the occupied region.
The introduction of bizonal currency marked an important shift in German economic policy.
Historians often study the effects of the bizonal agreements on postwar recovery.

Synset Examples

Bizonal currency was used in occupied Germany after World War II

Etymology

From prefix 'bi-' meaning 'two' and 'zonal' (pertaining to a zone).
