starlit

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɑːrˌlɪt / (AmE), / ˈstɑː.lɪt / (BrE)
lit only by the light of stars; illuminated by starlight.
lit by starlight

Examples

They walked hand in hand under the starlit sky.
The campsite was quiet in the starlit darkness.
Her face was barely visible in the starlit night.

Synset Examples

the starlit darkness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moonlit - lit by the light of the moon

Etymology

Formed from 'star' + 'lit', with 'lit' being the past participle of 'light'.
