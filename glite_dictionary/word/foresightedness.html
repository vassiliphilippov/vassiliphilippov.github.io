foresightedness

1. NOUN

/ fɔrˈsaɪtɪdnɪs / (AmE), / fɔːˈsaɪtɪdnɪs / (BrE)
The ability to anticipate and prepare for future needs or events; prudence in planning for the future.
ability to anticipate and plan for the future

Examples

Her foresightedness allowed the company to avoid potential losses during the economic downturn.
We admire his foresightedness in investing in renewable energy when others hesitated.
Without foresightedness, it is easy to fall into avoidable difficulties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foresight - the ability to anticipate future events or needs
  • Antonym: shortsightedness - lack of ability to plan for the future

Etymology

From 'foresighted' (from 'fore-' meaning 'before' + 'sighted') + '-ness'.
