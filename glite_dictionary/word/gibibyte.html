gibibyte

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪbiˌbaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɪbɪbʌɪt / (BrE)
A unit of digital information equal to 1,073,741,824 bytes (2^30 bytes), or 1,024 mebibytes.
unit of digital information (1,073,741,824 bytes)

Examples

The new hard drive has a capacity of two gibibytes.
One gibibyte is exactly 1,073,741,824 bytes.
Most operating systems now report memory sizes in gibibytes instead of gigabytes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gigabyte - unit of digital information (1,000,000,000 bytes)
  • Degree Lower: mebibyte - unit of digital information (1,048,576 bytes)
  • Degree Higher: tebibyte - unit of digital information (1,099,511,627,776 bytes)

Etymology

Formed from 'gibi-' (a binary prefix meaning 2^30) and 'byte'.
