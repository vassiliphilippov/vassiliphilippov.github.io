sharp-eyed

1. ADJ

/ ʃɑrp aɪd / (AmE), / ʃɑːp aɪd / (BrE)
Having quick and keen perception or alertness, especially in noticing details.
keenly perceptive or observant

Examples

The sharp-eyed detective immediately noticed the tiny scratch on the window.
A sharp-eyed editor caught the typo before the article was printed.
Our sharp-eyed tour guide spotted a rare bird in the distance.

Synset Examples

quick-sighted into the faults of the time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: observant - quick to notice things; attentive
  • Synonym: alert - quick to notice and respond; vigilant
