to desalinize

1. VERB

/ diˈsæləˌnaɪz / (AmE), / diˈsælənaɪz / (BrE)
to remove salt from something, especially from water
remove salt from (usually water)

Examples

Many countries use special plants to desalinize seawater for drinking purposes.
It is costly to desalinize large volumes of ocean water for agricultural use.
Researchers are developing new methods to desalinize water more efficiently.

Synset Examples

desalinate water

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to desalinate - remove salt from (especially water)

Etymology

From de- (removal) + saline (salty) + -ize (verb-forming suffix).
