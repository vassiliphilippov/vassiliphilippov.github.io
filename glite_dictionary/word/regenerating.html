regenerating

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈdʒɛnəˌreɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a tissue, organ, or part) undergoing renewal or growth after being damaged or lost; in the process of being regenerated.
undergoing renewal or regrowth

Examples

The scientist observed the regenerating nerve cells under the microscope.
After the injury, the regenerating tissue began to restore the damaged area.
These plants are known for rapidly regenerating their leaves after being eaten by herbivores.

Synset Examples

regenerating fibers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regenerate - to grow again after being lost or damaged

Etymology

From Latin regenerare (to be born again); participle form of 'regenerate.'
