cytoplastic

1. ADJ

/ ˌsaɪtəˈplæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌsʌɪtəʊˈplastɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a cytoplast, the cell's contents excluding the nucleus
relating to a cytoplast
medical

Examples

The cytoplastic region of the cell was isolated for further study.
Researchers examined the cytoplastic changes that occurred after the nucleus was removed.
Advances in cytoplastic techniques have made cell manipulation more precise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cytoplasmic - relating to cytoplasm (the material within a cell, excluding the nucleus)

Etymology

From cytoplast (cell body excluding the nucleus) + -ic.
