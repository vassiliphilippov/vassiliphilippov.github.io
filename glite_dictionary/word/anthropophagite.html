anthropophagite

1. NOUN

/ ˌænθrəˈpɒfəˌdʒaɪt / (AmE), / ˌænθrəˈpɒfəˌdʒaɪt / (BrE)
a person who eats human flesh; a cannibal
person who eats human flesh

Examples

In the novel, the isolated tribe was rumored to harbor an anthropophagite among its members.
Legends of an anthropophagite lurking at the edge of the village have long frightened the children.
The explorer wrote about an encounter with an anthropophagite during his journey through the jungle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cannibal - a person who eats the flesh of other people

Etymology

From Greek anthrōpophagos (man-eating) + -ite.
