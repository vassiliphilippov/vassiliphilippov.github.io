aneurismatic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.jə.rɪzˈmæt̬.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌæn.juː.rɪzˈmæt.ɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or affected by an aneurysm.
relating to an aneurysm
medical

Examples

The surgeon noted the aneurismatic changes in the patient's artery.
Advanced imaging revealed an aneurismatic dilation near the cerebral vessels.
The patient’s symptoms were attributed to aneurismatic complications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aneurysmal - pertaining to or resembling an aneurysm

Etymology

From Greek aneurysma (dilation or widening) + -ic.
