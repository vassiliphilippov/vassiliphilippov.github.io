tinge

1. VERB

/ tɪndʒ / (AmE), / tɪndʒ / (BrE)
to slightly affect with a particular quality, feeling, or color; to influence subtly
slightly affect or influence

Examples

Her voice was tinged with sadness as she spoke of her childhood.
The speech was tinged with irony, making the audience smile knowingly.
Even his optimism was tinged by the memory of past failures.

Synset Examples

My personal feelings color my judgment in this case
The sadness tinged his life

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taint - to contaminate or affect with an undesirable quality

Etymology

From Latin tingere (to dye or color).

2. NOUN

/ tɪndʒ / (AmE), / tɪndʒ / (BrE)
a slight but noticeable amount of a quality, feeling, or color
a slight amount or trace

Examples

The sky had a tinge of pink at sunrise.
Her voice carried a tinge of sadness when she spoke his name.
There was a tinge of garlic in the soup, making it especially flavorful.

Synset Examples

this dish could use a touch of garlic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trace - a very small amount of something
  • Synonym: hint - a very slight or indirect amount or suggestion
  • Synonym: dash - a small quantity of something, especially a liquid

Etymology

From Latin 'tingere' (to dye, to color).

/ tɪndʒ / (AmE), / tɪndʒ / (BrE)

Examples

Her cheeks had a tinge of color after the long walk in the cold.
The painting is mostly gray with just a tinge of blue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stain - a discoloration or spot
  • Synonym: hue - a color or shade of color

Etymology

From Latin 'tingere' (to dye, color).

4. VERB

/ tɪndʒ / (AmE), / tɪndʒ / (BrE)
to add a slight amount of color or a quality to something
add a slight color or quality

Examples

The evening sky was tinged with pink as the sun set.
Her words were tinged with regret, hinting at her disappointment.
The water in the vase was tinged blue by the dye from the flowers.

Synset Examples

her greying hair was tinged blond
the leaves were tinged red in November

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: dye - to color something with a strong or lasting color
