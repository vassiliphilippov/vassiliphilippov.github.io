unpretentiously

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.prɪˈtɛn.ʃəs.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prɪˈtɛn.ʃəs.li / (BrE)
in a way that does not attempt to impress others or show off; simply and modestly.
in a simple, modest manner

Examples

The restaurant is unpretentiously decorated, creating a welcoming atmosphere for everyone.
She spoke unpretentiously about her achievements, never boasting despite her success.
Their home was unpretentiously furnished, focusing on comfort rather than style.

Synset Examples

she was unpretentiously dressed even though she was the guest of honor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: modestly - in a way that is not intended to attract attention or admiration
  • Synonym: humbly - in a way that shows modesty or low estimate of one's importance
  • Antonym: pretentiously - in a way that attempts to impress by affecting greater importance or merit than is actually possessed

Etymology

From 'unpretentious' (not attempting to impress), + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
