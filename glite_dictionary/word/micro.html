micro

1. ADJ

/ ˈmaɪkroʊ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊ / (BrE)
Extremely small in scale, scope, or capability; miniature.
extremely small; miniature

Examples

The micro sensors in the device can detect even the slightest movement.
Scientists are studying micro changes in the climate that could have big effects.
She prefers stories that focus on the micro details of everyday life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mini - very small; miniature
  • Antonym: macro - large-scale; opposite of micro

Etymology

From Greek mikros (small).
