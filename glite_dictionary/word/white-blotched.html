white-blotched

1. ADJ

/ ˈwaɪt ˌblɑtʃt / (AmE), / ˈwaɪt ˌblɒtʃt / (BrE)
Marked with irregular spots or patches of white color.
having blotches of white

Examples

The white-blotched snake camouflaged itself among the rocks.
She admired the white-blotched pattern on the butterfly's wings.
The artist painted a white-blotched dog on the canvas.
