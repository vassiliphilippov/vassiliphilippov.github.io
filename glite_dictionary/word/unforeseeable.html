unforeseeable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.fɔrˈsi.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.fɔːˈsiː.ə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be predicted or anticipated; impossible to foresee.
impossible to predict

Examples

The accident was caused by unforeseeable circumstances beyond anyone's control.
No one could have prepared for the unforeseeable impact of the economic crisis.
Changing weather makes travel plans subject to unforeseeable delays.

Synset Examples

unforeseeable consequences

Related Terms

  • Antonym: predictable - able to be predicted or foreseen
  • Antonym: foreseeable - able to be foreseen or anticipated

Etymology

Formed from un- (not) + foreseeable, from foresee.
