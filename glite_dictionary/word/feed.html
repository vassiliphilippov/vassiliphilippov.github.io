feed

1. VERB

/ fid / (AmE), / fiːd / (BrE)
to supply with fertilizers or nutrients, especially in order to help plants grow
supply with nutrients or fertilizers

Examples

Gardeners often feed their roses in early spring to promote abundant blooms.
You should feed the soil every few weeks during the growing season for best results.
The farmer feeds his crops with a natural organic fertilizer to improve the harvest.

Synset Examples

We should fertilize soil if we want to grow healthy plants

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fertilize - to add substances to soil to make plants grow better

Etymology

Old English *fēdan* (to nourish, supply with food), from *fōda* (food).
