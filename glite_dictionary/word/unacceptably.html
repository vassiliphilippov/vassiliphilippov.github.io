unacceptably

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.əkˈsɛptəbli / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əkˈsɛptəbli / (BrE)
To a degree or in a manner that is not acceptable or cannot be tolerated.
to an unacceptable degree

Examples

The noise from the construction site was unacceptably loud, disturbing everyone nearby.
Customer wait times at the restaurant have become unacceptably long this month.
Many residents found the level of air pollution unacceptably high and demanded action.

Synset Examples

The percentage of lead in our drinking water is unacceptably high

Related Terms

  • Antonym: acceptably - to a degree that is acceptable
  • Synonym: intolerably - to a degree that cannot be tolerated
