one-hundred-millionth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ˌhʌndrəd ˈmɪljənθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˌhʌndrəd ˈmɪljənθ / (BrE)
each of the hundred million equal parts into which something is divided; 1/100,000,000th part.
one part in one hundred million

Examples

Her calculations required measuring distances as small as a one-hundred-millionth of a meter.
Even a one-hundred-millionth change in temperature could affect the experiment's results.
They divided the profit into a one-hundred-millionth for each investor.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: millionth - one part in a million equal parts
  • Degree Higher: billionth - one part in a billion equal parts
