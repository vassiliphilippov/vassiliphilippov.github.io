oppa

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊ.pɑː / (AmE), / ˈɒ.pɑː / (BrE)
(in Korean culture) a term used by a female to address an older male who is a close friend, boyfriend, or older brother.
Korean term for older male (used by females)

Examples

She always calls her favorite K-pop idol oppa during fan meetings.
In Korean dramas, the lead actress often refers to her love interest as oppa.
Lisa affectionately calls her big brother oppa when speaking to him.

Etymology

Korean 오빠 (oppa), meaning 'older brother' or 'older male friend (to a female)'.
