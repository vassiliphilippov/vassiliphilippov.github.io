bosomy

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʊzəmi / (AmE), / ˈbɒzəmi / (BrE)
(of a woman) having large breasts and often an attractive, full figure.
having large breasts

Examples

The actress was known for her bosomy appearance in classic films.
In the painting, the artist depicted a bosomy figure reclining on a sofa.
She wore a dress that accentuated her bosomy silhouette.

Synset Examples

Hollywood seems full of curvaceous blondes
a curvy young woman in a tight dress

Related Terms

  • Synonym: busty - (of a woman) having a large bust

Etymology

From 'bosom' + '-y', forming adjectives meaning 'having a bosom'.
