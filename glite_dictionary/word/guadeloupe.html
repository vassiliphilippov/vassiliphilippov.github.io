Guadeloupe

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡwɑːdəˈluːp / (AmE), / ˌɡwɑːdəˈluːp / (BrE)
an island group and overseas region of France in the eastern Caribbean, known for its tourism industry
French island group in the Caribbean

Examples

I'm planning to visit Guadeloupe next winter for its beautiful beaches and warm climate.
After moving to Guadeloupe, she began working in the hotel industry.
Guadeloupe is famous for its blend of French and Caribbean culture.

Etymology

From French Guadeloupe, named by Christopher Columbus after the Spanish monastery of Santa María de Guadalupe.
