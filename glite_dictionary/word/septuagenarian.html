septuagenarian

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛptuədʒəˈnɛriən / (AmE), / ˌsɛptjʊədʒəˈneəriən / (BrE)
a person who is between 70 and 79 years old
person aged in their seventies

Examples

The oldest participant in the marathon was a septuagenarian who amazed everyone with his endurance.
As a proud septuagenarian, she remains active in community service and local politics.
Many septuagenarians find that retirement gives them more time to pursue their hobbies.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: octogenarian - a person aged between 80 and 89
  • Degree Lower: sexagenarian - a person aged between 60 and 69

Etymology

From Latin 'septuagenarius' (containing seventy), from 'septuageni' (seventy each).
