unsarcastic

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.sɑrˈkæs.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌn.sɑːˈkæs.tɪk / (BrE)
not sarcastic; lacking or free from sarcasm.
not sarcastic

Examples

Her unsarcastic response surprised everyone, as she was usually quite witty.
Despite the heated debate, his tone remained genuinely unsarcastic throughout the discussion.
I appreciated her unsarcastic compliment about my presentation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sarcastic - using irony to mock or convey contempt

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'sarcastic'.
