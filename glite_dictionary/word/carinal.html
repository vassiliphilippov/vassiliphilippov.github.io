carinal

1. ADJ

/ kəˈraɪ.nəl / (AmE), / kəˈraɪ.nəl / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a carina, especially a ridge or keel in anatomy or zoology.
relating to a carina (ridge or keel)
medical

Examples

The surgeon examined the carinal region of the trachea during the procedure.
Abnormalities in the carinal structure can affect respiratory function.
The study focused on the carinal cartilage in several bird species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carina - a ridge or keel-like structure in anatomy or zoology

Etymology

From Latin 'carina' (keel, ridge) plus the suffix '-al'.
