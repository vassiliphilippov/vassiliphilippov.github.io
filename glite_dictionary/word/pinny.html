pinny

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪni / (AmE), / ˈpɪni / (BrE)
A sleeveless dress or apron worn over other clothing to protect it from dirt or stains.
sleeveless apron-like dress
british

Examples

She wore a pinny while baking to keep her dress clean.
The preschoolers each put on a colorful pinny before painting.
My grandmother always had a pinny on in the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pinafore - a sleeveless apron-like garment worn over clothes

Etymology

Shortened from 'pinafore', itself from 'pin' and 'afore' (before).
