benefactor

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛnəˌfæktər / (AmE), / ˈbɛnɪˌfæktə / (BrE)
a person who gives money or other help to a person or organization
a person who gives help, especially financial

Examples

The generous benefactor donated a large sum to the new hospital wing.
She became a benefactor of the arts by supporting young musicians.
Without a benefactor, the charity would have struggled to survive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patron - a person who gives financial or other support to a person or cause
  • Antonym: beneficiary - a person who receives benefit or help

Etymology

From Latin 'benefacere' (to do good).
