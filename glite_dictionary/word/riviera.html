Riviera

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈvɪriə / (AmE), / rɪˈvɪəriə / (BrE)
the Mediterranean coastal region between La Spezia in Italy and Cannes in France, famous for its resorts and mild climate
Mediterranean coastal region in southern Europe

Examples

He spent his vacation relaxing on the Riviera and enjoying the summer sun.
The Riviera is renowned for its glamorous beaches and luxurious hotels.
Many artists and writers have been inspired by the beauty of the Riviera.

Synset Examples

the Riviera contains some of Europe's most popular resorts

Etymology

From Italian *riviera* (coastline).
