abdominovesical

1. ADJ

/ æbˌdɑmɪnoʊˈvɛsɪkəl / (AmE), / æbˌdɒmɪnəʊˈvɛsɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to both the abdomen and the urinary bladder
relating to abdomen and bladder
medical

Examples

The surgeon performed an abdominovesical procedure to correct the patient's condition.
Damage to the abdominovesical region can complicate both digestive and urinary functions.
An abdominovesical fistula is an abnormal connection between the abdomen and the bladder.

Etymology

From Latin *abdomen* (belly) + *vesica* (bladder).
