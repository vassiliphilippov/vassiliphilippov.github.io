shirtmaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɝtˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːtˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person or company whose occupation is to make shirts, especially tailored or custom shirts
person who makes shirts

Examples

The skilled shirtmaker measured each customer to create a perfect fit.
He apprenticed with a renowned shirtmaker in London for three years.
Many luxury brands employ a master shirtmaker to craft their bespoke products.

Etymology

From 'shirt' + 'maker'; 'maker' from Old English 'macian' (to make).
