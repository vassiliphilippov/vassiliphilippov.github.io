lusus naturae

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːsəs nəˈtʃʊəri, nəˈtʃʊri / (AmE), / ˈluːsʊs næˈtjʊəriː / (BrE)
a person or animal that is markedly unusual or deformed, as if a 'freak of nature'
a freak or anomaly of nature

Examples

The villagers regarded the boy as a lusus naturae, keeping him hidden from outsiders.
In Victorian times, people with rare conditions were often labeled a lusus naturae.
That two-headed calf was truly a lusus naturae and amazed everyone who saw it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freak - a person or animal markedly unusual or deformed

Etymology

Latin, literally ‘sport of nature’
