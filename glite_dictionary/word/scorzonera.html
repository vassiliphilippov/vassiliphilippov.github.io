Scorzonera

1. NOUN

/ skɔːrˈzoʊnərə / (AmE), / skɔːˈzɒnərə / (BrE)
A genus of European herbs with narrow leaves, belonging to the family Asteraceae.
genus of narrow-leaved European herbs

Examples

The roots of Scorzonera are sometimes cultivated as a vegetable in Europe.
Botanists have identified several species within the genus Scorzonera.
Wild Scorzonera grows abundantly in meadows across southern Europe.

Etymology

From Italian 'scorzonera', referring to the black salsify plant.
