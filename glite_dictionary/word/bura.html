Bura

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊrə / (AmE), / ˈbʊərə / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken south of Lake Chad, especially in parts of Nigeria such as Borno State and Adamawa State.
a Chadic language of Nigeria

Examples

The people of Biu primarily speak Bura in their daily lives.
She is fluent in both Hausa and Bura.
Researchers are working to document the grammar of the Bura language.
