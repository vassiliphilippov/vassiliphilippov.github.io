Aristopak

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛrɪstoʊˈpæk / (AmE), / ˌærɪstəˈpæk / (BrE)
A trademarked name for a synthetic corticosteroid medication (triamcinolone acetonide) used as an anti-inflammatory agent.
synthetic corticosteroid drug (brand name)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Aristopak for her severe allergic reaction.
Patients with joint inflammation may receive Aristopak injections as part of their treatment plan.
Before using Aristopak, consult your physician about possible side effects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kenalog - brand name for triamcinolone acetonide, a corticosteroid
  • Synonym: Aristocort - another brand name for triamcinolone acetonide, a corticosteroid
