violated

1. ADJ

/ ˈvaɪ.əˌleɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈvaɪ.əˌleɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
treated with lack of respect or as sacred; profaned or desecrated, especially in a religious context.
treated irreverently; desecrated

Examples

The ancient temple was violated by intruders who ignored its religious significance.
They felt that the cemetery had been violated when the gravestones were knocked over.
Some considered the artwork violated because modern graffiti covered its sacred images.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desecrated - treated with violent disrespect; profaned, especially a sacred place
  • Antonym: consecrated - made or declared sacred

Etymology

Past participle of 'violate', from Latin violare (to treat with violence, dishonor).
