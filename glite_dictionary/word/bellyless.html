bellyless

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛliləs / (AmE), / ˈbɛliləs / (BrE)
Having no belly or lacking a prominent belly.
lacking a belly

Examples

The artist drew a bellyless figure to emphasize fragility and grace.
Some snakes are almost bellyless compared to others, making them hard to spot in the wild.
After the long illness, he became noticeably bellyless and thin.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: belly - the front part of the human body below the ribs; the abdomen

Etymology

From 'belly' + '-less' (suffix meaning 'without').
