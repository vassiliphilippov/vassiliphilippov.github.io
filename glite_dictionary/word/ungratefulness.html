ungratefulness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈɡreɪtfəlnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡreɪtf(ə)lnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being ungrateful; lack of gratitude.
lack of gratitude

Examples

She was hurt by her son's ungratefulness despite all she had done for him.
Many people were shocked by the team's ungratefulness toward their dedicated coach.
His ungratefulness became clear when he complained about every favor he received.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: gratitude - the quality of being thankful; readiness to show appreciation
  • Synonym: ingratitude - the state of being ungrateful; lack of gratitude

Etymology

From 'ungrateful' + '-ness'.
