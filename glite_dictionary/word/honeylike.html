honeylike

1. ADJ

/ ˈhʌniˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈhʌniˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of honey, especially in appearance, texture, or taste.
resembling honey

Examples

The sauce had a honeylike sweetness that complemented the spicy chicken.
Sunlight gave the syrup a honeylike golden hue.
Her voice had a soft, honeylike warmth that comforted everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honeyed - having the taste, appearance, or sweetness of honey
