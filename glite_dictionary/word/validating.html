validating

1. ADJ

/ ˈvælɪˌdeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvælɪdeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
serving to support, corroborate, or confirm the truth or accuracy of something
supporting or corroborating

Examples

She presented several validating documents to support her claim.
The scientist looked for validating evidence in the experimental results.
A validating comment from a trusted colleague can boost your confidence.

Synset Examples

collateral evidence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corroborative - tending to confirm or support
  • Antonym: invalidating - making something unsupportable or undermining

Etymology

From the verb 'validate', which comes from Latin 'validus' (strong, powerful).
