nonastringent

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.əˈstrɪn.dʒənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.əˈstrɪn.dʒənt / (BrE)
not having an astringent or puckering effect; lacking the quality that causes dryness or contraction in the mouth or tissues.
not astringent; lacking puckering effect

Examples

This variety of persimmon is nonastringent, making it sweet and edible even when firm.
The doctor recommended a nonastringent lotion for my sensitive skin.
Unlike the previous solution, this one is completely nonastringent and gentle to use.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: astringent - having a puckering, drying effect, especially in taste

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'non-' (not) to 'astringent'.
