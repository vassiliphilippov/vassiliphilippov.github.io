Lozal

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊzæl / (AmE), / ˈləʊzæl / (BrE)
A trademark for a diuretic drug used to treat high blood pressure (hypertension).
trademark diuretic for hypertension
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Lozal to help control my blood pressure.
You should not stop taking Lozal without consulting your physician.
The pharmacist explained how to take Lozal safely every day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indapamide - a diuretic medication for hypertension; active ingredient in Lozal
