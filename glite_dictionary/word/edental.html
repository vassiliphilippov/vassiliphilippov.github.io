edental

1. ADJ

/ iˈdɛntəl / (AmE), / iˈdɛntəl / (BrE)
(of an animal) having few or no teeth.
having few or no teeth

Examples

Some mammals, such as anteaters, are edental and lack functional teeth.
Unlike most other mammals, the pangolin is edental and has no teeth at all.
Researchers studied the feeding habits of several edental species in the rainforest.

Synset Examples

anteaters are edentate animals

Related Terms

  • Synonym: edentate - (of an animal) having few or no teeth
  • Antonym: toothed - having teeth

Etymology

From Latin 'edentulus', from 'e-' (without) + 'dens, dentis' (tooth).
