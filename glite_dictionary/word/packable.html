packable

1. ADJ

/ ˈpæk.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈpak.ə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be packed easily, especially into a container, suitcase, or package
able to be packed

Examples

The packable jacket folds into a small pouch for easy travel.
We need to buy packable food for our hiking trip.
Her luggage included only lightweight and packable items.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unpackable - not able to be packed
  • Synonym: portable - able to be carried or moved easily

Etymology

From 'pack' (to put into a container) + '-able' (able to be).
