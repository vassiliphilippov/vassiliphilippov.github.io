unequalled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈikwəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈiːkwəld / (BrE)
so good or impressive that nothing else matches or surpasses it; having no equal
having no equal; unparalleled

Examples

Her unequalled dedication to the project earned her everyone’s respect.
The artist’s unequalled talent made his work highly sought after.
This bakery is known for its unequalled chocolate cake.

Synset Examples

he is alone in the field of microbiology
this theory is altogether alone in its penetration of the problem
Bach was unique in his handling of counterpoint
craftsmen whose skill is unequaled
unparalleled athletic ability
a breakdown of law unparalleled in our history

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unparalleled - having no parallel or equal; exceptional
  • Synonym: unique - being the only one of its kind; unlike anything else
  • Synonym: matchless - unable to be equaled; incomparable

Etymology

From un- (not) + equalled (past participle of 'equal'), meaning 'not equalled.'
