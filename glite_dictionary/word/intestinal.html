intestinal

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈtɛstɪnəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛstɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to or situated in the intestines.
relating to the intestines
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed him with an intestinal infection after evaluating his symptoms.
Certain bacteria help maintain a healthy intestinal environment.
She took medication to improve her intestinal health.

Synset Examples

intestinal disease

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intestinal tract - the whole passage through which food passes, including the intestine

Etymology

From Latin 'intestinus' (internal), from 'intestinum' (intestine).
