Angas

1. NOUN

/ ˈæŋ.ɡəs / (AmE), / ˈæŋ.ɡəs / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in northern Nigeria, closely related to Hausa and including dialects such as Hill Angas and Plain Angas
Chadic language of northern Nigeria

Examples

The Angas language is mainly spoken in Plateau State, Nigeria.
Linguists have studied the Angas dialects to understand their relationship to Hausa.
Many Angas speakers are bilingual in Hausa and English.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hausa - West Chadic language spoken mainly in Nigeria and Niger
