theosophical

1. ADJ

/ ˌθiəˈsɑfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌθiːəˈsɒfɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to theosophy or its teachings, especially those promoted by the Theosophical Society.
relating to theosophy

Examples

She studied the theosophical texts to better understand spiritual evolution.
The museum features several theosophical paintings by early 20th-century artists.
His approach to life was shaped by theosophical principles and mystical experiences.

Synset Examples

theosophical writings

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theosophy - a mystical philosophical system seeking direct knowledge of the divine

Etymology

From theosophy + -ical, ultimately from Greek theos (god) + sophia (wisdom).
