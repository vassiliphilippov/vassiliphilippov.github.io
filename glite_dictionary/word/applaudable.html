applaudable

1. ADJ

/ əˈplɔːdəbl̩ / (AmE), / əˈplɔːdəbl̩ / (BrE)
Deserving praise or approval; worthy of high commendation.
worthy of praise or approval

Examples

Her applaudable dedication to the community inspired many volunteers to join the cause.
His applaudable decision to donate the prize money was recognized by the entire organization.
The team's applaudable efforts led to measurable improvements in the quality of education.

Synset Examples

applaudable efforts to save the environment
a commendable sense of purpose
laudable motives of improving housing conditions
a significant and praiseworthy increase in computer intelligence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commendable - deserving praise or approval
  • Synonym: laudable - deserving praise and commendation
  • Synonym: praiseworthy - worthy of praise

Etymology

From Latin 'applaudere' (to clap), via 'applaud' + '-able'.
