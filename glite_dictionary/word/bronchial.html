bronchial

1. ADJ

/ ˈbrɑːŋ.ki.əl / (AmE), / ˈbrɒŋ.kɪ.əl / (BrE)
relating to the bronchi, the major air passages of the lungs.
relating to the bronchi
medical

Examples

The doctor said my cough was caused by a bronchial infection.
She was diagnosed with bronchial asthma during childhood.
Exposure to smoke can irritate the bronchial tubes and make breathing difficult.

Synset Examples

bronchial tubes
bronchial pneumonia

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bronchitis - inflammation of the bronchi

Etymology

From French *bronchial*, from Latin *bronchia* (the windpipe, bronchi).
