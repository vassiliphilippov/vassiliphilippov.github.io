clarinetist

1. NOUN

/ klæɹəˈnɛtɪst / (AmE), / klɑːrɪˈnɛtɪst / (BrE)
a musician who plays the clarinet
a person who plays the clarinet

Examples

The clarinetist performed a beautiful solo during the orchestra's concert.
My sister is a talented clarinetist in the school band.
The jazz ensemble features a renowned clarinetist as its lead musician.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clarinettist - a musician who plays the clarinet (alternative spelling)

Etymology

From 'clarinet' + '-ist' (denoting someone who plays a musical instrument).
