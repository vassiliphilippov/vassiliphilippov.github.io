hilt

1. NOUN

/ hɪlt / (AmE), / hɪlt / (BrE)
the handle of a sword, dagger, or similar weapon
handle of sword or dagger

Examples

He gripped the hilt of his sword tightly as he prepared to fight.
The jeweled hilt gleamed in the torchlight, drawing everyone's attention.
She quickly drew her dagger by the hilt and stepped back.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handle - the part of an object designed to be held or operated by hand

Etymology

Probably of Germanic origin; compare Old English hilt (handle of a sword).
