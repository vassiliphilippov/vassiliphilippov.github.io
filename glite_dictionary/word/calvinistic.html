Calvinistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkælvɪˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌkælvɪˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to Calvinism or its doctrines and adherents
relating to Calvinism

Examples

The church's beliefs were strongly Calvinistic in nature.
His upbringing in a Calvinistic tradition shaped his worldview.
She wrote a book on the influence of Calvinistic doctrine in Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Calvinist - a follower of the Christian teachings of John Calvin

Etymology

From Calvinism + -istic, relating to the teachings of John Calvin (1509–1564), French theologian.
