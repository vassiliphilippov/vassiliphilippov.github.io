BVD's

1. NOUN

/ biː viː ˈdiːz / (AmE), / biː viː ˈdiːz / (BrE)
a trademarked brand of men's underwear, often used to refer to men's briefs or undershorts in general
trademark: men's underwear brand

Examples

He quickly tossed on a clean pair of BVD's before getting dressed for work.
The laundry basket was full of socks and BVD's waiting to be washed.
As a kid, he always wore white BVD's under his school uniform.

Etymology

From the initials of the founders, Bradley, Voorhees & Day, American underwear manufacturers, late 19th century.
