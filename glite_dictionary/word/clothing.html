clothing

1. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkləʊðɪŋ / (BrE)
Items worn to cover the body; garments or apparel.
items worn to cover the body

Examples

He packed plenty of clothing for his trip to the mountains.
Winter clothing is essential when the temperature drops below freezing.
The store donates used clothing to families in need.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apparel - clothes; garments collectively
  • Synonym: garment - an item of clothing
  • Antonym: nakedness - the state of not wearing clothing; nudity

Etymology

From Old English *clāthing*, related to *clāth* (cloth).
