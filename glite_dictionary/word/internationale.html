Internationale

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.t̬ɚ.næʃ.əˈnæl / (AmE), / ɪnˌtɜː.næʃ.əˈnæl / (BrE)
the international socialist anthem adopted by various socialist, communist, and labor movements, originally written in French in the late 19th century
revolutionary socialist anthem

Examples

The workers gathered to sing the Internationale at the rally.
She recognized the melody of the Internationale from her history classes.
During the strike, the crowd united in singing the Internationale.

Etymology

From French 'L'Internationale', title of the anthem, based on 'internationale' (international).
