polystyrene

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɑliˈstaɪrin / (AmE), / ˌpɒliˈstʌɪriːn / (BrE)
a rigid, transparent thermoplastic material made by polymerizing styrene, often used for packaging and insulation
rigid transparent plastic polymer

Examples

The cups were made of polystyrene and were lightweight but sturdy.
To prevent damage during shipping, the electronics were packed in polystyrene foam.
Many takeaway containers are produced from expanded polystyrene because it is a good insulator.

Synset Examples

expanded polystyrene looks like a rigid white foam and is used as packing or insulation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: styrene - the monomer from which polystyrene is made

Etymology

From poly- (many) + styrene (the monomer from which it is made).
