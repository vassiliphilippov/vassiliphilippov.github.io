ropeway

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊpˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊp.weɪ / (BrE)
a system for transporting passengers or goods in carriers suspended from cables and supported by towers, typically over difficult terrain
cable transport system with suspended carriers

Examples

The newly built ropeway offers breathtaking views of the mountains as it transports tourists across the valley.
A ropeway was used to move construction materials to the remote site quickly and efficiently.
During the festival, the town's ropeway becomes one of the most popular attractions for visitors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cable car - vehicle for carrying passengers suspended from a cable
  • Synonym: funicular - cable railway on a steep incline

Etymology

From 'rope' + 'way', indicating a way or track made from rope or cables.
