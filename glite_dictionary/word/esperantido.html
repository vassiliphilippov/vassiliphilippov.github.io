Esperantido

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛspəˌrænˈtiːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɛspəˌranˈtiːdəʊ / (BrE)
An artificial language developed on the basis of Esperanto, including Ido and similar languages.
artificial language derived from Esperanto

Examples

Ido is a well-known Esperantido that was created to address perceived issues in Esperanto.
Several Esperantidos emerged in the early 20th century but none surpassed Esperanto in popularity.
The conference included a session on the history and structure of Esperantido languages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Esperanto - a constructed international auxiliary language
  • Synonym: Ido - a constructed language derived from Esperanto

Etymology

From Esperanto 'Esperantido', formed from 'Esperanto' + '-ido' (offspring, descendant).
