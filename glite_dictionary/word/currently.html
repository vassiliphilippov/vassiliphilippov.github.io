currently

1. ADV

/ ˈkɜr.ənt.li / (AmE), / ˈkʌr.ənt.li / (BrE)
At the present time; now.
at the present time, now

Examples

All museum galleries are currently open to the public.
She is currently studying law at university.
The company is currently seeking new employees for its marketing department.

Synset Examples

he is presently our ambassador to the United Nations
currently they live in Connecticut

Related Terms

  • Synonym: presently - at the present time; now
  • Antonym: formerly - in the past; previously

Etymology

From 'current' + '-ly', with 'current' from Latin 'currens' (running).
