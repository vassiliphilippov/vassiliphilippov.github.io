stableboy

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪbəlˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈsteɪb(ə)lˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy or young man employed in a stable to take care of horses
boy caring for horses in a stable

Examples

The stableboy woke up early every morning to feed and brush the horses.
As a teenager, he worked as a stableboy at the local riding school.
The wealthy owner relied on his stableboy to keep the stalls clean and the animals healthy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: groom - a person employed to care for horses
