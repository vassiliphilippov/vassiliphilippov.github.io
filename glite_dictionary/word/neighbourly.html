neighbourly

1. ADJ

/ ˈneɪbərli / (AmE), / ˈneɪbəli / (BrE)
Friendly, helpful, and considerate towards the people living nearby.
friendly and helpful to neighbours

Examples

Everyone on our street is very neighbourly and always willing to lend a hand.
She made a neighbourly gesture by bringing soup to the new residents.
It's important to be neighbourly, especially when someone is in need.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friendly - kind and pleasant; showing friendship
  • Antonym: hostile - unfriendly or antagonistic

Etymology

From neighbour + -ly, formed in Middle English based on Old English 'nēahgebūr'.
