Viverricula indica

1. NOUN

/ vɪˌvɛrɪˈkjuːlə ɪnˈdɪkə / (AmE), / vɪˌvɛrɪˈkjuːlə ɪnˈdɪkə / (BrE)
a species of small civet native to South and Southeast Asia, also known as the small Indian civet
a small Asian civet species

Examples

The Viverricula indica is frequently found in forests and plantations across India.
Researchers observed the nocturnal behavior of Viverricula indica in its natural habitat.
Farmers often encounter the Viverricula indica while working in rural parts of Southeast Asia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: small Indian civet - common name for Viverricula indica, a small civet species of Asia

Etymology

From Latin: 'viverricula' (diminutive of 'civet') and 'indica' (of India).
