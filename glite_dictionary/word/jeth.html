Jeth

1. NOUN

/ dʒeɪt / (AmE), / dʒeɪt / (BrE)
the third month of the Hindu calendar, typically falling in May and June of the Gregorian calendar.
third month of the Hindu calendar

Examples

The harvest festival is celebrated during Jeth in many parts of India.
According to the Hindu calendar, Jeth marks the beginning of the hot summer season.
Religious ceremonies in Jeth often involve prayers for rain and a good crop.

Etymology

From Hindi जेठ (jeṭh), from Sanskrit ज्येष्ठ (jyeṣṭha).
