agribusiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈæɡ.rəˌbɪz.nəs / (AmE), / ˈaɡ.rɪˌbɪz.nəs / (BrE)
a large-scale business engaged in farming and related commercial activities, often encompassing the production, processing, and distribution of agricultural products
large-scale commercial farming business

Examples

The government is considering new regulations to support sustainable agribusiness.
Rising costs have forced many family farms to merge into larger agribusinesses.
Many agribusiness companies invest heavily in advanced farming technologies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agribusiness - agricultural industry or farming business

Etymology

Blend of 'agriculture' and 'business', first used in the 1950s.
