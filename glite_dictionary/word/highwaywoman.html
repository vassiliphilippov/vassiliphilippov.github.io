highwaywoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪˌweɪˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌweɪˌwʊmən / (BrE)
(historically) A woman who robs travelers on a public road, especially one who stops vehicles and steals from them.
female highway robber

Examples

The infamous highwaywoman was finally apprehended after years of evading the authorities.
Legends spoke of a daring highwaywoman who haunted the roads outside London in the eighteenth century.
Dressed as a man, the highwaywoman stopped carriages and demanded their valuables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: highwayman - a man who robs travelers on a public road
  • Synonym: robber - someone who commits robbery

Etymology

Formed from 'highway' + 'woman', modeled after 'highwayman'.
