unquotable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkwoʊtəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwəʊtəbəl / (BrE)
not suitable or appropriate to be quoted or repeated
not appropriate to be quoted

Examples

The politician's remarks were considered too controversial and thus unquotable in the newspaper article.
Much of the movie dialogue is hilarious but rather unquotable in polite company.
She made several clever comments that were sadly unquotable due to their rudeness.

Synset Examples

what he said was funny but unquotable

Related Terms

  • Antonym: quotable - suitable or appropriate for being quoted

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'quotable'.
