undocumented

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈdɑːkjəˌmɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈdɒkjuməntɪd / (BrE)
not having the official documents required for legal permission to live, work, or do something in a country or organization.
lacking required official documents

Examples

Many undocumented immigrants face challenges when seeking basic services.
Working as an undocumented employee can carry legal risks for both the worker and the employer.
She was denied admission because her status was undocumented.

Synset Examples

undocumented aliens
undocumented tax deductions

Related Terms

  • Antonym: documented - having the required official papers or evidence

Etymology

From un- (not) + documented, meaning 'not furnished with documents'.
