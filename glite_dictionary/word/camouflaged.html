camouflaged

1. ADJ

/ ˈkæməˌflɑʒd / (AmE), / ˈkæməˌflɑːʒd / (BrE)
made difficult to see or hidden by means of protective coloring or disguise
hidden by protective coloring or disguise

Examples

The camouflaged soldiers moved quietly through the forest, blending with the foliage.
Some insects are naturally camouflaged to avoid being eaten by predators.
The building was camouflaged with netting to hide it from enemy aircraft.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concealed - kept out of sight; hidden
  • Antonym: visible - able to be seen

Etymology

From French 'camoufler' (to disguise), ultimately from Italian 'camuffare' (to disguise).
