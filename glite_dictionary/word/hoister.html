hoister

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔɪstər / (AmE), / ˈhɔɪstə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to operate a hoist, especially in an industrial setting
operator of a hoist

Examples

The hoister signaled to the team before raising the heavy load into position.
Safety training is required for every new hoister on the job site.
Experienced hoisters know how to handle both routine and emergency situations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hoist operator - person who operates a hoist
