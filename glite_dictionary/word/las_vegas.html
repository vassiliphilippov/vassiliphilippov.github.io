Las Vegas

1. PROPN

/ lɑs ˈveɪɡəs / (AmE), / læs ˈveɪɡəs / (BrE)
a city in southeastern Nevada, United States, renowned for its casinos, entertainment, and nightlife
city in Nevada famous for casinos and entertainment

Examples

She traveled to Las Vegas to celebrate her birthday with friends.
Many tourists visit Las Vegas for its famous casinos and vibrant nightlife.
The annual music festival in Las Vegas attracts thousands of visitors each year.

Etymology

Spanish for 'the meadows', referring to the area's spring-fed grasslands when first settled.
