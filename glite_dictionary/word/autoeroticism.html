autoeroticism

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔtoʊɪˈrɑtɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊɪˈrɒtɪˌsɪzəm / (BrE)
sexual arousal or gratification achieved by stimulating one's own body, rather than through contact with another person
self-induced sexual arousal or gratification

Examples

Some psychologists have studied autoeroticism to better understand human sexuality.
The novel explores themes of isolation and autoeroticism in the protagonist's private life.
He denied engaging in autoeroticism, even though he avoided romantic relationships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masturbation - sexual stimulation of one's own genitals, usually with the hand

Etymology

From Greek 'autos' (self) + 'erōtikos' (erotic).
