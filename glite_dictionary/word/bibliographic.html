bibliographic

1. ADJ

/ ˌbɪbliəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌbɪbliəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
Relating to bibliography or the listing, cataloging, or referencing of books and publications.
relating to bibliography or lists of books

Examples

The research paper included a detailed bibliographic section at the end.
She used several bibliographic databases to find relevant articles.
All sources must be properly cited in bibliographic format.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bibliographical - alternative form of bibliographic

Etymology

From French bibliographique, from Greek biblion (book) + -graphia (writing).
