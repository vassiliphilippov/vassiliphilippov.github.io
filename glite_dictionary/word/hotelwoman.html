hotelwoman

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈtɛlˌwʊmən / (AmE), / həʊˈtɛlˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who owns or manages a hotel
female hotel owner or manager

Examples

The hotelwoman greeted the guests personally when they arrived at the front desk.
As a successful hotelwoman, she manages multiple properties across the city.
The awards ceremony honored a hotelwoman known for her dedication to hospitality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hotelier - a person who owns or manages a hotel
  • Antonym: hotelman - a man who owns or manages a hotel
