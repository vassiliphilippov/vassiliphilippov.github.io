assuming

1. ADJ

/ əˈsuːmɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈsjuːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or behavior) excessively forward or presumptuous; taking liberties beyond what is considered proper.
excessively forward or presumptuous

Examples

Her assuming tone made everyone in the room uncomfortable.
I found his assuming manner rather off-putting at the dinner party.
He apologized for being assuming when discussing topics he knew little about.

Synset Examples

an assumptive person
on a subject like this it would be too assuming for me to decide
the duchess would not put up with presumptuous servants

Related Terms

  • Synonym: presumptuous - (of a person or their behavior) failing to observe the limits of what is permitted or appropriate
  • Antonym: modest - having a humble opinion of oneself; not overly confident or forward
