expo

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛkspoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɛkspəʊ / (BrE)
a large public exhibition of goods, services, or works of art
large public exhibition

Examples

The annual technology expo attracts thousands of visitors from around the world.
She showcased her artwork at the student expo last weekend.
Many companies launched new products during the international trade expo in Paris.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhibition - a public display of works of art or items of interest
  • Synonym: fair - a public event at which goods are bought and sold and entertainment is provided

Etymology

Shortened form of 'exposition', from French 'exposition' (display, exhibition).
