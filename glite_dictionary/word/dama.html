Dama

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑːmə / (AmE), / ˈdɑːmə / (BrE)
A genus of deer comprising the fallow deer, native to Eurasia.
genus of fallow deer

Examples

The genus Dama includes both the common and Persian fallow deer.
Researchers observed the behavior of Dama in their natural habitat.
The Dama population has been stable in some European reserves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fallow deer - a species of deer, Dama dama, native to Europe and western Asia

Etymology

From Latin 'dama' (fallow deer).
