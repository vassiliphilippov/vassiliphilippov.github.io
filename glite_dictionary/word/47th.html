47th

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrti ˈsɛvənθ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːti ˈsɛvənθ / (BrE)
Being the ordinal number for forty-seven; coming after the forty-sixth in a sequence.
ordinal number for forty-seven

Examples

She finished in 47th place out of a hundred runners.
This is the 47th anniversary of the school's founding.
Our office is located on the 47th floor of the building.
