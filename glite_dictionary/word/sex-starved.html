sex-starved

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛkˌstɑrvd / (AmE), / ˈseksˌstɑːvd / (BrE)
desiring but deprived of sexual activity or gratification
desiring but lacking sexual gratification

Examples

After months of working abroad, he felt completely sex-starved.
The main character is portrayed as a sex-starved individual seeking intimacy.
She joked that her sex-starved husband kept bringing her breakfast in bed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: libidinous - having or showing strong sexual desire
  • Synonym: frustrated - experiencing dissatisfaction due to unfulfilled needs
