faster

1. ADV

/ ˈfæstər / (AmE), / ˈfɑːstə / (BrE)
at a greater speed; more quickly than something or someone else
at a greater speed; more quickly

Examples

The train moved faster than I expected and arrived ahead of schedule.
If you work faster, you'll be able to finish before lunch.
The athlete ran faster in the final lap to win the race.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: slower - at a less rapid speed; less quickly
  • Degree Higher: fastest - at the greatest possible speed; most quickly
  • Degree Lower: fast - quickly; at high speed
