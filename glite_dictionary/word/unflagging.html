unflagging

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈflæɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈflæɡɪŋ / (BrE)
never becoming tired or losing strength or determination; persistent and unceasing.
persistent; never tiring or weakening

Examples

Her unflagging support for the charity inspired everyone involved.
The team's unflagging enthusiasm helped them finish the project on time.
Despite many setbacks, his unflagging optimism kept him moving forward.

Synset Examples

unfailing loyalty
unfailing good spirits
unflagging courtesy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tireless - having or showing great effort or energy; never seeming to get tired
  • Synonym: persistent - continuing firmly in a course of action in spite of difficulty
  • Antonym: flagging - becoming tired or less enthusiastic

Etymology

From the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'flag' (to become tired or less energetic).

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈflæɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈflæɡɪŋ / (BrE)
(of effort, enthusiasm, or energy) persistent and tireless; continuing strongly without becoming weaker or less determined.
persistent; not weakening or tiring

Examples

Her unflagging optimism inspired everyone around her to keep going.
The team's unflagging dedication led them to a well-deserved victory.
Despite many setbacks, his unflagging enthusiasm for the project never waned.

Synset Examples

an indefatigable advocate of equal rights
a tireless worker
unflagging pursuit of excellence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tireless - not showing fatigue; persistent
  • Synonym: indefatigable - incapable of being tired out; persistent
  • Synonym: persistent - continuing firmly or obstinately despite difficulty
  • Antonym: flagging - becoming weaker or less energetic

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + present participle of 'flag' (to become tired or weak).
