Natalie Wood

1. PROPN

/ ˈnæt̬əli wʊd / (AmE), / ˈnætəli wʊd / (BrE)
(1938–1981) A prominent American film actress known for her roles in 'Rebel Without a Cause,' 'West Side Story,' and 'Splendor in the Grass.'
American film actress (1938–1981)

Natalie Wood starred in several classic films during the 1950s and 1960s.
