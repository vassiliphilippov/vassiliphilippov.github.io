XL

1. NUM

/ ɛks ˈɛl / (AmE), / ɛks ˈɛl / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for 40.
Roman numeral for 40

Examples

She turned XL years old last summer and threw a big party.
The Super Bowl XL was one of the most memorable in recent history.
The manuscript is dated to the year XL in Roman numerals.

Etymology

From Latin numerals: X (10) placed before L (50) signifies 40 (50−10).
