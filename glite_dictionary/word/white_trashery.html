white trashery

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈtræʃəri / (AmE), / waɪt ˈtræʃəri / (BrE)
(offensive, slang) Behavior, style, or attitude stereotypically associated with or resembling those disparagingly called 'white trash'; crude or unrefined conduct or appearance linked to poor or marginalized white people.
crude conduct or style associated with 'white trash' (offensive, slang)
