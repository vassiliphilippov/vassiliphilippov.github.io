silky-leafed

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪlki ˌliːft / (AmE), / ˈsɪlki ˌliːft / (BrE)
(of a plant) having leaves that are glossy and smooth, resembling silk in appearance or texture.
having glossy, smooth leaves

Examples

The silky-leafed plant added a touch of elegance to the living room.
Gardeners often choose silky-leafed shrubs for their ornamental value.
Several silky-leafed species thrive in humid, tropical climates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glossy-leafed - having leaves that are shiny and reflective
