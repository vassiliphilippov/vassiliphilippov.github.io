anisometric

1. ADJ

/ ˌænˌaɪsəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌænɪˌsəʊˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
having parts with unequal dimensions or measurements; not symmetrical or isometric.
having unequal dimensions; asymmetrical

Examples

The crystal exhibits an anisometric structure, making it valuable for certain industrial applications.
Some cells have an anisometric shape due to the uneven stretching of their membranes.
The artist was interested in anisometric designs to break away from traditional symmetry in his work.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: isometric - having equal dimensions or measurements
  • Synonym: asymmetrical - not symmetrical; lacking symmetry

Etymology

From Greek 'an-' (not) + 'isometrikos' (equal measure).
