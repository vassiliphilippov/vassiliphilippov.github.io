half-clothed

1. ADJ

/ ˈhæf ˌkloʊðd / (AmE), / ˈhɑːf ˌkləʊðd / (BrE)
wearing only part of one's clothing; not fully dressed.
partially dressed

Examples

The children ran through the sprinklers, half-clothed and laughing.
She answered the door half-clothed, clearly not expecting visitors.
After being awakened suddenly, he stumbled into the hall half-clothed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: half-dressed - wearing only part of one's clothes
  • Degree Higher: naked - having no clothes on
  • Degree Higher: clothed - wearing clothes; dressed
  • Degree Higher: fully clothed - wearing all one's clothes
