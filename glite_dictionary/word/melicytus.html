Melicytus

1. NOUN

/ mɛlɪˈsaɪtəs / (AmE), / ˌmiːlɪˈsɪtəs / (BrE)
a genus of deciduous shrubs or small trees, native to New Zealand, Fiji, and the Solomon Islands, bearing berry-like fruit.
genus of shrubs or trees native to New Zealand and Pacific islands

Examples

The Melicytus genus contains several species unique to New Zealand habitats.
Gardeners in the Pacific often plant Melicytus shrubs for their ornamental berries.
Botanists recently discovered a new Melicytus species on the Solomon Islands.
