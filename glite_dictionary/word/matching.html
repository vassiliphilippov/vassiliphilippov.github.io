matching

1. ADJ

/ ˈmætʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmatʃɪŋ / (BrE)
(of two or more things) being exactly the same in appearance, pattern, or style.
identical in appearance or style

Examples

She wore matching earrings and necklace to the party.
We finally found a pair of matching socks in the drawer.
The twins arrived in matching outfits that made them look identical.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: identical - exactly alike or the same in every way
  • Antonym: different - not the same as another or each other

Etymology

From the verb 'match' + -ing suffix, early 19th century.
