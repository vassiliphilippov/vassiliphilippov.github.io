equipment

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
The necessary tools, machinery, or other objects needed for a particular purpose or activity.
tools or objects needed for a task

Examples

The laboratory had all the necessary equipment for conducting experiments safely.
New equipment was purchased to improve the gym's facilities.
Please return any borrowed equipment to the storage room after use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gear - tools or equipment needed for a particular activity

Etymology

From French 'équipement', from équiper (to equip), of Germanic origin.
