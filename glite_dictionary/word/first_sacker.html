first sacker

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɝst ˌsækər / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˌsækə / (BrE)
a player who occupies the position of first base in a baseball team; first baseman
baseball player at first base

Examples

The team's new first sacker made an impressive catch in the ninth inning.
As a skilled first sacker, she rarely missed a throw to first base.
Many Hall of Famers started their careers as a first sacker before moving to another position.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first baseman - the player at first base in baseball
