couture

1. NOUN

/ kuˈtʊr / (AmE), / kuːˈtjʊə / (BrE)
the design and making of fashionable, high-quality clothes by leading fashion houses
high-end fashion design and dressmaking

Examples

Many celebrities choose to wear couture gowns on the red carpet.
She dreams of working in couture after graduating from fashion school.
The exhibition showcased decades of stunning couture from renowned designers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: haute couture - the designing and making of exclusive, custom-fitted fashion

Etymology

From French couture (sewing, dressmaking), from Old French 'cousture', from Latin 'consutura' (a sewing together).
