empurpled

1. ADJ

/ ɛmˈpɝːpəld / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɜːpəld / (BrE)
(of language or style) excessively elaborate or showily expressed, often to the point of being overly ornate.
overly ornate or elaborate in style

Examples

The novel's empurpled descriptions sometimes distracted from the plot.
His empurpled speech was full of extravagant metaphors and complex sentences.
Critics often mock such empurpled prose for lacking clarity and subtlety.

Synset Examples

a writer of empurpled literature
many purple passages
an over-embellished story of the fish that got away

Related Terms

  • Synonym: florid - using elaborate or excessively intricate language
  • Synonym: ornate - elaborately or excessively decorated or embellished
  • Antonym: plain - clear and simple in style, not elaborate

Etymology

From 'empurple' (to make purple), referring metaphorically to ornate or florid style; root 'purple' from Latin 'purpura'.
