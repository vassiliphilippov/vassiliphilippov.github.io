chapatti

1. NOUN

/ tʃəˈpɑti / (AmE), / tʃəˈpæti / (BrE)
A type of flat, round, unleavened bread, cooked on a griddle and commonly found in South Asian cuisine.
unleavened South Asian flatbread

Examples

She served hot chapattis with the curry for dinner.
Making a perfect chapatti takes skill and practice.
The restaurant offers fresh chapattis with every meal.

Etymology

From Hindi čapāṭī, from čapāṭ (flat).
