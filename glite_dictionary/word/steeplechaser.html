steeplechaser

1. NOUN

/ ˈstiːpəlˌtʃeɪsər / (AmE), / ˈstiːpəlˌtʃeɪsə / (BrE)
a horse that is specially trained for steeplechase races, which involve jumping fences and ditches over a long course
horse trained for steeplechases

Examples

The steeplechaser cleared the final hurdle with impressive agility.
She owns a retired steeplechaser that once competed in famous races across Europe.
Trainers spent months preparing the young steeplechaser for its first big event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steeplechase - a horse race over obstacles

Etymology

From 'steeplechase', a type of horse race, and '-er' forming an agent noun.
