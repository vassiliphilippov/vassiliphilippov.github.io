Myotis leucifugus

1. NOUN

/ maɪˈoʊtɪs ˌluːsɪˈfjuːɡəs / (AmE), / mʌɪˈəʊtɪs ˌlʊːsɪˈfjuːɡəs / (BrE)
A small, common North American bat species, also known as the little brown bat, widely distributed across North America.
species of small North American bat

Examples

The Myotis leucifugus is one of the most widespread bat species in North America.
Researchers studied the hibernation habits of Myotis leucifugus in northern caves.
White-nose syndrome has significantly impacted Myotis leucifugus populations in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: little brown bat - Common name for Myotis leucifugus

Etymology

From Greek 'myotis' meaning 'mouse-eared' and Latin 'leucifugus' meaning 'fleeing from light'.
