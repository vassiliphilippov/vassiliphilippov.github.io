unhazardous

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhæz.ɚ.dəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈhæz.əd.əs / (BrE)
not involving or likely to cause danger or risk; safe.
not dangerous or risky

Examples

This chemical is considered unhazardous and requires no special handling procedures.
The cleaning agent was labeled unhazardous, making it suitable for use around children and pets.
Although the task seemed risky, it was actually proven to be unhazardous.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safe - not likely to cause harm or damage
  • Antonym: hazardous - involving risk or danger

Etymology

From the prefix un- (not) + hazardous (dangerous).
