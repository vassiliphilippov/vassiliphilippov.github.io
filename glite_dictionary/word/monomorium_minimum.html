Monomorium minimum

1. NOUN

/ ˌmoʊ.nəˈmɔː.ri.əm ˈmɪn.ɪ.məm / (AmE), / ˌmɒn.əˈmɔː.ri.əm ˈmɪn.ɪ.məm / (BrE)
A tiny, glossy black ant species that typically nests outdoors but may invade houses in search of food.
species of small black ant

Examples

The Monomorium minimum is frequently found invading kitchens during the summer months.
Pest control professionals are often called to eliminate Monomorium minimum infestations.
Researchers studied the colony structure of Monomorium minimum in urban environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: little black ant - common name for Monomorium minimum, a small black ant species

Etymology

From New Latin: Monomorium (genus name) and minimum (Latin for 'smallest').
