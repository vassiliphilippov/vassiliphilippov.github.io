Drambuie

1. NOUN

/ dræmˈbuːi / (AmE), / dræmˈbjuːi / (BrE)
A sweet, golden liqueur made from Scotch whisky, honey, herbs, and spices.
sweet Scotch whisky liqueur

Examples

He offered me a glass of Drambuie after dinner.
Many classic cocktails, such as the Rusty Nail, use Drambuie as a main ingredient.
The unique flavor of Drambuie comes from its blend of Scotch whisky, honey, and herbs.

Etymology

From Scottish Gaelic 'an dram buidheach' meaning 'the drink that satisfies'.
