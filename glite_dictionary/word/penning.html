penning

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɛnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of writing, especially creating literary or artistic works
the act of writing works

Examples

Her debut novel was the result of several months of dedicated penning.
The penning of an autobiography requires both honesty and reflection.
He devoted his mornings to the penning of poetry.

Synset Examples

writing was a form of therapy for him
it was a matter of disputed authorship

Related Terms

  • Synonym: writing - the activity or skill of writing

Etymology

From the verb 'pen' (to write), ultimately from Latin 'penna' (feather, quill).
