amber-green

1. ADJ

/ ˈæmbɚ ˌɡrin / (AmE), / ˈæmbə ˌɡriːn / (BrE)
Of a green color tinged or shaded with amber; green with a hint of amber.
green with an amber tinge

Examples

The painter carefully mixed an amber-green shade to capture the afternoon light on the trees.
Her eyes had a unique amber-green hue, changing subtly in different lighting.
The fabric was dyed an amber-green color reminiscent of early autumn leaves.
