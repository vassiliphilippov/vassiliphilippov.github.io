Kalka

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːlkə / (AmE), / ˈkælkə / (BrE)
a member of the Mongol people living in the central and eastern parts of Outer Mongolia
Mongol people of central and eastern Outer Mongolia

Examples

The Kalka have maintained many traditional customs despite modern influences.
Most of the Kalka people reside in central Mongolia.
Anthropologists have studied the social structure of the Kalka.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Khalkha - the largest subgroup of Mongols in Mongolia
