hasheesh

1. NOUN

/ hæˈʃiʃ / (AmE), / hæˈʃiːʃ / (BrE)
a purified resinous extract of the hemp plant, used chiefly as a hallucinogenic drug
resinous hemp extract used as a drug

Examples

In the 19th century, writers experimented with hasheesh for inspiration.
He was arrested for possession of hasheesh at the border.
The effects of hasheesh can include altered perception and hallucinations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hashish - purified resin from the cannabis plant, used as a drug

Etymology

From Arabic ḥashīsh (dry herb, grass, hemp).
