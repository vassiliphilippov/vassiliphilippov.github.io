grimoire

1. NOUN

/ ɡrɪmˈwɑr / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪmwɑː / (BrE)
a book of magic spells and instructions, especially one used for invoking spirits or demons
book of magic spells and rituals

Examples

The old wizard kept a dusty grimoire hidden in his private library.
According to legend, the grimoire contained powerful spells for summoning otherworldly beings.
She spent years searching for the fabled grimoire that could unlock ancient secrets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spellbook - a book containing a collection of spells or magical formulas

Etymology

From French 'grimoire', originally meaning 'grammar' or 'book of learning'.
