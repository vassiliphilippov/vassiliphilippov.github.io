telecaster

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌkæstər / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌkɑːstə / (BrE)
a person or organization that broadcasts television programs
television broadcaster

Examples

The local telecaster announced the results live on air.
As a leading telecaster, she brings the news to millions every night.
Several national telecasters competed for the rights to air the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broadcaster - a person or organization engaged in broadcasting

Etymology

Formed from 'television' and 'broadcaster'.
