Pipistrellus pipistrellus

1. NOUN

/ pɪpɪˈstrɛləs pɪpɪˈstrɛləs / (AmE), / pɪpɪˈstrɛləs pɪpɪˈstrɛləs / (BrE)
A small brown bat found throughout Europe, commonly known as the common pipistrelle.
a small European brown bat

Examples

The Pipistrellus pipistrellus is one of the most widespread bat species in Europe.
Researchers studied the roosting habits of Pipistrellus pipistrellus in urban parks.
At dusk, you can often spot Pipistrellus pipistrellus fluttering around streetlights in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: common pipistrelle - the common name for Pipistrellus pipistrellus

Etymology

From Latin: genus 'Pipistrellus', species 'pipistrellus', meaning 'bat' or 'little bat'.
