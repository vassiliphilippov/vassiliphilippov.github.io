to uncompress

1. VERB

/ ˌʌn.kəmˈprɛs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kəmˈprɛs / (BrE)
to restore data or files to their original size or form by reversing compression
restore data to original form

Examples

You need to uncompress the file before you can access its contents.
After downloading the archive, I had to uncompress it to view the documents.
This software can uncompress several different types of compressed files.

Synset Examples

decompress data

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to decompress - to restore compressed data to its original form
  • Antonym: compress - to reduce the size of data or files
