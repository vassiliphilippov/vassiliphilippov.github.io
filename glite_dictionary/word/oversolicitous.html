oversolicitous

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvɚsəˈlɪsɪtəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəsəˈlɪsɪtəs / (BrE)
Excessively attentive or concerned, especially to a degree that is unnecessary or intrusive.
excessively attentive or concerned

Examples

Her oversolicitous nature sometimes made her friends uncomfortable.
The oversolicitous waiter hovered by our table for most of the meal.
Parents can become oversolicitous when they worry too much about their children's safety.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: solicitous - showing concern or care for someone's well-being

Etymology

Formed from 'over-' (excessive) + 'solicitous' (showing concern), from Latin 'sollicitus'.
