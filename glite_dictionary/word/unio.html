Unio

1. PROPN

/ ˈjuːnioʊ / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪəʊ / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Unionidae, comprising certain freshwater mussels.
genus of freshwater mussels

Examples

The genus Unio includes several species of freshwater mussels found across Europe and Asia.
Researchers collected Unio specimens from the river to study their reproductive habits.
Some Unio species are important indicators of water quality in their natural habitats.

Etymology

From Latin 'unio', meaning 'a pearl' or 'union'. Used as the genus name in zoological taxonomy.
