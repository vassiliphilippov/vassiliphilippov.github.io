maturely

1. ADV

/ məˈtʃʊrli / (AmE), / məˈtjʊəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows maturity or adult qualities.
in a mature manner

Examples

Despite being only fifteen, he handled the difficult situation very maturely.
She spoke maturely about her plans for the future, surprising everyone in the room.
The child accepted the loss maturely, without throwing a tantrum.

Synset Examples

she acts very maturely for her age

Related Terms

  • Antonym: immaturely - in an immature manner
