rectal

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛktl̩ / (AmE), / ˈrɛktl̩ / (BrE)
relating to or involving the rectum, the final section of the large intestine
relating to the rectum
medical

Examples

The doctor scheduled a rectal exam as part of the check-up.
Signs of rectal bleeding should be evaluated by a medical professional.
They discussed the results of the rectal biopsy with the patient.

Synset Examples

rectal cancer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anal - relating to or involving the anus

Etymology

From Latin 'rectum', meaning 'straight intestine', plus the suffix '-al'.
