merged

1. ADJ

/ mɜrdʒd / (AmE), / mɜːdʒd / (BrE)
combined or united to form a single entity, especially after previously being separate.
combined or united into one

Examples

The two companies have merged departments to improve efficiency.
Several cultures have merged over centuries to form a unique tradition.
After the floods, the rivers had merged into a single, wide stream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: united - joined together for a common purpose or by a common feeling
  • Antonym: separated - not joined or united; existing apart
