Cm

1. NOUN

/ si ɛm / (AmE), / siː ɛm / (BrE)
The chemical symbol for curium, a radioactive transuranic metallic element produced by bombarding plutonium with helium nuclei.
symbol for curium (element)

Examples

Scientists detected traces of Cm in the reactor’s spent fuel rods.
Handling Cm requires strict safety precautions due to its radioactivity.
The periodic table entry for curium is represented by the symbol Cm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curium - radioactive transuranic metallic element, symbol Cm

Etymology

Named after Marie and Pierre Curie.
