saltbush

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔltˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɔːltˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
any of various shrubby plants of the genus Atriplex that thrive in dry, alkaline soils, often found in arid regions
shrubby plant of the genus Atriplex

Examples

The saltbush thrives in the arid soil of the Australian outback.
Cattle often graze on saltbush during dry seasons when other vegetation is scarce.
Researchers studied how saltbush adapts to environments with high salt concentrations.
