buxomly

1. ADV

/ ˈbʌksəmli / (AmE), / ˈbʌksəmli / (BrE)
In a curvaceous or voluptuous manner; with physical fullness or plumpness.
in a curvaceous manner

Examples

She walked buxomly into the party, turning heads as she passed.
The actress posed buxomly for the glamorous photoshoot.
Dressed in vintage attire, she danced buxomly across the stage.

Synset Examples

his date was curvaceously beguiling

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curvaceously - in a shapely and curvy way

Etymology

From "buxom" (Middle English, originally meaning 'obedient, pliant'; evolved to mean 'plump, healthy, full-bosomed').
