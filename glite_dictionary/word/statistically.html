statistically

1. ADV

/ stəˈtɪstɪkli / (AmE), / stəˈtɪstɪkəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to statistics or is based on statistical data or analysis
in terms of statistics

Examples

The results were statistically significant, meaning there was a clear pattern in the data.
He claimed that, statistically, the odds of winning were extremely low.
Statistically, women live longer than men in most countries.

Synset Examples

this is statistically impossible

Related Terms

  • Synonym: statistical - relating to statistics

Etymology

From 'statistical' + '-ly'. 'Statistical' from statistic (from New Latin statisticus) + -al.
