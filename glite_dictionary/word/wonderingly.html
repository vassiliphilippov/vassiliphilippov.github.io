wonderingly

1. ADV

/ ˈwʌndɚɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈwʌndərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows curiosity and amazement; with a sense of wonder or questioning
in a curious and amazed manner

Examples

She looked wonderingly at the strange and beautiful painting on the wall.
The child asked wonderingly about the stars sparkling in the night sky.
He listened wonderingly to the incredible story his grandmother told him.

Synset Examples

they turned their heads questioningly, as if awaiting further instructions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: questioningly - in a way that expresses doubt or curiosity

Etymology

From 'wonder' + '-ing' + '-ly', forming the adverb from the verb and noun 'wonder'.
