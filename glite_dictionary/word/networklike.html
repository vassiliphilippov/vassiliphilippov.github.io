networklike

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɛtˌwɝkˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈnɛtˌwɜːkˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Having the appearance or structure of a network, especially with interconnected veins or ribs.
resembling a network

Examples

The leaf's networklike pattern makes identification easy for botanists.
Her blouse had a delicate, networklike lace design.
Scientists studied the networklike structure of the coral reef.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reticulate - having a netlike structure
