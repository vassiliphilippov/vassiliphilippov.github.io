sgraffito

1. NOUN

/ sgrəˈfiːtoʊ / (AmE), / sgrəˈfiːtəʊ / (BrE)
A technique of decorating ceramics or walls by scratching through a surface layer to reveal a different color beneath.
decoration made by scratching to reveal a layer

Examples

The artist used sgraffito to create intricate patterns on the pottery.
Renaissance buildings often feature sgraffito on their facades.
You can achieve interesting textures by experimenting with sgraffito techniques in your ceramic work.

Etymology

From Italian sgraffito, past participle of sgraffiare (to scratch).
