hirsute

1. ADJ

/ hɜrˌsut / (AmE), / hɜːˈsjuːt / (BrE)
covered with hair; having a lot of hair on the body or face.
covered with hair; hairy

Examples

The hirsute man stood out in the group because of his thick beard and chest hair.
A hirsute caterpillar crawled across the leaf in the garden.
Some dog breeds are more hirsute than others, requiring regular grooming.

Synset Examples

Jacob was a hairy man
a hairy caterpillar

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairy - covered with hair
  • Antonym: glabrous - having a surface without hair

Etymology

From Latin 'hirsutus' (hairy, bristly).
