minicar

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪniˌkɑr / (AmE), / ˈmɪniˌkɑː / (BrE)
a very small car, even smaller than a subcompact, typically designed for urban use and efficiency
very small car, smaller than a subcompact

Examples

The city is full of minicars because they are easy to park and use little fuel.
She bought a bright red minicar for her daily commute through the narrow streets.
Many minicars are popular in Europe due to tight parking spaces and busy traffic.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: subcompact - a car slightly larger than a minicar

Etymology

From 'mini-' (small) + 'car', modeled after similar compound formations.
