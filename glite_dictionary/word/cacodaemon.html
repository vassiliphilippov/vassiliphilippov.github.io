cacodaemon

1. NOUN

/ ˌkækoʊˈdiːmən / (AmE), / ˌkakəʊˈdiːmən / (BrE)
An evil spirit or malevolent supernatural being.
evil spirit or demon
archaic

Examples

The ancient text warns of the cacodaemon that preys upon the souls of the unwary.
In some mythologies, a cacodaemon is believed to haunt desolate places.
The villagers performed rituals to ward off the lurking cacodaemon each winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demon - an evil supernatural being or devil
  • Antonym: angel - a benevolent or good spiritual being

Etymology

From Greek kakodaimōn, from kakos (bad) + daimōn (spirit).
