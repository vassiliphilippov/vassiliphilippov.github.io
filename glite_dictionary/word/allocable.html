allocable

1. ADJ

/ ˈæləkəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈaləkəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of a cost, resource, etc.) able to be distributed or assigned to a specific purpose or category.
able to be distributed or assigned

Examples

The company assessed which expenses were allocable to specific projects.
Only allocable costs will be reimbursed under this contract.
Funds are not allocable to activities outside the approved scope.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distributable - able to be distributed

Etymology

From Latin 'allocare' (to assign) and suffix '-able'.
