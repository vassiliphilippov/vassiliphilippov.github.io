stockinette

1. NOUN

/ ˌstɑk.ɪˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˌstɒk.ɪˈnɛt / (BrE)
A soft, stretchy knit fabric, commonly used for infants' wear and undergarments.
soft, stretchy knit fabric

Examples

The baby's blanket was made from stockinette for extra softness.
Under her dress, she wore a stockinette slip to stay comfortable all day.
Stockinette is often chosen for delicate clothing due to its gentle texture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jersey - a type of soft knitted fabric similar to stockinette

Etymology

From the French *stockinette*, diminutive of *stocking*.
