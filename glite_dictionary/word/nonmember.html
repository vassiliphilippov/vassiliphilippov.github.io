nonmember

1. NOUN

/ nɑnˈmɛmbɚ / (AmE), / nɒnˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
a person who is not a member of a particular group, club, or organization
a person who is not a member

Examples

Admission prices are higher for nonmembers than for club members.
This event is open to both members and nonmembers.
As a nonmember, you will need to register at the front desk.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: member - a person who belongs to a group, club, or organization

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'non-' (meaning 'not') to 'member'.
