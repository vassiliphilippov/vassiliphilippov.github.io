crack-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈkræk ʌp / (AmE), / ˈkræk ʌp / (BrE)
a sudden collapse in mental or physical health; a nervous or emotional breakdown
mental or physical breakdown

Examples

After months of stress at work, she had a complete crack-up and needed time off to recover.
Many believed the pressure of fame contributed to his crack-up last year.
His friends tried to support him as he went through a severe crack-up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breakdown - a collapse of physical or mental health
