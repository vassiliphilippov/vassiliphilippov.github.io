gloweringly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡlaʊ.ɚ.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈɡlaʊ.ər.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a manner that shows anger or sullen disapproval; with a glowering expression or attitude.
in an angrily sullen manner

Examples

The manager looked gloweringly at the employee who arrived late again.
She sat gloweringly in the corner after losing the argument.
He glanced gloweringly across the table, making everyone uncomfortable.

Synset Examples

he stared gloweringly at this morning's headlines

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glower - to look at someone or something in an angry or sullen way
