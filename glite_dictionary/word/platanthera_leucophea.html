Platanthera leucophea

1. NOUN

/ pləˈtænθərə luˈkoʊfiə / (AmE), / pləˈtanθərə luˈkəʊfiə / (BrE)
A rare and threatened orchid species native to central North America.
a threatened North American orchid species

Examples

Conservationists are working to protect the habitats of Platanthera leucophea in central North America.
The wet prairies where Platanthera leucophea thrives are disappearing due to development.
Botanists were excited to find a small population of Platanthera leucophea during their field survey.

Etymology

From the genus 'Platanthera' (Greek 'platys' meaning 'broad' + 'anthera' meaning 'anther') and species name 'leucophea' (Greek 'leukos' meaning 'white').
