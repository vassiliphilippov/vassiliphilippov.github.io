Malaysia

1. PROPN

/ məˈleɪʒə / (AmE), / məˈleɪziə / (BrE)
a country in Southeast Asia comprising Peninsular Malaysia and part of the island of Borneo; a constitutional monarchy that gained independence from the United Kingdom in 1957
country in Southeast Asia

Examples

After visiting Malaysia, she wrote a book about its diverse culture and beautiful landscapes.
Many students from Malaysia choose to study abroad for higher education.
The annual rainfall in Malaysia supports its lush rainforests and vibrant wildlife.

Etymology

From 'Malay' (ethnic group, language) + '-sia', modeled on names like 'Indonesia'.
