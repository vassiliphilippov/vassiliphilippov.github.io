racker

1. NOUN

/ ˈrækər / (AmE), / ˈrækə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to arrange pool or billiard balls into a rack before a game
person who racks billiard balls

Examples

The racker set up the balls quickly before each game started.
As a racker, his job was to ensure the table was always ready for players.
The tournament hired an experienced racker to keep the matches running smoothly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rack - to arrange (balls) in a triangle at the start of a game of billiards or pool
