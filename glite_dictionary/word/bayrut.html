Bayrut

1. PROPN

/ beɪˈruːt / (AmE), / beɪˈruːt / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Lebanon, located on the Mediterranean coast in western Lebanon
capital city of Lebanon

Examples

The city of Bayrut is known for its vibrant culture and history.
After the conference in Bayrut, she traveled to the Bekaa Valley.
Many tourists visit Bayrut to enjoy its Mediterranean cuisine and nightlife.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Beirut - the capital city of Lebanon; also spelled 'Bayrut'

Etymology

Variant transliteration of the Arabic بيروت (Bayrūt).
