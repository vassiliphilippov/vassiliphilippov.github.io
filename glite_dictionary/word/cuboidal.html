cuboidal

1. ADJ

/ kjuːˈbɔɪ.dəl / (AmE), / kjuːˈbɔɪ.dəl / (BrE)
having the shape of a cube or resembling a cube in form
shaped like a cube

Examples

The architect designed a cuboidal building to maximize space efficiency.
Under the microscope, the cuboidal cells could be easily distinguished from the flat ones.
A cuboidal block of ice was placed at the center of the table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cubical - having the shape of a cube

Etymology

From Latin 'cubus' (cube) + suffix '-oid' (like, resembling) + '-al' (forming adjectives).
