ferryman

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛriˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈfɛrɪmən / (BrE)
a man whose job is to operate a ferry, especially one who transports people across a river or other body of water
man who operates a ferry

Examples

The ferryman rowed us safely across the wide river.
According to the legend, a ferryman waits to carry souls across to the other side.
Each day, the ferryman greeted villagers as they boarded his boat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boatman - man who works with or operates boats, especially as a ferryman or boat operator

Etymology

From 'ferry' + 'man', referring to a person who operates a ferry.
