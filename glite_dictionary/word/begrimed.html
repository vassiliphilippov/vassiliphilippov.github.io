begrimed

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈɡraɪmd / (AmE), / bɪˈɡraɪmd / (BrE)
thickly covered with ingrained dirt or soot; very dirty or grimy
covered with ingrained dirt or soot

Examples

After hours in the coal mine, his begrimed hands were nearly black.
The old manuscripts were begrimed and fragile from centuries of neglect.
She wiped her begrimed face with a clean cloth after working in the garden.

Synset Examples

a miner's begrimed face
dingy linen
grimy hands
grubby little fingers
a grungy kitchen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grimy - covered with or characterized by grime
  • Degree Higher: filthy - extremely dirty

Etymology

From be- (an intensifying prefix) + Middle English grym (dirt, soot)
