inodorous

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈnoʊdərəs / (AmE), / ɪˈnəʊdərəs / (BrE)
having no smell; odorless
having no smell; odorless

Examples

Helium is an inodorous and tasteless gas.
The laboratory used an inodorous solvent to avoid interference with the experiment.
Some varieties of lilies are nearly inodorous despite their showy appearance.

Synset Examples

odorless gas
odorless flowers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: odorless - having no odor
  • Antonym: fragrant - having a pleasant smell

Etymology

From Latin 'in-' (not) + 'odorus' (having a smell).
