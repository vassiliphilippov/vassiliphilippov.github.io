undried

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdraɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdraɪd / (BrE)
not dried; still wet or moist.
not dried, still wet

Examples

The undried paint left stains on my shirt when I brushed against the wall.
Please make sure the clay is completely undried before trying to reshape it.
They harvested the undried herbs from the garden early in the morning.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dried - having had all moisture removed
  • Synonym: moist - slightly wet; damp

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'dried'.
