surgically

1. ADV

/ ˈsɝːdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˈsɜːdʒɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to, or by means of surgery.
by means of surgery

Examples

The tumor was surgically removed after it was discovered.
Her appendix was surgically extracted during the emergency operation.
The vet was able to surgically fix the dog’s injured leg.

Synset Examples

surgically removed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surgical - relating to surgery

Etymology

From 'surgical' + '-ly', with 'surgical' ultimately from Greek 'cheirourgikos' (working by hand).
