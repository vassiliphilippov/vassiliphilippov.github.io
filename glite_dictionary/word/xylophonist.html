xylophonist

1. NOUN

/ zaɪˈlɑːfəˌnɪst / (AmE), / zaɪˈlɒfənɪst / (BrE)
A person who plays the xylophone, especially as a performer or professional musician.
person who plays the xylophone

Examples

The talented xylophonist captivated the audience with a lively solo during the concert.
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a renowned xylophonist and performing around the world.
The jazz band featured a skilled xylophonist who added a unique sound to their music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musician - a person who plays a musical instrument, especially professionally

Etymology

Formed from 'xylophone' and the suffix '-ist'. 'Xylophone' from Greek 'xylon' (wood) + 'phone' (sound).
