attentively

1. ADV

/ əˈtɛn.tɪv.li / (AmE), / əˈtɛn.tɪv.li / (BrE)
In a manner that shows careful attention or concentration.
in an attentive or concentrated manner

Examples

She listened attentively to the teacher's instructions before starting the assignment.
The audience watched attentively as the magician performed his tricks.
He read the contract attentively, making sure he understood every clause.

Synset Examples

he listened attentively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carefully - in a careful or cautious manner
  • Antonym: inattentively - in a manner lacking attention

Etymology

From 'attentive' + '-ly'. 'Attentive' from Latin 'attentus' (attentive, heedful), from 'attendere' (to attend).
