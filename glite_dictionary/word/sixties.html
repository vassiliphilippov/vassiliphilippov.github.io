sixties

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪkstiz / (AmE), / ˈsɪkstiz / (BrE)
the decade from 1960 to 1969
the years 1960–1969

Examples

The music of the sixties continues to influence artists today.
Many social changes took place during the sixties in America.
She collects fashion magazines from the sixties for her vintage shop.

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɪk.stiz / (AmE), / ˈsɪk.stiːz / (BrE)
the period in a person's life between the ages of 60 and 70
the years of age from 60 to 70

Examples

Many people choose to retire in their sixties and pursue hobbies they never had time for before.
She began painting seriously in her sixties, proving it's never too late to start something new.
Regular exercise can help you stay healthy throughout your sixties and beyond.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: seventies - the period in a person's life between 70 and 80
  • Degree Lower: fifties - the period in a person's life between 50 and 60
