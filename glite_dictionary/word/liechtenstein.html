Liechtenstein

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪktənˌstaɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɪxtənʃtaɪn / (BrE)
a small landlocked country in central Europe, situated in the Alps between Austria and Switzerland; a constitutional monarchy known for its banking sector and picturesque landscapes.
small European country between Austria and Switzerland

Examples

She spent her summer vacation hiking in Liechtenstein's beautiful mountains.
The prince of Liechtenstein is known for his interest in art and culture.
Many tourists visit Liechtenstein to explore its medieval castles and alpine scenery.

Etymology

From the name of the ruling dynasty, the House of Liechtenstein, originating from Liechtenstein Castle in Lower Austria.
