agama

1. NOUN

/ əˈɡɑmə / (AmE), / əˈɡɑːmə / (BrE)
a small terrestrial lizard native to warm regions of the Old World, especially Africa and Asia, belonging to the family Agamidae
a type of Old World lizard

Examples

The brightly colored agama basked on a sunlit rock in the midday heat.
Researchers discovered a new species of agama in the savannahs of Central Africa.
Many agamas can rapidly change the color of their skin for camouflage or display.

Etymology

From Sranan Tongo and related West African languages (e.g., Yoruba: agáma).
