bunter

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌn.tə / (BrE)
A baseball player who hits the ball lightly without swinging, attempting to place it in play by bunting.
a batter who bunts in baseball

Examples

The skilled bunter helped his team by advancing the runner to second base.
As a bunter, he specialized in sacrificing himself for the good of the team.
The coach praised her for becoming the best bunter in the league.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bunt - to hit a baseball lightly without swinging, usually to advance a runner
