nympho

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪmfoʊ / (AmE), / ˈnɪmfəʊ / (BrE)
(slang) A woman who is considered to have excessive or abnormal sexual desires; short for nymphomaniac.
woman with excessive sexual desires (slang)
slang

Examples

Some people unfairly label women as nympho just for being open about their sexuality.
The film portrayed the female lead as a stereotypical nympho, which drew criticism.
She was tired of being called a nympho for expressing her desires.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nymphomaniac - a woman with uncontrollable or excessive sexual desire; original, clinical term

Etymology

Shortened from 'nymphomaniac,' from Greek nymphē (bride, nymph) + mania (madness).
