Aflaxen

1. NOUN

/ əˈflæksən / (AmE), / əˈflæksən / (BrE)
A trademarked brand of naproxen, a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used to reduce pain and inflammation.
brand of pain and inflammation medication (naproxen)

Examples

My doctor recommended Aflaxen for my back pain after I complained about stiffness and discomfort.
If you are allergic to ibuprofen, Aflaxen might be a suitable alternative for managing inflammation.
Always consult your physician before taking Aflaxen, especially if you are on other medications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naproxen - a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) for pain and inflammation
