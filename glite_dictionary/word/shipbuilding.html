shipbuilding

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪpˌbɪl.dɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpˌbɪl.dɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or industry of designing and constructing ships
the construction of ships

Examples

The town’s economy is heavily reliant on shipbuilding and fishing.
Advances in technology have transformed traditional shipbuilding methods.
He began his career in shipbuilding after graduating from engineering school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shipyard - a place where ships are built and repaired
  • Antonym: shipbreaker - one who dismantles and breaks up ships for scrap

Etymology

From 'ship' + 'building', referring to the construction of ships.
