headmistressship

1. NOUN

/ hɛdˈmɪstrəsˌʃɪp / (AmE), / hɛdˈmɪstrɪsˌʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or office of being a headmistress in a school
position of headmistress

Examples

Her appointment to the headmistressship was greeted with enthusiasm by the faculty.
She resigned from the headmistressship after a decade of dedicated service.
The headmistressship of such a prestigious school comes with many responsibilities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headmistress - a woman who is the head teacher of a school

Etymology

From 'headmistress' + '-ship' (suffix denoting a state or office).
