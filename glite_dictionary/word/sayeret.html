sayeret

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈɛrɛt / (AmE), / saɪˈɛrɛt / (BrE)
an elite Israeli military commando unit, typically specializing in counterterrorism, intelligence gathering, and hostage rescue missions
elite Israeli commando unit

Examples

The Israeli military deployed a sayeret to gather intelligence behind enemy lines.
Only the best soldiers are selected for the prestigious sayeret units.
During the operation, the sayeret rescued several hostages without being detected.

Etymology

Hebrew סַיֶּרֶת (sayeret), meaning 'patrol' or 'reconnaissance unit'.
