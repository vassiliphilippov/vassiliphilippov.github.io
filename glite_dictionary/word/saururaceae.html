Saururaceae

1. NOUN

/ sɔːˌrjʊˈreɪsiː / (AmE), / sɔːˌjʊəˈreɪsiː / (BrE)
A family of perennial aromatic herbs, including the genera Saururus, Anemopsis, and Houttuynia.
family of aromatic perennial herbs

Examples

The plant Houttuynia cordata belongs to the Saururaceae family.
Botanists often study the aromatic properties of Saururaceae members in different environments.
The distribution of Saururaceae spans temperate and tropical regions.

Etymology

From New Latin, based on genus Saururus plus the standard botanical suffix -aceae (family).
