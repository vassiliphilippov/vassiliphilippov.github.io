militarily

1. ADV

/ ˌmɪlɪˈtɛrəli / (AmE), / ˌmɪlɪˈtærɪli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to the armed forces or military affairs.
in a way related to the military

Examples

The country is not militarily prepared to face such a large-scale attack.
He argued that intervening militarily would have serious consequences for the region.
The region remains strategically and militarily important.

Synset Examples

on a militarily significant scale

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military - relating to the armed forces

Etymology

From 'military' + -ly, from Latin 'militarius' (of soldiers).
