Pidlimdi

1. NOUN

/ pɪdˈlɪmdi / (AmE), / pɪdˈlɪmdi / (BrE)
A three-tone Chadic language spoken in parts of Nigeria.
Chadic language of Nigeria

Examples

Linguists have documented the tonal system of Pidlimdi in recent field studies.
The community is working to preserve Pidlimdi among younger generations.
Several folk tales are still told in the Pidlimdi language.
