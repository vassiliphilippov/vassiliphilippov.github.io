Baluchi

1. NOUN

/ bəˈluːtʃi / (AmE), / bəˈluːtʃi / (BrE)
an Iranian language spoken in Pakistan, Iran, Afghanistan, Russia, and around the Persian Gulf
Iranian language of Pakistan, Iran, and region

Examples

She is fluent in both Urdu and Baluchi.
The Baluchi language has several distinct dialects.
Many traditional poems are preserved in Baluchi.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Balochi - alternate spelling of Baluchi, Iranian language spoken in the same regions

Etymology

From Persian 'Balūch', referring to the Baluch people, with '-i' denoting the language.
