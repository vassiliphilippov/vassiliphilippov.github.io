sculpturesque

1. ADJ

/ ˌskʌlp.tʃərˈɛsk / (AmE), / ˌskʌlptʃəˈrɛsk / (BrE)
having the form or appearance of sculpture; resembling a sculpture, especially in being graceful or well-formed.
resembling sculpture in form

Examples

The dancer struck a sculpturesque pose that captivated the entire audience.
Her sculpturesque profile was highlighted in the evening light.
The artist admired the sculpturesque quality of the marble figures.

Synset Examples

her finely modeled features
rendered with … vivid sculptural effect
the sculpturesque beauty of the athletes' bodies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sculptural - relating to or resembling sculpture

Etymology

From 'sculpture' + '-esque', the latter from French, meaning 'in the style of'.
