foot

1. NOUN

/ fʊt / (AmE), / fʊt / (BrE)
the act or manner of traveling by walking rather than using a vehicle or animal

Examples

She is swift of foot and often wins the race to school.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on foot - by walking rather than using a vehicle

From Old English 'fōt', from Proto-Germanic '*fōts', related to the Latin 'pes'.
