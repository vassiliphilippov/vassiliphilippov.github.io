beechnut

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːtʃˌnʌt / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃ.nʌt / (BrE)
the small, sweet, triangular nut produced by a beech tree
nut from a beech tree

Examples

The forest floor was covered with fallen beechnuts after the autumn winds.
Squirrels often gather and store beechnuts for food during the winter months.
Children tasted the beechnut and were surprised by its sweet flavor.

Etymology

From 'beech', the tree species, and 'nut'.
