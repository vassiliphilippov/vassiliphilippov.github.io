supremacism

1. NOUN

/ səˈprɛməsɪzəm / (AmE), / sjuːˈprɛməsɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
the belief that a particular group, race, or nation is inherently superior to others and should dominate or control them
belief in superiority of a group or race

Examples

The rise of supremacism in certain online forums has caused widespread concern.
Many laws were enacted to combat racial supremacism and promote equality.
He rejected both nationalism and supremacism in his political views.

Synset Examples

white supremacism

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racism - belief that races have distinct characteristics and some are superior to others
  • Antonym: egalitarianism - belief in the equality of all people

Etymology

From 'supremacy' + '-ism', modeled after similar formations like 'racism'.
