AFSPC

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪ ˌɛf ˌɛs ˌpiː ˈsiː / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˌɛf ˌɛs ˌpiː ˈsiː / (BrE)
a former command of the United States Air Force responsible for space operations and intercontinental ballistic missile operations
U.S. Air Force space and missile operations command (now inactive)

Examples

The AFSPC played a critical role in U.S. space defense for decades.
After its reorganization, many of AFSPC's responsibilities were transferred to the Space Force.
Personnel from AFSPC have contributed to major satellite launches and missile detection systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Air Force Space Command - the full form of AFSPC, a command of the United States Air Force responsible for space operations

Etymology

Acronym of Air Force Space Command.
