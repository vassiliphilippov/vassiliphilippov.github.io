lepidopterologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɛpɪˌdɑptəˈrɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌlɛpɪˌdɒptəˈrɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies or collects butterflies and moths
butterfly and moth specialist

Examples

The lepidopterologist spent years observing rare butterfly species in the Amazon rainforest.
As a lepidopterologist, she carefully documented the lifecycle of local moths.
At the museum, a lepidopterologist gave a fascinating talk about butterfly migration.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: entomologist - a scientist who studies insects

Etymology

From Greek 'lepis, lepid-' (scale) + 'pteron' (wing) + '-logist' (one who studies).
