honeyed

1. ADJ

/ ˈhʌnid / (AmE), / ˈhʌnid / (BrE)
(of food or drink) containing or flavored with honey.
containing or flavored with honey

Examples

She served a honeyed tea after dinner.
The baker offered honeyed pastries at the market.
He enjoyed the honeyed flavor of the fresh bread.

Etymology

From 'honey' + '-ed', meaning endowed with or containing honey.

2. ADJ

/ ˈhʌnid / (AmE), / ˈhʌnɪd / (BrE)
(of sound or speech) pleasantly smooth and sweet to hear; pleasing to the ear.
smooth, pleasing sound

Examples

The singer's honeyed voice captivated everyone in the audience.
He spoke in honeyed tones that were impossible to resist.
The advertisement featured a honeyed jingle that stayed in my mind all day.

Synset Examples

the dulcet tones of the cello

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dulcet - (especially of sound) sweet and soothing
  • Antonym: harsh - unpleasantly rough or jarring to the senses

Etymology

From 'honey' + '-ed', meaning sweet as honey.
