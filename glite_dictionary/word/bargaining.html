bargaining

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑrɡənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːɡənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of discussing and reaching agreement over the terms of a transaction or deal, especially to obtain a better price or conditions
negotiation over terms or prices

Examples

After hours of bargaining, they finally agreed on a price.
Collective bargaining has helped improve working conditions for many employees.
His skills in bargaining are well known in the business community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: negotiation - discussion aimed at reaching an agreement

Etymology

From 'bargain' (Middle English, from Old French, from bargaignier 'to haggle, exchange').
