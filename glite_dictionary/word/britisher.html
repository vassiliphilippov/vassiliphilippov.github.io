Britisher

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪt̬.ɪ.ʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈbrɪt.ɪ.ʃə / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Great Britain
person from Great Britain

Examples

The Britisher traveling abroad is often recognized by his accent and manners.
Many Americans have misconceptions about what it means to be a Britisher.
As a proud Britisher, she cherished her heritage and traditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Briton - a native or inhabitant of Britain
  • Synonym: Brit - informal term for a British person

Etymology

Formed by combining 'British' with the suffix '-er', modeled after words like 'villager' or 'New Zealander'.
