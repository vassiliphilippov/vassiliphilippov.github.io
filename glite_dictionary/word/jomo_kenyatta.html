Jomo Kenyatta

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒoʊmoʊ kɛnˈjɑːtə / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊməʊ kɛnˈjætə / (BrE)
(1893–1978) The first President of independent Kenya, a Kenyan statesman and key figure in the country's struggle for independence.
Kenya's first president (1893–1978)

Examples

Jomo Kenyatta is remembered as the founding father of modern Kenya.
A statue of Jomo Kenyatta stands proudly in central Nairobi.
During Jomo Kenyatta's leadership, Kenya achieved independence from British rule.
