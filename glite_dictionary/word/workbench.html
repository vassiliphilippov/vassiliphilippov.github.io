workbench

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɜrkˌbɛntʃ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌbɛntʃ / (BrE)
A sturdy table with a flat surface used by carpenters, mechanics, or other workers for making or repairing things.
sturdy table for working on projects

Examples

He spent hours at his workbench building model airplanes in the garage.
The old workbench was covered with scattered tools and sawdust.
She carefully repaired the broken watch on her grandfather’s workbench.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worktable - table used for working on various tasks

Etymology

From 'work' + 'bench', meaning a bench used for working.
