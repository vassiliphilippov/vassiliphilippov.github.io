cuck

1. NOUN

/ kʌk / (AmE), / kʌk / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) A man who is regarded as weak or inadequate, especially in relation to his partner's dominance or infidelity.
slang insult for a weak or submissive man
slang offensive

Examples

Many internet trolls tried to insult him by calling him a cuck during the debate.
He was repeatedly branded a cuck in online forums for his perceived lack of assertiveness.
The word cuck has become a controversial term in some circles due to its offensive use.

Synset Examples

He called him a cuck for allowing her to control everything.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cuckold - a man whose wife is unfaithful
  • Antonym: alpha male - a dominant or assertive male

Etymology

From 'cuckold', originating from Old French 'cocu', meaning cuckoo (a bird known for laying its eggs in other birds' nests).
