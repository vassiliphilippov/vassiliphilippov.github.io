sneezer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsniːzər / (AmE), / ˈsniːzə / (BrE)
a person who sneezes
person who sneezes

Examples

During allergy season, every sneezer in the office became a topic of conversation.
As a chronic sneezer, he always carried tissues wherever he went.
The sudden bright light turned her into an unstoppable sneezer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sneeze - to expel air from the nose and mouth involuntarily
