jurywoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɝːiˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈdʒʊəriˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who serves as a member of a jury
female member of a jury

Examples

The jurywoman listened attentively as the evidence was presented.
As the first jurywoman in her county, she felt proud to serve.
The jurywoman asked for clarification about a point of law during deliberations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: juror - a member of a jury
  • Synonym: juryman - a man who serves as a member of a jury

Etymology

Formed from 'jury' and 'woman', by analogy with 'juryman'.
