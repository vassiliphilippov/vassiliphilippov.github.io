dark-gray

1. ADJ

/ dɑrk ˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / dɑːk ˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
of a dark shade of grey; deeper and less reflective than standard gray.
of a dark shade of grey

Examples

He wore a dark-gray suit to the business meeting.
The dark-gray clouds signaled an approaching storm.
Her new car is a sleek dark-gray sedan.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gray - of a color between black and white; the base neutral shade
  • Degree Higher: black - the darkest color, resulting from absence of light
