Vespertilio

1. NOUN

/ vɛspərˈtɪlioʊ / (AmE), / vɛspəˈtɪlɪəʊ / (BrE)
A genus of bats in the family Vespertilionidae, including the parti-coloured bat and Asian parti-coloured bat.
genus of bats in Vespertilionidae

Examples

The genus Vespertilio includes several species of widespread bats.
Researchers discovered a new species within the genus Vespertilio in Asia.
The parti-coloured bat is classified scientifically under Vespertilio.

Etymology

From Latin 'vespertilio', meaning 'bat'.

2. NOUN

/ vɛspərˈtɪlioʊ / (AmE), / vɛspəˈtɪliəʊ / (BrE)
A genus of bats in the family Vespertilionidae, including the parti-coloured bat and Asian parti-coloured bat.
genus of bats in Vespertilionidae

Examples

The genus Vespertilio includes the parti-coloured bat found in Europe and Asia.
Researchers identified a new species within the Vespertilio genus during their field survey.
The flight habits of bats in the Vespertilio genus are adapted to open habitats near water.

Etymology

From Latin vespertilio (bat), from vesper (evening).
