septuple

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛp.tə.pəl / (AmE), / ˈsɛp.tjʊ.pəl / (BrE)
consisting of seven parts, units, or components; being seven times as much or as many.
having seven parts or units

Examples

The researchers studied the septuple star system discovered in the distant galaxy.
The committee approved a septuple division of the funds to distribute them equally among seven projects.
The box contained a septuple set of keys, each one labeled for a different purpose.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: quadruple - consisting of four parts or units
  • Degree Lower: quintuple - consisting of five parts or units
  • Degree Lower: sextuple - consisting of six parts or units
  • Degree Higher: octuple - consisting of eight parts or units
  • Degree Higher: nonuple - consisting of nine parts or units

Etymology

From Latin 'septuplus', from 'septem' (seven) + '-plus' (fold, times).
