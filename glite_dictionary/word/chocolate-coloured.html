chocolate-coloured

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɑklət ˌkʌlərd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having the dark brown color of chocolate
dark brown, like chocolate

Examples

She wore a beautiful chocolate-coloured dress to the party.
The horse's chocolate-coloured mane gleamed in the sunlight.
He chose a chocolate-coloured paint for the living room walls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brown - having a color like that of chocolate or coffee
