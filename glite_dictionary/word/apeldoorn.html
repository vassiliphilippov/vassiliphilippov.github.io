Apeldoorn

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑːpəlˌdɔrn / (AmE), / ˈaːpəldɔːn / (BrE)
a city in east central Netherlands, known as a tourist centre and the site of the Dutch royal family's summer residence
city in east central Netherlands

Examples
