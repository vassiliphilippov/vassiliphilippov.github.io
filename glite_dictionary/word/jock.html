jock

1. NOUN

/ dʒɑk / (AmE), / dʒɒk / (BrE)
A piece of supportive underwear for men, designed to protect the genitals during vigorous physical activity.
supportive male athletic underwear

Examples

He always wears a jock under his football shorts for extra protection.
Make sure your jock fits snugly before you start training.
Wearing a jock can help prevent injuries during contact sports.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jockstrap - an elasticized support for the genitals worn during sports

Etymology

Shortened from ‘jockstrap’, which originated in late 19th-century American English.

2. NOUN

/ dʒɑk / (AmE), / dʒɒk / (BrE)
an athlete, especially one who is enthusiastic about sports or regularly competes in sports, often used informally for male athletes in schools or colleges
athlete, especially in school or college
informal

Examples

He was a typical jock in high school, playing football and basketball every season.
The jocks and the chess club rarely mixed at our school.
Although he's known as a jock, he excels in his studies too.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: athlete - a person who is proficient in sports and other forms of physical exercise

Etymology

Originally Scottish nickname, short for 'John'; later generalized in American English to mean 'athlete'.
