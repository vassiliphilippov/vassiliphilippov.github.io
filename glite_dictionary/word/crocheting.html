crocheting

1. NOUN

/ kroʊˈʃeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / krəʊˈʃeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
The craft of making fabric by interlocking looped stitches of yarn, thread, or other material with a hooked needle.
making fabric with a hooked needle

Examples

She spends her evenings crocheting intricate patterns for scarves and hats.
The art of crocheting has been passed down through generations in her family.
He picked up crocheting as a relaxing hobby during the winter months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knitting - making fabric by interlocking loops of yarn with needles

Etymology

From French 'crocheter', from 'crochet' (small hook).

2. NOUN

/ kroʊˈʃeɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / krəʊˈʃeɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
The craft or process of making fabric by interlocking loops of yarn, thread, or other material using a crochet hook.
making fabric with a crochet hook

Examples

She spent the afternoon crocheting a blanket for her new niece.
Many people find crocheting relaxing and rewarding.
I learned crocheting from my grandmother when I was a child.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knitting - making fabric by interlocking loops of yarn with needles

Etymology

From 'crochet', from French 'crochet' meaning 'small hook'.
