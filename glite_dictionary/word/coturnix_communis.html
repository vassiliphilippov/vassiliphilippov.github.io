Coturnix communis

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈtɜrnɪks kəˈmjuːnɪs / (AmE), / kəʊˈtɜːnɪks kəˈmjuːnɪs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the common quail, a small migratory game bird native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.
scientific name for the common quail

Examples

The migratory pattern of the Coturnix communis extends from Europe to northern Africa each year.
Wild populations of Coturnix communis are declining due to intensive farming.
The Coturnix communis is prized as a game bird in many countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: common quail - A small migratory Old World game bird

Etymology

From Latin: 'Coturnix' meaning quail; 'communis' meaning common.
