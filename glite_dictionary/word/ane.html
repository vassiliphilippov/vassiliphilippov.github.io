ane

1. NUM

/ eɪn / (AmE), / eɪn / (BrE)
(Scottish) one; a single unit or thing.
Scottish for 'one' (number)
british

Examples

He bought ane apple from the market.
There was only ane person in the room when I arrived.
She handed me ane shilling as payment.

Synset Examples

‘ane’ is Scottish

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one - the number one; a single unit

Etymology

From Middle English 'an', Scots variant of 'one'.
