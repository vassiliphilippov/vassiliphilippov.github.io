obtainment

1. NOUN

/ əbˈteɪnmənt / (AmE), / əbˈteɪnmənt / (BrE)
the act of obtaining or gaining something
the act of obtaining

Examples

The obtainment of a visa can be a complex process in some countries.
Success depended on the obtainment of accurate information from multiple sources.
He celebrated the obtainment of his long-awaited degree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acquisition - the act of acquiring something

Etymology

From obtain + -ment, following the pattern of other nouns of action.
