narcotraffic

1. NOUN

/ nɑːrkoʊˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / nɑːkəʊˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
the illegal trade or smuggling of narcotic drugs
illegal trade in narcotic drugs

Examples

International efforts are underway to combat narcotraffic along major smuggling routes.
The authorities arrested several individuals involved in narcotraffic across the border.
Tackling narcotraffic requires cooperation between law enforcement agencies worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drug trafficking - the activity of trading or smuggling illegal drugs

Etymology

From Spanish 'narcotráfico', from 'narco-' (narcotics) + 'tráfico' (traffic, trade).
