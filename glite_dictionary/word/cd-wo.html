CD-WO

1. NOUN

/ si di ˈdʌbəlju oʊ / (AmE), / siː diː ˈdʌbljuː əʊ / (BrE)
A compact disc that can be written to only once and then acts as read-only memory.
write-once compact disc

Examples

She saved her important photos on a CD-WO to ensure they couldn't be accidentally erased.
Unlike a rewritable disc, a CD-WO allows data to be written only once.
Many early software distributions were shipped on CD-WO media.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: CD-R - Compact disc that can be written to once; synonym for CD-WO
  • Degree Lower: CD-ROM - Compact disc that is read-only and cannot be written to

Etymology

Abbreviation of Compact Disc Write Once.
