periodontia

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɪri.oʊˈdɑn.tʃə / (AmE), / ˌpɪəri.əˈdɒn.ti.ə / (BrE)
the branch of dentistry concerned with diseases and conditions affecting the gums and supporting structures of the teeth
dentistry branch for gums/teeth support
medical

Examples

She decided to specialize in periodontia after developing an interest in gum diseases during her dental studies.
Advancements in periodontia have greatly improved treatments for periodontal disease.
The conference featured several lectures on the latest research in periodontia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: periodontology - the study of the supporting structures of teeth and diseases that affect them
  • Degree Higher: dentistry - the medical field concerned with teeth and oral health

Etymology

From Greek 'peri' (around) + 'odous, odont-' (tooth).
