Platypoecilus maculatus

1. NOUN

/ ˌplætɪpoʊˈsaɪləs ˌmækjəˈleɪtəs / (AmE), / ˌplatɪpəʊˈsiːləs ˌmakjʊˈleɪtəs / (BrE)
a small, stocky freshwater fish native to Mexico, popular in home aquariums
a small Mexican aquarium fish

Examples

Many aquarium enthusiasts keep Platypoecilus maculatus because of its vibrant colors and hardy nature.
Native to Mexico, Platypoecilus maculatus thrives in a well-maintained freshwater tank.
Some breeders have developed striking new color varieties of Platypoecilus maculatus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: platy - a small freshwater aquarium fish of the genus Xiphophorus, related to Platypoecilus maculatus

Etymology

From Latinized Greek 'platys' (broad) and 'poikilos' (varied), and Latin 'maculatus' (spotted).
