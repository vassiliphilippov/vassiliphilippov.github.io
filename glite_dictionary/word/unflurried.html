unflurried

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈflɝːid / (AmE), / ʌnˈflʌrɪd / (BrE)
calm and not showing signs of emotional agitation or nervous tension
calm; not agitated

Examples

Despite the unexpected interruption, the speaker remained unflurried and answered every question calmly.
The unflurried doctor reassured his patient during the emergency.
She handled the busy day's challenges in an unflurried and efficient manner.

Synset Examples

the waiters were unflurried and good natured
with contented mind and unruffled spirit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unruffled - calm and not emotionally disturbed
  • Antonym: flurried - nervous or agitated

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'flurried' (agitated or confused).
