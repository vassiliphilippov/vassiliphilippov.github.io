bandleader

1. NOUN

/ ˈbændˌliːdər / (AmE), / ˈbændˌliːdə / (BrE)
the leader of a musical band, especially one who directs a dance band or orchestra
leader of a musical or dance band

Examples

The bandleader signaled the musicians to start playing a lively swing tune.
During the concert, the bandleader introduced each member of the orchestra to the audience.
Famous jazz bandleaders like Duke Ellington shaped the sound of an era.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conductor - a person who directs the performance of an orchestra or choir
  • Synonym: musician - a person who plays a musical instrument or is musically talented

Etymology

Formed from 'band' + 'leader', early 20th century.
