Ebro

1. PROPN

/ ˈiːbroʊ / (AmE), / ˈiːbrəʊ / (BrE)
A river in northeastern Spain, flowing into the Mediterranean Sea.
river in northeastern Spain

Examples

The Ebro is the second longest river in the Iberian Peninsula after the Tagus.
Hundreds of bird species can be found along the Ebro delta.
The city of Zaragoza is located on the banks of the Ebro river.
