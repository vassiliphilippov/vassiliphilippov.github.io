to attain

1. VERB

/ əˈteɪn / (AmE), / əˈteɪn / (BrE)
to succeed in reaching a particular level, age, or state, often after effort
reach a particular state or level

Examples

It may take years for the athlete to attain peak performance.
She attained the age of 18 last month, making her eligible to vote.
The tree had finally attained a height of twenty meters.

Synset Examples

The thermometer hit 100 degrees
This car can reach a speed of 140 miles per hour

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reach - to arrive at a particular state or point
  • Synonym: to achieve - to successfully reach a desired goal or level

Etymology

From Old French *atteindre*, from Latin *attingere* (to touch upon, reach).

2. VERB

/ əˈteɪn / (AmE), / əˈteɪn / (BrE)
to succeed in reaching a particular place or level, either physically or in an abstract sense
to reach a place or goal

Examples

After years of hard work, she finally attained her goal of becoming a doctor.
The hikers attained the summit just before sunset.
His speech was moving, as he described how he attained success against all odds.

Synset Examples

We hit Detroit by noon
The water reached the doorstep
We barely made it to the finish line
I have to hit the MAC machine before the weekend starts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reach - to arrive at a place or achieve a goal
  • Synonym: to achieve - to successfully bring about or reach a desired objective

Etymology

From Old French 'ataindre', from Latin 'attingere' (to touch upon, to reach).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02024442-v
Synset POS: v

3. VERB

/ əˈteɪn / (AmE), / əˈteɪn / (BrE)
to succeed in reaching or achieving something after effort
to achieve or reach with effort

Examples

After years of training, she finally attained the level of expertise required for the position.
It is difficult to attain perfection, but striving for it can lead to great improvement.
He attained his dream of becoming a published author through dedication and hard work.

Synset Examples

she achieved her goal despite setbacks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to achieve - to successfully bring about or reach a desired objective or result
  • Synonym: to accomplish - to succeed in doing or completing something

Etymology

From Old French 'ateindre', from Latin 'attingere' meaning 'to touch upon, reach'.
