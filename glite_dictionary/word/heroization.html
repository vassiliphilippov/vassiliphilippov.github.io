heroization

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɪroʊaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌhɪərəʊaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making someone into a hero, or treating them as a hero, often in a cultural or historical context
process of making someone a hero

Examples

The heroization of military leaders can significantly influence national identity.
His courageous actions during the crisis led to the heroization of his name in his hometown.
Recent debates question whether the heroization of tech founders is warranted by their actual contributions.

Synset Examples

Despite its great importance for Greek religious and cultural history, the heroization of historical persons in the Archaic and Classical periods has not received a full and systematic study.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deification - the act of treating someone as a god

Etymology

From hero + -ization, modeled after similar formations (e.g., realization).
