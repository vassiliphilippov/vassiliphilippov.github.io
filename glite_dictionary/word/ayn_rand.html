Ayn Rand

1. PROPN

/ aɪn rænd / (AmE), / aɪn rænd / (BrE)
(1905–1982) Russian-born American writer and philosopher, known for her novels and for developing the philosophical system of Objectivism.
Russian-American novelist and philosopher

Examples

Many of Ayn Rand's readers are drawn to her philosophy of individualism.
Ayn Rand moved to the United States in the 1920s and became a prominent writer.
Atlas Shrugged is considered Ayn Rand's magnum opus.
