sleepwear

1. NOUN

/ ˈsliːpˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈsliːpweə / (BrE)
clothes such as pajamas and nightgowns that are designed to be worn while sleeping.
clothing worn in bed

Examples

She always chooses soft cotton sleepwear to help her sleep comfortably.
The store has a wide selection of children's sleepwear, including both pajamas and nightgowns.
He packed his favorite sleepwear for the weekend trip to his grandparents' house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nightwear - clothing worn in bed
  • Synonym: pajamas - loose clothing worn for sleeping or lounging
  • Synonym: lingerie - women's underclothing and sleepwear

Etymology

From "sleep" + "wear", patterned after similar compounds like "outerwear" and "underwear".
