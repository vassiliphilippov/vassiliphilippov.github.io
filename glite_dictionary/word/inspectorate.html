inspectorate

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈspɛktərˌeɪt / (AmE), / ɪnˈspɛktərət / (BrE)
an official organization or body responsible for inspecting and supervising adherence to regulations or standards in a specific area
official body of inspectors

Examples

The food safety inspectorate conducted a thorough audit of all local processing plants.
A new national inspectorate was established to monitor environmental standards.
Schools are regularly visited by the education inspectorate to ensure quality is maintained.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspection - the act of examining or reviewing something carefully

Etymology

From 'inspector' + '-ate', denoting a collective body or office.
