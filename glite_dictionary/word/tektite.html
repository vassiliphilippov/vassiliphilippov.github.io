tektite

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛk.taɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɛk.taɪt / (BrE)
a small, glassy, rounded object formed from terrestrial debris ejected during meteorite impacts, thought to originate from molten material.
natural glass from meteorite impacts

Examples

The scientist analyzed a tektite found in Southeast Asia to learn more about ancient meteorite impacts.
Collectors often seek out rare tektites because of their unique shapes and origins.
A tektite can provide important clues about the Earth's geological history.

Etymology

From Greek tektos (melted), via German Tektit.
