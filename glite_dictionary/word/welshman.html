Welshman

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛlʃmən / (AmE), / ˈwɛlʃmən / (BrE)
a male native or inhabitant of Wales
male native of Wales

Examples

The Welshman spoke passionately about his homeland's traditions.
A young Welshman won the national poetry prize this year.
During their travels, they met a friendly Welshman who offered to show them around.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Welshwoman - a female native or resident of Wales

Etymology

From Welsh + man, denoting a male person from Wales.
