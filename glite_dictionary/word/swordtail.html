swordtail

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔrdˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɔːdteɪl / (BrE)
a small freshwater fish of Central America with a long, swordlike extension on the lower part of its tail fin, often kept in aquariums
tropical aquarium fish with swordlike tail

Examples

Many aquarium enthusiasts love the vibrant colors of the swordtail.
The male swordtail can be recognized by its distinctive tail extension.
We keep a few swordtails along with guppies in our fish tank.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: platy - a related freshwater fish often kept in aquariums
