glad

1. NOUN

/ ɡlæd / (AmE), / ɡlad / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Gladiolus, grown for its tall spikes of brightly colored, funnel-shaped flowers.
a garden plant with tall, colorful flowers

Examples

The glad in her garden bloomed earlier than usual this year.
He planted several varieties of glad along the fence for a burst of color.
Cut glads make elegant additions to floral arrangements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gladiolus - a plant with sword-shaped leaves and spiked flowers

Etymology

Shortened form of Gladiolus, from Latin 'gladius' meaning 'sword' (referring to the shape of the leaves).
