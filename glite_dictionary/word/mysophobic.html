mysophobic

1. ADJ

/ ˌmaɪsəˈfoʊbɪk / (AmE), / ˌmaɪsəˈfəʊbɪk / (BrE)
Abnormally afraid of dirt or contamination; suffering from the fear of germs or uncleanliness.
afraid of dirt or contamination
medical

Examples

Because he was so mysophobic, he washed his hands dozens of times a day.
Her mysophobic tendencies made her avoid shaking hands with anyone.
The mysophobic child refused to play in the sandbox at the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mysophobia - abnormal fear of dirt or of being contaminated

Etymology

From Greek 'mysos' (uncleanness) + '-phobic' (fear of).
