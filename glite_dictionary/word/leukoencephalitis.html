leukoencephalitis

1. NOUN

/ ˌluːkoʊˌɛnsəfəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌluːkəʊˌɛnˌsɛfəˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the white matter of the brain.
inflammation of brain white matter
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with leukoencephalitis after presenting neurological symptoms.
Recent studies have linked certain viral infections to cases of leukoencephalitis in adults.
Treatment for leukoencephalitis often includes corticosteroids to reduce inflammation.

Etymology

From Greek 'leukos' (white) + 'enkephalos' (brain) + '-itis' (inflammation).
