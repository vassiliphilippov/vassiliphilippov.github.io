nemophila

1. NOUN

/ nɪˈmɑːfɪlə / (AmE), / nɪˈmɒfɪlə / (BrE)
any plant of the genus Nemophila, comprising mostly annual herbs with delicate, bell-shaped flowers, native to western North America.
plant of the genus Nemophila

Examples

The delicate blue flowers of nemophila bloom each spring in the garden.
Many gardeners choose nemophila for its vibrant, sky-blue petals.
Nemophila grows well in cool, moist environments and is easy to cultivate.

Etymology

From Greek nemos (grove) + philos (loving).
