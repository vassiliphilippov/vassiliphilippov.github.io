Tulsa

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʌl.sə / (AmE), / ˈtʌl.sə / (BrE)
A major city in northeastern Oklahoma, United States, situated on the Arkansas River, historically known as the oil capital of the world.
city in northeastern Oklahoma, US

Examples

Thousands of people visit Tulsa each year for its vibrant music scene and festivals.
She relocated to Tulsa after getting a job with an energy company.
Many historic buildings in downtown Tulsa reflect its booming oil-era past.

Etymology

From Creek (Muscogee) word 'Tallasi', meaning 'old town'.
