report

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈpɔrt / (AmE), / rɪˈpɔːt / (BrE)
a written document that describes the findings or results of an investigation, study, or group effort
written account of findings or results

Examples

The researchers published a detailed report on their experiment in the journal.
Can you send me the report before the meeting tomorrow?
Her report highlighted several key areas needing improvement.

Synset Examples

this accords with the recent study by Hill and Dale

Related Terms

  • Synonym: study - a detailed investigation and analysis of a subject
  • Synonym: summary - a brief statement of the main points of something

Etymology

From Anglo-Norman and Old French 'report' (a bringing back), from Latin 'reportare' (to bring back).

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: n
