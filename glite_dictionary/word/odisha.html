Odisha

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈdiːʃə / (AmE), / əʊˈdiːʃə / (BrE)
a state in eastern India, located on the Bay of Bengal, known for its rich culture, temples, and natural beauty
state in eastern India

Examples

He spent several weeks traveling through Odisha, exploring its ancient temples and beaches.
The famous Jagannath Temple is located in Puri, a city in Odisha.
Many classical dance forms, including Odissi, originated in Odisha.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Orissa - former official name of Odisha, state in eastern India
