tie-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪ ʌp / (AmE), / ˈtaɪ ʌp / (BrE)
a connection or association, especially for business or social purposes
business or social connection

Examples

The two companies announced a tie-up to develop new technology together.
Their recent tie-up with an international partner has expanded their market presence.
The tie-up between the charities made fundraising efforts more efficient.
