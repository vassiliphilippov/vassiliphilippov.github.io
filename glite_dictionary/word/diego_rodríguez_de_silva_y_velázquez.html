Diego Rodríguez de Silva y Velázquez

1. PROPN

/ ˈdjeɪgoʊ roʊˈdrigeθ deɪ ˈsilvə i vəˈlɑːskeθ / (AmE), / ˈdjeɪɡəʊ rɒˈdriːɣɛθ deɪ ˈsɪlvə i vəˈlaskɛθ / (BrE)
(1599–1660) Spanish painter renowned for his portraits and significant influence on Western art, especially noted for works such as 'Las Meninas.'
Spanish Baroque painter (1599–1660)

Examples

Diego Rodríguez de Silva y Velázquez is best known for his masterpiece, 'Las Meninas'.
Art historians regard Diego Rodríguez de Silva y Velázquez as a central figure in the Spanish Golden Age.
Works by Diego Rodríguez de Silva y Velázquez are displayed in museums worldwide.

Etymology

From Spanish personal name Diego Rodríguez de Silva y Velázquez.
