wordplay

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝdˌpleɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːdˌpleɪ / (BrE)
the witty or clever use of words, especially involving puns or other forms of verbal humor
clever or humorous use of words

Examples

The author's love of wordplay is evident throughout the novel.
Comedians often use wordplay to make their jokes more entertaining.
Her sharp wordplay kept everyone laughing during the meeting.

Synset Examples

I do it for the pun of it
his constant punning irritated her

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pun - a joke exploiting the different possible meanings of a word
