forlornly

1. ADV

/ fɔrˈlɔrnli / (AmE), / fɔːˈlɔːnli / (BrE)
in a way that shows sad loneliness or abandonment; in a hopeless or miserable manner
in a sad, lonely manner

Examples

She gazed forlornly out of the window, waiting for someone to return.
He wandered forlornly through the empty streets after the parade had ended.
The dog sat forlornly by the closed door, hoping its owner would come back soon.

Synset Examples

a single chicken was scratching forlornly in the yard

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hopelessly - in a manner showing no hope
  • Antonym: cheerfully - in a happy, optimistic way

Etymology

From 'forlorn' + '-ly'; 'forlorn' from Old English 'forloren', past participle of 'forlēosan' (to lose).
