coss

1. NOUN

/ kɑs / (AmE), / kɒs / (BrE)
(in India, historical) a unit of length, varying between localities, formerly used in India and South Asia.
Indian unit of length (historical, varied)
archaic

Examples

According to old maps, the city was twenty coss away from the river.
In Mughal times, distances were often measured in cosses rather than miles or kilometers.
The traveler recorded that he covered three coss per day during his journey.

Etymology

From Hindi/Urdu 'kos', itself from Sanskrit 'krośa' (call, cry; also a measure of distance).
