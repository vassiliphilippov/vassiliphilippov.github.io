perinasal

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɛrɪˈneɪzəl / (AmE), / ˌpɛrɪˈneɪzəl / (BrE)
Situated or occurring near the nose.
located near the nose
medical

Examples

The doctor examined the perinasal area for signs of infection.
Some allergies cause a rash to develop on the perinasal skin.
A fracture to the perinasal bones can lead to swelling around the nose.

Etymology

From Latin 'peri-' (around, near) and 'nasus' (nose).
