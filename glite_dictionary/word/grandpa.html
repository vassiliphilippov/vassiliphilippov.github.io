grandpa

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrænˌpɑ / (AmE), / ˈɡranˌpɑː / (BrE)
the father of one's father or mother; one's grandfather
father of one's parent; grandfather

Examples

My grandpa tells the best stories about his childhood.
Every Sunday, we visit grandpa at his house in the countryside.
When I was little, grandpa would take me fishing by the lake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandfather - father of one's father or mother
  • Antonym: grandma - mother of one's father or mother

Etymology

Colloquial shortening of 'grandfather', from 'grand' + 'pa' (an affectionate form for 'father').
