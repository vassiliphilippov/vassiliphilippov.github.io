Hamas

1. PROPN

/ hɑˈmɑs / (AmE), / hɑˈmɑːs / (BrE)
an Islamic Palestinian political and militant organization, seeking to establish an Islamic state in place of Israel
Palestinian Islamic political and militant group

Examples

The political leader of Hamas delivered a speech on the situation in Gaza.
Many countries have designated Hamas as a terrorist organization.
Negotiations between Israel and Hamas have often ended in stalemate.

Etymology

From the Arabic Ḥamās, meaning 'zeal' or 'enthusiasm'; an acronym for Ḥarakat al-Muqāwamah al-Islāmiyyah ('Islamic Resistance Movement').
