thirty-eight

1. NUM

/ ˌθɜrtiˈeɪt / (AmE), / ˌθɜːtiˈeɪt / (BrE)
the number 38; being eight more than thirty
the number 38

Examples

There are thirty-eight students in my class this year.
He celebrated his thirty-eight birthday with a big party.
Only thirty-eight people attended the lecture.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirty-seven - the number 37; one less than thirty-eight
  • Degree Higher: thirty-nine - the number 39; one more than thirty-eight

Etymology

Formed from the words 'thirty' and 'eight'.
