Soviet

1. ADJ

/ ˈsoʊ.vi.ət / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.vi.ət / (BrE)
of or relating to the former Soviet Union (USSR) or its people or characteristics
relating to the former Soviet Union

Examples

The Soviet government implemented sweeping economic changes in the 1960s.
Her grandfather was a prominent Soviet scientist during the Cold War.
Many films from the Soviet era remain popular in former republics.

Synset Examples

Soviet leaders

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Russian - relating to Russia, its people, or language

Etymology

From Russian 'sovet' meaning 'council'; used as the name of governing councils in the USSR and to refer to the state itself.
