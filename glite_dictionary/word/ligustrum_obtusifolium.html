Ligustrum obtusifolium

1. NOUN

/ lɪˈɡʌstrəm əbˌtuːsɪˈfoʊliəm / (AmE), / lɪˈɡʌstrəm ɒbˌtjuːsɪˈfəʊlɪəm / (BrE)
A small deciduous shrub with graceful arching branches and lush foliage, commonly known as border privet.
a shrub; border privet

Examples

The hedge was composed of Ligustrum obtusifolium to create a lush, green border around the garden.
Gardeners often choose Ligustrum obtusifolium for its graceful arching branches and dense foliage.
In spring, the leaves of Ligustrum obtusifolium emerge fresh and vibrant, enhancing the landscape's beauty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: privet - a shrub of the genus Ligustrum

Etymology

From Latin, with 'Ligustrum' meaning privet and 'obtusifolium' meaning 'blunt-leaved'.
