antennary

1. ADJ

/ ænˈtɛnˌɛri / (AmE), / ænˈtɛn.əri / (BrE)
relating to or connected with antennae, especially those of insects or other arthropods.
relating to antennae (biology)

Examples

The scientist examined the antennary segments under the microscope for structural differences.
Certain sugars have a unique antennary pattern, influencing their biological functions.
Changes in the antennary morphology can affect an insect's sense of smell.

Synset Examples

antennal senses of insects

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antennal - relating to an antenna or antennae

Etymology

From Modern Latin 'antenna' (feeler, sensory appendage) + English '-ary' (relating to).
