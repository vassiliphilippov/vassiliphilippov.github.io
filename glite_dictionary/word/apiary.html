apiary

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ.piˌɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.pi.əri / (BrE)
a place where beehives are kept, typically a shed or area for raising and caring for bees
place where beehives are kept

Examples

The beekeeper checked each apiary early in the morning to monitor the health of the colonies.
Our local farm has a large apiary where they produce organic honey.
Visiting an apiary offers a fascinating glimpse into the world of bees and pollination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beehive - a structure where bees are kept

Etymology

From Latin 'apiarium', from 'apis' meaning 'bee'.
