aluminous

1. ADJ

/ əˈluːmɪnəs / (AmE), / əˈluːmɪnəs / (BrE)
Relating to or containing aluminum or alum.
containing or related to aluminum or alum

Examples

The rock was composed of several aluminous minerals.
This clay is especially aluminous and is used in ceramics.
Engineers tested the aluminous content of the soil before construction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alum - a chemical compound containing aluminum and used in dyeing and tanning
  • Synonym: aluminum - a chemical element, a light silvery metal

Etymology

From Latin 'alumen' (alum).
