Decadron

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛkəˌdrɑn / (AmE), / ˈdɛkəˌdrɒn / (BrE)
A trademarked corticosteroid drug (dexamethasone) used to treat allergies, inflammation, and other medical conditions.
trademarked corticosteroid drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Decadron to reduce the swelling in her joints.
Children with severe croup may be treated with Decadron in the emergency room.
Some patients experience side effects when taking Decadron for extended periods.
