empathetic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛmpəˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛmpəˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
showing an ability to understand and share the feelings of others; characterized by empathy.
showing understanding of others' feelings

Examples

The manager was always empathetic toward her team's personal struggles.
An empathetic listener can make a big difference to someone in distress.
He is known for being empathetic and understanding, especially with new employees.

Synset Examples

a sensitive and empathetic school counselor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sympathetic - showing feelings of pity and compassion for someone else
  • Antonym: unfeeling - lacking compassion or empathy

Etymology

From Greek 'em-' (in) and 'pathos' (feeling), via 'empathy'.
