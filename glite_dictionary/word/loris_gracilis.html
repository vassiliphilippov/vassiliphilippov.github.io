Loris gracilis

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔrɪs ɡrəˈsaɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˈlɒrɪs ɡrəˈseɪlɪs / (BrE)
A species of slender-bodied, nocturnal primate found in southern India and Sri Lanka, known as the slender loris.
species of slender-bodied primate

Examples

The Loris gracilis is known for its gentle, quiet nature and slow movements.
Conservation efforts are underway to protect the endangered Loris gracilis in southern India.
Despite its small size, the Loris gracilis has a remarkable ability to climb through dense foliage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slender loris - Common name for Loris gracilis, a slender-bodied primate species

Etymology

From Latin, with 'loris' of uncertain origin and 'gracilis' meaning slender or graceful.
