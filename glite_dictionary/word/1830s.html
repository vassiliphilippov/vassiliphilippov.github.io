1830s

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪˈtiːn ˈθɜrtiz / (AmE), / ˌeɪˈtiːn ˈθɜːtiz / (BrE)

Examples

The Industrial Revolution accelerated rapidly during the 1830s.
