leaping

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈliːpɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or process of springing upward or forward with force and agility.
the act of jumping upward or forward

Examples

The dancer's leaping amazed everyone at the performance.
Mountain goats are known for their remarkable leaping abilities.
His sudden leaping from the chair startled the others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jumping - the act of propelling oneself off the ground
  • Degree Lower: hopping - a movement by jumping with one foot or in short quick jumps
