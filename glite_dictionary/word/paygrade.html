paygrade

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪˌɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈpeɪˌɡreɪd / (BrE)
a level or classification of pay and responsibility, especially in the military or government organizations
level of rank and pay (esp. military)

Examples

Promotions in the army are often accompanied by an increase in paygrade.
She is not authorized to access documents above her paygrade.
Each paygrade in the Air Force has its own set of duties and privileges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rank - a position in a hierarchy, especially in the armed forces

Etymology

Formed from 'pay' + 'grade', indicating a category based on salary scale.
