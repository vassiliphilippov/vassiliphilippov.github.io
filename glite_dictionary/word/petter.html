petter

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛtər / (AmE), / ˈpɛtə / (BrE)
a person who caresses and fondles a loved one in an affectionate or sexual manner
one who fondles affectionately or sexually

Examples

He became known as a gentle petter who always respected his partner's boundaries.
Some teenagers worry about being labeled a 'heavy petter' by their peers.
At the party, she joked that her boyfriend was an enthusiastic petter.

Synset Examples

they are heavy petters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fondler - a person who caresses or hugs, especially in a sexual way

Etymology

From the verb 'pet' (to caress), with agentive -er suffix.
