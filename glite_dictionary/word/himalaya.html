Himalaya

1. PROPN

/ hɪˈmɑːləjə / (AmE), / hɪˈmælɪə / (BrE)
a massive mountain range in southern Asia, stretching about 1,500 miles along the border between India and Tibet, and home to the world's highest peaks, including Mount Everest
major Asian mountain range, home to Mount Everest

Examples

The Himalaya attracts mountaineers and trekkers from all over the globe.
Many rivers in Asia originate in the Himalaya.
She has always dreamed of hiking through the Himalaya to see Everest up close.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Mount Everest - the highest mountain in the world, located in the Himalaya
  • Synonym: Andes - the longest continental mountain range, in South America

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'himālaya' ('abode of snow'): 'hima' (snow) + 'ālaya' (abode).
