cheekbone

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃikˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːkˌbəʊn / (BrE)
the bone below the eye that forms the prominence of the cheek
bone under the eye forming the cheek

Examples

She had a bruise just below her cheekbone after the accident.
His high cheekbones gave his face a striking look.
You could see her cheekbone clearly when she smiled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zygoma - the bony arch of the cheek
  • Degree Lower: jawbone - the bone forming the lower or upper jaw
