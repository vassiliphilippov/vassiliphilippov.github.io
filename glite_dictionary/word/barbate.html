barbate

1. ADJ

/ bɑrˈbeɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbeɪt / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) having hair on the cheeks and chin; bearded.
having hair on the cheeks and chin

Examples

The biologist noted that the barbate species could be distinguished by their prominent facial hair.
He portrayed a barbate figure in the ancient mural, complete with a thick, flowing beard.
Some male birds display a barbate throat patch during the mating season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bearded - having a beard
  • Antonym: clean-shaven - having no hair on the face

Etymology

From Latin barbātus (bearded), from barba (beard).
