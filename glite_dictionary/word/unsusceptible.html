unsusceptible

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.səˈsɛp.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.səˈsɛp.tɪ.bəl / (BrE)
Not easily influenced, affected, or harmed by something.
not easily affected or influenced

Examples

The new crop variety is unsusceptible to most common plant diseases.
Some people seem unsusceptible to cold weather and rarely catch a chill.
Her strong will made her unsusceptible to peer pressure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insusceptible - not susceptible; immune or resistant
  • Antonym: susceptible - easily influenced or affected by something

Etymology

From un- (not) + susceptible (from Latin susceptibilis, capable of undergoing).
