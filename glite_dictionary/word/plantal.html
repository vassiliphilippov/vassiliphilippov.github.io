plantal

1. ADJ

/ ˈplæn.təl / (AmE), / ˈplæn.təl / (BrE)
Of or relating to plants.
relating to plants

Examples

The researcher published a detailed study of plantal tissues.
Her interest in plantal biology led her to pursue a degree in botany.
The artwork featured several plantal motifs inspired by the local flora.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: botanical - relating to plants or plant life

Etymology

From Latin 'planta' (plant) + '-al' (forming adjectives).
