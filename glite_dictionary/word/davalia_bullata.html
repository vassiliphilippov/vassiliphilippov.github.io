Davalia bullata

1. NOUN

/ dəˈvæliə bʊˈleɪtə / (AmE), / dəˈvælɪə bʊˈleɪtə / (BrE)
A feathery fern species native to tropical Asia and Malaysia, often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
feathery fern of tropical Asia and Malaysia

Examples

The delicate fronds of Davalia bullata are popular with houseplant enthusiasts.
Gardeners often choose Davalia bullata for its feathery and attractive foliage.
Davalia bullata thrives best in humid, shaded environments that mimic its tropical origins.

Etymology

Genus name from Latinized form of surname Davall; species name bullata meaning 'blistered' in Latin.
