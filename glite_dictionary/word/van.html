van

1. NOUN

/ væn / (AmE), / væn / (BrE)
(British) A closed railway vehicle used for carrying baggage or freight.
closed railway car for baggage/freight (Brit.)
british

Examples

The guard loaded the luggage into the van before the train departed.
Several crates of produce were transported in a van to the next station.
The railway company added an extra van for the holiday rush.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boxcar - an enclosed railroad freight car (US)

2. NOUN

/ væn / (AmE), / væn / (BrE)
a vehicle, typically larger than a car and smaller than a truck, with an enclosed cargo area, used for transporting goods or people
vehicle with enclosed cargo space

Examples

The delivery company loaded the packages into the van for distribution across the city.
We rented a van to move our furniture to the new apartment.
The plumber arrived in a white van filled with his tools and equipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: truck - a large motor vehicle used for transporting goods

Etymology

From Dutch 'wagen' (wagon, vehicle), later shortened in English.

3. NOUN

/ væn / (AmE), / van / (BrE)
the foremost part or leading units of a moving group, especially at the head of an army
foremost part of a moving group, esp. army

Examples

The general marched at the van of his troops as they entered the city.
The van of the army pushed forward into enemy territory ahead of the main force.
In the van of the procession, the flag bearers led the way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vanguard - the forefront of an action or movement, or those leading it

Etymology

Shortened from 'vanguard', from Old French 'avant-garde' (fore-guard).

4. NOUN

/ væn / (AmE), / væn / (BrE)
A vehicle, often modified or specially equipped, that contains living quarters and is used for camping or traveling.
vehicle with living quarters; camper

Examples

They converted their old van into a cozy camper for summer road trips.
The couple traveled across Europe in their comfortable van, stopping wherever they pleased.
This van sleeps four and comes with a small kitchenette and shower.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: campervan - a van equipped as a mobile home
  • Synonym: motorhome - a large vehicle designed for living in while traveling
