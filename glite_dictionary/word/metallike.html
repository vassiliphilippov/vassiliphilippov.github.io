metallike

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛtəlˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəlˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the appearance, characteristics, or qualities of metal
resembling metal

Examples

The statue had a metallike sheen that caught the evening sunlight.
Beneath the paint, the surface felt strangely metallike to the touch.
Scientists discovered a new material with metallike properties but made from recycled plastic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metallic - having properties or appearance of metal
