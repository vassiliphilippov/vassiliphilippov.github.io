Gainesville

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡeɪnzˌvɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪnzˌvɪl / (BrE)
A city in north central Florida, known for being the home of the University of Florida.
city in Florida, home to University of Florida

Examples

After graduating from high school, she moved to Gainesville to attend college.
The annual music festival in Gainesville draws crowds from across the state.
Gainesville is known for its vibrant arts scene and historic architecture.

Etymology

Named after Edmund P. Gaines, a United States Army officer.
