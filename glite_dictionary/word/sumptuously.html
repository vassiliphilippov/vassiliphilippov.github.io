sumptuously

1. ADV

/ ˈsʌmp.tʃu.əs.li / (AmE), / ˈsʌmp.tjʊ.əs.li / (BrE)
in a way that is luxurious, lavish, and richly decorated or impressive
in a luxurious or opulent way

Examples

The ballroom was sumptuously decorated for the royal banquet.
Guests dined sumptuously on gourmet dishes and fine wines.
The movie set was sumptuously designed to recreate the grandeur of ancient Rome.

Synset Examples

this government building is sumptuously appointed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lavishly - in a very generous or extravagant manner

Etymology

From 'sumptuous' (from Latin sumptuosus, meaning expensive or costly) + -ly.
