yellowish

1. ADJ

/ ˈjɛloʊɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈjɛlɪʊɪʃ / (BrE)
having a somewhat yellow color; slightly yellow in appearance
slightly yellow in color

Examples

The walls were painted a yellowish shade that seemed to change in sunlight.
She noticed a yellowish stain on her favorite shirt after spilling tea.
The kitten's fur was yellowish compared to its siblings'.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: yellow - of the color between green and orange in the spectrum; resembling the yolk of an egg
  • Degree Lower: greenish - having a somewhat green color; slightly green
  • Degree Lower: orangeish - having a somewhat orange color; slightly orange

Etymology

Formed from 'yellow' + suffix '-ish' (of the nature of, somewhat).
