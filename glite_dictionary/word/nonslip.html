nonslip

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˈslɪp / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈslɪp / (BrE)
designed to reduce or prevent slipping
reduces or prevents slipping

Examples

The kitchen floor was covered with a nonslip mat for safety.
Her work shoes have nonslip soles to prevent accidents.
Install nonslip tape on the stairs to protect against falls.

Synset Examples

a nonslip surface

Etymology

Formed from the prefix "non-" (not) and "slip".
