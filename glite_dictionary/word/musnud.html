musnud

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsnʊd / (AmE), / ˈmʌsnʊd / (BrE)
a cushioned seat or throne, especially one used by an Indian prince
throne or cushioned seat for Indian princes

Examples

The prince ascended the musnud with great ceremony.
Visitors bowed before the musnud as a mark of respect.
The ornate musnud was placed in the center of the royal court.

Etymology

From Persian 'masnad' (seat, throne).
