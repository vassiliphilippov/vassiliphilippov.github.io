to reflectorise

1. VERB

/ rɪˈflɛktəˌraɪz / (AmE), / rɪˈflɛktəraɪz / (BrE)
to provide something with reflectors or with a reflective surface, often for safety purposes
make something reflective for safety
british

Examples

The construction company decided to reflectorise all the road signs for nighttime visibility.
After the road was reflectorised, accidents in the area decreased significantly.
You should reflectorise your bicycle if you plan to ride at dusk.

Synset Examples

the driveway was reflectorized for safety reasons

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to reflectorize - to provide with reflectors (American spelling)

Etymology

From 'reflector' + '-ise', the British spelling of the verb suffix '-ize'.
