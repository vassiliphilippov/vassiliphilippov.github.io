practicality

1. NOUN

/ ˌpræk.tɪˈkæ.lə.ti / (AmE), / ˌpræk.tɪˈkæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
The quality or state of being practical; concern with actual use or practice rather than theory or speculation.
quality of being practical

Examples

The practicality of the new design impressed the engineers.
We need to consider the practicality of your proposal before moving forward.
Her decision was based more on practicality than emotion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utility - the state of being useful or beneficial
  • Antonym: theory - abstract ideas as opposed to practical application

Etymology

From practical + -ity, modeled on French 'practicalité'.
