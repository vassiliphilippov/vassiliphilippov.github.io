frump

1. NOUN

/ frʌmp / (AmE), / frʌmp / (BrE)
a woman or girl regarded as dull, plain, or unfashionable in appearance or behavior
dull or unfashionable woman

Examples

At the party, she worried about looking like a frump among all the stylish guests.
Despite her talent, some unfairly labeled her a frump because of her plain wardrobe.
He told her not to dress down or she'd end up feeling like a frump at work.

Synset Examples

she got a reputation as a frump
she's a real dog

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dowdy - a woman who is unfashionably dressed
  • Antonym: fashionista - a person who follows the latest fashions

Etymology

Origin unknown; first recorded in English in the mid-16th century.
