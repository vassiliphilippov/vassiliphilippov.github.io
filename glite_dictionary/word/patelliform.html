patelliform

1. ADJ

/ pəˈtɛl.əˌfɔrm / (AmE), / pəˈtɛl.ɪ.fɔːm / (BrE)
Having the shape of a dish or pan; resembling a shallow bowl or plate.
shaped like a dish or pan

Examples

The shell of this marine snail is distinctly patelliform and easily recognized.
Some mushrooms exhibit a patelliform cap when mature.
The mineral formed patelliform crystals on the rock surface.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cup-shaped - having the shape of a cup

Etymology

From Latin patella (a small pan or dish) + -form (having the form of).
