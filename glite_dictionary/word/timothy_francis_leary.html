Timothy Francis Leary

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɪməθi ˈfrænsɪs ˈlɪəri / (AmE), / ˈtɪməθi ˈfrænsɪs ˈlɪəri / (BrE)
(1920–1996) An American psychologist noted for his advocacy of psychedelic drugs, especially LSD, and his influential role in the counterculture movement.
American psychologist, LSD advocate (1920–1996)

Examples

Timothy Francis Leary became a counterculture icon in the 1960s due to his promotion of psychedelic drugs.
Many people credit Timothy Francis Leary with popularizing LSD in American society.
The research of Timothy Francis Leary remains a subject of debate among psychologists and historians.
