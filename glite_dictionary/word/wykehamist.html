Wykehamist

1. NOUN

/ waɪˈkɛmɪst / (AmE), / wɪˈkiːəmɪst / (BrE)
A student or graduate of Winchester College in England.
student or graduate of Winchester College
british

Examples

As a proud Wykehamist, he often spoke fondly of his school days at Winchester College.
Several prominent politicians in Britain have been Wykehamists.
The annual reunion dinner was attended by many former Wykehamists from across the country.

Etymology

From William of Wykeham, founder of Winchester College, with the suffix -ist indicating association.
