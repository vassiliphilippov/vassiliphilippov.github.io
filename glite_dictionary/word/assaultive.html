assaultive

1. ADJ

/ əˈsɔltɪv / (AmE), / əˈsɔːltɪv / (BrE)
disposed or inclined to attack or be physically aggressive
aggressive or likely to attack

Examples

The defendant's assaultive behavior was cited as evidence during the trial.
Security staff are trained to handle assaultive individuals without escalating the situation.
His assaultive tendencies made it difficult for him to keep a job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aggressive - ready or likely to attack or confront
  • Antonym: peaceful - not inclined to attack or aggression

Etymology

From 'assault' + '-ive'; 'assault' from Old French 'asaut', based on Latin 'assultus'.
