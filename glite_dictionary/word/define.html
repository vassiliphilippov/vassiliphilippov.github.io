to define

1. VERB

/ dɪˈfaɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈfaɪn / (BrE)
to state or describe the essential qualities or nature of something
state essential qualities or nature

Examples

It can be difficult to define what makes a work of art truly valuable.
The law seeks to define the essential qualities of a legal contract.
Philosophers have long tried to define the nature of consciousness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to characterize - to describe the distinctive qualities of someone or something

Etymology

From Latin definire (to limit, to set boundaries), from de- (completely) + finire (to bound, to limit).

/ dɪˈfaɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈfaɪn / (BrE)
