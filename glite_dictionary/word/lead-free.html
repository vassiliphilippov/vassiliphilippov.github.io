lead-free

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛd fri / (AmE), / ˈliːd ˌfriː / (BrE)
(of a substance, especially gasoline) not containing lead or lead compounds.
not containing lead

Examples

Many countries now require lead-free gasoline to reduce air pollution.
The pipes in the new house are made with lead-free materials.
Using lead-free solder is safer for both workers and the environment.

Synset Examples

lead-free gasoline

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unleaded - (of gasoline) not containing lead additives
