potterer

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːtərər / (AmE), / ˈpɒtərə / (BrE)
a person who moves about or spends time in a leisurely, aimless, or ineffective manner, often doing small, unimportant tasks
person who potters or putters about

Examples

My grandfather is a potterer, always tinkering with things in the garage.
Despite being a slow potterer, she enjoyed every minute spent in her garden.
He's more of a potterer than someone who tackles big projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: putterer - a person who putters about

Etymology

From the verb 'potter' (to occupy oneself in a leisurely, ineffective way) + '-er' (agent suffix).
