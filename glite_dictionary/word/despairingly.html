despairingly

1. ADV

/ dɪˈspɛrɪŋli / (AmE), / dɪˈspɛərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows despair or loss of hope
in a manner showing despair

Examples

She looked despairingly at the pile of unfinished work on her desk.
He called out despairingly for help, but there was no response.
As the rain continued to pour, the team watched despairingly from the sidelines.

Synset Examples

‘Why can't you understand?’ she asked despairingly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hopefully - in a hopeful manner
  • Synonym: desperately - in a way that shows great urgency or despair

Etymology

From 'despairing' + '-ly', where 'despair' comes from Old French 'desperer', from Latin 'desperare' (to lose hope).
