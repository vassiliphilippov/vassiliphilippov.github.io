interfaith

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərˈfeɪθ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈfeɪθ / (BrE)
involving, relating to, or between people of different religious faiths
involving different religions

Examples

The community organized an interfaith dialogue to encourage understanding among different religious groups.
They attended an interfaith wedding ceremony that included traditions from both families.
An interfaith service was held to pray for peace in the region.

Synset Examples

an interfaith marriage
interfaith good will

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecumenical - promoting or relating to unity among religious groups, especially Christian churches

Etymology

From inter- (between) + faith (religious belief).
