Divine

1. NOUN

/ dəˈvaɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪn / (BrE)
(in Christian theology) God or a god; the Supreme Being.
God or deity (Christian theology)

Examples

They devoted their lives to serving the Divine through prayer and worship.
In her poem, she speaks of the Divine guiding her through difficult times.
The cathedral was built as an offering to the Divine in gratitude for blessings received.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deity - a god or goddess; a divine being
  • Synonym: God - the supreme being and principal object of faith in monotheistic religions

Etymology

From Latin divinus (of a god), from divus (god, deity).
