tape-recorded

1. ADJ

/ ˈteɪp rɪˌkɔrdɪd / (AmE), / ˈteɪp rɪˌkɔːdɪd / (BrE)
(of sound or speech) recorded on magnetic tape.
recorded on magnetic tape

Examples

The meeting was tape-recorded for future reference.
She listened to the tape-recorded interview repeatedly.
All phone conversations are tape-recorded in this facility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recorded - captured by any means of recording

Etymology

From 'tape-record', meaning to record on magnetic tape.
