roadman

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊdˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdˌman / (BrE)
a man whose job is to repair or maintain roads
a man who repairs or maintains roads

Examples

The roadman worked tirelessly to fix the potholes after the storm.
You could see a roadman laying asphalt along the main street early this morning.
The council hired another roadman to help with the increased repairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: road worker - a person who repairs or maintains roads

2. NOUN

/ ˈroʊdˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdˌman / (BrE)
a travelling salesman who visits customers, especially covering a particular region
travelling salesman

Examples

The roadman made his weekly rounds to local businesses to take new orders.
As a successful roadman, he was on the road for most of the month visiting clients.
In the past, a roadman played a crucial role in connecting factories with remote customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: travelling salesman - a salesperson who visits customers in different locations
  • Synonym: salesperson - a person whose job is to sell products
