sutura internasalis

1. NOUN

/ suːˈtuːrə ˌɪntərnəˈsælɪs / (AmE), / suːˈtʃʊərə ˌɪntənəˈsælɪs / (BrE)
the suture between the two nasal bones
suture between the nasal bones
medical

Examples

During the operation, the surgeon carefully examined the sutura internasalis for any abnormalities.
Fractures near the sutura internasalis can complicate nasal reconstruction procedures.
The growth of the nasal bones is sometimes assessed by looking at the sutura internasalis in imaging studies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nasal suture - the suture joining the nasal bones

Etymology

From Latin 'sutura' (seam) and 'internasalis' (between the nasal bones).
