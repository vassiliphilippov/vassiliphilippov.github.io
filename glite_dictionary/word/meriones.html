Meriones

1. NOUN

/ məˈraɪəˌniːz / (AmE), / məˈraɪəˌniːz / (BrE)
A genus of rodents in the family Cricetidae, commonly called jirds, native to Asia and northern Africa.
a genus of rodents (jirds)

Examples

Several species of Meriones are found throughout deserts in North Africa and Asia.
Scientists studied the burrowing behavior of Meriones to understand their adaptation to arid climates.
The most commonly kept pet jird belongs to the genus Meriones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jird - a common name for rodents in the genus Meriones

Etymology

From Greek Μηρίονας (Mēriónas), used as a genus name in scientific classification.
