yellow-brown

1. ADJ

/ ˌjɛloʊˈbraʊn / (AmE), / ˌjɛləʊˈbraʊn / (BrE)
Of a medium to dark brownish yellow color.
medium to dark brownish-yellow color

Examples

The leaves turned a distinctive yellow-brown as autumn approached.
He wore a jacket of yellow-brown suede that matched his boots.
The artist mixed the paints to create a subtle yellow-brown shade for the landscape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tan - a pale tone of brownish yellow; similar color
  • Synonym: ochre - an earthy pigment containing ferric oxide, typically with a yellow-brown color; similar color
