sportswriter

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrtsˌraɪtər / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtsˌraɪtə / (BrE)
A journalist who specializes in writing about sports events and topics.
journalist who covers sports

Examples

As a sportswriter, she has covered every Olympic Games since 1996.
The sportswriter interviewed the coach after the championship match.
Many aspiring journalists hope to become a sportswriter for a major newspaper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: journalist - person who writes for newspapers or magazines or prepares news to be broadcast on radio or television
  • Synonym: reporter - person who reports news, especially for newspapers, magazines, or broadcasting

Etymology

Formed from 'sports' + 'writer', modeled after similar compounds (e.g., 'newswriter').
