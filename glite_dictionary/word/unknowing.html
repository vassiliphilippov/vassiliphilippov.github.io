unknowing

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈnoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈnəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
not aware of something because of a lack of knowledge or information
unaware due to lack of knowledge

Examples

She gave an unknowing response, not realizing the true situation.
He walked into the room unknowing of the surprise party awaiting him.
Her words hurt him, but she was entirely unknowing of their impact.

Synset Examples

he was completely ignorant of the circumstances
an unknowledgeable assistant
his rudeness was unwitting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness
  • Synonym: unwitting - not aware of the full facts; unintentional
  • Antonym: knowing - having knowledge or awareness

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'knowing,' from Old English 'cnāwan' (to know).
