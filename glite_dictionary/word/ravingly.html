ravingly

1. ADV

/ ˈreɪvɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈreɪvɪŋli / (BrE)
in a wildly irrational, enthusiastic, or frenzied manner
in a frenzied or wildly irrational way

Examples

The rumors were ravingly exaggerated by the gossip magazines.
He spoke ravingly about his favorite football team after they won the championship.
The fans cheered ravingly when the band came on stage.

Synset Examples

raving mad

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wildly - in a wild or uncontrolled manner
  • Antonym: calmly - in a calm or composed manner

Etymology

From 'raving' (wild, irrational) + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
