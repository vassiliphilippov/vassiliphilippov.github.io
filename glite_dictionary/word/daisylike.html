daisylike

1. ADJ

/ ˈdeɪziˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdeɪziˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a daisy, typically in appearance or shape
resembling a daisy

Examples

The daisylike flowers covered the meadow in early spring.
Her new dress had a bright, daisylike pattern.
Many asters have daisylike blossoms, though they are not true daisies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: daisy - a small, white-petaled flower with a yellow center
