postoperatively

1. ADV

/ ˌpoʊstˈɑpəˌreɪtɪvli / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈɒpərətɪvli / (BrE)
After a surgical operation has been performed.
after a surgical operation
medical

Examples

The patient's progress was closely monitored postoperatively for any signs of infection.
Pain management is crucial postoperatively to ensure a smooth recovery.
Instructions for care postoperatively were given to both the patient and their family.

Synset Examples

remove postoperatively

Related Terms

  • Antonym: preoperatively - before a surgical operation

Etymology

From post- (after) + operative (relating to a surgical operation) + -ly.
