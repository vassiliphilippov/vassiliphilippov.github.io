unmindfulness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈmaɪndfəlnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈmaɪndfəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being inattentive, especially by forgetting or ignoring one's responsibilities
lack of attention or care

Examples

His unmindfulness of the deadline caused serious issues for the team.
The manager noticed a pattern of unmindfulness in her work habits.
Unmindfulness often leads to overlooked details and unfinished tasks.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mindfulness - the quality or state of being conscious or aware of something
  • Synonym: carelessness - lack of care or attention

Etymology

From un- (not) + mindfulness, modeled on similar formations.
