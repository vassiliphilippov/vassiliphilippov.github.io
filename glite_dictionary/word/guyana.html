Guyana

1. PROPN

/ ɡaɪˈænə / (AmE), / ɡaɪˈɑːnə / (BrE)
A country in northeastern South America, formerly a British colony, which gained independence in 1966.
country in northeastern South America

Examples

The capital city of Guyana is Georgetown.
She traveled to Guyana to conduct research on its rainforests.
After independence, Guyana became a member of the Commonwealth.

Etymology

From an indigenous Amerindian word meaning 'land of many waters'.
