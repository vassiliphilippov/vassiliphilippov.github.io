hasp

1. NOUN

/ hæsp / (AmE), / hæsp / (BrE)
A fastening device consisting of a hinged metal plate that fits over a staple and is secured with a pin or padlock, used to secure doors or lids.
hinged metal fastener for doors or lids

Examples

The shed door was secured with a sturdy hasp and padlock.
He replaced the rusty hasp on the old chest to keep its contents safe.
Before installing the hasp, make sure both parts are properly aligned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: latch - a fastening or locking device for a door or gate

Etymology

Old English hæpse (clasp, fastening), related to Dutch haspel (clasp, hasp).

2. VERB

/ hæsp / (AmE), / hæsp / (BrE)
to secure or fasten with a hasp, especially to lock a door, lid, or container
secure using a hasp

Examples

Be sure to hasp the gate before you leave the property.
She carefully hasped the old wooden chest to keep its contents safe.
After closing the shed door, he hasped it to prevent animals from getting in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lock - to fasten or secure something with a lock
