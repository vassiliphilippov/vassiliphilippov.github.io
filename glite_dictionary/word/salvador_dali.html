Salvador Dali

1. PROPN

/ ˌsæl.vəˈdɔr dɑːˈli / (AmE), / ˌsæl.vəˈdɔː dɑːˈliː / (BrE)
(1904–1989) A Spanish surrealist painter known for his striking and imaginative works, including melting clocks and dreamlike imagery.
Spanish surrealist painter (1904–1989)

Examples

The works of Salvador Dali continue to inspire artists around the world.
A major exhibition of Salvador Dali's paintings attracted thousands of visitors.
Many recognize Salvador Dali for his iconic painting 'The Persistence of Memory.'

Etymology

From the Spanish name Salvador Dalí.
