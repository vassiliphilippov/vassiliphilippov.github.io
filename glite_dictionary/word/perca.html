Perca

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜːrkə / (AmE), / ˈpɜːkə / (BrE)
A genus of freshwater fish in the family Percidae, including the typical perches.
genus of freshwater fishes (perches)

Examples

The yellow perch belongs to the genus Perca and is found in North American lakes.
Scientists studied several species within the Perca genus to understand their distribution.
The fish in the Perca genus are popular among freshwater anglers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perch - a common name for fish in the genus Perca

Etymology

From Latin *perca*, meaning 'perch,' from Ancient Greek *pérkē*.
