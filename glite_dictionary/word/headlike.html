headlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛdˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a projection or shape that resembles a head
resembling a head in shape

Examples

The caterpillar has a headlike bulge at its front end.
Some mushrooms develop a headlike cap as they mature.
The statue featured a headlike form at the top of the pillar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head-shaped - having the shape of a head
