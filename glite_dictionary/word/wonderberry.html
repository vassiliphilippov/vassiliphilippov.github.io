wonderberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌndɚˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈwʌndəˌbɛri / (BrE)
an improved garden variety of black nightshade (Solanum melanocerasum) cultivated for its small edible orange or black berries
an edible garden variety of black nightshade

Examples

The wonderberry produces clusters of shiny black fruit in late summer.
She tried making jam from the ripe wonderberries in her garden.
Gardeners often debate whether the wonderberry is sweeter than other nightshades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nightshade - a plant of the genus Solanum, which includes both toxic and edible species
