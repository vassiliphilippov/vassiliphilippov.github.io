Heinrich Engelhard Steinway

1. PROPN

/ ˈhaɪnˌrɪk ˈɛŋəlˌhɑrd ˈstaɪnˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪnrɪk ˈɛŋelhɑːd ˈstaɪnweɪ / (BrE)
(1797–1871) German-born American piano maker who founded the Steinway & Sons company in New York.
German-born founder of Steinway & Sons pianos

Examples

Heinrich Engelhard Steinway immigrated to the United States and revolutionized piano manufacturing.
The legacy of Heinrich Engelhard Steinway lives on through the world-renowned pianos that bear his name.
Many musicians prefer pianos designed by Heinrich Engelhard Steinway for their exceptional sound quality.
