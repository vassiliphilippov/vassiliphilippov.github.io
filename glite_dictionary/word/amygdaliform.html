amygdaliform

1. ADJ

/ əˈmɪɡdələˌfɔrm / (AmE), / əˈmɪɡdəlɪˌfɔːm / (BrE)
having the shape of an almond; almond-shaped.
shaped like an almond

Examples

The artist carved an amygdaliform pendant from a piece of ivory.
Certain seeds are noted for their distinctive amygdaliform shape.
In the microscope view, the cell nucleus appeared distinctly amygdaliform.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amygdaloid - resembling an almond in shape

Etymology

From Greek 'amygdalē' (almond) + Latin 'forma' (shape, form).
