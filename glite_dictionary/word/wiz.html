wiz

1. NOUN

/ wɪz / (AmE), / wɪz / (BrE)
a person who is highly skilled or talented in a particular field or activity
exceptionally skilled person
informal

Examples

Olivia is a real wiz at solving complex math problems.
After a few lessons, he became a computer wiz and was helping others.
The team hired a marketing wiz to boost their sales.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genius - exceptionally intelligent or talented person
  • Synonym: expert - a person with comprehensive knowledge or ability in a field

Etymology

Shortened form of 'wizard', originally meaning a wise or skilled person.
