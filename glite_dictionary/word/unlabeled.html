unlabeled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈleɪbəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈleɪbəld / (BrE)
not having a label or tag; not marked or identified by a label.
not having a label or tag

Examples

The unlabeled package was set aside until someone could identify it.
She accidentally drank from an unlabeled bottle in the refrigerator.
Many students turned in unlabeled assignments, making it hard for the teacher to track grades.

Synset Examples

unlabeled luggage is liable to be lost

Related Terms

  • Antonym: labeled - having a label or tag
