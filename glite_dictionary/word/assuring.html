assuring

1. ADJ

/ əˈʃʊrɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈʃʊərɪŋ / (BrE)
giving confidence or comfort; making someone feel certain or less worried
giving confidence or comfort

Examples

Her assuring words helped calm everyone during the emergency.
The manager's assuring smile made the interview less intimidating.
He gave me an assuring pat on the back before my speech.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reassuring - making someone feel less anxious or worried
  • Antonym: unsettling - causing anxiety or uneasiness

Etymology

From the verb 'assure,' derived from Old French 'asseurer,' from Latin 'ad-' (to) + 'securus' (secure).
