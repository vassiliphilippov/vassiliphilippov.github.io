millennian

1. ADJ

/ mɪˈlɛniən / (AmE), / mɪˈlɛniən / (BrE)
Relating to a millennium or a period of a thousand years.
of or relating to a millennium

Examples

The ancient city celebrated its millennian existence with grand festivals.
Scholars study the millennian shifts in climate to understand long-term changes.
Some religious traditions center on millennian prophecies and time spans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: millennial - relating to a period of a thousand years or a millennium

Etymology

From Latin 'millennium' (a thousand years) + '-an' (adjectival suffix).
