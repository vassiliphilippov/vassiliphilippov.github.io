unquenchable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkwɛntʃəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwɛntʃəbl / (BrE)
Impossible to satisfy or extinguish; not able to be quenched.
impossible to satisfy or extinguish

Examples

Her unquenchable curiosity led her to explore every corner of the library.
He had an unquenchable thirst for adventure that nothing could satisfy.
The team's unquenchable spirit carried them through even the toughest challenges.

Synset Examples

unquenchable thirst

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insatiable - impossible to satisfy

Etymology

From un- (not) + quench + -able.
