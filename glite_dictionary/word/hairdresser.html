hairdresser

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛrˌdrɛsər / (AmE), / ˈheəˌdrɛsə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to cut, style, and beautify hair, especially as a profession
person who cuts and styles hair

Examples

I have an appointment with my hairdresser tomorrow morning.
The hairdresser suggested a new hairstyle that would suit her face shape.
He became a professional hairdresser after completing his training.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barber - a person who cuts men's hair and shaves or trims beards
  • Synonym: stylist - a person who styles hair as a profession

Etymology

From 'hair' + 'dresser', with 'dresser' meaning someone who arranges or styles.
