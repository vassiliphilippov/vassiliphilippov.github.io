ailment

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪlmənt / (AmE), / ˈeɪlmənt / (BrE)
A minor illness or medical disorder, often one that lasts for a long time.
minor illness or disorder

Examples

After traveling, she developed a minor ailment that kept her home for days.
The doctor examined several patients suffering from the same mysterious ailment.
Even a small ailment can affect your daily routine if not treated properly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disease - an illness that affects a person, animal, or plant
  • Synonym: malady - a disease or ailment

Etymology

Probably from Latin 'alere' (to nourish), through Old French 'ailement' (trouble, pain).
