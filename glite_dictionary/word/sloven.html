sloven

1. NOUN

/ ˈslʌvən / (AmE), / ˈslʌvən / (BrE)
A person who is habitually untidy, dirty, or careless in appearance or habits.
untidy or careless person

Examples

Despite repeated warnings, the sloven never bothered to clean his messy apartment.
She thought he was charming, but his sloven-like habits soon became intolerable.
No one wanted to share a dorm room with a notorious sloven who left dirty clothes everywhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slattern - a woman who is habitually dirty and untidy
  • Antonym: neat freak - a person who is excessively concerned with cleanliness and order

Etymology

From Middle French 'sloven', of uncertain origin, possibly related to Dutch 'slodder' (a slovenly person).
