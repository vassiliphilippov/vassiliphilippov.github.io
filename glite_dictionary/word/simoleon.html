simoleon

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈmoʊliən / (AmE), / sɪˈməʊliən / (BrE)
an informal term for money, especially dollars; cash
slang word for money
informal slang

Examples

He spent a few simoleons on drinks for his friends at the bar.
It'll cost you a hundred simoleons to fix that old car.
She's always looking for ways to save some extra simoleons each month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dough - slang for money
  • Synonym: bucks - slang for dollars
  • Synonym: money - currency or coins in general use

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly an invented or fanciful formation in late 19th-century American English.
