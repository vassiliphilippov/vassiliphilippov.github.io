bespectacled

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈspɛktəkəld / (AmE), / bɪˈspɛktək(ə)ld / (BrE)
Wearing eyeglasses; having the face adorned with spectacles.
wearing eyeglasses

Examples

The bespectacled librarian smiled warmly as she handed me the book.
A group of bespectacled students gathered in the front row of the lecture hall.
He was a bespectacled man with a thoughtful expression and a stack of papers in his hand.

Synset Examples

a bespectacled grandmother
the monocled gentleman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spectacled - wearing or having spectacles

Etymology

Formed from 'be-' (intensive prefix) + 'spectacled' (from 'spectacles').
