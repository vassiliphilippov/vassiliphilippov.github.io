Colette

1. PROPN

/ koʊˈlɛt / (AmE), / kəˈlɛt / (BrE)
(1873–1954) French novelist noted for her works exploring female experience and sexuality, including 'Gigi' and the Claudine series.
French novelist (1873–1954)

Examples

Colette's novels are celebrated for their vivid evocation of Parisian life.
Many critics regard Colette as a pioneering feminist writer.
I recently read 'Gigi' by Colette and was impressed by her sharp wit.

Etymology

Pen name of Sidonie-Gabrielle Colette, French author.
