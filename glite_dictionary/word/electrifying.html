electrifying

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈlɛktrəˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
causing a sudden and intense feeling of excitement or enthusiasm
causing intense excitement or thrill

Examples

The singer's electrifying performance left the audience cheering wildly.
The champion delivered an electrifying speech that inspired everyone in the room.
It was an electrifying moment when the final goal was scored.

Synset Examples

she gave an electrifying performance
a thrilling performer to watch

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thrilling - causing excitement or suspense
  • Antonym: boring - dull and unexciting

Etymology

From 'electrify' + '-ing'; 'electrify' from Latin 'ēlectricus' (amber, producing electricity) via French.
