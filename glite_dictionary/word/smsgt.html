SMSgt

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛs ˌɛm ˈɛs ˌdʒiː ˈtiː / (AmE), / ˌɛs ˌɛm ˈɛs ˌdʒiː ˈtiː / (BrE)
abbreviation for Senior Master Sergeant, a senior noncommissioned officer rank in the U.S. Air Force, comparable to master sergeant in the Army.
U.S. Air Force senior noncommissioned officer rank
american

Examples

The SMSgt addressed the squadron with authority and experience.
After twenty years of service, he was promoted to SMSgt last month.
Every SMSgt is responsible for mentoring junior enlisted personnel in the Air Force.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Senior Master Sergeant - senior noncommissioned officer rank in the U.S. Air Force
  • Degree Lower: Master Sergeant - a comparable noncommissioned officer rank in the Army

Etymology

Abbreviation for Senior Master Sergeant.
