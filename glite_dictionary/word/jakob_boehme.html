Jakob Boehme

1. PROPN

/ ˈboʊmə / (AmE), / ˈbøːmə / (BrE)
(1575–1624) A German mystic and theosophist known for founding modern theosophy and influencing later spiritual thinkers.
German mystic and founder of modern theosophy

Examples

Many of Jakob Boehme's works explored the relationship between God and the universe.
Scholars often discuss Jakob Boehme as a key figure in Western mysticism.
The writings of Jakob Boehme influenced numerous religious thinkers in Europe.
