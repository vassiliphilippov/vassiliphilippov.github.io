ecumenic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛk.juˈmɛn.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌiː.kjʊˈmiː.nɪk / (BrE)
concerned with promoting unity or cooperation among different churches or religions, especially within Christianity
promoting church/religion unity

Examples

The committee adopted an ecumenic approach to foster cooperation between the different denominations.
She is known for her ecumenic vision, often organizing events that bring together people from various faiths.
Their annual conference focuses on ecumenic dialogue to encourage greater understanding among Christian communities.

Synset Examples

ecumenical thinking
ecumenical activities
the ecumenical movement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecumenical - promoting unity among churches or religions

Etymology

From Greek oikoumenikos (general, universal), from oikoumene (the inhabited world).
