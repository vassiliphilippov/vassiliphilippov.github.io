pot-trained

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɑt treɪnd / (AmE), / ˈpɒt treɪnd / (BrE)
(of a child) having been taught to use the toilet instead of diapers.
trained to use the toilet (child)

Examples

Our daughter was pot-trained by the time she was two and a half years old.
Some parents find it challenging to get their children pot-trained before preschool starts.
The daycare only accepts pot-trained toddlers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toilet-trained - (of children or pets) trained to use the toilet or a designated place for waste
