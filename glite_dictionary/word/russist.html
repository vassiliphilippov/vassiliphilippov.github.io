russist

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʊsɪst / (AmE), / ˈrʌsɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates Russian policies or culture
supporter of Russian culture or policies

Examples

She was labeled a russist for her open support of Russian political ideals.
During the debate, accusations of being a russist were directed at several participants.
His russist views made him unpopular among some of his colleagues.

Synset Examples

Accusations of being a russist can be divisive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: russophile - a person who loves or admires Russia or Russian culture
  • Antonym: russophobe - a person who has a dislike of Russia or Russian policies/culture

Etymology

Formed from 'Russia' or 'Russian' with the suffix '-ist' (denoting a supporter or advocate).
