desk

1. NOUN

/ dɛsk / (AmE), / dɛsk / (BrE)
A piece of furniture with a flat or sloped surface for writing, reading, or working, often equipped with drawers or compartments.
writing table with drawers

Examples

She placed her laptop on the desk and began to work.
The teacher asked him to return to his desk after lunch.
A stack of papers was neatly arranged in the corner of the desk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: table - a piece of furniture with a flat top and one or more legs

Etymology

From Medieval Latin *desca* (table to write on), from Greek *diskos* (disk, dish).
