litoral

1. NOUN

/ lɪˈtɔrəl / (AmE), / lɪˈtɒrəl / (BrE)
the region of the shore of a lake, sea, or ocean; the coastal area where land meets water
region along a shoreline

Examples

Researchers collected soil samples from the litoral to study the effects of erosion.
The litoral provides a unique habitat for many species of plants and animals.
Development along the litoral must be managed to protect coastal ecosystems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: littoral - region of the coast, seashore or lake shore

Etymology

From Latin 'litoralis', from 'litus' (shore).
