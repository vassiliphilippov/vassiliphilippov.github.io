vanquishable

1. ADJ

/ ˈvæŋkwɪʃəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈvaŋkwɪʃəbl̩ / (BrE)
Able to be defeated or overcome; susceptible to being vanquished.
able to be defeated

Examples

The army seemed strong, but with new tactics, they proved surprisingly vanquishable.
In the story, the monster was not as vanquishable as the villagers had hoped.
Her fears became vanquishable once she faced them with confidence.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: invincible - impossible to defeat or overcome
  • Synonym: defeatable - able to be defeated

Etymology

From 'vanquish' (defeat), + '-able' (capable of).
