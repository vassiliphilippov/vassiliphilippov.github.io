duds

1. NOUN

/ dʌdz / (AmE), / dʌdz / (BrE)
informal clothing; clothes, especially in a casual or playful sense.
informal term for clothes
informal

Examples

He picked out his best duds for the party on Saturday night.
After the game, they all changed out of their uniforms into street duds.
She showed off her new duds when she arrived at school on Monday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clothes - garments worn to cover the body

Etymology

Possibly from Middle English dudde (cloak, mantle), later extended in American English slang to mean clothes in general.
