nipple

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪpəl / (AmE), / ˈnɪpəl / (BrE)
a flexible rubber or silicone cap with a small hole, attached to a baby's feeding bottle or pacifier for sucking and feeding
part of a baby's bottle or pacifier for sucking

Examples

Please make sure the nipple is clean before using the bottle.
He replaced the worn-out nipple on the baby's pacifier.
The flow of milk depends on the size of the holes in the nipple.

2. NOUN

/ ˈnɪpəl / (AmE), / ˈnɪpəl / (BrE)
the small raised part in the center of a mammary gland through which milk can be secreted
projecting part of a mammary gland

Examples

The baby gently sucked at her nipple during breastfeeding.
He noticed a small mole just above his left nipple.
Some mammals have more than one nipple on each side of their bodies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teat - the nipple of a mammary gland, especially of animals

Etymology

Possibly from diminutive of nip (to pinch) or Low German *Nippel*.
