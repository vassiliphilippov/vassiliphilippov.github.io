reefer

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːfər / (AmE), / ˈriːfə / (BrE)
a cigarette containing marijuana leaves, rolled for smoking; a marijuana joint
marijuana cigarette
slang

Examples

He lit a reefer and passed it to his friend.
Police found several reefers hidden in his backpack.
She claimed she had never tried a reefer before her twenties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joint - a cigarette containing marijuana
  • Synonym: blunt - a cigar hollowed out and filled with marijuana

Etymology

Probably from Spanish 'grifa' (marijuana), or possibly a reference to 'reef', meaning to roll up (as in a sail).
