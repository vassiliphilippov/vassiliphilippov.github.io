farmerette

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɑːrməˈrɛt / (AmE), / ˌfɑːməˈrɛt / (BrE)
a woman who works on a farm, especially as a farm laborer
woman farm worker

Examples

During World War I, many farmerettes replaced men who had gone to fight.
The local newspaper ran a story about the hardworking farmerettes harvesting crops.
She worked as a farmerette every summer throughout her college years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm

Etymology

Formed from 'farmer' + diminutive suffix '-ette', denoting a female.
