uncivilised

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɪv.ə.laɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪv.ɪ.laɪzd / (BrE)
lacking the cultural, social, or moral development associated with advanced societies; not polite or educated.
lacking civilization or refinement

Examples

Many considered their violent behavior to be uncivilised and shocking.
The explorer wrote about the uncivilised customs he observed during his travels.
It was seen as uncivilised to eat with your hands at the banquet.

Synset Examples

barbarian invaders
barbaric practices
a savage people
fighting is crude and uncivilized especially if the weapons are efficient
wild tribes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barbaric - savagely cruel; exceedingly brutal; primitive
  • Antonym: cultured - characterized by refined taste and manners; educated and sophisticated

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'civilised', from Latin 'civilis' (relating to a citizen).
