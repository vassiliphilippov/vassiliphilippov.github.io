doctorial

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɑːktɔriəl / (AmE), / ˈdɒktəriəl / (BrE)
relating to a doctor or doctorate degree; pertaining to doctoral studies or candidates
relating to a doctor or doctorate

Examples

She submitted her doctorial thesis after four years of research.
Several doctorial candidates attended the academic conference.
The university offers generous funding for doctorial studies.

Synset Examples

doctoral dissertation
doctorial candidates

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doctoral - relating to a doctor or doctorate

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'doctorialis', from 'doctor' (teacher, learned man).
