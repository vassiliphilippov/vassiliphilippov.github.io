oof-bird

1. NOUN

/ uf bɝd / (AmE), / uːf bɜːd / (BrE)
A fictional or imaginary bird, often described as unusual or with humorous characteristics.
fictional or unusual bird

Examples

The oof-bird is featured in several children's books as a comically odd character.
Legend says the elusive oof-bird only appears at dawn in magical forests.
