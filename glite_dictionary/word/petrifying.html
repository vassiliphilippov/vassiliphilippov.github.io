petrifying

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛtrəˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɛtrɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
causing someone to be so frightened that they are unable to move; extremely terrifying
extremely terrifying

Examples

The thought of speaking in public is absolutely petrifying to me.
She heard a petrifying scream in the middle of the night.
Climbing the narrow, exposed ridge was a petrifying experience for the hikers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrifying - extremely frightening
  • Degree Lower: frightening - causing fear or alarm

Etymology

From Latin 'petra' (rock) + English '-fy' (to make) + '-ing'.
