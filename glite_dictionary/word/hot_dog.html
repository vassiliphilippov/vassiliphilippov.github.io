hot dog

1. NOUN

/ hɑt dɔg / (AmE), / hɒt dɒg / (BrE)
a person who performs dangerous or showy stunts to attract attention, especially in sports
person who shows off with daring stunts
slang

Examples

The crowd cheered as the hot dog pulled off an impressive mid-air spin on his skateboard.
He's known as a hot dog on the ski slopes, always showing off with risky tricks.
Don't be such a hot dog; it's not worth getting hurt just to impress people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: show-off - a person who acts in a way intended to attract attention
  • Synonym: boaster - a person who brags or boasts
