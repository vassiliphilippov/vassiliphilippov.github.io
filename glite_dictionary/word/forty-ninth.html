forty-ninth

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɔr.tiˈnaɪnθ / (AmE), / ˌfɔː.tiˈnaɪnθ / (BrE)
Ordinal number indicating the position of someone or something that is number 49 in a sequence; the ordinal form of forty-nine.
ordinal number for 49th

Examples

He crossed the finish line in forty-ninth place, just behind his teammate.
This is the forty-ninth time the festival has been held in the city.
The book is shelved on the forty-ninth row of the library.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty-eighth - ordinal number for 48th
  • Degree Higher: fiftieth - ordinal number for 50th
