unreproducibly

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnrɪprəˈdusəbli / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪprəˈdjuːsəbli / (BrE)
In a way that cannot be reproduced or repeated.
in a manner that cannot be reproduced

Examples

The experiment failed because the results were obtained unreproducibly each time.
She solved the puzzle unreproducibly, using a method no one else could replicate.
The artist painted unreproducibly, ensuring each piece was unique and never to be copied.

Synset Examples

he has an inimitably verbose style

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inimitably - in a way that cannot be imitated
