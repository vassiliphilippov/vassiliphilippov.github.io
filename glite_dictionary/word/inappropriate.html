inappropriate

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnəˈproʊpriət / (AmE), / ˌɪnəˈprəʊpriət / (BrE)
not suitable or proper in a particular situation
not suitable or proper

Examples

His comment was highly inappropriate given the seriousness of the situation.
Wearing jeans to a formal wedding would be considered inappropriate.
The teacher quickly stopped the inappropriate behavior in class.

Synset Examples

completely inappropriate behavior

Related Terms

  • Antonym: appropriate - suitable or proper in the circumstances

Etymology

From Latin 'in-' (not) + 'appropriate' (suitable for a particular purpose).

2. ADJ

/ ˌɪnəˈproʊpriət / (AmE), / ˌɪnəˈprəʊpriət / (BrE)
not suitable or proper for a particular situation or purpose
not suitable or proper

Examples

It would be inappropriate to wear jeans to a formal wedding ceremony.
His remarks were considered inappropriate by everyone at the meeting.
The teacher warned the students not to use inappropriate language in class.

Synset Examples

noise seems inappropriate at a time of sadness
inappropriate shoes for a walk on the beach
put inappropriate pressure on them

Related Terms

  • Antonym: appropriate - suitable or proper for a particular situation

Etymology

From Latin in- (not) + appropriare (to make one's own).

Synset POS: a
