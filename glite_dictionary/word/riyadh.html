Riyadh

1. PROPN

/ riˈjɑd / (AmE), / riˈjɑːd / (BrE)
the capital city of Saudi Arabia, located in the central part of the country
capital city of Saudi Arabia

Examples

Many international companies have their regional headquarters in Riyadh.
The climate in Riyadh is hot and dry for most of the year.
She moved to Riyadh to work as a teacher at an international school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mecca - holy city in Saudi Arabia; spiritual center of Islam

Etymology

From Arabic الرياض‎ (ar-Riyāḍ), meaning 'the gardens'.
