nonmetamorphic

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˌmɛtəˈmɔrfɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnmɛtəˈmɔːfɪk / (BrE)
(of rock or material) not having undergone metamorphism; not metamorphic.
not having undergone metamorphism

Examples

The geologist identified several nonmetamorphic rocks within the formation.
Fossils are more likely to be preserved in nonmetamorphic sediments.
They compared the composition of nonmetamorphic and metamorphic minerals.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: metamorphic - (of rock) having undergone metamorphism

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'non-' (not) to 'metamorphic'.
