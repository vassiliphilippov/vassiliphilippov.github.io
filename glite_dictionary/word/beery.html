beery

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɪri / (AmE), / ˈbɪəri / (BrE)
smelling of, tasting of, or reminiscent of beer
smelling or tasting of beer

Examples

The old tavern had a distinctly beery aroma that lingered all night.
After several hours at the festival, his shirt smelled oddly beery.
Their laughter echoed through the beery halls of the local pub.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malty - having a flavor or aroma suggestive of malt

Etymology

From beer + -y, forming adjectives meaning 'characteristic of or like [noun]'.
