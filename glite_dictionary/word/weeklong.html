weeklong

1. ADJ

/ ˈwiːkˌlɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈwiːkˌlɒŋ / (BrE)
lasting for or continuing throughout an entire week.
lasting a week

Examples

She went on a weeklong business trip to New York.
The museum offered a weeklong workshop for aspiring artists.
Their weeklong celebration ended with a grand party on Sunday.

Synset Examples

her weeklong vacation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: week - a period of seven days
