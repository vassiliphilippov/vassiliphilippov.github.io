corrida

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈriːdə / (AmE), / kɒˈriːdə / (BrE)
a traditional Spanish, Portuguese, or Latin American public bullfight in which a matador faces and usually kills a bull in an arena
Spanish/Portuguese-style bullfight

Examples

The annual corrida attracts thousands of tourists to the city every summer.
Critics argue that the corrida is a form of animal cruelty and should be banned.
He attended his first corrida while traveling through Spain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullfight - a public spectacle in which a matador fights a bull

Etymology

From Spanish 'corrida', meaning 'run' (as in 'la corrida de toros', 'the running of bulls').
