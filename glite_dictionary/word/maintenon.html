Maintenon

1. PROPN

/ maɪn.təˈnɒn / (AmE), / mɛ̃t.nɔn / (BrE)
(c. 1635–1719) The second, secret wife of Louis XIV of France, known as Madame de Maintenon.
French consort of Louis XIV

Examples

Madame de Maintenon played a significant role in shaping the later years of Louis XIV's reign.
Historians often debate the influence Maintenon had over French court politics.
After marrying Louis XIV, Maintenon became one of the most powerful women in France.

Etymology

From her husband's title, Marquis de Maintenon, after the estate of Maintenon in France.
