Dangaleat

1. NOUN

/ dæŋɡəˈliːæt / (AmE), / daŋɡəˈliːat / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in Chad, characterized by seven vowels and distinctions in vowel length
a Chadic language of Chad

Examples

Linguists are studying Dangaleat to better understand Chadic language structures.
He grew up speaking both French and Dangaleat at home in central Chad.
The Dangaleat language is known for its unique vowel system.
