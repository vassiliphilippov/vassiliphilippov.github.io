gentianella

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒɛn.tʃəˈnɛl.ə / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛn.tɪ.əˈnɛl.ə / (BrE)
A low-growing alpine plant of the genus Gentianella, cultivated for its dark, glossy green basal leaves and showy, solitary, bell-shaped blue flowers.
a genus of alpine plants with blue, bell-shaped flowers

Examples

The garden featured a rare species of gentianella in full bloom.
Alpine meadows are often colored by the brilliant blue of gentianella flowers.
She carefully tended her collection of gentianella, ensuring the soil remained moist.

Etymology

Diminutive of 'gentian', from Latin 'gentiana', the name of the plant.
