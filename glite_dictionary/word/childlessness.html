childlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪldləsnɪs / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪldləsnɪs / (BrE)
The state of not having any children.
the condition of having no children

Examples

Her childlessness was often questioned by her relatives at family gatherings.
Many couples openly discuss childlessness and its effects in support groups.
The reasons for childlessness can vary widely, from personal choice to medical issues.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fertility - the natural capability to produce offspring

Etymology

From 'childless' + '-ness', where 'childless' comes from Old English 'cildlēas' (without children).
