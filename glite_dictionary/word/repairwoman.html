repairwoman

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈpɛrˌwʊmən / (AmE), / rɪˈpeəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to repair machines, equipment, or buildings
female skilled worker who repairs things

Examples

The repairwoman fixed the washing machine in less than an hour.
As a skilled repairwoman, she is called for all kinds of home repairs.
We waited for the repairwoman to arrive and restore the broken heater.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: repairman - a man whose job is to repair things
