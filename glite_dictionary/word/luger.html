Luger

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːɡər / (AmE), / ˈluːɡə / (BrE)
a German semiautomatic pistol, especially the models designed by Georg Luger and widely used in the early to mid-20th century
German semiautomatic pistol

Examples

The officer pulled out his Luger and ordered everyone to freeze.
Collectors often seek out the rarest Luger models from World War I.
He inherited a Luger from his grandfather, who served in the military.

Etymology

Named after Georg Luger (1849–1923), Austrian firearms designer.
