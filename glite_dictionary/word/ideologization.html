ideologization

1. NOUN

/ aɪˌdiɑːlədʒɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌaɪdiːˌɒlədʒaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of giving something an ideological character or interpretation, especially in relation to sociopolitical ideologies
process of making something ideological

Examples

The ideologization of education policies can lead to biased curricula in schools.
Recent debates have focused on the ideologization of climate science in public discourse.
Many historians warn against the ideologization of historical facts for political purposes.

Synset Examples

However, the ideologization of Islam today, differs markedly from the politicization of Islam in the past in several respects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: politicization - the process of making something political

Etymology

From ideology + -ization, modeled on similar noun formations.
