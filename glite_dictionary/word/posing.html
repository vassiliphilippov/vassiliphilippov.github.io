posing

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of assuming a particular position, especially for a photograph or portrait
assuming a position for a photograph or portrait

Examples

The photographer praised her posing during the photo shoot for its natural elegance.
He practiced posing in front of the mirror before the big portrait session.
Good posing can make a simple photograph far more interesting and lively.

Synset Examples

he wanted his portrait painted but couldn't spare time for the sitting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sitting - the act of sitting for a portrait or photograph
