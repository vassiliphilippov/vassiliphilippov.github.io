izzard

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪzərd / (AmE), / ˈɪzəd / (BrE)
An archaic name for the letter 'Z', formerly used in English.
archaic name for the letter Z
archaic

Examples

In old grammar books, the letter Z was sometimes called izzard.
He recited the alphabet from A to izzard without a mistake.
Some regional dialects once pronounced izzard in place of 'zee' or 'zed'.
