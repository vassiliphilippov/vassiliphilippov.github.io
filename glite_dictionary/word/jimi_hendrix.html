Jimi Hendrix

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɪmi ˈhɛndrɪks / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪmi ˈhɛndrɪks / (BrE)
(1942–1970) American guitarist, singer, and songwriter known for his innovative electric guitar playing and major influence on rock music.
influential American rock guitarist (1942–1970)

Examples

Many consider Jimi Hendrix one of the greatest guitarists in the history of rock music.
The influence of Jimi Hendrix can still be heard in modern guitar playing.
A tribute concert was held to celebrate the legacy of Jimi Hendrix.
