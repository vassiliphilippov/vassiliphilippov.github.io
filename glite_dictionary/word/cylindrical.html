cylindrical

1. ADJ

/ sɪˈlɪndrɪkəl / (AmE), / sɪˈlɪndrɪkəl / (BrE)
Having the shape or form of a cylinder; long and circular with parallel sides.
shaped like a cylinder

Examples

The machine was fitted with a cylindrical metal drum that rotated at high speeds.
Her new vase has a simple cylindrical design, making it easy to clean and fill with flowers.
The cylindrical shape of the tunnel allows for efficient movement of air and vehicles.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: circular - having the shape of a circle
  • Degree Higher: spherical - having the shape of a sphere

Etymology

From French 'cylindrique', from Greek 'kylindrikos', from 'kylindros' (cylinder).
