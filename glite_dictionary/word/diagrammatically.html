diagrammatically

1. ADV

/ ˌdaɪəˌɡræməˈtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəɡræməˈtɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that uses or resembles a diagram; by means of diagrams.
in the manner of a diagram

Examples

The report presents the company's structure diagrammatically to make it easy to understand.
The teacher explained the process diagrammatically on the whiteboard.
Maps can illustrate terrain features both visually and diagrammatically.

Synset Examples

the landscape unit drawn diagrammatically illustrates the gentle rolling relief, with a peat-filled basin

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diagrammatic - using or resembling a diagram

Etymology

From 'diagrammatic' + '-ally', after 'diagram'.
