fan-leafed

1. ADJ

/ ˈfæn ˌliːft / (AmE), / ˈfan ˌliːft / (BrE)
(of a plant) having leaves shaped like a fan.
having fan-shaped leaves

Examples

The palm tree is easily recognized by its fan-leafed fronds.
Some garden plants are fan-leafed, making them attractive in landscaping.
This fan-leafed species thrives in Mediterranean climates.
