primus

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪməs / (AmE), / ˈprʌɪməs / (BrE)
the presiding bishop of the Episcopal Church of Scotland.
presiding bishop of Scottish Episcopal Church

Examples

The new primus was elected after a lengthy deliberation among the bishops.
As primus, she presided over major meetings of the church in Scotland.
The role of the primus is unique within the Anglican communion.

Etymology

From Latin 'primus' meaning 'first'.
