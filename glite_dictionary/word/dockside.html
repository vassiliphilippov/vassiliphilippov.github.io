dockside

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑkˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɒkˌsaɪd / (BrE)
the area next to a dock, especially where boats are loaded or unloaded
area next to a boat dock

Examples

The fishermen unloaded their catch at the dockside early in the morning.
Shops and restaurants lined the bustling dockside near the harbor.
We took a walk along the dockside, watching the boats come and go.
