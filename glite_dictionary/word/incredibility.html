incredibility

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˌkrɛdəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ɪnˌkrɛdəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being incredible; the state of being difficult or impossible to believe
the quality of being incredible

Examples

The sheer incredibility of his story made everyone doubt its truth.
She reacted to the news with a mixture of amazement and incredibility.
Many listeners responded to the claim with outright incredibility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unbelievability - the quality of being difficult to believe
  • Antonym: credibility - the quality of being trusted and believed in

Etymology

From Latin incredibilitas, from incredibilis (incredible).
