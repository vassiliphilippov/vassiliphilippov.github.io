arteria meningea

1. NOUN

/ ɑːrˈtɪəriə məˈnɪndʒiə / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə mɛˈnɪndʒiə / (BrE)
Any of the three arteries that supply blood to the meninges of the brain and nearby structures.
artery supplying the brain's meninges
medical

Examples

Damage to the arteria meningea can result in life-threatening brain hemorrhages.
The neurosurgeon carefully avoided the arteria meningea during the procedure.
A CT scan revealed a tear in the arteria meningea following the accident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meningeal artery - artery supplying the meninges of the brain

Etymology

From Latin, meaning 'meningeal artery'.
