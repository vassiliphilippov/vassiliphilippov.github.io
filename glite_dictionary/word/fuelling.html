fuelling

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjulɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of supplying or taking on fuel for a machine, vehicle, or process.
supplying or taking on fuel

Examples

The fuelling of the aircraft was completed in less than twenty minutes.
Proper fuelling is essential for the smooth operation of the generator.
After several hours of fuelling, the ships were ready to continue their voyage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refueling - the act of refueling; supplying fuel again
  • Synonym: fuel - a material burned or used to produce energy; also, to supply with fuel
