different

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɪf(ə)rənt / (AmE), / ˈdɪf(ə)r(ə)nt / (BrE)
not the same as another; distinct or separate from the first or from what has been mentioned.
not the same; separate or distinct

Examples

This is a different problem than the one we solved yesterday.
She approached the question from a different angle than her colleagues.
We need to consider different options before making a final decision.

Synset Examples

that's another (or different) issue altogether

Related Terms

  • Antonym: similar - having characteristics in common
  • Synonym: distinct - recognizably different in nature

Etymology

From Latin 'differentem', present participle of 'differre' (to set apart).
