duplicable

1. ADJ

/ ˈduːplɪkəbl̩, ˈdjuːplɪkəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈdjuːplɪkəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be copied or reproduced; capable of being duplicated.
able to be copied or reproduced

Examples

The software license specifies which content is duplicable and which is not.
A duplicable technique can be shared and applied by other researchers.
Only duplicable data will be archived for public access.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: duplicate - to make an exact copy of something

Etymology

From Latin 'duplicare' (to double) + '-able'.
