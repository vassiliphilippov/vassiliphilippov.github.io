urochesia

1. NOUN

/ ˌjʊroʊˈkiːziə / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəʊˈkiːziə / (BrE)
the passage of urine from the anus, usually as a result of an abnormal connection between the urinary tract and the rectum or anus
urine passage from the anus
medical

Examples

The surgeon diagnosed urochesia after observing urine leaking through the patient's anus.
Urochesia can indicate the presence of a fistula between the urinary tract and the rectum.
The patient reported symptoms of urochesia following pelvic trauma.

Etymology

From Greek 'ouron' (urine) + 'chesis' (voiding).
