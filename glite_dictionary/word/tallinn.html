Tallinn

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɑːlɪn / (AmE), / ˈtælɪn / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Estonia, located on the northern coast along the Gulf of Finland
capital city of Estonia

Examples

We spent three days exploring the medieval old town of Tallinn.
Tallinn has become a popular destination for tourists from all over Europe.
The conference will be held in Tallinn next summer.

Etymology

From Estonian Tallinn, possibly meaning 'Danish town' or 'castle' (from Taani-linn).
