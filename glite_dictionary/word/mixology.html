mixology

1. NOUN

/ mɪkˈsɑlədʒi / (AmE), / mɪkˈsɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the art or skill of preparing mixed drinks, especially cocktails
the art of making mixed drinks

Examples

She studied mixology in order to craft unique cocktails for the new bar.
The world of mixology has evolved with the introduction of molecular techniques and exotic ingredients.
His passion for mixology is evident in the creative drinks he serves to his guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bartending - the occupation or activity of serving drinks at a bar

Etymology

Blend of 'mix' and '-ology', modeled after combining forms indicating branches of knowledge.
