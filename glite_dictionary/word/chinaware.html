chinaware

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪnəˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪnəˌweə / (BrE)
dishes, cups, and other tableware made from high-quality porcelain.
porcelain dishware

Examples

The museum displayed an exquisite collection of chinaware dating back to the 18th century.
We use our best chinaware on special occasions like weddings and holidays.
She accidentally chipped a piece of her grandmother's antique chinaware.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: porcelain - a white vitrified translucent ceramic

Etymology

From 'China' (the country of origin of porcelain) + 'ware' (goods, articles).
