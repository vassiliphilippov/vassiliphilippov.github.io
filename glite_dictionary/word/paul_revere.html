Paul Revere

1. PROPN

/ pɔl rəˈvɪər / (AmE), / pɔːl rɪˈvɪə / (BrE)
(1735–1818) An American silversmith and patriot famous for his midnight ride in 1775 to warn colonists of the approaching British forces.
American patriot and silversmith famous for his midnight ride

Examples

Paul Revere is famous for warning the American colonists of the British attack in 1775.
Many schoolchildren learn about Paul Revere and his midnight ride during American history lessons.
A statue of Paul Revere stands in Boston as a tribute to his historic journey.
