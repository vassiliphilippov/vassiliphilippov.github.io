Edmund Kean

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛdmənd ˈkiːn / (AmE), / ˈɛdmənd ˈkiːn / (BrE)
(1789–1833) An English actor renowned for his performances in Shakespearean tragic roles.
famous English Shakespearean actor (1789–1833)

Examples

Edmund Kean revolutionized the portrayal of Shakespeare's tragic heroes on the English stage.
The intense acting style of Edmund Kean made him a legendary figure in theatre history.
Many critics consider Edmund Kean to be one of the greatest actors of the nineteenth century.
