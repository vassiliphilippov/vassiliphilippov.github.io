plant-eating

1. ADJ

/ ˈplæntˌitɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈplɑːntˌiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of animals) feeding primarily or exclusively on plants; herbivorous.
feeding on plants (of animals)

Examples

Koalas are well known for their plant-eating habits, especially their preference for eucalyptus leaves.
The documentary focused on various plant-eating dinosaurs and their adaptation to prehistoric environments.
Many insects are plant-eating, feeding on leaves, stems, or roots throughout their lifecycles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: herbivorous - feeding on plants (especially animals that eat only plants)
  • Antonym: carnivorous - feeding on other animals (meat-eating)
