praiseworthy

1. ADJ

/ ˈpreɪzˌwɝːði / (AmE), / ˈpreɪzˌwɜːði / (BrE)
Deserving approval or admiration; worthy of praise.
deserving praise or admiration

Examples

Her dedication to helping others is truly praiseworthy.
The team's praiseworthy achievements were recognized at the ceremony.
Donating to charity is often seen as a praiseworthy act.

Synset Examples

applaudable efforts to save the environment
a commendable sense of purpose
laudable motives of improving housing conditions
a significant and praiseworthy increase in computer intelligence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commendable - deserving praise or approval
  • Synonym: laudable - worthy of praise
  • Antonym: blameworthy - deserving blame; culpable

Etymology

From 'praise' + 'worthy', meaning worthy of praise.
