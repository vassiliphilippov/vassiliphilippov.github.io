fifteen

1. NUM

/ ˌfɪfˈtiːn / (AmE), / ˌfɪfˈtiːn / (BrE)
the number 15; the cardinal number equal to the sum of fourteen and one
the number 15

Examples

She will turn fifteen years old next week.
There are fifteen students in our class this semester.
We ordered fifteen pizzas for the party.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fourteen - the number before fifteen
  • Degree Higher: sixteen - the number after fifteen

Etymology

From Old English *fīftīene*, from *fīf* (five) + *tīene* (ten).
