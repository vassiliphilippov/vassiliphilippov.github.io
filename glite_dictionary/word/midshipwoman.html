midshipwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪdˌʃɪpˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈmɪdʃɪpˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female officer in training in the navy, holding a temporary rank similar to that of a midshipman
female naval officer in training

Examples

The navy welcomed its first midshipwoman at the academy this year.
As a midshipwoman, she underwent rigorous training alongside her male counterparts.
Several midshipwomen graduated with honors at the naval commissioning ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: midshipman - a young naval officer in training holding the rank of midshipman

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'midshipman', substituting 'woman' for 'man'.
