galore

1. ADJ

/ ɡəˈlɔr / (AmE), / ɡəˈlɔː / (BrE)
in great quantities; in abundance
in abundance; plentiful

Examples

There were prizes galore for everyone at the fair.
After the storm, there was debris galore on the streets.
With restaurants galore, the city is a food lover's paradise.

Synset Examples

abounding confidence
whiskey galore

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abundant - existing or available in large quantities
  • Antonym: scarce - in short supply; not plentiful

Etymology

From Irish Gaelic go leor (enough, plenty).
