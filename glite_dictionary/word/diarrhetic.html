diarrhetic

1. ADJ

/ daɪ.əˈrɛt.ɪk / (AmE), / daɪ.əˈrɛt.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or causing diarrhea.
relating to diarrhea
medical

Examples

The physician noted a diarrhetic reaction after the patient took the medication.
Some bacteria can produce diarrhetic toxins that affect gut function.
Researchers studied the diarrhetic effects of certain food additives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diarrheal - relating to or characterized by diarrhea

Etymology

From Greek 'diarrhoia' (diarrhea) and suffix '-etic' indicating relating to.
