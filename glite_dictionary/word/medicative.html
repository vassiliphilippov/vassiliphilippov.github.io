medicative

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛdɪˌkeɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪˌkeɪtɪv / (BrE)
having the properties or qualities of medicine; tending to cure or heal
having medicinal properties

Examples

The doctor prescribed a medicative ointment to treat the patient's rash.
Many herbal teas are believed to possess medicative qualities.
He sought relief in the medicative effects of natural remedies.

Synset Examples

medicative drugs
medicinal herbs
medicinal properties

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medicinal - having healing properties; used as a medicine

Etymology

From Medieval Latin medicativus, from Latin medicari (to heal).
