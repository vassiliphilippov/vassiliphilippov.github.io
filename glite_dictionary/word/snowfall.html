snowfall

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnoʊˌfɔl / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌfɔːl / (BrE)
the amount of snow that falls in a particular place during a particular period of time
precipitation of snow

Examples

Last night's snowfall made the roads slippery and difficult to drive on.
The region experienced record snowfall this winter, delighting skiers and snowboarders.
A sudden snowfall covered the city in a blanket of white by morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rainfall - precipitation of rain

Etymology

Formed from 'snow' + 'fall', indicating the act or result of snow falling.
