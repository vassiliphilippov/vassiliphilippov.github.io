escapee

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌskeɪˈpi / (AmE), / ɪˌskeɪˈpiː / (BrE)
a person who has escaped from captivity or confinement, especially from prison
a person who has escaped custody

Examples

The police are searching for the escapee who broke out of jail last night.
After days on the run, the escapee was finally apprehended in a neighboring town.
News reports warned residents to be cautious as the escapee could be dangerous.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fugitive - a person who has escaped from a place or is in hiding, especially to avoid arrest or persecution

Etymology

Formed from 'escape' + '-ee' (denoting the person affected or concerned).
