snowsuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnoʊˌsuːt / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌsjuːt / (BrE)
A one-piece outer garment, typically worn by children, designed to provide warmth and protection in cold or snowy weather.
child's one-piece cold weather overgarment

Examples

The toddler zipped up his snowsuit before going outside to play in the snow.
Her bright red snowsuit kept her warm all day at the ski resort.
Parents often buy a new snowsuit for their children every winter as they grow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parka - A warm, hooded jacket for cold weather
  • Synonym: overall - A garment with trouser legs and a bib, sometimes used for protection
