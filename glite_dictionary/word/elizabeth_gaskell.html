Elizabeth Gaskell

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈlɪzəbəθ ˈɡæskəl / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪzəbəθ ˈɡɑːskəl / (BrE)
(1810–1865) An English novelist and biographer, noted for her social novels and her biography of Charlotte Brontë.
English novelist and biographer (1810–1865)

Examples

Elizabeth Gaskell wrote several important novels highlighting the social issues of Victorian England.
