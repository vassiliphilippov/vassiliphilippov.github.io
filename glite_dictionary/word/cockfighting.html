cockfighting

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑkˌfaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒkˌfaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of setting two roosters to fight each other for sport, often as a form of gambling entertainment
the sport of fighting roosters

Examples

Cockfighting has been banned in many countries due to concerns about animal cruelty.
The village was known for its illegal cockfighting tournaments every spring.
Authorities raided a barn where cockfighting events were being held.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dogfighting - the sport of pitting dogs against each other in a fight

Etymology

From 'cock' (rooster) + 'fighting'. Documented since the 17th century.
