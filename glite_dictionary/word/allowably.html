allowably

1. ADV

/ əˈlaʊ.ə.bli / (AmE), / əˈlaʊ.ə.bli / (BrE)
In a way that is permitted or allowable by rules, laws, or standards.
in a permissible manner

Examples

The company acted allowably under the current regulations.
He argued that the expenses were allowably incurred during the project.
The materials can be allowably disposed of in this facility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: permissibly - in a way that is permitted; synonym of 'allowably'
  • Antonym: impermissibly - in a way that is not permitted; antonym of 'allowably'

Etymology

From the adjective 'allowable' + the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
