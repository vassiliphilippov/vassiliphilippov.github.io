horniness

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrni.nəs / (AmE), / ˈhɔːni.nɪs / (BrE)
a state of sexual arousal or desire
sexual arousal; sexual desire
informal vulgar

Examples

After weeks apart, he couldn't deny his overwhelming horniness.
Some people feel embarrassed talking about their horniness openly.
The film was criticized for focusing more on horniness than on an actual story.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arousal - the state of being physiologically or emotionally stimulated
  • Synonym: lust - strong sexual desire

Etymology

From the adjective "horny" meaning sexually excited, + -ness forming a noun.
