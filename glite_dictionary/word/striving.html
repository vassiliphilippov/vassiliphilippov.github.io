striving

1. NOUN

/ ˈstraɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstraɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making a great effort to achieve or obtain something
earnest effort to achieve a goal

Examples

Her constant striving for perfection inspired everyone on the team.
Despite many obstacles, his striving finally led to success.
The artist’s striving to express deep emotions is evident in her latest works.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: effort - a vigorous or determined attempt
  • Synonym: struggle - a forceful or violent effort to get free of restraint or resist attack

Etymology

From the verb 'strive', from Old French *estriver* (to compete, quarrel).
