prepuce

1. NOUN

/ ˈpriː.pjus / (AmE), / ˈpriː.pjuːs / (BrE)
A fold of skin that covers the tip of the clitoris or the glans of the penis.
fold of skin covering genitals
medical

Examples

The prepuce protects sensitive tissues at the tip of the clitoris.
In some medical conditions, the prepuce may require special care or hygiene.
The anatomy textbook included a diagram of the prepuce in both male and female genitalia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foreskin - the retractable fold of skin covering the end of the penis

Etymology

From Latin 'praeputium' (foreskin).
