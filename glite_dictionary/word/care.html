care

1. NOUN

/ kɛr / (AmE), / keə / (BrE)
the activity or work required to keep something in good condition or working order
maintenance activity to keep something in good condition

Examples

Regular care of your bicycle will extend its lifespan and keep it running smoothly.
The new homeowner invested in proper lawn care to improve the garden's appearance.
Skin care routines can help prevent dryness and other common problems.

Synset Examples

he wrote the manual on car care

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maintenance - the process of keeping something in good condition

Etymology

From Old English *caru*, meaning 'sorrow, anxiety', later extended to include maintenance and attention.
