Ndebele

1. NOUN

/ ɛn.dəˈbɛ.leɪ / (AmE), / en.dəˈbeː.li / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken in southern Africa, sometimes regarded as a dialect of Zulu
a Bantu language of southern Africa

Examples

The Ndebele language is spoken by millions in Zimbabwe and South Africa.
She is fluent in both Zulu and Ndebele.
Linguists often debate whether Ndebele is a separate language or a dialect of Zulu.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Zulu - a Bantu language of South Africa

Etymology

From the Ndebele self-designation, related to isiNdebele.
