to enclothe

1. VERB

/ ɛnˈkloʊð / (AmE), / ɪnˈkləʊð / (BrE)
to provide with clothes or to put clothes on someone or something
to dress or clothe

Examples

The charity seeks to enclothe homeless individuals during the winter months.
She took it upon herself to enclothe and feed every orphan in the village.
Tradition required them to enclothe the statue before the annual festival.

Synset Examples

Parents must feed and dress their child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to clothe - to put clothes on someone or provide clothing
  • Synonym: dress - to put clothes on (someone)

Etymology

From prefix en- + clothe, modelled after similar forms.
