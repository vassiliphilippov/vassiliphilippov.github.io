patiently

1. ADV

/ ˈpeɪʃəntli / (AmE), / ˈpeɪʃəntli / (BrE)
in a patient manner; without becoming annoyed or anxious, especially while waiting or enduring hardship
in a patient manner

Examples

She waited patiently for her turn to speak during the meeting.
The teacher patiently explained the problem to each student.
He patiently endured the long delays at the airport.

Synset Examples

he patiently played with the child

Related Terms

  • Antonym: impatiently - in an impatient manner; not able to wait calmly

Etymology

Formed from 'patient' + suffix '-ly'. 'Patient' from Latin 'patientem' (suffering, enduring).
