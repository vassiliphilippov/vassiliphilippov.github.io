Allen Ginsberg

1. PROPN

/ ˈælən ˈɡɪnzbɜːrɡ / (AmE), / ˈælən ˈɡɪnzbɜːɡ / (BrE)
(1926–1997) An American poet and leading figure of the Beat Generation, known for works such as 'Howl' and his influence on 20th-century literature and counterculture.
American poet and Beat Generation icon

Examples

Allen Ginsberg was a central figure in the Beat movement, influencing poets and writers around the world.
Many students study the poetry of Allen Ginsberg in American literature courses.
The poem 'Howl' by Allen Ginsberg is still considered groundbreaking more than sixty years after its publication.
