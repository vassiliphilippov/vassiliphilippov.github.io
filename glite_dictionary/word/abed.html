abed

1. ADV

/ əˈbɛd / (AmE), / əˈbɛd / (BrE)
in bed; lying or sleeping in bed
in bed
archaic

Examples

He was still abed when the sun rose high in the sky.
The children were sent abed early after the long day.
She was found abed, reading her favorite novel.

Etymology

From Middle English 'abedde', from Old English 'on bedde' meaning 'in bed'.
