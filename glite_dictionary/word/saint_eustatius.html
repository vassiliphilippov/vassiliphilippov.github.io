Saint Eustatius

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt juːˈsteɪʃəs / (AmE), / seɪnt juːˈsteɪʃəs / (BrE)
an island in the Caribbean Netherlands, part of the Dutch Caribbean, located in the northern Leeward Islands.
Caribbean island, part of the Dutch Caribbean

Examples

The volcanic island of Saint Eustatius is known for its rich colonial history.
Many scientists visit Saint Eustatius to study its unique marine biodiversity.
Flights to Saint Eustatius are limited, so most tourists arrive by boat.

Etymology

Named after Saint Eustace (Eustatius), an early Christian martyr.
