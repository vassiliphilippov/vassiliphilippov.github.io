Georges de La Tour

1. PROPN

/ ʒɔːʒ də lɑː tuːr / (AmE), / ʒɔːʒ də lɑː ˈtʊə / (BrE)
(1593–1652) A French painter known for his religious works and mastery of chiaroscuro, especially candlelit scenes.
French Baroque painter (1593–1652)

Examples

Georges de La Tour was renowned for his dramatic use of light in his religious paintings.
Many art historians have studied the influence of Georges de La Tour on Baroque art.
A major exhibition featured the candlelit masterpieces of Georges de La Tour.
