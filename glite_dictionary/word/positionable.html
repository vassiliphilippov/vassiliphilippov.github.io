positionable

1. ADJ

/ pəˈzɪʃənəbəl / (AmE), / pəˈzɪʃənəbl / (BrE)
Able to be placed or arranged in a particular position.
capable of being positioned

Examples

The monitor is positionable at various angles for optimal viewing comfort.
This camera comes with a positionable lens for capturing images from different perspectives.
The positionable shelves allow you to customize the storage space.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: movable - able to be moved

Etymology

From 'position' + '-able', where '-able' (from Latin '-abilis') means 'capable of'.
