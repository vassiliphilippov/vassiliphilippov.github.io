Sylvia Plath

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪlviə plæθ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlviə plɑːθ / (BrE)
(1932–1963) A United States writer and poet known for her confessional style of poetry and her novel 'The Bell Jar'.
American poet and novelist (1932–1963)

Examples

Many students study Sylvia Plath in their modern poetry courses.
The haunting imagery in Sylvia Plath's work continues to influence writers today.
