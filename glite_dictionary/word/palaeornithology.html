palaeornithology

1. NOUN

/ ˌpeɪliˌɔːr.nɪˈθɑː.lə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌpalɪˌɔː.nɪˈθɒl.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of fossil birds and their evolution
study of fossil birds

Examples

She specialized in palaeornithology, focusing her research on the evolution of prehistoric birds.
Recent discoveries in palaeornithology have revealed new species from the Cretaceous period.
His lecture on palaeornithology attracted both ornithologists and paleontologists.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: ornithology - the scientific study of birds
  • Synonym: paleontology - the scientific study of life in the geological past

Etymology

From Greek 'palaios' (ancient) + 'ornis' (bird) + '-logy' (study of).
