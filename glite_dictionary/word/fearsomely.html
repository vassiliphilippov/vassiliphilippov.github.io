fearsomely

1. ADV

/ ˈfɪr.səm.li / (AmE), / ˈfɪə.səm.li / (BrE)
In a way that causes fear or is impressively intimidating.
in an intimidating or frightening manner

Examples

The lion roared fearsomely, sending the herd scattering in panic.
He glared fearsomely at anyone who dared to question his authority.
Thunder crashed fearsomely as the storm swept across the coast.

Synset Examples

a sabre slammed fearsomely through the thicket in all directions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fearsome - causing fear; very frightening or impressive
