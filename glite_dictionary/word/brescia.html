Brescia

1. PROPN

/ ˈbrɛʃ(i)ə / (AmE), / ˈbrɛsɪə / (BrE)
an ancient city in central Lombardy, northern Italy, known for its historical architecture and cultural heritage
city in central Lombardy, Italy

Examples

During their trip to Italy, they visited Brescia to see its Roman ruins.
Brescia has a long history dating back to pre-Roman times.
The art museum in Brescia is famous for its Renaissance paintings.

Etymology

From Latin 'Brixia', the ancient name of the city.
