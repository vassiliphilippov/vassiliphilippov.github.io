wife

1. NOUN

/ waɪf / (AmE), / waɪf / (BrE)
A married woman; a female partner in a marriage.
a married woman

Examples

He introduced his wife to his colleagues at the office party.
After twenty years of marriage, she still enjoyed being his wife.
His wife is an accomplished painter and sculptor.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: husband - a married man; a woman's partner in marriage
  • Synonym: spouse - a husband or wife; a partner in marriage

Etymology

From Old English wīf (woman, wife), related to German Weib (woman).
