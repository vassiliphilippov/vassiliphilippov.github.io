flooring

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɔːrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
material such as wood, tile, or carpet used to cover the surface of a floor.
material covering a floor

Examples

The company offers a wide range of flooring options, including hardwood and laminate.
Water damage ruined the old flooring in the kitchen.
They chose bamboo flooring for its durability and modern look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: floor - the lower surface of a room, on which one may walk

Etymology

From 'floor' + '-ing', meaning material for floors.

2. NOUN

/ ˈflɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
the lower inside surface of a room, hallway, tent, or other structure on which one walks or places things
the lower inside surface of a room or structure

Examples

The carpet covered the entire flooring of the living room.
Rainwater had seeped through, soaking the tent's flooring.
Before laying tiles, the builders inspected the wooden flooring for damage.

Synset Examples
