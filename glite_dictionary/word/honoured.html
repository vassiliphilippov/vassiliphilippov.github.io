honoured

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːnərd / (AmE), / ˈɒnəd / (BrE)
regarded with great respect or esteem; celebrated for achievements or reputation
respected; esteemed

Examples

She felt truly honoured to receive the award in front of the entire community.
After decades of service, he became one of the most honoured professors at the university.
The ceremony brought together many honoured guests from across the country.

Synset Examples

our esteemed leader
a prestigious author

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respected - admired by others for abilities or qualities
  • Antonym: disgraced - having lost respect or honor

Etymology

From Old French 'honorer', from Latin 'honorare' (to honor).
