perspicuously

1. ADV

/ pərˈspɪkjuəsli / (AmE), / pəˈspɪk.ju.əs.li / (BrE)
in a way that is clear and easy to understand
in a clear and understandable way

Examples

The author explains complex theories perspicuously, making them accessible to all readers.
Her instructions were perspicuously detailed, leaving no room for confusion.
The scientific paper was perspicuously organized and easy to follow.

Synset Examples

this is a lucidly written book

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clearly - in a clear manner
  • Antonym: obscurely - in an unclear or difficult to understand manner

Etymology

From Latin 'perspicuus' (transparent, clear) + English adverbial suffix '-ly'.
