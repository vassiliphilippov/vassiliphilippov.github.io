pansexual

1. NOUN

/ pænˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / panˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
a person who is sexually or romantically attracted to people regardless of their gender identity or biological sex
person attracted to all genders

Examples

As a pansexual, Alex believes that attraction is not limited by gender.
Many pansexuals advocate for greater acceptance of all gender identities.
She came out to her friends as pansexual last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bisexual - a person attracted to both men and women
  • Antonym: asexual - a person who does not experience sexual attraction

Etymology

From Greek prefix pan- (all) and English sexual.
