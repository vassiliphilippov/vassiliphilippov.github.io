Christopher

1. PROPN

/ ˈkrɪstəfər / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstəfə / (BrE)
a Christian martyr and patron saint of travelers, venerated especially in the 3rd century
Christian martyr; patron saint of travelers

Examples

Many people carry a Saint Christopher medal for protection while traveling.
Christopher is often depicted carrying the Christ child across a river in religious art.
Stories about Christopher helping travelers have been told for centuries.

Etymology

From Greek Christophoros, meaning 'bearing Christ'.
