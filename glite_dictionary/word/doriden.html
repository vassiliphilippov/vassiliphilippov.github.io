Doriden

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔrɪdɛn / (AmE), / ˈdɒrɪdɛn / (BrE)
A trademarked sedative medication formerly used to treat certain sleep disorders.
trademarked sedative drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Doriden to help her manage her insomnia.
Concerns about side effects led to Doriden being discontinued in many countries.
Some patients relied on Doriden for years before newer medications became available.
