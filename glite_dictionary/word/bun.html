bun

1. NOUN

/ bʌn / (AmE), / bʌn / (BrE)
A small, round bread roll, which may be plain or sweet.
small rounded bread roll

Examples

He bought a freshly baked bun from the bakery for breakfast.
Would you like a cheese bun with your soup?
The picnic basket was filled with sweet buns and fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roll - small, round or oblong bread; bread roll
  • Degree Higher: loaf - a quantity of bread shaped and baked in one piece

Etymology

Perhaps from Middle Dutch *bone* (swelling, bump), or from Old French *bun* (kind of cake).
