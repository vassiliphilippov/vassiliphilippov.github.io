Sumatra

1. PROPN

/ suˈmɑː.trə / (AmE), / suˈmɑː.trə / (BrE)
a large, mountainous island in western Indonesia, lying to the west of Java and south of the Malay Peninsula
mountainous island in western Indonesia

Examples

The jungles of Sumatra are home to rare species such as the Sumatran tiger.
She spent a summer trekking across the rugged terrain of Sumatra.
Coffee from Sumatra is renowned for its rich, earthy flavor.

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Suvarṇadvīpa' (island of gold), adapted through Malay 'Sumatera'.
