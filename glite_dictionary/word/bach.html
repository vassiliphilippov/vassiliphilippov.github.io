to bach

1. VERB

/ bætʃ / (AmE), / bætʃ / (BrE)
(informal) To live alone and manage as a bachelor, especially as a man without a spouse or partner.
live as an unmarried man
informal american

Examples

After his wife left on a business trip, he had to bach it for a week.
I don't mind baching for a while, but it gets lonely sometimes.
He bacched in his apartment during the summer before his fiancée moved in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bachelor - an unmarried man

Etymology

Shortened from 'bachelor.'
