unrestricted

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈstrɪktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈstrɪktɪd / (BrE)
Not limited or controlled in any way; free to act or be used as desired.
not limited or controlled

Examples

The unrestricted access allowed researchers to view all the confidential data.
He was given unrestricted freedom to design the project as he wished.
Membership in the club gives you unrestricted use of the facilities.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: restricted - limited or controlled

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + restricted.
