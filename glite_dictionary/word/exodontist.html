exodontist

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛk.soʊˈdɑn.tɪst / (AmE), / ˌɛk.səʊˈdɒn.tɪst / (BrE)
a dentist who specializes in the extraction of teeth
dentist specializing in tooth extraction
medical

Examples

The patient was referred to an exodontist for the removal of her impacted wisdom teeth.
Working as an exodontist requires advanced training beyond general dental school.
After years of practice, he became the leading exodontist in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oral surgeon - a dental specialist who performs surgery on the mouth, teeth, and jaws

Etymology

From Greek 'exo-' (outside) + 'odous, odont-' (tooth) + '-ist' (agent noun).
