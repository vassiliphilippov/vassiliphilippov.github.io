callipygous

1. ADJ

/ ˌkæləˈpɪɡəs / (AmE), / ˌkælɪˈpɪɡəs / (BrE)
(of a person) having well-shaped or attractive buttocks.
having well-shaped buttocks

Examples

The artist preferred to sculpt callipygous figures inspired by classical forms.
