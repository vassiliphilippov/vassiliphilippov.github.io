undernourished

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndərˈnɜrɪʃt / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈnʌrɪʃt / (BrE)
not having enough food or nutrients for good health and proper growth.
lacking sufficient nourishment

Examples

The doctor was concerned about the undernourished children in the refugee camp.
Many street animals are undernourished and need regular feeding.
The child appeared weak and undernourished after weeks without adequate food.

Synset Examples

gaunt underfed children
badly undernourished

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malnourished - suffering from poor nutrition
  • Antonym: well-nourished - having enough food and nutrients for good health

Etymology

From 'under-' (not enough) + 'nourish' (to provide with food), influenced by French *malnourri* and Latin *nutrire* (to feed).
