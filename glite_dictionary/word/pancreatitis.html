pancreatitis

1. NOUN

/ ˌpæŋkriəˈtaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌpaŋkriːəˈtaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the pancreas, often causing abdominal pain and other symptoms.
inflammation of the pancreas
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with pancreatitis after experiencing severe abdominal pain.
Heavy alcohol consumption is a common cause of acute pancreatitis.
Doctors monitored her closely during treatment for pancreatitis.

Etymology

From pancreas + -itis (Greek for inflammation).
