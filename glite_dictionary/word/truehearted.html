truehearted

1. ADJ

/ truːˈhɑːrtɪd / (AmE), / truːˈhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
Loyal, steadfast, and unwavering in devotion, especially to a friend, a cause, or an oath.
steadfastly loyal or faithful

Examples

He remained a truehearted friend, supporting her through every difficulty.
The novel features a truehearted hero who never betrays his comrades.
She is known for her truehearted devotion to her family and her principles.

Synset Examples

a firm ally
loyal supporters
the true-hearted soldier … of Tippecanoe
fast friends

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loyal - showing firm and constant support or allegiance
  • Synonym: faithful - remaining loyal and steadfast
  • Antonym: fickle - changing frequently in loyalty or affection

Etymology

From 'true' + 'hearted', indicating having a heart that is true or sincere.
