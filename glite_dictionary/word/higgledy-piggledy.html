higgledy-piggledy

1. ADV

/ ˌhɪɡəldi ˈpɪɡəldi / (AmE), / ˌhɪɡldiˈpɪɡldi / (BrE)
in a state of utter disorder or confusion; not arranged neatly
in utter disorder

Examples

Children left their toys higgledy-piggledy all over the living room.
The books lay higgledy-piggledy on the shelves, with no sense of arrangement.
After the move, furniture was scattered higgledy-piggledy throughout the house.

Synset Examples

a disorderly pile of clothes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disorganized - lacking order or method
  • Antonym: orderly - arranged or organized neatly

Etymology

Reduplicative nonsense formation from the 16th century, evocative of confusion or disorder.

2. ADV

/ ˈhɪɡəldi ˈpɪɡəldi / (AmE), / ˈhɪɡldi ˈpɪɡldi / (BrE)
In a disorderly or chaotic manner; without order or arrangement.
in a disorderly or jumbled manner

Examples

Papers and books were scattered higgledy-piggledy across the desk.
After the move, their belongings were packed higgledy-piggledy into boxes without any order.
Toys lay higgledy-piggledy on the living room floor after the children finished playing.

Synset Examples

they were piled up higgledy-piggledy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chaotically - in a state of complete disorder and confusion
  • Antonym: neatly - in an orderly and tidy manner

Etymology

Originated in the late 16th century as a reduplicative phrase of fanciful formation, imitating a jumbled or disorderly sound.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00257334-r
Synset POS: r
