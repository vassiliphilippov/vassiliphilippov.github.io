Yahwe

1. PROPN

/ ˈjɑː.weɪ / (AmE), / ˈjɑː.weɪ / (BrE)
A name for God in the Hebrew Bible, transliterated from the Hebrew consonants YHVH (Yahweh or Jehovah).
a name for God in Hebrew Bible

Examples

The ancient Israelites prayed to Yahwe as their one true God.
Scholars debate the pronunciation and significance of the name Yahwe.
Some translations of the Bible use Yahwe instead of 'the LORD' in certain passages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Yahweh - alternative transliteration for the name of God in Hebrew Bible
  • Synonym: Jehovah - another transliteration of the tetragrammaton YHVH

Etymology

From Hebrew יְהוָה (YHWH), the tetragrammaton representing the name of God.
