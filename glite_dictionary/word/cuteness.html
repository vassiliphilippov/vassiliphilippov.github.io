cuteness

1. NOUN

/ ˈkjuːtnəs / (AmE), / ˈkjuːtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being attractive or charming, especially in a delicate or endearing way
quality of being attractive and charming

Examples

Her cuteness was the first thing everyone noticed at the party.
The puppy's cuteness made it impossible for anyone to resist picking it up.
He relied on his little sister's cuteness to convince their parents to get a new pet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charm - a quality of pleasing or attracting others
  • Antonym: ugliness - the quality of being unpleasant to look at

Etymology

From 'cute' (shortened form of 'acute', meaning clever or pretty) + '-ness'.
