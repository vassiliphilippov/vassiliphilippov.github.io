savoring

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪvɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈseɪvərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of tasting and enjoying a small amount of food or drink to assess its quality
tasting and enjoying small amounts

Examples

He took his time with the savoring of the first ripe strawberry from his garden.
The art of savoring each bite can transform an ordinary meal into a memorable experience.
Her savoring of the aged wine impressed even the most experienced connoisseur.

Synset Examples

cooking was fine but it was the savoring that he enjoyed most

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tasting - the act of sampling a food or drink for flavor

Etymology

From verb 'savor', from Old French 'savourer', from Latin 'sapere' (to taste).
