kind-heartedness

1. NOUN

/ ˌkaɪndˈhɑrtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌkaɪndˈhɑːtɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being kind, caring, and sympathetic toward others
the quality of being kind and caring

Examples

Her kind-heartedness was evident in the way she cared for stray animals.
Many people admired his kind-heartedness and generosity toward those in need.
Acts of kind-heartedness can make a big difference in someone's life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate
  • Antonym: cruelty - the quality of being cruel

Etymology

From 'kind-hearted' (kind + hearted) with the noun suffix '-ness'.
