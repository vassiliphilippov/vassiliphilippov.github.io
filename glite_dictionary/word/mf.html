MF

1. NOUN

/ ɛm ɛf / (AmE), / ɛm ɛf / (BrE)
medium frequency; the range of radio frequencies from 300 to 3000 kilohertz, used for AM radio broadcasting and other communications.
medium frequency (300–3000 kHz, radio)

Examples

Many AM radio stations broadcast in the MF range.
Ships use MF frequencies for certain types of communication.
Interference can occur between different signals in the MF band.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: LF - low frequency (30–300 kHz, radio)
  • Degree Higher: HF - high frequency (3–30 MHz, radio)

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'medium frequency'.
