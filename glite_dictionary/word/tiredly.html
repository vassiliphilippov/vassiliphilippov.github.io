tiredly

1. ADV

/ ˈtaɪɚdli / (AmE), / ˈtaɪədli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows tiredness or exhaustion.
in a weary or exhausted manner

Examples

She sighed tiredly after a long day of work.
He answered the question tiredly, barely lifting his head.
The children trudged tiredly up the hill after their hike.

Synset Examples

he walked around tiredly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wearily - in a way that shows tiredness or fatigue
  • Antonym: energetically - in a way that shows energy or enthusiasm
