unsmooth

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsmuð / (AmE), / ʌnˈsmuːð / (BrE)
having or marked by an uneven or irregular surface; not smooth
not smooth; having an uneven surface

Examples

The carpenter sanded the unsmooth edges until they were even.
Her hands felt unsmooth after gardening without gloves.
The wall's unsmooth finish gave the room a rustic charm.

Synset Examples

trees with rough bark
rough ground
rough skin
rough blankets
his unsmooth face

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rough - having an uneven or irregular surface
  • Antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'un-' (meaning 'not') + 'smooth'.
