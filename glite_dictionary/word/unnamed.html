unnamed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈneɪmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈneɪmd / (BrE)
not having a name mentioned, known, or disclosed; anonymous or unidentified.
not named or identified

Examples

The company received a generous gift from an unnamed donor.
An unnamed government official commented on the situation.
She received an unnamed award for her outstanding contribution.

Synset Examples

a poem by an unknown author
corporations responsible to nameless owners
an unnamed donor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anonymous - having no known name or identity

Etymology

From the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'named'.
