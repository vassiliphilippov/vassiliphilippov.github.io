Mugil liza

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːɡɪl ˈliːzə / (AmE), / ˈmjuːɡɪl ˈliːzə / (BrE)
a species of mullet similar to the striped mullet, found in the Caribbean region
a Caribbean species of mullet

Examples

The Mugil liza is commonly found in the shallow coastal waters of the Caribbean.
Fishermen in the region often catch Mugil liza due to its commercial value.
Unlike other species, Mugil liza thrives in both freshwater and marine environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: striped mullet - a related mullet species found in other regions

Etymology

From Latin; Mugil meaning 'mullet', liza being the species name.
