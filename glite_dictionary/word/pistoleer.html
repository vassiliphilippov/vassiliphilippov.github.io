pistoleer

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɪstəˈlɪr / (AmE), / ˌpɪstəˈlɪə / (BrE)
A person, especially a soldier, who is armed with a pistol.
person armed with a pistol

Examples

The legendary pistoleer became a symbol of fear on the frontier.
At dawn, each pistoleer took his mark for the duel.
Few soldiers could match the speed of the seasoned pistoleer during combat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gunman - someone who uses or is armed with a gun
  • Synonym: marksman - a person skilled in shooting

Etymology

From French *pistolier* (pistol-bearing soldier), from *pistolet* (small pistol).
