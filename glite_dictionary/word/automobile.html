automobile

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑtəmoʊˌbil / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəməˌbiːl / (BrE)
A road vehicle with four wheels that is powered by an engine and used for transporting people.
motor vehicle with four wheels

Examples

She bought a new automobile last spring and loves driving it on weekends.
The automobile industry has gone through significant changes over the past decade.
His first automobile was a used sedan that barely started in the winter.

Synset Examples

he needs a car to get to work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car - a motor vehicle with four wheels; used for transportation

Etymology

From French 'automobile', from Greek 'auto-' (self) + Latin 'mobilis' (movable).
