heterosexual

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɛt̬əroʊˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌhɛtərəʊˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
A person who is sexually attracted to people of the opposite sex.
person attracted to opposite sex

Examples

As a heterosexual, he felt comfortable discussing his relationships openly.
The survey included questions for both heterosexual and homosexual participants.
Many heterosexuals support equal rights for the LGBTQ+ community.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: homosexual - person attracted to the same sex

Etymology

From Greek heteros (other) + Latin sexus (sex).

attracted to opposite sex
