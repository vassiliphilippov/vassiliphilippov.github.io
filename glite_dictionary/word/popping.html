popping

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
a sharp, explosive sound, as made by a gunshot, bursting balloon, or opening a bottle cork
a sharp explosive sound

Examples

There was a loud popping as they opened the bottle of champagne.
The popping of fireworks echoed across the night sky.
We could hear the popping of popcorn coming from the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bang - a sudden loud noise, often from an impact or explosion
  • Synonym: crack - a sudden sharp noise like something breaking
