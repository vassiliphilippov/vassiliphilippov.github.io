lasciviousness

1. NOUN

/ ləˈsɪviəsnəs / (AmE), / ləˈsɪvɪəsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of expressing or showing strong and often offensive sexual desire; lewdness or lustfulness
lewd or lustful behavior

Examples

The court found him guilty of lasciviousness due to his inappropriate conduct.
Her novel was criticized for its open portrayal of lasciviousness.
The film was banned for its scenes of excessive lasciviousness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lewdness - lewd or indecent behavior
  • Antonym: chastity - moral or sexual purity; abstention from sexual activity

Etymology

From Latin lascīvius (wanton, playful) + -ness.
