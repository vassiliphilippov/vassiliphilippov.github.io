foreplay

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrˌpleɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌpleɪ / (BrE)
sexual activity, such as kissing and touching, that happens before sexual intercourse to increase arousal and intimacy.
sexual activity before intercourse

Examples

They spent a long time on foreplay before making love.
Some couples find that foreplay enhances their overall intimacy.
He believes that good foreplay is essential for a healthy sexual relationship.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: intercourse - sexual activity involving penetration

Etymology

From fore- (before) + play, first used in the early 20th century.
