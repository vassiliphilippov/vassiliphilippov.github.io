Fordhooks

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔːrdˌhʊks / (AmE), / ˈfɔːdˌhʊks / (BrE)
A variety of large, flat lima beans, often used as a vegetable.
a type of large lima bean

Examples

We planted Fordhooks in our vegetable garden this spring.
The soup was made with fresh Fordhooks and garden herbs.
I prefer the taste of Fordhooks to other types of lima beans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lima bean - an edible flat white bean

Etymology

Named after the Fordhook Farm, an experimental farm founded by plant breeder W. Atlee Burpee in Pennsylvania, USA.
