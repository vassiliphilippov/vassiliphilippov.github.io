cuspidal

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌsp.ɪ.dəl / (AmE), / ˈkʌs.pɪ.dəl / (BrE)
Having cusps or points; shaped like or relating to a cusp.
having cusps or points

Examples

The mathematician studied a cuspidal curve with several pointed intersections.
In dental anatomy, the canine tooth is classified as cuspidal due to its pointed shape.
The architect admired the cuspidal arches of the Gothic cathedral.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cusp - a pointed end or tip; a projection on a tooth

Etymology

From Latin 'cuspidalis', from 'cuspis' meaning 'point, spear'.
