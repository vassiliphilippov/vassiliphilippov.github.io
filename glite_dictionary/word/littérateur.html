littérateur

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɪt̬.ə.rəˈtɝː / (AmE), / ˌlɪt.ə.ræˈtɜː / (BrE)
A person who writes literary works, especially one who is devoted to literary pursuits.
writer of literary works

Examples

As a renowned littérateur, she was invited to speak at universities around the world.
Many critics regard him not only as a novelist but as a true littérateur.
Her essays established her reputation as a talented littérateur in literary circles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: writer - person who writes books, stories, articles, etc.
  • Synonym: author - a person who writes books or other works

Etymology

From French *littérateur* (man of letters), from Latin *littera* (letter).
