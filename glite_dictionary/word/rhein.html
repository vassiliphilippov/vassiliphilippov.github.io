Rhein

1. PROPN

/ raɪn / (AmE), / raɪn / (BrE)
the German name for the Rhine, a major European river that flows into the North Sea
German name for the Rhine river

Examples

The Rhein is known for its scenic beauty along the Middle Rhine Valley.
Many historic castles line the banks of the Rhein.
The Lorelei rock on the Rhein is famous in German legend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Rhine - a major European river flowing through several countries to the North Sea

Etymology

German 'Rhein', from Latin 'Rhenus', referring to the major European river.
