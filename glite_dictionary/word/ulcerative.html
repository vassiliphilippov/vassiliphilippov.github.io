ulcerative

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌlsɚˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈʌlsərətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by ulcers or ulceration
relating to or marked by ulcers
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with ulcerative colitis after reviewing her symptoms and test results.
Chronic use of certain medications can cause ulcerative lesions in the stomach lining.
Research continues into better treatments for ulcerative diseases affecting the digestive tract.

Synset Examples

ulcerative colitis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ulcer - an open sore on the skin or mucous membrane

Etymology

From Latin 'ulcus' (sore, ulcer) + '-ative' (forming adjectives).
