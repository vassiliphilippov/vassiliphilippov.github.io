Gustavus VI

1. PROPN

/ ɡʊˈstɑːvəs sɪksθ / (AmE), / ɡʊˈstɑːvəs sɪksθ / (BrE)
(1882–1973) King of Sweden from 1950 to 1973, the last monarch to hold significant political power in the country.
King of Sweden (1950–1973)

Examples

Gustavus VI was widely respected for his scholarly interests and passion for archaeology.
During the reign of Gustavus VI, Sweden underwent significant constitutional changes.
Many Swedes remember Gustavus VI as a humble monarch devoted to public service.
