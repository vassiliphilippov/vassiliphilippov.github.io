ladyfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪdiˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈleɪdiˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a slender, silvery game fish resembling a small tarpon, found in warm coastal waters
a slender game fish similar to a small tarpon

Examples

The angler was thrilled to catch a ladyfish on his trip to Florida.
Ladyfish are known for their energetic leaps when hooked.
The biologist studied the migration patterns of the ladyfish along the coast.
