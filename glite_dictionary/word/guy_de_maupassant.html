Guy de Maupassant

1. PROPN

/ ɡi də moʊˌpæsˈɑːnt / (AmE), / ɡi də məʊˈpæsɒ̃ / (BrE)
(1850–1893) A French writer renowned for his short stories and novels, considered a master of the modern short story.
French writer famous for short stories

Examples

Guy de Maupassant is regarded as one of the greatest short story writers in literary history.
Many of Guy de Maupassant's works explore themes of irony and the human condition.
For my literature class, I chose to analyze a short story by Guy de Maupassant.

Etymology

French personal name.
