Victor Emanuel II

1. PROPN

/ ˈvɪktər ɪˈmænjuːəl səˈkənd / (AmE), / ˈvɪktə ɪˈmænjuːəl səˈkənd / (BrE)
(1820–1878) The king of Italy who completed the unification of Italy by acquiring Venice and Rome.
king of Italy who unified the country

Examples

Victor Emanuel II became the first king of a united Italy in 1861.
The acquisition of Venice and Rome was achieved during the reign of Victor Emanuel II.
Many monuments in Italy are dedicated to Victor Emanuel II for his role in the country's unification.
