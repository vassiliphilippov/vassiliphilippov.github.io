choc-ice

1. NOUN

/ tʃɑk aɪs / (AmE), / tʃɒk aɪs / (BrE)
A block of vanilla ice cream coated in a layer of chocolate, typically served as an individual portion.
chocolate-coated ice cream bar
british informal

Examples

She bought a choc-ice from the corner shop to enjoy on the walk home.
Would you like a choc-ice or a regular ice lolly for dessert?
As a child, my favorite treat was a choc-ice on a summer day.

Etymology

Shortening of 'chocolate ice (cream)'.
