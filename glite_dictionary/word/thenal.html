thenal

1. ADJ

/ ˈθiːnəl / (AmE), / ˈθiːnəl / (BrE)
relating to the palm of the hand or to the area at the base of the thumb
of the palm or base of the thumb
medical

Examples

He experienced numbness in the thenal region after the injury.
The doctor examined her thenal muscles for signs of weakness.
Swelling was noted on the patient's thenal eminence.

Etymology

From Greek 'thenar' (palm of the hand, sole of the foot).
