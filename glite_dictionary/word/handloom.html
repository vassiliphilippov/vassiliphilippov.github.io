handloom

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændˌlum / (AmE), / ˈhændˌluːm / (BrE)
a loom that is operated manually rather than by machine power
loom operated by hand

Examples

The artisan worked carefully at the handloom to create intricate patterns in the fabric.
Traditional handloom weaving is still practiced in many parts of the world.
Her business specializes in importing textiles made on a handloom.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: power loom - a loom powered by mechanical means, not manual

Etymology

From 'hand' + 'loom', meaning a device for weaving operated by hand.
