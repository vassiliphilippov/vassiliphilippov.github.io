tie-in

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪ ɪn / (AmE), / ˈtaɪ ɪn / (BrE)
a fastener or device used to join or connect two or more things, especially in construction or engineering
fastener for joining or connecting

Examples

The steel tie-ins are embedded in the concrete to reinforce the structure.
Make sure to secure the cables using appropriate tie-ins for safety.
During the renovation, builders installed new tie-ins to connect the extension to the original wall.

Synset Examples

the walls are held together with metal links placed in the wet mortar during construction

Related Terms

  • Synonym: connector - a device for joining things together
  • Synonym: fastener - a device that joins two or more things together
