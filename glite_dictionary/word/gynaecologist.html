gynaecologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡaɪnəˈkɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɡaɪniˈkɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A doctor who specializes in the medical care of women's reproductive systems and sexual health.
doctor specializing in women's reproductive health
medical british

Examples

She scheduled an appointment with her gynaecologist for her annual checkup.
The gynaecologist explained the results of the test in detail.
Many women feel more comfortable discussing sensitive health issues with a trusted gynaecologist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gynecologist - doctor specializing in women's reproductive health (US spelling)

Etymology

From Greek 'gynē' (woman) + 'logos' (study) via French and Latin.
