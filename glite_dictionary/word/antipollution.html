antipollution

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.ti.pəˈluː.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌæn.ti.pəˈluː.ʃən / (BrE)
designed to prevent or reduce pollution, especially of the environment
designed to reduce pollution

Examples

The government introduced new antipollution regulations for factories last year.
Car manufacturers are developing more antipollution technologies to meet global emission standards.
Using antipollution measures, the city saw a significant improvement in air quality.

Synset Examples

antipollution laws
antipollution devices on automobile exhaust systems

Related Terms

  • Synonym: environmental - concerned with the environment
  • Antonym: polluting - causing pollution

Etymology

From anti- (against) + pollution.
