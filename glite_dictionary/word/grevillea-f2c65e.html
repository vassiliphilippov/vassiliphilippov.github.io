grevillea

1. NOUN

/ ɡrəˈvɪliə / (AmE), / ɡrəˈvɪliə / (BrE)
Any shrub or tree of the genus Grevillea, native mainly to Australia, known for its diverse flowers and foliage.
type of shrub or tree from the genus Grevillea

Examples

The garden features many native plants, including a beautiful grevillea near the entrance.
We planted several grevilleas to attract birds to our backyard.
That grevillea produces bright red flowers throughout the spring.

Etymology

Named after Charles Francis Greville (1749–1809), a British botanist.
