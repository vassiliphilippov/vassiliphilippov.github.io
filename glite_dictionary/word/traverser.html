traverser

1. NOUN

/ trəˈvɜrsɚ / (AmE), / trəˈvɜːsə / (BrE)
a person who moves or passes across something, especially difficult or challenging terrain
person moving across something

Examples

The experienced traverser led the group safely over the frozen river.
Each traverser must assess the risks before attempting the crossing.
As a seasoned mountain traverser, she knew exactly where to find the safest path.

Synset Examples

the traversers slowly ascended the mountain

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traveler - a person who travels or journeys
  • Synonym: cross - to move or go across something; its verb form is a synonym

Etymology

From the verb 'traverse,' from Old French *traverser*, from Vulgar Latin *transversare* (to cross)
