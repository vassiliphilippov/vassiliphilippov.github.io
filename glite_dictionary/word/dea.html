DEA

1. NOUN

/ di i ˈeɪ / (AmE), / diː iː ˈeɪ / (BrE)
the Drug Enforcement Administration, a United States federal agency responsible for enforcing laws related to narcotics and controlled substances
US federal agency on drug enforcement
american

Examples

The DEA launched an investigation into the international drug cartel.
She applied for a job with the DEA as a forensic chemist.
The DEA works closely with local police to fight drug trafficking.

Etymology

Initialism of Drug Enforcement Administration.
