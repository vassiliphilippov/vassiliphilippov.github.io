salwar

1. NOUN

/ sɑːlˈwɑːr / (AmE), / sælˈwɑː / (BrE)
a pair of light, loose-fitting trousers that are tight at the ankles, traditionally worn by women in South Asia, typically with a kameez.
loose-fitting trousers worn in South Asia

Examples

She wore a beautiful blue salwar to the wedding ceremony.
Many stores in the city now sell traditional clothing like the salwar and kameez.
Her mother taught her how to sew her own salwar for festivals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shalwar - alternative spelling of 'salwar', traditional South Asian trousers

Etymology

From Persian *shalwār* (trousers).
