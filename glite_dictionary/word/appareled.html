appareled

1. ADJ

/ əˈpɛrəld / (AmE), / əˈparəld / (BrE)
dressed or clothed, especially in fine or distinctive attire; often used in combination or literary contexts.
dressed or clothed, often finely

Examples

The guests arrived appareled in their finest evening wear for the gala.
She was splendidly appareled in a silk gown and pearls.
Ancient statues are often depicted as appareled in elaborate robes.

Synset Examples

the elegantly attired gentleman
neatly dressed workers
monks garbed in hooded robes
went about oddly garmented
professors robed in crimson
tuxedo-attired gentlemen
crimson-robed Harvard professors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attired - dressed in a particular way, especially referring to special or formal clothing
  • Synonym: garbed - dressed in distinctive or specific clothing

Etymology

From Middle English 'apparel', from Old French 'appareil' (preparation, adornment), from Vulgar Latin *apparicellus.
