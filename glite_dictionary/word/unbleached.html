unbleached

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbliːt͡ʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈbliːtʃt / (BrE)
(of fabric, hair, or other material) not made whiter or lighter in color by the use of chemicals or bleach.
not bleached; untreated by bleach

Examples

The baker prefers to use unbleached flour in his bread recipes.
These towels are made from 100% unbleached cotton.
Her unbleached hair gave her a very natural look.

Synset Examples

unbleached blonde hair
her hair is uncolored
undyed cotton

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bleached - made whiter or lighter in color by bleach

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'bleached', past participle of 'bleach'.
