undying

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈdaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
lasting forever; never dying or ending.
never dying; everlasting

Examples

She expressed her undying gratitude to those who helped her.
His poetry is remembered for its undying beauty and emotion.
They pledged undying love to each other on their wedding day.

Synset Examples

his undying fame

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eternal - lasting or existing forever; without end or beginning
  • Synonym: immortal - living forever; never dying or decaying
  • Antonym: temporary - lasting for only a limited period of time; not permanent

Etymology

From the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' + 'dying', present participle of 'die'.
