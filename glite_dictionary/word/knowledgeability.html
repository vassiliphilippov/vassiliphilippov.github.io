knowledgeability

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɑlɪdʒəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌnɒlɪdʒəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of having extensive or detailed knowledge; the state of being knowledgeable
quality of being knowledgeable

Examples

Her knowledgeability about art history made her an excellent museum guide.
The interviewers were impressed by the candidate's knowledgeability of the subject matter.
We value knowledgeability and curiosity in all our team members.

Synset Examples

his knowledgeability impressed me

Related Terms

Etymology

From 'knowledgeable' + '-ity,' after 'knowledge'.
