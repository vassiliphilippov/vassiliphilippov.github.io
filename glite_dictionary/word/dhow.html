dhow

1. NOUN

/ daʊ / (AmE), / daʊ / (BrE)
A traditional Arab sailing vessel with one or more lateen sails, used in the Indian Ocean.
Arab sailing vessel with lateen sails

Examples

The fishermen set out before dawn in their dhow to catch the morning’s best fish.
We admired the graceful silhouette of a dhow sailing along the Zanzibar coast.
Several dhows participated in the annual regatta, their triangular sails billowing in the wind.

Etymology

From Swahili 'dau', likely of Arabic origin.
