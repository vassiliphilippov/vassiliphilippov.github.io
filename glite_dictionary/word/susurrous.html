susurrous

1. ADJ

/ suˈsɝː.əs / (AmE), / suˈsʊə.rəs / (BrE)
(literary) making or characterized by a soft, whispering, or rustling sound.
making a soft, whispering sound

Examples

The susurrous breeze drifted through the branches, creating a tranquil atmosphere in the garden.
A susurrous whisper passed through the audience as the lights dimmed and the orchestra prepared to play.
She listened to the susurrous hum of conversation that filled the café in the early evening.

Synset Examples

a murmurous brook
a soughing wind in the pines
a slow sad susurrous rustle like the wind fingering the pines

Related Terms

  • Synonym: murmurous - full of or abounding in murmurs; soft and low sound
  • Synonym: rustling - making a soft, muffled crackling sound like that caused by the movement of dry leaves or paper

Etymology

From Latin 'susurrosus' (full of whispering), from 'susurrus' (a whisper).
