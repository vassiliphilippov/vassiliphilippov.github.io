blanquillo

1. NOUN

/ blæŋˈkiːjoʊ / (AmE), / blæŋˈkiːjəʊ / (BrE)
A name for various important marine food fishes of the tilefish family, found chiefly in the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans.
a marine food fish (tilefish)

Examples

Local fishermen caught several blanquillo during the early summer season.
The restaurant featured grilled blanquillo as one of its seafood specialties.
Scientists monitored the blanquillo population to help maintain sustainable fishing practices.

Etymology

From Spanish 'blanquillo', diminutive of 'blanco' meaning 'white', referring to pale coloration of the fish.
