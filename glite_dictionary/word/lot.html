Lot

1. PROPN

/ lɑt / (AmE), / lɒt / (BrE)
(in the Bible) nephew of Abraham, who was spared from the destruction of Sodom and Gomorrah.
biblical nephew of Abraham

Examples

Many biblical stories mention Lot as the nephew of Abraham.
The angels warned Lot to flee from Sodom before its destruction.
Religious scholars often discuss the significance of Lot and his family's escape.

Etymology

From Hebrew לֹוט (Lot), meaning 'veil' or 'covering'.
