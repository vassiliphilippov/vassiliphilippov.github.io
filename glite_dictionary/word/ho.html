Ho

1. NOUN

/ hoʊ / (AmE), / həʊ / (BrE)
the symbol for holmium, a trivalent metallic element of the rare earth group
symbol for holmium (chemical element)

Examples

The element Ho has unusual magnetic properties important for certain scientific applications.
Scientists discovered a trace amount of Ho in the mineral sample.
The periodic table lists Ho with the atomic number 67.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holmium - a soft, silvery-white metallic element of the rare earth group (symbol Ho)
  • Degree Lower: ytterbium - a related rare earth element occurring with holmium in ores
  • Degree Lower: erbium - another rare earth element chemically similar to holmium

Etymology

From the name Holmia, New Latin for Stockholm, where the element was first isolated.
