ho

1. NOUN

/ hoʊ / (AmE), / həʊ / (BrE)
an offensive slang term for a woman who is considered sexually promiscuous
offensive slang; sexually promiscuous woman
slang offensive american

Examples

He was criticized for calling her a ho in his song lyrics.
Some comedians use the word ho, but it's offensive to many listeners.
The term ho is often heard in rap music, though it's widely condemned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whore - offensive term for a prostitute or sexually promiscuous woman
  • Synonym: prostitute - a person who engages in sexual activity for payment

Etymology

Variant of 'whore', influenced by African American Vernacular English pronunciation.
