intermittently

1. ADV

/ ˌɪn.tɚˈmɪ.tənt.li / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈmɪt.ənt.li / (BrE)
In a manner that stops and starts at irregular intervals; not continuously.
at irregular intervals; sporadically

Examples

The signal was lost intermittently throughout the night due to bad weather.
She worked intermittently on her novel over several years before finally finishing it.
Rain fell intermittently all afternoon, making it hard to plan outdoor activities.

Synset Examples

intermittently we questioned the barometer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sporadically - at irregular intervals; occasionally
  • Antonym: continuously - without interruption; steadily

Etymology

From 'intermittent' + '-ly', ultimately from Latin 'intermittere' (to interrupt).
