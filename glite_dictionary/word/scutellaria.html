Scutellaria

1. NOUN

/ skuːtəˈlɛriə / (AmE), / skjuːtəˈlɛːrɪə / (BrE)
a genus of flowering plants in the mint family, commonly known as skullcaps, which includes several species used in herbal medicine
genus of skullcap plants

Examples

Several species of Scutellaria are known for their medicinal properties and have been used in traditional remedies.
The blue flowers of Scutellaria brighten the edges of the woodland garden every spring.
Researchers are studying extracts from Scutellaria to evaluate their potential health benefits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skullcap - common name for plants in the genus Scutellaria

Etymology

From Latin 'scutella' (a small dish or shield), referring to the shape of the flower's calyx.
