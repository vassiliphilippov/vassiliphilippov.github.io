abattoir

1. NOUN

/ ˈæbəˌtwɑr / (AmE), / ˈæbətwɑː / (BrE)
A building where animals are slaughtered for meat.
place where animals are slaughtered

Examples

The old abattoir was closed due to health concerns.
Cattle were transported to the abattoir early in the morning.
There are strict regulations for hygiene in every abattoir.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slaughterhouse - a place where animals are killed for their meat

Etymology

From French abattoir (slaughterhouse), from abattre (to strike down).
