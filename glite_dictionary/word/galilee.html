Galilee

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡæləˌli / (AmE), / ˈɡæləliː / (BrE)
a region in northern Israel, formerly known as the northern part of Palestine and the ancient kingdom of Israel, famed as the setting for much of Jesus's ministry
region in northern Israel, site of Jesus's ministry

Examples

Many Christians travel to Galilee to visit the historic sites mentioned in the Bible.
The Sea of Galilee is renowned for its beauty and religious significance.
Archaeologists have uncovered ancient villages in Galilee dating back to Roman times.

Etymology

From Latin Galilaea, from Ancient Greek Galilaía, from Hebrew גליל (Galil), meaning 'district, region'.
