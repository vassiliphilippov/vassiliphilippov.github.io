Cinnamomum loureirii

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪnəməˌmʊm luːˈreɪriˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˌsɪnəməˌmʊm luːˈreəriːaɪ / (BrE)
a tropical southeast Asian tree with aromatic bark, the bark of which is used medicinally
a southeast Asian tree with medicinal aromatic bark

Examples

Extracts from Cinnamomum loureirii are used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
The aromatic bark of Cinnamomum loureirii is valued in herbal remedies across Southeast Asia.
Researchers studied the chemical properties of Cinnamomum loureirii for potential health benefits.

Etymology

From Latin, with 'Cinnamomum' meaning cinnamon and 'loureirii' honoring the botanist João de Loureiro.
