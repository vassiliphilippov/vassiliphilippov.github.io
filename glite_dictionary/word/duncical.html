duncical

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʌnsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈdʌnsɪkəl / (BrE)
(informal) Stupid or foolish; resembling a dunce.
stupid; like a dunce
informal

Examples

His duncical mistake cost the team the entire match.
The professor dismissed the theory as utterly duncical.
Don't be so duncical—think before you act!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stupid - lacking intelligence or common sense
  • Synonym: dunce - a person who is slow to learn or acts foolishly
  • Antonym: intelligent - having or showing intelligence, especially of a high level

Etymology

From 'dunce' (a slow-witted person) + '-ical' (adjectival suffix).
