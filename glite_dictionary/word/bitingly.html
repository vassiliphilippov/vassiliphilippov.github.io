bitingly

1. ADV

/ ˈbaɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbaɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
in an extremely and sharply intense way; causing a sharp sensation, as of cold, criticism, or wit
in an extremely sharp or intense way

Examples

The wind was bitingly cold as we stepped outside the cabin.
She responded bitingly to his foolish remarks, silencing the room.
The critic wrote a bitingly honest review of the new play.

Synset Examples

it was bitterly cold
bitter cold

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bitterly - in an intense or sharply painful way

Etymology

From 'biting' + '-ly', with 'biting' from Old English 'bītan' (to bite).
