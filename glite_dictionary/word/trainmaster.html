trainmaster

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪnˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a manager responsible for operations and supervision within a railway yard
supervisor in a railway yard

Examples

The trainmaster coordinated all the switching operations at the busy freight yard.
After the derailment, the trainmaster ensured that safety protocols were strictly followed.
Every new employee in the yard receives orientation from the trainmaster.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yardmaster - a supervisor in charge of a railroad yard

Etymology

Formed from 'train' (railway vehicle) + 'master' (person in charge).
