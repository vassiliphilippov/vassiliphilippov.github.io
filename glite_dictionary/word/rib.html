rib

1. NOUN

/ rɪb / (AmE), / rɪb / (BrE)
a supporting or strengthening part resembling an anatomical rib in an animal or plant
a riblike supporting part

Examples

The cactus's fleshy ribs help it store water efficiently.
Some leaves have a central rib that provides structural support.
The fish's rib bones were clearly visible in the X-ray.

Etymology

From Old English *ribb*, related to German *Rippe* and Dutch *rib*, referring to the anatomical structure.
