loverlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈlʌvərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈlʌvəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
behaving in the manner of a lover; showing affection or tenderness typically associated with a lover
in the manner of a lover

Examples

He greeted her with a loverlike tenderness that surprised everyone in the room.
Her words had a loverlike softness, full of warmth and devotion.
They exchanged glances that were unmistakably loverlike under the dim lights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affectionate - showing fondness or tenderness
  • Antonym: aloof - not friendly or forthcoming; cool and distant
