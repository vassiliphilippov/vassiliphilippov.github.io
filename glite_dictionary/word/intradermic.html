intradermic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntrəˈdɜrmɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈdɜːmɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or situated between the layers of the skin.
situated between the skin layers
medical

Examples

The nurse administered an intradermic injection into the patient's forearm.
An intradermic test is used to check for certain allergic reactions.
Researchers observed swelling and redness at the intradermic site after the procedure.

Synset Examples

an intradermal injection

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intradermal - relating to or situated within the layers of the skin

Etymology

From Latin 'intra-' (within) + Greek 'derma' (skin) + English '-ic'.
