sheika

1. NOUN

/ ʃeɪˈkɑː / (AmE), / ʃeɪˈkɑː / (BrE)
the wife of a sheik, especially in Middle Eastern contexts.
wife of a sheik

Examples

The sheika greeted the guests with grace and dignity at the palace entrance.
As the sheika of the tribe, she played an important role in community events.
He introduced his wife as the sheika during the diplomatic ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sheik - an Arab chief or leader

Etymology

From Arabic شيخة (shaykha), feminine of شيخ (sheikh).
