unbendable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbɛndəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɛndəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be influenced, changed, or made weaker; showing firm determination or resolution
not able to be influenced or changed; resolute

Examples

Her unbendable will helped her overcome many obstacles in her career.
The committee admired his unbendable stance on ethical issues.
Despite pressure from his peers, his unbendable principles kept him on the right path.

Synset Examples

firm convictions
a firm mouth
steadfast resolve
a man of unbendable perseverance
unwavering loyalty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resolute - admirably purposeful, determined, and unwavering
  • Synonym: unyielding - not giving way to pressure; firm or inflexible
  • Antonym: flexible - willing or able to change; adaptable

Etymology

From un- (not) + bend (to yield) + -able.
