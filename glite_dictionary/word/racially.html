racially

1. ADV

/ ˈreɪʃəli / (AmE), / ˈreɪʃəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or is based on race
in relation to race

Examples

The school became racially integrated in the 1960s.
The study examines racially motivated incidents in urban areas.
His comments were considered racially insensitive by many listeners.

Synset Examples

racially integrated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racial - relating to race

Etymology

From 'racial' + '-ly', where 'racial' is from race (people of common ancestry) + -al (forming adjectives).
