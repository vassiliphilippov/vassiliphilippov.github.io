professionally

1. ADV

/ prəˈfɛʃənəli / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃənəli / (BrE)
In a professional manner; in a way that shows skill, competence, or standards expected of a profession.
in a professional manner

Examples

The tasks were completed professionally and ahead of schedule.
She handled the client's complaint professionally, leaving everyone impressed.
All staff are required to dress and behave professionally at all times.

Synset Examples

professionally trained staff

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unprofessionally - in an unprofessional manner

Etymology

From professional (adj.) + -ly (adverb suffix).
