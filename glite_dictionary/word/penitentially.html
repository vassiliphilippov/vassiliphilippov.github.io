penitentially

1. ADV

/ ˌpɛnɪˈtɛnʃəli / (AmE), / ˌpɛnɪˈtɛnʃəli / (BrE)
In a manner expressing or showing remorse or penitence.
in a remorseful or penitent manner

Examples

He bowed his head penitentially after realizing his mistake.
She penitentially apologized to everyone she had wronged.
He walked penitentially to the front of the room to confess his error.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remorsefully - in a manner filled with remorse
  • Antonym: defiantly - in a manner showing resistance or bold disobedience

Etymology

From 'penitential' + '-ly'; 'penitential' from Latin 'paenitentia' (repentance).
