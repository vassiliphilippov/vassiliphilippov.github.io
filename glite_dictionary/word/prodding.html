prodding

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɒdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of encouraging or urging someone to do something, especially repeatedly or persistently
persistent verbal encouragement or urging

Examples

He achieved his goals through his mentor's constant prodding.
Without my friend's prodding, I might never have applied for the job.
After much prodding from her family, she finally visited the doctor.

Synset Examples

the ceaseless prodding got on his nerves

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urging - the act of strongly encouraging someone to do something
  • Synonym: encouragement - words or actions that give someone confidence to do something

Etymology

From the verb 'prod', meaning to poke or urge, plus the noun-forming suffix '-ing'.
