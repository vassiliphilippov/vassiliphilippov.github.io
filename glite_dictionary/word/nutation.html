nutation

1. NOUN

/ nuˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / njuːˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
an involuntary or uncontrolled nodding movement, especially of the head, often due to a medical condition
involuntary nodding movement
medical

Examples

The patient exhibited frequent nutation of the head when attempting to sit up.
Certain neurological disorders can cause nutation as a symptom.
Doctors observed a persistent nutation during the examination.

Etymology

From Latin 'nutatio' (nodding), from 'nutare' (to nod).
