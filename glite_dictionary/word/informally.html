informally

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈfɔrməli / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɔːməli / (BrE)
in a way that uses colloquial or everyday language rather than formal language
in a colloquial or casual way

Examples

He greeted everyone informally, using first names and jokes.
The new policy was informally announced at the company picnic before the official email went out.
She explained the rules informally, making everyone feel at ease.

Synset Examples

this building is colloquially referred to as The Barn

Related Terms

  • Antonym: formally - in a way that follows established conventions or formal language

2. ADV

/ ɪnˈfɔrməli / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɔːməli / (BrE)
in a relaxed or unofficial manner; without strict formality
in a relaxed or unofficial manner

Examples

She greeted her guests informally, wearing jeans and a sweater.
The meeting was conducted informally over coffee in the lounge.
Students were allowed to address the professor informally during the seminar.

Synset Examples

he visited us informally

Related Terms

  • Antonym: formally - in a formal or official manner
