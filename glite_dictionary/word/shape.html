SHAPE

1. PROPN

/ ʃeɪp / (AmE), / ʃeɪp / (BrE)
the Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe; the principal military headquarters responsible for advising NATO on military matters and overseeing Allied Command Europe.
NATO's main military headquarters in Europe

Examples

The annual summit was held at SHAPE near Mons, Belgium.
She was appointed to a senior strategic role at SHAPE in 2019.
Reports from SHAPE indicated a shift in NATO's operational priorities.

Etymology

Acronym from Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe.
