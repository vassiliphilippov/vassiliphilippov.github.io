massively

1. ADV

/ ˈmæsɪvli / (AmE), / ˈmasɪvli / (BrE)
To a very great degree; extremely or on a large scale.
to a great or large extent

Examples

The stadium was massively crowded for the championship game.
This technology has massively improved our ability to process data.
She was massively disappointed when the event was canceled.

Synset Examples

tonight the haddock were shoaling massively in three hundred fathoms

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enormously - to a very great degree; extremely
  • Synonym: hugely - to a great degree; extremely

Etymology

From 'massive' + '-ly', where 'massive' derives from Middle French 'massif'.
