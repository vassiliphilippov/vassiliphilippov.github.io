scuffer

1. NOUN

/ ˈskʌfər / (AmE), / ˈskʌfə / (BrE)
a lightweight, flexible sandal with a sturdy sole, worn as play shoes by children and as sportswear by adults
lightweight, flexible sandal or play shoe

Examples

He bought his daughter a new pair of scuffers for summer playtime in the park.
Many adults choose scuffers for comfort when participating in casual sports.
The worn-out scuffers were still her favorite shoes for gardening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sandal - open type of footwear, consisting of a sole and straps
