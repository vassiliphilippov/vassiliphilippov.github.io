south-east

1. ADV

/ saʊθˈist / (AmE), / saʊθˈiːst / (BrE)
In, towards, or into the southeast direction.
in or towards the southeast

Examples

The birds migrated south-east for the winter to find warmer climates.
We drove south-east from the city until we reached the coastline.
Clouds moved south-east across the sky, bringing a change in weather.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: northeast - in or toward the northeast direction
  • Antonym: north-west - in or toward the northwest direction
  • Antonym: south-west - in or toward the southwest direction
