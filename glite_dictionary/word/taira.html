taira

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪrə / (AmE), / ˈtaɪərə / (BrE)
a long-tailed, tree-dwelling mammal of the weasel family found in Central and South America
a long-tailed mustelid of Central/South America

Examples

The taira is rarely seen during the day, as it is most active at dusk and dawn.
Farmers in the rainforest sometimes spot a taira hunting rodents in the trees.
Researchers are studying how the taira adapts to fragmented forest environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tayra - alternative spelling for the same animal
