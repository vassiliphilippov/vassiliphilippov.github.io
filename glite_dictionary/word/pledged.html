pledged

1. ADJ

/ plɛdʒd / (AmE), / plɛdʒd / (BrE)
bound by or as if by an oath or formal promise; committed or sworn to a cause or action
bound by a formal promise or oath

Examples

She remained pledged to uphold the organization's ethical standards.
The two leaders are pledged to work together toward peace.
Once pledged to secrecy, the members never revealed the plan.

Synset Examples

according to an early tradition became his sworn brother
sworn enemies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sworn - bound by having made a formal oath

Etymology

From pledge + -ed, meaning someone or something that has given a pledge or been pledged.
