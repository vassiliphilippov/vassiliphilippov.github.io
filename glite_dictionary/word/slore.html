slore

1. NOUN

/ slɔːr / (AmE), / slɔː / (BrE)
An offensive slang term for a woman regarded as promiscuous or sexually indiscriminate.
offensive slang for promiscuous woman
slang offensive

Examples

You should never call someone a slore; it's disrespectful and offensive.
Some people online use the term slore, but it's considered highly inappropriate.
He was criticized for referring to his classmate as a slore in the conversation.

Synset Examples

People should avoid using the word slore, as it's offensive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slut - a woman who is considered to have many casual sexual partners
