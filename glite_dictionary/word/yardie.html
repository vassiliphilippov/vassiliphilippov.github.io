yardie

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɑːrdi / (AmE), / ˈjɑːdi / (BrE)
A member of a criminal gang of Jamaican origin, especially one involved in drug trafficking and violence.
Jamaican gang member, especially in drugs/crime

Examples

The police arrested a notorious yardie suspected of leading the drug ring.
Many communities fear the influence of yardies due to their violent activities.
He was rumored to have connections with yardie gangs in London.

Synset Examples

A much publicized raid on a yardie stronghold had first been simulated at Riot City

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gangster - a member of an organized criminal group
  • Synonym: criminal - a person who commits crimes

Etymology

From Jamaican English, originally referring to people from 'government yards' (public housing) in Kingston, Jamaica.
