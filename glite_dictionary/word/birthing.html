birthing

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɜrðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of giving birth to a baby
the process of giving birth

Examples

The midwife assisted with the birthing process in the calm, quiet room.
She attended several birthing classes to prepare for her baby's arrival.
Many hospitals now offer specialized birthing suites to provide comfort during labor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: childbirth - the act or process of giving birth to a baby
  • Synonym: labor - the physical process of giving birth

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'birth' + '-ing' suffix.
