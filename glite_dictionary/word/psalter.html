Psalter

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔltər / (AmE), / ˈsɔːltə / (BrE)
a book containing the Book of Psalms, often arranged for liturgical or devotional use
book of Psalms for worship

Examples

The medieval manuscript featured a beautifully illustrated Psalter used by monks during prayer.
She inherited her grandmother's Psalter, which had been passed down for generations.
During the service, the choir chanted verses from the Psalter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Psalms - a sacred song or hymn, specifically those collected in the biblical Book of Psalms

Etymology

From Old English 'psalter', via Latin 'psalterium', from Greek 'psaltērion' (stringed instrument, later Psalms book).
