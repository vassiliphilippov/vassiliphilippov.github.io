Culebra

1. PROPN

/ kuˈleɪbrə / (AmE), / kuˈleɪbrə / (BrE)
a small island off the coast of Puerto Rico, known for its beautiful beaches and clear waters
island off Puerto Rico

Examples

Many tourists visit Culebra every year to enjoy its pristine beaches.
She took a ferry to Culebra for a weekend getaway.
The coral reefs surrounding Culebra are popular with snorkelers and divers.

Etymology

From Spanish 'culebra' meaning 'snake', likely referring to the shape of the island.
