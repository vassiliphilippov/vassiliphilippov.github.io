to pinkify

1. VERB

/ ˈpɪŋ.kə.faɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋ.kɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
to make or become pink in color
make (something) pink

Examples

The artist decided to pinkify the mural to brighten up the street.
Some brands pinkify their products during Breast Cancer Awareness Month.
She pinkified her old sneakers with a fresh coat of paint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to redden - to make or become red
  • Antonym: to decolorize - to remove color from

Etymology

Formed from 'pink' + '-ify', following the pattern of verbs like 'beautify'.
