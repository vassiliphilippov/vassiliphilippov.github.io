uplifting

1. NOUN

/ ʌpˈlɪftɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌpˈlɪftɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of being raised or rising upward.
act of rising or being raised

Examples

The sudden uplifting of the earth's surface was caused by tectonic movement.
The uplifting of the fog made the surrounding mountains visible by noon.
Scientists studied the uplifting of the coastline after the earthquake.

Synset Examples

the uplifting of the clouds revealed the blue of a summer sky

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ascent - the act of rising or climbing upward
  • Antonym: subsidence - the sinking or lowering of something

Etymology

From the verb 'uplift' plus the suffix '-ing', forming a noun for the action or result.
