summer-blooming

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌmɚ ˌbluːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə ˌbluːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of plants) producing flowers during the summer season.
producing flowers in summer

Examples

These summer-blooming perennials bring vibrant color to the garden.
We planted several summer-blooming shrubs along the fence last year.
The summer-blooming lilies attract a variety of butterflies.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: winter-blooming - (of plants) producing flowers during the winter season
