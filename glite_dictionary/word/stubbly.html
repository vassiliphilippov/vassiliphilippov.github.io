stubbly

1. ADJ

/ ˈstʌbli / (AmE), / ˈstʌbli / (BrE)
(of hair, especially on the face) having a short, rough, and bristly growth, like stubble.
having short, rough hair growth

Examples

He ran his hand over his stubbly chin, thinking he needed a shave.
Her stubbly legs scratched against the sheets after just one day without shaving.
The stubbly growth on his face gave him a rugged look.

Synset Examples

his stubbled chin

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clean-shaven - having no facial hair; completely shaven
  • Degree Higher: bearded - having a beard; facial hair grown long
  • Synonym: stubble - short, rough hair left after shaving; also dry stalks of grain left after the harvest

Etymology

Derived from 'stubble' (short stalks left after crops are cut) + -y (adjectival suffix).
