annexational

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.ɛkˈseɪ.ʃən.əl / (AmE), / ˌæn.ɪkˈseɪ.ʃən.əl / (BrE)
Relating to or involving annexation; concerning the act of incorporating a territory into another political entity.
relating to annexation

Examples

Several countries condemned the annexational policies pursued by the regime.
The treaty was rejected due to its annexational implications for neighboring regions.
His report outlined the economic impact of the annexational process on local communities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annexation - the act of adding or attaching, especially territory to a country

Etymology

From annexation + -al.
