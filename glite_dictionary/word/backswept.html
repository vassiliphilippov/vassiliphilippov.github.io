backswept

1. ADJ

/ ˈbækˌswɛpt / (AmE), / ˈbækˌswɛpt / (BrE)
(of hair) brushed or styled so that it is swept toward the back of the head.
hair brushed toward the back

Examples

Her backswept hair added elegance to her evening look.
He wore his backswept hair under a stylish hat.
The model's backswept hairstyle highlighted her facial features perfectly.

Etymology

From 'back' + 'swept', past participle of 'sweep'.
