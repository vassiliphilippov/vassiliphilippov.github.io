Lesotho

1. PROPN

/ lɛˈsoʊθoʊ / (AmE), / lɛˈsəʊtəʊ / (BrE)
a landlocked country in southern Africa, an independent constitutional monarchy since 1966
country in southern Africa

Examples

Tourists are drawn to Lesotho for its dramatic mountain landscapes and rich culture.
After gaining independence in 1966, Lesotho established itself as a constitutional monarchy.
The river that originates in Lesotho supplies water to much of South Africa.

Etymology

From the Sesotho language, meaning 'land of the Sesotho-speaking people.'
