speedboat

1. NOUN

/ ˈspiːdˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈspiːdbəʊt / (BrE)
a small, fast motorboat designed for racing or fast travel
a fast motorboat

Examples

We hired a speedboat to tour the coastal islands.
The speedboat zipped across the lake, leaving a trail of waves behind.
She dreamed of owning a red speedboat one day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motorboat - a boat powered by an engine rather than sails or oars

Etymology

From 'speed' (fastness) + 'boat'.
