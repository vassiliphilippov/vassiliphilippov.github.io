ux.

1. NOUN

/ ʌks / (AmE), / ʌks / (BrE)
(in legal contexts) wife; used chiefly as an abbreviation for the Latin 'uxor' (wife) in legal documents.
legal abbreviation for wife
legal

Examples

The deed listed John Smith and his ux. as the property's new owners.
In early court records, 'John Doe et ux.' refers to John Doe and his wife.
Some genealogical documents use ux. to denote the wife of a male ancestor.

Etymology

From Latin 'uxor' (wife).
