Felis chaus

1. NOUN

/ ˌfiːlɪs ˈkaʊs / (AmE), / ˌfiːlɪs ˈkaʊs / (BrE)
A small wildcat found in Asia, also known as the jungle cat, with a yellowish-gray or reddish-brown coat.
small Asian wildcat (jungle cat)

Examples

The Felis chaus is often spotted near wetlands and reed beds in its natural habitat.
Zoologists studied the behavior of Felis chaus in protected reserves across India.
With its slender build and long legs, the Felis chaus stands out from other wildcats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jungle cat - another name for Felis chaus, a small Asian wildcat

Etymology

From Latin; 'Felis' meaning cat and 'chaus' from Greek 'χαῦς' (chaûs), meaning wildcat.
