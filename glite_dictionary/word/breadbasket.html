breadbasket

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛdˌbæskɪt / (AmE), / ˈbrɛdˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
a basket used for holding and serving bread at a meal
basket for serving bread

Examples

The waiter placed a breadbasket full of warm rolls in the center of the table.
She bought a woven breadbasket for their family dinners.
Please pass the breadbasket so I can have another piece of baguette.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛdˌbæskɪt / (AmE), / ˈbrɛdˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
(informal) The stomach; the main muscular organ of digestion in the body.
informal term for the stomach
informal slang

Examples

After eating too much pizza, my breadbasket was aching for hours.
He took a hard hit right in the breadbasket during the game.
She laughed so hard her breadbasket hurt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stomach - the organ in the body where food begins to be digested

Etymology

From bread + basket, likening the organ to a basket where food (bread) is kept.

3. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛdˌbæskət / (AmE), / ˈbrɛdˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
a geographic region that supplies most of the grain for a country or area
region that supplies most of the grain

Examples

The Midwest is often called the breadbasket of America because of its vast grain production.
Ukraine has long been known as the breadbasket of Europe.
Drought threatened the region’s status as the nation’s breadbasket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: granary - a place or region where grain is stored or produced

Etymology

From 'bread' + 'basket', referring metaphorically to a region producing staple food crops.
