cakehole

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪkˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈkeɪkˌhəʊl / (BrE)
(informal, British) the mouth, often used humorously or as a mild insult.
slang for mouth
informal slang british

Examples

Shut your cakehole and listen for a minute.
He stuffed a big piece of pie into his cakehole.
She couldn't stop laughing, her cakehole wide open.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mouth - the opening in the face used for eating and speaking

Etymology

British slang, from 'cake' (sweet baked food) + 'hole'.
