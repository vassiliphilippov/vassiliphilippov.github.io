porno

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔrnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɔːnəʊ / (BrE)
Creative works such as books, pictures, or films intended primarily to arouse sexual desire, typically considered to lack artistic or literary value.
sexually explicit material
informal slang

Examples

He found a stash of old porno magazines in the attic.
Some people argue that porno has a negative impact on society.
The bookstore removed all porno from its shelves after the change in ownership.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pornography - sexually explicit material intended to arouse sexual desire

Etymology

Shortened form of 'pornography', from Greek 'pornographos' (writing about prostitutes).
