water-washed

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɔtər ˌwɑʃt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌwɒʃt / (BrE)
washed or swept by water, especially by waves of the sea
washed by water or waves

Examples

The water-washed pebbles gleamed in the sunlight along the shore.
Ancient, water-washed stones littered the riverbed after the flood.
The cliff face was smooth and water-washed from centuries of crashing waves.
