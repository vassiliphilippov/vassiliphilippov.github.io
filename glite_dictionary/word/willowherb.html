willowherb

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪloʊˌhɜrb / (AmE), / ˈwɪləʊˌhɜːb / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Epilobium, typically having pink or yellow flowers with seeds covered in silky hairs.
plant of the genus Epilobium

Examples

The hedgerow was full of blooming willowherb in early summer.
Many species of willowherb thrive in disturbed soils and open spaces.
The silky seeds of the willowherb floated away on the breeze.

Etymology

From 'willow' (tree of the genus Salix) and 'herb', possibly in reference to the leaves' resemblance to willow leaves.
