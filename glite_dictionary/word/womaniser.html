womaniser

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊm.əˌnaɪ.zɚ / (AmE), / ˈwʊm.əˌnaɪ.zə / (BrE)
a man who has numerous short sexual relationships with women, often considered unfaithful or insincere
a man with many short sexual relationships

Examples

He earned a reputation as a womaniser because of his many brief affairs.
People began to gossip about his past as a womaniser in the small town.
She warned her friend to stay away from him, calling him a notorious womaniser.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: philanderer - a man who frequently enters into casual sexual relationships with women
  • Antonym: faithful - remaining loyal to one partner or spouse

Etymology

From 'woman' + '-iser', patterned after similar agent nouns.
