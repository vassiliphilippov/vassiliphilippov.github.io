marlberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrlˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈmɑːlˌbɛri / (BrE)
A tropical American shrub or small tree with brown wood and dark berries, commonly found in coastal areas.
tropical American shrub with dark berries

Examples

The marlberry thrives in sandy soils near the coast.
Gardeners choose the marlberry for its attractive dark fruits and foliage.
Wildlife often feed on the berries produced by the marlberry tree.
