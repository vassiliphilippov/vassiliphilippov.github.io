indescribably

1. ADV

/ ˌɪndɪˈskraɪbəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈskraɪbəbli / (BrE)
to a degree that cannot be described; extremely or inexpressibly
to an inexpressible degree; extremely

Examples

The view from the mountaintop was indescribably beautiful at sunrise.
She felt indescribably happy when she heard the news.
The dessert was indescribably delicious, unlike anything I had tasted before.

Synset Examples

she was looking very young tonight, and, as usual, indescribably beautiful, in a simple strapless dress of a green and white silky cotton

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inexpressibly - in a manner that cannot be expressed; extremely
  • Synonym: unutterably - to an extent that cannot be uttered; extremely

Etymology

From 'indescribable' + '-ly'; 'indescribable' from in- (not) + describable.
