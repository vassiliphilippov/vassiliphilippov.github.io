wellborn

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛlˌbɔrn / (AmE), / ˈwɛlˌbɔːn / (BrE)
Of noble or upper-class birth or lineage.
of noble or upper-class birth

Examples

She came from a wellborn family known throughout the city for their philanthropy.
In the novel, the protagonist falls in love with a wellborn gentleman despite her modest background.
The wellborn children attended the most prestigious schools in the country.

Synset Examples

a rich and wellborn husband

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noble - of high social or political status by birth
  • Antonym: common - lacking noble or upper-class birth

Etymology

From 'well-' (good) + 'born'.
