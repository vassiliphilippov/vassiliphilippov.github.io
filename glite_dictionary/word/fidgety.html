fidgety

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪdʒɪti / (AmE), / ˈfɪdʒɪti / (BrE)
unable to stay still or relax because of nervousness or restlessness.
unable to stay still due to nervousness

Examples

The children grew fidgety after sitting in the waiting room for an hour.
I felt fidgety all day before the big presentation.
He became fidgety when asked to wait in silence.

Synset Examples

a constant fretful stamping of hooves
a restless child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restless - unable to rest or relax, constantly in motion
  • Antonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness or restlessness

Etymology

From 'fidget' (to move restlessly), + -y.
