France

1. PROPN

/ fræns / (AmE), / frɑːns / (BrE)
A republic in Western Europe; the largest country wholly located in Europe.
country in Western Europe

Examples

Many tourists visit France each year to see the Eiffel Tower and try authentic French cuisine.
After studying abroad in France, she became fluent in French.
Wine from France is famous for its high quality and variety.

Etymology

From Latin 'Francia', meaning 'land of the Franks'.
