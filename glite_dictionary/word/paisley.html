paisley

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪzli / (AmE), / ˈpeɪzli / (BrE)
A soft wool or cotton fabric with a colorful pattern of swirling, curved shapes, known as paisley.
fabric with colorful swirled pattern

Examples

She wore a scarf with a beautiful paisley design to the party.
The curtains in the living room featured a bold paisley print.
His tie, covered in paisley patterns, stood out at the meeting.

Etymology

From the town of Paisley in Scotland, where such textiles were produced.
