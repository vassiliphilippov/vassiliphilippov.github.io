zymology

1. NOUN

/ zaɪˈmɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / zaɪˈmɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of fermentation processes, especially as used in brewing, winemaking, or distilling
study of fermentation processes

Examples

He decided to pursue a career in zymology after developing an interest in brewing craft beers.
Modern zymology has led to significant improvements in the wine industry.
Her research in zymology focuses on the microbes that drive fermentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fermentation - chemical breakdown of substances, especially in brewing or winemaking

Etymology

From Greek *zymē* (leaven) + *-logy* (study of).
