Tai Nuea

1. NOUN

/ taɪ ˈnjuː.ə / (AmE), / taɪ ˈnjuː.ə / (BrE)
a Tai language spoken primarily in Southwest Yunnan Province, China, including Dehong Dai and Jingpo autonomous prefectures, and nearby areas
Tai language of Southwest Yunnan, China

Examples

The Tai Nuea language is mainly spoken in the Dehong region of Yunnan Province.
Linguists have studied how Tai Nuea differs from other Tai languages.
He is fluent in both Mandarin and Tai Nuea.
