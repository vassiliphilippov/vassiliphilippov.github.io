pulchritudinous

1. ADJ

/ ˌpʌl.krɪˈtuː.dɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˌpʌl.krɪˈtjuː.dɪ.nəs / (BrE)
(of a person) very physically beautiful or attractive.
physically beautiful

Examples

The magazine featured several pulchritudinous models on its cover.
Despite his pulchritudinous appearance, he remained modest and kind-hearted.
She entered the room with a pulchritudinous smile that captivated everyone.

Synset Examples

pulchritudinous movie stars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beautiful - having qualities that give great pleasure or satisfaction to see; physically attractive
  • Antonym: ugly - unpleasant or repulsive in appearance

Etymology

From Latin pulchritudo (beauty), from pulcher (beautiful).
