realized

1. ADJ

/ ˈriəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈrɪəlaɪzd / (BrE)
(of ambitions, dreams, plans, etc.) successfully completed or brought into reality; achieved.
achieved or brought into reality

Examples

She felt a sense of pride with her realized ambition to become a doctor.
The museum is the realized vision of its founder, now open to the public.
After years of hard work, their realized dream was celebrated by family and friends.

Synset Examples

his mission accomplished, he took a vacation
the completed project
the joy of a realized ambition overcame him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fulfilled - brought to completion or satisfied
  • Antonym: unrealized - not achieved or not brought to completion

Etymology

From the verb 'realize', from French réaliser (to make real), from 'réel' (real).
