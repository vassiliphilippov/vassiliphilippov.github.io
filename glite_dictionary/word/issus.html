Issus

1. PROPN

/ ˈaɪ.səs / (AmE), / ˈɪs.əs / (BrE)
a major battle in 333 BC where Alexander the Great defeated the Persian army of Darius III
ancient battle between Alexander and the Persians

Examples

The Battle of Issus marked a turning point in Alexander the Great's campaign against Persia.
Historians often study the tactics used at Issus to understand ancient warfare.
A famous mosaic in Pompeii depicts the confrontation between Alexander and Darius at Issus.
