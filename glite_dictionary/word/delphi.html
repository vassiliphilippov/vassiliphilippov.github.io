Delphi

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɛlfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈdɛlfi / (BrE)
an ancient Greek city located on the slopes of Mount Parnassus, famous as the site of the oracle of Delphi, where prophecies were delivered to seekers by the priestess of Apollo.
ancient Greek city and oracle site

Examples

The ruins of Delphi attract thousands of tourists every year.
In ancient Greece, kings and commoners alike traveled to Delphi to consult the oracle.
Archaeologists have uncovered remarkable artifacts at Delphi dating back to the 8th century BCE.

Etymology

From Ancient Greek 'Δελφοί' (Delphoí), the name of the city.
