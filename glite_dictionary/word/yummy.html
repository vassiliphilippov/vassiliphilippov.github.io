yummy

1. ADJ

/ ˈjʌmi / (AmE), / ˈjʌmi / (BrE)
(informal) Very tasty or delicious; extremely pleasing to the sense of taste.
very tasty; delicious
informal

Examples

The chocolate cake was so yummy that everyone asked for seconds.
She made a yummy pasta dish for dinner last night.
Those strawberries look really yummy—I can't wait to try them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delicious - highly pleasing to the taste
  • Synonym: tasty - having a pleasant flavor
  • Antonym: disgusting - extremely unpleasant, especially to the taste or smell
