Swaziland

1. PROPN

/ ˈswɑːziˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈswɑːzɪlænd / (BrE)
the former name of Eswatini, a landlocked monarchy in southeastern Africa, which gained independence from the United Kingdom in 1968
former name of Eswatini, African country

Examples

My grandparents visited Swaziland before it was renamed Eswatini.
The independence of Swaziland in 1968 marked an important moment in African history.
He researched the political changes in Swaziland during the 20th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Eswatini - current official name of Swaziland

Etymology

From Swazi (the name of the predominant ethnic group) + land.
