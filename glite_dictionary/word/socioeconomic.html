socioeconomic

1. ADJ

/ ˌsoʊ.si.oʊˌɛk.əˈnɑm.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌsəʊ.si.əʊˌiː.kəˈnɒm.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with the interaction of social and economic factors.
relating to social and economic factors

Examples

The local government's policies aim to reduce socioeconomic disparities among neighborhoods.
Education is strongly influenced by socioeconomic background and family income.
Many health outcomes are linked to socioeconomic status.

Synset Examples

socioeconomic status

Related Terms

  • Synonym: economic - relating to economics or the economy

Etymology

Formed from 'social' and 'economic' (from Greek oikonomikos, meaning household management).
