all-rounder

1. NOUN

/ ɔlˈraʊndər / (AmE), / ɔːlˈraʊndə / (BrE)
a person who is skilled at many different activities or has a wide range of abilities
person skilled at many things

Examples

She is the team's most valuable all-rounder, always performing well in every task.
As an all-rounder, he excels at both academics and sports.
The company values all-rounders who can adapt to different roles.

Synset Examples

she's the best all-rounder they've seen in years

Related Terms

  • Antonym: specialist - a person who is highly skilled in a specific area

Etymology

Formed from 'all' + 'round' + '-er', indicating a person who is good in all areas.
