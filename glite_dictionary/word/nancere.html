Nancere

1. NOUN

/ ˈnænˌkɛr.eɪ / (AmE), / ˈnan.sɛr.eɪ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in the Tandjile Region of Chad, chiefly in Béré, Delban, and Kélo areas
a Chadic language of Chad

Examples

Linguists have studied Nancere to learn more about the diversity of Chadic languages.
Fewer young people in the region are fluent in Nancere than in previous generations.
He compiled a dictionary of Nancere to help preserve the language.
