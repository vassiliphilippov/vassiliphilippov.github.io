monaurally

1. ADV

/ məˈnɔrəli / (AmE), / məˈnɔːrəli / (BrE)
In a way that involves or relates to only one ear or channel of audio.
using or relating to one ear

Examples

The signals were delivered monaurally to the participant's right ear for analysis.
She listened to the audio monaurally to compare the effects with binaural playback.
During the experiment, the music was played monaurally through a single earpiece.

Synset Examples

the stimuli were presented monaurally

Related Terms

  • Antonym: binaurally - in a binaural manner; relating to or involving both ears

Etymology

From 'monaural' (from mono- meaning 'one' and Latin 'auris' meaning 'ear') + -ly.
