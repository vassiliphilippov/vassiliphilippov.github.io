graphical

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈɡrafɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or presented by means of a graph or graphs, especially in data presentation or statistical analysis.
relating to or using graphs

Examples

The results were presented in a graphical format, making them easier to understand.
Our software offers a graphical interface for visualizing complex data sets.
A graphical representation of the survey findings highlighted key trends.

Synset Examples

a graphic presentation of the data

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graphic - relating to visual art, especially drawing, illustration, or graphs

Etymology

From Greek graphikos (of or for writing or drawing), from graphein (to write, draw), with -al forming the adjective.

2. ADJ

/ ˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈɡræfɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving written, drawn, or visual representation.
relating to visual or drawn representation

Examples

The document contains several graphical symbols to illustrate the instructions.
Modern video games are known for their impressive graphical quality and realism.
The graphical representation of the data made the trends easy to understand.

Synset Examples

graphic symbols

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graphic - relating to visual art or drawing

Etymology

From Greek graphikos (of or for writing, drawing), via Latin graphicus.
