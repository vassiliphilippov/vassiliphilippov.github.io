undiminished

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.dɪˈmɪn.ɪʃt / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈmɪn.ɪʃt / (BrE)
not reduced in strength, amount, or quality; remaining at its original level
not reduced in strength or amount

Examples

Her enthusiasm for the project remained undiminished despite the setbacks.
The orchestra played with undiminished passion throughout the performance.
After decades, his love for music is still undiminished.

Synset Examples

unrelieved suffering

Related Terms

  • Antonym: diminished - reduced in size, strength, or amount

Etymology

From un- (not) + diminished, from Latin deminuere (to lessen).
