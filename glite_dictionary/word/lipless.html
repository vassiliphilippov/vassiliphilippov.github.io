lipless

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪpləs / (AmE), / ˈlɪpləs / (BrE)
having no lip or lips.
having no lips

Examples

The statue had a lipless mouth that gave it an eerie appearance.
Some fish species are naturally lipless, making their teeth more prominent.
His face appeared almost lipless after the surgery, transforming his expression.

Etymology

From 'lip' + suffix '-less', meaning 'without'.
