Emilia flammea

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmɪliə ˈflæmiə / (AmE), / ɪˈmɪliə ˈflæmiə / (BrE)
A tropical African annual plant with scarlet, tassel-shaped flower heads, sometimes classified in the genus Cacalia.
tropical African plant with scarlet flowers

Examples

The bright flowers of Emilia flammea attract butterflies to gardens in tropical regions.
Botanists sometimes debate whether Emilia flammea should be classified under the genus Cacalia.
The gardener planted Emilia flammea along the border for its striking scarlet blossoms.
