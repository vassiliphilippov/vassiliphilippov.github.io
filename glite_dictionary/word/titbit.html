titbit

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪtˌbɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɪtbɪt / (BrE)
a small, tasty piece of food, often offered as a snack or appetizer
small, tasty piece of food
british

Examples

She offered her guests a titbit from the platter before dinner.
The dog waited patiently for a titbit as a reward for good behavior.
At the party, the table was filled with delicious titbits and appetizers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tidbit - a small, tasty piece of food (mainly American spelling)

Etymology

From earlier 'tidbit' (variant), influenced by 'tit' meaning small; chiefly British spelling variant.
