broke-ass

1. ADJ

/ ˈbroʊk ˌæs / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊk ˌas / (BrE)
Very poor; having no money or resources (slang, often used as an intensifier).
very poor; having no money (slang)
slang informal

Examples

I can't stand my broke-ass car breaking down again.
He showed up to the party in his same old broke-ass jeans.
After rent was due, she was feeling seriously broke-ass.

Synset Examples

I can't go out; I'm feeling broke-ass this week.
His broke-ass couldn't afford the concert tickets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broke - having no money
  • Antonym: well-off - having plenty of money

Etymology

Formed from 'broke' (having no money) + slang intensifier '-ass', common in African American Vernacular English and popular culture.
