outlandishly

1. ADV

/ aʊtˈlændɪʃli / (AmE), / aʊtˈlændɪʃli / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely unusual, bizarre, or unfamiliar.
in a bizarre or very unusual way

Examples

He always dresses outlandishly to stand out from the crowd.
The movie's costumes were outlandishly colorful and imaginative.
She decorated her apartment outlandishly, with neon lights and bright animal prints.

Synset Examples

the Bavarian was outlandishly dressed in lederhosen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bizarrely - in a very strange or unusual manner
  • Antonym: normally - in a usual or expected manner
