receptively

1. ADV

/ rɪˈsɛptɪvli / (AmE), / rɪˈsɛptɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that shows willingness to receive ideas, suggestions, or information.
in a receptive manner

Examples

The audience listened receptively to the speaker’s new ideas.
She nodded receptively during the brainstorming session, encouraging her team to share their thoughts.
He always responds receptively to constructive criticism, aiming to improve his work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: receptive - willing to consider or accept new suggestions and ideas
