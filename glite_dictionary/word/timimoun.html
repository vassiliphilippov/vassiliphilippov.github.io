Timimoun

1. PROPN

/ tɪˈmiːmuːn / (AmE), / tɪˈmiːmuːn / (BrE)
A town in central Algeria, located in the Atlas Mountains, known for its oasis and distinctive red clay architecture.
town in central Algeria

Examples

Many tourists visit Timimoun to see its famous red clay architecture and oasis landscape.
The desert climate of Timimoun makes it a unique destination in Algeria.
She documented her travels through Timimoun in a series of stunning photographs.
