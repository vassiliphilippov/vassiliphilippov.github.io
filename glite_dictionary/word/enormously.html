enormously

1. ADV

/ ɪˈnɔrməsli / (AmE), / ɪˈnɔːməsli / (BrE)
to a very great degree; extremely
to a great degree; extremely

Examples

She is enormously talented and admired by her colleagues.
The price of the house increased enormously in just a few years.
He was enormously grateful for their support and kindness.

Synset Examples

he was enormously popular

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hugely - to a very great degree; extremely
  • Synonym: immensely - to a great extent; extremely

Etymology

From 'enormous' + '-ly', where 'enormous' is from Latin 'enormis' (irregular, vast).
