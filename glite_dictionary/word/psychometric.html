psychometric

1. ADJ

/ ˌsaɪkoʊˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌsʌɪkəʊˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
Relating to psychometrics or the measurement of psychological traits, abilities, and processes.
relating to psychological measurement
medical

Examples

She specializes in psychometric assessments for educational and clinical purposes.
The research was published in a leading psychometric journal.
New psychometric tools are constantly being developed to improve the accuracy of personality testing.

Synset Examples

psychometric journals

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychometrics - the science of measuring mental capacities and processes

Etymology

From psychometrics (the science of psychological measurement), derived from Greek 'psyche' (soul, mind) and 'metron' (measure).
