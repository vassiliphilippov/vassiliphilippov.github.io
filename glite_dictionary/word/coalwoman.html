coalwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊlˌwʊm.ən / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlˌwʊm.ən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to deliver coal to homes or businesses
female coal deliverer

Examples

The coalwoman arrived early in the morning to deliver the winter's supply.
As a coalwoman, she braved the cold streets to serve her community.
The local history museum featured photographs of a famous coalwoman from the 1930s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coalman - a man who delivers coal
  • Synonym: coalminer - a person who mines coal

Etymology

From 'coal' + 'woman', modeled on 'coalman'.
