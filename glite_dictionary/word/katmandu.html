Katmandu

1. PROPN

/ ˌkætˈmændu / (AmE), / ˌkatˈmæn.duː / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Nepal, located in the central part of the country in the Kathmandu Valley
capital city of Nepal

Examples

Many tourists travel to Katmandu to experience its unique culture and history.
The earthquake caused significant destruction in Katmandu in 2015.
She plans to study at a university in Katmandu next year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kathmandu - alternative spelling of Katmandu, capital of Nepal

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Kastha-mandap' meaning 'wooden pavilion', referring to a historical building in the city.
