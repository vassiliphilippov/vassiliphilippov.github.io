prospering

1. ADJ

/ ˈprɑspərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɒspərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person, business, or place) growing and developing successfully; flourishing, especially financially.
growing or flourishing successfully

Examples

The local technology sector is prospering thanks to recent investments and favorable policies.
Several prospering businesses have opened in the renovated district downtown.
During the 1990s, many previously struggling companies became prospering enterprises.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thriving - growing vigorously; flourishing
  • Synonym: flourishing - developing rapidly and successfully; thriving
  • Antonym: failing - not succeeding; becoming weaker or less successful

Etymology

Present participle of the verb 'prosper', from Latin 'prosperare' (to succeed).
