northwestwardly

1. ADV

/ nɔrθˈwɛstwərdli / (AmE), / nɔːθˈwɛstwədli / (BrE)
In a direction toward the northwest.
in a northwestward direction

Examples

The birds migrated northwestwardly in search of a cooler climate.
The explorers traveled northwestwardly across the open plain for several days.
Clouds began to move northwestwardly as the storm changed direction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: northwestward - in the direction toward the northwest

Etymology

Formed from 'northwestward' + '-ly' suffix.
