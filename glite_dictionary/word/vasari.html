Vasari

1. PROPN

/ vəˈsɑri / (AmE), / vəˈsɑːri / (BrE)
(1511–1574) Italian painter, architect, and art historian, best known for his biographies of Renaissance artists.
Italian painter and art historian (1511–1574)

Examples

Many art historians regard Vasari as the first great historian of Renaissance art.
The works of Vasari provide valuable insights into the lives of Italian painters.
Vasari designed several important buildings in Florence.

Etymology

Italian surname; refers to Giorgio Vasari, influential in art history.
