cuplike

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌp.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈkʌp.laɪk / (BrE)
Having a shape or form that resembles a cup.
resembling a cup in shape

Examples

The flower has a cuplike structure that holds rainwater.
Her hands formed a cuplike shape as she caught the snowflakes.
The fungus is known for its distinctive cuplike appearance.
