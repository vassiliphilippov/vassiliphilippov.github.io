Saxifraga stellaris

1. PROPN

/ ˌsæksəˈfræɡə ˈstɛlərɪs / (AmE), / ˌsæksɪˈfrɑːɡə ˈstɛlərɪs / (BrE)
A small alpine plant forming mats, with starlike white flowers, native to Europe; commonly known as starry saxifrage.
alpine plant with starlike white flowers

Examples

While hiking in the Alps, I spotted a patch of Saxifraga stellaris among the rocks.
Botanists admire the delicate beauty of Saxifraga stellaris during the short alpine summer.
Photographers are drawn to the starlike blooms of Saxifraga stellaris in mountain meadows.

Etymology

From Latin: 'saxifraga' meaning 'stone-breaker' and 'stellaris' meaning 'star-like'.
