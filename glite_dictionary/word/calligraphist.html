calligraphist

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlɪɡrəfɪst / (AmE), / kəˈlɪɡrəfɪst / (BrE)
a person skilled in the art of beautiful handwriting or calligraphy
a skilled practitioner of calligraphy

Examples

The invitations were addressed by a professional calligraphist using elegant, flowing script.
She dreams of becoming a renowned calligraphist and sharing her artwork around the world.
A skilled calligraphist can turn ordinary text into a work of art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calligrapher - a person who writes in a beautiful and artistic manner

Etymology

From Greek kalligraphos (beautiful writing) + English suffix -ist (one who practices).
