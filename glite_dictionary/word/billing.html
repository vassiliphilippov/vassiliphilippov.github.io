billing

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of requesting payment for goods or services provided
request for payment for goods or services

Examples

The billing department handles all customer payments and invoices.
He disputed the last month's billing for additional services he didn't order.
Prompt billing helps ensure faster payments from clients.

Synset Examples

they submitted their charges at the end of each month

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invoice - a document requesting payment for goods or services supplied
  • Synonym: charge - an amount billed to someone for goods or services

Etymology

From 'bill' (statement of charges) + '-ing' (forming nouns of action).
