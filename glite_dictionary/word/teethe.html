to teethe

1. VERB

/ ˈtið / (AmE), / ˈtiːð / (BrE)
(of a baby or young child) to grow one's first set of teeth.
grow baby teeth

Examples

Our daughter started to teethe when she was about six months old.
Babies can become irritable when they teethe.
The puppy began to teethe and started chewing on everything.

Synset Examples

The little one is teething now
