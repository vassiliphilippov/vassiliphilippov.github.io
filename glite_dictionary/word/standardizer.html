standardizer

1. NOUN

/ ˈstændɚˌdaɪzɚ / (AmE), / ˈstændədaɪzə / (BrE)
a person or organization that establishes or imposes a standard for others to follow
person or body that sets standards

Examples

The standardizer ensured that all products met the industry's quality benchmarks.
As a global standardizer, the organization influences regulations in many countries.
Her role as the company's standardizer required extensive knowledge of international norms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: standard - an accepted or approved example of something against which others are judged or measured
  • Synonym: regulator - a person or body that supervises a particular industry or practice

Etymology

From 'standard' + '-izer', following the pattern of 'standardize'.
