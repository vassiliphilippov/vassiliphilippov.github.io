apposite

1. ADJ

/ ˈæpəsɪt / (AmE), / ˈapəsɪt / (BrE)
highly appropriate and relevant to a particular situation or subject
highly appropriate or relevant

Examples

Her comments were particularly apposite to the current debate.
The lawyer provided an apposite case to support his argument.
That quotation was apposite to the discussion and made everyone think.

Synset Examples

the successful copywriter is a master of apposite and evocative verbal images
an apt reply

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appropriate - suitable or proper in the circumstances
  • Antonym: irrelevant - not connected with or relevant to something

Etymology

From Latin 'appositus', past participle of 'appōnere' (to put near).
