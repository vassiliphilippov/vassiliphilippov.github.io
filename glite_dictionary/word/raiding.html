raiding

1. ADJ

/ ˈreɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈreɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
engaged in or characteristic of raiding, especially involving plundering or pillaging
characterized by or engaged in raiding

Examples

During the night, the villagers spotted a raiding party approaching from the hills.
The documentary explored the history of raiding tribes along the border.
Armed with torches, the raiding band swept through the countryside.

Synset Examples

bands of marauding Indians
predatory warfare
a raiding party

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marauding - roaming in search of things to steal or people to attack
  • Synonym: predatory - seeking to exploit or oppress others

Etymology

Present participle of the verb "raid", from Scottish raid (a foray or attack).
