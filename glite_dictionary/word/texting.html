texting

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛkstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɛkstɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of sending written messages electronically, typically via SMS, using a mobile phone or similar device
sending messages via SMS

Examples

She was texting her friend during the whole train ride.
It's dangerous to be texting while driving.
More teenagers prefer texting to making phone calls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: messaging - the act of sending messages electronically

Etymology

From 'text' (verb: to send a text message) + '-ing', originating in the digital communications era.
