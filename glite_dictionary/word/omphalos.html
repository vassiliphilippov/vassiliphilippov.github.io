omphalos

1. NOUN

/ ɑmˈfeɪləs / (AmE), / ɒmˈfeɪlɒs / (BrE)
the navel; the scar on the abdomen marking where the umbilical cord was attached.
the navel or bellybutton

Examples

The ancient Greeks considered the omphalos to be the center of the world.
He had a small birthmark near his omphalos.
In anatomy class, we examined the location and structure of the omphalos.

Synset Examples

you were not supposed to show your navel on television
they argued whether or not Adam had a navel
she had a tattoo just above her bellybutton

Related Terms

  • Synonym: navel - the small, central scar on the abdomen left after the umbilical cord is cut; bellybutton
  • Synonym: bellybutton - informal term for navel

Etymology

From Ancient Greek ὀμφαλός (omphalós) meaning 'navel'.
