helve

1. NOUN

/ hɛlv / (AmE), / hɛlv / (BrE)
the handle of a tool or weapon, such as an axe
handle of a tool or weapon

Examples

The blacksmith carved a new helve for the old hammer.
After the helve broke, she could no longer use the shovel.
He gripped the helve of his axe tightly as he prepared to split the wood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handle - the part of something designed to be held

Etymology

From Old English helfe (handle), related to German Helm (handle).
