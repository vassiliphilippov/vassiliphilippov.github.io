explicitness

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈsplɪsɪtnəs / (AmE), / ɪkˈsplɪsɪtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being stated clearly and with no room for confusion or doubt
quality of being clear and explicit

Examples

The explicitness of the instructions made the assembly process remarkably simple.
Teachers appreciated the explicitness in his explanations, as it helped students understand difficult topics.
She admired the explicitness with which the author detailed the procedure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clarity - the quality of being clear

Etymology

From explicit + -ness, modeled after similar English noun formations.
