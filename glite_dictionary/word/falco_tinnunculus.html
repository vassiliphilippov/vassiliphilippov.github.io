Falco tinnunculus

1. NOUN

/ ˈfælkoʊ tɪˈnʌŋkjələs / (AmE), / ˈfæl.kəʊ tɪˈnʌŋ.kjʊl.əs / (BrE)
A small falcon native to Europe, Asia, and Africa, known for hovering in the air while hunting; also called the common kestrel.
small kestrel species of falcon

Examples

The Falco tinnunculus is often seen hovering above open fields searching for prey.
Birdwatchers were excited to spot a Falco tinnunculus near the edge of the forest.
The distinctive flight of Falco tinnunculus makes it easy to recognize among other birds of prey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kestrel - a small falcon, especially the common kestrel

Etymology

From Latin, 'falco' meaning falcon and 'tinnunculus' meaning kestrel.
