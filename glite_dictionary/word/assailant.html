assailant

1. NOUN

/ əˈseɪlənt / (AmE), / əˈseɪl(ə)nt / (BrE)
a person who attacks someone physically or verbally
person who attacks another

Examples

The police quickly identified the assailant after reviewing the security footage.
Her injuries were caused by an unknown assailant who fled the scene.
Witnesses described the assailant as tall and wearing a dark jacket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attacker - a person who attacks
  • Antonym: defender - a person who protects from attack

Etymology

From Old French 'assaillant', present participle of 'assaillir' (to attack), from Latin 'assilire'.
