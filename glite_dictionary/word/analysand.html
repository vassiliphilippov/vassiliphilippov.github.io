analysand

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.əˈlɪ.sænd / (AmE), / ˌan.əˈlɪ.sand / (BrE)
a person undergoing psychoanalysis as a patient
person undergoing psychoanalysis
medical

Examples

During each session, the analysand is encouraged to speak freely about their thoughts and feelings.
The relationship between analyst and analysand is central to the psychotherapeutic process.
A skilled analyst listens carefully to the analysand to uncover unconscious patterns.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: analyst - a psychoanalyst; the person conducting the psychoanalysis

Etymology

From French 'analysand', based on 'analyse' + Latin gerundive ending '-andus'.
