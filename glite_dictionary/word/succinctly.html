succinctly

1. ADV

/ səkˈsɪŋktli / (AmE), / səkˈsɪŋktli / (BrE)
in a brief and clear manner; using few words to express an idea clearly and effectively
in a concise, clear manner

Examples

She summarized the report succinctly in just a few sentences.
The instructions were explained succinctly for easy understanding.
He always answers questions succinctly, never wasting words.

Synset Examples

Please state your case as succinctly as possible
he wrote compactly but clearly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concisely - expressing much in few words; brief and to the point

Etymology

From Latin 'succinctus' (girded, concise) + English adverb suffix '-ly'.
