nonresinous

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈrɛzɪnəs / (AmE), / nɒnˈrɛzɪnəs / (BrE)
Not containing, producing, or resembling resin.
not containing or producing resin

Examples

The manufacturer prefers nonresinous woods for certain types of furniture to avoid sticky surfaces.
Some insects are attracted only to nonresinous trees for laying their eggs.
This adhesive is ideal for bonding nonresinous materials in woodworking projects.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: resinous - containing, producing, or resembling resin

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) and 'resinous' (containing resin).
