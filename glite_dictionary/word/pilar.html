pilar

1. ADJ

/ ˈpaɪlɑr / (AmE), / ˈpaɪlɑː / (BrE)
Relating to or affecting the hair.
relating to hair
medical

Examples

The dermatologist diagnosed the patient with a pilar cyst on her scalp.
Certain medications can affect pilar structures, leading to hair loss.
The biopsy revealed inflammation in the pilar region of the skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: capillary - relating to hair (as a structural term)

Etymology

From Latin 'pilus' (hair).
