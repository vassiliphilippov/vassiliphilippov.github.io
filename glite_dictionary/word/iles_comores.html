Iles Comores

1. PROPN

/ il komɔːˈrɛz / (AmE), / il kəˈmɔːreɪ / (BrE)
a group of three main islands and numerous islets in the Indian Ocean, located between Mozambique and Madagascar; also known as the Comoros Islands
island group in the Indian Ocean

Examples

The remote beauty of the Iles Comores attracts visitors seeking unspoiled beaches.
Political unrest has affected the economy of the Iles Comores in recent decades.
The Iles Comores are renowned for their unique blend of African, Arab, and French cultures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Comoros - the country composed of the Comoros Islands

Etymology

From French 'Îles Comores', meaning 'Comoros Islands'.
