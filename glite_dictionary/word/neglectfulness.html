neglectfulness

1. NOUN

/ nɪˈɡlɛktfəlnəs / (AmE), / nɪˈɡlɛktfəlnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of habitually failing to pay proper attention to responsibilities or duties.
habit of neglecting duties

Examples

Her neglectfulness at work eventually led to a warning from her supervisor.
A parent's neglectfulness can have a long-lasting impact on a child's development.
His neglectfulness with deadlines frustrated the entire team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carelessness - lack of attention or care
  • Antonym: diligence - careful and persistent work or effort

Etymology

From 'neglect' + '-fulness', formed on the model of 'carefulness', 'thoughtfulness', etc.
