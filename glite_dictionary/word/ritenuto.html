ritenuto

1. ADJ

/ ˌriːtəˈnuːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌriːtɛˈnjuːtəʊ / (BrE)
(music) Gradually decreasing in tempo; played with a sudden or immediate slowing down in speed.
music: sudden reduction in tempo

Examples

The pianist noticed the ritenuto marking and slowed the passage significantly.
Be sure to play the final bars ritenuto to emphasize the ending.
At the conductor's signal, the orchestra transitioned into a pronounced ritenuto.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ritardando - (music) gradually slowing down; usually more gradual than ritenuto
  • Antonym: accelerando - (music) gradually speeding up in tempo

Etymology

From Italian 'ritenuto', past participle of 'ritenere' (to hold back).
