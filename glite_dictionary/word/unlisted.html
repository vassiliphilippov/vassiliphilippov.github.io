unlisted

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlɪstɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪstɪd / (BrE)
(of something, especially a telephone number) not included in a published list or directory.
not included in a published list

Examples

She keeps her unlisted number private to avoid unwanted calls.
The company operates from an unlisted address to maintain a low profile.
He preferred to have an unlisted phone so telemarketers couldn't reach him.

Synset Examples

an unlisted telephone number

Related Terms

  • Antonym: listed - included in a published list or directory
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01421220-a
Synset POS: a

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlɪstɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪstɪd / (BrE)
(of a person) not having one's name entered on an official list, especially for voting or registration purposes.
not on an official list (esp. for voting)

Examples

Several residents were unable to vote because they were unlisted on the electoral roll.
The committee could not find his name because he was an unlisted voter.
If you are unlisted, you will not receive an official ballot for the election.

Synset Examples

an unlisted voter

Related Terms

  • Antonym: registered - having one's name officially entered on a list, especially for voting
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01965587-s
Synset POS: s
