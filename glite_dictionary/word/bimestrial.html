bimestrial

1. ADJ

/ baɪˈmɛstriəl / (AmE), / baɪˈmɛstriəl / (BrE)
lasting for two months; continuing over a period of two months
lasting two months

Examples

The grant supported a bimestrial research project that ran from January to March.
She received bimestrial updates from the committee tracking the two-month progress.
A bimestrial review helps the team assess their progress on longer-term goals.

Etymology

From Latin 'bimestris' (of two months) + English '-al'.
