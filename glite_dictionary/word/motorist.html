motorist

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊ.t̬ɚ.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.tər.ɪst / (BrE)
a person who drives or travels in a car
person driving/traveling in a car

Examples

The motorist pulled over to the side of the road to check the flat tire.
During the snowstorm, every motorist was advised to drive carefully.
As a careful motorist, she always obeyed the speed limit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: driver - a person who drives a vehicle

Etymology

From 'motor' (automobile) + '-ist' (denoting a person involved with a specified thing), early 20th century.
