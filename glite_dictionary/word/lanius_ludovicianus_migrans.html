Lanius ludovicianus migrans

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪni.əs ˌluːdəˈvɪʃi.ənəs ˈmaɪɡrænz / (AmE), / ˈleɪnɪ.əs ˌljuːdəˈvɪʃɪənəs ˈmɪɡrænz / (BrE)
A subspecies of loggerhead shrike found in central North America, wintering mainly in Texas and the southern Mississippi valley.
subspecies of loggerhead shrike (bird) from central North America

Examples

The Lanius ludovicianus migrans migrates south to Texas during the winter months.
Birdwatchers spotted a rare Lanius ludovicianus migrans in the southern Mississippi valley last week.
Conservationists are tracking the habitat of Lanius ludovicianus migrans to understand its population decline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loggerhead shrike - Common name for Lanius ludovicianus, a medium-sized predatory songbird

Etymology

From Latin: genus name 'Lanius' (butcher), species 'ludovicianus', subspecies 'migrans' meaning migrating.
