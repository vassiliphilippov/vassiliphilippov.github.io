Perilla frutescens crispa

1. NOUN

/ pəˈrɪlə fruːˈtɛsənz ˈkrɪspə / (AmE), / pəˈrɪlə fruːˈtɛsɛnz ˈkrɪspə / (BrE)
A variety of perilla plant cultivated for its attractive red or purple ornamental leaves, often used in gardens and as decoration.
ornamental plant with red or purple foliage

Examples

The deep purple leaves of Perilla frutescens crispa make it a popular choice for garden borders.
Many chefs use Perilla frutescens crispa as a garnish due to its distinctive color and texture.
In landscaping designs, Perilla frutescens crispa is valued for its striking ornamental appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perilla - an aromatic plant of the mint family, grown for edible and ornamental use

Etymology

From Latin; 'Perilla' is the genus, 'frutescens' means becoming shrubby, and 'crispa' means curled or wavy.
