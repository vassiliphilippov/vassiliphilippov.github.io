uncoloured

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkʌlərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌləd / (BrE)
not having any color; transparent or colorless.
lacking color; colorless

Examples

The solution remained uncoloured after the chemical reaction.
Artists sometimes prefer uncoloured paper for sketching to provide a neutral background.
Pure diamonds are valued for being completely uncoloured and free from impurities.

Synset Examples

pure water is uncolored

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colorless - having no color
  • Antonym: coloured - having color

Etymology

Prefix 'un-' (not) + 'coloured' (having color), formed in English.
