swordsmanship

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔrdsmənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈsɔːdsmənˌʃɪp / (BrE)
the skill or art of fighting with a sword, especially in fencing or historical combat
skill of fighting with a sword

Examples

Her years of training were evident in her impressive swordsmanship during the tournament.
Early legends often praised the swordsmanship of their heroes in battle.
He studied the art of swordsmanship with a renowned fencing master.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fencing - the sport of fighting with swords

Etymology

From 'sword' + 'manship', a suffix denoting skill or craft.
