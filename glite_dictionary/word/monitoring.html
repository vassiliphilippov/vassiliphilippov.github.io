monitoring

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑː.nə.tɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɒn.ɪ.tər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of regularly observing something, often recording details or progress for information or control
regular observation or checking

Examples

Continuous monitoring of air quality ensures that pollution levels remain within safe limits.
Effective wildlife conservation relies on the monitoring of animal populations and habitats.
The monitoring of patients' vital signs is crucial in the intensive care unit.

Synset Examples

the monitoring of enemy communications plays an important role in war times

Related Terms

  • Synonym: observation - the act of closely watching something

Etymology

From the verb 'monitor', which derives from Latin 'monitor' (warner, adviser).
