Seseli

1. PROPN

/ səˈsɛli / (AmE), / sɪˈsiːlaɪ / (BrE)
A genus of Eurasian and North African plants in the family Apiaceae, including species commonly known as moon carrots.
genus of Eurasian and North African plants

Examples

The Seseli genus includes several species known for their delicate umbels of white flowers.
Botanists discovered a new Seseli species in the mountains of southern Europe.
The moon carrot, a member of the Seseli genus, thrives in dry grasslands.

Etymology

From Latin 'Seseli', the classical name for a kind of aromatic plant.
