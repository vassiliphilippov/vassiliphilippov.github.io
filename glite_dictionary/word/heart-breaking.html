heart-breaking

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑrtˌbreɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtˌbreɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing extreme sadness or distress.
causing extreme sadness

Examples

The movie's ending was so heart-breaking that many people left the theater in tears.
It was heart-breaking to watch the old house being torn down after so many years.
Hearing about the loss of their pet was truly heart-breaking for the entire family.

Synset Examples

The news of the disaster was truly heart-breaking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devastating - causing severe shock, distress, or grief
  • Antonym: uplifting - making someone feel happier or more hopeful

Etymology

From 'heart' (the seat of emotion) + 'breaking', referring to severe emotional pain.
