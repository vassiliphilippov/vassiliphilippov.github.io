unnecessary

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈnɛsəˌsɛri / (AmE), / ʌnˈnɛsəsəri / (BrE)
Not needed; more than is required or wanted.
not needed; not required

Examples

It was an unnecessary expense that could have been avoided.
Please avoid making unnecessary comments during the meeting.
The delay caused a lot of unnecessary stress for everyone involved.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: necessary - required to be done, achieved, or present; needed
  • Synonym: superfluous - unnecessary, especially through being more than enough

Etymology

From Latin 'necessarius' (necessary) with the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not'.
