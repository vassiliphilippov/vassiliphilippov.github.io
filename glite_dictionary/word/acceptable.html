acceptable

1. ADJ

/ əkˈsɛptəb(ə)l / (AmE), / əkˈsɛptəb(ə)l / (BrE)
considered satisfactory or in accordance with an approved standard or usage
in accordance with approved standard or usage

Examples

Her behavior was considered acceptable by the school administration.
The report must be written in acceptable academic English.
Certain informal expressions are not acceptable in professional writing.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unacceptable - not satisfactory or not in accordance with standards
  • Synonym: satisfactory - fulfilling expectations or needs; acceptable

Etymology

From Old French acceptable, from Latin acceptabilis, from acceptare (to accept).
