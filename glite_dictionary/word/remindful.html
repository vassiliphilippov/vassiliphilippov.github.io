remindful

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈmaɪndfəl / (AmE), / rɪˈmaɪndf(ə)l / (BrE)
serving to bring something to mind; evocative or suggestive of something else
serving to bring to mind; evocative

Examples

The old photograph was remindful of happier times spent with family.
Her voice was remindful of a famous singer from the past.
The scent of fresh bread is remindful of my grandmother's kitchen.

Synset Examples

cannot forbear to close on this redolent literary note
a campaign redolent of machine politics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evocative - bringing strong images, memories, or feelings to mind
  • Synonym: suggestive - tending to suggest something; evocative

Etymology

From the verb "remind" + suffix "-ful" (full of).
