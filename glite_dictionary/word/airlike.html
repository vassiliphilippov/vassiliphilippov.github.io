airlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛrˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈeəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling air in appearance, quality, or form; having the nature of air
having qualities or appearance of air

Examples

The ghost moved through the room in an airlike manner, barely disturbing the curtains.
She wore an airlike gown that seemed to float around her.
Mist rose in airlike shapes above the still surface of the lake.
