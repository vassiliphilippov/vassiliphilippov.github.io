Meshed

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɛʃɛd / (AmE), / ˈmɛʃɛd / (BrE)
A holy city of Shiite Muslims, located in northeastern Iran, known for the shrine of Imam Reza.
holy city in northeastern Iran

Examples

Every year, thousands of pilgrims travel to Meshed to visit the shrine of Imam Reza.
The city of Meshed is an important religious center in Iran.
He wrote an article describing his experiences in Meshed during the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mashhad - Alternative spelling of the city in Iran; same city as Meshed

Etymology

From Persian 'Mashhad', meaning 'place of martyrdom'.
