hyperthermal

1. ADJ

/ ˌhaɪpərˈθɜrməl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈθɜːməl / (BrE)
relating to or affected by abnormally high body temperature (hyperthermia)
relating to abnormally high temperature (hyperthermia)
medical

Examples

The patient was placed in a hyperthermal environment for experimental treatment.
Researchers examined the effects of hyperthermal conditions on metabolic rates.
Some bacteria thrive only in hyperthermal waters near volcanic vents.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hypothermal - relating to abnormally low body temperature (hypothermia)

Etymology

From 'hyperthermia', from Greek 'hyper' (above) + 'thermos' (hot).
