Nikola Tesla

1. PROPN

/ ˈniːkələ ˈtɛslə / (AmE), / ˈnɪkələ ˈtɛslə / (BrE)
(1856–1943) A Serbian-American inventor and electrical engineer known for pioneering work on alternating current, the induction motor, and the Tesla coil.
Serbian-American inventor and electrical engineer

Examples

Nikola Tesla is widely recognized for his contributions to the development of the modern electrical power system.
Many historians regard Nikola Tesla as one of the most influential inventors of the 19th and 20th centuries.
The Tesla coil, invented by Nikola Tesla, is still used in radio technology today.
