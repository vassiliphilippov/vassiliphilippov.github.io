O.E.D.

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊ.iːˈdiː / (AmE), / ˌəʊ.iːˈdiː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for the Oxford English Dictionary, an unabridged dictionary constructed on historical principles.
Oxford English Dictionary (abbr.)

Examples

She consulted the O.E.D. to find the origin of the word.
Several rare words are only documented in the O.E.D..
The citation was referenced from the O.E.D. online edition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Oxford English Dictionary - The complete historical dictionary of the English language, published by Oxford University Press

Etymology
