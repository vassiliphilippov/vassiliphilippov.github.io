inhumanely

1. ADV

/ ɪn.hjuˈmeɪn.li / (AmE), / ɪn.hjuˈmeɪn.li / (BrE)
in a manner that shows a lack of compassion or regard for human suffering; cruelly or brutally.
in a cruel or brutal way

Examples

Many animals in factory farms are sometimes treated inhumanely to maximize efficiency.
The refugees were forced to live inhumanely crowded in makeshift camps.
International law prohibits soldiers from acting inhumanely towards prisoners of war.

Synset Examples

the prisoners of war were treated inhumanely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cruelly - in a cruel manner; causing pain or suffering
  • Antonym: humanely - in a compassionate or kind manner

Etymology

From 'inhumane' + '-ly', where 'inhumane' comes from Latin 'inhumanus' (not human, uncivilized).
