broiled

1. ADJ

/ brɔɪld / (AmE), / brɔɪld / (BrE)
(of food) cooked by exposure to direct radiant heat, typically from above, as under a grill or broiler.
cooked with direct radiant heat

Examples

I ordered the broiled salmon with a side of vegetables.
The restaurant is famous for its broiled steaks and fresh seafood.
She prefers her chicken broiled rather than fried.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grilled - cooked over direct heat, often above or below a heat source
