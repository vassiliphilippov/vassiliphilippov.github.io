Pahang

1. PROPN

/ pɑːˈhɑːŋ / (AmE), / pɑːˈhɑːŋ / (BrE)
A sultanate and one of the 13 states of Malaysia, located on the eastern part of Peninsular Malaysia.
state in Malaysia; a sultanate

Examples

The rainforests of Pahang are among the oldest in the world.
He traveled to Pahang to explore its national parks and wildlife.
Many beautiful beaches can be found along the coast of Pahang.

Etymology

From Malay 'Pahang', the local name for the region.
