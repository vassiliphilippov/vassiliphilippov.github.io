tenrec

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛn.rɛk / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.rɛk / (BrE)
a small, often spiny insectivorous mammal native to Madagascar, resembling a hedgehog
spiny insectivorous mammal of Madagascar

Examples

The tenrec is found only in Madagascar and is known for its spiky fur.
Unlike hedgehogs, a tenrec can give birth to remarkably large litters.
Researchers studied the tenrec to learn more about unique mammalian adaptations.

Etymology

From French 'tenrec', from Malagasy 'tetrahika'.
