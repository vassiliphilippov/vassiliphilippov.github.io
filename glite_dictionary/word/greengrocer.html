greengrocer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrinˌɡroʊsər / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnˌɡrəʊsə / (BrE)
a shopkeeper who sells fresh fruit and vegetables.
a fruit and vegetable seller

Examples

I stopped by the greengrocer to pick up some apples and carrots.
The local greengrocer always has the freshest produce in town.
Many greengrocers display their fruits and vegetables outside the shop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grocer - a person who sells food and small household goods

Etymology

From green (referring to vegetables) + grocer.
