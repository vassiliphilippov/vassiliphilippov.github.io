westerner

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛstərnər / (AmE), / ˈwɛstənə / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from the western part of a country, especially the western United States
person from the western area, esp. U.S.

Examples

After moving to Texas, she quickly adapted to life as a westerner.
Many westerners are proud of their region's frontier heritage.
As a westerner, he often felt out of place when visiting the East Coast.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: easterner - a person from the eastern part of a country, especially the eastern United States
