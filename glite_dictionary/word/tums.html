Tums

1. NOUN

/ tʌmz / (AmE), / tʌmz / (BrE)
A trademarked brand of chewable tablets used as an antacid to relieve heartburn and indigestion.
brand of antacid tablets

Examples

After eating spicy food, I took two Tums to settle my stomach.
I always keep a bottle of Tums in my bag for occasional heartburn.
She offered me some Tums when I complained of indigestion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antacid - a substance that neutralizes stomach acidity
