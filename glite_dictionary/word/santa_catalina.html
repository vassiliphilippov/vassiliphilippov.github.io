Santa Catalina

1. PROPN

/ ˌsæntə kætəˈliːnə / (AmE), / ˌsæntə kætəˈliːnə / (BrE)
an island resort located off the southwestern coast of California in the Pacific Ocean, known for its tourism and natural beauty
island resort off California

Examples

Hundreds of tourists visit Santa Catalina each summer to enjoy its beaches and hiking trails.
We took a ferry from Los Angeles to Santa Catalina for a weekend getaway.
Santa Catalina is famous for its clear waters and serene landscapes.

Etymology

Spanish for 'Saint Catherine'.
