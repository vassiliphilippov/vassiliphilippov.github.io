willowy

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪloʊ.i / (AmE), / ˈwɪləʊ.i / (BrE)
(of a person) attractively slender and graceful.
slender and graceful

Examples

The willowy dancer glided effortlessly across the stage.
She had a willowy figure that many envied.
A willowy model posed for the artist in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slender - thin, especially in an attractive way
  • Degree Lower: lanky - ungracefully thin or tall and thin

Etymology

From 'willow' (the slender tree) + '-y' (adjective forming suffix), likening slenderness to a willow's form.
