Belarusian

1. NOUN

/ bɛləˈruːʒən / (AmE), / bɛləˈruːziən / (BrE)
the Slavic language spoken primarily in Belarus
Slavic language of Belarus

Examples

Many people in Belarus speak Belarusian as their first language.
She decided to learn Belarusian to communicate better with her grandparents.
Belarusian uses the Cyrillic script, similar to Russian and Ukrainian.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Russian - the Slavic language spoken in Russia
  • Synonym: Ukrainian - the Slavic language spoken in Ukraine

Etymology

From Belarus + -ian, indicating association with Belarus.
