boneless

1. ADJ

/ ˈboʊn.ləs / (AmE), / ˈbəʊn.ləs / (BrE)
(of meat, animals, or body parts) having no bones.
without bones

Examples

The chef prepared a delicious boneless chicken breast for dinner.
Jellyfish are boneless creatures found in oceans worldwide.
I prefer to buy boneless cuts of meat because they are easier to cook.

Synset Examples

jellyfish are boneless

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bony - containing or resembling bone(s)
