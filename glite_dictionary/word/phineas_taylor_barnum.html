Phineas Taylor Barnum

1. PROPN

/ ˈfɪniəs ˈteɪlər ˈbɑrnəm / (AmE), / ˈfɪniəs ˈteɪlə ˈbɑːnəm / (BrE)
(1810–1891) United States showman who popularized the circus and was famous for founding the Barnum & Bailey Circus.
famous American circus showman (1810–1891)

Examples

Phineas Taylor Barnum became world-famous for his extravagant circus productions and publicity stunts.
Many credit Phineas Taylor Barnum with inventing modern popular entertainment.
The legacy of Phineas Taylor Barnum lives on in contemporary circus culture.
