pipewort

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪpˌwɔrt / (AmE), / ˈpaɪpˌwɜːt / (BrE)
an aquatic perennial plant with translucent green leaves in a basal spiral and dense clusters of tiny white flowers, found in North America, Ireland, and the Hebrides
aquatic perennial plant with buttonlike flower clusters

Examples

The shallow pond was covered in patches of pipewort along the edges.
Researchers studied how pipewort populations have declined due to water pollution.
In summer, the delicate flowers of pipewort brighten the boggy meadows.
