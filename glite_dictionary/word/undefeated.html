undefeated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.dɪˈfiː.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈfiː.tɪd / (BrE)
Never having been beaten or defeated in a competition, contest, or conflict.
never having been beaten

Examples

The team remained undefeated throughout the entire season.
Despite many challenges, she was still undefeated after five rounds.
Their undefeated record made them the favorites to win the championship.

Synset Examples

undefeated in battle
an undefeated team

Related Terms

  • Antonym: beaten - having been defeated
  • Synonym: victorious - having won a victory

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'defeated', past participle of 'defeat.'
