masturbator

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæstɚˌbeɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstəbeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who masturbates; someone who practices masturbation
person who masturbates

Examples

He was embarrassed to admit he was a regular masturbator.
Some people believe that being a masturbator is taboo, while others see it as normal.
The novel portrayed the main character as a lonely masturbator seeking intimacy.

Related Terms

Etymology

From English 'masturbate' + '-or' (agent suffix).
