buoyantly

1. ADV

/ ˈbɔɪ.ənt.li / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪ.ənt.li / (BrE)
in a cheerful, optimistic, or lively manner
in a cheerful or lively manner

Examples

She floated buoyantly into the meeting, full of positive energy and fresh ideas.
Despite the setbacks, the team continued buoyantly toward their goals.
He accepted the news buoyantly, seeing only opportunities in the challenges ahead.

Synset Examples

we accepted the opportunity buoyantly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheerfully - in a happy and optimistic way
  • Antonym: gloomily - in a sad or pessimistic way

Etymology

From "buoyant" (from French *bouyant*, from bouyer 'to float'), with suffix -ly.
