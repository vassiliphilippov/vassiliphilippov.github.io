Milwaukee

1. PROPN

/ mɪlˈwɔːki / (AmE), / mɪlˈwɔːki / (BrE)
the largest city in Wisconsin, located on the western shore of Lake Michigan, known for its brewing industry and agricultural significance
largest city in Wisconsin, USA

Examples

She moved to Milwaukee to start a new job at a technology company.
The brewery tour in Milwaukee is famous for its rich history and delicious samples.
Many concerts and festivals take place on the lakefront in Milwaukee every summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Madison - capital city of Wisconsin
  • Synonym: Chicago - major city near Milwaukee, also on Lake Michigan

Etymology

Named after the Milwaukee River; from Algonquian languages, possibly meaning 'good land' or 'gathering place by the water'.
