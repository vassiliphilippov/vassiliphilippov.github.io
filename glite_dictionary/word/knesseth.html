Knesseth

1. PROPN

/ kəˈnɛsɛθ / (AmE), / kəˈnɛsɛθ / (BrE)
the unicameral national parliament of Israel, responsible for making laws and overseeing the government
parliament of Israel

Examples

The Israeli Knesseth passed a new law regarding education reform.
Debates within the Knesseth often reflect the country's diverse political views.
She visited the Knesseth during her trip to Jerusalem.

Etymology

From Modern Hebrew 'Knesset', meaning 'assembly', from the Hebrew root 'kanas' (to gather).
