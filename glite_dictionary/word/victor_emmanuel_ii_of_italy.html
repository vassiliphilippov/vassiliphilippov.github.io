Victor Emmanuel II of Italy

1. PROPN

/ ˈvɪktər ɪˈmænjuəl səˈkənd əv ɪˈtæli / (AmE), / ˈvɪktə ɪˈmænjuəl səˈkənd əv ˈɪtəli / (BrE)
(1820–1878) The first king of a united Italy, who completed the country's unification by acquiring Venice and Rome.
first king of a united Italy (1820–1878)

Examples

Victor Emmanuel II of Italy played a central role in the unification of the country.
The capture of Rome in 1870 completed the ambitions of Victor Emmanuel II of Italy.
Many streets in Italy are named after Victor Emmanuel II of Italy in honor of his contributions.
