paedophile

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːdəˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈpiːdəʊfaɪl / (BrE)
an adult who is sexually attracted to children
an adult attracted sexually to children

Examples

The police arrested a known paedophile living in the neighbourhood.
She wrote an article about the dangers posed by paedophiles on the internet.
Laws are strict when it comes to the treatment of convicted paedophiles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pedophile - alternative spelling, especially in American English, of 'paedophile'

Etymology

From Greek pais (child) + -philos (loving).
