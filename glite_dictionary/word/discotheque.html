discotheque

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪs.kəˌtɛk / (AmE), / ˈdɪs.kəˌtɛk / (BrE)
a nightclub or venue where people dance to recorded popular music
nightclub for dancing to recorded music

Examples

The city has a famous discotheque that stays open until dawn every weekend.
We met for the first time on the dance floor of a crowded discotheque.
She loves going to the discotheque with her friends to dance to the latest hits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nightclub - a club for entertainment and dancing, often open at night

Etymology

From French 'discothèque', based on 'disque' (disc, record) and '-thèque' (collection).
