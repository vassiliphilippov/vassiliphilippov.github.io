respected

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈspɛktɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈspɛktɪd / (BrE)
admired or held in high esteem by others; regarded with respect.
admired or esteemed

Examples

She is a respected leader in her field, known for her integrity and expertise.
The school is highly respected for its rigorous academic programs.
He became a respected member of the community through years of dedicated service.

Synset Examples

a respected family

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admired - regarded with admiration or approval
  • Antonym: disrespected - not given respect; treated with a lack of regard

Etymology

From the verb 'respect', which comes from Latin 'respectus' meaning 'regard, consideration'.
