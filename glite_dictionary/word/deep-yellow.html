deep-yellow

1. ADJ

/ dip ˈjɛloʊ / (AmE), / diːp ˈjɛləʊ / (BrE)
having a strong, intense shade of yellow, similar to the color of a ripe pumpkin
strong, intense yellow color

Examples

She wore a deep-yellow dress that made her stand out in the crowd.
The walls in the dining room are painted a warm deep-yellow shade.
The flowers bloomed in a vibrant deep-yellow, brightening the entire garden.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: golden - having a deep, rich yellow color
