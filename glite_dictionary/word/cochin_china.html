Cochin China

1. NOUN

/ ˌkoʊ.tʃɪn ˈtʃaɪ.nə / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.tʃɪn ˈtʃaɪ.nə / (BrE)
an Asian breed of large domestic fowl distinguished by dense plumage and feathered legs
Asian breed of large fowl

Examples

The farm specializes in raising Cochin China chickens for their distinctive plumage.
Many poultry enthusiasts admire the gentle nature of the Cochin China.
Their barn houses several rare breeds, including a splendid Cochin China.

Etymology

From the name of the Cochinchina region in southern Vietnam, where the breed was believed to have originated.
