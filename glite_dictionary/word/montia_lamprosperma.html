Montia lamprosperma

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑntiə læmˌprɑspɜrmə / (AmE), / ˈmɒntɪə lamˌprɒspɜːmə / (BrE)
A species of small Indian lettuce native to northern regions, belonging to the genus Montia.
species of Indian lettuce

Examples

Montia lamprosperma thrives in moist, shaded areas of northern woodlands.
Botanists recently discovered a new population of Montia lamprosperma in Alaska.
The leaves of Montia lamprosperma are tender and sometimes eaten as a wild green.

Etymology

Montia: named after Italian botanist Giuseppe Monti; species name from Greek 'lampros' (bright, shining) and 'sperma' (seed).
