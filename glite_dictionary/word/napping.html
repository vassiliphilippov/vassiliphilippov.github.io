napping

1. ADJ

/ ˈnæpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnapɪŋ / (BrE)
not prepared or alert to danger or opportunity; off guard
not alert or prepared

Examples

The thief broke in while the security guard was napping and not paying attention to the monitors.
Their rivals took advantage of the team being napping and scored a surprise goal.
If you don't stay focused in this job, you might be napping when a key opportunity arises.

Synset Examples

the blow caught him napping
caught in an off-guard moment
found him off his guard

Related Terms

  • Antonym: alert - quick to notice and respond; attentive
  • Antonym: vigilant - keeping careful watch for possible danger or difficulties
  • Synonym: off guard - not prepared or vigilant; unprepared
