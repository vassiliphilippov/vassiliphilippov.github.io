sawfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɔːfɪʃ / (BrE)
A large, primitive ray with a long, flattened snout edged with sharp teeth, resembling a saw, found in warm coastal waters.
large ray with a saw-like snout

Examples

A sawfish can be easily identified by its long, saw-like snout.
Scientists are working to protect endangered sawfish populations in coastal waters.
The aquarium exhibit featured a live sawfish, fascinating visitors with its unique appearance.
