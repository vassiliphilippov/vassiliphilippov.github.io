spitting

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of forcefully expelling saliva from the mouth.
forceful expulsion of saliva

Examples

Public spitting is prohibited in some cities due to hygiene concerns.
He was criticized for his habit of spitting during sports matches.
The sight of someone spitting on the sidewalk bothered many pedestrians.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spit - to expel saliva from the mouth
