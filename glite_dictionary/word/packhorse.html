packhorse

1. NOUN

/ ˈpækˌhɔrs / (AmE), / ˈpækˌhɔːs / (BrE)
a horse used for carrying loads on its back, especially along paths or in rugged terrain
a horse used to carry loads

Examples

The narrow mountain trails were only passable by packhorse during the expedition.
In the past, traders relied on the packhorse to transport goods across long distances.
The packhorse carried heavy bags of supplies up the steep hillside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pack animal - an animal used for carrying loads
