singles

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋɡəlz / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡlz / (BrE)
A form of tennis or similar games in which each side is represented by only one player.
one-on-one tennis match

Examples

She prefers to compete in singles rather than doubles.
The singles final will be played at the center court this afternoon.
He won his first singles title at the age of nineteen.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: doubles - tennis played with two players on each side

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋɡəlz / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡəlz / (BrE)
A game or match in badminton, tennis, or similar sports played with one player on each side.
one-on-one game in racket sports

Examples

He prefers to play singles rather than doubles in tournaments.
The singles final will take place on Sunday afternoon.
She trained hard to improve her performance in women's singles.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: doubles - a game in racket sports with two players on each side
