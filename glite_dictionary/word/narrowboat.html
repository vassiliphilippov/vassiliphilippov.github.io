narrowboat

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛroʊboʊt / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊbəʊt / (BrE)
a long, narrow boat designed for use on canals, typically used to carry freight or people
long boat for canals

Examples

We spent our holiday traveling along the canals in a beautifully restored narrowboat.
A traditional narrowboat can be seen cruising through the heart of the English countryside.
The museum displays a historic narrowboat that once carried coal along the Grand Union Canal.
