Burmese-Yi language

1. NOUN

/ bɜrˌmiz ˈji ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / bɜːˌmiːz ˈjiː ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Tibeto-Burman language spoken in northern Myanmar and Yunnan province of China
Tibeto-Burman language of northern Myanmar and Yunnan

Examples

The Burmese-Yi language is spoken by several ethnic groups in northern Myanmar.
Researchers are working to preserve oral histories in the Burmese-Yi language.
Many children in Yunnan grow up bilingual, speaking both Chinese and the Burmese-Yi language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Yi language - another name for the Sino-Tibetan language spoken in Yunnan
