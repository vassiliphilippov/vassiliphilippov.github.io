Maracaibo

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɑːrəˈkaɪboʊ / (AmE), / ˌmærəˈkaɪbəʊ / (BrE)
a port city in northwestern Venezuela, known as a major oil center
port city in Venezuela

Examples

Maracaibo is famous for its rich oil fields and vibrant culture.
Large tankers frequently arrive at the port of Maracaibo to transport petroleum.
She was born and raised in Maracaibo before moving to Caracas for university.

Etymology

From Spanish Maracaibo, possibly from indigenous language origins.
