LTTE

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛlˌtiːˌtiːˈiː / (AmE), / ˌɛlˌtiːˌtiːˈiː / (BrE)
an armed separatist organization in Sri Lanka that sought to create an independent Tamil state, known for its guerilla and terrorist tactics
Sri Lankan Tamil separatist group

Examples

The LTTE was involved in a lengthy civil conflict in Sri Lanka that ended in 2009.
Many countries have designated the LTTE as a terrorist organization due to its violent tactics.
The defeat of the LTTE marked a turning point in Sri Lanka's history.

Synset Examples

the Tamil Tigers perfected suicide bombing as a weapon of war

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tamil Tigers - Alternative name for the LTTE separatist group in Sri Lanka

Etymology

Acronym of Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam.
