melodiously

1. ADV

/ məˈloʊdiəsli / (AmE), / mɪˈləʊdiəsli / (BrE)
In a way that is pleasant to listen to; in a tuneful or musical manner.
in a melodious, tuneful manner

Examples

The birds sang melodiously in the early morning light.
She played the violin melodiously, captivating her audience.
The wind chimes tinkled melodiously in the gentle breeze.

Synset Examples

she sang melodiously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tunefully - in a tuneful manner
  • Antonym: harshly - in a harsh or unpleasant manner (sound)

Etymology

From 'melodious' (from Latin 'melodia' meaning 'song') + '-ly'.
