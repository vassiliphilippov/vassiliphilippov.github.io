cylindricalness

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈlɪndrɪkəlnəs / (AmE), / sɪˈlɪndrɪk(ə)lnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having the shape or roundness characteristic of a cylinder
quality of being cylindrical

Examples

The cylindricalness of the tank allows for even distribution of pressure inside.
Engineers tested the pipe's cylindricalness to ensure it met manufacturing standards.
He admired the perfect cylindricalness of the marble columns in the ancient temple.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cylindrical - having the shape of a cylinder

Etymology

From 'cylindrical' (from Greek 'kylindros', meaning 'roller') + '-ness' (forming nouns denoting a state or quality).
