supernaturally

1. ADV

/ ˌsuː.pɚˈnætʃ.rə.li / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈnætʃ.rə.li / (BrE)
In a way that is beyond or not explainable by the laws of nature; in a supernatural manner.
in a supernatural manner

Examples

Some believed the ruins were supernaturally protected from intruders.
The forest was supernaturally quiet after the storm passed.
The child seemed supernaturally gifted at playing the piano.

Synset Examples

she was preternaturally beautiful

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preternaturally - in a way that is unusual or beyond what is normal or natural

Etymology

Formed from 'supernatural' + '-ly'; 'supernatural' from Latin 'supernaturalis' (above nature).
