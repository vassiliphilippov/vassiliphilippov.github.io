Mozambique

1. PROPN

/ ˌmoʊ.zəmˈbiːk / (AmE), / ˌməʊ.zæmˈbiːk / (BrE)
a country in southeastern Africa, located on the Indian Ocean coast, which became independent from Portugal in 1975
country in southeastern Africa

Examples

She traveled through Mozambique during her gap year to volunteer at a medical clinic.
The official language of Mozambique is Portuguese, a legacy of its colonial past.
After gaining independence in 1975, Mozambique faced several years of civil conflict.

Etymology

From the name of the Mozambique Channel and Island, likely from the Arabic 'Musa Malik' (Moses the king), a 16th-century sultan.
