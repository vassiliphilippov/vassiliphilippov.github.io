tying

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of fastening or binding things together with a string, rope, or similar material
act of binding things together

Examples

The tying of the package was done with a bright red ribbon.
Proper tying of the knots is essential for safety.
He specialized in the tying of fishing flies for his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: binding - the act of tying or fastening things together
