Nashville

1. PROPN

/ ˈnæʃvɪl / (AmE), / ˈnaʃvɪl / (BrE)
The capital city of Tennessee in the United States, situated on the Cumberland River and known as a center for country music.
capital city of Tennessee, USA

Examples

Many aspiring musicians move to Nashville to pursue a career in country music.
The Grand Ole Opry is one of the most famous attractions in Nashville.
The city of Nashville is often called the "Music City" due to its vibrant music scene.
