pointsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔɪntsmən / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪntsmən / (BrE)
A police officer stationed at a road intersection to direct traffic.
traffic-directing police officer

Examples

The pointsman skillfully directed cars through the busy intersection during rush hour.
After the lights failed, a pointsman was sent to keep traffic moving.
The city relies on a pointsman to manage traffic during large public events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traffic officer - a police officer who controls and manages the flow of traffic
  • Synonym: policeman - a male member of a police force

Etymology

From 'point' (as in a place or position) + 'man'; relating to positioning at a crucial spot for control.
