onomancy

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊ.nəˌmæn.si / (AmE), / ˈɒn.əˌmæn.si / (BrE)
divination by interpreting the letters of a person's name to predict their future or reveal hidden traits.
divination by name letters

Examples

In ancient times, onomancy was practiced to reveal an individual's fate through their name.
Some fortune tellers still use onomancy as part of their mystical services.
He dismissed onomancy as mere superstition, despite its popularity in certain cultures.

Etymology

From Greek 'onoma' (name) and 'manteia' (divination).
