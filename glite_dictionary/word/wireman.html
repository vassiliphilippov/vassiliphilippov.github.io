wireman

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪərmən / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəmən / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, who installs and repairs electric wiring
electrical wiring installer or repairer

Examples

The wireman spent hours replacing the faulty wires in the old house.
As a skilled wireman, he was responsible for ensuring all the electrical connections were safe.
They called a wireman to fix the broken lighting circuit in the building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electrician - a person who installs, maintains, or repairs electrical systems

Etymology

Formed from 'wire' + 'man'.
