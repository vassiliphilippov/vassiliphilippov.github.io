slaty-grey

1. ADJ

/ ˈsleɪti ˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈsleɪti ˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
of a grey color resembling that of slate; dull bluish-grey.
grey-blue color like slate

Examples

The mountains appeared a slaty-grey in the early morning light.
She chose a slaty-grey paint for the living room to create a calm atmosphere.
The bird's slaty-grey feathers helped it blend into the rocky cliffs.

Synset Examples

the slaty sky of dawn

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grey - color between black and white
  • Synonym: bluish-grey - a shade of grey with blue undertones

Etymology

From 'slaty' (having the color or characteristics of slate) + 'grey'.
