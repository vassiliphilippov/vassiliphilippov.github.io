auld langsyne

1. NOUN

/ ɔld læŋˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ɔːld læŋˈzaɪn / (BrE)
times long past, especially times remembered with nostalgia or affection
days long past; nostalgia for the past

Examples

Every New Year's Eve, we sing auld langsyne to remember cherished moments.
He often looks back on his college days as the best of auld langsyne.
The reunion was filled with laughter and stories of auld langsyne.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nostalgia - a sentimental longing for the past

Etymology

Scots, literally 'old long since', from auld (old) + lang (long) + syne (since).
