undeciphered

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɪˈsaɪfərd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈsaɪfəd / (BrE)
not having been interpreted or translated; not solved or decoded.
not interpreted, unsolved, or not decoded

Examples

The ancient manuscript remains undeciphered despite many scholars' efforts.
Some inscriptions at the archaeological site are still undeciphered and mysterious.
The code was so complex that it stayed undeciphered for decades.

Synset Examples

Linear A is still undeciphered

Related Terms

  • Antonym: deciphered - interpreted or solved; decoded
  • Synonym: untranslated - not translated into another language

Etymology

From the prefix un- (not) + deciphered, from decipher (from French déchiffrer, from dechiffrer ‘to decrypt’).
