Gastrocybe

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡæs.trəˈsaɪ.bi / (AmE), / ˌɡas.trəˈsʌɪ.bi / (BrE)
A genus of fungi in the family Secotiaceae, known for their distinctive spore-bearing structures.
genus of fungi in Secotiaceae

Examples

The mushroom identified as Gastrocybe was new to the regional flora.
Researchers discovered a rare Gastrocybe species in the rainforest soil.
The family Secotiaceae includes the genus Gastrocybe, known for its unusual morphology.

Etymology

From Greek 'gastro-' meaning 'stomach' and 'cybe' meaning 'head', referring to the fruiting body's shape.
