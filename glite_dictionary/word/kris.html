kris

1. NOUN

/ krɪs / (AmE), / krɪs / (BrE)
a Malayan dagger with a distinctive wavy blade, often used as a weapon or ceremonial object in Southeast Asia
Malayan wavy-bladed dagger

Examples

The museum displayed an ornately decorated kris from the 18th century.
He admired the intricate patterns on the blade of the traditional kris.
In Javanese culture, the kris is believed to possess mystical powers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dagger - a short pointed knife used as a weapon

Etymology

From Malay keris (dagger).
