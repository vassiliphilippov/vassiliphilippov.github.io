Dublin

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʌblən / (AmE), / ˈdʌblɪn / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Ireland, located on the east coast at the mouth of the River Liffey
capital city of Ireland

Examples

Many tourists visit Dublin each year to explore its history and culture.
She plans to study at a university in Dublin next autumn.
The famous River Liffey flows through the heart of Dublin.

Etymology

From Irish 'Dubhlinn' meaning 'black pool'.
