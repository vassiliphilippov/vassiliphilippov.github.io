Cryptotermes

1. NOUN

/ krɪp.toʊˈtɜr.miːz / (AmE), / krɪp.təʊˈtɜː.miːz / (BrE)
A genus of drywood termites found worldwide, sometimes classified as a subgenus of Kalotermes.
genus of worldwide drywood termites

Examples

Scientists discovered a new species of Cryptotermes inhabiting coastal buildings.
Infestations by Cryptotermes can cause significant damage to wooden furniture.
The genus Cryptotermes is closely related to Kalotermes in termite taxonomy.

Etymology

From Greek kryptos (hidden) + termes (woodworm, termite).
