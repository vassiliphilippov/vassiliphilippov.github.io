unforgivable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnfərˈɡɪvəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnfəˈɡɪvəbəl / (BrE)
so bad as to be impossible to forgive or excuse
impossible to forgive or excuse

Examples

His behavior last night was simply unforgivable and shocked everyone.
The betrayal felt unforgivable, leaving her unable to trust him again.
Some mistakes are minor, but others seem truly unforgivable.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: forgivable - able to be forgiven or excused

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + forgivable, itself from forgive.
