bluestone

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈbluːstəʊn / (BrE)
A bluish-grey sandstone commonly used for paving and building.
bluish-grey sandstone for construction

Examples

The walkway was constructed from durable bluestone slabs.
Many historic buildings feature bluestone in their foundations and facades.
They decided to pave their patio with locally sourced bluestone.
