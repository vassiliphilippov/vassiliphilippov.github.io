Phnom Penh

1. PROPN

/ pnoʊm pɛn / (AmE), / pnom pɛn / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Cambodia, located in the southern part of the country at the junction of three rivers
capital city of Cambodia

Examples

I spent two weeks exploring the vibrant markets and temples of Phnom Penh.
After landing in Cambodia, their first stop was Phnom Penh for a business meeting.
Phnom Penh has experienced rapid growth and development over the past decade.
