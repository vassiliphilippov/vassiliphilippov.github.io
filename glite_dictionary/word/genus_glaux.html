Genus Glaux

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈɡleɪ.ʌks / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈɡlɔːks / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the primrose family, known commonly as sea milkwort.
plant genus; sea milkwort

Examples

The coastal marshes are home to the rare Genus Glaux, also known as sea milkwort.
Botanists recently reclassified Genus Glaux within the primrose family due to genetic studies.
Conservation efforts focus on protecting habitats where Genus Glaux naturally occurs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea milkwort - a low flowering plant often found near coasts; single species in Genus Glaux

Etymology

From Latin 'glaux', borrowed from Ancient Greek γλαῦξ (glaûx), meaning 'owl', a name historically applied to this genus.
