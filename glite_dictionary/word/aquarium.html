aquarium

1. NOUN

/ əˈkwɛriəm / (AmE), / əˈkwɛːrɪəm / (BrE)
a tank or container filled with water in which fish and other aquatic animals and plants are kept
a water tank for keeping fish

Examples

She has a large aquarium in her living room, filled with colorful tropical fish.
The children watched the shark glide silently across the aquarium at the marine center.
He spent hours cleaning the aquarium and arranging the plants inside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fish tank - a container for keeping live fish, synonym for aquarium

Etymology

From Latin 'aquārium', from 'aqua' meaning 'water'.
