environment

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈvaɪrənmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈvaɪərənmənt / (BrE)
The surrounding conditions or influences affecting a person, place, or thing.
surrounding conditions or influences

Examples

He longed for the peaceful environment of his home after a busy day at work.
A supportive environment can help students learn and succeed.
The restaurant creates a relaxing environment for its diners.

Synset Examples

he longed for the comfortable environment of his living room

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surroundings - the things and conditions around someone or something

Etymology

From French environnement (the action of surrounding), from environner (to surround).

2. NOUN

/ ɪnˈvaɪrənmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈvʌɪərənmənt / (BrE)
the surroundings or conditions in which a person, animal, or thing lives or operates
the surrounding conditions or area

Examples

The rainforest provides a unique environment for thousands of plant and animal species.
Children need a safe and nurturing environment to learn and grow.
The company is trying to create a positive work environment for its employees.

Synset Examples

the country — the flat agricultural surround

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surroundings - the things and conditions around a person or place

Etymology

From French 'environner' (to surround), from 'environ' (round about).
