yesteryear

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛstərˌjɪr / (AmE), / ˈjɛstəˌjɪə / (BrE)
A time in the recent or distant past, especially when seen as better or different from the present.
the past; former times

Examples

Many reminisce about the golden days of yesteryear with fondness.
He wore a classic suit that harked back to the fashion of yesteryear.
Old photographs help us glimpse the lives and stories of yesteryear.

Synset Examples

forget the past

Related Terms

  • Synonym: past - the time before the present
  • Antonym: present - the current time or moment
  • Antonym: future - the time yet to come

Etymology

Formed from 'yester-' (meaning last or previous) + 'year'. Patterned after the archaic 'yesteryear' idiom.
