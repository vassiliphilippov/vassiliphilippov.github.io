bitumastic

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɪtjuˈmæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌbɪtjuːˈmæstɪk / (BrE)
a protective coating made from asphalt and filler, used to shield structural metals from weathering and corrosion
asphalt-based protective coating for metals

Examples

The engineers applied a layer of bitumastic to protect the steel beams from corrosion.
To prolong the lifespan of the bridge, workers coated its supports with bitumastic.
Regular inspection and maintenance of the bitumastic can prevent serious structural damage.

Etymology

Blend of 'bitumen' and 'mastic'.
