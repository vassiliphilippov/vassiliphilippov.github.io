Frenchman

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛntʃmən / (AmE), / ˈfrɛntʃmən / (BrE)
A man who is a national or native of France.
male person from France

Examples

The Frenchman greeted us warmly in his Parisian apartment.
As a Frenchman, he was proud of his country's rich cultural heritage.
The artist, a renowned Frenchman, exhibited his paintings in New York.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Frenchwoman - a female person of French nationality
  • Synonym: French people - people of French nationality or descent

Etymology

From French 'Français' (French) + 'man'.
