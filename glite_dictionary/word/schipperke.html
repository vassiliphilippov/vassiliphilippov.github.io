schipperke

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪpərki / (AmE), / ˈskɪpəki / (BrE)
A small, stocky black dog breed originally developed as a watchdog, particularly on boats, in the Netherlands and Belgium.
small black Belgian dog breed

Examples

The family adopted a schipperke because of its alert and energetic temperament.
On their canal boat, a loyal schipperke kept watch over the deck.
Many dog shows highlight the unique features of the schipperke.

Etymology

From Flemish, diminutive of 'schipper' (skipper, boatman), referring to its use as a boat watchdog.
