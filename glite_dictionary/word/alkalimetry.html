alkalimetry

1. NOUN

/ ˌælkəˈlɪmətri / (AmE), / ˌalkəˈlɪmɪtri / (BrE)
the quantitative determination of acids by using standard solutions of alkali in volumetric analysis
determination of acids using alkali solutions
medical

Examples

The laboratory uses alkalimetry to determine the concentration of acids in solution.
Students learned alkalimetry techniques in their analytical chemistry class.
Accurate alkalimetry is essential for quality control in pharmaceutical manufacturing.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: acidimetry - volumetric analysis using acid solutions to measure alkali

Etymology

From French 'alcalimétrie', from 'alkali' and Greek 'metron' (measure).
