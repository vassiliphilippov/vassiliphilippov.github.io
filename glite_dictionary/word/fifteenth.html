fifteenth

1. ADJ

/ fɪfˈtinθ / (AmE), / fɪfˈtiːnθ / (BrE)
coming after the fourteenth in a sequence; 15th in position or order
ordinal number: 15th

Examples

Her birthday falls on the fifteenth of August every year.
He finished in fifteenth place in the marathon.
This is the fifteenth time I've asked you to clean your room.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fourteenth - 14th in position or order
  • Degree Higher: sixteenth - 16th in position or order

Etymology

From Middle English fifteenthe, formed from 'fifteen' + '-th', following the pattern of English ordinals.

2. NOUN

/ ˌfɪfˈtinθ / (AmE), / ˌfɪfˈtiːnθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number corresponding to the number 15 in a sequence; the person or thing in the fifteenth position.
ordinal number for position 15th

Examples

Jessica finished in fifteenth out of over a hundred runners.
Today is the fifteenth anniversary of their wedding.
The winning ticket was drawn by the fifteenth person in line.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fourteenth - the ordinal number for position 14th
  • Degree Higher: sixteenth - the ordinal number for position 16th
