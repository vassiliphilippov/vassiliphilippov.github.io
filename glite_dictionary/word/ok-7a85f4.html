ok

1. ADJ

/ oʊˈkeɪ / (AmE), / əʊˈkeɪ / (BrE)
satisfactory or acceptable in quality, condition, or performance; adequate but not exceptional.
satisfactory; acceptable
informal american

Examples

The hotel was ok, but nothing special.
I feel ok about the results of the test.
Is everything ok with your new job?

Synset Examples

an all-right movie
the passengers were shaken up but are all right
is everything all right?
everything's fine
things are okay
dinner and the movies had been fine
another minute I'd have been fine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acceptable - satisfactory or allowable
  • Synonym: fine - of satisfactory quality; acceptable
  • Degree Higher: excellent - of exceptionally high quality
  • Antonym: unacceptable - not satisfactory; not allowed

Etymology

Originally an abbreviation of 'oll korrect', a humorous misspelling of 'all correct' in early 19th-century American English.
