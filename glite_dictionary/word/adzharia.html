Adzharia

1. PROPN

/ ədˈʒɑːriə / (AmE), / ədˈʒɑːriə / (BrE)
an autonomous province in southwestern Georgia, located on the Black Sea coast
autonomous province in Georgia

Examples

Many tourists visit Adzharia each year to experience its beautiful beaches and diverse culture.
The capital of Adzharia is Batumi, known for its seaside resorts and vibrant nightlife.
After the dissolution of the Soviet Union, Adzharia became an autonomous republic within Georgia.

Etymology

From the Russian 'Adzhariya', from Georgian 'Ačara'.
