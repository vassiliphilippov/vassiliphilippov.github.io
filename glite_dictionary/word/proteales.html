Proteales

1. NOUN

/ proʊˈtiːeɪlz / (AmE), / prəʊˈtiːeɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of flowering plants that includes the family Proteaceae and related families
order of flowering plants (Proteaceae and others)

Examples

Recent studies have helped clarify the evolutionary relationships within the Proteales.
The Proteales include both the Proteaceae and Platanaceae families.
Many unique plant species in Australia belong to the Proteales order.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Proteaceae - the family of flowering plants included in Proteales

Etymology

From New Latin 'Proteales', based on 'Protea', a genus in the Proteaceae family.
