asserter

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɝːtɚ / (AmE), / əˈsɜːtə / (BrE)
a person who confidently states or affirms something as true
person who asserts or affirms

Examples

The asserter of the theory presented his findings at the conference.
In every debate, there is an asserter and a skeptic.
She became known as a frequent asserter of unpopular opinions.

Synset Examples

a bold asserter
a declarer of his intentions
affirmers of traditional doctrine
an asseverator of strong convictions
an avower of his own great intelligence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affirmant - a person who affirms something
  • Antonym: denier - a person who denies something

Etymology

From Latin 'assertor' (one who claims or affirms), from 'asserere' (to assert).
