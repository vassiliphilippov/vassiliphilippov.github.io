sixteenth

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪkstiːnθ / (AmE), / ˈsɪks.tiːnθ / (BrE)
coming after the fifteenth in a sequence; 16th
ordinal number: 16th

Examples

He finished in sixteenth place at the tournament.
The sixteenth chapter of the book reveals the mystery.
This is the sixteenth time I've watched that movie.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fifteenth - ordinal number following the fourteenth, 15th
  • Degree Higher: seventeenth - ordinal number following the sixteenth, 17th

Etymology

Formed from 'sixteen' + '-th', the English ordinal suffix.

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɪksˌtinθ / (AmE), / ˈsɪksˌtiːnθ / (BrE)
One part of something that has been divided into sixteen equal parts.
one of sixteen equal parts

Examples

She divided the cake into sixteen pieces and gave me one sixteenth.
A sixteenth of the company's profits will go to charity this year.
Each student contributed a sixteenth to the group project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sixteenth - the ordinal number matching 16th in a sequence
  • Synonym: sixteenth note - a note having one sixteenth the duration of a whole note in music
  • Degree Lower: quarter - one of four equal parts
  • Degree Higher: thirty-second - one of thirty-two equal parts

Etymology

Formed from "sixteen" with the ordinal suffix "-th".

3. NOUN

/ sɪksˈtinθ / (AmE), / sɪksˈtiːnθ / (BrE)
The person or thing in the position number 16 in a sequence; one that comes after the fifteenth.
the 16th position in a sequence

Examples

She finished in sixteenth place out of twenty competitors.
This is the sixteenth time I've visited the museum.
The sixteenth chapter explains the results in detail.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fifteenth - the 15th position in a sequence
  • Degree Higher: seventeenth - the 17th position in a sequence
