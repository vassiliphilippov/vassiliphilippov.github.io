light-tight

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪtˌtaɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt.taɪt / (BrE)
not allowing any light to pass through; impervious to light.
impervious to light

Examples

The film must be stored in a light-tight container to prevent exposure.
We need light-tight curtains for the laboratory.
Her camera bag is specially designed to be light-tight for sensitive equipment.

Synset Examples

lightproof containers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lightproof - not allowing light to pass through
