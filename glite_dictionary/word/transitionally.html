transitionally

1. ADV

/ trænˈzɪʃənəli / (AmE), / trænˈzɪʃnəli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or characteristic of a transition; as an intermediate step or phase.
in a transitional manner

Examples

The company will operate transitionally until the new management team takes over.
The two species are believed to be transitionally related in their evolutionary history.
She lived transitionally between two cities before settling down permanently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transitional - relating to or characteristic of a transition
