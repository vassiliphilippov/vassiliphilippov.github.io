Brage

1. PROPN

/ ˈbrɑː.ɡə / (AmE), / ˈbrɑː.ɡə / (BrE)
(in Norse mythology) the god of poetry and music, son of Odin.
Norse god of poetry and music

Examples

In ancient tales, Brage was said to inspire poets and musicians alike.
The poems were dedicated to Brage, hoping for his favor and inspiration.
Many Norse bards believed their skill was a gift bestowed by Brage.

Etymology

From Old Norse Bragi, the name of the Norse god of poetry.
