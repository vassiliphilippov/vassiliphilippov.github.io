consistently

1. ADV

/ kənˈsɪstəntli / (AmE), / kənˈsɪstəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is always the same or in agreement with something; regularly and reliably
in a regular and reliable way

Examples

She has consistently delivered excellent results in all her projects.
The team consistently meets its deadlines, no matter how tight the schedule.
If you exercise consistently, you will likely see improvement in your fitness.

Synset Examples

they systematically excluded women

Related Terms

  • Synonym: systematically - in an organized and methodical way
  • Antonym: inconsistently - not in a regular or predictable way

Etymology

From 'consistent' + '-ly'. 'Consistent' from Latin 'consistens' (standing firm).
