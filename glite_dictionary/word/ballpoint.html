ballpoint

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔlˌpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlpɔɪnt / (BrE)
a pen in which the ink is delivered to the writing surface by a small rotating metal ball at its tip
pen with a tiny metal ball tip

Examples

I reached for my ballpoint to sign the document quickly.
She prefers writing with a ballpoint rather than a fountain pen.
The ink in this ballpoint doesn't smudge easily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ballpoint pen - a complete form; the full term for 'ballpoint'
  • Antonym: fountain pen - pen with a metal nib and refillable reservoir

Etymology

Formed from 'ball' and 'point', referring to the ball-bearing tip that delivers ink.
