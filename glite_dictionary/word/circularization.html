circularization

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɜrkjələrəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɜːkjʊləraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of distributing printed notices or advertisements to a large number of people
distribution of printed advertisements

Examples

The company increased its circularization to promote the new product nationwide.
Many small businesses rely on circularization as a cost-effective way to attract customers.
Changes in postal regulations affected the circularization of advertising materials.

Etymology

From 'circular', meaning 'printed advertisement', plus '-ization'.
