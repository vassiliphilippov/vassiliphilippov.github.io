copulation

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːpjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒpjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of sexual intercourse, especially the insertion of the penis into the vagina
sexual intercourse (formal/biological term)

Examples

The biologist observed the copulation of the two birds in their natural habitat.
In many species, copulation is closely tied to the breeding season.
She wrote a paper detailing the rituals that precede copulation among various mammals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intercourse - sexual activity involving penetration
  • Synonym: coitus - sexual intercourse between male and female

Etymology

From Latin copulatio (a joining together), from copulare (to join together).
