dark-blue

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɑrk blu / (AmE), / ˈdɑːk bluː / (BrE)
Of a deep or dark shade of the color blue.
deep or dark shade of blue

Examples

She wore a dark-blue dress to the party, which made her stand out.
The dark-blue sky was filled with stars as night fell.
They chose dark-blue paint for the walls of the living room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: navy blue - a very dark shade of blue
  • Antonym: light blue - a pale or lighter shade of blue
