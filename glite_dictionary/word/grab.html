grab

1. NOUN

/ ɡræb / (AmE), / ɡræb / (BrE)
an act of catching or seizing something quickly with the hands
quick act of catching with the hands

Examples

She made a grab for the falling glass before it hit the floor.
The dog made a quick grab at the toy as it rolled by.
With one swift grab, he caught the hat that blew off his head.

Synset Examples

the infielder's snap and throw was a single motion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snatch - an act of quickly taking something with the hands
  • Synonym: catch - the act of catching something
