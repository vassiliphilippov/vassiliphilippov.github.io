William Shakespeare

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪljəm ˈʃeɪkspɪr / (AmE), / ˈwɪljəm ˈʃeɪkspɪə / (BrE)
(1564–1616) English poet and playwright, regarded as one of the greatest writers in the English language.
famous English playwright and poet

Examples

William Shakespeare wrote some of the most renowned plays in history, including Hamlet and Macbeth.
Many scholars have studied the language and themes of William Shakespeare's works.
