fetishist

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛt̬ɪʃɪst / (AmE), / ˈfɛtɪʃɪst / (BrE)
a person who has a sexual fetish, or who derives sexual pleasure from a specific object, part of the body, or practice
person with a sexual fetish

Examples

It was revealed that he was a well-known fetishist within certain online communities.
The character in the novel is portrayed as a fetishist obsessed with high-heeled shoes.
She suspected her ex-boyfriend was a fetishist because of his unusual collections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fetishism - sexual attraction to objects, body parts, or practices
  • Synonym: voyeur - person who derives sexual pleasure from watching others

Etymology

From French 'fétichiste', from 'fétiche' (fetish), ultimately from Portuguese 'feitiço'.
