evilly

1. ADV

/ ˈiːvɪli / (AmE), / ˈiːvɪli / (BrE)
In a wicked or evil manner; with evil intent.
in a wicked or evil manner

Examples

The villain evilly plotted against the heroes, waiting for the perfect moment to strike.
She smiled evilly as she revealed her plan to the group.
The haunted house owner laughed evilly to scare the visitors.

Synset Examples

act wickedly
grin evilly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wickedly - in a wicked or evil manner

Etymology

From 'evil' + '-ly', forming the adverb.
