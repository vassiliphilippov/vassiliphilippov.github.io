unmemorable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmɛmərəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɛmərəbl / (BrE)
not worth remembering; lacking distinctive or lasting impact.
not worth remembering

Examples

The movie was so unmemorable that I forgot the plot by the next day.
His speech was surprisingly unmemorable despite all the effort he put into it.
The restaurant’s food and atmosphere were entirely unmemorable.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: memorable - worth remembering; easily remembered

Etymology

Formation from prefix un- (not) + memorable.
