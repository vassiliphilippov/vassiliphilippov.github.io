apiece

1. ADV

/ əˈpis / (AmE), / əˈpiːs / (BrE)
for each person or thing; to or for each one considered separately
for each one individually

Examples

The tickets cost $15 apiece for adults.
We were given three cookies apiece at the party.
The team members won a medal apiece for their efforts.

Synset Examples

they received $10 each

Related Terms

  • Synonym: each - for every one of two or more people or things, considered separately

Etymology

From Middle English 'a piece', meaning 'for each piece'.
