Phenergan

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛnərˌɡæn / (AmE), / ˈfɛnəˌɡæn / (BrE)
A trademarked antihistamine drug (promethazine) used to treat allergies and as an antiemetic for motion sickness.
brand-name antihistamine (promethazine)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Phenergan to relieve her allergy symptoms.
If you get motion sick during travel, Phenergan may help prevent nausea.
Some people feel drowsy after taking Phenergan for their allergies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promethazine - an antihistamine used to treat allergy symptoms and nausea
