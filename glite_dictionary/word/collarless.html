collarless

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑlər.ləs / (AmE), / ˈkɒl.ə.ləs / (BrE)
(of clothing) having no collar.
having no collar (of clothing)

Examples

He wore a collarless shirt to the casual dinner party.
The new jacket design is entirely collarless for a modern look.
A collarless dress can appear both elegant and contemporary.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: collared - having a collar

Etymology

Formed from 'collar' plus the suffix '-less', indicating absence.
