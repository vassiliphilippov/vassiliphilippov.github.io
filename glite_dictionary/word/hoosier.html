Hoosier

1. NOUN

/ ˈhuːʒər / (AmE), / ˈhuːzɪə / (BrE)
a native or resident of the US state of Indiana
native or resident of Indiana (US)
american

Examples

My grandfather is a proud Hoosier who grew up on a farm outside Indianapolis.
Several famous athletes are Hoosiers, including basketball players and race car drivers.
During college, I met my first Hoosier roommate from Indiana.

Etymology

Origin uncertain; 19th-century US regional usage referring to Indiana inhabitants.
