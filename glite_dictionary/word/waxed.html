waxed

1. ADJ

/ wækst / (AmE), / wækst / (BrE)
treated by applying or coating with wax, typically to give a smooth or shiny surface
treated or coated with wax

Examples

The waxed floor gleamed in the sunlight, making the entire room look polished and clean.
He twisted the ends of his waxed mustache before leaving for the party.
Many old skis were waxed regularly to help them glide smoothly over the snow.

Synset Examples

waxed floors
waxed mustache

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unwaxed - not treated with wax
