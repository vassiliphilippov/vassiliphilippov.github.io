Combretum

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈbriːtəm / (AmE), / kɒmˈbriːtəm / (BrE)
A genus of tropical and subtropical small shrubs and trees in the family Combretaceae.
genus of tropical shrubs and trees

Examples

The Combretum genus includes several species valued for their medicinal properties.
In southern Africa, many Combretum trees grow along riverbanks.
Researchers are studying Combretum leaves for their potential antibacterial effects.

Etymology

From Latin Combretum, the classical name for a climbing plant.
