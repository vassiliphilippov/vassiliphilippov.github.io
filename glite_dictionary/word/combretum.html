combretum

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈbriːtəm / (AmE), / kəmˈbriːtəm / (BrE)
A shrub or small tree of the genus Combretum, having spikes of small flowers, native to tropical and subtropical regions.
a genus of shrubs or small trees

Examples

The combretum thrives in well-drained soil and warm climates.
Bright red flowers adorn the combretum during the summer months.
Several species of combretum are used in traditional medicine.

Etymology

From modern Latin Combretum, the genus name.
