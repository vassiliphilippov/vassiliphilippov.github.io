spiritedly

1. ADV

/ ˈspɪrɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtɪdli / (BrE)
in a lively, energetic, or enthusiastic manner
in a lively or energetic manner

Examples

She defended her position spiritedly during the debate.
The children played spiritedly in the park all afternoon.
He responded spiritedly to the challenge, impressing everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: energetically - in a way that shows energy and enthusiasm
  • Degree Lower: lively - full of energy and enthusiasm (adjective)

Etymology

From 'spirited', from 'spirit' + '-ed' + '-ly'.
