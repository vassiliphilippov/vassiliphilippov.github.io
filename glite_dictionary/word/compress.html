compress

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈprɛs / (AmE), / kəmˈprɛs / (BrE)
A cloth pad or dressing, sometimes medicated, applied firmly to a part of the body to relieve discomfort, reduce fever, or stop bleeding.
cloth pad applied to body

Examples

She placed a cold compress on his forehead to reduce the fever.
The nurse applied a warm compress to the swollen area to ease the pain.
Doctors often recommend using a moist compress for minor injuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressing - a piece of material applied to a wound to promote healing

Etymology

From Old French *compresser* and Late Latin *compressare*, meaning 'to press together'.

2. VERB

/ kəmˈprɛs / (AmE), / kəmˈprɛs / (BrE)
to make something smaller or more compact by pressing, squeezing, or reducing its volume, often by removing air or space
make more compact by pressing

Examples

The software can compress large files into a smaller size for easy storage.
He used his hands to compress the packing material into the suitcase.
Modern algorithms allow us to compress video without losing much quality.

Synset Examples

compress the data

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squeeze - to press something firmly together
  • Antonym: to expand - to increase in size or volume

Etymology

From Latin 'comprimere', from 'com-' (together) + 'premere' (to press).

3. VERB

/ kəmˈprɛs / (AmE), / kəmˈprɛs / (BrE)
to press or squeeze something into a smaller space; to make more compact by applying pressure
press or squeeze together

Examples

When you compress the files, they take up less disk space.
She compressed her lips in anger, refusing to speak.
The technician used a special machine to compress the material into thin sheets.

Synset Examples

she compressed her lips
the spasm contracted the muscle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squeeze - to firmly press from opposite sides
  • Antonym: to expand - to become or make larger

Etymology

From Latin 'compressare', frequentative of 'comprimere' (to press together), from 'com-' (together) + 'premere' (to press).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01390440-v
Synset POS: v
