brolly

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɑːli / (AmE), / ˈbrɒli / (BrE)
An informal British term for an umbrella.
informal British word for umbrella
informal british

Examples

Don't forget your brolly, it looks like rain today.
She left her brolly on the bus and got soaked walking home.
He always carries a brolly with him, even on sunny days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: umbrella - a device for protection against rain or sun

Etymology

Probably a playful shortening and alteration of 'umbrella'.
