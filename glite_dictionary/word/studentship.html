studentship

1. NOUN

/ ˈstuːdəntʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈstjuːdəntʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or status of being a student, sometimes also a scholarship or award for students
position or status of a student; scholarship

Examples

After receiving the studentship, she was able to concentrate fully on her studies.
His studentship at Oxford was renewed for a second year.
Many universities offer a studentship to help cover tuition fees for postgraduate students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scholarship - an award of financial aid for a student

Etymology

Formed from 'student' + suffix '-ship', denoting state or condition.
