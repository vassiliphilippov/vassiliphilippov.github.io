stainless

1. ADJ

/ ˈsteɪnləs / (AmE), / ˈsteɪnləs / (BrE)
(of reputation or character) free from moral blemish or dishonor; pure and without fault.
free from moral blemish; untainted

Examples

Despite years in politics, her stainless reputation remained untouched by scandal.
The hero was admired for his stainless character and unwavering honesty.
They trusted him completely, believing his motives to be stainless.

Synset Examples

his unsullied name
an untarnished reputation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unsullied - not spoiled or made impure; untainted
  • Synonym: untarnished - not made less valuable or reputable; unblemished
  • Antonym: blemished - damaged or marked; not pure

Etymology

Formed from 'stain' + '-less', originally in reference to being unmarked or clean.
