Merida

1. PROPN

/ məˈriːdə / (AmE), / məˈriːdə / (BrE)
the capital city of the Mexican state of Yucatán, known for its colonial architecture and rich cultural heritage
capital of Yucatán, Mexico

Examples

Many tourists visit Merida to explore its vibrant markets and historic city center.
Merida is renowned for its annual cultural festivals showcasing music, dance, and cuisine.
Flights to Merida are available from several major international airports.

Etymology

From Spanish Mérida, named after the city of Mérida in Spain.
