Vermonter

1. NOUN

/ vɚˈmɑːntər / (AmE), / vəːˈmɒntə / (BrE)
a native or resident of the state of Vermont in the United States
a person from Vermont, US
american

Examples

Her father is a proud Vermonter who loves the Green Mountains.
The festival brought together Vermonters from every corner of the state.
As a lifelong Vermonter, she knows all the best hiking trails.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: New Yorker - a native or resident of New York
  • Synonym: Vermont - a state in the northeastern United States

Etymology

Formed from 'Vermont' (the US state) with the suffix '-er' denoting origin or association.
