NGO

1. NOUN

/ ɛn dʒi oʊ / (AmE), / ɛn dʒiː əʊ / (BrE)
An organization that operates independently from any government, typically to address social, environmental, or humanitarian issues.
non-governmental organization

Examples

Many international NGOs provide disaster relief in areas affected by natural calamities.
She works for an environmental NGO that fights deforestation.
Several NGOs attended the conference to discuss human rights issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonprofit - an organization not intended to make a profit

Etymology

Initialism of 'non-governmental organization'.
