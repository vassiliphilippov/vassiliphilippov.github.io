handbag

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌbæɡ / (BrE)
a small bag used for carrying money, personal items, and accessories, especially by women
small bag for personal items

Examples

She put her keys and phone into her handbag before leaving the house.
He bought his mother a new leather handbag for her birthday.
The thief tried to snatch the woman's handbag in the busy street.

Synset Examples

she reached into her bag and found a comb

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purse - a small bag, especially for coins or money

Etymology

From 'hand' + 'bag', indicating a bag carried in the hand.
