spearpoint

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪrˌpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈspɪəpɔɪnt / (BrE)
the pointed and sharpened head of a spear, typically made of metal or stone
pointed head of a spear

Examples

Archaeologists discovered a bronze spearpoint in the ancient burial site.
The warrior carefully sharpened his spearpoint before the battle.
The museum display included a collection of spearpoints from different historical periods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arrowhead - the pointed head of an arrow, often made of stone or metal
  • Synonym: spearhead - the sharp, pointed tip of a spear
