spheroidal

1. ADJ

/ sfɪˈrɔɪ.dl̩ / (AmE), / sfɪəˈrɔɪ.dl̩ / (BrE)
having the shape or nature of a sphere or ellipsoid; nearly spherical in form
nearly spherical in shape

Examples

The planet is not perfectly round but rather spheroidal in shape due to its rotation.
Certain bacteria adopt a spheroidal structure under specific growth conditions.
The gemstone's spheroidal appearance made it resemble a small marble.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spherical - having the shape of a sphere

Etymology

From Greek sphaira (sphere) and the suffix -oid (like, form).
