serially

1. ADV

/ ˈsɪriəli / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəli / (BrE)
In a serial manner; one after another in a sequence.
in a serial manner, sequentially

Examples

The experiments were conducted serially rather than all at once.
The data were processed serially to maintain strict order.
Chapters in this book are arranged serially for a logical flow.

Synset Examples

serially composed music

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sequentially - in a sequence, in order
  • Antonym: simultaneously - at the same time

Etymology

From 'serial' (from French 'sériel', from Latin 'series'), plus adverbial suffix '-ly'.
