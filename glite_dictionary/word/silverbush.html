silverbush

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəbʊʃ / (BrE)
a European shrub with silvery, hairy leaves, evergreen foliage, and pale yellow flowers
silvery-leaved European shrub

Examples

The silverbush adds a touch of brightness to gardens with its unique foliage.
We planted a row of silverbushes along the path to create a striking border.
The silverbush thrives in well-drained, sunny areas and is valued for its evergreen leaves.

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɪl.vər.bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɪl.və.bʊʃ / (BrE)
A deciduous North American shrub with silvery leaves and fruits, often grown for ornamental purposes.
deciduous North American shrub with silvery foliage

Examples

The striking silver leaves of the silverbush make it a popular choice for gardens.
We planted a row of silverbushes along the fence to create a natural border.
The silverbush thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and attracts a variety of birds.

Etymology

From 'silver' (referring to the leaf color) and 'bush' (shrub).
