quirkiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɝːkinəs / (AmE), / ˈkwɜːkɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being unusual in an interesting or appealing way; a peculiar habit or characteristic
unusual or peculiar quality; peculiarity

Examples

Her quirkiness makes her stand out from the crowd in the most charming way.
The quirkiness of the café's décor attracted many curious customers.
He is loved for his intelligence and his quirkiness as well.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peculiarity - an unusual or distinctive feature or habit
  • Antonym: normality - the state of being usual or expected

Etymology

From 'quirky' + '-ness'; 'quirky' itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to 'quirk' (a peculiar trait).
