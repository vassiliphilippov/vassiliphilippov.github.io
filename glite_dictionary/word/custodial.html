custodial

1. ADJ

/ kəˈstoʊdiəl / (AmE), / kʌsˈtəʊdiəl / (BrE)
relating to or providing protective supervision, especially of people or property; responsible for safekeeping or guardianship.
providing protective supervision

Examples

The children are under custodial supervision while their parents are at work.
She was given a custodial role, ensuring the safety of museum artifacts.
The facility offers both medical and custodial care for elderly residents.

Synset Examples

daycare that is educational and not just custodial
tutelary gods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tutelary - serving as a guardian or protector
  • Synonym: guardian - a person who protects or supervises someone or something
  • Antonym: negligent - failing to take proper care in doing something

Etymology

From Latin custodia (guardianship) + -al.
