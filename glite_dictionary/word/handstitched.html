handstitched

1. ADJ

/ ˈhænd.stɪtʃt / (AmE), / ˈhænd.stɪtʃt / (BrE)
sewn by hand rather than by machine
sewn by hand, not machine

Examples

The delicate dress was handstitched with great care.
Each handstitched quilt showed the artisan's skill.
She preferred handstitched details on her clothing for a personal touch.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: machine-stitched - sewn by machine rather than by hand
