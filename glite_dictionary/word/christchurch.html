Christchurch

1. PROPN

/ ˈkraɪstˌtʃɜrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈkraɪst.tʃɜːtʃ / (BrE)
A city in southeastern New Zealand, known as the largest city on the South Island and a center of agriculture and industry.
city in southeastern New Zealand

Examples

The city of Christchurch is famous for its beautiful gardens and innovative architecture.
After the earthquake, Christchurch underwent major reconstruction and development.
Many tourists visit Christchurch as the gateway to exploring New Zealand's South Island.

Etymology

Named after Christ Church, Oxford, England, by early British settlers.
