ago

1. ADJ

/ əˈɡoʊ / (AmE), / əˈɡəʊ / (BrE)
used to indicate how much time has passed since a particular event or point in the past
indicating a time in the past

Examples

She left for Paris three years ago.
I saw that movie a long time ago, but I still remember it well.
Two hours ago, it was raining heavily outside.

Synset Examples

two years ago
‘agone’ is an archaic word for ‘ago’

Etymology

From Old English 'āgān' (to pass (of time)), past participle of 'agan' (to go).

WordNet ID: oewn-01732708-s
Synset POS: s

/ əˈɡoʊ / (AmE), / əˈɡəʊ / (BrE)
used to indicate how much time has passed since a particular event occurred
indicating time in the past

Examples

It seems like just yesterday, but it was actually months ago.
