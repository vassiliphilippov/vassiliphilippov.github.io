compsognathus

1. NOUN

/ kɑmpˈsɑgnəθəs / (AmE), / kɒmpˈsɒgnəθəs / (BrE)
a very small, bipedal, carnivorous dinosaur from the late Jurassic period, found in Bavaria
small Jurassic carnivorous dinosaur

Examples

The compsognathus was among the smallest dinosaurs ever discovered.
A fossilized compsognathus was unearthed in the Bavarian limestone.
Paleontologists believe that compsognathus hunted insects and small vertebrates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dinosaur - any of a group of extinct reptiles of the Mesozoic era

Etymology

From Greek kompsos (elegant) + gnathos (jaw), referring to its slender jaw.
