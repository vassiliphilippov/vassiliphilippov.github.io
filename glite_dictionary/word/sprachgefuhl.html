sprachgefuhl

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃprɑːxɡəˌfuːl / (AmE), / ˈʃprɑːxɡəˌfuːl / (BrE)
An intuitive sense for the natural idiom and usage of a language; a feel for what sounds idiomatic or correct to native speakers.
intuitive sense of idiomatic language

She seemed to have remarkable sprachgefuhl, always choosing just the right expression in every situation.
A good translator relies on sprachgefuhl as much as on dictionaries and grammar books.
Developing sprachgefuhl takes years of reading, listening, and speaking with native speakers.

Etymology
