robustly

1. ADV

/ rəˈbʌstli / (AmE), / rəˈbʌstli / (BrE)
In a strong and healthy way; in a vigorous or forceful manner.
in a strong or vigorous manner

Examples

The company grew robustly throughout the last decade, despite economic challenges.
She argued robustly in favor of the new policy during the meeting.
The engine is designed to perform robustly in harsh environmental conditions.

Synset Examples

he was robustly built

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vigorously - in a way that involves physical strength, effort, or energy
