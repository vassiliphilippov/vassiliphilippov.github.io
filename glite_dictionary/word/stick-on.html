stick-on

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɪk ɑn / (AmE), / ˈstɪk ɒn / (BrE)
(of an object) having a surface already coated with adhesive, so that it can be attached simply by pressing into place.
having a self-adhesive surface

Examples

I bought a pack of stick-on labels for organizing my files.
She used stick-on stamps to mail all the invitations quickly.
The kids decorated their folders with colorful stick-on shapes and stars.

Synset Examples

stick-on labels

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-adhesive - able to stick without additional glue or paste
