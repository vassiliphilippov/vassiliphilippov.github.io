cubiform

1. ADJ

/ ˈkjuːbɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈkjuːbɪˌfɔːm / (BrE)
having the shape of a cube; cube-shaped.
cube-shaped

Examples

The crystal has a distinctly cubiform appearance under the microscope.
Architects sometimes design cubiform buildings to create a modern look.
The mineral's cubiform structure makes it easy to identify among other samples.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cuboid - shaped like a rectangular box, similar to a cube
  • Antonym: spherical - shaped like a sphere

Etymology

From Latin 'cubus' (cube) + '-form' (shaped).
