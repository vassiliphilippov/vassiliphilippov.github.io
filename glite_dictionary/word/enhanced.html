enhanced

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈhænst / (AmE), / ɪnˈhɑːnst / (BrE)
increased or improved in value, beauty, or quality
improved or increased in value or quality

Examples

The new software features an enhanced user interface that is both intuitive and visually appealing.
After renovation, the house had enhanced value and attracted many potential buyers.
Her reputation was enhanced by her dedication and professionalism.

Synset Examples

her enhanced beauty was the result of a good night's sleep rather than makeup
careful cleaning was responsible for the enhanced value of the painting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: improved - made better; increased in quality or value
  • Antonym: diminished - made smaller or reduced in value, importance, or intensity

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'enhance,' from Middle English 'enhauncen', from Anglo-French 'enhauncer' (to raise).
