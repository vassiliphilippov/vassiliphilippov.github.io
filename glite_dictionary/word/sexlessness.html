sexlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛksləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈseksləsnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of having no evident sex or sexual organs
absence of sex or sexual organs

Examples

The scientist noted the sexlessness of the newly discovered species.
The sculpture's sexlessness was intentional, challenging traditional representations of the human body.
Some plants can reproduce asexually due to their inherent sexlessness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asexuality - the quality or state of lacking sexual feelings or characteristics
