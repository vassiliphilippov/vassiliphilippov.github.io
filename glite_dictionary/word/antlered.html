antlered

1. ADJ

/ ˈænt.lɚd / (AmE), / ˈænt.ləd / (BrE)
(of an animal) having antlers on its head.
having antlers

Examples

The antlered stag emerged cautiously from the forest's edge.
Only mature males in this species are antlered, making them easy to identify during mating season.
An antlered moose can be an impressive and intimidating sight in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horned - having horns; provided with horns

Etymology

From 'antler' + '-ed', forming an adjective meaning 'provided with antlers'.
