goldfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a small freshwater fish with golden or orange-red coloring, commonly kept in ponds or aquariums as a pet
small golden ornamental aquarium fish

Examples

My brother keeps a goldfish in a bowl on his desk.
The children were fascinated by the bright colors of the goldfish swimming in the pond.
We bought a new aquarium and filled it with several goldfish.
