arching

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑrtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɑːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
forming or resembling an arch in shape or structure.
curved like an arch

Examples

The tall windows had arching frames that let in plenty of sunlight.
A gentle, arching bridge crossed the stream in the garden.
She admired the arching branches of the willow tree above her.

Synset Examples

an arched ceiling

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arched - having a curved shape like an arch
