surroundings

1. NOUN

/ səˈraʊn.dɪŋz / (AmE), / səˈraʊn.dɪŋz / (BrE)
The conditions and things that exist around a person, place, or thing, especially as they affect how someone feels or behaves.
environmental conditions around something

Examples

She quickly became comfortable in her new surroundings at college.
The animal's surroundings play a major role in its behavior.
It's important to keep your surroundings clean and tidy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: environment - the overall conditions around someone or something
  • Synonym: setting - the place or type of surroundings where something is positioned

2. NOUN

/ səˈraʊndɪŋz / (AmE), / səˈraʊndɪŋz / (BrE)
The area or conditions around a person, place, or thing.
area or conditions around something

Examples

She quickly adapted to her new surroundings after moving to the city.
The hotel is known for its beautiful natural surroundings.
Children learn better when their surroundings are comfortable and stimulating.

Synset Examples

the country — the flat agricultural surround

Related Terms

  • Synonym: environment - The physical conditions that surround someone or something

Etymology

From 'surround' (to encircle) + plural noun suffix '-ings', first used in this sense in the late 18th century.
