36th

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɝːti sɪksθ / (AmE), / ˈθɜːti sɪksθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number that comes after thirty-fifth in counting order; being number thirty-six in a sequence
ordinal number for thirty-six

Examples

She finished in 36th place out of a hundred runners.
The 36th anniversary of the event will be celebrated next month.
He lives on the 36th floor of the skyscraper.
