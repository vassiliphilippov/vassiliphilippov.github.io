Andrei Gromyko

1. PROPN

/ ˈændreɪ groʊˈmiːkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈandreɪ grɒˈmiːkəʊ / (BrE)
(1909–1989) Soviet diplomat and politician, ambassador to the United States and the United Nations, and later Foreign Minister and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet.
Soviet diplomat and politician (1909–1989)

Examples

Andrei Gromyko played a key role in negotiating major arms control agreements during the Cold War.
The Soviet delegation, led by Andrei Gromyko, addressed the United Nations General Assembly in 1960.
Biographies of Andrei Gromyko often highlight his reputation as a skilled and pragmatic diplomat.
