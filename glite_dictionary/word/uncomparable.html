uncomparable

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.kəmˈpɛr.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌn.kəmˈpær.ə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be compared because of lack of similarity or relevant features; unsuitable for comparison
not suitable for comparison

Examples

The data collected from different countries is uncomparable due to varying standards of measurement.
These two paintings are uncomparable because they belong to completely different artistic movements.
Some experiences are so unique that they remain uncomparable to anything else in life.

Synset Examples

an incomparable week of rest and pleasure
the computer proceeds with its incomparable logic and efficiency
this report is incomparable with the earlier ones because of different breakdowns of the data

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incomparable - too good or extreme to be compared; having no equal

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'comparable'.
