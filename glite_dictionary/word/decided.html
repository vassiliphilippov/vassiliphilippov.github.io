decided

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈsaɪdɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈsaɪdɪd / (BrE)
clearly noticeable or marked; definite and unmistakable.
clearly noticeable; marked

Examples

She was at a decided disadvantage during the negotiations.
There was a decided improvement in his health after the treatment.
The crowd gave a decided response to the proposal.

Synset Examples

noticed a distinct improvement
at a distinct (or decided) disadvantage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distinct - clearly noticeable, easy to see or recognize
  • Synonym: obvious - easy to perceive or understand; clear, self-evident

Etymology

From the past participle of 'decide,' from Latin 'decidere' (to determine, settle).
