splintering

1. NOUN

/ ˈsplɪntərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsplɪntərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of breaking or chipping into small, sharp fragments
breaking into small sharp fragments

Examples

The old chair showed signs of splintering along the legs after years of use.
Wearing gloves is recommended to avoid splintering when handling rough wooden boards.
Repeated hammering resulted in the splintering of the fence post.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragmentation - the process of breaking into fragments
  • Synonym: shattering - the act or process of breaking into many pieces

Etymology

From the verb 'splinter', which comes from Middle Dutch 'splinter' (a splint or chip).
