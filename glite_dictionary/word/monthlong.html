monthlong

1. ADJ

/ ˈmʌnθˌlɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈmʌnθˌlɒŋ / (BrE)
lasting for an entire month; continuing for a month in duration.
lasting for a month

Examples

She completed a monthlong training program before starting her new job.
Their monthlong vacation in Europe was a dream come true.
Due to renovations, the museum had a monthlong closure.

Synset Examples
