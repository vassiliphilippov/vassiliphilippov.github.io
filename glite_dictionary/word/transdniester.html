Transdniester

1. PROPN

/ trænsˈdniː.ɛstər / (AmE), / tranzˈdniː.ɛstə / (BrE)
A self-declared, de facto unrecognized state in Eastern Europe that has declared independence from Moldova, also known as Transnistria.
unrecognized breakaway state in Eastern Europe

Examples

Many international organizations do not recognize Transdniester as an independent country.
The dispute between Moldova and Transdniester has persisted since the early 1990s.
Travelers to Moldova are advised to avoid the region of Transdniester due to political instability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Transnistria - Alternative name for Transdniester, unrecognized state in Eastern Europe

Etymology

From Russian and Romanian for 'beyond the Dniester' (River Dniester).
