loveless

1. ADJ

/ ˈlʌv.ləs / (AmE), / ˈlʌv.ləs / (BrE)
lacking love, affection, or warmth; without love
without love or affection

Examples

She felt trapped in a loveless marriage for many years.
The novel tells the story of a loveless childhood and its consequences.
He grew up in a cold, loveless environment that shaped his personality.

Synset Examples

a loveless marriage

Related Terms

  • Antonym: affectionate - showing fondness or tenderness

Etymology

Formed from 'love' + suffix '-less' (meaning 'without').

2. ADJ

/ ˈlʌv.ləs / (AmE), / ˈlʌv.lɪs / (BrE)
without love; lacking affection or warmth of feeling.
without love or affection

Examples

The protagonist grew up in a loveless home, which shaped his personality.
Their marriage had become cold and loveless over the years.
She often spoke of her loveless childhood in interviews.

Synset Examples

a loveless childhood

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loving - showing love or affection
  • Antonym: affectionate - showing fondness or tenderness

Etymology

From 'love' + '-less' (without).
