Vasarely

1. PROPN

/ vɑːsəˈrɛli / (AmE), / vasəˈrɛli / (BrE)
Victor Vasarely (1908–1997), French painter born in Hungary, known as a pioneer of op art.
Hungarian-born French op art painter

Examples

Vasarely is often credited with bringing optical art to international attention.
A new exhibition showcases the geometric paintings of Vasarely.
Many designers have been inspired by the visual illusions created by Vasarely.

Etymology

From the Hungarian surname Vasarely, taken by Victor Vasarely after his birthplace, Vásárhely.
