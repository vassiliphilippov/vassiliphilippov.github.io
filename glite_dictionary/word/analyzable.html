analyzable

1. ADJ

/ əˈnæ.ləˌzaɪ.bəl / (AmE), / əˈnæl.ɪ.zə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be analyzed or divided into parts for examination or study.
capable of being analyzed

Examples

The data collected from the experiment were analyzable using advanced statistical methods.
Her writing style is complex and not easily analyzable by traditional linguistic approaches.
Only analyzable information will be considered for the upcoming research report.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unanalyzable - not able to be analyzed
  • Synonym: divisible - capable of being divided

Etymology

From 'analyze' + '-able', after 'analyzable' in 19th-century scientific writing.
