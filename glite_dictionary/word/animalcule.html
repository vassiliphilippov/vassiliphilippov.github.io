animalcule

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.ɪˈmæl.kjul / (AmE), / ˌæn.ɪˈmæl.kjuːl / (BrE)
a microscopic organism, especially a protozoan such as an amoeba or paramecium
microscopic organism, especially protozoan

Examples

Through the microscope, the scientist observed several curious animalcules swimming in the pond water.
In the eighteenth century, many believed animalcules were responsible for spreading diseases.
Some early biologists were fascinated by the shapes and movements of tiny animalcules.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microorganism - a microscopic living organism
  • Synonym: bacterium - a type of microscopic organism; member of Bacteria

Etymology

From Latin 'animalculum', diminutive of 'animal' (living being).
