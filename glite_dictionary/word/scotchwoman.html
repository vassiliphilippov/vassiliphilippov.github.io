Scotchwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɑʧˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈskɒʧˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female native or inhabitant of Scotland
female native of Scotland

Examples

The famous author is a proud Scotchwoman who often writes about her homeland.
As a Scotchwoman, she celebrates traditional Scottish holidays with great enthusiasm.
The Scotchwoman wore a tartan skirt to the cultural festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Scotswoman - a female native or inhabitant of Scotland
  • Antonym: Scotchman - a male native or inhabitant of Scotland

Etymology

From 'Scotch', an older term for Scottish, and 'woman'.
