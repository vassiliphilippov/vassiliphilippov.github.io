numerable

1. ADJ

/ ˈnuː.mɚ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.mərə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be counted; capable of being enumerated.
able to be counted

Examples

The museum's collection contains thousands of numerable items from various historical periods.
Her investments included only numerable assets that could be easily tracked and valued.
With numerable errors on the page, the editor asked for a thorough revision.

Synset Examples

countable sins
numerable assets

Related Terms

  • Synonym: countable - able to be counted
  • Antonym: innumerable - too many to be counted

Etymology

From Latin 'numerabilis', from 'numerare' (to count).
