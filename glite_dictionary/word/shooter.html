shooter

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈʃuːtə / (BrE)
a person who throws the dice in the game of craps
person who throws dice in craps

Examples

The shooter held the dice tightly before making his throw.
In craps, the shooter gets to roll until they lose.
Everyone at the table cheered when the shooter rolled a winning combination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: craps - a gambling game played with two dice

4. NOUN

/ ˈʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈʃuːtə / (BrE)
a person who uses a gun, bow, or other weapon to shoot, especially judged by their skill or accuracy
person who shoots a weapon

Examples

He is an excellent shooter and rarely misses his target.
The team relied on their best shooter to score the winning point.
As a novice shooter, she practiced at the range every weekend.

Synset Examples

he is a crack shot
a poor shooter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marksman - a person skilled in shooting
  • Synonym: sniper - a person who shoots from a concealed place, often at long range
  • Antonym: non-shooter - a person who does not shoot

Etymology

From shoot + -er, attested since Middle English.
