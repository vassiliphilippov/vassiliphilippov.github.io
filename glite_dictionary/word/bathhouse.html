bathhouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæθˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈbɑːθˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a building with facilities for people to change clothes and sometimes to bathe, especially near a swimming area or beach
public building for bathers to change or bathe

Examples

The old bathhouse by the lake is now a community center.
After swimming at the beach, the children changed in the bathhouse.
The city renovated the historic bathhouse to preserve its architectural charm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: changing room - a room for changing clothes, especially at a public pool or gym

2. NOUN

/ ˈbæθˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈbɑːθˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a building in which people can bathe, usually one containing public baths
public building for bathing

Examples

The old bathhouse on Main Street is now a museum.
Many people used to visit the local bathhouse for relaxation and socializing.
The city restored the historic bathhouse as part of its heritage project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spa - a place with mineral springs for bathing and health treatments
