lentiginose

1. ADJ

/ lɛnˈtɪdʒɪˌnoʊs / (AmE), / lɛnˈtɪdʒɪnəʊs / (BrE)
covered with or resembling freckles; exhibiting freckles or small pigmentation spots on the skin.
resembling or covered with freckles
medical

Examples

Her skin had a lentiginose complexion after spending the summer outdoors.
The dermatologist noted several lentiginose patches on his arms.
Certain genetic conditions can lead to a lentiginose appearance early in life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freckled - covered with small brownish spots on the skin, especially from sun exposure

Etymology

From Latin 'lentiginosus', from 'lentigo' meaning 'freckle'.
