tanglebush

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæŋɡəlˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈtæŋɡl̩ˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
a spiny, branching deciduous shrub of the southwestern United States with clusters of small yellow-white flowers appearing before the leaves, followed by black berrylike fruits
spiny southwestern U.S. shrub with black fruits

Examples

We spotted a tanglebush growing near the desert trail, its branches covered in dark berries.
The delicate flowers of the tanglebush appear before any leaves in early spring.
Wildlife in the area often feed on the berries produced by the tanglebush.
