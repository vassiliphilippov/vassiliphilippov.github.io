Haemulon album

1. NOUN

/ hiːˈmjuːlɒn ˈæl.bəm / (AmE), / hiːˈmjuːlɒn ˈæl.bəm / (BrE)
A species of marine fish in the grunt family, noted for its red mouth, found in the western Atlantic from Florida to Brazil.
species of grunt fish with a red mouth

Examples

The coral reefs here are home to the colorful Haemulon album, easily recognized by its distinctive red mouth.
While diving off the coast of Brazil, we spotted several Haemulon album near the rocky outcrops.
Fishermen in Florida sometimes catch Haemulon album among other grunts in their nets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white grunt - common name for related fish in the grunt family

Etymology

From Latin: 'haemulon' (a kind of sea fish) and 'album' meaning 'white', referring to its coloration.
