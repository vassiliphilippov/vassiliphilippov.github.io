USAF

1. NOUN

/ ˌjuː ɛs eɪ ˈɛf / (AmE), / ˌjuː ɛs eɪ ˈɛf / (BrE)
abbreviation for United States Air Force, the aerial and space warfare service branch of the United States Armed Forces.
United States Air Force (abbreviation)

Examples

He served in the USAF for more than twenty years before retiring.
The USAF conducted a series of exercises to test their new aircraft.
Graduates from the academy often aspire to become officers in the USAF.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: United States Air Force - the full name of the USAF
  • Synonym: RAF - Royal Air Force, the UK's air force

Etymology

Acronym formed from United States Air Force.
