intracutaneous

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.trə.kjuˈteɪ.ni.əs / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trə.kjuːˈteɪ.ni.əs / (BrE)
Situated, occurring, or applied within the layers of the skin; intradermal.
within the layers of the skin
medical

Examples

The doctor administered an intracutaneous injection to test for allergies.
Some medications are better absorbed when given intracutaneous rather than subcutaneously.
Researchers observed a mild reaction at the intracutaneous test site.

Synset Examples

an intradermal injection

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intradermal - situated or applied within the layers of the skin

Etymology

From Latin "intra" (within) + "cutaneous" (of the skin, from Latin "cutis" meaning skin).
