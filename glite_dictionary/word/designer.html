designer

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈzaɪnər / (AmE), / dɪˈzʌɪnə / (BrE)
A person who plans and decorates the interiors of buildings professionally.
interior design professional

Examples

She hired a renowned designer to transform her living room into a modern space.
The designer presented several mood boards to help the clients visualize their new office.
Many people rely on a professional designer for advice on color schemes and furniture placement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interior decorator - a person whose profession is decorating the inside of buildings

Etymology

From design + -er, meaning 'one who designs'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10230249-n
Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ dɪˈzaɪnər / (AmE), / dɪˈzʌɪnə / (BrE)
a person who creates the plans or designs for something, especially buildings, clothing, or other objects
a person who creates designs or plans

Examples

As a leading designer, she has contributed to several award-winning buildings.
The company hired a designer to create the plans for their new headquarters.
Many people dream of becoming a famous fashion designer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: architect - a person who designs buildings

Etymology

From design + -er; 'design' from Latin 'designare' (to mark out), and '-er' denoting an agent.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09824898-n
Synset POS: n

/ dɪˈzaɪnər / (AmE), / dɪˈzʌɪnə / (BrE)

person who creates visual concepts

Examples

She worked as a designer for a major advertising agency.
The company is looking to hire a talented graphic designer to refresh their brand image.
As a freelance designer, he created logos and brochures for small businesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graphic artist - a person who creates visual art, especially for commercial purposes
  • Synonym: illustrator - a person who draws or creates pictures for books, magazines, or advertising

Etymology

From design + -er, meaning a person who designs.

4. NOUN

/ dɪˈzaɪnər / (AmE), / dɪˈzʌɪnə / (BrE)
a person who devises secret plans or schemes, often with a negative or conspiratorial purpose
person who devises secret plots

Examples

The investigation revealed that he was the chief designer of the complex financial scam.
She acted as the designer behind the elaborate scheme to defraud investors.
Authorities arrested the alleged designer of the plot before it could be carried out.

Synset Examples

he is believed to be the principal designer of the terrorist bombing attack

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plotter - person who secretly plans something, especially something illegal or harmful
  • Synonym: conspirator - person who takes part in a secret plan to do something unlawful or harmful
  • Antonym: victim - person harmed by a plot or scheme

Etymology

From design + -er; design from Latin 'designare' (to mark out).
