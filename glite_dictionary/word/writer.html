writer

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtər / (AmE), / ˈraɪtə / (BrE)
a person who writes books, articles, or other texts; someone who is able to write and has written something
person who writes texts

Examples

She is a famous writer who has published several novels and short stories.
The writer delivered an inspiring speech to the audience about creativity.
Every writer develops their own unique voice over time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: author - the creator of a written work

Etymology

From Old English 'writa' (scribe, author), from 'writan' (to write).

2. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtər / (AmE), / ˈraɪtə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to write books, stories, articles, or other texts.
person who writes professionally

Examples

She became a successful writer after publishing her first novel.
The writer received an award for his investigative journalism.
As a freelance writer, she contributes articles to several magazines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: author - a person who writes books or other works
  • Antonym: editor - a person who prepares written material for publication

Etymology

From Old English writere (one who writes), from writan (to write).
