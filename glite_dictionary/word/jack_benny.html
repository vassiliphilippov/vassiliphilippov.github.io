Jack Benny

1. PROPN

/ dʒæk ˈbɛni / (AmE), / dʒæk ˈbeni / (BrE)
(1894–1974) a United States comedian celebrated for his exceptional comic timing, delivery, and self-deprecating humor.
famous American comedian (1894–1974)

Examples

Many comedians have cited Jack Benny as an inspiration for their own style of humor.
The timeless wit of Jack Benny continues to make audiences laugh even decades after his performances.
I recently watched a classic episode of Jack Benny's radio show and was impressed by his comedic timing.
