Michigander

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪʃɪˈɡændər / (AmE), / ˌmɪʃɪˈɡændə / (BrE)
a native or resident of the state of Michigan, USA
a person from Michigan

Examples

She proudly calls herself a Michigander after living in Detroit for twenty years.
Many Michiganders enjoy spending summer weekends on the Great Lakes.
The governor addressed the crowd, thanking every Michigander for their resilience and unity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Michiganian - a native or resident of Michigan

Etymology

From Michigan + -ander (by humorous analogy with gander, attested from mid-19th century).
