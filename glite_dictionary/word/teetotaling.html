teetotaling

1. NOUN

/ tiˈtoʊtəlɪŋ / (AmE), / tiːˈtəʊtəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice or act of abstaining completely from alcoholic drinks
abstaining from alcohol

Examples

My grandfather's teetotaling was a source of pride in the family.
Her strict teetotaling surprised everyone at the party.
They admired his teetotaling during college, despite the pressures to drink.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abstinence - the practice of refraining from something, especially alcohol

Etymology

From 'teetotal', possibly an emphatic reduplication of 'total' from English dialect.
