wonderworking

1. ADJ

/ ˈwʌndɚˌwɝkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwʌndəˌwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
performing or able to perform wonders or miracles.
performing miracles or wonders

Examples

Many believed the wonderworking saint could heal the sick with a single touch.
The legend tells of a wonderworking wizard who lived in the forest.
He was known for his wonderworking abilities, which amazed the entire village.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miraculous - having the nature of a miracle; amazing, supernatural

Etymology

From 'wonder' (something that causes astonishment) + 'working' (doing, performing).
