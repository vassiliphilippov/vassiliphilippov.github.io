huisache

1. NOUN

/ wiːˈsɑːtʃeɪ / (AmE), / wiːˈsæ.tʃeɪ / (BrE)
A tropical American thorny shrub or small tree (Acacia farnesiana), with fragrant yellow flowers often used in perfumery.
tropical thorny shrub with fragrant yellow flowers

Examples

The huisache filled the air with its sweet fragrance after the spring rain.
Local artisans often harvest huisache flowers for use in traditional perfumes.
The huisache is commonly found growing along roadsides in Texas and Mexico.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acacia - A genus of shrubs and trees found worldwide, often with compound leaves.

Etymology

From Mexican Spanish 'huisache', from Nahuatl 'huīzāxītl' (thorny shrub).
