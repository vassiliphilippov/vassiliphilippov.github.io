signboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪnˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a board or surface displayed in a public place, used to show advertisements, information, or instructions
board for advertisements or notices

Examples

They installed a large signboard at the entrance advertising the new store.
Many roadside signboards were damaged during the storm.
The old signboard was faded and hard to read.

Synset Examples

the highway was lined with signboards

Related Terms

  • Synonym: billboard - a large outdoor board for displaying advertisements
  • Synonym: placard - a sign for public display, typically carried or posted for information or protest
