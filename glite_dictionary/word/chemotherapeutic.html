chemotherapeutic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkiːmoʊˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɛm.əʊˌθɛr.əˈpjuː.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to or used in the treatment of disease, especially cancer, by means of chemotherapy
relating to chemotherapy
medical

Examples

The patient showed little response to the chemotherapeutic drugs prescribed by her oncologist.
New advances in chemotherapeutic agents have improved survival rates for some cancers.
Doctors carefully monitor patients during chemotherapeutic treatment for potential side effects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chemotherapy - treatment of disease by chemical substances, especially cancer

Etymology

From chemo- (short for chemical) + therapeutic, referring to therapy using chemicals.
