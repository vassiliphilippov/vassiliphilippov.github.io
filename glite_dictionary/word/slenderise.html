to slenderise

1. VERB

/ ˈslɛndəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈslɛndəˌraɪz / (BrE)
to make something slender or appear to be slender
make slender or seem slender
british

Examples

The tailored dress was designed to slenderise the figure and create a more elegant silhouette.
Some people use vertical stripes in clothing to slenderise their appearance.
The new fitness program claims to slenderise your body in just eight weeks.

Synset Examples

slenderizing skirts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to slenderize - make or appear slender (American spelling)

Etymology

Formed from 'slender' + '-ise' (British spelling equivalent to American '-ize'), meaning 'to make slender.'
