rust-red

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌstˌrɛd / (AmE), / ˈrʌstˌrɛd / (BrE)
of a red color tinged with the shade of rust; reddish-brown
reddish-brown in color

Examples

The old barn was painted a rust-red color that blended with the autumn leaves.
She wore a rust-red scarf that complemented her brown coat.
The soil in that region has a distinctive rust-red hue due to its iron content.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reddish-brown - of a color that is a mix of red and brown; similar shade
