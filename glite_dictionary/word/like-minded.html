like-minded

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪkˌmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪkˌmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
Having similar opinions, interests, or attitudes.
having similar opinions or interests

Examples

She enjoyed spending time with other like-minded people who shared her love of literature.
The club was formed by a group of like-minded individuals hoping to make a difference.
Finding a supportive network of like-minded colleagues helped him succeed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: open-minded - willing to consider new ideas; unprejudiced
  • Synonym: broad-minded - tolerant or liberal in one's views
