letterer

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛt̬ɚɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɛtərə / (BrE)
a person who paints or inscribes letters, especially professionally as on signs or artwork
person who paints or inscribes letters

Examples

The letterer carefully painted the shop’s sign with elegant script.
She hired a skilled letterer to inscribe names on the award plaques.
As a comic book letterer, he added dialogue and captions to the artwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: signpainter - a person who paints signs as a profession

Etymology

From 'letter' + agent noun suffix '-er'.
