heavyhearted

1. ADJ

/ ˌhɛviˈhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˌhɛviˈhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
feeling sad, depressed, or weighed down by sorrow; deeply unhappy.
feeling sad or depressed

Examples

She felt heavyhearted as she watched her best friend move away.
After hearing the bad news, he grew heavyhearted and quiet.
Despite his heavyhearted mood, he tried to smile for his children.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lighthearted - feeling cheerful and carefree
  • Synonym: melancholy - a feeling of deep sadness or gloom

Etymology

From 'heavy' + 'hearted', originally indicating a heart weighed down by sorrow.
