Habacuc

1. PROPN

/ ˌhæbəˈkʌk / (AmE), / ˌhæbəˈkʌk / (BrE)
an Old Testament book recounting the prophecies of the prophet Habakkuk
Old Testament book of prophecies

Examples

Many biblical scholars have written commentaries on Habacuc.
The prophecies in Habacuc address themes of justice and faith.
In church, the pastor read a passage from the book of Habacuc.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Habakkuk - variant spelling/name of the prophet and the biblical book

Etymology

From Latin 'Habacuc', from Hebrew 'Ḥăbhaqquq', the name of the prophet.
