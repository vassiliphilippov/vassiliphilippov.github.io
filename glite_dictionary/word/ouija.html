Ouija

1. NOUN

/ ˈwiːdʒə / (AmE), / ˈwiːdʒə / (BrE)
a board marked with the letters of the alphabet, used with a planchette to spell out messages in attempts to communicate with the spirits of the dead
board for communicating with spirits

Examples

They gathered around the table to use the Ouija board during the storm.
Many people believe the Ouija can reveal messages from the spirit world.
She refused to touch the Ouija, saying it gave her chills.

Etymology

From French and German words for 'yes': 'oui' (French) and 'ja' (German).
