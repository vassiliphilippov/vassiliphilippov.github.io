entertainer

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛn.tɚˈteɪ.nɚ / (AmE), / ˌɛn.təˈteɪ.nə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to amuse or interest an audience, especially in the performing arts
a person who amuses or interests an audience

Examples

The entertainer made the whole crowd laugh with his comedy routine.
Many children dream of becoming an entertainer on stage or television.
The award-winning entertainer traveled around the world, performing at sold-out venues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: performer - a person who performs in front of an audience
  • Antonym: audience - the group of people watching an entertainer or performance

Etymology

From entertain + -er, formed in English.
