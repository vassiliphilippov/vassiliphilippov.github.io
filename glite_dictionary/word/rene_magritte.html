Rene Magritte

1. PROPN

/ rəˈneɪ mæˈgriːt / (AmE), / reɪˈneɪ mæˈgriːt / (BrE)
(1898–1967) Belgian surrealist painter known for his witty and thought-provoking images that challenge observers’ perceptions of reality.
Belgian surrealist painter (1898–1967)

Examples

Rene Magritte is famous for paintings that blur the line between reality and illusion.
An exhibition of Rene Magritte's work drew art lovers from around the world.
The influence of Rene Magritte can be seen in many contemporary surrealist artists.
