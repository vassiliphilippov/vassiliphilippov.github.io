iritic

1. ADJ

/ aɪˈrɪtɪk / (AmE), / aɪˈrɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or situated near the iris of the eye
relating to the iris (eye)
medical

Examples

The doctor noted an iritic inflammation during the examination.
Symptoms of iritic pain may include redness and sensitivity to light.
He specialized in treating iritic disorders in his ophthalmology practice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iris - the colored part of the eye surrounding the pupil

Etymology

Formed from 'iris' (the colored part of the eye) plus the adjectival suffix '-ic'.
