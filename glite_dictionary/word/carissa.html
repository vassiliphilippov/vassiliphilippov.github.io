Carissa

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɪsə / (AmE), / kəˈrɪsə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical evergreen shrubs, often spiny, native to the Old World.
a genus of tropical evergreen shrubs

Examples

The Carissa genus produces edible fruit known in some regions as natal plum.
Several species within the Carissa genus are cultivated as ornamental shrubs.
Botanists recently discovered a new species of Carissa in South Africa.

Etymology

From Latinized form of Sanskrit *karira*, the local name for the plant.
