Columba palumbus

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈlʌmbə pəˈlʌmbəs / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmbə pəˈlʌmbəs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the common wood pigeon, a Eurasian pigeon with white patches on its wings and neck.
scientific name of the common wood pigeon

Examples

The Columba palumbus is commonly found in European woodlands and parks.
During migration season, flocks of Columba palumbus can be seen across the countryside.
Birdwatchers noted the distinctive white neck patch of the Columba palumbus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wood pigeon - common name for Columba palumbus

Etymology

From Latin: 'columba' meaning 'pigeon' and 'palumbus' meaning 'wood pigeon'.
