Durban

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɜrbən / (AmE), / ˈdɜːbən / (BrE)
a port city in eastern South Africa on the Indian Ocean, known as a major resort and industrial center.
city in eastern South Africa

Examples

Many tourists visit Durban for its beautiful beaches and warm climate.
The port of Durban handles much of South Africa's maritime trade.
My cousin moved to Durban to work in the shipping industry.

Etymology

Named after Sir Benjamin d’Urban, British colonial administrator.
