toreador

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔriədɔr / (AmE), / ˈtɒriədɔː / (BrE)
a bullfighter, especially one in traditional Spanish bullfighting
Spanish bullfighter

Examples

The toreador entered the ring wearing a colorful costume and carrying a red cape.
Many people travel to Spain to watch a skilled toreador perform in the bullring.
The crowd cheered as the toreador executed a daring maneuver.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: matador - the main bullfighter who kills the bull
  • Synonym: bullfighter - a general term for someone who fights bulls

Etymology

From Spanish 'toreador', from 'torear' (to fight bulls), from 'toro' (bull).
