garbagewoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrbɪdʒˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to collect and dispose of household or commercial refuse
woman collecting and disposing of refuse

Examples

The garbagewoman arrives early every Monday to collect our household waste.
As a garbagewoman, she takes pride in keeping the city clean and tidy.
The children waved to the garbagewoman as she emptied their bins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garbageman - a man employed to collect and dispose of refuse

Etymology

Formed from 'garbage' + 'woman', using the common English occupational suffix pattern.
