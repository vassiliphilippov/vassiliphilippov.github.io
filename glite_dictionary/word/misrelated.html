misrelated

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪtɪd / (BrE)
incorrectly or mistakenly related or connected; showing a wrong relationship
incorrectly related or connected

Examples

The historian realized that several events in the timeline had been misrelated due to a transcription error.
Her genealogy report included some misrelated ancestors, which caused confusion in her family tree.
In the discussion, two otherwise separate issues were misrelated, leading the group to a faulty conclusion.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unrelated - not connected or associated
  • Antonym: related - connected by some relationship

Etymology

From mis- (wrongly) + related, meaning wrongly connected.
