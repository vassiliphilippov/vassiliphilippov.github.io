Macrodantin

1. NOUN

/ ˌmækroʊˈdæntɪn / (AmE), / ˌmakrəʊˈdæntɪn / (BrE)
A proprietary name for nitrofurantoin, an antibacterial medicine used to treat urinary tract infections.
trademark for antibacterial drug treating UTIs
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Macrodantin for her recurring urinary tract infections.
