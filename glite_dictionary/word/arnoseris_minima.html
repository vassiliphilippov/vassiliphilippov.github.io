Arnoseris minima

1. NOUN

/ ɑːrˈnoʊsərɪs mɪˈnaɪmə / (AmE), / ɑːˈnɒsərɪs mɪˈnɪmə / (BrE)
A small herb native to Europe, bearing small yellow flowers and belonging to the family Asteraceae.
small European herb with yellow flowers

Examples

The meadow was dotted with Arnoseris minima, its tiny yellow flowers brightening the field.
Botanists discovered a rare specimen of Arnoseris minima in the European countryside.
She photographed the Arnoseris minima growing wild along the path.

Etymology

From New Latin, combining the genus name Arnoseris and Latin minima meaning 'smallest'.
