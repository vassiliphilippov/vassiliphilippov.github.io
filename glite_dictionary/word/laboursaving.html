laboursaving

1. ADJ

/ ˈleɪbɚˌseɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈleɪbəˌseɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a device or method) designed to reduce or eliminate the need for human labor, especially manual work.
reducing or eliminating human labor

Examples

The new dishwasher is a laboursaving appliance that makes kitchen chores much easier.
Many farmers are investing in laboursaving machinery to boost efficiency during the harvest season.
Installing a laboursaving irrigation system greatly reduced the amount of manual work required in the garden.

Synset Examples

laborsaving devices like washing machines

Related Terms

  • Synonym: time-saving - (of a device or method) reducing the amount of time required

Etymology

From 'labour' (work) + 'saving', indicating something that saves effort.
