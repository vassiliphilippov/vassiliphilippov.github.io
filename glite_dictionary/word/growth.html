growth

1. NOUN

/ ɡroʊθ / (AmE), / ɡrəʊθ / (BrE)
The gradual process by which a living organism increases in size and complexity.
biological development in organisms

Examples

A healthy diet is essential for normal growth in children.
Scientists study the growth of bacteria under different conditions.
Rapid growth occurs during the early years of life.

Synset Examples

he proposed an indicator of osseous development in children

Related Terms

  • Synonym: development - the process of developing or being developed, especially in biology

2. NOUN

/ ɡroʊθ / (AmE), / ɡrəʊθ / (BrE)
an abnormal mass of tissue, such as a tumor, resulting from uncontrolled cell multiplication
abnormal tissue mass (tumor)
medical

Examples

The doctor discovered a suspicious growth on her lung during the examination.
After a biopsy, the growth was found to be benign and not cancerous.
He was worried about the small growth that appeared on his skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tumor - an abnormal swelling of tissue, often a synonym in medical contexts

/ ɡroʊθ / (AmE), / ɡrəʊθ / (BrE)
