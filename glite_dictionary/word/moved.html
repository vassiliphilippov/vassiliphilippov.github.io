moved

1. ADJ

/ muːvd / (AmE), / muːvd / (BrE)
feeling strong emotion, especially as a result of something touching or inspiring
emotionally affected; touched

Examples

She was deeply moved by the heartfelt speech at the ceremony.
Many people were moved to tears by the film's ending.
He felt moved to help after hearing about the family's situation.

Synset Examples

too moved to speak
very touched by the stranger's kindness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: touched - feeling emotionally affected; moved
  • Antonym: unmoved - not affected emotionally; not touched
