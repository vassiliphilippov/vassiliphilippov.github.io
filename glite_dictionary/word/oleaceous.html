oleaceous

1. ADJ

/ oʊliˈeɪʃəs / (AmE), / əʊliˈeɪʃəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of trees or shrubs of the olive family (Oleaceae)
relating to the olive family (Oleaceae)

Examples

Many oleaceous plants are valued for their ornamental flowers and fragrant blossoms.
The garden features several oleaceous trees, including lilacs and jasmine.
Researchers are studying the medicinal properties of oleaceous species such as the olive and ash tree.

Etymology

From Latin 'olea' (olive) + '-aceous' (of the nature of).
