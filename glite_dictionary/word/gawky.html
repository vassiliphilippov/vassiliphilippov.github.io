gawky

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɔki / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːki / (BrE)
awkward or clumsy, especially in movement or posture; lacking grace.
awkward or clumsy in movement

Examples

The gawky teenager tripped over his own feet during gym class.
She felt gawky in her new high heels, stumbling down the hallway.
Despite his gawky appearance, he was quick to help anyone in need.

Synset Examples

a gawky lad with long ungainly legs
clumsy fingers
what an ungainly creature a giraffe is
heaved his unwieldy figure out of his chair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or handling things
  • Antonym: graceful - having or showing grace or elegance

Etymology

Probably from the Scottish 'gawk', meaning a fool or simpleton.
