shrilly

1. ADV

/ ˈʃrɪli / (AmE), / ˈʃrɪli / (BrE)
In a shrill or piercing manner; with a high-pitched and penetrating sound.
in a shrill manner

Examples

The alarm rang shrilly in the quiet house, waking everyone instantly.
She called his name shrilly across the busy playground.
The referee's whistle sounded shrilly above the noise of the crowd.

Synset Examples

she sang rather shrilly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shrill - high-pitched and piercing in sound or tone
