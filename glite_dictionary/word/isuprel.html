Isuprel

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.sjʊˌprɛl / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.sjuːˌprɛl / (BrE)
A trademarked drug used to treat bronchial asthma and to stimulate the heart; brand name for isoproterenol.
trade drug for asthma and heart stimulation
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Isuprel to help manage the patient's asthma symptoms.
