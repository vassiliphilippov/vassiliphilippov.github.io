teenage

1. ADJ

/ ˈtiːnˌeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtiːn.eɪdʒ / (BrE)
of an age between 13 and 19 years old; relating to teenagers.
aged 13–19; relating to teenagers

Examples

She became a mother during her teenage years.
Many teenage students enjoy participating in sports after school.
The teenage market is a major focus for advertisers.

Synset Examples

teenage mothers
the teen years

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teenager - a person aged 13 to 19

Etymology

Formed from 'teen' (from 'thirteen' to 'nineteen') and the suffix '-age', in the 1920s.
