Cecil B. DeMille

1. PROPN

/ ˈsiːsəl bi dɪˈmɪl / (AmE), / ˈsiːsəl biː dɛˈmɪl / (BrE)
(1881–1959) An American film director and producer known for his grand and elaborate epic movies.
famous American film director and producer

Examples

Cecil B. DeMille was known for directing monumental films that set new standards in Hollywood production.
The extravagant style of Cecil B. DeMille influenced generations of filmmakers.
Few directors matched the scope and ambition seen in Cecil B. DeMille's epics.
