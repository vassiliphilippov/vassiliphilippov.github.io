outturn

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtˌtɝn / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌtɜːn / (BrE)
the amount of goods or material produced in a specified period; output or yield.
amount produced; output

Examples

The factory's outturn increased significantly after the new machinery was installed.
Last year's outturn of grain exceeded all expectations due to favorable weather.
We must analyze the monthly outturn to improve our production efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: output - the amount produced by a person, machine, or industry
  • Synonym: yield - the full amount of an agricultural or industrial product

Etymology

From 'out' + 'turn', indicating what is turned out or produced.
