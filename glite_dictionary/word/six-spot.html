six-spot

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪkˌspɑt / (AmE), / ˈsɪks.pɒt / (BrE)
A playing card, domino, or die showing six pips on its upward face.
card, domino, or die showing six pips

Examples

He picked up the six-spot and placed it next to the five in the domino game.
Drawing a six-spot from the deck changed the course of the card game.
The die landed with the six-spot facing up, much to her delight.
