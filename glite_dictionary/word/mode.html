mode

1. NOUN

/ moʊd / (AmE), / məʊd / (BrE)
a particular functioning condition, arrangement, or setting of a device, machine, or system
a specific operating state or setting

Examples

You can put your phone into airplane mode to disable all wireless connections.
The computer switched to sleep mode after being idle for ten minutes.
Set the washing machine to eco mode for greater energy efficiency.

Synset Examples

switched from keyboard to voice mode

Related Terms

  • Synonym: setting - the way in which a device or system is configured to operate
  • Synonym: configuration - the arrangement or set-up of the parts of a system

Etymology

From Latin modus (measure, manner, method).
