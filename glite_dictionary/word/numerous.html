numerous

1. ADJ

/ ˈnuː.mə.rəs / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.mə.rəs / (BrE)
Existing or occurring in large numbers; many.
existing in large numbers

Examples

She has received numerous awards for her work in the community.
The company has numerous offices around the world.
We faced numerous challenges during the project, but we succeeded in the end.

Synset Examples

numerous times
the family was numerous
Palomar's fans are legion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: many - a large number of; a great quantity
  • Antonym: few - a small number of

Etymology

From Latin 'numerosus' (numerous, abundant), from 'numerus' (number).
