Irak

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈrɑk / (AmE), / ɪˈrɑːk / (BrE)
An alternative spelling of Iraq, a country in the Middle East, known for the region of ancient Mesopotamia.
alternative spelling of Iraq (country)

Examples

Many historical documents refer to Irak instead of Iraq.
French newspapers often use the spelling Irak to denote the country in the Middle East.
The region known as ancient Mesopotamia is now part of modern Irak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Iraq - a country in the Middle East, site of ancient Mesopotamia

Etymology

From Arabic العراق (al-ʿIrāq).
