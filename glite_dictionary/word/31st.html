31st

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɝːti ˈfɜrst / (AmE), / ˈθɜːti ˈfɜːst / (BrE)
coming next after the thirtieth in a sequence; the ordinal number for thirty-one
ordinal number after thirtieth

Examples

She will celebrate her birthday on the 31st of December.
This is the 31st time we've held this event.
I finished in 31st place out of all the runners.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirtieth - the ordinal number for thirty
  • Degree Higher: 32nd - the ordinal number for thirty-two
