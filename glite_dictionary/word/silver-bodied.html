silver-bodied

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪlvɚ ˌbɑdid / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˌbɒdid / (BrE)
having a body that is silver in color or appearance.
having a silver-colored body

Examples

The silver-bodied fish glimmered beneath the surface of the lake.
Collectors admired the silver-bodied model car for its realistic finish.
The bird, with its silver-bodied plumage, stood out among the foliage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silvery - having a shiny gray-white color like silver
