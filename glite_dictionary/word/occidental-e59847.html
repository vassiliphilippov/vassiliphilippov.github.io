occidental

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑːk.sɪˈden.təl / (AmE), / ˌɒk.sɪˈden.təl / (BrE)
Relating to the countries of the West, especially Europe and the Americas.
of the Western world; European or American

Examples

Many scholars have studied the influences of occidental philosophy on modern thought.
Her paintings reflect both oriental and occidental artistic traditions.
The restaurant offers a blend of occidental and Asian cuisines.

Synset Examples

occidental civilization
Hesperian culture

Related Terms

  • Antonym: oriental - relating to the East, especially Asia

Etymology

From Latin 'occidentalis' (western), from 'occidens' (the west, sunset).
