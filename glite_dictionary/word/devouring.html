devouring

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈvaʊərɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈvaʊərɪŋ / (BrE)
Having an intense or overwhelming desire; ardently or excessively eager for something.
intensely desirous or eager

Examples

He felt a devouring curiosity about the stranger’s past.
Her devouring ambition drove her to work day and night.
A devouring love for music shaped his entire life.

Synset Examples

avid for adventure
an avid ambition to succeed
fierce devouring affection
the esurient eyes of an avid curiosity
greedy for fame

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avid - having or showing a keen interest or enthusiasm for something
  • Synonym: greedy - having an intense desire for something, especially wealth or power
  • Antonym: apathetic - showing or feeling no interest or enthusiasm

Etymology

Present participle of 'devour', from Old French *devorer*, from Latin *devorare* (to swallow down).
