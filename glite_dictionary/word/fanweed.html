fanweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈfænˌwid / (AmE), / ˈfanˌwiːd / (BrE)
a foul-smelling Eurasian weed with round, flat seed pods, now naturalized in North America
a smelly Eurasian weed with round pods

Examples

A patch of fanweed invaded the corner of the vegetable garden last spring.
Gardeners often struggle to control fanweed due to its rapid spread.
The foul odor of fanweed makes it easy to identify among other weeds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weed - a wild plant growing where it is not wanted
