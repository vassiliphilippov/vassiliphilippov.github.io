irrelevantly

1. ADV

/ ɪˈrɛləvəntli / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛlɪvəntli / (BrE)
In a manner that is not connected with the subject at hand or not relevant.
in an irrelevant way

Examples

She kept bringing up unrelated topics irrelevantly during the meeting.
He answered irrelevantly when asked about the main issue.
The witness commented irrelevantly, straying from the lawyer's questions.

Synset Examples

suddenly and irrelevantly, she asked him for money

Related Terms

  • Antonym: relevantly - in a relevant manner

Etymology

From 'irrelevant' + '-ly', with 'irrelevant' from Latin 'irrelevare' (not relevant).
