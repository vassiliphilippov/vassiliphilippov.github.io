Nauclea diderrichii

1. NOUN

/ ˌnaʊˈkliːə dɪˌdɛrɪˈkaɪ / (AmE), / ˌnaʊˈkliːə dɪˌdɛrɪˈkaɪ / (BrE)
A large African forest tree that produces strong, hard yellow to golden brown timber, sometimes classified in the genus Sarcocephalus.
large African tree yielding durable timber

Examples

The durable wood of the Nauclea diderrichii is highly valued in West African construction.
Several furniture makers prefer using Nauclea diderrichii because of its attractive yellow-brown timber.
Conservationists are working to protect Nauclea diderrichii from excessive logging in its native forests.
