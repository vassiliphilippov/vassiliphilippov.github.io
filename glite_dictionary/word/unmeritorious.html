unmeritorious

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnmɛrɪˈtɔriəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnmɛrɪˈtɔːriəs / (BrE)
Not deserving praise, reward, or attention; lacking merit or worth.
lacking merit or worth

Examples

The judge dismissed the unmeritorious lawsuit before it went to trial.
She ignored the unmeritorious claims made by her competitors.
They were quick to defend him from unmeritorious criticism.

Synset Examples

protect … from unmeritorious criticism

Related Terms

  • Antonym: meritorious - deserving reward or praise; having merit

Etymology

Formed from un- (not) + meritorious (deserving praise or reward).
