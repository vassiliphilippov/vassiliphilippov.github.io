Oceanites oceanicus

1. NOUN

/ oʊʃiˈænaɪtiːz oʊsiˈænɪkəs / (AmE), / əʊʃɪˈænaɪtiːz əʊsiˈænɪkəs / (BrE)
A species of small seabird in the storm petrel family, commonly known as Wilson's storm-petrel, widely distributed across the southern oceans.
a species of storm petrel seabird

Examples

The Oceanites oceanicus is known for its remarkable migratory journeys across the southern oceans.
Researchers observed several Oceanites oceanicus nesting on remote Antarctic islands.
Birdwatchers were thrilled to spot an Oceanites oceanicus during their expedition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Wilson's storm-petrel - the common English name for Oceanites oceanicus, a small seabird

Etymology

From New Latin, genus Oceanites + species epithet from Latin oceanicus (of the ocean).
