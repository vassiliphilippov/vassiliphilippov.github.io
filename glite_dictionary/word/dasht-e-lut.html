Dasht-e-Lut

1. PROPN

/ dæʃt eɪ luːt / (AmE), / dæʃt eɪ luːt / (BrE)
a large desert in eastern Iran, known for its extremely high temperatures and unique landscape
desert in eastern Iran

Examples

The Dasht-e-Lut is famous for recording some of the highest ground temperatures on Earth.
Many scientists visit the Dasht-e-Lut to study its extreme climate and geology.
Vast salt plains stretch across the heart of the Dasht-e-Lut in Iran.

Etymology

From Persian 'dasht-e lūt' meaning 'Lut desert'.
