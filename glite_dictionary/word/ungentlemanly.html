ungentlemanly

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdʒɛntəlˌmænli / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʒɛntlmənli / (BrE)
(of a person's behavior) not characteristic of or appropriate to a gentleman; rude or dishonorable.
not appropriate for a gentleman

Examples

His ungentlemanly conduct at the dinner party shocked everyone present.
The referee warned the player for his ungentlemanly behavior on the field.
Accusing his opponent without evidence was considered ungentlemanly by many viewers.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: gentlemanly - polite and honorable as befitting a gentleman

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'gentlemanly'.
