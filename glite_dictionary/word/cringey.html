cringey

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɪn.dʒi / (AmE), / ˈkrɪndʒ.i / (BrE)
Embarrassing or awkward in a way that makes you cringe; causing discomfort through awkwardness or embarrassment.
causing embarrassment; awkward
informal

Examples

His attempt at humor during the meeting was so cringey that everyone looked away in embarrassment.
I can't watch that old video of myself—it’s just too cringey!
She wore a cringey costume to the party, and nobody recognized who she was supposed to be.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cringe - to react with embarrassment or discomfort; feel awkward
  • Synonym: awkward - causing difficulty, embarrassment, or discomfort
  • Antonym: cool - impressively stylish, excellent

Etymology

From 'cringe' + suffix '-y', meaning 'having the quality of causing to cringe'.
