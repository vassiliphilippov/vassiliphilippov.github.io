cowl-shaped

1. ADJ

/ kaʊl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / kaʊl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape or form of a cowl, typically referring to a rounded, hood-like covering.
having the shape of a cowl

Examples

The architect designed a cowl-shaped dome for the cathedral.
She wore a cowl-shaped scarf that draped elegantly around her head.
The machine's cowl-shaped cover protected its delicate components from dust.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hooded - having a covering for the head or face resembling a hood
