unconverted

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.kənˈvɜr.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kənˈvɜː.tɪd / (BrE)
not changed from its original form, condition, or belief; not transformed or adapted for a new use or belief.
not changed or adapted from original form

Examples

The old warehouse remains unconverted despite recent plans for redevelopment.
Several apartments in the building are still unconverted and have their original features.
Many people in the region are unconverted to the new policy and continue their previous practices.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: converted - changed from one form, use, or belief to another
