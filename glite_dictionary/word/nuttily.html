nuttily

1. ADV

/ ˈnʌtəli / (AmE), / ˈnʌtɪli / (BrE)
in a mildly crazy or eccentric manner
in a mildly eccentric way
informal

Examples

She laughed nuttily at his bizarre suggestion, making everyone else uncomfortable.
He danced nuttily around the kitchen, singing at the top of his voice.
Her nuttily creative approach brought a fresh perspective to the team.

Synset Examples

the old lady is beginning to behave quite dottily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentrically - in an unconventional or strange manner
  • Antonym: rationally - in a sensible or reasonable manner

Etymology

From 'nutty' meaning eccentric or crazy, with '-ly' forming an adverb.
