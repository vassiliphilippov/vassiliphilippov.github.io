Konakri

1. PROPN

/ koʊˈnɑːkri / (AmE), / kəʊˈnækri / (BrE)
the capital city and chief port of Guinea, located on the Atlantic coast
capital city of Guinea

Examples

She traveled to Konakri on business and was impressed by its vibrant port.
The conference will be held in Konakri this year, attracting international delegates.
Flights to Konakri are available from several major European cities.

Etymology

From French 'Conakry', possibly of Susu origin.
