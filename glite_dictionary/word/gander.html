gander

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡændər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːndə / (BrE)
a mature male goose
male goose

Examples

The farm has several ganders and a large flock of geese.
During mating season, the gander becomes very protective of his mate.
A gander usually leads the way when the flock moves to a new pond.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: goose - a large waterbird with a long neck and webbed feet

Etymology

From Old English 'gandra' (male goose), related to Dutch 'gander', German 'Ganter'.
