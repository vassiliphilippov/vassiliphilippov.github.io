tasseled

1. ADJ

/ ˈtæsəld / (AmE), / ˈtasəld / (BrE)
decorated or fringed with tassels; having tassels attached as an ornament.
decorated with tassels

Examples

She wore a tasseled shawl over her shoulders for the celebration.
The tasseled lampshade added a vintage touch to the room.
His graduation cap proudly displayed a golden tasseled cord.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fringed - having a border or edge of dangling threads, often decorative

Etymology

From 'tassel' (from Old French *tassel*, diminutive of *tasse* ‘clasp’) + -ed.
