cow-orker

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊ ˌɔrkər / (AmE), / ˈkaʊ ˌɔːkə / (BrE)
A humorous or jocular spelling of 'co-worker', used to refer to a colleague or someone you work with.
humorous term for a colleague
informal

Examples

My cow-orker always makes everyone laugh with her witty jokes.
Every Friday, our cow-orker brings in homemade cookies to share.
He introduced his new cow-orker to the rest of the office as a joke.

Synset Examples

We all appreciate the effort of our cow-orker.
She joked that her cow-orker needs to stop slacking.
My cow-orker always brings snacks for the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: co-worker - person you work with at the same job

Etymology

Humorous alteration of 'co-worker', popularized in internet and tech circles.
