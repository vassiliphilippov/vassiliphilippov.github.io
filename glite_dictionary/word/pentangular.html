pentangular

1. ADJ

/ pɛnˈtæŋɡjələr / (AmE), / pɛnˈtaŋɡjʊlə / (BrE)
having five angles or sides; shaped like a pentagon.
having five angles; pentagon-shaped

Examples

The ancient coin was distinctly pentangular and unique among others in the collection.
They built a pentangular table for the geometry classroom.
The pentangular frame provided better support for the structure's roof.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pentagonal - having five sides and five angles; related to a pentagon

Etymology

From Greek 'penta-' (five) and Latin 'angulus' (angle).
