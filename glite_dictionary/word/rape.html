rape

1. NOUN

/ reɪp / (AmE), / reɪp / (BrE)
the crime of forcing someone to have sexual intercourse against their will
sexual assault by force; criminal act

Examples

The police investigated the alleged rape that was reported last night.
She became an advocate for victims after surviving a violent rape.
The law defines rape as sexual intercourse carried out by force or threat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexual assault - unwanted sexual contact or behavior toward another
  • Antonym: consensual sex - sexual intercourse with mutual agreement

Etymology

From Latin 'rapere' (to seize, carry off).
