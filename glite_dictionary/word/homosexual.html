homosexual

1. ADJ

/ ˌhoʊməˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌhɒməˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
Sexually attracted to people of the same sex.
sexually attracted to same sex

Examples

She realized she was homosexual during her late teens.
The film explores the experiences of homosexual individuals in a conservative society.
Some countries still discriminate against people because they are homosexual.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heterosexual - Sexually attracted to people of the opposite sex
  • Synonym: bisexual - Sexually attracted to both men and women

Etymology

From Greek 'homo-' (same) and Latin 'sexualis' (relating to sex).

2. NOUN

/ ˌhoʊməˈsɛksjuəl / (AmE), / ˌhəʊməˈsɛksjʊəl / (BrE)
A person who is sexually attracted to people of the same sex.
person attracted to the same sex

Examples

Many homosexuals have fought for equal rights and recognition.
He revealed that he was a homosexual to his close friends and family.
The documentary explores the challenges faced by homosexual individuals in different parts of the world.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heterosexual - a person attracted to people of the opposite sex
  • Degree Lower: bisexual - a person attracted to both men and women
  • Synonym: gay - another term for a homosexual person, especially a man

Etymology

From Late Latin 'homosexualis', from Greek 'homo-' (same) + Latin 'sexualis' (sexual).

Synset POS: n
