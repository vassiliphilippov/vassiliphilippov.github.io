duncish

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʌn.tʃɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʌn.tɪʃ / (BrE)
(informal) Stupid or slow to understand; resembling a dunce.
stupid or slow to understand
informal

Examples

His duncish response made the whole class laugh.
Calling someone duncish isn't exactly polite, you know.
The team's duncish mistakes cost them the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dunce - a person who is slow to learn or foolish
  • Antonym: clever - quick to understand, intelligent

Etymology

Formed from 'dunce' (a slow-witted person) + '-ish' (adjective suffix).
