skiing

1. NOUN

/ ˈskiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskiːɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of gliding over snow on skis
the sport of travelling on skis

Examples

She has loved skiing ever since her first trip to the Alps.
Many people take lessons before trying skiing on more difficult slopes.
They went skiing in Colorado during winter break.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: snowboarding - the sport of descending snow-covered slopes on a board

Etymology

From 'ski', from Norwegian 'ski' (a split piece of wood, ski) + the suffix '-ing'.
