araceous

1. ADJ

/ əˈreɪsiəs / (AmE), / əˈreɪsɪəs / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of plants of the family Araceae.
of the Araceae plant family

Examples

Some tropical forests are home to numerous araceous species with striking foliage.
The flower structure is typical of araceous plants, featuring a spadix and spathe.
She specializes in the cultivation of rare araceous houseplants like Anthurium and Philodendron.

Etymology

From modern Latin Araceae (the plant family) + -ous.
