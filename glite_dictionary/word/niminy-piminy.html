niminy-piminy

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɪmɪni ˈpɪmɪni / (AmE), / ˈnɪmɪni ˈpɪmɪni / (BrE)
Affectedly dainty or refined, especially in an exaggerated, artificial, or overly delicate way.
affectedly dainty or refined
archaic

Examples

Her niminy-piminy manners amused some guests and annoyed others at the party.
The novel’s heroine was too niminy-piminy for the tastes of contemporary readers.
He dismissed her niminy-piminy airs as mere pretension.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prissy - overly proper or prim
  • Synonym: affected - artificial in behavior or manner
  • Antonym: natural - free from artificiality or affectation
