Dean Martin

1. PROPN

/ diːn ˈmɑːrtən / (AmE), / diːn ˈmɑːtɪn / (BrE)
(1917–1995) an American singer, actor, and comedian, best known as a member of the Rat Pack and for his smooth singing style.
American singer and entertainer

Examples

Dean Martin was famous for his relaxed singing style and charismatic performances.
Many people still enjoy listening to classic Dean Martin songs like 'That's Amore' and 'Volare'.
Dean Martin starred in numerous films and television variety shows throughout his career.
