complexifier

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈplɛksɪˌfaɪər / (AmE), / kəmˈplɛksɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a person or thing that makes something more complex or complicated
one who makes things more complex

Examples

Sometimes, a good complexifier can reveal unseen dimensions in a problem.
Software developers often act as complexifiers when they add unnecessary features.
Instead of being a simplifier, his role on the team was that of a complexifier.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: simplifier - a person or thing that makes something simpler

Etymology

Formed from 'complex' + '-ify' (to make) + '-er' (one who does).
