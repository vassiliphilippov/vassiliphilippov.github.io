superstar

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːpərˌstɑr / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌstɑː / (BrE)
A person who is extremely famous or talented in a particular field.
exceptionally famous or talented person

Examples

After his latest film, he became a true superstar internationally.
Many young athletes dream of becoming a superstar in their sport someday.
Her innovative ideas quickly made her a superstar in the tech industry.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: star - a highly celebrated or talented person

Etymology

Formed from 'super-' (from Latin 'super', meaning 'above') + 'star'.
