mystified

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɪstəˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɪstɪfaɪd / (BrE)
Completely unable to understand or explain something; puzzled or bewildered.
utterly perplexed; unable to understand

Examples

She looked mystified when she opened the strange letter and found no explanation inside.
The sudden change in policy left many employees mystified and worried about their future.
Despite his attempts to explain, his actions only mystified his friends even more.

Synset Examples

all this duncical nonsense has my brains metagrobolized

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confused - unable to think clearly or to understand something
  • Synonym: puzzled - unable to understand; perplexed
  • Antonym: enlightened - having understanding or knowledge; not confused

Etymology

From the verb 'mystify', from French 'mystifier', from 'mystère' (mystery).
