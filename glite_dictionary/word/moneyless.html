moneyless

1. ADJ

/ ˈmʌniˌləs / (AmE), / ˈmʌniˌləs / (BrE)
Not using or involving money; not based on the possession or use of money.
not involving money

Examples

In a moneyless system, goods are exchanged through bartering rather than purchase.
Some communities experiment with moneyless living to promote sustainability and cooperation.
The documentary explored a moneyless society where resources are shared freely.

Synset Examples

a moneyless economy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cashless - not using cash for transactions
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02033575-a
Synset POS: a

2. ADJ

/ ˈmʌniˌlɛs / (AmE), / ˈmʌniˌlɛs / (BrE)
Having no money; lacking financial resources.
having no money

Examples

After losing his job, he found himself moneyless and struggling to pay the bills.
The charity aims to support moneyless families in the city.
Many moneyless students rely on part-time work to cover their expenses.

Synset Examples

virtually moneyless rural regions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: penniless - having no money; very poor
  • Synonym: destitute - without the basic necessities, especially money
  • Antonym: wealthy - having a great deal of money or resources

Etymology

Formed from 'money' + '-less' (meaning 'without').

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02031165-s
Synset POS: s
