transactor

1. NOUN

/ trænˈzæktər / (AmE), / trænˈzæktə / (BrE)
a person who conducts or carries on business or negotiations
person doing business or negotiations

Examples

Each transactor in the system is assigned a unique identification number.
The transactor was responsible for approving the financial documents.
During the meeting, every transactor had to present their business proposal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agent - a person who acts on behalf of another in business or negotiations
  • Synonym: negotiator - someone who negotiates agreements or deals

Etymology

From transact +‎ -or, modeled on agent nouns in English and Latin.
