conspectus

1. NOUN

/ kənˈspɛktəs / (AmE), / kənˈspɛktəs / (BrE)
A summary or general survey that provides an overall view of a subject.
summary or overview

Examples

The report gives a useful conspectus of recent research in the field.
She presented a conspectus of the company's financial health at the meeting.
The book opens with a conspectus of ancient philosophical ideas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summary - a brief statement or account of the main points
  • Synonym: overview - a general review or summary

Etymology

From Latin 'conspectus' (a looking at, view, survey).
