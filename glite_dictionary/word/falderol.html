falderol

1. NOUN

/ ˈfældəˌrɑl / (AmE), / ˈfældərəʊl / (BrE)
ornamental objects or decorations that are considered to be of little value; trinkets or frippery
showy ornament of little value

Examples

The old chest was filled with falderol collected over the years.
Her shelves displayed nothing but falderol and dust.
He saw the falderol as a waste of space, preferring simplicity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trinket - a small ornament or item of little value
  • Synonym: bauble - a small showy trinket or decoration
  • Synonym: frippery - showy or unnecessary ornamentation

Etymology

Probably a variant of "falderal", an alteration of 'fol-de-rol', a meaningless refrain in old songs.
