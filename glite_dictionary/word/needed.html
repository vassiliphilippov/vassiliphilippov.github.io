needed

1. ADJ

/ ˈniːdɪd / (AmE), / ˈniːdɪd / (BrE)
required or necessary for a particular purpose or to achieve relief or supply.
required or necessary

Examples

Financial support was greatly needed after the sudden loss of income.
A flashlight is needed if you plan to hike after sunset.
He brought the needed documents to complete the application process.

Synset Examples

provided them with all things needful

Related Terms

  • Synonym: necessary - required to be done, achieved, or present; needed
  • Antonym: unnecessary - not needed or required
  • Synonym: required - officially demanded or compulsory; needed
