graspable

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡræspəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːspəbl̩ / (BrE)
easy to understand or capable of being mentally apprehended.
easy to understand

Examples

The professor explained the theory in a way that made it more graspable for students.
The instructions were written in clear, graspable language.
Her arguments were complex but ultimately graspable after careful consideration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comprehensible - able to be understood; intelligible
  • Antonym: incomprehensible - impossible to understand; unintelligible

Etymology

From grasp + -able suffix, indicating capability.
