tobogganing

1. NOUN

/ təˈbɑɡənɪŋ / (AmE), / təˈbɒɡənɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of riding on a toboggan down a slope, especially over snow or ice
riding a toboggan down a slope

Examples

We spent the afternoon tobogganing down the snowy hill behind our house.
Many winter resorts offer safe areas for children to enjoy tobogganing.
His favorite childhood memory is tobogganing with friends after the first big snowfall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sledding - the activity of traveling on a sled

Etymology

From 'toboggan', which comes from Canadian French 'tabagane', from Algonquian (likely Mi'kmaq or Maliseet) for 'sled'.
