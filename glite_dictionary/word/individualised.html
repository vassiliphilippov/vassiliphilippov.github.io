individualised

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪndəˈvɪdʒuəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈvɪdjʊəlaɪzd / (BrE)
made or adjusted to suit a particular individual; personalized.
made for a particular individual
british

Examples

Each student received an individualised study plan tailored to their strengths and needs.
Our service offers individualised advice based on your health profile.
The therapy program was individualised to address each patient's unique situation.

Synset Examples

personalized luggage
personalized advice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personalized - made or adjusted for a specific person

Etymology

Formed from 'individual' + '-ise' (British spelling of '-ize'), meaning to make individual.
