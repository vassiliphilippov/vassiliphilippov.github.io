supernaturalist

1. ADJ

/ ˌsuː.pɚˈnætʃ.ɚ.əl.ɪst / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈnætʃ.ər.ə.lɪst / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of belief in supernatural forces or supernaturalism
relating to supernaturalism

Examples

Many early philosophers held supernaturalist views about the origins of the universe.
Her interpretations of the text are strongly supernaturalist rather than scientific.
The debate contrasted materialist and supernaturalist explanations for the phenomenon.

Synset Examples

supernaturalist beliefs

Related Terms

  • Antonym: naturalist - relating to naturalism; one who rejects supernatural explanations

Etymology

From 'supernatural' + '-ist'; 'supernatural' from Latin supernaturalis.
