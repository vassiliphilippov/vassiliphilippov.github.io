prankishness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpræŋkɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈpraŋkɪʃnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being inclined to play mischievous or playful pranks
the quality of being mischievous or playful

Examples

His prankishness often got him into trouble at school.
There was a sense of prankishness in her smile as she set up the surprise.
The comedian’s prankishness is a big part of his appeal on television.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mischievousness - the quality of causing trouble in a playful way

Etymology

From 'prankish' + '-ness', where 'prankish' derives from 'prank' (a mischievous act).
