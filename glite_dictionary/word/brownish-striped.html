brownish-striped

1. ADJ

/ ˈbraʊnɪʃ ˈstraɪpt / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnɪʃ ˈstraɪpt / (BrE)
having stripes that are brownish in color
having brownish stripes

Examples

The caterpillar was brownish-striped along its entire back.
She wore a brownish-striped shirt to the picnic.
The museum displayed a brownish-striped shell found on the beach.

Related Terms
