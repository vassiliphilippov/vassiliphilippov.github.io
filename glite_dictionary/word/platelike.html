platelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈpleɪtˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpleɪtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling a flat, thin plate or layer, especially in shape or structure.
resembling a flat, thin plate

Examples

The shark's skin is covered with platelike scales for protection.
Under the microscope, the mineral appeared as a series of platelike crystals.
The scientist described the structure as platelike, noting its thin, flat layers.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: plate - a flat, typically round dish or object
