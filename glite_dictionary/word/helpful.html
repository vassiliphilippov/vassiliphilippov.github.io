helpful

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛlp.fəl / (AmE), / ˈhɛlp.fəl / (BrE)
Willing to provide assistance or making it easier for someone to do something by offering support or useful advice.
giving assistance or support

Examples

The staff at the hotel were extremely helpful during our stay.
Your suggestions were very helpful in solving the problem.
He always tries to be helpful to his classmates.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unhelpful - not providing assistance or support
  • Synonym: useful - able to be used for a practical purpose or in several ways

Etymology

From Middle English help + -ful (full of).
