sportswear

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrtsˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtsˌwɛə / (BrE)
clothes designed for physical exercise or casual wear, often associated with sports.
casual or athletic clothing

Examples

He prefers to wear sportswear even when he's not going to the gym.
The store specializes in high-quality sportswear for men and women.
Modern sportswear combines style, comfort, and functionality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: athleisure - casual, comfortable clothing designed for both exercise and everyday wear
  • Antonym: formalwear - clothing suited for formal occasions
