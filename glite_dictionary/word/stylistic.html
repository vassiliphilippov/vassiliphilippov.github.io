stylistic

1. ADJ

/ staɪˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / staɪˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to style, especially in the way language or art is expressed or performed.
relating to style in language or art

Examples

The novel demonstrates several stylistic innovations that set it apart from traditional works.
She received praise for her stylistic choices in the painting.
Despite their differences, the films share many stylistic similarities.

Synset Examples

stylistic devices

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stylistical - alternative adjective form meaning relating to style
  • Antonym: nonstylistic - not related to style

Etymology

From style + -istic, forming adjectives relating to style.
