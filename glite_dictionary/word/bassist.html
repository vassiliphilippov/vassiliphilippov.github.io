bassist

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsɪst / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsɪst / (BrE)
a musician who plays the bass, especially the bass guitar or double bass, in a band or orchestra.
musician who plays the bass

Examples

The bassist laid down a steady groove that drove the whole song.
At the concert, the bassist played an impressive solo that amazed the crowd.
She joined the band as their new bassist last spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guitarist - a musician who plays the guitar

Etymology

From 'bass' (referring to the musical instrument) + '-ist' (a person who practices or is concerned with something).
