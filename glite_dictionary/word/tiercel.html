tiercel

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪr.səl / (AmE), / ˈtɪə.səl / (BrE)
A male hawk, especially a male peregrine or gyrfalcon.
male hawk, esp. peregrine or gyrfalcon

Examples

The trained tiercel swooped down gracefully to catch its prey.
Historically, falconers preferred using a tiercel for hunting smaller game.
She studied the flight pattern of the tiercel from her vantage point.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: falcon - a bird of prey with long wings and a notched beak

Etymology

From Old French 'terçel' (male hawk), from Latin 'tertiolus' (a third), possibly referring to the belief that one in three eggs hatches a male.
