combination

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːmbɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒmbɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a sequence of numbers or letters that must be entered to open a combination lock
lock-opening code sequence

Examples

She couldn't remember the combination to her locker after the summer holidays.
To reset the safe, you need to enter the old combination first.
He keeps the combination written down in a secure place just in case he forgets it.

Synset Examples

he forgot the combination to the safe

Related Terms

  • Synonym: code - a system of words, letters, or numbers used for secrecy or access

Etymology

From Latin combinatio (a joining together), from combinare (to combine).
