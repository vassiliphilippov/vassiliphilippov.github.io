unmarred

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmɑrd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɑːd / (BrE)
not spoiled, damaged, or marked; free from physical or moral flaws or blemishes.
not spoiled or damaged

Examples

Despite the storm, the beach remained unmarred by debris or litter.
Her reputation was unmarred after the thorough investigation found no wrongdoing.
The painting has survived for centuries, its colors unmarred by time.

Synset Examples

an unblemished record
an unblemished complexion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unblemished - not damaged or marked in any way; perfect
  • Antonym: marred - spoiled or damaged

Etymology

from prefix un- (not) + marred (past participle of 'mar', meaning to spoil or damage)
