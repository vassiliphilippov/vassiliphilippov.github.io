Roumanian

1. ADJ

/ ruˈmeɪniən / (AmE), / ruːˈmeɪniən / (BrE)
of or relating to Romania, its people, or its language; variant spelling of 'Romanian'
relating to Romania or its people/language

Examples

The museum features a collection of traditional Roumanian costumes and art.
She learned to speak Roumanian during her year abroad in Bucharest.
We enjoyed delicious Roumanian dishes at the local festival.

Synset Examples

Romanian folk music

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Romanian - of or relating to Romania, its people, or its language

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'Romanian', from French 'roumain', ultimately from Latin 'Romanus' (Roman).
