to obtain

1. VERB

/ əbˈteɪn / (AmE), / əbˈteɪn / (BrE)
to get or acquire something, especially through effort or request
get or acquire

Examples

She worked hard to obtain a scholarship for university.
Information about the new policy can be obtained from the official website.
Before entering the country, you must obtain a valid visa.

Synset Examples

How did you obtain the visa?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to acquire - to get or gain something
  • Antonym: to lose - to be deprived of or cease to have something

Etymology

From Latin 'obtinere' meaning 'to hold, obtain'; 'ob-' (toward) + 'tenere' (to hold).
