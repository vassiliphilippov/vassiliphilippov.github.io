appreciatively

1. ADV

/ əˈpriʃiətɪvli / (AmE), / əˈpriːʃiətɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that shows appreciation or gratitude; with a feeling of thankfulness or recognition of value.
in a grateful or appreciative manner

Examples

She nodded appreciatively when he offered her a cup of tea.
The audience clapped appreciatively after the heartfelt speech.
He smiled appreciatively at the gift his friends gave him.

Synset Examples

he accepted my offer appreciatively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gratefully - in a manner that expresses gratitude
  • Antonym: indifferently - in a way that shows no interest or concern

Etymology

From 'appreciative' + '-ly', ultimately from Latin 'appretiatus' (valued).
