Rhinoceros unicornis

1. NOUN

/ ˌraɪˈnɑːsərəs juːˈnɪkɔrnɪs / (AmE), / ˌraɪˈnɒsərəs juːˈnɪkɔːnɪs / (BrE)
a species of rhinoceros native to the Indian subcontinent, notable for having a single horn; also known as the Indian rhinoceros.
species of rhinoceros with one horn

Examples

The Rhinoceros unicornis is primarily found in the grasslands of northern India and Nepal.
Conservation efforts have helped increase the population of Rhinoceros unicornis in recent decades.
The unique single horn distinguishes Rhinoceros unicornis from other rhinoceros species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Indian rhinoceros - the common name for Rhinoceros unicornis

Etymology

From Latin: 'rhinoceros' meaning 'nose-horn', and 'unicornis' meaning 'one-horned'.
