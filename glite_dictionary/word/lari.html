lari

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑːri / (AmE), / ˈlɑːri / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Georgia, equal to 100 tetri
the currency of Georgia

Examples

Prices in Tbilisi are usually quoted in lari.
She exchanged her dollars for lari at the airport.
The Georgian lari has remained stable against the euro this year.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: tetri - a subunit of currency in Georgia, 1/100 of a lari

Etymology

From Georgian ლარი (lari), meaning 'hoard' or 'property'.
