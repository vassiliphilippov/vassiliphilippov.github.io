Kabbalism

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæb.əˌlɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈkab.əˌlɪz.əm / (BrE)
the doctrines and teachings of the Kabbalah, a Jewish mystical and esoteric tradition
the teachings of Jewish Kabbalah

Examples

Many scholars have devoted years to the study of Kabbalism and its influence on Western thought.
The artist's work is deeply inspired by the symbolism found in Kabbalism.
Debates about the origins of Kabbalism continue among religious historians.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kabbalah - a system of Jewish mystical thought

Etymology

From Hebrew *qabbālāh* (tradition) + English suffix -ism.
