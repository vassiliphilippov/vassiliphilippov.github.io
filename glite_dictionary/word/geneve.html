Geneve

1. PROPN

/ ʒəˈnɛv / (AmE), / ʒəˈnɛv / (BrE)
A city in southwestern Switzerland at the western end of Lake Geneva; known as the headquarters of numerous international organizations.
city in Switzerland, international center

Examples

The United Nations has a major office in Geneve.
Many diplomats travel to Geneve for international conferences.
The beautiful Lake Geneva is a key feature of Geneve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Geneva - English name for the same Swiss city

Etymology

French form of the city's name (Geneva in English); from Latin 'Genava'.
