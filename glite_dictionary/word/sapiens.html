sapiens

1. ADJ

/ ˈseɪ.pi.ənz / (AmE), / ˈseɪ.pɪ.ənz / (BrE)
relating to, or characteristic of, the species Homo sapiens (modern humans).
relating to Homo sapiens

Examples

The discovery of new fossils provided more insight into early sapiens communities.
Scientists analyzed sapiens DNA to compare genetic variation across continents.
Art is a uniquely sapiens form of expression, reflecting complex thought and culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: human - of or relating to people or mankind

Etymology

From Latin 'sapiēns', meaning 'wise', used in taxonomy for Homo sapiens ('wise man').
