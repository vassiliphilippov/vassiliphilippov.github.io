hypothermic

1. ADJ

/ ˌhaɪpəˈθɜrmɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈθɜːmɪk / (BrE)
having or relating to abnormally low body temperature; affected by hypothermia.
having abnormally low body temperature
medical

Examples

The mountain climber became hypothermic after being exposed to freezing temperatures for hours.
Doctors quickly treated the hypothermic patient with warm fluids and blankets.
A hypothermic state can be life-threatening without immediate medical intervention.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hyperthermic - having or relating to abnormally high body temperature
  • Synonym: hypothermia - condition of having abnormally low body temperature

Etymology

From hypothermia + -ic, meaning relating to or having low body temperature.
