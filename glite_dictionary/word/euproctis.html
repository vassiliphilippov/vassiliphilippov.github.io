Euproctis

1. NOUN

/ juˈprɑktɪs / (AmE), / juˈprɒktɪs / (BrE)
A genus of moths in the family Lymantriidae, some species of which are known as tussock moths.
genus of moths (Lymantriidae)

Examples

The caterpillars of Euproctis species are known to cause skin irritation in humans.
Several Euproctis moths have become significant pests in agriculture and forestry.
Researchers are studying the distribution of Euproctis throughout temperate and tropical regions.

Etymology

From Greek 'eu' (good/well) + 'proktos' (anus), used in taxonomy.
