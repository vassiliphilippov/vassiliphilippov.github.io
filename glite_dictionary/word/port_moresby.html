Port Moresby

1. PROPN

/ pɔrt ˈmɔr(z)bi / (AmE), / pɔːt ˈmɔːzbi / (BrE)
the administrative capital and largest city of Papua New Guinea, located on its southeastern coast
capital city of Papua New Guinea

Examples

She plans to visit Port Moresby during her business trip to Papua New Guinea.
Political leaders gathered in Port Moresby for the international summit.
The climate in Port Moresby is tropical, with a distinct wet and dry season.

Etymology

Named after Captain John Moresby, a British naval officer who explored the area in 1873.
