stacks

2. NOUN

/ stæks / (AmE), / stæks / (BrE)
the area of a library where books are stored on large sets of shelves, often not open to the public
library shelving area

Examples

Only library staff are allowed to enter the stacks to retrieve rare books.
She found the old manuscript deep within the stacks of the university library.
The stacks house thousands of volumes that are not on public display.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: stack - a pile of objects, typically neatly arranged
