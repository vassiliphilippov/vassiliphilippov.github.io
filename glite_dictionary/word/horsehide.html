horsehide

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrsˌhaɪd / (AmE), / ˈhɔːshaɪd / (BrE)
leather made from the skin of a horse, used especially for durable goods such as gloves, boots, or baseballs
leather from the hide of a horse

Examples

The old baseball was made of genuine horsehide.
Leatherworkers value horsehide for its strength and durability.
The company specializes in jackets crafted from horsehide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leather - material made from animal hides, tanned and prepared for use
