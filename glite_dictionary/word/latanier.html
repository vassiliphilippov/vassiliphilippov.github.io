latanier

1. NOUN

/ ləˈtɑːni.eɪ / (AmE), / ləˈtænieɪ / (BrE)
a type of fan palm native to the southern United States and the Caribbean region
fan palm of the southern U.S. and Caribbean

Examples

The latanier thrives in the sandy soils along the southern coast.
Local artisans use latanier leaves to weave baskets and hats.
During our trip to the Caribbean, we saw several species of latanier in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fan palm - a palm with fan-shaped leaves

Etymology

From French 'latanier', referring to certain fan palms.
