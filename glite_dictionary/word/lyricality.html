lyricality

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɪrɪˈkæləti / (AmE), / ˌlɪrɪˈkalɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being suitable for singing, especially in terms of musical or poetic expression
quality of being suitable for singing

Examples

The lyricality of her poetry makes it ideal for setting to music.
Many composers admire the lyricality of Italian opera.
Critics have praised the lyricality in his songwriting style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musicality - quality of being musical or having musical talent

Etymology

From lyric + -ality, patterned after musicality.
