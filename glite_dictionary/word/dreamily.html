dreamily

1. ADV

/ ˈdriːmɪli / (AmE), / ˈdriːmɪli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a sense of being lost in thought, imagination, or pleasant dreams.
in a dreamy or absent-minded way

Examples

She gazed dreamily out of the window, lost in her thoughts.
He spoke dreamily, as if his mind were far away from the conversation.
The sunlight filtered dreamily through the curtains, filling the room with a soft glow.

Synset Examples

‘She would look beautiful in the new dress,’ Tommy said dreamily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absentmindedly - in an absent-minded manner
  • Synonym: pensively - in a thoughtful or reflective manner

Etymology

From 'dreamy' + '-ly', where 'dreamy' derives from 'dream'.
