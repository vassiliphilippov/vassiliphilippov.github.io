slanguage

1. NOUN

/ ˈslæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈslæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
language that makes excessive use of slang or cant, often difficult for outsiders to understand
language with excessive slang

Examples

Teenagers often use slanguage that confuses older generations.
The movie script was full of New York slanguage that gave it authenticity.
His stories are entertaining, but the slanguage makes them hard to follow for outsiders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slang - very informal language used by a particular group

Etymology

Blend of 'slang' and 'language'.
