antimonic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.tɪˈmoʊ.nɪk / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪˈməʊ.nɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or derived from antimony, especially in which antimony has a higher valence.
relating to antimony or its compounds

Examples

The laboratory synthesized antimonic compounds for industrial research purposes.
The mineral sample contained traces of antimonic acid.
Their study focused on the oxidation states of antimonic and antimonious oxides.

Synset Examples

antimonious oxide

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: antimonious - relating to antimony with a lower valence

Etymology

From antimony, with the suffix -ic indicating a higher oxidation state.
