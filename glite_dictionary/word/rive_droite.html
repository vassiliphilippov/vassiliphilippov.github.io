Rive Droite

1. NOUN

/ riv drwat / (AmE), / riːv drwat / (BrE)
the northern part of Paris situated on the north bank of the River Seine, often regarded for its commercial and cultural importance
northern bank of the Seine in Paris

Examples

Many famous fashion houses are located on the Rive Droite of Paris.
Visitors often compare the artistic vibe of the Rive Gauche with the sophistication of the Rive Droite.
She preferred to stay on the Rive Droite, closer to the city’s shopping districts.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Rive Gauche - the southern part of Paris on the south bank of the Seine

Etymology

French, literally 'right bank'.
