Leucanthemum superbum

1. NOUN

/ luˈkænθəməm suˈpɝːbəm / (AmE), / ljuːˈkænθɪməm sjuːˈpɜːbəm / (BrE)
A hybrid garden plant of the daisy family, with large white flower heads, commonly called the Shasta daisy.
a hybrid daisy-like garden plant

Examples

The gardener planted a row of Leucanthemum superbum along the path to brighten the border.
Many people know Leucanthemum superbum by its common name, the Shasta daisy.
In full bloom, the Leucanthemum superbum adds a classic touch to any summer garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Shasta daisy - Common name for Leucanthemum superbum, a popular garden flower

Etymology

From Greek leukos (white) and anthemon (flower); Latin superbum (superb).
