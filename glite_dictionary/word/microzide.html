Microzide

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪkroʊˌzaɪd / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌzaɪd / (BrE)
a trademark for a diuretic drug (hydrochlorothiazide) used to treat high blood pressure and fluid retention
trademark for a diuretic drug
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Microzide to help manage my high blood pressure.
Some patients experience frequent urination when starting Microzide.
If you miss a dose of Microzide, take it as soon as you remember.
