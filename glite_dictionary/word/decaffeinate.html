to decaffeinate

1. VERB

/ diˈkæfəˌneɪt / (AmE), / diˈkæfɪneɪt / (BrE)
to remove caffeine from (especially from coffee or tea)
remove caffeine from (a drink)

Examples

Many manufacturers decaffeinate coffee beans before roasting them.
She prefers to decaffeinate her tea to avoid insomnia.
This plant can decaffeinate beverages using a natural water process.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: caffeinate - to add caffeine to (a drink or food)

Etymology

From de- (removal) + caffeinate (to add caffeine), modelled after caffeine.
