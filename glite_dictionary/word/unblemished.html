unblemished

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈblɛmɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈblɛmɪʃt / (BrE)
free from any physical flaws or moral faults; not marked by imperfections, stains, or wrongdoing
without physical or moral flaw

Examples

Her reputation has remained unblemished throughout her career in public service.
He prided himself on keeping an unblemished driving record for over thirty years.
The antique vase was valuable because it was in completely unblemished condition.

Synset Examples

an unblemished record
an unblemished complexion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flawless - without any imperfections or defects
  • Synonym: perfect - having no mistakes or flaws
  • Antonym: blemished - having physical or moral imperfections or stains

Etymology

From un- (not) + blemished, from Old French ble(s)mir (to make pale, injure)
