Honiara

1. PROPN

/ hoʊniˈɑːrə / (AmE), / hɒniˈɑːrə / (BrE)
the capital city of the Solomon Islands, located on the island of Guadalcanal in the southwestern Pacific Ocean
capital city of the Solomon Islands

Examples
