Asparagus setaceus

1. NOUN

/ əˈspærəɡəs sɛˈteɪsiəs / (AmE), / əˈspærəɡəs sɪˈteɪsiəs / (BrE)
a fernlike climbing plant native to South Africa, grown as an ornamental for its feathery foliage
fernlike ornamental plant of South Africa

Examples

Many gardeners choose Asparagus setaceus for its delicate, feathery foliage in hanging baskets.
The Asparagus setaceus can be invasive if not managed in warm climates.
She decorated the centerpiece with sprigs of Asparagus setaceus for a soft, lush effect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asparagus fern - common name for Asparagus setaceus, an ornamental plant

Etymology

From the genus name Asparagus and Latin setaceus meaning 'bristly' or 'having bristles.'
