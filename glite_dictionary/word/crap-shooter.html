crap-shooter

1. NOUN

/ ˈkræp ˌʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈkræp ˌʃuːtə / (BrE)
a person who plays or gambles at the game of craps by throwing dice
person gambling at craps

Examples

The experienced crap-shooter watched the dice intently as they tumbled across the felt.
At the casino, a group of crap-shooters gathered around the table, hoping for a winning streak.
She never considered herself a gambler until she became an avid crap-shooter on her trips to Las Vegas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gambler - a person who wagers money on games or events
  • Synonym: craps - a dice game in which players bet on the outcome of a throw

Etymology

From 'crap(s)' (dice game) + 'shooter' (one who throws, especially dice).
