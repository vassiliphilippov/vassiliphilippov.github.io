advertising

1. NOUN

/ ˈædvərˌtaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈadvətaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or profession of producing and spreading advertisements to promote products, services, or ideas
promotion of products or services

Examples

The company increased its budget for advertising to boost sales.
Internet advertising has become increasingly important in recent years.
He works in advertising and creates commercials for major brands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: publicity - the giving out of information to attract public attention
  • Synonym: marketing - the activities involved in promoting and selling products or services

Etymology

From advertise + -ing; advertise from Old French 'advertir', from Latin 'advertere' (turn toward, pay attention).

3. NOUN

/ ˈæd.vɚˌtaɪ.zɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈadvətaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity or business of creating and distributing public notices to promote products, services, or events.
promotion of products or services

Examples

She works in advertising and helps companies reach new customers.
The rise of digital advertising has changed how businesses attract buyers.
They invested heavily in online advertising to boost brand awareness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promotion - activity to increase sales or public awareness
  • Synonym: marketing - the commercial processes involved in promoting and selling products

Etymology

Present participle of 'advertise,' which comes from Middle English 'advertisen,' from Old French 'advertir' (to turn toward) and Latin 'advertere.'
