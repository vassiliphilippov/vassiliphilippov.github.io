backbend

1. NOUN

/ ˈbækˌbɛnd / (AmE), / ˈbækˌbɛnd / (BrE)
An acrobatic movement in which a person bends their trunk backward from a standing position until their hands touch the floor.
acrobatic movement bending backward to the floor

Examples

She practiced her backbend every day to improve her flexibility.
The gymnast executed a perfect backbend during the floor routine.
Learning a backbend requires strength and patience.

Etymology

From 'back' + 'bend', describing the movement.
