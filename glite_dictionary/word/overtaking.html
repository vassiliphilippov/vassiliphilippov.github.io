overtaking

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊvɚˈteɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈteɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the action of going past a moving vehicle or person in order to get in front of them, especially while driving
passing a moving vehicle to get ahead

Examples

She was fined for dangerous overtaking on the narrow country road.
Proper signaling is essential before attempting overtaking another vehicle.
Failure to observe the rules on overtaking can result in accidents.

Synset Examples

she drove but well but her reckless passing of every car on the road frightened me

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passing - the act of going past a moving vehicle or person
