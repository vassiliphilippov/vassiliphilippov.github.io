good-neighborliness

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈneɪbərli.nəs / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈneɪbəlɪ.nəs / (BrE)
A friendly and helpful attitude or behavior toward one's neighbors.
friendly helpfulness to neighbors

Examples

The community thrived thanks to the good-neighborliness of its residents.
Her good-neighborliness was evident when she helped care for the children next door.
Promoting good-neighborliness can lead to safer and more supportive neighborhoods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neighborliness - the quality of being friendly and helpful to neighbors
