goldsmithery

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldˌsmɪθəri / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌsmɪθəri / (BrE)
the work, art, or trade of making or dealing in articles of gold
the craft or trade of a goldsmith

Examples

The museum displays exquisite examples of Florentine goldsmithery from the Renaissance period.
She dedicated her life to mastering the techniques of goldsmithery.
Artisans studied ancient goldsmithery methods to recreate the jewelry found in the tomb.

Synset Examples

The history of Florentine goldsmithery is also the history of its economy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldsmithing - the art or process of working gold; similar to goldsmithery

Etymology

From 'goldsmith' (worker in gold) + '-ery' (denoting a place of work or trade).
