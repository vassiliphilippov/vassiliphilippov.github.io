jocote

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈkoʊteɪ / (AmE), / hɒˈkəʊteɪ / (BrE)
A tropical American shrub or small tree (Spondias purpurea) that produces edible purplish or red fruit.
tropical shrub or its fruit

Examples

We bought a basket of fresh jocote from the local market.
The jocote tree thrives in warm, tropical climates throughout Central America.
Children enjoy eating ripe jocote as a sweet and tangy snack.

Etymology

From Spanish 'jocote', from Nahuatl 'xocotl' (fruit).
