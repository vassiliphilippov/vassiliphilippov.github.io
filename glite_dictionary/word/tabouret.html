tabouret

1. NOUN

/ təˈbuːreɪ / (AmE), / ˈtabʊreɪ / (BrE)
a low, often drum-shaped stool, sometimes used as a seat or stand
low, drum-shaped stool

Examples

The living room was furnished with a colorful tabouret near the fireplace.
She bought a vintage tabouret at the antique market to use as a plant stand.
He sat quietly on the tabouret, sketching by the window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stool - a simple seat without a back or arms

Etymology

From French 'tabouret' (small stool), diminutive of 'tabour' (drum).
