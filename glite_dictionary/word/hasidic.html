Hasidic

1. ADJ

/ hɑːˈsɪdɪk / (AmE), / həˈsɪdɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Hasidism, a Jewish religious movement, or its members, beliefs, or practices.
relating to Hasidism or its members

Examples

Many Hasidic communities maintain distinctive dress and customs.
The film explores the experiences of a young woman in a Hasidic neighborhood of Brooklyn.
His Hasidic upbringing influenced his approach to spirituality and tradition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hasidism - Jewish religious movement emphasizing mysticism and spirituality

Etymology

From Hebrew ḥasid (pious), via Yiddish khasidish (Hasidic).
