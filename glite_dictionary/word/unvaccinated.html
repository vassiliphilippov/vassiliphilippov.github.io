unvaccinated

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈvæksɪˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈvæksɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having received a vaccine against a particular disease.
not vaccinated

Examples

Many hospitalizations are occurring among the unvaccinated.
The school requires all students to show proof of vaccination if they are not unvaccinated.
He chose to remain unvaccinated despite the available information.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: vaccinated - having received a vaccine against a disease

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + vaccinated, from Latin vaccinus (from cows) by way of vaccine.
