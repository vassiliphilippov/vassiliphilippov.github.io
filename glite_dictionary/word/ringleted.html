ringleted

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪŋlətɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋlɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of hair) having or formed into ringlets.
(of hair) formed into ringlets

Examples

She wore her ringleted hair loose for the celebration.
A child with ringleted golden locks ran through the garden.
He noticed her ringleted tresses glistening in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curly - (of hair) having curls or a tendency to curl

Etymology

From 'ringlet' (small ring or curl of hair) + '-ed' (forming adjectives).
