incredulously

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈkrɛdʒələsli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkrɛd.jʊ.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a manner that shows disbelief or skepticism.
in a manner showing disbelief

Examples

She stared incredulously at the news anchor, unable to believe the announcement.
He laughed incredulously when told he had won the lottery.
The students listened incredulously as the professor described the impossible experiment.

Synset Examples

the woman looked up at her incredulously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skeptically - in a skeptical manner
  • Antonym: credulously - in a credulous (gullible or too trusting) manner

Etymology

From 'incredulous', from Latin incredulus ('in-' not + 'credulus' believing), plus -ly adverbial suffix.
