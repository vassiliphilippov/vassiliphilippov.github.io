educationally

1. ADV

/ ˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to education or educational methods
in an educational manner

Examples

The new program will benefit the community educationally and culturally.
She has always tried to support her children educationally by helping with their homework.
The school is educationally focused, with an emphasis on critical thinking skills.

Synset Examples

the assistant masters formed a committee of their own to consider what could be done educationally for the town

Related Terms

  • Synonym: academically - in a manner that relates to education or scholarship

Etymology

From 'education' + '-ally', after the pattern of 'educational'.
