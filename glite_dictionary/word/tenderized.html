tenderized

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛn.dɚ.aɪzd / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.də.raɪzd / (BrE)
(of meat) made more tender by marinating, pounding, or other methods.
made more tender (meat)

Examples

The chef recommended the tenderized steak for extra juiciness and flavor.
She always uses a mallet to prepare tenderized pork chops for dinner.
Store-bought tenderized meat often cooks more quickly and evenly.

Synset Examples

tenderized meat

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: tender - easy to cut or chew; not tough (of food, especially meat)

Etymology

From the verb 'tenderize', formed by adding the suffix -ize to 'tender'.
