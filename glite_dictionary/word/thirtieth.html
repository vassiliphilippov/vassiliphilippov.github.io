thirtieth

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɜrtiəθ / (AmE), / ˈθɜːtiɪθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number corresponding to thirty; being the position number 30 in a sequence.
ordinal number for position 30

Examples

He finished in thirtieth place in the marathon.
The meeting will be held on the thirtieth of this month.
She celebrated her thirtieth birthday with a big party.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twentieth - ordinal number for position 20
  • Degree Higher: fortieth - ordinal number for position 40

Etymology

From "thirty" + "-eth", an ordinal suffix in English.

2. ADJ

/ ˈθɜrtiɪθ / (AmE), / ˈθɜːtiɪθ / (BrE)
coming next after the twenty-ninth in position; being number thirty in a sequence
ordinal number: 30th in a sequence

Examples

She finished in thirtieth place out of a hundred runners.
His birthday falls on the thirtieth of September.
This is the thirtieth anniversary of the school's founding.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-ninth - ordinal number: 29th in a sequence
  • Degree Higher: thirty-first - ordinal number: 31st in a sequence

Etymology

From 'thirty' + '-eth', the ordinal suffix used in English.
