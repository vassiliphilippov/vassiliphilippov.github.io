unsubduable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.səbˈduː.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.səbˈdjuː.ə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to overcome, defeat, or bring under control
impossible to subdue or conquer

Examples

The army's spirit proved unsubduable even in the face of overwhelming odds.
Her unsubduable determination allowed her to achieve her goals despite many setbacks.
The region's unsubduable wilderness challenged even the most experienced explorers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invincible - too powerful to be defeated or overcome
  • Synonym: indomitable - impossible to subdue or defeat
  • Antonym: subduable - able to be subdued or overcome

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'subduable' (able to be subdued).
