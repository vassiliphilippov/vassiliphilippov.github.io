showtime

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌtaɪm / (BrE)
the time when a performance, event, or entertainment is scheduled to begin
scheduled start time of a show or event

Examples

The tickets said showtime was at 7:30 p.m., so we arrived early.
Please be seated by showtime to avoid disturbing the performers.
We checked the website to confirm the showtime before heading to the theater.

Synset Examples

they got an early start
she knew from the get-go that he was the man for her

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kickoff - the scheduled start time of a sports event or activity
  • Synonym: curtain time - the time a theatrical performance is scheduled to begin
