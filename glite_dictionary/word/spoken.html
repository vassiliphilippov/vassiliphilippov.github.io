spoken

1. ADJ

/ ˈspoʊkən / (AmE), / ˈspəʊkən / (BrE)
expressed or communicated by means of speech rather than writing; uttered by speaking
expressed or communicated by speech

Examples

Instructions should be given in spoken English for the new employees.
Her spoken words carried more weight than any letter.
The play will be performed entirely in spoken French.

Synset Examples

a spoken message
the spoken language
a soft-spoken person
sharp-spoken

Related Terms

  • Antonym: written - expressed in writing rather than speech

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'speak', from Old English *specan* or *sprecan* (to speak).
