plagiarized

1. ADJ

/ ˈpleɪdʒəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ˈpleɪdʒərʌɪzd / (BrE)
copied from someone else's work and presented as if it is your own
copied and passed off as original

Examples

The professor discovered that large portions of the essay were plagiarized.
Her article included several plagiarized paragraphs from various sources.
Submitting plagiarized work can result in severe academic penalties.

Synset Examples

used plagiarized data in his thesis
a work dotted with plagiarized phrases

Related Terms

  • Antonym: original - not copied or derived from something else
  • Synonym: to plagiarize - to copy someone else's work and claim it as one's own
