congou

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑŋɡu / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡuː / (BrE)
A high-quality black tea produced in China, known for its carefully rolled leaves and delicate flavor.
Chinese black tea of high quality

Examples

She prefers to drink congou in the afternoon because of its smooth flavor.
At the tea shop, we sampled a rare congou imported directly from China.
The sommelier recommended a strong congou to accompany the dessert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oolong - Chinese tea produced through partial oxidation, between green and black tea

Etymology

From Chinese (Cantonese) 'kung fu' (skill, effort), referring to the care taken in processing the tea.
