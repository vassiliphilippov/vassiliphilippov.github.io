suitableness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuː.tə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsuː.tə.bl̩.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being appropriate or fitting for a particular purpose or use
appropriateness for a specific purpose

Examples

The suitableness of this fabric for outdoor furniture impressed the interior designer.
We must assess the suitableness of the candidate before making an offer.
Questions arose regarding the suitableness of their methods for such a sensitive project.

Synset Examples

an important requirement is suitability for long trips

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suitability - the quality of being right or appropriate for a specific purpose
  • Antonym: unsuitableness - the quality of not being right or suitable

Etymology

From suitable + -ness, with suitable from Late Latin *suitabilis* and -ness denoting a quality or state.
