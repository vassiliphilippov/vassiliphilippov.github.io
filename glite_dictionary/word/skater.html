skater

1. NOUN

/ ˈskeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈskeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who moves on ice or a hard surface using skates, especially as a sport or hobby
person who skates, e.g. on ice or roller skates

Examples

The skater glided effortlessly across the frozen lake.
A group of teenage skaters practiced tricks at the skate park.
The Olympic skater performed a flawless routine on the ice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skateboarder - a person who rides a skateboard
  • Synonym: ice skater - a person who skates on ice
