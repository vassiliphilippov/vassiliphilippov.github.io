iproclozide

1. NOUN

/ aɪˈproʊkləˌzaɪd / (AmE), / aɪˈprəʊkləˌzaɪd / (BrE)
an antidepressant drug that functions as a monoamine oxidase inhibitor, formerly used to treat depression
antidepressant (MAOI drug)
medical

Examples

Iproclozide was withdrawn from the market due to concerns about its safety profile.
The psychiatrist considered prescribing iproclozide for patients who did not respond to other antidepressants.
Researchers studied the effects of iproclozide on mood improvement in clinical trials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antidepressant - a drug used to alleviate depression
  • Synonym: monoamine oxidase inhibitor - a class of drugs that inhibit the activity of monoamine oxidase enzymes
