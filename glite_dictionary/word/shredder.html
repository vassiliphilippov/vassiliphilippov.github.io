shredder

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃrɛdər / (AmE), / ˈʃrɛdə / (BrE)
A machine that cuts documents into small pieces to prevent unauthorized access or reading.
machine for destroying documents

Examples

After the meeting, she fed the confidential papers into the shredder for disposal.
The office shredder jammed because someone tried to destroy a thick stack of files at once.
To prevent identity theft, it's wise to use a shredder for old bills and documents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: destroyer - thing or person that destroys something
  • Antonym: protector - thing or person that keeps something safe
