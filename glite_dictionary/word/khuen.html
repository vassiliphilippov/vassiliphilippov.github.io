Khuen

1. NOUN

/ kuːˈɛn / (AmE), / kuːˈɛn / (BrE)
A branch of the Tai languages spoken mainly in Loungnamtha Province, Nale, Sing, and Viangphoukha districts of Laos.
a Tai language of Laos

Examples

The Khuen language is mainly used in the northern provinces of Laos.
Linguists have studied the structure of Khuen to understand Tai language development.
Fewer people speak Khuen today as younger generations shift to other languages.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Tai - the group of languages to which Khuen belongs
  • Degree Lower: Lamet - a neighboring language spoken near the Khuen area
