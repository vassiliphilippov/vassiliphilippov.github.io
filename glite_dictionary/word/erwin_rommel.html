Erwin Rommel

1. PROPN

/ ˈɜrwɪn ˈrɑməl / (AmE), / ˈɜːwɪn ˈrɒməl / (BrE)
(1891–1944) A German field marshal renowned for his strategic leadership in North Africa during World War II.
German World War II field marshal

Examples

Erwin Rommel was respected by both his troops and his adversaries for his tactical genius in desert warfare.
Many military historians study Erwin Rommel's innovative strategies in the North African campaign.
The nickname 'Desert Fox' was famously given to Erwin Rommel due to his effectiveness in battle.
