bellman

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛlmən / (AmE), / ˈbɛlmən / (BrE)
a man employed in a hotel to carry guests' luggage and run errands
hotel luggage porter

Examples

The bellman greeted us at the entrance and took our bags to our room.
She tipped the bellman for helping with her heavy suitcase.
While waiting in the lobby, the bellman offered to arrange a taxi for us.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bellhop - a hotel employee who carries luggage and performs errands
  • Synonym: porter - a hotel employee who carries guests' luggage

Etymology

From 'bell' (referring to the call bell in hotels) plus 'man'.
