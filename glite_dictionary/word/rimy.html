rimy

1. ADJ

/ ˈraɪmi / (AmE), / ˈraɪmi / (BrE)
covered with frost; having a coating of frost or hoarfrost
covered with frost

Examples

The fields were rimy under the pale morning sun.
A rimy coating sparkled on the old stone wall.
The rimy air hinted at a cold night ahead.

Related Terms

Etymology

From rime (frost), from Old English hrīm (hoarfrost).
