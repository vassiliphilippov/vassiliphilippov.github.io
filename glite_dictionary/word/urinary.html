urinary

1. ADJ

/ ˈjʊrəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈjʊərɪnəri / (BrE)
relating to urine or the organs and processes involved in the production and excretion of urine
relating to urine or the urinary system

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with a urinary tract infection after experiencing pain and discomfort.
Doctors are studying urinary disorders to better understand their causes and treatments.
Good hydration is important for maintaining a healthy urinary system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: renal - relating to the kidneys

Etymology

From Latin 'urinarius', from 'urina' (urine).

2. ADJ

/ ˈjʊrɪˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈjʊərɪnəri / (BrE)
relating to urine or the organs involved in the production and removal of urine from the body.
relating to urine or the urinary system
medical

Examples

The doctor suspected a urinary tract infection and ordered additional tests.
Proper hydration helps maintain a healthy urinary system.
Symptoms such as frequent urination may indicate a urinary disorder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: renal - relating to the kidneys

Etymology

From Latin urinarius (pertaining to urine), from urina (urine).
