unhearing

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɪə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
completely unable to hear; totally deaf
unable to hear; deaf

Examples

The unhearing audience failed to react to the announcement.
He shouted at his unhearing companion, but there was no response.
Nature seemed unhearing to her prayers, remaining silent and indifferent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deaf - lacking the power of hearing or having impaired hearing

Etymology

un- (not) + hear (to perceive sound) + -ing (adjective forming suffix).
