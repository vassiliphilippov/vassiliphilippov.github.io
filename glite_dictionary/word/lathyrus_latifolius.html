Lathyrus latifolius

1. NOUN

/ ləˈθaɪrəs ˌlætɪˈfoʊliəs / (AmE), / ləˈθaɪrəs ˌlætɪˈfəʊliəs / (BrE)
A perennial climbing plant of central and southern Europe with purple, pink, or white flowers, naturalized in North America; also known as perennial pea.
species of perennial climbing plant

Examples

The Lathyrus latifolius adds vibrant colors to many cottage gardens throughout the summer.
Gardeners often choose Lathyrus latifolius because it is hardy and attracts pollinators.
In some regions, Lathyrus latifolius has become naturalized along roadsides and fields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perennial pea - common name for Lathyrus latifolius, a perennial climbing plant

Etymology

From Latin, with 'Lathyrus' meaning pea and 'latifolius' meaning broad-leaved.
