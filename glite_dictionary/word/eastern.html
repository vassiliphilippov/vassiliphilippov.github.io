eastern

1. ADJ

/ ˈistərn / (AmE), / ˈiːstən / (BrE)
Situated in, directed toward, or facing the east.
situated in or facing the east

Examples

The museum is located on the eastern side of the city.
Many eastern states experience colder winters than those in the west.
The sunrise painted the eastern sky with beautiful colors.

Synset Examples

eastern cities

Related Terms

  • Antonym: western - situated in or facing the west

Etymology

From Old English ēastern, from ēast (east) + -ern (suffix forming adjectives).
