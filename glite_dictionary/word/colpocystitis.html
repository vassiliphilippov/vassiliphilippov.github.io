colpocystitis

1. NOUN

/ kɑl.poʊ.sɪsˈtaɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / kɒl.pə.sɪsˈtaɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of both the vagina and the bladder
inflammation of the vagina and bladder
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed the patient with colpocystitis after examining her symptoms.
Antibiotics are commonly prescribed for infections such as colpocystitis.
Chronic colpocystitis may require long-term treatment and regular medical follow-up.

Etymology

From Greek kolpos (vagina) + cystis (bladder) + -itis (inflammation).
