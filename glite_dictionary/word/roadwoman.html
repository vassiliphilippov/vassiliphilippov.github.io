roadwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊdˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who is employed to repair or maintain roads.
female road repair worker

Examples

The council hired a new roadwoman to help with repairs after the storm.
As a roadwoman, she spent long hours working outside in all weather conditions.
The team included both roadmen and a roadwoman to fix the damaged highway.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: roadman - a man employed to repair or maintain roads

Etymology

Formed from 'road' + 'woman', by analogy with 'roadman'.
