arcanum

1. NOUN

/ ɑɹˈkeɪnəm / (AmE), / ɑːˈkeɪnəm / (BrE)
a secret or mysterious knowledge, especially one known or understood by only a few people
secret or mysterious knowledge

Examples

The legendary chef finally revealed the arcanum behind his famous sauce.
Alchemists were preoccupied with discovering the arcanum of immortality.
The old manuscript claimed to contain the arcanum of lost civilizations.

Synset Examples

the secret of Cajun cooking

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secret - something kept hidden or unknown
  • Synonym: mystery - something difficult or impossible to understand or explain

Etymology

From Latin arcanum (secret), from arcanus (secret, hidden).
