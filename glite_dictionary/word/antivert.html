Antivert

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.tiˌvɝt / (AmE), / ˈæn.tɪˌvɜːt / (BrE)
A trademarked antihistamine medication used to treat or prevent motion sickness.
antihistamine used for motion sickness
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Antivert to help with my seasickness on the cruise.
She takes Antivert whenever she feels nauseous during long car rides.
The pharmacist recommended Antivert for his upcoming flight to prevent dizziness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meclizine - the generic name for the drug sold as Antivert
