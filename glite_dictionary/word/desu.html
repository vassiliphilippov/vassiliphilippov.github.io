desu

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛsu / (AmE), / ˈdɛsu / (BrE)
(in anime and manga fandom) a Japanese copula often added to the end of sentences for cuteness or emphasis, particularly by characters to sound polite or endearing.
copula used for cuteness in anime/manga

Examples

The character kept desu at the end of every sentence to sound especially cute.
Fans enjoy imitating their favorite anime characters by desu during conversations.
In online forums, users sometimes end posts with desu as a playful reference.

Synset Examples

In anime culture, saying 'desu' adds a cute touch.

Etymology

From Japanese です (*desu*), a copular verb used at the end of sentences, often for politeness.
