Jomada II

1. NOUN

/ dʒoʊˈmɑː.də ˈsɪk.sθ / (AmE), / dʒəʊˈmɑː.də ˈsɪksθ / (BrE)
the sixth month of the Islamic calendar, used in Islamic countries and by Muslims worldwide
sixth month of Islamic calendar

Examples

Many important events in Islamic history took place in Jomada II.
The holiday falls on the twelfth day of Jomada II this year.
Muslims use the lunar Hijri calendar, with months like Jomada II to mark specific dates.

Etymology

From Arabic جُمَادَى الآخِرَة (*Jumāda al-ākhira*), meaning 'the latter Jumada'.
