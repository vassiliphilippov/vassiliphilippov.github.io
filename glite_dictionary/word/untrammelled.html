untrammelled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtræmld / (AmE), / ʌnˈtræməld / (BrE)
Not restricted, confined, or limited; free and unrestrained.
not confined or limited

Examples

The artist enjoyed an untrammelled freedom to express her ideas.
In the wilderness, wildlife roams untrammelled by human interference.
His untrammelled imagination led to some truly innovative inventions.

Synset Examples

the gift of a fresh eye and an untrammeled curiosity
the untrammeled rush that the snows had shown in the first spring sun

Related Terms

  • Antonym: restrained - kept under control or within limits
  • Synonym: free - not under the control or in the power of another

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'trammel' (to restrict), originally from Old French 'tramail' (a kind of net).
