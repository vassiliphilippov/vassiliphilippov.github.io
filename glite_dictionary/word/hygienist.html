hygienist

1. NOUN

/ haɪˈdʒinɪst / (AmE), / haɪˈdʒiːnɪst / (BrE)
a specialist trained in the practice and promotion of hygiene, especially in a medical or dental context
medical or dental hygiene specialist
medical

Examples

The dental hygienist gave me tips on how to maintain healthy gums.
As a hospital hygienist, she educates patients about preventing infection.
You should book an appointment with a hygienist for a regular cleaning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dentist - a person qualified to treat diseases and conditions of the teeth and gums
  • Antonym: patient - a person receiving medical care

Etymology

From French 'hygiéniste', from 'hygiène' (hygiene) plus '-iste' (specialist).
