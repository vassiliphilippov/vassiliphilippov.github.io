punster

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌn.stər / (AmE), / ˈpʌn.stə / (BrE)
a person who habitually makes puns, especially in an excessive or playful way
person fond of making puns

Examples

My uncle is a notorious punster who can't resist turning every conversation into wordplay.
Whenever a joke is needed, the resident punster in our group never disappoints.
At the comedy show, the punster delighted the audience with clever plays on words.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pun - a joke exploiting the different possible meanings of a word
  • Synonym: wit - the ability to use words in a clever and humorous way

Etymology

Formed from 'pun' + '-ster' (a suffix denoting a person who does something).
