Phacelia minor

1. NOUN

/ fəˈsiːliə ˈmaɪnər / (AmE), / fəˈsiːliə ˈmaɪnə / (BrE)
A desert plant native to southern California, characterized by blue or violet tubular flowers arranged in terminal racemes.
desert plant of southern California

Examples

Phacelia minor is easily recognized by its vibrant violet flowers blooming in the spring.
Many hikers enjoy spotting Phacelia minor while exploring the deserts of southern California.
The delicate blossoms of Phacelia minor attract numerous pollinators throughout the flowering season.

Etymology

From New Latin, genus name Phacelia and Latin minor meaning 'smaller'.
