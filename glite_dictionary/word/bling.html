bling

1. NOUN

/ blɪŋ / (AmE), / blɪŋ / (BrE)
flashy, expensive, and ostentatious jewelry, especially as worn to attract attention or display wealth
flashy jewelry, especially to show off wealth
slang

Examples

He loved to show off his bling at every party.
The celebrity's outfit was covered in diamonds and bling.
Some people think too much bling looks tacky.

Synset Examples

the rapper was loaded with bling

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bling-bling - an alternative form emphasizing the glitter of such jewelry

Etymology

Popularized in the late 1990s by hip-hop culture, possibly imitative of the sound of light reflecting from jewelry.
