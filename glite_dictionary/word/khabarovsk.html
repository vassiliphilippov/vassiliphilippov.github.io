Khabarovsk

1. PROPN

/ kəˈbɑːrəfsk / (AmE), / kəˈbɑːrəfsk / (BrE)
a city in southeastern Russia, located on the Amur River near the border with China; the administrative center of Khabarovsk Krai.
city in southeastern Russia on the Amur River

Examples

The Trans-Siberian Railway passes through Khabarovsk on its journey across Russia.
Khabarovsk is known for its beautiful riverfront and vibrant cultural life.
He spent three years working in Khabarovsk before moving to Moscow.

2. PROPN

/ kəˈbɑːrəvsk / (AmE), / kəˈbɑːrəfsk / (BrE)
a territory in the Russian Far East, located on the eastern coast of Siberia
territory in eastern Russia

Examples

The city of Khabarovsk serves as the administrative center of the region.
She traveled across Siberia and spent several days in Khabarovsk.
Trade between China and Khabarovsk has increased in recent years.

Etymology

From Russian Хабаровск (Khabarovsk), named after explorer Yerofey Khabarov.
