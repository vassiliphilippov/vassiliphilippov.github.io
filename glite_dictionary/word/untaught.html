untaught

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtɔt / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɔːt / (BrE)
lacking formal education or instruction; not having been taught or schooled.
lacking formal education or instruction

Examples

The museum offers art classes to untaught children in the neighborhood.
Her untaught singing voice possessed a raw and natural beauty.
The report highlighted the challenges faced by untaught rural communities.

Synset Examples

untaught people whose verbal skills are grossly deficient
an untutored genius
uneducated children

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uneducated - lacking education or schooling
  • Synonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness
  • Antonym: instructed - having received instruction or teaching

Etymology

From un- (not) + taught, past participle of teach.
