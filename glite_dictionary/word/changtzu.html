Changtzu

1. PROPN

/ tʃɑːŋˈtsuː / (AmE), / tʃɑːŋˈtsuː / (BrE)
a mountain in the central Himalayas, located on the border of Tibet and Nepal, standing 24,780 feet (7,550 meters) high.
a Himalayan mountain on the Tibet-Nepal border

Examples

Mountaineers trained for months before attempting to climb Changtzu.
The snow-capped peak of Changtzu is visible from miles away in clear weather.
Changtzu lies between Tibet and Nepal, attracting climbing enthusiasts from around the world.
