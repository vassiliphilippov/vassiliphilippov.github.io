Viola tricolor hortensis

1. NOUN

/ vaɪˈoʊlə ˈtraɪkəlɔr hɔːrˈtɛnsɪs / (AmE), / vaɪˈəʊlə ˈtrɪkəlɔː hɔːˈtɛnsɪs / (BrE)
a garden plant with large, velvety, multicolored flowers, bred chiefly from the European wild pansy
a garden plant with large, colorful flowers

Examples

The Viola tricolor hortensis added a splash of vivid color to the spring garden.
Many gardeners enjoy cultivating Viola tricolor hortensis for its lovely, velvety petals.
She arranged a bouquet featuring several Viola tricolor hortensis blossoms for the centerpiece.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pansy - common name for garden hybrids of Viola tricolor

Etymology

From Latin: 'Viola' meaning violet, 'tricolor' meaning three-colored, and 'hortensis' meaning 'of the garden'.
