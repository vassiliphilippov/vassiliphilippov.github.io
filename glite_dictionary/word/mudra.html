mudra

1. NOUN

/ muˈdrɑ / (AmE), / ˈmʊdrə / (BrE)
a symbolic hand gesture used in Hindu and Buddhist ceremonies, art, and dance, often to convey a particular spiritual meaning or intention
symbolic hand gesture in Hinduism/Buddhism

Examples

In classical Indian dance, each mudra has a unique symbolic significance.
The priest performed sacred mudras during the temple ritual.
Practicing the Gyan mudra is common in meditation to promote concentration.

Etymology

From Sanskrit मुद्रा (mudrā), meaning 'seal' or 'gesture'.
