scientifically

1. ADV

/ saɪənˈtɪfɪkli / (AmE), / saɪənˈtɪfɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to science or is based on methods and principles of science.
in a scientific manner

Examples

The theory has been scientifically verified by several independent researchers.
The product has not been scientifically tested for safety or effectiveness.
He approaches problems scientifically, using evidence and logical reasoning.

Synset Examples

this is scientifically interesting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scientific - relating to science or using the methods of science

Etymology

From scientific + -ally, patterned after Latin-derived adverbs.
