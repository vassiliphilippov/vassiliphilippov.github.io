taillike

1. ADJ

/ ˈteɪlˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈteɪl.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the characteristics of a tail.
resembling a tail

Examples

The comet left a taillike trail of dust as it moved across the sky.
Some plants have taillike appendages that help with seed dispersal.
The costume featured a taillike extension at the back for a more animalistic appearance.

Etymology

From 'tail' + '-like', meaning 'resembling a tail'.
