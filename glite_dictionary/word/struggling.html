struggling

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrʌɡəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstrʌɡlɪŋ / (BrE)
trying hard to overcome difficulties, especially lack of money, success, or recognition
trying to overcome hardship or lack of success

Examples

She opened a foundation to help struggling artists gain recognition for their work.
The struggling company had to lay off several employees to stay afloat.
Many struggling families depend on government assistance to meet their basic needs.

Synset Examples

a financially struggling theater
struggling artists

Related Terms

  • Antonym: thriving - doing well and being successful
