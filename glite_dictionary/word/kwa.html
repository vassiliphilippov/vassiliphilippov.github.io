Kwa

1. NOUN

/ kwɑ / (AmE), / kwɑː / (BrE)
a group of closely related languages in the Niger-Congo family, spoken from the Ivory Coast to Nigeria
a group of West African languages

Examples

The Kwa languages are spoken in several countries across West Africa.
Researchers have studied the structure of Kwa to trace historical migrations.
The vocabulary of Kwa languages often shares similarities with other Niger-Congo languages.
