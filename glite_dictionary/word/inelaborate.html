inelaborate

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnɪˈlæb(ə)rət / (AmE), / ˌɪnɪˈlæb(ə)rət / (BrE)
simple or lacking in rich or complex detail; not elaborate.
not elaborate or detailed

Examples

The architect chose an inelaborate design for the rural community center.
Her explanation was refreshingly inelaborate, making the topic accessible to everyone.
The decorations at the ceremony were inelaborate but tasteful.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: elaborate - involving many careful details or complexity
  • Synonym: simple - easily understood or basic, not elaborate

Etymology

From Latin prefix in- (not) + elaborate.
