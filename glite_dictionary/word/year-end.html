year-end

1. ADJ

/ jɪr ˈɛnd / (AmE), / jɪə ˈɛnd / (BrE)
Taking place or done at the close of a fiscal year.
relating to the end of a fiscal year

Examples

The company completed its year-end report ahead of schedule.
Many employees are busy with year-end closing tasks in December.
Our year-end audit will begin next week.

Synset Examples

year-end audit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annual - occurring once every year
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01014344-s
Synset POS: s

2. NOUN

/ jɪr ˈɛnd / (AmE), / jɪə ˈɛnd / (BrE)
the end of a calendar year, especially as a time for evaluating accounts or concluding activities
end of the calendar year

Examples

Many companies prepare detailed financial statements at the year-end.
We usually have a big celebration to mark the year-end.
Inventory must be counted before the official year-end.

Synset Examples

he had to unload the merchandise before the year-end

Related Terms

  • Antonym: year-beginning - the start of the calendar year
