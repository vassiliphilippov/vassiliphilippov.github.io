womanizer

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊməˌnaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈwʊmənʌɪzə / (BrE)
a man who habitually seeks the company of many women, often engaging in short-term sexual relationships with them
man who pursues many women

Examples

He earned a reputation as a womanizer during his college years.
Despite his charm, everyone warned her that he was a notorious womanizer.
The tabloids were quick to label the celebrity a womanizer after his latest scandal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: philanderer - a man who readily or frequently enters into casual sexual relationships with women
  • Antonym: faithful - remaining loyal and true, especially in relationships

Etymology

Formed from 'woman' + '-izer', modelled on similar agent nouns like 'organizer'.
