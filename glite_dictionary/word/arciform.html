arciform

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːr.sɪ.fɔrm / (AmE), / ˈɑː.sɪ.fɔːm / (BrE)
having the shape or form of an arch; arched.
shaped like an arch

Examples

The architect designed an arciform window that became the focal point of the entryway.
The arciform structure of the bridge allowed for greater weight distribution.
In anatomy, certain muscles have an arciform appearance due to their curved fibers.

Synset Examples

an arched ceiling

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arched - having a curved shape like an arch

Etymology

From Latin 'arcus' (arch) + '-form' (form, shape).
