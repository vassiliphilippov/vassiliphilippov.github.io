ECOSOC

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːkoʊsɒk / (AmE), / ˈiːkəsɒk / (BrE)
the Economic and Social Council, a permanent body of the United Nations responsible for coordinating economic and social policies and activities
UN council on economic and social issues

Examples

The annual report of ECOSOC addressed global inequality and sustainable development goals.
Representatives from multiple countries presented their progress at the ECOSOC summit.
The United Nations relies on ECOSOC to coordinate various agencies working on health and education.

Etymology

Acronym from Economic and Social Council, the formal name of the UN body.
