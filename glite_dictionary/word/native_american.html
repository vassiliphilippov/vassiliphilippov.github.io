Examples

The museum features a Native American pottery collection dating back several centuries.
She is fluent in a Native American language spoken only by a few elders in her tribe.
They attended a Native American cultural festival over the weekend.

Synset Examples

Native American religions

Etymology

Formed from 'native' (of or relating to someone born in a place) and 'American', referring to the Americas.
