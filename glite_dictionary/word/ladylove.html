ladylove

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪdiˌlʌv / (AmE), / ˈleɪdilʌv / (BrE)
a woman who is a man's sweetheart or beloved
a man's female sweetheart

Examples

He dedicated the poem to his ladylove, hoping to win her heart.
Throughout the novel, the hero never stops thinking about his ladylove.
He brought flowers to his ladylove every Sunday afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweetheart - a person with whom someone is in love
  • Synonym: girlfriend - a female partner in a romantic relationship

Etymology

Formed from 'lady' + 'love', first recorded in the 19th century.
