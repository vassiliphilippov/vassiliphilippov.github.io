WbN

1. NOUN

/ wɛst baɪ nɔrθ / (AmE), / wɛst baɪ nɔːθ / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point north of due west, corresponding to west by north (at 281°15').
compass point one point north of west

Examples

The ship changed course to WbN to avoid the rocks.
On the old map, the treasure was marked exactly at WbN from the harbor.
The pilot set the plane's heading to WbN as instructed by the tower.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: W - due west, the cardinal compass point at 270°
  • Degree Higher: WNW - west-northwest, the next compass point northward (at 292°30')

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'west by north.'
