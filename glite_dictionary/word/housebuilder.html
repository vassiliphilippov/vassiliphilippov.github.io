housebuilder

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌbɪldər / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌbɪldə / (BrE)
a person or company that builds houses for sale or as a business
person/company building houses

Examples

The local housebuilder is launching a new development next spring.
As a successful housebuilder, she oversees dozens of projects each year.
Many housebuilders are adopting eco-friendly construction methods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: builder - a person who constructs buildings
