pipework

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪpˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈpaɪpˌwɜːk / (BrE)
the system of pipes comprising the flues and stops on a pipe organ, responsible for producing different sounds and tones
organ pipes and stops system

Examples

The church organ's pipework needed repair before the concert could happen.
Examining the pipework revealed how each stop modifies the sound produced by the organ.
The new organ boasts intricate pipework designed for a richer musical experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pipes - tubes producing sound in an organ
