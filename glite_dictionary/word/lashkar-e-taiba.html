Lashkar-e-Taiba

1. PROPN

/ ˌlæʃkər eɪ taɪˈiːbə / (AmE), / ˌlæʃkə eɪ taɪˈiːbə / (BrE)
an Islamist militant organization based in Pakistan, active mainly in Kashmir, that carries out attacks against India with the stated aim of restoring Islamic rule to India
Islamist militant group active in Kashmir

Examples

The Indian government has accused Lashkar-e-Taiba of organizing several deadly attacks in Mumbai.
International authorities have sanctioned Lashkar-e-Taiba due to its involvement in acts of terrorism.
Security forces intensified their operations in the region following intelligence reports about Lashkar-e-Taiba activities.

Synset Examples

Lashkar-e-Toiba has committed mass murders of civilian Hindus

Etymology

From Urdu لشکرِ طیبہ (laškar-e ṭayyiba), meaning 'Army of the Pure'.
