sarcolemmic

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɑːr.kəˈlɛm.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɑː.kəˈlɛm.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or associated with the sarcolemma, the thin membrane surrounding a muscle fiber
relating to the sarcolemma
medical

Examples

The researchers examined sarcolemmic changes in patients with muscular dystrophy.
Damage to the sarcolemmic membrane can lead to impaired muscle function.
An influx of calcium ions through sarcolemmic channels was observed during the experiment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sarcolemma - the thin membrane surrounding a striated muscle fiber

Etymology

From 'sarcolemma' (from Greek 'sarx' meaning flesh + 'lemma' meaning sheath) + '-ic'.
