pinchgut

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪntʃˌɡʌt / (AmE), / ˈpɪntʃˌɡʌt / (BrE)
a person who is excessively frugal, especially one who denies themselves and others sufficient food; a miser
excessively frugal or miserly person
archaic

Examples

Everyone in the household grew thin under the rule of a strict pinchgut.
Her uncle was a notorious pinchgut, counting every penny and cooking tiny meals.
The town mocked the old pinchgut but envied his hidden savings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miser - a person who hoards wealth and spends as little as possible

Etymology

From 'pinch' (to squeeze tightly) + 'gut' (stomach or belly), suggesting one whose frugality leads to hunger.
