dalasi

1. NOUN

/ dəˈlɑːsi / (AmE), / dəˈlɑːsi / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Gambia, equal to 100 bututs
currency unit of Gambia

Examples

The shopkeeper gave me change in dalasi after I paid with a large bill.
The exchange rate for the dalasi has remained stable this year.
Tourists are advised to carry some dalasi when visiting small villages in Gambia.

Etymology

Adopted in 1971 as the name for Gambia's currency; possibly from Wolof or Mandinka term for 'five' or 'ten'.
