untoughened

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtʌfənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʌfənd / (BrE)
not made tough; lacking in physical toughness or resilience; not hardened by use or exposure.
not toughened; lacking toughness

Examples

After weeks of rest, his untoughened muscles ached during the first workout.
The hikers suffered from untoughened feet after walking long distances in new boots.
This type of untoughened glass is more likely to shatter upon impact.

Synset Examples

tender feet

Related Terms

  • Antonym: toughened - made tougher or more resilient
  • Degree Higher: tough - strong; not easily damaged

Etymology

Formed by the prefix 'un-' (not) added to 'toughen' + '-ed'.
