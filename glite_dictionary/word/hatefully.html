hatefully

1. ADV

/ ˈheɪtfəli / (AmE), / ˈheɪtfəli / (BrE)
In a manner showing intense dislike or hatred.
in a hateful manner

Examples

She glared hatefully at her rival across the room.
The critics wrote hatefully about his latest film, sparing no harsh words.
He spoke hatefully about his former colleagues, making everyone uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hateful - showing or feeling hatred
  • Antonym: lovingly - in a loving manner
