pushy

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʊʃi / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃi / (BrE)
behaving in a way that is rudely forceful or overly assertive in order to achieve personal goals
aggressively assertive or forceful

Examples

Her pushy attitude sometimes makes her colleagues uncomfortable.
The salesman was so pushy that we left the store without buying anything.
If you are too pushy during the interview, it might hurt your chances.

Synset Examples

a pushful insurance agent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assertive - confidently self-assured; able to express opinions or needs clearly
  • Antonym: meek - quiet, gentle, and easily imposed on; submissive
