weigela

1. NOUN

/ waɪˈdʒiːlə / (AmE), / waɪˈdʒiːlə / (BrE)
a deciduous shrub of the honeysuckle family, widely grown in gardens for its clusters of white, pink, or red flowers
flowering garden shrub

Examples

The weigela in her front yard burst into pink blossoms every spring.
Gardeners often choose weigela for its showy flowers and easy maintenance.
After pruning the weigela, new growth appeared rapidly.

Etymology

Named after German botanist Christian Ehrenfried Weigel (1748–1831).
