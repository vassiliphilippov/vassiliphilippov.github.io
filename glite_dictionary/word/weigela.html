Weigela

1. NOUN

/ waɪˈdʒiːlə / (AmE), / waɪˈdʒiːlə / (BrE)
a genus of deciduous flowering shrubs native to East Asia, cultivated for their showy, tubular flowers
genus of East Asian flowering shrubs

Examples

The garden was filled with blooming Weigela shrubs in shades of pink and red.
Many gardeners plant Weigela for its long-lasting and colorful flowers.
In spring, the Weigela attracts hummingbirds with its bright blossoms.

Etymology

Named after German scientist Christian Ehrenfried Weigel (1748–1831).
