disentangled

1. ADJ

/ ˌdɪsənˈtæŋɡəld / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˈtæŋɡəld / (BrE)
freed from being tangled or complicated; untangled or made clear
untangled; made clear

Examples

After several hours of patient work, the necklace was finally disentangled and ready to wear again.
The disentangled wires made fixing the computer much easier.
Her thoughts became disentangled as she talked through her problems with a friend.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tangled - twisted together in a confused mass; complicated
  • Synonym: untangled - not tangled; free from knots or complications

Etymology

Past participle of 'disentangle', from 'dis-' (expressing reversal) + 'entangle'.
