La Paz

1. PROPN

/ lɑ ˈpɑz / (AmE), / lɑː ˈpaz / (BrE)
the capital city of Bolivia, located in the west of the country, and the administrative seat of Bolivia's government
capital city of Bolivia

Examples

Many tourists visit La Paz for its vibrant markets and unique high-altitude setting.
International conferences are often held in La Paz, attracting visitors from around the globe.
The government buildings in La Paz are situated in the heart of the city.

Etymology

From Spanish 'la paz', meaning 'the peace'.
