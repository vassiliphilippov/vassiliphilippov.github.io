varicelliform

1. ADJ

/ ˌvɛr.ɪˈsɛl.ɪ.fɔrm / (AmE), / ˌvar.ɪˈsel.ɪ.fɔːm / (BrE)
resembling a rash or lesions similar to those of chickenpox
resembling chickenpox rash
medical

Examples

The patient exhibited a varicelliform eruption on her arms and torso.
Doctors noted varicelliform lesions typical of certain viral infections.
His skin developed a varicelliform rash, prompting further investigation.

Etymology

From Latin 'varicella' (chickenpox) + '-form' (having the form of).
