arborical

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːrbərɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈɑːbərɪkəl / (BrE)
of or relating to trees; formed by or resembling trees.
relating to trees

Examples

The garden features several arborical elements designed to provide shady retreats.
That building is noted for its arborical architecture, with tree-shaped columns throughout.
The arborical theme in the paintings brought a calming, natural feel to the gallery.

Synset Examples

an arborous roof

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arborescent - resembling a tree; tree-like in growth or appearance
  • Synonym: arborous - of or relating to or formed by trees

Etymology

From Latin arbor (tree) + English -ical (forming adjectives).
