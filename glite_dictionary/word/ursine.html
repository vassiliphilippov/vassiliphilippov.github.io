ursine

1. ADJ

/ ˈɜːr.saɪn / (AmE), / ˈɜː.saɪn / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling a bear.
bear-like; pertaining to bears

Examples

The zoologist noted the ursine features of the large animal.
Her ursine strength surprised everyone in the competition.
The costume was designed to give the actor an ursine appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bearish - resembling or characteristic of a bear, especially in appearance or behavior

Etymology

From Latin ursinus, from ursus meaning 'bear'.
