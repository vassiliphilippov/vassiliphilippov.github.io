besotted

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈsɑtɪd / (AmE), / bɪˈsɒtɪd / (BrE)
Very drunk; strongly affected by alcohol.
very drunk

Examples

After five glasses of wine, he was completely besotted and could barely stand.
The besotted revelers sang loudly as they staggered through the streets.
She frowned at his besotted expression, realizing he had been drinking all evening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inebriated - drunk; affected by alcohol
  • Antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol; not drunk

Etymology

Past participle of 'besot', from 'be-' + 'sot' (a habitual drunkard).
