unpurified

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpjʊərəˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpjʊərɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not made pure; containing impurities or not having undergone purification.
not made pure; containing impurities

Examples

The laboratory warned that the unpurified water was unsafe for drinking.
Many natural minerals are found in an unpurified state before processing.
Consuming unpurified honey could expose you to harmful bacteria.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pure - free from any contamination or imperfection

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'purified', from Latin 'purificare' (to make pure).
