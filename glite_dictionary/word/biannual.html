biannual

1. ADJ

/ baɪˈænjuəl / (AmE), / baɪˈanjʊəl / (BrE)
happening or produced twice each year
twice a year

Examples

The company holds biannual meetings in April and October.
You should visit your dentist for a biannual check-up to maintain oral health.
The biannual festival attracts visitors from all over the region.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: biennial - occurring every two years

Etymology

From Latin 'bi-' (two) + 'annualis' (yearly).
