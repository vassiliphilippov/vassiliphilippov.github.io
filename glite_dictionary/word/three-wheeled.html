three-wheeled

1. ADJ

/ θriːˈwiːld / (AmE), / θriːˈwiːld / (BrE)
(of a vehicle) having three wheels.
having three wheels (of a vehicle)

Examples

The delivery company uses three-wheeled scooters to navigate the narrow city streets efficiently.
He designed a unique three-wheeled car that is both compact and stable.
Children often learn to ride using a three-wheeled bicycle before switching to a regular bike.

Synset Examples

a three-wheel bike

Related Terms

  • Antonym: four-wheeled - having four wheels (of a vehicle)
  • Antonym: two-wheeled - having two wheels (of a vehicle)
