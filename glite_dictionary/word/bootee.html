bootee

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuːti / (AmE), / ˈbuːti / (BrE)
A soft, woolen shoe or slipper, typically worn by babies to keep their feet warm.
soft slipper for babies

Examples

The newborn wore tiny bootees that her grandmother had knitted.
Warm bootees help keep a baby's feet comfortable throughout the night.
He bought a pair of soft woolen bootees as a shower gift for the baby.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bootie - alternative spelling of bootee, soft slipper for babies

Etymology

Diminutive of 'boot', influenced by French 'bottine' (small boot).
