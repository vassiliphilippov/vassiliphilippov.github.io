arguing

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrɡjuɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡjuːɪŋ / (BrE)
an occasion when people strongly disagree and express their opposing views, often in a heated manner
act of verbal disagreement or dispute

Examples

Her parents' constant arguing made it hard for her to concentrate on her homework.
The neighbors complained about the loud arguing coming from the apartment next door.
There was a lot of arguing during the meeting, but no final decision was made.

Synset Examples

they were involved in a violent argument

Related Terms

  • Synonym: argument - a verbal exchange expressing disagreement or different opinions
  • Synonym: quarrel - an angry disagreement, especially between people who know each other
  • Antonym: agreement - a situation in which people have the same opinion
