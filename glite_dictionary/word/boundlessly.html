boundlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈbaʊn(d)lɪsli / (AmE), / ˈbaʊn(d)ləsli / (BrE)
without limits; in an unlimited or infinite manner
in an unlimited or infinite manner

Examples

He loved his children boundlessly, never holding back his affection.
The ocean stretched boundlessly before them, merging with the sky at the horizon.
Her creativity seemed to flow boundlessly, inspiring everyone around her.

Synset Examples

he is infinitely wealthy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infinitely - without limits; endlessly
  • Synonym: limitlessly - without any limit; in a limitless way

Etymology

From 'boundless' (without bounds) + '-ly' (adverb suffix).
