black-and-blue

1. ADJ

/ ˌblæk ən ˈblu / (AmE), / ˌblæk ən ˈbluː / (BrE)
having the darkened appearance of skin caused by bruising; discolored from a bruise.
discolored by bruising; bruised skin

Examples

She was black-and-blue after falling off her bicycle.
His arm turned black-and-blue where the ball had hit him.
After the accident, his legs were covered in black-and-blue marks.

Synset Examples

beaten black and blue
livid bruises

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bruised - having discoloration from a physical blow; showing signs of bruising
