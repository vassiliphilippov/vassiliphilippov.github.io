supertanker

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuː.pɚˌtæŋ.kɚ / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pəˌtæŋ.kə / (BrE)
A very large ship specially built for transporting oil in bulk, typically the largest class of oil tankers.
very large oil-transporting ship

Examples

The supertanker was carrying millions of barrels of crude oil across the Atlantic Ocean.
Port authorities prepared to receive the massive supertanker after its two-week journey from the Middle East.
Environmentalists protested when the supertanker threatened to spill oil near the coastline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oil tanker - a ship designed to carry oil in bulk

Etymology

Formed from 'super-' (above, very large) + 'tanker'.
