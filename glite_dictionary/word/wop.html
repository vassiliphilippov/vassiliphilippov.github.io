wop

1. NOUN

/ wɑp / (AmE), / wɒp / (BrE)
An offensive, derogatory term for a person of Italian descent.
offensive term for an Italian person
offensive

Examples

He was called a wop by his classmates, which deeply offended him.
The use of the word wop is considered highly insulting and unacceptable today.
She cringed when she heard someone use wop in a conversation about immigrants.

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from Italian dialectal 'guappo' (dandy, swaggerer), or from the acronym 'without papers,' though the latter origin is widely disputed.
