marriageability

1. NOUN

/ ˌmærɪdʒəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌmærɪdʒəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or condition of being suitable or eligible for marriage
suitability or eligibility for marriage

Examples

The family's focus on her marriageability influenced many of her decisions while growing up.
Education can greatly enhance a person's marriageability in some cultures.
His parents constantly evaluated his marriageability based on his career and social status.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eligibility - the state of being qualified or entitled to be chosen

Etymology

From 'marriage' + '-ability', patterned after similar formations like 'suitability'.
