archetypal

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑːrkɪˈtaɪpəl / (AmE), / ˌɑːkɪˈtaɪpəl / (BrE)
very typical of a certain kind of person or thing; representing the most perfect or typical example of something.
typical or original example

Examples

He is considered the archetypal hero of medieval literature.
The company is an archetypal example of successful innovation.
Her archetypal response was calm and measured, setting the tone for everyone else.

Synset Examples

archetypal patterns
she was the prototypal student activist

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prototypical - serving as a typical example of a particular kind

Etymology

From Greek 'archetypon' (original pattern), via Medieval Latin 'archetypus'.
