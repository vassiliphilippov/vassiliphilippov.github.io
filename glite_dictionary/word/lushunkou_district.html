Lushunkou District

1. PROPN

/ luːˈʃuːnˌkoʊ dɪˈstrɪkt / (AmE), / luːˈʃʊŋˌkəʊ dɪˈstrɪkt / (BrE)
a district in northeastern China, situated on the Liaodong Peninsula, known historically as a major port and formerly called Port Arthur; now part of Dalian
district and port area in northeastern China

Examples

The historic city of Lushunkou District was once known as Port Arthur.
Many battles have taken place in the strategic Lushunkou District over the centuries.
The port facilities in Lushunkou District play an important role in the regional economy.
