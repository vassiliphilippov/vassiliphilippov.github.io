well-timed

1. ADJ

/ wɛlˈtaɪmd / (AmE), / wɛlˈtaɪmd / (BrE)
Happening or done at the most suitable or opportune moment.
done at the right time

Examples

Her advice proved to be well-timed just before the project deadline.
A well-timed joke broke the tension in the meeting.
His well-timed arrival saved us from missing the train.

Synset Examples

your letter arrived apropos

Related Terms

  • Synonym: timely - done or occurring at a favorable or useful time
  • Antonym: ill-timed - done at an unsuitable or inappropriate time
