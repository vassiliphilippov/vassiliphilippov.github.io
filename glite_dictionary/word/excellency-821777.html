excellency

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛk.sə.lən.si / (AmE), / ˈɛk.səl.ən.si / (BrE)
an outstanding quality or feature in which someone or something excels
outstanding quality or feature

Examples

One of the excellencies of her performance is her emotional range.
The garden's main excellency is its collection of rare orchids.
Among the many excellencies of the book is its vivid description of historical events.

Synset Examples

a center of manufacturing excellence
the use of herbs is one of the excellencies of French cuisine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excellence - the quality of being outstanding or extremely good

Etymology

From Latin excellentia (excellence, superiority).
