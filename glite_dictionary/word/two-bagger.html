two-bagger

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuː ˌbæɡər / (AmE), / ˈtuː ˌbæɡə / (BrE)
(Baseball) a base hit that allows the batter to reach second base safely; a double.
baseball term for double hit

Examples

The rookie celebrated after hitting a two-bagger in the seventh inning.
She recorded a two-bagger that brought in two runs for her team.
His first major league hit was a two-bagger down the right-field line.

Synset Examples

he hit a double to deep centerfield

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double - a base hit on which the batter reaches second base safely
