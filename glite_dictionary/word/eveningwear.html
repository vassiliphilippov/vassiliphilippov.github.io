eveningwear

1. NOUN

/ ˈivnɪŋˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈiːvnɪŋˌwɛə / (BrE)
clothing worn for formal occasions in the evening, such as dinners or parties
formal clothing for evening events

Examples

She looked stunning in her elegant eveningwear at the charity gala.
Most department stores have a section dedicated to eveningwear for special occasions.
He rented his eveningwear because he rarely attends black-tie events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: formalwear - clothing suitable for formal occasions, often including eveningwear

Etymology

From 'evening' + 'wear', meaning clothing for the evening.
