Sinology

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / sɪˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the academic study of Chinese history, language, literature, and culture
study of Chinese history and culture

Examples

She chose to specialize in Sinology at university because of her fascination with Chinese history.
The conference on Sinology attracted scholars from all over the world.
His extensive research in Sinology has contributed greatly to our understanding of ancient Chinese philosophy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chinese studies - interdisciplinary academic study of China

Etymology

From French *sinologie* or New Latin *Sinologia*: 'Sinae' (Latin for 'China') + 'logy' (study of).
