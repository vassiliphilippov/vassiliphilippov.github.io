southeastwardly

1. ADV

/ ˌsaʊtˈiːstwərdli / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈiːstwədli / (BrE)
In or toward a southeastward direction.
in a southeastward direction

Examples

The birds migrated southeastwardly in search of a warmer climate.
The explorers traveled southeastwardly across the plains, following the river.
Clouds moved southeastwardly ahead of the approaching storm.

Synset Examples

the river flows southeastward to the gulf

Related Terms

  • Synonym: southeasterly - in a southeastward direction
