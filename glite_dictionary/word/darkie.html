darkie

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑrki / (AmE), / ˈdɑːki / (BrE)
An extremely offensive and dated term for a Black person, regarded as a racial slur.
offensive term for a Black person
offensive

Examples

The novel contained the word darkie, reflecting the prejudices of its era.
Using darkie is deeply offensive and should never be tolerated in modern conversation.
She was shocked to hear someone utter the term darkie in public.

Etymology

Diminutive form of 'dark', referencing skin color.
