twenty-third

1. ADJ

/ ˌtwɛntiˈθɜrd / (AmE), / ˌtwɛntiˈθɜːd / (BrE)
Coming next after the twenty-second in position in a sequence; 23rd.
23rd in order or position

Examples

She finished in twenty-third place in the marathon.
My birthday falls on the twenty-third of August every year.
This is the twenty-third time I've been to this museum.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-second - Coming next before the twenty-third in order; 22nd.
  • Degree Higher: twenty-fourth - Coming next after the twenty-third in order; 24th.

Etymology

Formed from 'twenty' and 'third'.
