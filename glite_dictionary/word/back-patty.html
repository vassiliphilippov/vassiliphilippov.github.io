back-patty

1. NOUN

/ bækˈpæti / (AmE), / bakˈpæti / (BrE)
an informal act or gesture of congratulating oneself, typically for an achievement
self-congratulatory gesture or acknowledgment
informal

Examples

After publishing her novel, she allowed herself a little back-patty for her accomplishment.
It's okay to give yourself a back-patty when things go well at work.
He indulged in a private back-patty after receiving the employee of the month award.

Synset Examples

She gave herself a back-patty after finishing the project ahead of schedule.
After the successful presentation, he enjoyed a well-deserved back-patty.
A little back-patty never hurts after a long day at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pat on the back - an act of praising or congratulating someone
