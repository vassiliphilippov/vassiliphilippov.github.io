bioclimatic

1. ADJ

/ ˌbaɪ.oʊ.klaɪˈmæt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əʊ.klaɪˈmat.ɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the interaction between climate and living organisms.
relating to climate and living organisms

Examples

The bioclimatic conditions of a region influence which plant species can thrive there.
Many architects design buildings with bioclimatic principles to improve energy efficiency.
Recent studies focus on how bioclimatic zones are shifting due to global warming.

Etymology

Formed from bio- (life) and climatic (relating to climate).
