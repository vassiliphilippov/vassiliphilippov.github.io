nephthytis

1. NOUN

/ nɛfˈθaɪtɪs / (AmE), / nɛfˈθɪtɪs / (BrE)
A tropical plant of the genus Nephthytis, cultivated for its attractive foliage.
tropical plant genus; ornamental foliage plant

Examples

The nephthytis thrived in the indirect sunlight by the window.
Gardeners often choose nephthytis as an attractive houseplant.
The leaves of nephthytis are arrow-shaped and variegated.

Etymology

From New Latin Nephthytis, from Egyptian goddess Nephthys, due to the resemblance of the plant's parts to the ancient symbol.
