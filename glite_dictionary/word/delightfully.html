delightfully

1. ADV

/ dɪˈlaɪtfəli / (AmE), / dɪˈlaɪtfəli / (BrE)
In a delightful manner; causing or characterized by great pleasure or delight.
in a delightful manner

Examples

The garden was delightfully arranged with flowers of every color.
She found the movie delightfully amusing and laughed throughout.
The air was delightfully cool after the summer rain.

Synset Examples

the farm house, though in itself a small one, is delightfully situated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pleasantly - in a pleasant manner
  • Synonym: happily - in a happy manner
