asthenopia

1. NOUN

/ ˌæsθəˈnoʊpiə / (AmE), / ˌasθɪˈnəʊpɪə / (BrE)
Eye fatigue or discomfort caused by prolonged close work, especially in people with uncorrected vision problems.
eye fatigue due to strain
medical

Examples

After hours of working at the computer, he experienced asthenopia and needed to rest his eyes.
Symptoms like headaches and blurred vision may indicate asthenopia in people who read for long periods.
If you suffer from asthenopia, an eye exam could reveal whether corrective lenses are needed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eye strain - fatigue or pain in the eyes from overuse

Etymology

From Greek asthenēs (weak) + ōps (eye).
