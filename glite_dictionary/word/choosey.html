choosey

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃuːzi / (AmE), / ˈtʃuːzi / (BrE)
very particular about what one chooses; difficult to please due to being highly selective
very particular or difficult to please

Examples

My daughter is very choosey about what she eats for breakfast.
You can't afford to be choosey when there are so few jobs available.
Some customers are so choosey that it's impossible to satisfy them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: choosy - very particular or difficult to please; main spelling
