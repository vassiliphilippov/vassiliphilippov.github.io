Kartik

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːr.tɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.tɪk / (BrE)
the eighth month of the Hindu calendar, typically falling in October or November.
eighth month of the Hindu calendar

Examples

Diwali is celebrated in the month of Kartik according to the Hindu calendar.
Many devotees visit temples during Kartik, considering it an auspicious time for religious rituals.
The festival of Kartik Purnima falls on the full moon day in Kartik.

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Kārtika', the name of the month.
