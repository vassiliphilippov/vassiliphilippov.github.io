Homona

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈmoʊnə / (AmE), / həʊˈməʊnə / (BrE)
A genus of moths in the family Tortricidae, commonly known as tea tortrix, some species of which are significant agricultural pests.
genus of moths (tea tortrix)

Examples

The Homona genus contains several species that are major pests of tea plantations.
Damage caused by Homona larvae can lead to significant crop losses in Asia.
Researchers have developed biological control methods to manage Homona infestations in agriculture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tortrix - a moth of the family Tortricidae

Etymology

Modern Latin; used in zoological taxonomy.
