tape

1. NOUN

/ teɪp / (AmE), / teɪp / (BrE)
a long, thin strip of plastic coated with iron oxide, used for recording sound, video, or data, especially in cassettes or reels
plastic strip for recording or storing sound, video, or data

Examples

He found an old tape of his favorite band from the 1980s.
She labeled each tape so she wouldn't mix up the recordings.
The museum archived historical interviews on magnetic tape for preservation.

Synset Examples

he took along a dozen tapes to record the interview

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cassette - a small, flat case containing magnetic tape for audio or video recording

Examples

Synset Examples
