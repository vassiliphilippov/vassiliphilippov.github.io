Dubliner

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌb.lɪ.nɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʌb.lɪ.nə / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from Dublin, the capital of Ireland
person from Dublin, Ireland

Examples

As a proud Dubliner, she enjoys showing visitors around the city.
The novel tells the story of several Dubliners struggling with daily life.
He moved to London but still identifies himself as a Dubliner at heart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dublin - the capital city of Ireland

Etymology

From Dublin, the capital city of Ireland, with the suffix -er denoting origin or residence.
