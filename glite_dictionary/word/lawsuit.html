lawsuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔˌsuːt / (AmE), / ˈlɔːsuːt / (BrE)
a claim or dispute brought before a court of law for a legal remedy
legal case brought to court

Examples

The company filed a lawsuit against its competitor for copyright infringement.
After months of negotiation failed, she decided to pursue a lawsuit in court.
The lawsuit was settled out of court before reaching trial.

Synset Examples

the family brought suit against the landlord

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suit - a proceeding brought to court to obtain a legal remedy
  • Synonym: action - a legal process brought before a court

Etymology

From 'law' + 'suit', influenced by Anglo-Norman and Middle English legal usage.
