Dusseldorf

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʊsəldɔrf / (AmE), / ˈdʊsəlˌdɔːf / (BrE)
a city in western Germany on the Rhine River, known as an industrial and cultural center
city in western Germany on the Rhine

Examples

She moved to Dusseldorf for a new job opportunity in the fashion industry.
The Rhine River runs through the city of Dusseldorf, giving it a picturesque waterfront.
Many international art fairs are held annually in Dusseldorf.

Etymology

From German Düsseldorf, originally meaning 'village (Dorf) on the Düssel (a small river)'.
