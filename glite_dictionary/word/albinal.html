albinal

1. ADJ

/ ælˈbaɪ.nəl / (AmE), / ælˈbaɪ.nəl / (BrE)
relating to or affected by albinism; having the characteristics of an albino
relating to albinism
medical

Examples

Some animals display an albinal appearance due to a genetic mutation affecting pigmentation.
The study focused on the albinal traits seen in certain plant species.
Rare albinal children require special eye care because of their sensitivity to light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: albinism - a condition characterized by absence of pigment in the skin, hair, and eyes
  • Synonym: albino - a person or animal having albinism

Etymology

From 'albino' + English adjectival suffix '-al', influenced by Latin 'albus' (white).
