Forficula

1. NOUN

/ fɔrˈfɪkjʊlə / (AmE), / fɔːˈfɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
the type genus of the family Forficulidae, which includes common earwigs.
genus of earwigs

Examples

The common European earwig belongs to the genus Forficula.
Many species of Forficula are found in gardens and damp places.
Scientists have studied the mating behavior of Forficula for decades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: earwig - insect with pincer-like appendages of the order Dermaptera

Etymology

From Latin 'forficula' meaning 'little scissors,' referring to the pincer-like cerci of earwigs.

2. NOUN

/ fɔrˈfɪkjələ / (AmE), / fɔːˈfɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
A genus of earwigs in the family Forficulidae, which includes common earwig species.
type genus of earwigs (Forficulidae)

Examples

The common earwig, Forficula auricularia, is often found in gardens across Europe and North America.
Scientists have studied the social behavior of Forficula species extensively.
Several members of the genus Forficula exhibit unique maternal care among insects.

Etymology

From Latin 'forficula', meaning 'little scissors', referring to the pincers of earwigs.
