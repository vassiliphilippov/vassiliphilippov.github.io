bended

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛn.dɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɛn.dɪd / (BrE)
(chiefly in set phrases) bent, curved, or stooped, especially in reference to the back or knees.
bent or stooped, especially of the body
archaic

Examples

He approached the altar on bended knee, solemn and respectful.
The old man walked slowly, his bended back a sign of many years.
In the story, the knight begged for mercy on bended knees.

Synset Examples

on bended knee
with bent (or bended) back

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bent - curved, not straight; past participle of 'bend'

Etymology

Past participle and archaic form of 'bend.'
