earthtongue

1. NOUN

/ ˈɜrθˌtʌŋ / (AmE), / ˈɜːθˌtʌŋ / (BrE)
A club-shaped fungus of the genus Geoglossum, typically found in soil and known for its black or dark club-like fruiting body.
club-shaped fungus (genus Geoglossum)

Examples

The dark earthtongue emerged from the mossy ground after the rain.
Several earthtongues were spotted in the ancient woodland by amateur mycologists.
Photographers are often drawn to the distinctive appearance of the earthtongue.
