jamboree

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒæm.bəˈri / (AmE), / ˌdʒæm.bəˈriː / (BrE)
a large celebration or party, especially a lively or cheerful gathering with entertainment
large festive gathering or celebration

Examples

The city hosts an annual jamboree to celebrate the arrival of spring.
Hundreds gathered for the music jamboree that lasted all weekend.
The scouts organized a jamboree filled with games, songs, and laughter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: festival - a day or period of celebration, typically religious or cultural
  • Synonym: gala - a social occasion with special entertainments or performances
  • Synonym: celebration - the action of marking one's pleasure at an important event or occasion

Etymology

Origin uncertain; first used in late 19th century, popularized by Scouting movement.
