boyishly

1. ADV

/ ˈbɔɪɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪɪʃli / (BrE)
In a way that is characteristic of or resembles a boy, especially in appearance, manner, or behavior.
in a boy-like manner

Examples

He grinned boyishly when he received the award.
She ruffled her hair boyishly and gave a confident smile.
Despite his age, he still laughed boyishly at silly jokes.

Synset Examples

he smiled boyishly at his fiancee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boyish - having qualities typically associated with a boy
