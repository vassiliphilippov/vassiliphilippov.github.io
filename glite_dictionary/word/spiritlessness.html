spiritlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪrɪtləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtləsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of lacking enthusiasm, energy, or interest
lack of enthusiasm or energy

Examples

Her spiritlessness was evident during the team meeting, as she barely participated in the discussion.
The movie suffered from a certain spiritlessness that left audiences unengaged.
After weeks of bad weather, a mood of spiritlessness settled over the whole town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apathy - lack of interest or concern
  • Antonym: enthusiasm - intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval

Etymology

From 'spirit' + '-less' + '-ness'.
