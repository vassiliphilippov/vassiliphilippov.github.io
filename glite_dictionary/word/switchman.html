switchman

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪtʃˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈswɪtʃmən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to operate railroad switches, directing trains to different tracks
person who operates railroad switches

Examples

The switchman signaled the engineer to slow down before changing the track.
After many years as a switchman, he became an expert on train movements.
A diligent switchman helped prevent an accident at the railway yard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: signalman - person who operates railway signals or points

Etymology

From 'switch' (railroad track device) + 'man'.
