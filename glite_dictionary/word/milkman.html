milkman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlkˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkmən / (BrE)
a man whose job is to deliver milk to people's homes
man who delivers milk

Examples

The milkman delivers fresh bottles to our doorstep every morning.
When I was a child, the milkman was a familiar figure in our neighborhood.
Many people now buy milk at supermarkets instead of waiting for the milkman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milkwoman - a woman who delivers milk

Etymology

From 'milk' + 'man'; used since the 19th century for men delivering milk.
