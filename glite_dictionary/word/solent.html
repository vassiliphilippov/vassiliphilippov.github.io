Solent

1. PROPN

/ ˈsoʊlənt / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlənt / (BrE)
a strait of the English Channel separating Hampshire from the Isle of Wight in southern England
strait between Hampshire and Isle of Wight

Examples

Many yachts gather in the Solent each summer for the famous regatta.
The ferry crossed the Solent as the sun began to set over the water.
Strong tides in the Solent can make sailing challenging for beginners.
