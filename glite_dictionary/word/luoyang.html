Luoyang

1. PROPN

/ lwɑːˈjɑːŋ / (AmE), / lɔːˈjɑːŋ / (BrE)
a city in east-central China, historically significant as the capital of several ancient Chinese dynasties
historical city in central China

Examples

The ancient city of Luoyang attracts many tourists due to its historical significance.
During the Han Dynasty, Luoyang served as the imperial capital.
Our travel itinerary includes a visit to Luoyang and its famous Longmen Grottoes.
