beauteousness

1. NOUN

/ ˈbjuː.t̬i.əs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈbjuː.ti.əs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being beautiful or attractive
the quality of being beautiful

Examples

The beauteousness of the garden left visitors speechless.
She admired the beauteousness of the mountain scenery from her window.
The poet wrote verses about the beauteousness of his beloved.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty - the quality of being pleasing to the senses or mind

Etymology

From 'beauteous' (from beauty + -ous) + -ness.
