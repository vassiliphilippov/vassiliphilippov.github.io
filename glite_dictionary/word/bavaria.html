Bavaria

1. PROPN

/ bəˈvɛriə / (AmE), / bəˈvɛːriə / (BrE)
a federal state in southeastern Germany, known for its cultural traditions, beer, and automobile industry
region in southeastern Germany

Examples

Every autumn, people from around the world visit Bavaria to experience Oktoberfest.
Bavaria boasts beautiful alpine scenery and lively folk traditions.
Munich, the capital of Bavaria, is internationally known for its breweries and car manufacturing.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'Bavaria', from the name of the Bavarii, a Germanic tribe.
