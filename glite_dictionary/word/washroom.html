washroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑːʃˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃˌruːm / (BrE)
A room equipped with washing facilities and toilets, especially one in a public building.
public lavatory; restroom

Examples

Could you tell me where the nearest washroom is located?
The restaurant's washroom was clean and well-stocked with supplies.
Please wash your hands in the washroom before returning to work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restroom - a public room equipped with toilets and washing facilities
  • Synonym: bathroom - a room containing a toilet, especially in a home or hotel
  • Synonym: toilet - a room or fixture for urination and defecation
