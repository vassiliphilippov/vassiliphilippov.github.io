slant-eye

1. NOUN

/ ˈslænt ˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈslɑːnt ˌaɪ / (BrE)
(offensive, slang) A highly offensive term used to refer to an Asian person, especially North Vietnamese soldiers during the Vietnam War.
offensive slang for an Asian person
slang offensive

Examples

The soldier used the word slant-eye, showing his prejudice during the war.
That movie was criticized for its repeated use of the term slant-eye.
It is never acceptable to refer to anyone as a slant-eye.
