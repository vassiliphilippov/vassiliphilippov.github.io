Wilde

1. PROPN

/ waɪld / (AmE), / waɪld / (BrE)
(Oscar) Wilde (1854–1900), Irish playwright, poet, and wit renowned for his sharp humor and works such as 'The Importance of Being Earnest'.
Irish writer and wit (1854–1900)
american british
