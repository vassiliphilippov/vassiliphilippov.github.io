estrous

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛstɹuːs / (AmE), / ˈiːstrəʊs / (BrE)
(of female mammals, especially lower mammals) showing or in a state of estrus; in heat.
in a state of sexual receptivity (in animals)
medical

Examples

The veterinarian confirmed that the cat was estrous and exhibiting mating behaviors.
Many species have a predictable estrous cycle that determines their fertility periods.
During the estrous state, female animals are receptive to mating.

Synset Examples

the estrous state
the estrous cycle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: estrus - period of sexual receptivity in female mammals

Etymology

From estrus + -ous, first used in scientific literature in the early 20th century.
