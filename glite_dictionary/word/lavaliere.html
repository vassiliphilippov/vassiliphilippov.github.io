lavaliere

1. NOUN

/ ˌlævəˈlɪr / (AmE), / ˌlævəˈlɪə / (BrE)
A jeweled pendant, often ornate, worn on a chain around the neck.
jeweled pendant on a chain

Examples

She wore a delicate diamond lavaliere that sparkled under the lights.
The antique lavaliere passed down from her grandmother was her most cherished possession.
At the gala, many guests admired the elegant gold lavaliere around his neck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pendant - an ornament that hangs from a chain worn around the neck

Etymology

From French 'lavallière', originally named after Louise de La Vallière, a mistress of Louis XIV, who popularized the style.
