Lavrentiy Beria

1. PROPN

/ lɑːvˈrɛn.ti bəˈri.ə / (AmE), / lʌvˈrɛnti bəˈriːə / (BrE)
(1899–1953) Soviet chief of secret police under Joseph Stalin, known for overseeing the security apparatus and involved in political purges; executed after Stalin's death.
Soviet chief of secret police under Stalin

Examples

Lavrentiy Beria played a significant role in the political purges during Stalin's regime.
After Stalin's death, Lavrentiy Beria was arrested and executed by his rivals.
Historians still debate the extent of Lavrentiy Beria's influence within the Soviet government.
