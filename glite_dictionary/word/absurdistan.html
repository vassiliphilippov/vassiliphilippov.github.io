Absurdistan

1. PROPN

/ əbˈsɝd.ɪ.stæn / (AmE), / əbˈsɜː.dɪ.stæn / (BrE)
a fictional or imaginary place characterized by absurd, illogical, or nonsensical bureaucracy, systems, or rules
fictional place of ridiculous bureaucracy

Examples

Living in this city sometimes feels like you've moved to Absurdistan.
The new tax regulations have turned our company into a branch office of Absurdistan.
Critics claim that the policy changes have pushed the country closer to becoming Absurdistan.

Synset Examples

The rules seem to come from a place called Absurdistan.

Etymology

Formed from 'absurd' + the suffix '-stan' (from Persian for 'land'), as a satirical name for an absurd country.
