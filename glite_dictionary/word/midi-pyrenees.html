Midi-Pyrenees

1. PROPN

/ ˌmiːdi piːraɪˈniːz / (AmE), / ˌmiːdi piːrəˈneɪ / (BrE)
a region in southwestern France, formerly an administrative area before being merged into Occitanie in 2016
region in southwestern France

Examples

The Midi-Pyrenees is famed for its picturesque countryside and medieval towns.
During our trip to France, we explored the food markets of Midi-Pyrenees.
Many artists are inspired by the landscapes of Midi-Pyrenees.

Etymology

French: from 'Midi' (south, midday) and 'Pyrénées' (Pyrenees mountains)
