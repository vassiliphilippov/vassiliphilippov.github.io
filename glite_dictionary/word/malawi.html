Malawi

1. PROPN

/ məˈlɑːwi / (AmE), / məˈlɑːwi / (BrE)
A landlocked country in southeastern Africa, formerly known as Nyasaland, which gained independence from the United Kingdom in 1964.
country in southeastern Africa

Examples

Malawi is renowned for the beauty of Lake Malawi and its national parks.
She spent two years volunteering in Malawi as a healthcare worker.
The economy of Malawi relies heavily on agriculture, particularly tobacco and tea.

Etymology

From the name of the Maravi people, historically inhabiting the region.
