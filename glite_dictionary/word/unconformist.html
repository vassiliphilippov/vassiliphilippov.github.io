unconformist

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkənˈfɔrmɪst / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈfɔːmɪst / (BrE)
not conforming to established customs, attitudes, or norms; behaving or thinking differently from what is generally accepted
not conforming to social norms

Examples

He has always been an unconformist in his approach to art and creativity.
The company's unconformist policies attracted both admiration and criticism.
Her unconformist outlook often set her apart from her peers.

Synset Examples

their rabidly nonconformist deportment has made them legendary
the old stubborn nonconformist spirit of the early settlers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonconformist - not conforming to established customs or norms

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'conformist' (one who conforms); modeled on 'nonconformist'.
