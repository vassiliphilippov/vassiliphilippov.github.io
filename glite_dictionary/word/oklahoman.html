Oklahoman

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊkləˈhoʊmən / (AmE), / ˌəʊkləˈhəʊmən / (BrE)
a native or resident of the US state of Oklahoma
person from Oklahoma

Examples

She met an Oklahoman while attending college in New York.
The Oklahoman pride in their state is evident at local festivals.
Many Oklahomans have deep roots in farming and ranching.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Texan - a native or resident of Texas
  • Synonym: Californian - a native or resident of California

Etymology

Formed from Oklahoma + -an (denoting association).
