Slo-Bid

1. NOUN

/ ˈsloʊ bɪd / (AmE), / ˈsləʊ bɪd / (BrE)
A trademarked preparation of theophylline used as a bronchodilator to treat symptoms of asthma and other respiratory conditions.
trademarked theophylline bronchodilator
medical

Examples

After starting Slo-Bid, his breathing improved significantly.
The doctor prescribed Slo-Bid to help manage her asthma symptoms.
Some patients experience mild side effects when taking Slo-Bid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theophylline - a colorless crystalline alkaloid used as a bronchodilator
