moraceous

1. ADJ

/ mɔˈreɪʃəs / (AmE), / mɒˈreɪʃəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of plants of the family Moraceae.
of the plant family Moraceae

Examples

Several moraceous trees provide shade and fruit in the town square.
The figs are among the most diverse and widespread moraceous plants.
Her research focuses on moraceous species native to tropical regions.

Etymology

From modern Latin Moraceae (the mulberry family) + -ous.
