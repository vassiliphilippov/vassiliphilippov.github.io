John Milton

1. PROPN

/ dʒɑn ˈmɪltən / (AmE), / dʒɒn ˈmɪltən / (BrE)
(1608–1674) English poet, best known as the author of the epic poem Paradise Lost, chronicling humanity's fall from grace.
English poet, author of Paradise Lost

Examples

John Milton is widely regarded as one of the greatest English poets of the seventeenth century.
His major work, Paradise Lost, established John Milton's reputation for grand, complex epic poetry.
Many students must read John Milton's writings in literature classes around the world.
