splenic

1. ADJ

/ ˈsplɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˈspliːnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the spleen.
relating to the spleen
medical

Examples

The splenic artery supplies blood to the spleen and part of the stomach.
He was diagnosed with a splenic rupture after the accident.
Doctors sometimes perform a splenic biopsy to diagnose certain diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spleen - an organ involved in the production and removal of blood cells

Etymology

From Latin *splenicus*, from Greek *splēnikos*, from *splēn* (spleen).
