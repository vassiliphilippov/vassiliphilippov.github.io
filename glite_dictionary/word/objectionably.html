objectionably

1. ADV

/ əbˈdʒɛk.ʃə.nə.bli / (AmE), / əbˈdʒɛk.ʃə.nəb.li / (BrE)
In a way that is unpleasant, offensive, or likely to cause disapproval.
in an offensive or unpleasant way

Examples

He behaved objectionably at the dinner party by insulting several guests.
The advertisement was objectionably sexist and quickly pulled from television.
She spoke so objectionably that the audience began to boo.

Synset Examples

he said so in one of his more offensively intellectually arrogant sentences

Related Terms

  • Synonym: offensively - in an unpleasant or insulting manner
