kobo

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊboʊ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊbəʊ / (BrE)
a monetary unit of Nigeria, equal to one hundredth of a naira
Nigerian currency unit (1/100 of naira)

Examples

I only had a few kobos left in my pocket after shopping.
The price of bread has risen by fifty kobos this year.
He collected several kobos as change from the market vendor.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: naira - the basic monetary unit of Nigeria
