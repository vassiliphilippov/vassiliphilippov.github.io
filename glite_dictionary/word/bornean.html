Bornean

1. NOUN

/ bɔrˈniːən / (AmE), / bɔːˈniːən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Borneo
native or inhabitant of Borneo

Examples

Many Borneans take pride in the island's rich cultural heritage.
The researcher spent several months living among the Bornean villagers.
A group of Borneans presented traditional dances at the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Borneo - the third-largest island in the world, located in Southeast Asia

Etymology

From Borneo, the name of the island in Southeast Asia, plus the suffix -an.
