Montreal

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɑntriˈɔl / (AmE), / ˌmɒntriˈɒl / (BrE)
A city in southern Quebec, Canada, on the Saint Lawrence River; the largest city in Quebec and the second largest in Canada; noted for its French-speaking population and cultural heritage.
largest city in Quebec, Canada

Examples

Many tourists visit Montreal to experience its vibrant arts scene and festivals.
He moved to Montreal for university and quickly fell in love with the city.
The winters in Montreal can be very cold, but the city remains lively throughout the year.

Etymology

From French 'Montréal', from 'Mont Royal' meaning 'Royal Mountain', the name of the hill in the city.
