to dropforge

1. VERB

/ ˈdrɑp.fɔrdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdrɒp.fɔːdʒ / (BrE)
to shape metal by hammering it in a drop forge
to shape metal in a drop forge

Examples

The factory workers carefully dropforge each steel component for extra strength.
Special tools are needed to dropforge parts used in the automotive industry.
They dropforged the axle to ensure it could withstand high pressure.

Synset Examples

drop-force the metal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forge - to shape metal by heating and hammering

Etymology

From 'drop' and 'forge', referring to the drop hammer technique in forging.
