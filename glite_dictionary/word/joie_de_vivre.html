joie de vivre

1. NOUN

/ ʒwɑ də ˈviːvrə / (AmE), / ʒwa də ˈviːvʁə / (BrE)
a feeling of exuberant enjoyment and enthusiasm for life
keen enjoyment of life

Examples

Her infectious joie de vivre made everyone around her feel more alive.
He approaches every day with remarkable joie de vivre, no matter the circumstances.
Traveling to new places always fills her with a renewed sense of joie de vivre.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zest - eager enjoyment and enthusiasm

Etymology

From French, literally ‘joy of living’.
