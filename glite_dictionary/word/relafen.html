Relafen

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛl.ə.fɛn / (AmE), / ˈrɛl.ə.fɛn / (BrE)
Trademark for a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (nabumetone) used to treat pain and inflammation.
anti-inflammatory drug brand name
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Relafen to help manage my arthritis pain.
After taking Relafen for a week, he noticed a significant reduction in swelling.
Patients with sensitivity to other NSAIDs should use Relafen with caution.
