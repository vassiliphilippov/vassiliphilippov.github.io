slantwise

1. ADV

/ ˈslæntˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈslɑːntˌwaɪz / (BrE)
at a slant; in a direction that is neither straight up and down nor straight across
at a slant; diagonally

Examples

She placed the painting slantwise on the shelf to make the arrangement more interesting.
The rain was blowing slantwise through the open window during the storm.
He crossed the street slantwise, taking the shortest route to the café.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diagonally - in a slanted or oblique direction

Etymology

Formed from 'slant' + '-wise' (after the pattern of 'sidewise'), meaning 'in the manner of a slant'.
