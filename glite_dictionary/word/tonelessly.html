tonelessly

1. ADV

/ ˈtoʊn.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈtəʊn.ləs.li / (BrE)
in a monotone; without expression or variation in tone
without variation in tone

Examples

She replied tonelessly, her face betraying no emotion.
He read the list of names tonelessly from the paper.
The announcement was made tonelessly, as if it meant nothing to her.

Synset Examples

‘Come in,’ she said tonelessly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monotonously - in a monotonous way, without variation

Etymology

From 'toneless' + adverb suffix '-ly'.
