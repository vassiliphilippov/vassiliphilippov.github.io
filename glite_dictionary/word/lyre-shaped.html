lyre-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪər ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪə ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape of a lyre; curved outward at the ends and narrow in the middle.
shaped like a lyre

Examples

The ancient vase featured a lyre-shaped handle that elegantly arched over the rim.
A skilled blacksmith crafted a lyre-shaped ornament for the iron gate.
Many Victorian chairs have beautifully carved lyre-shaped details on their backs.
