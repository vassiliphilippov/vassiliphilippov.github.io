well-nourished

1. ADJ

/ wɛlˈnɝːɪʃt / (AmE), / wɛlˈnʌrɪʃt / (BrE)
Receiving or provided with enough food or nutrients to maintain good health and growth.
having adequate nutrition

Examples

The children appeared well-nourished and healthy during their check-up.
A well-nourished body can better resist infections and recover from illness.
Her main concern was ensuring her baby remained well-nourished throughout infancy.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: malnourished - lacking proper nutrition
