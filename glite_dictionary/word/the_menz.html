the menz

1. NOUN

/ ðə mɛnz / (AmE), / ðə mɛnz / (BrE)
An informal and humorous term for a group of men, used especially among friends.
informal term for a group of men
informal slang

Examples

She enjoys hanging out with the menz every Friday night.
A photo of the menz from last summer's barbecue made everyone laugh.
Whenever the menz get together, something hilarious always happens.

Synset Examples

She joined the menz for a weekend camping trip.
