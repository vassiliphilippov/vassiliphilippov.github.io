wienerwurst

1. NOUN

/ ˈwiːnərˌwʊrst / (AmE), / ˈviːnəˌwʊəst / (BrE)
A smooth-textured sausage made of minced beef or pork, usually smoked and often served in a bread roll.
type of smoked sausage

Examples

At the picnic, Carl grilled a large wienerwurst and served it with spicy mustard.
Many people enjoy a wienerwurst in a soft bun with onions and relish.
The deli offered homemade wienerwurst as a specialty for their Saturday customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frankfurter - a similar type of smoked sausage
  • Synonym: Vienna sausage - a type of small cooked and smoked sausage

Etymology

German, from Wiener (of Vienna) + Wurst (sausage).
