astereognosis

1. NOUN

/ ˌæstɛriɑgˈnoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌastɛrɪɒgˈnəʊsɪs / (BrE)
the inability to recognize objects by touch, despite normal sensation in the hand
loss of object recognition by touch
medical

Examples

The patient's astereognosis became apparent when she could not identify familiar objects placed in her hand.
Due to astereognosis, he was unable to distinguish a key from a coin by touch alone.
Neurological tests confirmed the presence of astereognosis in her right hand.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stereognosis - the ability to recognize objects by touch

Etymology

From Greek a- (without) + stereos (solid) + gnosis (knowledge).
