Inger

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡə / (BrE)
a member of a western Finnish people who formerly lived in the Baltic province where Saint Petersburg was built
member of a former Baltic Finnish people

Examples

The Inger community had its own unique traditions before the region became part of Russia.
Historians have studied the migration patterns of the Inger people around the Baltic Sea.
Many descendants of the Inger now live in Finland and Estonia.
