ill-treated

1. ADJ

/ ɪlˈtritɪd / (AmE), / ɪlˈtriːtɪd / (BrE)
having been subjected to cruel or harsh treatment; badly treated.
treated cruelly or harshly

Examples

The shelter took in several ill-treated animals rescued from neglect.
He spoke with compassion about his ill-treated childhood friends.
Reports revealed that the workers had been ill-treated by their employer.

Synset Examples

an abused wife

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abused - subjected to cruel or violent treatment
  • Synonym: mistreated - treated badly or unfairly
  • Antonym: well-treated - having been treated well
