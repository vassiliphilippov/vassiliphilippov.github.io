fostering

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑstɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɒstərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of encouraging or promoting the development of something
encouragement or support for development

Examples

Teachers play a key role in fostering creativity in their classrooms.
The organization is dedicated to fostering innovation in the technology sector.
Government policies can make a significant difference in fostering economic growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: encouragement - the act of giving support, confidence, or hope
  • Antonym: hindrance - the act of preventing or impeding progress

Etymology

From the verb 'foster', from Old English *fostrian* (to supply with food, nourish).
