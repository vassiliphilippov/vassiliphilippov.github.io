articulatio cubiti

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˌtɪkjəˈleɪʃioʊ ˈkjuːbɪtaɪ / (AmE), / ɑːˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃɪəʊ ˈkjuːbɪtaɪ / (BrE)
the hinge joint at the elbow between the forearm and upper arm (or the analogous joint in the forelimb of a quadruped)
the elbow joint (anatomical)
medical

Examples

An injury to the articulatio cubiti can limit a person's ability to flex their arm.
The veterinarian examined the horse's articulatio cubiti for signs of swelling or pain.
The textbook included a detailed diagram of the articulatio cubiti and its surrounding ligaments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elbow joint - the joint connecting the upper arm and forearm

Etymology

From Latin: 'articulatio' (joint) + 'cubiti' (of the elbow).
