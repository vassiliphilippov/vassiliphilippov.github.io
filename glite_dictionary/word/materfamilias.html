materfamilias

1. NOUN

/ ˌmeɪtərfəˈmɪliəs / (AmE), / ˌmeɪtəfəˈmɪlɪæs / (BrE)
the female head of a family or household
female head of a household

Examples

After her husband's death, she became the materfamilias of the clan, overseeing all important decisions.
The materfamilias presided over family gatherings with dignity and wisdom.
In many traditional societies, the materfamilias holds a position of great respect and authority.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: matriarch - a woman who is the head of a family or tribe
  • Antonym: paterfamilias - the male head of a family or household

Etymology

From Latin mater (mother) + familias (of the household).
