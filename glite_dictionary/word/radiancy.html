radiancy

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪ.di.ən.si / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.di.ən.si / (BrE)
the quality of being bright and emitting rays of light; radiance
state of being bright and radiant

Examples

The radiancy of the rising sun filled the entire valley with golden light.
She admired the radiancy of his smile, which seemed to brighten the entire room.
The diamond's radiancy was captivating under the museum lights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiance - the quality of being radiant or bright

Etymology

From Latin 'radiantia', from 'radius' (ray).
