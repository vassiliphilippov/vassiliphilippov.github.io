William Blake

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪljəm bleɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɪljəm bleɪk / (BrE)
(1757–1827) A visionary British poet, painter, and printmaker known for his influential poetry and imaginative artwork.
British poet and artist (1757–1827)
british

Examples

William Blake is celebrated for both his poetry and his unique engravings.
Many critics regard William Blake as one of the leading figures of the Romantic movement.
The works of William Blake continue to inspire poets and artists around the world.
