mulligan

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌlɪɡən / (AmE), / ˈmʌlɪɡən / (BrE)
A stew, typically made with meat and vegetables, regarded as the Irish version of burgoo.
Irish meat and vegetable stew

Examples

The chef served a hearty bowl of mulligan on the cold winter night.
After a long day, the group gathered for a meal of mulligan around the campfire.
She learned how to make mulligan from her grandmother's traditional recipe.
