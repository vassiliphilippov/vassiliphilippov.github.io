Belau

1. PROPN

/ bəˈlaʊ / (AmE), / bəˈlaʊ / (BrE)
an island chain of more than 200 islands in the western central Pacific Ocean, also known as Palau
island group in the western Pacific Ocean

Examples

The tropical beauty of Belau attracts divers from all over the world.
Researchers traveled to Belau to study its unique marine ecosystems.
The official language of Belau is Palauan, along with English.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Palau - another name for Belau, an island country in the western Pacific

Etymology

Native name for Palau; 'Belau' is the endonym in Palauan.
