piroxicam

1. NOUN

/ paɪˈrɒksɪˌkæm / (AmE), / pɪˈrɒksɪkæm / (BrE)
A nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used to treat arthritis and other inflammatory conditions.
anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed piroxicam to help manage his arthritis pain.
Some patients experience stomach upset when taking piroxicam for inflammation.
She discussed the possible side effects of piroxicam with her pharmacist.
