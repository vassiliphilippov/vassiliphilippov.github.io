grey-blue

1. ADJ

/ ɡreɪ blu / (AmE), / ɡreɪ bluː / (BrE)
Having a shade of blue tinged with grey.
bluish-grey color

Examples

The sky had a grey-blue hue just before the storm.
She wore a stunning dress in a soft grey-blue shade.
His eyes were a unique grey-blue that changed with the light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blue-grey - Of grey tinged with blue; a closely related color term
