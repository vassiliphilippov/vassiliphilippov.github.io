125th

1. ADJ

/ wʌn hʌndrəd ˈtwɛnti fɪfθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ənd ˈtwɛnti fɪfθ / (BrE)
being the ordinal number equal to one hundred twenty-five in a sequence; coming after the 124th and before the 126th.
ordinal number for 125th place

Examples

She finished the race in 125th place, just behind her teammate.
The company is celebrating its 125th anniversary this year with a special event.
Our school is on the 125th street, between Broadway and Amsterdam.
