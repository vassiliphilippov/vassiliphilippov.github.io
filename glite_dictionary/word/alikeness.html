alikeness

1. NOUN

/ əˈlaɪk.nɪs / (AmE), / əˈlaɪk.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being similar in appearance, character, or nature
similarity; resemblance

Many cultures share a surprising alikeness in their myths and legends.
