Victor Vasarely

1. PROPN

/ ˈvɪktər vəˈsɑːrəli / (AmE), / ˈvɪktə vəˈsɑːrəli / (BrE)
A French painter of Hungarian origin (1908–1997) who was a pioneer of op art, known for his geometric abstract works.
French op art painter (1908–1997)

Examples

Victor Vasarely is considered the father of op art due to his innovative use of geometric forms.
The museum featured a retrospective of Victor Vasarely's most famous optical illusions.
Art students often study Victor Vasarely to learn about visual perception and abstraction.
