impatiently

1. ADV

/ ɪmˈpeɪʃəntli / (AmE), / ɪmˈpeɪʃəntli / (BrE)
in a way that shows you are annoyed or eager for something to happen and unwilling to wait calmly
in an impatient manner

Examples

She tapped her foot impatiently while waiting for the train to arrive.
He glanced at his watch impatiently during the long meeting.
The audience waited impatiently for the concert to begin.

Synset Examples

he answered her impatiently

Related Terms

  • Antonym: patiently - in a patient manner; with calm endurance or without complaint

Etymology

From 'impatient' + '-ly', with 'impatient' from Latin 'impatientem' (not able to bear).
