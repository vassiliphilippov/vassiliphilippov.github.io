worriedly

1. ADV

/ ˈwɜːrɪdli / (AmE), / ˈwʌrɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows worry or anxiety.
in a worried manner

Examples

She glanced worriedly at the clock as the minutes ticked by.
He spoke worriedly about the results of the upcoming exam.
The parents waited worriedly for news from the hospital.

Synset Examples

‘I wonder what to do,’ she said worriedly
he paused worriedly before calling the bank

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anxiously - in a worried or uneasy manner

Etymology

From 'worried' + '-ly' adverb suffix.
