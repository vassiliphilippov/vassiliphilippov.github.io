unassertively

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.əˈsɜr.tɪv.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈsɜː.tɪv.li / (BrE)
in a manner that lacks confidence or assertiveness; showing little tendency to assert one's opinions or desires.
in a non-assertive manner

Examples

He spoke unassertively, never raising his voice above a whisper in the meeting.
She unassertively agreed to take on extra work, even though she was already busy.
The proposal was unassertively presented, which made it easy for others to dismiss.

Synset Examples

unassertively, she always follows her husband's suggestions

Related Terms

  • Antonym: assertively - in an assertive manner, showing confidence
