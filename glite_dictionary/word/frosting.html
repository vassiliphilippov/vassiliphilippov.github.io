frosting

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɔstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfrɒstɪŋ / (BrE)
A sweet, flavored mixture, usually made from sugar and other ingredients, used to cover or decorate cakes and other baked goods.
sweet topping for cakes

Examples

She spread a thick layer of frosting over the chocolate cake.
The birthday cupcakes were topped with pink frosting and sprinkles.
He licked the frosting off the spoon after baking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: icing - sweet, creamy coating for cakes
