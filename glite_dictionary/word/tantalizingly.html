tantalizingly

1. ADV

/ ˈtæn.təl.aɪ.zɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈtæn.tə.laɪ.zɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that provokes or arouses interest, desire, or excitement, especially by being attractive or difficult to obtain.
in a manner that entices or tempts

Examples

The aroma of fresh bread drifted tantalizingly from the kitchen, making everyone hungry.
She dangled the keys tantalizingly out of his reach, laughing as he tried to grab them.
The dessert was displayed tantalizingly in the shop window, tempting passersby.

Synset Examples

she smiled at him tantalizingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: temptingly - in a way that tempts or allures

Etymology

From 'tantalizing' + adverbial suffix '-ly'; 'tantalize' from Tantalus, a figure in Greek mythology.
