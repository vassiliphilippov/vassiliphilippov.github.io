to syllabicate

1. VERB

/ sɪˈlæbɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / sɪˈlæbɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to divide a word into its syllables
divide into syllables

Examples

The teacher asked the students to syllabicate long words on the board.
Some language learning exercises require you to syllabicate each term.
Knowing how to syllabicate can help improve spelling skills.

Synset Examples

syllabify the words

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to syllabify - divide into syllables

Etymology

From Medieval Latin syllabicatus, from Latin syllaba (syllable).
