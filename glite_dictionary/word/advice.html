advice

1. NOUN

/ ədˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ədˈvaɪs / (BrE)
an opinion or suggestion offered to someone about what they should do in a particular situation
suggestion on what to do

Examples

She gave me some very helpful advice about choosing a university.
It is always wise to seek advice before making big decisions.
Ignoring your doctor's advice could put your health at risk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recommendation - a suggestion or proposal about what someone should do; closely related to advice
  • Synonym: counsel - advice, especially that given formally
  • Antonym: command - an authoritative order, often contrasted with advice

Etymology

From Old French 'avis' (opinion), from Latin 'ad' (to) + 'visum' (seen, idea).
