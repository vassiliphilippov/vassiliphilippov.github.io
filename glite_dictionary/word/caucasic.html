Caucasic

1. ADJ

/ kɔːˈkæsɪk / (AmE), / kɔːˈkæsɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the geographical region of Caucasia or its peoples, languages, or cultures.
of or relating to the Caucasus region

Examples

The Caucasic languages display unique grammatical features not found elsewhere.
She specializes in Caucasic history and culture.
Many Caucasic traditions have influenced art across Eurasia.

Synset Examples

Caucasian languages

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Caucasian - relating to the Caucasus region, its peoples, or languages

Etymology

From Caucasus, the name of the mountain region between the Black and Caspian Seas, with the suffix -ic forming an adjective.
