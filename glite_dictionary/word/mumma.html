mumma

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌmə / (AmE), / ˈmʌmə / (BrE)
(informal) Mother; a term used by children or in affectionate address to one's mother.
informal term for mother
informal

Examples

I called out for my mumma when I woke up from my nap.
My mumma makes the best chocolate cake in the world.
Can you ask your mumma if you can come over to play?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mum - informal term for mother, chiefly British
  • Synonym: mama - informal term for mother
  • Synonym: mom - American English term for mother
