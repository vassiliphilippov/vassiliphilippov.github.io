Yale

1. PROPN

/ jeɪl / (AmE), / jeɪl / (BrE)
a prestigious university located in New Haven, Connecticut, USA, known for its academic excellence
prestigious university in Connecticut, USA

Examples

She was accepted to Yale after years of hard work and dedication.
Many influential leaders have graduated from Yale University.
The annual Yale–Harvard football game draws a large crowd every year.

Etymology

Named after Elihu Yale, an early benefactor of the university.
