aerially

1. ADV

/ ˈɛr.i.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈɛː.ri.əli / (BrE)
By means of aircraft; from or through the air.
by means of aircraft; through the air

Examples

The rescue team dropped supplies aerially to the stranded hikers.
The pesticide was applied aerially to cover the entire plantation quickly.
Some wildlife surveys are conducted aerially to gather data over large areas.

Synset Examples

the survey was carried out aerially

Etymology

From 'aerial' + '-ly', based on Latin 'aerius' (airy, from the air).
