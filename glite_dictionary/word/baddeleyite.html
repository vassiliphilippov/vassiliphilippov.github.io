baddeleyite

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæd.əˌli.aɪt / (AmE), / ˈbad.ɪˌli.aɪt / (BrE)
A mineral consisting of natural zirconium oxide, occurring as a rare crystal.
a mineral; natural zirconium oxide

Examples

The gemstone is primarily composed of baddeleyite, giving it unique physical properties.
Researchers discovered a significant deposit of baddeleyite in the region.
Baddeleyite is an important source of zirconium for industrial applications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zirconia - synthetic or natural zirconium dioxide
  • Degree Lower: zircon - a mineral consisting of zirconium silicate

Etymology

Named after Joseph Baddeley, a British railway engineer, who discovered it in 1892.
