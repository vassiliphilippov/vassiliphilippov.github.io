unspeakably

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈspiːkəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈspiːkəbli / (BrE)
to a degree that cannot be expressed in words; indescribably.
to an inexpressible degree

Examples

The loss of his childhood pet made him unspeakably sad for weeks.
The view from the mountain summit was unspeakably beautiful at sunrise.
She was unspeakably grateful to everyone who helped her during the crisis.

Synset Examples

she was looking very young tonight, and, as usual, indescribably beautiful, in a simple strapless dress of a green and white silky cotton

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indescribably - to a degree that cannot be adequately described

Etymology

From 'unspeakable' + '-ly'.
