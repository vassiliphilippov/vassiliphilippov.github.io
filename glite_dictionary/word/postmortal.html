postmortal

1. ADJ

/ ˌpoʊstˈmɔrtəl / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈmɔːtəl / (BrE)

occurring after death

Examples

Changes in body temperature and appearance are typical postmortal phenomena.

Etymology
