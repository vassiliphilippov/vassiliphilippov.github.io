shinpad

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪnˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈʃɪnˌpad / (BrE)
a stiff protective covering worn on the lower leg to protect the shins during sports such as hockey, football, or baseball
stiff leg covering for sports protection

Examples

He forgot to wear his shinpad and got a painful bruise during the match.
Most players make sure their shinpads are secure before going onto the field.
The referee checked that all players were wearing the correct shinpad equipment before the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shin guard - a protective covering for the shin, another term for shinpad

Etymology

From 'shin' (front part of the lower leg) + 'pad' (protective cushion).
