indifferent

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈdɪf.ər.ənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪf.ərənt / (BrE)
having no particular interest or concern; showing a lack of care or feeling
showing no interest or concern

Examples

He remained indifferent to the criticism directed at him.
Many people are indifferent to politics and rarely vote.
She gave an indifferent shrug when asked about the result.

Synset Examples

indifferent to the sufferings of others
indifferent to her plea

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apathetic - showing or feeling no interest or concern
  • Antonym: caring - showing concern or interest

Etymology

From Latin *indifferens* (not making a difference) via Old French.
