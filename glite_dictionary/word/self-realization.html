self-realization

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlfˌriələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˌrɪəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the achievement of one's full potential or the fulfillment of one's capacities and abilities
fulfillment of one's potential

Examples

After years of dedication and growth, she finally achieved self-realization through her art.
Many philosophers argue that self-realization is the highest goal of human life.
Meditation and reflection are often recommended as paths toward self-realization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-actualization - the realization or fulfillment of one's talents and potentialities

Etymology

From 'self' + 'realization', based on realize (from Latin 'realis', real) + -ation.
