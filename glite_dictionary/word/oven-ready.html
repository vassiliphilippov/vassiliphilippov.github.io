oven-ready

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌvən ˌrɛdi / (AmE), / ˈʌvən ˌrɛdi / (BrE)
(of food) prepared and sold so that it can be cooked immediately without further preparation.
prepared food ready for immediate cooking

Examples

The supermarket sells oven-ready chicken to save customers time in the kitchen.
She bought an oven-ready meal for dinner after a long day at work.
Most oven-ready products are packaged to go straight from the fridge to the oven.
