Pastor roseus

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæstər roʊˈziː.əs / (AmE), / ˈpæstə rəʊˈziː.əs / (BrE)
a glossy black bird with a pink back and abdomen, native chiefly to Asia; also called the rosy starling
Asian bird with pink back and abdomen

Examples

The Pastor roseus migrates through central Asia in large flocks each summer.
In India, the arrival of the Pastor roseus coincides with the monsoon season.
Birdwatchers were excited to spot a Pastor roseus among the local starling species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rosy starling - the common name for Pastor roseus, a pink and black bird native to Asia

Etymology

From Latin 'pastor' (shepherd) and 'roseus' (rosy, pink).
