areolate

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛəriəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛːrɪələt / (BrE)
having a surface divided into small spaces or areas, especially resembling or relating to areolae.
divided into small areas or spaces

Examples

Under the microscope, the areolate structure of the tissue became apparent.
The leaves of this cactus have an areolate pattern that helps conserve water.
In histology, certain glands are described as areolate due to their divided sections.

Synset Examples

areolar tissue

Related Terms

  • Synonym: areolar - relating to or resembling an areola

Etymology

From Latin 'areola' (small open space), diminutive of 'area'.
