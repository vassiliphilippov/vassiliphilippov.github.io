Bufo boreas

1. NOUN

/ ˌbjuːfoʊ ˈbɔːriəs / (AmE), / ˌbjuːfəʊ ˈbɔːriəs / (BrE)
a species of toad native to western North America, commonly known as the Western Toad, found from southern Alaska to Baja California west of the Rockies
a species of toad found in western North America

Examples

The Bufo boreas can be found in a range of habitats, from forests to grasslands.
Scientists are monitoring populations of Bufo boreas to assess environmental changes.
Unlike many amphibians, Bufo boreas adapts well to both wetland and upland environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Western toad - common English name for Bufo boreas

Etymology

From Latin; 'Bufo' meaning 'toad' and 'boreas' referring to the north.
