scrubbird

1. NOUN

/ ˈskrʌbˌbɜrd / (AmE), / ˈskrʌbˌbɜːd / (BrE)
a small, fast-running Australian bird that resembles a wren and is typically found in brush or scrub habitats
small Australian bird resembling a wren

Examples

A rare scrubbird was spotted darting through the undergrowth in the national park.
The scrubbird is known for its secretive nature and preference for dense vegetation.
Researchers studied the habitat requirements of the endangered western scrubbird.
