solemnly

1. ADV

/ ˈsɑːləmli / (AmE), / ˈsɒləmli / (BrE)
in a serious and dignified manner; with deep sincerity or gravity
in a serious, dignified manner

Examples

He solemnly promised to tell the truth in court.
The students listened solemnly as their teacher announced the sad news.
She solemnly swore never to reveal the secret.

Synset Examples

the judge sat there solemnly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seriously - in a serious manner

Etymology

From solemn + -ly; 'solemn' from Latin 'sollemnis' (customary, ceremonial).
