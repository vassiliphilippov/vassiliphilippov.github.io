earlobe

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪrˌloʊb / (AmE), / ˈɪəˌləʊb / (BrE)
the soft, rounded, and fleshy lower part of the external ear
the soft lower part of the ear

Examples

She touched her earlobe absentmindedly while listening to the music.
Many people choose to have their earlobe pierced for earrings.
A small scar was visible on his left earlobe.
