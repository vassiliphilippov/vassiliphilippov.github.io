meclizine

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛklɪˌziːn / (AmE), / ˈmiːklɪziːn / (BrE)
an antihistamine drug used to treat or prevent motion sickness and vertigo
an antihistamine for motion sickness
medical

Examples

After taking meclizine, her nausea from the long boat ride subsided.
Doctors often recommend meclizine to reduce symptoms of vertigo.
Be sure to read the label before using meclizine for motion sickness.
