unentitled

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪnˈtaɪtld / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈtaɪtld / (BrE)
Not having any right or claim; lacking entitlement.
not having any right or claim

Examples

She was unentitled to receive the benefits after missing the application deadline.
The defendant was found unentitled to the property in dispute.
Many citizens felt unentitled to special treatment despite their contributions.

Synset Examples

a distinction to which he was unentitled

Related Terms

  • Antonym: entitled - having a right or claim to something

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' and 'entitled'.
