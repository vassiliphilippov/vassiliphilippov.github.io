managership

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæn.ɪ.dʒɚ.ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈmæn.ɪ.dʒə.ʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or status of being a manager
the position of a manager

Examples

He was offered the managership of the sales department after years of hard work.
Her promotion to managership marked a turning point in her career.
The company praised his managership for improving team productivity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manager - a person responsible for controlling or administering an organization or group of staff

Etymology

From 'manager' + '-ship', denoting state or position.
