Ateles geoffroyi

1. NOUN

/ əˈtɛliz dʒiˈɒfrɔɪ.i / (AmE), / əˈtiːliːz dʒiˈɒfrɔɪ.i / (BrE)
A species of arboreal monkey found in Central America, known for its long slender limbs and prehensile tail.
species of Central American spider monkey

Examples

Researchers observed the Ateles geoffroyi moving quickly through the upper canopy of the rainforest.
Conservation efforts are being made to protect the habitats of the endangered Ateles geoffroyi.
The long, prehensile tail of the Ateles geoffroyi helps it navigate the treetops with ease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spider monkey - any monkey of the genus Ateles with long limbs and tail

Etymology

Scientific name from Greek 'atelēs' (incomplete, imperfect) and the species name after Étienne Geoffroy Saint-Hilaire, French naturalist.
