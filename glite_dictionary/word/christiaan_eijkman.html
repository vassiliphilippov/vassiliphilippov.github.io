Christiaan Eijkman

1. PROPN

/ krɪˈstiː.ɑn ˈaɪk.mɑn / (AmE), / krɪˈstiː.ɑn ˈaɪk.mən / (BrE)
(1858–1930) A Dutch physician known for discovering that beriberi is caused by a nutritional deficiency.
Dutch physician; discovered nutritional cause of beriberi

Examples

The Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine was awarded to Christiaan Eijkman for his important work on beriberi.
Christiaan Eijkman's discovery changed the understanding of nutritional diseases in the early 20th century.
Medical students often study the contributions of Christiaan Eijkman to public health and nutrition.
