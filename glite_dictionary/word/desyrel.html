Desyrel

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛzəˌrɛl / (AmE), / ˈdɛzərəɫ / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for the oral antidepressant drug trazodone, commonly used as a sedative and to treat depression.
brand name for trazodone (antidepressant)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Desyrel to help him manage his depression and insomnia.
Some patients experience drowsiness as a side effect of Desyrel.
After switching to Desyrel, she noticed an improvement in her sleep quality.
