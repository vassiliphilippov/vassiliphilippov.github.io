Orono

1. PROPN

/ ˈɔːrəˌnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɔːrəˌnəʊ / (BrE)
A town in east-central Maine, United States, situated on the Penobscot River, known for being the home of the University of Maine.
university town in Maine, USA

Examples

The University of Maine is located in Orono, making it a vibrant college town.
During our road trip through New England, we stopped in Orono for lunch.
Many students choose to live in Orono because of its proximity to campus.

Etymology

Named after Chief Orono, a Penobscot Indian leader.
