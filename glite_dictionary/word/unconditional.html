unconditional

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkənˈdɪʃənl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈdɪʃənl / (BrE)
not limited by or dependent on any conditions; absolute.
without conditions or limits

Examples

She showed unconditional love for her children, expecting nothing in return.
The charity offers unconditional support to those in need, regardless of their background.
He accepted the offer with unconditional trust, believing completely in their word.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: conditional - subject to or dependent on conditions

Etymology

From un- (not) + conditional (from Latin condicio, conditionis ‘agreement, condition’).
