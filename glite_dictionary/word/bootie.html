bootie

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuːti / (AmE), / ˈbuːti / (BrE)
A soft, often woolen slipper or sock for a baby, covering the foot and sometimes part of the leg.
soft slipper or sock for babies

Examples

The newborn was wearing tiny pink booties knitted by her grandmother.
These handmade booties will keep your baby's feet warm all winter.
She carefully placed the pair of blue booties in the baby's hospital bag.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: boot - a type of footwear covering the foot and ankle, sometimes higher
  • Synonym: slipper - a light, comfortable shoe for indoor use

Etymology

Diminutive form of 'boot', modeled after the French 'bottine' (small boot).
