Chin

1. NOUN

/ tʃɪn / (AmE), / tʃɪn / (BrE)
a group of Kamarupan languages spoken in western Myanmar (Burma), Bangladesh, and easternmost India
Kamarupan languages of western Myanmar, Bangladesh, and India

Examples

The Chin languages share many similarities with other Kuki-Chin tongues in South Asia.
Several communities in Mizoram, India, speak different Chin dialects.
Linguists have studied the complex structure of the Chin language family.

Etymology

From Burmese 'Chin', referring to the Chin people of the region.
