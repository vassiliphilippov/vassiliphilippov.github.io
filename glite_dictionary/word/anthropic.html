anthropic

1. ADJ

/ ænˈθrɑpɪk / (AmE), / ænˈθrɒpɪk / (BrE)
Relating to human beings or the period of humanity's existence.
relating to humans or humanity's era

Examples

The anthropic principle suggests that the universe's laws are fine-tuned for the existence of life.
Some scientists question whether anthropic explanations are sufficient to answer cosmological mysteries.
The study focused on anthropic impacts on the environment during the recent geological epoch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: human - relating to or characteristic of people

Etymology

From Greek anthrōpikos, from anthrōpos (human being).
