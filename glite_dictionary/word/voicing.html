voicing

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɔɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of adjusting an organ pipe or wind instrument to achieve the desired tone, pitch, and timbre
adjustment of organ pipes or wind instruments

Examples

The voicing of each organ pipe ensures that the instrument produces a harmonious sound.
During the repair, the technician focused on the voicing of the flute to improve its clarity.
Proper voicing is essential for maintaining the desired character of a church organ.
