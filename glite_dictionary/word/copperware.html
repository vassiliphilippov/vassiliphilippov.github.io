copperware

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑpərˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈkɒpəˌweə / (BrE)
items or utensils made wholly or partly of copper, especially kitchenware or decorative objects
utensils or items made of copper

Examples

The antique store has a fine collection of copperware on display.
She inherited several pieces of copperware from her grandmother's kitchen.
Regular polishing keeps the copperware bright and free from tarnish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brassware - utensils or items made of brass

Etymology

From 'copper' (the metal) + 'ware' (articles of the same material).
