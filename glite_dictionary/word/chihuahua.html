1. PROPN

/ tʃɪˈwɑːwə / (AmE), / tʃɪˈwɑːwə / (BrE)
a city in northern Mexico, serving as the capital of the state of Chihuahua and an important commercial center
city in northern Mexico

Examples

He moved to Chihuahua for work last summer.
Chihuahua is known for its rich history and colonial architecture.
Many companies have set up offices in Chihuahua due to its economic growth.

2. NOUN

/ tʃəˈwɑːwə / (AmE), / tʃɪˈwɑːwə / (BrE)
A very small dog breed with a rounded skull, large eyes, and usually smooth, short hair, originating from Mexico.
very small dog breed from Mexico

Examples

She adopted a Chihuahua from the animal shelter last year.
The Chihuahua shivered in the cold despite wearing a tiny sweater.
Many people admire the loyalty and bravery of their Chihuahua.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toy dog - a very small dog of a breed developed to be a pet

Etymology

Named after the Mexican state of Chihuahua, where the breed was discovered.
