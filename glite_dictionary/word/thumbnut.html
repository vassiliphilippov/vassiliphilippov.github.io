thumbnut

1. NOUN

/ ˈθʌmˌnʌt / (AmE), / ˈθʌm.nʌt / (BrE)
a nut with winglike projections, designed to be easily turned by thumb and forefinger without tools
a nut with thumb-turning wings

Examples

The lamp was secured by a single thumbnut at the base.
You can easily remove the panel by loosening the thumbnuts with your fingers.
He tightened the thumbnut to hold the bracket in place.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wing nut - a nut with two large metal wings for finger turning

Etymology

Formed from 'thumb' + 'nut', indicating a nut turned with the thumb.
