relinquished

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈlɪŋkwɪʃt / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪŋkwɪʃt / (BrE)
(of something) that has been voluntarily given up, withdrawn, or surrendered.
voluntarily given up or surrendered

Examples

The company sold off its relinquished assets as part of the restructuring process.
After the king abdicated, the relinquished throne remained unoccupied for months.
The charity distributed food and supplies from relinquished properties to the homeless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surrendered - given up or yielded
  • Antonym: retained - kept or continued to hold

Etymology

From Latin 'relinquere' (to leave behind).
