Cretan

1. NOUN

/ ˈkriːtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈkriːtn̩ / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Crete, an island in Greece.
native or inhabitant of Crete

Examples

The ancient Cretan civilization is known for the legendary palace of Knossos.
Many Cretans still celebrate traditional festivals unique to the island.
She met a friendly Cretan while traveling through the villages of Crete.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Greek - a native or inhabitant of Greece

Etymology

From Latin Cretanus, from Ancient Greek Krētē (Crete).
