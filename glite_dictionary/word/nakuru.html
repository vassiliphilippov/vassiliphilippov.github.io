Nakuru

1. PROPN

/ nəˈkuːruː / (AmE), / nəˈkʊəruː / (BrE)
A city in western Kenya, serving as a commercial center for the surrounding agricultural region.
city in western Kenya

Examples

Nakuru has become an important hub for trade and tourism in Kenya.
The market in Nakuru is famous for its variety of fresh produce.
We visited Lake Nakuru National Park, which is just outside Nakuru.
