remaining

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈmeɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈmeɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
still left after others have been used, removed, or dealt with; not used up or finished.
not used up; still left

Examples

We divided the remaining cake among the children.
The remaining chapters will be covered in the next class.
After paying the bills, she used the remaining money to buy groceries.

Synset Examples

leftover meatloaf
she had a little money left over so she went to a movie
some odd dollars left
saved the remaining sandwiches for supper
unexpended provisions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leftover - something that remains after the rest has been used
  • Antonym: spent - used up or exhausted

Etymology

Present participle of the verb 'remain', from Old French 'remaindre', from Latin 'remansare' (to stay behind).
