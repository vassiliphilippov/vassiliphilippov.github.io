undersize

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌndərˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌsaɪz / (BrE)
smaller than the usual, standard, or required size for its type or kind
smaller than normal size

Examples

The undersize puppy needed special care and attention.
That suitcase is undersize and fits perfectly under the plane seat.
The company was fined for selling undersize crabs to restaurants.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: oversize - larger than the usual or required size
  • Synonym: small - of a size that is less than normal or usual
