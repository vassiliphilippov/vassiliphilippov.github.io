awestricken

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔːˌstrɪkən / (AmE), / ˈɔːˌstrɪkən / (BrE)
filled with or showing a feeling of awe; experiencing a mixture of reverence, respect, wonder, and fear.
filled with awe or reverence
archaic

Examples

The audience was awestricken by the breathtaking performance on stage.
Standing at the edge of the Grand Canyon, she felt completely awestricken by its vastness.
We listened in awestricken silence as the ancient storyteller began his tale.

Synset Examples

stood in awed silence before the shrine
in grim despair and awestruck wonder

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awestruck - filled with awe; deeply impressed or moved
  • Antonym: unimpressed - not affected or moved by something; lacking awe or admiration

Etymology

Formed from 'awe' + 'stricken', following the pattern of similar adjectives like 'heartsick'.
