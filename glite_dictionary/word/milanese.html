Milanese

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɪləˈniz / (AmE), / ˌmɪləˈniːz / (BrE)
of or relating to Milan or its people
of Milan or its people

Examples

The Milanese cuisine is famous for its rich flavors and traditional ingredients.
Many Milanese designers lead the world in fashion trends.
He admired the Milanese architecture during his visit to northern Italy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Milan - a city in northern Italy

Etymology

From Italian 'Milanese', meaning of Milan.

2. NOUN

/ ˌmɪləˈniːz / (AmE), / ˌmɪləˈniːz / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Milan, a city in northern Italy.
person from Milan, Italy

Examples

She met a Milanese artist at the exhibition.
Many Milanese are proud of their city's fashion heritage.
As a Milanese, he knows all the best restaurants in the city.

Etymology

From Italian 'milanese', meaning 'of Milan'.
