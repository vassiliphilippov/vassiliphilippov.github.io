domineeringness

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɑː.məˈnɪr.ɪŋ.nəs / (AmE), / ˌdɒ.mɪˈnɪə.rɪŋ.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being overbearing and assertively controlling over others.
quality of being overbearing or controlling

Examples

His domineeringness made it difficult for others to share their opinions openly.
The manager's domineeringness created a tense and uncomfortable work environment.
Despite her domineeringness, she cared deeply for her team's success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: domineering - overbearing, asserting control over others
  • Antonym: submissiveness - quality of being willing to submit or yield to another

Etymology

From 'domineering,' itself from Latin 'dominari' (to rule), with the noun-forming suffix '-ness.'
