Aquarius

1. NOUN

/ əˈkwɛriəs / (AmE), / əˈkwɛːrɪəs / (BrE)
A person born when the sun is in the astrological sign Aquarius, typically between January 20 and February 18.
person born under the Aquarius zodiac sign

Examples

As an Aquarius, she's known for her originality and independence.
Many Aquarius individuals value intelligence and humanitarianism.
My best friend, an Aquarius, always surprises me with inventive ideas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pisces - person born under the Pisces zodiac sign
  • Synonym: Capricorn - person born under the Capricorn zodiac sign

Etymology

From Latin 'Aquarius' meaning 'water-carrier'.

3. NOUN

/ əˈkwɛriəs / (AmE), / əˈkwɛːrɪəs / (BrE)
A large constellation in the Southern Hemisphere, situated between Capricornus and Pisces, and traditionally associated with the zodiac sign of the water-bearer.
constellation between Capricornus and Pisces

Examples

The constellation Aquarius is best viewed from the Southern Hemisphere during late summer.
Astronomers often refer to Aquarius when mapping the stars near Capricornus and Pisces.
Many myths and legends are associated with Aquarius, also known as the water-bearer.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Pisces - constellation of the zodiac, next to Aquarius
  • Degree Lower: Capricornus - constellation of the zodiac, next to Aquarius

Etymology

From Latin 'Aquarius,' literally 'water carrier.'
