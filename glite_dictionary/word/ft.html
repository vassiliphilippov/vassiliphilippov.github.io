ft

1. NOUN

/ fɪt / (AmE), / fɪt / (BrE)
Abbreviation for 'foot', a unit of length equal to 12 inches or one third of a yard.
abbreviation for 'foot' (length unit)

Examples

The table measures 6 ft long and 3 ft wide.
She ran the 100-meter dash in under 13 seconds at just over 5 ft tall.
The mountain peak rises more than 10,000 ft above sea level.

Synset Examples

he is six feet tall

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foot - a unit of length equal to 12 inches

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'foot', from Old English 'fōt'.
