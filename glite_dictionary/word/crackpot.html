crackpot

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrækˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈkrækˌpɒt / (BrE)
a person who is considered eccentric, unconventional, or somewhat crazy, especially in their ideas or behavior
eccentric or crazy person

Examples

Everyone thought Uncle George was a crackpot with his wild inventions and strange stories.
The internet is full of crackpot theories about aliens and conspiracies.
Although his neighbors called him a crackpot, he didn't care and happily pursued his hobbies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - a person of unconventional and slightly strange views or behavior
  • Synonym: lunatic - a person who is mentally ill, extremely foolish, or crazy
  • Antonym: sane - mentally healthy and reasonable

Etymology

From 'crack' (crazy, foolish) + 'pot', popularized in early 20th-century American English.
