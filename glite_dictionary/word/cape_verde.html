Cape Verde

1. PROPN

/ keɪp ˈvɜrd / (AmE), / keɪp ˈvɜːd / (BrE)
an island country in the Atlantic Ocean off the coast of West Africa, officially known as the Republic of Cabo Verde.
island country off West Africa

Examples

Many tourists travel to Cape Verde for its beautiful beaches and warm climate.
The official language of Cape Verde is Portuguese.
In 1975, Cape Verde gained independence from Portugal.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Cabo Verde' meaning 'Green Cape'.
