undiplomatic

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌdɪpləˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪpləˈmætɪk / (BrE)
not having or showing skill in dealing with people or sensitive situations; lacking tact or diplomacy.
lacking tact or diplomacy

Examples

His comments were considered undiplomatic and offended several colleagues.
It would be undiplomatic to raise such a delicate issue at the meeting.
She admitted that her undiplomatic approach had made negotiations more difficult.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tactless - showing a lack of sensitivity in dealing with others
  • Antonym: diplomatic - skilled in dealing with sensitive matters or people

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'diplomatic'.
