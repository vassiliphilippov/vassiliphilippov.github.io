Fort Myers

1. PROPN

/ fɔrt ˈmaɪɚz / (AmE), / fɔːt ˈmaɪəz / (BrE)
A city in southwest Florida, United States, known for its tourism and proximity to the Gulf of Mexico.
city in southwest Florida, US

Examples

Fort Myers is a popular destination for tourists seeking sunshine and beautiful beaches.
The hurricane caused significant damage to homes and businesses in Fort Myers.
She moved to Fort Myers to enjoy the warm climate and coastal lifestyle.

Etymology

Named after Colonel Abraham C. Myers, a U.S. Army officer.
