unexpendable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnɪkˈspɛndəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnɪkˈspɛndəbəl / (BrE)
Not able or suitable to be expended; indispensable or necessary.
not able to be expended; indispensable

Examples

The most unexpendable members of the team were kept on the project.
Access to clean water is considered unexpendable in any disaster relief effort.
Trusted allies are unexpendable in a highly competitive industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indispensable - absolutely necessary, essential
  • Antonym: expendable - able to be used up or sacrificed

Etymology

Formed from the prefix un- (not) + expendable, meaning not expendable.
