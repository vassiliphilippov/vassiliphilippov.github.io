partaker

1. NOUN

/ pɑrˈteɪkər / (AmE), / pɑːˈteɪkə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in or shares something with others, especially an activity, experience, or benefit.
person who takes part in something

Examples

Every partaker in the ceremony was given a special token to commemorate the event.
She was an eager partaker of the team's success after months of hard work.
As a regular partaker in community service, he found great fulfillment in helping others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: participant - a person who takes part in something
  • Antonym: observer - someone who watches but does not take part

Etymology

From 'partake' + '-er'; 'partake' from Middle English 'part-taken', influenced by 'take'.
