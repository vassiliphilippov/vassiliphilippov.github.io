60

1. NUM

/ ˈsɪksti / (AmE), / ˈsɪksti / (BrE)
the number that is equal to six times ten; the number following 59 and preceding 61.
number; six times ten

Examples

Only 60 people attended the seminar despite the large venue.
She celebrated her father's 60th birthday with a big party.
The stadium can hold up to 60 thousand spectators during major events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sixty - the word form of the number 60
  • Degree Lower: 59 - the number preceding 60
  • Degree Higher: 61 - the number following 60
