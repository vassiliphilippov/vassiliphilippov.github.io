nem con

1. ADV

/ nɛm ˈkɑn / (AmE), / nɛm ˈkɒn / (BrE)
(of a decision or vote) without any dissent; with no one voting against.
without dissent; unanimously

Examples

The resolution was passed nem con by the committee.
The board agreed nem con to implement the new policy.
After a short discussion, the proposal was approved nem con.

Synset Examples

the Senate unanimously approved the bill
we voted unanimously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unanimously - with everyone in agreement; without opposition

Etymology

Shortening of Latin "nemine contradicente" (no one contradicting).
