gentlemanlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɛntəlˌmænˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛntl̩mənˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Having manners, appearance, or behavior considered appropriate to a gentleman; refined and courteous.
befitting a gentleman; refined, courteous

Examples

His gentlemanlike conduct impressed everyone at the dinner party.
The coach emphasized gentlemanlike sportsmanship on and off the field.
She admired his gentlemanlike manners and respectful attitude.

Synset Examples

gentlemanly behavior

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gentlemanly - showing the qualities or behavior of a gentleman

Etymology

Formed from "gentleman" + "-like" (having the characteristics of).
