Azactam

1. NOUN

/ ˈæz.æk.tæm / (AmE), / ˈæz.æk.tam / (BrE)
A brand name for the antibiotic aztreonam, used to treat severe infections with minimal side effects.
brand name antibiotic (aztreonam)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Azactam to treat the patient's severe bacterial infection.
Many hospitals use Azactam for patients who are allergic to penicillins.
Azactam is typically administered by injection in cases of serious illness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aztreonam - the generic name for the antibiotic marketed as Azactam
  • Synonym: antibiotic - a medicine that inhibits the growth of or destroys microorganisms
