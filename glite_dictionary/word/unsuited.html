unsuited

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsuːtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsuːtɪd / (BrE)
not able to be easily combined or matched in a harmonious or effective way
not easy to combine harmoniously

Examples

The bright red curtains were unsuited to the calm, neutral colors of the living room.
She realized that their talents were unsuited to working together on the project.
The ingredients in this recipe are unsuited, resulting in a strangely flavored dish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incompatible - unable to exist or work together without conflict
  • Antonym: suited - well matched or appropriate for a particular purpose or combination
