1. NOUN

/ ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
(slang) Marijuana, especially as referred to in street or subcultural contexts.
slang term for marijuana
slang

Examples

The jazz musicians would often smoke gage before playing a set.
He offered me some gage behind the club after the show.
In 1920s slang, gage was a popular term for marijuana.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marijuana - Psychoactive drug from the cannabis plant

Etymology

Early 20th century American slang, possibly from French 'herbe gage', or after a brand of cigars sometimes used to roll marijuana cigarettes.

2. NOUN

/ ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
a measuring instrument used to determine and display a quantity, such as thickness, amount of rain, or pressure
measuring instrument (variant of gauge)

Examples

The technician used a gage to check the thickness of the metal sheet.
A rain gage was installed in the backyard to measure daily rainfall amounts.
Before painting, he measured the humidity with a special gage designed for that purpose.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gauge - a measuring instrument for determining the amount or contents of something

Etymology

Alternative spelling of 'gauge', from Old French *jauge*, meaning 'standard measure'.
