kabob

1. NOUN

/ kəˈbɑb / (AmE), / kəˈbɒb / (BrE)
A dish consisting of pieces of meat, often marinated, and sometimes vegetables, cooked on a skewer.
skewered meat dish

Examples

We ordered chicken kabob with rice at the Mediterranean restaurant.
The chef grilled the kabob until the vegetables were perfectly charred.
Beef kabobs are popular at summer barbecues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kebab - dish of grilled meat and vegetables on a skewer

Etymology

From Arabic *kebab* via Turkish, meaning 'roast meat'.
