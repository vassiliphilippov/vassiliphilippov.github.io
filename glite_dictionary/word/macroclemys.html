Macroclemys

1. NOUN

/ ˌmækrəˈklɛmɪs / (AmE), / ˌmakrəˈklɛmɪs / (BrE)
A genus of very large freshwater turtles, including the alligator snapping turtle, found in North America.
genus of large freshwater turtles

Examples

The genus Macroclemys is best known for the formidable alligator snapping turtle.
Several researchers have studied the habitat preferences of Macroclemys species.
Specimens of Macroclemys can be found in many natural history museums.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alligator snapping turtle - a large freshwater turtle native to the southeastern United States

Etymology

From Greek makros (large) + klemmys (turtle).
