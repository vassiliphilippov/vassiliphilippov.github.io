zeta

1. NOUN

/ ˈzeɪtə / (AmE), / ˈziːtə / (BrE)
the sixth letter of the Greek alphabet (Ζ, ζ), used in mathematics and science, or in references to Greek alphabet letters
sixth letter of the Greek alphabet

Examples

The mathematician referenced zeta in his lecture on the Greek alphabet.
In physics, the symbol zeta is often used to represent certain variables or constants.
The fraternity is named after the letter zeta.

Etymology

From Greek zēta (ζῆτα), the name of the sixth letter of the Greek alphabet.
