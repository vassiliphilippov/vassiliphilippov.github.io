dinnertime

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnərˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈdɪnətaɪm / (BrE)
The usual time in the evening when people eat dinner.
the usual evening mealtime

Examples

We always try to be home by dinnertime so we can eat together as a family.
Please wash your hands before dinnertime starts.
At dinnertime, the house fills with the smell of delicious food.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunchtime - the usual time at which lunch is eaten
  • Synonym: breakfast time - the usual time at which breakfast is eaten

Etymology

Formed from 'dinner' + 'time', meaning the time for dinner.
