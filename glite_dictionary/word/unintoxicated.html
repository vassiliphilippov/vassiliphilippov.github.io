unintoxicated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪnˈtɑk.sɪ.keɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈtɒk.sɪ.keɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
Not affected by alcohol or drugs; not intoxicated or inebriated.
not intoxicated; sober

Examples

He insisted that he was unintoxicated and able to drive safely.
The bartender appreciated her unintoxicated customers who always paid attention to their limits.
Medical staff confirmed the patient was unintoxicated upon arrival at the hospital.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sober - not affected by alcohol or drugs
  • Antonym: intoxicated - affected by alcohol or drugs

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'intoxicated'.
