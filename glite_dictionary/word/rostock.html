Rostock

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɑːs.tɑk / (AmE), / ˈrɒs.tɒk / (BrE)
a city in northeastern Germany near the Baltic Sea, historically important as a member of the Hanseatic League in the 14th century.
city in northeastern Germany

Examples

The university in Rostock is one of the oldest in northern Europe.
During our trip through Germany, we spent a weekend exploring Rostock.
In the 14th century, Rostock was an influential member of the Hanseatic League.
