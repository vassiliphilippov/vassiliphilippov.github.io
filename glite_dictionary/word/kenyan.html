Kenyan

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛ.ni.ən / (AmE), / ˈkɛ.ni.ən / (BrE)
A person who is a native or inhabitant of Kenya.
native or inhabitant of Kenya

Examples

The Kenyan greeted us with a warm smile when we arrived in Nairobi.
Many Kenyans are proud of their rich cultural diversity.
A famous Kenyan marathon runner won the race last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kenya - country in East Africa

Etymology

From Kenya + -an (suffix forming demonyms and adjectives).
