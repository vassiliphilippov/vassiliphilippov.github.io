studded

1. ADJ

/ ˈstʌdɪd / (AmE), / ˈstʌdɪd / (BrE)
decorated or covered with studs or small objects, often used in combination to describe something adorned in this way
decorated or covered with studs

Examples

The musician wore a leather jacket that was studded with silver spikes.
She admired the diamond-studded watch on her wrist.
The ceiling was studded with tiny lights, creating a magical atmosphere.

Synset Examples

star-studded heavens
diamond-studded belt

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embellished - decorated or adorned, often to make more attractive
  • Antonym: plain - not decorated or adorned; simple

Etymology

From the verb 'stud' meaning to decorate with studs, ultimately from Old English 'studu' (post, support).
