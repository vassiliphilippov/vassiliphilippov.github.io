hryvnia

1. NOUN

/ ˈhrɪvniə / (AmE), / ˈhrɪvnɪə / (BrE)
the official monetary unit of Ukraine, equal to 100 kopiykas
the currency of Ukraine

Examples

The price of the book was listed as 150 hryvnias in the shop window.
After exchanging dollars for hryvnia, we were ready to travel across Ukraine.
The value of the hryvnia fluctuated against the euro last year.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: kopiyka - the subunit of the hryvnia, equal to one hundredth of a hryvnia

Etymology

From Ukrainian гривня (hryvnia), originally meaning 'mane' or 'necklace', a unit of weight in medieval Kievan Rus’.
