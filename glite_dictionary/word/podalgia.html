podalgia

1. NOUN

/ poʊˈdældʒiə / (AmE), / pəʊˈdældʒɪə / (BrE)
Pain occurring in the foot.
foot pain
medical

Examples

The patient complained of persistent podalgia after long periods of standing.
Treatment for podalgia may include orthotic devices or physical therapy.
Several conditions, such as plantar fasciitis, can cause severe podalgia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foot pain - pain in the foot

Etymology

From Greek 'pous' (foot) + 'algia' (pain).
