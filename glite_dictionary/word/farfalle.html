farfalle

1. NOUN

/ fɑrˈfɑːleɪ / (AmE), / fɑːˈfɑːleɪ / (BrE)
a type of pasta shaped like small bows or butterflies, with pinched centers and scalloped edges
bow-tie shaped pasta

Examples

She prepared a delicious salad using farfalle, cherry tomatoes, and feta cheese.
The farfalle cooked quickly and held the sauce beautifully.
At the Italian restaurant, I ordered farfalle with pesto and grilled chicken.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bow tie pasta - another name for farfalle pasta

Etymology

From Italian 'farfalle', meaning 'butterflies', plural of 'farfalla' (butterfly).
