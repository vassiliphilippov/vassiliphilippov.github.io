to rename

1. VERB

/ ˌriːˈneɪm / (AmE), / ˌriːˈneɪm / (BrE)
to give a new name to someone or something
give a new name to

Examples

After the merger, the company decided to rename its flagship product.
She plans to rename her business to better reflect its services.
The city council voted to rename the park in honor of the late mayor.

Synset Examples

Many streets in the former East Germany were renamed in 1990

Related Terms

  • Synonym: name - to give a name to

Etymology

From re- (again) + name (from Old English nama).
