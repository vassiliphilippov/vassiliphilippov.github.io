inkpad

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋkˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈɪŋkˌpæd / (BrE)
A small block or pad of absorbent material saturated with ink, used to apply ink to a rubber stamp.
pad for inking rubber stamps

Examples

He pressed the stamp onto the inkpad before marking the document.
Make sure the inkpad isn't dry before using it for crafts.
She replaced the old inkpad with a fresh, blue one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stamp pad - a pad used to ink stamps; synonym for inkpad

Etymology

From 'ink' + 'pad'.
