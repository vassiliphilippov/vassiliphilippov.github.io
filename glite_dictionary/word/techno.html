techno

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛk.noʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɛk.nəʊ / (BrE)
a style of fast, heavy electronic dance music, usually without vocals
electronic dance music genre

Examples

The club played only techno until sunrise, keeping everyone on the dance floor.
She prefers listening to techno when she works out at the gym.
Many popular DJs started their careers in the techno music scene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: house - a genre of electronic dance music with a repetitive four-on-the-floor beat
  • Synonym: trance - a genre of electronic dance music characterized by hypnotic rhythms and melodies
  • Antonym: rock - a genre of music with guitars and drums, contrasting with techno

Etymology

Shortening of 'technology', originally from the term 'techno-pop' in the 1980s.
