chafflike

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃæfˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːfˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling, consisting of, or covered with chaff; having the appearance or texture of chaff
resembling or covered with chaff

Examples

The chafflike covering on the seed ensures its protection during dispersal.
Some grasses produce a chafflike material that aids in wind pollination.
After threshing, the barn floor was littered with chafflike debris.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chaffy - having the attributes of chaff; covered with or resembling chaff

Etymology

Formed from 'chaff' + '-like'.
