human-rightism

1. NOUN

/ ˌhjuːmənˈraɪtɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌhjuːmənˈraɪtɪzəm / (BrE)
the advocacy or support of human rights and human rights ideologies
advocacy of human rights

Examples

Many international organizations are founded on the principles of human-rightism.
Some scholars debate whether human-rightism takes into account specific cultural contexts.
Raising awareness about injustice is a key aspect of human-rightism.

Synset Examples

Her advocacy for equality exemplifies her commitment to human-rightism.
Critics argue that human-rightism can sometimes overlook cultural differences.
He dedicated his career to advancing human-rightism across the globe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humanitarianism - the promotion of human welfare and social reform
  • Antonym: authoritarianism - enforcement or advocacy of strict obedience to authority at the expense of personal freedom

Etymology

Formed from 'human rights' + '-ism' (denoting a doctrine or ideology).
