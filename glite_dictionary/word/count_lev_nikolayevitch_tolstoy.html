Count Lev Nikolayevitch Tolstoy

1. PROPN

/ kaʊnt lɛv ˌniːkəˈlaɪəvɪtʃ tɒlˈstɔɪ / (AmE), / kaʊnt lɛv ˌnɪkəˈlaɪəvɪtʃ tɒlˈstɔɪ / (BrE)
A Russian author (1828–1910) regarded as one of the greatest novelists, best known for 'War and Peace' and 'Anna Karenina'.
Russian writer, author of 'War and Peace'

Examples

Many critics consider Count Lev Nikolayevitch Tolstoy to be the greatest novelist of all time.
'War and Peace' by Count Lev Nikolayevitch Tolstoy is regarded as a literary masterpiece.
The philosophy of Count Lev Nikolayevitch Tolstoy greatly influenced Mahatma Gandhi.
