stepmother

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɛpˌmʌðɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɛpˌmʌðə / (BrE)
A woman who is married to one's father but is not one's biological mother.
father's wife not one's biological mother

Examples

After her father remarried, Emily learned to get along with her new stepmother.
Cinderella's stepmother was famously unkind to her in the fairy tale.
Having a good relationship with your stepmother can make blended family life easier.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mother - female parent
  • Antonym: stepfather - the husband of your mother by a subsequent marriage

Etymology

Middle English, from step- (used for relationship by remarriage) + mother.
