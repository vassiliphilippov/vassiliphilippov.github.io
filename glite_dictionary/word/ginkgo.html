ginkgo

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪŋkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪŋkəʊ / (BrE)
A deciduous tree native to China with fan-shaped leaves and yellowish seeds, often grown as an ornamental street tree.
deciduous Chinese tree with fan-shaped leaves

Examples

The ginkgo is easily recognized by its distinctive fan-shaped leaves.
Several ginkgos were planted along the avenue to brighten the city in autumn.
The ancient ginkgo tree in the temple courtyard attracts many visitors each year.

Etymology

From Japanese *ginkyō*, from Chinese *yínxìng* (银杏), meaning 'silver apricot'.
