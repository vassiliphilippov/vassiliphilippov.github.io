Andrena

1. NOUN

/ ænˈdrɛnə / (AmE), / ænˈdriːnə / (BrE)
A genus of solitary burrowing bees with short tongues, commonly known as mining bees.
genus of solitary burrowing bees

Examples

The Andrena bee is often seen nesting in sandy soils in the spring.
Researchers discovered a new species within the Andrena genus.
Many flowers rely on Andrena bees for effective pollination.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin 'Andrena', the scientific name given to this genus by taxonomists.
