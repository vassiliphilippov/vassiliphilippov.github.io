human-centred

1. ADJ

/ ˈhjuːmən ˌsɛntərd / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən ˌsɛntəd / (BrE)
focused on prioritizing human needs, values, and welfare above all else
prioritizing human needs and welfare

Examples

The new product was designed with a human-centred approach to ensure it met users' needs.
Their company culture is distinctly human-centred, valuing employee well-being above profits.
Modern urban planning increasingly favors human-centred solutions to improve quality of life.

Synset Examples

released the prisoner for humanitarian reasons
respect and humanistic regard for all members of our species

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humanistic - showing concern for human values and needs
  • Synonym: humanitarian - concerned with promoting human welfare
