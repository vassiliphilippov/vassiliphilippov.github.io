daubing

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɔːbɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of applying plaster or a similar substance to a surface, typically in a rough or unskilled manner
the application of plaster

Examples

The rough daubing on the wall had to be smoothed out before painting.
Traditional houses featured a daubing of mud and straw on the walls.
After the daubing was complete, the room looked much brighter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plastering - the process of coating walls or ceilings with plaster

Etymology

From the verb 'daub', from Middle English dauben (to smear), from Old French dauber.
