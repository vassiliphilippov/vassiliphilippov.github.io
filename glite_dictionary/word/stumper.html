stumper

1. NOUN

/ ˈstʌmpɚ / (AmE), / ˈstʌmpə / (BrE)
a question or problem that is especially difficult to answer or solve
a difficult question or problem

Examples

The final question of the quiz was a real stumper that nobody could answer.
She loves crossword puzzles, especially when she encounters a stumper that makes her think.
That math problem was such a stumper that even the teacher had trouble solving it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poser - A tricky or puzzling question
  • Synonym: problem - A difficult issue or situation requiring a solution
  • Antonym: easy - Simple to solve or understand

Etymology

From 'stump' (to perplex or challenge someone) + '-er', denoting an agent or thing that stumps.
