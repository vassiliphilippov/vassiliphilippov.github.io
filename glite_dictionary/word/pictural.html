pictural

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɪk.tʃɚ.əl / (AmE), / ˈpɪk.tʃər.əl / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of pictures; pictorial.
relating to pictures

Examples

The museum displayed a pictural record of the city's development.
Scholars analyzed the pictural elements in the ancient manuscript.
Her presentation included a strong pictural component to convey complex ideas visually.

Synset Examples

pictorial perspective
pictorial records

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pictorial - relating to or consisting of pictures

Etymology

From Latin ‘pictura’ (painting, picture) via French ‘pictural’.
