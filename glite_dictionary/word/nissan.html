Nissan

1. NOUN

/ niːˈsɑːn / (AmE), / ˈniːsæn / (BrE)
the seventh month of the civil year and the first month of the ecclesiastical year in the Jewish calendar, falling in March or April
month in the Jewish calendar (March-April)

Examples

Passover is celebrated during the month of Nissan in the Jewish calendar.
This year, the month of Nissan begins in late March.
According to tradition, Nissan marks the anniversary of the Exodus from Egypt.

Etymology

From Hebrew 'Nīsān', borrowed from Akkadian 'Nisānu'.
