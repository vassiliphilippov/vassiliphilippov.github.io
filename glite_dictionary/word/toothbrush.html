toothbrush

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːθˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈtuːθ.brʌʃ / (BrE)
A small brush with a long handle, used for cleaning the teeth.
brush for cleaning teeth

Examples

He bought a new toothbrush during his trip to the pharmacy.
Always replace your toothbrush every three months for good oral hygiene.
She packed her toothbrush and toothpaste in her travel bag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toothpaste - a paste used with a toothbrush to clean teeth

Etymology

From 'tooth' + 'brush', indicating a brush for teeth.
