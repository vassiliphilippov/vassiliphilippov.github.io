fixedly

1. ADV

/ ˈfɪksɪdli / (AmE), / ˈfɪksɪdli / (BrE)
In a steady and unwavering manner; without moving or changing one's focus.
in a steady or unwavering manner

Examples

She looked fixedly at the painting, lost in thought.
The dog stared fixedly at the door, waiting for its owner to return.
He fixedly held his gaze on the speaker throughout the meeting.

Synset Examples

he stared at me fixedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steadily - in a steady manner, without wavering
  • Synonym: intently - with focused or concentrated attention

Etymology

Formed from 'fixed' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
