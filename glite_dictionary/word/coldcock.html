to coldcock

1. VERB

/ ˈkoʊlˌkɑk / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlˌkɒk / (BrE)
to knock someone down with a single forceful blow, especially to the head; to strike unexpectedly so as to render unconscious or incapacitated
strike and knock down with force
slang

Examples

The thief tried to escape, but the officer coldcocked him with a quick punch.
He never saw the punch coming and was coldcocked in the middle of the argument.
She threatened to coldcock anyone who tried to mess with her friends.

Synset Examples

He decked his opponent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deck - to knock someone to the ground with a punch
  • Synonym: strike - to hit with force
