metopion

1. NOUN

/ məˈtoʊ.pi.ən / (AmE), / məˈtəʊ.pi.ən / (BrE)
the craniometric point located midway between the frontal eminences of the skull
midpoint between frontal eminences of the skull
medical

Examples

In anthropological studies, the metopion helps researchers identify cranial features.
The surgeon marked the metopion before proceeding with the procedure.
Measuring from the metopion ensures accurate assessment of skull proportions.

Etymology

From Greek 'metōpion', diminutive of 'metōpon' (forehead).
