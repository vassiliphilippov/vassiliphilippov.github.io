Missourian

1. NOUN

/ mɪˈzʊəriən / (AmE), / mɪˈzʊəriən / (BrE)
a native or resident of the state of Missouri in the United States.
a person from Missouri

Examples

She is a proud Missourian who often talks about her childhood in St. Louis.
As a lifelong Missourian, he knows the best places to visit in the state.
The parade celebrated Missourian culture and traditions with music and food from the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Missouri - a state in the central United States

Etymology

From Missouri (U.S. state) + -an (denoting origin or association).
