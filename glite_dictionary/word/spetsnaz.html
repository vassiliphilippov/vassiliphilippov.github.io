Spetsnaz

1. NOUN

/ spɛtsˌnɑz / (AmE), / ˈspɛts.naz / (BrE)
A member of the Russian Special Forces, or the Russian Special Forces themselves.
Russian Special Forces

Examples

Russian Spetsnaz units are renowned for their rigorous training and high-risk missions.
The documentary covered the history and operations of the Spetsnaz during the Cold War.
He claimed to have served as a Spetsnaz soldier before moving abroad.

Etymology

From Russian 'спецназ' (spetsnaz), an abbreviation of 'специальное назначение' (spetsialnoye naznacheniye, special purpose).
