Coracias

1. NOUN

/ kəˈreɪsiəs / (AmE), / kɒˈreɪsiəs / (BrE)
The type genus of the bird family Coraciidae, comprising the rollers.
type genus of the roller birds (Coraciidae)

Examples

The genus Coracias includes several colorful species of rollers found across Africa and Eurasia.
Researchers observed the nesting behavior of Coracias birds in their natural habitat.
The European roller, a member of the genus Coracias, migrates to Africa during winter.

Etymology

From New Latin, from Greek korakías (a kind of bird), from korax (raven).
