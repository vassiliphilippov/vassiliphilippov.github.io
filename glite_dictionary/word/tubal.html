tubal

1. ADJ

/ ˈtuːbəl / (AmE), / ˈtjuːbəl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring in a tube, especially the Fallopian tube or Eustachian tube in the body
relating to a tube, esp. in anatomy
medical

Examples

After having a tubal ligation, she could no longer become pregnant.
A tubal pregnancy requires immediate medical attention to prevent complications.
The doctor explained that the infection was most likely tubal in origin.

Synset Examples

tubal ligation
tubal pregnancy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fallopian - relating to the Fallopian tubes
  • Antonym: uterine - relating to the uterus

Etymology

From Latin tubalis, from tubus meaning 'tube'.
