Sir James Augustus Henry Murray

1. PROPN

/ sɜr ˈdʒeɪmz ɔːˈɡʌstəs ˈhɛnri ˈmɜri / (AmE), / sə ˈdʒeɪmz ɔːˈɡʌstəs ˈhɛnri ˈmʌri / (BrE)
(1837–1915) Scottish philologist and lexicographer, best known as the primary editor of the Oxford English Dictionary.
Scottish lexicographer; editor of the OED

Examples

Sir James Augustus Henry Murray led the team that compiled the original Oxford English Dictionary.
The achievements of Sir James Augustus Henry Murray are celebrated by linguists worldwide.
Few people realize the lasting impact Sir James Augustus Henry Murray had on English lexicography.
