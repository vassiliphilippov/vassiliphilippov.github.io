dabbing

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdabɪŋ / (BrE)
A dance move in which the dancer drops their head while raising an arm and elbow, commonly resembling a sneezing gesture.
dance move resembling sneezing

Examples

The sports star celebrated his victory by dabbing on stage.
Many students performed dabbing during the school talent show.
She learned dabbing from viral internet videos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dab - to press lightly with a quick motion, or the act of doing so

2. NOUN

/ ˈdæbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdæbɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of pressing a marijuana concentrate onto a hot surface to vaporize and inhale it
inhaling vaporized marijuana concentrate
slang

Examples

He prefers dabbing to smoking traditional joints because it gives a cleaner high.
The popularity of dabbing has led to the development of specialized rigs and tools.
Some users warn that dabbing concentrated cannabis extracts can produce intoxicating effects very quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dab - a small amount of a marijuana concentrate for vaporization
  • Synonym: vaping - inhaling vapor produced by a heated substance, often nicotine or cannabis
