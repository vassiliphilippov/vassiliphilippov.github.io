GSPC

1. PROPN

/ dʒi ɛs pi si / (AmE), / dʒiː ɛs piː siː / (BrE)
an Algerian Islamist militant group formerly known as the Salafist Group for Preaching and Combat, once the largest and most active terrorist organization in Algeria and a supporter of al-Qaeda.
Algerian Islamist militant group

Examples

The GSPC emerged in the late 1990s as a powerful force opposing the Algerian government.
International intelligence agencies closely monitor the activities of the GSPC.
The GSPC later aligned itself with al-Qaeda, expanding its operations beyond Algeria.

Etymology

Abbreviation of French Groupe Salafiste pour la Prédication et le Combat (Salafist Group for Preaching and Combat).
