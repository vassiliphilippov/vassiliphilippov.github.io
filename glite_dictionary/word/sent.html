sent

1. NOUN

/ sɛnt / (AmE), / sɛnt / (BrE)
a former monetary unit of Estonia, equal to one hundredth of a kroon
former Estonian monetary unit

Examples

After the introduction of the euro, the sent and the kroon ceased to be legal tender.
An old collection of Estonian coins included several senti.
One kroon was divided into one hundred senti during Estonia's transition period.

Etymology

From Estonian 'sent', borrowed from German 'Cent', ultimately from Latin 'centum' (hundred).
