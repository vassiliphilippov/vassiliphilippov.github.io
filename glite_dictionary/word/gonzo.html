gonzo

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɑnzoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡɒnzəʊ / (BrE)
very strange, bizarre, or wildly unconventional, especially in style or approach.
unconventional; bizarre in style
informal american

Examples

The artist is known for his gonzo style, mixing clashing colors and peculiar shapes.
Her gonzo reporting brought a wild, firsthand perspective to the story.
The movie's gonzo humor isn't for everyone, but it definitely stands out.

Synset Examples

restaurants of bizarre design — one like a hat, another like a rabbit
famed for his eccentric spelling
a freakish combination of styles
his off-the-wall antics
the outlandish clothes of teenagers
outre and affected stage antics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - unconventional and slightly strange
  • Synonym: bizarre - very strange or unusual
  • Antonym: conventional - following accepted customs or standards

Etymology

Originating in US slang (1970s); popularized through 'gonzo journalism' by Hunter S. Thompson.
