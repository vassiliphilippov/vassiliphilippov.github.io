lamplit

1. ADJ

/ ˈlæmˌlɪt / (AmE), / ˈlam.lɪt / (BrE)
illuminated by the light of a lamp
illuminated by a lamp

Examples

The narrow lamplit alley felt mysterious in the quiet of the night.
She walked home along the lamplit path beneath the trees.
The painting depicted a cozy, lamplit room filled with books.

Synset Examples

our lamplit mountain retreat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: candlelit - illuminated by the light of candles
  • Antonym: sunlit - lighted by sunlight
  • Synonym: moonlit - illuminated by the light of the moon

Etymology

From 'lamp' + 'lit', past participle of 'light'.
