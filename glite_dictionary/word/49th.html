49th

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrtiˈnaɪnθ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtiˈnaɪnθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number corresponding to forty-nine in a sequence, used to indicate position in an ordered list.
ordinal number for forty-nine

Examples

She finished in 49th place out of over two hundred runners.
The company is celebrating its 49th anniversary this year.
This is the 49th time the festival has been held.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forty-ninth - alternative written/spoken form for 49th (ordinal number)
