help

1. VERB

/ hɛlp / (AmE), / hɛlp / (BrE)
to make it easier for someone to do something or for something to happen; to be of use or assistance
be of use; assist or make easier

Examples

Modern technology can help improve communication between teams.
Drinking water regularly helps maintain good health.
These instructions will help you assemble the furniture quickly.

Synset Examples

This will help to prevent accidents

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assist - to give help or support
  • Antonym: hinder - to make it more difficult for something to happen

Etymology

From Old English helpan (to assist, support).
