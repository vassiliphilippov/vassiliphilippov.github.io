godwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑdˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who is regarded as a spiritual leader or religious figure with divine powers or wisdom.
female spiritual leader

Examples

Many people sought guidance from the godwoman of the village.
The godwoman was respected for her wisdom and spiritual insight.
Devotees lined up to receive blessings from the renowned godwoman.

Synset Examples

Many turned to her, calling her a true godwoman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: godman - a male spiritual leader or religious figure with perceived divine powers

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'godman,' from 'god' + 'woman.'
