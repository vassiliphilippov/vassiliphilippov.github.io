hilarious

1. ADJ

/ hɪˈlɛriəs / (AmE), / hɪˈlɛːrɪəs / (BrE)
Extremely funny; causing loud laughter or great amusement.
extremely funny; causing loud laughter

Examples

The comedian's new show was absolutely hilarious, and the audience couldn't stop laughing.
She told a hilarious story about her childhood adventures at the family dinner.
That movie was so hilarious that I cried from laughing too hard.

Synset Examples

hilarious broad comedy
a screaming farce
uproarious stories

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uproarious - extremely funny or causing noisy laughter
  • Degree Lower: funny - amusing or making you laugh
  • Antonym: dull - not interesting or amusing; boring

Etymology

From Latin hilarus (cheerful), from Greek hilaros (cheerful, merry).
