discriminator

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈskrɪməˌneɪtər / (AmE), / dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that differentiates or makes distinctions between things
person or thing that differentiates

Examples

The security system used an advanced discriminator to distinguish between authorized and unauthorized users.
As a skilled discriminator, she was able to tell genuine antiques from clever fakes.
A color discriminator circuit is used to separate different hues in the image.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: differentiator - a person or thing that distinguishes between things

Etymology

From Latin discriminare (to divide, separate), via English discriminate + -or.
