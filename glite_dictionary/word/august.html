August

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔɡəst / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡəst / (BrE)
The eighth month of the year, following July and preceding September.
eighth month of the year

Examples

We always go on holiday in August because the weather is warm.
The new school year usually starts at the end of August.
My birthday falls in August every year.

Etymology

From Latin 'Augustus', the name given to the month in honor of the Roman emperor Augustus.
