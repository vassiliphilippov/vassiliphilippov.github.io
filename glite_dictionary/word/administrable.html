administrable

1. ADJ

/ ədˈmɪn.ə.rə.bəl / (AmE), / ədˈmɪn.ɪ.strə.bəl / (BrE)
capable of being administered, managed, or given
able to be administered or managed

Examples

The drug is only administrable by trained healthcare professionals in a clinical setting.
This policy contains provisions to ensure it is both effective and administrable.
Not all substances are administrable orally; some require injection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manageable - able to be managed or controlled

Etymology

From Latin administrare (to manage) + -able, meaning able to be managed.
