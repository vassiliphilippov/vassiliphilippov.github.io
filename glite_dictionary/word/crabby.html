crabby

1. ADJ

/ ˈkræbi / (AmE), / ˈkræbi / (BrE)
easily annoyed or irritable; in a bad mood
easily annoyed or irritable

Examples

She tends to get crabby when she hasn't had enough sleep.
Don't be so crabby just because it's raining outside.
He was feeling particularly crabby after a long day at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irritable - easily annoyed or provoked to anger
  • Synonym: grumpy - bad-tempered or easily annoyed
  • Antonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic

Etymology

From crab (the crustacean), with reference to the crab's sideways movements and perceived crankiness.
