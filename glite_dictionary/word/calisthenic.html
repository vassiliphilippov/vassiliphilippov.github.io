calisthenic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkælɪsˈθɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌkælɪsˈθɛnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of calisthenics, especially exercises that use body weight for resistance
relating to calisthenics

Examples

She follows a calisthenic routine every morning to stay fit.
The gym offers specialized calisthenic classes for beginners.
He designed a calisthenic workout that requires no equipment at all.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calisthenics - system of gymnastic exercises using body weight

Etymology

Formed from 'calisthenics', itself from Greek 'kallos' (beauty) + 'sthenos' (strength).
