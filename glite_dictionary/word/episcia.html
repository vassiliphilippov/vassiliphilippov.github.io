episcia

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈpɪʃiə / (AmE), / ɪˈpɪsɪə / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Episcia, typically a creeping and stoloniferous species grown for its colorful foliage and flowers.
creeping tropical plant with colorful foliage

Examples

The bright leaves of the episcia make it a popular choice for hanging baskets.
She grows several varieties of episcia in her indoor garden for their vibrant flowers.
Trailing stems of the episcia create a lush, cascading effect over the edges of pots.

Etymology

From New Latin *Episcia*, from Greek *episkios* meaning 'shaded' or 'shadowed'.
