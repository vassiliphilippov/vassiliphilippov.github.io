Rotterdam

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɑːtərˌdæm / (AmE), / ˈrɒtədæm / (BrE)
the second largest city in the Netherlands, located in the western part of the country near the North Sea, known for its major port and modern architecture.
major city and port in the Netherlands

Examples

The port of Rotterdam is one of the busiest in the world, handling millions of containers each year.
After visiting Amsterdam, they traveled south to explore Rotterdam and its impressive modern skyline.
Many international companies have their European headquarters in Rotterdam due to its central location.
