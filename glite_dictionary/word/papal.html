papal

1. ADJ

/ ˈpeɪpəl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəl / (BrE)
relating to or issued by the pope or the papacy; subject to the authority of the pope.
relating to the pope or the papacy

Examples

The new papal encyclical was discussed widely among theologians.
He received a papal blessing during his visit to the Vatican.
A papal decree changed the rules for the selection of bishops.

Synset Examples

papal dispensation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pontifical - relating to the pope; papal

Etymology

From Latin 'papalis', from 'papa' meaning 'pope'.
