San Salvador

1. PROPN

/ sæn sælˈvædɔr / (AmE), / san salˈvadɔː / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of El Salvador, located in Central America, often affected by recurrent earthquakes.
capital city of El Salvador

Examples

Many tourists visit San Salvador to experience its vibrant culture and historic landmarks.
After the earthquake, international aid arrived quickly in San Salvador.
San Salvador is known for its bustling markets and scenic volcanoes nearby.

Etymology

Spanish for 'Holy Savior'; named by Spanish colonists.
