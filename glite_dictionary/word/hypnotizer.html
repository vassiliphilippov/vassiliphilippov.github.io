hypnotizer

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪpnəˌtaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈhɪpnəˌtaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who induces hypnosis in others
person who induces hypnosis

Examples

The hypnotizer instructed the volunteer to relax and focus on his voice.
Many people are skeptical about what a hypnotizer can achieve during a session.
At the show, the hypnotizer quickly put several audience members into a trance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hypnotist - a person skilled in performing hypnosis

Etymology

From 'hypnotize' (to induce hypnosis) + '-er' (agent suffix).
