Bubalus mindorensis

1. NOUN

/ ˈbjuːbələs mɪnˌdɔːrɛnˈsɪs / (AmE), / ˈbjuːbələs mɪnˌdɒrɛnˈsɪs / (BrE)
A small, endangered species of buffalo native to the island of Mindoro in the Philippines, also known as the tamaraw.
small buffalo species native to Mindoro, Philippines

Examples

The Bubalus mindorensis is critically endangered due to habitat loss and hunting.
Researchers are working to protect the remaining Bubalus mindorensis in the wild.
The Bubalus mindorensis, or tamaraw, is a symbol of conservation efforts in the Philippines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tamaraw - the common name for Bubalus mindorensis, a small buffalo from Mindoro

Etymology

From the genus name Bubalus (Latin for ‘buffalo’) and Species mindorensis, referring to Mindoro island.
