unremunerative

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.rɪˈmjunəˌreɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / ʌn.rɪˈmjuː.nər.ə.tɪv / (BrE)
(of a job, work, or activity) not providing payment, profit, or reward.
not providing payment or profit

Examples

She left her unremunerative job to pursue a more profitable career.
Volunteering can be fulfilling even if the work is unremunerative.
The artist struggled for years in the unremunerative world of independent film.

Synset Examples

an unremunerative occupation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: remunerative - providing payment, profit, or reward

Etymology

From un- (not) + remunerative, from Latin remunerari (to reward).
