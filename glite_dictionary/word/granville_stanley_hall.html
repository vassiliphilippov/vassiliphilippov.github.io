Granville Stanley Hall

1. PROPN

/ ɡrænˈvɪl ˈstænli hɔl / (AmE), / ɡrænˈvɪl ˈstænli hɔːl / (BrE)
(1844–1924) An American psychologist and educator noted for his pioneering work in child and educational psychology.
American child psychologist and educator

Examples

Granville Stanley Hall was one of the founders of child psychology as a scientific discipline.
Many of Granville Stanley Hall's theories still influence modern educational approaches.
The lecture emphasized the contributions of Granville Stanley Hall to the study of adolescence.
