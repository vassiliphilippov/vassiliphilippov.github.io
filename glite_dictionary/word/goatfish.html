goatfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊtˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊtˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a brightly colored tropical marine fish with a pair of chin barbels, found in warm seas
a tropical fish with chin barbels

Examples

The coral reef was teeming with goatfish searching for food with their barbels.
Fishermen in the Mediterranean often catch goatfish for local markets.
A school of goatfish swam gracefully over the sandy seabed, their colors shimmering in the sunlight.

Etymology

From the resemblance of the chin barbels to a goat's beard.
