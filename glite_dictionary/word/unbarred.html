unbarred

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbɑrd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɑːd / (BrE)
not fastened, closed, or obstructed by a bar; open or lacking bars or barriers
not secured or blocked by a bar

Examples

The prisoners saw the unbarred window as their only chance of escape.
She walked through the unbarred gate and into the peaceful garden beyond.
During the inspection, the guards found an unbarred door at the end of the corridor.

Synset Examples

an unbarred door
went through the unlatched gate into the street
an unlocked room

Related Terms

  • Antonym: barred - fastened or blocked by a bar
  • Synonym: unlocked - not locked, not fastened with a lock

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'bar' (to fasten with a bar).
