trucking

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrʌkɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or business of transporting goods by truck
transporting goods by truck

Examples

The boom in online shopping has led to rapid growth in trucking across the country.
He worked in trucking for over twenty years before starting his own business.
The trucking industry faces challenges such as driver shortages and rising fuel costs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freighting - the activity of transporting goods, usually by large vehicles, ships, or aircraft
  • Synonym: shipping - the transport of goods, especially by sea or other vehicles

Etymology

Formed from 'truck' (vehicle) + '-ing', indicating the activity.
