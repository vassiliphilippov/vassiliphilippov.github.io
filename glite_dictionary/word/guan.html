guan

1. NOUN

/ ɡwɑːn / (AmE), / ɡwɑːn / (BrE)
a large, turkey-like game bird of the family Cracidae, native to the jungles of tropical America and resembling curassows, often valued as food
large game bird of tropical America

Examples

The guan is often hunted for its meat in rural areas of South America.
Bird watchers travel to see the rare black guan in its native habitat.
Unlike the domestic turkey, a guan prefers the dense forests of the tropics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curassow - a large bird of tropical America related to guans

Etymology

From Spanish 'guan', from a native South American (Tupi-Guarani) name for the bird.
