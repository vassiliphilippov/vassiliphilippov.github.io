riverbank

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪvərˌbæŋk / (AmE), / ˈrɪvəˌbæŋk / (BrE)
The ground along the edge of a river.
land beside a river

Examples

Children played along the riverbank until sunset.
After the heavy rain, the riverbank was flooded.
She sat quietly on the riverbank, watching the water flow past.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bank - the rising ground beside a body of water
