dachsie

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæksi / (AmE), / ˈdaksi / (BrE)
An informal term for a dachshund, a small, long-bodied dog breed with short legs and a sleek coat.
informal term for a dachshund dog
informal

Examples

My dachsie loves to dig in the garden and chase after squirrels.
We adopted a sweet little dachsie from the animal shelter last month.
Her grandmother always had a dachsie sleeping at the foot of her bed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dachshund - a small German breed of dog with a long body and short legs

Etymology

Shortened, informal form of "dachshund," from German "Dachshund" (badger dog).
