twofold

1. ADV

/ ˈtuˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈtuːfəʊld / (BrE)
By a factor of two; doubly or in two ways.
by a factor of two

Examples

Sales have increased twofold since the new product launched.
The company's profits grew twofold over the past year.
Her responsibilities have multiplied twofold after her promotion.

Synset Examples

the price increased twofold last year

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double - twice as much or as many

Etymology

Formed from 'two' + 'fold,' meaning multiplied or increased by two.
