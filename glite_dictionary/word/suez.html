Suez

1. PROPN

/ suˈɛz / (AmE), / suˈɛz / (BrE)
A city in northeastern Egypt at the northern tip of the Gulf of Suez and the southern end of the Suez Canal.
city in northeastern Egypt at the Suez Canal

Examples

The city of Suez played a crucial role during the construction of the canal.
Many ships pass through Suez on their way to the Mediterranean Sea.
Suez has grown significantly due to its location by the Suez Canal.
