slayer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsleɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈsleɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person or animal that kills another person or animal, especially deliberately
someone or something that kills

Examples

The legendary hero became famous as a slayer of monsters.
The police finally caught the slayer responsible for the crime.
Many cultures have stories of a dragon slayer who saves the day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: killer - someone who kills
  • Synonym: murderer - someone who unlawfully kills another person
  • Antonym: victim - someone who is killed or harmed

Etymology

From Old English 'slēan' (to strike, kill) + '-er' (agent suffix).
