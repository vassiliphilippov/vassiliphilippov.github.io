plantsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæntsmən / (AmE), / ˈplɑːntsmən / (BrE)
A person, often a man, who is skilled in or has a strong interest in cultivating and growing plants.
skilled person in plant cultivation

Examples

As a respected plantsman, he often shared advice about rare perennials at gardening clubs.
The renowned plantsman spent decades cultivating unique breeds of roses.
She aspired to become a plantsman, learning about soil, climate, and sustainable horticulture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horticulturist - an expert in garden cultivation and management

Etymology

From 'plant' + 'man'; patterning after similar compounds like 'craftsman'.
