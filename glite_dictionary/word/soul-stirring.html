soul-stirring

1. ADJ

/ ˈsoʊl ˌstɜrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊl ˌstɜːrɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing strong, deep, and moving emotions; deeply affecting or inspiring.
profoundly moving or inspiring

Examples

The movie was so soul-stirring that many people left the theater in tears.
Her soul-stirring performance captivated everyone in the audience.
They wrote a soul-stirring letter that inspired hope and courage among the recipients.

Synset Examples

a stirring speech

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moving - making you feel strong emotion, especially sadness or sympathy
  • Antonym: uninspiring - not exciting or emotionally moving
