highlife

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪˌlaɪf / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌlaɪf / (BrE)
a lifestyle characterized by extravagant living and excessive spending, often involving luxury and social activities
extravagant lifestyle; excessive spending

Examples

After winning the lottery, she started living the highlife and traveling around the world.
Many celebrities are drawn to the highlife with its glamorous parties and expensive tastes.
The magazine featured photos of socialites enjoying the highlife in Monte Carlo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luxury - the state of great comfort and extravagant living
  • Antonym: frugality - the quality of being economical with money or food
