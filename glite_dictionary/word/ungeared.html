ungeared

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɡɪrd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡɪəd / (BrE)
(of a device or machine part) having gears not engaged or disconnected from the drive mechanism.
not having gears engaged or connected

Examples

The technician inspected the ungeared shaft before beginning repairs.
Make sure the engine is ungeared before you attempt any maintenance.
The machine's ungeared pinion needs to be replaced to restore full functionality.

Synset Examples

the machine's ungeared pinion

Related Terms

  • Antonym: geared - having gears engaged or connected

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'geared' (fitted with gears).
