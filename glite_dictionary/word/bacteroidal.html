bacteroidal

1. ADJ

/ bækˈtɛrɔɪdəl / (AmE), / bækˈtɪə.rɔɪ.dəl / (BrE)
Resembling or having the characteristics of bacteria.
resembling bacteria
medical

Examples

The scientist observed a bacteroidal structure under the microscope.
Some plant root nodules contain bacteroidal cells essential for nitrogen fixation.
The shape of the microorganism was described as bacteroidal by the researchers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bacterial - of or relating to bacteria

Etymology

Formed from 'bacteria' + '-oid' (resembling) + '-al' (adjectival suffix).
