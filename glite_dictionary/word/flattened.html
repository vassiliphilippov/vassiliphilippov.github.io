flattened

1. ADJ

/ ˈflætənd / (AmE), / ˈflætənd / (BrE)
made or having become flat or more level than before.
made flat or level

Examples

The cardboard box arrived already flattened to save space during shipping.
After the storm, parts of the forest appeared flattened by the powerful winds.
He prefers his burgers slightly flattened on the grill for extra crispiness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flat - having a level surface; not curved or sloping
