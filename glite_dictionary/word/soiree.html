soiree

1. NOUN

/ swɑːˈreɪ / (AmE), / swɑːˈreɪ / (BrE)
an evening party or gathering, typically held at someone's home
evening party or gathering

Examples

We were invited to a lavish soiree at the ambassador's residence.
Her annual garden soiree has become the talk of the neighborhood.
Guests wore elegant attire for the soiree held to celebrate the new book.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party - social gathering for celebration or recreation

Etymology

From French soirée (evening), from soir (evening).
