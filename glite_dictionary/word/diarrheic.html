diarrheic

1. ADJ

/ ˌdaɪəˈriːɪk / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈriːɪk / (BrE)
of or relating to diarrhea; characterized by or causing diarrhea.
relating to diarrhea
medical

Examples

The patient exhibited a diarrheic condition after taking the medication.
A diarrheic stool sample was sent to the laboratory for analysis.
Some infections can cause diarrheic symptoms that last for several days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diarrheal - of or relating to diarrhea

Etymology

Formed from 'diarrhea' + '-ic'. 'Diarrhea' from Greek 'diarrhoia' (a flowing through).
