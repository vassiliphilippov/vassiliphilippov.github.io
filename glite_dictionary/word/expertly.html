expertly

1. ADV

/ ˈɛk.spɚt.li / (AmE), / ˈɛk.spɜːt.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows skill or expertise.
in a skillful or expert way

Examples

She expertly decorated the cake, making it look almost too beautiful to eat.
The surgeon expertly performed the delicate procedure, impressing everyone in the room.
He expertly handled the negotiations, ensuring both parties were satisfied.

Synset Examples

he repaired the TV set expertly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skillfully - with skill or ability
  • Antonym: clumsily - in a way that lacks skill

Etymology

Formed from 'expert' + '-ly', with 'expert' from Latin 'expertus' (tried, experienced).
