leafstalk

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːfˌstɔk / (AmE), / ˈliːfˌstɔːk / (BrE)
the slender stalk that attaches a leaf blade to a plant stem; a petiole
stalk connecting a leaf to the stem

Examples

The leafstalk bends easily in the wind, supporting the broad green blade above.
After the autumn leaves fell, only the leafstalks remained attached to the branch.
The botanist explained how the leafstalk connects the leaf to the rest of the plant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petiole - botanical term for the stalk attaching a leaf blade to the stem
