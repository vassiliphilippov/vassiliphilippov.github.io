outstandingly

1. ADV

/ aʊtˈstændɪŋli / (AmE), / aʊtˈstandɪŋli / (BrE)
in an outstanding manner; to an extremely high degree or exceptionally well
to an exceptional or outstanding degree

Examples

The scientist was outstandingly innovative in her field.
Their new restaurant was outstandingly popular with both locals and tourists.
He handled the crisis outstandingly, impressing everyone with his calm and skill.

Synset Examples

she was outstandingly successful in her profession

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exceptionally - to an exceptional or unusual degree
  • Synonym: remarkably - in a remarkable or extraordinary manner

Etymology

From the adjective 'outstanding' plus the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
