pachinko

1. NOUN

/ pəˈtʃiŋˌkoʊ / (AmE), / pəˈtʃɪŋkəʊ / (BrE)
a Japanese arcade game resembling pinball, played on a vertical board, in which players try to win small steel balls.
Japanese vertical pinball game

Examples

He spent the afternoon playing pachinko in a busy Tokyo arcade.
Many tourists are fascinated by the colorful lights and sounds of pachinko machines.
In Japan, pachinko parlors are as common as convenience stores.

Etymology

From Japanese パチンコ (pachinko).
