Anne Boleyn

1. PROPN

/ æn boʊˈlɪn / (AmE), / æn bəˈlɪn / (BrE)
(1501–1536) The second wife of King Henry VIII of England and mother of Queen Elizabeth I, executed on charges of adultery.
second wife of Henry VIII, mother of Elizabeth I

Examples

Many historians still debate the reasons behind Anne Boleyn's execution.
The story of Anne Boleyn has inspired countless books and films.
