sulindac

1. NOUN

/ sʌˈlɪn.dæk / (AmE), / sʌˈlɪn.dæk / (BrE)
A nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used to reduce pain and inflammation in conditions such as arthritis.
a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed sulindac to manage her arthritis pain effectively.
Some patients may experience mild side effects when taking sulindac.
Studies have shown that sulindac is effective in reducing inflammation in chronic conditions.
