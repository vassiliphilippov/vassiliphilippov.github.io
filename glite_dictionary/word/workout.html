workout

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɜrkaʊt / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkaʊt / (BrE)
a period or session of physical exercise, especially to improve fitness or health
session of physical exercise

Examples

She goes to the gym for a workout every morning before work.
After yesterday’s intense workout, my muscles feel sore.
The new fitness app helps you plan your workout routines.

Synset Examples

the doctor recommended regular exercise
he did some exercising
the physical exertion required by his work kept him fit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exercise - activity requiring physical effort, especially to improve fitness

Etymology

Formed from 'work' + 'out', originally meaning to exhaust oneself through labor or effort; applied to exercise from early 20th century American English.
