cross-banded

1. ADJ

/ krɔs ˈbændɪd / (AmE), / krɒs ˈbændɪd / (BrE)
(of an animal, especially a snake) having bands or stripes that cross the back.
having crossing bands on the back

Examples

The cross-banded krait is easily identified by the alternating dark and light bands on its body.
Researchers studied the cross-banded coloration pattern to understand its role in camouflage.
The cross-banded snakes are often mistaken for other harmless species due to their similar markings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banded - having one or more bands or stripes
