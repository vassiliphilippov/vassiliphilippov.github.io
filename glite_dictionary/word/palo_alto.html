Palo Alto

1. PROPN

/ ˌpæloʊ ˈæltoʊ / (AmE), / ˌpɑːləʊ ˈæltəʊ / (BrE)
A city in California, United States, known as a university town and as the center of Silicon Valley.
city in California, USA

Examples

She plans to visit Palo Alto to tour the Stanford University campus.
Many technology startups have their headquarters in Palo Alto.
After moving to Palo Alto, he quickly adapted to the Californian lifestyle.

Etymology

From Spanish 'palo alto' meaning 'tall stick' or 'tall tree.'
