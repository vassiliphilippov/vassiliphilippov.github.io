communistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑːmjəˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɒmjʊˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of communism or communist principles.
relating to communism

Examples

The country adopted communistic policies after the revolution.
Her views were considered too communistic for the mainstream party.
The museum displayed several pieces of communistic propaganda from the early 20th century.

Synset Examples

Communist Party
communist governments
communistic propaganda

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communist - relating to or supporting communism

Etymology

From 'communist' + '-ic', modeled after 'communism'.
