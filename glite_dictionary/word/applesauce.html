applesauce

1. NOUN

/ ˈæpəlˌsɔs / (AmE), / ˈap(ə)lˌsɔːs / (BrE)
nonsense talk or writing; words without real meaning or value.
nonsense; silly talk

Examples

Everything he said about the project was pure applesauce and didn't make any sense.
Don't listen to that applesauce—stick to the facts.
She dismissed the rumors as nothing more than applesauce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - words or ideas without meaning or value

2. NOUN

/ ˈæp.əlˌsɔs / (AmE), / ˈap.əl.sɔːs / (BrE)
A sweetened purée made from cooked apples, often flavored with sugar and spices.
sweet purée of cooked apples

Examples

She served pork chops with a side of applesauce.
Homemade applesauce tastes much better than the store-bought kind.
You can use applesauce as a substitute for oil in many baking recipes.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: apple butter - a thicker, more concentrated apple spread
