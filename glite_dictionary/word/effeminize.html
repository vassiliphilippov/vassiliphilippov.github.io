to effeminize

1. VERB

/ ɪˈfɛmɪˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛmɪnaɪz / (BrE)
to give a person or thing a more feminine or effeminate quality or appearance
make more feminine

Examples

The director sought to effeminize the hero's appearance for the new adaptation.
Some critics argue that fashion trends have begun to effeminize traditional men's styles.
The character's gentle voice and delicate manners helped to effeminize his overall image.

Synset Examples

This hairdo feminizes the man

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to feminize - to make or become more feminine
  • Antonym: to masculinize - to make or become more masculine

Etymology

From Latin effeminare (to make feminine), from ex- (out) + femina (woman).
