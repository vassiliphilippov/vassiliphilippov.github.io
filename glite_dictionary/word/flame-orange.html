flame-orange

1. ADJ

/ fleɪm ˈɔrɪndʒ / (AmE), / fleɪm ˈɒrɪndʒ / (BrE)
of a bright orange color tinged with red, resembling the color of flames.
orange tinged with red

Examples

The artist used a flame-orange paint to depict the vibrant sunset.
She wore a stunning flame-orange dress to the summer party.
The flame-orange leaves signaled the arrival of autumn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orange-red - of a color between orange and red
  • Synonym: red-orange - a color between red and orange
