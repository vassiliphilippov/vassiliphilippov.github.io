pul

1. NOUN

/ pʊl / (AmE), / pʊl / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Afghanistan, equal to one hundredth of an afghani.
smallest Afghan currency unit

Examples

The price was less than a single pul at the local market.
He exchanged 100 puls for one afghani.
Few items are sold for just a pul nowadays.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: afghani - the main currency unit of Afghanistan

Etymology

From Pashto پول (*pūl*), itself from Persian پول (*pūl*, meaning coin or money).
