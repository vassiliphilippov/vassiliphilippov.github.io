bigger

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɪɡər / (AmE), / ˈbɪɡə / (BrE)
of greater size or importance than another; comparative form of 'big'
comparative of 'big'

Examples

Elephants are bigger than most other land animals.
This problem is bigger than I expected it to be.
She wants a bigger slice of cake than her brother.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: big - of considerable size, extent, or intensity
  • Degree Higher: biggest - superlative form of 'big' - of the greatest size or importance
