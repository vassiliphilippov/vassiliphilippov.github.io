Genovese

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʒɛnəˈviːz / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛnəʊˈviːz / (BrE)
of or relating to Genoa, its people, or their culture.
of or relating to Genoa or its inhabitants

Examples

The Genovese dialect is distinct from standard Italian.
Many Genovese families emigrated to South America in the 19th century.
She prepared a traditional Genovese pesto for the guests.

Synset Examples

the Genoese sailor we call Columbus

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Genoese - of or relating to Genoa or its inhabitants (variant spelling)

Etymology

From Italian 'Genovese', meaning 'of Genoa'.
