Coryphaena hippurus

1. NOUN

/ kɔːˌraɪfiˈænə hɪˈpɔːrəs / (AmE), / kɒˌrɪfəˈiːnə hɪˈpɔːrəs / (BrE)
A large, brightly colored fish found in warm seas, commonly known as the dolphinfish or mahi-mahi, valued as food and game fish.
