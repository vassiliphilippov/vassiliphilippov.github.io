zoic

1. ADJ

/ ˈzoʊ.ɪk / (AmE), / ˈzəʊ.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to animals or animal life.
relating to animals or animal life

Examples

The Cenozoic era is known for its zoic characteristics, with an abundance of new animal species.
Marine biologists study the zoic composition of coral reefs to understand biodiversity.
Some geological layers are classified according to their dominant zoic forms.

Etymology

From Greek zōion (animal) + -ic.
