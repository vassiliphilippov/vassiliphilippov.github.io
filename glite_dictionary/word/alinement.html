alinement

1. NOUN

/ əˈlaɪnmənt / (AmE), / əˈlaɪnmənt / (BrE)
an organization of people or countries united by a pact or treaty
group united by a pact

Examples

Several nations formed an alinement to address regional security concerns.
The Southeast Asian alinement was designed to promote peace and economic growth.
Joining the new alinement was a strategic decision for the developing country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alliance - an association formed for mutual benefit, especially between countries or organizations
  • Synonym: alignment - an association of groups choosing to cooperate or share a common policy
