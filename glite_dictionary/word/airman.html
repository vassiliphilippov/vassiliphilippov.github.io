airman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈeə.mən / (BrE)
a member of an air force or a person who pilots or assists in operating an aircraft, typically a man
a member of an air force or aircraft crew

Examples

The airman skillfully guided the plane through the storm.
Each airman on the base must complete regular training exercises.
As an experienced airman, he was selected for the important mission.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pilot - a person who operates the controls of an aircraft
  • Synonym: aviator - a person who flies an aircraft

Etymology

Formed from 'air' + 'man', first used in the early 20th century with the rise of aviation.
