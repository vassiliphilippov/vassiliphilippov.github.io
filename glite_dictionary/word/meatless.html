meatless

1. ADJ

/ ˈmit.ləs / (AmE), / ˈmiːt.ləs / (BrE)
(of food or a meal) not containing meat.
not containing meat

Examples

We prepared a meatless lasagna for the dinner party.
Many people choose meatless options for health or environmental reasons.
The school cafeteria offers a meatless menu every Monday.

Synset Examples

meatless days

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetarian - containing no meat; suitable for vegetarians

Etymology

From 'meat' + suffix '-less', meaning 'without meat'.
