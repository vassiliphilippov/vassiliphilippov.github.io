optimization

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːptɪməˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɒptɪmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something as effective, perfect, or functional as possible
the act of making optimal

Examples

The engineers focused on the optimization of energy use in the new building design.
Effective optimization of resources can significantly increase a company's profitability.
Mathematical optimization techniques are crucial in solving complex real-world problems.

Synset Examples

the simultaneous optimization of growth and profitability
in an optimization problem we seek values of the variables that lead to an optimal value of the function that is to be optimized
to promote the optimization and diversification of agricultural products

Related Terms

  • Synonym: improvement - the act of making something better
  • Synonym: maximization - the act of increasing something as much as possible
  • Antonym: deterioration - the process of becoming worse

Etymology

From optimize + -ation; 'optimize' from Latin 'optimus' (best).
