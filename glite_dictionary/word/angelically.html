angelically

1. ADV

/ ænˈdʒɛlɪkli / (AmE), / ænˈdʒɛlɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that is very beautiful, pure, or innocent, like an angel
in a way resembling an angel

Examples

The choir sang angelically, filling the cathedral with pure, clear notes.
She smiled angelically at the crowd, winning their hearts instantly.
The baby slept angelically through the entire night.

Synset Examples

the child was sleeping angelically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angelic - having qualities of an angel, especially beauty or innocence

Etymology

From 'angelic' (from Late Latin angelicus, from Greek angelikos, from angelos meaning 'messenger') + '-ally'.
