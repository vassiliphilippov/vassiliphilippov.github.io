panencephalitis

1. NOUN

/ ˌpænˌɛnˌsɛfəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌpanˌɛnˌsɛfəˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A diffuse inflammation affecting the entire brain.
inflammation of the whole brain
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with panencephalitis after exhibiting neurological symptoms.
Researchers are investigating new treatments for panencephalitis caused by viral infections.
Early detection of panencephalitis significantly improves the chances of successful intervention.

Etymology

From Greek 'pan' (all) + 'enkephalos' (brain) + '-itis' (inflammation).
