orange-hued

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔɹɪndʒ hjud / (AmE), / ˈɒɹɪndʒ hjuːd / (BrE)
Having the color or tint of ripe oranges; tinged with orange.
having an orange color or tint

Examples

The artist painted a stunning sunset with orange-hued clouds along the horizon.
She wore an orange-hued dress that made her stand out in the crowd.
The leaves turned orange-hued as autumn approached.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orange - a color between yellow and red; the color of oranges
