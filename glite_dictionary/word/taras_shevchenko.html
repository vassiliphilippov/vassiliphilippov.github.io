Taras Shevchenko

1. PROPN

/ təˈrɑs ʃɛvˈtʃɛŋkoʊ / (AmE), / təˈrɑːs ʃɛvˈtʃɛŋkəʊ / (BrE)
(1814–1861) A Ukrainian poet, writer, artist, and political figure, regarded as the national poet of Ukraine.
Ukrainian national poet (1814–1861)

Examples

Taras Shevchenko is celebrated for his artistic and literary contribution to Ukrainian national identity.
The works of Taras Shevchenko are studied in schools throughout Ukraine and beyond.
A statue of Taras Shevchenko stands in the center of Kyiv, honoring his legacy.
