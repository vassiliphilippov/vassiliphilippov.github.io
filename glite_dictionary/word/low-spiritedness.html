low-spiritedness

1. NOUN

/ ˌloʊˈspɪrɪtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌləʊˈspɪərɪtɪdnəs / (BrE)
a feeling of sadness or a lack of enthusiasm; the state of being low in spirits
a state of feeling sad or lacking enthusiasm

Examples

After losing the match, a sense of low-spiritedness hung over the team for days.
Her extended period of low-spiritedness concerned her friends and family.
Despite his usual optimism, he couldn't shake his low-spiritedness that winter.

Synset Examples

he felt responsible for her lowness of spirits

Related Terms

  • Synonym: melancholy - a deep, persistent sadness or gloom
  • Synonym: despondency - a state of low spirits caused by loss of hope or courage
  • Antonym: cheerfulness - a state of being happy or full of good spirits
