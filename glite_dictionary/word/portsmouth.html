Portsmouth

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɔrtsməθ / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtsməθ / (BrE)
a port city on the south coast of England, known for its historic naval base and maritime heritage.
port city in southern England

Examples

The historic docks in Portsmouth attract thousands of visitors each year.
She moved to Portsmouth to study maritime history at the university.
After touring London, we took a train south to visit Portsmouth.
