pissass

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪsˌæs / (AmE), / ˈpɪsˌas / (BrE)
a foolish, irritating, or contemptible person (slang and vulgar)
foolish or annoying person (slang, vulgar)
slang vulgar

Examples

Nobody likes working with a pissass who always causes problems.
She called him a pissass after he ruined the joke for everyone.
Why are you being such a pissass tonight?

Synset Examples

He acted like a real pissass at the party last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a stupid or foolish person
  • Synonym: jerk - an annoying or contemptible person (informal)

Etymology

From 'piss' (slang, contempt) + 'ass' (slang for a foolish person).
