dipsomaniac

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪpsoʊˈmæniæk / (AmE), / ˌdɪpsəˈmeɪniæk / (BrE)
A person who has a habitual and uncontrollable craving for alcoholic drinks; an alcoholic.
person with uncontrollable craving for alcohol

Examples

Her father was a known dipsomaniac who struggled to stay sober for even a single day.
The novel's protagonist, a former dipsomaniac, tries to rebuild his life after years of addiction.
His reputation as a dipsomaniac often overshadowed his accomplishments in the community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alcoholic - a person addicted to alcohol
  • Antonym: teetotaler - a person who abstains from alcohol

Etymology

From Greek dipsa (thirst) + mania (madness, frenzy).
