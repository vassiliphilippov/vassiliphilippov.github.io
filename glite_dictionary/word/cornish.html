Cornish

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɔrnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnɪʃ / (BrE)
of or relating to Cornwall, its people, or the Cornish language.
relating to Cornwall, its people, or language

Examples

She learned a few words in the Cornish language during her visit.
The Cornish coastline is famous for its dramatic cliffs and beautiful beaches.
Many traditional Cornish dishes feature seafood and fresh vegetables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Welsh - of or relating to Wales, its people, or the Welsh language
  • Synonym: English - of or relating to England, its people, or the English language
  • Synonym: Cornwall - a county in southwest England

Etymology

From Cornwall, a county in southwest England, plus the English adjectival suffix -ish.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnɪʃ / (BrE)
a Celtic language spoken in Cornwall, England, now revived after having become extinct as a vernacular in the 18th century
Celtic language of Cornwall

Examples

Many people are working to revive the Cornish language in Cornwall.
He is fluent in both English and Cornish.
There are now Cornish classes available for learners of all ages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Welsh - a Celtic language spoken in Wales
  • Synonym: Breton - a Celtic language spoken in Brittany, France

Etymology

From Cornwall, the region in south-west England, with suffix -ish.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06974421-n
Synset POS: n

3. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnɪʃ / (BrE)
a compact British breed of domestic chicken, primarily raised for meat production and for crossbreeding to produce roasters
breed of domestic chicken for meat

Examples

The farm specializes in raising Cornish for their high-quality meat.
Many commercial broilers are the result of crossing Cornish with other breeds.
A purebred Cornish has a broad, muscular body ideal for roasting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Plymouth Rock - another breed of domestic chicken, often used in crossbreeding

Etymology

Named after Cornwall, a county in southwest England, where the breed originated.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01793039-n
Synset POS: n
