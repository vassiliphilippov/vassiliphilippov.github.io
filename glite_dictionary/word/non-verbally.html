non-verbally

1. ADV

/ nɑn ˈvɜrbəli / (AmE), / nɒn ˈvɜːbəli / (BrE)
in a way that does not use words or speech; by means other than language
without using words or speech

Examples

They expressed their excitement non-verbally by cheering and clapping wildly.
The trainer uses gestures to communicate non-verbally with the dolphins.
Children often convey their feelings non-verbally through facial expressions and posture.

Synset Examples

they communicated nonverbally

Related Terms

  • Antonym: verbally - using words or speech
