Paraguay

1. PROPN

/ ˌpærəˈɡwaɪ / (AmE), / ˌparəˈɡwaɪ / (BrE)
A landlocked country in South America, bordered by Argentina, Brazil, and Bolivia; gained independence from Spain in 1811.
country in South America

Examples

After several weeks of travel, we finally arrived in Paraguay and explored its vibrant capital, Asunción.
The climate of Paraguay ranges from subtropical to temperate, making it suitable for agriculture.
Football is one of the most popular sports in Paraguay, and the national team enjoys strong support.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Paraguay', from the indigenous Guaraní 'Paraguái', possibly meaning 'river that gives birth to the sea'.
