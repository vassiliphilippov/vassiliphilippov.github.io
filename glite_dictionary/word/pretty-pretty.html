pretty-pretty

1. ADJ

/ ˈprɪti ˈprɪti / (AmE), / ˈprɪti ˈprɪti / (BrE)
Ostentatiously or excessively pretty, often in a way that is considered superficial or lacking in taste.
ostentatiously or excessively pretty

Examples

The decor was a bit too pretty-pretty for my taste, all ribbons and pastel colors.
She criticized the film for its pretty-pretty visuals that distracted from the story.
His pretty-pretty style appealed to some, but others found it overly delicate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: showy - excessively ornamental or attention-seeking in appearance
  • Antonym: understated - not intended to attract attention, restrained
