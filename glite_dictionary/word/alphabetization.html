alphabetization

1. NOUN

/ ˌæl.fə.bə.taɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌæl.fə.bə.taɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
The act or process of arranging words or items in alphabetical order.
arranging in alphabetical order

Examples

Proper alphabetization is essential when organizing a list of names for easy reference.
The library staff completed the alphabetization of all the new books yesterday.
Mistakes in alphabetization can make it difficult to find entries quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to alphabetize - to put in alphabetical order

Etymology

From 'alphabetize' + '-ation', based on Greek 'alphabētos' ('alphabet').
