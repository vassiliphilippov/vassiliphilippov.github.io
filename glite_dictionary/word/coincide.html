to coincide

1. VERB

/ ˌkoʊɪnˈsaɪd / (AmE), / ˌkəʊɪnˈsaɪd / (BrE)
to be the same in position, nature, or time; to correspond exactly or agree
to be the same or to agree

Examples

Their holiday plans coincide with ours this year, so we'll all be away at the same time.
The ceremony coincided with her birthday, making the occasion extra special.
My opinion coincides completely with yours on this issue.

Synset Examples

our views on this matter coincided

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to agree - to have the same opinion or to be consistent
  • Antonym: to differ - to be unlike or not the same as

Etymology

From Medieval Latin coincidere, from Latin co- (together) + incidere (to fall upon).

2. VERB

/ ˌkoʊ.ɪnˈsaɪd / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.ɪnˈsaɪd / (BrE)
to occur at the same time as something else, or to correspond in position or character
to happen at the same time

Examples

The celebration will coincide with the national holiday this year.
Her vacation plans coincided perfectly with his business trip.
The two events coincide in both time and place.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to concur - to happen at the same time; agree in opinion
  • Antonym: conflict - to clash or not occur together; be incompatible

Etymology

From Medieval Latin coincidere, from Latin co- (together) + incidere (to fall upon).

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: v

3. VERB

/ ˌkoʊ.ɪnˈsaɪd / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.ɪnˈsaɪd / (BrE)
(of events or circumstances) to happen at the same time.
happen at the same time

Examples

The festival will coincide with the start of the school holidays this year.
My birthday and my sister's graduation coincided last spring, making it a memorable week for our family.
Their vacations rarely coincide, so it's difficult for them to travel together.

Synset Examples

The two events coincided

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to concur - occur at the same time
  • Antonym: conflict - to happen at the same time as to cause difficulty

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'coincidere', from Latin 'co-' (together) + 'incidere' (to fall upon, happen).

Synset POS: v
