motionless

1. ADJ

/ ˈmoʊʃənlɪs / (AmE), / ˈməʊʃənlɪs / (BrE)
not moving; completely still
not moving; still

Examples

The rabbit sat motionless in the grass, hoping the fox would not see it.
During the performance, the statue appeared completely motionless for several minutes.
He stood motionless with shock after hearing the unexpected news.

Synset Examples

She remained motionless, yet poised to take flight

Related Terms

  • Synonym: still - not moving; motionless
  • Antonym: moving - in motion; not still

Etymology

From 'motion' + '-less', forming an adjective meaning 'without motion'.
