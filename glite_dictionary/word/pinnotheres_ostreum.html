Pinnotheres ostreum

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɪnəˈθɪəriːz ɒˈstriːəm / (AmE), / ˌpɪnəˈθɪəriːz ɒˈstriːəm / (BrE)
A tiny soft-bodied crab that lives within the mantle cavity of oysters.
tiny crab living in oysters

Examples

The Pinnotheres ostreum can often be found inside the shells of live oysters.
Marine biologists studied how Pinnotheres ostreum affects oyster health.
She discovered a Pinnotheres ostreum while shucking fresh oysters.

Etymology

From Latin; Pinnotheres is a genus name for pea crabs, ostreum meaning 'oyster'.
