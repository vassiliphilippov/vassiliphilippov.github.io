essonite

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛsəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛsənʌɪt / (BrE)
a variety of garnet that ranges in color from yellow to brown, also known as hessonite
a yellow to brown variety of garnet

Examples

The essonite in her pendant shone with a warm amber hue.
Collectors highly value essonite for its unique brownish-orange color.
He identified the mineral sample as essonite, a rare type of garnet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hessonite - a yellow to brown variety of garnet, alternative name for essonite

Etymology

From French *essonite*, alteration of *hessonite*, from Greek *hēssōn* (less), referring to its relatively lower hardness.
