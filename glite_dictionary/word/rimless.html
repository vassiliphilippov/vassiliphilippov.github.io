rimless

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪmləs / (AmE), / ˈrɪmləs / (BrE)
(of an object, especially glasses) having no rim or frame.
without a rim or frame

Examples

She preferred rimless glasses for their lightweight feel and modern look.
The rimless design made the artwork appear to float on the wall.
He admired her rimless hat, which gave a minimalist touch to her outfit.

Synset Examples

rimless glasses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frameless - without a frame
  • Antonym: rimmed - having a rim or frame
