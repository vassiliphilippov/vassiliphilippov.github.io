pericardiac

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɛr.ɪˈkɑr.di.æk / (AmE), / ˌpɛr.ɪˈkɑː.di.æk / (BrE)
Situated around the heart; relating to or affecting the pericardium (the membrane enclosing the heart).
around the heart or relating to the pericardium
medical

Examples

The surgeon examined the pericardiac tissues for signs of inflammation.
There was fluid in the pericardiac space, suggesting possible infection.
Imaging revealed a pericardiac mass requiring further investigation.

Synset Examples

pericardial space

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pericardial - relating to the pericardium

Etymology

From Greek 'peri-' (around) + 'kardia' (heart).
