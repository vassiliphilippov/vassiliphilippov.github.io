mindfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪnd.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnd.f(ə)l.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being attentive and aware, especially of one’s responsibilities or the present moment
attentive awareness, especially to responsibilities

Examples

Practicing mindfulness can help you become more aware of your daily tasks and responsibilities.
She approaches every project with mindfulness, ensuring no detail is overlooked.
Many people use meditation to develop greater mindfulness in their personal and professional lives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awareness - the state of being conscious of something
  • Antonym: carelessness - lack of attention or consideration

Etymology

From 'mindful' + '-ness'.
