moong

1. NOUN

/ muŋ / (AmE), / muːŋ / (BrE)
the small, round, edible seed of the mung bean plant, used especially in South Asian cuisine
seed of the mung bean plant

Examples

The chef added soaked moong to the salad for extra protein.
Sprouted moong is a popular ingredient in many Indian dishes.
You can make a healthy soup using moong and spices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mung bean - the whole mung bean plant or its seeds

Etymology

From Hindi 'मूँग' (mūṅg), referring to the mung bean.

2. NOUN

/ muŋ / (AmE), / muːŋ / (BrE)
the seed of the mung bean plant, commonly used as a food, especially in South Asian cuisine
seed of the mung bean plant

Examples

Many Indian dishes feature moong as a key ingredient for its nutritional value.
She made a healthy salad using sprouted moong and fresh vegetables.
Moong is often used to prepare dal, a staple in South Asian cuisine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mung bean - the edible seed or plant Vigna radiata, also called green gram

Etymology

From Hindi 'mūng', from Sanskrit 'mudga' (green gram).
