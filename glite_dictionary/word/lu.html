Lu

1. NOUN

/ luː / (AmE), / luː / (BrE)
The chemical symbol for lutetium, a trivalent metallic element of the rare earth group, usually found associated with yttrium.
symbol for the element lutetium

Examples

The element with the symbol Lu is used in certain specialized alloys.
Lu has the atomic number 71 on the periodic table.
Scientists discovered that Lu often occurs alongside other rare earth elements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lutetium - a trivalent metallic element of the rare earth group

Etymology

From Latin Lutetia, the Roman name for Paris, where it was first isolated.
