commonly

1. ADV

/ ˈkɑːmənli / (AmE), / ˈkɒmənli / (BrE)
in most cases; usually or generally
usually; generally

Examples

It is commonly believed that the Earth orbits the Sun.
Apples are commonly found in grocery stores throughout the year.
He commonly takes the bus to work, but sometimes he walks.

Synset Examples

usually she was late

Related Terms

  • Synonym: usually - under normal conditions; most often
  • Antonym: rarely - not often; seldom

Etymology

From Middle English 'comunly', from Old French 'communement', from Latin 'communis' (common).
