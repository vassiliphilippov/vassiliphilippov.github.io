psychosexual

1. ADJ

/ ˌsaɪkoʊˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
relating to the psychological or emotional aspects of sexuality or sexual development
relating to psychological aspects of sexuality

Examples

The theory explores the psychosexual stages of human development.
Her research focuses on the psychosexual effects of early childhood experiences.
Freud's ideas about psychosexual development have influenced many areas of psychology.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: psychological - relating to the mind or mental processes

Etymology

From psycho- (mind) + sexual; first used in early 20th-century psychology.
