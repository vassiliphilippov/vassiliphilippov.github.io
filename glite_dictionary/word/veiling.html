veiling

1. NOUN

/ ˈveɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈveɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
A light net of transparent fabric with a loose, open weave, often used for making veils or as decorative material.
light netted transparent fabric

Examples

The bride's dress featured delicate veiling that draped over her shoulders.
Lace and veiling are popular choices for hat adornments at formal events.
She purchased several yards of veiling to decorate the wedding hall.

Etymology

From 'veil' + '-ing', denoting material used for making veils.
