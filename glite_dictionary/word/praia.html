Praia

1. PROPN

/ ˈpraɪ.ə / (AmE), / ˈprʌɪ.ə / (BrE)
the capital city of Cape Verde, located on the southern coast of Santiago Island.
capital city of Cape Verde

Examples

We visited Praia during our trip to Cape Verde last summer.
Many government offices are located in Praia, the country's bustling capital.
Flights from Lisbon to Praia arrive at Nelson Mandela International Airport.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'praia' meaning 'beach'.
