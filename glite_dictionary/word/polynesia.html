Polynesia

1. PROPN

/ ˌpɑː.ləˈniː.ʒə / (AmE), / ˌpɒ.lɪˈniː.zi.ə / (BrE)
a region in the central and southern Pacific Ocean, consisting of numerous islands forming a triangle with its corners at Hawaii, New Zealand, and Easter Island
a group of Pacific Ocean islands

Examples

Many languages are spoken throughout Polynesia, reflecting its rich cultural diversity.
He dreamed of traveling to Polynesia to see its famous islands and clear blue waters.
The artwork of Polynesia is renowned for its unique patterns and symbolic motifs.

Etymology

From Greek 'polynēsos', meaning 'many islands' (poly- 'many' + nēsos 'island').
