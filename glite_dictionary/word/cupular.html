cupular

1. ADJ

/ ˈkjuːpjələr / (AmE), / ˈkjuːpjʊlə / (BrE)
shaped like or supporting a cupule
shaped like a cupule

Examples

The acorn was nestled in a cupular structure on the branch.
Certain moss capsules are enclosed within a cupular covering.
The botanist described the seed's cupular base during her presentation.

Etymology

From Latin 'cupula' (small cup), diminutive of 'cupa' (cup).
