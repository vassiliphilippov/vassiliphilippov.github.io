ouzel

1. NOUN

/ ˈuːzəl / (AmE), / ˈuːzəl / (BrE)
a European thrush, especially the common blackbird
European black thrush

Examples

A solitary ouzel sang from the hedge at dawn.
The ouzel can be identified by its distinctive black plumage and yellow beak.
We spotted several ouzels foraging in the meadow during our hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blackbird - a common European bird with all-black plumage

Etymology

From Old English ōsle, from Proto-Germanic *amslō (blackbird).
