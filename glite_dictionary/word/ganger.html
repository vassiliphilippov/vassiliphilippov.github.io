ganger

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæŋər / (AmE), / ˈɡaŋə / (BrE)
a person in charge of a group of workers, especially on a railway, construction site, or similar manual project
foreman of a group of workers

Examples

The ganger directed the team as they laid tracks along the railway line.
Each morning, the ganger assigned tasks to the group before work began.
As the ganger, she was responsible for the crew's safety and efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foreman - a person in charge of a group of workers
