accredited

1. ADJ

/ əˈkrɛdɪtɪd / (AmE), / əˈkrɛdɪtɪd / (BrE)
officially recognized or given authorization to carry out a particular function or activity
given official approval or recognition

Examples

The university is an accredited institution recognized for its high academic standards.
Only accredited professionals are allowed to perform these medical procedures.
He enrolled in an accredited online course to further his qualifications.

Synset Examples

an accredited college
commissioned broker
licensed pharmacist
authorized representative

Related Terms

  • Synonym: licensed - having official permission or approval, especially by a legal authority
  • Synonym: authorized - granted official permission or approval
  • Antonym: unauthorized - not having official permission or approval

Etymology

From Latin 'accreditare' (to trust), via French 'accréditer'.
