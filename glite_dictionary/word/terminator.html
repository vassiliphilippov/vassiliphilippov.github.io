terminator

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝː.məˌneɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.mɪ.neɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to exterminate troublesome rodents and insects, especially as a pest control professional
exterminator of pests

Examples

The company hired a professional terminator to rid the house of cockroaches.
As a licensed terminator, he specializes in removing rodents from urban buildings.
After noticing signs of termites, we called a terminator for an inspection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exterminator - a person whose job is to destroy pests

Etymology

From Latin terminator (one who sets boundaries); sense extended to someone who ends or eliminates something.
