Austin

1. PROPN

/ ˈɔstɪn / (AmE), / ˈɔːstɪn / (BrE)
The capital city of Texas, located on the Colorado River; a cultural and educational center and the site of the University of Texas.
capital city of Texas, USA

Examples

Thousands of new residents move to Austin every year for its vibrant music scene.
The University of Texas in Austin is one of the largest universities in the country.
They attended a technology conference in Austin last spring.

Etymology

Named after Stephen F. Austin, known as the 'Father of Texas'.
