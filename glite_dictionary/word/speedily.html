speedily

1. ADV

/ ˈspiːdəli / (AmE), / ˈspiːdɪli / (BrE)
in a quick or prompt manner; without delay
quickly; without delay

Examples

The documents were processed speedily by the clerk.
She moved speedily through the crowded airport to catch her flight.
The repairs were completed speedily to minimize inconvenience for the residents.

Synset Examples

he works quickly
John got ready in short order

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quickly - at a fast speed; rapidly
  • Antonym: slowly - in a slow manner; not quickly

Etymology

From 'speedy' + '-ly', based on Middle English 'spede' (success, quickness) and Old English 'spēdan'.
