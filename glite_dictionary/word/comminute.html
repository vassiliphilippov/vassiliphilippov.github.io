to comminute

1. VERB

/ ˈkɑːməˌnjuːt / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɪˌnjuːt / (BrE)
to reduce to small pieces or particles by pounding, crushing, or grinding
reduce to small pieces by grinding or crushing

Examples

The pharmacist must comminute the tablets before mixing them into a suspension.
Certain industrial processes comminute minerals to facilitate chemical reactions.
The stone mill comminutes the grains into a fine powder.

Synset Examples

grind the spices in a mortar
mash the garlic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to pulverize - reduce to fine particles
  • Synonym: grind - break down into small particles by crushing
  • Synonym: crush - press or squeeze forcefully to break into pieces

Etymology

From Latin 'comminuere' (to break into pieces), from 'com-' (together) + 'minuere' (to lessen).
