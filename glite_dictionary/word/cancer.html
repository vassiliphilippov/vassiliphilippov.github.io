Cancer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkænsər / (AmE), / ˈkænsə / (BrE)
A genus of crabs that is the type genus of the family Cancridae, including several edible marine species.
genus of crabs (family Cancridae)

Examples

The species Cancer pagurus is commonly known as the edible crab.
Marine biologists studied the distribution of Cancer crabs along the coast.
Several members of the genus Cancer are important to commercial fisheries.

Etymology

From Latin 'cancer' meaning 'crab'.

Examples
