snakebite

1. NOUN

/ ˈsneɪkˌbaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsneɪkˌbaɪt / (BrE)
a wound caused by the bite of a snake, especially a venomous one
bite or wound caused by a snake

Examples

If untreated, a snakebite can be life-threatening due to the venom's effects.
The doctor quickly examined the snakebite on his ankle and started treatment.
Many rural clinics keep antivenom on hand to treat snakebite victims.
