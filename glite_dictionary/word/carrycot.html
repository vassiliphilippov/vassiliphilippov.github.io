carrycot

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛriˌkɑt / (AmE), / ˈkærikɒt / (BrE)
a portable, box-shaped bed with handles for carrying a baby, designed for the baby to sleep in while being transported
portable bed for carrying a baby

Examples

The newborn slept peacefully in the carrycot during their afternoon walk.
She lifted the carrycot carefully into the back seat of the car.
They bought a lightweight carrycot for traveling with their infant.

Etymology

From 'carry' + 'cot'; 'cot' from Old English 'cotte' (small bed).
