riveting

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪv.ɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪv.ɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
so interesting or exciting that it holds your complete attention
extremely interesting or captivating

Examples

The documentary was so riveting that I couldn't look away for even a moment.
She delivered a riveting performance that captured the entire audience's attention.
His new novel is riveting from start to finish, full of twists and suspense.

Synset Examples

a fascinating story

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fascinating - extremely interesting and attracting attention
  • Antonym: boring - not interesting; making you feel tired or uninterested

Etymology

From the verb 'rivet' meaning 'to hold or fasten tightly', figuratively extended.
