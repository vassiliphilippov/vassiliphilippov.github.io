boringness

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔrɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˈbɔːrɪŋnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being boring; extreme dullness or lack of interest.
quality of being boring

Examples

The boringness of the lecture made it hard to stay awake.
He complained about the boringness of his daily routine.
Despite the film's boringness, she managed to watch it until the end.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tedium - the state of being tedious; monotony
  • Antonym: interest - the feeling of wanting to know or learn about something or someone

Etymology

From 'boring' (present participle of 'bore') + '-ness'.
