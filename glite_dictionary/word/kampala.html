Kampala

1. PROPN

/ kæmˈpɑːlə / (AmE), / kamˈpɑːlə / (BrE)
The capital and largest city of Uganda, situated on the northern shore of Lake Victoria.
capital city of Uganda

Examples

Many international organizations have established their headquarters in Kampala.
Tourists often begin their journeys through Uganda by flying into Kampala.
The vibrant markets of Kampala offer a variety of local crafts and goods.
