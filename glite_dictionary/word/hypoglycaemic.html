hypoglycaemic

1. ADJ

/ ˌhaɪpoʊɡlaɪˈsiːmɪk / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəʊɡlʌɪˈsiːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by hypoglycemia (abnormally low blood sugar) or its effects.
relating to low blood sugar
medical british

Examples

The doctor prescribed a hypoglycaemic agent to manage her blood sugar levels.
Symptoms such as dizziness and sweating may indicate a hypoglycaemic episode.
Certain medications can induce a hypoglycaemic state in diabetic patients.

Synset Examples

hypoglycemic agents

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hypoglycemic - relating to hypoglycemia (US spelling)

Etymology

From hypo- (meaning 'under, below') + glycaemia (presence of glucose in the blood).
