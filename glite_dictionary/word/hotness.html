3. NOUN

/ ˈhɑtnəs / (AmE), / ˈhɒtnəs / (BrE)

quality of being hot

Examples

The hotness of the desert sun made it nearly impossible to walk outside at noon.
You can adjust the hotness of the shower to suit your preference.
Scientists measured the hotness of the material after it was exposed to high temperatures.
