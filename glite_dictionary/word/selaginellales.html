an order of spore-producing vascular plants that includes the spikemosses, sometimes classified within the Lycopodiales
order of spore-producing vascular plants

Examples

Selaginellales are distinguished by their unique leaf and spore structures among vascular plants.

2. NOUN

/ səˌlædʒɪnəˈleɪliːz / (AmE), / sɛˌlædʒɪnɛˈleɪliːz / (BrE)
An order of spore-bearing, lycophyte plants which includes the genus Selaginella, sometimes classified within Lycopodiales.
order of lycophyte plants

Examples

The Selaginellales are unique among lycophytes for their scale-like leaves and specialized spores.
Research on the Selaginellales has provided insight into plant evolution.
Some taxonomists group the Selaginellales with Lycopodiales based on shared characteristics.

Etymology

From modern Latin, from Selaginella (the main genus) + -ales (taxonomic order suffix).
