el

1. NOUN

/ ɛl / (AmE), / ɛl / (BrE)
a railway powered by electricity that runs on tracks elevated above street level, especially in a city
elevated railway system
american

Examples

We took the el downtown to avoid traffic.
The noise from the passing el woke me up this morning.
Chicago is famous for its el, which circles the city center.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: subway - an underground electric railway system

Etymology

Shortened form of 'elevated railway', used especially in cities like Chicago and New York.
