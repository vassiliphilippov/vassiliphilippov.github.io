eyebath

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪˌbæθ / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.bɑːθ / (BrE)
a small cup with a curved rim designed to fit around the eye, used to wash or apply medication to the eyeball
cup for washing or medicating the eye
medical british

Examples

The doctor recommended using an eyebath to help flush out the irritant from her eye.
He filled the eyebath with saline solution and gently rinsed his eye.
After getting dust in her eye while gardening, she found relief with an eyebath.

Synset Examples

an eyecup is called ‘an eyebath’ in Britain

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eyecup - a small cup for washing or applying solutions to the eye
