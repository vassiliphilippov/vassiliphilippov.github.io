purposelessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜrpəsləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpəsləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of lacking any definite aim or purpose
lack of purpose or aim

Examples

There was a sense of purposelessness after he lost his job.
The movie captures the purposelessness that some young people feel today.
She struggled with feelings of purposelessness during her gap year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aimlessness - the quality of lacking direction or goal
  • Antonym: purposefulness - the quality of having or showing purpose

Etymology

From 'purposeless' + '-ness', where 'purposeless' is formed from 'purpose' (from Latin 'propositum', meaning 'a thing proposed') and the suffix '-less'.
