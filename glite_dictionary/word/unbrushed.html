unbrushed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbrʌʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈbrʌʃt / (BrE)
(of hair or clothing) not having been brushed; untidy as a result of not being brushed.
not having been brushed (hair or clothing)

Examples

There was a pile of unbrushed clothes at the foot of the bed.
Her unbrushed hair made her look as though she had just woken up.
He appeared in court in a wrinkled, unbrushed suit.

Synset Examples

snarled unbrushed hair
the suit was wrinkled and unbrushed, as if it had been slept in

Related Terms

  • Antonym: brushed - (of hair or fabric) that has been brushed
  • Synonym: uncombed - (of hair) not combed
