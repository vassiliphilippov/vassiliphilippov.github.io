2. ADJ

/ ˈɡælɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡælɪk / (BrE)
Relating to France or its people, especially characterized by French attitudes or culture.
relating to France or the French

Examples

The comedian's sense of humor had a distinctly Gallic flavor, making it popular in Paris.
She made a classic Gallic gesture by shrugging her shoulders at the news.
The restaurant offers several Gallic dishes prepared with authentic French ingredients.

Synset Examples

French cooking
a Gallic shrug

Related Terms

  • Synonym: French - of or relating to France, its people, or language

Etymology

From Latin 'Gallicus' (of Gaul), referring originally to ancient Gaul (now largely France).
