to bombinate

1. VERB

/ ˈbɑːmbɪˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɒmbɪˌneɪt / (BrE)
to make a buzzing or humming noise; to buzz or drone
to make a buzzing or humming noise

Examples

The bees began to bombinate as soon as the sun came out.
At dusk, the cicadas bombinate in the trees, filling the air with sound.
He listened to the old machine bombinate softly in the corner of the workshop.

Synset Examples

bees were buzzing around the hive

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buzz - to make a continuous humming or murmuring sound
  • Synonym: drone - to make a low, continuous humming sound

Etymology

From Latin 'bombinare' (to buzz), from 'bombus' (a buzzing or booming sound).
