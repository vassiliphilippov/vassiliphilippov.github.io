Sault Ste. Marie

1. PROPN

/ suː seɪnt məˈriː / (AmE), / suː sənt məˈriː / (BrE)
A city in southern Ontario, Canada, located on the St. Marys River opposite Sault Ste. Marie, Michigan.
city in Ontario, Canada

Examples

They visited Sault Ste. Marie during their summer vacation through northern Ontario.
The international bridge connects Sault Ste. Marie in Canada to its twin city in Michigan.
The winter in Sault Ste. Marie can be very cold and snowy.

Etymology

From French 'sault' (jump, referring to rapids) and 'Sainte Marie' (Saint Mary).
