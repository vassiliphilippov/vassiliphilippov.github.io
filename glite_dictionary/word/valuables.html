valuables

1. NOUN

/ ˈvæljuəbəlz / (AmE), / ˈvaljʊəblz / (BrE)
items such as money, jewellery, or other possessions that are worth a lot of money or are important to someone
valuable personal possessions

Examples

Please keep your valuables with you at all times.
The safe deposit box is for storing valuables like passports and jewelry.
She worried that her valuables might be stolen during the move.

Synset Examples

Make sure your valuables are safe in the hotel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: possessions - things that someone owns

Etymology

From valuable + -s, meaning things that are valuable.
