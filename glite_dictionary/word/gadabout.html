gadabout

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡædəˌbaʊt / (AmE), / ˈɡæd.ə.baʊt / (BrE)
a person who frequently seeks out amusement and social activities, often moving from place to place
restless seeker of amusement and social company

Examples

She became known as a gadabout, attending every party in the city.
During summer, the gadabout flitted from beach to beach in search of fun.
Although he enjoyed solitude, his sister was a true gadabout who loved meeting new people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: socialite - a person well-known in fashionable society

Etymology

From verb 'gad' (to wander about), combined with 'about'.
