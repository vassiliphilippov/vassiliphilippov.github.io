Jackson Pollock

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒæk.sən ˈpɑ.lək / (AmE), / ˈdʒæk.sən ˈpɒ.lək / (BrE)
(1912–1956) An American artist noted for pioneering the drip painting technique and leading the abstract expressionist movement.
U.S. abstract expressionist artist

Examples

Many art historians consider Jackson Pollock a revolutionary figure in modern American painting.
The exhibition featured several iconic works by Jackson Pollock, including his famous drip paintings.
Students studied Jackson Pollock to better understand the development of abstract expressionism.
