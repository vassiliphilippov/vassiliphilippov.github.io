kummel

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːməl / (AmE), / ˈkʊməl / (BrE)
A sweet, colorless liqueur flavored mainly with caraway seed, cumin, and sometimes fennel.
caraway- and cumin-flavored liqueur

Examples

After dinner, we were served a small glass of kummel as a digestif.
The herbal flavor of kummel makes it a unique addition to cocktails.
He brought back a bottle of kummel from his trip to Germany.

Etymology

From German Kummel, meaning 'caraway', ultimately from Latin 'cuminum' (cumin).
