unexpressive

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪkˈsprɛs.ɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪkˈsprɛs.ɪv / (BrE)
showing little or no emotion or expression in appearance or manner
not showing emotion or feeling

Examples

Her face was completely unexpressive during the tense meeting.
The actor's unexpressive delivery added to the character's mysterious charm.
Despite the exciting news, his response remained unexpressive and calm.

Synset Examples

deadpan humor
his face remained expressionless as the verdict was read

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expressionless - without any visible emotion or expression
  • Synonym: deadpan - deliberately impassive or emotionless in manner or expression
  • Antonym: expressive - showing feelings or thoughts clearly

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) and 'expressive', from Latin 'expressus', past participle of 'exprimere' (to press out).
