cunnilingus

1. NOUN

/ ˌkʌnɪˈlɪŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˌkʌnɪˈlɪŋɡəs / (BrE)
oral stimulation of a woman's genitals, especially the vulva or clitoris, as a form of sexual activity.
oral stimulation of female genitals
vulgar

Examples

She discussed the importance of cunnilingus in fostering intimacy within a relationship.

Open conversations about cunnilingus can help reduce stigma around female sexuality.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fellatio - oral stimulation of the penis

Etymology

From Latin cunnus (vulva) + lingere (to lick).
