unexcited

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪkˈsaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈsaɪtɪd / (BrE)
not feeling or showing excitement; calm or emotionally unaffected
not excited; calm or unaffected

Examples

Despite the announcement, she remained unexcited throughout the meeting.
His unexcited reaction surprised everyone at the party.
They spoke in an unexcited manner, as if nothing out of the ordinary had happened.

Synset Examples

made an unexcited appraisal of the situation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: excited - feeling or showing excitement

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'excited'.
