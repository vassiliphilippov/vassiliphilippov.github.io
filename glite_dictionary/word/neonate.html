neonate

1. NOUN

/ ˈnioʊˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈniːəʊneɪt / (BrE)
a baby less than four weeks old, especially one in a hospital or medical setting
baby less than four weeks old
medical

Examples

Most complications in the neonate are detected within the first few days of life.
The neonate required special care in the hospital's intensive care unit.
Doctors monitor the weight and health of every neonate closely during the initial weeks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newborn - a recently born child or animal, especially a human baby less than a month old
  • Degree Higher: infant - a very young child or baby, especially one under one year old

Etymology

From Greek 'neos' (new) + 'genos' (birth, kind).
