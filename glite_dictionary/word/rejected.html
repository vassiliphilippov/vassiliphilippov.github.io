rejected

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈdʒɛktɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈdʒɛktɪd / (BrE)
(of a person) having been refused or rebuffed, especially in the context of love or affection.
refused or rebuffed, esp. in love

Examples

Feeling rejected, he left the party early and walked home alone.
She confided in her friends about being rejected by her longtime crush.
After his proposal was rejected, Mark took some time to heal before dating again.

Synset Examples

jilted at the altar

Related Terms

  • Antonym: accepted - regarded positively or received with approval
  • Synonym: jilted - suddenly rejected or abandoned, especially by a lover
