renting

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛnt.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of paying money to use something, such as a house, apartment, or car, for a period of time
the act of paying to use something temporarily

Examples

Renting an apartment in the city can be expensive during the summer months.
She is considering renting a car for her trip to the coast.
With renting, you have the flexibility to move without selling property.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lease - the act of granting or being granted the use of something for a specified period and payment
  • Antonym: ownership - the act, state, or right of possessing something; holding property
