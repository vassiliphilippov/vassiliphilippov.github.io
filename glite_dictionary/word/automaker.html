automaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔː.t̬oʊˌmeɪ.kɚ / (AmE), / ˈɔː.təʊˌmeɪ.kə / (BrE)
a company or business that manufactures automobiles
company manufacturing automobiles

Examples

Several Japanese automakers are introducing new electric models this year.
The country’s largest automaker announced a major factory expansion.
European automakers face increasing competition from Asian brands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carmaker - company that manufactures cars

Etymology

From 'auto-' (automobile) + 'maker'.
