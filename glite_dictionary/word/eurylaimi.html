Eurylaimi

1. NOUN

/ ˌjʊrɪˈlaɪmaɪ / (AmE), / ˌjʊərɪˈlaɪmɪ / (BrE)
A group of passerine birds commonly known as broadbills, found mainly in tropical Asia and Africa.
group of birds called broadbills

Examples

Several species within the Eurylaimi display vibrant plumage and unique vocalizations.
The Eurylaimi are easily recognized by their broad, flat bills.
Researchers conducted a study on the nesting habits of various Eurylaimi in Malaysia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broadbill - a bird of the Eurylaimi group, known for its wide bill

Etymology

From Greek 'eurys' (broad) and 'laimos' (throat), referring to their wide bills.
