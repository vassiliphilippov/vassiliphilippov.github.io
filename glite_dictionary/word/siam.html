Siam

1. PROPN

/ siˈæm / (AmE), / siˈam / (BrE)
the former official name of Thailand, a country in Southeast Asia
former name for Thailand

Examples

The ancient capital of Siam was renowned for its golden temples and bustling markets.
When the country changed its name from Siam to Thailand in 1939, many people were surprised.
He studied the rich history of Siam before traveling to Southeast Asia.

Synset Examples

Thailand is the official name of the former Siam

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Thailand - current name of the country formerly known as Siam

Etymology

From Thai สยาม (Siam), itself of unknown origin, popularized in European languages in the 19th century.
