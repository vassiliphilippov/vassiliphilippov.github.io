standard-bearer

1. NOUN

/ ˈstændərd ˌbɛrər / (AmE), / ˈstændəd ˌbɛərə / (BrE)
a leader or prominent representative of a cause, movement, or set of principles
leader or representative of a cause

Examples

As the standard-bearer of the movement, she inspired many to join the cause.
The party chose him to be their standard-bearer in the upcoming election.
He is widely recognized as the standard-bearer for reform in the industry.

Synset Examples

As a standard-bearer, he represented our values proudly.
The organization selected her as their standard-bearer at the event.
She became the standard-bearer for environmental awareness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: champion - a person who vigorously supports or defends a cause
  • Synonym: spokesperson - a person who speaks as the representative of a group

Etymology

Originally referred to a person who carried the standard (flag) in battle; now used figuratively.
