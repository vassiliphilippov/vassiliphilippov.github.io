Steffi Graf

1. PROPN

/ ˈstɛfi ˈɡræf / (AmE), / ˈstɛfi ˈɡrɑːf / (BrE)
(b. 1969) A German tennis player who won seven Wimbledon women's singles titles and is regarded as one of the greatest female tennis players of all time.
German tennis player, Wimbledon champion

Examples

Steffi Graf dominated women's tennis in the late 1980s and 1990s with her powerful forehand.
Many young athletes look up to Steffi Graf as a role model in the world of sports.
In 1988, Steffi Graf achieved a Golden Slam by winning all four Grand Slam tournaments and the Olympic gold medal.
