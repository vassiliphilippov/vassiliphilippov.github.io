Tri-Delt

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraɪˌdɛlt / (AmE), / ˈtraɪˌdɛlt / (BrE)
an informal term for a member of the Delta Delta Delta sorority, chiefly used in American universities
member of Delta Delta Delta sorority (informal)
informal american

Examples

She always wears her Tri-Delt letters with pride on campus.
During recruitment week, being a Tri-Delt kept her very busy.
Many Tri-Delt alumnae remain close friends after graduation.

Synset Examples

She’s proud to be a Tri-Delt at her university.
As a Tri-Delt, she values sisterhood and support.

Etymology

Shortened and informal form of the full sorority name 'Delta Delta Delta.'
