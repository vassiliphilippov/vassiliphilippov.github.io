Connecticuter

1. NOUN

/ kəˈnɛtɪˌkjuːtər / (AmE), / kəˈnɛtɪˌkjuːtə / (BrE)

native or resident of Connecticut

Examples

As a proud Connecticuter, she always cheers for her home state's teams.
Many Connecticuters enjoy visiting the shoreline during summer weekends.
He moved to New York after spending his childhood as a Connecticuter.

Related Terms

Etymology
