kappa

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæpə / (AmE), / ˈkapə / (BrE)
the tenth letter of the Greek alphabet, written as Κ (uppercase) or κ (lowercase)
Greek letter; tenth in Greek alphabet

Examples

The Greek letter kappa often represents a constant in scientific formulas.
Her fraternity's name included the letter kappa at the beginning.
Learning how to write kappa is part of mastering the Greek alphabet.

Etymology

From Greek κάππα (káppa), from Phoenician kaph.
