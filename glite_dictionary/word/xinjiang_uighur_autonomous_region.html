Xinjiang Uighur Autonomous Region

1. PROPN

/ ɕinˈdʒjɑːŋ ˈwiːɡʊər ˌɔːtəˈnɑːməs ˈriːdʒən / (AmE), / ˌʃɪnˈdʒæŋ ˈwiːɡʊə ɔːtəˈnɒməs ˈriːdʒən / (BrE)
an autonomous region in far northwestern China, bordering Mongolia and Kazakhstan; the largest region in China and the homeland of the Uighur people
autonomous region in northwestern China

Examples

The Xinjiang Uighur Autonomous Region is home to a diverse population including many Uighurs.
There has been international attention on human rights issues in the Xinjiang Uighur Autonomous Region.
Tourists are drawn to the stunning landscapes of the Xinjiang Uighur Autonomous Region.

Etymology

Xinjiang from Mandarin 新疆 (Xīnjiāng, meaning 'new frontier'); Uighur from Turkic Uyghur; Autonomous Region is a political designation in China.
