beltlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛltˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɛltˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or shaped like a belt; encircling in form or appearance
resembling a belt in shape

Examples

The snake left a beltlike trail through the sand as it moved silently.
A beltlike region of clouds encircles the planet near its equator.
The injury resulted in a beltlike bruise around his waist.
