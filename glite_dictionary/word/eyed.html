eyed

1. ADJ

/ aɪd / (AmE), / aɪd / (BrE)
having one or more eyes or eyelike features, especially as specified; often used in combination.
having eyes or eyelike features

Examples

The artist painted a large, eyed butterfly on the wall.
Some species of fish are brightly eyed to attract mates.
We admired the peacock's beautiful eyed feathers.
