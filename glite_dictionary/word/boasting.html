boasting

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊstɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of talking with excessive pride and self-satisfaction about one's achievements, possessions, or abilities
talking with excessive pride about oneself

Examples

His constant boasting about his success annoyed everyone at the party.
Tired of his boasting, she left the conversation to find some peace.
Professional athletes often avoid boasting and let their achievements speak for themselves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bragging - the act of boasting or swaggering talk
  • Antonym: humility - the quality of having a modest view of one's importance

Etymology

From the verb 'boast', from Middle English 'bosten', possibly of Scandinavian origin.
