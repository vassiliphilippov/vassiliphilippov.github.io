mater

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈmeɪtə / (BrE)
(informal or facetious, chiefly British) mother; used especially by schoolboys or in a joking manner.
mother (informal, chiefly British)
informal british

Examples

During the holidays, I usually visit my mater in the countryside.
He wrote a long letter to his mater asking for more pocket money.
At school, some boys jokingly call their mothers the mater.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mother - a female parent

Etymology

From Latin 'mater' (mother).
