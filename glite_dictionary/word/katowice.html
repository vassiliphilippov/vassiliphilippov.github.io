Katowice

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɑː.təˈviː.tseɪ / (AmE), / ˌka.təˈvɪ.tseɪ / (BrE)
a city in southern Poland known for its industrial heritage and as a major cultural and economic center.
industrial city in southern Poland

Examples

Many international conferences have been held in Katowice due to its central location.
After moving to Katowice, she quickly found a job in the technology sector.
The skyline of Katowice reflects its transformation from a mining center to a modern city.

Etymology

From Polish Katowice, possibly from the surname Katowicz or related to the word 'kat' (executioner).
