Hrvatska

1. PROPN

/ xərˈvɑtskə / (AmE), / xrɑːt͡skaː / (BrE)
the Croatian name for Croatia, a country in southeastern Europe on the Adriatic Sea, which became independent in 1991
the Croatian name for Croatia

Examples

The country is known as Hrvatska in its native language.
Many tourists visit Hrvatska for its beautiful coastline and historical sites.
On official documents, Hrvatska appears as the official name for Croatia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Croatia - country in southeastern Europe on the Adriatic Sea

Etymology

Croatian endonym for 'Croatia', from Medieval Latin 'Croatia'.
