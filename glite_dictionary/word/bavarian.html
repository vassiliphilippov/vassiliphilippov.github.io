Bavarian

1. ADJ

/ bəˈvɛriən / (AmE), / bəˈveəriən / (BrE)
relating to Bavaria, its people, or their culture
of Bavaria or its people

Examples

She enjoys drinking Bavarian beer on special occasions.
The Bavarian Alps are known for their stunning scenery and hiking trails.
He wore traditional Bavarian clothing to the Oktoberfest celebration.

Synset Examples

Bavarian beer

Etymology

From Bavaria, a region in southern Germany, + -an.

2. NOUN

/ bəˈvɛriən / (AmE), / bəˈveəriən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Bavaria, a region in southern Germany
a person from Bavaria, Germany

Examples

Many Bavarians celebrate Oktoberfest with traditional clothing and music.
As a Bavarian, he is proud of his regional heritage and customs.
The Bavarian greeted us warmly and offered local delicacies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: German - a person from Germany

Etymology

From Bavaria (region in Germany) + -an (forming demonyms and adjectives).
