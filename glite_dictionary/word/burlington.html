Burlington

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɝlɪŋtən / (AmE), / ˈbɜːlɪŋtən / (BrE)
the largest city in Vermont, located in northwestern Vermont on Lake Champlain, and the site of the University of Vermont
largest city in Vermont, USA

Examples

Many students from across the country attend the University of Vermont in Burlington.
The city of Burlington offers beautiful views of Lake Champlain, especially at sunset.
In winter, Burlington hosts several popular festivals and outdoor activities.

Etymology

Named after the English town of Bridlington, sometimes known as Burlington.
