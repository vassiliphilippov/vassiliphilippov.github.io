limning

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪmnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɪmnɪŋ / (BrE)
a drawing or representation that outlines the forms or shapes of objects, especially in art or illustration
outline drawing or artistic depiction

Examples

The artist's limning of the landscape emphasized its delicate contours.
Medieval manuscripts often featured intricate limning around the text.
Her exhibition displays several impressive limnings of historical figures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outline - a line or set of lines that show the shape or outer edge of something

Etymology

From Middle English limnen (to illuminate a manuscript), from Anglo-Norman *limner, from Latin luminare (to illuminate).
