twerp

1. NOUN

/ twɜrp / (AmE), / twəːp / (BrE)
an annoying or contemptible person, especially someone regarded as silly or insignificant
an annoying or silly person
informal

Examples

Stop being such a twerp and help us carry these bags.
He always acts like a twerp when the teacher isn't looking.
Nobody wanted to sit next to the new twerp in class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - a contemptible or annoying person
  • Synonym: idiot - a stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intellectual ability
