newspapering

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpərɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or profession of gathering, writing, and editing news for newspapers; journalism for newspapers
journalism for newspapers

Examples

She decided to pursue newspapering after graduating from college with a degree in English.
His long career in newspapering gave him a unique perspective on the changing landscape of media.
Many traditional skills of newspapering remain relevant even in the digital age.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: journalism - the activity or profession of writing for newspapers or magazines or of broadcasting news

Etymology

Formed from 'newspaper' + '-ing', denoting the practice or profession related to newspapers.
