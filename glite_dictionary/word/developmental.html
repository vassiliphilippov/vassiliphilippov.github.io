developmental

1. ADJ

/ dɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntl̩ / (AmE), / dɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntl̩ / (BrE)
relating to the process of developing or the stage of growth or advancement in something, especially in biology, psychology, or education.
relating to growth or development

Examples

She specializes in developmental biology at the university.
There are many developmental stages that children go through.
Early developmental screening can help identify learning difficulties.

Synset Examples

developmental psychology

Related Terms

  • Synonym: development - the process of growing or causing something to grow or become larger or more advanced
  • Degree Higher: mature - fully developed physically; full-grown

Etymology

From 'development' + '-al', the adjectival suffix.
