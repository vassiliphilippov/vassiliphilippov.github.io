cretin

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɛt̬n̩ / (AmE), / ˈkriːtɪn / (BrE)
an offensive term for a person who is considered to be very stupid or lacking in intelligence; historically, it referred to a person with a medical condition causing intellectual disability.
offensive term for an unintelligent person
offensive vulgar

Examples

He acted like a complete cretin during the meeting, ignoring everyone's suggestions.
Don't be such a cretin; use your common sense for once.
She couldn't believe the cretin who parked in the disabled spot without a permit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a person of very low intelligence
  • Antonym: genius - a person of very great intelligence

Etymology

From French 'crétin' (a person with congenital hypothyroidism), from Swiss French, perhaps related to Latin 'christianus' (Christian), implying innocent like a Christian child.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkrɛtɪn / (AmE), / ˈkriːtɪn / (BrE)
an offensive term for a person considered to be very stupid or foolish
very stupid or foolish person (offensive)
offensive

Examples

He called me a cretin just because I made a small mistake.
Only a cretin would try to fix the wiring without turning off the power.
Sometimes he acts like a complete cretin when he's tired.

Synset Examples

Don't be such a cretin; think before you speak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Synonym: moron - a person of low intelligence
  • Antonym: genius - someone of extraordinary intellectual power

Etymology

From French créTin (now crétin), originally referring to a person with a congenital thyroid deficiency; later used as an insult.
