Moban

1. PROPN

/ ˈmoʊˌbæn / (AmE), / ˈməʊˌbæn / (BrE)
A trademarked antipsychotic drug (molindone) used to treat schizophrenia and related disorders.
antipsychotic drug for schizophrenia
medical

Examples

The psychiatrist prescribed Moban to help manage her symptoms of schizophrenia.
Some patients may experience fewer side effects with Moban compared to other antipsychotics.
Please inform your doctor if you are taking Moban or any other medication.
