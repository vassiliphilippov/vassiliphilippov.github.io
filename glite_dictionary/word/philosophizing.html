philosophizing

1. NOUN

/ fəˈlɑːsəˌfaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / fɪˈlɒsəˌfaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of discussing or reasoning about philosophical ideas, often in a speculative or superficial way
discussion of philosophical ideas

Examples

His endless philosophizing began to bore the rest of the group after a while.
The book is filled with entertaining philosophizing about the meaning of life.
She tends to drift into philosophizing when faced with difficult questions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to philosophize - to think or reason as a philosopher
  • Synonym: speculation - the forming of a theory without firm evidence

Etymology

From verb 'philosophize' plus suffix -ing, ultimately from Greek 'philosophia' (love of wisdom).
