yellow-gray

1. ADJ

/ ˈjɛloʊˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
of a grey color tinged with yellow; having both yellow and grey hues
grey tinged with yellow

Examples

The walls were painted a subtle yellow-gray that complemented the wooden floor.
Her cat had a soft yellow-gray coat that gleamed in the sunlight.
Clouds with a yellow-gray tint signaled that a storm might be coming.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gray - of a color between black and white, as of ashes or an overcast sky
  • Degree Higher: yellow - of the color between green and orange in the spectrum, like that of egg yolk or gold
