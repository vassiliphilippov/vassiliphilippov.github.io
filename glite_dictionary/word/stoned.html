stoned

1. ADJ

/ stoʊnd / (AmE), / stəʊnd / (BrE)
under the influence of drugs, especially cannabis, and unable to think or act normally
under the influence of drugs, esp. cannabis
informal

Examples

He looked so stoned that he could barely keep his eyes open.
They got stoned and listened to music all night.
It's dangerous to drive while stoned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high - experiencing the effects of drugs or alcohol
  • Antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol or drugs

Etymology

From 'stone' (n.), possibly alluding to being as inert or insensible as a stone.
