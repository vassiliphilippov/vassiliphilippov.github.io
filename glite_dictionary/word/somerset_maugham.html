Somerset Maugham

1. PROPN

/ ˈsʌmərsɛt ˈmɔm / (AmE), / ˈsʌməsɛt ˈmɔːm / (BrE)
(1874–1965) English writer of novels, short stories, and plays, known for works such as 'Of Human Bondage' and 'The Razor's Edge.'
English novelist, short-story writer, and playwright

Examples

Somerset Maugham is often praised for his clear and unadorned prose style.
Many critics consider Somerset Maugham's 'Of Human Bondage' to be his masterpiece.
The works of Somerset Maugham continue to influence writers around the world.
