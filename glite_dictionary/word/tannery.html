tannery

1. NOUN

/ ˈtænəri / (AmE), / ˈtanəri / (BrE)
a place where animal skins and hides are tanned to produce leather
place where skins are tanned

Examples

The old tannery by the river has been closed for decades.
Working in a tannery requires strict safety procedures because of the chemicals used.
New housing was built on land where a tannery once operated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leather factory - factory where leather products are made

Etymology

From 'tan' (to convert hide into leather using tannins) + -ery (denotes a place of activity).
