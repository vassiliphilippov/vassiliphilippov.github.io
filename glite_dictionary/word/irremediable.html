irremediable

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪr.ɪˈmiː.di.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌɪr.ɪˈmiː.di.ə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to remedy, correct, or put right; incurable or irreversible.
impossible to remedy or correct

Examples

The company suffered an irremediable loss after the factory fire.
His reputation was damaged by an irremediable mistake in judgment.
Some injuries may leave irremediable consequences for the victim.

Synset Examples

an irremediable error
irremediable defects of character

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incurable - impossible to cure
  • Antonym: remediable - able to be remedied or corrected

Etymology

From Latin 'irremediabilis' (not to be cured), from prefix 'in-' (not) + 'remediabilis' (able to be cured).
