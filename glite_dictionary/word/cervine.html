cervine

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɝː.vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.vaɪn / (BrE)
relating to or resembling deer, especially in appearance or characteristics.
relating to deer

Examples

Her delicate features gave her a distinctly cervine grace.
The animal’s cervine limbs allowed it to run swiftly through the forest.
Scientists examined the cervine skull for evidence of evolutionary adaptations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deerlike - resembling or like a deer

Etymology

From Latin 'cervinus', from 'cervus' (deer).
