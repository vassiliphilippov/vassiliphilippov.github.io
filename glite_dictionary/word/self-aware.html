self-aware

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlf əˈwɛr / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf əˈweə / (BrE)
conscious of oneself as an individual, especially of one's actions, feelings, or character.
conscious of oneself

Examples

She became self-aware during the meeting and questioned her motivations.
It's important for leaders to be self-aware about their strengths and weaknesses.
The robot in the story was designed to be fully self-aware and capable of independent thought.

Synset Examples

self-conscious awareness
self-conscious about their roles as guardians of the social values

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-conscious - unduly aware of oneself, often with embarrassment
  • Antonym: unselfconscious - not self-conscious; lacking awareness of oneself or one's appearance

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'aware', modeled after 'self-conscious'.
