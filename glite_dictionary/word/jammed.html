jammed

1. ADJ

/ dʒæmd / (AmE), / dʒæmd / (BrE)
extremely crowded or filled to capacity.
extremely crowded; full to capacity

Examples

The subway car was jammed with commuters during rush hour.
His phone was jammed with unread notifications after the concert.
The restaurant was so jammed on Saturday night that we had to wait for an hour.

Synset Examples

a suitcase jammed with dirty clothes
stands jam-packed with fans
a packed theater

Related Terms

  • Synonym: packed - filled to capacity; extremely crowded
  • Antonym: empty - containing nothing; not filled
