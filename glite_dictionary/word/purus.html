Purus

1. PROPN

/ pʊˈruːs / (AmE), / pʊˈruːs / (BrE)
a river in western Brazil, about 2,960 km long, which is a major tributary of the Amazon River.
river in western Brazil, Amazon tributary

Examples

The Purus flows through remote areas of the Amazon rainforest.
Many indigenous communities live along the banks of the Purus.
Scientists studied wildlife diversity in the Purus region.
