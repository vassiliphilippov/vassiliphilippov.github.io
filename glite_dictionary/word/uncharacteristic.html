uncharacteristic

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌkɛrəktəˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌkarɪktəˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
not typical of a particular person, thing, or situation; unusual or not characteristic.
not typical; unusual for someone or something

Examples

The manager's sudden outburst was quite uncharacteristic of him.
Her uncharacteristic silence made everyone in the room uneasy.
It was uncharacteristic for the dog to bark at strangers.

Synset Examples

a book uncharacteristic of its author

Related Terms

  • Antonym: characteristic - typical feature or quality of someone or something
  • Synonym: typical - having the usual qualities of a particular group or thing

Etymology

un- (not) + characteristic.
