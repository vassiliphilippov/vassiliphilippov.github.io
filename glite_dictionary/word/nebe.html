NEbE

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛn.iː.biːˈiː / (AmE), / ˌɛn.iː.biːˈiː / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point east of northeast; precisely halfway between northeast and east-northeast, at 56.25° on the compass.
compass point; one point east of northeast

Examples

The ship altered its course to NEbE to avoid the storm ahead.
The wind was blowing steadily from NEbE throughout the afternoon.
He marked the direction as NEbE on the old navigational chart.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: northeast - the compass point at 45°, between north and east
  • Degree Higher: east-northeast - the compass point at 67.5°, between east and northeast

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'northeast by east,' formed from the initial letters of each word.
