the shits

1. NOUN

/ ðə ʃɪts / (AmE), / ðə ʃɪts / (BrE)
an episode of diarrhea, especially as experienced suddenly or severely (vulgar slang)
diarrhea; an attack of diarrhea (vulgar)
vulgar slang

He called in sick because he had the shits again.
