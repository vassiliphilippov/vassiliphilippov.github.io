Surinam

1. PROPN

/ ˈsʊrɪˌnɑm / (AmE), / ˈsjʊərɪnæm / (BrE)
A country in northeastern South America, on the Atlantic coast; formerly a Dutch colony, it became independent in 1975.
country in northeastern South America

Examples

My friend traveled to Surinam to study its diverse wildlife and rainforests.
After gaining independence in 1975, Surinam established its own government.
The capital city of Surinam is Paramaribo, located on the Atlantic coast.

Etymology

From Dutch 'Suriname', after the indigenous Surinen people.
