unpleasingness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈplizɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈpliːzɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being unpleasant or disagreeable to the senses
quality of being unpleasant

Examples

The unpleasingness of the odor made it hard to stay in the room.
Many critics remarked on the unpleasingness of the film’s sound design.
Despite the unpleasingness of his tone, his message was important.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unpleasantness - the state or quality of being unpleasant
