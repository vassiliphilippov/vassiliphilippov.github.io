pebbly

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛb.li / (AmE), / ˈpɛb.li / (BrE)
covered with or containing many small, rounded stones or pebbles.
covered with small stones

Examples

The pebbly shoreline made it uncomfortable to walk without shoes.
We found a pebbly path leading to the river.
Her garden was filled with pebbly soil that drained well.

Synset Examples

landed at a shingly little beach

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shingly - covered with small, rounded stones (shingle)

Etymology

From 'pebble' + '-y', where 'pebble' derives from Old English 'papolstan'.
