ossiculate

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːsɪkjʊˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈɒsɪkjʊleɪt / (BrE)
relating to or associated with the ossicles of the middle ear
of or relating to the ossicles in the ear
medical

Examples

The surgeon carefully examined the ossiculate structure during the middle ear operation.
Hearing loss can result from damage to ossiculate components in the ear.
Research on ossiculate abnormalities helps doctors diagnose certain auditory conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ossicular - relating to the ossicles (the small bones of the middle ear)

Etymology

From Latin 'ossiculum' (diminutive of 'os', bone), referring to the small bones of the middle ear.
