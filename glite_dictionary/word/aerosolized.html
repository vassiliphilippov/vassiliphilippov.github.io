aerosolized

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛr.əˌsɑː.laɪzd / (AmE), / ˈɛə.rəˌsɒ.laɪzd / (BrE)
in the form of ultramicroscopic solid or liquid particles dispersed or suspended in air or gas
converted to or existing as an aerosol
medical

Examples

The medication was aerosolized for delivery via an inhaler.
Exposure to aerosolized chemicals can pose health risks in poorly ventilated areas.
Researchers studied the spread of aerosolized viruses in the laboratory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aerosol - a suspension of fine solid or liquid particles in a gas

Etymology

From 'aerosol' + '-ize' + '-ed'; 'aerosol' from Greek 'aēr' (air) + Latin 'solutio' (solution).
