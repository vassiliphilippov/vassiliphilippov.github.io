elsewhere

1. ADV

/ ˈɛlsˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈɛlswɛə / (BrE)
in or to a different place; at, in, or to some other place
in or to another place

Examples

If you can't find the book here, try looking elsewhere.
Many of the city's young people have moved elsewhere in search of jobs.
The answer to your question lies elsewhere, not in the textbook.

Synset Examples

he went elsewhere
look elsewhere for the answer

Related Terms

  • Antonym: here - in, at, or to this place or position
  • Antonym: everywhere - in or to all places

Etymology

From Middle English, combining 'else' and 'where'.
