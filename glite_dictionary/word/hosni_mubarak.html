Hosni Mubarak

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɑsni muˈbɑːrək / (AmE), / ˈhɒsni mjuːˈbɑːrək / (BrE)
(1928–2020) Egyptian statesman and military leader who served as the President of Egypt from 1981 to 2011.
President of Egypt, 1981–2011

Examples

Hosni Mubarak led Egypt for nearly three decades before stepping down in 2011.
The resignation of Hosni Mubarak marked a significant turning point in Egyptian politics.
Many Egyptians had mixed feelings about the legacy of Hosni Mubarak after his presidency ended.
