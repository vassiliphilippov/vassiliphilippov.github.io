toastrack

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊstˌræk / (AmE), / ˈtəʊstˌræk / (BrE)
a rack with slots or divisions for holding slices of toast upright, typically used at the table.
rack for holding slices of toast

Examples

The silver toastrack held six perfect slices on the breakfast table.
She bought a vintage toastrack at the antique market for her kitchen.
Please put the hot toast in the toastrack so it doesn't get soggy.
