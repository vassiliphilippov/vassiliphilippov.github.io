Oregonian

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔːrəˈɡoʊniən / (AmE), / ˌɒrɪˈɡəʊniən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the US state of Oregon
native or resident of Oregon

Examples

Her grandparents are proud Oregonians who have lived in Portland all their lives.
As an Oregonian, he is used to the region's rainy weather and lush forests.
The young Oregonian decided to move east for college but missed the Pacific Northwest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Washingtonian - a native or resident of Washington (state)
  • Synonym: Californian - a native or resident of California

Etymology

Formed from 'Oregon' (the US state) + '-ian' (denoting belonging or relation).
