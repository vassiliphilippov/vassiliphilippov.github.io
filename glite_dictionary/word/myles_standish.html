Myles Standish

1. PROPN

/ ˈmaɪlz ˈstændɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪlz ˈstændɪʃ / (BrE)
(1584–1656) An English military officer and leader who played a key role in the establishment of the Plymouth Colony in America, serving as an advisor and military leader to the Pilgrims.
English military leader of the Plymouth Colony

Examples

The story of Myles Standish is often included in American history textbooks.
Myles Standish played a crucial role in defending the Plymouth Colony.
Statues commemorating Myles Standish can be found in several towns in New England.
