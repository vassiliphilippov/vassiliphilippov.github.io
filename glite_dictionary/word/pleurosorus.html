Pleurosorus

1. NOUN

/ pluˈroʊsərəs / (AmE), / plʊəˈrɒsərəs / (BrE)
A small genus of terrestrial ferns native to regions such as Chile, Spain, Morocco, Australia, and New Zealand.
a genus of terrestrial ferns

Examples

The genus Pleurosorus includes several species of ferns adapted to dry habitats.
Researchers discovered a new species within the Pleurosorus genus in southern Australia.
Some Pleurosorus ferns are known for their ability to survive on rocky outcrops.
