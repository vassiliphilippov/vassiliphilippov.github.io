appetency

1. NOUN

/ ˈæp.ə.tən.si / (AmE), / ˈap.ɪ.tən.si / (BrE)
a strong feeling of desire or craving for something
strong desire or craving

Examples

After years of travel, his appetency for adventure seemed unquenchable.
The appetency for recognition can drive people to extraordinary achievements.
Despite his success, he always felt an appetency for deeper meaning in his life.

Synset Examples

an appetite for life
the object of life is to satisfy as many appetencies as possible

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appetite - a natural desire for food or a strong wish for something
  • Synonym: craving - a powerful desire for something

Etymology

From Latin appetentia (desire, longing), from appetere (to seek after).
