Kuwaiti

1. NOUN

/ kuˈweɪti / (AmE), / kuˈweɪti / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Kuwait
person from Kuwait

Examples

She met a Kuwaiti during her studies in London.
The Kuwaiti explained his country's traditions to the group.
Many Kuwaitis travel abroad for education.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kuwaiti - of or relating to Kuwait or its people (adjective)

Etymology

From Kuwait, the name of the country, with English suffix -i forming demonyms.
