unrepaired

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnrɪˈpɛrd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈpeəd / (BrE)
not fixed or restored after being damaged; having necessary repairs not yet made.
not repaired or restored

Examples

Their car remained unrepaired for weeks after the accident.
The report listed several unrepaired bridges as safety hazards.
Water leaked through the unrepaired roof during the storm.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: repaired - fixed or restored after being damaged

Etymology

From the prefix un- (not) + repaired (past participle of repair).
