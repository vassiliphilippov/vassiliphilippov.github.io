inexterminable

1. ADJ

/ ɪnɪkˈstɝːmɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnɛkˈstɜːmɪnəbəl / (BrE)
Impossible to destroy completely or exterminate.
impossible to exterminate

Examples

Despite numerous attempts, the inexterminable cockroach population thrived in the old building.
He regarded racism as an inexterminable evil within society.
Some myths are nearly inexterminable, persisting no matter how often they are debunked.

Synset Examples

some weeds seem inextirpable

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indestructible - impossible to destroy
  • Antonym: perishable - liable to decay or be destroyed

Etymology

From Latin in- (not) + exterminare (to drive out, destroy).
