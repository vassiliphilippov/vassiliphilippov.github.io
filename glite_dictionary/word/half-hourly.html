half-hourly

1. ADJ

/ hæf ˈaʊr li / (AmE), / hɑːf ˈaʊə li / (BrE)
happening or appearing every thirty minutes; occurring every half hour
occurring every half hour

Examples

The half-hourly news updates kept listeners informed throughout the day.
A half-hourly bus service connects the town to the airport.
Weather reports are broadcast on a half-hourly basis during severe storms.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: hourly - happening every hour

2. ADV

/ ˌhæfˈaʊrli / (AmE), / ˌhɑːfˈaʊəli / (BrE)

every thirty minutes

Examples

Weather updates are broadcast half-hourly throughout the day.

Related Terms
