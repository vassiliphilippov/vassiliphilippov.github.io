satisfactorily

1. ADV

/ ˌsætɪsˈfæktərəli / (AmE), / ˌsætɪsˈfæktərəli / (BrE)
In a way that is good enough to meet expectations or requirements.
in a satisfactory way

Examples

The repairs were completed satisfactorily and the customer was pleased with the results.
She answered all the interview questions satisfactorily and got the job offer.
The project requirements were met satisfactorily, so the team celebrated their success.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unsatisfactorily - in an unsatisfactory manner
  • Synonym: adequately - to a sufficient or acceptable degree

Etymology

From 'satisfactory' + '-ly', meaning in a satisfactory manner.
