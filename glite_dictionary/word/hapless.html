hapless

1. ADJ

/ ˈhæpləs / (AmE), / ˈhæpləs / (BrE)
unlucky or unfortunate, especially in a way that arouses pity
unlucky; unfortunate (arousing pity)

Examples

He felt like a hapless bystander, unable to help as the events unfolded.
The hapless villagers suffered greatly during the storm.
Despite her hapless attempts to fix the leak, the roof continued to drip.

Synset Examples

a hapless victim
miserable victims of war
the shabby room struck her as extraordinarily pathetic
piteous appeals for help
pitiable homeless children
a pitiful fate
Oh, you poor thing
his poor distorted limbs
a wretched life

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unlucky - having or marked by bad luck
  • Antonym: fortunate - having good luck; lucky

Etymology

From English 'hap' (luck, fortune) + -less (without).
