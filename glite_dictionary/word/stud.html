stud

1. NOUN

/ stʌd / (AmE), / stʌd / (BrE)
a vertical post, typically one of a series, that forms the framework of a wall in building construction
upright support in wall framing

Examples

The carpenter installed each stud precisely to ensure the wall was straight.
Electrical wiring was run through the studs before the drywall was attached.
He used a stud finder to locate the stud in the wall for mounting the shelf.

/ stʌd / (AmE), / stʌd / (BrE)
