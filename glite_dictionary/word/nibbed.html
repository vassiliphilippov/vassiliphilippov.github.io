nibbed

1. ADJ

/ nɪbd / (AmE), / nɪbd / (BrE)
(of pens) having a writing point or nib, especially of a specified kind.
having a specified writing nib (of pen)

Examples

She prefers writing with a broad-nibbed fountain pen for calligraphy.
The artist used a fine-nibbed pen to add intricate details to the drawing.
A nibbed pen gives you more control over your handwriting style.

Synset Examples

a broad-nibbed pen

Etymology

From 'nib' (the pointed end of a pen) + -ed (forming adjectives)
