Evenk

1. NOUN

/ ˈiː.vɛŋk / (AmE), / ˈiː.veŋk / (BrE)
a member of a Tungusic-speaking nomadic people widely spread across eastern Siberia, closely related to the Manchu.
member of a nomadic Siberian people

Examples

Many Evenk continue to follow traditional nomadic lifestyles and herd reindeer in Siberia.
The culture of the Evenk is characterized by a deep knowledge of the Siberian taiga.
An Evenk elder shared stories of his ancestors’ migration across the tundra.

Etymology

From Russian Эвенк (Evenk), referring to the ethnic group, ultimately from their own self-designation.
