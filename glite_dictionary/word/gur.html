Gur

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊr / (AmE), / ɡʊə / (BrE)
a family of Niger-Congo languages spoken mainly in southeastern Mali and northern Ghana.
a group of West African languages

Examples

The Gur languages are primarily spoken in several West African countries.
Linguists have studied the grammatical structures of the Gur family extensively.
Several distinct languages within the Gur group are mutually unintelligible.
