Singaporean

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪŋəˈpɔriən / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋəˈpɔːriən / (BrE)
a person who is an inhabitant or native of Singapore
inhabitant of Singapore

Examples

The city was crowded with both tourists and Singaporeans celebrating National Day.
My neighbor is a proud Singaporean who enjoys sharing his culture.
Many Singaporeans are multilingual, often speaking English, Mandarin, and Malay.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Singapore - a country in Southeast Asia

Etymology

From the name of the country Singapore + -an, a suffix forming demonyms and adjectives.
