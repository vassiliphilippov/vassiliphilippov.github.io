frangipanni

1. NOUN

/ ˌfrændʒɪˈpæni / (AmE), / ˌfrændʒɪˈpæni / (BrE)
A tropical American shrub or small tree of the genus Plumeria, with milky sap and large, fragrant, funnel-shaped flowers of various colors.
tropical American flowering tree or shrub (Plumeria genus)

Examples

The frangipanni in the garden filled the air with its sweet scent every evening.
She wore a garland of frangipanni around her neck at the tropical wedding.
The milky sap of the frangipanni can irritate the skin if handled without gloves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plumeria - The genus of tropical American trees and shrubs with fragrant flowers

Etymology

From Italian 'frangipani', name of a 16th-century nobleman associated with a similar scent.
