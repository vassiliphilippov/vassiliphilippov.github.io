peach-wood

1. NOUN

/ ˈpitʃˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈpiːtʃˌwʊd / (BrE)
a tropical tree with a prickly trunk whose heavy red wood yields a red dye and is used for cabinetry
tropical tree with heavy red wood

Examples

The durable cabinets were crafted from rare peach-wood found in the tropics.
Artisans value peach-wood for its ability to produce a rich red dye.
The prickly trunk of the peach-wood tree distinguishes it from other tropical species.
