Jacques Louis David

1. PROPN

/ ʒɑːk luːˈi ˈdɑːvɪd / (AmE), / ʒak lwi daˈvid / (BrE)
(1748–1825) A French neoclassical painter known for his support of the French Revolution and influential works depicting historical events.
French neoclassical painter (1748–1825)

Examples

The paintings of Jacques Louis David greatly influenced the course of European art.
Jacques Louis David supported the ideals of the French Revolution in his works.
Many consider Jacques Louis David the leading artist of the neoclassical movement.
