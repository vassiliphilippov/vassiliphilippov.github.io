LGBT

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛl.dʒiː.biːˈtiː / (AmE), / ˌɛl.dʒiː.biːˈtiː / (BrE)
an initialism referring to the community of people who identify as lesbian, gay, bisexual, or transgender, and sometimes more broadly including other non-normative gender and sexual identities
lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender community

Examples

Support for LGBT rights has increased significantly over the past decade.
Many schools have established LGBT organizations to support students of diverse identities.
The city hosted its annual LGBT pride parade last Saturday.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: LGBTQ - initialism including queer and/or questioning along with LGBT

Etymology

Initialism from the words lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender.
