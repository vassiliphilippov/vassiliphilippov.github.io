LGBT+

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛl dʒiː biː ˈtiː plʌs / (AmE), / ˌɛl dʒiː biː ˈtiː plʌs / (BrE)
Relating to the community of people who identify as lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, or other non-normative gender and sexual identities.
community of non-normative gender and sexual identities

Examples

The LGBT+ community hosts annual Pride events to celebrate diversity and acceptance.
Many workplaces now offer support groups for LGBT+ employees.
She is an activist who focuses on LGBT+ rights and visibility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: LGBT - community of lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender people

Etymology

Acronym formed from the initial letters of lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender plus others.
