telephonic

1. ADJ

/ ˌtɛləˈfɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈfɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving telephones or telephone communications.
relating to telephones or telephony

Examples

The company established a dedicated telephonic support line for customer inquiries.
Due to a telephonic outage, all calls were temporarily redirected to mobile devices.
Researchers conducted telephonic interviews with participants across several countries.

Synset Examples

telephonic connection

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telephony - the technology or operation of telephones

Etymology

From French téléphonique, from téléphonie (telephony), ultimately from Greek tēle (far) + phōnē (voice, sound).
