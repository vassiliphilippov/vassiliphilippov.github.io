hundred-and-tenth

1. ADJ

/ ˌhʌndrəd ən ˈtɛnθ / (AmE), / ˌhʌndrɪd ən ˈtɛnθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number corresponding to one hundred and ten, coming after one hundred and ninth in a sequence.
ordinal number for 110th

Examples

She finished in hundred-and-tenth place out of two hundred competitors.
Today marks the hundred-and-tenth anniversary of the city’s founding.
The library holds its hundred-and-tenth book club meeting this Friday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one hundred tenth - ordinal number for 110 (alternative form, especially US)
  • Degree Lower: hundred-and-ninth - ordinal number for 109
  • Degree Higher: hundred-and-eleventh - ordinal number for 111
