Bartholomew Roberts

1. PROPN

/ ˌbɑrθəˈloʊmju ˈrɑbɚts / (AmE), / ˌbɑːθəˈləʊmjuː ˈrɒbəts / (BrE)
(1682–1722) A Welsh pirate, also known as Black Bart, famous for capturing over 400 ships during his career in the early 18th century.
notorious Welsh pirate (1682–1722)

Examples

Bartholomew Roberts amassed a fortune by capturing hundreds of ships during the Golden Age of Piracy.
The feared pirate Bartholomew Roberts was eventually killed in battle off the coast of West Africa.
History books often describe Bartholomew Roberts as one of the most successful pirates of all time.
