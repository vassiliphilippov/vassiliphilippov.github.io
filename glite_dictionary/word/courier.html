courier

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊriər / (AmE), / ˈkʊəriə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to carry and deliver messages, packages, or documents
person who delivers messages or packages

Examples

She worked as a courier in the city, delivering documents to law firms.
A bicycle courier raced through the busy streets to make his delivery on time.
During the war, a secret courier carried messages between the two countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: messenger - a person who carries messages or announcements
  • Synonym: carrier - a person or thing that carries or transports something

Etymology

From Old French 'courrier', from Latin 'currere' (to run).
