flame-coloured

1. ADJ

/ fleɪm ˈkʌlɚd / (AmE), / fleɪm ˈkʌləd / (BrE)
having a bright orange-red color similar to that of flames
bright orange-red in color
british

Examples

She wore a flame-coloured scarf that brightened the whole room.
The artist chose flame-coloured paint for the sunset.
Autumn leaves turn a stunning flame-coloured before they fall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fiery - having the color or quality of fire
  • Degree Lower: red - having the color of blood or fire
  • Synonym: scarlet - a brilliant red color
