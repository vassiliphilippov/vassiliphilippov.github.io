inaccurate

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈækjərət / (AmE), / ɪnˈækjʊərət / (BrE)
Not correct or precise; containing errors or mistakes.
not correct; containing errors

Examples

The report was criticized for being inaccurate in several key details.
If the data is inaccurate, the results of the experiment may be flawed.
He gave an inaccurate account of what had happened.

Synset Examples

an inaccurate translation
the thermometer is inaccurate

Related Terms

  • Antonym: accurate - free from error; correct or precise
  • Synonym: erroneous - containing error; mistaken

Etymology

From Latin 'inaccuratus' (not done with care), from 'in-' (not) + 'accuratus' (done with care).
