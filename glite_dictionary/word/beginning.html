beginning

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈɡɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈɡɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of starting something; the point in time or stage at which something starts.
the act or point of starting

Examples

The beginning of the ceremony was delayed due to rain.
Every story has a beginning, a middle, and an end.
At the beginning of the year, many people make resolutions.

Synset Examples

he was responsible for the beginning of negotiations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: start - the act of beginning something
  • Antonym: end - the final part or termination of something

Etymology

From Old English beginnan (to begin).
