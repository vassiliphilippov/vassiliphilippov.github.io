bialy

1. NOUN

/ biˈɑli / (AmE), / biˈɑːli / (BrE)
A flat, round bread roll with a crusty bottom, typically topped with chopped onions and sometimes poppy seeds, originating from Poland.
flat round onion-topped bread roll

Examples

I picked up a fresh bialy from the bakery for breakfast.
The menu offers a smoked salmon sandwich served on a toasted bialy.
She prefers a bialy with cream cheese over a bagel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bagel - a dense ring-shaped bread roll, often boiled before baking

Etymology

From Yiddish *bialy*, short for *bialystoker kuchen* (Białystok cake), after Białystok, a city in Poland.
