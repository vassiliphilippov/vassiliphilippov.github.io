ignitible

1. ADJ

/ ɪɡˈnaɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ɪɡˈnɪtɪbəl / (BrE)
capable of being ignited; able to catch fire and burn.
capable of being ignited

Examples

Keep ignitible materials away from open flames to prevent accidents.
The laboratory stores all ignitible substances in special safety cabinets.
Waste containing ignitible chemicals must be disposed of according to hazardous materials guidelines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flammable - easily set on fire; combustible
  • Synonym: inflammable - easily set on fire
  • Antonym: nonflammable - not capable of burning

Etymology

From Latin ignis (fire) + -ible (able to be).
