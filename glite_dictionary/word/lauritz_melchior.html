Lauritz Melchior

1. PROPN

/ ˈlaʊrɪts ˈmɛlkjɔr / (AmE), / ˈlaʊrɪts ˈmɛlkjɔː / (BrE)
(1890–1973) A Danish-born operatic tenor noted for his performances in Wagnerian roles, particularly in the United States.
Danish-born operatic tenor, Wagner specialist

Examples

Many critics consider Lauritz Melchior the greatest Wagnerian tenor of the 20th century.
Recordings of Lauritz Melchior continue to inspire opera enthusiasts around the world.
Lauritz Melchior performed at the Metropolitan Opera for over two decades.
