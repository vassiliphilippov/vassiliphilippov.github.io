carroty

1. ADJ

/ ˈkæɹəti / (AmE), / ˈkæɹəti / (BrE)
having a bright orange color, like that of a carrot, especially used to describe hair.
bright orange, like a carrot

Examples

His carroty hair made him stand out in the crowd.
She wore a carroty sweater that brightened up the room.
The fox's fur had a distinctly carroty hue in the sunlight.

Synset Examples

a boy with carroty hair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ginger - hair color ranging from orange-red to auburn

Etymology

From 'carrot' + '-y', meaning 'like a carrot'.
