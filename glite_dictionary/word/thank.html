to thank

1. VERB

/ θæŋk / (AmE), / θæŋk / (BrE)
to express gratitude or show appreciation to someone
express gratitude to someone

Examples

I would like to thank everyone for their hard work on this project.
She forgot to thank him for the thoughtful gift he gave her.
We should always thank those who help us in difficult times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to appreciate - recognize the value or significance of something or someone
  • Antonym: blame - assign responsibility for a fault or wrong

Etymology

From Old English 'pancian', from 'panc' (thought, gratitude).
