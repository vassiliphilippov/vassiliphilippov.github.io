unsugared

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsʌɡɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsʌɡəd / (BrE)
(of food or drink) with no sugar added.
without sugar added

Examples

He prefers to drink his coffee unsugared, enjoying the natural bitterness.
The nutritionist recommended unsugared cereals as part of a healthier diet.
You can order your iced tea unsugared and add your own sweetener if you wish.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sweetened - with sugar or another sweetener added
