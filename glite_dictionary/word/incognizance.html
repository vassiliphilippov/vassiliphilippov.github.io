incognizance

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈkɑːɡnɪzəns / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒɡnɪzəns / (BrE)
A lack of knowledge or awareness; not being conscious or mindful of something.
lack of knowledge or awareness

Examples

His complete incognizance of the consequences led to disastrous results.
The company's incognizance of local customs caused tension with the community.
She acted in incognizance of the true nature of the agreement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ignorance - lack of knowledge or awareness
  • Antonym: awareness - knowledge or perception of a situation or fact

Etymology

From Latin prefix in- (not) + cognizance (knowledge, awareness).
