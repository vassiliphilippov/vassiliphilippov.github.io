locality

1. NOUN

/ loʊˈkælɪti / (AmE), / ləʊˈkælɪti / (BrE)
an area or region near or surrounding a particular place; a neighborhood or district.
a nearby area or district

Examples

The museum is located in a quiet locality just outside the city center.
Several rare plant species can be found only in this locality.
After moving to a new locality, it took her a while to make friends.

Synset Examples

the plane crashed in the vicinity of Asheville
it is a rugged locality
he always blames someone else in the immediate neighborhood
I will drop in on you the next time I am in this neck of the woods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vicinity - the area near or surrounding a particular place
  • Synonym: neighborhood - a district or area in a city or town

Etymology

From Latin 'localitas', from 'locus' (place).
