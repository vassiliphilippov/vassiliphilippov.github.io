James Marshall Hendrix

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒeɪmz ˈmɑrʃəl ˈhɛndrɪks / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪmz ˈmɑːʃəl ˈhɛndrɪks / (BrE)
(1942–1970) United States guitarist whose innovative technique and use of electric guitars revolutionized rock music.
influential American rock guitarist (1942–1970)

Examples

Many consider James Marshall Hendrix to be the greatest electric guitarist in history.
The music of James Marshall Hendrix continues to inspire artists decades after his passing.
A tribute concert featured several of James Marshall Hendrix's most famous songs.
