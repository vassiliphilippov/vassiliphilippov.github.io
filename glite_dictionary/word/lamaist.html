Lamaist

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑː.maɪ.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈlɑː.mə.ɪst / (BrE)
A follower or adherent of Lamaism, the form of Tibetan Buddhism led by lamas.
adherent of Lamaism (Tibetan Buddhism)

Examples

As a devoted Lamaist, she traveled to Lhasa to study with the monks.
The Lamaist tradition places great importance on meditation and ritual.
Many Lamaists participate in elaborate ceremonies at Tibetan monasteries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Buddhist - a follower of Buddhism

Etymology

From 'lama' (Tibetan Buddhist teacher) + -ist (denoting follower).
