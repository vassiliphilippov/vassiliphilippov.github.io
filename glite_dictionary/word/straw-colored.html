straw-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrɔːˌkʌlərd / (AmE), / ˈstrɔːˌkʌləd / (BrE)
Having a pale yellow color, similar to that of dry straw.
pale yellow color like dry straw

Examples

The curtains in the living room were a soft, straw-colored fabric that brightened the space.
She wore a straw-colored dress that matched the sunflowers in the garden.
The field was full of straw-colored grasses swaying gently in the breeze.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: yellow - color between green and orange in the spectrum, resembling that of ripe lemons or gold
  • Degree Higher: golden - having the color or appearance of gold
