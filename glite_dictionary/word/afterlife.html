afterlife

1. NOUN

/ ˈæftɚˌlaɪf / (AmE), / ˈɑːftəˌlaɪf / (BrE)
the existence or state of life that is believed to occur after physical death
life after death

Examples

Many religions offer different visions of the afterlife to explain what happens when we die.
He was comforted by his belief in the afterlife during difficult times.
Debates about the nature of the afterlife have fascinated philosophers for centuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hereafter - the afterlife or a future existence beyond this life
  • Synonym: reincarnation - the belief in being born again after death
  • Antonym: death - the end of life; contrasts with afterlife

Etymology

Formed from 'after' + 'life' in English, first used in the early 19th century.
