floricultural

1. ADJ

/ ˌflɔːrəˈkʌltʃərəl / (AmE), / ˌflɒrɪˈkʌltʃərəl / (BrE)
Relating to the cultivation and management of flowering plants, especially for decorative purposes.
relating to flower cultivation

Examples

The college offers a floricultural program for aspiring horticulturists.
She attended a floricultural exhibition to learn about new varieties of roses.
Advances in floricultural science have led to longer-lasting blooms in bouquets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: floriculture - the cultivation and management of flowering and ornamental plants

Etymology

From 'floriculture' (Latin 'flos, floris' meaning 'flower' + 'culture' from Latin 'cultura'), with the suffix '-al'.
