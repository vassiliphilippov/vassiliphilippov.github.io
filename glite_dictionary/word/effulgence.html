effulgence

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈfʌldʒəns / (AmE), / ɪˈfʌldʒəns / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely bright and shining; brilliance or radiance
brilliant radiance; dazzling brightness

Examples

The effulgence of the rising sun filled the sky with golden light.
Her smile had a rare effulgence that lit up the entire room.
Astronomers marveled at the effulgence of the distant supernova.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiance - bright and shining quality
  • Antonym: dullness - lack of brightness or shine

Etymology

From Latin effulgentia (brightness), from effulgere (to shine out).
