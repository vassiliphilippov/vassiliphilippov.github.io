wearied

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪr.iɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɪə.rɪd / (BrE)
very tired or exhausted, especially after physical or mental effort.
tired; exhausted

Examples

After a long day of hiking, she felt utterly wearied by the time they reached their campsite.
The wearied soldiers finally rested after marching through the night.
He gave a wearied sigh, showing how much the endless meetings had drained him.

Synset Examples

my father's words had left me jaded and depressed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weary - feeling or showing tiredness
  • Synonym: exhausted - extremely tired
  • Antonym: refreshed - feeling rejuvenated or rested

Etymology

Past participle of "weary", from Old English *wērig*.
