loweringly

1. ADV

/ ˈloʊərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈləʊərɪŋli / (BrE)
In a menacing or scowling manner.
in a menacing or scowling way

Examples

He glanced loweringly at the intruder, making his intentions clear.
The sky hung loweringly over the quiet, anxious town.
She spoke loweringly, her eyes narrowed in disapproval.

Synset Examples

he looked up loweringly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: threateningly - in a way that expresses a threat or menace
  • Synonym: menacingly - in a menacing manner
  • Antonym: pleasantly - in a pleasant manner

Etymology

From 'lowering' (to look dark or sullen) + '-ly'.
