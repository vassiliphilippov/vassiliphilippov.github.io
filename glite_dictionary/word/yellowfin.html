yellowfin

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛlˌoʊˌfɪn / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊ.fɪn / (BrE)
A large species of tuna with yellowish fins, found in tropical oceans worldwide.
a large species of tuna with yellow fins

Examples

Fishermen were thrilled to catch a yellowfin off the coast of Hawaii.
The restaurant specializes in grilled yellowfin served with a citrus sauce.
Yellowfin are renowned for their speed and fighting strength when hooked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellowfin tuna - another name for the yellowfin fish
