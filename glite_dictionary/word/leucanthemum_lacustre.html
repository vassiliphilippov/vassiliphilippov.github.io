Leucanthemum lacustre

1. PROPN

/ luːˈkænθɪməm ləˈkʌstri / (AmE), / luːˈkænθɪməm ləˈkʌstri / (BrE)
A perennial plant species native to Portugal, similar in appearance to the oxeye daisy.
a Portuguese daisy-like perennial plant species

Examples

The wet meadows along the Portuguese coast are home to Leucanthemum lacustre.
Gardeners admire Leucanthemum lacustre for its daisy-like blossoms and resilience.
Unlike the common oxeye daisy, Leucanthemum lacustre prefers slightly wetter habitats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oxeye daisy - a common European daisy, closely related species

Etymology

From Latin: genus 'Leucanthemum' meaning 'white flower', and 'lacustre' meaning 'of lakes'.
