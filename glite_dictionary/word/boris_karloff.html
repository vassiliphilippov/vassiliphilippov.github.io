Boris Karloff

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɔrɪs ˈkɑrˌlɔf / (AmE), / ˈbɒrɪs ˈkɑːlɒf / (BrE)
(1887–1969) English-born American film actor known for his iconic roles in horror films, especially as Frankenstein's monster.
English-born American actor in horror films

Examples

Boris Karloff became world-famous for portraying Frankenstein's monster in the 1931 film.
Many horror film fans consider Boris Karloff the definitive actor for classic monster roles.
The museum features a special exhibit dedicated to Boris Karloff and his contributions to cinema.
