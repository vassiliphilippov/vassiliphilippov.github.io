therapeutical

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɛr.əˈpjuː.tɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌθɛr.əˈpjuː.tɪ.kəl / (BrE)
Relating to the treatment of disease or disorders by remedial agents or methods; pertaining to therapy.
relating to therapy or treatment

Examples

The doctor recommended a therapeutical intervention for her anxiety symptoms.
Several therapeutical approaches are being tested in the new clinical trial.
His research focuses on the therapeutical use of natural compounds in cancer treatment.

Synset Examples

therapeutic approach to criminality

Related Terms

  • Synonym: therapeutic - relating to the treatment of disease or healing

Etymology

From Late Latin therapeuticus, from Greek therapeutikos (inclined to serve, good for treatment), with English -al suffix.
