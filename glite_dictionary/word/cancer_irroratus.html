Cancer irroratus

1. NOUN

/ ˈkænsər ɪˌrɔːˈreɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈkænsə ɪˌrɒˈreɪtəs / (BrE)
A species of crab found along the eastern coast of North America, commonly known as the Atlantic rock crab.
species of crab of eastern North America

Examples

The Cancer irroratus is commonly found in the rocky coastal waters of New England.
Commercial fisheries often target Cancer irroratus for its meat.
Marine biologists study the lifecycle of Cancer irroratus to monitor ecosystem health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Atlantic rock crab - common name for Cancer irroratus

Etymology

From Latin: 'Cancer' meaning 'crab' and 'irroratus' meaning 'sprinkled' or 'spotted'.
