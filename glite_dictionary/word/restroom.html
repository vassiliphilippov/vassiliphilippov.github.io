restroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛstˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈrɛstrʊm / (BrE)
A room or public facility with toilets and sinks for use by the public; a public toilet.
public toilet facility
american

Examples

Could you tell me where the nearest restroom is?
The restroom was clean and well-stocked with supplies.
She waited outside the restroom for her friend to finish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lavatory - another term for a room with a toilet and sink
  • Synonym: toilet - a fixture or room for urination and defecation
  • Synonym: bathroom - (US) often used for a room containing a toilet and sink
  • Synonym: public convenience - British term for a public toilet

Etymology

From 'rest' + 'room'; first used in early 20th-century America as a euphemism for toilet.
