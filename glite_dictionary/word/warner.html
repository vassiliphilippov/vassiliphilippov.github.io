warner

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔrnər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːnə / (BrE)
a person who gives a warning to others
someone who gives a warning

Examples

The warner shouted loudly, alerting the villagers to the approaching storm.
History remembers him as a warner whose advice was sadly ignored.
Every group needs a warner—someone who notices trouble before anyone else.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to warn - to inform someone in advance of possible danger or problem

Etymology

From the verb 'warn' + -er suffix.
