motorcycling

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊtərˌsaɪklɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəˌsaɪklɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or sport of riding motorcycles
riding motorcycles as a sport or activity

Examples

He took up motorcycling to explore the countryside after work.
Motorcycling requires proper safety gear, such as a helmet and jacket.
Many enthusiasts believe that motorcycling is more than just transportation; it's a lifestyle.

Synset Examples

motorcycling is a dangerous sport

Related Terms
