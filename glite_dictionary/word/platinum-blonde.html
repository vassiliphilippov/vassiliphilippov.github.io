platinum-blonde

1. ADJ

/ ˈplæt.nəm ˈblɑnd / (AmE), / ˈplæt.nəm ˈblɒnd / (BrE)
(of hair) very light blond, almost white in color.
very light blond hair color

Examples

She dyed her hair platinum-blonde to give herself a striking new look.
The actress is famous for her iconic platinum-blonde hairstyle.
After several salon visits, his hair turned an icy platinum-blonde.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: blonde - hair of a light yellowish color

Etymology

From 'platinum' (a silvery-white metal) + 'blonde,' referencing the metallic, nearly white shade.
