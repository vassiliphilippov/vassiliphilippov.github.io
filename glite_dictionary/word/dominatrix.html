dominatrix

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɑːmɪˈneɪtrɪks / (AmE), / ˌdɒmɪˈneɪtrɪks / (BrE)
A woman who takes the dominant role in sadomasochistic sexual practices, often as a professional.
woman playing dominant role in BDSM

Examples

She hired a professional dominatrix to explore her interest in BDSM.
The novel featured a powerful dominatrix as one of the main characters.
As a dominatrix, she set strict boundaries and rules during sessions.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: submissive - a person who takes the submissive role in BDSM relationships

Etymology

From Latin 'domina' (lady, mistress) with the suffix '-trix' indicating a female agent.
