self-destructive

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlf.dɪˈstrʌk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf.dɪˈstrʌk.tɪv / (BrE)
tending to cause harm or damage to oneself, either physically, emotionally, or to one's own interests
causing harm to oneself or own interests

Examples

His self-destructive behavior led to repeated problems at work and in his relationships.
Choosing to skip necessary medication was a self-destructive decision.
The company’s self-destructive business strategy eventually resulted in huge losses.

Synset Examples

suicidal impulses
a suicidal corporate takeover strategy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-defeating - making it harder to achieve your own aims
  • Antonym: constructive - helpful or serving to improve or build up
