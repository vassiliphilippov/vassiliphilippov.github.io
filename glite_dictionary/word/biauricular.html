biauricular

1. ADJ

/ baɪ.ɔːˈrɪkjələr / (AmE), / baɪ.ɔːˈrɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
Relating to both of the ear openings, especially referring to measurements between them.
relating to both ear openings
medical

Examples

The anthropologist measured the biauricular diameter of the ancient skull.
Differences in biauricular width can be significant when studying population groups.
The surgeon referenced the biauricular plane to ensure accurate placement of the device.

Synset Examples

the biauricular diameter of the skull

Etymology

From Latin prefix 'bi-' (two) + 'auricularis' (of the ear).
