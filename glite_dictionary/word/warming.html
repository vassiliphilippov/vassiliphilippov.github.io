warming

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːmɪŋ / (BrE)
The process of becoming warmer or rising in temperature.
becoming warmer; increasing temperature

Examples

Scientists are concerned about global warming and its effects on the environment.
A sudden warming in the spring melted all the snow.
The warming of the room made everyone feel more comfortable.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cooling - the process of becoming cooler; a falling temperature

Etymology

From the verb 'warm' + -ing, forming a noun meaning the process of warming.
