Margaret Mitchell

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑrɡərət ˈmɪtʃəl / (AmE), / ˈmɑːɡərət ˈmɪtʃəl / (BrE)
(1900–1949) American novelist best known for 'Gone with the Wind', her novel about the American South during the Civil War.
American novelist, author of 'Gone with the Wind'

Examples

Margaret Mitchell's novel 'Gone with the Wind' won the Pulitzer Prize in 1937.
