one-sixty-fourth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn sɪksti fɔrθ / (AmE), / wʌn sɪksti fɔːθ / (BrE)
Each of sixty-four equal parts into which a whole is divided; the fraction 1/64.
1/64; a sixty-fourth part

Examples

The recipe calls for one-sixty-fourth of a teaspoon of saffron.
The measurement error was less than one-sixty-fourth of an inch.
He divided the land into one-sixty-fourth shares for each heir.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sixty-fourth - one of sixty-four equal parts; 1/64
