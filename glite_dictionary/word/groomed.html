groomed

1. ADJ

/ ɡruːmd / (AmE), / ɡruːmd / (BrE)
neat and smart in appearance; carefully maintained or cared for
neat and well cared for

Examples

She always looked impeccably groomed at every event she attended.
The groomed lawns of the estate impressed all the visitors.
His groomed beard complemented his professional appearance.

Synset Examples

the manager was a beautifully groomed young man
his horse was always groomed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unkempt - untidy or not well cared for in appearance
  • Synonym: tidy - neat and in good order in appearance

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'groom', from Middle English grome (boy, young servant).
