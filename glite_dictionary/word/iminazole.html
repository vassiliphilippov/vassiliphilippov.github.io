iminazole

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmɪn.əˌzoʊl / (AmE), / ɪˈmɪn.ə.zəʊl / (BrE)
A chemical compound, C3H4N2, classified as an organic base; known to act as a histamine inhibitor.
an organic base; histamine inhibitor
medical

Scientists studied the effects of iminazole on allergic reactions in patients.
