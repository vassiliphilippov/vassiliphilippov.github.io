ambassadorial

1. ADJ

/ æmˌbæsəˈdɔriəl / (AmE), / æmˌbæsəˈdɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to, characteristic of, or befitting an ambassador
of or relating to ambassadors

Examples

Her ambassadorial duties included attending international summits and negotiating treaties.
He received praise for his ambassadorial approach to settling disputes.
The newly decorated embassy reflected her ambassadorial status and importance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ambassador - an official envoy or representative, especially of a foreign state

Etymology

From 'ambassador' + '-ial', formed from the French 'ambassadeur'.
