preteenager

1. NOUN

/ ˌpriːˈtinˌeɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈtiːnˌeɪdʒə / (BrE)
a child who is between the ages of about 9 and 12, not yet a teenager
child aged 9 to 12; not yet a teenager

Examples

Many preteenagers start to develop new hobbies as they approach adolescence.
The movie is appropriate for preteenagers and older children.
Several preteenagers attended the summer science camp this year.

Synset Examples

little league is intended for the preteens

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preteen - a child between about 9 and 12; not yet a teenager

Etymology

Formed from 'pre-' (before) and 'teenager' (person aged 13–19).
