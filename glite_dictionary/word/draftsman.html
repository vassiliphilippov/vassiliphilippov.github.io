draftsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈdræftsmən / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːftsmən / (BrE)
a person who is skilled at drawing, especially as an artist or in preparing technical plans
person skilled at drawing (especially technical)

Examples

The draftsman was praised for his incredibly detailed architectural drawings.
As a talented draftsman, he contributed many illustrations to the book.
She hired a draftsman to create blueprints for her new house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: draughtsman - British spelling of draftsman; a person skilled at drawing
  • Synonym: artist - a person who creates art, especially drawing or painting

2. NOUN

/ ˈdræftsmən / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːftsmən / (BrE)
a person skilled in drawing detailed plans or technical drawings, especially for buildings or machinery
a person who draws technical plans

Examples

The company hired a draftsman to create detailed blueprints for the new office building.
Every draftsman in the firm must be proficient in computer-aided design software.
After years as a draftsman, he decided to study architecture at university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drafter - a person who prepares technical drawings; synonym, especially in American English
  • Synonym: architect - a person who designs buildings
  • Synonym: engineer - a person who designs or builds engines, machines, or structures

Etymology

From draft + man, referring to a person who drafts or prepares technical drawings.
