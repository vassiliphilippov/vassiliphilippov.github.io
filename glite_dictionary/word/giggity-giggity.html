giggity-giggity

1. INTJ

/ ˈɡɪɡɪti ˈɡɪɡɪti / (AmE), / ˈɡɪɡɪti ˈɡɪɡɪti / (BrE)
an exclamation expressing excitement, often used humorously or suggestively, especially in imitation of a character from the television show 'Family Guy'
humorous exclamation of excitement
informal american

Examples

He couldn't hide his excitement, shouting giggity-giggity when he saw his favorite band live.
Whenever something funny happens, she can't resist adding a quick giggity-giggity to the conversation.
The audience burst into laughter after he finished his joke with a playful giggity-giggity.

Synset Examples

He laughed and said, 'Giggity-giggity, let’s have some fun!'
Every time he makes a joke, he adds a 'giggity-giggity' for flair.

Etymology

Popularized by the character Glenn Quagmire in the animated TV show 'Family Guy' (American English, late 20th century).
