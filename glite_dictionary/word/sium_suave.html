Sium suave

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪ.əm ˈswɑv / (AmE), / ˈsɪu.əm ˈswɑːv / (BrE)
A stout perennial plant with white flowers, found wild in shallow freshwater areas of northern United States and Asia.
a perennial aquatic plant species

Examples

Sium suave is commonly found along the edges of ponds and slow-moving streams.
Local botanists are studying the growth patterns of Sium suave in different water conditions.
The delicate white flowers of Sium suave bloom in midsummer.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin genus name Sium + Latin suave meaning 'sweet', referring to the plant's mild taste.
