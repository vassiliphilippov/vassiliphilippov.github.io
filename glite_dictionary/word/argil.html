argil

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrdʒɪl / (AmE), / ˈɑːdʒɪl / (BrE)
A white clay, especially one used by potters for making ceramics.
white clay used in pottery

Examples

The potter molded the argil into an elegant vase.
High-quality argil is essential for creating fine porcelain.
Deposits of argil have been mined in this region for centuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clay - a natural earthly material that is plastic when wet, used in pottery

Etymology

From Latin *argilla* (clay), from Greek *argillos* (white clay).
