anlage

1. NOUN

/ ˈænˌlɑːɡə / (AmE), / ˈanlɑːɡə / (BrE)
An organ or tissue in its earliest stage of development, serving as the foundation for further growth.
early stage of organ/tissue development
medical

Examples

During embryonic development, the anlage of the heart appears before other organs begin to take shape.
The researchers observed that the neural anlage develops rapidly in the early embryo.
Abnormalities in the limb anlage can lead to congenital deformities.

Etymology

From German 'Anlage' (foundation, arrangement).
