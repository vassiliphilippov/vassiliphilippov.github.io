Antonio Stradivari

1. PROPN

/ ænˌtoʊni.oʊ ˌstrædəˈvɑːri / (AmE), / anˌtəʊniˈəʊ strædəˈvɑːri / (BrE)
(1644?–1737) An Italian violin maker renowned for developing the modern violin and for creating instruments of exceptional tonal quality.
famous Italian violin maker

Examples

Many musicians consider a Antonio Stradivari violin to be the finest instrument ever made.
The craftsmanship of Antonio Stradivari continues to be admired centuries after his death.
She dreamed of playing a priceless violin built by Antonio Stradivari.
