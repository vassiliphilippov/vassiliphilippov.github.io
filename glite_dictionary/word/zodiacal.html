zodiacal

1. ADJ

/ zoʊˈdaɪ.ək.əl / (AmE), / zəʊˈdaɪ.ək.əl / (BrE)
relating to or connected with the zodiac, especially the zodiacal constellations or signs.
relating to the zodiac

Examples

The telescope was aimed at a zodiacal constellation visible only at certain times of the year.
She read her zodiacal sign before making any big decisions.
Scientists study the zodiacal light to learn about interplanetary dust.

Synset Examples

zodiacal constellations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zodiac - a belt of the heavens divided into twelve signs

Etymology

From Late Latin zodiacalis, from zodiacus (zodiac).
