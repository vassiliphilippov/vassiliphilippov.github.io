parer

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛɹɚ / (AmE), / ˈpeərə / (BrE)
A small sharp knife used for paring or peeling fruits and vegetables.
small knife for paring fruits or vegetables

Examples

She used a parer to remove the skin from the apples quickly.
Every chef keeps a sharp parer in their kitchen drawer.
A parer is ideal for peeling potatoes and other vegetables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paring knife - a small knife used to pare or peel fruits and vegetables

Etymology

From the verb 'pare', meaning to trim or peel, from Old French *parer* (to trim, prepare).
