amiableness

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ.mi.ə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.mi.ə.bl̩.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being friendly and pleasant in manner
friendly and pleasant disposition

Examples

Her amiableness made her popular among her colleagues.
The amiableness of his manner put everyone at ease.
People admired the amiableness he showed even in difficult situations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amiability - the quality of being amiable or friendly
  • Antonym: unfriendliness - the state of being unfriendly or hostile

Etymology

From Latin 'amicābilis' (friendly), via Old French 'amiable' and Middle English 'amiable'.
