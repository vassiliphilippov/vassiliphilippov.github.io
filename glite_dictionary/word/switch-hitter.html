switch-hitter

1. NOUN

/ swɪtʃ ˈhɪtər / (AmE), / swɪtʃ ˈhɪtə / (BrE)
(slang) A bisexual person.
bisexual person (slang)
slang

Examples

She didn't realize at first that Mark was a switch-hitter until he mentioned his ex-boyfriend.
The magazine featured an article on famous switch-hitters in Hollywood.
Rumors about her being a switch-hitter soon spread throughout the small town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bisexual - a person who is sexually attracted to both men and women

Etymology

Originally from baseball, where it refers to a player who can bat both right- and left-handed. Later adopted as slang for bisexuality.
