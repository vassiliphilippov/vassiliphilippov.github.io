Filago germanica

1. NOUN

/ fəˈlɑːgoʊ dʒərˈmænɪkə / (AmE), / fɪˈlɑːgə ɡəˈmɑːnɪkə / (BrE)
A species of plant in the daisy family (Asteraceae), commonly known as common cudweed or German cudweed, found in dry, sandy places.
species of cudweed plant

Examples

Filago germanica often grows in sandy soils and is known for its woolly appearance.
Botanists frequently study Filago germanica due to its resilience in dry environments.
The fields were dotted with patches of Filago germanica blooming in early summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: common cudweed - a common name for Filago germanica, a plant of the daisy family
  • Degree Higher: Filago - a genus of herbaceous plants in the daisy family

Etymology

From Latin: genus name 'Filago' and the specific epithet 'germanica', meaning 'German'.
