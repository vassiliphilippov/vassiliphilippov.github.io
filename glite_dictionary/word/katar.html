Katar

1. PROPN

/ kəˈtɑr / (AmE), / kəˈtɑː / (BrE)
a peninsula extending northward from the Arabian mainland into the Persian Gulf, forming the territory of the State of Qatar
peninsula in the Persian Gulf (Qatar)

Examples

The Katar peninsula is almost entirely surrounded by the waters of the Persian Gulf.
Historians have traced ancient trade routes passing through the Katar region.
Many migratory birds use Katar as a stopover on their journey across the Gulf.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Qatar - country occupying the Katar peninsula in the Persian Gulf

Etymology

From Arabic قطر (Qaṭar), commonly anglicized as 'Qatar'.
