dished

1. ADJ

/ dɪʃt / (AmE), / dɪʃt / (BrE)
having a surface that curves inward like a dish or pan
inwardly curved like a dish

Examples

The satellite uses a dished reflector to focus the signal.
The wheel was slightly dished after the accident, affecting its balance.
She admired the elegant, dished design of the antique bowl.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: convex - having an outward-curved surface
