Kamba

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmbə / (AmE), / ˈkæmbə / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken by the Kamba people in Kenya
Bantu language spoken in Kenya

Examples

Many schools in eastern Kenya offer classes in Kamba alongside English and Swahili.
She is fluent in both Kamba and Kikuyu, which helps her communicate with a wider community.
The storytellers performed traditional tales in Kamba at the cultural festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bantu - a group of African languages spoken in central and southern Africa
