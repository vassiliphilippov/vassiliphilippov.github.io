Germany

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɜːrməni / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːməni / (BrE)
a country in central Europe, officially known as the Federal Republic of Germany, which was divided into East and West Germany after World War II and reunified in 1990
country in central Europe

Examples

My grandparents emigrated from Germany after the war.
Berlin, the capital of Germany, is known for its vibrant arts scene.
Germany is famous for its rich history and cultural heritage.

Etymology

From Latin 'Germania', the Roman name for the region.
