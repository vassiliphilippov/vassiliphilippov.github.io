sickle-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪkəl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈsɪkəl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a curved form similar to that of a sickle; crescent-shaped.
curved like a sickle

Examples

The bird had a long, sickle-shaped beak perfectly adapted for probing flowers.
The sickle-shaped moon cast a faint light over the desert.
Some leaves on the tree were distinctly sickle-shaped, making them easy to identify.

Synset Examples

a falcate leaf
falcate claws
the falcate moon

Related Terms

  • Synonym: falcate - curved like a sickle
  • Synonym: crescent-shaped - having the shape of a crescent or half-moon

Etymology

From "sickle" (a curved cutting tool) + "-shaped" (having the form of).
