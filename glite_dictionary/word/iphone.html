iPhone

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.foʊn / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.fəʊn / (BrE)
A popular smartphone designed and marketed by Apple Inc., known for its touchscreen interface and wide range of features.
Apple's popular smartphone

Examples

She upgraded to the latest iPhone because of its improved camera.
My iPhone battery lasts all day with regular use.
He used his iPhone to record the entire concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smartphone - a mobile phone with advanced features

Etymology

Blend of 'internet' and 'phone'; trademark of Apple Inc. since 2007.
