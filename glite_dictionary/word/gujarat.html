Gujarat

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡuːdʒəˈrɑːt / (AmE), / ˌɡuːdʒəˈrɑːt / (BrE)
a state in western India known for its industrial development and cultural heritage; its capital is Gandhinagar.
state in western India

Examples

Many international companies have established factories in Gujarat due to its strong infrastructure.
The traditional cuisine of Gujarat is famous for its unique flavors and vegetarian dishes.
Last year, we visited the beautiful stepwells and temples of Gujarat during our trip to India.
