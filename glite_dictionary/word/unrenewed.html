unrenewed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈnud / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈnjuːd / (BrE)
not revived, restored, or made fresh again; not renewed.
not revived or restored

Examples

Despite the passing years, the old treaty remained unrenewed.
His energy, unrenewed by rest, began to fade during the journey.
The contract expired and was left unrenewed at the end of the season.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: renewed - restored, revived, or made new again

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'renewed', from Latin 'renovare' (to make new again).
