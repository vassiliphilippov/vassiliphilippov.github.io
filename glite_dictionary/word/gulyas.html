gulyas

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːjɑs / (AmE), / ˈɡuːjɑːʃ / (BrE)
A rich Hungarian meat stew, highly seasoned with paprika and other spices.
Hungarian meat stew with paprika

Examples

My grandmother made a delicious pot of gulyas for Sunday dinner.
We ordered gulyas at the traditional Hungarian restaurant downtown.
The savory aroma of gulyas filled the entire house while it was simmering on the stove.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goulash - A stew or soup, especially a Hungarian dish made with meat and paprika

Etymology

Hungarian 'gulyás', meaning 'herdsman' and later the stew associated with them.
