clanswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈklænzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈklænzwʊmən / (BrE)
a female member of a clan
female member of a clan

Examples

As a proud clanswoman, she participated in every family tradition.
The Scottish festival welcomed every clanswoman in traditional attire.
Among the elders, a wise clanswoman shared stories of their ancestors.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clansman - a male member of a clan

Etymology

Formed from 'clan' (from Scottish Gaelic 'clann' meaning family) + 'woman'.
