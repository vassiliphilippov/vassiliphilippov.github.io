badgering

1. NOUN

/ ˈbædʒərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbadʒərɪŋ / (BrE)
persistent harassment or repeated attempts to annoy or coerce someone
persistent harassment

Examples

The constant badgering by reporters made her feel overwhelmed.
He faced badgering from the opposing lawyer during the entire cross-examination.
Her siblings' badgering finally convinced her to join the family trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harassment - the act of disturbing or tormenting persistently
  • Synonym: bullying - repeated aggressive behavior intended to hurt another

Etymology

From the verb 'badger' (to harass), which comes from badger (the animal), based on the notion of hounds harassing a badger.
