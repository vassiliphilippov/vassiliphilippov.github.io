sunroof

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌnˌruf / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌruːf / (BrE)
a panel in the roof of a car that can be opened to let in light and air
movable panel in a car roof

Examples

She opened the sunroof to enjoy the warm summer breeze as she drove.
Rain poured in through the sunroof that had accidentally been left open overnight.
The car came with a panoramic sunroof that made road trips much more enjoyable.

Synset Examples

‘sunshine-roof’ is a British term for ‘sunroof’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moonroof - a type of sunroof made of glass
  • Synonym: sunshine-roof - British term for sunroof
