myg

1. NOUN

/ maɪɡ / (AmE), / miːɡ / (BrE)
A unit of mass equal to one ten thousandth of a centner.
one ten thousandth of a centner (unit of mass)

Examples

The researchers measured the sample at exactly one myg to ensure accuracy.
A myg is an antiquated unit, rarely used in modern science.
The precision scale can detect changes as small as a single myg.
