sexagenarian

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛksədʒəˈnɛriən / (AmE), / ˌsɛksədʒəˈneəriən / (BrE)
a person who is between 60 and 69 years old
person aged 60–69

Examples

My grandmother became a sexagenarian last year when she turned sixty.
Many sexagenarians remain active in their careers well into their sixties.
The club caters to sexagenarian members with a variety of social and recreational activities.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: septuagenarian - a person aged 70–79
  • Degree Lower: quinquagenarian - a person aged 50–59

Etymology

From Latin 'sexagenarius' (containing sixty), from 'sexageni' (sixty each).
