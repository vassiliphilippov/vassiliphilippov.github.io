numerological

1. ADJ

/ ˌnuːməˈrɑːlədʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnjuːməˈrɒlədʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or based on numerology—the belief in the mystical significance of numbers
relating to numerology

Examples

The author offered a numerological interpretation of the events based on the significance of the number seven.
Many people seek numerological advice before choosing important dates for weddings or business launches.
She was fascinated by numerological patterns hidden within historical documents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: numerology - the belief in the mystical meaning of numbers

Etymology

From numerology + -ical, patterned after similar adjective formations.
