Martha Beatrice Potter Webb

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑrθə biˈætrɪs ˈpɑtɚ wɛb / (AmE), / ˈmɑːθə bɪˈætrɪs ˈpɒtə wɛb / (BrE)
(1858–1943) English social reformer, economist, and writer; a central member of the Fabian Society and co-founder of the London School of Economics.
English social reformer and writer
