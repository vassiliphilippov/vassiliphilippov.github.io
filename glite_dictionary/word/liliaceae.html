Liliaceae

1. NOUN

/ lɪˌliˈeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / lɪˌlɪˈeɪsiːˌiː / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants, commonly known as the lily family, including many ornamental and economically important species
the lily family of flowering plants

Examples

Several beautiful garden plants belong to the Liliaceae family.
Recent studies have reclassified some species formerly included in the Liliaceae.
Tulips, once considered part of Liliaceae, are now often placed in their own family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lily - a type of flowering plant, genus Lilium, in the Liliaceae family

Etymology

From New Latin Liliaceae, based on the genus Lilium (lily) + -aceae (a standard suffix for plant family names).
