falconine

1. ADJ

/ ˈfælkəˌnaɪn / (AmE), / ˈfɔːlkəˌnaɪn / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a falcon
relating to or like a falcon

Examples

The bird's falconine beak was perfectly adapted for tearing flesh.
Her features had a sharp, falconine elegance that made her stand out.
The artist gave the sculpture a falconine expression, with piercing eyes and a hooked nose.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accipitrine - relating to hawks or birds of prey

Etymology

From Latin 'falco' (falcon) + English suffix '-ine' (like, of the nature of).
