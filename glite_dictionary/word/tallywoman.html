tallywoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæliˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈtæliˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman whose job is to keep a record of numbers, quantities, or scores, especially as a tally clerk.
female tally clerk

Examples

The tallywoman carefully recorded the number of boxes delivered each day.
During the audit, the tallywoman checked every item against the shipping list.
She was the first female tallywoman to be hired by the company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tallyman - male tally clerk; a man whose job is to keep score or records

Etymology

From 'tally' (count, record) + 'woman'. Modeled after 'tallyman'.
