amateurish

1. ADJ

/ ˈæmətərɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈamətərɪʃ / (BrE)
lacking the skill, quality, or knowledge expected of a professional; characteristic of an amateur.
lacking professional skill or quality

Examples

The decorations looked amateurish compared to those at other events.
Her singing was pleasant but a bit amateurish for a public performance.
The company's website had an amateurish design that didn't inspire confidence.

Synset Examples

a very amateurish job
inexpert but conscientious efforts
an unskilled painting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unskilled - lacking skill or proficiency
  • Synonym: incompetent - not having the abilities required
  • Antonym: professional - showing the skill or quality of a professional

Etymology

From 'amateur' + '-ish'; 'amateur' from French 'amateur' (lover of).
