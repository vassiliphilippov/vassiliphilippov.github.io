hardwareman

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrdˌwɛrmæn / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌweəmæn / (BrE)
a man who sells hardware
man who sells hardware

Examples

The local hardwareman helped me find the right tools for my project.
As a hardwareman, he knew the best products to recommend for home repairs.
My grandfather was a hardwareman in a small village for thirty years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hardware dealer - person who sells hardware
  • Synonym: ironmonger - British term for person who sells hardware

Etymology

Formed from 'hardware' + 'man', modeled after similar occupational terms.
