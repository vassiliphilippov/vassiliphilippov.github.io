Igbo

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːɡboʊ / (AmE), / ˈɪɡbəʊ / (BrE)
a member of the largest ethnic group living in southeastern Nigeria
member of a Nigerian ethnic group

Examples

The Igbo have a rich tradition of music and storytelling.
Many Igbo in Nigeria speak both Igbo and English fluently.
She traveled to southeastern Nigeria to study Igbo culture.

Synset Examples

most Igbo are farmers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Yoruba - member of another major ethnic group in Nigeria
  • Synonym: Hausa - member of another major ethnic group in Nigeria

Etymology

From Igbo ‘Ìgbò’, the self-designation of the ethnic group.
