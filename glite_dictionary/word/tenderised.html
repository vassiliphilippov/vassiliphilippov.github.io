tenderised

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛndəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ˈtɛndəraɪzd / (BrE)
(of meat or food) made tender, especially by marinating or pounding.
(of food) made tender, usually by marinating or pounding
british

Examples

The chef used a mallet to ensure the tenderised steak would be easy to cut and chew.
Supermarkets often sell tenderised cuts of beef for quick meals.
After being tenderised with a marinade, the chicken was juicy and flavorful.

Synset Examples

tenderized meat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tenderized - (of food) made tender (American spelling)

Etymology

From 'tender' + '-ise'/'-ize,' meaning to make tender.
