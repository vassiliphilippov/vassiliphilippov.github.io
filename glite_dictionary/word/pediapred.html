Pediapred

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːdiəˌprɛd / (AmE), / ˌpiːdiəˈprɛd / (BrE)
A trade name for prednisolone, a glucocorticoid medication used to treat various inflammatory conditions and allergic reactions.
trademarked anti-inflammatory drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Pediapred to reduce the boy's severe allergic reaction.
After starting Pediapred, her asthma symptoms improved significantly within a few days.
Some children experience side effects when taking Pediapred for extended periods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prednisolone - a synthetic glucocorticoid drug used to treat inflammation
