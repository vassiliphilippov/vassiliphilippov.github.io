Richard Nixon

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɪtʃərd ˈnɪksən / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃəd ˈnɪksən / (BrE)
(1913–1994) The 37th President of the United States (1969–1974), previously Vice President (1953–1961) under Eisenhower; resigned after the Watergate scandal.
37th U.S. President; resigned after Watergate

Examples

President Richard Nixon resigned from office in 1974 following the Watergate scandal.
Many scholars debate the legacy of Richard Nixon in American politics.
Richard Nixon was Vice President under Dwight D. Eisenhower before becoming President.
