hoofed

1. ADJ

/ huft / (AmE), / huÀêft / (BrE)
(of animals) having hooves, or resembling hooves.
having hooves or hoof-like feet

Examples

Zebras are hoofed animals commonly found on the African savannah.
The museum displayed fossils of several hoofed creatures that lived millions of years ago.
Unlike cats, which have claws, horses are hoofed mammals.

Synset Examples

horses and other hoofed animals

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ungulate - hoofed mammal
  • Antonym: clawed - having or resembling claws

Etymology

From 'hoof' (Old English 'hof'), meaning the horn-covered foot of certain animals, plus the adjectival suffix '-ed'.
