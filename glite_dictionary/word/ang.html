/ ˌeɪ ˌɛn ˈdʒi / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˌɛn ˈdʒi / (BrE)
abbreviation for Air National Guard, the civilian reserve component of the United States Air Force, providing rapid mobilization during war and assistance in national emergencies.
abbreviation for Air National Guard (US Air Force Reserve)
american

Examples

The ANG was deployed to assist during the hurricane relief efforts.
He served as a pilot in the ANG for over a decade.
Members of the ANG train regularly to stay prepared for emergencies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Air National Guard - civilian reserve force of the US Air Force

Etymology

Acronym formed from 'Air National Guard'.
