Humperdinck

1. PROPN

/ ˈhʌmpərˌdɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈhʌmpədɪŋk / (BrE)
Engelbert Humperdinck (1854–1921), German composer best known for the opera Hansel and Gretel.
German composer (1854–1921)

Examples

Humperdinck is renowned for composing the beloved opera Hansel and Gretel.
Music scholars often study Humperdinck's innovative orchestration techniques.
The works of Humperdinck influenced many later composers of opera.

Etymology

German surname; famously borne by composer Engelbert Humperdinck (1854–1921).
