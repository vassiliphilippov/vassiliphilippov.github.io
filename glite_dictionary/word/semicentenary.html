semicentenary

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛmɪsɛnˈtɛːnəri / (AmE), / ˌsɛmɪsɛnˈtiːnəri / (BrE)
relating to or marking the 50th anniversary of an event or institution.
related to a 50th anniversary

Examples

The museum held a semicentenary celebration to honor its founding.
A special edition of the book was released for its semicentenary in 2020.
The semicentenary ceremony drew alumni from the past five decades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golden jubilee - the 50th anniversary of an event

Etymology

From Latin 'semi-' (half) + 'centenary' (hundred years), referring to half of a century or fifty years.

2. NOUN

/ ˌsɛmɪˌsɛnˈtɛːnəri / (AmE), / ˌsɛmɪsɛnˈtiːnəri / (BrE)
the 50th anniversary of an event or the celebration marking it
50th anniversary or its celebration

Examples

The museum will host a semicentenary celebration to commemorate its founding.
Alumni gathered from around the world for the university's semicentenary gala.
This year marks the semicentenary of the peace treaty's signing.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: centenary - 100th anniversary or celebration
  • Degree Lower: silver jubilee - 25th anniversary or celebration

Etymology

From Latin 'semi-' (half) + 'centenary' (relating to a hundred years).

Synset POS: n
