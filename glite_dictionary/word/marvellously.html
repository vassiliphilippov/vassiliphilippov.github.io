marvellously

1. ADV

/ ˈmɑrvələsli / (AmE), / ˈmɑːvələsli / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely good, impressive, or pleasing; excellently.
in an excellent or impressive way

Examples

She performed marvellously on stage, amazing the entire audience with her voice.
The cake you baked turned out marvellously delicious—everyone wanted a second slice.
The team worked marvellously together, finishing the project ahead of schedule.

Synset Examples

her voice is superbly disciplined
the colors changed wondrously slowly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wonderfully - in a wonderful or impressive way
  • Synonym: superbly - to an excellent or impressive degree
  • Antonym: badly - in a poor or undesirable way

Etymology

From 'marvellous' + '-ly', ultimately from Old French 'merveille' (wonder).
