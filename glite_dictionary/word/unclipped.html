unclipped

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈklɪpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈklɪpt / (BrE)
not trimmed or cut; left in its natural, uncut state.
not trimmed or cut

Examples

The unclipped hedges grew wildly along the edge of the garden.
She preferred to leave her dog’s coat unclipped during the winter for warmth.
Several unclipped coupons remained unused in his wallet.
