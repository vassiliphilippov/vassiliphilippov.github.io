swarthiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɔrðiˌnəs / (AmE), / ˈswɔːðɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having a dark or dusky complexion
darkness of complexion

Examples

His swarthiness gave him a mysterious and exotic appearance.
The swarthiness of her skin often drew admiring glances.
He was sensitive about his swarthiness, especially in a society that prized fair skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swarthy - having a dark or dusky complexion

Etymology

From 'swarthy' + '-ness'; 'swarthy' from Dutch 'zwart' (black).
