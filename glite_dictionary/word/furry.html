furry

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɜri / (AmE), / ˈfʊəri / (BrE)
Covered with a thick coat of soft, fine hair or fur.
covered with thick soft hair or fur

Examples

The kitten's furry paws left tiny prints on the floor.
He wore a furry hat to keep warm during the winter.
She bought a furry blanket that was incredibly soft to the touch.

Synset Examples

furred animals
a furry teddy bear

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairy - covered with hair
  • Antonym: bald - lacking hair or fur

Etymology

From 'fur' + '-y', meaning 'having the characteristics of fur'.
