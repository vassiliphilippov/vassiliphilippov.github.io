briarroot

1. NOUN

/ ˈbraɪərˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈbraɪəˌruːt / (BrE)
the hard, woody root of the briar plant (Erica arborea), often used for making smoking pipes
hard root of the briar plant, used for pipes

Examples

The craftsman carved a beautiful pipe from a single piece of briarroot.
Pipes made from briarroot are valued for their heat resistance and durability.
He imported high-quality briarroot from the Mediterranean region to create his signature pipes.
