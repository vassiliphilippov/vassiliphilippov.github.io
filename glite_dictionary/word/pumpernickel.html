pumpernickel

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌmpərˌnɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈpʌmpənɪkəl / (BrE)
a dark, dense bread made from coarsely ground whole rye grains or flour
a type of dark rye bread

Examples

The deli served thick slices of pumpernickel with smoked salmon.
She prefers pumpernickel to white bread for her sandwiches.
A traditional German dinner often includes hearty pumpernickel.

Etymology

From German 'Pumpernickel,' originally a humorous term for this bread.
