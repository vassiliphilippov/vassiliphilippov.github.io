freewheeler

1. NOUN

/ ˈfriˌwilər / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌwiːlə / (BrE)
a person who acts freely and often irresponsibly, without worrying about rules or conventions
person who acts freely or irresponsibly

Examples

As a freewheeler, he was never afraid to challenge the status quo.
Her freewheeler attitude often got her into trouble at work.
Some admired the freewheeler for his creativity, while others criticized his lack of discipline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rebel - a person who opposes authority or convention
  • Antonym: conformist - a person who conforms to accepted rules and standards

Etymology

From 'freewheel' (to operate freely without engagement) + '-er', meaning a person who freewheels.
