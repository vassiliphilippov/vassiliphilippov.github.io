primidone

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪməˌdoʊn / (AmE), / ˈprɪmɪdəʊn / (BrE)
an anticonvulsant drug used to treat seizures such as grand mal epilepsy and essential tremor
anticonvulsant medication
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed primidone to help control his epilepsy.
Patients taking primidone should be monitored for possible side effects.
She found that primidone was effective in reducing her essential tremor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anticonvulsant - a drug used to prevent or reduce the severity of epileptic fits
