culturati

1. NOUN

/ kʌltʃəˈrɑːti / (AmE), / kʌltʃəˈrɑːti / (BrE)
People who are interested in culture and cultural activities, often regarded as a social group.
people interested in culture

Examples

The city's culturati gathered for the opening night of the new theatre.
Modern art exhibitions tend to attract the local culturati.
As a member of the culturati, she never missed a major gallery event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: literati - educated people interested in literature or scholarly pursuits

Etymology

Modelled on 'literati' from Latin, combined with 'culture'.
