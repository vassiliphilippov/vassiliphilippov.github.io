nonnegative

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈnɛɡətɪv / (AmE), / nɒnˈnɛɡətɪv / (BrE)
(of a number or quantity) either positive or zero; not negative.
not negative; positive or zero

Examples

The results must be a nonnegative integer for the calculation to proceed.
All payments should be entered as nonnegative values in the system.
The function only returns nonnegative outputs for real-number inputs.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: positive - greater than zero
  • Antonym: negative - less than zero

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) and 'negative'.
