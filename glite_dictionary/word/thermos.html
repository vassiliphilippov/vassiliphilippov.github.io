thermos

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɜr.moʊs / (AmE), / ˈθɜː.mɒs / (BrE)
A container with a double-walled vacuum that keeps drinks hot or cold for long periods.
vacuum flask for hot or cold drinks

Examples

She poured hot coffee from the thermos while they sat by the lake.
I always bring a thermos of soup to work in winter.
His thermos kept the tea warm throughout the entire hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vacuum flask - insulated container that preserves temperature

Etymology

Originally a trademark from Greek *thermos* (hot).
