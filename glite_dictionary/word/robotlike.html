robotlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈroʊˌbɑtˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈrəʊbɒtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Behaving or functioning in a way that appears mechanical, unemotional, or lacking in independent thought.
mechanical or unemotional; like a robot

Examples

His movements were robotlike, showing no emotion or spontaneity.
During the interview, she gave robotlike answers, sticking strictly to rehearsed lines.
The customer service felt robotlike, as if a real person wasn't behind the responses.

Synset Examples

an automatic ‘thank you’
machinelike efficiency

Related Terms

  • Synonym: machinelike - resembling the operation of a machine; mechanical and automatic
  • Synonym: automatic - acting without conscious thought; mechanical or habitual
  • Antonym: human - characteristic of people with feelings and individual thought; not mechanical

Etymology

Formed from 'robot' (from Czech 'robota', forced labor) + '-like' (similar to).
