acapnotic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæk.æpˈnɑː.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌæk.æpˈnɒ.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to or showing acapnia, a state of reduced carbon dioxide in the blood.
relating to low blood carbon dioxide
medical

Examples

The patient exhibited distinct acapnotic symptoms during the respiratory assessment.
An acapnotic state can result from rapid, deep breathing.
The test results revealed an acapnotic condition, confirming the initial diagnosis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acapnia - abnormally low level of carbon dioxide in the blood

Etymology

From acapnia, modeled after the medical suffix -ic indicating pertaining to.
