cuckoldom

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌk.əl.dəm / (AmE), / ˈkʌk.əl.dəm / (BrE)
the state of being a man whose wife is unfaithful
state of being a cuckold

Examples

He endured the humiliation of cuckoldom in silence.
Literature from the Middle Ages often explored the theme of cuckoldom.
The fear of cuckoldom has influenced many social customs throughout history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cuckold - a man whose wife is unfaithful

Etymology

From Old French *cocu* (cuckoo, metaphor for an unfaithful spouse) + -dom (denoting state or condition).
