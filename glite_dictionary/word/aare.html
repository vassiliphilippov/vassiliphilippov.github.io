Aare

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑr ə / (AmE), / ˈɑːrə / (BrE)
a river in north-central Switzerland that flows northeast and joins the Rhine.
river in north-central Switzerland

Examples

The city of Bern is beautifully situated on a bend of the Aare.
We enjoyed a boat trip along the Aare during our vacation in Switzerland.
The Aare provides much of the region's hydroelectric power.

Etymology

From German 'Aare', the name of the river.
