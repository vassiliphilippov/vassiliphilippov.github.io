bombsight

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑm.saɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɒm.saɪt / (BrE)
a device in an aircraft used to assist in aiming and releasing bombs accurately
device for aiming bombs in aircraft

Examples

The pilot checked the bombsight carefully before beginning the bombing run.
During World War II, technological advances in the bombsight greatly improved bombing accuracy.
The museum exhibit included an original bombsight used by bomber crews in the 1940s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gunsight - a device for aiming a gun
