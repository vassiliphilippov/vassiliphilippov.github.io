uncured

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkjʊrd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkjʊəd / (BrE)
not preserved or treated, especially by chemical or seasoning processes; not cured
not preserved or treated (e.g. food, pelts)

Examples

The uncured pelts still needed to be processed before they could be used for clothing.
She prefers to buy uncured meats, which are free of added nitrates and nitrites.
The uncured resin requires additional time to harden completely.

Synset Examples

uncured pelts

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cured - preserved or treated, especially with salt or chemicals

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'cured' (from Latin curare, to take care of).
