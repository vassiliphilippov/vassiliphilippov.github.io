Lo/Ovral

1. NOUN

/ loʊ ˈoʊvrəl / (AmE), / ləʊ ˈəʊvrəl / (BrE)
A trade name for an oral contraceptive containing estradiol and norgestrel.
oral contraceptive (trade name)
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Lo/Ovral to help regulate my menstrual cycle.
Many women use Lo/Ovral as their preferred form of birth control.
Before starting Lo/Ovral, make sure to discuss possible side effects with your healthcare provider.
