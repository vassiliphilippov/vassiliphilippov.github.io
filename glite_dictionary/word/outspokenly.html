outspokenly

1. ADV

/ aʊtˈspoʊkənli / (AmE), / aʊtˈspəʊkənli / (BrE)
In a manner that is direct, open, and unafraid to express one's opinions, especially if they are critical or controversial.
in a direct and frank manner

Examples

She spoke outspokenly about the need for reform within the company.
The journalist outspokenly criticized government corruption during the interview.
He addressed the issue outspokenly, without fear of offending anyone.

Synset Examples

he was outspokenly critical of the Government's new social policy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frankly - in an open, honest, and direct manner
  • Synonym: bluntly - in a very direct manner, sometimes uncomfortably so
  • Antonym: tactfully - in a way that demonstrates sensitivity and consideration
