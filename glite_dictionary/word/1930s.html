1930s

1. NOUN

/ naɪnˈtiːn ˈθɜrtiz / (AmE), / naɪnˈtiːn ˈθɜːtiz / (BrE)
the decade beginning in 1930 and ending in 1939
the decade 1930–1939

Examples

The Great Depression had a profound impact on the world economy during the 1930s.
She loves collecting art deco furniture from the 1930s.
Many famous jazz musicians rose to prominence in the 1930s.
