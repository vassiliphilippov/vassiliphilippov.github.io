lwei

1. NOUN

/ lwɛɪ / (AmE), / lwɛɪ / (BrE)
A former monetary unit of Angola, equal to one hundredth of a kwanza, used until 1990.
former Angolan currency unit

Examples

Until 1990, one lwei was worth one hundredth of a kwanza in Angola.
The old coins were denominated in lwei before the currency reform.
Many collectors seek Angolan coins featuring the lwei denomination.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: kwanza - the principal currency of Angola
