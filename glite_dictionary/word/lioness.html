lioness

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪ.ə.nəs / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.ə.nɪs / (BrE)
a female lion
female lion

Examples

The lioness watched over her cubs as they played in the grass.
A lioness is typically the main hunter in a pride.
The zoo welcomed a new lioness to join its pride.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lion - a large tawny-colored cat found in Africa and northwest India
  • Degree Higher: lion - a large wild cat, Panthera leo

Etymology

From lion + -ess (feminine suffix in English, from Old French -esse, Latin -issa).
