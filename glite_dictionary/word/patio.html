patio

1. NOUN

/ ˈpætiˌoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpætɪəʊ / (BrE)
A paved outdoor area adjoining a house, used for dining or recreation.
paved outdoor area by a house

Examples

We ate dinner on the patio to enjoy the warm evening air.
The children played games on the patio while the adults talked inside.
She decorated her patio with string lights and potted plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrace - a level, paved area next to a building; often used similarly to patio

Etymology

From Spanish 'patio' (courtyard, inner court).
