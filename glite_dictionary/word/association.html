association

1. NOUN

/ əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a connection or relationship between people, organizations, or groups, especially for a particular purpose
social or business relationship

Examples

Her long-standing association with the charity brought many new opportunities for collaboration.
The company's association with several local partners helped it expand internationally.
He always valued his association with his former colleagues, even after changing careers.

Synset Examples

a valuable financial affiliation
he was sorry he had to sever his ties with other members of the team
many close associations with England

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affiliation - a close connection or association with a group or organization
  • Antonym: separation - the act or process of ending a relationship or connection

Etymology

From Latin 'associatio' (union, alliance), from 'associare' (to unite with).
