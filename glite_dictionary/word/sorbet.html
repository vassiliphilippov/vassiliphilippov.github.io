sorbet

1. NOUN

/ sɔrˈbeɪ / (AmE), / sɔːˈbeɪ / (BrE)
a frozen dessert made from sweetened fruit juice or purée, typically without milk or cream, with a soft, slushy texture
fruit-based frozen dessert

Examples

The raspberry sorbet was the perfect end to the warm summer meal.
She served lemon sorbet between courses to cleanse the palate.
I ordered a scoop of mango sorbet instead of ice cream for dessert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sherbet - a sweet flavored powder or frozen dessert, often milk-based

Etymology

From French sorbet, from Italian sorbetto, from Turkish şerbet (sherbet).
