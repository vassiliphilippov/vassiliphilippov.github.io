shitfucker

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtˌfʌkər / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtˌfʌkə / (BrE)
A highly offensive slang term for an extremely unpleasant or difficult person.
offensive term for a very unpleasant person
offensive vulgar slang

Nobody likes dealing with a shitfucker like him.
She called her ex a total shitfucker after what he did.
