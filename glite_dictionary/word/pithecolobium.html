Pithecolobium

1. NOUN

/ pɪˌθɛkəˈloʊbiəm / (AmE), / pɪˌθɛkəˈləʊbiəm / (BrE)
A genus of thorny shrubs and trees native to tropical and subtropical regions of America and Asia, belonging to the pea family.
genus of tropical thorny shrubs or trees
medical

Examples

The Pithecolobium trees in the garden provide excellent shade and striking flowers.
Many species of Pithecolobium are valued for their hard wood and ornamental appeal.
Researchers are studying Pithecolobium for its traditional uses in herbal medicine.

Etymology

From Greek 'pithēkos' (ape) + 'lobos' (pod, lobe), referencing the appearance of the fruit pods.
