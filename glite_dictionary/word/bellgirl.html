bellgirl

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛlˌɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈbɛlɡɜːl / (BrE)
a woman or girl employed to carry luggage and run errands for guests in a hotel
female hotel porter or errand runner

Examples

The hotel recently hired a bellgirl to assist guests with their luggage.
As a bellgirl, she greeted every guest with a friendly smile and helped with their bags.
The bellgirl quickly delivered room service trays to the suite on the top floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bellboy - male hotel porter or errand runner
  • Synonym: porter - person employed to carry luggage, especially in a hotel

Etymology

Formed from 'bell' (as in bellboy) and 'girl'.
