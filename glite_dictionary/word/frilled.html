frilled

1. ADJ

/ frɪld / (AmE), / frɪld / (BrE)
Decorated or edged with frills or ruffles, often referring to clothing or fabric.
decorated with frills or ruffles

Examples

The little girl's dress was frilled at the sleeves and neckline.
She wore a frilled blouse to the party, giving her outfit a delicate touch.
The curtains in the living room were frilled, adding a touch of elegance to the decor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frilly - having a lot of frills or decorative ruffles
