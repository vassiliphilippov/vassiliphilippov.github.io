unhealthiness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈhɛlθinɪs / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛlθinɪs / (BrE)
The state of being unhealthy or lacking good health; a condition in which normal, pain-free functioning is impaired.
state of being unhealthy

Examples

The unhealthiness of the food offered in the cafeteria concerned many parents.
Air pollution contributes greatly to the unhealthiness of urban living.
She was worried that her stressful lifestyle might increase her unhealthiness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illness - a disease or period of sickness
  • Antonym: health - the condition of being free from illness or injury

Etymology

From un- (not) + health + -iness (noun suffix).
