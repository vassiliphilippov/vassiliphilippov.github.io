Gilgamesh

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡɪlɡəˌmɛʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪlɡəmɛʃ / (BrE)
a legendary king of ancient Sumer and the main character in the Epic of Gilgamesh, one of the earliest known works of literature
legendary king, hero of ancient epic

Examples

Gilgamesh is considered one of the earliest literary heroes in recorded history.
Many scholars have studied the Epic of Gilgamesh to understand ancient Mesopotamian beliefs.
The legend of Gilgamesh has influenced countless later works in literature and mythology.

Etymology

From Sumerian 'Bilgamesh', later adapted as 'Gilgamesh' in Akkadian.
