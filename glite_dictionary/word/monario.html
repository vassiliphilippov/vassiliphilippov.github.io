Monario

1. NOUN

/ moʊˈnɑːrioʊ / (AmE), / məʊˈnɑːriəʊ / (BrE)
an artificial language devised by Aldo Lavagnini
constructed language by Aldo Lavagnini

Examples

Linguists have shown interest in studying Monario as an example of a constructed language.
Aldo Lavagnini published materials to help promote Monario in the early twentieth century.
Unlike natural languages, Monario was designed deliberately to be easy to learn.
