cytoplasmatic

1. ADJ

/ ˌsaɪtəpləzˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪtəplæzˈmatɪk / (BrE)
relating to or situated in the cytoplasm of a cell
relating to cell cytoplasm
medical

Examples

The researchers observed several cytoplasmatic changes during the cell's response to infection.
Protein synthesis is influenced by various cytoplasmatic factors within the cell.
Abnormal cytoplasmatic inclusions were identified in the diseased tissue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cytoplasmic - relating to the cytoplasm

Etymology

From 'cytoplasm' + '-atic', patterned after the more common 'cytoplasmic'.
