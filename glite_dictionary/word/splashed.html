splashed

1. ADJ

/ splæʃt / (AmE), / splæʃt / (BrE)
marked or covered with irregular patches of color or liquid, typically in a way that creates a striking or messy effect; often used in combination.
marked with patches of color or liquid

Examples

The garden was splashed with vibrant reds and yellows from the blooming flowers.
Her apron was splashed with paint after the art lesson.
Sunlight splashed across the stone floor, creating bright patches everywhere.

Synset Examples

waves dabbled with moonlight
a blood-spattered room
gardens splashed with color
kitchen walls splattered with grease

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spattered - marked with small drops of a liquid
  • Synonym: splattered - covered or marked with splashes of a liquid or other substance

2. ADJ

/ splæʃt / (AmE), / splæʃt / (BrE)
Covered with or marked by drops or masses of liquid that have been thrown or spilled.
covered or marked by scattered liquid

Examples

After the rainstorm, his jeans were splashed with muddy water.
The splashed paint on the canvas created a unique effect.
She cleaned up the kitchen after the floor became splashed with soup during cooking.

Synset Examples

with clothes wet by splashed water

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spattered - covered with small drops or spots of liquid
  • Degree Higher: soaked - completely wet
  • Antonym: dry - free from moisture or liquid
