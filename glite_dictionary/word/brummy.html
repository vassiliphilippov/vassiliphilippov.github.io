Brummy

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrʌmi / (AmE), / ˈbrʌmi / (BrE)
a native or resident of Birmingham, England
person from Birmingham, England
british

Examples

My friend is a proud Brummy who loves to talk about his hometown.
There are many famous Brummies in the world of music and sport.
Whenever I visit Birmingham, I always meet a friendly Brummy willing to show me around.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Brummie - alternative spelling and pronunciation for a person from Birmingham

Etymology

From 'Brum', a colloquial name for Birmingham, England.
