unlooked-for

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlʊkt fɔr / (AmE), / ʌnˈlʊkt fɔː / (BrE)
not expected or anticipated; unforeseen.
not expected; unforeseen

Examples

Her success was an unlooked-for joy for the entire family.
An unlooked-for opportunity arose when he received a call from an old friend.
They reacted quickly to the unlooked-for changes in the market.

Synset Examples

unanticipated and disconcerting lines of development
unforeseen circumstances
a virtue unlooked-for in people so full of energy
like a bolt out of the blue
unseen problems

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unexpected - not anticipated or predicted
  • Antonym: anticipated - expected or predicted
  • Antonym: foreseen - predicted or expected in advance

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'looked-for' (expected), using the sense of 'look for' as 'expect'.
