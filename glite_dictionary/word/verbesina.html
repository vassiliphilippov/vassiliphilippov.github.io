Verbesina

1. NOUN

/ vɜːrˈbiːsɪnə / (AmE), / vəːˈbiːsɪnə / (BrE)
A genus of herbaceous plants and shrubs native to warm regions of North America and Mexico, including species formerly classified under Actinomeris.
genus of North American herbs and shrubs

Examples

Several species of Verbesina grow wild along the roadsides in Texas and Mexico.
The distinctive yellow flowers of Verbesina attract many pollinators during late summer.
Botanists recently reclassified a few species into the genus Verbesina from Actinomeris.

Etymology

From Latin, genus name coined for these plants; the origin relates to 'vervain' (Verbena), with which they share some characteristics.
