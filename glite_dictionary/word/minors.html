minors

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪnɚz / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnəz / (BrE)
The lower-level professional sports leagues that are not part of the major league, especially in baseball.
lower-level professional sports leagues
american

Examples

After a year in the minors, he was finally called up to the major leagues.
Many talented players spend several seasons in the minors before making it big.
She began her coaching career in the minors before joining a major league team.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: majors - the principal or major professional sports league, especially in baseball
