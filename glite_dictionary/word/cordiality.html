cordiality

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɔːrdiˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌkɔːdiˈælɪti / (BrE)
warmth and friendliness in attitude or manner; sincere affection or kindness.
warmth and friendliness

Examples

Her cordiality toward guests made everyone feel welcome at the party.
The negotiations were conducted with remarkable cordiality on both sides.
There was a sense of genuine cordiality in his handshake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly
  • Antonym: hostility - unfriendly or antagonistic behavior or feeling

Etymology

From Latin 'cordialis' (of or for the heart), from 'cor' (heart).
