Soledad

1. PROPN

/ soʊləˈdɑd / (AmE), / ˌsɒləˈdæd / (BrE)
A city in northern Colombia that serves as a suburb of Barranquilla.
city in northern Colombia

Examples

Many tourists stay in Soledad when visiting Barranquilla due to its convenient location.
The population of Soledad has grown rapidly in recent years.
He was born and raised in Soledad, Colombia.

Etymology

From Spanish 'soledad' meaning 'solitude', named during Spanish colonization.
