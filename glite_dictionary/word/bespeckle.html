to bespeckle

1. VERB

/ bɪˈspɛkəl / (AmE), / bɪˈspɛkəl / (BrE)
to mark or cover with small spots or specks
to mark with small spots

Examples

Sunlight coming through the trees bespeckled the forest floor with golden patches.
The artist bespeckled the canvas with flecks of red and blue paint.
After the rain, mud bespeckled the lower part of his trousers.

Synset Examples

speckle the wall with tiny yellow spots

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speckle - to mark with small dots or spots; to cover with specks
  • Synonym: spatter - to scatter small drops or spots of a liquid or other substance

Etymology

From be- (prefix forming verbs) + speckle (mark with specks).
