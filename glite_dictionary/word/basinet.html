basinet

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæsɪˌnɛt / (AmE), / ˈbasɪnɪt / (BrE)
a type of medieval helmet made of steel, typically rounded and worn by knights for head protection
medieval steel helmet

Examples

The knight lowered his basinet before riding onto the battlefield.
Museum visitors admired the elaborately decorated basinet in the armor display.
Wearing a steel basinet, he was protected from sword blows to the head.

Etymology

From Old French 'bacinette', diminutive of 'bacin' (basin).
