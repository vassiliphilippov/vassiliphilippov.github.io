nudnik

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʊd.nɪk / (AmE), / ˈnʊd.nɪk / (BrE)
A person who is annoyingly persistent or boring; a tiresome nuisance.
a person who is an annoying pest

Examples

Stop being such a nudnik and let people relax in peace.
Her colleague is a real nudnik who always asks the same questions repeatedly.
Nobody wanted to sit next to the nudnik during the long meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pest - an annoying person, especially one who is a nuisance
  • Synonym: bore - a person who is dull and uninteresting

Etymology

From Yiddish *nudnik* (bore, pest), from Polish *nudzić* (to bore).
