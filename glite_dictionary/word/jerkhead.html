jerkhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɝːkˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːkˌhɛd / (BrE)
an insulting term for a person who is rude, obnoxious, or inconsiderate
rude or obnoxious person (insult)
informal slang

Examples

Stop being a jerkhead and listen to what she's saying.
Nobody likes working with someone who's always being a jerkhead about everything.
You were being a jerkhead last night; maybe you should apologize.

Synset Examples

Quit being a jerkhead and help your friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - a rude or mean person
  • Synonym: idiot - a stupid or foolish person (insult)

Etymology

Formed from 'jerk' (slang for an obnoxious person) + 'head' (pejorative suffix).
