advertisement

1. NOUN

/ ˌæd.vɚˈtaɪz.mənt / (AmE), / ədˈvɜː.tɪs.mənt / (BrE)
A notice, picture, or short film telling people about a product, job, or service, intended to persuade them to buy or use it.
public notice promoting product or service

Examples

The company placed an advertisement in the newspaper to announce its new product.
I saw an interesting advertisement for a job while browsing online.
There was a colorful advertisement on the bus promoting the new restaurant downtown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ad - informal abbreviation of 'advertisement'
  • Synonym: commercial - a paid message broadcast to promote products or services

Etymology

From advertise + -ment, from Middle French 'advertissement' (notice).
