Hollander

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːləndər / (AmE), / ˈhɒləndə / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Holland, a region in the Netherlands
native or inhabitant of Holland

Examples

As a proud Hollander, she celebrated King's Day with her family every year.
The famous painter was a Hollander known for his unique style.
Although he lives abroad, he still feels like a true Hollander at heart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dutchman - a native or inhabitant of the Netherlands

Etymology

From Dutch 'Hollander', meaning a person from Holland.
