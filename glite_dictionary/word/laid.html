laid

1. ADJ

/ leɪd / (AmE), / leɪd / (BrE)
(of objects) placed, set down, or arranged according to a plan or system.
arranged or set down according to a plan

Examples

The guests admired the beautifully laid table in the dining room.
Tiles were laid in a geometric pattern on the kitchen floor.
Every document was laid out on his desk for easy reference.

Synset Examples

a carefully laid table with places set for four people
stones laid in a pattern
