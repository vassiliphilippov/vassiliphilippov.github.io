layup

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪ.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.ʌp / (BrE)
A basketball shot made with one hand from close to the basket, typically banked off the backboard.
basketball shot close to the basket

Examples

She scored an easy layup after stealing the ball.
The player missed a simple layup during the final seconds of the game.
He practiced his layup technique to improve his accuracy near the basket.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: dunk - a basketball shot in which the ball is forcefully thrust down through the basket
  • Synonym: jump shot - a basketball shot made while jumping
