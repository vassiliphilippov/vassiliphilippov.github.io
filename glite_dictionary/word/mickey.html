Mickey

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪki / (AmE), / ˈmɪki / (BrE)
(offensive, slang) An insulting term for a person of Irish descent.
offensive term for an Irish person
offensive slang

Examples

The use of the word Mickey is offensive and should be avoided in conversation.
He was cruelly called a Mickey by the locals.
Calling someone a Mickey perpetuates harmful stereotypes and prejudice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Paddy - an offensive term for an Irish person
