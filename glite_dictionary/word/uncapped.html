uncapped

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkæpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkæpt / (BrE)
(of a tooth or other object) not provided with a protective covering or cap; not having an artificial crown.
not having a cap or covering, especially on a tooth

Examples

Her dentist recommended that the uncapped tooth be fitted with a crown.
The patient's front teeth remain uncapped after the accident.
Many people prefer the appearance of their natural, uncapped teeth.

Synset Examples

natural uncapped teeth

Related Terms

  • Antonym: capped - having a cap or covering, especially on a tooth

Etymology

Formed by prefixing un- (not) to capped.
