tightfitting

1. ADJ

/ ˈtaɪtˌfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtˌfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
fitting closely or snugly to the body or an object, with little or no looseness
fitting closely or snugly

Examples

The tightfitting lid prevented any leaks from the container.
She wore a tightfitting dress to the party, which accentuated her figure.
Cyclists often prefer tightfitting clothing to reduce wind resistance.

Synset Examples

a tightly-fitting cover
tight-fitting clothes

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loose-fitting - not fitting closely; having a lot of slack or looseness
  • Synonym: snug - fitting closely and comfortably
