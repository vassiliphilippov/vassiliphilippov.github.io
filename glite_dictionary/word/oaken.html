oaken

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊkən / (AmE), / ˈəʊkən / (BrE)
made of or consisting of oak wood.
made of oak wood

Examples

They admired the oaken beams supporting the ancient ceiling.
An oaken chest stood in the corner of the room, heavy and ornate.
The poet wrote about an oaken door that had survived for centuries.

Synset Examples

a solid oak table
the old oaken bucket

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oak - the hard wood of the oak tree

Etymology

From Old English 'āc' (oak) + -en (forming adjectives of material).
