oneness

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌn.nəs / (AmE), / ˈwʌn.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being one; unity or wholeness
the state of being one or united

Examples

The spiritual teacher spoke about the importance of experiencing oneness with all living beings.
Their shared purpose created a sense of oneness among the team members.
Meditation can foster a feeling of oneness with the universe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unity - the state of being united or joined as a whole
  • Antonym: separateness - the state of being separate or distinct

Etymology

From one + -ness, denoting the state or quality of being one.
