lesbianism

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛz.bi.əˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈlɛz.bɪ.ə.nɪ.zəm / (BrE)
sexual attraction or romantic relationships between women; female homosexuality
female homosexuality

Examples

She has been an outspoken advocate for lesbianism and LGBTQ rights.
The novel explores themes of lesbianism in a conservative society.
There is much more public awareness and acceptance of lesbianism today than decades ago.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homosexuality - sexual attraction or activity between members of the same sex
  • Antonym: heterosexuality - sexual attraction or activity between members of the opposite sex
  • Synonym: gay - attracted to people of the same sex (often used for men)

Etymology

From 'lesbian' (relating to Lesbos, island associated with Sappho) + '-ism'.
