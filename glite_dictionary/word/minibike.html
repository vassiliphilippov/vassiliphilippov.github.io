minibike

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪniˌbaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɪniˌbaɪk / (BrE)
a small motorcycle with a low frame, small wheels, and elevated handlebars, typically used for recreation or by children
small, lightweight motorcycle

Examples

He bought his son a minibike for his twelfth birthday.
Riders must wear helmets when operating a minibike at the park.
The minibike zipped down the trail, kicking up a cloud of dust.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motorbike - a motorcycle, especially a lightweight one
  • Synonym: motorcycle - a two-wheeled vehicle powered by an engine
  • Degree Lower: scooter - a lighter, smaller motorcycle with a step-through frame

Etymology

From 'mini-' (denoting something small) + 'bike' (short for bicycle or motorbike).
