hanuman

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːnʊˌmɑːn / (AmE), / ˈhænʊˌmɑːn / (BrE)
A large gray langur monkey of southern Asia, especially India, regarded as sacred in some traditions.
a sacred langur monkey of southern Asia

Examples

The temple courtyard was full of hanuman monkeys searching for food offered by visitors.
In India, the hanuman is often seen as a sacred animal and is protected in many areas.
A group of hanumans rested in the shade of a large banyan tree near the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: langur - a long-tailed arboreal Asian monkey

Etymology

From Hindi हनुमान (Hanumān), name of a Hindu deity often depicted as a monkey.
