Luscinia luscinia

1. NOUN

/ luːˈsɪniə luːˈsɪniə / (AmE), / lʌsˈsɪniə lʌsˈsɪniə / (BrE)
A large species of nightingale found in eastern Europe, noted for its powerful and melodious song.
large nightingale of eastern Europe

Examples

The Luscinia luscinia sings with remarkable clarity throughout the forests of eastern Europe.
During migration, ornithologists often track the movements of the Luscinia luscinia.
I was lucky enough to spot a Luscinia luscinia while birdwatching near the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nightingale - a small European songbird known for its beautiful singing

Etymology

From New Latin, based on Latin 'luscinia' meaning nightingale.
