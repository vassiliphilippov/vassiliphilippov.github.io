Goofy

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡuːfi / (AmE), / ˈɡuːfi / (BrE)
A tall, clumsy, and good-natured dog cartoon character created by Walt Disney, known for his distinctive laugh and attire.
Disney cartoon dog character

Examples

Goofy is one of Mickey Mouse's closest friends in the Disney universe.
My favorite Disney movie as a child always featured Goofy causing some comic trouble.
At the theme park, kids lined up to take pictures with a performer dressed as Goofy.
