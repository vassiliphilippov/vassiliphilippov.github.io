questioner

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɛs.tʃə.nɚ / (AmE), / ˈkwɛs.tʃə.nə / (BrE)
a person who asks a question, especially in a formal or public setting
person who asks a question

Examples

The questioner at the press conference was persistent in seeking an answer.
During the debate, the questioner challenged the speaker with tough inquiries.
The moderator called on the next questioner to address their query to the panel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inquirer - a person who asks for information

Etymology

From 'question' + agent suffix '-er'.
