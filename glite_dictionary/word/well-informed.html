well-informed

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɛl ɪnˈfɔrmd / (AmE), / ˌwɛl ɪnˈfɔːmd / (BrE)
having or showing thorough knowledge about a particular subject or about the world in general.
having thorough knowledge

Examples

The well-informed speaker answered every question with confidence.
It's important to remain well-informed about current events.
She consulted several sources to become well-informed before making her decision.

Synset Examples

well-informed readers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knowledgeable - having knowledge; well-informed
  • Antonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness
