untraceable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtreɪsəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtreɪsəbəl / (BrE)
Unable to be found, tracked, or identified by searching or investigation.
impossible to trace or track

Examples

The money was transferred through an untraceable offshore account.
The hacker used an untraceable email address to avoid detection.
Police found the threats came from an untraceable phone number.

Synset Examples

an untraceable source

Related Terms

  • Antonym: traceable - able to be found, tracked, or identified

Etymology

From un- (not) + traceable (able to be traced).
