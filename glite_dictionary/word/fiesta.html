fiesta

1. NOUN

/ fiˈɛstə / (AmE), / fiˈɛstə / (BrE)
A lively celebration or party, often held outdoors and featuring music, dancing, and food, especially with a Spanish or Latin American theme.
lively celebration or party

Examples

We attended a colorful fiesta in the town square last weekend.
The annual street fiesta features live music and traditional foods.
Everyone wore bright costumes to the fiesta in celebration of the holiday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: festival - a series of performances, events, or celebrations
  • Synonym: party - a social gathering of invited guests

Etymology

From Spanish ‘fiesta’ meaning ‘festival’ or ‘feast’.
