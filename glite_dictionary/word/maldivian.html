Maldivian

1. NOUN

/ mælˈdɪviən / (AmE), / mælˈdɪviən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the Maldives.
native/inhabitant of the Maldives

Examples

The Maldivian welcomed us to his island home with great hospitality.
Many Maldivians rely on fishing as their main source of income.
She learned about Maldivian traditions while traveling through the Maldives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Malaysian - native or inhabitant of Malaysia
  • Synonym: Maldivian - relating to the Maldives (adjective sense)

Etymology

From Maldives + -ian, a suffix forming demonyms and adjectives.
