SW

1. NOUN

/ ɛs ˈdʌbəl.juː / (AmE), / ɛs ˈdʌbəl.juː / (BrE)
the compass point that is midway between south and west, at an angle of 225 degrees
the direction midway between south and west

Examples

The storm was moving from the SW at high speed.
Their house faces the SW, getting plenty of afternoon sunlight.
The wind shifted to the SW during the night, bringing warmer air.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: southwest - the compass point midway between south and west

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'southwest'.
