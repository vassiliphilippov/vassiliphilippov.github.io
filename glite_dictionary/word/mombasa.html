Mombasa

1. PROPN

/ məmˈbɑsə / (AmE), / məmˈbɑːsə / (BrE)
a port city in southern Kenya, located on a coral island in the Indian Ocean
port city in southern Kenya

Examples

The coastal city of Mombasa attracts thousands of tourists each year for its beaches and historic sites.
She traveled from Nairobi to Mombasa by train, enjoying the scenic journey along the way.
Many traders pass through Mombasa due to its important seaport on the Indian Ocean.
