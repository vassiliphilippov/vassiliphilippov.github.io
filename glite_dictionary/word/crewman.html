crewman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkruːˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈkruːmən / (BrE)
a male member of a ship's crew
male member of a ship's crew

Examples

The crewman adjusted the sails as the storm approached.
Each crewman received safety instructions before boarding the vessel.
The new crewman quickly became popular among the sailors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crew member - a member of a ship's crew, gender neutral

Etymology

From 'crew' + 'man', modeled after terms like 'workman'; in use since the 19th century.
