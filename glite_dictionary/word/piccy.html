piccy

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɪki / (AmE), / ˈpɪki / (BrE)
(informal) very particular or fussy, especially about small details or choices.
very particular or fussy (informal)
informal

Examples

She's quite piccy when it comes to choosing restaurants for dinner.
Don't be so piccy—just pick a movie already!
My brother is piccy about the clothes he wears, and only buys certain brands.

Synset Examples

She can be quite piccy about her clothes and accessories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: picky - very particular or hard to please
