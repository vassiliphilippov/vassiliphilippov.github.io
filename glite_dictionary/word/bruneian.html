relating to Brunei or its people

2. NOUN

/ bruːˈniːən / (AmE), / bruːˈniːən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Brunei, a country on the island of Borneo in Southeast Asia
a native or inhabitant of Brunei

Examples

The Bruneian greeted us warmly and told us about his hometown.
Many Bruneian students study abroad to gain international experience.
She became friends with a Bruneian during her travels in Southeast Asia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Brunei - country in Southeast Asia on Borneo

Etymology

From Brunei + English suffix -an, denoting people or things related to a place.
