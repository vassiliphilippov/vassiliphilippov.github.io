indefinity

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.dɪˈfɪn.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌɪn.dɪˈfɪn.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being vague, unclear, or not precisely defined
the state of being vague or unclear

Examples

The indefinity of her reply left everyone unsure of what she truly meant.
Legal documents must avoid indefinity to prevent confusion or dispute.
There is a certain indefinity in his plans that makes them hard to follow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vagueness - the quality of being unclear or indistinct
  • Antonym: precision - the quality of being exact or clearly defined

Etymology

From 'indefinite' + '-ity', following the model of words like 'infinity'.
