Cordoba

1. PROPN

/ kɔrˈdoʊbə / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdəˌbɑː / (BrE)
A city in southern Spain, known for its historical significance as a center of Moorish culture.
city in southern Spain, Moorish heritage

Examples

The mosque-cathedral of Cordoba is a masterpiece of Islamic architecture.
Many tourists visit Cordoba each year to explore its rich history.
During the Middle Ages, Cordoba was a renowned center of learning and culture.

2. PROPN

/ ˈkɔrdəbə / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdəˌbɑː / (BrE)
A city in central Argentina, noted for its historical university founded in 1613 and as a major cultural and industrial center.
city in central Argentina

Examples

The university in Cordoba is one of the oldest in South America.
He spent a semester studying Spanish in Cordoba, Argentina.
Many tourists visit Cordoba for its colonial architecture and vibrant nightlife.
