Wight

1. PROPN

/ waɪt / (AmE), / waɪt / (BrE)
an island and ceremonial county in southern England, situated in the English Channel
island and county in southern England

Examples

We spent our summer holidays on the Wight, enjoying the beaches and walking trails.
The famous music festival on the Isle of Wight attracts thousands each year.
Many rare species can be found on Wight due to its mild climate.

Etymology

From Old English 'Wiht', the historical name for the island.
