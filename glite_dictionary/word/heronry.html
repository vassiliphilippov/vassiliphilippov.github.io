heronry

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛrənri / (AmE), / ˈhɛrənri / (BrE)
a breeding colony or nesting site of herons
heron breeding colony

Examples

The island in the lake serves as a heronry during the nesting season.
Several photographers gathered quietly near the heronry to observe the birds.
Deforestation has threatened the existence of the local heronry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rookery - a breeding colony of rooks, herons, or other birds

Etymology

Formed from 'heron' + '-ry' (denoting place or collection).
