sudsy

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌd.zi / (AmE), / ˈsʌd.zi / (BrE)
full of, resembling, or covered with suds or lather.
full of or resembling suds or lather

Examples

The water in the tub quickly became sudsy after she added the bubble bath.
He washed his hands with sudsy soap to remove all the dirt.
After mopping, the kitchen floor was left a little sudsy and needed rinsing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foamy - covered with or resembling foam

Etymology

From 'suds' + '-y', meaning full of suds.
