Kirghizstan

1. PROPN

/ kɪrˈɡɪzˌstæn / (AmE), / kɪəˈɡɪzstæn / (BrE)
a landlocked country in Central Asia, formerly part of the Soviet Union, now officially called Kyrgyzstan.
country in Central Asia (now Kyrgyzstan)

Examples

She traveled across Kirghizstan to explore its mountain ranges.
The official language of Kirghizstan is Kyrgyz.
After gaining independence, Kirghizstan underwent significant political changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kyrgyzstan - the official modern name of the country

Etymology

From Russian 'Киргизстан' (Kirgizstan), from 'Kirghiz' (the ethnic group) + 'stan' (Persian for 'land').
