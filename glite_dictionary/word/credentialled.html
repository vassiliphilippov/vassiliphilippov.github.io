credentialled

1. ADJ

/ krəˈdɛnʃəld / (AmE), / krɪˈdɛnʃəld / (BrE)
Having official recognition or certification, typically as evidence of professional qualification or competence.
officially certified; with credentials

Examples

All credentialled teachers must complete ongoing training every year.
The hospital only hires credentialled nurses for specialized departments.
He presented himself as a credentialled financial adviser.

Synset Examples

credentialled day care workers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: credentialed - having official recognition or certification

Etymology

From 'credential' + '-led', patterned after similar past participle adjectives.
