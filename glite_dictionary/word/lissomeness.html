lissomeness

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪsəmnəs / (AmE), / ˈlɪsəmnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being supple, flexible, and gracefully slender or agile
supple and graceful quality

Examples

The lissomeness of the ballet dancer captivated everyone in the audience.
Yoga helps improve both your strength and your lissomeness.
The cat's lissomeness allowed it to slip effortlessly through the narrow gap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suppleness - the quality of being soft and able to bend easily without breaking
  • Synonym: gracefulness - having or showing grace or elegance
  • Antonym: awkwardness - the quality of lacking grace or ease in movement

Etymology

From 'lissome,' variant of 'lithesome,' ultimately from Old English 'liðe' meaning 'gentle' or 'flexible.'
