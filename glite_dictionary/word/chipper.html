chipper

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪpə / (BrE)
cheerful and lively in mood or manner; showing sprightliness or self-confidence.
cheerful and lively

Examples

She always arrives at work looking chipper no matter how early it is.
He sounded especially chipper on the phone after winning the award.
Despite the rainy weather, everyone remained chipper during the hike.

Synset Examples

looking chipper, like a man … diverted by his own wit
life that is gay, brisk, and debonair
walked with a jaunty step
a jaunty optimist

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jaunty - having a lively, cheerful, and self-confident manner
  • Antonym: downcast - feeling sad or discouraged
