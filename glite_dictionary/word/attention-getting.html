attention-getting

1. ADJ

/ əˈtɛnʃən ˌɡɛtɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈtɛnʃən ˌɡɛtɪŋ / (BrE)
Attracting notice; tending to draw people's attention.
attracting notice; drawing attention

Examples

The designer used attention-getting colors to make the advertisement stand out.
Her attention-getting outfit made everyone in the room stop and stare.
Large, attention-getting signs were placed at the entrance to attract more visitors.

Synset Examples

eye-catching posters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eye-catching - immediately attracting attention
  • Antonym: unobtrusive - not attracting attention; blending in
