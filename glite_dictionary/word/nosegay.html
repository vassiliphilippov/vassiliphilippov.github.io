nosegay

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊzˌɡeɪ / (AmE), / ˈnəʊzˌɡeɪ / (BrE)
a small bunch of flowers, typically given as a present
small bouquet of flowers

Examples

She carried a delicate nosegay of wildflowers down the aisle.
He presented her with a fragrant nosegay on her birthday.
Each guest received a small nosegay as a token of appreciation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bouquet - an attractively arranged bunch of flowers
  • Synonym: posy - a small bunch of flowers

Etymology

From nose + gay, meaning 'something gay or bright for the nose', used since the 15th century.
