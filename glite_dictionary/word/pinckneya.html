Pinckneya

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋkˈniːə / (AmE), / pɪŋkˈniːə / (BrE)
a small genus of shrubs or small trees native to the southeastern United States and northern South America, known for their ornamental value
genus of shrubs or small trees

Examples

The Pinckneya is valued for its attractive white flowers in many Southern gardens.
Botanists recently discovered a new species within the Pinckneya genus.
Some homeowners plant Pinckneya trees for their ability to attract pollinators.

Etymology

Named after Charles Cotesworth Pinckney, an American statesman.
