metal-looking

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛt̬l ˌlʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmetl ˌlʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
having an appearance that resembles metal, especially in color, sheen, or texture.
resembling the appearance of metal

Examples

The sculpture had a metal-looking surface that gleamed in the sunlight.
She wore a metal-looking jacket that caught everyone's attention at the party.
The vase is made of plastic but is deceptively metal-looking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metallic - having the characteristics of metal, especially lustre
