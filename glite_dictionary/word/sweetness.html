sweetness

1. NOUN

/ ˈswitnəs / (AmE), / ˈswiːtnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of having a pleasant sugary taste, like that of sugar or honey
pleasant sugary taste

Examples

The sweetness of the honey balanced the tart flavor of the lemon.
She could taste the sweetness of the ripe strawberries in every bite.
Some people enjoy the sweetness of chocolate desserts more than anything else.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bitterness - the quality of having a sharp, unpleasant taste
  • Antonym: sourness - the quality of having a sharp, acidic taste
  • Antonym: saltiness - the quality of tasting of salt
  • Antonym: umami - the quality of having a savory taste

Etymology

From Middle English 'swetnesse', from Old English 'swētnes' (pleasantness), from 'swēte' (sweet).

2. NOUN

/ ˈswit.nəs / (AmE), / ˈswiːt.nɪs / (BrE)
a pleasingly sweet smell or fragrance
pleasant sweet smell

Examples

The sweetness of the blooming jasmine filled the night air.
She paused, enjoying the subtle sweetness of the perfume.
The sweetness drifting from the bakery tempted everyone who passed by.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragrance - a pleasant, sweet smell

Etymology

From Old English *swētnes* (sweetness), from *swēte* (sweet) + *-nes* (-ness).

3. NOUN

/ ˈswit.nəs / (AmE), / ˈswiːt.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or property of tasting as if it contains sugar
quality of having a sweet taste

Examples

The sweetness of ripe mangoes is irresistible in summer.
She added honey to her tea to increase its sweetness.
This dessert lacks sweetness and tastes rather bland.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bitterness - the quality of having a sharp, pungent taste opposite to sweetness
  • Antonym: sourness - the property of tasting acidic or tart

Etymology

From Old English swétnes, from swéte (sweet) + -ness.

4. NOUN

/ ˈswit.nəs / (AmE), / ˈswiːt.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being pleasant, charming, or delightful
pleasant or charming quality

Examples

Everyone admired the sweetness of her voice as she addressed the crowd.
There was a certain sweetness in their friendship that made it special.
The sweetness of the music filled the room and lifted our spirits.

Synset Examples

he was charmed by the sweetness of her manner

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pleasantness - the quality of being pleasing or agreeable
  • Antonym: harshness - the quality of being unpleasant or jarring

Etymology
