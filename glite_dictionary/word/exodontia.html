exodontia

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛk.soʊˈdɑn.ʃə / (AmE), / ˌɛk.səʊˈdɒn.ti.ə / (BrE)
the branch of dentistry concerned with the extraction of teeth
dentistry: tooth extraction specialty
medical

Examples

She decided to specialize in exodontia during her dental studies.
Advances in exodontia have made tooth extractions safer and less painful.
The clinic offers services in both restorative dentistry and exodontia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extraction - the removal of teeth

Etymology

From Greek 'exo-' (outside) and 'odous, odont-' (tooth).
