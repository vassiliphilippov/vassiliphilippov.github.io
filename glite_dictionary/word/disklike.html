disklike

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɪskˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdɪskˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the shape of a flat, round disk
flat and circular in shape

Examples

The red blood cells are disklike in shape, which helps them move easily through blood vessels.
The satellite seen from afar appeared disklike against the night sky.
He collected fossils that were perfectly disklike and smooth to the touch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disk-shaped - having the shape of a disk
