irrepressibility

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪrəˌprɛsəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌɪrəˌprɛsəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being lively and high-spirited in a way that cannot be restrained or controlled
uncontainable liveliness or high spirits

Examples

Her irrepressibility made her the life of every party she attended.
Despite the setbacks, his irrepressibility inspired everyone around him to keep going.
Their team's irrepressibility was evident even in the face of defeat.

Synset Examples

I admired his buoyancy and persistent good humor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buoyancy - cheerfulness and liveliness of spirit

Etymology

From 'irrepressible' + '-ity', after Latin 'irrepressibilis' (not able to be restrained).
