Maui

1. PROPN

/ ˈmaʊ.i / (AmE), / ˈmaʊ.i / (BrE)
the second largest of the Hawaiian Islands, known for its scenic beaches, resorts, and natural attractions
Hawaiian island; popular tourist destination

Examples

Many tourists visit Maui each year to enjoy its stunning beaches and lush scenery.
The Road to Hana is one of the most famous drives on Maui.
She spent her honeymoon on Maui, exploring its waterfalls and volcanoes.

Etymology

From Hawaiian Maui, named after the demigod Māui in Polynesian mythology.
