Lansium

1. PROPN

/ ˈlænsiəm / (AmE), / ˈlænsiəm / (BrE)
A genus of tropical Asian trees in the family Meliaceae, including species such as the langsat, known for their edible fruits.
genus of tropical Asian trees (Meliaceae)

Examples

The fruit of Lansium species, known as langsat, is popular in Southeast Asia.
Botanists have classified several tropical trees under the genus Lansium.
Researchers studied the medicinal properties found in Lansium extracts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: langsat - an edible fruit of Lansium domesticum

Etymology

From scientific Latin 'Lansium', established as a taxonomic name.
