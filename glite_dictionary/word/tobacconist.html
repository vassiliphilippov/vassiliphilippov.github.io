tobacconist

1. NOUN

/ təˈbækənɪst / (AmE), / təˈbækənɪst / (BrE)
a person or shop that sells tobacco and tobacco-related products
person or shop selling tobacco products

Examples

He stopped at the tobacconist to buy a new pipe and some cigars.
A small tobacconist on the corner sells rare blends for aficionados.
The tobacconist was known for his expertise in recommending fine tobacco.

Etymology

From tobacco + -nist, modeled after chemist, druggist, etc.

2. NOUN

/ təˈbækənɪst / (AmE), / təˈbækənɪst / (BrE)
a shop that sells tobacco products such as pipes, pipe tobacco, cigars, and cigarettes
shop selling tobacco products

Examples

He bought a box of cigars from the local tobacconist.
The tobacconist offered a wide range of imported cigarettes and accessories.
Many traditional tobacconist shops also sell magazines and snacks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newsagent - shop selling newspapers, magazines, and sometimes tobacco

Etymology

From 'tobacco' + '-nist', originally referring to a dealer in tobacco.
