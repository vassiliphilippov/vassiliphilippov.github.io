choc

1. NOUN

/ tʃɑk / (AmE), / tʃɒk / (BrE)
an informal term for chocolate, especially used in British English; often refers to a piece or box of chocolates
informal British term for chocolate
informal british

After dinner, they served coffee and chocs to the guests.
He bought a selection of chocs for his mum's birthday.
