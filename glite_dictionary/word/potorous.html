Potorous

1. NOUN

/ poʊˈtɔːrəs / (AmE), / pəˈtɔːrəs / (BrE)
A genus of small marsupials native to Australia, commonly known as potoroos.
genus of Australian marsupials (potoroos)

Examples

The endangered Potorous species are native only to certain regions of Australia.
Scientists are studying Potorous to better understand their unique diets and habitats.
Conservation efforts are underway to protect Potorous from extinction.

Etymology

From Modern Latin, based on an Aboriginal Australian word (potoroo, the animal's common name).
