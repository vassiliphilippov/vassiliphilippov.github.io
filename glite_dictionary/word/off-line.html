off-line

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔfˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɒfˌlaɪn / (BrE)
Not connected to or directly controlled by a computer or computer network.
not connected to a network

Examples

Many off-line resources are still valuable for research.
She saved the documents for off-line access during her flight.
The program can be used in both off-line and online modes.

Synset Examples

off-line resources

Related Terms

  • Antonym: online - connected to or available through a computer network

2. ADJ

/ ˈɔfˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɒfˌlaɪn / (BrE)
Not situated on the regular or main route of a transportation system; located away from the main service line.
not on a main transportation route

Examples

The new terminal is an off-line facility serving remote communities.
We purchased our tickets at an off-line ticket office near the old station.
Several hotels offer shuttle service from off-line locations to the main train route.

Synset Examples
