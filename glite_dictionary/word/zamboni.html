Zamboni

1. NOUN

/ zæmˈboʊni / (AmE), / zæmˈbəʊni / (BrE)
a machine used to resurface and smooth the ice in an ice-skating rink
ice-resurfacing machine for skating rinks

Examples

After every hockey period, the Zamboni cleaned and polished the ice to perfection.
Children watched with fascination as the Zamboni circled the rink between skating sessions.
The rink manager drove the Zamboni slowly, leaving a fresh, smooth layer of ice behind.

Etymology

Trademark from the name of its inventor, Frank J. Zamboni.
