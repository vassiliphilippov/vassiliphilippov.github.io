Sault Sainte Marie

1. PROPN

/ suː seɪnt məˈriː / (AmE), / suː sæ̃ ˈmɑːri / (BrE)
a city in southern Ontario, Canada, on the St. Marys River, opposite Sault Ste. Marie, Michigan, USA
city in Ontario, Canada, across from Michigan

Examples

Tourists often visit Sault Sainte Marie to see the historic locks on the St. Marys River.
My family drove through Sault Sainte Marie during our vacation to northern Ontario.
The bridge connecting Michigan and Sault Sainte Marie is a vital international crossing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sault Ste. Marie, Michigan - city in northern Michigan, USA, across the river from Sault Sainte Marie, Ontario

Etymology

From French 'Sault Sainte Marie', meaning 'Saint Mary's Rapids'.
