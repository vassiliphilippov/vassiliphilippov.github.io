ungulated

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌŋɡjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈʌŋɡjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
having or resembling hooves; hoofed.
having or resembling hooves

Most large herbivores in the region are ungulated, aiding their movement across rocky terrain.

Etymology

From Latin 'ungulatus' (hoofed), from 'ungula' (hoof).
