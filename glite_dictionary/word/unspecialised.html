unspecialised

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈspɛʃəlaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈspɛʃəlaɪzd / (BrE)
Not adapted or modified for a specific purpose or function; lacking specialisation.
not adapted for a specific function

Examples

His skills were quite unspecialised, making him suitable for various jobs.
The cells at this stage are still unspecialised and can develop into any type of tissue.
Many entry-level positions require unspecialised knowledge rather than technical expertise.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: specialised - adapted for a particular function or purpose
  • Synonym: general - not specialised; general in nature or use
