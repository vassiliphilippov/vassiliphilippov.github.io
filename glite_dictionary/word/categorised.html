categorised

1. ADJ

/ ˈkætəɡəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ˈkatɪɡərʌɪzd / (BrE)
arranged or placed into specific categories or groups according to shared characteristics
arranged into specific categories
british

Examples

All the files were categorised by date and importance for easy access.
Books in the library are categorised according to subject matter.
The survey responses were categorised into three distinct groups.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: categorized - arranged into specific categories (American spelling)
  • Antonym: uncategorised - not arranged into any specific categories (British spelling)

Etymology

From the verb 'categorise', ultimately derived from Greek 'katēgoria' (accusation, category).
