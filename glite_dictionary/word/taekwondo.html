taekwondo

1. NOUN

/ taɪˈkwɑn.doʊ / (AmE), / taɪˈkwɒn.dəʊ / (BrE)
A Korean martial art characterized by high, fast kicks and jumping and spinning kicks.
Korean martial art; high-kicking style

Examples

My daughter started taking taekwondo lessons to improve her fitness and discipline.
The taekwondo competition drew participants from many countries around the world.
He earned his black belt in taekwondo after years of hard work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: karate - a Japanese martial art emphasizing striking techniques

Etymology

From Korean 태권도 (taegwondo), composed of tae (to kick), kwon (fist), and do (way, art).
