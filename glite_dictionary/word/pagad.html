PAGAD

1. PROPN

/ pɑːˈɡɑːd / (AmE), / pɑːˈɡɑːd / (BrE)
a militant organization in South Africa, originally formed to combat drug-related crime, which has been linked to vigilante violence and terrorist activities.
South African militant group

Examples

PAGAD began as a community initiative but soon gained notoriety for its violent methods against drug dealers.
The South African government launched an investigation into the activities of PAGAD after several bombings in Cape Town.
Many residents were initially supportive of PAGAD before its tactics escalated to vigilante violence.

Etymology

Acronym of People Against Gangsterism and Drugs.
