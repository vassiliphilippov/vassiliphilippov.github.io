Amsterdam

1. PROPN

/ ˈæm.stɚ.dæm / (AmE), / ˈam.stə.dam / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of the Netherlands, known for its canals, museums, and as a center of trade and culture
capital city of the Netherlands

Examples

She visited Amsterdam last summer to see the famous Van Gogh Museum.
Many international companies have their European headquarters in Amsterdam.
Amsterdam is famous for its picturesque canals and vibrant nightlife.

Etymology

From Dutch 'Amsterdam', originally referring to a dam on the river Amstel.
