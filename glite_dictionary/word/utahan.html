Utahan

1. NOUN

/ juːˈtɑːn / (AmE), / juːˈtɑːn / (BrE)
A native or resident of the state of Utah in the United States.
a native or resident of Utah

Examples

Her grandfather is a proud Utahan who has lived in Salt Lake City all his life.
As a Utahan, she enjoys hiking in the beautiful mountains of her state.
Many Utahans celebrate their pioneer heritage with annual festivals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Utahn - alternative spelling for a native or resident of Utah

Etymology

Formed from the state name 'Utah' plus the English suffix '-an' (denoting belonging).
