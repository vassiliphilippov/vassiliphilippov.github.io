molindone

1. NOUN

/ moʊˈlɪnˌdoʊn / (AmE), / məʊˈlɪnˌdəʊn / (BrE)
an antipsychotic drug used to treat schizophrenia, formerly marketed under the brand name Moban
antipsychotic drug for schizophrenia
medical

Examples

The psychiatrist prescribed molindone to help manage the patient's symptoms.
Some patients experience fewer side effects with molindone compared to other antipsychotic drugs.
Due to supply issues, molindone may not be available in all pharmacies.

Related Terms
