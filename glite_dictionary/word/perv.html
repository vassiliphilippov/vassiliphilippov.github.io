perv

1. NOUN

/ pɝv / (AmE), / pɜːv / (BrE)
a person whose behavior is considered sexually inappropriate or deviant
sexually inappropriate person (informal)
informal slang

Examples

I think that guy staring at everyone is a total perv.
She called him a perv after he made another inappropriate joke.
Online forums warn users about pervs trying to contact young people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pervert - a person with abnormal sexual behavior

Etymology

Shortened from 'pervert', originally from Latin 'pervertere' (to turn around, corrupt).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10438792-n
Synset POS: n

2. VERB

/ pɜrv / (AmE), / pɜːv / (BrE)
Look at someone with sexual or amorous intentions, often in a way that is considered inappropriate or intrusive.
look at sexually or amorously
slang

Examples

He was accused of trying to perv on women at the beach.
Don't perv on people in the gym; it's creepy.
She caught him perving through the window and called security.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ogle - to look at someone in a way that shows sexual interest

Etymology

Shortened form of 'pervert', used colloquially in the late 20th century.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02168498-v
Synset POS: v
