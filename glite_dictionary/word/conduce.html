to conduce

1. VERB

/ kənˈdus / (AmE), / kənˈdjuːs / (BrE)
to help to bring about a particular result or situation; to contribute to something
to contribute to a result or situation

Examples

A quiet environment can conduce to better concentration during study sessions.
Regular exercise conduces to good health and longevity.
Their efforts conduced greatly to the success of the project.

Synset Examples

The use of computers in the classroom led to better writing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to contribute - to help to cause or bring about something
  • Antonym: hinder - to prevent or make more difficult

Etymology

From Latin 'conducere' meaning 'to bring together, contribute'
