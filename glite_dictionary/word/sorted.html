sorted

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɔrtɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɔːtɪd / (BrE)
Arranged or organized into groups or categories in a particular order.
arranged into groups or categories

Examples

The files are sorted by subject and easily accessible.
Please keep your receipts sorted to simplify your expenses report.
The candies were sorted into different jars according to their color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organized - arranged systematically; structured

2. ADJ

/ ˈsɔrtɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɔːtɪd / (BrE)
arranged or organized according to a particular order, such as size or type.
arranged or organized in order

Examples

All of the files are now sorted according to size and date.
The students were sorted into groups based on their interests.
The buttons were carefully sorted by color and size in separate boxes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organized - arranged systematically; ordered
  • Antonym: mixed - not sorted; in no particular order

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'sort', from Old French 'sortir' (to sort).
