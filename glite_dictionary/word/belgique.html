Belgique

1. PROPN

/ bɛlˈʒik / (AmE), / bɛlˈʒiːk / (BrE)
the French name for Belgium, a country in northwestern Europe that is a constitutional monarchy and the headquarters of the European Union and NATO
French name for Belgium (country)

Examples

The wine was imported from Belgique, known for its unique regional flavors.
Many European institutions have their headquarters in Belgique.
She spent a semester studying French in Belgique.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Belgium - a country in northwestern Europe; official English name

Etymology

French form of 'Belgium', ultimately from Latin 'Belgica', referring to the region of the ancient Belgae.
