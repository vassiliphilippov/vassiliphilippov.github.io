entomologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛntəˈmɑlədʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛntəməˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the scientific study of insects (entomology)
relating to entomology (study of insects)

Examples

The lab is known for its groundbreaking entomologic studies on pollinators.
Her entomologic expertise made her the leading scientist in the research team.
The conference featured several papers showcasing advances in entomologic methods.

Synset Examples

entomological research

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entomological - relating to the scientific study of insects; variant form

Etymology

From entomology + -ic; entomology from Greek entomon (insect) + -logia (study).
