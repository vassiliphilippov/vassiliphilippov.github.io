Mayaca

1. NOUN

/ maɪˈækə / (AmE), / maɪˈækə / (BrE)
A small genus of delicate, moss-like aquatic or bog plants, often bearing white or violet flowers.
genus of delicate aquatic bog plants

Examples

The researcher found Mayaca growing in abundance along the edges of the swamp.
Some aquarium enthusiasts add Mayaca to their tanks for its delicate appearance.
The white flowers of Mayaca stood out against the dark, mossy wetlands.
