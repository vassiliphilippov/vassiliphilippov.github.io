Cambodian

1. NOUN

/ kæmˈboʊdiən / (AmE), / kæmˈbəʊdiən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Cambodia.
a person from Cambodia

Examples

As a Cambodian, she is proud of her rich cultural heritage.
The museum features art by a contemporary Cambodian sculptor.
Many Cambodians celebrated the national holiday with traditional music and dance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cambodia - A country in Southeast Asia

Etymology

From Cambodia + -an, denoting belonging or origin.

2. ADJ

/ kæmˈboʊdiən / (AmE), / kæmˈbəʊdiən / (BrE)
of or relating to Cambodia, its people, or its language.
relating to Cambodia or its people

Examples

The Cambodian festival attracted visitors from around the world.
She wore a traditional Cambodian dress to the ceremony.
This restaurant specializes in authentic Cambodian cuisine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Khmer - the official language and main ethnic group of Cambodia
  • Antonym: Vietnamese - relating to Vietnam, its people, or its language

Etymology

From Cambodia + -an, denoting relation or belonging.
