fluffiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈflʌfi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈflʌf.i.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being light, soft, and airy, resembling the texture or appearance of fluff
the quality of being soft and airy

Examples

The fluffiness of the new pillow made it incredibly comfortable to sleep on.
She loves the fluffiness of fresh whipped cream on her dessert.
The kitten's fluffiness made it irresistible to cuddle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: softness - the quality of being easy to press or shape
  • Antonym: firmness - the quality of being solid or resistant to pressure

Etymology

From 'fluffy' + '-ness', denoting the state or condition.
