pummelo

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌməˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈpʌmɪləʊ / (BrE)
A Southeast Asian tree (Citrus maxima) that produces large, thick-skinned fruits similar to grapefruits, or the fruit itself.
large citrus fruit tree from Southeast Asia

Examples

The pummelo is known for its thick rind and sweet, mild flavor.
Farmers in Thailand cultivate pummelos for both domestic markets and export.
A pummelo can be much larger than an ordinary grapefruit and is often eaten fresh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pomelo - alternative spelling for 'pummelo'; the fruit of Citrus maxima

Etymology

From Malay *pamelo* or Portuguese *pomelo*, ultimately from Tamil *pampalimasu*.
