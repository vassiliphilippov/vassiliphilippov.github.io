Abu Dhabi

1. PROPN

/ ˌɑːbuː ˈdɑːbi / (AmE), / ˌɑːbuː ˈdɑːbi / (BrE)
the capital city of the United Arab Emirates, located on an island in the Persian Gulf; also the largest emirate in the country
capital of the United Arab Emirates

Examples

The Louvre Abu Dhabi houses an impressive collection of artwork from around the world.
She moved to Abu Dhabi for a new job opportunity.
Abu Dhabi is known for its modern skyline and luxurious hotels.

Etymology

From Arabic 'Abū Ẓabī', meaning 'father of the gazelle'.

2. PROPN

/ ˌɑːbuː ˈdɑːbi / (AmE), / ˌɑːbuː ˈdɑːbi / (BrE)
the capital city of the United Arab Emirates, located on an island in the Persian Gulf; also, one of the seven emirates forming the country.
capital city of the United Arab Emirates

Examples

Many international companies have established offices in Abu Dhabi due to its growing economic influence.
She took a flight from London to Abu Dhabi for her new job in finance.
The cultural festival in Abu Dhabi attracts visitors from all over the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dubai - one of the emirates of the UAE, known for its modern architecture

Etymology

From Arabic أبو ظبي (Abū Ẓabī), meaning 'Father of the Gazelle'.
