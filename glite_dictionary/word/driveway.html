driveway

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪvˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈdraɪv.weɪ / (BrE)
a short road or path leading from a street to a private house or garage, where vehicles can be parked
short road leading to a private house or garage

Examples

The moving truck blocked our driveway for almost an hour.
After shoveling the snow, she finally cleared the entire driveway.
You can leave your bike in the driveway until we're ready to go.

Synset Examples

they parked in the driveway

Related Terms

  • Synonym: road - a wide way leading from one place to another, especially one with a specially prepared surface for vehicles
