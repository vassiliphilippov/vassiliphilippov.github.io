cornbread

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrnˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌbrɛd / (BrE)
A type of bread made chiefly from cornmeal, often associated with Southern U.S. cuisine.
bread made from cornmeal

Examples

She served warm cornbread with honey butter at the family dinner.
A slice of cornbread pairs perfectly with a bowl of chili.
My grandmother taught me how to bake traditional Southern cornbread.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: johnnycake - a type of flat cornmeal bread
  • Synonym: corn muffin - a muffin made with cornmeal
