campaigning

1. NOUN

/ kæmˈpeɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / kamˈpeɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the organized efforts and activities undertaken by a candidate or group to win an election
organized election effort

Examples

The senator’s campaigning in rural areas helped win over undecided voters.
Media coverage can greatly influence how campaigning unfolds during an election season.
Effective campaigning often involves connecting with local communities and addressing their needs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: campaign - an organized effort to achieve a particular goal, especially in politics or business

Etymology

From 'campaign' + -ing; 'campaign' comes from French 'campagne', meaning field (of battle).
