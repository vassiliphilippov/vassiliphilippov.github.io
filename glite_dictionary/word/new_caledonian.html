New Caledonian

1. ADJ

/ nu ˌkælɪˈdoʊniən / (AmE), / njuː ˌkælɪˈdəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of New Caledonia, its people, or their culture.
of or relating to New Caledonia

Examples

The New Caledonian reef is home to many unique marine species.
They studied the traditional music of New Caledonian communities.
A New Caledonian festival was held in the city to celebrate the culture.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Caledonian - of or relating to Caledonia (an old name for Scotland)

Etymology

From New Caledonia (a group of islands in the southwest Pacific) + -ian (adjective and demonym suffix).
