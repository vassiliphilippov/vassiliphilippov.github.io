vivaciously

1. ADV

/ vɪˈveɪʃəsli / (AmE), / vɪˈveɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a lively and animated manner; with high-spirited energy.
in a lively and animated way

Examples

The actress spoke vivaciously about her new film, captivating the entire audience.
She danced vivaciously at the celebration, her energy infecting everyone around her.
His stories were always told vivaciously, making even ordinary events seem extraordinary.

Synset Examples

he describes his adventures vivaciously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vivacious - lively, full of energy

Etymology

From 'vivacious' + adverbial suffix '-ly'; 'vivacious' from Latin 'vivax' (full of life).
