gutlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌt.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɡʌt.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
lack of courage or determination; the quality of being afraid to take risks or face difficulties
lack of courage or determination

Examples

His gutlessness was evident when he refused to speak up for what was right.
Many critics blamed the government’s gutlessness for the failure to act decisively.
She overcame her gutlessness and finally confronted her fears.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cowardice - lack of bravery
  • Antonym: bravery - courageous behavior or character

Etymology

From gutless + -ness, meaning lacking physical or moral guts (courage).
