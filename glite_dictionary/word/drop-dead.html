drop-dead

1. ADV

/ ˈdrɑpˌdɛd / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpˌdɛd / (BrE)
used as an intensifier to emphasize that someone or something is extremely attractive or impressive; stunningly or breathtakingly.
extremely; stunningly or breathtakingly
informal

Examples

The actress looked drop-dead gorgeous in her red dress at the awards ceremony.
He walked into the room and saw a drop-dead beautiful view out the window.
Her style is always drop-dead fashionable, turning heads wherever she goes.

Synset Examples

she was drop-dead gorgeous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gorgeously - in a very beautiful or attractive manner
  • Antonym: plainly - in a simple or unremarkable manner
