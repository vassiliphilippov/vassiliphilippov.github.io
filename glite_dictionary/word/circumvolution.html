circumvolution

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɝː.kəm.vəˈluː.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɜː.kəm.vəˈluː.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of turning, winding, or folding around a central axis
turning or winding around an axis

Examples

The circumvolution of the vine around the trellis added a decorative touch to the garden.
In anatomy, a circumvolution refers to the twisting or folding of brain tissue.
He marveled at the circumvolution of the staircase as it spiraled upwards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: convolution - a coil or twist, especially one of many

Etymology

From Latin circumvolutio, from circumvolvere ‘to roll around’.
