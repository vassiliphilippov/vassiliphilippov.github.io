San Jose

1. PROPN

/ ˌsæn hoʊˈzeɪ / (AmE), / ˌsan həʊˈzeɪ / (BrE)
a city in western California at the southern end of San Francisco Bay, known for its computer and electronics industries
city in California, USA

Examples

Many tech companies have their headquarters in San Jose.
She moved to San Jose to work in the technology sector.
The climate in San Jose is generally warm and sunny throughout the year.

Etymology

From Spanish, meaning 'Saint Joseph'.
