unrestrainedly

1. ADV

/ ʌn.rɪˈstreɪnd.li / (AmE), / ʌn.rɪˈstreɪnd.lɪ / (BrE)
In a manner that is not controlled or limited; without restraint or inhibition.
without restraint or limitation

Examples

She danced unrestrainedly, letting the music guide her every move.
The children laughed unrestrainedly at the clown's silly antics.
He expressed his joy unrestrainedly, not caring who was watching.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freely - without restriction or limitation
  • Antonym: restrainedly - in a restrained or controlled manner
