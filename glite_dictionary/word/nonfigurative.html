nonfigurative

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˈfɪɡjərətɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈfɪɡjʊrətɪv / (BrE)
(of art) not representing or imitating real objects or scenes from nature; abstract.
not representing real objects; abstract (in art)

Examples

Many modern artists create nonfigurative works that rely solely on color and shape.
The museum features several impressive nonfigurative sculptures in its contemporary collection.
She prefers nonfigurative paintings because they allow for more personal interpretation.

Synset Examples

a large abstract painting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abstract - not representing recognizable reality; non-representational (in art)
  • Antonym: figurative - representing forms or objects from real world

Etymology

From non- (not) + figurative (representing forms or objects).
