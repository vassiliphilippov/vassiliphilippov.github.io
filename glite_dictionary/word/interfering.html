interfering

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntɚˈfɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈfɪə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
too interested in other people's affairs and trying to influence them in a way that is annoying or unwanted
meddling in other people's affairs

Examples

Her neighbors complained that she was always interfering in their personal lives.
I find his interfering attitude both irritating and unnecessary.
No one likes an interfering colleague who cannot mind their own business.

Synset Examples

an interfering old woman
bustling about self-importantly making an officious nuisance of himself
busy about other people's business

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meddlesome - fond of interfering in others' affairs
  • Synonym: officious - excessively eager to offer unwanted help or advice
  • Antonym: unobtrusive - not attracting attention or interfering
