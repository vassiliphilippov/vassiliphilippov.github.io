grayhen

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪˌhɛn / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪˌhɛn / (BrE)
the female of the black grouse, typically with brownish-grey plumage; also called 'greyhen' in British English
female black grouse

Examples

The grayhen is much less showy than the black-colored male.
We spotted a grayhen quietly feeding near the edge of the forest.
Unlike the noisy cocks, the grayhen tends to stay hidden among the underbrush.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black grouse - a Eurasian bird known for its black plumage in males and distinctive courtship display
