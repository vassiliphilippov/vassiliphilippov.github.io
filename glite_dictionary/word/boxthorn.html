boxthorn

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑksˌθɔrn / (AmE), / ˈbɒksˌθɔːn / (BrE)
any of various shrubs or vines of the genus Lycium, often with showy flowers and bright berries
shrub or vine of the genus Lycium

Examples

The farmer planted a row of boxthorn along the edge of the field as a natural barrier.
Some species of boxthorn produce edible berries that are rich in nutrients.
The vibrant flowers of the boxthorn attract many pollinators during spring.
