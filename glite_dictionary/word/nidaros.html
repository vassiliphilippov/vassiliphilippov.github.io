Nidaros

1. PROPN

/ ˈniːdəˌrɔs / (AmE), / ˈniːdəˌrɒs / (BrE)
a former name for Trondheim, a city and port in central Norway, located on Trondheim Fjord
historic name for Trondheim, Norway

Examples

The medieval city of Nidaros was once the capital of Norway.
Pilgrims traveled for miles to visit the cathedral in Nidaros.
Nidaros played a vital role in Norwegian history during the Viking Age.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Trondheim - modern name for the same city in Norway

Etymology

From Old Norse *Niðaróss*, meaning 'mouth of the River Nid'.
