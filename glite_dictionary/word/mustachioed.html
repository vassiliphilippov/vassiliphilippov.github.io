mustachioed

1. ADJ

/ məˈstæʃioʊd / (AmE), / məˈstɑːʃiəʊd / (BrE)
having a moustache, especially a prominent or well-groomed one.
having a moustache

Examples

The mustachioed detective entered the room with a stern expression.
A mustachioed gentleman tipped his hat as he strolled down the avenue.
With his mustachioed face and vintage suit, he looked straight out of a classic film.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clean-shaven - having no facial hair

Etymology

From Italian 'mustacchio' (moustache) combined with the English adjectival suffix '-ed'.
