Prague

1. PROPN

/ ˈprɑːɡ / (AmE), / ˈprɑːɡ / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of the Czech Republic, located in the western part of the country, known for its historical and cultural significance
capital city of the Czech Republic

Examples

My dream is to visit Prague and see its famous Old Town Square.
They spent their honeymoon in Prague, exploring its medieval streets and castles.
The international conference will be held in Prague next summer.

Etymology

From Czech 'Praha', the local name for the city.
