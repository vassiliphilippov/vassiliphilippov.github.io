sobralia

1. NOUN

/ soʊˈbrɑːliə / (AmE), / səʊˈbrɑːlɪə / (BrE)
any of a group of showy tropical orchids of the genus Sobralia, distinguished by their leafy stems and colorful, often solitary or clustered flowers resembling those of Cattleya.
a genus of showy tropical orchids

Examples

The sobralia is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its large, brightly colored blooms.
We saw a rare sobralia growing wild in the rainforest during our trip.
Several species of sobralia thrive in Central and South America, displaying spectacular flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cattleya - a genus of tropical American orchids with showy flowers

Etymology

Named after Francisco Sobral, a Spanish botanist.
