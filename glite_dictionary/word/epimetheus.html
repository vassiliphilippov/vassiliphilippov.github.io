Epimetheus

1. PROPN

/ ˌɛpɪˈmiːθiəs / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈmiːθiəs / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, the brother of Prometheus who accepted Pandora as his wife despite warnings, thus unleashing misfortunes upon humanity.
brother of Prometheus in Greek mythology

Examples

According to the myth, Epimetheus ignored his brother's advice and married Pandora.
Many ancient writers described Epimetheus as less wise than his brother Prometheus.
The story of Epimetheus and Pandora explains the origin of human suffering in Greek mythology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Prometheus - brother of Epimetheus who stole fire for humanity in Greek mythology
  • Synonym: Pandora - the first woman in Greek mythology, given to Epimetheus as a gift by Zeus

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Ἐπιμηθεύς (Epimētheús), meaning 'afterthought.'
