Pitymys

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪtɪˌmɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɪtɪˌmɪs / (BrE)
A genus of rodents known as pine mice, small burrowing mammals found in North America.
genus of rodents (pine mice)

Examples

The field researchers trapped several Pitymys in their study of woodland rodents.
Unlike other voles, Pitymys species prefer underground tunnels for nesting.
The population of Pitymys has declined due to habitat destruction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pine mouse - a small burrowing rodent of North America, member of Pitymys

Etymology

From New Latin, based on Greek 'pitys' (pine) + 'mys' (mouse).
