saleswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪlzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈseɪlzmʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to sell goods or services
female salesperson

Examples

The saleswoman helped me choose the right size for my shoes.
As a saleswoman, she always met her monthly targets.
The saleswoman answered all of my questions about the new product.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salesman - a man whose job is to sell goods or services
  • Synonym: salesperson - a person whose job is to sell goods or services, gender-neutral

Etymology

Formed from 'sales' + 'woman', modeled after 'salesman'.
