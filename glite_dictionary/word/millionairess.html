millionairess

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪljəˈnɛrɪs / (AmE), / ˌmɪljəˈnɛərɪs / (BrE)
a woman who has assets worth at least a million units of currency
female millionaire

Examples

The reclusive millionairess donated much of her fortune to charity.
Rumors about the young millionairess quickly spread through high society circles.
As a self-made millionairess, she became an inspiration to other businesswomen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: millionaire - a person whose assets are worth one million or more units of currency

Etymology

Formed by combining 'millionaire' with the feminine suffix '-ess'.
