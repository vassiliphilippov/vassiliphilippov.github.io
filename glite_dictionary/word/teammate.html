teammate

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiːmˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈtiːmmeɪt / (BrE)
a person who is a fellow member of the same team
fellow member of a team

Examples

Each teammate played a crucial role in securing the victory.
She always supports her teammates both on and off the field.
After scoring the goal, he celebrated with his teammates.

Synset Examples

it was his first start against his former teammates

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colleague - a person you work with, especially in a profession or business
  • Antonym: opponent - a person who competes against you or your team

Etymology

From 'team' + 'mate' (companion or partner).
