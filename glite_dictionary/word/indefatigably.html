indefatigably

1. ADV

/ ˌɪndɪˈfætɪɡəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈfatɪɡəbli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows no signs of tiring or giving up; with tireless energy.
in a tireless or persistent manner

Examples

She indefatigably pursued her research despite many setbacks.
The volunteers worked indefatigably to help those in need after the disaster.
He campaigned indefatigably for environmental awareness throughout his life.

Synset Examples

she watched the show indefatigably

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tirelessly - without becoming tired or losing persistence
  • Synonym: doggedly - in a manner that shows grim persistence
  • Antonym: lazily - in a manner that shows lack of effort or energy

Etymology

From 'indefatigable' (from Latin 'indefatigabilis': in- 'not' + defatigare 'to tire out') + -ly.
