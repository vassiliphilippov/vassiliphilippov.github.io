nauch

1. NOUN

/ nɑʊtʃ / (AmE), / naʊtʃ / (BrE)
an intricate traditional Indian dance performed by professional female dancers, often as entertainment at courts or social gatherings
traditional Indian dance by professional female dancers

Examples

The royal guests were entertained with a nauch performed by skilled dancers.
She studied the history of nauch as part of her research on Indian classical arts.
In the 19th century, a nauch was often a highlight of festive celebrations in northern India.

Etymology

From Hindi *nāc* (dance), from Sanskrit *nāṭya* (dance, drama).
