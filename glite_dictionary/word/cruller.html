cruller

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrʌlɚ / (AmE), / ˈkrʌlə / (BrE)
A small, sweet, deep-fried pastry twisted into a strip, usually richer than a doughnut.
twisted, deep-fried pastry

Examples

She ordered a cruller and a cup of coffee for breakfast.
The crullers at this bakery are glazed and deliciously crispy.
I prefer a cruller to a regular doughnut because of its unique texture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doughnut - a small fried cake of sweetened dough, typically ring-shaped

Etymology

From Dutch 'kruller', from 'krullen' (to curl).
