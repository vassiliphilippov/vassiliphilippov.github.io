Haemulon macrostomum

1. NOUN

/ hiːˈmjuːlɒn ˌmækrəˈstoʊməm / (AmE), / hiːˈmjuːlɒn ˌmækrəˈstəʊməm / (BrE)
A species of grunt fish found in the western Atlantic, known for producing grunting sounds.
a species of grunt fish

Examples

The Haemulon macrostomum is often found near coral reefs in the Caribbean.
Scientists observed how the Haemulon macrostomum communicates through low-frequency sounds.
Divers frequently spot Haemulon macrostomum while exploring coastal waters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grunt - common name for fish of the family Haemulidae, known for grunting sounds

Etymology

From Latin: *haemulon* (a kind of fish) and Greek *makros* (large) + *stoma* (mouth).
