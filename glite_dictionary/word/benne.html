benne

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛni / (AmE), / ˈbɛni / (BrE)
An East Indian annual plant (Sesamum indicum) cultivated for its seeds, which are used as food and as a source of sesame oil.
the sesame plant (Sesamum indicum)

Examples

Bakers sprinkled benne seeds on the bread for added flavor and texture.
The fields were full of flowering benne plants ready for harvest.
Southern recipes often call for benne seeds to achieve a traditional taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sesame - another name for the benne plant and its seeds

Etymology

From Hindi 'benī' or Tamil 'vēṇṇai', referring to sesame.
