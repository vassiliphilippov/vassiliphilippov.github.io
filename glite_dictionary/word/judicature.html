/ ˈdʒuːdɪˌkeɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːdɪkətʃə / (BrE)
the administration of justice according to law; the act of exercising judicial authority
the act of administering justice
legal

Examples

The supreme judicature of the country is responsible for upholding constitutional law.

He studied the principles of judicature during his legal education.

Related Terms
