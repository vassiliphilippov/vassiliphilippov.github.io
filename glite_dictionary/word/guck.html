guck

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌk / (AmE), / ɡʌk / (BrE)
any thick, sticky, or viscous substance, especially something unpleasant or messy
thick, sticky, messy substance
informal

Examples

The drain was clogged with some kind of black guck.
When he opened the jar, green guck oozed out onto the counter.
Please wipe the guck from your shoes before coming inside.
