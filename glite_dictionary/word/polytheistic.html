polytheistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɑːliθiˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪθiˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
Believing in or worshipping more than one god.
worshipping more than one god

Examples

Ancient Greek religion was highly polytheistic, with gods for nearly every aspect of life.
Some modern spiritual movements have revived polytheistic traditions.
The mythology course covers both monotheistic and polytheistic belief systems.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: monotheistic - Believing in only one god

Etymology

From Greek 'polytheos' (of many gods) + English '-istic'.
