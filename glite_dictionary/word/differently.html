differently

1. ADV

/ ˈdɪf.ɚ.ənt.li / (AmE), / ˈdɪf.ər.ənt.li / (BrE)
In a way that is not the same as something or someone else; in another manner.
in another or different manner

Examples

Despite facing the same problem, she handled it differently from her colleagues.
If you see things differently, please share your perspective.
People from different cultures often solve challenges differently.

Synset Examples

very soon you will know differently
she thought otherwise
there is no way out other than the fire escape

Related Terms

  • Antonym: similarly - in a similar way or manner

Etymology

From 'different' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
