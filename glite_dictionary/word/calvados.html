Calvados

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæl.vəˌdoʊs / (AmE), / ˈkæl.və.dɒs / (BrE)
A dry apple brandy made in the Normandy region of France.
apple brandy from Normandy, France

Examples

After dinner, he enjoyed a glass of Calvados as a digestif.
The region of Normandy is famous for producing Calvados of exceptional quality.
She brought back a bottle of Calvados from her trip to France.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brandy - a strong alcoholic spirit distilled from wine or fruit

Etymology

Named after Calvados, the region in Normandy, France where it is produced.
