orderer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdərər / (AmE), / ˈɔːdərə / (BrE)
a person who arranges or organizes things in an orderly way
one who arranges or organizes

Examples

As the chief orderer, she made sure every file was in its right place.
He became known as an orderer of chaos in his workplace.
The famous philosopher was an orderer of complex ideas into clear theories.

Synset Examples

Aristotle was a great orderer of ideas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organizer - a person who arranges or coordinates things
  • Antonym: disorganizer - a person who makes things disordered or chaotic

Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdərər / (AmE), / ˈɔːdərə / (BrE)
a person or organization that places an order to buy goods or services
person who places an order

Examples

The orderer must provide valid payment details before the request is processed.
All correspondence will be sent directly to the orderer of the product.
As the orderers changed their specifications, the supplier adjusted the shipment accordingly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buyer - a person who buys something
