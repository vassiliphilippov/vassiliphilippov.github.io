coverlet

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌvərlət / (AmE), / ˈkʌvəlɪt / (BrE)
a decorative bedspread, typically quilted, used as a covering for a bed
decorative quilted bedspread

Examples

She spread the new coverlet over the bed to complete the room's look.
The old-fashioned coverlet was hand-stitched by her grandmother.
A light blue coverlet added a touch of color to the guest room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedspread - a decorative cover for a bed, usually heavier and covering the whole bed
  • Synonym: quilt - a warm bed covering made of layers of fabric stitched together

Etymology

From Middle English, from Anglo-Norman *coverlet*, diminutive of Old French *covrir* (to cover).
