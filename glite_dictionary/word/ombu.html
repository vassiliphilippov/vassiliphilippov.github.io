ombu

1. NOUN

/ ɑmˈbu / (AmE), / ɒmˈbuː / (BrE)
a fast-growing evergreen tree native to South America, with a broad trunk containing high water content and dark green oval leaves
South American evergreen tree

Examples

The huge ombu provided much-needed shade in the Argentine plain.
Farmers rely on ombu trees as landmarks across the pampas.
The distinctive shape of the ombu makes it easy to spot from a distance.

Etymology

From Spanish 'ombú', from Guaraní language.
