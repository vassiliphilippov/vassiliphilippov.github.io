doorstop

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔːr.stɑːp / (AmE), / ˈdɔː.stɒp / (BrE)
An object placed on the floor or attached to a wall to prevent a door from closing or moving.
object for holding a door open or stopping it from moving

Examples

She used a rubber doorstop to keep the front door open while moving boxes.
The heavy metal doorstop prevented the wind from slamming the door shut.
Without a doorstop, the classroom door kept swinging closed during the lesson.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doorstopper - another word for an object keeping a door open or stopping it
