curly-leafed

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɝːli liːft / (AmE), / ˈkɜːli liːft / (BrE)
(of a plant) having leaves that are curled or wavy in shape.
having curly or wavy leaves

Examples

The curly-leafed parsley added texture to the salad.
Gardeners often choose curly-leafed varieties for their unique appearance.
Some species develop curly-leafed foliage in response to certain environmental conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curly - having curls or waves
