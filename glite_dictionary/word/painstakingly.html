painstakingly

1. ADV

/ ˈpeɪnˌsteɪkɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈpeɪnˌsteɪkɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows or involves great care and thoroughness
with great care and thoroughness

Examples

She painstakingly restored the old painting over several months.
The research team painstakingly collected data from hundreds of participants.
Each detail of the model ship was painstakingly crafted by hand.

Synset Examples

painstakingly learn the routines of selling

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carefully - with great attention or caution
  • Antonym: hastily - done with excessive speed or urgency

Etymology

From 'painstaking' (showing diligent care), formed as 'pain(s)' + verb 'take' + suffix '-ing', with adverbial '-ly'.
