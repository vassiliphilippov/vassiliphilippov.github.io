Groenendael

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡroʊ.nənˈdæl / (AmE), / ˌɡruː.nənˈdæl / (BrE)
A black-coated Belgian sheepdog breed with a long, dense coat and a heavily plumed tail.
Belgian black sheepdog breed

Examples

The Groenendael is renowned for its intelligence and graceful appearance.
At the dog show, her Groenendael won first prize in the herding group.
Many police forces utilize the Groenendael for search and rescue missions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Belgian Sheepdog - A breed of sheepdog from Belgium, also known as Groenendael

Etymology

From Groenendael, a village near Brussels, Belgium, where the breed originated.
