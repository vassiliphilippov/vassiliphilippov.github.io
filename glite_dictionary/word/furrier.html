furrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɜriər / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəriə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make, repair, or sell garments made from fur
person who makes or sells fur garments

Examples

My grandmother worked as a furrier in Paris during the 1950s.
The furrier skillfully repaired the tear in the expensive fur coat.
She brought her vintage coat to the local furrier for cleaning and restoration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tailor - a person whose occupation is making or altering clothing

Etymology

From Old French *fourrier* (dealer in furs), from *fourrure* (fur).
