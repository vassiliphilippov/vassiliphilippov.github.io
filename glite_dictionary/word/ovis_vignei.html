Ovis vignei

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊvɪs vɪɡˈnaɪ / (AmE), / ˈəʊvɪs vɪɡˈniː / (BrE)
A species of wild reddish-brown sheep with a beard, native to southern Asia; also known as the urial.
wild reddish sheep of southern Asia

Examples

The Ovis vignei is adapted to the rocky hills of the southern Asian mountains.
Researchers have observed the distinctive beard of the Ovis vignei during field studies.
Populations of Ovis vignei are declining due to habitat loss and hunting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urial - another name for Ovis vignei, wild sheep of southern Asia

Etymology

From Latin *Ovis* (sheep) + specific epithet from a local name in the region.
