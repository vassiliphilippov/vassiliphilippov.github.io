pinkish

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɪŋkɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋkɪʃ / (BrE)
having a slight or pale pink color; somewhat pink.
somewhat pink in color

Examples

The sky turned a beautiful pinkish hue as the sun set.
She wore a pinkish dress to the party, which looked elegant in the evening light.
The flower petals are pinkish, fading to white at the edges.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: pink - a color between red and white
