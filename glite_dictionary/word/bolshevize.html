to bolshevize

1. VERB

/ ˈboʊlʃəˌvaɪz / (AmE), / ˈbɒlʃɪvaɪz / (BrE)
to make (a country, institution, or activity) Communist or bring into line with Communist principles
make Communist; align with Communist principles

Examples

After the revolution, the new leaders attempted to bolshevize the entire educational system.
Efforts to bolshevize the industry met with fierce resistance from local workers.
Some historians debate how successful the regime was in trying to bolshevize rural communities.

Synset Examples

communize the government

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to communize - make Communist in character or organization

Etymology

From Bolshevik, the Russian Communist group, plus the suffix -ize.
