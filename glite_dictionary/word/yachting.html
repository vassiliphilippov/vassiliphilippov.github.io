yachting

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɑt̬ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈjɒtɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or sport of sailing or cruising in yachts for pleasure
sailing yachts for pleasure

Examples

He spends his weekends yachting along the Mediterranean coast with friends.
The international yachting competition attracted teams from all over the world.
Many people dream of yachting around the Caribbean islands on their holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boating - traveling or sailing in boats, especially for pleasure

Etymology

From 'yacht' (Dutch 'jacht', meaning 'hunt') + '-ing' suffix.
