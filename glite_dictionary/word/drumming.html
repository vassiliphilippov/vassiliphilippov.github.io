drumming

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or activity of playing drums, especially as a form of musical expression
the act of playing drums

Examples

Her passion for drumming led her to join the school band.
You could hear the sound of drumming from the studio late at night.
He improved his drumming skills by practicing every weekend.

Synset Examples

he practiced his drumming several hours every day

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drum - a musical instrument sounded by being struck
