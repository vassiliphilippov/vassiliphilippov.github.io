feather-foil

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛðər fɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈfɛðə fɔɪl / (BrE)
A small aquatic plant of the genus Hottonia, with finely divided leaves resembling feathers.
an aquatic plant (Hottonia genus) with feathery leaves

Examples

The pond was covered in feather-foil, its delicate leaves swaying underwater.
Botanists studied the ecosystem where feather-foil grows abundantly.
Gardeners sometimes introduce feather-foil to add interest to aquatic displays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hottonia - genus of aquatic plants with divided leaves, including feather-foil
