Indigirka

1. PROPN

/ ˌɪndɪˈɡɪrkə / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈɡɪːkə / (BrE)
a river in far eastern Siberia, Russia, that flows generally northward into the Arctic Ocean
river in eastern Siberia, Russia

Examples

The Indigirka freezes over for several months during the harsh Siberian winter.
Scientists conducted climate research along the Indigirka basin in recent years.
Trade routes once followed the course of the Indigirka to the Arctic Ocean.

Etymology

From Russian Индиги́рка (Indigirka), possibly from a local Yakut name.
