verticality

1. NOUN

/ ˌvɜːr.tɪˈkæ.lə.ti / (AmE), / ˌvɜː.tɪˈkæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being vertical; position at a right angle to the horizon
state of being vertical

Examples

The designer emphasized the verticality of the structure by using tall, slender columns.
Modern skyscrapers showcase the remarkable verticality possible with current engineering.
The photograph captures the forest's verticality, drawing the eye upward to the treetops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vertical - in a position that is at a right angle to the horizon
  • Antonym: horizontality - the quality or state of being horizontal

Etymology

From French 'verticalité', from Latin 'verticalis' (vertical).
