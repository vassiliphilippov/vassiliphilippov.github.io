Nimrod

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɪm.rɑd / (AmE), / ˈnɪm.rɒd / (BrE)
A legendary hunter and king mentioned in the Old Testament, renowned for his great hunting skills.
biblical hunter and king

Examples

In ancient tales, Nimrod is praised as the mightiest hunter of his time.
Many historians debate the true origins of Nimrod as described in the Old Testament.
The legend of Nimrod has inspired countless hunting stories throughout history.

Etymology

From Hebrew נִמְרוֹד (Nimrod), a biblical name.
