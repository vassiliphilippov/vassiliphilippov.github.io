westside

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛstˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɛstˌsaɪd / (BrE)
of or relating to the western part of a city or area.
of the western part of a city or area

Examples

Many new restaurants have opened on the westside in the past year.
She moved from the westside to be closer to her workplace downtown.
The westside neighborhood is known for its tree-lined streets and lively cafes.
