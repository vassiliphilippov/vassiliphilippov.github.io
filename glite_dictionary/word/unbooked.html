unbooked

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbʊkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈbʊkt / (BrE)
(of a seat, room, etc.) not reserved or arranged in advance.
not reserved in advance

Examples

Several unbooked seats remained available at the start of the performance.
We decided to try our luck and walk in, hoping there would be an unbooked table.
Last-minute travelers can sometimes find unbooked hotel rooms at discounted rates.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: booked - reserved or arranged in advance
  • Synonym: reserved - kept or set aside for a particular person or use

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'booked', the past participle of 'book' (to reserve).
