Bangui

1. PROPN

/ bɑŋˈɡi / (AmE), / bæŋˈɡi / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of the Central African Republic, located on the banks of the Ubangi River.
capital of the Central African Republic

Examples

The United Nations has an office in Bangui to assist with peacekeeping operations.
After flying into Bangui, we traveled upriver to visit local villages.
Many diplomatic missions in the Central African Republic are based in Bangui.
