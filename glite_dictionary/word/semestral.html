semestral

1. ADJ

/ səˈmɛstrəl / (AmE), / sɪˈmɛstrəl / (BrE)
occurring every six months or relating to a semester, especially in an academic context
every six months; relating to a semester

Examples

The university's semestral exams take place in December and June.
She paid her rent on a semestral basis rather than monthly.
The semestral report details the progress made over six months.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: annual - occurring once every year
  • Synonym: biannual - occurring twice a year

Etymology

From Late Latin 'semestris' (of six months), from 'sex' (six) + 'mensis' (month).
