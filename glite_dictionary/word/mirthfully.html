mirthfully

1. ADV

/ ˈmɜrθ.fə.li / (AmE), / ˈmɜːθ.fʊ.li / (BrE)
In a joyous or merrily cheerful manner.
in a joyous, cheerful way

Examples

She laughed mirthfully at his ridiculous costume.
The children played mirthfully in the sunshine, filling the air with laughter.
He recounted the story mirthfully, unable to hide his delight.

Synset Examples

they shouted happily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joyfully - in a happy or joyful way
  • Antonym: gloomily - in a sad or dismal way

Etymology

Formed from 'mirthful' (joyous) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
