unforceful

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfɔrsfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɔːsf(ə)l / (BrE)
Not having force or strength; weak or ineffective.
lacking strength or force

Examples

His apology was so unforceful that few believed he meant it.
The unforceful protest failed to sway the decision-makers.
Her argument was too unforceful to convince anyone in the meeting.

Synset Examples

a forceless argument

Related Terms

  • Antonym: forceful - having force; strong and assertive
  • Synonym: feeble - lacking physical strength, especially as a result of age or illness
