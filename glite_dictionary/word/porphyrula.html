Porphyrula

1. NOUN

/ pɔrˈfɪr.jʊ.lə / (AmE), / pɔːˈfɪr.jʊ.lə / (BrE)
A genus of birds in the rail family, including the American purple gallinules.
genus of birds (purple gallinules)

Examples

The Porphyrula is recognized by its bright plumage and long toes.
Scientists observed several Porphyrula feeding by the edge of the marsh.
Birdwatchers were excited to spot a rare Porphyrula during their morning walk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gallinule - A marsh bird of the rail family related to Porphyrula

Etymology

From Neo-Latin, from Ancient Greek 'porphyra' (purple).
