crochet

1. NOUN

/ kroʊˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / krəʊˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
Needlework made by forming interlocking loops of yarn, thread, or other material with a hooked needle.
needlework using a hooked needle

Examples

She displayed a beautiful lace tablecloth made with crochet at the craft fair.
My grandmother taught me how to do crochet when I was a child.
Many people find crochet to be a relaxing and creative hobby.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knitting - needlework made by interlacing yarn with two needles

Etymology

From French 'crochet', diminutive of 'croche' meaning 'hook'.
