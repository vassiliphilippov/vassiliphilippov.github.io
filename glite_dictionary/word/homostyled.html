homostyled

1. ADJ

/ ˈhoʊmoʊstaɪld / (AmE), / ˈhəʊməstaɪld / (BrE)
(of a flower) having styles all of the same length.
having styles of equal length (botany)
medical

Examples

Primula species can be either homostyled or heterostyled depending on their pollination strategy.
Researchers observed that the homostyled populations produced fewer seeds than heterostyled ones.
All the styles in the homostyled flowers reached the same height within the floral tube.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heterostyled - having styles of different lengths (botany)

Etymology

From Greek 'homo-' (same) + 'style' (pillar, referring to the style in botany).
