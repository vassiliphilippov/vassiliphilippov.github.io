bra

1. NOUN

/ brɑː / (AmE), / brɑː / (BrE)
An undergarment worn by women to support and cover the breasts.
woman's breast-supporting undergarment

Examples

She bought a new bra to match her dress.
After washing, she hung her bra on the clothesline to dry.
It's important to wear a well-fitting bra for comfort and support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brassiere - an undergarment for supporting the breasts

Etymology

Shortened form of 'brassiere', from French 'brassière' (upper arm, child's vest).
