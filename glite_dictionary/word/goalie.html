goalie

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊli / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊli / (BrE)
a player in sports such as ice hockey, soccer, or lacrosse whose job is to protect the goal and prevent the opposing team from scoring
player who guards the goal in team sports

Examples

The goalie made an incredible save in the last minute of the match.
Our team’s goalie wears bright orange gloves to be easily seen on the field.
After the goalie was injured, the backup had to take his place.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goalkeeper - person who guards the goal in sports such as football or hockey
  • Antonym: striker - offensive player, especially in soccer, who tries to score goals

Etymology

Diminutive form of 'goalkeeper'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊli / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊli / (BrE)
the player in sports such as soccer or hockey whose role is to prevent the opposing team from scoring by guarding the goal
player who guards the goal in sports

Examples

Our team's goalie made an incredible save in the final minutes of the match.
She has played as a goalie since she joined the hockey team last year.
During practice, the coach gave tips to the goalie on improving reaction time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goalkeeper - the player who guards the goal in soccer, hockey, etc.
  • Degree Lower: defender - a player whose role is to prevent the other team from scoring

Etymology

Informal shortening of "goalkeeper," from "goal" + "-ie" diminutive suffix.
