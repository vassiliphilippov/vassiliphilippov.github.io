self-explanatory

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlf ɪkˈsplænəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf ɪkˈsplænətri / (BrE)
Easy to understand and not needing any further explanation.
not needing explanation

Examples

The instructions on the box are self-explanatory and easy to follow.
His reason for leaving was self-explanatory given the circumstances.
This chart is quite self-explanatory, so you shouldn't have any trouble understanding it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obvious - Easily perceived or understood; clear, self-evident, or apparent.
  • Antonym: complicated - Consisting of many interconnecting parts or elements; intricate.

Etymology

From self- + explanatory; explanatory from Latin explicāre (to explain).
