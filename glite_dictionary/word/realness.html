realness

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being real or genuine; actuality or authenticity.
state of being real or genuine

Examples

Her realness was appreciated by everyone who met her.
The documentary captured the realness of life in the city.
He admired the realness of her performance on stage.

Synset Examples

the reality of his situation slowly dawned on him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
  • Antonym: fakeness - the state of being not genuine or false

Etymology

From 'real' + '-ness' (forming nouns denoting a state or quality).
