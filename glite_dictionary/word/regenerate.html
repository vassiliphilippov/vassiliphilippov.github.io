regenerate

1. VERB

/ rɪˈdʒɛnəˌreɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈdʒɛnəˌreɪt / (BrE)
to restore strength, energy, or vitality to someone or something; to revitalize
restore strength or energy

Examples

A good night's sleep can regenerate your energy after a long week.
The spa promised to regenerate both body and mind through relaxation treatments.
Fresh air and healthy food helped to regenerate the exhausted hiker.

Synset Examples

This food revitalized the patient

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to revitalize - to give new life or energy to
  • Synonym: to refresh - to give new strength or energy to
  • Antonym: to debilitate - to make weak; to sap strength

Etymology

From Latin regeneratus, past participle of regenerare (to create again, to renew).
