stonily

1. ADV

/ ˈstoʊnɪli / (AmE), / ˈstəʊnɪli / (BrE)
In a manner that is cold, unfeeling, or showing no emotion.
in a cold or unfeeling manner

Examples

She stared stonily ahead, refusing to answer any questions.
He listened stonily to their complaints, unmoved by their distress.
The manager regarded the situation stonily, offering no comfort or sympathy.

Synset Examples

stonily indifferent to time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coldly - in an unemotional or unfriendly way
  • Antonym: warmly - in a friendly or affectionate way

Etymology

From 'stony' + '-ly', stony from Old English 'stānig'.
