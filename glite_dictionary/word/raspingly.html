raspingly

1. ADV

/ ˈræspɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈrɑːspɪŋli / (BrE)
In a harsh, grating, or irritating manner, especially in relation to sound or tone.
in a harsh or grating way

Examples

The teacher spoke raspingly after losing her voice to a cold.
The old record player played raspingly, making the music unpleasant to hear.
He cleared his throat and raspingly warned us not to enter the warehouse.

Synset Examples

her voice fell gratingly on our ears

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harshly - in a rough or unpleasant way
  • Antonym: smoothly - in a gentle or even way, opposite of harshly or gratingly

Etymology

From 'rasping' + '-ly'. 'Rasp' from Old French 'rasper' (to scrape).
