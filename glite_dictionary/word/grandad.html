grandad

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrænˌdæd / (AmE), / ˈɡrænˌdad / (BrE)
the father of one's father or mother; grandfather.
father of one's parent; grandfather

Examples

My grandad used to tell us stories from his childhood every night.
We're visiting my grandad in the countryside this weekend.
After retiring, grandad took up gardening as a new hobby.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandfather - the father of one's father or mother
  • Antonym: grandma - the mother of one's father or mother

Etymology

Diminutive form of 'granddad' or 'grandfather', from 'grand-' (great) + 'dad' (father).
