glutinosity

1. NOUN

/ ɡluːˈtɪnəsɪti / (AmE), / ɡluːˈtɪnəsɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being sticky or having a jelly-like viscosity
stickiness; jelly-like viscosity

Examples

The glutinosity of the mixture made it difficult to pour from the bowl.
Scientists measured the glutinosity of the newly synthesized gel for potential industrial uses.
The dessert's unique glutinosity gave it an appealing, chewy texture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: viscosity - the thickness or stickiness of a fluid
  • Synonym: glutinous - having a sticky or gluey texture

Etymology

From Latin 'glutinosus' (glutinous, sticky) + English suffix '-ity'.
