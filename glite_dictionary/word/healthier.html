healthier

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛlθiər / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθɪə / (BrE)
in a better state of health; having improved health or physical condition, especially compared to another person or time
comparative of healthy; more healthy

Examples

After changing her diet, Maria felt much healthier within a few weeks.
Regular exercise can make you healthier both physically and mentally.
Research shows that people with pets tend to be healthier and happier.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: healthy - in good health; free from illness
  • Degree Higher: healthiest - in the best possible health; superlative of healthy
