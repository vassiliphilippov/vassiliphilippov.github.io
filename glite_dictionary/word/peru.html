Peru

1. PROPN

/ pəˈru / (AmE), / pəˈruː / (BrE)
A country in western South America, formerly the center of the Inca Empire, which gained independence from Spain in 1821.
a country in South America

Examples

She traveled to Peru to visit the ancient ruins of Machu Picchu.
The cuisine of Peru is famous for its unique flavors and ingredients.
After gaining independence, Peru established itself as a republic in 1821.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Perú', from Quechua 'Birú', the name of a local ruler at the time of Spanish contact.
