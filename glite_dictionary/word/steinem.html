Steinem

1. PROPN

/ ˈstaɪnəm / (AmE), / ˈstaɪnəm / (BrE)
Gloria Steinem (born 1934), a prominent American feminist, journalist, and social activist known for her leadership in the women's rights movement.
American feminist and activist

Examples

Steinem has written extensively on feminism and social issues since the 1960s.
Many people consider Steinem a key figure in the women's liberation movement.
The magazine co-founded by Steinem became a voice for gender equality.
