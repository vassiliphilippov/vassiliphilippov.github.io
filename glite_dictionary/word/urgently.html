urgently

1. ADV

/ ˈɝːdʒəntli / (AmE), / ˈɜːdʒəntli / (BrE)
in a way that requires immediate attention or action
with great urgency

Examples

We need to speak urgently about the situation before it gets worse.
The patient was rushed urgently to the hospital after the accident.
Please respond urgently if you require immediate assistance.

Synset Examples

health care reform is needed urgently
the soil desperately needed potash

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desperately - in a way that shows great need or urgency

Etymology

Formed from 'urgent' + '-ly'; 'urgent' from Latin 'urgent-', meaning pressing, urgent.
