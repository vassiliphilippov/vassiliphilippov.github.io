lunkhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌŋkˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈlʌŋkˌhɛd / (BrE)
A person who is regarded as stupid or foolish.
a stupid or foolish person
informal

Examples

If you keep acting like a lunkhead, you'll never get the job done.
Don't be such a lunkhead—read the instructions before starting!
She laughed and called her brother a lunkhead for forgetting his keys again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blockhead - a stupid person
  • Synonym: dunce - a person who is slow to learn or stupid
  • Antonym: genius - a very intelligent person

Etymology

Probably from 'lunk', of uncertain origin, combined with 'head' in the sense of a slow-witted person. Modelled after similar terms like 'blockhead'.
