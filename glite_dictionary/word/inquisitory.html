inquisitory

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈkwɪzətɔri / (AmE), / ɪnˈkwɪzɪtəri / (BrE)
characterized by diligent and thorough inquiry or investigation; searching or probing.
thorough and searching in inquiry

Examples

The committee's inquisitory approach uncovered several inconsistencies in the report.
Her inquisitory mind led her to ask many insightful questions during the meeting.
The inspector carried out an inquisitory review of the company's safety procedures.

Synset Examples

a probing inquiry
a searching investigation of their past dealings

Related Terms

  • Synonym: probing - examining closely or thoroughly; searching
  • Synonym: searching - examining carefully and thoroughly; penetrating

Etymology

From Latin 'inquisitorius,' from 'inquisitor' (inquirer, examiner).
