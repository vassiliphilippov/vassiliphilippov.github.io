mesquit

1. NOUN

/ mɛsˈkit / (AmE), / mɛsˈkiːt / (BrE)
a small spiny tree or shrub of the genus Prosopis, native to arid regions, producing sweet pods and often used for wood or as forage
small spiny tree or shrub of genus Prosopis

Examples

The mesquit provides important food and shelter for many desert animals.
After the storm, several mesquits had lost their branches along the arroyo.
The sweet pods of the mesquit are sometimes ground into flour by local communities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mesquite - a small spiny tree or shrub of the genus Prosopis

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'mesquite', from Spanish 'mezquite', from Nahuatl 'mizquitl'.
