executant

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˈzɛk.ju.ənt / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɛk.ju.ənt / (BrE)
a person who performs a musical or artistic work, especially with skill
performer of a musical or artistic work

Examples

The celebrated pianist is known as an outstanding executant of Chopin's works.
As an executant, she approaches each piece with both precision and creativity.
The competition seeks to discover the most promising young executant in the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: performer - a person who performs in front of an audience

Etymology

From Latin 'exsequi' (to carry out, accomplish), via French 'exécutant'.
