loathsome

1. ADJ

/ ˈloʊð.səm / (AmE), / ˈləʊð.səm / (BrE)
causing hatred or strong disgust; highly offensive or repulsive
causing disgust or hatred

Examples

The abandoned house had a loathsome odor that made everyone gag.
He considered the criminal's actions truly loathsome and without justification.
Many people find the practice of animal cruelty absolutely loathsome.

Synset Examples

a disgusting smell
distasteful language

the idea of eating meat is repellent to me
revolting food
a wicked stench

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disgusting - causing a strong feeling of dislike or disapproval
  • Synonym: repulsive - causing strong dislike or disgust
  • Antonym: delightful - very pleasing, attractive, or enjoyable

Etymology

From Middle English lothsum, from loth (to hate) + -some (causing).
