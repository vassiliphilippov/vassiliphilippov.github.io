Francis Hopkinson

1. PROPN

/ ˈfræn.sɪs ˈhɑp.kɪn.sən / (AmE), / ˈfræn.sɪs ˈhɒp.kɪn.sən / (BrE)

American Revolutionary leader and signer of Declaration
