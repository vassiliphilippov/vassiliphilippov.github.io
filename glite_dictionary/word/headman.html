headman

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈhɛd.mæn / (BrE)
a man whose job is to behead condemned persons, especially as an executioner
male executioner who beheads

Examples

The infamous headman was summoned to carry out the execution at dawn.
Stories of the medieval headman often include details of his grim duties.
The headman sharpened his axe before each public beheading.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: executioner - a person who carries out a death sentence

2. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈhɛd.mæn / (BrE)
the male leader or chief of a tribe or clan
male leader of a tribe or clan

Examples

The headman welcomed the visitors to the village and presided over the meeting.
As headman, he was responsible for resolving disputes within the clan.
The villagers gathered around the headman to listen to his advice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chieftain - the leader of a people or clan
  • Synonym: chief - the person in charge of a group or tribe
