unisex

1. ADJ

/ ˈjuːnɪˌsɛks / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪˌsɛks / (BrE)
Designed or intended to be suitable for both men and women; not distinguished by sex.
suitable for any gender

Examples

Many salons now offer unisex haircuts to appeal to a wider range of customers.
The gym provides unisex changing facilities for convenience.
She bought a unisex watch that suited both her and her partner.

Etymology

Formed from Latin prefix 'uni-' (one) and 'sex' (gender).
