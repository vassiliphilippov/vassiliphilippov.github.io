Thorshavn

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɔrzˌhɑvn / (AmE), / ˈtɔːzˌhɑːvn / (BrE)
the capital and administrative center of the Faroe Islands, located on the island of Streymoy.
capital of the Faroe Islands

Examples

She spent a summer living in Thorshavn while studying the Faroese language.
The ferry from Denmark arrives at the harbor of Thorshavn, the Faroe Islands' capital.
Many travelers are charmed by the colorful houses of Thorshavn.

Etymology

From Faroese 'Tórshavn', meaning 'Thor's harbor'.
