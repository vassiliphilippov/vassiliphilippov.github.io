uninteresting

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪnˈtɚrɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈtrɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
not attracting interest, attention, or excitement; dull or boring.
not interesting; dull

Examples

The lecture was so uninteresting that several students fell asleep.
She found the documentary uninteresting and turned it off halfway through.
Despite its hype, the movie was surprisingly uninteresting.

Synset Examples

a very uninteresting account of her trip

Related Terms

  • Antonym: interesting - arousing curiosity or attention
  • Synonym: boring - not interesting; tedious or dull

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'interesting'.
