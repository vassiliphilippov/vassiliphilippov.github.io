sheet

1. NOUN

/ ʃit / (AmE), / ʃiːt / (BrE)
A large piece of fabric, typically canvas, used to catch the wind and propel a sailing vessel.
canvas sail on a sailing ship

Examples

The crew quickly raised the sheet to catch the strong morning breeze.
With a torn sheet, the yacht struggled to pick up speed.
He repaired the old sheet before setting sail again.
