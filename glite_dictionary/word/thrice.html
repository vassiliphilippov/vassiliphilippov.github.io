thrice

1. ADV

/ θraɪs / (AmE), / θraɪs / (BrE)
Three times; on three occasions.
three times

Examples

He has visited London thrice since last year.
She thrice refused his generous offer.
The bell rang thrice to signal the start of the ceremony.

Synset Examples

I called you thrice last night

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: once - one time; on one occasion
  • Degree Lower: twice - two times; on two occasions
  • Degree Higher: four times - on four occasions

Etymology

From Middle English *thries*, from Old English *þriwa* (three times).
