Maxzide

1. NOUN

/ ˈmækˌzaɪd / (AmE), / ˈmæks.aɪd / (BrE)
A trade name for an antihypertensive medication containing hydrochlorothiazide and triamterene, used to treat high blood pressure and fluid retention.
antihypertensive drug brand name
medical

Examples

Her doctor prescribed Maxzide to help control her high blood pressure.
Some patients experience fewer side effects with Maxzide compared to other diuretics.
You should not take potassium supplements while using Maxzide unless directed by your physician.
