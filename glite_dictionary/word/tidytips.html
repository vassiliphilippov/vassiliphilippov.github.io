tidytips

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪdi tɪps / (AmE), / ˈtaɪdi tɪps / (BrE)
A California annual plant (genus Layia) with yellow flower heads that have white-tipped rays.
California flower with white-tipped yellow rays

Examples

The field was dotted with bright tidytips among the spring grasses.
She planted tidytips in her garden to attract pollinators.
Wildflowers like poppies and tidytips bloom throughout California in the spring.
