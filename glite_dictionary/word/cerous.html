cerous

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛr.əs / (AmE), / ˈsɪə.rəs / (BrE)
relating to or containing cerium with a valence of three (Ce3+)
containing cerium with valence 3

Examples

The chemist studied several cerous compounds to observe their unique properties.
Cerous oxide is used in various catalytic applications within the industry.
Unlike ceric salts, cerous salts contain cerium in the +3 oxidation state.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: ceric - relating to cerium with a valence of four (Ce4+)

Etymology

From cerium + -ous, indicating a lower oxidation state.
