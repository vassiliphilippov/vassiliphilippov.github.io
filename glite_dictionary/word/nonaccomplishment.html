nonaccomplishment

1. NOUN

/ nɑn.əˈkɑm.plɪʃ.mənt / (AmE), / nɒn.əˈkʌm.plɪʃ.mənt / (BrE)
failure to achieve an intended goal or objective.
failure to achieve a goal

Examples

His repeated nonaccomplishment of promised reforms led to a loss of trust.
The project ended in nonaccomplishment due to poor planning and communication.
After much effort, her nonaccomplishment became a source of personal frustration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: failure - lack of success in achieving something
  • Antonym: achievement - something accomplished successfully

Etymology

From prefix 'non-' (not) + 'accomplishment' (something achieved).
