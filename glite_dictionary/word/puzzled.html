puzzled

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʌzəld / (AmE), / ˈpʌzəld / (BrE)
unable to understand something or think clearly because you are confused or bewildered
confused or bewildered

Examples

She looked puzzled when I explained the new schedule.
The students were puzzled by the strange instructions on the exam.
His puzzled expression showed he had no idea what was happening.

Synset Examples

at a loss to understand those remarks
puzzled that she left without saying goodbye

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confused - unable to think clearly or understand something
  • Synonym: bewildered - completely puzzled or confused
  • Antonym: clear - not confused or certain in mind

Etymology

From the verb "puzzle," likely from earlier "pusle" (to bewilder), probably of imitative origin.
