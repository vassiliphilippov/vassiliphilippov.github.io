bottle-fed

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑːtlˌfɛd / (AmE), / ˈbɒtlˌfɛd / (BrE)
(of a baby or young animal) given milk or food from a bottle rather than being breastfed or suckled.
given milk or food from a bottle

Examples

The bottle-fed baby was content after finishing her formula.
Some orphaned kittens have to be bottle-fed every few hours to survive.
He was bottle-fed as an infant because his mother was unable to breastfeed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: breastfed - (of a baby or young animal) nourished at the breast or by suckling directly
