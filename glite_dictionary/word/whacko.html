whacko

1. NOUN

/ ˈwækoʊ / (AmE), / ˈwækəʊ / (BrE)
an eccentric or crazy person; someone who behaves in an unusual or mad way
eccentric or crazy person
informal slang

Examples

People thought the inventor was a total whacko until his ideas changed the world.
There’s always one whacko shouting at pigeons in the park.
My uncle has some wild theories, but he’s no whacko—he just likes to joke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wacko - an eccentric or crazy person, alternative spelling

Etymology

Altered form of 'wacko', informal, probably from 'whack' (to strike) or 'wacky' (crazy, eccentric).
