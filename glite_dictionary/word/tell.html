Tell

1. PROPN

/ tɛl / (AmE), / tɛl / (BrE)
(also William Tell) A legendary Swiss patriot of the early 14th century, renowned for shooting an apple off his son's head with a crossbow, according to folklore.
legendary Swiss folk hero; archer famed for shooting an apple from his son's head

Examples

The story of Tell has been retold in countless books and plays.
Many Swiss towns have monuments dedicated to Tell and his heroic deed.
Legend says that Tell inspired the Swiss people to rise against tyranny.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: William Tell - legendary Swiss folk hero and archer; shot an apple from his son's head

Etymology

From the German 'Wilhelm Tell,' the legendary name of the Swiss hero.
