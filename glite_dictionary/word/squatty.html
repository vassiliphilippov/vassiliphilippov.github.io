squatty

1. ADJ

/ ˈskwɑːti / (AmE), / ˈskwɒti / (BrE)
short and thick or broad in appearance, often with reference to body shape or objects; having a low, sturdy, and sometimes compact build.
short and thick in appearance

Examples

The cottage had a squatty chimney and moss growing on its roof.
That breed of dog is known for its squatty legs and long body.
The old man was a squatty figure, compact and sturdy as a tree stump.

Synset Examples

some people seem born to be square and chunky
a dumpy little dumpling of a woman
dachshunds are long lowset dogs with drooping ears
a little church with a squat tower
a squatty red smokestack
a stumpy ungainly figure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chunky - thick, solid, and short in build or form
  • Synonym: stumpy - short and thick; stubby
  • Antonym: slender - gracefully thin or slim

Etymology

From "squat" (short and thickset) + "-y" (adjective-forming suffix).
