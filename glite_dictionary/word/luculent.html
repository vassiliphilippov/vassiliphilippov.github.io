luculent

1. ADJ

/ ˈluːkjələnt / (AmE), / ˈluːkjʊlənt / (BrE)
(of language or expression) very clear and easy to understand.
clear and easily understandable (esp. of language)

Examples

The professor's luculent lecture made the complex topic accessible to all students.
She praised the report for its luculent explanations and straightforward reasoning.
His arguments were so luculent that even the opposing side could not misunderstand his point.

Synset Examples

writes in a limpid style
lucid directions
a luculent oration
pellucid prose
a crystal clear explanation
a perspicuous argument

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lucid - expressed clearly; easy to understand
  • Synonym: perspicuous - clearly expressed and easy to understand

Etymology

From Latin 'luculentus' (full of light, clear).
