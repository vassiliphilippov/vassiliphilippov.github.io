cheerfully

1. ADV

/ ˈtʃɪr.fə.li / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪə.fə.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows happiness, optimism, or willingness.
in a cheerful manner

Examples

She cheerfully greeted everyone at the door despite the early hour.
Even after a long day, he cheerfully helped his friends move furniture.
The students cheerfully volunteered for the community clean-up project.

Synset Examples

he cheerfully agreed to do it

Related Terms

  • Antonym: gloomily - in a gloomy or dismal manner

Etymology

From 'cheerful' + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
