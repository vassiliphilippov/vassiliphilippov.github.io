blossoming

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɑːs.əm.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblɒs.əm.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or period in which flowers bud and open; the development or flourishing of blossoms
process of flowers budding and opening

Examples

The blossoming of cherry trees in spring draws crowds of visitors every year.
Witnessing the blossoming of the garden over the weeks brought her great joy.
The blossoming of wildflowers transformed the valley into a sea of color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blooming - the process of producing blossoms or flowers

Etymology

From the verb 'blossom,' which comes from Old English blōstm or blōstma (flower, bloom).
