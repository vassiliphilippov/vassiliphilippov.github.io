defrayment

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfreɪmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈfreɪmənt / (BrE)
the act of paying money, especially to cover costs or expenses
the act of paying money

Examples

The defrayment of travel expenses will be processed after the conference concludes.
Your employer is responsible for the defrayment of all authorized costs incurred on business trips.
Government defrayment of medical fees is available for eligible citizens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: payment - the act of paying money
  • Synonym: reimbursement - the act of repaying someone for expenses incurred

Etymology

From French *défrayer* (to pay expenses), from Latin *fractus* (broken, used up).
