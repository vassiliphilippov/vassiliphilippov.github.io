bookkeeping

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊkˌkipɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌkiː.pɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity or process of recording the financial transactions of a business.
the recording of business transactions

Examples

Accurate bookkeeping is essential for any successful small business.
He hired an assistant to handle all the bookkeeping tasks.
Their company's bookkeeping was praised during the annual audit.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: accounting - the process of keeping financial accounts

Etymology

From book + keeping, referring to the practice of keeping records in account books.
