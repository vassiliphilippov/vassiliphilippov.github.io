admittable

1. ADJ

/ ədˈmɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ədˈmɪtəbəl / (BrE)
deserving or able to be allowed to enter or be admitted
deserving to be allowed entry

Examples

Only members with proper identification are admittable to the exclusive lounge.
The venue is not admittable to persons under the age of eighteen.
After a thorough check, the applicant was deemed admittable to the program.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admissible - able to be admitted; acceptable

Etymology

From English 'admit' + '-able', patterned after Latin roots.
