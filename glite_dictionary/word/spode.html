Spode

1. PROPN

/ spoʊd / (AmE), / spəʊd / (BrE)
A brand of fine English porcelain, known for its high quality and traditional designs.
brand of fine English porcelain

Examples

The museum has an extensive collection of Spode porcelain from the 18th century.
She received a set of Spode china as a wedding gift.
Collectors often seek out rare Spode patterns at antique shops.

Etymology

Named after Josiah Spode (1733–1797), English potter who founded the company.
