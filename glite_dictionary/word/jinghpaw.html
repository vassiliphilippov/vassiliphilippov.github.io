Jinghpaw

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪŋ.paʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪŋ.pəʊ / (BrE)
the Kachinic language spoken by the Jinghpaw people of northern Myanmar and neighboring regions
a Kachinic language of northern Myanmar

Examples

Many members of the Kachin community speak Jinghpaw as their first language.
She is studying Jinghpaw to better understand the culture of northern Myanmar.
Bilingual signs in both Burmese and Jinghpaw are common in the region.

Etymology

From the self-designated name of the Jinghpaw people.
