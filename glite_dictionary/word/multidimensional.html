multidimensional

1. ADJ

/ ˌmʌlti.daɪˈmɛnʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌmʌl.ti.dɪˈmɛn.ʃən.əl / (BrE)
Having or involving several dimensions or aspects; marked by complexity or variety.
having several dimensions or aspects

Examples

The scientist conducted a multidimensional analysis of the data to understand the underlying patterns.
Her thesis explores multidimensional approaches to solving urban housing challenges.
The main character is portrayed as a multidimensional figure with both strengths and flaws.

Synset Examples

multidimensional problems
a multidimensional proposition
a multidimensional personality

Related Terms

  • Synonym: complex - having many interconnected parts; complicated

Etymology

Formed from "multi-" (many) and "dimensional" (of dimension); based on Latin roots.
