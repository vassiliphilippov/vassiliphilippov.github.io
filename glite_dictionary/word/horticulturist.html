horticulturist

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɔrtɪˈkʌltʃərɪst / (AmE), / ˌhɔːtɪˈkʌltʃərɪst / (BrE)
A person who is an expert in the science and art of cultivating plants, especially fruits, flowers, vegetables, or ornamental plants.
expert in cultivating plants

Examples

The horticulturist designed the botanical garden's new rose section with great care.
After studying botany, she became a respected horticulturist in her community.
The horticulturist shared tips on growing healthier vegetables during the seminar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gardener - a person who tends and cultivates a garden

Etymology

From Latin hortus (garden) and cultura (cultivation), with the English suffix -ist.
