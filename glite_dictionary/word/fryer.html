fryer

1. NOUN

/ ˈfraɪər / (AmE), / ˈfraɪə / (BrE)
A kitchen appliance used for deep frying food in hot oil.
kitchen appliance for deep frying

Examples

The restaurant bought an industrial fryer to handle large batches of french fries.
Before cooking the chicken, make sure the fryer is set to the correct temperature.
She uses an air fryer to make healthier versions of her favorite snacks.

Synset Examples

Deep fryers are used for cooking many fast foods, and making them crisp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deep fryer - a kitchen appliance for deep frying

2. NOUN

/ ˈfraɪər / (AmE), / ˈfraɪə / (BrE)
the flesh of a young chicken, typically of medium size, suitable for frying
young chicken suitable for frying

Examples

The recipe calls for a fresh fryer to ensure tender meat.
Many Southern dishes are made with a whole fryer cut into pieces.
The butcher recommended buying a fryer for the crispy chicken dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broiler - young chicken suitable for broiling
  • Degree Higher: roaster - chicken suitable for roasting

Etymology

From fry + -er, originally referring to something used for frying, later applied to chickens suitable for this method.
