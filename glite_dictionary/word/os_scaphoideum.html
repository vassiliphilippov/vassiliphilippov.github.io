os scaphoideum

1. NOUN

/ ɑs skæfɔˈdiːəm / (AmE), / ɒs skæfəʊˈdiːəm / (BrE)
the largest carpal bone on the thumb side of the wrist; also known as the scaphoid bone
largest wrist bone on the thumb side
medical

Examples

A fracture of the os scaphoideum can lead to long-term complications if not treated properly.
The doctor explained that pain near the thumb might indicate an injury to the os scaphoideum.
X-rays revealed a small crack in the patient's os scaphoideum after his fall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scaphoid - the boat-shaped bone on the thumb side of the wrist

Etymology

From Latin os (bone) + scaphoideum (boat-shaped), referring to its shape.
