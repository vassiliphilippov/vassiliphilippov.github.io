star

1. VERB

/ stɑr / (AmE), / stɑː / (BrE)
to be the principal performer or main feature in a film, play, show, or event.
be the main performer in a film or show

Examples

The new Broadway play will star a well-known Hollywood actor.
She starred as the lead in several independent films.
The documentary stars local artists from the community.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feature - to include someone as a prominent actor or participant
  • Synonym: headline - to appear as the main performer in a show or event

3. NOUN

/ stɑr / (AmE), / stɑː / (BrE)

Examples

Many modern offices use a star configuration for their local area networks.
If the hub fails in a star topology, the entire network is affected.

Related Terms
