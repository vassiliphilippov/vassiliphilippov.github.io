stableness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪb(ə)lnəs / (AmE), / ˈsteɪb(ə)lnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being firm, steady, or resistant to change.
quality of being firm or steady

Examples

Her stableness in difficult times inspired everyone around her.
The stableness of the bridge reassured the travelers crossing it.
We admired the stableness of his character despite numerous challenges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stability - the state or quality of being stable

Etymology

Formed from 'stable' (from Latin stabilis) + '-ness'.
