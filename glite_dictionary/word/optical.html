optical

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑptɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈɒptɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the eye or resembling the structure or function of the eye.
relating to the eye

Examples

The doctor conducted several optical tests to assess the patient's vision.
Certain insects have complex optical organs that help them detect movement.
Damage to optical nerves can severely affect eyesight.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ocular - relating to the eye
  • Synonym: ophthalmic - relating to the eye or its diseases

Etymology

From Late Latin 'opticus', from Greek 'optikos' (of or for sight, optical).
