Olfersia

1. NOUN

/ ɔlˈfɜr.si.ə / (AmE), / ɒlˈfɜː.si.ə / (BrE)
A genus of ferns containing only one known species, native to tropical regions.
genus of ferns (monotypic)

Examples

The rare fern in the garden is classified under the genus Olfersia.
Botanists studied Olfersia to learn more about monotypic fern genera.
Specimens of Olfersia are often found in herbariums focused on tropical plants.

Etymology

Named after Ignaz von Olfers, a German naturalist (1793–1871).
