caffeinic

1. ADJ

/ kæˈfiː.nɪk / (AmE), / kæˈfiː.nɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing caffeine
containing caffeine

Examples

The caffeinic content of the sample was measured using chromatography.
Researchers tested the effects of caffeinic beverages on concentration and alertness.
Some plants naturally produce caffeinic compounds as a defense mechanism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caffeinated - containing or treated with caffeine

Etymology

Formed from 'caffeine' with the suffix '-ic', denoting 'of or pertaining to'.
