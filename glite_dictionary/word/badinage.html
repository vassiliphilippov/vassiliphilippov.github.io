badinage

1. NOUN

/ ˌbæd.ɪˈnɑːʒ / (AmE), / ˌbæd.ɪˈnɑːʒ / (BrE)
light and playful conversation; humorous or witty banter
light, playful conversation

Examples

The old friends exchanged badinage as they reminisced about their school days.
Light badinage between the hosts kept the audience entertained throughout the evening.
Their lunch meetings were always full of witty badinage and laughter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banter - playful and friendly exchange of teasing remarks

Etymology

From French 'badinage' (teasing, banter), from 'badiner' (to jest).
