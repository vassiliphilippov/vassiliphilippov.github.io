Manama

1. PROPN

/ məˈnɑːmə / (AmE), / məˈnɑːmə / (BrE)
The capital city of Bahrain, situated at the northern tip of Bahrain Island.
capital city of Bahrain

Examples

Many international businesses have their offices in Manama, making it Bahrain's financial center.
The climate in Manama is hot and dry for most of the year.
Tourists often visit the Bahrain National Museum located in Manama.
