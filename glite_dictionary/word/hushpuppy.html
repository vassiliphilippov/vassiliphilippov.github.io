hushpuppy

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌʃˌpʌpi / (AmE), / ˈhʌʃˌpʌpi / (BrE)
A small ball of cornmeal batter that is deep-fried, often served as a side dish in Southern United States cuisine.
deep-fried cornmeal ball
american

Examples

We ordered a basket of hushpuppies to go with our fried catfish.
The hushpuppy was crispy on the outside and soft on the inside.
She learned how to make hushpuppies from her grandmother's Southern recipe.

Etymology

Possibly from the practice of tossing fried scraps to dogs to 'hush the puppies.'
