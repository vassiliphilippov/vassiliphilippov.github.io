shopkeeper

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑpˌkipɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpˌkiːpə / (BrE)
A person who owns or manages a shop, especially a small retail business.
person who runs a shop

Examples

The shopkeeper greeted each customer with a friendly smile.
Many local shopkeepers struggle to compete with large supermarkets.
The shopkeeper closed his store early during the holiday season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storekeeper - person who manages or owns a store

Etymology

From 'shop' + 'keeper', first recorded in English in the late 17th century.
