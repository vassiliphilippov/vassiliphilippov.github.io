Chippewaian

1. NOUN

/ tʃɪˈpweɪən / (AmE), / tʃɪˈpweɪən / (BrE)
the language spoken by the Chipewyan people of Canada
language of the Chipewyan people

Examples

Few people today speak Chippewaian as their first language.
The Chippewaian language has a rich oral storytelling tradition.
Linguists have worked to document and preserve Chippewaian.

Etymology

From 'Chipewyan', the name of the indigenous group, with the suffix '-ian'.
