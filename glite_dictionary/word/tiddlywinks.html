tiddlywinks

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪdliˌwɪŋks / (AmE), / ˈtɪdliˌwɪŋks / (BrE)
a game in which players attempt to flip small plastic disks into a cup by pressing them on the edge with a larger disk
a game flicking disks into a cup

Examples

The children played tiddlywinks on the living room floor all afternoon.
He became the national tiddlywinks champion at just fourteen years old.
We bought a vintage tiddlywinks set at the flea market last weekend.

Etymology

Originated in the late 19th century, possibly imitative, referring to the flicking movement in the game.
