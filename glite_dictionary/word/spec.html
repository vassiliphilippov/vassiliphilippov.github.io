spec

1. NOUN

/ spɛk / (AmE), / spɛk / (BrE)
a detailed written description of the requirements, dimensions, materials, or standards needed for a piece of work, especially in technical or design contexts
written technical requirements or standards

Examples

The architect sent the updated spec to the contractor for review.
Before manufacturing begins, we need to finalize the technical spec.
The new software must meet every spec listed in the contract.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: specification - a detailed description of requirements for a project or product

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'specification'.
