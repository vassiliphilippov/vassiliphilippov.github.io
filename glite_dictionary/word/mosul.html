Mosul

1. PROPN

/ ˈmoʊsul / (AmE), / ˈməʊsuːl / (BrE)
A large city in northern Iraq, situated on the Tigris River, known for its historical and cultural significance.
city in northern Iraq

Examples

The city of Mosul was recaptured after a long and difficult battle.
Many ancient artifacts have been discovered near Mosul, reflecting its rich history.
Humanitarian aid was sent to Mosul to help civilians affected by the conflict.
