nonhairy

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈhɛri / (AmE), / nɒnˈheəri / (BrE)
not having hair; lacking hair or hair covering.
lacking hair

Examples

The dermatologist examined the nonhairy region of his arm for signs of irritation.
Certain medications are applied only to nonhairy skin surfaces for better absorption.
In some animals, the underside is typically nonhairy and more sensitive.

Synset Examples

tinea corporis is a fungal infection of the nonhairy parts of the skin

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hairy - covered with hair

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) and 'hairy' (having hair).
