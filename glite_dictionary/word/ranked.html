ranked

1. ADJ

/ ræŋkt / (AmE), / ræŋkt / (BrE)
arranged in a sequence according to grades, ranks, or levels of importance
arranged in order of rank or grade

Examples

The university released a list of the ranked departments based on research output.
Applicants were ranked according to their test scores and interviews.
The competition featured a ranked leaderboard that changed daily.

Synset Examples

stratified areas of the distribution

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ordered - arranged according to a particular order or sequence
  • Antonym: unranked - not arranged in a particular sequence of ranks

Etymology

From the verb 'rank', meaning to arrange in order; 'rank' from Old French 'ranc', from Frankish or Germanic origin.
