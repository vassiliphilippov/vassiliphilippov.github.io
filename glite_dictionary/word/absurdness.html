absurdness

1. NOUN

/ əbˈsɝːdnəs / (AmE), / əbˈsɜːdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being absurd; the state or condition of being unreasonable or logically inconsistent
state of being absurd or unreasonable

Examples

The absurdness of his explanation made everyone question his sincerity.
She laughed at the absurdness of the situation, unable to believe it was real.
Many critics pointed out the absurdness in the film's plot twists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absurdity - the quality or state of being ridiculous or wildly unreasonable
  • Antonym: reasonableness - the state of being reasonable or rational

Etymology

From absurd + -ness, following the pattern of English abstract nouns.
