advancing

1. ADJ

/ ədˈvænsɪŋ / (AmE), / ədˈvɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
Moving forward or making progress, especially in a gradual or continuous way.
moving forward or making progress

Examples

The advancing troops crossed the river before dawn.
With the advancing years, her wisdom grew.
The glacier is advancing slowly, covering more of the valley each year.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: retreating - moving backward, away from a position
  • Synonym: progressing - moving forward or developing gradually

Etymology

Present participle of 'advance', from Old French 'avancier', from Vulgar Latin 'abanteare'.
