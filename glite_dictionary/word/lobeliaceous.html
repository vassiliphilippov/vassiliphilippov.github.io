lobeliaceous

1. ADJ

/ loʊˌbiliˈeɪʃəs / (AmE), / ləʊˌbiːlɪˈeɪʃəs / (BrE)
Relating to or belonging to the plant family Lobeliaceae.
relating to the Lobeliaceae plant family

Examples

Many lobeliaceous species are prized for their vibrant flowers in horticulture.
The garden included several lobeliaceous plants, such as Lobelia erinus.
Researchers studied the medicinal properties of lobeliaceous herbs native to the region.

Etymology

From modern Latin Lobeliaceae (the botanical family name) + -aceous (forming adjectives meaning 'of or pertaining to').
