to heighten

1. VERB

/ ˈhaɪtən / (AmE), / ˈhaɪtn̩ / (BrE)
to become or make more intense or extreme; to increase in degree or intensity
become or make more intense

Examples

As the storm approached, the sense of urgency heightened among the residents.
His excitement heightened when he heard he had been accepted into the program.
The suspense in the film heightened as the plot unfolded.

Synset Examples

The tension heightened

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to intensify - to become or make more intense
  • Antonym: to lessen - to become less in degree or intensity

Etymology

From height + -en (Old English hehtan, to make high, with suffix -en forming verbs).
