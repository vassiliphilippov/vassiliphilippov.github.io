Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɛri ˈwʊlstənkræft ˈʃɛli / (AmE), / ˈmeəri ˈwʌlstənkrɑːft ˈʃɛli / (BrE)
(1797–1851) English writer best known as the author of 'Frankenstein,' and wife of the poet Percy Bysshe Shelley.
English author of 'Frankenstein'

Examples

Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley is celebrated for her groundbreaking novel 'Frankenstein.'
Many literary scholars have explored the themes introduced by Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley in her works.
The legacy of Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley continues to influence writers and filmmakers today.

  • Synonym: Percy Bysshe Shelley - English Romantic poet, husband of Mary Shelley
