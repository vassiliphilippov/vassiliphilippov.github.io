encephalomyelitis

1. NOUN

/ ɛnˌsɛfəloʊˌmaɪəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ɛnˌsɛfələʊˌmaɪəˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Medical: inflammation of both the brain and spinal cord.
inflammation of brain and spinal cord
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with encephalomyelitis after experiencing severe neurological symptoms.
Viral encephalomyelitis can cause long-term neurological complications if left untreated.
Researchers are studying new treatments for autoimmune encephalomyelitis in animal models.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meningitis - inflammation of the membranes (meninges) covering the brain and spinal cord
  • Synonym: encephalitis - inflammation of the brain

Etymology

From Greek 'enkephalos' (brain) + 'myelos' (marrow, spinal cord) + '-itis' (inflammation).
