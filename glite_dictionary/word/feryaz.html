feryaz

1. NOUN

/ fɛrˈjɑz / (AmE), / fɛrˈjɑːz / (BrE)
a broad kaftan with long sleeves, worn by the Russian nobility in the 16th and 17th centuries
historic Russian noble's long kaftan

Examples

The tsar appeared in a splendid feryaz trimmed with gold embroidery during the ceremony.
Museum exhibits of the feryaz illustrate the opulent fashion of Russia's aristocracy.
Over his tunic, he donned a traditional feryaz passed down through generations.
