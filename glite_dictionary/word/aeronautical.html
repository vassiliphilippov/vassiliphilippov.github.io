aeronautical

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛroʊˈnɑtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɛərəˈnɔːtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the science or practice of designing, building, or operating aircraft
relating to aircraft or aviation

Examples

She is pursuing a degree in aeronautical engineering at university.
The museum features several aeronautical displays, including historic airplanes.
Advancements in aeronautical technology have made air travel more efficient and safe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aviation - the design, development, and operation of aircraft

Etymology

From French 'aéronautique', based on Greek 'aēr' (air) and 'nautikos' (of ships).
