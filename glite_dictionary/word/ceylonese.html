Ceylonese

1. ADJ

/ ˌseɪləˈniːz / (AmE), / ˌsiːləˈniːz / (BrE)
of or relating to Ceylon (now Sri Lanka), its people, or its culture, especially before 1972 when the country was known as Ceylon
relating to Ceylon (Sri Lanka) or its people

Examples

The museum features Ceylonese artifacts from the colonial period.
She prepared a traditional Ceylonese curry for the dinner guests.
During the British era, many Ceylonese people migrated to work on tea plantations.

Synset Examples

Sri Lankan beaches
Sri Lankan forces fighting the Sinhalese rebels

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sri Lankan - of or relating to Sri Lanka, its people, or culture

Etymology

From Ceylon (former name of Sri Lanka) + -ese (denoting national or ethnic origin).
