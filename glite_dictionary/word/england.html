England

1. PROPN

/ ˈɪŋ.glənd / (AmE), / ˈɪŋ.glənd / (BrE)
A country in the southern part of Great Britain, and a division of the United Kingdom, with London as its capital.
country in the United Kingdom

Examples

She traveled to England last summer to visit historical sites.
The climate in England is generally mild and rainy throughout the year.
Many famous writers, including Shakespeare, were born in England.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: United Kingdom - a country consisting of England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland
  • Synonym: Scotland - a country in the United Kingdom, north of England
  • Synonym: Wales - a country in the United Kingdom, west of England

Etymology

From Old English Englaland (land of the Angles), from Angle (a Germanic tribe) + land.
