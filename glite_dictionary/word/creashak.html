creashak

1. NOUN

/ ˈkriːˌʃæk / (AmE), / ˈkriːˌʃak / (BrE)
An evergreen, mat-forming shrub native to North America and northern Eurasia, with small white flowers and red berries; its leaves turn red in autumn.
evergreen mat-forming shrub with red berries

Examples

The forest floor was covered with creashak and its distinctive red berries.
In autumn, the leaves of the creashak turn a bright shade of red.
We identified the shrub as creashak by its small white flowers and evergreen foliage.
