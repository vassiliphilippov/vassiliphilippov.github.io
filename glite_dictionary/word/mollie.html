mollie

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːli / (AmE), / ˈmɒli / (BrE)
a small, livebearing freshwater fish, popular in home aquariums, belonging to the genus Poecilia
a type of popular aquarium fish

Examples

My sister has three mollies in her aquarium at home.
Mollies are known for their peaceful temperament and vibrant colors.
The pet store had several tanks full of colorful mollies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guppy - a small, brightly colored aquarium fish

Etymology

From Latin Poecilia, the genus name; commonly used in aquarium trade.
