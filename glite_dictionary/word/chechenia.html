Chechenia

1. PROPN

/ tʃɛˈtʃɛniə / (AmE), / tʃɛˈtʃiːniə / (BrE)
an autonomous republic in southwestern Russia, located in the northern Caucasus Mountains and known for its declaration of independence in the 1990s and subsequent conflict with Russia; also known as Chechnya
autonomous republic in southwestern Russia

Examples

The conflict in Chechenia has drawn international attention for decades.
Many people from Chechenia have sought asylum in European countries due to ongoing violence.
The mountainous terrain of Chechenia makes it difficult for military operations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chechnya - alternative name for Chechenia, the autonomous republic in Russia
