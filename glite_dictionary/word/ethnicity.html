ethnicity

1. NOUN

/ ɛθˈnɪsəti / (AmE), / ɛθˈnɪsɪti / (BrE)
the fact or state of belonging to a particular ethnic group; the shared cultural, racial, or ancestral characteristics that distinguish one group from another
belonging to an ethnic group

Examples

Studies show that ethnicity can impact an individual's social opportunities.
The census collected information on age, gender, and ethnicity.
She takes pride in her family's ethnicity and traditions.

Synset Examples

ethnicity has a strong influence on community status relations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: race - each of the major divisions of humankind, having distinct physical characteristics
  • Synonym: nationality - the status of belonging to a particular nation

Etymology

From Greek 'ethnos' (nation, people), influenced by ethnic + -ity (suffix forming nouns denoting quality).
