Serer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛr ər / (AmE), / ˈsɛr ər / (BrE)
a West African language of the Niger–Congo family, closely related to Fula and spoken chiefly in Senegal and the Gambia
a West African language, mainly in Senegal and Gambia

Examples

She is fluent in Serer, as well as French and Wolof.
The Serer language has several distinct dialects spoken in different regions.
Researchers study the grammar of Serer to understand its connection to other Niger–Congo languages.
