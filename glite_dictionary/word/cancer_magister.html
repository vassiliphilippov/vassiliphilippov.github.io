Cancer magister

1. NOUN

/ ˈkænsər ˈmædʒɪstər / (AmE), / ˈkænsə ˈmædʒɪstə / (BrE)
a species of edible crab native to the Pacific coast of North America, commonly known as the Dungeness crab
edible crab from Pacific North America

Examples

Local fishermen caught several Cancer magister during their morning trip.
The restaurant specializes in fresh Cancer magister dishes sourced from the Pacific coast.
Researchers are studying the habitat of the Cancer magister to ensure sustainable fishing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dungeness crab - common name for Cancer magister, edible crab of the Pacific coast

Etymology

From Latin: 'Cancer' meaning crab and 'magister' meaning chief or master.
