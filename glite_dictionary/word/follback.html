to follback

1. VERB

/ ˈfɑl.bæk / (AmE), / ˈfɒl.bæk / (BrE)
To follow someone back on a social media platform after they have followed you.
reciprocate a social media follow
informal slang

Examples

If you follback, I'll send you a message and we can chat more.
She always follbacks her new followers on Instagram.
I noticed he follbacked nearly everyone who supported him online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: follow back - to follow someone in response to being followed on social media

Etymology

Blend of 'follow' and 'back', formed as internet slang.
