Masonic

1. ADJ

/ məˈsɑnɪk / (AmE), / məˈsɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to Freemasons or Freemasonry
of or relating to Freemasons

Examples

The historic building served as a Masonic lodge for over a century.
He wore a ring engraved with a Masonic symbol.
Their grandfather was a proud member of several Masonic organizations.

Synset Examples

Masonic lodge

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Freemason - a member of the international order of Freemasons

Etymology

From Mason (member of a guild of skilled stone workers) + -ic (forming adjectives).
