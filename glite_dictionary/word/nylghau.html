nylghau

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪlˌɡaʊ / (AmE), / ˈnɪlˌɡaʊ / (BrE)
a large antelope of northern India, the male being blue-grey with white markings and the female brownish without horns
large antelope native to India

Examples

The wildlife reserve is home to several nylghau, often observed grazing in open grasslands.
A male nylghau was spotted near the waterhole, easily recognized by its blue-grey coat.
Unlike the males, female nylghau lack horns and have a brownish coloration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nilgai - common alternative spelling for the Asian antelope

Etymology

From Hindi 'nīlgāī' (literally 'blue cow'), from Sanskrit 'nīla' (blue) + 'gāu' (cow).
