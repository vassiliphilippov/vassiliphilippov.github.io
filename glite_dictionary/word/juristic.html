juristic

1. ADJ

/ dʒʊˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / dʒʊəˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to law, legal rights, or legal obligations.
relating to law or legal rights

Examples

The contract was challenged on juristic grounds and eventually annulled.
Her research explored the juristic interpretations of international law.
A juristic analysis of the case revealed several procedural irregularities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legal - relating to the law

Etymology

From Latin 'juridicus' (of the law), via French 'juridique', with influence from 'jurist'.
