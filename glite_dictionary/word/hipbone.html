hipbone

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪpˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈhɪp.bəʊn / (BrE)
the large, flaring bone forming one half of the pelvis, made up of the ilium, ischium, and pubis
large bone forming half of the pelvis

Examples

The doctor pointed to the area near her hipbone to explain the pain.
He fractured his hipbone in a car accident last year.
During the procedure, the surgeon carefully avoided the hipbone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pelvis - the bony structure at the base of the spine to which the hipbones are attached
