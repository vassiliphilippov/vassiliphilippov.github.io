to chitter

1. VERB

/ ˈtʃɪtər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪtə / (BrE)
(of birds or small animals) to make repeated high-pitched sounds or twittering noises.
make high-pitched noises (as birds)

Examples

At dawn, the sparrows chitter excitedly as the sun rises over the rooftops.
The forest was alive with squirrels chittering as they chased each other through the branches.
We listened quietly as the bats chittered overhead in the evening light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twitter - to make a series of light, high-pitched sounds (as of birds)
  • Synonym: chirp - to make a short, sharp, high-pitched sound (as of birds)
