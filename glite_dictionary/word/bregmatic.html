bregmatic

1. ADJ

/ brɛɡˈmætɪk / (AmE), / brɛɡˈmatɪk / (BrE)
relating to the bregma, the anatomical point on the skull where the coronal and sagittal sutures meet
relating to the bregma (skull)
medical

Examples

The surgeon made an incision near the bregmatic area to access the cranial sutures.
A bregmatic fontanelle is commonly examined in infants for developmental assessment.
Cranial injuries involving the bregmatic region require careful evaluation due to underlying structures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bregma - the anatomical point where the coronal and sagittal sutures of the skull meet

Etymology

From bregma (Greek, 'front of the head') + -ic (forming adjectives).
