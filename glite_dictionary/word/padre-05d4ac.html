padre

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːdˌreɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːdri / (BrE)
a chaplain serving in one of the armed forces
military chaplain

Examples

The padre offered comfort and prayers to the soldiers before they left for the front.
During the ceremony, the padre spoke about hope and resilience.
Many troops turned to the padre for support during difficult times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chaplain - a member of the clergy attached to a private chapel, institution, or military unit

Etymology

From Spanish and Italian 'padre' meaning 'father'.
