petaled

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛt.əld / (AmE), / ˈpɛt.əld / (BrE)
(of flowers) having petals.
having petals (of flowers)

Examples

The petaled blossoms covered the entire field in pink and white.
Her favorite painting depicted a single, richly petaled rose.
Some desert plants produce flowers that are barely petaled or entirely without petals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petal - a segment of the corolla of a flower, often colorful

Etymology

From 'petal' + '-ed', forming an adjective meaning 'having petals'.
