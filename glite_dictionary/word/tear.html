tear

1. NOUN

/ tɛr / (AmE), / tɪə / (BrE)
an act of tearing something apart or making a rip or split in it
an act of tearing

Examples

He gave the envelope a quick tear to open it.
There was a large tear down the side of the paper.
The dress suffered a tear when it caught on a nail.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rip - an act of ripping; a tear or split made by pulling something apart

Etymology

From Old English teran (to tear).
