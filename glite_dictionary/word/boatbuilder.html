boatbuilder

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊtˌbɪldər / (AmE), / ˈbəʊtˌbɪldə / (BrE)
a person who builds boats, especially professionally or as a trade
person who builds boats

Examples

The boatbuilder spent months crafting the yacht by hand.
Many traditional boatbuilders use methods passed down through generations.
As a skilled boatbuilder, he was in high demand throughout the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shipwright - person who builds or repairs ships
