crapulent

1. ADJ

/ ˈkræpjʊlənt / (AmE), / ˈkræpjʊlənt / (BrE)
Suffering from the effects of excessive eating or drinking; given to or characterized by overindulgence in food or drink.
suffering from overeating or overdrinking
archaic

Examples

After the feast, he lay in a crapulent stupor, regretting his gluttony.
She awoke feeling crapulent after too many rich desserts and glasses of wine.
The guests departed, leaving only a crapulent silence in the once lively dining room.

Synset Examples

crapulent sleep
a crapulous stomach

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crapulous - given to excessive drinking or eating

Etymology

From Late Latin crapulentus (drunk, intoxicated), from Latin crapula (drunkenness), from Greek kraipalē (headache caused by drinking).
