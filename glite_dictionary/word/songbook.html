songbook

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔŋˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈsɒŋˌbʊk / (BrE)
A book that contains a collection of songs, typically with lyrics and sometimes with musical notation.
book with a collection of songs

Examples

She flipped through the songbook to find her favourite tune.
Each student received a new songbook at the start of the choir rehearsal.
The songbook includes classic folk songs as well as modern hits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hymnal - a book of religious songs or hymns
