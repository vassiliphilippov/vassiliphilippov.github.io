Mikir-Meithei

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiː.kɪr ˈmaɪ.theɪ / (AmE), / ˈmiː.kɪə ˈmaɪ.θiː / (BrE)
a group of Kamarupan languages spoken in the Indian states of Manipur and Assam
group of languages from Manipur and Assam

Examples

Scholars have studied the Mikir-Meithei languages to understand the region's linguistic history.
The Mikir-Meithei group includes several dialects spoken by different communities.
Efforts are being made to document the endangered Mikir-Meithei languages before they disappear.

Etymology

Mikir and Meithei are names of ethnic groups and languages in northeastern India.
