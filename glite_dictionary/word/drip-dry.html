drip-dry

1. ADJ

/ drɪp ˈdraɪ / (AmE), / drɪp ˈdraɪ / (BrE)
(of clothes or fabrics) able to be dried by hanging up after washing and not requiring ironing.
requiring no ironing after drying

Examples

These drip-dry shirts are ideal for traveling because they don’t need ironing.
After washing, just hang your drip-dry dress and it will be ready to wear.
He prefers drip-dry fabrics for their convenience and easy maintenance.

Synset Examples

drip-dry shirts for travel

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wash-and-wear - fabric or clothing that requires no ironing after washing

2. ADJ

/ drɪp ˈdraɪ / (AmE), / drɪp ˈdraɪ / (BrE)
(of fabric or clothing) treated so that it can be quickly washed and dried, requiring little or no ironing.
requiring little or no ironing after washing

Examples

My favorite shirts are all drip-dry because I don't like ironing.
The hotel provides drip-dry towels that are easy to clean and maintain.
He prefers drip-dry clothing for travel since it's convenient to wash and wear.

Synset Examples

a wash-and-wear shirt

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wash-and-wear - able to be washed and worn with little or no ironing

Etymology

Formed from the verbs 'drip' and 'dry,' first used in the mid-20th century with the advent of easy-care fabrics.
