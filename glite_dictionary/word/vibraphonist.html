vibraphonist

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪbrəˌfoʊnɪst / (AmE), / ˈvʌɪbrəˌfəʊnɪst / (BrE)
a musician who plays the vibraphone, a percussion instrument with tuned metal bars and resonators
musician who plays the vibraphone

Examples

The talented vibraphonist captivated the audience with a complex solo.
As a jazz vibraphonist, she has performed with many famous bands.
The orchestra was seeking a skilled vibraphonist for their upcoming concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vibraphone - a percussion instrument with metal bars and resonators

Etymology

Formed from 'vibraphone' + '-ist' (player or specialist suffix).
