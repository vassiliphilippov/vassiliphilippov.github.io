hideously

1. ADV

/ ˈhɪdiəsli / (AmE), / ˈhɪdiəsli / (BrE)
In an extremely ugly or shocking manner.
in an extremely ugly or shocking way

Examples

The room was hideously decorated with clashing colors and gaudy ornaments.
The villain was hideously scarred, making him even more intimidating.
She was hideously betrayed by someone she trusted.

Synset Examples

her face was hideously disfigured after the accident

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horribly - in a manner that causes horror or disgust
  • Antonym: beautifully - in a beautiful manner

Etymology

From 'hideous' + '-ly'. 'Hideous' from Old French 'hideus', from Latin 'horridus' (frightful).
