reviser

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈvaɪzər / (AmE), / rɪˈvaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who reviews, edits, and corrects text to prepare it for publication
person who edits or corrects text

Examples

The reviser ensured the manuscript was free of errors before it went to print.
As a professional reviser, Maria checks every document for clarity and accuracy.
The publisher assigned a skilled reviser to standardize the language throughout the report.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: editor - a person who prepares text for publication

Etymology

From revise + -er, based on French reviser (to inspect).
