to illume

1. VERB

/ ɪˈluːm / (AmE), / ɪˈluːm / (BrE)
to light up; to illuminate; to introduce light into something
to light up or illuminate

Examples

The artist used a single bulb to illume the sculpture in the otherwise dark gallery.
The rising sun began to illume the misty landscape outside her window.
Candles were lit to illume the hall during the power outage.

Synset Examples

This lamp lightens the room a bit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to illuminate - to light up; to provide with light

Etymology

From Latin illuminare (to light up), via French or by analogy to illuminate.
