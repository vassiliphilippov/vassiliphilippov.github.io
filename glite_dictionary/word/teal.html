teal

1. ADJ

/ tiﾋ人 / (AmE), / tiﾋ人 / (BrE)

Examples

3. NOUN

/ til / (AmE), / tiﾋ人 / (BrE)
a small, short-necked dabbling duck found in Europe and America, known for its swift flight and often colorful plumage
small river duck of Europe and America

Examples

A flock of teal skimmed over the surface of the lake at dawn.
The reserve is an important wintering site for many species, including the teal.
Birdwatchers spotted a rare green-winged teal among the ducks.

Etymology

From Middle English *tele*, of uncertain origin, possibly imitative or related to Old French *tiele*.
