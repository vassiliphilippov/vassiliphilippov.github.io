servicewoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɜːrvɪsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪswʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who serves as a member of the armed forces
woman in the armed forces

Examples

After years as a servicewoman, she now trains new recruits.
The documentary highlights the experiences of a servicewoman in combat.
Many servicewomen have contributed significantly to military operations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: serviceman - a man who serves in the armed forces

Etymology

Formed from 'service' and 'woman', on the pattern of 'serviceman'.
