holdall

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊlˌɔl / (AmE), / ˈhəʊl.dɔːl / (BrE)
A large bag or basket used for carrying clothes and other personal items, typically when travelling.
large bag or basket for carrying items

Examples

She packed all her clothes into a large holdall before leaving for university.
The athlete arrived at the gym carrying a holdall full of equipment.
He bought a new holdall for his weekend trip to the countryside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bag - a container made of flexible material used for carrying things
  • Synonym: suitcase - a rectangular case with a handle for carrying clothes when travelling
