Quoratean

1. NOUN

/ kwɔːˈreɪtiən / (AmE), / kwɒˈreɪtiən / (BrE)
A branch of the Hokan language family comprising languages originally spoken near the Salmon River area, named after Quoratem.
language group of Hokan family near Salmon River

Examples

Linguists continue to debate the classification of the Quoratean languages within the Hokan family.
She specialized in the study of Quoratean phonology during her research in California.
The ancient settlement near Salmon River was home to several Quoratean speaking communities.

Etymology

Named after Quoratem, an area near the Salmon River.
