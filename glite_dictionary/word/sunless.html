sunless

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌn.ləs / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.ləs / (BrE)
(of a place or weather) lacking sunlight; characterized by the absence of sunshine, often due to cloud cover.
lacking sunlight; cloudy

Examples

The landscape looked bleak and sunless after days of heavy rain.
They spent the winter in a sunless apartment that barely saw any daylight.
On that sunless afternoon, the skies were thick with gray clouds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cloudy - (of the weather or sky) covered with clouds; overcast
  • Antonym: sunny - (of weather or a place) having a lot of sunlight

Etymology

From 'sun' + the suffix '-less' (meaning 'without').
