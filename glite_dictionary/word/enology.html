enology

1. NOUN

/ iˈnɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / iˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The science and study of wine making.
science of wine making

Examples

She decided to study enology at university to learn about the production of fine wines.
His passion for enology led him to become a respected wine expert.
Advances in enology have improved the quality and variety of wines worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oenology - alternate spelling for the science of wine making

Etymology

From Greek oinos (wine) + -logia (study of).
