uranoplasty

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈrænəˌplæsti / (AmE), / jʊˈrænəˌplæsti / (BrE)
surgical correction or reconstruction of a defect in the palate
surgical repair of the palate
medical

Examples

The patient underwent uranoplasty to correct a congenital defect in her palate.
Recent advances in uranoplasty have improved outcomes for children with cleft palates.
Successful uranoplasty can greatly enhance speech and swallowing abilities in affected individuals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: palatoplasty - surgical repair of the palate

Etymology

From Greek 'ouranos' (palate) + '-plasty' (molding or formation).
