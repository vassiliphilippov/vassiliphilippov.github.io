well-favored

1. ADJ

/ wɛl ˈfeɪvɚd / (AmE), / wɛl ˈfeɪvəd / (BrE)
Pleasing in appearance, especially because of having features that conform to ideals of form and proportion; good-looking or attractive.
pleasing or attractive in appearance

Examples

She was considered well-favored among her peers for her classic beauty.
The hero of the story is described as tall and well-favored.
Despite his humble background, his well-favored looks drew much attention.

Synset Examples

a fine-looking woman
a good-looking man
better-looking than her sister
very pretty but not so extraordinarily handsome
our southern women are well-favored

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good-looking - attractive in appearance
  • Synonym: handsome - having a pleasing and impressive appearance (usually of a person)
  • Antonym: unattractive - not pleasing in appearance

Etymology

From Middle English, combining 'well' (in a good way) with 'favored' (having particular features or appearance).
