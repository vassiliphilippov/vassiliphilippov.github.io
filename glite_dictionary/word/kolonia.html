Kolonia

1. PROPN

/ koʊˈloʊniə / (AmE), / kɒˈləʊniə / (BrE)
the capital town of the Federated States of Micronesia, located on the island of Pohnpei in the western Pacific Ocean
capital of Micronesia (on Pohnpei island)

Examples

Kolonia serves as the administrative and economic center of Pohnpei Island.
The city's population has grown since Kolonia became the capital of Micronesia.
Many international flights to Micronesia arrive at the airport in Kolonia.

Etymology

From German 'Kolonie' (colony), after its former status as a colonial administrative center.
