Aldactone

1. NOUN

/ ælˈdæktoʊn / (AmE), / ælˈdæktəʊn / (BrE)
A trademarked drug used to treat high blood pressure and certain conditions involving excess aldosterone; generic name spironolactone.
trademarked drug for hypertension (spironolactone)
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Aldactone to help manage my blood pressure.
People taking Aldactone should have their potassium levels monitored regularly.
Aldactone is sometimes used to treat hormone-related conditions in addition to hypertension.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spironolactone - the generic name for Aldactone, a medication used to treat fluid retention and hypertension
