Azerbajdzhan

1. PROPN

/ ˌæzərbaɪˈdʒɑːn / (AmE), / ˌazəbaɪdˈʒɑːn / (BrE)
A landlocked country in southwestern Asia, formerly a republic of the Soviet Union; also known as Azerbaijan.
country in southwestern Asia

Examples

The capital city of Azerbajdzhan is Baku, a major cultural and economic center.
Oil is a significant part of Azerbajdzhan's economy.
Many tourists visit Azerbajdzhan for its rich history and scenic landscapes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Azerbaijan - official English name for the same country

Etymology

From Russian Азербайджан (Azerbajdzhan), itself from Azerbaijani Azərbaycan.
