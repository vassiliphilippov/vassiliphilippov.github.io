captainship

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæp.tənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈkæp.tɪn.ʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or term of being a captain, especially of a team or ship
position or term as captain

Examples

He was applauded for his outstanding captainship during the championship season.
Her captainship lasted three years and brought the team several victories.
Under his captainship, the crew remained motivated and disciplined.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: captaincy - the rank or post of being a captain

Etymology

Formed from 'captain' + suffix '-ship' (denoting state or condition).
