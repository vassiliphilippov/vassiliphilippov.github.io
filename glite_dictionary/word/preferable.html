preferable

1. ADJ

/ prəˈfɛrəbəl / (AmE), / prɪˈfɛrəbəl / (BrE)
more desirable or suitable than another; better or favored in comparison
more desirable than another

Examples

For most commuters, living close to work is preferable to spending hours in traffic.
If possible, fresh ingredients are preferable to frozen ones in this recipe.
An early morning meeting is preferable for those who have busy afternoons.

Synset Examples

coffee is preferable to tea
Danny's preferred name is ‘Dan’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to prefer - to like one thing or person better than another
  • Antonym: undesirable - not wanted or attractive; undesirable

Etymology

From Late Latin praeferibilis, from praeferre (to prefer).
