psycho

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪkəʊ / (BrE)
an informal and often offensive term for a person who is mentally ill, particularly someone with psychosis or perceived as dangerously unstable
mentally unstable or deranged person
informal offensive

Examples

The movie's villain is a violent psycho who terrorizes the town.
Don't call him a psycho; that's a really hurtful thing to say.
Some tabloids labeled the suspect a "psycho" without knowing the facts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunatic - an informal and offensive synonym for a person who is mentally ill or dangerously insane
  • Synonym: maniac - a person who behaves in a wildly foolish way, often used informally or insultingly

Etymology

Shortened from 'psychotic' or 'psychopath'.
