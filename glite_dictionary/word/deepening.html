deepening

1. NOUN

/ ˈdiːpənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdiːpənɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of becoming deeper, more intense, or more profound.
the act of becoming deeper or more intense

Examples

The deepening of the river allowed larger ships to pass through the channel.
Political tensions are increasing, with a deepening divide between the two parties.
The deepening crisis demands urgent and decisive action from the government.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intensification - the process of becoming more intense
  • Antonym: shallowing - the process of becoming shallower or less deep

Etymology

From the verb 'deepen' + suffix '-ing', forming a noun meaning 'the act or process of deepening'.
