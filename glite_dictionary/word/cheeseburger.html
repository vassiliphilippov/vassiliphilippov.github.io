cheeseburger

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃiːzˌbɝːɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzˌbɜːɡə / (BrE)
a hamburger topped with melted cheese, usually served in a bun
hamburger with melted cheese

Examples

She ordered a cheeseburger and fries at the diner.
The cheeseburger was topped with extra onions and pickles.
Instead of a plain burger, he chose a cheeseburger for lunch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hamburger - ground beef patty served in a bun

Etymology

From 'cheese' + 'burger', with 'burger' as a shortening of 'hamburger'.
