Pee Dee

1. PROPN

/ pi ˈdi / (AmE), / piː ˈdiː / (BrE)
a river in the southeastern United States, flowing through central North Carolina and northeastern South Carolina to the Atlantic Ocean
river in North and South Carolina

Examples

The Pee Dee River is known for its vital role in the economy of northeastern South Carolina.
Many historic sites can be found along the banks of the Pee Dee.
Heavy rains have caused the Pee Dee to flood several times over the past decade.

Etymology

Named after the Pee Dee Native American tribe of the region.
