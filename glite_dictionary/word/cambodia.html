Cambodia

1. PROPN

/ kæmˈboʊ.di.ə / (AmE), / kæmˈbəʊ.di.ə / (BrE)
A country in southeastern Asia, formerly part of French Indochina, known for its rich cultural heritage and history.
country in southeastern Asia

Examples

Many tourists visit Cambodia to see the famous Angkor Wat temple complex.
The history of Cambodia includes periods of both great prosperity and tragic conflict.
She spent a year teaching English in Cambodia after finishing university.

Etymology

From French 'Cambodge', from Khmer 'Kâmpŭchéa', the country's name in Khmer.
