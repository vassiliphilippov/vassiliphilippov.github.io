scorekeeper

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɔːrˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈskɔːˌkiːpə / (BrE)
an official responsible for recording the score in a game or sporting event
person who records the score in games

Examples

The scorekeeper updated the scoreboard after each goal.
During the tournament, the scorekeeper sat at the table near the court.
Our team thanked the scorekeeper for accurately tracking every point.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scorer - a person who keeps or announces the score in a contest
  • Synonym: referee - an official who oversees a sports game and enforces the rules

Etymology

Formed from 'score' (to keep a tally or record) + 'keeper' (one who keeps or maintains something); common productive formation.
