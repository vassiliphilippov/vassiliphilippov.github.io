ferociously

1. ADV

/ fəˈroʊʃəsli / (AmE), / fəˈrəʊʃəsli / (BrE)
In a fierce, violent, or intense manner.
in a fierce or violent manner

Examples

The lion roared ferociously when threatened by intruders.
She defended her ideas ferociously during the debate.
Waves crashed ferociously against the rocks during the storm.

Synset Examples

silence broken by dogs barking ferociously
they fought fiercely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fiercely - with intense or ferocious force
  • Antonym: gently - in a mild, soft, or tender manner

Etymology

From 'ferocious' + '-ly'. 'Ferocious' from Latin ferox (fierce, wild).
