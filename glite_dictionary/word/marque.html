marque

1. NOUN

/ mɑrk / (AmE), / mɑːk / (BrE)
a brand or make of a product, especially used of cars; a name or trademark identifying a particular product or manufacturer
brand or make of a product

Examples

This car is a classic example of a luxury marque.
Many collectors dream of owning a rare Ferrari marque.
Japanese marques have become popular for their reliability and design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brand - a particular make of product, often with a distinctive name or logo

Etymology

From French 'marque', meaning 'mark, brand'.
