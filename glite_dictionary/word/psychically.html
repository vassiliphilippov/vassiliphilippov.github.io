psychically

1. ADV

/ ˈsaɪkɪkli / (AmE), / ˈsʌɪkɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to the mind, soul, or paranormal psychic abilities.
in a psychic or mental way

Examples

Some people claim to communicate psychically with animals.
He was psychically drained after the intense séance.
She sensed the danger psychically, even before anyone said a word.

Synset Examples

he was psychically blind

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mentally - in a mental or intellectual way

Etymology

From psychic + -ally, with psychic from Greek 'psūkhikos' (of the soul or mind).
