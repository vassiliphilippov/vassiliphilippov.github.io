Brocadopa

1. NOUN

/ broʊˈkædəˌpoʊ / (AmE), / brəʊˈkædəˌpəʊ / (BrE)
A trade name for levodopa, the levorotatory form of dopa, used as a medication to treat Parkinson's disease.
trade name for levodopa, Parkinson's drug
medical

Examples

The neurologist prescribed Brocadopa to help manage her Parkinson's symptoms.
Some patients experience side effects when starting Brocadopa therapy.
Brocadopa is often used in combination with other medications to improve its effectiveness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: levodopa - the levorotatory form of dopa used in Parkinson's treatment
