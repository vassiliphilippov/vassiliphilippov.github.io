orbiculate

1. ADJ

/ ɔrˈbɪkjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ɔːˈbɪkjʊlət / (BrE)
Having a circular or nearly circular shape; rounded.
circular or nearly circular in shape

Examples

The plant has orbiculate leaves that are almost perfectly round.
Many marine organisms exhibit orbiculate shells for streamlined movement in water.
The orbiculate pattern on the butterfly's wings helps it blend into its surroundings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circular - having the form of a circle
  • Degree Lower: oval - having the shape of an egg or ellipse; slightly elongated circle
  • Synonym: disk-shaped - shaped like a disk

Etymology

From Latin orbiculatus (rounded, having the shape of a small disk), from orbiculus, diminutive of orbis (circle, disk).
