Eyeish

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈaɪɪʃ / (BrE)
A member of a Native American Caddo people originally inhabiting northeastern Texas.
a member of the Eyeish people, Caddo tribe

Examples

The Eyeish played an important role in the regional trade networks of early Texas.
According to tradition, the Eyeish were skilled in agricultural practices.
Artifacts from the Eyeish have been discovered by archaeologists near the Neches River.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Caddo - a member of a group of Native American peoples
