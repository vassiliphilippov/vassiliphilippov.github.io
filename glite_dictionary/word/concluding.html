concluding

1. ADJ

/ kənˈkluːdɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈkluːdɪŋ / (BrE)
coming at or forming the end or final part of something; bringing to a close.
ending or final

Examples

The speaker's concluding remarks summarized the key points of the conference.
In the concluding chapter, the mystery is finally solved.
They stood and applauded at the concluding scene of the play.

Synset Examples

his concluding words came as a surprise
the final chapter
the last days of the dinosaurs
terminal leave

Related Terms

  • Synonym: final - coming at the end; last in a series
  • Synonym: last - being, happening, or coming at the end

Etymology

From Latin 'concludere' (to end, to close); related to 'conclude' + -ing.
