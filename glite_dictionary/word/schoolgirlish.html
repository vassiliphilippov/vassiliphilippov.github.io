schoolgirlish

1. ADJ

/ ˈskuːlˌɡɝːlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈskuːlˌɡɜːlɪʃ / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a schoolgirl, especially in a youthful, naive, or innocent way
resembling a schoolgirl

Examples

Her laughter had a schoolgirlish quality that made everyone smile.
The pink bow felt a little too schoolgirlish for the business meeting.
She tried to hide her schoolgirlish excitement about the new project.

Synset Examples

girlish charm
a dress too schoolgirlish for office wear

Related Terms

  • Synonym: girlish - characteristic of or appropriate to a girl
