intelligently

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈtɛlədʒəntli / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows intelligence or good judgment.
in an intelligent manner

Examples

She approached the problem intelligently and found a creative solution.
He answered all the questions intelligently during the interview.
They debated the topic intelligently, supporting their points with strong evidence.

Synset Examples

she acted intelligently in this difficult situation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wisely - in a wise or prudent manner
  • Antonym: foolishly - in a foolish or unwise manner

Etymology

Formed from 'intelligent' + '-ly'. 'Intelligent' from Latin 'intelligens' (understanding).
