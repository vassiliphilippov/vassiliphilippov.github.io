Peiping

1. PROPN

/ ˈpeɪˌpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪˌpɪŋ / (BrE)
the former name of Beijing, the capital of China, especially used before 1949
former name of Beijing, China

Examples

In the 1930s, many foreign embassies were located in Peiping.
After the revolution, Peiping was officially renamed Beijing.
Historical accounts often refer to the city as Peiping during the Republic of China era.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Beijing - the present name of China's capital city

Etymology

From Chinese (Pinyin: Běipíng), meaning 'Northern Peace'.
