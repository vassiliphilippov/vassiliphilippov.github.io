I Peter

1. PROPN

/ ˈfaɪrst ˌpitər / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˌpiːtər / (BrE)
the first book of the New Testament traditionally attributed to Saint Peter the Apostle
first New Testament book of Peter

Examples

Many Christians turn to I Peter for guidance on enduring suffering faithfully.
In his sermon, the pastor quoted from I Peter to emphasize humility.
The themes found in I Peter are still relevant to modern readers.
