Rooseveltian

1. ADJ

/ ˌroʊzəˈvɛltiən / (AmE), / ˌrəʊzəˈvɛltɪən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Franklin D. Roosevelt or his policies, attitudes, or leadership style
of or relating to Franklin D. Roosevelt

Examples

Many historians admire the Rooseveltian approach to economic recovery during the Great Depression.
Her speech echoed Rooseveltian optimism and resolve in the face of adversity.
The senator advocated Rooseveltian policies to address the current financial crisis.

Etymology

Formed from the surname Roosevelt + -ian, after Franklin D. Roosevelt (1882–1945), 32nd President of the United States.
