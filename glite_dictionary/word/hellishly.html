hellishly

1. ADV

/ ˈhɛlɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈhɛlɪʃli / (BrE)
In an extremely intense, unpleasant, or diabolical manner.
in an extremely intense or unpleasant way

Examples

The weather was hellishly hot during the summer heatwave.
She found the instructions hellishly complicated to follow.
He was hellishly late for his appointment.

Synset Examples

infernally clever
hellishly dangerous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infernally - in a hellish or diabolical manner

Etymology

From 'hellish' + '-ly,' where 'hellish' derives from 'hell' (Old English 'hel').
