Dmitri Dmitrievich Shostakovich

1. PROPN

/ dɪˈmitri ˌdmiˈtrievɪtʃ ˌʃɒstəˈkoʊvɪtʃ / (AmE), / dɪˈmiːtri ˌdmiːˈtrɪevɪtʃ ˌʃɒstəˈkəʊvɪtʃ / (BrE)
(1906–1975) Russian composer renowned for his fifteen symphonies and significant influence on 20th-century classical music.
