Eire

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛr ə, ˈaɪ rə / (AmE), / ˈɛːrə, ˈaɪərə / (BrE)
the official name in Irish for the Republic of Ireland, a sovereign nation occupying most of the island of Ireland
official name for the Republic of Ireland

Examples

My grandparents emigrated from Eire to America in the early 20th century.
The official language of Eire is Irish, although English is widely spoken.
Tourists often remark on the beauty of the landscapes found throughout Eire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ireland - an island to the west of Britain, comprising the Republic of Ireland and Northern Ireland

Etymology

From Irish Éire, meaning 'Ireland'.
