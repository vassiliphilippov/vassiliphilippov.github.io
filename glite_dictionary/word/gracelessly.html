gracelessly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡreɪsləsli / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪsləsli / (BrE)
In a way that lacks grace or elegance; awkwardly or clumsily.
in an awkward or inelegant manner

Examples

She stumbled gracelessly across the dance floor, bumping into chairs along the way.
He accepted the compliment gracelessly, barely making eye contact with anyone.
The bird landed gracelessly on the branch, flapping its wings to keep its balance.

Synset Examples

she moves rather gracelessly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awkwardly - in an awkward manner; lacking grace or smoothness

2. ADV

/ ˈɡreɪsləsli / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪsləsli / (BrE)
In a manner that lacks grace or elegance; awkwardly or rigidly.
in an awkward or ungraceful manner

Examples

He stumbled gracelessly across the stage, nearly tripping over the microphone cable.
The dancers landed gracelessly, failing to impress the judges with their performance.
After the argument, she walked gracelessly out of the room, slamming the door behind her.

Synset Examples

they moved woodenly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awkwardly - in a clumsy or graceless manner

Etymology

From 'graceless' + '-ly'; 'graceless' from 'grace' (from Latin 'gratia') + '-less'.
