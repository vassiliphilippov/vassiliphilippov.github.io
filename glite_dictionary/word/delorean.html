DeLorean

1. NOUN

/ dəˈlɔːriən / (AmE), / dəˈlɔːriən / (BrE)
a sports car produced in the early 1980s by the DeLorean Motor Company, known for its distinctive stainless-steel body and gull-wing doors
1980s sports car model

Examples

When he arrived at the car show, everyone admired his restored DeLorean.
The DeLorean became legendary after featuring in the 'Back to the Future' films.
Many collectors seek the rare DeLorean for its unique design and pop culture history.

Synset Examples

His favorite car is the iconic DePlorean from the '80s.

Etymology

Named after John DeLorean, the founder of the DeLorean Motor Company.
