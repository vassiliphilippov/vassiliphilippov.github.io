well-defined

1. ADJ

/ wɛl dɪˈfaɪnd / (AmE), / wɛl dɪˈfaɪnd / (BrE)
clearly and precisely expressed or specified; stated in such a way that there is no ambiguity or uncertainty
clearly and precisely specified

Examples

The rules for this competition are well-defined and easy to follow.
Every scientific concept should have a well-defined meaning to avoid confusion.
She only works on problems that have well-defined goals and outcomes.

Synset Examples

a set of well-defined values

Related Terms

  • Antonym: vague - not clearly expressed or defined

2. ADJ

/ ˌwɛl dɪˈfaɪnd / (AmE), / ˌwɛl dɪˈfaɪnd / (BrE)
having clear and distinct edges, boundaries, or form; precisely delineated or easy to distinguish
having clear, distinct boundaries or form

Examples

The mountain range had well-defined peaks visible against the sky.
Her painting style is characterized by well-defined lines and shapes.
The doctor examined the X-ray and noted the well-defined edges of the tumor.

Synset Examples

well-defined features
