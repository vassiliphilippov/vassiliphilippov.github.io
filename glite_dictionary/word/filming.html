filming

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪlmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪlmɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of recording a movie or video using a camera
the act of making a film

Examples

The filming of the new documentary will take place in Kenya next month.
During filming, the crew faced unexpected weather conditions.
We visited the set to watch the filming of a popular TV series.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shooting - the act of recording film or video with a camera

Etymology

From the verb 'film' (to record a movie) + -ing suffix forming a noun.
