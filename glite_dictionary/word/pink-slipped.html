pink-slipped

1. ADJ

/ pɪŋk slɪpt / (AmE), / pɪŋk slɪpt / (BrE)
Having been dismissed from a job or position, especially due to being laid off.
having lost your job
informal american

Examples

After the company restructure, dozens of employees were pink-slipped in a single day.
She found out she was pink-slipped when she arrived at the office and saw the HR notice.
Many pink-slipped workers struggled to find new employment in the sluggish economy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fired - dismissed from a job
  • Synonym: laid off - let go from a job, often for economic reasons
  • Antonym: hired - employed for a job

Etymology

From the practice in the United States of giving employees a pink slip of paper as notice of dismissal.
