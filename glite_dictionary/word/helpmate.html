helpmate

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlpˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɛlpmeɪt / (BrE)
a person who helps and supports another, especially a spouse or partner
a helpful partner, especially a spouse

Examples

She has always been a devoted helpmate throughout their marriage.
He considered his wife not just his love but also his helpmate in life.
As a helpmate, she offered support and advice whenever he faced difficulties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partner - a person who takes part in an activity with another, especially in business or marriage

Etymology

Alteration of 'help meet', influenced by the wording in the King James Bible.
