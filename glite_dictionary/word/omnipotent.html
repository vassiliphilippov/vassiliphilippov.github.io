omnipotent

1. ADJ

/ ɑmˈnɪpətənt / (AmE), / ɒmˈnɪpətənt / (BrE)
having unlimited power and able to do anything
having unlimited power

Examples

Many cultures have legends about an omnipotent being who created the world.
The rulers acted as if they were omnipotent, ignoring all advice and criticism.
No one is truly omnipotent; everyone has some limitations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: almighty - having complete power; omnipotent
  • Antonym: powerless - without power; unable to act or influence

Etymology

From Latin 'omnipotens', from 'omni-' (all) + 'potens' (powerful).
