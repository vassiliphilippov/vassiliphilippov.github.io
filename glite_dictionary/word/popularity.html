popularity

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɑː.pjəˈlɛr.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌpɒp.jʊˈlær.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
The state of being liked, admired, or supported by many people.
being liked or admired by many

Examples

The band's sudden popularity took everyone by surprise.
Her popularity among the students grew after she organized the school festival.
Despite his popularity, he remained humble and approachable.

Synset Examples

his charm soon won him affection and popularity
the universal popularity of American movies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fame - the state of being widely known or recognized
  • Antonym: obscurity - the state of being unknown or unimportant

Etymology

From Latin popularitas (the favor of the people), from popularis (belonging to the people).
