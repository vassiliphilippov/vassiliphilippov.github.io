castled

1. ADJ

/ ˈkæsəld / (AmE), / ˈkɑːsəld / (BrE)
having or resembling repeated square indentations, especially like those found in the battlements of a castle; crenellated.
having battlement-like indentations

Examples

The estate was surrounded by a castled stone wall, giving it a medieval appearance.
Her imagination turned the old church façade into a castled fortress against the sky.
The architect proposed a castled design for the building’s rooftop to evoke a sense of history.

Synset Examples

a crenelated molding

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crenellated - having a pattern of repeated square indentations like in battlements

Etymology

From 'castle', referring to the characteristic battlements of castle walls.
