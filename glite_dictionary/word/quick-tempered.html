quick-tempered

1. ADJ

/ kwɪkˈtɛmpɚd / (AmE), / kwɪkˈtɛmpəd / (BrE)
Easily and rapidly becoming angry.
easily angered

Examples

She can be quick-tempered when things don't go as planned.
His quick-tempered nature made him difficult to work with under pressure.
Although he is brilliant, his quick-tempered outbursts often create tension in meetings.

Synset Examples

a hotheaded commander

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hotheaded - easily angered or rash in behavior
  • Antonym: even-tempered - not easily angered; calm and steady
