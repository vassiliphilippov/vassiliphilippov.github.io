forehead

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrˌhɛd, fəˈrɛd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːhɛd, fəˈrɛd / (BrE)
the part of the face above the eyebrows, forming the front part of the skull
part of the face above the eyebrows

Examples

She had a gentle smile and a high, broad forehead.
Sweat dripped down his forehead as he worked in the sun.
The doctor examined the bump on the child's forehead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brow - the forehead or the eyebrow region

Etymology

From Old English 'forheafod' (for- 'fore' + heafod 'head').

2. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrˌhɛd, fərˈhɛd / (AmE), / ˈfɒrɪd, ˈfɔːhɛd / (BrE)
the part of the face above the eyes and below the hairline
part of face above the eyes

Examples

She wiped the sweat from her forehead after a long run.
The child had a bandage on his forehead where he had bumped it.
Her forehead wrinkled in concentration as she solved the puzzle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brow - the forehead, especially the upper part above the eyes

Etymology

From Old English 'forheafod', from 'for-' (fore) + 'heafod' (head).

Synset POS: n
