stochastically

1. ADV

/ stoʊˈkæstɪkli / (AmE), / stəʊˈkæstɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner involving or based on random probability or chance.
involving random probability or chance

Examples

The simulation was run stochastically to account for random variations in the data.
Gene expression can be regulated stochastically in living cells.
The model estimates outcomes stochastically rather than using a deterministic approach.

Synset Examples

we estimated the answer stochastically

Related Terms

  • Antonym: deterministically - in a manner determined by fixed rules or laws, not random
  • Synonym: randomly - in a random manner, without method or conscious choice

Etymology

From stochastic + -ally, ultimately from Greek stokhastikos (able to guess).
