indolently

1. ADV

/ ˈɪn.dəl.ənt.li / (AmE), / ˈɪn.dəl.ənt.li / (BrE)
in a manner showing a tendency to avoid work or activity; lazily or idly.
in a lazy or idle manner

Examples

He indolently stretched out on the sofa, ignoring the chores waiting for him.
She gazed indolently at the clouds, making no effort to get up.
The cat indolently basked in the sun all afternoon.

Synset Examples

he lives indolently with his relatives

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lazily - in a lazy manner
  • Antonym: diligently - in a careful and hard-working manner

Etymology

From Latin 'indolentia' (freedom from pain), via 'indolent' + '-ly'.
