Krung Thep

1. PROPN

/ krʊŋ ˈtɛp / (AmE), / krʊŋ ˈteɪp / (BrE)
the Thai name for Bangkok, the capital and largest city of Thailand, known for its Buddhist temples and vibrant street life
Thai name for Bangkok, Thailand's capital

Examples

Many Thais refer to Bangkok as Krung Thep, which is the city's full native name.
During my travels in Krung Thep, I visited several stunning Buddhist temples.
The official documents list the city as Krung Thep Maha Nakhon, but it's internationally known as Bangkok.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bangkok - the capital and largest city of Thailand

Etymology

From Thai กรุงเทพ (Krung Thep), short for กรุงเทพมหานคร (Krung Thep Maha Nakhon), meaning 'City of Angels'.
