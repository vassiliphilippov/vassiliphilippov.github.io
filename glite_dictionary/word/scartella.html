Scartella

1. NOUN

/ skɑːrˈtɛlə / (AmE), / skɑːˈtɛlə / (BrE)
A genus of combtooth blennies in the family Blenniidae, found in the Atlantic Ocean and Mediterranean Sea.
a genus of combtooth blennies (fish)

Examples

The genus Scartella includes several species of small blennies found in coastal waters.
Researchers studied the habitat preferences of Scartella along the Mediterranean shoreline.
Scartella species are known for their comb-like teeth and shy behavior.

Etymology

From New Latin, used in taxonomic classification.
