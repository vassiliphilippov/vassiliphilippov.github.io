tinsnips

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪnˌsnɪps / (AmE), / ˈtɪnˌsnɪps / (BrE)
hand-operated shears designed for cutting sheet metal.
shears for cutting sheet metal

Examples

He used tinsnips to trim the sharp edges off the metal sheet.
Always wear gloves when handling tinsnips to protect your hands.
The craftsman selected his tinsnips for the delicate metalwork.

Etymology

From 'tin' (thin metal sheet) + 'snips' (shears or scissors for cutting).
