thickhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɪkˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈθɪkˌhɛd / (BrE)
an Australian and southeastern Asian bird of the family Pachycephalidae, known for its melodious whistling call
Australian/Southeast Asian songbird

Examples

The golden whistler is a type of thickhead found in forests across Australia.
Researchers studied the mating calls of several thickhead species for their musical complexity.
We spotted a thickhead in the garden, its song echoing through the trees.
