candidly

1. ADV

/ ˈkændɪdli / (AmE), / ˈkændɪdli / (BrE)
In an honest and straightforward manner; frankly or openly.
honestly; in a straightforward way

Examples

To be candidly honest, I don't enjoy working with him.
She spoke candidly about the challenges she faced during her career.
If I may say so candidly, your proposal needs some revision.

Synset Examples

honestly, I don't believe it
candidly, I think she doesn't have a conscience
frankly, my dear, I don't give a damn

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frankly - in an open, honest, and direct manner
  • Synonym: honestly - in a truthful or sincere way

Etymology

From Latin candidus (white, pure) via French candide + -ly.
