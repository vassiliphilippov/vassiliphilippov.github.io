to glycerolise

1. VERB

/ ˈɡlɪsəˌrɔːlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪsərəˌlaɪz / (BrE)
to treat or preserve with glycerol, especially by immersing something in it
treat with glycerol

Examples

The laboratory technician decided to glycerolise the tissue samples for long-term preservation.
It is important to glycerolise the specimen before storage to prevent dehydration.
Before analysis, researchers often glycerolise biological slides to maintain cellular integrity.

Etymology

From glycerol + -ise, modeled after similar technical verbs like 'caramelise' and 'pasteurise'.
