bonnily

1. ADV

/ ˈbɑː.nɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈbɒn.ɪ.li / (BrE)
In an attractive or pleasant manner; charmingly.
in an attractive or charming manner
archaic

Examples

The young lass danced bonnily across the meadow, her laughter echoing in the breeze.
He smiled bonnily at the guests, winning everyone's favor.
The cottage was set bonnily by the riverside, surrounded by wildflowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bonny - attractive or charming (especially of a person or place)

Etymology

From 'bonny' + '-ly', modeled after similar adverbs.
