lyonnaise

1. ADJ

/ liː.əˈneɪz / (AmE), / liː.əˈneɪz / (BrE)
(of food) prepared or served with onions, especially sliced and sautéed onions.
cooked or served with onions

Examples

He ordered the lyonnaise potatoes, which came topped with golden fried onions.
The chef recommended a lyonnaise sauce to accompany the grilled steak.
Her favorite side dish is green beans lyonnaise, sautéed with caramelized onions.

Etymology

From French 'à la lyonnaise' meaning 'in the style of Lyon', referring to Lyon, France, noted for its cuisine.
