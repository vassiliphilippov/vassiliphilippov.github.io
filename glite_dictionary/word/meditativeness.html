meditativeness

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛdəˈteɪtɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪˈteɪtɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being deeply thoughtful or contemplative
state of deep thoughtfulness

Examples

Her meditativeness often made people think she was lost in her own world.
The meditativeness of his writing encourages readers to pause and reflect.
Long walks in nature can inspire a sense of meditativeness in many people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtfulness - the state of being absorbed in thought
  • Synonym: meditation - the act of meditating; deep thinking or reflection

Etymology

Formed from 'meditative' + '-ness' (suffix forming nouns denoting quality or state).
