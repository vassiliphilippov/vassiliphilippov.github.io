talker

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔkɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɔːkə / (BrE)
a person who talks, especially one who talks a lot or who gives speeches in public
a person who talks, often a lot

Examples

Lisa is a great talker and always keeps conversations lively at parties.
He is more of a talker than a doer, often making grand promises.
The talker at the conference captivated everyone with her stories.

Synset Examples

the speaker at commencement
an utterer of useful maxims

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speaker - someone who speaks or gives a speech
  • Synonym: chatterbox - a very talkative person
  • Antonym: listener - a person who listens

Etymology

From Middle English talken (to talk) + -er (agent suffix).
