to shadowbox

1. VERB

/ ˈʃædoʊˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈʃædəʊˌbɒks / (BrE)
to spar or go through boxing movements without an opponent, usually as a training exercise
practice boxing movements solo

Examples

Every morning, he spends fifteen minutes shadowboxing in his living room to stay fit.
Boxers often shadowbox before a match to warm up their muscles.
She shadowboxed in front of the mirror to perfect her stance and punches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spar - to engage in practice fighting or mock combat in boxing
