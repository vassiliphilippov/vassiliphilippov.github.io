shouter

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃaʊtər / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊtə / (BrE)
a person who communicates by shouting; someone who speaks very loudly
person who shouts loudly

Examples

The shouter at the rally drew everyone's attention with his booming voice.
During the game, one particularly loud shouter could be heard above all others.
She became known as a shouter for her passionate speeches to the crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shout - to say something very loudly

Etymology

From 'shout' + '-er', denoting an agent or person who performs an action.
