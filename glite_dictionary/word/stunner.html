stunner

1. NOUN

/ ˈstʌnər / (AmE), / ˈstʌnə / (BrE)
an event or thing that is very surprising or astonishing
a surprising or astonishing event

Examples

The team's comeback victory was a real stunner for the fans.
Saturday's announcement was a stunner that nobody anticipated.
That last-minute goal was an absolute stunner in the championship match.

Synset Examples

the stunner was what happened on Saturday

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shock - a sudden upsetting or surprising event
  • Synonym: surprise - something that causes unexpected astonishment

Etymology

From 'stun' (to shock or astonish) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).

3. NOUN

/ ˈstʌnər / (AmE), / ˈstʌnə / (BrE)
a person or thing that is very impressive or surprising
very impressive or surprising thing or person
informal

Examples

That new sports car is a real stunner and turns heads everywhere it goes.
Her performance in the play was a stunner that received a standing ovation.
Last night's dress was a total stunner, and everyone kept complimenting her.

Synset Examples

The sunset was a stunner, painting the sky with colors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: showstopper - something that is spectacularly impressive and draws a lot of attention
  • Antonym: dud - something that is disappointing or fails to impress

Etymology

From the verb 'stun' + the suffix '-er'.
