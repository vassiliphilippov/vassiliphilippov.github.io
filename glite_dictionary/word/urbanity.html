urbanity

1. NOUN

/ ɝːˈbæn.ə.t̬i / (AmE), / ɜːˈbæn.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
polished courtesy or refinement of manner; elegance and sophistication in social interaction
polished courtesy; refined manner

Examples

Her urbanity charmed everyone at the dinner party.
Despite the tense discussion, he maintained an air of urbanity.
Many admired the diplomat’s urbanity and composure under pressure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: civility - polite and courteous behavior
  • Antonym: coarseness - lack of refinement or courtesy

Etymology

From Latin urbanitas (refinement, elegance), from urbanus (of a city).

2. NOUN

/ ɚˈbænəti / (AmE), / əˈbænɪti / (BrE)
the qualities or character typical of life in a city or town
quality of city life

Examples

The urbanity of downtown neighborhoods attracts people seeking vibrant social scenes.
She admired his urbanity and sophisticated manners at the dinner party.
Writers often contrast the urbanity of the metropolis with the simplicity of village life.

Synset Examples

there is an important difference between rusticity and urbanity

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rusticity - the quality or character of rural life; country manners and simplicity

Etymology

From Latin urbanitas (courtliness, refinement), from urbs (city).
