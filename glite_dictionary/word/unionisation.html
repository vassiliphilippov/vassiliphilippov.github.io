unionisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌjuːniənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌjuːniənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of forming or joining a labor union; the act or policy of organizing workers into unions
forming or joining labor unions

Examples

The company faced a wave of unionisation efforts among its warehouse staff.
Debates over unionisation continue to shape labor policies worldwide.
Management opposed the unionisation of their employees, fearing increased wage demands.

Synset Examples

the issue underlying the strike was unionization

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unionization - the process of forming or joining labor unions (American spelling)

Etymology

From union + -isation, patterned after 'unionize' and '-ation'.
