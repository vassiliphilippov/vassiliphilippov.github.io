Minipress

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɪn.iˌprɛs / (AmE), / ˈmɪn.iˌprɛs / (BrE)
A trademarked brand of the drug prazosin, used to treat high blood pressure and other conditions.
trademarked antihypertensive drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Minipress to help manage his high blood pressure.
Some patients may experience dizziness when taking Minipress for the first time.
If you miss a dose of Minipress, take it as soon as you remember unless it is almost time for your next dose.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prazosin - the generic drug for which Minipress is a brand name
