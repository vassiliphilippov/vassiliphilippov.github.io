cock-a-doodle-doo

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːk ə ˌduːd(ə)l ˈduː / (AmE), / ˌkɒk ə ˌduːd(ə)l ˈduː / (BrE)
an imitation of the crowing sound made by a cock (rooster)
imitation of a rooster's crow

Examples

The rooster let out a loud cock-a-doodle-doo at sunrise, waking the entire farm.
Every morning, his alarm sounds like a cock-a-doodle-doo to make getting up more cheerful.
Children laughed as they tried to mimic the sound with their own cock-a-doodle-doos.

Etymology

Imitative of the natural sound made by a cock or rooster.
