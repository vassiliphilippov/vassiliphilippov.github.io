tights

1. NOUN

/ taɪts / (AmE), / taɪts / (BrE)
A close-fitting garment made of knitted fabric, covering the body from the waist to the feet, worn especially by dancers, acrobats, and as hosiery by women and girls.
close-fitting garment from waist to feet

Examples

She wore a pair of black tights under her dress for extra warmth.
The ballet dancers all performed in matching pink tights and leotards.
Many athletes prefer tights for flexibility during their training sessions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stockings - close-fitting coverings for the foot and lower part of the leg
