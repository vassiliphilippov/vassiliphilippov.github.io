Vetluga River

1. PROPN

/ vɛtˌluːɡə ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / vɛtˌluːɡə ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in central Russia that flows generally southward into the Volga River
river in central Russia, tributary of the Volga

Examples

The Vetluga River is an important waterway for transportation in its region.
Several small towns are located along the banks of the Vetluga River.
The Vetluga River eventually merges with the Volga, Russia's longest river.
