hairless

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛr.ləs / (AmE), / ˈheə.ləs / (BrE)
having no hair or fur on the body or a particular part of the body
having no hair or fur

Examples

The breed of cat she owns is completely hairless and requires special skin care.
After undergoing treatment, he found that his head was almost entirely hairless.
The statue depicted a hairless dog standing alertly at the gate.

Synset Examples

a Mexican Hairless is about the size of a fox terrier and hairless except for a tufts on the head and tail

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bald - having no hair on the scalp or a part of the body
  • Antonym: hairy - covered with hair
