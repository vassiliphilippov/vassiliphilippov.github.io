comfortless

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌmfərtləs / (AmE), / ˈkʌmfətləs / (BrE)
lacking comfort; not providing any sense of ease or relief
lacking comfort or ease

Examples

The hotel room felt cold and comfortless after a long journey.
She spent a comfortless night on the hard floor.
His words were comfortless to those grieving the loss.

Synset Examples

a comfortless room

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uncomfortable - causing or feeling physical or mental discomfort
  • Antonym: comfortable - providing physical or mental ease

Etymology

From 'comfort' + suffix '-less', meaning 'without comfort'.
