jogging

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɑɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒɡɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of running at a slow, steady pace as a form of exercise.
running at a steady pace for exercise

Examples

Morning jogging has become part of her daily routine.
He enjoys jogging through the park after work.
Doctors often recommend jogging as a good way to improve cardiovascular health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: run - move quickly on foot, especially as exercise

Etymology

From the verb 'jog' meaning 'to run slowly,' with the gerund suffix '-ing.'
