localness

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊkəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəlnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being local or connected to a particular place.
quality of being local

Examples

The localness of the bakery made it a favorite among residents.
Her poetry reflects a strong sense of localness and connection to her hometown.
The company emphasized the localness of its products to attract customers.

Synset Examples

What the poet manifests in these poems is his localness, rootedness, his knowing of a particular place, his self-conscious relation to the place.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: locality - a particular place or area
  • Antonym: universality - the quality of being universal rather than local or limited

Etymology

From 'local' + '-ness'.
