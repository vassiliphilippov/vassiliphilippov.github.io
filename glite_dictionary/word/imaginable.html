imaginable

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈmædʒɪnəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪˈmadʒɪnəbl̩ / (BrE)
possible to be imagined; able to be thought of or conceived in the mind.
able to be imagined

Examples

The story explores every imaginable outcome of the experiment.
She used every imaginable excuse to avoid going to the party.
It's hard to believe anyone could have every imaginable gadget in their kitchen.

Synset Examples

that is a conceivable answer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conceivable - capable of being conceived or imagined
  • Antonym: unimaginable - impossible to be imagined

Etymology

From Latin 'imaginari' (to picture to oneself) via French 'imaginable'.
