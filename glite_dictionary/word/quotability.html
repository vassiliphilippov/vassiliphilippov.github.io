quotability

1. NOUN

/ ˌkwoʊtəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌkwəʊtəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being suitable or worthy to be quoted.
quality of being worthy of quotation

Examples

The quotability of his speeches made them popular with journalists.
She admired the quotability of classic literature.
A phrase with high quotability often becomes a popular saying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quotation - a passage or expression that is quoted or cited

Etymology

From 'quote' + '-ability', formed by adding the suffix '-ability' to 'quote'.
