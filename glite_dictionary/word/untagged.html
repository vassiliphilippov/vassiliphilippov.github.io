untagged

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtæɡd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtæɡd / (BrE)
not having a label, tag, or identifying mark attached.
not having a tag or label

Examples

All the untagged animals were later fitted with identification collars.
Please do not leave untagged wires exposed in the control panel.
The museum does not display untagged artifacts to visitors.

Synset Examples

unlabeled luggage is liable to be lost

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tagged - having a tag or label attached

Etymology

From the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'tagged' (having a tag).
