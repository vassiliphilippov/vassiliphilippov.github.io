upvote

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌpˌvoʊt / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌvəʊt / (BrE)
A vote to show approval for content on a website, typically by clicking an up arrow or similar icon.
a positive vote on a website

Examples

Her comment received over 500 upvotes in less than an hour.
If you like this answer, please give it an upvote.
The post didn't get many upvotes compared to others in the thread.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: downvote - a negative vote on content on social media or websites

Etymology

Formed from 'up' + 'vote', popularized by websites such as Reddit and Stack Overflow.

2. VERB

/ ˈʌpˌvoʊt / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌvəʊt / (BrE)
To register a positive vote for content on a website or social media platform, typically to show approval or agreement.
vote in favor of online content

Examples

If you find this answer helpful, please upvote it.
She always upvotes posts that provide clear explanations.
Many users upvoted the suggestion to add dark mode.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: downvote - to register a negative vote for online content
  • Synonym: like - to indicate approval or enjoyment, especially on social media

Etymology

Formed from 'up' + 'vote', by analogy with digital voting systems.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-91000861-v
Synset POS: v
