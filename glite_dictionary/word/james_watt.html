James Watt

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒeɪmz wɑt / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪmz wɒt / (BrE)
(1736–1819) Scottish engineer and inventor whose improvements to the steam engine were fundamental to the Industrial Revolution.
Scottish steam engine inventor (1736–1819)

Examples

The innovations of James Watt transformed the efficiency of industrial machinery.
James Watt is often credited with helping to launch the Industrial Revolution.
Many engineering students learn about the contributions of James Watt to steam technology.
