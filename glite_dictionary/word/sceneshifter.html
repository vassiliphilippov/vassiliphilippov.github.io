sceneshifter

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːnˌʃɪftər / (AmE), / ˈsiːnˌʃɪftə / (BrE)
a stagehand whose job is to move scenery during a theatrical performance
stagehand who moves scenery in theater

Examples

The sceneshifter quickly rearranged the backdrops between acts.
She worked as a sceneshifter at the local theater during college.
The director praised the sceneshifter for his smooth scene changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stagehand - a person who works backstage and assists with scenery and equipment

Etymology

From 'scene' + 'shifter', referring to shifting scenes in theater.
