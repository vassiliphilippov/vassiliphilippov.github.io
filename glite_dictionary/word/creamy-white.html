creamy-white

1. ADJ

/ ˌkrimiˈwaɪt / (AmE), / ˌkriːmiˈwaɪt / (BrE)
having a pale yellowish-white color, like fresh cream
pale yellowish-white color

Examples

The kitten had a soft, creamy-white coat that stood out against the dark furniture.
She chose creamy-white paint for the walls to create a warm, inviting atmosphere.
The wedding dress was made of creamy-white silk that shimmered in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ivory - a yellowish-white color similar to that of ivory
  • Synonym: off-white - a white color with a slight tint of another color
  • Synonym: creamy - having the consistency or color of cream
