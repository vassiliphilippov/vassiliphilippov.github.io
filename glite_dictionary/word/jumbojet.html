jumbojet

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌm.boʊˌdʒɛt / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌm.bəʊˌdʒɛt / (BrE)
a very large jet-powered passenger aircraft, typically designed to carry a substantial number of passengers over long distances
very large passenger jet plane

Examples

The airport was busy as a jumbojet prepared for its transatlantic flight.
Many travelers prefer flying on a jumbojet for long-haul journeys because of their comfort.
When the jumbojet landed, dozens of service vehicles rushed to meet it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airliner - a large passenger aircraft

Etymology

Blend of 'jumbo', meaning large, and 'jet', referring to a jet aircraft.
