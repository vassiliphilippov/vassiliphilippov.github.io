hornist

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrnɪst / (AmE), / ˈhɔːnɪst / (BrE)
a musician who plays a horn, especially a French horn
musician who plays a horn

Examples

The principal hornist performed a beautiful solo during the symphony.
She has been a professional hornist in various orchestras for over a decade.
Every aspiring hornist must practice daily to master the instrument.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horn player - a person who plays a horn instrument

Etymology

From 'horn' (the musical instrument) + '-ist' (denoting a person who practices or is concerned with something).
