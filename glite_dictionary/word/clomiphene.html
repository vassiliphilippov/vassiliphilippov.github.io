clomiphene

1. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊməˌfin / (AmE), / ˈklɒmɪfiːn / (BrE)
a synthetic drug used to stimulate ovulation, especially as a treatment for infertility in women
fertility drug used to stimulate ovulation
medical

Examples

Her doctor prescribed clomiphene to help induce ovulation.
Some women who take clomiphene may experience side effects such as hot flashes or headaches.
Because of clomiphene, the chances of having twins or triplets can increase.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Clomid - brand name for clomiphene as a fertility drug
