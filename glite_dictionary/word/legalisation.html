legalisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌliːɡəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌliːɡəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
The act or process of making something legal by passing a law or regulation.
the act of making something legal

Examples

The legalisation of same-sex marriage was a historic milestone in many countries.
Debates over the legalisation of cannabis continue in various parts of the world.
The government announced its plan for the legalisation of online gambling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legalization - Alternate, chiefly American spelling with the same meaning
  • Antonym: prohibition - The act of forbidding something by law

Etymology

From legal + -isation, patterned after French légalisation.
