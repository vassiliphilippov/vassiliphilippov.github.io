barbwire

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːrbˌwaɪr / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbwʌɪə / (BrE)
strong wire with sharp barbs at regular intervals, used for fencing to prevent passage
wire with barbs used for fencing

Examples

The farmer repaired the fence with new barbwire to keep the cattle from escaping.
Hiking through the field, we had to find a way around the old barbwire barrier.
Rust covered the stretch of barbwire that marked the property's boundary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barbed wire - strong wire with sharp barbs at intervals, used for fencing
