chocolate-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɑklət ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having a deep brown color similar to that of dark chocolate
of a deep brown color

Examples

She wore a beautiful chocolate-colored dress to the party.
The dog had a shiny chocolate-colored coat that gleamed in the sun.
His eyes were a deep, chocolate-colored shade that warmed his smile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brown - a color like that of earth or wood
