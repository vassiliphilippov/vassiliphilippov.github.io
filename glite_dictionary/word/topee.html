topee

1. NOUN

/ təˈpi / (AmE), / təˈpiː / (BrE)
A lightweight sun helmet made of pith or similar material, worn mainly in tropical regions for protection from the sun.
lightweight sun helmet for tropical climates

Examples

The explorer donned a topee before venturing into the jungle.
She bought a topee at the market to shield herself from the harsh midday sun.
Old photographs show soldiers in khaki uniforms and topees during colonial times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pith helmet - a similar lightweight helmet of pith material for sun protection
  • Synonym: sunhat - a hat worn for protection from the sun

Etymology

From Hindi tōpī (cap, hat).
