Pd

1. NOUN

/ piː diː / (AmE), / piː diː / (BrE)
The symbol for palladium, a silver-white metallic element of the platinum group, used in jewelry and industry.
chemical symbol for palladium

Examples

Jewelry made with Pd is known for its durability and resistance to tarnish.
Alloys containing Pd are often used in dental work and electronics.
The chemist noted a trace of Pd in the rock sample.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: palladium - a silver-white metallic element of the platinum group

Etymology

From the name 'palladium,' which was named after the asteroid Pallas.
