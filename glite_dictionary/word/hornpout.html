hornpout

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔːrnˌpaʊt / (AmE), / ˈhɔːnpaʊt / (BrE)
A type of small catfish found in the eastern United States, especially the brown bullhead (Ameiurus nebulosus).
a small eastern U.S. catfish

Examples

Many anglers in New England enjoy fishing for hornpout at dusk.
The pond is well stocked with hornpout and other native fish.
After catching a hornpout, he released it gently back into the water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullhead - another name for the brown bullhead catfish
  • Synonym: catfish - a type of freshwater fish with whisker-like barbels
