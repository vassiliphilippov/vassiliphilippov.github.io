Liege

1. PROPN

/ liːʒ / (AmE), / liːʒ / (BrE)
a city in eastern Belgium, the largest French-speaking city in the country, located on the Meuse River
city in eastern Belgium

Examples

Liege is renowned for its vibrant nightlife and historical landmarks.
After visiting Brussels, they took a train to Liege to explore the city’s museums.
The Meuse River flows through the center of Liege, offering scenic views and pleasant walks.

Etymology

From French Liège, from Latin Leodium.
