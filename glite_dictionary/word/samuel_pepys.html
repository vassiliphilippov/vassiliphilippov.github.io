Samuel Pepys

1. PROPN

/ ˈsæmjuəl ˈpiːps / (AmE), / ˈsæmjʊəl ˈpiːps / (BrE)
(1633–1703) English diarist known for his detailed diary describing daily life and major events in 17th-century England.
English diarist and civil servant (1633–1703)

Examples

Samuel Pepys recorded the Great Fire of London in his diary with remarkable detail.
Many historians study Samuel Pepys to gain insight into 17th-century English society.
The personal accounts in Samuel Pepys's diary provide a unique window into the past.
