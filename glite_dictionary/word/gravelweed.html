gravelweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrævəlˌwid / (AmE), / ˈɡravəlˌwiːd / (BrE)
A perennial herb with yellow flowers native to the southern and south central United States.
a yellow-flowered perennial herb of the southern US

Examples

We found a patch of gravelweed growing along the roadside near Austin.
The yellow blossoms of the gravelweed add color to the summer fields.
Botanists have studied the distribution of gravelweed in the southern states.
