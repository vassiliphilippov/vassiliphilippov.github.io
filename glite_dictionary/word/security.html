security

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈkjʊrəti / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərɪti / (BrE)
an electrical device that activates an alarm if someone tries to break in, used to protect property from unauthorized entry
alarm device against unauthorized entry

Examples

We installed a new security in our house after the break-in last month.
The security went off when the window was forced open.
Most banks use advanced security to protect against theft.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alarm system - a device or set of devices that gives a warning of danger, especially a break-in
