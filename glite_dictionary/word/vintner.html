vintner

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪnt.nɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɪnt.nə / (BrE)
a person who makes or sells wine
wine maker or seller

Examples

The vintner carefully checked the barrels to ensure the wine was aging properly.
As a renowned vintner, she takes great pride in every bottle that leaves her vineyard.
The family's tradition as vintners goes back several generations in this region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: winemaker - a person involved in making wine

Etymology

From Old French 'vinteneor', from 'vin' (wine) + '-ier' (agent suffix), from Latin 'vinum' (wine).

2. NOUN

/ ˈvɪntnɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɪntnə / (BrE)
a person whose business is selling wine or sometimes making wine
wine merchant or wine maker

Examples

The family has been vintners for generations, running a successful wine shop in town.
As a skilled vintner, she sources rare wines from around the world.
The vintner proudly described the unique qualities of the latest vintage to his customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wine merchant - a person or company that sells wine
  • Synonym: winemaker - a person who makes wine

Etymology

From Old French *vintener* (wine dealer), from Latin *vinum* (wine).
