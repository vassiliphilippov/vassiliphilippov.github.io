ricrac

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪkˌræk / (AmE), / ˈrɪkˌrak / (BrE)
a narrow, zigzag-shaped ribbon used as a decorative trimming on clothing or textiles
zigzag ribbon used for trimming

Examples

She sewed ricrac along the edge of the tablecloth for a festive touch.
The little girl's dress was decorated with bright blue ricrac.
Craft stores often carry many different colors and sizes of ricrac.
