cooling

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkuːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of becoming cooler or causing something to become cooler; a decrease in temperature
process of becoming cooler

Examples

The cooling of the engine is crucial to prevent overheating.
Rapid cooling of the metal strengthens its structure.
Modern buildings use advanced systems for air cooling during summer.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heating - the process of becoming warmer or increasing in temperature

2. NOUN

/ ˈkuːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkuːlɪŋ / (BrE)
a system or device for keeping something cool
system or device for cooling

Examples

Industrial plants require efficient cooling to prevent machinery from overheating.
They installed new fans as part of the cooling for the data center.
