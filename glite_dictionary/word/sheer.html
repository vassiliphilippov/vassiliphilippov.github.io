sheer

1. ADV

/ ʃɪr / (AmE), / ʃɪə / (BrE)
Straight up or down without deviation or interruption.
straight up or down (adverb)

Examples

The cliff drops sheer for hundreds of feet to the sea below.
The waterfall plunged sheer into the pool at its base.
He looked over the edge, where the wall fell sheer to the rocks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vertically - in a vertical direction, up or down
Source: WordNet

Synset POS: r

/ ʃɪr / (AmE), / ʃɪə / (BrE)
(adverb) Straight down or directly; completely or perpendicularly.
completely; straight down (adverb)

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: directly - in a straight line or manner

/ ʃɪr / (AmE), / ʃɪə / (BrE)
