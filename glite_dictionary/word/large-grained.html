large-grained

1. ADJ

/ lɑrdʒ ˈɡreɪnd / (AmE), / lɑːdʒ ˈɡreɪnd / (BrE)
Having a texture with relatively large, easily visible grains or particles.
having large, visible grains or texture

Examples

The countertop was made of large-grained granite, giving it a striking appearance.
You can see the large-grained structure in this sample of sand under the microscope.
Some types of bread use large-grained salt for added texture.

Synset Examples

coarse-grained wood
large-grained sand

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fine-grained - having a very small or fine texture
  • Synonym: coarse-grained - having a rough texture with large particles or grains
