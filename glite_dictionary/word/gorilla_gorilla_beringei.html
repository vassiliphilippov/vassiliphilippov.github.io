Gorilla gorilla beringei

1. NOUN

/ ɡəˈrɪlə ɡəˈrɪlə bəˈrɪndʒi / (AmE), / ɡəˈrɪlə ɡəˈrɪlə bəˈrɪndʒi / (BrE)
a subspecies of eastern gorilla native to the Kivu highlands of central Africa, commonly known as the mountain gorilla
mountain gorilla subspecies

Examples

The conservation status of the Gorilla gorilla beringei has drawn international attention due to dwindling numbers.
Researchers observed a small family group of Gorilla gorilla beringei in the Virunga Mountains last spring.
Tourists must follow strict guidelines when visiting areas inhabited by Gorilla gorilla beringei.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mountain gorilla - the common name for Gorilla gorilla beringei, a subspecies of gorilla

Etymology

From modern Latin: genus and species name 'Gorilla', with the subspecies term 'beringei' honoring explorer Captain Robert von Beringe.
