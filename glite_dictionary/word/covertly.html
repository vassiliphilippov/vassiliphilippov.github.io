covertly

1. ADV

/ ˈkoʊ.vɚt.li / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.vət.li / (BrE)
in a way that is secret or hidden; not openly or publicly
secretly; not openly

Examples

She covertly glanced at her phone during the meeting.
The documents were covertly removed from the office late at night.
He covertly monitored their conversation from behind the door.

Synset Examples

he did it covertly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secretly - in a secret manner, not openly
  • Antonym: openly - in a way that is not secret or hidden

Etymology

Formed from 'covert' (from Old French 'covrir', to cover) + '-ly'.
