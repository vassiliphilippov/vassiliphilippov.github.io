techie

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛki / (AmE), / ˈtɛki / (BrE)
a person who is highly skilled and enthusiastic about technology, especially computers and related fields.
technology enthusiast or expert
informal

Examples

My brother is a techie who loves building his own computers from scratch.
The company's IT department is full of friendly techies ready to help with any problem.
At the conference, she found herself surrounded by fellow techie enthusiasts discussing the latest gadgets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geek - a person with a strong interest in technical or intellectual subjects, especially computers
  • Synonym: nerd - a person who is very interested in academic or technical pursuits, often socially awkward

Etymology

Informal alteration of 'tech', short for 'technology', plus the diminutive suffix '-ie'.
