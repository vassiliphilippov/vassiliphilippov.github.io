kookie

1. ADJ

/ ˈkuːki / (AmE), / ˈkuːki / (BrE)
(informal, slang) Strange or eccentric in a way that suggests unusual or odd behavior; slightly crazy or mentally irregular.
strange or eccentric, slightly crazy
informal slang american

Examples

Everyone thought her new invention was a bit kookie, but she didn't mind.
My uncle is known for his kookie sense of humor at family gatherings.
He came up with a kookie plan that actually worked in the end.

Synset Examples

it used to drive my husband balmy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged or extremely eccentric
  • Antonym: sane - having a normal healthy mind; not mentally ill
  • Synonym: eccentric - unconventional and slightly strange

Etymology

Alteration of 'cookie,' popularized in the U.S. in the mid-20th century as slang for an odd person.
