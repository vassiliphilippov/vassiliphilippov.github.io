coliseum

1. NOUN

/ kəˈliːziəm / (AmE), / kɒˈliːziəm / (BrE)
A large oval stadium with tiers of seats, used for sporting events, contests, or public spectacles.
large oval stadium or arena

Examples

The city built a new coliseum to host concerts and sporting events.
We toured the ancient Roman coliseum during our vacation in Italy.
Thousands of fans packed the coliseum on the night of the championship game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arena - a level area surrounded by seats for sports or entertainment

Etymology

From Latin 'Colosseum', the name of the famous Roman amphitheater.
