torturously

1. ADV

/ ˈtɔrtʃɚəsli / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtʃərəsli / (BrE)
in a way that involves or causes severe pain or suffering; in an agonizing manner.
in a very painful manner

Examples

The journey through the mountains progressed torturously due to the difficult terrain.
She waited torturously for the results, unable to think about anything else.
The negotiations moved torturously, with every decision causing more debate and stress.

Synset Examples

the progress was agonizingly slow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agonizingly - in a manner that causes extreme pain or anguish
  • Synonym: painfully - in a way that causes pain or suffering

Etymology

From 'torturous' (relating to torture) + '-ly' adverbial suffix.
