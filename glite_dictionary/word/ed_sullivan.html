Ed Sullivan

1. PROPN

/ ɛd ˈsʌlɪvən / (AmE), / ɛd ˈsʌlɪvən / (BrE)
(1902–1974) American television host, best known for hosting the long-running variety series 'The Ed Sullivan Show.'
famous American TV host (1902–1974)
american

Examples

Many legendary performers got their big break on Ed Sullivan's variety show.
The Beatles made their famous American debut on Ed Sullivan in 1964.
Ed Sullivan was known for his distinctive mannerisms and presence on Sunday night television.
