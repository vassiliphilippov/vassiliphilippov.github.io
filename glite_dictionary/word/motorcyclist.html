motorcyclist

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊtərˌsaɪklɪst / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəˌsaɪklɪst / (BrE)
a person who rides or operates a motorcycle
person who rides a motorcycle

Examples

The motorcyclist skillfully navigated the winding mountain road.
A group of motorcyclists stopped for lunch at the roadside café.
The accident involved both a car and a motorcyclist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biker - a person who rides a motorcycle; synonym of motorcyclist

Etymology

From 'motorcycle' + '-ist' (person who practices or is concerned with something).
