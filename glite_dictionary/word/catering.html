catering

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪ.tɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.tər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or business of providing food and drink, and sometimes service, at social events or in workplaces
providing food and services at events

Examples

We hired a professional catering company for our wedding reception.
Event catering often includes not just the food, but tableware and staff as well.
She works in catering and travels to different venues every week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caterer - a person or company providing catering services
  • Synonym: restaurant - a place where people pay to sit and eat meals prepared and served on the premises

Etymology

From verb 'cater' (to provide food), derived from Middle French 'acater' (to buy, provide), from Latin 'acceptare' (to accept, receive).
