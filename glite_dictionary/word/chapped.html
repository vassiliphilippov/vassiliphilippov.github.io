chapped

1. ADJ

/ tʃæpt / (AmE), / tʃæpt / (BrE)
(of skin) rough, dry, and often cracked as a result of cold weather or exposure.
skin roughened by cold or exposure

Examples

Her chapped lips hurt whenever she tried to smile.
Winter winds can leave your hands chapped and sore.
He applied lotion to his chapped skin after working outside all day.

Synset Examples

chapped lips

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cracked - split or broken with lines on the surface, often due to dryness

Etymology

From Middle English 'chappen' (to crack), probably of imitative origin.
