yokel-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈjoʊkəl laɪk / (AmE), / ˈjəʊkəl laɪk / (BrE)
Having the qualities or appearance associated with a yokel; appearing unsophisticated, naive, or ignorant in a way stereotypically attributed to rural people.
like an unsophisticated rural person

Examples

She gave me a blank, yokel-like stare when I asked about the city's museums.
His yokel-like mannerisms made him stand out among the sophisticated crowd.
The character is portrayed with a deliberately yokel-like accent and behavior.

Synset Examples

the boy's empty yokel-like expression

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rustic - simple or unsophisticated, typically associated with the countryside
  • Antonym: urban - characteristic of a city or town; sophisticated

Etymology

Formed from 'yokel', meaning 'an uneducated or unsophisticated country person', and the suffix '-like'.
