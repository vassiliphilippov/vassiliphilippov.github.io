poteen

1. NOUN

/ poʊˈtiːn / (AmE), / pɒˈtiːn / (BrE)
illegally distilled whiskey made in Ireland, typically at home and without government authorization
illegally distilled Irish whiskey

Examples

In rural Ireland, some families still risk making poteen despite strict laws.
The taste of homemade poteen is much stronger than commercial whiskeys.
Authorities confiscated several gallons of illicit poteen from the remote distillery.

Etymology

From Irish Gaelic 'pota' (pot), referring to the small pot-stills used in production.
