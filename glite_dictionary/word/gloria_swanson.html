Gloria Swanson

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡlɔːriə ˈswɑːnsən / (AmE), / ˈɡlɔːrɪə ˈswɒnsən / (BrE)
(1899–1983) An American actress renowned for her roles in silent films, notably 'Sunset Boulevard'.
American silent film actress (1899–1983)

Examples

Gloria Swanson was celebrated for her iconic performance in the film 'Sunset Boulevard'.
Many silent film stars, including Gloria Swanson, made a successful transition to talking pictures.
As a pioneer of early Hollywood, Gloria Swanson left a lasting legacy on the film industry.
