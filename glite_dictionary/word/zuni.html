Zuni

1. NOUN

/ ˈzuːni / (AmE), / ˈzuːni / (BrE)
A member of the Pueblo people living primarily in western New Mexico.
member of a Pueblo people of New Mexico

Examples

The Zuni are renowned for their intricate pottery and jewelry.
She traveled to New Mexico to study the language of the Zuni.
Many traditions of the Zuni people have been preserved for centuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pueblo - a group of Native American peoples in the southwestern US

Etymology

From the Zuni people's own name for themselves, as rendered in English.
