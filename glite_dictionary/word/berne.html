Berne

1. PROPN

/ bɜrn / (AmE), / bɜːn / (BrE)
the capital city of Switzerland, located in the western part of the country
capital city of Switzerland

Examples

Berne is known for its medieval old town, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
Government offices and foreign embassies are located in Berne.
She traveled from Zurich to Berne by train last summer.

Etymology

From German 'Bern', the city’s local name.
