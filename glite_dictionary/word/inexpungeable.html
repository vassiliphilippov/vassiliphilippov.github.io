inexpungeable

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnɪkˈspʌndʒəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌɪnɪkˈspʌndʒəbl̩ / (BrE)
Impossible to remove, erase, or wipe out completely.
impossible to erase or remove

Examples

The inexpungeable memory of his voice remained with her for years.
There was an inexpungeable stain on the carpet that no cleaning could fix.
Some experiences leave an inexpungeable mark on our lives.

Synset Examples

the inexpungible scent of a bottle of perfume he had broken

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indelible - impossible to remove or erase
  • Antonym: expungeable - able to be removed or erased

Etymology

From Latin prefix in- (not) + English expunge (to erase) + -able.
