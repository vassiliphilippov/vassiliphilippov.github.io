christly

1. ADJ

/ ˈkraɪstli / (AmE), / ˈkraɪstli / (BrE)
Having qualities or showing the spirit of Christ; Christ-like in behavior or nature.
showing the spirit of Christ

Examples

Her christly compassion inspired everyone in the room.
He tried to behave in a christly manner, showing patience and forgiveness.
The charity's mission is guided by christly love for all people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: christlike - having qualities or spirit of Christ

Etymology

Formed from Christ + -ly, after the model of adjectives like 'kingly' or 'saintly'.
