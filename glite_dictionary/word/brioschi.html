Brioschi

1. NOUN

/ briˈoʊski / (AmE), / briˈɒski / (BrE)
A brand of effervescent antacid used to relieve indigestion and heartburn.
brand of effervescent antacid

Examples

After eating a heavy meal, she took some Brioschi to settle her stomach.
You can buy Brioschi at most pharmacies for quick relief from heartburn.
He always keeps a bottle of Brioschi at home for occasional indigestion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antacid - a substance that reduces stomach acidity
