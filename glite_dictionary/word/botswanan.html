Botswanan

1. ADJ

/ bɑːtˈswɑːnən / (AmE), / bɒtˈswɑːnən / (BrE)
relating to Botswana or its people
of or relating to Botswana

Examples

The cultural festival showcased traditional Botswanan music and dance.
She admired the intricate designs of Botswanan crafts.
Several Botswanan students attended the international conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Motswana - a person from Botswana
  • Synonym: Botswanan - a citizen of Botswana (noun form)
