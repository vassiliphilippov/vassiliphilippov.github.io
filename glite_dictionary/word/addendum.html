addendum

1. NOUN

/ əˈdɛndəm / (AmE), / əˈdɛndəm / (BrE)
a section of additional information or material added at the end of a book or document
supplementary section at book's end

Examples

The author included an addendum to address questions raised by readers.
An addendum at the back of the book clarified several complex terms.
Please consult the addendum for recent updates to the report.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appendix - supplementary material at the end of a book
  • Synonym: supplement - something added to complete or enhance

Etymology

From Latin addendum, neuter of addendus (thing to be added), from addere (to add).
