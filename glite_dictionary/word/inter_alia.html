inter alia

1. ADV

/ ɪn.tɚ ˈɑː.li.ə / (AmE), / ɪn.tə ˈɑː.li.ə / (BrE)
among other things; used to indicate that there are additional items or issues not specifically mentioned.
among other things
legal

Examples

The report suggested, inter alia, several measures to improve transparency.
The rules require, inter alia, a review of internal procedures every year.
She mentioned, inter alia, the need for better training in her summary.

Synset Examples

the committee recommended, inter alia, that he be promoted

Etymology

From Latin 'inter alia', meaning 'among other things'.
