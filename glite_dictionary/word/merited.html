merited

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛrɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɛrɪtɪd / (BrE)
Deserved or earned because of actions or qualities.
deserved; earned through merit

Examples

Her promotion was a merited achievement after years of dedication to the company.
The team received merited praise for their outstanding performance.
He believed his criticism of the proposal was merited given its many flaws.

Synset Examples

a merited success

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deserved - rightfully earned or justified

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'merit', from Latin 'meritum' (due reward).
