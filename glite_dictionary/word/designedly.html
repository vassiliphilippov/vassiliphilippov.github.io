designedly

1. ADV

/ dɪˈzaɪnɪdli / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪnɪdli / (BrE)
In an intentional or deliberate manner; on purpose.
intentionally; on purpose

Examples

She designedly avoided all mention of the controversy.
The painting was designedly displayed in the center of the gallery for maximum attention.
His remarks were designedly provocative to spark debate.

Synset Examples

he used that word intentionally
I did this by choice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intentionally - in a deliberate and purposeful way
  • Synonym: deliberately - in a careful, considered, or intentional manner
  • Antonym: accidentally - by chance; not on purpose

Etymology

From design + -ed + -ly, meaning 'in a manner that is designed/intended.'
