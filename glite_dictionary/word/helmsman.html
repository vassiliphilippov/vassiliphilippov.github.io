helmsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlmzmən / (AmE), / ˈhɛlmzmən / (BrE)
a person who steers a ship or boat
person steering a ship

Examples

The helmsman maintained a steady course despite the rough seas.
As helmsman, she was responsible for navigating the yacht through the regatta.
During the storm, the experienced helmsman kept the vessel pointed into the wind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steersman - person who steers a ship or boat

Etymology

From helm (the steering apparatus of a ship) + man.
