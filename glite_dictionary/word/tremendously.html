tremendously

1. ADV

/ trəˈmɛndəsli / (AmE), / trɪˈmɛndəsli / (BrE)
to a very great extent; extremely; immensely
to a very great extent; extremely

Examples

She was tremendously excited about her new job.
The weather improved tremendously after the storm passed.
His contribution to the project helped tremendously in meeting the deadline.

Synset Examples

he was enormously popular

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enormously - to a very great degree; extremely
  • Synonym: vastly - to a very great extent or degree

Etymology

From 'tremendous' + '-ly', deriving from Latin 'tremendus' (to be trembled at, fearful).
