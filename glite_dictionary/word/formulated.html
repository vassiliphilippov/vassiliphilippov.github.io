formulated

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrmjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmjʊˌleɪtɪd / (BrE)
devised or developed according to a systematic or orderly plan; carefully structured.
systematically devised or developed

Examples

She presented her formulated plan to improve the company's efficiency.
His formulated response demonstrated careful consideration of all the facts.
The scientist's formulated theory was based on years of research.

Synset Examples

he had well formulated opinions on schooling

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devised - planned or invented by careful thought
  • Antonym: improvised - created spontaneously without preparation

Etymology

From 'formulate' (from Latin 'formulatus', past participle of 'formulare', from 'formula').
