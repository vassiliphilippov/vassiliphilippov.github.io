assertive

1. ADJ

/ əˈsɝːtɪv / (AmE), / əˈsɜːtɪv / (BrE)
having or showing a confident and forceful personality; inclined to express one's opinions or desires strongly and with confidence.
confident and forceful in expression

Examples

She was very assertive in expressing her opinions during the meeting.
Children who are assertive tend to do better in group activities and discussions.
It is important to be assertive but not aggressive when asking for a raise.

Synset Examples

an energetic assertive boy who was always ready to argue
pointing directly at a listener is an assertive act

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aggressive - ready or likely to attack or confront; forceful; sometimes synonym in context
  • Antonym: passive - accepting or allowing what happens without active response or resistance; antonym

Etymology

From Latin 'assertus', past participle of 'asserere' (to assert, claim).
