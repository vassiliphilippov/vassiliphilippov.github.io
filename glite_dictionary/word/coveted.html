coveted

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌv.ɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈkʌv.ɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
Greatly desired or wanted by many people.
highly desired or wanted

Examples

Winning the coveted prize changed his life overnight.
Many authors dream of seeing their book on the coveted bestseller list.
She received a coveted invitation to the exclusive gala.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desirable - wanted or attractive
  • Antonym: unwanted - not desired or sought after

Etymology

From the verb 'covet,' from Old French 'coveter,' from Latin 'cupere' (to desire).
