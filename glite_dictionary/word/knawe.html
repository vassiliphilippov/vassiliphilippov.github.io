knawe

1. NOUN

/ nɑːv / (AmE), / nɑːv / (BrE)
A widely distributed low-growing Eurasian herb with narrow leaves and inconspicuous green flowers.
Eurasian herb with narrow leaves

Examples

The field was covered in patches of knawe between the wild grasses.
She identified the plant as knawe because of its characteristic narrow leaves.
Conservationists are studying how knawe interacts with other native species.
