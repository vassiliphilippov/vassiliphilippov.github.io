pyrographer

1. NOUN

/ paɪˈrɑːɡrəfər / (AmE), / paɪˈrɒɡrəfə / (BrE)
an artist who creates designs on wood or other materials by burning them with a heated tool
artist using burning to create designs

Examples

The pyrographer demonstrated her technique at the arts and crafts fair.
As a skilled pyrographer, he transformed simple wooden boxes into intricate works of art.
Many pyrographers use a variety of tips to achieve different shading effects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pyrography - the art of decorating wood or other materials with burn marks

Etymology

From pyrography + -er, where 'pyrography' comes from Greek 'pyr' (fire) + 'graphia' (writing).
