carrottop

1. NOUN

/ ˈkærətˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈkærətˌtɒp / (BrE)
a person who has red hair, especially bright or orange-red hair; often used informally.
person with red hair (informal)
informal

Examples

As a child, she was teased for being a carrottop in her class.
The comedian, known as Carrottop, played up his red-haired persona on stage.
Many people mistakenly think all carrottops have freckles as well.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: redhead - a person with red hair

Etymology

Formed from 'carrot' (referring to the orange color of carrots) + 'top' (head or hair); first recorded in the 19th century.
