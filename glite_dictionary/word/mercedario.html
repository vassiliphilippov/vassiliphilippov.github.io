Mercedario

1. PROPN

/ mɛrˈsɛdɑriˌoʊ / (AmE), / mɛˈsɛdɑːriəʊ / (BrE)
a mountain in the Andes of Argentina, rising to 22,210 feet (6,770 meters) in elevation
Andean mountain in Argentina

Examples

The expedition faced harsh weather while climbing Mercedario in the remote Andes.
Standing at the foot of Mercedario, we admired the mountain’s snow-capped peak.
Mountaineers often compare the challenge of Mercedario to that of Aconcagua.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Mercedario,' related to 'merced' (mercy), possibly referencing the religious Order of Mercy.
