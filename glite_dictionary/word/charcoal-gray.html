charcoal-gray

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɑrkoʊl ˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːkəʊl ˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
very dark grey in color, resembling the color of charcoal
very dark gray color

Examples

She wore a charcoal-gray suit to the interview, looking both professional and stylish.
The living room walls are painted in a charcoal-gray shade to create a cozy atmosphere.
He chose a charcoal-gray car because it was less likely to show dirt than lighter colors.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gray - of the color between black and white
  • Degree Higher: black - the darkest color, the absence of light
