tricentenary

1. ADJ

/ traɪˌsɛnˈtinəri / (AmE), / traɪˈsɛntɪnəri / (BrE)
Relating to or marking a period of 300 years or a 300th anniversary.
of or relating to 300 years

Examples

The museum is planning a tricentenary exhibition to celebrate 300 years since its founding.
The city marked its tricentenary year with parades and special events.
Several tricentenary medals were awarded to recognize the organization's long history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tercentenary - relating to a period of 300 years or a 300th anniversary

Etymology

From Latin 'tri-' meaning 'three' and 'centenary' meaning 'of a hundred years'.
