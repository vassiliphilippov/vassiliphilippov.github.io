barehanded

1. ADV

/ bɛrˈhændɪd / (AmE), / bɛəˈhændɪd / (BrE)
Using the hands without gloves or any other covering.
using the hands without gloves

Examples

He caught the snake barehanded and released it into the wild.
She managed to fix the wires barehanded despite the risk.
During the challenge, contestants must open oysters barehanded without any tools.

Synset Examples
