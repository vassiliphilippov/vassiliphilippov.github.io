unchristianly

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkrɪstʃənli / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrɪstʃənli / (BrE)
not suitable for or resembling the qualities expected of a Christian; lacking Christian virtues.
lacking Christian qualities

Examples

It was considered unchristianly to judge others so harshly.
She regretted acting in such an unchristianly manner during the argument.
His response was shockingly unchristianly given his beliefs.

Synset Examples

ashamed to have to recognize how unchristianly his assumptions and motives are

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unchristian - not showing qualities expected of a Christian

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'Christian' + '-ly' (adjectival suffix).
