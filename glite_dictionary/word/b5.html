B5

1. NOUN

/ bi faɪv / (AmE), / biː faɪv / (BrE)
A standard size of paper measuring 176 by 250 millimeters, commonly used internationally.
international standard paper size (176 x 250 mm)

Examples

Please print the document on B5 paper to fit the handbook format.
The notebook comes in several sizes, including B5 and A4.
Many Japanese books are published in the B5 size for convenience.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: A4 - international standard paper size (210 x 297 mm)
  • Degree Higher: B4 - international standard paper size (250 x 353 mm)
  • Degree Lower: B6 - international standard paper size (125 x 176 mm)

Etymology

Paper size B5 is part of the ISO 216 international paper size system, where 'B' series sizes are defined by their geometric properties.
