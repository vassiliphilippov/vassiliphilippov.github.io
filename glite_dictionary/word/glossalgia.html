glossalgia

1. NOUN

/ ɡlɑˈsældʒiə / (AmE), / ɡlɒˈsældʒiə / (BrE)
Pain located in the tongue.
pain in the tongue
medical

Examples

The patient complained of persistent glossalgia following his recent dental procedure.
Effective treatment of glossalgia can significantly improve a person's quality of life.
Several potential causes of glossalgia include allergies, nutritional deficiencies, and irritation from dental appliances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glossodynia - pain or burning sensation in the tongue

Etymology

From Greek 'glōssa' (tongue) and 'algia' (pain).
