higher-up

1. NOUN

/ ˌhaɪərˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌhaɪəˈʌp / (BrE)
a person of higher rank or authority in an organization
person of higher rank
informal

Examples

If you have any complaints, you'll need to discuss them with a higher-up.
The higher-ups made the final decision without consulting the rest of the team.
Sometimes it's hard to get your ideas noticed by the higher-ups in a large company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: superior - a person of higher rank or position
  • Antonym: subordinate - a person of lower rank or position
