symbolizing

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪmbəˌlaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪmbəlaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of representing something by means of a symbol
the act of representing by a symbol

Examples

The waving of flags during the ceremony was a powerful act of symbolizing national pride.
In art, the use of colors and shapes often serves as symbolizing broader concepts and emotions.
The dove is a classic example of symbolizing peace in cultures around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: representation - the action of depicting or standing for something
  • Antonym: literalness - the quality of being literal or exact, not symbolic

Etymology

From the verb 'symbolize', which is from Greek 'symbolon' (token, mark, symbol).
