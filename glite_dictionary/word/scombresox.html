Scombresox

1. NOUN

/ skəmˈbriːˌsɑks / (AmE), / skɒmˈbriːˌsɒks / (BrE)
A genus of marine fishes in the family Scomberesocidae, commonly known as sauries.
genus of saury fishes

Examples

The genus Scombresox includes several species of saury found in temperate seas.
Marine biologists studied the migration patterns of Scombresox during the spring.
Members of the genus Scombresox are elongated fish with beak-like jaws.

Etymology

From New Latin, based on Greek 'skombros' (mackerel) and 'esox' (pike).
