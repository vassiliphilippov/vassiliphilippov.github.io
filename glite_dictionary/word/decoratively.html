decoratively

1. ADV

/ dɪˈkɔːrətɪvli / (AmE), / dɪˈkɒrətɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that is intended to make something look attractive
in a decorative manner

Examples

The room was decoratively arranged with flowers for the ceremony.
Many cakes are decoratively iced to celebrate special occasions.
She hung the lights decoratively around the window frames for the holidays.

Synset Examples

used decoratively at Christmas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ornamentally - in an ornamental manner; for decoration
  • Antonym: plainly - in a simple or unadorned manner
