weaponless

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛpənləs / (AmE), / ˈwɛpənləs / (BrE)
not carrying or using any weapon; unarmed.
not carrying a weapon

Examples

The suspect was found weaponless when the police arrived.
He faced his opponents weaponless, relying only on his negotiation skills.
Many protestors marched weaponless to emphasize their peaceful intentions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unarmed - not having or carrying weapons
  • Antonym: armed - carrying or equipped with weapons
