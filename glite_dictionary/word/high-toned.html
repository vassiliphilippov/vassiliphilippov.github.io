high-toned

1. ADJ

/ haɪ ˈtoʊnd / (AmE), / haɪ ˈtəʊnd / (BrE)
Pretentiously or excessively elegant or refined, often in a showy or affected way.
pretentiously elegant or refined

Examples

The guests felt out of place in such a high-toned setting.
She prefers high-toned parties where everyone dresses up.
They opened a high-toned boutique in the most expensive part of town.

Synset Examples

a high-toned restaurant

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pretentious - attempting to impress by affecting greater importance or merit than possessed
  • Degree Lower: elegant - pleasingly graceful and stylish in appearance or manner
