forklift

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrklɪft / (AmE), / ˈfɔːklɪft / (BrE)
a small industrial vehicle with a powered forked platform at the front, used for lifting and moving heavy loads
industrial vehicle for lifting loads

Examples

You need special training to operate a forklift in this warehouse.
The company uses a forklift to load heavy pallets onto the trucks.
He drove the forklift carefully between the rows of stacked boxes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pallet jack - a device used to lift and move pallets
