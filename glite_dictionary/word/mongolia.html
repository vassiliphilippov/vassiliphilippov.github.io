Mongolia

1. PROPN

/ mɑŋˈɡoʊliə / (AmE), / mɒŋˈɡəʊliə / (BrE)
A region and country in East and Central Asia, including the nation of Mongolia and China's Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.
country and region in Asia

Examples

She traveled across Mongolia on horseback, experiencing the vast steppe landscapes firsthand.
The climate in Mongolia can be harsh, with cold winters and hot summers.
Mongolia has a rich history, once forming the heart of the Mongol Empire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Inner Mongolia - autonomous region in northern China

Etymology

From Medieval Latin Mongolia, from Mongol (the people/group name).

2. PROPN

/ məŋˈɡoʊliə / (AmE), / mɒŋˈɡəʊliə / (BrE)
A landlocked country in East and Central Asia, bordered by Russia and China.
country in East and Central Asia

Examples

He plans to travel across Mongolia by train next year.
The vast steppes of Mongolia are home to nomadic herders.
Ulaanbaatar is the capital city of Mongolia.

Etymology

From the native name Mongol, with the Latinized country suffix -ia.
