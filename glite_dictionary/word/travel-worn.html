travel-worn

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrævəl wɔrn / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl wɔːn / (BrE)
Tired and showing the effects of a long journey.
tired from travel

Examples

After days on the road, the travel-worn family finally reached their destination.
She looked travel-worn but happy as she stepped off the train.
His travel-worn coat was covered in dust from the long journey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weary - feeling or showing tiredness, especially as a result of excessive exertion or lack of sleep
  • Antonym: fresh - not tired; full of energy

Etymology

Formed from 'travel' and 'worn,' where 'worn' is the past participle of 'wear.'
