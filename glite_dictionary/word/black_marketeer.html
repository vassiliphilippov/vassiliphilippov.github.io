black marketeer

1. NOUN

/ ˌblæk ˌmɑr.kəˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˌblæk ˌmɑː.kɪˈtɪə / (BrE)
a person who illegally trades in scarce or controlled goods, especially for personal profit
illegal trader in restricted goods

Examples

During the war, a black marketeer could obtain food and fuel for the right price.
Authorities arrested the black marketeer for selling restricted medicines without a license.
Many black marketeers exploited the shortages by charging exorbitant prices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smuggler - person who illegally imports or exports goods
  • Degree Lower: trader - person engaged in trade or commerce
  • Synonym: trafficker - person who deals or trades in something illegal

Etymology

Formed from 'black market' + '-eer' (agent suffix).
