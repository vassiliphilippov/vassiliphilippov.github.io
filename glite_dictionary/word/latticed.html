latticed

1. ADJ

/ ˈlæt.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈlæt.ɪst / (BrE)
decorated or constructed with a pattern resembling a lattice, typically crisscrossed strips or bars
having a pattern like a lattice

Examples

Sunlight filtered through the latticed window, casting shadows on the floor.
The garden gate was made of latticed wood painted white.
She admired the latticed pattern carved into the old balcony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lattice - a structure of crossed strips or bars, usually arranged to form a diagonal pattern

Etymology

From 'lattice' (Old French 'lattis', from 'lat' meaning 'a lath' or 'strip') plus the suffix '-ed'.
