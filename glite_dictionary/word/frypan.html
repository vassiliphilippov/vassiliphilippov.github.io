frypan

1. NOUN

/ ˈfraɪˌpæn / (AmE), / ˈfraɪˌpæn / (BrE)
A flat-bottomed pan used for frying, searing, or browning foods.
a pan for frying food

Examples

She heated oil in the frypan before adding the vegetables.
The frypan needs to be cleaned after making breakfast.
Use a non-stick frypan to cook the pancakes evenly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frying pan - a pan with a flat bottom used for frying food
