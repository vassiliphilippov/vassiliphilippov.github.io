bellybutton

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛliˌbʌt(ə)n / (AmE), / ˈbɛliˌbʌt(ə)n / (BrE)
The small scar or depression in the center of the abdomen marking the spot where the umbilical cord was attached.
scar at site of umbilical cord attachment

Examples

She wore a cropped shirt that showed off her bellybutton.
He asked the doctor why everyone has a bellybutton.
The baby giggled when her mother gently touched her bellybutton.

Synset Examples

you were not supposed to show your navel on television
they argued whether or not Adam had a navel
she had a tattoo just above her bellybutton

Related Terms

  • Synonym: navel - the small, central scar on the abdomen, also called the bellybutton
  • Synonym: umbilicus - technical term for the navel or bellybutton

Etymology

Formed from "belly" + "button", likening the navel's appearance to a button.
