enkindled

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈkɪndəld / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɪndəld / (BrE)
set on fire; ignited or made to burn.
set on fire; ignited
archaic

Examples

The enkindled logs crackled and sent sparks into the night air.
A sense of hope was enkindled within the weary travelers as dawn broke.
Her enkindled passion for justice inspired everyone around her.

Synset Examples

the ignited paper
a kindled fire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ignited - set afire or made to burn
  • Antonym: extinguished - put out; ceased burning

Etymology

From en- (causative prefix) + kindled, from Middle English kindlen.
