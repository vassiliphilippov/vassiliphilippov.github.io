unalert

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈlɝːt / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈlɜːt / (BrE)
not paying attention to potential danger or to what is happening; not alert
not alert; inattentive to danger

Examples

The guard was unalert, missing the intruder as he slipped through the gate.
She felt unalert after staying up much too late the night before.
Drivers who are unalert may cause accidents on busy roads.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: alert - quick to notice danger or problems; attentive
  • Synonym: inattentive - not paying attention

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'alert'.
