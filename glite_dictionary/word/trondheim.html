Trondheim

1. PROPN

/ ˈtrɑːnˌhaɪm / (AmE), / ˈtrɒn.haɪm / (BrE)
A port city in central Norway situated on the Trondheim Fjord, known for its historical and cultural significance.
port city in central Norway

Examples

Many tourists visit Trondheim to see its famous Nidaros Cathedral.
The university in Trondheim attracts students from around the world.
Shipping is an important industry for Trondheim due to its location on the fjord.

Etymology

From Norwegian Trondheim, from Old Norse Þróndheimr (home of the strong ones, or place of the Trønder people).
