isogonic

1. ADJ

/ ˌaɪsəˈɡɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəˈɡɒnɪk / (BrE)
Having or making equal angles.
having equal angles

Examples

The triangle has isogonic properties, with all interior angles the same.
In some crystals, the axes are isogonic, resulting in symmetrical shapes.
Isogonic lines on a map show points where the magnetic declination is equal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: isogonal - having or forming equal angles

Etymology

From Greek isogonios, from iso- (equal) + gonia (angle).
