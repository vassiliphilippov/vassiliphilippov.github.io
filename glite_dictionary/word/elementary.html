elementary

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛləˈmɛn.tə.ri / (AmE), / ˌɛl.ɪˈmen.tər.i / (BrE)
relating to elementary school or elementary education, typically for young children in the first years of schooling.
relating to elementary school/education

Examples

She teaches elementary students in the second grade.
Most elementary schools offer basic courses in reading, writing, and math.
The science fair is open to all elementary classes in the district.

Synset Examples

the elementary grades
elementary teachers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: primary - relating to the first years of formal education, especially in British English

Etymology

From Latin 'elementarius', from 'elementum' meaning 'rudiment' or 'first principle'.

easy and uncomplicated

Examples

For beginners, it's best to start with elementary exercises before trying more advanced problems.

  • Synonym: uncomplicated - not difficult or complex
  • Antonym: complex - not simple; consisting of many different and connected parts
  • Antonym: complicated - difficult to analyze or understand
