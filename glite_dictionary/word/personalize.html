to personalize

1. VERB

/ ˈpɜrsənəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəlaɪz / (BrE)
to design or change something so that it is suitable for a particular person, or to make it show a person's name, interests, or style
make something personal or individual

Examples

You can personalize your profile by adding a photo and a short biography.
She ordered a mug and asked the company to personalize it with her father's name.
Many websites now personalize their content based on your browsing history.

Synset Examples

personalized service

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to customize - to modify something to suit a particular individual or task

Etymology

From personal + -ize, indicating to make or become personal.
