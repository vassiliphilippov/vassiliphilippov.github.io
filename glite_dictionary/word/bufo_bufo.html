Bufo bufo

1. NOUN

/ ˌbjuːfoʊ ˈbjuːfoʊ / (AmE), / ˌbjuːfəʊ ˈbjuːfəʊ / (BrE)
the scientific name for the common toad found throughout much of Europe
European common toad (species name)

Examples

The Bufo bufo is a familiar sight in gardens and forests across Europe.
Scientists conducted a population survey of Bufo bufo during the spring breeding season.
Children were excited to spot a large Bufo bufo while walking near the pond.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: common toad - the common toad of Europe, alternative common name for Bufo bufo

Etymology

From Latin, where 'bufo' means 'toad'; used as the genus and species name in zoological taxonomy.
