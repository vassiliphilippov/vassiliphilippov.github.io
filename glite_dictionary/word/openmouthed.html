openmouthed

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊpənˌmaʊðd / (AmE), / ˈəʊpənˌmaʊðd / (BrE)
with the mouth open, typically in surprise, wonder, or shock
with mouth open in surprise

Examples

She stared openmouthed at the unexpected fireworks display in the sky.
The children listened openmouthed to the incredible story their teacher told.
He stood openmouthed as he watched his favorite team win the championship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agape - with the mouth wide open in surprise or wonder
