bowel

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaʊ.əl / (AmE), / ˈbaʊ.əl / (BrE)
the part of the digestive system between the stomach and the anus, including the small and large intestines
part of the digestive tract between stomach and anus

Examples

The doctor explained that a healthy bowel is essential for good digestion.
She underwent surgery to remove a blockage in her bowel.
He experienced severe pain in his bowel after eating contaminated food.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intestine - the long tube in the body between the stomach and the anus

Etymology

From Old French 'boel', from Latin 'botellus' (intestine).
