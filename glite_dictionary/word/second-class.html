second-class

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛkənd klæs / (AmE), / ˌsɛkənd ˈklɑːs / (BrE)
considered to be of inferior status or quality; not enjoying the same privileges or value as others
of inferior status or quality

Examples

After the policy change, many workers felt they were being treated as second-class employees.
She refused to accept second-class treatment at the hotel.
The new regulations created a second-class tier of healthcare access.

Synset Examples

a second-class citizen
second-class accommodations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inferior - of lower quality or rank
  • Antonym: first-class - of the highest standard or quality
