Mazatlan

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɑːzəˈtlɑːn / (AmE), / ˌmæzəˈtlæn / (BrE)
a port city in western Mexico on the Pacific Ocean, known for its beaches and tourist attractions
port city in western Mexico

Examples

Many tourists visit Mazatlan each year to enjoy its beautiful beaches and vibrant nightlife.
The cruise ship docked at Mazatlan for a full day of sightseeing and shopping.
After moving to Mazatlan, she opened a small café near the harbor.

Etymology

From Nahuatl *Mazatlán* meaning 'place of deer'.
