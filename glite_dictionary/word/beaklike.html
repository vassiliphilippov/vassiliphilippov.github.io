beaklike

1. ADJ

/ ˈbiːkˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbiːkˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or shaped like the beak of a bird
resembling a bird's beak

Examples

The ancient fish had a beaklike jaw, perfect for crushing shells.
Her grandfather’s beaklike nose gave him a stern appearance.
Some orchids display beaklike structures to attract specific pollinators.

Synset Examples

a grizzled man with a beaklike nose

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beaked - having a beak or a prominent, beak-shaped part
