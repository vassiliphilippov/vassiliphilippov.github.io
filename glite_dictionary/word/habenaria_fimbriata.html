Habenaria fimbriata

1. NOUN

/ hæbəˈnɛriə fɪmˈbraɪətə / (AmE), / hæbəˈnɪəriə fɪmˈbraɪətə / (BrE)
A species of North American orchid with larger, paler flowers, similar to Habenaria psycodes.
North American orchid species

Examples

The Habenaria fimbriata orchid is prized by collectors for its distinctive fringed petals.
I photographed several specimens of Habenaria fimbriata blooming in the meadow during early summer.
Unlike its relative, Habenaria fimbriata has larger, paler flowers that are easy to identify.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Habenaria psycodes - another North American orchid species with similar appearance

Etymology

From Latin; genus 'Habenaria' and specific epithet 'fimbriata' meaning fringed.
