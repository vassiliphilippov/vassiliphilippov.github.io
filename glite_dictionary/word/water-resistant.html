water-resistant

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɔt̬ɚ rɪˈzɪstənt / (AmE), / ˌwɔːtə rɪˈzɪstənt / (BrE)
not easily penetrated or damaged by water; able to resist the passage or effects of water to some extent, but not entirely waterproof
not easily penetrated by water

Examples

The jacket is water-resistant, making it suitable for light rain.
Please check if your phone is labeled as water-resistant before taking it to the pool.
These shoes are water-resistant, which helps keep your feet dry on wet days.

Synset Examples

a water-repellent coat

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: waterproof - completely impervious to water
  • Synonym: water-repellent - designed to keep water off surfaces but not fully waterproof
