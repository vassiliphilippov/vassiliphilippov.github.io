seats

1. NOUN

/ siːts / (AmE), / siːts / (BrE)
an area or set of places where several people can sit, especially in a particular venue or room
places where several people can sit

Examples

The concert hall has 500 seats available for guests.
We arrived early to make sure we got good seats at the cinema.
There are enough seats in the auditorium for the entire class.

Synset Examples

there is seating for 40 students in this classroom

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seating - an area that includes seats for people
