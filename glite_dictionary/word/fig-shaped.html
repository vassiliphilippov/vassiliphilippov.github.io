fig-shaped

1. ADJ

/ fɪɡ ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / fɪɡ ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape or form of a fig; resembling a fig in outline or appearance.
resembling the shape of a fig

Examples

The vase had a distinctive fig-shaped body that made it stand out from the others.
Many ancient ornaments were fig-shaped, a popular motif in Mediterranean art.
She chose a fig-shaped pendant for her necklace, symbolizing abundance and fertility.
