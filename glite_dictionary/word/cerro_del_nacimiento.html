Cerro del Nacimiento

1. PROPN

/ ˌsɛɾoʊ dɛl nɑːsiˈmjɛntoʊ / (AmE), / ˌsɛrəʊ dɛl næsɪˈmiːntəʊ / (BrE)
a mountain peak in the Andes of Argentina, standing at 21,302 feet (6,491 meters) high.
Argentinian peak in the Andes

Examples

Climbers from around the world travel to Argentina to ascend Cerro del Nacimiento.
The snow-capped summit of Cerro del Nacimiento is visible from miles away on clear days.
Expeditions to Cerro del Nacimiento require both experience and careful acclimatization due to its altitude.

Etymology

From Spanish, meaning 'Hill of the Birth'.
