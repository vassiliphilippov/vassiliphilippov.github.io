curatorial

1. ADJ

/ ˌkjʊr.əˈtɔr.i.əl / (AmE), / ˌkjʊə.rəˈtɔː.ri.əl / (BrE)
relating to a curator or the work of managing and overseeing the care and organization of collections, especially in museums or galleries
relating to the work of a curator

Examples

She was responsible for the curatorial decisions for the new art exhibition.
His curatorial expertise was evident in the way each artifact was displayed.
The museum held a workshop on curatorial practices for aspiring professionals.

Synset Examples

curatorial duties

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curator - a person who manages or oversees a museum or collection

Etymology

From Latin 'curator' (guardian, overseer), with the English suffix '-ial.'
