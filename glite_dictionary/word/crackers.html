crackers

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrækɚz / (AmE), / ˈkrækəz / (BrE)
Very silly or crazy; not behaving in a sensible or reasonable way (informal, chiefly British).
informal; crazy or silly
informal british

Examples

My grandmother thought I was crackers to walk home alone at night.
Everyone said the inventor was crackers for trying to build a flying car.
You must be crackers if you think that plan will work.
