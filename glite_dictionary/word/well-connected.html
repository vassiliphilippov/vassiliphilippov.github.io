well-connected

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɛl.kəˈnɛk.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌwɛl.kəˈnɛk.tɪd / (BrE)
having influential friends or family, or belonging to a network of people of wealth or social status
having powerful or influential connections

Examples

Her father was a well-connected businessman, which helped her career considerably.
The lawyer is well-connected in political circles, giving him access to valuable information.
They preferred to hire consultants who were well-connected within the industry.

Synset Examples

a well-connected Edinburgh family

Related Terms

  • Synonym: influential - having great influence or power
  • Antonym: unconnected - having no relationships with influential people
