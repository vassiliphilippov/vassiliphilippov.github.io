Glossopsitta

1. NOUN

/ ɡlɑsəˈsɪtə / (AmE), / ɡlɒsəˈsɪtə / (BrE)
A genus of small parrots native to Australia, commonly known as lorikeets, belonging to the subfamily Loriinae.
genus of small Australian lorikeets

Examples

The Glossopsitta species are known for their vibrant plumage and brush-tipped tongues.
Many naturalists study Glossopsitta to understand their role in pollinating Australian plants.
A colorful member of Glossopsitta can often be spotted feeding on nectar in eucalyptus forests.

Etymology

From Greek 'glossa' (tongue) and 'psitta' (parrot).
