noctilucent

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑːktɪˈluːsənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒktɪˈluːsənt / (BrE)
shining or glowing by night; emitting a visible light in the darkness, especially used of clouds or organisms visible after sunset.
shining or glowing by night

Examples

The rare noctilucent clouds could be seen shimmering in the summer twilight.
Some marine animals produce a noctilucent glow in the ocean at night.
The cat's noctilucent eyes appeared to shine as they caught the moonlight.

Synset Examples

the noctilucent eyes of a cat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luminous - emitting or reflecting light, especially in the dark

Etymology

From Latin 'nocti-' (night) + 'lucent' (shining).
