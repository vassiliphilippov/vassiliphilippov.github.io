investigating

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈvɛstəˌɡeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɛstɪˌɡeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of examining something carefully and systematically in order to discover facts or information
examining something systematically

Examples

The police are currently investigating the cause of the fire.
Her investigating led to some surprising discoveries about the company.
Thorough investigating is essential before making any accusations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: investigation - the action of investigating something or someone

Etymology

From the verb 'investigate', which is from Latin investigare (to trace out, search after).
