chemist's

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛmɪsts / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪsts / (BrE)
a shop where medicines and other healthcare products are sold; a pharmacy (chiefly British English)
British English term for pharmacy or drugstore
british

Examples

I stopped at the chemist's to buy some painkillers.
She works part-time at the local chemist's on weekends.
You can pick up your prescription from the chemist's on the corner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pharmacy - a shop or store where medicinal drugs are dispensed and sold
  • Synonym: drugstore - an American English term for a shop selling medicines and various other items

Etymology

Uses the possessive form of 'chemist', referring to the shop of a chemist; common in British English.
