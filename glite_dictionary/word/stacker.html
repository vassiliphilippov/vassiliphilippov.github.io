stacker

1. NOUN

/ ˈstækɚ / (AmE), / ˈstækə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to build up or arrange stacks or piles of things, such as goods or materials
person who stacks items or builds piles

Examples

The stacker carefully arranged the boxes into neat, stable piles in the warehouse.
A team of stackers worked quickly to prepare the goods for shipment.
He found a job as a stacker at a local distribution center.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: packer - a person who packs goods or materials
