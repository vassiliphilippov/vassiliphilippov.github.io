cabinetry

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæbɪnɛtɹi / (AmE), / ˈkabɪnɪtɹi / (BrE)
the craft of making cabinets and other furniture, especially of high quality
making fine cabinets and furniture

Examples

He studied cabinetry at a technical school before starting his own workshop.
The museum displayed examples of eighteenth-century cabinetry crafted by master artisans.
Her passion for cabinetry led her to restore antique furniture as a profession.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpentry - the activity or occupation of making or repairing things in wood
  • Synonym: joinery - the wooden components of a building, such as stairs, doors, and window frames

Etymology

From cabinet + -ry, denoting a collective or related art.
