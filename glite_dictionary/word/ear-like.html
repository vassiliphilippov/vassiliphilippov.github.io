ear-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪrˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɪəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a shape or appearance that resembles an ear.
resembling an ear in shape

Examples

The leaves of the plant have a distinct ear-like shape.
She admired the ear-like appendages on the sides of the statue.
Some fungi grow in curious ear-like formations on tree trunks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auriform - having the shape of an ear
