onanist

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊ.nə.nɪst / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.nə.nɪst / (BrE)
a person who practices masturbation
person who masturbates

Examples

The Victorians often considered an onanist to be guilty of a moral failing.
He was accused of being an onanist in the heated argument.
Medical texts in the past routinely warned against becoming an onanist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masturbator - a person who masturbates

Etymology

From Latin Onan (a biblical character), from the story of Onan in Genesis, used as an eponym for masturbation.
