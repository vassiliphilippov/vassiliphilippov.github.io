childly

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃaɪldli / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪldli / (BrE)
Characteristic of or suitable for a young child; befitting a child.
befitting a young child

Examples

Her childly innocence made everyone smile.
He looked at the world with a childly sense of wonder.
The room was decorated in a childly style, bright and playful.

Synset Examples

childlike charm

Related Terms

  • Synonym: childlike - having qualities, such as innocence or simplicity, associated with a child
  • Synonym: childish - acting in a way typical of a child, often with a negative implication

Etymology

From child + -ly, forming an adjective meaning 'like a child'.
