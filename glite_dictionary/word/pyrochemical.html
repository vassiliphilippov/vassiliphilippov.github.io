pyrochemical

1. ADJ

/ ˌpaɪ.roʊˈkɛm.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌpaɪ.rəʊˈkɛm.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to or produced by chemical reactions at high temperatures
involving chemical reactions at high temperatures

Examples

The pyrochemical process was used to extract rare metals from the ore.
Researchers studied new pyrochemical techniques for nuclear waste treatment.
Industrial furnaces operate under pyrochemical conditions to achieve desired reactions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermochemical - relating to chemical changes and heat

Etymology

From Greek 'pyro-' (fire) + 'chemical'.
