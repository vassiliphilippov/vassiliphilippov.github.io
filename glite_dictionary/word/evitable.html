evitable

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛvɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɛvɪtəbəl / (BrE)
Able to be avoided or prevented.
capable of being avoided or prevented

Examples

The economic crisis was not evitable, given the warning signs.
With better planning, many mistakes are evitable.
Some accidents are evitable if proper safety measures are followed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inevitable - certain to happen; unavoidable

Etymology

From Latin evitabilis, from evitare (to avoid).
