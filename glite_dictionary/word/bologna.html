Bologna

1. PROPN

/ boʊˈloʊnjə / (AmE), / bəˈləʊnjə / (BrE)
A city in northern Italy, the capital of the Emilia-Romagna region, known for its historic center and university.
city in northern Italy

Examples

The University of Bologna is one of the oldest in the world.
During their trip to Italy, they spent several days exploring Bologna's historic streets.
The cuisine of Bologna is renowned for its rich flavors and traditional dishes.

Etymology

From Italian Bologna, from Latin Bononia.
