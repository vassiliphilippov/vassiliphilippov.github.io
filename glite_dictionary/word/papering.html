papering

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪ.pɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ.pər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of applying wallpaper to walls in order to decorate or cover them
application of wallpaper to walls

Examples

The papering of the living room took the decorator all afternoon.
They hired a professional to handle the papering because the pattern was difficult to match.
After the new papering, the hallway looked fresh and inviting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wallpapering - the act or process of putting wallpaper on a wall
