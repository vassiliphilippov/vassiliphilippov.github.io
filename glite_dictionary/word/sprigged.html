sprigged

1. ADJ

/ sprɪɡd / (AmE), / sprɪɡd / (BrE)
decorated with a pattern of small sprigs or sprays of leaves or flowers
decorated with sprig-like patterns

Examples

She wore a beautiful dress made of sprigged muslin to the garden party.
The curtains in the cottage were sprigged with tiny blue flowers.
He bought a set of sprigged plates with a delicate green leaf design.

Synset Examples

sprigged muslin

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patterned - decorated with a repeated design

Etymology

From 'sprig' (a small stem or shoot), with the adjectival suffix '-ed'.
