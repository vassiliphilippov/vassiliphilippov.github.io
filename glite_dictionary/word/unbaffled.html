unbaffled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbæfəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈbæfəld / (BrE)
not confused or perplexed by difficult or contradictory situations; not baffled.
not confused or perplexed

Examples

Despite the complexity of the instructions, Maria remained unbaffled and finished the task quickly.
He listened to the contradictory reports, but his mind remained unbaffled.
The detective was unbaffled by the misleading clues and solved the case efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: baffled - confused or perplexed
  • Synonym: confused - unable to think clearly or understand
