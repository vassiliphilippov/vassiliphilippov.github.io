Gustavus Adolphus

1. PROPN

/ gəˈsteɪvəs əˈdɑːlfəs / (AmE), / gʊsˈtɑːvəs əˈdɒlfəs / (BrE)
(1594–1632) King of Sweden noted for transforming Sweden into a major European power through military victories and domestic reforms, and for his role in the Thirty Years' War.
King of Sweden (1594–1632), military leader

Examples

Gustavus Adolphus was a pivotal leader during the Thirty Years' War in Europe.
Historians often praise Gustavus Adolphus for his innovative military strategies.
The reign of Gustavus Adolphus marked a turning point in Swedish history.

Etymology

Latinized form of the Swedish name Gustav Adolf.
