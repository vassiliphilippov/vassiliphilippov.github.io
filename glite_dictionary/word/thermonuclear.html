thermonuclear

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɝː.moʊˈnuː.kli.ɚ / (AmE), / ˌθɜː.məʊˈnjuː.klɪ.ə / (BrE)
Involving or relating to nuclear reactions that occur at extremely high temperatures, especially those based on nuclear fusion rather than fission.
relating to nuclear fusion reactions

Examples

The test involved a thermonuclear device with a much greater yield than previous atomic bombs.
Many countries have developed thermonuclear weapons as part of their defense programs.
A thermonuclear reaction requires extremely high temperatures to trigger nuclear fusion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nuclear - related to atomic nuclei or nuclear reactions
  • Antonym: fission - splitting of an atomic nucleus, contrasting with fusion

Etymology

From thermo- (relating to heat) and nuclear, referring to the high temperatures needed for nuclear fusion.
