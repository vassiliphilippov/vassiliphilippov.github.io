protectively

1. ADV

/ prəˈtɛktɪvli / (AmE), / prəˈtɛktɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a desire to protect someone or something from harm or danger.
in a protective manner

Examples

He stood protectively between his friend and the barking dog.
She wrapped her arms protectively around her child during the storm.
He hovered protectively by her side as they walked through the crowded street.

Synset Examples

he bent protectively over the woman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defensively - in a way intended to defend or protect
  • Antonym: aggressively - in a way that is ready or likely to attack or confront
