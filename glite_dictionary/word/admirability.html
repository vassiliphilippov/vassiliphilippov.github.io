admirability

1. NOUN

/ ˌæd.mɪ.rəˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌæd.mɪ.rəˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality of being admirable or worthy of admiration
the quality of being admirable

Examples

Her admirability stems from her unwavering dedication to helping others.
The admirability of his achievements has inspired many young people.
We were all struck by the admirability of her calm response under pressure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admirableness - the quality of being admirable

Etymology

From admirable + -ity, based on Latin 'admirabilis' (admirable) and '-itas' (-ity, denoting quality).
