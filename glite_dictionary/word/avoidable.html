avoidable

1. ADJ

/ əˈvɔɪdəbl̩ / (AmE), / əˈvɔɪdəbl / (BrE)
Able to be prevented or not allowed to happen.
able to be prevented

Examples

The accident was completely avoidable if proper safety measures had been taken.
Many health problems are avoidable through good nutrition and regular exercise.
The meeting ran late due to an avoidable misunderstanding.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unavoidable - impossible to prevent or escape
  • Synonym: preventable - able to be prevented from occurring

Etymology

From avoid +‎ -able; ultimately from Old French 'eviter' and Latin 'evitare' (to avoid).
