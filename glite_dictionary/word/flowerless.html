flowerless

1. ADJ

/ ˈflaʊ.ɚ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ə.ləs / (BrE)
(of a plant) lacking flowers or not producing flowers or seeds.
without flowers or blooms

Examples

The garden stayed green all winter with its flowerless shrubs and evergreens.
Some ancient plant species are flowerless and reproduce by spores instead of seeds.
The scientist studied a rare flowerless fern in the rainforest.

Synset Examples

a flowerless plant

Related Terms

  • Antonym: flowering - having flowers or producing flowers
