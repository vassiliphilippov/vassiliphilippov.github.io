flindosa

1. NOUN

/ flɪnˈdoʊsə / (AmE), / flɪnˈdəʊsə / (BrE)
A tall Australian timber tree that yields tough, hard wood used for staves and similar items.
Australian timber tree

Examples

The durable wood of the flindosa is prized for making tool handles and staves.
Many old fences in the region were built using flindosa timber.
The flindosa grows throughout several parts of eastern Australia.
