handcart

1. NOUN

/ ˈhænd.kɑrt / (AmE), / ˈhænd.kɑːt / (BrE)
A small vehicle with wheels, designed to be pushed by hand, used for carrying goods.
small wheeled vehicle pushed by hand

Examples

The street vendor loaded his handcart with fresh fruit every morning.
They used a handcart to transport the old books across campus.
After the storm, volunteers filled a handcart with debris from the park.

Synset Examples

he used a handcart to carry the rocks away
their pushcart was piled high with groceries

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pushcart - a small cart that is pushed by hand, especially for selling goods in the street

Etymology

From 'hand' + 'cart', indicating a cart moved by hand rather than by animal or engine power.
