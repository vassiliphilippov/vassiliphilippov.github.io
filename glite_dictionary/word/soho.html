SoHo

1. PROPN

/ ˈsoʊˌhoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊˌhəʊ / (BrE)
a fashionable district in southwestern Manhattan, New York City, known for its trendy shops, restaurants, art galleries, and converted lofts
fashionable district of Manhattan, New York City

Examples

Many artists moved to SoHo in the 1970s, attracted by its spacious lofts and vibrant cultural scene.
The boutique opened its first New York store in SoHo to attract fashion-conscious shoppers.
Tourists often explore the galleries and restaurants that make SoHo a popular destination.

Etymology

From 'South of Houston Street', referring to its location in Manhattan.
