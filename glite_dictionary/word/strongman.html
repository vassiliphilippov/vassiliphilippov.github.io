strongman

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɔŋˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈstrɒŋˌman / (BrE)
a man who demonstrates great physical strength, especially by performing feats of strength at fairs or circuses
man who performs feats of strength

Examples

The strongman amazed the crowd by bending steel bars with his bare hands.
As a child, she was fascinated by the strongman at the traveling circus.
Each year, the festival featured a strongman contest with impressive feats of strength.

2. NOUN

/ ˈstrɔŋˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈstrɒŋˌmæn / (BrE)
a male political leader who wields power through force, violence, or authoritarian control
authoritarian political leader

Examples

Several countries have been led by a strongman who suppressed dissent and controlled the media.
The strongman remained in power for decades through ruthless tactics and intimidation.
International organizations condemned the actions of the country's strongman after the violent crackdown.

Synset Examples

he is determined to bring down the Iraqi strongman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dictator - an absolute ruler with unrestricted power, often ruling oppressively
  • Synonym: autocrat - a ruler with absolute power over a country or organization
  • Antonym: democrat - an advocate or supporter of democracy or democratic government

Etymology

From 'strong' + 'man'; originally referred to a man of great physical strength, later extended to dictatorial leaders.
