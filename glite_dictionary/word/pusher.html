pusher

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊʃər / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃə / (BrE)
a person who sells illegal drugs, especially without a license
illegal drug dealer

Examples

The police arrested a notorious pusher operating near the school.
Parents are concerned about a pusher targeting teenagers in the neighborhood.
After years of addiction, he finally testified against his former pusher.
