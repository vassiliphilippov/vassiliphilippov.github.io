weightless

1. ADJ

/ ˈweɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈweɪtləs / (BrE)
having no detectable weight, or seeming to have no weight, especially because of the absence of gravity
having no weight; lacking gravity

Examples

Astronauts experience a weightless sensation while orbiting the Earth.
The scarf was so weightless that it barely felt like she was wearing it.
In the film, objects floated around in a weightless environment.

Synset Examples

floating freely in a weightless condition
a baby bat … fluffy and weightless as a moth
jackets made of a weightless polyester fabric

Related Terms

  • Synonym: light - having little weight; not heavy
  • Antonym: heavy - of great weight; difficult to lift or move

Etymology

from 'weight' + suffix '-less', meaning 'without weight'
