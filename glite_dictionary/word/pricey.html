pricey

1. ADJ

/ ˈpraɪsi / (AmE), / ˈpraɪsi / (BrE)
(informal) costing a lot of money; expensive.
expensive, high in price
informal

Examples

That boutique downtown is a bit too pricey for my budget.
We wanted to buy tickets, but they were surprisingly pricey this year.
The restaurant is known for its pricey yet delicious meals.
