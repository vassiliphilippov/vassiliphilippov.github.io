chiller

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪlə / (BrE)
a story, film, or event that causes fear or intense excitement; something designed to thrill or frighten
frightening story, film, or event

Examples

Last night we watched a chiller that kept us on the edge of our seats.
Her latest novel is a real chiller filled with supernatural events.
He avoided chillers because they gave him nightmares.

Synset Examples

the movie was an old-fashioned hair-raiser

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thriller - an exciting or suspenseful story or film
