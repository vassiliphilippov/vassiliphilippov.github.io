augitic

1. ADJ

/ ɔːˈdʒɪtɪk / (AmE), / ɔːˈdʒɪtɪk / (BrE)
of, relating to, or containing augite, a dark green or black mineral of the pyroxene group
containing or relating to augite mineral

Examples

The rock sample shows a distinctly augitic texture under the microscope.
Geologists identified augitic inclusions within the basalt formation.
The augitic content of the specimen was higher than in surrounding rocks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: augite - a dark green or black mineral of the pyroxene group

Etymology

From augite (mineral name), with the suffix -ic forming adjectives.
