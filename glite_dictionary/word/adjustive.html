adjustive

1. ADJ

/ əˈdʒʌstɪv / (AmE), / əˈdʒʌstɪv / (BrE)
Helping or tending to bring about adjustment or adaptation.
helping adjustment or adaptation

Examples

The program incorporates an adjustive approach to help students transition smoothly.
Therapists often use adjustive techniques to assist patients during big life changes.
An adjustive environment can reduce stress for employees facing organizational changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adaptive - having a capacity for adaptation; able to adjust to new conditions

Etymology

Formed from 'adjust' + '-ive', denoting a quality or tendency.
