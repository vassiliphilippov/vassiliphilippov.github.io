husbandry

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌzbəndɹi / (AmE), / ˈhʌzbəndɹi / (BrE)
the cultivation of crops and the rearing of animals; farming and agriculture
farming; cultivation of land and animals

Examples

Good animal husbandry ensures healthier livestock and greater productivity on the farm.
He studied crop rotation and soil conservation as part of modern husbandry techniques.
With advances in technology, the methods of husbandry have become more efficient and sustainable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farming - the activity or business of growing crops and raising livestock
  • Synonym: agriculture - the science or practice of farming, including cultivation and livestock

Etymology

From Middle English *husbondrie*, from husband (the master of a house), originally denoting the management of a household.
