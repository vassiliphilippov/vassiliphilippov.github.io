San Mateo

1. PROPN

/ sæn məˈteɪ.oʊ / (AmE), / san məˈteɪ.əʊ / (BrE)
A city in California, located south of San Francisco, known for its residential areas and proximity to Silicon Valley.
city in California south of San Francisco

Examples

Many tech companies have their offices in San Mateo due to its location near Silicon Valley.
After moving to San Mateo, she found the climate and community very pleasant.
The train from San Francisco to San Mateo is a popular option for daily commuters.

Etymology

From Spanish, meaning 'Saint Matthew'.
