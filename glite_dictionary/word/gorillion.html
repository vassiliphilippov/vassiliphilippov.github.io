gorillion

1. NOUN

/ ɡəˈɹɪljən / (AmE), / ɡəˈɹɪljən / (BrE)
an extremely large, indefinite number; used humorously or hyperbolically to suggest a countless amount
humorously large, indefinite number
informal slang

Examples

The stadium can hold a gorillion people, or at least that's how it feels.
I've told you a gorillion times to clean your room!
There are a gorillion things I'd rather do than paperwork.

Synset Examples

The event attracted a gorillion fans from all over.
There were a gorillion stars visible that night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zillion - a very large but unspecified number (humorous)
  • Synonym: bazillion - an extremely large, indeterminate number (humorous)

Etymology

Formed by combining 'gorilla' (for humorous effect) with the suffix '-illion' as in 'million.'
