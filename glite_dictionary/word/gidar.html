Gidar

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡiːdɑr / (AmE), / ˈɡiːdɑː / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in northern Cameroon, particularly south of Lake Chad.
Chadic language spoken in Cameroon

Examples

The linguist spent several years studying the Gidar language and documenting its grammar.
Many people in the Mayo-Louti division speak Gidar as their first language.
A set of folk tales was recently translated from Gidar into French for a local educational project.
