Donbas

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɑːnbɑs / (AmE), / ˈdɒnbæs / (BrE)
an industrial region in eastern Ukraine, known for its coal mining and heavy industry
industrial region in eastern Ukraine

Examples

The ongoing conflict in Donbas has drawn international attention.
Many coal mines in the Donbas region have been operational for decades.
She grew up in Donbas and remembers the industrial landscape.

Etymology

From Russian Донбасс, a contraction of Донецкий бассейн (Donetskii basseyn, 'Donets Basin').
