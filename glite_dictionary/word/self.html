self

1. NOUN

/ sɛlf / (AmE), / sɛlf / (BrE)
a person's essential being that distinguishes them from others, especially regarded as the object of introspection or reflexive action
a person's own identity or being

Examples

Only by knowing your self can you truly understand others.
Her sense of self was shaken after the sudden job loss.
Meditation helped him become more aware of his own self.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: identity - the fact of being who or what a person or thing is

Etymology

From Old English 'self', from Proto-Germanic '*selbaz*' (self, same).
