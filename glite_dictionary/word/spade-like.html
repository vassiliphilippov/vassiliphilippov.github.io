spade-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈspeɪdˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈspeɪdˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a shape that resembles a spade, especially in outline or form.
resembling a spade in shape

Examples

The leaf has a distinct spade-like appearance, broad at the base and tapering to a point.
Her dog has a spade-like nose that always sniffs out treats.
The architect designed a spade-like window for the new building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spade-shaped - having the shape of a spade
