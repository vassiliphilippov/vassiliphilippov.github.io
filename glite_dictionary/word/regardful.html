regardful

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈɡɑrd.fəl / (AmE), / rɪˈɡɑːd.fəl / (BrE)
showing respect or consideration for others; attentive to the feelings or wishes of others
showing respect or consideration

Examples

She is always regardful of her colleagues' opinions during meetings.
The teacher was regardful of the students' diverse backgrounds.
Parents should be regardful when addressing their children's concerns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respectful - showing respect or deference
  • Antonym: disrespectful - showing a lack of respect or courtesy

Etymology

From 'regard' + '-ful', meaning full of regard.
