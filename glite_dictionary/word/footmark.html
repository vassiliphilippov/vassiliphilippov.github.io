footmark

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌmɑːk / (BrE)
A mark left by a foot or shoe on a surface.
mark left by a foot or shoe

Examples

There was a muddy footmark on the clean kitchen floor.
The detective noticed a single footmark near the broken window.
Rain had started to wash away the footmarks left behind by the intruder.

Synset Examples

the police made casts of the footprints in the soft earth outside the window

Related Terms

  • Synonym: footprint - an impression or mark left by a foot on a surface
