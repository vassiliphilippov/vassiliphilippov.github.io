Vincent van Gogh

1. PROPN

/ ˈvɪnsənt væn ˈɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈvɪnsənt van ˈɡɒx / (BrE)
(1853–1890) A Dutch Post-Impressionist painter renowned for his innovative use of color and expressive brushwork, and one of the most influential figures in Western art.
Dutch Post-Impressionist painter (1853–1890)

Examples

Vincent van Gogh is best known for iconic works such as 'Starry Night' and 'Sunflowers'.
Many art historians consider Vincent van Gogh one of the most influential painters of the 19th century.
The intense colors and swirling patterns are hallmarks of Vincent van Gogh's artistic style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Claude Monet - French painter, a founder of Impressionism
  • Synonym: Pablo Picasso - Spanish painter and sculptor, influential modern artist
