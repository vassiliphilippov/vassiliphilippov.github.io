climbable

1. ADJ

/ ˈklaɪməbəl / (AmE), / ˈklaɪməbəl / (BrE)
able to be climbed; suitable or possible for climbing
able to be climbed

Examples

The wall is easily climbable for an experienced mountaineer.
They searched for a climbable tree to get a better view of the campsite.
Many playgrounds feature climbable structures for children to enjoy.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unclimbable - not able to be climbed

Etymology

From 'climb' + '-able', forming an adjective meaning possible to climb.
