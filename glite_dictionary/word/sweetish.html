sweetish

1. ADJ

/ ˈswiːtɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈswiːtɪʃ / (BrE)
slightly or somewhat sweet in taste or smell; not strongly sweet
somewhat sweet

Examples

The sauce has a sweetish flavor that pairs well with spicy dishes.
This wine is sweetish, but not as sugary as a dessert wine.
He noticed a sweetish scent coming from the blooming flowers.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: sweet - having the taste characteristic of sugar or honey; very sugary
  • Degree Lower: bland - lacking strong flavor; not sweet
