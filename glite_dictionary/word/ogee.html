ogee

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊ.dʒi / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.dʒi / (BrE)
a molding with an S-shaped profile, having a convex curve above and a concave curve below
S-shaped molding profile

Examples

The cabinet doors were finished with a decorative ogee along the upper edges.
The architect specified an ornate ogee molding for the cornice of the building.
He skillfully carved an ogee into the wooden frame to match the original design.

Etymology

From French 'ogive', meaning a diagonal arch or rib; influenced by architectural terminology.
