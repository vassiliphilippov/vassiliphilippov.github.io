shareholding

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɛrˌhoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɛəˌhəʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
The ownership of shares in a company or corporation.
ownership of company shares

Examples

Her family's shareholding in the company has increased significantly over the years.
The investor sold his shareholding when the stock price hit a record high.
Foreign shareholding in local firms is steadily rising due to market reforms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stockholding - ownership of stock in a corporation
