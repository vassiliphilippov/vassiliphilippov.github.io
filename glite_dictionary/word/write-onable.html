write-onable

1. ADJ

/ ˈraɪt ɒnəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈraɪt ɒnəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of a surface or material) able to be written on, especially using a pen or marker.
able to be written on

Examples

These folders are write-onable, making them ideal for organizing paperwork.
The new tape is write-onable, so you can label it easily with a marker.
I always buy write-onable labels for my storage boxes.

Synset Examples

These labels are write-onable for easy identification.
The folders are write-onable, perfect for labeling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: writable - able to be written on or in; capable of having written marks made on it

Etymology

From the verb 'write on' plus the suffix '-able', meaning 'able to be'.
