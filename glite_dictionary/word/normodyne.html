Normodyne

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrməˌdaɪn / (AmE), / ˈnɔːməˌdaɪn / (BrE)
A trade name for labetalol, an antihypertensive drug that blocks both alpha and beta-adrenergic receptors to lower blood pressure.
trade name for an antihypertensive drug (labetalol)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Normodyne to help manage his high blood pressure.
Patients taking Normodyne should have their blood pressure monitored regularly.
She experienced dizziness as a side effect of Normodyne.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Trandate - another trade name for labetalol
  • Synonym: labetalol - the generic name of the drug Normodyne and Trandate
