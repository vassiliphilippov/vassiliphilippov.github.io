unexceptional

1. ADJ

/ ʌnɪkˈsɛpʃənəl / (AmE), / ʌnɪkˈsɛpʃənəl / (BrE)
Not special, unusual, or remarkable in any way; ordinary or typical.
not special or remarkable

Examples

The meal was perfectly adequate but rather unexceptional compared to their usual standards.
His latest novel was interesting, but ultimately unexceptional and predictable.
Despite her impressive résumé, her interview performance was surprisingly unexceptional.

Synset Examples

run-of-the-mill boxing
your run-of-the-mine college graduate
as unexceptional an incident as can be found in a lawyer's career

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ordinary - with no special or distinctive features; normal
  • Antonym: exceptional - unusually good; outstanding

Etymology

From un- (not) + exceptional.
