alert

1. NOUN

/ əˈlɝt / (AmE), / əˈlɜːt / (BrE)
an automatic signal, often a sound, that warns of danger or requires immediate attention
automatic warning signal

Examples

The fire alert sounded throughout the building, prompting everyone to evacuate.
A weather alert was issued for severe thunderstorms in the area.
She set an alert on her phone to warn her of high pollen levels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alarm - a device or signal that warns of danger

Etymology

From Italian all'erta (on the watch), from all' (to the) + erta (a lookout point).

Synset POS: n
