unluckily

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈlʌkɪli / (AmE), / ʌnˈlʌkɪli / (BrE)
By bad luck; in an unfortunate or unfavorable manner.
by bad luck; unfortunately

Examples

Unluckily, we missed the last train by just five minutes.
He had prepared well for the test, but unluckily fell ill on the exam day.
The weather, unluckily, turned bad just as the picnic began.

Synset Examples

unfortunately it rained all day
alas, I cannot stay

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fortunately - by good luck; in a fortunate manner
  • Synonym: unfortunately - unluckily; by bad luck

Etymology

Formed from 'unlucky' with the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
