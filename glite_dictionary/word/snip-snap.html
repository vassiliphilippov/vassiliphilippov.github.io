snip-snap

1. NOUN

/ snɪp snæp / (AmE), / snɪp snæp / (BrE)
quick and lively exchange of remarks or conversation
quick, lively conversation

Examples

The snip-snap between the hosts kept the audience entertained throughout the show.
She enjoyed the snip-snap of lunchtime conversations with her colleagues.
Their snip-snap made the whole dinner party feel more lively and enjoyable.

Synset Examples

The snip-snap of their conversation made it lively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banter - playful and friendly exchange of teasing remarks
