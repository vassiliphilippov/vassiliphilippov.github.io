to concertize

1. VERB

/ ˈkɑːnsərˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsətaɪz / (BrE)
to give concerts, especially as a professional musician; to perform in concerts
perform in concerts

Examples

Next year, the pianist will concertize throughout Asia and Europe.
She spent several years concertizing before turning to teaching.
Many classical musicians aspire to concertize internationally.

Synset Examples

My niece is off concertizing in Europe

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to perform - to present a musical, dramatic, or other entertainment before an audience

Etymology

Formed from 'concert' + '-ize', following standard English formation patterns for verbs.
