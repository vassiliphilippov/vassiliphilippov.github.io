industrialisation

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˌdʌstriəlɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˌdʌstriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
The process of developing industries in a country or region on a large scale.
development of large-scale industry
british

Examples

The rapid industrialisation of the country transformed the lives of millions.
Government policies played a vital role in fostering industrialisation throughout the nineteenth century.
Environmental challenges often accompany rapid industrialisation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: industrialization - development of industry on a large scale (US spelling)

Etymology

From industry + -al + -isation (suffix forming nouns), modelled on French 'industrialisation'.
