purely

1. ADV

/ ˈpjʊrli / (AmE), / ˈpjʊəli / (BrE)
only; limited to and involving nothing else.
only; exclusively

Examples

Her decision was based purely on logic, not emotion.
I'm asking purely out of curiosity, not for any other reason.
The movie is meant to entertain and is purely fictional.

Synset Examples

we talked strictly business

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strictly - only; entirely limited to
  • Antonym: partly - to some extent, but not completely

Etymology

From pure + -ly; 'pure' from Latin 'purus' (clean, unadulterated).
