mixed-up

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɪkstˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌmɪkstˈʌp / (BrE)
perplexed or confused, especially due to conflicting situations or information.
confused; bewildered

Examples

She was feeling especially mixed-up after hearing so many different opinions.
He's just a mixed-up teenager trying to figure out his place in the world.
The instructions were so unclear that everyone ended up mixed-up about what to do.

Synset Examples

obviously bemused by his questions
bewildered and confused
a cloudy and confounded philosopher
just a mixed-up kid
she felt lost on the first day of school

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confused - unable to think clearly or understand
  • Synonym: bewildered - extremely puzzled or confused
  • Antonym: clearheaded - able to think in a clear and sensible way
