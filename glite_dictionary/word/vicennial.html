vicennial

1. ADJ

/ vaɪˈsɛniəl / (AmE), / vɪˈsɛniəl / (BrE)
Occurring once every twenty years or lasting for twenty years.
happening every 20 years

Examples

The town celebrates a vicennial festival that brings together families from across the region.
The vicennial reunion of alumni is marked by speeches and special events.
After its vicennial review, the organization updated its long-term goals.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: biennial - occurring every two years
  • Degree Higher: centennial - occurring every one hundred years

Etymology

From Latin 'vicennium' (a period of twenty years), from 'viginti' (twenty) and 'annus' (year).
