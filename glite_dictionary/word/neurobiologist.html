neurobiologist

1. NOUN

/ nuːroʊbaɪˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / njʊərəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A scientist who specializes in the study of the nervous system and neurobiology.
a specialist in neurobiology
medical

Examples

After earning her doctorate, she became a leading neurobiologist at a major research institute.
The neurobiologist explained how neurons transmit signals in the brain.
Many neurobiologists work to find treatments for neurological disorders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biologist - a scientist specializing in biology
  • Synonym: neurobiology - the branch of biology that studies the nervous system

Etymology

From 'neuro-' (relating to nerves or the nervous system) + 'biologist' (specialist in biology).
