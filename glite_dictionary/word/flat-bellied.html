flat-bellied

1. ADJ

/ flæt ˈbɛlid / (AmE), / flæt ˈbɛlid / (BrE)
Having a stomach or abdomen that is not rounded or protruding.
having a flat stomach or abdomen

Examples

The athlete maintained a flat-bellied appearance throughout the competition.
She dreamed of becoming more flat-bellied after months of training.
The lizard is known for its distinctive flat-bellied body shape.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: round-bellied - having a rounded or protruding belly
