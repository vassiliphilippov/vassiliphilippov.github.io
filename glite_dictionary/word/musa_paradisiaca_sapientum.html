Musa paradisiaca sapientum

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːsə pəˌrædɪˈsaɪəkə sæˈpiːəntəm / (AmE), / ˈmjuːsə pəˌrædɪˈsaɪəkə sæˈpiːəntəm / (BrE)
A species of banana plant cultivated for its edible yellow fruit, commonly grown for commercial purposes.
species of banana plant

Examples

The commercial cultivation of Musa paradisiaca sapientum has expanded rapidly in tropical regions.
Many varieties of edible bananas are derived from Musa paradisiaca sapientum hybrids.
Scientists study Musa paradisiaca sapientum to improve fruit yield and disease resistance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banana - Edible fruit of various species in the genus Musa

Etymology

From Latin: genus name 'Musa', species 'paradisiaca' (of paradise) and 'sapientum' (of wise men).
