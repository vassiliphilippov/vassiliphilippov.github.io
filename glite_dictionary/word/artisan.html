artisan

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːr.tɪ.zən / (AmE), / ˌɑː.tɪˈzæn / (BrE)
a worker skilled in a particular craft, especially one that involves making things by hand
skilled manual worker or craftsperson

Examples

The bakery prides itself on its bread, which is made by a dedicated artisan.
Each piece of jewelry was crafted by a local artisan using traditional techniques.
As an artisan, she values quality and precision in every item she creates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: craftsman - a person skilled in a particular craft

Etymology

From Old French 'artisan', from Italian 'artigiano', ultimately from Latin 'artitus', past participle of 'artire' (to instruct in the arts).
