schtikl

1. NOUN

/ ʃtɪkl / (AmE), / ʃtɪkl / (BrE)
A small piece; a little bit or portion, especially of food.
small piece or portion (esp. food)

Examples

He asked for just a schtikl of cake after dinner.
There's a schtikl of cheese left in the fridge if you're hungry.
Let me have a schtikl of your sandwich; I just want a taste.

Synset Examples

have a shtikl cake

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: shtik - a piece; a portion
  • Synonym: piece - a part or portion of something

Etymology

From Yiddish שטיקל (shtikl), diminutive of שטיק (shtik), meaning 'piece.'
