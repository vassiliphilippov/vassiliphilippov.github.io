blatantly

1. ADV

/ ˈbleɪtəntli / (AmE), / ˈbleɪt(ə)ntli / (BrE)
In an open and unashamed manner; in a way that is very obvious and often offensive.
in an obvious or unashamed way

Examples

He blatantly ignored the instructions and did what he wanted.
The advertisement was blatantly misleading to customers.
She blatantly lied about her qualifications during the interview.

Synset Examples

they blatantly violated the laws

Related Terms

  • Synonym: openly - in a way that is not secret or hidden
  • Antonym: secretly - in a secret or hidden way
