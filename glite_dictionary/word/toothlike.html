toothlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈtuθˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtuːθˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the shape or appearance of a tooth
resembling a tooth

Examples

The fossil showed a toothlike projection along its jaw.
Some leaves have toothlike edges that help protect them from predators.
The sculpture’s toothlike shapes gave it a jagged appearance.
