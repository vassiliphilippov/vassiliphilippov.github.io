peopled

1. ADJ

/ ˈpiːpəld / (AmE), / ˈpiːpəld / (BrE)
(of a place) inhabited or occupied by people.
inhabited; occupied by people

Examples

The once-empty valley is now peopled with farmers and their families.
Many legends spoke of distant islands peopled by mysterious ancient tribes.
The novel is peopled with vividly drawn characters from all walks of life.

Synset Examples

sparsely peopled arctic regions

Related Terms

  • Antonym: uninhabited - (of a place) not lived in by people
  • Synonym: settled - (of a place) occupied by people who live there
