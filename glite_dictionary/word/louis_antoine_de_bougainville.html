Louis Antoine de Bougainville

1. PROPN

/ luːˈiː ˌænˈtwɑn də ˌbuːɡənˈviːl / (AmE), / lwi ˈɑ̃twan də buɡɛ̃vil / (BrE)
(1729–1811) A French explorer and admiral, noted for being the first Frenchman to circumnavigate the globe and for his contributions to scientific exploration.
French explorer and circumnavigator (1729–1811)

Examples

The botanist joined Louis Antoine de Bougainville on his historic voyage around the world.
Louis Antoine de Bougainville is renowned for his contributions to geographical and scientific discovery.
In 1766, Louis Antoine de Bougainville set out to circumnavigate the globe under the French flag.
