philatelist

1. NOUN

/ fɪˈlætəlɪst / (AmE), / fɪˈlætəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who collects and studies postage stamps
postage stamp collector

Examples

As a dedicated philatelist, she traveled to international exhibitions to find rare stamps.
The philatelist carefully added a valuable 19th-century stamp to his expansive collection.
Many philatelists meet regularly to exchange knowledge and trade collectible stamps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stamp collector - someone who collects postage stamps
  • Synonym: philately - the collection and study of postage stamps

Etymology

From French 'philatéliste', based on 'philatélie' (philately), itself from Greek 'phil-' (loving) and 'atelēs' (exempt from tax, referring to postage).
