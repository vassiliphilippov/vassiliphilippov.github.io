y

1. NOUN

/ waɪ / (AmE), / waɪ / (BrE)
the twenty-fifth letter of the modern English alphabet
25th letter of the English alphabet

Examples

The word 'yellow' begins with the letter y.
In mathematics, the variable y is commonly used to represent an unknown quantity.
She wrote her name, making sure to cross every 't' and dot every y.

Etymology

From Latin 'Y', derived from Greek 'upsilon', itself from Phoenician 'waw'.
