gifted

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɪftɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡɪftɪd / (BrE)
having exceptional natural talent or ability
having great natural talent

Examples

She is a gifted pianist who began playing concerts at the age of six.
The school has special programs for gifted children.
His gifted storytelling captivated everyone in the room.

Synset Examples

a gifted writer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talented - having a natural aptitude or skill for something
  • Antonym: ungifted - lacking special talent or ability

Etymology

From 'gift' (talent, natural endowment) + '-ed', indicating possessing a gift or talent.
