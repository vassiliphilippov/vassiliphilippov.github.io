viscometric

1. ADJ

/ ˌvɪskoʊˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌvɪskəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving the measurement of viscosity
relating to viscosity measurement

Examples

The viscometric analysis of the polymer sample revealed useful information about its molecular weight.
During the experiment, a viscometric method was used to determine the solution’s flow characteristics.
She wrote her thesis on the viscometric determination of protein sizes in biological samples.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: viscosity - the quality or state of being viscous; a fluid's resistance to flow

Etymology

From 'viscosity' + '-metric' (measuring).
