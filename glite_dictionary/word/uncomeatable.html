uncomeatable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.kʌmˈæt.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kʌmˈæt.ə.bəl / (BrE)
(archaic) Difficult to reach or attain; not easily accessible or obtainable.
difficult to reach or attain (archaic)
archaic

Examples

The old castle stood on an uncomeatable cliff, challenging even the bravest explorers.
He found the truth about his ancestry frustratingly uncomeatable, buried deep in forgotten records.
In her loneliness, happiness seemed almost uncomeatable to her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inaccessible - not able to be reached or entered
  • Synonym: unattainable - unable to be achieved or obtained

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'come at' (to approach or reach) + '-able' (capable of).
