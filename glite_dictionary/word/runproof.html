runproof

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌnˌpruːf / (AmE), / ˈrʌnˌpruːf / (BrE)
(of fabric, especially hosiery) resistant to runs or ladders forming in the material.
resistant to runs in fabric/hosiery

Examples

These runproof tights are perfect for everyday wear and last much longer than regular ones.
She preferred runproof stockings to avoid embarrassing snags during her presentation.
The company developed a new runproof material for high-performance sportswear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ladder-proof - (of hosiery) resistant to ladders (British term for runs)

Etymology

Formed from 'run' (a tear in fabric) + 'proof' (resistant to).
