cycloidal

1. ADJ

/ saɪˈklɔɪ.dəl / (AmE), / saɪˈklɔɪ.dəl / (BrE)
Having the shape or characteristics of a cycloid; resembling a circle or circular arc.
resembling a cycloid or circle

Examples

The gears moved in a cycloidal path, ensuring smooth mechanical motion.
The architect incorporated cycloidal arches into the building's facade.
This crystal has a distinct cycloidal shape, similar to a perfect loop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circular - Having the form of a circle

Etymology

From cycloid + -al, ultimately from Greek kyklos (circle, wheel).
