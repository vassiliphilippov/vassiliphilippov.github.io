Walbiri

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑːlˌbiːri / (AmE), / ˈwɔːlˌbɪəri / (BrE)
an Australian Aboriginal language spoken in the Northern Territory of Australia
Australian Aboriginal language

Examples

Several linguists have studied the Walbiri language to document its complex grammar.
Many children in remote communities grow up speaking Walbiri as their first language.
The preservation of Walbiri is important for maintaining Indigenous cultural heritage.

Etymology

From the endonym used by speakers of the language; also spelled Warlpiri.
