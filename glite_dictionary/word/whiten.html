to whiten

1. VERB

/ ˈwaɪtən / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtən / (BrE)
to make or become white or whiter in color
make or become white

Examples

This toothpaste can whiten your teeth if used regularly.
The snow began to whiten the rooftops as it fell.
Fear caused his face to whiten in an instant.
