airheaded

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛrˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈeəˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
(of a person) silly, frivolous, or lacking seriousness or intelligence.
silly or frivolous (of a person)
informal

Examples

She can be a bit airheaded at times, but she means well.
His airheaded remarks made everyone laugh, even though they weren't very helpful.
Don't be so airheaded—this is an important meeting.

Synset Examples

a dizzy blonde
light-headed teenagers
silly giggles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scatterbrained - having a disorganized or forgetful mind
  • Antonym: serious - showing deep thought or care; not playful or silly

Etymology

Formed from 'airhead' (a person perceived as foolish or empty-headed) + '-ed'.
