brokenheartedness

1. NOUN

/ ˌbroʊ.kənˈhɑr.tɪd.nəs / (AmE), / ˌbrəʊ.kənˈhɑː.tɪd.nəs / (BrE)
intense sorrow or grief caused by the loss of a loved one, especially through death or separation
intense sorrow from loss of a loved one

Examples

Her brokenheartedness was evident after her husband’s sudden passing.
Few understand the brokenheartedness that comes from losing a lifelong friend.
He tried to hide his brokenheartedness, but his eyes betrayed his pain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grief - deep sorrow, especially caused by someone's death

Etymology

From 'brokenhearted' (deeply sad) + '-ness' (noun suffix).
