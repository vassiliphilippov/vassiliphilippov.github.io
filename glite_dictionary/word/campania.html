Campania

1. PROPN

/ kæmˈpeɪniə / (AmE), / kamˈpaːnɪə / (BrE)
a region of southwestern Italy on the Tyrrhenian Sea, including the islands of Capri and Ischia
region of southwestern Italy

Examples

Many tourists visit Campania each year for its beautiful coastline and historical sites.
The cuisine of Campania is famous for dishes like pizza and fresh seafood.
Naples is the capital city of the Italian region of Campania.

Etymology

From Latin 'Campania', an ancient name for the region.
