folderal

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑldərˌæl / (AmE), / ˈfɒldərˌæl / (BrE)
ornamental or trivial objects of little or no value; trifles
trivial or ornamental objects
archaic

Examples

The antique shop was filled with folderal that caught the eye but served no purpose.
She dusted the shelves crowded with folderal collected over years of travel.
He dismissed the decorations as mere folderal, preferring a minimalist style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: falderal - trivial or nonsensical things
  • Synonym: bauble - a small, decorative, and inexpensive trinket

Etymology

Perhaps a variant of 'falderal', a refrain in old songs, later meaning nonsense or trifles.
