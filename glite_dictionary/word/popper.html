popper

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑpər / (AmE), / ˈpɒpə / (BrE)
a container or device used for cooking popcorn, often on a stove or in a microwave
container for cooking popcorn

Examples

He bought a new popper to make movie-night snacks at home.
This microwave popper makes popcorn quickly and with less mess.
The old-fashioned stove-top popper gives popcorn a special flavor.

2. NOUN

/ ˈpɑpər / (AmE), / ˈpɒpə / (BrE)
a small container of amyl nitrate or butyl nitrite, used as a recreational stimulant and inhaled for its euphoric effects
container for inhalant stimulant drug
slang

Examples

Some club-goers use popper to enhance their experience on the dance floor.
He bought a popper from the pharmacy before the party.
The effects of inhaling a popper are usually short-lived but intense.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amyl nitrate - the chemical compound used as a stimulant inhalant
  • Synonym: butyl nitrite - another compound used similarly as an inhalant 'popper'
