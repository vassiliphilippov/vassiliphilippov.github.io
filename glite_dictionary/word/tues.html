Tues

1. NOUN

/ tuz / (AmE), / tjuÀêz / (BrE)
Abbreviation for Tuesday, the third day of the week; the second working day in many countries.
abbreviation for Tuesday

Examples

Our team meeting is scheduled for Tues at 10 a.m.
I won't be available this Tues due to a doctor's appointment.
Please return the report by Tues afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tuesday - the third day of the week

Etymology

Abbreviation of "Tuesday."
