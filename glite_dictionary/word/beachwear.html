beachwear

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːtʃˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃˌwɛə / (BrE)
clothing designed to be worn at the beach, such as swimsuits, cover-ups, and sandals
clothing for the beach

Examples

She packed her favorite beachwear for their tropical vacation.
Stores display a wide selection of beachwear every summer.
His colorful beachwear made him easy to spot by the water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimwear - clothing designed for swimming
