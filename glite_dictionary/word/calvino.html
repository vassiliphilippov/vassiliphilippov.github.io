Calvino

1. PROPN

/ kalˈviːnoʊ / (AmE), / kalˈviːnəʊ / (BrE)
Italo Calvino (1923–1987), an Italian novelist and short story writer born in Cuba, known for works blending fantasy, allegory, and realism.
Italian novelist and short story writer

Examples

Many readers consider Calvino one of the most innovative writers of the twentieth century.
I recently finished reading Calvino's 'Invisible Cities', and it was fascinating.
