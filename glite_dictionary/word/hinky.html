hinky

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈhɪŋki / (BrE)
suspicious or odd in a way that suggests something is not quite right or may be untrustworthy.
suspicious or odd; not quite right
informal

Examples

His story seemed hinky from the very beginning, so nobody believed him.
There's something hinky about this deal—let's double-check the documents.
If you notice anything hinky while reviewing these accounts, let me know immediately.

Synset Examples

His behavior seemed a bit hinky during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suspicious - having or showing a cautious distrust of someone or something
  • Synonym: strange - unusual or surprising in a way that is unsettling
  • Antonym: trustworthy - able to be relied on as honest or truthful
