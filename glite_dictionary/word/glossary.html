glossary

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlɑːs(ə)ri / (AmE), / ˈɡlɒs(ə)ri / (BrE)
An alphabetical list of terms relating to a particular subject, often with explanations, typically found at the end of a book or article.
list of specialized terms with definitions

Examples

At the end of the textbook, there is a helpful glossary of scientific terms.
If you are unfamiliar with the jargon, refer to the glossary for definitions.
The glossary explains all the technical words used in the report.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dictionary - a reference book or online resource containing an alphabetical list of words with definitions

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'glossarium', from 'glossa' (obsolete or foreign word needing explanation), from Greek 'glōssa' (tongue, language).
