9-membered

1. ADJ

/ ˈnaɪn ˌmɛmbɚd / (AmE), / ˈnaɪn ˌmɛmbəd / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) having a ring structure consisting of nine atoms or members.
having a nine-membered ring (chemistry)

Examples

The chemist synthesized a 9-membered ring compound for the experiment.
Many antibiotics feature a 9-membered lactone ring as a critical part of their structure.
Nona-azacyclononane is an example of a 9-membered ring in organic chemistry.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 8-membered - (of a chemical compound) having a ring structure with eight members
  • Degree Higher: 10-membered - (of a chemical compound) having a ring structure with ten members
