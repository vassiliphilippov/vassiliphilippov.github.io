clangorous

1. ADJ

/ ˈklæŋərəs / (AmE), / ˈklæŋərəs / (BrE)
having a loud, resonant, metallic sound; making a clanging noise
having a loud, metallic sound

Examples

The clangorous alarm echoed through the empty warehouse late at night.
Their arguments grew clangorous, filling the house with loud, metallic voices.
A clangorous crash reverberated as the steel beams collided on the construction site.

Synset Examples

the clangorous locomotive works
a clanging gong

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clanging - making a loud resonant metallic sound
  • Synonym: cacophonous - producing a harsh, discordant mixture of sounds

Etymology

From Latin 'clangor' (a ringing, clamorous sound) + English '-ous'.
