chromatographically

1. ADV

/ kroʊˌmæt̬əˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / krəˌmatəˈɡrafɪkli / (BrE)
By means of chromatography; using a chromatographic process.
by means of chromatography
medical

Examples

The compounds were separated chromatographically before analysis.
The drug purity was determined chromatographically using high-performance liquid chromatography.
Researchers often analyze complex mixtures chromatographically for greater accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chromatography - separation technique using a chromatographic process

Etymology

From 'chromatography' + '-ally', after the pattern of 'chromatographic'.
