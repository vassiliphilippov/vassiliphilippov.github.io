75th

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛv(ə)nˈti fɪfθ / (AmE), / ˌsɛv(ə)nˈtiː fɪfθ / (BrE)
Being number seventy-five in a sequence; coming after seventy-fourth and before seventy-sixth.
ordinal number for seventy-five

Examples

This year marks the 75th anniversary of the founding of the organization.
She finished in 75th place after a challenging marathon.
The company celebrated its 75th birthday with a special gala.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seventy-fifth - alternative spelling of the ordinal number 75th
  • Degree Lower: 74th - ordinal number for seventy-four
  • Degree Higher: 76th - ordinal number for seventy-six
