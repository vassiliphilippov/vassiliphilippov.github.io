Ulan Bator

1. PROPN

/ uːˌlɑn ˈbɑːtɔr / (AmE), / ˌuːlæn ˈbɑːtɔː / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Mongolia, situated in north-central Mongolia on the Tuul River
capital city of Mongolia

Examples

During his trip to Mongolia, he spent several days exploring Ulan Bator.
The climate of Ulan Bator is known to be extremely cold in winter.
Many international diplomats are stationed in Ulan Bator due to its political importance.

Etymology

From Mongolian 'Ulaanbaatar', meaning 'Red Hero'.
