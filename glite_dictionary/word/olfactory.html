olfactory

1. ADJ

/ ɑlˈfæktəri, oʊlˈfæk-/ / (AmE), / ɒlˈfæktəri / (BrE)
relating to the sense of smell.
relating to smell

Examples

Dogs have an exceptionally sensitive olfactory system, allowing them to detect even faint scents.
The olfactory nerve transmits information from the nose to the brain.
Scientists are studying olfactory receptors to understand how humans perceive different smells.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: gustatory - relating to the sense of taste
  • Synonym: nasal - relating to the nose

Etymology

From Latin olfactorius (relating to smelling), from olfacere (to smell).
