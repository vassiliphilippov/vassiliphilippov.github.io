balletomane

1. NOUN

/ ˌbælɪˈtoʊmeɪn / (AmE), / ˌbælɪˈtəʊmeɪn / (BrE)
a person who is an enthusiastic admirer or devotee of ballet
ballet enthusiast

Examples

As a devoted balletomane, she attends every new production in the city.
The balletomane was thrilled to meet her favorite dancer after the performance.
Many balletomanes collect rare programs and memorabilia from famous ballets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enthusiast - a person who is highly interested in a particular activity or subject

Etymology

From French 'balletomane', from 'ballet' (ballet) + '-mane' (mania).
