self-pleasing

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlfˈplizɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈpliːzɪŋ / (BrE)
Concerned primarily with satisfying one's own desires or needs, often at the expense of other considerations.
gratifying one's own desires

Examples

Her self-pleasing attitude sometimes made her unpopular with her colleagues.
A self-pleasing approach can hinder teamwork and cooperation.
He acknowledged that his self-pleasing decisions were not always in the best interest of the group.

Synset Examples

Self-pleasing can sometimes distract from important tasks.
His self-pleasing habits often led to procrastination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-indulgent - allowing oneself excessive gratification of one's own desires
  • Antonym: altruistic - showing selfless concern for the well-being of others

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'pleasing', following common English compounding patterns.
