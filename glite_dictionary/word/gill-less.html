gill-less

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɪl ləs / (AmE), / ˈɡɪl ləs / (BrE)
(of an animal) not having gills.
lacking gills

Examples

Unlike most fish, this rare species is gill-less and breathes through its skin.
Scientists studied the gill-less larvae to understand their development better.
The gill-less condition limits the habitat options for these aquatic creatures.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: gilled - having gills
