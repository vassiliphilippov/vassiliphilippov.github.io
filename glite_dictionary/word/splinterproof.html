splinterproof

1. ADJ

/ ˈsplɪntɚˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈsplɪntəˌpruːf / (BrE)
Designed to resist splintering or breaking into sharp fragments.
resistant to splintering

Examples

Cyclists wore splinterproof goggles to protect their eyes from debris on the track.
The laboratory installed splinterproof glass in all its doors and windows.
Their new riot shields are made from splinterproof polycarbonate for added safety.

Synset Examples

shatterproof automobile windows

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shatterproof - resistant to shattering or breaking

Etymology

From 'splinter' + 'proof', patterned after compounds like 'shatterproof'.
