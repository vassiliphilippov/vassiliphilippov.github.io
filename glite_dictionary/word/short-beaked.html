short-beaked

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɔrt ˌbikt / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt ˌbiːkt / (BrE)
(of an animal, especially a bird) having a short beak.
having a short beak

Examples

The short-beaked echidna is native to Australia and New Guinea.
Researchers observed that short-beaked finches adapted differently than their long-beaked counterparts.
This sanctuary is home to a rare short-beaked goose species.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: long-beaked - having a long beak
