Europeanisation

1. NOUN

/ jʊˌroʊpiənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / jʊəˌrəʊpiənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of adopting European culture, ideas, or practices, or assimilating into European society
adoption of European culture or ideas

Examples

The country's rapid Europeanisation affected its laws and social customs.
Debates about the Europeanisation of education have increased in recent years.
Some critics argue that Europeanisation led to the loss of traditional values.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Europeanization - the US spelling of the same concept

Etymology

From 'European' + '-isation', following the pattern of 'to Europeanise'.
