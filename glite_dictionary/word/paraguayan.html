Paraguayan

1. ADJ

/ ˌpærəˈɡwaɪən / (AmE), / ˌparəˈɡwaɪən / (BrE)
relating to Paraguay or its people or culture
of or relating to Paraguay

Examples

The Paraguayan team performed exceptionally well in the tournament.
She enjoys Paraguayan cuisine, especially the traditional soups and stews.
We visited a Paraguayan art exhibition during our trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Paraguay - a landlocked country in South America

Etymology

From Paraguay + -an, denoting origin or relation.

2. NOUN

/ ˌpærəˈɡwaɪən / (AmE), / ˌparəˈɡwʌɪən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Paraguay
person from Paraguay

Examples

The museum hired a Paraguayan to curate its South American collection.
Several Paraguayans attended the international student exchange program this year.
He became friends with a Paraguayan while traveling through South America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Paraguayan - of or relating to Paraguay, or its people or culture (adjective)
  • Synonym: Paraguay - a country in central South America

Etymology

From Paraguay + -an, denoting people or things from Paraguay.
