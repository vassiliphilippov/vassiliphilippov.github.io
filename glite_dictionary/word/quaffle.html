quaffle

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɑːfəl / (AmE), / ˈkwɒfəl / (BrE)
a large, red ball used in the fictional game of Quidditch in the Harry Potter series, used by Chasers to score points by throwing it through goal hoops
large ball used in Quidditch (Harry Potter)

Examples

In the match, Ginny Weasley quickly grabbed the quaffle and sped toward the goal hoops.
Players must avoid the Bludgers while keeping control of the quaffle during play.
Hermione explained the rules of Quidditch, pointing out the role of the quaffle to the new students.
