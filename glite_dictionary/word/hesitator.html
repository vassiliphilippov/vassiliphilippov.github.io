hesitator

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛzɪˌteɪtər / (AmE), / ˈhɛzɪˌteɪtə / (BrE)
a person who hesitates, especially out of fear or uncertainty
one who hesitates

Examples

As a natural hesitator, she often missed out on opportunities.
The hesitator in the group slowed down the decision-making process.
Even a hesitator can gain confidence with enough practice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hesitater - a person who hesitates
  • Antonym: decider - a person who makes decisions promptly

Etymology

From 'hesitate' (verb) + '-or' (agent suffix).
