Tiflis

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɪflɪs / (AmE), / ˈtɪflɪs / (BrE)
the former name for Tbilisi, the capital and largest city of Georgia, located on the Kura river
former name for Tbilisi, Georgia

Examples

He traveled through Tiflis on his journey to the Caucasus.
The history of Tiflis reveals its importance on trade routes between Europe and Asia.
Many 19th-century writers referred to Georgia's capital as Tiflis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tbilisi - the capital of Georgia, formerly known as Tiflis

Etymology

From Georgian 'T’pilisi', meaning 'warm place'.
