station

/ ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
the frequency assigned to a radio or television broadcasting station on which it transmits its signal

Examples

Each station operates on its own unique frequency.

/ ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
(Nautical) The location to which a ship or fleet is assigned for duty.
assigned duty location (nautical)

Examples

Each vessel must report to its assigned station before sunrise.
During wartime, the admiral changed the station of several ships for strategic reasons.

Related Terms
