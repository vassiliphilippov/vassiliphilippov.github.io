worthlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈwɝːθlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈwɜːθləsli / (BrE)
In a manner that lacks value, usefulness, or merit.
in a worthless manner

Examples

He threw the broken tool worthlessly onto the pile of scrap.
She realized the old clothes would worthlessly occupy space in her closet.
The contract was written so poorly that it was almost worthlessly vague.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worthless - having no value or usefulness

Etymology

Formed from 'worthless' + '-ly', where 'worthless' comes from 'worth' + the suffix '-less'.
