transatlantic

1. ADJ

/ ˌtrænz.ətˈlæn.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌtrænz.ətˈlæn.tɪk / (BrE)
crossing or relating to crossing the Atlantic Ocean, especially between Europe and America
crossing the Atlantic Ocean

Examples

She took a transatlantic flight from New York to London.
The company has strong transatlantic business ties with several European partners.
A new transatlantic cable was laid to improve communications between the continents.

Synset Examples

transatlantic flight

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transpacific - crossing the Pacific Ocean

Etymology

From Latin 'trans' (across) + 'Atlantic' (the Atlantic Ocean).
