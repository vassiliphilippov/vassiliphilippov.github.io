anymore

1. ADV

/ ˌɛniˈmɔr / (AmE), / ˌeniˈmɔː / (BrE)
(usually in negatives) any longer; at the present time or from now on.
no longer; any longer (esp. with negatives)

Examples

I don't go to that gym anymore since I moved to a new city.
She doesn't eat meat anymore.
He isn't working here anymore, so you should call his replacement.

Synset Examples

Alice doesn't live here anymore
the children promised not to quarrel any more

Related Terms

  • Synonym: any longer - no more; at the present time or from now on (with negatives)
