asstard

1. NOUN

/ ˈæs.tɑrd / (AmE), / ˈæs.tɑːd / (BrE)
A highly offensive and vulgar slang term for a person acting extremely foolishly or obnoxiously.
slang insult for a very foolish person
slang vulgar offensive

Examples

If you keep acting like a complete asstard, people will stop taking you seriously.
She called him a complete asstard after he ruined the presentation.
Online gamers sometimes insult each other by using terms like complete asstard.

Synset Examples

He's acting like a complete asstard today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently
  • Antonym: genius - a person with extraordinary intellectual power

Etymology

Formed from 'ass' (offensive slang for a foolish person) and 'tard' (from 'retard', considered highly offensive). Use of '-tard' is widely condemned as ableist and derogatory.
