watchband

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑtʃˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃˌbænd / (BrE)
A band of cloth, leather, or metal links that is attached to a wristwatch and worn around the wrist.
strap or band for a wristwatch

Examples

He replaced the broken watchband with a new leather one.
Her gold watchband matched the jewelry she was wearing.
The watchband was too tight, so I adjusted it for comfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strap - a strip of material used for fastening or holding things together
