Nephthytis afzelii

1. NOUN

/ nɛfˈθaɪtɪs æfˈzɛliː / (AmE), / nɛfˈθɪtɪs æfˈzɛliː / (BrE)
A tropical plant species with large arrow-shaped leaves, cultivated as an ornamental.
tropical ornamental plant with arrow-shaped leaves

Examples

The lush leaves of Nephthytis afzelii make it a popular choice for indoor decoration.
Gardeners often appreciate Nephthytis afzelii for its striking foliage and easy care.
You can spot Nephthytis afzelii in many botanical gardens across the tropics.

Etymology

From the genus name Nephthytis and the specific epithet honoring Swedish botanist Adam Afzelius.
