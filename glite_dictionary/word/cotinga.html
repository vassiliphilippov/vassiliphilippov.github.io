cotinga

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈtɪŋɡə / (AmE), / kəˈtɪŋɡə / (BrE)
A brightly colored passerine bird found in the tropical forests of Central and South America, known for its vibrant plumage.
a tropical bird with bright plumage

Examples

Several species of cotinga are admired for their vivid blue feathers.
During our trip to the Amazon, we spotted a rare cotinga in the canopy.
Birdwatchers visit Ecuador to catch a glimpse of the beautiful cotingas that inhabit the region.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'cotinga,' probably from a Tupi word meaning 'brightly colored bird.'
