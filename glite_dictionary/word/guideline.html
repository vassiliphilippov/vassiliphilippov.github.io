guideline

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡaɪdˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪdˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a faint line drawn as a reference to help align or position letters in handwriting, drawing, or printing
reference line for aligning letters

Examples

Please draw a guideline before you begin writing to keep your letters straight.
The artist sketched a faint guideline across the canvas to position the text.
You can erase the guideline after finishing the calligraphy for a clean look.

2. NOUN

/ ˈɡaɪdˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪdˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a general rule, principle, or piece of advice that helps you decide what to do in a particular situation
general rule or advice

Examples

New safety guidelines were issued for staff working in the laboratory.
The doctor followed strict guidelines when prescribing medication to patients.
We have to adhere to the company's guidelines when making financial decisions.

Synset Examples

the president said he had a road map for normalizing relations with Vietnam

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rule - a statement that tells you what is or is not allowed in a particular situation
  • Synonym: recommendation - a suggestion or proposal as the best course of action
  • Synonym: instruction - detailed information telling how something should be done

Etymology

From 'guide' + 'line', modeled after 'guide line' (a rope used to guide).

3. NOUN

/ ˈɡaɪdˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪd.laɪn / (BrE)
a rule or principle that gives advice or direction about how something should be done
advisory rule or principle

Examples

The company issued new guidelines to ensure workplace safety.
Teachers provide guidelines to help students understand the assignment requirements.
These guidelines are not strict rules, but they can help you make better choices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rule - a principle or regulation governing conduct, action, procedure, arrangement, etc.
  • Synonym: recommendation - an advised course of action

Etymology

Formed from 'guide' + 'line', originally meaning a line that guides the course of a ship, later generalized to rules or instructions.
