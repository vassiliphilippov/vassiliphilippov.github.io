intensified

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈtɛn.sɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛn.sɪˌfaɪd / (BrE)
made more intense; increased in strength, degree, or force.
made more intense

Examples

Security around the building was intensified after several threats were received.
The intensified heat wave caused widespread discomfort throughout the region.
Thanks to intensified efforts, the community was able to clean up the park faster than expected.

Synset Examples

the intensified scrutiny of the candidate's background

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intense - having a high degree of force, strength, or energy
  • Antonym: diminished - made smaller or less

Etymology

From the verb 'intensify' + -ed.
