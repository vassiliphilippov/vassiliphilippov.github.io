Lombardia

1. PROPN

/ lɔmˈbɑrdiə / (AmE), / lɒmˈbɑːdɪə / (BrE)
a region in north-central Italy bordering Switzerland, known for its cultural, economic, and historical significance
region of north-central Italy

Examples

Many tourists visit Lombardia each year to explore its lakes and historic cities.
Milan, the capital of Lombardia, is a global center for fashion and finance.
The food and wine of Lombardia reflect its rich agricultural tradition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lombardy - the English name for Lombardia, a region in Italy

Etymology

Italian name for the region; known as 'Lombardy' in English, from Medieval Latin 'Longobardia' (land of the Lombards).
