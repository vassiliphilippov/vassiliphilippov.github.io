INH

1. NOUN

/ aɪ ɛn eɪtʃ / (AmE), / aɪ ɛn eɪtʃ / (BrE)
an antibacterial drug, also known as isoniazid, used especially to treat tuberculosis
antibacterial drug for tuberculosis
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed INH to begin treatment for tuberculosis.
Some patients may experience side effects from taking INH for long periods.
Regular monitoring is necessary when a patient is on INH therapy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: isoniazid - antibacterial drug used to treat and prevent tuberculosis

Etymology

Abbreviation of isoniazid.
