waterleaf

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚˌlif / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəliːf / (BrE)
any of several North American plants of the genus Hydrophyllum, characterized by their lobed leaves and clusters of small, usually blue or white flowers
a plant of the genus Hydrophyllum

Examples

The shady woodland was carpeted with waterleaf in early spring.
Botanists discovered a rare variety of waterleaf near the riverbank.
Deer often graze on waterleaf and other native wildflowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hydrophyllum - genus of herbs including waterleaf
