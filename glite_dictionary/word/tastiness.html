tastiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪstinəs / (AmE), / ˈteɪstinɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being pleasing to the taste; deliciousness
pleasant taste; deliciousness

Examples

The tastiness of the homemade bread impressed all the guests.
Food critics often judge a dish by its presentation and tastiness.
She tries to balance nutrition with tastiness when cooking for her family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deliciousness - the quality of being delicious
  • Antonym: blandness - lack of strong flavor or taste

Etymology

From "tasty" + "-ness", modeled on "taste".
