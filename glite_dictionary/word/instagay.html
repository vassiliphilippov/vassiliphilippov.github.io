instagay

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnstəˌɡeɪ / (AmE), / ˈɪnstəˌɡeɪ / (BrE)
A gay man, especially one with a prominent presence or following on Instagram, often through sharing curated photos of himself and aspects of gay lifestyle.
a gay man with a popular Instagram presence
informal slang

Examples

He became an instagay after his vacation photos went viral on social media.
Many instagays share fitness routines and travel tips with their followers.
The party was filled with local instagays posing for photos and updating their feeds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: influencer - a person with the ability to influence others on social media
  • Synonym: Instagrammer - a person who uses Instagram, especially regularly or professionally

Etymology

Blend of 'Instagram' and 'gay', formed in 21st-century English.
