Volvocales

1. NOUN

/ vɑlvoʊˈkeɪliːz / (AmE), / vɒlvəʊˈkeɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of chiefly freshwater green algae that may occur as solitary cells or in colonies
order of freshwater green algae

Examples

The genus Volvox is a well-known member of the Volvocales.
Researchers study the Volvocales to understand the evolution of multicellularity.
Many species within the Volvocales can be found in freshwater ponds and lakes.

Etymology

From New Latin, based on genus Volvox (type genus of the order) and the suffix -ales (indicating order).
