to sensibilize

1. VERB

/ ˈsɛnsɪbəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsɪbɪlaɪz / (BrE)
to make someone or something sensitive or aware, especially to certain stimuli or issues
make sensitive or aware

Examples

The training program aims to sensibilize employees to the importance of workplace diversity.
Campaigns like these help sensibilize the public to the effects of climate change.
Exposure to various cultures can sensibilize students to different perspectives.

Synset Examples

He was not sensitized to her emotional needs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sensitize - make sensitive or aware, especially to physical or emotional stimuli

Etymology

From French sensibiliser, from Latin sensibilis meaning ‘sensitive’.
