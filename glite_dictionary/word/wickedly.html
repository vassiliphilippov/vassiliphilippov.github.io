wickedly

1. ADV

/ ˈwɪkɪdli / (AmE), / ˈwɪkɪdli / (BrE)
In an evil, morally wrong, or deliberately cruel manner.
in an evil or cruel manner

Examples

She smiled wickedly as she announced the surprise quiz to the class.
The villain laughed wickedly before revealing his sinister plan.
He wickedly sabotaged his opponent’s chances of winning the competition.

Synset Examples

act wickedly
grin evilly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evilly - in a manner that is morally wrong or evil
