Nairobi

1. PROPN

/ naɪˈroʊbi / (AmE), / naɪˈrəʊbi / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Kenya, known as a major center for travel and commerce in East Africa.
capital city of Kenya

Examples

Many tourists begin their safari adventures in Nairobi before heading into the national parks.
After moving to Nairobi, she quickly adapted to city life.
Nairobi has grown rapidly, becoming a hub for international business in Africa.

Etymology

From the Maasai phrase 'Enkare Nairobi' meaning 'cool water', referring to the Nairobi River.
