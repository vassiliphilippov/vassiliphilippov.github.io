teary

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɪri / (AmE), / ˈtɪəri / (BrE)
(of a person or their eyes) full of tears or likely to cry.
having eyes full of tears

Examples

The movie was so touching it left her teary for several minutes.
He gave his mother a long, teary hug before leaving home.
Her teary eyes revealed how deeply the news had affected her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tearful - showing or characterized by tears

Etymology

From 'tear' (a drop of clear salty liquid secreted by glands in your eyes) + '-y' (forming adjectives).
