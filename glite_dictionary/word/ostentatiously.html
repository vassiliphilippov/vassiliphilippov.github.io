ostentatiously

1. ADV

/ ˌɑː.stənˈteɪ.ʃəs.li / (AmE), / ˌɒs.tənˈteɪ.ʃəs.li / (BrE)
In a way that is intended to attract notice and impress others; showily or pretentiously.
in a showy or pretentious way

Examples

She ostentatiously displayed her new diamond necklace at the party.
He ostentatiously donated a large sum of money in front of the cameras.
The billionaire ostentatiously arrived at the event in a gold-plated limousine.

Synset Examples

Mr Khrushchev ostentatiously wooed and embraced Castro at the U.N. general assembly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: showily - in a showy manner
  • Antonym: modestly - in a modest or unassuming manner

Etymology

From 'ostentatious' + '-ly'; 'ostentatious' from Latin 'ostentatio' (display).
