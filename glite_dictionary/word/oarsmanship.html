oarsmanship

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrz.mənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈɔːz.mən.ʃɪp / (BrE)
skill or proficiency in rowing a boat with oars
skill in rowing with oars

Examples

His oarsmanship helped the team win the regatta.
She admired his graceful oarsmanship as he guided the boat down the river.
Years of practice had perfected his oarsmanship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rowing - the sport or activity of propelling a boat with oars

Etymology

From 'oarsman' + '-ship' (denoting skill or condition).
