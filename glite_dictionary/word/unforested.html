unforested

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfɔr.ɪ.stɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɒr.ɪ.stɪd / (BrE)
(of land or an area) not covered with forest; lacking trees or woodland.
not covered with forest

Examples

The researchers studied how wildlife returned to the unforested regions after the fire.
Much of the valley has remained unforested due to frequent grazing by livestock.
Economic activities in unforested areas differ significantly from those in densely wooded landscapes.

Synset Examples

unforested lands

Related Terms

  • Antonym: forested - covered with forest

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'forested' (covered with forest).
