hipline

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪpˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɪpˌlaɪn / (BrE)
the line formed around the body at the widest part of the hips, typically used for measurement in tailoring and fashion
measurement line around the widest part of the hips

Examples

The tailor took her hipline measurement to ensure the dress would fit perfectly.
Many fashion designers pay close attention to the natural hipline when creating evening gowns.
His hipline had increased slightly after a year of little exercise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waistline - line where the waist is narrowest, often measured for clothing

2. NOUN

/ ˈhɪp.laɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɪp.laɪn / (BrE)
the line formed by the lower edge of a hip-length garment, especially as it defines the contour of the hips
lower edge of hip-length garment

Examples

The hipline of the dress hugged her curves perfectly.
Designers often accentuate the hipline to create a flattering silhouette.
She added a belt at the hipline for a more defined shape.
