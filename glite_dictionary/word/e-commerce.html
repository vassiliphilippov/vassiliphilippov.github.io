e-commerce

1. NOUN

/ iˈkɑmɚs / (AmE), / ˈiːˌkɒmɜːs / (BrE)
The activity of buying and selling goods and services over the internet.
online buying and selling

Examples

Many businesses now rely on e-commerce to reach customers around the world.
The rise of e-commerce has transformed the retail industry.
Setting up a secure payment system is crucial for any e-commerce website.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: online shopping - shopping for products or services on the internet

Etymology

Blend of 'electronic' and 'commerce' (from Latin commercium).
