ribbonlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪb.ənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈrɪb.ənˌlaɪk / (BrE)
long and thin in shape; resembling a ribbon
having the shape of a ribbon

Examples

The pasta was cut into ribbonlike strips perfect for holding sauce.
A ribbonlike stream wound its way through the meadow.
The snake's ribbonlike body allowed it to slip easily between the rocks.

Synset Examples

ribbonlike noodles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ribbon - a long, narrow strip of fabric

Etymology

Formed from 'ribbon' + 'like', meaning 'like a ribbon'.
