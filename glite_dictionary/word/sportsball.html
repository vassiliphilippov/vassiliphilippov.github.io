sportsball

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrtsˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtsˌbɔːl / (BrE)
(informal, sometimes condescending) Any sport involving a ball, especially when referred to in a generic or dismissive way by someone uninterested or unfamiliar with such sports.
any ball sport (often dismissive)
informal slang

Examples

Whenever someone mentions the big game, I just say I'm not interested in sportsball.
My coworkers kept talking about sportsball events I don't follow.
She joked that her knowledge of sportsball ends with knowing the ball is usually round.

Etymology

Formed humorously from 'sports' + 'ball' as a mock-generic word for team sports with balls.
