splitting

1. ADJ

/ ˈsplɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsplɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a noise or sound) resembling a violent or tearing sound, as of something being ripped apart or split by force.
noise like something being torn or ripped

Examples

We heard a splitting crash as the old branch broke from the tree.
The sudden, splitting noise of the explosion echoed across the valley.
A splitting crack rang out as lightning struck the rooftop.

Synset Examples

the tree split with a great ripping sound
heard a rending roar as the crowd surged forward

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rending - (of a sound) violent or tearing; similar in effect to 'splitting'
  • Synonym: tearing - (of a sound) like something being torn; similar to 'splitting'

Etymology

Present participle of 'split', from Middle Dutch splitten (to split), related to terms for tearing or breaking.
