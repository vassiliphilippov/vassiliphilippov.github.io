armillary

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːrmɪˌlɛri / (AmE), / ɑːˈmɪl.əri / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling a bracelet or bracelets.
relating to bracelets

Examples

The ancient queen wore an armillary band on her wrist as a symbol of status.
He admired the intricate armillary designs used by goldsmiths in Renaissance jewelry.
Certain bangles are considered armillary ornaments in classical art discussions.

Etymology

From Latin 'armilla' (bracelet) + '-ary' (pertaining to).
