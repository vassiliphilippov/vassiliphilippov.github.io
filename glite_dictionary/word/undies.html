undies

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌn.diz / (AmE), / ˈʌn.diːz / (BrE)
Informal term for underwear, especially women's underwear such as panties, bras, or camisoles.
informal term for women's underwear
informal

Examples

She bought a new set of undies for her vacation.
There was a sale on undies at the department store last weekend.
It's embarrassing when your undies show above your jeans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: underwear - clothes worn under outer clothes
  • Synonym: panties - women's underpants

Etymology

Shortened, informal form of 'underwear'.
