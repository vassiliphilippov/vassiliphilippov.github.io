antisatellite

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.tiˈsæt.əl.aɪt / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈsæt.əl.aɪt / (BrE)
relating to or designed for destroying satellites in orbit
designed to destroy satellites

Examples

The country successfully tested an antisatellite missile last year.
International treaties have been proposed to ban antisatellite weapons in space.
Concerns about antisatellite technology have grown with the rise of space-based assets.

Synset Examples

antisatellite weapons

Related Terms

  • Antonym: satellite - an artificial body placed in orbit around the earth or moon

Etymology

anti- (against) + satellite, first used in the context of military technology in the mid-20th century.
