pantsuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈpænˌsuːt / (AmE), / ˈpɑːntˌsuːt / (BrE)
A set of clothing for women consisting of a pair of trousers and a matching jacket.
women's matching trousers and jacket outfit

Examples

She wore a stylish pantsuit to the business conference.
The designer’s new collection features a bold red pantsuit.
Many women prefer a pantsuit for professional settings rather than a dress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trouser suit - British term for a woman's matching jacket and trousers set

Etymology

Formed from 'pant(s)' + 'suit', first popularized in the 1960s.
