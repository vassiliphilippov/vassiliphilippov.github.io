gala

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪ.lə / (AmE), / ˈɡɑː.lə / (BrE)
A large, festive occasion or celebration, often with entertainment or a special purpose.
large festive event or celebration

Examples

The charity held its annual gala to raise funds for medical research.
Guests arrived in elegant attire for the museum's gala opening night.
The spring gala featured live music and a spectacular dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: festival - a series of special events, performances, or activities
  • Synonym: party - a social gathering of invited guests

Etymology

From Italian 'gala' (festive dress), from Old French 'gale' (rejoicing).
