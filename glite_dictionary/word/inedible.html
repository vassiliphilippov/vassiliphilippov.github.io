inedible

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈnɛdəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪˈnɛdɪbl̩ / (BrE)
not suitable for eating; not fit to be consumed as food
not suitable for eating

Examples

The berries looked appealing, but they were inedible and could cause illness if eaten.
She accidentally burned the cake, making it completely inedible.
Some mushrooms are inedible and even poisonous to humans.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: edible - suitable or safe to eat

Etymology

From Latin 'inedibilis', from 'in-' (not) + 'edibilis' (edible).
