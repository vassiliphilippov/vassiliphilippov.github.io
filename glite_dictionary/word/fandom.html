fandom

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæn.dəm / (AmE), / ˈfan.dəm / (BrE)
all the fans of a particular sport, person, team, or work of entertainment, regarded collectively as a community or subculture
community of fans of an activity or person

Examples

The Harry Potter fandom is known for its creativity and dedication.
Soccer fandom spans countries and brings people together from around the world.
She became more active in the online fandom after attending her first concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fanbase - the body of fans of a particular person, team, or work
  • Degree Lower: audience - a group of people who watch or listen to something

Etymology

Formed from 'fan' (enthusiast) + '-dom' (denoting domain or condition).
