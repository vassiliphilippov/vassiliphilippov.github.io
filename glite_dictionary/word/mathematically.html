mathematically

1. ADV

/ ˌmæθəˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌmæθəˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or is consistent with mathematics.
in a mathematical manner

Examples

It is mathematically impossible to divide by zero.
Her solution was elegant, concise, and mathematically rigorous.
The team proved the theory mathematically before attempting any experiments.

Synset Examples

mathematically impossible

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arithmetically - in a manner relating to arithmetic

Etymology

From mathematical + -ly.
