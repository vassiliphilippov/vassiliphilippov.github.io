crotchety

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɑː.tʃə.ti / (AmE), / ˈkrɒtʃ.ə.ti / (BrE)
irritable and easily annoyed, especially in a slightly eccentric or unpredictable way
irritable; easily annoyed

Examples

My grandfather can be a bit crotchety in the mornings before his coffee.
The crotchety neighbor yelled at the children for playing near his yard.
As he got older, he became increasingly crotchety and difficult to please.

Synset Examples

a cantankerous and venomous-tongued old lady

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cantankerous - bad-tempered, argumentative, and uncooperative
  • Antonym: good-natured - pleasant, cheerful, and friendly

Etymology

From 'crotchet' (whim, eccentricity) + '-y'.
