pouf

1. NOUN

/ puf / (AmE), / puːf / (BrE)
(offensive) An insulting term for a homosexual man, regarded as derogatory and highly offensive.
offensive term for a homosexual man
offensive

Examples

He was bullied at school and called a pouf by his classmates.
The movie was criticized for its use of the word pouf to describe a gay character.
Many people are unaware of the history and offensiveness of the term pouf.

2. NOUN

/ puːf / (AmE), / puːf / (BrE)
A thick, low cushion that is used as a seat or footstool.
thick cushion used as a seat or footstool

Examples

She purchased a colorful pouf for the corner of her living room.
The children sat comfortably on the large pouf while playing games.
He propped his feet up on the soft pouf after a long day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ottoman - a padded, upholstered seat or bench without a back or arms, often used as a footstool

Etymology

From French 'pouf', originally describing a type of large, padded hairstyle or cushion.
