copybook

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑpiˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈkɒpiˌbʊk / (BrE)
a book containing examples of handwriting for students to imitate, used for teaching penmanship
book for practicing handwriting

Examples

The teacher handed each student a copybook to practice their cursive letters.
In the nineteenth century, children often learned to write using a copybook.
Her neat handwriting was the result of years spent with a copybook in primary school.
