recentish

1. ADJ

/ ˈriːsəntɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈriːsəntɪʃ / (BrE)
fairly recent; somewhat recent, but not strictly so
somewhat recent
informal

Examples

I read a recentish article about climate change.
Her job interview was only recentish, so she's still waiting for a response.
We moved into this apartment recentish and are still getting settled.

Synset Examples

I read a recentish article about climate change.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recent - having happened or started only a short time ago

Etymology

Formed by adding the informal suffix -ish to 'recent'.
