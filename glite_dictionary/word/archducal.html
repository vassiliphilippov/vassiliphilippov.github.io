archducal

1. ADJ

/ ɑːrtʃˈduːkəl / (AmE), / ɑːtʃˈdjuːkəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of an archduke or his territory (archduchy)
of or relating to an archduke or archduchy

Examples

The palace was decorated in an extravagant archducal style, befitting its royal heritage.
She admired the archducal jewels displayed in the museum's European royalty collection.
The archducal family played a pivotal role in the region's political history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ducal - relating to a duke or dukedom

Etymology

Formed from 'archduke' + '-al', from Middle Latin 'archidux, archiducis'.
