relatedness

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈleɪtɪdnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪtɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being connected or related; the manner or degree of connection between things or people
the state of being related or connected

Examples

Scientists study the relatedness of different animal species to learn about evolution.
The sense of relatedness among team members contributed to their success.
Genetic testing can reveal the relatedness between distant family members.

Synset Examples

the relatedness of all living things

Related Terms

  • Synonym: connection - a relationship in which a person, thing, or idea is linked or associated with something else
  • Antonym: unrelatedness - the state of not being related or connected

Etymology

From 'related' + '-ness', formed in English.
