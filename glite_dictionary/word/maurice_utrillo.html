Maurice Utrillo

1. PROPN

/ moʊˈriːs juːˈtrɪloʊ / (AmE), / mɒˈriːs juːˈtrɪloʊ / (BrE)
(1883–1955) French painter known for his depictions of Parisian street scenes.
French painter of Paris street scenes
