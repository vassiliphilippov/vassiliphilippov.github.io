bare-breasted

1. ADJ

/ ˌbɛrˈbrɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˌbeəˈbrɛstɪd / (BrE)
(of a woman) having the breasts uncovered; not wearing any covering over the breasts.
with breasts uncovered

Examples

Some indigenous tribes have traditionally worn bare-breasted attire.
The painting depicted a bare-breasted goddess draped in silk.
Several protestors marched bare-breasted to make a statement about body positivity.

Synset Examples

topless waitresses
a topless cabaret

Related Terms

  • Synonym: topless - (of a woman) having the breasts uncovered

Etymology

From 'bare' (exposed, uncovered) + 'breasted' (having breasts); recorded since the late 19th century.
