reimbursement

1. NOUN

/ ˌriːɪmˈbɝːsmənt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪmˈbɜːsmənt / (BrE)
the act of paying back money to someone for expenses, losses, or damages they have incurred
repayment for money spent or losses

Examples

The company offered reimbursement for all work-related travel expenses.
She submitted her receipts to receive reimbursement from her employer.
Your insurance policy may include reimbursement for medical costs.

Synset Examples

he received reimbursement for his travel expenses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compensation - payment given to make up for loss or injury
  • Synonym: repayment - the act of paying back money borrowed or spent

Etymology

From French 'rembourser' (to pay back), from Old French 're-' (again) + 'bourser' (to purse, pay).
