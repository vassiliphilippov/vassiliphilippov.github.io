unsolicited

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.səˈlɪs.ɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.səˈlɪs.ɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
not asked for or requested; given or sent without being solicited
not asked for or requested

Examples

We received several unsolicited phone calls from telemarketers late at night.
She resented the unsolicited advice her colleagues kept offering her at work.
The company sends out unsolicited promotional emails to potential customers.

Synset Examples

unasked-for advice
advice unasked for can be greatly resented
unsolicited junk mail

Related Terms

  • Antonym: solicited - asked for; requested
  • Synonym: uninvited - not invited or not requested

Etymology

From Latin 'sollicitare' (to disturb, solicit), with 'un-' meaning 'not'.
