unpatterned

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpætərnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpætənd / (BrE)
lacking any pattern, especially in terms of color or design; plain or uniform
without a pattern; plain

Examples

The architect chose an unpatterned fabric for the sofa to keep the look minimal.
She prefers unpatterned wallpapers because they make the room feel more spacious.
The dress was completely unpatterned, drawing attention to its simple silhouette.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: patterned - having a decorative design or pattern
  • Synonym: plain - simple or undecorated in appearance

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + patterned.
