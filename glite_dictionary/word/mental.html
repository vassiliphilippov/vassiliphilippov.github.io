mental

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛn.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.tl̩ / (BrE)
of or relating to a chin- or liplike structure in certain insects and mollusks.
relating to a chinlike structure (zoology)

Examples

The entomologist noted the prominent mental plate on the beetle's lower jaw.
In some mollusks, the mental lobes assist in feeding.
The insect's mental area is crucial for identifying its species.

Etymology

From Latin *mentum* (chin), used in scientific contexts.
