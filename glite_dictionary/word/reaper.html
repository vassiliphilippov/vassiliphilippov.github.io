Reaper

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːpɚ / (AmE), / ˈriːpə / (BrE)
Death personified as an old man or skeleton, often depicted carrying a scythe.
personification of death

Examples

Many stories feature the Reaper coming to claim the souls of the departed.
In the painting, the Reaper looms over the battlefield with his scythe.
Some cultures imagine the Reaper as a silent, hooded figure guiding the dead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Grim Reaper - a common figure personifying death, depicted as a cloaked skeleton with a scythe
