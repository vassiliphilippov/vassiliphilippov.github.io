Tombigbee

1. PROPN

/ tɑmˈbɪɡbi / (AmE), / tɒmˈbɪɡbi / (BrE)
a river in the southeastern United States, rising in northeastern Mississippi and flowing south through western Alabama to join the Alabama River and form the Mobile River
river in Mississippi and Alabama, USA

Examples

The explorers traveled along the Tombigbee during their journey across the South.
Flooding along the Tombigbee River has affected several communities this year.
The Tombigbee is known for its scenic beauty and abundant wildlife.

Etymology

From Choctaw, possibly meaning 'box maker' or 'coffin maker'.
