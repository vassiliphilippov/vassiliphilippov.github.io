toddy

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑdi / (AmE), / ˈtɒdi / (BrE)
A hot alcoholic drink made with liquor, water, sugar, and spices.
hot alcoholic mixed drink

Examples

He made a toddy to help soothe his sore throat on the cold evening.
A hot toddy is a traditional remedy for colds in many cultures.
She requested a toddy with extra honey at the bar.

Etymology

From Hindi ṭaṛī (palm wine).
