wastebasket

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪstˌbæskət / (AmE), / ˈweɪstˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
a container with an open top, used for holding paper and other small items of rubbish
container for rubbish or waste paper

Examples

He tossed the crumpled paper into the wastebasket by his desk.
The office wastebasket was overflowing with old documents.
Please empty your wastebasket before leaving for the day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bin - container for waste; especially British English
  • Synonym: trash can - American English term for a container for rubbish
  • Synonym: dustbin - British English term for a rubbish bin

Etymology

Formed from 'waste' (rubbish) + 'basket' (container).
