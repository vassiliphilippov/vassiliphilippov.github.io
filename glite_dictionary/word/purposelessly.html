purposelessly

1. ADV

/ ˈpɜrpəs.ləsli / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpəs.ləsli / (BrE)
In a manner lacking a clear purpose or aim.
without purpose or aim

Examples

He wandered purposelessly through the empty corridors, searching for something to do.
The papers were scattered purposelessly across the desk, as if tossed by the wind.
She scrolled purposelessly through her phone, not really paying attention to anything.

Synset Examples

let's not purposelessly dispense the aid

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aimlessly - without any definite purpose or direction

Etymology

From 'purposeless' + '-ly'.
