rosehip

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊzˌhɪp / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzˌhɪp / (BrE)
the small, round, typically red or orange fruit of a rose plant, often used in making tea, jams, or oils
fruit of a rose plant

Examples

She brewed a pot of rosehip tea to enjoy on the cool afternoon.
The hedgerows were dotted with bright red rosehips in late autumn.
Many vitamin C supplements are made from rosehip extract.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hip - the fruit of a rose plant
