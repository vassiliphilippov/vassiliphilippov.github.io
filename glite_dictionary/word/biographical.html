biographical

1. ADJ

/ ˌbaɪ.əˈɡræf.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əˈɡræf.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to the facts or details of a person's life; connected with biography
relating to a biography

Examples

The author included many biographical details in the introduction to the book.
During the interview, she shared some fascinating biographical anecdotes about the scientist's early life.
All biographical information must be accurate before publication.

Synset Examples

biographical data

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: autobiographical - relating to a person's own life story
  • Degree Lower: historical - relating to history or past events

Etymology

From Greek biographia (life writing) via French biographique and English biography.
