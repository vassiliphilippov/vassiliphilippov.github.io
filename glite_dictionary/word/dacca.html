Dacca

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɑːkə / (AmE), / ˈdækə / (BrE)
the former official English name for Dhaka, the capital and largest city of Bangladesh
former name for Dhaka, Bangladesh

Examples

During the colonial era, Dacca was renowned for its fine muslin textiles.
My grandfather studied at the University of Dacca before the country's independence.
Many historical documents refer to the city as Dacca rather than Dhaka.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dhaka - capital and largest city of Bangladesh; current official English spelling

Etymology

From Bengali ঢাকা (Ḍhākā); 'Dacca' was the English spelling during British colonial rule.
