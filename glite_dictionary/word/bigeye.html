bigeye

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪɡˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɪɡˌaɪ / (BrE)
A small red fish of tropical American coastal waters, noted for its large eyes and rough scales.
tropical red fish with large eyes

Examples

The bigeye is often found hiding among coral reefs during the day.
Divers spotted several bigeyes swimming near the rocky outcrop.
Because of its large eyes, the bigeye is adapted to hunting in dim waters.
