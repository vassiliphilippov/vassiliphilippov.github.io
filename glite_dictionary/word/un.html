UN

1. PROPN

/ ˌjuːˈɛn / (AmE), / ˌjuːˈen / (BrE)
an international organization formed in 1945 to promote peace, security, and cooperation among countries; abbreviation of the United Nations
abbreviation for the United Nations

Examples

The UN held an emergency meeting to discuss the crisis.
Many countries turn to the UN for support during international conflicts.
She has worked with the UN on humanitarian projects in Africa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: United Nations - an international organization founded in 1945 to promote global peace and cooperation

Etymology

Abbreviation of United Nations, founded 1945.
