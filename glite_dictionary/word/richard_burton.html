Richard Burton

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɪtʃərd ˈbɜrtən / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃəd ˈbɜːtən / (BrE)
(1821–1890) An English explorer, geographer, and writer, notable for being among the first Europeans to explore Lake Tanganyika with John Speke.
English explorer (1821–1890)

Examples

Richard Burton documented his journeys across Africa in vivid detail.
Many historians regard Richard Burton's exploration of Lake Tanganyika as a groundbreaking achievement.
In 1857, Richard Burton and John Speke set out to find the source of the Nile.
