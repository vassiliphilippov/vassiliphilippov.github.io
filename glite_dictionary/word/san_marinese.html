San Marinese

1. NOUN

/ sæn məˈriːniːz / (AmE), / san məˈriːniːz / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of San Marino
person from San Marino

Examples

The new ambassador is a distinguished San Marinese diplomat.
Many San Marinese celebrate their national holiday with great enthusiasm.
As a proud San Marinese, she always supports her country's team at international events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sammarinese - alternative demonym for a person from San Marino

Etymology

From San Marino, the name of the country, with the English suffix '-ese' denoting origin or nationality.

Examples
