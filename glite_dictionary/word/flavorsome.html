flavorsome

1. ADJ

/ ˈfleɪvɚsəm / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvəsəm / (BrE)
Having a strong and pleasant taste; full of flavor.
full of flavor; tasty

Examples

The chef's new recipe is particularly flavorsome and has earned rave reviews.
The soup was light yet surprisingly flavorsome for such simple ingredients.
Customers love the flavorsome spices used in this traditional dish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flavorful - having a strong and pleasant taste
  • Antonym: bland - lacking strong flavor
