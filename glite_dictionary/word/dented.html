dented

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɛntɪd / (BrE)
(of an object, especially made of metal) having a hollow area caused by being hit or pressed in.
having a surface hollow from being hit

Examples

The bicycle had a dented frame after the accident.
She returned the borrowed pan, apologizing for the dented lid.
The mechanic examined the dented bumper and suggested it be replaced.

Synset Examples

bent nails
a car with a crumpled front end
dented fenders

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crumpled - creased or crushed out of shape
  • Antonym: undamaged - not harmed or dented
