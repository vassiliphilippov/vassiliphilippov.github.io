Port-au-Prince

1. PROPN

/ ˌpɔːrt oʊ ˈprɪns / (AmE), / ˌpɔː təʊ ˈpræ̃s / (BrE)
The capital and largest city of Haiti, located on the Gulf of Gonâve.
capital city of Haiti

Examples

After the earthquake, aid began pouring into Port-au-Prince from around the world.
He grew up in Port-au-Prince before moving to the United States for college.
The bustling markets of Port-au-Prince attract visitors with their vibrant colors and lively atmosphere.

Etymology

French, meaning 'Port of the Prince'.
