umteenth

1. ADJ

/ ʌmˈtiːnθ / (AmE), / ʌmˈtiːnθ / (BrE)
(informal) Used to indicate the last in an indefinitely numerous series; very many or countless, often implying exasperation or repetition.
very many; in a countless series
informal

Examples

I've told you for the umteenth time to clean your room.
After the umteenth interruption, she lost her patience entirely.
They moved again for the umteenth time this decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: umpteenth - (informal) Last in an indefinitely numerous series; countless times

Etymology

Variant of 'umpteenth', modeled after 'umpteen' (a humorous extension of 'teen' as in thirteen, fourteen, etc.) formed in early 20th-century English.
