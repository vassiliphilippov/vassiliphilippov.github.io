Chislev

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪz.lɛv / (AmE), / ˈkɪz.lɛv / (BrE)
the third month of the civil year and the ninth month of the ecclesiastical year in the Jewish calendar, usually falling in November and December.
month in the Jewish calendar (Nov-Dec)

Examples

Hanukkah usually begins in the month of Chislev according to the Jewish calendar.
The festival fell on the twenty-fifth day of Chislev this year.
Many significant events in Jewish history occurred during the month of Chislev.

Etymology

From Hebrew Kislēw, name of a month in the Jewish calendar.
