seven-day

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛvən deɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɛv.ən deɪ / (BrE)
lasting for a period of seven days; extending through an entire week.
lasting for a week

Examples

The hotel offers a seven-day spa package for its guests.
His illness required a strict seven-day quarantine.
They completed a seven-day trek across the desert.
