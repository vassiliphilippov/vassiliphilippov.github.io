Humphrey Bogart

1. PROPN

/ ˈhʌmfri boʊˌɡɑrt / (AmE), / ˈhʌmfri ˈbəʊɡɑːt / (BrE)
(1899–1957) A renowned American film actor, best known for his roles in classic movies such as 'Casablanca' and 'The Maltese Falcon'.
famous American film actor (1899–1957)

Examples

The classic film 'Casablanca' starred Humphrey Bogart and Ingrid Bergman.
Many critics consider Humphrey Bogart one of the greatest actors of Hollywood's Golden Age.
In 'The Maltese Falcon,' Humphrey Bogart played the iconic detective Sam Spade.
