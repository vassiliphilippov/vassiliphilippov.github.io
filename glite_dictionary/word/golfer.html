golfer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑːlfər / (AmE), / ˈɡɒlfə / (BrE)
a person who plays the game of golf, either professionally or for pleasure
person who plays golf

Examples

As an experienced golfer, she participates in tournaments around the world.
He took up the sport and soon became a passionate golfer.
Many golfers practice their swings for hours to improve their game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golf - a game played on a large outdoor course, the object being to hit a small ball into a series of holes with as few strokes as possible

Etymology

From 'golf' (the sport) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
