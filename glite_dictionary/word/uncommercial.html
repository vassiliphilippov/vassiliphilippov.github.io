uncommercial

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkəˈmɜːrʃəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈmɜːʃəl / (BrE)
not likely to bring financial gain or succeed in business; not conducive to commercial success.
not likely to be commercially successful

Examples

The director feared his film was too uncommercial for mainstream audiences.
Despite positive reviews, the band's sound remained distinctly uncommercial.
His uncommercial approach to art prioritized creativity over profit.

Synset Examples

might prove arty and hence uncommercial

Related Terms

  • Antonym: commercial - likely to make a profit; intended to succeed in business

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'commercial'.
