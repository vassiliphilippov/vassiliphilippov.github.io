hitty-missy

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɪti ˈmɪsi / (AmE), / ˈhɪti ˈmɪsi / (BrE)
(of an outfit, style, or decor) mismatched or eclectic; composed of elements that do not conform to a uniform style.
mismatched or eclectic in style

Examples

His hitty-missy sense of style caught everyone's eye at the school fashion show.
She chose a hitty-missy look for the art gallery opening, combining patterns and colors boldly.
The restaurant’s hitty-missy interior featured mismatched chairs and quirky decorations.

Synset Examples

He wore a hitty-missy outfit that made him stand out.
Her hitty-missy dress was perfect for the casual party.
The hitty-missy décor added charm to the gathering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eclectic - deriving ideas, style, or taste from a broad and diverse range of sources
  • Synonym: mismatched - not matching; consisting of incompatible or different elements
