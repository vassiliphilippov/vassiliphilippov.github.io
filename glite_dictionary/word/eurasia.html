Eurasia

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈreɪʒə / (AmE), / jʊəˈreɪʒə / (BrE)
the landmass comprising the continents of Europe and Asia considered together as a single continent
the combined landmass of Europe and Asia

Examples

Many species of animals are native to Eurasia, ranging from tundra to temperate forest regions.
The Silk Road was an ancient trade network that linked East and West across Eurasia.
Geologists study the movements of tectonic plates that span the vast area of Eurasia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Europe - one of the world's seven continents, west of Asia
  • Synonym: Asia - the largest continent, east of Europe

Etymology

Formed from 'Europe' and 'Asia'.
