Halle

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɑː.lə / (AmE), / ˈha.lə / (BrE)
A city in the Saxony-Anhalt region of Germany, situated on the Saale River, historically a member of the Hanseatic League in the 13th and 14th centuries.
city in eastern Germany

Examples

The university in Halle is renowned for its research in natural sciences.
During my summer vacation, I visited the old market square in Halle.
Halle played an important role in the medieval Hanseatic League.
