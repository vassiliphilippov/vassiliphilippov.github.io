voluminously

1. ADV

/ vəˈluːmɪnəsli / (AmE), / vəˈluːmɪnəsli / (BrE)
In a manner that is large, ample, or full; with great volume or fullness.
in a voluminous manner

Examples

The curtains hung voluminously over the tall windows, casting soft shadows on the floor.
She spoke voluminously on the subject, covering every aspect in elaborate detail.
Clouds gathered voluminously above the mountains, threatening an afternoon storm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: voluminous - large and full in size or amount

Etymology

From 'voluminous' + '-ly'. 'Voluminous' from Latin 'volumin-', from 'volumen' (roll, volume).
