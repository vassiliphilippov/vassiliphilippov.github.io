verbally

1. ADV

/ ˈvɜrbəli / (AmE), / ˈvɜːbəli / (BrE)
by means of spoken or written words; using language rather than other means
by means of language or words

Examples

The instructions were given verbally, not in writing.
She was verbally threatened by an anonymous caller last night.
Applicants can request information verbally or in a written format.

Synset Examples

verbally expressive

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orally - by mouth; spoken rather than written

Etymology

From Latin 'verbalis' (pertaining to words), from 'verbum' (word).
