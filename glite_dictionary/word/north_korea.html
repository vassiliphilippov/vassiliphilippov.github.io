North Korea

1. PROPN

/ nɔrθ kəˈriːə / (AmE), / nɔːθ kəˈriːə / (BrE)
A country in East Asia, occupying the northern part of the Korean Peninsula, officially the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.
country in northern Korean Peninsula

Examples

North Korea has maintained a closed, authoritarian government since its establishment in 1948.
International negotiations with North Korea have focused on nuclear disarmament and security in East Asia.
Travel to North Korea is highly restricted and requires special permission from its government.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: South Korea - country in the southern Korean Peninsula
