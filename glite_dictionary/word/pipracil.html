Pipracil

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪp.rə.sɪl / (AmE), / ˈpɪp.rə.sɪl / (BrE)
A brand name for piperacillin, a synthetic penicillin antibiotic used to treat moderate to severe infections.
penicillin antibiotic (brand name)
medical

Examples

After being diagnosed with a severe infection, the doctor prescribed Pipracil intravenously.
Hospitalized patients sometimes receive Pipracil to combat resistant bacterial infections.
The pharmacy staff carefully reviewed the dosage instructions for Pipracil before administering it to the patient.
