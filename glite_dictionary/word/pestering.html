pestering

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛstərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɛstərɪŋ / (BrE)
constantly annoying or bothering someone
causing persistent annoyance

Examples

Stop your pestering and let me finish my work in peace.
His pestering questions became too much to handle after a while.
The pestering children kept asking for more candy during the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annoying - causing irritation or slight anger
  • Synonym: bothersome - causing trouble or annoyance
  • Antonym: soothing - having a calming effect; reducing irritation

Etymology

Present participle of the verb 'pester', from French 'pester' (to plague).
