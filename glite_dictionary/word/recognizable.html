recognizable

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɛkəɡˈnaɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛkəɡˈnaɪzəbəl / (BrE)
able to be recognized or identified from previous experience or knowledge.
able to be recognized

Examples

The city skyline is instantly recognizable to anyone who has visited before.
She wore a hat that made her barely recognizable in the crowd.
His distinctive voice is easily recognizable on the radio.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: identifiable - able to be identified

Etymology

From recognize + -able, ultimately from Latin recognoscere (to recall, recognize).

2. ADJ

/ ˈrɛk.əɡ.naɪ.zə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈrɛk.əɡ.nʌɪ.zə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be identified or distinguished easily; easy to notice or become aware of.
able to be identified easily

Examples

The logo is instantly recognizable to people all over the world.
She spoke with a recognizable accent that reminded me of home.
The symptoms were recognizable to any experienced doctor.

Synset Examples

this situation produces recognizable stress symptoms

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unrecognizable - not able to be identified or recognized
  • Synonym: distinct - clearly different or easy to notice

Etymology

From recognize + -able; 'recognize' from Latin recognoscere (to recall, examine).
