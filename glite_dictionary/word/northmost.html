northmost

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɔrθˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈnɔːθməʊst / (BrE)
Situated at the farthest northern point; most northerly.
farthest north; most northerly

Examples

The city is located at the northmost tip of the country.
The northmost island experiences colder weather than the southern ones.
Researchers set up camp near the northmost glacier to study climate change.

Synset Examples

Alaska is our northernmost state

Related Terms

  • Synonym: northernmost - farthest north; most northerly

Etymology

Formed from 'north' + 'most', following the same pattern as 'foremost' or 'utmost'.
