lymphoblast-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪmfoʊˌblæst laɪk / (AmE), / ˈlɪmfəʊˌblɑːst laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the characteristics of a lymphoblast, especially in morphology or appearance
resembling a lymphoblast
medical

Examples

Under the microscope, the cells appeared lymphoblast-like with large nuclei and scant cytoplasm.
The doctor noted a lymphoblast-like morphology in several abnormal leukocytes.
Certain viral infections can induce a lymphoblast-like transformation in lymphocytes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lymphoblast - an immature white blood cell
