ascendable

1. ADJ

/ əˈsɛndəbəl / (AmE), / əˈsɛndəbl / (BrE)
Able to be ascended; capable of being climbed or gone up.
capable of being ascended

Examples

The steep hillside was barely ascendable without special equipment.
Many hikers find the rocky path ascendable despite its challenging incline.
The ancient staircase remained ascendable after centuries of use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: climbable - able to be climbed

Etymology

From 'ascend' + '-able', patterned after the Latin ascendere (to climb up).
