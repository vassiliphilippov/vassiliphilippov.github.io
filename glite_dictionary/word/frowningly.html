frowningly

1. ADV

/ ˈfraʊnɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈfraʊnɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a frown; in a manner characterized by frowning.
in a way that shows a frown

Examples

She glanced frowningly at the noisy children in the back row.
The teacher responded frowningly to the student's careless mistake.
He listened frowningly as the report detailed several unexpected problems.

Synset Examples

he looked at her frowningly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sternly - in a strict or severe manner
