newspaper

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
A cheap paper, usually made from wood pulp, used for printing newspapers and sometimes for wrapping or packing.
cheap paper for printing newspapers

Examples

We wrapped the fragile dishes in newspaper before shipping them.
The floor was covered with newspaper to catch any paint drips.
Artists sometimes use strips of newspaper to create papier-mâché sculptures.

Synset Examples

they used bales of newspaper every day

2. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
A publication issued daily or weekly, printed on large sheets of folded paper, containing news, articles, and advertisements.
printed news publication

Examples

She picked up the newspaper and quickly scanned the headlines.
Every morning, he likes to read the newspaper with his coffee.
There was an interesting article about climate change in yesterday's newspaper.

Synset Examples

he read his newspaper at breakfast

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magazine - a periodical publication containing articles and illustrations
  • Degree Lower: tabloid - a newspaper with a compact page size, often sensational

Etymology

From 'news' + 'paper', first used in the 17th century to describe printed news sheets.

3. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
a printed publication consisting of folded sheets, containing news, articles, advertisements, and other items, issued regularly by a publisher
printed publication with news and articles

Examples

He folded the newspaper and placed it on the table after finishing the article.
She used a newspaper to shield herself from the sudden downpour.
A fresh newspaper is delivered to their doorstep every morning.

Synset Examples

when it began to rain he covered his head with a newspaper

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magazine - a periodical publication containing articles and illustrations
  • Synonym: journal - a newspaper or magazine that deals with a particular subject or professional activity

Etymology

From 'news' + 'paper', referring to paper that contains news.
