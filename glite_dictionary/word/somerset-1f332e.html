somerset

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌm.ərˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈsʌm.ə.sɛt / (BrE)
an acrobatic movement in which a person rolls or flips their body forward or backward so that their feet pass over their head and return to a standing or prone position; a somersault
an acrobatic rolling or flipping movement
archaic

Examples

The gymnast performed a perfect somerset across the mat, impressing the crowd with her agility.
In old adventure tales, heroes would escape danger with a daring somerset.
He lost his balance and tumbled into a somerset down the grassy hill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: somersault - an acrobatic movement where the body rolls forward or backward head over heels

Etymology

Variant form of 'somersault,' from Old French sombresault.
