SBA

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛs.biːˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛs.biːˈeɪ / (BrE)
an independent agency of the United States government that supports and advocates for small businesses, ensuring they have access to resources and government contracts
US agency supporting small businesses
american

Examples

The SBA provides loans and guidance to entrepreneurs starting new companies.
Many small businesses relied on SBA support during the economic downturn.
The company secured a contract thanks to the SBA's advocacy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Small Business Administration - U.S. government agency supporting small businesses

Etymology

Abbreviation of Small Business Administration.
