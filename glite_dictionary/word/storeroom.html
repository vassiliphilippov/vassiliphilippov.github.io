storeroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɔrˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈstɔːˌruːm / (BrE)
a room used for storing things, especially goods, supplies, or other items not in immediate use
room for storing things

Examples

The old books were kept in the storeroom at the back of the house.
Please check the storeroom to see if we have any extra chairs.
After the renovation, the storeroom was organized and labeled for easy access.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storage room - a room used for storage

Etymology

Formed from 'store' + 'room'.
