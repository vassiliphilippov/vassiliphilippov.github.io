liza

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːzə / (AmE), / ˈliːzə / (BrE)
A fish of the mullet family, similar to the striped mullet, found in the Caribbean region.
Caribbean mullet-like fish

Examples

Fishermen in Puerto Rico often catch liza for local markets.
The liza is commonly found in shallow Caribbean waters.
Grilled liza is a popular dish in coastal villages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mullet - any of various fishes of the family Mugilidae, especially the striped mullet

Etymology

From Spanish 'liza', meaning mullet.
