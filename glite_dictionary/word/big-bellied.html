big-bellied

1. ADJ

/ bɪɡ ˈbɛlid / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈbɛlid / (BrE)
having a large or prominent belly
having a prominent belly

Examples

The big-bellied man laughed heartily at the joke.
Some ancient statues depict big-bellied gods as symbols of abundance.
She rubbed her big-bellied dog as he lay in the sun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: potbellied - having a large, protruding belly
  • Antonym: slender - having a slim or thin physique
