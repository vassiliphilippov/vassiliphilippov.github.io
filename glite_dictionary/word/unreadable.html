unreadable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈriːdəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈriːdəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of text or handwriting) difficult or impossible to read or decipher.
difficult or impossible to read

Examples

His handwriting was so unreadable that nobody could make sense of the note.
The old label on the bottle had faded and become almost unreadable.
Some emails appear as unreadable text because of incompatibility between programs.

Synset Examples

indecipherable handwriting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illegible - impossible or very hard to read (especially handwriting)

Etymology

From un- (not) + readable, formed in English.
