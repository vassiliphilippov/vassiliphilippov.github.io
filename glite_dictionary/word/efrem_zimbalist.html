Efrem Zimbalist

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛfrəm zɪmˈbælɪst / (AmE), / ˈɛfrəm zɪmˈbælɪst / (BrE)
(1889–1985) United States violinist, born in Russia, celebrated for his virtuosity and influential teaching career.
American violinist and teacher (1889–1985)

Examples

Efrem Zimbalist was renowned for both his performances and his work as director of the Curtis Institute of Music.
The concert featured rare recordings of Efrem Zimbalist playing Brahms.
Music historians often cite Efrem Zimbalist as one of the leading violinists of the early twentieth century.
