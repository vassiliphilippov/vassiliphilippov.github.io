travel-stained

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrævəl steɪnd / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl steɪnd / (BrE)
(of clothing or appearance) dirty or marked as a result of having traveled.
marked or dirty from travel

Examples

He arrived at the inn in his travel-stained coat, drawing curious glances from the locals.
Her travel-stained boots left dusty prints on the polished floor.
After weeks on the road, their travel-stained faces revealed the hardships of the journey.

Synset Examples

travel-soiled clothes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soiled - dirty or stained
