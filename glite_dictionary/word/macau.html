Macau

1. PROPN

/ məˈkaʊ / (AmE), / məˈkaʊ / (BrE)
A Special Administrative Region of China on the south coast, formerly a Portuguese province until its return to China in 1999.
Chinese territory; former Portuguese colony

Examples

Tourists flock to Macau for its casinos and vibrant nightlife.
Macau was handed back to China from Portuguese rule in 1999.
Many international companies have established offices in Macau due to its unique status.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Macau', possibly from Cantonese '媽閣' (Māgok), referring to the A-Ma Temple.
