socioeconomically

1. ADV

/ ˌsoʊsioʊˌɛkəˈnɑmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsiəʊˌiːkəˈnɒmɪkli / (BrE)
In relation to both social and economic factors.
relating to social and economic factors

Examples

The two neighborhoods are very different socioeconomically despite their geographic proximity.
Programs need to address communities that are disadvantaged socioeconomically.
The study compared students who were socioeconomically diverse.

Synset Examples

they are far apart socioeconomically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: economically - in a way that relates to economics or finances
  • Synonym: socially - in a way that relates to society or social factors

Etymology

Formed from 'socio-' (relating to society) and 'economic', with the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
