rubel

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːbɛl / (AmE), / ˈruːbɛl / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Belarus
Belarusian currency unit

Examples

The price was listed as 50 rubel at the market in Minsk.
He exchanged his dollars for rubel before traveling to Belarus.
Economic inflation caused the value of the rubel to decrease significantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rouble - the basic unit of money in Russia

Etymology

From Belarusian рубель (rubel’), ultimately from Russian рубль (rouble).
