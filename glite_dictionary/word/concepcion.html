Concepcion

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɔn.sɛpˈsiː.oʊn / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.sɛpˈsiː.ɒn / (BrE)
an industrial city in southern Chile, located to the south of Santiago
a city in southern Chile

Examples

She traveled to Concepcion on business last year.
Many major industries are based in Concepcion.
After the earthquake, Concepcion underwent significant rebuilding.

Etymology

From Spanish Concepción, meaning 'conception', referring to the Immaculate Conception.
