topographical

1. ADJ

/ ˌtɑːpəˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌtɒpəˈɡræfɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or concerned with the physical features of a place or region, especially as represented on maps.
relating to topography; land features

Examples

The geologist prepared a topographical map to highlight the area's elevations and valleys.
The topographical survey revealed several hidden streams and hills on the property.
Her research focused on the topographical differences between various national parks.

Synset Examples

a topographical engineer
a topographical survey
topographic maps

Related Terms

  • Synonym: topographic - alternate form; relating to the physical features of a place

Etymology

From Greek topos (place) and -graphia (writing, description), via Late Latin topographia.
