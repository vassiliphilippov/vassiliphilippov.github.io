face lifting

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪs ˌlɪftɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪs ˌlɪftɪŋ / (BrE)
Plastic surgery to reduce wrinkles and other signs of aging on the face by removing excess skin and tightening underlying tissues.
cosmetic facial plastic surgery
medical

Examples

She decided to undergo a face lifting to rejuvenate her appearance.
The clinic specializes in face lifting and other cosmetic procedures.
Some celebrities have openly discussed having face lifting surgeries.

Synset Examples

some actresses have more than one face lift

Related Terms

  • Synonym: facelift - Plastic surgery to give a more youthful appearance to the face
