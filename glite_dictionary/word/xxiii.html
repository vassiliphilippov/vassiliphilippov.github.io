XXIII

1. NUM

/ ˌɛks ˌɛks aɪ ˈaɪ aɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛks ˌɛks aɪ ˈaɪ aɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for the number 23
Roman numeral for 23

Examples

She was born on May XXIII, according to the old documents.
The clock's face was marked with the Roman numeral XXIII for decoration.
In the championship, Team XXIII won the final match by two points.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 23 - the number twenty-three
  • Degree Lower: XXII - Roman numeral for 22
  • Degree Higher: XXIV - Roman numeral for 24

Etymology

From Latin numerals: X (10) + X (10) + III (3).
