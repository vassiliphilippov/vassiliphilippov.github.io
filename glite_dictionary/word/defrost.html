to defrost

1. VERB

/ diˈfrɔst / (AmE), / diːˈfrɒst / (BrE)
to remove frost or ice from (something), or to become free of frost or ice
remove frost or ice

Examples

I need to defrost the windshield before I can drive to work.
Please defrost the chicken before cooking it for dinner.
The freezer automatically defrosts every few hours to prevent ice buildup.

Synset Examples

Defrost the car window

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thaw - to become unfrozen or to cause something frozen to become unfrozen

Etymology

From de- (removal) + frost; first recorded in the early 20th century.
