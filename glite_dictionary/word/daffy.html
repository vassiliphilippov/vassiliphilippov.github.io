daffy

1. ADJ

/ ˈdæfi / (AmE), / ˈdafi / (BrE)
silly or foolish; slightly crazy, often in a harmless or amusing way.
silly or foolish

Examples

Stop being daffy and focus on your work.
Her daffy sense of humor always makes me laugh.
The daffy antics of the children lightened the mood of the room.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silly - lacking in seriousness, foolish
  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged or extremely foolish

Etymology

Possibly an alteration of 'daff' (foolish, simpleton).
