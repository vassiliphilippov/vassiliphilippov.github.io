Hussein of Jordan

1. PROPN

/ huːˈseɪn əv ˈdʒɔːrdən / (AmE), / hʊˈseɪn əv ˈdʒɔːdən / (BrE)
(1935–1999) King of Jordan from 1952 to 1999, known for establishing internal stability and pursuing peace with Israel.
former King of Jordan (1935–1999)

Examples

Hussein of Jordan was widely respected for his efforts to maintain peace in a turbulent region.
During the 1970s, Hussein of Jordan played a key role in Middle Eastern politics.
Many books have been written about the reign of Hussein of Jordan and his legacy.
