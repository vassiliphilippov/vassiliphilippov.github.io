veery

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪri / (AmE), / ˈvɪəri / (BrE)
a small North American thrush with tawny-brown plumage, known for its melodious song
North American thrush with a melodious song

Examples

We spotted a veery in the woods, its song echoing through the trees.
The distinctive spiral song of the veery is a delight to birdwatchers every spring.
Although small and elusive, the veery plays a vital role in forest ecosystems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thrush - a family of songbirds, including the veery

Etymology

Probably imitative of its song; first recorded in the mid 19th century.
