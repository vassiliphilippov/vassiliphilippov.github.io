anciently

1. ADV

/ ˈeɪnʃəntli / (AmE), / ˈeɪn(t)ʃəntli / (BrE)
At a time long ago; in ancient times.
in ancient times; long ago
archaic

Examples

The city was anciently famous for its grand temples and palaces.
It was anciently believed that the earth was the center of the universe.
Many traditions practiced anciently have faded into obscurity.

Synset Examples

a concern with what may have happened anciently

Related Terms

  • Synonym: formerly - in the past; previously
  • Antonym: recently - not long ago

Etymology

From 'ancient' + '-ly', first recorded in Middle English.
