Dugong

1. NOUN

/ duˈɡɑŋ / (AmE), / djuːˈɡɒŋ / (BrE)
A genus of marine mammals in the family Dugongidae, consisting of the dugong, a large herbivorous sea mammal.
genus of marine mammals (dugongs)

Examples

The Dugong is the only existing species in its genus, distinguishing it from other sirenians.
Scientists have classified the Dugong under the family Dugongidae due to its unique characteristics.
Marine biologists recently observed a female Dugong with her calf in the protected lagoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manatee - a large aquatic mammal related to the dugong

Etymology

From Malay 'duyung' meaning 'lady of the sea'.
