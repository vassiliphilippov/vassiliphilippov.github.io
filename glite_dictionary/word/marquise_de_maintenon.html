Marquise de Maintenon

1. PROPN

/ mɑrˈkiz də mɛntˈnɒn / (AmE), / mɑːˈkiːz də mɛntˈnɒ̃ / (BrE)
(1635–1719) The French consort of Louis XIV, who secretly married the king following the death of his first wife.
French consort of Louis XIV (1635–1719)

Examples

The letters of Marquise de Maintenon reveal her influence on the royal court.
Historians debate whether Marquise de Maintenon was ever crowned queen.
Marquise de Maintenon played a significant role in Louis XIV's private life.

Etymology

French, from her noble title and estate at Maintenon.
