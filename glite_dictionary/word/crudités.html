crudités

1. NOUN

/ ˌkruː.dɪˈteɪ / (AmE), / ˌkruː.dɪˈteɪ / (BrE)
raw vegetables cut into bite-sized strips and served with a dip, often as an appetizer
raw vegetables served with dip

Examples

A platter of crudités was placed on the table as an appetizer before dinner.
He arranged the crudités around a bowl of creamy dip for the party.
Guests snacked on crudités and cheese while waiting for the main course.

Etymology

Borrowed from French crudités, from Latin cruditas (rawness).
