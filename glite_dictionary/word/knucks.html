knucks

1. NOUN

/ nʌks / (AmE), / nʌks / (BrE)
A small metal weapon worn over the knuckles on the back of the hand, typically used for striking.
metal weapon worn over knuckles

Examples

He carried a pair of knucks for his own protection.
The police confiscated several illegal knucks from the suspects.
Wearing knucks can result in serious legal consequences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brass knuckles - a similar weapon made of brass and worn over the knuckles
