cola

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊ.lə / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.lə / (BrE)
A sweet, carbonated soft drink flavored with extracts from kola nuts and often containing caffeine.
sweet carbonated soft drink

Examples

She ordered a cola with her burger at the diner.
The children drank ice-cold cola at the summer picnic.
Many people prefer diet versions of cola to reduce calorie intake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soda - sweetened carbonated beverage
  • Synonym: soft drink - non-alcoholic carbonated drink

Etymology

From West African languages via Spanish and Portuguese; from the kola nut, used historically for flavor and caffeine.
