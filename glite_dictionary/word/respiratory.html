respiratory

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛspəˌrɛˌtɔri, rɪˈspɪrəˌtɔri / (AmE), / rɪˈspɪrətəri, ˈrɛspərətəri / (BrE)
relating to or affecting respiration (breathing) or the organs involved in breathing
relating to breathing or the respiratory system

Examples

The patient showed signs of respiratory distress after running up the stairs.
Doctors are monitoring his respiratory function closely during recovery.
Many viruses cause respiratory infections in both children and adults.

Synset Examples

respiratory assistance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pulmonary - relating to the lungs

Etymology

From Latin respiratorius, from respirare (to breathe out), from re- (again) + spirare (to breathe).
