armhole

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrmˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈɑːmˌhəʊl / (BrE)
an opening in a garment through which the arm passes and to which a sleeve may be attached
opening in a garment for the arm

Examples

The tailor measured the armhole before sewing the sleeves onto the shirt.
Make sure the armhole is comfortable and not too tight when trying on the dress.
She adjusted the armhole to give herself more room to move her arms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleeve - a part of a garment that covers the arm
