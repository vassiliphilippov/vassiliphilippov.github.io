anemographic

1. ADJ

/ ˌænəmoʊˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌænɪməˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the recording or measurement of wind speed and direction.
relating to wind measurement recording
medical

Examples

The researchers used anemographic data to analyze wind patterns over the course of the year.
An anemographic chart was presented during the meteorology seminar.
Advancements in anemographic equipment have improved the accuracy of weather forecasting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anemometer - instrument for measuring the speed of the wind

Etymology

From Greek 'anemos' (wind) + '-graphic' (writing, recording).
