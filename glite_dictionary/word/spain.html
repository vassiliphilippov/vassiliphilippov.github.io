Spain

1. PROPN

/ speɪn / (AmE), / speɪn / (BrE)
a country in southwestern Europe occupying most of the Iberian Peninsula; a parliamentary monarchy and former colonial power
country in southwestern Europe

Examples

She traveled to Spain last summer to explore its rich history and vibrant culture.
Spain is known for its diverse regions and delicious cuisine.
The art of flamenco dancing originated in Spain, particularly in the region of Andalusia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Spanish - the language spoken in Spain and many countries worldwide
  • Synonym: Iberia - the Iberian Peninsula, which includes Spain and Portugal

Etymology

From Latin 'Hispania', the Roman name for the Iberian Peninsula.
