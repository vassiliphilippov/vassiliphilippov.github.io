needfully

1. ADV

/ ˈnid.fə.li / (AmE), / ˈniːd.fʊ.li / (BrE)
In an essential or necessary manner; in a way that is required or needed.
in a necessary manner

Examples

The charity used its funds needfully to provide shelter for the homeless.
All resources must be needfully allocated to areas of greatest urgency.
He spoke needfully about the changes required in the system.

Synset Examples

such expenses are necessarily incurred

Related Terms

  • Synonym: necessarily - in a way that could not be otherwise; inevitably

Etymology

From 'needful' + '-ly'.
