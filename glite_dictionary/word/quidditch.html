quidditch

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɪdɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈkwɪdɪtʃ / (BrE)
A real-life team sport inspired by the fictional game from the Harry Potter series, played with broomsticks and combining elements of rugby, dodgeball, and tag.
real-life sport based on Harry Potter game

Examples

The university started a quidditch team that quickly gained popularity.
Fans dressed up as their favorite characters to watch the regional quidditch tournament.
She broke her broom during the quidditch match but still finished the game.

Etymology

Invented by J.K. Rowling in the Harry Potter series.
