Quidditch

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɪd.ɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈkwɪd.ɪtʃ / (BrE)
a fictional sport played on flying broomsticks, invented in the Harry Potter series
fictional sport in Harry Potter

Examples

He dreamed of playing Quidditch as a seeker for Gryffindor.
The rules of Quidditch are explained in the first Harry Potter book.
At themed events, fans often organize their own Quidditch matches.

Etymology

Coined by J.K. Rowling for the Harry Potter books.
