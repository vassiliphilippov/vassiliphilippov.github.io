visualized

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɪʒuəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒjʊəlaɪzd / (BrE)
seen in the mind as a mental image or picture; imagined visually
seen as a mental image

Examples

The author described the visualized world of her novel in vivid detail.
His visualized plan of the building helped architects make the design a reality.
For meditation, some people use a visualized image of a peaceful landscape.

Synset Examples

the glory of his envisioned future
the snow-covered Alps pictured in her imagination
the visualized scene lacked the ugly details of real life

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imagined - formed in the mind; not real
  • Synonym: envisioned - imagined as a future possibility; mentally pictured
