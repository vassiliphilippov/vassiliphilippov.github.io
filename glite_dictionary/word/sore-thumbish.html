sore-thumbish

1. ADJ

/ sɔr ˈθʌm ɪʃ / (AmE), / sɔː ˈθʌm ɪʃ / (BrE)
awkwardly or unfavorably conspicuous; feeling or appearing out of place
unfavorably conspicuous or out of place
informal

Examples

His mismatched tie was sore-thumbish during the formal business meeting.
The bright yellow house looked sore-thumbish among the rows of brick buildings.
She always felt sore-thumbish at fancy parties in her casual clothes.

Synset Examples

His new haircut looks sore-thumbish compared to his usual style.
Wearing sandals in winter feels sore-thumbish to me.
That bright orange car looks sore-thumbish next to the others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conspicuous - clearly visible or attracting notice
  • Antonym: inconspicuous - not clearly visible or attracting attention

Etymology

From the simile 'stand out like a sore thumb' + -ish suffix forming adjectives.
