teleconferencing

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛləˈkɑːn.fɚən.sɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈkɒn.fər.əns.ɪŋ / (BrE)
The use of telecommunications equipment, such as video or audio links, to hold a conference between people in different locations.
holding conferences by telecommunications

Examples

Many businesses rely on teleconferencing to connect teams spread across multiple cities.
The project was managed through weekly teleconferencing sessions with all stakeholders.
Advancements in technology have made teleconferencing more accessible and efficient for remote workers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: videoconferencing - conducting a conference with participants in different locations using video links

Etymology

Formed from 'tele-' (distance) and 'conferencing', from 'conference'.
