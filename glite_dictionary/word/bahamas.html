Bahamas

1. PROPN

/ bəˈhɑː.məz / (AmE), / bəˈhɑː.məz / (BrE)
An island country in the Atlantic Ocean, to the east of Florida and Cuba, known as a popular winter resort destination.
island country east of Florida

Examples

Tourists flock to the Bahamas every year for its sunny beaches and clear blue waters.
After their wedding, they spent their honeymoon in the Bahamas.
The Bahamas is famous for its coral reefs and vibrant marine life.

Etymology

From Spanish 'bajamar' (shallow sea).
