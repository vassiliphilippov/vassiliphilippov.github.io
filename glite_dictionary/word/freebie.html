freebie

1. NOUN

/ ˈfriː.bi / (AmE), / ˈfriː.bi / (BrE)
something that is given free of charge, typically as part of a promotional offer
something given for free, especially as a promotion
informal

Examples

They handed out freebies at the product launch to attract more people.
He loves going to events just for the freebies.
At the conference, every attendee received a freebie bag with samples and coupons.

Synset Examples

the road map was a freebie

Related Terms

  • Synonym: giveaway - something given out for free to promote something

Etymology

Probably derived from 'free' with the suffix '-bie' (as in 'oldie', 'softie'), originating in American English in the mid-20th century.
