Ping

1. PROPN

/ pɪŋ / (AmE), / pɪŋ / (BrE)
a river in western Thailand, which is a major tributary of the Chao Phraya River.
river in western Thailand

Examples

The Ping River supports important agricultural regions in western Thailand.
Many villages along the Ping depend on fishing as a livelihood.
The Ping joins with other rivers to form the Chao Phraya.
