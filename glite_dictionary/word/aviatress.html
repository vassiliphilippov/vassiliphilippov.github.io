aviatress

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪviˌeɪtrəs / (AmE), / ˈeɪvɪətrɪs / (BrE)
A female aviator; a woman who operates an aircraft.
female aviator
archaic

Examples

Amelia Earhart was a pioneering aviatress admired by many for her courage.
The aviatress soared gracefully through the clouds during the airshow.
In the early days of flight, it was rare to see an aviatress in the cockpit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aviator - a person who flies an aircraft
  • Synonym: pilot - a person who operates the controls of an aircraft

Etymology

Formed from 'aviator' + suffix '-ess' indicating female gender.
