An Nefud

1. PROPN

/ æn nəˈfuːd / (AmE), / an nɛˈfuːd / (BrE)
a desert in northern Saudi Arabia known for its red sand and fierce winds
desert in northern Saudi Arabia

Examples

The An Nefud desert is famous for its striking red sands stretching across northern Saudi Arabia.
Explorers have long been challenged by the harsh winds and arid conditions of the An Nefud.
Archaeological discoveries in the An Nefud have provided insight into early human migration.

Etymology

From Arabic 'nefud', meaning desert or sandy expanse.
