impartation

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˌpɑːrˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪmˌpɑːˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of communicating or conveying information, knowledge, or qualities to someone
the act of conveying information or knowledge

Examples

The impartation of knowledge is a key role of teachers in every society.
During the seminar, there was a valuable impartation of ideas among the participants.
Effective impartation of skills can greatly enhance employee performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transmission - the act of passing something from one person or place to another

Etymology

From Latin 'impartire' (to share), via Late Latin 'impartatio'.
