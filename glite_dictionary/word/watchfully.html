watchfully

1. ADV

/ ˈwɑtʃ.fə.li / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃ.fʊ.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows careful observation or attention to possible danger or trouble.
in an attentive or vigilant way

Examples

The guard moved watchfully along the perimeter, scanning for any signs of movement.
She listened watchfully to the noises outside her window late at night.
The cat sat watchfully beside the mouse hole, waiting for its chance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vigilantly - in a vigilant manner; alertly
  • Antonym: carelessly - in a careless manner; without attention
