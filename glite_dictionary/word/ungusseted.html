ungusseted

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɡʌs.ɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡʌs.ɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
(of a garment or item) not having gussets; lacking a gusset for reinforcement or shape.
without gussets

Examples

The ungusseted bag could not expand to accommodate extra items.
Many modern socks are gusseted, but older designs were often ungusseted.
If the envelope is ungusseted, it will lie flat and not bulge.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: gusseted - having gussets; provided with a gusset

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'gusset' (a piece of material sewn into a garment).
