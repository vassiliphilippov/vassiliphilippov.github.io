atypic

1. ADJ

/ eɪˈtɪpɪk / (AmE), / eɪˈtɪpɪk / (BrE)
not representative of a group, class, or type; deviating from the norm or standard.
not typical; unrepresentative

Examples

The patient's symptoms were considered atypic and puzzled the medical team.
Researchers discovered an atypic variant of the plant in the wild.
The artwork stood out for its atypic use of color and shape.

Synset Examples

a class of atypical mosses
atypical behavior is not the accepted type of response that we expect from children

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atypical - not representative of a type, group, or class

Etymology

From Greek a- (not) + typikos (typical).
