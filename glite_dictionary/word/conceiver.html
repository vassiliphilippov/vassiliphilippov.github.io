conceiver

1. NOUN

/ kənˈsiːvər / (AmE), / kənˈsiːvə / (BrE)
a person who invents or originates new ideas, plans, or things
person who invents or originates ideas

Examples

As the conceiver of the project, she was responsible for its overall vision.
Many great artists are not only skilled craftsmen but also brilliant conceivers of new concepts.
The conceiver of the theory spent years developing the foundational ideas behind it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creator - someone who brings something into existence
  • Synonym: originator - someone who initiates or originates something
  • Synonym: inventor - a person who creates or designs new things

Etymology

From conceive + -er, formed from Latin 'concipere' (to take in, devise) with agent suffix '-er'.
