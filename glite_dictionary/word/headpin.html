headpin

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌpɪn / (BrE)
the front pin in the triangular arrangement of ten pins in bowling
front bowling pin in ten-pin arrangement

Examples

If you miss the headpin, it's much harder to get a strike.
The headpin was knocked down by the first ball.
He aimed carefully and hit the headpin straight on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kingpin - the central pin in bowling, often the headpin
