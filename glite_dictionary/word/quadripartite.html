quadripartite

1. ADJ

/ ˌkwɑdrɪˈpɑrˌtaɪt / (AmE), / ˌkwɒdrɪˈpɑːtaɪt / (BrE)
involving or composed of four parties, groups, or parts
involving four parties or parts

Examples

A quadripartite agreement was reached between the four nations.
The commission is governed by a quadripartite structure including government, industry, labor, and environmental groups.
They established a quadripartite task force to manage the project.

Etymology

From Latin 'quadri-' (four) + 'partitus' (divided, parted).
