Khomeini

1. PROPN

/ xoʊˈmeɪni / (AmE), / xəʊˈmeɪni / (BrE)
(1900–1989) Iranian religious and political leader who led the 1979 Iranian Revolution and became the Supreme Leader of Iran.
Iranian leader (1900–1989), led 1979 revolution

Examples

Khomeini returned to Iran from exile in 1979 to lead the revolution against the Shah.
The policies introduced by Khomeini shaped the future of the Islamic Republic of Iran.
Many Iranians viewed Khomeini as both a spiritual and political authority.

Etymology

From Persian خُمِینی‎ (Xomeynī), referring to his birthplace, Khomeyn.
