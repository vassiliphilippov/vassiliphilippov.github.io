liposuction

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɪpoʊˈsʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌlɪpəʊˈsʌkʃən / (BrE)
a cosmetic surgical procedure in which unwanted fat is removed from beneath the skin by suction
cosmetic surgical fat removal
medical

Examples

She considered getting liposuction to remove stubborn fat from her abdomen.
The clinic offers advanced liposuction techniques to help patients improve their appearance.
After liposuction, patients are usually advised to rest for several days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lipectomy - surgical removal of fatty tissue

Etymology

From Greek 'lipos' (fat) + English 'suction'.
