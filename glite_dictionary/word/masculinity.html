masculinity

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæskjəˈlɪnəti / (AmE), / ˌmæskjʊˈlɪnɪti / (BrE)
The qualities, characteristics, or behaviors traditionally associated with men.
qualities or behaviors typical of men

Examples

Traditional cultures often value masculinity as a sign of strength and authority.
He questioned the narrow definitions of masculinity in modern society.
Sports are sometimes linked to ideals of masculinity in popular media.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: femininity - the qualities or behaviors considered typical of women

Etymology

From Latin masculinus (male, masculine) + -ity, denoting a quality or state.

2. NOUN

/ ˌmæskjəˈlɪnəti / (AmE), / ˌmæskjʊˈlɪnəti / (BrE)
qualities or attributes traditionally associated with men or the male sex, such as strength, assertiveness, or vigor.
qualities traditionally associated with men

Examples

The novel explores different aspects of masculinity in modern society.
Many cultures have their own definitions of masculinity and what it means to be a man.
He challenged traditional notions of masculinity by expressing his emotions openly.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: femininity - qualities or attributes traditionally associated with women or the female sex

Etymology

From Latin masculinitas, from masculinus (male, masculine).
