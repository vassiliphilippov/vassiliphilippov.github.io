beadlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈbiːd.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈbiːd.laɪk / (BrE)
Small, round, and shiny in appearance, resembling a bead.
resembling a small, shiny bead

Examples

She gazed at him with beadlike eyes that sparkled in the dim light.
Tiny beadlike droplets formed on the leaves after the rain.
The artist decorated the dress with beadlike buttons down the front.

Synset Examples

bright beady eyes
black buttony eyes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beady - having small, round, shiny eyes or features
