to counter-drill

1. VERB

/ ˈkaʊn.tərˌdrɪl / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəˌdrɪl / (BrE)
to drill in the opposite direction, often in relation to a previous drilling operation
drill in the opposite direction

Examples

If you accidentally drill too deep, you can counter-drill to correct the angle.
The repair manual suggests counter-drilling before inserting the new screws.
He counter-drilled the hole to reverse the initial drilling mistake.
