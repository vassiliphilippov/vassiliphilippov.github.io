shifter

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪftər / (AmE), / ˈʃɪftə / (BrE)
a mechanical device used to engage and disengage gears in a motor vehicle or machine.
gear-changing device in a vehicle

Examples

He reached for the shifter to put the car into drive.
The classic car had a chrome shifter mounted on the steering column.
Some cyclists prefer a thumb shifter for quick gear changes.

Synset Examples

in Britain they call a gearshift a ‘gear lever’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gearshift - a device for changing gears in a vehicle
  • Synonym: gear lever - the British term for a device for changing gears in a vehicle

2. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪftər / (AmE), / ˈʃɪftə / (BrE)
a stagehand whose job is to move scenery during a theatrical production
stagehand who moves scenery

Examples

The shifter quickly removed the backdrop during the scene change.
Each shifter must know the schedule for moving props.
Without an experienced shifter, the set changes would take much longer.
