houseboat

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs.bəʊt / (BrE)
A boat designed or equipped for people to live in, used as a dwelling on water.
boat used as a dwelling

Examples

They decided to live on a houseboat on the river for the summer.
The houseboat had all the comforts of a small apartment.
Tourists can rent a houseboat for a unique vacation experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barge - A flat-bottomed boat for carrying goods, sometimes used as a houseboat

Etymology

Formed from 'house' + 'boat', indicating a boat that serves as a house.
