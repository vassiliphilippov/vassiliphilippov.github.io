umbrellawort

1. NOUN

/ ʌmˈbɹɛləˌwɔɹt / (AmE), / ʌmˈbɹɛləˌwɔːt / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Mirabilis, known for its often umbrella-like clusters of flowers.
plant of the genus Mirabilis

Examples

The vibrant flowers of the umbrellawort add color to many gardens in summer.
Researchers studied the medicinal properties of the local umbrellawort species.
She identified the plant as an umbrellawort by its distinctive flower clusters.

Etymology

Formed from 'umbrella' + 'wort' (from Old English 'wyrt', meaning 'plant' or 'herb').
