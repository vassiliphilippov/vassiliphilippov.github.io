ben

1. NOUN

/ bɛn / (AmE), / bɛn / (BrE)
(Scottish) A mountain or tall hill, especially used in place names.
Scottish mountain or tall hill
british

Examples

Many hikers want to climb a ben when visiting the Highlands.
The view from the top of the ben was breathtaking on a clear day.
Ben Nevis is the highest ben in Scotland.
