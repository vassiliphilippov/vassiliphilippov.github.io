counsellorship

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊn.sə.lɚˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.səl.əˌʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or office of a counselor
position of a counselor

Examples

She was offered the counsellorship at the school after several years as a teacher.
His counsellorship in the organization requires strong communication skills and empathy.
The university established a new counsellorship to better support international students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: counselor - a person trained to give guidance on personal or psychological problems

Etymology

From 'counselor' + '-ship' (suffix denoting status, office, or condition).
