attested

1. ADJ

/ əˈtɛstɪd / (AmE), / əˈtɛstɪd / (BrE)
recognized as genuine or authentic; proven to be true or valid, typically by evidence or official confirmation
established as genuine; proven authentic

Examples

The manuscript is an attested document dating back to the fifteenth century.
Only attested facts were admitted as evidence in court.
Her claim to the property is attested by several official records.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: authenticated - proved or shown to be true or genuine
  • Antonym: unverified - not confirmed as true or authentic

Etymology

From attest + -ed; ultimately from Latin attestari (to bear witness).
