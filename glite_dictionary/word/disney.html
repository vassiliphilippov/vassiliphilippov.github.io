Disney

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɪzni / (AmE), / ˈdɪzni / (BrE)
Walt Disney (1901–1966), American film producer and pioneer of animated cartoons, known for creating characters such as Mickey Mouse and founding Disneyland.
Walt Disney, American film producer and animator

Examples

Disney created many beloved cartoon characters, including Mickey Mouse and Donald Duck.
The influence of Disney on the animation industry is still felt today.
Many families dream of visiting Disney's most famous creation, Disneyland.
