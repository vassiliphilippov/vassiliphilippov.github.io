sage-green

1. ADJ

/ seɪdʒ ˈɡrin / (AmE), / seɪdʒ ˈɡriːn / (BrE)
of a grey-green color resembling that of sage leaves
grey-green color like sage leaves

Examples

She painted the kitchen walls a soothing sage-green for a calm, natural look.
His new shirt is a stylish sage-green that goes well with khakis.
The decorator suggested sage-green curtains to bring a touch of nature indoors.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: forest green - a dark green color
  • Degree Lower: mint green - a pale green color
