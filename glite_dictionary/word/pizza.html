pizza

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːtsə / (AmE), / ˈpiːtsə / (BrE)
An Italian dish made from a flat, round base of baked dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various other ingredients such as meats and vegetables.
Italian baked dish with toppings

Examples

We ordered a large pizza with pepperoni and mushrooms for dinner.
The aroma of freshly baked pizza filled the entire restaurant.
She prefers to make her own pizza at home with fresh ingredients.

Etymology

From Italian 'pizza', meaning 'pie'.
