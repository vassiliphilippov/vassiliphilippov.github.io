retrogaming

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛtroʊˌɡeɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛtrəʊˌɡeɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
The hobby of playing or collecting older video games and computer games, typically from previous decades.
playing or collecting older video games

Examples

Many enthusiasts have turned retrogaming into a passionate and rewarding hobby.
Shops that specialize in retrogaming often carry rare and vintage consoles.
She enjoys retrogaming because it reminds her of her childhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retro - style or fashion from the recent past

Etymology

Formed from 'retro-' (referring to the past) + 'gaming'.
