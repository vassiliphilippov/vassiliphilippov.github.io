humanitarian

1. ADJ

/ hjuˌmænɪˈtɛriən / (AmE), / hjuːˌmanɪˈteəriən / (BrE)
concerned with or seeking to promote human welfare and social reform; motivated by compassion and a desire to help others
concerned with promoting human welfare

Examples

Many charities are driven by humanitarian principles rather than profit.
The flood victims received humanitarian aid from several countries.
She took a humanitarian approach to the refugee crisis, advocating for their rights.

Synset Examples

released the prisoner for humanitarian reasons
respect and humanistic regard for all members of our species

Related Terms

  • Synonym: altruistic - showing a selfless concern for the well-being of others

Etymology

From French *humanitaire*, from Latin *humanitas* (humanity).

2. NOUN

/ hjuˌmænɪˈtɛriən / (AmE), / hjuːˌmænɪˈteəriən / (BrE)
A person who is concerned with promoting human welfare and social reform, especially through charitable activities or advocacy.
person who promotes human welfare

Examples

The humanitarian organized food drives for families affected by the disaster.
Many humanitarians dedicate their lives to improving global health and education.
She was recognized as a leading humanitarian for her work with refugees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: philanthropist - a person who seeks to promote the welfare of others, especially by generous donation
