Arabian

1. ADJ

/ əˈreɪ.bi.ən / (AmE), / əˈreɪ.bi.ən / (BrE)
of or relating to the Arabian breed of horse, known for its endurance, high tail carriage, and distinctive head shape.
relating to Arabian horses

Examples

She rides an Arabian stallion at the equestrian club.
The Arabian breed is famous for its stamina and elegance.
Many horse shows feature competitions just for Arabian horses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Arabian horse - a breed of horse renowned for endurance and refined features
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03055976-a
Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ əˈreɪ.bi.ən / (AmE), / əˈreɪ.bi.ən / (BrE)
a breed of horse known for its spirited, graceful, and intelligent nature, originally from the Arabian Peninsula
a breed of horse from Arabia

Examples

The Arabian is prized all over the world for its endurance and beauty.
She rode her favorite Arabian across the open fields at dusk.
Many horse shows feature an Arabian class to celebrate the breed's elegance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Arabian horse - a horse of the Arabian breed
  • Antonym: thoroughbred - a breed of horse developed for racing, different from Arabian

Etymology

From Arabia, the historical region of the Arabian Peninsula, indicating the horse's origin.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02382560-n
Synset POS: n

3. NOUN

/ əˈreɪ.bi.ən / (AmE), / əˈreɪ.bi.ən / (BrE)
a member of a Semitic people originally from the Arabian Peninsula and surrounding regions, especially one who speaks Arabic and inhabits the Middle East or North Africa
a person from the Arabian Peninsula or nearby regions

Examples

The ancient Arabians played a key role in the development of early mathematics and astronomy.
As an Arabian, he retains strong ties to his family's heritage in the region.
Many Arabians travel to celebrate Eid with their relatives across the Middle East.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Arab - a member of a Semitic people inhabiting much of the Middle East and North Africa

Etymology

From Latin Arabicus, from Greek Arabikos, from Arabia (Arabian Peninsula).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09748872-n
Synset POS: n

4. ADJ

/ əˈreɪ.bi.ən / (AmE), / əˈreɪ.bi.ən / (BrE)
relating to Arabia or its people, language, or culture.
of or relating to Arabia

Examples

The legendary Arabian horses are prized for their speed and endurance.
We sailed across the Arabian Sea during our trip to India.
The Arabian desert is one of the largest deserts in the world.

Synset Examples

Arabian Sea

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Arab - relating to the Arabs (sometimes used in place of 'Arabian')
