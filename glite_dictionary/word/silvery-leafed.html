silvery-leafed

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪlvəri liːft / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəri liːft / (BrE)
(of a plant) having leaves that are silvery in color or appearance.
having silvery leaves

Examples

The silvery-leafed sage caught the morning sunlight in the garden.
Some gardeners prefer silvery-leafed plants to add contrast among greener foliage.
This region is known for its silvery-leafed eucalyptus trees.
