congo

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
a type of black tea grown in China
Chinese black tea

Examples

She preferred a strong cup of congo in the morning to wake her up.
The merchant offered several varieties of congo at the market.
Historically, congo was prized in England for its rich flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Congou - a type of Chinese black tea, historically romanized as 'congo'

Etymology

Variant of 'Congo', a historical anglicized spelling of Chinese 'Congou', from Mandarin 'gōngfu' (工夫), referring to 'skill' or 'labor' in tea making.
