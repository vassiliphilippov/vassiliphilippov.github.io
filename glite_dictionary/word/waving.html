waving

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈweɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of moving your hand to signal or greet someone or attract attention
the act of moving the hand to signal

Examples

The waving of her hand told us she had seen us.
The excited waving from the crowd greeted the winning team.
His waving was meant to get the driver's attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wave - to move one's hand back and forth as a signal or greeting

Etymology

From the verb 'wave', from Old English 'wafian' (to wave with the hand).
