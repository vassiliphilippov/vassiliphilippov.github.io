oily

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔɪli / (AmE), / ˈɔɪli / (BrE)
Covered with or containing oil; having the appearance or texture of oil.
covered with or containing oil

Examples

The mechanic wiped his oily hands with a rag before shaking mine.
She avoided walking through the oily puddles after the rain.
