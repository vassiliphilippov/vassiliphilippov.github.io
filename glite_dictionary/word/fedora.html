fedora

1. NOUN

/ fəˈdɔrə / (AmE), / fɪˈdɔːrə / (BrE)
A soft felt hat with a lengthwise crease along the crown and a brim, commonly worn by men.
a felt hat with a creased crown

Examples

He wore a stylish fedora tilted jauntily on his head.
She bought a vintage fedora at the flea market for her collection.
A classic fedora can add flair to any outfit.

Etymology

From the name of the play 'Fédora' (1882) by Victorien Sardou, in which the heroine wore such a hat.
