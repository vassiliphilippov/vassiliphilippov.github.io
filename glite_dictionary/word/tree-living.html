tree-living

1. ADJ

/ ˈtriˌlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtriːˌlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an animal or organism) inhabiting or spending most of its life in trees.
living in or inhabiting trees

Examples

Many monkeys are tree-living creatures, spending most of their lives among the branches.
Koalas are tree-living marsupials native to Australia, rarely descending to the ground.
Scientists study tree-living insects to understand the diversity of forest ecosystems.

Synset Examples

arboreal apes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arboreal - (of animals) living in trees
