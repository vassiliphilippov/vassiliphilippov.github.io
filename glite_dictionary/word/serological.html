serological

1. ADJ

/ sɛr.əˈlɑ.dʒɪ.kəl / (AmE), / sɪə.rəˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving serology, the scientific study or diagnostic examination of blood serum, especially with regard to the response of the immune system to pathogens or introduced substances.
relating to serology (blood serum study)
medical

Examples

The diagnosis was confirmed through serological testing for antibodies.
Recent outbreaks have highlighted the importance of serological surveys in tracking infections.
The lab specializes in serological analysis to detect various diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: serology - the scientific study or diagnostic examination of blood serum

Etymology

From 'serology' + '-ical'; ultimately from Latin 'serum' (whey) and Greek 'logos' (study).
