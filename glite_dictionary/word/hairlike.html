hairlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛrˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈheəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
very thin and slender, resembling a strand of hair in appearance or shape
very thin and slender, like hair

Examples

The plant’s roots spread out in hairlike filaments through the soil.
Tiny hairlike structures cover the surface of the leaf.
Scientists examined the hairlike fibers under a microscope.

Synset Examples

a capillary tube

Related Terms

  • Synonym: capillary - having a very small internal diameter; like a hair
