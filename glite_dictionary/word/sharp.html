5. NOUN

/ ʃɑːrp / (AmE), / ʃɑːp / (BrE)
a musical symbol (♯) indicating that the note should be played one semitone higher in pitch.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: flat - musical symbol (♭) indicating a note is to be played one semitone lower
