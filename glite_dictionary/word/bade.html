Bade

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːdeɪ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in northern Nigeria, particularly in Yobe, Jigawa, and Bauchi States
a Chadic language of northern Nigeria

Examples

The Bade language is spoken by thousands in northeastern Nigeria.
Linguists have documented many traditional stories in Bade.
She is fluent in both Hausa and Bade.
