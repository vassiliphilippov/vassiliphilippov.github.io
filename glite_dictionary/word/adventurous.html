adventurous

1. ADJ

/ ədˈvɛntʃərəs / (AmE), / ədˈvɛntʃərəs / (BrE)
Willing to take risks or try out new, exciting experiences or activities.
willing to take risks or seek new experiences

Examples

Her adventurous spirit led her to explore remote parts of the world.
If you're feeling adventurous, you should try the spicy curry.
The company encouraged adventurous thinking to drive innovation.

Synset Examples

adventurous pioneers
the risks and gains of an adventuresome economy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: daring - willing to take risks; bold
  • Antonym: cautious - not willing to take risks; careful

Etymology

From adventure + -ous, with adventure from Old French aventure.
