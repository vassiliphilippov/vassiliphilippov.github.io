East Pakistani

1. ADJ

/ iːst pɑːˈkɪstɑːni / (AmE), / iːst pækɪˈstɑːni / (BrE)
relating to the former East Pakistan (now Bangladesh), its people, or its language; often used in a historical context before Bangladesh's independence in 1971.
relating to former East Pakistan (Bangladesh)

Examples

Many refugees fled from East Pakistani regions during the 1971 conflict.
The East Pakistani dialects differ significantly from those in the west.
Historical records mention the contributions of East Pakistani intellectuals before independence.

Synset Examples

Bangladeshi dialects

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bangladeshi - relating to Bangladesh or its people

Etymology

Formed from 'East Pakistan' (the region that became Bangladesh in 1971) and the suffix '-i' denoting nationality or relation.
