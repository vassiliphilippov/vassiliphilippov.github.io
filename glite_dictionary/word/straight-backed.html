straight-backed

1. ADJ

/ streɪt bækt / (AmE), / streɪt bækt / (BrE)
having a straight back, especially used to describe chairs or a person with upright posture
having a straight back

Examples

He chose a straight-backed chair for better posture at his desk.
The straight-backed dancer moved gracefully across the stage.
She admired the elegant, straight-backed furniture in the old house.

Synset Examples

a straight-backed wooden chair
a slim straight-backed dancer
