yogistic

1. ADJ

/ joʊˈɡɪstɪk / (AmE), / jəʊˈɡɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to yoga, its practices, or its philosophy.
of or relating to yoga

Examples

She developed a yogistic routine to improve her flexibility and reduce stress.
The seminar explored yogistic approaches to mindfulness and meditation.
His lifestyle reflected a yogistic philosophy, emphasizing balance and self-awareness.

Synset Examples

yogic exercises

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yogic - relating to yoga or its practices

Etymology

Formed from 'yoga' + '-istic', a suffix forming adjectives from nouns.
