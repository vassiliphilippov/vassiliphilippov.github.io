maxillomandibular

1. ADJ

/ ˌmæksɪloʊˌmændɪˈbjulɚ / (AmE), / ˌmæksɪləʊˌmændɪˈbjuːlə / (BrE)
relating to the upper jaw (maxilla) and lower jaw (mandible), especially in anatomy or medicine
relating to upper and lower jaws
medical

Examples

The surgeon performed a maxillomandibular fixation to stabilize the patient's fractured jaw.
A maxillomandibular relationship is important in orthodontic treatments.
Radiographs showed normal maxillomandibular alignment after the procedure.

Etymology

From Latin 'maxilla' (jawbone) and 'mandibula' (lower jaw).
