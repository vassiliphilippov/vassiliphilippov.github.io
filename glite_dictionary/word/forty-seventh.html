forty-seventh

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɔr.tiˈsɛv.ənθ / (AmE), / ˌfɔː.tiˈsɛv.ənθ / (BrE)
(ordinal number) relating to the position in a sequence that is number forty-seven; coming after forty-sixth and before forty-eighth.
ordinal number for 47th place

Examples

She finished in forty-seventh place out of a hundred runners.
This is the forty-seventh edition of the annual festival.
He lives on the forty-seventh floor of the skyscraper.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty-sixth - preceding ordinal in sequence, 46th
  • Degree Higher: forty-eighth - following ordinal in sequence, 48th
