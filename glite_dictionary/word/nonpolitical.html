nonpolitical

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.pəˈlɪt.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.pəˈlɪt.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
Not connected with or related to politics or political matters.
not related to politics

Examples

The committee is strictly nonpolitical and focuses only on community issues.
She preferred to keep her work environment nonpolitical and free from debates on government policy.
Many charities make a point of being nonpolitical so they can help all groups equally.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: political - connected with politics or government

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (meaning 'not') and 'political'.
