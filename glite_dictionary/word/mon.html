Mon

1. NOUN

/ mɑn / (AmE), / mɒn / (BrE)
the Austroasiatic language spoken by the Mon people of Myanmar and Thailand
language of the Mon people

Examples

She is studying Mon as part of her linguistic research.
Many older inscriptions in Myanmar are written in Mon.
The preservation of the Mon language is important for cultural heritage.

Etymology

From Burmese မန် (man), referring to the Mon people and their language.

2. NOUN

/ mɑn / (AmE), / mɒn / (BrE)
A member of a Buddhist ethnic group native to Myanmar and adjacent parts of Thailand.
member of a Southeast Asian ethnic group

Examples

The Mon have preserved their cultural traditions for centuries despite influences from neighboring groups.
A community of Mon settled along the river in Thailand.
She is proud of her Mon heritage and often visits her ancestral village.

Etymology

From the Mon language and Burmese မုန် (mun), referring to the people.

3. NOUN

/ mʌn / (AmE), / mʌn / (BrE)
An abbreviation for Monday, the second day of the week and the first day of the standard working week in many countries.
abbreviation for Monday

Examples

She has a dentist appointment scheduled for Mon at 10 a.m.
The office will be closed on Mon due to the holiday.
I usually feel tired on Mon mornings after the weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Monday - the second day of the week; the day following Sunday
