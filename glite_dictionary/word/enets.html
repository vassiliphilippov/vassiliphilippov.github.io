Enets

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛnɛts / (AmE), / ˈɛnɛts / (BrE)
the Uralic language spoken by the Enets people in northern Siberia, Russia.
a Uralic language of Siberia

Examples

The Enets language is considered endangered, with only a few dozen native speakers remaining.
Researchers recorded folk tales in Enets to preserve the cultural heritage of the community.
He is studying Enets to better understand the linguistic diversity of northern Asia.
