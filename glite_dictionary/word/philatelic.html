philatelic

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɪləˈtɛlɪk / (AmE), / ˌfɪləˈtɛlɪk / (BrE)
relating to the collection and study of postage stamps and related items
relating to stamp collecting

Examples

The museum's new exhibit features several rare philatelic treasures from around the world.
He has a philatelic collection that includes stamps issued in the nineteenth century.
Their annual conference attracts enthusiasts and experts from the global philatelic community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: philately - the collection and study of postage stamps

Etymology

Formed from 'philately' (French philatélie, from Greek philo- 'loving' and atelos 'without tax').
