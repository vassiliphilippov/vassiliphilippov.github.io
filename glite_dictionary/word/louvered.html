louvered

1. ADJ

/ ˈluːvɚd / (AmE), / ˈluːvəd / (BrE)
fitted with louvers, or slats, usually to allow air or light to pass through while keeping out rain or direct sun
fitted with angled slats or louvers

Examples

The house had beautiful louvered shutters on every window.
We replaced the old closet door with a louvered one to improve airflow.
The mechanic recommended installing a louvered vent on the roof for better ventilation.

Synset Examples

a louvered door

Related Terms

  • Synonym: louver - an angled slat or set of slats for ventilation or privacy

Etymology

From 'louver', from Old French 'lover', a turret or structure with slats for ventilation.
