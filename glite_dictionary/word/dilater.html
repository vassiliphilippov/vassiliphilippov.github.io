dilater

1. NOUN

/ daɪˈleɪtər / (AmE), / daɪˈleɪtə / (BrE)
a surgical instrument used to dilate or widen an opening or an organ
instrument for dilating openings/organs
medical

Examples

The surgeon selected a dilater to gently widen the narrowed passage.
After sterilizing the dilater, the doctor proceeded with the procedure.
Various sizes of dilater are available to accommodate different medical needs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dilator - a surgical instrument for dilating a bodily passage or cavity

Etymology

From Latin 'dilatare' (to widen, enlarge).
