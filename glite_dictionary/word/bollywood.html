Bollywood

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑliˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈbɒliˌwʊd / (BrE)
the Hindi-language film industry of India, based in Mumbai
Indian Hindi-language film industry

Examples

Many international audiences are now familiar with Bollywood films and stars.
He dreams of making it big in Bollywood one day.
Bollywood is known for its colorful music and dramatic storytelling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hollywood - the American film industry based in Los Angeles
  • Synonym: Tollywood - the Telugu or Bengali film industry in India

Etymology

Blend of 'Bombay' (now Mumbai) and 'Hollywood'.
