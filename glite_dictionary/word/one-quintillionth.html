one-quintillionth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn kwɪnˈtɪljənθ / (AmE), / wʌn kwɪnˈtɪljənθ / (BrE)
one part of a quintillion equal parts; the fraction represented by 1 divided by 1,000,000,000,000,000,000
one part in a quintillion

Examples

The amount of hydrogen in that tiny droplet is less than one-quintillionth of the total Earth's supply.
Her chances of winning the lottery were about one-quintillionth, making it almost impossible.
Even one-quintillionth of a second can be significant in particle physics experiments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quintillionth - one of one-quintillion equal parts
