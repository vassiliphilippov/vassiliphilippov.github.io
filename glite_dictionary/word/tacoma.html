Tacoma

1. PROPN

/ təˈkoʊmə / (AmE), / təˈkəʊmə / (BrE)
A city in west central Washington State, USA, located on an arm of Puget Sound to the south of Seattle.
city in Washington State, USA

Examples

She moved to Tacoma to start a new job at the university.
The view of Mount Rainier from Tacoma is truly stunning.
After visiting Seattle, we spent a weekend in Tacoma exploring the museums.

Etymology

Named after the nearby Mount Tacoma (now Mount Rainier), possibly from the Lushootseed Native American word təqʷúʔbə.
