reachable

1. ADJ

/ ˈriːtʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈriːtʃəbl / (BrE)
able to be approached or entered without difficulty; easy to get to.
easily approached or accessed

Examples

The summit is only reachable by a narrow mountain path.
Is this island reachable by boat during high tide?
The conference center is easily reachable from the main highway.

Synset Examples

a site approachable from a branch of the Niger

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inaccessible - impossible or hard to reach
  • Synonym: approachable - easy to approach or access

Etymology

From 'reach' + '-able', suffix forming adjectives meaning 'able to be'.
