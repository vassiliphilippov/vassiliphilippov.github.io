stripping

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstrɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of removing a covering or layer from something
removal of a covering or layer

Examples

The stripping of old paint from the door took several hours.
After the stripping of the wallpaper, the room looked bare and empty.
A complete stripping of insulation was necessary before replacing the wire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: removal - the act of taking something away

Etymology

From the verb 'strip', of Germanic origin, meaning 'to remove coverings'.
