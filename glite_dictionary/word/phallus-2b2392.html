phallus

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæləs / (AmE), / ˈfal.əs / (BrE)
the penis, especially as a symbol of male sexual power or fertility
the penis; male sex organ

Examples

In many ancient cultures, a phallus was used as a symbol of fertility and protection.
The statue was carved in the shape of a phallus to honor the god of fertility.
He explained that the word phallus refers to the male sex organ in biological terms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: penis - the male genital organ

Etymology

From Greek phallos (penis).
