mirrored

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɪrərd / (AmE), / ˈmɪrəd / (BrE)
having the appearance of a mirror image; exactly corresponding in position or arrangement as if reflected in a mirror.
like a mirror image

Examples

The dancers executed each move in mirrored perfection across the stage.
Her room had a large mirrored wardrobe that made the space seem bigger.
The logo included a mirrored letter design for a striking effect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reflected - thrown back from a surface; mirrored
