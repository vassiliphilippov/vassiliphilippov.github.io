daiquiri

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪkəri / (AmE), / ˈdækəri / (BrE)
a cocktail made with rum, lime or lemon juice, and sometimes sugar, served shaken and chilled
rum-based cocktail with citrus juice

Examples

I ordered a daiquiri by the pool to cool off in the afternoon sun.
Her favorite drink is a strawberry daiquiri when she goes out with friends.
The bartender showed us how to make a classic daiquiri at home.

Etymology

Named after Daiquirí, a village near Santiago de Cuba, where the cocktail was invented.
