antitumour

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.tiˈtu.mɚ / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈtjuː.mə / (BrE)
(of a substance or treatment) effective against tumors or used in the treatment of cancer.
effective against tumors
medical

Examples

The new drug has shown significant antitumour activity in laboratory studies.
Researchers are studying several antitumour compounds for potential use in chemotherapy.

Related Terms

Etymology

From Greek anti- (against) + tumour (swelling, mass).
