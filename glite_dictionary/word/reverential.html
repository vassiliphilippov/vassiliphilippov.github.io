reverential

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɛvəˈrɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛvəˈrɛnʃəl / (BrE)
showing or expressing deep respect and awe; characterized by veneration.
showing deep respect or veneration

Examples

The choir performed the hymn in a reverential manner that moved the congregation.
She spoke in a reverential tone when discussing her mentor.
There was a reverential silence as the historian entered the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respectful - showing respect
  • Antonym: irreverent - showing a lack of respect or veneration

Etymology

From Latin reverentia (awe, respect) via Late Middle English.
