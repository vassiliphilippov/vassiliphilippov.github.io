jenny

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛni / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛni / (BrE)
A female donkey.
female donkey

Examples

The farmer introduced the new jenny to the herd, hoping she would mate with the jack.
It is easier to train a jenny than a wild male donkey.
The jenny brayed loudly when separated from her foal.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: jack - male donkey

Etymology

Diminutive of the given name Jenny, originally used for female animals (compare Jack for males).
