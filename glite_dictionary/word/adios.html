adios

1. NOUN

/ ɑːˈdiːoʊs / (AmE), / ˌæd.iˈɒs / (BrE)
used to say goodbye; a farewell remark
expression of farewell

Examples

With a wave and a smile, she said adios to her friends at the station.
After finishing his work, he muttered a quick adios and left the office.
The last scene showed the cowboy tipping his hat and whispering adios.

Synset Examples

they said their good-byes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - an expression used to bid farewell
  • Antonym: hello - an expression used to greet someone

Etymology

From Spanish 'adiós', meaning 'to God', used as a parting phrase.
