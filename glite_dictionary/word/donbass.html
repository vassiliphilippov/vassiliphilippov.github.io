Donbass

1. PROPN

/ dɑnˈbɑs / (AmE), / dɒnˈbɑs / (BrE)
an industrial and mining region in eastern Ukraine, known for its coal production and heavy industry
industrial region in eastern Ukraine

Examples

The Donbass region has long been a center of coal mining in Ukraine.
Conflict in the Donbass has affected millions of residents since 2014.
Many factories in the Donbass contributed significantly to the Soviet industrial output.

Etymology

From Russian Донба́сс (Donbáss), an abbreviation of Донецкий бассейн (Donetsky basseyn, 'Donets Basin').
