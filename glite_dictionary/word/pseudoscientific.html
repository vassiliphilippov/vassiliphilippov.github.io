pseudoscientific

1. ADJ

/ ˌsuːdoʊˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪk / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəʊˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪk / (BrE)
Based on ideas, methods, or practices mistakenly regarded as being scientific.
falsely claimed to be scientific

Examples

Many alternative medicine treatments are criticized as being pseudoscientific and lacking empirical support.
The author dismisses astrology as a pseudoscientific belief rather than a true science.
His argument sounded pseudoscientific, relying on complex terms without real evidence.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: scientific - based on or characterized by the methods and principles of science

Etymology

Formed from pseudo- (false) and scientific.
