bubble-head

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌbəlˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈbʌbəlˌhɛd / (BrE)
an informal term for a silly or foolish person
a silly or foolish person (informal)
informal

Examples

Don't be a bubble-head; think critically about the issue.
He's such a bubble-head when it comes to serious topics.
Stop being a bubble-head and focus on your work.
