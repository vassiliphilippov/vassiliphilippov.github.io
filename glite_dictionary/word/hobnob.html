to hobnob

1. VERB

/ ˈhɑb.nɑb / (AmE), / ˈhɒb.nɒb / (BrE)
to mix socially, especially with people of higher status or influence
socialize with influential people

Examples

He loves to hobnob with celebrities at exclusive parties.
During the conference, she hobnobbed with industry leaders and innovators.
Journalists often hobnob with politicians to gain inside information.

Synset Examples

He hobnobs with the best of society

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to socialize - to interact with others in a friendly way
  • Synonym: to mingle - to move among and engage with others at a social event

Etymology

From earlier hob and nob (to drink together), probably from archaic to-and-fro words to mean 'give and take'.
