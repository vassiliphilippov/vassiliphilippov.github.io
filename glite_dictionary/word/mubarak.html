Mubarak

1. PROPN

/ muˈbɑːrək / (AmE), / mjuˈbærək / (BrE)
Hosni Mubarak (1928–2020), Egyptian politician and military officer who served as President of Egypt from 1981 to 2011.
Egyptian president (1981–2011)

Examples

Mubarak led Egypt for almost three decades before stepping down during the 2011 Arab Spring.
Many Egyptians have differing opinions on the legacy of President Mubarak.
The resignation of Mubarak marked a turning point in Egypt's modern history.
