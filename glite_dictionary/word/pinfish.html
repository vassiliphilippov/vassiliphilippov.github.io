pinfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪnˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a small spiny-finned fish (Lagodon rhomboides) found in bays along the southeastern coast of the United States, similar to the sea bream
small spiny-finned fish of the southeastern US

Examples

We caught several pinfish while fishing off the Florida coast.
The aquarium displayed a colorful pinfish among other native species.
Young anglers often use pinfish as bait to catch larger game fish.
