to declaw

1. VERB

/ diˈklɔ / (AmE), / diˈklɔː / (BrE)
to remove the claws from an animal, especially a cat
remove the claws from an animal

Examples

The veterinarian refused to declaw healthy cats without medical necessity.
Some people believe it is inhumane to declaw household pets.
After they decided to declaw the kitten, they regretted their decision.

Synset Examples

declaw a cat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to detrain - remove the fangs from an animal

Etymology

Prefix de- (removal, reversal) + claw (from Middle English clawe).
