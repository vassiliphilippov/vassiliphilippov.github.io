lymphoid

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪmˌfɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪm.fɔɪd / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling lymph or lymphatic tissue, which is involved in the immune system.
relating to lymphatic tissue
medical

Examples

The lymphoid tissue in the spleen helps fight infection.
Scientists discovered abnormal growth in the lymphoid organs of the patient.
The tonsils are considered lymphoid structures within the body.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lymphatic - relating to lymph or the lymphatic system

Etymology

From lymph + -oid (from Greek -oeides, meaning 'form, like').
