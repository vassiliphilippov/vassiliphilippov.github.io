doxology

1. NOUN

/ dɑkˈsɑlədʒi / (AmE), / dɒkˈsɒlədʒi / (BrE)
A short hymn or verse in Christian worship that praises God.
hymn praising God in worship

Examples

The choir sang the doxology at the end of the service.
Many congregations recite a doxology as a final song of praise.
He composed a new doxology for the Easter celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hymn - a religious song of praise

Etymology

From Greek doxologia, from doxa (glory) + -logia (saying).
