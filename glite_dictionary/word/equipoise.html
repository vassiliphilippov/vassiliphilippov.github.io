equipoise

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːkwɪˌpɔɪz / (AmE), / ˈiːkwɪˌpɔɪz / (BrE)
a state of balance or equal distribution of weight, forces, or importance
a state of balance or equality

Examples

During the experiment, researchers maintained equipoise to ensure unbiased results.
A delicate equipoise exists between the powers of the two countries.
She tried to achieve equipoise between her work and personal life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: balance - a condition in which different elements are equal or in the correct proportions
  • Antonym: imbalance - lack of proportion or relation between corresponding things

Etymology

From Latin ‘aequus’ (equal) + ‘poise’ (balance).
