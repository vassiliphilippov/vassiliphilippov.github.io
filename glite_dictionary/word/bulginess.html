bulginess

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌl.dʒi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈbʌl.dʒi.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being bulgy; having a rounded, swollen, or protruding form
the quality of being bulgy or convex

Examples

The bulginess of the pillow made it extra comfortable to rest on.
He tried to hide the bulginess of the package under his coat.
After repairing the tire, there was still a slight bulginess on one side.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulginess - roundness or convexity; synonym for bulginess
  • Antonym: flatness - the quality of being flat or level; antonym of bulginess

Etymology

From bulgy + -ness.
