BuSpar

1. NOUN

/ ˈbjuːˌspɑr / (AmE), / ˈbjuːˌspɑː / (BrE)
A trademarked drug used to treat anxiety disorders, containing the active ingredient buspirone.
anti-anxiety medication (trade name)
medical

Examples

After several months on BuSpar, her anxiety symptoms noticeably diminished.
The doctor prescribed BuSpar as a non-addictive option for managing his condition.
Some patients prefer BuSpar because it has fewer sedative effects than other anxiolytics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buspirone - the generic drug used to treat anxiety disorders
