burred

1. ADJ

/ bɝd / (AmE), / bɜːd / (BrE)
having or covered with burs, barbs, spines, thorns, or similar prickly projections.
covered with burs, spines, or sharp projections

Examples

The burred stem made it difficult to pick the plant with bare hands.
Several animals avoid eating burred fruits because of their sharp projections.
The child's sweater became stuck to the burred leaves during their walk in the woods.

Synset Examples

a horse with a short bristly mane
bristly shrubs
burred fruits
setaceous whiskers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prickly - covered with or full of sharp points

Etymology

From 'bur' (a prickly seed vessel) + '-ed'.
