vocalizing

1. NOUN

/ ˈvoʊ.kəˌlaɪ.zɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvəʊ.kəˌlaɪ.zɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of singing, especially as a vocal exercise or performance.
act of singing or vocal exercise

Examples

Daily vocalizing helps singers strengthen their voices and expand their range.
She spent the afternoon vocalizing scales to prepare for her audition.
Proper vocalizing is essential for maintaining healthy vocal cords.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: singing - the act of producing musical tones with the voice

Etymology

From 'vocalize' (to sing or utter with the voice) + '-ing'.
