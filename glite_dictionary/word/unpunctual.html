unpunctual

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpʌŋktʃuəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈpʌŋktjʊəl / (BrE)
not doing something or arriving at the expected or agreed time; not punctual.
not punctual; late

Examples

His unpunctual arrival frustrated everyone waiting for the meeting.
She is often criticized at work for being unpunctual.
Clients expect their lawyers not to be unpunctual with deadlines.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: punctual - doing something or arriving at the expected time

Etymology

Prefix un- (not) + punctual (from Latin punctualis, from punctum 'point, moment').
