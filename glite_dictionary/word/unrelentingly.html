unrelentingly

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈlɛn.tɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈlɛn.tɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that is persistent and never becoming less intense or severe.
in a relentless, persistent manner

Examples

She pursued her research unrelentingly despite numerous obstacles.
The storm battered the coastline unrelentingly for hours.
Critics were unrelentingly harsh in their reviews of the film.

Synset Examples

he worked relentlessly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relentlessly - in a relentless manner; showing no signs of stopping

Etymology

Formed from 'unrelenting' + '-ly', with 'unrelenting' from un- (not) + relenting (from Latin 'relentare', to soften).
