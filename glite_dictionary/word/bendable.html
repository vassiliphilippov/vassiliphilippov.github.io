bendable

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛndəbəl / (AmE), / ˈbɛndəbəl / (BrE)
capable of being bent or flexed without breaking
able to be bent or flexed

Examples

The pipe is made from a bendable plastic that won't crack under pressure.
These wires are highly bendable, making them perfect for use in intricate electronics.
You need a bendable ruler for this craft project so it can curve around the edges.

Synset Examples

a flexible wire
a pliant young tree

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flexible - able to bend easily without breaking
  • Antonym: rigid - unable to bend or be forced out of shape; not flexible

Etymology

From 'bend' + suffix '-able', meaning 'able to be bent'.
