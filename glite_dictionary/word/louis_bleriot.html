Louis Bleriot

1. PROPN

/ luˈiː bleɪˈrjoʊ / (AmE), / ˈluːi ˈblɛrɪəʊ / (BrE)
(1872–1936) A French aviator who, in 1909, made the first successful flight across the English Channel.
French pioneer aviator, first to fly across English Channel

Examples

Louis Bleriot made history by flying his airplane across the English Channel in 1909.
Many museums feature exhibits dedicated to Louis Bleriot and his pioneering flight.
The achievements of Louis Bleriot inspired a generation of aviators in the early twentieth century.
