affordable

1. ADJ

/ əˈfɔrdəbl̩ / (AmE), / əˈfɔːdəbəl / (BrE)
Inexpensive enough to be bought or used by most people; within one's financial means.
inexpensive; within one's means

Examples

The city is working to make housing more affordable for low-income families.
Many students choose community colleges because tuition is more affordable.
We found several affordable restaurants near the hotel.

Synset Examples

low-cost housing

Related Terms

  • Antonym: expensive - costing a lot of money; not affordable
  • Synonym: cheap - low in price; not expensive or costly

Etymology

From 'afford' + '-able', indicating capability.
