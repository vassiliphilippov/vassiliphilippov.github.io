Sverige

1. PROPN

/ ˈsvɛ.rɪ.ə / (AmE), / ˈsvɛ.rɪ.ə / (BrE)
The Swedish name for Sweden, a Scandinavian country located in the eastern part of the Scandinavian Peninsula.
Swedish name for Sweden

Examples

She spent the summer traveling across Sverige and visiting its historic cities.
The documentary explores the culture and traditions unique to Sverige.
Many international companies have their headquarters in Sverige.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sweden - A Scandinavian country in northern Europe

Etymology

From Swedish 'Sverige', meaning 'Sweden'.
