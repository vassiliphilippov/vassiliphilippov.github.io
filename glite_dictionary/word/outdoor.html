outdoor

1. ADJ

/ ˈaʊtˌdɔr / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌdɔː / (BrE)
located, happening, or used outside rather than inside a building; relating to activities done in the open air.
outside, open-air; not indoors

Examples

Our family enjoys many outdoor activities such as hiking and camping.
The concert will take place in an outdoor arena, so bring a jacket.
He prefers outdoor jobs to working at a desk all day.

Synset Examples

outdoor education is the area of teacher training concerned with training for outdoor activities

Related Terms

  • Antonym: indoor - located, happening, or used inside a building

2. ADJ

/ ˈaʊtˌdɔr / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌdɔː / (BrE)
Located, suited for, or taking place in the open air rather than inside a building.
taking place or used outside

Examples

The festival hosted several outdoor concerts throughout the summer.
We enjoyed an outdoor barbecue with friends in the backyard.
These outdoor activities are perfect for families who love adventure.

Synset Examples

outdoor clothes
badminton and other outdoor games
a beautiful outdoor setting for the wedding

Related Terms

  • Antonym: indoor - located or taking place inside a building

Etymology

From 'out' + 'door', first used to mean 'out of doors' in the mid-19th century.
