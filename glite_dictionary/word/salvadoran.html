Salvadoran

1. NOUN

/ ˌsælvəˈdɔrən / (AmE), / ˌsælvəˈdɔːrən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of El Salvador
person from El Salvador

Examples

She is a proud Salvadoran who moved to the United States ten years ago.
The restaurant is run by a Salvadoran family and serves traditional pupusas.
Many Salvadorans maintain close ties with their relatives back home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: El Salvadoran - a native or inhabitant of El Salvador

Etymology

From El Salvador (country in Central America) + English suffix -an (denoting inhabitant)

2. ADJ

/ ˌsælvəˈdɔɹən / (AmE), / ˌsalvəˈdɔːrən / (BrE)
relating to El Salvador or its people
of El Salvador or its people

Examples

The restaurant is known for its delicious Salvadoran pupusas.
She specializes in Salvadoran folk art and crafts.
Many Salvadoran families settled in the city after the civil war.

Synset Examples

Salvadoran coffee
Salvadoran guerillas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Salvadoran - a person from El Salvador
  • Synonym: Salvadorian - alternative form of Salvadoran

Etymology

From El Salvador plus the English suffix -an, denoting association or origin.
