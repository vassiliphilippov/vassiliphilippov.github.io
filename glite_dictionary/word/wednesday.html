Wednesday

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛnzdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɛnzdeɪ / (BrE)
The day of the week between Tuesday and Thursday, typically considered the fourth day of the week in international standard (ISO 8601).
the day after Tuesday and before Thursday

Examples

On Wednesday, the team meets to discuss their weekly progress.
The museum is closed every Wednesday for maintenance.
Our yoga classes are held on Wednesdays and Fridays.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Tuesday - the day before Wednesday
  • Degree Higher: Thursday - the day after Wednesday

Etymology

From Old English 'Wōdnesdæg', meaning ‘day of Woden’ (the Germanic god Odin), modeled after Latin 'dies Mercurii' (Mercury's day).
