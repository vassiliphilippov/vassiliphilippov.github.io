Romansh

1. NOUN

/ roʊˈmænʃ / (AmE), / rəʊˈmanʃ / (BrE)
the Rhaeto-Romance language spoken in southeastern Switzerland and recognized as an official language of Switzerland
official language of Switzerland; a Rhaeto-Romance language

Examples

Many people in the canton of Grisons speak Romansh as their first language.
Romansh is one of Switzerland's four national languages.
The government provides official documents in German, French, Italian, and Romansh.

Etymology

From Romansh *rumantsch* (meaning 'Romanic').

2. ADJ

/ roʊˈmænʃ / (AmE), / rəʊˈmanʃ / (BrE)
of or relating to the Romansh language, one of the official languages of Switzerland.
relating to the Romansh language

Examples

She is studying Romansh literature for her linguistics degree.
The Romansh community in Switzerland preserves its unique traditions.
Many signs in this region are written in Romansh as well as German and Italian.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Romansh - the language spoken in parts of Switzerland

Etymology

From Romansh *rumantsch*, the name of the language.
