turnverein

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɜrn.vərˌaɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɜːn.vəˌraɪn / (BrE)
A club or association for gymnasts or for promoting physical exercise, especially in German-speaking communities.
gymnastics club or society

Examples

The local turnverein held its annual gymnastics exhibition last weekend.
Many German immigrants joined the city's turnverein to stay active and connect with their community.
As a child, she learned gymnastics routines at the neighborhood turnverein.

Etymology

From German Turnverein, from Turnen (to do gymnastics) + Verein (association, club).
