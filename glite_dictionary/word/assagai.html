assagai

1. NOUN

/ ˈæsəˌɡaɪ / (AmE), / ˈasəɡʌɪ / (BrE)
A slender spear traditionally used by Bantu-speaking peoples of southern Africa.
slender African spear

Examples

The warrior skillfully threw his assagai at the approaching enemy.
Early explorers described the distinctive shape of the assagai in their journals.
The museum displayed a collection of assagais alongside other traditional African weapons.

Etymology

From Afrikaans 'assegai', from Arabic 'az-zaġāyah' (spear), via Spanish and Portuguese.
