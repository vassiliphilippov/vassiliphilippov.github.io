Englishman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃmən / (BrE)
a man who is a native or inhabitant of England
man from England

Examples

He is a proud Englishman who always celebrates national holidays.
As an Englishman, he enjoys a cup of tea every afternoon.
The famous writer was an Englishman born in Yorkshire.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Englishwoman - a woman who is a native or inhabitant of England
  • Synonym: Briton - a person from Britain

Etymology

From English 'English' + 'man'; used since Middle English period.
