Ewenki

1. NOUN

/ ɛˈwɛnki / (AmE), / ɛˈwɛŋki / (BrE)
the Tungusic language spoken by the Evenki people in eastern Siberia
Tungusic language of the Evenki in Siberia

Examples

The Ewenki language is primarily spoken in eastern Siberia and parts of China.
Linguists study Ewenki to better understand the Tungusic language family.
He is fluent in both Russian and Ewenki.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Evenki - alternate spelling for the people and language

Etymology

From the self-designation of the Evenki people.

2. NOUN

/ ɛˈwɛnki / (AmE), / ɛˈwɛŋki / (BrE)
a member of an indigenous people inhabiting areas of northern Mongolia and eastern Siberia
member of a northern Mongolian/Siberian people

Examples

The Ewenki have maintained their traditional reindeer herding practices for generations.
She conducted research among the Ewenki communities in eastern Siberia.
Many Ewenki are bilingual, speaking both their native language and Russian.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Evenki - another spelling of Ewenki, referring to the same people

Etymology

From Russian Эвенки (Evenki), from the self-designation of the people.
