aflicker

1. ADJ

/ əˈflɪkɚ / (AmE), / əˈflɪkə / (BrE)
shining or glowing with an unsteady, flickering light.
shining unsteadily

Examples

The candle on the table was aflicker in the gentle evening breeze.
She watched the dying fire, its embers aflicker in the darkness.
Through the fog, he saw the lighthouse beam aflicker across the sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flickering - shining with a rapid, unsteady light
  • Antonym: steady - continuous, unwavering light
