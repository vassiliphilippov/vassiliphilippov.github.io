Jacques Cousteau

1. PROPN

/ ʒæk kuːˈstoʊ / (AmE), / ʒak kuːˈstəʊ / (BrE)
(1910–1997) French marine explorer, conservationist, and filmmaker noted for his pioneering work in underwater exploration and documentaries.
French underwater explorer and filmmaker

Examples

The documentaries of Jacques Cousteau inspired generations to care about ocean life.
As a child, she dreamed of joining Jacques Cousteau on one of his famous expeditions.
Jacques Cousteau was renowned for his invention of scuba devices and marine research.
