girlhood

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɝːlˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːlˌhʊd / (BrE)
the period in a girl's life when she is a child or young person
period of being a girl

Examples

She often reminisced about her carefree girlhood spent in the countryside.
The novel explores the challenges of girlhood in a changing society.
Her girlhood dreams inspired her future career as an artist.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: boyhood - the period of being a boy

Etymology

Formed from 'girl' + 'hood', where 'hood' means 'state' or 'condition' (from Old English '-hād').
