Leucanthemum maximum

1. NOUN

/ luːˈkænθɪməm ˈmæksɪməm / (AmE), / luːˈkænθɪməm ˈmaksɪməm / (BrE)
a perennial flowering plant native to Europe, similar to the oxeye daisy, commonly known as the max chrysanthemum
a European perennial plant similar to the oxeye daisy

Examples

The garden was full of Leucanthemum maximum, their white petals bright in the sunlight.
She planted Leucanthemum maximum alongside other perennials for a striking summer display.
Many gardeners prefer Leucanthemum maximum for its hardiness and long blooming season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oxeye daisy - a common European daisy with white petals and a yellow center

Etymology

From Greek 'leukos' (white) and 'anthemon' (flower), and Latin 'maximum' (greatest, largest).
