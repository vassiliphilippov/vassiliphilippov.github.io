Montevideo

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɑːntiˌvɪdeɪoʊ / (AmE), / ˌmɒntɪvɪˈdeɪəʊ / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Uruguay, situated on the country's southern coast and known as a major port and cultural center
capital city of Uruguay

Examples

She traveled to Montevideo to experience its rich history and vibrant culture.
The conference was held in Montevideo, attracting participants from across the world.
Goods shipped from Montevideo play a vital role in international trade.

Etymology

From the Spanish name Montevideo, possibly meaning 'I saw a mount' or referring to its hilltop location.
