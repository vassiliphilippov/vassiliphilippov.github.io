bunghole

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌŋˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋˌhəʊl / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) Anus; the opening through which the contents of the bowels are discharged.
vulgar slang for anus
vulgar slang

Examples

He made a rude joke about his bunghole at the party, making everyone uncomfortable.
Some comedians often use the word bunghole in their stand-up routines for shock value.
Please avoid using the word bunghole in formal conversations, as it is considered very vulgar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anus - the opening at the end of the digestive tract

Etymology

Originally referred to the hole in a cask (from 'bung' + 'hole'), later adopted as vulgar slang for the anus.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbʌŋˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋˌhəʊl / (BrE)
A hole in a barrel or cask through which it can be filled or emptied.
hole in a barrel or cask

Examples

The brewer removed the bunghole stopper to fill the barrel with ale.
After cleaning the cask, she inspected the bunghole for leaks.
Wine is often poured into the barrel through the bunghole during production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bung - a stopper for closing the bunghole of a cask or barrel

Etymology

From Middle English 'bunge' (stopper) + 'hole'; 'bung' from Middle Dutch 'bonge'.
