woolly-haired

1. ADJ

/ ˈwʊliˌhɛrd / (AmE), / ˈwʊliˌheəd / (BrE)
having hair or fur that is dense, often matted, and curly like wool
having dense, curly, wool-like hair

Examples

The woolly-haired lambs frolicked in the field.
That breed of dog is known for its woolly-haired coat, which needs regular grooming.
Some people have woolly-haired cats that look like tiny sheep.

Synset Examples

woolly lambs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curly-haired - having hair forming curves or spirals
  • Antonym: straight-haired - having hair that does not curl

2. ADJ

/ ˈwʊliˌhɛrd / (AmE), / ˈwʊliˌhɛəd / (BrE)
Having hair that is dense, curly, or matted, resembling wool.
having dense, curly, or matted hair

Examples

The woolly-haired puppy stood out among its siblings with its thick, curly coat.
Some sheep breeds are known for their woolly-haired appearance.
The scientist studied the genetic basis of the woolly-haired trait in certain populations.

Related Terms
