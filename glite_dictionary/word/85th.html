85th

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪti ˈfɪfθ / (AmE), / ˈeɪti ˈfɪfθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number corresponding to the number eighty-five, used to indicate position or order in a sequence.
ordinal number for eighty-five

Examples

He finished in 85th place out of 200 runners in the marathon.
This year marks the 85th anniversary of the school's founding.
Her office is on the 85th floor of the skyscraper.
