bedclothes

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛdˌkloʊðz / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌkləʊðz / (BrE)
the sheets, blankets, and other coverings used on a bed
coverings for a bed

Examples

She changed the bedclothes and made the bed fresh for her guests.
It is important to wash your bedclothes regularly to maintain good hygiene.
The hotel provided clean bedclothes every day during our stay.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedding - bedclothes and other materials for making up a bed

Etymology

From Old English 'bedd' (bed) + 'clāþas' (cloths, coverings).
