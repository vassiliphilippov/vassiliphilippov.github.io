singlestick

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋɡl̩ˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl̩ˌstɪk / (BrE)
a wooden stick, used as a substitute for a sword in fencing practice.
stick used for fencing practice

Examples

The students practiced their fencing techniques using a singlestick instead of real blades.
In the 19th century, singlestick was a popular sport in Britain.
He suffered a bruise on his arm from a singlestick match.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: sword - a weapon with a long metal blade

Etymology

From 'single' + 'stick', referring to fencing with a single stick instead of a sword.
