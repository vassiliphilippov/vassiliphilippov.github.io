supporting

1. ADJ

/ səˈpɔrtɪŋ / (AmE), / səˈpɔːtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a structure or part) capable of bearing or holding up a load; serving to support.
capable of bearing a load; serving to support

Examples

The engineer confirmed that the supporting beams could handle the weight of the new equipment.
Before demolishing a wall, make sure it is not a supporting structure.
The renovation plans required reinforcing the supporting columns in the basement.

Synset Examples

a supporting wall

Related Terms

  • Synonym: load-bearing - capable of carrying weight or supporting a load

2. ADJ

/ səˈpɔrtɪŋ / (AmE), / səˈpɔːtɪŋ / (BrE)
giving help, encouragement, or emotional support to someone or something
giving encouragement or help

Examples

The teacher’s supporting attitude made a big difference to her anxious students.
He played a supporting role in helping her recover from the setback.
A supporting environment at work is essential for employee well-being.

Synset Examples

the anxious child needs supporting and accepting treatment from the teacher

Related Terms

  • Synonym: encouraging - giving someone support, confidence, or hope
  • Antonym: unsupportive - not giving help, encouragement, or support
