Xe

1. NOUN

/ zi ˈɑn / (AmE), / zi ˈɒn / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for xenon, a colorless, odorless, inert gaseous element occurring in trace amounts in the Earth's atmosphere
chemical symbol for xenon element

Examples

The periodic table lists Xe as the symbol for xenon.
Researchers filled the tube with Xe to test its conductivity.
In chemistry class, we learned that Xe is used in certain types of lamps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: xenon - a chemical element; a colorless, odorless, inert gas

Etymology

From xenon (from Greek *xenos*, meaning 'strange' or 'foreign'); 'Xe' adopted as the standard chemical symbol.
