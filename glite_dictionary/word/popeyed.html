popeyed

1. ADJ

/ ˈpoʊpˌaɪd / (AmE), / ˈpəʊp.aɪd / (BrE)
Having the eyes wide open in surprise or amazement.
having eyes wide in surprise

Examples

The children stared popeyed at the magician's tricks, unable to believe their eyes.
He walked into the room looking completely popeyed after the sudden crash outside.
Her popeyed expression showed just how shocked she was by the unexpected news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wide-eyed - having the eyes open wide, typically in surprise or innocence

2. ADJ

/ ˈpoʊpˌaɪd / (AmE), / ˈpəʊpˌaɪd / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) having eyes that bulge or protrude noticeably.
having bulging eyes

Examples

The frightened child stared at the clown with popeyed astonishment.
She accidentally turned on the lights and saw a popeyed cat fleeing the room.
He listened to the incredible news, his face popeyed with disbelief.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bug-eyed - having eyes that bulge out; wide-eyed

Etymology

Formed from 'pop' (as in pop out) + 'eyed' (having a certain kind of eyes).
