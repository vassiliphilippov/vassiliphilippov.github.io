Slovene

1. NOUN

/ sloʊˈviːn / (AmE), / sləʊˈviːn / (BrE)
the South Slavic language spoken primarily in Slovenia by the Slovenes
South Slavic language of Slovenia

Examples

She is fluent in both English and Slovene.
The book was translated from Slovene into several languages.
Many children in Slovenia learn Slovene as their first language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Slovenian - the South Slavic language spoken in Slovenia

Etymology

From Slovene *slovenski* (Slovenian).

2. NOUN

/ sloʊˈvin / (AmE), / sləʊˈviːn / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Slovenia
a native or inhabitant of Slovenia

Examples

The city was filled with proud Slovenes celebrating their national holiday.
As a Slovene, she grew up speaking the Slovenian language and following local customs.
Many Slovenes have contributed to art, science, and literature throughout history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Slovenian - a person from Slovenia

Etymology

From Slovene 'Slovenec' (Slovenian person), ultimately from 'Slovenija' (Slovenia).
