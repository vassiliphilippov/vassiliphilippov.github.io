poolroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈpuːlˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈpuːlˌruːm / (BrE)
A room or establishment equipped with pool tables for playing pool.
room with pool tables for playing pool

Examples

We met at the poolroom for a few games after work.
The town's only poolroom had a friendly, relaxed atmosphere.
He spent his teenage years hanging out in the local poolroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: billiard hall - a place with several billiard or pool tables where people can play for recreation or competition
