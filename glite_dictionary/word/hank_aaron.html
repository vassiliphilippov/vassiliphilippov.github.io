Hank Aaron

1. PROPN

/ hæŋk ˈɛrən / (AmE), / hæŋk ˈɛːrən / (BrE)
(1934–2021) An American professional baseball player who broke Babe Ruth’s career home run record, widely regarded as one of the greatest hitters in baseball history.
American baseball player, broke Babe Ruth's home run record

Examples

Hank Aaron was celebrated for breaking Babe Ruth’s longstanding home run record.
The Atlanta Braves honored Hank Aaron with a statue outside their stadium.
Many consider Hank Aaron to be one of baseball's all-time greatest players.
