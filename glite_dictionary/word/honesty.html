honesty

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːnəsti / (AmE), / ˈɒnɪsti / (BrE)
The quality of being honest; the state of being truthful and fair.
quality of being honest

Examples

Honesty is highly valued in both personal relationships and business dealings.
She admired his honesty when he admitted the mistake.
Children are often taught that honesty is the best policy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: truthfulness - the state of being truthful
  • Antonym: dishonesty - lack of honesty; lying or cheating

Etymology

From Old French *honesté*, from Latin *honestās* (honor, respectability).

2. NOUN

/ ˈɑnəsti / (AmE), / ˈɒnɪsti / (BrE)
A southeastern European plant (Lunaria annua) cultivated for its fragrant purplish flowers and distinctive round, flat, silver-white seedpods used for decoration.
plant with decorative, silvery seedpods

Examples

The dried seedpods of honesty add a delicate touch to floral arrangements.
Many gardeners plant honesty for its striking appearance in both spring and autumn.
After the flowers fade, honesty produces translucent pods that resemble silver coins.
