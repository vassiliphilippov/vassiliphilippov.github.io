gifset

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪfˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈɡɪfˌsɛt / (BrE)
A collection of related images in the GIF format, typically displayed together to illustrate a sequence, moment, or theme, especially on websites and social media.
set of related GIF images

Examples

The new episode inspired dozens of creative gifsets on Tumblr within hours of airing.
She shared a gifset of her favorite movie scenes in one post.
His viral gifset perfectly captured the actor's range of emotions.

Etymology

Blend of 'GIF' (Graphics Interchange Format) and 'set'.
