Pieter Bruegel

1. PROPN

/ ˈpiːtər ˈbrɔɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈpiːtə ˈbrɔɪɡl̩ / (BrE)
(1525–1569) A renowned Flemish painter celebrated for his detailed landscapes and peasant scenes.
Flemish painter of landscapes and peasant scenes

Examples

Pieter Bruegel's works offer a vivid portrayal of 16th-century peasant life in the Netherlands.
The museum's collection includes several masterpieces by Pieter Bruegel.
Students studied Pieter Bruegel to understand the evolution of landscape painting.
