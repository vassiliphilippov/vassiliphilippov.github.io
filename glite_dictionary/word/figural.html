figural

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪɡjərəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡjʊrəl / (BrE)
consisting of or forming representations of human or animal figures, especially in art or design.
depicting human or animal forms

Examples

The museum features an exhibit of figural sculptures from ancient Greece.
She specializes in figural painting, carefully rendering the human form in her work.
The carpet's figural pattern includes both animals and mythological creatures.

Synset Examples

a figural design
the figurative art of the humanistic tradition

Related Terms

  • Synonym: figurative - representing forms that are recognizably derived from life; not abstract

Etymology

From Latin figurālis, from figura (shape, figure).
