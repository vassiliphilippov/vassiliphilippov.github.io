Latium

1. PROPN

/ ˈleɪʃiəm / (AmE), / ˈleɪtiəm / (BrE)
an ancient region of west central Italy, southeast of Rome, bordering the Tyrrhenian Sea
ancient region of Italy, southeast of Rome

Examples

The Romans originally lived in Latium before expanding their empire.
Many ancient myths are rooted in the history of Latium.
Historians have studied the culture and language of Latium extensively.

Etymology

From Latin Latium, the name of the region inhabited by the Latins.
