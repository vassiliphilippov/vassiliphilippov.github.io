humiliatingly

1. ADV

/ hjuˈmɪliˌeɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / hjuːˈmɪlɪeɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that causes someone to feel ashamed or loses their dignity.
in a way that causes humiliation

Examples

She was humiliatingly dismissed from her job in front of her colleagues.
He apologized humiliatingly for his mistake during the ceremony.
The team humiliatingly lost their championship game by a wide margin.

Synset Examples

the painting was reproduced humiliatingly small

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shamefully - in a manner causing shame; synonym for 'humiliatingly'
  • Antonym: proudly - in a way that shows pride; antonym for 'humiliatingly'

Etymology

From 'humiliating' + '-ly'. 'Humiliating' derives from Latin 'humiliare' (to humble).
