Poochie-fication

1. NOUN

/ ˈpuːtʃi fɪˌkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈpuːtʃi fɪkəˌeɪʃən / (BrE)
The process of making something excessively cute, trendy, or oriented towards dogs and pet culture.
making things overly cute or dog-friendly
informal

Examples

Many residents objected to the Poochie-fication of the city center, saying it excluded people without pets.
The cafe's Poochie-fication included dog treats on the menu and canine-friendly seating areas.
Some believe the Poochie-fication of retail spaces is just a marketing ploy to attract dog owners.

Synset Examples

She complained about the Poochie-fication ruining the local park.
The Poochie-fication of the neighborhood led to stricter pet laws.
The Poochie-fication trend made the area more dog-friendly.

Etymology

Formed from the word 'pooch' (slang for dog) and the suffix '-ification' (process of making).
