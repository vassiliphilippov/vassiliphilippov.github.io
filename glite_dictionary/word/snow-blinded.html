snow-blinded

1. ADJ

/ snoʊˈblaɪndɪd / (AmE), / snəʊˈblaɪndɪd / (BrE)
unable to see temporarily because of exposure to light reflected from snow or ice
temporarily blinded by snow-reflected light

Examples

After hours of skiing without goggles, he became snow-blinded and had to rest indoors.
The team struggled across the glacier, several members snow-blinded by the intense sunlight.
Climbers are warned to protect their eyes to avoid becoming snow-blinded during the ascent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snow-blind - temporarily unable to see due to bright snow or ice
