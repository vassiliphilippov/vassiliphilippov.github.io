most-valuable

1. ADJ

/ moʊst ˈvæljuəbəl / (AmE), / məʊst ˈvæljʊəbl / (BrE)
(in sports) designating the player considered the most important or outstanding in a team or competition.
designating the top-performing player in sports

Examples

She was named the most-valuable player of the tournament after her outstanding performance.
The team celebrated their captain as the season’s most-valuable contributor.
Winning the most-valuable player award was a dream come true for him.

Synset Examples

the most-valuable player award

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: valuable - being worth a lot of money or having great importance
