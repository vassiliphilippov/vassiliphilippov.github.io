bark

1. NOUN

/ bɑrk / (AmE), / bɑːk / (BrE)
the sharp, loud sound made by a dog
the sound a dog makes

Examples

The bark of the dog could be heard all night long.
Her puppy has a surprisingly loud bark for its size.
A sudden bark startled the birds in the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: woof - another word for a dog's bark

Etymology

From Old English *beorc* (bark, cry of a dog).
