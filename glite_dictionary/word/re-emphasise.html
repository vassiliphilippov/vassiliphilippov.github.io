to re-emphasise

1. VERB

/ ˌriːˈɛmfəˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɛm.fə.saɪz / (BrE)
to emphasize something again or give renewed emphasis to it
emphasize again
british

Examples

The report re-emphasises the importance of early intervention.
She re-emphasised her commitment to improving safety standards.
In the meeting, the manager re-emphasised the company's core values.

Synset Examples

The director re-emphasized the need for greater productivity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reemphasize - to emphasize again (American spelling)
  • Degree Lower: to emphasise - to give special importance or prominence to something
