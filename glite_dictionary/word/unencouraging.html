unencouraging

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪnˈkɝː.ɪdʒ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈkʌr.ɪdʒ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
Not giving support, confidence, or hope; not encouraging.
not giving support or hope

Examples

The results of the experiment were rather unencouraging for the research team.
His unencouraging attitude made it harder for us to stay motivated.
The market reports were unencouraging, suggesting a tough year ahead.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: encouraging - giving support, confidence, or hope
  • Synonym: discouraging - causing someone to lose confidence or enthusiasm

Etymology

From un- (prefix meaning 'not') + encouraging.
