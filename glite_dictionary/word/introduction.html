introduction

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntrəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
a book or text that provides basic or elementary information about a subject, often for beginners
elementary instructional book or text

Examples

This course uses an introduction to biology as its primary textbook.
He wrote an excellent introduction to the history of art for beginners.
If you are new to coding, start with a gentle introduction before tackling advanced topics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: primer - an elementary textbook for teaching reading or an introduction to a subject
  • Synonym: manual - a book giving instructions or information

Etymology

From Latin introductio (a leading in), from introducere (to lead in).
