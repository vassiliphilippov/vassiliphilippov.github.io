roundheaded

1. ADJ

/ ˈraʊndˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈraʊndˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) having a broad, short, and rounded head; brachycephalic.
having a broad, rounded head

Examples

The puppy's unusually roundheaded appearance made it especially cute to visitors.
Some prehistoric skulls show distinctly roundheaded features, unlike those of later populations.
The roundheaded eagle is easily distinguished from its relatives with more elongated skulls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brachycephalic - having a short, broad head
