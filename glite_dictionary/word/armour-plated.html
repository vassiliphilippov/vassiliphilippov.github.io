armour-plated

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːrmər ˌpleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɑːmə ˌpleɪtɪd / (BrE)
having a surface or exterior covered with heavy metal plates for protection, especially against attack or damage
covered with protective metal plates

Examples

The military deployed several armour-plated vehicles to protect its personnel.
The bank van was armour-plated to resist any attempted robberies.
Their new truck is armour-plated and nearly impossible to penetrate.

Synset Examples

armor-plated vehicles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armored - covered with armor or protective metal

Etymology

From 'armour' (Middle English, from Old French 'armaure') + 'plated'.
