time-honoured

1. ADJ

/ taɪm ˈɑnərd / (AmE), / taɪm ˈɒnəd / (BrE)
Respected or established because it has existed or been accepted for a long time.
respected due to long history

Examples

They followed the time-honoured tradition of sending cards at Christmas.
The time-honoured ceremony attracts visitors from all over the world.
She prepared the dish using a time-honoured family recipe.

Synset Examples

time-honored customs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traditional - existing as part of a tradition; customary

2. ADJ

/ taɪmˌɑnɚd / (AmE), / taɪmˌɒnəd / (BrE)
respected or valued because it has existed for a long time or has a long tradition
respected due to long tradition

Examples

The university has many time-honoured customs that are still observed today.
Their wedding included several time-honoured rituals passed down through generations.
Respect for elders is a time-honoured value in many cultures.
