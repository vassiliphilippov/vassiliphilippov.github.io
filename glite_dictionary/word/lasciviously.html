lasciviously

1. ADV

/ ləˈsɪviəsli / (AmE), / ləˈsɪviəsli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows overt or offensive sexual desire.
in a sexually offensive or lewd manner

Examples

He winked lasciviously at her from across the room, making her feel uncomfortable.
The villain grinned lasciviously as he revealed his sinister intentions.
She accused her coworker of looking at her lasciviously during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: modestly - in a manner that is unassuming or shows a lack of sexual boldness

Etymology

From 'lascivious' + '-ly'. 'Lascivious' from Latin lasciviosus (wanton).
