muffin

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌf.ɪn / (AmE), / ˈmʌf.ɪn / (BrE)
A small, round, sweet bread made from batter and baked in individual cup-shaped portions, often eaten for breakfast or as a snack.
small sweet bread baked in a cup shape

Examples

She enjoyed a blueberry muffin with her morning coffee.
Would you like a chocolate chip muffin or a pastry for breakfast?
The bakery makes fresh muffins every day.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: cupcake - a small cake baked in a cup-shaped mold, usually with icing

Etymology

From Low German *muffen* (small cakes) or Old French *moflet* (a kind of soft bread).
