wonderfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌndɚf(ə)lnəs / (AmE), / ˈwʌndəf(ə)lnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being extremely good, delightful, or admirable; excellence that inspires wonder.
the quality of being wonderful

Examples

The wonderfulness of the evening left everyone speechless and full of joy.
She never ceased to be amazed by the wonderfulness of nature.
They talked for hours, appreciating the wonderfulness of their friendship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excellence - the quality of being outstanding or extremely good

Etymology

Formed from 'wonderful' + '-ness' (forming nouns indicating a state or quality).
