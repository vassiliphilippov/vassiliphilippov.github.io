sinuously

1. ADV

/ ˈsɪnjuəsli / (AmE), / ˈsɪnjuːəsli / (BrE)
In a winding, curving, or serpentine manner.
in a winding or curving way

Examples

The river flowed sinuously through the valley, weaving between the hills.
She moved sinuously across the dance floor, every motion graceful and fluid.
Smoke rose sinuously in the quiet air, forming twisting patterns as it ascended.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sinuous - having many curves and turns
