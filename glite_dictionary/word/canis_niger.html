Canis niger

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪ.nɪs ˈnaɪ.dʒər / (AmE), / ˈkaː.nɪs ˈniː.gə / (BrE)
A reddish-grey wolf native to southwestern North America, also known as the red wolf.
reddish-grey wolf of southwestern North America

Examples

The Canis niger population has been declining due to habitat loss.
Biologists observed a pack of Canis niger near the river last spring.
Conservation efforts are underway to protect Canis niger in its native range.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red wolf - A wolf species native to southeastern United States

Etymology

From Latin: 'canis' meaning 'dog', 'niger' meaning 'black'.
