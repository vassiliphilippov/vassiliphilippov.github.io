Tofieldia

1. PROPN

/ toʊˈfɪldiə / (AmE), / təˈfɪldiə / (BrE)
a genus of perennial herbs found in cool temperate regions, sometimes classified in the family Melanthiaceae
genus of perennial temperate herbs

Examples

Tofieldia species prefer moist habitats in cool climates.
The botanist discovered a rare Tofieldia growing along the riverbank.
Members of the genus Tofieldia are sometimes confused with similar-looking lilies.

Etymology

Named after Thomas Tofield, an English botanist (1730–1779).
