Kulanapan

1. NOUN

/ kuːˈlænəˌpæn / (AmE), / kuːˈlænəˌpan / (BrE)
a group of seven languages belonging to the Hokan language family, traditionally spoken in regions bounded by the Pacific Ocean and interior Northern California
Hokan language group of Northern California

Examples

Linguists have studied the Kulanapan languages to better understand the history of Native California.
The Kulanapan group is known for its diverse dialects spread across northern California.
Some tribes that spoke Kulanapan languages lived near the Russian River.
