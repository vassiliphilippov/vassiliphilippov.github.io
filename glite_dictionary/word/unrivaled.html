unrivaled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈraɪvəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈraɪvəld / (BrE)
Having no equal; better than any other; without rival.
without equal; having no rival

Examples

She achieved unrivaled success in her field, inspiring countless others to follow in her footsteps.
The restaurant is known for its unrivaled service and exquisite cuisine.
His unrivaled commitment to quality set him apart from the competition.

Synset Examples

matchless beauty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: matchless - having no equal
  • Synonym: peerless - so good as to have no equal
  • Synonym: incomparable - so good or impressive as to be beyond comparison
  • Synonym: unmatched - better than any others; having no match

Etymology

From "un-" (not) + "rivaled" (having a rival), from Latin "rivalis" (a competitor or adversary).
