Sokoro

1. PROPN

/ soʊˈkoʊroʊ / (AmE), / səʊˈkɔːrəʊ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in Chad, primarily in the Guera Region and surrounding areas
Chadic language of Chad

Examples

The Sokoro language is mainly spoken in the Guera region of Chad.
Linguists have documented many traditional songs in Sokoro.
Efforts are being made to preserve Sokoro as it faces the threat of extinction.
