1. NOUN

/ pɪpˌtædəˈniːə / (AmE), / pɪpˌtædəˈniːə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical American trees and shrubs belonging to the legume family, often classified within other related genera.
genus of tropical American trees and shrubs

Examples

The tropical forest was home to several rare Piptadenia species.
