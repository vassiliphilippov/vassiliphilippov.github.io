airpost

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈeəˌpəʊst / (BrE)
a system of transporting mail by aircraft
mail system using aircraft

Examples

Goods sent via airpost usually arrive faster than those sent by sea.
The company switched to airpost to ensure quicker delivery overseas.
International airpost services have become essential for global communication.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airmail - letters and parcels sent by air; the service of sending mail by aircraft

Etymology

Formed from 'air' and 'post', referring to mail delivery by air.
