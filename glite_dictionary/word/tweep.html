tweep

1. NOUN

/ twip / (AmE), / twiÀêp / (BrE)
A person who uses the social media platform Twitter.
Twitter user
informal

Examples

Many tweeps shared their thoughts on the trending hashtag.
After joining Twitter, she quickly gained hundreds of tweeps as followers.
He met several of his closest friends through the tweep community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twitterer - A person who posts on Twitter

Etymology

Blend of 'Twitter' and 'peep' (slang for person).
