Cicindelidae

1. NOUN

/ sɪˌsɪnˈdɛləˌdi / (AmE), / sɪˌsɪndɛˈlɪːdiː / (BrE)
A family of beetles commonly known as tiger beetles, noted for their fast running and predatory behavior.
family of tiger beetles

Examples

The Cicindelidae are recognized for their metallic coloration and incredible speed.
Researchers observed several species of Cicindelidae hunting on the sandy riverbank.
Members of the Cicindelidae family play important roles as predators in many ecosystems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiger beetle - A fast-moving predatory beetle belonging to the family Cicindelidae

Etymology

From New Latin Cicindelidae, based on the type genus Cicindela.
