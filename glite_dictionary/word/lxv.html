LXV

1. NUM

/ ɛl ɛks vi / (AmE), / ɛl ɛks viː / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for 65
Roman numeral for 65

Examples

The year LXV of the era was marked by significant political changes.
She read the inscription 'Chapter LXV' at the start of the new section.
In Roman numerals, sixty-five is written as LXV.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 65 - the number sixty-five

Etymology

From Latin numerals: 'L' (50), 'X' (10), and 'V' (5), combined to form 65.
