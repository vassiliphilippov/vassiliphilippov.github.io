anticipator

1. NOUN

/ ænˈtɪs.ɪ.peɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ænˈtɪs.ɪ.peɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person who expects or looks forward to something in advance
person who anticipates

Examples

As an anticipator of market trends, she always invests wisely.
Every anticipator in the project submitted their predictions in advance.
He became known as an anticipator of technological advancements in his field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foreseer - a person who foresees, or predicts what will happen

Etymology

From anticipate + -or, following Latin agentive suffix pattern.
