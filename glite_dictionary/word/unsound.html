unsound

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsaʊnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsaʊnd / (BrE)
(of a person) suffering from severe mental illness; not sane, especially as recognized in legal or formal contexts.
not sane; suffering mental illness
legal

Examples

The court declared her unsound and unable to manage her own affairs.
Family members became concerned that his behavior indicated he was unsound.
Decisions made by a person of unsound mind may later be challenged in court.

Synset Examples

of unsound mind

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insane - suffering from a disorder of the mind; not sane
  • Antonym: sane - of sound mind; mentally healthy

Etymology

From un- (not) + sound (healthy, in good condition).
