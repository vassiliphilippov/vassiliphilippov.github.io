avens

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ.vənz / (AmE), / ˈiː.vənz / (BrE)
Any of various perennial herbaceous plants of the genus Geum, with usually pinnate basal leaves and variously colored flowers.
perennial plants of the genus Geum

Examples

The garden was full of blooming avens in shades of red and yellow.
Several species of avens thrive in alpine meadows and woodland edges.
She learned to identify avens by their distinctive pinnate leaves and bright flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Geum - the genus of plants to which avens belong

Etymology

From Latin 'avena' (wild oats), earlier applied to various similar plants.
