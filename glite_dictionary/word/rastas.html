Rastas

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑːstəz / (AmE), / ˈrɑːstəz / (BrE)
Members of the Rastafarian religious and social movement originating in Jamaica, known for wearing dreadlocks and associated with reggae music and marijuana use.
members of Rastafarian movement

Examples

Many Rastas gather to celebrate significant religious holidays in Jamaica.
The philosophy and style of Rastas have influenced global music and youth culture.
Some tourists are fascinated by the distinctive dreadlocks worn by Rastas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Rasta - a member of the Rastafarian religious movement
  • Synonym: Rastafarian - a member of the Rastafarian religion and cultural movement
  • Synonym: Rastafarianism - the religious and social movement known as Rastafarianism

Etymology

Plural of 'Rasta', from Rastafarian, from Ras Tafari, the pre-regnal name of Haile Selassie I of Ethiopia.
