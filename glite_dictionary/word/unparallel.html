unparallel

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpærəˌlɛl / (AmE), / ʌnˈparəlɛl / (BrE)
not parallel; not having the same direction or distance apart at every point
not parallel

Examples

The designer chose unparallel lines to create a sense of movement in the artwork.
Please check that none of the support beams are unparallel to the floor.
The roads run at slightly unparallel angles, making the neighborhood layout unusual.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: parallel - being side by side and having the same distance continuously between them
  • Synonym: nonparallel - not parallel

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (not) to 'parallel'; from Greek 'parallēlos' (beside one another).
