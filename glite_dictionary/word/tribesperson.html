tribesperson

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraɪbzˌpɜr.sən / (AmE), / ˈtraɪbzˌpɜː.sən / (BrE)
a member of a tribe, especially one living according to the tribe's traditional customs
member of a tribe

Examples

The village chief is a respected tribesperson who makes important decisions for the community.
Anthropologists interviewed every tribesperson to understand their customs and beliefs.
As a young tribesperson, she learned traditional skills from her elders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tribesman - male member of a tribe
  • Synonym: tribeswoman - female member of a tribe

Etymology

Formed from 'tribe' + person (modelled after 'spokesperson').
