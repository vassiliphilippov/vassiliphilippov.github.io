plantswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæntswʊmən / (AmE), / ˈplɑːntswʊmən / (BrE)
A woman with expert knowledge of or great enthusiasm for cultivating plants.
female expert in plant cultivation

Examples

The renowned plantswoman gave a lecture on rare alpine flowers.
As a dedicated plantswoman, she transformed her backyard into a vibrant botanical oasis.
Many aspiring gardeners seek advice from the local plantswoman on how to grow heritage vegetables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plantsman - person (typically male or unspecified) expert in plant cultivation

Etymology

Formed from 'plant' + 'woman', patterned after 'plantsman'.
