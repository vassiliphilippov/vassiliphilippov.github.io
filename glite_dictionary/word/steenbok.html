steenbok

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪn.bɑk / (AmE), / ˈstiːn.bɒk / (BrE)
a small African antelope with large ears and short, straight horns, found in open country in southern and eastern Africa
small antelope of southern Africa

Examples

The steenbok darted through the dry grass, almost invisible to the untrained eye.
During the safari, we spotted a solitary steenbok resting beneath a thorn tree.
Unlike larger antelope, a steenbok relies on its agility and camouflage to evade predators.

Etymology

From Afrikaans 'steenbok', from Dutch 'steenbok' (ibex), applied to this antelope.
