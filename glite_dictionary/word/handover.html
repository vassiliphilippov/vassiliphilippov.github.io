handover

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændˌoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌəʊvə / (BrE)
the act of transferring possession, control, or responsibility to another person or group
transfer of possession or responsibility

Examples

The police supervised the handover of evidence to the prosecutors.
The official handover ceremony took place yesterday afternoon.
He was present during the handover of the keys to the new owner.

Synset Examples

the handover of occupied territory

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transfer - the act of moving something from one place or person to another
  • Antonym: retention - the act of keeping possession, control, or responsibility
