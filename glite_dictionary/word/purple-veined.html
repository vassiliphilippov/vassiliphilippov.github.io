purple-veined

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝpl ˌveɪnd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˌveɪnd / (BrE)
(of flowers) having purple markings or streaks that resemble veins.
having purple vein-like markings (esp. in flowers)

Examples

The purple-veined petals made the orchid especially striking in the sunlight.
Several species in this region are known for their purple-veined flowers.
A purple-veined variety of iris was newly catalogued by the botanists.
