volatilised

1. ADJ

/ vəˈlæt̬.ə.laɪzd / (AmE), / vəˈlæt.ɪ.laɪzd / (BrE)
converted into a gas or vapor by the process of volatilization.
converted into a gas or vapor
british

Examples

When heated, the liquid was quickly volatilised and escaped from the container.
Some chemicals are easily volatilised at room temperature, requiring careful storage protocols.
During the experiment, mercury was volatilised and detected in the air samples.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volatilized - converted into a gas or vapor (American spelling)

Etymology

From Latin 'volatilis' (fleeting, flying) via French 'volatiliser'; British spelling of 'volatilized'.
