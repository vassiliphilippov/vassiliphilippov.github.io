micrometry

1. NOUN

/ maɪˈkrɑːmətri / (AmE), / mɪˈkrɒmətri / (BrE)
The process or technique of measuring dimensions using a micrometer.
measurement using a micrometer
medical

Examples

Micrometry is essential for accurately determining the diameter of microscopic cells.
Recent advances in micrometry have improved the precision of measurements in materials science.
Proper micrometry requires calibration of the instrument before each use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: micrometer - an instrument for making precise measurements

Etymology

From micrometer (measuring instrument) + -y (suffix forming nouns).
