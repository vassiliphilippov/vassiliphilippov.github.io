loverly

1. ADJ

/ ˈlʌvərli / (AmE), / ˈlʌvəli / (BrE)
Characteristic of or resembling a lover; in a loving or amorous manner.
like or in the manner of a lover

Examples

She gazed at him with a loverly expression that softened his heart.
His loverly words left her blushing for the rest of the evening.
The poem was written in a loverly tone, filled with tenderness and longing.

Etymology

Formed from 'lover' + '-ly', similar in construction to 'friendly'.
