snorty

1. ADJ

/ ˈsnɔrti / (AmE), / ˈsnɔːti / (BrE)
(British, informal) Bad-tempered or easily annoyed.
ill-tempered; easily annoyed (British, informal)
informal british

Examples

He became quite snorty when asked about his delayed project.
There’s no need to get snorty over such a small mistake.
She was in a snorty mood all morning after missing her train.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irritable - easily annoyed or angered
  • Antonym: calm - not easily angered or upset
