lawwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔːˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈlɔːˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female officer who enforces the law
female law enforcement officer

Examples

The town hired its first lawwoman to improve trust with the community.
As a respected lawwoman, she set an example for future generations.
The lawwoman handled the situation with professionalism and care.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lawman - a male officer who enforces the law
  • Synonym: policewoman - a female member of a police force

Etymology

Formed from 'law' and 'woman', by analogy with 'lawman' and 'policewoman'.
