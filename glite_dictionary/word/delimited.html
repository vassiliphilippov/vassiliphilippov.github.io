delimited

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈlɪmɪtɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪmɪtɪd / (BrE)
Having clearly defined or established limits or boundaries.
having clearly defined limits

Examples

The area is delimited by rivers on three sides and by a mountain range on the fourth.
The database fields are delimited by commas in the CSV file.
Access to the delimited section is restricted without proper authorization.

Synset Examples

a delimited frontier through the disputed region

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bounded - having limits or boundaries defined
  • Antonym: unbounded - without limits or boundaries

Etymology

From the verb 'delimit', from Latin 'delimitare' (to mark boundaries).
