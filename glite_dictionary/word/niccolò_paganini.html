Niccolò Paganini

1. PROPN

/ niˈkoʊloʊ ˌpɑːgəˈniːni / (AmE), / niˈkɒləʊ ˌpæɡəˈniːni / (BrE)
(1782–1840) An Italian violinist and composer, renowned for his virtuoso performances and influential compositions for the violin.
Italian violinist and composer (1782–1840)

Examples

Many regard Niccolò Paganini as the greatest violin virtuoso of all time.
The music of Niccolò Paganini remains a challenge for even the most skilled violinists.
