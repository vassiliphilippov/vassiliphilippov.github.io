John Wayne

1. PROPN

/ dʒɑn weɪn / (AmE), / dʒɒn weɪn / (BrE)
(1907–1979) An American film actor renowned for his roles as tough, heroic characters, especially in Westerns and war films.
famous American actor known for Westerns

Examples

Many fans of classic cinema admire John Wayne for his commanding screen presence.
The new film's protagonist was modeled after the rugged style of John Wayne.
Growing up, my grandfather was a huge admirer of John Wayne and watched all his movies.
