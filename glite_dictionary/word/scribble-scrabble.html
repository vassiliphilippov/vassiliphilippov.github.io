scribble-scrabble

1. NOUN

/ ˈskrɪbəl ˌskræbəl / (AmE), / ˈskrɪbəl ˌskræbəl / (BrE)
messy or chaotic writing or drawing, consisting of a jumbled mixture of lines or colors
messy writing or drawing
informal

Examples

The toddler covered the paper with a colorful scribble-scrabble during art time.
I couldn't read her scribble-scrabble on the back of the envelope.
The mural was an energetic scribble-scrabble that brought life to the building's wall.

Synset Examples

The artist's sketch was a beautiful scribble-scrabble of colors.
The kids made a scribble-scrabble on the whiteboard.
His notes were just a scribble-scrabble of ideas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scribble - to write or draw carelessly or hurriedly
  • Synonym: doodle - to draw or scribble absentmindedly
