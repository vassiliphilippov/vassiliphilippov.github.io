ultrasonically

1. ADV

/ ˌʌltrəˈsɑnɪkli / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈsɒnɪkli / (BrE)
by means of or using ultrasonic waves or technology.
using ultrasonic waves or technology

Examples

The engineer cleaned the delicate instrument ultrasonically to avoid damaging it.
The plaque was removed from the teeth ultrasonically for a thorough cleaning.
Some surgical procedures are now performed ultrasonically to minimize tissue damage.

Synset Examples

the kidney stones were removed ultrasonically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ultrasonic - relating to sound waves with frequencies above the audible range

Etymology

Formed from 'ultrasonic' + '-ally', indicating the adverbial form.
