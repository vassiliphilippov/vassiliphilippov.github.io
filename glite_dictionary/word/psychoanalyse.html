to psychoanalyse

1. VERB

/ ˌsaɪkoʊəˈnælaɪz / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊəˈnælaɪz / (BrE)
to treat someone using the methods of psychoanalysis
treat with psychoanalysis

Examples

After her traumatic experience, she decided to psychoanalyse herself with the help of a therapist.
The doctor had psychoanalysed dozens of patients over his long career.
He wondered if being psychoanalysed would help him understand his dreams better.

Synset Examples

I was analyzed in Vienna by a famous psychiatrist

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to analyze - to examine methodically and in detail

Etymology

From psycho- + analyse, adapted from the noun 'psychoanalysis' (from Greek 'psyche', meaning 'soul', and French 'analyse').
