to bespangle

1. VERB

/ bɪˈspæŋɡəl / (AmE), / bɪˈspæŋɡl / (BrE)
to decorate or cover with spangles or glittering objects
adorn with spangles or sparkles

Examples

The designer decided to bespangle the dress for extra glamour on the runway.
Stars bespangle the night sky, creating a magical scene above the city.
The banner was bespangled with tiny silver sequins that caught the light beautifully.

Synset Examples

the star-spangled banner

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to bedazzle - to decorate or impress with brilliance or showiness

Etymology

From 'be-' (intensive prefix) + 'spangle', originally Middle English 'spangel' (a small sparkling object).
