Realtor

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːəltər / (AmE), / ˈriːəltə / (BrE)
A real estate agent who is a member of the National Association of Realtors.
registered real estate agent (US)
american

Examples

After months of searching, we finally found a trustworthy Realtor to help us buy our first home.
It is important to verify that your Realtor is certified by the National Association of Realtors.
The Realtor showed us several properties in different neighborhoods before we made our decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: real estate agent - a person who buys and sells properties as a profession

Etymology

Trademarked in the US; from 'real(estate)' + '-or' by analogy with 'administrator'.
