gay-feather

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪˌfɛðər / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪˌfɛðə / (BrE)
A North American plant of the genus Liatris, with spikes of small, usually purple, flower heads.
a North American plant of genus Liatris

Examples

The gay-feather bloomed brightly along the edge of the prairie in late summer.
Gardeners often plant gay-feather to attract butterflies to their yards.
She added a bouquet of gay-feather to the dining table for a splash of color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blazing star - another common name for plants of genus Liatris
