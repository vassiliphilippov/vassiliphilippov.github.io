touched

1. ADJ

/ tʌtʃt / (AmE), / tʌtʃt / (BrE)
deeply affected by emotion, especially in a way that moves one to respond
emotionally affected; moved

Examples

She was clearly touched by the heartfelt letter he sent her.
I felt genuinely touched when they remembered my birthday.
He looked touched as the audience applauded his performance.

Synset Examples

too moved to speak
very touched by the stranger's kindness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moved - feeling strong emotion, especially sympathy or sadness
Source: WordNet

2. ADJ

/ tʌtʃt / (AmE), / tʌtʃt / (BrE)
having come into contact with something
having come into contact

Examples

Make sure the paint hasn't been touched before you move the canvas.
None of the documents had been touched since last week.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: untouched - not having come into contact

/ tʌtʃt / (AmE), / tʌtʃt / (BrE)
