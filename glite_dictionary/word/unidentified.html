unidentified

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnaɪˈdɛntɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈdɛntɪfaɪd / (BrE)
being or having an unknown or unnamed source or origin.
unknown or unnamed in origin

Examples

The authorities are searching for the unidentified person seen in the footage.
Several unidentified objects were found near the crash site.
She received a generous donation from an unidentified source.

Synset Examples

a poem by an unknown author
corporations responsible to nameless owners
an unnamed donor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anonymous - of unknown name or authorship
  • Antonym: identified - recognized or known

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'identified', from Latin 'identificare' (to make the same).

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌaɪˈdɛntəˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.aɪˈdɛn.tɪ.faɪd / (BrE)
not known or recognized; not yet identified or named.
not yet identified or recognized

Examples

The police are searching for the unidentified suspect seen in the security footage.
Scientists discovered an unidentified object at the bottom of the ocean.
Several unidentified callers left messages on her voicemail.

Synset Examples

an unidentified species
an unidentified witness

Related Terms

  • Antonym: identified - recognized or established as a particular person or thing

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' and 'identified', from Latin 'identificare' (to make the same).

Synset POS: s
