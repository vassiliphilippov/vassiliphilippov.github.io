tosh

1. NOUN

/ tɑʃ / (AmE), / tɒʃ / (BrE)
Silly or pretentious talk; nonsense.
nonsense; silly talk
british

Examples

He dismissed her argument as utter tosh.
Don't believe a word of that tosh he was spouting at dinner.
The article was full of tosh about miracle cures.
