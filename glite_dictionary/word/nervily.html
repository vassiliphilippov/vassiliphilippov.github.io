nervily

1. ADV

/ ˈnɝːvəli / (AmE), / ˈnɜːvɪli / (BrE)
In a brash or bold manner; showing cheek or audacity.
in a brash or bold manner

Examples

She nervily challenged the manager's decision during the meeting.
He nervily demanded a refund in front of the entire queue.
The candidate nervily turned the difficult question back on the interviewer.

Synset Examples

brashly, she asked for a rebate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brashly - in a confident, aggressive, or rude way
  • Antonym: timidly - in a shy or lacking confidence way

Etymology

Formed from 'nervy' (bold, brash) + '-ly'.
