dillenia

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈlɛniə / (AmE), / dɪˈliːniə / (BrE)
Any of several evergreen trees or shrubs of the genus Dillenia, grown for their attractive foliage and nodding magnolia-like flowers, often followed by edible fruits used in curries, jellies, and preserves.
a genus of evergreen trees or shrubs with edible fruit

Examples

The garden showcases a beautiful dillenia tree with its striking yellow blossoms.
She made a delicious chutney using the fruit from a dillenia plant.
Botanists studied the unique leaf patterns of the genus dillenia.

Etymology

From the genus name Dillenia, named after German botanist Johann Jacob Dillenius (1684–1747).
