stitched

1. ADJ

/ stɪtʃt / (AmE), / stɪtʃt / (BrE)
fastened or joined together by stitches, especially with needle and thread
fastened by stitches

Examples

The handmade quilt was carefully stitched by my grandmother.
The wound was stitched closed by the doctor after the surgery.
Her dress was beautifully stitched with intricate patterns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sewn - joined or fastened with stitches
