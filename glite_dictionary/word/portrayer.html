portrayer

1. NOUN

/ pɔrˈtreɪər / (AmE), / pɔːˈtreɪə / (BrE)
a person who paints or draws portraits
person who creates portraits

Examples

The famous portrayer captured the likeness of dozens of historical figures in her paintings.
He is a skilled portrayer renowned for his lifelike drawings of celebrities.
As a portrayer, she spent years perfecting her technique with charcoal and oils.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: portraitist - an artist who specializes in making portraits

Etymology

From the verb 'portray' (from Anglo-Norman 'purtraire'), plus agent noun suffix '-er'.
