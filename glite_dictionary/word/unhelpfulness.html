unhelpfulness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈhɛlpfəlnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛlpfəlnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of not being helpful or providing assistance.
lack of helpfulness

Examples

The unhelpfulness of the staff made a stressful situation even worse.
Her unhelpfulness during the project delayed everyone's progress.
He was surprised by the unhelpfulness of his colleagues when he asked for advice.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: helpfulness - the quality of being helpful or willing to assist
  • Synonym: unhelpful - not helpful or assisting

Etymology

Formed from 'unhelpful' + '-ness'.
