macron

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪkrɑn / (AmE), / ˈmækrɒn / (BrE)
a diacritical mark (¯) placed above a vowel to indicate that it is pronounced with a long sound
diacritical mark indicating long vowel

Examples

In Latin, a macron is used to signify a long vowel sound.
Some dictionaries print a macron above vowels to help with pronunciation.
The teacher explained the function of the macron in phonetic transcription.

Etymology

From Greek makrón (neuter of makrós, meaning 'long').
