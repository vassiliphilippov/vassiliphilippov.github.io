Lebanon

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɛbənɑn / (AmE), / ˈlɛbənɒn / (BrE)
a country in Western Asia on the eastern shore of the Mediterranean Sea, officially known as the Lebanese Republic
country in Western Asia

Examples

My friend has traveled to Lebanon several times for business.
Phoenician civilization once flourished in what is now Lebanon.
The cuisine of Lebanon is famous for its use of fresh herbs and spices.

Etymology

From Latin 'Libanus', from Ancient Greek 'Λίβανος' (Libanos); possibly from a Semitic root meaning 'white', referring to the snow-capped Mount Lebanon.
