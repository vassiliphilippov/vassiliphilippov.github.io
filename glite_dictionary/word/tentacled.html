tentacled

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛn.tə.kəld / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.tə.kld / (BrE)
Having one or more tentacles.
having tentacles

Examples

The octopus is a tentacled creature living in the ocean depths.
She drew a tentacled monster attacking a ship in her sketchbook.
The aquarium exhibit featured several tentacled marine animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tentacle - a long, flexible, arm-like appendage found in some animals

Etymology

From 'tentacle' + '-ed', forming an adjective indicating the presence of tentacles.
