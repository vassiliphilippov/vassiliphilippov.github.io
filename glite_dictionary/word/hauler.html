hauler

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔlər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːlə / (BrE)
a company or person engaged in the business of transporting goods by road; a haulage contractor
company or person transporting goods by road

Examples

The shipping company hired a specialized hauler to move the construction equipment to the site.
Many independent haulers operate their own trucks across the country.
The hauler picked up the cargo at dawn and delivered it by midday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carrier - a person or company that transports goods or people
  • Synonym: trucker - a person who drives a truck as an occupation
