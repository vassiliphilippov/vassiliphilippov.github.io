to scrivened

1. VERB

/ ˈskrɪvənd / (AmE), / ˈskrɪvənd / (BrE)
written out or copied by hand; transcribed manually.
written or copied by hand
archaic

Examples

The old manuscript was carefully scrivened onto parchment by the apprentice.
All official records in the monastery were scrivened by the resident scribe.
Legal contracts needed to be scrivened by hand before the invention of printing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transcribed - written or typed copy of something spoken or written

Etymology

From "scriven", from Middle English scriven, from Old French escrivre (to write), ultimately from Latin scribere (to write).
