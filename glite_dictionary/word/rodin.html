Rodin

1. PROPN

/ roʊˈdæn / (AmE), / rəʊˈdæ̃ / (BrE)
(1840–1917) A French sculptor famous for his expressive renderings of the human form, such as 'The Thinker' and 'The Kiss.'
French sculptor (1840–1917)

Examples

The museum features several works by Rodin, including his famous sculpture 'The Thinker.'
Many art students study Rodin for his mastery of the human figure in sculpture.
Rodin's influence can be seen in modern sculpture around the world.

Etymology

From the surname of Auguste Rodin, French artist.
