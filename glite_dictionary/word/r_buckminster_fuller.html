R. Buckminster Fuller

1. PROPN

/ ˌɑr ˌbʌkmɪnstər ˈfʊlər / (AmE), / ˌɑː ˌbʌkmɪnstə ˈfʊlə / (BrE)
(1895–1983) United States architect, inventor, and visionary best known for inventing the geodesic dome and his influential ideas on design and sustainability.
U.S. architect and inventor, geodesic dome creator

Examples

R. Buckminster Fuller is famous for inventing the geodesic dome, a revolutionary architectural structure.
Many of R. Buckminster Fuller's ideas about sustainability were ahead of his time.
The exhibition included detailed models of buildings inspired by R. Buckminster Fuller's designs.
