minute

/ ˈmɪnɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
an official written record of what is discussed or decided at a meeting
official written record of a meeting

Examples

She referred to the minutes to confirm what was agreed last week.
The board approved the minutes from the last session before continuing.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: record - a written account of something kept for future reference
