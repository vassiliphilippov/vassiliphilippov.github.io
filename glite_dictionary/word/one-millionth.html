one-millionth

1. NOUN

/ ˌwʌn mɪlˈjʊnθ / (AmE), / ˌwʌn mɪlˈjʊnθ / (BrE)
each of the million equal parts into which something can be divided; the fraction equal to 1/1,000,000.
one part in a million; 1/1,000,000

Examples

Only a one-millionth of the sample was contaminated with the chemical.
Scientists can measure concentrations as small as a one-millionth of a gram.
The error margin was less than a one-millionth of a percent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: millionth - the ordinal/part representing one in a million
