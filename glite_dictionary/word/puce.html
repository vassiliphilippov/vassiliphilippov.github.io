puce

1. NOUN

/ pjus / (AmE), / pjuÀês / (BrE)
a dark color that varies between purplish-brown and dark red
a dark purplish-brown or dark red color

Examples

She chose a puce dress for the evening gala.
The artist added a touch of puce to bring warmth to the landscape painting.
His living room walls were painted a muted shade of puce.

Etymology

From French 'puce' (flea), alluding to the color of a flea.
