resourcefully

1. ADV

/ rɪˈsɔɹs.fə.li / (AmE), / rɪˈzɔːs.fəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows the ability to deal effectively with difficult situations and find quick and clever solutions.
in a resourceful manner

Examples

She handled the unexpected problem resourcefully and kept the team on track.
When the power went out, they resourcefully used their camping gear to cook dinner.
He resourcefully negotiated with multiple suppliers to get the best deal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resourceful - having the ability to find quick and clever ways to overcome difficulties

Etymology

From 'resourceful' + -ly, formed in English.
