tingling

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪŋɡlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋɡlɪŋ / (BrE)
A sensation of slight prickling or stinging, often felt as if many tiny pins are touching the skin.
slight prickling sensation on the skin

Examples

She felt a sudden tingling in her fingers after holding the ice.
The medicine caused a mild tingling sensation in his lips.
After sitting with her legs crossed, she noticed a tingling in her foot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prickling - a sensation of being punctured by small sharp points
  • Antonym: numbness - loss of sensation or feeling

Etymology

From the verb 'tingle', from Middle English 'tynglen'.
