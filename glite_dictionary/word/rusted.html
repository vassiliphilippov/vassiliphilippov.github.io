rusted

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌstɪd / (AmE), / ˈrʌstɪd / (BrE)
(of metal) covered with or affected by rust.
covered with or affected by rust

Examples

The old bicycle was completely rusted after years in the rain.
She struggled to open the rusted gate at the end of the path.
These rusted tools need cleaning before we can use them again.

Synset Examples

rusted hinges

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rusty - covered with rust; affected by rust

Etymology

From the verb 'rust' (to become covered with rust), ultimately from Old English 'rūst'.
