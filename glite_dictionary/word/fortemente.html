fortemente

1. ADV

/ foʊr.təˈmɛn.teɪ / (AmE), / fɔː.təˈmɛn.teɪ / (BrE)
(in music) Played loudly and with strength; with great force or emphasis.
loudly and strongly (music)

Examples

The composer marked the passage fortemente to create a dramatic effect.
The pianist attacked the chords fortemente, filling the hall with sound.
This section should be played fortemente according to the score.

Etymology

Italian, from Latin 'fortis' (strong).
