Chunnel

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃʌnəl / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌnəl / (BrE)
the railway tunnel beneath the English Channel linking France and England
the Channel Tunnel between France and England

Examples

Thousands of passengers travel through the Chunnel every day between England and France.
We took the train from Paris to London via the Chunnel.
The construction of the Chunnel was a major engineering achievement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Channel Tunnel - the official name for the railway tunnel under the English Channel

Etymology

Blend of 'Channel' and 'tunnel', referring to the tunnel under the English Channel.
