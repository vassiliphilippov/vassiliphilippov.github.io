to bituminize

1. VERB

/ bɪˈtuːməˌnaɪz / (AmE), / bɪˈtjuːmɪnaɪz / (BrE)
to treat or coat (a surface or material) with bitumen
treat or coat with bitumen

Examples

Engineers decided to bituminize the access road to make it more durable and weather-resistant.
The company plans to bituminize old railway sleepers as part of a recycling initiative.
Before finishing the playground, workers will bituminize the base to provide a smooth surface.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asphalt - to cover with asphalt

Etymology

From bitumen + -ize, meaning to make or treat with bitumen.
