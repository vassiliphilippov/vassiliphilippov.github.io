Lucknow

1. PROPN

/ ˈlʌkˌnaʊ / (AmE), / ˈlʌk.naʊ / (BrE)
a city in northern India, the capital of Uttar Pradesh, known for its historical significance during the Indian Mutiny of 1857.
capital city of Uttar Pradesh, India

Examples

She was born in Lucknow and spent her childhood exploring its vibrant markets.
Many tourists visit Lucknow to see its historical monuments and rich cultural heritage.
The siege of Lucknow was a significant event during the Indian Mutiny.
