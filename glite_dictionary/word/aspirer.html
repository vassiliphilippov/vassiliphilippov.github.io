aspirer

1. NOUN

/ əˈspaɪərər / (AmE), / əˈspaɪərə / (BrE)
a person who has ambitions to achieve something, especially in their career or personal development
person with ambitions or aspirations

Examples

As an aspirer, she always looked for opportunities to advance her career.
Many aspirers attended the business seminar hoping to network with industry leaders.
The film documents the struggles of a young aspirer trying to break into the art world.

Synset Examples

a lofty aspirant
two executive hopefuls joined the firm
the audience was full of Madonna wannabes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aspirant - a person who aspires to achieve a particular goal
  • Synonym: hopeful - a person who hopes to succeed or be chosen

Etymology

From the verb 'aspire', from Latin 'aspirare' (to breathe upon, to aim at).
