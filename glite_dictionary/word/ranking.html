ranking

1. ADJ

/ ˈræŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈræŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person) having a high rank or position, especially within an organization or hierarchy.
having a high rank

Examples

The board will meet with the ranking members of each department.
She is the ranking officer at the scene.
As the ranking diplomat, he represented his country at the event.

Synset Examples

superior officer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: senior - of a higher rank or position
  • Antonym: junior - of a lower rank or position

2. NOUN

/ ˈræŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
a position or level in a list that shows how important, successful, or good someone or something is compared to others
position or level on a list

Examples

Her university's ranking has improved significantly this year.
The team celebrated their number one ranking in the national tournament.
We checked the company's global ranking before investing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: position - a place where someone or something is located or has been put
  • Synonym: standing - the position, status, or reputation of someone or something

Etymology

From the verb 'rank' + -ing, used as a noun.
