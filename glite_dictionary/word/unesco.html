UNESCO

1. PROPN

/ juːˈnɛsˌkoʊ / (AmE), / juːˈnɛs.kəʊ / (BrE)
an agency of the United Nations that promotes international collaboration in education, science, culture, and communication
UN educational, science, and culture agency

Examples

The World Heritage Committee is a part of UNESCO and is responsible for maintaining the list of heritage sites.
UNESCO has launched several programs to promote literacy in developing countries.
Many historic sites have been added to the UNESCO World Heritage list over the years.

Etymology

Acronym from United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization.
