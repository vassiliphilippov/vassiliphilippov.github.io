twenty-twenty

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɛnti ˈtwɛnti / (AmE), / ˈtwɛnti ˈtwɛnti / (BrE)
Normal visual acuity, as measured by the ability to read letters on a chart at a distance of 20 feet.
normal visual acuity (20/20 vision)

Examples

After her eye surgery, she was thrilled to have twenty-twenty vision again.
Doctors confirmed that the boy's eyesight was twenty-twenty during his check-up.
Good lighting can help you make the most of your twenty-twenty eyesight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: normal vision - the state of having no visual impairment
  • Antonym: myopia - nearsightedness—difficulty seeing distant objects clearly
  • Antonym: hyperopia - farsightedness—difficulty seeing close objects clearly

Etymology

Refers to the Snellen chart standard, denoting seeing at 20 feet what should normally be seen at that distance.
