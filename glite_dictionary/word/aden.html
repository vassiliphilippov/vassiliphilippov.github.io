Aden

1. PROPN

/ ˈeɪdən / (AmE), / ˈɑːdn / (BrE)
A port city in southern Yemen, situated on the Gulf of Aden, noted for its strategic location and long history as a major trading center.
major port city in Yemen

Examples

The port of Aden has been a key commercial hub for centuries.
Many ships pass through Aden on their way to the Suez Canal.
Due to its location, Aden has played a major role in regional trade.
