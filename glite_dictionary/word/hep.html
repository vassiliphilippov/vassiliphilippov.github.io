hep

1. ADJ

/ hɛp / (AmE), / hɛp / (BrE)
aware of and knowledgeable about the latest trends or fashions; up to date.
informed about latest trends
slang archaic

Examples

She always knew the best clubs to go to and was considered really hep by her friends.
To be truly hep, you needed to dress in the latest fashions and know all the popular songs.
Even in his seventies, the jazz musician remained hep to everything going on in the scene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hip - aware of and knowledgeable about the latest trends; up to date

Etymology

Originally US slang, possibly a variant of 'hip', emerged in the early 20th century jazz scene.
