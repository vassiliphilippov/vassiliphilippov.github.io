smotherer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmʌðərɚ / (AmE), / ˈsmʌðərə / (BrE)
a person who smothers, stifles, or suppresses something or someone
person who smothers or suppresses

Examples

The overprotective mother was a classic smotherer, never allowing her son any independence.
As a smotherer of creativity, his harsh criticism discouraged innovative ideas in the team.
Nobody wants to be labeled a smotherer in their relationships.

Synset Examples

he is a real conversation stifler
I see from all the yawn smotherers that it is time to stop

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stifler - a person who stifles or suppresses

Etymology

From 'smother' + '-er', indicating one who performs the action.
