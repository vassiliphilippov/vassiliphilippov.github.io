arteria gastrica breves

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈtɪriə ɡæsˈtrɪkə ˈbreɪviːz / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə ɡæsˈtrɪkə ˈbreɪviːz / (BrE)
small arteries branching from the splenic artery to supply the greater curvature of the stomach
small arteries to the stomach's greater curvature
medical

Examples

During the surgery, the arteria gastrica breves were carefully ligated to prevent bleeding.
Damage to the arteria gastrica breves can compromise blood supply to part of the stomach.
The arteria gastrica breves arise from the end of the splenic artery near the spleen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: short gastric artery - a synonym for 'arteria gastrica breves', the small arteries to the stomach

Etymology

From Latin: 'arteria' (artery), 'gastrica' (of the stomach), 'breves' (short).
