closed-circuit

1. ADJ

/ kloʊzd ˈsɜːrkɪt / (AmE), / kləʊzd ˈsɜːkɪt / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of a circuit that is complete so that current can flow or signals are confined within an enclosed system
involving a self-contained electric or signal circuit

Examples

The building is monitored by a closed-circuit security system.
They watched the event on closed-circuit television from another room.
Hospitals often use closed-circuit cameras to observe patients discreetly.

Synset Examples

closed-circuit television

Related Terms

  • Antonym: open-circuit - relating to a circuit that is not complete, so current cannot flow
