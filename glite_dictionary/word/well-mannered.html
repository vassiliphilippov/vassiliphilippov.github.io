well-mannered

1. ADJ

/ wɛlˈmænɚd / (AmE), / wɛlˈmænəd / (BrE)
behaving in a polite and socially correct way
polite; socially correct in behavior

Examples

The children were praised for being well-mannered during the ceremony.
He is always well-mannered and considerate, even in stressful situations.
A well-mannered guest knows to thank their host before leaving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polite - having or showing behavior that is respectful and considerate of other people
  • Antonym: ill-mannered - rude or impolite in behavior
