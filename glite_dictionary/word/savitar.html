Savitar

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɑːvɪtɑr / (AmE), / ˈsævɪtɑː / (BrE)
A major Hindu god associated with the sun, regarded as representing the sun's life-giving power.
Hindu sun god representing life-giving power

Examples

Many hymns in the Rigveda are dedicated to Savitar, praising his role as the god of renewal.
The rising sun was seen as a manifestation of Savitar by ancient Hindus.
Artists often depict Savitar surrounded by rays of light, symbolizing his vital energy.

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Savitar', meaning 'stimulator' or 'vivifier', an epithet of the sun god.
