fifty

1. NUM

/ ˈfɪf.ti / (AmE), / ˈfɪf.ti / (BrE)
the number 50; ten more than forty
the number 50

Examples

She turned fifty years old last month.
There are fifty students in the auditorium for the assembly.
The speed limit on this road is fifty miles per hour.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty - the number 40
  • Degree Higher: sixty - the number 60

Etymology

Old English 'fīftig', from 'fīftig' (fifty), related to 'fīf' (five) + '-tig' (group of ten).
