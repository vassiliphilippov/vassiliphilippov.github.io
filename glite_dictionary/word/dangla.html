Dangla

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæŋɡlə / (AmE), / ˈdaŋɡlə / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in Chad, characterized by seven vowels and distinctions in vowel length
Chadic language spoken in Chad

Examples

Many linguists specialize in the phonetic characteristics of Dangla.
She learned to greet people in Dangla while living in Chad.
The local radio broadcast was partially in Dangla to reach a wider audience.
