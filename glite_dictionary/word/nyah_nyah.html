nyah nyah

1. NOUN

/ njɑː njɑː / (AmE), / njɑː njɑː / (BrE)
an exclamation or sound used to mock or tease someone, especially by children, often in a sing-song manner
mocking or teasing exclamation
informal

Examples

The children chanted nyah nyah after winning the game.
She stuck out her tongue and said nyah nyah to annoy her brother.
You could hear the playground erupt in nyah nyah as they teased each other.

Synset Examples
