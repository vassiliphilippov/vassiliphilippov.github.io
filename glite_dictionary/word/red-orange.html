red-orange

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛdˌɔrɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌɒrɪndʒ / (BrE)
A color that is a blend of orange and red, appearing as orange tinged with red.
color between orange and red

Examples

The sky turned a beautiful red-orange as the sun set over the mountains.
She chose a red-orange dress for the summer party because it was bright and cheerful.
The artist mixed the perfect shade of red-orange for the autumn leaves in his painting.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: orange - the color between red and yellow in the spectrum
  • Degree Higher: red - the color at the end of the visible spectrum next to orange and opposite violet
