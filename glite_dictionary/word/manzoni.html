Manzoni

1. PROPN

/ mænˈzoʊni / (AmE), / manˈtsəʊni / (BrE)
Alessandro Manzoni (1785–1873), an Italian novelist and poet best known for his novel 'The Betrothed'.
Italian novelist and poet (1785–1873)
