nonexplosive

1. ADJ

/ nɑnɪkˈsploʊsɪv / (AmE), / nɒnɪkˈspləʊsɪv / (BrE)
not capable of exploding or causing an explosion; not explosive.
not capable of explosion

Examples

The laboratory is designed to store only nonexplosive chemicals for safety reasons.
They switched to a nonexplosive fuel to reduce the risk of accidents.
Helium is a nonexplosive gas commonly used in balloons.

Synset Examples

nonexplosive gases
a nonexplosive fuel

Related Terms

  • Antonym: explosive - able or likely to explode

Etymology

From prefix 'non-' (not) + 'explosive'.
