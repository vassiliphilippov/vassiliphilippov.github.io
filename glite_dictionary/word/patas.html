patas

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːtəs / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtəs / (BrE)
a reddish long-tailed monkey (Erythrocebus patas) native to West Africa, known for its speed and terrestrial habits
a reddish long-tailed African monkey

Examples

The patas is known for being one of the fastest primates on land.
In the wild, patas monkeys often travel in large groups across the savanna.
Researchers observed a troop of patas foraging for insects near the river.

Etymology

From Wolof *patas*, the local name for the monkey.
