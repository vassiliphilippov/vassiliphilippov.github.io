unblushing

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈblʌʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈblʌʃɪŋ / (BrE)
showing no shame or embarrassment; shameless
showing no shame or embarrassment

Examples

The unblushing liar repeated her story without a hint of remorse.
He gave an unblushing defense of policies that most found indefensible.
With unblushing pride, she accepted the dubious award.

Synset Examples

a shameless imposter
an unblushing apologist for fascism

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shameless - showing no shame, moral restraint, or modesty
  • Antonym: blushing - showing embarrassment or shame by reddening of the face

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'blushing' (showing embarrassment or shame by reddening).
