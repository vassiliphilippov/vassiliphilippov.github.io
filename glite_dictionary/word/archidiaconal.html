archidiaconal

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑːrkɪˌdaɪəˈkoʊnəl / (AmE), / ˌɑːkɪˌdaɪəˈkəʊnəl / (BrE)
relating to an archdeacon or the office of an archdeacon
relating to an archdeacon's office

Examples

The archidiaconal duties included overseeing church property and discipline within the diocese.
The council met in the archidiaconal offices adjacent to the cathedral.
Her appointment to an archidiaconal position marked a significant step in her ecclesiastical career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: archdeacon - a senior Christian cleric ranking just below a bishop

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'archidiaconalis', from 'archidiaconus' (archdeacon).
