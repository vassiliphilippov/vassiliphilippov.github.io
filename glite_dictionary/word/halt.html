halt

1. NOUN

/ hɑlt / (AmE), / hɔːlt / (BrE)
A temporary stop or interruption in progress or movement.
temporary stop or interruption

Examples

The construction work came to a halt due to heavy rain.
After decades of expansion, the company brought its overseas operations to a halt.
There was a sudden halt in traffic following the accident.

Synset Examples

a nuclear freeze

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pause - a short period in which something is stopped before it is started again
  • Synonym: stop - a complete cessation of activity or movement
