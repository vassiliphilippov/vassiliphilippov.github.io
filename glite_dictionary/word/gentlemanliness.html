gentlemanliness

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛntəlmənlinəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛntlmənlinəs / (BrE)
The qualities, conduct, or characteristics befitting a gentleman, such as courtesy, honor, and refinement.
qualities of being a gentleman

Examples

His gentlemanliness was evident in every interaction, from his polite speech to his respectful gestures.
Many admired the gentlemanliness of old-fashioned movie stars and wished society still valued it.
She was impressed by his gentlemanliness, especially when he helped the elderly woman across the street.

Synset Examples

The trauma and loss of young male lives during World War I destabilized the ideals of imperial gentlemanliness and the nation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gentility - the quality of being genteel or well-mannered
  • Synonym: chivalry - qualities of bravery and courteous conduct, especially towards women

Etymology

From 'gentleman' + '-liness', patterned after words like 'manliness'.
