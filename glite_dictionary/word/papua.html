Papua

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɑːpjuə / (AmE), / ˈpæpjuə / (BrE)
the southeastern part of Papua New Guinea, forming a distinct region within the country
southeastern region of Papua New Guinea

Examples

The province of Papua is known for its rich natural resources and diverse cultures.
Many languages are spoken across Papua and its neighboring regions.
After traveling through Papua, she wrote a book about its people and landscapes.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Papua New Guinea - country in Oceania on the eastern half of the island of New Guinea

Etymology

From Malay 'papuwah' (frizzy-haired), referring to the indigenous people.
