piccaninny

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɪkəˈnɪni / (AmE), / ˌpɪkəˈnɪni / (BrE)
An offensive and dated term for a Black child; widely recognized as a racial slur and considered highly insulting.
offensive term for a Black child
offensive archaic

Examples

The word piccaninny is now recognized as a deeply offensive racial slur.
Many classic books contain the term piccaninny, reflecting prejudices of their time.
She explained why using piccaninny is inappropriate and hurtful.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'pequenino' (diminutive of 'pequeno', meaning 'small').
