detailing

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈteɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈteɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
a thorough or individualized description of something, often providing specific details or features
individualized description with details

Examples

The report included a detailing of each team member’s responsibilities.
Her presentation began with a detailing of the project's objectives.
The contract features a detailing of all payment terms and conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: description - a spoken or written account of something

Etymology

From the verb 'detail' + -ing.
