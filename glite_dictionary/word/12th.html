12th

1. ADJ

/ twɛlfθ / (AmE), / twɛlfθ / (BrE)
coming after the eleventh and before the thirteenth in a sequence; being number twelve in order or position
ordinal number for position twelve

Examples

She finished in 12th place in the marathon.
His office is on the 12th floor of the building.
The concert is scheduled for the 12th of June.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eleventh - coming after the tenth and before the twelfth; being number eleven
  • Degree Higher: thirteenth - coming after the twelfth and before the fourteenth; being number thirteen
