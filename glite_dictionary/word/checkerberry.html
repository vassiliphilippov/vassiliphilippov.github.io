checkerberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛkɚˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛkəˌbɛri / (BrE)
A low-growing shrub native to eastern North America, bearing shiny aromatic leaves, white bell-shaped flowers, and spicy red berries from which wintergreen oil is obtained.
creeping shrub with spicy red berries; wintergreen

Examples

Many hikers enjoy chewing on checkerberry leaves for their refreshing wintergreen flavor.
The dense carpet of checkerberry brightens the forest floor in autumn with its red berries.
Extracts from the checkerberry plant are used to make essential wintergreen oil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wintergreen - another name for the same plant; refers to its aromatic leaves yielding wintergreen oil

2. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛkərˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛkəbri / (BrE)
A small, spicy red fruit, also known as wintergreen, from which wintergreen oil is obtained.
spicy red fruit; source of wintergreen oil

Examples

We found a patch of checkerberry on our woodland walk.
The checkerberry plant is valued for its flavor and medicinal oil.
Children picked checkerberry from the mossy ground and tasted the spicy fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wintergreen - plant whose leaves and berries are the source of wintergreen oil
