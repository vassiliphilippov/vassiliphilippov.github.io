gladfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlæd.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɡlæd.fəl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of experiencing joy and pleasure; gladness
state of joy and pleasure

Examples

Her gladfulness was evident as she smiled throughout the celebration.
There was a certain gladfulness in his tone when he spoke of his achievements.
The children's gladfulness filled the playground with laughter and joy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gladness - the state or quality of being glad
  • Antonym: sadness - the state of being sad

Etymology

Formed from 'glad' + '-fulness', modeled after 'gladness'.
