nondeductible

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.dɪˈdʌk.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.dɪˈdʌk.tɪ.bəl / (BrE)
(of an expense or item) not permitted to be subtracted from taxable income or not allowable as a deduction.
not allowable as a deduction

Examples

Medical expenses that exceed a certain amount may still be nondeductible under current tax laws.
Contributions to that fund are nondeductible for most taxpayers.
He was surprised to learn that the interest paid on his loan was nondeductible.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: deductible - (of an expense) able to be subtracted from taxable income

Etymology

Formed from non- (not) + deductible (that can be deducted).
