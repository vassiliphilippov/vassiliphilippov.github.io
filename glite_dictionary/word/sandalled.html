sandalled

1. ADJ

/ ˈsændld / (AmE), / ˈsand(ə)ld / (BrE)
wearing sandals or fitted with sandals on the feet
wearing sandals

Examples

The sandalled children ran across the hot pavement.
She admired the sandalled feet of the statue in the garden.
His sandalled footsteps echoed on the ancient temple floor.

Etymology

From 'sandal' + '-ed' suffix, meaning 'provided with sandals'.
