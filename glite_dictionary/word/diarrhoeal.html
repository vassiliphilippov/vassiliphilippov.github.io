diarrhoeal

1. ADJ

/ ˌdaɪəˈroʊəl / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈriːəl / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by diarrhea
relating to diarrhea
medical british

Examples

The increase in diarrhoeal diseases was attributed to contaminated water supplies.
Improving sanitation is crucial for reducing diarrhoeal infections among children.
Many developing countries continue to struggle with high rates of diarrhoeal illness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diarrheal - relating to or characterized by diarrhea (American spelling)

Etymology

From diarrhoea (spelled 'diarrhea' in American English) with adjectival suffix '-al'.
