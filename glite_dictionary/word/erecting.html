erecting

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈrɛktɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛktɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of building or putting up a structure or object
the act of building or putting up

Examples

The erecting of the new bridge will begin next month.
During the erecting of the statue, the crew faced several challenges.
Strict safety protocols are followed in the erecting of skyscrapers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: construction - the process of building something, especially large structures

Etymology

From Latin 'erectus' (upright), from 'erigere' (to raise, set up), via English verb 'erect'.
