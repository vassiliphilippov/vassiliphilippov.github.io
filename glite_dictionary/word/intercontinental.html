intercontinental

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərˌkɑntɪˈnɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəkɒntɪˈnɛntəl / (BrE)
extending or occurring between or among continents, especially referring to activities, travel, or connections that involve more than one continent.
between continents

Examples

The new airline offers several intercontinental flights each week.
Scientists organized an intercontinental research project to study climate change.
The competition attracted intercontinental teams from Europe, Asia, and North America.

Synset Examples

intercontinental exploration
intercontinental flights

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: continental - relating to a continent or continents

Etymology

From inter- (between) + continental (of or relating to a continent), first attested in the early 20th century.
