insusceptible

1. ADJ

/ ɪn.səˈsɛp.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ɪn.səˈsɛp.tɪ.bəl / (BrE)
not likely to be influenced or affected by something; not susceptible.
not affected or influenced by something

Examples

Some children seem insusceptible to common colds no matter the season.
These materials are insusceptible to damage from moisture or heat.
He was surprisingly insusceptible to peer pressure in high school.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: susceptible - easily influenced or harmed by something

Etymology

From Latin in- (not) + susceptibilis (capable of receiving, susceptible).
