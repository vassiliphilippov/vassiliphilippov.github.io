Ca

1. NOUN

/ si eɪ / (AmE), / siː eɪ / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for calcium, a white metallic element that is essential to living organisms and is the fifth most abundant element in the earth's crust
chemical symbol for calcium

Examples

The periodic table lists Ca as the symbol for calcium.
The doctor checked my Ca levels to make sure my bones were healthy.
Limestone contains a high percentage of Ca, which is used in construction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calcium - a white metallic element essential to living organisms

Etymology

From New Latin 'calcium', with symbol 'Ca' based on the first two letters.
