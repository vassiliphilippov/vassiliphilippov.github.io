fan-leaved

1. ADJ

/ ˈfæn liːvd / (AmE), / ˈfan liːvd / (BrE)
(of a plant) having leaves shaped like a fan.
having fan-shaped leaves

Examples

The fan-leaved palm creates a dramatic silhouette in the garden.
Many fan-leaved plants are well adapted to hot, dry climates.
A fan-leaved fern adds an exotic touch to the shady corner of the yard.
