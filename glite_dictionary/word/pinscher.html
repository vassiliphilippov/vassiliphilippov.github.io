pinscher

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪnʃər / (AmE), / ˈpɪnʃə / (BrE)
a dog of any of several breeds characterized by a short-haired coat and usually with cropped ears and tail, originally bred in Germany
a breed of short-haired dog

Examples

The pinscher is known for its alertness and loyalty to its family.
After much research, they decided to adopt a pinscher from the local animal shelter.
Many dog owners appreciate the playful nature of the pinscher.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Doberman - a large breed of domestic dog known as Doberman Pinscher
  • Synonym: Miniature Pinscher - a small breed of dog, also called Min Pin

Etymology

From German 'Pinscher', possibly from English 'pinch' (referring to cropped ears).
