Masonry

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪsənɹi / (AmE), / ˈmeɪsənri / (BrE)
The Freemasons collectively; the worldwide organization of Freemasons.
the Freemasons collectively

Examples

Many people are fascinated by the rituals and symbols associated with Masonry.
He dedicated much of his life to the study of Masonry and its history.
Questions about the influence of Masonry sometimes arise in discussions about politics and society.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Freemasonry - the system and practices of Freemasons

Etymology

From 'Mason' (a stoneworker) and the organization of Freemasons that emerged from medieval guilds.
