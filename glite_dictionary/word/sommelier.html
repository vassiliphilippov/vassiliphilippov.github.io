sommelier

1. NOUN

/ səˈmɛljəɪ / (AmE), / sɒˈmɛljeɪ / (BrE)
a person in a restaurant or hotel who is responsible for wine service and advising guests on wine choices
wine specialist in restaurants

Examples

The sommelier recommended an excellent Pinot Noir to complement our meal.
As a sommelier, she takes pride in curating an extensive wine list for the restaurant.
We asked the sommelier for advice, and he suggested a perfect pairing for our seafood dish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wine steward - a person who manages wine service in a restaurant

Etymology

From French 'sommelier' (wine steward).
