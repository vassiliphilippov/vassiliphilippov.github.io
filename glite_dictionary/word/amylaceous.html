amylaceous

1. ADJ

/ ˌæm.jəˈleɪ.ʃəs / (AmE), / ˌam.ɪˈleɪ.ʃəs / (BrE)
resembling starch, or containing starch; starchy
resembling or containing starch
medical

Examples

The laboratory technician observed an amylaceous substance coating the surface of the tuber.
Some fungi exhibit amylaceous reactions when tested with iodine solutions.
Their diet consisted mainly of amylaceous foods such as potatoes and rice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: starchy - containing or resembling starch

Etymology

From Latin amylum (starch) + -aceous (resembling or belonging to).
