Al Ummah

1. PROPN

/ ˌæl ʊˈmʌmə / (AmE), / ˌæl ʊˈmʌmə / (BrE)
a terrorist organization formed in India in 1992, thought to be responsible for bombings in southern India in 1998.
Indian terrorist organization

Examples

The Indian authorities increased security measures after the activities of Al Ummah came to light.
Several members of Al Ummah were arrested in connection with the 1998 bombings.
Press reports linked Al Ummah to a series of attacks in southern India.

Etymology

From Arabic 'al-ummah' meaning 'the community' or 'the nation.'
