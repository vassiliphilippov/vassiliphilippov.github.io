anonym

1. NOUN

/ əˈnɑː.nɪm / (AmE), / əˈnɒn.ɪm / (BrE)
a fictitious or assumed name used by a person, especially when adopting a different social role; a pseudonym.
fictitious or assumed name; pseudonym

Examples

Many writers in the 19th century published their works under an anonym.
The artist chose to use an anonym to keep his real identity secret.
In online forums, it is common to post opinions under an anonym.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pseudonym - a fictitious name used by an author or artist
  • Synonym: alias - an assumed name used to conceal identity

Etymology

From German 'Anonym', from Greek 'anōnymos' (without a name).
