curtainless

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɝː.tən.lɪs / (AmE), / ˈkɜː.tən.lɪs / (BrE)
having no curtains or drapes
without curtains

Examples

The abandoned house had dark, curtainless windows that gave it a bleak appearance.
Light streamed in through the curtainless bedroom, waking him at dawn.
They stared out of the curtainless glass, hoping to catch a glimpse of the arriving guests.

Synset Examples

blank, curtainless windows stared back at her

Related Terms

  • Antonym: curtained - having curtains
