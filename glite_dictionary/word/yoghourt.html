yoghourt

1. NOUN

/ ˈjoʊ.ɡɚt / (AmE), / ˈjɒɡ.ət / (BrE)
A semi-solid food made from milk fermented by bacteria, resulting in a tart, creamy texture; also spelled 'yogurt'.
fermented milk food; variant of 'yogurt'

Examples

She enjoys eating plain yoghourt with fresh fruit for breakfast.
Adding a spoonful of yoghourt can make soups and sauces creamier.
The recipe calls for Greek yoghourt to add texture and flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yogurt - A food made by fermenting milk with bacteria; standard modern spelling

Etymology

From Turkish 'yoğurt', via earlier languages; multiple spellings exist in English.
