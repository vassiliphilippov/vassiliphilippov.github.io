pyelogram

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪ.əˌloʊ.ɡræm / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ.əl.əˌɡræm / (BrE)
an X-ray image of the kidneys and ureters, typically used to diagnose disorders of the urinary tract
X-ray of kidneys and ureters
medical

Examples

The doctor recommended a pyelogram to investigate the cause of her persistent back pain.
A pyelogram can reveal blockages or abnormalities in the urinary tract.
He underwent a pyelogram to diagnose a suspected kidney stone.

Etymology

From Greek 'pyelos' (trough, pelvis) + 'gramma' (drawing, record).
