to acetylise

1. VERB

/ əˈsiːtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / əˈsiːtɪlaɪz / (BrE)
to introduce an acetyl group into (a chemical compound)
introduce an acetyl group (chemistry)

Examples

Many enzymes are able to acetylise proteins during metabolic processes.
The compound was acetylised to increase its solubility in organic solvents.
Researchers often acetylise aspirin as part of the synthesis procedure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to acetylate - to introduce an acetyl group into (a chemical compound)

Etymology

From 'acetyl' (a chemical group) + '-ise' (verb-forming suffix).
