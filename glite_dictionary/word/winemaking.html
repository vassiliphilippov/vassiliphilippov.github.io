winemaking

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪnˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪnˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process and technique of growing grapes and producing wine
the process of making wine

Examples

He developed a passion for winemaking after visiting vineyards in France.
Modern winemaking combines traditional methods with advanced technology.
The region is famous for its long history of winemaking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vinification - the process of making wine by fermentation

Etymology

Formed from the noun 'wine' and the present participle of 'make', patterned after similar terms like 'cheesemaking'.
