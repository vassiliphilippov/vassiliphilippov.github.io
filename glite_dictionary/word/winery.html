winery

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪnəri / (AmE), / ˈwaɪnəri / (BrE)
a place where wine is produced, especially commercially
establishment where wine is made

Examples

We toured a famous winery in California's Napa Valley last summer.
The family has operated their small winery for three generations.
Visitors can sample different wines right at the winery after watching how they're made.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vineyard - a plantation of grapevines, typically for winemaking

Etymology

From wine + -ery (denoting place of business or activity).
