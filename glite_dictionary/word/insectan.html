insectan

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈsɛktən / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛktən / (BrE)
of or relating to insects, especially those of the class Insecta
relating to insects (Insecta)

Examples

The scientist analyzed several insectan features under the microscope.
Her research focuses on insectan anatomy and physiology.
Certain insectan species are vital for pollination in this ecosystem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insect - a small arthropod animal of the class Insecta

Etymology

From Latin 'insectum' (insect) with the suffix '-an'.
