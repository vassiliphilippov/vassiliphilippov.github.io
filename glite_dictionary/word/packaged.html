packaged

1. ADJ

/ ˈpækɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˈpækɪdʒd / (BrE)
(of goods) enclosed in a container or protective covering, especially for sale or transport.
enclosed in packaging

Examples

Most packaged foods in supermarkets include a list of ingredients on the label.
The packaged toys were neatly stacked on the shelves, ready for sale.
Fresh fruit is often healthier than packaged snacks.

Synset Examples

packaged cereals

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unpackaged - not enclosed in a container or protective covering
