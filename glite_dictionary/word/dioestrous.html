dioestrous

1. ADJ

/ daɪˈoʊstrəs / (AmE), / daɪˈəʊstrəs / (BrE)
(of certain animals) in or relating to a period of sexual inactivity between estrous cycles within a breeding season.
period of sexual inactivity between estrous cycles
medical

Examples

During the dioestrous phase, the female dog shows no interest in mating.
The veterinarian explained that reproductive hormones are low in dioestrous animals.
Some species have a short dioestrous period between breeding cycles.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: estrous - displaying or relating to the period of sexual receptivity in female mammals

Etymology

From Greek di- (twice) + oistros (gadfly, frenzy).
