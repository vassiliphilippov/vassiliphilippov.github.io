cymbalist

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪmbəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɪmbəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who plays the cymbals, especially as part of a musical ensemble
person who plays the cymbals

Examples

The orchestra's cymbalist prepared to deliver a dramatic crash during the finale.
As a dedicated cymbalist, she practiced her timing every day.
The band hired a new cymbalist for their upcoming tour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: percussionist - a person who plays percussion instruments

Etymology

From 'cymbal' + '-ist', indicating a person who plays a particular instrument.
