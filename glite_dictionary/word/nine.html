nine

1. NUM

/ naɪn / (AmE), / naɪn / (BrE)
The cardinal number equal to eight plus one; 9.
cardinal number; 9

Examples

There are nine players on a baseball team.
She scored nine points in the quiz competition.
We waited nine days for the package to arrive.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eight - the cardinal number that is one less than nine
  • Degree Higher: ten - the cardinal number that is one more than nine

Etymology

Old English *nigon*, from Proto-Germanic *niwun*; related to Latin *novem* and Greek *ennea*.

5. NOUN

/ naɪn / (AmE), / naɪn / (BrE)

playing card with nine pips

Examples

If you play the nine now, you might have a better chance to win.
There are only four nines in a standard deck of cards.
