broncobuster

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɑːŋ.kəˌbʌs.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈbrɒŋ.kəˌbʌs.tə / (BrE)
a person who breaks wild or untrained horses, especially in the American West
person who breaks wild horses

Examples

The legendary broncobuster could tame even the wildest mustang on the range.
After years as a broncobuster, he had more than a few scars to show for it.
The rodeo featured a famous broncobuster demonstrating his skills in the arena.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrangler - a person in charge of horses or livestock on a ranch

Etymology

From 'bronco' (Spanish for 'rough, unbroken horse') + 'buster' (one who breaks or tames).
