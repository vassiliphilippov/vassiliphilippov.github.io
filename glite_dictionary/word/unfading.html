unfading

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfeɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈfeɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
never losing freshness, color, or vitality; not fading
never fading; always fresh or bright

Examples

Her unfading beauty was admired by all who met her.
The artist captured the unfading charm of the countryside in her paintings.
They spoke of a legend about an unfading flower that symbolizes eternal love.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fading - losing color, freshness, or brightness gradually

Etymology

From un- (not) + fading (present participle of 'fade').
