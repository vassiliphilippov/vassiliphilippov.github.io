Saivism

1. NOUN

/ ˈshaɪˌvɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈʃaɪvɪzəm / (BrE)
one of the major traditions within Hinduism, centered on the worship of the god Shiva as the supreme being
Hindu tradition worshipping Shiva

Examples

Many temples across South India are dedicated to Saivism, reflecting its long history in the region.
He spent years studying Saivism and its philosophical texts.
Pilgrimages to sacred sites are an important aspect of Saivism for many followers.

Synset Examples

However, we should also note that in the original Shaivism (of the Pashupatas) the concept of the spiritual path was exceedingly mystical, bizarre, and controversial.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Shiva - the principal Hindu deity worshipped in Saivism
  • Antonym: Vaishnavism - worship of Vishnu; another major Hindu tradition

Etymology

From Sanskrit *śaiva* (relating to Shiva) + English *-ism*.
