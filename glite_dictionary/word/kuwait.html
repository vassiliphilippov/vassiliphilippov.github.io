Kuwait

1. PROPN

/ kuˈweɪt / (AmE), / kuˈweɪt / (BrE)
the capital city and main seaport of Kuwait, located on the Persian Gulf
capital city of Kuwait

Examples

The skyline of Kuwait is dotted with modern skyscrapers and historic mosques.
After moving to Kuwait, she quickly adapted to the bustling city life.
Many international companies have their headquarters in Kuwait due to its strategic location.

2. PROPN

/ kuːˈweɪt / (AmE), / kuːˈweɪt / (BrE)
A country in Western Asia, located on the northwest coast of the Persian Gulf, known for its petroleum resources and as an Arab kingdom.
country in Western Asia; Arab kingdom

Examples

Oil exports are a major part of Kuwait's economy.
She traveled to Kuwait for a business conference last year.
The culture and traditions of Kuwait are influenced by its history as an Arab kingdom.

Etymology

From Arabic الكويت‎ (al-Kuwayt), diminutive of كوت‎ (kūt, 'fortress').
