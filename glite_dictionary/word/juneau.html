Juneau

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒuːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːnəʊ / (BrE)
the capital city of Alaska, located in the southeast part of the state
capital city of Alaska

Examples

Juneau is only accessible by plane or boat, making it unique among U.S. state capitals.
The cruise ship docked in Juneau for a day during our Alaskan tour.
The government offices of Alaska are headquartered in Juneau.

Etymology

Named after gold prospector Joe Juneau.
