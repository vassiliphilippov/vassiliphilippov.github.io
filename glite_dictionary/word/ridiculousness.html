ridiculousness

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈdɪkjələsnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈdɪkjʊləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being absurd, laughable, or at variance with reason; absurdity
the quality of being ridiculous or absurd

Examples

The ridiculousness of his argument was apparent to everyone in the room.
She laughed at the ridiculousness of the situation, unable to take it seriously.
Sometimes the ridiculousness of modern life makes me want to turn off the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absurdity - the quality of being absurd or inconsistent with reason

Etymology

From 'ridiculous' + '-ness'; 'ridiculous' from Latin ridiculus (laughable, absurd).
