Matamoros

1. PROPN

/ ˌmætəˈmɔːrəs / (AmE), / ˌmatəˈmɔːrɒs / (BrE)
A city in northeastern Mexico, located across the Rio Grande from Brownsville, Texas.
city in northeastern Mexico

Examples

Many tourists cross the border to visit Matamoros for its markets and cultural attractions.
The city of Matamoros experienced rapid growth due to its proximity to the U.S. border.
After visiting Brownsville, we decided to spend the afternoon exploring Matamoros.

Etymology

From Spanish, meaning 'Moor killer', a common surname in Spain and also used for several towns.
