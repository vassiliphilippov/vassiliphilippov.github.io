manlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈmænˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmæn.laɪk / (BrE)
Having qualities or characteristics typical of a man.
having qualities of a man

Examples

The statue had a manlike figure, broad-shouldered and tall.
His manlike sense of responsibility impressed everyone at the company.
She spoke with a strong, manlike voice that commanded attention.

Synset Examples

a deep male voice
manly sports

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manly - having qualities traditionally associated with men
  • Synonym: masculine - having characteristics associated with men

2. ADJ

/ ˈmænˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmæn.laɪk / (BrE)
Possessing qualities or characteristics considered typical of a man.
having qualities typical of a man

Examples

His manlike bravery impressed everyone at the ceremony.
The statue had a distinctly manlike appearance, despite being made of stone.
She faced her fears in a manlike manner, showing strength and resolve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masculine - having qualities or appearance traditionally associated with men
  • Antonym: womanlike - having qualities or characteristics considered typical of a woman

Etymology

From Old English 'mann' (man) + 'like' (having the characteristics of).

3. ADJ

/ ˈmænˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmænˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having qualities or appearance resembling a man or human being.
resembling a man or human being

Examples

The statue's manlike features made it seem almost alive.
Some ancient myths describe manlike creatures living deep in the forests.
Her robot design was intentionally manlike to make it more approachable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humanlike - resembling a human; more general form
  • Synonym: manly - having qualities traditionally associated with men
  • Antonym: unlike - dissimilar or not alike
