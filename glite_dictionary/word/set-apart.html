set-apart

1. ADJ

/ sɛt əˈpɑrt / (AmE), / sɛt əˈpɑːt / (BrE)
being or feeling separated or kept apart from others; isolated or distinct
separated or isolated from others

Examples

The old house had a set-apart atmosphere that made visitors feel uneasy.
She felt set-apart from her colleagues due to her unique background.
Despite being invited, he remained set-apart during the entire celebration.

Synset Examples

she felt detached from the group
could not remain the isolated figure he had been
thought of herself as alone and separated from the others
had a set-apart feeling

Related Terms

  • Synonym: isolated - separated from others; alone
  • Synonym: detached - emotionally or physically separate from others
  • Antonym: integrated - incorporated as part of a larger group
