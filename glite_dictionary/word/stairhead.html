stairhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈsterˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈsteəˌhɛd / (BrE)
the platform or landing at the top of a staircase
platform at top of a staircase

Examples

A painting hung above the stairhead, overlooking the bedrooms below.
She paused at the stairhead to catch her breath before entering the hallway.
From the stairhead, you can see all the way down to the front door.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: landing - the level area at the top or bottom of a staircase

Etymology

From 'stair' + 'head', indicating the top of a staircase.
