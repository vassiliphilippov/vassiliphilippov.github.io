Osaka

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈsɑːkə / (AmE), / əʊˈsɑːkə / (BrE)
a large port city on southern Honshu, Japan, known as a major commercial and industrial center
major city and port in Japan

Examples

I plan to visit Osaka during my trip to Japan next spring.
Osaka is famous for its modern architecture and vibrant street food culture.
The conference will be held in Osaka this year, attracting delegates from around the world.
