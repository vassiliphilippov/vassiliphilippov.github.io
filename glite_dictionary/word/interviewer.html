interviewer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪntə(r)ˌvjuːər / (AmE), / ˈɪntəvjuːə / (BrE)
a person who asks questions to obtain information from someone, especially in a formal setting such as a job interview or news program
person who conducts an interview

Examples

The interviewer asked thoughtful questions during the job interview.
She impressed the interviewer with her clear and confident answers.
As a seasoned interviewer, he is skilled at making guests feel at ease.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: interviewee - person who is interviewed

Etymology

From 'interview' + '-er', indicating 'one who interviews'.
