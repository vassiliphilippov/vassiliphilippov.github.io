resplendent

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈsplɛndənt / (AmE), / rɪˈsplɛndənt / (BrE)
Shining brilliantly; very bright and impressive in appearance.
shining brightly; very impressive

Examples

The bride looked resplendent in her white gown decorated with pearls and lace.
The palace gardens were resplendent with blooming flowers of every color imaginable.
He wore a resplendent uniform adorned with medals and golden buttons.

Synset Examples

a glorious spring morning
a glorious sunset
splendid costumes
a kind of splendiferous native simplicity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glorious - having great beauty and splendor
  • Synonym: splendid - magnificent and very impressive

Etymology

From Latin resplendent-, resplendēre (to shine brightly).
