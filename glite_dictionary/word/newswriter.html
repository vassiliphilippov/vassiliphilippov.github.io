newswriter

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌraɪtər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌraɪtə / (BrE)
a journalist who writes news stories for newspapers, magazines, or broadcast media
journalist who writes news stories

Examples

The newswriter submitted her article just before the deadline.
As a seasoned newswriter, he covered international politics with great insight.
Many aspiring journalists hope to become a respected newswriter one day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: journalist - a person who writes or reports news
  • Synonym: reporter - a person who investigates and reports news stories

Etymology

From 'news' + 'writer', modelled after similar occupational terms.
