Halcion

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæl.si.ɑn / (AmE), / ˈhæl.si.ɒn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand of the drug triazolam, a benzodiazepine used as a sleeping pill for people with insomnia.
trademarked sleeping pill (triazolam)
medical

Examples

Her doctor prescribed Halcion to help with her insomnia.
After taking Halcion, he found it much easier to fall asleep at night.
Patients are often warned about potential side effects before being given Halcion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triazolam - The generic name for the drug sold under the brand name Halcion
