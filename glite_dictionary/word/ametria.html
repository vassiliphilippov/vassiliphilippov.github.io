ametria

1. NOUN

/ əˈmiː.tri.ə / (AmE), / əˈmiː.tri.ə / (BrE)
The congenital absence of the uterus.
congenital absence of the uterus
medical

Examples

The rare condition known as ametria can have significant implications for reproductive health.
Diagnosis of ametria often relies on advanced imaging techniques.
She learned she had ametria after undergoing evaluation for absent menstruation.

Etymology

From Greek a- (without) + mētra (womb, uterus).
