MEd

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛm ˈɛd / (AmE), / ˌɛm ˈɛd / (BrE)
an abbreviation for Master of Education, a postgraduate degree in education.
abbreviation for Master of Education degree

Examples

She earned her MEd last year and now works as a school counselor.
Applicants with an MEd may have an advantage for teaching positions.
His MEd focused on curriculum development and educational leadership.

Etymology

Abbreviated from Master of Education.
