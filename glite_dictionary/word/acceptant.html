acceptant

1. ADJ

/ əkˈsɛp.tənt / (AmE), / əkˈsɛp.tənt / (BrE)
willing to accept or receive new ideas or things
willing to accept

Examples

She has an acceptant attitude toward change and new experiences.
The school encouraged an acceptant environment for students from diverse backgrounds.
His acceptant nature made him a great listener and friend.

Synset Examples

acceptive of every new idea
an acceptant type of mind

Related Terms

  • Synonym: receptive - willing to consider or accept new suggestions or ideas
  • Antonym: resistant - unwilling to accept something new or different

Etymology

From Latin 'acceptans', present participle of 'acceptare' (to accept).
