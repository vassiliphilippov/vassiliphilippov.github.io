parang

1. NOUN

/ pəˈrɑŋ / (AmE), / pəˈraŋ / (BrE)
a large, heavy, straight-edged knife used especially in Malaysia and Indonesia for cutting through dense vegetation or as a tool or weapon
Malaysian/Indonesian machete-like knife

Examples

The guide cleared a path through the jungle with a parang.
He bought a handmade parang as a souvenir during his trip to Indonesia.
Using his parang, he trimmed the thick vines blocking the trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: machete - a broad, heavy knife used as an implement or weapon

Etymology

From Malay 'parang' (a large knife or machete).
