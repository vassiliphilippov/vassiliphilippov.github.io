dozy

1. ADJ

/ ˈdoʊ.zi / (AmE), / ˈdəʊ.zi / (BrE)
feeling sleepy or only half awake; inclined to doze.
sleepy; half awake

Examples

After lunch, I always feel a bit dozy at my desk.
The dozy child could barely keep her eyes open during the car ride.
He gave a dozy smile and drifted off to sleep.

Synset Examples

made drowsy by the long ride
it seemed a pity to disturb the drowsing (or dozing) professor
a tired dozy child
the nodding (or napping) grandmother in her rocking chair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drowsy - feeling sleepy and lethargic
  • Antonym: alert - quick to notice and respond; fully awake
