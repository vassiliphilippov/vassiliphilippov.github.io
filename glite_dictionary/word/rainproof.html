rainproof

1. ADJ

/ ˈreɪnˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈreɪnˌpruːf / (BrE)
Not allowing water to pass through; resistant to rain.
resistant to rain; waterproof

Examples

My new jacket is completely rainproof, so I stay dry even during heavy storms.
These rainproof boots are perfect for hiking in wet conditions.
We used a rainproof cover to protect our equipment from the downpour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waterproof - impervious to water; does not allow water to pass through
