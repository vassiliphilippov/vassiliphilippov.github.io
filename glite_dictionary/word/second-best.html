second-best

1. ADJ

/ ˈsek.ənd ˌbɛst / (AmE), / ˌsek.ənd ˈbest / (BrE)
ranking immediately after the best in quality, achievement, or status; next to the highest standard.
next in rank after the best

Examples

Even though she wore her second-best dress, she still looked elegant.
This team is currently the second-best in the league after their recent victories.
He offered me his second-best solution when the first one didn’t work.

Synset Examples

his second-best bed

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: best - of the highest quality or standing
  • Degree Lower: third-best - ranking immediately after the second-best

2. ADV

/ ˌsɛkənd ˈbɛst / (AmE), / ˌsɛkənd ˈbɛst / (BrE)
in the position of being second most successful or less favorable than the first; as the next best.
in second place; next best

Examples

Despite his efforts, he came off second-best in the competition.
No one likes feeling like the second-best choice.
They fought hard but finished second-best to their rivals.

Synset Examples

he came off second-best

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: best - in first place; the most excellent or desirable
  • Degree Lower: worst - in last place; the least good

Synset POS: r
