hemal

1. ADJ

/ ˈhiːməl / (AmE), / ˈhiːməl / (BrE)
relating to the blood vessels or blood.
relating to blood or blood vessels
medical

Examples

The hemal system in vertebrates is responsible for circulating blood throughout the body.
During surgery, special care is taken to avoid damaging major hemal structures.
Researchers studied the hemal arches of fish to understand the evolution of vertebrate anatomy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hematologic - relating to the blood or blood-forming organs

Etymology

From Greek 'haima' (blood).
