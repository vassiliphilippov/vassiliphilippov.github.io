entertaining

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛn.tɚˈteɪ.nɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɛn.təˈteɪ.nɪŋ / (BrE)
Interesting and enjoyable; providing amusement or pleasure.
amusing; enjoyable

Examples

The book was surprisingly entertaining despite its serious subject matter.
She finds cooking shows particularly entertaining on a rainy afternoon.
The comedian’s stories are always highly entertaining for the audience.

Synset Examples

an entertaining puppet show
films should be entertaining

Related Terms

  • Antonym: boring - not interesting; causing boredom
  • Synonym: amusing - causing laughter or enjoyment

Etymology

From entertain + -ing, from Old French entretenir (hold together, support) and Latin tenere (to hold).
