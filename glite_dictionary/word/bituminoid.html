bituminoid

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈtuːməˌnɔɪd / (AmE), / bɪˈtjuːmɪˌnɔɪd / (BrE)
resembling or having the properties of bitumen
like or resembling bitumen

Examples

The pavement was coated with a bituminoid material for added durability.
Certain rocks show bituminoid qualities due to their hydrocarbon content.
The laboratory analyzed the bituminoid residue found at the construction site.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bituminous - containing or resembling bitumen

Etymology

From 'bitumen' (a viscous mixture of hydrocarbons) and the suffix '-oid' meaning 'like' or 'resembling'.
