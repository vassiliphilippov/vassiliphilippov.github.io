Esthonian

1. NOUN

/ ɛsˈθoʊniən / (AmE), / ɛsˈθəʊniən / (BrE)
the official language of Estonia, belonging to the Baltic-Finnic branch of the Uralic language family
the official language of Estonia
archaic

Examples

My grandfather learned to speak Esthonian when he moved to Tallinn.
Few people outside of Estonia are fluent in Esthonian.
He appreciates literature written in Esthonian for its unique linguistic features.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Estonian - the official language of Estonia

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'Estonian,' from the name Estonia + -ian.
