mapmaking

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæpˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmæpˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or skill of creating maps or charts
the creation of maps

Examples

Advances in technology have revolutionized mapmaking over the past few decades.
She developed an early interest in geography and mapmaking.
The workshop will introduce children to the basics of mapmaking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cartography - the science or practice of drawing maps
