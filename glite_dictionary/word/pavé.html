pavé

1. NOUN

/ pɑːˈveɪ / (AmE), / paˈveɪ / (BrE)
A setting in jewelry where small precious stones are so closely set together that almost no metal is visible.
jewelry setting of closely set stones

Examples

The necklace featured a stunning pavé of diamonds that sparkled in the light.
Her engagement ring was designed with a pavé setting for maximum brilliance.
The jeweler recommended a pavé to enhance the beauty of the central gemstone.

Etymology

From French 'pavé', meaning 'paved', referring to how the stones resemble paving stones.
