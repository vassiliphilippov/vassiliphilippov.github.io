peoples

1. NOUN

/ ˈpipəlz / (AmE), / ˈpiːplz / (BrE)
the members of different nations, communities, or ethnic groups considered as distinct groups
distinct groups of human beings

Examples

The UN strives to protect the rights of indigenous peoples worldwide.
Different peoples have unique traditions and histories that enrich global culture.
The festival celebrated the music and dance of various peoples from around the world.

Synset Examples

the indigenous peoples of Australia

Related Terms

  • Synonym: people - human beings in general or members of a community

Etymology

Plural of 'people,' from Old French 'pople,' from Latin 'populus' (people, nation).
