Yamaltu

1. NOUN

/ jɑːˈmɑl.tuː / (AmE), / jɑːˈmæl.tuː / (BrE)
A three-tone Chadic language spoken in eastern Nigeria.
Chadic language of eastern Nigeria

Examples

Linguists have documented the phonology of Yamaltu in several academic studies.
Children in some Nigerian communities grow up speaking Yamaltu as their first language.
Yamaltu is classified as a member of the Chadic branch of the Afroasiatic language family.
