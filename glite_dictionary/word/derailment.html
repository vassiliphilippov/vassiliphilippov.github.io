derailment

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈreɪlmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈreɪlmənt / (BrE)
an accident in which a train comes off its tracks
train coming off its tracks

Examples

The investigation into the derailment revealed that faulty tracks were to blame.
Emergency crews responded quickly after the derailment near the station.
News reports detailed the derailment and its impact on train schedules nationwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: train accident - an accident involving a train

Etymology

From 'derail' + '-ment'; 'derail' from French 'dérailler', meaning 'to go off the rails'.
