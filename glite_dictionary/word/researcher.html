researcher

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsɜrtʃər / (AmE), / rɪˈsɜːtʃə / (BrE)
a person, especially a scientist, who conducts systematic investigation or study in order to discover new information or reach new conclusions
person who does scientific research

Examples

The researcher presented her findings at the international science conference.
As a researcher, he spends countless hours in the laboratory each week.
A team of medical researchers is working on developing a new vaccine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scientist - a person who conducts scientific research

Etymology

From 'research' (to investigate), ultimately from Old French *recercher* (to seek out).
