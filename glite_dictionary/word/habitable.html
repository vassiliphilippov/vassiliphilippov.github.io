habitable

1. ADJ

/ ˈhæbətəbəl / (AmE), / ˈhæbɪtəb(ə)l / (BrE)
Suitable or good enough for people to live in.
fit to be lived in

Examples

After years of renovation, the old farmhouse is now habitable again.
Scientists are searching for habitable planets outside our solar system.
The landlord must ensure the apartment is habitable before new tenants move in.

Synset Examples

the habitable world

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inhabitable - able to be lived in
  • Antonym: uninhabitable - not suitable for living in

Etymology

From Latin habitabilis, from habitare (to inhabit).
