edified

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛdɪfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈɛdɪfaɪd / (BrE)
instructed and encouraged in moral, intellectual, or spiritual improvement
instructed for moral or intellectual improvement

Examples

After attending the seminar, she felt truly edified and inspired to make positive changes.
The students left the lecture edified by the professor's wisdom and passion.
Many found reading the philosopher's works to be profoundly edified experiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enlightened - having or showing a rational, modern, and well-informed outlook
  • Antonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness in general

Etymology

From Latin 'aedificare' (to build), via Old French 'edifier' (to build up, instruct morally).
