to revisit

1. VERB

/ riˈvɪzɪt / (AmE), / riˈvɪzɪt / (BrE)
to visit a place again, especially after some time has passed
visit a place again

Examples

After many years abroad, she decided to revisit her hometown.
We plan to revisit the museum during our trip next summer.
He loves to revisit old favorite restaurants whenever he is in the city.

Synset Examples

We revisited Rome after 25 years

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visit - to go to see a person or place

Etymology

From re- (again) + visit (from Latin visitare, to go to see).
