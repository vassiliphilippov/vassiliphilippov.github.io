firelight

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪrˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəlaɪt / (BrE)
the light produced by a fire, especially in a fireplace or open hearth
light from a fire

Examples

They sat together in the warm firelight, talking about old times.
The room was filled with the soft glow of firelight on a winter evening.
Shadows danced on the walls as the firelight flickered in the hearth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: candlelight - the light produced by a candle
  • Synonym: moonlight - the light of the moon
  • Synonym: sunlight - the light from the sun
