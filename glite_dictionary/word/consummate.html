consummate

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑnsəmət / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsjʊmət / (BrE)
showing a high degree of skill and flair; complete or perfect in every way
complete or perfect; highly skilled

Examples

She delivered a consummate performance that brought the audience to their feet.
He is a consummate professional, respected by colleagues throughout the industry.
The chef's consummate skills were evident in every dish served.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfect - having all the required or desirable elements; as good as it is possible to be
  • Antonym: imperfect - not complete or perfect; having flaws or deficiencies
