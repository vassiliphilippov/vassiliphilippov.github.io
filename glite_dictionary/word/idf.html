IDF

1. NOUN

/ ˌaɪ ˌdi ˈɛf / (AmE), / ˌaɪ ˌdiː ˈɛf / (BrE)
the armed forces of Israel, comprising its ground, air, and naval branches
armed forces of Israel

Examples

The IDF launched a series of operations in response to the escalating tensions.
Many citizens are required to serve in the IDF after finishing high school.
The IDF has played a significant role in the country's history.

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'Israel Defense Forces'.
