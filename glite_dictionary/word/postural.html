postural

1. ADJ

/ ˈpoʊs.tʃə.rəl / (AmE), / ˈpɒs.tʃər.əl / (BrE)
relating to or involving posture or the position in which someone holds their body
relating to posture or body position

Examples

The therapist recommended postural exercises to improve his back health.
Standing desks can help reduce postural problems caused by sitting all day.
Yoga classes often focus on postural alignment and balance.

Synset Examples

postural exercises

Related Terms

  • Synonym: posture - the position in which someone holds their body

Etymology

From 'posture' + suffix '-al', from Latin 'postura' (position, attitude).
