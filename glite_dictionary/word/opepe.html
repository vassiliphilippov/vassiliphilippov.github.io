opepe

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈpɛpi / (AmE), / əʊˈpɛpi / (BrE)
a large African forest tree (Nauclea diderrichii) yielding strong, hard yellow to golden brown timber, sometimes placed in the genus Sarcocephalus
African timber tree; wood used in construction

Examples

The durable wood of opepe is widely used for outdoor construction and decking.
Many boats in West Africa are built from the tough timber of opepe.
Demand for opepe has increased due to its resistance to fungi and insects.
