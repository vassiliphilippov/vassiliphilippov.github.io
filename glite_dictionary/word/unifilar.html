unifilar

1. ADJ

/ ˌjuːnəˈfaɪlər / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈfʌɪlə / (BrE)
having or using only one filament, especially in electrical or scientific contexts.
having only one filament

Examples

The unifilar wire was used in the experiment to measure electrical resistance accurately.
A unifilar coil can simplify circuit analysis in certain cases.
He chose a unifilar arrangement for the transformer design.

Etymology

From Latin 'uni-' (one) + 'filum' (thread, filament).
