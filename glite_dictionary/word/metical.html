metical

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈmɛtɪkəl / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Mozambique, equal to 100 centavos
Mozambican currency unit

Examples

The price of the goods is listed in metical rather than dollars.
She exchanged her dollars for Mozambican metical at the airport.
The restaurant charged 400 meticais for dinner.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'metical', itself from Arabic 'mithqāl' (unit of weight).
