furiousness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjʊriəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəriəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely angry, violent, or intense
state of being furious

Examples

The furiousness of the debate took everyone by surprise.
Her furiousness was evident as she slammed the door behind her.
The furiousness of the storm caused widespread destruction throughout the region.

Synset Examples

the storm's violence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fury - intense, violent anger
  • Synonym: anger - strong feeling of displeasure or hostility
  • Antonym: calmness - the state of being calm

Etymology

From 'furious' + '-ness', after Latin 'furia' (fury).
