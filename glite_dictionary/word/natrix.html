Natrix

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪ.trɪks / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.trɪks / (BrE)
A genus of non-venomous water snakes found in Eurasia and North Africa, commonly known as grass snakes or water snakes.
genus of water snakes

Examples

The Natrix species are often found near rivers and lakes across Europe.
Researchers studied the diet of Natrix to better understand their ecological impact.
A Natrix can be easily identified by the characteristic collar-like markings around its neck.

Etymology

From Latin *natrix* (water snake).
