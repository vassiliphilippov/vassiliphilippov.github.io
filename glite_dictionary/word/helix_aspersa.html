Helix aspersa

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiːlɪks æˈspɜrsə / (AmE), / ˈhiːlɪks æˈspɜːsə / (BrE)
a species of land snail commonly known as the garden snail, recognized by its brown shell with pale zigzag markings; often considered a garden pest and found in many regions worldwide
species of garden snail; garden pest

Examples

Helix aspersa is often blamed for damaging vegetable crops in home gardens.
In biology class, we studied the anatomy of Helix aspersa under a microscope.
Many gardeners use special barriers to deter Helix aspersa from eating their plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garden snail - common name for Helix aspersa, a widespread land snail species

Etymology

From Latin: 'helix' meaning spiral or snail and 'aspersa' meaning sprinkled.
