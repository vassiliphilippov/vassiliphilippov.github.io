hardbacked

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑrdˌbækt / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌbækt / (BrE)
(of a book) having a hard cover.
having a hard cover (book)

Examples

He prefers to buy hardbacked novels because they last longer on his shelf.
The hardbacked edition of the book was released before the paperback version.
Libraries usually invest in hardbacked textbooks for durability.

Synset Examples

hardback books

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hardback - a book bound with a rigid protective cover
  • Antonym: paperback - a book bound with a flexible, paper cover
