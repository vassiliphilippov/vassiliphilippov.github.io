Boise

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɔɪzi / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪzi / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of the US state of Idaho, located in the southwestern part of the state
capital city of Idaho, USA
american

Examples

I visited Boise last summer and enjoyed the lively downtown scene.
The state capitol building in Boise is a popular tourist attraction.
Many people move to Boise for its outdoor recreational opportunities.

Etymology

From French boisé (wooded), referring to the trees along the Boise River.
