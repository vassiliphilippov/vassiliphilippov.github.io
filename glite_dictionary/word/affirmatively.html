affirmatively

1. ADV

/ əˈfɜrmətɪvli / (AmE), / əˈfɜːmətɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that expresses agreement or approval; in an affirmative manner.
in an affirmative manner

Examples

She nodded affirmatively when asked if she had finished her work.
The witness answered affirmatively to all of the lawyer’s questions.
He responded affirmatively, showing his support for the proposal.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: negatively - in a negative manner

Etymology

Formed from 'affirmative' + '-ly'. 'Affirmative' from Latin 'affirmativus'.
