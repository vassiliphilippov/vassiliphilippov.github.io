joss

1. NOUN

/ dʒɑs / (AmE), / dʒɒs / (BrE)
(in Chinese or East Asian contexts) a god, idol, or religious image, particularly one worshipped in Chinese folk religion.
a Chinese god or idol

Examples

The temple was filled with smoke from incense burning before the gilded joss.
Many families place offerings in front of a household joss to seek protection.
A brightly painted joss stood in the corner of the shop, surrounded by flowers and candles.

Etymology

From Portuguese *deus* (god), via Chinese Pidgin English.
