Rhode Islander

1. NOUN

/ ˌroʊd aɪˈlændər / (AmE), / ˌrəʊd aɪˈlændə / (BrE)
a person who was born in or is a resident of Rhode Island, USA
native or resident of Rhode Island

Examples

Her grandfather was a proud Rhode Islander who never wanted to move away from Providence.
Many Rhode Islanders enjoy spending their summers on the coast.
As a Rhode Islander, she is used to the state's distinctive local cuisine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: New Yorker - a native or resident of New York
