blackish-grey

1. ADJ

/ ˈblækɪʃ ˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈblakɪʃ ˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
of a dark grey color that is tinged with black; intermediate between black and grey
dark grey with a hint of black

Examples

The storm clouds turned a blackish-grey as the sun disappeared behind them.
He wore a blackish-grey suit to the formal event, blending classic and modern styles.
The old photograph had faded to a blackish-grey, losing much of its original color.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: grey - a color between black and white; neutral shade
  • Degree Higher: black - the very darkest color, the absence of light
