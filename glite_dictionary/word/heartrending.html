heartrending

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑrtˌrɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtˌrɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
causing intense sorrow or distress; deeply moving or distressing
causing deep sorrow or distress

Examples

The news of the disaster was truly heartrending for everyone in the village.
She gave a heartrending account of her childhood experiences during the war.
There was a heartrending scene at the funeral, as friends and family said goodbye.

Synset Examples

a grievous loss
a grievous cry
her sigh was heartbreaking
the heartrending words of Rabin's granddaughter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartbreaking - causing overwhelming distress or sorrow
  • Antonym: uplifting - making someone feel happier or more hopeful

Etymology

From heart + rending, meaning 'tearing the heart'.
