Kenya

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɛniə / (AmE), / ˈkiːniə / (BrE)
a country in eastern Africa, known for its diverse landscapes, wildlife, and archaeological sites; achieved independence from the United Kingdom in 1963.
country in eastern Africa

Examples

She traveled to Kenya to go on a safari and see the unique wildlife.
Important fossils have been discovered in the Great Rift Valley of Kenya.
After gaining independence in 1963, Kenya became known for its vibrant culture and natural beauty.
