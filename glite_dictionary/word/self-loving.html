self-loving

1. ADJ

/ sɛlfˈlʌvɪŋ / (AmE), / sɛlfˈlʌvɪŋ / (BrE)
Having an excessively high opinion of oneself; tending to show great self-admiration or vanity.
excessively admiring oneself

Examples

The self-loving celebrity constantly posted photos of herself on social media.
His self-loving attitude alienated many of his colleagues at the office.
She gave a self-loving speech, praising her own achievements above all others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: egotistical - excessively self-centered or conceited
  • Antonym: modest - having a humble opinion of oneself
  • Antonym: selfless - concerned more with the needs of others than with oneself

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + present participle of 'love'.
