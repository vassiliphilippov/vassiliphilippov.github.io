Kiev

1. PROPN

/ ˈkiɛv, ˈkiːɛv / (AmE), / ˈkiːɛf, ˈkiɛv / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Ukraine, known as a major manufacturing and transportation center
capital and largest city of Ukraine

Examples

Many tourists visit Kiev to admire its stunning Orthodox churches and historical sites.
The train from Lviv to Kiev takes approximately six hours.
Following independence, Kiev experienced rapid economic growth and modernization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kyiv - official romanization and preferred form for the capital of Ukraine

Etymology

From Russian Киев (Kiyev) and Ukrainian Київ (Kyiv).
