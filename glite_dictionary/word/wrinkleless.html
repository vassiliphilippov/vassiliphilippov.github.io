wrinkleless

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪŋkəl.ləs / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋkəl.ləs / (BrE)
(of a surface, fabric, or skin) having no wrinkles or creases; perfectly smooth.
without wrinkles or creases

Examples

After ironing, her shirt looked completely wrinkleless and ready for the meeting.
He admired the wrinkleless pages of the new book.
The hotel's sheets were always crisp and wrinkleless when she arrived.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wrinkled - having wrinkles or creases
  • Synonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; free from projections or roughness
