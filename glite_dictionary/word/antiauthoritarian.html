antiauthoritarian

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.ti.əˌθɔː.ɹɪˈtɛɹ.i.ən / (AmE), / ˌæn.ti.ɔːˌθɒ.ɹɪˈteə.ri.ən / (BrE)
opposed to or actively resisting authoritarian principles, control, or figures of authority.
opposed to authoritarianism or authority

Examples

The protesters adopted an antiauthoritarian stance, rejecting all forms of centralized control.
Her antiauthoritarian views often put her at odds with managerial staff.
Many punk bands have an antiauthoritarian message in their lyrics.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: authoritarian - favoring or enforcing strict obedience to authority

Etymology

anti- (against) + authoritarian (favoring strict obedience to authority).
