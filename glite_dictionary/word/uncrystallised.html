uncrystallised

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈkrɪstəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkrɪstəlaɪzd / (BrE)
not fully formed or developed; not yet made definite or clear
not fully formed or definite

Examples

Her uncrystallised plans made it difficult to proceed with the project.
The concept remained uncrystallised until further discussion clarified the details.
He struggled to articulate his uncrystallised thoughts during the meeting.

Synset Examples

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈkrɪstəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkrɪstəlaɪzd / (BrE)
(of a substance) not having formed into crystals or lacking a definite crystalline structure.
not having a crystalline structure

Examples

The mineral sample remained uncrystallised even after prolonged cooling.
Some sugars are deliberately left uncrystallised in syrups to improve texture.
The glass’s uncrystallised nature makes it valuable for optical applications.

Synset Examples

an amorphous mineral
amorphous structure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amorphous - lacking a clearly defined shape or crystalline structure
