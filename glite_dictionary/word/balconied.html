balconied

1. ADJ

/ ˈbæl.kə.nid / (AmE), / ˈbæl.kən.id / (BrE)
having one or more balconies; provided with a balcony or balconies.
having balconies

Examples

They stayed in a beautiful, balconied hotel overlooking the sea.
The balconied apartments provided residents with spectacular views of the city.
Their balconied home became the envy of the entire neighborhood.

Synset Examples

the balconied houses of New Orleans

Related Terms

  • Synonym: balcony - a platform projecting from the wall of a building, typically with a railing

Etymology

Formed from "balcony" + "-ed" (adjectival suffix).
