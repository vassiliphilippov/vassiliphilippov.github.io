Austria

1. PROPN

/ ˈɔstriə / (AmE), / ˈɒstriə / (BrE)
A country in central Europe, known for its mountainous terrain and long history as a political and cultural center, especially during the Habsburg rule.
country in central Europe

Examples

Austria is famous for its beautiful Alpine scenery and winter sports resorts.
Mozart was born in Salzburg, a city in Austria.
After World War I, Austria became a republic, ending centuries of Habsburg rule.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Vienna - the capital city of Austria
  • Synonym: Germany - neighboring country to the northwest of Austria
