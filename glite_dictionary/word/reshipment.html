reshipment

1. NOUN

/ riˈʃɪpmənt / (AmE), / riˈʃɪpmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of shipping goods again, especially by transferring them to another ship or carrier
the act of shipping again

Examples

The goods underwent reshipment at the port before reaching their final destination.
Due to a change in schedule, reshipment was necessary to meet the delivery deadline.
The company specializes in the reshipment of cargo between international locations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shipment - the act of shipping goods

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 're-' (again) and 'shipment' (the act of shipping).
