siliceous

1. ADJ

/ sɪˈlɪʃəs / (AmE), / sɪˈlɪsɪəs / (BrE)
containing, resembling, or relating to silica.
containing or relating to silica

Examples

The volcanic ash formed a siliceous layer across the landscape.
This region is known for its siliceous rocks and rich mineral deposits.
Microscopic analysis revealed siliceous particles embedded in the sample.

Synset Examples

gritrock is siliceous sandstone

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silica - hard, unreactive, colorless compound (SiO2) found in quartz, sand, etc.

Etymology

From Latin 'siliceus' (of flint), from 'silex, silic-' (flint).
