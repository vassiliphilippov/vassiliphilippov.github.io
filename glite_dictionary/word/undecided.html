undecided

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɪˈsaɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈsaɪdɪd / (BrE)
not having made a decision or choice yet; characterized by indecision.
not yet decided; uncertain

Examples

After hearing both arguments, she remained undecided about which side to support.
Many voters are still undecided just days before the election.
The match was so close that the result was undecided until the final moments.

Synset Examples

some who had been on the fence came out in favor of the plan
too many voters still declare they are undecided

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indecisive - unable to make decisions easily
  • Antonym: determined - having made a firm decision
  • Antonym: decided - having been resolved or determined

Etymology

Prefix 'un-' (not) + 'decided' (settled, resolved).

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɪˈsaɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈsaɪdɪd / (BrE)
not having made a decision or commitment yet; not having chosen or settled on a course of action
not yet decided or committed

Examples

Many undecided voters waited until the last day to choose a candidate.
She remains undecided about accepting the job offer.
The committee is still undecided on the location for the next meeting.

Synset Examples

undecided voters

Related Terms

  • Antonym: decided - having made a decision; resolved or certain
  • Synonym: hesitant - unsure or slow in acting or speaking
  • Synonym: uncertain - not sure; having doubts

Etymology

from un- (not) + decided (past participle of decide, from Latin decidere)

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00521028-s
Synset POS: s

3. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.dɪˈsaɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈsaɪdɪd / (BrE)
not yet having reached a conclusion or decision; open to further consideration or determination
not yet resolved or decided

Examples

The election results remain undecided until all the votes are counted.
After hours of discussion, the committee was still undecided on the proposal.
Her plans for the summer are undecided as she weighs different options.

Synset Examples

an open question
our position on this bill is still undecided
our lawsuit is still undetermined

Related Terms

  • Antonym: decided - having a clear or firm decision or conclusion
  • Synonym: undetermined - not yet settled or decided; unresolved

Etymology

From un- (not) + decided (past participle of decide).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02138201-s
Synset POS: s
