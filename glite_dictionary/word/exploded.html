exploded

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈsploʊdɪd / (AmE), / ɪkˈspləʊdɪd / (BrE)
(of a diagram or illustration) showing the parts of something separated but arranged to display their correct relationship to each other.
parts shown separated in correct relation (as in diagrams)

Examples

The instruction manual included an exploded diagram to help with assembly.
An exploded view of the gearbox made it easier to understand its structure.
The technical drawing showed the engine's components in an exploded format.

Synset Examples

the manufacturer provided an exploded view of the apparatus

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cutaway - a drawing or model that shows the inside of something by cutting away part of its exterior
