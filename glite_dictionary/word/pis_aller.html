pis aller

1. NOUN

/ pi aˈleɪ / (AmE), / pi aˈlɛr / (BrE)
a course of action or solution adopted only when no better alternative is available; a last resort
last resort; desperate expedient

Examples

She considered borrowing money only as a pis aller when all other options were exhausted.
Using the old typewriter was a pis aller after her computer broke down.
The compromise was accepted merely as a pis aller by both parties.

Synset Examples

‘pis aller’ is French for ‘worst going’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: last resort - the final option available when all else fails
  • Synonym: expedient - a means to an end, especially one that is convenient but not necessarily right

Etymology

From French, literally ‘worse to go’ or ‘worst going’.
