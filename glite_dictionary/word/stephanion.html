stephanion

1. NOUN

/ stəˈfæniˌɑn / (AmE), / stɛˈfæniˌɒn / (BrE)
the craniometric point located on the coronal suture above the acoustic meatus of the skull
craniometric point on skull
medical

Examples

The surgeon identified the stephanion before beginning the cranial procedure.
Precise measurements from the stephanion are crucial in anthropological studies of the skull.
The location of the stephanion is important for certain neurological assessments.

Etymology

From Greek 'stephanē' (crown) + -ion (diminutive suffix).
