Castries

1. PROPN

/ kæsˈtriːz / (AmE), / kæsˈtriːz / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Saint Lucia, located on the northwest coast of the island and serving as its main port
capital city of Saint Lucia

Examples

Many cruise ships dock at Castries each year, bringing tourists to Saint Lucia.
The government offices of Saint Lucia are located in Castries.
We explored the vibrant markets of Castries during our Caribbean vacation.

Etymology

Named after Charles Eugène Gabriel de La Croix, Marquis de Castries, a French marshal.
