fondling

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑn.dlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɒn.dlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of caressing or touching affectionately or intimately, often as a form of sexual foreplay without direct contact with genital organs
affectionate or intimate touching

Examples

The couple enjoyed gentle fondling as they watched the sunset together.
Public fondling is generally considered inappropriate in most social settings.
He initiated some playful fondling before their conversation turned more intimate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caressing - the act of touching or stroking gently in an affectionate manner
  • Synonym: petting - physical affection or foreplay short of intercourse, especially touching and stroking

Etymology

From 'fondle' (to caress) + -ing, formed in the late 18th century.
