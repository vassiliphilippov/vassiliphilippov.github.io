fruity

1. ADJ

/ ˈfruːti / (AmE), / ˈfruːti / (BrE)
having a taste or smell that is rich and reminiscent of fruit
having a fruit-like taste or smell

Examples

The wine has a fruity aroma with hints of cherry and plum.
I love this tea’s fruity flavor, especially in the summertime.
The chef prepared a fruity dessert that was both refreshing and sweet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: citrusy - having a taste or smell reminiscent of citrus fruit

Etymology

From fruit +‎ -y, meaning 'like or pertaining to fruit'.

2. ADJ

/ ˈfruːti / (AmE), / ˈfruːti / (BrE)
(informal, slang) Eccentric, strange, or acting in a mentally irregular manner; crazy or odd.
eccentric or mentally irregular (slang)
informal slang

Examples

Some people thought his new theories were a bit fruity and hard to believe.
After being alone for so long in the cabin, he started acting a little fruity.
The movie's main character is charming but undeniably fruity in his ideas.

Synset Examples

it used to drive my husband balmy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged or extremely eccentric
  • Antonym: sane - of sound mind; not mentally ill or eccentric
