MRD

1. NOUN

/ ɛm ɑr di / (AmE), / ɛm ɑː diː / (BrE)
a machine-readable dictionary; a standard dictionary presented in a format suitable for computer processing, typically organized alphabetically.
machine-readable dictionary
informal

Examples

The linguist downloaded an MRD to analyze word frequencies across multiple languages.
Developers often rely on an MRD when creating language processing software.
The project involved converting a printed dictionary into an MRD format.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: machine-readable - able to be processed by a computer
  • Synonym: dictionary - a reference book containing words listed alphabetically with information about their meanings and uses

Etymology

Acronym of 'machine-readable dictionary'.
