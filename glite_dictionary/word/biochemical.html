biochemical

1. ADJ

/ ˌbaɪ.oʊˈkɛm.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊˈkɛm.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to the chemical processes and substances that occur within living organisms
pertaining to chemical processes in living things

Examples

The doctor studied the biochemical changes that occur during stress.
Many diseases are caused by biochemical imbalances in the brain.
Researchers are developing new drugs to target specific biochemical pathways.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biochemistry - the branch of science concerned with the chemical processes within living organisms

Etymology

From 'biochemistry' + '-al'. 'Biochemistry' is from Greek 'bios' (life) + 'chemistry'.
