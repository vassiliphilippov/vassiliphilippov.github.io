greyish

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪɪʃ / (BrE)
having a color that is somewhat like grey; tinged with grey.
somewhat grey in color

Examples

The sky was greyish before the storm began.
He wore a greyish sweater that matched his eyes.
The kitten's fur was a soft, greyish color.

Synset Examples

the little grey cells
gray flannel suit
a man with greyish hair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grey - of a color between black and white
  • Synonym: grayish - alternative US spelling; somewhat gray in color

Etymology

From 'grey' + suffix '-ish', meaning 'somewhat' or 'like'.
