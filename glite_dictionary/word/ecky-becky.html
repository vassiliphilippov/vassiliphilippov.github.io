ecky-becky

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛki ˈbɛki / (AmE), / ˈɛki ˈbɛki / (BrE)
(slang) Describing behavior that is petty, annoying, or characterized by quirky humor.
petty or quirky behavior (slang)
slang

Her ecky-becky jokes always make the whole class laugh.
I wish he would stop acting so ecky-becky about every little thing.
Their ecky-becky behavior made the meeting drag on longer than necessary.
