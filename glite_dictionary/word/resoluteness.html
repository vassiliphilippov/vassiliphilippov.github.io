resoluteness

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛzəluˌtnəs / (AmE), / ˈrɛzəluːtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being determined or unwavering in purpose or action
firm determination; unwavering quality

Examples

Her resoluteness inspired those around her to keep striving for their goals.
Despite the obstacles, his resoluteness never wavered throughout the journey.
They admired the resoluteness with which she stood by her principles.

Synset Examples

his resoluteness carried him through the battle
it was his unshakeable resolution to finish the work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: determination - the quality of being firm in one's purpose
  • Antonym: indecision - lack of decision or firmness

Etymology

From 'resolute' + '-ness', with 'resolute' from Latin 'resolutus' (loosened, set free).
