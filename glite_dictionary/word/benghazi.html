Benghazi

1. PROPN

/ bɛnˈɡɑzi / (AmE), / bɛnˈɡɑːzi / (BrE)
A port city in northern Libya, located on the Gulf of Sidra, formerly a joint capital of Libya with Tripoli.
city in northern Libya

Examples

During the revolution, Benghazi became a focal point for opposition forces.
Many international organizations have set up operations in Benghazi to address humanitarian needs.
The port of Benghazi is vital for trade and commerce in eastern Libya.
