veinlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈveɪnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈveɪnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having markings or features that resemble veins, especially in appearance or pattern
resembling veins in appearance

Examples

The marble countertop had a veinlike pattern running through it.
She admired the veinlike lines on the delicate leaf.
The wings of the dragonfly were transparent with intricate veinlike structures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: veined - marked with or having veins
