fringe

1. NOUN

/ frɪndʒ / (AmE), / frɪndʒ / (BrE)
a border of hair that is cut short and hangs across the forehead, especially as a hairstyle
hair cut short across the forehead
british

Examples

She always wears her fringe swept to one side for a softer look.
After her haircut, her new fringe gave her a fresh appearance.
Many celebrities have opted for a blunt fringe this season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bangs - hair cut straight across the forehead (chiefly US)

3. NOUN

/ frɪndʒ / (AmE), / frɪndʒ / (BrE)
one of the light or dark bands produced by the interference or diffraction of light, as seen in physics experiments involving wave phenomena
light or dark band from interference/diffraction

Examples

The scientist measured the distance between each fringe produced by the double-slit experiment.
When monochromatic light passes through a thin film, a pattern of concentric fringes appears.
A shift in the position of a fringe can indicate a change in wavelength or medium.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: band - a strip or elongated area with a different appearance
