tubed

1. ADJ

/ tuːbd / (AmE), / tjuːbd / (BrE)
(of a tire) having an inner tube, as opposed to being tubeless.
having an inner tube (of tire)

Examples

The mechanic recommended replacing the old tubed tires with modern tubeless ones.
Vintage bicycles often come with tubed tires, which require more maintenance.
Make sure you purchase the right size of tubed tire for your classic car.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tubeless - (of a tire) not having an inner tube
