calligraphic

1. ADJ

/ kəˌlɪɡrəˈfɪk / (AmE), / kəˌlɪɡrəˈfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of calligraphy; written or executed in a decorative or elegant script
relating to calligraphy or elegant script

Examples

The artist used calligraphic strokes to create an elegant invitation.
Her handwriting had a calligraphic quality that caught everyone's eye.
The certificate was decorated with calligraphic flourishes around the border.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calligraphy - the art of beautiful handwriting

Etymology

From Greek kalligraphia (beautiful writing), via French calligraphique.
