agriculturalist

1. NOUN

/ əˌɡrɪˈkʌl.tʃər.ə.lɪst / (AmE), / əˌɡrɪˈkʌl.tʃər.ɪ.lɪst / (BrE)
a person who is concerned with the science, art, or business of cultivating the soil; a specialist in agriculture
person specializing in agriculture

Examples

The agriculturalist advised the community on sustainable farming practices.
As an experienced agriculturalist, she developed new methods for improving crop yields.
Modern agriculturalists often utilize advanced technology to monitor soil conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agronomist - an expert in the science of soil management and crop production
  • Synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm

Etymology

From agriculture + -ist, indicating a practitioner or specialist.
