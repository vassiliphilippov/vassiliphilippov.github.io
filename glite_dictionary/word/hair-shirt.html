hair-shirt

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛr ˌʃɝt / (AmE), / ˈheə ˌʃɜːt / (BrE)
(of a person, attitude, or manner) self-sacrificing or displaying unnecessary self-denial and austerity.
self-sacrificing or austere (adjective)

Examples

She adopted a hair-shirt approach to her finances, refusing to spend money on any luxuries.
His hair-shirt morality made life difficult for those around him.
The novel is critical of the hair-shirt tendency to reject pleasure as a virtue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: austere - severe or strict in manner, attitude, or appearance
  • Antonym: self-indulgent - characterized by doing or tending to do exactly what one wants

Etymology

From the historical practice of wearing a shirt made of coarse animal hair as self-punishment, symbolizing penance and austerity.
