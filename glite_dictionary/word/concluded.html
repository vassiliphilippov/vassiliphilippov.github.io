concluded

1. ADJ

/ kənˈkluːdɪd / (AmE), / kənˈkluːdɪd / (BrE)
Having come or been brought to an end; finished.
ended; finished

Examples

The debate finally concluded after several hours of discussion.
Once the project is concluded, we will review the results as a team.
The investigation was concluded with a written summary of findings.

Synset Examples

the harvesting was complete
the affair is over, ended, finished
the abruptly terminated interview

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finished - having been completed or ended
  • Antonym: unfinished - not finished; not yet complete
