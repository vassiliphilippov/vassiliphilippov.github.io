Nanomia

1. NOUN

/ nəˈnoʊ.mi.ə / (AmE), / nəˈnəʊ.mi.ə / (BrE)
A genus of marine siphonophores belonging to the family Agalmatidae, consisting of colonial, pelagic organisms found in the ocean.
genus of marine siphonophores

Examples

Researchers discovered a new species within the Nanomia genus during their ocean expedition.
The Nanomia are noted for their complex colonial structure and bioluminescence.
Marine biologists study Nanomia to better understand siphonophore diversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: siphonophore - a marine invertebrate animal of the order Siphonophora
