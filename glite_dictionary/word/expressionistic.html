expressionistic

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˌsprɛʃəˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / ɪkˌsprɛʃəˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of expressionism, especially in art, literature, or music, where subjective emotions and responses are emphasized.
relating to expressionism in art or literature

Examples

The director used an expressionistic style to convey the protagonist's inner turmoil.
Many critics praised the expressionistic elements of her latest novel.
The film's lighting and set design reflected an expressionistic influence.

Synset Examples

expressionist art

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expressionism - an art movement emphasizing expression of emotions

Etymology

From 'expressionism' + '-istic' (adjectival suffix).
