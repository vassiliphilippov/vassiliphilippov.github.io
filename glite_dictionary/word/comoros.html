Comoros

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɑːməˌroʊz / (AmE), / ˈkɒmərəʊz / (BrE)
an island country in the Indian Ocean, located at the northern end of the Mozambique Channel between northern Madagascar and the African mainland
island country in the Indian Ocean

Examples

The Comoros is known for its beautiful beaches and unique wildlife.
Many people in the Comoros speak Comorian, French, and Arabic.
She traveled to the Comoros to study marine biodiversity.

Etymology

From French 'Comores', itself from Arabic 'Juzur al-Qamar' (Islands of the Moon).
