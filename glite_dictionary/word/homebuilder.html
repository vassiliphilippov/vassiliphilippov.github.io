homebuilder

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊmˌbɪldər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌbɪldə / (BrE)
a person or company that builds houses as a profession or business
person or company that builds houses

Examples

The homebuilder completed twenty new houses in the suburb last year.
As a successful homebuilder, she is known for using sustainable materials in her projects.
Many people relied on the homebuilder to turn their dream homes into reality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: builder - a person who constructs something by putting parts or material together

Etymology

From 'home' + 'builder', meaning one who constructs homes.
