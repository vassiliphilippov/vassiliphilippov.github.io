enzymatic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛnˌzaɪˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛnˌzɪˈmatɪk / (BrE)
relating to or caused by an enzyme; involving the action of enzymes
of or produced by an enzyme
medical

Examples

Recent research focuses on the enzymatic breakdown of pollutants in wastewater treatment.
Many digestive processes depend on enzymatic activity in the stomach and intestines.
The scientists measured the enzymatic rates of reaction under different temperature conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enzymic - relating to or produced by an enzyme

Etymology

From enzyme + -atic, patterned after words like 'enzymic' and 'systematic'.
