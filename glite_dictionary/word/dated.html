dated

1. ADJ

/ ˈdeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtɪd / (BrE)
no longer modern; showing features typical of a past time and therefore old-fashioned or out of style.
old-fashioned; belonging to the past

Examples

Her outfit looked a bit dated compared to the latest fashion trends.
The film's special effects seem dated by today's standards.
Although the house is charming, some of its decor is rather dated.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times; up to date
  • Synonym: outdated - no longer useful or fashionable; obsolete
