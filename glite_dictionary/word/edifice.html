edifice

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛd.ə.fɪs / (AmE), / ˈɛd.ɪ.fɪs / (BrE)
A large, impressive building, especially one of imposing size or appearance.
large or imposing building

Examples

The new museum is an impressive edifice that dominates the city skyline.
The ancient edifice has been carefully preserved as a historical monument.
They marveled at the grand edifice that housed the royal court.

Synset Examples

there was a three-story building on the corner
it was an imposing edifice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: building - a structure with a roof and walls

Etymology

From Latin 'aedificium' (building).
