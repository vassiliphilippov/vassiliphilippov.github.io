cissy

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪsi / (AmE), / ˈsɪsi / (BrE)
(offensive) Having or showing qualities considered overly feminine or unmanly, especially in a man or boy.
having qualities viewed as overly feminine
offensive

Examples

Some classmates teased him for his cissy mannerisms.
He was criticized for wearing what some called a cissy outfit.
Using the word cissy to describe someone is considered offensive and outdated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: effeminate - having or showing qualities regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly
  • Antonym: manly - having qualities traditionally associated with men, such as strength or courage
