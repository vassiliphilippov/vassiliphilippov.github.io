smoulderingly

1. ADV

/ ˈsmoʊldərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsməʊldərɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows barely suppressed anger, passion, or intensity.
with barely suppressed anger or passion

Examples

She stared at him smoulderingly, her eyes full of silent fury.
The actor delivered his lines smoulderingly, capturing the character's pent-up emotions.
He replied smoulderingly, clearly struggling to contain his anger.

Synset Examples

‘I can't wait,’ she answered smolderingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angrily - in an angry manner

Etymology

From 'smoulder' (to burn slowly with smoke but no flame) + adverbial suffix '-ing' + '-ly'.
