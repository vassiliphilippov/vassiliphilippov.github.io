Hondo

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɑndoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhɒndəʊ / (BrE)
the former name for the largest of the four main islands of Japan, now commonly known as Honshu
former name for Japan's main island (Honshu)

Examples

During his travels, he spent a month on Hondo exploring ancient temples and bustling cities.
Many rivers flow from the mountains of Hondo to the Pacific Ocean.
The term Hondo was once widely used by Westerners to refer to Japan's main island.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Honshu - the largest island of Japan, the current official name

Etymology

From Japanese 本土 (Hondo), meaning 'mainland'.
