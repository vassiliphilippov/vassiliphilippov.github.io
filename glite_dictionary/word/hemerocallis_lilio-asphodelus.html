Hemerocallis lilio-asphodelus

1. NOUN

/ hɛm.ə.roʊˈkæl.ɪs ˌlɪl.i.oʊˈæs.fəˌdɛl.əs / (AmE), / hɛm.ə.rəʊˈkæl.ɪs ˌlɪl.i.əʊˈæs.fəˌdɛl.əs / (BrE)
A species of day lily with fragrant yellow flowers, commonly grown as an ornamental plant.
species of yellow-flowered day lily

Examples

The garden features several varieties of Hemerocallis lilio-asphodelus in bloom each spring.
Botanists have long admired the fragrance of Hemerocallis lilio-asphodelus's yellow flowers.
She planted Hemerocallis lilio-asphodelus along the path for a burst of summer color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: day lily - Common name for plants of the genus Hemerocallis

Etymology

From Greek *hēmera* (day) + *kallos* (beauty); species name relates to the asphodel lily.
