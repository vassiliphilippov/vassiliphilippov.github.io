nursed

1. ADJ

/ nɝst / (AmE), / nɜːst / (BrE)
(of an infant) having been breast-fed.
breast-fed (of an infant)

Examples

The doctor asked whether the baby was nursed or bottle-fed.
Many nursed infants are less likely to have certain allergies.
She felt proud to have successfully nursed her child for six months.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bottle-fed - (of an infant) fed with a bottle rather than breast-fed
