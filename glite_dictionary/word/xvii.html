XVII

1. NUM

/ ɛks ˌvi ː ˈaɪ / (AmE), / ɛks ˌviː ˈaɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for the number seventeen (17)
Roman numeral for seventeen

Examples

The monarch ascended the throne in the year XVII of her reign.
Chapters XVII and XVIII cover the final events of the war.
He carefully read article XVII of the treaty before signing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seventeen - the cardinal number 17
  • Degree Lower: XVI - Roman numeral for sixteen (16)
  • Degree Higher: XVIII - Roman numeral for eighteen (18)

Etymology

From Latin numerals: X (10) + V (5) + II (2), totaling 17.
