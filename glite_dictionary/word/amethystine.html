amethystine

1. ADJ

/ ˈæməˌθɪstiːn / (AmE), / ˌamɪˈθɪstiːn / (BrE)
Containing or resembling amethyst; having the color or qualities of amethyst, typically a purple or violet hue.
resembling or colored like amethyst

Examples

The sky took on an amethystine glow as the sun set over the mountains.
She wore an amethystine necklace that sparkled brilliantly in the light.
The artist used amethystine tones to convey a sense of mystery in the painting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purple - a color similar to that of amethyst
  • Synonym: violet - a bluish-purple color

Etymology

From Latin 'amethystinus', from Greek 'amethystos' (not intoxicated, amethyst).
