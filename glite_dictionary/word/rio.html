Rio

1. PROPN

/ ˈrioʊ / (AmE), / ˈriːəʊ / (BrE)
A popular shortened name for Rio de Janeiro, the former capital and second largest city of Brazil, known for its tourist attractions and major port.
shortened name for Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Examples

Many tourists flock to Rio for its famous Carnival celebrations every year.
She took a flight from London to Rio to enjoy the beaches and vibrant culture.
The 2016 Summer Olympics were held in Rio, bringing the city into the global spotlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Rio de Janeiro - the full name of the city in Brazil referred to as Rio
