roentgenographic

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɛntɡənəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌrɛntɡənəˈɡrafɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or produced by roentgenography (the process of obtaining images using X-rays).
relating to X-ray imaging
medical

Examples

The roentgenographic examination revealed a small fracture in the patient's wrist.
Accurate roentgenographic interpretation is essential for effective dental diagnosis and treatment planning.
Their research focused on the roentgenographic differences between benign and malignant bone tumors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiographic - relating to or produced by radiation imaging, especially X-rays

Etymology

Formed from 'roentgen' (after Wilhelm Röntgen, discoverer of X-rays) + '-graphic' (relating to writing or imaging).
