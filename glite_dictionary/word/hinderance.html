hinderance

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪndɚəns / (AmE), / ˈhɪnd(ə)rəns / (BrE)
the act of hindering, obstructing, or impeding; something that delays or prevents progress
the act of hindering or obstructing

Examples

Her lack of experience proved to be a significant hinderance to her progress.
The construction work was a major hinderance for drivers during rush hour.
Despite every hinderance, the team managed to finish the project on time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hindrance - the act of hindering or something that hinders
  • Synonym: obstacle - something that blocks progress
  • Antonym: help - the act of assisting or making easier

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'hindrance', from Middle English 'hinderaunce', from 'hinder' + '-ance'.
