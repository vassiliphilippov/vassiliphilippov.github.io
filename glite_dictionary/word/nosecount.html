nosecount

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊsˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈnəʊzˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
an act or process of counting the number of people in a particular group or area, often for statistical or census purposes
counting of people, census

Examples

The school conducted a nosecount to ensure all students were present after the fire drill.
During the annual nosecount, volunteers went door to door collecting headcounts for the municipality.
The government relies on the national nosecount for planning infrastructure and services.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: census - an official count or survey of a population

Etymology

From 'nose' (a person's nose, representing an individual) + 'count'.
