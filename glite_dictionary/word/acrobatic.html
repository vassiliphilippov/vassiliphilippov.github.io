acrobatic

1. ADJ

/ ˌækrəˈbætɪk / (AmE), / ˌækrəˈbætɪk / (BrE)
involving or displaying energetic and agile physical movements, like those performed by an acrobat
energetic and agile in movement

Examples

The children gave an acrobatic performance at the talent show, amazing everyone with their flips and jumps.
Her acrobatic style of dancing requires both strength and flexibility.
The gymnast impressed the judges with his acrobatic maneuvers on the rings.

Synset Examples

an acrobatic dance
an athletic child
athletic playing
gymnastic exercises

Related Terms

  • Synonym: athletic - physically strong, fit, or active
  • Synonym: agile - able to move quickly and easily

Etymology

From French acrobatique, from acrobate (acrobat), from Greek akrobatēs (walker on tiptoe).
