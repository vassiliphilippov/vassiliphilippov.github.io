Dame Joan Sutherland

1. PROPN

/ deɪm dʒoʊn ˈsʌðərlənd / (AmE), / deɪm dʒəʊn ˈsʌðələnd / (BrE)
(1926–2010) An Australian operatic soprano renowned for her powerful voice and performances in bel canto roles.
famous Australian operatic soprano

Examples

Many critics regard Dame Joan Sutherland as one of the greatest sopranos of the twentieth century.
The opera house paid tribute to Dame Joan Sutherland with a special concert.
Recordings featuring Dame Joan Sutherland continue to inspire new generations of opera singers.
