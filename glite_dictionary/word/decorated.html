decorated

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɛkəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɛkəreɪtɪd / (BrE)
adorned or embellished with decorations to make something more beautiful or distinctive.
adorned; embellished with decorations

Examples

The walls were decorated with paintings and murals.
She wore a beautifully decorated dress to the gala.
Every cookie was decorated with colorful icing and sprinkles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adorned - made more beautiful or attractive
  • Antonym: plain - not decorated or embellished

Etymology

Past participle of 'decorate', from Latin 'decorare' meaning 'to adorn'.
