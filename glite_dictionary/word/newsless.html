newsless

1. ADJ

/ ˈnuːz.ləs / (AmE), / ˈnjuːz.ləs / (BrE)
not having or receiving news or information.
having no news or information

Examples

The small, newsless village remained unaffected by events in the outside world.
During the blackout, we spent several newsless days wondering what was happening.
She grew anxious in her newsless isolation from her family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uninformed - not having information or knowledge about something
  • Antonym: aware - having knowledge or information

Etymology

Formed from 'news' + suffix '-less' (meaning 'without').

2. ADJ

/ ˈnuːz.ləs / (AmE), / ˈnjuːz.ləs / (BrE)
not providing news or information; lacking news coverage or updates
not providing news or information

Examples

The small town was so newsless that even local gossip faded quickly.
After a week without television or internet, I felt completely newsless.
It was a newsless day, with nothing significant reported anywhere in the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uninformative - not providing useful or interesting information
  • Antonym: newsworthy - worthy of being reported as news
