lettered

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛt̬ɚd / (AmE), / ˈlɛtəd / (BrE)
having a high level of education; well read or knowledgeable
highly educated or knowledgeable

Examples

She grew up in a lettered household where books were highly valued.
Only the most lettered scholars were invited to the symposium.
His reputation as a lettered critic attracted many young writers.

Synset Examples

knowing instructors
a knowledgeable critic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: learned - having much knowledge acquired by study
  • Antonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness

Etymology

From Middle English, from Latin 'litteratus' (learned, scholarly), from 'littera' (letter).
