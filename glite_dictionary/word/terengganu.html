Terengganu

1. PROPN

/ təˌrɛŋɡəˈnu / (AmE), / təˌrɛŋɡəˈnuː / (BrE)
A sultanate and one of the thirteen states of the Federation of Malaysia, located on the eastern coast of Peninsular Malaysia.
state of Malaysia on the eastern coast

Examples

Many tourists visit Terengganu for its beautiful beaches and islands.
The capital city of Terengganu is Kuala Terengganu.
Traditional crafts are an important part of Terengganu's cultural heritage.

2. PROPN

/ təˈrɛŋɡəˌnu / (AmE), / təˈrɛŋɡəˌnu / (BrE)
A state and former sultanate on the east coast of Peninsular Malaysia, known for its coastline, islands, and traditional Malay culture.
state in eastern Peninsular Malaysia

Examples

Tourists flock to Terengganu for its pristine beaches and the famous Redang Island.
The traditional crafts of Terengganu, such as batik and songket weaving, are well known across Malaysia.
Heavy monsoon rains often affect Terengganu during the end of the year.

Etymology

From Malay 'Terengganu', possibly derived from 'Terang Ganu' meaning 'bright rainbow' or 'bright land'.
