cadaveric

1. ADJ

/ kəˈdæv.ɚ.ɪk / (AmE), / kəˈdæv.ər.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to a cadaver or corpse; characteristic of a dead body.
relating to a cadaver or corpse

Examples

The hospital has strict protocols for handling cadaveric tissue donations.
He showed a cadaveric pallor after the long illness.
The researchers studied the effects of preservation on cadaveric organs.

Synset Examples

we had long anticipated his cadaverous end

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corpse-like - resembling or characteristic of a corpse
  • Antonym: living - being alive; the state opposite of dead or lifeless

Etymology

From Latin 'cadaver' (dead body) + '-ic' (forming adjectives).
