wood-fired

1. ADJ

/ wʊdˌfaɪɚd / (AmE), / wʊdˌfaɪəd / (BrE)
(of an oven, stove, etc.) heated or powered by burning wood as fuel.
powered by burning wood (e.g., an oven or stove)

Examples

The restaurant boasts a wood-fired pizza oven that gives their pizzas a unique flavor.
They installed a wood-fired hot tub on their deck for chilly evenings.
The cabin is heated by a wood-fired stove during the winter months.

Synset Examples

a wood-burning stove

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wood-burning - (of a stove, heater, etc.) using wood as fuel
