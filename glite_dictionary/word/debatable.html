debatable

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈbeɪtəbl̩ / (AmE), / dɪˈbeɪtəbl̩ / (BrE)
Open to question or dispute; able to be challenged, doubted, or disproved.
open to question or dispute

Examples

The claim that the earth is flat is highly debatable given current scientific evidence.
Whether the new policy will be effective remains debatable among experts.
Some historians consider the evidence for the event to be debatable at best.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: indisputable - impossible to question or doubt
  • Synonym: arguable - open to argument or debate

Etymology

From Old French 'debatre' (to contend) + -able.

/ dɪˈbeɪdəbl̩ / (AmE), / dɪˈbeɪtəb(ə)l / (BrE)
Open to discussion or argument; not certain or settled.
open to argument or question

Examples

It is debatable whether this painting is an original or a copy.
The benefits of the policy remain debatable among experts.

open to discussion or argument; not yet settled or decided
open to discussion or argument

Examples
