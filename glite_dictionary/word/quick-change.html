quick-change

1. ADJ

/ ˈkwɪkˌtʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈkwɪkˌtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
able to change quickly from one thing to another, especially in changing costumes or roles.
able to change rapidly from one thing to another

Examples

He is a quick-change artist who can switch roles in seconds.
The magician amazed the audience with her quick-change acts.
A quick-change performer must be both skilled and creative.

Synset Examples

a quick-change artist

Related Terms

  • Synonym: versatile - able to adapt or be adapted to many different functions or activities
  • Antonym: static - lacking movement or change
