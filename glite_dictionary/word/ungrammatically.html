ungrammatically

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnɡrəˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɡrəˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that does not follow the rules of grammar.
in an ungrammatical way

Examples

He often speaks ungrammatically when he is nervous.
The essay was written ungrammatically and was difficult to understand.
If you answer ungrammatically in the interview, it may leave a bad impression.

Synset Examples

this child speaks ungrammatically

Related Terms

  • Antonym: grammatically - in a way that follows grammatical rules

Etymology

From ungrammatical + -ly.
