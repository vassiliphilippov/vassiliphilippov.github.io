questionably

1. ADV

/ ˈkwɛs.tʃən.ə.bli / (AmE), / ˈkwɛs.tʃən.ə.bli / (BrE)
In a way that is doubtful, suspicious, or open to question.
in a doubtful or dubious way

Examples

The company was questionably involved in several suspicious transactions.
His credentials were questionably obtained, which raised concerns among the committee.
She questionably omitted important details from her report.

Synset Examples

these were estates his father questionably acquired

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dubiously - in a doubtful or questionable manner
  • Antonym: unquestionably - in a way that is not open to question; undoubtedly

Etymology

From the adjective 'questionable' plus the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
