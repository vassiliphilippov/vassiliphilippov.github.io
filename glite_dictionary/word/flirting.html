flirting

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɝː.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflɜː.tɪŋ / (BrE)
Behavior involving playful or romantic gestures intended to attract sexual interest.
playful romantic behavior

Examples

She was accused of flirting with her coworker at the office party.
His constant flirting made everyone laugh, but she started to take it seriously.
Some people enjoy flirting just for the fun and excitement it brings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: courtship - the process of pursuing a romantic relationship
  • Synonym: teasing - making fun or provoking in a playful way, sometimes linked to flirting

Etymology

From the verb 'flirt', origin uncertain, possibly imitative or from obsolete French 'fleureter' (to talk sweet nonsense).
