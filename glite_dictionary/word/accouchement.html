accouchement

1. NOUN

/ əˈkuːʃ.mɑːn / (AmE), / əˈkuːʃ.mɒ̃ / (BrE)
The act or process of giving birth to a child; childbirth.
the process of giving birth
medical

Examples

The doctor was present throughout the accouchement to ensure the safety of mother and child.
She wrote a detailed account of her accouchement in her diary.
Modern medical facilities have greatly improved the outcomes of accouchement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: childbirth - the process of giving birth to a baby

Etymology

From French 'accouchement', from 'accoucher' (to give birth).
