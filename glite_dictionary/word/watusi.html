Watusi

1. NOUN

/ wɑˈtusi / (AmE), / wɑːˈtuːsi / (BrE)
A member of a Bantu-speaking ethnic group living chiefly in Rwanda and Burundi, also known as Tutsi.
member of a central African ethnic group

Examples

Many historians have studied the traditions of the Watusi people.
The Watusi have played a significant role in the history of Rwanda and Burundi.
He visited a village where several Watusi families lived.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tutsi - A member of the same ethnic group, alternative name
