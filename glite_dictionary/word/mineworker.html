mineworker

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪnˌwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to work in a mine, especially extracting minerals, coal, or other materials from the ground
person who works in a mine

Examples

The mineworker carefully descended into the shaft to begin his shift.
A group of mineworkers protested for better safety conditions underground.
Becoming a mineworker requires physical strength and resilience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miner - person who works in a mine
