graveness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪv.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪv.nəs / (BrE)
A manner or quality of being serious and solemn.
serious and solemn manner

Examples

The graveness of his expression suggested the importance of the situation.
She spoke with graveness during the ceremony, giving her words great weight.
His graveness was apparent to everyone in the room as he delivered the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gravity - seriousness or solemnity of manner
  • Synonym: seriousness - the quality of being serious

Etymology

Formed from 'grave' (serious) + '-ness' (noun-forming suffix).
