cragswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkræɡzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈkræɡzwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who climbs steep or vertical rock faces; a female rock climber.
female rock climber

Examples

The experienced cragswoman navigated the sheer rock face with confidence and skill.
As a passionate cragswoman, she has climbed some of the toughest cliffs in Europe.
The documentary featured a renowned cragswoman conquering several challenging mountain routes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cragsman - a man who climbs steep rock faces
  • Synonym: climber - a person who climbs, especially mountains or cliffs

Etymology

From 'crag' (a steep or rugged cliff or rock face) + 'woman'.
