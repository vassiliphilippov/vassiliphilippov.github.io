excitableness

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈsaɪtəbəlnəs / (AmE), / ɪkˈsaɪtəbəlnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being easily excited or aroused
quality of being easily excited

Examples

Her excitableness made even mundane events seem thrilling.
The teacher admired the children's excitableness during the science project.
Because of his excitableness, he found it hard to remain calm in tense situations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excitability - quality of being easily excited

Etymology

From 'excitable' + '-ness', the suffix forming nouns of quality from adjectives.
