iliac

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪliˌæk / (AmE), / ˈɪlɪak / (BrE)
relating to the ilium, the broad, uppermost bone of the pelvis
relating to the ilium (pelvis bone)
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that the pain was caused by inflammation of the iliac artery.
A fracture in the iliac bone requires prompt medical attention.
She pointed to the area near her iliac crest when describing her discomfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ilium - the large, broad bone forming the upper part of each half of the pelvis

Etymology

From Latin *iliacus*, from *ilium* (flank, groin, or hip bone).
