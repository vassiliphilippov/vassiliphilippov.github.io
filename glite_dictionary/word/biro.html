Biro

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪɹoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbaɪərəʊ / (BrE)
A type of pen with a small metal ball at its point that rotates to transfer ink onto paper; a ballpoint pen.
type of ballpoint pen
british

Examples

Could you hand me that Biro so I can sign the form?
He always carries a blue Biro in his pocket for quick notes.
My essay was written entirely with a Biro.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ballpoint - a pen with a small rotating metal ball to dispense ink

Etymology

From the name of its inventor, László Bíró, a Hungarian journalist.
