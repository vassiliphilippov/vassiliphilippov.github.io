elaborately

1. ADV

/ ɪˈlæbərətli / (AmE), / ɪˈlæbərətli / (BrE)
In a detailed and complicated way; with much decoration, care, or complexity.
in a detailed, complicated way

Examples

The room was elaborately decorated with gold and crystal chandeliers.
She elaborately explained her plan, leaving no detail unaddressed.
The ceremony was elaborately organized, impressing all the guests.

Synset Examples

it was elaborately spelled out

Related Terms

  • Antonym: simply - in a plain or uncomplicated way

Etymology

From 'elaborate' + '-ly', with 'elaborate' from Latin 'elaboratus' (worked out).
