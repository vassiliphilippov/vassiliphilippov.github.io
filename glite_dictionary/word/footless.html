footless

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʊtləs / (AmE), / ˈfʊtləs / (BrE)
Having no feet or similar limb-like appendages.
having no feet or foot-like limbs

Examples

The footless creature slid silently across the damp jungle floor.
Some worms are footless, moving instead by contracting their bodies.
The mythological serpent was described as a footless monster in ancient texts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legless - having no legs; lacking legs or leg-like appendages
