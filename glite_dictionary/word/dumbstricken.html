dumbstricken

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʌmˌstrɪkən / (AmE), / ˈdʌmˌstrɪkən / (BrE)
So shocked or astonished as to be rendered temporarily speechless; overcome with surprise or amazement.
rendered speechless with astonishment

Examples

When he realized he had won the lottery, he stood dumbstricken in the middle of the street.
She was dumbstricken by the unexpected announcement of her promotion.
The audience sat dumbstricken after the magician’s astonishing final trick.

Synset Examples

a circle of policemen stood dumbfounded by her denial of having seen the accident
the flabbergasted aldermen were speechless
was thunderstruck by the news of his promotion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dumbfounded - so amazed or shocked as to be unable to speak
  • Synonym: thunderstruck - extremely surprised or astonished

Etymology

Formed from 'dumb', meaning speechless, and 'stricken', meaning affected suddenly and strongly.
