ianfu

1. NOUN

/ janˈfuː / (AmE), / janˈfuː / (BrE)
A woman forced into prostitution by the Japanese military during World War II; also known as a 'comfort woman'.
woman forced into prostitution by Japanese military in WWII

Examples

The testimonies of former ianfu have been crucial in raising awareness about wartime atrocities.
Historians continue to debate the extent of the Japanese military's involvement with the ianfu system.
A museum exhibit featured photos and personal belongings of women who were once ianfu.

Synset Examples

she wrote a book about her harsh experiences as a comfort woman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comfort woman - A woman forced into sexual slavery by the Japanese military during World War II

Etymology

From Japanese 慰安婦 (ianfu), meaning 'comfort woman'.
