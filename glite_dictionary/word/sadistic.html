sadistic

1. ADJ

/ səˈdɪstɪk / (AmE), / səˈdɪstɪk / (BrE)
Deriving pleasure, especially sexual pleasure, from inflicting pain, suffering, or humiliation on others.
taking pleasure in inflicting pain

Examples

The film's villain was portrayed as sadistic, tormenting his victims without remorse.
Some people find sadistic humor disturbing and offensive.
Her sadistic actions shocked even those who were used to rough competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cruel - willingly causing pain or suffering to others
  • Antonym: masochistic - deriving pleasure from one's own pain or humiliation

Etymology

From French sadisme, named after the Marquis de Sade (1740–1814), noted for writings describing such behaviors.
