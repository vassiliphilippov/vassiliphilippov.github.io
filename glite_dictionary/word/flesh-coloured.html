flesh-coloured

1. ADJ

/ flɛʃ ˈkʌlɚd / (AmE), / flɛʃ ˈkʌləd / (BrE)
having a pale pink or reddish color resembling the typical color of Caucasian human skin
pale pink or reddish color like skin

Examples

The artist chose a flesh-coloured paint for the portrait's background.
She wore a flesh-coloured dress that almost matched her skin tone.
The toy had a flesh-coloured face with painted eyes and lips.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pink - a pale red color

Etymology

Formed from 'flesh' (human skin) + 'coloured', used since the 17th century.
