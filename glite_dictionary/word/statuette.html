statuette

1. NOUN

/ ˌstætʃuˈɛt / (AmE), / ˌstætʃuˈɛt / (BrE)
a small statue, especially one carved or molded to represent a person, animal, or object
small statue or figurine

Examples

The bronze statuette was displayed prominently on the mantelpiece.
She received an Oscar statuette for her outstanding performance.
The museum exhibit includes a 3000-year-old Egyptian statuette.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: statue - a large sculpted figure of a person or animal
  • Synonym: figurine - a small ornamental figure of a person or animal

Etymology

From French 'statuette', diminutive of 'statue'.
