William Henry Fox Talbot

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪljəm ˈhɛnri fɑks ˈtælˌbət / (AmE), / ˈwɪljəm ˈhɛnri fɒks ˈtælbət / (BrE)
(1800–1877) English inventor and pioneer in photography who published the first book illustrated with photographs.
English photography pioneer and inventor

Examples

William Henry Fox Talbot is credited with inventing the calotype process in photography.
The first book illustrated with photographs was published by William Henry Fox Talbot.
Historians often mention William Henry Fox Talbot as a central figure in the development of early photography.
