Gaetano Donizetti

1. PROPN

/ ɡaɪˈeɪtoʊ ˌdoʊnɪˈzɛti / (AmE), / ɡaɪˈætəʊ ˌdɒnɪˈzɛti / (BrE)
(1797–1848) Italian composer known for his operas, including 'Lucia di Lammermoor' and 'L'elisir d'amore'.
