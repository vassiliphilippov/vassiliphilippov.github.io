sun-loving

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌn ˌlʌvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌn ˌlʌvɪŋ / (BrE)
(of plants) thriving or flourishing in strong sunlight.
thriving in strong sunlight (of plants)

Examples

Lavender is a sun-loving plant that needs at least six hours of sunlight each day.
The garden features many sun-loving flowers that bloom in the summer heat.
If you want your cacti to thrive, place them in a sun-loving spot by the window.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: shade-loving - (of plants) thriving in shade rather than sunlight
