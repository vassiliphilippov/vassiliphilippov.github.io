bricklaying

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪkleɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrɪkleɪɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or skill of building with bricks, especially as a trade or craft
the craft of building with bricks

Examples

He began learning bricklaying as an apprentice after leaving school.
The house was built by a team skilled in bricklaying.
Some people take evening classes in bricklaying as a hobby.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masonry - the craft or work of building with stone, brick, or concrete
