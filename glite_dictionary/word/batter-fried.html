batter-fried

1. ADJ

/ ˈbæɾɚ ˌfraɪd / (AmE), / ˈbætə ˌfraɪd / (BrE)
(of food) coated in batter and then deep-fried.
coated in batter and fried

Examples

The restaurant's batter-fried fish is always crispy and delicious.
He ordered a plate of batter-fried vegetables as an appetizer.
Many people enjoy batter-fried chicken at summer fairs and festivals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deep-fried - cooked by frying in deep fat or oil
