Volans

1. PROPN

/ ˈvoʊˌlænz / (AmE), / ˈvəʊ.lænz / (BrE)
a small constellation in the southern sky near Dorado and Carina, representing a flying fish.
constellation in southern sky

Examples

The constellation Volans is best observed from the Southern Hemisphere during autumn.
Unlike the more famous Orion, Volans is relatively faint and often overlooked by amateur astronomers.
In star charts, Volans is depicted as a flying fish near the constellations Dorado and Carina.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dorado - a constellation near Volans in the southern sky
  • Synonym: Carina - a constellation near Volans in the southern sky

Etymology

From Latin 'volans' meaning 'flying', short for 'piscis volans' (flying fish).
