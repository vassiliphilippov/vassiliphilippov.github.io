hatband

1. NOUN

/ ˈhætˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈhatˌband / (BrE)
A strip of material encircling the crown of a hat, typically just above the brim, often for decoration or to secure the hat's fit.
decorative band around a hat crown

Examples

He chose a feathered hatband to complement his elegant suit.
The old straw hat was missing its hatband, making it look unfinished.
She tied a silk scarf as a makeshift hatband around her sunhat.

Etymology

From 'hat' + 'band'; attested since the 15th century.
