itching

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
An uncomfortable skin sensation that produces a desire to scratch.
uncomfortable skin sensation causing need to scratch

Examples

After hiking through the forest, I felt a persistent itching on my arms and legs.
Allergy medication can help relieve the itching caused by insect bites.
The itching became so intense that she had to scratch her skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pruritus - medical term for itching

Etymology

From Old English *giccan* (to itch).
