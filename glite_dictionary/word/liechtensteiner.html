Liechtensteiner

1. ADJ

/ ˌlɪktənˈstaɪnər / (AmE), / ˌlɪktənˈstaɪnə / (BrE)
relating to Liechtenstein or its people
of or relating to Liechtenstein

Examples

The museum displays many Liechtensteiner artifacts from centuries past.
Several Liechtensteiner artists contributed to the international exhibition.
We enjoyed tasting authentic Liechtensteiner cuisine during our trip.

Synset Examples

Liechtensteiner castles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Liechtenstein - a small landlocked country in Central Europe

Etymology

From Liechtenstein, the name of the European country, with English adjectival suffix '-er'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈlɪk.tənˌstaɪ.nər / (AmE), / ˈlɪk.tənˌstaɪ.nə / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Liechtenstein
person from Liechtenstein

Examples

Several Liechtensteiners attended the international conference in Geneva.
The traditions of a Liechtensteiner are influenced by both Swiss and Austrian cultures.
As a proud Liechtensteiner, she always celebrates the country's national holiday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Liechtenstein - a small landlocked country in central Europe

Etymology

From Liechtenstein (country) + -er (demonymic suffix).
