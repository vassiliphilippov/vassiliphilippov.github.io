Ventolin

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛn.tə.lɪn / (AmE), / ˈvɛn.tə.lɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand of the bronchodilator drug salbutamol (albuterol), used to relieve symptoms of asthma and other respiratory conditions.
brand of asthma bronchodilator
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Ventolin to help control my asthma attacks.
You should always carry your Ventolin inhaler in case of sudden breathing difficulties.
After using Ventolin, some people experience a slight tremor or fast heartbeat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salbutamol - the active drug ingredient in Ventolin
  • Synonym: albuterol - alternative name for the active ingredient in Ventolin
