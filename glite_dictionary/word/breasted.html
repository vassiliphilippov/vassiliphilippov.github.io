breasted

1. ADJ

/ ˈbrɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˈbrɛstɪd / (BrE)
having a breast or breasts, or having breasts of a specified type or appearance (used chiefly in compounds).
having a breast or breasts; chiefly in compounds

Examples

The museum showcased the vibrant plumage of the red-breasted robin.
She preferred a single-breasted jacket for its sleek silhouette.
Among the species spotted today was a yellow-breasted chat.

Synset Examples

small-breasted
red-breasted sandpiper

Etymology

Formed from 'breast' + '-ed', indicating possession or presence.
