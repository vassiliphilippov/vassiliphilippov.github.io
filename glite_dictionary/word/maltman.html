maltman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔltˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmɔːltˌmæn / (BrE)
a man who makes or works with malt, especially in a brewery or distillery
male maker of malt

Examples

The old brewery hired a skilled maltman to ensure the quality of the grain.
As a maltman, he took pride in his ability to produce the finest malt.
Historical records list his occupation as maltman, living on the outskirts of town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maltster - a person who makes or deals in malt

Etymology

From 'malt' (grain that has been germinated and dried) + 'man'.
