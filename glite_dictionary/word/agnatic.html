agnatic

1. ADJ

/ æɡˈnætɪk / (AmE), / æɡˈnætɪk / (BrE)
related to or tracing descent through the male line, especially the father's side of the family
related through the father's line

Examples

The inheritance was passed down strictly along agnatic lines in that ancient society.
Only agnatic relatives were included in the traditional family council.
In some royal families, succession is limited to agnatic descendants.

Synset Examples

a paternal aunt

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cognatic - related through either the male or female line
  • Synonym: paternal - related on the father's side
  • Antonym: maternal - related on the mother's side

Etymology

From Latin agnatus (related by descent through males).
