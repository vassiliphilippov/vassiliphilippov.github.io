Changan

1. PROPN

/ tʃænˈgɑn / (AmE), / tʃænˈɡæn / (BrE)
a historic city in central China, formerly the capital of several ancient Chinese dynasties, notably during 221–206 BC
historic city in central China

Examples

The ancient city of Changan was once the cultural center of East Asia.
Many historical sites in Changan attract tourists from around the world.
During the Qin dynasty, Changan served as the imperial capital.
