analyzer

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.əˌlaɪ.zɚ / (AmE), / ˈæn.əˌlaɪ.zə / (BrE)
An instrument or device used to perform analyses, especially scientific or technical examinations of substances or data.
device for analyzing substances or data

Examples

The laboratory purchased a new analyzer to test water samples for contaminants.
A gas analyzer can measure the concentration of various chemicals in the air.
Technicians use a blood analyzer to quickly obtain patient test results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: analyser - British spelling of 'analyzer', meaning a device for analysis

Etymology

From 'analyse' + '-er', denoting an agent or instrument.
