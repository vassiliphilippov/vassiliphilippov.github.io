unwished-for

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈwɪʃt fɔr / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɪʃt fɔː / (BrE)
not wanted or not welcome; undesired
not wanted or welcome

Examples

She found herself the subject of unwished-for attention from the media.
The letter brought some unwished-for news that changed his plans.
There are times when unwished-for visitors drop by unannounced.

Synset Examples

unwelcome publicity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unwelcome - not wanted; not received with pleasure
  • Synonym: undesired - not wanted or wished for

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'wished' + 'for', meaning not wished for.
