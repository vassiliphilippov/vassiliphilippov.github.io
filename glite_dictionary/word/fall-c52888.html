A strange sound struck my ears

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strike - (of light or sound) to hit or reach a surface
  • Synonym: shine - (of light) to emit or reflect light on a surface

Etymology

From Old English *feallan* (to fall, to die), related to Old Norse *falla*, German *fallen*.
