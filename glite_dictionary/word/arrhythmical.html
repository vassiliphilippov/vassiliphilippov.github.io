arrhythmical

1. ADJ

/ əˈrɪðmɪkəl / (AmE), / əˈrɪðmɪkəl / (BrE)
lacking a regular or consistent rhythm; without regard for rhythm.
lacking rhythm

Examples

The song's arrhythmical beat made it hard for dancers to follow the music.
Her arrhythmical footsteps echoed unpredictably down the hallway.
The poet recited in an arrhythmical manner, giving a unique effect to the performance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rhythmical - having rhythm or regular variation
  • Synonym: arrhythmic - also lacking rhythm; often in medical or musical contexts

Etymology

From Greek prefix a- (not) + rhythmical.
