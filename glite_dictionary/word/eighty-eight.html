eighty-eight

1. NUM

/ ˌeɪtiˈeɪt / (AmE), / ˌeɪtiˈeɪt / (BrE)
the number equal to eighty plus eight; 88
the number 88

Examples

There are eighty-eight keys on a standard piano.
We expect around eighty-eight participants at the conference this year.
She turned eighty-eight years old last month.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eighty-seven - the number 87
  • Degree Higher: eighty-nine - the number 89

the number 88

Examples

Related Terms
