hagiology

1. NOUN

/ ˌheɪdʒiˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌhadʒiˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study or literature concerning the lives and legends of saints
study of lives of saints

Examples

She spent years researching medieval hagiology for her thesis.
The library houses a rich collection of Christian hagiology.
Modern scholars often analyze hagiology to understand religious traditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hagiography - a biography of a saint or venerated person

Etymology

From Greek hagios (holy) and -logia (study of).
