successfulness

1. NOUN

/ səkˈsɛsfəlnəs / (AmE), / səkˈsɛsfʊlnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being successful; having achieved prosperity or good fortune
the state of being successful

Examples

The successfulness of the company can be attributed to strong leadership and innovation.
We discussed the successfulness of different marketing strategies during the meeting.
Her successfulness as a lawyer inspired many young professionals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: success - the accomplishment of an aim or purpose
  • Antonym: failure - lack of success

Etymology

Formed from 'successful' + '-ness', ultimately from Latin 'succedere' (to succeed) via 'success'.
