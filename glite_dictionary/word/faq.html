FAQ

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛf.eɪˈkjuː / (AmE), / ˌɛf.eɪˈkjuː / (BrE)
a document or section that lists common questions and answers about a particular subject
list of frequently asked questions

Examples

The website's FAQ page helped me solve my problem quickly.
If you have questions, check the FAQ before contacting support.
The FAQ provides detailed information about our services.

Etymology

Acronym of 'Frequently Asked Questions'.
