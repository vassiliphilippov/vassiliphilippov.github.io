gathered

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡæðərd / (AmE), / ˈɡæðəd / (BrE)
(of things) brought together or collected in one place, often arranged in folds or pleats.
brought or collected together

Examples

The dress has gathered sleeves for a more dramatic look.
The data from multiple sources was gathered into a single report.
She admired the gathered fabric around the waist of the skirt.

Synset Examples

the collected works of Milton
the gathered folds of the skirt

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collected - brought or gathered together into a group or mass
