Corticium solani

1. NOUN

/ kɔrˈtɪsiəm soʊˈlɑːnaɪ / (AmE), / kɔːˈtɪsiəm səʊˈlɑːnaɪ / (BrE)
a species of fungus that causes bottom rot in lettuce and other plants
fungus causing bottom rot in lettuce

Examples

Corticium solani is a major cause of bottom rot in commercial lettuce crops.
Researchers are developing new strategies to control Corticium solani infections in greenhouses.
The lettuce was lost to Corticium solani before harvest could begin.

Etymology

From Latin: 'corticium' (little bark) + 'solani' (of Solanum, a plant genus).
