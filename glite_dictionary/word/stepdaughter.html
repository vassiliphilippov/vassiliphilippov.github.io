stepdaughter

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɛpˌdɔtər / (AmE), / ˈstɛpˌdɔːtə / (BrE)
the daughter of your spouse from a previous marriage or relationship
daughter of your spouse from a previous marriage

Examples

She has a stepdaughter from her husband's previous marriage who visits every summer.
After the wedding, he officially became Ella's stepdaughter.
The proud mother introduced both her daughter and stepdaughter at the family reunion.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stepson - the son of your spouse from a previous marriage

Etymology

From 'step-' (Old English stēop, indicating relationship by remarriage) + 'daughter'.
