Medinilla magnifica

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛdɪˈnɪlə mægˈnɪfɪkə / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪˈnɪlə mægˈnɪfɪkə / (BrE)
A tropical evergreen epiphytic shrub native to the Philippines, grown for its large panicles of pink flowers and lush foliage.
tropical shrub with pink flowers, native to the Philippines

Examples

The Medinilla magnifica thrives in humid environments with filtered sunlight and regular misting.
Botanical gardens often display the striking pink blooms of the Medinilla magnifica.
Collectors prize Medinilla magnifica for its ornamental foliage and spectacular floral displays.

Etymology

Genus name 'Medinilla' honors José de Medinilla y Pineda; 'magnifica' is Latin for 'magnificent'.
