khukuri

1. NOUN

/ kuːˈkuːri / (AmE), / kuːˈkuːri / (BrE)
A curved steel knife with a razor-sharp edge, traditionally used by the Gurkhas and holding cultural and religious importance in Nepal.
curved knife of the Gurkhas (Nepal)

Examples

The soldier carried a khukuri at his side during the ceremony.
Collectors value the traditional khukuri for its craftsmanship and history.
He presented a beautifully decorated khukuri as a gift to the visiting dignitary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kukri - alternative spelling for the Gurkha knife

Etymology

From Nepali खुकुरी (khukuri), referring to this traditional knife.
