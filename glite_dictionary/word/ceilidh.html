ceilidh

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪli / (AmE), / ˈkeɪli / (BrE)
an informal social event with Scottish or Irish folk music, singing, dancing, and storytelling
Scottish/Irish social event with folk music and dancing

Examples

We attended a lively ceilidh in Edinburgh where everyone joined in the dancing.
The band played traditional tunes at the ceilidh last weekend.
She learned several folk dances at her first ceilidh in Ireland.

Etymology

From Scottish Gaelic 'cèilidh' (visit, social gathering).
