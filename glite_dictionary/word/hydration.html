hydration

1. NOUN

/ haɪˈdreɪʃən / (AmE), / haɪˈdreɪʃən / (BrE)
The process of combining with water or the absorption of water by a substance.
process of combining with water

Examples

Proper hydration is essential for good health during exercise.
The hydration of cement is a crucial step in the setting process.
Skin hydration levels can be improved by using a good moisturizer.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dehydration - the process of removing water from something

Etymology

From Greek hydor (water) via French hydratation.
