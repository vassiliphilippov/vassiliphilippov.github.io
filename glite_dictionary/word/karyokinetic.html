karyokinetic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkæri.oʊ.kɪˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌkæri.əʊ.kɪˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the division of a cell's nucleus during mitosis or meiosis
relating to nuclear division in cells
medical

Examples

Observing the karyokinetic figures under the microscope helped the students understand mitotic processes.
The disorder interrupts normal karyokinetic activity in developing cells.
Chemotherapy drugs often target karyokinetic functions to prevent cancer cell proliferation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mitotic - relating to mitosis, the process of cell division
  • Synonym: karyokinesis - the process of division of a cell's nucleus

Etymology

From Greek 'karyon' (nut, kernel, nucleus) + 'kinētikos' (moving, setting in motion).
