Lagidium

1. NOUN

/ ləˈɡɪdiəm / (AmE), / ləˈɡɪdiəm / (BrE)
A genus of small South American rodents in the family Chinchillidae, commonly known as mountain viscachas.
genus of South American rodents (mountain viscachas)

Examples

The Lagidium genus includes several species of mountain viscachas native to South America.
Researchers observed a Lagidium colony high in the Andes during their field study.
Unlike its cousin, the chinchilla, the Lagidium prefers rocky mountainous habitats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chinchilla - a related genus in the family Chinchillidae

Etymology

From New Latin, based on Greek 'lagos' (hare), referencing the animals' appearance.
