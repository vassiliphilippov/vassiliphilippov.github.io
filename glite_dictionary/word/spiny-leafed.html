spiny-leafed

1. ADJ

/ ˈspaɪni ˌliːft / (AmE), / ˈspaɪni ˌliːft / (BrE)
having leaves covered with or bearing spines or sharp points
having spiny leaves

Examples

The spiny-leafed plants along the path made it difficult to walk without scratching yourself.
Gardeners often avoid spiny-leafed shrubs because they can be hard to trim safely.
Many desert species are spiny-leafed to protect themselves from herbivores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiny - covered with or having spines
  • Synonym: thorny-leaved - having leaves with thorns
