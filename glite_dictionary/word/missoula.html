Missoula

1. PROPN

/ mɪˈzuːlə / (AmE), / mɪˈzuːlə / (BrE)
A city in western Montana, United States, known as a university town and regional cultural center.
city in western Montana, US

Examples

The University of Montana is located in Missoula, attracting students from across the country.
After moving to Missoula, she quickly became involved in the local arts scene.
Every summer, Missoula hosts a popular music festival that draws thousands of visitors.
