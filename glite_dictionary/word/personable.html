personable

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝːsənəbəl / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəbəl / (BrE)
(of a person) having a pleasant appearance and manner that makes others like or admire them.
pleasant in appearance and personality

Examples

The new manager is very personable and easy to talk to.
Her personable nature makes her popular with colleagues and clients alike.
He gave a personable impression during the interview, smiling and engaging with everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charming - very pleasant or attractive in manner or appearance
  • Antonym: unpleasant - not attractive or agreeable in manner or appearance

Etymology

From Middle French *personable*, from Old French, ultimately from Latin *persona* (person).
