one-sixteenth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn sɪkˈstiːnθ / (AmE), / wʌn sɪkˈstiːnθ / (BrE)
Each of the sixteen equal parts into which a whole can be divided; 1/16.
one of sixteen equal parts; 1/16

Examples

Add one-sixteenth of a teaspoon of salt to the mixture for better flavor.
The carpenter needed to cut one-sixteenth of an inch off the end of the board.
Each piece represents one-sixteenth of the whole pie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sixteenth - one of sixteen equal parts; 1/16
  • Degree Higher: one-eighth - one of eight equal parts; 1/8
  • Degree Lower: one-thirty-second - one of thirty-two equal parts; 1/32
