Panonychus ulmi

1. NOUN

/ pænəˈnɪkəs ˈʌlmaɪ / (AmE), / pænəˈnɪkəs ˈʌlmi / (BrE)
A species of small web-spinning mite that is a serious pest of fruit trees in orchards.
species of orchard-damaging mite

Examples

The apple orchard suffered heavy damage from Panonychus ulmi infestations this year.
Panonychus ulmi can significantly reduce crop yields if not managed properly.
Researchers are developing new ways to control Panonychus ulmi without harming beneficial insects.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin, Panonychus (genus); ulmi, Latin for 'of the elm'.
