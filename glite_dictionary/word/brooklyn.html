Brooklyn

1. PROPN

/ ˈbrʊklɪn / (AmE), / ˈbrʊklɪn / (BrE)
a borough of New York City, located on the western end of Long Island, known for its cultural diversity and large population
New York City borough

Examples

She moved to Brooklyn to pursue her career in the arts.
Many popular music festivals are held annually in Brooklyn.
The skyline of Brooklyn offers stunning views of Manhattan.

Etymology

Named after the Dutch village of Breukelen.
