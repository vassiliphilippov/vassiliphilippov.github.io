weightlifting

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪtˌlɪftɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈweɪtˌlɪftɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity or sport of lifting weights, typically for exercise, bodybuilding, or competition.
lifting weights as exercise or sport

Examples

He goes to the gym every morning for weightlifting to build his strength.
Competitive weightlifting requires intense skill, focus, and physical training.
After months of weightlifting, she noticed significant muscle growth in her arms and legs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powerlifting - sport of lifting maximum weights in a few specific lifts
  • Synonym: bodybuilding - developing muscles through exercise, especially for display

Etymology

From 'weight' + 'lifting.'
