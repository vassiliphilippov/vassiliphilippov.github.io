bristled

1. ADJ

/ ˈbrɪsəld / (AmE), / ˈbrɪsəld / (BrE)
having or covered with bristles, barbs, quills, spines, or similar stiff, hair-like structures.
covered with bristles or barbs

Examples

The caterpillar's body was bristled with tiny spines for protection.
Many desert plants are bristled with thorns to deter animals from eating them.
The fruit was bristled with barbs that clung to the animal's fur.

Synset Examples

a horse with a short bristly mane
bristly shrubs
burred fruits
setaceous whiskers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bristly - having a stiff and prickly texture due to bristles
