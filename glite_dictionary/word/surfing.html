surfing

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝː.fɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.fɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport of riding on the crest of waves toward the shore while standing or lying on a surfboard.
sport of riding waves on a board

Examples

He spent every summer morning surfing at the beach.
Professional surfing competitions attract athletes from all over the world.
Many tourists try surfing for the first time when visiting Hawaii.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bodyboarding - the sport of riding waves lying on a small board
  • Synonym: windsurfing - the sport of sailing on water on a board with an attached sail

Etymology

From 'surf' (waves breaking on the shore) + '-ing', indicating the activity.
