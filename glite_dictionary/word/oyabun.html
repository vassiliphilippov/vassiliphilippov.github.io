oyabun

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔɪəˈbʊn / (AmE), / ˌɔɪəˈbʊn / (BrE)
(in Japan) a leader or boss, especially of a yakuza crime syndicate.
leader of a yakuza syndicate

Examples

The oyabun is responsible for the overall strategy and protection of the group.
Each oyabun is regarded with great respect by his followers.
Police have linked the notorious oyabun to several illegal activities.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: kobun - a subordinate or follower in a yakuza group

Etymology

Japanese 親分 (oyabun), meaning 'parent part' or 'boss'.
