unmatchable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmætʃ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈmatʃ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
too good to be equaled by anyone or anything; having no equal or rival; matchless.
impossible to equal; matchless

Examples

Her unmatchable talent as a pianist left the audience in awe.
The chef is known for his unmatchable creativity in the kitchen.
They believed the ancient monument possessed an unmatchable beauty.

Synset Examples

matchless beauty
the team's nonpareil center fielder
she's one girl in a million
the one and only Muhammad Ali
a peerless scholar
infamy unmatched in the Western world
wrote with unmatchable clarity
unrivaled mastery of her art

Related Terms

  • Synonym: matchless - having no equal; incomparable
  • Synonym: peerless - without equal; unrivaled
  • Synonym: unrivaled - having no rival; better than any other
  • Antonym: mediocre - of only average quality; not very good

Etymology

From un- (not) + match (to equal) + -able (capable of).
