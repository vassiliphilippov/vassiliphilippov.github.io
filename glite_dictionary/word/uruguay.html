Uruguay

1. PROPN

/ ˌjʊrəˈɡwaɪ / (AmE), / ˈjʊərəˌɡwaɪ / (BrE)
a country in southeastern South America, bordered by Brazil and Argentina, with Montevideo as its capital.
country in South America

Examples

She traveled to Uruguay last summer to explore its vibrant cities and beaches.
The football team from Uruguay won two FIFA World Cups in the twentieth century.
Exports like beef and wool play a major role in the economy of Uruguay.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Uruguay', from the Uruguay River, which derives from the Guaraní language meaning 'river of painted birds'.
