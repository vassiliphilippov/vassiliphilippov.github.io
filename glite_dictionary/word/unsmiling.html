unsmiling

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsmaɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsmaɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
showing no sign of a smile; serious or stern in expression
not smiling; serious expression

Examples

Her unsmiling face made everyone feel nervous during the meeting.
The teacher gave him an unsmiling glance as he entered the classroom late.
Despite the celebration, he remained unsmiling throughout the evening.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smiling - having a smile; showing a smile
  • Synonym: serious - not joking or playful; solemn

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'smiling'.
