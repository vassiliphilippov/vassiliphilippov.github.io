Same

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːmi / (AmE), / ˈsɑːmi / (BrE)
the language or group of languages spoken by the Sami people in northern Scandinavia and the Kola Peninsula
the language(s) of the Sami people

Examples

The Same language is spoken by communities across Norway, Sweden, Finland, and Russia.
She studied Same to communicate with local Sami elders during her research.
There are several dialects of Same, each unique to different regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sami - the indigenous people of northern Scandinavia and the Kola Peninsula

Etymology

From Sami *sámegiella* ('Sami language'). Sometimes anglicized from obsolete forms like 'Lapp' or 'Same'.
