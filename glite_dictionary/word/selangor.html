Selangor

1. PROPN

/ səˈlɑːŋɡɔr / (AmE), / səˈlæŋɔː / (BrE)
a sultanate and one of the thirteen states forming the Federation of Malaysia, located on the west coast of Peninsular Malaysia
state of Malaysia; a sultanate

Examples

Many tourists visit Selangor to experience its vibrant cultural festivals and historical landmarks.
The capital of Selangor is Shah Alam, which is known for its impressive blue mosque.
She moved to Selangor after receiving a job offer from a multinational company based there.

Etymology

From Malay 'Selangor', possibly from 'selangau' (a type of fly) or an old river name.
