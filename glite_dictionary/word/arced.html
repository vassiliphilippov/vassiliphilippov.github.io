arced

1. ADJ

/ ɑrkt / (AmE), / ɑːkt / (BrE)
Shaped in the form of an arch or curve; forming or resembling an arch.
shaped like an arch or curve

Examples

The bridge's arced design is both elegant and functional.
Sunlight filtered through the arced branches above the path.
The ceiling was arced, giving the room a spacious feel.

Synset Examples

an arched ceiling

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arched - having a shape or form like an arch
