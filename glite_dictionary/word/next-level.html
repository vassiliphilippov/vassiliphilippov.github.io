next-level

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɛkst ˌlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈnɛkst ˌlɛvəl / (BrE)
of a higher quality, standard, or advancement; surpassing what is usual or expected
superior or more advanced than the norm
informal

Examples

Her new recipe is truly next-level and impresses every guest she has.
The phone’s camera has next-level features that competitors lack.
Their teamwork in this match was absolutely next-level compared to last season.

Synset Examples

Her performance was next-level, impressing everyone in the audience.
The new software offers next-level features for users.
They took their game to next-level strategy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advanced - more highly developed or skilled
  • Synonym: cutting-edge - at the latest or most advanced stage
  • Antonym: basic - simple; having only the essential features
