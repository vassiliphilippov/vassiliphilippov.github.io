smiley

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmaɪli / (AmE), / ˈsmaɪli / (BrE)
an image or symbol, typically formed using keyboard characters or graphics, representing a smiling face, used to convey friendliness or happiness in digital communication
emoticon or symbol of a smiling face

Examples

She ended her message with a smiley to show she was joking.
Many people add a smiley at the end of their texts to appear friendly.
I love using the classic yellow smiley in my chat conversations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emoticon - a representation of a facial expression using keyboard characters
