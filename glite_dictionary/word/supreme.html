supreme

1. ADJ

/ səˈprim / (AmE), / suːˈpriːm / (BrE)
highest in rank, authority, or importance; greatest or most extreme.
greatest in status or authority

Examples

The court is the supreme authority on constitutional matters.
Winning the championship was his supreme achievement.
In times of crisis, she displayed supreme confidence and leadership.

Synset Examples

a supreme tribunal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paramount - more important than anything else; supreme
  • Antonym: inferior - lower in rank, status, or quality

Etymology

From Latin supremus, superlative of superus (that is above) from super (above).
