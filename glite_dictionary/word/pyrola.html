Pyrola

1. NOUN

/ paɪˈroʊlə / (AmE), / paɪˈrəʊlə / (BrE)
a genus of short-stemmed perennial herbs of cool or temperate regions, also known as wintergreen or shinleaf, belonging to the family Ericaceae
a genus of perennial herbs (wintergreen or shinleaf)

Examples

The forest floor was covered with flowering Pyrola among the moss and ferns.
Botanists documented several species within the Pyrola genus during their survey.
Some medicinal traditions use Pyrola leaves for their reputed healing properties.

Etymology

From New Latin Pyrola, diminutive of Latin pyrus (pear), referring to leaves resembling those of a pear.
