rockfoil

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑkˌfɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈrɒkˌfɔɪl / (BrE)
Any of various small flowering plants of the genus Saxifraga, often found growing in rocky places.
plant of the Saxifraga genus

Examples

Many species of rockfoil display delicate white or pink flowers in spring.
The alpine garden was filled with various types of rockfoil nestled between the stones.
She planted rockfoil in the cracks of her stone wall, where it thrived.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Saxifraga - the botanical genus to which rockfoil belongs
  • Synonym: saxifrage - another common name for plants in the genus Saxifraga

Etymology

From Latin 'saxifraga' meaning 'stone-breaker', after the plant's ability to grow in rocky places.
