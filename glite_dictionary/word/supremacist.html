supremacist

1. NOUN

/ səˈprɛməsɪst / (AmE), / sjuːˈprɛməsɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that one particular group, especially a racial group, is superior to all others and seeks to promote its dominance
someone advocating group or racial superiority

Examples

He was identified as a known supremacist during the investigation.
Several organizations denounced the supremacist rhetoric found in the manifesto.
The documentary explores the history of supremacist movements in the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racist - a person who believes that a particular race is superior to others

Etymology

From 'supremacy' + '-ist', patterned after similar formations denoting advocacy or practices.
