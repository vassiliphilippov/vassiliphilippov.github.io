self-improvement

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlf ɪmˈpruvmənt / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf ɪmˈpruːvmənt / (BrE)
the process of seeking to improve one's knowledge, character, or abilities through one's own efforts
the process of improving oneself

Examples

She devoted her evenings to self-improvement by taking online courses.
Many people view reading as a valuable tool for self-improvement.
His journey of self-improvement inspired those around him to pursue their own goals.

Synset Examples

He focused on self-improvement through daily habits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personal development - the process of improving personal skills and qualities
  • Antonym: self-neglect - failure to care for or improve oneself

Etymology

From self- (referring to oneself) + improvement (from Old French 'emprover', from Latin 'improbare', to improve).
