diesinker

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪˌsɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈdaɪˌsɪŋkə / (BrE)
a person whose occupation is making dies, especially engraved dies for stamping coins, medals, or other objects
person who engraves dies for stamping

Examples

The diesinker created intricate patterns for the new commemorative coins.
She apprenticed with a renowned diesinker to learn the art of engraving metal dies.
Many historical medals bear the mark of a skilled diesinker from the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: engraver - a person who cuts or carves designs on a hard surface
  • Synonym: die-maker - a person who makes dies for manufacturing or minting

Etymology

From 'die' (an engraved stamp) + 'sinker' (from 'sink,' to cut or engrave into a surface).
