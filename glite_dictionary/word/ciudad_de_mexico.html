Ciudad de Mexico

1. PROPN

/ sjuˈðɑð ðe ˈmexɪkoʊ / (AmE), / sjuˈðað de ˈmeksɪkəʊ / (BrE)
The capital and largest city of Mexico, serving as its political, cultural, commercial, and industrial center; also known as Mexico City.
capital and largest city of Mexico

Examples

Tourists from around the world flock to Ciudad de Mexico to experience its vibrant culture and cuisine.
The air pollution problem in Ciudad de Mexico has prompted major environmental initiatives.
Many international companies have their headquarters in Ciudad de Mexico due to its economic importance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mexico City - the capital and largest city of Mexico

Etymology

From Spanish 'Ciudad de México', meaning 'City of Mexico'.
