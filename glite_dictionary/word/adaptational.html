adaptational

1. ADJ

/ əˌdæp.təˈneɪ.ʃən.əl / (AmE), / əˌdaptəˈneɪʃənəl / (BrE)
Relating to or resulting from adaptation, especially in biology or psychology.
relating to adaptation

Examples

The scientist studied the adaptational mechanisms of desert plants.
Her research focused on the adaptational changes that occur in response to stress.
Adaptational responses can be observed in both behavioral and physical traits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adaptive - having the ability to adapt or adjust

Etymology

From 'adaptation' + '-al', forming adjectives meaning 'pertaining to'.
