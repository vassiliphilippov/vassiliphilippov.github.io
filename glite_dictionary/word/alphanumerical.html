alphanumerical

1. ADJ

/ ˌæl.fə.nuːˈmɛr.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌæl.fə.njuːˈmɛr.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
Consisting of, or relating to, both letters and numbers.
containing letters and numbers

Examples

Please enter your alphanumerical password to log in.
The code must be alphanumerical, not just numeric.
Student IDs are usually alphanumerical rather than strictly numbers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alphanumeric - consisting of both letters and numbers

Etymology

From 'alpha' (the first letter of the Greek alphabet) + 'numerical' (relating to numbers).
