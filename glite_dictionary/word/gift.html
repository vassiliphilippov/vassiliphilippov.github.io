gift

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪft / (AmE), / ɡɪft / (BrE)
something that is given to someone without payment; a present
something given without payment

Examples

She received a beautiful gift for her birthday.
He wrapped the gift before bringing it to the party.
The watch was a gift from his parents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: present - something given to someone on a special occasion as a gift

Etymology

From Old Norse 'gift' (gift, present), related to 'give'.
