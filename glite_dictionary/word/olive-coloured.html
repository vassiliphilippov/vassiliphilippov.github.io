olive-coloured

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːlɪv ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
Having the color of green olives; a yellowish-green shade.
yellowish-green in color

Examples

She wore a beautiful olive-coloured dress to the party last night.
The artist mixed paints to achieve the perfect olive-coloured backdrop for the landscape.
His eyes were a striking olive-coloured shade that caught everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: olive-green - having an olive-like green color
