underfelt

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌndərˌfɛlt / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌfɛlt / (BrE)
A layer of thick felt material placed beneath a carpet to provide cushioning and insulation.
pad of felt under a carpet

Examples

The installer placed the underfelt carefully before rolling out the new carpet.
Upgrading the underfelt made the floor softer and helped with heat insulation.
They chose a dense underfelt to reduce noise between the apartment floors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: underlay - a material laid beneath a carpet for insulation or padding

Etymology

Formed from 'under-' (beneath) + 'felt' (textile fabric), referring to felt placed underneath.
