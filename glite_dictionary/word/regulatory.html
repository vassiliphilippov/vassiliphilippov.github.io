regulatory

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛɡjəˌleɪtɔri / (AmE), / rɪˈɡjʊlətəri / (BrE)
having the function of controlling or governing according to rules or principles.
related to control by rules

Examples

The new policy sets strict regulatory standards for air quality in urban areas.
Certain enzymes play a crucial regulatory role in cellular processes.
The company must comply with regulatory requirements imposed by the government.

Synset Examples

a regulatory gene

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regulative - serving or intended to regulate or control

Etymology

From 'regulate' + '-ory', patterned after words like 'advisory'.
