pedology

1. NOUN

/ piˈdɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / pɪˈdɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with the medical care and treatment of infants and children
medical care of infants and children
medical

Examples

She is studying pedology as part of her medical specialization.
Advances in pedology have greatly reduced infant mortality rates.
The hospital hired a doctor with a background in pedology to lead the new children's ward.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pediatrics - the branch of medicine dealing with infants and children

Etymology

From Greek pais, paid- (child) + -logia (study of).
