finalization

1. NOUN

/ ˌfaɪnələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfaɪnəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something final or complete
the act of making final or complete

Examples

The finalization of the contract took several months due to legal complexities.
We are awaiting the finalization of travel plans before booking the tickets.
After years of negotiation, the finalization of the merger was officially announced.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: completion - the act of finishing something
  • Synonym: termination - the act of bringing something to an end
  • Antonym: initiation - the act of beginning something

Etymology

From 'finalize' (from final + -ize) + -ation.
