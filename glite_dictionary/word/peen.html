peen

1. NOUN

/ pin / (AmE), / piÀên / (BrE)
the end of a hammerhead opposite the flat striking surface, which may be shaped in various ways for specific uses
end of a hammer opposite the striking face

Examples

A ball peen hammer has a rounded end for shaping metal.
The mechanic used the peen to bend the metal slightly.
Different types of peen can be found on different hammers depending on their intended use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hammerhead - the heavy part of a hammer used for striking

Etymology

Possibly from Middle English *pene* or Old French *pine* (edge), but etymology is uncertain.
