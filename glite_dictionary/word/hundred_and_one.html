hundred and one

1. NUM

/ ˈhʌndrəd ənd ˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˈhʌndrəd ənd ˈwʌn / (BrE)
being one more than one hundred; the number 101.
the number 101

Examples

There are hundred and one reasons to be grateful this year.
Her story was one of the hundred and one tales told at camp.
The jar contained exactly hundred and one jelly beans after counting.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: one hundred - the number 100
  • Degree Higher: one hundred and two - the number 102
