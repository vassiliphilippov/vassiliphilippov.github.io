Crocuta crocuta

1. NOUN

/ kroʊˈkjuːtə kroʊˈkjuːtə / (AmE), / krəʊˈkjuːtə krəʊˈkjuːtə / (BrE)
the scientific name for the spotted hyena, a large carnivorous African mammal known for its distinctive howl
scientific name for the spotted hyena

Examples

The Crocuta crocuta is recognized for its powerful jaws and social behavior.
Researchers observed a pack of Crocuta crocuta hunting cooperatively on the African savannah.
The distinctive howl of Crocuta crocuta can be heard from miles away during the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotted hyena - common name of Crocuta crocuta, a large carnivorous mammal from Africa

Etymology

From Latin, where 'crocuta' refers to the hyena, derived from Greek 'krokottas.'
