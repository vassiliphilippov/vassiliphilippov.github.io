Solingen

1. PROPN

/ ˈzoʊlɪŋən / (AmE), / ˈzɒlɪŋən / (BrE)
A city in western Germany, widely known for its production of cutlery and edged tools.
German city known for cutlery

Examples

The knives were manufactured in Solingen, which is famous for its high-quality blades.
She bought a beautiful set of cutlery imported from Solingen.
Many collectors seek antique swords crafted in Solingen, Germany.
