blindfolded

1. ADJ

/ ˈblaɪnˌfoʊldɪd / (AmE), / ˈblaɪnˌfəʊldɪd / (BrE)
wearing a blindfold over the eyes, unable to see
wearing a blindfold

Examples

The children were blindfolded before playing the game of pin the tail on the donkey.
Investigators led a blindfolded suspect into the courtroom.
She tried to walk across the room blindfolded as part of the challenge.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sighted - having the ability to see or not being blindfolded
