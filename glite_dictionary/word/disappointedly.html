disappointedly

1. ADV

/ dɪsəˈpɔɪntɪdli / (AmE), / dɪsəˈpɔɪntɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows disappointment; in a disappointed manner.
in a disappointed manner

Examples

He looked disappointedly at the empty shelf where the book had been.
She sighed disappointedly after hearing the bad news.
The child stared disappointedly at his melted ice cream.

Synset Examples

she left the gambling table disappointedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disappointed - feeling sad or displeased because something was not as good as expected

Etymology

From 'disappointed' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
