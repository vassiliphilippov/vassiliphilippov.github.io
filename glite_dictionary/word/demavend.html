Demavend

1. PROPN

/ ˌdɛməˈvɛnd / (AmE), / ˌdɛməˈvɛnd / (BrE)
an active volcano in northern Iran, and the highest peak in the country
volcano and highest peak in Iran

Examples

Mount Demavend is a popular destination for climbers and hikers exploring Iran's natural wonders.
The snow-capped peak of Demavend is visible from many kilometers away on a clear day.
According to Persian mythology, the dragon Zahhak was imprisoned inside Demavend.
