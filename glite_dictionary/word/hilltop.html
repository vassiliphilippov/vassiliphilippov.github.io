hilltop

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪlˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈhɪl.tɒp / (BrE)
The highest point or summit of a hill.
the summit of a hill

Examples

The old castle stood proudly on the hilltop, visible for miles around.
They reached the hilltop just as the sun began to rise.
From the hilltop, we could see the entire valley spread out below us.

Synset Examples

the sun set behind the brow of distant hills

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summit - the highest point of a hill or mountain
  • Antonym: valley - a low area of land between hills or mountains
