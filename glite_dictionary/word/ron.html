Ron

1. NOUN

/ rɑn / (AmE), / rɒn / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in Plateau State and surrounding areas of northern Nigeria
a Chadic language of northern Nigeria

Examples

The Ron language is spoken by communities in the Plateau State of Nigeria.
Linguists have documented many traditional stories in Ron.
