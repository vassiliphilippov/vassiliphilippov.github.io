ha'p'orth

1. NOUN

/ ˈheɪpɚθ / (AmE), / ˈheɪpəθ / (BrE)
an amount that can be bought for a halfpenny; a very small amount
a very small amount (historical)
archaic british

Examples

He only gave me a ha'p'orth of sweets for my penny.
There isn't a ha'p'orth of sense in what he's saying.
She left not even a ha'p'orth of bread in the kitchen.

Etymology

Contraction of 'halfpennyworth'. From halfpenny + worth.
