hater

1. NOUN

/ ˈheɪtər / (AmE), / ˈheɪtə / (BrE)
a person who greatly dislikes a particular person or thing, often expressing this dislike publicly or repeatedly
person who strongly dislikes or criticizes
informal

Examples

Don't pay attention to the haters; just do your best.
Social media often gives haters a platform to spread negativity.
Even successful people deal with a hater or two along the way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: critic - person who expresses disapproval or finds faults
  • Antonym: admirer - person who regards with respect or warm approval

Etymology

From English 'hate' + '-er', forming an agent noun.
