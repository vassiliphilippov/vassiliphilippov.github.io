collins

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑlɪnz / (AmE), / ˈkɒlɪnz / (BrE)
A tall iced cocktail made with liquor, usually gin, mixed with lemon juice and soda water, often served with a slice of lemon or other fruit.
tall iced cocktail with liquor and fruit juice

Examples

I ordered a collins at the bar to enjoy with my meal.
A classic collins is refreshing on a hot summer afternoon.
She mixed a collins with gin, lemon juice, and sparkling water for the party guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tom collins - a specific type of collins cocktail made with gin, lemon juice, sugar, and carbonated water

Etymology

Named after John Collins, a London waiter of the 19th century.
