depilous

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈpɪləs / (AmE), / dɪˈpɪləs / (BrE)
Lacking hair; completely hairless.
completely hairless
medical

Examples

The breed is known for being depilous, making it popular among allergy sufferers.
Some reptiles naturally have depilous skin throughout their lives.
After the treatment, his legs became completely depilous for several weeks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairless - having no hair at all
  • Antonym: hirsute - covered with hair

Etymology

From Latin 'depilus', from 'de-' (removal) + 'pilus' (hair).
