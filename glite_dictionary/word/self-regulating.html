self-regulating

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlf ˈrɛɡjəˌleɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf ˈrɛɡjʊˌleɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Able to adjust, control, or govern itself without external intervention.
able to control itself automatically

Examples

The new greenhouse uses a self-regulating heating system to keep the temperature stable.
Some ecosystems are remarkably self-regulating and can recover from disturbances without human help.
He invented a self-regulating valve to automatically adjust water flow under different conditions.

Synset Examples

a self-activating sprinkler system

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automatic - operating with minimal or no external control
  • Antonym: manual - requiring human operation or intervention

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'regulating', meaning to control or govern, indicating autonomous regulation.
