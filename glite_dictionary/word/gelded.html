gelded

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɛldɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡɛldɪd / (BrE)
(of a male animal) having had the testicles surgically removed; castrated.
having testicles removed (of a male animal)

Examples

The farmer introduced the new gelded horse to the rest of the herd.
After the procedure, the gelded bull became much calmer.
Veterinarians recommend gelded alpacas for pets to prevent aggressive behavior.

Synset Examples

a cut horse

Related Terms

  • Synonym: castrated - (of a male animal) having the testicles removed
  • Antonym: intact - (of a male animal) not castrated

Etymology

From 'geld', Middle English, from Old Norse 'gelda' (to castrate).
