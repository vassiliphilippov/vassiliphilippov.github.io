Ashgabat

1. PROPN

/ ˌɑːʃɡəˈbɑːt / (AmE), / ˌæʃɡəˈbæt / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Turkmenistan, located near the border with Iran.
capital city of Turkmenistan

Examples

The president delivered his annual speech in Ashgabat to a crowd of thousands.
During their journey across Central Asia, they spent several days exploring the sights of Ashgabat.
Many international embassies are located in Ashgabat due to its status as the nation's capital.

Etymology

From Russian Ашхабад (Ashkhabad), itself from Persian عشق‌آباد (Eshqābād, "city of love").
