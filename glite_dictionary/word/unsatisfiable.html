unsatisfiable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˌsætɪsˈfaɪəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˌsætɪsˈfaɪəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be satisfied or fulfilled; impossible to satisfy.
impossible to satisfy

Examples

Her curiosity was unsatisfiable, no matter how many questions she asked.
The mathematician encountered an unsatisfiable equation in his research.
An unsatisfiable demand from the client caused endless frustration.

Synset Examples

he knew the trap of unsatisfiable longing

Related Terms

  • Antonym: satisfiable - able to be satisfied
  • Synonym: insatiable - impossible to satisfy (typically of appetite or desire)

Etymology

From un- (not) + satisfiable, from Latin satisfacere (to satisfy).
