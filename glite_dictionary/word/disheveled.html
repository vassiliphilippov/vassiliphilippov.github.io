disheveled

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈʃɛvəld / (AmE), / dɪˈʃɛvəld / (BrE)
(of a person or their appearance) untidy; with hair, clothes, or surroundings in disorder.
untidy or in disorder

Examples

After the long flight, Emily's disheveled hair made it obvious she needed rest.
He walked into the meeting in a disheveled suit, drawing everyone's attention.
The disheveled room showed signs of a hurried departure.

Synset Examples

her clothing was disheveled
powder-smeared and frowzled
a rumpled unmade bed
a bed with tousled sheets
his brown hair was tousled, thick, and curly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: untidy - not arranged neatly; messy
  • Antonym: tidy - neat and in good order

Etymology

From Old French deschevelé (with hair uncovered or loose), from Latin capillus (hair).
