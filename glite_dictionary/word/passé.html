passé

1. ADJ

/ pæˈseɪ / (AmE), / paˈseɪ / (BrE)
no longer fashionable or in use; outdated or old-fashioned
out of fashion; outdated

Examples

The style of that dress looks rather passé these days.
Some of his theories are now considered passé in academic circles.
She worried that her website design seemed a bit passé compared to newer trends.

Synset Examples

a suit of rather antique appearance
demode (or outmoded) attire
outmoded ideas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outdated - old-fashioned, no longer useful or fashionable
  • Antonym: modern - currently fashionable or up-to-date

Etymology

From French passé (past participle of passer, 'to pass').
