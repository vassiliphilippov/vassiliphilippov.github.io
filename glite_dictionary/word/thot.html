thot

1. NOUN

/ θɑt / (AmE), / θɒt / (BrE)
(slang, derogatory) An insulting term for a woman considered to be sexually promiscuous.
derogatory slang for a promiscuous woman
slang offensive

Examples

Some rappers have been criticized for using the word thot in their lyrics.
He got into trouble for calling his classmate a thot online.
Using the term thot is considered disrespectful and offensive by many people.

Synset Examples

He called her a thot for her provocative outfit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hoe - slang term for a promiscuous woman
  • Synonym: slut - offensive term for a promiscuous woman

Etymology

Originated as Internet slang, generally believed to be an acronym for 'that ho over there.'
