Spyeria

1. NOUN

/ spaɪˈɪəriə / (AmE), / spaɪˈɪəriə / (BrE)
A genus of butterflies in the family Nymphalidae, commonly known as greater fritillaries, found chiefly in North America.
genus of butterflies (greater fritillaries)

Examples

The Spyeria butterflies are commonly found in North American woodlands.
