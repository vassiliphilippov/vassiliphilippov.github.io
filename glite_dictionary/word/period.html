/ ˈpɪriəd / (AmE), / ˈpɪəriəd / (BrE)

Examples

Scientists study rock layers to determine the period in which they formed.
This mineral deposit dates back to the Devonian period.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: era - a major division of geological time, longer than a period
  • Degree Lower: epoch - a subdivision of a period in geological time
