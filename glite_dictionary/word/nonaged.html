nonaged

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɑn.eɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˈnɒn.eɪdʒd / (BrE)
not having reached the legal age of adulthood; underage.
not of legal age; underage
legal

Examples

Certain contracts signed by a nonaged individual may be considered voidable by law.
The organization offers support to nonaged persons seeking emancipation from their parents.
The estate was left in trust until the heirs ceased to be nonaged.

Synset Examples

minor children

Related Terms

  • Synonym: underage - not yet of legal age; too young to have full legal rights
  • Synonym: minor - a person who is not yet legally an adult
  • Antonym: adult - a person who has reached the age of legal maturity

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'non-' (not) + 'aged' (having a specified age).
