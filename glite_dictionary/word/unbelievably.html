unbelievably

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.bɪˈliv.ə.bli / (AmE), / ˌʌn.bɪˈliː.və.bli / (BrE)
In a way that is very surprising, hard to believe, or extraordinary.
in an extraordinary or surprising way

Examples

The story she told was unbelievably strange and left everyone speechless.
This cake is unbelievably delicious—you have to try it!
He ran unbelievably fast and broke the school record.

Synset Examples

he was unbelievably angry

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incredibly - in a manner that is difficult to believe; extremely
  • Antonym: believably - in a believable or plausible manner

2. ADV

/ ʌn.bɪˈliv.əb.li / (AmE), / ʌn.bɪˈliː.vəb.li / (BrE)
In a way that is difficult or impossible to believe; to an extreme or remarkable degree.
in an incredible or extreme way

Examples

The mountain was unbelievably steep, making the climb much harder than we expected.
She finished the project unbelievably quickly, surprising everyone in the office.
The story was unbelievably strange, and few people actually believed it.

Synset Examples

incredibly, she survived the crash

  • Synonym: incredibly - in a way that is difficult to believe; extremely
