manicurist

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænəkjʊrɪst / (AmE), / ˈmanɪkjʊərɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to clean, trim, and polish fingernails, usually as a beauty treatment
nail care professional

Examples

The manicurist carefully shaped and polished each of my nails.
She visits her favorite manicurist every few weeks for a fresh manicure.
A skilled manicurist can make even the roughest hands look elegant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beautician - a person whose job is to give beauty treatments to women

Etymology

From French 'manucure' (manicure) + '-ist' (one who practices).
