compassionate

1. ADJ

/ kəmˈpæʃənət / (AmE), / kəmˈpæʃənət / (BrE)
feeling or showing concern and sympathy for the suffering of others
feeling or showing sympathy

Examples

She gave a compassionate response when she heard about his troubles.
Volunteers at the shelter are caring and compassionate toward the animals.
His compassionate nature made him beloved by the community.

Synset Examples

heard the soft and compassionate voices of women

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sympathetic - showing understanding and care for someone else's suffering
  • Antonym: merciless - showing no compassion or mercy

Etymology

From Latin 'compassionatus', past participle of 'compati' (to sympathize with).
