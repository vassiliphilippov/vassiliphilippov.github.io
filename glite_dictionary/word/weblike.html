weblike

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛbˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɛb.laɪk / (BrE)
Having open spaces or a structure that resembles a web.
resembling a web in structure

Examples

Delicate threads formed a weblike pattern in the spider's nest.
The artist painted a weblike design that covered the entire ceiling.
Sunlight filtered through the weblike branches of the old willow tree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: webby - having or resembling the structure of a web
