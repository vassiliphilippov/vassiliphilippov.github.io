unselfishly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈsɛl.fɪʃ.li / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛl.fɪʃ.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows concern for others and not for oneself; selflessly.
in a selfless manner

Examples

She unselfishly volunteered her time to help the community.
He unselfishly shared his notes with his classmates before the exam.
The doctor unselfishly devoted many hours to caring for her patients.

Synset Examples

he acted unselfishly when he helped her get the promotion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: selflessly - in a way that shows concern for others over oneself
  • Antonym: selfishly - in a way that cares only for oneself
