30th

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɝːtiəθ / (AmE), / ˈθəːtiəθ / (BrE)
Coming next after the twenty-ninth in position; being number thirty in a sequence.
ordinal number: number thirty in a sequence

Examples

This is her 30th birthday celebration with the family.
He finished in 30th place out of a hundred runners.
The company was founded on the 30th of January.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thirtieth - Numeral or ordinal form meaning 'number thirty' in a sequence
