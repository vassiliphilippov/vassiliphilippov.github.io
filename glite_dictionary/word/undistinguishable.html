undistinguishable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəb(ə)l / (BrE)
not able to be distinguished or differentiated from something else; impossible to tell apart
impossible to tell apart

Examples

The difference between the fake and the original was almost undistinguishable to the untrained eye.
For years, the artist's copies remained undistinguishable from the authentic works.
In the fog, the outlines of the houses became completely undistinguishable.

Synset Examples

the two specimens are actually different from each other but the differences are almost indistinguishable
the twins were indistinguishable
a colorless person quite indistinguishable from the colorless mass of humanity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indistinguishable - impossible to differentiate or perceive as different

Etymology

From un- (not) + distinguish + -able, following the pattern of English adjectives.
