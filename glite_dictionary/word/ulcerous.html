ulcerous

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌl.sɚ.əs / (AmE), / ˈʌl.sər.əs / (BrE)
having or characterized by an ulcer or ulcers; affected by or resembling an ulcer.
having or affected by an ulcer
medical

Examples

The doctor examined the ulcerous tissue in the patient's mouth.
His leg developed an ulcerous sore that required immediate medical attention.
The ulcerous condition of her gums caused persistent discomfort and bleeding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ulcer - an open sore on the skin or mucous membrane
  • Synonym: cankerous - affected by or resembling a canker

Etymology

From Latin 'ulcerosus', from 'ulcus' meaning 'sore, ulcer'.
