nowness

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaʊ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈnaʊ.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being present or existing now; presentness
the quality of being present; presentness

Examples

The overwhelming nowness of the experience made everything else fade away.
Artists often try to capture the feeling of nowness in their work.
Her writing embraces the nowness of modern life, focusing on the present moment.

Synset Examples

a study of the pastness of the present and … of the presentness of the past

Related Terms

  • Synonym: presentness - the quality of being present; immediacy

Etymology

From 'now' + '-ness', denoting the state or quality of being now.
