milkwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlk.wɔrt / (AmE), / ˈmɪlk.wɜːt / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Polygala, typically with small, colorful flowers, found in various regions worldwide
plant of the Polygala genus

Examples

The field was dotted with delicate milkwort blossoms of purple and white.
Certain species of milkwort are known for their medicinal properties.
We found a cluster of milkwort growing among the heather on the hillside.

Etymology

From 'milk' and 'wort' (Old English for plant or herb), believed to increase milk production in cattle.
