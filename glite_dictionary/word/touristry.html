touristry

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʊrɪstri / (AmE), / ˈtʊərɪstri / (BrE)
the business or activity of providing services and facilities for tourists
the business of serving tourists

Examples

The rapid growth of touristry has transformed the local economy and created new job opportunities.
Some critics argue that excessive touristry can damage the authentic culture of a region.
Government policies encouraged the development of touristry in rural areas to boost income and infrastructure.

Synset Examples

Tourism is a major business in Bermuda

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tourism - the business or practice of traveling for pleasure and the services connected with it

Etymology

From 'tourist' + '-ry', denoting activities related to tourists.
