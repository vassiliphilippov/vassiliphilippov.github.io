column

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːləm / (AmE), / ˈkɒləm / (BrE)
a tubular or pillar-like supporting structure in the body
tubular body structure
medical

Examples

The vertebral column protects the spinal cord and supports the body.
The doctor discovered a fracture in the cervical column.
Damage to the anterior column of the spinal cord can affect motor function.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pillar - a tall vertical structure used to support a building or as a monument

Etymology

From Latin columna (pillar).
