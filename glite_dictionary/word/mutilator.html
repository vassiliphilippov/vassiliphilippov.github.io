mutilator

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːtɪˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈmjuːtɪleɪtə / (BrE)
a person who mutilates, destroys, disfigures, or cripples, especially by causing serious bodily harm
person who mutilates or disfigures

Examples

The police arrested the mutilator responsible for a string of attacks on local statues.
Historians debated whether the artwork was damaged by a careless restorer or a deliberate mutilator.
The farm lost several animals to an unknown mutilator last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defacer - a person who damages or spoils something, especially its appearance
  • Synonym: vandal - a person who deliberately destroys or damages property

Etymology

From Latin 'mutilare' (to maim or mutilate).
