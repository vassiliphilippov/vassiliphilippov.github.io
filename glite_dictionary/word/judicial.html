judicial

1. ADJ

/ dʒuˈdɪʃəl / (AmE), / dʒuˈdɪʃəl / (BrE)
relating to the administration of justice, courts of law, or judges
relating to courts, justice, or judges

Examples

The judicial system plays a crucial role in upholding the law.
She has a keen interest in judicial processes and courtroom procedures.
An independent judicial branch is essential for any democracy.

Synset Examples

judicial system

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legal - relating to the law; allowed by law
  • Antonym: legislative - relating to the making of laws

Etymology

From Latin 'judicialis', from 'judicium' (judgement, court of law).
