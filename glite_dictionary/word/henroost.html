henroost

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛnˌruːst / (AmE), / ˈhɛnˌruːst / (BrE)
a perch or structure where hens sleep or rest at night
perch or shelter for hens at night

Examples

The farmer built a new henroost to keep the chickens safe from foxes.
Every evening, the hens return to their henroost as the sun sets.
Cleaning the henroost is an important part of keeping healthy poultry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken coop - a shed or enclosure for keeping chickens
  • Synonym: roost - a place where birds regularly settle to rest at night
