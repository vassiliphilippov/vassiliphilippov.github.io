comparing

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / kəmˈpeərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of examining similarities between things or people
the act of examining similarities

Examples

The comparing of results from different studies revealed several important patterns.
In mathematics, comparing fractions helps students develop number sense and reasoning skills.
Their comparing of prices before making a purchase saved them quite a bit of money.

Synset Examples

they made a comparison of noise levels

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comparison - the act of comparing two or more things

Etymology

From Latin comparare (to pair, match) via Old French comparer.
