jampot

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒæmˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈdʒam.pɒt / (BrE)
a small jar used for holding jam, jellies, or preserves
jar for jam or preserves
british

Examples

She put the homemade strawberry preserve into a sparkling clean jampot.
There were several empty jampots lined up on the windowsill, waiting to be filled.
He reached for the jampot and smeared a thick layer of raspberry jam onto his toast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jam jar - a jar for storing jam or preserves

Etymology

Formed from 'jam' + 'pot', primarily used in British English.
