Pieridae

1. NOUN

/ ˌpaɪəˈrɪdiː / (AmE), / ˌpɪəˈrɪdɪˌiː / (BrE)
A family of butterflies that includes cabbage butterflies and sulphur butterflies.
family of butterflies (cabbages, sulphurs)

Examples

The Pieridae are known for their brightly colored wings and widespread distribution.
Many gardeners are familiar with the cabbage white butterfly, a member of the Pieridae family.
Researchers studied the migration patterns of Pieridae to understand their seasonal movements.

Etymology

From New Latin 'Pieridae', named after the type genus 'Pieris'.
