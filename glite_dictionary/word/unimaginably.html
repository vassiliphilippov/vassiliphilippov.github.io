unimaginably

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnɪˈmædʒɪnəbli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈmadʒɪnəbli / (BrE)
To a degree or extent that cannot be imagined or conceived.
to an unimaginable extent

Examples

The cost of the project became unimaginably high as unexpected expenses piled up.
She was unimaginably happy when she received the good news about her family.
The universe is unimaginably vast, stretching far beyond what we can comprehend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incredibly - to a great or extraordinary degree
  • Synonym: impossibly - to an impossible or inconceivable degree
