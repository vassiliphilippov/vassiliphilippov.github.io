pandiculation

1. NOUN

/ ˌpændɪkjuːˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpændɪkjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of yawning and stretching, especially when waking up from sleep
yawning and stretching on waking

Examples

After a long nap, her instinctive pandiculation signaled that she was ready to start the day.
The kitten greeted the morning with an adorable pandiculation before jumping off the bed.
Frequent pandiculation helps relieve muscle stiffness on waking from sleep.

Etymology

From Latin pandiculatio, from pandiculāri (to stretch oneself).
