Hygrotrama

1. NOUN

/ haɪɡroʊˈtræmə / (AmE), / haɪɡrəˈtrɑːmə / (BrE)
A genus of fungi in the family Hygrophoraceae.
genus of fungi in Hygrophoraceae

Examples

Species in the genus Hygrotrama are found in various temperate forests around the world.
The identification of Hygrotrama requires microscopic examination of the spores.
Mycologists recently discovered a new species within Hygrotrama.

Etymology

From Greek 'hygro-' (wet, moist) and 'trama' (that which is woven, web), referring to the internal tissue structure of mushrooms.
