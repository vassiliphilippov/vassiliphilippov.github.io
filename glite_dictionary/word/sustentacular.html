sustentacular

1. ADJ

/ ˌsʌstən'tækjələr / (AmE), / ˌsʌstɛnˈtækjʊlə / (BrE)
serving to support or sustain a structure or function, especially used of certain cells in biological tissues
supporting or sustaining (biology, medicine)
medical

Examples

The sustentacular cells in the olfactory epithelium provide metabolic support for neurons.
Some endocrine glands rely on sustentacular tissue to maintain their structure.
Research indicates that sustentacular elements play a crucial role in tissue repair.

Synset Examples

sustentacular cells

Etymology

From Latin sustentaculum (support), from sustentare (to sustain).
