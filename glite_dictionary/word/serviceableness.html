serviceableness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝː.vɪ.sə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.vɪ.sə.bəl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being useful, helpful, or able to provide good service
usefulness; ability to provide good service

Examples

The serviceableness of this old tool is remarkable despite its age.
We need to assess the serviceableness of the equipment before purchase.
Her serviceableness to the community earned her widespread respect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: usefulness - the quality of being useful
  • Synonym: serviceable - able to provide useful service; fit for use
  • Antonym: uselessness - the quality of being of no use

Etymology

From 'serviceable' + '-ness', where 'serviceable' is from Old French 'serviciable'.
