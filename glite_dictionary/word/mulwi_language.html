Mulwi language

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌlwi læŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmʌlwi læŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Chadic language spoken south of Lake Chad, especially in the Far North Region of Cameroon, in the Mayo-Danay division, Maga subdivision.
a Chadic language of Cameroon

Examples

The Mulwi language is spoken by several thousand people in northern Cameroon.
Linguists have studied the Mulwi language to document its grammar and vocabulary.
Efforts are being made to preserve the Mulwi language as it faces the threat of extinction.
