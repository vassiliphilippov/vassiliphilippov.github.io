matched

1. ADJ

/ mæʧt / (AmE), / mæʧt / (BrE)
having an opponent or competitor of equal ability or strength; evenly balanced in competition or contest
evenly matched in competition

Examples

The two boxers were perfectly matched, making for an exciting fight.
The chess tournament featured several matched pairs who competed fiercely.
The teams were so evenly matched that the game ended in a tie.

Synset Examples

matched teams

Related Terms

  • Antonym: uneven - not evenly balanced or matched in competition
  • Synonym: equal - having the same ability, quantity, or value

2. ADJ

/ mætʃt / (AmE), / mætʃt / (BrE)
(of things or people) going well together or having harmonizing qualities.
having harmonizing qualities

Examples

The matched curtains and cushions gave the living room a cohesive look.
They are a perfectly matched couple, sharing many interests and values.
The jeweler displayed a set of matched pearls in the window.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unmatched - not matched; not going well together
  • Synonym: coordinated - well-arranged or harmonized

Etymology

From the verb 'match', itself from Middle English 'macchen', meaning 'to mate or fit together'.
