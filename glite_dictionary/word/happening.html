happening

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæpənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhapənɪŋ / (BrE)
an event or occurrence, especially one that is considered significant or unusual
noteworthy event or occurrence

Examples

The strange happening last night left everyone at the party talking.
There was an exciting happening in the city center this weekend.
Reporters rushed to cover every major happening during the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: event - something that happens, especially an important occurrence
  • Synonym: incident - a particular occurrence, especially one of minor importance

Etymology

From the verb 'happen' + -ing, originally used in the 20th century to describe spontaneous events in art and culture.
