nailbrush

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪlˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈneɪlˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)
a small stiff-bristled brush used for cleaning the fingernails
brush for cleaning fingernails

Examples

He keeps a nailbrush next to the sink to scrub his hands after gardening.
After working with clay, she used a nailbrush to remove the dirt from under her nails.
The hotel bathroom provided a clean nailbrush and soap for guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brush - tool with bristles used for cleaning or painting

Etymology

From 'nail' (fingernail) + 'brush'.
