arcella

1. NOUN

/ ɑːrˈsɛl.ə / (AmE), / ɑːˈsɛl.ə / (BrE)
A genus of amoeba-like protozoans characterized by a chitinous shell resembling an umbrella.
genus of amoeba-like protozoans

Examples

The freshwater pond was teeming with arcella observed under the microscope.
Scientists noted the unique chitinous shell of arcella during their research on protozoans.
Arcella species play an important role in aquatic ecosystems as part of the microbial community.

Etymology

From New Latin Arcella, the genus name, possibly from Latin 'arcula' (little chest or box).
