Arcella

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈsɛlə / (AmE), / ɑːˈsɛlə / (BrE)
a genus of single-celled amoeboid protozoans with a characteristic dome-shaped shell, belonging to the family Arcellidae
a genus of shelled amoeboid protozoans

Examples

Scientists discovered several Arcella specimens in the freshwater pond sediment.
The characteristic shell of Arcella distinguishes it from other amoeboid genera.
Students observed the movement of Arcella under the microscope during biology class.

Etymology

From New Latin Arcella, the genus name assigned by taxonomists.
