toolmaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːlˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈtuːlˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who is skilled in making or repairing tools, especially as a trade or profession
someone skilled at making tools

Examples

After years of training, he became a respected toolmaker at the factory.
The company hired a toolmaker to create custom parts for their machinery.
As a skilled toolmaker, she crafted precision instruments for the automotive industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: machinist - a person who operates machine tools
