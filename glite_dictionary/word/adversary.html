adversary

1. NOUN

/ ˈæd.vɚˌsɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈæd.və.sər.i / (BrE)
a person, group, or force that opposes or fights against another; an opponent or enemy
opponent or enemy

Examples

The politician viewed her rival as a formidable adversary in the upcoming election.
In the final match, the team faced their longtime adversary and played with determination.
Every hero needs a strong adversary to truly test their abilities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opponent - a person who competes or fights against another
  • Synonym: enemy - a person who is actively opposed or hostile
  • Antonym: ally - a person or group that cooperates with or helps another

Etymology

From Latin adversarius (opponent), from adversus (against).
