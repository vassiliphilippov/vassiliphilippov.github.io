dictatorially

1. ADV

/ ˌdɪkˌteɪˈtɔriəli / (AmE), / ˌdɪkˌteɪˈtɔːriəli / (BrE)
In an overbearingly domineering or autocratic manner; like a dictator.
in a domineering, autocratic manner

Examples

The CEO ran the company dictatorially, leaving no room for employees' opinions.
She spoke dictatorially, shutting down all opposing views during the meeting.
The new principal implemented the rules dictatorially, without consulting the staff.

Synset Examples

this manager acts dictatorially toward his colleagues

Related Terms

  • Synonym: autocratically - in a domineering, self-governing manner
  • Antonym: democratically - in a way that involves equal participation by all

Etymology

From 'dictatorial' + '-ly', based on Latin 'dictator' (speaker, magistrate).
