rya

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːə / (AmE), / ˈrʌɪə / (BrE)
a Scandinavian handwoven shag rug with a thick pile, traditionally made in Sweden
a Swedish shag rug

Examples

The antique rya in the living room added warmth and texture to the space.
She bought a handwoven rya from a Swedish craft market.
Ryas are celebrated for their vibrant patterns and plush, thick pile.

Etymology

From Swedish 'rya', which refers to a type of shaggy bedcover or rug.
