sweet potato

1. NOUN

/ swit pəˈteɪtoʊ / (AmE), / swiːt pəˈteɪtəʊ / (BrE)
an egg-shaped terracotta wind instrument with a mouthpiece and finger holes; also known as an ocarina.
egg-shaped wind instrument (ocarina)

Examples

He learned to play the sweet potato in just a few months.
The band featured a unique solo on the sweet potato during the concert.
Collectors often seek vintage ceramic sweet potato instruments from Italy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ocarina - egg-shaped wind instrument with a mouthpiece and finger holes
