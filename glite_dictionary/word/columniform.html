columniform

1. ADJ

/ kəˈlʌm.əˌfɔrm / (AmE), / kəˈlʌm.ɪ.fɔːm / (BrE)
having the form or shape of a column; column-shaped.
having the form of a column

Examples

The architect chose columniform pillars to create a sense of grandeur in the main hall.
Some cacti display a distinctly columniform growth pattern, rising straight and narrow from the ground.
The ancient stone monument was columniform, towering above the landscape in an elegant, upright shape.

Synset Examples

trees with columniform trunks
columnar forms
a columnlike tree trunk

Related Terms

  • Synonym: columnar - having the shape of a column

Etymology

From Latin 'columna' (column) + '-form' (having the form of).
