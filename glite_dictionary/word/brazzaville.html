Brazzaville

1. PROPN

/ ˈbræzəˌvɪl / (AmE), / ˈbræzəˌvɪl / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of the Republic of the Congo, located on the Congo River
capital of the Republic of the Congo

Examples

The conference will be held in Brazzaville next summer.
Many international embassies are located in Brazzaville.
He traveled from Kinshasa to Brazzaville by ferry across the river.

Etymology

Named after Italian-French explorer Pierre Savorgnan de Brazza.
