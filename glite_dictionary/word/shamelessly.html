shamelessly

1. ADV

/ ˈʃeɪmləsli / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪmləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no shame or embarrassment.
without shame or embarrassment

Examples

She shamelessly lied to get out of trouble.
The company shamelessly promoted its products despite the controversy.
He shamelessly took all the credit for the team's success.

Synset Examples

he unashamedly abandoned the project when he realized he would not gain from it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unashamedly - in a way that shows no shame or embarrassment

Etymology

From 'shameless' + '-ly', forming the adverb.
