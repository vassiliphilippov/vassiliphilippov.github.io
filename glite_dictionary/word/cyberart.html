cyberart

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪbərˌɑrt / (AmE), / ˈsaɪbəˌɑːt / (BrE)
art created using computer hardware and software, often involving digital techniques and technology
art produced with computers or digital technology

Examples

The museum is hosting an exhibition of cyberart featuring interactive digital installations.
She specializes in cyberart that combines animation and virtual reality.
As technology has advanced, cyberart has become an increasingly prominent part of the art world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: digital art - art created using digital technology

Etymology

Formed from 'cyber-' (relating to computers or cyberspace) and 'art'.
