silverwork

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəˌwɜːk / (BrE)
decorative or artistic objects made of silver, often involving skilled craftsmanship
decorative objects made of silver

Examples

The museum displays exquisite silverwork from the Victorian era.
She specializes in restoring antique silverwork for collectors.
The artisans are renowned for their intricate silverwork jewelry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldwork - decorative work made of gold

Etymology

From 'silver' + 'work', referring to items crafted from silver.
