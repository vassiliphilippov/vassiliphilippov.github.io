supercharged

1. ADJ

/ ˌsuːpərˈtʃɑrdʒd / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈtʃɑːdʒd / (BrE)
(of an engine or vehicle) having increased power from being fitted with a supercharger.
having increased engine power

Examples

The new sports car has a supercharged engine capable of incredible acceleration.
After installing a supercharged system, the boat's performance improved dramatically.
Many muscle cars from the 1960s were factory supercharged for extra power.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turbocharged - (of an engine) fitted with a turbocharger to boost power

Etymology

Formed from 'super-' (above, beyond) + 'charged'; referring to engines fitted with a supercharger.

2. ADJ

/ ˈsuː.pɚˌtʃɑrdʒd / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pəˌtʃɑːdʒd / (BrE)
Filled with intense or heightened emotion; emotionally charged.
filled with strong emotion

Examples

The debate became increasingly supercharged as both sides refused to back down.
There was a supercharged atmosphere in the stadium before the final match.
Her supercharged speech inspired everyone in the audience.

Synset Examples

an atmosphere charged with excitement
an emotionally charged speech

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charged - filled with emotion
