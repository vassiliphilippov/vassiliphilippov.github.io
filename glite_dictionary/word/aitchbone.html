aitchbone

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪtʃˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈeɪtʃbəʊn / (BrE)
a cut of beef from the rump that includes the H-shaped hip bone
beef cut from the rump with hip bone

Examples

The butcher recommended the aitchbone for our Sunday roast.
After roasting the aitchbone, she carved thick slices for dinner.
Some traditional recipes call for a boiled aitchbone served with vegetables.

Etymology

From 'aitch', variant of 'h', referring to the shape of the bone.
