perilously

1. ADV

/ ˈpɛr.əl.əs.li / (AmE), / ˈpɛr.ɪl.əs.li / (BrE)
In a way that involves or exposes one to danger; in a dangerous manner.
in a dangerous manner

Examples

The climber hung perilously from the edge of the cliff, searching for a foothold.
Icicles dangled perilously above the front door after the frost.
She perilously crossed the busy street during rush hour traffic.

Synset Examples

he came dangerously close to falling off the ledge

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dangerously - in a way that is likely to cause harm or injury

Etymology

From 'perilous' + -ly; ultimately from Latin 'periculosus' (dangerous).
