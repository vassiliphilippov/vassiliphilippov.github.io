puppyish

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʌpiɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpʌpiɪʃ / (BrE)
Having the characteristics of a puppy, especially playfulness, enthusiasm, or innocence.
resembling a puppy, especially in behavior

Examples

She gave him a puppyish grin that melted everyone's heart.
His puppyish enthusiasm was infectious and brought energy to the room.
The new recruit's puppyish eagerness reminded me of my own first day.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: puppy - a young dog
  • Synonym: childish - suitable for or characteristic of a child

Etymology

From 'puppy' + '-ish', forming adjectives meaning 'like' or 'characteristic of'.
