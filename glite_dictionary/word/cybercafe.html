cybercafe

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪbərˌkæfeɪ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪbəkæfeɪ / (BrE)
A café where customers can use computers with internet access while having food or drinks.
café offering computers with internet access

Examples

We found a cybercafe near the train station so we could check our emails.
While traveling abroad, she often works from a local cybercafe.
The cybercafe was busy with students doing research and playing online games.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: internet café - A cafe offering internet access to patrons

Etymology

Blend of "cyber-" (relating to computers or the internet) and "café" (from French).
