millivoltmeter

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪliˈvoʊltˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˌmɪlɪˈvɒltˌmiːtə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure electrical voltage in millivolts
instrument measuring voltage in millivolts

Examples

The technician used a millivoltmeter to check the weak signals in the circuit.
A high-quality millivoltmeter is essential for measuring tiny electrical voltages accurately.
Before making adjustments, she double-checked the readings with a millivoltmeter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: voltmeter - instrument for measuring electric potential difference in volts

Etymology

From milli- (thousandth) + volt (after Alessandro Volta) + -meter (measuring device).
