thingumabob

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɪŋəməˌbɑb / (AmE), / ˈθɪŋəməˌbɒb / (BrE)
an object whose name you do not know or have forgotten
object with unknown or forgotten name
informal

Examples

Can you pass me that thingumabob next to the toolbox?
I used a thingumabob to fix the handle, whatever it's called.
Whenever I lose the remote, I always end up calling it the thingumabob.

Synset Examples

she eased the ball-shaped doodad back into its socket
there may be some great new gizmo around the corner that you will want to use

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thingamajig - an object whose name is unknown or forgotten
  • Synonym: whatchamacallit - an object whose name you cannot remember

Etymology

Blend of 'thing' and an arbitrary ending, modeled on patterns like 'thingamajig.'
