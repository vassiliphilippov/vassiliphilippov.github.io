copasetic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkoʊpəˈsɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌkəʊpəˈsɛtɪk / (BrE)
completely satisfactory; in excellent order or condition.
completely satisfactory; fine
informal

Examples

After checking all the details, she confirmed that everything was copasetic.
The project ran smoothly, and the team assured the client that all was copasetic.
I'm glad to hear your new apartment is both comfortable and copasetic.

Synset Examples

his smile said that everything was copacetic
You had to be a good judge of what a man was like, and the English was copacetic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: satisfactory - meeting requirements or expectations
  • Synonym: fine - in good condition or excellent
  • Antonym: unsatisfactory - not good enough; not meeting requirements

Etymology

Of uncertain origin; possibly from African-American Vernacular English or from Louisiana French.
