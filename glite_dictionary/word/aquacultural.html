aquacultural

1. ADJ

/ ˌækwəˈkʌltʃərəl / (AmE), / ˌakwəˈkʌltʃərəl / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with aquaculture, the cultivation of aquatic plants or animals for food or other products.
relating to aquaculture

Examples

The government invested in aquacultural research to improve fish farming techniques.
Innovative aquacultural systems can help meet the growing demand for seafood.
Many universities now offer courses in aquacultural sciences.

Synset Examples

aquacultural methods
hydroponic lettuce

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aquaculture - the cultivation of aquatic organisms such as fish or plants

Etymology

Formed from 'aquaculture' + '-al', following the pattern of similar terms like 'agricultural'. 'Aquaculture' derives from Latin 'aqua' (water) and 'culture' (cultivation).
