to yarn-dye

1. VERB

/ jɑrn daɪ / (AmE), / jɑːn daɪ / (BrE)
(of fabric) to dye the fibers before they are spun into yarn.
to dye fibers before spinning

Examples

Many manufacturers yarn-dye fabrics to achieve vibrant, long-lasting colors.
To create checks and stripes, it is common to yarn-dye the threads before weaving.
She prefers to yarn-dye cotton to ensure the colors do not fade with washing.
