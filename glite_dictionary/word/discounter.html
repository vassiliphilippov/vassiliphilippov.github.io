discounter

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪskaʊntər / (AmE), / ˈdɪskaʊntə / (BrE)
a retail business or store that sells goods at prices lower than the usual market rate
store selling goods at reduced prices

Examples

The new discounter in town offers groceries at unbeatable prices.
Many shoppers prefer to buy electronics from a discounter to save money.
Several major discounters have expanded their product lines to include clothing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discount store - a store that sells goods at reduced prices

Etymology

From 'discount' + '-er', denoting an agent noun (one who discounts).
