balagan

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɑːləˈɡɑːn / (AmE), / ˌbæləˈɡæn / (BrE)
A situation marked by chaos, confusion, or disorder; a fiasco.
chaotic or confused situation

Examples

After the new software update, the office IT systems turned into a complete balagan.
Negotiations quickly descended into a balagan as everyone spoke over each other.
Trying to organize the family reunion became a balagan with so many conflicting schedules.

Synset Examples

it was utter and complete balagan!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fiasco - a complete failure, especially in a ludicrous or humiliating way
  • Synonym: chaos - complete disorder or confusion

Etymology

From Modern Hebrew בָּלָגָן (balagan), itself a loan from Russian балаган (balagán, meaning 'mess, shambles').
