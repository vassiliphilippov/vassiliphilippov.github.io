bridoon

1. NOUN

/ brɪˈduːn / (AmE), / brɪˈduːn / (BrE)
a type of bit, similar to a snaffle, used with a separate curb in a double bridle for horses
a type of horse bit used with a curb

Examples

The double bridle consists of a bridoon and a curb bit used together.
She carefully adjusted the bridoon before leading the horse out of the stable.
A properly fitted bridoon ensures the horse's comfort during dressage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snaffle - a simple type of bit used in horse bridles
  • Degree Higher: curb bit - a type of bit with a lever action used in horse bridles

Etymology

From French bridon (small bridle).
