highflier

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪˌflaɪər / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌflaɪə / (BrE)
a person who has or is likely to achieve a great deal due to exceptional ability and ambition
person of great ability and ambition

Examples

Helen is a real highflier in the corporate world, already managing a team at 28.
Universities compete to attract highfliers from around the globe.
Though young, he quickly earned a reputation as a highflier in the tech industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: achiever - a person who achieves great things
  • Antonym: underachiever - a person who does not reach their potential

Etymology

Formed from 'high' + 'flier', metaphorically referring to someone who soars high like a bird.
