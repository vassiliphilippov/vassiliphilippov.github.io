intimal

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪn.tə.məl / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tɪ.məl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring in the intima, the innermost layer of a blood vessel or organ
relating to the innermost layer of a vessel
medical

Examples

An intimal tear can lead to serious vascular complications if left untreated.
The surgeon observed thickening of the intimal layer during the procedure.
Researchers are studying intimal hyperplasia following artery injury.

Etymology

From Latin 'intimus' meaning 'innermost'.
