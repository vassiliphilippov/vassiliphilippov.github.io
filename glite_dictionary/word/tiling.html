tiling

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of covering a surface with tiles
application of tiles to a surface

Examples

The kitchen was transformed after the new tiling was installed.
He specializes in bathroom tiling and floor installations.
Poor tiling can lead to water leakage and damage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tile - a flat, thin piece used for covering surfaces

Etymology

From 'tile' + '-ing', a suffix forming nouns indicating an action or process.
