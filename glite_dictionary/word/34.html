34

1. NUM

/ ˈθɝːti ˈfɔr, ˈθɝːti ˈfoʊr / (AmE), / ˈθɜːti ˈfɔː / (BrE)
The number equivalent to four more than thirty; a cardinal number.
the number thirty-four

Examples

She will turn 34 years old next month.
There are 34 students in the science class this year.
The winning lottery number was 34.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirty-three - the number one less than thirty-four
  • Degree Higher: thirty-five - the number one more than thirty-four
