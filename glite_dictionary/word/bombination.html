bombination

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɑːm.bəˈneɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌbɒm.bɪˈneɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a buzzing or humming sound, especially one caused by rapid vibration
buzzing or humming sound

Examples

The bombination of bees surrounded the blooming garden.
The quiet bombination of machinery filled the workshop late into the night.
He listened to the bombination of summer insects outside his window.

Synset Examples

the buzz of a bumble bee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buzz - a low, continuous humming or murmuring sound
  • Synonym: hum - a low, steady continuous sound

Etymology

From Latin bombinatio (buzzing, humming).
