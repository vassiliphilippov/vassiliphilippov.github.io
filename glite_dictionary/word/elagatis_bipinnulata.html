Elagatis bipinnulata

1. NOUN

/ ɛˈlɑːɡətɪs baɪˌpɪnjəˈleɪtə / (AmE), / ɛˈlæɡətɪs baɪˌpɪnjʊˈlɑːtə / (BrE)
a species of streamlined, cigar-shaped jack fish found in warm ocean waters, valued as a game fish
a species of jack fish; game fish

Examples

The Elagatis bipinnulata is prized among anglers for its strength and speed.
We spotted a school of Elagatis bipinnulata while diving off the coast.
Restaurants in the region often serve dishes featuring the fresh Elagatis bipinnulata.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rainbow runner - common name for Elagatis bipinnulata

Etymology

From scientific Latin; genus name Elagatis, species epithet bipinnulata referring to its fin structure.
