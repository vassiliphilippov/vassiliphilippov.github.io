aircraftswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌkræftsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈeəˌkrɑːftsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female member holding a noncommissioned rank in the British Royal Air Force
female RAF noncommissioned officer
british

Examples

She was promoted to aircraftswoman after completing her training in the Royal Air Force.
An aircraftswoman offered her assistance during the pre-flight checks.
The ceremony honored every aircraftswoman who served in the British RAF.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aircraftsman - a noncommissioned rank in the British Royal Air Force

Etymology

Formed from 'aircraft' and 'woman', paralleling 'aircraftsman'.
