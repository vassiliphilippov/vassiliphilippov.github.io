pedagogical

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɛdəˈɡɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpɛdəˈɡɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the theory or practice of teaching and education
relating to teaching or education

Examples

The teacher's pedagogical approach encouraged active student participation in class discussions.
Her research explores the pedagogical methods used in bilingual education.
We attended a seminar about new pedagogical theories in mathematics.

Synset Examples

pedagogical significance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: educational - relating to the provision of education
  • Synonym: didactic - intended to teach or instruct, especially in having a moral instruction as an ulterior motive

Etymology

From Greek paidagōgikos (pertaining to teaching), from paidagōgos (teacher).
