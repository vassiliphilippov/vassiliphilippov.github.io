smoothy

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmuːði / (AmE), / ˈsmuːði / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, who behaves in a confident and charming, often ingratiating manner
confident and charming person

Examples

Everyone said Mark was a smoothy who could charm his way into any crowd.
The party was full of smoothies trying to impress the host.
She didn't trust such a smoothy with important decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoothie - a person with a suave and ingratiating manner

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'smoothie', from 'smooth' (even, suave) + -y (diminutive suffix).
