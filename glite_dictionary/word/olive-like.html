olive-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑlɪv laɪk / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of an olive, especially in appearance, size, or shape
resembling an olive

Examples

The plant produces olive-like fruits that are small and green.
Her dress was a deep, olive-like shade that suited her complexion.
The insect's olive-like body camouflaged well among the tree leaves.
