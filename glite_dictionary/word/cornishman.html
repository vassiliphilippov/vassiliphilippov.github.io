Cornishman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrnɪʃˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnɪʃmən / (BrE)
a man who is a native or inhabitant of Cornwall, a county in southwestern England.
man from Cornwall, England

Examples

The old Cornishman spoke proudly of his heritage and traditions.
As a Cornishman, he grew up near the rugged coastline of southwestern England.
The book tells the story of a young Cornishman who leaves his village to seek his fortune.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cornishwoman - a woman who is a native or inhabitant of Cornwall

Etymology

From Cornish (referring to Cornwall) + man.
