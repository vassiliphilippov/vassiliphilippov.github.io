cannibalic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkænəˈbælɪk / (AmE), / ˌkænɪˈbælɪk / (BrE)
characteristic of a cannibal; extremely savage or barbarous
relating to cannibals or extreme savagery

Examples

The invaders' cannibalic cruelty shocked the civilized world.
Some horror films depict cannibalic rituals to create a sense of terror.
His cannibalic rage made him a terrifying opponent in battle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cannibalistic - relating to or characteristic of cannibals

Etymology

From 'cannibal' + '-ic' (forming adjectives meaning 'of or pertaining to').
