clofibrate

1. NOUN

/ kloʊˈfaɪbreɪt / (AmE), / kləʊˈfɪbreɪt / (BrE)
a drug used to lower lipid levels in the blood and to treat certain cardiovascular diseases
drug for lowering blood lipids
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed clofibrate to help reduce his cholesterol levels.
Patients taking clofibrate should have their liver function monitored regularly.
