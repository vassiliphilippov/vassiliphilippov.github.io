dishwashing

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪʃˌwɑʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɪʃˌwɒʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of cleaning dishes, utensils, and cookware after use
the act of washing dishes

Examples

Dishwashing is my least favorite household chore.
She listened to music while dishwashing after dinner.
The restaurant hired someone for evening dishwashing duties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to wash up - to clean dishes after a meal

Etymology

From 'dish' + 'washing', combining the words for the items being cleaned and the act.
