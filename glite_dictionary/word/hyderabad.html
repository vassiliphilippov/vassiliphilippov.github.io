Hyderabad

1. PROPN

/ ˌhaɪ.dɹəˈbɑːd / (AmE), / ˌhaɪ.dəˈbɑːd / (BrE)
A city in southern Pakistan, located on the Indus River, known for its historical significance and cultural heritage.
city in southern Pakistan

Examples

The heat in Hyderabad can be intense during the summer months.
Many traditional Sindhi handicrafts are produced in Hyderabad.
The old fort in Hyderabad is a popular tourist attraction.

2. PROPN

/ ˌhaɪdɹəˈbɑːd / (AmE), / ˌhaɪdɹəˈbɑːd / (BrE)
a major city in south-central India, the capital of Telangana state, known for its historical monuments and technology industry
city in south-central India

Examples

Hyderabad is famous for its rich history, vibrant culture, and delicious cuisine.
The IT industry has been booming in Hyderabad for the past two decades.
Their family moved to Hyderabad when his father accepted a new job there.

Etymology

From Persian 'Haydar' (lion) + 'ābād' (city), meaning 'city of Haydar.'
