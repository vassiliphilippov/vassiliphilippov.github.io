encircling

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈsɜrklɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɜːklɪŋ / (BrE)
surrounding or forming a circle around something; enclosing on all sides.
surrounding; enclosing all around

Examples

He wrapped his encircling arms gently around the child.
The castle was protected by an encircling moat.
Dense fog formed an encircling blanket around the mountains.

Synset Examples

his encircling arms
the room's skirting board needs painting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surrounding - enclosing or encircling on all sides

Etymology

From the verb 'encircle' (en- + circle), meaning 'to form a circle around'.
