inapplicable

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.əˈplɪk.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.əˈplɪk.ə.bəl / (BrE)
not relevant or suitable to be applied in a particular case or situation
not relevant or suitable

Examples

Certain tax laws are inapplicable to small businesses.
The warranty is inapplicable if the product is damaged by misuse.
Her medical training proved inapplicable during the business negotiation.

Synset Examples

rules inapplicable to day students

Related Terms

  • Antonym: applicable - relevant or suitable to be applied

Etymology

From in- (not) + applicable, ultimately from Latin applicābilis.
