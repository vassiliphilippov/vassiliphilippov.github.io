harmonically

1. ADV

/ hɑːrˈmɑːnɪkli / (AmE), / hɑːˈmɒnɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or concerns harmony, especially in music or acoustics.
in relation to harmony (music, acoustics)

Examples

The piece was harmonically complex, blending unexpected chords throughout the melody.
She analyzed the song harmonically to understand its structure better.
The choir's performance was harmonically rich and pleasing to the ear.

Synset Examples

harmonically interesting piece

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harmonic - relating to or characterized by harmony

Etymology

From harmony + -ically, ultimately from Greek harmonia (joining, agreement).
