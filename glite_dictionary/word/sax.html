sax

1. NOUN

/ sæks / (AmE), / sæks / (BrE)
an informal term for saxophone, a single-reed woodwind instrument with a conical bore
informal term for saxophone
informal

Examples

He plays a mean sax in the jazz band downtown.
The solo featured an incredible sax melody that wowed the audience.
She picked up her sax and started practicing for tomorrow’s concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saxophone - a musical instrument in the woodwind family

Etymology

Shortened from saxophone, named after Adolphe Sax, its inventor.
