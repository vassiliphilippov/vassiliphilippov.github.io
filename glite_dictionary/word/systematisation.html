systematisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪstəmətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪstəmətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
The process of organizing something in a systematic way, according to a specific plan or rationale.
methodical organization process

Examples

The systematisation of records made information retrieval much faster.
Her role involved the systematisation of workflow procedures across departments.
A clear systematisation of the data is essential before analysis can begin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: systematization - Alternative spelling, chiefly American

Etymology

From French *systématisation*, from Greek *systema* (system).
