scared

1. ADJ

/ skɛrd / (AmE), / skɛəd / (BrE)
feeling afraid or frightened; fearful because of danger, pain, or uncertainty.
afraid; feeling fear

Examples

She was scared to walk home alone after dark.
The thunderstorm made the dog scared and he hid under the bed.
Even though he was scared, he decided to confront his fears.

Synset Examples

the frightened child cowered in the corner
too shocked and scared to move

Related Terms

  • Synonym: afraid - feeling fear or worry about something that may happen
  • Antonym: fearless - without fear; not afraid
