softheaded

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɔftˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɒfthɛdɪd / (BrE)
foolish or lacking in sound judgment; easily swayed or lacking good sense.
foolish; lacking sound judgment

Examples

Only a softheaded person would invest in such a risky venture without research.
His softheaded ideas often led to trouble for the whole team.
She called the plan softheaded and doomed to fail from the very start.

Synset Examples

a crazy scheme
half-baked ideas
a screwball proposal without a prayer of working

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment
  • Antonym: sensible - having or showing good sense or judgment

Etymology

From 'soft' + 'headed', implying a soft or weak mind.
