gymnast

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪmˌnæst / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪmnæst / (BrE)
an athlete who is trained or skilled in performing gymnastics routines and exercises
athlete skilled in gymnastics

Examples

The young gymnast executed a flawless routine on the balance beam.
Simone Biles is celebrated as a world-class gymnast for her extraordinary skills.
Every gymnast must practice for hours each day to perfect their technique.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acrobatic - relating to acts of agility and coordination
  • Synonym: athlete - a person proficient in sports and physical exercise

Etymology

From Ancient Greek 'gymnastēs' (trainer, athlete), from 'gymnazein' (to train, exercise naked).
