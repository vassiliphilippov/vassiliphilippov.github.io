Minocin

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪnoʊˌsɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɪnəsɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for a tetracycline antibiotic (minocycline) used to treat a range of bacterial and rickettsial infections.
brand of tetracycline antibiotic
medical

Examples

Her doctor prescribed Minocin to treat her persistent acne.
The patient experienced mild side effects after starting Minocin.
If you are allergic to tetracyclines, you should avoid taking Minocin.

Related Terms
