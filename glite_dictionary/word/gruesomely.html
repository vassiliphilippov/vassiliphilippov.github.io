gruesomely

1. ADV

/ ˈɡruːsəmli / (AmE), / ˈɡruːsəmli / (BrE)
In a manner that causes horror or disgust; in a gruesome way.
in a horrifying or disgusting manner

Examples

The story was gruesomely detailed, leaving the audience unsettled.
The killer gruesomely mutilated his victims, shocking the entire town.
Some horror movies are gruesomely violent and not for the faint-hearted.

Synset Examples

he was gruesomely wounded

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gruesome - causing horror or disgust
