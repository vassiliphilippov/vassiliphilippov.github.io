lansat

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæn.sæt / (AmE), / ˈlan.sat / (BrE)
An East Indian fruit with tart, yellow, berrylike flesh, botanically known as Lansium domesticum.
East Indian tart yellow fruit

Examples

We tasted the fresh lansat while traveling through Malaysia.
The market vendor sold ripe lansat alongside mangosteens and rambutans.
After peeling the thin skin, the juicy lansat revealed its tart, translucent pulp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: langsat - another spelling for the same fruit, Lansium domesticum

Etymology

From Malay 'lansat', referring to the tree and its fruit.
