shaved

1. ADJ

/ ʃeɪvd / (AmE), / ʃeɪvd / (BrE)
Having had the hair or beard cut off close to the skin.
having hair or beard cut close to the skin

Examples

After months of growing a beard, he finally appeared shaved at the meeting.
The athlete remained shaved for hydrodynamic advantage in the pool.
A freshly shaved face often looks younger and cleaner.

Related Terms
