modishly

1. ADV

/ ˈmoʊdɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈməʊdɪʃli / (BrE)
In a fashionable or stylish way; following current trends in appearance or behavior.
in a fashionable manner

Examples

She always dresses modishly, no matter the occasion.
The restaurant was modishly decorated with sleek lines and modern colors.
He arrived at the party modishly attired in a tailored blazer and loafers.

Synset Examples

He was smartly dressed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fashionably - in a manner following current fashion
  • Antonym: outmodedly - in a manner that is out of fashion

Etymology

From 'modish' (fashionable), itself from 'mode' (style, fashion) + -ish + -ly.
