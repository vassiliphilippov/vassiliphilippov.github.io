redemptory

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈdɛmptəri / (AmE), / rɪˈdɛmptəri / (BrE)
relating to or resulting in redemption; having the quality of redeeming or saving.
relating to redemption

Examples

The novel explores the redemptory power of forgiveness through its main character.
Many religious texts focus on the redemptory aspects of suffering and sacrifice.
Her redemptory actions helped restore her reputation in the community.

Synset Examples

a redemptive theory about life

Related Terms

  • Synonym: redemptive - relating to or marked by redemption

Etymology

From Late Latin redemptorius, from Latin redemptio (a buying back, redemption).
