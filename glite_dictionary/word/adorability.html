adorability

1. NOUN

/ əˌdɔr.əˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / əˌdɔː.rəˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely attractive or charming; charm that inspires adoration
quality of being adorable or charming

Examples

The adorability of the puppies melted everyone's hearts at the shelter.
Her adorability was highlighted by her constant laughter and cheerful nature.
Many toys are designed to maximize their adorability to appeal to children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adorableness - quality of being adorable or lovable

Etymology

From 'adorable' + suffix '-ity', patterned after 'adorable'.
