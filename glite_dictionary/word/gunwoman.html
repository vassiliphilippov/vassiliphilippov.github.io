gunwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌnˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is a professional killer or criminal who uses a gun
female professional killer with a gun

Examples

The notorious gunwoman was finally apprehended after months of investigation.
Rumors spread quickly about the gunwoman who carried out the daring heist.
In the film, a skilled gunwoman is hired to eliminate the rival gang leader.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gunman - a man who uses a gun, especially as a criminal

Etymology

Formed from 'gun' + 'woman', modeled after 'gunman'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌnˌwʊm.ən / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnˌwʊm.ən / (BrE)
A woman who uses or is skilled at shooting a gun.
a woman who shoots a gun

Examples

The notorious gunwoman was finally caught after a lengthy pursuit across the state.
She trained for years to become the best gunwoman in the region's shooting competitions.
Newspapers reported that a masked gunwoman had held up the local bank.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gunman - a man who uses or is skilled at shooting a gun

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'gunman', from 'gun' + 'woman'.

WordNet ID: oewn-84169208-n
Synset POS: n
