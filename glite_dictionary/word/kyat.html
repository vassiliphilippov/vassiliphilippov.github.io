kyat

1. NOUN

/ kjɑt / (AmE), / kjæt / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Myanmar (Burma)
currency unit of Myanmar

Examples

The price of the goods was listed in both US dollars and kyat.
He exchanged his dollars for kyat at the airport currency counter.
The value of the kyat fluctuated significantly during the economic crisis.

Etymology

From Burmese ကျပ် (kyap), meaning 'a unit of currency.'
