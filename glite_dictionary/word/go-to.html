go-to

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊ tu / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ tuː / (BrE)
a person or thing that is regularly relied on or chosen for a particular purpose or in a particular situation
person or thing regularly relied on

Examples

Whenever I need advice, Sarah is my go-to.
Chocolate is my go-to snack when I'm feeling stressed.
For quick lunches, sandwiches are always my go-to.

2. ADJ

/ ˈɡoʊ tu / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ tuː / (BrE)
(of a person, place, or thing) relied on or preferred for a particular purpose or as a source of help or information.
reliably preferred choice or resource
informal

Examples

Coffee is my go-to drink when I need an energy boost.
She's the go-to person for advice about college applications.
That Italian restaurant is our go-to spot for family celebrations.
