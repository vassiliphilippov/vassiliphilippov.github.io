interestingly

1. ADV

/ ˈɪntrəstɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɪntrəstɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is interesting or arouses curiosity.
in an interesting way

Examples

Interestingly, the study found a link between sleep habits and memory retention.
She told the story interestingly, capturing everyone's attention.
Interestingly, no one noticed the unusual detail in the painting.

Synset Examples

when he ceases to be just interestingly neurotic and … gets locked up

Related Terms

  • Antonym: boringly - in a boring manner

Etymology

Formed from 'interesting' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
