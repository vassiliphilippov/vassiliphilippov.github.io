libtard

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪbˌtɑrd / (AmE), / ˈlɪbˌtɑːd / (BrE)
A highly offensive slang term used to insult or disparage someone with liberal political views.
offensive term for a liberal
slang offensive

Examples

He dismissed anyone who disagreed with him as a libtard on social media.
The word libtard is considered extremely offensive and should be avoided in polite conversation.
Using terms like libtard only serves to increase hostility between political groups.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liberal - a person who holds liberal political views
  • Antonym: conservative - a person holding traditional values, especially in politics

Etymology

Blend of 'liberal' and the offensive suffix '-tard' (from 'retard').
