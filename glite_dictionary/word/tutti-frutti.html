tutti-frutti

1. NOUN

/ ˌtuːtiˈfruːti / (AmE), / ˌtʊtiˈfruːti / (BrE)
A type of ice cream made with mixed chopped candied fruits.
ice cream with candied fruits

Examples

After dinner, she ordered a bowl of tutti-frutti as a sweet treat.
The new ice cream shop offers tutti-frutti alongside more traditional flavors.
He remembered the taste of homemade tutti-frutti from his childhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fruitcake - cake containing dried or candied fruits and nuts

Etymology

From Italian 'tutti i frutti' meaning 'all the fruits'.
