childbirth

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪldˌbɝθ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑɪldˌbɜːθ / (BrE)
the process of giving birth to a baby
the act of giving birth

Examples

Advances in medicine have greatly improved safety during childbirth.
She took classes to prepare herself for childbirth.
After a long and difficult childbirth, the baby girl was born healthy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delivery - the act of giving birth; the process of delivering a baby

Etymology

Formed from 'child' + 'birth'; Old English 'cild' (child) + 'byrth' (birth).
