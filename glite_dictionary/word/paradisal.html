paradisal

1. ADJ

/ ˌpærəˈdaɪzəl / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈdaɪzəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of paradise; heavenly or blissful.
relating to paradise; heavenly

Examples

The island's lush landscape created a paradisal setting for the retreat.
She described the garden as a paradisal haven of peace and beauty.
Many people dream of finding a paradisal beach untouched by tourists.

Synset Examples

together in that paradisal place
paradisiacal innocence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paradisiacal - resembling or relating to paradise; blissful

Etymology

From paradise + -al, modeled after the noun 'paradise.'
