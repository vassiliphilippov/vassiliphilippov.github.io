shambolic

1. ADJ

/ ʃæmˈbɑːlɪk / (AmE), / ʃamˈbɒlɪk / (BrE)
disorderly, chaotic, or very badly organized; in a state of confusion
disorderly or chaotic
british informal

Examples

The meeting rapidly became shambolic as everyone tried to talk at once.
Their finances are in a shambolic state after years of neglect.
Many passengers complained about the shambolic organization at the airport.

Synset Examples

it's a shambolic system

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disorderly - lacking organization; chaotic
  • Synonym: chaotic - in a state of complete confusion and disorder
  • Antonym: orderly - well organized and controlled

Etymology

Formed from 'shambles', meaning a state of complete disorder, plus the suffix '-ic'.
