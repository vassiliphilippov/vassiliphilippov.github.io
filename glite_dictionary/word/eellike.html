eellike

1. ADJ

/ ˈiːlˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈiːlˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling an eel in being long, thin, and sinuous.
resembling an eel

Examples

The animal moved in an eellike fashion through the water.
Her eellike body allowed her to slip through tight spaces.
The cable was eellike, twisting and turning across the floor.

Etymology

Formed from 'eel' + '-like', meaning 'like an eel'.
