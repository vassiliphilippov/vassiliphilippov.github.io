fiat

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiː.æt, ˈfaɪ.ət / (AmE), / ˈfiː.æt, ˈfaɪ.ət / (BrE)
an official order or decree, especially one issued by a legal authority or by someone in power
official order; legal decree
legal

Examples

The government issued a fiat banning all export of medical supplies.
Changes cannot be made simply by administrative fiat; proper procedures must be followed.
The new tax law was implemented by royal fiat.

Synset Examples

a friend in New Mexico said that the order caused no trouble out there

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decree - an official order issued by a legal authority
  • Synonym: order - a directive issued by a legal or authoritative body
  • Synonym: mandate - an official command or authorization

Etymology

From Latin 'fiat' (let it be done), from 'facere' (to do).
