consentient

1. ADJ

/ kənˈsɛn.ti.ənt / (AmE), / kɒnˈsɛn.ti.ənt / (BrE)
In complete agreement; having the same opinion or feeling as others.
in complete agreement

Examples

The committee was consentient in its support for the new policy.
After much discussion, the partners finally reached a consentient decision.
It is rare to find a room of experts so consentient on such a controversial topic.

Synset Examples

a unanimous decision

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unanimous - (of a group) fully in agreement
  • Antonym: divided - not in agreement; differing in opinion

Etymology

From Latin 'consentiens', present participle of 'consentire' (to agree, to consent).
