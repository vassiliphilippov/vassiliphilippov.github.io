Mindanao

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɪndəˈnaʊ / (AmE), / ˌmɪndəˈnaʊ / (BrE)
the second largest island of the Philippines, located at the southern end of the country and known for its mountainous and volcanic landscape
the second largest island of the Philippines

Examples

Many tourists visit Mindanao to hike its volcanic mountains and explore its natural beauty.
Cocoa farming plays a vital role in Mindanao's economy and local communities.
Mindanao has a diverse cultural heritage distinct from other regions of the Philippines.

Etymology

From Spanish Mindanao, derived from Maguindanaon 'Mindanaw.'
