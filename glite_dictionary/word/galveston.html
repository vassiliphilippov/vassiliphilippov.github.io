Galveston

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡæl.və.stən / (AmE), / ˈɡæl.və.stən / (BrE)
A city and port on Galveston Island in southeast Texas, known for its beaches, historic sites, and as a major Gulf Coast port.
city and port in southeast Texas

Examples

We spent our summer vacation in Galveston, enjoying the beaches and local attractions.
Galveston was hit by a devastating hurricane in 1900.
The port of Galveston handles a large amount of cargo every year.

Etymology

Named after Bernardo de Gálvez, a Spanish colonial administrator.
