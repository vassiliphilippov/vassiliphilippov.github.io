unpersuadable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.pərˈsweɪ.də.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.pəˈsweɪ.də.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be convinced or influenced to change one's mind or decision
not able to be persuaded

Examples

Despite my best arguments, he remained unpersuadable throughout the discussion.
She is unpersuadable when it comes to breaking the rules, no matter the reason.
The committee found the CEO's position on the merger completely unpersuadable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intransigent - unwilling to change one's views or to agree about something
  • Synonym: inflexible - unwilling to change or compromise
  • Antonym: persuadable - able to be persuaded or convinced

Etymology

From un- (not) + persuadable (able to be persuaded).
