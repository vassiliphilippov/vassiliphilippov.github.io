huge

1. ADJ

/ hjudʒ / (AmE), / hjuːdʒ / (BrE)
Extremely large in size, amount, or degree.
extremely large in size or amount

They live in a huge house on the outskirts of the city.
The concert attracted a huge crowd of enthusiastic fans.
