vermillion

1. ADJ

/ vərˈmɪliən / (AmE), / vəˈmɪlɪən / (BrE)
of a vivid red to reddish-orange color
vivid red or reddish-orange color

Examples

The artist chose a vermillion background to create a sense of warmth in the painting.
She wore a striking vermillion dress to the evening gala.
Sunset turned the sky a brilliant vermillion that faded into darkness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scarlet - bright red color slightly orange-tinged
  • Synonym: crimson - deep red color inclining to purple

Etymology

From Latin 'vermiculus', diminutive of 'vermis' (worm), originally referring to a dye made from insects.
