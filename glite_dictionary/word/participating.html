participating

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɑːrtɪˈpeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpɑːtɪˈpeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Taking part in an activity or event; involved as a participant.
taking part; involved

Examples

All participating students must sign the attendance sheet at the start of the workshop.
The participating teams will receive certificates for completing the tournament.
Several participating authors attended the book launch and answered questions from readers.

Synset Examples

an active member of the club
he was politically active
the participating organizations

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inactive - not participating; not involved
  • Synonym: to participate - to take part in an activity or event
