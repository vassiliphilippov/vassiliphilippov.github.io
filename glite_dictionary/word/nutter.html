nutter

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌtər / (AmE), / ˈnʌtə / (BrE)
an eccentric or mad person; someone regarded as crazy or very strange
eccentric or mad person
informal british

Examples

Everyone at the party thought he was a nutter for dancing on the table.
Her uncle is a real nutter when it comes to conspiracy theories.
Don't pay attention to that nutter shouting in the street.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunatic - a person who is mentally ill and behaves in a very foolish way
  • Synonym: madman - a man who is mentally ill or behaves in a wildly foolish way
  • Antonym: sane - having a healthy mind; not mad

Etymology

From 'nut' (slang for head, eccentric person) + -er (agent noun suffix).
