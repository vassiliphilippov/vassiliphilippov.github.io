introverted

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪn.trəˌvɝː.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈɪn.trəˌvɜː.tɪd / (BrE)
more interested in own thoughts and feelings than in seeking external stimulation or social interaction; tending to look inward
reserved; inward-looking

Examples

She has an introverted personality and prefers spending evenings reading at home.
Although he seems introverted, he enjoys meaningful conversations with close friends.
The workshop helped introverted students feel more comfortable in group settings.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: extroverted - outgoing and socially confident
  • Synonym: reserved - slow to reveal emotions or opinions

Etymology

From Latin 'introvertere' (to turn inward), from 'intro-' (inward) + 'vertere' (to turn).
