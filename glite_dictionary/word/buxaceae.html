Buxaceae

1. NOUN

/ bʌkˈseɪsiː / (AmE), / bʌkˈsiːsiː / (BrE)
a family of widely distributed evergreen shrubs and trees, including boxwoods
family of evergreen shrubs and trees

Examples

The Buxaceae family includes popular ornamental plants like boxwoods.
Many gardens feature members of the Buxaceae for their dense, evergreen foliage.
Researchers study the Buxaceae to better understand plant distribution and evolution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boxwood - an evergreen shrub or small tree of the genus Buxus

Etymology

From New Latin Buxaceae, based on the type genus Buxus (boxwood).
