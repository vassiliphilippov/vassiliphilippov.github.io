Luo

1. NOUN

/ ˈluː.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈluː.əʊ / (BrE)
a Nilotic language spoken chiefly in the Adamawa Region and parts of eastern Nigeria and Cameroon
a Nilotic language of central Africa

Examples

He specializes in the study of the Luo language and its dialects.
Many stories and folktales have been preserved in Luo.
She is fluent in English, French, and Luo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nilotic - relating to the Nilotic peoples or their languages

Etymology

From the self-designation of the Luo people.
