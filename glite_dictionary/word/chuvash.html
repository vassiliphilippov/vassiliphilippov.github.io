Chuvash

1. NOUN

/ tʃuˈvɑːʃ / (AmE), / tʃʊˈvɑːʃ / (BrE)
the Turkic language spoken by the Chuvash people, primarily in the Chuvash Republic of Russia.
a Turkic language of Russia

Examples

She is fluent in both Russian and Chuvash.
The Chuvash language has preserved many ancient Turkic features.
He studied Chuvash as part of his research into minority languages of Russia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Turkish - a major Turkic language spoken in Turkey

Etymology

From Russian Чуваш (Chuvash), the name of the people and language.

2. NOUN

/ tʃuˈvɑʃ / (AmE), / tʃuˈvɑːʃ / (BrE)
a member of a Turkic-speaking ethnic group living primarily in the Volga region of eastern Russia
member of a Turkic people in eastern Russia

Examples

Many Chuvash have migrated from rural villages to larger cities in search of work.
The traditions of the Chuvash remain vibrant, especially during cultural festivals in the Volga region.
He met a family of Chuvash who shared stories about their heritage and language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chuvash - the Turkic language spoken by the Chuvash people

Etymology

From Russian Чуваш (Chuvash), the name of the people and their language.
