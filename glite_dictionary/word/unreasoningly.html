unreasoningly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈriːzənɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈriːzənɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is not based on or guided by reason or logic; irrationally or instinctively.
irrationally; without logical reasoning

Examples

She reacted unreasoningly to the slightest criticism, refusing to consider any explanation.
The crowd unreasoningly panicked when the lights went out, despite there being no real danger.
He opposed the proposal unreasoningly, dismissing all logical arguments in its favor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irrationally - in a way that is not based on reason or logic
  • Antonym: reasonably - in a way that is based on reason or logic

Etymology

From 'unreasoning' + '-ly'.
