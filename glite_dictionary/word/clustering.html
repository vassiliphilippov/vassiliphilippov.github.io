clustering

1. NOUN

/ ˈklʌstərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklʌstərɪŋ / (BrE)
a group or grouping of similar things, people, or objects close together
grouping of similar things

Examples

There was a clustering of small cottages at the edge of the village.
The clustering of data points on the graph suggests a strong correlation.
Wildflowers grew in a tight clustering along the path.

Synset Examples

a bunch of trees
a cluster of admirers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cluster - a group of similar things or people positioned closely together

Etymology

From the verb 'cluster' (to form into a group), itself from Middle English 'clustren'.
