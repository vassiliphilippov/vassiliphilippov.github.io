scrimshanker

1. NOUN

/ ˈskrɪmˌʃæŋkər / (AmE), / ˈskrɪmʃæŋkə / (BrE)
a person who avoids work or duty; a shirker
person who shirks duties
british slang

Examples

Everyone knew he was a scrimshanker who tried to dodge every unpleasant job.
The army sergeant was tired of dealing with scrimshankers in his platoon.
It's hard to get any work done when there's a scrimshanker on the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shirker - a person who avoids work or responsibility

Etymology

British slang, possibly related to 'scrimshank' (to avoid work) of uncertain origin.
