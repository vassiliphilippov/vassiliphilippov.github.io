Hans Holbein the Elder

1. PROPN

/ hɑnz ˈhoʊlbaɪn ði ˈɛldər / (AmE), / hans ˈhɒlbaɪn ði ˈɛldə / (BrE)
(1465–1524) A German painter known for religious works and as the father of Hans Holbein the Younger.
German painter of religious works (1465–1524)

Examples

Many art historians regard Hans Holbein the Elder as a master of German Renaissance painting.
The religious altarpieces created by Hans Holbein the Elder are displayed in several European museums.
Hans Holbein the Elder was also the father of Hans Holbein the Younger, another influential artist.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Hans Holbein the Younger - German painter, son of Hans Holbein the Elder, renowned for portraits (1497–1543)
