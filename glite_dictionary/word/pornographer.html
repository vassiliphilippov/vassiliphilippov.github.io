pornographer

1. NOUN

/ pɔrˈnɑɡrəfər / (AmE), / pɔːˈnɒɡrəfə / (BrE)
a person who produces, publishes, or distributes sexually explicit material, especially for commercial purposes
producer of sexually explicit material

Examples

The police arrested the pornographer for distributing obscene magazines in the community.
She criticized the film industry for tolerating pornographers who ignore legal limits.
As a journalist, he interviewed a reputed pornographer about his controversial business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: publisher - a person or company that prepares and issues books, journals, or other works for sale

Etymology

From pornography + -er, indicating a person involved in pornography.
