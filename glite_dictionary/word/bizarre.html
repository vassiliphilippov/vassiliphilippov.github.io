bizarre

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈzɑr / (AmE), / bɪˈzɑː / (BrE)
strikingly strange or unconventional; extremely unusual or unexpected in appearance, style, or behavior
very strange or unconventional

Examples

The museum featured a bizarre assortment of modern artworks that fascinated visitors.
His explanation for being late was so bizarre that no one believed him.
She wore a bizarre costume to the party, turning heads as she entered.

Synset Examples

restaurants of bizarre design — one like a hat, another like a rabbit
famed for his eccentric spelling
a freakish combination of styles
his off-the-wall antics
the outlandish clothes of teenagers
outre and affected stage antics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - unconventional and slightly strange
  • Synonym: outlandish - looking or sounding bizarre or unfamiliar
  • Antonym: ordinary - with no special or distinctive features; normal

Etymology

From French 'bizarre' (odd, fantastic), possibly of Basque origin.
