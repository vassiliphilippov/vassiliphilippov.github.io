promposal

1. NOUN

/ prɑmˈpoʊzəl / (AmE), / prɒmˈpəʊzəl / (BrE)
An elaborate or creative act of inviting someone to attend a prom with you.
elaborate invitation to prom

Examples

He spent weeks planning the perfect promposal for his girlfriend.
Her elaborate promposal went viral on social media.
The students shared their best promposal ideas with the whole class.

Etymology

Blend of 'prom' (promenade, a formal dance) and 'proposal'.
