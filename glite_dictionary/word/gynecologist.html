gynecologist

1. NOUN

/ ɡaɪnəˈkɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɡaɪnɪˈkɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A doctor who specializes in gynecology—the medical practice dealing with the female reproductive system.
doctor specializing in female reproductive system
medical

Examples

I have an appointment with my gynecologist this afternoon for a routine checkup.
The gynecologist explained the treatment options carefully to her patient.
Many women visit their gynecologist annually for preventive care and health advice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obstetrician - a doctor specializing in pregnancy and childbirth

Etymology

From Greek 'gynē' (woman) + 'logia' (study).
