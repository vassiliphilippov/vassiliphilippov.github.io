three-seeded

1. ADJ

/ ˈθriˌsidəd / (AmE), / ˈθriːˌsiːdɪd / (BrE)
(of a plant, fruit, or part) having three seeds.
having three seeds (esp. in botany)

Examples

The three-seeded capsule is characteristic of this plant species.
Some varieties yield a three-seeded fruit, making them easy to identify.
Botanists classified the new shrub as a three-seeded specimen due to its reproductive features.
