vestiture

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛstɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈvɛstɪtjʊə / (BrE)
(Archaic) Clothing; garments collectively.
archaic term for clothing
archaic

Examples

The nobleman arrived, resplendent in fine vestiture befitting his station.
The ancient manuscript described the vestiture of priests in great detail.
Her vestiture reflected the fashions of a bygone era.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clothing - clothes collectively; apparel
  • Synonym: garment - an item of clothing

Etymology

From Latin vestītūra, from vestīre (to clothe).
