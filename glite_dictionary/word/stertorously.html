stertorously

1. ADV

/ stɚˈtɔrəsli / (AmE), / stɜːˈtɔːrəsli / (BrE)
in a noisy and labored manner, especially with heavy breathing
noisily and heavily (esp. breathing)

Examples

The patient was breathing stertorously after the surgery, prompting concern from the nurses.
He snored stertorously throughout the night, keeping his roommate awake.
She could hear her father's stertorously labored breaths from the next room.

Synset Examples

he was breathing stertorously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noisily - in a noisy manner
  • Antonym: quietly - in a quiet manner

Etymology

From 'stertorous' + adverbial suffix -ly; 'stertorous' from Latin 'stertor' (snoring).
