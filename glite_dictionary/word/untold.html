untold

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtoʊld / (AmE), / ʌnˈtəʊld / (BrE)
too great or numerous to be counted or measured; incalculable.
too great to be counted

Examples

The refugees endured untold hardship during their journey across the desert.
She inherited untold wealth from her late uncle.
The disaster caused untold damage to the small village.

Synset Examples

untold suffering
incalculable riches

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incalculable - too great to be calculated or estimated
  • Synonym: immeasurable - too large or extensive to be measured
