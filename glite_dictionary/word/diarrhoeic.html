diarrhoeic

1. ADJ

/ daɪˈɛriɪk / (AmE), / daɪ.əˈriː.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by diarrhea
relating to diarrhea
medical british

Examples

The diarrhoeic child was admitted to the hospital for treatment.
Doctors monitored diarrhoeic symptoms closely during the outbreak.
A diarrhoeic episode can lead to severe dehydration if untreated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diarrheic - relating to or affected by diarrhea (primarily US spelling)

Etymology

From Greek diarrhoia (a flowing through) via Late Latin diarrhoea.
