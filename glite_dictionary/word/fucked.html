fucked

1. ADJ

/ fʌkt / (AmE), / fʌkt / (BrE)
Severely damaged, disordered, or in a state that is hopelessly wrong; ruined.
severely damaged or ruined (vulgar, informal)
vulgar informal

Examples

After the accident, the car was completely fucked and couldn't be driven anymore.
My computer is fucked—it won't even turn on now.
We missed the deadline, so our whole project is pretty much fucked now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broken - damaged and no longer working properly
  • Synonym: ruined - completely destroyed or spoiled
  • Antonym: fixed - restored to a good condition

Etymology

From the past participle of the verb 'fuck'.
