Yquem

1. NOUN

/ iːˈkɛm / (AmE), / iːˈkɛm / (BrE)
A sweet, white French wine produced at the Château d'Yquem estate in the Sauternes region.
sweet French white wine from Château d'Yquem

Examples

She served a glass of Yquem with the dessert course.
Wine connoisseurs consider Yquem to be among the finest sweet wines in the world.
They opened a rare bottle of Yquem to celebrate the occasion.

Etymology

From Château d'Yquem, a renowned French wine estate in Sauternes, Bordeaux.
