fanwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈfænˌwɔrt / (AmE), / ˈfanˌwɔːt / (BrE)
A common aquatic plant of eastern North America with floating and submerged leaves and white yellow-spotted flowers.
aquatic plant with white yellow-spotted flowers

Examples

The pond was filled with fanwort, making it difficult to see the fish below.
Gardeners often struggle to control fanwort because it spreads so quickly in freshwater habitats.
The white yellow-spotted flowers of the fanwort added a delicate beauty to the quiet lake.
