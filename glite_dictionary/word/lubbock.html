Lubbock

1. PROPN

/ ˈlʌbək / (AmE), / ˈlʌbək / (BrE)
A city in northwest Texas, United States, south of Amarillo.
city in northwest Texas, US

Examples

Lubbock is known for being the birthplace of musician Buddy Holly.
The university in Lubbock attracts students from all over the state.
After moving to Lubbock, Sarah quickly found a sense of community.

Etymology

Named after Thomas Saltus Lubbock, a Texas Ranger and Confederate officer.
