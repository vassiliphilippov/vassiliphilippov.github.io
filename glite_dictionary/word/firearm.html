firearm

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚˌɑːrm / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌɑːm / (BrE)
A gun that can be carried and fired by hand, such as a pistol or rifle.
portable gun; handheld weapon

Examples

Carrying a firearm without a licence is illegal in many countries.
The suspect was found in possession of a loaded firearm.
Police officers are trained to handle a firearm safely.

Synset Examples

he wore his firearm in a shoulder holster

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gun - A weapon that shoots bullets or shells
  • Degree Lower: pistol - A small handheld firearm
  • Degree Higher: rifle - A long-barreled firearm designed for precision shooting

Etymology

From fire + arm, referring to weapons that use gunpowder or another propellant to fire a projectile.
