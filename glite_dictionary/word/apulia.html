Apulia

1. PROPN

/ əˈpuːliə / (AmE), / əˈpjuːliə / (BrE)
a region in southeastern Italy, located on the Adriatic coast.
region of southeastern Italy

Examples

The countryside of Apulia is famous for its olive groves and historic towns.
During my trip to Italy, I spent several days exploring Apulia.
Many tourists visit Apulia to enjoy its beautiful beaches and unique cuisine.

Etymology

From Latin 'Apulia', the Roman name for the region.
