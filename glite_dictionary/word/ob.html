Ob

1. PROPN

/ ɑb / (AmE), / ɒb / (BrE)
a major river in western Siberia that flows north and west to the Gulf of Ob and the Kara Sea
major river in western Siberia

Examples

The Ob River is one of the longest rivers in the world, stretching across western Siberia.
Cargo ships frequently navigate the Ob during the ice-free months of the year.
Many settlements along the Ob rely on the river for fishing and transportation.
