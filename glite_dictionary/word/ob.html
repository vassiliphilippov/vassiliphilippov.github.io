OB

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈbi / (AmE), / əʊˈbiː / (BrE)
An abbreviation for obstetrics, the branch of medicine concerned with childbirth and the care of women giving birth.
abbreviation for obstetrics (childbirth medicine)
medical american

Examples

She visited the OB for her prenatal checkups during pregnancy.
The hospital has an excellent OB department with experienced nurses and doctors.
After her first visit to the OB, Sarah felt reassured about her pregnancy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obstetrics - the branch of medicine dealing with childbirth

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'obstetrics', from Latin 'obstetrix' (midwife).
