to notify

1. VERB

/ ˈnoʊ.t̬ə.faɪ / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ.tɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
to inform someone officially about something
formally inform

Examples

Please notify us if you decide to change your address.
The school will notify parents of any emergencies by phone.
She was notified about the meeting in advance.

Synset Examples

I advised him that the rent was due

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to inform - to give someone facts or information
  • Synonym: to advise - to tell someone what you think they should do
  • Synonym: alert - to warn someone of danger or a problem

Etymology

From Latin notificare, from notus (known) + facere (make).
