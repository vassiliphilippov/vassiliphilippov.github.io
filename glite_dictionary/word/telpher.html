telpher

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlfər / (AmE), / ˈtɛlfə / (BrE)
a small vehicle or car that runs on suspended cables, used in telpherage systems for carrying goods or passengers
cable car in a telpherage system

Examples

The engineer inspected the telpher as it glided smoothly along the overhead cable.
Each telpher could carry several tons of coal across the valley efficiently.
Old photographs show a telpher transporting supplies to remote construction sites.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cable car - vehicle suspended or guided by a cable, used for transport
  • Degree Higher: telpherage - system of aerial transport using suspended cable cars

Etymology

From Greek 'tele-' (far) + 'pherein' (to carry), coined in the late 19th century for cable transport systems.
