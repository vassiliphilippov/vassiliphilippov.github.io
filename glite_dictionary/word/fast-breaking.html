fast-breaking

1. ADJ

/ fæst ˈbreɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / fɑːst ˈbreɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
(of news or events) developing or occurring very quickly in rapid succession.
developing or occurring very quickly

Examples

The reporter provided updates on the fast-breaking developments from the disaster site.
We interrupted our program to bring you fast-breaking news about the storm.
Social media plays a key role in spreading fast-breaking stories around the world.

Synset Examples

the broadcast was interrupted by a fast-breaking news story about the invasion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breaking - (of news) newly received and currently developing
