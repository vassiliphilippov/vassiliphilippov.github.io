sofa

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊfə / (AmE), / ˈsəʊfə / (BrE)
A long, comfortable seat with a back and arms, designed to accommodate two or more people.
long upholstered seat for several people

Examples

She bought a new sofa for the living room.
Three people sat comfortably on the big sofa during the movie night.
The cat loves to nap on the soft sofa by the window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: couch - a long, upholstered seat for reclining or seating several people

Etymology

From Arabic ṣuffa (bench or couch), via Turkish and then French.
