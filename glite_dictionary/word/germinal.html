Germinal

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɝː.mɪ.nəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜː.mɪ.nəl / (BrE)
the seventh month of the French Revolutionary calendar, corresponding approximately to March and April; the month of buds
7th month of French Revolutionary calendar

Examples

The trial took place in Germinal of the year II, during the spring of 1794.
Many revolutionary events were recorded as happening in Germinal, according to the French calendar.
The month of Germinal saw the first signs of new growth in the countryside.

Etymology

From French 'Germinal', from Latin 'germen' (bud, sprout); named for the sprouting of plants in spring.
