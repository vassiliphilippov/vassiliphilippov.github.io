berrylike

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛriˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɛriˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling or having the appearance of a berry.
resembling a berry

Examples

The bright, berrylike fruits attract many birds to the garden.
Some fungi produce berrylike structures that are often mistaken for berries.
The shrub had clusters of berrylike seeds hanging from its branches.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: berry - a small roundish fruit

Etymology

Formed from "berry" + "-like" suffix meaning 'resembling.'
