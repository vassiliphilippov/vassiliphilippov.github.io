inefficiently

1. ADV

/ ˌɪnɪˈfɪʃəntli / (AmE), / ˌɪnɪˈfɪʃəntli / (BrE)
In a way that is not effective or does not achieve the desired results with minimal waste of time or resources.
in a manner lacking efficiency

Examples

The company handled customer complaints inefficiently, leading to many dissatisfied clients.
She completed her tasks inefficiently because she was unfamiliar with the new software.
If resources are used inefficiently, the project may go over budget.

Synset Examples

he dealt inefficiently with the crisis

Related Terms

  • Antonym: efficiently - in a way that achieves maximum productivity with minimum wasted effort
