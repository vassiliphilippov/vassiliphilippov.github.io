jodhpur

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɑd.pɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒd.pə / (BrE)
A short riding boot that fastens with a buckle at the side, typically worn for horseback riding.
short riding boot with side buckle

Examples

The rider adjusted her jodhpur before mounting the horse.
He bought a new pair of jodhpur boots for the riding competition.
Classic jodhpur styles remain popular among equestrians worldwide.

Etymology

From Jodhpur, a city in India, where this style of boot originated.
