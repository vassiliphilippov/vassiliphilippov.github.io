hammered

1. ADJ

/ ˈhæmɚd / (AmE), / ˈhæməd / (BrE)
(of metal) shaped or worked using a hammer, typically showing hammer marks as a result.
shaped with a hammer (typically metal)

Examples

The craftsman created a beautiful hammered silver bowl.
The hammered copper panels added texture to the kitchen backsplash.
She admired the hammered finish on the artisan jewelry.

Synset Examples

a bowl of hammered brass
