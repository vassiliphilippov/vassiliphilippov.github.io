relaxedness

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlækstnɪs / (AmE), / rɪˈlakstnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being relaxed or at ease.
state of being relaxed

Examples

She envied his relaxedness during the high-pressure interview.
A general air of relaxedness made the café a pleasant place to spend an afternoon.
Despite the chaos, her relaxedness reassured everyone around her.

Synset Examples

Those who did not know the captain might have been taken aback at the peculiar mixture of his accurate briefing and his relaxedness, but those who had been serving him for some time knew that it was the latter quality that he had trouble projecting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relaxation - the state of being free from tension or anxiety
  • Antonym: tension - mental or emotional strain
