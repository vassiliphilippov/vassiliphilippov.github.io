Oman

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈmɑːn / (AmE), / əʊˈmɑːn / (BrE)
a country located on the southeastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula, known for its strategic position and economy dominated by oil
country on the Arabian Peninsula

Examples

He spent several years working in Oman as an engineer.
The coastal city of Muscat is the capital of Oman.
Tourism has been growing rapidly in Oman in recent years.

Etymology

From Arabic عُمَان‎ (‘Umān), the name of the country and its capital region.
