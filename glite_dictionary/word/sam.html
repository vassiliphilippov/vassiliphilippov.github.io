SAM

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛs ˈeɪ ˈɛm / (AmE), / ˈɛs ˈeɪ ˈɛm / (BrE)
a guided missile launched from land or ship against an airborne target
surface-to-air missile
american

Examples

The enemy aircraft was brought down by a SAM.
The convoy was protected by mobile SAM launchers.
Military forces deployed several SAMs along the border for air defense.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surface-to-air missile - missile launched from ground or sea to destroy aircraft

Etymology

Acronym of 'surface-to-air missile.'
