archiepiscopal

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑːr.ki.ɪˈpɪs.kə.pəl / (AmE), / ˌɑː.ki.ɪˈpɪs.kə.pəl / (BrE)
relating to or belonging to an archbishop or the office of an archbishop.
relating to an archbishop

Examples

The cathedral holds significant archiepiscopal authority in the region.
An archiepiscopal residence is provided for the leader of the diocese.
The ceremony was attended by several members of the archiepiscopal council.

Synset Examples

an archiepiscopal see

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: episcopal - relating to a bishop or bishops

Etymology

From Medieval Latin archiepiscopalis, from Late Latin archiepiscopus (archbishop).
