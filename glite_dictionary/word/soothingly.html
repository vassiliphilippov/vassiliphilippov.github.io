soothingly

1. ADV

/ ˈsuðɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsuːðɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that calms, comforts, or relieves distress
in a calming or comforting way

Examples

She spoke soothingly to the frightened puppy until it stopped shaking.
The music played soothingly in the background, helping everyone relax.
He rubbed her back soothingly as she fell asleep.

Synset Examples

the mother talked soothingly to her child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calmly - in a quiet and peaceful manner
  • Antonym: harshly - in a rough or unpleasant manner
