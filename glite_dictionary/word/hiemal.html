hiemal

1. ADJ

/ ˈhaɪəməl / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.ɪ.məl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of winter; wintry.
relating to winter

Examples

The hiemal landscape was covered in a thick blanket of snow.
Migratory birds leave before the hiemal months begin.
Her poetry often captures the stillness of hiemal evenings.

Synset Examples

bears in brumal sleep

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brumal - relating to winter
  • Antonym: vernal - relating to spring

Etymology

From Latin 'hiemalis' (of winter), from 'hiems' (winter).
