activity

1. NOUN

/ ækˈtɪv.ə.ti / (AmE), / ækˈtɪv.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
a natural process or function occurring within a living organism, such as movement, metabolism, or secretion
biological process in the body

Examples

Physical exercise increases muscular activity throughout the body.
The doctor measured the electrical activity of the patient's heart.
Some medications slow down the digestive activity in the stomach.

Synset Examples

respiratory activity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: process - a series of actions or steps taken to achieve a particular end

Etymology

From Latin 'activitas' (activeness), from 'activus' (active).
