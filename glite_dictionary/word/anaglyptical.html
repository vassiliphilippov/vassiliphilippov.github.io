anaglyptical

1. ADJ

/ ˌænəˈɡlɪptɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌanəˈɡlɪptɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of anaglyphs or anaglyphy, especially in art or printing; involving designs molded or carved in relief.
relating to anaglyphs or relief work

Examples

The museum displayed several anaglyptical coins, their raised designs clearly visible to the touch.
She specialized in making anaglyptical illustrations used for educational materials.
The anaglyptical patterns on the vase created a striking play of light and shadow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anaglyphic - relating to anaglyphs; another adjective form

Etymology

From Greek ana- (up) + glyphein (to carve), via 'anaglyph' + suffix '-ical'.
