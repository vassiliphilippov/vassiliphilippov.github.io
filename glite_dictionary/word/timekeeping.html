timekeeping

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪmˌkipɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmˌkiːpɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or process of measuring or recording the passage of time.
measuring or recording time

Examples

The accuracy of timekeeping is essential for fair competition in sports events.
Advances in technology have greatly improved the precision of timekeeping devices.
Reliable timekeeping is crucial for coordinating international business meetings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chronometry - the scientific measurement of time
  • Synonym: timekeeper - a person or device that measures or records the time

Etymology

From 'time' + 'keeping', meaning keeping track of time.
