fuckhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌkˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈfʌkˌhɛd / (BrE)
A contemptuous and vulgar term for a very stupid or obnoxious person.
vulgar insult for a stupid person
offensive vulgar slang

Examples

Don't be such a fuckhead, just listen to what I'm saying.
He acted like a total fuckhead at the meeting and embarrassed us all.
I can't believe that fuckheads like him get promoted.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a person of low intelligence or common insult
  • Synonym: jerk - an annoying or contemptible person
