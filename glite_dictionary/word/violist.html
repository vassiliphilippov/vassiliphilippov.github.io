violist

1. NOUN

/ viˈoʊlɪst / (AmE), / vaɪˈəʊlɪst / (BrE)
a musician who plays the viola
player of the viola

Examples

The violist performed a haunting solo during the orchestra's concert.
As a professional violist, she travels around the world with her quartet.
The string quartet featured two violinists, a cellist, and a violist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: violinist - a musician who plays the violin

Etymology

From 'viola' + '-ist', denoting a person who performs or is skilled in a particular activity.
