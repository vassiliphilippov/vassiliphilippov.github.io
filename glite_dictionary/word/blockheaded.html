blockheaded

1. ADJ

/ ˈblɑkˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈblɒkˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
(informal) Stupid; lacking intelligence or common sense.
stupid; lacking intelligence (informal)
informal

Examples

His blockheaded decision cost the company a lot of money.
I can't believe how blockheaded he was during the meeting.
Even after my explanation, she remained oddly blockheaded about the issue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stupid - lacking intelligence or common sense
  • Synonym: dumb - informal, lacking intelligence; stupid
  • Antonym: intelligent - having or showing intelligence

Etymology

Formed from 'blockhead' + '-ed', where 'blockhead' (a foolish person) dates from the 16th century, alluding to a wooden block used as a model of a head.
