14th

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrˌtinθ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌtiːnθ / (BrE)
coming next after the thirteenth in position; being number fourteen in a series
number fourteen in a sequence

Examples

She finished in 14th place in the race.
The 14th floor has an amazing view of the city.
This is the 14th year the festival has been held.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirteenth - being number thirteen in a sequence
  • Degree Higher: fifteenth - being number fifteen in a sequence
