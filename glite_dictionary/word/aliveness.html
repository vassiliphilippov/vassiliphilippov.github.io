/ əˈlaɪvnəs / (AmE), / əˈlaɪvnɪs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being alive, animated, or full of life.
state of being alive or animated

Examples

There was a sense of aliveness in the air after the storm passed.
Yoga helped her rediscover her physical and emotional aliveness.
Art seeks to capture the fleeting aliveness of everyday moments.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lifelessness - the state of lacking life or animation
  • Synonym: vitality - the state of being strong and active; energy

Etymology

Formed from 'alive' + '-ness', denoting state or quality.
