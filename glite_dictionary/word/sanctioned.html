sanctioned

1. ADJ

/ ˈsæŋkʃənd / (AmE), / ˈsaŋkʃənd / (BrE)
formally approved and granted official or legal authority
formally approved; given legal authority

Examples

The newly sanctioned policy aims to improve public health standards across the city.
Only sanctioned vendors are allowed to sell food at the festival.
The report outlines the actions taken by sanctioned government agencies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: authorized - granted official permission or approval
