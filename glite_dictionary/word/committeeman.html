committeeman

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɪt̬.imæn / (AmE), / kəˈmɪt.iˌmæn / (BrE)
a man who is a member of a committee, especially within a political or organizational context
male member of a committee

Examples

The committeeman attended every meeting and was known for his dedication.
After years of volunteering, he was finally elected as the senior committeeman in the district.
Each political party selects a committeeman to represent them at the state convention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: committee member - a person who is a member of a committee
  • Antonym: committeewoman - a woman who is a member of a committee

Etymology

From 'committee' + 'man', patterned after similar formations like 'congressman'.
