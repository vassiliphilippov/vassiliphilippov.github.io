Tony Blair

1. PROPN

/ ˈtoʊni blɛr / (AmE), / ˈtəʊni blɛː / (BrE)
(born 1953) British statesman who served as Prime Minister from 1997 to 2007.
former British Prime Minister (1997–2007)

Examples

Tony Blair played a significant role in shaping British politics during the late 1990s and early 2000s.
Many historians analyze the impact of Tony Blair's policies on foreign affairs.
Tony Blair led the Labour Party to three consecutive general election victories.
