brownish-red

1. ADJ

/ ˈbraʊnɪʃ ˈrɛd / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnɪʃ ˈrɛd / (BrE)
Of a color that is a mix of brown and red, typically darker and sometimes tinged with purple.
dark reddish-brown color

Examples

The leaves turned a beautiful brownish-red in the autumn sunlight.
She wore a brownish-red scarf wrapped tightly around her neck.
The artist mixed several pigments to achieve just the right brownish-red hue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maroon - a dark brownish-red color
