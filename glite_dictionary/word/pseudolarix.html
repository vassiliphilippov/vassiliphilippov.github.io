Pseudolarix

1. NOUN

/ suːdoʊˈlærɪks / (AmE), / sjuːdəʊˈlarɪks / (BrE)
a genus of deciduous coniferous trees in the family Pinaceae, comprising the golden larch, native to eastern China
genus of coniferous trees (golden larch)

Examples

The Pseudolarix is admired for its vibrant golden needles in autumn.
Many gardeners plant Pseudolarix to add seasonal color and texture to their landscapes.
The wood of Pseudolarix is sometimes used for ornamental carving due to its fine grain.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin, combining pseudo- (false) and Larix (the larch genus), referring to its similarity to true larches.
