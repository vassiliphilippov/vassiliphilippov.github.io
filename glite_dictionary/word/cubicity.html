cubicity

1. NOUN

/ kjuːˈbɪsəti / (AmE), / kjuːˈbɪsɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of resembling a cube in shape
quality of resembling a cube

Examples

The cubicity of the sculpture gave it a modern and geometric appeal.
Architects admired the building’s cubicity, which made it stand out among its neighbors.
In crystal structures, cubicity affects the material's strength and appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cubical - having the shape of a cube

Etymology

From 'cube' + '-icity', patterning after 'cubic'.
