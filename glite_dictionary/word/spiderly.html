spiderly

1. ADJ

/ ˈspaɪdɚli / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdəli / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a spider or members of the class Arachnida.
resembling a spider

Examples

Her movements were so spiderly that they sent a chill down my spine.
The plant's spiderly leaves stretched out in all directions, giving it a peculiar appearance.
He described the mysterious figure as having a spiderly grace, silent and deliberate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arachnid - an animal of the class Arachnida, such as a spider or scorpion
