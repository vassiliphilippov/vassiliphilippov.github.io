limacoid

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪməˌkɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪməˌkɔɪd / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of a slug.
slug-like; resembling a slug

Examples

The garden creature's limacoid body glistened in the morning dew.
Some amphibians have a limacoid appearance due to their slimy texture.
Her costume was intentionally limacoid, complete with long antennae and a soft, moist texture.

Etymology

From Latin 'limax' (slug) + English '-oid' (resembling).
