zestfully

1. ADV

/ ˈzɛstfəli / (AmE), / ˈzɛstfəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows great enthusiasm and energy; with zest.
with great enthusiasm and energy

Examples

She cleaned the kitchen zestfully, humming her favorite tune.
The children played zestfully in the park despite the drizzle.
He tackled every new challenge zestfully, inspiring those around him.

Synset Examples

she scrubbed the floors of the new apartment zestfully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enthusiastically - in a way that shows intense enjoyment, interest, or approval
  • Antonym: listlessly - without energy or enthusiasm

Etymology

From "zestful" + "-ly"; "zestful" from "zest" (enthusiasm) + "-ful".
