ballplayer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔlˌpleɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlˌpleɪə / (BrE)
An athlete who plays baseball, especially at a professional or competitive level.
baseball player

Examples

The young ballplayer signed his first professional contract last week.
Many fans waited outside the stadium to catch a glimpse of their favorite ballplayer.
She dreams of becoming a ballplayer and playing in the major leagues one day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baseball player - a person who plays baseball
  • Antonym: cricketer - an athlete who plays cricket
