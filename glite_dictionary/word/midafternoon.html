midafternoon

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪdˌæftərˈnun / (AmE), / ˌmɪdˌɑːftəˈnuːn / (BrE)
The middle part of the afternoon, usually around 2:00 to 4:00 p.m.
middle of the afternoon

Examples

I usually feel sleepy in the midafternoon after lunch.
The midafternoon sun was especially strong today.
She scheduled the meeting for midafternoon to accommodate everyone.

Etymology

Formed from 'mid-' (middle) and 'afternoon'.
