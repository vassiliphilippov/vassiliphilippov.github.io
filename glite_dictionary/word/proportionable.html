proportionable

1. ADJ

/ prəˈpɔːrʃənəbl / (AmE), / prəˈpɔːʃənəbl / (BrE)
corresponding in size, degree, or amount to something else; proportionate
corresponding in degree or amount; proportionate
archaic

Examples

The punishment must be proportionable to the crime committed.
They sought a proportionable response to the magnitude of the threat.
We desire rewards that are proportionable to our efforts and commitment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proportionate - corresponding in size or amount to something else

Etymology

From Latin 'proportionabilis', from 'proportio' (proportion).
