Qatar

1. PROPN

/ kəˈtɑr / (AmE), / kəˈtɑː / (BrE)
A peninsula in western Asia, extending northward from the Arabian mainland into the Persian Gulf; also the name of the country occupying this peninsula.
peninsula and country in the Persian Gulf

Examples

The Qatar peninsula juts into the Persian Gulf, bordering Saudi Arabia to the south.
Much of Qatar is covered by desert, with flat, barren land dominating the peninsula.
Several major cities, including Doha, are located on the Qatar peninsula.

2. PROPN

/ kəˈtɑr, ˈkɑːtɑr / (AmE), / kəˈtɑː, ˈkætɑː / (BrE)
A country in Western Asia occupying the Qatar Peninsula on the northeastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula, known for its oil-rich economy and independence from the United Kingdom in 1971.
country in Western Asia; peninsula on Persian Gulf

Examples

The 2022 FIFA World Cup was hosted by Qatar, attracting fans from around the world.
She traveled to Qatar for a business conference in Doha.
Oil exports are a major source of income for Qatar.

Etymology

From Arabic قطر‎ (Qaṭar), the native name for the country.
