souvlakia

1. NOUN

/ suːˈvlɑkiə / (AmE), / suːˈvlækiə / (BrE)
A Greek dish consisting of small pieces of skewered and grilled meat, traditionally lamb, sometimes served with vegetables or pita bread.
Greek grilled meat skewers

Examples

We ordered a plate of souvlakia with a side of Greek salad.
The restaurant serves delicious souvlakia made with tender lamb and fresh herbs.
Street vendors in Athens often grill souvlakia over open flames for hungry locals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kebab - skewered and grilled meat dish

Etymology

From Modern Greek σουβλάκι (souvláki), diminutive of σουβλά (souvlá, skewer).
