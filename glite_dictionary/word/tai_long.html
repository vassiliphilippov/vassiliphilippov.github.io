Tai Long

1. NOUN

/ taɪ lɔŋ / (AmE), / taɪ lɒŋ / (BrE)
a branch of the Tai languages spoken in Louangphabang Province, Laos.
Tai language variety in Laos

Examples

Linguists have documented the Tai Long language in Louangphabang Province of Laos.
Many people in that region speak Tai Long as their mother tongue.
The grammar of Tai Long shares similarities with other Tai languages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tai languages - a family of languages spoken in Southeast Asia, including Thai and Lao
