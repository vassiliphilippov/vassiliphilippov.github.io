outsize

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.saɪz / (BrE)
a garment size that is unusually large, especially larger than standard sizes
an unusually large clothing size

Examples

Stores often carry a limited range of outsize for customers who need larger clothing.
She ordered an outsize to ensure the jacket would fit comfortably over her sweater.
The shop specializes in outsize for both men and women.

2. ADJ

/ ˈaʊtˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.saɪz / (BrE)
much larger than usual or expected for its type or purpose
larger than normal for its type

Examples

He wore an outsize jacket that hung almost to his knees.
The outsize balloons floated above the parade, catching everyone’s attention.
They ordered an outsize pizza for the whole team to share.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oversized - exceptionally large; bigger than normal

Etymology

Formed from 'out' + 'size', indicating exceeding the usual size.
