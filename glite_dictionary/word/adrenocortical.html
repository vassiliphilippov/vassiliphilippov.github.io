adrenocortical

1. ADJ

/ əˌdriːnoʊˈkɔrtɪkəl / (AmE), / əˌdriːnəʊˈkɔːtɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or derived from the cortex of the adrenal glands.
of the adrenal gland cortex
medical

Examples

The doctor ordered tests to check for adrenocortical hormone levels in the blood.
Addison's disease is caused by insufficient adrenocortical activity.
Certain tumors can disrupt normal adrenocortical function.

Etymology

From adreno- (relating to the adrenal glands) + cortical (from cortex, the outer layer).
