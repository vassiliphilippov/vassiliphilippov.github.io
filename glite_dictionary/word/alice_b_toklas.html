Alice B. Toklas

1. PROPN

/ ˈælɪs bi ˈtoʊkləs / (AmE), / ˈælɪs bi ˈtəʊkləs / (BrE)
(1877–1967) American writer and lifelong companion of Gertrude Stein, known for her memoir and literary influence.
American writer, companion of Gertrude Stein

Examples

The autobiography of Alice B. Toklas is an important work in modernist literature.
Many found Alice B. Toklas's cookbook both charming and innovative.
Gertrude Stein and Alice B. Toklas were central figures in Paris's literary community.
