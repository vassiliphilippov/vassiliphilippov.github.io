draining

1. ADJ

/ ˈdreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing exhaustion, fatigue, or a feeling of being worn out; debilitating.
causing exhaustion or fatigue

Examples

Working long hours in the heat can be incredibly draining.
She found the constant travel both exciting and draining.
That meeting was so draining that I need a break.

Synset Examples

an exhausting job in the hot sun

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhausting - causing extreme tiredness
  • Antonym: invigorating - making one feel strong, healthy, and full of energy

Etymology

From the verb 'drain', meaning to cause the gradual loss of strength or energy.
