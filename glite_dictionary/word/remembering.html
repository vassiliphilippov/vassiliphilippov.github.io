remembering

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈmɛm.bɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈmɛm.bər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or process of recalling or retaining information or experiences from the past.
the act of recalling past experiences

Examples

Remembering the good times they shared always brought a smile to her face.
He spent the afternoon quietly remembering his childhood adventures with his brother.
For many people, remembering important dates is not an easy task.

Synset Examples

he can do it from memory
he enjoyed remembering his father

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recollection - the action of remembering or recalling something
  • Antonym: forgetting - the act of being unable to remember

Etymology

From the verb 'remember,' from Old French 'remembrer,' from Latin 'rememorari' (to recall to mind).
