careworn

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɛrˌwɔrn / (AmE), / ˈkɛəˌwɔːn / (BrE)
looking tired, anxious, or stressed as a result of worry or overwork
tired/stressed from worry or work

Examples

She looked careworn after weeks of worrying about her exams.
His careworn face revealed years of hardship and responsibility.
After dealing with so many problems, he became noticeably careworn.

Synset Examples

looking careworn as she bent over her mending
her face was drawn and haggard from sleeplessness
that raddled but still noble face
shocked to see the worn look of his handsome young face

Related Terms

  • Synonym: haggard - looking exhausted and unwell, especially from fatigue or worry
  • Antonym: fresh-faced - having a healthy, youthful, or innocent appearance

Etymology

Formed from 'care' + 'worn', indicating worn out by care or worry.
