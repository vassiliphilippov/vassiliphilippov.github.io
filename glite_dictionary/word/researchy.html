researchy

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈsɜrtʃi / (AmE), / rɪˈsɜːtʃi / (BrE)
having qualities or characteristics of academic research, often technical or overly scholarly in tone or style.
having an academic or technical style
informal

Examples

The team's latest report was much less researchy and easier for a general audience to understand.
He wanted the presentation to sound less researchy so everyone could follow along.
Could you make this summary less researchy and more readable for non-experts?

Synset Examples

I prefer articles that are less researchy and more engaging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: academic - relating to education or scholarship, formal in style
  • Antonym: casual - informal or relaxed in style or tone

Etymology

Formed from 'research' with informal adjectival '-y' suffix.
