unfearing

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
not experiencing fear or intimidation; showing no fear
not afraid; fearless

Examples

Despite the storm, the unfearing hikers pressed on toward the summit.
She faces every challenge with an unfearing heart and a determined mind.
His unfearing attitude inspired others to join the cause.

Synset Examples

audacious explorers
fearless reporters and photographers
intrepid pioneers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fearless - not afraid; lacking fear
  • Synonym: intrepid - very brave; showing great courage
  • Antonym: timid - easily frightened; showing a lack of courage

Etymology

From un- (not) + fearing (present participle of fear).
