oversexed

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈsɛkst / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈsɛkst / (BrE)
Having or showing excessive sexual desire or being perceived as excessively sexually attractive.
having excessive sexual desire or appeal

Examples

The novel features an oversexed character who constantly seeks new romantic adventures.
Some critics argue that films today often portray teenagers as oversexed and reckless.
He was unfairly labeled as oversexed simply because of his outgoing attitude.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: libidinous - having a strong sexual drive; lustful
  • Antonym: frigid - lacking sexual desire or responsiveness

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'over-' (too much) and 'sexed' (relating to sex).
