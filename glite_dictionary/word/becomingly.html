becomingly

1. ADV

/ bɪˈkʌmɪŋli / (AmE), / bɪˈkʌmɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is attractive or appropriate, especially with reference to appearance or dress.
in an attractive or appropriate way

Examples

She was becomingly dressed for the occasion, wearing a simple yet elegant gown.
He smiled becomingly, putting everyone at ease with his warmth and charm.
The living room was becomingly decorated with soft lighting and fresh flowers.

Synset Examples

she was becomingly dressed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unbecomingly - in a way that is inappropriate or unattractive

Etymology

From 'becoming' (suitable, attractive) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
