uncurled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkɝld / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɜːld / (BrE)
not curled; straightened or lacking any curl or coiled form
not curled; straightened

Examples

The snake lay uncurled in the sunlight, basking peacefully on the rock.
She prefers to see her hair uncurled and flowing straight down her back.
The ribbon was carefully uncurled before being tied around the gift box.

Synset Examples

lay uncurled on the bed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: curled - having a curved or coiled form

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'un-' (indicating reversal or absence) and 'curled.'

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkɝld / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɜːld / (BrE)
not curled; in a straight or uncoiled state.
not curled

Examples

The leaves had uncurled by morning, revealing their fresh green surface.
She prefers her hair uncurled and loose, letting it fall naturally.
Once the ribbon uncurled from the spool, it lay flat on the table.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: curled - in a curled or coiled state
