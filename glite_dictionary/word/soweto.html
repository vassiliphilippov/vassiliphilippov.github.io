Soweto

1. PROPN

/ soʊˈweɪtoʊ / (AmE), / səˈwɛtəʊ / (BrE)
A large urban area comprising several townships southwest of Johannesburg, South Africa, historically inhabited by Black Africans and known for its role in the anti-apartheid movement.
urban area near Johannesburg, South Africa

Examples

Many pivotal events in South Africa's struggle against apartheid took place in Soweto.
He grew up in Soweto and witnessed significant changes in the community over the years.
Tourists often visit Soweto to learn about its rich history and vibrant culture.

Etymology

A shortened form of 'South Western Townships.'
