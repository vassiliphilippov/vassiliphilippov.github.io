blue-blind

1. ADJ

/ bluːˌblaɪnd / (AmE), / bluːˌblaɪnd / (BrE)
Unable to see the color blue or to distinguish between blue and yellow, due to a visual impairment.
unable to see blue; blue-yellow color blindness
medical

Examples

People who are blue-blind may confuse blue and green objects.
The blue-blind condition is a rare form of color vision deficiency.
Her grandfather was blue-blind and found it hard to tell blue from yellow.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: color-blind - unable to distinguish some or all colors
  • Synonym: blue-yellow color blindness - color blindness affecting blue and yellow discrimination
