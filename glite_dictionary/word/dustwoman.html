dustwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌstˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈdʌstˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to collect and dispose of household refuse
female refuse collector

Examples

The dustwoman arrived early in the morning to collect the bins.
In her role as a dustwoman, she worked alongside the local waste management team.
Many people are unaware of the challenges faced by a dustwoman in a busy city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dustman - a man employed to collect and dispose of refuse

Etymology

Formed by analogy to 'dustman', combining 'dust' (refuse) and 'woman'.
