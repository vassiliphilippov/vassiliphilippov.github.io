scrappy

1. ADJ

/ ˈskræpi / (AmE), / ˈskræpi / (BrE)
having a determined, aggressive, or feisty character; full of fighting spirit.
full of fighting spirit

Examples

She may be small, but she's extremely scrappy when it comes to defending her friends.
The team was scrappy and never gave up, even when they were losing.
Despite his size, the scrappy boxer surprised everyone with his determination.

Synset Examples

a scrappy admiral

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feisty - lively, determined, and courageous
  • Antonym: timid - lacking in courage or self-confidence
