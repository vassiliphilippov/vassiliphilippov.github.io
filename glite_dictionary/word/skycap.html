skycap

1. NOUN

/ ˈskaɪˌkæp / (AmE), / ˈskaɪˌkæp / (BrE)
a porter at an airport who assists passengers with their baggage
airport baggage porter
american

Examples

The skycap greeted us at the curb and loaded our suitcases onto a cart.
We tipped the skycap for helping us with our heavy bags.
If you're running late for your flight, a skycap can speed up your check-in process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: porter - a person employed to carry luggage and other loads

Etymology

From 'sky' (as in aviation) + 'cap' (originally referring to the porter's cap).
