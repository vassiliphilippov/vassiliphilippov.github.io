lamented

1. ADJ

/ ləˈmɛntɪd / (AmE), / ləˈmɛntɪd / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) deeply missed, mourned, or grieved for after loss or death, often used in formal or respectful mention.
mourned or sadly missed

Examples

The lamented author was remembered fondly by his readers at the memorial service.
They named the scholarship after their lamented teacher, who inspired many students.
His lamented passing left the entire community in mourning.

Synset Examples

the imprint of our wise and lamented friend

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mourned - deeply grieved for or missed after death or loss
  • Antonym: forgotten - not remembered or missed

Etymology

From the verb 'lament' (to express grief), from Latin *lamentari* (to wail, bewail).
