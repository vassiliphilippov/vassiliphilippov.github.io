endomorphic

1. ADJ

/ ˌen.doʊˈmɔr.fɪk / (AmE), / ˌen.dəʊˈmɔː.fɪk / (BrE)
having a soft, round body build characterized by a high proportion of body fat; relating to or denoting a physical type with a squat and fleshy build
having a soft, rounded body build
medical

Examples

He has an endomorphic physique, making it difficult for him to stay lean despite regular exercise.
The coach designed a special training routine to help endomorphic players improve their agility.
Certain health risks are more common among people with an endomorphic body type.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ectomorphic - having a lean and slender body build
  • Antonym: mesomorphic - having a muscular or athletic body build

Etymology

From Greek 'endo-' (within) + 'morphē' (form, shape).
