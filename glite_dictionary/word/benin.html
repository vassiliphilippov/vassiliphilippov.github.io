Benin

1. PROPN

/ bəˈniːn / (AmE), / bəˈniːn / (BrE)
a country in West Africa, formerly a French colony, located on the Gulf of Guinea
country in West Africa

Examples

She spent a year teaching English in Benin.
The capital city of Benin is Porto-Novo, but the seat of government is in Cotonou.
Historical sites in Benin attract visitors interested in West African culture.

Etymology

From the Benin River, named after the historical Kingdom of Benin; unrelated to modern Nigeria's Benin City.
