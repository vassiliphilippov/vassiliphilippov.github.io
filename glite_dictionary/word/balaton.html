Balaton

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɑːlətɒn / (AmE), / ˈbælətɒn / (BrE)
a large, shallow lake in western Hungary, popular as a holiday destination
large lake in western Hungary

Examples

Many tourists visit Balaton each summer for its scenic beaches and resorts.
The region around Balaton is famous for its vineyards and thermal springs.
Sailing on Balaton is a favorite activity among both locals and visitors.
