uncomprehended

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌkɑmprɪˈhɛndɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌkɒmprɪˈhɛndɪd / (BrE)
not fully understood or grasped
not fully understood

Examples

The motives behind the artist's work remained uncomprehended by many critics.
Much of the universe is still largely uncomprehended by scientists.
She felt surrounded by uncomprehended emotions and confusing sensations.

Synset Examples

an uncomprehended mystery

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incomprehensible - impossible or very difficult to understand
  • Antonym: understood - fully grasped or comprehended

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'comprehended' (past participle of comprehend).
