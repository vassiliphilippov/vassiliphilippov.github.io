pubic

1. ADJ

/ ˈpjuːbɪk / (AmE), / ˈpjuːbɪk / (BrE)
relating to or situated near the pubis, the front part of the pelvic bone
relating to the pubis (pelvic bone)

Examples

The pubic bones form the front part of the pelvis.
She was worried about irritation in her pubic area after shaving.
Doctors discussed the role of pubic hair in protecting the skin from friction.

Synset Examples

pubic bones
pubic hair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pelvic - relating to the pelvis

Etymology

From Latin pubicus (pertaining to the pubes or pubic region).
