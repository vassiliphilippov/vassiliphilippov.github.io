unquestioningly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈkwɛstʃənɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwɛstʃənɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows no doubt or hesitation; without questioning or challenging.
without doubt or questioning

Examples

She obeyed the rules unquestioningly, never once doubting their purpose.
He accepted his supervisor's decision unquestioningly, even though he disagreed.
The loyal dog followed its owner unquestioningly through the crowded park.

Synset Examples

he followed his leader unquestioningly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unquestioning - not doubting or challenging something
  • Antonym: doubtfully - in a manner that shows doubt or uncertainty
