liveliness-osity

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪvlinɪsˌɑsəti / (AmE), / ˈlaɪvlɪnɪsˌɒsɪti / (BrE)
A humorous or playful term for liveliness or spiritedness; the quality of being full of life or energy.
playful term for liveliness
informal

Examples

The festival had an infectious liveliness-osity that made everyone want to dance.
Her performance brought a unique liveliness-osity to the otherwise dull meeting.
You could feel the liveliness-osity of the market on a Saturday morning.

Synset Examples

The city's vibrancy reflects its liveliness-osity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liveliness - the quality of being lively or full of life
  • Synonym: vitality - the state of being strong and full of energy

Etymology

Humorous blend of 'liveliness' and the suffix '-osity' as in 'curiosity'.
