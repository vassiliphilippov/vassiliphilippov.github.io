sideway

1. ADV

/ ˈsaɪdˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪd.weɪ / (BrE)
In a direction from the side; obliquely.
obliquely; from the side

Examples

The sunlight came in sideway through the open window, casting long shadows on the floor.
The artist painted the subject sideway to create a dramatic effect.
She glanced sideway at him, unsure of what he was thinking.

Synset Examples

a picture lit sideways
scenes viewed sidewise

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sideways - in a direction to or from the side

2. ADV

/ ˈsaɪdˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪd.weɪ / (BrE)
With one side forward or to the front; in a sideways direction or manner.
with one side forward; sideways

Examples

He glanced sideway at me before answering the question.
The crab moves sideway across the sand to avoid predators.
Without warning, the car skidded sideway on the icy road.

Synset Examples

turned sideways to show the profile
crabs seeming to walk sidewise

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sideways - with one side forward; in a sideways direction
  • Synonym: sidewise - with one side forward or to the front; in a sideways direction
