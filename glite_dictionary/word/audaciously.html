audaciously

1. ADV

/ ɔːˈdeɪʃəsli / (AmE), / ɔːˈdeɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a willingness to take bold risks; boldly or daringly.
in a bold or daring manner

Examples

The young artist audaciously challenged traditional styles in her first major exhibition.
He audaciously ignored the warnings and proceeded with his plan.
She audaciously asked the CEO for a meeting to pitch her idea.

Synset Examples

an idea so daring and yet so audaciously tempting that a shiver of excitement quivered through him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boldly - in a bold or courageous manner
  • Antonym: cautiously - in a careful or wary manner

Etymology

From 'audacious' + '-ly'. 'Audacious' from Latin 'audacia' (boldness, daring).
