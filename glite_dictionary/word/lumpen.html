lumpen

1. ADJ

/ ˈlʌmpən / (AmE), / ˈlʌmpən / (BrE)
(of a person or their behavior) mentally sluggish, unintelligent, or crude.
mentally sluggish or crude

Examples

The candidate’s lumpen responses to every question did little to inspire confidence.
His lumpen way of thinking made the complex discussion nearly impossible for him to follow.
Throughout the film, the character remains a lumpen figure, oblivious to the world around him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dull - lacking interest or intelligence; slow to understand
  • Synonym: stupid - lacking intelligence or common sense

Etymology

From German Lumpenproletariat, where 'Lumpen' means 'ragged' or 'unrefined'.
