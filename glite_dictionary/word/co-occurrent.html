co-occurrent

1. ADJ

/ koʊ əˈkɜːrənt / (AmE), / kəʊ əˈkʌrənt / (BrE)
Occurring or existing at the same time as something else; coincident.
happening at the same time as something else

Examples

The study found several co-occurrent symptoms among patients with the disease.
Rainfall and lightning are often co-occurrent during summer storms.
Linguists analyze the co-occurrent use of words in spoken language.

Synset Examples

a series of coincident events

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coincident - existing or happening at the same time
  • Synonym: simultaneous - occurring or done at the same time

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'co-' (together) and 'occurrent' (from Latin 'occurrere', to happen, befall).
