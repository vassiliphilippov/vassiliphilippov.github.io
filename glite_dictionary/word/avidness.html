avidness

1. NOUN

/ ˈævɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈævɪdnəs / (BrE)
keen enthusiasm or eagerness for something; the quality of being very enthusiastic or passionate about a pursuit or goal.
enthusiastic eagerness or passion

Examples

She attacked her new role with remarkable avidness, impressing everyone on the team.
The avidness with which he pursues knowledge is truly inspiring.
Despite setbacks, her avidness for success never diminished.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enthusiasm - intense and eager enjoyment or interest
  • Synonym: eagerness - keen or enthusiastic desire
  • Antonym: apathy - lack of interest or enthusiasm

Etymology

From 'avid' (keen, eager) + -ness; 'avid' from Latin avidus (eager, greedy).
