wirewoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪərˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female worker who installs and repairs electric wiring
female installer/repairer of electric wiring

Examples

The company hired a skilled wirewoman to complete the electrical work on the site.
Becoming a wirewoman requires both technical expertise and hands-on training.
The experienced wirewoman quickly diagnosed and fixed the faulty wiring in the building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wireman - male or generic worker who installs and repairs electric wiring
  • Synonym: electrician - person who installs and repairs electrical systems

Etymology

From 'wireman' (electrician specializing in wiring), formed with 'woman' as female equivalent.
