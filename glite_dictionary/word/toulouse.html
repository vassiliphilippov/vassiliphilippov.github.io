Toulouse

1. PROPN

/ tuˈluːz / (AmE), / tuːˈluːz / (BrE)
a city on the Garonne River in southern France, known for its rich history and as a cultural center of medieval Europe
city in southern France

Examples

The aerospace industry has become a major employer in Toulouse over the past decades.
Toulouse is famous for its vibrant pink terracotta buildings, giving it the nickname 'La Ville Rose'.
During the Middle Ages, Toulouse was a prominent center of cultural and academic life.

Etymology

From French Toulouse, from Latin Tolosa.
