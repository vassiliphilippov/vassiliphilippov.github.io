to hygienise

1. VERB

/ haɪˈdʒinˌaɪz / (AmE), / haɪˈdʒiːnʌɪz / (BrE)
to make sanitary by cleaning or sterilizing
make sanitary by cleaning or sterilizing

Examples

All laboratory equipment must be hygienised before use to prevent contamination.
The staff were instructed to hygienise the area after every patient.
Properly hygienising your hands helps reduce the spread of germs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sanitize - to make clean and hygienic

Etymology

From hygiene + -ise, formed by analogy to 'sanitize'.
