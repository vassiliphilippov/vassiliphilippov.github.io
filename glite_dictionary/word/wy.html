WY

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʌbəl.juː ˈwaɪ / (AmE), / ˌdʌbəl.juː ˈwaɪ / (BrE)
The standard postal abbreviation for Wyoming, a state in the western United States.
abbreviation for Wyoming (U.S. state)
american

Examples

Her family's ranch is located in WY near the foothills of the Rockies.
The package was sent to Cheyenne, WY, last Monday.
License plates from WY are sometimes hard to spot on the East Coast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Wyoming - a western U.S. state; mountainous in the west and north

Etymology

Formed from the initial letters of 'Wyoming', following U.S. postal abbreviation conventions.
