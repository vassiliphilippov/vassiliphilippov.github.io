Kenalog

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛn.ə.lɔg / (AmE), / ˈkɛn.əlɒg / (BrE)
a trademarked synthetic corticosteroid drug used as an anti-inflammatory, often prescribed for allergic and autoimmune conditions
