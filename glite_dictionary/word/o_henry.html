O. Henry

1. PROPN

/ ˌoʊ ˈhɛnri / (AmE), / ˌəʊ ˈhɛnri / (BrE)
the pen name of William Sydney Porter (1862–1910), an American writer known for his witty short stories, often with surprise endings.
American short story writer's pen name

Examples

The stories of O. Henry are famous for their clever twists and unexpected endings.
Many literature classes study O. Henry for his unique storytelling style.
I read an anthology of O. Henry tales during my vacation.

Etymology

Pen name adopted by William Sydney Porter.
