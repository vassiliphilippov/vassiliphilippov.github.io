segno

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪn.joʊ / (AmE), / ˈseɪn.jəʊ / (BrE)
(music) a sign used in musical notation to indicate the beginning or end of a section to be repeated.
musical repeat sign

Examples

In the sheet music, the conductor asked us to return to the segno after finishing the chorus.
Please look for the segno symbol so you know where the repeat begins.
The segno is marked just before the solo section to guide performers.

Etymology

From Italian 'segno' meaning 'sign', from Latin 'signum'.
