crinkled

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɪŋkəld / (AmE), / ˈkrɪŋkəld / (BrE)
having small wrinkles or waves on the surface; uneven and slightly creased or wavy.
having small wrinkles or waves

Examples

She ran her fingers over the crinkled paper, smoothing out the tiny ridges.
The crinkled leaves rustled in the autumn breeze.
His eyes lit up as he smiled, creases forming in his crinkled face.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrinkled - having small folds or ridges on the surface
  • Antonym: smooth - having an even and flat surface without wrinkles or waves

Etymology

Past participle of 'crinkle', from Middle English crinklen, of imitative origin.
