fragrant

1. ADJ

/ ˈfreɪɡrənt / (AmE), / ˈfreɪɡrənt / (BrE)
Having a pleasant or sweet smell.
having a pleasant smell

Examples

The fragrant flowers filled the garden with a delightful aroma.
She lit a fragrant candle to make the room smell fresh.
A fragrant breeze drifted through the open window, carrying the scent of jasmine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aromatic - having a noticeable and pleasant smell
  • Synonym: scented - having a fragrant aroma, usually due to added perfume or natural oils
  • Antonym: odorless - having no smell

Etymology

From Latin 'fragrans', present participle of 'fragrare' (to smell sweet).
