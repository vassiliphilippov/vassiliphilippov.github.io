spelling

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
The process or activity of forming words with letters in the correct and accepted sequence.
forming words with letters correctly

Examples

Her spelling has improved significantly since last year.
Contestants in the bee were judged on the accuracy of their spelling.
He won first prize for his flawless spelling of the word 'encyclopedia'.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orthography - the conventional spelling system of a language
  • Antonym: misspelling - incorrect spelling of a word

Etymology

From the verb 'spell', from Middle English 'spellen', from Old French 'espeller', of Germanic origin.
