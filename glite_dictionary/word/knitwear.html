knitwear

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪtwɛr / (AmE), / ˈnɪtwɛə / (BrE)
Clothing that is made by knitting, especially items such as sweaters, cardigans, and scarves.
clothing made by knitting

Examples

She designs her own knitwear for winter collections.
The store has a large selection of handmade knitwear from local artisans.
Woollen knitwear is perfect for keeping warm during cold weather.

Etymology

From 'knit' (to make fabric by interlacing yarn) + 'wear' (clothing).
