urogenital

1. ADJ

/ ˌjʊroʊˈdʒɛnɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəʊˈdʒɛnɪtəl / (BrE)
relating to or affecting both the urinary and reproductive organs or systems.
relating to urinary and reproductive systems
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that the infection was located in the urogenital tract.
Many urogenital disorders require specialized treatment from both urologists and gynecologists.
They studied urogenital development as part of their medical training.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genitourinary - relating to the genital and urinary organs

Etymology

From uro- (urinary) + genital (relating to reproduction).
