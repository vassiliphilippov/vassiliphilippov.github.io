Tegucigalpa

1. PROPN

/ təˌɡuːsɪˈɡɑːlpə / (AmE), / təˌɡuːsɪˈɡælpə / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Honduras
capital of Honduras

Examples

During her trip to Honduras, Maria spent several days exploring Tegucigalpa.
Tegucigalpa is known for its mountainous terrain and bustling city life.
The embassy is located in the heart of Tegucigalpa.

Etymology

From Nahuatl *Taguz-galpa*, meaning 'silver hills'.
