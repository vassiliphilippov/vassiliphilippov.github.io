intelligibly

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəbli / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is clear and easy to understand; in an intelligible manner
in a clear, understandable way

Examples

Despite his accent, he explained the process very intelligibly.
The instructions were written so intelligibly that anyone could follow them.
She managed to speak intelligibly after her surgery, much to everyone's relief.

Synset Examples

the foreigner spoke to us quite intelligibly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clearly - in a clear manner
  • Antonym: incomprehensibly - in a way that cannot be understood

Etymology

From Latin 'intelligibilis' (that can be understood), from 'intelligere' (to understand).
