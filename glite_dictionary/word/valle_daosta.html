Valle d'Aosta

1. PROPN

/ ˌvæleɪ dəˈɔstə / (AmE), / ˌvalɛ daɪˈɒstə / (BrE)
an autonomous region in northwestern Italy, known for its Alpine landscapes and bilingual Italian-French culture
region of northwestern Italy

Examples

We spent our vacation hiking in Valle d'Aosta, surrounded by beautiful mountain scenery.
The cuisine of Valle d'Aosta reflects both Italian and French influences.
Every winter, Valle d'Aosta attracts skiers from all over Europe.

Etymology

From Italian 'Valle d'Aosta' meaning 'Valley of Aosta'.
