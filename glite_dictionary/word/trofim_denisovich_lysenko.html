Trofim Denisovich Lysenko

1. PROPN

/ troʊˈfiːm dəˈniːsəvɪtʃ laɪˈsɛŋkoʊ / (AmE), / trəʊˈfiːm dɛˈniːsəvɪtʃ laɪˈsɛŋkəʊ / (BrE)
(1898–1976) A Soviet biologist and agronomist known for promoting Lamarckian inheritance and rejecting Mendelian genetics, influencing Soviet science and agriculture under Stalin.
Soviet biologist and agronomist (1898–1976)

Examples

The policies of Trofim Denisovich Lysenko had a lasting impact on Soviet agriculture.
Many scientists were persecuted for opposing Trofim Denisovich Lysenko's unorthodox views on genetics.
The legacy of Trofim Denisovich Lysenko remains controversial among historians of science.
