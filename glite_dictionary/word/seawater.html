seawater

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
water from the sea or ocean that contains dissolved salts, especially sodium chloride
water from the sea or ocean

Examples

She collected a bottle of seawater for her science experiment.
Many marine animals rely on seawater for their survival.
The desalination plant removes salt from seawater to make it drinkable.

Synset Examples

the water in the ocean is all saltwater

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saltwater - water that contains dissolved salts, especially from the sea
  • Antonym: freshwater - water that is not salty and found in rivers, lakes, or streams
