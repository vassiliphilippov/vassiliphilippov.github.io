flaring

1. ADJ

/ ˈflɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfleərɪŋ / (BrE)
streaming, flapping, or spreading wide as if in a current of air
spreading or streaming out wide

Examples

The dancer's flaring skirt spun around her as she twirled on stage.
His coat was flaring out behind him as he ran through the hallway.
Rows of flaring banners lined the street for the festival.

Synset Examples

ran quickly, her flaring coat behind her
flags aflare in the breeze

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fluttering - moving quickly or lightly through the air
