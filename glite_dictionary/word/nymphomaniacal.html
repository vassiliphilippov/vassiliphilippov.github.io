nymphomaniacal

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɪmfoʊməˈnaɪəkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɪmfəʊməˈnaɪəkəl / (BrE)
(of a woman) having or characterized by excessive or uncontrollable sexual desire.
excessively sexually desirous (women)

Examples

Her nymphomaniacal behavior became the subject of gossip throughout the small town.
The novel features a nymphomaniacal character who is constantly seeking attention from men.
Critics described the film as portraying its female lead in a nymphomaniacal manner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nymphomaniac - a woman with excessive sexual desire
  • Antonym: satyriatic - affected with excessive sexual desire (of men)

Etymology

Formed from 'nymphomania' (from Greek nymphē ‘bride’ + mania ‘madness’) + '-acal', an adjective suffix.
