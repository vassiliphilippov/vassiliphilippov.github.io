coatroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊtˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtˌruːm / (BrE)
a room in a public building where people can temporarily leave their coats and other belongings
room for leaving coats temporarily

Examples

Please leave your umbrella and coat in the coatroom before entering the hall.
The museum's coatroom was crowded on the rainy afternoon.
She handed her jacket to the attendant at the coatroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cloakroom - room in a public building for leaving coats and belongings
