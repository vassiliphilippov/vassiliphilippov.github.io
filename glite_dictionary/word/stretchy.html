stretchy

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrɛtʃi / (AmE), / ˈstrɛtʃi / (BrE)
Able to be easily stretched and to return to its original shape or size.
capable of stretching and resuming shape

Examples

These yoga pants are made from a stretchy fabric that moves with you.
The bandage is stretchy, so it fits snugly around your arm.
She prefers stretchy jeans for comfort during long days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elastic - able to resume original shape after stretching
  • Antonym: rigid - not able to be bent or stretched
