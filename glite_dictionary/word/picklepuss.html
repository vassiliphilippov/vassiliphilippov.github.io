picklepuss

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪkəlˌpʊs / (AmE), / ˈpɪkəlˌpʊs / (BrE)
a person who habitually has a sullen or gloomy expression
person with a habitually gloomy expression
informal

Examples

No one expects a smile from old picklepuss in the morning.
Don't be such a picklepuss—try to enjoy the party!
Her picklepuss demeanor made her seem unapproachable to new colleagues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grouch - person who complains or is often dissatisfied
  • Synonym: grump - person who is easily annoyed or in a bad mood

Etymology

Blend of 'pickle' (perhaps from the sourness of a pickle) and 'puss' (slang for face).
