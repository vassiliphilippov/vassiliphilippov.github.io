energetic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛnəˈdʒɛtɪk / (BrE)
having or showing a lot of energy; vigorous and active.
having or showing much energy

Examples

Despite her age, she remained energetic and took long walks every morning.
The energetic child ran around the playground laughing and shouting.
They launched an energetic campaign to raise money for charity.

Synset Examples

an energetic fund raiser for the college
an energetic group of hikers
it caused an energetic chemical reaction

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vigorous - strong, healthy, and energetic
  • Synonym: lively - full of life and energy
  • Antonym: lethargic - sluggish, lacking energy

Etymology

From Greek energētikos (active), from energein (to be active).
