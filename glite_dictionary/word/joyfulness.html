joyfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɔɪ.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪ.fəl.nəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being full of joy; great happiness
great happiness; state of being joyful

Examples

Her joyfulness was evident when she received the good news.
The children's joyfulness filled the classroom with laughter and excitement.
Despite the challenges, he maintained a sense of joyfulness throughout the day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: happiness - the state of being happy
  • Antonym: sadness - the state of being sad; sorrow

Etymology

From 'joyful' + '-ness'; 'joy' from Old French 'joie', from Latin 'gaudia'.
