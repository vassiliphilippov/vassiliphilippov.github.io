pining

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
A feeling of deep, often melancholic longing for someone or something.
deep longing or yearning

Examples

Her silent pining for her childhood home was evident in her letters.
He tried to hide his pining, but his friends saw the sadness in his eyes.
Throughout the long winter, a constant pining filled her heart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yearning - an intense feeling of desire or longing
  • Synonym: longing - a strong feeling of wanting something
  • Antonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern

Etymology

From the verb 'pine,' which comes from Old English 'pīnian' (to torture, cause to suffer), related to Latin 'poena' (punishment).
