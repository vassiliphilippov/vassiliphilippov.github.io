Twitter

1. PROPN

/ ˈtwɪd̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈtwɪtə / (BrE)
A social media platform where users post and interact with short messages called tweets, originally limited to 140 characters.
short-message social media platform

Examples

She shares news updates on Twitter almost every day.
Many celebrities use Twitter to connect with their fans directly.
Companies often announce new products through their official Twitter accounts.
