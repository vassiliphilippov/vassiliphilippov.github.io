wiggliness

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪɡl̩inəs / (AmE), / ˈwɪɡlɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of moving or being moved with quick, small, or jerky movements from side to side or back and forth
quality of being wiggly

Examples

The wiggliness of the puppy made it difficult to hold during the vet visit.
Her drawing had a certain wiggliness that gave it a playful charm.
Trying to balance on the log, he couldn't control the wiggliness of his legs.

Synset Examples

he walked with the wiggliness of a child on high heels

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wiggly - moving with quick, twisting movements
  • Antonym: rigidity - the quality of being stiff and unmoving
