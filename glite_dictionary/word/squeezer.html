squeezer

1. NOUN

/ ˈskwizər / (AmE), / ˈskwiːzə / (BrE)
A kitchen utensil used to extract juice from fruit by pressing or squeezing.
utensil for extracting juice from fruit

Examples

She used a squeezer to get every last drop of lemon juice for the recipe.
Do you have a squeezer for making fresh orange juice in the morning?
The squeezer made preparing cocktails much easier.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: juicer - a device for extracting juice from fruit or vegetables
