Charleroi

1. PROPN

/ ʃɑr.ləˈrɔɪ / (AmE), / ʃɑː.ləˈrɔɪ / (BrE)
A city in southwestern Belgium known for its industrial heritage and history as a center of manufacturing.
city in southwestern Belgium

Examples

The train from Brussels to Charleroi takes about an hour.
Many tourists visit Charleroi to explore its fascinating industrial architecture.
After years of decline, Charleroi is reinventing itself as a hub for art and innovation.

Etymology

From French, named after Charles II of Spain ('Charleroi' meaning 'Charles the King').
