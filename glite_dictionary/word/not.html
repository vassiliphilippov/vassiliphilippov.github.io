not

1. PART

/ nɑt / (AmE), / nɒt / (BrE)
used to make a word, statement, or group of words negative
negation word

Examples

He does not like spicy food, so we ordered something mild.
The package has not arrived yet, but it should come tomorrow.
You should not worry about making a small mistake.

Synset Examples

he does not speak French
she is not going
they are not friends
not many
not much
not at all

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no - word expressing negation or refusal
  • Degree Higher: never - at no time; not ever

Etymology

From Old English *nāt*, contraction of *ne* (not) + *ā* (ever), influenced by Old English *nōt*; ultimately from Proto-Germanic.
