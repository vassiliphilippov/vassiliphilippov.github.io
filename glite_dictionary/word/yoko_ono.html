Yoko Ono

1. PROPN

/ ˈjoʊkoʊ ˈoʊnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈjəʊkəʊ ˈəʊnəʊ / (BrE)
(1933–) Japanese-born American artist, musician, and peace activist, known for her avant-garde work and marriage to John Lennon.
Japanese-born American artist and musician

Examples

Yoko Ono has produced numerous albums exploring experimental music and sound art.
The work of Yoko Ono often challenges traditional perceptions of art and performance.
Many people first learned about Yoko Ono through her collaboration with John Lennon.
