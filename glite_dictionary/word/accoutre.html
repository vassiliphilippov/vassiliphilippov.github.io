to accoutre

1. VERB

/ əˈkuːtər / (AmE), / əˈkuːtə / (BrE)
to provide with equipment, especially for military or special use
equip with gear, especially military

Examples

The officer was accoutred with a sword and pistol as he entered the parade ground.
Before battle, the soldiers were accoutred in full armor and helmets.
They accoutred the expedition team with high-tech gear for the journey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to equip - to supply with necessary items for a particular purpose

Etymology

From French 'accoutrer' (to equip, dress), from Old French 'acoutrer'.
