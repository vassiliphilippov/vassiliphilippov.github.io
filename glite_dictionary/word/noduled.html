noduled

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɑːdʒuld / (AmE), / ˈnɒd.juːld / (BrE)
Having nodules or occurring in the form of nodules.
having or forming nodules

Examples

The roots of the plant were noduled due to a beneficial bacterial infection.
Geologists discovered noduled mineral deposits scattered throughout the hillside.
After years underground, the pipe's surface became rough and noduled.

Synset Examples

nodular ores

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nodular - having small rounded lumps or nodules

Etymology

From 'nodule' + suffix '-ed', indicating possession of nodules.
