clamminess

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæmi.nɪs / (AmE), / ˈklæmɪnɪs / (BrE)
an unpleasant, damp and sticky feeling, often referring to the skin
unpleasant dampness or moisture

Examples

The clamminess of his hands made her uneasy during the handshake.
A sudden clamminess on her forehead warned her she might be falling ill.
He shivered, feeling the clamminess in the air despite the warmth of the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dampness - slight wetness
  • Antonym: dryness - the state or quality of being dry

Etymology

From the adjective 'clammy' plus the noun suffix '-ness'.
