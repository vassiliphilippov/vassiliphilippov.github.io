Oakley

1. PROPN

/ ˈoʊkli / (AmE), / ˈəʊkli / (BrE)
Annie Oakley (1860–1926), an American sharpshooter and exhibition shooter famous for her performances in Buffalo Bill's Wild West Show.
Annie Oakley, American sharpshooter (1860–1926)

Examples

Oakley's skill with a rifle quickly made her a star in Buffalo Bill's Wild West Show.
Many people consider Oakley to be one of the greatest sharpshooters in American history.
The life of Oakley inspired numerous books and films celebrating her remarkable achievements.
