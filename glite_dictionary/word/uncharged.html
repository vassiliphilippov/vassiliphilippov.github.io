uncharged

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtʃɑrdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɑːdʒd / (BrE)
not carrying an electric charge; having no net electrical charge.
having no electric charge

Examples

Neutrons are uncharged particles found in the nucleus of an atom.
The device stopped working because its battery was completely uncharged.
Distilled water is typically composed of uncharged molecules.

Synset Examples

an uncharged particle
an uncharged battery

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neutral - having no net electric charge
