communicational

1. ADJ

/ kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or used in communication, especially as a formal term in technical or academic contexts.
relating to communication (formal)

Examples

The team's success depended on effective communicational strategies in all departments.
They studied the communicational patterns between doctors and patients.
Advances in technology have transformed our communicational abilities in the workplace.

Synset Examples

he had few communicational grooves available for use

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communicative - willing or able to communicate readily

Etymology

From 'communication' + '-al', patterned after similar English adjectives.
