undistributed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɪˈstrɪbjətɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈstrɪbjʊtɪd / (BrE)
(of investments or assets) not having been distributed or allocated among a number of recipients or categories.
not distributed or allocated

Examples

The earnings remained undistributed at the end of the fiscal year.
These assets are currently undistributed and will be allocated to shareholders next quarter.
He advised against keeping investments undistributed for a long period.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: distributed - having been given out or spread among recipients or categories

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'distributed', from Latin 'distributus' (divided, allotted).
