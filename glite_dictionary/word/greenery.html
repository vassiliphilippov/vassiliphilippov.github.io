greenery

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrinəri / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnəri / (BrE)
Green plants or foliage, especially when used for decoration.
green plants or foliage

Examples

The room was filled with greenery to create a fresh and lively atmosphere.
They decorated the wedding venue with flowers and greenery.
After the rain, the greenery in the park looked especially vibrant.
