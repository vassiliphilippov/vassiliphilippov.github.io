lustfully

1. ADV

/ ˈlʌst.fəli / (AmE), / ˈlʌst.fʊli / (BrE)
In a way that shows strong sexual desire.
in a sexually desirous manner

Examples

He gazed lustfully at the actress as she crossed the stage.
The villain laughed lustfully as he revealed his plan.
She was annoyed by the way he stared at her lustfully during the dinner.

Synset Examples

he looked at the young woman lustfully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lustful - having or showing strong sexual desire
  • Antonym: chastely - in a way that does not show sexual desire
