bolivar

1. NOUN

/ boʊˈliːvɑr / (AmE), / bəˈliːvɑː / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Venezuela, equal to 100 centimos.
currency unit of Venezuela

Examples

The price of coffee has increased to 30,000 bolivars per kilogram.
She exchanged her dollars for bolivars at the airport in Caracas.
Due to inflation, the value of the bolivar has declined sharply in recent years.

Etymology

Named after Simón Bolívar, South American liberator.
