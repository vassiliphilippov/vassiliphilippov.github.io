Barish

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːrɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːrɪʃ / (BrE)
a group of Kamarupan languages spoken in the state of Assam in northeastern India
a group of languages of Assam, India

Examples

Researchers have documented the unique features of the Barish languages in Assam.
The linguistic diversity of Barish communities reflects the region’s rich heritage.
Few people outside northeastern India are familiar with the Barish language group.
