clitoris

1. NOUN

/ klɪˈtɔr.ɪs / (AmE), / klɪˈtɒr.ɪs / (BrE)
a small, sensitive erectile part of the female genitals, homologous to the penis in males
female sexual organ

Examples

The clitoris contains thousands of nerve endings, making it highly sensitive.
Doctors are becoming more aware of the importance of the clitoris in sexual health.
Educational materials increasingly include diagrams showing the location of the clitoris.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: penis - male sexual organ homologous to the clitoris

Etymology

From Greek kleitoris (κλειτορίς), originally of uncertain origin.
