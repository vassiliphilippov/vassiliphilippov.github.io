Aberdonian

1. ADJ

/ ˌæbərˈdoʊniən / (AmE), / ˌæbəˈdəʊniən / (BrE)
of or relating to Aberdeen, a city in northeast Scotland, or its inhabitants.
of or relating to Aberdeen, Scotland

Examples

The Aberdonian landscape is famous for its granite architecture and rugged coastline.
She took an interest in Aberdonian history while studying in Scotland.
Many Aberdonian traditions are celebrated during local festivals.

Etymology

From Aberdeen (city in Scotland) + -ian (suffix forming adjectives and nouns).
