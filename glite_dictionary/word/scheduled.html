scheduled

1. ADJ

/ ˈskɛdʒ.uːld / (AmE), / ˈʃed.juːld / (BrE)
planned or arranged to happen at a particular time or times
arranged to occur at a set time

Examples

The scheduled performance will begin at 7 p.m. sharp.
All scheduled classes were moved online due to the weather.
There was a delay in the scheduled maintenance of the system.

Synset Examples

the scheduled meeting
the scheduled flights had to be cancelled because of snow

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unscheduled - not planned or arranged for a particular time
  • Synonym: planned - decided in advance, arranged before needed
