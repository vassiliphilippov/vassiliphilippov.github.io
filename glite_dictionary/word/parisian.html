Parisian

1. NOUN

/ pəˈrɪʒən / (AmE), / pəˈrɪz.i.ən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Paris, the capital city of France.
native or resident of Paris

Examples

The Parisian greeted us with a warm smile at the café.
Many Parisians enjoy strolling along the Seine on weekends.
Although he lives abroad now, he still considers himself a true Parisian.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Paris - the capital city of France

Etymology

From French 'Parisien', from 'Paris', the capital of France.
