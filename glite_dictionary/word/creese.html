creese

1. NOUN

/ kris / (AmE), / kriÀês / (BrE)
A Malayan dagger with a typically wavy blade, used as a weapon or ceremonial object.
Malayan dagger with a wavy blade

Examples

The museum displayed an ornate creese with an ivory handle in its Southeast Asian weapons collection.
He bought a creese as a souvenir during his trip to Malaysia.
The warrior drew his creese before entering the ceremonial dance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kris - A dagger from Southeast Asia, especially Malaysia and Indonesia, with a distinctive wavy blade

Etymology

From Malay 'keris', referring to a traditional dagger.
