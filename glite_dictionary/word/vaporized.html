vaporized

1. ADJ

/ ˈveɪpɚˌaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈveɪpəraɪzd / (BrE)
converted into a gas or vapor, especially by heating or evaporation.
converted into a gas or vapor

Examples

The vaporized liquid quickly filled the room with a faint mist.
After heating, the chemicals were completely vaporized and left no residue behind.
He inhaled the vaporized medicine through a special mask prescribed by his doctor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to vaporize - to convert or be converted into vapor

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'vaporize', from Latin 'vapor' (steam).
