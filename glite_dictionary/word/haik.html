haik

1. NOUN

/ haɪk / (AmE), / haɪk / (BrE)
a large piece of white cloth, worn as an outer garment by men and women in northern Africa
North African outer garment of white cloth

Examples

The Berber woman draped her haik gracefully around her shoulders.
In Algiers, both men and women can be seen wearing a traditional haik.
His white haik protected him from the intense desert sun.

Etymology

From Arabic *ḥā'ik* (something that wraps or covers).
