betulaceous

1. ADJ

/ bɛtʃuˈleɪʃəs / (AmE), / bɛtjʊˈleɪʃəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of trees of the birch family (Betulaceae).
of the birch (Betulaceae) family

Examples

The forest contains several betulaceous species, including birches and alders.
Pollen from betulaceous plants can cause allergies in some people.
Many betulaceous trees thrive in temperate regions around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birch - a type of tree in the Betulaceae family

Etymology

From Latin 'Betula' (birch) + '-aceous' (of or pertaining to).
