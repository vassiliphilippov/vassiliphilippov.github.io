pilosity

1. NOUN

/ paɪˈlɑsəti / (AmE), / pɪˈlɒsɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of having hair; hairiness
hairiness; quality of being hairy

Examples

Excessive pilosity on the face can affect self-esteem in some individuals.
The researcher studied differences in pilosity among various mammal species.
Genetic disorders sometimes lead to abnormal patterns of pilosity on the body.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairiness - the state of being covered with hair

Etymology

From Latin pilositas, from pilosus (hairy).
