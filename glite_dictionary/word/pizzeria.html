pizzeria

1. NOUN

/ piːtsəˈriːə / (AmE), / piːtsəˈriːə / (BrE)
A restaurant or shop where pizzas are made and sold.
shop or restaurant selling pizza

Examples

Let's meet at the new pizzeria on Main Street for dinner tonight.
The pizzeria was famous for its wood-fired oven and crispy crusts.
After the movie, they grabbed a slice at their favorite pizzeria.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restaurant - a place where people pay to sit and eat meals
  • Degree Lower: bakery - a shop where bread and cakes are made and sold

Etymology

From Italian 'pizzeria', from 'pizza'.
