sheeprun

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃiːpˌrʌn / (AmE), / ˈʃiːpˌrʌn / (BrE)
A large area of land or farm used for raising sheep.
farm for raising sheep

Examples

The old family sheeprun covers thousands of acres in the outback.
He inherited the sheeprun from his uncle and now manages hundreds of sheep.
After a dry season, the sheeprun struggled to provide enough pasture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sheep farm - farm where sheep are raised
