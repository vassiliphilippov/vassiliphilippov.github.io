wamble-cropped

1. ADJ

/ ˈwæmbəl ˌkrɑpt / (AmE), / ˈwɒmbəl ˌkrɒpt / (BrE)
(of hair) cut short in an uneven or messy way; having an untidy, irregular short haircut.
uneven/messy short haircut

Examples

After his friend gave him a trim, his hair was left wamble-cropped and uneven.
She wore her wamble-cropped hair with pride, making it part of her signature look.
The children came home with wamble-cropped locks after their adventurous play in the yard.
