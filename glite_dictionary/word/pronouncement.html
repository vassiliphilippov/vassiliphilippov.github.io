pronouncement

1. NOUN

/ prəˈnaʊnsmənt / (AmE), / prəˈnaʊnsmənt / (BrE)
an official or authoritative statement or declaration, especially one that is publicly announced
authoritative declaration

Examples

The minister's pronouncement was broadcast to the entire nation.
Despite his pronouncement of innocence, the evidence was overwhelming.
A royal pronouncement changed the course of the country's history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: declaration - a formal or explicit statement or announcement
  • Synonym: statement - a definite or clear expression of something in speech or writing
  • Synonym: edict - an official order or proclamation issued by an authority

Etymology

From Middle English, from Old French *pronuncier*, from Latin *pronuntiare* (to proclaim, declare).
