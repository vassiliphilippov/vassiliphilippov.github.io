kohl

1. NOUN

/ koʊl / (AmE), / kəʊl / (BrE)
a black powder or paste, traditionally used around the eyes as a cosmetic, especially in Middle Eastern and South Asian cultures
traditional eye cosmetic (powder or paste)

Examples

Many women in the region use kohl to accentuate their eyes.
She applied a line of kohl along her eyelids before the celebration.
Historically, kohl has also been believed to protect the eyes from sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eyeliner - a type of cosmetic used to define the eyes

Etymology

From Arabic *kuḥl*, denoting a fine powder used as eye makeup.
