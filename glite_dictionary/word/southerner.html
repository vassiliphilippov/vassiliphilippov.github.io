Southerner

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌðərnər / (AmE), / ˈsʌðənə / (BrE)
a person from the southern region of the United States
person from the U.S. South

Examples

My grandfather was a proud Southerner who loved sharing stories about his childhood in Georgia.
As a Southerner, she enjoys cooking traditional dishes from her region.
Many Southerners take great pride in their hospitality and local culture.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Northerner - a person from the northern region of the United States
