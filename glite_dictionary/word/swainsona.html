Swainsona

1. NOUN

/ swɛnˈsɑːnə / (AmE), / sweɪnˈsəʊnə / (BrE)
a genus of Australian herbs and subshrubs in the pea family, commonly known as darling peas
genus of Australian herbs (darling peas)

Examples

The Swainsona genus is well known for its vibrant flowers in Australian landscapes.
One of the most famous species in Swainsona is Sturt's desert pea.
Botanists study Swainsona for its unique adaptations to arid environments.

Etymology

Named after English botanist Isaac Swainson (1746–1812).
