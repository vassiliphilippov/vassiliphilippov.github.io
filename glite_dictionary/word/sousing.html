sousing

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaʊsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaʊsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of thoroughly soaking or drenching something with liquid
the act of soaking with liquid

Examples

After hours of sousing the laundry, she finally got all the stains out.
The torrential rain gave the hikers a complete sousing before they found shelter.
The children delighted in the sousing of one another during the water fight.

Synset Examples

he gave it a good drenching

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drenching - the act of wetting thoroughly; soaking

Etymology

From the verb 'souse', Middle English ‘sowsen’, possibly from Old French ‘saucer’ meaning ‘to plunge in liquid’.
