to scotch tape

1. VERB

/ skɑtʃ teɪp / (AmE), / skɒtʃ teɪp / (BrE)
to fasten or attach something using transparent adhesive tape
attach using transparent adhesive tape
american

Examples

She scotch taped her schedule to the refrigerator where everyone could see it.
Please scotch tape the broken picture frame so it doesn't fall apart.
They scotch taped the decorations to the walls for the party.

Synset Examples

The hotel manager scotch taped a note to the guest's door

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tape - to attach or fasten using tape

Etymology

From the trademarked brand name 'Scotch Tape', a product first marketed by 3M in the 1930s.
