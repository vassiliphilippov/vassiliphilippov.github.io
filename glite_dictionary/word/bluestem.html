bluestem

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluːˌstɛm / (AmE), / ˈbluː.stɛm / (BrE)
a tall North American grass with smooth bluish leaf sheaths, grown especially for hay and forage
North American grass grown for hay

Examples

The farmer harvested the bluestem for winter feed.
Tall bluestem grasses waved in the summer breeze across the prairie.
Researchers studied how bluestem responds to drought conditions.
