wolfman

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊlfˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈwʊlf.mæn / (BrE)
(in myth and fiction) a monster or person who can change from human form to a wolf and back again, especially during a full moon; a werewolf.
mythical monster that changes from human to wolf

Examples

The villagers feared the wolfman who was said to haunt the nearby forest each full moon.
In the movie, the wolfman struggled against his transformation into a wild beast.
Legends describe the wolfman as both man and wolf, cursed forever to shift between two worlds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: werewolf - a person who changes into a wolf, especially when there is a full moon
