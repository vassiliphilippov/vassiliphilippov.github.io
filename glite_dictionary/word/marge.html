marge

1. NOUN

/ mɑrdʒ / (AmE), / mɑːdʒ / (BrE)
A spread made mainly from vegetable oils and used as a substitute for butter; margarine.
spread used as butter substitute
informal

Examples

I prefer marge on my toast instead of butter.
We always keep a tub of marge in the fridge for sandwiches.
She used marge when baking to make the cake dairy-free.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: margarine - a spread made from vegetable oils, used as a butter substitute
  • Antonym: butter - a dairy product made from churned cream, used as a spread

Etymology

Shortened form of 'margarine.'
