abdominous

1. ADJ

/ æbˈdoʊ.mə.nəs / (AmE), / æbˈdɒm.ɪ.nəs / (BrE)
Having a large or protruding belly; potbellied.
having a large belly

Examples

The abdominous man struggled to fasten his belt after dinner.
At the fair, a jovial and abdominous chef handed out samples of pie.
Despite his abdominous appearance, he was surprisingly light on his feet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: potbellied - having a fat, protruding belly
  • Antonym: slender - having a slim or thin body

Etymology

From Latin 'abdomen' (belly) + English suffix '-ous'.
