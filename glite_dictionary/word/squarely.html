squarely

1. ADV

/ ˈskwɛrli / (AmE), / ˈskwɛəli / (BrE)
in a straightforward or direct manner, without deviation or ambiguity
in a direct, straightforward manner

Examples

She looked him squarely in the eye and told the truth.
The blame for the error rests squarely on management.
The ball hit him squarely on the shoulder.

Synset Examples

looked him squarely in the eye
ran square into me

Related Terms

  • Synonym: directly - without deviation, in a straight line or manner

Etymology

From the adjective 'square' with adverbial suffix '-ly'.
