z

1. NOUN

/ ziː / (AmE), / zɛd / (BrE)
the 26th and last letter of the English alphabet
the letter 'z'

Examples

In American English, the letter z is pronounced "zee" instead of "zed".
The word "zebra" begins with the letter z.
He traced a large z on the blackboard with chalk.

Synset Examples

the British call Z zed and the Scots call it ezed but Americans call it zee
he doesn't know A from izzard

Etymology

From Latin 'zed', from Greek 'zēta'.
