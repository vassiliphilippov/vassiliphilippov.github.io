Floridian

1. NOUN

/ fləˈrɪdiən / (AmE), / fləˈrɪdiən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or resident of Florida, USA
native or resident of Florida

Examples

Many Floridians choose to retire in their home state because of the warm climate.
During hurricane season, Floridians often prepare for storms more thoroughly than visitors.
The Floridian spoke passionately about preserving the Everglades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Floridian - of or relating to Florida

Etymology

From 'Florida' (U.S. state) + English adjectival/noun suffix '-ian'.
