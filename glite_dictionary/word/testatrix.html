testatrix

1. NOUN

/ tɛˈsteɪtrɪks / (AmE), / tɛˈsteɪtrɪks / (BrE)
A woman who makes a will; a female testator.
female maker of a will
legal

Examples

The testatrix clearly stated her intentions in the will.
The lawyer explained that the testatrix was of sound mind at the time the document was signed.
All assets were distributed according to the wishes of the testatrix.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: testator - a person who makes a will or testament

Etymology

From Latin testātrix, feminine of testātor (person who makes a will).
