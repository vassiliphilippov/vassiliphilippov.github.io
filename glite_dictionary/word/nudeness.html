nudeness

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːdnəs / (AmE), / ˈnjuːdnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being nude or without clothing; nakedness.
the state of being nude

Examples

The painting beautifully captures the nudeness of the human form.
In certain cultures, nudeness is celebrated as a symbol of purity.
She spoke candidly about her feelings regarding nudeness in art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nakedness - the state of being without clothes; being naked

Etymology

From 'nude' + '-ness', with 'nude' from Latin 'nudus' (bare, naked).
