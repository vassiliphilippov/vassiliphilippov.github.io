Algerian

1. NOUN

/ ælˈdʒɪriən / (AmE), / ælˈdʒɪəriən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Algeria
a native or inhabitant of Algeria

Examples

The famous writer is an Algerian who now lives in Paris.
Many Algerians celebrate Independence Day with large gatherings and music.
He met several Algerians during his trip across North Africa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Algeria - a country in North Africa

Etymology

From Algeria + -ian (suffix forming nouns related to countries or places).

/ ælˈdʒɪriən / (AmE), / ælˈdʒɪəriən / (BrE)
