AMEX

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ mɛks / (AmE), / ˈeɪ mɛks / (BrE)
the American Stock Exchange, a stock exchange in New York City, formerly known as the New York Curb Exchange
American Stock Exchange (New York)

Examples

Shares of several technology firms are traded on the AMEX.
The AMEX has a long history as a marketplace for small-cap stocks.
After merging with the NYSE, the AMEX continued to offer distinct trading services.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: NYSE - New York Stock Exchange, a major stock exchange in New York

Etymology

Acronym from American Stock Exchange.
