isometrical

1. ADJ

/ ˌaɪ.səˈmɛ.trɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.səˈmɛt.rɪ.kəl / (BrE)
Having equal dimensions or measurements; composed of parts or elements of equal size.
having equal dimensions or measurements

Examples

The engineer designed an isometrical structure to ensure balance and stability.
In geometry, an isometrical transformation preserves lengths of shapes.
The artwork was admired for its isometrical proportions and symmetry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: isometric - having equal dimensions or measurements

Etymology

From Greek isos (equal) + metron (measure) + English -ical.
