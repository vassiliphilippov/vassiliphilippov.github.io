counterinsurgency

1. NOUN

/ ˌkaʊntɚɪnˈsɜrdʒənsi / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntərɪnˈsɜːdʒənsi / (BrE)
Military, political, and social actions taken by a government to combat and defeat insurgency.
measures to defeat insurgency

Examples

The government launched a large-scale counterinsurgency campaign to restore order in the region.
Successful counterinsurgency operations require cooperation between military and civilian agencies.
Many experts study counterinsurgency tactics to improve responses to internal conflicts.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: insurgency - violent uprising or rebellion against authority, especially a government

Etymology

Formed from 'counter-' (against) + 'insurgency' (revolt, uprising).
