numberless

1. ADJ

/ ˈnʌmbərləs / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbələs / (BrE)
too many to be counted; countless
too numerous to be counted

Examples

Tourists flocked to see the numberless paintings in the famous art museum.
The desert night revealed numberless stars twinkling across the sky.
He faced numberless obstacles on his journey to success.

Synset Examples

countless hours
an infinite number of reasons
innumerable difficulties
the multitudinous seas
myriad stars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: countless - too many to be counted
  • Synonym: innumerable - too many to be counted
  • Synonym: myriad - a countless or extremely great number

Etymology

From Middle English 'noumbreles', from number + -less.
