Havana

1. PROPN

/ həˈvænə / (AmE), / həˈvanə / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Cuba, located in the western part of the island
capital city of Cuba

Examples

We spent a week exploring the colorful streets of Havana.
The music and dance culture of Havana is famous around the world.
After the revolution, many historic buildings in Havana were carefully restored.

Etymology

From Spanish 'La Habana', the city’s name in Spanish.

/ həˈvɑːnə / (AmE), / həˈvænə / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Cuba, located in the west of the country and known for its historical significance
capital city of Cuba

Examples

Many tourists visit Havana to experience its vibrant culture and architecture.
The classic cars driving through Havana are a famous sight in the old city.
She researched the history of Havana for her travel article.

From Spanish 'La Habana'.
