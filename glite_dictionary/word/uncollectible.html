uncollectible

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkəˈlɛktɪbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈlɛktɪbəl / (BrE)
(of a debt or payment) not able to be collected or recovered.
not able to be collected

Examples

After months of attempts, the loan was declared uncollectible by the bank.
The company wrote off the account as an uncollectible receivable in its annual report.
Tax authorities sometimes allow deductions for uncollectible debts.

Synset Examples

a bad (or uncollectible) debt

Related Terms

  • Antonym: collectible - able to be collected

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'collectible' (able to be collected).
