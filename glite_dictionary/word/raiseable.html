raiseable

1. ADJ

/ ˈreɪzəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈreɪzəbl̩ / (BrE)
Able to be raised or lifted into a higher position.
capable of being raised

Examples

The car comes with a raiseable sunroof for extra ventilation.
Some desks have raiseable legs to adjust their height.
The raiseable barrier prevented unauthorized vehicles from entering the area.

Synset Examples

the roadster had a raisable sunroof

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lowerable - capable of being lowered

Etymology

Formed from 'raise' + suffix '-able', meaning capable of being raised.
