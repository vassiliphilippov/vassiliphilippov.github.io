ungarbed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɡɑrbd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡɑːbd / (BrE)
not wearing any clothing; having removed one's clothing
not wearing any clothing

Examples

She stood ungarbed before the mirror, contemplating her reflection in the soft morning light.
The artist chose to paint his subject ungarbed, capturing a sense of vulnerability and truth.
After the swim, the children ran ungarbed through the garden, laughing freely.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clothed - wearing clothes; dressed
  • Synonym: naked - not wearing any clothes
  • Synonym: bare - not clothed or covered

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'garbed' (dressed, clothed), from Old French 'garber' (to dress).
