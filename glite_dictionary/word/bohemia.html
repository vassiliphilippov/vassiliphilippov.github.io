Bohemia

1. PROPN

/ boʊˈhiːmiə / (AmE), / bəʊˈhiːmiə / (BrE)
a historical region and former kingdom in central Europe, now part of the Czech Republic
historical region in the Czech Republic

Examples

In the 19th century, Bohemia was renowned for its glassmaking industry.
She traced her ancestors back to Bohemia, now part of the Czech Republic.
Many artists and intellectuals once flocked to Bohemia for its vibrant culture.

Etymology

From Latin 'Boiohaemum', meaning 'home of the Boii' (a Celtic tribe).
