perianal

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɛriˈeɪnəl / (AmE), / ˌpɛriˈeɪnəl / (BrE)
situated or occurring around the anus
around the anus (medical)
medical

Examples

The doctor noted redness in the perianal area during the examination.
Good hygiene is important to prevent perianal infections.
Some conditions, such as perianal abscesses, require minor surgical procedures.

Etymology

From Greek peri- (around) + anus (Latin, ring, anus).
