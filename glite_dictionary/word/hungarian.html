Hungarian

1. NOUN

/ hʌnˈɡɛriən / (AmE), / hʌŋˈɡɛːrɪən / (BrE)
the official language of Hungary, belonging to the Ugric family of languages
official language of Hungary

Examples

She is fluent in both English and Hungarian.
The novel was originally written in Hungarian and later translated into several languages.
Learning Hungarian can be challenging because of its unique grammar and vocabulary.

Etymology

From Hungary + -an, ultimately from Medieval Latin Hungaria.

/ hʌŋˈɡɛriən / (AmE), / hʌŋˈɡɛːriən / (BrE)

Examples

The Hungarian greeted us warmly and offered directions.
