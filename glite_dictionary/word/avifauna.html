avifauna

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪvəˈfɔnə / (AmE), / ˌeɪvəˈfɔːnə / (BrE)
The birds of a particular region or period considered as a group.
birds of a region or period

Examples

The unique avifauna of the Galápagos Islands attracts birdwatchers from around the world.
Changes in climate have had a significant impact on the region's avifauna.
Researchers published a comprehensive survey of the avifauna found in the Amazon rainforest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fauna - the animals of a particular region, habitat, or geological period

Etymology

From Latin 'avis' (bird) and 'fauna' (animals of a region).
