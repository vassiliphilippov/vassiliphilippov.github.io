straight-up

1. ADV

/ streɪt ʌp / (AmE), / streɪt ʌp / (BrE)
(informal) Completely; honestly; without exaggeration or concealment.
completely; honestly (informal adverb)
informal

Examples

That movie was straight-up terrifying from start to finish.
She told me the straight-up truth about what happened.
I'm straight-up exhausted after working for twelve hours.

Synset Examples

He was straight-up wrong about the facts.
I’m straight-up tired of all the drama.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honestly - in a truthful or direct way
  • Synonym: absolutely - completely; totally
