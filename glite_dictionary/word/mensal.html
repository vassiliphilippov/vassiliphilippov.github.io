mensal

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛn.səl / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.səl / (BrE)
relating to or used at the dining table; pertaining to meals
relating to the dining table or meals
archaic

Examples

The grand hall was adorned with mensal decorations for the banquet.
He inherited his father’s collection of mensal silverware, used on special occasions.
The mensal linen was always pressed and spotless before the feast.

Etymology

From Latin 'mensalis', from 'mensa' meaning 'table'.
