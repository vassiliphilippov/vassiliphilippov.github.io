insecureness

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.sɪˈkjʊr.nəs / (AmE), / ˌɪn.sɪˈkjʊə.nɪs / (BrE)
the state of being insecure, especially vulnerability to risk or feelings of uncertainty and anxiety
state of being insecure or anxious

Examples

His constant worry was a sign of his deep insecureness about the future.
The insecureness of the region made it difficult for people to settle there.
She spoke about her insecureness in social situations and how she tries to overcome it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insecurity - the feeling or state of being insecure or unsafe

Etymology

From insecure + -ness, formed after similar nouns from adjectives.
