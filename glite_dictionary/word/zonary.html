zonary

1. ADJ

/ ˈzoʊˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈzəʊnəri / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of a zone, especially in reference to division into distinct areas.
relating to a zone or zones

Examples

The zonary pattern of the vegetation reflected the varying soil types across the region.
Paleontologists studied the zonary arrangement of fossils in the sediment layers.
Certain animals have a zonary placenta, in which the placenta forms a band around the embryo.

Synset Examples

the zonal frontier

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zonal - relating to or characteristic of a zone

Etymology

From Latin 'zonarius' (of a belt or girdle), from 'zona' (belt, zone).
