brown-speckled

1. ADJ

/ ˈbraʊn ˌspɛk.əld / (AmE), / ˈbraʊn ˌspɛk.əld / (BrE)
Marked with or having small brown spots or speckles.
having brown spots or speckles

Examples

The bird's brown-speckled eggs blended perfectly with the forest floor.
She wore a brown-speckled scarf that matched her jacket.
We saw a brown-speckled butterfly resting on the windowsill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotted - having spots; marked with spots
  • Antonym: plain - without any spots, marks, or speckles
