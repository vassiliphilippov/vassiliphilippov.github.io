tricentennial

1. ADJ

/ ˌtraɪsɛnˈtɛniəl / (AmE), / ˌtraɪsɛnˈtɛniəl / (BrE)
relating to a 300th anniversary or a period of 300 years
relating to 300 years or a 300th anniversary

Examples

The city organized a tricentennial celebration to mark 300 years since its founding.
They preserved historical documents as part of the university’s tricentennial events.
This statue was erected during the nation’s tricentennial in honor of its heritage.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: centennial - relating to a 100th anniversary or period of 100 years
  • Degree Lower: bicentennial - relating to a 200th anniversary or period of 200 years

Etymology

Formed from Latin prefix 'tri-' (three) + 'centennial' (of a hundred years).
