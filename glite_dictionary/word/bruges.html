Bruges

1. PROPN

/ bruːʒ / (AmE), / bruːʒ / (BrE)
a historic city in northwestern Belgium, known for its canals, medieval architecture, and former membership in the Hanseatic League
historic city in Belgium

Examples

Many tourists visit Bruges to admire its medieval city center and scenic canals.
The film 'In Bruges' brought international attention to the city's unique charm.
Art lovers flock to Bruges for its renowned museums and Flemish masterpieces.
