lacework

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪswɜrk / (AmE), / ˈleɪswɜːk / (BrE)
A delicate fabric or decorative pattern consisting of or resembling lace, typically made by weaving threads in intricate open designs.
fabric or decoration made like lace

Examples

The bride's veil was adorned with intricate lacework that shimmered in the light.
Delicate lacework patterns covered the sleeves of the vintage dress.
The artist created metal sculptures featuring elegant lacework designs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lace - a delicate, open fabric of threads
