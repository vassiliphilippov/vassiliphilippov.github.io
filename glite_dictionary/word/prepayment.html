prepayment

1. NOUN

/ priˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / priːˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
the act of paying for something before it is due or before receiving goods or services
payment made in advance

Examples

A prepayment is required to secure your reservation at the hotel.
The company offers a discount for prepayment of annual subscriptions.
Her loan agreement allows for early prepayment without penalty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advance payment - payment made ahead of time
  • Antonym: postpayment - payment made after goods or services are received

Etymology

From 'pre-' (before) + 'payment'.
