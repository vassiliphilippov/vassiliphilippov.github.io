hebdomad

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛb.dəˌmæd / (AmE), / ˈhɛb.də.mad / (BrE)
a period of seven consecutive days; a week
seven-day period; week

Examples

He completed his project within a single hebdomad.
The ritual is performed once every hebdomad by the community.
Historical records were updated at the close of each hebdomad.

Synset Examples

it rained for a week

Related Terms

  • Synonym: week - a period of seven days

Etymology

From Greek hebdomas (week, group of seven), from hepta (seven).
