nitwitted

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɪtˌwɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈnɪtˌwɪtɪd / (BrE)
Lacking intelligence or good judgment; foolish or stupid.
foolish or lacking sense
informal

Examples

Everyone thought his nitwitted remark made the meeting awkward.
She dismissed his nitwitted plan to fix the car with duct tape.
Only a truly nitwitted person would try to climb that fence without a ladder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment
  • Antonym: sensible - showing wisdom or good judgment

Etymology

From 'nitwit' (a foolish person) + '-ed'.
