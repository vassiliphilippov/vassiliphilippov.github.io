crisply

1. ADV

/ ˈkrɪspli / (AmE), / ˈkrɪspli / (BrE)
in a clear, precise, or sharply defined manner
in a precise, clear way

Examples

The instructions were delivered crisply, making them easy to follow.
Autumn mornings feel crisply cool and refreshing.
The artist's lines are drawn crisply, giving the image a strong sense of detail.

Synset Examples

the new style of Minoan pottery was sharply defined

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sharply - in a clear or distinct manner
  • Antonym: blurrily - in an indistinct or unclear way

Etymology

From 'crisp' + '-ly' suffix, with 'crisp' from Old English 'crisp' or Latin 'crispus' (curled).
