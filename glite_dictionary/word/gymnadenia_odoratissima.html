Gymnadenia odoratissima

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪm.nəˈdiː.ni.ə oʊˌdoʊ.rəˈtɪs.ɪ.mə / (AmE), / dʒɪm.nəˈdiː.ni.ə əʊˌdəʊ.rəˈtɪs.ɪ.mə / (BrE)
A species of orchid similar to Gymnadenia conopsea, with smaller flowers on shorter stems and much shorter spurs.
a species of orchid

Examples

The Gymnadenia odoratissima is admired by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate fragrance.
In the Alpine meadows, we discovered several clusters of Gymnadenia odoratissima in bloom.
Unlike its close relative, Gymnadenia odoratissima has shorter stems and smaller blossoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gymnadenia conopsea - A related species of orchid with larger flowers and longer spurs

Etymology

From Greek 'gymnos' (naked) + 'aden' (gland) and Latin 'odoratissima' (most fragrant).
