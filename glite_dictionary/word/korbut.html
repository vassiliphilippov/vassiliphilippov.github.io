Korbut

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɔr.bʊt / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.bʊt / (BrE)
Olga Korbut (born 1955), a Soviet gymnast famous for her performances and innovations in gymnastics during the 1972 and 1976 Olympics.
famous Soviet gymnast

Examples

Many consider Korbut a pioneer in the evolution of women's gymnastics.
The daring moves introduced by Korbut are still celebrated today.
Few athletes have influenced their sport as much as Korbut did in the 1970s.
