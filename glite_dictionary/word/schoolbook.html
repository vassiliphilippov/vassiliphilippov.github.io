schoolbook

1. NOUN

/ ˈskuːlˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈskuːlˌbʊk / (BrE)
a book specifically designed and prepared for use by students in schools or colleges
book for use in education

Examples

The teacher asked everyone to bring their schoolbook to class on Monday.
She found an old schoolbook from her primary school days in the attic.
Each subject requires a different schoolbook for the course.

Synset Examples

his economics textbook is in its tenth edition
the professor wrote the text that he assigned students to buy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: textbook - a book used as a standard source of information on a particular subject
