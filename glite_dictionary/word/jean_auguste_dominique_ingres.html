Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres

1. PROPN

/ ʒɑ̃ oʊˈɡyst dɒmiˈnik ˈæŋɡrəs / (AmE), / ʒɑ̃ oːˈɡyst dɔmiˈnik ˈɑ̃ɡʀə / (BrE)
(1780–1867) A French classical painter known for his mastery of line and portraits, and a leading figure in 19th-century academic art.
French classical painter, 1780–1867

Examples

A painting by Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres hangs in the museum's European art gallery.
Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres is renowned for his striking neoclassical portraits.
Many art students study the techniques of Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres when learning about 19th-century French painting.
