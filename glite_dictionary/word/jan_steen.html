Jan Steen

1. PROPN

/ jɑn steɪn / (AmE), / jæn stiːn / (BrE)
(1626–1679) A Dutch painter known for lively and humorous scenes of everyday life.
Dutch genre painter (1626–1679)

Examples

Many art historians consider Jan Steen to be a master of depicting bustling household scenes.
The museum's new exhibition features several works by Jan Steen.
Jan Steen's paintings are famous for their vivid storytelling and humor.
