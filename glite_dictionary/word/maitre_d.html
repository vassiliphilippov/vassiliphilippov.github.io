maitre d'

1. NOUN

/ ˌmeɪtɚ ˈdi / (AmE), / ˌmeɪtrə ˈdiː / (BrE)
a person in a restaurant who is in charge of the waiters and takes care of seating and welcoming customers
head waiter in a restaurant

Examples

The maitre d' greeted us warmly and showed us to our table.
After a brief wait, the maitre d' found us a seat by the window.
She spoke to the maitre d' about reserving a private dining room for the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head waiter - the main waiter in a restaurant

Etymology

Short for French 'maître d'hôtel' (master of the house).
