to defog

1. VERB

/ diˈfɑɡ / (AmE), / diˈfɒɡ / (BrE)
to remove fog or mist from (a surface), especially from glass or windows
remove fog or mist (esp. from windows)

Examples

Please defog the bathroom mirror before you leave.
The driver quickly defogged the windshield for better visibility.
She turned on the heating system to defog all the car windows.

Synset Examples

demist the car windows

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clear - to make something free of a covering, obstruction, or unwanted substance

Etymology

From de- (removal of) + fog (mist).
