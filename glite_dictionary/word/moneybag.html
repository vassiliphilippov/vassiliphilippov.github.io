moneybag

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌniˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈmʌniˌbæɡ / (BrE)
a small bag with a drawstring used for holding money, especially coins
a drawstring bag for money or coins

Examples

The thief grabbed the moneybag from the counter and ran out of the store.
She kept her coins in a leather moneybag inside her purse.
The old merchant untied his moneybag to pay for the goods.
