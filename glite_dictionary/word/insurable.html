insurable

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈʃʊrəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃɔːrəbəl / (BrE)
capable of being insured or eligible for insurance coverage
able to be insured

Examples

The house is not insurable because it is located in a flood zone.
Only insurable cars are allowed on the racetrack for safety reasons.
His proposal included several insurable risks that the company was willing to cover.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: uninsurable - not able to be insured

Etymology

From insure + -able, indicating capability.
