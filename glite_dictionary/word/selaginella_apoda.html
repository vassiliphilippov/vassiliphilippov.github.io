Selaginella apoda

1. NOUN

/ sɛləˌdʒɪnələ əˈpoʊdə / (AmE), / sɛləˌdʒɪnələ əˈpəʊdə / (BrE)
A species of spikemoss native to eastern North America, known for forming dense mats.
species of spikemoss, eastern North America

Examples

The forest floor was carpeted with Selaginella apoda after the spring rains.
Researchers are studying Selaginella apoda for its unique drought resistance.
Botanists easily identified Selaginella apoda by its mat-forming growth habit.

Etymology

From New Latin: genus name 'Selaginella' and species epithet 'apoda' meaning 'footless' in Latin.
