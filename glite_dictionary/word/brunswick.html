Brunswick

1. PROPN

/ ˈbrʌnzwɪk / (AmE), / ˈbrʌnzwɪk / (BrE)
a town in southwestern Maine, United States, known as the location of Bowdoin College.
university town in southwestern Maine, USA

Examples

He was born and raised in Brunswick, Maine, and attended Bowdoin College.
The vibrant downtown area of Brunswick is popular with both students and locals.
Several historic landmarks can be found throughout Brunswick.

Etymology

Named after the German city of Braunschweig, anglicized as Brunswick.
