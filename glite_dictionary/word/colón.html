Colón

1. PROPN

/ koʊˈloʊn / (AmE), / kəˈlɒn / (BrE)
a port city at the Caribbean entrance to the Panama Canal, in Panama
Caribbean port city in Panama

Examples

The cargo ship docked at Colón before entering the Panama Canal.
Tourists often visit Colón for its duty-free shopping zone.
Historically, Colón has been a vital hub for maritime trade.

Etymology

Named after Christopher Columbus (Spanish: Cristóbal Colón).
