storax

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɔˌræks / (AmE), / ˈstɔːræks / (BrE)
a fragrant resin with a vanilla-like scent obtained from various trees of the genus Styrax, used in perfumery and medicines
fragrant resin from Styrax trees

Examples

The storax was used in ancient rituals for its pleasant aroma.
She added a few drops of storax to the homemade perfume.
Some traditional medicines include storax as an ingredient for its soothing properties.

Etymology

From Latin 'storax', from Greek 'stúrax', meaning the balsam of the storax tree.
