baby-faced

1. ADJ

/ ˈbeɪbi ˌfeɪst / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbi ˌfeɪst / (BrE)
Having a face that looks unusually young or childlike.
having a youthful-looking face

Examples

The actor was often cast as a teenager because of his baby-faced appearance.
Although she is in her thirties, her baby-faced features make her look much younger.
Some models are celebrated for their unique, baby-faced look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: youthful-looking - having the appearance of youth
  • Antonym: mature-looking - having an appearance older or more developed than one's age
