marking

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making a visible mark on a surface
the act of making a mark

Examples

The marking of the exam papers took several hours.
Graffiti artists are sometimes fined for the illegal marking of public property.
Please avoid the marking of desks with pens or pencils.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mark - a small area on a surface having a different color from its surroundings

Etymology

From 'mark' + '-ing', denoting the action or result of marking.
