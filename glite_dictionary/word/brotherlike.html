brotherlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈbrʌðərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbrʌðəlaɪk / (BrE)
Having qualities or characteristics typical of a brother; showing affection or loyalty like that of a brother.
resembling or characteristic of a brother

Examples

His brotherlike concern for his friends made him popular in the group.
She appreciated his brotherlike advice during difficult times.
Their brotherlike bond was evident in how they looked out for each other.

Synset Examples

brotherly feelings
close fraternal ties

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brotherly - showing affection or concern characteristic of brothers
  • Antonym: sisterly - showing affection or concern characteristic of sisters
