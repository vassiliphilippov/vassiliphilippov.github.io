Mylar

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪlɑr / (AmE), / ˈmaɪlɑː / (BrE)
a strong, thin polyester film used in packaging, insulation, and other applications
a thin, strong polyester film

Examples

The balloons were made of Mylar to keep them floating longer.
She used a sheet of Mylar to cover the greenhouse windows.
Emergency blankets are often constructed from thin Mylar material.

Etymology

Trademark, originally from DuPont, introduced in 1952.
