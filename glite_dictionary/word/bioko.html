Bioko

1. PROPN

/ biˈoʊkoʊ / (AmE), / biˈəʊkəʊ / (BrE)
an island in the Gulf of Guinea that is part of Equatorial Guinea, off the west coast of Africa
island in Equatorial Guinea, West Africa

Examples

Malaria has been a persistent issue on Bioko for decades.
Scientists traveled to Bioko to study the island's unique wildlife.
The capital city of Equatorial Guinea, Malabo, is located on Bioko.
