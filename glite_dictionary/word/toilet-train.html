to toilet-train

1. VERB

/ ˈtɔɪlət treɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlɪt treɪn / (BrE)
to teach a young child to use the toilet instead of diapers
teach a child to use the toilet

Examples

It took several months to toilet-train their daughter successfully.
They started to toilet-train him when he turned two years old.
Parents often face challenges when they toilet-train a stubborn toddler.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: potty-train - to train a child to use a potty or toilet

Etymology

From 'toilet' + 'train', in reference to the process of training children to use the toilet.
