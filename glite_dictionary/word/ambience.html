ambience

1. NOUN

/ ˈæmbiəns / (AmE), / ˈæmbɪəns / (BrE)
the character, feeling, or mood of a place or situation
mood or atmosphere of a place

Examples

The dim lighting and soft music created a relaxing ambience in the restaurant.
She enjoyed the lively ambience of the city during the festival.
The designer aimed to improve the office's ambience with plants and bright colors.

Synset Examples

there was an atmosphere of excitement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atmosphere - the mood or feeling in a place

Etymology

From French 'ambiance' (surroundings), from Latin 'ambire' (to go around).

2. NOUN

/ ˈæmbiˌɑns / (AmE), / ˈæmbiəns / (BrE)
the character, feeling, or mood of a place or situation
atmosphere or mood of a place

Examples

The restaurant's ambience made it the perfect spot for their romantic dinner.
Soft lighting and quiet music added to the cozy ambience of the lounge.
Many customers return for the warm ambience as much as for the food.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atmosphere - the character or mood of a place, situation, or piece of music
  • Synonym: environment - the surroundings or conditions in which a person, animal, or plant lives or operates

Etymology

From French 'ambiance', from Latin 'ambire' (to go around).
