Swiss

1. ADJ

/ swɪs / (AmE), / swɪs / (BrE)
relating to Switzerland, its people, or their culture.
of or relating to Switzerland

Examples

The annual Swiss festival attracts visitors from all over the world.
He enjoys traditional Swiss cheese and chocolate.
The Swiss Alps are famous for their breathtaking scenery.

Synset Examples

the Swiss army

Etymology

From Middle French Suisse, from German Schweiz (Switzerland).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02972520-a
Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ swɪs / (AmE), / swɪs / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Switzerland; a person of Swiss nationality.
a person from Switzerland

Examples

Several Swiss attended the international conference on climate change.
He became friends with a group of Swiss while backpacking in Europe.
The Swiss are known for their expertise in watchmaking and banking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Swiss - of or relating to Switzerland or its people

Etymology

From Middle French Suisse, from German Schweizer (Swiss person), from Schweiz (Switzerland).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09752747-n
Synset POS: n
