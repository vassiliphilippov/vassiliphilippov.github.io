Nalfon

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæl.fɑn / (AmE), / ˈnal.fɒn / (BrE)
A brand name for fenoprofen, a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used to treat arthritis and other inflammatory disorders.
anti-inflammatory drug (brand name)
medical american

Examples

After being diagnosed with arthritis, her doctor prescribed Nalfon to help manage the pain.
Patients should take Nalfon exactly as directed to minimize the risk of side effects.
Some people experience stomach upset when taking Nalfon for long-term pain relief.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fenoprofen - the generic drug name for Nalfon
  • Degree Higher: NSAID - any nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug
