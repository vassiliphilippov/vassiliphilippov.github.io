to wow

1. VERB

/ waʊ / (AmE), / waʊ / (BrE)
to impress someone greatly or cause admiration
impress greatly; cause admiration
informal

Examples

The magician's amazing tricks wowed the entire crowd.
She always knows how to wow her clients during presentations.
The new restaurant wows diners with its inventive dishes.

Synset Examples

The speaker wowed the audience

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impress - to make someone feel admiration and respect
  • Antonym: bore - to make someone feel uninterested or unexcited
