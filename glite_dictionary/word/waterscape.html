waterscape

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑːtərˌskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌskeɪp / (BrE)
a painting, drawing, or other artistic representation of a scene featuring a large expanse of water, such as the sea, a lake, or a river
a painting or scene featuring water

Examples

She admired the artist's latest waterscape, which depicted a tranquil lake at sunset.
The gallery displayed several impressive waterscapes alongside mountain landscapes.
He specializes in painting waterscapes inspired by his travels along the coast.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: landscape - a painting or representation of natural scenery on land

Etymology

Formed from 'water' + 'scape', by analogy with 'landscape'.
