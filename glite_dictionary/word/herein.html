herein

1. ADV

/ hɪrˈɪn / (AmE), / hɪərˈɪn / (BrE)
in this document, matter, or place
in this (document, matter, or place)

Examples

All relevant details are provided herein for your review.
The company accepts no liability except as stated herein.
The policies described herein apply to all employees.

Synset Examples

I shall discuss the question herein

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hereinafter - in the following part of this document
  • Synonym: hereto - to this (document, matter, or place)

Etymology

From Middle English, combining 'here' + 'in'.
