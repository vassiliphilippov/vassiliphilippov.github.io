Izaak Walton

1. PROPN

/ ˈaɪzæk ˈwɔltən / (AmE), / ˈaɪzæk ˈwɔːltən / (BrE)
(1593–1683) English writer best known for 'The Compleat Angler,' a classic treatise on fishing.
English writer on fishing

Examples

Many anglers consider Izaak Walton the founder of modern fishing literature.
The book club selected Izaak Walton's 'The Compleat Angler' for its next discussion.
Izaak Walton wrote not only about fishing, but also about friendship and the countryside.
