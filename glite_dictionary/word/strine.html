Strine

1. NOUN

/ straɪn / (AmE), / straɪn / (BrE)
Australian English, especially as spoken with a broad accent and including characteristic slang and expressions.
Australian English dialect

Many new arrivals find Strine expressions confusing at first.

Humorous alteration of 'Australian' as pronounced in a broad Australian accent.
