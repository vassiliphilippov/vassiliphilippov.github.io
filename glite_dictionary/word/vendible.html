vendible

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɛndəbəl / (AmE), / ˈvɛndɪbəl / (BrE)
fit to be offered for sale; able to be sold
able to be sold

Examples

The artwork was not considered vendible due to legal restrictions.
Most of the produce from the farm is vendible at the local market.
He separated the vendible goods from those that needed further processing.

Synset Examples

marketable produce

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marketable - able or fit to be sold or marketed

Etymology

From Latin 'vendibilis', from 'vendere' (to sell).
