welfare-statist

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛlˌfɛr ˈsteɪtɪst / (AmE), / ˈwɛlˌfeə ˈsteɪtɪst / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a welfare state, especially in terms of government policies or societal structure.
relating to a welfare state

Examples

The government adopted a welfare-statist approach to address social inequality.
Critics argue that welfare-statist policies can discourage individual enterprise.
His proposals reflect a decidedly welfare-statist philosophy regarding social welfare and economic intervention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welfare state - a system in which the government protects the health and well-being of its citizens

Etymology

Formed from 'welfare state' + '-ist', indicating relation or advocacy.
