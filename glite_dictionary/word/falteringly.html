falteringly

1. ADV

/ ˈfɔltərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːltərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a hesitant or unsteady manner, showing lack of confidence or certainty
in a hesitant or unsteady way

Examples

She spoke falteringly as she tried to explain what had happened.
He walked falteringly onto the stage, looking unsure of himself.
The child reached out falteringly to take his mother's hand.

Synset Examples

he walked unsteadily toward the exit
The wounded soldier was swinging unsteadily on his legs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hesitantly - in a hesitant manner
  • Antonym: confidently - in a confident manner

Etymology

From 'falter' (to hesitate or waver), with adverbial suffix '-ly'.
