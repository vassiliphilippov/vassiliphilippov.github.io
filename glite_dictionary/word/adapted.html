adapted

1. ADJ

/ əˈdæp.tɪd / (AmE), / əˈdæp.tɪd / (BrE)
changed or modified to be suitable for a particular purpose or situation
changed to be suitable

Examples

The equipment was adapted for use by people with disabilities.
These novels have been adapted for younger readers.
Our products can be easily adapted to meet your specific needs.

Synset Examples

seeds precisely adapted to the area
instructions altered to suit the children's different ages

Related Terms

  • Synonym: modified - altered or changed in form or character
  • Antonym: unaltered - not changed or adjusted

Etymology

From Latin 'adaptare' (to fit to), via the verb 'adapt.'
