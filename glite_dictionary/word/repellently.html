repellently

1. ADV

/ rɪˈpɛləntli / (AmE), / rɪˈpɛləntli / (BrE)
In a way that causes strong dislike or aversion; in a repellent manner.
in a repellent manner

Examples

The food was repellently greasy and cold, making it inedible.
He behaved repellently during the meeting, offending everyone present.
The room was repellently dirty, with trash scattered on every surface.

Synset Examples

repellently fat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repulsive - causing strong dislike or disgust
