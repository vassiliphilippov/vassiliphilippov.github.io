tested

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɛstɪd / (BrE)
proved to be reliable, effective, or correct through testing or experience
proven reliable or effective

Examples

We chose a tested approach to ensure the best results.
The tested software was released after thorough examination.
She prefers tested recipes rather than experimenting with new ones.

Synset Examples

a tested method

Related Terms

  • Antonym: untested - not proved by testing or experience

2. ADJ

/ ˈtɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɛstɪd / (BrE)
Shown by trial or experience to be reliable or effective.
proven reliable by trial

Examples

The vaccine has been thoroughly tested before being approved for use.
They prefer using tested methods rather than trying something new.
In a crisis, only a tested leader can inspire real confidence.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: untried - not yet tested or proven
  • Synonym: proven - demonstrated by evidence or argument to be true or reliable
