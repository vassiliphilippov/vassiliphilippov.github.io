Kennewick

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɛnəˌwɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɛnɪwɪk / (BrE)
a city in southern Washington State, United States, located on the Columbia River
city in southern Washington, USA

Examples

She was born and raised in Kennewick before moving to Seattle for college.
The annual boat races in Kennewick attract visitors from across the region.
Kennewick is known for its scenic views along the Columbia River.

Etymology

Named after a Native American word, possibly meaning 'grassy place' or 'winter haven'.
