Demogorgon

1. PROPN

/ ˌdɛməˈɡɔrɡən / (AmE), / ˌdiːməˈɡɔːɡən / (BrE)
a mysterious and terrifying deity of the underworld in Greek mythology, often considered a primordial being associated with darkness and secrecy
mythical deity of the underworld

Examples

According to legend, the Demogorgon dwells deep beneath the earth, ruling over primal chaos.
Medieval scholars wrote about the Demogorgon as a powerful force beyond human comprehension.
Many works of literature portray the Demogorgon as a symbol of the unknown and the terrifying.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin Demogorgon, probably created as a misreading or misinterpretation of Greek texts.
