bonzer

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑnzər / (AmE), / ˈbɒnzə / (BrE)
Excellent; remarkable or wonderful, especially in Australian English.
remarkable; excellent (Australian)
informal british

Examples

The party last night was bonzer—everyone had a fantastic time.
We've had some bonzer weather this week, perfect for the beach.
That was a bonzer meal, thanks for inviting me over.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excellent - of the highest quality; outstanding
  • Synonym: marvellous - causing great wonder; extraordinarily good
  • Antonym: terrible - very bad or unpleasant; of poor quality

Etymology

Australian slang, origin uncertain but perhaps from English dialect 'bon' (good) and '-zer' as an intensifier.
