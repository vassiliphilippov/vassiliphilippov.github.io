raunch

1. NOUN

/ rɑntʃ / (AmE), / rɔːntʃ / (BrE)
the quality of lacking taste, refinement, or decency; vulgarity or coarseness
vulgar or tasteless quality
informal slang american

Examples

The comedian's act was full of raunch, pushing the boundaries of good taste.
Some critics argue that the movie's raunch overshadows its clever writing.
She found the explicit raunch of the lyrics offensive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vulgarity - the quality of being vulgar

Etymology

Probably a blend of 'raw' and 'haunch', developed as 20th-century American slang.
