fulgurance

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌlɡjərəns / (AmE), / ˈfʌlɡjʊərəns / (BrE)
Brilliance, radiance, or shining excellence; a display of dazzling brightness or outstanding talent.
brilliance or shining excellence

Examples

The pianist played with fulgurance, dazzling the entire audience.
Her fulgurance on stage left the judges speechless.
The novel is notable for the fulgurance of its prose.

Synset Examples

She described his talent with sheer fulgurance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brilliance - great brightness or excellence
  • Synonym: radiance - bright and shining quality

Etymology

From Latin 'fulgurans' (shining, flashing), from 'fulgur' (lightning).
