granddad

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrænˌdæd / (AmE), / ˈɡranˌdad / (BrE)
the father of one’s father or mother; grandfather
one's grandfather

Examples

My granddad used to tell us stories about his childhood during family dinners.
After school, Tim would often visit his granddad and help him in the garden.
Even at ninety, my granddad still enjoys going for long walks every morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandfather - father of one's father or mother
  • Antonym: grandma - mother of one's father or mother
