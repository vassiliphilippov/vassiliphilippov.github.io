racing

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport in which competitors try to be the fastest to complete a course or reach a finish line
sport of speed contests

Examples

He has been involved in racing since he was a teenager.
Horse racing attracts large crowds and significant betting activity every year.
The city is famous for its annual boat racing festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: race - a competition of speed between runners, horses, vehicles, etc.
  • Synonym: competition - an event in which people compete to win

Etymology

From the verb 'race' (to compete in speed), itself from Old Norse 'rās' (a running, rush).
