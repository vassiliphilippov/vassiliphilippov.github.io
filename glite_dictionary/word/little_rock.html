Little Rock

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪt̬l ˌrɑk / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl ˌrɒk / (BrE)
the capital city of Arkansas, located in the central part of the state on the Arkansas River
capital city of Arkansas, USA

Examples

The city of Little Rock hosts several annual cultural festivals.
He traveled to Little Rock for a business conference last month.
Little Rock is known for its pivotal role in the civil rights movement.

Etymology

Named for a small rock formation on the south bank of the Arkansas River noticed by 18th-century French explorer Jean-Baptiste Bénard de la Harpe (petite roche, French for 'little rock').
