oenophile

1. NOUN

/ ˈinoʊˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈiːnəʊfaɪl / (BrE)
A person who enjoys or has a deep appreciation of wine.
person who appreciates wine

Examples

As a passionate oenophile, she has a cellar filled with rare French vintages.
The magazine caters to the oenophile with detailed wine reviews and tasting notes.
Any true oenophile would appreciate the complexity of this old Merlot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wine enthusiast - someone who enjoys wine

Etymology

From Greek oinos (wine) + -phile (lover of).
