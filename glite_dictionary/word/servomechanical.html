servomechanical

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɜr.voʊ.məˈkæn.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌsɜː.vəʊ.məˈkæn.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving servomechanisms; controlled by a feedback-based automatic control system.
relating to servomechanisms

Examples

The servomechanical system adjusts the position of the telescope automatically.
Robotic arms often rely on servomechanical components for precise movement.
Proper maintenance is essential for any servomechanical apparatus used in manufacturing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: servo - an automatic device that uses error-sensing feedback to correct its operation

Etymology

From 'servo-' (from Latin 'servus', meaning 'slave' or 'servant') + 'mechanical'.
