heatable

1. ADJ

/ ˈhiːtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈhiːtəbəl / (BrE)
able to be made hot; capable of being heated.
capable of being heated

Examples

The heatable mug keeps your coffee at the perfect temperature.
Many laboratory instruments have heatable surfaces for specific experiments.
Please ensure that only heatable glassware is used in the oven.

Synset Examples

the heatable tip of a soldering iron

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heat-resistant - able to resist heat without being damaged
  • Degree Higher: flammable - easily set on fire; able to catch fire easily
