amiably

1. ADV

/ ˈeɪ.mi.əb.li / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.mi.əb.li / (BrE)
In a friendly and pleasant manner.
in a friendly and pleasant way

Examples

He greeted each of his guests amiably, making them feel instantly at ease.
She amiably declined the invitation but thanked them for thinking of her.
They discussed their differences amiably over coffee.

Synset Examples

‘Come and visit me,’ he said amiably

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pleasantly - in a pleasant manner
  • Antonym: hostilely - in a hostile manner

Etymology

From 'amiable' + '-ly'. 'Amiable' from Old French, from Latin 'amicabilis' (friendly).
