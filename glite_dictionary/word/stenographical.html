stenographical

1. ADJ

/ ˌstɛnoʊˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌstɛnəʊˈɡræfɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving the method or practice of stenography, especially shorthand writing
relating to shorthand writing

Examples

The court proceedings were recorded in a stenographical transcript for official records.
She took stenographical notes using her specialized shorthand skills.
The reporter's stenographical expertise allowed her to capture every word of the interview.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stenographic - relating to or involving stenography

Etymology

From 'stenography' (shorthand writing) and the suffix '-al' (forming adjectives).
