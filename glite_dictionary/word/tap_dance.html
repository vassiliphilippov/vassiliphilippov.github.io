to tap dance

1. VERB

/ tæp dæns / (AmE), / tæp dɑːns / (BrE)
to perform a style of dance characterized by rhythmic tapping sounds made with the feet, usually wearing special shoes fitted with metal plates.
perform a rhythmic dance with tapping steps

Examples

She learned to tap dance at a young age and performed in several shows.
During the audition, he had to tap dance for the judges.
I enjoy watching people tap dance because of the energetic rhythms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tap dancer - a person who performs tap dance

Etymology

From 'tap', referring to the tapping sound, and 'dance'.
