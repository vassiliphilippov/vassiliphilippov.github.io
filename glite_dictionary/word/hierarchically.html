hierarchically

1. ADV

/ ˌhaɪəˈrɑrkɪkli / (AmE), / ˌhaɪəˈrɑːkɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner arranged or organized according to levels of importance or rank.
in a hierarchical manner

Examples

The company is structured hierarchically, with clear levels of management.
Academic departments are often organized hierarchically to facilitate administration.
Tasks are hierarchically distributed based on employees' positions.

Synset Examples

hierarchically organized

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hierarchical - arranged in order of rank

Etymology

From 'hierarchical' (from Greek hierarkhēs 'sacred ruler') + -ly.
