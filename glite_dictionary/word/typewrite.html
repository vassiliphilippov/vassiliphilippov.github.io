to typewrite

1. VERB

/ ˈtaɪpˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈtaɪp.raɪt / (BrE)
to write using a typewriter or keyboard by pressing keys
write with a typewriter or keyboard

Examples

She learned to typewrite over a summer course at college.
Back in the 1960s, most official documents were typewritten rather than handwritten.
He can typewrite quickly without looking at the keys.

Synset Examples

type the acceptance letter, please

Related Terms

  • Synonym: type - to write using a keyboard

Etymology

From 'type' (as in typewriter) + 'write'.
