mismanagement

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
careless, inefficient, or inappropriate management of something, especially an organization or resource
careless or inefficient management

Examples

The company suffered huge losses due to financial mismanagement at the highest level.
Years of mismanagement left the hospital in crisis.
Allegations of mismanagement led to an investigation by the board.

Synset Examples

he accomplished little due to the mismanagement of his energies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maladministration - inefficient or dishonest administration, especially in government
  • Antonym: management - the act or manner of managing; handling, direction, or control

Etymology

From the prefix 'mis-' (wrongly) and 'management'.
