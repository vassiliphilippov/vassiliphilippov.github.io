ashy

1. ADJ

/ ˈæʃi / (AmE), / ˈaʃi / (BrE)
having a pale gray color, like that of ashes
pale gray in color

Examples

Smoke drifted through the ashy sky as evening approached.
Her complexion looked ashy in the cold winter light.
The old carpet had faded to an ashy shade over the years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gray - a color intermediate between black and white
  • Synonym: ashen - ashen: very pale or gray in color, resembling ashes

Etymology

From 'ash' (the powdery residue after burning) + '-y' (suffix meaning 'having the quality of').
