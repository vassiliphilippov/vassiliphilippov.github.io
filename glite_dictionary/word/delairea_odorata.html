Delairea odorata

1. NOUN

/ dəˈlɛəriə oʊˈdɔrətə / (AmE), / dəˈlɛəriə əʊˈdɔːrətə / (BrE)
A South African evergreen climbing plant with yellow flowers, grown mainly as a houseplant for its attractive foliage; sometimes classified within the genus Senecio.
a South African evergreen climbing plant

Examples

The gardener carefully trimmed the Delairea odorata to prevent it from overtaking the walkway.
Many people grow Delairea odorata indoors to enjoy its decorative leaves throughout the year.
In some regions, Delairea odorata has become invasive and threatens native species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Senecio - a large genus of flowering plants often including Delairea odorata
