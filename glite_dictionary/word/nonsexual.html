nonsexual

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
not involving or relating to sex or sexual activity
not involving sex or sexuality

Examples

The organization promotes nonsexual friendships between men and women.
Some plants reproduce through nonsexual means, such as budding or fragmentation.
