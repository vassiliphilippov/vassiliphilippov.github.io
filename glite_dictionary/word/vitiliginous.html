vitiliginous

1. ADJ

/ ˌvɪtɪˈlɪdʒənəs / (AmE), / ˌvɪtɪˈlɪdʒɪnəs / (BrE)
relating to or affected by vitiligo; having characteristics of vitiligo (a condition marked by loss of skin pigmentation in patches)
relating to or having vitiligo (skin condition)
medical

Examples

The patient presented with vitiliginous patches on her arms and hands.
Dermatologists often distinguish vitiliginous lesions from other pigment disorders.
The study focuses on the psychological effects of vitiliginous skin changes in adolescents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vitiligo - a skin condition causing loss of pigment in patches

Etymology

From Latin vitiligo (a type of skin blemish) + English -ous (adjectival suffix).
