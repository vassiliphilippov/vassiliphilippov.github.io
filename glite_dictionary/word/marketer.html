marketer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrkətər / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to promote or sell goods or services
person who promotes or sells goods or services

Examples

As a marketer, Sarah developed creative campaigns to boost product awareness.
The company hired an experienced marketer to increase its online presence.
Social media has become an essential tool for every modern marketer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promoter - a person who encourages or organizes the growth or sales of something
  • Synonym: salesperson - someone whose job is to sell goods or services

Etymology

From market + -er, meaning one who operates in a market.
