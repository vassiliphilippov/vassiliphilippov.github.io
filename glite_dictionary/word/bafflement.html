bafflement

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæf.əl.mənt / (AmE), / ˈbæf.əl.mənt / (BrE)
A feeling of confusion or puzzlement, typically when something is hard to understand.
state of confusion or puzzlement

Examples

She stared at the complicated diagram in utter bafflement.
His explanation only deepened my bafflement about the process.
The sudden change in her attitude was met with bafflement from her colleagues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confusion - the state of being bewildered or unclear in one's mind
  • Antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear or easy to understand

Etymology

From 'baffle' (to confuse or perplex) + '-ment' (forming nouns of action or result).
