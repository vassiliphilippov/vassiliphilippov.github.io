frequently

1. ADV

/ ˈfriːkwəntli / (AmE), / ˈfriːkwəntli / (BrE)
Often; many times at short intervals.
often; many times

Examples

She frequently travels abroad for work and has visited over fifty countries.
The teacher frequently asked questions to keep the students engaged during class.
He frequently checks his email throughout the day for important updates.

Synset Examples

we often met over a cup of coffee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: often - on many occasions; frequently
  • Antonym: rarely - not often; seldom

Etymology

From Latin frequentia (a crowd, multitude), via frequent (adj.).
