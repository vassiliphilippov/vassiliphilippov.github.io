well-branched

1. ADJ

/ wɛl ˈbræntʃt / (AmE), / wɛl ˈbrɑːntʃt / (BrE)
(of a plant or tree) having many or healthy branches; having a good branching structure.
having many or healthy branches

Examples

The well-branched shrub provided ample shade in the garden.
For a fuller appearance, gardeners often choose well-branched varieties of ornamental trees.
The well-branched tomato plant produced more fruit than the others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: branched - having branches; divided into branches
