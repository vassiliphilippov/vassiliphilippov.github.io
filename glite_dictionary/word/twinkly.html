twinkly

1. ADJ

/ ˈtwɪŋkli / (AmE), / ˈtwɪŋkli / (BrE)
having a bright, cheerful, or sparkling expression, especially suggesting happiness or optimism
having a cheerful, sparkling expression

Examples

Her grandfather's twinkly eyes made everyone feel at ease.
The twinkly lights in the garden matched her cheerful mood perfectly.
He greeted us with a wide, twinkly smile that was impossible to resist.

Synset Examples

Come to my arms, my beamish boy!
a room of smiling faces
a round red twinkly Santa Claus

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sparkling - shining brightly with flashes of light; lively or spirited in character
  • Antonym: dull - lacking brightness, liveliness, or interest; not shining

Etymology

From 'twinkle' + -y, indicating the quality of twinkling.
