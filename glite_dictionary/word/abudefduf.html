Abudefduf

1. NOUN

/ ˌæbjədəˈdʌf / (AmE), / ˌæbjuːdɛfˈdʌf / (BrE)
A genus of tropical marine fish in the family Pomacentridae, commonly known as sergeant-majors, found in warm seas worldwide.
genus of tropical marine fish (sergeant-majors)

Examples

The coral reef was teeming with colorful Abudefduf darting between the rocks.
Researchers studied several species within the Abudefduf genus for their unique social behavior.
Many aquarists admire the bold stripes and vibrant hues of Abudefduf in home aquariums.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sergeant-major - a common name for fish of the genus Abudefduf

Etymology

From Arabic, likely meaning 'father of Damselfish' or similar, used in scientific Latin.
