aquatics

1. NOUN

/ əˈkwætɪks / (AmE), / əˈkwatɪks / (BrE)
sports or activities that involve swimming, diving, or other exercises in or on water
water-based sports or activities

Examples

The school offers aquatics as part of its physical education program.
She excels in aquatics, especially synchronized swimming and water polo.
Many children learn to love aquatics during summer swim camp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimming - the sport or activity of moving through water using the limbs

Etymology

From Latin 'aquaticus' (relating to water), from 'aqua' (water).
