Jell-O

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛl oʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛl əʊ / (BrE)
A fruit-flavored gelatin dessert made from a commercially prepared powder, originally a trademark.
fruit-flavored gelatin dessert
american

Examples

She brought a bowl of Jell-O to the picnic for everyone to share.
For dessert, we had strawberry Jell-O with whipped cream on top.
The kids love watching the Jell-O jiggle before they eat it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gelatin dessert - a sweet dish made with gelatin, often flavored and molded

Etymology

Trademark name introduced in the late 19th century for a gelatin dessert.
