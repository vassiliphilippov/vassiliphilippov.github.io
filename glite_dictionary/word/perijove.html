perijove

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛr.iˌdʒoʊv / (AmE), / ˈpɛr.ɪˌdʒəʊv / (BrE)
the point in an orbit around Jupiter at which the orbiting body is closest to the planet
closest orbital point to Jupiter
medical

Examples

The spacecraft made its closest approach at perijove, gathering valuable data about Jupiter's atmosphere.
Mission scientists eagerly awaited images taken during perijove on the latest orbital pass.
Each time the probe reaches perijove, its instruments are directed at new features on Jupiter's surface.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: apojove - the point in an orbit around Jupiter farthest from the planet

Etymology

From Greek 'peri-' (around, near) and 'Jove' (an alternative name for Jupiter).
