faddish

1. ADJ

/ ˈfæd.ɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfæd.ɪʃ / (BrE)
Intensely fashionable or popular for a short period of time; characteristic of a fad.
briefly fashionable or trendy

Examples

The faddish design of the shoes quickly went out of style within a few months.
Many diets are dismissed as faddish because they become popular briefly and then disappear.
Some people avoid faddish trends and prefer classic clothing that never goes out of fashion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trendy - very fashionable or up to date
  • Antonym: timeless - not affected by the passage of time or by changes in fashion

Etymology

From "fad" (a temporary fashion) + -ish (forming adjectives).
