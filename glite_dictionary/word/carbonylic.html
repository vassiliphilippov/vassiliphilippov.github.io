carbonylic

1. ADJ

/ kɑːrˈbɑːnɪlɪk / (AmE), / kɑːˈbɒnɪlɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing a carbonyl group (a functional group composed of a carbon atom double-bonded to an oxygen atom)
relating to the carbonyl group in chemistry
medical

The carbonylic compounds play a crucial role in organic synthesis.
