Samarang

1. PROPN

/ sæməˈrɑːŋ / (AmE), / sæməˈrɑːŋ / (BrE)
a port city in northern Java, Indonesia, known for its role as a commercial and administrative center
port city in northern Java, Indonesia

Examples

Many ships dock at Samarang before continuing to other Indonesian ports.
He spent two weeks exploring the markets of Samarang on his travels through Java.
Trade in Samarang thrived during the Dutch colonial period.

Etymology

From Javanese 'Semarang', adopted in colonial English sources as 'Samarang'.
