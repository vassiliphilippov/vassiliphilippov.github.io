sutura intermaxillaris

1. NOUN

/ suːˈtʊr ə ˌɪntərˌmæksɪˈlɛərɪs / (AmE), / suːˈtjʊərə ˌɪntəmækˈsɪlɪərɪs / (BrE)
the suture between the two maxillae in the upper jawbone
suture between upper jawbones
medical

Examples

The sutura intermaxillaris is important in dental surgery involving the maxilla.
Growth at the sutura intermaxillaris can affect the alignment of teeth.
Radiologists can often identify the sutura intermaxillaris on cranial x-rays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intermaxillary suture - the suture between the two maxillae in the upper jaw

Etymology

From Latin: 'sutura' (seam) and 'intermaxillaris' (between the maxillae).
