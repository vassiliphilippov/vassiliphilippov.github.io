nonparticipant

1. NOUN

/ nɑn.pɑrˈtɪs.ə.pənt / (AmE), / nɒn.pɑːˈtɪs.ɪ.pənt / (BrE)
a person who does not take part in a particular activity, event, or situation
person not taking part

Examples

Only a few nonparticipants remained seated while the game was being played.
The survey included both participants and nonparticipants to ensure unbiased results.
As a nonparticipant, she observed the debate without joining the discussion.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: participant - a person who takes part in something

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'non-' (meaning 'not') to 'participant'.
