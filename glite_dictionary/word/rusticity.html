rusticity

1. NOUN

/ rʌˈstɪsəti / (AmE), / rʌsˈtɪsɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being rustic, simple, or unsophisticated in manner or appearance
quality of being rustic or simple

Examples

The rusticity of the old farmhouse was part of its unique charm.
Her clothes and manners had a certain rusticity that set her apart from city dwellers.
He was drawn to the rusticity and tranquility of rural life.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sophistication - the quality of being sophisticated
  • Synonym: rustic - having a simplicity and charm typical of the countryside

Etymology

From Latin 'rusticus' (rural, simple).
