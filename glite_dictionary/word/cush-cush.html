cush-cush

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊʃˌkʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈkʊʃˌkʊʃ / (BrE)
a tropical American yam with small yellow edible tubers, cultivated for food
tropical American yam with edible tubers

Examples

Farmers in Venezuela often grow cush-cush for its nutritious tubers.
The small yellow tubers of cush-cush are commonly boiled or roasted as a staple food.
In some regions, cush-cush is an important crop during the rainy season.
