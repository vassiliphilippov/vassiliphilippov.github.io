multiplied

1. ADJ

/ ˈmʌl.təˌplaɪd / (AmE), / ˈmʌl.tɪˌplaɪd / (BrE)
Having grown or increased greatly in number or amount, especially as a result of multiplication.
greatly increased in number or amount

Examples

The city has faced multiplied challenges due to rapid population growth.
With multiplied resources, the team achieved remarkable progress in a short time.
Their efforts resulted in multiplied opportunities for everyone involved.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: increased - made greater in size, amount, or degree
  • Antonym: diminished - made smaller or less
