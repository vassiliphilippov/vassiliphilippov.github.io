Lesvos

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɛz.voʊs / (AmE), / ˈlɛz.vɒs / (BrE)
an island of eastern Greece in the eastern Aegean Sea, historically known for its lyric poetry
Greek island in the eastern Aegean Sea

Examples

She spent her summer vacation exploring the ancient ruins on Lesvos.
Lesvos is renowned for its beautiful beaches and traditional Greek villages.
The poet Sappho was born on Lesvos in the 7th century BC.

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Λέσβος (Lésbos), the island's original name.
