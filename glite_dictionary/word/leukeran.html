Leukeran

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːkəˌræn / (AmE), / ˈluːkəˌræn / (BrE)
a brand name for the alkylating agent chlorambucil, used in the treatment of certain cancers such as leukemia and lymphoma
a chemotherapy drug (chlorambucil)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Leukeran as part of her chemotherapy regimen.
Leukeran is commonly used to treat certain forms of leukemia and lymphoma.
After taking Leukeran, the patient was monitored closely for side effects.
