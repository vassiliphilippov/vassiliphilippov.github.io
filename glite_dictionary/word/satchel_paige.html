Satchel Paige

1. PROPN

/ ˈsætʃəl peɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsætʃəl peɪdʒ / (BrE)
(1906–1982) United States baseball player, renowned as an African American pitcher noted for his long playing career and remarkable skill.
African American baseball pitcher (1906–1982)

Examples

Many baseball historians consider Satchel Paige one of the greatest pitchers of all time.
Satchel Paige made his Major League debut at an age when most players retire.
The stories about Satchel Paige's fastball and longevity have become legendary in American sports.
