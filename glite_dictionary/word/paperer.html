paperer

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpərər / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpərə / (BrE)
a person whose job is decorating walls with wallpaper
wallpaper decorator

Examples

We hired a skilled paperer to decorate our living room with floral wallpaper.
As a professional paperer, she always brings her own tools to every job.
The paperer finished the hallway in just two days, leaving the walls looking elegant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wallpaperer - a person who applies wallpaper

Etymology

From 'paper' + suffix '-er' indicating a person who does an action.
