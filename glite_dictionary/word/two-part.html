two-part

1. ADJ

/ ˈtuˌpɑrt / (AmE), / ˈtuːˌpɑːt / (BrE)
consisting of or involving two parts or elements
consisting of two parts

Examples

The novel is structured as a two-part narrative, each from a different character's perspective.
We watched a two-part documentary on World War II last night.
The proposal included a two-part solution to address both immediate and long-term needs.

Synset Examples

a bipartite document
a two-way treaty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bipartite - divided into or composed of two parts
