nonuple

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɑːn.juː.pəl / (AmE), / ˈnɒn.juː.pəl / (BrE)
consisting of nine parts, units, or elements; being nine times as great or as many
having nine units or components

Examples

The artist created a nonuple panel arrangement for the exhibition, using nine unique paintings.
The investigation revealed a nonuple increase in output over the past decade.
Our choir performed a nonuple harmony, dividing the singers into nine vocal groups.

Etymology

From Latin 'nonus' (nine) + '-uple' (forming adjectives indicating multiplication).
