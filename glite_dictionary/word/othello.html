Othello

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈθɛloʊ / (AmE), / əˈθɛləʊ / (BrE)
the Moorish general of Venice and protagonist in William Shakespeare's tragedy 'Othello', who is manipulated into doubting his wife's fidelity
protagonist of Shakespeare's tragedy 'Othello'

Examples

Many actors aspire to play Othello due to the depth of the character.
The story of Othello explores jealousy, trust, and betrayal.
In literature classes, Othello is often studied for its tragic themes.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Desdemona - Othello's wife in Shakespeare's tragedy 'Othello'
  • Degree Lower: Iago - the antagonist in Shakespeare's 'Othello' who manipulates Othello
