spaghettini

1. NOUN

/ spəˌɡetˈtiːni / (AmE), / spəˌɡetˈtiːni / (BrE)
A type of pasta similar to spaghetti but thinner.
thin pasta, thinner than spaghetti

Examples

The chef recommended using spaghettini for a lighter pasta dish.
She prefers spaghettini with olive oil and garlic because of its delicate texture.
Boil the spaghettini for just five minutes to keep it al dente.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: spaghetti - long, thin cylindrical pasta

Etymology

Italian, diminutive of 'spaghetti', meaning 'little strings'.
