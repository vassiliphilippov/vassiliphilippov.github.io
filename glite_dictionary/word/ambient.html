ambient

1. ADJ

/ ˈæm.bi.ənt / (AmE), / ˈæm.bi.ənt / (BrE)
relating to the surrounding area; completely enveloping or encompassing something.
relating to the surrounding environment

Examples

The ambient noise in the café made it difficult to hold a conversation.
As the sun set, the ambient temperature dropped considerably.
He adjusted the thermostat to match the ambient air conditions.

Synset Examples

the ambient air
ambient sound
the ambient temperature

Etymology

From Latin 'ambire' (to go around, surround).
