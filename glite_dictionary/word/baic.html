Baic

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪ.ɪk / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.ɪk / (BrE)
the Tibeto-Burman language spoken in the Dali region of Yunnan, China.
Tibeto-Burman language of Yunnan

Examples

The Baic language is spoken by some ethnic groups in the Dali region of Yunnan.
Linguists are studying Baic to understand its grammar and vocabulary.
He is fluent in Baic as well as Mandarin Chinese.
