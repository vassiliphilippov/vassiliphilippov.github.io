Sulawesi

1. PROPN

/ ˌsuːləˈweɪzi / (AmE), / ˌsuːləˈweɪzi / (BrE)
a large mountainous island in eastern Indonesia, known for its distinctive shape and diverse wildlife
mountainous island in Indonesia

Examples

The island of Sulawesi is famous for its unique wildlife and complex geography.
Many traditional villages can be found in the highlands of Sulawesi.
Researchers discovered a new species of orchid native to Sulawesi.

Etymology

From Indonesian Sulawesi, of uncertain origin; possibly from local languages, combining 'sula' (island) and 'besi' (iron).
