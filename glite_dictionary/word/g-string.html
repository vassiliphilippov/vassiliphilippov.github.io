G-string

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiˌstrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.strɪŋ / (BrE)
A very narrow strip of fabric forming a minimal garment that covers the pubic area, passes between the thighs, and is held up by a waistband, typically worn as underwear or by entertainers such as striptease performers.
minimal garment; narrow strip of fabric worn as underwear

Examples

She wore a sparkling G-string during her stage performance.
Many fashion brands now sell G-strings as part of their lingerie collections.
He found a G-string among the costumes for the burlesque show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thong - a similar type of minimal underwear or swimwear

Etymology

From the initial 'G' for 'groin' or 'genital', plus 'string'.
