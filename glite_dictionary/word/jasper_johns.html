Jasper Johns

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒæspər ˈdʒɑnz / (AmE), / ˈdʒæspə ˈdʒɒnz / (BrE)
(born 1930) an American artist known for his significant contributions to pop art and for his innovative use of everyday imagery in painting and printmaking.
American pop artist (born 1930)

Examples

The Museum of Modern Art recently acquired a Jasper Johns painting for its permanent collection.
Art students often study Jasper Johns for his innovative approach to everyday objects.
A retrospective of Jasper Johns attracted record crowds at the gallery.
