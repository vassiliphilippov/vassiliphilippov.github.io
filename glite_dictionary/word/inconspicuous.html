inconspicuous

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.kənˈspɪk.ju.əs / (AmE), / ˌɪn.kənˈspɪk.ju.əs / (BrE)
Not easily seen or attracting attention; not prominent or noticeable.
not easily noticed or prominent

Examples

He chose an inconspicuous seat at the back of the classroom to avoid attention.
The spy wore inconspicuous clothing to blend in with the crowd.
An inconspicuous crack had formed in the wall, barely noticeable to the untrained eye.

Synset Examples

he pushed the string through an inconspicuous hole
the invisible man

Related Terms

  • Antonym: noticeable - easily seen or detected; attracting attention
  • Synonym: unobtrusive - not conspicuous or attracting attention

Etymology

From Latin in- (not) + conspicuus (visible, remarkable).
