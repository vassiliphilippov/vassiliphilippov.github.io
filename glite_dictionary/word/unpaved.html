unpaved

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpeɪvd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpeɪvd / (BrE)
(of a road or surface) not covered with a hard, smooth material such as asphalt or concrete.
not covered with paving

Examples

The old country lanes were unpaved and became muddy after rain.
We had to drive slowly down the unpaved road to reach the cabin.
Many hiking trails in the park are unpaved, so sturdy shoes are recommended.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: paved - covered with a hard surface such as concrete or asphalt

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'paved'.
