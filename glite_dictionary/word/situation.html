situation

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪtʃuˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪtʃuˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the set of circumstances or the condition in which someone or something finds themselves; a state of affairs
a set of conditions or circumstances

Examples

She handled the situation calmly despite the pressure from her boss.
The city is doing its best to resolve the traffic situation.
He found himself in a difficult situation after losing his wallet.

Synset Examples

the unpleasant situation (or position) of having to choose between two evils
found herself in a very fortunate situation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circumstance - fact or condition connected with an event or action
  • Synonym: predicament - a difficult, unpleasant, or embarrassing situation
  • Antonym: solution - the act of solving a problem or dealing with a difficult situation

Etymology

From Latin 'situatio' (position, place), from 'situare' (to place).
