sclerosed

1. ADJ

/ skləˈroʊzd / (AmE), / skləˈrəʊzd / (BrE)
affected by or relating to sclerosis; abnormally hardened, especially of tissue or blood vessels.
hardened by sclerosis (medical)
medical

Examples

The ultrasound revealed that the artery was sclerosed and restricting blood flow.
In older patients, sclerosed tissues may impede natural healing.
Her diagnosis was confirmed after biopsies showed sclerosed lymph nodes.

Synset Examples

a sclerotic patient

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sclerotic - relating to or affected by sclerosis; hardened

Etymology

From Greek 'skleros' (hard), via medical Latin 'sclerosis' (hardening).
