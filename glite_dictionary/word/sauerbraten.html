sauerbraten

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaʊərˌbrɑːtən / (AmE), / ˈzaʊəˌbraːtən / (BrE)
A German pot roast of beef marinated several days in seasoned vinegar and spices before cooking, often served with potato dumplings.
German marinated pot roast

Examples

My grandmother makes delicious sauerbraten every winter, marinating the beef for several days.
We ordered sauerbraten at the German restaurant, and it came with red cabbage and potato dumplings.
The recipe for sauerbraten requires patience, but the tangy flavor is worth the wait.

Etymology

From German ‘Sauerbraten’ meaning ‘sour roast’, from ‘sauer’ (sour) + ‘braten’ (roast).
