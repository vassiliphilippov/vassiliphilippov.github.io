coffeepot

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːfiˌpɑːt / (AmE), / ˈkɒfiˌpɒt / (BrE)
a tall container with a spout, used for brewing and serving coffee
container for brewing coffee

Examples

She poured the freshly brewed coffee from the coffeepot into her favorite mug.
The old silver coffeepot sat in the center of the breakfast table.
Please remember to wash out the coffeepot after the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teapot - a pot used for brewing tea

Etymology

From 'coffee' + 'pot', combining the beverage and the vessel.
