brachyurous

1. ADJ

/ brəˈkjuːrəs / (AmE), / brəˈkjʊərəs / (BrE)
relating to or belonging to the suborder Brachyura, which includes the true crabs
relating to true crabs (Brachyura)
medical

Examples

Unlike lobsters, brachyurous crustaceans possess a very short, folded abdomen.
The museum features several brachyurous specimens collected from coastal waters.
She conducted research on the brachyurous fauna of the coral reef.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Brachyura - the scientific suborder including true crabs

Etymology

From New Latin Brachyura (the crab suborder), from Greek brakhys (short) + oura (tail).
