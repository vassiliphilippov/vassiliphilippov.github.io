menstrual

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛnstruəl / (AmE), / ˈmɛnstrʊəl / (BrE)
relating to menstruation or the monthly discharge of blood from the uterus in women and female animals.
relating to menstruation

Examples

She kept track of her menstrual cycle with a calendar app.
Some women experience menstrual cramps during their period.
Doctors may ask about any irregularities in your menstrual history during a checkup.

Synset Examples

menstrual period

Related Terms

  • Synonym: menstruation - the process in which blood is discharged from the uterus

Etymology

From Latin 'menstruus' (monthly), from 'mensis' (month).
