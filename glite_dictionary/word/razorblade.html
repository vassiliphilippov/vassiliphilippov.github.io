razorblade

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪzərˌbleɪd / (AmE), / ˈreɪzəˌbleɪd / (BrE)
A small, very sharp blade, especially one used in a razor for shaving.
very sharp blade for shaving

Examples

Be careful when handling a razorblade, as it can cause serious cuts.
He replaced the old razorblade in his shaver before his morning routine.
The artist used a razorblade to make precise cuts in the paper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: razor - device with a handle and a blade for shaving
  • Synonym: knife - tool with a cutting blade and handle

Etymology

From 'razor' (device for shaving) + 'blade'.
