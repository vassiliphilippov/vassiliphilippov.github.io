naughtiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔti.nəs / (AmE), / ˈnɔːt.i.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being naughty or mischievous, especially in children
state of being naughty or mischievous

Examples

Her naughtiness got her sent to the principal's office more than once.
There was a hint of naughtiness in his cheeky smile.
Parents sometimes struggle to manage their children's naughtiness without losing patience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naughty - behaving badly or in a disobedient way, especially in children
  • Antonym: obedience - the quality of being obedient or compliant

Etymology

From the adjective 'naughty' + suffix '-ness'.
