workmate

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɜːrkˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌmeɪt / (BrE)
a person who works with you, especially someone with the same employer or at the same workplace
colleague; fellow worker

Examples

I had lunch with my workmate at the company cafeteria.
Her workmate helped her finish the project before the deadline.
Many workmates attended the farewell party after John retired.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colleague - a person you work with, especially in a profession or business
  • Synonym: coworker - a person with whom one works, typically someone in a similar role or organization

Etymology

Formed from 'work' + 'mate', first used in the 19th century.
