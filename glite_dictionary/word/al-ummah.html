al-Ummah

1. PROPN

/ æl ˈʊmɑː / (AmE), / æl ˈʊmə / (BrE)
a terrorist organization formed in India in 1992, believed to be responsible for bombings in southern India in 1998
Indian terrorist organization (1992–)

Examples

The government increased security after al-Ummah was linked to several bombings in southern India.
Authorities arrested several members of al-Ummah following a major investigation.
News reports identified al-Ummah as a key player in the 1998 Coimbatore bombings.

Etymology

From Arabic الأمة‎ (al-ummah), meaning 'the community' or 'the nation'.
