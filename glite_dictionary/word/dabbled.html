dabbled

1. ADJ

/ ˈdæbəld / (AmE), / ˈdæbəld / (BrE)
Covered or marked with bright patches, often used to describe surfaces touched by light or color.
covered with bright patches

Examples

The forest floor was dabbled with sunlight filtering through the leaves.
Her dress was dabbled with paint after the art class ended.
The old wall appeared dabbled with moss and patches of lichen.

Synset Examples

waves dabbled with moonlight
a blood-spattered room
gardens splashed with color
kitchen walls splattered with grease

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spattered - marked with spots or patches, typically of liquid
  • Synonym: splashed - marked or covered with splashes
  • Synonym: stained - marked or colored with patches or spots

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'dabble,' meaning to splash gently or dip lightly.
