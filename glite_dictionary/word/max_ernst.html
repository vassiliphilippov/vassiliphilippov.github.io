Max Ernst

1. PROPN

/ mæks ˈɜrnst / (AmE), / maks ˈɛːnst / (BrE)
(1891–1976) German painter and sculptor, a cofounder of Dadaism and a pioneer of collage techniques.
German painter and Dadaist artist

Examples

Max Ernst was a leading figure in the Dada and Surrealist movements of the early twentieth century.
The Museum of Modern Art features several paintings by Max Ernst.
Many art historians credit Max Ernst with popularizing the technique of collage in modern art.
