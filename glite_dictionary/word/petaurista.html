Petaurista

1. NOUN

/ ˌpiːtəˈrɪstə / (AmE), / ˌpiːtəˈrɪstə / (BrE)
A genus of very large Asiatic flying squirrels known for their gliding ability.
genus of large Asiatic flying squirrels

Examples

The Petaurista species are noted for their remarkable gliding abilities between trees.
Researchers observed a Petaurista in the forests of Southeast Asia.
Unlike smaller flying squirrels, Petaurista can cover greater distances thanks to their size.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin genus name, ultimately from Greek 'petauristēs' meaning 'tightrope walker, acrobat'.
