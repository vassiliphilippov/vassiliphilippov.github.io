meticulosity

1. NOUN

/ məˌtɪkjuˈlɑsəti / (AmE), / mɪˌtɪkjʊˈlɒsɪti / (BrE)
The quality of paying strict attention to minute details; meticulousness.
strict attention to minute details

Examples

Her meticulosity in reviewing each page made her an excellent editor.
The artist's meticulosity was evident in every brushstroke of the painting.
He approached the project with such meticulosity that nothing was overlooked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meticulousness - the quality of being very careful and precise
  • Antonym: carelessness - lack of attention to detail

Etymology

From Latin 'meticulosus' (fearful, overly careful) + English '-ity'.
