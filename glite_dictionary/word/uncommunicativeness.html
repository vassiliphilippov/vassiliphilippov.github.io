uncommunicativeness

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.kəˌmjuː.nɪˈkeɪ.tɪv.nəs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kəˌmjuː.nɪˈkeɪ.tɪv.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being unwilling or unable to communicate freely or openly
the state of not communicating freely

Examples

His persistent uncommunicativeness made it difficult for his colleagues to understand what he was thinking.
The interviewer's questions were met with a surprising uncommunicativeness from the usually open athlete.
Her uncommunicativeness during meetings created a barrier to effective teamwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reticence - the quality of being reserved in speech
  • Antonym: communicativeness - the quality of being willing or able to communicate

Etymology

From un- (not) + communicative + -ness.
