Forseti

1. PROPN

/ fɔrˈsɛti / (AmE), / fɔːˈsɛti / (BrE)
(in Norse mythology) the god of justice, son of Balder and Nanna.
Norse god of justice

Examples

According to legend, Forseti presided over disputes among the gods and men.
In the story, Forseti is revered for his wisdom and fairness.
The temple dedicated to Forseti was said to be a place where quarrels were settled peacefully.

Etymology

From Old Norse 'Forseti' meaning 'president' or 'chairman'.
