Kampuchea

1. PROPN

/ kæmˈpuːtʃiə / (AmE), / kæmˈpuːtʃiə / (BrE)
the former name of Cambodia, a country in Southeast Asia; used especially during periods of 20th-century history
former name of Cambodia

Examples

The official name of Cambodia between 1976 and 1989 was Kampuchea.
Many historical texts refer to the country as Kampuchea instead of Cambodia.
She studied the history of Kampuchea for her master's thesis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cambodia - modern name for the Southeast Asian country formerly known as Kampuchea

Etymology

From Khmer Kampuchea, the name for Cambodia in the Khmer language.
