clearweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɪrˌwid / (AmE), / ˈklɪəwiːd / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Pilea, characterized by drooping green flower clusters and smooth, translucent stems and leaves
a translucent-stemmed plant of the genus Pilea

Examples

The translucent stems of the clearweed make it easy to identify along shaded woodland paths.
Gardeners often mistake clearweed for other common weeds due to its lush green leaves.
After the rain, droplets cling beautifully to the leaves of clearweed in the backyard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pilea - genus of herbaceous plants including clearweed
