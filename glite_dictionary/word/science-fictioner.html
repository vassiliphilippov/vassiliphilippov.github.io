science-fictioner

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaɪəns ˈfɪkʃənər / (AmE), / ˌsaɪəns ˈfɪkʃənə / (BrE)
a person who writes or is an enthusiastic fan of science fiction
person who loves or writes science fiction

Examples

Every science-fictioner I know has a favorite alien race.
The science-fictioner created elaborate worlds in his stories.
As a true science-fictioner, he loved reading Asimov's works.

Synset Examples

Etymology

Formed from 'science fiction' + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
