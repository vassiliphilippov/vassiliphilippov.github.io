fill

1. NOUN

/ fɪl / (AmE), / fɪl / (BrE)
material used to fill a space, hole, or container, especially in construction or landscaping.
material that fills a space or container

Examples

The contractors dumped several truckloads of fill to level the building site.
After removing the old tree, the gardener used gravel as fill for the hole.
We need more fill to support the foundation of the retaining wall.

Synset Examples
