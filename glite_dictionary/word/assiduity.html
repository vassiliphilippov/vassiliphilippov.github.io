assiduity

1. NOUN

/ ˌæsəˈduːəti / (AmE), / ˌæsɪˈdjuːɪti / (BrE)
careful and persistent attention or effort towards a task
persistent diligence or attention

Examples

Her assiduity in researching every detail won her the respect of her colleagues.
The project succeeded because of his assiduity and careful planning.
Without assiduity, even the most talented individuals may fail to reach their goals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diligence - persistent and careful work or effort
  • Antonym: negligence - failure to take proper care in doing something

Etymology

From Latin 'assiduitas', from 'assiduus' meaning 'constant, persistent'.
