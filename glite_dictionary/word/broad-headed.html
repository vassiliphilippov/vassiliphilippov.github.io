broad-headed

1. ADJ

/ ˈbrɔdˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
Having a relatively wide or broad head in proportion to its length; brachycephalic.
having a wide or broad head

Examples

This species of lizard is broad-headed, which helps it hunt larger prey.
The dog was noted for its unique broad-headed appearance in the show.
Some ancient populations are classified as broad-headed in anthropological studies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brachycephalic - having a short, broad head (biology/anatomy)
