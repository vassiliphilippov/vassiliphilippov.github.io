misunderstood

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɪs.ʌndərˈstʊd / (AmE), / ˌmɪs.ʌndəˈstʊd / (BrE)
wrongly interpreted or not correctly understood by others
wrongly interpreted or understood

Examples

His motives were completely misunderstood by the public.
She felt hurt after her comment was misunderstood during the meeting.
The artist is often described as a misunderstood genius.

Synset Examples

a misunderstood criticism
a misunderstood question

Related Terms

  • Antonym: understood - comprehended correctly

Etymology

From mis- (wrongly) + understood, past participle of understand.
