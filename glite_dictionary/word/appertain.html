to appertain

1. VERB

/ ˌæpərˈteɪn / (AmE), / ˌapəˈteɪn / (BrE)
(formal) to belong to or be connected with something, especially as a rightful attribute or part
belong to; be connected with

Examples

Many legal responsibilities appertain to the office of trustee.
These privileges appertain exclusively to members of the royal family.
The document lists the rights that appertain to all citizens of the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to pertain - to relate or be relevant to something

Etymology

From Latin 'appertinere' (to pertain to, to belong to).
