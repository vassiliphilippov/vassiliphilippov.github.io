all-powerful

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔlˈpaʊər.fəl / (AmE), / ˌɔːlˈpaʊə.fəl / (BrE)
having complete or unlimited power; omnipotent.
having unlimited power

Examples

The villain in the story believed he was all-powerful and could not be defeated.
Some ancient cultures worshipped all-powerful gods.
The new technology seemed all-powerful to those who first encountered it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: omnipotent - having unlimited power and able to do anything
  • Antonym: powerless - without power or ability
