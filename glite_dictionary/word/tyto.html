Tyto

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɪtəʊ / (BrE)
A genus of owls that includes barn owls, belonging to the family Tytonidae.
genus of barn owls

Examples

The genus Tyto is known for its distinctive heart-shaped facial discs.
Several species within Tyto can be found on islands around the world.
Researchers conducted a study on the nesting habits of Tyto owls.

Etymology

From Greek tytō (an owl).
