contumelious

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑːn.tjuːˈmiː.li.əs / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.tjʊˈmiː.li.əs / (BrE)
insultingly or arrogantly disrespectful; showing contempt or scornful insolence
arrogantly disrespectful or insolent
archaic

Examples

The ambassador was dismissed for his contumelious remarks towards the host nation.
Her contumelious tone alienated the entire committee during the meeting.
He could not believe the contumelious insults hurled at him by his former friend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insolent - showing a rude and arrogant lack of respect
  • Antonym: respectful - showing politeness or honor towards someone

Etymology

From Latin contumeliosus, meaning 'insolent, abusive'.
