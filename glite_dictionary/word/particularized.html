particularized

1. ADJ

/ pərˈtɪkjəˌlɚˌaɪzd / (AmE), / pəˈtɪkjʊləraɪzd / (BrE)
Directed toward or limited to a specific person, thing, or situation; made specific or individualized.
made specific or individualized

Examples

The report offered a particularized account of each incident.
Her advice was not general but rather highly particularized to each student's needs.
We need a particularized approach instead of a one-size-fits-all solution.

Synset Examples

particularized thinking as distinct from stereotyped sloganeering

Related Terms

  • Antonym: generalized - not specific, covering many cases
  • Synonym: specific - clearly defined or identified

Etymology

From the verb 'particularize', based on 'particular' from Latin 'particularis' (of a part).
