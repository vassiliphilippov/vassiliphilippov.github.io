third-year

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɝd jɪr / (AmE), / ˈθɜːd jɪə / (BrE)
Relating to the third year of a course of study, especially in United States high schools or colleges.
in the third year of study (US education)

Examples

She was elected president of the third-year class at her college.
Many third-year students begin working on internships over the summer.
The third-year project requires a significant amount of independent research.

Synset Examples

the junior class
a third-year student

Related Terms

  • Synonym: junior - a student in the third year of high school or college (US)
