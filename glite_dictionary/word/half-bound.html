half-bound

1. ADJ

/ ˌhæfˈbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˌhɑːfˈbaʊnd / (BrE)
(of books) having the spine covered in one material, usually leather, and the sides covered in a different material, often cloth or paper.
book with spine and sides bound in different materials

Examples

The library displayed a rare half-bound edition of the novel in its special collections.
Collectors often value half-bound books for their classic aesthetic and durable construction.
She inherited several old half-bound volumes from her grandfather's study.
