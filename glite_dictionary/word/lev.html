lev

1. NOUN

/ lɛv / (AmE), / lɛv / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Bulgaria, equal to 100 stotinki
Bulgarian unit of currency

Examples

He exchanged his dollars for Bulgarian lev at the bank.
The price of the ticket is 10 lev per person.
After dinner, she paid the bill in lev.

Etymology

From Bulgarian 'lev', meaning 'lion'.
