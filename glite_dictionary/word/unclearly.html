unclearly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈklɪrli / (AmE), / ʌnˈklɪəli / (BrE)
In a way that is not clear or is difficult to understand.
in an unclear manner

Examples

She spoke so unclearly that no one could follow her argument.
The instructions were written unclearly, which made assembling the furniture difficult.
He expressed his ideas unclearly, leaving the audience confused.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clearly - in a clear or easy-to-understand manner
