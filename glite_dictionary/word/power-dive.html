to power-dive

1. VERB

/ ˈpaʊərˌdaɪv / (AmE), / ˈpaʊəˌdaɪv / (BrE)
(of an aircraft) to descend rapidly in a steep and controlled dive using engine power.
descend rapidly in a steep dive (aviation)

Examples

The fighter jet began to power-dive toward its target.
Pilots are trained to power-dive safely during emergency maneuvers.
After spotting the runway, the aircraft power-dived through the clouds.

Synset Examples

The airplane power-dived

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dive - descend steeply through the air
