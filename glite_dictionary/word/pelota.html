pelota

1. NOUN

/ pəˈloʊtə / (AmE), / pɛˈlɒtə / (BrE)
a traditional Basque or Spanish ball game, played in a walled court with a ball struck by hand or with a wickerwork racket
Basque or Spanish ball game

Examples

He competed in a pelota tournament during the festival.
Pelota is especially popular in the Basque region of Spain and France.
Spectators filled the court to watch a fast-paced game of pelota.

Etymology

Spanish, from Latin 'pilotta', diminutive of 'pila' (ball).
