Labrador

1. PROPN

/ ˈlæbrəˌdɔr / (AmE), / ˈlæbrədɔː / (BrE)
the mainland region of the Canadian province of Newfoundland and Labrador, located in the northeastern part of the Labrador Peninsula.
mainland region of Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada

Examples

The wild landscape of Labrador attracts adventurers and nature lovers from around the world.
Many indigenous peoples have lived in Labrador for thousands of years.
Snow often blankets Labrador for much of the year, making transportation difficult.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'lavrador' meaning 'farmer'; named after João Fernandes Lavrador, Portuguese explorer.
