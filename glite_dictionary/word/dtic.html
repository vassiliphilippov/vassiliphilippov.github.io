DTIC

1. NOUN

/ diː tiː aɪ siː / (AmE), / diː tiː aɪ siː / (BrE)
the agency within the U.S. Department of Defense responsible for providing scientific and technical information to federal agencies and contractors
Department of Defense technical information agency

Examples

The DTIC plays a vital role in distributing scientific research within the Department of Defense.
Contractors can request documents through the DTIC portal online.
Much of the technological innovation in the military can be traced back to reports archived by the DTIC.

Etymology

Acronym for Defense Technical Information Center.
