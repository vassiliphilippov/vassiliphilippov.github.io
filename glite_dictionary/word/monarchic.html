monarchic

1. ADJ

/ məˈnɑːrkɪk / (AmE), / məˈnɑːkɪk / (BrE)
ruled by or relating to a monarch; having a system of government in which a monarch holds supreme power
relating to rule by a monarch

Examples

The country maintained a monarchic form of government for centuries.
Historians have studied the transition from monarchic to republican systems in Europe.
Despite some reforms, the state continued to operate under a monarchic constitution.

Synset Examples

monarchal government
monarchical systems

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monarchical - relating to a monarchy or monarchs
  • Antonym: republican - relating to a republic (as contrasted with a monarchy)

Etymology

From Greek monarkhikos, from monarkhia (monarchy).
