double-knotting

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌbəl ˌnɑtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl ˌnɒtɪŋ / (BrE)
The action of tying a knot using two loops to make it more secure.
tying a knot with two loops

Examples

She always double-knots her shoelaces so they don’t come undone during gym class.
After double-knotting the rope, he was confident it would hold the tent securely in place.
It took him a moment to undo the double-knotting in his sneakers.

Synset Examples

He tied his shoes tightly, double-knotting the lace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knot - an interlacement of the parts of one or more flexible bodies, forming a lump or knob, for fastening or securing something
  • Synonym: tie - to fasten something with a cord or rope
