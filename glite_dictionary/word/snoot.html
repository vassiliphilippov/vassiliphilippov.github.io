snoot

1. NOUN

/ snut / (AmE), / snuːt / (BrE)
(informal) a person's nose.
nose (informal)
informal

Examples

The cold wind stung his snoot as he walked outside.
She wrinkled her snoot at the unpleasant smell.
Wipe your snoot before coming to the table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nose - the part of the face used for smelling and breathing
  • Synonym: snout - the projecting nose or jaws of an animal

Etymology

Probably a variant of "snout."

2. NOUN

/ snut / (AmE), / snuːt / (BrE)
a person who acts in a condescending or arrogant manner, and is therefore considered annoying
an arrogant, annoying person
informal slang

Examples

Everyone thinks he's a total snoot just because he went to an Ivy League school.
She didn't want to hang out with the snoots at the art gallery opening.
Don't be such a snoot; no one likes it when you act superior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snob - a person who believes they are superior and looks down on others
  • Synonym: elitist - a person who believes or acts as if they belong to an elite group
  • Antonym: egalitarian - someone who believes all people are equal and should be treated as such

Etymology

Variant of 'snout', possibly influenced by dialect forms meaning 'nose' and thus by extension someone who looks down their nose at others.
