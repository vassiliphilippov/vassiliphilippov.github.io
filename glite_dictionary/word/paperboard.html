paperboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪ.pɚˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ.pəˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a thick, stiff paper-based material, often used for making posters, packaging, and cartons.
thick, stiff paper material

Examples

The sign was mounted on a large sheet of paperboard for stability.
She used colored paperboard to create a striking visual display for the project.
Many cereal boxes are made from recycled paperboard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cardboard - a stiff, heavy paper used for making boxes and posters
