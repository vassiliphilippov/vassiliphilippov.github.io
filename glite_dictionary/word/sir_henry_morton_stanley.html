Sir Henry Morton Stanley

1. PROPN

/ sɚ ˈhɛnri ˈmɔrtən ˈstænli / (AmE), / sə ˈhɛnri ˈmɔːtən ˈstænli / (BrE)
(1841–1904) Welsh journalist and explorer, best known for his expedition to Africa in search of David Livingstone and his later explorations.
Welsh journalist and explorer (1841–1904)

Examples

Sir Henry Morton Stanley is famous for finding Dr. Livingstone in Central Africa in 1871.
The adventurous life of Sir Henry Morton Stanley inspired many later explorers.
Many books chronicle the expeditions of Sir Henry Morton Stanley across Africa.
