Psenes

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːniːz / (AmE), / ˈsiːniːz / (BrE)
A genus of driftfishes in the family Stromateidae, found in tropical and subtropical seas.
a genus of driftfishes

Examples

The genus Psenes includes several species of driftfish found worldwide.
Researchers studied the migratory patterns of Psenes in the Atlantic Ocean.
Juvenile Psenes are often found drifting with floating seaweed.

Etymology

From Greek *psēnēs* (a kind of fish).
