unconvinced

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkənˈvɪnst / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈvɪnst / (BrE)
not persuaded or certain; lacking conviction or belief in something
not persuaded or lacking conviction

Examples

She listened politely but looked unconvinced by his explanation.
Many experts remain unconvinced that the new policy will work.
Despite the evidence, I am still unconvinced by their claims.

Synset Examples

I remain unconvinced

Related Terms

  • Antonym: convinced - certain or persuaded about something

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'convinced'.
