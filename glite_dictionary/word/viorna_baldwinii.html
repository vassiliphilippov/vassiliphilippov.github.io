Viorna baldwinii

1. NOUN

/ viˈɔrnə bɔlˈdwɪni / (AmE), / viˈɔːnə bɔːlˈdwɪni / (BrE)
A species of erect clematis native to Florida, characterized by pink to purple flowers.
a species of clematis with pink to purple flowers in Florida

Examples

The Viorna baldwinii adds a splash of color to gardens across Florida with its vibrant blooms.
Botanists identified the unusual flower as Viorna baldwinii during their field survey.
We spotted a rare Viorna baldwinii growing wild along the hiking trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clematis - a genus of climbing or sprawling plants with showy flowers

Etymology

Named after American botanist William Baldwin.
