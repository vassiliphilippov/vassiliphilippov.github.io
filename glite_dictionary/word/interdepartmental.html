interdepartmental

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərdɪˌpɑrtməntˈæl / (AmE), / ˌɪntədɪˌpɑːtmənˈtæl / (BrE)
Existing or occurring between two or more departments within an organization.
between departments

Examples

The company encouraged interdepartmental communication to improve collaboration and efficiency.
We organized an interdepartmental meeting to address shared challenges.
An interdepartmental committee was formed to review all safety policies.

Synset Examples

interdepartmental competition

Etymology

From Latin prefix 'inter-' (between) and 'departmental' (relating to a department).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03071519-a
Synset POS: a

2. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərdɪˌpɑrtˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntədɪˌpɑːtˈmɛntl / (BrE)
existing or occurring between departments within an organization, such as a business or university
between departments

Examples

The project required close interdepartmental collaboration to meet its goals.
An interdepartmental memo was sent to all managers outlining the new procedures.
Effective interdepartmental communication is vital for large organizations to function smoothly.

Synset Examples

this memo was circulated interdepartmentally

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intradepartmental - existing or occurring within a single department

Etymology

Formed from inter- (between) + departmental (relating to a department).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00381934-r
Synset POS: r
