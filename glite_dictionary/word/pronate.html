to pronate

1. VERB

/ ˈproʊˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.neɪt / (BrE)
to turn the forearm or hand so that the palm faces downward or backward
turn palm downward (hand/forearm)
medical

Examples

When you pronate your hand, the palm faces downwards on the table.
The therapist instructed her to pronate her forearm before lifting the weight.
Some people naturally pronate their feet slightly while walking.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to supinate - to turn the palm or forearm upward or forward

Etymology

From Latin 'pronatus' (bent forward), past participle of 'pronare'.
