unshaven

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈʃeɪvən / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃeɪvən / (BrE)
(of a person or part of the body) not having been shaved; showing stubble or hair growth.
not shaved; showing stubble

Examples

He looked tired and unshaven after the long journey.
The actor played an unshaven detective in the film.
She preferred the rugged, unshaven look for casual weekends.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: shaven - (of a person or part of the body) having been shaved; smooth from shaving
  • Degree Higher: bearded - having a beard; covered with a growth of hair on the chin and cheeks

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'shaven' (past participle of shave).
