reaffiliation

1. NOUN

/ ˌriːəˌfɪliˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːəˌfɪliˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of affiliating again or being affiliated anew, especially after a period of separation or disaffiliation
act of affiliating again

Examples

After years of independence, the club's reaffiliation with the national association was welcomed by its members.
The union's reaffiliation came after prolonged negotiations and significant organizational change.
He stressed that any reaffiliation would require adhering to the group's core principles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affiliation - the process or state of being officially attached or associated

Etymology

Formed from re- (again) + affiliation.
