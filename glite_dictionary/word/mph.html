mph

1. NOUN

/ ɛm piː eɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɛm piː eɪtʃ / (BrE)
an abbreviation for miles per hour, a measurement of speed equal to the number of miles traveled in one hour
abbreviation for miles per hour (speed)
american british

Examples

The speed limit on the highway is 65 mph.
Her new car can reach 120 mph in just seconds.
The runner was clocked at 18 mph during the final sprint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: km/h - abbreviation for kilometers per hour, a measurement of speed

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'miles per hour.'
