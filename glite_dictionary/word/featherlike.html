featherlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɛðərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɛðəlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or suggesting a feather or feathers, especially in shape, lightness, or texture.
resembling a feather

Examples

The artist used featherlike strokes to create a sense of movement in the painting.
The snow fell in featherlike flakes, drifting softly to the ground.
She wore a scarf with a featherlike texture that felt gentle against her skin.

Synset Examples

feathery palm trees

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feathery - covered with or resembling feathers
