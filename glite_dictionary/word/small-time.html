small-time

1. ADJ

/ ˈsmɔl taɪm / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːl taɪm / (BrE)
of minor importance, status, or scale; not prominent or significant
of minor or insignificant importance

Examples

He's just a small-time thief, not a major criminal.
Many small-time businesses struggle to compete with large corporations.
As a small-time actor, she found it hard to land leading roles.

Synset Examples

a nickel-and-dime operation run out of a single rented room
a small-time actor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minor - of lesser importance or seriousness
  • Synonym: insignificant - too small or unimportant to be worth consideration
  • Antonym: big-time - of great importance, scale, or fame
