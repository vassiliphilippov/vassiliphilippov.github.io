meteorological

1. ADJ

/ ˌmiːtiərəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmiːtiərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the science of weather and atmospheric conditions.
relating to weather or atmospheric science

Examples

The meteorological data suggested a storm was approaching the coast.
She works as a meteorological analyst for a national weather service.
The pilot checked the meteorological report before planning the flight route.

Synset Examples

meteorological factors
meteorological chart
meteoric (or meteorological) phenomena

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meteorology - the scientific study of the atmosphere and weather

Etymology

From French météorologique, from Greek meteōrologia (study of phenomena in the sky).
