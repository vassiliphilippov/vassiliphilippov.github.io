earphone

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪrˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɪəfəʊn / (BrE)
A device held over or inserted into the ear, used to convert electrical signals into sound for listening to audio privately.
audio device worn in or over the ear

Examples

He listened to music quietly through his earphones on the train.
Please make sure the earphones are plugged in before starting the call.
She prefers wireless earphones for jogging because they don't get tangled.

Synset Examples

it was not the typing but the earphones that she disliked

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headphone - a pair of audio devices worn over both ears

Etymology

From 'ear' + 'phone', patterned after 'telephone'.
