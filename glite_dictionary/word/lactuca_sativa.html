Lactuca sativa

1. NOUN

/ lækˈtuːkə səˈtaɪvə / (AmE), / lækˈtjuːkə səˈtiːvə / (BrE)
The scientific name for lettuce, an annual or perennial garden plant with succulent leaves, widely grown for use in salads.
scientific name for lettuce plant

Examples

Lactuca sativa is cultivated worldwide as the main source of fresh lettuce in salads.
The crisp leaves of Lactuca sativa make it a favorite ingredient in sandwiches and wraps.
Researchers often study Lactuca sativa to understand the genetics of leafy vegetables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lettuce - common name for Lactuca sativa, edible leafy vegetable

Etymology

From Latin; 'Lactuca' meaning 'lettuce' and 'sativa' meaning 'cultivated'.
