Loestrin

1. NOUN

/ loʊˈɛstrɪn / (AmE), / ləʊˈɛstrɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for an oral contraceptive pill containing estradiol and norethindrone.
oral contraceptive brand name
medical

Examples

After her doctor prescribed Loestrin, she noticed fewer side effects compared to her previous birth control.
Many women rely on Loestrin for its low-dose hormone formula.
He picked up a refill of Loestrin at the pharmacy for his partner.
