annelidan

1. ADJ

/ əˈnɛlɪdən / (AmE), / əˈnɛlɪdən / (BrE)
relating to, belonging to, or characteristic of worms of the phylum Annelida (segmented worms).
relating to segmented worms (Annelida)

Examples

The marine biologist studied several annelidan species found along the coastline.
Their movement is an example of typical annelidan locomotion, using body segments to contract and expand.
Some annelidan worms build tubes in the mud as a form of protection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annelid - a worm of the phylum Annelida

Etymology

From New Latin Annelida (the scientific name of the phylum) + English suffix -an.
