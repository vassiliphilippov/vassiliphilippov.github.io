cancelled

1. ADJ

/ ˈkæn.səld / (AmE), / ˈkæn.səld / (BrE)
(of an event, arrangement, etc.) no longer planned or scheduled; called off.
(of events) no longer planned or scheduled

Examples

Due to heavy snow, tonight's performance has been cancelled.
All flights were cancelled because of the strike.
The meeting is cancelled and will be rescheduled for next week.

Synset Examples

the wedding is definitely off

Related Terms

  • Synonym: off - (of events) cancelled or not happening
  • Antonym: scheduled - planned or arranged to happen at a particular time

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'cancel', from Latin 'cancellare' (to cross out, annul).
