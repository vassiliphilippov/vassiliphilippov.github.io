headshot

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈhɛdʃɒt / (BrE)
an attempt to score a goal in soccer by striking the ball with one's head
soccer attempt using the head

Examples

He scored with a brilliant headshot in the second half of the match.
The goalkeeper made a fantastic save to block the headshot from close range.
She practiced her headshot technique every day to improve her chances of scoring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: header - striking the ball with the head in soccer

3. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈhɛdʃɒt / (BrE)
A shot or blow aimed directly at a person's head, often with the intent to incapacitate or kill.
a shot aimed at the head

Examples

The sniper waited patiently for the perfect moment to take a headshot.
In many video games, scoring a headshot deals the most damage to your opponent.
The detective concluded that the victim died instantly from a single headshot.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: body shot - a shot aimed at the body
