oropharyngeal

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔːroʊfəˈrɪndʒiəl / (AmE), / ˌɔːrəʊfəˈrɪndʒiəl / (BrE)
relating to or situated near the oropharynx, the part of the throat at the back of the mouth.
relating to the oropharynx
medical

Examples

The doctor performed an oropharyngeal examination to check for signs of infection.
Oropharyngeal cancer rates have been increasing in recent years due to changes in risk factors.
Proper airway management may require the use of an oropharyngeal airway device.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oropharynx - the part of the pharynx that lies between the soft palate and the epiglottis

Etymology

From 'oropharynx' (combination of Greek 'oros' meaning 'mouth' and 'pharynx') plus suffix '-eal' meaning 'relating to'.
