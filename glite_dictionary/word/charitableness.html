charitableness

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃær.ɪ.tə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtʃær.ɪ.tə.bəl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being kind and generous, especially in giving help or money to those in need
generosity; quality of being charitable

Examples

Her charitableness was known throughout the community, as she was always willing to help those in need.
The organization's success depends on the charitableness of its donors.
He admired her charitableness and tried to follow her example by volunteering at the shelter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charity - the voluntary giving of help to those in need
  • Synonym: generosity - the quality of being generous
  • Antonym: selfishness - the quality of putting one's own interests before others

Etymology

Formed from 'charitable' + '-ness', ultimately from Old French 'charité' and Latin 'caritas' (dearness, charity).
