terrifying

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛr.əˌfaɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɛr.ɪ.faɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
causing intense fear or terror; extremely frightening
causing extreme fear

Examples

The movie’s terrifying scenes had the audience gripping their seats.
She heard a terrifying roar outside her window in the middle of the night.
Climbing the steep cliff without ropes was a terrifying experience for him.

Synset Examples

a terrifying wail

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frightening - causing fear or alarm
  • Antonym: comforting - making one feel less afraid; giving comfort

Etymology

From Latin 'terrificare' (to frighten), via 'terrify' + '-ing'.
