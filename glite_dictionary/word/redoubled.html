redoubled

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈdaʊbəld / (AmE), / rɪˈdaʊbəld / (BrE)
having become much greater in intensity, size, or amount; increased considerably or with greater force.
greatly increased; intensified

Examples

The team made a redoubled effort to finish the project on time.
With redoubled determination, she set out to achieve her goals.
After the setback, they launched a redoubled campaign to win support.

Synset Examples

we faced redoubled attacks from the enemy
despite our redoubled efforts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intensified - having become stronger or more intense

Etymology

From the prefix 're-' (again) and 'double', originally meaning 'to double again'.
