unbanded

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbændɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbændɪd / (BrE)
Not marked or identified with a band, especially referring to animals or objects without a distinguishing band or ring.
not marked with a band

Examples

The researcher recorded the sighting of an unbanded owl in the forest.
Many unbanded birds migrate each year, making it more difficult to track their movements.
The animal rescue team released several unbanded turtles into the river.

Synset Examples

an unbanded bird

Related Terms

  • Antonym: banded - marked or identified with a band
