babbitting

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæbɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbæbɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of lining a surface or bearing with Babbitt metal to reduce friction and wear
lining bearings with Babbitt metal

Examples

The mechanic performed babbitting to restore the worn bearing surface.
Proper babbitting is essential for the longevity of heavy machinery bearings.
He specializes in babbitting for vintage automobile engines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Babbitt metal - a soft alloy used for making bearings

Etymology

From Babbitt, the name of the inventor Isaac Babbitt, combined with the suffix -ing.
