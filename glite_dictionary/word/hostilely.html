hostilely

1. ADV

/ ˈhɑː.stəl.li / (AmE), / ˈhɒs.taɪl.li / (BrE)
In a hostile or unfriendly manner; showing or feeling hostility.
in a hostile or unfriendly way

Examples

She glared hostilely at her opponent across the table.
The two groups spoke hostilely to each other during the debate.
He responded hostilely to any suggestion that he change his plans.

Synset Examples

he pushed her against the wall belligerently

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aggressively - in an aggressive or forceful way
  • Antonym: amicably - in a friendly or peaceable manner

Etymology

From 'hostile' + '-ly', forming the adverb.
