ruining

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈruːɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of causing the destruction or severe damage of something; the process of ruining.
act of causing destruction or damage

Examples

The constant flooding led to the ruining of many historic homes in the town.
Carelessness in handling the artifact resulted in the complete ruining of its original paint.
The ruining of the landscape by the new construction project upset local residents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: destruction - the act of destroying; the state of being destroyed
  • Antonym: preservation - the act of keeping something in its original state or in good condition

Etymology

From the verb 'ruin' + -ing (noun-forming suffix).
