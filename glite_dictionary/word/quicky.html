quicky

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɪki / (AmE), / ˈkwɪki / (BrE)
A hurried or makeshift repair or adjustment done quickly, often for a temporary fix.
hurried or makeshift repair
informal

Examples

He managed a quicky to patch the leak until a plumber arrived.
Don't expect the quicky on your car to last very long.
Sometimes all you have time for is a quicky to keep things running.
