perspicuity

1. NOUN

/ pɜrspɪˈkjuːɪti / (AmE), / ˌpɜːspɪˈkjuːɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being clear and easy to understand; clarity of expression.
clarity; ease of understanding

Examples

The perspicuity of her writing made complex topics accessible to a wide audience.
Legal documents often lack the perspicuity expected in plain language communication.
He was admired for the perspicuity with which he explained difficult scientific concepts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clarity - the quality of being clear, in particular
  • Antonym: obscurity - the state of being difficult to understand

Etymology

From Latin perspicuitas (clearness), from perspicuus (transparent, clear).
