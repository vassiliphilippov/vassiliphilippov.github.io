touristed

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʊrɪstɪd / (AmE), / ˈtʊərɪstɪd / (BrE)
(of a place) frequently visited by large numbers of tourists; crowded with tourists.
heavily visited by tourists

Examples

Some locals avoid the touristed areas during the summer months.
The ancient city has become increasingly touristed in recent years.
Although beautiful, the beach is now one of the most touristed spots in the country.

Synset Examples

of the three American Virgin islands St. Thomas is the most touristed
tourists descend in busloads … so the whole place is rather touristy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: touristy - (of a place) attractive to or frequented by tourists, often with a negative connotation of being commercialized
