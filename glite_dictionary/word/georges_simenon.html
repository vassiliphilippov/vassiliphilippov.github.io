Georges Simenon

1. PROPN

/ ʒɔrʒ siˈmeɪnɒn / (AmE), / ʒɔːʒ siˈmeɪnɒ̃ / (BrE)
(1903–1989) Belgian-born French writer best known for his detective novels featuring Inspector Maigret.
