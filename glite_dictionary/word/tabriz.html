Tabriz

1. PROPN

/ təˈbriːz / (AmE), / təˈbriːz / (BrE)
A historic city in northwestern Iran, known for its cultural, commercial, and historical importance.
ancient city in northwestern Iran

Examples

Tabriz is famous for its traditional bazaars and handmade carpets.
The earthquake near Tabriz drew international attention to the city.
Many historical mosques and museums can be found in Tabriz.
