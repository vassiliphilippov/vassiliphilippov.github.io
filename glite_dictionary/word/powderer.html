powderer

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊdərər / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdərə / (BrE)
a person who applies or scatters powder, especially onto the body or hair
person who applies or scatters powder

Examples

The powderer prepared the actor's face before filming began.
In the eighteenth century, a powderer would dust wigs with perfumed powder.
She worked as a powderer in a busy beauty salon downtown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powder - fine dry particles, or any substance in this form

Etymology

From 'powder' + agent noun suffix '-er'.
