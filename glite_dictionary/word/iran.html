Iran

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈrɑn / (AmE), / ɪˈrɑːn / (BrE)
A theocratic Islamic republic in the Middle East, formerly known as Persia; known for its rich history and oil resources.
Country in the Middle East, formerly Persia

Examples

The ancient civilization of Iran has contributed greatly to world history.
She traveled to Iran to study its unique cultural heritage.
Iran is known for its rich reserves of oil and natural gas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Persia - historical name for Iran

Etymology

Modern name adopted in 1935; from Persian 'Īrān', meaning 'land of the Aryans'.
