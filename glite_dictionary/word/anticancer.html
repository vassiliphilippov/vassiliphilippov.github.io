anticancer

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.tiˈkæn.sɚ / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈkæn.sə / (BrE)
(of a substance, drug, or treatment) used to prevent or treat cancer.
used to prevent or treat cancer
medical

Examples

Etymology
