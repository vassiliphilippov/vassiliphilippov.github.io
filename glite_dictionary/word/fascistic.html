fascistic

1. ADJ

/ fəˈʃɪstɪk / (AmE), / fəˈʃɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of fascism or fascists.
relating to fascism

Examples

Some critics described the government's policies as fascistic in their approach to dissent.
The film depicted a fascistic regime suppressing all forms of opposition.
His fascistic rhetoric alarmed many people in the audience.

Synset Examples

fascist propaganda

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fascist - A person who supports or practices fascism
  • Antonym: democratic - Relating to or supporting democracy

Etymology

Formed from 'fascist' + '-ic', where 'fascist' is from Italian 'fascista' (member of a fascio, a bundle or group).
