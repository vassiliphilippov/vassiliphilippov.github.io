funds

1. NOUN

/ fʌndz / (AmE), / fʌndz / (BrE)
money that is available for a particular purpose or as a resource.
money available for use

Examples

The company needed additional funds to expand its operations.
Due to a lack of funds, the project was postponed until next year.
She donated her funds to support local education initiatives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: money - general term for currency in any form, especially circulating coins and banknotes
  • Synonym: capital - wealth in the form of money or assets, used for investment

Etymology

Plural of 'fund', from Latin 'fundus' (bottom, foundation), later meaning a sum of money set aside.
