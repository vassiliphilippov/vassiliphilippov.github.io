afterworld

1. NOUN

/ ˈæftərˌwɝld / (AmE), / ˈɑːftəˌwɜːld / (BrE)
the world or existence believed to follow death, often as conceived in religion or mythology
existence or realm after death

Examples

Many cultures have myths describing what happens in the afterworld.
She wondered if there would be peace waiting for her in the afterworld.
The book explores beliefs about the afterworld from different religions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: afterlife - existence or life after death

Etymology

Formed from 'after' + 'world', meaning the world that comes after this one.
