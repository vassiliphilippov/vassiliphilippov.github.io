bassoonist

1. NOUN

/ bəˈsuːnɪst / (AmE), / bəˈsuːnɪst / (BrE)
a musician who plays the bassoon
musician who plays the bassoon

Examples

The orchestra hired a new bassoonist for the upcoming concert season.
She has been a professional bassoonist for over twenty years.
The talented bassoonist performed a challenging solo piece at the recital.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musician - a person who plays a musical instrument or is musically talented

Etymology

From 'bassoon' + suffix '-ist', denoting a person who plays or is skilled in a particular instrument.
