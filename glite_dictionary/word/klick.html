klick

1. NOUN

/ klɪk / (AmE), / klɪk / (BrE)
an informal term for a kilometer, especially used by military personnel
informal term for kilometer
informal slang

Examples

The convoy was delayed when it was just two klicks from the base.
Our objective is only five klicks north of here.
He radioed that he was less than one klick away from the rendezvous point.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kilometer - a metric unit of length equal to 1000 meters

Etymology

Origin unknown; popularized in military slang, possibly from mishearing of 'kilometer' or use in U.S. military radio jargon.
