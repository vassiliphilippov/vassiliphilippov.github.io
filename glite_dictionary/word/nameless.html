nameless

1. ADJ

/ ˈneɪmləs / (AmE), / ˈneɪmləs / (BrE)
(of a person, source, or thing) having an unknown or unspecified name; not identified by name.
having an unknown or unspecified name

Examples

The donation came from a nameless benefactor who wished to remain anonymous.
The city was filled with monuments to nameless heroes who sacrificed their lives.
She found an old letter signed only by a nameless admirer.

Synset Examples

a poem by an unknown author
corporations responsible to nameless owners
an unnamed donor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anonymous - having no known name or identity
  • Antonym: identified - having a known or stated name

Etymology

From name + -less (Old English nameless).
