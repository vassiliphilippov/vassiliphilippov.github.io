looker

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʊkər / (AmE), / ˈlʊkə / (BrE)
an attractive or strikingly beautiful person, especially a woman
attractive or strikingly beautiful person
informal

Examples

Everyone at the party agreed she was quite a looker in that dress.
He couldn't take his eyes off the looker who walked into the café.
Back in college, my aunt was considered a real looker by her peers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty - a beautiful woman
