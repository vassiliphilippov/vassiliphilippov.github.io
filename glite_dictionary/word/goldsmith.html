goldsmith

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldˌsmɪθ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌsmɪθ / (BrE)
a person who makes jewelry and other objects from gold
artisan working with gold

Examples

The goldsmith carefully shaped the delicate necklace from a piece of pure gold.
Centuries ago, a goldsmith held great importance in the royal court.
She apprenticed with a skilled goldsmith to learn the art of making fine jewelry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silversmith - a person who makes objects out of silver

Etymology

Old English 'goldsmith', from 'gold' + 'smith'.
