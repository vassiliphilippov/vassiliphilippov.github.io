showboy

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊ.bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ.bɔɪ / (BrE)
a man who dances in a chorus line, especially in theatrical or musical productions
male chorus line dancer

Examples

He auditioned for the role of showboy in the upcoming musical.
The showboys dazzled the audience with their perfectly synchronized dance routines.
As a showboy, he spent hours rehearsing with the rest of the chorus line.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: showgirl - a woman who dances or performs in a chorus line

Etymology

Formed from 'show' (performance) + 'boy'. Patterned after 'showgirl'.
