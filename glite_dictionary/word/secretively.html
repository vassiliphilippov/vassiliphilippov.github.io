secretively

1. ADV

/ ˈsiːkrəˌtɪvli / (AmE), / ˈsiːkrətɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner that is inclined to secrecy or the concealment of information or intentions.
in a secretive manner

Examples

He glanced secretively at the envelope before slipping it into his pocket.
The children whispered secretively in the corner, plotting their surprise.
She secretively avoided answering questions about her weekend plans.

Synset Examples

the teacher raised a hand to him, slightly and secretively, because he was her favorite

Related Terms

  • Antonym: openly - in a manner that is not secretive; publicly
  • Synonym: secretly - in a secret manner or without others knowing
