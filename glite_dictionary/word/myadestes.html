Myadestes

1. NOUN

/ maɪˈædəstiːz / (AmE), / mɪˈadəstiːz / (BrE)
A genus of thrush-like songbirds known as solitaires, found mainly in Central and South America and Hawaii.
genus of thrush-like songbirds (solitaires)

Examples

The Myadestes genus comprises several species of solitaires with melodious songs.
Researchers discovered a new Myadestes species in the mountain forests of Costa Rica.
Many native Hawaiian forests are home to endangered Myadestes birds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solitaire - a type of bird in the genus Myadestes
  • Degree Higher: thrush - a songbird related to those in the genus Myadestes

Etymology

From Greek myadestēs, related to blowing the nose (possibly referencing the bird’s call).
