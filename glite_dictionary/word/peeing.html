peeing

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpiːɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of urinating; passing urine
informal term for urination
informal

Examples

He was caught peeing behind the bushes during the outdoor concert.
Children often find peeing in the potty exciting during toilet training.
Public peeing is prohibited in most cities.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urination - the process of discharging urine
  • Antonym: defecation - the act of discharging feces from the body

Etymology

From the verb 'pee', which is based on the initial letter of 'piss'.
