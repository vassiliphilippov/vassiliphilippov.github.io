to demystify

1. VERB

/ diˈmɪstəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / diˈmɪstɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to make something easier to understand by removing mystery or confusion from it
make something easier to understand

Examples

Her class aims to demystify complex scientific concepts for high school students.
The documentary helped demystify the process of wine making for viewers.
Can you demystify this software for beginners who have never used it before?

Synset Examples

let's demystify the event by explaining what it is all about

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to clarify - to make something clear or easier to understand

Etymology

From de- (removal) + mystify (from French mystifier, based on mystère 'mystery')
