nonparticipation

1. NOUN

/ nɑnˌpɑrtɪsɪˈpeɪʃən / (AmE), / nɒnˌpɑːtɪsɪˈpeɪʃən / (BrE)
The act of not taking part or being involved in an activity or group.
lack of participation

Examples

Their nonparticipation in the meeting raised concerns among the other members.
The committee was disappointed by the high level of nonparticipation this year.
Her nonparticipation in group activities made her feel isolated.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: participation - the act of taking part or being involved in something

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) + 'participation'.
