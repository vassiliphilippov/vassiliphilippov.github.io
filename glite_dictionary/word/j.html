J

1. NOUN

/ dʒeɪ / (AmE), / dʒeɪ / (BrE)
the symbol for the joule, a unit of energy in the International System of Units (SI) equal to the work done when a force of one newton moves an object one metre
symbol for joule (unit of energy)

Examples

The energy required is 500 J according to the calculation.
This battery provides 2000 J of energy to the circuit.
He measured the output in J for consistency with SI units.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joule - the SI unit of energy equal to one newton metre

Etymology

Adopted as the symbol from the name of physicist James Prescott Joule.
