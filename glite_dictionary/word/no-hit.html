no-hit

1. ADJ

/ ˈnoʊ hɪt / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ hɪt / (BrE)
(of a game or pitching) in which a pitcher allows the opposing team no hits.
baseball: game where no hits are allowed

Examples

The pitcher threw a no-hit game, making history for his team.
Thanks to a strong defense and perfect pitching, last night's match was no-hit.
Few athletes can boast of a no-hit performance in their careers.

Synset Examples

a no-hit game
The visiting pitcher no-hit the home team today
The home team no-hit the visiting team last week

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no-hitter - a baseball game in which a pitcher does not allow any hits
