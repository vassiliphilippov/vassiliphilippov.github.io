sportiveness

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔːrtɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtɪvnəs / (BrE)
lively and playful high-spirited behavior; playfulness or frolicsomeness
lively playfulness

Examples

Her sportiveness was evident at every party she attended, delighting everyone around her.
The children's sportiveness filled the playground with laughter and energy.
There was a charming sportiveness in his manner that made him popular among his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: playfulness - the quality of being full of fun and desire to play
  • Antonym: seriousness - the quality of being serious, not playful or lively

Etymology

From 'sportive' + '-ness'; 'sportive' from Old French *sportif*, from 'sport'.
