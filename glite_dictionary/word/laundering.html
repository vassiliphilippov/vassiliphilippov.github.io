laundering

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑndərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɔːndərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of washing clothes, bed linens, and similar fabrics
washing clothes and linens

Examples

The laundering of uniforms takes place every Friday at the hotel.
After a weekend trip, she faced a mountain of laundering to get through.
Automated laundering services can save time for busy families.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laundry - clothes and linens that need to be washed or have been washed

Etymology

From the verb 'launder', which derives from French 'lavander' (to wash) and Latin 'lavare' (to wash).
