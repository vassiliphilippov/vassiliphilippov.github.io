beigel

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈbaɪɡəl / (BrE)
A glazed, yeast-raised, doughnut-shaped bread roll with a hard crust, traditionally made in Jewish communities.
Jewish glazed bread roll with hard crust

Examples

I bought a fresh beigel from the old bakery on Brick Lane.
The beigel was chewy inside and perfectly crispy on the outside.
She prefers her beigel with smoked salmon and cream cheese.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bagel - A ring-shaped bread roll similar to beigel

Etymology

From Yiddish *beygl*, from Middle High German *böugel* (ring, bracelet).
