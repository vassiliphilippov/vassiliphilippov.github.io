inspiriting

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈspɪrɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈspɪrɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
cheerfully encouraging; making someone feel more cheerful or hopeful
cheerfully encouraging

Examples

The coach gave an inspiriting speech before the final game.
Listening to her inspiriting words, the team felt ready to face any challenge.
His inspiriting attitude helped everyone stay hopeful during difficult times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspiring - encouraging, making you feel enthusiastic or excited
  • Antonym: discouraging - causing someone to lose confidence or enthusiasm

Etymology

From the verb 'inspire' + -iting, modeled on the present participle; ultimately from Latin 'inspirare' (to breathe into).
