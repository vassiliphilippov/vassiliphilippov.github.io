strictly

1. ADV

/ ˈstrɪktli / (AmE), / ˈstrɪktli / (BrE)
only involving or limited to what is specified, with no other elements included; exclusively.
restricted to what is specified

Examples

During the meeting, we discussed strictly
Admission to the club is strictly by invitation only.
The policy applies strictly to employees; visitors are not affected.

Synset Examples

we talked strictly business

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exclusively - used to refer to something done or available only for a particular purpose or group
  • Synonym: solely - not involving anyone or anything else, only

Etymology

From strict + -ly; 'strict' from Latin 'strictus', past participle of 'stringere' (to draw tight).

2. ADV

/ ˈstrɪktli / (AmE), / ˈstrɪktli / (BrE)
In a stringent or exact manner; with close adherence to rules or standards.
in a stringent or exact manner

Examples

Visitors must follow the rules strictly at all times.
The diet is strictly supervised by professional nutritionists.
Access is strictly limited to authorized personnel only.

Synset Examples

the laws are stringently enforced
stringently controlled

Related Terms

  • Antonym: leniently - in a permissive or tolerant manner

Etymology

From strict + -ly; 'strict' from Latin 'strictus' (drawn tight).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00471219-r
Synset POS: r

3. ADV

/ ˈstrɪktli / (AmE), / ˈstrɪktli / (BrE)
In a rigorous, precise, or exact manner; adhering closely to rules or standards.
in a rigorous or exact manner

Examples

The exam is timed and must be completed strictly within one hour.
Access to the laboratory is strictly limited to authorized personnel.
She followed the recipe strictly, measuring each ingredient carefully.

Synset Examples

he had been trained rigorously by the monks

Related Terms

  • Antonym: leniently - in a permissive or tolerant manner
  • Synonym: precisely - in an exact or accurate manner

Etymology

From strict + -ly (adverbial suffix), strict from Latin strictus (drawn tight).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00227027-r
Synset POS: r
