Molise

1. PROPN

/ moʊˈliːzeɪ / (AmE), / məˈliːzeɪ / (BrE)
A region of south central Italy, known for its mountainous landscape and historical towns.
region of south central Italy

Examples

She spent her summer vacation exploring the medieval villages of Molise.
Local cuisine in Molise features fresh pasta and mountain cheeses.
Many people are unaware of Molise, one of Italy's least populated regions.

Etymology

From Italian 'Molise', derived from the name of a Norman feudal family that once ruled the area.
