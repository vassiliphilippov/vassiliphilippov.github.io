armrest

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrmˌrɛst / (AmE), / ˈɑːmˌrɛst / (BrE)
A part of a chair, seat, or sofa that supports the arm when sitting.
support for the arm on a seat or chair

Examples

She adjusted the armrest on her office chair for better comfort.
The movie theater seats have padded armrests for extra luxury.
One of the armrests on the old sofa was broken.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headrest - a support for the head on a seat or chair
