hayloft

1. NOUN

/ ˈheɪ.lɒft / (AmE), / ˈheɪ.lɒft / (BrE)
a loft in a barn or stable used for storing hay
a loft for storing hay in a barn

Examples

The old barn's hayloft was stacked high with freshly cut grass.
When I was a child, I loved climbing into the hayloft to play and hide.
They stored enough feed in the hayloft to last through the winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barn - a large farm building for storing grain, hay, or for housing livestock
  • Synonym: loft - an upper story or attic in a building, often open and used for storage

Etymology

Compound of 'hay' (Old English *hēg*) and 'loft' (Old Norse *lopt*, upper room).
