foamer

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊmɚ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊmə / (BrE)
An enthusiastic supporter or fan of craft beer, often passionate about its brewing and tasting.
craft beer enthusiast
informal

Examples

He's such a foamer for craft beer that he brews his own.
The bar was packed with foamers eager to try the latest seasonal ales.
As a dedicated foamer, she travels to beer festivals across the country.
