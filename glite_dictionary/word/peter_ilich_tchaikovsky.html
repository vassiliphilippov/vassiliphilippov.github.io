Peter Ilich Tchaikovsky

1. PROPN

/ ˈpiːtər ˈiːlɪtʃ tʃaɪˈkɒfski / (AmE), / ˈpiːtə ˈiːlɪtʃ tʃaɪˈkɒfski / (BrE)
(1840–1893) A Russian composer known for his expressive melodies and major contributions to classical music.
Russian composer (1840–1893)

Examples

Many music lovers consider Peter Ilich Tchaikovsky one of the greatest composers of the Romantic era.
The ballet ‘Swan Lake’ was composed by Peter Ilich Tchaikovsky and remains popular worldwide.
Students analyzed the symphonies of Peter Ilich Tchaikovsky in their music history class.
