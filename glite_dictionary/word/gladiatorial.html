gladiatorial

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡlædiˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / ˌɡlædiəˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to gladiators or resembling their combat, especially in terms of intense or brutal competition
relating to gladiators or their combat

Examples

The debates became increasingly gladiatorial as the election approached.
The film depicted the brutal gladiatorial contests in ancient Rome.
Their rivalry reached a gladiatorial level during the final match.

Synset Examples

gladiatorial combats

Related Terms

  • Synonym: martial - relating to war or the military, warlike

Etymology

From Latin 'gladiatorius', from 'gladiator' (swordsman, fighter).
