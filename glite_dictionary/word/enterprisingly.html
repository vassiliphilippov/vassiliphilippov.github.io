enterprisingly

1. ADV

/ ˌɛn.tərˈpraɪ.zɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˌɛn.təˈpraɪ.zɪŋ.li / (BrE)
in a way that shows a willingness to take risks or try new and difficult things
in an enterprising manner

Examples

She acted enterprisingly by volunteering for the most difficult project at work.
The children enterprisingly built their own treehouse from leftover wood.
He enterprisingly suggested a new approach when the usual methods failed.

Synset Examples

‘Let's go up that mountain,’ she said enterprisingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adventurously - in a way that is willing to take risks; daringly

Etymology

From 'enterprising' + '-ly' (adverbial suffix). 'Enterprising' from French 'entreprise' (undertaking).
