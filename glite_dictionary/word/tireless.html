tireless

1. ADJ

/ ˈtaɪɚləs / (AmE), / ˈtaɪələs / (BrE)
showing sustained energy or enthusiasm without becoming tired
not becoming tired; persistent

Examples

She worked tireless hours to make sure the project was completed on time.
The tireless volunteers cleaned the entire beach in one afternoon.
His tireless pursuit of knowledge inspired everyone around him.

Synset Examples

an indefatigable advocate of equal rights
a tireless worker
unflagging pursuit of excellence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indefatigable - persisting tirelessly; never becoming fatigued
  • Synonym: unflagging - never becoming tired or less enthusiastic

Etymology

From 'tire' (to become weary) + '-less' (without).

2. ADJ

/ ˈtaɪərləs / (AmE), / ˈtaɪələs / (BrE)
Showing or involving great energy and persistent effort without becoming tired.
showing persistent energy and effort

Examples

She was a tireless advocate for human rights throughout her life.
The volunteers worked tireless hours to help rebuild the town.
His tireless efforts resulted in a successful fundraising campaign.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indefatigable - never getting tired; persistent
  • Antonym: weary - feeling or showing tiredness

Etymology

From 'tire' + '-less', meaning without tiring.

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: s
