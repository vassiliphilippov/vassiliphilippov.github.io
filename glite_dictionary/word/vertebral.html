vertebral

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɝtɪbrəl / (AmE), / ˈvɜːtɪbrəl / (BrE)
relating to the vertebrae, the series of bones forming the spine
relating to vertebrae or the spine
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that her pain was caused by a vertebral fracture.
Certain animals have a highly specialized vertebral column for flexibility and strength.
The MRI scan revealed abnormalities in the vertebral discs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spinal - relating to the spine or spinal cord

Etymology

From Latin 'vertebralis', from 'vertebra' (joint of the spine).
