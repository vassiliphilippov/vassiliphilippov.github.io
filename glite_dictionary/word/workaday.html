workaday

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɝːkəˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkəˌdeɪ / (BrE)
ordinary and unremarkable; typical of everyday life
ordinary or everyday; unremarkable

Examples

Her workaday existence made her crave adventure on the weekends.
The novel explores the workaday challenges faced by factory workers in the early 20th century.
Despite his fame, he tried to maintain a workaday lifestyle.

Synset Examples

a placid everyday scene
it was a routine day
there's nothing quite like a real … train conductor to add color to a quotidian commute

Related Terms

  • Synonym: everyday - common; typical of ordinary days
  • Synonym: routine - regular and unvarying; ordinary
  • Antonym: extraordinary - very unusual; remarkable

Etymology

From the phrase 'workaday world,' originally meaning a day set aside for work as opposed to a holiday.
