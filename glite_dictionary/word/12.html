12

1. NUM

/ twɛlv / (AmE), / twɛlv / (BrE)
The cardinal number representing the sum of eleven and one; twelve.
the number twelve

Examples

There are 12 months in a year.
She bought 12 eggs from the market.
The clock struck 12 at midnight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twelve - the word for the number 12

Etymology

From Old English 'twelf', related to 'two' and 'left' (that is, 'two left' after ten).
