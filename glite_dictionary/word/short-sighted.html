short-sighted

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɔrtˌsaɪt̬ɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːtˌsaɪtɪd / (BrE)
Lacking foresight or consideration for the future; making decisions without regard for long-term consequences.
lacking foresight or planning

Examples

The board's short-sighted policy ignored the environmental impact of the project.
Critics argue that funding cuts are a short-sighted response to economic pressure.
Her short-sighted approach failed to account for future market changes.

Synset Examples

He criticized the government's populism as short-sighted.
His short-sighted decisions cost the company dearly.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: far-sighted - having or showing an awareness of and preparation for the future
