usefulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈjus.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈjuːs.fl̩.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being useful or able to be used for a practical purpose.
the quality of being useful

Examples

The usefulness of this tool becomes obvious when you need to make precise measurements.
Many people doubt the usefulness of some modern gadgets, but they can be helpful in daily life.
She questioned the usefulness of memorizing facts that can easily be found online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utility - the state of being useful, profitable, or beneficial
  • Antonym: uselessness - the quality of having no practical use

Etymology

From 'useful' + '-ness'.
