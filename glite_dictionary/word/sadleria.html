Sadleria

1. NOUN

/ sædˈlɪəriə / (AmE), / sædˈlɪəriə / (BrE)
A genus of low tree ferns with large fronds, native to Hawaiian rainforests and lava flows.
genus of Hawaiian tree ferns

Examples

Sadleria ferns are a distinctive feature of the Hawaiian rainforest understory.
The researchers discovered new growth among the Sadleria on the cooled lava flow.
Botanists classified the unusual fern as a species within the Sadleria genus.

Etymology

Named after Joseph Sadler (1819–1889), Hungarian botanist.
