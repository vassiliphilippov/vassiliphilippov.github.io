to meliorate

1. VERB

/ ˈmiːliəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈmiːlɪəˌreɪt / (BrE)
to make or become better; to improve something or improve in condition
to improve or become better

Examples

Medical care has helped meliorate the condition of many patients.
Efforts were made to meliorate the effects of pollution in the river.
Over time, the reforms began to meliorate living standards across the country.

Synset Examples

The weather improved toward evening

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to improve - to make or become better
  • Antonym: to worsen - to become worse; to deteriorate

Etymology

From Late Latin melioratus, past participle of meliorare (to improve), from melior (better).
