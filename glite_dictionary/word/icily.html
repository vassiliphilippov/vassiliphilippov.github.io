icily

1. ADV

/ ˈaɪ.sɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.sɪ.li / (BrE)
In a cold, unfriendly, or unemotional manner.
in a cold or unfriendly way

Examples

She replied icily, making it clear she was not pleased with the suggestion.
He stared at the intruder icily, showing no sign of fear or surprise.
Her voice was icily calm, betraying none of her true feelings.

Synset Examples

‘Mr. Powell finds it easier to take it out of mothers, children and sick people than to take on this vast industry,’ Mr Brown commented icily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coldly - in an unemotional or unfriendly manner
  • Antonym: warmly - in a friendly or affectionate manner

Etymology

From 'icy' + '-ly', forming the adverb from the adjective 'icy'.
