one-celled

1. ADJ

/ wʌn sɛld / (AmE), / wʌn sɛld / (BrE)
Consisting of a single cell; not divided into multiple cells.
consisting of a single cell

Examples

Many bacteria are one-celled organisms found in water and soil.
The amoeba is a classic example of a one-celled animal.
Scientists study one-celled algae to understand basic life processes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unicellular - consisting of a single cell
