to glamourize

1. VERB

/ ˈɡlæmərˌaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɡlæməraɪz / (BrE)
to make something seem glamorous or more attractive than it really is
make glamorous or attractive

Examples

The film has been criticized for glamourizing violence.
Certain magazines tend to glamourize unrealistic body images.
They tried to glamourize the life of a criminal, ignoring the consequences.

Synset Examples

This new wallpaper really glamorizes the living room!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to romanticize - to make something appear more romantic or appealing than it is

Etymology

From 'glamour' + '-ize', modelled on similar English formations.
