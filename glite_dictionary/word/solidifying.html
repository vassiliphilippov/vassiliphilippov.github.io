solidifying

1. NOUN

/ səˈlɪdəˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / səˈlɪdɪfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of becoming hard or solid by cooling, drying, or crystallization
process of becoming solid

Examples

The solidifying of the molten metal took several minutes.
As temperatures dropped, the solidifying process in the pipes began quickly.
Proper solidifying of the mixture is essential for achieving the correct texture.

Synset Examples

the hardening of concrete
he tested the set of the glue

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hardening - the process of becoming hard or harder
  • Antonym: melting - the process of becoming liquid due to heat

Etymology

From 'solidify' + '-ing'; 'solidify' from French *solidifier* or Latin *solidus* (solid).
