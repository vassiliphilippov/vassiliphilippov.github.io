San Antonio

1. PROPN

/ ˌsæn ænˈtoʊnioʊ / (AmE), / ˌsan ænˈtəʊniəʊ / (BrE)
a large city in south-central Texas, known for its historical sites such as the Alamo, military bases, and as a popular tourist destination
city in south-central Texas, USA
american

Examples

We visited San Antonio last summer and toured the Alamo.
San Antonio has a vibrant cultural scene with festivals throughout the year.
She moved to San Antonio to be closer to her family.

Etymology

From Spanish 'San Antonio', meaning 'Saint Anthony', named by Spanish explorers in the late 17th century.
