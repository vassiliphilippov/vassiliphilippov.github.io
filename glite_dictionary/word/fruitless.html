fruitless

1. ADJ

/ ˈfruːtləs / (AmE), / ˈfruːtlɪs / (BrE)
failing to achieve the desired results; unproductive or unsuccessful.
unsuccessful; unproductive

Examples

After a fruitless search for his keys, he finally gave up and called a locksmith.
Their efforts to improve the system proved fruitless despite months of discussion.
She made one last fruitless attempt to persuade him to stay.

Synset Examples

a fruitless search
futile years after her artistic peak
a sleeveless errand
a vain attempt

Related Terms

  • Synonym: futile - incapable of producing any useful result; pointless
  • Synonym: vain - producing no result; useless
  • Antonym: successful - achieving the desired aim or result

Etymology

From Middle English 'fruteles', from 'fruit' + '-less'.
