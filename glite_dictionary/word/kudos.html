kudos

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːdoʊs / (AmE), / ˈkjuːdɒs / (BrE)
praise and honor received for an achievement.
praise and honor

Examples

She received kudos from her colleagues for organizing the successful event.
The film won critical kudos at several international festivals.
He enjoys the kudos that come with being the team captain.

Synset Examples

he always appreciated praise for his work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: praise - expression of warm approval or admiration
  • Antonym: blame - the act of saying or thinking that someone is responsible for something bad

Etymology

From Greek 'kydos' (glory).
