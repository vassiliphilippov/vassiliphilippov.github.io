Karachi

1. PROPN

/ kəˈrɑtʃi / (AmE), / kəˈrɑːtʃi / (BrE)
the largest city in Pakistan, a major industrial center and seaport on the Arabian Sea; formerly the capital of Pakistan
largest city in Pakistan

Examples

After moving to Karachi, she started working in the textile industry.
The bustling streets of Karachi are filled with diverse cultures and traditions.
Their cargo ship docked at the busy port of Karachi last week.

Etymology

From Urdu کراچی (Karācī).
