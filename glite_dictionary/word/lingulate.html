lingulate

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪŋɡjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋɡjʊlət / (BrE)
shaped like a tongue; tongue-shaped.
tongue-shaped

Examples

The seed has a lingulate appendage that aids in its dispersal.
Some leaves are described as lingulate due to their elongated, rounded form.
The shell's lingulate shape is a distinctive feature of this brachiopod species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tongue-shaped - having the shape of a tongue

Etymology

From Latin 'lingulatus', from 'lingula' (diminutive of 'lingua' meaning 'tongue').
