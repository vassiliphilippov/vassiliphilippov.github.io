unpressed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈprɛst / (AmE), / ʌnˈprɛst / (BrE)
(of clothing) not smoothed or flattened by ironing or heat.
not ironed; not smoothed by heat

Examples

She wore an unpressed shirt to the meeting, looking slightly rumpled.
The pile of unpressed laundry in the corner kept growing each day.
His suit looked unpressed after the long flight.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pressed - smoothed or flattened by ironing or heat

Etymology

Prefix 'un-' (not) + 'pressed' (from press, meaning to smooth with a heated iron).
