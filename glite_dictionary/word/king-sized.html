king-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɪŋ.saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋ.saɪzd / (BrE)
Of a size larger than the standard; especially used to describe a bed or item that is extra large.
extra large in size

Examples

We bought a king-sized bed for the guest room to ensure maximum comfort.
He ordered a king-sized burger that was almost too big to eat.
They replaced the couch with a king-sized one to fit their entire family.

Synset Examples

a king-size bed

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: queen-sized - of a size larger than standard but smaller than king-sized
  • Degree Lower: standard-sized - of average or regular size

Etymology

Formed from 'king' (suggesting royalty, largeness) and 'sized' (having a particular size), by analogy with 'king-size'.
