creepy-crawlies

1. NOUN

/ ˌkripiˈkrɑliz / (AmE), / ˌkriːpiˈkrɔːliz / (BrE)
A feeling of discomfort or anxiety, often caused by something unpleasant, frightening, or unnerving.
feeling of discomfort or anxiety
informal

Examples

The old abandoned house gave me the creepy-crawlies whenever I walked by.
Just thinking about spiders gives my sister the creepy-crawlies.
Watching that horror movie late at night gave me the creepy-crawlies for hours.
