jamb

1. NOUN

/ dʒæm / (AmE), / dʒam / (BrE)
the vertical side member of a door or window frame
vertical side of a door or window frame

Examples

The carpenter carefully measured the jamb before installing the new door.
Paint had chipped off the window jamb, exposing the bare wood underneath.
She leaned against the jamb, watching the rain through the open doorway.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lintel - horizontal support across the top of a door or window

Etymology

From Old French *jambe* (leg), referring to the vertical support.
