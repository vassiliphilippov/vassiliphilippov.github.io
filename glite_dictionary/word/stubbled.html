stubbled

1. ADJ

/ ˈstʌbəld / (AmE), / ˈstʌbəld / (BrE)
having a short, rough growth of beard or hair, especially on the face
having short, rough facial hair

Examples

He ran his hand across his stubbled jaw, feeling the roughness of a day's growth.
The actor appeared on the red carpet with a newly stubbled chin, giving him a rugged look.
She liked the way his stubbled face felt against her cheek.

Synset Examples

his stubbled chin

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clean-shaven - having no facial hair after shaving
  • Degree Higher: bearded - having a full growth of facial hair
  • Degree Lower: smooth - having no stubble or roughness on the face
