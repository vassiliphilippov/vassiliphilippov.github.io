flapcake

1. NOUN

/ ˈflæpˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈflæpkeɪk / (BrE)
A flat cake made from thin batter and fried on both sides on a griddle.
flat cake fried on a griddle

Examples

She served homemade flapcakes with maple syrup for breakfast.
The smell of flapcakes sizzling on the griddle filled the kitchen.
He flipped the flapcake to cook the other side until it was golden brown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pancake - a thin, flat cake of batter, fried on both sides
