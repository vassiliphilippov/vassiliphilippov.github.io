housecoat

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊs.koʊt / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs.kəʊt / (BrE)
A loose, comfortable robe worn by women at home, especially as a dressing gown.
loose robe for women worn at home

Examples

She put on her housecoat before heading to the kitchen for breakfast.
The floral housecoat hung behind her bedroom door, ready for the chilly morning.
After her bath, she slipped into a soft pink housecoat and relaxed on the sofa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressing gown - a loose robe, typically worn after getting up or before dressing
  • Synonym: bathrobe - a robe worn before or after bathing

Etymology

Formed from 'house' and 'coat', indicating a coat worn at home.
