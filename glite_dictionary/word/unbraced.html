unbraced

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbreɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈbreɪst / (BrE)
not supported with braces or props; lacking reinforcement or structural support.
not supported with braces

Examples

The temporary shelter collapsed because it was unbraced during the storm.
He stood unbraced as the impact hit, causing him to stumble backwards.
The engineer warned that the unbraced wall was at risk of falling during an earthquake.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: braced - reinforced or supported with braces
