Ononis spinosa

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈnoʊ.nɪs spaɪˈnoʊ.sə / (AmE), / əʊˈnəʊ.nɪs spaɪˈnəʊ.sə / (BrE)
A Eurasian plant with spiny stems, tough roots, and loose racemes of pink or purple flowers; also known as spiny restharrow.
Eurasian plant with spiny stems and pink flowers

Examples

Ononis spinosa is commonly found growing along roadsides in much of Europe.
The herbalist recommended a tea made from the roots of Ononis spinosa.
Gardeners appreciate Ononis spinosa for its resilience and attractive pink flowers.

Etymology

From Latin 'Ononis' (a plant genus) and 'spinosa' (spiny).
