senorita

1. NOUN

/ ˌseɪnjəˈritə / (AmE), / ˌseɪnjəˈriːtə / (BrE)
a Spanish title or form of address for an unmarried woman, equivalent to 'Miss' in English.
Spanish title for an unmarried woman

Examples

The young woman greeted everyone as senorita at the front desk.
He politely addressed her as senorita during their conversation.
Many tourists like to use the word senorita when visiting Spain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miss - title used for an unmarried woman in English

Etymology

From Spanish señorita, diminutive of señora (lady).
