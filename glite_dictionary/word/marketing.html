marketing

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːrkɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the activities involved in promoting, selling, and distributing products or services
promotion and sale of products/services

Examples

The marketing team developed a new campaign to attract younger customers.
Many companies invest heavily in marketing to increase brand awareness.
She is studying marketing at university and hopes to work for a major firm.

Synset Examples

most companies have a manager in charge of marketing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertising - the activity of making products or services known to the public, often through paid announcements
  • Synonym: sales - the exchange of goods or services for money; the action of selling something

Etymology

From market + -ing, first recorded in this sense in the late 19th century.
