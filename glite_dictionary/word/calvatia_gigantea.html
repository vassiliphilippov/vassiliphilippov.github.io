Calvatia gigantea

1. NOUN

/ kælˈveɪʃə dʒaɪˈgænˌtiə / (AmE), / kalˈveɪʃɪə dʒɪˈgænˌtɪə / (BrE)
a large, edible species of puffball fungus, sometimes reaching up to 2 feet in diameter and 25 pounds in weight
large edible puffball fungus

Examples

The Calvatia gigantea found in the field weighed nearly fifteen pounds.
Many mushroom enthusiasts seek out Calvatia gigantea for its mild flavor and impressive size.
A single Calvatia gigantea can provide enough mushroom for several meals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: giant puffball - common English name for Calvatia gigantea, a large edible puffball fungus

Etymology

From Latin: 'Calvatia' (from 'calvus', meaning bald) and 'gigantea' (meaning gigantic).
