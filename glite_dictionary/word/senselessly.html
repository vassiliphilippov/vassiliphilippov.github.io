senselessly

1. ADV

/ ˈsɛnsləsli / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsləsli / (BrE)
in a meaningless, purposeless, or unreasonable manner; without sense or justification
without meaning or justification

Examples

The protestors were senselessly attacked by the authorities, despite their peaceful demonstration.
He spent his money senselessly on things he never used or needed.
Several trees were senselessly cut down in the park, shocking the community.

Synset Examples

these innocent bystanders were senselessly killed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pointlessly - in a way that lacks a purpose or point
  • Antonym: meaningfully - in a purposeful or significant manner

Etymology

From 'senseless' (lacking sense) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).

2. ADV

/ ˈsɛnslɪsli / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsləsli / (BrE)
In a way that lacks sense, reason, or purpose; without justification or meaning.
in a way that lacks sense or reason

Examples

The animal was killed senselessly, with no reason or mercy.
The vandals senselessly destroyed the public art installation.
He was senselessly insulted by strangers online.

Synset Examples

these temples were mindlessly destroyed by the Red Guards

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mindlessly - in a manner lacking thought or intelligence
  • Antonym: rationally - in a reasonable or logical manner

Etymology

From 'senseless' + '-ly', where 'senseless' is from Old French 'senseles'.
