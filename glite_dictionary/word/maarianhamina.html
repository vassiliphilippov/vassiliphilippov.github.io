Maarianhamina

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɑːriˌɑːnˈhɑːmiˌnɑː / (AmE), / ˌmɑːriˌænˈhæmɪnə / (BrE)
the capital and chief port of the Åland Islands, an autonomous region of Finland in the Baltic Sea
capital and port of the Åland Islands

Examples

The ferry from Stockholm docks in Maarianhamina every afternoon.
We visited Maarianhamina during our tour of the Baltic Sea islands.
Maarianhamina is renowned for its picturesque waterfront and maritime history.

Etymology

Finnish name for the city, from Maria (a given name) + hamn (Swedish for 'harbor'), reflecting both Finnish and Swedish influences.
