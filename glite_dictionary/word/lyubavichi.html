Lyubavichi

1. PROPN

/ ljuˈbɑːvɪtʃi / (AmE), / ljuːˈbævɪtʃi / (BrE)
a town in Belarus that served as the center of the Chabad Hasidic movement during part of the 19th century
town in Belarus, Chabad movement center

Examples

The village of Lyubavichi played a pivotal role in the history of the Chabad movement.
Every year, many people visit Lyubavichi to explore its religious and historical heritage.
My grandfather's family once lived in Lyubavichi before immigrating to another country.

Etymology

From Russian Люба́вичи (Lyubavichi), the name of the town.
