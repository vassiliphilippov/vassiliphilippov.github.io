Lietuva

1. PROPN

/ ˈljeɪtuːvɑː / (AmE), / ˈljeːtuːvɑː / (BrE)
The Lithuanian name for Lithuania, a republic in northeastern Europe on the Baltic Sea, with capital Vilnius.
Lithuanian name for Lithuania

Examples

The country's official name is Lietuva in the Lithuanian language.
Her grandparents emigrated from Lietuva to the United States in the early 20th century.
In their history class, students learned that Lietuva was once part of the Grand Duchy of Lithuania.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lithuania - A country in northeastern Europe on the Baltic Sea, with capital Vilnius

Etymology

Lithuanian endonym for Lithuania.
