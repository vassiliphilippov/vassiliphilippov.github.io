Adjara

1. PROPN

/ əˈdʒɑːrə / (AmE), / əˈdʒɑːrə / (BrE)
an autonomous region in southwestern Georgia, situated on the Black Sea coast
autonomous region in Georgia

Examples

The lush mountains of Adjara attract hikers and nature lovers from around the world.
Political autonomy gives Adjara a distinct identity within Georgia.
Batumi, the capital of Adjara, is known for its seaside resorts and vibrant nightlife.

Etymology

From Georgian აჭარა (Ach’ara).
