starkers

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɑrkərz / (AmE), / ˈstɑːkəz / (BrE)
(British informal) completely naked; not wearing any clothes.
completely naked (British informal)
informal british

Examples

He ran out into the garden starkers to catch the cat.
I can't believe you answered the door starkers!
She found him standing starkers in the hallway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naked - not wearing any clothes
  • Antonym: clothed - wearing clothes

Etymology

Formed from 'stark naked' with addition of -ers, British slang formation.
