unfalteringly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈfɔltɚɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɔːltərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that does not weaken or waver; with steady determination or firmness
with unwavering determination

Examples

She pursued her goals unfalteringly, never letting setbacks stop her progress.
The rescue team worked unfalteringly through the night to find survivors.
He spoke unfalteringly about his convictions, despite facing opposition.

Synset Examples

he clung to the past determinedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steadfastly - in a resolutely firm and unwavering manner
  • Synonym: determinedly - with firm determination
  • Antonym: hesitantly - in an uncertain or wavering manner

Etymology

From 'unfaltering' + '-ly'; 'falter' from Old French 'faulter', from Latin 'fallere' (to deceive, fail).
