auditory

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔdəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪtəri / (BrE)
relating to hearing or the sense of hearing.
relating to hearing

Examples

The auditory nerves carry sound signals from the ear to the brain.
She has an auditory processing disorder that makes listening in noisy places difficult.
Researchers studied the auditory responses of children to different music genres.

Synset Examples

auditory processing
an audile person

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aural - relating to the ear or the sense of hearing

Etymology

From Latin audītorium (pertaining to hearing), from audīre (to hear).
