pitwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪtˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈpɪtˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who works in a coal mine
woman coal miner

Examples

The museum display honored the pitwoman who risked her life underground every day.
She became the first female pitwoman in her mining community.
Many stories were told about the resilience of a pitwoman during the industrial revolution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitman - a man who works in a coal mine

Etymology

From 'pit', meaning a mine, and 'woman'. Modeled after 'pitman'.
