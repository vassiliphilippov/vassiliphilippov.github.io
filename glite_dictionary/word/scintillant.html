scintillant

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪn.tɪ.lənt / (AmE), / ˈsɪn.tɪ.lənt / (BrE)
shining or flashing briefly with small bright sparks or flashes of light; sparkling intermittently.
flashing or sparkling with brief light

Examples

The surface of the lake was scintillant under the midday sun, reflecting thousands of tiny flashes.
Her dress was embroidered with scintillant beads that caught the stage lights beautifully.
At night, the city skyline appeared scintillant against the dark sky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glittering - shining with a strong, reflected light; sparkling
  • Synonym: sparkling - shining with bright flashes of light

Etymology

From Latin scintillans, present participle of scintillare (to spark, glitter).
