christella

1. NOUN

/ krɪˈstɛl.ə / (AmE), / krɪˈstɛl.ə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical ferns characterized by thin, brittle fronds, belonging to the family Thelypteridaceae
a genus of tropical ferns

Examples

The humid greenhouse was filled with the delicate fronds of christella thriving in the artificial warmth.
Botanists discovered a new species of christella in the rainforests of Southeast Asia.
Gardeners often cultivate christella for its graceful and airy foliage.

Etymology

Named after botanist Konrad Christ (1833–1933) and the Latin diminutive suffix -ella.
