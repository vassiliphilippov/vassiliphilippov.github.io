footgear

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌɡɪə / (BrE)
coverings worn on the feet, such as shoes, boots, or sandals
coverings worn on the feet

Examples

You should wear sturdy footgear when hiking in rough terrain.
Specialized footgear is required for some sports, like football or skiing.
In the entryway, there was a pile of wet footgear after the storm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: footwear - clothing worn on the feet, such as shoes and boots

Etymology

Formed from 'foot' + 'gear', meaning equipment for the feet.
