pizzazz

1. NOUN

/ pɪˈzæz / (AmE), / pɪˈzaz / (BrE)
an attractive combination of vitality, style, and energy, especially in a person’s manner or performance
vitality and stylish energy

Examples

Her stage performance had so much pizzazz that the audience couldn't take their eyes off her.
This room needs a little pizzazz—let's add some colorful artwork and lively decorations.
He delivered his presentation with extra pizzazz, making the topic engaging for everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vitality - lively and animated character
  • Synonym: verve - enthusiasm or vigor in expression
  • Synonym: sparkle - brightness or liveliness in personality

Etymology

Possibly an alteration of 'pizazz' (meaning glamour or vitality), 20th-century US origin.
