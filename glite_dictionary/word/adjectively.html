adjectively

1. ADV

/ ədˈʒɛktɪvli / (AmE), / ədˈdʒɛktɪvli / (BrE)
in the manner of or as an adjective; with an adjectival function
as or like an adjective

Examples

In the phrase 'chicken soup', 'chicken' is used adjectively.
Many nouns can function adjectively to modify other nouns.
The author explained how the color terms could be used adjectively in different contexts.

Synset Examples

nouns are frequently used adjectively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adjective - a word that describes a noun

Etymology

Formed from 'adjective' + '-ly', making it the adverbial form.
