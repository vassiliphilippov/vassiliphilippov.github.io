malposition

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæl.pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˌmal.pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
an incorrect or abnormal position, especially of a part of the body or a mechanical object
incorrect or abnormal position

Examples

The X-ray revealed a malposition of the hip joint after surgery.
Device failure sometimes occurs due to malposition during installation.
Doctors corrected the malposition of the unborn baby before delivery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: misalignment - incorrect alignment or arrangement

Etymology

From French 'malposition', from Latin 'malus' (bad) + 'position' (position).
