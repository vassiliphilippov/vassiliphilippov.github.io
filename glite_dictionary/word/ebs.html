EbS

1. NOUN

/ iː biː ɛs / (AmE), / iː biː ɛs / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point (11°15') south of due east.
compass point south of due east

Examples

The ship was heading EbS for most of the night.
He marked their course as EbS on the navigation chart.
In old sailing terminology, EbS was a common direction for plotting routes.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: east - the cardinal compass direction at 90 degrees
  • Degree Higher: east-southeast - the compass point between east and southeast

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'East by South'.
