infectiously

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈfɛkʃəsli / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɛkʃəsli / (BrE)
In a way that spreads quickly from one person to another, especially of feelings, moods, or behaviors.
in a contagious manner

Examples

Her infectiously cheerful attitude lifted everyone's spirits in the office.
The comedian laughed infectiously, making the entire audience burst into giggles.
His enthusiasm for music spread infectiously among his friends.

Synset Examples

she was contagiously bubbly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contagiously - in a contagious manner
