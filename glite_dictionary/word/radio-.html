radio-

1. ADJ

/ ˈreɪdioʊ / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊ / (BrE)
(in compounds) Relating to radiation or radioactivity.
prefix meaning relating to radiation

Examples

The field of radiochemistry focuses on chemical aspects of radioactive materials.
The scientist studied radioisotopes to understand their decay processes.
Protective suits are required in areas of radioactivity.

Synset Examples

radiochemistry

Etymology

From Latin 'radius' (ray, beam).
