Burundian

1. NOUN

/ bəˈrʊndiən / (AmE), / bəˈrʊndiən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Burundi
native or citizen of Burundi

Examples

The Burundian greeted us warmly as we arrived in Bujumbura.
Many Burundians have a deep pride in their country's history.
She met a Burundian while studying abroad in Belgium.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Rwandan - a native or inhabitant of Rwanda

Etymology

From Burundi, the country in East Africa, plus English suffix -an.

/ bəˈrʊndiən / (AmE), / bəˈrʊndiən / (BrE)
relating to Burundi or its people or culture

Examples

The Burundian government announced new educational reforms.
