Chattanooga

1. PROPN

/ ˌtʃætəˈnuːɡə / (AmE), / ˌtʃætəˈnuːɡə / (BrE)
A city in southeastern Tennessee, United States, located on the Tennessee River.
city in Tennessee, USA

Examples

The band played a concert in Chattanooga last weekend.
She moved to Chattanooga for a new job opportunity.
The scenery around Chattanooga is famous for its mountains and river.

Etymology

From the Cherokee word for 'rock coming to a point', referring to Lookout Mountain near the city.
