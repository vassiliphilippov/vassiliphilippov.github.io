brandysnap

1. NOUN

/ ˈbræn.diˌsnæp / (AmE), / ˈbræn.di.snæp / (BrE)
A crisp, sweet biscuit or wafer, usually flavored with ginger and sometimes brandy, often rolled into a tube shape and eaten as a dessert or snack.
a crisp, sweet ginger-flavored biscuit or wafer

Examples

She served brandysnaps filled with whipped cream for dessert.
The bakery's brandysnaps are famous for their delicate crunch and rich flavor.
Would you like a brandysnap with your cup of tea?
