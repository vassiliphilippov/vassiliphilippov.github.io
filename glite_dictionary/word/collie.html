collie

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːli / (AmE), / ˈkɒli / (BrE)
a breed of dog with a long, silky coat, a long, narrow head, and a prominent ruff, developed in Scotland and often used as a sheepdog
a Scottish breed of sheepdog

Examples

The family's beloved collie watched over the children in the yard.
She adopted a rescue collie with a beautiful, shiny coat.
Farmers often rely on their collies to help herd the sheep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sheepdog - a dog trained to herd sheep

Etymology

From Scottish English, possibly from Gaelic cù (dog) and diminutive suffix -ie.
