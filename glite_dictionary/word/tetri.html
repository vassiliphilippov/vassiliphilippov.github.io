tetri

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛtri / (AmE), / ˈtɛtri / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Georgia, equal to one hundredth of a lari.
Georgian monetary unit

Examples

The cashier gave me ten tetri as change.
A one tetri coin is very small in value.
Prices are listed in lari and tetri throughout Georgia.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: lari - the basic monetary unit of Georgia

Etymology

From Georgian თეთრი (tetri), meaning 'white' or 'silver coin'.
