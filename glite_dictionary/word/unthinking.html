unthinking

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or their actions) showing a lack of consideration or thought for others; careless or thoughtless.
showing lack of thought or consideration

Examples

His unthinking remark offended several people at the meeting.
She regretted her unthinking actions once she saw their consequences.
It was an unthinking comment that hurt her friend deeply.

Synset Examples

the thoughtless saying of a great princess on being informed that the people had no bread: ‘Let them eat cake’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtless - showing lack of consideration for others

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + thinking.

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or their behavior) characterized by mental sluggishness or a lack of active thought; inattentive or slow to use reason.
mentally sluggish; not thoughtful

Examples

His unthinking response revealed how little attention he was paying to the details.
The unthinking crowd followed the leader without question or hesitation.
She blamed her unthinking actions on exhaustion and a lack of sleep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtless - lacking consideration; without thinking
  • Antonym: reflective - characterized by deep thought or careful consideration

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'thinking'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00443289-s
Synset POS: s

3. ADJ

/ ʌnˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or action) not exhibiting or characterized by careful thought; done without considering the consequences.
not showing careful thought

Examples

His unthinking comments hurt her feelings greatly.
It was an unthinking decision that led to unnecessary problems.
She apologized for her unthinking behavior at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtless - lacking consideration for others or not thinking carefully
  • Synonym: careless - not giving sufficient attention or thought

Etymology

From the prefix un- (not) + thinking (present participle of think).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02429777-s
Synset POS: s
