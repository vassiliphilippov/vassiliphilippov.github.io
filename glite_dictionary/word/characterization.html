characterization

1. NOUN

/ ˌkærəktəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkærəktəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of portraying a character in a play, film, or other performance through speech, actions, and gestures
portrayal of a character in drama or film

Examples

Her characterization of Lady Macbeth was widely praised by the critics.
The actor's subtle gestures added depth to his characterization.
Excellent characterization can transform even a simple story into a compelling drama.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: portrayal - the act of depicting or representing a character

Etymology

From characterize + -ation; characterize from Greek kharaktēr (engraver's tool, person marked by a distinctive trait).

2. NOUN

/ ˌker.ɪktər.əˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌkær.ɪktər.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of describing or presenting the distinctive qualities or features of someone or something
describing distinctive characteristics

Examples

The novel's characterization of its main protagonist is both subtle and compelling.
Accurate characterization of the symptoms is essential for a correct diagnosis.
The documentary was criticized for its unfair characterization of certain groups.

Synset Examples

the media's characterization of Al Gore as a nerd

Related Terms

  • Synonym: description - a statement that gives details about what someone or something is like

Etymology

From character + -ization; ultimately from Greek 'kharaktēr' (engraved mark, character).

3. NOUN

/ ˌkɛrɪktərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkærɪktəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
a graphic or vivid verbal description, especially in literature or speech
graphic verbal description

Examples

The author's characterization of the city brought its streets and people vividly to life.
Her detailed characterization of the old mansion was so precise that readers could picture every room.
The pamphlet included brief characterizations of each historical figure mentioned.

Synset Examples

too often the narrative was interrupted by long word pictures
the author gives a depressing picture of life in Poland
the pamphlet contained brief characterizations of famous Vermonters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: description - a statement that represents something in words
  • Synonym: depiction - representation in image or words

Etymology

From character + -ization, modeled after French caractérisation.
