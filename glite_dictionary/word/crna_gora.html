Crna Gora

1. PROPN

/ tsrna ˈɡɔrə / (AmE), / tsrna ˈɡɔːrə / (BrE)
the native name for Montenegro, a country in southeastern Europe on the Adriatic Sea
native name for Montenegro
offensive

Examples

Many locals refer to their country as Crna Gora rather than Montenegro.
The mountains of Crna Gora attract hikers from all over the world.
Diplomatic documents sometimes mention both Montenegro and its native name, Crna Gora.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Montenegro - a country in southeastern Europe

Etymology

From South Slavic (Montenegrin/Serbian) 'Crna Gora', meaning 'Black Mountain'.
