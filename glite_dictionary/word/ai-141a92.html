ai

1. NOUN

/ aɪ / (AmE), / aɪ / (BrE)
a species of three-toed sloth found in South America, known for its slow movements and long claws
a species of three-toed sloth

Examples

The ai spends most of its life hanging from trees in the rainforest.
Unlike other sloths, the ai is completely diurnal and rarely active at night.
Researchers observed an ai crossing the river by swimming slowly but steadily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sloth - a slow-moving arboreal mammal of Central and South America

Etymology

From Portuguese 'ai', imitative of its call.
