Rogaine

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊˌɡeɪn / (AmE), / ˈrəʊˌɡeɪn / (BrE)
A proprietary name for a medicated lotion containing minoxidil, used to promote hair growth in cases of male-pattern baldness and sometimes for other types of hair loss.
hair-growth medication (proprietary name)
medical

Examples

Many men experiencing thinning hair use Rogaine to slow hair loss and encourage regrowth.
My dermatologist recommended Rogaine as part of my treatment for male-pattern baldness.
The effectiveness of Rogaine can vary from person to person.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minoxidil - the generic name for the medication in Rogaine
