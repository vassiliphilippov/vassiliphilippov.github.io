Michelangelo

1. PROPN

/ ˌmaɪkəlˈændʒəˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˌmɪkəlˈændʒɪˌləʊ / (BrE)
(1475–1564) Italian sculptor, painter, and architect, renowned as one of the leading figures of the Renaissance.
Renaissance Italian artist (1475–1564)

Examples

The Sistine Chapel ceiling was painted by Michelangelo between 1508 and 1512.
Michelangelo's statue of David is celebrated for its exquisite detail and beauty.
Art students visited Florence to study works by Michelangelo and his contemporaries.

Etymology

Italian, literally 'Michael the Angel.'
