Epipremnum aureum

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛpɪˈprɛmnəm ɔˈriəm / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈprɛmnəm ɔːˈriːəm / (BrE)
an evergreen climbing plant with variegated leaves, widely cultivated as a houseplant; also known as golden pothos or devil's ivy.
a popular climbing houseplant with variegated leaves

Examples

Many offices choose Epipremnum aureum to decorate their spaces because it thrives in low light.
My grandmother’s living room was filled with hanging Epipremnum aureum plants cascading from baskets.
If you want an easy-care plant that purifies air, Epipremnum aureum is a great choice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pothos - another common name for Epipremnum aureum, a climbing houseplant
  • Synonym: devil's ivy - common name for Epipremnum aureum, a popular houseplant

Etymology

From Neo-Latin genus name Epipremnum and species epithet aureum (Latin for 'golden').
