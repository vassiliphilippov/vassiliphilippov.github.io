Socratic

1. ADJ

/ soʊˈkrætɪk / (AmE), / səˈkrætɪk / (BrE)
relating to Socrates or his method of asking questions to stimulate critical thinking and illuminate ideas
relating to Socrates or his teaching method

Examples

The professor used a Socratic approach to encourage students to think for themselves.
Her Socratic questioning helped the class reach deeper understanding.
Many law schools emphasize Socratic discussion in their teaching methods.

Synset Examples

Socratic teaching

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maieutic - relating to the Socratic method of eliciting knowledge through questioning

Etymology

From Latin Socraticus, from Greek Sōkratikos, meaning 'of Socrates'.
