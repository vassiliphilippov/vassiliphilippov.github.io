limitlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪmɪtlɪsnəs / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪtlɪsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having no limits or bounds; infinity
quality of having no limits

Examples

She was captivated by the limitlessness of the night sky, stretching far beyond what the eye could see.
The limitlessness of the internet can be overwhelming for some people.
His imagination knew no limitlessness, leading to innovative solutions and ideas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infinity - the state of being infinite or endless
  • Antonym: finiteness - the quality of being finite or having limits

Etymology

From 'limitless', formed by adding the suffix '-ness' to indicate a state or quality.
