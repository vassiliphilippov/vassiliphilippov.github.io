erythematous

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛrəˈθɛmɪtəs / (AmE), / ˌɛrɪˈθɛmətəs / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by erythema, or abnormal redness of the skin or mucous membranes.
showing abnormal redness of the skin
medical

Examples

The patient presented with an erythematous rash on her arms and legs.
Doctors noted an erythematous area around the surgical wound.
The infection caused the skin to become swollen and erythematous.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: erythema - redness of the skin or mucous membranes

Etymology

From Greek 'erythema' (redness) + English '-ous'.
