A5

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪ ˈfaɪv / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˈfaɪv / (BrE)
a standard paper size measuring 148 by 210 millimeters, commonly used for notepads, booklets, and stationery
paper size: 148 x 210 mm

Examples

Please print the document on A5 paper to fit into the folder.
The invitation cards were designed on A5 sheets for a more elegant look.
She always carries an A5 notebook in her bag for jotting down ideas.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: A4 - standard paper size, 210 x 297 mm
  • Degree Lower: A6 - standard paper size, 105 x 148 mm

Etymology

From ISO standard 216, with 'A' denoting the series and '5' its position in the sequence.
