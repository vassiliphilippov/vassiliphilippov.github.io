stinky

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋki / (BrE)
(informal) Very bad, unpleasant, or of poor quality.
very bad; unpleasant
informal

Examples

That was a stinky movie—nothing about it was any good.
The team had a stinky performance in last night's game.
Honestly, that was a pretty stinky attempt at cooking.

Synset Examples

a lousy play
it's a stinking world

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lousy - very poor or bad; inferior in quality

2. ADJ

/ ˈstɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋki / (BrE)
having a strong, unpleasant, or offensive smell
having an unpleasant smell

Examples

After playing outside all day, his socks were stinky and needed to be washed.
The dog rolled in something stinky at the park, so it needed a bath.
You can always tell when the garbage is stinky and has to be taken out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smelly - having a strong or unpleasant odor
  • Antonym: fragrant - having a pleasant smell

Etymology

From English 'stink' (to have a strong unpleasant smell) + '-y' (forming adjectives).
