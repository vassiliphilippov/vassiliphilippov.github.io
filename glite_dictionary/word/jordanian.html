Jordanian

1. NOUN

/ dʒɔrˈdeɪniən / (AmE), / dʒɔːˈdeɪniən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Jordan
a person from Jordan

Examples

The new ambassador is a distinguished Jordanian with years of diplomatic experience.
Many Jordanians are proud of their heritage and traditions.
He became friends with a Jordanian while studying abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jordanian - of or relating to Jordan or its people (adjective)

Etymology

From Jordan + -ian (suffix forming demonyms and adjectives).

2. ADJ

/ dʒɔrˈdeɪniən / (AmE), / dʒɔːˈdeɪniən / (BrE)
relating to Jordan or its people
of or relating to Jordan

Examples

The museum has an impressive collection of Jordanian pottery.
She prepared a traditional Jordanian meal for her guests.
Several Jordanian authors attended the international literary festival.

Synset Examples

Jordanian archeological sites

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jordanian - a person from Jordan
  • Synonym: Jordan - a country in the Middle East

Etymology

Formed from 'Jordan' (the country) + English adjective suffix '-ian'.
