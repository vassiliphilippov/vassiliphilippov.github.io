Valetta

1. PROPN

/ vəˈlɛtə / (AmE), / vəˈlɛtə / (BrE)
the capital city of Malta, located on the northeastern coast of the island
capital city of Malta

Examples

The historic buildings in Valetta attract thousands of tourists each year.
She spent her summer vacation exploring the streets and markets of Valetta.
Many international conferences are held in Valetta due to its rich cultural heritage.

Etymology

From Italian Valletta, named after Jean Parisot de la Valette, a Grand Master of the Knights Hospitaller.
