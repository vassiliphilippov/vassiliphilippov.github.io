hashtag

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæʃˌtæɡ / (AmE), / ˈhæʃ.tæɡ / (BrE)
A word or phrase preceded by the hash symbol (#) on social media websites and applications, used to identify messages on a specific topic.
metadata tag on social media

Examples

Add a hashtag to your post to join the conversation on climate change.
The company's new marketing campaign used the hashtag #InnovationForAll.
Her photo went viral after she included a trending hashtag in the caption.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tag - label or keyword used to categorize content online

Etymology

Formed from 'hash' (the symbol #) and 'tag' (label), popularized by Twitter in the late 2000s.
