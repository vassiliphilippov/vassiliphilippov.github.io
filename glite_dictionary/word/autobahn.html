autobahn

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔtoʊˌbɑn / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊˌbɑːn / (BrE)
an expressway, especially one in Germany or Austria, designed for fast traffic, often with no speed limit on some sections
German or Austrian expressway

Examples

Driving on the autobahn can be an exciting experience for visitors to Germany.
Sections of the autobahn have no maximum speed limit, attracting car enthusiasts from around the world.
The severe accident occurred on a busy stretch of autobahn near Munich.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motorway - a major road for fast traffic, typically outside urban areas
  • Synonym: expressway - a major divided highway with limited access

Etymology

From German 'Autobahn', from 'Auto' (car) + 'Bahn' (road).
