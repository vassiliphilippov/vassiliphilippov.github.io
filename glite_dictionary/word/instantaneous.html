instantaneous

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.stənˈteɪ.ni.əs / (AmE), / ˌɪn.stənˈteɪ.ni.əs / (BrE)
happening or done immediately, with no delay.
happening immediately; with no delay

Examples

The response from the support team was instantaneous, which surprised everyone.
The medicine provides instantaneous relief from headaches.
An instantaneous flash of lightning lit up the entire sky.

Synset Examples

relief was instantaneous
instant gratification

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immediate - happening or done at once; without delay
  • Antonym: delayed - happening later than expected or intended

Etymology

From Latin 'instantaneus', from 'instans' (present, urgent).
