rueful

1. ADJ

/ ˈruːfəl / (AmE), / ˈruːfəl / (BrE)
feeling or showing regret, remorse, or sorrow
feeling or showing regret or sorrow

Examples

She gave a rueful smile as she apologized for her mistake.
His rueful expression showed he truly regretted his harsh words.
After the prank, they exchanged rueful glances, realizing they'd gone too far.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regretful - feeling or showing regret
  • Synonym: remorseful - full of deep regret or guilt
  • Antonym: unrepentant - not feeling regret for one's actions or sins

Etymology

From Middle English 'reuful', from Old English 'hreowan' (to rue, regret).
