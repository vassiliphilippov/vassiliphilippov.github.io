earnestly

1. ADV

/ ˈɜrnəstli / (AmE), / ˈɜːnɪstli / (BrE)
in a serious and sincere way; with deep and heartfelt intention
in a serious and sincere manner

Examples

She spoke earnestly about the importance of helping others.
He listened earnestly, eager to understand every detail of the plan.
The volunteers worked earnestly to finish the project before the deadline.

Synset Examples

talking earnestly with his son
she started studying snakes in earnest
a play dealing seriously with the question of divorce

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seriously - in a serious way; with seriousness
  • Antonym: lightly - in a way that is not serious or sincere

Etymology

From Middle English 'ernest' (seriousness), from Old French 'ernest', from Latin 'earnestus'.
