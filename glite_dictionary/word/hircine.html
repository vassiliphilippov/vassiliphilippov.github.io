hircine

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɜr.saɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɜː.sɪn / (BrE)
of, relating to, or characteristic of a goat, especially having a strong, goat-like odor
like or relating to a goat (esp. odor)

Examples

The sweater had a hircine smell after being stored in the barn for months.
Some cheeses are prized for their hircine flavors and aromas.
His hircine beard and wild manner made him stand out in the crowd.

Etymology

From Latin hircinus, from hircus (goat).
