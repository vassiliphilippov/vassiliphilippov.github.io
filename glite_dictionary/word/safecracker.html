safecracker

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪfˌkrækər / (AmE), / ˈseɪfˌkrakə / (BrE)
a criminal who breaks into safes to steal valuables
thief who breaks open safes

Examples

The notorious safecracker was finally caught after months of elaborate heists.
He became a skilled safecracker, using his talents for crime rather than good.
Police believe the safecracker used special tools to open the vault undetected.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burglar - a person who illegally enters buildings to steal
  • Synonym: robber - a person who takes property unlawfully, typically by force

Etymology

From 'safe' (a strongbox) + 'cracker' (one who breaks open).
