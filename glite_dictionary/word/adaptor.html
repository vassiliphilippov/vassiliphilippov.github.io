adaptor

1. NOUN

/ əˈdæptər / (AmE), / əˈdaptə / (BrE)
a device that enables equipment or parts to be used together when they would otherwise be incompatible, or used in a way other than originally intended
device for making things compatible

Examples

I bought an adaptor to connect my headphones to the new laptop.
The travel adaptor lets you use your devices in different countries.
You will need an adaptor if your plug doesn't fit this socket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adapter - alternative spelling for adaptor (device for compatibility)

Etymology

From Latin 'adaptare' (to fit to), via 'adapt'.
