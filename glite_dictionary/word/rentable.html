rentable

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛntəbəl / (AmE), / ˈrɛntəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of property or equipment) able or suitable to be rented.
able to be rented

Examples

The apartment is rentable from the first of June.
Most of the equipment is rentable by the hour or by the day.
Due to recent renovations, the property has become much more rentable.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unrentable - not able or fit to be rented
  • Synonym: lettable - able or suitable to be let (British English)

Etymology

From 'rent' + '-able', formed by adding the suffix '-able' meaning 'capable of, fit for'.
