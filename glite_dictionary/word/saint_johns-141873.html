Saint Johns

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt ˈdʒɑnz / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈdʒɒnz / (BrE)
a river in northeastern Florida that flows northward through Jacksonville and empties into the Atlantic Ocean
river in northeastern Florida

Examples

The Saint Johns is one of the few rivers in North America that flows northward.
Many people enjoy boating and fishing along the Saint Johns each year.
The city of Jacksonville grew along the banks of the Saint Johns River.
