pocketable

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɑkətəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈpɒkətəbl̩ / (BrE)
Small enough to be carried in a pocket; easily fitting into a garment pocket.
small enough to fit in a pocket

Examples

This camera is so pocketable that I bring it everywhere.
The company introduced a new range of pocketable smartphones last year.
She keeps a pocketable notebook for jotting down ideas on the go.

Synset Examples

pocket-size paperbacks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: portable - able to be easily carried or moved

Etymology

Formed from 'pocket' + '-able'.
