evaporable

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈvæpərəbəl / (AmE), / ɪˈvæpərəbəl / (BrE)
(of a substance) capable of being evaporated or volatilized into a vapor.
able to be evaporated

Examples

The solvent is highly evaporable, making storage in a sealed container essential.
Some perfumes contain evaporable oils to help the scent disperse quickly.
To prevent loss, always minimize the exposure of evaporable liquids to open air.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volatile - (of a substance) easily evaporated at normal temperatures
  • Antonym: nonvolatile - (of a substance) not easily vaporized

Etymology

From 'evaporate' + '-able', following the pattern of Latin-based adjectives.
