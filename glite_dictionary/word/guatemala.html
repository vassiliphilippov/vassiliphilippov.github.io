Guatemala

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡwɑːtəˈmɑːlə / (AmE), / ˌɡwɑːtəˈmɑːlə / (BrE)
A country in Central America, bordering Mexico, known for its rich Mayan heritage and diverse geography. Gained independence from Spain in 1821.
country in Central America

Examples

She traveled to Guatemala to study ancient Mayan ruins.
Coffee from Guatemala is prized for its unique flavor.
After gaining independence, Guatemala faced political and social challenges.

Etymology

From Nahuatl 'Cuauhtēmallān' (land of many trees), via Spanish.
