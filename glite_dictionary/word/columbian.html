Columbian

1. ADJ

/ kəˈlʌmbiən / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmbiən / (BrE)
relating to Christopher Columbus or his discoveries.
relating to Christopher Columbus

Examples

The Columbian voyages changed the course of world history.
Many artifacts from the Columbian era are preserved in museums.
The Columbian exchange refers to the widespread transfer of plants, animals, and culture after Columbus's journeys.

Etymology

From Latinized form of Columbus (Christopher Columbus), with English suffix -ian.
