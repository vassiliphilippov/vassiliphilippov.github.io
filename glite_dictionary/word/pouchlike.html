pouchlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈpaʊtʃˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpaʊtʃˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Having the shape or characteristics of a pouch; resembling a pouch.
resembling a pouch in shape

Examples

The animal has a pouchlike fold of skin on its belly for carrying its young.
Some flowers have a pouchlike shape to attract pollinators.
The tumor was found to be enclosed in a pouchlike membrane.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saclike - having the form or characteristics of a sac
