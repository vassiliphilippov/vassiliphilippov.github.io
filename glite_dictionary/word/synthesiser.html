synthesiser

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪnθəˌsaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsɪnθɪsaɪzə / (BrE)
an electronic musical instrument that creates and modifies sounds electronically, often played with a keyboard and capable of imitating various other instruments
electronic musical instrument

Examples

He played a catchy melody on the synthesiser during the concert.
Many pop songs from the 1980s feature the synthesiser as a prominent instrument.
With her new synthesiser, she explored creating unique electronic sounds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keyboard - a musical instrument played by means of keys
  • Degree Lower: piano - a large keyboard musical instrument

Etymology

From synthesize + -er, referring to an instrument that synthesizes sounds.
