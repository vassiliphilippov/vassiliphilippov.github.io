microbiologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌmaɪkroʊbaɪˈɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies or specializes in microbiology, the branch of science dealing with microorganisms
scientist specializing in microbiology

Examples

After earning her degree, she worked as a microbiologist in a pharmaceutical laboratory.
The microbiologist discovered a new strain of bacteria in the soil sample.
Many hospitals employ a microbiologist to monitor infections and advise on treatments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biologist - a scientist who studies living organisms
  • Synonym: microbiology - the scientific study of microorganisms

Etymology

From 'microbiology' (from Greek mikros 'small' + bios 'life' + -logy) + -ist, denoting a person who practices.
