soiled

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɔɪld / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪld / (BrE)
dirty or stained with dirt, grime, or other unwanted substances; not clean
dirty; not clean

Examples

The mechanic's hands were soiled with grease after fixing the truck.
Please avoid sitting on the soiled couch until it has been cleaned.
She changed her soiled dress after spilling coffee all over it.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dirty - covered or marked with dirt; not clean
  • Antonym: clean - free from dirt, marks, or stains

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'soil,' from Old French *soillier* (to make dirty).
