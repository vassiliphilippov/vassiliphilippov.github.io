parheliacal

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɑːrˌhiːliˈækəl / (AmE), / ˌpɑːˌhiːliˈækəl / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling a parhelion (a bright spot in the sky on either side of the sun, often called a sundog).
relating to a parhelion or sundog

Examples

The sky was filled with parheliacal halos as the sun rose above the horizon.
Scientists noted several parheliacal phenomena during the unusual weather event.
The painting captures the parheliacal glow seen in cold winter skies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parhelion - A bright spot appearing on either side of the sun, known as a sundog

Etymology

From Greek 'parēlion' (parhelion) with the suffix '-al' meaning 'pertaining to.'
