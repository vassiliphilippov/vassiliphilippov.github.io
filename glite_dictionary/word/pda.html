PDA

1. NOUN

/ ˌpiː ˌdiː ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌpiː ˌdiː ˈeɪ / (BrE)
a lightweight portable electronic device, such as a palmtop computer, used especially as a personal organizer or diary and often equipped with communication functions
personal digital assistant (handheld electronic organizer)

Examples

Before smartphones, many professionals used a PDA to manage their schedules and contacts.
I misplaced my PDA, so I lost my entire address book for the conference.
Her old PDA still works, but she prefers her new smartphone for planning appointments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organizer - a personal electronic device for keeping contact details, calendar, etc.
  • Degree Higher: smartphone - a mobile phone with advanced computing capabilities
  • Degree Higher: notebook - a portable computer, generally larger and more powerful than a PDA

Etymology

Abbreviation of Personal Digital Assistant, a term popularized in the 1990s.
