humpbacked

1. ADJ

/ ˈhʌmpˌbækt / (AmE), / ˈhʌmpˌbækt / (BrE)
having a hump on the back, especially as a result of a spinal abnormality such as kyphosis.
having a hump on the back

Examples

The old man was humpbacked from years of hard labor.
She recognized the humpbacked silhouette immediately as Mr. Grimes walked down the street.
A humpbacked whale gracefully surfaced near the boat, its curved shape arching through the water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kyphotic - having or relating to kyphosis; abnormally curved spine
  • Antonym: straight-backed - having a straight back, without curvature
