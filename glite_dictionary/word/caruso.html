Caruso

1. PROPN

/ kəˈruːsoʊ / (AmE), / kəˈruːsəʊ / (BrE)
Enrico Caruso (1873–1921), an Italian operatic tenor renowned for his powerful voice and influential recordings.
famous Italian operatic tenor (1873–1921)

Examples

Many consider Caruso one of the greatest tenors in the history of opera.
The recording features arias famously sung by Caruso in the early twentieth century.
Opera enthusiasts often reference Caruso when discussing vocal excellence.

Etymology

Italian surname; Enrico Caruso, renowned Italian tenor.
