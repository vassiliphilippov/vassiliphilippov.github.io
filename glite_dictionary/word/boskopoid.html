boskopoid

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑs.kəˌpɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɒs.kəˌpɔɪd / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling Boskop man, an extinct hominid type from South Africa.
of or like Boskop man (archaic anthropology)
archaic

Examples

The skull was described as boskopoid in shape by early anthropologists.
Several fossils found in the region display boskopoid cranial features.
The term boskopoid is rarely used in modern anthropology due to outdated classification systems.

Etymology

From Boskop, a site in South Africa where remains were found, plus the suffix -oid (resembling).
