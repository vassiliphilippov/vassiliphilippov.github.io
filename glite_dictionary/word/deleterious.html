deleterious

1. ADJ

/ ˌdɛl.əˈtɪr.i.əs / (AmE), / ˌdɛl.ɪˈtɪə.ri.əs / (BrE)
causing harm or damage, especially to living things
harmful; damaging

Examples

Prolonged exposure to the chemical has been shown to have deleterious effects on human health.
The organization warned about the deleterious impact of pollution on marine life.
Some foods contain additives that could be deleterious to children if consumed in large amounts.

Synset Examples

deleterious chemical additives

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harmful - causing or capable of causing harm
  • Antonym: benign - not harmful or dangerous

Etymology

From Latin 'deleterius', from Greek 'dēlētērios' (noxious).
