Puerto Rican

1. NOUN

/ ˌpwɛrtoʊ ˈriːkən / (AmE), / ˌpwɛːtəʊ ˈriːkən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Puerto Rico or a person of Puerto Rican descent
a person from Puerto Rico

Examples

Many Puerto Ricans celebrate their heritage with traditional music and food.
She met a group of Puerto Ricans while visiting New York City.
The Puerto Rican community plays an important role in the city's culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Puerto Rico - a Caribbean island and unincorporated U.S. territory

Etymology

From Puerto Rico, the name of the Caribbean island, with '-an' forming the demonym.
