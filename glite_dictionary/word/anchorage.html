Anchorage

1. PROPN

/ ˈæŋkərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈæŋkərɪdʒ / (BrE)
the largest city in Alaska, located in the south central part of the state
the largest city in Alaska

Examples

I visited Anchorage last summer and was amazed by its natural beauty.
Many people move to Anchorage for its business opportunities and vibrant arts scene.
Snowfall in Anchorage can be heavy during the winter months.

Synset Examples

Anchorage is the largest city in Alaska

Etymology

Named from the anchorage site at Cook Inlet, used by early ships.
