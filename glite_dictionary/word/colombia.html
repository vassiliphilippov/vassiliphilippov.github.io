Colombia

1. PROPN

/ kəˈloʊmbiə / (AmE), / kəˈlɒmbiə / (BrE)
A country in northwestern South America, bordered by the Pacific Ocean and the Caribbean Sea, with Spanish as its official language.
country in northwestern South America

Examples

Colombia is known for its rich cultural heritage and beautiful landscapes.
The coffee produced in Colombia is famous worldwide for its quality.
She traveled to Colombia to improve her Spanish and experience local traditions.

Etymology

Named after Christopher Columbus (Latin: Christophorus Columbus).
