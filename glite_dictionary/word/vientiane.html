Vientiane

1. PROPN

/ viˌɛn(t)iˈæn / (AmE), / viˌɛn(t)iˈæn / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Laos, located on the banks of the Mekong River.
capital of Laos

Examples

The conference was held in Vientiane, attracting delegates from across Southeast Asia.
Tourists often visit Vientiane to see its Buddhist temples and colonial architecture.
He moved to Vientiane for work, adjusting quickly to the pace of the city.

Etymology

From French 'Viangchan', from Lao ວຽງຈັນ (Viangchan), meaning 'city of sandalwood'.
