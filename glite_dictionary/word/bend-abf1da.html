bend

1. NOUN

/ bɛnd / (AmE), / bɛnd / (BrE)
a curved part of a road, river, or similar structure
curved segment; road or river curve

Examples

The car slowed down as it approached the bend in the road.
Mist rose from the river at each bend through the valley.
The train derailed at a sharp bend just outside the station.

  • Synonym: curve - a shape or line with no straight parts
