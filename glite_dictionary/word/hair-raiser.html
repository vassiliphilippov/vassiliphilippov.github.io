hair-raiser

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛr ˌreɪzər / (AmE), / ˈheə ˌreɪzə / (BrE)
an event, story, or experience that causes intense excitement or fear; something very thrilling or frightening
thrilling or frightening event or story

Examples

The roller coaster was such a hair-raiser that people screamed the whole way through.
Last night's storm was a real hair-raiser—the thunder kept everyone awake.
That ghost story turned out to be a genuine hair-raiser and kept me up all night.

Synset Examples

the movie was an old-fashioned hair-raiser

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thriller - an exciting story or movie, especially one involving crime or espionage
  • Synonym: chiller - a story or film intended to scare or thrill; something frightening
