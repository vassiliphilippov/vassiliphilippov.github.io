disbelievingly

1. ADV

/ ˌdɪsbɪˈlivɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌdɪsbɪˈliːvɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows disbelief or a lack of acceptance that something is true.
in a manner showing disbelief

Examples

She stared disbelievingly at the unexpected letter in her hands.
The audience gasped disbelievingly at the magician's final trick.
He laughed disbelievingly, unable to accept what he had just heard.

Synset Examples

the woman looked up at her incredulously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incredulously - in a manner indicating disbelief
  • Antonym: believingly - in a manner showing belief
