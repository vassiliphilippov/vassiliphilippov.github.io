Fes

1. PROPN

/ fɛz / (AmE), / fɛz / (BrE)
A city in north-central Morocco, known as a major religious, cultural, and historical center.
city in north-central Morocco

Examples

The ancient medina of Fes is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
Many visitors to Morocco are drawn to Fes for its historic architecture and bustling markets.
Religious scholars have long gathered in Fes to study at its famed university.
