panetela

1. NOUN

/ ˌpænəˈtɛlə / (AmE), / ˌpanɪˈtɛlə / (BrE)
a long, slender cigar, typically longer and narrower than most standard cigars
long, slender cigar

Examples

He enjoyed a panetela after dinner, savoring its smooth flavor.
The shop specializes in imported panetelas for cigar aficionados.
She bought a box of panetela cigars as a gift for her father.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cigar - a cylindrical roll of tobacco for smoking

Etymology

From Spanish 'panetela', perhaps derived from Italian 'panatella', meaning a type of bread roll, referencing its shape.
