mirthfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɝθ.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmɜːθ.fəl.nəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being full of merriment or joyous laughter.
great merriment; joyful laughter

Examples

His mirthfulness was contagious, spreading laughter throughout the room.
Every holiday season, the house was filled with mirthfulness and cheer.
The children’s mirthfulness made the entire playground come alive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joyfulness - the state of being joyful; gladness
  • Antonym: gloominess - the state of being gloomy or depressed

Etymology

From 'mirthful' (full of mirth), with suffix '-ness'. 'Mirth' from Old English 'myrgth' (joy, pleasure).
