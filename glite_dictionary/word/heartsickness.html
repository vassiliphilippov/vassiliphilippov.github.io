heartsickness

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrtˌsɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtˌsɪknəs / (BrE)
A feeling of deep sadness, emotional pain, or hopelessness, especially caused by disappointment or longing.
deep sadness or emotional pain

Examples

She was overwhelmed by heartsickness after receiving the bad news.
The novel explores themes of heartsickness and longing for lost love.
He fought through his heartsickness to carry on with daily life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: despair - the complete loss or absence of hope
  • Synonym: hopelessness - a state of having no expectation of good or success
  • Antonym: cheerfulness - the quality of being noticeably happy and optimistic
