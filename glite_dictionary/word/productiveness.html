productiveness

1. NOUN

/ prəˈdʌktɪvnəs / (AmE), / prəˈdʌktɪvnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being productive or effective at creating or achieving results
quality of being productive

Examples

Her productiveness increased after she started using a daily planner.
The productiveness of the land impressed everyone on the tour.
Managers value employees for their dedication and productiveness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: productivity - the state or quality of producing something, especially in large amounts

Etymology

Formed from 'productive' + '-ness' (noun suffix).
