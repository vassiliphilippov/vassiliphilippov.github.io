youthfully

1. ADV

/ ˈjuːθfəli / (AmE), / ˈjuːθfəli / (BrE)
In a manner characteristic of youth, especially in being energetic, enthusiastic, or fresh.
in a manner like a young person

Examples

Even in his seventies, he approaches new projects youthfully and with genuine excitement.
She danced youthfully, her movements vibrant and full of life.
The author writes youthfully, with an energy that belies her age.

Synset Examples

he is still youthfully enthusiastic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: youthful - having the qualities of youth
