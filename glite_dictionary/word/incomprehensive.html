incomprehensive

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnkəmˈprɛhənsɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪnkəmˈprɛhənsɪv / (BrE)
Not comprehensive; not including all or nearly all elements or aspects.
not comprehensive; incomplete in scope

Examples

The report was criticized for being incomprehensive and missing key data.
Her incomprehensive knowledge of the topic led to several misunderstandings.
This manual is rather incomprehensive; it fails to cover advanced features.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: comprehensive - including all or nearly all elements or aspects

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix in- (not) to comprehensive.
