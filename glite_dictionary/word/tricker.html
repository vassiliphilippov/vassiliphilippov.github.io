tricker

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɪkər / (AmE), / ˈtrɪkə / (BrE)
a person who plays practical jokes or tricks on others
person who plays practical jokes

Examples

He has a reputation as the office tricker, always pulling lighthearted stunts on his coworkers.
As a child, she was a notorious tricker at family gatherings, always hiding shoes or switching name tags.
The group found the mysterious tricker who had filled their lockers with confetti.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prankster - someone who plays pranks or practical jokes
  • Synonym: trickster - a person who deceives or cheats people, often in folklore
