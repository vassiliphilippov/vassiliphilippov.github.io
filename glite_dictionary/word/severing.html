severing

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛvərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɛvərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of cutting off or separating something by cutting
the act of cutting off or separating

Examples

The sudden severing of diplomatic ties shocked the international community.
After the severing of the cable, the entire network went down.
His severing of old friendships left him feeling isolated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: separation - the act of moving or being moved apart
  • Antonym: joining - the act of joining or uniting things

Etymology

From Middle English 'severen', from Old French 'sevrer', from Latin 'separare' (to separate).
