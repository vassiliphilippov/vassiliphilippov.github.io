Coleridgean

1. ADJ

/ ˌkoʊləˈrɪdʒiən / (AmE), / ˌkəʊləˈrɪdʒɪən / (BrE)
of or relating to Samuel Taylor Coleridge or his writings.
relating to Coleridge or his works

Examples

Her poem displays a distinctly Coleridgean sense of the mysterious and sublime.
The critic praised the Coleridgean imagination evident in the novel's structure.
He wrote an essay on Coleridgean themes in English Romantic literature.

Etymology

From the name of Samuel Taylor Coleridge (1772–1834), English poet and philosopher, with the suffix -an.
