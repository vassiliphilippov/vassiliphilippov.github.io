pictographic

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɪk.təˈɡræf.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɪk.təˈɡræf.ɪk / (BrE)
consisting of or characterized by the use of pictographs; relating to pictographs.
relating to pictographs

Examples

The ancient tablet contains pictographic symbols representing words and ideas.
Some early languages were written using a pictographic script, not alphabetic letters.
The museum displayed pictographic drawings from prehistoric caves.

Synset Examples

a pictographic script
pictographic stage in the development of writing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pictorial - consisting of or related to pictures; illustrated

Etymology

From pictograph + -ic, indicating relation to pictographs.
