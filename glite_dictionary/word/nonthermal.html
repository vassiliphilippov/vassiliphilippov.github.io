nonthermal

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈθɜrməl / (AmE), / nɒnˈθɜːməl / (BrE)
Not involving or produced by heat.
not involving heat

Examples

The experiment detected nonthermal radiation emitted from the plasma.
The process is considered nonthermal because it does not raise the temperature of the material.
Their research focuses on nonthermal mechanisms of energy transfer in molecules.

Synset Examples

nonthermal luminescence

Related Terms

  • Antonym: thermal - relating to heat

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) and 'thermal' (relating to heat).
