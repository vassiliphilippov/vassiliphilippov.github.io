kafir

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːfɪr / (AmE), / ˈkæfɪə / (BrE)
An offensive and insulting term for a Black African, used mainly in South Africa; considered highly offensive and racist.
offensive term for Black Africans (South Africa)
offensive

Examples

The use of the word kafir is strictly condemned and considered hate speech in South Africa.
She was deeply hurt by the racist slur kafir directed at her.
Modern literature and law recognize kafir as an unacceptable term embodying historical racism.

Etymology

From Arabic *kāfir* (unbeliever, infidel); in South Africa, appropriated as a racial slur.
