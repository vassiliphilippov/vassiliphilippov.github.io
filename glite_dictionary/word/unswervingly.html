unswervingly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈswɜrvɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈswɜːvɪŋli / (BrE)
In a steady and unwavering manner; without deviating or changing direction or purpose.
in a steady, unwavering manner

Examples

She remained unswervingly loyal to her principles despite criticism from her peers.
The leader pursued his vision unswervingly, inspiring confidence in his followers.
The company has unswervingly focused on quality since its founding.

Synset Examples

the ship travelled unswervingly through the night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steadfastly - in a firm and unwavering manner
  • Antonym: waveringly - in a way that lacks steadiness or determination

Etymology

Formed from 'unswerving' (not turning aside) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).

in a steady, unwavering way

Examples

Related Terms
