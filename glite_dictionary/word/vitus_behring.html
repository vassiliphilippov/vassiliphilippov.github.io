Vitus Behring

1. PROPN

/ ˈviːtəs ˈbeɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈviːtəs ˈbɛːrɪŋ / (BrE)
(1681–1741) A Danish explorer employed by Russia, known for exploring the northern Pacific Ocean and discovering the Bering Strait.
Danish explorer, discovered Bering Strait

Examples

Vitus Behring led two major expeditions that mapped parts of Siberia and Alaska.
The Bering Strait was named after Vitus Behring in recognition of his discoveries.
Many geographic features in the Arctic are named after Vitus Behring.
