metallic-coloured

1. ADJ

/ məˈtælɪk ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / məˈtælɪk ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having a color that resembles the sheen or brightness of metal
having a metallic-looking color
british

Examples

The beetle had a metallic-coloured shell that shimmered in the sunlight.
She wore a metallic-coloured dress to the party, catching everyone's attention.
The artist used a metallic-coloured paint to achieve a striking effect on the canvas.

Related Terms
