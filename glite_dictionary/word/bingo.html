bingo

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɪŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
A game in which players mark off numbers on cards as the corresponding numbered balls are drawn at random, typically aiming to complete a specified pattern and call out 'bingo' to win.
a game with numbered cards and random draws

Examples

Every Wednesday night, the retirement home organizes a bingo game for its residents.
After marking the last number, she shouted 'bingo!' and won the round.
He bought new cards for the weekly bingo session at the community center.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lotto - a gambling game where players match selected numbers to numbers drawn at random

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly imitation of the call made by a winner.
