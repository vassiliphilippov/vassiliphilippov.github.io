spelunker

1. NOUN

/ spəˈlʌŋkər / (AmE), / spəˈlʌŋkə / (BrE)
a person who explores caves, especially as a hobby
person who explores caves

Examples

As a spelunker, she has explored some of the deepest caves in Europe.
The club welcomes both experienced spelunkers and beginners interested in caving.
He became fascinated with geology after his first trip as a spelunker in high school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caver - person who explores caves

Etymology

From Latin 'spelunca' (cave), via American English, first used early 20th century.
