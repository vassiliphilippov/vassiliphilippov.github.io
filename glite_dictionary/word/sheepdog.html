sheepdog

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃipˌdɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈʃiːpdɒɡ / (BrE)
A dog of any of various breeds, typically with long hair, bred and trained to herd and guard sheep.
dog bred to herd sheep

Examples

The sheepdog rounded up the flock with remarkable skill.
Farmers often rely on a sheepdog to help manage their sheep.
Her sheepdog is trained to respond to whistle commands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: herding dog - a dog bred for herding livestock
  • Synonym: guard dog - a dog trained to protect property or livestock
