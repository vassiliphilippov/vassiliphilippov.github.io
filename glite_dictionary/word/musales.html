Musales

1. NOUN

/ mjuːˈseɪliːz / (AmE), / mjuːˈseɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of tropical flowering plants, including bananas and related families
order of tropical flowering plants

Examples

The Musales comprise several families of tropical plants, most notably the bananas.
Botanists studied the unique flower structures found in members of the Musales.
Some edible tropical fruits, such as plantains, belong to the Musales.

Etymology

From modern Latin Musales, from the genus Musa (banana).
