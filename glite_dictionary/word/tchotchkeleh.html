tchotchkeleh

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑtʃ.kə.lə / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒtʃ.kə.lə / (BrE)
(Yiddish) An attractive, unconventional woman.
attractive, unconventional woman
informal

Examples

Everyone noticed the new tchotchkeleh at the art show, with her bold style and infectious energy.
She was always a bit of a tchotchkeleh, standing out from the crowd with her charm.
In the close-knit neighborhood, the arrival of a young tchotchkeleh created a buzz among the locals.

Etymology

From Yiddish 'tshatshkele', diminutive of 'tshatshke' (trinket, bauble).
