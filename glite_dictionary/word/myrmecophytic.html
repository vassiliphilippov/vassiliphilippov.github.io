myrmecophytic

1. ADJ

/ mɜrmɛkəˈfɪtɪk / (AmE), / mɜːmɪkəˈfɪtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of myrmecophytes—plants that have a mutualistic association with ants.
relating to plants associated with ants

Examples

Some orchids are known for their myrmecophytic relationships, housing ants within their structures.
The myrmecophytic adaptation of the plant provides shelter for an ant colony in exchange for protection.
Botanists are studying several myrmecophytic species in the tropical rainforest ecosystem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: myrmecophyte - A plant living in association with ants

Etymology

From Greek myrmex (ant) + -phytic (relating to plants).
