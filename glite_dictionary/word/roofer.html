roofer

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːfər / (AmE), / ˈruːfə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to lay or repair roofs
person who installs or repairs roofs

Examples

We hired a roofer to replace the old shingles on our house.
The roofer arrived early to inspect the leak in the roof.
Many roofers work outdoors year-round, regardless of the weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: builder - person who constructs buildings
  • Synonym: carpenter - person who builds or repairs wooden structures

Etymology

From 'roof' + '-er', denoting a person who works with roofs.
