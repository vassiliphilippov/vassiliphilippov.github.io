trimotored

1. ADJ

/ traɪˈmoʊtərd / (AmE), / traɪˈməʊtəd / (BrE)
(of a vehicle or aircraft) having three motors or engines.
having three motors

Examples

The company developed a trimotored seaplane to improve performance over long distances.
Their latest model is a trimotored vehicle designed for greater reliability.
In the early days of aviation, trimotored aircraft were popular for commercial flights.

Etymology

From 'tri-' meaning three and 'motored' (equipped with a motor).
