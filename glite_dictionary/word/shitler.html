Shitler

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtlɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtlə / (BrE)
A highly offensive and vulgar insult for a leader, formed by blending 'shit' and 'Hitler,' intended to equate the person with both incompetence and tyranny.
derogatory insult likening someone to Hitler using vulgar language
offensive vulgar slang

Examples

Many activists have begun referring to the unpopular president as Shitler on social media.
Using the word Shitler in public discourse is guaranteed to provoke strong reactions and controversy.
He was furious when protesters held signs calling him Shitler during the rally.

Synset Examples

He called the corrupt leader Shitler in his speech.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Hitler - adopted name of Adolf Hitler, leader of Nazi Germany
  • Synonym: dictator - a ruler with total power over a country, typically one who has obtained power by force

Etymology

Blend of 'shit' (vulgar term for excrement) and 'Hitler,' used to insult a disliked leader.
