greening

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡriːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or phenomenon by which vitality and freshness are restored, especially in plants or landscapes after a dormant period
restoration of vitality or freshness (esp. in nature)

Examples

The greening of the countryside after winter was a welcome sight to the villagers.
There has been a noticeable greening of urban spaces thanks to new environmental policies.
The annual greening of the park draws visitors who come to enjoy the vibrant new growth.

Synset Examples

the annual rejuvenation of the landscape

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rejuvenation - the action or process of making something look or feel better, younger, or more vital again
