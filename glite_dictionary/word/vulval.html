vulval

1. ADJ

/ ˈvʌl.vəl / (AmE), / ˈvʌl.vəl / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the vulva
relating to the vulva
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with a vulval infection and prescribed appropriate medication.
Certain skin conditions can affect the vulval area and cause discomfort.
Doctors recommend regular self-examination to check for changes in vulval health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vulvar - relating to the vulva (variant spelling, synonym)

Etymology

From 'vulva' + -al (forming adjectives of relation), from Latin 'vulva' (womb, female external genitals).
