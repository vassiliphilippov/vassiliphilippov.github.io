Mumbai

1. PROPN

/ mʊmˈbaɪ / (AmE), / mʊmˈbaɪ / (BrE)
a port city in western India, located on the coast of the Arabian Sea, and the country's largest city by population.
major port city in western India

Examples

I visited Mumbai last year and was amazed by its vibrant atmosphere.
Many international companies have their headquarters in Mumbai.
The film industry in Mumbai is known as Bollywood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bombay - former name of Mumbai, major city in India

Etymology

From Marathi मुंबई (Mumbaī), named after the local Hindu goddess Mumbā Devī.
