specialness

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛʃ.əl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃ.əl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being special or distinctive; a distinguishing trait or feature
the quality of being special

Examples

Her specialness was obvious to everyone in the room.
The specialness of this gift lies in its personal meaning.
They celebrate the specialness that each child brings to the class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uniqueness - the quality of being unique or one of a kind
  • Antonym: ordinariness - the quality of being ordinary or not special

2. NOUN

/ ˈspɛʃəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃəlnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being special, unique, or pertaining to a specific case or instance
the quality of being special or unique

Examples

Everyone recognized the specialness of the occasion and behaved accordingly.
She wanted to give her children a sense of specialness and belonging.
The artist’s work has a specialness that sets it apart from others.

Synset Examples

the particularity of human situations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uniqueness - the quality of being one of a kind
  • Antonym: ordinariness - the quality of being ordinary or not special

Etymology

From 'special' + '-ness', a common suffix forming nouns indicating a state or quality.
