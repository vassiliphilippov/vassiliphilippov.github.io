procurance

1. NOUN

/ proʊˈkjʊrəns / (AmE), / prəˈkjʊərəns / (BrE)
the act of acquiring or obtaining possession of something
the act of obtaining something

Examples

The procurance of rare artifacts required both skill and patience.
He contributed significantly to the procurance of essential supplies during the crisis.
All matters relating to the procurance of evidence were handled by the legal team.

Synset Examples

he was responsible for the procurement of materials and supplies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: procurement - the act of obtaining or acquiring something, especially for business or government purposes
  • Synonym: acquisition - the act of acquiring or gaining possession

Etymology

From Latin 'procurare' (to take care of, manage), via Anglo-Norman and Middle English forms.
