kepi

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪ.pi / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.pi / (BrE)
A cap with a flat circular top and a visor, commonly worn as part of some military uniforms, especially in France.
flat-topped military cap

Examples

The French officer adjusted his kepi before stepping out into the bright sunlight.
During the parade, each soldier wore a neatly pressed uniform and a blue kepi.
He bought an old kepi at the military memorabilia shop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cap - a soft, flat hat without a brim

Etymology

From French képi, from Swiss German Käppi (diminutive of Kappe 'cap').
