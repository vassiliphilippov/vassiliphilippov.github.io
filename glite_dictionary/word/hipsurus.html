Hipsurus

1. NOUN

/ hɪpˈsʊrəs / (AmE), / hɪpˈsjʊərəs / (BrE)
A genus of marine fish in the family Embiotocidae, known as surfperches, native to coastal waters of the Pacific Ocean.
genus of marine fish (surfperches)

Examples

The surfperch species in the genus Hipsurus are commonly found along the Pacific Coast.
Marine biologists studied the unique reproductive behavior of Hipsurus during spawning season.
One of the distinguishing features of Hipsurus is its laterally compressed body shape.
