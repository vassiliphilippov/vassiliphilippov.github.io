quadrangular

1. ADJ

/ kwɑˈdræŋɡjələr / (AmE), / kwɒˈdræŋɡjʊlə / (BrE)
having four angles and four sides; relating to or shaped like a quadrangle.
having four angles or sides

Examples

The ancient temple was built on a quadrangular foundation.
The park features a quadrangular courtyard at its center.
A quadrangular tower rose above the castle walls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quadrilateral - a four-sided and four-angled shape

Etymology

From Latin 'quadrangularis', from 'quadri-' (four) + 'angulus' (angle).
