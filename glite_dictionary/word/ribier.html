ribier

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈbiːər / (AmE), / rɪˈbiːə / (BrE)
a variety of dark reddish-purple table grape grown chiefly in California
a type of California table grape

Examples

The salad was garnished with fresh ribier grapes for a burst of sweetness.
California farmers exported a record harvest of ribier last year.
He prefers ribier over other grapes because of its rich color and flavor.
