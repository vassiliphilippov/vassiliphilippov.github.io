egoistical

1. ADJ

/ ˌiːɡoʊˈɪstɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌiːɡəʊˈɪstɪkəl / (BrE)
Thinking only of yourself and your own interests, not considering others; selfish.
selfish; self-centered

Examples

Her egoistical attitude made it difficult for her to form close friendships.
The manager's egoistical behavior alienated many of his employees.
They accused him of being egoistical and ignoring the needs of the team.

Synset Examples

Selfish men were … trying to make capital for themselves out of the sacred cause of civil rights
The merely egoistic satisfactions of fame are easily nullified
However, because of their strong-minded nature, they are often perceived as difficult and egoistical individuals.
I will never forgive her for her egocentric and shameful conduct towards Gilliatt
What I can't believe is that people are so stupid and self-centered that they think they HAVE to comment on things they don't like.
We're asked to see Rachel as this spoilt, self-centred woman but the rest of them are just as bad, if not worse.
As the crying baby, we express purely the self-serving thoughts that arise in our minds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: selfish - lacking consideration for others; concerned chiefly with one's own personal profit or pleasure
  • Antonym: altruistic - showing a disinterested and selfless concern for the well-being of others

Etymology

From ego + -istical, based on Latin 'ego' (I, self).
