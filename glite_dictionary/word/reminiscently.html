reminiscently

1. ADV

/ ˌrɛməˈnɪsəntli / (AmE), / ˌrɛmɪˈnɪsəntli / (BrE)
in a way that recalls or suggests past experiences or memories
in a way that recalls the past

Examples

She reminiscently described the summers she spent at her grandparents' house by the lake.
He smiled reminiscently as he recounted stories from his childhood.
The author writes reminiscently about her travels through Europe in the 1970s.

Synset Examples

she spoke reminiscently of her days in college

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nostalgically - in a way that expresses longing for the past

Etymology

From 'reminiscent' (from Latin reminiscens, present participle of reminisci 'remember') + -ly.
