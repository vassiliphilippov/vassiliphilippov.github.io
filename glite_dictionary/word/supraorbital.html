supraorbital

1. ADJ

/ ˌsuːprəˈɔːrbɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˈɔːbɪtəl / (BrE)
Situated or occurring above the eye socket.
located above the eye socket
medical

Examples

The supraorbital ridge is prominent in some primate species.
A supraorbital nerve block is administered to anesthetize the forehead.
Swelling developed in the supraorbital region after the injury.

Etymology

From Latin 'supra' (above) + 'orbita' (orbit, eye socket).
