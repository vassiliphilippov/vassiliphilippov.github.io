Bursa

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɜrsə / (AmE), / ˈbɜːsə / (BrE)
a city in northwestern Turkey, historically significant as the first major capital of the Ottoman Empire and an industrial center today
city in northwestern Turkey

Examples

Bursa is known for its beautiful mosques and historic sites dating back to the Ottoman Empire.
After moving to Bursa, she quickly adapted to the city's vibrant pace and culture.
Tourists often include Bursa in their itinerary for its renowned hot springs and picturesque mountain views.

Etymology

From Turkish Bursa, named after its founder Prusias (Prusa), king of Bithynia.
