approbation

1. NOUN

/ ˌæp.rəˈbeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌæp.rəˈbeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
official approval or formal praise; endorsement by an authority
official approval; formal praise

Examples

The mayor gave her approbation to the new community project.
His plan received approbation from the board of directors after a thorough review.
Winning the award was an act of public approbation for her years of hard work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: approval - the action of officially agreeing to something
  • Antonym: disapprobation - strong disapproval, typically on moral grounds

Etymology

From Latin approbatio (approval), from approbare (to approve).
