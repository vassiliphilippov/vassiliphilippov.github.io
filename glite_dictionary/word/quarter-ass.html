to quarter-ass

1. VERB

/ ˈkwɔɚɾɚˌæs / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtəˌas / (BrE)
to do something with very little effort or in a half-hearted way
do with minimal effort (slang)
slang informal

Examples

You can't just quarter-ass your assignments and expect to get good grades.
He tends to quarter-ass things he doesn't care about.
The team was warned not to quarter-ass the final presentation.

Synset Examples

Don’t quarter-ass your work if you want to succeed.
She was criticized for her quarter-ass effort on the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: half-ass - to do something with only moderate effort or care

Etymology

Formed by analogy with the slang 'half-ass', using 'quarter' to indicate an even lower effort.
