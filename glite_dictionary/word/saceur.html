SACEUR

1. NOUN

/ seɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / seɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
the Supreme Allied Commander Europe; the NATO officer who is the senior military commander in Europe
NATO's senior military commander in Europe
american

Examples

General Wolters currently serves as the SACEUR for NATO.
The SACEUR oversees all NATO military operations in Europe.
Appointment of the new SACEUR was announced at the NATO summit.

Etymology

Acronym from Supreme Allied Commander Europe.
