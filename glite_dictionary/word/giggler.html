giggler

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪɡlər / (AmE), / ˈɡɪɡlə / (BrE)
a person who giggles, especially in a nervous or silly way
someone who giggles

Examples

Whenever she was nervous, the giggler in her would come out and disrupt the meeting.
The teacher asked the giggler in the back to quiet down during the presentation.
He became known as the office giggler because he couldn't stop laughing at small jokes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laugher - a person who laughs
  • Degree Lower: smiler - a person who smiles
