overgreedy

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈɡriːdi / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈɡriːdi / (BrE)
Excessively greedy or gluttonous; wanting far more than is reasonable or fair.
excessively greedy or gluttonous

Examples

The overgreedy businessman cared little for ethics or fairness in his pursuits.
Their overgreedy appetite led them to finish all the food before anyone else could have some.
She warned him not to be overgreedy, reminding him that too much ambition can lead to trouble.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: greedy - having an intense or selfish desire for something, especially wealth or food
  • Synonym: gluttonous - excessively eating or drinking

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'over-' (excessively) + 'greedy'.
