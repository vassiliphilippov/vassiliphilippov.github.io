to hydrogen-bomb

1. VERB

/ ˈhaɪdrədʒən ˌbɑm / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrədʒən ˌbɒm / (BrE)
to attack or destroy with a hydrogen bomb
attack with a hydrogen bomb

Examples

The military threatened to hydrogen-bomb the enemy's capital if negotiations failed.
During war games, the scenario involved one country hydrogen-bombing another as a last resort.
Historians debated whether any nation would ever actually hydrogen-bomb a populated area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nuclear-bomb - to attack or destroy with a nuclear bomb
