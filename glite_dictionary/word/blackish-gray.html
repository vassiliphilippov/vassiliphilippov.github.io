blackish-gray

1. ADJ

/ ˈblækɪʃ ˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈblækɪʃ ˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
Dark gray in color, resembling the shade between black and gray.
a dark shade of gray

Examples

The old building's blackish-gray stones gave it a somber appearance.
She wore a blackish-gray coat that matched the stormy sky.
The artist mixed paints to achieve the perfect blackish-gray shade for the shadows.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gray - a color between black and white
  • Degree Higher: black - the darkest color, absence of light
