essayist

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛseɪˌɪst / (AmE), / ˈɛseɪɪst / (BrE)
a person who writes essays, especially as a literary form
person who writes essays

Examples

Virginia Woolf was not only a novelist but also a distinguished essayist.
The conference invited an acclaimed essayist to discuss the evolution of personal narrative.
Many essayists explore complex themes through short, reflective prose.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: author - a writer of a book, article, or document

Etymology

From French *essayiste*, from *essayer* (to try, to attempt).
