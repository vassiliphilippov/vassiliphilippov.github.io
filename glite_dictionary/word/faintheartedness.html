faintheartedness

1. NOUN

/ ˌfeɪntˈhɑrtɪdnɪs / (AmE), / ˌfeɪntˈhɑːtɪdnɪs / (BrE)
lack of boldness or courage; timidity or fearfulness in facing challenges or danger.
lack of courage or boldness

Examples

Her faintheartedness kept her from protesting the unfair decision.
A leader must show bravery, not faintheartedness, in difficult times.
His faintheartedness made him hesitate before taking any risks.

Synset Examples

faintness of heart and infirmity of purpose

Related Terms

  • Synonym: timidity - lack of courage or confidence
  • Antonym: courage - the ability to do something that frightens one

Etymology

From fainthearted (timid) + -ness (noun-forming suffix).
