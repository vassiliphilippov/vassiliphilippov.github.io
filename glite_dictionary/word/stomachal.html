stomachal

1. ADJ

/ stəˈmæk.əl / (AmE), / stəˈmak.əl / (BrE)
Relating to or involving the stomach.
of or relating to the stomach
medical

Examples

The patient experienced severe stomachal pain after eating a heavy meal.
Research focuses on the stomachal effects of various medications.
Doctors examined the child's stomachal symptoms to diagnose the illness.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

Etymology

From Late Latin stomachalis, from Latin stomachus (stomach).
