low-backed

1. ADJ

/ loʊˈbækt / (AmE), / ləʊˈbækt / (BrE)
having a back that is low in height, especially in reference to chairs or furniture.
having a low back (esp. of furniture)

Examples

He chose a low-backed chair so it would not block the view from the window.
The restaurant features elegant low-backed booths for a more open dining experience.
She preferred low-backed sofas because they made the room feel larger.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: high-backed - having a back that is high in height (especially of furniture)
