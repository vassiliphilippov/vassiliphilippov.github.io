motorbike

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊtərˌbaɪk / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəbaɪk / (BrE)
a small motorcycle with a low frame, small wheels, and elevated handlebars
a small motorcycle

Examples

She learned to ride a motorbike on quiet country roads when she was sixteen.
The gang zipped through the city on their noisy motorbikes.
He repaired his old motorbike in the garage over the weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motorcycle - a two-wheeled motor vehicle

Etymology

From 'motor' + 'bike', short for 'motorcycle bicycle'.

2. VERB

/ ˈmoʊtərˌbaɪk / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.tə.baɪk / (BrE)
to ride a motorcycle
ride a motorcycle

Examples

He loves to motorbike along the coastal roads on weekends.
They motorbiked all the way from Paris to Madrid last summer.
After getting his license, he started motorbiking with his friends every Saturday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ride - to sit on and control the movement of a vehicle or animal
