ramona

1. NOUN

/ rəˈmoʊnə / (AmE), / rəˈməʊnə / (BrE)
A shrubby plant with aromatic greyish-green leaves, used as a cooking herb.
aromatic herbaceous plant

Examples

The chef added fresh ramona leaves to the stew for extra flavor.
Wild ramona grows abundantly in this region and is prized for its aroma.
She dried ramona to use as a seasoning throughout the winter.
