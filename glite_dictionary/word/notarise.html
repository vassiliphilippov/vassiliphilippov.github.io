to notarise

1. VERB

/ ˈnoʊtəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtəraɪz / (BrE)
to officially authenticate a document or signature as a notary public
authenticate as a notary
british

Examples

The lawyer was asked to notarise the power of attorney before it could be submitted.
You must notarise these documents for them to be accepted by the embassy.
She arranged for her landlord to notarise the lease agreement.

Synset Examples

We had to have the signature notarized

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to notarize - authenticate as a notary (American English spelling)

Etymology

From notary + -ise, patterned after French 'notariser' and American English 'notarize'.
