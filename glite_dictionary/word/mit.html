MIT

1. PROPN

/ ɛm aɪ ti / (AmE), / ɛm aɪ ti / (BrE)
an abbreviation for the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, a prestigious university in Cambridge, Massachusetts, specializing in science and engineering
prestigious US university, acronym
american

Examples

She was thrilled to be accepted into MIT for her graduate studies.
Many tech company founders are graduates of MIT.
The robotics team from MIT won first prize at the international competition.

Etymology

Acronym formed from the initial letters of Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
