lustfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌst.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈlʌst.fəl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having strong sexual desire
strong sexual desire

Examples

His lustfulness was evident in the way he spoke and acted around others.
The novel explores the dangers of unchecked lustfulness and temptation.
He struggled to control his lustfulness even after making several promises to his partner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: libidinousness - the quality of having or showing excessive sexual desire
  • Antonym: chastity - the practice or state of refraining from sexual activity

Etymology

From 'lustful' + '-ness'; 'lustful' from Old English 'lust' (desire, pleasure).
