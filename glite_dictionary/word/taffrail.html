taffrail

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæfˌreɪl / (AmE), / ˈtæf.reɪl / (BrE)
the rail or railing around the stern (rear part) of a ship or boat
railing at the stern of a ship

Examples

The captain stood by the taffrail, gazing out over the calm sea.
She leaned against the taffrail to watch the dolphins swimming behind the ship.
Salt spray coated the wooden taffrail after the stormy night.

Etymology

From Dutch 'tafereel' (panel, picture), later used for the ornamented rail at the stern of a ship.
