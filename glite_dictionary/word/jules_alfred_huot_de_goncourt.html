Jules Alfred Huot de Goncourt

1. PROPN

/ ʒuːl alˈfʁɛd ɥo də ɡɔ̃kuʁ / (AmE), / ʒuːl ˈælfrɛd ɥɔː də ˈɡɒnkʊər / (BrE)
(1830–1870) French writer known for collaborating with his brother Edmond de Goncourt on influential literary works.
French writer; collaborated with Edmond de Goncourt

Jules Alfred Huot de Goncourt played a major role in the literary scene of nineteenth-century France.
