Jan

1. NOUN

/ dʒæn / (AmE), / dʒan / (BrE)
Abbreviation for January, the first month of the year in the Gregorian calendar.

american british

Examples

Our next meeting is scheduled for Jan 15th.

Related Terms

Etymology

Abbreviation of January, from Latin 'Ianuarius', named after the Roman god Janus.
