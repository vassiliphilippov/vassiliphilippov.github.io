individualized

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzd / (BrE)
made or tailored for a particular individual; adapted to suit the needs or preferences of one person
made or tailored for an individual

Examples

The school offers individualized instruction to meet each student's unique needs.
He received a highly individualized treatment plan from his doctor.
Her individualized training program helped her achieve better results than a generic routine.

Synset Examples

personalized luggage
personalized advice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personalized - made or adapted to suit a particular person

Etymology

From 'individual' + '-ize' + '-ed', indicating the action of making something suited to an individual.
