pertinaciously

1. ADV

/ ˌpɝː.tɪˈneɪ.ʃəs.li / (AmE), / ˌpɜː.tɪˈneɪ.ʃəs.li / (BrE)
in a determined and stubborn manner; with persistent effort
with determination or persistence

Examples

She held to her beliefs pertinaciously, refusing to let criticism sway her.
The scientist pertinaciously pursued the answer to the complex problem.
He pertinaciously worked on the project, overcoming every obstacle.

Synset Examples

he struggled pertinaciously for the new resolution

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doggedly - in a stubbornly persistent way
  • Synonym: tenaciously - in a way that shows strong determination

Etymology

From Latin 'pertinax' (holding fast, tenacious) + English adverbial suffix '-ly'.
