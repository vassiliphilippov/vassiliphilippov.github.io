patterned

1. ADJ

/ ˈpætɚnd / (AmE), / ˈpætənd / (BrE)
Having a decorative or distinctive design formed by repeated lines, shapes, or colors.
decorated with repeated designs or patterns

Examples

She chose a patterned dress for the party, covered with small blue flowers.
The wallpaper in the hallway is brightly patterned with geometric shapes.
He prefers plain shirts over patterned ones for work.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: plain - not decorated with a pattern or design

Etymology

From 'pattern' + '-ed', indicating 'having a pattern'.
