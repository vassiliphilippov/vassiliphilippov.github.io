favor

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪvər / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvə / (BrE)
an act of kindness or support, often done to help someone without expecting anything in return
an act of kindness or support

Examples

She did me a huge favor by watching my kids last night.
Could you do me a favor and lend me your pen?
He returned the favor by helping her with her project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: service - an act of helpful activity

Etymology

From Old French 'faveur', from Latin 'favorem' (good will, kindness).
