Jimmy Stewart

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɪmi ˈstuːərt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪmi ˈstjuːət / (BrE)
(1908–1997) An American film actor known for portraying honest and modest heroes in classic movies such as 'It's a Wonderful Life' and 'Rear Window'.
famous American film actor (1908–1997)

Examples

Jimmy Stewart starred in many classic Hollywood films during his long career.
Many people admire Jimmy Stewart for his portrayal of honest and humble characters.
The timeless appeal of Jimmy Stewart continues to influence actors today.
