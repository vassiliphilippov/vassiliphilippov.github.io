haired

1. ADJ

/ hɛrd / (AmE), / heəd / (BrE)
having hair of a particular kind or in a particular amount, especially used in combination
having (a type of) hair; often used in combination

Examples

The museum displayed a lock of red-haired Queen Elizabeth I's hair.
She admired her dark-haired friend from across the room.
Some breeds of dog are short-haired, while others are long-haired.

Synset Examples

Jacob was a hairy man
a hairy caterpillar

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairy - covered with hair
