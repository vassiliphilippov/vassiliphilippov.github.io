.38-calibre

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɜrtiˈeɪt ˈkælɪbɚ / (AmE), / ˌθɜːtiˈeɪt ˈkælɪbə / (BrE)
Relating to the bore of a firearm or its ammunition measuring 0.38 inches in diameter.
relating to a 0.38 inch gun bore

Examples

The detective found a .38-calibre revolver hidden in the drawer.
Police recovered several .38-calibre bullets at the crime scene.
He preferred to carry a .38-calibre handgun for personal protection.

Synset Examples

a .38 caliber shell

Etymology

From the measurement .38 (thirty-eight hundredths of an inch) and 'calibre,' from French calibre.
