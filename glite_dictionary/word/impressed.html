impressed

1. ADJ

/ ɪmˈprɛst / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛst / (BrE)
feeling admiration or respect for someone or something, or being deeply affected by something's quality or skill
affected or influenced; admiring

Examples

She was impressed by the artist's unique technique and creativity.
I was really impressed with how smoothly the event was organized.
His dedication to helping others left everyone impressed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unimpressed - not feeling admiration or being unaffected
  • Synonym: inspired - filled with the urge or ability to do or feel something creative or exceptional

Etymology

Past participle of 'impress', from Latin 'imprimere' (to press into, imprint).
