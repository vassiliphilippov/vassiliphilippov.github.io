hotelman

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈtɛlˌmæn / (AmE), / həʊˈtɛlˌman / (BrE)
a man who owns or manages a hotel
male hotel owner or manager

Examples

The hotelman greeted every guest with individual attention and warmth.
After years of experience, he became a respected hotelman in the industry.
Many people admired the hotelman for turning the historic building into a thriving inn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hotelier - a person who owns or manages a hotel
