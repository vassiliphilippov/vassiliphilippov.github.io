Latvianness

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæt.vi.ə.nəs / (AmE), / ˈlat.vɪ.ə.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being Latvian; the distinctive characteristics, culture, or identity associated with Latvia or its people
the quality or identity of being Latvian

Examples

The poet's work explores themes of Latvianness and national identity.
She takes great pride in her Latvianness, celebrating traditional customs and language.
Debates about Latvianness often arise within the Latvian diaspora community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Latvian - relating to Latvia or its people

Etymology

From Latvian + -ness, a suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality.
