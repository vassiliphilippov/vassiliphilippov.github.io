nonchristian

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˈkrɪstʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈkrɪstʃən / (BrE)
not believing in or adhering to the Christian faith or doctrine.
not believing in Christ or Christianity

Examples

Many ancient traditions have nonchristian origins that predate Christianity.
The festival attracted both nonchristian and Christian visitors from around the world.
Some schools celebrate holidays from nonchristian cultures to promote diversity.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Christian - a person who follows or believes in Jesus Christ and his teachings

Etymology

From prefix 'non-' (not) + 'Christian' (follower of Christ).
