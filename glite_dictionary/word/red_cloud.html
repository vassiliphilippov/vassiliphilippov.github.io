Red Cloud

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɛd klaʊd / (AmE), / ˈrɛd klaʊd / (BrE)
(1822–1909) A chief of the Oglala Lakota Sioux who led resistance against the United States government's expansion into Native American territory in Wyoming and Montana.
Oglala Lakota chief and leader (1822–1909)

Examples

Red Cloud was a prominent chief who played a crucial role in Native American resistance.
The battle led by Red Cloud is often cited as a turning point in U.S.–Lakota relations.
Many historians regard Red Cloud as one of the most influential indigenous leaders of the 19th century.
