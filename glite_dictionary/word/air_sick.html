air sick

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛr ˌsɪk / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌsɪk / (BrE)
feeling sick or nauseous as a result of flying in an aircraft.
feeling nauseous from flying in an aircraft

Examples

She became air sick during the turbulent flight and needed a paper bag.
If you tend to get air sick, it's best to choose a seat near the wings.
Motion tablets help many passengers avoid feeling air sick on long journeys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airsickness - nausea and discomfort caused by flying in an aircraft
  • Synonym: seasick - feeling sick due to motion of a boat or ship
