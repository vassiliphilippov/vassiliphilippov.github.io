here

1. ADV

/ hɪr / (AmE), / hɪə / (BrE)
At this time; now.
at this time; now

Examples

We need to stop here and review our progress before continuing.
Let's pause here and continue after the break.
The meeting will adjourn here until tomorrow morning.

Synset Examples

we'll adjourn here for lunch and discuss the remaining issues this afternoon

Etymology

From Old English 'hēr' meaning 'in this place, at this time'.
