Xi'an

1. PROPN

/ ʃiˈɑn / (AmE), / ʃiˈæn / (BrE)
A city in central China; historically the capital of several ancient Chinese dynasties, notably during 221–206 BC.
city in central China

Examples

The Terracotta Army was discovered near Xi'an in the 1970s.
Many tourists visit Xi'an to explore its ancient city walls and rich history.
The flight from Beijing to Xi'an takes about two hours.
