Xian

1. PROPN

/ ʃjæn / (AmE), / ʃiˈæn / (BrE)
a city in central China, known as the capital of several ancient Chinese dynasties including during 221–206 BC
city in central China; ancient capital

Examples

The Terracotta Army was discovered near Xian in central China.
Many tourists visit Xian to see its ancient city wall and historic sites.
Xian was once the starting point of the Silk Road, connecting China to the West.
