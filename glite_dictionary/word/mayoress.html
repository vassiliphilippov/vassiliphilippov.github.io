mayoress

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪ.oʊˌrɛs / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.əʊ.rɛs / (BrE)
A woman who holds the office of mayor.
female mayor

Examples

She became the first mayoress of the town after winning the election.
The mayoress delivered a powerful speech at the city council meeting.
Many residents came to see the new mayoress at the community event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mayor - an elected head of a city, town, or municipality

Etymology

Formed from 'mayor' + '-ess', a suffix denoting a female person.

2. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪ.oʊˌrɛs / (AmE), / ˈmeɪərɪs / (BrE)
The wife of a mayor, or in some contexts, a woman who holds the office of mayor.
wife of a mayor (or female mayor)
british

Examples

The mayoress attended the ceremony alongside the mayor.
As mayoress, she supported many local charities in the community.
The mayoress delivered a speech at the town hall dinner last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mayor - the elected head of a town or city

Etymology

Formed by adding the feminine suffix -ess to 'mayor'.
