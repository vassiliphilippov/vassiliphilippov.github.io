enquirer

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈkwaɪərər / (AmE), / ɪnˈkwaɪərə / (BrE)
a person who asks for information or poses questions
person who asks questions

Examples

If you have any questions, please speak to the enquirer at the front desk.
The press conference was filled with eager enquirers looking for more details.
As an enquirer, she always seeks out the facts before forming an opinion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inquirer - a person who inquires or asks questions

Etymology

From Middle English 'enqueren,' from Old French 'enquerre,' from Latin 'inquirere' (to seek after).
