goatherd

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊthɜrd / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊthɜːd / (BrE)
a person whose job is to take care of goats
person who looks after goats

Examples

The goatherd led his flock up the steep hillside every morning.
As a young goatherd, she learned the value of patience and vigilance.
Local legend says a goatherd once discovered the village's famous caves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shepherd - a person who tends and rears sheep
  • Synonym: cowherd - a person who tends and herds cows

Etymology

Formed from 'goat' + 'herd', where 'herd' means a person who tends animals.
