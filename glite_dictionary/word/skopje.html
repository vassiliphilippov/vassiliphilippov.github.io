Skopje

1. PROPN

/ ˈskoʊpjeɪ / (AmE), / ˈskɒpjeɪ / (BrE)
the capital city of North Macedonia, situated in the north of the country
capital of North Macedonia

Examples

The travelers spent a week exploring the vibrant markets and historic sites in Skopje.
After landing at the international airport, we took a taxi directly to our hotel in Skopje.
Many embassies and government offices are located in Skopje, making it the political center of the country.

Etymology

From Macedonian Скопје (Skopje), ultimately from Latin Scupi, the ancient name of the city.
