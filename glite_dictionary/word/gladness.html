gladness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlædnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡlædnəs / (BrE)
A feeling of joy, pleasure, or happiness.
joy; pleasure; happiness

Examples

The news of his recovery filled her with gladness.
There was a sense of gladness in the room after the team's victory.
He could not hide his gladness when he saw his friends again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joy - a feeling of great pleasure and happiness
  • Antonym: sadness - the condition or quality of being sad

Etymology

From Old English 'glædnes', from 'glæd' (glad, bright) + '-ness' (state, condition).
