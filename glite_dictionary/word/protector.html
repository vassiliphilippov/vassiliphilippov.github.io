protector

1. NOUN

/ prəˈtɛktər / (AmE), / prəˈtɛktə / (BrE)
a person who defends or looks after someone or something; a guardian or defender.
person who defends or looks after others

Examples

As a child, she always saw her older brother as her protector.
Wildlife rangers act as protectors of endangered animals in the national park.
He took pride in his role as a protector of his family's legacy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guardian - someone who protects or takes care of another
  • Synonym: defender - someone who protects against harm or danger

Etymology

From Latin 'protector' (one who protects), from 'protegere' (to cover in front).
