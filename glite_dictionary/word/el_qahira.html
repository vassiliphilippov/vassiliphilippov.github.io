El Qahira

1. PROPN

/ ɛl kɑˈhiːrə / (AmE), / ɛl kɑːˈhiːrə / (BrE)
the Arabic name for Cairo, the capital of Egypt and the largest city in Africa
Arabic name for Cairo, Egypt

Examples

She dreamed of visiting El Qahira to see the ancient pyramids and bustling markets.
The flight from London landed in El Qahira early in the morning.
Many Egyptians refer to their capital as El Qahira rather than Cairo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cairo - the capital of Egypt and largest city in Africa

Etymology

From Arabic القاهرة (al-Qāhira), meaning 'the victorious'.
