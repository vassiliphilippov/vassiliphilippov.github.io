Liederkranz

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːdərˌkrænz / (AmE), / ˈliːdəˌkrants / (BrE)
a soft American cheese with a strong odor and flavor, similar to Limburger
soft, strong-flavored American cheese

Examples

The deli carries Liederkranz along with several other specialty cheeses.
Her father enjoyed slices of Liederkranz on rye bread as a late-night snack.
Many people compare the aroma of Liederkranz to that of Limburger cheese.

Etymology

From German 'Liederkranz' meaning 'wreath of songs,' originally the name of a singing society; later used as a brand name for the cheese.
