maturational

1. ADJ

/ ˌmætʃəˈreɪʃənl / (AmE), / ˌmatjʊˈreɪʃənl / (BrE)
relating to or involved in the process of maturation; pertaining to growth or development to maturity
relating to maturation or developing maturity

Examples

Adolescent behavior is influenced by a variety of maturational factors.
Delayed maturational changes can impact a child's development.
The study focused on the brain's maturational trajectory during early childhood.

Synset Examples

maturational process

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maturation - the process of becoming mature or fully developed

Etymology

From maturation + -al; 'maturation' from Latin 'maturatio', meaning ripening or maturing.
