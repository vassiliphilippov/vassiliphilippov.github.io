sulphuric

1. ADJ

/ səlˈfjʊrɪk / (AmE), / sʌlˈfjʊərɪk / (BrE)
of, relating to, or containing sulfur, especially as a constituent in chemical compounds.
containing or relating to sulfur

Examples

The laboratory technician handled the sulphuric acid with extreme care.
Factories must prevent the release of sulphuric fumes into the environment.
The reaction produced a strong sulphuric smell in the room.

Synset Examples

sulphuric esters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sulfuric - alternate (US) spelling of sulphuric

Etymology

From Latin 'sulphur' (sulfur), with the suffix '-ic' indicating an adjective.
