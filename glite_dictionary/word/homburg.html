homburg

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑm.bɝɡ / (AmE), / ˈhɒm.bɜːɡ / (BrE)
a man's formal hat made of felt, with a narrow brim and a creased crown
man's formal felt hat with a creased crown

Examples

He wore a classic homburg to the formal dinner.
The homburg became popular in the early twentieth century.
My grandfather always matched his suit with a gray homburg.

Etymology

Named after Bad Homburg, a town in Germany where the style originated.
