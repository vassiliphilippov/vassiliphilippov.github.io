equiangular

1. ADJ

/ ˌiːkwɪˈæŋɡjələr / (AmE), / ˌiːkwɪˈæŋɡjʊlə / (BrE)
(of a shape or figure) having all angles equal in measure.
having all angles equal

Examples

A regular hexagon is an equiangular polygon.
The architect designed an equiangular triangle for the building's window.
Not all rectangles are equiangular unless all sides are also the same length.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equilateral - having all sides equal (especially in polygons)

Etymology

From Latin 'aequi-' (equal) + 'angulus' (angle).
