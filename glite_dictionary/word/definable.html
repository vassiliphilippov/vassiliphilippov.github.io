definable

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈfaɪnəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈfaɪnəbl / (BrE)
able to be defined, limited, or explained clearly.
able to be defined or explained

Examples

The contract must use definable terms to avoid confusion.
Certain emotions are not easily definable in words.
She set definable rules for the project to ensure clarity.

Synset Examples

definable terms
definable rules

Related Terms

  • Antonym: indefinable - impossible to define or describe
  • Synonym: describable - able to be described or explained

Etymology

From 'define' + suffix '-able', indicating capability.
