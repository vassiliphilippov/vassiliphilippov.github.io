Lolita

1. NOUN

/ loʊˈliːtə / (AmE), / ləʊˈliːtə / (BrE)
a sexually precocious young girl, especially one viewed as attractive to older men
sexually precocious young girl

Examples

The media accused the singer of acting like a Lolita to attract attention.
She was troubled by rumors that her daughter was being seen as a Lolita at school.
The term Lolita has sparked debates about the sexualization of young girls in popular culture.

Etymology

From the title character of Vladimir Nabokov's 1955 novel 'Lolita'.
