Port Louis

1. PROPN

/ pɔrt ˈluːɪs / (AmE), / pɔːt ˈluːi / (BrE)
The capital and chief port of Mauritius, located on the northwestern coast of the island.
capital city of Mauritius

Examples

The government buildings in Port Louis are a blend of colonial and modern architecture.
Tourists often visit the bustling markets of Port Louis for souvenirs and local foods.
After arriving in Mauritius, we took a short drive to Port Louis to explore the city.

Etymology

French origin: 'Port Louis' named in honor of King Louis XV of France.
