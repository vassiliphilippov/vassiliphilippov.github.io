Bidens trichosperma

1. NOUN

/ baɪˈdɛnz trɪkoʊˈspɜrmə / (AmE), / baɪˈdɛnz trɪkəʊˈspɜːmə / (BrE)
A North American species of bur marigold notable for its large yellow flowers.
species of North American bur marigold

Examples

Bidens trichosperma can be found in wetlands across the northeastern United States.
The large yellow flowers of Bidens trichosperma attract a variety of pollinators.
Gardeners sometimes plant Bidens trichosperma to enhance biodiversity in native gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bur marigold - common name for several species in the genus Bidens

Etymology

From Latin; 'Bidens' meaning 'two-toothed' (referring to the seeds), 'trichosperma' from Greek 'tricho-' (hair) and 'sperma' (seed), referring to the hairy seeds.
