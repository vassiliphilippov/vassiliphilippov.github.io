Serzone

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɜrˌzoʊn / (AmE), / ˈsɜːzəʊn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for an antidepressant drug containing nefazodone, used in the treatment of depression.
brand name of an antidepressant drug
medical

Examples

After several months on Serzone, she noticed a significant improvement in her mood.
The doctor prescribed Serzone as part of his treatment for major depressive disorder.
Because of possible side effects, patients taking Serzone require careful monitoring.
