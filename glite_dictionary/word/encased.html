encased

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈkeɪst / (AmE), / ɪnˈkeɪst / (BrE)
covered or protected with or as if with a case
covered or enclosed in a case

Examples

The ancient mummy was encased in layers of linen and resin.
Her phone is encased in a sturdy waterproof shell to protect it from drops.
The chocolates were encased in elegant gold wrappers for the special occasion.

Synset Examples

knights cased in steel
products encased in leatherette

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enclosed - surrounded or closed off on all sides
  • Antonym: exposed - not protected or covered; open to view
