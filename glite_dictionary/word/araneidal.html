araneidal

1. ADJ

/ ˌærəˈnaɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌærəˈnaɪdəl / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling a spider.
relating to or like a spider

Examples

The biologist noted the araneidal web structure spun across the branches.
Her costume had an araneidal design, resembling a spider closely.
Some flowers develop araneidal markings to deter herbivores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arachnid - any member of the class Arachnida, including spiders, scorpions, and mites

Etymology

From Latin 'aranea' (spider) + -idal (forming adjectives).
