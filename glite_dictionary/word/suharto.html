Suharto

1. PROPN

/ suːˈhɑːrtoʊ / (AmE), / suːˈhɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
(1921–2008) Indonesian military leader and politician who served as President of Indonesia from 1967 to 1998, seizing power from Sukarno.
President of Indonesia (1967–1998)

Examples

Suharto ruled Indonesia for more than three decades, shaping the country's modern history.
The resignation of Suharto in 1998 marked the end of an authoritarian era in Indonesia.
Many Indonesians have differing opinions on the legacy of Suharto's leadership.
