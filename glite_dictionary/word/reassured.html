reassured

1. ADJ

/ ˌriːəˈʃʊrd / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈʃɔːd / (BrE)
Feeling confident again because someone or something has removed your doubts or fears.
having confidence restored; no longer anxious

Examples

She looked reassured after hearing the good news from her doctor.
His calm explanation left everyone feeling reassured about the situation.
The teacher's supportive smile made the nervous student feel reassured.

Synset Examples

reassured by her praise he pressed on

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confident - feeling sure about your ability or success
  • Antonym: anxious - worried or concerned about something

Etymology

Past participle of 'reassure', from French 'rassurer', from Latin 're-' + 'assicurare' (to assure).
