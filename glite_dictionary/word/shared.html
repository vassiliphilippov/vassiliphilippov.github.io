shared

1. ADJ

/ ʃɛrd / (AmE), / ʃeəd / (BrE)
held or experienced by two or more people in common; belonging to or used by more than one person or group
held or experienced in common

Examples

They rented a shared apartment in the city center.
Their shared passion for jazz brought them together as friends.
The report included the shared opinions of all team members.

Synset Examples

two shared valence electrons forming a bond between adjacent nuclei
a shared interest in philately

Related Terms

  • Synonym: common - belonging to or shared by two or more people
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02159631-a
Synset POS: a

2. ADJ

/ ʃɛrd / (AmE), / ʃeəd / (BrE)
(of something) distributed in portions among people, often equally, according to a plan or purpose.
distributed among people

Examples

The roommates split the shared expenses equally to avoid disagreements.
They enjoyed their shared responsibilities, knowing everyone contributed to the outcome.
Access to the shared workspace allowed the team to collaborate efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: divided - split into parts or portions among people
  • Antonym: individual - relating to a single person or thing, not shared
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00543409-s
Synset POS: s
