uke

1. NOUN

/ juːk / (AmE), / juːk / (BrE)
An informal term for a ukulele, a small guitar-like musical instrument with four strings.
informal term for a ukulele
informal

Examples

He played a cheerful tune on his uke at the party.
My sister carries her uke everywhere she goes, just in case there's a chance to play.
Learning the uke is easier than picking up a regular guitar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ukulele - a small four-stringed guitar-like instrument associated with Hawaiian music

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'ukulele,' originating from Hawaiian.
