Aucuba

1. NOUN

/ ɑːˈkuːbə / (AmE), / ɔːˈkuːbə / (BrE)
A genus of hardy evergreen shrubs and small trees native to eastern Asia, especially Japan, often grown as ornamentals.
genus of evergreen shrubs from Japan

Examples

The Aucuba in our garden thrives even in deep shade.
Many landscapers prefer Aucuba for its attractive spotted leaves.
I bought an Aucuba to add some evergreen color to the backyard.

Etymology

From Japanese aokiba, meaning 'green leaves'.
