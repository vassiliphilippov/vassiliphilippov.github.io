Chuck Berry

1. PROPN

/ tʃʌk ˈbɛri / (AmE), / tʃʌk ˈberi / (BrE)
(1926–2017) An American singer, songwriter, and guitarist, widely regarded as one of the pioneers of rock and roll music.
pioneering American rock and roll musician

Examples

Chuck Berry is often called one of the founding fathers of rock and roll.
The energetic guitar riffs of Chuck Berry influenced generations of musicians.
Many people recognize songs like "Johnny B. Goode" as classic works by Chuck Berry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Elvis Presley - Famous American rock and roll singer
  • Synonym: Little Richard - Influential American rock and roll musician
