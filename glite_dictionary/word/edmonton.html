Edmonton

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛd.mən.tən / (AmE), / ˈɛd.mən.tən / (BrE)
the capital city of the province of Alberta, Canada, situated on the North Saskatchewan River.
capital city of Alberta, Canada

Examples

Edmonton experiences cold winters and warm summers due to its northern location.
Many major festivals are held annually in Edmonton, attracting tourists from around the world.
The Oilers are a professional ice hockey team based in Edmonton.

Etymology

Named after Edmonton, a district in north London, England.
