leathered

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛðərd / (AmE), / ˈlɛðəd / (BrE)
having a surface or texture resembling leather; tough but flexible.
tough, leather-like in appearance or feel

Examples

Her leathered hands told the story of years spent working outdoors.
The old chair had become leathered and cracked after decades of use.
He admired the leathered finish on the vintage book's cover.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leathery - having a tough, leather-like texture
