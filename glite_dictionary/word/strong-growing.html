strong-growing

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrɔŋ ˌɡroʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstrɒŋ ˌɡrəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
growing or spreading quickly or vigorously, especially referring to plants or tumors.
spreading or growing quickly

Examples

This strong-growing variety of ivy covered the wall in just one summer.
The doctor was concerned about a strong-growing tumor that had doubled in size within a month.
We selected only strong-growing seedlings for the new plantation.

Synset Examples

an aggressive tumor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vigorous - growing or spreading with force and energy
  • Antonym: slow-growing - growing or spreading at a slow rate
