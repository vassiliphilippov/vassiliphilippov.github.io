strewing

1. NOUN

/ ˈstruɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstruːɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of scattering or spreading things carelessly or over a surface
the act of scattering

Examples

The strewing of flower petals along the aisle created a beautiful pathway for the bride.
Her morning routine included the strewing of birdseed across the lawn for the sparrows.
During the festival, the strewing of herbs on the floor was believed to bring good luck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scattering - the act of throwing or spreading things over an area

Etymology

From the verb 'strew', from Old English 'streowian' (to scatter).
