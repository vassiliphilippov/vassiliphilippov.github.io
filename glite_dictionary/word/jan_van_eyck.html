Jan van Eyck

1. PROPN

/ jɑn væn ˈeɪk / (AmE), / jæn væn ˈaɪk / (BrE)
(c. 1390–1441) A Flemish painter regarded as one of the founders of the Flemish school of painting, renowned for pioneering modern techniques of oil painting.
Flemish pioneer of oil painting (c. 1390–1441)

Examples

The works of Jan van Eyck are celebrated for their intricate detail and brilliant use of color.
Many art historians regard Jan van Eyck as a master innovator in oil painting techniques.
A trip to Belgium would not be complete without viewing paintings by Jan van Eyck.
