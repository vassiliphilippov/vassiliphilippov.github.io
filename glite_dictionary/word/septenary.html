septenary

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛp.təˌnɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈsɛptɪnəri / (BrE)
the number seven; a group or set of seven.
the number seven; a set of seven

Examples

The ancient text describes the creation of the world in a septenary of days.
He divided the students into groups of septenary for the project.
In musical theory, a scale can sometimes be organized as a septenary of notes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seven - the cardinal number after six

Etymology

From Latin septenarius, from septem (seven).
