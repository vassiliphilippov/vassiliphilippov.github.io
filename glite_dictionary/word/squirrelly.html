squirrelly

1. ADJ

/ ˈskwɝːrli / (AmE), / ˈskwɪəli / (BrE)
(informal) feeling restless, nervous, or jittery; behaving in a strange or unpredictable way.
restless or jittery (informal)
informal

Examples

After drinking three cups of coffee, I squirrelly and couldn't focus on my work.
The children became squirrelly during the long car ride and wouldn't sit still.
I always get a little squirrelly before giving a big presentation.

Synset Examples

I felt squirrelly after too much coffee this morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restless - unable to relax or stay still
  • Antonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness, anger, or other emotions

Etymology

From 'squirrel', referencing the animal's quick, unpredictable movements, suffixed with '-ly'.
