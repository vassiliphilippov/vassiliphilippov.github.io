Marxist

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrksɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɑːksɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or believes in the political and economic theories of Karl Marx, especially advocating for the overthrow of capitalist systems and the establishment of a classless society
supporter of Marxist political theory

Examples

Many revolutionaries in the early 20th century identified themselves as Marxists seeking radical change.
The group accused the government of suppressing Marxist ideology in their publications.
As a Marxist, she advocated for the abolition of private property and greater social equality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communist - a person who supports or believes in the principles of communism
  • Antonym: capitalist - a person who supports or believes in capitalism

Etymology

From the name of Karl Marx (1818–1883), German philosopher and political theorist, + -ist.

2. ADJ

/ ˈmɑːrksɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɑːksɪst / (BrE)
of, relating to, or supporting the political and economic theories of Karl Marx and Friedrich Engels, especially regarding class struggle and socialism.
relating to the theories of Marx and Engels

Examples

Many Marxist thinkers have contributed to social theory in the 20th century.
The group organized a Marxist study circle for discussing political philosophy.
Her analysis reflects a distinct Marxist perspective on history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communist - relating to or supporting communism
  • Antonym: capitalist - supporting or relating to capitalism

Etymology

From the name of Karl Marx (1818–1883), German philosopher and social theorist, combined with the suffix -ist.

3. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːrksɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɑːksɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or believes in the political and economic theories of Karl Marx
supporter of Marxism

Examples

She has always considered herself a proud Marxist and supports workers' rights.
The professor's views were influenced by Marxist theory and socialist ideas.
During the debate, he was labeled a Marxist because of his economic stance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: socialist - a person who advocates socialism
  • Antonym: capitalist - a person who supports capitalism, opposite ideology

Etymology

From the name Karl Marx (1818–1883), German political philosopher, with the suffix -ist.
