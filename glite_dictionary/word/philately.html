philately

1. NOUN

/ fɪˈlætəli / (AmE), / fɪˈlætəli / (BrE)
the collection and study of postage stamps and related items
collection and study of postage stamps

Examples

His lifelong interest in philately led him to amass an impressive stamp collection.
The museum hosted a special exhibition on the history of philately last summer.
Many people take up philately as a relaxing and educational hobby.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stamp collecting - the hobby of collecting postage stamps

Etymology

From French 'philatélie', coined in the 19th century from Greek 'philos' (loving) + 'ateleia' (exemption from tax).
