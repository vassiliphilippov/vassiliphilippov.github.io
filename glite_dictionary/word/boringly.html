boringly

1. ADV

/ ˈbɔr.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈbɔː.rɪŋ.li / (BrE)
in a manner that is tedious or uninteresting; causing boredom.
in a tedious or uninteresting way

Examples

The lecture was delivered so boringly that several students fell asleep.
She explained the instructions boringly, making it hard to stay focused.
The movie progressed boringly until the last ten minutes.

Synset Examples

boringly slow work
he plodded tediously forward

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tediously - in a boring and monotonous manner
