consommé

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑn səˈmeɪ / (AmE), / ˈkɒn sə meɪ / (BrE)
A clear soup made from richly flavoured stock, usually of beef, veal, or chicken, that has been clarified.
clear clarified soup

Examples

The chef served a delicate consommé to start the meal.
She sipped her bowl of chicken consommé slowly, enjoying its clarity and aroma.
A good consommé requires careful preparation to achieve its crystal-clear appearance.

Etymology

From French 'consommé' (consumed, perfected), past participle of 'consommer'.
