stairwell

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɛrˌwɛl / (AmE), / ˈsteəˌwɛl / (BrE)
A space in a building consisting of a vertical shaft or enclosure around a staircase.
vertical space around a staircase

Examples

Smoke was coming up through the stairwell from the basement.
The emergency exit is located in the far stairwell at the end of the hall.
They decorated the stairwell with framed photographs and plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: staircase - a set of stairs and its surrounding structure
