beau

1. NOUN

/ boʊ / (AmE), / bəʊ / (BrE)
a man who is the romantic partner or lover of someone, especially a woman's boyfriend
male romantic partner; boyfriend

Examples

She introduced her new beau to her friends at the party.
Rumor had it that the movie star's beau was a famous musician.
In the old photographs, my grandmother is pictured with her youthful beau.

Synset Examples

if I'd known he was her boyfriend, I wouldn't have asked
When the law changed, Pet could finally marry his long-time boyfriend Jim

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boyfriend - a man with whom someone is having a romantic or sexual relationship
  • Antonym: girlfriend - a woman with whom someone is having a romantic or sexual relationship

Etymology

From French 'beau' meaning 'beautiful (man), suitor, admirer'.

2. NOUN

/ boʊ / (AmE), / bəʊ / (BrE)
a man who is very concerned with his dress and appearance; a fashionable or dandyish man
fashionable man; a dandy

Examples

He was a real beau, always dressed in the latest fashions.
The beau admired his reflection before heading out for the evening.
A group of beaux gathered at the ball, each competing for attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dandy - a man who is unduly concerned with looking stylish

Etymology

From French 'beau' (handsome), from Latin 'bellus' (pretty, handsome).
