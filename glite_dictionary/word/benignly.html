benignly

1. ADV

/ bəˈnaɪnli / (AmE), / bɪˈnaɪnli / (BrE)
In a kind, gentle, or harmless manner; with gentleness or benevolence.
in a kind or gentle manner

Examples

She smiled benignly at the children playing in the park.
The teacher spoke benignly to calm the worried students.
The critic benignly overlooked the minor flaws in the performance.

Synset Examples

this drug is benignly soporific

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kindly - in a kind or benevolent manner
  • Antonym: malignly - in a harmful or evil manner

Etymology

From 'benign' + '-ly', ultimately from Latin 'benignus' (kind, good).
