to discussing

1. VERB

/ dɪˈskʌsɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈskʌsɪŋ / (BrE)
talking about something with someone, especially in order to exchange ideas or opinions
talking about a topic with others

Examples

She enjoys discussing books over coffee with her friends.
We were discussing the plan when the phone rang.
Many avoid discussing controversial topics at family gatherings.

Synset Examples

He avoided discussing politics for fear of arguments.
He loves discussing science with his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: debating - arguing about something in a formal way
  • Antonym: ignoring - deliberately not talking about something
