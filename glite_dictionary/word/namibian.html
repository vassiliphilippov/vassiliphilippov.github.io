Namibian

1. NOUN

/ nəˈmɪbiən / (AmE), / nəˈmɪbiən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Namibia, a country in southern Africa.
person from Namibia

Examples

Many Namibians celebrate their Independence Day with music and dance.
The museum exhibited art from several talented Namibians.
He met a group of Namibians during his travels in southern Africa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Namibian - of or relating to Namibia (adjective form)

Etymology

From Namibia, the name of the country, plus the English suffix -ian.
