puffiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌfinəs / (AmE), / ˈpʌfinəs / (BrE)
a swelling or abnormal enlargement of a part of the body, often caused by fluid retention or irritation
abnormal swelling or enlargement

Examples

The puffiness around his eyes made him look tired and unwell.
She noticed some puffiness in her ankles after the long flight.
The cream promises to reduce puffiness and dark circles under the eyes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swelling - an abnormal enlargement of a part of the body, typically as a result of an injury or illness
  • Antonym: flatness - the quality of being flat, lacking in raised surfaces or swelling

Etymology

From 'puffy' + '-ness', with 'puffy' deriving from 'puff' (to swell) in Middle English.
