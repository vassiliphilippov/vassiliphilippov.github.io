cabassous

1. NOUN

/ kəˈbæs.uːs / (AmE), / kəˈbæs.uːs / (BrE)
A genus of naked-tailed armadillos found in tropical South America.
a genus of naked-tailed armadillos

Examples

The cabassous is known for its distinctive bare tail, unlike other armadillos.
Scientists recently discovered a new species belonging to the genus cabassous.
The cabassous inhabits forests and grasslands across South America.
