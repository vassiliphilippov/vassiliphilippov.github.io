firebase

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪərˌbeɪs / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌbeɪs / (BrE)
a military base providing artillery support to advancing troops, typically established in a combat zone
military artillery support base

Examples

The soldiers set up a firebase on the hill to provide cover for the advancing infantry.
After a week under attack, the firebase finally received much-needed reinforcements.
Supplies were airlifted daily to the remote firebase during the campaign.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: artillery base - a base supporting artillery operations
