Ameritard

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪˌtɑrd / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪˌtɑːd / (BrE)
A deeply offensive and derogatory slang term used to insult Americans or describe behavior, opinions, or commentary considered foolish, ignorant, or incompetent, particularly from an American perspective.
offensive slang insult for Americans
offensive slang

Examples

The comment section was filled with Ameritard remarks mocking foreign cultures.
Online forums often ban users for using the term Ameritard due to its offensive nature.
He accused his opponent of making yet another Ameritard statement during the debate.

Synset Examples

He called out the Ameritard behavior of the politicians.
The Ameritard commentary on social media was overwhelming.
Her Ameritard opinions often spark debates.

Etymology

Blend of 'American' and offensive term '-tard', a clipped form of 'retard' (offensive slur for intellectual disability).
