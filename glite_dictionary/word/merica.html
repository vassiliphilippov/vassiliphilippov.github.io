merica

1. NOUN

/ məˈrɪkə / (AmE), / məˈrɪkə / (BrE)
(informal, humorous) The United States of America; used to refer to the country, often to evoke stereotypes of American culture or patriotism.
informal term for the United States
informal slang american

Examples

Every Independence Day, people celebrate merica with fireworks and barbecues.
He wore an eagle shirt and yelled, "This is merica!" at the parade.
The commercial was full of classic merica imagery like flags and pickup trucks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: America - The United States of America, a country in North America
  • Synonym: United States - Another term for the United States of America

Etymology

Altered pronunciation and spelling of 'America,' often used humorously in reference to American patriotism.
