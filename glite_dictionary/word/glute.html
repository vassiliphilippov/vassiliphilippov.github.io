glute

1. NOUN

/ ɡluːt / (AmE), / ɡluːt / (BrE)
any of the three large muscles in each buttock, responsible for movement of the thigh
buttock muscle

Examples

Doing squats is a great way to strengthen your glutes and improve lower body power.
The athlete complained of soreness in his left glute after the race.
Targeted exercises can help tone and shape your glutes for better posture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gluteus - any of the muscles forming the buttock

Etymology

Shortened from 'gluteus', from Greek 'gloutos' meaning buttock.
