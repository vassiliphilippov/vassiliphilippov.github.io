E. O. Lawrence

1. PROPN

/ ˌiː oʊ ˈlɔːrəns / (AmE), / ˌiː əʊ ˈlɒrəns / (BrE)
(1901–1958) United States physicist known for inventing the cyclotron.
U.S. physicist; invented the cyclotron

Examples
