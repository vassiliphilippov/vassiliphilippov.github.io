antepartum

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.tiˈpɑːr.təm / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈpɑː.təm / (BrE)
occurring or existing before birth, especially referring to the period before labor or childbirth
before birth; pre-labor (medical)
medical

Examples

The doctor monitored the mother's antepartum health to ensure a safe delivery.
Certain medications are not recommended during the antepartum period due to potential risks to the fetus.
Regular antepartum visits are essential for tracking the baby's growth and development.

Synset Examples

the prenatal period
antenatal care

Related Terms

  • Antonym: postpartum - occurring after childbirth
  • Synonym: prenatal - existing or occurring before birth, especially during pregnancy

Etymology

From Latin 'ante' (before) + 'partus' (birth, labor).
