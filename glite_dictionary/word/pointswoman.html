pointswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔɪntsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪntsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a policewoman stationed at an intersection to direct traffic
female police traffic director

Examples

The pointswoman skillfully managed traffic during the busy morning rush hour.
As a pointswoman, she became well-known for her efficient and friendly manner.
The city hired a new pointswoman to improve traffic flow at the main intersection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pointsman - a police officer (male) who directs traffic at an intersection

Etymology

Formed from 'point', in the sense of 'directing traffic', and 'woman'. Patterned after 'pointsman'.
