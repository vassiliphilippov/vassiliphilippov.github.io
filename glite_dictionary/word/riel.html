riel

1. NOUN

/ riˈɛl / (AmE), / riˈɛl / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Cambodia, equal to 100 sen
Cambodian currency unit

Examples

The price was listed in both US dollars and riel at the market.
She exchanged all her foreign currency for riel upon arriving in Cambodia.
You can pay with either riel or dollars in most shops in Phnom Penh.

Etymology

From Khmer រៀល (riəl).
