culinary

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌlɪˌneri / (AmE), / ˈkʌlɪnəri / (BrE)
connected with cooking or kitchens; relating to the preparation of food.
relating to cooking or kitchens

Examples

She developed her culinary skills by attending cooking classes.
The restaurant is famous for its innovative culinary creations.
He wrote a book about the culinary traditions of Italy.

Etymology

From Latin culinarius, from culina (kitchen).
