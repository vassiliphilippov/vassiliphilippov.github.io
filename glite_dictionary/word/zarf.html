zarf

1. NOUN

/ zɑrf / (AmE), / zɑːf / (BrE)
An ornamental metal holder, often cup-shaped, used to hold a small, handleless coffee cup, especially in Middle Eastern traditions.
ornamental metal coffee cup holder

Examples

The delicate porcelain cup was set inside a beautifully decorated zarf.
Collectors admire the intricate designs found on antique Ottoman zarfs.
He served Turkish coffee in a traditional zarf to preserve the heat and for elegance.

Etymology

From Turkish 'zarf', from Arabic 'ẓarf' (container, envelope).
