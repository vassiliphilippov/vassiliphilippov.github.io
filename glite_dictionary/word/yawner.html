yawner

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɔnər / (AmE), / ˈjɔːnə / (BrE)
a person who yawns
person who yawns

Examples

The teacher caught another yawner in the back row during her lecture.
Every yawner in the meeting made the presenter feel less confident.
At the dentist's office, the nervous yawner struggled to keep his mouth closed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yawn - to open the mouth wide and inhale deeply due to tiredness or boredom
