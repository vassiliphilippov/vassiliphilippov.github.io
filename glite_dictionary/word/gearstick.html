gearstick

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪr.stɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡɪə.stɪk / (BrE)
a lever in a motor vehicle used to change gears manually
lever for changing gears in a vehicle
british

Examples

He gripped the gearstick and smoothly shifted into second gear.
Some cars have a gearstick mounted on the steering column instead of between the seats.
She prefers driving manual cars because she enjoys using the gearstick to change gears.

Synset Examples

in Britain they call a gearshift a ‘gear lever’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gearshift - a device for changing gears in a vehicle (American English)
  • Synonym: gear lever - another term for gearstick, common in British English

Etymology

From 'gear' + 'stick'; 'gear' from Old Norse 'gervi' (apparatus), 'stick' from Old English 'sticca'.
