BA

1. NOUN

/ ˌbiː ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌbiː ˈɑː / (BrE)
a first-level university degree in arts, social sciences, or humanities
Bachelor of Arts degree

Examples

After four years of study, she finally earned her BA in History.
He listed his BA from Oxford on his resume.
Many job postings require applicants to have at least a BA or a related degree.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: BS - Bachelor of Science degree

Etymology

Abbreviation of Bachelor of Arts.
