Ba

1. NOUN

/ biː eɪ / (AmE), / bɑː / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for barium, a soft silvery metallic element of the alkaline earth group
chemical symbol for barium (element)

Examples

The chemical symbol Ba stands for barium on the periodic table.
In chemistry class, we learned that Ba compounds are used in medical imaging.
When testing minerals, the presence of Ba helps identify barite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barium - a soft silvery metallic element of the alkaline earth group

Etymology

From New Latin barium; symbol taken from the first two letters of the element's name.
