hiplength

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɪpˌlɛŋkθ / (AmE), / ˈhɪp.lɛŋθ / (BrE)
(of clothing) extending to or just over the hips.
reaching to the hips (of clothing)

Examples

She wore a stylish hiplength coat over her dress.
A hiplength cardigan is perfect for layering during cool autumn days.
The designer introduced a new collection featuring hiplength jackets in vibrant colors.

Synset Examples

a hiplength jacket

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: knee-length - reaching to the knee (of clothing)
  • Degree Lower: waist-length - reaching the waist (of clothing)
