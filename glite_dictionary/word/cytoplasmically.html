cytoplasmically

1. ADV

/ ˌsaɪtəˈplæzmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsaɪtəʊˈplæzmɪkli / (BrE)
By means of or through the cytoplasm; in a manner relating to the cytoplasm of a cell.
by means of the cytoplasm
medical

Examples

The protein moves cytoplasmically before entering the nucleus.
Some genetic material is inherited cytoplasmically rather than through the nucleus.
The dye spreads cytoplasmically within the cell after injection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cytoplasmic - relating to the cytoplasm of a cell

Etymology

From 'cytoplasm' + '-ically', with 'cytoplasm' from Greek 'kytos' (cell) and 'plasma' (formed or molded).
