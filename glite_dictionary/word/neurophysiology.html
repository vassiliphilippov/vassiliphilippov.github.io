neurophysiology

1. NOUN

/ ˌnʊroʊˌfɪziˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˌfɪziˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of neuroscience that studies the functions and activities of the nervous system
study of nervous system function
medical

Examples

She decided to specialize in neurophysiology during her medical training.
Recent advancements in neurophysiology have led to a better understanding of brain disorders.
The conference featured lectures from leading neurophysiology researchers around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physiology - the branch of biology dealing with the functions of living organisms
  • Synonym: neuroscience - the scientific study of the nervous system

Etymology

From Greek neuron (nerve) + physiology, meaning the study of the functions of living systems.
