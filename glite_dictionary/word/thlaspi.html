Thlaspi

1. NOUN

/ ˈθlæspi / (AmE), / ˈθlaspi / (BrE)
A genus of herbs in the mustard family, commonly known as pennycress, found in temperate regions.
genus of herbs, pennycress

Examples

Thlaspi species are often found in fields and along roadsides across Europe and Asia.
The seeds of Thlaspi have been studied for their oil content and potential uses.
Several gardeners cultivate Thlaspi for its attractive flowers and hardy nature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pennycress - common name for plants in the Thlaspi genus

Etymology

From Greek *thlaspis*, the name for pennycress.
