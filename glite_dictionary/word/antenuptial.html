antenuptial

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.t̬iˈnʌp.tʃəl / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈnʌp.tʃʊ.əl / (BrE)
Relating to events or agreements occurring before marriage.
before marriage; pre-marital
legal

Examples

They signed an antenuptial agreement to clarify financial arrangements before marrying.
The antenuptial party brought both families together to celebrate the upcoming wedding.
She sought legal advice regarding her antenuptial rights to protect her property.

Synset Examples

prenuptial agreement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prenuptial - relating to events before marriage

Etymology

From Latin ante- (before) and nuptialis (of marriage).
