solarium

1. NOUN

/ səˈlɛriəm / (AmE), / səˈleəriəm / (BrE)
a room, typically with glass walls and roof, designed to allow exposure to sunlight
room with glass for sunlight

Examples

They built a solarium to enjoy the sunshine during winter.
The house has a spacious solarium overlooking the garden.
She spent her afternoons reading in the bright solarium.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunroom - a room, typically at the back of a house, designed for relaxing and with many windows to let in sunlight

Etymology

From Latin 'solarium' (sundial, place where the sun is enjoyed), from 'sol' (sun).
