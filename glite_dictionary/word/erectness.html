erectness

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈrɛkt.nəs / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛkt.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being upright or vertical, especially at right angles to the horizon
state of being upright or vertical

Examples

The erectness of the statue made it visible from a great distance.
She maintained perfect erectness during her yoga pose.
The erectness of the trees gave the forest a stately appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uprightness - the condition of being vertical or standing up straight

Etymology

From Latin 'erectus' (upright, raised), past participle of 'erigere' (to raise, set up).

2. NOUN

/ ɪˈrɛktnəs / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛktnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being upright in posture or position.
uprightness of posture or position

Examples

The erectness of her posture gave her an air of confidence during the interview.
After years of training, his erectness while standing was noticeable on stage.
Good erectness is important for preventing back pain and promoting healthy alignment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uprightness - the state of being vertical or erect

Etymology

From Latin ērectus (upright, raised), past participle of ērigere (to raise).
