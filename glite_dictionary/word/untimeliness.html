untimeliness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈtaɪmliˌnɪs / (AmE), / ʌnˈtaɪmliːnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of happening at an inappropriate or inconvenient time.
occurring at an inconvenient time

Examples

The untimeliness of his arrival disrupted the entire meeting.
Many complained about the untimeliness of the announcement, as it came just before the deadline.
Her absence was made worse by the untimeliness of the bad news.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: timeliness - the quality of being timely or appropriate in time

Etymology

From un- (not) + timeliness.

2. NOUN

/ ˌʌnˈtaɪmli.nəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈtaɪmli.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of happening at an inappropriate or unsuitable time
occurrence at a wrong or unsuitable time

Examples

His untimeliness cost him the job interview, as he arrived just after the doors had closed.
Many criticized the untimeliness of the announcement, which caught everyone unprepared.
The untimeliness of his visit meant that nobody was home to welcome him.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: timeliness - the quality of occurring at a suitable or opportune time

Etymology

From un- (not) + timeliness, based on timely, itself from Old English tīmlice (in time).
