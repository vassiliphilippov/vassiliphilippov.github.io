blewits

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluɪts / (AmE), / ˈbluːɪts / (BrE)
a type of edible mushroom with a pale lilac cap when young, belonging to the genus Lepista or Clitocybe
an edible pale lilac mushroom

Examples

We found several blewits growing in the damp woodland after the rain.
The chef prepared a delicious dish using wild blewits and fresh herbs.
When young, blewits are easily recognized by their distinctive lilac caps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mushroom - a fungal fruiting body, often edible
