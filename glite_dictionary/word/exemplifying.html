exemplifying

1. ADJ

/ ɪɡˈzɛmplɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɛmplɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
clarifying or illustrating by providing examples
clarifying by giving examples

Examples

The professor gave an exemplifying case study to help students understand the theory.
She has an exemplifying way of teaching that makes complex ideas easier to grasp.
His speech was filled with exemplifying anecdotes that clarified his main points.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illustrative - serving as an example or explanation
