convergency

1. NOUN

/ kənˈvɝːdʒənsi / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːdʒənsi / (BrE)
the property of approaching a definite, finite limit, especially by an infinite series or sequence in mathematics
approach to a finite limit (mathematics)

Examples

The convergency of the series was evident from its rapidly decreasing terms.
Mathematicians discussed the convergency of the new algorithm in their latest paper.
You need to check the convergency before summing an infinite geometric series.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: convergence - the process or state of converging; especially in mathematics, the property of a sequence or series approaching a limit

Etymology

Formed from 'converge' + '-ency', modeled after 'convergence'.
