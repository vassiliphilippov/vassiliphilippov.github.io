nervelessly

1. ADV

/ ˈnɜrvləsli / (AmE), / ˈnɜːvləsli / (BrE)
in a composed, calm, and unconcerned manner; without nervousness or agitation
in a calm, unconcerned way

Examples

Despite the mounting pressure, she handled the negotiations nervelessly and secured a favorable deal.
He performed his duties nervelessly, never once showing a hint of fear.
The surgeon worked nervelessly throughout the complicated procedure, impressing everyone in the operating room.

Synset Examples

without more ado Barker borrowed a knife from his brigade Major and honed it on a carborundum stone as coolly as a butcher

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calmly - in a calm, relaxed manner
  • Antonym: anxiously - in a worried or nervous manner

Etymology

From 'nerveless' + '-ly'. 'Nerveless' from nerve + -less.
