car

1. NOUN

/ kɑr / (AmE), / kɑː / (BrE)
A road vehicle with four wheels powered by an engine, used for transporting people.
motor vehicle for transporting people

Examples

She bought a new car last month to make her commute easier.
The car was parked outside the restaurant when we arrived.
They traveled across the country by car during their summer vacation.

Synset Examples

he needs a car to get to work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automobile - A motor vehicle used for transportation, especially one with four wheels.
  • Antonym: truck - A large motor vehicle for transporting goods, not people.

Etymology

Shortened from 'motorcar', from Latin 'carrus' (wheeled vehicle).

/ kɑr / (AmE), / kɑː / (BrE)

Examples

Tourists stood in line to board the car for a scenic ride to the summit.

Related Terms
