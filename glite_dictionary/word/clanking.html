clanking

1. ADJ

/ ˈklæŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklæŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
making or characterized by a hard, sharp, nonresonant metallic sound.
making a hard metallic sound

Examples

The clanking machinery echoed throughout the empty factory floor.
He awoke to the clanking sound of pipes in the basement.
The knight's clanking armor drew everyone's attention as he entered the hall.

Synset Examples

clanking chains
the clanking arms of the soldiers near him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clanking - producing a series of sharp metallic sounds (verb form)
  • Synonym: clattering - making a loud, rattling sound, often metallic

Etymology

From the verb 'clank' (imitative origin).
