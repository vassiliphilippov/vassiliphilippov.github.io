usableness

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuːzəblnəs / (AmE), / ˈjuːzəblnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being usable or capable of providing effective service
quality of being usable

Examples

The usableness of the new software impressed both experts and casual users alike.
She questioned the usableness of the outdated equipment in the laboratory.
Regular maintenance ensures the ongoing usableness of our public services.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: usability - the quality of being easy and effective to use

Etymology

From usable + -ness, forming a noun indicating a quality or state.
