chawbacon

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɔˌbeɪkən / (AmE), / ˈtʃɔːˌbeɪkən / (BrE)
an unsophisticated or uncultured person, regarded as unintelligent or lacking in refinement
unsophisticated or uncultured person
archaic

Examples

The city folk laughed at the chawbacon who had never been to a museum.
He dismissed the critics as mere chawbacons who wouldn't understand fine art.
Despite being labeled a chawbacon, he quickly adapted to urban life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yokel - an unrefined country dweller
  • Synonym: bumpkin - an unsophisticated or socially awkward person from the countryside

Etymology

Originally American English, probably from 'chaw' (chew) and 'bacon', referring to rural folk, c. 19th century.
