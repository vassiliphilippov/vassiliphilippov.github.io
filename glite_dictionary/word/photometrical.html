photometrical

1. ADJ

/ ˌfoʊtoʊˈmɛtrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəˈmɛtrɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to photometry, the science of measuring light intensity
relating to the measurement of light

Examples

The photometrical data was crucial for calibrating the telescope's sensors.
He specializes in photometrical techniques to analyze astronomical images.
Recent advances have improved the photometrical accuracy of satellite imaging systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photometric - relating to the measurement of light

Etymology

From Greek 'phos, phot-' (light) + 'metron' (measure) + English '-ical'.
