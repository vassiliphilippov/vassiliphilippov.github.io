Blériot

1. PROPN

/ bleɪrioʊ / (AmE), / bleəriːoʊ / (BrE)
Louis Blériot (1872–1936), French aviator who made the first successful flight across the English Channel in 1909.
French aviator, first Channel flight

Examples

The world was astonished when Blériot successfully flew across the English Channel in 1909.
Many aviation museums feature exhibits dedicated to Blériot and his pioneering aircraft.
Blériot's achievement inspired a new era of aviation development and exploration.

Etymology

From the French surname Blériot; Louis Blériot was his full name.
