low-cal

1. ADJ

/ ˌloʊˈkæl / (AmE), / ˌləʊˈkæl / (BrE)
containing fewer calories than usual; low in calories
low in calories

Examples

She switched to a low-cal salad dressing to keep her meals lighter.
Many people choose low-cal snacks when trying to lose weight.
This restaurant offers several low-cal options on their menu.

Synset Examples

light (or lite) beer
lite (or light) mayonnaise
a low-cal diet
cola light

Related Terms

  • Synonym: low-calorie - containing few calories; reduced calorie content
  • Antonym: high-calorie - containing a large number of calories

Etymology

Shortened form of 'low-calorie', from 'low' + 'calorie'.
