oophoritis

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊ.ə.fəˈraɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˌəʊ.ə.fəˈraɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of one or both ovaries.
inflammation of the ovaries
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with oophoritis after experiencing lower abdominal pain and fever.
If left untreated, oophoritis can lead to chronic pelvic pain and infertility.
Antibiotics are often prescribed in cases of oophoritis caused by bacterial infection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salpingitis - inflammation of the fallopian tubes

Etymology

From Greek 'oophoron' (ovary) + '-itis' (inflammation).
