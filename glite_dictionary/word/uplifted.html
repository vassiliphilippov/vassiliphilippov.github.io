uplifted

1. ADJ

/ ʌpˈlɪftɪd / (AmE), / ʌpˈlɪftɪd / (BrE)
feeling happy and elevated in spirit, often with a sense of pride or inspiration
emotionally elevated or inspired

Examples

She felt uplifted by the encouraging words of her mentor.
The entire room seemed uplifted by the joyful announcement.
After receiving the award, he walked with an uplifted spirit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elated - marked by high spirits and excitement
  • Antonym: dejected - sad and depressed; dispirited

Etymology

From 'uplift' (to raise), formed with up + lift + -ed.
