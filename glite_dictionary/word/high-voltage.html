high-voltage

1. ADJ

/ haɪ ˈvoʊltɪdʒ / (AmE), / haɪ ˈvəʊltɪdʒ / (BrE)
Vigorously energetic, intense, or forceful; full of excitement or tension.
very energetic or intense

Examples

The debate became a high-voltage exchange, with both sides passionately defending their positions.
Her high-voltage performance captivated the entire audience from start to finish.
The company's high-voltage marketing campaign generated a lot of excitement and attention.

Synset Examples

a high-octane sales manager

high-powered executives

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high-powered - having great energy or force; dynamic
  • Synonym: high-octane - very energetic or dynamic, especially in a figurative sense
