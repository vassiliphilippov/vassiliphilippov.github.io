masterstroke

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæstɚˌstroʊk / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstəstrəʊk / (BrE)
an action or achievement that demonstrates exceptional skill, ingenuity, or effectiveness
brilliant action or achievement

Examples

Her negotiation was a real masterstroke that saved the company millions.
The artist’s decision to use light in that way was a masterstroke.
Launching the product before any competitors was a masterstroke of timing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coup - a sudden, brilliant, and successful act
  • Antonym: blunder - a careless or stupid mistake

Etymology

From master + stroke, indicating a skillful or decisive action.
