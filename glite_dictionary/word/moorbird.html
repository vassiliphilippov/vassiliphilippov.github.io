moorbird

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrbɝd / (AmE), / ˈmɔːbɜːd / (BrE)
a reddish-brown grouse found in upland moors of Great Britain, also known as the red grouse
a grouse of British upland moors
british

Examples

The moorbird is well adapted to the harsh conditions of the Scottish Highlands.
Hunters often seek the elusive moorbird during the game season.
The call of the moorbird echoes across the foggy moors at dawn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red grouse - common name for the moorbird
