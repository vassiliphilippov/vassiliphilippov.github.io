hairpin

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛrˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈheə.pɪn / (BrE)
a double-pronged pin, usually made of metal or plastic, used to hold hair in place
double-pronged pin for holding hair

Examples

She secured her bun with a hairpin before leaving the house.
A hairpin lay forgotten on the bathroom counter.
He used a hairpin to pick the lock in the movie.

Etymology

Formed from 'hair' + 'pin'.
