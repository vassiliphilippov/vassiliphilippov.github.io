XTC

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛksˌtiːˈsiː / (AmE), / ˌɛksˌtiːˈsiː / (BrE)
a street name for the recreational drug MDMA (ecstasy), commonly used in informal contexts
street name for MDMA (ecstasy)
slang

Examples

He was arrested for possession of XTC at the club.
Many young people are unaware of the risks associated with taking XTC.
Police seized a large amount of XTC during the raid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecstasy - the drug MDMA, a synthetic drug used recreationally

Etymology

A play on the pronunciation of 'ecstasy,' as used for the drug MDMA.
