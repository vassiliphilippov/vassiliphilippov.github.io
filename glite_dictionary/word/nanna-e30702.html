nanna

1. NOUN

/ ˈnænə / (AmE), / ˈnanə / (BrE)
(informal) a person's grandmother; the mother of one's father or mother.
informal word for grandmother
informal

Examples

My nanna always bakes my favorite cookies when I visit her on weekends.
Every summer we spend a week at nanna's house by the sea.
I called my nanna to wish her a happy birthday.
