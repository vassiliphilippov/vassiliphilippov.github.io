assembling

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɛmbəlɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈsɛmblɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of gathering or bringing things or people together; the process of putting parts together to form a whole
the act of gathering or putting together

Examples

The assembling of the new furniture took several hours.
During the festival, the assembling of the crowd caused delays in traffic.
Careful assembling of all the pieces is crucial for the machine to function properly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collection - an act or process of collecting

Etymology

From the verb assemble, from Old French *assembler*, from Latin *assimulare* ‘to bring together’.
