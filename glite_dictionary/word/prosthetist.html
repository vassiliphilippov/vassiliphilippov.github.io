prosthetist

1. NOUN

/ prɑsˈθɛt̬ɪst / (AmE), / prɒsˈθɛtɪst / (BrE)
A medical specialist who designs, fits, and provides prosthetic limbs and devices for people who have lost a limb or body part.
specialist in prosthetic devices
medical

Examples

After his accident, he worked closely with a prosthetist to find the best artificial leg.
The hospital hired a new prosthetist to improve patient care for amputees.
Her prosthetist adjusted the fit of her arm prosthesis to make it more comfortable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orthotist - a person who makes and fits orthopedic braces

Etymology

From prosthesis, based on the Greek 'prostithenai' (to add to) + -ist (denoting a specialist).
