undomestic

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndoʊˈmɛstɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈmɛstɪk / (BrE)
not domestic; not interested in or suited to home life or household activities.
not suited to home life; not domestic

Examples

She was so undomestic that she preferred dining out every night rather than cooking at home.
His undomestic nature made him shy away from chores and household routines.
Despite her many talents, her friends joked about her being completely undomestic.

Synset Examples

had established herself in her career at the price of being so undomestic she didn't even know how to light the oven

Related Terms

  • Antonym: domestic - relating to the home or household

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'domestic' (from Latin 'domus' meaning house).
