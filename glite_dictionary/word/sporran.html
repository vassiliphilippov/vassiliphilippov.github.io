sporran

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrən / (AmE), / ˈspɒrən / (BrE)
a fur or leather pouch, worn at the front of a kilt as part of traditional Scottish Highland dress
Scottish pouch worn with a kilt

Examples

He wore a ceremonial kilt and a silver-decorated sporran to the wedding.
The sporran adds both practicality and tradition to Scottish Highland dress.
His family crest was engraved on the clasp of his leather sporran.

Etymology

From Scottish Gaelic 'sporan', meaning 'purse'.
