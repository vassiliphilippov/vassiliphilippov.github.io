paraboloidal

1. ADJ

/ pəˌræbəˈlɔɪdəl / (AmE), / pəˌræbəˈlɔɪdəl / (BrE)
having the shape or form of a paraboloid; resembling a paraboloid.
having the shape of a paraboloid

Examples

The satellite dish has a paraboloidal surface to focus signals efficiently.
Engineers designed the reflector with a paraboloidal shape to optimize light collection.
Many telescopes use paraboloidal mirrors to concentrate incoming light onto a detector.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paraboloid - a three-dimensional surface shaped like a parabola rotated around its axis

Etymology

Formed from 'paraboloid' (from Greek 'parabolē', meaning 'comparison, analogy') + '-al'.
