sagaciously

1. ADV

/ səˈɡeɪʃəsli / (AmE), / səˈɡeɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a shrewd and wise manner; showing keen judgment.
in a wise or shrewd manner

Examples

She sagaciously navigated the complex negotiations, ensuring the best possible outcome for her company.
He sagaciously avoided risky investments, preferring a more cautious approach to growing his wealth.
By sagaciously considering both options, they were able to make a decision that benefited everyone.

Synset Examples

he invested his fortune astutely
he was acutely insightful

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wisely - in a wise manner
  • Antonym: foolishly - in a foolish manner

Etymology

From 'sagacious' + '-ly'; 'sagacious' from Latin sagāx (wise, shrewd).
