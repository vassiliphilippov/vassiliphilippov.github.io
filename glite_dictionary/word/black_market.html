to black market

1. VERB

/ blæk ˈmɑr.kɪt / (AmE), / blæk ˈmɑː.kɪt / (BrE)
to buy or sell goods illegally, especially in contravention of government regulations.
deal illegally in goods

Examples

For years, he black marketed imported electronics to avoid government taxes.
Some gangs black market arms to fund their activities in the region.
During the shortage, people black marketed food at inflated prices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to smuggle - to move goods illegally into or out of a country
