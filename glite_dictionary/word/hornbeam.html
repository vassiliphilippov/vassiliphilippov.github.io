hornbeam

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrnˌbim / (AmE), / ˈhɔːnbiːm / (BrE)
a deciduous tree of the genus Carpinus, with smooth gray bark and hard wood, found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere
tree of the genus Carpinus

Examples

A row of hornbeam trees lines the front of the estate.
The furniture was crafted from the durable wood of hornbeam.
Gardeners recommend planting hornbeam for making strong, long-lasting hedges.

Etymology

From horn + beam; 'beam' is an old word for 'tree' or 'wood'.
