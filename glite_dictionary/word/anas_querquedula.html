Anas querquedula

1. NOUN

/ ˌænəs kwɜrˈkwɛdʒʊlə / (AmE), / ˌænəs kwəːˈkwɛdjʊlə / (BrE)
A small species of Eurasian teal, also known as the garganey, found in Europe and western Asia.
species of small Eurasian teal (garganey)

Examples

The Anas querquedula migrates to Africa for the winter months.
Birdwatchers were excited to spot an Anas querquedula at the local wetland reserve.
Unlike other teals, the Anas querquedula has a distinctive bold white stripe over the eye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garganey - another name for Anas querquedula, a small Eurasian teal

Etymology

From Latin: 'anas' meaning duck, 'querquedula' meaning teal.
