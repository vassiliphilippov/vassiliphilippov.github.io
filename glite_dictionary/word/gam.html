gam

1. NOUN

/ ɡæm / (AmE), / ɡam / (BrE)
a group or herd of whales gathered together
herd of whales

Examples

A gam of whales was spotted near the coast early this morning.
Researchers observed the behavior of the gam as the whales migrated south.
The captain was thrilled to encounter a large gam during the expedition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pod - a group of marine mammals such as whales or dolphins

Etymology

Possibly from a dialectal English word meaning 'a social visit'.
