couthy

1. ADJ

/ ˈkuːθi / (AmE), / ˈkuːθi / (BrE)
(chiefly Scottish) Friendly, warm, and sociable; pleasantly familiar or genial.
(Scottish) friendly and sociable
british

Examples

The innkeeper greeted us with a couthy smile, making us feel instantly at home.
Her couthy manner put everyone at ease during the lively evening.
They enjoyed a couthy chat beside the fire after supper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genial - friendly and cheerful
  • Antonym: unfriendly - not friendly or pleasant

Etymology

From Scots 'couth', meaning friendly or pleasant, with -y as an adjectival suffix.
