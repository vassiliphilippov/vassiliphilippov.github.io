blocking

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblɒkɪŋ / (BrE)
The action of preventing someone from moving or advancing by placing oneself in their way.
the act of obstructing someone's movement

Examples

The defender's blocking was crucial in stopping the goal.
Her blocking prevented her opponent from passing.
Proper blocking techniques are essential in American football.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obstruction - the action of blocking or obstructing something
