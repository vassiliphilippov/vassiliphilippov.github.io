Allionia

1. NOUN

/ əˈlaɪ.ə.ni.ə / (AmE), / əˈlaɪ.ə.ni.ə / (BrE)
A genus of small mostly American herbs in the family Nyctaginaceae.
genus of mostly American herbs

Examples

Several species in the genus Allionia are adapted to arid environments.
The botanist collected samples of Allionia during her expedition in the American Southwest.
Flowers of Allionia are often called windmills due to their unique shape.

Etymology

Named after Italian botanist Carlo Allioni (1728–1804).
