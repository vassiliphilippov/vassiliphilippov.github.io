Vaslav Nijinsky

1. PROPN

/ vɑːˌslɑːv nɪˈʒɪnski / (AmE), / ˌvas.lav nɪˈʒɪnski / (BrE)
(1890–1950) Russian ballet dancer and choreographer, regarded as one of the greatest male dancers of the 20th century.
famous Russian ballet dancer (1890–1950)

Examples

Vaslav Nijinsky revolutionized ballet with his innovative choreography and expressive dancing.
Many historians regard Vaslav Nijinsky as the greatest male ballet dancer of his era.
My sister admires Vaslav Nijinsky for his legendary performances in the Ballets Russes.
