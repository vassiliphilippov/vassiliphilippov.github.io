Iliamna ruvularis

1. NOUN

/ ɪliˈæmnə ruːˈvuːlərɪs / (AmE), / ɪliˈamnə ruːˈvjuːlərɪs / (BrE)
A perennial plant of northwestern United States and western Canada, resembling a hollyhock and having white or pink flowers.
perennial plant of NW US and W Canada

Examples

The Iliamna ruvularis can often be found growing in mountainous meadows.
Botanists documented the unique flowering patterns of Iliamna ruvularis in western Canada.
Gardeners admire the delicate pink blossoms of Iliamna ruvularis during the late spring.
