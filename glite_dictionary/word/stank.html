stank

1. NOUN

/ stæŋk / (AmE), / stæŋk / (BrE)
An unpleasant or strong smell, especially one associated with genitals or sex.
strong, unpleasant sexual odor

Examples

After their workout, an unmistakable stank lingered in the locker room.
The sheets reeked of a musky stank that couldn't be ignored.
Even after a shower, a faint stank seemed to follow him around.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stench - a very unpleasant smell
  • Synonym: reek - a strong and unpleasant smell
