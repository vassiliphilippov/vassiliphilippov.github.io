tempura

1. NOUN

/ tɛmˈpʊrə / (AmE), / tɛmˈpʊərə / (BrE)
a Japanese dish consisting of vegetables or seafood dipped in batter and deep-fried until crisp
Japanese battered and fried food

Examples

I ordered tempura shrimp with a side of rice at the Japanese restaurant.
Their vegetable tempura was light, crispy, and full of flavor.
We learned how to make authentic tempura in our Japanese cooking class.

Etymology

From Japanese 天ぷら (tenpura), originally from Portuguese 'tempero' (seasoning).
