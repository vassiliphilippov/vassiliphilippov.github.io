unsolved

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɑlvd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɒlvd / (BrE)
Not explained, resolved, or dealt with; not solved.
not solved or resolved

Examples

The case of the missing painting remains unsolved after several years of investigation.
Despite extensive research, the scientific puzzle is still unsolved.
He was fascinated by unsolved mysteries and devoted his life to studying them.

Synset Examples

many crimes remain unsolved
many problems remain unresolved

Related Terms

  • Antonym: solved - explained or resolved
  • Synonym: resolved - decided or solved successfully

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'solved' (past participle of solve, from Latin 'solvere' - to loosen or solve).
