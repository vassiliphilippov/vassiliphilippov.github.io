Ringo Starr

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɪŋɡoʊ stɑr / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋɡəʊ stɑː / (BrE)
(born 1940) English rock musician and drummer, best known as a member of the Beatles.
English drummer for the Beatles

Examples

Ringo Starr played drums on many of the Beatles' most famous songs.
Many fans admire Ringo Starr for his unique drumming style and laid-back personality.
After the Beatles disbanded, Ringo Starr launched a successful solo career.

Etymology

Stage name of Richard Starkey; 'Ringo' for his rings, 'Starr' shortened from his surname.
