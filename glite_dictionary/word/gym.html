gym

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪm / (AmE), / dʒɪm / (BrE)
A place equipped with facilities for physical exercise, sports, or training.
place for exercise or sports

Examples

I go to the gym every morning before work to keep fit.
The new gym in town offers yoga and spinning classes.
She forgot her shoes at the gym after her last workout.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gymnasium - A large hall for physical education or sports; original full term
  • Synonym: fitness center - A place with exercise and training equipment, focusing on fitness

Etymology

Shortened form of 'gymnasium', from Greek 'gymnasion' (place to exercise naked).
