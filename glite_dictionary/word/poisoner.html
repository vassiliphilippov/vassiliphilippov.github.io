poisoner

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔɪzənər / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪzənə / (BrE)
a person who kills or attempts to kill using poison
person who kills using poison

Examples

The detective realized the poisoner had carefully planned the crime.
In the novel, the main suspect was a skilled poisoner with a dark past.
The poisoner was finally caught after several mysterious deaths in the town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: murderer - a person who commits murder

Etymology

From poison + -er, denoting someone who administers poison.
