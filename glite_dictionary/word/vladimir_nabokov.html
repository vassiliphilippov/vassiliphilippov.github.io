Vladimir Nabokov

1. PROPN

/ vlɑːˈdɪːmɪr nəˈboʊkəf / (AmE), / vlæˈdɪːmɪr nəˈbɒkɒf / (BrE)
(1899–1977) Russian-born American novelist, poet, and critic, known for works such as 'Lolita' and 'Pale Fire'.
Russian-American novelist, author of 'Lolita'

Examples

Vladimir Nabokov is best known for his controversial novel 'Lolita'.
Many critics consider Vladimir Nabokov one of the greatest writers of the twentieth century.
The literary style of Vladimir Nabokov is admired for its sophistication and wit.
