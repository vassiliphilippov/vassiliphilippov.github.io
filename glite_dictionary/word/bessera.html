Bessera

1. NOUN

/ bəˈsɛrə / (AmE), / bɛˈsɪərə / (BrE)
A small genus of perennial flowering plants with corms, native to Mexico, sometimes classified in the family Alliaceae.
genus of Mexican cormous perennial plants

Examples

Species of Bessera are prized for their attractive bell-shaped flowers in ornamental gardens.
The botanist discovered several rare Bessera plants during his expedition in Mexico.
Bessera is sometimes placed in the same family as onions and garlic due to floral similarities.
