tackler

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæklər / (AmE), / ˈtaklə / (BrE)
a player in football whose role is to tackle the ball carrier or opponent
football player who tackles

Examples

The defensive tackler brought the running back down just short of the goal line.
Each tackler on the team practices form to avoid penalties.
As a skilled tackler, she led the league in stops this season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tackle - to stop the progress of a ball carrier by physically bringing them to the ground

Etymology

From 'tackle' + '-er', denoting someone who performs a tackle.
