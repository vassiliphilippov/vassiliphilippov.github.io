stirringly

1. ADV

/ ˈstɜr.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈstəː.rɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that arouses strong emotion or enthusiasm.
in a way that arouses emotion

Examples

The president spoke stirringly about the nation's future during his speech.
Her memoir is written stirringly, drawing readers into her remarkable experiences.
The coach addressed the team stirringly before the final match, motivating everyone.

Synset Examples

he talked stirringly about his days during the war

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspiringly - in a way that inspires, similar to stirringly
  • Antonym: apathetically - in a way that shows or feels little emotion or interest

Etymology

From 'stirring' + '-ly', modeled after the adjective 'stirring'.
