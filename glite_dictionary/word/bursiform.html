bursiform

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɜrsəˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈbɜːsɪˌfɔːm / (BrE)
Shaped like a pouch or sac, especially as described in biological structures.
shaped like a pouch or sac

Examples

The microscopic structure included a bursiform cavity near the cellular lining.
Some plants develop bursiform appendages to store nutrients or seeds.
The gland was described as bursiform due to its pouch-like appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sacciform - shaped like a sac

Etymology

From Latin 'bursa' (purse, bag) + '-form' (having the form of).
