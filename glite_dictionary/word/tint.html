tint

1. NOUN

/ tɪnt / (AmE), / tɪnt / (BrE)
a cosmetic product used to dye or color the hair
hair dye or coloring product

Examples

She decided to use a burgundy tint on her hair for the wedding.
Most salons offer a wide range of tints to suit different hair colors.
This new tint claims to cover grey hair completely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dye - a substance used to change the color of hair, cloth, or other material
  • Antonym: bleach - a substance used to lighten hair color

2. NOUN

/ tɪnt / (AmE), / tɪnt / (BrE)
a slight or barely perceptible amount of a quality or characteristic
a slight trace or hint

Examples

There was a tint of sadness in her smile despite the celebration.
The room had a tint of excitement as everyone awaited the announcement.
His apology carried a tint of insincerity that was hard to ignore.

Synset Examples

he speaks French with a trace of an accent
a hint mockery in her manner
a tint of glamour

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trace - a very small amount
  • Synonym: hint - a very slight or hardly noticeable amount

Etymology

From Latin 'tinctus', past participle of 'tingere' (to dye or color).
