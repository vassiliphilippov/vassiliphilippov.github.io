neckless

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɛkləs / (AmE), / ˈnɛkləs / (BrE)
lacking or apparently lacking a neck
without a neck

Examples

The wrestler's neckless build made him look even more intimidating in the ring.
That dinosaur is often depicted as a neckless creature in some children's books.
He drew a neckless cartoon character, giving it a comical appearance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: necked - having a neck, especially in reference to shape or appearance
