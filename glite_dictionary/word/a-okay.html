a-okay

1. ADJ

/ ˌeɪ oʊˈkeɪ / (AmE), / ˌeɪ əʊˈkeɪ / (BrE)
In perfect condition or order; completely satisfactory.
in perfect condition or order
informal

Examples

After the repairs, the engine was a-okay and ready for takeoff.
Just checking to make sure you're a-okay after the incident yesterday.
The test results show that everything is a-okay with your health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: okay - fine; satisfactory; in good order
  • Degree Higher: excellent - exceptionally good; of the highest quality
  • Synonym: fine - of high quality; satisfactory

Etymology

Probably originated from U.S. astronaut slang in the 1960s, modifying 'OK.'
