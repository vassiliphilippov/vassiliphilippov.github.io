A-okay

1. ADJ

/ ˌeɪ.oʊˈkeɪ / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.əʊˈkeɪ / (BrE)
completely satisfactory; in perfect condition or order; indicating approval or agreement
completely satisfactory; fine
informal

Examples

After a quick check by the mechanic, he assured me that the car was A-okay.
Don’t worry, everything is A-okay with your order, and it will arrive on time.
She gave her friend a thumbs-up to show she was feeling A-okay after the illness.

Synset Examples

Her health check-up results came back A-okay.
He gave me an A-okay nod before leaving.
Everything is A-okay with the project timeline.
She was just a-okay after the long meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: okay - satisfactory; acceptable
  • Synonym: all right - acceptable; satisfactory or safe

Etymology

Popularized by NASA in the 1960s, possibly as a variation on 'okay' with 'A' for emphasis.
