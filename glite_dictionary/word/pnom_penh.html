Pnom Penh

1. PROPN

/ pəˌnɒm ˈpɛn / (AmE), / pəˌnɒm ˈpɛn / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Cambodia, located in the south-central part of the country.
capital city of Cambodia

Examples

After visiting Pnom Penh, they continued their journey to Siem Reap.
The Mekong River flows through Pnom Penh, making it an important port city.
Many French colonial buildings can still be seen in downtown Pnom Penh.
