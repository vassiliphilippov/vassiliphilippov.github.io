to hyphenate

1. VERB

/ ˈhaɪ.fəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.fə.neɪt / (BrE)
to divide or join words with a hyphen (-)
divide or join with a hyphen

Examples

Please hyphenate the compound adjective in this sentence.
Some editors prefer not to hyphenate words at the end of a line.
She hyphenated her last name after getting married.

Synset Examples

hyphenate these words and names

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dash - a punctuation mark often confused with a hyphen
  • Synonym: combine - to unite or merge two or more things into one

Etymology

From 'hyphen' (from Greek 'huphen', together) plus the verb-forming suffix '-ate'.
