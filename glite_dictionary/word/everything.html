everything

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛvriˌθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛvriˌθɪŋ / (BrE)
all that is important to a person; all that matters most to someone
all that is important to someone

Examples

To her, her family means everything and always comes first.
He lost everything that mattered to him in the fire.
When she said music was everything to her, she really meant it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all - the whole quantity or extent of a particular group or thing
  • Antonym: nothing - not anything; no single thing or part
