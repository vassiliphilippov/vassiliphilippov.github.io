ambassadress

1. NOUN

/ æmˈbæsəˌdrɛs / (AmE), / æmˈbæsədrɪs / (BrE)
A female ambassador; a woman appointed as an ambassador.
female ambassador

Examples

The ambassadress delivered a moving speech at the international summit.
As the first female ambassadress to France, she played a vital diplomatic role.
The reception was hosted by the American ambassadress at her residence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ambassador - a diplomat who is the official representative of their country abroad

Etymology

From French 'ambassadrice', feminine form of 'ambassadeur' (ambassador).
