Serengeti

1. PROPN

/ ˌsɛrənˈɡɛti / (AmE), / ˌsɛrənˈɡɛti / (BrE)
a vast plain in northern Tanzania, famous for its rich wildlife and annual migrations
famous plain in Tanzania known for wildlife

Examples

Many tourists travel to the Serengeti each year to witness the great wildebeest migration.
Documentaries often showcase the incredible diversity of animals inhabiting the Serengeti.
The Serengeti is considered one of the best places in the world for a safari adventure.
