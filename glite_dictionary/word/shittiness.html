shittiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪt.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈʃɪt.i.nɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being extremely bad, unpleasant, or miserable; the degree to which something is 'shitty'.
state of being very bad or unpleasant
vulgar

Examples

I can’t believe the shittiness of this customer service experience.
Sometimes the shittiness of a situation just makes you laugh out of disbelief.
We joked about the shittiness of our old apartment after we finally moved out.

Synset Examples

This place has half the shittiness ratio of New Jersey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shitty - extremely bad, unpleasant, or of poor quality
  • Synonym: awfulness - the quality of being extremely bad or unpleasant
  • Antonym: excellence - the quality of being exceptionally good

Etymology

Formed from 'shitty' + '-ness', following English noun-forming patterns.
