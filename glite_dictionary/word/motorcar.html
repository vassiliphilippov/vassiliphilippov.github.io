motorcar

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊt̬ɚˌkɑr / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəkɑː / (BrE)
a road vehicle with four wheels, typically powered by an internal combustion engine, used for transporting people
four-wheeled motor vehicle for people

Examples

He restored a vintage motorcar from the 1920s as a hobby.
The classic motorcar show attracted enthusiasts from across the country.
Regulations required that every motorcar display proper registration plates.

Synset Examples

he needs a car to get to work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car - a road vehicle for transporting people

Etymology

Formed from 'motor' and 'car' in the late 19th century, after the invention of motor vehicles.
