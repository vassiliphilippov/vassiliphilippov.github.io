Aberdeen

1. PROPN

/ ˌæbərˈdin / (AmE), / ˌæbəˈdiːn / (BrE)
a city in northeastern Scotland, situated on the North Sea
city in northeastern Scotland

Examples

Aberdeen is known for its granite architecture and vibrant port economy.
She decided to attend university in Aberdeen to study marine biology.
The oil industry has brought significant economic growth to Aberdeen in recent decades.

Etymology

From Middle English Aberden, from Scottish Gaelic Obar Dheathain (confluence of the Dee).
