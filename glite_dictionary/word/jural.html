jural

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒʊr.əl / (AmE), / ˈdʒʊə.rəl / (BrE)
relating to law or to legal rights and obligations
relating to law or legal rights
legal

Examples

The constitution guarantees certain jural rights to all citizens.
His arguments were grounded in jural principles rather than moral ones.
A jural relationship exists between the employer and employee under contract law.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legal - relating to the law
  • Antonym: illegal - forbidden by law

Etymology

From Latin iūrālis, from iūs, iūris (law, right).
