Weissbier

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪsbiːr / (AmE), / ˈvaɪsbiːə / (BrE)
a pale, cloudy, and effervescent wheat beer made by top fermentation, traditionally in Bavaria
pale, cloudy wheat beer

Examples

The brewery is famous for its traditional Weissbier.
On a hot day, I enjoy drinking a cold glass of Weissbier.
He ordered a Weissbier to pair with his meal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wheat beer - beer brewed primarily with wheat

Etymology

German, from 'weiß' (white) + 'Bier' (beer)
