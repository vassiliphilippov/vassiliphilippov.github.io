apojove

1. NOUN

/ ˌæpoʊˈdʒoʊv / (AmE), / ˌapəʊˈdʒəʊv / (BrE)
the point in the orbit of a satellite or object around Jupiter that is farthest from the planet
farthest orbital point from Jupiter

Examples

After the spacecraft passed apojove, it began to descend toward Jupiter again.
The timing of observations was coordinated to coincide with apojove for clearer measurements.
Scientists analyzed the changes in speed as the probe moved away from apojove toward perijove.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: perijove - the point in the orbit of a satellite or object around Jupiter that is closest to the planet
  • Synonym: apoapsis - the point in the orbit of any object where it is farthest from the body it orbits

Etymology

Formed from 'apo-' (Greek for 'away from') and 'Jove' (Latin name for Jupiter).
