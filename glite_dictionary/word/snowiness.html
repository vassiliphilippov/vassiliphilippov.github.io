snowiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnoʊ.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊ.i.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being snowy or covered with snow.
the state of being snowy or snow-covered

Examples

The snowiness of the fields made the whole landscape seem otherworldly.
Her painting captured the snowiness outside their chalet in breathtaking detail.
Due to the unusual snowiness this year, local roads have remained closed well into spring.

Synset Examples

The illustration is filled with whiteness, a depiction of the snowiness of the Angola scenery that hints as well at the isolation of the winter landscape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snowy - covered with or resembling snow

Etymology

From "snowy" + "-ness" (abstract noun suffix).
