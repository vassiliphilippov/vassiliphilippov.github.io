pomaded

1. ADJ

/ pəˈmeɪdɪd, pəˈmɑːdɪd / (AmE), / pɒˈmeɪdɪd / (BrE)
(of hair) styled or treated with pomade to appear smooth and shiny.
hair styled with pomade

Examples

His pomaded hair gleamed under the ballroom lights.
The actor entered, his pomaded locks slicked perfectly back.
She preferred a natural look, avoiding the pomaded styles popular among her peers.

Synset Examples

pomaded hair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pomade - a scented ointment for hair styling

Etymology

Formed from 'pomade,' from French 'pommade' (ointment); -ed forms past participle/adjective.
