head-shrinker

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdˌʃrɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌʃrɪŋkə / (BrE)
a person, especially among certain indigenous groups, who cuts off and preserves the heads of enemies as trophies
person who preserves victims' heads as trophies

Examples

The tribe was once known for having a feared head-shrinker among them.
Stories of the head-shrinker terrified generations of villagers.
He collected artifacts once belonging to a legendary head-shrinker in South America.

2. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdˌʃrɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌʃrɪŋkə / (BrE)
an informal term for a psychiatrist; a doctor specializing in mental health or psychiatry
informal term for psychiatrist
informal

Examples

Some people think only crazy people go to a head-shrinker, but that's a common misconception.
After months of stress, he finally decided to see a head-shrinker to talk through his problems.
The detective often consulted a head-shrinker to get inside the mind of the criminal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychiatrist - a medical doctor specializing in mental illness
  • Synonym: psychologist - a specialist in psychology, sometimes contrasted with psychiatrist

Etymology

From 'head' referring to the mind or brain and 'shrinker', slang for someone who 'reduces' mental disturbances; popularized in mid-20th-century U.S. slang.
