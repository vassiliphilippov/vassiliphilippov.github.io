monogram

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːnəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəˌɡræm / (BrE)
a decorative motif made by combining two or more letters, usually initials, into a single design, often used on personal items or clothing
decorative design of combined initials

Examples

She had her towels embroidered with a gold monogram.
The artist designed a stylish monogram for the new company logo.
His shirt cuffs were personalized with his initials in a subtle monogram.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emblem - a symbolic object representing a concept or entity

Etymology

From Greek 'monogrammon', from 'monos' (single) + 'gramma' (letter).
