Gregory of Nazianzen

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡrɛɡəri əv næziˈænzən / (AmE), / ˈɡrɛɡəri əv ˌnazɪˈanzən / (BrE)
(329–391) An early Christian theologian and Church Father, recognized as a saint and Doctor of the Church, noted for his opposition to heresies and influential theological writings.
early Christian theologian and Church Father

Examples

Gregory of Nazianzen played a significant role in shaping early Christian doctrine.
Many of Gregory of Nazianzen's sermons are still studied by theologians today.
As a Doctor of the Church, Gregory of Nazianzen is honored in both the Eastern and Western Christian traditions.
