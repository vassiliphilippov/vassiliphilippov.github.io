to switch-hit

1. VERB

/ swɪtʃ hɪt / (AmE), / swɪtʃ hɪt / (BrE)
(of a batter in baseball or cricket) to bat right-handed against a left-handed pitcher or bowler, and left-handed against a right-handed pitcher or bowler.
bat from both sides against opposite-handed pitchers

Examples

He can switch-hit, making him a valuable player against any pitcher.
Several players on the team were taught to switch-hit from a young age.
She switch-hit during the game, surprising the opposing team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bat - to hit at the ball with a bat in sports
