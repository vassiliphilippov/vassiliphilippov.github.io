Denmark

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɛnˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈdɛnmɑːk / (BrE)
a country in northern Europe, consisting of the mainland Jutland and numerous islands between the North Sea and the Baltic Sea; a constitutional monarchy.
country in northern Europe

Examples

Copenhagen is the capital city of Denmark.
She traveled to Denmark last summer to experience Scandinavian culture.
The majority of Denmark is situated on the Jutland Peninsula and offshore islands.

Etymology

From Old Norse Danmark, meaning 'border district of the Danes'.
