unassertiveness

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.əˈsɜːr.tɪv.nəs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈsɜː.tɪv.nəs / (BrE)
a lack of assertiveness; the quality of being reluctant to assert oneself or promote one's own interests
lack of assertiveness

Examples

Her unassertiveness at work often led to her opinions being overlooked in meetings.
The team's unassertiveness made them hesitate to challenge unfair decisions by management.
He struggled with unassertiveness in social situations, finding it hard to express his needs.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: assertiveness - the quality of being self-assured and confident without being aggressive
  • Synonym: diffidence - shyness or lack of self-confidence
