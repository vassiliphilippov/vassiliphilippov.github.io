sitting

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
an occasion when someone poses for a photograph or portrait
posing session for photo or portrait

Examples

He had to schedule a sitting with the photographer next week.
They managed to finish the entire portrait in just one sitting.
The artist requires at least two sittings to complete a detailed painting.

Synset Examples

he wanted his portrait painted but couldn't spare time for the sitting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: session - a period during which an activity takes place
  • Synonym: shoot - a session of taking photographs

Examples
