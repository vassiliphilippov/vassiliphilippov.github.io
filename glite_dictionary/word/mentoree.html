mentoree

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛn.təˈri / (AmE), / ˌmɛn.təˈri / (BrE)
a person who is being mentored by someone, especially in a professional or educational setting
person being mentored

Examples

As a mentoree, she found the regular feedback from her mentor extremely helpful.
Each mentoree will be paired with an experienced staff member for six months.
The program offers guidance to every mentoree based on their specific career goals.

Synset Examples

A student mentor may support up to seven mentees

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mentee - a person who is being mentored

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'mentor', using the suffix '-ee' denoting one who is the recipient of an action.
