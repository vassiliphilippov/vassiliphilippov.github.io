Napaea

1. NOUN

/ nəˈpiːə / (AmE), / nəˈpiːə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae, consisting of only one species, commonly known as glade mallow.
a plant genus in the mallow family

Examples

The unique habitat requirements of Napaea make it a rare find in the wild.
Botanists recently surveyed the last remaining population of Napaea in the area.
Conservation efforts are underway to protect glade mallow, the sole species in the genus Napaea.
