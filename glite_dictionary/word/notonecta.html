Notonecta

1. NOUN

/ ˌnoʊtəˈnɛktə / (AmE), / ˌnəʊtəˈnɛktə / (BrE)
a genus of aquatic insects in the family Notonectidae, commonly known as backswimmers, characterized by their habit of swimming on their backs
genus of aquatic insects (backswimmers)

Examples

The pond was full of Notonecta gliding smoothly just beneath the surface.
Scientists observed how Notonecta capture their prey while swimming upside down.
A single Notonecta can often be identified by its pale wings and unique movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backswimmer - an aquatic insect that swims on its back, of the Notonectidae family

Etymology

From Greek notos (back) + nēktēs (swimmer), referring to their backswimming habit.
