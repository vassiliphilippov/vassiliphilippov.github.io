documental

1. ADJ

/ ˌdɑː.kjəˈmɛn.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˌdɒ.kjʊˈmɛn.tl̩ / (BrE)
relating to, consisting of, or derived from documents; involving or based on documentation.
relating to documents or documentation

Examples

The court's decision was based entirely on documental evidence provided by both parties.
Their research focuses on analyzing documental sources from the 19th century.
We require documental proof of your identity before processing your application.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: documentary - consisting of or derived from documents; factual

Etymology

From Latin 'documentum' (lesson, proof, example) with the suffix '-al' denoting 'pertaining to'.
