helpdesk

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlpˌdɛsk / (AmE), / ˈhɛlpˌdɛsk / (BrE)
a service that provides information and assistance to users, especially for technical or computer-related problems
service desk for technical assistance

Examples

Please contact the helpdesk if you experience any technical difficulties accessing the network.
The company set up a new helpdesk to handle employee IT issues more efficiently.
She called the helpdesk and quickly received the support she needed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: support desk - a desk or service that offers assistance to users
  • Synonym: customer service - assistance provided by a company to those who buy or use its products or services

Etymology

From 'help' + 'desk', reflecting its function as a place to seek help.
