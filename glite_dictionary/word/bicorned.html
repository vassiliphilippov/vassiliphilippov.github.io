bicorned

1. ADJ

/ ˈbaɪ.kɔrnd / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ.kɔːnd / (BrE)
Having two horns or horn-shaped projections.
having two horns or horn-like parts

Examples

The goat's bicorned skull was displayed in the museum's natural history section.
Certain species of beetles have striking bicorned heads used in mating battles.
The doctor explained that the patient had a rare condition known as a bicorned uterus.

Synset Examples

a bicornuate uterus

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unicorned - having one horn or horn-shaped part

Etymology

From Latin 'bicornis', from 'bi-' (two) + 'cornu' (horn).
