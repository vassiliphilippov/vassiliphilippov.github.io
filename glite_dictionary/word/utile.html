utile

1. ADJ

/ ˈjuːtəl / (AmE), / ˈjuːtaɪl / (BrE)
useful; capable of providing benefit or being of service
useful; beneficial

Examples

The new software proved utile in managing the company's finances efficiently.
Her advice was particularly utile during that difficult period.
Many found the pocket dictionary utile for quick reference when traveling abroad.

Synset Examples

the girl felt motherly and useful
a useful job
a useful member of society

Related Terms

  • Synonym: useful - able to be used for a practical purpose or in several ways

Etymology

From Latin utilis (useful), from uti (to use).
