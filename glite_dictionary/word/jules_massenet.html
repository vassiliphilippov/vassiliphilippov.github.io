Jules Massenet

1. PROPN

/ ʒul maˈsɛn.eɪ / (AmE), / ʒyl ma.səˈneɪ / (BrE)
(1842–1912) A French composer known for his operas, particularly popular during the late 19th and early 20th centuries.
French composer of operas

Examples

The music of Jules Massenet still captivates opera audiences around the world.
Many critics consider Jules Massenet’s opera 'Manon' to be his masterpiece.
As a student, he was inspired by the romantic style of Jules Massenet.
