musicianship

1. NOUN

/ mjuˈzɪʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / mjuːˈzɪʃənʃɪp / (BrE)
the skill, artistry, or competence of a musician in performing music
skill or artistry in performing music

Examples

Her exceptional musicianship impressed everyone at the concert.
The band's musicianship has grown considerably since their debut album.
Critics praised the pianist's expressive musicianship in last night's performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: virtuosity - great technical skill in music or another artistic pursuit

Etymology

From musician + -ship, denoting state or quality.
