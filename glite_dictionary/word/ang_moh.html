ang moh

1. NOUN

/ ɑŋ moʊ / (AmE), / æŋ məʊ / (BrE)
A colloquial term in Singapore and Malaysia referring to a foreigner, especially a person of Western or Caucasian descent.
foreigner, especially Westerner (Singapore/Malaysia slang)
informal slang

Examples

Many locals were curious about the new ang moh who moved into the neighborhood.
The ang moh picked up some common phrases in Malay to communicate better.
Despite being an ang moh, she quickly adapted to Singaporean customs.

Synset Examples

The ang moh tourist loved exploring the city.
The ang moh was amazed by the vibrant culture.
He’s an ang moh who enjoys local cuisine.

Etymology

From Hokkien Chinese 'ang' (red) and 'moh' (hair), originally referring to red-haired Westerners.
