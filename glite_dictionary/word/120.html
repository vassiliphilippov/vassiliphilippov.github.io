120

1. NUM

/ wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈtwɛnti / (AmE), / wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈtwɛnti / (BrE)
The number one hundred twenty; the cardinal number after 119 and before 121.
the number one hundred twenty

Examples

There are 120 students participating in the science fair this year.
The train can reach speeds of up to 120 kilometers per hour.
He celebrated his grandmother's 120th birthday with a big party.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 119 - the number one hundred nineteen
  • Degree Higher: 121 - the number one hundred twenty-one
