readably

1. ADV

/ ˈriː.dəb.li / (AmE), / ˈriː.dəb.li / (BrE)
In a way that is easy to read or legible.
in a legible or readable manner

Examples

The document was printed readably so that everyone could easily understand the information.
Please fill out the form readably to avoid any confusion during processing.
The professor writes readably on the board, making it easy for students to take notes.

Synset Examples

you must write legibly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legibly - in a clear and readable manner

Etymology

Formed from 'readable' + '-ly', following standard adverb formation in English.
