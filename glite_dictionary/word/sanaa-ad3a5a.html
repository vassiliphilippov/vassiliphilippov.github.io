Sana'a

1. PROPN

/ sɑːˈnɑː / (AmE), / sɑːˈnɑː / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Yemen, located on the central plateau of the country.
capital city of Yemen

Examples

Sana'a is known for its distinctive architecture and ancient city walls.
After landing in Yemen, we spent our first night in Sana'a.
The old city of Sana'a has been designated a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Etymology

From Arabic صنعاء (Ṣan‘ā’).
