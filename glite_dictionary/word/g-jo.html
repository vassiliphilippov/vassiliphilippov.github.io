G-Jo

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒi ˌdʒoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː ˌdʒəʊ / (BrE)
a therapeutic method involving the application of finger pressure to specific points on the body to relieve symptoms or pain
pressure-point therapy technique

Examples

Some people use G-Jo to manage chronic headaches instead of medication.
She attended a workshop to learn basic G-Jo techniques for stress relief.
After a session of G-Jo, he reported feeling more relaxed and less sore.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acupressure - a therapy involving pressure on specific points of the body
