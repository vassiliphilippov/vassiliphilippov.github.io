milklike

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɪlkˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling milk, especially in color or opacity; having a whitish or cloudy appearance
having a milk-like appearance or color

Examples

The pond had a milklike quality after the heavy rainstorm.
She wore a dress made from a milklike fabric that shimmered softly in the light.
A milklike mist covered the valley, obscuring the distant hills.

Synset Examples

milky glass

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milky - having the appearance or characteristics of milk
  • Antonym: clear - completely transparent or easy to see through
