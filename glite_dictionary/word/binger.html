binger

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈbɪŋə / (BrE)
(slang) A person addicted to crack cocaine, or one who frequently uses it in binges.
person addicted to crack cocaine (slang)
slang

Examples

The police said the apartment was a known hangout for local bingers.
After losing his job, he became a full-time binger and rarely left his room.
Social workers are concerned about the rising number of teenage bingers in the neighborhood.
