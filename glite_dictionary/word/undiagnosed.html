undiagnosed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈdaɪəɡˌnoʊzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈdaɪəɡnəʊzd / (BrE)
(of an illness, problem, or condition) not having been identified or recognized by diagnosis.
not diagnosed; not yet identified
medical

Examples

The patient was suffering from an undiagnosed infection for several weeks.
Many cases of the rare disorder remain undiagnosed due to lack of awareness.
Researchers are investigating the effects of undiagnosed conditions in adolescents.

Synset Examples

undiagnosed disease

Related Terms

  • Antonym: diagnosed - having been identified by diagnosis

Etymology

From un- (not) + diagnose + -ed.
