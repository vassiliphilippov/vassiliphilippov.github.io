Zanzibar

1. PROPN

/ ˈzænzɪˌbɑr / (AmE), / ˈzænzɪˌbɑː / (BrE)
an island in the Indian Ocean off the east coast of Africa, now part of Tanzania
island off the east coast of Africa, part of Tanzania

Examples

Many tourists visit Zanzibar to enjoy its white-sand beaches and rich cultural history.
After exploring mainland Tanzania, we took a ferry to Zanzibar for a relaxing holiday.
The spices of Zanzibar are famous around the world for their exceptional quality.

Etymology

From Arabic Zanjibār, itself from Persian Zangbār, meaning 'coast of the blacks'.
