red-streaked

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɛdˈstriːkt / (AmE), / ˌrɛdˈstriːkt / (BrE)
Having red stripes or lines on the surface.
marked with red stripes

Examples

The painter created a red-streaked pattern on the canvas for dramatic effect.
She chose a red-streaked marble countertop for the new kitchen.
The sunset sky was red-streaked with bold colors stretching across the horizon.
