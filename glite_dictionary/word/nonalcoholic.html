nonalcoholic

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˌælkəˈhɑlɪk / (AmE), / nɒnˌælkəˈhɒlɪk / (BrE)
(of a drink or product) not containing alcohol.
not containing alcohol

Examples

The restaurant offers a wide variety of nonalcoholic drinks for guests of all ages.
She prefers to serve nonalcoholic punch at company parties.
He decided to order a nonalcoholic cocktail to stay refreshed without consuming alcohol.

Synset Examples

nonalcoholic beverages

Related Terms

  • Antonym: alcoholic - containing alcohol

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'non-' (not) + 'alcoholic'.
