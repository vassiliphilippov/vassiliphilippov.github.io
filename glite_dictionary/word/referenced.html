referenced

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛf.ɚənst / (AmE), / ˈrɛf.ərənst / (BrE)
(of a book, article, etc.) containing or supported with written references or citations to sources.
supported with references or citations

Examples

The report is thoroughly referenced to ensure all statements can be verified.
She submitted a well-referenced research paper for her final project.
A referenced article gives readers confidence in the reliability of the information.

Synset Examples

a carefully referenced biography

Related Terms

  • Synonym: documented - supported by documentary evidence or records
