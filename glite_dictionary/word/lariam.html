Lariam

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛəri.əm / (AmE), / ˈlæri.əm / (BrE)
a trademarked antimalarial drug, containing mefloquine, used especially in cases resistant to chloroquine
antimalarial drug (trade name)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Lariam for my upcoming trip to a malaria-endemic region.
Some travelers report vivid dreams as a side effect of Lariam.
Due to its potential neuropsychiatric effects, Lariam is not recommended for everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mefloquine - an antimalarial drug, active ingredient in Lariam
