nonwoody

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˈwʊdi / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈwʊdi / (BrE)
not consisting of or resembling wood; not woody, especially referring to plants without woody stems.
not woody; lacking woodiness

Examples

Many garden herbs are nonwoody and die back in winter.
The researchers studied the habitats of nonwoody plants in the grassland area.
Unlike trees, most annuals are nonwoody species.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: woody - consisting of, or resembling wood
