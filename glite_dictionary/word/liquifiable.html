liquifiable

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪkwəˌfaɪəbəl / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪˌfaɪ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
Capable of being converted into a liquid form.
capable of being liquefied

Examples

At certain temperatures, the gas becomes liquifiable under moderate pressure.
Some metals are liquifiable at relatively low temperatures, making them useful in various industries.
Helium is not easily liquifiable by pressure alone and requires extremely low temperatures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liquefiable - capable of being liquefied

Etymology

From liquefy + -able, based on Latin 'liquefacere' (to make liquid).
