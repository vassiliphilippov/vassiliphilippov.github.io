shedder

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɛdər / (AmE), / ˈʃɛdə / (BrE)
a person or animal that sheds or spills blood, especially as an attacker or killer
a person who sheds blood

Examples

The ruthless warrior was known as a fierce shedder of blood on the battlefield.
Legends tell of a mysterious shedder who haunted the midnight forests.
He despised being called a shedder, despite his violent reputation.

Synset Examples

a great hunter and spiller of blood

Related Terms

  • Synonym: killer - a person or thing that kills
  • Antonym: pacifist - someone opposed to violence or killing

Etymology

From the verb 'shed' + -er, indicating 'one who sheds'.
