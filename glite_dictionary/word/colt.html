Colt

1. NOUN

/ koʊlt / (AmE), / kəʊlt / (BrE)
A type of revolver, especially one manufactured by the Colt's Patent Firearms Manufacturing Company; also used generically to refer to revolvers of this make.
type of revolver

Examples

The detective carried a Colt in his shoulder holster at all times.
He proudly displayed his grandfather's vintage Colt at the exhibition.
The outlaw was known for his quick draw and his trusted Colt.

Etymology

From the name of the American firearms company founded by Samuel Colt (1814–1862).
