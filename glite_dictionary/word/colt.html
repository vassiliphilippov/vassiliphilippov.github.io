colt

1. NOUN

/ koʊlt / (AmE), / kəʊlt / (BrE)
A young male horse, especially one under the age of four.
young male horse (under 4 years)

Examples

The farmer showed us a spirited colt that was only two years old.
Last spring, their mare gave birth to a healthy colt.
The trainer carefully prepared the young colt for its first race.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: filly - a young female horse under the age of four

Etymology

From Old English 'colt' (a young horse).
