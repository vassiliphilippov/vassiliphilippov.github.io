Curitiba

1. PROPN

/ kuɾiˈtʃiːbə / (AmE), / kʊˌrɪˈtiːbə / (BrE)
a city in southeastern Brazil, known as the capital of the state of Paraná
city in southeastern Brazil

Examples

Curitiba is famous for its innovative urban planning and green spaces.
My friends visited Curitiba last summer and enjoyed the lively street markets.
The city of Curitiba has become a model for sustainable development in Brazil.

Etymology

From Portuguese, possibly from the Tupi language, meaning 'pine nut land' or 'many pine seeds'.
