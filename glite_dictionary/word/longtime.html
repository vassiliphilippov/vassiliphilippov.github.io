longtime

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɔŋ.taɪm / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋ.taɪm / (BrE)
having existed, persisted, or continued for a long period of time, especially in a particular role or relationship
existing or continuing for a long time

Examples

He is a longtime supporter of environmental causes.
Sarah had lunch with her longtime colleague from college.
Their company employee was honored for his longtime service.

Synset Examples

a longtime friend

Related Terms

  • Synonym: long-standing - having existed or continued for a long time
