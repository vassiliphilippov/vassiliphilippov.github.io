aquavit

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːkwəˌvit / (AmE), / ˈakwəviːt / (BrE)
A strong Scandinavian alcoholic spirit, usually flavored with caraway or other spices.
Scandinavian alcoholic spirit

Examples

He offered his guests a glass of aquavit before dinner.
In Sweden, aquavit is often enjoyed during festive occasions.
The distinctive flavor of aquavit comes from caraway seeds and sometimes dill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schnapps - A strong alcoholic drink, typically from Germany or Scandinavia

Etymology

From Latin 'aqua vitae' meaning 'water of life', via Scandinavian languages.
