thirty-fifth

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɝːti ˈfɪfθ / (AmE), / ˌθɜːti ˈfɪfθ / (BrE)
ordinal number matching the position of 35 in a sequence; being number 35 in order or rank.
ordinal number, thirty-five

Examples

The team finished in thirty-fifth place out of fifty competitors.
His office is on the thirty-fifth floor of the skyscraper.
This is the thirty-fifth anniversary of the company’s founding.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirty-fourth - ordinal number for 34 in order
  • Degree Higher: thirty-sixth - ordinal number for 36 in order
