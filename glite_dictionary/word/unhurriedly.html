unhurriedly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈhɜrɪdli / (AmE), / ʌnˈhʌrɪdli / (BrE)
in a slow and calm way, without hurry
in a slow, calm, and unhurried way

Examples

She walked unhurriedly through the garden, enjoying the morning air.
He spoke unhurriedly, making sure everyone understood his points.
The waiter moved unhurriedly between the tables, creating a relaxed atmosphere.

Synset Examples

she proceeded unhurriedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leisurely - in a relaxed, unhurried manner
  • Antonym: quickly - at a fast speed; rapidly
  • Synonym: slowly - at a slow speed; not quickly

Etymology

From 'unhurried' + '-ly', where 'unhurried' is formed from 'un-' (not) + 'hurried'.
