Freetown

1. PROPN

/ ˈfriːˌtaʊn / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌtaʊn / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Sierra Leone, located on the Atlantic coast; a major port city
capital city of Sierra Leone

Examples

Freetown is known for its vibrant markets and rich colonial history.
Their flight to Sierra Leone landed in Freetown late at night.
After spending a week in Freetown, they traveled to other parts of the country.

Etymology

Named for its foundation as a settlement for freed slaves in the late 18th century.
