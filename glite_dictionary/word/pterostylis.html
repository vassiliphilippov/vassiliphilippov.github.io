Pterostylis

1. PROPN

/ tɛrəˈstaɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˌptɛrəˈstɪlɪs / (BrE)
A genus of terrestrial orchids native to Australia, New Zealand, and the western Pacific region.
genus of terrestrial orchids

Examples

Many native Australian forests are home to several species of Pterostylis.
The greenhood orchids, belonging to the genus Pterostylis, are prized by botanists for their unique flowers.
She photographed a rare Pterostylis during her trip to New Zealand.

Etymology

From Greek pteron (wing) and stylos (pillar), referring to the wing-like structures of the flower's column.
