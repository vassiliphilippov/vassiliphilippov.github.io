sanctitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæŋktəˌtuːd / (AmE), / ˈsaŋktɪtjuːd / (BrE)
the state or quality of being holy or sacred; holiness
holiness; sacredness

Examples

The poem reflects on the sanctitude of the ancient temple.
Many regard the mountains with a sense of sanctitude and awe.
His work among the poor was inspired by a feeling of moral sanctitude.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holiness - the quality of being holy
  • Synonym: sacredness - the state of being sacred

Etymology

From Late Latin sanctitudo (holiness), from sanctus (holy).
