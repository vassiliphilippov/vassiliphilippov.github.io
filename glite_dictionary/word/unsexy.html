unsexy

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɛksi / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛksi / (BrE)
not sexually attractive or arousing; lacking qualities that are considered appealing or exciting.
not sexually attractive

Examples

Many people regard math as an unsexy subject, but it's actually quite fascinating.
The new shoes were comfortable, though decidedly unsexy.
Despite their unsexy appearance, these cars are incredibly reliable.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sexy - sexually attractive or arousing

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'sexy'.
