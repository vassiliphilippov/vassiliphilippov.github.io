Pashto

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːʃtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpæʃtəʊ / (BrE)
an Iranian language spoken primarily in Afghanistan and Pakistan, and one of the official languages of Afghanistan
Iranian language of Afghanistan and Pakistan

Examples

He is fluent in both Dari and Pashto.
Many official documents in Afghanistan are written in Pashto.
She decided to learn Pashto before traveling to Kabul.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dari - the other official language of Afghanistan
  • Synonym: Persian - the Iranian language closely related to Pashto

Etymology

From Pashto پښتو (paṣ̌tō), the native name for the language.
