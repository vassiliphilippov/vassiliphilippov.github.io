actuated

1. ADJ

/ ˈæktʃuˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈæktjʊeɪtɪd / (BrE)
moved to take action; motivated or prompted to act.
moved to act; motivated

Examples

He was actuated by a strong sense of duty to his community.
The machinery is actuated by a hydraulic system.
Her decision was actuated by a desire to make a difference.

Synset Examples

a man actuated by unworthy desires

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motivated - given a reason or incentive to act
  • Synonym: driven - motivated or compelled to act by a particular force or influence

Etymology

Past participle of 'actuate,' from Medieval Latin actuator (one who sets in motion).
