physician

1. NOUN

/ fəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / fɪˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
a person qualified and licensed to practice medicine, especially one who diagnoses and treats illnesses or injuries
licensed medical doctor
medical

Examples

After years of training, she finally became a respected physician at the local hospital.
A physician can help diagnose the cause of your symptoms and recommend treatment.
Many people choose to see their family physician for regular check-ups.

Synset Examples

I felt so bad I went to see my doctor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doctor - a person licensed to practice medicine

Etymology

From Old French *fisicien* (doctor), from Latin *physica* (natural science).
