Patrai

1. PROPN

/ pæˈtraɪ / (AmE), / ˈpætraɪ / (BrE)
a port city in western Greece, historically an important trade center, located in the northwestern Peloponnese on the Ionian Sea
port city in western Greece

Examples

The ancient city of Patrai played a major role in Greek commerce.
Archaeologists uncovered rare artifacts in Patrai dating back to the 5th century BC.
During the Middle Ages, Patrai regained much of its former commercial significance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Patras - modern name of Patrai, a city in western Greece
