apparitional

1. ADJ

/ əˌpɛrɪˈʃənəl / (AmE), / əˌparɪˈʃənl / (BrE)
relating to or resembling an apparition; ghostly or spectral in appearance or character
ghostlike; relating to apparitions

Examples

The old mansion was said to be haunted by an apparitional figure that prowled its halls at night.
Her face took on an apparitional quality in the dim light, seeming almost not of this world.
A chill ran through the crowd as an apparitional glow appeared in the cemetery.

Synset Examples

a ghostly face at the window
a phantasmal presence in the room
spectral emanations
spiritual tappings at a seance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spectral - relating to or resembling a specter or ghost
  • Synonym: phantasmal - like a phantom; illusory or ghostly

Etymology

From 'apparition' (from Latin 'apparitio', appearance) + '-al' (forming adjectives).
