therapeutically

1. ADV

/ ˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or is intended to heal or treat illness or injury.
for healing or treatment purposes
medical

Examples

The medication is used therapeutically to treat chronic pain conditions.
Some plants are valued not just for nutrition but also for their therapeutically beneficial properties.
Massage can be applied therapeutically to relieve muscle tension and promote relaxation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: therapeutic - relating to the healing of disease

Etymology

From 'therapeutic' + '-ally'.
