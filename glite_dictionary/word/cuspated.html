cuspated

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌsˌpeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkʌspeɪtɪd / (BrE)
having cusps or pointed projections; pointed or cusped in shape
having cusps or pointed projections

Examples

The church window features a beautifully cuspated arch that draws the eye upward.
Some leaves on this tree species are cuspated, ending in sharp, pointed tips.
The artist designed a cuspated pattern for the building's façade, giving it a striking silhouette.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cuspidate - having a cusp or pointed tip; pointed

Etymology

From Latin 'cuspis' (point, spear, tip) + English adjectival suffix '-ated'.
