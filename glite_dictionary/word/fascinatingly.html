fascinatingly

1. ADV

/ ˈfæsɪˌneɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈfæsɪneɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that arouses intense interest or attraction.
in a captivating or interesting way

Examples

The young inventor spoke fascinatingly about her innovative projects.
The book is fascinatingly written, making complex ideas easy to understand.
He described the ancient ruins fascinatingly, holding everyone's attention.

Synset Examples

her face became fascinatingly distorted

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interestingly - in an interesting manner
  • Antonym: boringly - in a boring manner
