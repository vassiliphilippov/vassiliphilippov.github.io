Texan

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛks.ən / (AmE), / ˈtɛks.ən / (BrE)
relating to Texas or its people
from or relating to Texas

Examples

The Texan landscape is known for its wide-open plains and vibrant bluebonnets.
She wore a Texan cowboy hat during the rodeo.
Local cuisine reflects Texan traditions, especially when it comes to barbecue.

2. NOUN

/ ˈtɛksən / (AmE), / ˈtɛksən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the state of Texas in the United States
person from Texas, USA

Examples

He proudly told everyone he was a Texan even after moving to New York.
Many Texans celebrate their state's independence with festivals and parades.
As a lifelong Texan, she loves barbecue and country music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Louisianan - a native or resident of Louisiana, USA
  • Antonym: non-Texan - someone who is not a native or resident of Texas

Etymology

From Texas + -an (suffix forming demonyms and adjectives).
