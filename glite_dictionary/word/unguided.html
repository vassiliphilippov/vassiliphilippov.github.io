unguided

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɡaɪdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡaɪdɪd / (BrE)
(of a missile or object) not directed or controlled after being launched or set in motion.
not directed after launch or set in motion

Examples

The enemy fired several unguided rockets toward the border town.
This model simulates the flight path of an unguided missile under various conditions.
Unlike unguided projectiles, these advanced weapons can change direction in midair.

Synset Examples

unguided missiles

Related Terms

  • Antonym: guided - directed or controlled after launch or set in motion

Etymology

From un- (not) + guided; 'guide' from Old French 'guider', of Germanic origin.
