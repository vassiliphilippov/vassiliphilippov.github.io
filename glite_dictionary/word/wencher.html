wencher

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛntʃər / (AmE), / ˈwɛntʃə / (BrE)
a man who habitually seeks the company of prostitutes or engages in frequent sexual relations with women
man who frequents prostitutes

Examples

The old play depicted him as a notorious wencher much to the dismay of respectable citizens.
Rumors of the politician being a wencher spread quickly through the small town.
He tried to distance himself from his past as a reckless wencher.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: womanizer - a man who habitually seeks casual sexual encounters with women

Etymology

From 'wench' (woman, especially a prostitute or promiscuous woman) + -er.
