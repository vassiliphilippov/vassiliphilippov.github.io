11th

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈlɛvənθ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛvənθ / (BrE)
coming next after the tenth and just before the twelfth in a sequence; ordinal form of eleven
ordinal number; eleventh in a sequence

Examples

Her birthday is on the 11th of March each year.
This is the 11th time he has visited the museum.
The meeting will be held on the 11th floor of the building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eleventh - ordinal number coming after tenth
  • Degree Lower: tenth - ordinal number for number ten
  • Degree Higher: twelfth - ordinal number for number twelve
