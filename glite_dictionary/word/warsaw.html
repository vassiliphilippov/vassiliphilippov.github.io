Warsaw

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɔr sɔ / (AmE), / ˈwɔː sɔː / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Poland, located in central Poland
capital city of Poland

Examples

She traveled to Warsaw for a conference on European history.
The Vistula River runs through the heart of Warsaw.
After years abroad, he decided to return and live in Warsaw.

Etymology

From Polish 'Warszawa', originating from the personal name Warsz and the Slavic suffix -awa.
