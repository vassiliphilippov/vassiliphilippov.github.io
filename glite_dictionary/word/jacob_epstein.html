Jacob Epstein

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒeɪkəb ˈɛpstaɪn / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪkəb ˈɛpstiːn / (BrE)
(1880–1959) British sculptor, born in the United States, known for his portrait busts and large, often controversial sculptures.
British sculptor (1880–1959)

Examples

The work of Jacob Epstein often provoked strong reactions from the public and art critics.
A major exhibition of Jacob Epstein's sculptures was held at the Tate Gallery in London.
Many of Jacob Epstein's most famous busts depict significant figures from the early twentieth century.
