Cairene

1. NOUN

/ kaɪˈriːn / (AmE), / kaɪˈriːn / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Cairo, Egypt.
native or resident of Cairo

Examples

As a proud Cairene, she delights in sharing her city's history with visitors.
Many Cairenes commute daily across the Nile to reach their workplaces.
He became friends with a Cairene during his studies abroad.

Etymology

From Cairo + English suffix -ene, denoting inhabitants of a place.
