Pipile

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪpaɪli / (AmE), / ˈpɪpɪli / (BrE)
A genus of large, crested guans known as piping guans, native to Central and South America.
genus of large crested piping guans

Examples

The Pipile species are found primarily in tropical forests of South America.
Scientists recently discovered a new member of the Pipile genus in Brazil.
Conservation efforts are underway to protect several endangered Pipile species.

Etymology

From Latin pipilus, referring to the piping call of the birds.
