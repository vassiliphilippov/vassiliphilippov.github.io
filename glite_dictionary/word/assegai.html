assegai

1. NOUN

/ ˈæsɪˌɡaɪ / (AmE), / ˈæsɪˌɡʌɪ / (BrE)
a slender spear traditionally used by Bantu-speaking peoples of southern Africa, often for hunting or warfare
slender African spear

Examples

The warrior carried an assegai as he patrolled the savannah.
In the museum, you can see an ornately decorated assegai from the Zulu nation.
She read a novel set in Africa that described how hunters used the assegai to track game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spear - a weapon with a pointed tip, used for thrusting or throwing

Etymology

From Afrikaans 'assegai', from Arabic 'az-zaġāyah' (the spear), via Berber.
