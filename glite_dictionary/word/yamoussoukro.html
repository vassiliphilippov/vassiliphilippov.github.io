Yamoussoukro

1. PROPN

/ jæˌmuːsuːˈkroʊ / (AmE), / jæˌmʊsuːˈkrəʊ / (BrE)
the official capital city of Côte d'Ivoire (Ivory Coast), located in the central part of the country
capital city of Côte d'Ivoire

Examples

The presidential palace in Yamoussoukro is a popular tourist attraction.
After moving the capital from Abidjan, Yamoussoukro officially became the administrative center of the country.
International conferences are frequently hosted in Yamoussoukro due to its central location.
