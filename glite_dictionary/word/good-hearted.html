good-hearted

1. ADJ

/ ɡʊd ˈhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
Kind, sympathetic, and generous; having or showing a kind and compassionate nature.
kind and compassionate

Examples

Her good-hearted nature made her popular with everyone she met.
He offered good-hearted advice to those in need of encouragement.
Many volunteers at the shelter are warm and good-hearted people.

Synset Examples

was charitable in his opinions of others
kindly criticism
a kindly act
sympathetic words
a large-hearted mentor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kind-hearted - having a kind and sympathetic nature
  • Antonym: mean-spirited - having or showing a cruel or unkind nature
