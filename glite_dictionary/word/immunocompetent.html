immunocompetent

1. ADJ

/ ɪˌmjunəˈkɑmpətənt / (AmE), / ɪˌmjuːnəˈkɒmpɪtənt / (BrE)
able to develop an immune response upon exposure to an antigen; having a normally functioning immune system.
able to mount an immune response
medical

Examples

Only immunocompetent individuals are able to fight off the infection effectively.
The vaccine was administered to both immunocompetent and immunocompromised patients in the study.
Researchers used an immunocompetent mouse model to test the new therapy.

Synset Examples

immunocompetent cells

Related Terms

  • Antonym: immunodeficient - having an impaired or weakened immune system

Etymology

From 'immune' (relating to immunity) + 'competent' (having the necessary ability), formed in medical terminology.
