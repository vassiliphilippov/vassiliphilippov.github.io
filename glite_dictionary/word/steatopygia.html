steatopygia

1. NOUN

/ stiˌætəˈpɪdʒiə / (AmE), / stiˌætəˈpɪdʒiə / (BrE)
The condition of having an extreme accumulation of fat on the buttocks, often considered a physiological characteristic in certain populations.
extreme accumulation of fat on the buttocks
medical

Examples

Anthropologists have documented steatopygia as a distinguishing feature among certain populations in southern Africa.
The medical journal article provided a detailed analysis of steatopygia and its evolutionary implications.
Hereditary steatopygia is sometimes observed in the genetic history of specific ethnic groups.

Etymology

From Greek stear (fat) + pygē (buttocks), describing the physical trait.
