paling

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
a fence made of upright wooden or metal stakes, typically placed close together
a fence of upright pickets

Examples

The old cottage was surrounded by a white paling that needed repainting.
Children leaned against the paling to watch the parade go by.
He repaired several broken slats in the garden paling over the weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: picket fence - a type of fence with upright pointed wooden slats

Etymology

From Middle English 'palynge', from 'pale' (a stake), ultimately from Latin 'palus' (stake).
