Atrovent

1. PROPN

/ ˈætɹoʊˌvɛnt / (AmE), / ˈætrəʊˌvɛnt / (BrE)
a brand name for an inhaled bronchodilator medication used to relieve bronchospasm, particularly in conditions like asthma and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)
inhaled bronchodilator medication (brand name)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Atrovent to help improve his breathing.
Many patients with COPD use Atrovent as part of their daily treatment routine.
Make sure to use your Atrovent inhaler exactly as directed by your healthcare provider.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ipratropium - the generic name of the drug marketed as Atrovent
