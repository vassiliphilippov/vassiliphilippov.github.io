Shin Bet

1. PROPN

/ ʃɪn ˈbɛt / (AmE), / ʃɪn ˈbɛt / (BrE)
the Israeli domestic counterintelligence and internal security agency.
Israeli internal security agency

Examples

The Shin Bet increased security measures after intelligence of a possible attack was received.
Ruth worked as an analyst for the Shin Bet during her service in Israel.
The Shin Bet has played a pivotal role in preventing numerous terrorist threats.

Synset Examples

the Shin Bet also handles overall security for Israel's national airline

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Mossad - the Israeli foreign intelligence agency
  • Synonym: MI5 - the British internal security service

Etymology

From Hebrew 'Shin Bet', an abbreviation of 'Sherut ha-Bitachon ha-Klali' (General Security Service), using the Hebrew initials shin (ש) and bet (ב).
