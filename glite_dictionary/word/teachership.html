teachership

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiːtʃərʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈtiːtʃəʃɪp / (BrE)
the position, status, or office of being a teacher
the position of a teacher

Examples

Her dedication during her teachership earned her the respect of students and colleagues alike.
He aspired to teachership from a young age, inspired by his favorite instructors.
The university offered a teachership to promising graduate students seeking classroom experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teachership - the position of a teacher (variant spelling)

Etymology

Formed from 'teacher' + '-ship' (denoting position or status).
