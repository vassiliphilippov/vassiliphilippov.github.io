load-bearing

1. ADJ

/ ˈloʊd ˌbɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈləʊd ˌbeərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a part of a structure) capable of supporting weight or a structural load above it.
able to support structural weight

Examples

We discovered that the wall between the kitchen and living room is load-bearing and cannot be easily removed.
Make sure to check if any alterations affect a load-bearing structure before renovation.
The architect explained which beams in the building were load-bearing and which were purely decorative.

Synset Examples

a supporting wall

Related Terms

  • Antonym: non-load-bearing - not capable of supporting a structural load
  • Synonym: supporting - providing structural support
