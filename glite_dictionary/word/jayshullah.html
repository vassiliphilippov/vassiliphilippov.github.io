Jayshullah

1. NOUN

/ dʒaɪˈʃuːlɑː / (AmE), / dʒaɪˈʃʊlɑː / (BrE)
an indigenous Islamic terrorist group active in Azerbaijan, known for attempting to bomb the United States embassy in 1999
Islamic terrorist group in Azerbaijan, 1999

Examples

The United States condemned the actions of Jayshullah after the attempted embassy bombing in 1999.
Authorities believe Jayshullah was responsible for several terrorist plots in Azerbaijan.
Reports on Jayshullah highlight the challenges of combating domestic terrorism in the region.

Etymology

From Arabic 'jaysh' (army) and 'Allah' (God), meaning 'Army of God'.
