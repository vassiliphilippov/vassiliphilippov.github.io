strong-boned

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrɔŋ ˌboʊnd / (AmE), / ˈstrɒŋ ˌbəʊnd / (BrE)
having strong or sturdy bones; possessing a robust bone structure.
having strong bones

Examples

The doctor said she was strong-boned and in excellent health for her age.
Certain dog breeds are known to be particularly strong-boned and muscular.
Despite his slender appearance, he is actually quite strong-boned and resilient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: big-boned - having large bones or a large bone structure
