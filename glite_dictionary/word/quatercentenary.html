quatercentenary

1. NOUN

/ ˌkwɔːtərsɛnˈtɛnəri / (AmE), / ˌkwɔːtəsɛnˈtiːnəri / (BrE)
The 400th anniversary of an event or the celebration of it.
400th anniversary or its celebration

Examples

The town held a special parade to mark its quatercentenary in 2020.
Numerous books were published for the college's quatercentenary celebrations.
The quatercentenary of Shakespeare's death was commemorated worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: quincentenary - The 500th anniversary or celebration of it
  • Degree Lower: tercentenary - The 300th anniversary or celebration of it

Etymology

From Latin roots: 'quater' (four times) + 'centenary' (hundred years).
