epithelioid

1. ADJ

/ ɪˌpɪθiˈliːɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈθiːliɔɪd / (BrE)
Resembling or relating to epithelial cells or tissue.
resembling epithelium
medical

Examples

Under the microscope, the tumor cells appeared epithelioid in shape.
The pathologist described the structure as an epithelioid lesion.
Some granulomas show a characteristic epithelioid appearance.

Synset Examples

epithelial layer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: epithelial - of or relating to epithelium

Etymology

From epithelial + -oid (Greek -oeidēs, meaning 'like, form, shape').
