musicological

1. ADJ

/ ˌmjuːzɪkəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmjuːzɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the scholarly study or theory of music (musicology)
relating to musicology

Examples

She published several musicological articles on the history of Baroque opera.
The conference attracts leading musicological scholars from around the world.
His musicological research focuses on traditional folk songs.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: musicology - the scholarly study of music

Etymology

From 'musicology' (the scholarly study of music) plus the suffix '-ical'.
