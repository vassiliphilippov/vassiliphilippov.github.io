shock-absorbent

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɑk əbˌzɔrbənt / (AmE), / ˈʃɒk əbˌzɔːbənt / (BrE)
Able to absorb the energy of an impact and reduce the transmitted force or shock.
able to absorb impact

Examples

Running shoes with shock-absorbent soles help protect your joints from injury.
The construction workers wore shock-absorbent helmets for extra safety.
This mat is made from a shock-absorbent material that cushions your fall.

Synset Examples

the material absorbs shock and is used for shock-absorbent insoles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shock-absorbing - able to absorb impact or shock
