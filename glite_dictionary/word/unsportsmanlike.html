unsportsmanlike

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈspɔrtsmənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ʌnˈspɔːtsmənˌlaɪk / (BrE)
not conforming to accepted standards of fair play, ethics, or sportsmanship, especially in sports or competition.
not showing fair play or sportsmanship

Examples

The player was penalized for his unsportsmanlike conduct during the match.
Cheating to win the game is considered highly unsportsmanlike by coaches and fans alike.
Swearing at the referee was an unsportsmanlike act that led to his suspension.

Synset Examples

a dirty fighter
used foul means to gain power
a nasty unsporting serve
fined for unsportsmanlike behavior

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sportsmanlike - showing fairness, respect, and graciousness in competition

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'sportsmanlike', meaning not characteristic of a sportsman.
