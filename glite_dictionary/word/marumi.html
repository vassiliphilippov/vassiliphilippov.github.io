marumi

1. NOUN

/ məˈruːmi / (AmE), / məˈruːmi / (BrE)
A shrub of the kumquat family that bears small, round, edible orange fruit, commonly known as round kumquat.
shrub bearing round kumquats

Examples

The marumi is especially valued for its sweet, round fruit in home gardens.
She planted a marumi near the patio to enjoy its fragrant blossoms and fruit.
Farmers cultivate marumi trees for both ornamental purposes and fruit production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kumquat - small tropical tree with edible orange-like fruit

Etymology

From Japanese marumi, meaning 'round' (maru) with suffix 'mi' for fruit.
