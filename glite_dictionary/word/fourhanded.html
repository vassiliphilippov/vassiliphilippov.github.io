fourhanded

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɔrˈhændɪd / (AmE), / ˌfɔːˈhændɪd / (BrE)
(of a card game) involving or requiring four players.
card game for four players

Examples

We played a fourhanded game of bridge last night.
The tournament only allows fourhanded poker matches for this round.
A fourhanded session requires exactly four people to participate.

Etymology

From 'four' + 'handed', meaning with four hands (players).
