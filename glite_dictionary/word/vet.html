vet

1. NOUN

/ vɛt / (AmE), / vɛt / (BrE)
a doctor who is qualified to treat sick or injured animals
animal doctor

Examples

We took our dog to the vet because she was limping.
The vet gave the cat a thorough check-up during the annual visit.
If your pet is ill, a vet can diagnose and treat most animal conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: veterinarian - a doctor who treats animals; formal term for 'vet'
  • Antonym: physician - a doctor who treats human patients

Etymology

Shortened form of 'veterinarian', from Latin 'veterinarius' (of cattle, relating to beasts of burden).
