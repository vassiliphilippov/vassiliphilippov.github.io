Oreo

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːrioʊ / (AmE), / ˈɔːriəʊ / (BrE)
a brand of chocolate sandwich cookie consisting of two chocolate wafers with a sweet white cream filling between them
chocolate sandwich cookie with cream filling

Examples

She dunked her Oreo in a glass of milk and took a bite.
We bought a pack of Oreos for the road trip.
He crumbled Oreos on top of the ice cream for extra flavor.

Etymology

Originated as the brand name for the cookie introduced by Nabisco in 1912.
