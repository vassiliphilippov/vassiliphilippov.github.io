Arequipa

1. PROPN

/ ˌɑrəˈkipə / (AmE), / ˌɑːrɪˈkiːpə / (BrE)
a city in southern Peru, founded in 1540, known for its colonial architecture and proximity to active volcanoes
city in southern Peru

Examples

Arequipa is renowned for its beautiful white stone architecture known as sillar.
Many travelers visit Arequipa as a gateway to the Colca Canyon.
The historic center of Arequipa has been designated a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
