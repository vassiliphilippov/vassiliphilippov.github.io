mincer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪnsər / (AmE), / ˈmɪnsə / (BrE)
a kitchen utensil or machine used for cutting or chopping food, especially meat, into very small pieces
kitchen utensil for chopping food

Examples

She put the beef through the mincer to make fresh ground meat for burgers.
The old-fashioned mincer in my grandmother's kitchen was made of heavy iron.
Vegetables can also be processed in a mincer for soups and sauces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grinder - a device for grinding or crushing substances
  • Synonym: chopper - a device or machine for chopping food or other substances

Etymology

From 'mince' + '-er', meaning one that minces; 'mince' from Middle English, from Old French 'mincier'.

Examples
