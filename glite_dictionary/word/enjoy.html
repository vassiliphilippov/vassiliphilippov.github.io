to enjoy

1. VERB

/ ɪnˈdʒɔɪ / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɔɪ / (BrE)
to take pleasure in something; to find something enjoyable or satisfying
take pleasure in; find satisfying

Examples

I always enjoy spending time with my family on weekends.
Children enjoy playing outside in the sunshine.
She really enjoyed the concert last night.

Synset Examples

I love cooking

Related Terms

  • Synonym: like - to find something agreeable or enjoyable
  • Antonym: dislike - to not find pleasure in something

Etymology

From Old French enjoir, based on Latin gaudere (to rejoice).
