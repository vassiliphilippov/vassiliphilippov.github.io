farting

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑrtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːtɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of expelling gas from the intestines through the anus.
release of gas from the anus
informal

Examples

Excuse me for my farting, but I had a lot of beans for dinner.
She burst out laughing at the sound of his farting during the meeting.
Farting can sometimes be a sign of a healthy digestive system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flatulence - release of gas from the digestive system through the anus

Etymology

From Middle English 'farten', from Old English 'feortan', related to German 'furzen'.
