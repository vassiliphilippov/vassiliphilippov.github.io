Waikiki

1. PROPN

/ waɪkiˈki / (AmE), / waɪkiˈki / (BrE)
a famous beach and resort district on the island of Oahu, southeast of Honolulu, Hawaii
famous beach and resort area on Oahu

Examples

Many tourists dream of surfing at Waikiki because of its legendary waves and scenic views.
During our trip to Hawaii, we spent three days relaxing on the sands of Waikiki.
Waikiki offers a vibrant nightlife alongside its beautiful beaches and luxury hotels.

Etymology

From Hawaiian 'Waikīkī', meaning 'spouting fresh water'.
