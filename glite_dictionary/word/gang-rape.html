to gang-rape

1. VERB

/ ˈɡæŋˌreɪp / (AmE), / ˈɡaŋˌreɪp / (BrE)
to rape (someone) by a group of people acting together, typically one after another; to commit sexual assault involving multiple offenders against a single victim
to rape by a group acting together
offensive

Examples

The court heard that the victim was gang-raped by several men after the party.
Survivors of war often testify about being gang-raped by soldiers during conflicts.
He was convicted for having gang-raped a woman with three accomplices.

Synset Examples

The prisoner was gang-raped

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rape - to force someone into sexual intercourse against their will

Etymology

Formed from 'gang' (a group) and 'rape' (to force sexual intercourse).
