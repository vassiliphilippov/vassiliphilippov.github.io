Klamath

1. PROPN

/ ˈklæməθ / (AmE), / ˈklæməθ / (BrE)
a river in the western United States, flowing from southern Oregon through northern California to the Pacific Ocean
river in Oregon and California, USA

Examples

The Klamath is one of the largest rivers in California's north coast region.
Several Native American tribes have lived along the Klamath for centuries.
After heavy rains, the Klamath can rise rapidly and impact the surrounding communities.

Etymology

Named after the Klamath people, a Native American tribe indigenous to the region.
