color blind

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌlɚ blaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə blaɪnd / (BrE)
unable to distinguish certain colors, especially red and green, due to a deficiency or absence of particular color-sensitive cells in the eye
unable to distinguish certain colors

Examples

Because he is color blind, he finds it difficult to read certain colored charts.
The designer made her website accessible for color blind users.
His color blind condition was discovered during a routine eye exam.

Synset Examples

Being color blind, he often confuses red and green.
His color blind condition makes picking clothes challenging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: color vision deficiency - partial or total inability to perceive colors normally
