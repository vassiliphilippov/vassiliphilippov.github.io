findom

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪn.dəm / (AmE), / ˈfaɪn.dɒm / (BrE)
A practice or relationship in which one person (typically a man) gives money or gifts to another (typically a woman) who exerts financial domination over them, often as a form of sexual fetish or power dynamic.
practice of financial domination
slang

Examples

She entered the world of findom after learning about it from online forums.
Some people find the dynamics of findom empowering, while others see it as exploitative.
The rise of findom has sparked debate about power and consent in online relationships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: financial domination - The broader term for the practice of findom; dominant financial relationship, often sexualized

Etymology

Blend of 'financial' and 'domination'.
