hoydenish

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɔɪdənɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɔɪdɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
(of a girl) behaving in a wild, noisy, or boisterous way; tomboyish.
wild and boisterous (of a girl)

Examples

The hoydenish girl climbed trees and played football with the boys every afternoon.
Her hoydenish laughter echoed down the quiet hallway, shocking some of the teachers.
Despite her hoydenish ways, she was beloved by all for her honest and energetic spirit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tomboyish - exhibiting characteristics of a tomboy; behaving in a boyish or boisterous manner

Etymology

From 'hoyden' (a boisterous girl), plus the suffix -ish.
