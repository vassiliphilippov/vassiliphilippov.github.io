encounter

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈkaʊntər / (AmE), / ɪnˈkaʊntə / (BrE)
A meeting, especially one that happens unexpectedly or by chance, with a person or thing.
unexpected or casual meeting

Examples

Our first encounter was completely by accident at the grocery store.
She described her encounter with the famous singer as brief but memorable.
During his hike, he had an unexpected encounter with a wild deer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meeting - an assembly or gathering of people

Etymology

From Old French 'encontre' (meeting, confrontation), from 'encontrer' (to meet), based on Late Latin 'incontrare'.

Source: WordNet
