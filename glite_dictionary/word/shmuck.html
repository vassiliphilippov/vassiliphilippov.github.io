shmuck

1. NOUN

/ ʃmʌk / (AmE), / ʃmʌk / (BrE)
A contemptible or foolish person; someone regarded as obnoxious or annoying.
foolish or obnoxious person (slang)
slang vulgar

Examples

Don't be such a shmuck—just apologize and move on.
He acted like a real shmuck at the meeting, interrupting everyone.
Sometimes I feel like a shmuck for believing his lies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - an annoying or contemptible person (slang)
  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person

Etymology

From Yiddish *shmok* (penis, fool), of Slavic origin.
