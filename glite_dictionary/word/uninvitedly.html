uninvitedly

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnɪnˈvaɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈvaɪtɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that is without invitation; without being asked or welcomed.
without invitation; not invited

Examples

She walked uninvitedly into the meeting, surprising everyone with her presence.
Several guests showed up uninvitedly, causing some discomfort for the hosts.
He entered the conversation uninvitedly and offered his opinion.

Synset Examples

the women arrived uninvitedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unasked - not having been requested or invited

Etymology

From 'uninvited' + '-ly', with 'uninvited' meaning not having been asked to attend.
