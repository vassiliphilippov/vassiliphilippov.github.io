Maurice Chevalier

1. PROPN

/ moʊˈris ʃəˈvælieɪ / (AmE), / mɒˈriːs ʃəˈvælieɪ / (BrE)
(1888–1972) A French actor and cabaret singer known for his suave persona and distinctive singing style, famous for performances in both France and Hollywood.
French actor and cabaret singer (1888–1972)

Examples

The classic film featured Maurice Chevalier as the charming lead.
Many admired the signature straw hat worn by Maurice Chevalier during his performances.
Maurice Chevalier remains an icon of early 20th-century entertainment.
