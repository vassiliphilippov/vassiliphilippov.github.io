deific

1. ADJ

/ diˈɪfɪk / (AmE), / diˈɪfɪk / (BrE)
Having the nature of a god; divine or godlike.
divine or godlike

Examples

The ancient statue was believed to possess a deific presence that inspired awe.
In many myths, heroes are granted deific powers by the gods.
Her voice carried a deific quality that captivated everyone in the hall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: divine - having characteristics of or relating to a god or deity

Etymology

From Latin 'deificus', from 'deus' (god) + '-ficus' (making), meaning 'making divine'.
