queerly

1. ADV

/ ˈkwɪrli / (AmE), / ˈkwɪəli / (BrE)
in a strange or unusual way; in a manner that is odd or unconventional
in a strange or unusual way

Examples

She looked at him queerly, as if she couldn't believe what he had just said.
The old house was queerly silent at night, making visitors uneasy.
His explanation struck everyone as queerly implausible, but no one challenged him.

Synset Examples

this money had been queerly come by

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strangely - in a strange or unusual way
  • Antonym: normally - in a normal or expected way

2. ADV

/ ˈkwɪrli / (AmE), / ˈkwɪəli / (BrE)
In a strange, odd, or peculiar manner.
in a strange or peculiar manner

Examples

She looked at him queerly, as if she didn't recognize him.
The house was decorated queerly for the holidays, with mismatched ornaments and unusual colors.
He smiled queerly before revealing the surprise.

Synset Examples

a queerly inscribed sheet of paper

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strangely - in a strange or unusual way
  • Antonym: normally - in a usual or expected manner

Etymology

From the adjective 'queer' (strange, odd) + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
