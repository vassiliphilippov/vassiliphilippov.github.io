coition

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈɪʃən / (AmE), / kəʊˈɪʃən / (BrE)
Sexual intercourse between individuals, especially involving vaginal penetration.
sexual intercourse (formal)

Examples

The textbook explained the biological processes involved in coition.
Certain animals have elaborate rituals before coition begins.
Researchers studied the hormonal changes that occur after coition in mammals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: copulation - sexual intercourse, especially the act of a man and a woman having sex
  • Synonym: intercourse - sexual activity involving penetration

Etymology

From Latin 'coitio', from 'coire' meaning 'to go together, meet for sex'.
