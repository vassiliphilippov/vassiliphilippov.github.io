patty-pan

1. NOUN

/ ˈpætiˌpæn / (AmE), / ˈpætiˌpan / (BrE)
a shallow baking dish used for cooking patties, pasties, or small pies
shallow dish for cooking patties or pies

Examples

The baker placed each meat pie in a patty-pan before sliding them into the oven.
She bought a new set of patty-pans for making tartlets during the holidays.
Traditional Cornish pasties are often baked in a patty-pan to keep their shape.
