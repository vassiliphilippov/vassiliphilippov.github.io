Kolam

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈlɑːm / (AmE), / kəˈlɑːm / (BrE)
A member of an indigenous community originating from south central India.
member of an Indian indigenous group

Examples

The Kolam have maintained many of their traditional customs despite modernization.
Researchers have studied the languages spoken by the Kolam across different regions.
A member of the Kolam shared stories about the group's ancient heritage.
