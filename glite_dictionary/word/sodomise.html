to sodomise

1. VERB

/ ˈsɑːdəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɒdəmaɪz / (BrE)
To have sexual intercourse with an animal.
engage in sexual act with an animal
legal medical vulgar

Examples

Engaging in the act to sodomise an animal is considered a criminal offense in many countries.
The court case involved allegations that the defendant attempted to sodomise livestock on a farm.
Laws strictly prohibit anyone from trying to sodomise animals due to ethical and public health concerns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bestiality - sexual intercourse between a person and an animal

Etymology

From Old French *sodomiser*, from the biblical city of Sodom, associated with certain sexual practices.

vulgar legal british

Examples

Related Terms
