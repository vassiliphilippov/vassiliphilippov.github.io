plutocratic

1. ADJ

/ pluːtəˈkrætɪk / (AmE), / ˌpluːtəˈkrætɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of plutocrats or a society ruled by the wealthy.
relating to rule by the wealthy

Examples

The country was run by a plutocratic elite who controlled most major industries.
Some critics argue that the current system has become dangerously plutocratic in nature.
She lamented the plutocratic influences shaping government policy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aristocratic - relating to a class of nobles or aristocracy
  • Antonym: democratic - relating to or supporting democracy or its principles

Etymology

From Greek 'ploutokratia' (rule by the wealthy), via French 'ploutocratique'.
