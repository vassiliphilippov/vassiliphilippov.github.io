mercurous

1. ADJ

/ mərˈkjʊrəs / (AmE), / mɜːˈkjʊərəs / (BrE)
(of a compound) containing mercury with a valence of one (+1 oxidation state).
containing mercury(+1); chemical term
medical

Examples

The scientist analyzed a mercurous compound in the laboratory.
Mercurous chloride is sometimes referred to as calomel.
Some mercurous compounds can be hazardous if mishandled.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: mercuric - containing mercury with a valence of two (+2 oxidation state)

Etymology

From Latin 'Hydrargyrus' (mercury), with the suffix '-ous' indicating a lower oxidation state.
