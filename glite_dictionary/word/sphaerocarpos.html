Sphaerocarpos

1. PROPN

/ sfɪr.oʊˈkɑːr.pəs / (AmE), / sfɪə.rəˈkɑː.pɒs / (BrE)
A genus of liverworts in the family Sphaerocarpaceae, characterized by a small, many-lobed, usually round thallus.
genus of liverworts with small, lobed thallus

Examples

Sphaerocarpos species are often found in damp, shaded habitats.
The orbicular thallus of Sphaerocarpos distinguishes it from many other liverworts.
Researchers have studied the reproductive structures of Sphaerocarpos to better understand liverwort evolution.

Etymology

From Greek 'sphaira' (sphere) and 'karpos' (fruit).
