cubital

1. ADJ

/ ˈkjuːbɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈkjuːbɪtəl / (BrE)
relating to the elbow or the area of the forearm closest to the elbow
of or relating to the elbow
medical

Examples

The nurse inserted the IV into the cubital vein for easy access.
A cubital tunnel syndrome can cause numbness in the hand and fingers.
He complained of pain in the cubital region after the fall.

Etymology

From Latin cubitus (elbow).
