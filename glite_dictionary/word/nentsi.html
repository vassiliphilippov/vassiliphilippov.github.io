Nentsi

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛntsi / (AmE), / ˈnɛntsi / (BrE)
The Uralic language spoken by the Nenets people of northern Siberia.
Uralic language of northern Siberia

Examples

The Nentsi language is part of the Uralic family.
Researchers are studying the grammar of Nentsi to better understand its origins.
Fewer people speak Nentsi today due to cultural changes in northern Siberia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nenets - a member of the Samoyedic people of northern Siberia

Etymology

From the self-designation 'Nenets' referring to the people who speak the language.
