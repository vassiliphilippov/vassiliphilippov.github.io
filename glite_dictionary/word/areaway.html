areaway

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr.iˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈeə.ri.weɪ / (BrE)
a passage or open area, often between buildings or leading to a basement, especially for entry of light or access
passageway between buildings or to a basement

Examples

The cleaning crew swept the areaway behind the apartment building early in the morning.
Leaves accumulated in the areaway, blocking access to the basement door.
A metal grate covered the old stone areaway, letting light into the lower level.
