stapler

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪpələr / (AmE), / ˈsteɪplə / (BrE)
A device for inserting staples into sheets of paper to fasten them together.
device for fastening papers with staples

Examples

She reached for the stapler to attach the pages together before handing in her report.
The office ran out of staples, so the stapler couldn't be used until they bought more.
He accidentally jammed the stapler while trying to fasten a large stack of papers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: staple - a small, bent metal wire used to fasten papers

Etymology

From 'staple' (the metal fastener) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
