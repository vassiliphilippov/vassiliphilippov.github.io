Sir Edward Elgar

1. PROPN

/ sɜr ˌɛd.wɚd ˈɛl.ɡɑr / (AmE), / sə ˌɛd.wəd ˈɛl.ɡɑː / (BrE)
(1857–1934) English composer known for choral and orchestral works, including the Enigma Variations and the Pomp and Circumstance marches.
English composer (1857–1934)

Examples
