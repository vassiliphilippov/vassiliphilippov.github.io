Dari

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑri / (AmE), / ˈdɑːri / (BrE)
an Iranian language spoken in Afghanistan, serving as one of the country’s two official languages
Iranian language of Afghanistan

Examples

Many people in Kabul speak Dari as their first language.
The government documents are published in both Pashto and Dari.
She is fluent in Dari, as well as English and French.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Persian - the Iranian language of Iran, closely related to Dari

Etymology

From Persian دری‎ (darī), originally meaning court (language of the royal court).
