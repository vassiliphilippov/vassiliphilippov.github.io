Nicola Amati

1. PROPN

/ niˈkoʊlə ɑːˈmɑːti / (AmE), / nɪˈkəʊlə əˈmɑːti / (BrE)
(1596–1684) An Italian violin maker from Cremona, known for his craftsmanship and for teaching Guarneri and Stradivari.
Italian violin maker (1596–1684)

Examples

The craftsmanship of Nicola Amati set the standard for violin making in the 17th century.
Many famous luthiers, including Stradivari, studied under Nicola Amati in Cremona.
A violin attributed to Nicola Amati is considered a valuable collector's item.
