parchesi

1. NOUN

/ pɑrˈtʃiːzi / (AmE), / pɑːˈtʃiːzi / (BrE)
an ancient board game resembling backgammon, played on a cross-shaped board
an ancient board game resembling backgammon

Examples

Every Sunday, we gather as a family to play parchesi in the living room.
The rules of parchesi are easy to learn, making it a favorite game among children.
He bought a colorful new parchesi board during his trip to India.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backgammon - a board game for two players involving moving pieces according to dice rolls

Etymology

From Hindi 'pachisi', the name of the Indian game on which parchisi is based.
