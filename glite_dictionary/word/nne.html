NNE

1. NOUN

/ ɛn ɛn i / (AmE), / ɛn ɛn i / (BrE)
the direction or compass point midway between north and northeast, at an angle of 22.5° east of due north
compass point between north and northeast

Examples

The boat turned to face NNE as the weather changed.
Winds are expected from the NNE at 15 miles per hour tonight.
He marked their position as 10 miles NNE of the island.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: north - the direction corresponding to 0° on a compass
  • Degree Higher: northeast - the direction or point on the compass halfway between north and east

Etymology

Acronym of 'north-northeast'.
