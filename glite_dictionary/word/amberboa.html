Amberboa

1. NOUN

/ ˌæm.bərˈboʊ.ə / (AmE), / ˌæm.bəˈbəʊ.ə / (BrE)
a genus of flowering herbs native to the Mediterranean and central Asia, cultivated for their showy flowers
genus of flowering herbs

Examples

Many gardeners cultivate Amberboa for its vibrant purple blooms.
The Amberboa species are well suited to dry, sunny locations.
Botanists studied several varieties of Amberboa during their trip to central Asia.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin Amberboa, a modern botanical name assigned to the genus.
