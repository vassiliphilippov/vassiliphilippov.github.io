Luxembourger

1. NOUN

/ ˌlʌksəmˈbɜːrɡər / (AmE), / ˌlʌksəmˈbɜːɡə / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Luxembourg
person from Luxembourg

Examples

As a proud Luxembourger, she often shares stories about her homeland.
There are only a few hundred thousand Luxembourgers living in the country.
The museum exhibit highlighted the traditions of Luxembourger culture.

Etymology

From Luxembourg + -er, denoting origin or association.
