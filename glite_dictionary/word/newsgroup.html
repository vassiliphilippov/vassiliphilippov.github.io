newsgroup

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌɡruːp / (BrE)
an online discussion forum dedicated to a specific topic, allowing users to post and read messages on computer networks, especially the internet
online discussion forum on a specific topic

Examples

Many technology enthusiasts participate in newsgroups to share their knowledge and ask questions.
Before social media, newsgroups were a popular platform for community discussions online.
She subscribed to several newsgroups about programming and computer security.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forum - an online message board or site for discussion

Etymology

Blend of 'news' and 'group', referring to a group for sharing news or information online.
