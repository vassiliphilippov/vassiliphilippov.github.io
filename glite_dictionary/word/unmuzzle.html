to unmuzzle

1. VERB

/ ʌnˈmʌzəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈmʌzəl / (BrE)
to remove a muzzle from, especially from a dog
remove a muzzle from (a dog)

Examples

The owner decided to unmuzzle the dog after they left the park.
Make sure you unmuzzle your dog gently to avoid startling it.
I watched as the handler unmuzzled the German shepherd for its break.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: muzzle - a device placed over an animal's mouth to prevent biting

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'muzzle'.
