Chagga

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑːɡə / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɡə / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken by the Chaga people in northern Tanzania
Bantu language of northern Tanzania

Examples

The Chagga language is still widely spoken on the slopes of Mount Kilimanjaro.
She learned basic greetings in Chagga before traveling to Tanzania.
Many songs and oral histories are preserved in Chagga.

Etymology

From the name of the Chaga people of northern Tanzania.
