Eye-talian

1. ADJ

/ aɪˈtæliən / (AmE), / aɪˈtæliən / (BrE)
A humorous or playful term for Italian cuisine, imitating a mispronunciation of 'Italian.'
humorous term for Italian food
informal american

Examples

I've always loved Eye-talian food, especially homemade pasta and pizza.
They joked about trying some authentic Eye-talian recipes for dinner.
He calls every Italian restaurant in town an Eye-talian joint.

Synset Examples

Her favorite cuisine is Eye-talian, rich in flavors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Italian - of or relating to Italy, its people, or its language

Etymology

Imitates a regional or exaggerated mispronunciation of 'Italian.'
