unembodied

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɛmˈbɑdid / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪmˈbɒdid / (BrE)
not having or existing in a physical or material body; incorporeal.
without a physical body

Examples

The novel explores the idea of unembodied consciousness after death.
Philosophers have debated whether minds can exist in an unembodied state.
She described her dream as an unembodied presence watching over her.

Synset Examples

bodiless ghosts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bodiless - without a body; incorporeal
  • Antonym: embodied - having a physical or material form

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'embodied' (given a physical form).
