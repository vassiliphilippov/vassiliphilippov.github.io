to overgorge

1. VERB

/ ˌoʊvərˈɡɔrdʒ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈɡɔːdʒ / (BrE)
To eat excessively or immoderately; to consume food beyond what is considered healthy or polite.
eat excessively or immoderately

Examples

He overgorged himself at the holiday buffet and felt ill all evening.
After the long hike, they overgorged on pizza and snacks.
Be careful not to overgorge during the festival; you'll regret it later.

Synset Examples

She stuffed herself at the dinner
The kids binged on ice cream

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to overeat - to eat more food than necessary or healthy
  • Synonym: binge - to eat or drink excessively in a short period

Etymology

From 'over-' (excessively) + 'gorge' (eat greedily or to excess), ultimately from Old French 'gorger'.
