grouchily

1. ADV

/ ˈɡraʊtʃɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊtʃɪli / (BrE)
In an ill-tempered or sulky manner.
in an ill-tempered way

Examples

He grouchily agreed to help after much persuasion.
She grouchily answered the phone early in the morning.
The child grouchily trudged to school on a rainy day.

Synset Examples

she looked at her husband crossly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grumpily - in a bad-tempered or sulky way

Etymology

From 'grouchy' (ill-tempered) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
