to intone

1. VERB

/ ɪnˈtoʊn / (AmE), / ɪnˈtəʊn / (BrE)
to say or chant something slowly with a regular rhythm and often in a dull or monotonous voice
say something in a measured, monotonous voice

Examples

The priest began to intone the evening prayers in the dimly lit chapel.
He would intone the rules in a flat, unchanging voice at every meeting.
As the bell rang, the teacher intoned the students' names in a rhythmic manner.

Synset Examples

The students chanted the same slogan over and over again

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chant - to say or shout repeatedly in a singsong tone
  • Synonym: to recite - to say aloud from memory, often formally

Etymology

From Latin 'intonare', from 'in-' (in) + 'tonare' (to thunder).

2. VERB

/ ɪnˈtoʊn / (AmE), / ɪnˈtəʊn / (BrE)
to say or recite something in a slow, even, and often musical tone, especially as a chant or in a ritual
recite in a slow, musical tone

Examples

At the ceremony, the priest began to intone the ancient prayers.
She would intone the names slowly, giving each one special significance.
Every evening, the monks intone their hymns in the chapel.

Synset Examples

The rabbi chanted a prayer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chant - to sing or recite in a rhythmic, musical tone

Etymology

From Latin 'intonare' (to thunder, to sing), from 'in-' (in) + 'tonare' (to thunder).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01051609-v
Synset POS: v

3. VERB

/ ɪnˈtoʊn / (AmE), / ɪnˈtəʊn / (BrE)
to say something in a slow and serious voice, with little variation in tone or pitch
to speak in a measured, monotone voice

Examples

The priest began to intone the opening words of the ceremony.
She would intone every sentence, making her stories sound almost like music.
He intoned the warning in a low, serious voice that caught everyone's attention.

Synset Examples

please intonate with sadness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chant - to sing or say something in a repetitive tone

Etymology

From Latin 'intonare' meaning 'to thunder in, chant' via French 'intoner'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01052185-v
Synset POS: v
