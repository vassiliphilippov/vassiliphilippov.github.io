craving

1. NOUN

/ ˈkreɪ.vɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkreɪ.vɪŋ / (BrE)
A strong or intense desire for something.
intense desire for something

Examples

She had a sudden craving for chocolate late at night.
After giving up smoking, he experienced intense cravings for cigarettes.
Pregnant women sometimes report unusual cravings for certain foods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desire - a strong feeling of wanting something
  • Antonym: aversion - a strong dislike or disinclination

Etymology

From Old English *cræfian* (to demand, beg), related to Middle Low German *kraven* (to demand).
