they/them (pronoun)

1. PRON

/ ðeɪ ðɛm / (AmE), / ðeɪ ðɛm / (BrE)
Used as gender-neutral singular pronouns to refer to a person whose gender is unknown, unspecified, or nonbinary.
gender-neutral singular pronouns for a person

Examples

When someone shares that they use 'they/them' pronouns, it's respectful to honor their preference.
Jordan said they would arrive by noon, so let's wait for them.
Each applicant must submit their portfolio if they wish to be considered.

Synset Examples

When referring to Alex, use they/them pronouns.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: he/him (pronoun) - Pronouns used to refer to a male individual
  • Antonym: she/her (pronoun) - Pronouns used to refer to a female individual
