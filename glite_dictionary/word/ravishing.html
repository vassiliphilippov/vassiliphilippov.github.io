ravishing

1. ADJ

/ ˈrævɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈravɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
extremely attractive or beautiful; stunningly lovely.
extremely beautiful or attractive

Examples

She looked absolutely ravishing in her red evening gown.
The scenic view from the mountain top is simply ravishing.
He couldn't help but stare at her ravishing smile.

Synset Examples

a ravishing blonde

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beautiful - pleasing the senses or mind aesthetically
  • Synonym: gorgeous - very beautiful or attractive
  • Antonym: plain - not beautiful or remarkable in appearance; ordinary

Etymology

From Middle English ravischen, from Old French raviss-, stem of ravir (to seize), from Latin rapere (to seize).
