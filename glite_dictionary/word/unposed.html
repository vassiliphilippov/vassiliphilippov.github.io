unposed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpoʊzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpəʊzd / (BrE)
(of a photograph or scene) not deliberately arranged or staged; natural and spontaneous.
not deliberately arranged; natural

Examples

The magazine featured several unposed shots of the actors backstage.
She prefers taking unposed pictures to capture genuine emotions.
Most of the wedding album was filled with candid, unposed moments.

Synset Examples

unposed photographs

Related Terms

  • Antonym: posed - deliberately arranged for effect, especially in photographs or scenes
