hugely

1. ADV

/ ˈhjuːdʒli / (AmE), / ˈhjuːdʒli / (BrE)
to a very great extent; extremely or enormously.
to a very great extent

Examples

The new movie was hugely successful at the box office.
She was hugely impressed by the team's hard work and dedication.
His research contributed hugely to advances in medical science.

Synset Examples

he was enormously popular

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enormously - to a very great degree; extremely
  • Synonym: vastly - to a very great extent

Etymology

From 'huge' + '-ly', forming an adverb from the adjective 'huge'.
