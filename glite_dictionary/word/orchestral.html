orchestral

1. ADJ

/ ɔrˈkɛstrəl / (AmE), / ɔːˈkɛstrəl / (BrE)
relating to or composed for an orchestra; characteristic of or suitable for performance by an orchestra.
relating to an orchestra

Examples

The new ballet features beautiful orchestral arrangements that highlight every section of the ensemble.
He prefers listening to orchestral versions of classic rock songs.
The film's soundtrack is richly orchestral, adding drama to each scene.

Synset Examples

orchestral score

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchestral - relating to an orchestra
  • Antonym: chamber - relating to chamber music, typically for a small group of instruments

Etymology

From orchestra + -al, denoting 'pertaining to an orchestra'.
