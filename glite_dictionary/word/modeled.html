modeled

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɑːdld / (AmE), / ˈmɒdld / (BrE)
(of a surface or feature) shaped or fashioned in a way that resembles sculpture; having clear, well-defined contours or form.
shaped like sculpture

Examples

The actress had modeled cheekbones that made her stand out on camera.
Sunlight highlighted the modeled muscles of the ancient statue.
His face had a modeled quality, as if carved by an artist.

Synset Examples

her finely modeled features
rendered with … vivid sculptural effect
the sculpturesque beauty of the athletes' bodies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sculptural - having the qualities of sculpture; shaped like a sculpture

Etymology

From the verb 'model', ultimately from Italian 'modello', meaning 'a model or pattern'.
