Sergei Rachmaninoff

1. PROPN

/ sɚˈgeɪ rækməˌnɔf / (AmE), / səˈgeɪ rækˈmænɪˌnɒf / (BrE)
(1873–1943) a Russian composer, pianist, and conductor known for his virtuosic piano works and Romantic style.
Russian composer and pianist (1873–1943)

Examples

Many musicians consider Sergei Rachmaninoff one of the greatest piano composers of the twentieth century.
The concert featured several works by Sergei Rachmaninoff, including his famous Piano Concerto No. 2.
As a pianist, Sergei Rachmaninoff was renowned for his exceptional technical skill and expressive playing.

Etymology

Transliteration of Russian Сергей Рахманинов (Sergey Rakhmaninov).
