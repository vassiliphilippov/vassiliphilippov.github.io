Levis

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːvaɪz / (AmE), / ˈliːvaɪz / (BrE)
A popular brand of denim jeans made by Levi Strauss & Co., often used to refer to jeans in general.
brand of denim jeans

Examples

He wore his favorite Levis to the party last night.
I've owned this pair of Levis for over ten years and they're still in great shape.
Whenever I need new jeans, I always buy Levis because they fit me best.

Etymology

From the name Levi Strauss, founder of Levi Strauss & Co. in the 19th century.
