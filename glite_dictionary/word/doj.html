DoJ

1. NOUN

/ di oʊ dʒeɪ / (AmE), / diː əʊ dʒeɪ / (BrE)
An abbreviation for the United States Department of Justice, the federal agency responsible for enforcing federal laws and overseeing justice administration.
US Department of Justice (initialism)
american

Examples

The DoJ announced new charges against several companies for violating antitrust laws.
Lawyers from the DoJ argued the case before the Supreme Court.
After a lengthy investigation, the DoJ released its findings to the public.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Department of Justice - the United States federal department responsible for law enforcement

Etymology

Initialism of 'Department of Justice'.
