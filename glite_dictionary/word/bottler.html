bottler

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑt̬lər / (AmE), / ˈbɒtlə / (BrE)
a company or person that manufactures and bottles beverages, especially soft drinks, for sale
beverage manufacturer and bottler

Examples

The local bottler has increased production to meet the summer demand for soda.
Many large beverage companies rely on independent bottlers to distribute their products.
After the merger, the new bottler expanded its offerings to include fruit juices and carbonated drinks.

Etymology

From 'bottle' (container), with the agent noun suffix '-er'.
