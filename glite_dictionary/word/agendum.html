agendum

1. NOUN

/ əˈdʒɛndəm / (AmE), / əˈdʒɛndəm / (BrE)
An item or matter to be considered or discussed, especially as part of a list for a meeting.
item for discussion at a meeting

Examples

The main agendum for today's meeting is the upcoming product launch.
Each agendum will be addressed in the order listed in the document.
Safety improvements were added as an agendum shortly before the board convened.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: agenda - a list of items to be discussed at a meeting

Etymology

Latin, singular of 'agenda', meaning 'thing to be done'.
