appreciably

1. ADV

/ əˈpriːʃəbli / (AmE), / əˈpriːʃəbli / (BrE)
to a noticeable or significant degree; enough to be observed or measured.
to a noticeable degree

Examples

House prices have risen appreciably in the past year.
His health improved appreciably after changing his diet.
The noise became appreciably louder as we approached the construction site.

Synset Examples

the weather was appreciably colder

Related Terms

  • Synonym: significantly - to a degree that is important or notable
  • Antonym: barely - only just; by a small margin

Etymology

From 'appreciable' + '-ly', with 'appreciable' from Late Latin 'appretiabilis'.
