Edgar Degas

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛdɡɑr deɪˈɡɑ / (AmE), / ˈeɡɑː deɪˈɡɑː / (BrE)
(1834–1917) a French painter and sculptor famous for his work with the Impressionist movement, especially known for his paintings of dancers.
French Impressionist painter and sculptor

Examples

Many art lovers admire Edgar Degas for his exquisite paintings of ballet dancers.
The museum's current exhibition features several works by Edgar Degas.
In her essay, she analyzed the unique techniques used by Edgar Degas in his impressionist masterpieces.
