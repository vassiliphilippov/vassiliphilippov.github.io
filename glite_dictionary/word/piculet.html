piculet

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪk.jʊ.lət / (AmE), / ˈpɪk.juː.lɛt / (BrE)
A small woodpecker found in South America, Africa, and the East Indies, distinguished by soft, rounded tail feathers.
small woodpecker with soft tail feathers

Examples

The ornithologist spotted a rare piculet in the South American rainforest.
Unlike many woodpeckers, the piculet uses its soft tail feathers for balance on branches.
The piculet's tapping could be heard across the quiet jungle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: woodpecker - a bird of the family Picidae known for pecking at wood

Etymology

From French *piculet*, diminutive of *pic* meaning 'woodpecker'.
