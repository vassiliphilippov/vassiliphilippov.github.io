thumbnail

1. NOUN

/ ˈθʌmˌneɪl / (AmE), / ˈθʌm.neɪl / (BrE)
the nail on the thumb of the hand
the nail of the thumb

Examples

She accidentally broke her thumbnail while opening the can.
The painter had a streak of blue paint under his thumbnail.
He trimmed his thumbnail shorter than the others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fingernail - the nail on any finger
  • Antonym: toenail - the nail on a toe

Etymology

From 'thumb' + 'nail', referring to the nail on the thumb.
