freely

1. ADV

/ ˈfriːli / (AmE), / ˈfriːli / (BrE)
In a free, unrestricted, or uninhibited manner.
in an unrestricted or uninhibited way

Examples

The artist moved the brush freely across the canvas, creating bold shapes.
Volunteers freely offered their time to help rebuild the community center.
She expressed her opinions freely during the discussion without fear of judgment.

Synset Examples

the painting featured freely brushed strokes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: openly - In an open manner; without concealment or secrecy
  • Antonym: restrictively - In a limited or controlled manner

Etymology

Formed from 'free' + '-ly', from Old English 'frēo' (free) and '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
