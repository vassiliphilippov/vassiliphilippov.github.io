luxuriance

1. NOUN

/ lʌgˈʒʊriəns / (AmE), / lʌgˈzjʊəriəns / (BrE)
the quality of being rich, lush, and abundant, often giving great pleasure to the senses
lush richness or abundance

Examples

The luxuriance of the tropical rainforest was breathtaking in its depth and color.
She admired the luxuriance of his garden, with its vibrant flowers and thick foliage.
The painting captured the luxuriance of summer, full of lush greenery and sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luxuriant - having rich and abundant growth, especially of vegetation
  • Antonym: barrenness - the state of being unproductive or lacking growth

Etymology

From Latin luxuriantia (exuberance, lushness), from luxurians (luxuriant).
