subfigure

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌbˌfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌfɪɡjə / (BrE)
a labeled part or section of a larger figure, especially in scientific or technical illustrations
part of a larger figure or diagram

Examples

Each subfigure in the diagram illustrates a different stage of the experiment.
Please refer to subfigure 2b for the temperature distribution results.
The article's main figure contains three subfigures labeled a, b, and c.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: figure - a drawing, diagram, or illustration, especially in academic work

Etymology

From Latin 'sub-' (under, part of) and 'figure' (shape, diagram).
