undifferentiated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɪˈfɛrənʃieɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈfɛrənʃieɪtɪd / (BrE)
not divided or distinguished into different types or categories; lacking distinct or specialized features.
not divided into types; lacking distinction

Examples

The cells remain undifferentiated until they receive specific development signals.
Many critics find the film’s characters undifferentiated and hard to relate to.
In geological terms, an undifferentiated rock mass shows no clear layering or subdivision.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: differentiated - distinct or made different in some way
  • Synonym: indistinct - not clearly marked or defined

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'differentiated', from Latin 'differentiare' (to make different).
