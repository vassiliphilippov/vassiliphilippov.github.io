crestfallen

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɛstˌfɑlən / (AmE), / ˈkrɛstˌfɔːlən / (BrE)
sad and disappointed, especially after having hopes or expectations dashed
sad and disappointed

Examples

She looked crestfallen when she heard the disappointing news.
After losing the match, the team walked off the field crestfallen.
He came home crestfallen, having failed the exam he had worked so hard for.

Synset Examples

left us fatigued and deflated spiritually

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dejected - sad and dispirited
  • Antonym: elated - very happy and excited

Etymology

From 'crest' (top of a helmet or animal’s head) + 'fallen', originally describing a bird with its crest drooping.
