rescindable

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈsɪndəbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈsɪndəbəl / (BrE)
Capable of being rescinded, annulled, or made void, especially by legal authority.
capable of being rescinded or annulled
legal

Examples

The contract was found to be rescindable under certain circumstances.
Only rescindable agreements can be canceled without penalty in this jurisdiction.
The judge declared the previous order rescindable due to procedural errors.

Synset Examples

the judgment was rescindable
voidable contracts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: voidable - capable of being voided or annulled

Etymology

From 'rescind' + '-able', modeled on Latin roots.
