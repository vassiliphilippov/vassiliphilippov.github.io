Petaurista petaurista

1. NOUN

/ pɛˌtɔrɪstə pɛˌtɔrɪstə / (AmE), / pɛˌtɔːrɪstə pɛˌtɔːrɪstə / (BrE)
A species of large flying squirrel native to Southeast Asia, also known as the red giant flying squirrel, recognized for its ability to glide between trees.
species of large Southeast Asian flying squirrel

Examples

The Petaurista petaurista is known for its impressive gliding abilities among forest canopies.
Researchers observed a Petaurista petaurista building its nest high in the trees.
Unlike other rodents, Petaurista petaurista can travel long distances between trees without touching the ground.

Etymology

From Modern Latin, combining genus 'Petaurista' and species 'petaurista', referring to its classification.
