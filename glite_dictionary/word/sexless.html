sexless

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛksləs / (AmE), / ˈsɛksləs / (BrE)
Not sexually attractive or lacking sexual appeal.
not sexually attractive

Examples

The play portrayed a sexless marriage that left both partners feeling isolated.
He felt awkward and sexless in the company of his glamorous friends.
Her outfit was deliberately sexless, emphasizing comfort over allure.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: attractive - pleasing or appealing, especially in a sexual way

Etymology

sex + -less (lacking), from Old English -lēas.

2. ADJ

/ ˈsɛksləs / (AmE), / ˈsɛksləs / (BrE)
having no sexual desire or interest in sex
having no sexual desire

Examples

He had always been a sexless person, never showing interest in romantic relationships.
After the treatment, she felt almost completely sexless and indifferent to intimacy.
Some people live happy and fulfilling sexless lives without any desire for sexual activity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asexual - having no sexual feelings or desires
  • Antonym: libidinous - showing excessive sexual drive; lustful

Etymology

From 'sex' + '-less', Old English '-lēas' meaning 'without'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02142474-s
Synset POS: s

3. ADJ

/ ˈsɛksləs / (AmE), / ˈsɛksləs / (BrE)
having no, or imperfectly developed or nonfunctional, sex organs.
having no or nonfunctional sex organs

Examples

Some plants are sexless and reproduce through other means besides flowers.
The scientist studied a sexless worm that could not produce offspring.
Certain medical conditions can result in a person being born sexless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asexual - not involving sexual organs or processes; lacking sexual characteristics

Etymology

From 'sex' (biological distinction) + '-less' (without).
