gayal

1. NOUN

/ ɡaɪˈɑl / (AmE), / ɡaɪˈɑːl / (BrE)
a large domesticated ox of Southeast Asia, sometimes considered a breed of gaur, known for its robust build and gentle temperament
domesticated ox of Southeast Asia

Examples

The gayal is prized by local farmers for its strength and docile nature.
Conservationists are working to protect the gayal in its native habitat.
The herd of gayals grazed peacefully on the hillside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gaur - large wild ox native to South and Southeast Asia

Etymology

From Hindi 'गयाल' (gayāl), likely of Tibeto-Burman origin.
