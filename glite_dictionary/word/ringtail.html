ringtail

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪŋˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋ.teɪl / (BrE)
a monkey of Central and South America with thick hair on its head resembling a monk's cowl
monkey with thick head hair, Central/South America

Examples

The ringtail is known for its distinctive mane that looks like a hood.
While traveling in Costa Rica, we spotted a group of playful ringtails in the forest canopy.
Researchers observed the ringtail feeding on fruits high in the treetops.

Examples

The ringtail darted across the rocks and disappeared into the shadows.
Zoo visitors were fascinated by the ringtail's striped tail as it climbed through the branches.
Researchers tracked the ringtail to study its nocturnal habits in the desert.
