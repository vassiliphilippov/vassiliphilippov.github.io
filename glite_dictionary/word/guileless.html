guileless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡaɪl.lɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪl.lɪs / (BrE)
Innocent and free from cunning or deceit; sincere and straightforward.
innocent; free of deceit

Examples

Her guileless smile made everyone trust her instantly.
He was guileless in his intentions, never trying to trick anyone.
Children are often guileless, speaking honestly without any hidden motives.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: deceitful - intending to deceive or mislead
  • Synonym: innocent - not guilty of a crime or offense; pure

Etymology

From 'guile' (deceit, trickery) + '-less' (without).
