ginmill

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪnˌmɪl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪnˌmɪl / (BrE)
an informal or slang term for a bar or cheap establishment where alcoholic drinks are served
slang for a bar or saloon
slang informal american

Examples

After work, they met at the local ginmill to unwind and share stories.
He spent most nights drinking at a rundown ginmill on the corner.
The old ginmill was crowded with noisy regulars and the clink of glasses.

Synset Examples

he drowned his sorrows in whiskey at the bar

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bar - an establishment where alcoholic drinks are served over a counter
  • Synonym: saloon - a public room or establishment for drinking and socializing

Etymology

From 'gin' (a type of alcoholic drink) + 'mill' (suggesting a place of production or activity); popularized in American slang in the early 20th century.
