Jonathan Trumbull

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɑnəθən ˈtrʌmbəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒnəθən ˈtrʌmbəl / (BrE)
(1710–1785) American Revolutionary leader who served as governor of Connecticut and provided supplies for the Continental Army.
American Revolutionary leader; Connecticut governor

Examples

Jonathan Trumbull was known for his steadfast support of the Revolutionary cause.
The Continental Army relied on the supplies provided by Jonathan Trumbull and the state of Connecticut.
Many historians regard Jonathan Trumbull as an essential figure in the American Revolution.
