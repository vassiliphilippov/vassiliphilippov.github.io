iPad

1. PROPN

/ ˈaɪˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌpad / (BrE)
a brand of tablet computer designed and marketed by Apple Inc.
Apple's brand of tablet computer
american

Examples

She bought an iPad for her daughter as a birthday gift.
I often use my iPad to read e-books and watch movies.
The teacher uses an iPad in class to display interactive lessons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tablet - a portable, flat, touch-screen computer

Etymology

Blend of 'internet' and 'pad', created by Apple Inc.
