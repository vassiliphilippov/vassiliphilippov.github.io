rima oris

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːmə ˈɔrɪs / (AmE), / ˈriːmə ˈɔːrɪs / (BrE)
In anatomy, the opening of the mouth through which food enters and vocal sounds are emitted.
mouth opening (anatomy)
medical

Examples

During the examination, the doctor inspected the rima oris for any signs of infection.
Certain diseases can cause swelling at the rima oris, making eating difficult.
A laceration near the rima oris may require immediate medical attention.

Synset Examples

he stuffed his mouth with candy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mouth - the opening in the face used for eating and speaking

Etymology

Latin: 'rima' (cleft, crack) + 'oris' (of the mouth).
