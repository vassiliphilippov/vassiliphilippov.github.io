Slovak

1. NOUN

/ ˈsloʊ.væk / (AmE), / ˈsləʊ.væk / (BrE)
the Slavic language spoken in Slovakia
Slavic language of Slovakia

Examples

He is fluent in both English and Slovak.
The book has been translated into Slovak for local readers.
She decided to learn Slovak before moving to Bratislava.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Czech - the West Slavic language closely related to Slovak, spoken in the Czech Republic

Etymology

From Slovak slovenský (relating to Slovakia), ultimately from Slovensko (Slovakia).

2. NOUN

/ ˈsloʊ.væk / (AmE), / ˈsləʊ.vak / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Slovakia
person from Slovakia

Examples

Many Slovaks celebrate traditional festivals with folk music and dancing.
She met a Slovak while traveling in Eastern Europe last summer.
As a proud Slovak, he often speaks of his country's rich history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Slovakian - a native or inhabitant of Slovakia

Etymology

From Slovak *Slovák*, meaning 'Slav' or 'Slovak person'.
