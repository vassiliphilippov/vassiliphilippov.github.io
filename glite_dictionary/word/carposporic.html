carposporic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑːr.poʊˈspɔːr.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɑː.pəˈspɔː.rɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling a carpospore, a type of spore produced by red algae.
relating to a carpospore (botany)

Examples

The carposporic stage is crucial in the life cycle of red algae.
Researchers studied the carposporic development under laboratory conditions.
Some species can only be identified by examining their carposporic structures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpospore - a non-motile spore formed by red algae

Etymology

From 'carpospore' (fruit spore) with suffix '-ic' (pertaining to).
