Coelogyne

1. NOUN

/ siːˈlɒdʒɪni / (AmE), / siːˈlɒdʒɪni / (BrE)
a genus of orchids found in Asia, known for their clusters of fragrant, often delicately colored flowers
genus of Asian orchids with fragrant flowers

Examples

The Coelogyne orchid is prized for its elegant, scented blooms.
Botanists discovered a new species within the Coelogyne genus in the rainforests of Borneo.
She added a rare Coelogyne to her collection of unusual orchids.

Etymology

From Greek 'koilos' (hollow) and 'gynē' (woman, referring to the lip structure of the flower).
