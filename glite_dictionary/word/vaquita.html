vaquita

1. NOUN

/ vəˈkiːtə / (AmE), / vəˈkiːtə / (BrE)
a small, endangered porpoise found only in the Gulf of California
small endangered porpoise of Gulf of California

Examples

The vaquita is the world's most endangered marine mammal.
Conservationists are working hard to protect the remaining vaquitas from extinction.
Illegal fishing practices have drastically reduced the vaquita population in recent years.

Etymology

From Spanish 'vaquita', diminutive of 'vaca' (cow), probably because of the animal's appearance.
