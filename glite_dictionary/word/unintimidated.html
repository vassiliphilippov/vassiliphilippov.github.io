unintimidated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪnˈtɪməˌdeɪtəd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not feeling or showing fear in the face of danger or opposition; not intimidated.
not feeling intimidated or afraid

Examples

She stood unintimidated by the angry crowd, refusing to back down from her beliefs.
Despite the threats, the journalist remained unintimidated and continued to report the truth.
His unintimidated attitude inspired others to voice their concerns openly.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intimidated - feeling frightened or overawed, especially in order to make someone do what one wants
  • Synonym: fearless - not afraid of anything; very brave

Etymology

Formed from the prefix un- (not) + intimidated.
