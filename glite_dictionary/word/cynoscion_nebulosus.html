Cynoscion nebulosus

1. NOUN

/ saɪˌnɒsɪˌɒn nəˈbjuːləsəs / (AmE), / sɪˌnɒsiˈɒn nɛˈbjuːləsəs / (BrE)
A species of fish, also known as the spotted seatrout or speckled trout, found along the southern Atlantic and Gulf Coasts of the United States.
spotted seatrout fish species

Examples

Anglers along the Gulf Coast often catch Cynoscion nebulosus for sport and food.
The Cynoscion nebulosus population is an important part of the region's coastal ecosystem.
Researchers studied the spawning habits of Cynoscion nebulosus in estuarine environments.

Etymology

From Greek 'kyon' (dog) and 'skion' (a kind of fish); 'nebulosus' is Latin for 'cloudy' or 'misty'.
