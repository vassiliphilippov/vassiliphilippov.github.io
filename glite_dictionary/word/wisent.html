wisent

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪzənt / (AmE), / ˈviːzənt / (BrE)
A large European bison, closely related to the American bison but having a smaller and higher head.
European bison species

Examples

The wisent once roamed the forests of Europe in large herds.
Conservation efforts have helped increase the wisent population in Poland.
The wisent differs from its American relative in both size and the shape of its head.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bison - A large wild animal with a shaggy mane and large head, native to North America and Europe

Etymology

From German 'Wisent', from Old High German 'wisant', related to bison.
