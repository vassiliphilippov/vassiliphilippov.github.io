gran

1. NOUN

/ ɡræn / (AmE), / ɡran / (BrE)
(informal) your grandmother; the mother of your father or mother.
grandmother (informal)
informal

Examples

My gran used to bake the best apple pies every Sunday.
Whenever I visit my gran, she tells me stories about her childhood.
I called my gran to wish her a happy birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandmother - the mother of one's father or mother
  • Antonym: granddad - informal term for grandfather

Etymology

Shortened form of 'grandmother'.
