Turkey

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɝː.ki / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.ki / (BrE)
A country in both southeastern Europe and western Asia, officially known as the Republic of Turkey, established as a republic in 1923 after the collapse of the Ottoman Empire.
country in southeastern Europe and western Asia

Examples

Istanbul is one of the largest cities in Turkey and a major cultural center.
After traveling through Greece, they continued their journey into Turkey.
The government of Turkey introduced new economic reforms in recent years.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'Turcia', from Turkish 'Türkiye'.
