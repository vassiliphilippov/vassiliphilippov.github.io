Hans Bethe

1. PROPN

/ hɑnz ˈbeɪtə / (AmE), / hanz ˈbeɪtə / (BrE)
(1906–2005) United States physicist, born in Germany, known for his research in astrophysics and nuclear physics.
German-American physicist (1906–2005)

Examples

Hans Bethe made significant contributions to the understanding of nuclear fusion in stars.
The Nobel Prize in Physics was awarded to Hans Bethe in 1967 for his theoretical work.
Many students in physics admire the legacy of Hans Bethe.
