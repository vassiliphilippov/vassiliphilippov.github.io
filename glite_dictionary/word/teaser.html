teaser

1. NOUN

/ ˈtizər / (AmE), / ˈtiːzə / (BrE)
an attention-grabbing segment or introduction shown at the beginning of a television show to attract viewers' interest
TV show's attention-grabbing opening

Examples

The episode began with a short teaser that hinted at the main mystery.
Before the opening credits, there's often a teaser to engage the audience.
The producers created a dramatic teaser to boost ratings for the new series.

Examples
