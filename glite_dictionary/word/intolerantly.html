intolerantly

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈtɑːlərəntli / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɒlərəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows unwillingness to accept views, beliefs, or behaviors that differ from one's own.
in an intolerant way

Examples

He spoke intolerantly about anyone who disagreed with his opinions.
The committee acted intolerantly towards new and unconventional ideas.
Some political leaders behave intolerantly during heated debates.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tolerantly - in a tolerant way

2. ADV

/ ɪnˈtɑːlərəntli / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɒlərəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows unwillingness to accept views, beliefs, or behavior that differ from one's own.
in an unaccepting or narrow-minded manner

Examples

He reacted intolerantly to any opinion that differed from his own.
The group spoke intolerantly about those with different beliefs.
She behaved intolerantly when confronted with unfamiliar customs.

Synset Examples

his illiberally biased way of thinking

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tolerantly - in a way that shows willingness to accept others' views or behavior
