fawn-colored

1. ADJ

/ fɔn ˈkʌlɚd / (AmE), / fɔːn ˈkʌləd / (BrE)
having a pale yellowish-brown color, like that of a young deer
pale yellowish-brown color

Examples

She chose a fawn-colored dress to wear to the spring picnic.
The walls of the living room were painted a soft fawn-colored shade.
A fawn-colored puppy slept quietly in the corner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tan - a pale brown color, similar to fawn-colored

Etymology

From 'fawn' (a young deer) and 'colored', referring to the animal's typical coat.
