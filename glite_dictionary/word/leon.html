Leon

1. PROPN

/ leɪˈɒn / (AmE), / leɪˈɒn / (BrE)
a city in northwestern Spain at the base of the Cantabrian Mountains, known for its historical architecture and cultural heritage
city in northwestern Spain

Examples

Many tourists visit Leon to admire its stunning Gothic cathedral.
The train from Madrid arrives at Leon in about three hours.
Leon hosts an annual festival celebrating its medieval heritage.

Etymology

From Latin 'Legio', originally a Roman military camp established by the VI Victrix Legion.
