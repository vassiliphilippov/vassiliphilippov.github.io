Damascus

1. PROPN

/ dəˈmæskəs / (AmE), / dəˈmɑːskəs / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Syria, regarded as one of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities
capital city of Syria

Examples

Many pilgrims have traveled to Damascus to visit its historic sites.
The bustling markets of Damascus are famous throughout the Middle East.
Paul's conversion to Christianity is said to have happened on the road to Damascus.

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Damaskos, from Aramaic Damméseq.
