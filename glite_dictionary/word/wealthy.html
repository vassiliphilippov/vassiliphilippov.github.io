wealthy

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛlθi / (AmE), / ˈwɛlθi / (BrE)
having a great deal of money, resources, or valuable possessions; rich
rich; having much money or property

Examples

Many wealthy individuals donate large sums of money to charity.
She grew up in a wealthy neighborhood on the east side of the city.
After years of hard work, he finally became wealthy and traveled the world.

Synset Examples

an affluent banker
a speculator flush with cash
not merely rich but loaded
moneyed aristocrats
wealthy corporations
a substantial family

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rich - having a great deal of money or assets
  • Antonym: poor - having little or no money or possessions

Etymology

From wealth + -y, dating from Middle English 'welethy' (rich in happiness), now meaning rich in wealth.
