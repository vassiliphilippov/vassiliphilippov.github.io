bardic

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑrdɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɑːdɪk / (BrE)
relating to a bard or to the poetry and traditions of bards.
of or relating to a bard

Examples

Several scholars have studied the influence of bardic traditions on Celtic literature.
Her performance captured the spirit of ancient bardic poetry.
The festival featured a competition for the best bardic verse.

Synset Examples

bardic poetry

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bard - a poet, especially one reciting epics and associated with a particular oral tradition

Etymology

From 'bard' (poet-singer), ultimately from Old Celtic 'bardos'.
