free-living

1. ADJ

/ ˈfriˌlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an organism) living independently and not as a parasite on another organism.
living independently; not parasitic

Examples

Some bacteria are free-living, while others cause diseases in plants or animals.
The pond is filled with free-living protozoa that do not depend on a host.
Scientists studied the differences between parasitic and free-living nematodes.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: parasitic - (of an organism) living as a parasite
