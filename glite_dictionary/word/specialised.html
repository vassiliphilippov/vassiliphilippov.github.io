specialised

1. ADJ

/ ˈspɛʃəlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃəlaɪzd / (BrE)
designed or developed for a particular activity, function, or purpose; not general
designed for a specific purpose
british

Examples

Only specialised equipment can perform this kind of delicate surgery.
The university offers several specialised courses in marine biology.
He works for a specialised firm that designs custom medical implants.

Synset Examples

a specialized tool

Related Terms

  • Synonym: specialized - American English spelling of 'specialised'
  • Antonym: general - not limited to a specific activity or function; applicable to many things

Etymology

From 'special' (Middle English, from Old French *especial*, from Latin *specialis*) + '-ise/-ize' suffix.
