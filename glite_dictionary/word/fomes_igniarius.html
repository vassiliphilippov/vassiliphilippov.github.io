Fomes igniarius

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊmiːz ɪɡˈnaɪəriəs / (AmE), / ˈfəʊmiːz ɪɡˈnaɪəriəs / (BrE)
a species of fungus used in the preparation of tinder, especially for fuses
a fungus used as tinder

Examples

Early firefighters used Fomes igniarius as a natural source of tinder for starting flames.
The shelf-like bodies of Fomes igniarius can often be found growing on old birch trees.
Researchers studied how Fomes igniarius was traditionally used in making punk for fuses.

Etymology

From Latin; 'fomes' meaning tinder and 'igniarius' relating to fire.
