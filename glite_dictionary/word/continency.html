continency

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːn.tɪ.nən.si / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tɪ.nən.si / (BrE)
self-restraint, especially with regard to sexual activity; abstinence or moderation in sexual matters
self-restraint in sexual matters

Examples

Despite many temptations, he practiced continency throughout his life.
Religious texts often praise the virtue of continency and urge followers to adopt it.
She admired his continency in an age that celebrated indulgence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abstinence - the practice of restraining oneself from indulgence, especially in alcohol or sex
  • Synonym: chastity - the state or practice of refraining from extramarital, or especially from all, sexual intercourse

Etymology

From Latin 'continentia' (restraint, moderation).
