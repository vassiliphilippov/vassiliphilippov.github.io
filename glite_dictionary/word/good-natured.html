good-natured

1. ADJ

/ ɡʊdˈneɪtʃərd / (AmE), / ɡʊdˈneɪtʃəd / (BrE)
Having a pleasant, easygoing, and cheerful disposition; friendly and agreeable.
cheerful and friendly disposition

Examples

She is known for her good-natured sense of humor and willingness to help others.
The good-natured dog greeted everyone who came to the house with a wagging tail.
Despite the teasing, his response was always good-natured and never offended anyone.

Synset Examples

too good-natured to resent a little criticism
the good-natured policeman on our block
the sounds of good-natured play

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amiable - having or displaying a friendly and pleasant manner
  • Antonym: ill-natured - having a bad or unpleasant temperament

Etymology

From 'good' + 'natured', indicating a kind and pleasant temperament.
