motortruck

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊtərˌtrʌk / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəˌtrʌk / (BrE)
an automotive vehicle designed for transporting goods; a truck
automotive vehicle for hauling; truck

Examples

The company uses a motortruck to deliver supplies to all its stores.
He learned to drive a motortruck when he was eighteen.
After the storm, a large motortruck removed the fallen trees from the road.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: truck - a motor vehicle for carrying goods and materials

Etymology

From 'motor' (engine-powered) + 'truck' (vehicle for carrying goods).
