homophobic

1. ADJ

/ hoʊməˈfoʊbɪk / (AmE), / həʊməˈfəʊbɪk / (BrE)
having or showing a dislike of or prejudice against homosexual people.
prejudiced against homosexual people

Examples

The manager was fired after making several homophobic remarks during a staff meeting.
She decided not to attend the event after learning about its homophobic undertones.
Peaceful demonstrations were held in response to the homophobic legislation passed by the government.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homophobia - dislike of or prejudice against homosexual people
  • Antonym: tolerant - showing openness or acceptance of differences

Etymology

From 'homo-' (homosexual) + '-phobic' (having a fear or aversion), modeled after 'homophobia'.
