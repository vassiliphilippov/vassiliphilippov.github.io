Cary Grant

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɛri ɡrænt / (AmE), / ˈkæri ɡrɑːnt / (BrE)
(1904–1986) a British-born American actor known for his charm and sophisticated roles in classic Hollywood films.
leading man in classic films

Examples

Cary Grant starred in "North by Northwest," which remains a classic thriller.
Many critics consider Cary Grant one of the greatest actors of Hollywood's golden age.
She collected movie posters featuring Cary Grant from the 1940s and 1950s.
