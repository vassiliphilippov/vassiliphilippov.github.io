saporous

1. ADJ

/ ˈsæpərəs / (AmE), / ˈsæpərəs / (BrE)
Having a strong or pleasant taste; full of flavor.
full of flavor; flavorful

Examples

The chef's new dish was surprisingly saporous, bursting with a rich blend of spices.
She prefers saporous cheeses over mild ones for their intense taste.
Fresh herbs make this soup especially saporous and delightful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flavorful - having a lot of flavor
  • Antonym: bland - lacking strong flavor

Etymology

From Latin 'saporosus', from 'sapor' meaning 'taste' or 'flavor'.
