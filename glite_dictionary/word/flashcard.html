flashcard

1. NOUN

/ ˈflæʃ.kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈflæʃ.kɑːd / (BrE)
A small card bearing information, such as words, numbers, or pictures, shown briefly to aid learning or memorization.
card used for learning or memorization

Examples

The teacher held up a flashcard with the word 'apple' for the students to read.
Using a flashcard app on your phone can help you memorize vocabulary efficiently.
Before the test, he reviewed every flashcard in the stack.

Etymology

Formed from 'flash' (to show briefly) + 'card'.
