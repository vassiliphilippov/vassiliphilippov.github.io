ooga booga

1. INTJ

/ ˈuːɡə ˈbuːɡə / (AmE), / ˈuːɡə ˈbuːɡə / (BrE)
an exclamation used playfully to frighten someone or as a silly, nonsensical expression
playful or silly exclamation
informal

Examples

He jumped out from behind the sofa and shouted ooga booga to startle his sister.
During the costume party, everyone started dancing and yelling ooga booga for fun.
With a mischievous grin, she whispered ooga booga in a spooky voice.

Synset Examples

She playfully yelled ooga booga to scare her friends.
The kids shouted ooga booga during their game.
