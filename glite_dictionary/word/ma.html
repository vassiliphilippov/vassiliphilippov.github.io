ma

1. NOUN

/ mɑ / (AmE), / mɑː / (BrE)
(informal) one’s mother; used as a familiar or affectionate term for a mother.
informal term for mother
informal

Examples

I called my ma to tell her the good news.
Whenever I’m sick, my ma makes me soup.
Did you remember to thank your ma for the gift?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mom - informal term for mother, chiefly American English
  • Synonym: mum - informal term for mother, chiefly British English
  • Synonym: mother - a woman in relation to her child or children
  • Antonym: pa - informal term for father

Etymology

Variant of 'mama' or 'mamma', from early childhood speech; similar forms found in many languages.
