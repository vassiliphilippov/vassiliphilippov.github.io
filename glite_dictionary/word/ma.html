MA

1. NOUN

/ ɛm eɪ / (AmE), / ɛm eɪ / (BrE)
abbreviation for Massachusetts, a state in the northeastern United States, with capital Boston; one of the original thirteen colonies.
abbreviation for Massachusetts (US state)
american

Examples

She grew up in Cambridge, MA, before moving to California.
Harvard University is located in Boston, MA.
The package was shipped to an address in Springfield, MA.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Massachusetts - a state in New England, northeastern US, with capital Boston

Etymology

Abbreviation of Massachusetts, first used in US postal codes.

2. NOUN

/ ˌɛm ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌem ˈɑː / (BrE)
a master's degree in arts and sciences, typically awarded after completing postgraduate study
master's degree in arts and sciences

Examples

After earning her MA in history, she decided to pursue a PhD.
He listed his MA from Oxford University on his résumé.
Applicants must have an MA or equivalent qualification to be considered for the position.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: master's degree - an academic degree awarded after a bachelor's degree

Etymology

Abbreviation of Latin 'Magister Artium' (Master of Arts).

Synset POS: n
