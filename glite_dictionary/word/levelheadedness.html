levelheadedness

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɛvəlˈhɛdɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌlɛvəlˈhɛdɪdnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being calm, sensible, and able to make good decisions, especially in difficult situations.
the quality of having sound judgment

Examples

Her levelheadedness helped the team navigate the crisis without panic.
Despite the stressful environment, his levelheadedness inspired confidence in everyone around him.
The manager's levelheadedness was crucial during the negotiations with the new clients.

Synset Examples

It is a tribute to her levelheadedness and strength that despite the most bizarre life imaginable after her childhood, she remained intact, true to herself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: composure - calmness and self-control, especially in a difficult situation
  • Antonym: rashness - the quality of acting without careful consideration

Etymology

From 'levelheaded' + '-ness', where 'levelheaded' likely derives from the metaphor of the head being 'level' or steady.
