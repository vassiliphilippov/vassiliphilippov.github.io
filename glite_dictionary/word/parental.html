parental

1. ADJ

/ pəˈrɛntəl / (AmE), / pəˈrɛntəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a parent or parents
relating to parents

Examples

The school requires parental consent for all field trips.
The movie was rated PG for parental guidance.
Her parental duties made her schedule very busy.

Synset Examples

parental guidance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maternal - relating to a mother
  • Synonym: paternal - relating to a father

Etymology

From Latin 'parentalis', from 'parens' (parent).

2. ADJ

/ pəˈrɛntəl / (AmE), / pəˈrɛntəl / (BrE)
relating to or designating the generation of organisms from which hybrid offspring are produced.
of the parent generation in biology

Examples

The parental generation in this experiment consists of purebred tall and dwarf pea plants.
Scientists observed how traits are inherited from the parental organisms to their hybrid offspring.
In a Punnett square, the alleles from the parental generation are shown at the top and side.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: filial - relating to the generation(s) resulting from the parental generation (offspring, especially in genetics)

Etymology

From Latin 'parentalis', from 'parens' meaning parent.
