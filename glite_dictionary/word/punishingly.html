punishingly

1. ADV

/ ˈpʌnɪʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈpʌnɪʃɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that causes great difficulty, hardship, or discomfort.
in a harsh or severe manner

Examples

The marathon runners pushed through the punishingly hot weather to reach the finish line.
He trained punishingly hard, leaving little time for rest or recovery.
The hike was punishingly steep, challenging even experienced climbers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: severely - to a very great degree; harshly
  • Antonym: leniently - in a mild, gentle, or permissive manner
