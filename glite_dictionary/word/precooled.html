precooled

1. ADJ

/ priːˈkuːld / (AmE), / priːˈkuːld / (BrE)
Cooled in advance to a lower temperature before use.
cooled in advance

Examples

The precooled gas entered the chamber for further processing.
Make sure the syringes are precooled before starting the experiment.
She served the dessert in precooled bowls for a refreshing touch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chilled - cooled to a low temperature

Etymology

From prefix 'pre-' (before) + 'cooled' (past participle of cool).
