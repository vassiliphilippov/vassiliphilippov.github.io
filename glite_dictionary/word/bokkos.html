Bokkos

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːk.oʊs / (AmE), / ˈbɒk.ɒs / (BrE)
A Chadic language spoken in northern Nigeria, especially in Plateau State and neighboring areas.
Chadic language of Nigeria

Examples

The Bokkos language is one of many Chadic languages spoken in Plateau State, Nigeria.
Researchers have been documenting stories in Bokkos to help preserve the language.
Many children in the region grow up learning both Hausa and Bokkos.
