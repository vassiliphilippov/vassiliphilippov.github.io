Carpentaria

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɑːr.pənˈtɛr.i.ə / (AmE), / ˌkɑː.pənˈteə.ri.ə / (BrE)
a wide, shallow gulf of the Arafura Sea in northern Australia, bordered by Queensland and Northern Territory.
gulf in northern Australia

Examples

The coastline of Carpentaria is known for its stunning natural beauty.
Fishermen travel to Carpentaria for its rich marine life.
Tropical storms sometimes develop over the Gulf of Carpentaria.

Etymology

Named after the Dutch explorer Pieter de Carpentier.
