Yenisei-Samoyed

1. NOUN

/ ˌjɛnɪˌseɪ səˈmɔɪɛd / (AmE), / ˌjɛnɪˌseɪ səˈmɔɪɛd / (BrE)
the Uralic language spoken by the Yeniseians, historically in central Siberia
a Uralic language of central Siberia

Examples

Linguists are studying the grammar of Yenisei-Samoyed to better understand its historical development.
The Yenisei-Samoyed language is now considered extinct, with only a few historical records remaining.
Scholars believe that Yenisei-Samoyed shares some features with other Uralic languages.

Etymology

From Yenisei (a river and region in Siberia) + Samoyed (an older term for certain Uralic peoples).
