spitball

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪtˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈspɪt.bɔːl / (BrE)
A small ball of chewed paper, often made by chewing a piece of paper and shaping it into a sphere, typically used as a playful projectile.
chewed paper ball used as a projectile

Examples

During class, Jake secretly threw a spitball at his friend across the room.
After lunch, the desks were littered with spitballs and scraps of paper.
The teacher caught the student making a spitball and sent him to the principal's office.

Etymology

From 'spit' + 'ball', referring to a ball formed by spitting and rolling paper.

2. NOUN

/ ˈspɪtˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈspɪtbɔːl / (BrE)
(Baseball) An illegal pitch in which a foreign substance, such as saliva or Vaseline, is applied to the ball to alter its motion.
illegal baseball pitch with altered ball

Examples

The pitcher was ejected after being caught throwing a spitball during the game.
Veteran players sometimes joked about the old days when the spitball was still legal.
He developed a reputation for his skill with the spitball, baffling many batters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curveball - a baseball pitch that curves as it approaches the plate
  • Antonym: fastball - a baseball pitch thrown at high speed, relatively straight

3. VERB

/ ˈspɪtˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈspɪtˌbɔːl / (BrE)
to suggest ideas or brainstorm informally, typically in a relaxed or unstructured manner
suggest ideas informally; brainstorm
informal

Examples

We spent the afternoon spitballing ways to improve our new product.
During the meeting, everyone began to spitball ideas for the advertising campaign.
She's great at spitballing solutions when we hit a roadblock.

Synset Examples

Let's spitball a few solutions to this problem.
He decided to spitball some ideas during the brainstorming session.
The team began to spitball various marketing strategies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to brainstorm - to generate ideas freely, often in a group

Etymology

Originally from the noun 'spitball' (a paper ball moistened with saliva), used metaphorically for tossing ideas around.
