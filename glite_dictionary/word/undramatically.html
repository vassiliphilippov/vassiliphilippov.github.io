undramatically

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.drəˈmæt.ɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌʌn.drəˈmæt.ɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a way that is not dramatic; without drama or excitement.
without drama or excitement

Examples

She responded to the news undramatically, barely raising an eyebrow.
The meeting ended undramatically, with everyone quietly leaving the room.
He undramatically admitted his mistake, avoiding any emotional display.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dramatically - in a dramatic manner
