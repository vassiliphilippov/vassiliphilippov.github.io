Leontocebus rosalia

1. NOUN

/ liˌɑntəˈsibəs roʊˈzɑːliə / (AmE), / lɪˌɒntəˈsiːbəs rəʊˈzɑːliə / (BrE)
A small golden South American monkey with long, soft hair forming a mane, commonly known as the golden lion tamarin.
golden lion tamarin monkey

Examples

Conservation efforts have helped protect the Leontocebus rosalia from extinction in Brazil.
The striking mane of the Leontocebus rosalia makes it easily recognizable among other tamarins.
Zoos around the world collaborate to breed Leontocebus rosalia and support their wild populations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golden lion tamarin - common name for Leontocebus rosalia

Etymology

From New Latin: genus name 'Leontocebus' meaning 'lion monkey' and species name 'rosalia'.
