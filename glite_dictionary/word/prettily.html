prettily

1. ADV

/ ˈprɪt̬.ɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈprɪt.ɪ.li / (BrE)
In a way that is attractive or pleasing in appearance.
in a pretty or attractive way

Examples

The flowers were arranged prettily in the vase on the table.
Children dressed prettily for the spring festival entertained the crowd.
She smiled prettily at the compliment, clearly pleased.

Synset Examples

all this is most prettily done

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: beautifully - in a beautiful or very pleasing way
  • Antonym: ugly - in an unattractive or unpleasant way

Etymology

Formed from 'pretty' with the adverbial suffix '-ly'; 'pretty' from Old English 'prættig', meaning 'cunning, crafty'.
