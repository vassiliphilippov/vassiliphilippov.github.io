bulimarexia

1. NOUN

/ bjuːˌlɪməˈrɛksiə / (AmE), / bjuːˌlɪməˈrɛksiə / (BrE)
an eating disorder characterized by alternating periods of compulsive overeating and aversion to food, often involving self-induced vomiting or fasting
eating disorder with cycles of binging and aversion
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with bulimarexia after exhibiting both binge eating and fasting behaviors.
Many mental health professionals emphasize the dangers of bulimarexia and its impact on physical health.
Her struggle with bulimarexia led to severe nutritional deficiencies and required specialized treatment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulimia - eating disorder marked by binge eating and purging
  • Synonym: anorexia nervosa - eating disorder involving self-starvation and excessive weight loss

Etymology

Blend of 'bulimia' (from Greek boulimia, ravenous hunger) and 'anorexia' (from Greek anoreksia, lack of appetite).
