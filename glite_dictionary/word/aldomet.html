Aldomet

1. NOUN

/ ˈældəˌmɛt / (AmE), / ˈældəˌmɛt / (BrE)
A trademark for an antihypertensive drug containing methyldopa, used to treat high blood pressure.
trademarked antihypertensive drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Aldomet to help control her blood pressure.
Some patients experience drowsiness as a side effect of Aldomet.
After starting Aldomet, his hypertension was much better managed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: methyldopa - an antihypertensive medication; active ingredient in Aldomet
