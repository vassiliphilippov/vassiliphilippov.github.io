lxxxv

1. NUM

/ ɛl ɛks ɛks ɛks vi / (AmE), / ɛl ɛks ɛks ɛks vi / (BrE)
the number eighty-five written as a Roman numeral
Roman numeral for 85

Examples

The page number was marked as lxxxv in the book's introduction.
In ancient inscriptions, lxxxv often signified the age or year of an event.
She used lxxxv as a code for her favorite number.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 85 - the number eighty-five

Etymology

From Latin numerals: L (50) + XXX (30) + V (5).
