keyboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiːˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈkiːbɔːd / (BrE)
A panel of keys that operate a musical instrument, computer, typewriter, or similar device.
panel of keys for instruments or devices

Examples

She typed the entire report using her keyboard.
The piano keyboard has eighty-eight keys.
Dust had collected between the keys of his old computer keyboard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keypad - a small panel of keys, usually numeric, on a device

Etymology

From 'key' + 'board', first used in the context of musical instruments in the 17th century.
