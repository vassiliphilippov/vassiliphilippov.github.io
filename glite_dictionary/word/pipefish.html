pipefish

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪpˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪp.fɪʃ / (BrE)
A slender fish with a long tubular snout and a body covered with bony plates, related to seahorses.
a slender, tubular-snouted fish

Examples

The aquarium added a new pipefish exhibit showcasing their unique bodies.
A pipefish glided gracefully among the seagrass, barely visible to the eye.
Scientists study the mating habits of the pipefish due to their unusual reproductive roles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seahorse - a small marine fish with a horse-like head and curled tail
