inconclusively

1. ADV

/ ˌɪnkənˈklusɪvli / (AmE), / ˌɪnkənˈkluːsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that does not produce a clear or definite result or decision.
without a definite result

Examples

The negotiations ended inconclusively after several hours of debate.
The committee discussed the proposal inconclusively, without reaching any agreement.
Investigations into the incident have so far proceeded inconclusively.

Synset Examples

the meeting ended inconclusively

Related Terms

  • Antonym: conclusively - in a way that produces a clear or definite result
  • Synonym: inconclusive - not leading to a firm conclusion; not settling an issue
