roomily

1. ADV

/ ˈruːmɪli / (AmE), / ˈruːmɪli / (BrE)
In a manner that allows ample space; spaciously.
in a spacious manner

Examples

The furniture was roomily arranged to create an open and inviting space.
They danced roomily in the large hall, enjoying the freedom to move.
Books were roomily stacked on the wide shelves, making them easy to browse.

Synset Examples

the furniture was spaciously spread out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spaciously - in a spacious or roomy manner

Etymology

Formed from 'roomy' (spacious) + '-ly'.
