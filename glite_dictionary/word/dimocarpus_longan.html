Dimocarpus longan

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪmoʊˈkɑːrpəs ˈloʊŋɡən / (AmE), / ˌdɪməˈkɑːpəs ˈlɒŋən / (BrE)
A tropical tree native to Southeast Asia, chiefly grown for its sweet, edible fruit resembling the lychee.
tropical Asian fruit tree

Examples

Dimocarpus longan is widely cultivated across Southeast Asia for its delicious fruit.
The fruit of Dimocarpus longan is similar in taste and texture to lychee.
Researchers are studying the medicinal properties of Dimocarpus longan seeds and leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: longan - the edible fruit of the Dimocarpus longan tree
  • Degree Higher: lychee - a similar sweet fruit from Southeast Asia
