Meriones unguiculatus

1. NOUN

/ mɛrˈaɪ.əˌniːz ˌʌŋ.ɡwɪˈkjuː.lə.təs / (AmE), / mɛrˈɪ.ə.niːz ˌʌŋ.ɡwɪˈkjuː.lə.təs / (BrE)
A species of gerbil, commonly known as the Mongolian gerbil, often kept as a pet.
Mongolian gerbil species, popular pet

Examples

The Meriones unguiculatus is often chosen as an ideal pet for families with children.
Researchers studied the behavior of Meriones unguiculatus in both wild and domestic settings.
Pet shops typically refer to the Meriones unguiculatus simply as the Mongolian gerbil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gerbil - small rodent often kept as a pet
  • Synonym: Mongolian gerbil - common name for Meriones unguiculatus, a type of gerbil kept as a pet

Etymology

From Greek 'merion' (thigh) and Latin 'unguiculatus' (having claws).
