emasculated

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈmæskjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ɪˈmæskjʊˌleɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a male animal) having had the testicles removed; castrated.
(of a male animal) castrated
medical

Examples

The veterinarian ensured the stallion was emasculated before returning him to the herd.
After being emasculated, the bull became much calmer and easier to handle.
Farmers commonly keep emasculated animals to prevent uncontrolled breeding.

Synset Examples

a cut horse

Related Terms

  • Synonym: castrated - (of a male animal) having had the testicles removed

Etymology

From Latin *emasculatus*, past participle of *emasculare* (to castrate), from *e-* (out) + *masculus* (male).
