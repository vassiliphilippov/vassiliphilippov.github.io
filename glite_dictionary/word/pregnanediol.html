pregnanediol

1. NOUN

/ ˌprɛɡ.nəˈniː.ɑl / (AmE), / ˌprɛɡ.nəˈniː.ɒl / (BrE)
a compound found in the urine of women during certain phases of the menstrual cycle and of pregnant women, used as an indicator of progesterone metabolism
compound found in women's urine, pregnancy indicator
medical

Examples

High levels of pregnanediol may indicate increased progesterone production.
The laboratory tested her urine for pregnanediol as part of the fertility evaluation.
Elevated pregnanediol levels are commonly found during pregnancy.

Etymology

From pregnane (a steroid hydrocarbon) + -diol (indicating two alcohol groups).
