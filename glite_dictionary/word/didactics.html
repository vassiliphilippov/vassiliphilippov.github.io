didactics

1. NOUN

/ daɪˈdæktɪks / (AmE), / dɪˈdaktɪks / (BrE)
the art or science of teaching; the principles and methods of instruction
the study or method of teaching

Examples

He studied didactics to improve his teaching methods.
Modern didactics focuses on student engagement and active learning.
The professor wrote a book on didactics for new educators.

Synset Examples

he received no formal education
our instruction was carefully programmed
good classroom teaching is seldom rewarded

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pedagogy - the method and practice of teaching

Etymology

From Greek didaktikos (apt at teaching), related to didaskein (to teach).
