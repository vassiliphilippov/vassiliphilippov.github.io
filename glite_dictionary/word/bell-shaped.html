bell-shaped

1. ADJ

/ bɛl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / bɛl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a rounded, outwardly curved form similar to that of a bell
shaped like a bell

Examples

The flowers bloomed with bell-shaped petals that hung gracefully from the stems.
Clouds formed a bell-shaped pattern before the evening rain began.
The scientist observed microorganisms under a microscope with a distinctly bell-shaped body.
