provision

1. NOUN

/ prəˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / prəˈvɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
a supply or stock of something, especially food or other necessities, that is kept for future use
stored supply of necessities

Examples

The hikers made sure to pack enough provisions for their week-long trip.
During the storm, the family survived on the provisions stored in their basement.
The refugees depended on emergency provisions delivered by aid organizations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supply - an amount of something that is available for use
  • Synonym: stockpile - a large accumulated stock of goods or materials

Etymology

From Latin provisio (a foreseeing, providing), from providere (to provide).
