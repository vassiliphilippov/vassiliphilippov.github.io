2. NOUN

/ ˈkɪliwi / (AmE), / ˈkɪliwi / (BrE)
A member of a Native American people living in northern Baja California.
member of a Baja California Native American people

Examples

The Kiliwi have preserved many of their traditional customs despite external influences.
Archaeologists studied ancient tools made by the Kiliwi.
A member of the Kiliwi tribe shared stories about their ancestral lands.
