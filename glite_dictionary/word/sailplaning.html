sailplaning

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪlˌpleɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈseɪlˌpleɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of flying a glider for sport or recreation, typically without an engine
flying a glider for sport

Examples

He dedicated his weekends to sailplaning over the countryside.
Learning the basics of sailplaning can be challenging but rewarding.
The club organizes annual sailplaning competitions for enthusiasts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gliding - the activity of flying in a glider

Etymology

From "sailplane", meaning a type of glider, combined with the suffix "-ing" denoting the activity.
