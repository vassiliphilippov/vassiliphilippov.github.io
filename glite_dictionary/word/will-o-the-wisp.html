will-o'-the-wisp

1. NOUN

/ ˌwɪloʊ ðə ˈwɪsp / (AmE), / ˌwɪl ə ðə ˈwɪsp / (BrE)
An illusion or hope that leads one on but is impossible to reach or achieve; something that misleads or cannot be attained.
something that misleads or cannot be achieved

Examples

Chasing financial success became a will-o'-the-wisp for him, always out of reach.
Her hope of finding the perfect house in that price range was just a will-o'-the-wisp.
He realized that his dream of finding treasure in the garden was a will-o'-the-wisp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mirage - an optical illusion caused by atmospheric conditions
  • Synonym: chimera - something hoped for but impossible to achieve

Etymology

Originally from a folk term for mysterious lights seen at night, from 'will (the) wisp' ('Will' as a name and 'wisp' meaning 'torch').
