unauthentic

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɔːˈθɛntɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnɔːˈθɛntɪk / (BrE)
not genuine; not real or true; false or fake
not genuine; fake

Examples

The painting was dismissed as unauthentic after a thorough examination by experts.
She bought an unauthentic designer bag from a street vendor.
The museum owner unknowingly displayed several unauthentic artifacts.

Synset Examples

a spurious work of art

Related Terms

  • Antonym: authentic - genuine; real; not false or copied
  • Synonym: fake - not genuine; a forgery or imitation

Etymology

Formed from the prefix "un-" (not) + "authentic" (genuine).
