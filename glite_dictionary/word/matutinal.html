matutinal

1. ADJ

/ məˈtuːtɪnəl / (AmE), / məˈtjuːtɪnəl / (BrE)
Relating to or occurring in the morning.
of or in the morning

Examples

He enjoyed his matutinal coffee while watching the sunrise each day.
The birds' matutinal songs filled the air before most people were awake.

Related Terms

Etymology
