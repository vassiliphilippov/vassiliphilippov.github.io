reply-paid

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈplaɪ peɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈplaɪ peɪd / (BrE)
(of a letter, postcard, envelope, etc.) having the cost of the reply prepaid by the original sender.
with reply postage paid by sender

Examples

Please use the reply-paid envelope provided to send us your feedback.
The survey included a reply-paid postcard, making it easy to respond.
We sent customers a reply-paid form to encourage prompt replies.

Synset Examples

reply-paid postcard
reply-paid envelope
