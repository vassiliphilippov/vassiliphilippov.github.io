Santiago

1. PROPN

/ sænˈtjɑːɡoʊ / (AmE), / sænˈtjɑːɡəʊ / (BrE)
The capital and largest city of Chile, located in central Chile.
capital city of Chile

Examples

Santiago is known for its vibrant nightlife and stunning views of the Andes Mountains.
The conference will be held in Santiago next spring.
After moving to Santiago, she quickly adapted to city life.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Santiago', literally 'Saint James'.
