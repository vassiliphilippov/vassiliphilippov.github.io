apiarist

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ.pi.ə.rɪst / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.pi.ə.rɪst / (BrE)
a person who keeps and tends bees, especially for their honey
beekeeper; person who keeps bees

Examples

The apiarist inspected the hives to ensure the bees were healthy and thriving.
As an experienced apiarist, she harvests honey several times each year.
Local schools invited a professional apiarist to teach students about bees' role in pollination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beekeeper - a person who keeps bees, especially for commercial purposes

Etymology

From Latin 'apis' (bee), influenced by 'apiary'.
