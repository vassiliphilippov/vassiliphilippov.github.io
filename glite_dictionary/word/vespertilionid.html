vespertilionid

1. NOUN

/ ˌvɛspərˈtɪliənɪd / (AmE), / ˌvɛspəˈtɪliənɪd / (BrE)
A bat of the family Vespertilionidae, which comprises a large group of insect-eating bats found worldwide.
a bat of the Vespertilionidae family

Examples

The caves are home to a rare species of vespertilionid that feeds on moths.
Scientists have discovered a new vespertilionid in the Amazon rainforest.
Many vespertilionids play an important role in controlling insect populations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bat - a nocturnal flying mammal

Etymology

From Latin 'vespertilio' (bat), the type genus of the family Vespertilionidae.
