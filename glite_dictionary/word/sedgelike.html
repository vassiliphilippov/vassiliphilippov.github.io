sedgelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛdʒˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɛdʒˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of sedge plants, especially in appearance or form
resembling sedge in appearance

Examples

The plant has narrow, sedgelike leaves that thrive in wetlands.
Many grass species develop a sedgelike appearance in marshy habitats.
The field was covered in sedgelike vegetation along the banks of the river.

Etymology

From 'sedge' (a type of grasslike plant) + 'like' (similar to).
