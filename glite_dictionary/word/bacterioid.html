bacterioid

1. ADJ

/ bækˈtɪərioʊd / (AmE), / bakˈtɪərɪɔɪd / (BrE)
resembling or having the characteristics of bacteria
resembling bacteria

Examples

Some cells adopt a bacterioid form during certain stages of symbiosis.
The bacterioid appearance of the structure surprised the researchers.
The scientist described the organism as bacterioid because of its rod-like shape.

Etymology

From 'bacteria' + -oid ('resembling'), from Greek 'eidos' (form).
