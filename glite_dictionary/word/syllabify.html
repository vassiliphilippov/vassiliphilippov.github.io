to syllabify

1. VERB

/ sɪˈlæbəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / sɪˈlæbɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to divide a word into its syllables
to divide into syllables

Examples

In order to help children read, teachers often syllabify difficult words.
The software can automatically syllabify any text you enter.
Linguists sometimes syllabify foreign words before analyzing their structure.

Synset Examples

syllabify the words

Related Terms

  • Synonym: syllable - a unit of pronunciation having one vowel sound

Etymology

From French syllabifier, from Latin syllaba (syllable) + -ify.
