cased

1. ADJ

/ keɪst / (AmE), / keɪst / (BrE)
covered or protected with or as if with a case
covered or protected in a case

Examples

The delicate instrument was cased in a sturdy metal shell for transport.
She admired the antique books cased in beautiful leather bindings.
Many security cameras are cased in weatherproof enclosures to protect against rain and dust.

Synset Examples

knights cased in steel
products encased in leatherette

Related Terms

  • Synonym: encased - enclosed or covered in a case or close-fitting surround

2. ADJ

/ keɪst / (AmE), / keɪst / (BrE)
enclosed in or protected by a case or covering.
enclosed within a case

Examples

The fragile instrument was carefully cased to prevent damage during transport.
They presented a beautifully cased set of antique silverware at the auction.
All equipment must be cased before being loaded onto the truck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: encased - enclosed or covered in a case or close-fitting surround
