Oaxaca de Juarez

1. PROPN

/ waˈhɑːkə deɪ ˈwɑːɾes / (AmE), / wəˈhækə deɪ ˈhwɑːrez / (BrE)
a city in southeastern Mexico, the capital of the state of Oaxaca
city in southeastern Mexico

Examples

We visited Oaxaca de Juarez during our trip to southern Mexico.
The colorful markets in Oaxaca de Juarez are famous for their traditional crafts.
Each year, Oaxaca de Juarez hosts vibrant celebrations for the Day of the Dead.

Etymology

Spanish, from the Nahuatl name 'Oaxaca' and honorific 'de Juarez' for Benito Juárez.
