artwork

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrtˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈɑːtwɜːk / (BrE)
photographs, illustrations, or other visual representations used in a printed publication, such as a book or magazine
visual elements in a publication

Examples

The artwork in the magazine really brought the feature article to life.
She was responsible for all the artwork in the annual report.
The publisher hired a professional to create the artwork for the children's book.

Synset Examples

the publisher was responsible for all the artwork in the book

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illustration - a picture or diagram that helps explain or decorate a text
