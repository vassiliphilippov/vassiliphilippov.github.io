flavourless

1. ADJ

/ ˈfleɪvərləs / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvələs / (BrE)
having little or no taste; lacking in flavor or distinctive quality.
lacking flavor or taste
british

Examples

The soup was completely flavourless, so I added extra salt and pepper.
Many people complain that supermarket strawberries are flavourless compared to homegrown ones.
He pushed aside the flavourless rice and reached for the spiced vegetables.

Synset Examples

a bland diet
insipid hospital food
flavorless supermarket tomatoes
vapid beer
vapid tea

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bland - lacking strong flavor or taste
  • Synonym: insipid - lacking flavor; dull or uninteresting
  • Synonym: tasteless - without flavor

Etymology

From 'flavour' + '-less', with 'flavour' from Old French 'flavor', from Latin 'flos' (flower, as in a fragrant smell).
