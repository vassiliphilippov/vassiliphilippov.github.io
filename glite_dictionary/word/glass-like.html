glass-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡlæs ˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːs ˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling glass in appearance, texture, or other qualities
resembling glass

Examples

The rocks had a glass-like surface that shimmered in the sunlight.
She admired the glass-like transparency of the newly formed ice.
The vase was made of a material with a glass-like clarity and smoothness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glassy - having a smooth, shiny appearance like glass
