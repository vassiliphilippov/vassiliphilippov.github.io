Honduras

1. PROPN

/ hɑnˈdʊrəs / (AmE), / hɒnˈdjʊərəs / (BrE)
a country in Central America, known for its rich Mayan history and having gained independence from Spain in 1821
country in Central America

Examples

Many tourists visit Honduras to explore ancient Mayan ruins such as Copán.
The president of Honduras addressed the nation in a televised speech yesterday.
Coffee is one of the major exports produced in Honduras.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Guatemala - country in Central America, borders Honduras to the west
  • Synonym: Nicaragua - country in Central America, borders Honduras to the southeast

Etymology

From Spanish 'Honduras,' meaning 'depths,' likely referring to the deep waters off the country's coast.
