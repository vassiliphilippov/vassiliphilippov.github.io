regardant

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈɡɑrdənt / (AmE), / rɪˈɡɑːd(ə)nt / (BrE)
(especially in heraldry) looking backward over the shoulder.
looking backward, esp. in heraldry
archaic

Examples

In heraldic shields, a lion regardant gazes over its shoulder, as though watching its own back.
The statue was sculpted with a regardant expression, suggesting vigilance and awareness of its surroundings.
The old painting depicted a knight regardant, glancing warily behind him as he rode forth.

Etymology

From Old French regardant, present participle of regarder (to look).
