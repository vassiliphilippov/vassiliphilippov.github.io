Mexitil

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛksɪtɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɛksɪtɪl / (BrE)
A brand name for the drug mexiletine, used to treat abnormal heart rhythms (ventricular arrhythmias).
antiarrhythmic drug (brand name)
medical

Examples

After starting Mexitil, the patient's heart rhythm stabilized significantly.
The cardiologist prescribed Mexitil to prevent further ventricular arrhythmias.
Her doctor explained the potential side effects of Mexitil during the consultation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mexiletine - the generic name for Mexitil, an antiarrhythmic drug
