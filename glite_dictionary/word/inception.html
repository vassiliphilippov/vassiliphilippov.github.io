inception

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛpʃən / (BrE)
the beginning or start of something; the point at which something begins
beginning or start of something

Examples

The company has grown rapidly since its inception in 2010.
From its inception, the project faced several major challenges.
The tradition has been observed every year since the club’s inception.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beginning - the point in time or space at which something begins
  • Antonym: end - the final part of something

Etymology

From Latin 'inceptio', from 'incipere' (to begin).
