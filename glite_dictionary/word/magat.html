MAGAt

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæg.æt / (AmE), / ˈmæɡ.æt / (BrE)
a derogatory slang term for a zealous supporter of Donald Trump, often identified by wearing a red 'Make America Great Again' hat.
(slang) zealous Trump supporter
slang offensive american

Examples

He identifies as a MAGAt and frequently attends rallies wearing a red cap.
Social media debates often become heated when MAGAts join the conversation.
The comedian's routine poked fun at both liberals and MAGAts alike.

Synset Examples

As a proud MAGAt, he wore his red hat everywhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Trump supporter - a person who supports Donald Trump

Etymology

From the acronym MAGA (Make America Great Again) combined with 'maggot' to create a derogatory pun.
