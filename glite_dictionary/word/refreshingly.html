refreshingly

1. ADV

/ rɪˈfrɛʃɪŋli / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɛʃɪŋli / (BrE)
in a pleasantly new, surprising, or different way
in a pleasantly surprising manner

Examples

The story was refreshingly honest, offering a new perspective on a familiar topic.
Her refreshingly direct approach made the meeting much more productive than usual.
The decor in the restaurant was refreshingly simple compared to other trendy places.

Synset Examples

she was refreshingly free from shyness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surprisingly - in an unexpected manner
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00442014-r

2. ADV

/ rɪˈfrɛʃɪŋli / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɛʃɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that gives new energy, relief, or pleasure
in a way that provides renewal or relief

Examples

The lemonade tasted refreshingly tart after a long, hot walk in the sun.
She spoke refreshingly honestly about the challenges she had faced.
A cold swim in the lake felt refreshingly invigorating on the humid afternoon.

Synset Examples

the air was refreshingly cool

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invigoratingly - in a manner that gives energy or strength

Etymology

From 'refresh' + '-ing' + '-ly', ultimately from Old French 'refreschir'.
