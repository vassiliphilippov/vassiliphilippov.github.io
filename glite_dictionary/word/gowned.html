gowned

1. ADJ

/ ɡaʊnd / (AmE), / ɡaʊnd / (BrE)
wearing a gown, especially a formal dress or ceremonial robe
wearing a gown

Examples

The guests arrived gowned in elegant silks and sparkling jewelry.
The newly gowned graduates cheered as they tossed their caps in the air.
Only gowned patients are allowed into the operating room area.

Synset Examples

beautifully gowned women

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ungowned - not wearing a gown

Etymology

From 'gown' (noun) + -ed, denoting state or condition.
