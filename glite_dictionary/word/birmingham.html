2. PROPN

/ ˈbɜrmɪŋˌhæm / (AmE), / ˈbɜːmɪŋəm / (BrE)
a city in central England, the second largest city in England, known for its industry and transportation networks.
second largest city in England

Examples

Many major companies have their headquarters in Birmingham, making it a key economic hub.
She moved to Birmingham to study engineering at the university there.
The canals of Birmingham are an important part of the city's history and charm.

Etymology

From Old English 'Beorma inga ham', meaning 'homestead of the people of Beorma'.
