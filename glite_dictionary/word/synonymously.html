synonymously

1. ADV

/ sɪˈnɑːnɪməsli / (AmE), / sɪˈnɒnɪməsli / (BrE)
In a way that means the same as another word or expression; in a synonymous manner.
in a synonymous manner

Examples

The two scientific terms are often used synonymously in recent literature.
In many contexts, 'auto' and 'car' can appear synonymously without confusion.
The names were not used synonymously in older texts, but have become interchangeable today.

Synset Examples

the two terms are used synonymously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: synonymous - having the same or nearly the same meaning as another word

Etymology

From 'synonymous' + '-ly'.
