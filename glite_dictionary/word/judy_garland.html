Judy Garland

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒudi ˈɡɑrlənd / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːdi ˈɡɑːlənd / (BrE)
(1922–1969) United States singer and film actress known for her powerful voice and starring role in 'The Wizard of Oz'.
