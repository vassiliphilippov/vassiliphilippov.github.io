Charles Schulz

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʃɑrlz ʃʊlts / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːlz ʃʊlz / (BrE)
(1922–2000) American cartoonist best known as the creator of the comic strip Peanuts, featuring characters such as Snoopy and Charlie Brown.
American cartoonist, creator of Peanuts

Examples

Charles Schulz created some of the world's most beloved comic characters, including Snoopy and Charlie Brown.
The work of Charles Schulz has inspired generations of cartoonists and readers alike.
Many fans celebrate the legacy of Charles Schulz by collecting Peanuts memorabilia.
