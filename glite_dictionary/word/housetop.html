housetop

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs.tɒp / (BrE)
the roof of a house
roof of a house

Examples

The cat perched quietly on the housetop, gazing over the neighborhood.
As the storm approached, the old shingles on the housetop rattled loudly.
He threatened to shout the news from the housetop if nobody listened.

Synset Examples

shout it from the housetops

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roof - the top covering of a building

Etymology

From 'house' + 'top', signifying the top part of a house.
