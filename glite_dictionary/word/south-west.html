south-west

1. ADV

/ ˌsaʊθˈwɛst / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈwɛst / (BrE)
To, toward, or in the direction of the southwest.
toward the southwest direction

Examples

They drove south-west for several hours until they reached the coast.
The storm is moving south-west across the state.
If you keep heading south-west, you'll eventually arrive in the next town.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: north-east - to, toward, or in the northeast
  • Degree Lower: south-east - to, toward, or in the southeast
  • Degree Higher: west - to, toward, or in the west

Etymology

From Old English 'sūþ' (south) + 'west' (west).
