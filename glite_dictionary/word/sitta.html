Sitta

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪtə / (AmE), / ˈsɪtə / (BrE)
the genus of birds in the family Sittidae, commonly known as nuthatches
genus of nuthatches (birds)

Examples

The Eurasian nuthatch, classified as Sitta europaea, is found across Europe and Asia.
Many ornithologists study different species within the genus Sitta.
Charles named his favorite bird, the red-breasted nuthatch, as Sitta canadensis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nuthatch - a small bird of the genus Sitta, known for climbing trees headfirst

Etymology

From Latin *Sitta*, from Ancient Greek σίττη (sittē), a type of bird, especially the nuthatch.
