immunotherapeutic

1. ADJ

/ ɪˌmjunəˌθɛrəˈpjutɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌmjuːnəʊˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving immunotherapy, a treatment that uses the body's immune system to fight disease
relating to immunotherapy
medical

Examples

Researchers are developing new immunotherapeutic agents to treat cancer more effectively.
The immunotherapeutic approach has shown promise in managing chronic viral infections.
Certain immunotherapeutic drugs can enhance the body's own defenses against tumors.

Synset Examples

various immunotherapeutic techniques have been employed with AIDS patients

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immunotherapy - treatment that uses the immune system to fight disease

Etymology

From 'immuno-' relating to the immune system, and 'therapeutic' meaning related to treatment.
