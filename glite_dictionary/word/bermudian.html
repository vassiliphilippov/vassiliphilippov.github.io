Bermudian

1. NOUN

/ bərˈmjuːdiən / (AmE), / bəˈmjuːdiən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Bermuda
person from Bermuda

Examples

As a proud Bermudian, she celebrates her island's cultural heritage every year.
Many Bermudians work in the tourism industry, welcoming visitors from around the world.
He met a Bermudian who shared fascinating stories about life on the island.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bermuda - a British Overseas Territory in the North Atlantic Ocean

Etymology

From Bermuda, with the English suffix -ian denoting nationality or origin.
