Dhu'l-Hijjah

1. NOUN

/ duːl hɪˈdʒɑː / (AmE), / duːl hɪˈdʒɑː / (BrE)
the twelfth month of the Islamic calendar, during which the hajj pilgrimage takes place
12th month of the Islamic calendar

Examples

Many Muslims travel to Mecca during Dhu'l-Hijjah to perform the hajj pilgrimage.
The festival of Eid al-Adha falls on the tenth day of Dhu'l-Hijjah.
Dhu'l-Hijjah is considered a sacred month in the Islamic calendar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hajj - the Muslim pilgrimage to Mecca

Etymology

From Arabic Ḏū al-Ḥijja, meaning ‘possessor of the pilgrimage’.
