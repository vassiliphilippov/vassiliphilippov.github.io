Ninkharsag

1. PROPN

/ nɪnˈkɑːrˌsɑːg / (AmE), / nɪŋˈkɑːsɑːg / (BrE)
an ancient Mesopotamian mother goddess, worshipped as the goddess of fertility and creation, also known as Aruru, Mama, and Nintu
Mesopotamian mother goddess

Examples

The ancient Sumerians built temples to honor Ninkharsag as the mother of all gods.
In Mesopotamian mythology, Ninkharsag played a central role in the creation of humanity.
Archaeologists discovered statues dedicated to Ninkharsag in the ruins of Lagash.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aruru - another name for the Mesopotamian mother goddess
  • Synonym: Nintu - alternate name for the goddess Ninkharsag
  • Synonym: Mama - another title for the mother goddess in Mesopotamian mythology

Etymology

From Sumerian *Ninhursag* ('Lady of the Mountain'), an early name in Mesopotamian myth.
