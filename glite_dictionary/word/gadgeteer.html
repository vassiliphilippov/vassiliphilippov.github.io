gadgeteer

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡædʒəˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˌɡædʒɪˈtɪə / (BrE)
A person who enjoys designing, building, or using small mechanical or electronic devices (gadgets).
person who enjoys making or using gadgets

Examples

As a lifelong gadgeteer, he always carries the latest electronic devices in his bag.
The gadgeteer quickly fixed the broken drone with tools he invented himself.
Many gadgeteers love tinkering in their workshops on weekends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inventor - a person who invents new devices or processes
  • Synonym: technophile - a person who loves or is enthusiastic about technology

Etymology

Formed from 'gadget' plus the agent suffix '-eer', modelled after 'engineer' and similar words.
