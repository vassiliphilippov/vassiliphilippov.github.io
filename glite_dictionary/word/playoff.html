playoff

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪˌɑf / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌɒf / (BrE)
a series of games or a single game played to determine the champion or winner among the top-ranking teams or competitors, usually following a regular season.
post-season competition to decide a champion

Examples

The team advanced to the playoff after a successful regular season.
Fans eagerly awaited the results of the playoff to see who would be crowned champions.
This year, the playoff format was changed to include more teams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: final - the last and decisive match in a tournament

Etymology

From 'play' + 'off', indicating playing to decide an outcome; popularized in American sports from early 20th century.
