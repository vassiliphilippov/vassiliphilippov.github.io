trampoline

1. NOUN

/ ˈtræmpəˌliːn / (AmE), / ˈtræmpəliːn / (BrE)
a gymnastic apparatus consisting of a strong, flexible fabric sheet attached by springs to a metal frame, used for jumping and acrobatic exercises
springy gymnastic apparatus for jumping

Examples

The children spent the afternoon jumping on the trampoline in the backyard.
A safety net was installed around the trampoline to prevent injuries.
During the competition, her flips on the trampoline impressed the judges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rebounder - a small exercise trampoline

Etymology

From Italian 'trampolino', meaning 'springboard'.
