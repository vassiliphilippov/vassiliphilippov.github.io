Zaar

1. NOUN

/ zɑːr / (AmE), / zɑː / (BrE)
A Chadic language spoken in northern Nigeria, particularly in Bauchi State.
Chadic language of northern Nigeria

Examples

The Zaar language is primarily spoken in communities within Bauchi State.
Linguists have conducted extensive research on the grammar of Zaar.
Many children learn both Hausa and Zaar as their native languages.
