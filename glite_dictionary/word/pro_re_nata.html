pro re nata

1. ADV

/ proʊ reɪ ˈnɑːtə / (AmE), / prəʊ reɪ ˈnɑːtə / (BrE)
as needed; as the situation arises, used especially in medicine to indicate that a medication or procedure should be performed only when necessary
as needed (esp. medically)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed the painkiller to be taken pro re nata to manage severe discomfort.
Nurses administer certain medications pro re nata, rather than at fixed intervals, depending on the patient's needs.
You should only use your inhaler pro re nata, not on a regular schedule.

Synset Examples

add water as needed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as needed - when required; as necessary
  • Synonym: as required - when necessary, as the situation demands

Etymology

From Latin, meaning 'for the thing born' or 'for the occasion.'
