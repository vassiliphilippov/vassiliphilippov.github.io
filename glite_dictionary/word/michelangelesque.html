Michelangelesque

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɪkəˌlændʒəˈlɛsk / (AmE), / ˌmɪkəlændʒəˈlɛsk / (BrE)
In the manner or style of Michelangelo, especially his art or sculpture; resembling the work or qualities of Michelangelo.
in the style of Michelangelo

Examples

The statue's powerful physique and dramatic posture are distinctly Michelangelesque.
Art critics described the painting as Michelangelesque in its bold use of color and muscular figures.
Her sculptures possessed a Michelangelesque grandeur that impressed everyone at the exhibition.

Etymology

From the name Michelangelo (Italian artist, 1475–1564) + -esque (in the manner of).
