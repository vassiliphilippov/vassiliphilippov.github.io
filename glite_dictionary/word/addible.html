addible

1. ADJ

/ ˈædəbəl / (AmE), / ˈadɪb(ə)l / (BrE)
capable of being added or added to; able to be added.
capable of being added

Examples

Only addible components can be included in this list.
The numbers in the set are all addible under these rules.
Please ensure that any new field is addible to the existing structure.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: subtractable - capable of being subtracted

Etymology

From Latin addibilis, from addere (to add).
