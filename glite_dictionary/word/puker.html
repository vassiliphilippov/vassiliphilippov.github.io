puker

1. NOUN

/ ˈpjuːkər / (AmE), / ˈpjuːkə / (BrE)
a person who vomits, especially someone who does so frequently
person who vomits often
informal

Examples

He was known as the puker of the group on road trips.
After the roller coaster, she became an instant puker.
Nobody wanted to sit next to the puker during the boat ride.

Synset Examples

She is such a puker when it comes to spicy food.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vomiter - a person who vomits

Etymology

From the verb 'puke' (to vomit) + '-er' (agent suffix).
