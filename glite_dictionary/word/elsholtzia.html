Elsholtzia

1. NOUN

/ ɛlˈʃoʊltziə / (AmE), / ɛlˈʃɒltziə / (BrE)
a genus of aromatic herbs native to Asia and Africa, belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae)
genus of aromatic herbs in Lamiaceae

Examples

Several species of Elsholtzia are widely used in traditional Chinese medicine.
The Elsholtzia genus is known for its aromatic leaves and small purple flowers.
Scientists recently discovered a new species within the Elsholtzia genus in Southeast Asia.

Etymology

Named after Johann Sigismund Elsholtz, a German botanist (1623–1688).
