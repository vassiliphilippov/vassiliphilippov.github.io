elsholtzia

1. NOUN

/ ɛlˈʃɒlts.i.ə / (AmE), / ɛlˈʃɒlts.i.ə / (BrE)
Any of various aromatic herbs of the genus Elsholtzia, characterized by blue or purple flowers arranged in one-sided spikes.
aromatic herb genus Elsholtzia

Examples

The garden featured several varieties of elsholtzia blooming in late summer.
Researchers studied extracts from elsholtzia for their potential medicinal properties.
The purple flower spikes of elsholtzia attract many pollinators throughout the season.

Etymology

Named after Johann Sigismund Elsholtz, a German botanist.
