to double-date

1. VERB

/ ˈdʌbəl deɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʌb(ə)l deɪt / (BrE)
to go out socially on a date with one's partner and another couple
go on a date with another couple

Examples

We decided to double-date with our friends last Friday night.
They often double-date to make gatherings more fun.
Are you free to double-date with us this weekend?

Synset Examples

let's double date this Saturday

Related Terms

  • Synonym: date - to go out with someone socially in a romantic way
