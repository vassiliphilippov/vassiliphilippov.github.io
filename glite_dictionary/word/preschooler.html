preschooler

1. NOUN

/ ˈpriˌskuːlɚ / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌskuːlə / (BrE)
a young child, especially one who attends a preschool or kindergarten
young child in preschool or kindergarten

Examples

The new playground was designed with preschooler safety in mind.
As a preschooler, she loved story time and building blocks.
Each preschooler received a small backpack on their first day.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: toddler - a very young child who is just beginning to walk
  • Synonym: kindergartner - a child attending kindergarten

Etymology

From 'pre-' (before) + 'school' + '-er' (person suffix), meaning a person before school age.
