amazement

1. NOUN

/ əˈmeɪzmənt / (AmE), / əˈmeɪzmənt / (BrE)
A strong feeling of great surprise or wonder.
strong feeling of surprise or wonder

Examples

She stared at the magician's trick in amazement, unable to believe her eyes.
His promotion came as a complete amazement to everyone in the office.
The children shouted in amazement when they saw the fireworks.

Synset Examples

he looked at me in astonishment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astonishment - a feeling of great surprise
  • Synonym: wonder - a feeling caused by something extraordinary

Etymology

From the verb 'amaze' (Middle English 'amasen'), meaning 'to bewilder, to stupefy'; influenced by Old English 'amasian' (to confound).
