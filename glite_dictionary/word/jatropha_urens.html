Jatropha urens

1. NOUN

/ jəˈtroʊfə juˈrɛnz / (AmE), / dʒəˈtrəʊfə jʊˈrɛnz / (BrE)
A stinging herbaceous plant of tropical America, known for its irritating sap.
a stinging herb of tropical America

Examples

The leaves of Jatropha urens can cause a painful irritation if touched.
Researchers are studying the potential medicinal uses of Jatropha urens.
In some regions, farmers avoid fields where Jatropha urens is abundant due to its stinging properties.

Etymology

From Latin genus name 'Jatropha' and species name 'urens', meaning 'stinging' or 'burning' in Latin.
