rewardful

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈwɔrd.fəl / (AmE), / rɪˈwɔːd.fəl / (BrE)
offering or producing a reward; giving satisfaction or benefit as a result of effort
offering or yielding reward or benefit

Examples

She found her volunteer work to be deeply rewardful even though it was challenging.
Choosing a rewardful career is important for long-term happiness.
Their efforts in the community project proved to be rewardful for everyone involved.

Synset Examples

rewardful pursuits

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rewarding - giving satisfaction or benefit; providing reward

Etymology

Formed from 'reward' + '-ful' (full of, characterized by).
