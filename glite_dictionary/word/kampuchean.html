Kampuchean

1. NOUN

/ kæmˌpuˈtʃiːən / (AmE), / kæmˌpjuːˈtʃiːən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Cambodia
a person from Cambodia

Examples

Many Kampucheans were affected by the political changes in their country.
A group of Kampucheans celebrated their national holiday abroad.
The cuisine reflects the traditions of the Kampuchean people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cambodian - a native or inhabitant of Cambodia

Etymology

From Kampuchea, the former official name of Cambodia.

2. ADJ

/ kæmˈpuːtʃiən / (AmE), / kamˈpuːtʃɪən / (BrE)
relating to Cambodia, its people, or its language
relating to Cambodia or its people/language

Examples

The Kampuchean government adopted a new flag after the revolution.
She is conducting research on Kampuchean history and culture.
A Kampuchean restaurant opened in our neighborhood last month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cambodian - relating to Cambodia or its people/language

Etymology

From Kampuchea, the official name of Cambodia in Khmer (កម្ពុជា).
