Illecebrum

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛsəbrəm / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛsɪbrəm / (BrE)
A small genus of flowering plants in the family Caryophyllaceae, consisting of one species, commonly known as coral necklace.
genus of flowering plant (coral necklace)

Examples

The tiny flowers of Illecebrum add a delicate charm to rock gardens.
Illecebrum, also called coral necklace, thrives in sandy, open habitats.
Botanists often study Illecebrum for its unique position within the Caryophyllaceae family.

Etymology

From Latin *illecebra* (allurement, attraction), referring to the attractive nature of the plant.
