Rider Haggard

1. PROPN

/ ˈraɪdər ˈhæɡərd / (AmE), / ˈraɪdə ˈhæɡəd / (BrE)
(1856–1925) British author best known for his romantic adventure novels, such as 'King Solomon's Mines' and 'She'.
British author of adventure novels

Examples

Many early adventure stories were inspired by Rider Haggard's imaginative novels.
I recently finished reading 'King Solomon's Mines' by Rider Haggard.
Rider Haggard was a prominent figure in late Victorian literature.
