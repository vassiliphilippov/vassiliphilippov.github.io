Mensa

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɛnsə / (AmE), / ˈmɛnsə / (BrE)
a faint constellation in the southern polar region, containing part of the Large Magellanic Cloud
a southern constellation

Examples

Astronomers observed a rare phenomenon in the Mensa constellation last night.
Mensa is not visible from most locations in the Northern Hemisphere.
The Large Magellanic Cloud is partly located within Mensa.

Etymology

From Latin mensa (table), named for its resemblance to Table Mountain in South Africa.
