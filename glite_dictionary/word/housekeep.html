to housekeep

1. VERB

/ ˈhaʊsˌkiːp / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌkiːp / (BrE)
to maintain and manage the affairs and cleanliness of a household
manage or maintain a household

Examples

She decided to housekeep for her elderly parents after they fell ill.
He chose to housekeep full-time while his partner returned to work.
Many people housekeep and manage other household responsibilities simultaneously.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to housekeep - to do cleaning and running of a household
  • Antonym: neglect - to fail to care for something properly

Etymology

Formed from 'house' and 'keep,' patterned after 'housekeeping.'
