suburban

1. ADJ

/ səˈbɝːbən / (AmE), / səˈbɜːbən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a suburb or suburbs.
relating to a suburb or suburbs

Examples

They moved to a suburban neighborhood for a quieter lifestyle.
The school is known for serving mostly suburban families.
That restaurant is located in a busy suburban shopping center.

Synset Examples

suburban population

Related Terms

  • Antonym: urban - relating to a city or town
  • Antonym: rural - relating to the countryside

Etymology

From Latin 'suburbānus', from 'suburbium' (suburb), from 'sub-' (near) + 'urbs' (city).
