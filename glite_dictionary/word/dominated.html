dominated

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɑməˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɒmɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
controlled or ruled by a more powerful person, group, or force
controlled or ruled by authority

Examples

The company was dominated by a single powerful investor for many years.
In history, some regions have been dominated by foreign powers for centuries.
The conversation was dominated by political topics rather than personal stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: subjugated - brought under domination or control
  • Antonym: autonomous - acting independently or having the freedom to do so
