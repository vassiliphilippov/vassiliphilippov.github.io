mesmerizing

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛzməˌraɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɛzməraɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
capturing and holding the complete attention of someone as if by magic or enchantment
holding attention like a spell

Examples

The mesmerizing performance kept the entire audience in absolute silence.
She found the ocean's endless waves utterly mesmerizing during her walk on the beach.
His mesmerizing storytelling made hours pass by unnoticed.

Synset Examples

read the bedtime story in a hypnotic voice
she had a warm mesmeric charm
the sheer force of his presence was mesmerizing
a spellbinding description of life in ancient Rome

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hypnotic - producing a trance-like state or captivating attention
  • Synonym: spellbinding - holding one's attention completely as if by magic

Etymology

From mesmerize, named after Franz Mesmer (1734–1815), an Austrian physician known for hypnosis experiments.
