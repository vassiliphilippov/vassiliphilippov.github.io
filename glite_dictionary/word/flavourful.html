flavourful

1. ADJ

/ ˈfleɪvɚfəl / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvəf(ə)l / (BrE)
having a strong or rich taste; full of flavor
having a rich or strong taste
british

Examples

The soup was incredibly flavourful, with layers of spices and fresh herbs.
She prefers flavourful cheeses to those with a milder taste.
Roasting the vegetables made them much more flavourful than boiling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tasty - having a pleasant, distinctive flavour
  • Antonym: bland - lacking strong flavor or taste

Etymology

Formed from 'flavour' + '-ful', following regular English patterns.
