busy-bodied

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɪziˌbɑdɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɪziˌbɒdɪd / (BrE)
Intrusively meddlesome or officious; inclined to interfere in others' affairs in a way that is annoying or inappropriate.
officious; meddlesome

Examples

She found her new neighbor insufferably busy-bodied, always prying into private matters.
His busy-bodied questions made everyone at the party uncomfortable.
No one appreciated the busy-bodied approach she took to organizing other people's tasks.

Synset Examples

an interfering old woman
bustling about self-importantly making an officious nuisance of himself
busy about other people's business

Related Terms

  • Synonym: officious - assertively meddlesome or interfering, especially in trivial matters
  • Synonym: meddlesome - fond of interfering in others' affairs

Etymology

From 'busybody' (noun, a meddling person) + -ed, forming the adjective.
