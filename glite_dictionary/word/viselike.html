viselike

1. ADJ

/ ˈvaɪsˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈvaɪsˌlaɪk / (BrE)
extremely tight or compressing, as if held by a vise
extremely tight or clamped

Examples

He held the rope in a viselike grip, refusing to let go.
The door was stuck shut, wedged with viselike pressure.
She felt a viselike pain in her chest and struggled to breathe.

Synset Examples

a viselike grip

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tight - firmly held; not loose
  • Antonym: loose - not firmly held or tightly fastened

Etymology

Formed from 'vise' + '-like', indicating resemblance to a vise.
