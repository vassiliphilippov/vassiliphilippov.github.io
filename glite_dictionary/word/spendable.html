spendable

1. ADJ

/ ˈspɛndəbəl / (AmE), / ˈspɛndəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of money or funds) available to be spent, especially after necessary deductions such as taxes have been made.
available funds for spending

Examples

After paying her taxes, she calculated her spendable income for the month.
It's important to know how much spendable cash you have before making big purchases.
His bonus made his savings account much more spendable this year.

Synset Examples

spendable income

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disposable - available for use, especially after obligations are met

Etymology

Formed from 'spend' + '-able', meaning able to be spent.
