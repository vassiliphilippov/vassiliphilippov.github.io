reddish-orange

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈɔːrɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈɒrɪndʒ / (BrE)
of an orange color with a tinge of red; between red and orange
orange tinged with red

Examples

The sky turned a beautiful reddish-orange at sunset.
She wore a reddish-orange scarf that matched the autumn leaves.
The fruit had a reddish-orange hue, making it look especially ripe.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: orange - a color between red and yellow in the visible spectrum
  • Degree Higher: red - the color at the end of the visible spectrum next to orange
