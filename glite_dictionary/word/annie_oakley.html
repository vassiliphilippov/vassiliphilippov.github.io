Annie Oakley

1. PROPN

/ ˈæni oʊkli / (AmE), / ˈæni ˈəʊkli / (BrE)
(1860–1926) An American sharpshooter and exhibition shooter who gained fame as a star in Buffalo Bill's Wild West Show.
American sharpshooter and performer

Examples

Annie Oakley became a symbol of the American West through her incredible marksmanship and showmanship.
Many young girls looked up to Annie Oakley as a trailblazer for women in sports and entertainment.
The biography of Annie Oakley details her rise from humble beginnings to international acclaim.
