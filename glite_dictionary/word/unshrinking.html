unshrinking

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈʃrɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃrɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
not shrinking from danger, difficulty, or responsibility; showing courage and resolve
not recoiling from danger; courageous

Examples

She faced the accusations with unshrinking honesty and determination.
His unshrinking courage inspired those around him to stand tall.
The explorer advanced unshrinking into the unknown, undaunted by risk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unflinching - not showing fear or hesitation in the face of danger or difficulty
  • Antonym: shrinking - hesitating or recoiling from something difficult or unpleasant

Etymology

From un- (not) + shrinking (present participle of 'shrink'), meaning not recoiling or hesitating.
