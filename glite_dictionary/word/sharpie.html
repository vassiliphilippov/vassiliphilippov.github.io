Sharpie

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑːrpi / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːpi / (BrE)
a brand of permanent marker pen with indelible ink that can write on most surfaces
permanent marker pen (brand)

Examples

He labeled all his boxes with a Sharpie before moving to the new apartment.
The teacher used a black Sharpie to write on the poster board.
Don't forget to bring a Sharpie to autograph the T-shirts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marker - a pen with a broad, felt tip for writing or drawing

Etymology

Trademarked name of marker pens introduced by the Sanford Ink Company in 1964.
