gooselike

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡuːs.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡuːs.laɪk / (BrE)
silly or lacking in intelligence; foolish in manner or behavior.
foolish or stupid in manner

Examples

His gooselike response to the question left everyone shaking their heads.
She made a gooselike mistake during the meeting that embarrassed the whole team.
Despite his intelligence, he sometimes acts in a gooselike manner when he's nervous.

Synset Examples

ridiculous anserine behavior
a dopey answer
a dopey kid
some fool idea about rewriting authors' books

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment
  • Synonym: asinine - extremely stupid or foolish

Etymology

Formed from 'goose' (the bird) and the suffix '-like', by analogy to foolish behavior attributed to geese.
