cobber

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑbər / (AmE), / ˈkɒbə / (BrE)
(Australian, informal) a close friend or companion; mate.
Australian slang for friend or mate
informal slang american british

Examples

Charlie is an old cobber of mine from university days.
G’day, cobber, fancy a drink at the pub?
He introduced me to his cobber who had just arrived from Sydney.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mate - (Australian) a friend or companion
  • Antonym: enemy - a person who is actively opposed to someone

Etymology

Australian English, probably from British dialect cob (to take a liking to), or possibly from Yiddish khaver (friend).
