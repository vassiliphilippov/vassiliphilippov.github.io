reddish-striped

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛdɪʃ ˌstraɪpt / (AmE), / ˈrɛdɪʃ ˌstraɪpt / (BrE)
having stripes that are red or somewhat red in color
having red or reddish stripes

Examples

The caterpillar's reddish-striped body helps it blend in with certain leaves.
She wore a reddish-striped sweater that stood out in the crowd.
The museum displayed a reddish-striped vase from the ancient civilization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: striped - having stripes
  • Synonym: red-striped - having red stripes
