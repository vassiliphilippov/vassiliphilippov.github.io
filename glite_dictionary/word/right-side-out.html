right-side-out

1. ADJ

/ ˌraɪt saɪd ˈaʊt / (AmE), / ˌraɪt saɪd ˈaʊt / (BrE)
(of fabric or clothing) with the intended outer surface facing outward; not inside out.
with outer surface facing out (fabric/clothing)

Examples

Check that the shirt is right-side-out before ironing it.
The seam should be hidden when the bag is turned right-side-out.
She wore her dress right-side-out after fixing the tag that was showing.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inside out - with the inner surface turned outward
