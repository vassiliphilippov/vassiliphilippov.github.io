spermous

1. ADJ

/ ˈspɜr.məs / (AmE), / ˈspɜː.məs / (BrE)
consisting of or resembling spermatozoa (male reproductive cells).
consisting of or like spermatozoa
medical

Examples

The scientist observed spermous material under the microscope for further analysis.
Some marine organisms release a spermous substance during reproduction.
The stains were used to confirm the presence of spermous cells in the sample.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spermatic - relating to or resembling sperm

Etymology

From Greek *sperma* (seed) + English suffix *-ous* (having the qualities of).
