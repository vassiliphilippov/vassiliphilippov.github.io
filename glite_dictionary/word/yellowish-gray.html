yellowish-gray

1. ADJ

/ ˈjɛloʊɪʃ ˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊ.ɪʃ ˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
(of a color) grey tinged with yellow.
grey with a yellow tinge

Examples

The old photograph had a yellowish-gray tint due to aging.
Mist hung over the fields, giving everything a yellowish-gray appearance at dawn.
His coat was a soft yellowish-gray shade, blending in with the dry grass.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gray - a color between black and white; neutral tone
  • Synonym: yellow-gray - a grayish color with a yellow tint
