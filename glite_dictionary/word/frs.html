FRS

1. NOUN

/ ɛf ɑr ɛs / (AmE), / ɛf ɑː ɛs / (BrE)
abbreviation for the Federal Reserve System, the central banking system of the United States.
abbreviation for Federal Reserve System (U.S. central bank)

Examples

The FRS plays a critical role in regulating the nation's monetary policy.
Economists closely watch the decisions made by the FRS regarding interest rates.
Many banks in the U.S. are governed by the rules set forth by the FRS.

Synset Examples

the Fed seeks to control the United States economy by raising and lowering short-term interest rates and the money supply

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Federal Reserve System - the central banking system of the United States
  • Synonym: Fed - informal abbreviation for Federal Reserve System
