cityfied

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪtiˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪtɪfaɪd / (BrE)
Having the manners, customs, or style characteristic of city people; characteristic of urban life.
having characteristics typical of city people

Examples

Moving to New York made her quite cityfied in both style and manners.
After a few years in the metropolis, his cityfied accent became obvious.
Despite his cityfied appearance, he still missed the quiet of country life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urban - relating to a city or town
  • Antonym: rural - relating to the countryside

Etymology

Formed from 'city' + '-fied', patterned after similar adjectives such as "countryfied".
