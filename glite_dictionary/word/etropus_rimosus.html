Etropus rimosus

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛ.trə.pəs raɪˈmoʊ.səs / (AmE), / ˈɛ.trə.pəs rɪˈməʊ.səs / (BrE)
A species of flounder found from North Carolina to Florida and in the eastern Gulf of Mexico.
species of flounder

Examples

The Etropus rimosus can be found along the coast of the southeastern United States.
Researchers studied the habits of Etropus rimosus in the Gulf of Mexico.
The diet of Etropus rimosus mainly consists of small invertebrates found on the ocean floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flounder - a type of flatfish
