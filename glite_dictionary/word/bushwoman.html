bushwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊʃˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈbʊʃˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who lives in or comes from the Australian bush country.
female inhabitant of the Australian bush

Examples

The bushwoman was skilled at finding her way through the dense forests.
As a bushwoman, she was accustomed to living far from the city.
The documentary featured a bushwoman who shared stories of her daily life in the outback.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bushman - a man who lives in the Australian bush country

Etymology

Formed from 'bush', meaning rural or wild area (especially in Australia), and 'woman'.
