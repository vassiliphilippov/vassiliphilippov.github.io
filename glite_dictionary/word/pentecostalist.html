Pentecostalist

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɛn.tɪˈkɑː.stə.lɪst / (AmE), / ˌpɛn.tɪˈkɒs.tə.lɪst / (BrE)
a member of a Pentecostal religious group or church that emphasizes direct personal experience with God through baptism with the Holy Spirit
member of a Pentecostal religious group

Examples

Her grandmother is a devoted Pentecostalist who attends church services every week.
The city has a growing community of Pentecostalists who organize annual religious festivals.
As a Pentecostalist, he believes in the power of spiritual gifts and speaking in tongues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pentecostal - relating to the Pentecostal movement or its beliefs

Etymology

From Pentecostal (relating to Pentecost and the Christian movement) + -ist (denoting a follower or member).
