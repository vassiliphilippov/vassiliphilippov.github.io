gunman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌnmæn / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnmən / (BrE)
a man who uses a gun, especially one who carries out violent attacks or crimes with a firearm
man who uses a gun, often in crime

Examples

The police are still searching for the gunman who robbed the bank yesterday.
Witnesses said the gunman fired several shots before fleeing the scene.
The notorious gunman was finally apprehended after a long manhunt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shooter - a person who fires a gun
  • Antonym: gunwoman - a woman who uses a gun

Etymology

Formed from 'gun' + 'man', common in English since the 18th century.

2. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌnmæn / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnmən / (BrE)
a man who uses a gun to commit violent acts, especially a professional killer or criminal
armed male criminal or hired killer

Examples

The police are searching for the gunman who robbed the bank this morning.
Witnesses said the gunman fired several shots before fleeing.
After the attack, the gunman disappeared into the crowded streets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hitman - a person hired to kill someone; professional killer
  • Synonym: assassin - a person who murders an important figure, often for political reasons
