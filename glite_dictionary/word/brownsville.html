Brownsville

1. PROPN

/ ˈbraʊnzˌvɪl / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnzˌvɪl / (BrE)
A city in southern Texas, located on the Rio Grande near its mouth at the Gulf of Mexico.
city in southern Texas on the Rio Grande
american

Examples

The port of Brownsville is vital for commercial shipping in southern Texas.
Tourists visit Brownsville for its cultural heritage and proximity to the Gulf of Mexico.
During the annual festival, Brownsville attracts visitors from both sides of the border.
