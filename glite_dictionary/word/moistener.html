moistener

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔɪs.tən.ər / (AmE), / ˈmɔɪs.tən.ə / (BrE)
a device used to make something slightly wet or damp, especially for a specific purpose such as ironing or sealing envelopes
device for making things damp

Examples

The letter was sealed automatically by a moistener built into the machine.
He refilled the moistener to prepare the clothes for ironing.
Most office supply stores carry a moistener for envelopes and stamps.

Synset Examples

he used a dampener to moisten the shirts before he ironed them

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dampener - a device that makes something damp

Etymology

From 'moisten' (to make damp), itself from Old French *moiste* (damp).
