prefrontal

1. ADJ

/ priˈfrʌn.t̬l̩ / (AmE), / priˈfrʌn.təl / (BrE)
situated in front of the frontal bone or region, especially of the brain or skull
in front of the frontal bone or region
medical

Examples

Damage to the prefrontal cortex can affect decision making and personality.
The neuroscientist studied the activity of prefrontal neurons during the experiment.
Fossils show that the prefrontal bone is present in some reptiles and amphibians.

Synset Examples

a prefrontal bone
prefrontal lobes

Etymology

From Latin 'prae-' (before) + 'frons, frontis' (forehead).
