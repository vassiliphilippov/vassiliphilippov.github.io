trustfully

1. ADV

/ ˈtrʌst.fə.li / (AmE), / ˈtrʌst.fʊl.i / (BrE)
In a manner that shows trust or confidence in someone or something.
in a trusting manner

Examples

The child looked up trustfully at her father, believing he would fix everything.
She gave her consent trustfully, confident that her friends would not let her down.
The dog followed the stranger trustfully, without any sign of fear or hesitation.

Synset Examples

his mouth grinned trustfully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trustingly - in a trusting manner; showing trust
  • Antonym: suspiciously - in a distrustful or suspicious manner

2. ADV

/ ˈtrʌst.fə.li / (AmE), / ˈtrʌst.fʊl.i / (BrE)
In a trusting manner; showing confidence or reliance on someone or something.
in a trusting manner

Examples

She smiled trustfully at her new teacher, believing everything would be all right.
The puppy followed its owner trustfully through the busy park.
They looked trustfully to the leader for guidance during the crisis.

Synset Examples

she looked at her father trustingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trustingly - in a way that shows trust
  • Antonym: suspiciously - in a manner showing distrust or suspicion

Etymology

From the adjective 'trustful' + suffix '-ly'.
