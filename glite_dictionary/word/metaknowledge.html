metaknowledge

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛtəˈnɑlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈnɒlɪdʒ / (BrE)
Knowledge about knowledge, especially awareness or understanding of one's own knowledge or of knowledge systems.
knowledge about knowledge

Examples

His research focuses on metaknowledge, examining how experts are aware of their own limitations.
Teachers often encourage students to develop metaknowledge to improve their learning strategies.
Understanding one's metaknowledge is critical in effective decision-making.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metacognition - awareness and understanding of one's own thought processes

Etymology

Formed from meta- (Greek, meaning 'about' or 'beyond') + knowledge.
