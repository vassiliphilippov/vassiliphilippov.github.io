hooknose

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʊkˌnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈhʊk.nəʊz / (BrE)
A nose with a prominent, slightly hooked or aquiline bridge.
nose with a hooked or aquiline bridge

Examples

He had a distinctive hooknose that made his profile unmistakable.
Her grandfather’s hooknose was the talk of family gatherings.
Artists often exaggerate a character’s hooknose to give them a dramatic appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aquiline nose - a nose with a curved or hooked shape like an eagle's beak
