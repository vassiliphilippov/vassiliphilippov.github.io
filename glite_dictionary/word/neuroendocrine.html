neuroendocrine

1. ADJ

/ ˌnʊroʊˈɛndəˌkrin / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈɛndəˌkriːn / (BrE)
Relating to the nervous and endocrine systems, especially as they function together.
relating to nervous and endocrine systems
medical

Examples

Many cancers exhibit neuroendocrine features that affect hormone secretion.
The neuroendocrine system plays a crucial role in the body's response to stress.
Researchers examined neuroendocrine pathways to understand hormonal regulation.

Etymology

From neuro- (related to nerves) + endocrine (relating to glands secreting hormones).
