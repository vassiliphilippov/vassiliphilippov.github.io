potency

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊtənsi / (AmE), / ˈpəʊtənsi / (BrE)
the power or authority to give orders or make decisions
power or authority to act

Examples

The king's potency over his subjects was undisputed in ancient times.
Through constitutional reform, the parliament increased its potency within the government.
His position brings considerable potency in shaping state policy.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: authority - the power or right to give orders or make decisions
