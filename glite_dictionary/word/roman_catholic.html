Roman Catholic

1. ADJ

/ ˌroʊ.mən ˈkæθ.ə.lɪk / (AmE), / ˌrəʊ.mən ˈkæθ.ə.lɪk / (BrE)
of or relating to the Roman Catholic Church, its members, or its beliefs and practices
relating to the Roman Catholic Church

Examples

She attended a Roman Catholic school for twelve years.
The wedding ceremony was held in a traditional Roman Catholic church.
His beliefs are strongly influenced by his Roman Catholic upbringing.

Synset Examples

the Roman Catholic Church

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Catholic - of or relating to the universal Christian church; or to the Roman Catholic Church

Etymology

Roman referring to Rome (the seat of the papacy), and Catholic from Greek *katholikos* (universal)
