instagood

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪnstəˌɡʊd / (AmE), / ˈɪnstəˌɡʊd / (BrE)
used to describe a photo or post on Instagram that is visually appealing or likely to receive many likes and upvotes.
likely to be popular on Instagram
informal slang

Examples

Her latest selfie was so instagood that it quickly went viral among her followers.
Sunset photos usually get tagged as instagood because they are popular and eye-catching.
He always tries to make his brunch look as instagood as possible before posting.

Etymology

Blend of 'Instagram' and 'good'.
