Ficus deltoidea

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪkəs dɛlˈtɔɪdiə / (AmE), / ˈfaɪkəs dɛlˈtɔɪdɪə / (BrE)
A shrub or small tree of the fig genus, often grown as a houseplant, with foliage resembling that of mistletoe.
a fig shrub or small tree, houseplant

Examples

The Ficus deltoidea is prized for its distinctive, mistletoe-like foliage.
She added a Ficus deltoidea to her collection of indoor plants.
Botanists often study Ficus deltoidea for its unique adaptation to dry climates.

Etymology

From Latin; 'ficus' meaning 'fig' and 'deltoidea' referring to the triangular (delta-shaped) leaves.
