Trentino-Alto Adige

1. PROPN

/ trɛnˌtinoʊ ˌæltoʊ ɑːˈdiʒeɪ / (AmE), / trɛnˌtiːnoʊ ˌæltoʊ ælˈtɪːdʒeɪ / (BrE)
a region in northeastern Italy bordering Austria, known for its mountainous landscapes and bilingual Italian-German culture.
region of northeastern Italy

Examples

The Dolomites are one of the top attractions in Trentino-Alto Adige.
Trentino-Alto Adige is known for its stunning mountains and multicultural heritage.
Many people visit Trentino-Alto Adige each year for skiing and hiking.

Etymology

Named from the cities of Trento and Alto Adige (Upper Adige), referring to the River Adige. Italian origin.
