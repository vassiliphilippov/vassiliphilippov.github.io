stalkless

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɔk.ləs / (AmE), / ˈstɔːk.ləs / (BrE)
(of a plant part or organism) attached directly by the base, without a stalk.
without a stalk, attached directly by the base

Examples

Unlike most leaves, the stalkless leaves of this species grow directly from the stem.
Several stalkless flowers can be found clustered at the top of the plant.
The barnacle is described as stalkless because it attaches directly to rocks.

Synset Examples

sessile flowers
the shell of a sessile barnacle is attached directly to a substrate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sessile - (of a plant part or organism) attached directly by the base, not having a stalk

Etymology

Formed from 'stalk' + '-less' (without).
