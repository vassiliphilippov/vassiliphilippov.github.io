cowman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈkaʊˌmæn / (BrE)
a man employed to take care of cattle, especially on a ranch or farm, often working on horseback
man who looks after cattle

Examples

The cowman rode out early each morning to check on the herd.
After years of experience, he became the most trusted cowman on the ranch.
The young boy dreamed of becoming a cowman like his father.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cowboy - a man who herds and tends cattle, especially in the western US, usually on horseback
  • Synonym: cattleman - a person who owns or tends cattle

Etymology

Formed from 'cow' + 'man', meaning a man who works with cows.
