revivification

1. NOUN

/ rɪˌvɪvəfəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˌvɪvɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of bringing something back into activity, existence, or prominence
restoration to activity or prominence

Examples

The revivification of the historic district has drawn tourists back to the town.
Some scientists are researching the revivification of ancient seeds.
The director was praised for the revivification of classic theater works in modern settings.

Synset Examples

the revival of trade
a revival of a neglected play by Moliere
the Gothic revival in architecture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revival - an instance of something becoming active or important again
  • Synonym: reanimation - the act of bringing something back to life or activity

Etymology

From Latin 'revivificare' (to make alive again), via French 'révivification'.
