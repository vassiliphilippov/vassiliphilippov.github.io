philanderer

1. NOUN

/ fəˈlændərər / (AmE), / fɪˈlændərə / (BrE)
a man who has casual or short-term sexual relationships with many women
man with many casual relationships

Examples

Rumors spread that he was a notorious philanderer in his youth.
Despite his reputation as a philanderer, he claimed to be looking for true love.
The novel's main character is a charming but irresponsible philanderer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: womanizer - a man who engages in numerous casual sexual affairs with women
  • Antonym: monogamist - a person who has only one partner at a time or throughout life

Etymology

From Greek 'philandros' (fond of men), from 'philos' (loving) + 'anēr' (man).
