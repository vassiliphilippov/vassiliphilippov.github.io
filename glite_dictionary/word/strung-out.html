strung-out

1. ADJ

/ strʌŋd aʊt / (AmE), / strʌŋd aʊt / (BrE)
addicted to drugs, especially to the extent that one's appearance or behavior is adversely affected
addicted to drugs
informal

Examples

After years of substance abuse, he looked completely strung-out and exhausted.
The counselor works with strung-out teens to help them recover from addiction.
She was found strung-out in the alley by the police.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: addicted - having a compulsive need for a habit-forming substance
  • Antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol or drugs; not drunk or addicted
