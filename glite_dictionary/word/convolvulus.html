convolvulus

1. NOUN

/ kənˈvɑlvjələs / (AmE), / kənˈvɒlvjʊləs / (BrE)
A twining or trailing plant of the genus Convolvulus, which includes various species commonly known as bindweed or morning glory.
plant of the genus Convolvulus

Examples

The garden was overrun with convolvulus climbing up the fences and shrubs.
She identified the white-flowered convolvulus growing along the roadside.
Farmers often find convolvulus a troublesome weed due to its rapid spreading nature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bindweed - common name for various Convolvulus species
  • Synonym: morning glory - common name for several flowering plants of the Convolvulaceae family

Etymology

From Latin 'convolvulus', meaning bindweed, from 'convolvere' (to wind together).
