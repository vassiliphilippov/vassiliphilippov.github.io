toothpowder

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːθˌpaʊdər / (AmE), / ˈtuːθˌpaʊdə / (BrE)
A substance in powdered form used to clean teeth instead of toothpaste.
powdered substance for cleaning teeth

Examples

Some people still prefer using toothpowder instead of toothpaste for oral hygiene.
The pharmacist recommended a herbal toothpowder for sensitive teeth.
He sprinkled a bit of toothpowder onto his wet toothbrush before brushing his teeth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toothpaste - a paste used for cleaning the teeth
