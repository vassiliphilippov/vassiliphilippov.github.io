mutative

1. ADJ

/ ˈmjuːt̬eɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈmjuːtətɪv / (BrE)
relating to, causing, or marked by genetic mutation
relating to genetic mutation

Examples

Some viruses undergo rapid mutative changes, making them hard to control with a single vaccine.
Scientists are studying the mutative effects of radiation on plant DNA.
The film explores the mutative nature of identity in a changing world.

Synset Examples

the mutative processes of nature

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mutational - relating to or resulting from mutation

Etymology

From Latin 'mutare' (to change), via mutation + -ive.
