Madrid

1. PROPN

/ məˈdrɪd / (AmE), / məˈdrɪd / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Spain, located in the center of the country, known for its cultural, political, and economic importance and renowned art museums
capital city of Spain

Examples

My friend moved to Madrid to work at a technology company in the city center.
The Prado Museum in Madrid houses masterpieces by renowned Spanish artists.
After visiting Barcelona and Seville, we spent a week exploring the sights of Madrid.

Etymology

From Spanish Madrid, of uncertain origin, possibly from Arabic or pre-Roman roots.
