linkswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪŋksˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋksˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who plays the game of golf
female golfer

Examples

The club held a special tournament for experienced linkswomen last weekend.
As a passionate linkswoman, she spends most weekends on the course.
The championship was won by a young linkswoman making her debut.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golfer - person who plays golf
  • Synonym: linksman - man who plays the game of golf

Etymology

Patterned after 'linksman', from 'links' (golf course) + 'woman'.
