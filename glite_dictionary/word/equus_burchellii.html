Equus burchellii

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːkwəs bɜːrˈkɛli.aɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛkwəs bɜːˈkɛlaɪ / (BrE)
the scientific name for the plains zebra, a species of zebra native to central and eastern Africa
scientific name for the plains zebra

Examples

The Equus burchellii is the most common and widespread species of zebra in Africa.
Researchers are studying the migration patterns of Equus burchellii across the Serengeti.
The black and white stripes of Equus burchellii help it blend into the grassy plains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plains zebra - zebra species native to African plains

Etymology

From Latin; 'Equus' meaning 'horse' and 'burchellii' in honor of explorer William John Burchell.
