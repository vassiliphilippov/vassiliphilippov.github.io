diagonally

1. ADV

/ daɪˈæɡənəli / (AmE), / daɪˈaɡənəli / (BrE)
In a diagonal direction; along or in the manner of a diagonal line.
in a diagonal direction or manner

Examples

The chess piece moves diagonally across the board.
Sunlight entered the room diagonally through the window, casting long shadows.
We parked diagonally to fit both cars in the driveway.

Synset Examples

she lives diagonally across the street from us

Related Terms

  • Antonym: vertically - in an up-and-down direction
  • Antonym: horizontally - in a side-to-side direction

Etymology

From diagonal + -ly, where 'diagonal' is from Greek *diagōnios* (from corner to corner).
