nourished

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɜrɪʃt / (AmE), / ˈnʌrɪʃt / (BrE)
Provided with the food or substances necessary for good health and growth.
having received sufficient nourishment

Examples

After weeks of care, the rescued animals finally looked nourished and healthy.
Properly nourished children have more energy and focus at school.
The plants appeared strong and well nourished after the rain.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: malnourished - suffering from lack of proper nutrition
  • Synonym: well-nourished - having received proper and sufficient nourishment

Etymology

From the verb 'nourish' + past participle suffix '-ed'; ultimately from Latin *nutrire* (to feed, nourish).
