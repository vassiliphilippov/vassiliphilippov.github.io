Atlantis

1. PROPN

/ əˈtlæntɪs / (AmE), / əˈtlantɪs / (BrE)
a legendary island, first described by Plato, said to have been located in the Atlantic Ocean and destroyed by a natural disaster
legendary island said to have sunk

Examples

Many explorers have searched for the lost city of Atlantis over the centuries.
The myth of Atlantis has inspired countless books and movies.
According to legend, Atlantis vanished beneath the waves in a single day and night.

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Ἀτλαντίς (Atlantís), meaning 'island of Atlas'.
