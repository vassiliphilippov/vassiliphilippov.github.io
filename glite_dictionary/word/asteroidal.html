asteroidal

1. ADJ

/ ˌæstəˈrɔɪd(ə)l / (AmE), / ˌæstəˈrɔɪd(ə)l / (BrE)
of or relating to an asteroid, or resembling an asteroid in form or characteristics
relating to an asteroid

Examples

The spacecraft sent back images of the asteroidal surface in stunning detail.
Scientists are studying asteroidal debris to learn about the early solar system.
The object had an asteroidal shape, appearing rocky and irregular.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asteroid - a small rocky body orbiting the sun

Etymology

From 'asteroid' + -al.
