evolutionist

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌiːvəˈluːʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes in or supports the theory of evolution, especially in biology
person who supports theory of evolution

Examples

As an evolutionist, she often debated with colleagues about the mechanisms of natural selection.
Many evolutionists support the evidence presented by fossils and genetics.
His research established him as a leading evolutionist in the scientific community.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: creationist - a person who believes the universe and life originated from specific acts of divine creation

Etymology

From 'evolution' + suffix '-ist', indicating a person who practices or is concerned with something.
