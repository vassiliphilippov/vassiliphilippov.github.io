sandaled

1. ADJ

/ ˈsæn.dəld / (AmE), / ˈsæn.dəld / (BrE)
wearing sandals on one's feet
wearing sandals

Examples

The sandaled children played happily in the sun-drenched courtyard.
Her sandaled feet left prints in the soft, white sand as she walked along the shore.
A group of sandaled tourists gathered around the ancient temple, listening to their guide.

Etymology

From sandal + -ed, forming an adjective meaning 'having sandals on'.
