quesadilla

1. NOUN

/ keɪsəˈdiːjə / (AmE), / keɪsəˈdiːjə / (BrE)
A Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla filled with cheese, often grilled or toasted until the cheese melts.
Mexican cheese-filled tortilla dish

Examples

I ordered a chicken quesadilla for lunch at the Mexican restaurant.
The melted cheese inside the quesadilla made it extra delicious.
She likes to add mushrooms and peppers to her homemade quesadillas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taco - A Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various mixtures
  • Synonym: burrito - A Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla rolled around a filling

Etymology

From Spanish 'quesadilla', from 'queso' meaning 'cheese'.
