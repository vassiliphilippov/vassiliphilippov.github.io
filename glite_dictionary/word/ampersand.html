ampersand

1. NOUN

/ ˈæm.pɚ.sænd / (AmE), / ˈæm.pə.sænd / (BrE)
the symbol ‘&’, used as a punctuation mark to represent the word ‘and’
the ‘&’ punctuation mark meaning ‘and’

Examples

The ampersand is often used in company names instead of ‘and’.
She replaced the word ‘and’ with an ampersand in the title.
Many fonts have a unique design for the ampersand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: and - the conjunction used to connect words or phrases

Etymology

From the phrase 'and per se and', meaning 'and [the symbol] by itself is and'.
