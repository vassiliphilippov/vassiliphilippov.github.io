ledgewoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛdʒˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈlɛdʒˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman employed in a quarry to split off blocks of stone from a ledge.
female quarry worker who splits stone

Examples

The experienced ledgewoman skillfully separated the granite blocks from the quarry wall.
As a ledgewoman, she spent long hours in the open, working with heavy tools.
The company hired an additional ledgewoman to keep up with the increasing demand for stone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ledgeworker - a person who splits off blocks of stone in a quarry
  • Synonym: ledge man - a male quarry worker who splits off blocks of stone

Etymology

Formed from 'ledge' (a slab or layer of stone) and 'woman'.
