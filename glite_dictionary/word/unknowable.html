unknowable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈnoʊ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈnəʊ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to know or understand; not able to be known.
impossible to know or understand

Examples

Some aspects of the universe may remain unknowable to science.
She found his true feelings completely unknowable.
The origins of consciousness might be forever unknowable.

Synset Examples

the unknowable mysteries of life

Related Terms

  • Antonym: knowable - able to be known or understood

Etymology

From un- (not) + knowable (able to be known).
