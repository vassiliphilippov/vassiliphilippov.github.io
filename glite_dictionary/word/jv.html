JV

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒeɪ ˈvi / (AmE), / ˌdʒeɪ ˈviː / (BrE)
a sports team representing a school or college that competes at a level below the varsity team
junior-level school sports team
american

Examples

My brother plays on the JV basketball team at his high school.
After a season on JV, she hopes to make varsity next year.
The school's JV soccer squad won their game 3-1.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: varsity - the main or principal team representing a school or college in sports

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'junior varsity'.
