lively

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪvli / (AmE), / ˈlaɪvli / (BrE)
Full of life, energy, or enthusiasm; animated or spirited.
full of life and energy

Examples

The children played a lively game in the backyard until sunset.
Her lively personality made her popular among her classmates.
We enjoyed a lively debate during the meeting.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: energetic - showing or involving great activity or vitality
  • Antonym: dull - lacking interest or energy

Etymology

From Middle English 'lyvely', from Old English 'līflīc', meaning 'living' or 'animated'.
