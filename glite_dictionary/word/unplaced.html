unplaced

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpleɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈpleɪst / (BrE)
not ranked among the first three competitors in a race or contest; not achieving a top-three position.
not in the top three in a competition

Examples

Despite training hard all season, she finished unplaced in the regional finals.
Of the eight horses he entered in the race, three were unplaced.
He was disappointed to remain unplaced after such a strong performance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: placed - ranked among the first three in a competition

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'placed' (from place as in a rank or position).
