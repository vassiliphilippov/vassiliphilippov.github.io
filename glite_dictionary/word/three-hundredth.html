three-hundredth

1. ADJ

/ ˌθriː ˈhʌndrədθ / (AmE), / ˌθriː ˈhʌndrɪdθ / (BrE)
in the position numbered 300 in a sequence; one of 300 equal parts of something
ordinal number for 300th place or part

Examples

He finished in three-hundredth place out of a thousand runners.
This is the three-hundredth anniversary of the city's founding.
Only one three-hundredth of the sample was contaminated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: three-hundred - the cardinal number 300
  • Degree Higher: three-hundred-first - the ordinal number following three-hundredth (301st)
  • Degree Lower: two-hundred-ninety-ninth - the ordinal number preceding three-hundredth (299th)
