curry

1. VERB

/ ˈkɜri / (AmE), / ˈkʌri / (BrE)
To season food with a mixture of spices, especially in the style of Indian cooking.
season with Indian-style spices

Examples

She likes to curry vegetables with a blend of fragrant spices.
The chef curried the chicken before simmering it in coconut milk.
He is learning how to curry lentils using traditional Indian recipes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spice - to add spices or flavoring to food

Etymology

From Tamil *kari* (sauce, relish for rice).

2. VERB

/ ˈkɜri / (AmE), / ˈkʌri / (BrE)
to treat (leather) during tanning by incorporating fats, oils, or dressing agents to make it supple
treat leather with fat during tanning

Examples

The workers curry the hides to make high-quality leather.
After tanning, the leather was curried with special oils to soften it.
Traditional processes involved currying to enhance the look and durability of leather goods.

Synset Examples

curry tanned leather

Etymology

From Middle English 'curayen', from Old French 'corroyer' (to prepare, dress leather).

3. VERB

/ ˈkɜːri / (AmE), / ˈkʌri / (BrE)
to groom an animal, especially by brushing its coat to give a neat appearance
to groom an animal by brushing

Examples

She used a soft brush to curry the horse after riding.
Grooms carefully curry their animals before a show to ensure they look their best.
Regularly currying your dog helps maintain a healthy and shiny coat.

Synset Examples

groom the dogs
dress the horses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: groom - to clean or maintain the appearance of an animal by brushing, combing, etc.

Etymology

From Middle English 'curreien', from Old French 'correer' (to curry a horse), from Vulgar Latin *conredare (to equip).

Source: WordNet

4. NOUN

/ ˈkɜːri / (AmE), / ˈkʌri / (BrE)
A spicy dish originating in South Asia, made with meat, vegetables, or both, cooked in a sauce of aromatic spices, often served with rice.
spicy South Asian dish with sauce

Examples

We enjoyed a delicious chicken curry for dinner last night.
Vegetarians often make curry with chickpeas and potatoes.
She ordered a spicy lamb curry with basmati rice at the restaurant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stew - a dish of meat and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid

Etymology

From Tamil 'kari' (sauce, relish for rice), adopted into English during colonial times.

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: n
