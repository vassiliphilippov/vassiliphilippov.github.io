Nebcin

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛbˌsɪn / (AmE), / ˈnɛb.sɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked antibiotic drug particularly effective against Gram-negative bacteria; brand name for tobramycin.
antibiotic drug (brand of tobramycin)
medical

Examples

After her infection was identified as Gram-negative, the doctor prescribed Nebcin to treat it.
The pharmacy stocks Nebcin as one of their injectable antibiotics.
Patients receiving Nebcin need periodic hearing tests due to potential side effects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tobramycin - the generic antibiotic of which Nebcin is a brand
