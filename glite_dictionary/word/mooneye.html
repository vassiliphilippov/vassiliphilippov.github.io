mooneye

1. NOUN

/ ˈmunˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈmuːn.aɪ / (BrE)
A recurrent inflammatory condition of the eye in horses, sometimes leading to blindness.
recurrent inflammatory eye disease in horses
medical

Examples

The veterinarian diagnosed the horse with mooneye after it showed signs of recurrent eye inflammation.
Failure to treat mooneye early may result in permanent blindness in affected horses.
Farmers must watch for symptoms of mooneye to ensure their livestock's eye health.
