mutely

1. ADV

/ ˈmjuːtli / (AmE), / ˈmjuːtli / (BrE)
without speaking; in a silent manner
without speaking

Examples

He sat mutely in the corner, watching the conversation unfold.
The children listened mutely to their teacher's instructions.
She nodded mutely, unable to find the right words.

Synset Examples

he sat mutely next to her

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silently - in a quiet or noiseless manner

Etymology

From 'mute' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
