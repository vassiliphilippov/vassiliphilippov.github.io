Saimiri sciureus

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈmaɪri saɪˈkwɪəriəs / (AmE), / saɪˈmɪəri saɪˈkwɪəriəs / (BrE)
A small, long-tailed monkey of Central and South America with greenish fur and a black muzzle, also known as the common squirrel monkey.
species of small Central/South American monkey

Examples

Researchers observed the Saimiri sciureus in its natural rainforest habitat.
The Saimiri sciureus is known for its agility and playful behavior.
Zoos often house the Saimiri sciureus because of its popularity with visitors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squirrel monkey - common name for monkeys of the genus Saimiri

Etymology

From Latin: genus Saimiri and species sciureus, meaning squirrel-like.
