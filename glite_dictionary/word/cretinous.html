cretinous

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɛt.nəs / (AmE), / ˈkrɛt.ɪ.nəs / (BrE)
afflicted with cretinism; extremely foolish or idiotic
afflicted with cretinism; very foolish

Examples

The plan he proposed was so cretinous that no one supported it.
She was frustrated by her boss's cretinous decision-making.
Calling someone cretinous is both rude and inaccurate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiotic - extremely stupid or foolish
  • Synonym: cretin - a person afflicted with cretinism; a very stupid person

Etymology

From French crétin (person with cretinism), ultimately from Swiss French dialect.
