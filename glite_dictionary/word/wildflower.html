wildflower

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪldˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪldˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A flower that grows naturally in the wild, not deliberately planted or cultivated.
flower growing wild, not cultivated

Examples

In the meadow, colorful wildflowers bloomed among the tall grasses.
She picked a bouquet of wildflowers during her morning walk in the countryside.
Many wildflowers attract bees and butterflies with their vibrant colors.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: weed - an unwanted wild plant
