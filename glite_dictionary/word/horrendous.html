horrendous

1. ADJ

/ həˈrɛndəs / (AmE), / hɒˈrɛndəs / (BrE)
extremely shocking, dreadful, or causing fear or horror
extremely dreadful or frightening

Examples

The city suffered a horrendous earthquake that destroyed hundreds of buildings.
She was in a horrendous car accident but survived without serious injuries.
The movie depicted horrendous acts of violence that were difficult to watch.

Synset Examples

the awful war

a fearful howling

a terrible curse

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrible - causing great fear or suffering
  • Synonym: dreadful - extremely bad, unpleasant, or shocking
  • Synonym: awful - causing fear, horror, or shock
  • Antonym: pleasant - giving a sense of happy satisfaction or enjoyment

Etymology

From Latin 'horrendus' (to be shuddered at), from 'horrere' (to bristle, shudder).
