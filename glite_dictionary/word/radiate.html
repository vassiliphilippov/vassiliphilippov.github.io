radiate

1. ADJ

/ ˈreɪdiˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈreɪdɪeɪt / (BrE)
having parts arranged like rays emerging from a central point; spreading out from a common center.
arranged like rays from a center

Examples

The petals of the daisy flower are radiate, extending outward from the yellow center.
Many starfish have a clearly radiate structure, with arms pointing out in all directions.
The architects designed the plaza in a radiate pattern, with pathways spreading from the central fountain.

Related Terms
