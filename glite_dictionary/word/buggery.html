buggery

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌɡəri / (AmE), / ˈbʌɡəri / (BrE)
anal intercourse, especially between men; historically, a legal term for such acts when regarded as a crime
anal intercourse (legal/historical term)
vulgar legal

Examples

During the 19th century, buggery was considered a serious criminal offense in many countries.
The court found him guilty of buggery under the outdated statute.
Activists have campaigned to repeal laws that criminalize buggery and similar acts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sodomy - anal or oral intercourse, especially between men; legal term

Etymology

From Middle French 'bougrerie', from 'bougre' (heretic, sodomite), from Medieval Latin 'Bulgarus' (Bulgarian), referring to heretics in Bulgaria.
