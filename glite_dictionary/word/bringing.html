bringing

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of delivering or distributing something, such as goods, mail, or information
the act of delivering something

Examples

The bringing of fresh supplies restored the team's energy.
No one wanted to take responsibility for the bringing of bad news.
His job involved the bringing of important documents to the office every morning.

Synset Examples

his reluctant delivery of bad news

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delivery - the act of bringing something to a particular person or place
  • Synonym: distribution - the act of sharing or spreading something over an area

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'bring' with the suffix '-ing'.
