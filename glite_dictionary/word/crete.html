Crete

1. PROPN

/ krit / (AmE), / kriːt / (BrE)
the largest Greek island in the Mediterranean, known as the center of the ancient Minoan civilization
largest Greek island; site of Minoan civilization

Examples

Many tourists visit Crete to explore its ancient ruins and beautiful beaches.
The Minoan civilization on Crete is considered one of the earliest in Europe.
She spent a summer studying archaeology on Crete with her university class.

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Κρήτη (Krētē).
