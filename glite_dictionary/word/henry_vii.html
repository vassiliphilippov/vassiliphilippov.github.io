Henry VII

1. PROPN

/ ˌhɛnri ðə ˈsɛvənθ / (AmE), / ˌhɛnri ðə ˈsɛvənθ / (BrE)
the first Tudor king of England (reigned 1485–1509), who ended the Wars of the Roses and united the houses of York and Lancaster.
first Tudor king of England, 1485–1509

Examples

Henry VII established a strong central government after decades of civil war in England.
After defeating Richard III at the Battle of Bosworth, Henry VII was crowned king in 1485.
The marriage of Henry VII to Elizabeth of York helped to end the dynastic conflict known as the Wars of the Roses.
