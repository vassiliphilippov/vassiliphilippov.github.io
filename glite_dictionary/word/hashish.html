hashish

1. NOUN

/ hæˈʃiʃ / (AmE), / hæˈʃiːʃ / (BrE)
a drug made from the resin of the hemp plant, used for its psychoactive effects
psychoactive drug from hemp resin

Examples

The police seized several kilograms of hashish during the raid.
He claimed that smoking hashish helped him relax and relieve stress.
Some countries have strict laws against the possession of hashish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marijuana - dried leaves and flowers of the hemp plant used as a drug
  • Synonym: cannabis - the hemp plant, especially as a drug

Etymology

From Arabic ḥashīsh (dry herb, grass), adopted into European languages in the 19th century.
