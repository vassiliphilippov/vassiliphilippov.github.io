unrivalled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈraɪvəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈraɪvəld / (BrE)
better or greater than anyone or anything else; having no rival or equal.
without equal or competition

Examples

Her unrivalled performance in the competition impressed both judges and spectators.
The restaurant is famous for its unrivalled views of the city skyline.
Thanks to his unrivalled expertise, the project finished ahead of schedule.

Synset Examples

matchless beauty
the team's nonpareil center fielder

the one and only Muhammad Ali
a peerless scholar
infamy unmatched in the Western world
wrote with unmatchable clarity
unrivaled mastery of her art

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peerless - without equal; unmatched
  • Synonym: matchless - unable to be matched or equaled
  • Synonym: unequaled - better or greater than anything else

Etymology

From un- (not) + rivalled, based on rival (from Latin rivālis, competitor).
