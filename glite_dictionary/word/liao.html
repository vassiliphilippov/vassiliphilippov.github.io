Liao

1. PROPN

/ liˈaʊ / (AmE), / liˈaʊ / (BrE)
a dynasty that ruled Manchuria and northeastern China from 947 to 1125
Chinese dynasty (947–1125)

Examples

The Liao dynasty was known for its sophisticated administrative system.
Scholars have studied the art and culture that flourished under the Liao.
The Liao ruled a vast region that included parts of modern-day northern China and Mongolia.

Etymology

From Mandarin 遼 (Liáo), the name of the dynasty.
