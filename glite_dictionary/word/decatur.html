Decatur

1. PROPN

/ dɪˈkeɪtər / (AmE), / dɪˈkeɪtə / (BrE)
A city in central Illinois, United States, known as a place where Abraham Lincoln practiced law.
city in Illinois, USA

Examples

Abraham Lincoln worked as a lawyer in Decatur before becoming president.
The soybean industry plays a vital role in the economy of Decatur.
During our Midwest road trip, we spent two nights in Decatur.

Etymology

Named after Stephen Decatur, an American naval hero.
