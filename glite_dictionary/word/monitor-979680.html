monitor

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːnɪtər / (AmE), / ˈmɒnɪtə / (BrE)
a device that displays images or information from a computer or other electronic source on a screen
device that displays images or data on a screen

Examples

The new computer comes with a high-resolution monitor that makes everything look sharper.
She adjusted the height of her monitor to improve her posture while working.
The technician connected the laptop to the external monitor for a larger display.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: display - an electronic device used for showing images or information

Etymology

From Latin 'monitor' (warner, adviser), later applied to electronic displays in the mid-20th century.
