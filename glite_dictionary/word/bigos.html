bigos

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːɡoʊs / (AmE), / ˈbiːɡɒs / (BrE)
a traditional Polish stew made from cabbage and various kinds of meat, often including sauerkraut and sausage
Polish cabbage and meat stew

Examples

My grandmother makes the best bigos every holiday season, filling the house with its rich aroma.
A bowl of steaming bigos is especially comforting during the cold winter months.
Many Polish restaurants abroad offer bigos on their menu as a traditional specialty.

Etymology

From Polish 'bigos' (stew of cabbage and meat).
