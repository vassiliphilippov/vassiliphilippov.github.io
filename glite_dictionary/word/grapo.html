GRAPO

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrɑːpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡræpəʊ / (BrE)
a Spanish left-wing militant group active from the 1970s, seeking to overthrow the government and establish a Marxist-Leninist regime
Spanish left-wing militant group

Examples

The Spanish police intensified efforts to dismantle GRAPO after a series of attacks.
Several members of GRAPO were arrested during a raid in Madrid.
GRAPO has been designated a terrorist organization by the Spanish government.

Synset Examples

GRAPO is vehemently opposed to the United States

Etymology

From Spanish, acronym for 'Grupos de Resistencia Antifascista Primero de Octubre' (Antifascist Resistance Groups First of October).
