Tours

1. PROPN

/ tʊərz / (AmE), / tʊəz / (BrE)
A city in western France, situated on the Loire River, known for its historical significance and architecture.
city in western France on the Loire River

Examples

She visited Tours last summer to see the famous cathedrals.
The Loire Valley, including Tours, is renowned for its beautiful châteaux.
Many tourists stop in Tours when exploring western France.

Etymology

From French 'Tours', originating from the Latin 'Turones', the name of the local Gallic tribe.
