Hans Christian Andersen

1. PROPN

/ hænz ˈkrɪstʃən ˈændərsən / (AmE), / hans ˈkrɪstjən ˈandəsən / (BrE)
(1805–1875) A Danish author famous for his fairy tales such as 'The Little Mermaid' and 'The Ugly Duckling'.
famous Danish fairy tale author

Examples

Many children grew up hearing stories written by Hans Christian Andersen.
The fairy tales of Hans Christian Andersen have inspired countless adaptations in film and theatre.
Copenhagen honors Hans Christian Andersen with a statue in his memory.
