Citta del Vaticano

1. PROPN

/ ˌtʃiːtə dɛl vəˌtɪˈkɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˌtʃɪtə dɛl vəˌtɪˈkɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
The capital city of Vatican City State, an independent city-state enclaved within Rome, Italy, and the headquarters of the Roman Catholic Church.
capital city of Vatican City

Examples

Many pilgrims travel to Citta del Vaticano to attend religious ceremonies at St. Peter’s Basilica.
The archives of Citta del Vaticano contain centuries of historical documents.
She studied Renaissance art during her visit to Citta del Vaticano last summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Vatican City - the independent city-state enclosed within Rome, home to the Pope

Etymology

Italian, literally 'City of the Vatican'.
