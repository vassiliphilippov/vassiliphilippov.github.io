stand-up

1. ADJ

/ ˈstændˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈstandˌʌp / (BrE)
(of a bar, desk, or similar object) designed or intended to be used while standing rather than sitting.
designed for use while standing

Examples

The new office features a stand-up desk to encourage employees to move more during the day.
We had drinks at a trendy stand-up bar in the city center.
Using a stand-up counter makes it easier for people to mingle at parties.

Synset Examples

a stand-up bar
a stand-up comic
