novelization

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɑːvələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒvələˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of adapting a story, film, or other work into a novel
the adaptation of a work into a novel

Examples

The novelization of the popular film became a bestseller shortly after its release.
She was hired to write the novelization of the television series.
Many readers enjoy the extra details found in a good novelization of a movie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adaptation - the process of changing something to suit a new purpose or medium

Etymology

From 'novel' + '-ization', patterned on other nouns denoting the process of making into a form.
