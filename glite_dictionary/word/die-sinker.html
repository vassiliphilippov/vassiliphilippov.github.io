die-sinker

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪˌsɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈdaɪˌsɪŋkə / (BrE)
a person who engraves or makes dies, typically for stamping or coining metal
person who engraves or makes dies

Examples

The company hired a skilled die-sinker to design its new series of commemorative coins.
As a die-sinker, she spent hours perfecting intricate patterns on metal stamps.
The craftsmanship of a master die-sinker is evident in every detail of the medal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: engraver - a person who carves or etches designs onto a surface

Etymology

From 'die' (engraved stamp for shaping or marking) + 'sinker' (one who causes to sink or engraves).
