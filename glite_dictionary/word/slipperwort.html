slipperwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈslɪpɚˌwɝt / (AmE), / ˈslɪpəˌwɜːt / (BrE)
a garden plant of the genus Calceolaria, with flowers having a large, inflated, slipper-shaped lower lip
ornamental plant with slipper-shaped flowers

Examples

The vibrant yellow slipperwort bloomed in the corner of the greenhouse.
Gardeners often choose slipperwort for its unusual, decorative flowers.
A single slipperwort can brighten up a shaded window sill with its unique shape.

Etymology

Named for its slipper-shaped flowers; from 'slipper' and 'wort' (Old English for plant or herb).
