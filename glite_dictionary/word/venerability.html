venerability

1. NOUN

/ ˌvɛnərəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌvɛnərəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being worthy of respect or reverence
quality of being worthy of respect

Examples

The venerability of the ancient philosopher earned him admiration across generations.
When addressing the elders, everyone recognized their venerability and wisdom.
The venerability of the historic church was evident in the way visitors spoke in hushed tones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: veneration - great respect or reverence
  • Synonym: reverence - deep respect for someone or something

Etymology

From Latin 'venerabilis' (worthy of respect) + English '-ity'.
