undiplomatically

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnˌdɪpləˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌdɪpləˈmatɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner lacking tact or diplomacy; without being diplomatic
without diplomacy; tactlessly

Examples

He spoke undiplomatically about his colleague's mistakes at the meeting.
She undiplomatically criticized their proposal, making everyone uncomfortable.
The manager handled the complaint undiplomatically, upsetting the client further.

Synset Examples

she declined the invitation undiplomatically

Related Terms

  • Antonym: diplomatically - in a diplomatic or tactful manner

Etymology

From 'undiplomatic' + '-ally', where 'undiplomatic' means lacking diplomacy or tact.
