chiropody

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɑːpədi / (AmE), / kɪˈrɒpədi / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the feet
medical care of the feet
medical british

Examples

She visited a chiropody clinic for treatment of her foot pain.
The hospital offers chiropody services for elderly patients.
He decided to study chiropody after suffering from chronic foot problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: podiatry - the medical care and treatment of the feet

Etymology

From Greek 'cheir' (hand) and 'pous, podos' (foot), originally referring to care for both hands and feet.
