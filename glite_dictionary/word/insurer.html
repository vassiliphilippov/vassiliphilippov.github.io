insurer

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈʃʊrər / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃʊərə / (BrE)
a company or person that provides insurance, especially as a business
a company that provides insurance

Examples

The insurer refused to pay out because the claim was not covered under the policy.
Many homeowners shop around to find an insurer offering competitive rates.
After the accident, her insurer processed the claim quickly and paid for the damages.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: policyholder - the person who owns an insurance policy

Etymology

From insure + -er, following the pattern of agent nouns.
