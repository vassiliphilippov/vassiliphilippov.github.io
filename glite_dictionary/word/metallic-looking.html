metallic-looking

1. ADJ

/ məˈtælɪk ˈlʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / məˈtalɪk ˈlʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
having an appearance similar to that of metal; resembling metal in sheen or texture.
resembling metal in appearance

Examples

The sculpture had a metallic-looking surface that reflected the sunlight beautifully.
She wore a metallic-looking dress that shimmered under the party lights.
The new phone case is made of plastic but is designed to be metallic-looking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metallic - having the characteristics of metal
