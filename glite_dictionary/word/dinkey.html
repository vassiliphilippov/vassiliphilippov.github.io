dinkey

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈdɪŋki / (BrE)
A small locomotive, often used on industrial or narrow-gauge railways for light shunting or hauling tasks.
small locomotive

Examples

The old factory still operated a dinkey to move goods between warehouses.
A small dinkey chugged along the narrow tracks, hauling a load of coal.
Rail fans admired the restored dinkey at the railway museum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: locomotive - a powered railway vehicle for pulling trains
