schlockmeister

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃlɑkˌmaɪstər / (AmE), / ˈʃlɒkˌmaɪstə / (BrE)
(slang) a person who sells or produces shoddy or inferior goods, especially for profit.
dealer in inferior goods (slang)
slang

Examples

The old store was run by a well-known schlockmeister who cared little for quality.
Critics accused the movie producer of being a schlockmeister churning out quick, low-budget films.
Some shoppers avoid the market stalls operated by a schlockmeister eager to make a fast buck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: huckster - a person who sells goods in an aggressive or dishonest way

Etymology

From Yiddish 'shlak' (worthless, trash) + German 'Meister' (master).
