XLIV

1. NUM

/ ɛks ɛl aɪ vi / (AmE), / ɛks ɛl aɪ vi / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for forty-four
Roman numeral for 44

Examples

Chapter XLIV of the book provides a turning point in the narrative.
The monument was engraved with the year XLIV to mark the anniversary.
She found a coin marked XLIV among the ancient Roman artifacts.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: XLIII - the Roman numeral for forty-three
  • Degree Higher: XLV - the Roman numeral for forty-five

Etymology

From Latin numerals X (10), L (50), and IV (4), combining to form 44.
