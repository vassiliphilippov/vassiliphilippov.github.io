Jane Seymour

1. PROPN

/ dʒeɪn ˈsiːmɔr / (AmE), / dʒeɪn ˈsiːmɔː / (BrE)
(1509–1537) Queen of England, the third wife of Henry VIII and mother of Edward VI.
Queen of England, third wife of Henry VIII

Examples

Jane Seymour was the only one of Henry VIII's wives to give him a male heir.
Many historians credit Jane Seymour for restoring good relations between Henry VIII and his daughter Mary.
Jane Seymour died shortly after giving birth to the future King Edward VI.
