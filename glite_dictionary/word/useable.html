useable

1. ADJ

/ ˈjuːzəbəl / (AmE), / ˈjuːzəbəl / (BrE)
fit or ready to be used; in such a condition as to be usable or functional
fit or ready for use

Examples

After being repaired, the device was once again useable for everyday tasks.
Please save all useable parts from the broken machine for future repairs.
Most of the donated clothing was useable, but some items were too worn to keep.

Synset Examples

the toaster was still functional even after being dropped
the lawnmower is a bit rusty but still usable
an operational aircraft

Related Terms

  • Synonym: usable - fit or ready for use

Etymology

From 'use' + '-able', modeled after the standard spelling 'usable'.
