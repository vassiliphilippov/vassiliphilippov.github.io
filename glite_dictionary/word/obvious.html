obvious

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːbviəs / (AmE), / ˈɒbviəs / (BrE)
Easily perceived or understood; clear and apparent.
easily perceived or understood

Examples

It was obvious that he had forgotten her birthday.
The solution to the puzzle is not immediately obvious.
She pointed out the most obvious errors in my essay.

Synset Examples

obvious errors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clear - easy to perceive, understand, or interpret
  • Synonym: evident - plain or obvious; clearly seen or understood
  • Antonym: subtle - not obvious, difficult to perceive or understand
  • Antonym: ambiguous - open to more than one interpretation; not having one obvious meaning

Etymology

From Latin 'obvius' (in the way, obvious), from 'ob-' (against) + 'via' (way).
