unexplainable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪkˈspleɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈspleɪnəbl / (BrE)
impossible to account for or explain; incapable of being understood or justified.
impossible to explain

Examples

The lights flickered for an unexplainable reason during the storm.
She felt an unexplainable sense of dread as she entered the room.
There was an unexplainable noise coming from the attic last night.

Synset Examples

perceptible only as unaccountable influences that hinder progress
an unexplainable fear

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inexplicable - impossible to explain or understand

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'explain' + '-able', meaning 'not able to be explained'.
