adipose

1. ADJ

/ ˈædəˌpoʊs / (AmE), / ˈadɪpəʊs / (BrE)
(especially of tissue) consisting of or relating to animal fat.
composed of animal fat
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that adipose cells store energy in the form of fat.
Obesity is often caused by an excessive accumulation of adipose tissue.
During surgery, the adipose layer beneath the skin can be clearly observed.

Synset Examples

adipose tissue constitutes the fat of meat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fatty - containing or resembling fat

Etymology

From Latin adiposus (fatty), from adeps (fat).
