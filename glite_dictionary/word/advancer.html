advancer

1. NOUN

/ ədˈvænsɚ / (AmE), / ədˈvɑːnsə / (BrE)
a person or thing that moves forward or makes progress, especially to a higher stage or position
person or thing that advances

Examples

The company recognized her as a key advancer in the development of new technologies.
In the tournament, only one advancer from each group moves on to the finals.
As an early advancer in his field, he inspired many others to follow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: progressor - someone who progresses or moves forward
  • Antonym: retreater - someone who retreats or moves back

Etymology

From the verb 'advance' + '-er' suffix forming agent nouns.
