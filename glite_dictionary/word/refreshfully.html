refreshfully

1. ADV

/ rɪˈfrɛʃfəli / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɛʃfəli / (BrE)
in a way that relieves fatigue and restores energy; in a refreshing manner.
in a manner that restores energy

Examples

The mountain breeze blew refreshfully across the valley after a long, hot day.
She woke refreshfully after an uninterrupted night’s sleep in the countryside.
The spa’s mineral waters flow refreshfully, promising to revive tired travelers.

Synset Examples

the air was refreshingly cool

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refreshingly - in a way that is pleasingly fresh or invigorating

Etymology

From 'refreshful' (refresh + -ful) + -ly.
