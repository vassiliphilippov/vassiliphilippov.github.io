reanimated

1. ADJ

/ ˌriˈænɪˌmeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌriːˈænɪmeɪtɪd / (BrE)
given new life, energy, or vigor; revived or restored to activity
brought back to life or energy

Examples

The story follows a reanimated corpse struggling to find its place in society.
Hope was reanimated among the team after their recent victory.
The old tradition was reanimated by the younger generation.

Synset Examples

stirred by revived hopes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revived - restored to life or consciousness
  • Synonym: resuscitated - brought back to life or consciousness

Etymology

Formed from 'reanimate' (from Latin 'reanimare,' to restore to life) plus -ed.
