vesicular

1. ADJ

/ vɪˈsɪkjələr / (AmE), / vɪˈsɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or involving vesicles, especially in biological or medical contexts.
relating to small sacs or vesicles
medical

Examples

The doctor noted vesicular lesions on the patient's skin during the examination.
Vesicular breathing sounds are normal when listening to healthy lungs.
Some viral infections are characterized by vesicular eruptions on the oral mucosa.

Synset Examples

normal vesicular breathing

Etymology

From Latin 'vesicula' (small bladder, blister), diminutive of 'vesica' (bladder).
