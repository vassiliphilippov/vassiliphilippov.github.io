Robert Frost

1. PROPN

/ ˈroʊbɚt frɑst / (AmE), / ˈrəʊbət frɒst / (BrE)
(1874–1963) An American poet known for his lyrical poems about rural life in New England.
American poet (1874–1963)

Examples

Robert Frost is often celebrated for his vivid depictions of rural New England life.
Many students study the poetry of Robert Frost in their English classes.
The works of Robert Frost have influenced generations of American poets.
