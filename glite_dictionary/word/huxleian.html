Huxleian

1. ADJ

/ ˈhʌksliən / (AmE), / ˈhʌksliːən / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Thomas Huxley, the English biologist and advocate of evolutionary theory.
of or relating to Thomas Huxley

Examples

The museum features a Huxleian exhibit focusing on 19th-century evolutionary theory.
Her Huxleian arguments defended scientific rationalism against dogma.
A Huxleian perspective shaped the academic debate on natural selection.

Etymology

Formed from the surname Huxley with the suffix -ian, patterned after other eponymous adjectives.
