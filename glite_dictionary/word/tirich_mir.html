Tirich Mir

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɪrɪtʃ ˈmɪr / (AmE), / ˈtɪrɪtʃ ˈmɪə / (BrE)
the highest mountain in the Hindu Kush range, located in Pakistan, with an elevation of 7,708 meters (25,230 feet)
mountain in the Hindu Kush, Pakistan

Examples

Mountaineers from around the world travel to Tirich Mir for challenging climbs in the Hindu Kush.
The snow-capped peak of Tirich Mir dominates the landscape in northern Pakistan.
Local legends tell mysterious tales about the spirits said to inhabit Tirich Mir.
