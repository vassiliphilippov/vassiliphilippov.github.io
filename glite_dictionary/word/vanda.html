Vanda

1. NOUN

/ ˈvændə / (AmE), / ˈvandə / (BrE)
a genus of showy epiphytic orchids found in the Himalayas to Malaysia, known for their brightly colored flowers
genus of showy epiphytic orchids

Examples

The Vanda orchid is prized for its vibrant and long-lasting blooms.
Botanists discovered a rare species of Vanda in the mountains of northern Thailand.
Many gardeners cultivate Vanda hybrids for their stunning appearance.

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'bandha' (a name for certain orchids).
