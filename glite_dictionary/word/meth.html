meth

1. NOUN

/ mɛθ / (AmE), / mɛθ / (BrE)
a powerful, addictive stimulant drug, short for methamphetamine, often used illegally as a recreational drug or stimulant
methamphetamine; powerful stimulant drug
informal slang

Examples

The police seized a large amount of meth from the suspect's apartment.
He struggled for years with addiction to meth before seeking help.
Reports show that meth abuse has increased in several rural areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: methamphetamine - the full chemical name for meth; synthetic stimulant drug

Etymology

Shortened form of methamphetamine, based on its chemical name.
