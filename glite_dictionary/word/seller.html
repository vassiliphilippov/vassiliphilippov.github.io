seller

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɛlə / (BrE)
a person or business that sells goods or services in exchange for money
person or business selling goods or services

Examples

The house was sold directly by the seller without a real estate agent.
Online sellers often compete on price and shipping speed.
Each seller at the market offers something different to attract customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vendor - a person or company offering something for sale
  • Antonym: buyer - a person who purchases goods or services

Etymology

From Middle English 'sellen' (to sell) + -er (agent noun suffix).
