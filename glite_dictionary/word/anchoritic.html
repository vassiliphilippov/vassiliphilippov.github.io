anchoritic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæŋkəˈrɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌaŋkəˈrɪtɪk / (BrE)
living in solitude for religious reasons; relating to or characteristic of an anchorite or a hermit-like ascetic life.
relating to religious solitude

Examples

The monk led an anchoritic existence in a remote mountain cave.
Her anchoritic lifestyle was marked by silence and prayer.
He wrote poetry inspired by the anchoritic traditions of early Christianity.

Synset Examples

the eremitic element in the life of a religious colony
his hermitic existence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hermitic - relating to or characteristic of a hermit; solitary
  • Synonym: eremitic - characteristic of or resembling a hermit or recluse

Etymology

From Medieval Latin anchoriticus, from anchorita ('anchorite', a hermit).
