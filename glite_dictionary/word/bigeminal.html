bigeminal

1. ADJ

/ baɪˈdʒɛmɪnəl / (AmE), / bɪˈdʒɛmɪnəl / (BrE)
(chiefly medical) occurring in pairs, especially referring to abnormal heartbeats or other physiological events.
occurring in pairs, esp. in medicine
medical

Examples

The patient exhibited a bigeminal pulse during the arrhythmia episode.
Electrocardiograms can reveal bigeminal rhythms in some cardiac disorders.
A bigeminal pattern may indicate underlying heart conditions that require further investigation.

Synset Examples

a bigeminal pulse

Etymology

From Latin 'bigeminus', from 'bis' (twice) + 'geminus' (twin).
