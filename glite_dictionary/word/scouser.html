Scouser

1. NOUN

/ ˈskaʊsər / (AmE), / ˈskaʊsə / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Liverpool, England
native or resident of Liverpool
british

Examples

As a proud Scouser, she never misses a Liverpool football match.
He spoke with a thick Scouser accent that was immediately recognizable.
Many Scousers are known for their distinct sense of humor and friendliness.

Etymology

From 'scouse', a type of stew associated with Liverpool, plus the suffix '-er'.
