Guayaquil

1. PROPN

/ ˌgwaɪəˈkil / (AmE), / ˌgwaɪəˈkiːl / (BrE)
the largest city in Ecuador, located on the country's Pacific coast and serving as its main port
largest city in Ecuador

Examples

She was born in Guayaquil and later moved to the capital, Quito.
Many tourists visit Guayaquil to explore its vibrant riverside and historic districts.
After the earthquake, international aid poured into Guayaquil to help with recovery efforts.
