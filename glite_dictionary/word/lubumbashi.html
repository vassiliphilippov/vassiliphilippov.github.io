Lubumbashi

1. PROPN

/ luˌbumˈbɑːʃi / (AmE), / luˌbʊmˈbæʃi / (BrE)
A city in southeastern Democratic Republic of the Congo, near the border with Zambia, known as a major copper mining center; formerly called Elisabethville until 1966.
city in southeastern DRC, copper mining center

Examples

The mining industry is a major source of employment in Lubumbashi.
After flying to Lubumbashi, we drove south toward the Zambian border.
Many international conferences have been hosted in Lubumbashi over the last decade.
