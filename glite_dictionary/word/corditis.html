corditis

1. NOUN

/ kɔrˈdaɪtɪs / (AmE), / kɔːˈdaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the spermatic cord, often accompanied by pain in the testis and typically caused by infection or other medical conditions.
inflammation of the spermatic cord
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with corditis after experiencing severe pain in his groin area.
Untreated infections in the urinary tract can sometimes lead to corditis.
Antibiotics are commonly prescribed to help manage the inflammation seen in corditis.

Etymology

From Latin 'cordis' (cord) + '-itis' (inflammation).
