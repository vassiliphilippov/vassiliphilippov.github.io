prefectural

1. ADJ

/ priːˈfɛktʃərəl / (AmE), / prɪˈfɛktjʊərəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a prefecture, especially as an administrative division.
relating to a prefecture

Examples

The new prefectural building opened to serve local citizens last year.
She works at a prefectural hospital in Kyoto.
Tourists often visit the prefectural museum to learn about regional culture.

Synset Examples

a prefectural museum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: municipal - relating to a city or town and its governing body

Etymology

From 'prefecture', ultimately from Latin 'praefectūra'.
