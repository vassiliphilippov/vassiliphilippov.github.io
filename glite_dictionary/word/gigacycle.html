gigacycle

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪɡəˌsaɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɪɡəˌsʌɪkəl / (BrE)
A unit of frequency equal to one billion (1,000,000,000) cycles per second.
a billion cycles per second (frequency)

Examples

The processor's speed is measured in gigacycles per second.
Scientists recorded electromagnetic waves oscillating at nearly three gigacycles per second.
One gigacycle is equivalent to one gigahertz in frequency measurement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gigahertz - a unit of frequency equal to one billion hertz

Etymology

From 'giga-' (meaning billion) and 'cycle', after pattern of 'megacycle'.
