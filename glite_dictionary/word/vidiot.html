vidiot

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪdiˌɑt / (AmE), / ˈvɪdiət / (BrE)
a person who behaves foolishly or obsessively while playing video games
person behaving foolishly with video games
slang

Examples

He was such a vidiot last night, playing until 3 a.m. without a break.
Her parents worry she's becoming a vidiot because of all the time she spends gaming.
Stop acting like a vidiot and come outside for some fresh air.

Synset Examples

Don't be a vidiot; remember to take breaks while gaming!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: addict - a person who is addicted to something, especially a harmful activity

Etymology

Blend of 'video' and 'idiot'.
