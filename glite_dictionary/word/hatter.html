hatter

1. NOUN

/ ˈhætər / (AmE), / ˈhætə / (BrE)
a person who makes and sells hats
person who makes and sells hats

Examples

The hatter displayed his new spring collection in the shop window.
In the 19th century, the village had a skilled hatter known for his fine craftsmanship.
She visited the local hatter to buy a stylish hat for the wedding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milliner - a person, usually a woman, who makes or sells women's hats

Etymology

From 'hat' + '-er', indicating a person who makes or deals in hats.
