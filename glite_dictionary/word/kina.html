kina

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiːnə / (AmE), / ˈkiːnə / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Papua New Guinea, equal to 100 toea
currency of Papua New Guinea

Examples

She exchanged her dollars for kina at the airport in Port Moresby.
The price was listed as twenty kina for a bag of coffee beans.
During my trip, I learned that one kina is divided into 100 toea.

Etymology

From Tok Pisin and Hiri Motu kina, originally referring to a kind of shell used as currency.
