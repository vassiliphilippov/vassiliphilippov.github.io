extolment

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈstoʊlmənt / (AmE), / ɪkˈstəʊlmənt / (BrE)
Formal or literary term for the act of praising or commending someone or something highly.
formal/literary praise or commendation

Examples

The mayor received much extolment for her dedication to public service.
The writer's new novel garnered critical extolment from literary reviewers.
He humbly accepted the extolment bestowed upon him by the board.

Synset Examples

he always appreciated praise for his work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: praise - expression of approval or admiration
  • Synonym: commendation - formal or official praise
  • Antonym: criticism - the expression of disapproval or finding fault

Etymology

From Latin 'extollere' (to raise, praise).
