gutless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡʌt.ləs / (AmE), / ˈɡʌt.ləs / (BrE)
Lacking courage or determination; cowardly or lacking vitality.
lacking courage or vitality

Examples

He was too gutless to stand up to the bullies at school.
Many saw the politician's refusal to act as a gutless move.
The film's hero refused to be gutless in the face of danger.

Synset Examples

he was a yellow gutless worm
a spineless craven fellow

Related Terms

  • Antonym: courageous - having or showing courage
  • Synonym: cowardly - lacking courage; easily frightened

Etymology

From 'gut' (referring to courage or resolve, from the sense of having 'guts') + '-less' (without).
