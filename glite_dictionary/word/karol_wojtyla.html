Karol Wojtyla

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɑːroʊl vɔɪˈtɪl.jə / (AmE), / ˈka.rɒl vɔɪˈtiːl.jə / (BrE)
The birth name of Pope John Paul II (1920–2005), the first Pope born in Poland and the first non-Italian Pope in more than 450 years.
birth name of Pope John Paul II

Examples

Before becoming Pope, Karol Wojtyla was Archbishop of Kraków.
Many Poles take pride in the fact that Karol Wojtyla became Pope John Paul II.
A museum in Wadowice is dedicated to the early life of Karol Wojtyla.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: John Paul II - Pope John Paul II (1920–2005), head of the Catholic Church from 1978 to 2005

Etymology

Karol Józef Wojtyła is a Polish personal name.
