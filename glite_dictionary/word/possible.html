possible

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɑːsəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈpɒsəbl̩ / (BrE)
that can happen or exist; able to be done or achieved
able to happen or exist

Examples

It is possible that the train will arrive on time despite the delay.
Please let me know as soon as possible.
New technology makes it possible to communicate instantly across the globe.

Synset Examples

a breakthrough may be possible next year
anything is possible
warned of possible consequences

Related Terms

  • Antonym: impossible - not able to happen or exist
  • Degree Higher: probable - likely to happen or be true
  • Degree Lower: unlikely - not likely to happen

Etymology

From Latin possibilis (that can be done), from posse (to be able).

able to exist or happen

Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: impossible - not able to exist, happen, or be done
