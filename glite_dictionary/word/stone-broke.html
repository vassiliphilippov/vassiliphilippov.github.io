stone-broke

1. ADJ

/ stoʊn ˈbroʊk / (AmE), / stəʊn ˈbrəʊk / (BrE)
Completely without money; having no funds at all.
completely without money
informal slang

Examples

After losing his job, he was stone-broke and had to move back home.
I can't go on holiday this year because I'm stone-broke.
By the end of the month, many students find themselves stone-broke.

Synset Examples

‘skint’ is a British slang term

Related Terms

  • Synonym: penniless - having no money at all
  • Synonym: skint - British slang for having no money
  • Synonym: broke - without any money

Etymology

Possibly from the image of being so destitute that only stones are left; 'broke' as slang for penniless, intensified by 'stone' (emphatic).
