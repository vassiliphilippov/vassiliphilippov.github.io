sociably

1. ADV

/ ˈsoʊʃəbli / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is friendly and enjoys the company of others; in a sociable manner.
in a sociable manner

Examples

She greeted everyone sociably at the dinner party, making guests feel welcome.
The new neighbors sociably invited everyone over for coffee on their first weekend.
He always behaves sociably at work events, joining group conversations and activities.

Synset Examples

sociably, the new neighbors invited everyone on the block for coffee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sociable - friendly and willing to talk and engage with others
  • Antonym: unsociably - in an unsociable manner; not wanting to interact with others

2. ADV

/ ˈsoʊ.ʃəb.li / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ʃəb.li / (BrE)
in a friendly and pleasant manner; in a way that shows a liking for the company of others
in a friendly, outgoing manner

Examples

She greeted everyone sociably, making newcomers feel at ease right away.
The group mingled sociably at the networking event, exchanging ideas and stories.
He always behaves sociably at parties, chatting with guests he barely knows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sociable - friendly and enjoys being with other people
  • Antonym: unsociably - in an unfriendly or withdrawn manner

Etymology

From 'sociable' + '-ly', ultimately from Latin 'sociabilis' (sociable).
