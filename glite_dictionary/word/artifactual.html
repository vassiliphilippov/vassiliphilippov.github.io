artifactual

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑːr.tɪˈfæk.tʃu.əl / (AmE), / ˌɑː.tɪˈfæk.tʃu.əl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of artifacts, especially objects made or shaped by human hands.
relating to artifacts

Examples

The museum's collection included several artifactual items uncovered during the excavation.
Some artifactual evidence provides valuable insight into ancient societies.
He specializes in the study of artifactual remains from prehistoric settlements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: artifact - an object made by a human being, typically of historical or cultural interest

Etymology

From artifact + -al, denoting 'of or relating to.'
