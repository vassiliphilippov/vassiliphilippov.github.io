etymologizing

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛtɪˈmɑlədʒaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɛtɪˈmɒlədʒaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
an explanation of the historical origins of a word or phrase
explanation of a word's origin
medical

Examples

The professor's etymologizing revealed surprising connections between English and ancient Greek.
His passionate etymologizing kept the seminar engaged for hours.
Many dictionaries include brief etymologizing after each entry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: etymology - the study of the origin of words and their meanings

Etymology

Formed from 'etymologize' (to explain the origin of a word) + '-ing'.
