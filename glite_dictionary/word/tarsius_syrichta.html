Tarsius syrichta

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑr.si.əs səˈrɪk.tə / (AmE), / ˈtɑː.sɪ.əs sɪˈrɪk.tə / (BrE)
A species of small, nocturnal primate native to the Philippines, commonly known as the Philippine tarsier.
species of Philippine tarsier

Examples

The Tarsius syrichta is renowned for its oversized eyes relative to its body size.
Many tourists visit Bohol to see the Tarsius syrichta in its natural habitat.
Conservation efforts have increased to protect the endangered Tarsius syrichta from habitat loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Philippine tarsier - Small nocturnal primate native to the Philippines, known for its large eyes

Etymology

From genus name Tarsius (referring to its extended tarsal bones) and specific epithet syrichta.
