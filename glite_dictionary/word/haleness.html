haleness

1. NOUN

/ ˈheɪlnəs / (AmE), / ˈheɪlnɪs / (BrE)
the condition of being healthy and free from illness; robust good health
state of good health

Examples

After months of careful diet and exercise, she finally regained her haleness.
The doctor's main concern was the haleness of the children after the outbreak.
He attributed his advanced age to the haleness he enjoyed throughout his life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: health - the state of being free from illness or injury
  • Antonym: illness - a condition of poor health or disease

Etymology

From 'hale' (healthy) + '-ness' (state or quality).
