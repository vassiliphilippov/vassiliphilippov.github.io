hominal

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑːmɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈhɒmɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to humankind considered as a species; pertaining to humans collectively.
of or relating to humankind as a species

Examples

The archaeologist studied the hominal remains found at the ancient burial site.
Discussions of hominal evolution often explore the development of language and culture.
Her research focused on the hominal aspects that distinguish humans from other primates.

Synset Examples

the hominal kingdom

Etymology

From Latin *homin-, homo* (man, human being) + -al.
