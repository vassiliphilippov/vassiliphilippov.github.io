unpreventable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.prɪˈvɛnt.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prɪˈvɛnt.ə.bəl / (BrE)
Not able to be prevented from happening; impossible to stop or avert.
not able to be prevented

Examples

The doctor explained that some genetic diseases are unpreventable despite advances in medicine.
Heavy flooding in the region was an unpreventable disaster given the unexpected rainfall.
Their argument turned into unpreventable chaos as emotions ran high.

Synset Examples

unpreventable hysteria

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: inevitable - certain to happen; unavoidable
  • Antonym: avoidable - able to be prevented or avoided
  • Antonym: preventable - able to be prevented or avoided

Etymology

From un- (not) + preventable.
