Braga

1. PROPN

/ ˈbrɑːɡə / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːɡə / (BrE)
An ancient city in northern Portugal, known for its historical and cultural significance.
ancient city in northern Portugal

Examples

Many tourists visit Braga to admire its Roman and Baroque architecture.
The city of Braga is famous for its religious festivals and ancient monuments.
She studied abroad in Braga and fell in love with Portuguese culture.

Etymology

From Latin 'Bracara Augusta', its Roman-era name.
