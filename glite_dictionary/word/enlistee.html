enlistee

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈlɪsti / (AmE), / ɪnˈlɪsti / (BrE)
a person who has recently joined an organization, especially one who has enlisted for service in the armed forces
a recent recruit, especially in the armed forces

Examples

The army offered bonuses to every new enlistee during the recruitment drive.
As an enlistee, she attended basic training before being assigned to her unit.
The support program helps each enlistee adjust to life in the military.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recruit - a person who has recently joined an organization or the armed forces

Etymology

From the verb 'enlist' + '-ee', denoting a person who is the object of an action.
