North Dakotan

1. NOUN

/ nɔrθ dəˈkoʊtən / (AmE), / nɔːθ dəˈkəʊtən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the state of North Dakota, USA
person from North Dakota

Examples

Her grandmother is a proud North Dakotan who cherishes her Midwestern roots.
A group of North Dakotans gathered to celebrate the state's anniversary.
He became a North Dakotan after moving there for work.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: South Dakotan - a native or inhabitant of South Dakota
