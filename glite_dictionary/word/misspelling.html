misspelling

1. NOUN

/ mɪsˈspɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / mɪsˈspɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
an incorrect spelling of a word
incorrect spelling

Examples

The essay contained several misspellings that needed to be corrected.
A common misspelling of 'definitely' is 'definately'.
He was embarrassed when the teacher pointed out the misspelling on the whiteboard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: typo - a typographical error, especially in printed material
  • Antonym: correction - the action of correcting a mistake

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'mis-' (wrong, badly) + 'spelling'.
