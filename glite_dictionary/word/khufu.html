Khufu

1. PROPN

/ ˈkuːfuː / (AmE), / ˈkuːfuː / (BrE)
the Egyptian Pharaoh of the 27th century BC who commissioned the Great Pyramid at Giza
Egyptian Pharaoh who built the Great Pyramid

Examples

The Great Pyramid was built during the reign of Khufu in ancient Egypt.
Many historians study the legacy of Khufu and his monumental architecture.
Artifacts from Khufu's era are displayed in museums around the world.

Etymology

From ancient Egyptian, also rendered as Cheops in Greek tradition.
