stupe

1. NOUN

/ stuːp / (AmE), / stjuːp / (BrE)
an unintelligent or slow-witted person (informal, slang)
a stupid or dull person
informal slang

Examples

Don't be such a stupe, read the instructions first.
He called me a stupe just because I made a silly mistake.
Only a stupe would forget their own birthday.

Synset Examples

The economy, stupid!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - a person of extraordinary intelligence

Etymology

From Latin stupere (to be amazed, stunned), via French stupe.
