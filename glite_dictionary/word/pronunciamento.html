pronunciamento

1. NOUN

/ proʊˌnʌnsiəˈmentoʊ / (AmE), / prəˌnʌnsiəˈmɛntəʊ / (BrE)
an official public declaration, especially one issued by a political party or government, often announcing intentions or policies
public political declaration

Examples

The military leaders issued a pronunciamento outlining their demands to the government.
After the pronunciamento, widespread unrest broke out across the region.
The rebel group's pronunciamento marked the beginning of a political crisis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proclamation - an official public announcement

Etymology

From Spanish 'pronunciamiento' (a proclamation), from 'pronunciar' (to pronounce).
