footbath

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌbæθ / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌbɑːθ / (BrE)
a small bath or container for washing, warming, or disinfecting the feet
small bath for the feet

Examples

After walking all day, she relaxed with her feet in a footbath.
The spa offered a herbal footbath before the massage treatment began.
Doctors sometimes recommend a disinfectant footbath to treat minor foot infections.
