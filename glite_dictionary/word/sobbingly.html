sobbingly

1. ADV

/ ˈsɑːbɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsɒbɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner marked by sobbing; with sobs
with sobs; in a sobbing way

Examples

She confessed sobbingly that the story was true.
He spoke sobbingly as he recounted his loss.
The child clung to her mother, repeating her words sobbingly.

Synset Examples

sobbingly, the teenager admitted killing the baby

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tearfully - in a way marked by tears; crying
