Buffalo Bill

1. PROPN

/ ˈbʌfəloʊ bɪl / (AmE), / ˈbʌfələʊ bɪl / (BrE)
the nickname of William F. Cody (1846–1917), an American showman famous for his Wild West shows
American showman, creator of the Wild West Show

Examples

Buffalo Bill traveled across America with his famous Wild West show.
Many people remember Buffalo Bill as a legendary figure of the American frontier.
The museum displays artifacts related to Buffalo Bill and his touring performances.

Etymology

Nickname derived from his skills as a buffalo hunter; real name William Frederick Cody.
