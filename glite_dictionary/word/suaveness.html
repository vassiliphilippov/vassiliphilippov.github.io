suaveness

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɑv.nəs / (AmE), / ˈswɑːv.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being smooth, gracious, and charming in manner.
smooth and charming manner

Examples

His remarkable suaveness put everyone at ease during the tense negotiation.
People admired her suaveness at social gatherings, as she always knew the right thing to say.
The politician's suaveness masked his true opinions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suavity - the quality of being suave; smoothness and sophistication
  • Antonym: bluntness - the quality of being direct and ungracious

Etymology

From Latin 'suavis' (sweet, agreeable); influenced by 'suave'.
