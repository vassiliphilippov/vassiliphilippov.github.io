sustentation

1. NOUN

/ ˌsʌstənˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsʌstənˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of sustaining life by providing food or other means of support
sustaining life; providing subsistence

Examples

The charity's efforts ensured the sustentation of hundreds of displaced families.
Without adequate sustentation, the community faced severe hardship during the drought.
Regular meals are a basic form of sustentation necessary for good health.

Synset Examples

they were in want of sustenance
fishing was their main sustainment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sustenance - food or nourishment needed to live

Etymology

From Latin sustentatio (support, maintenance), from sustentare (to uphold, sustain).
