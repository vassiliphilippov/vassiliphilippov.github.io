astutely

1. ADV

/ əˈstuːtli / (AmE), / əˈstjuːtli / (BrE)
in a clever and perceptive way; showing sharp judgment or insight
in a clever, shrewd manner

Examples

She handled the negotiations astutely, ensuring a favorable outcome for her team.
He astutely avoided the pitfalls that trapped his competitors.
By astutely analyzing the data, she discovered an overlooked pattern.

Synset Examples

he invested his fortune astutely
he was acutely insightful

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shrewdly - in a clever or astute manner
