logotype

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊɡəˌtaɪp / (AmE), / ˈləʊɡəˌtaɪp / (BrE)
a symbol or design adopted by a company or organization to identify its products, brand, or services
company or organization symbol

Examples

The company unveiled a new logotype as part of its rebranding campaign.
Many famous brands are recognized instantly by their distinctive logotype.
Our design team specializes in creating memorable logotypes for startups and small businesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: logo - a symbol or design used by an organization for identification

Etymology

From Greek logos (word) + -type (form, impression).
