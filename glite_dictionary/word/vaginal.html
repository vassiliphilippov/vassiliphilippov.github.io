vaginal

1. ADJ

/ ˈvædʒənl̩ / (AmE), / ˈvædʒɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the vagina.
relating to the vagina
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed a vaginal cream to treat the infection.
She chose a vaginal delivery for her baby's birth.
Routine exams may include a vaginal swab to check for infections.

Synset Examples

vaginal suppository

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uterine - relating to the uterus
  • Antonym: penile - relating to the penis

Etymology

From Latin 'vagina' (sheath, scabbard) with the suffix '-al' forming an adjective.
