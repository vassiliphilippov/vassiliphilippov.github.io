leadless

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛd.ləs / (AmE), / ˈlɛd.lɪs / (BrE)
not containing or treated with lead, especially referring to materials or products made without the use of lead
not containing lead

Examples

All solder used in the electronics must be leadless to meet safety regulations.
The company now manufactures leadless paints for children's toys.
Environmental groups advocate for the adoption of leadless gasoline worldwide.

Synset Examples

unleaded gasoline

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unleaded - not containing lead, especially as an additive
