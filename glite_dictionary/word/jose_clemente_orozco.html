Jose Clemente Orozco

1. PROPN

/ hoʊˌseɪ klɛˈmɛn.teɪ oʊˈrɔːs.koʊ / (AmE), / həʊˌseɪ klɛˈmɛntiː əˈrɒskoʊ / (BrE)
(1883–1949) A Mexican painter known for his monumental murals depicting social and political themes.
Mexican muralist (1883–1949)

Examples

Many art historians consider Jose Clemente Orozco one of the most influential Mexican muralists.
The murals by Jose Clemente Orozco can be seen in Guadalajara and Mexico City.
Students studied the bold style and political themes of Jose Clemente Orozco's works in their art class.
