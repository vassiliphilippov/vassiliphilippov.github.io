ogler

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊɡlər / (AmE), / ˈəʊɡlə / (BrE)
a person who looks at someone with obvious sexual or flirtatious interest, often in a way that is considered inappropriate or offensive
person who looks flirtatiously or lewdly

Examples

She felt uncomfortable as the ogler continued to stare at her across the room.
The subway is no place for a ogler trying to get attention.
He complained to security about a persistent ogler in the gym.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ogle - to look at someone in a way that shows sexual or flirtatious interest

Etymology

From 'ogle' (to look at amorously), which is of uncertain origin, possibly from German *äugeln* (to eye).
