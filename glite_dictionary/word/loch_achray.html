Loch Achray

1. PROPN

/ lɑk əˈkreɪ / (AmE), / lɒx əˈkreɪ / (BrE)
A small lake in central Scotland, located within the Trossachs area of the Scottish Highlands.
lake in central Scotland

Examples

We took a peaceful walk along the shores of Loch Achray last summer.
The picturesque landscape of Loch Achray attracts many visitors to the Trossachs National Park.
Photographers often come to Loch Achray for its stunning reflections of the surrounding mountains.

Etymology

From Scottish Gaelic, 'loch' meaning 'lake' and 'Achray' from a local placename.
