palsied

1. ADJ

/ ˈpælzid / (AmE), / ˈpɔːlzid / (BrE)
affected with palsy or marked by uncontrollable tremor or paralysis, especially of the muscles.
affected by palsy or tremor
medical

Examples

The old man's palsied hands could barely hold the cup.
Her palsied limbs made walking extremely difficult.
The writer described the character's palsied movements in great detail.

Synset Examples

palsied hands

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paralyzed - affected by paralysis
  • Synonym: tremulous - shaking or quivering slightly

Etymology

From 'palsy' plus the suffix '-ed', indicating a state or condition.
