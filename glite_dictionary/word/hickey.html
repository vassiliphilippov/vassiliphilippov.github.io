hickey

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪki / (AmE), / ˈhɪki / (BrE)
A temporary reddish mark or bruise on the skin caused by kissing or sucking, usually as a display of affection.
mark on skin from kissing or sucking
informal

Examples

She wore a scarf to hide the hickey on her neck.
He was embarrassed when his friends noticed the hickey left by his girlfriend.
The hickey faded after a few days without leaving a scar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: love bite - A mark on the skin caused by kissing or sucking, especially in British English

2. NOUN

/ ˈhɪki / (AmE), / ˈhɪki / (BrE)
A small, inflamed bump on the skin, such as a pustule or papule, commonly associated with acne.
small skin elevation or acne bump
medical

Examples

The dermatologist examined the hickey on her forehead and recommended a topical cream.
Acne often appears as a hickey or pimple, especially during adolescence.
He was embarrassed by the red hickeys that appeared before his big presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pustule - a small, inflamed, pus-filled swelling on the skin
  • Synonym: papule - a small, raised, solid pimple or swelling on the skin
