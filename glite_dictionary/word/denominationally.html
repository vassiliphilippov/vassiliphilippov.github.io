denominationally

1. ADV

/ dɪˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənəli / (AmE), / dɪˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or concerns religious denominations or branches
in relation to religious denominations

Examples

The participants came from denominationally varied backgrounds.
Some schools are denominationally affiliated, while others are secular.
The event was designed to be denominationally inclusive, welcoming all faiths.

Synset Examples

denominationally diverse audiences

Related Terms

  • Synonym: denominational - relating to a religious denomination

Etymology

From 'denominational' + '-ly'. 'Denominational' from 'denomination', influenced by Latin 'nominatio' (naming).
