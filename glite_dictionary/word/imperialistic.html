imperialistic

1. ADJ

/ ɪmˌpɪriəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ɪmˌpɪəriəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or supporting imperialism or the extension of a country's power through colonization, use of military force, or other means.
relating to imperialism

Examples

The country's imperialistic ambitions led to numerous conflicts with its neighbors.
Many historians criticize the imperialistic policies of the early twentieth century.
Her speech denounced imperialistic intervention in foreign affairs.

Synset Examples

imperialistic wars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imperialist - a supporter or advocate of imperialism
  • Antonym: anti-imperialist - opposed to imperialism

Etymology

From imperial, after imperialism + -istic.
