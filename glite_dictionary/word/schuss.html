to schuss

1. VERB

/ ʃʊs / (AmE), / ʃʊs / (BrE)
to ski straight down a slope at high speed, typically without turning.
ski downhill quickly

Examples

The skier decided to schuss the final section of the slope for maximum speed.
She expertly schussed down the mountain, leaving the others far behind.
They watched as he schussed past the finish line without slowing down.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: slalom - to ski downhill in a zigzag course between obstacles

Etymology

From German 'Schuss' (shot), referring to the speed and straightness of the descent.
