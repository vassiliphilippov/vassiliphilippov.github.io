disinterestedly

1. ADV

/ dɪsˈɪntrəstɪdli / (AmE), / dɪsˈɪntrɪstɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows a lack of bias or selfish motive; impartially
in an impartial or unbiased way

Examples

The judge listened to both sides disinterestedly before making a decision.
She reviewed the applications disinterestedly, ensuring fairness for all candidates.
He tried to advise them disinterestedly, without letting personal feelings interfere.

Synset Examples

he decided the case disinterestedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impartially - in a fair and unbiased manner
  • Synonym: objectively - in a manner not influenced by personal feelings or opinions
  • Antonym: interestedly - in an engaged or biased way

Etymology

Formed from 'disinterested' (from dis- + interested) + -ly.
