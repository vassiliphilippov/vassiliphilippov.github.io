hankering

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæŋkɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhæŋkərɪŋ / (BrE)
A strong desire or longing for something.
strong desire or craving

Examples

She had a sudden hankering for chocolate cake late at night.
His hankering to travel grew stronger with each passing year.
Despite having dinner, he still felt a hankering for something sweet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: longing - a strong feeling of wanting something
  • Synonym: craving - a powerful desire for something
  • Antonym: aversion - a strong dislike or unwillingness

Etymology

From Dutch *hankeren* (to hanker, long for).
