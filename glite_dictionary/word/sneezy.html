sneezy

1. ADJ

/ ˈsniːzi / (AmE), / ˈsniːzi / (BrE)
Often feeling the need to sneeze; inclined to sneeze frequently.
prone to sneezing

Examples

Allergy season makes me feel especially sneezy in the mornings.
The dust in the old library left everyone a bit sneezy.
After catching a cold, he became very sneezy and kept reaching for tissues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: achoo - the sound of a sneeze or the act of sneezing
