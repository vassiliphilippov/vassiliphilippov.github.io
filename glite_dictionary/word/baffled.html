baffled

1. ADJ

/ ˈbæfəld / (AmE), / ˈbæfəld / (BrE)
confused or perplexed by something difficult to understand or explain
perplexed; confused by something puzzling

Examples

She looked baffled when the instructions made no sense to her at all.
The detective was baffled by the mysterious disappearance of the documents.
Despite several explanations, the students remained baffled by the complex theory.

Synset Examples

obviously bemused by his questions
bewildered and confused
a cloudy and confounded philosopher
just a mixed-up kid
she felt lost on the first day of school

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perplexed - completely puzzled or bewildered
  • Synonym: bewildered - very confused and unable to think clearly
  • Synonym: confused - unable to think clearly or to understand something
  • Antonym: clear - easy to understand, not confused

Etymology

Past participle of "baffle," which may be from French "bafouer" (to mock, scoff at), origin uncertain.
