clawhammer

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɔːˌhæmər / (AmE), / ˈklɔːˌhamə / (BrE)
a hammer with a curved, cleft end used for pulling out nails
a hammer for pulling nails

Examples

He pulled the old boards apart with a clawhammer.
The carpenter reached for his clawhammer to remove the stubborn nail.
Every basic toolbox includes a clawhammer for construction work.
