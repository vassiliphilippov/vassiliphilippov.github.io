7-membered

1. ADJ

/ sɛvən ˈmɛmbɚd / (AmE), / sɛvən ˈmɛmbəd / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) having a ring structure consisting of seven atoms or members.
having a ring of seven atoms (chemistry)

Examples

Cycloheptane is a classic example of a 7-membered ring compound.
The 7-membered ring structure is less common than six-membered rings in organic chemistry.
Researchers synthesized a new molecule with a 7-membered lactone core.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: six-membered - having a ring with six members (chemistry)
  • Degree Higher: eight-membered - having a ring with eight members (chemistry)
