maltreated

1. ADJ

/ mælˈtritɪd / (AmE), / mælˈtriːtɪd / (BrE)
subjected to cruel or harsh treatment; abused or ill-treated
treated cruelly or harshly

Examples

The shelter provided care for maltreated animals rescued from unsafe environments.
She spoke about her experiences as a maltreated child in hope of helping others.
New laws aim to protect maltreated workers from exploitation and abuse.

Synset Examples

an abused wife

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abused - subjected to cruel or violent treatment
  • Antonym: well-treated - treated kindly and humanely

Etymology

From 'maltreat', itself from mal- (badly) + treat, influenced by French 'maltraiter' (to treat badly).
