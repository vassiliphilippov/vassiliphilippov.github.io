folium

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊ.li.əm / (AmE), / ˈfəʊ.li.əm / (BrE)
a thin layer or stratum of rock, especially in metamorphic rocks
thin layer of rock

Examples

Geologists examined the folium to understand the region's metamorphic history.
Thin folia within the schist were clearly visible under the microscope.
Each folium contributed to the overall structure of the layered rock formation.

Etymology

From Latin *folium* (leaf), reflecting the thin, leaf-like form of the layer.
