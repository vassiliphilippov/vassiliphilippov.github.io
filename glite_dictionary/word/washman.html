washman

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑʃmən / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃmən / (BrE)
(historical) a woman who is employed to wash clothes, especially taking in washing as paid work.
a woman employed to wash clothes

Examples

The washman collected clothes from the neighbors every Monday morning.
She worked as a washman to support her family during hard times.
In the past, a washman was a familiar figure in many English towns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laundress - a woman whose job is washing clothes
  • Synonym: washerwoman - a woman employed to wash clothes
