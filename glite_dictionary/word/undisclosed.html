undisclosed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.dɪsˈkloʊzd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪsˈkləʊzd / (BrE)
not revealed or made known; kept secret
not made known; secret

Examples

The company was acquired for an undisclosed sum.
She left her job for undisclosed reasons.
The donor wished to remain undisclosed to protect their privacy.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: revealed - made known to others
  • Synonym: secret - kept hidden or unknown from others

Etymology

Formed from the prefix un- (not) + disclosed (from Latin disclosus, past participle of discindere, 'to divide apart, reveal').
