Tierra del Fuego

1. PROPN

/ tiˌɛɾɾə dɛl ˈfweɪgoʊ / (AmE), / tiˌɛrə dɛl ˈfweɪgəʊ / (BrE)
an archipelago off the southern tip of South America, separated from the mainland by the Strait of Magellan and divided between Chile and Argentina
archipelago in southern South America

Examples

Many adventurous travelers dream of visiting Tierra del Fuego at the southern tip of South America.
The climate in Tierra del Fuego is often cold and windy, making it a challenging place to explore.
Much of Tierra del Fuego is wilderness, attracting scientists and nature lovers alike.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Tierra del Fuego' meaning 'Land of Fire'.
