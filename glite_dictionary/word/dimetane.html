Dimetane

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪməˌteɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɪmɪteɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked antihistamine drug used to treat allergic reactions such as rhinitis.
antihistamine drug (trade name)
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Dimetane to help with my seasonal allergies.
If you experience drowsiness while taking Dimetane, avoid driving or operating machinery.
Dimetane can be effective for relieving symptoms of hay fever and other allergic conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brompheniramine - the generic drug that is the active ingredient in Dimetane
