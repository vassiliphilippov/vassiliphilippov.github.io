cornetist

1. NOUN

/ kɔrˈnetɪst / (AmE), / kɔːˈnɛtɪst / (BrE)
A musician who plays the cornet or trumpet, especially as a professional or skilled performer.
musician who plays the cornet

Examples

The cornetist performed a beautiful solo during the jazz concert.
She began her career as a cornetist in a local brass band.
Many famous jazz musicians started out as cornetists before switching to trumpet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trumpeter - a musician who plays the trumpet

Etymology

From 'cornet' (the brass instrument) plus '-ist' (denoting a person who practices or is concerned with something).
