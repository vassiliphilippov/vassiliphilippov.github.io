metrestick

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiː.tɚˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈmiː.təˌstɪk / (BrE)
a measuring stick one meter long, typically marked in centimeters and millimeters
measuring stick, one meter long
british

Examples

The technician measured the desk with a metrestick to ensure accuracy.
Students are often provided with a metrestick during physics experiments.
She found an old metrestick in the back of the supply closet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meterstick - a measuring stick one meter long
  • Antonym: yardstick - a measuring stick one yard long

Etymology

From metre + stick; 'metre' is the British spelling of 'meter'.
