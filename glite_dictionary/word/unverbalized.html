unverbalized

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈvɜrbəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈvɜːbəlaɪzd / (BrE)
not expressed or stated explicitly in words; not made explicit
not made explicit; not expressed in words

Examples

There was an unverbalized understanding between the two colleagues during the meeting.
Much of their communication remained unverbalized, relying on gestures and glances instead of words.
Her unverbalized disappointment was evident in her expression even though she didn't say a word.

Synset Examples

the unexpressed terms of the agreement
things left unsaid
some kind of unspoken agreement
his action is clear but his reason remains unstated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unspoken - not expressed in speech or writing; tacit
  • Synonym: unstated - not stated or declared; implicit
  • Antonym: explicit - stated clearly and in detail; leaving no room for confusion or doubt

Etymology

Formed from prefix un- (not) + verbalize (to put into words) + -ed.
