Micronor

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪkrəˌnɔr / (AmE), / ˈmɪkrənɔː / (BrE)
A trade name for an oral contraceptive pill containing the progestin compound norethindrone.
brand of oral contraceptive pill
medical

Examples

Her doctor recommended Micronor when she requested a progestin-only birth control pill.
Some women prefer Micronor because it does not contain estrogen.
You must take Micronor at the same time every day for it to be effective.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: norethindrone - a synthetic progestin used in birth control pills
