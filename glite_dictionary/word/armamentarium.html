armamentarium

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːrməmenˈtɛriəm / (AmE), / ˌɑːməmenˈteəriəm / (BrE)
the complete set of equipment and techniques available for use, especially in the practice of medicine or science
equipment and methods used in medicine or science
medical

Examples

The hospital's armamentarium now includes cutting-edge diagnostic imaging devices.
A physician's armamentarium is constantly expanding with new drugs and technologies.
Sterilization procedures are a crucial part of the surgical armamentarium.

Etymology

From Latin 'armamentarium' (arsenal, armory), from 'armamenta' (tools, implements).
