instantaneousness

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪnstənˈteɪniəsnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪnstənˈteɪniəsnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of occurring or being done immediately, without delay.
immediacy; quality of happening instantly

Examples

The instantaneousness of the computer's response impressed everyone in the office.
We were amazed by the instantaneousness with which the alarm system activated.
Modern news platforms strive for instantaneousness in delivering information to their audiences.

Synset Examples

the immediacy of their response
the instancy of modern communication

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immediacy - the quality of bringing one into direct and instant involvement
  • Antonym: delay - a period of time by which something is late or postponed

Etymology

From 'instantaneous' + '-ness', modeled after Latin-based forms.
