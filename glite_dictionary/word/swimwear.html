swimwear

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪmˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈswɪmˌweə / (BrE)
clothes designed to be worn for swimming, especially in a pool or at the beach.
clothing for swimming

Examples

This store offers a wide variety of swimwear for both men and women.
She packed her favorite swimwear before heading to the beach.
Modern swimwear comes in many different styles and colors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bathing suit - a garment worn for swimming
  • Synonym: swimsuit - a tight-fitting garment worn for swimming

Etymology

From 'swim' + 'wear', formed in English.
