importee

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪm.pɔːrˈti / (AmE), / ˌɪm.pɔːˈtiː / (BrE)
a person who has been brought into a country from abroad, especially for work or service
person brought from abroad

Examples

Following the labor shortage, many importees worked on plantations for years.
As an importee, he faced many challenges adapting to the new society.
The museum exhibit highlighted the stories of importees from different centuries.

Synset Examples

the lead role was played by an import from Sweden
they are descendants of indentured importees

Related Terms

  • Synonym: import - to bring in from abroad, especially goods or products

Etymology

Formed from 'import' + suffix '-ee', indicating a person affected by an action.
