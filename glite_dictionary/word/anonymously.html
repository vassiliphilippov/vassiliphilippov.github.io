anonymously

1. ADV

/ əˈnɑːnɪməsli / (AmE), / əˈnɒnɪməsli / (BrE)
without revealing one's name or identity
without giving a name

Examples

The author donated a large sum of money anonymously to the hospital.
Critics speculated about who wrote the review since it was published anonymously.
Many tips are submitted anonymously to the police to protect the informants' identities.

Synset Examples

she wrote these letters anonymously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secretly - in a secret manner, without others knowing

Etymology

From 'anonymous' (from Greek anōnymos 'without a name') + -ly.
