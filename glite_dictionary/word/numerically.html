numerically

1. ADV

/ njuˈmɛrɪkli / (AmE), / njuˈmɛrɪkli / (BrE)
in terms of numbers or quantity; with respect to numbers
with regard to numbers

Examples

The results were analyzed numerically to determine the growth rate.
Women remain underrepresented numerically in the engineering profession.
Although the teams looked similar, Team A was numerically stronger.

Synset Examples

in ten years' time the Oxbridge mathematicians, scientists, and engineers will not be much more significant numerically than the Oxbridge medical schools are now

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quantitatively - in terms of quantity or amount

Etymology

From 'numerical' (from Latin numerus meaning 'number') + -ly.
