fittingly

1. ADV

/ ˈfɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈfɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that is appropriate or suitable for the circumstances.
in an appropriate manner

Examples

The president was fittingly honored at the ceremony for his decades of service.
She fittingly wore traditional attire to the cultural celebration.
It rained on their wedding day, which fittingly matched their shared love of thunderstorms.

Synset Examples

he was appropriately dressed
If you don't behave properly, you'll have to leave!
I met the junior senator from Illinois and I was duly impressed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appropriately - in a proper or suitable manner
  • Synonym: properly - in a way that is correct or suitable
  • Antonym: unsuitably - in an unsuitable or inappropriate manner

Etymology

From the adjective 'fitting' plus the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
