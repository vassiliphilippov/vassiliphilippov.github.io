uranology

1. NOUN

/ ˌjʊrəˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of celestial bodies and the universe as a whole; astronomy
study of celestial bodies and the universe

Examples

She pursued a doctorate in uranology to deepen her understanding of the cosmos.
The museum featured an entire exhibit dedicated to the history of uranology.
His lifelong fascination with stars eventually led him into the field of uranology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astronomy - the scientific study of celestial objects, space, and the physical universe as a whole

Etymology

From Greek ouranos (sky, heaven) + -logy (study of).
