eidos

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪdoʊs / (AmE), / ˈaɪdɒs / (BrE)
(Anthropology) The distinctive expression of the cognitive or intellectual character of a culture or social group.
distinctive intellectual character of a culture

Examples

Anthropologists analyzed the eidos of the tribe to understand their worldview.
The eidos of classical Greek society valued reason and debate.
Every culture has its own unique eidos that shapes its traditions and beliefs.

Etymology

From Greek εἶδος (eidos), meaning 'form' or 'essence'.
