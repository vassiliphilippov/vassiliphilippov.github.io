CF

1. NOUN

/ si ɛf / (AmE), / siː ɛf / (BrE)
abbreviation for cystic fibrosis, a hereditary disease in which thick mucus clogs the lungs, intestines, and pancreas
abbreviation for cystic fibrosis (hereditary disease)
medical

Examples

The doctor suspected that the child might have CF due to his persistent cough and breathing issues.
Many advances in gene therapy are giving hope to people living with CF.
Routine screening for CF is now recommended for newborns in several countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cystic fibrosis - hereditary disease causing thick mucus to clog lungs and other organs

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'cystic fibrosis'.
