salai

1. NOUN

/ səˈlaɪ / (AmE), / səˈlaɪ / (BrE)
An East Indian tree (Boswellia serrata) that yields a resin used medicinally and as incense.
East Indian tree yielding medicinal resin

Examples

The resin from the salai tree is valued in traditional Ayurvedic medicine.
Many people burn salai resin as incense during religious ceremonies.
Researchers are studying the medicinal properties of compounds found in salai.

Etymology

From Hindi 'salai', referring to the tree Boswellia serrata.
