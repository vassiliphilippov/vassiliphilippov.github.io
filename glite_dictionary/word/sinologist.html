Sinologist

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈnɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / sɪˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a specialist in the study of Chinese language, history, or culture
expert in Chinese studies

Examples

She became a renowned Sinologist after years of researching ancient Chinese manuscripts.
As a Sinologist, he frequently traveled to China to collaborate with local historians.
Many Sinologists are fluent in Mandarin and have extensive knowledge of Chinese philosophy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sinology - the study of Chinese language, literature, and civilization

Etymology

From French 'sinologie' (Chinese studies), from Late Latin 'Sinae' (the Chinese) + '-logy' (study of).
