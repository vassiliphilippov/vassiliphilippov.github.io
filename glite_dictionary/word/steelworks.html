steelworks

1. NOUN

/ ˈstiːlˌwɜrks / (AmE), / ˈstiːlˌwɜːks / (BrE)
a factory or group of factories where steel is manufactured
factory where steel is produced

Examples

The old steelworks have been converted into a museum and art center.
Many families in the town depended on the steelworks for employment.
Pollution from the steelworks affected the local environment for decades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steel mill - factory where steel is manufactured
  • Degree Lower: foundry - workshop or factory for casting metal

Etymology

Formed from 'steel' and 'works', meaning a place where a specific product is produced.
