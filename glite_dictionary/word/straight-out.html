straight-out

1. ADJ

/ ˌstreɪtˈaʊt / (AmE), / ˌstreɪtˈaʊt / (BrE)
Without reservation, qualification, or exception; outright or direct.
outright, without reservation

Examples

Her comments were a straight-out denial of any wrongdoing.
He gave a straight-out answer to the difficult question.
It's a straight-out lie, and everyone knows it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outright - without reservation or qualification; direct or total
  • Antonym: qualified - limited or restricted in some way
