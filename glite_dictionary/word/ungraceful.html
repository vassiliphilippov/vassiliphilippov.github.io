ungraceful

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɡreɪsfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡreɪsfəl / (BrE)
lacking grace, elegance, or smoothness in movement or form; clumsy or awkward.
lacking grace or elegance

Examples

Her ungraceful movements made it clear she was new to ballet.
The sculpture was oddly ungraceful compared to the artist's other works.
He tried to apologize, but his manner was abrupt and ungraceful.

Synset Examples

a graceless production of the play
his stature low … his bearing ungraceful

Related Terms

  • Antonym: graceful - having elegance or beauty of movement or form
  • Synonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or action; lacking skill or grace

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'graceful'.
