Darfur

1. PROPN

/ dɑrˈfʊr / (AmE), / dɑːˈfʊə / (BrE)
a region in western Sudan, known for its history, ethnic diversity, and humanitarian crises
region of western Sudan

Examples

The conflict in Darfur has drawn international attention and aid efforts.
Many families in Darfur have been displaced due to violence and instability.
Reports from Darfur describe severe shortages of food and medical supplies.

Synset Examples

Darfur was a semi-independent sultanate until 1917 and is ethnically distinct from central Sudan
