corneous

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɔrniəs / (AmE), / ˈkɔːniəs / (BrE)
made of horn or of a substance resembling horn; horny in texture or composition.
made of or resembling horn

Examples

The turtle's shell has a corneous layer that provides extra protection.
Many snails possess a corneous operculum to close their shells.
The scientist noted the corneous texture of the specimen's beak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horny - having a texture or composition like horn

Etymology

From Latin 'corneus' (horny), from 'cornu' (horn).
