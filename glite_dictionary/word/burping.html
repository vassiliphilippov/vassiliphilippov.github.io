burping

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝː.pɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɜː.pɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of expelling gas noisily from the stomach through the mouth; a belch.
noisy expulsion of stomach gas (belch)

Examples

After drinking the soda too quickly, he couldn't stop burping in front of everyone.
Burping is a natural way for babies to release swallowed air after feeding.
She apologized for burping loudly at the dinner table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: belching - the act of expelling gas from the stomach through the mouth
