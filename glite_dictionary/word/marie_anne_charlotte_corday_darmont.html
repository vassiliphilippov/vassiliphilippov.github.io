Marie Anne Charlotte Corday d'Armont

1. PROPN

/ mɑːˈriː ˈæn ʃɑːrˈlɒt kɔːrˈdeɪ dɑːrˈmɒnt / (AmE), / maˈriː an ʃaːˈlɔt kɔːˈdɛ d‿aʀˈmɔ̃ / (BrE)
(1768–1793) French revolutionary figure known for assassinating Jean-Paul Marat during the French Revolution; regarded as a heroine by some for her opposition to tyranny.
French revolutionary who assassinated Marat
