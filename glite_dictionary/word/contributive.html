contributive

1. ADJ

/ kənˈtrɪb.jə.tɪv / (AmE), / kənˈtrɪb.jʊ.tɪv / (BrE)
Helping to bring about a particular result; partly responsible for something.
helping to bring about a result

Examples

Many factors were contributive to the company's recent success.
Poor communication proved contributive to the team's failure to meet the deadline.
The survey identified several contributive causes of employee dissatisfaction.

Synset Examples

working conditions are not conducive to productivity
the seaport was a contributing factor in the growth of the city
a contributory factor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contributory - helping to bring about; playing a part in something

Etymology

From Latin 'contributivus', from 'contributus' (to bring together), from 'contribuere' (to contribute).
