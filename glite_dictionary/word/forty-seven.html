forty-seven

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɔrtiˈsɛvən / (AmE), / ˌfɔːtiˈsɛvən / (BrE)
being seven more than forty; the cardinal number 47.
the number 47

Examples

There are forty-seven students enrolled in this course.
The team scored forty-seven points in the final game.
She celebrated her forty-seventh birthday last week.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty-six - the number 46; being six more than forty
  • Degree Higher: forty-eight - the number 48; being eight more than forty

Etymology

Formed from 'forty' and 'seven', following regular English numeric construction.
