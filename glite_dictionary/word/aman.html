A'man

1. PROPN

/ ɑːˈmɑn / (AmE), / ɑːˈman / (BrE)
the Israeli military intelligence agency responsible for producing comprehensive national intelligence assessments for the prime minister and cabinet
Israeli military intelligence agency

Examples

The prime minister requested an urgent report from A'man following the security incident.
A'man plays a crucial role in shaping Israel's defense policies.
A former A'man officer published a book about his experiences in intelligence.

Etymology

From Hebrew אמן (Aman), abbreviation for אגף המודיעין (Agaf HaModi'in), meaning 'Intelligence Directorate'.
