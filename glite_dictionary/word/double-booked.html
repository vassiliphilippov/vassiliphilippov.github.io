double-booked

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʌb.əl bʊkt / (AmE), / ˈdʌb.əl bʊkt / (BrE)
having been scheduled for two appointments or events at the same time by mistake or oversight.
scheduled for two events at the same time

Examples

I realized I was double-booked for the conference and a doctor's appointment on Thursday.
Due to a scheduling error, the meeting room was double-booked by two different teams.
She apologized for being double-booked and promised to reschedule one of the commitments.

Synset Examples

She found out she was double booked for the dinner.
He was double booked for both events this weekend.
I accidentally double booked my schedule for the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overbooked - having more bookings than available capacity
