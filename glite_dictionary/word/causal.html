causal

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɔzəl / (AmE), / ˈkɔːzəl / (BrE)
relating to, involving, or constituting a cause; acting as a cause of something
relating to cause and effect

Examples

There is a causal link between smoking and lung cancer.
Researchers are investigating the causal factors behind the disease.
Understanding causal relationships is crucial for scientific studies.

Synset Examples

a causal relationship between scarcity and higher prices

Related Terms

  • Antonym: effectual - producing an intended result, effective

Etymology

From Latin causa (cause).
