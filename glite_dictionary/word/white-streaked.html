white-streaked

1. ADJ

/ waɪt strikt / (AmE), / waɪt striːkt / (BrE)
Marked or characterized by streaks of white color.
marked with white streaks

Examples

The bird's wings were white-streaked, making it easy to spot among the foliage.
She admired the white-streaked marble pillars in the ancient building.
The painter chose a white-streaked fabric to add texture to her design.
