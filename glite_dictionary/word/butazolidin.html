Butazolidin

1. PROPN

/ ˌbjuː.təˈzɑː.lə.dɪn / (AmE), / ˌbjuː.təˈzɒl.ɪ.dɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked anti-inflammatory drug used to treat pain and inflammation, especially in arthritis.
anti-inflammatory drug (brand name)

Examples

The doctor prescribed Butazolidin to help manage her joint pain.
Some patients experience side effects after taking Butazolidin for an extended period.
Butazolidin was once widely used to reduce inflammation in horses as well.
