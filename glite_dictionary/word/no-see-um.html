no-see-um

1. NOUN

/ ˌnoʊˈsiː.əm / (AmE), / ˌnəʊˈsiː.əm / (BrE)
A very small biting midge or fly, especially one that feeds on the blood of mammals and birds.
tiny biting midge or fly

Examples

I ended up covered in itchy bites from no-see-ums after the hike.
No-see-ums can be a real nuisance when camping near the lake.
To avoid no-see-ums, we set up our tent with a fine mesh screen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biting midge - a small fly that bites and sucks blood
  • Antonym: mosquito - winged insect known for biting and sucking blood

Etymology

From "no see 'em," referring to their small size and the difficulty of seeing them.
