arrayed

1. ADJ

/ əˈreɪd / (AmE), / əˈreɪd / (BrE)
dressed or decorated, especially in ceremonial or impressive attire or equipment.
dressed in ceremonial or impressive attire

Examples

The graduates were arrayed in blue gowns and caps for the ceremony.
The knight was arrayed in shining armor, ready for battle.
All the guests were arrayed in their finest clothing for the royal banquet.

Synset Examples

professors arrayed in robes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressed - wearing clothes; clothed
  • Synonym: adorned - decorated or made beautiful
  • Antonym: plain - not decorated or elaborate; simple

Etymology

From the past participle of 'array,' from Old French 'areyer' (to arrange, prepare).
