prestigiousness

1. NOUN

/ prɛˈstɪdʒəsnəs / (AmE), / prɛˈstɪdʒəsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being respected or admired because of success, influence, or wealth
quality of being prestigious

Examples

She was drawn to the prestigiousness of the university and its distinguished faculty.
The prestigiousness of the award made it highly sought after among artists.
Companies often use the prestigiousness of their brand to attract top-level clients.

Synset Examples

he wanted to achieve power and prestige

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prestige - high status or respect gained by achievements

Etymology

From 'prestigious' + '-ness', where 'prestigious' derives from Latin 'praestigiae' (illusions, tricks).
