teasingly

1. ADV

/ ˈtizɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtiːzɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that playfully provokes or mocks someone, often to amuse or annoy them lightly.
in a playfully provoking manner

Examples

She smiled teasingly at her brother after hiding his phone.
He teasingly suggested that she was afraid to try the new food.
The cat batted at the string teasingly, never quite catching it.

Synset Examples

‘You hate things to be out of order, don't you?’ she said teasingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: playfully - in a fun or lighthearted way
  • Degree Higher: mockingly - in a scornful or ridiculing way
