Laos

1. PROPN

/ ˈlaʊs / (AmE), / ˈlaʊs / (BrE)
A mountainous, landlocked country in Southeast Asia, officially the Lao People's Democratic Republic; known for its communist government and independence from France in 1949.
country in Southeast Asia

Examples

She traveled to Laos last year to explore the beautiful mountains and ancient temples.
After gaining independence from France, Laos became a communist state.
The Mekong River forms a natural border between Laos and Thailand.

Etymology

From the Lao people's endonym and French 'Laos'.
