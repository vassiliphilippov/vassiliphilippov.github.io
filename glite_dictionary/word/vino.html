vino

1. NOUN

/ ˈviːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈviːnəʊ / (BrE)
(informal) Wine, especially red wine, or any type of wine in general.
informal term for wine
informal

Examples

We picked up a nice bottle of vino for dinner last night.
After a long week, she enjoys relaxing with some vino and good company.
They served homemade pizza and plenty of red vino at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wine - an alcoholic drink made from fermented grape juice

Etymology

From Italian and Spanish 'vino', meaning 'wine', from Latin 'vinum'.
