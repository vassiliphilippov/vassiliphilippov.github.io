Trautvetteria

1. NOUN

/ traʊtˌvɛtəriˈa / (AmE), / traʊtˌvɛtɛˈriːə / (BrE)
a small genus of perennial herbs in the buttercup family (Ranunculaceae), commonly known as false bugbane
genus of perennial herbs (false bugbane)

Examples

The shade garden features Trautvetteria, valued for its delicate white flowers.
Botanists recently discovered a new species within the genus Trautvetteria.
Trautvetteria prefers moist, woodland habitats and blooms in late spring.

Etymology

Named after Russian botanist Ernst Rudolf von Trautvetter (1809–1889).
