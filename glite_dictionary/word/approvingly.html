approvingly

1. ADV

/ əˈpruvɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈpruːvɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows approval or praise.
in a manner showing approval

Examples

She smiled approvingly at her son's performance.
The teacher nodded approvingly as the student gave the correct answer.
He listened approvingly while his colleague explained the plan.

Synset Examples

she nodded approvingly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: disapprovingly - in a way that shows disapproval
