countywide

1. ADJ

/ ˈkaʊntiˌwaɪd / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntiˌwaɪd / (BrE)
Occurring or extending throughout the whole of a county.
throughout an entire county

Examples

The new health initiative will be implemented countywide next year.
Voters showed strong support for the countywide recycling program.
Officials called for countywide road repairs after the harsh winter.

Synset Examples

a countywide war on drugs
countywide elections

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: nationwide - Occurring or extending throughout a whole nation or country.
  • Degree Lower: local - Relating to a specific area, not widespread.

Etymology

Formed from 'county' + 'wide', modeled on similar compounds like 'nationwide'.
