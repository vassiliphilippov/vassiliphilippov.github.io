goggle-eye

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑɡl̩ aɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡɒɡl aɪ / (BrE)
a fish of Atlantic coastal waters, commonly used for bait
fish used as bait in the Atlantic

Examples

Fishermen often catch goggle-eye for use as bait when targeting larger fish.
The waters off the coast are teeming with goggle-eye during the summer months.
I put a fresh goggle-eye on my hook before casting into the surf.
