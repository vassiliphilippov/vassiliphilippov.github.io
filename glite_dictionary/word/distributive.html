distributive

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈstrɪbjətɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɪbjʊtɪv / (BrE)
serving to distribute, allot, or disperse something; relating to distribution.
serving to distribute or allot

Examples

The teacher used distributive methods to ensure each student received supplies.
In grammar, a distributive adjective refers to members of a group individually.
The funds were allocated in a distributive manner among the various departments.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: collective - relating to or shared by all members of a group

Etymology

From Latin distributivus, from distribuere (to divide, distribute).
