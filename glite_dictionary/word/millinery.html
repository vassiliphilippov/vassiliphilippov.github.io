millinery

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪnəri / (BrE)
A shop that sells women's hats and other articles made or sold by a milliner.
shop selling women's hats

Examples

She bought her new hat at the town's oldest millinery.
The millinery displayed elegant hats in the front window.
Years ago, every high street had a millinery specializing in women's fashion.

Etymology

From milliner (a maker or seller of women's hats), from Milan, Italy, once famed for fine wares.

2. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪnəri / (BrE)
women's hats or the business of making and selling women's hats
women's hats; hat-making business

Examples

She has always loved vintage millinery and collects unique hats from every era.
The shop specializes in handmade millinery for special occasions such as weddings and races.
Elegant millinery adorned the windows, attracting customers with its vibrant feathers and silk ribbons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hat - a covering for the head, usually with a brim

Etymology

From milliner (hat-maker), originally from Milan, Italy, famed for fine wares.
