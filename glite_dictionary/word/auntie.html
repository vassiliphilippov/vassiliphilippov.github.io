auntie

1. NOUN

/ ˈænti / (AmE), / ˈɑːnti / (BrE)
the sister of one's father or mother, or the wife of one's uncle; an affectionate term for aunt
father's or mother's sister or uncle's wife

Examples

My auntie always bakes the best cookies during the holidays.
We visited our auntie and uncle in the countryside last summer.
As a child, I loved spending weekends with my favorite auntie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aunt - the sister of one's father or mother, or the wife of one's uncle
  • Antonym: uncle - the brother of one's father or mother, or the husband of one's aunt

Etymology

Diminutive form of 'aunt'.
