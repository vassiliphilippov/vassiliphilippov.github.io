shaggy-haired

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃæɡiˌhɛrd / (AmE), / ˈʃaɡiˌheəd / (BrE)
Having thick, long, and untidy or poorly groomed hair.
with thick and untidy hair

Examples

The old sheepdog was shaggy-haired after months without a haircut.
She preferred her actors shaggy-haired for the rugged mountain movie role.
The shaggy-haired youth stood out in the crowd of well-groomed students.

Synset Examples

bushy locks
a shaggy beard

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shaggy - having long, thick, and untidy hair or fur
