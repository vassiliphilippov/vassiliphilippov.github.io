babbling

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbæblɪŋ / (BrE)
the sounds made by babies before they learn to speak, or unintelligible speech resembling these sounds
baby's preverbal sounds or gibberish

Examples

The baby's babbling filled the room with joyful noise.
All I could hear from the next table was the babbling of children pretending to talk on the phone.
His explanation sounded like mere babbling to the confused audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gibberish - meaningless or unintelligible speech
  • Synonym: prattle - foolish or inconsequential talk
