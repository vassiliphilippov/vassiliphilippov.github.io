Mulwi

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʊlwi / (AmE), / ˈmʊlwi / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken south of Lake Chad, mainly in the Far North Region of Cameroon, in the Mayo-Danay division, Maga subdivision.
Chadic language spoken in Cameroon

Examples

The Mulwi language is still spoken in several villages near Lake Chad.
Linguists are working to document the grammar of Mulwi.
She grew up speaking both French and Mulwi at home.
