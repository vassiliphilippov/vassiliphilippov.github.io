gummite

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmaɪt / (BrE)
a yellow to orange, gummy mineral mixture of uranium oxides and silicates formed by the alteration of pitchblende.
gummy uranium mineral mixture

Examples

The miners discovered a vein of gummite near the old uranium deposit.
Samples of gummite are often studied for their unique chemical composition.
The yellow-orange color of gummite distinguishes it from other uranium minerals.
