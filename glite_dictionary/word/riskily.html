riskily

1. ADV

/ ˈrɪskəli / (AmE), / ˈrɪskɪli / (BrE)
In a manner that involves or exposes to risk or danger; in a dangerous or risky way.
in a risky or dangerous way

Examples

He drove riskily through the snowy streets, barely avoiding several accidents.
She invested riskily in start-up companies, hoping for huge returns.
The climber riskily attempted the steep ascent without any safety gear.

Synset Examples

he lost the game by playing too riskily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recklessly - without regard to danger or consequences
