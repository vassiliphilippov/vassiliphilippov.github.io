celerity

1. NOUN

/ səˈlɛrəti / (AmE), / səˈlɛrəti / (BrE)
Swiftness of movement or rapidity; great speed.
swiftness; rapid movement

Examples

She completed the project with remarkable celerity, astonishing her colleagues.
The troops moved with celerity to secure their positions along the border.
The chef chopped vegetables with such celerity that it impressed everyone in the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speed - the rate at which someone or something moves or operates
  • Antonym: slowness - the quality of being slow; lack of speed

Etymology

From Latin celeritas, from celer (swift, fast).
