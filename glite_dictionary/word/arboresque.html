arboresque

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑːr.bəˈrɛsk / (AmE), / ˌɑː.bəˈrɛsk / (BrE)
having a form or branching structure that resembles a tree; tree-like in appearance.
tree-like in form or branching structure

Examples

The large coral formation was distinctly arboresque, with branches spreading in all directions.
Artists admired the arboresque patterns seen in ancient bronze sculptures.
The sponges grew in an arboresque manner, their limbs spreading upward like miniature trees.

Synset Examples

arborescent coral found off the coast of Bermuda
dendriform sponges

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arborescent - resembling a tree in growth or appearance
  • Synonym: dendritic - branched like a tree; tree-shaped

Etymology

From Latin 'arbor' (tree) + French '-esque' (in the style of).
