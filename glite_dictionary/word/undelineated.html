undelineated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.dəˈlɪn.i.eɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈlɪn.i.eɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
not clearly or precisely defined or represented; lacking clear boundaries or details
not clearly defined or outlined

Examples

The edges of the painting were intentionally undelineated, giving it a dreamlike quality.
The boundaries between the two regions remain undelineated to this day.
His role in the project was somewhat undelineated and ambiguous.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indistinct - not clearly marked or defined
  • Antonym: delineated - clearly outlined or defined

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (not) to 'delineated', from Latin delineare (to outline, to sketch).
