sharpy

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑːrpi / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːpi / (BrE)
an alert and energetic person, especially one who is quick to notice or respond to things
an alert, energetic person

Examples

Everyone likes working with sharpy who always has bright ideas.
She proved herself to be a real sharpy on the first day of the job.
Don't underestimate him—he's a sharpy and notices every detail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: live wire - a person who is very active, lively, and energetic

2. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑːrpi / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːpi / (BrE)
A professional card player who makes a living by cheating at card games.
card cheat; professional cheating gambler

Examples

The poker game was rigged by a notorious sharpy who always managed to win big.
People avoided letting the sharpy join their tables at the casino.
Rumors spread quickly when a known sharpy arrived in town, looking for his next victim.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cardsharp - a person who cheats at card games; swindler at cards
  • Synonym: cardsharper - a professional card player who cheats
