oboist

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊboʊɪst / (AmE), / ˈəʊbəʊɪst / (BrE)
A musician who plays the oboe.
musician who plays the oboe

Examples

The principal oboist captivated the audience with a beautiful solo during the concert.
As an aspiring oboist, she practiced for hours every day to perfect her technique.
The orchestra was looking to hire a talented oboist for their upcoming season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oboe - a woodwind instrument with a double reed

Etymology

From oboe + -ist, meaning someone who performs or practices the oboe.
