pieplant

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈpaɪˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
a plant with long, thick, pinkish, sour leafstalks that are usually cooked and sweetened, especially for use in pies; another name for rhubarb
rhubarb plant used in cooking

Examples

The recipe calls for two cups of chopped pieplant and plenty of sugar.
Every spring, she harvests fresh pieplant from her garden to make tarts.
Stewed pieplant is a traditional dessert in many rural areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rhubarb - a plant whose sour stalks are used in cooking; synonym of pieplant
