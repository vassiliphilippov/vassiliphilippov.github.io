lusty

1. ADJ

/ ˈlʌsti / (AmE), / ˈlʌsti / (BrE)
full of healthy vigor, energy, or robust passion; displaying strong enthusiasm or vitality
full of vigor or robust passion

Examples

His lusty singing could be heard throughout the entire hall.
The lusty cheers of the crowd inspired the team to victory.
She gave a lusty laugh that filled the room with warmth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vigorous - strong, healthy, and full of energy
  • Antonym: frail - weak or lacking in strength

Etymology

From Middle English *lusti* (pleasant, vigorous), from *lust* (pleasure, desire).
