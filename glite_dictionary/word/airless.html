airless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛr.ləs / (AmE), / ˈeə.ləs / (BrE)
(of a place) lacking fresh air; stuffy or poorly ventilated.
lacking fresh air

Examples

The old library felt airless after being locked up all summer.
We spent the night in an airless room at the back of the building.
The subway platform was hot and airless during rush hour.

Synset Examples

a dusty airless attic
the dreadfully close atmosphere
hot and stuffy and the air was blue with smoke

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stuffy - lacking fresh air; close or oppressive
