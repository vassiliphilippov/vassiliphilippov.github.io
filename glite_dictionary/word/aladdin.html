Aladdin

1. PROPN

/ əˈlædɪn / (AmE), / əˈlædɪn / (BrE)
A boy in the Arabian Nights tales who acquires a magic lamp that allows him to summon a powerful genie.
boy in Arabian Nights with a magic lamp

Examples

The story of Aladdin and his magic lamp has inspired countless films and musicals.
Many children want to dress up as Aladdin for Halloween after seeing the animated movie.
Aladdin's adventures remind us that resourcefulness and kindness can change one's fate.

Etymology

From Arabic ‘Alāʼ ad-Dīn, meaning 'nobility of religion'; introduced to English via the French translation of the Arabian Nights.
