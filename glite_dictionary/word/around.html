around

1. ADV

/ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to many different places or with movement in no particular direction; here and there.

Examples

Rumors quickly spread around after the announcement was made.
She likes to travel around during the summer, visiting new countries each year.
