pugilistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌpjuːdʒəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌpjuːdʒɪˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to boxing or boxers; characteristic of or resembling a boxer's fighting style
relating to boxing or boxers

Examples

His pugilistic instincts made him a natural in the boxing ring.
The debate quickly took on a pugilistic tone as both sides exchanged harsh words.
She admired the pugilistic discipline of professional fighters.

Synset Examples

a pugilistic career

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boxing - the sport or practice of fighting with fists
  • Antonym: nonpugilistic - not related to boxing or boxers

Etymology

From Latin 'pugil' (boxer), via 'pugilism'.
