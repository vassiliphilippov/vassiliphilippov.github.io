qindarka

1. NOUN

/ kinˈdarkə / (AmE), / kinˈdaːkə / (BrE)
A monetary subunit of Albania, equal to one hundredth of a lek.
Albanian currency subunit

Examples

The coin was worth just one qindarka, making it the smallest denomination available.
He collected rare qindarka from different years during his travels in Albania.
Prices were once listed in leks and qindarka before the subunit ceased to be used.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: lek - The basic monetary unit of Albania

Etymology

From Albanian 'qindarkë', from 'qind' meaning 'hundred'.
