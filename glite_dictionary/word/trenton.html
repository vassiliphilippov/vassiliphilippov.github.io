Trenton

1. PROPN

/ ˈtrɛn.tən / (AmE), / ˈtrɛn.tən / (BrE)
The capital city of the state of New Jersey, located in western New Jersey on the Delaware River.
capital city of New Jersey, US

Examples

Trenton is known for its rich history and role in the American Revolutionary War.
