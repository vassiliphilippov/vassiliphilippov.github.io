bronzer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɑːnzər / (AmE), / ˈbrɒnzə / (BrE)
A cosmetic powder, cream, or liquid applied to the skin to give it a tanned appearance.
cosmetic for creating tanned skin look

Examples

She dusted a light layer of bronzer on her cheeks for a sun-kissed glow.
Many beauty enthusiasts use bronzer to contour and define their facial features.
Finding the right shade of bronzer can make a big difference in your makeup look.

Synset Examples

Sculpting your face with a bronzing powder will make it appear slimmer.
If you don't like sunbathing, you can try a temporary tanner, like a bronzer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blush - A cosmetic for giving color to the cheeks
  • Synonym: highlighter - A cosmetic that emphasizes parts of the face with a light-reflecting effect
