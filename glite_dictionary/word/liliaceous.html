liliaceous

1. ADJ

/ ˌlɪliˈeɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌlɪlɪˈeɪʃəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of plants of the lily family (Liliaceae)
relating to the lily family (Liliaceae)

Examples

The garden featured many liliaceous flowers, including tulips and lilies.
Researchers studied the medicinal properties of liliaceous plants.
Many liliaceous species are valued for their ornamental beauty.

Etymology

From Latin 'liliaceus' (made of lilies), from 'lilium' (lily) + '-aceous' (having the nature of).
