/ ˈbæstərdli / (AmE), / ˈbɑːstədli / (BrE)

illegitimate; born out of wedlock
archaic

Examples

Many bastardly children faced discrimination in medieval society.
In historical records, a bastardly birth could affect inheritance rights.
