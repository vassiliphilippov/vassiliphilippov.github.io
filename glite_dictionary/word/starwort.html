starwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑːr.wɜrt / (AmE), / ˈstɑː.wɜːt / (BrE)
A low-growing plant of the Northern Hemisphere, bearing small white star-shaped flowers, especially of the genus Stellaria.
a low-growing plant with star-shaped flowers

Examples

The meadow was dotted with delicate starwort in full bloom.
Gardeners often plant starwort to attract pollinators to their gardens.
She identified the small white flowers as starwort during her nature walk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Stellaria - the genus of flowering plants known as starworts

Etymology

From 'star' (referring to the shape of the flowers) + 'wort', an old English word for plant or herb.
