unexplained

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪkˈspleɪnd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈspleɪnd / (BrE)
not accounted for or made clear by explanation; having no known or understood cause or reason.
not explained or accounted for

Examples

The cause of the blackout remains unexplained despite many investigations.
There was an unexplained delay in the delivery of the package.
Scientists are puzzled by an unexplained rise in temperature in the region.

Synset Examples

accomplished by some unexplained process

Related Terms

  • Antonym: explained - accounted for by reasoning or information

Etymology

From un- (not) + explained, past participle of explain.

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪkˈspleɪnd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪkˈspleɪnd / (BrE)
not described or accounted for; having no known cause or reason
having no known cause or explanation

Examples

The doctor mentioned several unexplained symptoms during the examination.
There was an unexplained delay in the delivery of the package.
The lights in the sky remain unexplained despite numerous investigations.

Synset Examples

an unexplained error

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inexplicable - impossible to explain or account for
  • Antonym: explained - having had the reason or cause made clear

Etymology

From English prefix 'un-' (not) + 'explained', past participle of 'explain'.
