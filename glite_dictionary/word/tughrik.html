tughrik

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʊɡrɪk / (AmE), / ˈtʊɡrɪk / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Mongolia, equal to 100 mongo
basic monetary unit of Mongolia

Examples

The price of the meal was 15,000 tughrik at the restaurant.
She exchanged her dollars for tughrik before traveling to Ulaanbaatar.
Inflation has caused the value of the tughrik to fluctuate in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mongolian tögrög - another spelling of Mongolia's currency

Etymology

From Mongolian 'төгрөг' (tögrög), meaning 'circle' or 'coin'.
