yahoo

1. NOUN

/ jæˈhu / (AmE), / jɑːˈhuː / (BrE)
an uncultured or boorish person who is not very intelligent or interested in culture
uncultured or unintelligent person

Examples

Don't be such a yahoo—try to appreciate the art instead of making fun of it.
The theater was ruined by a group of yahoos shouting and throwing popcorn.
Some considered him a yahoo for his loud and uncivilized behavior at the dinner party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boor - a rude or unmannerly person
  • Synonym: philistine - a person who is indifferent to culture and the arts
  • Antonym: gentleman - a well-mannered or cultured man

Etymology

From the name of a race of brutish creatures in Jonathan Swift's 'Gulliver's Travels' (1726).
