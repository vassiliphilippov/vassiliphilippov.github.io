Platichthys

1. PROPN

/ pləˈtɪkθɪs / (AmE), / pləˈtɪkθɪs / (BrE)
A genus of flatfish in the family Pleuronectidae, which includes the starry flounder.
genus of flatfish (starry flounders)

Examples

The genus Platichthys includes species such as the starry flounder.
Researchers studied the habitat preferences of Platichthys in coastal waters.
Several members of Platichthys are important to commercial fisheries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: starry flounder - a common species within the Platichthys genus

Etymology

From Greek 'platys' (broad) and 'ichthys' (fish).
