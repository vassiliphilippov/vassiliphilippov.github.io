encephalomeningitis

1. NOUN

/ ɛnˌsɛfəloʊməˌnɪndʒaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ɛnˌsɛfələʊmɛnɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the brain and spinal cord together with their meninges.
inflammation of brain and spinal cord meninges
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with encephalomeningitis after exhibiting severe neurological symptoms.
Prompt treatment is crucial in cases of encephalomeningitis to prevent complications.
Researchers are studying vaccines that could reduce the incidence of encephalomeningitis worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meningitis - inflammation of the meninges (membranes covering the brain and spinal cord)

Etymology

From Greek *enkephalos* (brain), *mēninx* (membrane), and the suffix *-itis* (inflammation).
