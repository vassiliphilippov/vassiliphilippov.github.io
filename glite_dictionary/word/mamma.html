mamma

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːmə / (AmE), / ˈmæmə / (BrE)
(informal) mother; a child's word for their mother.
informal term for mother
informal

Examples

When I fell down, I ran crying to my mamma for comfort.
His mamma always kissed him goodnight before bed.
Little children often call their mother mamma or mama.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mom - informal American term for mother
  • Synonym: mum - informal British term for mother
  • Synonym: mother - a female parent

Etymology

From Italian and other Romance languages, related to 'mama', a child's word for mother.

2. NOUN

/ ˈmæmə / (AmE), / ˈmamə / (BrE)
the milk-secreting organ of female mammals; a mammary gland
milk-secreting gland in female mammals
medical

Examples

The scientist explained how the mamma functions in nursing young mammals.
In some mammals, each mamma produces milk independently.
The vet examined the cat’s mamma for signs of infection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breast - the chest or mammary gland of a woman
  • Synonym: mammary gland - the organ in female mammals that produces milk

Etymology

From Latin 'mamma' (breast, udder).
