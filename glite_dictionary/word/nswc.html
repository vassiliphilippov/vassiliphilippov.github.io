NSWC

1. NOUN

/ ɛn ɛs dʌbəlˈju si / (AmE), / ɛn ɛs dʌbəlˈjuː siː / (BrE)
abbreviation for Naval Surface Warfare Center, the agency that provides scientific, engineering, and technical support for surface warfare within the United States Navy.
Naval Surface Warfare Center (U.S. Navy agency)
american

Examples

After graduating, she accepted a research position with the NSWC in Maryland.
The NSWC recently developed a new radar system for Navy ships.
Members of the NSWC team presented their findings at the defense technologies conference.

Etymology

Acronym for Naval Surface Warfare Center.
