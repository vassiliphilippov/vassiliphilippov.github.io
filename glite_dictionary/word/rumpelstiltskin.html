Rumpelstiltskin

1. PROPN

/ ˌrʌmpəlˈstɪltskɪn / (AmE), / ˌrʌmpəlˈstɪltskɪn / (BrE)
a fictional dwarf in a fairy tale by the Brothers Grimm who spins straw into gold, demanding a woman's firstborn unless she can guess his name
fairy-tale dwarf in Grimm stories

Examples

The story of Rumpelstiltskin has fascinated children for generations with its magical elements and unique twist.
She felt like Rumpelstiltskin, keeping her own secret hidden from everyone at work.
In literature classes, we analyzed the character of Rumpelstiltskin as a symbol of cunning and deception.

Etymology

From German 'Rumpelstilzchen', meaning 'little rattle stilt', the name of the character in the Brothers Grimm fairy tale.
