spacecraft

1. NOUN

/ ˈspeɪsˌkræft / (AmE), / ˈspeɪsˌkrɑːft / (BrE)
A vehicle designed for travel or operation in outer space.
vehicle for travel in outer space

Examples

The spacecraft entered Mars’s orbit after a six-month journey.
Scientists are designing a new spacecraft to explore the outer planets.
The damaged spacecraft was safely guided back to Earth by the ground crew.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spaceship - a vehicle designed for travel in outer space

Etymology

Formed from 'space' + 'craft'.
