pyramidical

1. ADJ

/ pɪˈræmɪdɪkəl / (AmE), / pɪˈramɪdɪkəl / (BrE)
Having the shape or characteristics of a pyramid; resembling a pyramid.
resembling a pyramid in shape

Examples

The architect designed a pyramidical roof that dominated the skyline.
Several ancient structures in the desert have a pyramidical form.
A pyramidical arrangement of boxes makes the display more eye-catching.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pyramidal - having the shape of a pyramid

Etymology

From pyramid + -ical, after Latin 'pyramidalis'.
