expandible

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈspændəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪkˈspændɪbəl / (BrE)
able to be expanded or increased in size, especially referring to gases or materials.
capable of being expanded

Examples

Helium is an expandible gas under laboratory conditions.
The container was designed to hold expandible substances safely.
Any expandible material requires careful handling to prevent accidents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expandable - able to be expanded; often used interchangeably with 'expandible'

Etymology

Formed from 'expand' + '-ible', modeled after Latin -ibilis ('able to be') endings.

2. ADJ

/ ɪkˈspændəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪkˈspændɪbəl / (BrE)
Able to expand or be expanded; capable of enlargement or extension.
able to expand or be enlarged

Examples

The software includes an expandible menu that reveals more options when needed.
This suitcase is expandible, allowing travelers to pack extra items for their return trip.
The expandible platform can be adjusted to accommodate more users as the demand increases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expandable - able to expand or be expanded

Etymology

From Latin 'expandere' (to spread out) + English '-ible'.
