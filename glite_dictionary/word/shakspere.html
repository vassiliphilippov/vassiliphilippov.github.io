Shakspere

1. PROPN

/ ˈʃeɪkspɪr / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪkspɪə / (BrE)
an alternative spelling of Shakespeare, referring to William Shakespeare, the renowned English poet and playwright (1564–1616)
alternate spelling of Shakespeare

Examples

Many early documents record Shakspere as the spelling of the playwright's surname.
Some scholars debate why Shakspere himself used multiple spellings of his name.
The folio edition listed the author's name as Shakspere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Shakespeare - the most common spelling of the renowned English playwright and poet's name

Etymology

Variant spelling based on historical documents; original signatures by Shakespeare varied in form.
