cardcase

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːrdˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdˌkeɪs / (BrE)
a small, flat case for carrying business cards or similar cards
case for carrying business cards

Examples

He took a business card from his cardcase and handed it to her.
My new leather cardcase holds up to twenty cards.
She gave him an engraved silver cardcase as a graduation gift.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wallet - a small folding case for holding money, cards, etc.
