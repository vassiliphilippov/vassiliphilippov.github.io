Amedeo Modigliani

1. PROPN

/ əˌmeɪdioʊ ˌmoʊdiˈljɑːni / (AmE), / aˌmeːdɛoː ˌmɒdɪlˈjɑːni / (BrE)
(1884–1920) An Italian painter and sculptor known for his distinctive elongated portraits and nudes.
