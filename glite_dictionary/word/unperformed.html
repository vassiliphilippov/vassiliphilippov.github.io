unperformed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnpərˈfɔrmd / (AmE), / ˌʌnpəˈfɔːmd / (BrE)
not carried out, done, or performed; not put into action or practice
not performed or carried out

Examples

Several of his early works remain unperformed to this day.
The festival will feature readings of unperformed scripts by emerging playwrights.
Many unperformed pieces were discovered in the composer's archive.

Synset Examples

the author of numerous unperformed plays

Related Terms

  • Antonym: performed - carried out, done, or executed

Etymology

From the prefix un- (not) + performed, from Old French 'parfournir' (to finish, accomplish).
