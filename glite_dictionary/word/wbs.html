WbS

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʌbəl.juː biː ˈɛs / (AmE), / ˌdʌbəl.juː biː ˈɛs / (BrE)
the compass point that is one point south of due west, equal to 258.75° on a compass
compass point; west by south

Examples

The ship set its course to WbS to avoid the approaching storm.
On the chart, the bearing was close to WbS, just south of west.
He adjusted the compass until the needle pointed to WbS.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: west - the direction toward the point of the horizon where the sun sets
  • Degree Higher: west-southwest - the compass point between west and southwest

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'west by south', a traditional compass direction.
