Hispaniolan

1. ADJ

/ hɪˌspæniˈoʊlən / (AmE), / hɪˌspæniˈəʊlən / (BrE)
of or relating to the island of Hispaniola in the West Indies
relating to Hispaniola (Caribbean island)

Examples

The Hispaniolan solenodon is a unique mammal found only on the island.
She studied the Hispaniolan culture during her journey to the Caribbean.
Many Hispaniolan bird species are threatened by habitat loss.

Etymology

From Hispaniola, the name of the Caribbean island, itself from Spanish 'La Española'.
