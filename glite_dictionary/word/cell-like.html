cell-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛlˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɛl.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or similar to a cell in shape, structure, or function
resembling a cell

Examples

The scientist observed a cell-like structure under the microscope.
Some bacteria form cell-like compartments to organize their activities.
The artist created a sculpture with cell-like shapes and membranes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cellular - relating to cells; made up of cells
