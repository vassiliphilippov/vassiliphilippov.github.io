tictac

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪkˌtæk / (AmE), / ˈtɪkˌtæk / (BrE)
a steady, recurring ticking sound, such as that made by a clock
recurrent ticking sound

Examples

The tictac of the old grandfather clock echoed through the silent hallway.
She found the gentle tictac of the wall clock comforting at night.
As the storm raged outside, the only sound inside was the steady tictac from the mantelpiece clock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ticktock - repetitive ticking sound as of a clock

Etymology

Imitative of the sound produced by a clock or similar device.
