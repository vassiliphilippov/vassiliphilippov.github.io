Somalian

1. NOUN

/ soʊˈmɑːliən / (AmE), / səʊˈmɑːliən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Somalia, or a person of Somali descent
person from Somalia

Examples

After moving to Canada, the Somalian community established several cultural organizations.
He interviewed a Somalian about traditional music and customs.
Many Somalians living abroad send money back home to support their families.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Somali - a native or inhabitant of Somalia; the main ethnic group in Somalia

Etymology

From Somalia + -an (adjectival and demonymic suffix)

2. ADJ

/ soʊˈmɑːliən / (AmE), / səˈmɑːliən / (BrE)
of or relating to Somalia, its people, language, or culture.
of Somalia or its people

Examples

The Somalian community in Minneapolis is thriving and diverse.
Her research focuses on Somalian cuisine and traditional dishes.
He wore a beautiful Somalian shawl to the ceremony.

Synset Examples

Somali coffee is excellent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Somali - of or relating to Somalia, its people, or language

Etymology

From Somalia, the country in East Africa, plus the suffix -an forming adjectives and demonyms.
