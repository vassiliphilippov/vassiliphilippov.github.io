stateless

1. ADJ

/ ˈsteɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtləs / (BrE)
Not recognized as a citizen or national by any country; lacking official citizenship or nationality.
without nationality or citizenship

Examples

Many refugees are considered stateless when no country accepts them as nationals.
He faced challenges obtaining a passport because he was stateless.
International law seeks to protect the rights of stateless individuals.

Synset Examples

stateless persons

Related Terms

  • Synonym: citizenless - without citizens; lacking citizens

Etymology

Formed from 'state' + '-less', where '-less' denotes absence or lack of.
