GIA

1. NOUN

/ dʒiː aɪ ˈeɪ / (AmE), / dʒiː aɪ ˈeɪ / (BrE)
a militant Islamist group (Armed Islamic Group of Algeria) formed in 1992 that conducted violent attacks to overthrow Algeria's secular government and establish an Islamic state
Algerian Islamist militant group (1990s)

Examples

The GIA was responsible for a wave of attacks targeting civilians in Algeria.
Security forces intensified their operations against the GIA in the late 1990s.
Several members of the GIA were captured by Algerian authorities last year.

Synset Examples

the GIA has embarked on a terrorist campaign of civilian massacres

Etymology

Initialism of French Groupe Islamique Armé ('Armed Islamic Group').
