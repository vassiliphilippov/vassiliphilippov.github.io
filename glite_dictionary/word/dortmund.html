Dortmund

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɔrt.mʊnd / (AmE), / ˈdɔːt.mʊnd / (BrE)
An industrial city in northwestern Germany, historically important as a member of the Hanseatic League from the 13th to 17th century.
industrial city in northwestern Germany

Examples

Dortmund is renowned for its football team and its lively cultural scene.
During the Middle Ages, Dortmund was a prominent member of the Hanseatic League.
After the war, Dortmund rebuilt its industries and became a center for technology and innovation.
