July

1. NOUN

/ dʒuˈlaɪ / (AmE), / dʒuˈlaɪ / (BrE)
The seventh month of the year, following June and preceding August.
seventh month of the year

Examples

School is out in July, so many families go on vacation then.
Her birthday falls in July every year.
The weather is usually very hot during July in this region.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: June - the month preceding July
  • Degree Higher: August - the month following July

Etymology

From Latin 'Julius', named after Julius Caesar.
