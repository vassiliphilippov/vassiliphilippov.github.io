Nell Gwynne

1. PROPN

/ nɛl ˈɡwɪn / (AmE), / nɛl ˈɡwɪn / (BrE)
(1650–1687) An English actress and comedienne famed as a mistress of King Charles II.
English actress and mistress of Charles II

Examples

Nell Gwynne became one of the most celebrated actresses of Restoration England.
Many historical novels feature Nell Gwynne as a central character in the court of Charles II.
Paintings of Nell Gwynne can be found in several British museums.
