spirited

1. ADJ

/ ˈspɪr.ɪ.t̬ɪd / (AmE), / ˈspɪr.ɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
full of energy, enthusiasm, or determination; lively and animated.
full of energy and enthusiasm

Examples

The children performed a spirited dance at the school event.
Her spirited response lifted everyone's mood in the meeting.
The team put up a spirited fight despite losing the game.

Synset Examples

a bouncing gait
bouncy tunes
the peppy and interesting talk
a spirited dance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lively - full of life and energy
  • Synonym: animated - full of life or excitement
  • Antonym: listless - lacking energy or enthusiasm

Etymology

From spirit + -ed, meaning 'having the qualities associated with spirit.'
