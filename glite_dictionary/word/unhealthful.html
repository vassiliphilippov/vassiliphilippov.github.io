unhealthful

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhɛlθfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛlθfʊl / (BrE)
Not conducive to good health; likely to be harmful to health.
harmful to health; not sanitary

Examples

Poor ventilation made the room quite unhealthful for its occupants.
Consuming contaminated water is considered unhealthful and dangerous.
Doctors advised him to avoid unhealthful foods high in trans fats.

Synset Examples

unsanitary open sewers
grim and unsanitary conditions

Related Terms

  • Antonym: healthful - conducive to good health
  • Synonym: unhealthy - harmful or injurious to health

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'healthful'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02120729-a
Synset POS: a

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhɛlθfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛlθfʊl / (BrE)
Harmful to one's health; detrimental to physical well-being.
harmful to health

Examples

The doctor warned that a diet high in saturated fat can be unhealthful.
Workers protested the unhealthful conditions in the old factory building.
It is unhealthful to sleep in a room without proper ventilation.

Synset Examples

unhealthful air pollution
unhealthful conditions in old apartments with peeling lead-based paint

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unhealthy - not in good health; harmful to health

Etymology

Formed from "un-" (not) + "healthful".

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01171361-a
Synset POS: a

3. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhɛlθfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛlθfəl / (BrE)
Harmful to health; causing or likely to cause injury or disease.
harmful to health

Examples

Some experts warn that excessive sugar consumption can be unhealthful over time.
He moved away from the city’s unhealthful air for the sake of his lungs.
The doctor advised her to avoid unhealthful foods high in artificial additives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unhealthy - harmful to health
  • Antonym: healthful - promoting good health

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' to 'healthful.'

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02570304-s
Synset POS: s
