numerologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌnuːməˈrɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌnjuːməˈrɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A person who believes in or practices numerology, the study of the alleged mystical significance of numbers.
a person who practices numerology

Examples

The numerologist interpreted the numbers in my birth date to reveal my personality traits.
Some people consult a numerologist for advice on important life decisions.
Despite skepticism, the numerologist claimed her predictions came true for many clients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: numerology - the belief in the mystical significance of numbers

Etymology

From numerology + -ist, modeled on similar terms.
