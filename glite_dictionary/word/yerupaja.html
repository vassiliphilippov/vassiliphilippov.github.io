Yerupaja

1. PROPN

/ jɛˌruːpɑːˈhɑː / (AmE), / jɛˌruːpɑːˈhɑː / (BrE)
a mountain peak in the Andes of Peru, rising to 21,709 feet (6,617 meters) and noted as the second highest mountain in Peru
Andean mountain peak in Peru

Examples

The snow-capped summit of Yerupaja is visible from miles away in the Cordillera Huayhuash.
Climbers from around the world travel to Peru to attempt an ascent of Yerupaja.
Yerupaja is known for its steep slopes and challenging technical routes.
