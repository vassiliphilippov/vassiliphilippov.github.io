alated

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪ.leɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.leɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
(of seeds, insects, or leaves) having winglike extensions or appendages.
having winglike extensions (biology)

Examples

The alated seeds of the maple twirl as they drift through the air.
Many ants develop into alated forms during their reproductive phase.
The botanist noted the presence of alated leaves on several plant species.

Synset Examples

alate leaves
alate seeds of a maple tree

Related Terms

  • Synonym: winged - having wings or winglike parts
  • Antonym: apterous - lacking wings or winglike extensions

Etymology

From Latin 'alatus', past participle of 'ala' meaning 'wing'.
