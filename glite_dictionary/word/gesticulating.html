gesticulating

1. ADJ

/ dʒɛˈstɪkjəˌleɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / dʒɛˈstɪkjʊleɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
making gestures, especially while speaking, to express or emphasize something
making gestures while speaking

Examples

She spoke passionately, her gesticulating hands reflecting every emotion.
The gesticulating teacher captured the students' attention during the lively discussion.
His gesticulating arms made his excitement clear to everyone in the room.

Synset Examples

her gesticulating hands and arms made words almost unnecessary

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to gesticulate - to make expressive gestures while talking

Etymology

From Latin gesticulatus, past participle of gesticulari (to gesture).
