Catherine Howard

1. PROPN

/ ˈkæθərɪn ˈhaʊərd / (AmE), / ˈkæθərɪn ˈhaʊəd / (BrE)
Queen of England (c. 1520–1542), the fifth wife of Henry VIII, who was executed on charges of adultery.
fifth wife of Henry VIII, executed 1542

Examples

Catherine Howard was only a teenager when she married Henry VIII.
Many historians debate how much Catherine Howard understood about the allegations against her.
The tragic fate of Catherine Howard has inspired numerous books and films.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Anne Boleyn - second wife of Henry VIII, also executed
  • Synonym: Henry VIII - King of England, husband of Catherine Howard
