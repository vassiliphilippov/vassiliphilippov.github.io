zayin

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɑːjɪn / (AmE), / ˈzeɪjɪn / (BrE)
the seventh letter of the Hebrew alphabet.
seventh letter of Hebrew alphabet

Examples

The word starts with a zayin, which resembles the shape of a sword.
Students are learning to write the zayin in their Hebrew class.
In the Torah scroll, every zayin is written with a special flourish.

Etymology

From Hebrew זַיִן (záyin), meaning 'weapon', name of the seventh letter of the Hebrew alphabet.
