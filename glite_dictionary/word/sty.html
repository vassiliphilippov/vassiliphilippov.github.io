sty

1. NOUN

/ staɪ / (AmE), / staɪ / (BrE)
an infection of the sebaceous gland at the edge of the eyelid, causing a red, painful swelling
eyelid infection; red swelling
medical

Examples

Sarah woke up with a painful sty on her upper eyelid.
If you frequently get a sty, it's important to keep your eyes clean.
A sty usually heals on its own within a week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hordeolum - medical term for a sty

Etymology

From Old English stigend (rising, tumor).

2. NOUN

/ staɪ / (AmE), / staɪ / (BrE)
a small enclosure or pen where pigs are kept
pen or enclosure for pigs

Examples

The old farm had a wooden sty behind the house.
Every morning, the farmer cleaned the sty before feeding the pigs.
Mud covered the floor of the sty after the heavy rain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pigsty - enclosure for pigs; a dirty place
  • Degree Higher: barn - large farm building for storing grain or housing livestock

Etymology

Old English *stig* (hall, sty); related to Old Norse *stī* (path).
