uncurbed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkɝbd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɜːbd / (BrE)
not restrained or controlled; lacking limits or checks
not restrained or controlled

Examples

After months of uncurbed spending, the company finally faced a financial crisis.
His uncurbed enthusiasm sometimes made him seem reckless to others.
The uncurbed growth of the weed threatened to overwhelm the entire garden.

Synset Examples

unbridled rage
an unchecked temper
ungoverned rage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unrestrained - not controlled or restricted
  • Synonym: unchecked - not held in check; not controlled
  • Antonym: curbed - restrained; kept in check

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'curbed' (from 'curb': to restrain).
