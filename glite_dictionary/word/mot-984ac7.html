mot

1. NOUN

/ moʊ / (AmE), / məʊ / (BrE)
a witty or clever remark; a witty saying or bon mot
a clever or witty remark

Examples

His after-dinner mot caused the whole table to erupt in laughter.
The book is filled with memorable mots and observations.
She was always ready with a clever mot at any gathering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: witticism - a witty remark
  • Synonym: quip - a clever or witty remark

Etymology

From French mot (word, saying).
