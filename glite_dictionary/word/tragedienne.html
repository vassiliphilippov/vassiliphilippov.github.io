tragedienne

1. NOUN

/ trædʒəˈdiːˌɛn / (AmE), / trædʒ.əˈdiː.ɛn / (BrE)
an actress who specializes in performing tragic roles, especially in plays or theatre
actress specializing in tragic roles

Examples

The famous tragedienne captivated the audience with her intense performance.
She was celebrated as a great tragedienne of her generation, known for her roles in Shakespearean plays.
A talented tragedienne can evoke deep emotions through the portrayal of sorrowful characters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tragedian - an actor who specializes in tragic roles, generally used for any gender

Etymology

From French 'tragédienne', feminine of 'tragédien' (tragedian).
