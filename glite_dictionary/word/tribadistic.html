tribadistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌtrɪbəˈdɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌtrɪbəˈdɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to female homosexual activity that imitates or simulates heterosexual intercourse, typically involving genital-to-genital contact.
of lesbian behavior simulating heterosexual sex

Examples

The film included a scene portraying tribadistic intimacy between the two leading women.
Her research paper discusses the historical perception of tribadistic practices in different cultures.
Some texts use tribadistic to describe particular positions in lesbian sexuality.

Etymology

From Greek 'tribas', meaning 'woman who simulates sex with a woman', from 'tribein' (to rub).
