pimply

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɪmpli / (AmE), / ˈpɪmpli / (BrE)
having many small, raised spots or blemishes on the skin, especially on the face.
having many pimples on the skin

Examples

During his teenage years, Tom felt self-conscious about his pimply complexion.
The actor played a pimply teenager in the coming-of-age film.
After using the new medication, her pimply skin started to clear up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blemished - having imperfections, especially on the skin
  • Antonym: pimple-free - without any pimples or skin blemishes
