frumpishly

1. ADV

/ ˈfrʌmpɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈfrʌmpɪʃli / (BrE)
in a manner that is dowdy, unfashionable, or lacking in style
in a dowdy or unfashionable manner

Examples

She walked into the party frumpishly dressed in outdated clothes.
Though her sister dressed stylishly, Marianne preferred to dress frumpishly.
The critic claimed the play’s costumes were frumpishly old-fashioned.

Synset Examples

she dresses dowdily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dowdily - in a dowdy or unfashionable manner

Etymology

From 'frumpish' (like a frump: dowdy or unfashionable) + -ly.
