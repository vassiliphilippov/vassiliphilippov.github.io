C1

1. NOUN

/ si wʌn / (AmE), / siː wʌn / (BrE)
An international standard envelope size measuring 648 x 917 millimeters.
envelope size, 648 x 917 mm

Examples

The C1 envelope is large enough to fit an A1 sheet of paper without folding.
For the exhibition poster, we used a C1 envelope to mail it safely.
She ordered a pack of C1 envelopes for her design studio's oversized prints.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: A4 - standard paper size, 210 x 297 mm

Etymology

Part of the ISO 269 international standard for envelope sizes.
