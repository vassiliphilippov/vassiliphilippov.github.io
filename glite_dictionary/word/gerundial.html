gerundial

1. ADJ

/ dʒəˈrʌndiəl / (AmE), / dʒəˈrʌndɪəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of a gerund.
pertaining to a gerund

Examples

In English, '-ing' is a common gerundial suffix.
The phrase 'swimming is fun' contains a gerundial construction.
Her dissertation focused on gerundial forms in different languages.

Synset Examples

the gerundial suffix ‘-ing’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gerund - a noun formed from a verb, e.g. 'running' in 'Running is fun.'

Etymology

From 'gerund' + '-ial', after Latin 'gerundium'.
