unpretentiousness

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.prɪˈtɛn.ʃəs.nəs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prɪˈtɛn.ʃəs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being natural, simple, and without pretensions or affectation
lack of pretension; simplicity

Examples

Her unpretentiousness made everyone around her feel comfortable and relaxed.
The unpretentiousness of the restaurant's décor matched its simple, delicious food.
People admired his unpretentiousness, as he always treated everyone equally.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: modesty - the quality of being modest and unassuming
  • Synonym: humility - the quality of having a humble view of oneself
  • Antonym: pretentiousness - the quality of being pretentious or showy

Etymology

From unpretentious + -ness; 'unpretentious' from un- (not) + pretentious.
