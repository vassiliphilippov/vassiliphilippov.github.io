Queensland

1. PROPN

/ ˈkwiːnzˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈkwiːnz.lənd / (BrE)
a state in the northeastern part of Australia, known for its tropical climate, coastline, and major cities like Brisbane
state in northeastern Australia

Examples

Many tourists visit Queensland to see the Great Barrier Reef each year.
The climate in Queensland is generally warm and sunny.
She moved to Queensland after graduating from university.

Etymology

Named after Queen Victoria of the United Kingdom.
