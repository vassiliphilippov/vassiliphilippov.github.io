cords

1. NOUN

/ kɔrdz / (AmE), / kɔːdz / (BrE)
Trousers made of corduroy fabric, typically cotton and characterized by raised ribs.
corduroy trousers

Examples

He wore baggy cords and a checked shirt to the meeting.
My old green cords are the most comfortable trousers I own.
During autumn, I usually switch from jeans to cords for extra warmth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corduroys - Trousers made from corduroy fabric

Etymology

Shortened from 'corduroys', from 'corduroy' (the ribbed fabric) and pluralized to refer to trousers made from it.
