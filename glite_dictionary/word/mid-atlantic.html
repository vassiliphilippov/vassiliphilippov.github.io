mid-Atlantic

1. ADJ

/ mɪd ətˈlæntɪk / (AmE), / mɪd ətˈlæntɪk / (BrE)
relating to the region of the United States that includes (variously) New York, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Delaware, Maryland, and Virginia
of the US region from New York to Virginia

Examples

She grew up in the mid-Atlantic region and is familiar with its climate.
The mid-Atlantic states have a rich history dating back to colonial times.
Many people travel to the mid-Atlantic for its beautiful beaches and vibrant cities.

Synset Examples

mid-Atlantic states

  • Degree Lower: Southeast - region of the US including states further south and east
