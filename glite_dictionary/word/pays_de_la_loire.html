Pays de la Loire

1. PROPN

/ peɪ də lɑː ˈlwɑːr / (AmE), / peɪ də lɑː ˈlwaː / (BrE)
a region in western France bordering the Bay of Biscay, known for its agriculture and historical cities
region in western France

Examples

They spent their summer holiday exploring castles in Pays de la Loire.
The vineyards of Pays de la Loire produce some acclaimed French wines.
Many traditional festivals are held annually throughout Pays de la Loire.

Etymology

French, literally 'lands of the Loire', from the Loire River crossing the region.
