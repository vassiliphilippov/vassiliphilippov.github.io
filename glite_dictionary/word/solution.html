solution

1. NOUN

/ səˈluːʃən / (AmE), / səˈluːʃən / (BrE)
a way of solving a problem or dealing with a difficult situation; an answer to a question or puzzle.
answer to a problem or puzzle

Examples

After hours of discussion, they finally reached a solution to the issue.
The teacher asked the students to write down the solution to each math problem.
Scientists are constantly searching for a solution to climate change.

Synset Examples

they were trying to find a peaceful solution
the answers were in the back of the book
he computed the result to four decimal places

Related Terms

  • Synonym: answer - a solution to a question or problem
  • Antonym: problem - something that needs to be solved; the opposite of a solution

Etymology

From Latin 'solutio', from 'solvere' (to loosen, solve).
