pint-sized

1. ADJ

/ paɪnt saɪzd / (AmE), / paɪnt saɪzd / (BrE)
significantly smaller or shorter than average; very small
very small; below average in size

Examples

Although small, the pint-sized puppy had a big personality.
She was a pint-sized champion on the gymnastics team.
He laughed at the pint-sized superhero costume his child wore for Halloween.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiny - extremely small
  • Synonym: petite - small and slender (typically for women or girls)

Etymology

From 'pint', a unit of liquid measure, and 'sized', meaning 'of a particular size'; figuratively referring to something as small as a pint.
