contentedly

1. ADV

/ kənˈtɛntɪdli / (AmE), / kənˈtɛntɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows quiet happiness or satisfaction
in a satisfied or happy manner

Examples

The cat purred contentedly beside the fireplace all evening.
She smiled contentedly as she listened to her favorite music.
He sat contentedly in the garden, enjoying the peaceful afternoon.

Synset Examples

‘I bought it,’ she said contentedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: happily - in a happy or cheerful way
  • Antonym: discontentedly - in a dissatisfied or unhappy way

Etymology

From 'contented' + '-ly' adverbial suffix.
