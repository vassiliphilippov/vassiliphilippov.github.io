well-nigh

1. ADV

/ wɛlˈnaɪ / (AmE), / wɛlˈnaɪ / (BrE)
Almost; nearly but not completely.
almost; nearly

Examples

After months of hard work, the task was well-nigh complete.
His explanation left me well-nigh speechless with amazement.
It is well-nigh impossible to predict the outcome with certainty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: almost - nearly but not completely
  • Synonym: nearly - not quite; almost
  • Synonym: virtually - almost; practically

Etymology

Formed by combining 'well' (adverb) and archaic 'nigh' (near, close).
