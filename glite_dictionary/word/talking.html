talking

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɔːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of exchanging ideas or information through spoken conversation
exchange of ideas by conversation

Examples

There was a lot of talking in the hallway after the meeting ended.
Serious talking is needed before we make any important decisions.
They were tired of endless talking with no action.

Synset Examples

let's have more work and less talk around here

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conversation - a spoken exchange of thoughts, opinions, or feelings
  • Synonym: discussion - the act of talking about something in order to reach a decision or to exchange ideas
  • Antonym: silence - the absence of sound or spoken words

Etymology

From the verb 'talk' (Middle English: talken), with '-ing' suffix.
