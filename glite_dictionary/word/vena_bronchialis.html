vena bronchialis

1. NOUN

/ ˈveɪ.nə brɒŋˈkaɪ.əl.ɪs / (AmE), / ˈviː.nə brɒŋˈkaɪ.ə.lɪs / (BrE)
a vein that drains blood from the bronchi and empties into the azygos vein
vein draining the bronchi
medical

Examples

The vena bronchialis carries deoxygenated blood from the bronchi to the azygos vein.
During the dissection, the surgeon carefully identified the vena bronchialis.
Obstruction of the vena bronchialis can result in impaired bronchial circulation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bronchial vein - vein that returns blood from the bronchi
  • Degree Higher: azygos vein - a major vein of the thorax into which the vena bronchialis drains

Etymology

From Latin: 'vena' (vein) and 'bronchialis' (of the bronchi).
