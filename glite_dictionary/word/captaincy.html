captaincy

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæp.tɪn.si / (AmE), / ˈkap.tɪn.si / (BrE)
the position or rank of being a captain, especially of a sports team or ship.
position of being a captain

Examples

She was awarded the captaincy after leading her team to several victories.
His captaincy brought new discipline and energy to the basketball squad.
After many years of dedication, he finally earned the captaincy of the ship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: captain - the person in command of a ship, aircraft, or sports team

Etymology

From "captain" + "-cy", forming a noun denoting office or rank.
