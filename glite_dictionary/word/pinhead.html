pinhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪnˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌhɛd / (BrE)
the small, rounded end of a pin
the rounded end of a pin

Examples

The pinhead is so tiny that you can barely see it.
He pressed the pinhead into the fabric to keep it in place.
A droplet of water balanced perfectly on the pinhead.

2. NOUN

/ ˈpɪnˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌhɛd / (BrE)
An ignorant or foolish person.
foolish or ignorant person
informal

Examples

Don't be such a pinhead—read the instructions before you try to fix it.
He felt like a complete pinhead after forgetting his own anniversary.
Sometimes my brother acts like a real pinhead when he's tired.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very foolish or stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - an exceptionally intelligent person

Etymology

Formed from 'pin' and 'head', originally referring to the head of a pin (very small), later used figuratively for a small-minded person.
