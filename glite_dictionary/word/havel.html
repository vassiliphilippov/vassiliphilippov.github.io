Havel

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɑːvɛl / (AmE), / ˈhævəl / (BrE)
Václav Havel (1936–2011), Czech playwright, dissident, and statesman, who served as the last president of Czechoslovakia and the first president of the Czech Republic.
