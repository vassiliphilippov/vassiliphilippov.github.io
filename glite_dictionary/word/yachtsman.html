yachtsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɑtsmən / (AmE), / ˈjɒtsmən / (BrE)
a man who owns or sails a yacht, especially as a hobby or sport
man who sails or owns a yacht

Examples

The experienced yachtsman navigated around the island with ease despite strong winds.
As a seasoned yachtsman, he has competed in several international regattas.
The club is open to any yachtsman interested in participating in weekend races.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: yachtswoman - a woman who owns or sails a yacht
  • Synonym: sailor - a person who works on or sails a boat or ship

Etymology

From 'yacht' (originally from Dutch 'jacht' meaning 'hunt' or 'swift light vessel') + 'man'.
