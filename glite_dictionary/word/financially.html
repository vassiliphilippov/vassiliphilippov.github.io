financially

1. ADV

/ fəˈnænʃəli / (AmE), / faɪˈnænʃəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to money, finance, or the management of funds
relating to finance or money

Examples

The company is financially stable despite the recent market downturn.
He decided that moving abroad would not be financially practical at this time.
They are struggling financially after the unexpected medical expenses.

Synset Examples

this was financially unattractive

Related Terms

  • Synonym: economically - in a way that relates to the economy or economics

Etymology

From 'financial' (from French 'financier', relating to finance) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
