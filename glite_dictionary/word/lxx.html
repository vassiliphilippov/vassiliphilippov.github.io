LXX

1. NOUN

/ ɛl ɛks ɛks / (AmE), / ɛl ɛks ɛks / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for the number seventy (70)
Roman numeral for 70

Examples

The manuscript was dated to LXX AD.
He wrote the number seventy in Roman numerals as LXX.
The pages were numbered using LXX at the start of each chapter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seventy - the cardinal number 70

Etymology

Roman numeral, from Latin numerals L (50) and X (10), with L + X + X meaning 70.
