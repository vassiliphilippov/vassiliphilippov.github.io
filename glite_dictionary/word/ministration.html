ministration

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪnəˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of giving help or care, especially in a time of need or difficulty
the act of giving aid or care

Examples

During her illness, she was comforted by the ministration of her devoted friends.
The relief team worked tirelessly to provide ministration to the storm's survivors.
He recovered quickly under the gentle ministration of the hospital staff.

Synset Examples

the contributions provided some relief for the victims

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assistance - the act of helping or supporting someone
  • Synonym: aid - help, typically of a practical nature

Etymology

From Latin ministratio (service, attendance), from ministrare (to serve).
