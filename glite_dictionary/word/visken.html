Visken

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪskən / (AmE), / ˈvɪskən / (BrE)
A trademarked name for an oral beta blocker (pindolol) used to treat high blood pressure (hypertension).
trade name for a blood pressure medication
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Visken to help lower his blood pressure.
Patients taking Visken should follow their physician's instructions carefully.
It is important to discuss any side effects of Visken with your healthcare provider.

Related Terms
