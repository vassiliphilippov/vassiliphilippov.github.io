Johannesburg

1. PROPN

/ dʒoʊˈhænɪsbɜːrɡ / (AmE), / dʒəˈhænɪzbɜːɡ / (BrE)
A major city in northeastern South Africa, known as a commercial and industrial center, especially for the diamond and gold industries.
city in northeastern South Africa

Examples

She moved to Johannesburg to pursue her career in finance.
Gold mining has played a significant role in the economy of Johannesburg.
Many international flights to South Africa land at Johannesburg's O.R. Tambo International Airport.

Etymology

Named after Johann Rissik and Christiaan Johannes Joubert, officials involved in the city's founding.
