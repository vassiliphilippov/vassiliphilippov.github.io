Louise Nevelson

1. PROPN

/ luˈis ˈnɛvəlˌsən / (AmE), / luˈiːz ˈnɛvəlsən / (BrE)
(1899–1988) an American sculptor, born in Russia, known for her large-scale wooden assemblages and monochromatic painted creations.
American sculptor known for wooden assemblages

Examples

A retrospective of Louise Nevelson's art attracted record crowds at the museum.
Many students study the innovative sculptures of Louise Nevelson in art history classes.
Critics praised Louise Nevelson for her unique use of assembled, painted wood in her installations.
