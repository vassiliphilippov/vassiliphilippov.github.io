unwittingly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈwɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is done without being aware or without intending to.
without being aware or intending to

Examples

She unwittingly offended her host with a careless comment.
The agent unwittingly revealed sensitive information during the interview.
He unwittingly walked into the middle of a heated argument.

Synset Examples

he unwittingly deleted the references

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unknowingly - without realizing or being aware
  • Antonym: deliberately - in a way that is intended or planned

Etymology

Formed from 'unwitting' (not knowing, unaware) + '-ly'.
