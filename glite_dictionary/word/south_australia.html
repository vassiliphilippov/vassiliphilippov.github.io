South Australia

1. PROPN

/ saʊθ əˈstreɪliə / (AmE), / saʊθ əˈstreɪliə / (BrE)
a state located in the south-central part of Australia, known for its capital Adelaide and wine regions
state in south-central Australia

Examples

The vineyards of South Australia produce some of the country's finest wines.
Tourists often visit South Australia for its stunning coastline and wildlife.
