vesiculitis

1. NOUN

/ vəˌsɪkjəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / vɪˌsɪkjʊˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of a seminal vesicle, often occurring together with prostatitis
inflammation of a seminal vesicle
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with vesiculitis after experiencing persistent pelvic pain.
Chronic vesiculitis can contribute to male infertility if left untreated.
Antibiotic therapy was prescribed to treat his acute vesiculitis.

Etymology

From Latin 'vesicula' (small bladder) + '-itis' (inflammation).
