bushperson

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊʃˌpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈbʊʃˌpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who lives in the Australian bush, especially someone familiar with or adapted to remote country life
Australian bush dweller

Examples

The remote region is home to many bushpeople who value independence and self-reliance.
As a child, she grew up as a bushperson, learning to survive in the wild Australian landscape.
Bushpeople are often skilled in tracking and recognizing local plants and wildlife.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bushman - a person who lives in the bush, especially in Australia or southern Africa

Etymology

From 'bush' (Australian outback or rural area) + 'person'.
