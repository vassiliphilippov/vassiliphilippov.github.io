rod-shaped

1. ADJ

/ rɑd ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / rɒd ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the form of a straight, narrow cylinder; shaped like a rod.
having a cylindrical, rodlike shape

Examples

Many bacteria are classified as rod-shaped due to their elongated structure.
The scientist observed several rod-shaped organisms under the microscope.
Iron particles that are rod-shaped tend to align themselves with magnetic fields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bacillus - a rod-shaped bacterium
  • Antonym: spherical - having the shape of a sphere
