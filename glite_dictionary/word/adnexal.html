adnexal

1. ADJ

/ ædˈnɛksəl / (AmE), / ædˈnɛksəl / (BrE)
relating to or associated with adnexa, especially accessory or adjoining anatomical structures such as glands or tissues.
relating to anatomical adnexa
medical

Examples

The ultrasound revealed an adnexal mass that required further investigation.
Adnexal involvement can complicate the diagnosis of pelvic infections.
Doctors evaluated the patient's adnexal structures during the gynecological exam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adnexa - accessory or adjoining anatomical structures

Etymology

From Latin 'adnexus', past participle of 'adnectere' (to bind to).
