postmillennial

1. ADJ

/ ˌpoʊst.mɪˈlɛn.i.əl / (AmE), / ˌpəʊst.mɪˈlɛn.i.əl / (BrE)
Relating to the period following the millennium, especially in historical or religious contexts.
after the millennium period

Examples

Many scholars debate the significance of postmillennial events in Christian theology.
The postmillennial era saw significant technological and social advancements.
Her research focuses on postmillennial art and its global influences.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: premillennial - relating to the period before the millennium
  • Synonym: millennial - relating to a millennium or a period of a thousand years

Etymology

From prefix 'post-' (after) + 'millennial' (relating to a millennium).
