twenty-fourth

1. ADJ

/ ˌtwɛnti ˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / ˌtwenti ˈfɔːθ / (BrE)
coming next after the twenty-third in a sequence; the ordinal number corresponding to twenty-four
ordinal number for position 24th

Examples

This is the twenty-fourth time we've held the event.
She finished in twenty-fourth place in the race.
The twenty-fourth chapter contains the book's conclusion.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-third - ordinal number for position 23rd
  • Degree Higher: twenty-fifth - ordinal number for position 25th
