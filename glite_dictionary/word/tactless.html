tactless

1. ADJ

/ ˈtæktləs / (AmE), / ˈtaktləs / (BrE)
Showing a lack of sensitivity, care, or social awareness in dealing with others or delicate situations.
lacking sensitivity or social awareness

Examples

His tactless comment made everyone at the table uncomfortable.
It was tactless of her to ask about his recent divorce.
The manager’s tactless handling of the complaint upset the entire team.

Synset Examples

an inept remark
it was tactless to bring up those disagreeable

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tactful - showing sensitivity and skill in dealing with others
  • Synonym: insensitive - showing or feeling no concern for others' feelings

Etymology

From 'tact' (sensitivity in dealing with others) + '-less' (without).

2. ADJ

/ ˈtæktləs / (AmE), / ˈtæktləs / (BrE)
having or showing a lack of sensitivity or consideration in dealing with others; insensitive in speech or behavior
insensitive; lacking consideration

Examples

It was tactless of him to mention her recent breakup in front of everyone.
She made a tactless remark about his weight without realizing it would hurt his feelings.
His tactless handling of the issue offended several members of the team.

Synset Examples

in the circumstances it was tactless to ask her age

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tactful - showing skill and sensitivity in dealing with others or with difficult issues

Etymology

From 'tact' (sensitivity in dealing with others) + '-less' (without).
