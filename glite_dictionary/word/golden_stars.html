golden stars

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən stɑrz / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən stɑːz / (BrE)
A California plant with grasslike leaves and showy orange flowers, scientifically known as Bloomeria crocea.
California plant with orange flowers

Examples

Each spring, the hills were dotted with golden stars in full bloom.
The golden stars added a vibrant splash of color to the garden.
Hikers spotted clusters of golden stars growing along the California trail.
