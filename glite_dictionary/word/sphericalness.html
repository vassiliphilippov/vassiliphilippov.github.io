sphericalness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsfɪr.ɪ.kəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsfɪə.rɪ.kəl.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being spherical; the property of having the shape of a sphere.
quality of being spherical

Examples

The sphericalness of the planet was confirmed by satellite images.
Engineers tested the sphericalness of each ball bearing to ensure smooth operation.
Artists admired the perfect sphericalness of the crystal globe on display.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roundness - the quality of being round or circular in shape

Etymology

From sphere + -ical + -ness, meaning the state of being spherical.
