Linus Pauling

1. PROPN

/ ˈlaɪnəs ˈpɔːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪnəs ˈpɔːlɪŋ / (BrE)
(1901–1994) United States chemist renowned for his work on the nature of chemical bonding and a recipient of two Nobel Prizes.
American chemist and Nobel laureate

Examples

Linus Pauling made groundbreaking discoveries in the field of chemistry.
Many students learn about Linus Pauling's research on the nature of the chemical bond.
Linus Pauling is one of the few people to have received two separate Nobel Prizes.
