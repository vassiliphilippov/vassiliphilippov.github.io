disambiguator

1. NOUN

/ dɪs.æmˈbɪɡ.juˌeɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / dɪs.æmˈbɪɡ.juˌeɪ.tə / (BrE)
(computing) a software tool or program designed to determine the intended meaning of a word or phrase from its linguistic context.
software that resolves word meaning in context

Examples

The new disambiguator improved the search engine’s ability to interpret user queries correctly.
Many language translation systems rely on a disambiguator to ensure accurate translations.
Our team developed a disambiguator that can resolve multiple meanings of technical terms in scientific papers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disambiguation - the process of resolving ambiguity in meaning

Etymology

From 'disambiguate' (to remove ambiguity) + '-or' (agent noun suffix).
