Niamey

1. PROPN

/ niˈɑːmeɪ / (AmE), / niˈɑːmeɪ / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Niger, located in the southwest of the country on the Niger River.
capital city of Niger

Examples

She traveled to Niamey for a conference on development in West Africa.
The climate in Niamey is characterized by hot, dry weather for much of the year.
Niamey is situated on the banks of the Niger River, making it a vital hub for trade.
