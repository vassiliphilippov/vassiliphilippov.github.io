telepathic

1. ADJ

/ ˌtɛləˈpæθɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈpæθɪk / (BrE)
able to communicate thoughts or feelings directly from one mind to another without using the known senses.
relating to mental communication

Examples

The twins claim to have a telepathic connection and often finish each other's sentences.
Some people believe that animals possess telepathic abilities to sense danger or emotion.
The novel explores the consequences of telepathic communication among an advanced alien species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychic - having abilities beyond normal sensory perception
  • Degree Lower: nonverbal - not involving or using words or speech

Etymology

From Greek tele- (far) + pathos (feeling).
