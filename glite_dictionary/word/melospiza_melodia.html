Melospiza melodia

1. NOUN

/ məˌlɑspɪzə məˈloʊdiə / (AmE), / mɛˌlɒspɪzə mɛˈləʊdiə / (BrE)
the scientific name for the song sparrow, a small songbird commonly found in North America
scientific name for song sparrow

Examples

The field guide listed Melospiza melodia as one of the region's most common songbirds.
Researchers observed the nest-building behavior of Melospiza melodia during the spring season.
Birdwatchers often recognize Melospiza melodia by its distinctive and melodious song.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: song sparrow - a species of North American sparrow known for its melodious song

Etymology

From Neo-Latin: 'melospiza' (from Greek 'melos', song + 'spiza', finch), 'melodia' meaning melody or song.
