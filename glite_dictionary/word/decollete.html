decollete

1. ADJ

/ deɪˈkɔləˌteɪ / (AmE), / ˌdeɪ.kɒl.əˈteɪ / (BrE)
(of clothing) having a low neckline that exposes the neck and the upper part of the chest.
low-cut neckline (of clothing)

Examples

She wore a stunning decollete dress to the evening gala.
The actress appeared on the red carpet in a daringly decollete gown.
Fashion magazines often feature decollete styles in their spring collections.

Synset Examples

a low-cut neckline

Related Terms

  • Synonym: low-cut - (of clothing) having a neckline that exposes the chest or shoulders

Etymology

From French décolleté, past participle of décolleter (to bare the neck and shoulders).
