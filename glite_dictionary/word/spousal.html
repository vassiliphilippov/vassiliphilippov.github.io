spousal

1. ADJ

/ ˈspaʊzəl / (AmE), / ˈspaʊz(ə)l / (BrE)
relating to a spouse or marriage.
relating to a spouse or marriage

Examples

They attended a workshop focused on improving spousal communication.
The court considered his spousal rights during the divorce proceedings.
Their anniversary ritual is a cherished spousal tradition.

Synset Examples

a fitting symbol of spousal love

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marital - relating to marriage or the relationship between married people
  • Synonym: conjugal - relating to marriage or the relationship between spouses
  • Antonym: spouseless - without a spouse; unmarried

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'sponsalis' (of or pertaining to a spouse), from Latin 'sponsa' (bride).

relating to marriage or a spouse
