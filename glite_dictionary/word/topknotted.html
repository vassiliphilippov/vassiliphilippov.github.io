topknotted

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɑpˌnɑtɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɒpˌnɒtɪd / (BrE)
(of an animal or bird) having a tuft or ornamental bunch of feathers or hair on the top of the head.
having a crest or tuft on the head

Examples

The topknotted pigeon is easily recognized by the prominent plume on its head.
Some species of antelope are topknotted, with tufts of hair topping their foreheads.
He sketched a topknotted parrot with vivid colors in his nature journal.

Synset Examples

golden crested
crested iris
crested oriole
tufted duck
tufted loosestrife

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crested - having a tuft, plume, or similar ornament on the head
  • Synonym: tufted - having a bunch or cluster of feathers, hair, or similar items
