large-hearted

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɑrdʒˌhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɑːdʒˌhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
generous, kind, and showing sympathy and understanding toward others
generous and sympathetic

Examples

The large-hearted woman welcomed every guest with warmth and understanding.
His large-hearted actions inspired others to help those less fortunate.
We need more large-hearted leaders willing to listen and offer support.

Synset Examples

was charitable in his opinions of others
kindly criticism
a kindly act
sympathetic words
a large-hearted mentor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kind-hearted - having a kind and sympathetic nature
  • Synonym: generous - willing to give or share freely
  • Antonym: mean-spirited - not generous or kind; spiteful

Etymology

Formed from 'large' + 'hearted', first recorded in the mid-19th century.
