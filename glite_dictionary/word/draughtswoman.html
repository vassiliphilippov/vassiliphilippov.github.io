draughtswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈdræftsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːftsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female skilled worker whose job is to draw detailed plans of buildings, machines, or other structures
female technical drawing specialist

Examples

As a talented draughtswoman, she was responsible for the detailed blueprints of the new skyscraper.
The company is seeking a skilled draughtswoman to join its engineering team.
Throughout her career as a draughtswoman, she has contributed to many award-winning architectural projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: draughtsman - a person skilled at drawing plans of buildings or machines
  • Synonym: draftswoman - chiefly US spelling of draughtswoman

Etymology

From 'draughtsman' (“person who draws designs”), with 'woman' as the feminine equivalent.
