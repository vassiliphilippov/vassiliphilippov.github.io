boughten

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɔtən / (AmE), / ˈbɔːtn / (BrE)
(US, regional or archaic) (of goods) purchased rather than homemade; store-bought.
purchased; not homemade
archaic american

Examples

She proudly wore her boughten dress to the party, even though it wasn't homemade.
In his family, biscuits were always boughten, never made from scratch.
Some people prefer handmade gifts, but I don't mind receiving boughten ones.

Synset Examples

my boughten clothes
a store-bought dress

Related Terms

  • Synonym: store-bought - purchased from a store; not homemade

Etymology

Formed from 'bought' (past participle of 'buy'), with the adjectival ending '-en', patterned after words like 'wooden'.
