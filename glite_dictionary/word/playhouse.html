playhouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a small model of a house, usually for children to play inside
children's toy house to play in

Examples

The children spent hours in their new playhouse in the backyard.
We built a wooden playhouse together last summer.
Her favorite birthday gift was a pink plastic playhouse.

Etymology

From 'play' + 'house', indicating a house for play.
