cooccurring

1. ADJ

/ ˌkoʊ.əˈkɜr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.əˈkɜː.rɪŋ / (BrE)
happening or existing at the same time as something else, especially by chance or in parallel
happening at the same time

Examples

The researchers investigated several cooccurring symptoms in the patient population.
Climate change and habitat loss are cooccurring threats to biodiversity.
Two cooccurring trends are reshaping the job market: automation and remote work.

Synset Examples

a series of coincident events

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simultaneous - happening or existing at the same time
  • Synonym: synchronous - occurring at the same time or rate
  • Antonym: independent - not occurring at the same time or unrelated

Etymology

From co- (together) + occur, based on Latin 'occurrere' (to run to meet, occur).
