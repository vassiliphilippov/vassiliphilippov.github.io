naturopathy

1. NOUN

/ ˌnætʃəˈrɑːpəθi / (AmE), / ˌnatjʊˈrɒpəθi / (BrE)
a system of alternative medicine that treats disease by emphasizing natural remedies, such as diet, exercise, and heat, to support the body's healing processes
system using natural remedies to treat disease

Examples

She decided to try naturopathy after conventional treatments failed to improve her condition.
Many people believe that naturopathy encourages the body to heal itself naturally.
Courses in naturopathy are offered at several alternative medicine colleges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homeopathy - a system of alternative medicine using highly diluted substances

Etymology

From Latin 'natura' (nature) and Greek 'pathos' (disease, suffering).
