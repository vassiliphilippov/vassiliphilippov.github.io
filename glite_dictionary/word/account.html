account

1. NOUN

/ əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
a detailed statement of money owed for goods supplied or services provided, often showing individual items and amounts
itemized statement of money owed

Examples

The account listed every item he had purchased over the month.
She settled the account with the supplier by bank transfer.
The company sent an account to the client for payment at the end of the project.

Synset Examples

send me an account of what I owe

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bill - an itemized statement of money owed for goods or services
  • Synonym: invoice - a detailed bill sent to a buyer for goods or services supplied

Etymology

From Old French 'acompte', from Latin 'computare' (to calculate, reckon).
