pediatrician

1. NOUN

/ ˌpiːdiəˈtrɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpiːdɪəˈtrɪʃən / (BrE)
A doctor who specializes in the medical care of children and infants.
doctor specializing in children's health
medical

Examples

After completing medical school, she decided to become a pediatrician.
Many parents consult a pediatrician for their child's yearly check-ups.
The hospital is hiring a new pediatrician to join the children's ward.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paediatrician - British spelling of 'pediatrician', doctor specializing in children's health

Etymology

From Greek 'pais, paidos' (child) and '-iatros' (physician), via French and modern Latin.
