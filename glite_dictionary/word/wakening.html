wakening

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪkənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈweɪkənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of waking or becoming awake from sleep
the act of waking from sleep

Examples

She dreaded the sudden wakening that came with her alarm each morning.
The gentle wakening of the forest was accompanied by birdsong.
After the storm, the village experienced a slow wakening to normal life.

Synset Examples

it was an early awakening
it was the waking up he hated most

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awakening - the act of waking or becoming aware
  • Synonym: waking - the act of ceasing to sleep

Etymology

From the verb 'waken' + -ing, related to Old English 'wacian'.
