dairying

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛri.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdeəri.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the business or practice of managing a dairy, especially the production and processing of milk and milk products
business of managing a dairy

Examples

Modern dairying relies on efficient production and strict hygiene standards.
She studied dairying at college before starting her own farm.
Advances in technology have greatly improved the profitability of dairying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dairy farming - the practice of raising cattle for milk production

Etymology

From 'dairy' + '-ing', denoting the activity or process.
