popsicle

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑpˌsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈpɒpˌsɪkəl / (BrE)
a frozen sweet treat made of flavored and sweetened liquid, such as juice or water, served on a stick
frozen treat on a stick
american

Examples

After a hot day at the park, she cooled off with a cherry popsicle.
The children lined up at the truck to buy a popsicle after school.
He spilled grape popsicle on his shirt and it left a stain.

Synset Examples

in England a popsicle is called an ‘ice lolly’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice lolly - frozen sweet treat on a stick (British English)

Etymology

Originally a trademark; from 'pop' (ice pop) + 'icicle'.
