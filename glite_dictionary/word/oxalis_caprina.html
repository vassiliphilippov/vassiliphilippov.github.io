Oxalis caprina

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈzælɪs kəˈpriːnə / (AmE), / ɒkˈsælɪs kəˈpriːnə / (BrE)
A short-stemmed plant native to South Africa with bluish flowers, belonging to the oxalis genus.
South African plant with bluish flowers

Examples

The garden featured several clumps of Oxalis caprina blooming in the spring.
Botanists studied the adaptations of Oxalis caprina to South African soils.
The bluish flowers of Oxalis caprina make it a popular choice among collectors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wood sorrel - Any plant of the genus Oxalis, with sour-tasting leaves

Etymology

From Latin: 'Oxalis' is the genus name, meaning 'sour' (due to oxalic acid); 'caprina' means 'of a goat'.
