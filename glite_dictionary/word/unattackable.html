unattackable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈtæk.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈtæk.ə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to attack or to undermine; immune to attack or tampering
unable to be attacked or tampered with

Examples

The new encryption system is believed to be almost unattackable by conventional hacking methods.
The fortress was considered unattackable due to its high walls and strategic location.
His logic was so airtight that his theory appeared unattackable by critics.

Synset Examples

an impregnable fortress
fortifications that made the frontier inviolable
a secure telephone connection

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impregnable - not able to be captured or broken into
  • Synonym: invulnerable - impossible to harm or damage
  • Antonym: vulnerable - able to be attacked or harmed

Etymology

Formed by combining the prefix un- (not) with attackable (able to be attacked).
