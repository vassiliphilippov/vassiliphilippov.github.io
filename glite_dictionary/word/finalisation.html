finalisation

1. NOUN

/ faɪnəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / faɪnəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something final or complete
the act of making something final
british

Examples

The finalisation of the contract is expected by next week.
After months of negotiation, the finalisation of the merger was announced.
Accurate data is essential for the finalisation of the report.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finalization - the act or process of making something final (American spelling)

Etymology

Formed from "finalise" (British spelling of "finalize") + "-ation".
