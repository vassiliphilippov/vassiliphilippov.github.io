Khartoum

1. PROPN

/ kɑrˈtum / (AmE), / kɑːˈtuːm / (BrE)
the capital city of Sudan, located at the meeting point of the Blue Nile and White Nile rivers
capital city of Sudan

Examples

The president gave a speech in Khartoum during his visit to Sudan.
Many travelers admire the view where the two Niles meet in Khartoum.
The university in Khartoum is one of the oldest in Africa.
