XXII

1. NUM

/ ɛks ɛks aɪ aɪ / (AmE), / ɛks ɛks aɪ aɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for twenty-two (22)
Roman numeral for 22

Examples

The page number was written as XXII at the bottom of the chapter.
Her birthday falls on the XXII of April each year.
The event took place in the year MCMLXXII.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 22 - the cardinal number twenty-two
  • Degree Lower: XXI - the Roman numeral for 21
  • Degree Higher: XXIII - the Roman numeral for 23

Etymology

From Latin numerals: 'X' (10) and 'I' (1).
