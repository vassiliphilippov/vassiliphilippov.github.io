xxii

1. NUM

/ ɛks ɛks aɪ aɪ / (AmE), / ɛks ɛks aɪ aɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral representing the number 22
Roman numeral for 22

Examples

The appendix begins on page xxii of the book.
In the tournament, Team xxii faced Team xiii in the finals.
The table of contents lists Chapter xxii as starting in the next section.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 22 - the cardinal number twenty-two
  • Degree Lower: xxi - the Roman numeral representing the number 21
  • Degree Higher: xxiii - the Roman numeral representing the number 23

Etymology

From Latin numerals, where 'XX' is 20 and 'II' is 2.
