utilisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌjuːtɪləˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌjuːtɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of using something for a particular purpose
the act of using something

Examples

The utilisation of solar energy is increasing across the world.
Proper utilisation of available resources can help a company thrive.
Her utilisation of new technology improved the team's efficiency.

Synset Examples

he warned against the use of narcotic drugs
skilled in the utilization of computers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: use - the act of using or employing something
  • Synonym: utilization - American spelling of 'utilisation'

Etymology

From Latin utilitas (usefulness) via French utilisation.
