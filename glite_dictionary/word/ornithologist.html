ornithologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔrnɪˈθɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɔːnɪˈθɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies birds and their behavior, classification, and habitats
a person who studies birds

Examples

The ornithologist spent years observing rare birds in the Amazon rainforest.
As an ornithologist, she often gives talks about bird migration patterns at local schools.
Several ornithologists collaborated on a global survey of endangered species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zoologist - a scientist who studies animals

Etymology

From Greek ornith- (bird) + -logist (one who studies).
