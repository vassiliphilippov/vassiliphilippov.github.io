finish

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
the designated point or event at which a contest or race ends

Examples

At the finish, excitement was at its peak as the results were announced.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: start - beginning point of a race or contest
  • Synonym: finish line - the line marking the end of a race
