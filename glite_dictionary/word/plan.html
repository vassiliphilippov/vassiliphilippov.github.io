plan

1. NOUN

/ plæn / (AmE), / plæn / (BrE)
a detailed scale drawing representing the design or layout of a building, structure, or area
scale drawing of a structure

Examples

The architect showed us the plan of the new library building.
Before construction began, the contractors studied the plan carefully.
A copy of the original plan is kept in the city archives.

Synset Examples

the plans for City Hall were on file

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blueprint - a detailed technical drawing, often used for construction
  • Synonym: map - a representation of an area showing physical features

Etymology

From French 'plan' (ground plan, drawing), from Latin 'planum' (level surface).
