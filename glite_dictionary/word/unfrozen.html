unfrozen

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfroʊzən / (AmE), / ʌnˈfrəʊzən / (BrE)
not frozen; in a liquid or unfrozen state
not frozen; thawed

Examples

After hours in the sun, the unfrozen lake reflected the sky clearly.
You must use unfrozen berries for this recipe to get the best texture.
The early spring melt left the soil unfrozen and muddy.

Synset Examples

unfrozen ground

Related Terms

  • Antonym: frozen - turned into ice or another solid state due to cold
