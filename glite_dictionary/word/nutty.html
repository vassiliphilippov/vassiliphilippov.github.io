nutty

1. ADJ

/ ˈnʌti / (AmE), / ˈnʌti / (BrE)
having a flavor or taste resembling that of nuts
tasting like nuts

Examples

This cheese has a pleasantly nutty flavor that pairs well with fruit.
The coffee was rich and nutty, with hints of chocolate.
I prefer a nutty taste in my breakfast cereals.

Synset Examples

a nutty sherry

2. ADJ

/ ˈnʌti / (AmE), / ˈnʌti / (BrE)
(informal) Crazy, slightly mad, or eccentric.
crazy or eccentric (informal)
informal

Examples

My roommate's ideas can be nutty, but they often make me laugh.
Some people thought the inventor was nutty for trying such a risky project.
Don't mind her; she gets a bit nutty when she's excited.

Synset Examples

it used to drive my husband balmy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged or wildly eccentric
  • Antonym: sane - of sound mind; not mentally ill

Etymology

Derived from 'nut' as slang for 'head' or 'brain', used to imply craziness.
