cragsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkræɡzmən / (AmE), / ˈkræɡzmən / (BrE)
a man who climbs steep or vertical rock faces, especially as a sport
male rock climber

Examples

The cragsman surveyed the rocky face before planning his ascent.
As an experienced cragsman, he preferred challenging climbs over easy ones.
Several local cragsmen were featured in the adventure magazine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mountaineer - a person who climbs mountains
  • Synonym: climber - someone who climbs, especially mountains or cliffs

Etymology

From 'crag' (a steep or rugged cliff) + 'man'.
