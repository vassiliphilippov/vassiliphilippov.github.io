associableness

1. NOUN

/ əˈsoʊʃiəbəlnəs / (AmE), / əˈsəʊʃiəbəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being easily associated, joined, or connected in thought
ability to be easily associated or connected

Examples

The associableness of these concepts makes them easy to understand together.
Her idea lacked the associableness needed to form strong mental connections with other theories.
The team valued the associableness of the new information, which linked easily to earlier findings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: associability - the quality of being able to associate or be associated

Etymology

From associable + -ness, forming a noun indicating the state or condition.
