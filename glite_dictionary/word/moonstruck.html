moonstruck

1. ADJ

/ ˈmuːnˌstrʌk / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌstrʌk / (BrE)
historically, affected with madness believed to result from the influence of the moon; now more commonly, dreamily romantic or distracted by one's thoughts.
affected by the moon; mad or dreamy
archaic

Examples

The villagers believed he had become moonstruck and wandered the hills at night.
She gazed at the sky with a moonstruck expression, lost in her own thoughts.
In old tales, those who behaved oddly during a full moon were said to be moonstruck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunatic - formerly, a person considered insane due to the phases of the moon
  • Antonym: sane - of sound mind; not affected by mental disturbance

Etymology

From 'moon' + 'struck', inspired by old beliefs about the moon affecting mental health.
