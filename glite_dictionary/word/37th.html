37th

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɜrti ˈsɛvənθ / (AmE), / ˈθɜːti ˈsɛvənθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number corresponding to thirty-seven in a sequence; coming after the thirty-sixth and before the thirty-eighth.
ordinal number: thirty-seven

Examples

She finished in 37th place in the marathon, a personal best for her.
The 37th chapter of the book covers the most important discoveries.
Tickets for the 37th annual film festival sold out within hours.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 36th - the ordinal number thirty-six, preceding '37th'
  • Degree Higher: 38th - the ordinal number thirty-eight, following '37th'
