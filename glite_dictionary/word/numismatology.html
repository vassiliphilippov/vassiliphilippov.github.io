numismatology

1. NOUN

/ njuːˌmɪzməˈtɑlədʒi / (AmE), / njuːˌmɪzməˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study or collection of coins, paper money, and medals
study and collection of coins and money

Examples

She has a deep interest in numismatology, spending weekends researching rare coins in museums.
Numismatology has helped historians understand ancient civilizations through their monetary systems.
Many students join the club to learn about numismatology and expand their coin collections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: numismatics - the study or collection of coins and currency

Etymology

From Latin numisma (coin) + Greek -logia (study of).
