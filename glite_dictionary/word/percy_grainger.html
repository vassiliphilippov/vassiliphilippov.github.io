Percy Grainger

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɜrsi ˈɡreɪndʒər / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsi ˈɡreɪndʒə / (BrE)
(1882–1961) An Australian-born composer, arranger, and pianist who became known for his collection of English folk songs and his innovative musical works; he spent much of his life in the United States and Britain.
Australian-born composer and collector of English folk songs

Examples

Percy Grainger was celebrated for his innovative arrangements of traditional English folk melodies.
The musical legacy of Percy Grainger continues to inspire modern composers around the world.
As a performer, Percy Grainger delighted audiences with his virtuosic piano playing and charismatic personality.
