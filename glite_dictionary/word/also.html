also

1. ADV

/ ˈɔlˌsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɔːl.səʊ / (BrE)
in addition; as well as; too.
in addition; as well as

Examples

I want a piece of cake also, not just ice cream.
She speaks French and also understands Italian.
The tickets are expensive, also hard to get.

Synset Examples

he has a Mercedes, too

Related Terms

  • Synonym: too - in addition; also; as well
  • Synonym: as well - in addition; too; used for adding more information

Etymology

From Old English 'eallswā', meaning 'quite so, in just the same way'.
