nondenominational

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.dɪˌnɑ.məˈneɪ.ʃə.nəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.dɪˌnɒ.mɪˈneɪ.ʃə.nəl / (BrE)
Not restricted or affiliated to any particular religious denomination.
not restricted to any religious denomination

Examples

The couple chose a nondenominational ceremony for their wedding.
Our community center provides nondenominational counseling services.
He attends a nondenominational church that welcomes everyone regardless of their background.

Synset Examples

a nondenominational church

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interdenominational - involving several religious denominations
  • Antonym: denominational - restricted or affiliated to a specific religious denomination

Etymology

From non- (not) + denominational (relating to a denomination).
