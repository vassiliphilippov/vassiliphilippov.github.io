sonsy

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɑn.zi / (AmE), / ˈsɒn.zi / (BrE)
(of a woman) having a plump and attractive figure; curvaceous.
plump and attractive; curvaceous (woman)

Examples

She was a sonsy lass, with rosy cheeks and a ready smile.
The artist specialized in portraits of sonsy women from Scottish folklore.
The magazine featured a cover photo of a notably sonsy actress.

Synset Examples

Hollywood seems full of curvaceous blondes
a curvy young woman in a tight dress

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curvaceous - having an attractively curved shape (woman)
  • Antonym: slender - gracefully thin (contrasting body type)

Etymology

Scots origin, possibly from 'sonsie' meaning 'lucky' or 'pleasant'.
