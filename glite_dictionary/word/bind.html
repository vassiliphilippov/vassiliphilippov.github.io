bind

1. NOUN

/ baɪnd / (AmE), / baɪnd / (BrE)
a difficult situation or problem that is hard to escape from or resolve
difficult or restrictive situation

Examples

I'm really in a bind now that I've lost my wallet.
The unexpected rain put us in a bind during our outdoor event.
She found herself in a bind when both jobs wanted her to start on Monday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: predicament - a difficult, unpleasant, or embarrassing situation
  • Synonym: fix - an awkward or difficult situation
  • Antonym: solution - an answer to a difficult situation
