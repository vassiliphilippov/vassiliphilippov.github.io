arthralgia

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈθrældʒiə / (AmE), / ɑːˈθraldʒɪə / (BrE)
Pain in one or more joints.
joint pain (medical term)
medical

Examples

The patient complained of severe arthralgia affecting her knees and wrists.
During flu infections, arthralgia is a common symptom that may persist for weeks.
He was prescribed analgesics to relieve his arthralgia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joint pain - pain felt in one or more joints

Etymology

From Greek arthron (joint) + -algia (pain).
