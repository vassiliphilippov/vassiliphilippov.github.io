Putin

1. PROPN

/ ˈpuː.tɪn / (AmE), / ˈpjuː.tɪn / (BrE)
Vladimir Vladimirovich Putin (born 1952), Russian politician who has served as president and prime minister of the Russian Federation.
Russian president and statesman

Examples

Putin has been a dominant figure in Russian politics for more than two decades.
Many world leaders have met with Putin to discuss international issues.
The foreign policy of Putin often draws significant attention from the global community.

Etymology

Russian surname Пу́тин (Putin).
