popliteal

1. ADJ

/ pɑːˈplɪtiəl / (AmE), / pɒˈplɪtɪəl / (BrE)
relating to or situated in the area at the back of the knee joint
relating to the area behind the knee
medical

Examples

The doctor checked the popliteal pulse to assess blood flow to the lower leg.
Pain in the popliteal region can be a sign of a Baker's cyst.
He had swelling in the popliteal area after his knee injury.

Etymology

From Latin 'popliteus', from 'poples' meaning 'ham (back of the knee)'.
