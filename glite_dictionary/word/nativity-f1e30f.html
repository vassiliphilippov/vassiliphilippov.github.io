nativity

1. NOUN

/ nəˈtɪv.ə.ti / (AmE), / nəˈtɪv.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the event of being born; birth, especially relating to a person's origins or birth circumstances
birth; the event of being born

Examples

The church service celebrated the nativity of Christ.
Many cultures have traditions marking the nativity of a newborn.
The documentary explored the nativity and early years of several famous authors.

Synset Examples

they celebrated the birth of their first child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birth - the event of being born; coming into existence

Etymology

From Latin 'nativitas' (birth), from 'nasci' (to be born).
