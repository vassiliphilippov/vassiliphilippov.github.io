Elizabeth Barrett Browning

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈlɪzəbəθ ˈbærət ˈbraʊnɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪzəbəθ ˈbærɪt ˈbraʊnɪŋ / (BrE)
(1806–1861) English poet renowned for her love sonnets and influential works, especially those dedicated to her husband, Robert Browning.
English poet (1806–1861) known for love sonnets

Examples

Many scholars consider Elizabeth Barrett Browning one of the most important Victorian poets.
The love sonnets of Elizabeth Barrett Browning are still celebrated for their depth and emotion.
Students often analyze the relationship between Elizabeth Barrett Browning and Robert Browning in literature classes.
