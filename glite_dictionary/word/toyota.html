Toyota

1. PROPN

/ toʊˈjoʊtə / (AmE), / təʊˈjəʊtə / (BrE)
an industrial city in southern Honshu, Japan, known for being the headquarters of the Toyota Motor Corporation.
industrial city in Japan, Honshu

Examples

The city of Toyota is famous for its connection to the automobile industry.
Many people visit Toyota to tour the Toyota Motor Corporation's headquarters.
Toyota has grown rapidly due to its strong manufacturing sector.

Etymology

Named after the Toyota Motor Corporation, which is headquartered there.
