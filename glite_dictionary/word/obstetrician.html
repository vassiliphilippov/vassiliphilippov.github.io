obstetrician

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑb.stəˈtrɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˌɒb.stəˈtrɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in pregnancy, childbirth, and the care of women giving birth
doctor specializing in childbirth and pregnancy
medical

Examples

She visited her obstetrician regularly during her pregnancy.
An experienced obstetrician can handle even the most complicated deliveries.
The hospital is looking to hire a new obstetrician to join their maternity team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gynecologist - a doctor specializing in women's reproductive systems

Etymology

From French obstétricien, from Latin obstetrīx (midwife), from obstāre (to stand before, assist at childbirth).
