Brasilia

1. PROPN

/ brəˈzɪl.i.ə / (AmE), / brəˈzɪl.i.ə / (BrE)
the capital city of Brazil, built on the central plateau and officially inaugurated in 1960
capital city of Brazil

Examples

Many government offices are located in Brasilia, the modern capital of Brazil.
The architectural design of Brasilia is renowned worldwide for its innovative planning.
They flew from Rio de Janeiro to Brasilia for an important conference.
