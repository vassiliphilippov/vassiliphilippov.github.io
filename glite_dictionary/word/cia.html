CIA

1. PROPN

/ siˈaɪˈeɪ / (AmE), / siˈaɪˈeɪ / (BrE)
a US government agency responsible for gathering and analyzing foreign intelligence, primarily to support national security.
US foreign intelligence agency
american

Examples

The CIA played a crucial role in collecting intelligence during the Cold War.
She applied for a job with the CIA after graduating from university.
Many movies depict the CIA as being involved in international espionage.

Related Terms
