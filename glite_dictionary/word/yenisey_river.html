Yenisey River

1. PROPN

/ ˈjɛnɪseɪ ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈjɛnɪseɪ ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A major river in Siberia, Russia, rising near the Mongolian border and flowing north into the Kara Sea.
river in Siberia, Russia

Examples

The Yenisey River freezes over each winter, creating breathtaking icy landscapes.
Several towns along the Yenisey River rely on its waters for transportation and fishing.
Explorers have long been fascinated by the length and power of the Yenisey River.
