carwash

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːr.wɑːʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.wɒʃ / (BrE)
an act or process of cleaning a car, usually with water and detergent, often at a commercial facility
the process of cleaning a car

Examples

After a long road trip, my car really needed a carwash.
The local school is holding a carwash fundraiser this Saturday.
Rain started just after I got a carwash at the gas station.

Synset Examples

We should have a carwash there.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car wash - the act or place of washing cars; alternate spelling
