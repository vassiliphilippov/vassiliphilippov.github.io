recuperative

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈkuːpərətɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈkjuːpərətɪv / (BrE)
helping someone to recover health, strength, or energy after illness, fatigue, or stress
helping recovery or restoration

Examples

After his surgery, he relied on the recuperative effects of rest and good nutrition.
The spa offered several recuperative treatments aimed at improving overall wellness.
Her recuperative powers amazed the doctors, as she healed much faster than expected.

Synset Examples

recuperative powers
strongly recuperative remedies
restorative effects of exercise

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restorative - having the ability to restore health, strength, or well-being

Etymology

From Latin recuperare (to recover) + -ive.
