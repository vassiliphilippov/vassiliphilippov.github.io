unappreciated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnəˈpriʃieɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈpriːʃieɪtɪd / (BrE)
not valued or recognized; not given the praise or reward deserved
not valued or rewarded

Examples

She felt unappreciated at work despite all her efforts on the project.
Many volunteers do valuable but unappreciated tasks behind the scenes.
His unappreciated kindness eventually won him respect from the community.

Synset Examples

grading papers is a thankless task

Related Terms

  • Antonym: appreciated - recognized and valued; receiving praise or reward
  • Synonym: thankless - not likely to be rewarded or appreciated

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈpri.ʃi.eɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈpriː.ʃi.eɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
Not having one's value, efforts, or qualities recognized or acknowledged.
not recognized or valued

Examples

She often feels unappreciated at work despite her dedication and hard work.
The artist's genius went unappreciated during his lifetime.
Many household chores are necessary but go largely unappreciated by others.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: appreciated - having value that is recognized or acknowledged
  • Synonym: valued - considered important or useful

Etymology

Prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' + 'appreciated' from Latin 'appretiare' (to value).
