pepperoni

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɛpəˈroʊni / (AmE), / ˌpɛpəˈrəʊni / (BrE)
a highly seasoned pork and beef sausage, usually in thin slices, commonly used as a pizza topping
spicy pork and beef sausage

Examples

She always orders extra pepperoni on her pizza.
The deli sells pepperoni by the slice or by the stick.
He made a sandwich with cheese, tomato, and pepperoni.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salami - a type of seasoned, cured sausage often made from pork or beef

Etymology

From Italian 'peperoni', plural of 'peperone' meaning 'bell pepper'; in American English, it refers to a type of spicy sausage.
