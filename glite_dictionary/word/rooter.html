rooter

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːtər / (AmE), / ˈruːtə / (BrE)
a person who enthusiastically supports and cheers for a sports team or player
enthusiastic sports fan
american informal

Examples

The stadium was filled with excited rooters waving team flags.
As a lifelong rooter for the Red Sox, he rarely missed a game.
The team thanked their loyal rooters after the championship victory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fan - a person with strong interest or admiration for something, especially sports

Etymology

From 'root' (to support, cheer) + -er (agent noun suffix), chiefly North American usage.
