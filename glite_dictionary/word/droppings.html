droppings

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɑpɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpɪŋz / (BrE)
Feces of animals, especially as found in small pellets or piles.
animal feces, especially pellets

Examples

We found bird droppings all over the windowsill after the storm.
Rabbit droppings are often scattered throughout the garden beds.
The zookeeper cleaned the elephant droppings from the enclosure every morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manure - animal dung used for fertilizing land
  • Synonym: feces - waste matter discharged from the bowels
