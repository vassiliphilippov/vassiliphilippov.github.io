humanely

1. ADV

/ hjuˈmeɪnli / (AmE), / hjʊˈmeɪnli / (BrE)
In a way that shows compassion, kindness, and concern for others, especially in the treatment of people or animals.
in a compassionate, kind manner

Examples

The shelter ensured all animals were treated humanely during their stay.
Doctors and nurses have a duty to care for patients humanely at all times.
He advocated for prisoners to be treated humanely regardless of their crimes.

Synset Examples

let's treat the prisoners of war humanely

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cruelly - in a cruel or unkind manner

Etymology

From 'humane' (from Latin 'humanus', meaning human, kind), with the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
