cardiorespiratory

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑːrdi.oʊˈrɛspɪˌrɛtɔri / (AmE), / ˌkɑːdiəʊrɪˈspɪrətəri / (BrE)
Relating to or affecting both the heart and the lungs and their functions.
of or relating to the heart and lungs
medical

Examples

Regular exercise can have significant benefits for cardiorespiratory health.
The study measured cardiorespiratory endurance in athletes.
Certain diseases impact cardiorespiratory function, making breathing and circulation less efficient.

Synset Examples

cardiopulmonary resuscitation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cardiopulmonary - relating to the heart and lungs

Etymology

From cardio- (relating to the heart) and respiratory (relating to breathing or the lungs).
