understandability

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌndərˌstændəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˌstændəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being easy to understand; the degree to which something is comprehensible.
quality of being easy to understand

Examples

The understandability of the instructions was praised by all the workshop participants.
Technical documents are often revised to improve their understandability.
She values clarity and understandability in every presentation she gives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comprehensibility - quality of being easy to comprehend
  • Antonym: obscurity - quality of being difficult to understand

Etymology

From 'understand' + '-ability', modeled after similar English formations.
