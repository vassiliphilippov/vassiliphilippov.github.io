Cymric

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪm.rɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɪm.rɪk / (BrE)
the Celtic language of Wales; Welsh.
the Welsh language

Examples

He studied the ancient poems written in Cymric.
The book includes a glossary of Cymric words and phrases.
Only a few villagers still spoke Cymric fluently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Welsh - the Celtic language spoken in Wales

Etymology

From Latin Cymricus, from Cymru (the Welsh name for Wales) + -ic.
