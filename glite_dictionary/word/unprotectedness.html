unprotectedness

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.prəˈtɛkt.ɪd.nəs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prəˈtɛk.tɪd.nəs / (BrE)
The condition or state of lacking protection, making one vulnerable to harm or attack.
state of lacking protection

Examples

The village’s unprotectedness made it an easy target for raiders seeking plunder.
Her sense of unprotectedness grew as darkness fell and she realized she was alone.
Lawmakers discussed the unprotectedness of marginalized groups during the heated debate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vulnerability - susceptibility to harm or attack; openness to danger
  • Antonym: security - state of being free from danger or threat

Etymology

Formed from 'unprotected' + '-ness', using the common English suffix for nouns of state or quality.
