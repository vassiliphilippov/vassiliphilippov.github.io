fortnight

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrtˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtnaɪt / (BrE)
a period of fourteen consecutive days; two weeks
two-week period
british

Examples

We only have a fortnight to finish the project, so we must work quickly.
They planned a holiday that would last exactly one fortnight.
It's been a fortnight since I last saw her.

Synset Examples

most major tennis tournaments last a fortnight

Etymology

From Middle English 'fortenight', contraction of 'fourteen night'.
