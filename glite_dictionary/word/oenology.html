oenology

1. NOUN

/ iːˈnɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / iːˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study of wine and wine making
the study of wine making

Examples

She decided to pursue a degree in oenology to learn more about wine production.
His passion for oenology led him to travel to vineyards across Europe.
Recent advances in oenology have improved the quality of many wines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: viticulture - the cultivation of grapevines for winemaking

Etymology

From Greek oinos (wine) + -logia (study of).
