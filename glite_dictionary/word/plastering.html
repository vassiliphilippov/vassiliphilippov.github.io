plastering

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæstərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of applying plaster to walls, ceilings, or other surfaces to form a smooth or decorative finish
the application of plaster to surfaces

Examples

The plastering on the new ceiling was expertly finished and looked flawless.
We hired professionals to handle the plastering of our living room walls.
His skill in plastering became evident as the old house was restored.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plaster - a mixture applied to walls or ceilings to form a smooth surface

Etymology

From 'plaster' (Middle English, from Old French plastre, from Latin emplastrum) + '-ing'.
