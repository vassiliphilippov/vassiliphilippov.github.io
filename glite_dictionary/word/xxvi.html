xxvi

1. ADJ

/ ɛks ɛks vi ˈsɪksˌtin / (AmE), / ɛks ɛks vi ˈsɪksˌtiːn / (BrE)
the Roman numeral representing the number 26
Roman numeral for 26

Examples

The book's twenty-sixth chapter was marked with xxvi at the top of the page.
She numbered her list items using Roman numerals, ending at xxvi.
The year was engraved as xxvi on the antique coin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twenty-six - the cardinal number 26
  • Degree Lower: xxv - Roman numeral for 25
  • Degree Higher: xxvii - Roman numeral for 27

Etymology

From Latin numerals: 'XX' for twenty (10 + 10), 'VI' for six (5 + 1).
