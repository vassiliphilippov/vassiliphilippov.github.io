Hillary Clinton

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɪləri ˈklɪntən / (AmE), / ˈhɪləri ˈklɪntən / (BrE)
(born 1947) American politician, diplomat, and lawyer; former First Lady, U.S. Senator, Secretary of State, and presidential candidate.
American politician and former Secretary of State

Examples

Hillary Clinton served as the U.S. Secretary of State from 2009 to 2013.
Many people remember Hillary Clinton's historic run for president in 2016.
Hillary Clinton was elected to the U.S. Senate from New York in 2000.
