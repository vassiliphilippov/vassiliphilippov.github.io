uselessly

1. ADV

/ ˈjuːslɪsli / (AmE), / ˈjuːsləsli / (BrE)
In a manner that serves no useful purpose; ineffectively or without result.
in a way that is not useful

Examples

He waved his hands uselessly as the car moved farther away.
The soldiers searched uselessly for any sign of the missing hikers.
She complained uselessly, knowing nothing would change.

Synset Examples

the furniture was sitting around uselessly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: usefully - in a useful manner
