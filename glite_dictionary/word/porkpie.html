porkpie

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔrkˌpaɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɔːkˌpaɪ / (BrE)
a man's hat with a low, flat crown and a small, turned-up brim
hat with a flat crown and upturned brim

Examples

He wore a sharp suit and a matching porkpie to the party.
The jazz musician was instantly recognizable by his trademark porkpie.
Vintage shops often sell classic porkpie hats alongside other retro accessories.

Etymology

So called from its resemblance to a traditional British pork pie.
