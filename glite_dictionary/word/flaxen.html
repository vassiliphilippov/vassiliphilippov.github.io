flaxen

1. ADJ

/ ˈflæksən / (AmE), / ˈflæksən / (BrE)
(especially of hair) pale yellowish or yellowish-brown in color, like that of flax.
pale yellowish (hair color)

Examples

Her flaxen hair glowed in the sunlight as she walked through the field.
The child had a crown of soft, flaxen curls framing his face.
Legends often describe fair maidens with long, flaxen locks.

Synset Examples

flaxen locks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blond - light yellowish hair color
  • Degree Higher: golden - having a deep yellow color like gold

Etymology

From Old English *fleaxen*, from *fleax* ('flax') + *-en* ('made of, resembling').
