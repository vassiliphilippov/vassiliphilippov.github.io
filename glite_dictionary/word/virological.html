virological

1. ADJ

/ ˌvaɪrəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌvaɪərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to virology or the scientific study of viruses
relating to the science of virology
medical

Examples

She attended a virological conference to present her latest findings on coronaviruses.
The team published a virological study that identified several new strains of the virus.
Accurate virological testing is essential for tracking the outbreak.

Synset Examples

virological research

Related Terms

  • Synonym: virology - the scientific study of viruses

Etymology

From 'virology' + '-ical', ultimately from Latin 'virus' (poison, slime).
