XXVIII

1. NUM

/ ɛks ɛks v aɪ ɪ t i / (AmE), / ɛks ɛks v aɪ ɪ t i / (BrE)
The Roman numeral for the number 28.
Roman numeral for 28

Examples

The book was published in the year XXVIII of the king's reign.
On the clock tower, the number XXVIII is marked in Roman numerals.
He solved problem number XXVIII in the mathematics competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 28 - the cardinal number following 27

Etymology

From Latin numerals: 'XX' means 20, 'VIII' means 8.
