Laconian

1. NOUN

/ ləˈkoʊniən / (AmE), / ləˈkəʊniən / (BrE)
A native or resident of Laconia, a region of southern Greece.
resident of Laconia, Greece

Examples

The ancient Laconians were renowned for their brevity of speech.
Several artifacts from Laconian culture have been discovered in recent excavations.
As a proud Laconian, he shared stories about the region's history.

Etymology

From Latin *Laconius*, from Greek *Lakōn* (Λάκων), meaning 'person from Laconia'.
