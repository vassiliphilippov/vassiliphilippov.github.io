lunar

1. ADJ

/ ˈluːnɚ / (AmE), / ˈluːnə / (BrE)
relating to, or resembling the moon
relating to the moon

Examples

The astronauts collected rocks from the lunar surface to bring back to Earth.
Many ancient calendars were based on lunar cycles rather than solar ones.
The lunar eclipse was visible across the entire continent last night.

Synset Examples

lunar surface
lunar module

Related Terms

  • Antonym: solar - relating to the sun

Etymology

From Latin lunaris, from luna (moon).
