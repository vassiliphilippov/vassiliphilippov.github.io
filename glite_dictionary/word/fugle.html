to fugle

1. VERB

/ ˈfjuːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈfjuːɡl / (BrE)
to find a way to escape from or cleverly manipulate a situation
to maneuver out of a situation

Examples

She tried to fugle her way out of the conversation when the topic became uncomfortable.
Politicians are often accused of trying to fugle difficult questions instead of answering them directly.
If you keep fugling your responsibilities, sooner or later it will catch up with you.

Synset Examples

She tried to fugle her way out of the conversation.
He decided to fugle the situation by avoiding the topic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to evade - to avoid or escape from something or someone
  • Antonym: to confront - to face up to and deal with a problem or situation
