EDP

1. NOUN

/ ˌiː ˌdiː ˈpiː / (AmE), / ˌiː ˌdiː ˈpiː / (BrE)
automatic data processing by electronic means, especially without the use of punched cards or tapes; electronic data processing.
electronic data processing (computing)

Examples

The company upgraded its EDP systems to improve data management efficiency.
Advances in EDP have revolutionized accounting and business administration.
Many organizations rely on EDP to handle large volumes of information securely.

Etymology

Acronym of Electronic Data Processing.
