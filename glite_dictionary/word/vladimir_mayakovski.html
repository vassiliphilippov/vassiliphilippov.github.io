Vladimir Mayakovski

1. PROPN

/ vlɑːˈdɪmɪr maɪəˈkɒfski / (AmE), / vləˈdɪmɪə maɪəˈkɒfski / (BrE)
(1893–1930) Russian Soviet poet and playwright; a leading figure in Russian Futurism and influential in Soviet literature.
Russian Futurist poet (1893–1930)

Examples

Vladimir Mayakovski was a prominent voice of the Russian Futurist movement in the early 20th century.
The poems of Vladimir Mayakovski are studied in Russian literature courses around the world.
Many critics consider Vladimir Mayakovski the most innovative Soviet poet of his time.
