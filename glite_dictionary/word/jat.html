Jat

1. PROPN

/ dʒɑt / (AmE), / dʒɑːt / (BrE)
a member of an Indo-European ethnic group found mainly in northern India and Pakistan, with communities among Hindus, Muslims, and Sikhs.
a member of a North Indian ethnic group

Examples

Many Jat farmers own vast tracts of land in Haryana and Punjab.
The Jat community has a rich history of military service in India.
There are both Hindu and Sikh Jat populations in northern India.

Etymology

From Hindi जाट (jāṭ), possibly from Sanskrit याट (yāṭa).
