semipro

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛm.iˌproʊ / (AmE), / ˈsɛmiˌprəʊ / (BrE)
a person, especially an athlete, who plays a sport for payment but not as a full-time professional
part-time paid athlete

Examples

After college, he played as a semipro while working a regular job during the week.
Many semipros dream of turning their passion into a full-time career.
She balanced her commitments as a semipro with a demanding day job.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: amateur - a person who does something for pleasure, not payment
  • Antonym: professional - a person who does something as a paid occupation, especially an athlete

Etymology

Abbreviation of semi-professional.
