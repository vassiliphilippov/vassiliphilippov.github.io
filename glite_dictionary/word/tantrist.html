Tantrist

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæntrɪst / (AmE), / ˈtan.trɪst / (BrE)
a person who follows or practices Tantrism, a tradition found in Hinduism and Buddhism
adherent of Tantrism

Examples

The Tantrist devoted years to studying ancient scriptures and practicing sacred rituals.
As a Tantrist, she participated in meditation ceremonies unique to her spiritual tradition.
The book explores the beliefs and practices of the modern Tantrist community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tantrism - a Hindu or Buddhist tradition focused on esoteric rituals and practices

Etymology

From Tantra + -ist, modeled after similar religious terms.
