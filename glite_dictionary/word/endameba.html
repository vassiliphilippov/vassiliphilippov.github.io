endameba

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛndəˈmiːbə / (AmE), / ˌɛndəˈmiːbə / (BrE)
Any amoeba of the genus Endamoeba, a group of single-celled protozoans.
genus of single-celled amoebae

Examples

Scientists observed the endameba under a microscope to study its cellular structure.
The digestive tracts of certain animals often contain species of endameba.
Some types of endameba are important for understanding basic protozoan biology.

Etymology

From New Latin 'Endamoeba': 'end-' (within) + 'amoeba'.
