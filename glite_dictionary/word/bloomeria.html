Bloomeria

1. NOUN

/ bluˈmɪriə / (AmE), / bluˈmɪəriə / (BrE)
A small genus of bulbous perennial herbs native to the southwestern United States and Mexico, sometimes classified in the family Alliaceae.
genus of bulbous perennial herbs

Examples

The rare Bloomeria produces delicate yellow flowers in early summer.
We saw several varieties of Bloomeria while hiking in southern California.
The botanist wrote a paper on the distribution of Bloomeria species in Mexico.

Etymology

Named after Hiram Green Bloomer, an American botanist.
