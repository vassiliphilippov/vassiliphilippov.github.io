rumkin

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌm.kɪn / (AmE), / ˈrʌm.kɪn / (BrE)
a small quantity or container for cider or honey
small container for cider or honey

Examples

He carefully measured a rumkin of honey for the recipe.
At the festival, she was served a rumkin of fresh cider.
Old taverns sometimes used a rumkin as a standard measure for drinks.

Synset Examples

He enjoyed a small rumkin of cider at the fair.
The recipe called for a rumkin of honey for sweetness.
