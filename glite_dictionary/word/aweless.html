aweless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔːləs / (AmE), / ˈɔːləs / (BrE)
Not feeling or showing awe or respect; lacking a sense of reverence or fear.
lacking awe or respect

Examples

The aweless attitude of the crowd shocked the organizers.
Despite his reputation, she remained aweless in his presence.
His aweless disregard for tradition unsettled many in the community.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: respectful - showing respect or consideration
  • Antonym: awe-struck - filled with awe or wonder

Etymology

From 'awe' + suffix '-less', meaning 'without awe'.

2. ADJ

/ ˈɔləs / (AmE), / ˈɔːləs / (BrE)
Lacking any feeling of awe, respect, or reverence.
lacking awe or reverence

Examples

Her aweless attitude toward tradition shocked the elders of the community.
The critics described the film as aweless, missing the grandeur of the original.
He spoke in an aweless tone, unimpressed by the historic surroundings.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: awe - a feeling of reverential respect mixed with fear or wonder

Etymology

Formed from 'awe' + '-less' (without).
