architectural

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑːrkɪˈtɛktʃərəl / (AmE), / ˌɑːkɪˈtɛktʃərəl / (BrE)
relating to the art and practice of designing and constructing buildings
relating to architecture or building design

Examples

The old library is an architectural landmark in the city.
She studied architectural design at university before starting her career.
Many tourists admire the architectural features of the ancient cathedral.

Synset Examples

architectural history
architectural design

Related Terms

  • Synonym: architectonic - relating to architecture or inherent structural design

Etymology

From architecture + -al, tracing ultimately to Latin architectura.
