federated

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɛdəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈfɛdərɛɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of states, organizations, etc.) united under a central government while retaining some internal autonomy.
united under a central government

Examples

Several independent republics formed a federated country to strengthen their collective security.
The organization is made up of federated units that manage their local operations autonomously.
Australia is a federated nation consisting of six states united under a central government.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: federal - relating to a system of government in which several states form a unity but remain independent in internal affairs

Etymology

From Latin 'foederatus' (bound by treaty), via French 'fédéré'.
