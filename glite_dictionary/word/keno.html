keno

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkiːnəʊ / (BrE)
A game of chance in which players choose numbers to match those drawn at random, with prizes awarded for matching specific patterns.
game of chance with numbered draws

Examples

Many casinos offer daily keno games with substantial cash prizes.
She played keno every week, hoping her lucky numbers would be drawn.
The rules of keno are simple to learn, making it popular among new players.

Etymology

From French 'quine' (a set of five winning numbers in lotto), adapted in American English.
