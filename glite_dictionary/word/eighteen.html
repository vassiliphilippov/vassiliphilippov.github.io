eighteen

1. NUM

/ ˌeɪˈtin / (AmE), / ˌeɪˈtiːn / (BrE)
the number 18; one more than seventeen
the number 18

Examples

Eighteen students signed up for the photography workshop this year.
He finally got his driver's license when he turned eighteen.
There are eighteen chapters in the new book.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: seventeen - the number 17; one less than eighteen
  • Degree Higher: nineteen - the number 19; one more than eighteen

Etymology

From Old English eahtatīene, from eahta (eight) + tīene (ten).
