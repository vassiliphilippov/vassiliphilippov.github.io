Salvador

1. PROPN

/ ˌsæl.vəˈdɔr / (AmE), / ˌsal.vəˈdɔː / (BrE)
A republic in Central America on the Pacific coast, officially known as El Salvador.
country in Central America

Examples

Tourists often visit Salvador for its beautiful beaches and rich culture.
The capital city of Salvador is San Salvador.
Many families emigrated from Salvador during the civil war.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: El Salvador - the official name of Salvador, a country in Central America

Etymology

From Spanish, meaning 'the savior'.
