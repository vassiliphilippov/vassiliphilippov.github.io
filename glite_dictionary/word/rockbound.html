rockbound

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɑkˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈrɒkˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
(of a place) abounding in or bordered by rocks or rocky cliffs.
full of or bordered by rocks

Examples

The fishing village was situated on a rockbound peninsula facing the Atlantic Ocean.
We hiked along the rockbound coast, marveling at the sheer cliffs.
The lighthouse stands watch over the rockbound shores, warning ships of the danger.

Synset Examples

the rock-ribbed coast of Maine
