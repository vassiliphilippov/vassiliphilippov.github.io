capitol

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæp.ɪ.təl / (AmE), / ˈkæp.ɪ.təl / (BrE)
A building where a state legislature meets and conducts its business.
building for state legislature

Examples

The state governor delivered his annual address at the capitol yesterday.
Protesters gathered outside the capitol to voice their concerns about the new law.
The tour included a visit to the historic capitol in the city center.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: capitol building - A similar term referring to the building in which a legislative assembly meets
  • Antonym: parliament - The supreme legislative body in some countries

Etymology

From Latin 'Capitōlium', the temple of Jupiter in Rome.
