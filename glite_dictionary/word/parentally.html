parentally

1. ADV

/ pəˈrɛntəli / (AmE), / pəˈrɛntəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or is typical of parents; in a parental manner.
in a parental manner

Examples

The child was parentally supervised at all times during the event.
Certain behaviors are strongly influenced parentally in early development.
The program is designed to support children who are parentally neglected.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parental - relating to parents

Etymology

From 'parental' + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
