Bahasa Melayu

1. NOUN

/ bɑˈhɑsə məˈlaɪu / (AmE), / bɑːˈhɑːsə məˈlaɪu / (BrE)
The standard form of the Malay language spoken in Malaysia and nearby countries.
official standard Malay language

Examples

Many students in Malaysia learn Bahasa Melayu as their first language in school.
Instructions were provided in both English and Bahasa Melayu for the participants.
The translation of the book into Bahasa Melayu helped it reach a wider local audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Malay - the language of the Malay people, spoken in several Southeast Asian countries

Etymology

From Malay 'bahasa' (language) + 'Melayu' (Malay).
