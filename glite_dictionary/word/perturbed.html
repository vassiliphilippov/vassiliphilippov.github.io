perturbed

1. ADJ

/ pərˈtɜrbd / (AmE), / pəˈtɜːbd / (BrE)
feeling anxious, upset, or disturbed; thrown into confusion or agitation
anxious or disturbed

Examples

She seemed visibly perturbed by the unexpected news.
His perturbed expression showed he was worried about the results.
Even when challenged, she tried not to appear perturbed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: calm - not feeling anxious or disturbed
  • Synonym: agitated - feeling nervous or troubled

Etymology

From Latin 'perturbare' (to disturb thoroughly), from 'per-' (thoroughly) + 'turbare' (to disturb).
