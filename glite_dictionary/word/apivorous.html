apivorous

1. ADJ

/ ˌeɪ.pəˈvɔːr.əs / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.pɪˈvɔː.rəs / (BrE)
(of an animal) feeding on bees.
feeding on bees (biology)

Examples

The honey buzzard is an apivorous bird known for raiding beehives.
Some mammals exhibit apivorous behavior during times when bees are abundant.
Researchers observed that the bat's diet was primarily apivorous in certain regions.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: insectivorous - feeding on insects

Etymology

From Latin 'apis' (bee) + '-vorous' from Latin 'vorare' (to devour).
