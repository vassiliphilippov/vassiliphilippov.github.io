Rana tarahumarae

1. NOUN

/ ˈrænə tɑːrəhuːˈmɑːreɪ / (AmE), / ˈreɪnə tɑːrəhuːˈmɑːreɪ / (BrE)
A species of Mexican frog typically found near water, known as the Tarahumara frog.
Mexican frog species (Tarahumara frog)

Examples

The Rana tarahumarae is often spotted near streams in northern Mexico.
Conservationists are concerned about the declining population of Rana tarahumarae due to habitat loss.
Researchers studied the ecology of Rana tarahumarae to better understand its adaptation to arid environments.

Etymology

From Latin 'Rana' (frog) and the Tarahumara region/people of Mexico, where the species is found.
