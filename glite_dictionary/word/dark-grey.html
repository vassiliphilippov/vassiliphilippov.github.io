dark-grey

1. ADJ

/ dɑrk ˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / dɑːk ˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a shade of grey that is closer to black than to white; having a deep, subdued grey color.
of a dark shade of grey

Examples

The sky was covered in dark-grey clouds before the storm began.
She wore a dark-grey coat that matched her boots perfectly.
The cat's fur was a beautiful dark-grey shade.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: grey - having a color between black and white
  • Degree Higher: black - the darkest color, resulting from the absence of light
