normalization

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɔrmələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɔːməlʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of establishing or applying standards or regulations to ensure uniformity or consistency
imposition of standards or regulations

Examples

The government introduced new regulations aimed at normalization of safety procedures across all industries.
The normalization of technical terms helps facilitate international collaboration in science.
Education reform included the normalization of curricula to ensure all students received similar instruction.

Synset Examples

a committee was appointed to recommend terminological standardization

Related Terms

  • Synonym: standardization - the process of making something conform to a standard

Etymology

From French 'normalisation', from 'normal' + '-ization'.
