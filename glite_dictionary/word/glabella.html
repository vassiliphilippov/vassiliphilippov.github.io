glabella

1. NOUN

/ ɡləˈbɛlə / (AmE), / ɡləˈbɛlə / (BrE)
the smooth area of the forehead between and just above the eyebrows
area between the eyebrows
medical

Examples

The doctor pressed gently on the glabella to check for tenderness.
Wrinkles often develop above the glabella as people age.
In anatomy class, we learned to identify the glabella on a human skull.

Etymology

From Latin 'glabellus', meaning 'smooth, hairless'.
