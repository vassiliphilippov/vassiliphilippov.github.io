unlaurelled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlɔr.əld / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɔː.rəld / (BrE)
Not honored, crowned, or acclaimed with a laurel; having received no recognition or reward.
not given honor or acclaim

Examples

Her unlaurelled achievements were known only to a select few close friends.
The unlaurelled efforts of volunteers are often vital to the success of a project.
He endured years of unlaurelled service, never seeking public recognition.

Synset Examples

the unlaureled heroism of endurance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unrewarded - not given a reward or recognition
  • Antonym: laurelled - honored or crowned with laurel; acclaimed

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) and 'laurelled' (crowned with laurel, a symbol of honor).
