Buganda

1. PROPN

/ buˈɡɑːndə / (AmE), / bjuˈɡændə / (BrE)
a region in central Uganda, formerly a powerful Bantu kingdom and now a traditional kingdom within Uganda
traditional kingdom and region in Uganda

Examples

Many tourists visit Buganda to learn about its rich cultural heritage.
The Kabaka is the traditional king of Buganda.
Buganda played a major role in the history of Uganda.
