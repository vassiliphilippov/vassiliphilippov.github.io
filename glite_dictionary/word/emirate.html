emirate

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛmɪˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛmɪərət / (BrE)
the office or territory governed by an emir
territory or office of an emir

Examples

The small state was officially recognized as an emirate in the late 19th century.
He took great pride in leading the emirate into a period of prosperity.
Each emirate retained a degree of autonomy within the federation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: principality - territory ruled by a prince

Etymology

From Arabic 'imāra' (princedom, command), from 'amīr' (commander, prince).

2. NOUN

/ ˈɛmɪˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛmɪəreɪt / (BrE)
A territory or state ruled by an emir.
territory ruled by an emir

Examples

The United Arab Emirates is a country consisting of seven emirates on the Arabian Peninsula.
Each emirate has its own local government but shares power with the federal government.
Dubai is the most populous emirate within the UAE.

Etymology

From Arabic 'imārah' (command, princedom), from 'amīr' (prince, ruler).
