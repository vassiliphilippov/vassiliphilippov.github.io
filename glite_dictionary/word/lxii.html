LXII

1. NUM

/ ɛl ɛks aɪ aɪ / (AmE), / ɛl ɛks aɪ aɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for sixty-two (62)
Roman numeral for 62

Examples

The ancient inscription was marked with the year LXII.
She numbered the volume as LXII in the collection.
The competition was held for the LXII time since its inception.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 62 - the number sixty-two

Etymology

From Latin numerals: L (50), X (10), II (2), together forming 62.
