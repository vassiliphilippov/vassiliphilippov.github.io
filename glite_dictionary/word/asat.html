ASAT

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪ ˌɛs ˌeɪ ˈti / (AmE), / ˈeɪ ˌɛs ˌeɪ ˈti / (BrE)
relating to a device or system intended to destroy satellites in orbit.
relating to anti-satellite systems

Examples

The military successfully tested an ASAT missile last year.
New treaties may restrict the development of ASAT technologies.
Concern is growing about the proliferation of ASAT weapons among space-faring nations.

Synset Examples

antisatellite weapons

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anti-satellite - designed to destroy satellites in orbit

Etymology

Acronym of 'anti-satellite'.
