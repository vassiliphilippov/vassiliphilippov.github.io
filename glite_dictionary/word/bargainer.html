bargainer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːrɡənər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːɡɪnə / (BrE)
a person who negotiates the terms and conditions of a transaction or agreement
person who negotiates a deal

Examples

She proved to be a skilled bargainer during the contract discussions.
As a tough bargainer, he rarely accepted the first offer.
The company hired an experienced bargainer to negotiate better rates.

Synset Examples

he is a hard bargainer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: negotiator - person who conducts negotiations

Etymology

From bargain + -er, forming an agent noun.
