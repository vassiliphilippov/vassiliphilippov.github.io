woodworking

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdˌwɝːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or skill of making items from wood, especially as a craft or hobby
the craft of making things from wood

Examples

He took up woodworking after he retired, crafting beautiful tables and chairs in his garage.
Many high schools offer woodworking classes to teach students practical skills.
My grandfather passed down his woodworking tools to me as a family tradition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpentry - the activity or skill of making or repairing wooden objects
