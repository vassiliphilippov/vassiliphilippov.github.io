Guyanese

1. NOUN

/ ɡaɪ.əˈniz / (AmE), / ɡaɪ.əˈniːz / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Guyana.
person from Guyana

Examples

Many Guyanese have settled in cities around the world.
He spoke passionately about his experiences as a Guyanese.
A group of Guyanese gathered to celebrate their national holiday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Surinamese - a native or inhabitant of Suriname

Etymology

From Guyana, the name of the country, plus the suffix -ese (denoting origin or nationality).
