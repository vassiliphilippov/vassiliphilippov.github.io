bimillennium

1. NOUN

/ ˌbaɪ.mɪˈlɛni.əm / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.mɪˈleni.əm / (BrE)
A period or span of two thousand years.
a period of 2,000 years

Examples

Some historians reflect on the changes that have occurred over the last bimillennium.
The ancient city was founded more than a bimillennium ago.
Archaeologists uncovered artifacts dating back nearly a bimillennium.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: millennium - a period of one thousand years

Etymology

From Latin prefix 'bi-' meaning 'two' and 'millennium' meaning 'a thousand years.'
