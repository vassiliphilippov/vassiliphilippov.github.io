appetisingness

1. NOUN

/ ˌæpɪˈtaɪzɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˌæpɪˈtʌɪzɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being appealing to the appetite or stimulating a desire to eat
quality of stimulating the appetite

Examples

The chef focused on enhancing the appetisingness of each dish with fresh ingredients and vibrant colors.
Photographs cannot capture the true appetisingness of the freshly baked bread.
The restaurant's success is largely due to the appetisingness of its signature meals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appetising - appealing to the appetite; making you want to eat

Etymology

From appetising + -ness, formed from Latin appetitus (desire for food).
