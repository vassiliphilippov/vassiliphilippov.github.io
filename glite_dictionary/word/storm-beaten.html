storm-beaten

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɔrm ˌbitn̩ / (AmE), / ˈstɔːm ˌbiːtn̩ / (BrE)
Damaged or worn as a result of exposure to storms.
damaged by storms

Examples

The storm-beaten ship finally reached the safety of the harbor.
Their storm-beaten cottage stood testimony to years of harsh weather.
The storm-beaten trees leaned heavily toward the ground after the hurricane.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weather-beaten - worn or damaged by exposure to weather
