petalless

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛt̬ləs / (AmE), / ˈpɛtləs / (BrE)
(of flowers) having no petals.
without petals (of flowers)

Examples

Some plants produce petalless flowers to conserve energy.
The petalless blooms of this species rely on wind for pollination.
Botanists classified the new variety as petalless due to its unique structure.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: petaled - having petals (of flowers)

Etymology

From 'petal' + '-less' (a suffix meaning 'without').
