callback

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔːlˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈkɔːlbæk / (BrE)
an official request by a manufacturer for customers to return a defective product, usually for repair or replacement
product recall by manufacturer

Examples

The company issued a callback after discovering a fault in the brakes of several car models.
There was a major callback of baby cribs due to safety concerns last year.
Customers affected by the callback will receive free repairs or replacements for the defective products.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recall - an official order for the return of defective goods
