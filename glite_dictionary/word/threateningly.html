threateningly

1. ADV

/ ˈθrɛtənɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈθrɛtənɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that suggests harm, danger, or hostility; in a menacing manner.
in a menacing manner

Examples

She glared threateningly at anyone who dared to approach.
The dog barked threateningly as the stranger came closer.
Dark clouds gathered threateningly on the horizon, foreshadowing a storm.

Synset Examples

the voice at the other end of the line dropped menacingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: menacingly - in a menacing or threatening manner
