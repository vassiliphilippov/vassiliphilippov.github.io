cooker

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊkər / (AmE), / ˈkʊkə / (BrE)
a device or piece of equipment used for cooking food, such as an oven or stove
device for cooking food

Examples

We bought a new cooker for the kitchen last weekend.
Please make sure the cooker is turned off after dinner.
The recipe says to put the dish in the cooker for forty minutes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stove - an apparatus for cooking or heating, typically fueled by gas or electricity
  • Synonym: oven - a chamber used for baking, roasting, or heating food

Etymology

From 'cook' + '-er', meaning 'that which cooks'.
