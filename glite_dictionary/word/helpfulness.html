helpfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlpfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈhɛlpfəlnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being willing or able to provide useful assistance to others
quality of being helpful

Examples

Her helpfulness was evident when she stayed late to assist her colleagues with their work.
The helpfulness of the instructions made the assembly process much easier.
We greatly appreciate your helpfulness during our stay at the hotel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: usefulness - the quality of being useful or serving a purpose
  • Antonym: unhelpfulness - the quality of being not helpful

Etymology

From 'helpful' + '-ness', forming a noun to denote the state or quality.

2. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlpfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈhɛlpfəlnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being willing to help or assist others; a disposition to be helpful and friendly
willingness to help or assist others

Examples

Her helpfulness made a great impression on the new employees.
We truly appreciate your helpfulness during our transition to the new office.
His helpfulness in answering questions was noted by the students.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unhelpfulness - the quality of not being helpful or willing to assist
  • Synonym: kindness - the quality of being kind and considerate toward others

Etymology

From 'helpful' + '-ness', forming a noun indicating the state or quality.
