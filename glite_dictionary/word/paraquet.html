paraquet

1. NOUN

/ ˈpærəkɛt / (AmE), / ˈparəkɛt / (BrE)
any of numerous small, slender, long-tailed parrots, also known as parakeets.
small long-tailed parrot

Examples

The bright green paraquet perched on the windowsill, chirping cheerfully.
Many bird enthusiasts keep paraquets as pets because of their playful nature.
A flock of wild paraquets can be seen flying over the rainforest canopy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parakeet - a small long-tailed parrot

Etymology

Variant of 'parakeet', from French 'paroquet', of uncertain origin.
