Ohioan

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈhaɪoʊ.ən / (AmE), / əʊˈhaɪəʊ.ən / (BrE)
a native or resident of the state of Ohio in the United States.
native or resident of Ohio

Examples

As an Ohioan, she is proud of her Midwestern roots.
Many Ohioans enjoy outdoor activities, especially during the summer.
The governor invited every Ohioan to participate in the community event.

Etymology

Formed from the state name 'Ohio' with the suffix '-an' to denote origin or association.
