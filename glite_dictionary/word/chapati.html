chapati

1. NOUN

/ tʃəˈpɑti / (AmE), / tʃəˈpɑːti / (BrE)
a thin, round flatbread from the Indian subcontinent, typically cooked on a griddle or skillet
type of flatbread from South Asia

Examples

She served the curry with freshly made chapati on the side.
He learned how to make soft chapatis from his grandmother.
A stack of warm chapati was placed in the center of the table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roti - another type of unleavened Indian flatbread

Etymology

From Hindi चपटी (chapṭī), meaning 'flat'.
