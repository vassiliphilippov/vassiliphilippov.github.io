plonker

1. NOUN

/ ˈplɑŋkər / (AmE), / ˈplɒŋkə / (BrE)
A foolish, silly, or inept person, especially in British informal usage.
foolish or silly person (slang, UK)
slang british

Examples

He made a complete mess of the report—what a plonker!
Stop acting like a plonker and listen carefully.
She called him a plonker for forgetting her birthday.

Synset Examples

Don't be such a plonker, just ask for help.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - a very intelligent or clever person
