pre-game

1. NOUN

/ ˈpriˌɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌɡeɪm / (BrE)
an informal social gathering held before a party or sporting event, often involving drinks or preparation for the main event
gathering before party or game
informal

Examples

We're meeting at Jake's apartment for the pre-game before heading to the concert.
The pre-game at the tailgate was almost as much fun as the football match itself.
She always plans a fun pre-game so everyone can relax and get in the mood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pregame - gathering before a party or event (variant spelling)
