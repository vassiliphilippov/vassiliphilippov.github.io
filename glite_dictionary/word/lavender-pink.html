lavender-pink

1. ADJ

/ ˈlævəndɚ pɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈlævəndə pɪŋk / (BrE)
A pale pink color with a tint of lavender or light purple.
a pink shade tinged with lavender

Examples

She chose a lavender-pink dress for the garden party, which complemented her complexion beautifully.
The walls were painted a soft lavender-pink, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere.
A bouquet of lavender-pink roses added a delicate touch to the centerpiece.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: pink - a pale red color
  • Degree Higher: lavender - a pale purple color
