bobby

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːbi / (AmE), / ˈbɒbi / (BrE)
An informal term for a British police officer.
British police officer (informal)
informal british

Examples

The bobby on the corner directed traffic during the Queen's parade.
When I visited London, I stopped to ask a bobby for directions.
A bobby patrolled the quiet streets in his distinctive helmet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: policeman - a male police officer
  • Synonym: cop - an informal term for police officer

Etymology

From the given name 'Bobby', referring to Sir Robert Peel who founded the Metropolitan Police in 1829.
