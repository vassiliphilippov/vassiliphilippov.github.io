salesroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪlzˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈseɪlzruːm / (BrE)
a room or area where goods, especially cars or other merchandise, are displayed for sale; a showroom
room for displaying goods for sale
british

Examples

The new cars were lined up in the salesroom for customers to inspect.
She spent the afternoon touring the salesroom looking for a good deal on a used car.
After renovations, the furniture salesroom reopened with a fresh, modern look.

Synset Examples

in Britain a showroom is called a ‘salesroom’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: showroom - a room where goods for sale, especially cars or furniture, are displayed
