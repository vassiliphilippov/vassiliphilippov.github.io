blocky

1. ADJ

/ ˈblɑki / (AmE), / ˈblɒki / (BrE)
resembling a block; having a chunky, rectangular, or solid shape or appearance
resembling a block in shape

Examples

The new apartment buildings have a blocky design that stands out in the city skyline.
Her style of painting features blocky shapes and bold colors.
The robot's blocky arms made its movements seem mechanical and awkward.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chunky - thick and solid in form or size; resembling a chunk
  • Antonym: slender - thin or narrow in shape
