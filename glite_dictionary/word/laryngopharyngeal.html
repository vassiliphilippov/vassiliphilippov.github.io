laryngopharyngeal

1. ADJ

/ ləˌrɪŋɡoʊfəˈrɪndʒiəl / (AmE), / ləˌrɪŋɡəʊfəˈrɪndʒɪəl / (BrE)
Relating to both the larynx and the pharynx.
relating to larynx and pharynx
medical

Examples

The surgeon carefully examined the laryngopharyngeal region for any signs of abnormal growth.
Chronic acid reflux can sometimes lead to laryngopharyngeal irritation and discomfort.
New imaging techniques have improved our understanding of laryngopharyngeal disorders.

Etymology

From Greek larynx (voice box) + pharynx (throat) + -eal (relating to).
