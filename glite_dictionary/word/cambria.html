Cambria

1. PROPN

/ ˈkæmbriə / (AmE), / ˈkambrɪə / (BrE)
a literary or historical name for Wales, one of the countries of the United Kingdom
literary name for Wales

Examples

In ancient literature, Cambria is often used to refer to Wales.
The poet wrote an ode to Cambria and its rugged mountains.
Legends of Cambria are found throughout medieval British history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Wales - a country in the United Kingdom, commonly known by this name

Etymology

From Latin 'Cambria', medieval name for Wales, possibly of Celtic origin.
