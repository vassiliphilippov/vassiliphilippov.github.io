whatsis

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌtsɪs / (AmE), / ˈwɒtsɪs / (BrE)
a thing whose name is unknown or has been forgotten; something unspecified
thing with unknown or forgotten name
informal

Examples

I can never remember the name of that whatsis you use to open bottles.
She handed me the little whatsis from the toolbox and hoped it was the right tool.
Do you know where I put the whatsis for the coffee machine?

Synset Examples

she eased the ball-shaped doodad back into its socket
there may be some great new gizmo around the corner that you will want to use

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thingamajig - an object or gadget whose name is unknown or forgotten
  • Synonym: whatchamacallit - a term used for something whose name is forgotten or unknown
  • Synonym: gizmo - a gadget, especially one whose name the speaker does not know or cannot recall

Etymology

From the phrase 'what is it?' combined and used as a single word.
