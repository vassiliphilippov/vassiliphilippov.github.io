divers

1. ADJ

/ ˈdaɪvərz / (AmE), / ˈdaɪvəz / (BrE)
several; various; of different kinds or sorts.
various; several kinds
archaic

Examples

The island was home to divers species of exotic birds.
He is skilled in divers arts, from painting to sculpture.
The committee is composed of divers professionals from across the country.

Synset Examples

tourist offices of divers nationalities
a person of diverse talents

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diverse - showing a great deal of variety; very different

Etymology

From Old French 'divers', from Latin 'diversus' (turned different ways, diverse).
