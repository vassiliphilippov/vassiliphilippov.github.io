harvestfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrvɪstˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːvɪstˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a species of butterfish, up to about a foot long, found in Atlantic waters from Chesapeake Bay to Argentina
an Atlantic species of butterfish

Examples

The crew caught a large harvestfish during their morning trawl off the coast.
Local markets now offer fresh harvestfish, prized for its delicate texture.
Divers observed a school of harvestfish swimming near the coral reefs.
