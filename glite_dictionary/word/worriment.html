worriment

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɜri.mənt / (AmE), / ˈwʌri.mənt / (BrE)
a source of worry or something that causes anxiety or concern
thing that causes worry or anxiety

Examples

Financial problems have been a constant worriment for her family this year.
The new regulations are a major worriment for small business owners.
He spoke openly about the worriment the situation brought into his life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry or unease
  • Synonym: trouble - a cause of inconvenience or distress
  • Antonym: relief - the removal of worry or distress

Etymology

From 'worry' + '-ment', forming a noun indicating the state or product of worrying.
