rim

1. NOUN

/ rɪm / (AmE), / rɪm / (BrE)
the raised or outer edge of a circular object
edge of a circular object

Examples

The coffee cup had a chip on its rim.
She carefully wiped the rim of her wine glass before drinking.
The basketball bounced off the rim and missed the hoop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: edge - the border or boundary of a surface

Etymology

From Old English 'rima' (edge, border).
