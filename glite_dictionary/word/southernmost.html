southernmost

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌðərnˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈsʌðənˌməʊst / (BrE)
Farthest towards the south; located at the most southern point within a particular area.
farthest to the south

Examples

Ushuaia is often described as the southernmost city in the world.
We traveled to the southernmost tip of Africa to see the Cape Agulhas lighthouse.
Their expedition reached the southernmost point ever recorded in that region.

Synset Examples

Key West is the southernmost city in the continental United States

Related Terms

  • Antonym: northernmost - situated farthest north
  • Synonym: easternmost - situated farthest east
  • Synonym: westernmost - situated farthest west

Etymology

Formed from 'southern' + 'most', following the pattern of similar directional superlatives (e.g., northernmost).
