XXIV

1. NUM

/ ɛks ɛks aɪ ˈvi / (AmE), / ɛks ɛks aɪ ˈviː / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for the number 24
Roman numeral for 24

Examples

The clock face was marked with XXIV at the top to indicate midnight.
The chapter titled XXIV was the longest in the book.
He wore a jersey with XXIV on the back, representing his favorite number.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 24 - the cardinal number after 23

Etymology

From Latin Roman numerals: X (10) + X (10) + IV (4).
