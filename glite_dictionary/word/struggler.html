struggler

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrʌɡlər / (AmE), / ˈstrʌɡlə / (BrE)
a person who faces difficulties or makes great effort to overcome challenges
person who faces difficulties

Examples

Despite many setbacks, the struggler never gave up on achieving her dreams.
The documentary tells the inspiring story of a struggler from a small town.
Every successful person was once a struggler fighting through tough times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fighter - a person who fights or resists adversity

Etymology

From English 'struggle' + '-er' suffix, forming agent nouns.
