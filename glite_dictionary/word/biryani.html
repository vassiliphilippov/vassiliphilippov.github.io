biryani

1. NOUN

/ bɪrˈjɑni / (AmE), / bɪərˈjɑːni / (BrE)
an Indian dish of highly seasoned rice cooked with meat, fish, or vegetables
Indian rice dish with meat, fish, or vegetables

Examples

I ordered a spicy chicken biryani for lunch.
She loves to cook vegetable biryani for special occasions.
The aroma of the freshly prepared biryani filled the entire house.

Etymology

From Urdu 'بریانی' (biryānī), from Persian 'بریان' (biryān, 'fried, roasted').
