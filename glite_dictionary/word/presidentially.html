presidentially

1. ADV

/ ˌprɛzɪˈdɛnʃəli / (AmE), / ˌprɛzɪˈdɛnʃəli / (BrE)
In a manner befitting a president; in a way characteristic of a president.
in a presidential manner

Examples

She spoke presidentially during the press conference, commanding respect from all present.
The candidate addressed the issues presidentially, showing composure and authority.
He handled the crisis presidentially, making sound decisions under pressure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: presidential - relating to a president or presidency
