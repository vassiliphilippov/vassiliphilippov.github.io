lxxxviii

1. NUM

/ ˌɛl ˌɛks ˌɛks ˌvi ˈaɪ ˈaɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛl ˌɛks ˌɛks ˌvi ˈaɪ ˈaɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral representing the number eighty-eight (88)
Roman numeral for 88

Examples

The ancient manuscript was dated lxxxviii years after the founding of the city.
He numbered the pages in lxxxviii to match the Roman style of the period.
A total of lxxxviii students graduated that year, according to the official report.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eighty-eight - the number 88

Etymology

From Latin numerals: L = 50, XXX = 30, VIII = 8.
