shirty

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɜːrti / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːti / (BrE)
(British informal) Bad-tempered or easily annoyed, especially over a trivial matter.
easily annoyed; bad-tempered (British informal)
informal british

Examples

He got a bit shirty when I pointed out the mistake he made.
Don’t get shirty with me just because you’re tired.
She tends to become shirty if people disagree with her plans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irritable - easily annoyed or angered
  • Antonym: calm - not showing anger or annoyance; peaceful

Etymology

From British English slang; possibly from 'shirt' in the phrase 'to get shirty' (to get angry), but the exact origin is uncertain.
