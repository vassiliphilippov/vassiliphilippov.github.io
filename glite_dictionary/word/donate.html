to donate

1. VERB

/ ˈdoʊˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈdəʊneɪt / (BrE)
to give money, goods, or time to a charity or good cause, often voluntarily and without expecting anything in return
give to charity or good cause

Examples

Many people donate old clothes to local shelters each winter.
She plans to donate a portion of her salary to cancer research every year.
Hospitals are always seeking volunteers willing to donate blood.

Synset Examples

I donated blood to the Red Cross for the victims of the earthquake
donate money to the orphanage
She donates to her favorite charity every month

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to contribute - to give something, especially money, in order to help achieve or provide something
  • Antonym: to receive - to be given or presented with something

Etymology

From Latin *donare* (to give as a gift).
