clarinettist

1. NOUN

/ kləˈrɪnɪtɪst / (AmE), / kləˈrɪnɪtɪst / (BrE)
A person who plays the clarinet, especially as a professional musician.
person who plays the clarinet

Examples

The orchestra's principal clarinettist performed a stunning solo during the concert.
She has been a professional clarinettist since graduating from the conservatory.
The jazz band hired a talented clarinettist for their summer tour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clarinetist - musician who plays the clarinet (alternate spelling, esp. US)

Etymology

From 'clarinet' + '-ist', following standard English pattern for musicians.
