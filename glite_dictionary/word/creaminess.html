creaminess

1. NOUN

/ ˈkriːmi.nɪs / (AmE), / ˈkriː.mi.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being smooth and thick like cream
smooth, thick quality like cream

Examples

The creaminess of the soup made it feel rich and satisfying.
Yogurt is prized for its creaminess and delicate flavor.
She loved the creaminess of the cheesecake, which melted in her mouth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoothness - the quality of being smooth
  • Antonym: graininess - the quality of having a coarse or rough texture

Etymology

From 'creamy' (adjective) + '-ness' (noun-forming suffix).
