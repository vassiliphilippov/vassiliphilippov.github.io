ataraxis

1. NOUN

/ ˌætəˈræksɪs / (AmE), / ˌatəˈraksɪs / (BrE)
A state of serene calmness; the absence of mental stress or anxiety.
state of serene calmness

Examples

After years of meditation, he finally achieved a sense of ataraxis that nothing could disturb.
The gentle sound of the waves brought her a rare moment of ataraxis.
Many seek ataraxis in the chaos of modern life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tranquility - a state of calm and peace
  • Antonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry or unease

Etymology

From Greek 'ataraxia' (imperturbability, tranquility).
