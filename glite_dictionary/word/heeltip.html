heeltip

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiːl.tɪp / (AmE), / ˈhiːl.tɪp / (BrE)
the bottom piece of a shoe or boot heel, typically made of plastic or metal, that comes into contact with the ground and can be replaced when worn
replaceable bottom of a shoe heel

Examples

She brought her shoes to the cobbler to replace the worn heeltip.
The sound of his footsteps echoed loudly, thanks to the metal heeltip on his boots.
Always check your heeltips regularly to avoid damaging the rest of the heel.

Synset Examples

The bottom of most heels usually has a plastic or metal heel tip that wears away with use and can be easily replaced.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heel - the rear part of the foot or bottom of a shoe
