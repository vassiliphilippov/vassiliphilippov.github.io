cinnamon-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪnəmən ˈkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈsɪnəmən ˈkʌləd / (BrE)
having a light brown color similar to that of ground cinnamon
light brown like cinnamon

Examples

The kitten had a cinnamon-colored coat that gleamed in the sunlight.
She wore a cinnamon-colored scarf that matched her boots perfectly.
The walls were painted a warm cinnamon-colored hue, creating a cozy atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: cinnamon - the spice made from the bark of certain trees, known for its characteristic brown color
