theine

1. NOUN

/ ˈθaɪˌin / (AmE), / ˈθiːiːn / (BrE)
caffeine as occurring specifically in tea, formerly thought to be a distinct compound.
caffeine found in tea

Examples

The levels of theine in green tea are generally lower than in black tea.
Some people avoid theine by choosing herbal infusions instead of traditional tea.
He researched the effects of theine in comparison to those of caffeine in coffee.

Synset Examples

You need a larger dose of theine than caffeine to achieve the same effect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caffeine - a stimulant compound found in coffee, tea, and some other plants

Etymology

From French 'théine', from 'thé' (tea); introduced when caffeine in tea and coffee was thought to differ.
