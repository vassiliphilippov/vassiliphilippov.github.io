genipap

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛnɪˌpæp / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnɪˌpap / (BrE)
A succulent orange-sized tropical fruit with a thick rind, native to tropical America.
tropical fruit with thick rind

Examples

The genipap has a sweet, aromatic flavor that is popular in tropical desserts.
While traveling in Brazil, I tasted a fresh genipap for the first time.
Local markets often sell genipaps alongside other exotic fruits.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'genipapo', from Tupi-Guarani 'jenipapo'.
