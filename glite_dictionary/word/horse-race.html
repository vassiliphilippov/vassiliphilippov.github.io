to horse-race

1. VERB

/ ˈhɔrsˌreɪs / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsˌreɪs / (BrE)
(of a person or horse) to compete in a horse race.
compete in a horse race

Examples

Several stables decided to horse-race their best fillies at the county fair.
He began to horse-race professionally after leaving school.
They have never horse-raced together before this season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: race - to compete in a race
