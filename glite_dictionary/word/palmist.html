palmist

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑlmɪst / (AmE), / ˈpɑːmɪst / (BrE)
A person who claims to foretell the future by interpreting the lines and features of the palms of the hands.
person who reads palms to tell the future

Examples

The palmist studied the lines on my hand before making her prediction.
People often visit a palmist out of curiosity about their future.
He became fascinated with palm reading after meeting a famous palmist in India.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fortuneteller - person who claims to predict the future

Etymology

From 'palm' (the inside surface of the hand) + '-ist' (denoting a person who practices or is concerned with something).
