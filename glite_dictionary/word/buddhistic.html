Buddhistic

1. ADJ

/ bʊˈdɪstɪk / (AmE), / bʊˈdɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Buddhism or its followers
relating to Buddhism

Examples

The museum displayed an impressive collection of Buddhistic art and artifacts.
Some scholars specialize in Buddhistic philosophy and ancient texts.
She decorated her meditation room with Buddhistic symbols and statues.

Synset Examples

Buddhist sculpture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Buddhist - a follower or adherent of Buddhism; also relating to Buddhism

Etymology

Formed by adding the suffix -ic to 'Buddhist,' referring to Buddhism (from Sanskrit Buddha, meaning 'enlightened one').
