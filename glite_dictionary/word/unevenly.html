unevenly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈivənli / (AmE), / ʌnˈiːvənli / (BrE)
in a way that is not equal, regular, or balanced
in an unequal or irregular way

Examples

The paint was applied unevenly, leaving some patches darker than others.
The teachers distributed the supplies unevenly among the students, causing complaints.
After a heavy storm, the ground dried unevenly in the sun.

Synset Examples

profits were distributed unevenly
angry at being dealt with so unequally

Related Terms

  • Antonym: evenly - in a regular or equal way

Etymology

From un- (not) + evenly (adverb from even).

2. ADV

/ ʌnˈivənli / (AmE), / ʌnˈiːvənli / (BrE)
In a way that is not level, equal, or regular; irregularly or inconsistently.
in an irregular or inconsistent way

Examples

The paint was applied unevenly, leaving streaks all over the wall.
Her efforts were distributed unevenly across the different projects.
The cake rose unevenly in the oven, making one side higher than the other.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irregularly - in a way that is not regular or consistent
  • Antonym: smoothly - in an even and regular way

3. ADV

/ ʌnˈivənli / (AmE), / ʌnˈiːvənli / (BrE)
in a way that is not smooth, regular, or equal; in an irregular or ragged manner.
in an irregular or uneven manner

Examples

The paint was applied unevenly, leaving streaks across the wall.
After the earthquake, the ground had shifted unevenly under the houses.
He breathed unevenly as he ran up the steep hill.

Synset Examples

I took the cigarette he offered, drawing at it raggedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irregularly - in a way that is not regular or uniform
  • Antonym: evenly - in a regular or equal manner
