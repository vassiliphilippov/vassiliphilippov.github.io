visually

1. ADV

/ ˈvɪʒuəli / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒuəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to seeing or vision.
in relation to vision or sight

Examples

The new website is visually appealing and easy to navigate.
She is visually impaired and uses a guide dog to get around.
The painting is visually striking because of its bold colors.

Synset Examples

visually distorted

Related Terms

  • Antonym: audibly - in a way that can be heard
  • Synonym: visually impaired - having impaired or limited vision

Etymology

From 'visual' + adverbial suffix '-ly'; 'visual' from Latin 'visualis' (of seeing).
