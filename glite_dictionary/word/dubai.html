Dubai

1. PROPN

/ duˈbaɪ / (AmE), / djuˈbaɪ / (BrE)
a port city and emirate in the United Arab Emirates, located on the southeastern coast of the Persian Gulf and known for its modern architecture, luxury shopping, and vibrant nightlife.
city and emirate in the UAE

Examples

Many tourists visit Dubai each year for its luxury resorts and shopping malls.
The skyline of Dubai features some of the tallest skyscrapers in the world.
He accepted a job offer and moved to Dubai to work in the finance sector.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Abu Dhabi - the capital city and largest emirate of the UAE
