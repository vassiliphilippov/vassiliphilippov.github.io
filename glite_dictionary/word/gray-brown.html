gray-brown

1. ADJ

/ ɡreɪ ˈbraʊn / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈbraʊn / (BrE)
Of a brown color mixed with or tinged by grey.
brown mixed with grey

Examples

The old leather chair had a gray-brown hue that showed its age.
She wore a gray-brown woolen scarf during the chilly winter morning.
A gray-brown bird perched quietly on the stone wall, blending into its surroundings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taupe - a dark grayish brown color
  • Degree Lower: brown - a color resembling that of wood or earth
  • Degree Higher: gray - a color between black and white
