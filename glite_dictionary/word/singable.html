singable

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪŋəbəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋəbəl / (BrE)
(of music) easy or suitable to sing.
easy or suitable to sing

Examples

This is a truly singable song that everyone can enjoy at a party.
The composer focused on crafting a singable melody for the choir.
Her new album is filled with catchy, singable tunes.

Synset Examples

singable melody is the essence of music

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unsingable - not easy or possible to sing
  • Synonym: melodic - having a pleasant tune; tuneful

Etymology

From 'sing' + suffix '-able,' meaning 'capable of being sung.'
