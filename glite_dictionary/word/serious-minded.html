serious-minded

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɪriəsˈmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˌsɪəriəsˈmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
Having a solemn and sincere manner; showing deep thought or seriousness in attitude or actions.
having a solemn, sincere manner

Examples

Her serious-minded approach to her studies impressed her teachers.
As a serious-minded leader, he always puts the team's needs before his own.
People describe Jessica as serious-minded, but she also has a good sense of humor.

Synset Examples

a sensible young man
The serious-minded doctor carefully considered what to do next.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: earnest - showing deep sincerity or seriousness
