Hyperoodon

1. NOUN

/ haɪˈpɪrəˌdɒn / (AmE), / haɪˈpɪərəʊˌdɒn / (BrE)
A genus of large toothed whales known as bottle-nosed whales, found in deep ocean waters.
genus of bottle-nosed whales

Examples

The Hyperoodon genus includes both the northern and the southern bottlenose whale species.
Researchers tracked the migratory patterns of Hyperoodon across the North Atlantic Ocean.
Members of Hyperoodon are known for their bulbous foreheads and deep-diving behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bottlenose whale - a large beaked whale belonging to the genus Hyperoodon

Etymology

From Greek 'hyperoon' (palate) and 'odon' (tooth), referring to features of the whale's skull.
