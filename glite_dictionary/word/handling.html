handling

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhændlɪŋ / (BrE)
the action of carrying, moving, delivering, or working with something, especially with the hands or by mechanical means
the act of carrying or working with something

Examples

Proper handling of fragile items is essential to avoid damage during shipping.
The warehouse invested in machines to improve the handling of heavy goods.
She received training in the safe handling of chemical substances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manipulation - the act of handling or controlling something skillfully
  • Degree Higher: transportation - the act of transporting something from one place to another

Etymology

From handle + -ing; handle from Old English handlian (to touch, manage).

2. NOUN

/ ˈhændlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhændlɪŋ / (BrE)
the action of using the hands or a tool to touch, move, or control something, especially with skill
use or manipulation with the hands or tools

Examples

The delicate handling of the ancient vase ensured it was not damaged.
Proper handling of chemicals is essential for safety in the laboratory.
Her handling of the difficult situation impressed everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manipulation - the action of handling or controlling something in a skillful manner

Etymology

From the verb 'handle' + -ing; 'handle' from Old English 'handlian' (to touch or manage with the hands).

3. NOUN

/ ˈhændlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhændlɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of managing or dealing with someone or something
management or dealing with something

Examples

Proper handling of dangerous substances is required in the laboratory.
The handling of customer complaints improved significantly after the new policies were introduced.
She criticized the government's handling of the situation as inadequate.

Synset Examples

the handling of prisoners
the treatment of water sewage
the right to equal treatment in the criminal justice system

Related Terms

  • Synonym: management - the process of dealing with or controlling things or people
  • Synonym: treatment - the manner in which someone behaves toward or deals with someone or something

From the verb 'handle' + -ing.
