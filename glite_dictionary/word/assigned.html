assigned

1. ADJ

/ əˈsaɪnd / (AmE), / əˈsaɪnd / (BrE)
officially appointed or designated to a particular post, duty, or task
appointed to a duty or post

Examples

Only assigned staff members are allowed access to this secure area.
She completed the assigned readings before the next class discussion.
The assigned officer reported for duty at the designated time.

Synset Examples

assigned personnel
assigned duties

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unassigned - not appointed or allocated to any specific duty or post

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'assign', from Latin 'assignare' (to mark out, allocate).
