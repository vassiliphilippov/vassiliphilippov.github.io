revery

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛvəri / (AmE), / ˈrɛvəri / (BrE)
A state of being lost in one’s thoughts; daydreaming or abstracted absorption.
state of abstraction; daydreaming

Examples

She was lost in a revery, oblivious to the conversation around her.
The quiet afternoon inspired a gentle revery as he stared out the window.
His revery was interrupted by the sudden ring of the telephone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reverie - a state of being pleasantly lost in one's thoughts
  • Synonym: daydream - a series of pleasant thoughts that distract one's attention from the present

Etymology

Variant of 'reverie', from French *rêverie* (daydream, fancy).
