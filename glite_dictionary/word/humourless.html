humourless

1. ADJ

/ ˈhjuːmərləs / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmələs / (BrE)
Lacking a sense of humor; unable to appreciate or express humor.
lacking a sense of humor
british

Examples

The manager's humourless approach made staff meetings tedious and dull.
She remained humourless even when everyone around her was laughing at the joke.
His humourless expression showed he did not appreciate the prank.

Synset Examples

it was a humorless wink; a wink of warning

Related Terms

  • Antonym: humorous - having or showing a sense of humor

Etymology

From 'humour' (chiefly British spelling of 'humor') + '-less' (without).
