goop

1. NOUN

/ ɡup / (AmE), / ɡuːp / (BrE)
any thick, sticky or viscous substance, often unpleasant or messy
thick, sticky substance
informal

Examples

The child smeared goop all over the kitchen table while playing with his art supplies.
I used a special goop to fix the leak in the pipe.
She found some unidentified goop stuck to the bottom of her shoe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gunk - unpleasantly sticky or oily substance
  • Synonym: slime - wet, slippery substance, often viscous

2. NOUN

/ ɡup / (AmE), / ɡuːp / (BrE)
A slang term for gamma hydroxybutyrate (GHB), a powerful depressant drug, especially when referring to its use as a recreational or street drug.
slang term for GHB (drug)
slang

Examples

The club was raided after police found batches of goop hidden in the back room.
Some users mistakenly believe goop is harmless, but its effects can be very dangerous.
Dealers on the street often refer to GHB simply as goop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gamma hydroxybutyrate - a depressant drug, also known as GHB
