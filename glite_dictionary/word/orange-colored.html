orange-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ ˌkʌlərd / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having the color of ripe oranges; of an orange hue.
having an orange color

Examples

She wore an orange-colored dress to the party that really caught everyone's attention.
The sky glowed with orange-colored hues during the sunset.
We painted the walls a soft, orange-colored shade to brighten up the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orange - the color between red and yellow; resembling the fruit's color
  • Degree Lower: red - the color at the end of the visible spectrum next to orange; a primary color
  • Degree Lower: yellow - the color next to orange in the spectrum, between green and orange
