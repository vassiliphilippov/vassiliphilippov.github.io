Sunday

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌnˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.deɪ / (BrE)
The first day of the week, considered a day of rest and worship by most Christians.
the first day of the week

Examples

We always have a family dinner on Sunday.
Many shops are closed on Sunday in some countries.
She enjoys going for a walk every Sunday morning.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Saturday - the day before Sunday
  • Degree Higher: Monday - the day after Sunday

Etymology

From Old English 'Sunnandæg', meaning 'day of the sun', translating Latin 'dies solis'.
