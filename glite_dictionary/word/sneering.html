sneering

1. ADJ

/ ˈsni.rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsniə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
Showing or expressing contempt or scorn, often through facial expression or tone of voice.
showing contempt; scornful

Examples

He gave her a sneering look that made her feel uncomfortable.
Her sneering remarks angered everyone in the room.
His sneering tone made it clear he did not respect their opinions.

Synset Examples

curled his lip in a supercilious smile
spoke in a sneering jeering manner
makes many a sharp comparison but never a mean or snide one

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scornful - showing or feeling scorn or contempt
  • Synonym: supercilious - behaving or looking as though one thinks one is superior to others
  • Antonym: respectful - showing respect or consideration
