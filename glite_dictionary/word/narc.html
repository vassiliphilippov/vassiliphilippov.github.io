narc

1. NOUN

/ nɑrk / (AmE), / nɑːk / (BrE)
a law enforcement officer specializing in the apprehension of drug offenders; a narcotics agent
slang for narcotics officer
slang american

Examples

The young detectives suspected there was a narc in the group.
After the raid, everyone realized Sam had been a narc all along.
Some neighborhoods are wary of anyone they think might be a narc.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undercover cop - police officer working in disguise

Etymology

Shortened from 'narcotics agent.'

2. NOUN

/ nɑrk / (AmE), / nɑːk / (BrE)
a police officer or informant who works undercover to report illegal drug activity; used especially as slang for an informant
police informant (slang)
slang american

Examples

After he told the teacher about the cheating, the other students labeled him a narc at school.
She was unfairly labeled a narc because she refused to join their illegal activities.
Nobody wanted to talk to Tom after he was labeled a narc by the group.

Synset Examples

He got labeled a narc after reporting the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: informer - someone who gives information about others, especially to the police
  • Synonym: snitch - someone who informs on others, especially for personal gain; slang

Etymology

Shortened from 'narcotics agent'.

3. NOUN

/ nɑrk / (AmE), / nɑːk / (BrE)
a person who informs the authorities about others' illegal drug activities, especially as a member of law enforcement or as an informer
informer on drug activities
slang

Examples

He was called a narc after telling the teacher about the students smoking in the bathroom.
Nobody wanted to hang out with her anymore because they thought she was a narc.
Some movies portray the undercover narc as a hero, while others depict them as a villain.

Synset Examples

Don't be such a narc; let us have some fun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snitch - a person who informs on others, especially to the police
  • Synonym: informant - a person who gives information to another, especially to the police
  • Synonym: grass - (British slang) a person who informs the police

Etymology

Shortening of "narcotics agent" or "narcotics officer".
