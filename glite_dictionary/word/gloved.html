gloved

1. ADJ

/ ɡlʌvd / (AmE), / ɡlʌvd / (BrE)
Wearing gloves or having the hands covered with gloves.
wearing gloves

Examples

The surgeon entered the operating room with gloved hands.
He picked up the ancient artifact with gloved fingers to avoid damaging it.
In winter, children can often be seen throwing snowballs with their gloved hands.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ungloved - not wearing gloves

Etymology

From the noun 'glove' plus the adjectival suffix '-ed'.
