ultima

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌltɪmə / (AmE), / ˈʌltɪmə / (BrE)
the last syllable of a word
last syllable of a word

Examples

In the word 'banana,' the ultima is 'na.'
Some languages place stress on the ultima rather than the penult.
Knowing the ultima helps linguists analyze word pronunciation patterns.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: penult - the second-to-last syllable in a word
  • Antonym: antepenult - the third-to-last syllable in a word

Etymology

From Latin 'ultima', feminine of 'ultimus' meaning 'last'.
