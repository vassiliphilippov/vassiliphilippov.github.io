Luxor

1. PROPN

/ ˈlʌk.sɔr / (AmE), / ˈlʌk.sɔː / (BrE)
a city in central Egypt on the east bank of the Nile, known for its proximity to the ruins of ancient Thebes and major archaeological sites
city in central Egypt; tourist site near ancient Thebes

Examples

Many tourists visit Luxor to explore the Valley of the Kings and other ancient monuments.
The temples at Luxor are among the most impressive in all of Egypt.
A cruise down the Nile often includes a stop in Luxor for sightseeing.
