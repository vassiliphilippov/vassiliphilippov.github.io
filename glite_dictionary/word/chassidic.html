Chassidic

1. ADJ

/ hɑːˈsɪdɪk / (AmE), / haˈsɪdɪk / (BrE)
relating to the Jewish Hasidim, their beliefs, practices, or members
of or relating to Hasidic Jews

Examples

She grew up in a Chassidic community in Brooklyn, observing strict religious traditions.
The Chassidic movement places emphasis on joy and mysticism in worship.
Many Chassidic Jews wear distinctive clothing as a sign of their faith.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hasidic - relating to the Hasidim or their beliefs and practices

Etymology

From Hebrew ḥasid (pious), via Yiddish khasidish, relating to the Hasidim.
