pencil-necked

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛnsl nɛkt / (AmE), / ˈpɛnsl nɛkt / (BrE)
(informal) Having a thin and weak-looking neck or appearance, often implying a lack of physical strength.
thin and weak-looking (informal)
informal

Examples

The pencil-necked kid sat quietly at the back of the classroom.
He was always teased for being so pencil-necked during gym class.
Despite his pencil-necked frame, he surprised everyone with his determination.

Synset Examples

The pencil-necked kid was always bullied at school.
Despite being pencil-necked, he was a brilliant coder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scrawny - unattractively thin and weak-looking
  • Synonym: skinny - very thin, especially in an unattractively thin way
