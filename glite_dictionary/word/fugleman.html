fugleman

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuːɡəlmən / (AmE), / ˈfjuːɡlɪmən / (BrE)
A leader, organizer, or spokesman, especially in a political context.
leader or spokesperson, especially political

Examples

The senator was seen as a fugleman of the new reform movement.
She acted as a fugleman for those advocating for environmental change.
In the heated debate, he emerged as the chief fugleman of the opposition.

Synset Examples

a fugleman for the radical right

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spokesperson - a person who speaks for another or on behalf of a group
  • Synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group

Etymology

From German 'Flügelmann' (man in the wing, guide for military formations).
