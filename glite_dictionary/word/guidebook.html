guidebook

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡaɪdˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪd.bʊk / (BrE)
a book that provides information, advice, or instructions, especially about a place, activity, or subject
book giving information or guidance

Examples

I bought a guidebook before visiting Rome to help plan my sightseeing.
The guidebook contains maps and recommendations for restaurants and hotels.
If you are new to birdwatching, this guidebook will be very helpful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manual - a book giving instructions on how to do something
  • Synonym: handbook - a short book giving information or instructions about a particular subject
