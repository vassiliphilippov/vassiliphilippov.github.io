Vedalia

1. NOUN

/ vəˈdeɪliə / (AmE), / vɪˈdeɪliə / (BrE)
A genus of small Australian ladybugs, some species of which have been used in biological control of pests.
genus of Australian ladybugs

Examples

The Vedalia beetle was introduced to California to control pest populations.
Scientists study the Vedalia genus for its effectiveness in natural pest management.
Several species within the Vedalia genus are native to Australia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ladybug - a small beetle, often brightly colored, from the family Coccinellidae
