noncyclical

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˈsɪklɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈsɪklɪkəl / (BrE)
Not occurring in cycles; not characterized by regular repetition or periodicity.
not occurring in cycles

Examples

The company specializes in noncyclical industries like food and healthcare.
Unlike cyclical stocks, noncyclical stocks often perform well during economic downturns.
Education is considered a noncyclical sector because it is not subject to economic fluctuations.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cyclical - occurring in cycles or having a regular pattern

Etymology

Formed from prefix non- (not) + cyclical (from cycle, from Greek kyklos meaning 'circle').
