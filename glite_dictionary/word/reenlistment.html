reenlistment

1. NOUN

/ ˌriːɪnˈlɪstmənt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈlɪstmənt / (BrE)
the act of enlisting again, especially in the military after a previous term of service
act of enlisting again, especially in military

Examples

After his reenlistment, he was stationed overseas.
The army offers bonuses as incentives for reenlistment.
Her decision to pursue reenlistment surprised her family.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: enlistment - the act of enrolling or being enrolled for military service

Etymology

From re- (again) + enlistment (the act of enlisting).
