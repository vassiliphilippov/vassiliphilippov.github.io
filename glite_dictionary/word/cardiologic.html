cardiologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑrdioʊˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɑːdiəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study or practice of cardiology, or to the heart and its diseases
pertaining to cardiology or the heart
medical

Examples

The doctor reviewed the cardiologic evidence before making a diagnosis.
New cardiologic treatments have improved the survival rate for heart patients.
A cardiologic examination was scheduled for the patient with chest pain.

Synset Examples

cardiologic evidence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cardiology - the branch of medicine dealing with the heart

Etymology

From 'cardiology' (from Greek kardia 'heart' + -logia 'study of') + -ic suffix forming adjectives.
