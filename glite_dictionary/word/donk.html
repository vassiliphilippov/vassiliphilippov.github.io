donk

1. NOUN

/ dɑŋk / (AmE), / dɒŋk / (BrE)
a forceful knock, hit, or thump that is loud or distinct
loud, forceful knock or hit
informal

Examples

There was a donk on the wall as someone kicked the ball inside the house.
She heard a loud donk at the door and hurried to answer it.
The mechanic dropped a wrench and it landed with a heavy donk on the concrete.

Synset Examples

He gave a loud donk on the door to get attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thump - a heavy dull sound, such as that made by an object falling or a blow
  • Synonym: knock - a sharp, forceful sound made by striking something
