self-whispered

1. ADJ

/ sɛlfˈwɪspɚd / (AmE), / sɛlfˈwɪspəd / (BrE)
spoken or uttered in a whisper to oneself; as if whispered privately to one's own ears
spoken in a whisper to oneself

Examples

She said the words in a self-whispered tone, barely audible to anyone else.
His self-whispered confession revealed more than he intended.
A self-whispered reminder helped her stay calm during the meeting.
