moldiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊldinəs / (AmE), / ˈməʊldinəs / (BrE)
the quality of smelling or tasting old, stale, or covered with mold
quality of being moldy or stale

Examples

The moldiness of the bread was obvious as soon as I opened the bag.
After being stored for years, the basement's moldiness was overwhelming.
He complained about the moldiness of the old cheese in the refrigerator.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mustiness - quality of smelling stale, damp, or moldy
  • Antonym: freshness - the quality of being fresh, clean, and new

Etymology

From 'moldy' + '-ness', indicating the state or quality of being moldy.
