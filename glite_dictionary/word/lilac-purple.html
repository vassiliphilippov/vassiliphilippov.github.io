lilac-purple

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪlək ˈpɜrpl̩ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪlək ˈpɜːpl / (BrE)
Having a shade of purple tinged with lilac; a light, slightly pinkish or bluish-purple colour.
purple shade tinged with lilac

Examples

The walls of the nursery were painted a soft lilac-purple to create a calming atmosphere.
She wore a stunning lilac-purple dress to the spring wedding.
A bouquet of lilac-purple flowers brightened up the kitchen table.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: purple - a colour intermediate between red and blue
  • Degree Higher: lilac - a pale purple colour
