indigestible

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛstəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛstɪbəl / (BrE)
(of food or substances) difficult or impossible to digest.
difficult to digest

Examples

Some foods are completely indigestible for certain people with food intolerances.
The meal was heavy and indigestible, leaving everyone feeling uncomfortable.
Raw cabbage can be quite indigestible if not prepared properly.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: digestible - able to be digested easily

Etymology

From Latin 'in-' (not) + 'digestibilis' (digestible).
