tinting

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of adding a slight amount of color to something
the act of adding color

Examples

The stylist recommended a gentle tinting to enhance the natural shade of her hair.
Window tinting can help reduce glare and keep your car cooler in summer.
The old photographs had a subtle tinting that gave them a nostalgic feel.

Synset Examples

the hairdresser gave her hair a modest tinting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coloring - the act of applying color to something
  • Synonym: staining - the act of coloring or dyeing materials in a way that is usually more permanent or intense

Etymology

From "tint" (from Latin *tingere*, to dye or color).
