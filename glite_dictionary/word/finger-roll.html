finger-roll

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪŋɡɚ ˌroʊl / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə ˌrəʊl / (BrE)
a basketball shot made by gently rolling the ball off the tips of the fingers, typically in a high arc toward the basket
basketball shot using fingertip roll

Examples

He executed a beautiful finger-roll over the outstretched hands of the defender.
The rookie showed off his smooth finger-roll during the second quarter.
With a quick drive and a finger-roll, she put her team ahead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: layup - a close-range shot taken after driving to the basket
