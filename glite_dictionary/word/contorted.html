contorted

1. ADJ

/ kənˈtɔrtɪd / (AmE), / kənˈtɔːtɪd / (BrE)
Twisted out of its normal shape, often because of pain, struggle, or distortion.
twisted out of normal shape

Examples

His face was contorted with pain after the accident.
She gave a contorted grin that showed her discomfort.
The metal bar became contorted in the intense heat of the fire.

Synset Examples

his mad contorted smile
writhed lips
my writhen features

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twisted - forced out of normal shape by bending or turning
  • Synonym: distorted - pulled or twisted out of shape

Etymology

From Latin 'contortus', past participle of 'contorquere' (to twist together).
