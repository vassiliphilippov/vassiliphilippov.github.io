why

1. NOUN

/ waɪ / (AmE), / waɪ / (BrE)
the reason or cause for something; an explanation, especially when asking for or considering the underlying motive or cause of an action or situation
reason or explanation (noun)

Examples

We spent hours discussing the whys of his decision.
She never explained the why behind her sudden departure.
The report explores the whys and hows of climate change.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reason - an explanation or cause for something
  • Synonym: wherefore - the reason or purpose for something
