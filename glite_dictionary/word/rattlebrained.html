rattlebrained

1. ADJ

/ ˈrætlˌbreɪnd / (AmE), / ˈræt.əl.breɪnd / (BrE)
Lacking good sense or discretion; scatterbrained or foolish.
lacking good sense; scatterbrained

Examples

Her rattlebrained ideas often got her into trouble at work.
It's hard to make progress in a meeting with so many rattlebrained suggestions.
He admitted his rattlebrained behavior was due to lack of sleep.

Synset Examples

his rattlebrained crackpot ideas
how rattlepated I am! I've forgotten what I came for

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scatterbrained - disorganized and lacking concentration
  • Antonym: sensible - showing good sense

Etymology

Formed from 'rattle' (to move quickly with repeated sounds) and 'brained', suggesting someone whose mind is noisy or incoherent.
