Vinylite

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪnəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈvɪnɪlaɪt / (BrE)
a trademarked name for any of various synthetic resin materials made from polymers of vinyl chloride or similar compounds, used especially in plastics and coatings
synthetic vinyl resin, used in plastics and coatings

Examples

The laboratory ordered a supply of Vinylite for the new batch of plastic components.
Records made from Vinylite became popular in the early 20th century due to their durability.
She specialized in the analysis of Vinylite materials for industrial applications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polyvinyl chloride - a common synthetic plastic polymer made from vinyl chloride

Etymology

Formed from 'vinyl' and the suffix '-ite' (as in other plastics trade names).
