zestful

1. ADJ

/ ˈzɛstfəl / (AmE), / ˈzɛstfʊl / (BrE)
full of energy and enthusiasm; marked by spirited enjoyment.
full of enthusiasm or energy

Examples

Her zestful laughter was infectious and brightened the entire room.
He gave a zestful performance that captivated the audience from start to finish.
The chef's zestful approach to cooking made every meal exciting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enthusiastic - showing intense and eager enjoyment or interest
  • Antonym: listless - lacking energy or enthusiasm

Etymology

From 'zest' (enthusiasm or energy), ultimately from French 'zeste'.
