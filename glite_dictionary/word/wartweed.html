wartweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔrtˌwid / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtˌwiːd / (BrE)
a European weed with flowers that turn toward the sun; sometimes considered not unattractive
European weed with sun-facing flowers

Examples

The field was covered with wartweed, its flowers facing the morning sun.
Gardeners often remove wartweed from their lawns, though some find its blooms attractive.
Local folklore once claimed that wartweed could be used to treat minor skin irritations.
