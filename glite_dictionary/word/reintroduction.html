reintroduction

1. NOUN

/ ˌriː.ɪn.trəˈdʌk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌriː.ɪn.trəˈdʌk.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of introducing something or someone again, especially after a period of absence or withdrawal
the act of introducing again

Examples

The reintroduction of wolves to the park changed the ecosystem dramatically.
After years of absence, the reintroduction of the show was met with excitement.
The reintroduction of vintage fashion has become a major trend in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: introduction - the act of bringing something into use or operation for the first time

Etymology

From re- (again) + introduction (from Latin introductio)
