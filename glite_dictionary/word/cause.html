cause

1. NOUN

/ kɔz / (AmE), / kɔːz / (BrE)
A person, event, or thing that brings about a result or makes something happen; the reason for an action or condition.
origin or reason for something happening

Examples

Investigators could not find the cause of the mysterious fire.
Stress is often cited as a cause of illness.
The heavy rain was the main cause of flooding in the city.

Synset Examples

they are trying to determine the cause of the crash

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reason - a basis or explanation for something
  • Antonym: effect - a result or consequence produced by a cause

Etymology

From Latin causa (reason, motive, occasion).
