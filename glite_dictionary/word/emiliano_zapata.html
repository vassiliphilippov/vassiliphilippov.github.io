Emiliano Zapata

1. PROPN

/ eɪˌmiːliˈɑːnoʊ zəˈpɑːtə / (AmE), / eɪˌmiːliˈɑːnə zəˈpɑːtə / (BrE)
(1879–1919) A Mexican revolutionary leader who fought for agrarian reforms and played a key role in the Mexican Revolution.
Mexican revolutionary leader (1879–1919)

Examples

The influence of Emiliano Zapata can still be seen in Mexican social movements today.
Many people respect Emiliano Zapata for fighting for land and farmers' rights.
A statue of Emiliano Zapata stands proudly in the center of Cuernavaca.
