sixthly

1. ADV

/ ˈsɪksθli / (AmE), / ˈsɪksθli / (BrE)
Used to introduce the sixth item in a list or sequence of statements.
in the sixth place; sixth in a sequence

Examples

Sixthly, we need to address the issue of funding for the project.
The report was organized methodically: firstly, secondly, and so on, until sixthly.
Sixthly, remember to check all the data for accuracy before submission.

Synset Examples

sixthly, we cannot afford a vacation

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fifthly - in the fifth place in a list or sequence
  • Degree Higher: seventhly - in the seventh place in a list or sequence

Etymology

Formed from "sixth" + "-ly" suffix, following the pattern of ordinal adverbs.
