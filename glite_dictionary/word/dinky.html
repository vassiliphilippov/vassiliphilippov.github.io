dinky

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈdɪŋki / (BrE)
small and unimpressive; not attractive or of little value.
small and unimpressive

Examples

We were disappointed to find our room was just a dinky little box at the end of the hallway.
She lives in a dinky apartment above a café in the city center.
The company gave their employees a dinky bonus that barely covered lunch.

3. NOUN

/ ˈdɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈdɪŋki / (BrE)
A small locomotive, especially one used for light or local railway tasks.
small locomotive

Examples

The old dinky hauled coal cars through the narrow mountain tunnels.
Rail enthusiasts gathered to photograph the vintage dinky at the local rail yard.
During construction, a dinky was used to move materials along the temporary tracks.
