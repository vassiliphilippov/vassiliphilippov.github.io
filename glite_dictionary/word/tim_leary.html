Tim Leary

1. PROPN

/ tɪm ˈlɪəri / (AmE), / tɪm ˈlɪəri / (BrE)
(1920–1996) United States psychologist known for his research and advocacy of psychedelic drugs, especially LSD.
American psychologist and LSD advocate

Examples

Tim Leary became famous for his promotion of LSD and psychedelic research in the 1960s.
Many people view Tim Leary as a controversial figure in the history of psychology.
The counterculture movement often cited Tim Leary as an inspiration for their exploration of consciousness.
