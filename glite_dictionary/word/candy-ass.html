candy-ass

1. NOUN

/ ˈkændi æs / (AmE), / ˈkændi æs / (BrE)
a person regarded as weak, timid, or lacking courage, often used as an insult
derogatory slang for a weak or cowardly person
slang offensive

Examples

He got called a candy-ass after refusing to join the game in the rain.
Stop being such a candy-ass and stand up for yourself.
They teased him as a candy-ass because he didn't want to try the spicy food.

Synset Examples

She thought I was a candy-ass for skipping the gym.
Don’t be such a candy-ass; just try it.
He called me a candy-ass for not going skydiving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage
  • Synonym: wimp - a weak or ineffectual person
  • Antonym: brave - showing courage
