harp-shaped

1. ADJ

/ hɑrp ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / hɑːp ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
shaped like or resembling a harp.
in the shape of a harp

Examples

The museum displayed a harp-shaped sculpture made from bronze.
She admired the harp-shaped earrings in the jewelry store window.
They commissioned a harp-shaped sign for the entrance to the music academy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harp - a large musical instrument with strings stretched upright in a frame
