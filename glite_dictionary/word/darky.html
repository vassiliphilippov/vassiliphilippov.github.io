darky

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑːrki / (AmE), / ˈdɑːki / (BrE)
(Offensive) An ethnic slur for a Black person; strongly offensive and should not be used.
offensive slur for a Black person
offensive

Examples

The use of the word darky is considered highly offensive and is unacceptable in modern language.
The novel was criticized for its repeated references to darky characters, reflecting the prejudices of its time.
Historians discuss how terms like darky reveal the racism embedded in certain periods of literature.

Etymology

Diminutive form of 'dark', referencing skin color. Used as a slur from the 19th century onward.
