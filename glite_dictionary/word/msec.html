msec

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛm sɛk / (AmE), / ˈɛm sɛk / (BrE)
An abbreviation for millisecond; one thousandth (10^-3) of a second.
abbreviation for millisecond
medical

Examples

The reaction time was measured at 120 msec.
Each process lasts only a few msec.
Signal transmission occurs in less than 10 msec.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: millisecond - One thousandth of a second

Etymology

Abbreviation of millisecond, from milli- (thousandth) and second.
