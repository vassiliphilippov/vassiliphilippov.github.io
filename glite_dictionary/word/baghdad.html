Baghdad

1. PROPN

/ bæɡˈdæd / (AmE), / bæɡˈdæd / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Iraq, situated on the Tigris River
capital city of Iraq

Examples

Many ancient manuscripts are preserved in Baghdad's National Library.
After months of planning, they finally traveled to Baghdad for a business conference.
The Tigris River flows through the heart of Baghdad, shaping its landscape and history.

Synset Examples

Baghdad is one of the great cities of the Muslim world

Etymology

From Arabic 'Baghdād', possibly from Persian 'Bagh' (God) and 'dād' (given), meaning 'God-given'.
