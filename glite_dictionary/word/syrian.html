Syrian

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪriən / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriən / (BrE)
relating to Syria, its people, or its culture
from or relating to Syria

Examples

The Syrian cuisine is famous for its variety of flavors and ingredients.
International organizations are working to help Syrian refugees displaced by the conflict.
She admired the traditional Syrian architecture in the old city of Damascus.

Synset Examples

the Syrian government

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Syrian - a person from Syria

Etymology

From Syria + -an, denoting belonging or relating to.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03028032-a
Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɪriən / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Syria
native or inhabitant of Syria

Examples

The refugee camp provided shelter for hundreds of displaced Syrians.
As a proud Syrian, she shared stories about her homeland.
Many Syrians celebrate their cultural heritage through music and cuisine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Syrian - of or relating to Syria, its people, or their language (adjective)

Etymology

From Syria, the name of the country, + -an (denoting nationality).

WordNet ID: oewn-09752891-n
Synset POS: n
