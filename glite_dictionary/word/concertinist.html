concertinist

1. NOUN

/ kɑn.sɚˈtiː.nɪst / (AmE), / kɒn.səˈtiː.nɪst / (BrE)
a person who plays the concertina, a small musical instrument with bellows and buttons
person who plays the concertina

Examples

The renowned concertinist captivated the audience with her intricate melodies.
After years of practice, he became the principal concertinist in the folk band.
A skilled concertinist can evoke a wide range of emotions through their music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accordionist - a person who plays the accordion

Etymology

From 'concertina' + '-ist', following the pattern of musician-related nouns.
