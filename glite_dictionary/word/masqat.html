Masqat

1. PROPN

/ məsˈkɑt / (AmE), / mʌsˈkæt / (BrE)
the capital city and main port of Oman, located on the Gulf of Oman
capital city of Oman

Examples

The historic port of Masqat has long been a hub of trade in the region.
Tourists are drawn to Masqat for its vibrant markets and stunning coastline.
Oman’s government is based in Masqat, making it the country’s political center.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Muscat - alternative spelling and common form for the capital city of Oman

Etymology

From Arabic مَسْقَط‎ (Masqaṭ). The more common English spelling is 'Muscat.'
