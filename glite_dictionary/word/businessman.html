businessman

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznɪsˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪsmən / (BrE)
A man who works in business, especially one with an important position in a company or who owns a business.
a man involved in business

Examples

He is a successful businessman who owns several companies.
The conference was attended by every leading businessman in the city.
As a young businessman, he quickly learned the value of networking.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: businesswoman - a woman engaged in business
  • Synonym: entrepreneur - a person who organizes and operates a business, taking risks

Etymology

From 'business' + 'man', formed in English in the 19th century.
