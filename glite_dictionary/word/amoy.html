Amoy

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɔɪ / (AmE), / əˈmɔɪ / (BrE)
a dialect of Chinese spoken in the southern part of Fujian province, China, and by Chinese communities in Southeast Asia
dialect of Chinese spoken in Fujian, China

Examples

He grew up speaking Amoy with his family in Xiamen.
The Amoy dialect is widely used among overseas Chinese in Southeast Asia.
She studied Amoy to communicate better with her grandparents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hokkien - another name for the Southern Min group of Chinese dialects, including Amoy

Etymology

From Amoy, a former name for the city of Xiamen in Fujian, China, via Portuguese usage.
