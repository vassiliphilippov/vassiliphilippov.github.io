Basotho

1. NOUN

/ bɑˈsoʊtoʊ / (AmE), / bɑˈsəʊtəʊ / (BrE)
a member of the Sotho ethnic group, especially one from Lesotho
a person from the Sotho group in Lesotho

Examples

Many Basotho work in neighboring South Africa to support their families.
The Basotho have a rich tradition of music and storytelling.
He learned about the culture and customs of the Basotho during his travels in Lesotho.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mosotho - a single person from the Sotho people
  • Synonym: Lesotho - the country inhabited by the Basotho people

Etymology

From Sesotho, the native language of the Sotho people, meaning 'people of the south'.
