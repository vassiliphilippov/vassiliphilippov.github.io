arenicolous

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛrɪˈnɪkələs / (AmE), / ˌærɪˈnɪkələs / (BrE)
(of an organism) living, growing, or burrowing in sand.
living or growing in sand

Examples

Many species of arenicolous worms can be found on sandy beaches.
The arenicolous beetle is uniquely adapted to survive in desert sands.
These arenicolous plants have roots that spread out just beneath the surface.

Synset Examples

arenicolous worms

Etymology

From Latin 'arena' (sand) + 'colous' (inhabiting, dwelling).
