mockernut

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑkərˌnʌt / (AmE), / ˈmɒkənʌt / (BrE)
A North American hickory tree (Carya tomentosa) with smooth bark, 7 to 9 leaflets per leaf, and a hard-shelled edible nut.
type of North American hickory tree

Examples

The forest was dominated by towering mockernut trees, their nuts scattered across the ground.
Squirrels eagerly gather the hard-shelled mockernut in autumn.
The leaves of the mockernut have seven to nine distinct leaflets.
