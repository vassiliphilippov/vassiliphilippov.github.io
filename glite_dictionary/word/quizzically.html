quizzically

1. ADV

/ ˈkwɪzɪkli / (AmE), / ˈkwɪzɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows mild amusement, puzzlement, or curiosity; in a quizzical and questioning way.
in a quizzical or questioning manner

Examples

She raised an eyebrow and looked quizzically at her friend, waiting for an explanation.
The teacher smiled quizzically after hearing the student's unusual answer.
He glanced quizzically at the painting, unsure of what it was meant to represent.

Synset Examples

they looked quizzically at the doctor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inquisitively - in a curious or questioning manner

Etymology

From 'quizzical' + suffix '-ly'. 'Quizzical' ultimately from 'quiz' (odd person, question).
