emphysematous

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛm.fəˈzim.ə.təs / (AmE), / ˌɛm.fɪˈziː.mə.təs / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling emphysema; affected by or characteristic of emphysema.
relating to or resembling emphysema
medical

Examples

The x-ray revealed emphysematous changes in the patient's lungs.
Some bacteria can cause emphysematous infections in the urinary bladder.
His emphysematous condition required frequent monitoring by a pulmonary specialist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emphysema - chronic lung disease caused by over-inflation of alveoli

Etymology

From emphysema + -ous, from Greek *emphysēma* (inflation) and suffix *-ous* meaning 'pertaining to'.
