roughish

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrʌfɪʃ / (BrE)
Somewhat rough; having a mildly uneven or coarse surface or quality.
somewhat rough in texture or quality

Examples

After sanding the wood, the table still felt roughish to the touch.
The road was roughish, but we managed to drive through it safely.
She wore a jacket made from a roughish fabric that was durable yet comfortable.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: rough - having an uneven or coarse surface; not smooth or soft
  • Antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; free from perceptible projections, lumps, or indentations
