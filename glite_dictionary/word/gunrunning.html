gunrunning

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌnˌrʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnˌrʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
the secret and illegal transportation or smuggling of guns and ammunition into a country
illegal smuggling of firearms

Examples

The authorities uncovered a massive gunrunning operation at the docks.
He was arrested for his involvement in international gunrunning.
Efforts to stop gunrunning in the region have increased in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arms trafficking - the illegal trade or smuggling of weapons
  • Synonym: drug trafficking - the illegal trade or smuggling of drugs

Etymology

From 'gun' + 'running', after 'rum-running' (prohibition-era smuggling).
