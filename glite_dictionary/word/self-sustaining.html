self-sustaining

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlf səˈsteɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf səˈsteɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
able to maintain or support oneself or itself without outside assistance
able to support itself independently

Examples

The community established a self-sustaining garden that produces enough food for all its members.
After years of support, the organization finally became self-sustaining and no longer relied on external funding.
Solar panels have made the cabin almost completely self-sustaining in terms of energy.

Synset Examples

a self-sufficing economic unit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-sufficient - able to provide for oneself without the help of others
