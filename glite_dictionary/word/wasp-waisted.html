wasp-waisted

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɑsp ˌweɪstɪd / (AmE), / ˌwɒsp ˈweɪstɪd / (BrE)
(of a person or figure) having a markedly small or narrow waist in proportion to the rest of the body.
having a very small or narrow waist

Examples

The model's wasp-waisted silhouette captured everyone's attention at the fashion show.
Victorian-era corsets were designed to give women a wasp-waisted appearance.
His drawing depicted a wasp-waisted heroine in elaborate costume.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slender - gracefully thin; having a slim figure
  • Antonym: thick-waisted - having a thick or wide waist

Etymology

From the resemblance of a small waist to that of a wasp's segmented body; 'wasp' + 'waisted.'
