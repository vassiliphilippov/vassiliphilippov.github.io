Edward VI of England

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛd.wɚd sɪksθ əv ˈɪŋ.glənd / (AmE), / ˈɛd.wəd sɪksθ əv ˈɪŋ.glənd / (BrE)
the King of England and Ireland from 1547 to 1553, son of Henry VIII and Jane Seymour, who died at age 15
King of England (1547–1553)

Examples

Edward VI of England ascended the throne in 1547 at the age of nine.
The reign of Edward VI of England marked significant religious changes in the country.
Historians debate the influence of Edward VI of England on the English Reformation.
