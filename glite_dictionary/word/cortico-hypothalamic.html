cortico-hypothalamic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɔrtɪkoʊ haɪpoʊθəˈlæmɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɔːtɪkəʊ haɪpəʊθəˈlamɪk / (BrE)
Relating to both the cortex and the hypothalamus of the brain.
concerning cortex and hypothalamus
medical

Examples

Research shows that cortico-hypothalamic pathways play a vital role in regulating emotional responses.
The disruption of cortico-hypothalamic connections may lead to hormonal imbalances.
Scientists are investigating cortico-hypothalamic interactions to better understand stress-related disorders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cortical - relating to the cortex of the brain
  • Synonym: hypothalamic - relating to the hypothalamus

Etymology

From Latin cortex (bark, outer layer) and hypothalamus, combining forms used in medical terminology.
