enthusiast

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈθuziˌæst / (AmE), / ɪnˈθjuːziæst / (BrE)
a person who is highly interested in and passionate about a particular activity, subject, or hobby
person passionate about something

Examples

As a music enthusiast, she attends concerts almost every weekend.
The photography enthusiast spent hours capturing the perfect shot at the park.
He's an automotive enthusiast with a passion for restoring vintage cars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fan - a person who is very interested in and devoted to something
  • Antonym: dilettante - a person who takes up an art, activity, or subject merely for amusement

Etymology

From Greek enthousiasmos (inspiration, possession by a god), via French enthousiaste and Latin enthusiasta.

2. NOUN

/ ɪnˈθuziˌæst / (AmE), / ɪnˈθjuːziæst / (BrE)
a person who is very interested in and passionate about a particular activity, subject, or person
person with keen interest or passion

Examples

As a music enthusiast, she attends concerts almost every weekend.
The club is full of photography enthusiasts who share tips and techniques.
A car enthusiast will spend hours restoring classic vehicles in their garage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fan - a person who has a strong interest in or admiration for a person or thing

Etymology

From Greek 'enthousiastēs' (a person inspired by a god), from 'enthousiazein' (to be inspired).
