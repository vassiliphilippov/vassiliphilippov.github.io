standoffish

1. ADJ

/ ˌstændˈɒfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˌstændˈɒfɪʃ / (BrE)
Not friendly or willing to engage with others; distant or reserved in manner.
unfriendly; reserved in manner

Examples

She can seem quite standoffish at first, but she warms up once you get to know her.
His standoffish demeanor made it hard for new colleagues to approach him.
Although he was polite, his standoffish attitude discouraged any real conversation.

Synset Examples

a standoffish manner

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aloof - not friendly or forthcoming; cool and distant
  • Antonym: approachable - friendly and easy to talk to

Etymology

Formed from 'stand off' plus the suffix '-ish', meaning having the quality of standing apart.
