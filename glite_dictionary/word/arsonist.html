arsonist

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːr.sən.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈɑː.sən.ɪst / (BrE)
a person who illegally sets fire to property on purpose
person who sets property on fire illegally

Examples

The police arrested the arsonist just hours after the warehouse fire was reported.
Firefighters believe an arsonist is responsible for a string of recent blazes in the city.
The building was destroyed by a fire started by an unknown arsonist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firebug - person who deliberately sets fires, especially as a crime

Etymology

From arson + -ist, with 'arson' from Old French arsion, from Latin ardēre (to burn).
