montane

1. ADJ

/ mɑnˈteɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɒn.teɪn / (BrE)
(of plants, animals, or climates) inhabiting or relating to mountainous regions, especially those below the tree line.
inhabiting mountainous regions

Examples

The park is home to many montane species of birds and mammals.
Scientists studied the effects of climate change on montane forests.
These flowers are typical of montane habitats in the Himalayas.

Synset Examples

montane flowers

Etymology

From Latin 'montanus' (of a mountain), from 'mons' (mountain).
