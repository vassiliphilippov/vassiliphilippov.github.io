councilorship

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊn.sə.lɚ.ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.sɪ.lə.ʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or office of being a member of a council
position of council member

Examples

He aspired to councilorship after years of community service.
Her councilorship brought significant changes to the local school system.
The duties of councilorship include attending all town meetings and voting on important matters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: councilor - a member of a council
  • Degree Higher: mayorship - the position of mayor

Etymology

From "councilor" + "-ship", indicating status or position.
