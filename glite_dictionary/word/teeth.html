teeth

1. NOUN

/ tiθ / (AmE), / tiːθ / (BrE)
the kind, number, and arrangement of teeth collectively in a person or animal
the collective arrangement of teeth

Examples

The veterinarian examined the horse's teeth to estimate its age.
Dentists can learn about diet and health from the teeth of ancient humans.
Sharks continually replace their teeth throughout their lives.
