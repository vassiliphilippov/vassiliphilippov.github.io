one-seeded

1. ADJ

/ ˌwʌnˈsiːdɪd / (AmE), / ˌwʌnˈsiːdɪd / (BrE)
(of a fruit or plant) containing a single seed.
having a single seed

Examples

Avocado is an example of a one-seeded fruit.
The one-seeded pods help botanists identify certain species of plants.
Each berry produced by the shrub was one-seeded and glossy.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: many-seeded - containing many seeds
