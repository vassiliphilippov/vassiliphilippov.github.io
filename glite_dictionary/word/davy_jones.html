Davy Jones

1. NOUN

/ ˌdeɪvi ˈdʒoʊnz / (AmE), / ˌdeɪvi ˈdʒəʊnz / (BrE)
a mythical place at the bottom of the sea, regarded as the burial place of drowned sailors
mythical sea grave or ocean bottom

Examples

Superstitious sailors believed that drowned men ended up in Davy Jones.
The ship sank, taking its secrets to Davy Jones.
Legends say the treasure was lost forever in Davy Jones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Davy Jones's locker - the figurative place at the bottom of the sea where drowned sailors go

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from the name of a legendary sailor or a nautical slang term dating from the 18th century.
