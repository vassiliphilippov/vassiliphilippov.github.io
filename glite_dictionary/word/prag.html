Prag

1. PROPN

/ prɑːk / (AmE), / prɑːk / (BrE)
The German name for Prague, the capital city of the Czech Republic.
German name for Prague

Examples

Many German tourists refer to Prag instead of Prague when discussing the city.
On older European maps, you may find the city labeled as Prag.
The composer Mozart had several works premiered in Prag during his lifetime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Prague - Capital city of the Czech Republic

Etymology

German form of 'Prague,' from Czech 'Praha'.
