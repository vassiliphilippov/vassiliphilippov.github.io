Procardia

1. NOUN

/ proʊˈkɑːrdiə / (AmE), / prəʊˈkɑːdiə / (BrE)
A trademarked name for nifedipine, a calcium channel blocker used to treat high blood pressure and angina.
trade name for a calcium channel blocker drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Procardia to help manage her high blood pressure.
Some patients may experience side effects when taking Procardia for angina.
You should not stop taking Procardia without consulting your physician.
