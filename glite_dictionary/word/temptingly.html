temptingly

1. ADV

/ ˈtɛmptɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtɛmptɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is likely to attract or entice someone; in a manner that tempts.
in an enticing or attractive manner

Examples

The aroma of freshly baked bread wafted temptingly through the open window.
She smiled temptingly at her dinner guests as she unveiled the dessert.
The offer was presented so temptingly that it was hard to refuse.

Synset Examples

she smiled at him seductively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seductively - in a seductive or alluring manner
  • Synonym: alluringly - in an attractive or appealing way

Etymology

From 'tempting' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
