edu-babble

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛdʒuˌbæbəl / (AmE), / ˈɛdjuːˌbæbəl / (BrE)
educational jargon or technical talk that is confusing, verbose, or lacks clear meaning
meaningless educational jargon or talk
informal

Examples

The teacher's feedback was full of edu-babble that left the students puzzled.
She tried to avoid using edu-babble when explaining the new policy.
Let's cut the edu-babble and focus on practical solutions.

Synset Examples

He couldn't stand the edu-babble during the presentation.
The meeting was filled with edu-babble that confused everyone.
The report was just edu-babble without real insights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jargon - special words or expressions used by a particular group; technical language
  • Synonym: gibberish - meaningless or unintelligible language
  • Antonym: plain English - language that is clear and easy to understand

Etymology

Blend of 'education' and 'babble' (meaning trivial or foolish talk).
