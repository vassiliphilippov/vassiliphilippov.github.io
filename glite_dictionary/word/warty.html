warty

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɔrti / (AmE), / ˈwɔːti / (BrE)
(of skin or a surface) covered with warts or with projections resembling warts.
covered with warts or wart-like bumps

Examples

The frog's warty skin helps it blend into the forest floor.
She was concerned about a warty patch that appeared on her hand.
The warty surface of the pumpkin made it stand out at the market.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wartless - (of skin or a surface) having no warts or wart-like projections

Etymology

From 'wart' + '-y', where '-y' forms adjectives meaning 'characterized by'.
