owned

1. ADJ

/ oʊnd / (AmE), / əʊnd / (BrE)
having an owner; used to describe something that is possessed, often in combination with another word (e.g., state-owned, privately owned)
having an owner; possessed

Examples

The owned portion of the land was marked with clear boundary signs.
Several government-owned companies have recently been privatized.
All owned vehicles must be registered with local authorities.

Synset Examples

state-owned railways

Related Terms

  • Synonym: possessed - owned; held as property
  • Antonym: unowned - not having an owner
