pupal

1. ADJ

/ ˈpjuːpəl / (AmE), / ˈpjuːpəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a pupa, the life stage of some insects between larva and adult.
relating to the pupa stage of insects

Examples

The butterfly remains in its pupal form for several days before emerging as an adult.
Researchers studied the effects of temperature on the pupal development of moths.
In many insects, the pupal stage is a time of significant transformation.

Synset Examples

the pupal stage

Related Terms

  • Antonym: larval - relating to the larva stage of insects
  • Degree Higher: adult - fully grown, mature stage of insects

Etymology

From Latin 'pupa' (girl, doll, pupa).
