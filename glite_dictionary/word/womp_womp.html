womp womp

1. INTJ

/ wɑmp wɑmp / (AmE), / wɒmp wɒmp / (BrE)
an informal exclamation or sound effect expressing disappointment, failure, or anticlimax
exclamation of disappointment or failure
informal

Examples

Everyone groaned and said womp womp when the plan was rejected.
After her phone died right before the concert, she shook her head and muttered womp womp.
He failed the quiz, so his friend teased him with a dramatic womp womp.

Synset Examples

She laughed and said 'womp womp' after his failed attempt.
When he missed the shot, everyone went 'womp womp'.
The joke fell flat, and the audience responded with a 'womp womp'.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wah-wah - sound effect used to signify disappointment or letdown, similar in usage
