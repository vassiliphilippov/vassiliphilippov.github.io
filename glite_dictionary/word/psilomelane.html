psilomelane

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaɪləˈmiːleɪn / (AmE), / ˌsɪləməˈleɪn / (BrE)
a mineral consisting of hydrated basic oxide of manganese and barium, used as a source of manganese
manganese and barium mineral

Examples

The geologist identified the black crystals as psilomelane in the rock sample.
Mines in the area are rich in psilomelane, making them valuable sources of manganese.
Collectors prize specimens of psilomelane for their lustrous surfaces and unique forms.

Etymology

From Greek psilos (smooth) + melas (black), referring to the mineral's typical appearance.
