Gantrisin

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæntrɪsɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡantrɪsɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked sulfonamide drug used to treat urinary tract infections.
antibiotic drug (trade name) for UTIs
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Gantrisin to help clear up the urinary tract infection.
Due to an allergy, she could not take Gantrisin and was given a different antibiotic.
Cases of resistance to Gantrisin have prompted doctors to monitor its use carefully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sulfisoxazole - a sulfonamide antibiotic; generic name for Gantrisin
