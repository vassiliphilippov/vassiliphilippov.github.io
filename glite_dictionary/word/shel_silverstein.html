Shel Silverstein

1. PROPN

/ ʃɛl ˈsɪlvərstiːn / (AmE), / ʃɛl ˈsɪlvəstiːn / (BrE)
(1932–1999) A United States poet, cartoonist, and author best known for his stories and poems for children.

Shel Silverstein wrote many beloved children's books, including 'Where the Sidewalk Ends'.
