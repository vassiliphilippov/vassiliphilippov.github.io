adaption

1. NOUN

/ əˈdæpʃən / (AmE), / əˈdæpʃən / (BrE)
the process of adjusting or becoming suited to new conditions, often in response to changes in the environment
process of adapting to conditions

Examples

Many species show remarkable adaption to harsh desert environments.
The company's quick adaption to market changes kept it competitive.
Her adaption to living abroad was impressive and swift.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adaptation - the process of adapting or being adapted to new conditions

Etymology

Variant of 'adaptation,' from Latin adaptātiō.
