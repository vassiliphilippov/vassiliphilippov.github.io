bookbinding

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊkˌbaɪndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌbaɪndɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or craft of assembling and securing the pages of a book within a cover
the craft of binding books

Examples

The art of bookbinding has been practiced for centuries across many cultures.
He enrolled in a workshop to learn traditional bookbinding skills.
Many rare manuscripts are preserved thanks to careful bookbinding techniques.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: binder - person who binds books
  • Synonym: bookbinder - a person whose job is bookbinding

Etymology

From 'book' + 'binding', referring to the act of binding books.
