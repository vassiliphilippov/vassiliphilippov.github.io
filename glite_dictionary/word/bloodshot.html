bloodshot

1. ADJ

/ ˈblʌdˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈblʌdʃɒt / (BrE)
(of eyes) reddened and inflamed as a result of congested blood vessels.
reddened, inflamed eyes

Examples

His bloodshot eyes revealed how little sleep he had gotten.
After crying for hours, her eyes became bloodshot and sore.
The doctor examined his bloodshot eyes for signs of infection.

Synset Examples

bloodshot eyes

Etymology

From 'blood' + 'shot', indicating the shot-through appearance of blood in the eye.
