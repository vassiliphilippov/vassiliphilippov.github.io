Luis de Góngora y Argote

1. PROPN

/ ˈluːis deɪ ˈɡɔːŋɡərə i ɑːrˈgoʊteɪ / (AmE), / ˈluːɪs deɪ ˈɡɒŋɡərə i ɑːˈɡəʊteɪ / (BrE)
(1561–1627) A Spanish poet renowned for his influential, ornate style and significant impact on Baroque literature.
Spanish Baroque poet (1561–1627)

Examples

Luis de Góngora y Argote was a central figure in the Spanish literary movement known as Culteranismo.
The elaborate verse of Luis de Góngora y Argote set a new standard for poetic complexity in the 17th century.
Scholars continue to debate the influence of Luis de Góngora y Argote on modern Spanish literature.
