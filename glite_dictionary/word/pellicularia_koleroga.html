Pellicularia koleroga

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɛlɪˈkjuːlɛriə ˌkoʊləˈroʊɡə / (AmE), / ˌpɛlɪˈkjuːlɪəriə ˌkɒlɪˈrəʊɡə / (BrE)
a species of fungus that causes disease in coffee and some other tropical plants, leading to crop damage
fungus causing disease in coffee plants

Examples

Pellicularia koleroga causes a destructive disease known as coffee leaf rot in tropical plantations.
Agronomists are researching methods to control Pellicularia koleroga in affected coffee crops.
The spread of Pellicularia koleroga can significantly impact the yield of several tropical plants.
