sit-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪt ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈsɪt ʌp / (BrE)
an exercise in which a person lies on their back and moves to a sitting position without using their arms, to strengthen the abdominal muscles
abdominal strengthening exercise

Examples

He does fifty sit-ups every morning as part of his workout routine.
The coach demonstrated the correct way to perform a sit-up to avoid back strain.
After a set of push-ups, she moved on to sit-ups to strengthen her core.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crunch - an exercise similar to a sit-up but with a smaller range of movement, working the abdominal muscles

Etymology

From the verb phrase 'sit up', referring to the action performed during the exercise.
