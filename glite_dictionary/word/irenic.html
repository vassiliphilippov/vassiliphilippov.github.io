irenic

1. ADJ

/ aɪˈrɛnɪk / (AmE), / aɪˈriːnɪk / (BrE)
Promoting peace or reconciliation; aiming to reduce conflict or foster harmony.
promoting peace or harmony

Examples

The new leader adopted an irenic approach to international relations.
Her irenic spirit helped resolve many disputes in the community.
Discussion remained tense until someone made an irenic suggestion that everyone could accept.

Synset Examples

irenic without being namby-pamby
an irenic attitude toward former antagonists

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conciliatory - intended or likely to placate or pacify
  • Antonym: belligerent - hostile and aggressive

Etymology

From Greek *eirēnikos* (of or for peace), from *eirēnē* (peace).
