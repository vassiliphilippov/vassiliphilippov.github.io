Pissis

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɪsɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɪsɪs / (BrE)
a mountain in the Andes in Argentina, one of the highest in the Americas, reaching 22,241 feet (6,793 meters)
mountain in the Andes, Argentina

Examples

Mount Pissis attracts climbers from all over the world because of its challenging conditions.
The snow-covered summit of Pissis is visible for miles across the Argentine desert.
Expeditions to Pissis require careful preparation due to its high altitude.
