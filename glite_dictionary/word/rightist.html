rightist

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtɪst / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or is a member of a right-wing political party or holds conservative political views
a member of a right-wing political group

Examples

He has always identified as a rightist and supports conservative economic policies.
During the debate, the rightist expressed strong opposition to government intervention.
Several rightists attended the rally to voice their opinions on tax reform.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: leftist - a member of a left wing political party or someone with left-wing political views

Etymology

From 'right', referring to the conservative side of the political spectrum, plus the suffix '-ist'.

2. ADJ

/ ˈraɪ.tɪst / (AmE), / ˈraɪ.tɪst / (BrE)
Believing in or supporting the views and policies of the political right, especially in politics.
supporter of the political right

Examples

She holds rightist views on economic policies and prefers limited government intervention.
The party's rightist wing called for stricter immigration controls.
In recent years, support for rightist politicians has grown in several countries.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: leftist - supporter of the political left

Etymology

From 'right' (the conservative side in politics) + '-ist'.
