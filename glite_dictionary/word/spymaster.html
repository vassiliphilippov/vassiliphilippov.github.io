spymaster

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈspaɪˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person who directs or controls secret intelligence operations, especially for a government
person in charge of secret intelligence operations

Examples

The notorious spymaster coordinated covert missions across several countries.
Rumors spread that the chief spymaster had information about every government official.
As a skilled spymaster, she ensured that no secrets left the agency undetected.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: agent - a person who acts on behalf of a government or organization, often in secret
