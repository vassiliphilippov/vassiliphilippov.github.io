associational

1. ADJ

/ əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to associations or to the doctrine of associationism, especially in psychology or social contexts.
relating to associations or associationism

Examples

Her research focused on the associational memory of elderly patients.
The club provides an associational link between local businesses.
The philosophy department discussed associational theories in their seminar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: associationist - one who advocates or follows associationism

Etymology

Formed from 'association' + '-al', meaning 'relating to an association.'
