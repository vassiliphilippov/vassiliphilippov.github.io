alkylic

1. ADJ

/ ælˈkɪlɪk / (AmE), / ælˈkɪlɪk / (BrE)
relating to or derived from an alkyl group
relating to an alkyl group
medical

Examples

The compound possesses several alkylic side chains attached to the main framework.
Chemists identified an alkylic group as responsible for the molecule's reactivity.
Experiments showed the reagents reacted differently with alkylic substances than with aromatic compounds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alkyl - any of a group of organic chemical radicals derived from alkanes

Etymology

From 'alkyl' plus the suffix '-ic,' indicating relation.
