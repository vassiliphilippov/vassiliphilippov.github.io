honky

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑŋki / (AmE), / ˈhɒŋki / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) A derogatory term for a White person, especially a White man.
offensive slang for a White person
slang offensive

Examples

In the heated argument, someone insultingly called him a honky.
The comedian was criticized for using the term honky in his routine.
She disapproved when her friend referred to someone as a honky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cracker - an offensive term for a poor White person in the southern United States
  • Synonym: whitey - an offensive slang term for a White person

Etymology

Origin uncertain; perhaps from 'hunky', originally a slur for Central European immigrants, or possibly from African-American English.
