Rumania

1. PROPN

/ ruˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ruːˈmeɪniə / (BrE)
A country in southeastern Europe, bordered by the Black Sea, also known as Romania.
country in southeastern Europe

Examples

The capital city of Rumania is Bucharest.
She traveled through Rumania on her way to Bulgaria.
Rumania joined the European Union in 2007.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Romania - country in southeastern Europe, alternate spelling

Etymology

From Latin 'Romania' meaning 'land of the Romans'; variant spelling of 'Romania'.
