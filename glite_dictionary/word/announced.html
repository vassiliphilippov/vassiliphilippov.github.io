announced

1. ADJ

/ əˈnaʊnst / (AmE), / əˈnaʊnst / (BrE)
declared publicly or officially; made known to many people
declared publicly or officially

Examples

The company outlined its announced strategy for entering new markets.
Despite their announced intentions, nothing changed within the department.
Her announced retirement surprised many of her longtime fans.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proclaimed - declared publicly; formally announced
  • Antonym: unannounced - not declared or made known beforehand

Etymology

Past participle of 'announce', from Old French annoncer, from Latin annuntiare (to announce).
