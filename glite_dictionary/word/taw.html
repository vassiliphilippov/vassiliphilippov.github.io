taw

1. NOUN

/ tɑː / (AmE), / tɑː / (BrE)
The 23rd letter of the Hebrew alphabet.
the 23rd Hebrew letter

Examples

The Hebrew word for truth begins with aleph and ends with taw.
In modern Hebrew, taw can also represent a 't' sound.
Scholars often refer to taw when discussing the order of the Hebrew alphabet.

Etymology

From Hebrew 'tav' or 'taw', the name of the letter.
