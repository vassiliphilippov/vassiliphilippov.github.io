Peterburg

1. PROPN

/ ˈpiːtərˌbɜːrɡ / (AmE), / ˈpiːtəbɜːɡ / (BrE)
an alternative name for Saint Petersburg, a major city in northwestern Russia, formerly the country's capital.
alternative name for Saint Petersburg, Russia

Examples

Many tourists visit Peterburg each year to admire its historic architecture.
The Hermitage Museum in Peterburg is world-renowned for its art collections.
After moving to Peterburg, she quickly adapted to the cultural life of the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Saint Petersburg - a major city in northwestern Russia, former capital

Etymology

From the Russian Петeрбург (Peterburg), a shortened form of Санкт-Петербург (Sankt-Peterburg), meaning 'Saint Petersburg.'
