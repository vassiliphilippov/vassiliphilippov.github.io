Maine

1. PROPN

/ meɪn / (AmE), / meɪn / (BrE)
a state in the northeastern United States, located in the region of New England, with Augusta as its capital
US state in New England
american british

Examples

Every summer, my family visits Maine to enjoy the beautiful coastline and fresh seafood.
The author grew up in Maine and often sets her novels there.
Snow is common in Maine during the winter months, attracting many tourists for skiing.

Etymology

From the French province of Maine, possibly named by early settlers, or from the English word 'main' meaning 'mainland.'
