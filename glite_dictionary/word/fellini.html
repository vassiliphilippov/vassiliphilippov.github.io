Fellini

1. PROPN

/ fəˈliːni / (AmE), / fəˈliːni / (BrE)
Federico Fellini (1920–1993), an Italian film director and screenwriter known for his influential and imaginative cinematic style.
famous Italian film director (1920–1993)

Examples

The films of Fellini have inspired generations of directors worldwide.
Many critics consider Fellini a master of surreal and dreamlike cinema.
We watched a retrospective of Fellini at the local art theater last weekend.

Etymology

Italian surname; refers to Federico Fellini.
