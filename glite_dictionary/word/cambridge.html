Cambridge

1. PROPN

/ ˈkæm.brɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkæm.brɪdʒ / (BrE)
A city in Massachusetts, USA, located just north of Boston, known for being the site of Harvard University and the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
city in Massachusetts, USA

Examples

Many students dream of attending Harvard University in Cambridge, Massachusetts.
The city of Cambridge is famous for its academic atmosphere and historic architecture.
She moved to Cambridge to pursue her studies at MIT.

Etymology

Named after Cambridge, England.
