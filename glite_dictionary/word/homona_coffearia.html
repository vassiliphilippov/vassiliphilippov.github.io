Homona coffearia

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈmoʊnə kəˈfɛəriə / (AmE), / həʊˈməʊnə kɒˈfiːəriə / (BrE)
a small Indian moth that infests tea and coffee plants, known as a pest in agriculture
moth species infesting tea and coffee plants

Examples

The Homona coffearia moth is responsible for significant damage to tea plantations across South Asia.
Farmers monitor their crops closely to prevent infestations by Homona coffearia.
Research is ongoing into sustainable ways to control Homona coffearia populations.
