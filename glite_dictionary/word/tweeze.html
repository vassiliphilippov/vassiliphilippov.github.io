to tweeze

1. VERB

/ twiz / (AmE), / twiÀêz / (BrE)
to pluck or remove (especially hair or small objects) using tweezers
pluck or remove with tweezers

Examples

She carefully tweezed her eyebrows before the meeting.
You should tweeze any stray hairs for a cleaner look.
He tweezed a tiny splinter from his finger with steady hands.

Synset Examples

tweeze facial hair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pluck - to remove something (such as a hair) by pulling it out
