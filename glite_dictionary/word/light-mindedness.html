light-mindedness

1. NOUN

/ ˌlaɪtˈmaɪndɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌlaɪtˈmaɪndɪdnɪs / (BrE)
a lack of seriousness or showing inappropriate levity, especially when serious behavior is expected
lack of seriousness; levity

Examples

His light-mindedness during the meeting annoyed his more serious colleagues.
The play's light-mindedness brought much-needed laughter, despite the dark subject matter.
She was criticized for her light-mindedness in situations that called for a sense of responsibility.

Synset Examples

Annika reacted to Tom's flippancy with a cool stare

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frivolity - lack of seriousness; lightheartedness
  • Antonym: seriousness - the opposite of levity; the quality of being serious
