everlastingly

1. ADV

/ ˌɛvərˈlæstɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌɛvəˈlɑːstɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that continues for an infinitely long time; forever.
for an infinitely long time

Examples

She believed their friendship would last everlastingly, no matter what happened.
The river flows everlastingly to the sea, never ceasing its journey.
He claimed he would love her everlastingly, through all the years ahead.

Synset Examples

no one can live forever
brightly beams our Father's mercy from his lighthouse evermore

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eternally - in a way that lasts forever or for all time
  • Synonym: forever - for all future time; for always
  • Antonym: temporarily - for a limited period of time

Etymology

From 'everlasting' + '-ly', formed in Middle English.
