tum

1. NOUN

/ tʌm / (AmE), / tʌm / (BrE)
an informal term for the stomach or belly, especially used by or to children
informal word for stomach, belly
informal british

Examples

My tum is rumbling; I think it's time for lunch.
The little boy held his tum and complained of a stomachache.
After eating too much cake, her tum felt uncomfortably full.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stomach - the organ in the body where food is digested
  • Synonym: belly - the front part of the human body below the chest
