La Marseillaise

1. PROPN

/ lɑ mɑr.seɪˈjɛz / (AmE), / la maˈseɪjɛz / (BrE)
the national anthem of France, composed and adopted during the French Revolution
the French national anthem

Examples

At the ceremony, the orchestra played La Marseillaise as the French flag was raised.
Many people stood to attention when La Marseillaise began to play in the stadium.
He recognized La Marseillaise immediately from its stirring opening notes.

Etymology

French, meaning 'the (song) from Marseille', after the city of Marseille.
