backside

1. NOUN

/ ˈbækˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈbæk.saɪd / (BrE)
the side of something that is opposite to the front; the rear side.
rear side of an object

Examples

The package label was on the backside of the box, not the front.
Please check the backside of the building for the delivery entrance.
There was a small tear on the backside of his shirt.

Synset Examples

his room was toward the rear of the hotel

Related Terms

  • Antonym: front - the side or part of something that faces forward or is most often seen or used
  • Synonym: rear - the back part or side of something
