Tripoli

1. PROPN

/ ˈtrɪpəli / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpəli / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Libya, located in northwestern Libya on the Mediterranean Sea
capital city of Libya

Examples

Many tourists visit Tripoli to explore its rich history and Mediterranean coastline.
After years abroad, his family finally returned to their home in Tripoli.
The consulate is located in the heart of Tripoli, near the old city.

Etymology

From Greek Tripolis (meaning 'three cities').

2. PROPN

/ ˈtrɪpəli / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpəli / (BrE)
A port city and commercial center in northwestern Lebanon, located on the Mediterranean Sea.
port city in Lebanon

Examples

Tripoli is Lebanon’s second-largest city and a major port on the Mediterranean coast.
The souks of Tripoli are famous for their spices and traditional crafts.
During the summer, many tourists visit Tripoli to explore its ancient citadel and vibrant markets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tripoli - city in northern Libya on the Mediterranean Sea

Etymology

From Arabic Ṭarābulus, itself from Greek Tripolis (meaning 'three cities').
