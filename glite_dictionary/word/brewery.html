brewery

1. NOUN

/ ˈbruː.ə.ri / (AmE), / ˈbrʊə.ri / (BrE)
a place where beer is made commercially by the process of fermentation
beer-making establishment

Examples

The city is famous for its historic brewery that has been operating for over a century.
We toured the brewery to learn how different types of beer are made.
Many craft brewery owners experiment with unique flavors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distillery - a place where alcoholic spirits are made
  • Synonym: winery - a place where wine is produced

Etymology

From brew + -ery, modeled after 'bakery'.
