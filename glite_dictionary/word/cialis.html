Cialis

1. NOUN

/ siˈælɪs / (AmE), / siˈælɪs / (BrE)
a trade name for a drug (tadalafil) used to treat erectile dysfunction in men
brand of erectile dysfunction medication

Examples

Many men have found Cialis effective in treating their erectile dysfunction.
The doctor prescribed Cialis as an alternative to other medications.
You should not take Cialis if you are also using nitrate drugs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Viagra - trade name for a drug (sildenafil) treating erectile dysfunction
