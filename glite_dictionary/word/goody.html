goody

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʊdi / (AmE), / ˈɡʊdi / (BrE)
a sweet or tasty food treat; something considered especially delicious to eat
sweet or tasty food treat

Examples

The children were excited to find a basket full of goodies on the kitchen table.
She always brings homemade goodies to share at the office.
After dinner, the guests enjoyed a selection of chocolate goodies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delicacy - a delicious and rare food
  • Synonym: treat - something enjoyable to eat, often sweet or special

Etymology

From 'good', with suffix -y, originally American English around mid-19th century.
