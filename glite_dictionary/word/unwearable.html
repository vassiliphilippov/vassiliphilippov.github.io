unwearable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈwɛrəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛːrəbl / (BrE)
not suitable or fit to be worn; impossible or inappropriate to wear
not fit to be worn

Examples

The coat was so torn and stained that it became completely unwearable.
These shoes are unwearable after years of heavy use.
He found the costume unwearable because it was far too small.

Synset Examples

shoes so dilapidated as to be unwearable

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wearable - suitable to be worn

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' and 'wearable' (capable of being worn).
