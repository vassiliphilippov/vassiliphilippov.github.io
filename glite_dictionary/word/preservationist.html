preservationist

1. NOUN

/ ˌprɛzərˈveɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌprɛzəˈveɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who advocates or works for the protection of historical sites, endangered species, or natural areas
one who supports preservation of sites or species

Examples

The preservationist worked tirelessly to save the old library from demolition.
Many preservationists are concerned about the loss of natural habitats due to urban development.
As a dedicated preservationist, she advocates for both wildlife protection and cultural heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conservationist - a person who advocates for the protection of the environment and natural resources

Etymology

From 'preservation' + '-ist', denoting a person who supports a cause.
