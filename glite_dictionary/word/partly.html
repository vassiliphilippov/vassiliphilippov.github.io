partly

1. ADV

/ ˈpɑrt.li / (AmE), / ˈpɑːt.li / (BrE)
To some extent but not completely; in some degree or measure.
to some extent, not completely

Examples

The decision was partly based on financial concerns.
She is partly responsible for organizing the event.
The sky was partly cloudy throughout the afternoon.

Synset Examples

I felt partly to blame
He was partially paralyzed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: completely - totally, to the full extent
  • Synonym: partially - in part, to a limited extent

Etymology

From 'part' + '-ly'; 'part' from Latin 'partem'.
