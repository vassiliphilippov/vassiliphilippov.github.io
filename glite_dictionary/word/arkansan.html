Arkansan

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈkænzən / (AmE), / ɑːˈkænzən / (BrE)
a native or resident of the state of Arkansas in the United States
person from Arkansas

Examples

Her grandmother is a proud Arkansan who grew up near the Ozark Mountains.
Many Arkansans attend the annual state fair every autumn.
After moving to Little Rock, he quickly became friends with several local Arkansans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Arkansas - a state in the southern United States
