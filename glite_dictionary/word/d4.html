D4

1. NOUN

/ di ˈfɔr / (AmE), / diː ˈfɔː / (BrE)
a four-sided die, especially as used in role-playing games and tabletop gaming
four-sided gaming die

Examples

You need to roll a D4 to determine the damage.
The wizard grabbed his D4 before starting the next round.
Most role-playing games use a set that includes a D4.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: die - a small object marked with spots for gaming or gambling
  • Degree Higher: D6 - a six-sided die, standard in many board games

Etymology

From 'D', short for 'die' or 'dice', plus '4' indicating the number of sides.
