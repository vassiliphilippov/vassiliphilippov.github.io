mythologist

1. NOUN

/ mɪˈθɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / mɪˈθɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
an expert or specialist in myths and mythology
expert in mythology

Examples

She became a respected mythologist after years of researching ancient Greek legends.
The mythologist explained the significance of dragons in various cultures.
As a mythologist, he was often asked to consult for documentary films.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mythology - the study of myths

Etymology

From 'mythology' + '-ist', patterned after analogous formations for people who study a subject.
