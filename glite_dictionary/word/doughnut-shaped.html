doughnut-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈdoʊ.nʌt ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈdəʊ.nət ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape of a ring, like a doughnut.
shaped like a ring

Examples

The planet Saturn is surrounded by a doughnut-shaped ring of debris.
She wore a doughnut-shaped float in the swimming pool.
Scientists observed a doughnut-shaped cloud of gas around the black hole.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ring-shaped - having the form of a ring
