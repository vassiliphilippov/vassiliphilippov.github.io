oaten

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊtən / (AmE), / ˈəʊtn̩ / (BrE)
made from, containing, or relating to oats.
made from or related to oats

Examples

She enjoyed a bowl of oaten porridge every morning.
The farmer bundled up the oaten straw after the harvest.
Traditional Scottish bannocks are sometimes made with oaten flour.

Synset Examples

oaten bread

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oat - a cereal plant cultivated for its grain

Etymology

From oat + -en, with -en denoting 'made of' or 'from.'
