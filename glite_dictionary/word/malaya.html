Malaya

1. PROPN

/ məˈleɪə / (AmE), / məˈleɪə / (BrE)
the part of Malaysia located on the Malay Peninsula, historically known as Malaya and sharing a northern border with Thailand
region of Malaysia on Malay Peninsula

Examples

The southern part of Malaya is home to the bustling city of Johor Bahru.
During the colonial era, Malaya was a major exporter of tin and rubber.
She traveled across Malaya to study its unique cultural diversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Malaysia - country in Southeast Asia comprising Peninsular Malaysia (Malaya) and East Malaysia

Etymology

From Malay 'Melayu', the indigenous name for the region.
