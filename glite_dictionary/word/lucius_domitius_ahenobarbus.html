Lucius Domitius Ahenobarbus

1. PROPN

/ ˈluːʃi.əs doʊˈmɪʃi.əs æˌhɛnəˈbɑːrbəs / (AmE), / ˈluːsi.əs dəʊˈmɪti.əs əˌhɛnəˈbɑːbəs / (BrE)
The birth name of Nero, Roman Emperor from 54 to 68 AD, notorious for his extravagance and alleged cruelty.
birth name of Nero, Roman Emperor (37–68 AD)

Examples

Before becoming emperor, Lucius Domitius Ahenobarbus was known for his distinguished family heritage.
Many historians now refer to Lucius Domitius Ahenobarbus as Nero, his imperial title.
The early life of Lucius Domitius Ahenobarbus influenced many events during his rule as emperor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nero - Roman Emperor from 54 to 68 AD, known for extravagance and tyranny

Etymology

Latin origin: Lucius (personal name), Domitius (family name), Ahenobarbus ('bronze-beard').
