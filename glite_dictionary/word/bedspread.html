bedspread

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛdˌsprɛd / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌsprɛd / (BrE)
A decorative cover that is spread over a bed, usually covering the sheets and blankets.
decorative cover for a bed

Examples

She bought a new bedspread to match the curtains in her room.
After making the bed, he neatly folded the bedspread at the foot.
The hotel room was decorated with a floral bedspread on each bed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blanket - a large piece of woolen or similar material used as a covering on a bed
  • Synonym: quilt - a warm bed covering made of padding enclosed between layers of fabric and kept in place by lines of stitching
  • Synonym: comforter - a thick, quilted, fluffy blanket used to keep you warm

Etymology

Formed from 'bed' + 'spread', indicating something spread over a bed.
