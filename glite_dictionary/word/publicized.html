publicized

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʌblɪˌsaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪˌsaɪzd / (BrE)
Made known to the public, especially widely or officially announced.
widely made known; announced

Examples

The publicized launch of the new product attracted significant media coverage.
Her publicized resignation sparked a debate within the organization.
Many scandals remain unreported, but this one became highly publicized.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to publicize - make known to the public; announce widely
  • Antonym: concealed - kept secret; not made known
