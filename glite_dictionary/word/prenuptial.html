prenuptial

1. ADJ

/ priˈnʌp.tʃu.əl / (AmE), / priˈnʌp.tjʊ.əl / (BrE)
Happening or arranged before marriage, especially in reference to agreements or events.
before marriage; esp. of agreements

Examples

They signed a prenuptial agreement to clarify their finances before marrying.
Their prenuptial photo shoot captured the excitement leading up to the wedding.
A prenuptial party was held for close family and friends.

Synset Examples

prenuptial agreement

Related Terms

  • Antonym: postnuptial - happening or arranged after marriage

Etymology

From Latin 'præ-' (before) + 'nuptialis' (of marriage).
