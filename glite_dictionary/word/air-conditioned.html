air-conditioned

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛr kənˌdɪʃənd / (AmE), / ˈeə kənˌdɪʃənd / (BrE)
(of a building, room, or vehicle) cooled by a system that controls the temperature and humidity of the air.
cooled by air conditioning

Examples

We booked an air-conditioned hotel room to escape the summer heat.
The air-conditioned office felt refreshing after walking outside all morning.
Most modern cars are air-conditioned for passenger comfort.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heated - (of a space) made warm, typically by artificial means

Etymology

From 'air' + 'conditioned', referring to temperature regulation; based on 'air conditioning'.
