thyroidal

1. ADJ

/ θaɪˈrɔɪ.dl̩ / (AmE), / θaɪˈrɔɪ.dl̩ / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the thyroid gland
relating to the thyroid gland
medical

Examples

The doctor noted a reduced thyroidal uptake in the patient's test results.
Certain medications can affect thyroidal function and hormone levels in the body.
A thyroidal disorder may cause symptoms such as fatigue and weight changes.

Synset Examples

thyroid deficiency
thyroidal uptake

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thyroid - the thyroid gland; a large gland in the neck that produces hormones

Etymology

From thyroid + -al, the suffix forming adjectives meaning 'pertaining to'.
