cuckolding

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌk oʊl dɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌk əʊl dɪŋ / (BrE)
A sexual practice in which a married person has sex with someone else while their spouse observes.
sex act involving a spouse watching infidelity
vulgar

Examples

They openly discussed their interest in cuckolding during the interview.
Some couples explore cuckolding as a means to enhance intimacy in their relationship.
Documentaries have examined the psychology behind the appeal of cuckolding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cuckold - A man whose wife is unfaithful

Etymology

From cuckold, ultimately from Old French 'cucuault' (cuckoo, metaphor for an adulterous spouse).
