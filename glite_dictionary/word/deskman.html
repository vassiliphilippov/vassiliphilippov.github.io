deskman

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛskˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈdɛskˌman / (BrE)
a male police officer who serves as a desk sergeant, typically handling administrative tasks at a police station desk
male desk sergeant (police)

Examples

The deskman answered the phone and took down the details of the complaint.
After years on patrol, he was promoted to deskman at the precinct.
As a deskman, he handled paperwork and directed officers to emergencies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desk sergeant - police officer in charge of the front desk at a station

Etymology

Formed from 'desk' and 'man', following a common English pattern for occupational titles.
