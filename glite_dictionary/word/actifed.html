Actifed

1. NOUN

/ ˈæk.tɪˌfɛd / (AmE), / ˈæk.tɪ.fɛd / (BrE)
A proprietary name for a drug combining an antihistamine and a decongestant, used to relieve symptoms of colds, allergies, and upper respiratory conditions.
trade name for cold/allergy medicine

Examples

My doctor recommended Actifed to help with my allergy symptoms.
You can find Actifed at most pharmacies without a prescription.
She took Actifed for her cold, and it quickly relieved her congestion.

Etymology

Trade name.
