Lome

1. PROPN

/ loʊˈmeɪ / (AmE), / lɒˈmeɪ / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Togo, situated in the south of the country on the Gulf of Guinea
capital city of Togo

Examples

She traveled to Lome for a business conference last spring.
The harbor of Lome is one of the busiest in West Africa.
After years abroad, he decided to return to his hometown of Lome.
