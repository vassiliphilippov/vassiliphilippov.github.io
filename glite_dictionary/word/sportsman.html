sportsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrts.mən / (AmE), / ˈspɔːts.mən / (BrE)
a man who plays sports, especially in a skilled or competitive way
male athlete or participant in sports

Examples

The young sportsman trained daily to improve his performance on the track.
As a successful sportsman, he was admired for his dedication and fairness.
Several young sportsmen competed in the regional swimming championship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: athlete - a person who is proficient in sports and physical exercise
  • Antonym: sportswoman - a woman who engages in sports

Etymology

Formed from 'sport' + 'man' in English.
