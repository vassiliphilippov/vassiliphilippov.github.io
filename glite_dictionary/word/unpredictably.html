unpredictably

1. ADV

/ ʌn.prɪˈdɪk.təb.li / (AmE), / ʌn.prɪˈdɪk.təb.li / (BrE)
In a way that cannot be predicted or foreseen; in an erratic or uncertain manner.
in an unpredictable way

Examples

The weather changed unpredictably throughout the day, making it hard to plan outdoor events.
Stock prices can move unpredictably in response to global news.
Children sometimes behave unpredictably when faced with new situations.

Synset Examples

economic changes are proceeding erratically

Related Terms

  • Antonym: predictably - in a way that can be predicted
  • Synonym: randomly - in a way that lacks a definite plan, purpose, or pattern

Etymology

From un- (not) + predict + -ably (adverbial suffix).
