Saint Kitts

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt kɪts / (AmE), / seɪnt kɪts / (BrE)
the larger of the two main islands forming the country of Saint Kitts and Nevis in the Caribbean Sea.
island in the Caribbean Sea

Examples

We spent our vacation relaxing on the beaches of Saint Kitts.
The capital of Saint Kitts is Basseterre.
Many cruise ships dock at Saint Kitts during their Caribbean routes.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Nevis - the smaller island forming the country of Saint Kitts and Nevis

Etymology

From 'Saint Christopher', the original name for the island, which was shortened to 'Saint Kitts', with 'Kitts' being a nickname for Christopher.
