blockish

1. ADJ

/ ˈblɑːkɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈblɒkɪʃ / (BrE)
Resembling or shaped like a block; having the form or appearance of a block.
resembling a block in shape

Examples

The sculpture had a blockish appearance that made it stand out from the others.
He preferred simple, blockish designs for his furniture.
The mountain’s blockish silhouette was visible against the evening sky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cuboid - resembling a cube in shape
