ICU

1. NOUN

/ aɪ siː juː / (AmE), / aɪ siː juː / (BrE)
a hospital unit equipped and staffed to provide intensive care to patients with severe or life-threatening conditions
hospital unit for intensive care
medical

Examples

After the accident, she was admitted to the ICU for close monitoring.
The hospital expanded its ICU to accommodate more critically ill patients.
Doctors in the ICU work around the clock to stabilize their patients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intensive care unit - a hospital department for critical care patients

Etymology

Abbreviation of Intensive Care Unit.
