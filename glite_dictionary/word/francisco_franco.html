Francisco Franco

1. PROPN

/ frɑnˈsɪskoʊ ˈfræŋkoʊ / (AmE), / frænˈsɪskəʊ ˈfræŋkəʊ / (BrE)
(1892–1975) Spanish general and dictator who ruled Spain from 1939 until his death.
Spanish dictator (ruled 1939–1975)

Examples

Francisco Franco was the ruler of Spain for nearly four decades.
After the death of Francisco Franco, Spain transitioned to a constitutional monarchy.
Historians have debated the legacy of Francisco Franco for many years.
