disbandment

1. NOUN

/ dɪsˈbændmənt / (AmE), / dɪsˈbændmənt / (BrE)
the act of breaking up or dissolving an organized group, such as a band, organization, or military unit
the act of breaking up a group

Examples

The announcement of the disbandment of the committee was met with disappointment by its members.
After years of playing together, the disbandment of the band surprised many fans.
The country experienced instability following the sudden disbandment of its armed forces.

Synset Examples

the orchestra faced the prospect of disbandment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dissolution - the act of formally ending or dismissing an assembly or organization
  • Antonym: formation - the action of forming or creating a group or organization

Etymology

From 'disband' + '-ment' (forming a noun), influenced by French 'débandement'.
