Robitussin

1. PROPN

/ ˌroʊbəˈtʌsɪn / (AmE), / ˌrəʊbɪˈtʌsɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked name for an over-the-counter expectorant medicine used to relieve chest congestion and make coughs more productive.
a brand of expectorant medicine
american

Examples

My doctor recommended Robitussin to help clear up my chest congestion.
I picked up a bottle of Robitussin from the pharmacy before heading home.
If your cough persists, try taking Robitussin as directed on the label.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guaifenesin - an expectorant drug commonly used in cough medicines
