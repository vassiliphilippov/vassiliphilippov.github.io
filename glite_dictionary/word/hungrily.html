hungrily

1. ADV

/ ˈhʌŋɡrəli / (AmE), / ˈhʌŋɡrɪli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a strong need or desire for food.
in a manner showing hunger

Examples

The children ate their lunch hungrily after playing outside all morning.
She looked hungrily at the cake on the table, unable to resist.
He accepted the sandwich and bit into it hungrily, not having eaten all day.

Synset Examples

he pounced on the food hungrily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hungry - feeling or showing the need for food
  • Synonym: eagerly - in a way that shows keen interest or enthusiasm

Etymology

From "hungry" + "-ly" adverbial suffix.
