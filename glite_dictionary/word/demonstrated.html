demonstrated

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɛmənˌstreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɛmənˌstreɪtɪd / (BrE)
having been proved or shown clearly; verified beyond doubt.
proved or verified beyond doubt

Examples

His demonstrated ability to solve complex problems impressed the interview panel.
There is a demonstrated need for better public transportation in rural areas.
The medication's effectiveness has been demonstrated in numerous clinical trials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proven - shown to be true; demonstrated by evidence
  • Antonym: unproven - not established as true; not verified
