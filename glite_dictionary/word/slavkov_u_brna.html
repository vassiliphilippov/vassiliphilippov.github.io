Slavkov u Brna

1. PROPN

/ ˈslɑːf.kɔv uː ˈbrə.nə / (AmE), / ˈslɑːf.kɒv u ˈbrʌ.nə / (BrE)
A town in the Czech Republic, historically known as the site of the Battle of Austerlitz in 1805.
town in Czech Republic; site of Austerlitz battle

Examples

Many tourists visit Slavkov u Brna to see the historic battlefield where Napoleon won the Battle of Austerlitz.
The Treaty of Pressburg was signed shortly after the famous conflict near Slavkov u Brna.
Today, Slavkov u Brna attracts history enthusiasts from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Austerlitz - historical name for Slavkov u Brna, especially in the context of the battle
