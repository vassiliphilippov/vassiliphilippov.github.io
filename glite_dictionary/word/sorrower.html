sorrower

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːr.oʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɒr.əʊ.ə / (BrE)
a person who is experiencing deep sadness or grief, especially over a loss or death
a person who is grieving

Examples

The room was silent as each sorrower remembered their loved one.
At the memorial, a sorrower placed flowers on the grave.
She comforted the sorrower with kind words and a gentle embrace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mourner - a person attending a funeral or expressing grief
  • Synonym: griever - someone who grieves, especially for a death
  • Antonym: celebrant - a person who celebrates, especially the opposite emotional state

Etymology

From English 'sorrow' + '-er', denoting a person who sorrows.
