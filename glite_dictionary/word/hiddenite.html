hiddenite

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪdəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɪd.ə.naɪt / (BrE)
A green transparent variety of the mineral spodumene, used as a gemstone.
green transparent gemstone spodumene

Examples

She wore a necklace with a stunning hiddenite pendant.
Collectors often seek rare gems like hiddenite for their collections.
The jewelry store displayed a ring set with a bright green hiddenite.

Etymology

Named after William Earl Hidden, who discovered the mineral in North Carolina, USA in 1879.
