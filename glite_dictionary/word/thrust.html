thrust

1. NOUN

/ θrʌst / (AmE), / θrʌst / (BrE)
a strong, sudden push or blow with a knife or other sharp pointed object
a stab or blow with a pointed weapon

Examples

With a swift thrust, the fencer scored a point against his opponent.
The assassin ended the struggle with a single thrust of his dagger.
She parried the sword and countered with a powerful thrust to his side.

Synset Examples

one strong stab to the heart killed him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stab - a sudden, forceful thrust with a pointed weapon

Etymology

From Old Norse *þrýsta* (to press, squeeze).

Examples
