incredibleness

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈkrɛdəblnɪs / (AmE), / ɪnˈkrɛdəblnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being incredible or hard to believe; extraordinariness
the quality of being incredible

Examples

The incredibleness of the story left the audience in awe.
She admired the sheer incredibleness of the mountain landscape.
Many doubted the incredibleness of his daring escape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incredibility - the quality of being incredible or implausible

Etymology

Formed from 'incredible' + '-ness', a standard noun-forming suffix.
