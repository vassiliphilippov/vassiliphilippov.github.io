fruitcake

1. NOUN

/ ˈfruːtˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈfruːtkeɪk / (BrE)
A rich cake that contains dried fruit, nuts, and citrus peel, often served during celebrations or holidays.
rich cake with dried fruit and nuts

Examples

She baked a homemade fruitcake for the holiday party.
The fruitcake was filled with almonds, cherries, and raisins.
Every December, Grandma sends us a delicious fruitcake from her bakery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fruit bread - bread containing dried fruits and sometimes nuts

2. NOUN

/ ˈfruːtˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈfruːtkeɪk / (BrE)
A person who behaves in a very eccentric, odd, or crazy way, often in a whimsical or unusual manner (informal, often humorous).
eccentric or crazy person (informal)
informal

Examples

My uncle is a real fruitcake, always coming up with the weirdest inventions.
Don't pay attention to her wild theories—she's harmless, just a bit of a fruitcake.
Everyone in the office thinks Harry's a fruitcake because of his bizarre sense of humor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutcase - an eccentric or crazy person
  • Synonym: lunatic - a person who is mentally ill or wildly foolish
  • Antonym: sane - mentally healthy and rational

Etymology

Originally referring to the fruit-filled cake and later used humorously to liken someone's behavior to the 'mixed-up' or 'nutty' nature of the cake.
