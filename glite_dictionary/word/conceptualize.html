to conceptualize

1. VERB

/ kənˈsɛptʃuəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / kənˈsɛptjʊəlaɪz / (BrE)
to form an idea or concept of something in the mind; to imagine or devise the idea for something
form or create a concept or idea

Examples

It is often difficult to conceptualize abstract scientific theories.
The architect began to conceptualize a modern, eco-friendly building for the new museum.
Students were asked to conceptualize their own solutions to the environmental problem.

Synset Examples

He conceived of a robot that would help paralyzed patients
This library was well conceived

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to envision - to imagine as a future possibility; visualize
  • Synonym: to conceive - to form or devise (a plan or idea) in the mind

Etymology

From concept +‎ -ualize, modelled after French conceptualiser.
