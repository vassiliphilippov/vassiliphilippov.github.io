caring

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkeərɪŋ / (BrE)
displaying concern and empathy for the well-being of others
showing empathy and concern

Examples

Her caring attitude towards her patients made her an excellent nurse.
He is known as a caring father who always puts his children first.
The community came together in a caring response after the storm.

Synset Examples

caring friends

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compassionate - showing sympathy and concern for others
  • Antonym: uncaring - not showing care, concern, or sympathy

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'care' + suffix '-ing'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkeərɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of displaying kindness and concern for others; a loving feeling or attitude.
kindness; loving concern for others

Examples

Her caring for others made her beloved by everyone in the community.
There's a genuine caring in his voice when he talks about his family.
The nurse's caring was evident in every gentle gesture she made.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compassion - sympathetic pity and concern for the sufferings of others
  • Antonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern

Etymology

From the verb 'care', ultimately from Old English 'carian' (to be anxious, to grieve).
