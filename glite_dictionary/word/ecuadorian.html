Ecuadorian

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛk.wəˈdɔːr.i.ən / (AmE), / ˌɛk.wəˈdɔːr.i.ən / (BrE)
relating to Ecuador or its people, language, or culture
of or relating to Ecuador

Examples

The museum showcased traditional Ecuadorian art and crafts.
She prepared an Ecuadorian dish for the international food festival.
They studied the influence of Ecuadorian music on South American culture.

Synset Examples

Ecuadorian folklore

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Ecuador - a country in northwestern South America

Etymology

From Ecuador + -ian, meaning 'of or relating to Ecuador'.

2. NOUN

/ ˌɛkwəˈdɔriən / (AmE), / ˌɛkwəˈdɔːriən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Ecuador
person from Ecuador

Examples

Her grandmother is an Ecuadorian who moved to the United States in the 1980s.
The museum is showcasing the work of a talented young Ecuadorian.
Many Ecuadorians celebrate their national holidays with traditional music and dance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ecuadorian - relating to Ecuador or its people (adjective)

Etymology

From Ecuador + -ian (Latin adjectival and demonymic suffix).
