Shiva

1. PROPN

/ ˈʃiːvə / (AmE), / ˈʃiːvə / (BrE)
A principal deity in Hinduism, known as 'the auspicious one' and worshipped as the destroyer and transformer within the Hindu trinity.
major Hindu deity; destroyer and transformer

Examples

Many devotees visit the temple each year to worship Shiva during the festival of Maha Shivaratri.
Depictions of Shiva often show him meditating on Mount Kailash, his mythical abode.
The legend says that Shiva performed the cosmic dance of creation and destruction.

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Śiva' (auspicious, gracious).
