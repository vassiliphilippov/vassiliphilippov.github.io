adulterously

1. ADV

/ əˈdʌltɚəsli / (AmE), / əˈdʌltərəsli / (BrE)
In a manner involving adultery or characteristic of an adulterer.
in an adulterous way

Examples

The judge ruled that he had acted adulterously throughout the marriage.
Their relationship began adulterously, causing much distress in both families.
She accused him of living adulterously while they were still married.

Synset Examples

he behaved adulterously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adulterous - involving or characterized by adultery
