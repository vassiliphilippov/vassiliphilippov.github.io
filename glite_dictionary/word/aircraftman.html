aircraftman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌkræftmən / (AmE), / ˈɛəˌkrɑːftmən / (BrE)
the lowest noncommissioned rank in the British Royal Air Force, typically held by newly enlisted personnel
lowest RAF rank (British military)
british

Examples

After basic training, he became an aircraftman in the Royal Air Force.
As an aircraftman, your duties will include maintenance and support tasks.
The young aircraftman was eager to prove himself during his first posting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airman - a member of an air force; a similar term in US and other air forces

Etymology

From 'aircraft' + 'man', coined in early 20th century with the formation of the RAF.
