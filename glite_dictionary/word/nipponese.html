Nipponese

1. ADJ

/ nɪˌpoʊˈniz / (AmE), / nɪˌpəˈniːz / (BrE)
of or relating to Japan, its people, culture, or language.
relating to Japan or its people

Examples

The museum features a wide range of Nipponese art from ancient to modern times.
She studied Nipponese traditions before visiting Tokyo.
Many Nipponese films have received international acclaim.

Synset Examples

the Japanese Emperor
Japanese cars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Japanese - of or relating to Japan, its people, or language

Etymology

From Nippon (the Japanese name for Japan) + -ese (denoting origin or language).

2. NOUN

/ nɪpəˈniːz / (AmE), / nɪpəˈniːz / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Japan
person from Japan

Examples

A group of Nipponese tourists visited the art museum yesterday.
The Nipponese have a rich cultural heritage that is admired worldwide.
He spoke with several Nipponese during his business trip to Tokyo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Japanese - a native or inhabitant of Japan

Etymology

From 'Nippon', the Japanese name for Japan, combined with the English adjective-forming suffix '-ese'.
