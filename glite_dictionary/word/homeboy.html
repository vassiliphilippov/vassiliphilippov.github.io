homeboy

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊm.bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊm.bɔɪ / (BrE)
a male friend from one's own neighborhood or hometown, especially used as slang
male friend from one's neighborhood
slang

Examples

I've known my homeboy since we were both kids in the neighborhood.
He always hangs out with his homeboys at the basketball court after school.
When I visited my old town, I caught up with my homeboy from high school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homegirl - a female friend from one's neighborhood or hometown

2. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊmˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a male friend or acquaintance from one's own neighborhood or group, originally used to refer to a fellow member of a youth gang
male friend from one's group or neighborhood
slang

Examples

He grew up with his homeboy on the streets of Los Angeles.
She trusts her homeboy more than anyone else in the crew.
The two homeboys always looked out for each other.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homegirl - a female friend or acquaintance from one's own group or neighborhood

Etymology

Slang from 'home' + 'boy,' popularized in African American English in the 20th century.
