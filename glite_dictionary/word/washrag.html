washrag

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑʃˌræɡ / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃˌræɡ / (BrE)
A small piece of cloth used for washing the face or body, typically in bath or shower.
cloth for washing body/face

Examples

She grabbed a clean washrag from the linen closet before taking her shower.
He used a damp washrag to wipe the dirt from his son's face.
Please put the dirty washrag in the laundry basket after you're done.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: washcloth - a small cloth for washing the face or body
