ballerina

1. NOUN

/ ˌbæləˈrinə / (AmE), / ˌbæləˈriːnə / (BrE)
a female dancer who specializes in ballet, typically performing leading roles.
female ballet dancer

Examples

The young ballerina captivated the audience with her graceful performance.
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a ballerina on the world’s greatest stages.
The renowned ballerina retired after a long and illustrious career.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: danseur - a male ballet dancer
  • Synonym: dancer - a person who dances, especially professionally

Etymology

From Italian 'ballerina', feminine of 'ballerino' (dancer), from 'ballare' (to dance).
