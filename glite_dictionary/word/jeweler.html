jeweler

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːələr / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːələ / (BrE)
a person or business that makes, sells, or repairs jewelry
person/business dealing in jewelry

Examples

The jeweler showed us a beautiful selection of engagement rings.
After the necklace broke, I took it to the jeweler to be repaired.
She's been working as a jeweler in the city for over twenty years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldsmith - a person who makes objects from gold, especially jewelry

Etymology

From Old French joaillier (dealer in jewels), from joiel (jewel).

2. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːələr / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːələ / (BrE)
a person who makes, sells, or repairs jewelry
person who makes/sells jewelry

Examples

The jeweler carefully set the diamond into the gold ring.
She brought her broken necklace to the jeweler for repair.
Many people trust the local jeweler for unique, handcrafted pieces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldsmith - a person who makes or repairs objects in gold

Etymology

From Old French *juelier*, from *jouel* (jewel).
