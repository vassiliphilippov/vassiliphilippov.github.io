procyclidine

1. NOUN

/ proʊˈsaɪklɪˌdin / (AmE), / prəʊˈsɪklɪdiːn / (BrE)
a drug used to treat tremors and muscle stiffness, especially in Parkinson's disease and similar conditions
drug for Parkinson's tremors and stiffness
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed procyclidine to help manage his Parkinsonian symptoms.
After starting procyclidine, she noticed a significant reduction in muscle stiffness.
Procyclidine is sometimes used as an adjunct therapy for patients experiencing tremors.
