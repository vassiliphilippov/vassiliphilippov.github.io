Elvis Presley

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛlvɪs ˈprɛzli / (AmE), / ˈɛlvɪs ˈprɛzli / (BrE)
(1935–1977) United States singer and musician known as the 'King of Rock and Roll', famous for his energetic performing style and major influence on popular music.
famous American rock singer, 1935–1977

Examples

Many consider Elvis Presley the most influential rock musician of the twentieth century.
My grandparents still have all of Elvis Presley's original vinyl records.
The museum tour included a special exhibit about Elvis Presley and his music.
