2. ADJ

/ ˈmuːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmuːvɪŋ / (BrE)
causing strong feelings of sadness, sympathy, or admiration; arousing deep emotion
arousing deep emotion

Examples

The film’s ending was especially moving and brought many people to tears.
Her moving speech inspired the entire room to give a standing ovation.
It was a moving tribute to those who sacrificed their lives.

Synset Examples

she laid her case of destitution before him in a very moving letter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: touching - arousing strong feelings of tenderness or sympathy
  • Synonym: stirring - producing strong feelings or excitement
  • Antonym: uninspiring - not capable of causing emotion or excitement

Etymology

Present participle of 'move,' from Latin 'movere' (to move).
