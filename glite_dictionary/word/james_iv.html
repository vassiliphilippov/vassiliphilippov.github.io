James IV

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒeɪmz fɔrθ / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪmz ðə fɔːθ / (BrE)
(1473–1513) King of Scotland from 1488 to 1513, noted for his marriage to Margaret Tudor and his death at the Battle of Flodden.
King of Scotland (reigned 1488–1513)

Examples

James IV was known for his efforts to modernize Scotland during his reign.
The death of James IV at Flodden marked a significant turning point in Scottish history.
James IV strengthened Scotland's ties with Europe through his marriage to Margaret Tudor.
