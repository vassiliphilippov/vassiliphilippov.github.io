Ankara

1. PROPN

/ ˈæŋ.kə.rə / (AmE), / ˈæŋ.kə.rə / (BrE)
the capital city of Turkey, located in the west-central part of the country, formerly known as Angora
capital city of Turkey

Examples

Next summer, I am planning to visit Ankara to explore its historical sites.
The government of Turkey is based in Ankara, not Istanbul.
In the early 20th century, Ankara became the capital, replacing Istanbul.

Etymology

Formerly known as Angora, from Greek 'Ankyra' meaning 'anchor'.
