metallic-colored

1. ADJ

/ məˈtælɪk ˈkʌlɚd / (AmE), / məˈtælɪk ˈkʌləd / (BrE)
Having a shimmering, lustrous color similar to that of metal.
having a color like metal

Examples

The artist used metallic-colored paints to add a shimmering effect to the skyline.
She wore a stunning metallic-colored dress to the gala, catching everyone's attention.
The car comes in several metallic-colored finishes, including silver, bronze, and gold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metallic - resembling metal in appearance or properties
