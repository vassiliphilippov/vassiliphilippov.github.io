dendrology

1. NOUN

/ dɛnˈdrɑlədʒi / (AmE), / dɛnˈdrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of woody plants, such as trees and shrubs
study of woody plants

Examples

She decided to specialize in dendrology during her postgraduate studies in forestry.
The course on dendrology included field trips to identify different species of trees.
His fascination with trees began long before he learned the scientific principles of dendrology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silviculture - the growing and cultivation of trees
  • Degree Higher: botany - the scientific study of plants

Etymology

From Greek dendron (tree) + -logy (study of).
