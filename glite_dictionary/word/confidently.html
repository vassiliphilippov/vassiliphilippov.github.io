confidently

1. ADV

/ ˈkɑːn.fə.dənt.li / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.fɪ.dənt.li / (BrE)
In a confident manner; with certainty or self-assurance.
in a confident way

Examples

She spoke confidently about her achievements during the interview.
The student answered the questions confidently, impressing the entire panel.
He walked into the room confidently, smiling at everyone he met.

Synset Examples

we have to do what is right confidently

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assuredly - in a confident or certain manner
  • Antonym: timidly - in a shy or lacking confidence manner

Etymology

From 'confident' + '-ly', where 'confident' comes from Latin 'confīdere' (to trust).
