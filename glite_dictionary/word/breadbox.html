breadbox

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛdˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈbrɛdˌbɒks / (BrE)
a container for storing bread, usually to keep it fresh
a container for storing bread

Examples

She keeps all the rolls in the breadbox to stay fresh.
The vintage breadbox on the counter belonged to my grandmother.
Is there any bread left in the breadbox or should I buy more?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bread bin - a British English term for a container for storing bread
