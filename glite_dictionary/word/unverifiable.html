unverifiable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.vəˈraɪ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.vɛˈrɪə.fə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be proven or confirmed as true, accurate, or real
impossible to prove or confirm

Examples

Much of the information online remains unverifiable without reliable sources.
The witness gave an unverifiable account of the incident.
Many urban legends spread quickly because they are intriguing yet unverifiable.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: verifiable - able to be proven or confirmed as true or accurate

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'verifiable' (from Latin 'verificare', to verify).
