grumpy

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡrʌmpi / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌmpi / (BrE)
easily annoyed or in a bad mood; irritable.
easily annoyed or irritable

Examples

He was feeling especially grumpy after not sleeping well last night.
Don’t be so grumpy just because it’s raining outside.
The grumpy old man yelled at the children for making too much noise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irritable - easily annoyed or made angry
  • Antonym: cheerful - in a good mood, happy

Etymology

Probably from the obsolete verb 'grump' (to sulk or complain), of imitative origin.
