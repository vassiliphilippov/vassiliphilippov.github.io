Charles Goodyear

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʃɑrlz ˈɡʊdˌjɪr / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːlz ˈɡʊdɪə / (BrE)
(1800–1860) United States inventor known for developing the process of vulcanizing rubber.
US inventor of vulcanized rubber

Examples

Charles Goodyear revolutionized the rubber industry with his invention of vulcanization.
Many products we use daily exist thanks to the discoveries of Charles Goodyear.
The impact of Charles Goodyear on modern materials cannot be overstated.
