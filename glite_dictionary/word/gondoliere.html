gondoliere

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑːnˌdoʊliˈɛr / (AmE), / ɡɒnˌdɒliˈɛə / (BrE)
a Venetian boatman who propels a gondola, especially in Venice, Italy
Venetian gondola boatman

Examples

The gondoliere expertly navigated the narrow canals of Venice.
Tourists waved as the gondoliere serenaded them with an Italian folk song.
He aspired to become a gondoliere after watching them at work during his vacation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gondolier - a person who propels a gondola

Etymology

From Italian 'gondoliere', from 'gondola' (gondola) + '-iere' (person associated with).
