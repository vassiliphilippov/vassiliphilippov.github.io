SOP

1. NOUN

/ ɛs oʊ pi / (AmE), / ɛs əʊ pi / (BrE)
a prescribed procedure to be followed routinely in operations or activities, especially in organizations
standard operating procedure

Examples

All employees must follow the company's SOP for handling customer complaints.
The laboratory revised its SOP to include new safety measures.
Before starting the machine, technicians are required to review the SOP.

Synset Examples

rote memorization has been the educator's standard operating procedure for centuries

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protocol - an official procedure or system of rules
  • Synonym: guideline - a general rule, principle, or piece of advice
  • Antonym: unstructured - lacking a set routine or organization

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'standard operating procedure'.
