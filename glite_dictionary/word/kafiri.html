Kafiri

1. NOUN

/ kæˈfɪri / (AmE), / kɑːˈfɪəri / (BrE)
A Dardic language spoken by the Kafir people in northeastern Afghanistan.
Dardic language of northeastern Afghanistan

Examples

The Kafiri language is now spoken by a diminishing number of people in Afghanistan.
Many researchers have studied Kafiri to preserve its unique linguistic heritage.
She learned some phrases in Kafiri before traveling to northeastern Afghanistan.
