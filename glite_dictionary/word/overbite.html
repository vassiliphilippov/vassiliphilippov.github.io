overbite

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊvərˌbaɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌbaɪt / (BrE)
A condition in which the upper front teeth extend notably over the lower front teeth, especially when the mouth is closed.
dental condition where upper teeth overlap lower
medical

Examples

Her dentist recommended braces to correct her overbite.
An overbite can sometimes affect speech and chewing.
He felt self-conscious about his overbite until he had orthodontic treatment.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: underbite - a condition where the lower teeth extend beyond the upper teeth

Etymology

From 'over-' (above) + 'bite' (the act of biting); first appeared in the late 19th century in dentistry.
