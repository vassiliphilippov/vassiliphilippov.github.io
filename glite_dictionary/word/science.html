science

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
a particular branch of scientific knowledge or study
a branch of scientific knowledge

Examples

She decided to specialize in the science of chemistry at university.
Genetics is a rapidly developing science that impacts many fields.
Environmental science plays a crucial role in addressing climate change.

Synset Examples

the science of genetics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discipline - an area of knowledge, especially one studied in higher education

Etymology

From Latin scientia (knowledge), from scire (to know).
