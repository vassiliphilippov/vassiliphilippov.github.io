resuscitated

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈsʌsɪˌteɪtɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈsʌsɪˌteɪtɪd / (BrE)
Restored to life or consciousness, especially after seeming dead or unconscious.
restored to life or consciousness

Examples

The resuscitated patient slowly opened her eyes and tried to speak.
Firefighters pulled the resuscitated dog from the burning house, saving its life.
After a few minutes of CPR, the resuscitated man began to breathe on his own again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revived - given new life or strength; brought back to consciousness

Etymology

From Latin 'resuscitatus', past participle of 'resuscitare' (to revive).
