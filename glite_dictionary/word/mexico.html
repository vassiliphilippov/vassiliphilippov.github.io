Mexico

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɛk.sɪˌkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɛk.sɪ.kəʊ / (BrE)
A country in southern North America, bordered to the north by the United States, known officially as the United Mexican States.
country in southern North America

Examples

I have always wanted to visit Mexico and experience its vibrant culture.
Many species of cactus are native to Mexico.
After their road trip, they crossed the border into Mexico.

Etymology

From Spanish 'México', from Classical Nahuatl 'Mēxihco', the name of the capital of the Aztec Empire.
