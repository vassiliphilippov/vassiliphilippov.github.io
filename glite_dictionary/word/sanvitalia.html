Sanvitalia

1. PROPN

/ sænˈvɪtəliə / (AmE), / sænˈvɪtəliə / (BrE)
A small genus of tropical American annual herbs commonly known as creeping zinnia, belonging to the family Asteraceae.
genus of tropical American annual herbs

Examples

Sanvitalia thrives in sunny gardens with well-drained soil.
Gardeners often plant Sanvitalia as a colorful ground cover.
The bright yellow flowers of Sanvitalia attract butterflies in the summer.

Etymology

Named after Italian botanist Federico Sanvitali (1704–1761).
