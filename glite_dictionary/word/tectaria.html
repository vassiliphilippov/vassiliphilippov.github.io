Tectaria

1. NOUN

/ tɛkˈtɛɹ.i.ə / (AmE), / tɛkˈtɛə.ri.ə / (BrE)
A genus of terrestrial or epilithic ferns found in tropical rain forests.
genus of tropical rain forest ferns

Examples

The botanist discovered a new species of Tectaria in the remote Amazon rainforest.
Many Tectaria ferns thrive on mossy rocks beside tropical waterfalls.
Her research focused on the adaptation mechanisms of Tectaria in epilithic environments.

Etymology

From Latinized form of Greek 'tektōn' (builder, carpenter), related to construction, possibly alluding to form or structure of the ferns.
