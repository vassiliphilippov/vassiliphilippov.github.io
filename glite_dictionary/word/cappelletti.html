cappelletti

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːpəˈlɛti / (AmE), / ˌkapɛˈlɛti / (BrE)
Small circular or square pieces of pasta filled with savory ingredients, typically meat or cheese, and folded to resemble little hats.
type of filled pasta

Examples

We ordered a bowl of cappelletti in broth as a starter.
The chef makes his own cappelletti stuffed with ricotta and spinach.
She learned to fold cappelletti by hand from her grandmother.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tortellini - small ring-shaped filled pasta

Etymology

Italian, plural of 'cappelletto' (diminutive of 'cappello' meaning 'hat').
