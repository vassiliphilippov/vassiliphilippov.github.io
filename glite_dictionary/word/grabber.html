grabber

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡræb.ər / (AmE), / ˈɡræb.ə / (BrE)
an unpleasant or impolite person who grabs things inconsiderately or eagerly, often disregarding others
inconsiderate person who grabs things
informal

Examples

Don't be such a grabber at the buffet—let others have a chance too.
At holiday parties, there always seems to be one grabber who snatches the best gifts first.
Kids learn quickly not to be a grabber when sharing toys with friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grab - to seize or take hold of something quickly or roughly
