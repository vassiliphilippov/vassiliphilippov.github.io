enviably

1. ADV

/ ˈɛn.vi.əb.li / (AmE), / ˈɛn.vi.əb.li / (BrE)
In a way that arouses envy; so as to be enviable or desirable.
in an enviable or desirable way

Examples

She is enviably talented, making her achievements seem effortless.
They live enviably close to the beach and enjoy swimming every day.
The young actor is enviably confident on stage for his age.

Synset Examples

she was enviably fluent in French

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enviable - desirable or likely to arouse envy

Etymology

From 'enviable' + '-ly'. 'Enviable' from Old French *enviable*, from Latin *invidiabilis*.
