backer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbækər / (AmE), / ˈbækə / (BrE)
a person or organization that gives financial support to a project, business, or cause
person who provides financial support

Examples

The startup quickly found a backer willing to invest a substantial amount of money.
Without a reliable backer, the project would not have gotten off the ground.
The film was completed thanks to several generous backers who believed in the director's vision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sponsor - person or organization that supports a project financially or by other means
  • Synonym: supporter - person who gives support, especially financially or emotionally

Etymology

From 'back' (to support), plus the agentive suffix '-er'.
