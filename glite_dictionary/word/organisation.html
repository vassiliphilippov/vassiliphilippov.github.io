organisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
A group of people who work together in a structured way for a particular purpose, such as a business, club, or association.
group of people working together
british

Examples

The environmental organisation promoted recycling in local communities.
She volunteers for a charitable organisation that provides food to the homeless.
Every organisation needs clear communication to function efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organization - American spelling of 'organisation'
  • Synonym: company - A business or association, often a synonym in commercial contexts
  • Antonym: individual - A single person, contrasting with a group or organisation

Etymology

From French 'organisation', from Latin 'organum' meaning 'instrument, tool'.
