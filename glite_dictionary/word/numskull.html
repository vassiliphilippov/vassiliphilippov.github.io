numskull

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌmˌskʌl / (AmE), / ˈnʌmˌskʌl / (BrE)
a person who is considered to be very stupid or lacking in intelligence
a stupid person

Examples

Tom can be such a numskull when it comes to following instructions.
Don't be a numskull—read the directions carefully before you start.
Calling someone a numskull is not a very nice way to encourage improvement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blockhead - a person who is stupid or not intelligent
  • Antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence

Etymology

From 'numb' (insensible) + 'skull', referring humorously to a dull or empty head.
