developmentally

1. ADV

/ dɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəli / (AmE), / dɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the process of development, especially in terms of physical, emotional, or intellectual growth.
in relation to development

Examples

The new curriculum is developmentally appropriate for young children.
Some children may progress developmentally at a different rate than their peers.
The program supports students who are developmentally delayed.

Synset Examples

developmentally retarded

Related Terms

  • Synonym: developmental - relating to development
