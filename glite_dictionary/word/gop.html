GOP

1. NOUN

/ dʒi oʊ ˈpi / (AmE), / dʒiː əʊ ˈpiː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'Grand Old Party', a nickname for the Republican Party, one of the two major political parties in the United States
Republican Party (US, acronym)
american

Examples

Many conservative voters support the GOP in national and local elections.
The GOP has announced its platform for the upcoming presidential race.
Several GOP senators voiced their opposition to the proposed legislation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Republican Party - one of the two main political parties in the US
  • Antonym: Democratic Party - the other main political party in the US

Etymology

Acronym of 'Grand Old Party,' a nickname for the Republican Party.
