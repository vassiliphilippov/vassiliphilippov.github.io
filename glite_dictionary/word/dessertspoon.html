dessertspoon

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈzɝːtˌspun / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːtˌspuːn / (BrE)
A measure of capacity equal to the amount held by a dessert spoon, typically about 10 millilitres or 2 teaspoons in volume.
unit of measure, about 10 ml

Examples

Add one dessertspoon of sugar to the mixture and stir well.
The recipe calls for two dessertspoons of olive oil.
She measured out a dessertspoon of honey to drizzle onto the pancakes.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: tablespoon - a spoon or measure about three times the size of a teaspoon
  • Degree Lower: teaspoon - a small spoon or measure, typically one third the size of a tablespoon

Etymology

Formed from 'dessert' + 'spoon'.
