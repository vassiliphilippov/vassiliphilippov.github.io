quadrillionth

1. ADJ

/ kwɑːˈdrɪljənθ / (AmE), / kwɒˈdrɪljənθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number matching the value one quadrillion in a sequence; positioned at number one quadrillion in a series.
ordinal form of quadrillion

Examples

The star is the quadrillionth object catalogued in the database.
She said she was the quadrillionth customer to use the new website.
Calculating to the quadrillionth digit of pi requires advanced computational power.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: quadrillion - the cardinal number 1,000,000,000,000,000

Etymology

From quadrillion (patterned after millionth, billionth, etc.), with '-th' forming the ordinal.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02220583-s
Synset POS: s

2. NOUN

/ kwɑːˈdrɪljənθ / (AmE), / kwɒˈdrɪljənθ / (BrE)
Each of one quadrillion equal parts into which something is divided; the ordinal number corresponding to one divided by a quadrillion.
one part in a quadrillion

Examples

The measurement was accurate to a quadrillionth of a meter.
A change as small as a quadrillionth of a second can be significant in quantum experiments.
She owned less than a quadrillionth of the company's total stock.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: millionth - one part in a million equal parts
  • Degree Lower: billionth - one part in a billion equal parts
  • Degree Lower: trillionth - one part in a trillion equal parts

Etymology

From 'quadrillion' (a number equal to 1 followed by 15 zeros) plus the ordinal suffix '-th'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13762074-n
Synset POS: n
