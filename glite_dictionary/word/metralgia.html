metralgia

1. NOUN

/ mɛˈtrældʒə / (AmE), / mɛˈtraldʒɪə / (BrE)
pain in the uterus; uterine pain
pain in the uterus
medical

Examples

The patient complained of persistent metralgia during her menstrual cycle.
Some women experience metralgia due to hormonal changes.
Effective treatment is available for those suffering from chronic metralgia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uterine pain - pain occurring in the uterus

Etymology

From Greek mētra (womb) + -algia (pain).
