creamer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkriːmər / (AmE), / ˈkriːmə / (BrE)
a small container with a spout, used for serving cream, typically at the table
small pitcher for serving cream

Examples

She poured a little milk into her coffee from the creamer on the table.
The porcelain creamer matched the sugar bowl and teapot perfectly.
Please pass the creamer so I can add some to my tea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitcher - a large container with a handle and spout, used for pouring liquids

Etymology

From 'cream' + '-er', denoting a thing associated with cream.
