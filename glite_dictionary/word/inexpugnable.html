inexpugnable

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.ɪkˈspjuːɡ.nə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.ɪkˈspjuːɡ.nə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to overcome, challenge, or refute; invincible or irrefutable.
impossible to overcome or refute

Examples

The lawyer presented an inexpugnable case that convinced everyone in the courtroom.
Her inexpugnable logic made the debate impossible for the opponent to win.
The theory was supported by inexpugnable evidence, leaving critics without a valid counterargument.

Synset Examples

an impregnable argument
impregnable self-confidence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impregnable - impossible to defeat or overcome

Etymology

From Latin inexpugnabilis, from in- (not) + expugnare (to take by assault).
