welcomer

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛlkəmər / (AmE), / ˈwɛlkəmə / (BrE)
a person who greets and welcomes others, especially when they arrive at a place or event
a person who welcomes others

Examples

As a welcomer at the conference, Sarah made each new guest feel at ease.
The church appointed several welcomers to greet attendees at the door.
He volunteered as a welcomer for international students at the start of the semester.

Synset Examples

the newcomers were met by smiling greeters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greeter - a person who greets or welcomes people

Etymology

From 'welcome' (verb) + '-er' suffix forming agent nouns.
