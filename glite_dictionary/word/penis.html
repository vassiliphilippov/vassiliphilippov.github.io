penis

1. NOUN

/ ˈpinɪs / (AmE), / ˈpiːnɪs / (BrE)
The male sexual organ, which is also used for urination in mammals, including humans.
male sex organ

Examples

During the medical examination, the doctor checked the penis for any abnormalities.
The anatomy textbook provides a detailed diagram of the penis and its functions.
Circumcision is a surgical procedure involving the removal of the foreskin from the penis.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: vagina - the female sex organ; canal leading from the uterus to outside the body

Etymology

From Latin 'penis' (tail, penis).
