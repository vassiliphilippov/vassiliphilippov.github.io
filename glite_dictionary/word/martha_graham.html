Martha Graham

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑrθə ˈɡræm / (AmE), / ˈmɑːθə ˈɡræm / (BrE)
(1893–1991) A prominent American dancer and choreographer known for her influential contributions to modern dance, characterized by austerity and technical rigor.
American modern dancer and choreographer

Examples

Martha Graham revolutionized modern dance with her innovative choreography and powerful performances.
Many consider Martha Graham to be one of the most influential figures in American dance history.
The techniques developed by Martha Graham are still taught in dance schools around the world.
