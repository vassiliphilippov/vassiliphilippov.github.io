dendriform

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɛn.drɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈdɛn.drɪ.fɔːm / (BrE)
having a form or branching structure resembling that of a tree; tree-shaped.
tree-shaped; resembling a tree

Examples

The coral exhibited a strikingly dendriform pattern, with delicate branches spreading outward.
Certain fungi grow in a dendriform manner, resembling miniature trees on fallen logs.
The artist used metal wires to create a dendriform sculpture that mimicked the branching of an oak.

Synset Examples

arborescent coral found off the coast of Bermuda
dendriform sponges

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arborescent - having the shape or characteristics of a tree; tree-like

Etymology

From Greek dendron (tree) + -form (having the form of).
