sociobiologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌsoʊsiˌoʊbaɪəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsiəʊbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of sociobiology, the study of the biological basis of social behavior
relating to sociobiology

Examples

The research team proposed a sociobiologic explanation for how animal communities form hierarchies.
Her dissertation focused on the sociobiologic roots of human cooperation.
Some critics challenge the sociobiologic view that aggression is inherited.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sociobiological - relating to sociobiology

Etymology

From 'sociobiology' (combining 'socio-' and 'biology'), with the suffix '-ic' forming an adjective.
