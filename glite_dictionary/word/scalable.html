scalable

1. ADJ

/ ˈskeɪ.lə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈskeɪ.lə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be scaled, climbed, or expanded in size or scope; especially, capable of being enlarged to accommodate growth.
capable of being scaled or expanded

Examples

The software is highly scalable and can handle millions of users without slowing down.
They searched for a scalable solution to accommodate the company's rapid growth.
The scalable rock face attracted experienced climbers from all over the world.

Synset Examples

the scalable slope of a mountain

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expandable - able to be made larger or more extensive
  • Antonym: unscalable - not able to be scaled or expanded

Etymology

From 'scale' (to climb; to arrange according to size) + suffix '-able'.
