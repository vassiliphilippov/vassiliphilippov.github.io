five-membered

1. ADJ

/ faɪv ˈmɛmbɚd / (AmE), / faɪv ˈmɛmbəd / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) having a ring structure consisting of five atoms or groups.
having a ring of five atoms (chemistry)
medical

Examples

The compound contains a five-membered aromatic ring that is highly reactive.
Researchers identified a five-membered heterocycle in the new molecule.
We studied the stability of five-membered rings compared to six-membered rings.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: six-membered - having a ring with six atoms (chemistry)
  • Degree Lower: four-membered - having a ring with four atoms (chemistry)
