vibrational

1. ADJ

/ vaɪˈbreɪʃənəl / (AmE), / vaɪˈbreɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to, or characterized by vibration; involving or caused by vibrations
relating to vibration

Examples

The vibrational frequency of the molecule determines its infrared absorption.
Earthquakes generate vibrational waves that travel through the ground.
Her meditation practice focuses on increasing positive vibrational energy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vibration - an instance of vibrating or a regular oscillating motion

Etymology

From vibration + -al, patterned after similar adjectives like 'rotational'.
