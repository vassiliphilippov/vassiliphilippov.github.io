Delawarian

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɛləˈwɛriən / (AmE), / ˌdɛləˈwɛːrɪən / (BrE)
a native or resident of Delaware, a state in the United States
a person from Delaware, USA

Examples

The new senator is a proud Delawarian who has served her state for decades.
Many Delawarians enjoy spending summer weekends at the beaches along the coast.
As a lifelong Delawarian, he knows the history of the state quite well.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Delawarean - a native or resident of Delaware

Etymology

From the state name Delaware + suffix -ian.
