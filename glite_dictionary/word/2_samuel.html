2 Samuel

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɛkənd ˈsæmjʊəl / (AmE), / ˈsekənd ˈsæmjʊəl / (BrE)
the second of two books of the Old Testament that recount the reigns of Saul and David as kings of Israel
book of the Old Testament; story of Saul and David

Examples

Many scholars study 2 Samuel to understand the history of ancient Israel.

Every Sunday, our pastor reads a passage from 2 Samuel during the service.
