rollickingly

1. ADV

/ ˈrɑːlɪkɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈrɒlɪkɪŋli / (BrE)
In a carefree and exuberant manner; in a lively, joyful way.
in a lively, carefree manner

Examples

She laughed rollickingly at his jokes, filling the room with joy.
The party was rollickingly cheerful, with everyone singing and dancing until late.
Their rollickingly carefree attitude was infectious, making the afternoon unforgettable.

Synset Examples

she was rollickingly happy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carefreely - in a carefree manner
  • Synonym: exuberantly - in a lively, energetic, and enthusiastic way

Etymology

From 'rollicking' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
