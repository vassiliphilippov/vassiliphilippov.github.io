incredible

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈkrɛdəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈkrɛdɪbəl / (BrE)
impossible or very difficult to believe; extraordinary or astonishing.
impossible to believe; astonishing

Examples

The magician performed an incredible trick that stunned the audience.
She showed incredible strength during the competition.
At incredible speed, the car raced down the track.

Synset Examples

at incredible speed
the book's plot is simply incredible

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unbelievable - impossible or very difficult to believe; astonishing
  • Antonym: credible - able to be believed; convincing

Etymology

From Latin incredibilis ('not to be believed'), from in- ('not') + credibilis ('believable').
