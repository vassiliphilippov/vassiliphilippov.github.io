legislating

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛdʒɪˌsleɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛdʒɪˌsleɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of making or enacting laws
making or enacting laws

Examples

The process of legislating can often take months or even years to complete.
Effective legislating requires careful debate and compromise among lawmakers.
Public input is a crucial part of legislating in democratic societies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legislation - the act of making or enacting laws
  • Synonym: enacting - the act of making into law

Etymology

From the verb 'legislate', derived from Latin 'lex' (law) + '-late' (to bring), modeled after 'legislation'.
