isolated

1. ADJ

/ ˈaɪ.səˌleɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.sə.leɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
Situated far away from other places, people, or things; remote and separate from others, either physically or socially.
remote; set apart from others

Examples

They lived in an isolated farmhouse miles from the nearest town.
The community felt isolated after the bridge was destroyed by flooding.
Because of its isolated location, few tourists visit the island.

Synset Examples

existed over the centuries as a world apart
preserved because they inhabited a place apart
tiny isolated villages remote from centers of civilization
an obscure village

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remote - far away from other places or people
  • Antonym: connected - joined or linked to others, not isolated

Etymology

From Medieval Latin isolatus, from Latin insulatus (made into an island).
