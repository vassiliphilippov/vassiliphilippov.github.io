elasticised

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈlæstəˌsaɪzd / (AmE), / ɪˈlæstɪˌsaɪzd / (BrE)
Made with strands or inserts of elastic, allowing stretch or flexibility.
made with elastic material
british

Examples

The elasticised cuffs kept the cold wind from entering his sleeves.
She loves wearing elasticised waistbands for added comfort.
These elasticised socks stay up all day without feeling too tight.

Synset Examples

slacks with an elasticized waistband

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elasticized - made with strands or inserts of elastic (American spelling)

Etymology

From 'elastic' (from Greek 'elastikos', meaning ductile) + '-ised', a suffix forming adjectives (British spelling of '-ized').
