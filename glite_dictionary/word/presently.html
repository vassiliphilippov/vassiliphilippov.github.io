presently

1. ADV

/ ˈprɛzəntli / (AmE), / ˈprɛz(ə)ntli / (BrE)
at the present time; now; currently
at this time; now

Examples

The manager will be with you presently, so please take a seat.
She is presently working on her doctoral thesis at the university.
The restaurant is presently closed for renovations.

Synset Examples

he is presently our ambassador to the United Nations
currently they live in Connecticut

Related Terms

  • Synonym: currently - at the present time
  • Antonym: soon - in a short time; shortly

Etymology

From Middle English 'presently' (immediately), from 'present' + '-ly'.

2. ADV

/ ˈprɛzəntli / (AmE), / ˈprɛzəntli / (BrE)
in a short time; soon; before long
soon; before long

Examples

The manager will join us presently to discuss the project.
Dinner will be served presently, so please take your seats.
I expect the guests to arrive presently, so let’s finish setting the table.

Synset Examples

the doctor will soon be here
the book will appear shortly
she will arrive presently
we should have news before long

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soon - in a short time, before long
  • Antonym: immediately - at once, without delay
