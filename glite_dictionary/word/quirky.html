quirky

1. ADJ

/ ˈkwɝːki / (AmE), / ˈkwɜːki / (BrE)
Unusual in an interesting or appealing way; characterized by unexpected or unconventional traits, especially in behavior or style.
unconventional or eccentric in an appealing way
informal

Examples

Her quirky sense of humor always makes everyone laugh at parties.
The film is filled with quirky characters who are both odd and endearing.
He wore a quirky hat that immediately caught everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - unconventional and slightly strange
  • Antonym: conventional - typical or normal according to accepted standards

Etymology

From 'quirk' (peculiarity, idiosyncrasy) + '-y'.
