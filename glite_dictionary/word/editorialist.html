editorialist

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌɛdɪˈtɔːrɪəlɪst / (BrE)
a journalist who writes editorials for a newspaper or magazine
journalist who writes editorials

Examples

The new editorialist offered a fresh perspective on national politics.
She aspired to become an editorialist for a major newspaper.
As an experienced editorialist, he tackled controversial topics with care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: editor - person who edits or is in charge of editorial content

Etymology

Formed from 'editorial' + '-ist', indicating a person who does a particular job.
