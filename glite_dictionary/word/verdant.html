verdant

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɝːdənt / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdənt / (BrE)
(of land or countryside) green with grass or other rich vegetation.
green with lush vegetation

Examples

The valley looked especially verdant after a week of steady rain.
They enjoyed hiking through the verdant hills surrounding the village.
Spring brings a verdant transformation to the city park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lush - growing luxuriantly, especially of plants
  • Antonym: barren - lacking vegetation or green growth

Etymology

From Latin 'viridis' meaning 'green'.
