darkish

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɑrkɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdɑːkɪʃ / (BrE)
fairly dark in color or shade; somewhat dark
somewhat dark or fairly dark

Examples

She wore a darkish blue sweater that matched her eyes.
The living room was painted a darkish shade of green, giving it a cozy feel.
His hair is a darkish brown, especially in the winter months.

Synset Examples

darkish red

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: dark - having very little light; closer to black in color
