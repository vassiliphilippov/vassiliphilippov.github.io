faithlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪθləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈfeɪθləsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being unfaithful, unreliable, or treacherous
lack of faith or loyalty

Examples

His faithlessness was revealed when he broke every promise he had made.
The novel explores themes of betrayal and faithlessness in personal relationships.
After years of faithlessness, she decided she could no longer trust him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unfaithfulness - the state of being unfaithful
  • Antonym: fidelity - faithfulness or loyalty

Etymology

Formed from 'faithless' + '-ness', where 'faithless' is from 'faith' + '-less'.
