memo

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɛməʊ / (BrE)
a written message, especially in business, used to inform or remind someone about something
written message or reminder

Examples

I received a memo this morning about the new office policies.
Please send me a memo if there are any updates to the schedule.
Sarah wrote a quick memo to remind her team about the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: memorandum - a written record or communication
  • Synonym: note - a short written message

Etymology

Shortened form of 'memorandum', from Latin 'memorandum' (to be remembered).
