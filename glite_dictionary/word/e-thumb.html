e-thumb

1. NOUN

/ ˈiː ˌθʌm / (AmE), / ˈiː ˌθʌm / (BrE)
an approval or endorsement given online, typically by clicking a 'thumbs up' or similar button
online approval or endorsement

Examples

She gave my post an e-thumb to show her support.
A single e-thumb can boost someone's confidence online.
His video received hundreds of e-thumbs within an hour of being posted.

Synset Examples

Her e-thumb was always up for the latest memes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: like - an indication of approval on social media

Etymology

Formed from 'e-' (electronic) and 'thumb' (from thumbs-up gesture for approval).
