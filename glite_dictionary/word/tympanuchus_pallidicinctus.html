Tympanuchus pallidicinctus

1. PROPN

/ tɪm.pəˈnuː.kəs ˌpæl.ɪ.dɪkˈsɪŋk.təs / (AmE), / tɪm.pəˈnjuː.kəs ˌpæl.ɪ.dɪŋkˈtɪŋk.təs / (BrE)
A species of small prairie grouse native to western Texas and nearby regions, commonly known as the lesser prairie chicken.
a species of prairie grouse (lesser prairie chicken)

Examples

The Tympanuchus pallidicinctus population has declined due to habitat loss in recent decades.
Conservationists are implementing measures to protect Tympanuchus pallidicinctus from extinction.
Scientists studied the mating rituals of Tympanuchus pallidicinctus in western Texas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lesser prairie chicken - the common name for Tympanuchus pallidicinctus, a species of grouse native to North America

Etymology

From New Latin: genus name Tympanuchus, species name from Latin 'pallidus' (pale) + 'cinctus' (banded).
