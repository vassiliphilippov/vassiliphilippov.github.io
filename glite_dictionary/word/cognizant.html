cognizant

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑːɡnɪzənt / (AmE), / ˈkɒɡnɪzənt / (BrE)
having knowledge or awareness of something; conscious or informed about a particular fact or situation
having knowledge or awareness

Examples

The company must remain cognizant of environmental regulations when planning new projects.
She is fully cognizant of the possible consequences of her actions.
Judges must be cognizant of their legal and ethical responsibilities.

Synset Examples

was aware of his opponent's hostility
became aware of her surroundings
aware that he had exceeded the speed limit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aware - having knowledge or perception of a situation or fact
  • Antonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness

Etymology

From Latin 'cognoscere' (to know, become acquainted with).
