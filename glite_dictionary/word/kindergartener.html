kindergartener

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪndɚˌɡɑrdnɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɪndəˌɡɑːdnə / (BrE)
a child who attends a preschool or kindergarten, typically around five or six years old
child in preschool or kindergarten
american

Examples

The kindergartener proudly showed her parents the picture she had drawn in class.
Each kindergartener wore a colorful hat for the end-of-year parade.
It's important for a kindergartener to learn how to share and cooperate with others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kindergartner - alternative spelling for a child attending kindergarten

Etymology

From German 'Kindergarten' (children's garden) + English '-er' (agent noun suffix).
