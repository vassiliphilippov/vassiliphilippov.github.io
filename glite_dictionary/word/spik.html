spik

1. NOUN

/ spaɪk / (AmE), / spaɪk / (BrE)
(offensive) An ethnic slur for a person of Latin American descent, considered highly derogatory and insulting.
offensive term for people of Latin American descent
offensive

Examples

The use of the word spik is deeply offensive and should be avoided in all situations.
He was angered by the graffiti that included the term spik on the wall.
It's important to challenge anyone who uses slurs like spik in conversation.

Etymology

Probably from the mocking phrase 'no speak English' as said by non-native speakers.
