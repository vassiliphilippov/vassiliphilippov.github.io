uncommitted

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkəˈmɪt̬ɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈmɪtɪd / (BrE)
not involved in an exclusive romantic or sexual relationship; not pledged to a particular partner.
not in an exclusive relationship

Examples

He prefers to remain uncommitted until he finds someone truly special.
Many people choose to stay uncommitted while focusing on their careers.
Jack is currently uncommitted, enjoying dating without exclusivity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: single - not married or in a romantic relationship
  • Antonym: attached - in a romantic relationship, especially an exclusive one
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00159672-a
Synset POS: a

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkəˈmɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈmɪtɪd / (BrE)
not bound or pledged to a particular course of action, cause, or person; not having made a firm decision or commitment.
not pledged or bound

Examples

Many voters remained uncommitted until the final days of the election campaign.
She decided to keep her schedule uncommitted so she could accept new opportunities.
The funds are currently uncommitted and may be allocated as needed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: committed - pledged or bound to a cause, course, or person

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'committed', from Latin 'committere' (to entrust).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00520641-a
Synset POS: a

3. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkəˈmɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈmɪtɪd / (BrE)
not busy or occupied; not already engaged or pledged to another task or activity.
not busy or already engaged

Examples

I have two uncommitted hours this afternoon if you want to meet.
All employees were assigned tasks except for a few uncommitted staff members.
Because her time was uncommitted, she agreed to help organize the event.

Synset Examples

he was not available for comment
he was available and willing to accompany her

Related Terms

  • Synonym: free - not occupied or busy; available for use
  • Synonym: available - able to be used or obtained; not otherwise engaged
  • Antonym: busy - actively engaged in work or a pastime

Etymology

Formed by combining the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'committed'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01066320-s
Synset POS: s
