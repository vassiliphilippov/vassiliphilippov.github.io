unanalyzable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnəˈnælɪzəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈnælɪzəbl̩ / (BrE)
impossible to analyze or break down into simpler parts or elements
impossible to analyze or divide further

Examples

The chemical compound was so complex that it was considered unanalyzable by traditional methods.
She described the emotion as an unanalyzable mixture of hope and fear.
Some aspects of consciousness remain unanalyzable despite advances in neuroscience.

Synset Examples

a feeling is a simple and undecomposable mental state
this weight of evidence is something mystical and unanalyzable

Related Terms

  • Antonym: analyzable - able to be analyzed or broken down
  • Synonym: irreducible - not able to be reduced or simplified

Etymology

From un- (not) + analyzable (capable of being analyzed).
