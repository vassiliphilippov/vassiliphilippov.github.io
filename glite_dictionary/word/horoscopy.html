horoscopy

1. NOUN

/ həˈrɑː.skə.pi / (AmE), / hɒˈrɒs.kə.pi / (BrE)
the practice of preparing and interpreting horoscopes, typically to predict or reflect aspects of a person's life or future
preparation and interpretation of horoscopes

Examples

She studied horoscopy in depth during her years as an astrology student.
The practice of horoscopy can be found in many cultures throughout history.
Many people consult experts in horoscopy to gain insight into their future.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astrology - study of the movements and relative positions of celestial bodies interpreted as having an influence on human affairs
  • Synonym: horoscope - a chart or diagram representing the positions of the Sun, Moon, and planets at the time of an event, especially a person's birth

Etymology

From Greek 'horoskopos' (observer of the hour) via late Latin 'horoscopium'.
