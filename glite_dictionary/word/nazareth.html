Nazareth

1. PROPN

/ ˈnæzərəθ / (AmE), / ˈnæzərəθ / (BrE)
A historic town in northern Israel, traditionally known as the home of Joseph and Mary and associated with the early life of Jesus Christ.
historic town in northern Israel

Examples

Thousands of Christian pilgrims visit Nazareth each year to see its historic churches.
The story of Jesus's childhood is closely linked to the town of Nazareth.
Modern Nazareth is known for its vibrant markets and diverse culture.

Etymology

From Hebrew Naṣrat (נָצְרַת‎), via Greek Nazareth (Ναζαρέθ).
