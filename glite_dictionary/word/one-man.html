one-man

1. ADJ

/ wʌn mæn / (AmE), / wʌn mæn / (BrE)
designed for or restricted to a single person; intended for use or performance by one person only
for or by one person

Examples

The artist put on a stunning one-man show at the gallery last night.
He bought a one-man kayak for his solo trips on the lake.
This one-man tent is perfect for hikers who travel alone.

Synset Examples

a one-man show
a one-person tent
Sarah Silverman's hilarious one-woman show

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solo - done by one person alone, without assistance
