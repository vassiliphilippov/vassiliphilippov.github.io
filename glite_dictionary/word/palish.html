palish

1. ADJ

/ ˈpeɪlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪlɪʃ / (BrE)
Slightly pale; having a somewhat pale appearance.
somewhat pale in appearance

Examples

She looked a bit palish after staying up all night with the baby.
His palish skin made it clear he had not spent much time outside recently.
The dress was a palish shade of blue, almost like faded denim.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: pale - having a light or less intense color than usual

Etymology

From 'pale' + '-ish', an English adjectival suffix meaning 'somewhat'.
