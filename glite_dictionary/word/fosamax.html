Fosamax

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑːsəˌmæks / (AmE), / ˈfɒsəˌmæks / (BrE)
A trademarked tablet prescribed to prevent or treat osteoporosis in postmenopausal women.
osteoporosis medication (trade name)
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Fosamax to help strengthen my bones after menopause.
If you experience side effects while taking Fosamax, consult your physician immediately.
Fosamax is often recommended for women at risk of osteoporosis.
