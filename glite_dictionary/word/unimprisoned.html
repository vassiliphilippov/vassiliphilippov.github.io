unimprisoned

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪmˈprɪz.ənd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪmˈprɪz.ənd / (BrE)
Not confined in prison or not physically restrained; free from imprisonment.
not confined or imprisoned

Examples

After years locked away, the newly unimprisoned man took his first steps into the sunlight.
The bird, once unimprisoned, soared high above the trees.
In her poem, the spirit remains unimprisoned despite all hardships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: free - not under physical restraint or confinement
  • Antonym: imprisoned - confined in prison or physically restrained

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'imprisoned'.
