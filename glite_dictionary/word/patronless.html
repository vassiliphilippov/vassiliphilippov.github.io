patronless

1. ADJ

/ ˈpeɪtrənləs / (AmE), / ˈpeɪtrənləs / (BrE)
having few or no patrons or clients; without regular customers or supporters.
having few or no patrons or clients

Examples

The once popular café now sits patronless during most afternoons.
After the scandal, the exclusive club became almost patronless overnight.
The hotel's lobby was eerily patronless except for a single guest reading a newspaper.

Synset Examples

a restaurant unpatronized by the elite

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unpatronized - not having received patronage or support

Etymology

From 'patron' (supporter, customer) + suffix '-less' (without).
