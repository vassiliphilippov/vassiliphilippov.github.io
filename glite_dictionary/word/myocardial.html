myocardial

1. ADJ

/ ˌmaɪ.oʊˈkɑr.di.əl / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.ə(ʊ)ˈkɑː.di.əl / (BrE)
Relating to the muscular tissue of the heart (myocardium).
relating to heart muscle
medical

Examples

The patient suffered a myocardial infarction, commonly known as a heart attack.
Elevated enzyme levels can indicate myocardial damage.
The study examined myocardial function in patients with diabetes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cardiac - relating to the heart

Etymology

From Greek 'myo-' (muscle) + 'kardia' (heart) + '-al' (adjective suffix).
