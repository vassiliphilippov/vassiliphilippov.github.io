beatitude

1. NOUN

/ biˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / biˈætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
a state of utmost bliss or blessedness; supreme happiness
supreme blessedness or happiness

Examples

After years of searching, he finally found beatitude in a peaceful rural life.
The saints are often described as living in a state of pure beatitude.
Many people spend their lives seeking beatitude, believing it to be the ultimate goal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bliss - perfect happiness; great joy
  • Synonym: ecstasy - an overwhelming feeling of great happiness or joyful excitement
  • Antonym: misery - a state or feeling of great distress or discomfort of mind or body

Etymology

From Latin 'beatus' (blessed, happy) + suffix '-tudo' (state or condition).
