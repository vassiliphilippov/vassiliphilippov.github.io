Beatitude

1. NOUN

/ biˈæt̬.əˌtud / (AmE), / biˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
any of the eight blessings pronounced by Jesus at the beginning of the Sermon on the Mount, each beginning with 'Blessed are...'
one of the blessings from Jesus' Sermon on the Mount

Examples

Her favorite Beatitude is 'Blessed are the meek, for they shall inherit the earth.'
The pastor discussed the meaning of each Beatitude during the sermon.
The Beatitudes have inspired countless works of art and literature throughout the centuries.

Synset Examples

her favorite Beatitude is ‘Blessed are the meek for they shall inherit the earth’

Etymology

From Latin 'beatus' (blessed).
