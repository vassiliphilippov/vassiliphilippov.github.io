chimneypot

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪmniˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪmniˌpɒt / (BrE)
A short pipe, typically made of earthenware, placed on top of a chimney to improve the draft.
pipe atop a chimney to increase draft

Examples

The old house still had a red chimneypot on its roof.
A missing chimneypot can affect how well your fireplace draws smoke.
The mason carefully replaced the cracked chimneypot to restore the chimney's function.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chimney - A vertical structure for carrying away smoke from a fire
