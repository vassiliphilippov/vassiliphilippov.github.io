kabbala

1. NOUN

/ kəˈbɑːlə / (AmE), / kəˈbælə / (BrE)
A system of esoteric or mystical knowledge, especially one resembling or relating to the traditional Kabbalah.
esoteric mystical system

Examples

He spent years studying the hidden meanings in ancient kabbala.
Some claim that the manuscript contains a lost branch of kabbala.
Interest in kabbala has grown among those seeking spiritual wisdom outside mainstream religion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kabbalah - Jewish mystical tradition interpreting the Bible

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'cabbala', from Hebrew 'qabbālāh' (tradition, received lore).
