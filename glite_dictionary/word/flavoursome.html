flavoursome

1. ADJ

/ ˈfleɪvərsəm / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvəsəm / (BrE)
(of food or drink) having a lot of flavor; tasty and full of taste.
having a lot of flavor
british

Examples

The soup was particularly flavoursome thanks to the fresh herbs and spices.
She prefers flavoursome cheeses over mild varieties.
The restaurant is known for its flavoursome vegetarian dishes that burst with taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flavorful - full of flavor
  • Antonym: bland - lacking strong flavor

Etymology

From flavour + -some, patterned after other adjectives like 'wholesome'.
