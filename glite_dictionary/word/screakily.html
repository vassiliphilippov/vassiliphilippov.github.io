screakily

1. ADV

/ ˈskriːkəli / (AmE), / ˈskriːkɪli / (BrE)
In a screaking or creaky manner; making a high-pitched, harsh, or grating sound.
in a creaky, screaking way

Examples

The rusty gate swung sreakily in the wind, making everyone wince at the noise.
He opened the ancient book sreakily, its spine protesting with every movement.
The cart rolled sreakily down the cobblestone street on its old, unoiled wheels.

Synset Examples

the old boat was moving along creakily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creakily - in a creaky manner

Etymology

From screak (variant of screech) + -ily.
