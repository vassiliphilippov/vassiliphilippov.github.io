Maurice Ravel

1. PROPN

/ moʊˈriːs rəˈvɛl / (AmE), / mɒˈriːs raˈvɛl / (BrE)
(1875–1937) French composer and leading figure in Impressionist music, known for works such as Boléro and Daphnis et Chloé.
French Impressionist composer (1875–1937)

Examples

Maurice Ravel composed the famous orchestral piece Boléro in 1928.
The influence of Maurice Ravel can be heard in many contemporary classical works.
A concert dedicated to Maurice Ravel featured his piano and orchestral compositions.
