countrywoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌn.triˌwʊ.mən / (AmE), / ˈkʌn.trɪˌwʊ.mən / (BrE)
a woman from your own country; a female compatriot
a woman from one's own country

Examples

She welcomed her fellow countrywoman with open arms at the embassy event.
The athlete was proud to compete alongside another talented countrywoman in the finals.
During her travels, she met a countrywoman who became her close friend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: countryman - a man from your own country; a male compatriot

Etymology

Formed from 'country' + 'woman'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkʌntriˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈkʌn.triˌwʊm.ən / (BrE)
a woman who lives in or comes from the countryside, especially one with rural habits or manners
a woman from the countryside

Examples

As a proud countrywoman, she preferred the peace and quiet of rural life.
The countrywoman sold fresh vegetables at the local market every Saturday.
He was amazed by the skill and resourcefulness of the elderly countrywoman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: countryman - a man from the countryside

Etymology

Formed from 'country' + 'woman', in use since the 17th century.
