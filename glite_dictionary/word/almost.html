almost

1. ADV

/ ˈɔlˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈɔːlməʊst / (BrE)
not quite; very nearly
very nearly, not quite

Examples

We almost missed the train, but we made it just in time.
She almost cried when she heard the news.
It's almost midnight, so we should get going.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nearly - very close to but not completely or exactly
  • Antonym: hardly - only just; almost not

Etymology

From Old English 'ealmǣst' (very nearly), from 'eall' (all) + 'mǣst' (most).
