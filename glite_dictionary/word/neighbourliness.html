neighbourliness

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪbərli.nəs / (AmE), / ˈneɪbəlɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being friendly, helpful, and considerate towards one's neighbours
friendliness and helpfulness to neighbours

Examples

Their neighbourliness made moving to the new area much easier for us.
The festival was a chance to celebrate community and neighbourliness.
Acts of neighbourliness can help build trust and connection among residents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly
  • Antonym: hostility - unfriendliness or opposition

Etymology

From 'neighbourly' + '-ness', ultimately from Old English 'nēahgebūr' (near dweller).
