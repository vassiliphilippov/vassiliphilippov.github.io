NASDAQ

1. PROPN

/ ˈnæzˌdæk / (AmE), / ˈnæz.dæk / (BrE)
a computerized system and stock exchange providing brokers with price quotations and facilitating the trading of securities, especially for over-the-counter (OTC) markets.
U.S. electronic stock exchange and system
american

Examples

Many technology companies are listed on the NASDAQ, making it a key index for the sector.
The NASDAQ closed higher today after strong earnings reports from several major firms.
He monitors stock prices on the NASDAQ every morning before making investment decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stock exchange - an organization where stocks and other securities are traded

Etymology

Acronym from National Association of Securities Dealers Automated Quotations.
