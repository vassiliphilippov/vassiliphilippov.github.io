chef-d'oeuvre

1. NOUN

/ ʃɛf ˈdəʊvrə / (AmE), / ʃɛf ˈdɜːv(rə) / (BrE)
an outstanding work of art or craftsmanship; a masterpiece
an outstanding work of art; a masterpiece

Examples

The painting is widely regarded as the artist's chef-d'oeuvre.
Her novel was celebrated as a literary chef-d'oeuvre upon publication.
The architect's chef-d'oeuvre is a landmark known around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masterpiece - a work of outstanding artistry or skill

Etymology

From French 'chef-d'oeuvre' meaning 'chief work'.
