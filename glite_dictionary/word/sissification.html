sissification

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪsi.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪsi.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A sexual practice in which a man is made to act effeminately and dress in traditionally feminine clothing, often as part of erotic humiliation or role-play.
sexual practice involving forced feminization

Examples

Some couples enjoy sissification as a way to explore power dynamics in their relationship.
The theme of sissification often appears in certain types of erotic literature.
He discussed his experience with sissification in an online support group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feminization - the process of making or becoming more feminine, especially in a sexual context

Etymology

From 'sissy' (an effeminate man or boy) + '-fication' (making or causing to become).
