C5

1. NOUN

/ si faɪv / (AmE), / siː faɪv / (BrE)
A standardized envelope size measuring 162 x 229 millimeters, typically used for A5 paper or an A4 sheet folded in half.
standard envelope size (162 x 229 mm)

Examples

The contract was mailed in a C5 envelope to ensure it arrived flat and secure.
She bought a pack of C5 envelopes to send personalized invitations for her event.
A folded A4 letter fits perfectly into a C5 envelope.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: A5 - a standardized paper size (148 x 210 mm)
  • Degree Higher: A4 - a standardized paper size (210 x 297 mm)
  • Degree Lower: DL - a commonly used envelope size (110 x 220 mm)

Etymology

Part of the ISO 269 international envelope size standard, based on the ISO 216 paper size system.
