self-involved

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlf ɪnˈvɑlvd / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf ɪnˈvɒlvd / (BrE)
Only interested in yourself and your own activities, thoughts, or feelings; not considering other people.
absorbed in one's own interests/thoughts

Examples

Her self-involved attitude made it hard for others to connect with her.
The novel's protagonist is a self-involved young man who ignores his friends' problems.
He was too self-involved to notice the needs of his family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-absorbed - excessively interested in oneself and one's own activities
  • Synonym: self-centered - preoccupied with oneself and one's own affairs
  • Antonym: altruistic - showing a selfless concern for others
