repast

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈpæst / (AmE), / rɪˈpɑːst / (BrE)
a meal; the food served and eaten at a particular time
meal; food served at a time

Examples

They enjoyed a simple repast of bread and cheese beside the fire.
After the funeral, the family gathered for a quiet repast together.
The restaurant offers a sumptuous repast every Sunday afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meal - an occasion when food is eaten

Etymology

From Latin 'repastus' (a meal), past participle of 'repascere' (to feed again).
