occipital

1. ADJ

/ əkˈsɪp.ə.t̬əl / (AmE), / ɒkˈsɪp.ɪ.təl / (BrE)
relating to the occiput or the back part of the head or skull
relating to the back of the head/skull
medical

Examples

The occipital bone forms the back and base of the skull.
She suffered a minor injury to the occipital region during the accident.
The doctor explained the headache was originating from the occipital nerves.

Synset Examples

occipital bone

Etymology

From Latin occipitalis, from occiput (back of the head).
