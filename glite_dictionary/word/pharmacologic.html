pharmacologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɑːrməkəˈlɑːdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌfɑːməkəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving pharmacology, especially the actions of drugs on living systems
relating to pharmacology or drug action
medical

Examples

The study examined the pharmacologic properties of the new medication.
She experienced several pharmacologic side effects after starting the treatment.
Researchers are exploring pharmacologic interventions for managing chronic pain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pharmacological - relating to pharmacology or the action of drugs

Etymology

From pharmacology + -ic, deriving from Greek 'pharmakon' (drug) and 'logos' (study).
