stockinged

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɑkɪŋd / (AmE), / ˈstɒkɪŋd / (BrE)
wearing stockings on the feet or legs.
wearing stockings

Examples

She entered the room in her stockinged feet, looking for her shoes.
He padded softly across the wooden floor, stockinged and silent.
The dancers performed the routine stockinged to match the play’s historical costume.

Synset Examples

walks about in his stockinged feet

Related Terms

  • Antonym: barefoot - without any covering on the feet

Etymology

From 'stocking' + the adjectival suffix '-ed'.
