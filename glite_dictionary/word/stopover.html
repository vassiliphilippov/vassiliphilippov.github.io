stopover

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑpˌoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈstɒpˌəʊvə / (BrE)
A short stay at a place between parts of a journey, especially when changing planes or continuing to another destination.
temporary stop during a journey

Examples

We had a brief stopover in Singapore before continuing our flight to Sydney.
The journey included a two-day stopover in Rome for sightseeing.
Due to delays, our stopover in Paris was much longer than expected.

Synset Examples

there is a stopover to change planes in Chicago

Related Terms

  • Synonym: layover - a period of waiting between parts of a journey, especially in air travel
