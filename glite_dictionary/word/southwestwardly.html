southwestwardly

1. ADV

/ ˌsaʊθˈwɛstwɚdli / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈwɛstwədli / (BrE)
In a southwestward direction.
in a southwestward direction

Examples

The wind blew southwestwardly across the open plain.
They traveled southwestwardly until they reached the coast.
The current flowed southwestwardly, pushing the boat off its charted course.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: southwestward - toward the southwest

Etymology

From 'southwestward' + '-ly'.
