nevus

1. NOUN

/ ˈnivəs / (AmE), / ˈniːvəs / (BrE)
A congenital blemish on the skin, such as a birthmark or mole.
congenital skin blemish (birthmark or mole)
medical

Examples

The doctor examined the large nevus on the patient's shoulder for any unusual changes.
Her nevus had been present since birth and posed no health concerns.
A congenital nevus sometimes requires close monitoring by a dermatologist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mole - A pigmented spot on the skin, often benign
  • Synonym: birthmark - A colored mark on the skin present from birth

Etymology

From Latin 'naevus' meaning birthmark or mole.
