necklace

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛk.lɪs / (AmE), / ˈnɛk.lɪs / (BrE)
A piece of jewelry consisting of a chain, string, or cord, often with gems or decorations, worn around the neck.
ornamental chain worn around the neck

Examples

She wore a beautiful diamond necklace to the party.
The artist crafted a unique necklace from beads and shells.
He bought his mother a gold necklace for her birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: choker - a close-fitting necklace worn high on the neck
  • Antonym: bracelet - an ornamental band or chain worn on the wrist

Etymology

From Old French 'nicolace', related to 'neck' + 'lace'.
