ambitiously

1. ADV

/ æmˈbɪʃəsli / (AmE), / æmˈbɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows ambition, energy, and determination to achieve success.
in an ambitious and energetic way

Examples

She ambitiously started her own business right after finishing university.
The city ambitiously planned to become carbon neutral by 2030.
He ambitiously tackled the most challenging problems in his field.

Synset Examples

she pursued her goals ambitiously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ambitious - having a strong desire for success or achievement

Etymology

From 'ambitious' + '-ly', ultimately from Latin 'ambitio' (a going around, canvassing for votes).
