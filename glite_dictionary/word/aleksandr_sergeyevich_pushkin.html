Aleksandr Sergeyevich Pushkin

1. PROPN

/ əˈlɛksændər sərˈɡjeɪvɪtʃ ˈpʊʃkɪn / (AmE), / alɪkˈsandə səˈɡjɛvɪtʃ ˈpʊʃkɪn / (BrE)
(1799–1837) A major Russian poet and writer, regarded as the founder of modern Russian literature.
Russian poet and writer (1799–1837)

Examples
