naval

1. ADJ

/ ˈneɪ.vəl / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.vəl / (BrE)
connected with, belonging to, or used by a navy
relating to a navy

Examples

The country has increased its naval presence in the disputed waters.
He served as a naval officer during the war.
The museum showcases an impressive collection of naval artifacts.

Synset Examples

naval history
naval commander
naval vessels

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military - relating to armies, soldiers, or armed forces

Etymology

From Latin 'navalis' (pertaining to ships), from 'navis' (ship).
