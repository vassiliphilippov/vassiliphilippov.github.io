C3

1. NOUN

/ si ˈθri / (AmE), / siː ˈθriː / (BrE)
An international envelope size measuring 324 x 458 mm, designed to hold A3 sheets without folding.
international envelope size (324 x 458 mm)

Examples

Please send the documents in a C3 envelope to avoid folding them.
We need to order more C3 envelopes for the office supplies room.
A C3 envelope can fit an A3 sheet without any creases.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: C4 - an international envelope size measuring 229 x 324 mm
  • Degree Higher: C2 - an international envelope size measuring 458 x 648 mm

Etymology

Standardized in the ISO 269 envelope series, related to the ISO 216 A-series paper formats.
