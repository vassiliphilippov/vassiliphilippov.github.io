XIII

1. NUM

/ θɜrˈtiːn / (AmE), / ˌθɜːˈtiːn / (BrE)
the number 13 represented in Roman numerals.
Roman numeral for 13

Examples

The clock struck XIII, an impossible time in reality.
The king’s reign began in the year XIII of the new calendar.
She carved XIII into the tree as a mysterious symbol.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 13 - the Arabic numeral for thirteen

Etymology

From Latin numeral system, where 'X' means 10 and 'III' means 3.
