cloveroot

1. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊ.vəˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈkləʊ.vəˌruːt / (BrE)
A hairy Eurasian plant with small yellow flowers and an astringent root formerly used in medicine.
hairy Eurasian plant with astringent root

Examples

Early herbalists believed that cloveroot could be used to treat digestive issues.
The fields were dotted with patches of cloveroot in full bloom.
Traditional remedies sometimes called for dried cloveroot as an ingredient.
