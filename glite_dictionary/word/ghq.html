GHQ

1. NOUN

/ dʒi eɪtʃ kju / (AmE), / dʒiː eɪtʃ kjuː / (BrE)
abbreviation for General Headquarters, the administrative center from which a military commander directs and organizes armed forces
military command center (abbreviation)

Examples

The general issued orders to the troops directly from the GHQ.
During the conflict, all major decisions were coordinated at the GHQ.
The new location for the GHQ offers better security and communications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: General Headquarters - the central administrative command for military operations

Etymology

Abbreviation of General Headquarters.
