to computerize

1. VERB

/ kəmˈpjuːtəˌraɪz / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtəraɪz / (BrE)
to use a computer to control or operate a function, process, or system, often to increase efficiency or automation
to control or operate by computer

Examples

Many companies have decided to computerize their payroll systems for greater accuracy.
The factory aims to computerize all of its production lines within the next year.
They plan to computerize the traffic lights to improve traffic flow in the city.

Synset Examples

They computerized the car industry
we live in a cybernated age
cybernate a factory

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to automate - to use machines or computers to do work previously done by people

Etymology

From computer + -ize, formed in English in the 20th century as computers became widespread.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01722811-v
Synset POS: v

2. VERB

/ kəmˈpjuːtəˌraɪz / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtəraɪz / (BrE)
to equip with computers or convert to a system operated or controlled by computers
equip or convert with computers

Examples

The company decided to computerize its payroll system to increase efficiency.
After months of planning, the hospital finally managed to computerize all patient records.
Many libraries have started to computerize their catalogs for easier public access.

Synset Examples

Our office is fully computerized now

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to automate - to make a process operate automatically, especially by computers or machines

Etymology

From 'computer' + '-ize', formed in English in the mid-20th century with the spread of computers.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02342360-v
Synset POS: v

3. VERB

/ kəmˈpjutəˌraɪz / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtəraɪz / (BrE)
to store information in a computer, or to convert a system or process to be operated or controlled by computers
to store or process by computer

Examples

More companies are planning to computerize their records for efficiency and easy retrieval.
All patient information will be computerized by the end of the year.
The project aims to computerize library catalogs to improve access for students.

Synset Examples

computerized dictionary

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to digitize - to convert information into a digital format

Etymology

From 'computer' + '-ize', formed in the mid-20th century with the rise of computers.
