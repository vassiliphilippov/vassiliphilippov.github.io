Silex

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪlɛks / (AmE), / ˈsaɪlɛks / (BrE)
A vacuum coffee maker consisting of two chambers where vapor pressure and vacuum produce brewed coffee.
type of vacuum coffee maker

Examples

My grandmother still uses her old Silex for making coffee every morning.
The museum featured a display of vintage Silex coffee makers from the 1930s.
Coffee enthusiasts often seek out a Silex for its unique brewing process and distinctive flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vacuum coffee maker - coffee brewing device using vacuum to brew

Etymology

Originally a trademark, from Silex Company, the maker of this coffee pot, early 20th century.
