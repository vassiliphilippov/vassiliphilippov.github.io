puckishly

1. ADV

/ ˈpʌkɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈpʌkɪʃli / (BrE)
in a playfully mischievous or impishly bold manner
in a mischievous, playful way

Examples

She smiled puckishly as she revealed her surprise plan.
He puckishly rearranged his colleague's desk to confuse him.
The child looked puckishly at his mother before hiding her keys.

Synset Examples

she asked him impishly to come in

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impishly - in a mischievously playful manner

Etymology

From 'puckish', based on Puck, a mischievous fairy character in English folklore and Shakespeare.
