double-chinned

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʌbl ˌtʃɪnd / (AmE), / ˌdʌbl ˈtʃɪnd / (BrE)
having sagging folds of flesh beneath the chin or lower jaw
having a double chin

Examples

The old man was double-chinned and wore wire-rimmed glasses.
She became a little double-chinned after the holidays, but didn't mind.
In the photograph, he appears red-faced and double-chinned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double chin - an extra layer of fat beneath the chin
