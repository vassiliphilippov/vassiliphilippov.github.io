ethnologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛθnəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛθnəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the scientific study of different peoples and their cultures (ethnology).
relating to ethnology or the study of cultures

Examples

The museum displayed several ethnologic collections gathered from different continents.
He conducted ethnologic research during his time in Papua New Guinea.
Her dissertation focused on ethnologic theories applied to indigenous societies.

Synset Examples

ethnological field work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ethnological - relating to ethnology

Etymology

From ethnology + -ic, ultimately from Greek ethnos (nation, people).
