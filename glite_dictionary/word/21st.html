21st

1. ADJ

/ ˈtwɛnti fɜrst / (AmE), / ˈtwɛnti fɜːst / (BrE)
coming next after the twentieth in position; ordinal form of twenty-one
ordinal number for 21

Examples

He finished in 21st place out of 100 participants.
Her birthday is on the 21st of September every year.
The company will celebrate its 21st anniversary next month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twenty-first - the ordinal number matching 21; alternative spelling
  • Degree Higher: 22nd - coming next after the twenty-first in position
  • Degree Lower: 20th - coming next before the twenty-first in position
