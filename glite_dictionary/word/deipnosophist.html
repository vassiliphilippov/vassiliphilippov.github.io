deipnosophist

1. NOUN

/ daɪpˈnɑːsəfɪst / (AmE), / daɪpˈnɒsəfɪst / (BrE)
A person who is skilled in lively or learned conversation at the dinner table.
person skilled in dinner table conversation

Examples

She quickly proved herself a true deipnosophist, charming everyone at the dinner with her witty stories.
As a renowned deipnosophist, he was always the center of attention at social gatherings involving meals.
The party lacked a deipnosophist, and the conversation fizzled after the main course.

Etymology

From Greek deipnosophistēs, meaning 'banquet wise man,' from deipnon (dinner) + sophistes (wise man, expert).
