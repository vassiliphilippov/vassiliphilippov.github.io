adjustable

1. ADJ

/ əˈdʒʌstəbəl / (AmE), / əˈdʒʌstəbl / (BrE)
able to be changed or moved to suit different needs or conditions
able to be changed or moved

Examples

The adjustable desk lamp allows you to direct the light where you need it.
She bought an adjustable chair to make her workspace more comfortable.
The adjustable straps on the backpack ensure a perfect fit for everyone.

Synset Examples

adjustable seat belts

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fixed - not able to be changed or moved
  • Synonym: adaptable - able to change or be modified for different situations

Etymology

From 'adjust' + '-able', indicating capability.
