trombonist

1. NOUN

/ trɑmˈboʊnɪst / (AmE), / trɒmˈbəʊnɪst / (BrE)
a person who plays the trombone, especially as a professional musician
person who plays the trombone

Examples

She is a renowned trombonist who has performed with several major orchestras.
The jazz band hired a new trombonist to strengthen their brass section.
As a skilled trombonist, he often gives master classes to students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musician - a person who plays a musical instrument, especially professionally

Etymology

From Italian 'trombone' (large trumpet) + English '-ist' (a person who practices a certain art).
