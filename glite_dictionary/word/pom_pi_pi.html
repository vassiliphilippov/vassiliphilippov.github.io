pom pi pi

1. NOUN

/ pɑm pi pi / (AmE), / pɒm pi pi / (BrE)
A rhythmic sound or beat, especially as imitated in music or children's songs.
rhythmic sound or beat (music)
informal

Examples

The drummer tapped out a pom pi pi that got everyone clapping along.
During the parade, the marching band kept a steady pom pi pi on their snare drums.
The children's song begins with a catchy pom pi pi that repeats throughout the melody.
