George Harrison

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɔrdʒ ˈhærəsən / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒ ˈhærɪsən / (BrE)
(1943–2001) English rock musician and songwriter, best known as the lead guitarist of the Beatles.
English musician; Beatles guitarist

Examples

George Harrison wrote several classic songs for the Beatles, including 'Here Comes the Sun'.
Many fans admire George Harrison for his contributions to music and his interest in Indian culture.
After the Beatles split, George Harrison enjoyed a successful solo career.
