neuroglial

1. ADJ

/ nʊˈroʊɡliəl / (AmE), / njʊəˈrɒɡlɪəl / (BrE)
Relating to or consisting of neuroglia, the supportive tissue of the nervous system.
relating to neuroglia
medical

Examples

The microscope revealed abnormal neuroglial cells within the brain tissue.
Researchers are studying the role of neuroglial interactions in neurodegenerative diseases.
Damage to neuroglial tissue can severely affect nerve cell function.

Synset Examples

neuroglial tissue

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glial - relating to glia, supportive cells in the nervous system

Etymology

From 'neuro-' (nerve) and 'glia' (glue) in Greek; referring to the supportive cells of the nervous system.
