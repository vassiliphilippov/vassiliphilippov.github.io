gentleman

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛn.tl̩.mən / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛn.tl̩.mən / (BrE)
A man who is courteous, honorable, and has refined manners and behavior.
a courteous, refined man

Examples

He acted like a true gentleman, holding the door open for everyone.
Despite the heated debate, James remained a gentleman throughout the discussion.
She thanked the gentleman who helped her carry her bags up the stairs.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lady - a woman who is polite and refined
  • Antonym: gentlewoman - a woman of high social standing and refinement
  • Synonym: gentlemen - plural form of 'gentleman'

Etymology

From Middle English 'gentilman', from Old French, from 'gentil' (of noble birth) + 'homme' (man).
