languorously

1. ADV

/ ˈlæŋɡərəsli / (AmE), / ˈlaŋɡərəsli / (BrE)
In a manner that is relaxed, slow, and showing a lack of energy or vitality.
in a slow, relaxed, and lazy manner

Examples

She stretched languorously in the morning sun, enjoying the warm breeze from the window.
The cat lay languorously across the windowsill, blinking at the world outside.
He walked languorously along the beach, letting the soft sand slow his steps.

Synset Examples

he was sprawling languorously on the sofa

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lazily - in a lazy manner
  • Antonym: energetically - in an energetic manner

Etymology

From 'languorous' + '-ly'; 'languorous' from Latin 'languere' (to be weak or faint).
