n

1. NOUN

/ ɛn / (AmE), / ɛn / (BrE)
the fourteenth letter of the English alphabet
the 14th letter of the alphabet

Examples

The word 'night' begins with the letter n.
Please write an uppercase N on the board.
The letter n is pronounced /ɛn/ in English.

Etymology

From Latin letter 'N', from Greek 'nu', from Phoenician 'nun'.
