sentimentalist

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛn.təˈmɛn.təl.ɪst / (AmE), / ˌsɛn.tɪˈmɛn.təl.ɪst / (BrE)
a person who is prone to excessive emotional feelings or sentimentality
person given to excessive sentimentality

Examples

As a true sentimentalist, she kept every letter her grandmother had ever written to her.
The author was criticized as a sentimentalist for his overly emotional writing style.
Although he called himself a realist, many saw him as more of a sentimentalist at heart.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: realist - a person who accepts a situation as it is and deals with it accordingly
  • Synonym: sentimental - having or showing tender, emotional feelings, often excessively so

Etymology

From 'sentimental' + '-ist', influenced by French 'sentimentaliste'.
