estranging

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈstreɪndʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈstreɪndʒɪŋ / (BrE)
causing one to feel out of place or alienated
causing alienation or disconnection

Examples

The city's rapid changes had an estranging effect on longtime residents.
Her estranging behavior made it difficult for her to make friends.
There was something estranging about the abandoned, silent house.

Synset Examples

the landscape was estranging

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alienating - causing someone to feel isolated or estranged

Etymology

From the verb "estrange," which comes from Old French "estrangier," from Latin "extraneare" (treat as a stranger).
