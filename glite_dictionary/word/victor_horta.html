Victor Horta

1. PROPN

/ ˈvɪktər ˈhɔrtə / (AmE), / ˈvɪktə ˈhɔːtə / (BrE)
(1861–1947) Belgian architect considered a leader in art nouveau architecture.
Belgian art nouveau architect (1861–1947)

Examples

The work of Victor Horta had a profound influence on the development of art nouveau in Europe.
One of the most famous buildings designed by Victor Horta is the Hôtel Tassel in Brussels.
Architectural historians often cite Victor Horta as a pioneer of modernist design.
