quiaquia

1. NOUN

/ kiːəˈkiːə / (AmE), / kiːəˈkiːə / (BrE)
a small fusiform fish found in the western Atlantic Ocean, typically characterized by its slender, tapering body
small fish of the western Atlantic

Examples

Fishermen often catch quiaquia near the coastal reefs of the Caribbean.
The slender shape of the quiaquia makes it easily recognizable among other local fish.
