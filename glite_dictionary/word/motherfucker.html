motherfucker

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌðərˌfʌkər / (AmE), / ˈmʌðəˌfʌkə / (BrE)
A highly offensive and vulgar term of abuse for a person regarded with contempt or anger.
offensive vulgar term of abuse
offensive vulgar

Examples

He called his opponent a motherfucker during the heated argument.
Some people use the word motherfucker to shock or insult others.
The film was rated R because a character shouted 'motherfucker!' several times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bastard - offensive term for an unpleasant or despicable person
  • Synonym: asshole - offensive and vulgar term for an annoying or despicable person
  • Antonym: gentleman - a polite or honorable man, used as an antonym in context

2. NOUN

/ ˈmʌðərˌfʌkər / (AmE), / ˈmʌðəˌfʌkə / (BrE)
A highly offensive and vulgar term used to insult someone, often implying that they are irritating, contemptible, or mean.
offensive, vulgar insult for a person
offensive vulgar

Examples

I can't believe that motherfucker lied to me again.
The movie's villain was a real motherfucker who showed no mercy.
He shouted at the driver, calling him a motherfucker in a fit of rage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bastard - an offensive term for a despicable person
  • Synonym: jerk - informal term for an annoying person
  • Antonym: gentleman - a polite, honorable man (opposite of insult)
