teenageritis

1. NOUN

/ ˌtiːnˌeɪdʒərˈaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌtiːnˌeɪdʒərˈaɪtɪs / (BrE)
humorous term for the typical, often exasperating behaviors associated with teenagers, such as laziness or moodiness
humorous term for typical teenage behavior
informal slang

Examples

Her refusal to clean her room is just another teenageritis moment.
The teacher joked that half his class was suffering from teenageritis during exams.
Whenever he sleeps past noon on weekends, his parents call it a case of teenageritis.

Synset Examples

His constant laziness is a clear case of teenageritis.

Etymology

Formed from 'teenager' + '-itis', mimicking medical conditions to suggest 'a disease of being a teenager'.
