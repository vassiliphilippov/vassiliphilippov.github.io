podlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɑdˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɒdˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or shaped like a pod
resembling a pod in shape or form

Examples

The plant produces podlike fruits that resemble green beans.
Some marine animals have podlike bodies adapted for streamlined movement in water.
The architect designed podlike structures for the futuristic housing development.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: pod - an elongated seed vessel of a plant, such as a pea or bean; a case containing seeds

Etymology

From 'pod' + '-like', indicating similarity in shape.
