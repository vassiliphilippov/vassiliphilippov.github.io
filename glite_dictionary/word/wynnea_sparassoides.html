Wynnea sparassoides

1. NOUN

/ wɪnˈiː.ə spærəˈsɔɪdiːz / (AmE), / wɪnˈiː.ə spærəˈsɔɪdiːz / (BrE)
a species of fungus with a long, solid stalk embedded in soil and a yellow-brown head resembling a cauliflower
species of stalked fungus with cauliflower-like head

The striking appearance of Wynnea sparassoides makes it easy to spot in forest undergrowth.
