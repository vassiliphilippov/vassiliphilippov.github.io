Geomys

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒiːəˌmaɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːəʊˌmɪs / (BrE)
a genus of burrowing rodents in the family Geomyidae, known as pocket gophers, native to North America
genus of burrowing rodents (pocket gophers)

Examples

Researchers observed that the Geomys populations had adapted well to the altered grassland environment.
The burrows of Geomys can significantly impact soil composition and aeration.
Several species within the genus Geomys are commonly referred to as pocket gophers in North America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pocket gopher - common name for rodents in the family Geomyidae

Etymology

From Greek geo- (earth) + mys (mouse), meaning 'earth mouse'.
