Thunbergia

1. NOUN

/ θʌnˈbɜrɡiə / (AmE), / θʌnˈbɜːɡiə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical herbs or climbing vines of the family Acanthaceae, some species of which are grown as ornamental plants
a genus of tropical vines or herbs

Examples

The Thunbergia vine added a splash of color to their garden fence.
Several species of Thunbergia are popular as ornamental plants in tropical regions.
We planted Thunbergia seeds along the trellis to create a natural green screen.

Etymology

Named after Carl Peter Thunberg, Swedish botanist (1743–1828).
