horse-drawn

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɔrsˌdrɔn / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsˌdrɔːn / (BrE)
(of a vehicle or implement) pulled by a horse or horses rather than powered by an engine.
pulled by a horse or horses

Examples

The town offers a historic tour in a horse-drawn carriage each weekend.
We enjoyed a peaceful ride through the park in a horse-drawn buggy.
Many cities once relied on horse-drawn streetcars for public transport.

Synset Examples

a horse-drawn carriage

Related Terms

  • Antonym: motorized - (of a vehicle or implement) powered by a motor or engine
