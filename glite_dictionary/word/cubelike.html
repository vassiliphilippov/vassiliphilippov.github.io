cubelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈkjuːbˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈkjuːbˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the shape or appearance of a cube
shaped like a cube

Examples

The ancient stone had a distinctly cubelike form, with sharp edges and even faces.
Engineers designed a cubelike structure to maximize storage efficiency.
His sculpture featured a small, cubelike animal perched on a plinth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cube-shaped - having the shape of a cube
  • Antonym: spherical - shaped like a sphere
