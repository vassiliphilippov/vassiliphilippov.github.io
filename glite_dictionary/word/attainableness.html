attainableness

1. NOUN

/ əˈteɪnəblnəs / (AmE), / əˈteɪnəblnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being possible to achieve or reach
state of being achievable

Examples

The attainableness of the project's goals was questioned by several board members.
She admired the attainableness of his dreams, as they were both ambitious and realistic.
Debating the attainableness of world peace is a common topic in international forums.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attainability - the possibility of being achieved or attained
  • Antonym: unattainableness - the state of being impossible to obtain or achieve

Etymology

From attainable + -ness, modeled on standard English word formation.
