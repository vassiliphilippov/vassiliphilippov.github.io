Dubuque

1. PROPN

/ dəˈbjuːk / (AmE), / djʊˈbjuːk / (BrE)
A city in eastern Iowa located on the Mississippi River, known for its historic architecture and riverfront.
city in eastern Iowa, USA

Examples

We visited Dubuque last summer and enjoyed a riverboat cruise on the Mississippi.
The National Mississippi River Museum is one of the highlights of Dubuque.
She grew up in Dubuque before moving to Chicago for college.

Etymology

Named after Julien Dubuque, a French-Canadian explorer.
