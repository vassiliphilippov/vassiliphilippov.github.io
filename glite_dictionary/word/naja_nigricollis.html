Naja nigricollis

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑː.dʒə nɪˈɡrɪk.ə.lɪs / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.dʒə nɪˈɡrɪk.ə.lɪs / (BrE)
An aggressive species of cobra widely distributed in Africa, known for rarely biting but spitting venom that may cause blindness.
a spitting cobra species of Africa

Examples

The Naja nigricollis is feared not for its bite but for its ability to spit venom accurately.
Researchers studied the behavior of Naja nigricollis in its natural African habitat.
If the venom of Naja nigricollis enters the eyes, it can cause temporary or permanent blindness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black-necked spitting cobra - Common name for Naja nigricollis, an African spitting cobra

Etymology

From Latin; genus name 'Naja' from Sanskrit 'nāga' (snake), species name means 'black-necked'.
