sodomist

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːdəmɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɒdəmɪst / (BrE)
a person who engages in anal intercourse, especially a man with another man
person engaging in anal intercourse

Examples

The law historically punished sodomists with severe penalties.
He was wrongfully labeled a sodomist in the scandalous article.
Activists argued that the term sodomist carried negative connotations and reflected outdated attitudes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bugger - person who engages in anal intercourse

Etymology

From Medieval Latin sodomista, from Sodom (the biblical city).
