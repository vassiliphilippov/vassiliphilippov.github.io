Kursk

1. PROPN

/ kʊrsk / (AmE), / kʊəsk / (BrE)
a city in southwestern Russia, known for its historical significance and as the site of a major World War II battle
city in southwestern Russia

Examples

The Battle of Kursk was one of the largest tank battles in history.
She traveled across Russia, finally arriving in Kursk for a business meeting.
Kursk is known for its cultural landmarks and architectural heritage.
