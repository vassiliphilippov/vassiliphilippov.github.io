mistreated

1. ADJ

/ mɪsˈtritɪd / (AmE), / mɪsˈtriːtɪd / (BrE)
treated in a cruel or unfair way; subject to mistreatment.
treated cruelly or unfairly

Examples

The shelter took in several mistreated animals who needed urgent care.
She spoke openly about her experience as a mistreated employee in the company.
Laws now protect children from being mistreated by adults.

Synset Examples

an abused wife

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abused - treated with cruelty or violence
  • Antonym: well-treated - treated in a kind or fair manner

Etymology

From 'mis-' (wrongly) + 'treated', past participle of 'treat'.
