souchong

1. NOUN

/ suːˈtʃɔŋ / (AmE), / suːˈtʃɒŋ / (BrE)
a variety of black tea from China, noted for its large leaves and distinctive flavor.
type of Chinese black tea

Examples

We enjoyed a cup of souchong after dinner, savoring its robust aroma.
The tea shop offers several varieties, including souchong and Earl Grey.
She prefers souchong to other black teas because of its unique taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lapsang souchong - a type of souchong black tea with a smoky flavor

Etymology

From Chinese (Cantonese) 'siu zung', meaning 'small kind'.
