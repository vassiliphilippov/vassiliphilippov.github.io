relievo

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈliːvoʊ / (AmE), / rɪˈliːvəʊ / (BrE)
sculpture in which shapes are carved or modeled so as to stand out from a flat background; relief sculpture.
sculpture projecting from a flat background

Examples

The museum features a remarkable relievo depicting scenes from ancient mythology.
A marble relievo adorned the facade of the historic building.
The artist spent months crafting the intricate details of the relievo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relief - sculpture in which the figures project from a supporting background

Etymology

From Italian 'rilievo', from 'rilievare' (to raise), ultimately from Latin 'levis' (light, raised).
