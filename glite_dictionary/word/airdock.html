airdock

1. NOUN

/ ˈer.dɑk / (AmE), / ˈeə.dɒk / (BrE)
a large building at an airport used for storing and maintaining aircraft
large airport building for aircraft storage

Examples

The new airdock can accommodate even the largest passenger jets.
Several technicians worked late into the night inside the airdock.
During the storm, the airplane was safely sheltered in the airdock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hangar - a large building for housing aircraft

Etymology

Formed from 'air' + 'dock', modelled after 'dock' as a place for servicing vehicles or ships.
