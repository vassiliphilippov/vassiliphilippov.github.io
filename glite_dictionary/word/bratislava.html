Bratislava

1. PROPN

/ ˌbrɑːtɪˈslɑːvə / (AmE), / ˌbrætɪˈslɑːvə / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Slovakia, situated on the Danube River near the borders with Austria and Hungary
capital city of Slovakia

Examples

She spent two weeks exploring the historic old town of Bratislava.
The conference will take place in Bratislava next month.
Many tourists visit Bratislava for its vibrant culture and beautiful architecture.

Etymology

From Slovak Bratislava, formerly known in German as Pressburg and in Hungarian as Pozsony.
