to revive

1. VERB

/ rɪˈvaɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈvaɪv / (BrE)
to make someone regain consciousness or become alert again after being unconscious or fainting
to bring someone back to consciousness

Examples

The paramedics managed to revive her after she fainted at the concert.
Splashing cold water on his face helped revive him.
They tried for several minutes to revive the swimmer who had stopped breathing.

Synset Examples

The doctors revived the comatose man

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to resuscitate - to revive someone from unconsciousness or apparent death

Etymology

From Latin 'revivere' (to live again), from 're-' (again) + 'vivere' (to live).
