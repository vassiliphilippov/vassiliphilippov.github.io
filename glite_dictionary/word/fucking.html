3. ADJ

/ ˈfʌkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfʌkɪŋ / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) Used to emphasize the degree of something, often to express anger, surprise, or intensity.
vulgar intensifier (emphasizes degree)
informal vulgar

Examples

I can't believe how fucking cold it is outside today.
That was a fucking amazing concert last night!
He was fucking furious when he found out what happened.

Related Terms

Etymology

Derived from the verb 'fuck' with the -ing suffix, first used as an intensifier in the 20th century.
