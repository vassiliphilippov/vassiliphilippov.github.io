unthought

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈθɔt / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɔːt / (BrE)
(of something) not imagined, considered, or expected; not thought of before.
not imagined or expected

Examples

The discovery brought an unthought advantage to the researchers.
She faced challenges from an unthought direction.
His generosity was an unthought blessing in a difficult time.

Synset Examples

an unhoped-for piece of luck
an unthought advantage
an unthought-of place to find the key

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unimaginable - impossible to imagine or comprehend
  • Synonym: unexpected - not expected or anticipated

Etymology

Formed from prefix un- (not) + past participle of think.
