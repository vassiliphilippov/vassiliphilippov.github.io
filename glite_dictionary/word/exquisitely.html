exquisitely

1. ADV

/ ɪkˈskwɪzɪtli / (AmE), / ɪkˈskwɪzɪtli / (BrE)
In a very delicate, refined, or beautiful manner; with great care or detail.
in a delicate or refined manner

Examples

The violinist played the piece exquisitely, captivating the entire audience.
Her home was exquisitely decorated with hand-painted tiles and delicate fabrics.
He crafted the necklace exquisitely, paying attention to every tiny detail.

Synset Examples

finely shaped features
her fine drawn body

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beautifully - in a beautiful manner
  • Antonym: crudely - in a rough or unrefined manner

Etymology

From exquisite + -ly; 'exquisite' from Latin 'exquisitus' (carefully sought out).
