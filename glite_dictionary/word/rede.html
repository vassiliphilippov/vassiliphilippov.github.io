to rede

1. VERB

/ riːd / (AmE), / riːd / (BrE)
to interpret or explain the meaning of something (archaic)
interpret or explain (archaic)
archaic

Examples

The wise elder was asked to rede the mysterious signs found in the forest.
She attempted to rede the ancient script inscribed on the stone tablet.
In days of old, scholars would rede the stars to predict the fortunes of kings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to interpret - to explain the meaning of something
  • Synonym: to explain - to make something clear by description or detail

Etymology

Old English rǣdan (to advise, interpret, read).
