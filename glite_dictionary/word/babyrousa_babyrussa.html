Babyrousa babyrussa

1. NOUN

/ bəˌbaɪˌroʊsə bəˈbaɪˌrʌsə / (AmE), / bəˌbaɪˌruːsə bəˈbaɪˌrʌsə / (BrE)
a wild pig native to Indonesia, notable for its large, curved upper canine teeth
wild pig of Indonesia with curved tusks

Examples

The Babyrousa babyrussa is known for its distinctive upward-curving tusks.
Researchers observed a herd of Babyrousa babyrussa in the Sulawesi forests.
Unlike other pigs, the Babyrousa babyrussa uses its tusks more for display than for fighting.

Etymology

From Indonesian and Malay 'babirusa', meaning 'pig-deer'.
