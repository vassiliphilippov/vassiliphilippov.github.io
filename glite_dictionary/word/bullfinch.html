bullfinch

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlˌfɪntʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʊl.fɪntʃ / (BrE)
a small European finch with a mostly black and white body and a bright red throat and breast, known for its distinctive coloring and mellow call
a small European songbird with a red breast

Examples

A male bullfinch perched on the branch, its red breast bright against the snow.
Gardeners often spot a bullfinch feeding quietly among the blossoms.
The guide pointed out a flock of bullfinches in the woodland clearing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finch - a small to medium-sized songbird of the family Fringillidae
