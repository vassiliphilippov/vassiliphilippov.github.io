sliding

1. ADJ

/ ˈslaɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈslaɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
moving smoothly and continuously, or designed to move in this way.
moving smoothly and continuously

Examples

The sliding doors opened silently as she approached.
A sliding panel revealed a secret room behind the bookshelf.
We installed a sliding window in the kitchen for better ventilation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gliding - moving smoothly and continuously, similar to sliding

Etymology

Present participle of the verb 'slide', ultimately from Old English 'slīdan'.
