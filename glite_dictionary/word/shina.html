Shina

1. NOUN

/ ʃiː.nə / (AmE), / ˈʃiː.nə / (BrE)
a Dardic language spoken in northern Kashmir, primarily by the Shina people
a language of northern Kashmir

Examples

The Shina language is considered one of the oldest in the region.
She is fluent in both Shina and Urdu.
Linguists have documented several dialects of Shina across different valleys.
