Dar es Salaam

1. PROPN

/ ˌdɑr ɛs səˈlɑm / (AmE), / ˌdɑːr ɛs səˈlɑːm / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Tanzania, located on the eastern coast along the Indian Ocean
largest city of Tanzania

Examples

Many tourists visit Dar es Salaam before traveling to Zanzibar or the Serengeti.
The port of Dar es Salaam handles much of Tanzania’s international trade.
He moved to Dar es Salaam for work and soon grew to love the city's vibrant markets.

Etymology

From Arabic دار السلام (dār as-salām), meaning 'abode of peace'.
