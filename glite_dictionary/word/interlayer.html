interlayer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.tɚˌleɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈɪn.təˌleɪ.ə / (BrE)
a layer that is placed between other layers, often to separate, bind, or add functional properties
layer placed between others

Examples

The glass used in windshields contains an interlayer to prevent shattering.
Researchers studied the properties of the interlayer between the metal sheets.
A special adhesive interlayer was used to bond the different materials together.

Etymology

From inter- (between) + layer.
