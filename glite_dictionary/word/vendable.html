vendable

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɛndəbəl / (AmE), / ˈvɛndəbəl / (BrE)
suitable or fit to be offered for sale; marketable.
fit to be sold

Examples

The store stocks only vendable items that meet strict quality standards.
Before a product launch, companies ensure it is vendable in major markets.
The farmer worked to make his produce more vendable by improving packaging.

Synset Examples

marketable produce

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marketable - able to be sold or suitable for sale

Etymology

From Latin 'vendere' (to sell) via Old French 'vendable'.
