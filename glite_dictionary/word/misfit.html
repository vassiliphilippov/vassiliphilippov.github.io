misfit

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪsˌfɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɪs.fɪt / (BrE)
a person whose behavior or attitudes set them apart from others and who is unable to adapt to their circumstances
person who does not fit in

Examples

As a child, he always felt like a misfit at school.
The club was a haven for misfits and nonconformists.
Despite being called a misfit, she embraced her uniqueness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outsider - a person who does not belong to a particular group
  • Antonym: conformist - a person who behaves in accordance with established standards

Etymology

From prefix 'mis-' (wrongly, badly) + 'fit' (suitable).
