noncomprehensively

1. ADV

/ nɑnˌkɑmprɪˈhɛnsɪvli / (AmE), / nɒnˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that is not comprehensive; in a limited or incomplete manner.
in a limited or incomplete way

Examples

The report covered the issue noncomprehensively, leaving out several important factors.
We reviewed the cases noncomprehensively due to time constraints.
He answered the question noncomprehensively, omitting key details.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: comprehensively - in a thorough and complete way
  • Synonym: partially - to some extent but not fully

Etymology

From 'noncomprehensive' (not comprehensive) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
