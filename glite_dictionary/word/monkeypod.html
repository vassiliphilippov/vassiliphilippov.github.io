monkeypod

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌŋkiˌpɑd / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋkiˌpɒd / (BrE)
A large tropical American tree (Samanea saman) with bipinnate leaves, clusters of flowers with crimson stamens, and seed pods often eaten by cattle; also called the rain tree.
a tropical American tree with showy flowers and seed pods

Examples

The monkeypod tree provides ample shade in tropical gardens.
Artisans often use monkeypod wood to craft beautiful bowls and furniture.
Cattle in the pasture fed on the fallen pods of the monkeypod tree.
