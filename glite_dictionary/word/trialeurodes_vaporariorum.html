Trialeurodes vaporariorum

1. NOUN

/ ˌtraɪəˌljʊroʊˌdiːz væˌpɔrəriˈɔːrəm / (AmE), / ˌtraɪəˌljʊərəˌdiːz vəˌpɒrəriˈɔːrəm / (BrE)
A species of small white winged insect, commonly known as the greenhouse whitefly, that infests and damages greenhouse plants.
species of greenhouse whitefly

Examples

Trialeurodes vaporariorum can cause serious damage to tomato plants in greenhouses.
Biological control methods are often used to reduce Trialeurodes vaporariorum populations.
The spread of Trialeurodes vaporariorum has increased due to international trade in greenhouse plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whitefly - small, white-winged insect pest

Etymology

From Neo-Latin genus name Trialeurodes and species epithet vaporariorum, meaning 'of greenhouses' in Latin.
