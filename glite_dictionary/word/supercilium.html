supercilium

1. NOUN

/ suː.pɚˈsɪl.i.əm / (AmE), / suː.pəˈsɪl.i.əm / (BrE)
the arch of hair growing above each eye; the eyebrow, especially in anatomical or zoological contexts
the eyebrow; arch of hair above the eye

Examples

The bird's prominent supercilium made it easy to identify among similar species.
In primates, the supercilium often serves as a visual cue in social interactions.
The patient complained of pain along the right supercilium after the accident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eyebrow - the strip of hair growing on the ridge above a person's eye

Etymology

From Latin supercilium (eyebrow, ridge above the eye).
