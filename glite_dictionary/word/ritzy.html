ritzy

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪt.si / (AmE), / ˈrɪt.si / (BrE)
Luxuriously elegant; fashionable and expensive.
luxuriously elegant; fashionable

Examples

They stayed at a ritzy hotel overlooking the ocean.
She wore a ritzy dress that sparkled under the lights.
The restaurant is too ritzy for my usual budget.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luxurious - Expensive and very comfortable or elegant
  • Synonym: opulent - Very rich and luxurious or lavish
  • Antonym: shabby - In poor condition through long or hard use or lack of care; the opposite of luxurious

Etymology

From Ritz, the name of several luxurious hotels established by César Ritz.
