Larotid

1. NOUN

/ ləˈroʊtɪd / (AmE), / ləˈrəʊtɪd / (BrE)
A trade name for amoxicillin, a semisynthetic penicillin antibiotic used to treat a variety of bacterial infections.
trade name for amoxicillin antibiotic
