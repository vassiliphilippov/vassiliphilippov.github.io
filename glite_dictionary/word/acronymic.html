acronymic

1. ADJ

/ ˌækrəˈnɪmɪk / (AmE), / ˌakrəˈnɪmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by the use of acronyms.
relating to acronyms

Examples

The report was written in an acronymic style, making it difficult for outsiders to understand.
Many scientific papers are notoriously acronymic, filled with abbreviations known only to specialists.
Her presentation was less acronymic than most, making it accessible to the general audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acronym - a word formed from the initial letters of a phrase

Etymology

From ‘acronym’ + suffix ‘-ic’.
