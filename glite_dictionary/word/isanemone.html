isanemone

1. NOUN

/ ˌaɪ.səˈnɛmoʊn / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.səˈniːməni / (BrE)
A line drawn on a map connecting points of equal wind speed.
line showing equal wind speed

Examples

The weather map used an isanemone to show areas sharing the same wind speed.
Meteorologists drew several isanemones to analyze changing wind conditions across the region.
Each isanemone on the chart indicated points where the wind velocity was constant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: isobar - line connecting points of equal atmospheric pressure
  • Synonym: isotherm - line connecting points of equal temperature

Etymology

From Greek 'isos' (equal) and 'anemos' (wind).
