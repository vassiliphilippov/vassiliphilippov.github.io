unforethoughtful

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.fɔːrˈθɔt.fəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.fɔːˈθɔːt.fəl / (BrE)
Lacking forethought; not considering or planning for the future.
lacking forethought

Examples

His unforethoughtful remarks led to confusion during the meeting.
It was unforethoughtful of her to refuse without considering the consequences.
They criticized the proposal as unforethoughtful and poorly planned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtless - lacking consideration or attention; unthinking
  • Synonym: inconsiderate - not showing careful thought for the needs or feelings of others
  • Antonym: forethoughtful - showing forethought; planning ahead

Etymology

From un- (not) + forethought (prior consideration) + -ful (forming adjectives).
