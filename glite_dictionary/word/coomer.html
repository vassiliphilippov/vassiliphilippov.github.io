coomer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːmər / (AmE), / ˈkuːmə / (BrE)
(slang) A stereotype of an internet user, especially a man, perceived as addicted to pornography and excessive masturbation, often depicted as lacking self-control or motivation.
internet stereotype of a compulsive porn user
slang

Examples

Many posts in online forums mock the typical coomer for his lack of self-control.
The coomer meme is often criticized for reinforcing negative stereotypes about men and internet culture.
He joked that he was a recovering coomer after spending less time on certain websites.

Synset Examples

Online discussions often feature stereotypes of the coomer.

Etymology

Formed from slang verb 'coom' (a variant of 'cum', slang for ejaculate), often appearing in internet memes.
