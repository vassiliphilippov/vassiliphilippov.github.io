Triangulum

1. PROPN

/ traɪˈæŋɡjələm / (AmE), / traɪˈaŋɡjʊləm / (BrE)
a small northern constellation situated between Andromeda and Aries, near Perseus
a northern constellation between Andromeda and Aries

Examples

The Triangulum galaxy lies within the boundaries of the constellation Triangulum.
Through the telescope, I located Triangulum near the more prominent constellation of Andromeda.
Triangulum is one of the smaller constellations in the northern sky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Andromeda - a constellation located near Triangulum
  • Synonym: Aries - a constellation bordering Triangulum

Etymology

From Latin 'triangulum' meaning 'triangle'.
