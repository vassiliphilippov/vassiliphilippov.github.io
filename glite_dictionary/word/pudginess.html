pudginess

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌdʒi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈpʌdʒi.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being plump and round, especially in a slightly overweight or chubby way
quality of being plump or chubby

Examples

His pudginess made him look younger than his actual age.
The puppy's pudginess was endearing to everyone at the shelter.
She decided to embrace her pudginess and focus on being healthy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chubbiness - the quality of being slightly overweight and round
  • Antonym: slenderness - the quality of being slim or thin

Etymology

From 'pudgy' (of uncertain origin) + '-ness', forming nouns indicating a state or quality.
