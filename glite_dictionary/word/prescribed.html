prescribed

1. ADJ

/ prɪˈskraɪbd / (AmE), / prɪˈskraɪbd / (BrE)
ordered or set down as a rule, guide, or requirement; officially established or required.
ordered or set down as a rule

Examples

You must follow the prescribed format when submitting your application.
The medication should be taken in the prescribed dosage only.
The prescribed safety procedures are strictly enforced at the factory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: required - necessary according to a rule or law
  • Antonym: prohibited - officially forbidden by rule or law

Etymology

Past participle of 'prescribe', from Latin 'praescribere' (to write before, order).
