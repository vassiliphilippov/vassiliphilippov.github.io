Bob Marley

1. PROPN

/ bɑb ˈmɑrli / (AmE), / bɒb ˈmɑːli / (BrE)
(1945–1981) A Jamaican singer, songwriter, and musician who popularized reggae music worldwide and became an international symbol of Rastafarian culture and social change.
Jamaican reggae singer and icon

Examples

Bob Marley is remembered for his inspiring music and message of peace and unity.
My father has a collection of Bob Marley albums from the 1970s.
Many people around the world recognize Bob Marley as a symbol of reggae culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reggae - a popular music genre that originated in Jamaica in the late 1960s
