Tribonema

1. NOUN

/ traɪˈboʊnəmə / (AmE), / trɪˈbəʊnɪmə / (BrE)
A genus of filamentous yellow-green algae in the family Tribonemataceae, found mostly in freshwater environments.
genus of yellow-green algae

Examples

Tribonema is often studied in freshwater algal research because of its distinctive filaments.
Samples collected from the lake showed abundant Tribonema under microscopic examination.
The growth of Tribonema can help indicate the nutrient levels in aquatic ecosystems.

Etymology

From New Latin, coined from Greek 'tribos' (rubbing) + 'nēma' (thread), referring to the thread-like shape.
