projectionist

1. NOUN

/ prəˈdʒɛkʃənɪst / (AmE), / prəˈdʒɛkʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to operate the projector in a cinema or movie theater
person who operates a cinema projector

Examples

The projectionist carefully adjusted the focus before the film began.
When the digital system malfunctioned, the experienced projectionist managed to fix it quickly.
She dreamed of becoming a projectionist after her first visit to an old cinema.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: operator - person who operates equipment or machinery

Etymology

From 'projection' + '-ist', denoting a person involved in projecting films.
