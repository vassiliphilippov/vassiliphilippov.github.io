vibist

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪbɪst / (AmE), / ˈvaɪbɪst / (BrE)
a musician who plays the vibraphone
musician who plays vibraphone

Examples

The jazz band featured a talented vibist who stole the show with his solo.
Many famous vibists have contributed to the evolution of modern jazz music.
She decided to become a vibist after hearing the unique sound of the instrument at a concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vibraphonist - a musician who plays the vibraphone

Etymology

Formed from 'vibes' (short for vibraphone) + '-ist' (person who practices or is concerned with something).
