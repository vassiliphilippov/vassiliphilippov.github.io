wormlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɜrmlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɜːmlaɪk / (BrE)
excessively submissive or lacking in self-assertion; behaving in a servile or self-effacing manner
excessively submissive; servile

Examples

His wormlike obedience made him unpopular with his colleagues.
She adopted a wormlike attitude, never voicing any objection even when treated unfairly.
He felt ashamed of his wormlike submission to the boss's unreasonable demands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: submissive - willing to submit to the authority or will of others
  • Synonym: servile - having or showing an excessive willingness to serve or please others
  • Antonym: assertive - confident and forceful in claiming one's rights or putting forward one's views

Etymology

Formed from 'worm' + 'like', drawing on the metaphor of a worm's lowliness and lack of resistance.
