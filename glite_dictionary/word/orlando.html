Orlando

1. PROPN

/ ɔːrˈlændoʊ / (AmE), / ɔːˈlændəʊ / (BrE)
a city in central Florida, United States, known for its theme parks including Walt Disney World and Universal Studios.
city in central Florida, USA

Examples

We visited Orlando last summer to explore the famous theme parks.
The weather in Orlando is typically warm and humid year-round.
Many international conferences are held in Orlando due to its excellent facilities.

Etymology

Named after Orlando Reeves, a soldier from the Second Seminole War.
