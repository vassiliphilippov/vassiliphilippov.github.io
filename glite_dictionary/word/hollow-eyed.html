hollow-eyed

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑːloʊ aɪd / (AmE), / ˈhɒləʊ aɪd / (BrE)
Having sunken or deep-set eyes, giving an appearance of tiredness, illness, or suffering.
having sunken or deep-set eyes

Examples

After several sleepless nights, she looked hollow-eyed and worn.
The refugees arrived, hollow-eyed and exhausted after their long journey.
His hollow-eyed stare made it clear he had not been well.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunken-eyed - having eyes that appear to sink into the face, suggesting tiredness or illness
