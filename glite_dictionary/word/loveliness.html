loveliness

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌvlinəs / (AmE), / ˈlʌvlinɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being attractive, beautiful, or charming
the quality of being attractive or beautiful

Examples

The loveliness of the garden took my breath away.
She was admired for her grace and loveliness at the event.
The artist tried to capture the loveliness of the sunset in her painting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty - the quality of being pleasing to the senses or mind
  • Antonym: ugliness - the quality of being unpleasant to look at; unattractiveness

Etymology

From 'lovely' + '-ness', modeled after similar noun forms.
