Bechuana

1. NOUN

/ bəˈtʃwɑːnə / (AmE), / bɪˈtʃwɑːnə / (BrE)
a member of a Bantu people living mainly in Botswana and western South Africa
member of a Bantu people of Botswana and South Africa

Examples

The Bechuana have a rich cultural heritage and a long history in southern Africa.
He traveled to Botswana to study the traditions of the Bechuana people.
The community event was attended by several prominent Bechuana leaders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tswana - a member of a Bantu people of Botswana and South Africa

Etymology

From Tswana *Batswana*, the plural of *Motswana* (person of Tswana ethnicity).
