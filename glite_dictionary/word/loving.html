loving

1. ADJ

/ ˈlʌvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlʌvɪŋ / (BrE)
feeling or showing love and affection for someone or something.
feeling or showing affection

Examples

She gave her children a loving hug before they went to school.
He wrote a loving note to his partner, expressing his feelings.
They were known as a loving and supportive couple in their community.

Synset Examples

loving parents
loving glances

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affectionate - showing fondness or tenderness; synonym of 'loving'
  • Antonym: cold - lacking affection or warmth; antonym of 'loving'

Etymology

From the verb 'love' + -ing, used as an adjective since Middle English.
