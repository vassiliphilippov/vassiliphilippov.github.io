renowned

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈnaʊnd / (AmE), / rɪˈnaʊnd / (BrE)
widely known and highly esteemed; famous
famous and highly respected

Examples

The Nobel laureate was a renowned expert in molecular biology.
She attended a lecture by a renowned historian last night.
The restaurant is renowned for its excellent seafood.

Synset Examples

a famous actor
a celebrated musician
a famed scientist
an illustrious judge
a notable historian
a renowned painter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: famous - known about by many people
  • Antonym: obscure - not well known; not easily understood or seen

Etymology

From past participle of Middle English renoun (to make known), from Old French renouner, based on Latin renominare ‘to name again, report, or make famous’.
