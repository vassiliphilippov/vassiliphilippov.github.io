proctalgia

1. NOUN

/ prɑkˈtældʒiə / (AmE), / prɒkˈtældʒɪə / (BrE)
Pain occurring in the rectum.
pain in the rectum
medical

Examples

The patient complained of severe proctalgia after his rectal surgery.
Chronic proctalgia can significantly affect a person's quality of life.
Doctors conducted several tests to determine the cause of her recurring proctalgia.

Etymology

From Greek proktos (anus, rectum) + -algia (pain).
