legible

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛdʒəbəl / (AmE), / ˈlɛdʒɪbəl / (BrE)
(of handwriting or print) clear enough to be read easily; able to be deciphered.
able to be read clearly

Examples

Her notes were surprisingly legible despite being written in a hurry.
Please make sure your answers are legible for the examiner to read.
The faded sign was barely legible from the street.

Synset Examples

legible handwriting

Related Terms

  • Antonym: illegible - (of handwriting or print) impossible or hard to read

Etymology

From Latin 'legibilis', from 'legere' (to read).
