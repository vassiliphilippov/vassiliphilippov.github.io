Meprin

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːprɪn / (AmE), / ˈmiːprɪn / (BrE)
a trade name for a sedative and tranquilizer (meprobamate) used to treat anxiety and muscle tension
trade name for a sedative (meprobamate)

Examples

The doctor prescribed Meprin to help manage her anxiety and muscle spasms.
After taking Meprin, he reported feeling noticeably calmer and more relaxed.
Due to its side effects, Meprin is now prescribed less frequently than in the past.
