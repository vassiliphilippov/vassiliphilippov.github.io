unconfused

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.kənˈfjuzd / (AmE), / ʌn.kənˈfjuːzd / (BrE)
not perplexed or bewildered; free from confusion or uncertainty.
not perplexed or bewildered

Examples

After reading the instructions again, she finally felt unconfused about the process.
The teacher explained the concept so well that all the students looked unconfused.
His unconfused response showed he clearly understood the situation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: confused - perplexed or unable to think clearly

Etymology

Un- (a prefix of negation) + confused (from Latin 'confundere', to mix together).
