dish

1. NOUN

/ dɪʃ / (AmE), / dɪʃ / (BrE)
an amount of food or other substance that a dish can contain, especially as served to someone
the amount a dish can hold; serving portion

Examples

She brought me a dish of fresh fruit for breakfast.
They all shared a dish of spicy noodles at the restaurant.
Would you like a second dish of soup before the main course?

Synset Examples

they served me a dish of rice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: portion - an amount of food served to one person
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13789036-n
Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ dɪʃ / (AmE), / dɪʃ / (BrE)
a particular kind of food that is prepared and served as part of a meal
a type of prepared food or recipe

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms
