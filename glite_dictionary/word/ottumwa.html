Ottumwa

1. PROPN

/ əˈtʌmwə / (AmE), / əˈtʌmwə / (BrE)
a city in southeastern Iowa, United States, situated on the Des Moines River
city in southeastern Iowa, USA

Examples

The National Balloon Classic was held in Ottumwa last summer.
She grew up in Ottumwa before moving to Chicago for college.
Ottumwa is known for its scenic riverfront and historic architecture.

Etymology

From the Native American (possibly Fox or Sauk) word, likely meaning ‘rippling waters’.
