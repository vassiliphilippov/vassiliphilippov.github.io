unfastener

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈfæsnər / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɑːsnə / (BrE)
a person who unfastens, unwraps, or opens something
person who unfastens or opens

Examples

As the designated unfastener, she quickly opened all the packages.
He prides himself on being an expert unfastener of stubborn knots.
Children are sometimes the fastest unfastener of their own shoelaces.

Synset Examples

children are talented undoers of their shoelaces

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fastener - a person or thing that fastens something
  • Synonym: opener - a person or device that opens something
