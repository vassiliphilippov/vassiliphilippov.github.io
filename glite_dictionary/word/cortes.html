Cortes

1. PROPN

/ kɔrˈteɪz / (AmE), / kɔːˈteɪz / (BrE)
(c. 1485–1547) Spanish conquistador who defeated the Aztecs and conquered Mexico.
Spanish conquistador who conquered Mexico

Examples

Cortes led the expedition that caused the fall of the Aztec Empire.
Many historians consider Cortes a controversial figure for his treatment of indigenous peoples.
The conquest of Mexico by Cortes changed the course of world history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hernán Cortés - Full name of Cortes, Spanish conquistador of the Aztecs

Etymology

Spanish surname Cortés.
