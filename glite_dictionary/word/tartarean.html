Tartarean

1. ADJ

/ tɑːrˈtɛəriən / (AmE), / tɑːˈtɛəriən / (BrE)
Of or relating to Hades or Tartarus, the underworld in Greek mythology; infernal or hellish.
relating to Hades or Tartarus; infernal

Examples

The hero ventured into the Tartarean depths, facing unimaginable dangers.
A Tartarean gloom hung over the battlefield after the sun had set.
Her novel depicts a Tartarean world filled with hopelessness and despair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infernal - relating to hell or the underworld

Etymology

From Latin Tartareus, from Greek Tartaros (the deepest region of the underworld).
