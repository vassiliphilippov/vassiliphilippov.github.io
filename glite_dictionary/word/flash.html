flash

1. NOUN

/ flæʃ / (AmE), / flæʃ / (BrE)
a short news announcement providing immediate information about an ongoing event or developing news story
brief breaking news announcement

Examples

The network interrupted regular programming with a flash about the earthquake.
Newsrooms rely on a flash to alert them to any urgent developments.
A flash appeared on the screen announcing the election results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulletin - a brief official statement or news report
