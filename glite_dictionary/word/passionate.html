passionate

1. ADJ

/ ˈpæʃənət / (AmE), / ˈpæʃənət / (BrE)
having or showing strong feelings or intense emotion
having or showing strong feelings

Examples

She gave a passionate speech about human rights.
He is a passionate supporter of the local football team.
Their passionate argument echoed through the hallway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fervent - having or displaying a passionate intensity
  • Antonym: apathetic - showing or feeling no interest or enthusiasm

Etymology

From Old French 'pasioné' (moved by passion), from Medieval Latin 'passionatus'.
