to toughen

1. VERB

/ ˈtʌfən / (AmE), / ˈtʌfən / (BrE)
to make or become tougher or more resilient, either physically or mentally
make or become tougher

Examples

The difficult training helped toughen the athletes for competition.
Exposure to harsh weather can toughen your skin over time.
She believes that challenges toughen a person's character.

Synset Examples

This experience will toughen her

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to harden - make or become hard or harder

Etymology

From the adjective 'tough' + suffix '-en' (to make or become).
