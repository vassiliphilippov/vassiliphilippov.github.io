lexicologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɛksɪˈkɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌlɛksɪˈkɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies the vocabulary of a language, especially one who compiles or writes dictionaries
expert in vocabulary or dictionary-making

Examples

The renowned lexicologist gave a lecture on the history of word usage.
She aspires to become a lexicologist and work on major dictionary projects.
As a lexicologist, he meticulously analyzed the evolution of English vocabulary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lexicographer - a person who compiles dictionaries

Etymology

From French 'lexicologie' + '-ist', ultimately from Greek 'lexis' (word) and 'logos' (study).
