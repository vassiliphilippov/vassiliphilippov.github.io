intrasentential

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.trə.sɛnˈtɛn.ʃəl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trə.senˈten.ʃəl / (BrE)
Relating to elements or structures that occur within a single sentence.
within a sentence (linguistics)

Examples

The researcher analyzed intrasentential code-switching in the participants' speech patterns.
Clear reference is crucial for understanding intrasentential pronouns in complex sentences.
Linguists often study both intrasentential and intersentential relationships between clauses.

Synset Examples

intrasentential co-occurrence

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intersentential - Relating to or occurring between sentences

Etymology

From Latin 'intra-' (within) and 'sententialis' (of a sentence).
