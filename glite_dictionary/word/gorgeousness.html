gorgeousness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɔrdʒəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːdʒəsnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being extremely beautiful or attractive.
the quality of being very beautiful

Examples

The gorgeousness of the sunset left everyone in awe.
Her room radiated a sense of gorgeousness with its elegant decor and soft lighting.
He admired the gorgeousness of the ancient cathedral's stained glass windows.

Synset Examples

Years later, Kazuko's mother gave her the real story: enraptured by her gorgeousness, the young man had asked Kazuko's mother to leave her father.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty - the quality of being pleasing to the senses or mind
  • Antonym: ugliness - the quality of being unpleasant to look at

Etymology

From 'gorgeous' + '-ness,' with 'gorgeous' from Old French 'gorgias' (elegant, fashionable).
