camerawoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæmərəˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈkamərəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who operates a movie camera, especially professionally
female camera operator

Examples

The camerawoman adjusted her lens to capture the perfect shot.
As the only camerawoman on the crew, she inspired young filmmakers.
The documentary was filmed by a talented camerawoman from Spain.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cameraman - a man who operates a movie camera
  • Synonym: camera operator - a person who operates a camera, gender-neutral

Etymology

From 'camera' + 'woman', modeled after 'cameraman'.
