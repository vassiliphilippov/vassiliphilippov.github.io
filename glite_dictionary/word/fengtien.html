Fengtien

1. PROPN

/ fɛŋˈtjɛn / (AmE), / fɛŋˈtjɛn / (BrE)
Former name for Shenyang, a major city in northeastern China.
historical name for Shenyang, China

Examples

In the early 20th century, Fengtien was an important industrial center in Manchuria.
Many historical documents refer to Shenyang as Fengtien.
The Treaty of Portsmouth discussed the status of Fengtien among other territories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Shenyang - the modern name for the city formerly known as Fengtien

Etymology

From Chinese 奉天 (Fèngtiān), meaning 'mandated by heaven'.
