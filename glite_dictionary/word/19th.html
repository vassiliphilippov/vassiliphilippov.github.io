19th

1. ADJ

/ ˈnaɪnˌtiːnθ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪnˌtiːnθ / (BrE)
Coming next after the eighteenth in position; being number nineteen in a sequence.
ordinal number; nineteenth

Examples

He was the 19th person in line for concert tickets.
Their anniversary falls on the 19th of July every year.
This is the 19th time they've hosted the annual charity event.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eighteenth - ordinal number: coming after the seventeenth
  • Degree Higher: twentieth - ordinal number: coming after the nineteenth

Etymology

Formed from 'nineteen' (from Old English 'nigontīene') with the ordinal suffix '-th'.
