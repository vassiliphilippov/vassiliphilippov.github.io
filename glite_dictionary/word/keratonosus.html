keratonosus

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɛrətoʊˈnoʊsəs / (AmE), / ˌkɛərətəˈnəʊsəs / (BrE)
Any disease affecting the cornea.
disease of the cornea
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed him with keratonosus after a thorough eye examination.
Research into treatments for keratonosus has advanced significantly over the past decade.
Patients suffering from keratonosus often experience blurred or impaired vision.

Etymology

From Greek 'kerato-' (horn, cornea) and '-nosus' (disease).
