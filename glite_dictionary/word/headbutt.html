to headbutt

1. VERB

/ ˈhɛdˌbʌt / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌbʌt / (BrE)
to strike someone or something forcefully with the head
strike with the head

Examples

The soccer player was sent off after he headbutted his opponent.
He threatened to headbutt anyone who tried to steal his bag.
She accidentally headbutted her friend while they were playing around.

Synset Examples

The soccer player headbutted his opponent and was sent off the field

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butt - to hit or push against with the head or horns
