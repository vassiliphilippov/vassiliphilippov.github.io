seriocomical

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɪrioʊˈkɑːmɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌsɪəriəʊˈkɒmɪkəl / (BrE)
Combining serious and comic elements, with humor predominating.
mixing serious and comic, humor dominates

Examples

The author's latest novel has a seriocomical tone that keeps the reader both amused and thoughtful.
Her performance was seriocomical, prompting laughter even as it addressed serious social issues.
The movie's seriocomical style makes it unique among romantic comedies.

Related Terms

Etymology

From Latin 'serius' (serious) + 'comicus' (comic).
