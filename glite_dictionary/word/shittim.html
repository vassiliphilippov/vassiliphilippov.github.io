shittim

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪt.ɪm / (AmE), / ˈʃɪt.ɪm / (BrE)
A shrubby, thorny, deciduous tree native to the southeastern United States, having white flowers and small black drupaceous fruit.
a thorny tree of the southeastern US

Examples

The dense wood of the shittim was prized in early American settlements.
We spotted a cluster of shittim in bloom alongside the riverbank.
Botanists are studying the ecological role of shittim in southeastern forests.

Etymology

From Hebrew *shittīm*, plural of *shittah*, a type of acacia tree mentioned in the Bible.
