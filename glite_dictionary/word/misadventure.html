misadventure

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪs.ədˈvɛn.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˌmɪs.ədˈvɛn.tʃə / (BrE)
an unfortunate incident; a mishap or accident, typically one that is minor or not seriously harmful
unfortunate incident; mishap

Examples

His hiking trip ended in misadventure when he twisted his ankle on the trail.
What began as a fun day at the beach turned into a misadventure when their boat drifted away.
They laughed about the misadventure after realizing no serious harm had been done.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accident - an unexpected and undesirable event
  • Degree Higher: disaster - a sudden event causing great damage or suffering
  • Antonym: fortune - chance or luck as an external, arbitrary force

Etymology

From Old French 'mesaventure' (misfortune), from 'mes-' (bad) + 'aventure' (fortune, chance).
