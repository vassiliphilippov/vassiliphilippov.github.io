to prescribe

1. VERB

/ prɪˈskraɪb / (AmE), / prɪˈskraɪb / (BrE)
to recommend a course of action or remedy as being beneficial or advisable
recommend as beneficial

Examples

Many doctors prescribe regular exercise to improve overall health.
Her nutritionist prescribes eating more vegetables and fruits every day.
Parents often prescribe rest and fluids for children with a cold.

Synset Examples

my mother prescribes a good night's sleep as the cure for all ills

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to recommend - to suggest that something is good or suitable

Etymology

From Latin praescribere (to write before, order), from prae- (before) + scribere (to write).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01074513-v
Synset POS: v

2. VERB

/ prɪˈskraɪb / (AmE), / prɪˈskraɪb / (BrE)
(of a doctor) to officially tell someone to take a particular medicine or have a particular treatment, usually in writing.
order medicine or treatment

Examples

The doctor will prescribe antibiotics to treat your infection.
Physicians often prescribe physical therapy after surgery.
If your symptoms worsen, please let me know before I prescribe any new medications.

Synset Examples

the doctor prescribed steroids for my ear trouble

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to proscribe - to officially forbid something, especially by law

Etymology

From Latin praescribere (to write before, order).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01074284-v
Synset POS: v

3. VERB

/ prəˈskraɪb / (AmE), / prɪˈskraɪb / (BrE)
to state officially or authoritatively what should be done in a particular situation; to lay down as a rule or order
to issue official rules or orders

Examples

The school regulations prescribe a strict dress code for all students.
International law prescribes the duties of states in such situations.
The company prescribed new safety measures to prevent accidents.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to proscribe - to officially forbid something
  • Synonym: order - to instruct or command authoritatively

Etymology

From Latin praescribere, meaning 'to write before, order in writing'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00749139-v
Synset POS: v
