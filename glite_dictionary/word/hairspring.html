hairspring

1. NOUN

/ ˈherˌsprɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈheəˌsprɪŋ / (BrE)
a fine spiral spring that controls the motion of the balance wheel in a watch or clock to regulate its timekeeping
spiral spring in a watch regulating movement

Examples

The hairspring is crucial for accurate timekeeping in mechanical watches.
A damaged hairspring can cause a watch to run too fast or too slow.
Watchmakers often need special tools to adjust the hairspring during repairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: balance wheel - the wheel in a timepiece regulated by the hairspring
