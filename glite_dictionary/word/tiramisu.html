tiramisu

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɪrəmiˈsu / (AmE), / ˌtɪə.rə.miːˈsuː / (BrE)
An Italian dessert made of layers of sponge cake or ladyfingers soaked in coffee and liqueur, layered with mascarpone cheese and topped with cocoa or grated chocolate.
Italian dessert with coffee, cheese, and chocolate

Examples

He ordered tiramisu for dessert at the Italian restaurant.
Her homemade tiramisu was the highlight of the dinner party.
Would you like to try some tiramisu with your coffee?

Etymology

Italian, from tirami su meaning ‘pick me up’ or ‘cheer me up’.
