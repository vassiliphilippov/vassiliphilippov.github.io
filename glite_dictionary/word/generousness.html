generousness

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛn.ə.rəs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛn.ər.əs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being willing to give money, time, or help freely to others
quality of being giving or generous

Examples

Her generousness towards others has earned her great respect in the community.
He showed remarkable generousness by donating both time and money to the cause.
Many people were touched by the generousness of his actions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: generosity - willingness to give money, time, or help freely

Etymology

From generous + -ness, denoting the state or quality.
