Abukir

1. PROPN

/ ˌɑː.buˈkɪr / (AmE), / ˌɑː.bjuːˈkɪə / (BrE)
a bay on the Mediterranean Sea in northern Egypt, known for its historical significance
bay in northern Egypt

Examples

The Battle of Abukir was a decisive moment in British naval history.
Many tourists visit Abukir to explore its beaches and historical sites.
Archaeologists have discovered ancient ruins near Abukir Bay.

Etymology

From Arabic أبو قير (Abū Qīr), the name of the bay and adjoining town.
