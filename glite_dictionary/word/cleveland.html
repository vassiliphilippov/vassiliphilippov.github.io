Cleveland

1. PROPN

/ ˈkliːvlənd / (AmE), / ˈkliːvlənd / (BrE)
A city in northeastern Ohio, United States, situated on Lake Erie; the largest city in Ohio and an important port on the Great Lakes.
city in northeastern Ohio, USA
american

Examples

Cleveland is known for its vibrant arts scene and world-class museums.
The Cleveland Cavaliers represent Cleveland in the National Basketball Association.
Many industries and shipping companies are based in Cleveland because of its location on Lake Erie.

Etymology

Named after General Moses Cleaveland, the city's founder.
