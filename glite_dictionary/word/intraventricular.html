intraventricular

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.trə.vɛnˈtrɪk.jə.lɚ / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trə.vɛnˈtrɪk.jʊ.lə / (BrE)
Situated or occurring within the ventricles of the brain.
within the brain's ventricles
medical

Examples

The doctor monitored the intraventricular pressure to assess the patient's risk of brain injury.
An intraventricular hemorrhage can lead to serious complications in newborns.
The MRI revealed an intraventricular mass that required further investigation.

Synset Examples

intraventricular pressure

Etymology

From Latin 'intra-' (within) + 'ventriculus' (small belly, ventricle).
