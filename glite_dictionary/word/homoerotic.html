homoerotic

1. ADJ

/ ˌhoʊmoʊɪˈrɑtɪk / (AmE), / ˌhəʊməʊɪˈrɒtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or suggesting a sexual attraction between members of the same sex, often in art, literature, or imagery.
relating to same-sex eroticism

Examples

The film was noted for its homoerotic undertones, especially in the scenes featuring the two main characters.
Some art historians interpret ancient Greek sculptures as having homoerotic elements.
His latest novel explores homoerotic themes within the context of a close male friendship.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heteroerotic - relating to sexual attraction between members of the opposite sex

Etymology

From Greek 'homos' (same) + 'erōtikos' (erotic).
