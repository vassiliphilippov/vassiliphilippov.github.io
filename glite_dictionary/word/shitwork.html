shitwork

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtwɝk / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtwɜːk / (BrE)
Unpleasant, menial, or tedious work considered trivial and unrewarding.
trivial or tedious menial work
informal vulgar

Examples

As a junior employee, she was often assigned the shitwork that no one else wanted to do.
He refused to be stuck with shitwork while his colleagues handled more important projects.
Interns sometimes feel frustrated by the amount of shitwork they have to handle.

Synset Examples

the hospital hired him to do scut work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scutwork - Menial or routine tasks that are tedious or unpleasant
