taleteller

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪlˌtɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˈteɪlˌtɛlə / (BrE)
a person who reveals secrets or gossips indiscreetly
a person who gossips or reveals secrets

Examples

Everyone knew Sarah was a taleteller who could not keep a secret for long.
No one wants to be friends with a taleteller in the office.
They quickly realized there was a taleteller among their group, spreading rumors everywhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gossip - a person who habitually reveals personal or sensational facts about others

Etymology

From 'tale' (story) + 'teller' (one who tells).
