excruciatingly

1. ADV

/ ɪkˈskruːʃieɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪkˈskruːʃieɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In an extremely painful or intense manner.
in a very painful or intense way

Examples

The wait for the test results was excruciatingly long.
She described the memory as excruciatingly painful.
The movie was excruciatingly slow and difficult to sit through.

Synset Examples

the progress was agonizingly slow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agonizingly - in a manner that causes great pain or anguish
  • Synonym: painfully - in a way that causes pain

Etymology

From 'excruciating' + -ly; 'excruciating' from Latin 'excruciare' (to torment, to torture).
