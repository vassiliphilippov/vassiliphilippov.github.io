balsamy

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɔlsəmi / (AmE), / ˈbɑːlsəmi / (BrE)
having the qualities of or containing balsam; aromatic or fragrant like balsam.
having the qualities of balsam

Examples

The air in the pine forest was filled with a balsamy scent that lingered after the rain.
She loved the balsamy fragrance of the ointment, which reminded her of her grandmother's home remedies.
A balsamy breeze drifted through the window, bringing with it a subtle, soothing aroma.

Synset Examples

a balsamic fragrance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: balsamic - having the qualities or aroma of balsam

Etymology

From 'balsam' + '-y', forming an adjective meaning 'like or containing balsam'.
