trussed

1. ADJ

/ trʌst / (AmE), / trʌst / (BrE)
Bound or secured tightly with ropes, string, or similar; especially with arms and legs tied together or with limbs bound close to the body.
bound or tied up tightly

Examples

The burglar was discovered trussed behind the counter, unable to move.
She prepared the roast chicken by making sure it was tightly trussed before cooking.
The hostages were found trussed with their hands and feet bound together.

Synset Examples

the guard was found trussed up with his arms and legs securely tied
a trussed chicken

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tied up - having hands or body bound with rope or similar
