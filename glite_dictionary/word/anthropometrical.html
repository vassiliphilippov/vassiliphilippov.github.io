anthropometrical

1. ADJ

/ ˌænθrəpəˈmɛtrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌænθrəpəˈmɛtrɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to the measurement of the human body and its proportions.
relating to measurement of the human body

Examples

The researchers used anthropometrical data to compare the growth patterns of children from different regions.
Advancements in anthropometrical techniques have improved the accuracy of ergonomic assessments.
He published several papers on anthropometrical differences among athletes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anthropometric - relating to the measurement of the human body
  • Synonym: anthropometry - the scientific study of the measurements and proportions of the human body

Etymology

From 'anthropometry' + '-ical'; ultimately from Greek 'anthropos' (man) and 'metron' (measure).
