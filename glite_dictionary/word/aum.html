Aum

1. PROPN

/ aʊm / (AmE), / aʊm / (BrE)
A Japanese religious group, known as Aum Shinrikyo, designated as a terrorist organization after carrying out attacks such as the 1995 Tokyo subway sarin gas attack.
Japanese religious terrorist group
american

Examples

The leader of Aum was charged for masterminding the sarin gas attack in Tokyo.
Authorities raided several Aum facilities during the investigation.
International communities condemned the actions of Aum after the 1995 subway attack.

Synset Examples

in 1995 Aum members released deadly sarin gas on a Tokyo subway train

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aum Shinrikyo - Full name of the Japanese cult and terrorist organization

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Om' (a sacred sound in Hinduism and Buddhism); adopted as the name of the group.
