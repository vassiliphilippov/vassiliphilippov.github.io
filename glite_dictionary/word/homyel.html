Homyel

1. PROPN

/ hoʊˈmjɛl / (AmE), / hɒˈmjɛl / (BrE)
An industrial city in southeastern Belarus, located southeast of Minsk.
industrial city in Belarus

Examples

The annual trade fair in Homyel attracts business representatives from around the region.
After leaving Minsk, the train makes its next major stop at Homyel.
