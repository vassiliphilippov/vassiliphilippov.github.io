1850s

1. NOUN

/ eɪtˈiːn ˈfɪftiːz / (AmE), / eɪˈtiːn ˈfɪftiz / (BrE)
the decade from 1850 to 1859
the decade 1850–1859

Examples

Many important scientific discoveries occurred during the 1850s.
The fashion of the 1850s reflected both elegance and practicality.
Industrialization accelerated rapidly in Europe throughout the 1850s.
