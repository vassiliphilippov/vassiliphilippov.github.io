Veracruz

1. PROPN

/ ˌvɛrəˈkruːz / (AmE), / ˌvɛrəˈkruːz / (BrE)
A major port city on the Gulf of Mexico in the state of Veracruz, eastern Mexico.
port city in eastern Mexico

Examples

Veracruz is known for its vibrant port and historical significance in Mexican history.
The annual Carnival of Veracruz attracts thousands of tourists from around the world.
After docking in Veracruz, the goods were transported to inland cities across Mexico.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Veracruz', meaning 'True Cross'.
