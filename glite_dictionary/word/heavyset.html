heavyset

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛviˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈhɛviˌsɛt / (BrE)
Having a solid, broad, and heavy body build; stocky or thickset in appearance.
having a solid or broad body build

Examples

She was described as a heavyset woman with broad shoulders and a warm smile.
The heavyset man moved quickly despite his size, surprising everyone at the race.
He wore a suit that looked a bit tight on his heavyset frame.

Synset Examples

a wrestler of compact build
he was tall and heavyset
stocky legs
a thickset young man

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stocky - having a broad, sturdy build
  • Antonym: slender - thin in an attractive or graceful way
  • Synonym: thickset - broad and solidly built

Etymology

Formed from 'heavy' + 'set', dating from the late 19th century.
