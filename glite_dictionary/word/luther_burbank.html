Luther Burbank

1. PROPN

/ ˈluːθər ˈbɜrbæŋk / (AmE), / ˈluːθə ˈbɜːbæŋk / (BrE)
(1849–1926) United States horticulturist who developed many new varieties of fruits, vegetables, and flowers.
American horticulturist and plant breeder

Examples

Luther Burbank introduced more than 800 new plant varieties during his career.
The city of Santa Rosa honors Luther Burbank with a garden named after him.
Many gardeners are inspired by the innovations of Luther Burbank.
