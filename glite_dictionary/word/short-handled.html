short-handled

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɔrt ˌhændəld / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt ˌhændld / (BrE)
having a handle that is shorter than usual or desired
having a short handle

Examples

The gardener preferred a short-handled spade for working in tight spaces.
She bought a short-handled brush to clean the inside of jars.
Some tools, like the short-handled hoe, are ideal for close-up work in the garden.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: long-handled - having a long handle
