numerology

1. NOUN

/ ˌnuːməˈrɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌnjuːməˈrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the belief in or study of the mystical significance and influence of numbers on human life and events
study of mystical significance of numbers

Examples

Many people interested in numerology believe that certain numbers can predict events in their lives.
The ancient practice of numerology assigns unique meanings to each number from one to nine.
Despite its popularity, numerology is considered a pseudoscience by most scientists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astrology - the study of the movements and relative positions of celestial bodies interpreted as having an influence on human affairs
  • Antonym: science - systematic enterprise that builds and organizes knowledge in the form of testable explanations and predictions; opposed to pseudoscience

Etymology

From Latin numerus (number) + Greek -logia (study).
