Moravian

1. ADJ

/ məˈreɪviən / (AmE), / məˈreɪviən / (BrE)
of or relating to Moravia or its people, language, or culture.
of or relating to Moravia or its people

Examples

The Moravian tradition values music, art, and religious tolerance.
Many villages in the Moravian region are renowned for their colorful folklore festivals.
She studied Moravian architecture as part of her research on Central European styles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Czech - of or relating to the Czech Republic or its people
  • Synonym: Bohemian - of or relating to Bohemia or its people

Etymology

From Moravia, a historical region in the east of the Czech Republic, with the suffix '-an'.
