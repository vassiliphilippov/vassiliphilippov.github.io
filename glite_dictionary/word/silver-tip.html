silver-tip

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ tɪp / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə tɪp / (BrE)
a powerful brownish-yellow bear, also called the grizzly bear, found in the uplands of western North America
large North American bear (grizzly)

Examples

The silver-tip is renowned for its immense strength and surprising speed in the wild.
Early settlers often feared encounters with a silver-tip in the Montana wilderness.
Researchers tracked several silver-tips as part of a conservation project in Yellowstone Park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grizzly bear - a large subspecies of brown bear found in North America
