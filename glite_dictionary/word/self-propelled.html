self-propelled

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlf prəˈpɛld / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf prəˈpɛld / (BrE)
(of a vehicle, device, or entity) able to move itself forward by means of its own power or momentum, without external force.
able to move by its own power

Examples

The self-propelled lawn mower made yard work much easier.
They developed a self-propelled robot for industrial cleaning purposes.
During the demonstration, the self-propelled artillery impressed the military officials.

Synset Examples

a self-propelled egotist
the arms program is now self-propelled

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motorized - powered by a motor
  • Antonym: manual - operated by hand or non-automatic means

Etymology

self + propelled, from Latin ‘pro’ (forward) + ‘pellere’ (to drive, push).
