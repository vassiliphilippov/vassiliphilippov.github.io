made-up

1. ADJ

/ ˌmeɪdˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌmeɪdˈʌp / (BrE)
(of a road or surface) covered or finished with a hard material such as asphalt, concrete, or gravel.
surfaced with asphalt or similar material
british

Examples

The village was finally connected by a made-up road, replacing the old dirt track.
It's much easier to cycle on a made-up path than a muddy one.
Their cottage sits at the end of a newly made-up lane.
