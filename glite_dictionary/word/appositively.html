appositively

1. ADV

/ əˈpɑzətɪvli / (AmE), / əˈpɒzətɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or as an appositive; used in apposition.
in an appositive manner

Examples

The noun phrase was placed appositively for clarity.
This term is often used appositively in educational materials.
You can describe a name appositively to give more information.

Synset Examples

this adjective is used appositively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appositive - a grammatical construction where one noun phrase follows another to explain or identify it

Etymology

From 'appositive' + '-ly'. 'Appositive' from Latin 'appositus', past participle of 'appōnere' (to place near).
