haptically

1. ADV

/ ˈhæptɪkli / (AmE), / ˈhæptɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or involving the sense of touch
by means of touch
medical

Examples

The virtual reality system provides feedback haptically as users interact with objects.
Some animals navigate their environment haptically, relying mainly on their sense of touch.
Information can be transmitted haptically through vibrations felt on the skin.

Synset Examples

he perceives shapes tactually

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tactually - by means of the sense of touch

Etymology

From Greek haptikos (able to touch or perceive) via 'haptic' + -ly.
