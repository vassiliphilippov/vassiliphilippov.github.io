communique

1. NOUN

/ kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪ / (AmE), / kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪ / (BrE)
an official statement or report, especially one made to the media or public by a government or organization
official statement or report

Examples

The foreign ministry issued a communique outlining the details of the agreement.
After the summit, a joint communique was released by the two countries.
The press eagerly awaited the official communique to learn more about the ongoing negotiations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: statement - an official account or declaration

Etymology

From French communiqué (communicated), past participle of communiquer (to communicate), from Latin communicare.
