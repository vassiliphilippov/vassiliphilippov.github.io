phalacrosis

1. NOUN

/ fəˈlækɹəsɪs / (AmE), / fəˈlakɹəsɪs / (BrE)
Medical term for baldness; the condition of having no hair on the top of the head.
medical term for baldness
medical

Examples

Some forms of phalacrosis can be treated with medication or surgery.
The patient was seeking advice about the best ways to manage phalacrosis.
Early onset phalacrosis is often influenced by genetic factors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baldness - the state of having no hair on the scalp

Etymology

From Greek phalakros (bald) + -osis (condition).
