baggy

1. ADJ

/ ˈbæɡi / (AmE), / ˈbaɡi / (BrE)
(of clothing) fitting loosely and not closely to the body.
loose-fitting (of clothing)

Examples

He wore a pair of baggy jeans and an oversized shirt.
The baggy sweater kept her warm and comfortable all day.
These baggy shorts are perfect for lounging around the house.

Synset Examples

baggy trousers
a loose-fitting blouse is comfortable in hot weather

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loose - not fitting tightly or closely
  • Antonym: tight - fitting closely to the body
