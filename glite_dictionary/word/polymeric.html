polymeric

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɑː.lɪˈmɛr.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɒl.ɪˈmɛr.ɪk / (BrE)
Relating to, consisting of, or characteristic of a polymer.
relating to or made of polymers

Examples

This plastic has polymeric properties that make it both durable and lightweight.
The scientist studied the polymeric structure of the new material.
They synthesized a polymeric film for use in medical devices.

Synset Examples

a polymeric compound

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polymer - a substance consisting of large molecules made up of many repeating units

Etymology

From polymer + -ic, from Greek polus (many) + meros (part).
