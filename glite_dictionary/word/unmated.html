unmated

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmeɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of an animal or person) not having a sexual mate; not paired for breeding.
not paired for breeding

Examples

The scientist observed the behavior of the unmated birds during the breeding season.
Most unmated animals are more likely to roam in search of partners.
She raised several unmated rabbits in separate cages for the experiment.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mated - having a sexual mate; paired for breeding

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'mated' (from 'mate').

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not paired or matched with another; without a mate or counterpart.
not paired with another

Examples

After the laundry was done, several unmated socks remained in the basket.
The chessboard had one unmated knight left on the field.
He searched the drawer for an unmated glove to replace his lost one.

Synset Examples

unpaired socks
an odd glove

Related Terms

  • Antonym: matched - paired with or corresponding to another
  • Synonym: paired - joined with or having a mate

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'mated' (paired).
