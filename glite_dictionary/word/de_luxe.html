de luxe

1. ADJ

/ də ˈlʌks / (AmE), / də ˈlʌks / (BrE)
of superior quality and elegance; luxurious or sumptuous
luxurious; of superior quality

Examples

They spent a week in a de luxe hotel overlooking the ocean.
The new model comes with a de luxe interior featuring leather seats and wood trim.
For her birthday, she received a box of de luxe chocolates from Paris.

Synset Examples

a deluxe car
luxe accommodations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luxurious - extremely comfortable or elegant, especially in a way that involves great expense
  • Synonym: sumptuous - splendid and expensive-looking

Etymology

From French de luxe, meaning 'of luxury'.
