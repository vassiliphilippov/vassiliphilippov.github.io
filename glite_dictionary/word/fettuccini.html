fettuccini

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɛtʊˈtʃini / (AmE), / ˌfɛtʊˈtʃiːni / (BrE)
Pasta in the form of long, flat ribbons that are wider than linguine.
ribbon-shaped pasta, wider than linguine

Examples

He ordered fettuccini Alfredo for dinner at the Italian restaurant.
You can serve fettuccini with a variety of creamy or tomato-based sauces.
The chef sprinkled Parmesan cheese over freshly cooked fettuccini.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: linguine - pasta in long, flat, narrow strips

Etymology

From Italian 'fettuccine', plural of 'fettuccina', meaning 'little ribbon', from 'fetta' (slice).
