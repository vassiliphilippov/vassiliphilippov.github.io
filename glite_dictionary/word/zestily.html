zestily

1. ADV

/ ˈzɛstɪli / (AmE), / ˈzɛstɪli / (BrE)
In a lively and enthusiastic manner; with zest or great energy.
in a lively, enthusiastic way

Examples

She attacked her new project zestily, impressing everyone with her enthusiasm.
The children sang zestily during the school play, filling the room with energy.
He zestily embraced the opportunity to travel abroad for work.

Synset Examples

she scrubbed the floors of the new apartment zestfully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enthusiastically - in a way that shows intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval

Etymology

From 'zest' + '-ily', the adverbial suffix.
