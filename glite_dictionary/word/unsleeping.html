unsleeping

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsliːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsliːpɪŋ / (BrE)
not sleeping; always awake or vigilant.
not sleeping; always awake

Examples

The unsleeping city buzzed with activity even in the early morning hours.
Her unsleeping mind raced with thoughts and ideas throughout the night.
He watched over the camp as an unsleeping guardian, alert to every sound.

Synset Examples

the unsleeping city
so excited she was wide-awake all night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awake - not asleep; fully conscious
  • Antonym: asleep - in a state of sleep

Etymology

From un- (not) + sleep + -ing.
