Hejaz

1. PROPN

/ hiˈdʒɑːz / (AmE), / hiˈdʒɑːz / (BrE)
a coastal region in western Saudi Arabia along the Red Sea, including Mecca and Medina; historically an independent kingdom before joining with Nejd to form Saudi Arabia
region in western Saudi Arabia including Mecca and Medina

Examples

The city of Mecca is located within the historic region of Hejaz.
Many important events in Islamic history took place in the Hejaz.
The Hejaz was an independent kingdom before becoming part of modern Saudi Arabia.

Etymology

From Arabic الحجاز‎ (al-Ḥijāz), meaning 'the barrier'.
