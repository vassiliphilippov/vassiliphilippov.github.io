likening

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪkənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪkənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of comparing one thing with another for similarities
act of making a comparison

Examples

Her likening of the two writers revealed surprising similarities in their styles.
The likening of the human brain to a computer is common in popular science.
In his speech, he made a likening between leadership and gardening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comparison - the act of examining similarities or differences

Etymology

From Middle English likening, from liken (to compare) + -ing.
