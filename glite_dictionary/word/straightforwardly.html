straightforwardly

1. ADV

/ ˌstreɪtˈfɔrwərdli / (AmE), / ˌstreɪtˈfɔːwədli / (BrE)
in a direct, honest, and uncomplicated manner; without evasion or ambiguity
in a direct or honest way

Examples

She explained the problem straightforwardly, making it easy for everyone to understand.
The manager spoke straightforwardly about the challenges facing the company.
Please answer my questions straightforwardly so we can resolve this quickly.

Synset Examples

he stood foursquare for religious liberty and toleration
dealt straightforwardly with all issues

Related Terms

  • Synonym: directly - without deviation; in a straight way
  • Antonym: deviously - in a roundabout or underhanded manner

Etymology

Formed from 'straightforward' + suffix '-ly'. 'Straightforward' from 'straight' + 'forward'.
