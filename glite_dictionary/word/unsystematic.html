unsystematic

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌsɪstəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌsɪstəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
not done or arranged according to a system, plan, or method; lacking order or organization.
not systematized or organized

Examples

His notes were so unsystematic that it was difficult to study from them.
The investigation was unsystematic, leading to many overlooked details.
They searched the area in an unsystematic way, missing several important clues.

Synset Examples

unsystematic and fragmentary records
he works in an unsystematic manner

Related Terms

  • Antonym: systematic - done or acting according to a fixed plan or system

Etymology

Formed from the prefix un- (not) and systematic.
