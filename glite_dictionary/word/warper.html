warper

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔrpər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːpə / (BrE)
a machine used in the textile industry to arrange yarns in parallel for weaving.
textile machine for arranging yarns

Examples

The yarn was transferred from the spools to the warper for preparation before weaving.
A malfunction in the warper caused delays in the production schedule.
Modern textile factories often use automated warpers to improve efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warping mill - a machine used to prepare the warp for weaving
