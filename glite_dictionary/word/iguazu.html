Iguazu

1. PROPN

/ ˌiːɡwəˈzuː / (AmE), / ˌiːɡwɑːˈzuː / (BrE)
A large and spectacular waterfall located on the border between Argentina and Brazil, known for its natural beauty and volume.
large waterfall on the Argentina-Brazil border

Examples

The breathtaking view of Iguazu attracts thousands of tourists every year.
We took a boat tour to get closer to the powerful Iguazu Falls.
The mist from Iguazu can be seen from miles away.

Etymology

From Guarani *y* (water) + *ûasú* (big).
