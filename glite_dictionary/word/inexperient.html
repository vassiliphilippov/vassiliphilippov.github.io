inexperient

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.ɪkˈspɪriənt / (AmE), / ˌɪn.ɪkˈspɪərɪənt / (BrE)
lacking practical experience or training; inexperienced.
lacking practical experience

Examples

As an inexperient manager, she made several avoidable mistakes in her first week.
The committee was wary of hiring someone so inexperient for such a critical role.
Because he was inexperient in public speaking, he felt nervous addressing the large audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inexperienced - lacking experience or skill
  • Antonym: experienced - having knowledge or skill in a particular field

Etymology

From Latin inexperiens (untried, inexperienced), from in- (not) + experiri (to try).
