Uup

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuːˌjuːˈpiː / (AmE), / ˈjuːˌjuːˈpiː / (BrE)
the former chemical symbol for moscovium, a radioactive transuranic element with atomic number 115
former symbol for moscovium (element 115)

Examples

Before being officially named moscovium, element 115 was known as Uup.
The discovery of Uup was confirmed by several international laboratories.
In scientific papers, Uup was listed as an element awaiting an official name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moscovium - a radioactive transuranic element with atomic number 115

Etymology

From systematic element naming: un (1), un (1), pent (5); symbolizing atomic number 115.
