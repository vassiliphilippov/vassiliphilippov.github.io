beatable

1. ADJ

/ ˈbiː.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈbiː.tə.bəl / (BrE)
Capable of being defeated or overcome.
able to be defeated

Examples

The team is strong, but they are still beatable if we play well.
Many thought the champion was unbeatable, but the new contender proved him beatable.
The computer program was surprisingly beatable once you learned its strategy.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: invincible - impossible to defeat or overcome
  • Antonym: unbeatable - impossible or extremely difficult to defeat

Etymology

From 'beat' + '-able', patterned on similar adjective formations.
