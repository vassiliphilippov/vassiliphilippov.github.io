to evince

1. VERB

/ ɪˈvɪns / (AmE), / ɪˈvɪns / (BrE)
to show or demonstrate clearly; to make evident
show or demonstrate clearly

Examples

Her actions evince a deep sense of responsibility.
He evinced little interest in our conversation.
The painting evinces the artist's passion for nature.

Synset Examples

She showed her disappointment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to demonstrate - to show evidence or make clear by reasoning or evidence
  • Synonym: show - to make visible or apparent

Etymology

From Latin 'evincere' (to prove, demonstrate).
