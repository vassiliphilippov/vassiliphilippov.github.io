mosstone

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɑsˌtoʊn / (AmE), / ˈmɒsˌtəʊn / (BrE)
of a moderate, slightly dull yellow-green color, resembling the shade of moss.
moderate dull yellow-green color

Examples

The artist chose a mosstone fabric for the sofa, giving the room an earthy feel.
Her dress was a beautiful shade of mosstone that complemented the spring landscape.
They painted the kitchen in mosstone to create a calm and natural atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: olive - a dark yellowish-green color
  • Degree Higher: chartreuse - a color between yellow and green

Etymology

From 'moss' (the plant) and 'stone' (possibly from 'tone', meaning shade or hue), suggesting color of moss.
