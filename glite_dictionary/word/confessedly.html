confessedly

1. ADV

/ kənˈfɛsɪdli / (AmE), / kənˈfɛsɪdli / (BrE)
As acknowledged or admitted; by one's own confession.
as admitted or acknowledged

Examples

Confessedly, I am not an expert on this subject, but I will do my best.
He was confessedly surprised by the outcome of the meeting.
The policy is confessedly difficult to implement in rural areas.

Synset Examples

true, she is the smartest in her class

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admittedly - as acknowledged or recognized; by admission
  • Degree Higher: undeniably - impossible to deny; unquestionably

Etymology

From 'confessed' + '-ly', patterned after the verb 'confess'.
