ugli

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌɡli / (AmE), / ˈʌɡli / (BrE)
A large, sweet, juicy citrus fruit, a hybrid between a tangerine and a grapefruit, with thick, wrinkled skin.
hybrid citrus fruit (tangerine and grapefruit)

Examples

I bought an ugli at the market and enjoyed its sweet, juicy flesh.
The ugli has a thick, wrinkled skin that peels easily.
Have you ever tried an ugli? It's a unique citrus fruit with a delicious flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tangelo - a citrus fruit hybrid of tangerine and pomelo or grapefruit

Etymology

From 'ugly' (due to its wrinkled appearance), trademarked as 'UGLI fruit' in Jamaica.
