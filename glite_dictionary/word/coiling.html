coiling

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɔɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɔɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
spiraling or curving in the shape of a coil
spiraling; shaped like a coil

Examples

A coiling snake rested on the warm rock, blending into its surroundings.
The artist painted a coiling pattern along the edges of the vase.
The garden hose lay in a coiling heap beside the shed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiral - winding in a continuous curve around a center point or axis
