Eucarya acuminata

1. NOUN

/ juːˈkeɪ.ri.ə əˌkjuː.mɪˈneɪ.tə / (AmE), / juːˈkeərɪə əˌkjuːmɪˈneɪtə / (BrE)
An Australian tree with edible flesh and a nutlike edible seed.
Australian tree with edible fruit and seed

Examples

The fruit of the Eucarya acuminata is popular among indigenous communities for its unique flavor.
Botanists are studying the Eucarya acuminata for its potential nutritional benefits.
The Eucarya acuminata thrives in arid regions of Australia and is valued for its edible seeds.
