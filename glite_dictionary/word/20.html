20

1. NUM

/ ˈtwɛnti, ˈtwɛni / (AmE), / ˈtwɛnti, ˈtwɛni / (BrE)
the number equivalent to the sum of nineteen and one; ten more than ten; two tens
the number twenty (20)

Examples

She turned 20 years old this month.
There are 20 students in the class.
The shirt costs 20 dollars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twenty - the number equivalent to 20

Etymology

From Old English 'twēntig', from 'twēgen' (two) + '-tig' (group of ten).
