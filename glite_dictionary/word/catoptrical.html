catoptrical

1. ADJ

/ kəˈtɑːptrɪkəl / (AmE), / kəˈtɒptrɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or produced by mirrors or the science of reflection; concerning catoptrics.
relating to mirrors or reflection

Examples

The design featured a catoptrical arrangement to create the illusion of infinite space.
Certain optical instruments rely on catoptrical principles to reflect and focus light.
His research focused on the catoptrical properties of ancient Roman mirrors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catoptric - relating to mirrors or reflected light

Etymology

From Greek katoptrikos, from katoptron (mirror), from katopein (to observe).
