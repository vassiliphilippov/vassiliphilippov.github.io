beefcake

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːfˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈbiːfkeɪk / (BrE)
A photograph or image featuring a muscular man, often in minimal clothing, intended to be sexually appealing or to show off physique.
photograph of a muscular, scantily clad man

Examples

The magazine added a special beefcake section featuring athletes in swimwear.
He became famous after his beefcake photo went viral online.
The actor often poses for beefcake shots to appeal to his fans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheesecake - photograph of an attractive woman in little clothing

Etymology

From 'beef' (muscle, flesh) + 'cake' (slang for an attractive person); modeled after 'cheesecake' for female pin-ups.
