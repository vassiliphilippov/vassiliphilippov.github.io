tailflower

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪlˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈteɪlˌflaʊə / (BrE)
Any of various tropical American plants, especially of the genus Anthurium, cultivated for their ornamental foliage and flowers.
tropical ornamental plant

Examples

The vibrant red tailflower brightened the entrance of the botanical garden.
Many interior designers use tailflowers in arrangements for their dramatic appearance.
She learned to care for her tailflower after discovering its unique watering needs.
