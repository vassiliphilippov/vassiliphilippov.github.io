hilly

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɪli / (AmE), / ˈhɪli / (BrE)
(of land or an area) having a lot of hills; full of hills.
having many hills

Examples

The countryside outside the city is hilly and covered with trees.
Cycling in such hilly terrain can be quite challenging.
We chose a hilly route for our morning hike to enjoy the views.

Synset Examples

hilly terrain

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: mountainous - (of a region) having a lot of mountains
  • Antonym: flat - (of land) having no hills or mountains; level

Etymology

From hill + -y, meaning 'having the characteristics of a hill'.
