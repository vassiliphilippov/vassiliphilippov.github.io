Umbundu

1. NOUN

/ umˈbʊndu / (AmE), / ʌmˈbʊndu / (BrE)
the Bantu language spoken primarily in central Angola, especially by the Ovimbundu people
a Bantu language of Angola

Examples

Many people in central Angola speak Umbundu as their first language.
She is learning Umbundu to communicate with local communities during her research.
Radio stations in Angola often broadcast news in Umbundu.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ovimbundu - the ethnic group primarily speaking Umbundu

Etymology

From Umbundu, the name used by speakers of the language themselves.
