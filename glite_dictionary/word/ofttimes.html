ofttimes

1. ADV

/ ˈɑːftˌtaɪmz / (AmE), / ˈɒftˌtaɪmz / (BrE)
On many occasions; frequently.
frequently; many times
archaic

Examples

In the past, poets would ofttimes write about love and nature.
He ofttimes visited his grandmother when he was a child.
Challenges ofttimes arise when you least expect them.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: often - on many occasions; frequently
  • Antonym: rarely - not often; seldom

Etymology

From Middle English 'oftimes', from 'oft' (often) + 'times'.
