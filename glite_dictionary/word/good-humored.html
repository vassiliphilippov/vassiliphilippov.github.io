good-humored

1. ADJ

/ ɡʊd ˈhjuːmɚd / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈhjuːməd / (BrE)
cheerful and pleasant; having or showing a friendly and agreeable temper
pleasant and cheerful

Examples

Despite the long delay, the crowd remained good-humored throughout the evening.
She gave a good-humored response to the teasing from her colleagues.
His good-humored attitude made him popular at work.

Synset Examples

an amiable villain with a cocky sidelong grin

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amiable - friendly and pleasant in manner
  • Antonym: bad-tempered - easily annoyed or angered, irritable
