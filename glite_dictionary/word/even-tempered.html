even-tempered

1. ADJ

/ ˈivən ˌtɛmpɚd / (AmE), / ˌiːvənˈtɛmpəd / (BrE)
calm and not easily annoyed or angered; having a steady or mild temperament
calm and not easily irritated

Examples

Sara is known for being even-tempered, even when things get stressful at work.
It's easier to negotiate with someone who is even-tempered and patient.
Despite the criticism, he remained even-tempered throughout the meeting.

Synset Examples

an equable temper
not everyone shared his placid temperament
remained placid despite the repeated delays

Related Terms

  • Synonym: placid - calm and not easily excited or upset
  • Antonym: irritable - easily annoyed or angered

Etymology

Formed from 'even' (level, calm) + 'tempered' (having a particular temperament or disposition).
