Mecholyl

1. NOUN

/ məˈkoʊlɪl / (AmE), / məˈkəʊlɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked parasympathomimetic drug that stimulates secretions and increases smooth muscle activity.
trademarked parasympathomimetic drug
medical

Examples

The doctor decided to administer Mecholyl to assess the patient's autonomic function.
Due to its effects on smooth muscles, Mecholyl is sometimes used in diagnostic procedures.
Researchers studied the pharmacological response to Mecholyl in various tissues.
