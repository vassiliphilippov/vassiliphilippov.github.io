to etherify

1. VERB

/ ˈiːθɚˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈiːθərɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to convert (a substance, especially an alcohol) into an ether by a chemical reaction
convert into an ether (chemistry)
medical

Examples

The chemist managed to etherify the compound by following a modified procedure.
It is important to etherify the alcohol completely for the reaction to succeed.
Researchers must take safety precautions when they etherify substances in the lab.

Synset Examples

etherify an alcohol

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to etherize - to convert into ether; to anaesthetize with ether

Etymology

From ether + -ify, meaning 'to make into an ether'.
