neurobiological

1. ADJ

/ ˌnʊroʊˌbaɪəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the biological study of the nervous system
pertaining to the biology of the nervous system
medical

Examples

Recent research explores the neurobiological basis of memory formation in the brain.
She specializes in neurobiological mechanisms underlying stress response.
The study revealed important neurobiological changes associated with aging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neurological - relating to the nervous system (especially structure and diseases)

Etymology

From 'neuro-', relating to nerves, and 'biological', from Greek 'bios' (life) and 'logos' (study).
