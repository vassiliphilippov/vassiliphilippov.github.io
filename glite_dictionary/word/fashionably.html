fashionably

1. ADV

/ ˈfæʃ.ən.əb.li / (AmE), / ˈfæʃ.ən.əb.li / (BrE)
In a manner that follows the latest styles or trends; stylishly.
in a stylish or trendy manner

Examples

She always dresses fashionably no matter the occasion.
The restaurant is fashionably decorated with modern art and velvet chairs.
He arrived fashionably late to the party, making a dramatic entrance.

Synset Examples

his voice had no trace of the drawl that you would expect to find in a fashionably dressed young man

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stylishly - in a stylish manner

Etymology

From 'fashionable' + '-ly', where 'fashionable' comes from 'fashion' (from Old French 'façon', from Latin 'factio').
