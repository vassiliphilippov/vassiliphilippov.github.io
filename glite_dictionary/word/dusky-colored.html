dusky-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʌski ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈdʌski ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
Having a dark or somewhat dark color; shaded or shadowy in tone.
having a dark color

Examples

The bird's dusky-colored feathers helped it blend into the dense forest.
She admired the dusky-colored roses blooming in the garden at dusk.
The artist selected a dusky-colored palette for his evocative nightscape painting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dark - having very little light or a deep color
