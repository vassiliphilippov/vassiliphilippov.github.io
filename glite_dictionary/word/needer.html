needer

1. NOUN

/ ˈniːdər / (AmE), / ˈniːdə / (BrE)
a person who wants or requires something
person who wants or requires something

Examples

She became a needer of constant reassurance after the accident.
The charity focuses on helping every needer in the community.
An owner of many things and needer of none is a rare person indeed.

Synset Examples

an owner of many things and needer of none

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wanter - a person who wants something
  • Antonym: giver - a person who gives something

Etymology

Formed from 'need' + agent noun suffix '-er'.
