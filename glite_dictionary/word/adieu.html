adieu

1. NOUN

/ əˈdu / (AmE), / əˈdjuː / (BrE)
An expression used to bid farewell, especially when leaving for a long time or forever.
farewell remark

Examples

She waved one last time and whispered a soft adieu before boarding the train.
They sang a heartfelt adieu at the end of the ceremony.
With a heavy heart, he bid his hometown adieu as he left for a new life abroad.

Synset Examples

they said their good-byes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - a farewell remark; parting phrase

Etymology

From French 'adieu', meaning 'to God', used as a farewell.
