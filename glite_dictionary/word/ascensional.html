ascensional

1. ADJ

/ əˈsɛnʃənl / (AmE), / əˈsɛnʃənəl / (BrE)
Tending to rise or move upward.
tending to rise or move upward

Examples

The ascensional movement of the hot air balloon was steady and smooth.
Scientists studied the ascensional currents in the atmosphere to predict weather changes.
The design takes advantage of the ascensional force of heated air to improve ventilation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ascending - moving or going upward; increasing
  • Antonym: descending - moving or going downward

Etymology

From Latin 'ascension-' (a rising, ascent) + '-al' (forming adjectives).
