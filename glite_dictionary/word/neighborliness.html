neighborliness

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪbərli.nəs / (AmE), / ˈneɪbəlɪnɪs / (BrE)
A friendly and helpful attitude or behavior towards people who live near you.
friendly helpfulness to neighbors

Examples

The neighborliness in our small town makes everyone feel welcome.
Acts of neighborliness can strengthen the sense of community in a neighborhood.
She was admired for her neighborliness, always ready to lend a hand to those in need.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly
  • Antonym: hostility - unfriendly or antagonistic behavior

Etymology

Formed from 'neighborly' + '-ness'.
