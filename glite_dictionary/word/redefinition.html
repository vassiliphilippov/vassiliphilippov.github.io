redefinition

1. NOUN

/ ˌriːˌdɛfəˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌdɛfɪˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of defining something again or differently, often giving it a new meaning or interpretation
the act of defining again or differently

Examples

The recent social debate led to a redefinition of traditional family roles.
With every technological breakthrough, there is a need for the redefinition of certain job descriptions.
The company underwent a major redefinition of its goals and values last year.

Synset Examples

words like ‘conservative’ require periodic redefinition
she provided a redefinition of his duties

Related Terms

  • Synonym: definition - a statement of the exact meaning of a word or term

Etymology

From re- (again) + definition, originally from Latin 'definitio'.
