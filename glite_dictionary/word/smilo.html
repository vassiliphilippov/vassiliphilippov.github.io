smilo

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmaɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsmaɪləʊ / (BrE)
a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region, known as mountain rice, and introduced into North America
a Mediterranean perennial grass, mountain rice

Examples

The introduction of smilo to North America has benefited grazing livestock.
Botanists are studying the growth patterns of smilo in Mediterranean climates.
Wild smilo can often be seen covering hillsides in Spain.
