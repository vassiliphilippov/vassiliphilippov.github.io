sales

1. NOUN

/ seɪlz / (AmE), / seɪlz / (BrE)
the total amount of money received from selling goods or services during a particular period
total revenue from goods or services sold

Examples

Last quarter, our sales exceeded expectations by 15 percent.
The company reported higher sales compared to the previous year.
Holiday promotions usually boost sales in retail stores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revenue - total income of a business from its activities
  • Antonym: expenses - costs incurred in the process of earning revenue

Etymology

Plural of 'sale', from Old English sala (a sale) and Old Norse sala.
