Jirrbal

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪrbəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪə.bæl / (BrE)
a language of the Australian Aboriginal people, spoken in northeast Queensland
Australian Aboriginal language of Queensland

Examples

The Jirrbal language has been studied for its unique grammatical structures.
Fewer people speak Jirrbal today than in the past, as younger generations learn English.
Researchers are working with elders to preserve the Jirrbal language and its rich oral traditions.
