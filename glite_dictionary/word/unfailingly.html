unfailingly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈfeɪlɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈfeɪlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that does not fail; always or unfalteringly, without exception
always; without fail

Examples

She unfailingly supports her friends no matter what happens.
The trains in Japan are unfailingly punctual.
He unfailingly greeted everyone with a smile each morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: always - at all times; on every occasion
  • Antonym: sometimes - on some occasions; not always

Etymology

From 'unfailing' + '-ly', where 'unfailing' means 'not failing'.
