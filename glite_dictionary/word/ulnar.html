ulnar

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌlnɑr / (AmE), / ˈʌlnɑː / (BrE)
Relating to or situated near the ulna (the bone on the inner side of the forearm).
relating to the ulna bone
medical

Examples

The doctor examined the ulnar side of my forearm for tenderness.
She experienced numbness along the ulnar nerve after the injury.
The fracture was close to the ulnar head near the wrist.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: radial - relating to or near the radius bone of the forearm

Etymology

From modern Latin 'ulnaris', from Latin 'ulna' (forearm, elbow).
