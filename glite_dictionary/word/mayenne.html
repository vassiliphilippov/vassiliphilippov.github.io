Mayenne

1. PROPN

/ maɪˈɛn / (AmE), / maɪˈɛn / (BrE)
a department in the Pays de la Loire region of northwestern France, known for its rural landscapes and historic towns
department in northwestern France

Examples

The picturesque countryside of Mayenne attracts visitors seeking a quiet escape from city life.
They spent their summer holidays exploring historic castles throughout Mayenne.
Many regional cheeses are produced in the lush pastures of Mayenne.

Etymology

Named after the Mayenne River; from Latin 'Mediana'.
