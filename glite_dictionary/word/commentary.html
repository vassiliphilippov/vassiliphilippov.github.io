commentary

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːm.ən.ˌter.i / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.ən.tər.i / (BrE)
a set of written explanations or critical notes added to a book, text, or other material to explain, interpret, or discuss its content
written explanation added to a text

Examples

The edition includes a detailed commentary after each poem.
Her commentary on the novel provides important historical context for readers.
The professor’s commentary addresses several controversial interpretations of the manuscript.

Synset Examples

he wrote an extended comment on the proposal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annotation - a note added to a text to explain or comment on it
  • Synonym: gloss - an explanation or translation of a word or phrase in a text

Etymology

From Latin commentarius (notebook, commentary), from commentari (to comment).
