4WD

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɔr ˌˈdʌbəlˈjuː ˈdiː / (AmE), / ˌfɔː ˌdʌbəlˈjuː ˈdiː / (BrE)
a motor vehicle with a system that provides power to all four wheels, typically for improved traction on rough or slippery surfaces
vehicle with four-wheel drive system

Examples

We took my 4WD up the mountain trail without any problems.
He prefers to drive a 4WD for off-road adventures.
The rental company offered both standard cars and 4WD vehicles for the trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: four-wheel drive - a system that powers all four wheels of a vehicle

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'four-wheel drive'.

2. NOUN

/ fɔr ˌwiː diː / (AmE), / fɔː ˌwiː diː / (BrE)
a transmission system that powers all four wheels of a vehicle simultaneously, used especially for off-road driving
four-wheel-drive vehicle or system

Examples

Many SUVs come with 4WD for better traction on rough terrain.
He switched his truck into 4WD to navigate the snowy road safely.
Some drivers prefer 4WD vehicles for outdoor adventures and off-roading.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: four-wheel drive - a transmission that provides power to all four wheels

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'four-wheel drive'.
