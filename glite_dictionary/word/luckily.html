luckily

1. ADV

/ ˈlʌk.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈlʌk.ɪ.li / (BrE)
By good fortune; as a result of luck or chance, especially in a favorable way.
by good fortune; fortunately

Examples

Luckily, we arrived at the station just as the train was pulling in.
Luckily, no one was injured in the accident.
They lost their keys, but luckily someone found them and handed them in.

Synset Examples

fortunately the weather was good

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fortunately - by good fortune; in a fortunate manner
  • Antonym: unluckily - by bad luck; unfortunately

Etymology

From 'lucky' + '-ly', where 'lucky' derives from luck (noun) + -y.
