Sao Tome e Principe

1. PROPN

/ saʊ təˈmeɪ i ˈprɪnsəˌpeɪ / (AmE), / saʊ təˈmeɪ i ˈprɪnsɪpeɪ / (BrE)
an island nation in the Gulf of Guinea, off the west coast of Central Africa, which gained independence from Portugal in 1975
island nation in West Africa

Examples

Many tourists visit Sao Tome e Principe for its unique biodiversity and beautiful beaches.
Oil exploration is a growing industry in Sao Tome e Principe.
After gaining independence in 1975, Sao Tome e Principe established its own government.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'São Tomé e Príncipe', meaning 'Saint Thomas and Prince'.
