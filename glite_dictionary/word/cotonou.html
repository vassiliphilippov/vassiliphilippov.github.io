Cotonou

1. PROPN

/ ˌkoʊtəˈnu / (AmE), / ˌkɒtəˈnuː / (BrE)
the chief port and largest city of Benin, located on the Bight of Benin in West Africa
chief port and city of Benin

Examples

The city of Cotonou is an important economic hub in West Africa.
She traveled to Cotonou on business last summer.
Many goods are imported through the port of Cotonou.
