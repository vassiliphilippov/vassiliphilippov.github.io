untypically

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈtɪpɪkli / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɪpɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is not typical or usual; uncharacteristically.
in a manner that is not typical

Examples

He was untypically nervous before the meeting, surprising his colleagues.
The weather has been untypically warm for this time of year.
She answered untypically quickly, without her usual hesitation.

Synset Examples

she was atypically quiet

Related Terms

  • Antonym: typically - in a manner that is typical

Etymology

Formed by adding the adverbial suffix -ly to 'untypical' (from un- + typical).
