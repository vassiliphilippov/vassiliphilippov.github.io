bromic

1. ADJ

/ ˈbroʊmɪk / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing bromine, especially where bromine has a valence of five (pentavalent bromine).
relating to pentavalent bromine
medical

Examples

Bromates are examples of bromic compounds commonly studied in chemistry.
The chemist explained the difference between bromic and bromous acids.
When handling bromic substances, always use proper safety equipment.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: bromide - a compound containing bromine in the -1 oxidation state

Etymology

From 'bromine' + '-ic', following naming conventions for chemical adjectives.
