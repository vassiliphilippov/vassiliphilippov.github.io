silvery-bodied

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪlvəri ˈbɑdid / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəri ˈbɒdid / (BrE)
having a body that is silver in color or appearance.
with a silver-colored body

Examples

The newly discovered fish species has a silvery-bodied appearance, making it easily visible in deep water.
Collectors prized the silvery-bodied coins for their brilliant sheen and near-mint condition.
The artist painted a silvery-bodied horse galloping across the moonlit field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silvery - having the appearance or qualities of silver
