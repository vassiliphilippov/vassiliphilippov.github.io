Gondi

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊn.di / (AmE), / ˈɡɒn.di / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken by the Gond people in south-central India
Dravidian language of the Gond people

Examples

The Gondi language is an important part of the Gond people's cultural heritage.
Scholars are compiling dictionaries to preserve Gondi for future generations.
He spoke Gondi fluently, having grown up in a remote village in Madhya Pradesh.

Etymology

From the name of the Gond people of India.
