satisfactoriness

1. NOUN

/ ˌsætɪsˈfæktəriˌnɪs / (AmE), / ˌsætɪsˈfæktərɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being adequate to meet a demand, requirement, or expectation
quality of being satisfactory

Examples

The satisfactoriness of the solution was confirmed by all parties involved.
She questioned the satisfactoriness of the results before approving the report.
Regular assessments ensure the satisfactoriness of our customer service standards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: satisfactoriness - quality of being satisfactory
  • Antonym: unsatisfactoriness - the quality of not being satisfactory

Etymology

From satisfactory + -ness, modeled on English formation indicating 'state or quality of.'
