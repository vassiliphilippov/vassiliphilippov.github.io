sexiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛksi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsɛksi.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being sexually attractive or arousing sexual desire
sexual attractiveness; arousing desire

Examples

Her sexiness turned all heads when she entered the room in her red dress.
The movie relied on a combination of wit and sexiness to attract its audience.
He admired her confidence and natural sexiness more than any other quality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attractiveness - the quality of being attractive
  • Antonym: unattractiveness - the quality of being unattractive
  • Synonym: sensuality - the enjoyment, expression, or pursuit of physical, especially sexual, pleasure

Etymology

From 'sexy' + '-ness'. 'Sexy' from English 'sex' + '-y'.
