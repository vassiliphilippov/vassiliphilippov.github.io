metalworks

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛt̬lˌwɝks / (AmE), / ˈmɛtlˌwɜːks / (BrE)
a factory where metal objects are manufactured or metal castings are produced
factory for producing metal products

Examples

The old metalworks by the river was converted into an art center.
New safety regulations were implemented at the metalworks after the recent accident.
He found work as a machinist at the local metalworks after leaving school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foundry - a workshop or factory for casting metal
