monggo

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɒŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
the seed of the mung bean plant, commonly used as food in Asian cuisine
seed of the mung bean plant

Examples

In Filipino cuisine, monggo is commonly used to make hearty stews and soups.
She bought a bag of dried monggo from the Asian grocery store.
Green monggo is often sprouted and used in fresh salads.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mung bean - the plant producing edible green seeds

Etymology

From Filipino 'monggo', borrowed from Spanish 'mongo', ultimately from Tamil 'mūṅgai' (mung bean).

2. NOUN

/ ˈmɔŋˌgoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɒŋgəʊ / (BrE)
the seed of the mung bean plant used as food, especially in Southeast Asian cooking
seed of the mung bean plant (food)

Examples

For dinner, she cooked a stew with monggo and vegetables.
I bought some monggo from the Asian market to make a traditional Filipino dish.
Steamed rice and monggo soup is a comforting meal in many households.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mung bean - the small, green legume whose seeds and sprouts are widely used in Asian cuisine

Etymology

From Filipino 'monggo', ultimately from Spanish 'mongó', referring to mung bean.
