spiraling

1. ADJ

/ ˈspaɪrəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspaɪərəlɪŋ / (BrE)
Winding or moving continuously around a central point in a curve, like a coil; forming or shaped as a spiral.
shaped like a spiral or coil

Examples

The spiraling staircase led them to the top of the tower.
A spiraling shell was resting on the beach, its curves catching the sunlight.
Smoke rose in a spiraling column above the campfire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiral - curve that winds around a central point

Etymology

From the verb 'spiral', itself from Latin 'spira' (coil) via French and Italian.
