sci-fier

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈfaɪ.ər / (AmE), / saɪˈfaɪ.ə / (BrE)
an enthusiast or fan of science fiction
science fiction fan
informal

Examples

Being a sci-fier, she dreams of space travel.
As a true sci-fier, he attends all comic conventions.
The sci-fier in me can't resist new tech gadgets.
