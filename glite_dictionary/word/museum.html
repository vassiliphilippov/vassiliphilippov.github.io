museum

1. NOUN

/ mjuˈziəm / (AmE), / mjuːˈzɪəm / (BrE)
A building or institution where objects of scientific, historical, or artistic importance are collected and exhibited to the public.
institution for displaying valuable objects

Examples

The city recently opened a new museum featuring interactive science exhibits for children.
We spent the afternoon exploring the art collections at the national museum.
The fossils on display at the natural history museum fascinated the students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gallery - a room or building for displaying works of art

Etymology

From Latin 'museum' (place of study), from Greek 'mouseion' (seat of the Muses).
