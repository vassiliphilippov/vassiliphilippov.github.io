volumetrically

1. ADV

/ ˌvoʊ.ljəˈmɛ.trɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌvɒl.jʊˈmɛ.trɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to measurement or consideration of volume.
with respect to volume

Examples

The chemical solution was measured volumetrically to ensure accuracy in the experiment.
Soil porosity can be determined volumetrically rather than by weight.
The concentration was calculated volumetrically using precise laboratory equipment.

Synset Examples

analyzed volumetrically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volumetric - relating to the measurement of volume

Etymology

From 'volumetric' + '-ally', from Latin 'volumen' (roll, volume).
