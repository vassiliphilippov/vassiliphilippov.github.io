pemphigous

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛm.fɪ.ɡəs / (AmE), / ˈpɛm.fɪ.ɡəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of pemphigus, a group of rare blistering skin diseases
relating to pemphigus (skin disease)
medical

Examples

The dermatologist diagnosed the patient with a rare pemphigous disorder affecting her skin.
Biopsies revealed pemphigous lesions consistent with autoimmune blistering disease.
Treatment of pemphigous conditions often requires long-term immunosuppressive therapy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pemphigus - a group of rare autoimmune blistering diseases affecting the skin and mucous membranes

Etymology

From Greek 'pemphigodes' (pertaining to blisters), via 'pemphigus' (blister).
