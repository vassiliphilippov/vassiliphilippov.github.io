Ruholla Khomeini

1. PROPN

/ ruːˈhoʊlɑː koʊˈmeɪni / (AmE), / ruːˈhɒlə kəʊˈmeɪni / (BrE)
(1900–1989) Iranian religious and political leader, founder of the Islamic Republic of Iran and its Supreme Leader after the 1979 revolution.
Iranian religious and political leader (1900–1989)

Examples

Ruholla Khomeini led the Iranian Revolution that overthrew the Shah in 1979.
The new constitution created by Ruholla Khomeini granted him supreme authority in Iran.
Many Iranians refer to Ruholla Khomeini as the founder of the Islamic Republic.
