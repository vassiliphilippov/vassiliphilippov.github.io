auburn

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔbɚn / (AmE), / ˈɔːbən / (BrE)
(especially of hair) having a moderate reddish-brown color.
moderate reddish-brown color (esp. hair)

Examples

Her auburn hair glowed in the afternoon sunlight, adding warmth to her features.
He admired her auburn locks, which stood out in the crowd.
The actress dyed her hair auburn for her latest film role.

Synset Examples

auburn hair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chestnut - of a deep reddish-brown color (esp. hair)

Etymology

From Old French 'auborne', from Medieval Latin 'alburnus' (whitish, from 'albus' meaning white), but the sense shifted to 'reddish-brown' in English.
