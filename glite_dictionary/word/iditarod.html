Iditarod

1. PROPN

/ ˌaɪ.dɪˈtɑːr.ɑːd / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.dɪˈtɑː.rɒd / (BrE)
An annual long-distance dogsled race held in Alaska, covering the Iditarod Trail.
annual Alaskan dogsled race

Examples

The Iditarod attracts mushers from around the world to compete in Alaska's harsh winter conditions.
She trained for years before finally entering the grueling Iditarod dogsled race.
Spectators cheered as the teams crossed the finish line at the annual Iditarod.

Etymology

From the name of the Iditarod River and Trail in Alaska.
