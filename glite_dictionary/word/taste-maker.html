taste-maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪstˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈteɪstˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
A person who influences or helps to popularize new fashions, styles, or trends.
person who sets or popularizes trends

Examples

As a leading taste-maker, she is often the first to wear new designers' styles.
The magazine credits him as the taste-maker behind several major fashion trends.
Social media has made it easier than ever for influencers to become a taste-maker in their communities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trendsetter - a person who leads the way in fashion or ideas

Etymology

Formed from 'taste' (discernment in matters of style) + 'maker'.
