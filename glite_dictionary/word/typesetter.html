typesetter

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪpˌsɛtər / (AmE), / ˈtaɪpˌsɛtə / (BrE)
a person who arranges text and images for printing by setting type
person who sets type for printing

Examples

The typesetter carefully aligned each letter to ensure perfect spacing on the page.
Before digital publishing, every newspaper hired at least one skilled typesetter.
As a typesetter, she took pride in her attention to detail and craftsmanship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compositor - person who arranges text for printing

Etymology

From type + setter, referring to someone who sets type for printing.
