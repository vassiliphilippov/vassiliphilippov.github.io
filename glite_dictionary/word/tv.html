TV

1. NOUN

/ tiː ˈviː / (AmE), / tiː ˈviː / (BrE)
a system or medium for broadcasting visual images and sound that people can watch on a screen
broadcasting visual images and sound

Examples

He spent the evening watching TV with his family.
There was a breaking news update on TV last night.
Many people get their information from TV rather than newspapers.

Synset Examples

she is a star of screen and video
Television is a medium because it is neither rare nor well done

Related Terms

  • Synonym: television - the system or process of transmitting visual images electronically

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'television'.
