Tunis

1. PROPN

/ ˈtuːnɪs / (AmE), / ˈtjuːnɪs / (BrE)
the capital city and main port of Tunisia, located in North Africa on the Mediterranean coast
capital city of Tunisia

Examples

Many tourists visit Tunis each year to explore its historic medina.
The government of Tunisia is headquartered in Tunis.
He flew from Paris to Tunis for an international conference.

Etymology

From Arabic تونس‎ (Tūnis).

2. PROPN

/ ˈtuːnɪs / (AmE), / ˈtuːnɪs / (BrE)
the capital and principal port of Tunisia, located in the north of the country near the Mediterranean Sea
capital city of Tunisia

Examples

Many tourists visit Tunis to explore its historic medina and lively markets.
The business conference will be hosted in Tunis next spring.
As the economic and political center, Tunis plays a significant role in Tunisia's development.

Etymology

From Arabic تونس‎ (Tūnis), meaning 'Tunisia'.
