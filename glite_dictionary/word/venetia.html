Venetia

1. PROPN

/ vəˈniːʃə / (AmE), / vɪˈniːtɪə / (BrE)
a region in northeastern Italy, located on the Adriatic Sea, historically known as Venetia.
region in northeastern Italy

Examples

The city of Venice lies in the heart of Venetia and has a rich cultural history.
Much of Venetia is known for its stunning landscapes and unique architecture.
During the 19th century, Venetia was contested by Austria and Italy.

Etymology

From Latin Venetia, referring to the land of the ancient Veneti people.
