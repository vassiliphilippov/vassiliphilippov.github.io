uveous

1. ADJ

/ ˈjuːviəs / (AmE), / ˈjuːvɪəs / (BrE)
Relating to the uvea, the pigmented layer of the eye between the retina and the sclera.
relating to the uvea of the eye
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with uveous inflammation after a thorough eye examination.
Certain autoimmune conditions can lead to uveous involvement, affecting vision.
Specialists are trained to recognize diseases that impact uveous tissues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uveal - pertaining to the uvea of the eye

Etymology

From Latin 'uvea' (grape), referring to the grape-like appearance of the layer.
