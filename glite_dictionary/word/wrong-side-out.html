wrong-side-out

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɔŋ saɪd ˈaʊt / (AmE), / ˌrɒŋ saɪd ˈaʊt / (BrE)
(of clothing or fabric) with the inside surface facing outward; not turned so the outside is showing.
having the inside surface outward

Examples

He accidentally put his shirt on wrong-side-out this morning.
The seam shows because the dress is wrong-side-out.
You can tell at a glance if a sweater is wrong-side-out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inside-out - with the inner surface turned outward
