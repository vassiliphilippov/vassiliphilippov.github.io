somniloquy

1. NOUN

/ sɑmˈnɪləkwi / (AmE), / sɒmˈnɪləkwɪ / (BrE)
The act or habit of talking during sleep.
talking during sleep

Examples

His somniloquy kept his roommate awake throughout the night.
Episodes of somniloquy are often harmless but can be surprising to family members.
She recorded her somniloquy to learn if she revealed any secrets while asleep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleep-talking - the act of talking during one's sleep

Etymology

From Latin 'somnus' (sleep) + 'loqui' (to speak).
