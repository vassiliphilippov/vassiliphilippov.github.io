transmigrante

1. NOUN

/ trænz.miˈɡrɑn.teɪ / (AmE), / tranz.mɪˈɡran.teɪ / (BrE)
a Latin American who buys used goods in the United States and transports them to Latin America for resale
Lat. American who resells US goods in Latin America

Examples

Many transmigrantes drive trucks filled with merchandise across the Texas-Mexico border each year.
Local authorities set up special lanes for transmigrantes transporting goods to Central America.
The boom in used car exports created new opportunities for transmigrante businesses.

Etymology

From Spanish 'transmigrante', meaning 'migrant' or 'one who moves across' (from Latin 'trans-' across + 'migrare' to move).
