Cefobid

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːfoʊbɪd / (AmE), / ˈsiːfəʊbɪd / (BrE)
A trademarked cephalosporin antibiotic administered by injection, used to treat severe bacterial infections.
injectable cephalosporin antibiotic (trade name)
medical

Examples

The patient was prescribed Cefobid to combat a severe respiratory infection.
Hospital staff administered Cefobid intravenously for optimal treatment effectiveness.
Due to its broad spectrum of activity, Cefobid is often used in critical care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cefoperazone - the generic form of the antibiotic sold as Cefobid

Etymology

Trade name for the drug cefoperazone.
