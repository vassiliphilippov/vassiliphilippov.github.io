jewelled

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒuːəld / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːəld / (BrE)
decorated or encrusted with jewels, beads, or sequins
decorated with jewels or sequins

Examples

She wore a stunning jewelled necklace for the ceremony.
The costume designer chose a jewelled mask to add glamour to the scene.
The queen's crown was heavily jewelled and sparkled under the lights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bejeweled - adorned with jewels or precious stones

Etymology

From 'jewel' (Middle English, from Old French 'jouel'), with the -ed participle ending.
