Decapterus macarellus

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈkæptərəs məˈkærələs / (AmE), / dɪˈkæptərəs məˈkarɪləs / (BrE)
A small silvery fish of the jack mackerel family, found in the Atlantic from Nova Scotia to Brazil.
species of small silvery fish

Examples

The Decapterus macarellus is an important catch for local fisheries along the Brazilian coast.
Researchers studied the migratory patterns of Decapterus macarellus in the western Atlantic.
You can find Decapterus macarellus in shallow waters during certain times of the year.

Etymology

From New Latin: genus Decapterus + species macarellus (diminutive of 'mackerel').
