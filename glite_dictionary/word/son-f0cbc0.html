son

1. NOUN

/ sʌn / (AmE), / sʌn / (BrE)
a male child in relation to his parents
male child of one's parents

Examples

Their son graduated from college last year.
She introduced her son to everyone at the party.
As a son, he always tried to make his parents proud.

Synset Examples

their son became a famous judge
his boy is taller than he is

Related Terms

  • Antonym: daughter - a female child in relation to her parents

Etymology

Old English sunu; related to German Sohn and Latin filius (son).
