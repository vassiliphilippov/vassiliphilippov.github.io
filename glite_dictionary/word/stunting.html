stunting

1. NOUN

/ ˈstʌn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstʌn.tɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of performing stunts, especially while flying an aircraft
performing aerial stunts

Examples

The pilot impressed the crowd with his stunting at the airshow.
Regulations strictly limit stunting during commercial flights for safety reasons.
She trained for years to master advanced stunting maneuvers in her biplane.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aerobatics - the practice of performing acrobatic maneuvers in an aircraft

Etymology

From the verb 'stunt' (to perform daring feats), with the suffix '-ing' indicating the action or process.
