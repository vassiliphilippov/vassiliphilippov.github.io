unrepentantly

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈpɛnt.ənt.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈpɛnt.ənt.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows no regret or remorse for one's actions or beliefs.
without regret or remorse

Examples

She unrepentantly defended her controversial opinions during the interview.
He continued to act unrepentantly despite repeated warnings from his supervisor.
The company spokesperson unrepentantly justified the unpopular decision at the press conference.

Synset Examples

he repeated his position unrepentantly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unapologetically - in a manner that shows no regret or apology

Etymology

Formed from 'unrepentant' (not feeling remorse) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
