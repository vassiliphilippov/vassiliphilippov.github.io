interesting

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪntrəstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪntərɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
Attracting your attention because it is exciting, unusual, or thought-provoking.
arousing curiosity or attention

Examples

That was an interesting book full of surprising facts and theories.
Her story about traveling to the Arctic was really interesting to everyone at the table.
The museum had many interesting exhibits that caught the children's attention.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: boring - not interesting; dull, tedious
  • Degree Higher: fascinating - extremely interesting or charming

Etymology

From interest + -ing; 'interest' from Old French and Latin 'interesse' (to concern, make a difference).
