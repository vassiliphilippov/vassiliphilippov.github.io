Ali Baba

1. PROPN

/ ˌɑːli ˈbɑːbə / (AmE), / ˌæli ˈbɑːbə / (BrE)
a character in Arabian Nights who discovers a thieves' treasure cave opened by the phrase 'open sesame'
fictional woodcutter in Arabian Nights

Examples

As a child, I loved hearing the story of Ali Baba and the forty thieves.
The phrase 'open sesame' became famous because of Ali Baba's adventure.
That old movie features Ali Baba sneaking into the thieves' hidden cave.

Etymology

From Arabic, name of the main character in 'Ali Baba and the Forty Thieves'.
