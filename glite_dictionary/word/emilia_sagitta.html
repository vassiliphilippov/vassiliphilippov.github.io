Emilia sagitta

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmɪliə səˈdʒɪtə / (AmE), / ɪˈmɪliə səˈɡɪtə / (BrE)
A tropical Asiatic annual plant, cultivated for its small, tassel-shaped heads of scarlet flowers.
tropical annual with scarlet tassel-shaped flowers

Examples

The gardener planted Emilia sagitta along the border to add vibrant color to the summer garden.
With its brilliant scarlet flowers, Emilia sagitta quickly became a favorite among horticulturists.
Botanists studied Emilia sagitta to better understand its adaptability to tropical climates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Emilia - genus of flowering plants in the daisy family

Etymology

From New Latin, combining the genus name Emilia and the Latin species epithet sagitta meaning 'arrow'.
