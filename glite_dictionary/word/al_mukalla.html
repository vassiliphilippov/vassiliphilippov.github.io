Al Mukalla

1. PROPN

/ æl mʊˈkɑːlə / (AmE), / æl mʊˈkælə / (BrE)
A port city in southern Yemen on the Gulf of Aden, east of Aden.
port city in southern Yemen

Examples

The ship docked at Al Mukalla before continuing its journey along the coast.
Humanitarian aid was delivered to Al Mukalla after the cyclone struck Yemen.
Trade has long flourished in Al Mukalla due to its strategic location on the Gulf of Aden.

Etymology

From Arabic ٱلْمُكَلَّا (al-mukallā), meaning 'the sheltered harbor'.
