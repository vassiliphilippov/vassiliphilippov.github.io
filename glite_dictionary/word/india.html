India

1. PROPN

/ ˈɪndiə / (AmE), / ˈɪndiə / (BrE)
A country in southern Asia, located on the Indian subcontinent, renowned for its diverse culture, large population, and history; became an independent republic in 1947.
country in southern Asia

Examples

Many tourists travel to India each year to experience its vibrant culture and historic sites.
The economy of India has grown rapidly in recent decades.
Gandhi played a crucial role in leading India to independence from British rule.

Etymology

From Latin 'India,' from Ancient Greek 'India', ultimately from Sanskrit 'Sindhu' (the Indus River).
