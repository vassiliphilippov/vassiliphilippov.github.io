Kepler

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɛplɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɛplə / (BrE)
(1571–1630) A German astronomer who formulated the laws of planetary motion, significantly advancing the field of astronomy.
German astronomer; laws of planetary motion

Examples

Many astronomers regard Kepler as a pioneer in understanding the motion of planets.
The Kepler laws revolutionized how we view our solar system.
I read an article about Kepler and his contributions to astronomy.
