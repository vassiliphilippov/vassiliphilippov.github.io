Epicurean

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛpɪˈkjʊriən / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈkjʊəriən / (BrE)
relating to the Greek philosopher Epicurus or his philosophy, which advocates the pursuit of pleasure and tranquility as the highest good.
of Epicurus or his philosophy

Examples

His views on happiness are rooted in Epicurean philosophy.
They attended a lecture on Epicurean ethics at the university.
Her approach to life was influenced by Epicurean teachings.

Synset Examples

Epicurean philosophy

Etymology

From Latin Epicureus, from Ancient Greek Ἐπικούρειος (Epikoureiōs), pertaining to Epicurus.
