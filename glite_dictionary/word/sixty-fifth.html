sixty-fifth

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪksti fɪfθ / (AmE), / ˈsɪksti fɪfθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number corresponding to sixty-five, being the position after sixty-fourth and before sixty-sixth in a sequence.
the ordinal number 65th

Examples

He finished in sixty-fifth place in the marathon this year.
This is the sixty-fifth anniversary of the school's founding.
Turn to page sixty-fifth in your textbook for tonight's reading.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sixty-fourth - the ordinal number 64th
  • Degree Higher: sixty-sixth - the ordinal number 66th
