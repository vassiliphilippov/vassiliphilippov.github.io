Asadha

1. PROPN

/ ɑːˈsɑːdə / (AmE), / ɑːˈsɑːdə / (BrE)
The fourth month of the Hindu calendar, typically falling in June or July of the Gregorian calendar.
fourth month of the Hindu calendar

Examples

The festival takes place every year during Asadha in many parts of India.
In the Hindu calendar, Asadha usually corresponds to June or July.
Many traditional rituals are performed throughout the month of Asadha.

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Āṣāḍha,' the name of the month in ancient Indian calendars.
