Praha

1. PROPN

/ ˈprɑː.hɑː / (AmE), / ˈprɑː.hɑː / (BrE)
the Czech name for Prague, the capital and largest city of the Czech Republic
Czech name for Prague

Examples

We visited Praha last summer and were amazed by its medieval architecture.
The Charles Bridge in Praha is a popular tourist attraction.
Many international conferences are held each year in Praha.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Prague - the capital and largest city of the Czech Republic

Etymology

Czech name for the city known in English as Prague.
