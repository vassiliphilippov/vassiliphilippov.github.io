algebraist

1. NOUN

/ ælˈdʒeɪbrəɪst / (AmE), / ælˈdʒiːbrɪɪst / (BrE)
a mathematician who specializes in algebra
mathematician specializing in algebra

Examples

The algebraist delivered a lecture on modern group theory at the university.
As a dedicated algebraist, she has published many papers on abstract algebra.
Several algebraists attended the conference to discuss recent advances in their field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mathematician - an expert or specialist in mathematics

Etymology

Formed from 'algebra' + '-ist', denoting specialization.
