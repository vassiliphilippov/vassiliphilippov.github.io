Lakshmi

1. PROPN

/ ˈlʌkʃmi / (AmE), / ˈlʌkʃmi / (BrE)
the Hindu goddess of fortune, prosperity, and wealth, worshipped as the consort of Vishnu
Hindu goddess of fortune and prosperity

Examples

Many Hindus pray to Lakshmi during Diwali to seek blessings for wealth and prosperity.
A beautiful statue of Lakshmi adorned the family's prayer room.
The festival featured a traditional Lakshmi puja followed by a feast.

Etymology

From Sanskrit लक्ष्मी (lakṣmī), meaning 'fortune' or 'prosperity'.
