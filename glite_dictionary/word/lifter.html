lifter

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪftɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɪftə / (BrE)
an athlete who competes in weightlifting by lifting barbells
athlete who lifts barbells

Examples

The lifter broke the national record with an impressive clean and jerk.
As a competitive lifter, she trains intensely six days a week.
Several experienced lifters attended the international weightlifting championship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weightlifter - an athlete who competes in the sport of weightlifting

Etymology

From 'lift' + '-er', denoting a person who lifts.

2. NOUN

/ ˈlɪftər / (AmE), / ˈlɪftə / (BrE)
a thief who steals goods from a store, typically by concealing them
a thief who steals from stores

Examples

The security guard caught the lifter trying to leave the store with unpaid items.
Store owners have become more vigilant in identifying potential lifters.
She was accused of being a lifter after the missing merchandise was found in her bag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoplifter - someone who steals goods from a shop while pretending to be a customer
