fettuccine

1. NOUN

/ ˌfetʊˈtʃini / (AmE), / ˌfetʊˈtʃiːneɪ / (BrE)
A type of pasta in flat, thick strips, wider than linguine.
wide flat pasta strips

Examples

She ordered the fettuccine Alfredo at her favorite Italian restaurant.
The chef tossed the fettuccine with a creamy mushroom sauce.
Homemade fettuccine tastes much better than the store-bought version.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: linguine - pasta in long, narrow, flat strips
  • Degree Higher: tagliatelle - ribbon-shaped pasta, similar to but generally wider than fettuccine

Etymology

From Italian 'fettuccine', plural of 'fettuccina', meaning 'little ribbon'.
