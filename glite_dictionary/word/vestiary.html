vestiary

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɛstiˌɛri / (AmE), / ˈvɛstiˌɑːri / (BrE)
relating to clothing, especially to vestments or garments worn for ceremonial or religious purposes.
relating to clothing or vestments

Examples

The museum displayed a collection of vestiary artifacts from the 15th century.
Her research focused on the vestiary symbolism in Renaissance paintings.
Each order had its own vestiary traditions for ceremonial attire.

Etymology

From Latin vestīmentum (garment), related to vestis (clothes).
