Seeland

1. PROPN

/ ˈziː.lənd / (AmE), / ˈziː.lənd / (BrE)
the largest island of Denmark, on which Copenhagen is located
largest island of Denmark

Examples

Many tourists visit Seeland to experience the vibrant city of Copenhagen.
The landscapes of Seeland are known for their beauty and diversity.
Denmark's population is concentrated on Seeland, particularly around its capital.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Zealand - the largest island of Denmark, on which Copenhagen is located (alternate spelling)

Etymology

From Danish 'Sjælland'; 'Seeland' is the German form, occasionally used in English.
