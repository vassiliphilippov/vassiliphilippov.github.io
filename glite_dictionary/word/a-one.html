A-one

1. ADJ

/ ˌeɪ ˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˈwʌn / (BrE)
of the very best quality; excellent
of the highest quality

Examples

The service at this restaurant is truly A-one—I would highly recommend it.
She received A-one medical care after the accident.
His skills as a negotiator are simply A-one.

Synset Examples

an ace reporter
a crack shot
a first-rate golfer
a super party
played top-notch tennis
an athlete in tiptop condition
she is absolutely tops

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first-rate - of the best quality; excellent
  • Synonym: top-notch - of the highest quality; excellent
  • Antonym: second-rate - of inferior or mediocre quality

Etymology

From the classification 'A1' used in Lloyd's Register to denote top quality ships in the 18th–19th centuries.
