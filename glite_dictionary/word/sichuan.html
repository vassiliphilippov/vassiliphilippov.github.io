Sichuan

1. PROPN

/ siˈtʃwɑn / (AmE), / siˈtʃwɑːn / (BrE)
a populous province in south central China, known for its spicy cuisine and varied landscapes
province in south central China

Examples

The Sichuan province is famous for its bold and spicy cuisine.
Many pandas live in the mountains of Sichuan.
She traveled through Sichuan to explore its rich cultural heritage.

Etymology

From Mandarin 四川 (Sìchuān), literally 'four rivers'.
