immensely

1. ADV

/ ɪˈmɛnsli / (AmE), / ɪˈmɛn(t)sli / (BrE)
to an extremely great extent; very much
to a very great extent

Examples

She was immensely grateful for their help during the crisis.
The view from the mountain was immensely beautiful and unforgettable.
This discovery will immensely improve the effectiveness of the new treatment.

Synset Examples

He had vastly overestimated his resources
was immensely more important to the project as a scientist than as an administrator

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enormously - to an extremely great degree
  • Synonym: vastly - to a very great extent
  • Antonym: slightly - to a small degree or extent

Etymology

From Latin immensus (immeasurable) + -ly.
