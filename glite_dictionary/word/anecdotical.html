anecdotical

1. ADJ

/ ˌænɪkˈdoʊtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌanɪkˈdəʊtɪkəl / (BrE)
Characterized by or given to telling anecdotes; relating to or consisting of anecdotes.
characterized by anecdotes

Examples

Her anecdotical style made the lecture lively and engaging for the audience.
The book provides an anecdotical account of the city's cultural history.
He was famous for his anecdotical storytelling at dinner parties.

Synset Examples

anecdotal conversation
an anecdotal history of jazz
he was at his anecdotic best

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anecdotal - based on or consisting of anecdotes

Etymology

From anecdote + -ical, based on anecdotal.
