estimator

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛstəˌmeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɛstɪmeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who is skilled at making calculations, especially of costs, quantities, or values, often as a professional occupation
person skilled at making calculations

Examples

As an experienced estimator, she quickly calculated the project's total cost.
The company hired a skilled estimator to determine how much material was needed.
Many construction firms rely on a professional estimator to prepare accurate bids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calculator - a person who performs mathematical calculations

Etymology

From Latin *aestimāre* (to value, estimate) via English 'estimate' + '-or' suffix.
