meatman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːtˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmiːtmæn / (BrE)
a man who sells meat; a male meat retailer or butcher
male meat retailer

Examples

The meatman delivered fresh cuts to our doorstep every Saturday morning.
As a child, he dreamed of becoming a meatman like his father before him.
At the market, the meatman greeted customers with a friendly smile and expert advice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butcher - a person who sells meat or slaughters animals for meat
