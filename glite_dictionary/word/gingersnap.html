gingersnap

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪndʒɚˌsnæp / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒəˌsnæp / (BrE)
A crisp, round cookie flavored with ginger and often spices such as cinnamon or cloves.
crisp ginger-flavored cookie

Examples

She served homemade gingersnaps with tea on a chilly afternoon.
The aroma of gingersnaps baking in the oven filled the house with a warm, spicy scent.
He dunked a gingersnap into his coffee for a sweet treat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gingerbread - sweet baked good flavored with ginger, often in loaf or cake form
