L. S. Lowry

1. PROPN

/ ɛl ɛs ˈlaʊri / (AmE), / ɛl ɛs ˈlaʊri / (BrE)
(1887–1976) an English painter known for his distinctive urban landscapes and depictions of industrial life in the North of England.
English painter famous for industrial scenes

Examples

L. S. Lowry is best known for his paintings of industrial northern England.
A major exhibition of L. S. Lowry's work was held at the Tate Britain.
Many art critics have praised L. S. Lowry for his unique style and perspective.
