filling

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
a food mixture used to fill pastry, sandwiches, or other baked goods
food mixture for pastries or sandwiches

Examples

The apple filling in this pie is both sweet and tart.
She made a chicken salad filling for the sandwiches.
The pastries had a creamy custard filling that everyone enjoyed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stuffing - a mixture of food that is used to fill another food, especially poultry

2. NOUN

/ ˈfɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the action or process of making something full or filling a space, container, or gap
the act of making something full

Examples

After the filling of the forms, please return them to the front desk.
The filling of the cracks helped restore the wall's appearance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: emptying - the act of making something empty

Etymology
