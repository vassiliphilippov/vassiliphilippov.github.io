poisonously

1. ADV

/ ˈpɔɪzənəsli / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪzənəsli / (BrE)
In a very malevolent, harmful, or malicious manner.
in a malevolent or harmful way

Examples

She spoke to him poisonously, her words biting with hidden malice.
Political debates sometimes turn poisonously personal, undermining any attempt at compromise.
He glared poisonously at his rival, unable to conceal his resentment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maliciously - in a malicious manner
  • Antonym: benevolently - in a kindly or charitable manner

Etymology

From 'poisonous' + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
