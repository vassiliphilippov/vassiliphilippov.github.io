Arizonan

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛrɪˈzoʊnən / (AmE), / ˌærɪˈzəʊnən / (BrE)
a native or resident of the U.S. state of Arizona
person from Arizona

Examples

As an Arizonan, she is accustomed to hot, dry climates.
My neighbor is a proud Arizonan who enjoys hiking in the desert.
Many Arizonans celebrate unique traditions during state holidays.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Arizona - a state in the southwestern United States
