reliant

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈlaɪənt / (AmE), / rɪˈlaɪənt / (BrE)
needing or depending on someone or something for help, support, or the means to function.
depending on something or someone

Examples

Many small businesses are reliant on steady cash flow to survive.
Modern societies have become increasingly reliant upon technology.
The company is reliant on imported materials for its production.

Synset Examples

dependent on Middle Eastern oil

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dependent - needing someone or something for support or help

Etymology

From French *reliant*, present participle of *relier* (to bind, connect).
