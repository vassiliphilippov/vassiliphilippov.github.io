yawn

1. NOUN

/ jɑn / (AmE), / jɔːn / (BrE)
An involuntary act of opening the mouth wide and inhaling deeply, usually caused by tiredness or boredom.
involuntary open-mouthed breath, usually from tiredness

Examples

I tried to hide my yawn during the long meeting.
A big yawn escaped her lips as midnight approached.
He stifled a yawn while reading the dull textbook.

Synset Examples

he could not suppress a yawn
the yawning in the audience told him it was time to stop
he apologized for his oscitancy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oscitancy - the state or quality of yawning or drowsiness

Etymology

Probably imitative in origin, possibly related to Old English *gionian* (to gape, yawn).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00838954-n
Synset POS: n

2. VERB

/ jɑn / (AmE), / jɔːn / (BrE)
(of a space or opening) to be wide open or to gape widely.
be wide open or gape (of a space)

Examples

A chasm seemed to yawn before them, blocking their path.
The doors yawned open to reveal the grand hall inside.
Vast empty windows yawned in the ruined building, filled with darkness.

Synset Examples

the deep gaping canyon

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gape - to open wide, especially of the mouth or an opening

Etymology

From Old English *geonian* (to open the mouth wide, to gape), related to Old Norse *gina* (to yawn, gape).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02724625-v
Synset POS: v

3. VERB

/ jɔn / (AmE), / jɔːn / (BrE)
to open your mouth wide and take a deep breath in a way that shows you are tired, bored, or lacking oxygen
to open mouth wide from tiredness or boredom

Examples

She couldn't stop yawning during the dull lecture.
He yawned and stretched before getting out of bed.
The baby yawned adorably while lying in her crib.

Synset Examples

The child yawned during the long performance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gape - to open the mouth wide, often in surprise or wonder

Etymology

From Old English 'ganian', meaning 'to open the mouth wide', related to similar words in other Germanic languages.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00007328-v
Synset POS: v
