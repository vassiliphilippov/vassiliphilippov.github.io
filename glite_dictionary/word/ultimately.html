ultimately

1. ADV

/ ˈʌltɪmətli / (AmE), / ˈʌltɪmətli / (BrE)
as the final result of a process or after everything else has been considered; in the end.
in the end; after everything

Examples

Ultimately, the decision will be made by the board of directors.
We ultimately chose the cheapest option after comparing all the offers.
Although they faced many difficulties, they ultimately achieved their goal.

Synset Examples

ultimately he had to give in
at long last the winter was over

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finally - after a long time or at the end; at last
  • Antonym: initially - at the beginning; in the early stages

Etymology

From Medieval Latin ultimately, from Latin ultimus (last, farthest).
