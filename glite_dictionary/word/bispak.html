bispak

1. NOUN

/ bɪsˈpɑk / (AmE), / bɪsˈpak / (BrE)
(Indonesian slang) A sexually promiscuous woman, particularly one who is viewed as available for sex, often used pejoratively.
sexually promiscuous woman (Indonesian slang)
slang offensive

Examples

The term bispak is frequently used in Indonesian internet forums to shame women who are seen as too sexually open.
Some people consider calling someone a bispak highly disrespectful and offensive.
The word bispak reflects cultural attitudes towards women's sexuality in some Southeast Asian contexts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slut - a woman perceived as sexually promiscuous
  • Antonym: prude - a person who is very proper or modest in behavior, especially about sex

Etymology

Indonesian slang, from 'bisa pakai' meaning 'can be used' or 'available to use'.
