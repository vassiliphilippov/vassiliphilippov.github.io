poulterer

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊltərər / (AmE), / ˈpəʊltərə / (BrE)
a person or shop that sells poultry and poultry products
person/shop selling poultry

Examples

The poulterer displayed fresh chicken and duck in his shop window.
Every Sunday, she visited her local poulterer to buy eggs and turkey.
The poulterer's advice helped me choose the best poultry for roasting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butcher - a person who sells meat

Etymology

From Middle English *pulter*, ultimately from Old French *pouletier* (dealer in poultry), from *poulet* (chicken).
