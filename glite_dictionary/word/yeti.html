yeti

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛti / (AmE), / ˈjɛti / (BrE)
a large, hairy, human-like creature said to live in the Himalayan mountains; a mythical ape-like being of folklore
mythical ape-like creature of the Himalayas

Examples

Many explorers have searched the Himalayas hoping to catch a glimpse of the elusive yeti.
The legend of the yeti has fascinated people around the world for generations.
He claimed he saw enormous footprints in the snow, possibly belonging to a yeti.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abominable snowman - another name for the yeti; mythical Himalayan creature

Etymology

From Tibetan གཡའ་དྲེད་ (g.ya' dred), meaning 'man-bear'.
