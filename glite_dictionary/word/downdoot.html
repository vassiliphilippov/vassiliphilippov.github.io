to downdoot

1. VERB

/ ˈdaʊn.duːt / (AmE), / ˈdaʊn.duːt / (BrE)
(slang) To give a negative rating or review to something, particularly online content.
give a negative rating (slang, online)
slang

Examples

Many users decided to downdoot the article after reading its inaccurate information.
If you really dislike the post, you can just downdoot it and move on.
He said he would downdoot anything that spreads misinformation online.

Synset Examples

He felt the need to downdoot the article for misleading facts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: downvote - to cast a vote expressing disapproval, especially online

Etymology

Blend of 'downvote' and 'doot', popularized in certain online communities.
