Shiraz

1. PROPN

/ ʃɪˈrɑz / (AmE), / ʃɪˈrɑːz / (BrE)
a city in south-central Iran, known for its cultural heritage and proximity to the ruins of ancient Persepolis.
city in Iran, near Persepolis

Examples

Shiraz is renowned for its poetry, gardens, and historic sites.
During their trip to Iran, they spent two days exploring Shiraz.
The ruins of Persepolis are located just a short drive from Shiraz.
