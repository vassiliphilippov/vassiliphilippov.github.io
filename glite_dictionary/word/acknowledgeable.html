acknowledgeable

1. ADJ

/ əkˈnɑː.lɪdʒ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / əkˈnɒ.lɪdʒ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
Capable of being acknowledged or recognized; able to be admitted as true or valid.
able to be acknowledged or recognized

Examples

Her contribution to the project was fully acknowledgeable and received proper recognition.
Only some of the sources are acknowledgeable in the official report.
The error was clearly acknowledgeable, showing transparency on the part of the organization.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unacknowledgeable - not able to be acknowledged

Etymology

From 'acknowledge' + '-able', formed in English.
