fortitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔːrtɪˌtuːd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtɪtjuːd / (BrE)
Courage and strength of mind in facing pain, adversity, or difficulty.
mental strength and courage

Examples

She showed great fortitude in coping with her illness and never gave up hope.
Despite the harsh conditions, the team's fortitude carried them through to the finish line.
It takes considerable fortitude to stand by your principles in the face of criticism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: courage - the ability to do something that frightens one
  • Antonym: cowardice - lack of bravery

Etymology

From Latin fortitudo (strength, firmness), from fortis (strong, brave).
