exclusively

1. ADV

/ ɪkˈskluːsɪvli / (AmE), / ɪkˈskluːsɪvli / (BrE)
Only; without including or involving anyone or anything else.
only; without others included

Examples

Membership is restricted exclusively to students of the university.
The restaurant serves exclusively vegetarian dishes.
She decided to shop exclusively online this year.

Synset Examples

was entirely to blame
a school devoted entirely to the needs of problem children
he works for Mr. Smith exclusively
did it solely for money
the burden of proof rests on the prosecution alone
a privilege granted only to him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solely - not involving anyone or anything else; only
  • Synonym: only - no one or nothing more besides; solely or exclusively
  • Antonym: inclusively - including everything or everyone

Etymology

From exclusive + -ly; ultimately from Latin 'excludere' (to shut out).
