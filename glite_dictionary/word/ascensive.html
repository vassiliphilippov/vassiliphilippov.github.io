ascensive

1. ADJ

/ əˈsɛnsɪv / (AmE), / əˈsɛnsɪv / (BrE)
tending or moving upward; directed toward rising or ascent
tending to move upward

Examples

The plant exhibits an ascensive growth pattern as it climbs toward the sunlight.
Her ascensive ambitions pushed her to continually strive for higher goals.
The smoke followed an ascensive path, curling upward into the morning sky.

Synset Examples

rooted and ascendant strength like that of foliage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ascending - moving or going upward
  • Antonym: descending - moving or directed downward

Etymology

From Latin ascendere (to rise, to climb).
