twirlingly

1. ADV

/ ˈtwɜrlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtwɜːlɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner involving twirls or spinning movements.
in a twirling manner

Examples

The couple moved twirlingly across the ballroom floor, delighting the audience.
She answered his question twirlingly, spinning around with a laugh.
The leaves fell twirlingly from the trees on a breezy autumn day.

Synset Examples

they had a most twirlingly magnificent time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spin - to turn around rapidly on an axis
  • Synonym: twist - to turn something repeatedly or to wind around

Etymology

Formed from 'twirl' + '-ing' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
