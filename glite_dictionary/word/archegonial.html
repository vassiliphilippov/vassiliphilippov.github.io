archegonial

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑr.kɪˈɡoʊ.ni.əl / (AmE), / ˌɑː.kɪˈɡəʊ.ni.əl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of an archegonium, the female reproductive organ in certain plants and fungi
relating to an archegonium
medical

Examples

The archegonial tissue develops at the apex of the gametophyte.

Related Terms
