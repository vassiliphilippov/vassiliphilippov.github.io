Kolami

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈlɑːmi / (AmE), / kəʊˈlɑːmi / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken by the Kolam people in central India
a Dravidian language of central India

Examples

The Kolami language is still spoken by many people in Maharashtra and Telangana.
Linguists have classified Kolami as part of the Dravidian language family.
Efforts are being made to preserve Kolami for future generations.
