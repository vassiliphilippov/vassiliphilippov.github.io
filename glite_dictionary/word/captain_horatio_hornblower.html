Captain Horatio Hornblower

1. PROPN

a fictional English naval officer, later admiral, active during the Napoleonic Wars, and the protagonist of novels by C. S. Forester
fictional English naval officer; hero of C. S. Forester novels

Examples

Many readers admire Captain Horatio Hornblower for his intelligence and bravery at sea.

C. S. Forester’s creation, Captain Horatio Hornblower, has inspired several film and television adaptations.
