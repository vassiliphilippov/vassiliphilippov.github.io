toothache

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːθˌeɪk / (AmE), / ˈtuːθ.eɪk / (BrE)
a pain in or around a tooth
pain in a tooth

Examples

She couldn't enjoy dinner because her toothache was so severe.
He went to the dentist after suffering from a persistent toothache.
Using clove oil provided temporary relief from the toothache.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headache - pain in the head
  • Synonym: stomachache - pain in the stomach or abdomen

Etymology

From Middle English 'tooth' + 'ache', patterned after Old English 'tōþæce'.
