wheel

1. NOUN

/ wil / (AmE), / wiːl / (BrE)
a circular hand-operated device used for steering a vehicle, especially a car, ship, or aircraft
device for steering vehicles

Examples

She gripped the wheel tightly as she navigated the sharp turn.
The captain stood at the wheel, guiding the ship through the storm.
Learning to keep both hands on the wheel is important for new drivers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steering wheel - a wheel used by the driver or helmsman to steer a vehicle

/ wil / (AmE), / wiːl / (BrE)

Examples

Related Terms
