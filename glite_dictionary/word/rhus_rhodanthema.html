Rhus rhodanthema

1. NOUN

/ ruːs roʊˈdænθəmə / (AmE), / ruːs rəʊˈdanθəmə / (BrE)
An evergreen tree native to Australia that yields a dark yellow wood.
Australian evergreen tree with yellow wood

Examples

The wood of Rhus rhodanthema is valued for its distinctive color and durability.
In botanical gardens, Rhus rhodanthema is admired for its evergreen foliage.
Furniture makers sometimes use Rhus rhodanthema for decorative woodwork pieces.

Etymology

From genus name Rhus and species name rhodanthema, following botanical Latin conventions.
