ninjutsu

1. NOUN

/ nɪnˈdʒuːtsu / (AmE), / nɪnˈdʒʊtsuː / (BrE)
a traditional Japanese martial art focusing on espionage, stealth, and camouflage techniques
Japanese martial art of stealth and espionage

Examples

He studied ninjutsu for years to master the art of stealth.
Historical records describe ninjutsu as a combination of espionage and survival skills.
Many martial arts schools now offer classes in ninjutsu techniques.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ninja - a person trained in ninjutsu; a traditional Japanese spy or assassin

Etymology

From Japanese 忍術 (ninjutsu), from 忍 (nin, stealth) + 術 (jutsu, art or technique).
