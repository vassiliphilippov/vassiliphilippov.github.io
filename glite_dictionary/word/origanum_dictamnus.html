Origanum dictamnus

1. NOUN

/ ɔːˈrɪgənəm dɪkˈtæmnəs / (AmE), / ɒˈrɪgənəm dɪkˈtamnəs / (BrE)
A dwarf aromatic shrub native to Crete, commonly known as dittany of Crete, with pink flowers and downy leaves.
dwarf aromatic shrub native to Crete

Examples

In traditional remedies, Origanum dictamnus is prized for its healing properties.
Her garden features many Mediterranean herbs, including Origanum dictamnus with its pinkish blooms.
Researchers studied the essential oils extracted from Origanum dictamnus to assess their antimicrobial effects.

Etymology

From Latin, based on Greek ὀρίγανον (origanon, 'oregano') and δίκταμνος (diktamnos, 'dittany').
