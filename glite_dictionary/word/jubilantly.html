jubilantly

1. ADV

/ ˈdʒuː.bɪ.lənt.li / (AmE), / ˈdʒuː.bɪ.lənt.li / (BrE)
in a way that shows great joy, excitement, or triumph
in a joyful or triumphant way

Examples

The crowd cheered jubilantly as the team lifted the trophy.
She jubilantly announced her acceptance into the university.
Fireworks exploded jubilantly in the night sky during the celebration.

Synset Examples

they shouted happily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joyfully - in a joyful manner
  • Synonym: cheerfully - in a cheerful manner
  • Antonym: sadly - in a sad manner

Etymology

From 'jubilant' + adverbial suffix '-ly'. 'Jubilant' from Latin jubilare (to shout for joy).
