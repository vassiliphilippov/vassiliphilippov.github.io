daddy

1. NOUN

/ ˈdædi / (AmE), / ˈdædi / (BrE)
an informal term for one's father, often used by children; dad
informal term for father
informal

Examples

Whenever I was scared at night, I called out for my daddy to comfort me.
Her daddy picked her up from school every Friday afternoon.
Even as an adult, he still referred to his father as daddy out of habit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dad - an informal term for one's father
  • Synonym: father - a male parent

Etymology

Imitative of baby talk (like 'dada'), probably related to 'dad'.
