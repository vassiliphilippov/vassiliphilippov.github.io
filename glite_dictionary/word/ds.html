DS

1. NOUN

/ di ɛs / (AmE), / diː ɛs / (BrE)
the Bureau of Diplomatic Security in the U.S. State Department, responsible for the security of diplomats and embassies overseas
U.S. State Dept. security bureau

Examples

The DS coordinated the evacuation of diplomats during the crisis.
He works for the DS to help protect U.S. embassies abroad.
The DS provides security training to embassy staff worldwide.

Etymology

Abbreviation of Diplomatic Security.
