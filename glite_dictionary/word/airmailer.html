airmailer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌmeɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈeəˌmeɪlə / (BrE)
an envelope or package designed for sending mail by air, typically lightweight and marked for airmail service
envelope for airmail

Examples

He placed the letter in an airmailer before mailing it overseas.
Please use an airmailer if you want your documents to arrive quickly.
The shop sells airmailers in different sizes for international delivery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airmail - mail sent by air transport

Etymology

Formed from 'airmail' + '-er'.
