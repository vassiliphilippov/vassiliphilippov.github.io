odorless

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊdɚləs / (AmE), / ˈəʊdələs / (BrE)
Having no smell; not producing any odor.
having no smell

Examples

Helium is an odorless and colorless gas used in balloons.
The cleaning solution is completely odorless, making it ideal for enclosed spaces.
She preferred odorless flowers in her bouquet to avoid triggering allergies.

Synset Examples

odorless gas
odorless flowers

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell
  • Antonym: smelly - having a strong or unpleasant smell

Etymology

Formed from 'odor' (from Latin 'odor') and the suffix '-less' (meaning 'without').
