Daniel Morgan

1. PROPN

/ ˈdænjəl ˈmɔrɡən / (AmE), / ˈdænjəl ˈmɔːɡən / (BrE)
(1736–1802) An American general and soldier famous for his role in the American Revolution, especially for leading the defeat of the British at the Battle of Cowpens, South Carolina.
American general in the Revolution

Examples

Daniel Morgan played a pivotal role in the victory at the Battle of Cowpens during the American Revolution.
Historians often cite Daniel Morgan as one of the key tactical leaders of the colonial forces.
Many monuments across the United States commemorate Daniel Morgan's military achievements.
