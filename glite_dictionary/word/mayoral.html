mayoral

1. ADJ

/ ˈmeɪ.ə.rəl / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.ə.rəl / (BrE)
relating to a mayor or the office of mayor
of or relating to a mayor

Examples

The city held its mayoral election last November.
Several candidates are campaigning for the mayoral position.
The mayoral debate focused on public transportation policies.

Synset Examples

mayoral elections

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mayor - the elected head of a city or municipal government

Etymology

From mayor + -al, meaning "of or pertaining to a mayor".
