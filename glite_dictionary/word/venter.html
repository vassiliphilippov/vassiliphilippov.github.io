venter

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛntər / (AmE), / ˈvɛntə / (BrE)
the bulging part of an organ or structure, especially the belly of a muscle or the underside of an animal or plant organ
bulging part of an organ, belly of a muscle

Examples

The venter of the muscle is located between its origin and insertion points.
The spider's venter can be seen when it is hanging from its web.
Botanists examined the venter of the plant structure to identify the species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: belly - the front part of the human trunk below the ribs, containing the stomach

Etymology

From Latin venter (belly, abdomen).
