pensionary

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛnʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈpɛnʃənəri / (BrE)
a person who receives money from a pension fund, especially as a regular payment after retirement
person receiving a pension

Examples

After decades of work, she became a pensionary and enjoyed her retirement.
The company provided support for every pensionary in its retirement scheme.
Many former employees are now listed as pensionary members of the fund.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pensioner - a person who receives a pension, typically after retirement

Etymology

From Latin 'pensiōnārius' (someone paid), via French 'pensionnaire'.
