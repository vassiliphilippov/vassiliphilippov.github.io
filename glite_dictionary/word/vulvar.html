vulvar

1. ADJ

/ ˈvʌlvər / (AmE), / ˈvʌlvə / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the vulva
relating to the vulva
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with a rare vulvar infection.
Routine exams sometimes include a vulvar inspection to check for abnormalities.
Some women may experience vulvar irritation due to allergic reactions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vulva - the external female genital organs

Etymology

From Latin 'vulva' (womb, female genitalia) with the suffix '-ar' forming adjectives.
