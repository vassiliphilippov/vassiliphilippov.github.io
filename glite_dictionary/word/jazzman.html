jazzman

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒæzˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈdʒazˌman / (BrE)
a male musician who plays or composes jazz music
male jazz musician

Examples

After years of practice, he finally became a respected jazzman in the local music scene.
The legendary jazzman played his trumpet with effortless grace at every concert.
Many young musicians look up to the great jazzmen of the past for inspiration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jazz musician - a musician who plays or composes jazz, regardless of gender

Etymology

Formed in English from 'jazz' + 'man'.
