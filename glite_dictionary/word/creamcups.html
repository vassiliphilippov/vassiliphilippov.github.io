creamcups

1. NOUN

/ ˈkriːmˌkʌps / (AmE), / ˈkriːmˌkʌps / (BrE)
a California wildflower with small, pale yellow cup-shaped flowers, commonly found in open grassy areas
California wildflower with yellow cup-shaped blooms

Examples

In spring, creamcups blanket the Californian hillsides with their pale yellow blooms.
We spotted a field of creamcups growing alongside the poppies during our hike.
The delicate flowers of creamcups are easy to recognize by their cup-shaped petals.
