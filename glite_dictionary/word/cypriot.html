/ ˈsɪpriət / (AmE), / ˈsɪpriət / (BrE)

Examples

The museum features Cypriot pottery from ancient times.

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɪpriət / (AmE), / ˈsɪprɪət / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Cyprus
native or inhabitant of Cyprus

Examples

Maria is a proud Cypriot who often speaks about her childhood in Nicosia.
The island's rich history attracts both Cypriot locals and international visitors every year.
A Cypriot won gold at the Mediterranean Games in track and field.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Greek Cypriot - a Cypriot of Greek ancestry or culture
  • Degree Lower: Turkish Cypriot - a Cypriot of Turkish ancestry or culture

Etymology

From Greek Kyprios (‘of Cyprus’), referring to the island's ancient name, Kypros.
