nonexistent

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑːnɪɡˈzɪstənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɪstənt / (BrE)
not existing; not real or present; having no existence or being.
not existing; not real

Examples

The risk of a tsunami in this region is practically nonexistent.
She searched for the records, but they were completely nonexistent.
After the company closed, support for the old software became nonexistent.

Synset Examples

chimeras are nonexistent

Related Terms

  • Antonym: existent - having existence or being

Etymology

From Latin non (not) + existent-, present participle of existere (to exist).
