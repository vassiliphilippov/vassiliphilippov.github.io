ownerless

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊnərləs / (AmE), / ˈəʊnələs / (BrE)
Having no owner; not owned by anyone.
not owned by anyone

Examples

The stray dog appeared ownerless as it wandered through the neighborhood.
Many ownerless bicycles were abandoned outside the train station after the storm.
He found an ownerless cat sheltering in his garden and decided to adopt it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unowned - not owned; having no owner
  • Antonym: owned - having an owner; possessed

Etymology

Formed from "owner" + "-less" (lacking).
