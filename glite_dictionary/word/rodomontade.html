rodomontade

1. NOUN

/ ˌroʊdəˌmɒnˈteɪd / (AmE), / ˌrəʊdəˌmɒnˈtɑːd / (BrE)
boastful or inflated talk or behavior, especially when empty or vain
vain or boastful talk

Examples

His speech was full of rodomontade but lacked any real substance.
The candidate's rodomontade made it hard to take his promises seriously.
Around the bar, he was known for his endless rodomontade about past adventures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: braggadocio - boastful or arrogant behavior
  • Antonym: humility - the quality of being humble

Etymology

From Italian 'rodomontata', from Rodomonte, a boastful character in Italian epic poetry.
