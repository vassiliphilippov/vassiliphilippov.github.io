Equus asinus

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛkwəs ˈæsɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈɛkwəs ˈæsɪnəs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the donkey, a domesticated animal used as a beast of burden, noted for its patience and stubbornness.
scientific name for the donkey

Examples

The Equus asinus is widely domesticated in many parts of the world.
Scientists conducted genetic studies on Equus asinus to learn about its ancestry.
Unlike horses, Equus asinus can survive on much rougher terrain and sparser vegetation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: donkey - a domesticated hoofed mammal used as a beast of burden

Etymology

From Latin 'Equus' (horse) + 'asinus' (ass, donkey).
