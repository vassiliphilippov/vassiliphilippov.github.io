cu in

1. NOUN

/ si ju ɪn / (AmE), / siː juː ɪn / (BrE)
An abbreviation for cubic inch, a unit of volume equal to a cube one inch on each side.
abbreviation for cubic inch (unit of volume)

Examples

The engine had a displacement of 350 cu in, making it very powerful for its time.
Please convert the measurements from cu in to liters for international customers.
This container holds only 10 cu in of water, which is a very small amount.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cubic centimetre - a unit of volume equal to a cube one centimetre on each side
  • Degree Higher: liter - unit of volume, metric system
