Joan Sutherland

1. PROPN

/ dʒoʊn ˈsʌðərlənd / (AmE), / dʒəʊn ˈsʌðələnd / (BrE)
(1926–2010) An Australian operatic soprano renowned for her powerful voice and performances in bel canto roles.
Australian operatic soprano (1926–2010)

Examples

Many critics consider Joan Sutherland one of the greatest sopranos of the twentieth century.
