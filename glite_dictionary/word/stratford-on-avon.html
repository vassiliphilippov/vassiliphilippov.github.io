Stratford-on-Avon

1. PROPN

/ ˌstræt.fɚd ən ˈeɪ.vən / (AmE), / ˌstræt.fəd ɒn ˈeɪ.vən / (BrE)
A town in central England, situated on the River Avon, famous as the birthplace and burial place of William Shakespeare.
town in England, birthplace of Shakespeare
british

Examples

Many tourists visit Stratford-on-Avon to see Shakespeare's birthplace and historical sites.
She attended a theater festival in Stratford-on-Avon last summer.
The River Avon flows through the picturesque town of Stratford-on-Avon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Avon - a river in England flowing through Stratford-on-Avon

Etymology

From the Old English 'Strǣtford' (ford by a Roman road) and 'Avon' from the Celtic word for river.
