forties

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔr.tiz / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.tiz / (BrE)
the decade from 1940 to 1949 in a particular century, especially the 20th century
the decade 1940–1949

Examples

Many influential jazz musicians rose to fame during the forties.
The forties were marked by World War II and significant social change.
Fashion trends in the forties were influenced by wartime restrictions.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirties - the decade from 1930 to 1939
  • Degree Higher: fifties - the decade from 1950 to 1959

2. NOUN

/ ˈfɔr.tiz / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.tiz / (BrE)
the period of a person's life between the ages of 40 and 49; the decade when someone is in their forties
the decade of one's life from 40 to 49

Examples

She found a renewed sense of purpose in her forties.
Many people experience career changes during their forties.
He took up running again when he entered his forties.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirties - the time of life between 30 and 39
  • Degree Higher: fifties - the time of life between 50 and 59
