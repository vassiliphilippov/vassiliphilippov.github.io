malnourished

1. ADJ

/ mælˈnɝːɪʃt / (AmE), / mælˈnʌrɪʃt / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) in a state of poor health due to a lack of proper nutrition.
lacking proper nutrition

Examples

Many children in the village are malnourished due to the ongoing drought.
The rescue team found several malnourished dogs at the abandoned house.
After months of inadequate meals, he became visibly malnourished.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undernourished - lacking sufficient food or nutrients

Etymology

From the prefix 'mal-' (bad, ill) and 'nourished', from Old French 'norir' (to nourish).
