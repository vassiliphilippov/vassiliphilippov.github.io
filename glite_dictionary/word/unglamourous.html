unglamourous

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɡlæmərəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡlæmərəs / (BrE)
not attractive, exciting, or challenging; dull and lacking in appeal or prestige
dull and lacking excitement or appeal

Examples

Cleaning the warehouse is a very unglamourous part of the job.
She accepted the unglamourous task of organizing files for the office.
Many essential but unglamourous jobs are overlooked by the public.

Synset Examples

an unglamorous job greasing engines

Related Terms

  • Antonym: glamorous - attractive or exciting in a special or magical way

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'glamourous', meaning lacking glamour.
