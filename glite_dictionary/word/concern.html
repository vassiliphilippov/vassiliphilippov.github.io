concern

1. NOUN

/ kənˈsɝn / (AmE), / kənˈsɜːn / (BrE)
A feeling of worry, anxiety, or unease about something important.
anxious feeling; worry

Examples

His mother’s concern for his health kept her awake at night.
There was growing concern about the safety of the building.
You could hear the concern in her voice as she spoke.

Synset Examples

care had aged him
they hushed it up out of fear of public reaction

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anxiety - A feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease
  • Antonym: calm - A state of being free from anxiety or worry

Etymology

From Middle French concerner, from Late Latin concernere (to sift, mix together).
