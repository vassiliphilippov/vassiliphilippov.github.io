arithmetically

1. ADV

/ əˌrɪθməˈtɪkli / (AmE), / əˌrɪθmɪˈtɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to arithmetic or basic mathematical calculation.
in terms of arithmetic

Examples

Adding these numbers arithmetically gives a quick result.
The average was calculated arithmetically rather than geometrically.
Some puzzles are arithmetically simple but logically challenging.

Synset Examples

this problem is arithmetically easy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mathematically - in a mathematical manner

Etymology

From 'arithmetic' + '-ally', formed on the model of similar adverbs.
