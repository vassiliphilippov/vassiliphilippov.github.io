granddaughter

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrænˌdɔtər / (AmE), / ˈɡrænˌdɔːtə / (BrE)
a daughter of one's son or daughter
female grandchild

Examples

Her granddaughter visited every weekend and brought her fresh flowers.
In the photo, she is holding her newborn granddaughter for the first time.
After retiring, he spent more time reading to his granddaughter in the evenings.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: grandson - a male grandchild
  • Degree Higher: grandchild - a child of one's son or daughter

Etymology

Formed from 'grand' + 'daughter', paralleling similar terms like 'grandson'.
