Hymenoxys grandiflora

1. PROPN

/ haɪ.mɪˌnɑksɪs ˌɡrændəˈflɔrə / (AmE), / hɪˌmiːnɒksɪs ˌɡrændɪˈflɔːrə / (BrE)
A species of mountain wildflower with large yellow daisy-like flower heads, found in mountainous areas of north central United States.
species of mountain wildflower

Examples

The meadows were filled with the bright yellow flowers of Hymenoxys grandiflora in early summer.
Botanists conducted a survey of Hymenoxys grandiflora populations in the Rocky Mountains.
Hikers admired the solitary blooms of Hymenoxys grandiflora along the alpine trail.

Etymology

From Latin: genus Hymenoxys, grandiflora meaning 'large-flowered'.
