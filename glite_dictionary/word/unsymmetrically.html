unsymmetrically

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.sɪˈmɛ.trɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌʌn.sɪˈmɛ.trɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a way that lacks symmetry; not arranged evenly or equally on both sides.
in an asymmetrical way

Examples

The picture frames were unsymmetrically hung along the wall, creating a modern art display.
Leaves often grow unsymmetrically on branches, giving each plant a unique appearance.
The stones were arranged unsymmetrically, suggesting they had been scattered by the wind.

Synset Examples

they were asymmetrically arranged

Related Terms

  • Antonym: symmetrically - in a symmetrical manner
  • Synonym: asymmetrically - in a manner that is not symmetrical

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' + 'symmetrical' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
