probe

1. NOUN

/ proʊb / (AmE), / prəʊb / (BrE)
a slender, flexible surgical instrument with a rounded end, used to explore wounds or body cavities
flexible surgical instrument for exploring wounds
medical

Examples

The surgeon used a probe to examine the depth of the wound.
A probe revealed a small fragment lodged inside the cavity.
After sterilizing the probe, the doctor gently explored the patient’s incision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: explorer - dental instrument used to explore cavities

Etymology

From Latin 'probare' (to test, try).
