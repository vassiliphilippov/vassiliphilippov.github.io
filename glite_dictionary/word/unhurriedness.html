unhurriedness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈhɜr.id.nəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈhʌr.id.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being without haste; calm and relaxed pace or manner.
absence of haste; calmness

Examples

Her unhurriedness in dealing with stressful situations made her an excellent manager.
The unhurriedness of village life was a welcome change from the city's rush.
He spoke with a gentle unhurriedness, drawing everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calmness - the state of being calm
  • Antonym: haste - excessive speed or urgency

Etymology

From 'unhurried' (not rushed) + '-ness' (forming nouns of quality or state).
