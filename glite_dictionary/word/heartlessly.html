heartlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈhɑrtləsli / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of compassion or sympathy; without kindness or feeling.
in a cruel or unfeeling way

Examples

The manager dismissed the employee heartlessly, without considering her personal situation.
He heartlessly ignored the pleas of the abandoned kitten.
The judge was criticized for ruling heartlessly in such a sensitive case.

Synset Examples

she behaves rather heartlessly toward her admirers

Related Terms

  • Antonym: compassionately - in a compassionate manner
  • Synonym: cruelly - in a cruel manner
