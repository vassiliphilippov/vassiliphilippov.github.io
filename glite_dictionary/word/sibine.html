Sibine

1. NOUN

/ siˈbiːni / (AmE), / sɪˈbiːni / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in the Tandjile Region of southern Chad
Chadic language of southern Chad

Examples

The Sibine language is spoken by several thousand people in Chad.
Linguists studying Sibine have documented its unique phonetic features.
Many children in the region grow up bilingual in French and Sibine.
