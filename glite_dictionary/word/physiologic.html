physiologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɪziəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌfɪziəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or consistent with an organism's normal functioning or vital processes
of normal body function
medical

Examples

Regular exercise has several physiologic benefits for the heart and lungs.
A physiologic response to stress includes an increase in heart rate.
The study measured physiologic parameters such as blood pressure and temperature.

Synset Examples

physiologic functions
physiological processes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physiological - relating to the normal functioning of living organisms
  • Antonym: pathological - relating to disease or abnormal processes

Etymology

From physiology + -ic, after physiological.
