Algerie

1. PROPN

/ al.ʒeˈʁi / (AmE), / al.ʒeˈʁi / (BrE)
The French name for Algeria, a republic in northwestern Africa on the Mediterranean Sea.
French name for Algeria (country in Africa)

Examples

Many French-language books refer to the country as Algerie rather than Algeria.
Her family emigrated from Algerie to France in the 1970s.
The film is a powerful depiction of life in Algerie after independence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Algeria - A republic in northwestern Africa; English exonym for 'Algerie'.

Etymology

French, from Arabic al-Jazā'ir.
