unloaded

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈloʊdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈləʊdɪd / (BrE)
(of a weapon) not loaded with ammunition; not containing a charge or cartridges.
not loaded with ammunition (for weapons)

Examples

He checked the gun to make sure it was unloaded before cleaning it.
The police officer insisted that all unloaded weapons be locked away safely.
Many accidents occur because people mistakenly believe their firearm is unloaded.

Synset Examples

many people are killed by guns thought to be unloaded

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loaded - (of a weapon) filled with ammunition
