jim-dandy

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʒɪmˈdændi / (AmE), / ˌdʒɪmˈdændi / (BrE)
excellent or first-rate; outstanding in quality or performance
excellent or outstanding
informal american

Examples

Your advice on this matter was jim-dandy and helped us find the perfect solution.
They threw a jim-dandy party to celebrate his promotion.
This old truck is still jim-dandy for getting around town.

Synset Examples

That was a jim-dandy performance at the talent show!
Her presentation was simply jim-dandy, full of insights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excellent - of the highest quality
  • Synonym: outstanding - extremely good or impressive
  • Antonym: mediocre - of only average quality; not very good

Etymology

19th-century American origin; 'Jim Dandy' may have originated as a fanciful name and was popularized by the 1844 song 'Jim Dandy to the Rescue.'
