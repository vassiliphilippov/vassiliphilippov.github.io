salty-ish

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɔltiˌɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɒltiˌɪʃ / (BrE)
Somewhat salty; having a mildly salty taste.
somewhat salty

Examples

The fries were salty-ish, which made them just right for my taste.
After adding the seasoning, the sauce became a bit more salty-ish than before.
She prefers her snacks salty-ish rather than extremely salty.

Synset Examples

The soup tasted bland and was a little salty-ish.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: salty - tasting of or containing salt

Etymology

Formed by adding the '-ish' suffix to 'salty' to indicate partial quality.
