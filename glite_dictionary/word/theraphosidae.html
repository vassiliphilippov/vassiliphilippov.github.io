Theraphosidae

1. NOUN

/ θɛrəˈfoʊsɪˌdiː / (AmE), / θɛrəˈfɒsɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of large, tropical, hairy spiders commonly known as tarantulas.
family of spiders (tarantulas)

Examples

The Theraphosidae family includes some of the world's largest known spiders.
Many species of Theraphosidae are kept as exotic pets due to their impressive size and appearance.
Researchers study Theraphosidae to better understand spider venom and its medical applications.

Related Terms
