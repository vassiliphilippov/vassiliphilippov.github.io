rechargeable

1. ADJ

/ riˈtʃɑrdʒəbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈtʃɑːdʒəbl / (BrE)
(of a battery or device) able to be restored to full power by means of electrical charging.
able to be recharged (battery/device)

Examples

I prefer to use rechargeable batteries to reduce waste and save money.
This flashlight comes with a rechargeable lithium-ion battery for convenience.
Many modern toothbrushes are rechargeable instead of using disposable batteries.

Synset Examples

a rechargeable battery

Related Terms

  • Antonym: disposable - (of objects) intended to be used once and thrown away

Etymology

From 'recharge' (to restore electrical energy) + '-able' (capable of).
