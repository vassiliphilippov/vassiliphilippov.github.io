femaleness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːmeɪlnəs / (AmE), / ˈfiːmeɪlnɪs / (BrE)
The qualities or conditions that characterize being female.
qualities of being female

Examples

Her femaleness was celebrated in the sculpture's graceful lines and gentle curves.
The novel explores the social perceptions of femaleness in different cultures.
For some species, femaleness can be determined by specific physical or genetic characteristics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: femininity - qualities or attributes regarded as characteristic of women
  • Antonym: maleness - the properties characteristic of the male sex

Etymology

From 'female' + '-ness' (forming a noun meaning 'quality or state of being').
