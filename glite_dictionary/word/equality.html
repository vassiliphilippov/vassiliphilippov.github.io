equality

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈkwɑlɪti / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɒlɪti / (BrE)
The state of being equal, especially in status, rights, or opportunities.
the state of being equal

Examples

The new law aims to promote equality among all citizens.
He fought passionately for equality and justice in his community.
Gender equality remains a central issue in many societies.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inequality - the state of being unequal or uneven
  • Synonym: parity - the state or condition of being equal, especially in status or pay

Etymology

From Latin aequalitas, from aequalis (equal).
