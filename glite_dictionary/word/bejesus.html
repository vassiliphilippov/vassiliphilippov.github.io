bejesus

1. INTJ

/ biˈdʒiːzəs / (AmE), / biˈdʒiːzəs / (BrE)
used as an exclamation to express surprise or shock
exclamation of surprise or shock
informal

Examples

Bejesus, that scared me half to death!
He cried out 'bejesus!' when the lights suddenly went out.
Bejesus, look at the size of that cake!

Synset Examples

She was so surprised, she just exclaimed bejesus!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gee whiz - an exclamation expressing surprise
  • Synonym: gadzooks - an exclamation expressing surprise or astonishment

Etymology

Alteration of "by Jesus" as a mild oath, chiefly Irish English in origin.
