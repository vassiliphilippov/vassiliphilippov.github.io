cropped

1. ADJ

/ krɑpt / (AmE), / krɒpt / (BrE)
(of land or soil) having been used to grow crops.
used for growing crops (soil/land)

Examples

Farmers rotated their cropped fields to restore soil fertility.
After several years as cropped land, the soil needed nutrients replenished.
This report analyzes the impact of irrigation on cropped soils in the region.

Synset Examples

cropped soil

Etymology

Past participle of 'crop' (to cut or harvest).
