emoji

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmoʊdʒi / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊdʒi / (BrE)
A small digital image or icon used to express an idea, emotion, or concept in electronic communication.
digital icon expressing emotion or idea

Examples

She added a laughing emoji to her text to show it was a joke.
The heart emoji is one of the most commonly used symbols online.
Kids today use several emojis in every message they send.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emoticon - Representation of a facial expression using keyboard characters

Etymology

From Japanese 'emoji' (絵文字), from 'e' (picture) + 'moji' (character, letter).
