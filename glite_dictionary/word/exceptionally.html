exceptionally

1. ADV

/ ɪkˈsɛpʃən(ə)li / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛpʃənəli / (BrE)
To an unusually high or remarkable degree; outstandingly.
to an unusual or remarkable degree

Examples

She performed exceptionally well on the final exam, earning the top score in the class.
The service at the restaurant was exceptionally prompt and friendly.
This year's harvest has been exceptionally abundant due to the favorable weather.

Synset Examples

it worked exceptionally well

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extremely - to a very great degree; extremely
  • Antonym: moderately - to an average or limited degree
  • Synonym: outstandingly - in a way that is extremely good or impressive

Etymology

From 'exceptional' + '-ly'; 'exceptional' from 'exception' (Latin: exceptio).
