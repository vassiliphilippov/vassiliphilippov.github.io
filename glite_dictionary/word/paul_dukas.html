Paul Dukas

1. PROPN

/ pɔl duːˈkɑː / (AmE), / pɔːl djuːˈkɑː / (BrE)
(1865–1935) A French composer best known for his orchestral work 'The Sorcerer's Apprentice.'
French composer (1865–1935)

Examples

Paul Dukas composed 'The Sorcerer's Apprentice,' which gained fame from its use in Disney's Fantasia.
Many students study the works of Paul Dukas in music history classes.
French impressionism in music was influenced by composers like Debussy and Paul Dukas.
