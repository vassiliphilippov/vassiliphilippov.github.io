pornographically

1. ADV

/ ˌpɔrnəˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌpɔːnəˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or resembles pornography; in a manner intended to sexually arouse.
in a pornographic manner

Examples

The film was criticized for being pornographically explicit in several scenes.
Some magazines display images that are pornographically suggestive.
The material was pornographically charged and not suitable for minors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pornographic - relating to or intended to cause sexual excitement, especially through explicit depiction of sexual subject matter

Etymology

From "pornographic" + "-ally".
