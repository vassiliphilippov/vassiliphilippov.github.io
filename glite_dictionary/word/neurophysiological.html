neurophysiological

1. ADJ

/ ˌnʊroʊˌfɪziəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˌfɪziəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the physiology of the nervous system or the scientific study of its functions
relating to the physiology of the nervous system
medical

Examples

The researchers measured the neurophysiological responses of the participants during the experiment.
Certain neurophysiological abnormalities can indicate early stages of neurological disorders.
He specializes in neurophysiological mechanisms underlying learning and memory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neurophysiology - the branch of physiology that studies the functions of the nervous system

Etymology

From 'neuro-' (relating to nerves) and 'physiological' (from Greek physis, 'nature' + logos, 'study').
