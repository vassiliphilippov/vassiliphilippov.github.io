cerebration

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛrəˈbreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛrɪˈbreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of thinking or using the mind, especially in a careful or deliberate way
the process of thinking

Examples

After much cerebration, the team finally decided on a new strategy.
The book encourages deep cerebration about the meaning of life.
His long cerebration on the problem yielded a creative solution.

Synset Examples

thinking always made him frown
she paused for thought

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thinking - the process of using your mind to consider or reason about something

Etymology

From Latin 'cerebrum' (brain) + '-ation'.
