Christian Dior

1. PROPN

/ ˌkrɪs.tʃən diˈɔr / (AmE), / ˌkrɪs.tʃən diˈɔː / (BrE)
(1905–1957) A French fashion designer, founder of the Dior fashion house, whose 1947 collection launched the iconic 'New Look'.
French fashion designer, founder of Dior brand

Examples

During Paris Fashion Week, several designers paid homage to Christian Dior's revolutionary impact on the industry.
The Christian Dior label remains one of the most prestigious names in haute couture to this day.
In 1947, Christian Dior introduced the 'New Look', which transformed women's fashion after World War II.
