affable

1. ADJ

/ ˈæfəbl / (AmE), / ˈafəbl / (BrE)
friendly, pleasant, and easy to talk to; showing warmth and friendliness.
friendly and easy to talk to

Examples

She had such an affable personality that everyone enjoyed her company.
His affable manner put the nervous newcomers at ease.
The manager greeted each customer with an affable smile.

Synset Examples

an affable smile
an amiable gathering
cordial relations
a cordial greeting
a genial host

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amiable - having a friendly and pleasant manner
  • Synonym: cordial - warm and friendly
  • Synonym: genial - friendly and cheerful
  • Antonym: unfriendly - not friendly or kind

Etymology

From Latin 'affabilis', from 'affari' (to speak to).
