Chingpo

1. NOUN

/ tʃɪŋˈpoʊ / (AmE), / tʃɪŋˈpəʊ / (BrE)
A member of the Kachinic group of languages, spoken by the Chingpo (Jingpo) people in parts of Myanmar (Burma) and neighboring regions.
a Kachinic language

Examples

The Chingpo language is used by the Jingpo people in northern Myanmar.
She is fluent in both Burmese and Chingpo.
Researchers are documenting the grammar and vocabulary of Chingpo for linguistic studies.

Etymology

From the self-designation of the Jingpo (Chingpo) people.
