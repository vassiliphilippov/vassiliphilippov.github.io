pleasure

1. NOUN

/ ˈplɛʒər / (AmE), / ˈplɛʒə / (BrE)
a person or thing that gives delight, satisfaction, or enjoyment
source of happiness or enjoyment

Examples

Spending time with old friends is always a pleasure.
The garden was a pleasure to explore in the spring sunshine.
He found great pleasure in sharing his knowledge with others.

Synset Examples

a joy to behold
the pleasure of his company
the new car is a delight

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delight - a high degree of pleasure or enjoyment
  • Synonym: joy - a feeling, or something that gives great pleasure or happiness

Etymology

From Latin 'placere' (to please) via Old French 'plesir' or 'plaisir'.
