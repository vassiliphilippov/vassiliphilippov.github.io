Tang

1. PROPN

/ tæŋ / (AmE), / tæŋ / (BrE)
the imperial dynasty of China that ruled from 618 to 907, noted for its cultural and political flourishing
imperial Chinese dynasty (618–907)

Examples

The Tang was a golden age of poetry, art, and cultural exchange in China.
Many inventions from the Tang period shaped Chinese civilization for centuries.
Tourists can still visit architectural wonders dating back to the Tang dynasty.

Etymology

From Chinese 唐 (Táng), the name of the dynasty.
