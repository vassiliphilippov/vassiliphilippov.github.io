copying

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑpiɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒpiɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of making a copy of something
the act of making a copy

Examples

Students were caught copying during the exam, which led to disciplinary action.
The copying of documents must be completed before the meeting begins.
She was busy with the copying of old family photographs for the reunion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imitation - the act of mimicking or replicating something

Etymology

From the verb 'copy', which comes from Latin 'copia' (abundance, transcript).
