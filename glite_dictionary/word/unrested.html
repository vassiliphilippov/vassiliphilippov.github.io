unrested

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈrɛstɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɛstɪd / (BrE)
Not having had enough rest or sleep; not refreshed.
not rested or refreshed

Examples

After staying up all night, she felt unrested and irritable at work.
He woke every hour, so he remained unrested despite being in bed for eight hours.
Even after the vacation, many employees returned to the office feeling unrested.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rested - having had enough rest or sleep; refreshed
