bilobated

1. ADJ

/ baɪˈloʊ.beɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / baɪˈləʊ.beɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
having or divided into two lobes; shaped with two distinct rounded projections or sections.
having two lobes

Examples

The bilobated structure of the fruit makes it easy to recognize among the other species.
Botanists identified the sample as a bilobated leaf from an oak tree.
Some medications can affect the appearance of the bilobated thyroid gland.

Synset Examples

a bilobate leaf

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bilobed - having or divided into two lobes

Etymology

From Latin 'bi-' (two) + 'lobatus' (having lobes).
