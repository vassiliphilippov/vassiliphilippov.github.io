bangtail

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæŋ.teɪl / (AmE), / ˈbæŋ.teɪl / (BrE)
A horse bred for racing, especially a thoroughbred used in horse races.
horse bred for racing

Examples

He placed a bet on the bangtail that had won two consecutive races.
The Kentucky Derby attracts the finest bangtails from around the world.
Her stable has produced several champion bangtails over the years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racehorse - A horse bred and trained for racing

Etymology

Bangtail refers to a horse with a trimmed tail, a feature common among racehorses.
