standardised

1. ADJ

/ ˈstændəɹdaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈstændədaɪzd / (BrE)
brought into conformity with an established standard; made uniform or consistent according to a set norm
made to conform to a standard
british

Examples

All students took the standardised test at the end of the term.
The government introduced standardised procedures to improve efficiency across departments.
A standardised format ensures that the data can be easily compared.

Synset Examples

standardized education

Related Terms

  • Synonym: standardized - brought into conformity with a standard (American spelling)

Etymology

Formed from 'standard' + past participle suffix '-ised', after 'standardize'.
