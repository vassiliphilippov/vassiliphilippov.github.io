Saipan

1. PROPN

/ saɪˈpæn / (AmE), / saɪˈpæn / (BrE)
an island in the western Pacific Ocean, the largest of the Northern Mariana Islands, once an important US military base during World War II
island in the Western Pacific; largest of the Northern Mariana Islands

Examples

US forces landed on Saipan during World War II to secure a strategic position in the Pacific.
After the war, many soldiers recounted their experiences on Saipan in memoirs and interviews.
Today, Saipan is a popular destination for its beaches and historical sites.

Examples
