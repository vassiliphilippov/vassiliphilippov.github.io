tech

1. NOUN

/ tɛk / (AmE), / tɛk / (BrE)
a school or college that teaches technical subjects, especially mechanical, industrial, or applied sciences.
technical school or college

Examples

He graduated from the local tech with a degree in engineering.
Many students at the tech are studying automotive technology.
After high school, she enrolled in a tech to learn computer programming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: technical college - an educational institution specializing in technical subjects
  • Synonym: polytechnic - a college offering courses in technical and applied sciences

Etymology

Shortened form of 'technical (school/college)'.
