sunlamp

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌnˌlæmp / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.læmp / (BrE)
a lamp that emits ultraviolet light, often used in medical or cosmetic treatments to simulate sunlight
UV-emitting lamp for therapy or tanning

Examples

She used a sunlamp during the winter to help with her seasonal depression.
The dermatologist recommended a sunlamp treatment for her skin condition.
He bought a sunlamp to maintain his tan during the colder months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tanning bed - an apparatus containing sunlamps used to produce an artificial suntan
