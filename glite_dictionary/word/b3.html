B3

1. NOUN

/ bi ˈθri / (AmE), / bi ˈθri / (BrE)
A standard size of paper measuring 353 by 500 millimeters, used mainly in printing and stationery.
international paper size (353 × 500 mm)

Examples

Could you please print these charts on B3 paper for the presentation?
The poster design fits perfectly on a B3 sheet without any need for resizing.
Artists sometimes prefer B3 pads for sketching larger illustrations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: A3 - An international paper size measuring 297 by 420 millimeters
  • Degree Higher: B2 - An international paper size measuring 500 by 707 millimeters
  • Degree Lower: B4 - An international paper size measuring 250 by 353 millimeters

Etymology

From ISO 216 international paper size standard, where 'B' denotes a series and the number indicates the rank in the series.
