phytologist

1. NOUN

/ faɪˈtɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / faɪˈtɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who specializes in the study of plants; a plant biologist
plant scientist; biologist specializing in plants

Examples

The phytologist examined the rare species growing in the rainforest.
Her work as a phytologist has led to important discoveries about plant adaptation.
A team of phytologists published new research on medicinal herbs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: botanist - a scientist who studies plants

Etymology

From Greek 'phyton' (plant) + '-logist' (one who studies).
