te

1. NOUN

/ teɪ / (AmE), / teɪ / (BrE)
the syllable used to name the seventh note (the subtonic) of a musical scale in solfège.
musical syllable for seventh scale note

Examples

In the scale, te comes just before the final do.
The choir practiced singing from do to te and back again.
She struggled to hit the note for te during her vocal exercises.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ti - the syllable naming the seventh (leading tone) note in solmization
  • Degree Lower: do - the syllable naming the first note of a musical scale in solmization

Etymology

From Italian solfeggio system, adapted from the initial syllables of a Latin hymn.
