Lassa

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɑːsə / (AmE), / ˈlɑːsə / (BrE)
the capital city of Tibet, historically significant as the religious and administrative center of Tibetan Buddhism, formerly known as the Forbidden City for its inaccessibility to outsiders
capital city of Tibet; Buddhist religious center

Examples

Thousands of pilgrims travel to Lassa each year to visit its sacred monasteries.
In the past, Lassa was known as the Forbidden City due to its isolation from the outside world.
The unique architecture of Lassa attracts historians and tourists alike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lhasa - capital of Tibet, center of Tibetan Buddhism

Etymology

From Tibetan ལྷ་ས་ (lha-sa), meaning 'place of the gods'.
