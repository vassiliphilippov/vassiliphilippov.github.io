Grenadian

1. ADJ

/ ɡrəˈneɪdiən / (AmE), / ɡrɛˈneɪdiən / (BrE)
of or relating to Grenada or its people or culture.
relating to Grenada or its people

Examples

The Grenadian government announced new policies this year.
She enjoyed learning about Grenadian cuisine during her visit.
The museum features artwork by prominent Grenadian artists.

Synset Examples

the Grenadian capital

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Grenada - island country in the Caribbean Sea

Etymology

From Grenada, the name of the Caribbean country, with English adjective suffix -ian.

2. NOUN

/ ɡrəˈneɪdiən / (AmE), / ɡrɪˈneɪdiən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Grenada, an island country in the Caribbean
native or inhabitant of Grenada

Examples

Many Grenadians celebrate their independence day with cultural festivals and parades.
The Grenadian greeted us warmly and shared stories about his homeland.
She enjoys cooking traditional Grenadian dishes for her family every weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Grenada - an island country in the Caribbean

Etymology

From Grenada + -ian, denoting association or origin.
