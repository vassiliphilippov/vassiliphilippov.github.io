summarily

1. ADV

/ səˈmɛrəli / (AmE), / sʌˈmarəli / (BrE)
without delay or formality; in a direct or immediate manner
without delay or formality

Examples

The employee was summarily dismissed after violating company policy.
During the hearing, the judge summarily rejected the request for bail.
Soldiers who deserted were sometimes summarily punished during wartime.

Synset Examples

the suspected spy was summarily executed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immediately - at once; without delay
  • Antonym: deliberately - in a careful and unhurried way

Etymology

From Late Latin summarius (of or pertaining to a summary), from Latin summa (sum, total).
