westwards

1. ADV

/ ˈwɛstwɚdz / (AmE), / ˈwɛstwədz / (BrE)
toward the west; in the direction of the west.
in the direction of the west

Examples

At sunset, the birds began flying westwards in a perfect V formation.
The river flows westwards before emptying into the bay.
We decided to drive westwards to explore the coastal towns.

Synset Examples

they traveled westward toward the setting sun

Related Terms

  • Antonym: eastwards - toward the east; in the direction of the east
  • Synonym: westward - toward the west; in the direction of the west (alternative form)

Etymology

From Old English 'west' (direction) + suffix '-wards' (toward).
