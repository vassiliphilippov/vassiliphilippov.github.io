grocer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡroʊsər / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊsə / (BrE)
a person who owns or manages a shop selling food and other small items for the home.
person selling food and household supplies

Examples

The local grocer always greets his customers with a friendly smile.
She worked as a grocer in her family's store for many years.
Our neighborhood grocer stocks fresh vegetables and homemade bread.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grocer's - a shop where a grocer sells goods (British English)

Etymology

From Anglo-Norman *grosser*, from Old French *grossier* (wholesaler), from Latin *grossus* (gross, thick).
