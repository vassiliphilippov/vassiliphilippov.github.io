loofa

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːfə / (AmE), / ˈluːfə / (BrE)
The dried fibrous part of the fruit of a luffa plant, used as a sponge for washing or as a strainer.
fibrous plant sponge

Examples

She exfoliates her skin daily with a loofa in the shower.
The loofa absorbs water quickly, making it perfect for bathing.
Farmers sometimes grow luffa plants to harvest loofa sponges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luffa - Sponge gourd plant from which loofas are made

Etymology

From Arabic 'lūf' or Egyptian Arabic 'lūfa', the name of the plant.
