stupendously

1. ADV

/ stuˈpɛndəsli / (AmE), / stjuːˈpɛndəsli / (BrE)
To an extremely impressive or astonishing degree; extraordinarily.
to an extraordinary degree

Examples

The museum's collection is stupendously valuable and draws visitors from around the world.
She performed stupendously on the final exam, impressing all her teachers.
The mountain rose stupendously above the valley, dominating the entire landscape.

Synset Examples

stupendously ignorant people

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extraordinarily - to an extraordinary or remarkable degree
  • Synonym: amazingly - in an astonishingly good or impressive way

Etymology

From 'stupendous' + '-ly'; 'stupendous' from Latin 'stupendus' (to be wondered at).
