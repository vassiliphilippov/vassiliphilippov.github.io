viziership

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪziərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈvɪziəʃɪp / (BrE)
the position, office, or term of a vizier (a high-ranking political advisor or minister, especially in Muslim countries)
office of a vizier

Examples

His appointment marked the beginning of a long viziership characterized by diplomatic reforms.
The viziership was often attained through a combination of loyalty and intelligence.
During her viziership, she implemented policies that stabilized the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vizier - a high-ranking political advisor or minister in Muslim countries

Etymology

Formed from 'vizier' (from Turkish 'vezir', via Arabic 'wazīr') + '-ship' (denoting state or office).
