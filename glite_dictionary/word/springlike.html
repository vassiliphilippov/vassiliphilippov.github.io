springlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈsprɪŋˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling a spring or having qualities similar to a spring, such as elasticity or the ability to return to a former shape
resembling a spring; elastic

Examples

The mattress has a springlike quality that provides excellent support.
She admired the springlike movement of the dancer's jump.
The metal rod bent easily, showing its springlike flexibility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elastic - able to resume original shape after being stretched or compressed
