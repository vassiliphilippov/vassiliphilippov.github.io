genus Armoracia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ɑːr.məˈreɪ.ʃi.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌɑː.məˈreɪ.sɪ.ə / (BrE)
A small genus of plants in the Brassicaceae family, which includes the horseradish.
genus of plants including horseradish

Examples

The spicy root comes from a plant in the genus Armoracia.
Scientists studied several species within the genus Armoracia to understand their medicinal properties.
Horseradish, a member of the genus Armoracia, is cultivated for its pungent root.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horseradish - a pungent root vegetable known for its spicy taste

Etymology

From Latin 'armoracea', the name for horseradish.
