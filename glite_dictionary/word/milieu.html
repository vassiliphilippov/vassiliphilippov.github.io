milieu

1. NOUN

/ miːˈljɜu / (AmE), / ˈmiːljɜː / (BrE)
The social or physical environment in which someone lives or an event takes place.
social or physical environment

Examples

The artist thrived in the creative milieu of New York City during the 1960s.
Growing up in a rural milieu shaped his values and beliefs.
Each family creates its own milieu in which children develop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: environment - the surroundings or conditions in which a person, animal, or plant lives or operates

Etymology

From French 'milieu' (middle, environment), from Latin 'medius' (middle) + 'lieu' (place).
