procurement

1. NOUN

/ prəˈkjʊrmənt / (AmE), / prəˈkjʊəmənt / (BrE)
the process of obtaining or acquiring goods, services, or supplies, especially for business or government purposes
the act of obtaining goods or services

Examples

The company's procurement department handles all purchasing contracts and supplier negotiations.
Careful procurement of materials is essential for successful construction projects.
Government procurement processes are designed to ensure transparency and fair competition among vendors.

Synset Examples

he was responsible for the procurement of materials and supplies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acquisition - the act of obtaining or getting something

Etymology

From Middle French 'procurer' (to obtain), from Latin 'procurare' (to take care of).
