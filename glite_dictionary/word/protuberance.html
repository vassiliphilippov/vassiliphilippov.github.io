protuberance

1. NOUN

/ prəˈtuːbɚəns / (AmE), / prəˈtjuːbərəns / (BrE)
a part or area that sticks out from a surface or body; a bulging or swelling.
something that sticks out or bulges

Examples

The mountain path was blocked by a large protuberance of rock.
He rubbed the protuberance on his forehead after hitting his head.
The sculpture's most distinctive feature is a protuberance on its back.

Synset Examples

the protuberance of his belly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulge - a rounded swelling or protruding part
  • Antonym: depression - an area that is lower than the surrounding surface

Etymology

From Latin protuberantia, from protuberare (to swell out).
