newlywed

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːliˌwɛd / (AmE), / ˈnjuːliˌwɛd / (BrE)
a person who has recently married
person recently married

Examples

The newlyweds spent their honeymoon in Italy.
As newlyweds, they are still adjusting to married life.
Neighbors decorated the house to welcome the newlyweds home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bride - a woman on her wedding day or just before and after the event
  • Synonym: groom - a man on his wedding day or just before and after the event

Etymology

From 'newly' (recently) + 'wed' (person who is married).
