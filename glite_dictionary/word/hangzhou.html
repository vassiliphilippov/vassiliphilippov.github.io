Hangzhou

1. PROPN

/ hɑŋˈdʒoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhæŋ dʒəʊ / (BrE)
A city in eastern China, the capital of Zhejiang province, located on Hangzhou Bay and historically renowned for its beauty and cultural significance.
city in eastern China, capital of Zhejiang

Examples

Marco Polo described Hangzhou as the most splendid city in the world during his travels.
The West Lake in Hangzhou is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a popular tourist destination.
After moving to Hangzhou, she quickly became accustomed to the city's vibrant culture and cuisine.
