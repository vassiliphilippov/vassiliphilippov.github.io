tirelessly

1. ADV

/ ˈtaɪɚləsli / (AmE), / ˈtaɪələsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows persistent and unwavering effort; without becoming tired.
without becoming tired or losing energy

Examples

She worked tirelessly to complete the community project on time.
The scientist tirelessly sought answers despite repeated setbacks.
Rescuers searched tirelessly through the night for survivors.

Synset Examples

she watched the show indefatigably

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indefatigably - in a way that cannot be fatigued or tired out

Etymology

From 'tireless' (not becoming tired) + '-ly' (adverb suffix).
