Armoracia

1. NOUN

/ ɑr.məˈreɪ.ʃi.ə / (AmE), / ɑː.məˈreɪ.sɪ.ə / (BrE)
A genus of plants in the mustard family, including the horseradish.
genus of plants including horseradish

Examples

The genus Armoracia includes the familiar horseradish plant used in cooking.
Botanists classify horseradish as Armoracia rusticana.
Research on Armoracia has contributed to understanding plant defense mechanisms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horseradish - A pungent root vegetable often used as a condiment; Armoracia rusticana

Etymology

From Latin 'armoracia', used in botanical nomenclature.
