slouchily

1. ADV

/ ˈslaʊtʃɪli / (AmE), / ˈslaʊtʃɪli / (BrE)
In a slouchy or careless, untidy manner.
in a slouchy or careless way

Examples

He walked slouchily into the room, looking half-awake.
She sat slouchily on the sofa, her hair unbrushed.
He often dressed slouchily, preferring comfort over style.

Synset Examples

slouchily dressed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slouchy - having a lazy or careless manner or appearance
