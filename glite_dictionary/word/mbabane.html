Mbabane

1. PROPN

/ əmˈbɑːbɑːneɪ / (AmE), / əmˈbɑːbɑːneɪ / (BrE)
the capital city of Eswatini (formerly Swaziland), situated in the northwestern part of the country
capital city of Eswatini (Swaziland)
