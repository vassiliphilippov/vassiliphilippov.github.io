untended

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtɛndɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɛndɪd / (BrE)
not cared for or looked after; lacking attention or maintenance.
not cared for; unattended

Examples

The untended garden became wild with weeds after just a few months.
Please make sure no equipment is left untended in the laboratory.
An untended wound can easily become infected without proper care.

Synset Examples

untended garden was soon overgrown with weeds
untended children

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neglected - not receiving proper care or attention
  • Antonym: tended - given care or attention

Etymology

From un- (not) + tended (past participle of tend, from Latin tendere 'to stretch, attend').
