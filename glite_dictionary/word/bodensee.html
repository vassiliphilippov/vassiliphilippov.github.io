Bodensee

1. PROPN

/ ˈboʊdənˌzeɪ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊdənˌzeɪ / (BrE)
the German name for Lake Constance, a large lake in central Europe on the border of Germany, Switzerland, and Austria
German name for Lake Constance

Examples

The ferry across the Bodensee offers stunning views of the distant Alps.
Many tourists cycle around the shores of the Bodensee each summer.
The Bodensee region is known for its picturesque towns and vineyards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lake Constance - a large lake bordered by Germany, Switzerland, and Austria

Etymology

German, from 'Boden' (a nearby town) + 'See' (lake).
