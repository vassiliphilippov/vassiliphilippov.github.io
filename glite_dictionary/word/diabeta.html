DiaBeta

1. PROPN

/ daɪəˈbeɪtə / (AmE), / daɪəˈbiːtə / (BrE)
A trademarked name for glyburide, an oral antidiabetic drug that stimulates the release of insulin from the pancreas.
brand name for an oral antidiabetic drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed DiaBeta to help control his blood sugar levels.
Patients taking DiaBeta should monitor for signs of low blood sugar.
She switched from insulin injections to DiaBeta tablets after her diagnosis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glyburide - the generic drug also known as DiaBeta, used to treat type 2 diabetes
