slider

/ ˈslaɪdər / (AmE), / ˈslaɪdə / (BrE)

freshwater turtle species

The pet store had several sliders swimming in their tanks, attracting the attention of children.
With proper care, a slider can live for many years as a household pet.
Some local farmers raise sliders commercially for the pet trade.

2. NOUN

/ ˈslaɪdɚ / (AmE), / ˈslaɪdə / (BrE)
A type of pitch in baseball that is thrown fast and breaks slightly away from the pitcher's throwing hand side.
baseball pitch with slight lateral break

Examples

The pitcher threw a nasty slider that left the batter frozen at the plate.
His slider breaks late, making it difficult for hitters to track.
She worked on perfecting her slider during the off-season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curveball - a pitch in baseball that curves as it approaches the batter

3. NOUN

/ ˈslaɪdər / (AmE), / ˈslaɪdə / (BrE)
a person who competes in luge races, sliding down an icy track on a small sled while lying supine.
luge competitor

Examples

The Olympic slider prepared for his final run down the dangerous ice track.
Fans cheered as the young slider set a new speed record in the luge event.
Every slider must combine courage and skill to navigate sharp turns at high speeds.
