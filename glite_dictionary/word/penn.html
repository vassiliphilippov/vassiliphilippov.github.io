Penn

1. PROPN

/ pɛn / (AmE), / pɛn / (BrE)
an informal name for the University of Pennsylvania, a prestigious university in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
informal name for University of Pennsylvania

Examples

My cousin was accepted to Penn and plans to study biology there.
The researchers at Penn have published groundbreaking work in neuroscience.
She wears a sweatshirt with Penn written across the front.

Etymology

Shortened form of 'University of Pennsylvania'.

2. PROPN

/ pɛn / (AmE), / pɛn / (BrE)
(William Penn) An Englishman and Quaker who founded the American colony of Pennsylvania in 1681.
English founder of Pennsylvania (1644–1718)

Examples

Penn negotiated peaceful treaties with the Native Americans after founding Pennsylvania.
The ideals of religious freedom promoted by Penn influenced the development of American democracy.
Penn's legacy is still celebrated throughout the state that bears his name.
