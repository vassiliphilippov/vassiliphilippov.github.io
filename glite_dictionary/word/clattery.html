clattery

1. ADJ

/ ˈklæt̬.ɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈklæt.ər.i / (BrE)
making a continuous rattling or clattering sound, typically as hard objects strike together
making a rattling or clattering noise

Examples

The old factory was filled with clattery machines that echoed throughout the building.
We were awakened by the clattery noise of dishes being washed in the kitchen.
Her clattery heels echoed loudly as she hurried down the tiled hallway.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: quiet - making little or no noise

Etymology
