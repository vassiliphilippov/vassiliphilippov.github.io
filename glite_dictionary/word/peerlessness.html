peerlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪrləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈpɪə.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being without equal; having no rival or match.
the state of having no equal

Examples

The peerlessness of her artistic talent left audiences in awe.
Historians often write about the peerlessness of certain ancient civilizations in architecture.
He took pride in the peerlessness of his craftsmanship, ensuring every detail was perfect.

Synset Examples

The negative opinions that the tenor received in the roles that turned him into a legend might come as a surprise, in view of all the claims of his matchlessness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: matchlessness - the state of being without equal or rival
  • Synonym: incomparability - the quality of being beyond comparison
  • Antonym: mediocrity - the quality of being average or unexceptional

Etymology

From 'peerless' + '-ness'; 'peerless' from 'peer' (equal) + '-less'.
