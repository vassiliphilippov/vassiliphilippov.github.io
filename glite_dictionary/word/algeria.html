Algeria

1. PROPN

/ ælˈdʒɪriə / (AmE), / ælˈdʒɪəriə / (BrE)
A country in northwestern Africa on the Mediterranean Sea, having Algiers as its capital.
country in northwestern Africa

Examples

Many tourists visit Algeria to explore its ancient Roman ruins and vibrant markets.
Following independence in 1962, Algeria experienced major social and economic changes.
My friend moved to Algeria for a year to work as a teacher in Algiers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Algiers - capital city of Algeria

Etymology

From French Algérie, ultimately from the name of the capital, Algiers.
