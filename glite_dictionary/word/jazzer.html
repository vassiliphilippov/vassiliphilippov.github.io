jazzer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒæzər / (AmE), / ˈdʒæzə / (BrE)
a person who is strongly enthusiastic about jazz music
enthusiastic jazz fan

Examples

My uncle is a true jazzer; he owns hundreds of classic vinyl records.
If you're a jazzer, you'll love the local club's weekly jam sessions.
The new documentary captures what it means to be a dedicated jazzer in today's music scene.

Synset Examples

He's a real jazzer, always finding the best tunes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jazz fan - someone who enjoys jazz music
  • Synonym: jazz musician - a person who plays jazz music

Etymology

Formed from 'jazz' + '-er', denoting a person associated with jazz.
