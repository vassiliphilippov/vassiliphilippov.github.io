egg-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ɛɡ ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ɛɡ ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a rounded shape similar to that of an egg; oval.
having the shape of an egg; oval

Examples

The architect designed an egg-shaped building that attracted much attention.
She cradled the delicate, egg-shaped stone in her hands.
The fruit has an egg-shaped form and a smooth, glossy skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oval - having a rounded, slightly elongated circular shape
