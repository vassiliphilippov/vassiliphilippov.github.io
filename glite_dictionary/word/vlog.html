vlog

1. NOUN

/ vlɑg / (AmE), / vlɒg / (BrE)
A video recording that is posted online, often featuring regular commentary or personal content by an individual or group.
a video blog posted online

Examples

She uploads a new vlog every week to share her travels with her audience.
Many teens watch their favorite vlog while doing homework.
His cooking vlog quickly gained thousands of subscribers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blog - A regularly updated website or web page, typically run by an individual or small group, in an informal or conversational style.

Etymology

Blend of 'video' and 'blog'.

3. NOUN

/ vlɑg / (AmE), / vlɒg / (BrE)
a video blog; a regular video recording posted online, typically featuring a person's daily activities or thoughts
video blog; online video diary

Examples

She uploaded a new vlog every week to share her travel experiences.
Many people find it easier to connect with their audience through a vlog rather than written posts.
His food vlog quickly gained thousands of subscribers interested in his unique recipes.
