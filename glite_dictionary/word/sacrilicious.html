sacrilicious

1. ADJ

/ ˌsæk.rɪˈlɪʃ.əs / (AmE), / ˌsak.rɪˈlɪʃ.əs / (BrE)
(informal) Extremely delicious or indulgently tasty, often to the point that it feels almost sinful or taboo.
extremely delicious or indulgent (informal)
informal

Examples

That chocolate cake was so sacrilicious I felt guilty eating it.
The chef's new burger is a sacrilicious treat for anyone who loves rich flavors.
People come from all over the city to taste his sacrilicious desserts.

Synset Examples

That new restaurant is known for its sacrilicious dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delicious - highly pleasing to the taste

Etymology

Blend of 'sacrilegious' and 'delicious'.
