integrating

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.təˌɡreɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tɪ.ɡreɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of incorporating people from different racial or religious groups into a community, organization, or society
incorporating minority groups into a community

Examples

The school district worked tirelessly on integrating students from different backgrounds.
Laws promoting integrating helped break down barriers in many communities.
Successful integrating requires both policy changes and shifts in public attitudes.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: segregating - the action of separating groups, especially by race or religion
  • Synonym: integration - the act or process of becoming part of a group or society and being treated the same as others
