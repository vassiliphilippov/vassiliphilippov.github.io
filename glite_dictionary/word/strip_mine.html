to strip mine

1. VERB

/ strɪp maɪn / (AmE), / strɪp maɪn / (BrE)
to extract ore or minerals by removing large strips of soil and rock (overburden) from the surface, rather than tunneling underground.
extract minerals by surface removal

Examples

The company plans to strip mine the hillside for coal over the next five years.
Environmentalists protested the decision to strip mine the once-pristine forest.
If we continue to strip mine these regions, the local ecosystem could be destroyed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quarry - to extract stone or minerals from an open excavation
