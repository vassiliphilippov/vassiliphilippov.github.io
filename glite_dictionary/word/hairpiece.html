hairpiece

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛrˌpis / (AmE), / ˈheəpiːs / (BrE)
an artificial covering of human or synthetic hair worn to disguise baldness or for decorative purposes
artificial hair covering

Examples

He wore a hairpiece to cover his receding hairline at the party.
The actor used a special hairpiece for his role in the historical drama.
Many people can't even tell that she has a hairpiece on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wig - a covering of artificial hair for the head
