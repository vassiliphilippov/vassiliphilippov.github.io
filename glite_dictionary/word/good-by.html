good-by

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊdˈbaɪ / (AmE), / ɡʊdˈbaɪ / (BrE)
an expression or act of saying farewell; something said to someone when parting
parting or farewell remark

Examples

She waved and whispered a soft good-by as the train pulled away.
After many heartfelt good-bys, the guests finally left the party.
He struggled to say good-by before leaving for the airport.

Synset Examples

they said their good-byes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farewell - an expression of good wishes at parting
  • Antonym: hello - a greeting or expression used when meeting

Etymology

From 'God be with ye', evolved through contraction in early Modern English.
