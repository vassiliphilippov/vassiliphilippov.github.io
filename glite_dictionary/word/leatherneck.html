leatherneck

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛðɚˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈlɛðəˌnɛk / (BrE)
a member of the United States Marine Corps, especially as a nickname
U.S. Marine Corps member (nickname)

Examples

As a proud leatherneck, he wore his uniform with honor.
The film tells the story of a young leatherneck serving overseas.
Several retired leathernecks attended the Marine Corps birthday celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Marine - a member of the United States Marine Corps

Etymology

Originally referring to the leather collar once part of the U.S. Marines' uniform.
