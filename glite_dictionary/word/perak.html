Perak

1. PROPN

/ pəˈrɑk / (AmE), / pɛˈrɑːk / (BrE)
a state in northwestern Malaysia, known as one of the 13 states of the country and formerly a sultanate
state of northwestern Malaysia

Examples

My friend is originally from Perak, and she often talks about its beautiful limestone hills.
The sultan of Perak plays an important role in the state's cultural traditions.
Tourists frequently visit Perak to explore its caves and heritage towns.

Etymology

From Malay 'perak,' meaning 'silver', possibly referring to the state's historical tin and silver resources.

2. PROPN

/ ˈpɛrɑk / (AmE), / ˈpɛrɑːk / (BrE)
A sultanate and one of the 13 states that make up the Federation of Malaysia, located on the western coast of Peninsular Malaysia.
state in Malaysia; sultanate

Examples

Perak is known for its tin mining history and beautiful limestone caves.
The capital city of Perak is Ipoh, famous for its cuisine and colonial architecture.
During our trip to Malaysia, we visited Perak to explore its stunning natural landscapes.
