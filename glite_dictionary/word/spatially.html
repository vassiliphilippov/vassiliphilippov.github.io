spatially

1. ADV

/ ˈspeɪ.ʃə.li / (AmE), / ˈspeɪ.ʃəl.i / (BrE)
in relation to space or the arrangement of things in space
in relation to space

Examples

The houses were spatially arranged in a perfect circle around the park.
Objects in the image are spatially distributed across different areas.
The study examines how memories are spatially organized in the brain.

Synset Examples

spatially limited

Related Terms

  • Antonym: temporally - in relation to time

Etymology

From spatial + -ly, where spatial derives from Medieval Latin spatialis ('relating to space').
