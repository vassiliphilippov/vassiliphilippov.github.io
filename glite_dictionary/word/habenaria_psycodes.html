Habenaria psycodes

1. NOUN

/ hæbəˈnɛəriə saɪˈkoʊdiːz / (AmE), / hæbəˈnɛːrɪə saɪˈkəʊdiːz / (BrE)
A North American orchid bearing clusters of fragrant, purple, fringed flowers.
a North American fringed orchid species

Examples

Botanists discovered a rare specimen of Habenaria psycodes near the edge of the woodland.
The fragrant purple flowers of Habenaria psycodes bloom in mid-summer across the northern United States.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitats where Habenaria psycodes naturally thrives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purple fringed orchid - common name for Habenaria psycodes

Etymology

From New Latin: genus name 'Habenaria' from Latin 'habena' (rein or strap), and species name 'psycodes' from Greek 'psyche' (butterfly), referring to flower shape.
