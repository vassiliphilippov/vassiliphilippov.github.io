Malti

1. NOUN

/ ˈmælti / (AmE), / ˈmalti / (BrE)
the Maltese language, which is the national language of Malta, derived from Arabic with significant influences from Italian, Spanish, and Norman-French
the Maltese language

Examples

Malti is recognized as one of the official languages of the European Union.
She studied Malti to better understand Maltese heritage and history.
Many Maltese people are fluent in both Malti and English.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Maltese - the language of Malta

Etymology

From Maltese 'Malti', meaning 'Maltese.'
