Dubyuh

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌb.ju / (AmE), / ˈdʌb.ju / (BrE)
An informal nickname for George W. Bush, the 43rd President of the United States.
nickname for George W. Bush
informal american

Examples

Many comedians referred to President Bush as Dubyuh during his time in office.
The satirical article poked fun at Dubyuh's latest press conference.
Some voters felt a strong connection to Dubyuh because of his folksy manner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: George W. Bush - 43rd President of the United States; son of George H. W. Bush

Etymology

Represents the Southern-accented pronunciation of the letter 'W', referencing George W. Bush's middle initial.
