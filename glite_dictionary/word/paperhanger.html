paperhanger

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪ.pɚˌhæŋ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ.pəˌhæŋ.ə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to put wallpaper on the interior walls of buildings
person who puts up wallpaper

Examples

The paperhanger arrived early to begin covering the living room walls.
She hired a skilled paperhanger to redecorate her home office.
It's best to let a professional paperhanger handle intricate wallpaper patterns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decorator - person who decorates the interior of buildings

Etymology

From 'paper' + 'hanger', describing one who hangs paper (wallpaper) on walls.

2. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpərˌhæŋər / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəhæŋə / (BrE)
a person who passes bad checks or counterfeit paper money
person who passes fake checks or money

Examples

The police arrested the paperhanger after he tried to cash a forged check at the bank.
She became known as a skilled paperhanger in the city’s underground criminal circles.
Authorities warned businesses to be on the lookout for a paperhanger circulating counterfeit twenties.
