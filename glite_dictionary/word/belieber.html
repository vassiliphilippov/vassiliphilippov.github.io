belieber

1. NOUN

/ bəˈliːbər / (AmE), / bɪˈliːbə / (BrE)
a fan of the pop singer Justin Bieber, especially one who is devoted and enthusiastic
fan of Justin Bieber
slang

Examples

She has been a proud belieber since Justin Bieber released his first single.
The concert was packed with screaming beliebers holding up signs for Justin.
He became a belieber after hearing Justin Bieber's music on the radio.

Etymology

Blend of 'Bieber' (Justin Bieber's surname) and 'believer'.
