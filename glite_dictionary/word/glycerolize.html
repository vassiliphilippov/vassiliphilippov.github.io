to glycerolize

1. VERB

/ ˈɡlɪsəˌrɑːlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪsərəlaɪz / (BrE)
to treat with or place in glycerol, especially for preservation or processing purposes
treat with or place in glycerol

Examples

The scientist decided to glycerolize the samples to prevent them from drying out.
Tissue cultures must be glycerolized before long-term storage at low temperatures.
Before analysis, they glycerolized the enzyme solution to stabilize its activity.

Etymology

From 'glycerol' + '-ize', forming a verb meaning to apply glycerol to something.
