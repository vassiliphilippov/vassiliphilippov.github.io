godmoder

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑdˌmoʊdər / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌməʊdə / (BrE)
A moderator in an online chat or gaming environment with special authority or control over participants.
chat or game moderator with extra authority
informal slang

Examples

The godmoder issued a warning to users who broke the chat rules.
When spam flooded the forum, the godmoder quickly took action.
Many respected the godmoder for keeping the chat civil and organized.

Synset Examples

The godmoder intervened when arguments erupted in the chat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moderator - person who oversees an online discussion or chat
  • Synonym: administrator - person with control over an online system or forum

Etymology

Formed from "god mode" (invincibility in games) and "moderator".
