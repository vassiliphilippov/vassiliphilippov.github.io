groundless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡraʊndləs / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊndləs / (BrE)
Not based on any good reason or facts; having no foundation in reality.
without a basis in fact or reason

Examples

There were groundless rumors about his resignation circulating through the office.
Her fears turned out to be completely groundless after she saw the evidence.
He made a groundless accusation that upset everyone in the room.

Synset Examples

baseless gossip
the allegations proved groundless
idle fears
unfounded suspicions
unwarranted jealousy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baseless - without foundation in fact, reason, or justification
  • Synonym: unfounded - not based on fact or evidence
  • Antonym: justified - having a good reason or basis

Etymology

Formed from 'ground' (reason, basis) + '-less' (without).
