Laurence Olivier

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɔːrəns oʊˈlɪvi.eɪ / (AmE), / ˈlɒrəns ɒˈlɪvi.eɪ / (BrE)
(1907–1989) An English actor and director, acclaimed for his performances in Shakespearean and other classic roles.
English actor and director (1907–1989)

Examples

Laurence Olivier is widely regarded as one of the greatest Shakespearean actors of the 20th century.
The film adaptation of "Hamlet" directed by Laurence Olivier won multiple Academy Awards.
Many critics believe that Laurence Olivier's interpretation of Othello set a new standard for stage performance.
