trunks

1. NOUN

/ trʌŋks / (AmE), / trʌŋks / (BrE)
short trousers ending at or above the knee, often worn for swimming, boxing, or athletic activities
short knee-length trousers, e.g. for swimming

Examples

He quickly changed into his trunks before jumping into the pool.
Boxers typically wear colorful trunks during matches.
His swimming trunks were hanging up to dry on the balcony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shorts - short trousers covering the upper part of the leg, usually casual wear
