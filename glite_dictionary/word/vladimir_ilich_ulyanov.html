Vladimir Ilich Ulyanov

1. PROPN

/ vləˈdɪmɪr ˈiːlɪtʃ juːlˈjænɒf / (AmE), / ˈvlædɪmɪə ˈiːlɪtʃ uːlˈjænɒf / (BrE)
(1870–1924) The Russian revolutionary known as Lenin, founder of the Bolshevik Party, leader of the Russian Revolution, and the first head of the USSR.
Russian revolutionary and first head of the USSR

Examples

The leadership of Vladimir Ilich Ulyanov profoundly changed the course of Russian and world history.
Few political figures have had as lasting an impact as Vladimir Ilich Ulyanov.
In 1917, Vladimir Ilich Ulyanov led the Bolsheviks during the October Revolution.
