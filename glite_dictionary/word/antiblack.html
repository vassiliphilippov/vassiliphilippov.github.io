antiblack

1. ADJ

/ ˌæntiˈblæk / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈblæk / (BrE)
prejudiced against or opposed to Black people; showing discrimination based on anti-Black bias
prejudiced against Black people

Examples

The report documented numerous instances of antiblack attitudes within the organization.
Many activists spoke out against the antiblack policies that persisted in the city.
Educational initiatives aim to confront and dismantle antiblack prejudice in society.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racist - showing or motivated by racial prejudice or discrimination
  • Antonym: pro-Black - supportive of Black people or their rights; the opposite perspective

Etymology

anti- (against) + black (referring to Black people); formed as an English compound.
