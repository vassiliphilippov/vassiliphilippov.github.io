tabooli

1. NOUN

/ təˈbuːli / (AmE), / təˈbuːli / (BrE)
A Middle Eastern salad made with finely chopped tomatoes, parsley, mint, scallions, and bulgur wheat, often dressed with olive oil and lemon juice.
Middle Eastern salad with bulgur and herbs

Examples

She served a bowl of tabooli alongside grilled chicken at the picnic.
The restaurant's tabooli was fresh and full of flavor.
I learned how to prepare tabooli from my grandmother.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tabbouleh - A similar Middle Eastern salad with bulgur, herbs, and tomatoes

Etymology

From Arabic *tabbūla* (the name of the dish).
