yes

1. NOUN

/ jɛs / (AmE), / jɛs / (BrE)
an affirmative answer or decision; the act of agreeing or consenting
affirmative answer; consent

Examples

Her application was met with a resounding yes from the committee.
He waited anxiously for her yes to his proposal.
The motion was carried with far more yes votes than no votes.

Synset Examples

I was hoping for a yes

Related Terms

  • Antonym: no - a negative answer or decision

Etymology

From Old English 'gēse' (so be it, yes).
