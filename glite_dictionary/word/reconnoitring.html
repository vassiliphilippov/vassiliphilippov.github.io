reconnoitring

1. NOUN

/ rɪˌkɑːnəˈtɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˌkɒnɪˈtɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of exploring or surveying an area, especially to gather military information
exploring to gather information

Examples

The soldiers spent hours reconnoitring the forest before moving into enemy territory.
Accurate maps were produced after days of reconnoitring the surrounding hills.
Their mission involved reconnoitring the coastline for any signs of hostile activity.

Synset Examples

scouting in enemy territory is very dangerous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scouting - the act of exploring or collecting information, especially in a military context

Etymology

From French 'reconnaître' (to recognize, to survey), based on Latin 'recognoscere'.
