finalist

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪ.nə.lɪst / (AmE), / ˈfaɪ.nə.lɪst / (BrE)
a person or team that has reached the final stage of a competition.
person or team in final stage of competition

Examples

She was thrilled to be named a finalist in the national art competition.
All the finalists will perform on stage tomorrow evening.
He became the youngest finalist in the show's history.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: winner - the contestant who wins the final stage
  • Degree Lower: contestant - a person who takes part in a competition

Etymology

From 'final' + '-ist', forming a noun meaning someone involved in the final round.
