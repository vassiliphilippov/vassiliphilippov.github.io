frizzly

1. ADJ

/ ˈfrɪzli / (AmE), / ˈfrɪzli / (BrE)
(of hair) having small, tight curls; frizzy or wiry in texture.
hair in small tight curls

Examples

Her frizzly hair was always difficult to comb in the mornings.
He admired her frizzly curls shining in the sunlight.
The wind made his frizzly locks even wilder than usual.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frizzy - (of hair) forming tight curls or being tangled
