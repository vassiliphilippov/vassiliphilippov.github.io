herpetologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɝː.pəˈtɑː.lə.dʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌhɜː.pɪˈtɒl.ə.dʒɪst / (BrE)
A scientist who studies reptiles and amphibians.
zoologist studying reptiles and amphibians

Examples

After years of research, the herpetologist discovered a new species of frog in the rainforest.
The documentary featured a herpetologist handling venomous snakes with great care.
Many herpetologists work in museums, zoos, or universities studying reptile and amphibian behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zoologist - a scientist who studies animals

Etymology

From Greek herpeton (creeping animal) + -logist (one who studies).
