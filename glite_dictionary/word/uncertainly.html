uncertainly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈsɝːtənli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɜːtənli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows a lack of confidence or certainty
in an uncertain way

Examples

She looked around uncertainly before deciding which path to take.
He spoke uncertainly, unsure if his answer was correct.
The child walked uncertainly toward the edge of the pool.

Synset Examples

he paused uncertainly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: confidently - in a self-assured or certain manner

Etymology

From 'uncertain' + '-ly', where 'uncertain' is from Latin 'incertus' (not sure).
