webcam

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛbˌkæm / (AmE), / ˈwɛb.kæm / (BrE)
A digital video camera that sends images or videos to a computer or over the internet, typically for live streaming or video calls.
internet-connected digital video camera

Examples

I set up my webcam for the video interview.
Many laptops now come with a built-in webcam.
She talked to her family overseas using a webcam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camera - A device for recording visual images

Etymology

Blend of 'web' (the internet) and 'camera'.
