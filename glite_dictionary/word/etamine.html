etamine

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛtəˌmiːn / (AmE), / ˈɛt.ə.miːn / (BrE)
A soft, loosely woven cotton or worsted fabric with an open mesh, used for curtains, clothing, and similar items.
soft open-mesh fabric

Examples

The dress was made of lightweight etamine, perfect for summer.
She chose white etamine curtains to let in plenty of sunlight.
Fine etamine is often used for embroidery backgrounds.

Etymology

From French *étamine*, meaning 'sieve', referring to its open mesh.
