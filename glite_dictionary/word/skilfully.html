skilfully

1. ADV

/ ˈskɪlfəli / (AmE), / ˈskɪlfəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows skill or expertise
in a skillful or expert way
british

Examples

The artist skilfully blended colors to create a beautiful landscape.
She skilfully negotiated with both parties to reach a fair agreement.
The magician skilfully performed a series of tricks that amazed the audience.

Synset Examples

fragments of a nearly complete jug, skillfully restored at the institute of archaeology

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skillfully - in a way that shows skill or expertise (American spelling)

Etymology

From 'skillful' + '-ly', chiefly British English spelling of 'skillfully'.
