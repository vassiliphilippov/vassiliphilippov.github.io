arcane

1. ADJ

/ ɑrˈkeɪn / (AmE), / ɑːˈkeɪn / (BrE)
understood by few; mysterious or secret
mysterious or secret; known by few

Examples

The professor's lecture on quantum physics included several arcane theories unfamiliar to most students.
She was fascinated by the arcane rituals described in the ancient manuscript.
Only members of the secret society understood the arcane symbols carved into the wall.

Synset Examples

the arcane science of dowsing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mysterious - difficult or impossible to understand, explain, or identify
  • Synonym: esoteric - intended for or likely to be understood by only a small number of people
  • Antonym: obvious - easily perceived or understood; clear, self-evident

Etymology

From Latin arcānus (secret, hidden), from arca (box, chest).
