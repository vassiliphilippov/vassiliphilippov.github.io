Maurois

1. PROPN

/ moʊˈrwɑː / (AmE), / ˈmɔːrwɑː / (BrE)
André Maurois (1885–1967), French writer best known for his biographies and historical works.
French biographer and writer (1885–1967)

Examples

Many students read Maurois's biography of Disraeli in their literature classes.
The literary style of Maurois blends narrative flair with historical accuracy.
Critics praised Maurois for making historical figures accessible to a wide audience.

Etymology

Pen name of Émile Salomon Wilhelm Herzog.
