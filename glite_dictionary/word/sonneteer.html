sonneteer

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɑː.nəˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˌsɒ.nɪˈtɪə / (BrE)
a poet who composes sonnets
poet who writes sonnets

Examples

As a dedicated sonneteer, she published a new collection each year.
The Elizabethan era produced many gifted sonneteers whose works are still studied today.
Though he was known as a sonneteer, his talents extended to other poetic forms as well.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poet - a person who writes poems

Etymology

From sonnet + -eer, modeled after similar agent nouns like pamphleteer.
