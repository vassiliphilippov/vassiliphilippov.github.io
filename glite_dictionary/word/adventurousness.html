adventurousness

1. NOUN

/ ədˈvɛntʃərəsnəs / (AmE), / ədˈvɛntʃərəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being willing to take risks and try new or exciting experiences
willingness to take risks or try new things

Examples

Her adventurousness led her to travel alone through South America.
He admired the adventurousness of her fashion choices.
The company's success was due to the adventurousness of its founders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adventurous - willing to take risks or try new things

Etymology

From 'adventurous' + '-ness', with 'adventurous' derived from 'adventure' (from Old French 'aventure').
