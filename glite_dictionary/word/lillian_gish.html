Lillian Gish

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪliən ˈɡɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈlɪliən ˈɡɪʃ / (BrE)
(1896–1993) A prominent American film actress known for her roles in early silent films, especially those directed by D. W. Griffith.
American silent film actress (1896–1993)

Examples

Lillian Gish was celebrated as one of the greatest actresses of the silent film era.
D. W. Griffith frequently cast Lillian Gish in leading roles in his movies.
Film historians often credit Lillian Gish with influencing the development of screen acting.
