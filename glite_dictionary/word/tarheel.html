Tarheel

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑrˌhil / (AmE), / ˈtɑːˌhiːl / (BrE)
a native or resident of North Carolina, US, especially used informally or as a nickname
a person from North Carolina, US

Examples

She proudly introduced herself as a Tarheel when she moved to California.
The university's basketball team is known as the Tarheels.
His accent immediately marked him as a Tarheel to other Southerners.

Etymology

Originally a nickname for North Carolinians, from the state's history in the tar trade.
