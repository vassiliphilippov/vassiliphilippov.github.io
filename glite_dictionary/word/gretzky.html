Gretzky

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡrɛtski / (AmE), / ˈɡrɛtski / (BrE)
Wayne Gretzky (born 1961), a Canadian ice hockey player widely regarded as one of the greatest in the history of the sport.
Canadian ice hockey player; all-time great

Examples

Gretzky holds the record for the most goals scored in NHL history.
Many young hockey players grow up idolizing Gretzky and his legendary skills.
In 1999, Gretzky retired from professional hockey, leaving a lasting legacy on the sport.
