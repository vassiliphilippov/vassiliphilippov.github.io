ejaculation

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌdʒækjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌdʒækjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of discharging semen from the male reproductive system, typically during orgasm
discharge of semen in males
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that ejaculation is a normal physiological process in adult males.
Premature ejaculation can be a source of anxiety for some men.
During sexual arousal, ejaculation usually occurs as part of the orgasmic response.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orgasm - the climax of sexual excitement

Etymology

From Latin ejaculatio (a throwing out), from ejaculari (to throw out).

2. NOUN

/ ɪˌdʒækjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌdʒækjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
an abrupt or sudden exclamation, especially one expressing strong emotion
sudden exclamation of emotion
archaic

Examples

At the surprising news, he uttered an involuntary ejaculation of joy.
Her sudden ejaculation startled everyone in the quiet room.
The character's frequent ejaculations reveal his passionate temperament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exclamation - a sudden cry or remark showing strong emotion

Etymology

From Latin *eiaculatio*, from *eiaculari* (to throw out).
