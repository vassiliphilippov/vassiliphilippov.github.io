Garcinia mangostana

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑrˈsɪniə ˌmæŋɡəˈstoʊnə / (AmE), / ɡɑːˈsɪnɪə ˌmæŋɡəˈstəʊnə / (BrE)
A tropical tree native to Southeast Asia, known for its thick, leathery leaves and fleshy, edible fruit called mangosteen.
tropical mangosteen tree

Examples

The Garcinia mangostana is widely cultivated for its delicious mangosteen fruit.
Botanists are researching the medicinal properties of Garcinia mangostana.
In tropical gardens, Garcinia mangostana adds both beauty and economic value.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mangosteen - the fruit produced by Garcinia mangostana

Etymology

From modern Latin, combining genus name Garcinia and species name mangostana, ultimately from Malay 'manggis.'
