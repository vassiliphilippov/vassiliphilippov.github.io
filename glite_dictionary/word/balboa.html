balboa

1. NOUN

/ bælˈboʊə / (AmE), / bælˈbəʊə / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Panama, equal to 100 centésimos
Panamanian currency unit

Examples

The price was listed as fifteen balboas at the market.
She exchanged her dollars for balboas upon arrival in Panama City.
He paid the taxi fare with a balboa coin.

Etymology

Named after Vasco Núñez de Balboa, a Spanish explorer.
