WMO

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʌbəl.juː ˌɛm ˈoʊ / (AmE), / ˌdʌbəl.juː ˌem ˈəʊ / (BrE)
abbreviation for World Meteorological Organization, the United Nations agency responsible for the international collection and exchange of meteorological data.
UN agency for meteorological data

Examples

The WMO coordinates weather observations across the globe.
According to the WMO, climate change is leading to more extreme weather events.
Meteorologists rely on standards set by the WMO for accurate data collection.

Etymology

Acronym from World Meteorological Organization.
