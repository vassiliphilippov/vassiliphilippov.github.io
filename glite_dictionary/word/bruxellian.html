Bruxellian

1. ADJ

/ bruːkˈsɛliən / (AmE), / bruːkˈsɛliən / (BrE)
Relating to the complex bureaucracy or characteristic style associated with Brussels, especially as it pertains to the European Union.
of EU bureaucracy or Brussels style

Examples

The committee's Bruxellian procedures slowed down the approval process considerably.
Her report was full of Bruxellian terminology that confused the smaller member states.
Journalists often complain about the Bruxellian approach to transparency and communication.

Synset Examples

The policy was criticized for its Bruxellian bureaucracy.
She navigated the Bruxellian maze of regulations with ease.
His Bruxellian style made him stand out in the meeting.

Etymology

Formed from 'Brussels', capital of Belgium and home of the EU, with the adjectival suffix '-ian'.
