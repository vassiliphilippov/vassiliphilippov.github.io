Disneyland

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɪzniˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈdɪzniˌlænd / (BrE)
A large amusement park in Anaheim, California, created in 1955 by Walt Disney and featuring themed lands and attractions based on Disney films and characters.
amusement park in Anaheim, California

Examples

Families from all over the world travel to Disneyland for its magical attractions.
The children were thrilled to meet their favorite characters at Disneyland.
Tickets to Disneyland often sell out during the holiday season.

Etymology

From "Disney" (Walt Disney, its creator) + "land".
