alopecic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæləˈpɛsɪk / (AmE), / ˌaləˈpɛsɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by the loss of hair, wool, or feathers
relating to hair or feather loss
medical

Examples

The doctor noted an alopecic patch on the patient's scalp during the examination.
Some birds may develop alopecic areas due to parasite infestations or nutritional deficiencies.
Researchers observed alopecic symptoms in several laboratory animals after they were exposed to the chemical.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bald - having no hair on the scalp

Etymology

From Greek alopekia (baldness), from alōpēx (fox, referencing mange in foxes).
