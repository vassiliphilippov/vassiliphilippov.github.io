forty-four

1. NUM

/ ˌfɔrti ˈfɔr / (AmE), / ˌfɔːti ˈfɔː / (BrE)
the number equal to four more than forty; 44
the number 44

Examples

There are forty-four students in the class this year.
He finished the race in forty-four minutes and ten seconds.
She celebrated her forty-fourth birthday with close friends and family.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty-three - the number 43, one less than forty-four
  • Degree Higher: forty-five - the number 45, one more than forty-four

2. NUM

/ ˌfɔrtiˈfɔr / (AmE), / ˌfɔːtiˈfɔː / (BrE)
equivalent to the number 44; being four more than forty
the number 44

Examples

There are forty-four students enrolled in the course this semester.
The room contains forty-four chairs, enough for the entire group.
She celebrated her forty-fourth birthday with a small gathering of friends.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty-three - the number 43
  • Degree Higher: forty-five - the number 45

Etymology

Formed from 'forty' (from Old English 'feowertig') plus 'four' (from Old English 'feower').
