Frostian

1. ADJ

/ ˈfrɑːstiən / (AmE), / ˈfrɒstiən / (BrE)
Characteristic of or relating to the style, themes, or manner of the poet Robert Frost.
of or relating to Robert Frost

Examples

The poem had a distinctly Frostian tone, evoking rural landscapes and quiet reflection.
Her Frostian writing style is marked by simple language and profound meaning.
Critics praise the Frostian themes of nature and everyday life in his latest collection.

Etymology

From the name of the American poet Robert Frost (1874–1963), plus suffix -ian.
