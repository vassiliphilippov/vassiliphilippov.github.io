evolutionary

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛvəˈluʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌiːvəˈluːʃənəri / (BrE)
relating to or resulting from the process of evolution
relating to evolution

Examples

Recent discoveries have shed light on the evolutionary history of birds.
The company's evolutionary approach to product design focuses on gradual improvement.
Darwin's theories had a profound impact on evolutionary biology.

Synset Examples

evolutionary biology

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evolution - the process by which different kinds of living organisms develop and diversify

Etymology

From evolution + -ary, after similar forms in Latin and French.
