schoolboyish

1. ADJ

/ ˈskuːlˌbɔɪɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈskuːlˌbɔɪɪʃ / (BrE)
having qualities or behavior typical of a young schoolboy, especially in being immature or mischievous
resembling a young schoolboy, esp. in behavior

Examples

His schoolboyish sense of humor made everyone feel at ease.
She rolled her eyes at his schoolboyish pranks during the meeting.
The actor brought a schoolboyish enthusiasm to the role that charmed audiences.

Synset Examples

a boyish grin
schoolboyish pranks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boyish - having characteristics typical of a boy

Etymology

From 'schoolboy' + '-ish'; 'schoolboy' from 'school' and 'boy'.
