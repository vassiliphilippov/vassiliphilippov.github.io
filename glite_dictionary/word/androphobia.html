androphobia

1. NOUN

/ ˌændrəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌandrəˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
A morbid or irrational fear of men.
morbid fear of men
medical

Examples

Her androphobia made it difficult for her to interact with male colleagues.
The patient was diagnosed with androphobia after therapy sessions revealed her intense anxiety around men.
Growing up in an all-female environment contributed to her androphobia.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: phobia - an extreme or irrational fear of or aversion to something
  • Antonym: gynophobia - a morbid fear of women

Etymology

From Greek 'anēr, andros' (man) + 'phobos' (fear).
