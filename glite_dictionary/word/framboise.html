framboise

1. NOUN

/ fræmˈbwɑz / (AmE), / frɑ̃ˈbwaːz / (BrE)
A variety of raspberry, or a liqueur made from raspberries, especially used in French cuisine.
raspberry or raspberry liqueur (French)

Examples

The dessert was topped with fresh framboise and a sprig of mint.
He enjoys a glass of framboise liqueur after dinner.
A tart made with framboise is one of my favorite French pastries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raspberry - a small red or black edible fruit

Etymology

From French 'framboise' meaning 'raspberry'.
