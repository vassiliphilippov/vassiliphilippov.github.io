pya

1. NOUN

/ pjɑ / (AmE), / pjɑː / (BrE)
a monetary unit of Myanmar, equal to one hundredth of a kyat.
Myanmar monetary unit

Examples

The price of the snack was only fifty pyas in the local market.
Coins of one pya are rarely used since their value is so low.
A hundred pyas make up one kyat in the Myanmar currency system.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: kyat - the basic monetary unit of Myanmar

Etymology

From Burmese ပိゃ (pya), referring to the currency unit.
