tendonitis

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛn.dəˈnaɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˌtɛn.dəˈnaɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of a tendon, usually causing pain and restricting movement
inflammation of a tendon
medical

Examples

He was diagnosed with tendonitis after complaining of persistent elbow pain.
Overusing your wrist while typing can lead to tendonitis.
Physical therapy is often recommended to treat tendonitis in athletes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tendinitis - alternative spelling for tendonitis

Etymology

From Latin 'tendo' (tendon) + Greek '-itis' (inflammation).
