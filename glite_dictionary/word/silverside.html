silverside

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvərˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəˌsaɪd / (BrE)
a small fish found along the Atlantic coast of the United States, characterized by a silver stripe along each side of its body
small fish with silver side stripe

Examples

Schools of silverside darted through the shallow water near the shore.
The fisherman caught several silverside in his net this morning.
We watched a silverside glinting in the sun as it swam past our boat.

Etymology

From 'silver' (the metallic color) + 'side' (referring to the stripe along its side).
