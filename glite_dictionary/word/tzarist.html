tzarist

1. ADJ

/ ˈzɑːrɪst / (AmE), / ˈzɑːrɪst / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a czar or the system of government ruled by a czar, especially in Russia before 1917.
relating to a czar or system of czarist rule

Examples

The tzarist regime fell during the Russian Revolution of 1917.
Many old palaces still bear the marks of tzarist architecture.
Under tzarist rule, many Russians lived in poverty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: czarist - relating to a czar or the system of czarist rule
  • Synonym: tsarist - relating to a tsar or the tsarist system of government

Etymology

From Russian 'tsar', ultimately from Latin 'caesar' (emperor).
