soundless

1. ADJ

/ ˈsaʊndləs / (AmE), / ˈsaʊndləs / (BrE)
completely silent; making no sound at all.
without any sound; silent

Examples

The owl glided through the air in soundless flight.
She opened the window with soundless care so as not to wake the baby.
The snow fell in a soundless blanket, muting the city below.

Synset Examples

a silent house
soundless footsteps on the grass
the night was still

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silent - not making or accompanied by any sound
  • Antonym: noisy - making a lot of noise

Etymology

From "sound" (noise) + "-less" (without).
