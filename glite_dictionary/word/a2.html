A2

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪ ˈtu / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˈtjuː / (BrE)
A standard paper size measuring 420 by 594 millimetres, as defined by ISO 216.
standard paper size (420 × 594 mm)

Examples

The poster was printed on A2 paper for better visibility.
Please submit your artwork on A2 sheets as specified in the competition rules.
We bought a pack of A2 drawing paper for the class project.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: A3 - a standard paper size (297 x 420 mm), one half of A2
  • Degree Higher: A1 - a standard paper size (594 x 841 mm), one step larger than A2

Etymology

From ISO 216 international paper size standard; 'A' series introduced in Germany in the 1920s.
