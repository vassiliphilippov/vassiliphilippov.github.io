soulful

1. ADJ

/ ˈsoʊl.fəl / (AmE), / ˈsəʊl.fəl / (BrE)
Full of or expressing deep feeling or emotion.
expressing deep emotion

Examples

Her soulful singing moved the entire audience to tears.
He gazed at her with soulful eyes, saying nothing but expressing everything.
The soulful melody brought back memories of his childhood.

Synset Examples

soulful eyes
soulful music

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emotional - showing strong emotion
  • Antonym: expressionless - showing no emotion

Etymology

Formed from 'soul' + '-ful' (full of).
