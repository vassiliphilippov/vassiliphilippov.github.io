Viña del Mar

1. PROPN

/ ˈvin.jɑ dɛl ˈmɑr / (AmE), / ˈviː.njə dɛl ˈmɑː / (BrE)
A resort city on the Pacific coast in central Chile, known for its beaches and gardens.
resort city in central Chile

Examples

Every summer, tourists flock to Viña del Mar for its beautiful beaches and lively atmosphere.
The annual music festival in Viña del Mar attracts artists from all over the world.
During our trip to Chile, we spent several days exploring Viña del Mar and its famous gardens.

Etymology

Spanish, meaning 'Vineyard of the Sea'.
