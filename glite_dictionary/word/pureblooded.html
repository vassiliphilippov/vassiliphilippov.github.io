pureblooded

1. ADJ

/ ˌpjʊrˈblʌdɪd / (AmE), / ˌpjʊəˈblʌdɪd / (BrE)
Having pure ancestry, especially of an animal, with documented lineage showing no mixing with other breeds.
of pure ancestry (animal breeding)

Examples

The kennel club only accepts pureblooded dogs into its breeding program.
Her horse is a pureblooded Arabian with an impeccable pedigree.
They take pride in raising pureblooded cattle for showing and breeding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purebred - an animal with pure ancestry
  • Antonym: crossbred - an animal resulting from the breeding of different breeds
