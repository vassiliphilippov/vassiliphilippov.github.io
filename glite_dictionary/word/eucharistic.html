Eucharistic

1. ADJ

/ juːkəˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / juːkəˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to the sacrament of the Eucharist, especially in Christian ceremonies involving Holy Communion
relating to the Eucharist (Christian sacrament)

Examples

The priest lifted the Eucharistic chalice during the service.
Many churches set aside Eucharistic bread before Mass.
The Eucharistic celebration is central to Catholic worship.

Synset Examples

the Eucharistic cup

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Eucharist - Christian sacrament commemorating the Last Supper

Etymology

From Greek 'eucharistikos' (thankful), from 'eucharistia' (gratitude).
