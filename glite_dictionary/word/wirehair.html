wirehair

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪərˌhɛr / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəhɛə / (BrE)
A dog, especially a terrier, with a coat of stiff, wiry hair.
dog with stiff, wiry hair

Examples

The breeder specializes in wirehair terriers for show competitions.
I adopted a charming wirehair from the animal shelter last year.
Her wirehair needs regular grooming to keep its coat healthy.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smooth-haired - having a smooth, glossy coat; the opposite of wire-haired in dogs

Etymology

From 'wire' + 'hair', originally describing the stiff, wire-like texture of the fur.
