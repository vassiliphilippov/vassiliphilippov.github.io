anime

1. NOUN

/ ˈænɪˌmeɪ / (AmE), / ˈænɪˌmeɪ / (BrE)
A hard, transparent resin (copal) obtained from certain African trees, used in varnishes and other products.
African tree resin (copal)

Examples

The varnish was made from high-quality anime collected in West Africa.
She imported anime to use in her restoration work on antique furniture.
Collectors prize rare specimens of anime for their clarity and natural color.

Etymology

From French 'animé', literally 'lively', applied to the resin for its bright quality.

2. NOUN

/ ˈænɪˌmi / (AmE), / ˈanɪˌmi / (BrE)
Any of various natural resins or oleoresins obtained from certain tropical trees, used in varnishes and lacquers.
a natural resin or oleoresin

Examples

The craftsman selected a type of anime to create a glossy finish on the wood.
For this restoration, we need a high-quality anime to mix into the polish.
Traditional varnishes often contain natural resins like anime or copal.

Etymology

From French 'anime' (resin), from Spanish 'anime', from Latin American Spanish, likely of Carib origin.

3. NOUN

/ ˈænɪˌmeɪ / (AmE), / ˈænɪmeɪ / (BrE)
a style of animation originating in Japan, characterized by colorful artwork and frequently featuring fantastical themes or stories, often aimed at both children and adults.
Japanese animated style or works

Examples

Fans of anime often attend conventions dressed as their favorite characters.
She enjoys watching Japanese anime because of its unique art style and storytelling.
His collection of anime DVDs spans several shelves in his room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: animation - the process or technique of making animated films
  • Synonym: cartoon - a film or TV program made using drawn or computerized animation

Etymology

From Japanese アニメ (anime), an abbreviation of アニメーション (animēshon, “animation”).
