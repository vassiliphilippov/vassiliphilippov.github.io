Ferdinand Victor Eugène Delacroix

1. PROPN

/ fɚˈdæn.dɪn vɪkˈtɔr yˈʒɛn dəˌlɑːˈkrwɑː / (AmE), / fɛəˈdæn.dɪn vɪkˈtɔː yˈʒɛn dəˌlæˈkrwɑː / (BrE)
(1798–1863) A French Romantic painter known for his expressive brushwork, dramatic use of color, and influential works such as 'Liberty Leading the People'.
French Romantic painter (1798–1863)

The painting style of Ferdinand Victor Eugène Delacroix greatly influenced the later Impressionists.
