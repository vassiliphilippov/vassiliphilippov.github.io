crackswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkræksˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈkraksˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A female thief who specializes in breaking open safes to steal valuable contents.
female safecracker

Examples

The notorious crackswoman eluded police for years before finally being captured.
Reports described the suspect as a skilled crackswoman operating in the city’s financial district.
She gained notoriety as a crackswoman after a string of daring bank heists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cracksman - a thief who breaks into safes or strongrooms to steal
  • Synonym: safecracker - a person who breaks open safes, usually to steal

Etymology

Formed from 'crack' (to break open) and 'woman,' patterned after 'cracksman.'
