abounding

1. ADJ

/ əˈbaʊndɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈbaʊndɪŋ / (BrE)
present in large quantities; existing in abundance
present in large quantities

Examples

The region is abounding in beautiful lakes and forests.
Her stories are abounding with vivid details and imagination.
Confidence was abounding among the team after their recent victory.

Synset Examples

abounding confidence
whiskey galore

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abundant - existing or available in large quantities
  • Antonym: scarce - in short supply or insufficient quantity

Etymology

Present participle of the verb 'abound', from Old French abonder, from Latin abundare (overflow).
