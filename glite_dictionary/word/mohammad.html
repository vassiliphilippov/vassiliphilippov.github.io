Mohammad

1. PROPN

/ moʊˈhɑːməd / (AmE), / məˈhɑːməd / (BrE)
the founder of Islam, regarded by Muslims as the last prophet and messenger of Allah (c. 570–632 CE)
the prophet and founder of Islam

Examples

Many Muslims around the world honor Mohammad as the final prophet of Islam.
The teachings of Mohammad are recorded in the Hadith and the Quran.
Mohammad's message emphasized compassion, justice, and faith in one God.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Muhammad - alternative transliteration for the Prophet of Islam

Etymology

From Arabic مُحَمَّد‎ (Muḥammad), meaning 'praised' or 'praiseworthy'.
