caterer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪtərər / (AmE), / ˈkeɪtərə / (BrE)
a person or company that provides food and drinks, typically for social or business events
someone who supplies food and drinks professionally

Examples

The caterer arrived early to set up the buffet for the wedding.
We hired a caterer to handle the food at our company's annual party.
As a professional caterer, she specializes in corporate events and private dinners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chef - a professional cook, especially the chief cook in a restaurant or hotel

Etymology

From 'cater', itself from Old French 'acater' (to buy) and Middle English 'catour'.
