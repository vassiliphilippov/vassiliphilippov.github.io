nutritive

1. ADJ

/ nuˈtrɪtɪv / (AmE), / njuˈtrɪtɪv / (BrE)
Providing nourishment; nourishing.
providing nourishment

Examples

This cereal is highly nutritive and suitable for a healthy breakfast.
They recommended a nutritive supplement for children with vitamin deficiencies.
The soup was not only delicious but also very nutritive.

Synset Examples

good nourishing stew

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nourishing - providing substances necessary for growth, health, and good condition
  • Synonym: nutritional - relating to the process of providing or obtaining food necessary for health and growth

Etymology

From Latin nutritivus (pertaining to nourishment).
