reliving

1. NOUN

/ riˈlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / riˈlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of experiencing something again, especially as a memory or emotion
the act of experiencing again

Examples

The reliving of trauma can be triggered by certain smells or sounds.
Through therapy, he found that the reliving of his childhood helped him heal.
Her novel captures the reliving of bittersweet memories with vivid detail.

Synset Examples

the reliving of a strong emotion can be therapeutic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recollection - the action of remembering something
  • Antonym: forgetting - the action of failing to remember

Etymology

From re- (again) + live; first used in 19th century.
