softhearted

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɔftˈhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˌsɒftˈhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
Easily affected by feelings of pity or compassion; having a kind and sympathetic nature.
kind and easily moved to pity

Examples

She is known as a softhearted person who always helps those in need.
The softhearted judge decided to give the young offender a second chance.
Despite his tough appearance, Mark proved to be surprisingly softhearted during sad movies.

Synset Examples

a softhearted judge

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kindhearted - having a kind and sympathetic nature
  • Antonym: hardhearted - lacking in sympathy or compassion

Etymology

Formed from 'soft' + 'hearted', meaning 'having a soft heart'.
