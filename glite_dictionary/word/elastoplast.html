Elastoplast

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlæstəˌplæst / (AmE), / ɪˈlæstəplɑːst / (BrE)
a type of elastic adhesive bandage used to cover and protect cuts or wounds
elastic adhesive bandage

Examples

She placed an Elastoplast over the cut to stop it from bleeding.
Do you have an Elastoplast for my scraped knee?
He replaced the old Elastoplast after his shower to keep the wound clean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bandage - a strip of material used to protect or support an injured part of the body
  • Synonym: plaster - a small piece of adhesive bandage for covering minor wounds

Etymology

From the trademark 'Elastoplast', first registered in 1928 by Beiersdorf AG, based on 'elastic' and 'plaster'.
