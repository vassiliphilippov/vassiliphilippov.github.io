micropylar

1. ADJ

/ ˌmaɪ.kroʊˈpaɪ.lɚ / (AmE), / ˌmʌɪ.krəˈpʌɪ.lɑː / (BrE)
relating to or situated at the micropyle, the small opening in the outer covering of an ovule or egg.
relating to a micropyle (small opening in egg/ovule)

Examples

The sperm enters the egg cell through the micropylar opening during fertilization.
Micropylar structures in seeds play a crucial role in the exchange of gases.
The orientation of the embryo is often determined by its position relative to the micropylar end.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: micropyle - small opening in the outer covering of an ovule or egg

Etymology

From micropyle + -ar (forming adjectives meaning 'of or pertaining to').
