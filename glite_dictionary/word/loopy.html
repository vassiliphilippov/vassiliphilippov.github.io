loopy

1. ADJ

/ ˈluːpi / (AmE), / ˈluːpi / (BrE)
informal; slightly crazy or eccentric; behaving in an odd or unpredictable way.
informal; eccentric or slightly crazy
informal

Examples

After working all night, I started feeling a bit loopy and couldn't focus.
Don't mind her; she's just acting loopy because she's excited about the trip.
His loopy sense of humor always makes us laugh.

Synset Examples

it used to drive my husband balmy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mad or mentally deranged
  • Antonym: sane - mentally healthy; reasonable
