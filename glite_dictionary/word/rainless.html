rainless

1. ADJ

/ ˈreɪnləs / (AmE), / ˈreɪnləs / (BrE)
(of a period or area) having no rain; experiencing a lack of rainfall.
having no rain

Examples

The farmers were worried after a rainless summer damaged their crops.
We went hiking during a rainless week and enjoyed clear, sunny days.
These regions are known for their rainless climate and harsh, arid conditions.

Synset Examples

a rainless month
rainless skies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dry - lacking moisture; not wet
  • Antonym: rainy - marked by or having rain

Etymology

From English 'rain' + suffix '-less' (meaning 'without').
