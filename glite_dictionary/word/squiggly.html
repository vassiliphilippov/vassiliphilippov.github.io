squiggly

1. ADJ

/ ˈskwɪɡli / (AmE), / ˈskwɪɡli / (BrE)
having a wavy, twisting, or curving form or appearance, like a squiggle
wavy or twisting in shape

Examples

He drew a squiggly line across the page.
The path looked squiggly as it wound through the hills.
She signed her name in squiggly handwriting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wavy - having a series of curves or undulations
  • Antonym: straight - not bending or curving; without a bend, angle, or curve

Etymology

From "squiggle" (irregular curving line) + -y (adjective suffix).
