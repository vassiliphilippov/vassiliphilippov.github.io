harmonised

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑːrmənaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈhɑːmənʌɪzd / (BrE)
involving or characterized by harmony, especially agreement or a pleasing arrangement of parts
characterized by harmony or agreement
british

Examples

The choir delivered a beautifully harmonised performance that moved the audience.
Their harmonised approach to problem-solving resulted in a smooth workflow.
The committee produced a harmonised set of guidelines to ensure consistency across departments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harmonized - characterized by harmony (American spelling)
  • Antonym: discordant - lacking harmony or agreement

Etymology

From harmony + -ise (British variant of -ize).
