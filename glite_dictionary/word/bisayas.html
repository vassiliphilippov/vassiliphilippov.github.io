Bisayas

1. PROPN

/ bɪˈsaɪəz / (AmE), / bɪˈsaɪəz / (BrE)
a group of islands in the central Philippines, forming one of the country's major regions
group of islands in central Philippines

Examples

The Bisayas are known for their beautiful beaches and vibrant festivals.
Tourists often travel to the Bisayas to visit islands like Cebu and Bohol.
Many Filipinos in the Bisayas speak several Visayan languages.
