hyson

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪsən / (AmE), / ˈhaɪsən / (BrE)
a variety of Chinese green tea characterized by thin, twisted leaves and a light, slightly sweet flavor
a type of Chinese green tea with twisted leaves

Examples

She prefers to drink hyson in the afternoon because of its delicate flavor.
The tea shop offers a wide selection, including oolong, jasmine, and hyson.
A pot of steaming hyson was placed on the table for the guests.

Etymology

From Chinese (Min Nan dialect) 'xi chūn' meaning 'flourishing spring'.
