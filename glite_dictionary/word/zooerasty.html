zooerasty

1. NOUN

/ zuːˈɛr.ə.sti / (AmE), / zuːˈɛr.ə.sti / (BrE)
sexual activity between a person and an animal
sexual activity with an animal
medical legal

Examples

Laws in many countries explicitly prohibit zooerasty due to concerns about animal welfare.
The medical text discussed rare psychological disorders such as zooerasty.
Historical records reveal that some ancient societies had specific penalties for acts of zooerasty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bestiality - sexual intercourse between a person and an animal

Etymology

From Greek 'zōion' (animal) + 'erasia' (sexual love).
