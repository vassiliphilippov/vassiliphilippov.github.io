ethical

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛθɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈɛθɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or concerned with the study of ethics or the principles of morality.
relating to the study of ethics

Examples

The professor gave a lecture on ethical theories in philosophy class.
Medical research must comply with strict ethical guidelines to protect participants.
She questioned the ethical implications of artificial intelligence.

Synset Examples

ethical codes
ethical theories

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moral - concerned with the principles of right and wrong behavior

Etymology

From Greek *ēthikos* (moral), from *ēthos* (custom, character).

/ ˈɛθɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈɛθɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or in accordance with moral principles; morally right or acceptable
morally right or principled

Examples

Doctors are bound by ethical standards in treating their patients.
