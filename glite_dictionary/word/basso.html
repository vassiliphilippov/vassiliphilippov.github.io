basso

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbæsəʊ / (BrE)
an adult male singer with the lowest vocal range, especially in opera or choral music.
male singer with lowest voice

Examples

The renowned opera featured a famous basso in the lead role.
As a basso, his deep voice resonated through the concert hall.
Our choir is searching for a new basso to balance the harmonies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bass - male voice or singer with the lowest vocal range
  • Degree Higher: baritone - adult male singing voice between bass and tenor
  • Degree Higher: tenor - adult male singing voice higher than baritone

Etymology

From Italian basso (low), from Latin bassus (thick, low).
