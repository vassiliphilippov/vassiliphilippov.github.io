biocatalytic

1. ADJ

/ ˌbaɪ.oʊˌkæt.əˈlɪt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əʊˌkæt.əˈlɪt.ɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or involving biocatalysts, especially the use of natural catalysts, such as enzymes, to accelerate chemical reactions.
relating to biocatalysts or their action
medical

Examples

The researchers observed a biocatalytic process that increased the reaction speed.
This laboratory focuses on developing biocatalytic methods for pharmaceutical synthesis.
Improvements in biocatalytic efficiency can reduce environmental impact in the chemical industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enzymatic - relating to enzymes or their action

Etymology

From bio- (relating to life) + catalytic (from Greek katalytikos, dissolving).
