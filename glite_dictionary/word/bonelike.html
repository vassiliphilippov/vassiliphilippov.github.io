bonelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈboʊnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbəʊnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having qualities similar to bone, especially in appearance, texture, or structure.
resembling bone

Examples

The mineral formed a bonelike layer around the implant.
The artist sculpted the material into a bonelike structure.
Doctors removed a bonelike mass from the patient's shoulder.

Synset Examples

a bonelike tumor

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: bone - the hard, dense connective tissue forming the skeleton of vertebrates
