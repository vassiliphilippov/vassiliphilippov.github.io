Jack Kerouac

1. PROPN

/ dʒæk ˈkɛr.u.æk / (AmE), / dʒæk ˈkɛr.u.æk / (BrE)
(1922–1969) An American writer and leading figure of the Beat Generation, known for books such as 'On the Road'.
American Beat Generation writer

Examples

Many consider Jack Kerouac to be the voice of a generation changed by postwar America.
The novel 'On the Road' cemented Jack Kerouac's status as an influential American writer.
Literature classes often discuss the impact of Jack Kerouac on modern prose styles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Allen Ginsberg - American poet and fellow Beat Generation writer
  • Synonym: On the Road - Novel by Jack Kerouac, central to Beat literature
