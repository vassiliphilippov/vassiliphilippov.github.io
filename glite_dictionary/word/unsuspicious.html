unsuspicious

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.səˈspɪʃ.əs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.səˈspɪʃ.əs / (BrE)
not having or showing suspicion; not inclined to suspect wrongdoing or danger.
not suspicious; unsuspecting

Examples

She appeared completely unsuspicious of their true intentions.
The package was left in an unsuspicious location near the entrance.
His unsuspicious nature made him an easy target for fraud.

Synset Examples

deceiving the unsuspecting public

Related Terms

  • Antonym: suspicious - having or showing a cautious distrust of someone or something
  • Synonym: unsuspecting - not aware of the presence of danger; not suspicious

Etymology

From un- (not) + suspicious.
