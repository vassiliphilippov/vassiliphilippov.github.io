Dragunov

1. NOUN

/ ˈdræɡjuˌnɔv / (AmE), / ˈdræɡjuːnɒf / (BrE)
a semi-automatic sniper rifle of Soviet design, typically equipped with a telescopic sight
Soviet semi-automatic sniper rifle

Examples

The soldier was trained to use the Dragunov for long-range engagements.
Collectors value the Dragunov for its historical significance and unique design.
He spotted the enemy through the scope of his Dragunov and prepared to take aim.

Etymology

Named after its designer, Soviet engineer Yevgeny Dragunov.
