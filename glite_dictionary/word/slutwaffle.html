slutwaffle

1. NOUN

/ ˈslʌtˌwɒfəl / (AmE), / ˈslʌtˌwɒf.əl / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) An insulting term for a person, typically implying indecisiveness or perceived promiscuity and foolishness.
slang insult for indecisive or foolish person
slang offensive

Examples

Don't be such a slutwaffle—just make up your mind already!
She called her friend a slutwaffle as a joke when he couldn't choose a restaurant.
Online forums sometimes use the word slutwaffle to mock indecisive users.

Synset Examples

He laughed when she called him a slutwaffle for being indecisive.

Etymology

Blend of 'slut' (a derogatory term for a promiscuous woman) and 'waffle' (to speak or act indecisively).
