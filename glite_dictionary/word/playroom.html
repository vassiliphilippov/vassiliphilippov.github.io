playroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌruːm / (BrE)
a room in a house or other building set aside for children to play in or for recreation and noisy activities
room for children's play or recreation

Examples

The children spent the afternoon playing in the playroom with their friends.
We converted the basement into a playroom for the kids to keep their toys and games.
After the party, the playroom was left in a complete mess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nursery - a room in a house set apart for young children
