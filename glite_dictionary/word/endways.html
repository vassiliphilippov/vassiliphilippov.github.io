endways

1. ADV

/ ˈɛndˌweɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛndˌweɪz / (BrE)
with the end forward or toward the observer; in a lengthwise direction
with the end forward; lengthwise

Examples

The logs were stacked endways to fit in the narrow truck bed.
Please place the loaf endways on the cutting board for easier slicing.
The houses in the village are built endways along the river.

Synset Examples

houses built endways

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endwise - with the end forward or in the direction of the end

2. ADV

/ ˈɛndˌweɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛndˌweɪz / (BrE)
In or toward the direction of the ends; lengthwise.
in the direction of the ends; lengthwise

Examples

The logs were stacked endways to make the most of the small space.
Please turn the beam endways so it fits through the doorway.
He applied pressure endways to test the strength of the rod.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lengthwise - in the direction of the length; along the length

3. ADV

/ ˈɛn(d)ˌweɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛn(d)weɪz / (BrE)
In a position with the end or ends up; on end or upright.
in an upright or end-up position

Examples

The books were stacked endways on the shelf to save space.
She placed the planks endways against the wall for painting.
The logs stood endways in the corner of the shed.

Synset Examples

sticks leaning against the wall endways

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endwise - in a position with the end or ends up; upright

Etymology

Formed from 'end' + 'ways', denoting the manner or direction related to the end.
