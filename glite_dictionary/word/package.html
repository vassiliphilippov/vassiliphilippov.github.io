package

1. VERB

/ ˈpækɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpækɪdʒ / (BrE)
to put something into a box, container, or wrapping, especially for storage or transportation
put into a box or container

Examples

We need to package the items carefully so they won't break during shipping.
The factory packages hundreds of products every day.
She packaged the cookies in small boxes to give as gifts.

Synset Examples

box the gift, please

Related Terms

  • Synonym: box - to put into a box
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01487784-v
Synset POS: v

2. NOUN

/ ˈpæk.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpæk.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
an object or group of objects wrapped in paper, cardboard, or other material, usually to be transported or delivered
wrapped container or parcel

Examples

The package was left on my doorstep while I was at work.
She carefully unwrapped the package to see what was inside.
We received a large package containing several smaller boxes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parcel - a wrapped bundle of items to be carried or sent by mail

Etymology

From Middle French *paquet*, diminutive of *pacque* (bundle, package).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03876761-n
Synset POS: n

3. NOUN

/ ˈpækɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpækɪdʒ / (BrE)
a collection of items wrapped or boxed together, typically for transportation or delivery
a collection of items wrapped together

Examples

The package arrived at my doorstep this morning.
She opened the package to find several carefully wrapped gifts inside.
Please make sure the package is securely sealed before shipping it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parcel - a wrapped bundle, especially for mailing
  • Synonym: bundle - a collection of things fastened together for carrying

Etymology

From Middle French paquet (bundle), from Old French pacquet, diminutive of pac (bundle, packet).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08024575-n
Synset POS: n

4. NOUN

/ ˈpækɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpækɪdʒ / (BrE)
(computing) a set of related programs, procedures, and documentation offered as a unit to perform specific tasks on a computer system.
set of software programs and documentation

Examples

Many developers use this package to process large data sets efficiently.
After installing the latest package, several new features became available in the application.
The IT department purchased a package that includes antivirus protection and backup tools.

Synset Examples

the market for software is expected to expand

Related Terms

  • Synonym: software - programs and other operating information used by a computer
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06578068-n
Synset POS: n
