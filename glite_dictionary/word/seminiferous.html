seminiferous

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛməˈnɪfərəs / (AmE), / ˌsɛmɪˈnɪfərəs / (BrE)
(of tissue or structures) bearing or producing seed or semen, especially referring to tubules in the testes.
producing seed or semen (biology)
medical

Examples

The seminiferous tubules are essential for sperm production.
Damage to the seminiferous epithelium can impact fertility.
Researchers examined the structure of seminiferous tissues under the microscope.

Synset Examples

seminiferous tubules

Etymology

From Latin 'seminifer' (seed-bearing), from 'semen' (seed) + 'ferre' (to bear).
