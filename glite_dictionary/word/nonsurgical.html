nonsurgical

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈsɜrdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɜːdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
not involving or requiring surgery; not surgical
not involving surgery

Examples

The doctor recommended a nonsurgical option for treating the injury.
Advances in nonsurgical cosmetic procedures have made them more popular than ever.
Her recovery was quicker because she chose a nonsurgical treatment.

Synset Examples

nonsurgical techniques

Related Terms

  • Antonym: surgical - relating to or used in surgery

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) + 'surgical' (relating to surgery).
