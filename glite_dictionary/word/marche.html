Marche

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑːrkeɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːʃeɪ / (BrE)
a region in central Italy, known for its rolling hills, Adriatic coastline, and capital city, Ancona.
region in central Italy

Examples

The Marche region is renowned for its picturesque landscapes and historic towns.
She spent a summer studying art history in Marche.
Many Italian wines from Marche are highly regarded for their unique flavors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ancona - capital city of Marche, Italy

Etymology

From Italian Marche, plural of Marca (march, border region), referring to its historical status as a border territory.
