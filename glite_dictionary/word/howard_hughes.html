Howard Hughes

1. PROPN

/ ˈhaʊ.ərd ˈhjuːz / (AmE), / ˈhaʊ.əd ˈhjuːz / (BrE)
(1905–1976) An American industrialist, aviator, and film producer known for his significant achievements in aviation and cinema, as well as for becoming a reclusive figure in his later years.
American industrialist, aviator, and film producer (1905–1976)

Examples

Howard Hughes was famous for breaking aviation records and pushing the limits of aircraft technology.
Many books and films have chronicled the mysterious later years of Howard Hughes.
The legacy of Howard Hughes still influences the aerospace industry today.
