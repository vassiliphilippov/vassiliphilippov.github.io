rupicolous

1. ADJ

/ ruːˈpɪkələs / (AmE), / ruːˈpɪkələs / (BrE)
(of a plant, animal, or inscription) living on, growing on, or inscribed on rocks.
growing/living on or inscribed on rocks

Examples

The rupicolous mosses thrived in the cracks of the granite boulders.
Researchers discovered rupicolous plants adapted uniquely to bare rock surfaces.
Many ancient rupicolous paintings have been preserved in mountain caves.

Synset Examples

rupestral drawings in the caves of Northern Spain

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rupestrian - existing, living, or growing on or among rocks; inscribed onto rocks

Etymology

From Latin 'rupes' (rock) + '-colous' (inhabiting).
