proudly

1. ADV

/ ˈpraʊdli / (AmE), / ˈpraʊdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows that you are pleased and satisfied about something you have done, own, or are associated with.
in a proud manner

Examples

The student proudly displayed her award to her family.
He proudly wore his grandfather's old watch to the ceremony.
Their team proudly marched onto the field before the game.

Synset Examples

he walked proudly into town

Related Terms

  • Antonym: humbly - in a humble manner; the opposite of proud behavior
