guttersnipe

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌt̬.ɚ.snaɪp / (AmE), / ˈɡʌt.ə.snaɪp / (BrE)
A child who spends most of their time in the streets, especially in slum areas.
street child, especially in slums

Examples

The social worker tried to help the young guttersnipe find a safe place to stay.
He grew up as a guttersnipe in the city's poorest neighborhood but later became successful.
Victorian literature often depicts the adventures of a guttersnipe struggling to survive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: street urchin - a poor child who lives or spends most of the time in the streets
  • Synonym: waif - a homeless or neglected child

Etymology

From 'gutter' (street drain) + 'snipe' (a type of bird), originally slang for children seen near gutters.
