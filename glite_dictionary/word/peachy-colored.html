peachy-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈpiːtʃi ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈpiːtʃi ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
Having the color of a ripe peach; pale pinkish-orange.
pale pinkish-orange color

Examples

She wore a peachy-colored dress to the garden party, making her stand out in the crowd.
The walls were painted a soft peachy-colored shade that made the room feel warm and inviting.
He handed her a bouquet of peachy-colored roses for their anniversary.
