Turkishness

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɜːrkɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈtɜːkɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being Turkish, especially in regard to culture, identity, or characteristics.
quality or state of being Turkish

Examples

The novel explores themes of Turkishness and cultural identity.
Debates about Turkishness continue to shape the nation's politics and society.
He questioned whether modern customs threaten the essence of Turkishness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Turkish - of or relating to Turkey, its people, or their language

Etymology

From Turkish + -ness, a suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality.
