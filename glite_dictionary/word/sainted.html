sainted

1. ADJ

/ ˈseɪntɪd / (AmE), / ˈseɪntɪd / (BrE)
regarded as having the qualities of a saint; extremely kind, virtuous, or revered, especially after death
regarded as saintly or extremely virtuous

Examples

She always spoke of her sainted mother with deep reverence and love.
The villagers remembered the sainted priest who helped everyone in times of need.
People described him as sainted because of his lifelong commitment to the poor.

Synset Examples

angelic beneficence
a beatific smile
a saintly concern for his fellow men
my sainted mother

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saintly - having the qualities of a saint; very holy or virtuous
  • Synonym: angelic - like an angel; very pure, kind, or beautiful
  • Antonym: wicked - morally wrong or evil; the opposite of saintly

Etymology

From 'saint' + '-ed', meaning 'made a saint' or 'like a saint'.
