dairy

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛri / (AmE), / ˈdeəri / (BrE)
a place, often a farm, where milk is collected and processed into products such as cheese, butter, and cream
farm for processing milk products

Examples

The family owns a small dairy that produces cheese and yogurt every day.
Fresh milk was delivered straight from the dairy each morning.
Visitors can tour the dairy to learn how butter and cheese are made.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creamery - a place where butter and cheese are made

Etymology

From Middle English deyery, from 'dæg(e)' (female servant, dairymaid).
