speechifier

1. NOUN

/ ˈspiːtʃɪˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈspiːtʃɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a person who delivers speeches, especially in a formal or pompous manner
person who delivers speeches

Examples

The speechifier held the audience's attention for over an hour with his passionate rhetoric.
At every town meeting, there seemed to be at least one speechifier eager to voice his opinions.
Critics dismissed him as a mere speechifier who rarely took substantive action.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orator - a skilled public speaker
  • Synonym: lecturer - a person who gives lectures

Etymology

Formed from 'speech' + '-ifier', analogous to 'speechify'. Patterned after other agent nouns.
