Eurasian

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈreɪʒən / (AmE), / jʊəˈreɪʒən / (BrE)
a person of mixed European and Asian ancestry.
person of mixed European and Asian descent

Examples

Her father is European and her mother is Chinese, making her a Eurasian.
Many Eurasians in the region play a vital role in cultural exchange.
The identity of being Eurasian can involve a blend of traditions from both continents.

Etymology

From Eurasia, the landmass combining Europe and Asia, plus -an.

2. ADJ

/ jʊˈreɪʒən / (AmE), / jʊəˈreɪʒən / (BrE)
relating to, or originating from, both Europe and Asia.
related to Europe and Asia

Examples

The Eurasian continent spans from Portugal in the west to Russia's Far East.
Several Eurasian cultures have influenced each other for centuries due to their proximity.
The Eurasian landmass is the largest on Earth.

Synset Examples

the Eurasian landmass is the largest in the world

Etymology

From 'Europe' + 'Asia' with English adjectival suffix '-an'.
