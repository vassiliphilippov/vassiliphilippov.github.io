Chisinau

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɪʃɪˈnaʊ / (AmE), / ˌkɪʃɪˈnaʊ / (BrE)
the capital city of Moldova, located in the central part of the country
capital city of Moldova

Examples

She visited Chisinau during her tour of Eastern Europe.
Many new businesses have opened in Chisinau over the past decade.
Chisinau is known for its tree-lined boulevards and historic architecture.

Etymology

From Romanian Chişinău, possibly derived from archaic Romanian 'chiși' (to spring, pour) and 'nou' (new).
