Georges Cuvier

1. PROPN

/ ʒɔrʒ kuˈvjeɪ / (AmE), / ʒɔːʒ kyˈvjeɪ / (BrE)
(1769–1832) French naturalist known as the father of comparative anatomy and paleontology.
French naturalist, father of comparative anatomy

Examples

The research of Georges Cuvier laid the foundation for modern comparative anatomy.
Many natural history museums feature exhibits inspired by the work of Georges Cuvier.
In his lectures, Georges Cuvier emphasized the importance of fossils in understanding Earth's history.

Etymology

Georges Cuvier is a French personal name; Cuvier is a surname.
