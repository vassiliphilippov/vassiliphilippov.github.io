violet-blue

1. ADJ

/ ˌvaɪəlɪtˈbluː / (AmE), / ˌvaɪəlɪtˈbluː / (BrE)
Of blue tinged with violet or lavender; a bluish-purple color.
bluish-purple color

Examples

The artist selected a violet-blue shade to capture the twilight sky.
Her dress was a stunning violet-blue that shimmered in the light.
He painted the walls violet-blue to create a calm and creative atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: blue - a color between green and violet in the spectrum
  • Degree Higher: violet - a bluish-purple color at the end of the spectrum
