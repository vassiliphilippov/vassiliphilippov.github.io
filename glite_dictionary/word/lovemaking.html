lovemaking

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌvˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlʌvˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of engaging in sexual activity, especially sexual intercourse, often with an emphasis on affection or intimacy
sexual activity between people

Examples

The couple's gentle lovemaking brought them closer together.
She wrote a novel that described lovemaking with tenderness and honesty.
They believed that open communication was essential for fulfilling lovemaking.

Synset Examples

his lovemaking disgusted her
he hadn't had any love in months
he has a very complicated love life

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intercourse - sexual connection or intercourse between people
  • Antonym: celibacy - state of abstaining from sexual activity

Etymology

From 'love' + 'making', originally meaning courtship or wooing, later referring to sexual activity.
