gen

1. NOUN

/ dʒɛn / (AmE), / dʒɛn / (BrE)
(informal) information or details about something.
informal: information, details
informal british

Examples

Can you give me the gen on how that machine works?
Here's the latest gen about the product launch.
I've got all the gen you need for your interview.

Synset Examples

give me the gen on your new line of computers

Related Terms

Etymology

Probably shortened from 'general information' or influenced by military slang.
