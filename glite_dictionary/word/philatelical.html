philatelical

1. ADJ

/ fɪˌlætəˈlɪkəl / (AmE), / fɪˌlætəˈlɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to philately or of interest to philatelists
relating to stamp collecting (philately)

Examples

The museum displayed a philatelical collection featuring rare stamps from the 19th century.
He wrote a philatelical article discussing the value of misprinted stamps.
Her philatelical interests have led her to travel to stamp exhibitions worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: philatelic - relating to philately or stamp collecting; more common form

Etymology

From French 'philatélique', from 'philatélie' (philately) + '-ical' (adjective suffix).
