lovesickness

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌvˌsɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈlʌvˌsɪknɪs / (BrE)
A deep feeling of longing, distress, or unhappiness caused by being separated from or unable to be with someone one loves.
emotional distress from longing for a loved one

Examples

His lovesickness made it hard for him to focus on anything but her absence.
During the long separation, she struggled with deep lovesickness and loneliness.
Many poems have been written about the pain of lovesickness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartache - emotional pain or distress over lost or unrequited love
  • Degree Lower: homesickness - distress caused by being away from home

Etymology

Formed from 'love' + 'sickness', modeled after the sense of illness from longing.
