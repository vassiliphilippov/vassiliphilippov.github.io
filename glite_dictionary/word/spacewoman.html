spacewoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈspeɪsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈspeɪsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is trained to travel and work in space as a member of a spacecraft crew
female astronaut

Examples

The first British spacewoman inspired a new generation of scientists.
She dreamed of becoming a spacewoman since she was a child.
Photos of the spacewoman floating in zero gravity appeared on the front page.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spaceman - a person trained to travel in a spacecraft; astronaut
  • Synonym: spacewoman - female astronaut
  • Synonym: astronaut - a person trained to travel in space

Etymology

Formed from 'space' and 'woman', modeled after 'spaceman'.
