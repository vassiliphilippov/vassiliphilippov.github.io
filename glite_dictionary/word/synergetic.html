synergetic

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɪnərˈdʒɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɪnəˈdʒɛtɪk / (BrE)
working together in a cooperative way to produce an enhanced or combined effect, especially used of groups or organizations
working together to produce a greater effect

Examples

The team's synergetic approach to the project led to impressive results.
When departments are synergetic, they achieve more together than alone.
The merger created a synergetic environment that fostered innovation.

Synset Examples

a synergistic effect

Related Terms

  • Synonym: synergistic - having an effect greater than the sum of individual effects

Etymology

From Greek 'synergos' (working together), via Modern Latin 'synergeticus'.
