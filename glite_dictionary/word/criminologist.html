criminologist

1. NOUN

/ krɪˈmɪnələdʒɪst / (AmE), / krɪˈmɪnələʤɪst / (BrE)
A person who studies or is an expert in criminology, the scientific study of crime and criminals.
expert in criminology

Examples

The criminologist presented her findings at the international crime conference.
As a criminologist, he focuses on the social causes of criminal behavior.
Police often consult a criminologist when dealing with unusual crime patterns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: criminology - the scientific study of crime and criminals

Etymology

From Latin 'crimen' (crime) + Greek '-logia' (study of) + English suffix '-ist'.
