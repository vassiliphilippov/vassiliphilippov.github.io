Beograd

1. PROPN

/ beɪˈoʊɡrɑːd / (AmE), / beɪˈɒɡrɑːd / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Serbia, situated at the confluence of the Danube and Sava rivers; also known as Belgrade
capital city of Serbia

Examples

She was born and raised in Beograd, the vibrant heart of Serbia.
Many international conferences have been held in Beograd over the years.
After his studies abroad, he decided to return to Beograd and start his career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Belgrade - English name for the capital of Serbia

Etymology

From Serbian 'Beograd', meaning 'white city' (beo 'white' + grad 'city').
