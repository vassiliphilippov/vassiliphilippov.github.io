microfarad

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪ.kroʊˌfɛr.æd / (AmE), / ˈmaɪ.krəʊˌfær.æd / (BrE)
a unit of capacitance equal to one millionth of a farad
unit of capacitance (10⁻⁶ farad)

Examples

The capacitor in this circuit has a value of 10 microfarads.
Engineers measured the device's capacitance in microfarad units for accuracy.
Older radios often used capacitors rated in microfarads instead of nanofarads.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: farad - the SI unit of capacitance
  • Degree Lower: picofarad - a unit of capacitance equal to one trillionth of a farad

Etymology

From 'micro-' (meaning one millionth) + 'farad' (named after Michael Faraday).
