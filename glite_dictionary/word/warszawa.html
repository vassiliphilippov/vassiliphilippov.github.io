Warszawa

1. PROPN

/ varˈʃa.və / (AmE), / varˈʃa.va / (BrE)
The capital and largest city of Poland, located in central Poland, known in English as Warsaw.
capital city of Poland (Warsaw)

Examples

They spent a week exploring the historic sites of Warszawa.
After moving to Warszawa, she quickly adapted to the vibrant city life.
The Vistula River flows through Warszawa, giving the city its unique charm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Warsaw - the capital city of Poland (English form)

Etymology

Polish Warszawa, the native name for Warsaw.
