chlorophyllous

1. ADJ

/ klɔːˈrɑː.fə.ləs / (AmE), / klɔːˈrɪl.fɪ.ləs / (BrE)
relating to, containing, or having the characteristics of chlorophyll.
containing or relating to chlorophyll

Examples

Leaves that appear chlorophyllous are able to perform photosynthesis efficiently.
The scientist examined several chlorophyllous algae under the microscope.
Some plant tissues are less chlorophyllous and therefore lighter in color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chlorophyll - the green pigment found in plants

Etymology

From 'chlorophyll' + '-ous', meaning 'having the qualities of'.
