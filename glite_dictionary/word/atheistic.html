atheistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌeɪθiˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌeɪθiˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by atheism; lacking belief in the existence of deities.
relating to atheism or lack of belief in gods

Examples

Her atheistic beliefs often sparked debates with her religious friends.
The country's government promoted an atheistic ideology during that period.
He spoke about his atheistic upbringing in a largely religious community.

Synset Examples

atheist leanings

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atheist - a person who does not believe in the existence of any god or gods
  • Antonym: theistic - relating to belief in one or more gods

Etymology

From Greek atheos (without god) + -istic.

2. ADJ

/ ˌeɪθiˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌeɪθiˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
rejecting or not believing in the existence of any gods
not believing in gods

Examples

Her views are distinctly atheistic and she argues strongly against the existence of divine beings.
The book offers an atheistic interpretation of the origins of morality.
Many atheistic philosophies emphasize human reason over religious faith.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atheist - a person who does not believe in any god or gods
  • Antonym: theistic - relating to belief in the existence of a god or gods

Etymology

From Greek atheos (without god) + -istic.
