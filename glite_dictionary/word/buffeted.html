buffeted

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʌf.ɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈbʌf.ɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
repeatedly struck or affected by strong forces, such as storms or difficulties
repeatedly struck or affected

Examples

The coastal town was buffeted by strong winds throughout the winter storm.
She felt buffeted by a series of personal and professional setbacks.
The ship, buffeted by enormous waves, struggled to reach the harbor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pounded - struck repeatedly and heavily
