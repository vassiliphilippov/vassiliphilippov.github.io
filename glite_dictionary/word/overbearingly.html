overbearingly

1. ADV

/ ˌoʊvɚˈbɛrɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈbeərɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that is domineering, arrogant, or excessively controlling.
in a domineering or arrogant way

Examples

His boss spoke overbearingly during the meeting, refusing to listen to anyone else's suggestions.
She rolled her eyes as her mother overbearingly critiqued every detail of her outfit.
The teacher overbearingly dismissed all opposing viewpoints in class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overbearing - domineering or arrogantly controlling
