8vo

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪtˌvoʊ / (AmE), / ˈeɪtˌvəʊ / (BrE)
A book size in which each sheet is folded three times, making eight leaves or sixteen pages.
book size with eight leaves per sheet

Examples

The rare first edition was printed in 8vo format, making it highly collectible.
His library contains numerous classic novels in beautifully bound 8vo volumes.
Collectors sometimes prefer the 8vo size for ease of handling and reading.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: octavo - book size with eight leaves per sheet

Etymology

Abbreviation of Latin 'octavo', from 'octavus' (eighth).
