droplet

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɑːplət / (AmE), / ˈdrɒplət / (BrE)
A very small drop of liquid.
a very small drop of liquid

Examples

A single droplet of water rolled down the window.
Tiny droplets of sweat formed on his forehead.
Scientists studied the behavior of an oil droplet under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: drop - a small amount of liquid in spherical form

Etymology

Diminutive of 'drop', formed with the suffix -let.
