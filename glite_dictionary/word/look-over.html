look-over

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʊkˌoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈlʊkˌəʊvə / (BrE)
a quick and superficial examination or inspection of something or someone
swift cursory inspection

Examples

She gave the document a quick look-over before the meeting.
The mechanic did a look-over of the engine and found a loose wire.
He cast a look-over around the room to check if anything was out of place.

Synset Examples

I gave him the once-over

Related Terms

  • Synonym: once-over - a quick and cursory inspection
