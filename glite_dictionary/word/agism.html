agism

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪdʒɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈeɪdʒɪzəm / (BrE)
discrimination or prejudice against individuals based on their age, especially against elderly people.
discrimination based on age

Examples

Many workplaces are addressing agism to create more inclusive environments for older employees.
The film explores the theme of agism in a society obsessed with youth.
She filed a lawsuit claiming agism after being overlooked for promotion due to her age.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ageism - discrimination or prejudice against people based on age, especially elderly people

Etymology

From 'age' + '-ism', modeled after similar formations like 'racism'.
