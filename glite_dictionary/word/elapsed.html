elapsed

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈlæpst / (AmE), / ɪˈlæpst / (BrE)
(of time) having passed or gone by; used to indicate the passage of a period of time.
(of time) having passed

Examples

The elapsed time since the experiment began was exactly two hours.
Please note the elapsed time on your stopwatch when you finish the race.
After a significant amount of elapsed time, the results finally arrived.

Synset Examples

elapsed time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to elapse - to pass or slip by (of time)

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'elapse', from Latin 'elapsus', past participle of 'elabi' (slip away).
