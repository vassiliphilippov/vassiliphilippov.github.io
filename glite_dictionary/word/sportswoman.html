sportswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrtsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who participates in sports, especially as an athlete.
female athlete

Examples

The young sportswoman won three gold medals at the national swimming championships.
As a dedicated sportswoman, she trains six days a week to stay in peak condition.
Every aspiring sportswoman needs both physical strength and mental determination.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sportsman - a man who participates in sports
  • Synonym: athlete - a person who participates in sports or other physical exercise
