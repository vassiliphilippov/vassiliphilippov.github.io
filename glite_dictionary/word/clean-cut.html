clean-cut

1. ADJ

/ ˈklin ˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈkliːn ˌkʌt / (BrE)
having a neat and smart appearance; well-groomed and tidy.
neat and tidy in appearance

Examples

He always looks clean-cut when he goes to work in his suit and tie.
The store prefers to hire clean-cut employees to greet customers at the entrance.
Despite his clean-cut appearance, he loves adventure sports in his free time.

Synset Examples

a clean-cut and well-bred young man
the trig corporal in his jaunty cap
a trim beard

Related Terms

  • Synonym: well-groomed - having a tidy and cared-for appearance
  • Antonym: sloppy - untidy or careless in appearance

2. ADJ

/ ˈklin ˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˌkliːn ˈkʌt / (BrE)
sharply defined or clear in appearance; easy to perceive or distinguish.
sharply defined; easy to see or distinguish

Examples

The mountain's peak stood clean-cut against the early morning sky.
She admired the clean-cut lines of the sculpture in the gallery.
A clean-cut shadow stretched across the white wall as the sun set.

Synset Examples

as clear as a whistle
clear footprints in the snow
the letter brought back a clear image of his grandfather
a spire clean-cut against the sky
a clear-cut pattern

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clear-cut - sharply outlined or defined; easy to perceive or understand
