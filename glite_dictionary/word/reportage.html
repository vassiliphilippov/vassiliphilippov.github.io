reportage

1. NOUN

/ rəˈpɔrt̬.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / rɪˈpɔː.tɑːʒ / (BrE)
the reporting of news and events by journalists, as presented in newspapers, radio, or television
journalistic news reporting

Examples

The documentary offered in-depth reportage on the humanitarian crisis unfolding in the region.
Critics praised the magazine's balanced reportage of the election results.
She specializes in investigative reportage for national television.

Synset Examples

they accused the paper of biased coverage of race relations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coverage - the reporting of news or events in the media

Etymology

From French 'reportage' (reporting), from 'reporter' (to report).
