kinky

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋki / (BrE)
(of sexual behavior) involving or suggesting unconventional, unusual, or deviant sexual practices or tastes.
unconventional in sexual behavior

Examples

Some couples enjoy exploring their kinky side in private.
He shared details about his kinky fantasies with his partner.
The film was criticized for including several kinky scenes.

Synset Examples

kinky sex
perverted practices

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perverted - showing sexual behavior that is considered abnormal or unacceptable
  • Antonym: normal - conforming to standard or conventional sexual behavior

2. ADJ

/ ˈkɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋki / (BrE)
Strikingly unconventional or unusual, especially in an interesting or provocative way.
strikingly unconventional; unusual
informal

Examples

Her kinky sense of fashion always makes her stand out at parties.
The restaurant's kinky decor surprised everyone with its bold colors and odd shapes.
He's known for having a kinky sense of humor that not everyone understands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - unconventional and slightly strange
  • Antonym: conventional - based on or in accordance with what is generally done or believed

Etymology

From kink + -y, likely influenced by use of 'kink' for an eccentricity since the 19th century.
