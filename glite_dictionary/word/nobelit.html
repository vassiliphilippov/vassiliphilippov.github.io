Nobelit

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊbəˌlɪt / (AmE), / ˈnəʊbəˌlɪt / (BrE)
A trade name for an emulsion explosive and blasting agent used in mining and construction.
trade name for an emulsion explosive

Examples

The mining company used Nobelit to safely blast through the rock.
Modern construction projects sometimes require Nobelit for controlled demolitions.
The engineer explained that Nobelit is preferred for its stability and ease of handling.
