legalization

1. NOUN

/ ˌliːɡəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌliːɡəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something legal by law
the act of making something legal

Examples

The legalization of cannabis has sparked debates across the country.
Activists gathered to support the legalization of same-sex marriage.
Following the legalization of gambling, new casinos quickly opened.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decriminalization - the act of removing criminal penalties for something
  • Antonym: banning - the act of making something illegal or forbidden by law

Etymology

From legal + -ization, modeled on French 'légalisation.'
