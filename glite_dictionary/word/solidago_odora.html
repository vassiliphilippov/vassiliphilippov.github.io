Solidago odora

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɑː.lɪˈdeɪ.ɡoʊ oʊˈdɔːrə / (AmE), / ˌsɒ.lɪˈdeɪ.ɡəʊ əʊˈdɔːrə / (BrE)
A North American goldenrod species with aromatic leaves, used to make medicinal tea.
aromatic North American goldenrod

Examples

The herbalist recommended a tea made from Solidago odora for digestive relief.
Native Americans have long used Solidago odora for its medicinal properties.
The garden was filled with the sweet scent of Solidago odora in late summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldenrod - a plant of the genus Solidago with yellow flowers

Etymology

From Latin: 'solidago' meaning 'to make whole, heal', and 'odora' meaning 'fragrant'.
