Naiki

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪki / (AmE), / ˈnaɪki / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken in south central India, especially in Andhra Pradesh and Maharashtra.
Dravidian language of south central India

Examples

The Naiki language is part of the Dravidian family spoken in central India.
Many people in Andhra Pradesh and Maharashtra still speak Naiki as their mother tongue.
Researchers are working to document the grammar and vocabulary of Naiki.
