bronchiolar

1. ADJ

/ brɑːŋˈkaɪ.ə.lɚ / (AmE), / brɒŋˈkaɪ.ələ / (BrE)
relating to or involving the bronchioles, which are the small air passages in the lungs.
of or relating to the bronchioles
medical

Examples

The doctor noticed bronchiolar inflammation in the patient's lungs.
Certain viruses can cause bronchiolar obstruction in infants and young children.
A bronchiolar disorder may lead to breathing difficulties and wheezing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bronchiole - a small branch of the bronchial tubes in the lungs

Etymology

From 'bronchiole' (a small bronchus) + '-ar' (forming adjectives).
