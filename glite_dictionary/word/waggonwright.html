waggonwright

1. NOUN

/ ˈwæɡənˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈwæɡ(ə)nraɪt / (BrE)
a person whose occupation is making or repairing wagons
wagon maker or repairer
archaic

Examples

The village once had a skilled waggonwright who supplied carts for local farmers.
He apprenticed as a waggonwright before moving to the city.
Old records list his occupation as a waggonwright, crafting sturdy wagons by hand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wheelwright - a person who makes or repairs wheels

Etymology

From 'waggon' (wagon) + 'wright' (maker or builder, from Old English wryhta).
