viticulturist

1. NOUN

/ ˌvɪt̬ɪˈkʌltʃərɪst / (AmE), / ˌvɪtɪˈkʌltʃərɪst / (BrE)
a person who cultivates grapevines, especially for winemaking
person who grows grapevines

Examples

The viticulturist carefully monitored the soil and weather conditions throughout the growing season.
As a renowned viticulturist, she advised many vineyards across the region.
Modern technology has greatly aided the work of the viticulturist in improving grape yields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vintner - a person who makes or sells wine

Etymology

From Latin 'vitis' (vine) + 'cultura' (cultivation); 'viticulture' + '-ist'.
