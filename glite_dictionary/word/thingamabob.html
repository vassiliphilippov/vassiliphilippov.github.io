thingamabob

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɪŋəməˌbɑb / (AmE), / ˈθɪŋəməˌbɒb / (BrE)
An object or thing whose name is unknown or forgotten.
unspecified object; whose name is forgotten
informal

Examples

Can you hand me that thingamabob on the table next to the lamp?
I fixed the sink with a little thingamabob I found in the toolbox.
Whenever he doesn't remember a word, he just says thingamabob.

Synset Examples

she eased the ball-shaped doodad back into its socket
there may be some great new gizmo around the corner that you will want to use

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thingamajig - an object whose name is forgotten or unknown
  • Synonym: gizmo - a gadget, especially one whose name is unknown or forgotten
  • Synonym: widget - a small device or gadget

Etymology

Formed from 'thing' and a playful suffix, modeled after similar words like 'thingamajig' and 'whatchamacallit.'

2. NOUN

/ ˈθɪŋəməˌbɑb / (AmE), / ˈθɪŋəməˌbɒb / (BrE)
an object whose name is unknown or has been forgotten; a thing used when you do not know or cannot remember the name
object with unknown or forgotten name
informal

Examples

Have you seen the thingamabob that opens bottles?
She used a little thingamabob to fix the necklace clasp.
He handed me some kind of thingamabob and said it would help.

Synset Examples

she eased the ball-shaped doodad back into its socket
there may be some great new gizmo around the corner that you will want to use

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thingamajig - an object whose name is unknown or forgotten
  • Synonym: gizmo - a gadget, especially one whose name is unknown or forgotten
  • Synonym: whatchamacallit - a thing whose name is unknown or forgotten

Etymology

Formed by alteration and extension of 'thing,' modeled after humorous pseudo-names like 'whatchamacallit.'
