Guatemalan

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡwɑtəˈmælən / (AmE), / ˌɡwɑːtəˈmælən / (BrE)
Relating to Guatemala or its people.
of or relating to Guatemala

Examples

The Guatemalan volcanoes attract geologists from around the world.
She brewed a cup of strong Guatemalan coffee for breakfast.
Many Guatemalan traditions date back to the ancient Maya civilization.

Synset Examples

Guatemalan coffee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Guatemala - A country in Central America

Etymology

Formed from the country name Guatemala + English suffix -an.

2. NOUN

/ ˌɡwɑːtəˈmɑːlən / (AmE), / ˌɡwɑːtəˈmɑːlən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Guatemala
a person from Guatemala

Examples

Many Guatemalans celebrate Independence Day with traditional music and dance.
The new restaurant is owned by a Guatemalan who moved here five years ago.
She is proud of her Guatemalan heritage and often visits her family there.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Guatemala - a country in Central America

Etymology

From Guatemala, the name of the country, + -an (denoting origin or affiliation).
