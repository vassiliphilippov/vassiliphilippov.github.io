Naias

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪ.əs / (AmE), / ˈnaɪ.as / (BrE)
a genus of aquatic plants, commonly known as water nymphs or naiads, and the sole genus of the family Naiadaceae
genus of aquatic plants (naiads)

Examples

The Naias genus thrives in slow-moving streams and shallow lakes.
Biologists discovered several new species within the Naias genus in tropical regions.
Many fish depend on Naias plants as their primary source of food and shelter.

Etymology

From Greek Naias, Naiad-, meaning 'water nymph'.
