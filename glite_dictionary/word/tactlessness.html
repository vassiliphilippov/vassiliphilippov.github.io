tactlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæktləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈtæktləsnəs / (BrE)
The quality of lacking sensitivity or skill in dealing with others or difficult situations.
lack of tact; insensitivity

Examples

His tactlessness often offended people without him even realizing it.
I was surprised by the tactlessness of her remarks at the meeting.
She regretted her tactlessness as soon as she saw the look on his face.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tact - skill and sensitivity in dealing with others
  • Synonym: insensitivity - lack of sensitivity; not being aware of others' feelings

Etymology

From 'tactless' + '-ness'.
