knapweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæpˌwid / (AmE), / ˈnapˌwiːd / (BrE)
any of several plants of the genus Centaurea, characterized by purple thistlelike flowers
plant with purple thistlelike flowers

Examples

The meadow was filled with blooming knapweed attracting dozens of butterflies.
Gardeners often remove knapweed to prevent it from spreading among native plants.
She identified the wildflowers as a species of knapweed native to the area.
