dinette

1. NOUN

/ daɪˈnɛt / (AmE), / daɪˈnɛt / (BrE)
a small area or room next to a kitchen used for casual dining
small dining area near a kitchen

Examples

They had breakfast together every morning in the dinette by the window.
The new apartment features a compact dinette just off the kitchen.
We placed a small round table in the dinette for casual meals.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: dining room - a room used for eating meals

Etymology

From French 'dînette', diminutive of 'dîner' (to dine).
