cutting-edge

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌtɪŋ ˌɛdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkʌtɪŋ ˌɛdʒ / (BrE)
At the forefront of the latest and most advanced ideas, technology, or style; highly innovative or fashionable.
highly innovative or fashionable

Examples

The company is known for its cutting-edge designs in smartphone technology.
Our research team is working on cutting-edge solutions to address climate change.
She always prefers cutting-edge fashion over more traditional styles.

Synset Examples

wears only the latest style
cutting-edge technology
a with-it boutique
add a sleek look to this with-it kitchen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: state-of-the-art - representing the latest stage of development
  • Synonym: innovative - featuring new methods; advanced and original
  • Antonym: outdated - no longer fashionable or useful

Etymology

Derived from the phrase 'cutting edge', originally meaning the sharp edge of a blade, later figuratively used for leading or pioneering positions.
