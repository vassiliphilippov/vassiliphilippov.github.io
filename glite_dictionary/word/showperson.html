showperson

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊˌpɜrsən / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌpɜːsən / (BrE)
A person who is skilled at making impressive or effective presentations, often in public or entertainment settings.
person skilled at presenting or entertaining

Examples

As a showperson, she captivated audiences with her dynamic presentations.
The company hired a talented showperson to lead their product launch event.
He developed his skills as a showperson through years of public speaking and theater.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: showman - a man skilled at entertaining or presenting
  • Synonym: showwoman - a woman skilled at entertaining or presenting
