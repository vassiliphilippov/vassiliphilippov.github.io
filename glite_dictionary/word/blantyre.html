Blantyre

1. PROPN

/ blænˈtaɪər / (AmE), / blænˈtaɪə / (BrE)
a city in southern Malawi that is the country's largest city and commercial center.
largest city in Malawi

Examples

Many international companies have their offices in Blantyre, the commercial hub of Malawi.
She traveled to Blantyre to attend a major business conference last year.
The University of Malawi has its College of Medicine located in Blantyre.

Etymology

Named after Blantyre, Scotland, the birthplace of explorer David Livingstone.
