toothpaste

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuθˌpeɪst / (AmE), / ˈtuːθpeɪst / (BrE)
A thick, soft substance applied to a toothbrush to clean and polish teeth.
paste for cleaning teeth

Examples

She squeezed a small amount of toothpaste onto her toothbrush before brushing her teeth.
This minty toothpaste helps prevent cavities and freshens breath.
We ran out of toothpaste and had to buy a new tube at the store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toothpowder - a powder used to clean teeth

Etymology

Formed from 'tooth' + 'paste', indicating a paste used for teeth.
