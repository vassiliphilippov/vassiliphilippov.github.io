Eritrea

1. PROPN

/ ˌɛrɪˈtreɪə / (AmE), / ˌɛrɪˈtriːə / (BrE)
a country in northeast Africa, bordering the Red Sea, which became independent from Ethiopia in 1993
country in northeast Africa

Examples

She traveled to Eritrea to volunteer with a local aid organization.
After years of conflict, Eritrea gained its independence from Ethiopia in 1993.
The Red Sea coastline of Eritrea is famous for its coral reefs.

2. PROPN

/ ˌɛrɪˈtreɪə / (AmE), / ˌɛrɪˈtriːə / (BrE)
a country in northeastern Africa, located on the Red Sea, north of Ethiopia, which gained independence from Ethiopia in 1993
country in northeastern Africa
american british

Examples

She traveled through Eritrea to experience its rich cultural heritage and diverse landscapes.
Eritrea gained its independence from Ethiopia in 1993 after a long struggle.
Many refugees have fled Eritrea due to political and economic challenges.

Etymology

From Italian Eritrea, from Latin Mare Erythraeum (Red Sea), from Ancient Greek Ἐρυθραία (Erythraia), meaning 'the red [land/sea]'.
