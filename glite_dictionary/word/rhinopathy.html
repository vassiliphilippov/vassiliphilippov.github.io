rhinopathy

1. NOUN

/ raɪˈnɑːpəθi / (AmE), / raɪˈnɒpəθi / (BrE)
any disease or abnormal condition affecting the nose
disease of the nose
medical

Examples

Chronic sinus infections may be classified as a form of rhinopathy.
The doctor is researching treatments for rare types of rhinopathy.
Advances in surgery have improved outcomes for patients with congenital rhinopathy.

Etymology

From Greek rhino- (nose) + -pathy (disease, suffering).
