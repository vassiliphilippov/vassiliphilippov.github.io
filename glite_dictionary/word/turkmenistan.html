Turkmenistan

1. PROPN

/ ˌtɝːkmɛnɪˈstæn / (AmE), / ˌtɜːkmɛnɪˈstɑːn / (BrE)
A country in Central Asia, east of the Caspian Sea, north of Iran, and south of Kazakhstan; formerly a Soviet republic (1925–1991).
country in Central Asia

Examples

She traveled across Turkmenistan during her gap year, visiting ancient Silk Road cities.
Natural gas is one of Turkmenistan's most important exports.
After the dissolution of the Soviet Union, Turkmenistan became an independent nation in 1991.

Etymology

From Turkmen (the Turkic ethnic group) + Persian suffix -stan (land), meaning 'land of the Turkmen'.
