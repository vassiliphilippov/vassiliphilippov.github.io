revilement

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈvaɪlmənt / (AmE), / rɪˈvaɪlmənt / (BrE)
an insulting or abusive expression intended to offend or hurt someone
insulting or abusive expression

Examples

His comments were met with a stream of revilement from the audience.
Online forums are often filled with revilement aimed at celebrities.
The politician did not respond to the revilement directed at him during the debate.

Synset Examples

when a student made a stupid mistake he spared them no abuse
they yelled insults at the visiting team

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insult - an offensive or disrespectful remark or action
  • Synonym: abuse - cruel and violent treatment or words

Etymology

From the verb 'revile' (to criticize in an abusive manner) + '-ment' (forming nouns of action or result).
