Vinca major

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪŋkə ˈmeɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈvɪŋkə ˈmeɪdʒə / (BrE)
A trailing evergreen plant with glossy, variegated leaves and blue or violet flowers, often used in window boxes and gardens.
evergreen trailing plant; garden ornamental

Examples

Vinca major is a popular choice for adding color and texture to shaded garden areas.
Gardeners often use Vinca major in window boxes for its attractive trailing habit.
The variegated foliage of Vinca major brightens up borders and containers throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: periwinkle - another name for Vinca major; a common name for the plant

Etymology

From Latin 'vinca' (periwinkle) and 'major' (greater), indicating its larger size compared to similar species.
