Sep

1. NOUN

/ sɛp / (AmE), / sɛp / (BrE)
abbreviation for September, the ninth month of the year in the Gregorian calendar
abbreviation for September (month)

Examples

Many students start the new school year in Sep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: September - the ninth month of the year in the Gregorian calendar

Etymology

Abbreviation of September, from Latin 'September'.
