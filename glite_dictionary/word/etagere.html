etagere

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪtəˈʒɛr / (AmE), / ˌeɪtɑːˈʒɛə / (BrE)
A piece of furniture with open shelves used for displaying small ornaments or decorative objects.
open-shelved display furniture

Examples

Her collection of figurines was beautifully arranged on the antique etagere in the living room.
The modern etagere by the window holds several potted plants and art books.
We decided to buy a small etagere to display our souvenirs from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shelf - a flat length of wood or other material, attached to a wall or forming part of a piece of furniture, that provides a surface for storing or displaying objects

Etymology

From French 'étagère', meaning shelf or set of shelves.
