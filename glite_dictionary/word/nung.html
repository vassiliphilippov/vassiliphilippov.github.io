Nung

1. NOUN

/ nʊŋ / (AmE), / nʊŋ / (BrE)
a Tai language spoken in northern Vietnam, especially in provinces such as Cao Bang and Lang Son
a Tai language of northern Vietnam

Examples

The Nung language is taught in several schools in northern Vietnam.
She is fluent in both Nung and Vietnamese.
Researchers are documenting the traditions of the Nung speakers in Bac Kan province.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tai - a family of languages spoken in Southeast Asia
