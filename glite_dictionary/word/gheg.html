Gheg

1. NOUN

/ ɡeɪɡ / (AmE), / ɡeɪɡ / (BrE)
the dialect of Albanian spoken primarily in northern Albania and surrounding regions
northern Albanian dialect

Examples

Many people in northern Albania speak Gheg as their native dialect.
The differences between Gheg and Tosk can be heard in pronunciation and vocabulary.
She is studying the grammar of Gheg for her thesis on Albanian linguistics.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Tosk - the southern Albanian dialect, counterpart to Gheg
