pakchoi

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːkˌtʃɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈpækˌtʃɔɪ / (BrE)
An Asian vegetable with crisp white stalks and dark green leaves, often used in stir-fries and salads.
Asian leafy vegetable

Examples

She stir-fried the pakchoi with garlic and ginger for a quick, healthy meal.
Fresh pakchoi can be found in most Asian grocery stores.
The soup was garnished with chopped pakchoi and sliced spring onions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bok choy - A common alternate spelling/referring to the same leafy vegetable

Etymology

From Cantonese 白菜 (baahk chòi), meaning 'white vegetable'.
