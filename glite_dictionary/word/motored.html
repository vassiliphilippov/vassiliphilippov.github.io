motored

1. ADJ

/ ˈmoʊtərd / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəd / (BrE)
equipped with a motor or motors; powered by one or more motors.
powered by a motor

Examples

The motored cart made transporting goods much easier around the factory.
He upgraded his old bicycle to a motored model for his daily commute.
Tourists enjoyed rides in motored boats along the river.

Synset Examples

a motorized wheelchair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motorized - equipped with a motor or motors; powered by a motor
