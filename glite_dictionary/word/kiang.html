kiang

1. NOUN

/ kiˈɑŋ / (AmE), / kiˈæŋ / (BrE)
a large wild ass native to the grasslands of Tibet and Mongolia
wild ass of Tibet and Mongolia

Examples

The kiang roams freely across the high plateaus of Tibet.
A herd of kiangs was spotted near the mountain pass during our expedition.
Unlike other wild asses, the kiang inhabits cold, arid regions of Central Asia.

Etymology

From Tibetan 'khyang', meaning wild ass.
