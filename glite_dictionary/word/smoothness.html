smoothness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmuðnəs / (AmE), / ˈsmuːðnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of having an even surface without roughness; being smooth to the touch
the quality of being smooth

Examples

The smoothness of the river stones made them pleasant to hold.
She admired the smoothness of the carved wooden table.
After polishing, the floor regained its original smoothness.

Synset Examples

admiring the slim smoothness of her thighs
some artists prefer the smoothness of a board

Related Terms

  • Antonym: roughness - the quality of being rough or uneven

Etymology

From 'smooth' + suffix '-ness', denoting a state or quality.
