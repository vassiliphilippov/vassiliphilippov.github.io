soh

1. NOUN

/ soʊ / (AmE), / səʊ / (BrE)
the syllable used to name the fifth (dominant) note of a musical scale in solfège or solmization
fifth note in solfège

Examples

Children learn to sing the scale using soh as the fifth note.
In this exercise, you will transition smoothly from soh to la.
Many teachers use hand signals to represent soh during music lessons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sol - alternative spelling for the fifth note in solfège

Etymology

From Italian 'sol', part of the solfège system, influenced by the hymn 'Ut queant laxis'.
