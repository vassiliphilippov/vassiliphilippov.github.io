succotash

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌk.əˌtæʃ / (AmE), / ˈsʌk.ə.tæʃ / (BrE)
A dish consisting primarily of fresh corn and lima beans, often cooked with butter or cream.
dish of corn and lima beans

Examples

My grandmother made a delicious succotash with fresh vegetables from the garden.
We served succotash as a side dish at Thanksgiving dinner.
The restaurant's version of succotash includes red peppers and herbs.

Etymology

From Narragansett (an Algonquian language) *msickquatash* meaning 'boiled corn kernels'.
