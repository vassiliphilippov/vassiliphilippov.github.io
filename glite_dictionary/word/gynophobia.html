gynophobia

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡaɪnəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌɡaɪnəˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
An abnormal or morbid fear of women.
morbid fear of women
medical

Examples

The psychologist diagnosed him with gynophobia after observing his extreme discomfort around women.
Her severe gynophobia made everyday social situations very stressful.
Overcoming gynophobia often requires therapy and gradual exposure.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: androphobia - an abnormal fear of men

Etymology

From Greek gunē (woman) + -phobia (fear).
