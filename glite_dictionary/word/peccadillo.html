peccadillo

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɛkəˈdɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˌpɛkəˈdɪləʊ / (BrE)
a minor or slight offense or fault; a petty misdeed
minor or trivial offense

Examples

Everyone forgave his little peccadillo and moved on.
The politician's peccadillo was reported in the local paper, but it did not damage his reputation.
In comparison to the scandal, her mistake was a mere peccadillo.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: offense - an illegal act; a breach of a rule or law
  • Degree Higher: crime - an act that is forbidden or the law, especially one punishable by law

Etymology

From Spanish 'pecadillo', diminutive of 'pecado' (sin), from Latin 'peccatum' (sin).
