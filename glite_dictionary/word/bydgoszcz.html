Bydgoszcz

1. PROPN

/ bɪdˈɡɒstʃ / (AmE), / bɪdˈɡɒstʃ / (BrE)
an industrial city and river port in northern Poland, situated on the Brda and Vistula rivers.
a city in northern Poland

Examples

She visited Bydgoszcz during her trip across Poland last summer.
The concert in Bydgoszcz attracted musicians from all over Europe.
Many international companies have recently opened offices in Bydgoszcz.

Etymology

From Polish Bydgoszcz, of Slavic origin.
