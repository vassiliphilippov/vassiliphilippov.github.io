pseudohermaphroditic

1. ADJ

/ ˌsuːdoʊˌhɜːrmæfəˈdraɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəʊˌhɜːməˈfrɪdɪtɪk / (BrE)
having internal reproductive organs of one sex and external sexual characteristics of the other sex
relating to mismatched internal and external sex characteristics
medical

Examples

The patient was found to have pseudohermaphroditic characteristics on examination.
Some reptiles can display pseudohermaphroditic development due to hormonal imbalances during embryonic growth.
Research on pseudohermaphroditic conditions has contributed to our understanding of sexual differentiation.

Etymology

From pseudo- (false) + hermaphroditic, from Greek Hermaphroditos (Hermaphroditus).
