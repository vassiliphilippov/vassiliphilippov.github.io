haematocolpometra

1. NOUN

/ ˌhiːmətəˌkɒlpəˈmiːtrə / (AmE), / ˌhiːmətəˌkɒlpəˈmiːtrə / (BrE)
a medical condition in which blood accumulates in both the vagina and the uterus
accumulation of blood in vagina and uterus
medical

Examples

The ultrasound revealed haematocolpometra as the cause of her abdominal pain.
Delayed diagnosis of haematocolpometra can lead to severe complications.
The patient was treated surgically for haematocolpometra after presenting with acute pelvic discomfort.

Etymology

From Greek haima (blood), kolpos (vagina), and metra (uterus).
