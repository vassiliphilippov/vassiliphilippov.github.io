ringlet

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪŋ.lət / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋ.lɪt / (BrE)
a lock of hair that grows or is arranged in the shape of a small spiral curl
spiral curl of hair

Examples

She wore her long hair in tight ringlets cascading over her shoulders.
A golden ringlet fell across the child's forehead as she played in the garden.
The bride's hair was styled in delicate ringlets for the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curl - a piece of hair that forms a curved or spiral shape

Etymology

From 'ring' + diminutive suffix '-let', indicating a small ring.

2. NOUN

/ ˈrɪŋ.lət / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋ.lɪt / (BrE)
a small ring; especially, a small curl of hair shaped like a ring
small ring or hair curl

Examples

Raindrops dangled from the ringlets of the old iron gate.
His brown ringlets framed his cheerful, round face.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curl - a piece of hair that forms a curved or spiral shape

Etymology

Diminutive of 'ring'.

3. NOUN

/ ˈrɪŋlət / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋlɪt / (BrE)
a small circular band or a round formation made up of concentric circles, such as those seen in leaves or flower petals
small circular formation of concentric circles

Examples

The rose's petals formed a delicate ringlet around the center.
Several ringlets appeared on the surface of the pond after the raindrop fell.
The fern displayed tiny ringlets at the tip of each new frond.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circle - a round plane figure whose boundary consists of points equidistant from the center

Etymology

From 'ring' + diminutive suffix '-let', meaning small ring.

Source: WordNet

4. NOUN

/ ˈrɪŋlət / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋlɪt / (BrE)
a butterfly of the subfamily Satyrinae (family Nymphalidae), typically brown with eyespots on the wings, commonly found in grassland habitats
a brown butterfly with eyespots

Examples

The ringlet fluttered gently above the summer meadow.
Many nature enthusiasts enjoy watching the ringlet among the tall grasses.
We spotted several ringlets near the edge of the woodland trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meadow brown - a common brown butterfly also in Satyrinae
  • Synonym: gatekeeper - another brown butterfly with eyespots, related species

Etymology

Diminutive of 'ring', referring to the ring-shaped markings (eyespots) on the butterfly's wings.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02280394-n
Synset POS: n
