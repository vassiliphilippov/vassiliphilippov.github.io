Saharawi Arab Democratic Republic

1. PROPN

/ səˈhɑːrɑːwi ˈɛrəb ˌdɛm.əˈkræt.ɪk rəˈpʌb.lɪk / (AmE), / səˈhɑːrɑːwi ˈærəb ˌdɛm.əˈkræt.ɪk rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
a partially recognized state in northwest Africa, claiming sovereignty over Western Sahara
partially recognized state in northwest Africa

Examples

The Saharawi Arab Democratic Republic declared independence in 1976, although its status remains disputed.
A number of countries have recognized the Saharawi Arab Democratic Republic as a sovereign state.
Talks for the future of Western Sahara include representatives from the Saharawi Arab Democratic Republic.
