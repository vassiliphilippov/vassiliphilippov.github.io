swizzle

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪz.əl / (AmE), / ˈswɪz.əl / (BrE)
A tall, frothy mixed drink, usually made with rum, lime juice, and sugar, shaken with ice.
a type of mixed drink or cocktail

Examples

He ordered a swizzle at the bar to cool off on a hot evening.
This tropical swizzle was garnished with a fresh sprig of mint.
The recipe calls for shaking the swizzle vigorously to create its signature froth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cocktail - an alcoholic mixed drink

Etymology

Probably from the Caribbean, possibly imitative of the swirling action ('swizzle stick') used in making the drink.
