bright-red

1. ADJ

/ braɪt rɛd / (AmE), / braɪt rɛd / (BrE)
of a vivid, intense red color like that of fresh raspberries or other brilliant red objects
vivid, intense red color

Examples

She wore a bright-red dress that caught everyone's attention at the party.
The artist used a bright-red paint for the bold strokes in his latest piece.
A bright-red apple stood out among the green leaves on the tree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scarlet - a brilliant red color with a slightly orange tinge
  • Synonym: crimson - a rich deep red color inclining to purple
  • Degree Lower: pale red - a lighter, less saturated shade of red
