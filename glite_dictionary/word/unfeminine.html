unfeminine

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfɛmɪnɪn / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɛmɪnɪn / (BrE)
not typical of or suitable for a woman; lacking qualities traditionally associated with femininity.
not characteristic of a woman

Examples

Her unfeminine laugh surprised everyone at the formal dinner.
Some people considered her choice of career to be unfeminine in that era.
He was struck by the unfeminine strength of her handshake.

Synset Examples

an unfeminine depth of voice

Related Terms

  • Antonym: feminine - having qualities traditionally associated with women

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'feminine' (from Latin 'femininus', pertaining to women).
