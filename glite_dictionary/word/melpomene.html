Melpomene

1. PROPN

/ mɛlˈpɑməni / (AmE), / mɛlˈpɒməni / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) the Muse of tragedy, often depicted with a tragic mask.
Greek Muse of tragedy

Examples

In the painting, Melpomene holds a tragic mask, symbolizing her role as the Muse of tragedy.
The playwright invoked Melpomene for inspiration while composing his latest drama.
A statue of Melpomene stands in the foyer of the national theatre.

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Μελπομένη (Melpomenē), meaning 'the celebrator' or 'the melodious one'.
