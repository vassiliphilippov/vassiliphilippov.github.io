priceless

1. ADJ

/ ˈpraɪsləs / (AmE), / ˈpraɪslɪs / (BrE)
So valuable that its worth cannot be calculated or measured; having incalculable monetary, intellectual, or spiritual value.
of incalculable value

Examples

The museum's collection contains several priceless artifacts dating back thousands of years.
Her advice to me at that difficult time proved absolutely priceless.
The look of joy on his face was simply priceless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invaluable - extremely valuable; beyond calculable worth
  • Antonym: worthless - having no value or use

Etymology

From price + -less, meaning 'without price.'
