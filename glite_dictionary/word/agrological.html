agrological

1. ADJ

/ ˌæɡ.rəˈlɑ.dʒɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌæɡ.rəˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to agrology, the science of soils in relation to crop production
relating to agrology (soil science and agriculture)

Examples

The team conducted an agrological survey to determine the best crops for the region.
Her research focuses on agrological factors affecting soil fertility.
We attended a seminar on agrological methods in sustainable agriculture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agrology - the science of soils in relation to crop production

Etymology

From agrology + -ical, patterned after scientific terms.
