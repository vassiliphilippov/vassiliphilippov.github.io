unabashedly

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnəˈbæʃɪdli / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈbæʃɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows no embarrassment, shame, or hesitation.
without embarrassment or shame

Examples

He unabashedly took credit for the team's success.
She unabashedly expressed her opinions at the meeting.
The child unabashedly sang in front of the entire class.

Synset Examples

unabashedly, he asked for more

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boldly - in a confident and courageous way
  • Synonym: shamelessly - in a way that shows no shame or embarrassment
  • Antonym: bashfully - in a shy or embarrassed manner

Etymology

Formed from 'unabashed' (not embarrassed) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
