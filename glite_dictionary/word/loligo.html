loligo

1. NOUN

/ loʊˈliːɡoʊ / (AmE), / ləˈliːɡəʊ / (BrE)
a genus of squid with a somewhat flattened, cylindrical body
a genus of flattened cylindrical squid

Examples

The biologist studied the behavior of the loligo during its migration season.
Fishermen often catch loligo along the Mediterranean coast.
The anatomy of a loligo includes a streamlined, cylindrical body and elongated fins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squid - a marine cephalopod mollusk with eight arms and two longer tentacles

Etymology

From Latin 'loligo', meaning squid.
