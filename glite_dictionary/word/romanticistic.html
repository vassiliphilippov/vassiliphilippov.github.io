romanticistic

1. ADJ

/ roʊˌmæntɪˈsɪstɪk / (AmE), / rəʊˌmantɪˈsɪstɪk / (BrE)
Belonging to or characteristic of Romanticism or the Romantic Movement in the arts.
of or relating to Romanticism in the arts

Examples

The artist adopted a distinctly romanticistic style in her latest paintings.
His romanticistic approach to music emphasized emotion over structure.
Critics noted the romanticistic themes present throughout the novel.

Synset Examples

romantic poetry

Related Terms

  • Synonym: romantic - relating to or characteristic of Romance or Romanticism
  • Antonym: classicistic - belonging to or characteristic of Classicism in the arts

Etymology

From 'Romanticism' + '-istic', indicating relation to a movement.
