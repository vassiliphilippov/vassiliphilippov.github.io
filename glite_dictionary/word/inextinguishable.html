inextinguishable

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.ɛkˈstɪŋ.ɡwɪ.ʃə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.ɛksˈtɪŋ.ɡwɪ.ʃə.bəl / (BrE)
Impossible to extinguish or put out; cannot be quenched or ended.
impossible to extinguish

Examples

The firefighters struggled against the inextinguishable blaze that raged for days.
She had an inextinguishable hope that things would get better.
His inextinguishable laughter filled the entire room.

Synset Examples

an inextinguishable flame
an inextinguishable faith

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unquenchable - impossible to satisfy or put out
  • Antonym: extinguishable - capable of being extinguished or put out

Etymology

From Latin 'inextinguibilis', from 'in-' (not) + 'extinguere' (to extinguish) + '-ibilis' (able to be).
