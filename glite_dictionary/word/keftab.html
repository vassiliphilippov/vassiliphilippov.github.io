Keftab

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛf.tæb / (AmE), / ˈkɛf.tæb / (BrE)
A trademarked cephalosporin antibiotic used to treat mild to moderately severe infections of the skin, ears, throat, lungs, or urinary tract.
trade name for a cephalosporin antibiotic
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Keftab to treat her persistent ear infection.
Patients allergic to penicillin should inform their physician before taking Keftab.
After a week on Keftab, his skin infection showed significant improvement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Keflex - another trade name for cephalexin, a cephalosporin antibiotic
  • Synonym: Keflin - trade name for cephalothin, a cephalosporin antibiotic
