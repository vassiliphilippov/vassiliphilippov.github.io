chiasmal

1. ADJ

/ kaɪˈæzməl / (AmE), / kaɪˈæzməl / (BrE)
relating to a chiasm, especially the optic chiasm in anatomy
relating to a chiasm (especially anatomical)
medical

Examples

The patient exhibited visual field deficits due to a chiasmal lesion.
Tumors in the chiasmal region can affect both eyes.
Doctors monitored the growth of the chiasmal mass with regular scans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chiasmatic - relating to a chiasm or crossing, often used synonymously with chiasmal

Etymology

From chiasm + -al, indicating relation to a crossing or intersection (from Greek 'chiasma').
