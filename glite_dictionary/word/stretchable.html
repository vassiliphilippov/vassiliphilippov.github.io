stretchable

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrɛtʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈstrɛtʃəbl / (BrE)
able to be easily stretched and able to return to its original size or shape
able to be stretched and return to original shape

Examples

These stretchable bands are perfect for resistance training exercises.
The fabric is highly stretchable, making the shirt comfortable to wear.
A stretchable phone case fits different models easily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elastic - able to resume normal shape after being stretched

Etymology

From 'stretch' + '-able', where '-able' means 'capable of'.
