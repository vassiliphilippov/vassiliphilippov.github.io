Zannichellia

1. NOUN

/ zænəˈkɛliə / (AmE), / zanɪˈkɛlɪə / (BrE)
A genus of aquatic plants commonly known as horned pondweed, found in freshwater and brackish environments.
genus of aquatic plants (horned pondweed)

Examples

Zannichellia is commonly found in slow-moving streams and ponds across Europe and North America.
The genus Zannichellia includes several species of submerged aquatic plants.
Researchers studied the distribution of Zannichellia in coastal wetlands to monitor water quality.

Etymology

Named after Giovanni Gerolamo Zannichelli, an Italian botanist.
