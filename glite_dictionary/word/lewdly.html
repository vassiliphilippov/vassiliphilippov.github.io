lewdly

1. ADV

/ ˈluːdli / (AmE), / ˈluːdli / (BrE)
In a sexually indecent or obscene manner.
in a sexually indecent manner

Examples

He glanced lewdly at the dancers as they passed by.
The actor was criticized for behaving lewdly on stage during the performance.
She accused him of speaking to her lewdly at the office party.

Synset Examples

he had seen how in their dances the white men and women held one another obscenely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obscenely - in an obscene or offensive manner
  • Antonym: modestly - in a reserved or decent manner

Etymology

Formed from 'lewd' + '-ly'. 'Lewd' from Old English *lǣwede* (unlearned, lay), later evolving to mean 'base' or 'lascivious'.
