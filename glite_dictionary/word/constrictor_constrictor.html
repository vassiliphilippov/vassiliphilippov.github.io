Constrictor constrictor

1. NOUN

/ kənˈstrɪktər kənˈstrɪktər / (AmE), / kənˈstrɪktə kənˈstrɪktə / (BrE)
a very large snake of tropical America and the West Indies, commonly known as the boa constrictor
large boa snake of tropical America

Examples

The Constrictor constrictor is capable of growing over three meters in length.
Locals often refer to the Constrictor constrictor as simply the boa snake.
Researchers observed the hunting behavior of the Constrictor constrictor in the rainforest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boa constrictor - large nonvenomous snake that kills by constriction

Etymology

From Latin, meaning 'the constrictor constrictor,' using the species naming convention in taxonomy.
