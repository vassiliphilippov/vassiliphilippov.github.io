2. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈkrɪstəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkrɪstəlaɪzd / (BrE)
not having a crystalline structure; lacking real or apparent crystal form.
not in a crystalline form

Examples

The mineral in its uncrystallized state is difficult to identify.
Some sugars are sold in an uncrystallized form to keep them smooth and spreadable.
The scientist examined the uncrystallized residue left after the reaction.

Synset Examples

an amorphous mineral
amorphous structure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amorphous - lacking a clear shape or form, especially without crystalline structure
  • Antonym: crystallized - having a clear crystalline structure

Etymology

Formed with prefix un- (not) + crystallized.
