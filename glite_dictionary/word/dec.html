Dec

1. NOUN

/ dɛk / (AmE), / dɛk / (BrE)
Abbreviation for December, the twelfth and final month of the year.
abbreviation for December (12th month)

Examples

My birthday is in Dec, just before the holidays.
The meeting will be held on Dec 15th this year.
We usually get our Christmas tree in early Dec.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: December - the twelfth month of the year

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'December', from Latin 'decem' (ten), as December was the tenth month in the Roman calendar.
