salted

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɔltɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɔːltɪd / (BrE)
(of food, especially meats) preserved or cured with salt.
preserved or cured with salt

Examples

The chef prepared a traditional dish using salted pork and beans.
Before refrigeration, people often relied on salted fish to keep protein through the winter.
She prefers salted meats for their unique flavor and long shelf life.

Related Terms
