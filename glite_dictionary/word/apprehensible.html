apprehensible

1. ADJ

/ ˌæprɪˈhɛnsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌaprɪˈhɛnsɪbəl / (BrE)
able to be understood or grasped mentally; comprehensible.
able to be understood

Examples

The instructions were written in an apprehensible manner for all students.
Despite the complex topic, the lecture remained apprehensible to most of the audience.
Her explanation made the complicated subject much more apprehensible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comprehensible - able to be understood; intelligible
  • Antonym: incomprehensible - impossible or very difficult to understand

Etymology

From Latin apprehensibilis, from apprehendere (to seize, grasp).
