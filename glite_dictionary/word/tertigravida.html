tertigravida

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɜrtɪˈɡrævɪdə / (AmE), / ˌtɜːtɪˈɡrævɪdə / (BrE)
A woman who is pregnant for the third time.
woman pregnant for third time
medical

Examples

The doctor noted that the patient was a tertigravida at her first antenatal visit.
As a tertigravida, she was familiar with the prenatal process and requirements.
Statistics show an increase in caesarean sections among tertigravida women.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: primigravida - a woman pregnant for the first time
  • Degree Lower: secundigravida - a woman pregnant for the second time

Etymology

From Latin 'terti-' (third) + 'gravidus' (pregnant).
