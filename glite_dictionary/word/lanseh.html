lanseh

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæn.seɪ / (AmE), / ˈlan.seɪ / (BrE)
An edible yellow, tart, berrylike fruit native to Southeast Asia, especially known in India and Indonesia.
a tart, yellow berrylike fruit of Southeast Asia

Examples

The tropical market was selling fresh lanseh along with other exotic fruits.
Many dishes in Indonesia use lanseh to add a tangy flavor.
She described the taste of lanseh as a blend of grape and grapefruit.
