calfskin

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæf.skɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɑːf.skɪn / (BrE)
fine leather made from the hide of a young calf, often valued for its smooth texture and durability
leather from the skin of a young calf

Examples

The gloves were made of the finest calfskin and felt incredibly soft.
He purchased a pair of elegant calfskin shoes for the wedding.
The book was bound in dark brown calfskin, giving it a classic appearance.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: leather - material made from animal hide, tanned for use
