yoga

1. NOUN

/ ˈjoʊɡə / (AmE), / ˈjəʊɡə / (BrE)
A Hindu system of physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation aimed at promoting physical and mental well-being.
system of physical and mental exercises

Examples

She attends yoga classes three times a week to improve her flexibility.
Practicing yoga regularly can help reduce stress and increase mindfulness.
Many people find that yoga enhances both their physical health and emotional balance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meditation - the practice of focusing the mind for relaxation or spiritual purposes

Etymology

From Sanskrit yoga (union, joining), related to yuj (to yoke, join, concentrate).

2. NOUN

/ ˈjoʊɡə / (AmE), / ˈjəʊɡə / (BrE)
an ancient system of physical, mental, and spiritual practices originating in India, aimed at attaining harmony, insight, and tranquility of body and mind
Hindu discipline of physical and spiritual practice

Examples

He practices yoga every morning to relax and clear his mind.
Many people turn to yoga to reduce stress and improve flexibility.
The philosophy behind yoga emphasizes both physical posture and spiritual development.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meditation - the practice of focusing the mind to achieve mental clarity or relaxation

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'yoga' (union, yoking together).
