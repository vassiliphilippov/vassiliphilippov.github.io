Coereba

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈɛrɪbə / (AmE), / kəʊˈɛrɪbə / (BrE)
A genus of small tropical birds in the family Coerebidae, including the bananaquit.
genus of tropical birds (bananaquit)

Examples

The Coereba genus is best known for the bananaquit, a common bird in tropical regions.
Scientists classified the new species under the existing Coereba genus after extensive research.
Birdwatchers were thrilled to spot a Coereba feeding on nectar in the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bananaquit - a small, active songbird of the genus Coereba

Etymology

Modern Latin, from Greek koirēbos (herald).
