streaked

1. ADJ

/ striːkt / (AmE), / striːkt / (BrE)
marked with lines, stripes, or long narrow stains or discolorations
marked with stripes or lines

Examples

The painter left the wall looking streaked after the rain washed over it.
Her blond hair was streaked with pink dye for a bold new look.
The glass was streaked with fingerprints and needed to be cleaned.

Synset Examples

streaked hair
fat legs and dirty streaky faces

Related Terms

  • Synonym: striped - marked or decorated with stripes
  • Antonym: spotless - completely clean; with no marks or stains

Etymology

From the verb 'streak' + -ed (forming the past participle or adjective).
