chart-topping

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɑrt ˌtɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːt ˌtɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a song, album, etc.) reaching number one in the music charts; extremely popular or successful in terms of music sales or streams.
reaching number one in music charts

Examples

The band released a chart-topping album that stayed at number one for weeks.
Her latest single became a chart-topping sensation across Europe and the US.
He is known for producing chart-topping tracks with several popular artists.

Synset Examples

The band released a chart-topping album last year.
Her new single is a chart-topping hit.
They celebrated their chart-topping success together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hit - a song or album that is very successful

Etymology

From 'chart' (as in music chart) + 'topping' (reaching the top).
