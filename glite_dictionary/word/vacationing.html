vacationing

1. NOUN

/ veɪˈkeɪʃənɪŋ / (AmE), / vəˈkeɪʃənɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of taking a vacation or spending time away from work, often for leisure or travel
the activity of taking a vacation

Examples

Many families enjoy vacationing at the beach during summer months.
She spent two weeks vacationing in Italy with her friends.
Despite vacationing far from home, he checked his emails daily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traveling - the act of making a journey, especially to distant places
  • Antonym: working - the act of engaging in work or labor

Etymology

From the verb 'vacation' + suffix '-ing', denoting the action or process.
