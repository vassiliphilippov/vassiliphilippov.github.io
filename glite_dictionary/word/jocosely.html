jocosely

1. ADV

/ dʒoʊˈkoʊsli / (AmE), / dʒəˈkəʊsli / (BrE)
in a humorous or joking manner
in a humorous or joking way

Examples

She replied jocosely, lightening the mood in the room.
Although he criticized the proposal, he did so jocosely, not intending to offend anyone.
Their debate was carried out jocosely, with both sides exchanging witty remarks.

Synset Examples

they tried to deal with this painful subject jocularly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jocularly - in a joking or humorous way

Etymology

From Latin 'jocosus' (full of jest, humorous) plus English adverbial suffix '-ly'.
