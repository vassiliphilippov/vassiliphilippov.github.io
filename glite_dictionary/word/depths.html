depths

1. NOUN

/ dɛpθs / (AmE), / dɛpθs / (BrE)
the deepest or most remote parts of something, often used figuratively or literally to refer to places that are farthest in or most concealed.
the deepest or most remote parts

Examples

Rescuers searched the depths of the ocean for the missing submarine.
The novel explores the depths of human emotion.
Astronomers detected a distant signal from the depths of the universe.

Synset Examples

from the depths of darkest Africa
signals received from the depths of space

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abyss - an extremely deep or bottomless chasm
