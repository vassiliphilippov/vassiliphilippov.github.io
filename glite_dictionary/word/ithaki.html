Ithaki

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈθɑːki / (AmE), / ɪˈθæki / (BrE)
A Greek island to the west of mainland Greece, traditionally known as the home of Odysseus in Homeric legend.
Greek island; home of Odysseus

Examples

Many tourists visit Ithaki each year to explore its beautiful beaches and historical sites.
According to ancient Greek mythology, Ithaki was the homeland of the hero Odysseus.
After years of wandering, Odysseus finally returned to Ithaki in the Odyssey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Odysseus - Legendary Greek hero, king of Ithaki in Homer's epic
  • Synonym: Ithaca - Alternative English spelling of Ithaki; Greek island in Homeric legend

Etymology

From Greek Ιθάκη (Itháki).
