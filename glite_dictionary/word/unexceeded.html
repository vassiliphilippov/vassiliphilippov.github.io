unexceeded

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪkˈsiːdɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈsiːdɪd / (BrE)
not surpassed or bettered; unmatched in quality or achievement.
not surpassed; unmatched

Examples

The artist's talent remains unexceeded to this day.
The team achieved an unexceeded level of success during the season.
Her generosity is unexceeded among her peers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unsurpassed - not bettered or equaled; the best so far
  • Synonym: incomparable - without equal or match; unique
  • Antonym: matched - equaled or surpassed

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'exceeded' (surpassed).
