n'est-ce pas

1. X

/ nɛs pɑ / (AmE), / nɛs pɑ / (BrE)
A French expression used in English to ask for confirmation or agreement, meaning 'isn't it?' or 'right?'
French tag meaning 'isn't it?'

Examples

The evening was unforgettable, n'est-ce pas?
You've heard this song before, n'est-ce pas?
She is an excellent chef, n'est-ce pas?

Synset Examples

It's a beautiful day, n'est-ce pas?
You love this movie, n'est-ce pas?
This cake is delicious, n'est-ce pas?

Etymology

From French 'n'est-ce pas' meaning 'is it not?'
