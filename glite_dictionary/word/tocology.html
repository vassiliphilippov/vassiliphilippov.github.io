tocology

1. NOUN

/ toʊˈkɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / təˈkɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with childbirth and the care of women during labor and delivery
study of childbirth and maternal care
medical

Examples

She decided to specialize in tocology during her medical studies.
Advances in tocology have greatly reduced maternal and infant mortality rates.
Her research in tocology focuses on safe practices during labor and delivery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obstetrics - the branch of medicine and surgery concerned with childbirth and midwifery

Etymology

From Greek *tokos* (childbirth) + *-logia* (study of).
