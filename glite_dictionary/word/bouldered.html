bouldered

1. ADJ

/ ˈboʊldərd / (AmE), / ˈbəʊldəd / (BrE)
(of land or a surface) covered with many boulders or large rocks.
covered with boulders

Examples

The climbers made their way carefully across the bouldered slope.
A narrow path wound through a bouldered valley dotted with wildflowers.
We stopped to rest beside a bouldered shoreline, watching the waves break on the rocks.

Synset Examples

rocky fields
stony ground
bouldery beaches

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rocky - covered with or full of rocks
  • Synonym: stony - covered with or full of stones
