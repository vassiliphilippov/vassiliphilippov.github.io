dottle

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑt̬.əl / (AmE), / ˈdɒt.əl / (BrE)
the unburned or partially burnt tobacco residue left in the bowl of a pipe after smoking
residue of tobacco in a smoked pipe

Examples

He tapped the dottle from his pipe before refilling it.
After a long smoke, only a bit of dottle remained in the bowl.
The old pipe was clogged with years of hardened dottle.
