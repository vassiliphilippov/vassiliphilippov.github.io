knucklehead

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌk.əlˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈnʌk.əlˌhɛd / (BrE)
An informal word for a person who is not very intelligent; a fool or simpleton.
stupid or foolish person (informal)
informal

Examples

Don't be such a knucklehead—read the instructions before you start assembling the desk.
He forgot his keys again; what a knucklehead!
Sometimes, her little brother acts like a real knucklehead around his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence
