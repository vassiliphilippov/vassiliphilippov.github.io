piousness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪ.əs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ.əs.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being pious; showing deep religious devotion or reverence.
quality of being pious or devout

Examples

Her piousness was evident in her daily prayers and charitable acts.
The community respected his piousness and sought his advice on spiritual matters.
Despite their piousness, they avoided judging others for their beliefs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piety - devoutness; the quality of being religious or reverent

Etymology

From pious + -ness, modeled after similar noun-forming patterns in English.
