Proserpina

1. PROPN

/ proʊˈsɜːrpɪnə / (AmE), / prəˈsɜːpɪnə / (BrE)
the Roman goddess of the underworld and springtime, identified with the Greek Persephone
Roman goddess of the underworld and springtime

Examples

Many ancient Roman myths describe Proserpina's abduction by Pluto.
The festival honoring Proserpina was celebrated each spring in Rome.
As the counterpart of Persephone, Proserpina played a vital role in Roman mythology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Persephone - Greek goddess of the underworld, equivalent to Proserpina

Etymology

From Latin Proserpina, the name of the Roman goddess.
