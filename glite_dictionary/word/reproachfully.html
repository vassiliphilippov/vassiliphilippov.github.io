reproachfully

1. ADV

/ rɪˈproʊtʃfəli / (AmE), / rɪˈprəʊtʃfəli / (BrE)
In a way that expresses disapproval or disappointment.
in a disapproving or disappointed way

Examples

She looked at him reproachfully after he forgot her birthday.
The teacher sighed reproachfully when the student arrived late again.
He answered his mother's question reproachfully, feeling misunderstood.

Synset Examples

she spoke to him reprovingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reprovingly - in a reproving manner

Etymology

From "reproachful" + "-ly"; "reproachful" from Old French "reproche" (reproach) + -ful.
