Matakam

1. NOUN

/ ˈmætəkæm / (AmE), / ˈmatəkam / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken south of Lake Chad, particularly in the Far North Region of Cameroon, mainly around Mokolo town
a Chadic language of Cameroon

Examples

The Matakam language is primarily spoken in the Mayo-Tsanaga division of Cameroon.
Several linguists have studied the grammar and vocabulary of Matakam.
Children in rural communities often grow up speaking Matakam at home.
