fueled

1. ADJ

/ ˈfjuəld / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəld / (BrE)
(of a machine, process, or event) heated, powered, or produced by burning fuel or by a source of energy.
powered or produced by fuel

Examples

The station is entirely fueled by natural gas, making it more efficient.
Her anger was fueled by rumors circulating around the office.
The company developed a new type of battery-fueled vehicle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powered - supplied with power, especially mechanical or electrical
