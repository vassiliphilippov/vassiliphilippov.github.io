recognisable

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɛkəɡˈnaɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛkəɡˈnaɪzəbəl / (BrE)
Able to be identified or acknowledged as being something or someone known before.
capable of being recognized
british

Examples

The Eiffel Tower is instantly recognisable as a symbol of Paris.
Her voice was faint but still recognisable over the phone.
The brand’s logo is highly recognisable even from a distance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recognizable - Able to be identified as something or someone previously seen or known (American spelling)
  • Antonym: unrecognisable - Not able to be recognized

Etymology

From 'recognize' + '-able'; common British spelling variant of 'recognizable'.
