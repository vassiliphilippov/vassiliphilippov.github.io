social

1. ADJ

/ ˈsoʊ.ʃəl / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ʃəl / (BrE)
(of activities or events) involving friendly companionship or interaction with others.
involving companionship with others

Examples

We had a social lunch with old friends at the park.
He prefers a social evening with neighbors to spending time alone.
Let’s have a social drink after work and catch up.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unsocial - not enjoying or seeking friendly companionship

Etymology

From Latin socialis, from socius (companion, ally).
