mind-expanding

1. ADJ

/ ˈmaɪnd ɪkˌspændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnd ɪkˌspændɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a drug or experience) giving a sense of heightened or broader awareness, often through altering perception or consciousness.
giving heightened or broader awareness

Examples

Some people believe that mind-expanding substances can lead to personal growth.
The artist described her trip to India as a truly mind-expanding experience.
He writes about the mind-expanding effects of certain psychedelic drugs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hallucinogenic - causing hallucinations or altered perceptions
