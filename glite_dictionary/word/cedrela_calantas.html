Cedrela calantas

1. NOUN

/ səˈdrɛlə kəˈlæntəs / (AmE), / sɪˈdriːlə kəˈlæntəs / (BrE)
A Philippine timber tree known for its hard, red, fragrant wood.
Philippine timber tree with fragrant red wood

Examples

The wood of Cedrela calantas is highly valued for making furniture and cabinets.
Many forests in the Philippines are home to the Cedrela calantas tree.
Exporters specialize in trading lumber from Cedrela calantas due to its quality and fragrance.
