Alessandro Cagliostro

1. PROPN

/ əˌlɛsɑnˌdroʊ kɑːlˈjoʊstroʊ / (AmE), / ˌæləsˈændrəʊ kæliˈɒstrəʊ / (BrE)
(1743–1795) An Italian adventurer and self-styled magician and alchemist, famous for his involvement in scandals and occult practices.
Italian adventurer and magician (1743–1795)

Examples

Many legends surround Alessandro Cagliostro and his so-called magical powers.
The trial of Alessandro Cagliostro captivated the public in 18th-century Europe.
Writers often use Alessandro Cagliostro as a symbol of mysticism and deception.
