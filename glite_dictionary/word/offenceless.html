offenceless

1. ADJ

/ əˈfɛns·ləs / (AmE), / əˈfɛns·ləs / (BrE)
Incapable of offending or attacking; harmless or inoffensive.
incapable of offending or attacking

Examples

She looked at him with offenceless eyes, seeking only peace.
In his offenceless state, he posed no threat to anyone around him.
The animal seemed offenceless, lying quietly in the grass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harmless - not able or likely to cause harm
  • Synonym: defenceless - without protection or defense

Etymology

Formed from 'offence' + '-less', meaning 'without offence'.
