African-American

1. ADJ

/ ˌæf.rɪ.kən əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən / (AmE), / ˌæf.rɪ.kən əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən / (BrE)
Relating to Americans of African ancestry or their culture.
of or relating to Black Americans

Examples

The city is known for its vibrant African-American cultural heritage.
Many famous jazz musicians are African-American.
The museum features African-American history exhibits.

Synset Examples

African-American culture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Black - Relating to people of African ancestry, especially in the US

Etymology

First used in the late 20th century, formed from 'African' + 'American' to highlight both heritage and nationality.
