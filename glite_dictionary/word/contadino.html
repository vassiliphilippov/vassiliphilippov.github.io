contadino

1. NOUN

/ koʊn.tɑːˈdiː.noʊ / (AmE), / kɒn.tɑːˈdiː.nəʊ / (BrE)
an Italian farmer, especially one working on a small farm or as a peasant
Italian peasant farmer

Examples

The museum exhibit features tools once used by a contadino in rural Tuscany.
After lunch, the contadini returned to the vineyards to tend the grapes.
She learned traditional recipes from her grandfather, who was a contadino all his life.

Etymology

Italian, from Latin 'contadinus' (from 'contado' countryside).
