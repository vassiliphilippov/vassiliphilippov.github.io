Lopid

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊpɪd / (AmE), / ˈləʊpɪd / (BrE)
a trademarked medicine (gemfibrozil) used to reduce triglyceride levels in the blood
trademarked medicine for lowering triglycerides
medical

Examples

Some patients with high cholesterol are prescribed Lopid to help lower their triglyceride levels.
After taking Lopid, his blood test showed significantly reduced triglycerides.
The doctor decided to switch her to Lopid after other medications proved ineffective.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gemfibrozil - the generic name of the drug sold as Lopid
