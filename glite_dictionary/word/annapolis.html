Annapolis

1. PROPN

/ əˈnæpəlɪs / (AmE), / əˈnapəlɪs / (BrE)
The capital city of the U.S. state of Maryland, known for being the home of the United States Naval Academy.
capital of Maryland, U.S.

Examples

The United States Naval Academy is located in Annapolis, Maryland.
After graduating, she moved to Annapolis to work in state government.
Historic mansions and colonial buildings make Annapolis a popular tourist destination.

Etymology

From Greek 'polis' (city), with reference to Princess Anne of Denmark.
