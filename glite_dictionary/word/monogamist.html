monogamist

1. NOUN

/ məˈnɑːɡəmɪst / (AmE), / məˈnɒɡəmɪst / (BrE)
a person who is married to or has a sexual relationship with only one partner at a time
person practicing monogamy

Examples

Sarah has always considered herself a proud monogamist and values committed relationships.
In many societies, being a monogamist is the cultural norm.
He identified as a monogamist despite pressures to adopt other lifestyles.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: polygamist - a person married to or having sexual relationships with more than one partner at a time
  • Synonym: monogamy - the practice of being married to only one person at a time

Etymology

From Greek monogamos (married to one) + -ist (one who practices).
