feist

1. NOUN

/ faɪst / (AmE), / faɪst / (BrE)
a small, energetic, and often noisy mongrel dog, typically characterized by nervous or excitable behavior
small, energetic mongrel dog

Examples

The little feist barked furiously at anyone who passed by the yard.
He adopted a friendly feist from the animal shelter last month.
Though small, the feist had a big personality and kept the entire house lively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mongrel - a dog of mixed or unknown breed
  • Degree Higher: terrier - a breed of small energetic dog often used for hunting vermin

Etymology

Probably from obsolete English 'fysting' (breaking wind), later associated with small dogs' behavior.
