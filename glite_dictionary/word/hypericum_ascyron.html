Hypericum ascyron

1. NOUN

/ haɪˈpɪrɪkəm ˈæskɪrɒn / (AmE), / hɪˈpɪərɪkəm ˈaskɪrən / (BrE)
A perennial shrub with large star-shaped yellow flowers arranged in narrowly pyramidal cymes, native to parts of Asia and North America.
perennial shrub with yellow star-shaped flowers

Examples

The garden's centerpiece was a magnificent Hypericum ascyron in full bloom.
Botanists studied Hypericum ascyron due to its uniquely shaped yellow flowers.
We planted Hypericum ascyron along the path to add color during summer.

Etymology

From genus name 'Hypericum,' of Greek origin, and species epithet 'ascyron,' from Greek possibly meaning 'not shade-enduring.'
