ventilated

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɛn.təˌleɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈvɛn.tɪ.leɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
(of a room or building) provided with a supply of fresh air; exposed to air.
supplied with fresh air; exposed to air

Examples

The hospital was designed to ensure each ward was ventilated and comfortable for patients.
It's important to keep your kitchen ventilated while cooking to avoid lingering smells.
After the renovation, the basement finally became a dry, ventilated storage space.

Synset Examples

a well ventilated room

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airy - having plenty of fresh air; well-ventilated
  • Antonym: stuffy - (of a room) lacking fresh air; poorly ventilated

Etymology

From Latin ventilare (to fan, ventilate) + English -ed.
