Isoetes

1. NOUN

/ aɪˈsoʊətiːz / (AmE), / aɪˈsəʊətiːz / (BrE)
A genus of aquatic or semi-aquatic plants, commonly known as quillworts, and the only living genus in the family Isoetaceae.
genus of aquatic plants (quillworts)

Examples

The wetland was home to several rare species, including the distinctive Isoetes plants.
Botanists discovered a new population of Isoetes in the remote mountain lake.
Many Isoetes species are considered endangered due to habitat loss.

Etymology

From Greek 'isos' (equal) and 'etos' (year), referring to perennial nature.
