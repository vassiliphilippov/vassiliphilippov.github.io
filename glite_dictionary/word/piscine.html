piscine

1. ADJ

/ ˈpaɪˌsin / (AmE), / ˈpɪsiːn / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of fish.
relating to fish

Examples

The aquarium housed several piscine species native to the Amazon River.
Scientists conducted research into piscine behavior under different environmental conditions.
Her piscine mural brought an underwater world to life on the classroom wall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fishy - smelling, tasting, or resembling fish

Etymology

From Latin 'piscis' (fish) via French 'piscin(e)'.
