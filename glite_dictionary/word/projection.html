projection

1. NOUN

/ prəˈdʒɛkʃən / (AmE), / prəˈdʒɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act of displaying an image, movie, or other visual representation onto a screen using light or electronic means
displaying an image on a screen

Examples

The projection of the movie was clear and bright, making the experience enjoyable for the audience.
During the lecture, the projection of slides onto the screen helped illustrate the key points.
A technical problem delayed the projection of the film at the cinema.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: display - the act of visually presenting something
  • Synonym: projectionist - a person who operates a film projector

Etymology

From Latin 'projectio', from 'proicere' meaning 'to throw forth'.
