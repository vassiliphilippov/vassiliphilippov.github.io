Wb

1. NOUN

/ wiː ˈbɪər / (AmE), / wiː ˈbɪə / (BrE)
the SI unit of magnetic flux, equal to one weber or 100,000,000 maxwells
SI unit of magnetic flux

Examples

The measurement of magnetic flux is denoted in Wb for scientific accuracy.
The coil produced a magnetic flux of 0.5 Wb during the experiment.
Maximum output should not exceed 2 Wb according to the specifications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weber - the SI unit of magnetic flux

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'weber', named after German physicist Wilhelm Eduard Weber (1804–1891).
