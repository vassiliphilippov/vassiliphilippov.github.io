unneighbourly

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈneɪbɚˌli / (AmE), / ʌnˈneɪb(ə)li / (BrE)
Not friendly, helpful, or considerate as a neighbor should be.
not friendly or helpful as a neighbor
british

Examples

It was unneighbourly of him to complain about the children playing in the garden.
Her unneighbourly attitude made it difficult for others to approach her.
Leaving rubbish outside the building is considered very unneighbourly.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: neighbourly - friendly, helpful, and considerate as a neighbor
  • Synonym: unfriendly - not friendly or kind

Etymology

Formed by combining the prefix 'un-' (not) with 'neighbourly'.
