competitively

1. ADV

/ kəmˈpɛt̬.ə.tɪv.li / (AmE), / kəmˈpɛt.ɪ.tɪv.li / (BrE)
in a way that involves or relates to competition; in competition with others
in competition with others

Examples

All the teams played competitively throughout the tournament.
She priced her products competitively to attract more customers.
The firm must bid competitively to win the government contract.

Synset Examples

the companies should bid competitively

Related Terms

  • Antonym: noncompetitively - in a way that does not involve competition
  • Synonym: competitively - in a manner showing competition

Etymology

From 'competitive' + '-ly', forming the adverbial form.
