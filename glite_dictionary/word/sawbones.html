sawbones

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔˌboʊnz / (AmE), / ˈsɔːbəʊnz / (BrE)
an informal or old-fashioned term for a surgeon; a physician who specializes in surgery
informal term for a surgeon
informal

Examples

The wounded soldier was taken straight to the sawbones for an emergency operation.
Despite his rough manner, the old sawbones saved countless lives during the war.
In frontier towns, a sawbones was often the only medical help available.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surgeon - a physician who performs surgery

Etymology

Originally slang, from 'saw' + 'bones', referring to the amputation of limbs by early surgeons.
