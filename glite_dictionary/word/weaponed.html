weaponed

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛpənd / (AmE), / ˈwɛpənd / (BrE)
carrying or equipped with weapons.
carrying weapons

Examples

The knights rode into battle weaponed and ready for the enemy.
He wandered the forest, constantly weaponed against any threat.
The book depicts a weaponed figure standing guard at the gate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armed - carrying weapons; equipped for combat

Etymology

Formed from 'weapon' + '-ed' (adjectival suffix).
