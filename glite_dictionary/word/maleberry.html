maleberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪlˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈmeɪlˌbɛri / (BrE)
A deciduous shrub with many branches, bearing dense clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers.
deciduous shrub with white bell-shaped flowers

Examples

The maleberry thrives in acidic soils along woodland edges and wetlands.
In the spring, the maleberry is covered with delicate white flowers.
Gardeners appreciate the maleberry for its ornamental clusters and hardy nature.
