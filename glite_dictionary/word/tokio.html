Tokio

1. PROPN

/ toʊˈki.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.ki.əʊ / (BrE)
An alternative spelling of Tokyo, the capital and largest city of Japan, serving as its economic and cultural center.
alternative spelling of Tokyo, Japan's capital

Examples

Historic maps sometimes mark the Japanese capital as Tokio instead of Tokyo.
The letter was sent from Tokio, arriving in London two weeks later.
In some 19th-century Western texts, Tokio is used instead of modern 'Tokyo'.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tokyo - the capital and largest city of Japan

Etymology

Variant of Japanese 東京 (Tōkyō), meaning 'eastern capital.'
