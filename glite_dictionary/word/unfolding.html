unfolding

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈfoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈfəʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
the gradual development of a situation, story, or process as it becomes clearer or more complete over time
gradual development or revelation

Examples

The unfolding of the investigation kept the public riveted to the news.
We watched the unfolding of events with increasing concern.
The novel captures the unfolding of the protagonist's inner journey.

Synset Examples

the flowering of antebellum culture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: development - the process of growing or becoming more advanced
  • Degree Higher: culmination - the highest or climactic point of something
