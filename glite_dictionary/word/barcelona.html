Barcelona

1. PROPN

/ bɑr.səˈloʊ.nə / (AmE), / ˌbɑː.səˈləʊ.nə / (BrE)
A major city and port in northeastern Spain, on the Mediterranean Sea, known for its cultural, historical, and commercial significance.
major city in northeastern Spain

Examples

Many tourists visit Barcelona each year to see its stunning architecture and vibrant art scene.
After graduating, she moved to Barcelona to work for an international company.
The football club FC Barcelona is famous worldwide for its talented players.

Etymology

From Catalan 'Barcelona', from Latin 'Barcino', the ancient Roman name of the settlement.
