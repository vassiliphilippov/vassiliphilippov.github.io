Jew-bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
a low tropical American shrub with strong emetic properties
a tropical American emetic shrub

Examples

The leaves of the Jew-bush are used in traditional medicine for their emetic effects.
We encountered a Jew-bush growing along the trail in the rainforest.
Researchers studied the chemical compounds found in the Jew-bush to better understand its medicinal properties.
