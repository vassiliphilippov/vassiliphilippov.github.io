Manglietia

1. NOUN

/ mæŋɡliˈɛtiə / (AmE), / maŋɡlɪˈɛtɪə / (BrE)
a genus of flowering trees in the magnolia family (Magnoliaceae), native to regions from the Malay Peninsula to southern China.
genus of Asian magnolia trees

Examples

The Manglietia genus includes several species prized for their attractive flowers and glossy leaves.
Botanists discovered a new species of Manglietia in the forests of southern China.
Many Manglietia trees are cultivated in botanical gardens for their ornamental value.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Magnolia - a related genus of flowering trees and shrubs in the family Magnoliaceae
