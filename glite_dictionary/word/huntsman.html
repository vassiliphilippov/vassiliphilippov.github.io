huntsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌntsmən / (AmE), / ˈhʌntsmən / (BrE)
a man who hunts wild animals, especially for sport
a man who hunts wild animals

Examples

The huntsman tracked the deer through the dense forest at dawn.
In the old tales, a brave huntsman would rescue those lost in the woods.
The local huntsman shared stories of his adventures with the children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hunter - a person who hunts wild animals

Etymology

From Middle English ‘huntesman’, from ‘hunt’ + ‘man’.
