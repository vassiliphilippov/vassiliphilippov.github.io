honey-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈhʌni ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈhʌni ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having the yellowish-brown or golden color of honey
having the color of honey

Examples

The honey-colored highlights in her hair caught the sunlight beautifully.
He admired the honey-colored wood of the old table.
A pair of honey-colored eyes glanced up at him from across the room.

Related Terms
