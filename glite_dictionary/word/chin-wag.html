chin-wag

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪnˌwæɡ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪnˌwæɡ / (BrE)
an informal conversation or chat, especially in a relaxed social setting
informal chat or conversation
informal british

Examples

They enjoyed a long chin-wag over coffee at the café.
Let's have a quick chin-wag before the meeting starts.
After dinner, the old friends settled into their usual chin-wag about work and family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chat - an informal conversation
  • Synonym: conversation - talk between two or more people

Etymology

Informal English, originally from 'chin' (talk; as in moving the chin while talking) plus 'wag' (to move to and fro).
