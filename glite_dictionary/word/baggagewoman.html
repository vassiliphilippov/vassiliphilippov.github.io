baggagewoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæɡɪdʒˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈbæɡɪdʒˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female attendant whose job is to take care of travelers' baggage
female attendant in charge of baggage

Examples

The baggagewoman helped me retrieve my suitcase from the storage room at the station.
As a baggagewoman, she ensured all travelers' belongings were safely loaded onto the train.
The baggagewoman was friendly and efficient during the busy morning rush.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baggageman - male attendant who takes care of baggage

Etymology

Formed from 'baggage' + 'woman', modeled after 'baggageman'.
