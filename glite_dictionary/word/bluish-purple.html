bluish-purple

1. ADJ

/ ˈbluː.ɪʃ ˈpɝː.pəl / (AmE), / ˈbluː.ɪʃ ˈpɜː.pəl / (BrE)
Of a purple color tinged with blue; having a shade between blue and purple.
purple tinged with blue

Examples

The sky deepened to a bluish-purple as the sun set.
She chose a bluish-purple dress for the evening gala.
Clusters of bluish-purple flowers bloomed along the garden path.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: violet - bluish-purple color resembling the flower
  • Degree Lower: bluish - tinged with blue
  • Degree Higher: purplish - tinged with purple, somewhat purple
