Gompers

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡɑmpərz / (AmE), / ˈɡɒmpəz / (BrE)
Samuel Gompers (1850–1924), United States labor leader and founding president of the American Federation of Labor, known for his influential role in American labor history.
American labor leader (1850–1924)

Examples

The influence of Gompers on the American labor movement is still recognized today.
Samuel Gompers was a founder and key leader of the American Federation of Labor.
Historians often discuss Gompers when examining the evolution of labor unions in the United States.
