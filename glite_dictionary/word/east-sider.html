East-sider

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːstˌsaɪdər / (AmE), / ˈiːstˌsaɪdə / (BrE)
a person who lives on the east side of Manhattan in New York City.
resident of Manhattan's east side

Examples

As an East-sider, she enjoyed easy access to Central Park and the Metropolitan Museum of Art.
Many East-siders prefer walking to work rather than taking the subway.
He always jokes that being an East-sider means never having to cross town for great food.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: West-sider - resident of the west side of Manhattan
