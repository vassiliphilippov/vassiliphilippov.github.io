goosy

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡuːsi / (AmE), / ˈɡuːsi / (BrE)
silly or foolish; showing lack of good sense or judgment.
silly or foolish
informal

Examples

He felt a bit goosy wearing the bright costume in front of everyone.
That was a goosy mistake, but we all make them sometimes.
Don't be goosy—think before you answer the question.

Synset Examples

ridiculous anserine behavior

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silly - showing lack of good sense; foolish
  • Synonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment
  • Antonym: sensible - having or showing good sense or judgment

Etymology

From 'goose' (the bird), used in English to denote a simpleton or silly person, with the suffix -y.
