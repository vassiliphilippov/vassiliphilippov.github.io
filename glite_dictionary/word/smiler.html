smiler

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmaɪlər / (AmE), / ˈsmaɪlə / (BrE)
a person who smiles, especially often or readily
a person who smiles

Examples

Jenna is a smiler even on difficult days, always brightening the mood around her.
People often call him a smiler because of his infectious positivity.
At the party, every smiler made the atmosphere feel more welcoming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grinner - a person who grins

Etymology

From 'smile' + suffix '-er', forming agent noun.
