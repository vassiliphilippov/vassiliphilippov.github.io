glorified

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡlɔːrɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡlɔːrɪˌfaɪd / (BrE)
treated as more important, impressive, or valuable than is actually the case; given sacrosanct or authoritative status.
treated as more important or impressive than it is

Examples

Her job title is just a glorified secretary, despite her impressive duties.
They live in what is essentially a glorified storage shed.
The meeting was nothing more than a glorified coffee break.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overrated - considered to be better or more important than is deserved

Etymology

Past participle of 'glorify,' from Latin 'glorificare' (to glorify).
