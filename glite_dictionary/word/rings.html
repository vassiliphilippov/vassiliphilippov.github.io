rings

1. NOUN

/ rɪŋz / (AmE), / rɪŋz / (BrE)
a pair of heavy metal circles suspended by ropes, used as a gymnastic apparatus for performing exercises
gymnastic apparatus with suspended circles

Examples

Competing on the rings requires immense upper body strength.
He practiced daily to master his routine on the rings.
The gymnast performed a flawless dismount from the rings during the finals.

Synset Examples

the rings require a strong upper body

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horizontal bar - a single bar used for gymnastic exercises
  • Synonym: pommel horse - a gymnastic apparatus with handles, used for routines
