aforementioned

1. ADJ

/ əˈfɔːrˌmɛnʃənd / (AmE), / əˈfɔːˌmɛnʃənd / (BrE)
mentioned previously; referred to earlier in the same text or conversation.
referenced earlier

Examples

The aforementioned issues must be addressed before we continue with the project.
Several of the aforementioned proposals were accepted by the committee.
Please see the aforementioned document for further details.

Synset Examples

works of all the aforementioned authors
said party has denied the charges

Related Terms

  • Synonym: said - referring to something previously mentioned

Etymology

From 'afore' (before) + 'mentioned'.
