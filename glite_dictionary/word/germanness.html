Germanness

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɜrmənnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːmənnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being German in character, culture, or origin.
quality of being German

Examples

The novelist's work explores the idea of Germanness in a multicultural world.
Aspects of Germanness can be seen in the architecture and cuisine of the region.
He questioned how his own identity was shaped by his sense of Germanness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: German - a native or inhabitant of Germany; the German language
