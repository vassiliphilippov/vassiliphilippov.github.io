terete

1. ADJ

/ təˈrit / (AmE), / təˈriːt / (BrE)
(especially of plant parts) cylindrical and gradually tapering; circular in cross-section.
cylindrical and tapering (esp. plants)

Examples

The stems of this plant species are terete, making them easy to distinguish from their angular relatives.
Some underwater plants have terete leaves that reduce water resistance.
The botanist described the roots as terete and tapering toward the tip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cylindrical - having the shape of a cylinder
  • Synonym: tapering - becoming gradually narrower toward one end

Etymology

From Latin 'teret-', 'teres' (rounded, smooth, cylindrical).
