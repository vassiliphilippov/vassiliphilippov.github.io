Andre Derain

1. PROPN

/ ɑnˈdreɪ dəˈræn / (AmE), / ɑ̃ˈdʁe dəˈʁɛ̃ / (BrE)
(1880–1954) A French painter known as a leading exponent of Fauvism, recognized for his bold use of color and innovative style.
French painter, Fauvism movement

Examples

Andre Derain was a pioneer of the Fauvist movement in early twentieth-century France.
Many museums feature several works by Andre Derain in their modern art collections.
The vibrant colors in Andre Derain's landscapes distinguish his paintings from those of his contemporaries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Fauvism - A style of painting with vivid expressionistic use of color
