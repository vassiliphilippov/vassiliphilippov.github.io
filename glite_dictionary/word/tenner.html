tenner

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnər / (AmE), / ˈtɛnə / (BrE)
An informal term for a ten-dollar bill (U.S. currency).
ten-dollar bill (U.S., informal)
informal american

Examples

He handed the cashier a tenner to pay for the groceries.
Do you have a tenner I can borrow until tomorrow?
She was thrilled to find a forgotten tenner in her old coat pocket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fiver - an informal term for a five-dollar bill
  • Synonym: ten-pound note - a British banknote worth ten pounds sterling
