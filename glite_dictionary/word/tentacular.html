tentacular

1. ADJ

/ tɛnˈtækjələr / (AmE), / tɛnˈtakjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or resembling tentacles; having or characterized by tentacles.
like or relating to tentacles

Examples

The jellyfish's tentacular arms drifted gracefully in the water.
The villain's tentacular influence extended into every aspect of city life.
Researchers observed the tentacular movements of the sea anemone under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tentacle - a slender, flexible limb or appendage in animals for grasping or feeling

Etymology

From Latin 'tentaculum' (feeler, tentacle), from 'tentare' (to feel).
