unconsumed

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.kənˈsuːmd / (AmE), / ʌn.kənˈsjuːmd / (BrE)
not eaten, used, or expended; remaining unused or untouched.
not eaten, used, or expended

Examples

Large amounts of unconsumed food were left after the event.
The unconsumed fuel was safely disposed of at the end of the experiment.
Please refrigerate any unconsumed portions to prevent spoilage.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: consumed - eaten, used, or expended
