speedpaint

1. NOUN

/ ˈspiːdˌpeɪnt / (AmE), / ˈspiːdˌpeɪnt / (BrE)
A painting, usually digital, created in a short period of time, often as an exercise or challenge.
painting done quickly, often digitally

Examples

The artist completed a speedpaint of the landscape in just thirty minutes.
Her portfolio includes several impressive speedpaints that highlight her digital art skills.
He livestreamed a speedpaint session to demonstrate his workflow to his followers.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sketch - a rough or unfinished drawing or painting

Etymology

Formed from 'speed' + 'paint', referencing the fast execution and the act of painting.
