deodorant

1. NOUN

/ diˈoʊdəɹənt / (AmE), / diˈəʊdərənt / (BrE)
A substance applied to the body to cover or prevent unpleasant body odors.
substance to mask body odors

Examples

She uses a floral-scented deodorant every morning after showering.
The store had a wide selection of deodorants for men and women.
He forgot to apply deodorant before going to the gym.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antiperspirant - a substance that prevents sweating

Etymology

From Latin 'de-' (removal) + 'odor' (smell) + '-ant'.
