repetitious

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɛpɪˈtɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌrɛpɪˈtɪʃəs / (BrE)
containing or characterized by repetition, especially when considered boring or unnecessary
containing much repetition

Examples

The movie was criticized for its repetitious dialogue and predictable plot.
Doing the same repetitious actions every day made the job monotonous.
Her writing style can be a bit repetitious, repeating the same ideas in every chapter.

Synset Examples

repetitive movement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repetitive - involving or characterized by repetition

Etymology

From Latin 'repetitio' (repetition) + English suffix '-ous'.
