Amanita rubescens

1. NOUN

/ əˈmænɪtə ruːˈbɛsns / (AmE), / əˈmanᵻtə ruːˈbɛsns / (BrE)
a yellowish edible mushroom that often turns red when touched, commonly known as the blusher
edible mushroom that turns red when touched

Examples

The Amanita rubescens is prized by some foragers for its distinctive blush when handled.
After identifying the Amanita rubescens, he carefully cooked the edible mushroom for dinner.
Experts warn that Amanita rubescens should not be confused with its poisonous relatives.

Etymology

From Latin: genus name 'Amanita', species epithet 'rubescens' meaning 'reddening'.
