inquirer

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈkwaɪərər / (AmE), / ɪnˈkwaɪərə / (BrE)
a person who asks a question or seeks information
someone who asks a question

Examples

The inquirer raised several insightful points during the panel discussion.
As an inquirer, she was determined to get to the bottom of the mystery.
Every inquirer was given the opportunity to voice their concerns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: questioner - a person who asks questions

Etymology

From the verb 'inquire', from Latin 'inquirere' (to seek for, ask).
