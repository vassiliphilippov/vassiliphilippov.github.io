chimeral

1. ADJ

/ kɪˈmɪrəl / (AmE), / kaɪˈmɪərəl / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling a chimera; fantastically imaginary or unreal.
fantastical; like a chimera

Examples

Many regarded his proposal as a chimeral vision rather than a feasible solution.
The novel is set in a chimeral world filled with strange, mythic creatures.
Her hopes for peace in the war-torn region seemed almost chimeral to the skeptical diplomats.

Synset Examples

his Utopia is not as chimeric commonwealth but a practical improvement on what already exists

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chimeric - having the characteristics of a chimera; wildly fanciful
  • Antonym: realistic - representing things as they are; practical

Etymology

From Latin 'chimaera', via Greek 'khimaira' (mythical she-goat), plus English suffix '-al'.
