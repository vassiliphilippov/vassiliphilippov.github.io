unwaxed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈwækst / (AmE), / ʌnˈwækst / (BrE)
not treated with or covered in wax.
not treated with wax

Examples

Please specify if you prefer your lemons unwaxed or waxed for cooking.
The unwaxed floor looked dull compared to the rest of the hallway.
She bought an unwaxed dental tape to avoid artificial coatings.

Synset Examples

the unwaxed floor

Related Terms

  • Antonym: waxed - treated with or covered in wax

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (not) to 'waxed'.
