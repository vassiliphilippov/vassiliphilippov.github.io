Cumana

1. PROPN

/ kuːˈmɑːnə / (AmE), / kjuːˈmɑːnə / (BrE)
A port city in northeastern Venezuela on the Caribbean Sea, recognized as the oldest European settlement in South America.
port city in northeastern Venezuela

Examples

The colonial architecture of Cumana attracts many tourists each year.
After the earthquake, Cumana underwent significant reconstruction.
He sailed from Cumana to several nearby Caribbean islands.
