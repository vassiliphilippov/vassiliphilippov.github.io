to mongrelise

1. VERB

/ ˈmɑŋɡrəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋɡrɪlaɪz / (BrE)
to cause (an animal or breed) to become a mongrel, especially by mixing breeds or types
make an animal or breed mixed

Examples

The breeder refused to mongrelise the pedigree dogs by allowing indiscriminate mating.
Some animal rights activists argue that to mongrelise a rare species endangers its unique traits.
They worried that crossbreeding might mongrelise the traditional cattle stock.

Synset Examples

mongrelized dogs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to mongrelize - variant American spelling; cause to become a mongrel
  • Antonym: to purify - make pure or more pure, often used about breed or lineage

Etymology

From 'mongrel' (a dog of mixed breed) + suffix '-ise' (to make or become).
