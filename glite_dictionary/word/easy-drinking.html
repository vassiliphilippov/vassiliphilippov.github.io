easy-drinking

1. ADJ

/ ˈiːzi ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈiːzi ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
(Of a drink) pleasant and smooth to consume, requiring little effort to enjoy; not harsh or strong in flavor.
smooth and pleasant to drink

Examples

This white wine is very easy-drinking and perfect for a summer evening.
Many people enjoy easy-drinking beers during social gatherings.
An easy-drinking cocktail is ideal for guests who prefer milder flavors.

Synset Examples

This wine is smooth and easy-drinking.
The beer is refreshing and easy-drinking.
He prefers easy-drinking cocktails at parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: palatable - (of food or drink) pleasant to taste
