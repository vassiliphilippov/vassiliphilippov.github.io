quadball

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɑdˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈkwɒdˌbɔːl / (BrE)
A team sport based on the fictional game of Quidditch from the Harry Potter series, adapted for real-life play with modified rules and equipment.
real-life team sport based on Quidditch

Examples

Several universities now have official quadball teams that compete nationally.
She joined the local quadball league after reading about the sport online.
The rules of quadball emphasize both athletic skill and teamwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Quidditch - the fictional sport from the Harry Potter series

Etymology

Formed from Latin 'quadri-' (four) and 'ball'; the sport was renamed from 'Quidditch' to avoid trademark issues.
