Sphyrna

1. NOUN

/ ˈsfɪrnə / (AmE), / ˈsfɪənə / (BrE)
a genus of hammerhead sharks, including several well-known species distinguished by their hammer-shaped heads
genus of hammerhead sharks

Examples

The genus Sphyrna includes both the scalloped and great hammerhead sharks.
Several species within Sphyrna are found in warm coastal waters around the world.
Researchers studied the migratory patterns of Sphyrna to understand their habitats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hammerhead shark - a shark with a flattened head that extends into lateral projections

Etymology

From Greek sphȳrna (hammer), referring to the distinctive shape of the sharks' heads.
