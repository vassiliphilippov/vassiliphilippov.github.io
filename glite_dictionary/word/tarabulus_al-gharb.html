Tarabulus Al-Gharb

1. PROPN

/ tɑːˈrɑː.bʊlʊs æl ˈɣɑrb / (AmE), / tɑːˈrɑː.bʊlʊs æl ˈɣɑːb / (BrE)
The capital and largest city of Libya, located in northwestern Libya on the Mediterranean Sea; also known as Tripoli.
capital and chief port of Libya

Examples

Many historical records refer to Tarabulus Al-Gharb as an important Mediterranean port.
The climate of Tarabulus Al-Gharb is typically Mediterranean, with mild, wet winters.
Tourists visiting Libya often start their journey in Tarabulus Al-Gharb.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tripoli - Capital city of Libya, also known as Tarabulus Al-Gharb

Etymology

From Arabic طرابلس الغرب (Ṭarābulus al-Gharb), meaning 'Western Tripoli.'
