wearer

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛɹɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɛəɹə / (BrE)
a person who wears or carries something as a body covering or accessory
person who wears something

Examples

The wearer of this badge is authorized to enter restricted areas.
Gloves protect the wearer from harmful substances.
The designer praised the confidence of the wearer in bold colors.

Synset Examples

the wearer of the crown

Related Terms

  • Synonym: user - a person who uses something

Etymology

From 'wear' + '-er', the agentive suffix from Old English '-ere'.
