grader

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪdər / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪdə / (BrE)
a person who assigns grades or marks to the work of others, especially in an academic setting
person who assigns grades

Examples

The grader returned our essays with helpful feedback and a final score.
Each assignment will be reviewed by a different grader to ensure fairness.
As a teaching assistant, she worked as a grader for the undergraduate physics course.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: examiner - a person who sets or grades examinations
  • Synonym: instructor - a person who teaches, especially in a college or university

Etymology

From the noun 'grade' + agent suffix '-er'.
