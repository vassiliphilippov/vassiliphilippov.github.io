moneyman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌniˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmʌniˌman / (BrE)
a man who is skilled at dealing with large sums of money, especially in business or finance
man skilled in large-scale finance
informal

Examples

The moneyman behind the company's expansion is rarely seen in public.
Several top moneymen were brought in to restructure the bank's finances.
As a skilled moneyman, he negotiated the multi-million dollar deal effortlessly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: financier - a person skilled in financial operations or management
