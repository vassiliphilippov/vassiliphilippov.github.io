dasyurid

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæsiˌjʊrɪd / (AmE), / ˈdæsɪjʊərɪd / (BrE)
a small carnivorous nocturnal marsupial of the family Dasyuridae, native to Australia and Tasmania
small carnivorous marsupial of Australia/Tasmania

Examples

Many dasyurids play a crucial role in controlling insect populations in their native habitats.
The tiger quoll is one of the largest dasyurids found in Australia.
Researchers observed the behavior of a nocturnal dasyurid in the dense Tasmanian forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marsupial - a mammal of the order Marsupialia, carrying young in a pouch

Etymology

From New Latin Dasyuridae, from Greek dasys (hairy) + oura (tail).
