workwear

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝrkˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌweə / (BrE)
clothing designed and worn for manual or physical work, typically made to be durable and functional.
clothing for physical or manual work

Examples

Construction workers are required to wear workwear such as steel-toed boots and high-visibility vests on site.
The company provides comfortable and durable workwear for all factory employees.
She purchased new workwear before starting her job as a mechanic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uniform - distinctive clothing worn by members of the same organization
