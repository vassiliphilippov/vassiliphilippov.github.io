utilization

1. NOUN

/ ˌjuːtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌjuːtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
The act or process of making use of something; the state of being used.
the act of using something

Examples

The utilization of renewable energy sources has increased significantly in recent years.
Improving resource utilization can help a company reduce its costs.
High utilization of hospital beds often leads to longer waiting times for patients.

Synset Examples

the rate of utilization

Related Terms

  • Synonym: use - the act of using or employing something
  • Synonym: consumption - the act of using up a resource

Etymology

From Latin 'utilis' (useful) via French 'utilisation'.
