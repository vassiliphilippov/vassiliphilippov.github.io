Bristol

1. PROPN

/ ˈbrɪstəl / (AmE), / ˈbrɪstəl / (BrE)
an industrial city and port in southwestern England, situated near the mouth of the River Avon
city and port in southwestern England

Examples

Bristol is famous for its vibrant street art and annual balloon festival.
She moved to Bristol to attend university and fell in love with the city.
The historic harbor area of Bristol attracts many tourists each year.

Etymology

From Old English Brycgstow, meaning 'the place at the bridge'.
