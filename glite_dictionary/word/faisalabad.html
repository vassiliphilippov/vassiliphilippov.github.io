Faisalabad

1. PROPN

/ faɪˈsɑːləbɑːd / (AmE), / faɪˈsɑːləbɑːd / (BrE)
A city in northeast Pakistan, known as the country's third largest metropolis and a center of industry.
city in northeast Pakistan

Examples

Faisalabad is famous for its thriving textile industry and bustling markets.
During my trip to Pakistan, I visited the historic Clock Tower of Faisalabad.
Many international companies have their factories based in Faisalabad due to its industrial significance.

Etymology

Named after King Faisal of Saudi Arabia; formerly known as Lyallpur.
