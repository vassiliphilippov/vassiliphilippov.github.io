tarpon

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑːr.pən / (AmE), / ˈtɑː.pən / (BrE)
a large, silvery game fish found in warm Atlantic coastal waters, especially off Florida, prized by sport fishers
large silvery Atlantic game fish

Examples

Anglers from around the world travel to Florida to catch tarpon during the summer months.
We saw a huge tarpon leap out of the water near the boat.
The tarpon is known for its impressive size and acrobatic jumps when hooked.

Etymology

From Spanish 'tarpón', origin uncertain.
