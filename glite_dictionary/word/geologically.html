geologically

1. ADV

/ ˌdʒiː.əˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː.əˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to geology or the geological features of an area
in relation to geology

Examples

Geologically, the region contains some of the oldest rocks on the planet.
Geologically speaking, the mountain range formed during the Paleozoic era.
The area is geologically active, with frequent minor earthquakes.

Synset Examples

geologically speaking, this area is extremely interesting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geological - relating to the study of the Earth's structure, rocks, and history

Etymology

From 'geological' + '-ly'. 'Geological' from Greek 'gē' (earth) + 'logos' (study).
