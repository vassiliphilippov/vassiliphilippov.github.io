response

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
a phrase recited or sung by the congregation in reply to a versicle spoken or sung by the priest or minister during a religious service
phrase said or sung by congregation

Examples

The choir led the response after the priest finished the opening prayer.
The congregation joined in the response during the evening service.
Her favorite part of the mass was singing the response with everyone.

Etymology

From Latin responsum (an answer), from respondēre (to respond).
