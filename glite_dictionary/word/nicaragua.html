Nicaragua

1. PROPN

/ ˌnɪkəˈrɑːɡwə / (AmE), / ˌnɪkəˈræɡjuə / (BrE)
A country in Central America, bordered by Honduras to the north and Costa Rica to the south, which became independent from Spain in 1821.
country in Central America

Examples

She spent a year studying biodiversity in Nicaragua after graduating.
The coffee from Nicaragua is highly prized by connoisseurs around the world.
Nicaragua is known for its beautiful lakes and volcanoes.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Nicaragua', likely from Nicarao (name of an indigenous people) and agua (water).
