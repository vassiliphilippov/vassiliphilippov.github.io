FEMA

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiː.mə / (AmE), / ˈfiː.mə / (BrE)
an independent agency of the United States government responsible for coordinating federal emergency preparedness, mitigation, response, and recovery efforts
US emergency management agency
american

Examples

The town received assistance from FEMA after the hurricane devastated the area.
Local officials coordinated with FEMA to provide shelter for displaced residents.
FEMA is often called upon in the aftermath of major natural disasters.

Etymology

Acronym for Federal Emergency Management Agency.
