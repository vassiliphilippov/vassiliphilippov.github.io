Libyan

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪbiən / (AmE), / ˈlɪbiən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Libya
person from Libya

Examples

Many Libyans participated in the celebrations following the national holiday.
A famous Libyan writer received an international award.
The museum featured exhibits about ancient Libyan culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Libya - a country in North Africa on the Mediterranean coast

Etymology

From Libya, the country in North Africa, from Greek 'Libyē'.

/ ˈlɪbiən / (AmE), / ˈlɪbiən / (BrE)
of or relating to Libya or its people or culture.
of or relating to Libya

Examples

Several Libyan dishes were featured at the international food fair.

Related Terms
