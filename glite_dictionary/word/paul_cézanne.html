Paul Cézanne

1. PROPN

/ pɔl seɪˈzæn / (AmE), / pɔːl seɪˈzan / (BrE)
(1839–1906) A French Post-Impressionist painter known for influencing the development of modern art by emphasizing the structural aspects of nature.
French Post-Impressionist painter

Examples

Many art historians consider Paul Cézanne to be the father of modern art.
The landscapes painted by Paul Cézanne greatly influenced the cubist movement.
A major exhibition of Paul Cézanne’s works opened at the museum this month.
