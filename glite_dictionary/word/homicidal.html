homicidal

1. ADJ

/ ˌhɑːməˈsaɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌhɒmɪˈsaɪdəl / (BrE)
Having a tendency to kill another human being, or characteristic of a person likely to commit murder.
having a tendency to kill

Examples

The defendant was found not guilty by reason of homicidal insanity.
After the argument, he displayed a homicidal rage that frightened everyone.
There are concerns that some prisoners have homicidal tendencies.

Synset Examples

a homicidal rage
murderous thugs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: murderous - having the desire or capability to kill; extremely violent

Etymology

From Latin homicida (a murderer), from homo (man) + caedere (to kill).
