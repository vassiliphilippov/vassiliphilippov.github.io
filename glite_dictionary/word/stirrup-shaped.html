stirrup-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɪr.əp ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈstɪr.əp ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape or outline of a stirrup.
shaped like a stirrup

Examples

The bone is stirrup-shaped, resembling the riding equipment.
A stirrup-shaped structure can be observed in the middle ear.
The artist crafted a stirrup-shaped handle for the ceramic jug.
