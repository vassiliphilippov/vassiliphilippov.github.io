mama

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑː.mə / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.mə / (BrE)
An informal word for mother, used especially by young children.
an informal term for mother
informal

Examples

She called out for her mama when she woke up from her nap.
His mama taught him how to cook traditional dishes.
Little children often run straight to their mama when they are upset.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mother - female parent
  • Synonym: mom - informal term for mother (North America)
  • Synonym: mummy - informal term for mother (British English)

Etymology

Imitative of children's early speech, similar forms in many languages.
