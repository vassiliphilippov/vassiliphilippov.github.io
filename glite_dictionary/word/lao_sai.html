lao sai

1. NOUN

/ laʊ saɪ / (AmE), / laʊ saɪ / (BrE)
(Singapore and Malaysia, informal) Diarrhea; an episode of loose or watery bowel movements.
colloquial term for diarrhea
informal

Examples

After eating the street food, he ended up with a bad case of lao sai.
She texted her boss to say she was late due to lao sai.
We laughed about our misadventures with lao sai while traveling in Southeast Asia.

Synset Examples

She joked about her lao sai after eating too much.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diarrhea - Frequent, loose, or watery bowel movements

Etymology

From Hokkien dialect (撈屎), commonly used in Singapore and Malaysia.
