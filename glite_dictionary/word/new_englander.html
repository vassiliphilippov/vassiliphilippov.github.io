New Englander

1. NOUN

/ nu ˈɪŋɡləndər / (AmE), / njuː ˈɪŋɡləndə / (BrE)
a person from or living in New England, a region in the northeastern United States
person from New England (U.S.)

Examples

As a proud New Englander, she never missed the annual fall foliage tours.
He moved to Boston and quickly felt at home among the friendly New Englanders.
Many New Englanders enjoy celebrating Patriots' Day every April.
