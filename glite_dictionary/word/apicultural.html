apicultural

1. ADJ

/ ˌæpɪˈkʌltʃɚl / (AmE), / ˌapɪˈkʌltʃərəl / (BrE)
relating to the care and breeding of bees, especially for producing honey
relating to beekeeping
medical

Examples

The region has a long history of apicultural practices, producing some of the finest honey in the country.
Many farmers are turning to apicultural methods to enhance crop pollination.
He wrote a guide on apicultural equipment for beginners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beekeeping - the activity of keeping and breeding bees for honey
  • Degree Lower: agricultural - relating to farming or agriculture

Etymology

From Latin 'apis' (bee) + 'culture' (cultivation); patterned after 'agricultural'.
