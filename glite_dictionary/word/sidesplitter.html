sidesplitter

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪdˌsplɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌsplɪtə / (BrE)
A joke, story, or event that is extremely funny and makes people laugh a lot.
extremely funny joke or event

Examples

The comedian's new routine was a real sidesplitter—the entire audience was in stitches.
It's hard to believe that such an old joke could still be a sidesplitter today.
That movie is full of sidesplitters; you won't stop laughing from start to finish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knee-slapper - a joke or story that is very funny

Etymology

From 'side' + 'splitter', referring to laughter so intense it feels as if one's sides might split.
