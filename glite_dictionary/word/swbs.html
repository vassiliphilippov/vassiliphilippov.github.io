SWbS

1. NOUN

/ ɛs ˌdʌbəlˈjuː bi ɛs / (AmE), / ɛs ˌdʌbəlˈjuː bi ɛs / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point (11.25°) south of southwest.
compass point south of southwest

Examples

The ship maintained a course of SWbS for several hours through the night.
Set the heading to SWbS to avoid the rocks near the shoreline.
According to the chart, the wind was blowing steadily from SWbS.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: southwest - the compass point between south and west
  • Degree Lower: south-southwest - the compass point between south and southwest

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'southwest by south.'
