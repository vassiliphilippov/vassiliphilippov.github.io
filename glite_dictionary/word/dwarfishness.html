dwarfishness

1. NOUN

/ ˈdwɔːrfɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈdwɔːfɪʃnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being dwarfish; having the characteristics or stature of a dwarf.
the quality of being dwarfish

Examples

His dwarfishness made him stand out in a crowd of tall athletes.
The character's dwarfishness was mentioned as a distinguishing feature in the novel.
She spoke openly about her dwarfishness and the challenges she had overcome.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dwarfish - of very small stature or size, like a dwarf

Etymology

Formed from 'dwarfish' (small in stature) + '-ness' (state or quality).
