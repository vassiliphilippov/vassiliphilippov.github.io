suprarenalectomy

1. NOUN

/ ˌsuːprəˌrəːnəˈlɛktəmi / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˌriːnəˈlɛktəmi / (BrE)
surgical removal of one or both adrenal glands
surgical removal of adrenal glands
medical

Examples

The patient underwent a suprarenalectomy to remove a tumor on her adrenal gland.
A bilateral suprarenalectomy may be necessary in cases of adrenal cancer.
After his suprarenalectomy, hormone replacement therapy was prescribed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adrenalectomy - surgical removal of the adrenal gland(s)

Etymology

From Latin supra- (above) + renalis (of the kidney) + -ectomy (removal).
