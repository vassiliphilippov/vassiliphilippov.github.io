sunnily

1. ADV

/ ˈsʌnɪli / (AmE), / ˈsʌnɪli / (BrE)
in a cheerful, good-humoured, or optimistic manner
in a cheerful or optimistic way

Examples

She greeted us sunnily despite the rain pouring down outside.
He sunnily reassured his team after the disappointing results.
The teacher sunnily encouraged her students to try their best.

Synset Examples

‘I'll do the dishes,’ he said pleasantly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheerfully - in a happy and optimistic manner
  • Antonym: gloomily - in a sad or downcast manner

Etymology

From 'sunny' + '-ly', indicating a manner resembling sunshine in mood.
