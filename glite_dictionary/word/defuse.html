to defuse

1. VERB

/ dəˈfjuz / (AmE), / dɪˈfjuːz / (BrE)
to make a bomb or explosive safe by removing its triggering device
make a bomb safe by removing trigger

Examples

The technician managed to defuse the bomb just before the timer reached zero.
Experts were called in to defuse several unexploded devices found at the construction site.
She carefully defused the homemade explosive to prevent any accidental detonation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to detonate - to cause a bomb or explosive to explode
  • Synonym: to disarm - to remove the weapons or means of attack from something or someone

Etymology

From 'de-' (remove) + 'fuse' (triggering device on a bomb).
