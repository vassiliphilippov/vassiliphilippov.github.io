disappointingly

1. ADV

/ ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.əˈpɔɪn.tɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that causes disappointment or fails to meet expectations.
in a manner that disappoints

Examples

The movie ended disappointingly, failing to resolve several key plot points.
She performed disappointingly on the test, despite her hours of preparation.
The weather was disappointingly cold for a summer picnic.

Synset Examples

the discoverer of argon, Sir William Ramsay, looked disappointingly ordinary

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disappointing - not as good as hoped or expected
  • Antonym: satisfyingly - in a manner that satisfies or fulfills expectations
