sottishly

1. ADV

/ ˈsɑːtɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈsɒtɪʃli / (BrE)
In a drunken, foolish, or stupid manner.
in a drunken or foolish manner

Examples

He staggered sottishly down the street after the celebration.
The guests laughed sottishly at even the simplest of jokes.
She eyed him disapprovingly as he behaved sottishly throughout the evening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drunkenly - in a drunken manner
  • Synonym: foolishly - in a foolish manner

Etymology

From 'sottish' (drunken, foolish) + '-ly'. 'Sottish' from 'sot' (a habitual drunkard), ultimately from Old French 'sot' (fool).
