parenthood

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛrəntˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈpærəntˌhʊd / (BrE)
the state or fact of being a parent; the condition of having and raising children
the state of being a parent

Examples

Many people find that parenthood brings both great joy and significant challenges.
The couple discussed their hopes and fears about parenthood before having children.
Adjusting to parenthood can be difficult, especially for first-time parents.

Synset Examples

to everyone's surprise, parenthood reformed the man

Related Terms

  • Antonym: childlessness - the state of not having children

Etymology

From 'parent' + 'hood', with 'hood' denoting state or condition.
