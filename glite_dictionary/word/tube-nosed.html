tube-nosed

1. ADJ

/ tuːb noʊzd / (AmE), / tjuːb nəʊzd / (BrE)
having nostrils or a nose shaped like a tube, typically referring to certain animals such as birds or bats.
having a tubular-shaped nose or nostrils

Examples

The tube-nosed bat is easily identified by its distinctive nasal shape.
Many seabirds, such as albatrosses, are classified as tube-nosed birds because of their tubular nostrils.
Researchers studied the tube-nosed structure to understand its adaptation to marine environments.
