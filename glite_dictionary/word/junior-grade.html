junior-grade

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒuːniər ɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːniə ɡreɪd / (BrE)
of lower rank or status within an organization or system; subordinate or less senior.
of lower rank or status

Examples

The company hired several junior-grade engineers to assist with basic tasks.
He felt frustrated being treated like a junior-grade member of the team despite his experience.
After his promotion, he no longer reported to a junior-grade supervisor.

Synset Examples

a lowly corporal
petty officialdom

Related Terms

  • Antonym: senior - having a higher rank or status
  • Synonym: subordinate - lower in rank or position

Etymology

From 'junior' (younger, lower in rank) + 'grade' (level or rank).
