sailmaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪlˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈseɪlˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make or repair sails for boats and ships
a person who makes sails

Examples

The sailmaker meticulously stitched panels of canvas together in his workshop.
After the storm, the damaged sails were taken to a local sailmaker for repair.
She apprenticed with a skilled sailmaker to learn the ancient techniques of the trade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sailor - a person who works on a ship or navigates by sea

Etymology

From 'sail' + 'maker'.
