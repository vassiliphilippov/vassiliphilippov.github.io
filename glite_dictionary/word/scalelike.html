scalelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈskeɪlˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈskeɪl.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the appearance of a scale, especially as seen in certain small, thin, flattened structures in plants or animals.
resembling a scale in form or appearance

Examples

The cactus has scalelike leaves that help reduce water loss in the desert environment.
Under the microscope, the insect's wing showed a pattern of scalelike structures.
The fossil's scalelike imprints suggest it belonged to a primitive species of reptile.

Synset Examples

scalelike leaves

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scaly - covered with or resembling scales

Etymology

Formed from 'scale' + 'like', meaning 'like a scale'.
