ginzo

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪnzoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪnzəʊ / (BrE)
An offensive slang term for a person of Italian descent.
offensive term for a person of Italian descent
offensive slang

Examples

He was upset when someone called him a ginzo in the street.
The word ginzo is considered highly offensive and derogatory.
Old movies sometimes use ginzo as a slur, reflecting the prejudices of the time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guinea - offensive term for a person of Italian descent

Etymology

Probably a corruption of 'Guinzo' or 'guinea,' both derogatory terms for Italian-Americans.
