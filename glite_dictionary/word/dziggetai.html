dziggetai

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪˈɡɛtaɪ / (AmE), / dziˈɡɛtaɪ / (BrE)
A subspecies of the Asiatic wild ass native to Mongolia, known for its reddish-brown coat and white belly.
Mongolian wild ass (animal)

Examples

The dziggetai roams the steppe in herds, facing threats from habitat loss and poaching.
Researchers are tracking the dziggetai population to ensure the species' survival in Mongolia.
Unlike domesticated donkeys, the dziggetai is adapted to harsh desert climates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: onager - another subspecies of the Asiatic wild ass

Etymology

From Russian дзиггитай (dziggitay), from Mongolian дзигаатай (dziggaatai), referring to the animal.
