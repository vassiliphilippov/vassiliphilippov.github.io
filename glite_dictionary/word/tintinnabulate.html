to tintinnabulate

1. VERB

/ ˌtɪntɪˈnæbjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˌtɪntɪˈnabjʊleɪt / (BrE)
(of a sound) to ring or tinkle like a small bell.
to ring like a small bell

Examples

The silver wind chimes tintinnabulate gently in the evening breeze.
Church bells began to tintinnabulate as the procession passed by.
In the distance, the icicles seemed to tintinnabulate as they struck one another.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tinkle - to make a light, ringing sound, like small bells

Etymology

From Latin 'tintinnabulum' (bell), from 'tintinnare' (to ring).
