tannish

1. ADJ

/ ˈtænɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈtænɪʃ / (BrE)
Somewhat tan in color; having a color that resembles tan.
somewhat tan in color

Examples

The curtains in the living room had a subtle tannish hue that complemented the furniture.
After hours in the sun, his skin developed a light, tannish tint.
She picked a tannish sweater that matched her fall wardrobe.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: tan - a pale brownish-yellow color
  • Synonym: beige - a pale sandy yellowish-brown color
  • Degree Lower: brownish - somewhat brown in color

Etymology

From 'tan' + suffix '-ish', meaning 'somewhat tan'.
