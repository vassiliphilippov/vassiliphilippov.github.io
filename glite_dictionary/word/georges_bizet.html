Georges Bizet

1. PROPN

/ ʒɔrʒ biˈzeɪ / (AmE), / ʒɔːʒ biˈzeɪ / (BrE)
(1838–1875) A French composer known especially for his operas, notably 'Carmen'.
French composer, wrote 'Carmen'

Examples

Many music historians regard Georges Bizet as one of the most influential composers of the 19th century.
The opera 'Carmen' by Georges Bizet remains a staple of the classical repertoire.
As a student, she performed an aria from Georges Bizet's 'Carmen' at her recital.
