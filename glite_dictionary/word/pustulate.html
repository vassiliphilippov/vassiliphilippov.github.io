pustulate

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʌstʃəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈpʌstjʊlət / (BrE)
(of skin or complexion) marked by the presence of pustules or blemished by skin imperfections.
blemished with pustules or skin imperfections

Examples

After weeks of stress, his pustulate skin required special treatment from a dermatologist.
The character in the novel is described as having a pustulate complexion, hinting at years of poor health.
She felt self-conscious about her pustulate cheeks during the school assembly.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clear - (of complexion) free from spots, blemishes, or imperfections
  • Synonym: pustular - of or relating to pustules; characterized by pustules

Etymology

From Latin pustulatus, from pustula (blister, pimple).
