numismatologist

1. NOUN

/ nuːˌmɪzməˈtɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / njuːˌmɪzməˈtɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A person who studies or collects coins, paper money, and medals.
coin and money collector or scholar

Examples

The numismatologist carefully examined the ancient coin for any signs of forgery.
As a skilled numismatologist, she contributed several articles to the journal on currency history.
Some numismatologists specialize in rare paper money rather than coins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: numismatist - a person who studies or collects coins, paper money, and medals

Etymology

From French numismatologue, based on Latin numisma (coin).
