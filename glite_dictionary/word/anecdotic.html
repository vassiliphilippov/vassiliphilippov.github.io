anecdotic

1. ADJ

/ ˌænɪkˈdɑːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌænɪkˈdəʊtɪk / (BrE)
characterized by or given to telling anecdotes; relating to or consisting of anecdotes
relating to or given to anecdotes

Examples

Her anecdotic remarks kept the whole dinner party entertained for hours.
The essay presents an anecdotic perspective on local history.
He was particularly anecdotic when reminiscing about his childhood adventures.

Synset Examples

anecdotal conversation
an anecdotal history of jazz
he was at his anecdotic best

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anecdotal - relating to anecdotes or based on personal accounts

Etymology

From French 'anecdotique', from 'anecdote' (anecdote).
