Kilimanjaro

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɪləˌmænˈdʒɑːroʊ / (AmE), / ˌkɪlɪmænˈdʒɑːrəʊ / (BrE)
the highest mountain in Africa, located in northeastern Tanzania and rising to 19,340 feet (5,895 meters) above sea level
the highest mountain in Africa

Examples

She plans to climb Kilimanjaro during her trip to Tanzania next year.
The snow-capped peak of Kilimanjaro is visible from miles away on clear days.
Many adventurers dream of reaching the summit of Kilimanjaro.

Etymology

From Swahili Kilimanjaro, possibly from 'kilima' (mountain) and Chagga 'njaro' (whiteness).
