Equus

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛk.wəs / (AmE), / ˈɛk.wəs / (BrE)
The genus of mammals in the family Equidae, including horses, zebras, and donkeys—the only surviving genus of this family.
genus including horses, zebras, donkeys

Examples

The horse is classified scientifically as Equus ferus caballus.
Zebras are members of the genus Equus found in Africa.
All living horses, donkeys, and zebras belong to Equus.

Etymology

Latin *equus* meaning 'horse'.
