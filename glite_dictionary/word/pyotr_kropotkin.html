Pyotr Kropotkin

1. PROPN

/ ˈpjoʊtər kroʊˈpɒtkɪn / (AmE), / ˈpjɒtə kropˈɒtkɪn / (BrE)
(1842–1921) Russian anarchist, geographer, and philosopher, known for his advocacy of anarchist communism and influential writings on mutual aid.
Russian anarchist and philosopher

Examples

Pyotr Kropotkin wrote extensively about the principles of mutual aid and cooperation in nature.
Many modern anarchists are influenced by the works of Pyotr Kropotkin.
The theories of Pyotr Kropotkin have played a significant role in political philosophy discussions.
