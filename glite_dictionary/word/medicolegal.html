medicolegal

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɛdɪkoʊˈliɡəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪkəʊˈliːɡəl / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with both medicine and law, especially the legal aspects of medical practice
relating to medicine and law
medical legal

Examples

The case raised important medicolegal issues regarding patient consent.
He is an expert in medicolegal matters, especially medical malpractice.
Hospitals often consult medicolegal advisors to review patient confidentiality policies.

Etymology

From Latin 'medicus' (physician) + 'legalis' (legal).
