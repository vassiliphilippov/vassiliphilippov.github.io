snuggery

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnʌɡəri / (AmE), / ˈsnʌɡəri / (BrE)
a small, comfortable, and secluded room, often used for relaxation or privacy
a small, cozy secluded room

Examples

After a long day, she retreated to her snuggery to read in peace.
They decorated the snuggery with soft pillows and warm lamps.
The cottage had a tiny snuggery that was perfect for afternoon tea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: den - a small, comfortable room in a house
  • Synonym: parlor - a sitting room in a house

Etymology

Formed irregularly from 'snug' + '-ery' (a suffix forming nouns denoting a place or business).
