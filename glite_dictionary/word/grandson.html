grandson

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrænˌsʌn / (AmE), / ˈɡrænˌsʌn / (BrE)
a son of one's son or daughter; a male grandchild
a male grandchild

Examples

My grandson loves to visit me every summer.
She proudly introduced her youngest grandson to her friends.
After years abroad, his grandson finally returned home.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: granddaughter - a female grandchild

Etymology

From 'grand-' (denoting a generation removed) + 'son'.
