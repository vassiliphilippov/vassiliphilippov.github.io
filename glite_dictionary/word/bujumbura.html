Bujumbura

1. PROPN

/ buːˌdʒuːmˈbʊrə / (AmE), / bjuːˌdʒʊmˈbʊərə / (BrE)
the largest city and former capital of Burundi, located on the northeastern shore of Lake Tanganyika
largest city of Burundi

Examples

The city of Bujumbura lies on the banks of Lake Tanganyika.
After independence, Bujumbura became a major commercial hub in Burundi.
Many travelers begin their journey through Burundi in Bujumbura.

Synset Examples

Usumbura was renamed Bujumbura when Burundi became independent in 1962
