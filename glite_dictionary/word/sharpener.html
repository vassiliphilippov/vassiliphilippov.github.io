sharpener

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑːr.pən.ər / (AmE), / ˈʃɑː.pən.ə / (BrE)
a device or tool used to make something sharper, such as the edge of a blade or the point of a pencil
device for making things sharper

Examples

I need a new sharpener for my colored pencils before art class.
The chef uses a special sharpener to keep his knives in perfect condition.
She bought an electric sharpener for the kitchen to make sharpening easier.

Synset Examples

a knife sharpener

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grinder - a machine or device for grinding, often used for sharpening edges
  • Antonym: blunter - a tool or device used to make something less sharp

Etymology

From 'sharpen' (to make sharp) + -er (agent noun suffix).
