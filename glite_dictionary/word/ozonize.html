to ozonize

1. VERB

/ ˈoʊ.zəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.zəˌnaɪz / (BrE)
to convert oxygen into ozone, typically using an electric discharge or ultraviolet light
convert oxygen into ozone

Examples

Scientists used a special device to ozonize the air in the laboratory for their experiments.
The company ozonizes water as part of its advanced purification process.
After the gas was ozonized, it became more effective for certain industrial applications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ozonate - to treat with ozone or convert into ozone

Etymology

From ozone + -ize; based on New Latin 'ozonum' (ozone) and Greek 'ozein' (to smell).
