centrality

1. NOUN

/ sɛnˈtræləti / (AmE), / sɛnˈtralɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being central or at the core of something
the quality of being central

Examples

The centrality of education in society cannot be overstated.
Her centrality to the project made her an invaluable team member.
In social networks, centrality measures can determine the most influential individuals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: central - being at or forming the center
  • Antonym: peripherality - the quality or state of being on the edge or periphery

Etymology

From central + -ity, after French centralité or Latin centralitas.
