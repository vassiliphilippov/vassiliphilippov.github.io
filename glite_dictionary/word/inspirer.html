inspirer

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈspaɪɹɚ / (AmE), / ɪnˈspaɪərə / (BrE)
a person who motivates or stimulates others to take action, often through encouragement or enthusiasm
one who inspires others to act

Examples

As an effective inspirer, she encouraged her team to overcome all challenges.
Many consider Martin Luther King Jr. to be a great inspirer of positive change.
The company was looking for an inspirer who could lead by example and energize the staff.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motivator - a person who motivates others to action
  • Antonym: discourager - a person who discourages or dissuades others from action

Etymology

From inspire + -er, formed after 'inspire' (to fill someone with the urge to do something).
