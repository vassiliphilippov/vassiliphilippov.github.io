postbox

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstˌbɒks / (BrE)
a public box in which letters and other mail are placed for collection and delivery by the postal service
public box for mail collection

Examples

She dropped the letter into the postbox on the corner before catching her bus.
The bright red postbox stood out against the old stone wall.
Some villages still have the original Victorian postbox outside their post office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mailbox - a public box for mail, especially used in American English
