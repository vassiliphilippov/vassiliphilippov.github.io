aliform

1. ADJ

/ ˈæ.lə.fɔrm / (AmE), / ˈæ.lɪ.fɔːm / (BrE)
Having or resembling wings.
wing-shaped; having wings

Examples

The seeds are aliform, allowing them to float gently on the wind.
The sculpture had an aliform design that evoked the image of birds in flight.
Researchers noted the aliform structures on the insect's thorax.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: winged - having wings or winglike appendages

Etymology

From Latin 'ala' (wing) + '-form' (shape, form).
