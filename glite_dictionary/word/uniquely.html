uniquely

1. ADV

/ juˈnikli / (AmE), / juːˈniːkli / (BrE)
In a way that is the only one of its kind or without an equal; in a manner that is unique.
in a unique way

Examples

The artist expresses ideas uniquely through abstract forms and colors.
Each fingerprint is uniquely different from all others in the world.
This method allows the data to be uniquely identified and tracked.

Synset Examples

he could determine uniquely the properties of the compound

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distinctively - in a way that is distinctive

Etymology

From unique (adj.) + -ly (adverb suffix).
