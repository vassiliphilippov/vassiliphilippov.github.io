Salonica

1. PROPN

/ səˈlɑːnɪkə / (AmE), / səˈlɒnɪkə / (BrE)
an older English name for Thessaloniki, a major port city in northeastern Greece on the Aegean Sea; the country's second largest city
former English name for Thessaloniki, Greece

Examples

During the early 20th century, Salonica was a cosmopolitan hub with diverse communities living side by side.
Many historical documents refer to Thessaloniki by its former English name, Salonica.
My grandfather was born in Salonica before it became widely known as Thessaloniki.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Thessaloniki - largest city in northern Greece, also known as Salonica

Etymology

From Italian 'Salonica', from Greek 'Thessaloníki', via Turkish 'Selanik'.
