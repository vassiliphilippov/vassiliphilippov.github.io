Gouda

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːdə / (AmE), / ˈɡaʊdə / (BrE)
a mild, creamy Dutch cheese with a smooth texture, usually shaped in round forms
a mild Dutch cheese

Examples

She bought a wedge of Gouda to serve with crackers at the party.
The sandwich was topped with melted Gouda for a rich, creamy flavor.
Dutch markets often display large wheels of Gouda alongside other cheeses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Edam - a semi-hard Dutch cheese similar to Gouda but usually firmer and sold in spheres coated in red wax

Etymology

From the Dutch city of Gouda, where the cheese originated.
