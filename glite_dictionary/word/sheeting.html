sheeting

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
fabric used for making bed sheets, usually sold by the yard or in rolls
fabric for making bed sheets

Examples

The store carries a wide selection of sheeting in different thread counts and colors.
Fine cotton sheeting is preferred for luxury bedding due to its softness.
She bought several meters of white sheeting to sew her own sheets.

Etymology

From 'sheet' + '-ing', originally referring to material intended for making sheets.
