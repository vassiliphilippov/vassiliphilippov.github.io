Los Angeles

1. PROPN

/ lɑs ˈændʒəˌləs / (AmE), / lɒs ˈændʒəlɪs / (BrE)
a city in southern California, known as the center of the U.S. film industry and the second most populous city in the United States
city in southern California

Examples

She moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in acting.
Los Angeles is famous for its year-round sunny weather and beaches.
Many major film studios are based in Los Angeles.

Etymology

From Spanish, meaning 'the angels'.
