livedo

1. NOUN

/ lɪˈveɪdoʊ / (AmE), / lɪˈviːdəʊ / (BrE)
A skin disorder characterised by mottled bluish-purple discoloration, usually in a net-like or patchy pattern.
mottled bluish skin discoloration
medical

Examples

The patient developed livedo on her lower legs during the course of her illness.
A doctor noticed patches of livedo on the patient's arms, prompting further investigation.
Certain autoimmune diseases can present with symptoms such as livedo and joint pain.

Etymology

From Latin 'lividus' (bluish), relating to bluish discoloration of the skin.
