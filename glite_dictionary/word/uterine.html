uterine

1. ADJ

/ ˈjuːtərɪn / (AmE), / ˈjuːtəriːn / (BrE)
relating to or involving the uterus
relating to the uterus
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with uterine fibroids during her annual check-up.
Doctors monitored the woman's uterine contractions closely during labor.
Research on uterine health has led to improved fertility treatments.

Synset Examples

uterine cancer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uterus - the organ in female mammals where offspring develop before birth

Etymology

From Latin 'uterinus', from 'uterus' (womb).
