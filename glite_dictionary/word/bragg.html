Bragg

1. PROPN

/ bræɡ / (AmE), / bræɡ / (BrE)
(1817–1876) A Confederate general in the American Civil War, defeated by Grant at the Battle of Chattanooga.
Confederate general in the American Civil War

Examples

General Bragg commanded the Confederate forces during the Battle of Chickamauga.
Historians often debate the leadership style of Bragg during the Civil War.
After his defeat at Chattanooga, Bragg resigned his command.
