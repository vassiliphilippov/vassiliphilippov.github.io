Carica

1. NOUN

/ kəˈraɪ.kə / (AmE), / kəˈraɪ.kə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical American trees in the family Caricaceae, including the papaya
genus of tropical trees including papaya

Examples

The genus Carica is best known for the papaya tree.
Botanists have classified several tropical fruit trees in the Carica genus.
Researchers studied the distribution of Carica in Central and South America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: papaya - the fruit or tree of Carica papaya

Etymology

From Latin 'carica', meaning 'a kind of fig', used as the genus name in botanical Latin.
