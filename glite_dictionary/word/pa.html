PA

1. PROPN

/ pi eɪ / (AmE), / piː eɪ / (BrE)
Abbreviation for Pennsylvania, a state in the northeastern United States, one of the original 13 colonies.
abbreviation for Pennsylvania (U.S. state)
american

Examples

She grew up in PA before moving to New York.
The conference will be held in Philadelphia, PA next summer.
Many of my relatives live in various towns across PA.

Etymology

Abbreviation of Pennsylvania, named after William Penn, its founder.

2. NOUN

/ piː eɪ / (AmE), / piː eɪ / (BrE)
an electronic amplification system used for making spoken announcements or playing music in public spaces
public address amplification system
informal

Examples

The airport PA announced a delay for Flight 17.
Please speak clearly into the PA so everyone can hear you.
The school used the PA to broadcast important information to all students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public address system - a system for amplifying sound to reach a large public area

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'public address.'

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04024549-n
Synset POS: n
