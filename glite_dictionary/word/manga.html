manga

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæŋ.ɡə / (AmE), / ˈmæŋ.ɡə / (BrE)
a style of Japanese graphic novel and comic book, typically with stylized artwork and stories for adults or young people
Japanese graphic novel or comic book

Examples

She spends hours reading manga series after school every day.
The bookstore has a special section dedicated to Japanese manga.
Many popular anime shows are adaptations of original manga stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anime - Japanese animated film or television entertainment

Etymology

From Japanese 漫画 (manga), meaning 'whimsical pictures'.
