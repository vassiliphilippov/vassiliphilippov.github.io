embryologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛm.briˈɑ.lə.dʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɛm.brɪˈɒl.ə.dʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist or physician who specializes in the study and development of embryos
specialist in embryo development
medical

Examples

After years of training, she became an accomplished embryologist at the fertility clinic.
An embryologist carefully studies cells during the earliest stages of development.
The conference featured embryologists from around the world sharing their research findings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embryology - the branch of biology that studies embryos

Etymology

From embryo + -logist, after embryology.
