rosebush

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊzˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊz.bʊʃ / (BrE)
A bush or shrub of the genus Rosa, which produces roses.
a bush that bears roses

Examples

The rosebush in our garden is covered with pink and red flowers every spring.
She pruned the old rosebush to encourage new growth next season.
A bee buzzed around the blooming rosebush, collecting nectar from its flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rose - a flower of the genus Rosa
  • Degree Lower: shrub - a woody plant smaller than a tree, usually with several stems
