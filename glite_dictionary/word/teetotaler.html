teetotaler

1. NOUN

/ tiˈtoʊtələr / (AmE), / tiːˈtəʊtlə / (BrE)
a person who never drinks alcoholic beverages
person who abstains from alcohol

Examples

She has been a teetotaler since her early twenties for health reasons.
Despite the party atmosphere, he remained a proud teetotaler all evening.
As a teetotaler, she often volunteers to drive her friends home after gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abstainer - a person who refrains from drinking alcohol
  • Antonym: drinker - a person who drinks alcoholic beverages

Etymology

Formed from 'teetotal' (total abstinence), with -er denoting a person; originated in 19th-century English temperance movement.
