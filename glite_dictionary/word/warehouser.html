warehouser

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛrˌhaʊsər / (AmE), / ˈweəˌhaʊzə / (BrE)
a person who manages or works in a warehouse, typically handling the storage, organization, and movement of goods
person who works in a warehouse

Examples

The company hired an experienced warehouser to oversee inventory and shipping operations.
As a warehouser, she ensures that all items are properly stored and documented.
Working as a warehouser requires attention to detail and the ability to operate heavy machinery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warehouseman - a person who works in or manages a warehouse

Etymology

Formed from 'warehouse' + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
