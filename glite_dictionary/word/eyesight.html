eyesight

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈaɪsʌɪt / (BrE)
the ability to see; the sense or faculty of vision
the ability to see

Examples

Her eyesight has improved since she started wearing glasses.
Regular checkups are important for maintaining good eyesight.
Working in poor light can strain your eyesight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vision - the faculty or state of being able to see

Etymology

From Middle English 'eye' + 'sight'; 'eye' from Old English 'ēage', 'sight' from Old English 'siht'.
