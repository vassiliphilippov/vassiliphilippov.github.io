spathiphyllum

1. NOUN

/ spæθɪˈfɪləm / (AmE), / spæθɪˈfɪləm / (BrE)
A tropical plant of the genus Spathiphyllum, known for its white or green spathe and fragrant flower spike, often grown as an ornamental houseplant.
a tropical ornamental plant; peace lily

Examples

The spathiphyllum thrives in low-light conditions, making it a popular office plant.
She placed a blooming spathiphyllum on her desk to brighten the room.
My grandmother grows several spathiphyllums in her living room for their fragrance and beauty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peace lily - common name for Spathiphyllum, an ornamental houseplant

Etymology

From Greek 'spathē' (spathe) + 'phyllon' (leaf), referring to the plant's characteristic spathe and leaves.
