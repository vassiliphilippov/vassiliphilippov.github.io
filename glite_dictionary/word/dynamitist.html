dynamitist

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪnəˌmaɪtɪst / (AmE), / ˈdʌɪnəmʌɪtɪst / (BrE)
a person who uses dynamite, especially for revolutionary or violent political purposes
person using dynamite in a revolutionary cause

Examples

The notorious dynamitist was finally apprehended after planting explosives at the factory.
In nineteenth-century Europe, several dynamitists became infamous for their radical actions.
The article traces the ideology and motivation behind early anarchist dynamitists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revolutionary - person involved in or supporting political revolution

Etymology

From dynamite + -ist, following the pattern of agent nouns.
