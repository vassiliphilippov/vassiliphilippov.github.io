ongoing

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑnˌɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɒnˌɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
continuing to exist or develop; currently happening
continuing; currently happening

Examples

There is an ongoing investigation into the matter.
She struggled to manage the ongoing demands of her job.
The ongoing renovations will be completed next month.

Synset Examples

an ongoing economic crisis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: continuous - happening without interruption
  • Antonym: finished - brought to an end; completed

Etymology

Formed from 'on' + 'going', meaning in progress or continuing.
