quilted

1. ADJ

/ ˈkwɪltɪd / (AmE), / ˈkwɪltɪd / (BrE)
made of layers of fabric sewn together by patterned stitching, often for warmth or decoration.
having layers of stitched fabric

Examples

She bought a quilted jacket to keep warm during the winter.
The bedspread was beautifully quilted with a floral pattern.
He sat on a quilted cushion that was both comfortable and decorative.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: padded - having extra material for comfort or protection

Etymology

From 'quilt' + -ed, indicating past participle or adjectival form.
