olive

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑlɪv / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv / (BrE)
Of a yellow-green color similar to that of an unripe olive.
yellow-green in color

Examples

She chose an olive dress that complemented her skin tone.
The walls were painted in an olive shade to give the room a natural feel.
His jacket was a dull olive, blending in with the forest surroundings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: olive-green - A shade of green resembling unripe olives

Etymology

From Latin oliva (olive tree, olive fruit), referring to the color of the unripe fruit.

Examples
