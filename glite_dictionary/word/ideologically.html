ideologically

1. ADV

/ ˌaɪdiəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌaɪdiəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to ideology or a system of ideas and beliefs.
in a way relating to ideology

Examples

The two parties are ideologically opposed on almost every major issue.
She approaches her work ideologically, always considering the underlying beliefs behind her actions.
They disagreed ideologically, but still managed to cooperate on some projects.

Synset Examples

ideologically, we do not see eye to eye

Related Terms

  • Synonym: politically - in a way relating to politics
