Ld.

1. NOUN

/ ɛl diː / (AmE), / ɛl diː / (BrE)
Abbreviation of 'Limited', used after the names of companies that offer limited liability to their owners, common in British and Commonwealth usage.
abbreviation for 'Limited' in company names
british

Examples

The company officially registered as Smith & Sons Ld. in 1921.
Several major engineering firms in the UK are designated with Ld. after their names.
He invested in Johnson Manufacturing Ld., attracted by its stable growth and limited liability status.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ltd. - Alternative abbreviation for 'Limited', especially in British company names

Etymology

Abbreviated from the English word 'Limited', as used in company law.
