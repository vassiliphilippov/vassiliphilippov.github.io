dressy

1. ADJ

/ ˈdrɛsi / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsi / (BrE)
(of clothes or occasions) suitable for formal or elegant events; smart or stylish.
suitable for formal or elegant occasions

Examples

The invitation said to wear something dressy, so she chose her favorite cocktail dress.
He bought a dressy pair of shoes for the wedding.
Even in jeans, she managed to look surprisingly dressy with her elegant blouse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: formal - suitable for a serious or official occasion
  • Antonym: casual - suitable for everyday, informal occasions

Etymology

From 'dress' plus the suffix '-y', meaning 'having the quality of'.
