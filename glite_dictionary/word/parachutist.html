parachutist

1. NOUN

/ ˈpærəˌʃutɪst / (AmE), / ˈpærəˌʃuːtɪst / (BrE)
a person who jumps from an aircraft using a parachute
person who jumps with a parachute

Examples

The experienced parachutist landed safely in the designated zone.
During the air show, a parachutist performed breathtaking stunts in the sky.
The parachutists were warmly welcomed by the cheering crowd after their jump.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skydiver - a person who makes a parachute jump for sport, often with acrobatics
  • Synonym: paratrooper - a member of the armed forces trained to parachute into an operation

Etymology

From 'parachute' (from French, ultimately from Italian 'para', to protect, and 'scudo', shield) plus English '-ist'.
