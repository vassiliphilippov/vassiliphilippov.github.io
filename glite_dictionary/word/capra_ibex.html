Capra ibex

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæprə ˈaɪˌbɛks / (AmE), / ˈkæprə ˈɪːbɛks / (BrE)
a wild goat native to mountainous regions of Eurasia and northern Africa, distinguished by its large, backward-curving horns
wild mountain goat species

Examples

The steep cliffs provide a safe habitat for the Capra ibex.
Zoologists have studied the Capra ibex to understand its climbing abilities.
Tourists were thrilled to spot a group of Capra ibex in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ibex - wild goat with large curved horns inhabiting mountainous regions

Etymology

Modern Latin, from Latin 'capra' (goat) and 'ibex' (wild goat).
