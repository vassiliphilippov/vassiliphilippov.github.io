flameproof

1. ADJ

/ ˈfleɪmˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈfleɪmˌpruːf / (BrE)
Resistant to catching fire; not easily ignited or set alight.
resistant to catching fire

Examples

All staff must wear flameproof clothing in the laboratory for safety reasons.
The company developed a new flameproof fabric for use in children's sleepwear.
You should check that the curtains are flameproof before installing them in the theater.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fireproof - resistant to fire
