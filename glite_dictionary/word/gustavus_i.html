Gustavus I

1. PROPN

/ ɡəˈsteɪvəs ðə fɜrst / (AmE), / ɡʊˈstɑːvəs ðə fɜːst / (BrE)
(1496–1560) King of Sweden who broke with the Catholic Church and established Lutheranism as the state religion.
King of Sweden (1496–1560); introduced Lutheranism

Examples

Gustavus I was instrumental in bringing about the Protestant Reformation in Sweden.
Many historians regard Gustavus I as the founder of modern Sweden.
The reign of Gustavus I marked the transition from Catholicism to Lutheranism in the country.
