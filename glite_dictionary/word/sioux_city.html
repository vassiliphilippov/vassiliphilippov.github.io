Sioux City

1. PROPN

/ suː ˈsɪti / (AmE), / suː ˈsɪti / (BrE)
A city in northeastern Iowa, United States, located at the confluence of the Big Sioux River and the Missouri River.
city in northeastern Iowa, USA

Examples

My cousins moved to Sioux City last summer for a new job opportunity.
The Missouri River runs along the edge of Sioux City, making it an important hub for trade.
Every year, Sioux City hosts a popular music and arts festival that attracts visitors from across the region.

Etymology

Named after the Sioux Native American people; 'Sioux' from the French adaptation of the Ojibwe 'Nadouessioux'.
