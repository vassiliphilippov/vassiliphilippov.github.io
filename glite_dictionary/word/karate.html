karate

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɑːti / (AmE), / kəˈrɑːti / (BrE)
A Japanese martial art using unarmed combat techniques, characterized by sharp blows and kicks to pressure points on the opponent's body.
Japanese martial art of unarmed combat

Examples

She has studied karate for several years and recently earned her black belt.
The fitness center offers classes in karate for both children and adults.
During the demonstration, he broke wooden boards with swift karate chops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: judo - a Japanese martial art focusing on throws and holds

Etymology

From Japanese 空手 (karate, literally ‘empty hand’).
