ballup

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔlˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlˌʌp / (BrE)
A confused, disorganized, or badly botched situation or event.
a muddled or badly handled situation
informal

Examples

The new project turned into a complete ballup after the team lost the files.
His first attempt at cooking was a total ballup, but he laughed it off.
There was such a ballup with the travel arrangements that we missed our flight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mess - a situation that is dirty or not tidy, or full of problems
  • Synonym: shambles - a condition of great disorder or confusion
