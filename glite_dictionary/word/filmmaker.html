filmmaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪlmˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈfɪlmˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes films, especially as a director or producer
person who makes films

Examples

The acclaimed filmmaker won several awards for his latest documentary.
Aspiring filmmakers often start by making short movies on a small budget.
She dreams of becoming a filmmaker and telling meaningful stories through cinema.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: director - person responsible for overseeing the artistic aspects of a film
  • Synonym: producer - person responsible for the financial and managerial aspects of a film

Etymology

From 'film' + 'maker', first used in the early 20th century.
