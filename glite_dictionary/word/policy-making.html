policy-making

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɑːl.ɪ.siˌmeɪ.kɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɒl.ɪ.siˌmeɪ.kɪŋ / (BrE)
concerned with the formulation of policies, rather than their administration or implementation.
concerned with formulating policies

Examples

The committee is responsible for policy-making at the highest level of government.
His policy-making role means he rarely deals with the day-to-day operations of the department.
Effective policy-making requires broad consultation and careful consideration of long-term impacts.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: administrative - relating to the running of a business, organization, or institution
