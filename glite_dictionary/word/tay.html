Tay

1. NOUN

/ teɪ / (AmE), / teɪ / (BrE)
the language of the Tay people, a member of the Tai language family spoken mainly in northern and central Vietnam
a Tai language spoken in Vietnam

Examples

About 1.6 million people speak Tay in northern Vietnam.
She is fluent in both Tay and Vietnamese.
Linguists have documented several dialects of the Tay language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tai - a family of languages spoken in Southeast Asia including Thai and Lao
