nominated

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɑːməˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈnɒmɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
officially appointed or proposed for a position, award, or honor by nomination.
appointed or proposed by nomination

Examples

Three artists have been nominated for the prestigious award this year.
She was nominated as the new chairperson by the committee.
Only nominated candidates can appear on the final ballot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appointed - officially assigned to a role or position
  • Antonym: elect - chosen by a vote, rather than nomination

Etymology

From Latin nominatus, past participle of nominare (to name) via French and English.
