ridgeling

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪdʒlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪdʒlɪŋ / (BrE)
A male horse, especially a colt, with one or both testicles undescended.
male horse with undescended testicles

Examples

The veterinarian diagnosed the colt as a ridgeling after the examination.
Because he was a ridgeling, the horse needed special surgery to correct the condition.
Breeders often check young stallions to make sure none are ridgelings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cryptorchid - an animal with undescended testicles (general)

Etymology

From ridge (possibly referring to the location of the undescended testicle) + -ling (diminutive/noun suffix).
