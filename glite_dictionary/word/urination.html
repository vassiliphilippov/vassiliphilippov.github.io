urination

1. NOUN

/ ˌjʊrɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌjʊərɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of discharging urine from the body
the discharge of urine

Examples

Frequent urination can be a sign of a medical condition such as diabetes.
Children are often taught proper urination habits during toilet training.
The doctor asked if he had any issues with urination or bladder control.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: micturition - the act of passing urine

Etymology

From Latin 'urinatio', from 'urinari' (to dive, to urinate), from 'urina' (urine).
