poulet

1. NOUN

/ puːˈleɪ / (AmE), / puːˈleɪ / (BrE)
The flesh of a chicken used as food, especially in French cuisine.
chicken meat (culinary)

Examples

The restaurant's signature dish is poulet rôti with herbs de Provence.
For tonight’s dinner, I’m preparing a classic French poulet fricassée.
She ordered poulet chasseur, a chicken dish cooked with mushrooms and wine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken - the flesh of a domestic fowl used as food

Etymology

From French 'poulet' (chicken).
