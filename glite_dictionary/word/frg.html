FRG

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛf ˌɑr ˈdʒi / (AmE), / ˌɛf ˌɑː ˈdʒiː / (BrE)
abbreviation for 'Federal Republic of Germany', referring especially to West Germany before German reunification in 1990.
abbreviation for 'Federal Republic of Germany' (West Germany)

Examples

The FRG was admitted to the United Nations in 1973.
Many West Germans traveled freely under the laws of the FRG.
After reunification, the FRG continued as the legal successor to West Germany.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: West Germany - the former country consisting of the western part of Germany before 1990
  • Antonym: East Germany - the former country consisting of the eastern part of Germany before 1990

Etymology

Initialism of Federal Republic of Germany.
