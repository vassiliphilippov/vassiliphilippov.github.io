telephonist

1. NOUN

/ təˈlɛfənɪst / (AmE), / təˈlɛfənɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to connect telephone calls and assist callers in reaching the correct person or department, especially at a large organization or telephone exchange
person connecting telephone calls

Examples

The telephonist answered my call promptly and directed me to the correct extension.
In the past, a telephonist was essential for connecting long-distance calls.
She worked as a telephonist in a busy city hospital.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: operator - a person who operates a telephone switchboard or assists with calls

Etymology

From telephone + -ist; modelled on French téléphoniste.
