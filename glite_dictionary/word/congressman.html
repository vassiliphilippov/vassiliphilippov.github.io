congressman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑŋɡrɪsmən / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡrɪsmən / (BrE)
A male member of the United States House of Representatives.
male U.S. House of Representatives member
american

Examples

The congressman spoke passionately about education reform during the debate.
A local congressman introduced the new bill to Congress this week.
Many voters contacted their congressman to express their concerns about the proposed law.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: congresswoman - a female member of the United States House of Representatives
  • Synonym: representative - member of the United States House of Representatives (regardless of gender)

Etymology

From 'congress' + 'man', referring to a male member of Congress.
