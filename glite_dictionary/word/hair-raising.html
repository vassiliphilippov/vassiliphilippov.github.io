hair-raising

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛrˌreɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈheəˌreɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely alarming or frightening; causing intense fear or excitement.
extremely alarming or frightening

Examples

The hikers had a hair-raising experience when they encountered a bear in the woods.
She told a hair-raising story about driving through a storm at night.
The movie’s hair-raising scenes kept everyone on the edge of their seats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrifying - causing intense fear
  • Synonym: spine-chilling - causing a feeling of fear or excitement

Etymology

From the idea that extreme fear or excitement makes the hair stand on end.
