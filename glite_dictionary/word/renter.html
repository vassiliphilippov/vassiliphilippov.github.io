renter

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɛnt.ə / (BrE)
a person who pays to use land, a building, a vehicle, or other property that belongs to someone else
person who pays to use property

Examples

The renter signed a one-year lease for the apartment.
If a renter damages the car, they are responsible for the repair costs.
Every renter in this building must follow the same set of rules.

Synset Examples

the landlord can evict a tenant who doesn't pay the rent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tenant - a person who occupies land or property rented from a landlord
  • Antonym: landlord - a person who owns and rents out property

Etymology

From English 'rent' + -er (agent noun suffix).
