stooping

1. ADJ

/ ˈstuːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstuːpɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person) having the back and shoulders rounded or bent forward; not standing upright.
having a bent or rounded posture

Examples

The stooping old man slowly made his way down the street.
Years of hard work in the fields left her with a stooping posture.
The doctor noticed the stooping figure of his patient as she entered the room.

Synset Examples

a little oldish misshapen stooping woman

Related Terms

  • Antonym: erect - standing upright or straight
  • Synonym: slouching - sitting, standing, or moving in a lazy, drooping way
