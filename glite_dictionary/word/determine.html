to determine

1. VERB

/ dɪˈtɝːmɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪn / (BrE)
to establish or fix the limits or boundaries of something
fix or establish boundaries

Examples

A fence will determine the exact edge of the park.
Surveyors were called in to determine the boundary between the two properties.
The river determines the southern limit of their land.

Synset Examples

the tree determines the border of the property

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to define - to mark out or describe the limits of something

Etymology

From Latin determinare, meaning to limit or settle.
