Noctiluca

1. NOUN

/ nɑk.tɪˈlu.kə / (AmE), / nɒk.tɪˈluː.kə / (BrE)
a genus of marine protozoans, containing the species Noctiluca scintillans, noted for producing bioluminescence known as 'sea sparkle'
genus of bioluminescent marine protozoans
medical

Examples

Scientists observed thousands of Noctiluca glowing in the coastal waters during the night.
The bioluminescence of Noctiluca can create a stunning blue glow in the ocean.
Noctiluca outbreaks can affect marine ecosystems and local fisheries.

Etymology

From Latin *noctiluca* meaning 'night-shiner', from *nox* (night) + *lucere* (to shine).
