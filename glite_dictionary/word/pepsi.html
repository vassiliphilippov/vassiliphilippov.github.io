Pepsi

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɛp.si / (AmE), / ˈpɛp.si / (BrE)
A trademarked brand of sweet, carbonated cola drink produced by PepsiCo.
brand of cola drink
american

Examples

She ordered a Pepsi with her lunch at the restaurant.
The vending machine ran out of Pepsi during the game.
Would you like a Pepsi or a Coke with your burger?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Coke - A competing brand of cola soft drink

Etymology

From the trademark 'Pepsi-Cola', coined in 1898, possibly from 'pepsin' and 'cola'.
