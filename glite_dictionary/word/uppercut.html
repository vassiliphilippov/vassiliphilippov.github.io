uppercut

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌpərkʌt / (AmE), / ˈʌpəkʌt / (BrE)
A punch in boxing or martial arts delivered in an upward motion, typically targeting the opponent's chin.
an upward punch in boxing

Examples

He knocked out his opponent with a powerful uppercut to the jaw.
During the match, she dodged a punch and landed a clean uppercut.
The textbook uppercut is one of the most effective moves in boxing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jab - a quick, straight punch in boxing
  • Synonym: hook - a punch in boxing delivered in a curving motion

Etymology

Formed from 'upper' + 'cut', referring to the upward motion of the strike.
