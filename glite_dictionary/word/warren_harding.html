Warren Harding

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɔːrən ˈhɑrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɒrən ˈhɑːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the 29th President of the United States (1865–1923), whose administration was marred by scandals such as Teapot Dome
29th U.S. President (1865–1923)

Examples

Warren Harding served as President of the United States from 1921 to 1923.
Many historians discuss the scandals that occurred during Warren Harding's administration.
The Teapot Dome scandal significantly affected Warren Harding's reputation after his death.
