unexampled

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪɡˈzæm.pəld / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪɡˈzɑːm.pəld / (BrE)
having no previous example, precedent, or parallel; unprecedented.
without precedent or parallel

Examples

The company achieved unexampled success in the international market that year.
Her kindness during the crisis was unexampled in the community's history.
It was a period of unexampled growth and innovation for the industry.

Synset Examples

a time of unexampled prosperity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unprecedented - never done or known before

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'exampled', based on 'example' (Latin exemplum).
