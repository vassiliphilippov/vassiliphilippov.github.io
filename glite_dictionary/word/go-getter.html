go-getter

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊ ˌɡɛtər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ ˌɡɛtə / (BrE)
a person who is energetic, determined, and ambitious, especially in achieving success in their career or goals
ambitious and energetic person

Examples

Samantha is a real go-getter who always seeks out new opportunities at work.
The company is looking for a go-getter to join their energetic sales team.
As a go-getter, he quickly rose through the ranks in just two years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high-flyer - a person who is very successful or likely to be successful
  • Antonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort
