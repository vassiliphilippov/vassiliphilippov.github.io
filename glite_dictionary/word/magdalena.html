Magdalena

1. PROPN

/ ˌmæɡdəˈleɪnə / (AmE), / ˌmaɡdəˈleɪnə / (BrE)
a major river in Colombia, rising in the Andes and flowing north to the Caribbean Sea at Barranquilla
river in Colombia

Examples

The Magdalena is the longest river in Colombia, supporting much of the country's trade.
Fishermen depend on the Magdalena for their daily catch and transportation.
During the rainy season, the Magdalena sometimes floods its surrounding areas.

Etymology

Named after Mary Magdalene; Spanish 'Magdalena'.
