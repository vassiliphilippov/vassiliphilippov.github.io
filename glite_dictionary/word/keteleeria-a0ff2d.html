keteleeria

1. NOUN

/ kɛˌtɛliˈɪriə / (AmE), / kɛˌtɛliˈɪəriə / (BrE)
A genus of Asiatic coniferous trees in the pine family, resembling firs.
genus of Asiatic coniferous trees

Examples

The keteleeria thrives in the mountain forests of eastern Asia.
Botanists recently discovered a rare species of keteleeria in China.
Unlike true firs, keteleeria cones fall apart to release their seeds.

Etymology

Named after Jean-Baptiste Keteleer (1813–1903), Belgian horticulturist.
