conversationist

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːn.vərˈseɪ.ʃən.ɪst / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.vəˈseɪ.ʃən.ɪst / (BrE)
a person who is skilled at engaging in conversation
person skilled at conversation

Examples

As a conversationist, she was always the center of attention at parties.
He's such a good conversationist that talking with him never feels awkward.
The company was looking for a skilled conversationist to handle client meetings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conversationalist - someone skilled at conversation

Etymology

From 'conversation' + '-ist' (person engaged in or skilled at something).
