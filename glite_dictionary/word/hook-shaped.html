hook-shaped

1. ADJ

/ hʊk ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / hʊk ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape or form of a hook.
shaped like a hook

Examples

The bird had a hook-shaped beak adapted for tearing meat.
She wore a hook-shaped earring in her left ear.
The river makes a hook-shaped bend just before entering the valley.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curved - having a smooth, rounded shape; bent
