humorous

1. ADJ

/ ˈhjuːmərəs / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmərəs / (BrE)
full of or characterized by humor; funny or amusing
funny; amusing

Examples

His humorous remarks always lighten the mood in the office.
The children loved the humorous cartoons in the magazine.
She wrote a humorous article about her travel mishaps.

Synset Examples

humorous stories
humorous cartoons
in a humorous vein

Related Terms

  • Synonym: funny - causing laughter or amusement
  • Antonym: serious - not characterized by humor; solemn

Etymology

From Latin 'humorosus' (full of fluid, moist, or wet), later influenced by associations with 'humor' (mood, comedy) in English.

funny; amusing; full of humor

Examples

humorous stories

Related Terms

  • Synonym: funny - causing laughter or amusement
