Richea dracophylla

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪkiə ˌdrækəˈfɪlə / (AmE), / ˈrɪkiə ˌdrakəˈfɪlə / (BrE)
a stout shrub native to Australia, with narrow leaves clustered at the ends of branches and terminal clusters of white or pink flowers
Australian shrub with narrow clustered leaves

Examples

The Richea dracophylla grows abundantly in the highlands of Tasmania.
In spring, the Richea dracophylla produces stunning clusters of pink flowers.
Botanists studied the adaptations of Richea dracophylla to its mountainous environment.
