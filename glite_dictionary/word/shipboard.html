shipboard

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɪpˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpˌbɔːd / (BrE)
existing, happening, or done on board a ship
on or relating to a ship

Examples

There was a sense of camaraderie among the shipboard crew during the long voyage.
The novel explores a shipboard romance that develops on a cruise ship.
They spent hours engaging in shipboard activities to pass the time at sea.
