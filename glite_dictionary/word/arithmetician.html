arithmetician

1. NOUN

/ əˌrɪθ.məˈtɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / əˌrɪθ.mɪˈtɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a person who specializes in or is skilled at arithmetic
person skilled in arithmetic

Examples

The famous arithmetician developed new methods for solving problems involving large numbers.
As an arithmetician, she was often consulted to verify financial calculations for the company.
The book highlighted how an arithmetician can make even complex computations simple to understand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mathematician - a person skilled or interested in mathematics
  • Synonym: arithmetist - an expert in arithmetic

Etymology

From Latin arithmetica via Old French arismeticien.
