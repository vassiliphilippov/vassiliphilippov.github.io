butt-ugly

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʌtˌʌɡli / (AmE), / ˈbʌtˌʌɡli / (BrE)
extremely unattractive or ugly; very unpleasant to look at
extremely unattractive; very ugly
informal slang

Examples

That building is just butt-ugly—I can't believe anyone would design it that way.
She refused to wear the butt-ugly hat her aunt gave her for Christmas.
Some people think the car is unique, but most agree it's butt-ugly.

Synset Examples

That design is just butt-ugly and needs a makeover.
He wore a butt-ugly sweater to the party.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: ugly - unpleasant or unattractive in appearance
  • Degree Higher: hideous - extremely ugly or shocking

Etymology

Blend of 'butt' (slang for buttocks) and 'ugly', used for exaggeration.
