Mytilene

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɪtɪˈliːni / (AmE), / ˌmɪtɪˈliːni / (BrE)
an island in eastern Greece, located in the eastern Aegean Sea, historically renowned for its lyric poetry in antiquity
island in eastern Greece, Aegean Sea

Examples

The poet Sappho was born on Mytilene, which greatly influenced her work.
Ancient Mytilene was a center of art and literature in the Greek world.
Tourists flock to Mytilene to explore its picturesque landscapes and historic sites.

Etymology

From Greek Μυtilηνή (Mytēlḗnē), the name of the city's capital and the island.
