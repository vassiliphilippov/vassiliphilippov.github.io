companionability

1. NOUN

/ kəmˌpæniənəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / kəmˌpanɪənəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being a pleasant or suitable companion
the quality of being companionable

Examples

Her companionability made her popular among her colleagues on group trips.
We immediately appreciated his companionability and sense of humor at the dinner table.
Many people value companionability over shared interests in relationships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: companionship - the state of being with someone; fellowship
  • Synonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly, kind, and pleasant

Etymology

From 'companionable' + '-ity', patterning after Latin-derived abstract nouns.
