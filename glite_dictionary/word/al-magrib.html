Al-Magrib

1. PROPN

/ æl ˈmæɡrɪb / (AmE), / æl ˈmæɡrɪb / (BrE)
An alternative name for Morocco, a kingdom in northwestern Africa with a largely Muslim population.
alternative name for Morocco

Examples

The ancient city in Al-Magrib is known for its vibrant markets and rich history.
Many travelers are drawn to the enchanting culture of Al-Magrib.
French is widely spoken in Al-Magrib due to its colonial past.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Morocco - A country in northwestern Africa, also known as the Kingdom of Morocco

Etymology

From Arabic 'al-Maghrib' meaning 'the west'.
