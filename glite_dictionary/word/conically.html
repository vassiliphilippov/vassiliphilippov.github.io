conically

1. ADV

/ ˈkɑːnɪkli / (AmE), / ˈkɒnɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner resembling or shaped like a cone.
in a cone-like manner

Examples

The mountain rises conically from the valley floor, dominating the landscape.
The seeds are arranged conically inside the pine cone.
Smoke billowed conically from the chimney on the old cottage.

Synset Examples

conically shaped

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conical - having the shape of a cone
