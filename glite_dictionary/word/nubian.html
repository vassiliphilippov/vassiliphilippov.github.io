Nubian

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuː.bi.ən / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.bi.ən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Nubia, a region along the Nile river in southern Egypt and northern Sudan
native or inhabitant of Nubia

Examples

Many Nubians were relocated after the construction of the Aswan High Dam.
The Nubian people have a rich and ancient cultural heritage.
He traveled to southern Egypt to study the traditions of the Nubian population.

Synset Examples

Nubians now form an ethnic minority in Egypt

Etymology

From Latin 'Nubia', referring to the region of Nubia.
