ratable

1. ADJ

/ ˈreɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈreɪtəbəl / (BrE)
(of property) liable to payment of locally assessed property taxes.
liable to local property taxes
legal

Examples

The city council debated whether the newly constructed building was ratable or exempt from taxes.
Some properties are not ratable if they belong to charitable organizations.
The government updated the list of ratable properties for the coming tax year.

Synset Examples

the ratable value of property

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxable - subject to tax

Etymology

From rate (tax or assessment) + -able, meaning 'able to be rated or assessed'.
