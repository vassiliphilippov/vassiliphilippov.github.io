Jeddah

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɛdə / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛdə / (BrE)
a port city in western Saudi Arabia on the Red Sea, near Mecca; the country's commercial center.
Saudi Arabian port city on the Red Sea

Examples

Jeddah is known for its beautiful waterfront and historic sites.
Many international flights to Saudi Arabia land at Jeddah before pilgrims travel to Mecca.
The economic growth of Jeddah has transformed it into a bustling metropolis.

Etymology

From Arabic جدة (Jidda or Jeddah).
