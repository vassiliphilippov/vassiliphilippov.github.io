vanadinite

1. NOUN

/ vəˈnædəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / vəˈnadɪnʌɪt / (BrE)
a red or yellow mineral consisting of lead chlorovanadate, and an important source of vanadium
heavy mineral; ore of vanadium

Examples

The geologist discovered a rare specimen of vanadinite while surveying the area.
Vanadinite is often mined for its high vanadium content.
The bright red crystals in the collection were identified as vanadinite.

Etymology

From vanadium (chemical element) + -ite (mineral suffix).
