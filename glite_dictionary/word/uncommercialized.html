uncommercialized

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkəˈmɝːʃələˌaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈmɜːʃəlaɪzd / (BrE)
not developed or exploited for commercial purposes; not made commercial
not made commercial

Examples

The village remained uncommercialized despite the increase in visitors each year.
She preferred the uncommercialized beaches that still retained their natural beauty.
Many tourists seek an uncommercialized experience when traveling to remote areas.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: commercialized - developed or exploited for commercial purposes
