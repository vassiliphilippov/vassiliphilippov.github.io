Bela Bartok

1. PROPN

/ ˈbeɪlə ˈbɑːrtoʊk / (AmE), / ˈbeɪlə ˈbɑːtɒk / (BrE)
(1881–1945) Hungarian composer and pianist known for collecting Hungarian folk music and for his influential works in classical music.
Hungarian composer and pianist (1881–1945)

Examples

Many music historians regard Bela Bartok as one of the greatest composers of the twentieth century.
During his lifetime, Bela Bartok was celebrated for integrating folk melodies into classical music.
The orchestra will perform several pieces by Bela Bartok in their upcoming concert.
