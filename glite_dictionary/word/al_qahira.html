Al Qahira

1. PROPN

/ æl kɑːˈhɪərə / (AmE), / æl kɑːˈhɪərə / (BrE)
The official Arabic name for Cairo, the capital and largest city of Egypt, located near the Nile delta.
official Arabic name for Cairo

Examples

Many travelers are fascinated by the rich history of Al Qahira and its ancient monuments.
The Nile River flows through Al Qahira, providing life to the city's millions of residents.
The museums and vibrant marketplaces in Al Qahira make it a top destination in Africa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cairo - the capital city of Egypt, known as Al Qahira in Arabic

Etymology

From Arabic القَاهِرَة (al-Qāhira), meaning 'The Conqueror'.
