Chinawoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪnəˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪnəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
An offensive term for a woman of Chinese descent.
offensive slur for Chinese woman
offensive

Examples

The word Chinawoman is widely regarded as highly offensive and should not be used.
He was criticized for referring to her as a Chinawoman in his remarks.
The pamphlet was condemned for its derogatory language, including the use of the word Chinawoman.
