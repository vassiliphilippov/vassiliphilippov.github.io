procurable

1. ADJ

/ proˈkjʊrəbəl / (AmE), / prəˈkjʊərəbəl / (BrE)
able to be obtained or acquired; obtainable.
able to be obtained

Examples

Many rare spices are not easily procurable in local shops.
She assured us that the required documents are procurable within a few days.
The medication is only procurable with a doctor's prescription.

Synset Examples

savings of up to 50 percent are obtainable

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obtainable - able to be obtained
  • Antonym: unattainable - unable to be obtained

Etymology

From Latin procurabilis, from procurare (to take care of, obtain).
