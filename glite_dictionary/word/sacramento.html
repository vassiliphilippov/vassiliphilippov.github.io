Sacramento

1. PROPN

/ ˌsækrəˈmɛntoʊ / (AmE), / ˌsækrəˈmɛntəʊ / (BrE)
the capital city of California, located in north central California on the Sacramento River
capital city of California, USA

Examples

We flew to Sacramento for a business conference last summer.
Sacramento has a rich history dating back to its founding during the Gold Rush era.
The California State Capitol is located in downtown Sacramento.

Etymology

From Spanish 'sacramento' meaning 'sacrament'.
