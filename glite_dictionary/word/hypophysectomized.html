hypophysectomized

1. ADJ

/ haɪˌpɑfəˈsɛktəˌmaɪzd / (AmE), / hɪˌpɒfɪˈsɛktəˌmaɪzd / (BrE)
Having had the pituitary gland removed by surgery.
having pituitary gland surgically removed
medical

Examples

Researchers used hypophysectomized rats to study hormonal regulation in the absence of pituitary function.
The drug had a different effect on hypophysectomized animals compared to those with intact pituitary glands.
Growth rates in hypophysectomized tadpoles are significantly reduced compared to normal specimens.

Synset Examples

hypophysectomized tadpoles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hypophysectomy - surgical removal of the pituitary gland

Etymology

From hypophysectomy (removal of the pituitary gland) + -ized (forming adjectives).
