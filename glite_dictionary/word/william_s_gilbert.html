William S. Gilbert

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪljəm ɛs ˈɡɪlbɚt / (AmE), / ˈwɪljəm ɛs ˈɡɪlbət / (BrE)
(1836–1911) English librettist and dramatist, best known for collaborating with Sir Arthur Sullivan on a series of comic operettas.
