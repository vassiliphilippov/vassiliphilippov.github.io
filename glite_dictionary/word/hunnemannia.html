Hunnemannia

1. NOUN

/ hʌnəˈmɛniə / (AmE), / hʌnɪˈmiːnɪə / (BrE)
A genus of herbaceous flowering plants in the poppy family, with a single species known for its bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers (commonly called golden cup).
genus of bright yellow-flowered plant (golden cup)

Examples

The Hunnemannia blooms profusely in sunny gardens, adding a splash of yellow to flower beds.
Botanists admire the unique structure of the Hunnemannia flower, which resembles a golden cup.
Many gardeners cultivate Hunnemannia for its striking color and relative hardiness.

Etymology

Named after English botanist John Hunnemann (1760–1839).
