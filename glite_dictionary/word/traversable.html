traversable

1. ADJ

/ trəˈvɝːsəbəl / (AmE), / trəˈvɜːsəbl / (BrE)
Able to be traversed or crossed, especially by walking, driving, or traveling over.
able to be crossed or traveled over

Examples

The mountain path was finally traversable after the snow melted.
They needed to find a traversable route through the dense forest.
The bridge made the river traversable for vehicles and pedestrians.

Synset Examples

highway crews soon had the roads travelable

Related Terms

  • Antonym: impassable - not able to be crossed or traveled over

Etymology

From traverse + -able, in turn from Latin transversare (to cross).
