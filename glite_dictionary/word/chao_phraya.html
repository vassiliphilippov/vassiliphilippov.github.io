Chao Phraya

1. PROPN

/ tʃaʊ prɑːˈjɑː / (AmE), / tʃaʊ prɑːˈjɑː / (BrE)
a major river in Thailand that flows south through Bangkok into the Gulf of Thailand
major river in Thailand

Examples

The Chao Phraya River is often referred to as the lifeblood of Bangkok.
Many traditional houses line the banks of the Chao Phraya as it passes through the city.
Tourists can take a boat tour along the Chao Phraya to see famous landmarks.
