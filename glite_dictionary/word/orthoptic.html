orthoptic

1. ADJ

/ ɔrˈθɑptɪk / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɒptɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving normal binocular vision or the correction of visual disorders affecting it.
relating to normal binocular vision
medical

Examples

The clinic specializes in orthoptic exercises to help improve eye coordination.
All patients received a thorough orthoptic assessment before treatment began.
She enrolled in an orthoptic training program to become a specialist in visual therapy.

Etymology

From Greek 'orthos' (straight, correct) + 'opsis' (sight).
