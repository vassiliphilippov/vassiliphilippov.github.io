elvish

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛlvɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɛlvɪʃ / (BrE)
Playfully mischievous in a manner resembling elves; impish or sprightly.
impish; mischievous like an elf

Examples

The child had an elvish glint in her eye as she plotted her prank.
He told the story with an elvish smile, enjoying the surprise it brought.
There was something delightfully elvish about her playful sense of humor.

Synset Examples

perpetrated a practical joke with elfin delight
elvish tricks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elfin - small, lively, and mischievous like an elf
  • Synonym: impish - mischievous or playful like an imp

Etymology

From elf + -ish, meaning 'like an elf'. 'Elf' from Old English 'ælf'.
