trencherwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɛntʃərˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈtrɛntʃəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who eats and drinks to excess; a female glutton.
woman who overeats or drinks to excess

Examples

The legend described her as a remarkable trencherwoman at feasts and celebrations.
Despite her petite frame, she had the reputation of being quite the trencherwoman at banquets.
In old comedies, the character of the trencherwoman was often used for humorous effect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trencherwoman - male equivalent: a man who eats and drinks to excess
  • Synonym: glutton - a person who eats and drinks excessively
  • Antonym: abstemious - not self-indulgent, especially with food or drink

Etymology

Formed from 'trencher' (a wooden plate or one who eats heartily) and 'woman', modeled on 'trencher-man'.
