RUF

1. PROPN

/ ɑr ju ɛf / (AmE), / ɑː juː ɛf / (BrE)
a rebel group in Sierra Leone (Revolutionary United Front), notorious for seeking to overthrow the government and committing widespread atrocities during the civil war in the 1990s.
Sierra Leone rebel group (civil war)

Examples

The RUF caused immense suffering during Sierra Leone's civil war from 1991 to 2002.
International agencies documented numerous human rights abuses committed by the RUF.
Many former child soldiers were forced to fight for the RUF during the conflict.

Etymology

Acronym of Revolutionary United Front, formed in Sierra Leone in the 1980s.
