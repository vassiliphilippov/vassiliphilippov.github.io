Belmont

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɛlˌmɑnt / (AmE), / ˈbɛl.mɒnt / (BrE)
a racetrack for thoroughbred horse racing in Elmont, Long Island, famous as the venue for the Belmont Stakes
major thoroughbred racetrack on Long Island
american

Examples

Every June, crowds flock to Belmont to witness the last leg of the Triple Crown.
The history of Belmont includes many legendary races and famous horses.
Tickets for this year’s Belmont sold out in record time.

Related Terms
