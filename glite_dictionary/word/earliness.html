earliness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɝːli.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɜːli.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or condition of being early or occurring before the expected or usual time
the state of being early

Examples

The earliness of his arrival took everyone by surprise.
She was complimented on the earliness of her submission.
Farmers are pleased with the earliness of this year's harvest.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lateness - the quality or condition of being late
  • Synonym: promptness - the quality of acting quickly or on time

Etymology

From 'early' + '-ness', forming a noun to denote the state or quality.
