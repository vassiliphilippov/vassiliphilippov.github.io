anti-choicer

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.tiˈtʃɔɪ.sɚ / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈtʃɔɪ.sə / (BrE)
a person who opposes abortion rights
opponent of abortion rights
informal

Examples

Several anti-choicer activists spoke at the city council meeting.
The article criticized the anti-choicer perspective on reproductive health.
He didn't want to be labeled an anti-choicer, but he had concerns about abortion.

Synset Examples

The anti-choicer group organized a protest downtown.
The anti-choicer rallied for stricter abortion laws.
She identified herself as an anti-choicer at the rally.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pro-choicer - supporter of abortion rights
  • Synonym: anti-abortionist - person opposed to abortion

Etymology

Formed from 'anti-' (against) and 'choice', referring to opposition to the pro-choice position.
