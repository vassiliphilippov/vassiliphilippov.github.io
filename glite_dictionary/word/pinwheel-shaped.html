pinwheel-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɪnˌwiːl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌwiːl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a round, spiral form similar to that of a pinwheel
in the form of a pinwheel

Examples

The architect designed a pinwheel-shaped building that stood out in the city skyline.
Children created pinwheel-shaped patterns with colored chalk on the sidewalk.
Scientists observed pinwheel-shaped galaxies through the powerful telescope.
