annum

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.əm / (AmE), / ˈan.əm / (BrE)
a year, especially used in phrases such as 'per annum' to mean 'per year'
a year (formal, esp. in 'per annum')

Examples

The account pays 5% interest per annum.
Her salary exceeds $60,000 per annum.
Membership fees are charged on an annum basis.

Synset Examples

per annum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: year - a period of twelve months or 365/366 days

Etymology

From Latin 'annum' (year).
