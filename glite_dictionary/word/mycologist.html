mycologist

1. NOUN

/ maɪˈkɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / maɪˈkɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who specializes in the study of fungi
a scientist studying fungi
medical

Examples

The mycologist spent years researching rare mushroom species in the rainforest.
A mycologist can identify hundreds of different fungi by sight alone.
When the strange fungus appeared in the garden, we consulted a local mycologist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: botanist - a scientist who studies plants
  • Synonym: fungologist - a specialist in the study of fungi

Etymology

From New Latin 'mycologia' (study of fungi) + '-ist'.
