Taiyuan

1. PROPN

/ taɪˈjʊɛn / (AmE), / taɪˈjʊən / (BrE)
an industrial city in northeastern China, known for its coal mining and steel production
industrial city in northeastern China

Examples

Many factories in Taiyuan contribute to the city's strong steel industry.
