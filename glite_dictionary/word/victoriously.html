victoriously

1. ADV

/ vɪkˈtɔriəsli / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔːriəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows victory or success after a struggle or competition.
in a victorious manner

Examples

The team celebrated victoriously after winning the championship match.
She smiled victoriously as she solved the final puzzle before anyone else.
They marched victoriously through the streets, cheered by the waiting crowds.

Synset Examples

Virginia had defended her land victoriously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triumphantly - in a way that shows triumph or great pride after a victory
  • Antonym: defeatedly - in a way that shows defeat or lack of success

Etymology

From 'victorious' + '-ly', ultimately from Latin 'victoria' (victory).
