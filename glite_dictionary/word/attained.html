attained

1. ADJ

/ əˈteɪnd / (AmE), / əˈteɪnd / (BrE)
achieved or reached, especially as a goal or level; having been attained
achieved or reached (adjective)

Examples

The team's attained goals were celebrated by everyone at the company.
Her attained age reflects a lifetime of experience and wisdom.
This document certifies your attained level of proficiency in English.

Synset Examples

the actual attained achievement test score

Related Terms

  • Synonym: achieved - successfully brought about or reached as a result of effort

Etymology

From the verb 'attain', which comes from Old French 'ataindre', from Latin 'attingere' (to reach, touch).
