dependableness

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɛndəbəlnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈpɛndəblnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being trustworthy and reliable; the state of being dependable.
the quality of being reliable or trustworthy

Examples

Her dependableness made her the ideal choice for team leader.
Employers value dependableness just as much as skill and experience.
The dependableness of the public transport system is often questioned by commuters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reliability - the quality of being reliable
  • Antonym: unreliability - the lack of reliability or dependableness

Etymology

Formed from 'dependable' + '-ness', where 'dependable' derives from 'depend' + '-able'.
