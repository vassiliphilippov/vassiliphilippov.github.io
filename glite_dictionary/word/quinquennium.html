quinquennium

1. NOUN

/ kwɪnˈkwɛniəm / (AmE), / kwɪnˈkwɛniəm / (BrE)
A period of five years.
period of five years

Examples

The museum's growth was remarkable during the last quinquennium.
Each quinquennium brings a new strategic plan for the company.
Statistics are reviewed at the end of every quinquennium to assess progress.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: decade - a period of ten years
  • Degree Lower: biennium - a period of two years

Etymology

From Latin 'quinquennium', from 'quinque' (five) + 'annus' (year).
