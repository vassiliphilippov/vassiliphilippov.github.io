hydropathic

1. ADJ

/ ˌhaɪdrəˈpæθɪk / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪdrəˈpaθɪk / (BrE)
relating to hydropathy or the treatment of disease by the use of water.
relating to hydropathy or water treatment
medical

Examples

The old resort was famous for its hydropathic treatments and mineral baths.
He attended a hydropathic establishment to cure his chronic pain through water therapy.
Many Victorian-era hospitals featured hydropathic wards where patients bathed in prescribed waters.

Synset Examples

hydropathic treatments

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hydrotherapeutic - relating to the therapeutic use of water

Etymology

From hydro- (water) + Greek pathos (suffering, disease).
