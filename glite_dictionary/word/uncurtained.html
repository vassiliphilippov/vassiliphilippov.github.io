uncurtained

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkɜrtnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɜːtənd / (BrE)
not having curtains; without curtains covering a window or opening
without curtains

Examples

The uncurtained windows let sunlight flood the room every morning.
She felt exposed living in a house with uncurtained glass doors facing the street.
Rain tapped softly against the uncurtained panes, filling the room with a grey light.

Synset Examples

blank, curtainless windows stared back at her

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curtainless - without curtains
