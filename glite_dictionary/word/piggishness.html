piggishness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪɡɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
An excessive or greedy desire for food; gluttony.
excessive desire for food

Examples

His piggishness at the buffet embarrassed everyone at the table.
She scolded the children for their piggishness during dinner.
Such piggishness is frowned upon in polite company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gluttony - extreme greed for food or drink

Etymology

From 'piggish' + '-ness', with 'piggish' meaning 'like a pig' due to pigs' reputation for eating greedily.
