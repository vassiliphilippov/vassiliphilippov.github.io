portrayed

1. ADJ

/ pɔrˈtreɪd / (AmE), / pɔːˈtreɪd / (BrE)
represented graphically by a sketch, drawing, or lines; depicted visually.
represented visually by drawing or sketch

Examples

The ancient king is portrayed in the mural with a crown and a scepter.
The villain was portrayed as a shadowy figure in the comic book.
On the cover, the heroine is portrayed with striking detail and vibrant colors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: depicted - shown visually, especially in art or illustration
  • Synonym: illustrated - furnished with pictures or drawings
