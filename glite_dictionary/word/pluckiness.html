pluckiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈplʌkinəs / (AmE), / ˈplʌkinɪs / (BrE)
the quality of showing courage and determination in difficult or dangerous situations
courage and determination

Examples

Her pluckiness inspired everyone during the rescue mission.
Despite the odds, his pluckiness never wavered throughout the competition.
The child's pluckiness was evident as she stood up to the bully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bravery - courageous behavior or character
  • Antonym: cowardice - lack of courage; excessive fear

Etymology

From 'plucky' (brave, spirited), from 'pluck' meaning spirited courage.
