leppy

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛpi / (AmE), / ˈlɛpi / (BrE)
A motherless calf in a range herd of cattle, especially in the western United States.
motherless calf in a herd

Examples

The cowboy spotted a leppy wandering alone near the creek.
After the storm, several leppies were brought back to the ranch for care.
Ranchers often bottle-feed a leppy until it is strong enough to join the herd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orphan calf - a calf that has lost its mother
