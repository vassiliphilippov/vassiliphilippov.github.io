share

1. VERB

/ ʃɛr / (AmE), / ʃeə / (BrE)
to have, give, or receive a portion of something with others
to divide and distribute with others

Examples

We decided to share our lunch with the new student.
She always shares her notes with her classmates.
The group shared the prize money equally among themselves.

Synset Examples

We shared the cake

Related Terms

  • Synonym: divide - to separate into parts or groups
  • Synonym: to distribute - to give out among several people or groups
  • Antonym: keep - to retain possession of something

Etymology

Old English *scearu* (a division, portion).

Source: WordNet
