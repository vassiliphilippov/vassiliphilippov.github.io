photoelectrically

1. ADV

/ ˌfoʊtoʊɪˈlɛktrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊɪˈlɛktrɪkli / (BrE)
By means of the photoelectric effect or relating to the use of photoelectric principles.
by means of the photoelectric effect

Examples

The electrical current was measured photoelectrically in the laboratory.
The device operates photoelectrically to detect changes in light intensity.
Scientists analyzed the surface properties photoelectrically to obtain precise measurements.

Synset Examples

they measured it photoelectrically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photoelectric - relating to the emission of electrons by light

Etymology

From 'photoelectric' + '-ly', where 'photoelectric' is from photo- (relating to light) and electric.
