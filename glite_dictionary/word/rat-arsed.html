rat-arsed

1. ADJ

/ ˈræt ˌɑrst / (AmE), / ˈræt ˌɑːst / (BrE)
very drunk; extremely intoxicated with alcohol
very drunk (slang, chiefly British)
slang british

Examples

After just a few drinks, he already looked rat-arsed and started slurring his words.
She got completely rat-arsed at her birthday and needed a ride home.
You can always tell when he's rat-arsed—he sings loudly and dances on tables.

Synset Examples

Don't go rat-arsed to work; you'll regret it.
He showed up to the meeting completely rat-arsed.
She was rat-arsed after the party last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plastered - very drunk; extremely intoxicated
  • Antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol; not drunk
