midsection

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪdˌsɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌsɛkʃən / (BrE)
the middle part of the human torso, especially the front area between the chest and the waist
middle area of the torso

Examples

She wore a crop top that revealed her midsection at the party.
Exercises like planks help strengthen the midsection of your body.
Doctors recommend checking your midsection for any unusual lumps or pain.

Synset Examples

young American women believe that a bare midriff is fashionable

Related Terms

  • Synonym: midriff - the region of the body between the chest and the waist

Etymology

Formed from 'mid-' (middle) and 'section' (part), first recorded in the 19th century.
