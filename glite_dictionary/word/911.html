9/11

1. NOUN

/ naɪn əˈlɛvən / (AmE), / naɪn əˈlɛvən / (BrE)
the terrorist attacks carried out on September 11, 2001, in the United States, when hijacked airliners were used to destroy the World Trade Center and damage the Pentagon
September 11, 2001, terrorist attacks in the US

Examples

The events of 9/11 changed the world forever.
Many memorial services are held each year on the anniversary of 9/11.
He lost a close friend in the attacks on 9/11.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: September 11 attacks - the terrorist attacks in the US on September 11, 2001

Etymology

Shortened form of 'September 11,' modeled after the US date notation.
