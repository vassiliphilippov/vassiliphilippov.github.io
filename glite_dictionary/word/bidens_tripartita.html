Bidens tripartita

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪ.dɛnz traɪˈpɑːr.tɪ.tə / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ.dɛnz trɪˈpɑː.tɪ.tə / (BrE)
A species of bur marigold native to temperate regions of Eurasia, with characteristic triple-lobed leaves and small yellow flowers.
species of bur marigold plant

Examples

The wetlands were dotted with Bidens tripartita growing along the water's edge.
Botanists identified the sample as Bidens tripartita during their field survey.
Some herbal remedies use extracts from Bidens tripartita for traditional medicine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bur marigold - a common name for plants in the genus Bidens

Etymology

From Latin: 'bidens' (two-toothed) and 'tripartita' (three-parted), describing the plant's form.
