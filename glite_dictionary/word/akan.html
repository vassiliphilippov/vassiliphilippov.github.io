Akan

1. NOUN

/ əˈkɑn / (AmE), / əˈkɑːn / (BrE)
a Kwa language spoken in Ghana and the Ivory Coast
Kwa language of Ghana and Ivory Coast

Examples

Many people in Ghana speak Akan as their first language.
She is studying Akan to communicate better with her relatives in Ivory Coast.
The Akan vocabulary includes words that are not found in English.

Etymology

From the Akan people of Ghana and Ivory Coast, self-designation: Akan.
