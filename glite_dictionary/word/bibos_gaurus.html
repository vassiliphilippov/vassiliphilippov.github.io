Bibos gaurus

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪbɒs ˈɡɔrəs / (AmE), / ˈbʌɪbɒs ˈɡɔːrəs / (BrE)
A large wild ox native to the mountainous regions of eastern India and Southeast Asia, also known as the gaur.
large wild ox of India and Southeast Asia

Examples

The Bibos gaurus is the largest species of wild cattle in the world.
Researchers observed a herd of Bibos gaurus grazing in the forested hills.
The conservation status of Bibos gaurus has become a concern due to habitat loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gaur - large wild ox native to South and Southeast Asia

Etymology

From modern Latin Bibos (genus name) and gaurus (from Greek gauros, a kind of wild ox).
