graded

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪdɪd / (BrE)
arranged or classified in a series according to rank, level, or quality
arranged in grades or ranks

Examples

The students were placed in graded reading groups based on ability.
The company uses a graded scale to determine employee bonuses.
The fabric was available in graded shades, from light to dark.

Synset Examples

stratified areas of the distribution

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ranked - arranged in order according to quality or status
  • Synonym: stratified - arranged in layers or classes

Etymology

From the verb 'grade', which comes from Latin 'gradus' (step, degree).
