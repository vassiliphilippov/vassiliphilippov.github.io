uncompounded

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkəmˈpaʊndɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəmˈpaʊndɪd / (BrE)
Not combined with other elements; not constituting a compound.
not forming a compound

Examples

The medicine is most effective in its uncompounded form.
His explanation was straightforward and uncompounded by unnecessary details.
In its uncompounded state, the mineral is rare and valuable.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: compound - composed of two or more parts or elements

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'compounded' (from Latin *componere*, to put together).
