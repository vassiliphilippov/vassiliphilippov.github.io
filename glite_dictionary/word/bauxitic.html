bauxitic

1. ADJ

/ bɔːkˈsɪtɪk / (AmE), / bɔːkˈsɪtɪk / (BrE)
Containing or resembling bauxite.
containing or resembling bauxite

Examples

The soil in that region is highly bauxitic, making it rich in aluminum minerals.
Geologists identified several bauxitic deposits during their survey of the plateau.
Plants struggle to grow in bauxitic soils due to their high mineral content.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bauxite - an aluminum ore; principal source of aluminum

Etymology

From bauxite + -ic, used to form adjectives.
