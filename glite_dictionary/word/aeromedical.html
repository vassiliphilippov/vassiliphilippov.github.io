aeromedical

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛroʊˈmɛdɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌeərəʊˈmɛdɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the medical aspects of aviation or the health care of people involved in flying.
relating to aviation medicine
medical

Examples

The aeromedical team responded quickly to the in-flight emergency.
He specialized in aeromedical research, focusing on pilot health and safety.
An aeromedical evacuation was arranged for the injured passengers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aviation medicine - the study and practice of medicine as applied to aviation and flight personnel

Etymology

Formed from aero- (relating to air or aviation) and medical.
