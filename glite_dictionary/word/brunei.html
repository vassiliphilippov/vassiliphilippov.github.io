Brunei

1. PROPN

/ bruːˈnaɪ / (AmE), / bruːˈnaɪ / (BrE)
A small sultanate on the northwest coast of Borneo in Southeast Asia; gained independence from Great Britain in 1984.
Southeast Asian sultanate on Borneo

Examples

My friends traveled to Brunei last summer and admired its magnificent mosques.
The Sultan of Brunei is one of the world's richest monarchs.
Oil production is a major contributor to Brunei's economy.

Etymology

From Malay 'Brunei', possibly from Sanskrit 'varunai' ('seafarers').
