snot-nosed

1. ADJ

/ ˈsnɑt ˌnoʊzd / (AmE), / ˈsnɒt ˌnəʊzd / (BrE)
(of a person, especially a child) having a nose dirty with nasal mucus; having a runny nose.
having a nose dirty with mucus

Examples

The daycare was full of snot-nosed toddlers crying and playing.
He wiped his snot-nosed face on his sleeve before speaking.
After playing outside in the cold, the children came home snot-nosed and shivering.

Synset Examples

a snotty nose
a house full of snot-nosed kids

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snotty-nosed - (of a person) having a runny or dirty nose

Etymology

Formed from 'snot' (nasal mucus) and 'nosed' (having a particular kind of nose).

2. ADJ

/ ˈsnɑtˌnoʊzd / (AmE), / ˈsnɒtˌnəʊzd / (BrE)
(informal, often derogatory) Conceited, arrogant, or presumptuous, especially when referring to a young person.
arrogant or conceited (esp. young person)
informal

Examples

The manager dismissed him as just another snot-nosed kid fresh out of college.
She couldn't stand the snot-nosed attitude of her little brother's friends.
He acted like a snot-nosed brat, refusing to listen to anyone else's opinion.

Synset Examples

a snotty little scion of a degenerate family
they're snobs — stuck-up and uppity and persnickety

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snotty - arrogant or conceited
  • Synonym: uppity - self-important or arrogant, especially in an upwardly presumptuous way
  • Antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low estimate of one's importance

Etymology

From 'snot' (nasal mucus) + 'nosed', originally implying a young child with a runny nose and then figuratively referring to impudent youth.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01896449-s
Synset POS: s

3. ADJ

/ ˈsnɑt ˌnoʊzd / (AmE), / ˈsnɒt ˌnəʊzd / (BrE)
(informal, usually disapproving) Describing someone, especially a child, as rude, impudent, or troublesome.
rude or troublesome (often for children)
informal

Examples

My neighbor's snot-nosed kids are always making a racket outside.
She refused to listen to the snot-nosed teenager's complaints.
The teacher is tired of dealing with these snot-nosed students who never follow the rules.

Synset Examples

That snot-nosed kid always bothers the other children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impudent - showing no respect, rude
  • Antonym: polite - showing good manners and respect
