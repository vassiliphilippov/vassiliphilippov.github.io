eighty-fifth

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪti ˈfɪfθ / (AmE), / ˈeɪti ˈfɪfθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number corresponding to eighty-five in a sequence; 85th.
ordinal number: 85th

Examples

This was her eighty-fifth birthday, and the family held a huge celebration.
Our school finished eighty-fifth out of one hundred in the national rankings.
He is the eighty-fifth person to complete the marathon this year.
