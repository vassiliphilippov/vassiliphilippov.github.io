Mauser

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊzər / (AmE), / ˈmaʊzə / (BrE)
A trademark for a range of repeating rifles and pistols manufactured by the German arms company Mauser.
trademarked rifle or pistol brand

Examples

He inherited a vintage Mauser rifle from his grandfather, who fought in the war.
Collectors value the distinctive design and reliability of the classic Mauser pistol.
The soldier carefully cleaned his Mauser after a day on the training range.

Etymology

From the name of the German arms manufacturer Mauser, founded by Wilhelm and Paul Mauser.
