denims

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛnɪmz / (AmE), / ˈdɛnɪmz / (BrE)
Trousers made from heavy denim fabric, typically worn for manual work or casual occasions.
trousers made of denim fabric

Examples

He wore his favorite denims and a plain white T-shirt to the party.
After a long day at work, she changed into comfortable denims for the evening.
These denims are durable enough for outdoor chores and stylish enough for a casual outing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jeans - casual trousers made of denim

Etymology

Plural of 'denim', from French *serge de Nîmes* (serge from Nîmes, France).
