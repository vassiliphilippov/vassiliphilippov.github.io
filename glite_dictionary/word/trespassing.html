trespassing

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrɛsˌpæsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrɛspəsɪŋ / (BrE)
Gradually intruding or encroaching without right or permission.
intruding or encroaching without right

Examples

The trespassing weeds slowly overtook the neglected garden.
Authorities are trying to stop trespassing campers from entering the protected wildlife area.
She was annoyed by the trespassing branches that reached over the fence into her yard.

Synset Examples

we moved back from the encroaching tide
invasive tourists
trespassing hunters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: encroaching - Gradually intruding or advancing beyond proper limits
  • Synonym: invasive - Tending to intrude or spread undesirably
