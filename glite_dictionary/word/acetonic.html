acetonic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæsɪˈtoʊnɪk / (AmE), / ˌasɪˈtəʊnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing acetone.
relating to acetone
medical

Examples

The chemical reaction was performed in an acetonic solution.
Urine samples showing high acetonic content may indicate metabolic disorders.
He used an acetonic mixture as a solvent in his laboratory experiments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acetone - a colorless, volatile, flammable liquid used as a solvent

Etymology

From acetone + -ic, meaning 'relating to acetone.'
