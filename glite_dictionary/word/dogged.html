dogged

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɔɡɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡɪd / (BrE)
showing persistent determination; not giving up easily
persistently determined

Examples

She showed dogged determination in finishing her research despite many setbacks.
His dogged efforts eventually led to a breakthrough in the project.
The detective's dogged pursuit of the truth impressed everyone on the force.

Synset Examples

dogged persistence
dour determination
the most vocal and pertinacious of all the critics
a mind not gifted to discover truth but tenacious to hold it
men tenacious of opinion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tenacious - holding fast; very determined
  • Synonym: stubborn - unwilling to change one's mind or attitude
  • Antonym: yielding - giving way; not stubborn or unyielding

Etymology

From the past participle of 'dog', relating to the tenacious, relentless qualities associated with dogs.
