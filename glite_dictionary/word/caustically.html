caustically

1. ADV

/ ˈkɔstɪkli / (AmE), / ˈkɔːstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is severely critical, biting, or sarcastic.
in a biting, sarcastic manner

Examples

He answered her caustically during the heated debate.
She remarked caustically about the state of the kitchen.
The critic caustically dismissed the new play as uninspired.

Synset Examples

he addressed her caustically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sarcastically - in a sarcastic manner
  • Degree Lower: mildly - in a gentle or moderate manner

Etymology

From caustic + -ally, ultimately from Greek kaustikos (capable of burning).
