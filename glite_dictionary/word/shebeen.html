shebeen

1. NOUN

/ ʃəˈbin / (AmE), / ʃəˈbiːn / (BrE)
An unlicensed establishment or house where alcoholic drinks are illegally sold and consumed.
unlicensed drinking establishment

Examples

The police raided the shebeen late at night, arresting several patrons.
Stories about the shebeen were passed down through generations in the village.
Many musicians got their start performing in a lively shebeen before moving on to bigger venues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speakeasy - an illicit liquor store or nightclub

Etymology

From Irish 'síbín' meaning 'small mug' or 'illicit whiskey'.
