pleadingly

1. ADV

/ ˈpliːdɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈpliːdɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows you are asking for something in an emotional or urgent way; beseechingly.
in a beseeching or imploring manner

Examples

She looked up at him pleadingly, hoping he would change his mind.
He asked pleadingly for another chance to explain himself.
The child’s eyes met hers pleadingly, silently begging for forgiveness.

Synset Examples

‘You must help me,’ she said imploringly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imploringly - in a manner showing you are begging or earnestly appealing
  • Antonym: demandingly - in an insistent or pressing manner
