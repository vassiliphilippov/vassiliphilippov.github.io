Kirgizstan

1. PROPN

/ kɪrˈɡɪzˌstæn / (AmE), / kɪəˈɡɪzˌstan / (BrE)
a country in Central Asia, bordered by Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, and China; also known as Kyrgyzstan
country in Central Asia, also called Kyrgyzstan

Examples

After its independence in 1991, Kirgizstan implemented political and economic reforms.
She traveled through Central Asia, visiting countries such as Kirgizstan and Uzbekistan.
The mountain ranges of Kirgizstan attract trekkers from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kyrgyzstan - official modern name of the same country

Etymology

Variant spelling influenced by Russian Киргизстан, formed from Kirgiz (Kyrgyz) + -stan (land).
