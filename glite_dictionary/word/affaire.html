affaire

1. NOUN

/ əˈfɛr / (AmE), / əˈfeə / (BrE)
a romantic or sexual relationship, especially a secret or illicit one
a secret romantic or sexual relationship

Examples

Their passionate affaire was the subject of much gossip in the small town.
She ended the affaire before it could destroy her marriage.
The novel revolves around a scandalous affaire between the protagonist and his neighbor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affair - a usually secret sexual or romantic relationship

Etymology

From French 'affaire' (matter, affair), related to Latin 'facere' (to do, make).
