transvestic

1. ADJ

/ trænzˈvɛstɪk / (AmE), / tranzˈvɛstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by obtaining sexual arousal or gratification from wearing clothing typically associated with the opposite sex.
relating to sexual arousal from cross-dressing
medical

Examples

The psychologist diagnosed him with transvestic disorder after a thorough evaluation.
She researched the distinction between transvestic behavior and gender identity issues.
The clinical manual describes transvestic fetishism as a specific paraphilia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transvestite - a person who dresses in clothes typically worn by the opposite sex

Etymology

From Latin 'trans' (across) + 'vestire' (to clothe), via 'transvestite'.
