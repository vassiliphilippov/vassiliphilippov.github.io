tooth-drawer

1. NOUN

/ tuːθ ˈdrɔɚ / (AmE), / tuːθ ˈdrɔːə / (BrE)
a tool used for extracting teeth, especially in historical or dental contexts
tool for extracting teeth

Examples

In the 18th century, a tooth-drawer was often part of a barber's equipment.
The antique tooth-drawer on display gave insight into early dental practices.
He examined the tooth-drawer before using it on the patient.

Synset Examples

The dentist used a tooth-drawer to remove the decayed molar.
