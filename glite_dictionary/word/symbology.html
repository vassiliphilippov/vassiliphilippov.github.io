symbology

1. NOUN

/ sɪmˈbɑlədʒi / (AmE), / sɪmˈbɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study or use of symbols and symbolism
study or use of symbols

Examples

She decided to specialize in symbology during her graduate studies.
The novel explores religious symbology throughout its plot.
Understanding ancient symbology helps archaeologists interpret artifacts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: symbolism - the use of symbols to represent ideas or qualities

Etymology

From Greek 'symbolon' (symbol) + '-logy' (study of).
