loungewear

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaʊndʒˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈlaʊndʒwɛə / (BrE)
comfortable, casual clothing designed to be worn while relaxing at home
casual clothes for relaxing at home

Examples

She changed into loungewear as soon as she got home from work.
During the pandemic, many people preferred wearing loungewear instead of formal clothes.
His favorite loungewear is a pair of old sweatpants and a soft T-shirt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleepwear - clothing designed for sleeping
  • Antonym: activewear - clothing designed for exercise or sports

Etymology

Formed from 'lounge' (to relax) + 'wear' (clothing).
