haschisch

1. NOUN

/ hæˈʃiʃ / (AmE), / hæˈʃiːʃ / (BrE)
a purified resinous extract of the hemp plant, used as a hallucinogenic drug
resinous cannabis extract used as a drug

Examples

The police seized a kilogram of haschisch during the raid.
She said she had never tried haschisch before her trip to Morocco.
The book describes the cultural significance of haschisch in 19th-century Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hashish - resinous cannabis extract used as a drug

Etymology

From Arabic ḥashīsh (dry herb, hemp), with this spelling influenced by French and German.
