Gambian

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡæmbiən / (AmE), / ˈɡæmbɪən / (BrE)
relating to Gambia or its people
of or relating to Gambia

Examples

The Gambian economy has seen significant growth in recent years.
She specializes in Gambian cuisine and often prepares traditional dishes.
Many tourists visit the Gambian coast for its beautiful beaches.

Synset Examples

Gambian game parks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gambia - a country in West Africa

Etymology

From Gambia (country name) + -an (adjectival suffix).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03078581-a
Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ ˈɡæmbiən / (AmE), / ˈɡæmbiən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Gambia, or a person of Gambian descent
person from Gambia

Examples

The restaurant is owned by a Gambian who moved here ten years ago.
Several Gambians participated in the international festival last summer.
She is proud of her Gambian heritage and culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Senegalese - person from Senegal
  • Synonym: Gambian - adjective relating to Gambia or its people

Etymology

From Gambia + -an, denoting belonging or origin.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09766826-n
Synset POS: n
