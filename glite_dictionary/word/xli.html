xli

1. ADJ

/ ɛks ɛl aɪ / (AmE), / ɛks ɛl aɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for forty-one (41)
Roman numeral for 41

Examples

The Super Bowl was designated as XLI in Roman numerals.
Chapter XLI of the book discusses ancient empires.
He marked the year as XLI on the commemorative plaque.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: xlii - Roman numeral for forty-two (42)
  • Degree Lower: xl - Roman numeral for forty (40)

Etymology

From Latin numerals: X (10), L (50), I (1); XL means 40 (50-10), and adding I makes 41.
