sword

1. NOUN

/ sɔrd / (AmE), / sɔːd / (BrE)
A weapon with a long metal blade and a hilt with a hand guard, used for cutting or thrusting.
long-bladed weapon for cutting or thrusting

Examples

The knight drew his sword and prepared for battle.
He displayed an antique sword above the fireplace in his study.
During the ceremony, each recruit received a ceremonial sword as a symbol of honor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blade - the cutting part of a weapon or tool

Etymology

From Old English sweord, related to Old High German swert and Old Norse sverð.
