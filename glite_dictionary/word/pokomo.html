Pokomo

1. NOUN

/ poʊˈkoʊmoʊ / (AmE), / pəˈkəʊməʊ / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken by the Pokomo people in the coastal areas of Kenya
Bantu language of coastal Kenya

Examples

The Pokomo language is mainly spoken along the Tana River in Kenya.
Researchers have documented traditional stories in Pokomo for linguistic studies.
She learned to greet the elders in Pokomo during her visit to the region.
