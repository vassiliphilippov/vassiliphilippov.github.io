frigidly

1. ADV

/ ˈfrɪdʒɪdli / (AmE), / ˈfrɪdʒɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that lacks warmth or enthusiasm; coldly and unemotionally.
in a cold or unemotional manner

Examples

She responded frigidly to his attempt at conversation, barely making eye contact.
He frigidly dismissed their concerns, showing no hint of empathy.
The manager frigidly informed us that there would be no holiday bonuses this year.

Synset Examples

‘Come in if you have to,’ he said frostily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coldly - in a manner lacking warmth or emotion
  • Antonym: warmly - in a manner showing warmth, friendliness, or enthusiasm

Etymology

Formed from 'frigid' (from Latin 'frigidus', meaning cold) plus '-ly'.
