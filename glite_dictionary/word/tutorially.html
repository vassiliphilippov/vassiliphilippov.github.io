tutorially

1. ADV

/ tuːˈtɔri.ə.li / (AmE), / tjuːˈtɔː.ri.ə.li / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or characteristic of tutorials, especially in teaching or instruction.
in a tutorial manner

Examples

The university's new program ensures students are instructed tutorially in small groups.
She prefers being taught tutorially rather than in large lectures.
Skills are often best developed when practiced tutorially under expert supervision.

Synset Examples

undergraduates are better taught tutorially

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tutorial - a period of instruction given by a tutor

Etymology

Formed from 'tutorial' + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
