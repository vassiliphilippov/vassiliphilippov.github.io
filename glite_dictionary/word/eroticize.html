to eroticize

1. VERB

/ ɪˈrɑː.tɪ.saɪz / (AmE), / ɪˈrɒt.ɪ.saɪz / (BrE)
to give an erotic character or aspect to something; to make something more sexually suggestive or appealing
give something an erotic character

Examples

The magazine chose to eroticize the images in their latest fashion spread.
Critics argue that the film tends to eroticize violence, which is troubling for some viewers.
She was uncomfortable with advertisements that eroticize everyday products to attract attention.

Synset Examples

eroticize the ads

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sexualize - to make something sexual in character or quality

Etymology

From 'erotic' + '-ize', ultimately from Greek 'erōtikos' (sexual love).
