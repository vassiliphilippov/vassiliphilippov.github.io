manufactured

1. ADJ

/ ˌmæn.jəˈfæk.tʃɚd / (AmE), / ˌmæn.jʊˈfæk.tʃəd / (BrE)
Produced in large quantities by industrial processes rather than made by hand or grown naturally.
produced by industrial process

Examples

Most of the furniture in this office is manufactured rather than handcrafted.
The company switched to manufactured ingredients to increase production efficiency.
He prefers to eat natural foods instead of manufactured snacks.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: natural - existing in or caused by nature; not made or caused by humankind
  • Synonym: artificial - made by humans, especially as a copy of something natural

Etymology

Past participle of 'manufacture', from Latin 'manu factus' (made by hand).
