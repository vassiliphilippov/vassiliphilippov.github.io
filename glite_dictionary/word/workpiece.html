workpiece

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝkˌpis / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌpiːs / (BrE)
a piece of material, such as metal or wood, that is being machined, shaped, or processed in manufacturing
item being processed in manufacturing

Examples

The lathe operator secured the workpiece before starting the machine.
During milling, it is crucial to cool the workpiece to prevent overheating.
Careful measurement ensures the workpiece meets the required specifications.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: raw material - unprocessed material used to make a product
  • Degree Higher: finished product - an item after all processing is complete
