alular

1. ADJ

/ ˈæljələr / (AmE), / ˈaljʊlə / (BrE)
relating to the alula, a small structure on the wing of a bird or insect.
relating to the alula (wing structure)

Examples

The alular feathers help birds control airflow during slow flight.
This species is distinguished by a prominent alular area on its wings.
Examination revealed an injury to the alular region of the bird.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alula - a small projection on the anterior edge of a bird's wing

Etymology

From Latin 'alula' (diminutive of 'ala', meaning 'wing') + '-ar'.
