Dubya

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʌbiə / (AmE), / ˈdʌbjə / (BrE)
a nickname for George W. Bush, the 43rd President of the United States
nickname for George W. Bush

Examples

Many comedians referred to President Bush simply as Dubya during his time in office.
The election of Dubya in 2000 sparked significant political debate.
Some voters preferred Dubya's straightforward speaking style over his opponent's.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: George W. Bush - 43rd President of the United States, son of George H. W. Bush

Etymology

Respelling of 'W.', representing the southern U.S. pronunciation of the letter W, for George W. Bush.
