to hang glide

1. VERB

/ hæŋ ɡlaɪd / (AmE), / hæŋ ɡlaɪd / (BrE)
to fly through the air by means of a lightweight, non-motorized glider suspended from the body
fly using a hang glider

Examples

She has always wanted to hang glide over the beautiful mountains.
They spent the weekend learning how to hang glide safely.
After taking lessons, he finally felt ready to hang glide on his own.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paraglide - to glide using a parachute-like wing
  • Synonym: to soar - to fly or rise high in the air
