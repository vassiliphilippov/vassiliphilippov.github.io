Ernesto Guevara

1. PROPN

/ ɛrˈnɛstoʊ ɡɛˈvɑrə / (AmE), / ɛˈnɛstəʊ ɡɛˈvɑːrə / (BrE)
(1928–1967) An Argentine Marxist revolutionary and major figure in the Cuban Revolution, known as Che Guevara; played key roles in Latin American revolutionary movements.
Argentine revolutionary and Cuban Revolution leader

Examples

Many students idolized Ernesto Guevara for his role in fighting imperialism in Latin America.
The book contains a detailed biography of Ernesto Guevara and his influence on global revolutionary movements.
A mural of Ernesto Guevara can be seen in many cities around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Che Guevara - Common name of Ernesto Guevara, revolutionary leader
  • Synonym: Fidel Castro - Cuban revolutionary leader and key associate of Ernesto Guevara
