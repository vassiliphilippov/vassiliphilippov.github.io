Gulo luscus

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːloʊ ˈlʌskəs / (AmE), / ˈɡjuːləʊ ˈlʌskəs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the wolverine, a stocky, shaggy-coated North American carnivorous mammal.
scientific name for the wolverine

Examples

Scientists studied the habitat of Gulo luscus in the remote forests of Alaska.
The Gulo luscus is renowned for its strength and tenacity despite its size.
Conservation efforts are underway to protect Gulo luscus from habitat loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wolverine - A large, fierce North American carnivorous mammal (Gulo gulo)

Etymology

From Latin, Gulo meaning 'glutton' and luscus meaning 'one-eyed' or 'blind in one eye.'
