boyfriend

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔɪˌfrɛnd / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪˌfrɛnd / (BrE)
a man with whom someone has a romantic or sexual relationship, typically not married to them
a male romantic partner

Examples

Her boyfriend surprised her with a bouquet of flowers on their anniversary.
After the movie, he introduced his boyfriend to his family for the first time.
She called her boyfriend to tell him about the great news.

Synset Examples

if I'd known he was her boyfriend, I wouldn't have asked
When the law changed, Pet could finally marry his long-time boyfriend Jim

Related Terms

  • Antonym: girlfriend - a woman with whom someone has a romantic or sexual relationship
  • Synonym: partner - a person with whom one has a romantic or sexual relationship

Etymology

From 'boy' + 'friend'; originally American English, first recorded in the late 19th century.
