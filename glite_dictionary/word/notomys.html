Notomys

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊtəˌmɪs / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtəˌmɪs / (BrE)
A genus of small Australian rodents known as hopping mice, characterized by their long hind legs and ability to hop.
genus of Australian hopping mice

Examples

The Notomys species survive in arid regions by obtaining moisture from their food.
Researchers discovered a new species within the Notomys genus in Western Australia.
Unlike common mice, Notomys have adapted to hop on their elongated hind legs.

Etymology

From Greek notos (south) + mys (mouse), referring to its southern, Australian origin.
