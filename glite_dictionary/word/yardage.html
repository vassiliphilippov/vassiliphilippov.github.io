yardage

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɑrdɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈjɑːdɪdʒ / (BrE)
the total distance measured in yards, especially in sports such as golf or football
distance measured in yards

Examples

The yardage for each hole is listed on the golf scorecard.
With only a few yards to go, the running back increased his yardage total for the game.
We need to recalculate the yardage before ordering new carpeting for the room.

Synset Examples

what is the yardage of this golf course?

Etymology

From 'yard' (unit of length) with the suffix '-age' indicating a collection or measure.
