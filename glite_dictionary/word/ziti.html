ziti

1. NOUN

/ ˈziː.ti / (AmE), / ˈziː.ti / (BrE)
A type of medium-sized, short, tube-shaped pasta, typically used in baked Italian dishes.
medium, tube-shaped pasta

Examples

We made baked ziti for dinner last night and it was delicious.
The restaurant offers ziti with marinara or Alfredo sauce.
She prefers ziti over spaghetti because it holds more sauce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: penne - short, diagonal-cut tube pasta
  • Synonym: rigatoni - large, ridged tube pasta

Etymology

From Italian 'ziti', plural of 'zita' (bride), originally a term for pasta served at weddings.
