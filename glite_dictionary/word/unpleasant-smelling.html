unpleasant-smelling

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈplɛzəntˌsmɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈplɛzəntˌsmɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
having a bad or offensive smell; malodorous
having a bad smell

Examples

The refrigerator needs to be cleaned because something inside is unpleasant-smelling.
We had to dispose of the unpleasant-smelling garbage immediately.
After the rain, the drains became unpleasant-smelling, making it hard to walk nearby.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malodorous - having a very unpleasant smell
  • Antonym: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell
