wherever

1. ADV

/ wɛrˈɛvɚ / (AmE), / wɛərˈɛvə / (BrE)
at or in any place, no matter where; in every or any location that
at any place; no matter where

Examples

You can sit wherever you like in the restaurant.
She takes her camera wherever she goes.
We will support you wherever you decide to live.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: everywhere - in all places
  • Degree Lower: somewhere - in or at some place

Etymology

From 'where' + 'ever', used since Middle English.
