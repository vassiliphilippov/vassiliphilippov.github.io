Sporanox

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔːrəˌnɑks / (AmE), / ˈspɒrəˌnɒks / (BrE)
A trademarked oral antifungal medication used to treat fungal infections, especially of the nails.
trademarked oral antifungal drug
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Sporanox to treat my persistent toenail infection.
Some patients experience side effects when taking Sporanox for fungal nail disease.
Sporanox is often chosen when topical treatments fail to cure a fungal infection.
