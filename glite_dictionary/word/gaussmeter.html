gaussmeter

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡaʊsˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈɡaʊsˌmiːtə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure the strength of magnetic fields
instrument for measuring magnetic fields

Examples

The technician used a gaussmeter to check the magnetic field of the coil.
Accurate readings from the gaussmeter are essential for calibrating laboratory equipment.
She demonstrated how to operate the gaussmeter during the physics experiment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magnetometer - an instrument for measuring magnetic forces

Etymology

From Gauss, after Carl Friedrich Gauss (German mathematician and physicist), and meter (measuring device).
