MANPAD

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈmænˌpæd / (BrE)
a portable shoulder-launched surface-to-air missile system designed to be operated by a single person
portable surface-to-air missile

Examples

The soldiers were equipped with MANPADs to protect the camp from enemy aircraft.
A single MANPAD poses a serious threat to low-flying helicopters and planes.
Security forces discovered several MANPADs during the raid on the warehouse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surface-to-air missile - a missile designed to be launched from the ground to destroy aircraft or other missiles

Etymology

Acronym from 'man-portable air-defense system'.
