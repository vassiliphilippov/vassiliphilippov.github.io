pulpwood

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌlp.wʊd / (AmE), / ˈpʌlp.wʊd / (BrE)
Wood from softwood trees used primarily in making paper pulp.
wood used to make paper pulp

Examples

Many pine forests are harvested for their pulpwood to supply paper mills.
The company specializes in exporting pulpwood to international markets.
Sustainable management of pulpwood resources is vital for the environment.
