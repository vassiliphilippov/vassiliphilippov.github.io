connection

1. NOUN

/ kəˈnɛkʃən / (AmE), / kəˈnɛkʃən / (BrE)
a means of transferring from one form of transportation to another during a journey, such as between flights or trains
transfer between forms of transport

Examples

If we hurry, we'll catch our connection to Paris in time.
She missed her connection due to the train delay.
Travelers often worry about having enough time between connections at busy airports.

Synset Examples

the plane was late and he missed his connection in Atlanta

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transfer - moving from one vehicle or mode of transport to another

Etymology

From Latin 'connectere' (to bind together).

/ kəˈnɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / kəˈnɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
