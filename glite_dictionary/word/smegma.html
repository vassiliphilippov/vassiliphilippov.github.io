smegma

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmɛɡmə / (AmE), / ˈsmɛɡmə / (BrE)
a white, oily secretion produced by the sebaceous glands, especially under the foreskin or around the clitoris
white secretion from genital glands
medical

Examples

Good hygiene helps prevent the buildup of smegma under the foreskin.
Doctors explained that smegma is a natural bodily secretion, but should be regularly cleaned away.
She read a medical article describing the formation of smegma in both males and females.

Etymology

From Greek smēgma (soap, ointment).
