sensorial

1. ADJ

/ sɛnˈsɔːriəl / (AmE), / sɛnˈsɔːrɪəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving the senses or sensation.
relating to the senses

Examples

The spa offered a sensorial journey designed to relax both body and mind.
Children develop language skills through rich sensorial experiences in their environment.
The artist aimed to create an immersive, sensorial installation using sound and light.

Synset Examples

sensory experience
sensory channels

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensory - relating to sensation or the senses

Etymology

From Late Latin sensorius (of the senses), from Latin sensus (sense).
