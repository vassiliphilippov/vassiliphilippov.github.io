standardiser

1. NOUN

/ ˈstændəɹˌaɪzɚ / (AmE), / ˈstændədaɪzə / (BrE)
a person or organization that establishes standards with which others must comply
person or body that sets standards
british

Examples

As a standardiser, she ensures all products meet international requirements.
The government appointed a standardiser to oversee the safety certifications.
He worked as a standardiser for a leading quality assurance agency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: standardizer - person who establishes standards; American spelling

Etymology

From 'standard' + '-iser', following British English spelling conventions.
