restaurant

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛst(ə)ˌrɑnt / (AmE), / ˈrɛst(ə)rɒnt / (BrE)
A place where meals are prepared and served to customers who pay to eat there.
place to buy and eat meals

Examples

We decided to have dinner at an Italian restaurant near the river.
After the show, the group met at a fancy restaurant downtown.
The new restaurant on Main Street is getting excellent reviews.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: café - a small and informal restaurant, especially one serving light refreshments
  • Synonym: diner - a small, inexpensive restaurant, especially in the US

Etymology

From French 'restaurant' (restorative), from 'restaurer' (to restore).
