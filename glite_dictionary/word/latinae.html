Latinae

1. NOUN

/ ləˈtiːniː / (AmE), / laˈtiːniː / (BrE)
A subfamily of fish in the family Centropomidae, which includes certain tropical marine and freshwater species.
fish subfamily in Centropomidae

Examples

The genus Lates belongs to the subfamily Latinae within the family Centropomidae.
Biologists study the evolutionary traits of Latinae to understand fish diversification.
Several commercially important fish species are classified under the Latinae subfamily.

Etymology

From Modern Latin, used in zoological nomenclature for subfamilies, typically derived from the name of a type genus.
