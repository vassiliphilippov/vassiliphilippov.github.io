tea-scented

1. ADJ

/ ti ˈsɛntɪd / (AmE), / tiː ˈsɛntɪd / (BrE)
having the scent or aroma of tea.
having the smell of tea

Examples

The soap was delicately tea-scented, filling the bathroom with a gentle fragrance.
She bought a tea-scented candle to create a calming atmosphere in her living room.
His bag carried a tea-scented sachet that reminded him of quiet afternoons.
