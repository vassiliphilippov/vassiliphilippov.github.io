seamanlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈsiː.mənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsiː.mənˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Characteristic of or appropriate to a seaman; showing skill and competence in seamanship.
showing skill or qualities of a seaman

Examples

He tied the knots in a seamanlike manner, impressing the seasoned sailors.
Her seamanlike conduct during the storm kept the crew calm and safe.
The captain praised their seamanlike response to the emergency.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unseamanlike - not characteristic of or befitting a seaman

Etymology

Formed from 'seaman' + '-like', denoting resemblance or suitability.
