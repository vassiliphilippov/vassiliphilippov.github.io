Balaena mysticetus

1. NOUN

/ bəˈliːnə mɪsˈtɪsətəs / (AmE), / bəˈliːnə mɪsˈtɪsɪtəs / (BrE)
the scientific name for the bowhead whale, a large baleen whale found in Arctic and sub-Arctic waters
Arctic baleen whale species (bowhead whale)

Examples

The Balaena mysticetus is specially adapted to survive icy Arctic waters.
Scientists conducted a population survey of Balaena mysticetus off the coast of Greenland.
Balaena mysticetus can live for over 200 years, making it one of the longest-lived mammals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowhead whale - large Arctic baleen whale; common name for Balaena mysticetus

Etymology

From Latin: 'Balaena' (whale) and 'mysticetus' (mustache, referring to baleen).
