lapdog

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæp.dɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈlæp.dɒɡ / (BrE)
a small dog kept as a pet, especially one that is small enough to be held on a person's lap
small pet dog often held on the lap

Examples

She pampered her lapdog with treats and expensive accessories.
The little lapdog curled up contentedly on the old man's knees.
Unlike larger breeds, a lapdog requires less space and exercise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toy dog - a small breed of dog bred as a pet
