custom-made

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌstəm meɪd / (AmE), / ˌkʌstəm ˈmeɪd / (BrE)
an item that has been made according to a customer's individual requirements
item made to customer's specifications

Examples

He ordered a custom-made suit for his wedding.
The jeweler crafted a custom-made ring based on her design.
We specialize in custom-made furniture for unique spaces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bespoke - made to order for a particular customer
  • Antonym: off-the-shelf - ready-made and available for sale immediately
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03157149-n
Synset POS: n

2. ADJ

/ ˈkʌstəm meɪd / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəm meɪd / (BrE)
(of an object) made according to the specific requirements or preferences of an individual.
made to individual specifications

Examples

She ordered a custom-made suit for her wedding.
The company offers custom-made furniture tailored to your home's measurements.
His bicycle was custom-made to meet his unique specifications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bespoke - made to order for a particular customer
  • Antonym: ready-made - made in standard sizes and not to individual order
