pfennig

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛnɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈfɛnɪɡ / (BrE)
a former German coin and monetary unit, equal to one hundredth of a Deutsche Mark
former German coin/unit (1/100 Deutsche Mark)

Examples

He kept a pfennig from his trip to Germany as a souvenir.
Before the euro, every mark was divided into 100 pfennigs.
Collectors often search for rare pfennig coins from the early twentieth century.

Etymology

From German Pfennig, ultimately from Old High German pfenning.
