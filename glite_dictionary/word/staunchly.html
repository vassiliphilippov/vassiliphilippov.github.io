staunchly

1. ADV

/ ˈstɑntʃli / (AmE), / ˈstɔːntʃli / (BrE)
in a firm, loyal, and determined manner; showing strong support for something or someone
in a firm and loyal manner

Examples

She staunchly opposed the proposal, refusing to compromise on her beliefs.
Despite criticism, he staunchly defended his friend throughout the ordeal.
They remained staunchly loyal to their leader even in difficult times.

Synset Examples

he staunchly defended his principles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steadfastly - in a resolutely firm and unwavering way
  • Antonym: weakly - in a weak or unsteady manner

Etymology

From staunch + -ly; 'staunch' from Old French estanche (watertight, steadfast).
