Uzbekistan

1. PROPN

/ uzˌbɛk.ɪˈstɑn / (AmE), / ʊzˌbɛk.ɪˈstɑːn / (BrE)
A landlocked country in west-central Asia, formerly part of the Soviet Union.
country in west-central Asia

Examples

She traveled to Uzbekistan to explore its rich Silk Road history.
The capital city of Uzbekistan is Tashkent.
Exports from Uzbekistan include cotton, gold, and natural gas.

Etymology

From Russian 'Uzbekistan', from Uzbek 'Oʻzbekiston', meaning 'Land of the Uzbeks'.
