snogging

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnɔɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsnɒɡɪŋ / (BrE)
(British informal) the act of kissing and cuddling amorously.
acting of amorous kissing and cuddling
informal british

Examples

They were caught snogging behind the bike sheds after school.
The movie features a lot of teenagers snogging at parties.
She laughed when her friend told her about snogging with her new boyfriend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: making out - engaging in amorous kissing or caressing

Etymology

From British slang 'snog' (to kiss passionately), origin unknown, emerged in mid-20th century UK slang.
