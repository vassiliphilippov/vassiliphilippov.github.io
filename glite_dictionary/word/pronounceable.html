pronounceable

1. ADJ

/ prəˈnaʊnsəbl̩ / (AmE), / prəˈnaʊnsəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be spoken or uttered clearly; capable of being pronounced.
able to be pronounced

Examples

The foreign name was surprisingly pronounceable for English speakers.
Are these words pronounceable by a child learning to read?
The new drug has a much more pronounceable name than its predecessor.

Synset Examples

a pronounceable group of letters

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unpronounceable - not able to be pronounced

Etymology

Formed from 'pronounce' + '-able', from Latin 'pronuntiare' (to proclaim, pronounce).
