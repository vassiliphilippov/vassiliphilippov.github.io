Oahu

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈɑːhuː / (AmE), / əʊˈɑːhuː / (BrE)
an island of central Hawaii, situated between Molokai and Kauai, and the most populous island of the state
island of Hawaii; the state’s chief island

Examples

We spent our honeymoon on Oahu and explored its beautiful beaches.
Honolulu, the capital of Hawaii, is located on Oahu.
Many tourists visit Oahu for its surfing and hiking opportunities.

Etymology

From Hawaiian Oʻahu, the native name for the island.
