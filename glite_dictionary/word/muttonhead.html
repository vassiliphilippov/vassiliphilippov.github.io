muttonhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌtnˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈmʌtnhɛd / (BrE)
a stupid or foolish person (used as an insult)
stupid or foolish person
informal

Examples

Don't be such a muttonhead—read the instructions before you start!
He was called a muttonhead for forgetting his own birthday.
Sometimes I feel like a real muttonhead when I make silly mistakes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blockhead - a stupid person
  • Synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely
  • Antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intellectual ability

Etymology

Formed from 'mutton' (sheep meat) + 'head', suggesting a head as empty as a sheep's.
