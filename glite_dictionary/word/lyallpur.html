Lyallpur

1. PROPN

/ laɪˈɑlpʊr / (AmE), / laɪˈælpʊə / (BrE)
the former name for Faisalabad, a major city in northeast Pakistan
former name of Faisalabad (city, Pakistan)

Examples

My grandfather was born in Lyallpur before it was renamed Faisalabad.
Many historical documents still refer to the city as Lyallpur.
The textile industry in Lyallpur played a crucial role in Pakistan's economy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Faisalabad - current name of the city previously known as Lyallpur

Etymology

Named after Sir James Lyall, a British colonial administrator.
