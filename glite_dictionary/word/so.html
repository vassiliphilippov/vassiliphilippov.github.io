/ soʊ / (AmE), / səʊ / (BrE)

4. NOUN

/ soʊ / (AmE), / səʊ / (BrE)
The fifth note of a musical scale in solmization, also called 'sol' or 'solfah.'
fifth note in solmization scale

Examples

In the scale, so comes after fa and before la.
She sang the notes do, re, mi, fa, so, la, ti, do effortlessly.
During the choir practice, everyone had to hold the note so for four beats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sol - The fifth note in solfège or solmization, also called 'so'.

Etymology

From Italian 'sol,' used in the Guidonian hand for solmization.
