Whitehorse

1. PROPN

/ ˈwaɪthɔrs / (AmE), / ˈwaɪthɔːs / (BrE)
The capital city of Yukon Territory in northwestern Canada, situated on the Yukon River.
capital city of Yukon, Canada

Examples

I visited Whitehorse last summer to explore the Yukon River and its history.
Whitehorse experiences long daylight hours during the summer months in northern Canada.
The annual Yukon Quest dog sled race starts in Whitehorse every February.

Etymology

Named after nearby White Horse Rapids, which resembled the mane of a white horse.
