aglow

1. ADJ

/ əˈɡloʊ / (AmE), / əˈɡləʊ / (BrE)
shining with a soft, bright, or radiant light; illuminated with a gentle glow
softly bright or glowing

Examples

The windows of the cottage were aglow with the warm light of candles.
Her face was aglow with excitement on the night of the celebration.
The city streets are aglow during the annual festival of lights.

Synset Examples

a house aglow with lights
glowing embers
lambent tongues of flame
the lucent moon
a sky luminous with stars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glowing - emitting light as a result of being heated or illuminated
  • Synonym: radiant - sending out light; shining or glowing brightly

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'a-' (in, on) + 'glow'.
