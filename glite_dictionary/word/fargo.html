Fargo

1. PROPN

/ ˈfɑrɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːɡəʊ / (BrE)
A city in eastern North Dakota, United States, situated on the Red River and the largest city in the state.
city in eastern North Dakota, USA

Examples

She moved to Fargo to attend North Dakota State University.
The winters in Fargo are known to be especially harsh.
A new museum opened in downtown Fargo last year.

Etymology

Named after William G. Fargo, a co-founder of the Wells Fargo Express Company.
