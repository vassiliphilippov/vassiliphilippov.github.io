one hundred sixty-five

1. NUM

/ wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˌsɪksti ˈfaɪv / (AmE), / wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˌsɪksti ˈfaɪv / (BrE)
The number that is equal to one hundred and sixty-five; the cardinal number after 164 and before 166.
the number 165

Examples

There are one hundred sixty-five pages in this book.
She received one hundred sixty-five votes in the election.
The museum was built one hundred sixty-five years ago.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: hundred - the number 100
  • Degree Higher: one hundred sixty-six - the number 166
