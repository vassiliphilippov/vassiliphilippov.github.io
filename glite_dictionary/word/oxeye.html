3. NOUN

/ ˈɑkˌsaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɒksaɪ / (BrE)
A Eurasian perennial herb with daisylike flowers, yellow petals, and dark centers, belonging to the genus Leucanthemum or related genera.
Eurasian plant with daisylike flowers

Examples

The field was full of blooming oxeye flowers swaying in the breeze.
Gardeners often plant oxeye for its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
A single oxeye stood out among the wild grasses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oxeye daisy - A common species of oxeye with large white petals and a yellow center

Etymology

From Old English *oxan ēage* (ox's eye), referring to the flower's appearance.
