grinner

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrɪnər / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪnə / (BrE)
a person who grins, often habitually or in a particular situation
person who grins

Examples

As soon as the joke was told, the class's resident grinner flashed his trademark smile.
His reputation as a constant grinner made him popular at parties.
The grinner in the photo stands out from the rest of the serious faces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grin - to smile broadly, especially by showing the teeth

Etymology

From the verb 'grin' + suffix '-er' (agent noun).
