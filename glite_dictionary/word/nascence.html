nascence

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæsəns / (AmE), / ˈnasəns / (BrE)
the process or state of being born or beginning to exist; birth or origin
birth; coming into existence

Examples

The nascence of modern technology changed the way we live.
Historians study the nascence of civilizations to understand their development.
Artists often draw inspiration from the nascence of new ideas.

Synset Examples

they celebrated the birth of their first child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birth - the emergence of a baby from the body of its mother
  • Synonym: genesis - the origin or mode of formation of something
  • Antonym: death - the end of life; cessation of all biological functions

Etymology

From Latin 'nascentia' (being born), from 'nasci' (to be born).
