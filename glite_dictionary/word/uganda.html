Uganda

1. PROPN

/ juˈɡændə / (AmE), / juˈɡɑːndə / (BrE)
A landlocked country in East Africa, which became independent from the United Kingdom in 1962.
country in East Africa

Examples

Lake Victoria lies to the south of Uganda and forms part of its border.
After graduating, she volunteered to teach in rural Uganda for a year.
Tourism is a growing industry in Uganda, known for its wildlife and national parks.
