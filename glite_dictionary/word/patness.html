patness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpætnɪs / (AmE), / ˈpætnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being exactly suitable or timely for a particular situation; aptness or appropriateness
timely suitability or aptness

Examples

The patness of his reply surprised everyone in the meeting.
She admired the patness of the solution offered to the difficult problem.
Some critics complained about the patness of the movie's ending.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aptness - the quality of being appropriate or suitable
  • Antonym: inappropriateness - the quality of being unsuitable or irrelevant

Etymology

From 'pat' (exactly suitable) + '-ness' (forming nouns denoting a state or quality).
