Cygnus olor

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪɡ.nəs ˈoʊ.lɔr / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡ.nəs ˈɒ.lɔː / (BrE)
The scientific name for the mute swan, a large Eurasian swan species known for its white plumage and silent nature, often domesticated.
scientific name of the mute swan

Examples

The elegant Cygnus olor glided gracefully across the lake, barely making a sound.
In many city parks, visitors admire the beauty of Cygnus olor as the swans swim silently by.
Wildlife biologists track populations of Cygnus olor to monitor their impact on local ecosystems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mute swan - a large white swan native to Eurasia, known for its silent behavior

Etymology

From Latin: 'cygnus' meaning swan and 'olor' meaning swan.
