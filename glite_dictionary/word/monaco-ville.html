Monaco-Ville

1. PROPN

/ məˌnɑːkoʊ ˈvɪl / (AmE), / məˌnækəʊ ˈviːl / (BrE)
the historic district and traditional capital of Monaco, located on a rocky outcrop overlooking the Mediterranean Sea
historic district and capital of Monaco

Examples

Many tourists visit Monaco-Ville to explore the Prince's Palace and its medieval streets.
Monaco-Ville offers stunning views of the harbor and the Mediterranean coastline.
During the national celebration, residents gather in Monaco-Ville for festivities and parades.

Etymology

French, literally 'Monaco-Town'.
