teleselling

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌsɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌsɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of promoting and selling products or services by telephone
selling products or services by telephone

Examples

Many companies rely on teleselling to reach new customers and increase sales.
He started his career in teleselling before moving into field sales.
Some people find teleselling to be more efficient than face-to-face marketing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telemarketing - the marketing of goods and services by telephone

Etymology

Blend of 'tele-' (telephone) and 'selling.'
