netlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɛtˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈnɛt.laɪk / (BrE)
having spaces or openings resembling those of a net or web; resembling a net in appearance or structure
resembling a net or web

Examples

The plant has netlike veins running through its leaves.
The artist created a netlike pattern on the sculpture's surface.
A netlike shadow was cast by the old iron gate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reticulated - having a pattern or structure like a net or network
