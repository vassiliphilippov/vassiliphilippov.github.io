dispensed

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈspɛnst / (AmE), / dɪˈspenst / (BrE)
given out or distributed in carefully measured portions, especially by a professional such as a pharmacist.
given out in measured amounts

Examples

The doctor explained how the dispensed medication should be taken daily.
Food packages were dispensed to families affected by the disaster.
Each prescription is dispensed according to strict pharmacy guidelines.

Synset Examples

medicines dispensed to the sick

Related Terms

  • Synonym: administered - given or applied (especially medicines or treatments) to someone

Etymology

Past participle of "dispense," from Latin dispensare (to distribute).
