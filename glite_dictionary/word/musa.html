Musa

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːsə / (AmE), / ˈmjuːsə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical plants in the banana family that includes bananas and plantains.
genus of plants including bananas

Examples

The genus Musa comprises both bananas and plantains.
Scientists studied several species within the Musa group.
Commercial cultivation of Musa is important in tropical countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banana - edible fruit of plants of the genus Musa

Etymology

From Latin Musa (the plant genus), from Arabic mawz (banana).

2. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːsə / (AmE), / ˈmjuːsə / (BrE)
the genus of tropical plants in the family Musaceae, which includes banana and plantain species
genus of banana plants

Examples

The banana is classified under the botanical genus Musa.
Scientists have discovered several new species within the genus Musa.
Plantains are also members of the Musa genus, widely cultivated for food.

Etymology

From Latin Musa, name for the banana plant, probably from Arabic mawz.
