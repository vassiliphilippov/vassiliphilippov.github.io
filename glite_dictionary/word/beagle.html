beagle

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈbiːɡəl / (BrE)
a small breed of hound with short legs, a smooth coat, and a keen sense of smell, often used for hunting.
small breed of hound dog

Examples

The beagle followed the scent trail through the forest.
She adopted a playful beagle from the animal shelter.
Beagles are known for their friendly temperament and keen noses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hound - a type of dog bred for hunting by scent

Etymology

Perhaps from Old French *beegueule* ('open throat', from 'bée' (open) + 'gueule' (throat)). Used for noisy, baying dogs.
