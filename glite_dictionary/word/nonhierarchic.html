nonhierarchic

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑːnhaɪˈɛrərkɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnhaɪˈɛərəkɪk / (BrE)
Not organized or classified in a hierarchical manner.
not organized in a hierarchy

Examples

The company adopted a nonhierarchic management structure to encourage collaboration.
Some schools prefer a nonhierarchic approach where all voices are valued equally.
Research indicates that nonhierarchic groups can be more innovative than rigidly structured ones.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hierarchic - organized in a hierarchy

Etymology

From 'non-' (not) + 'hierarchic' (pertaining to hierarchy).
