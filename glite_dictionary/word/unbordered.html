unbordered

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbɔrdɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɔːdəd / (BrE)
having no border or boundary; lacking an edge or frame
lacking a border or edge

Examples

The unbordered canvas gave the painting a free and open feel.
Her unbordered garden merged naturally into the surrounding forest.
They admired the unbordered scarf, which flowed seamlessly from color to color.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bordered - having a border or edge

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'bordered' (having a border).
