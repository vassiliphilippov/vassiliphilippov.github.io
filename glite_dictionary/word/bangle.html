bangle

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈbaŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
A rigid ornamental bracelet, usually made of metal, wood, or plastic, worn around the wrist for decoration.
a rigid decorative bracelet

Examples

She wore a gold bangle on her wrist that shimmered in the sunlight.
A set of colorful bangles completed her traditional outfit.
He bought his sister a silver bangle as a birthday gift.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bracelet - an ornamental band or chain worn around the wrist

Etymology

From Hindi *bāṅglī*, from Sanskrit *vaṅguli* (armlet).

2. NOUN

/ ˈbæŋ.ɡəl / (AmE), / ˈbaŋ.ɡl̩ / (BrE)
A rigid bracelet or ornament, typically inexpensive and showy, worn around the wrist or sometimes as a decoration on clothing.
rigid, showy bracelet or ornament

Examples

She wore a brightly colored bangle on each wrist to match her dress.
The market was filled with stalls selling cheap bangles and trinkets.
The bangle jingled as she gestured with her hand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bracelet - an ornamental band or circlet for the wrist or arm
  • Synonym: cuff - a stiff band, sometimes decorative, worn around the wrist

Etymology

From Hindi *bā̃glī* (bangle), from Sanskrit *vaṅguli* (armlet).
