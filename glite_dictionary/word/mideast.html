Mideast

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːˌdɪst / (AmE), / ˈmiːˌdɪːst / (BrE)
a region around the eastern Mediterranean, typically including countries from Turkey and Egypt to Iran; the site of ancient civilizations and continuous modern significance
region around eastern Mediterranean

Examples

Many major conflicts in the Mideast have shaped global history.
She specialized in the political systems of the Mideast during her graduate studies.
Drought continues to affect agriculture across the Mideast.

Synset Examples

the Middle East is the cradle of Western civilization

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Middle East - another term for the same region

Etymology

Shortened form of 'Middle East'.
