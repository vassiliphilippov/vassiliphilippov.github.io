JDAM

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒeɪ dæm / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪ dæm / (BrE)
A guidance kit attached to unguided gravity bombs, allowing them to be steered accurately to a target using GPS technology.
smart bomb guidance kit

Examples

The military used a JDAM to precisely strike the target with minimal collateral damage.
Engineers installed the JDAM kit onto regular bombs to enhance their accuracy.
Reports confirmed that several JDAMs were deployed during the operation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smart bomb - precision-guided munition for accurate targeting

Etymology

Acronym for Joint Direct Attack Munition.
