Placidyl

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæsɪˌdɪl / (AmE), / ˈplæsɪˌdɪl / (BrE)
A mild sedative-hypnotic drug, formerly used to treat insomnia; trademark name for ethchlorvynol.
sedative-hypnotic drug (brand name)
medical

Examples
