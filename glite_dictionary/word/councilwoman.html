councilwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊnsəlˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈkaʊnsəlˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A female member of a council, especially a local government council.
female council member

Examples

The councilwoman addressed the community about the new housing project.
After years of service, she became the first Black councilwoman in the city.
The councilwoman proposed several initiatives to improve local schools.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: councilman - a male council member
  • Synonym: councilor - a member of a council (gender-neutral)

Etymology

Formed from 'council' + 'woman', patterned after 'councilman'.
