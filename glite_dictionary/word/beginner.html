beginner

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈɡɪnər / (AmE), / bɪˈɡɪnə / (BrE)
a person who has just started to learn or do something and has little or no experience in it
person new to an activity

Examples

She's a beginner at playing the guitar, but she's learning quickly.
The class is designed specifically for beginners with no prior programming experience.
Even as a beginner, he was able to complete the basic climbing course.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: novice - a person new to and inexperienced in a job or situation
  • Antonym: expert - a person who is very knowledgeable about or skillful in a particular area

Etymology

From 'begin' + '-er', dating to Middle English.
