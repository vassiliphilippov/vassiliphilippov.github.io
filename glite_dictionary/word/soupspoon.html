soupspoon

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːpˌspuːn / (AmE), / ˈsuːpˌspuːn / (BrE)
A spoon with a large, rounded bowl designed for eating soup.
spoon for eating soup

Examples

She picked up her soupspoon to taste the hot broth.
The restaurant set the table with a soupspoon next to each bowl.
I need to buy a new soupspoon because mine went missing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tablespoon - a large spoon for serving or measuring food
  • Antonym: teaspoon - a small spoon used for stirring tea or coffee
