unshockable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈʃɑkəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃɒkəbl / (BrE)
not easily surprised or offended by shocking or unexpected events or behaviors.
not easily shocked or offended

Examples

After years of working in the emergency room, she had become truly unshockable.
His unshockable nature made him the perfect confidant for friends with unusual stories.
No matter what scandal was revealed, the senator remained publicly unshockable.

Synset Examples

he was warmhearted, sensible and unshockable

Related Terms

  • Antonym: shockable - easily shocked or surprised
  • Synonym: unflappable - not easily upset or confused, calm under pressure

Etymology

Formed from un- (not) + shock + -able (capable of).
