Schinus chichita

1. NOUN

/ skiːnəs tʃɪˈtʃiːtə / (AmE), / skiːnəs tʃɪˈtʃiːtə / (BrE)
a small resinous tree or shrub native to Brazil, belonging to the cashew family
tree or shrub native to Brazil

Examples

The Schinus chichita thrives in the subtropical regions of Brazil.
Botanists are studying the resin produced by Schinus chichita for its potential uses.
Farmers in Brazil sometimes plant Schinus chichita as a windbreak.

Etymology

From the genus name Schinus, derived from Greek 'schinos' (mastic tree), and the species epithet 'chichita'.
