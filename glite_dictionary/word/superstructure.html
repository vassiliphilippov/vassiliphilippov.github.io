superstructure

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːpərˌstrʌktʃər / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌstrʌktʃə / (BrE)
the part of a ship's structure that is above the main deck
structure above a ship's main deck

Examples

The ship's superstructure was damaged during the storm.
From the bridge to the funnel, the superstructure rose high above the deck.
The new cruise liner features a sleek superstructure designed for better aerodynamics.

Etymology

From Latin superstructura, from super- (above) + structura (a building).
