fitspo

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪtˌspoʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɪt.spəʊ / (BrE)
images or content intended to inspire fitness or promote exercise and healthy eating, especially as shared on social media.
fitness inspiration content (esp. online)
informal slang

Examples

She follows several fitspo accounts on Instagram to stay motivated for her workouts.
That post is pure fitspo, reminding people to make time for exercise every day.
Some worry that fitspo can create unrealistic expectations about health and fitness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thinspo - images or content promoting thinness (usually with negative connotation)

Etymology

Blend of 'fitness' and 'inspiration'.
