non-poisonous

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈpɔɪzənəs / (AmE), / nɒnˈpɔɪzənəs / (BrE)
Not containing poison; not harmful or dangerous to eat, touch, or use.
not containing poison; safe

Examples

You can eat these berries because they are non-poisonous.
The zookeepers only allow visitors to handle non-poisonous snakes.
The label assured customers that the paint was completely non-poisonous.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: poisonous - containing poison; harmful or dangerous if eaten or touched

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) + 'poisonous'.
