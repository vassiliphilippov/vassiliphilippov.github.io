yachtsperson

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɑtsˌpɜrsən / (AmE), / ˈjɒtsˌpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who owns or sails a yacht, regardless of gender
person who owns or sails a yacht

Examples

The seasoned yachtsperson skillfully navigated through the rough waters of the bay.
As an avid yachtsperson, she participates in regattas all around the world.
Every yachtsperson knows the importance of maintaining their vessel in top condition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yachtsman - a man who owns or sails a yacht
  • Synonym: yachtswoman - a woman who owns or sails a yacht

Etymology

Formed from 'yacht' + 'person', modeled on terms such as 'spokesperson'.
