neuronal

1. ADJ

/ nʊˈroʊ.nəl / (AmE), / njʊəˈrəʊ.nəl / (BrE)
relating to neurons or the nervous system.
relating to neurons

Examples

Neuronal activity in the brain can be observed using advanced imaging techniques.
The researchers studied the neuronal response to visual stimuli.
Certain diseases can impair normal neuronal function, leading to neurological disorders.

Synset Examples

neural network

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neural - relating to nerves or the nervous system

Etymology

From neuron, based on Greek neuron (nerve) + -al (adjective forming suffix).
