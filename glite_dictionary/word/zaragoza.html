Zaragoza

1. PROPN

/ ˌzærəˈɡoʊzə / (AmE), / ˌzærəˈɡəʊzə / (BrE)
A city in northeastern Spain, located on the Ebro River; formerly the capital of the Kingdom of Aragon.
city in northeastern Spain, former Aragon capital

Examples

We visited Zaragoza during our trip through northern Spain.
Zaragoza is known for its rich history and beautiful architecture.
The Ebro River runs through the city of Zaragoza.

Etymology

From Arabic Saraqusṭa, from Latin Caesaraugusta, named after the Roman Emperor Augustus.
