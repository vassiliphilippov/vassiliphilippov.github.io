multi-ethnic

1. ADJ

/ ˌmʌltiˈɛθnɪk / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈɛθnɪk / (BrE)
involving or relating to people from several different ethnic groups
involving several ethnic groups

Examples

The city is known for its multi-ethnic communities and vibrant cultural festivals.
Our school promotes a multi-ethnic environment where everyone feels welcome and valued.
The country’s multi-ethnic population speaks dozens of different languages at home.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: monolithic - consisting of a single, uniform group, especially in terms of ethnicity
  • Synonym: multi-racial - involving people from several different racial backgrounds

Etymology

From Latin multi- (many) + ethnic, from Greek ethnos (nation, people).
