standoffishly

1. ADV

/ ˌstændˈɔ.fɪʃ.li / (AmE), / ˌstændˈɒf.ɪʃ.li / (BrE)
In a way that is unfriendly, distant, or reserved; behaving with a lack of warmth or approachability.
in an unfriendly or reserved manner

Examples

He standoffishly avoided eye contact with everyone at the meeting.
She responded standoffishly to my attempts at friendly conversation.
The celebrity standoffishly declined autographs as she hurried past the crowd.

Synset Examples

standoffishly, he declined the invitation to the office party

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aloofly - in a distant or uninvolved manner
  • Antonym: sociably - in a friendly and willing to talk manner

Etymology

Formed from 'standoffish' + '-ly'. 'Standoffish' dates to early 19th century, based on 'stand off'.
