Pouteria zapota

1. NOUN

/ poʊˈtɪəriə zəˈpoʊtə / (AmE), / paʊˈtɪəriə zəˈpəʊtə / (BrE)
A tropical American tree with mahogany-like wood and sweet, edible, egg-shaped fruit, also known as the mamey sapote.
tropical tree; source of mamey sapote fruit

Examples

The fruit of the Pouteria zapota is prized for its smooth texture and sweet flavor.
Farmers in Central America often cultivate Pouteria zapota for its valuable wood and nutritious fruit.
Chefs use Pouteria zapota in smoothies and desserts due to its rich, orange flesh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mamey sapote - sweet, edible fruit of Pouteria zapota
