Fringilla coelebs

1. NOUN

/ frɪnˈdʒɪlə koʊˈɛlɛbs / (AmE), / frɪŋˈɡɪlə kəʊˈiːlɛbs / (BrE)
A small European finch with a cheerful song, commonly known as the chaffinch.
species of European finch (chaffinch)

Examples

The Fringilla coelebs is widespread throughout European woodlands and gardens.
Birdwatchers often look for the Fringilla coelebs because of its distinctive plumage and lively song.
The cheerful song of the Fringilla coelebs is a common sound in springtime Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chaffinch - common name for Fringilla coelebs

Etymology

From Latin; 'Fringilla' (finch) + 'coelebs' (bachelor), referring to the species' habits.
