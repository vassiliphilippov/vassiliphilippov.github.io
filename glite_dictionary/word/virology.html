virology

1. NOUN

/ vaɪˈrɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / vaɪˈrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of science that deals with the study of viruses and viral diseases
study of viruses
medical

Examples

She decided to specialize in virology after studying various infectious diseases.
Advancements in virology have led to the development of effective vaccines.
His groundbreaking research in virology helped combat the spread of the new virus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microbiology - the study of microorganisms, including bacteria, viruses, fungi, and protozoa

Etymology

From Latin 'virus' (poison) + Greek '-logia' (study of).
