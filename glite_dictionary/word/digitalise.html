to digitalise

1. VERB

/ ˈdɪdʒɪtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtəlaɪz / (BrE)
to convert information or data into a digital form that can be used by computers
convert to digital form

Examples

The museum plans to digitalise its entire art collection for online viewing.
Many old records were digitalised to preserve them for future generations.
He is working on a project to digitalise handwritten manuscripts.

Synset Examples

he bought a device to digitize the data

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to digitize - to convert data into digital form

Etymology

From 'digital' + '-ise'; related to American English 'digitize'.
