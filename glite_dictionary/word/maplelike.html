maplelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈmeɪpəlˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmeɪpəlˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of a maple, especially in appearance or form.
resembling a maple (tree or leaves)

Examples

The tree had maplelike leaves, making it difficult to distinguish from actual maples in the forest.
Some shrubs develop a maplelike shape as they mature, even though they belong to a different botanical family.
The artist painted a forest scene filled with maplelike foliage in brilliant autumn colors.

Synset Examples
