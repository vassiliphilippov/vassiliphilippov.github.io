brownish-yellow

1. ADJ

/ ˈbraʊnɪʃ ˈjɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnɪʃ ˈjɛləʊ / (BrE)
of a medium to dark color that is a blend of brown and yellow
medium to dark yellow-brown color

Examples

The walls of the old cottage were painted a warm brownish-yellow shade.
Leaves turn a brownish-yellow color before falling in autumn.
She chose a brownish-yellow scarf to match her coat.
