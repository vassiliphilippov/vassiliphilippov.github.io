musculus sphincter ani externus

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌs.kjə.ləs ˈsfɪŋk.tər ˈæ.ni ɪkˈstɜr.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.kjʊ.ləs ˈsfɪŋk.tər ˈɑː.ni ɛkˈstɜː.nəs / (BrE)
the external ring of voluntary (striated) muscle surrounding the anus and controlling its opening
muscle controlling the anal opening
medical

Examples

The musculus sphincter ani externus plays a crucial role in maintaining fecal continence.
Damage to the musculus sphincter ani externus can result in loss of voluntary control over defecation.
During surgery, care must be taken not to injure the musculus sphincter ani externus to avoid complications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anal sphincter - muscle controlling the anus

Etymology

From Latin: 'musculus' (muscle), 'sphincter' (binder), 'ani' (of the anus), 'externus' (outer).
