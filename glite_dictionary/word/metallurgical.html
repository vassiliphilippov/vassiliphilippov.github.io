metallurgical

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɛt̬l̩ˈɝːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmet.əlˈɜː.dʒɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with metallurgy, especially the properties, extraction, and processing of metals
relating to metallurgy

Examples

The company hired a metallurgical expert to improve its steel production process.
She is studying metallurgical engineering at the university.
Modern metallurgical techniques allow for the creation of stronger and lighter alloys.

Synset Examples

metallurgical engineer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metallurgy - the science and technology of metals

Etymology

From Greek metallourgos (worker in metal), via French métallurgique and Modern Latin metallurgicus.
