Tunisia

1. PROPN

/ tuˈniʒə / (AmE), / tjuˈniːziə / (BrE)
a country in North Africa on the Mediterranean coast; independent since 1956.
country in North Africa

Examples

Tunisia is renowned for its ancient ruins and beautiful Mediterranean beaches.
After gaining independence, Tunisia underwent significant political changes.
She traveled from Tunisia to Morocco by ferry.

Synset Examples

southern Tunisia is mostly desert

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tunis - capital city of Tunisia

Etymology

From French 'Tunisie', from Arabic 'Tūnis'.
