Tantric

1. ADJ

/ ˈtæn.trɪk / (AmE), / ˈtæn.trɪk / (BrE)
relating to Tantrism, a tradition of beliefs and practices within Hinduism and Buddhism characterized by esoteric rituals and teachings
relating to Tantrism or its practices

Examples

Many Tantric rituals focus on spiritual transformation through symbolic acts.
The temple is famous for its Tantric sculptures and religious artwork.
She studied Tantric philosophy as part of her interest in Eastern religions.

Synset Examples

Tantric rituals

Etymology

From Sanskrit tantra (loom, warp, doctrine, framework) via Hindi and Sanskrit roots.
