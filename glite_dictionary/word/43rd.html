43rd

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrti θɜrd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːti θɜːd / (BrE)
Coming after the forty-second in a sequence; ordinal number for forty-three.
ordinal number for forty-three

Examples

He finished in 43rd place out of a hundred runners.
The 43rd annual festival will be held next June.
She lives on the 43rd floor of the skyscraper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forty-third - written form of 43rd (ordinal number)
