Nagoya

1. PROPN

/ nəˈɡɔɪə / (AmE), / nəˈɡɔɪə / (BrE)
a major industrial city in southern Honshu, Japan, known for its manufacturing industries and as a cultural center.
industrial city in Japan

Examples

Nagoya is renowned for its automobile manufacturing industry and as the birthplace of Toyota.
The train from Tokyo to Nagoya takes less than two hours on the Shinkansen.
During our trip to Japan, we visited Nagoya Castle and enjoyed the local cuisine.

Etymology

From Japanese 名古屋 (Nagoya), the name of the city.
