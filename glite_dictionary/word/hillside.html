hillside

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪl.saɪd / (AmE), / ˈhɪl.saɪd / (BrE)
the sloping side of a hill
the slope of a hill

Examples

The sheep grazed peacefully on the hillside overlooking the valley.
A small cottage stood alone on a grassy hillside.
After the rain, wildflowers blossomed across the hillside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slope - an inclined surface or side of something
