uncontrived

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.kənˈtraɪvd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kənˈtraɪvd / (BrE)
Not planned or artificial; natural and genuine.
not artificial or forced

Examples

The documentary has an uncontrived feel, capturing real moments without staging.
She has an uncontrived charm that makes everyone feel comfortable around her.
The laughter at the party was uncontrived and infectious, spreading from group to group.

Synset Examples

an air of unstudied spontaneous utterance is apt to be painstakingly achieved
simple unstudied charm

Related Terms

  • Antonym: contrived - deliberately created rather than arising naturally
  • Synonym: artificial - made or produced by human beings rather than occurring naturally

Etymology

Formed from prefix un- (not) + contrived (from Latin contrivere 'to grind, devise').
