spermatogenesis

1. NOUN

/ spɚˌmæt̬əˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / spəˌmætəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs / (BrE)
the process by which sperm cells develop and mature, typically occurring in the male testes
development of sperm cells
medical

Examples

Disruption in spermatogenesis can lead to male infertility.
Researchers are studying the hormones that regulate spermatogenesis.
Proper nutrition is important for healthy spermatogenesis in mammals.

Etymology

From Greek sperma (seed, sperm) + genesis (origin, generation).
