obtainable

1. ADJ

/ əbˈteɪnəbl̩ / (AmE), / əbˈteɪnəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be acquired or gained; possible to obtain.
able to be obtained

Examples

Discounts on monthly subscriptions are obtainable for students with valid ID.
The evidence was easily obtainable through a simple online search.
Fresh fruit is obtainable in most supermarkets throughout the year.

Synset Examples

savings of up to 50 percent are obtainable

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unobtainable - not able to be obtained
  • Synonym: accessible - able to be reached or used

Etymology

From 'obtain' + '-able', where 'obtain' is from Latin 'obtinere' (to get hold of).
