Houston

1. PROPN

/ ˈhjuːstən / (AmE), / ˈhjuːstən / (BrE)
a large city in southeastern Texas near the Gulf of Mexico, known for its major role in space exploration and energy industries
largest city in Texas, U.S.

Examples

Many international companies have their headquarters in Houston.
After graduating, she moved to Houston to work for NASA.
The flight from New York to Houston takes about three hours.

Etymology

Named after Sam Houston, an American soldier and politician.
