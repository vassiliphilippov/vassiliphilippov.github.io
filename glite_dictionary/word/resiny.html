resiny

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛz.i.ni / (AmE), / ˈrɛz.ɪ.ni / (BrE)
Having the characteristics, smell, or texture of resin, pitch, or tar.
having qualities of resin or tar

Examples

The smoke from the burning pine logs gave the cabin a resiny aroma.
She noticed a resiny residue on her fingers after handling the tree branches.
The varnish left a slightly resiny finish on the wood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resinous - containing or resembling resin
  • Synonym: pitchy - having the characteristics of pitch

Etymology

From 'resin' + '-y', forming an adjective meaning 'like resin'.
