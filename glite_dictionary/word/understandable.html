understandable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɚˈstændəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈstændəbəl / (BrE)
able to be understood or comprehended; clear or easy to grasp.
able to be understood

Examples

Her explanation was clear and understandable, even to people with no background in the subject.
It's perfectly understandable that you're upset after everything that happened.
The teacher's handwriting made the notes barely understandable to the students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comprehensible - able to be understood; intelligible
  • Antonym: unintelligible - impossible to understand

Etymology

From 'understand' + '-able', with '-able' from Latin '-abilis', forming adjectives meaning 'capable of'.
