Chippewyan

1. NOUN

/ tʃɪpəˈwaɪən / (AmE), / tʃɪpəˈwaɪən / (BrE)
The Athabaskan language spoken by the Chipewyan people of northern Canada.
Athabaskan language of the Chipewyan people

Examples

She is studying Chippewyan as part of her research on Indigenous Canadian languages.
Many elders still speak Chippewyan in their daily lives.
A new dictionary aims to preserve and document the Chippewyan language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chipewyan - a member of the Chipewyan people of northern Canada
  • Degree Higher: Athabaskan - the family of languages including Chippewyan, Navajo, and others

Etymology

From the name 'Chipewyan,' an English adaptation of a Cree word meaning 'pointed skins,' referring to a style of clothing.
