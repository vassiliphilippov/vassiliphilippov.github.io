fieldswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːldzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈfiːldzwʊmən / (BrE)
a female member of the fielding side in cricket; a woman who fields rather than bats
female cricket fielder

Examples

The new captain is an excellent fieldswoman, always alert during every over.
As a fieldswoman, she set a record for most catches in a single match.
Young girls at the cricket academy aspire to become great fieldswomen like her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fieldsman - a member of the fielding side in cricket
  • Synonym: fielder - a player who fields in cricket or baseball

Etymology

Formed from 'field(s)' + 'woman', by analogy with 'fieldsman'.
