copywriter

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑː.piˌraɪ.tər / (AmE), / ˈkɒp.iˌraɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to write the text for advertisements or publicity material
person who writes advertising text

Examples

As a copywriter, she crafts compelling slogans for major brands.
He was hired as a copywriter at an advertising agency right after college.
The copywriter brainstormed catchy phrases to promote the new product.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertiser - a person or company that advertises a product or service

Etymology

From 'copy' (text for publication) + 'writer'; first used in early 20th century advertising.
