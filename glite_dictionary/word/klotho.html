Klotho

1. PROPN

/ ˈkloʊθoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkləʊθəʊ / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, the goddess of fate who spins the thread of human life.
Greek goddess of fate who spins life thread

Examples

In ancient myths, Klotho is depicted as spinning the delicate thread of each person's destiny.
The story of Klotho has inspired many works of art, symbolizing the beginning of life.
According to legend, Klotho worked with her sisters to determine the fate of mortals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Clotho - Alternate spelling of Klotho, Greek goddess of fate who spins the thread of life

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Κλωθώ (Klōthṓ), meaning 'the spinner'.
