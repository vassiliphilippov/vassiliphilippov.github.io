vinous

1. ADJ

/ ˈvaɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈvɪn.əs / (BrE)
relating to, resembling, or associated with wine
relating to wine

Examples

The sommelier described the sauce as having a distinctly vinous aroma.
Her artwork often reflected a vinous theme, featuring rolling vineyards and full glasses.
Experts noted the vinous character of the beverage, reminiscent of an aged red.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oenological - relating to the study of wines
  • Antonym: nonalcoholic - not containing alcohol

Etymology

From Latin 'vinum' (wine).
