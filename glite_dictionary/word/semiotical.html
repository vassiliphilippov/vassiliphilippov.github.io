semiotical

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛmiˈɑːtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌsɛmɪˈɒtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with semiotics, the study of signs and symbols and their use or interpretation.
relating to semiotics (study of signs and symbols)

Examples

The professor introduced semiotical theories to explain how language conveys meaning.
Their research focused on the semiotical aspects of advertising and brand symbolism.
A semiotical approach can uncover deeper cultural messages in works of art.

Synset Examples

semiotic analysis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: semiotic - relating to the study of signs and symbols (semiotics)

Etymology

From semiotic (from Greek sēmeiōtikós, 'of signs') + -al (forming adjectives).
