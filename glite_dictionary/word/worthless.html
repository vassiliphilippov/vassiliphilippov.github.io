worthless

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɜrθləs / (AmE), / ˈwɜːθləs / (BrE)
having no real value, importance, or usefulness
having no value or usefulness

Examples

He threw away the worthless gadget after it broke for the third time.
Many considered the old coins to be worthless until a collector showed interest.
She felt worthless after failing the exam, though her friends reassured her.

Synset Examples

a worthless idler

Related Terms

  • Antonym: valuable - having considerable value, usefulness, or importance

Etymology

From Middle English 'worth' (value) + '-less' (without).
