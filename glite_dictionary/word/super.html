super

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuː.pɚ / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pə / (BrE)
a person who manages the maintenance and daily operation of an apartment building, often handling repairs and collecting rent; short for superintendent
apartment building manager or caretaker
informal american

Examples

The super fixed the leaking faucet in my apartment today.
If you're having trouble with your heating, contact the super for help.
The new super is much friendlier than the last one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: superintendent - person who manages an apartment building

Etymology

Shortened form of superintendent.
