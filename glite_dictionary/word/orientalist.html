orientalist

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔːriˈɛntəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌɔːrɪˈɛntəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies or is an expert in the languages, cultures, or histories of Asian societies, especially those of East, South, or Southeast Asia
expert in Asian studies

Examples

The museum hired an orientalist to authenticate the ancient artifacts from Persia.
As an orientalist, she published several books about Japanese literature and art.
His career as an orientalist began with his studies of Arabic in university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asianist - a person specializing in Asian languages or cultures

Etymology

From French orientalisme, from Latin oriens (east); suffix -ist denotes a person engaged in the field.
