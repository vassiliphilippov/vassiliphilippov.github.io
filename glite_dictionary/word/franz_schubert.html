Franz Schubert

1. PROPN

/ frɑnts ˈʃuːbɚt / (AmE), / ˈfrants ˈʃuːbɜːt / (BrE)
(1797–1828) an Austrian composer renowned for his wide range of compositions, especially for voice and piano, and a significant figure in early Romantic music.
Austrian Romantic composer (1797–1828)
american british

Examples

Many music lovers admire Franz Schubert for his beautiful lieder and symphonies.

Franz Schubert's compositions remain popular in classical music performances worldwide.
