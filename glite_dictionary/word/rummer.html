rummer

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌmɚ / (AmE), / ˈrʌmə / (BrE)
a large drinking glass with an ovoid bowl on a stem, used especially for drinking toasts
large stemmed drinking glass

Examples

The antique shop displayed an elegant rummer in its window.
She raised her rummer high during the wedding toast.
Collectors often seek out 18th-century rummers for their unique shapes.

Etymology

From Dutch 'roemer', a type of wine glass.
