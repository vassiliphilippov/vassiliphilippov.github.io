upmost

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌpməʊst / (AmE), / ˈʌpməʊst / (BrE)
at or nearest to the top; highest in position or place.
nearest to the top

Examples

She placed the vase on the upmost shelf, out of the children's reach.
He reached for the upmost branch to pick the ripest apple.
The upmost floor of the building offers a spectacular view of the city.

Synset Examples

the uppermost book in the pile
on the topmost step

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uppermost - highest in position; nearest to the top
  • Synonym: topmost - at the very top; highest in position

Etymology

From Middle English, formed from 'up' + 'most'.
