spikelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈspaɪkˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈspaɪkˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling or having the characteristics of a spike; long, thin, and pointed.
resembling a spike

Examples

The plant has spikelike leaves that grow straight upward.
Her hair was styled in a series of spikelike points, giving her a bold look.
They noticed a spikelike projection sticking out from the metal surface.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiky - having many spikes or points; resembling or covered with spikes
