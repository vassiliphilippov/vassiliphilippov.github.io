chorizo

1. NOUN

/ tʃəˈriːzoʊ / (AmE), / tʃəˈriːthəʊ / (BrE)
a spicy pork sausage, especially of Spanish or Mexican origin, seasoned with paprika or chili
spicy Spanish or Mexican sausage

Examples

She added sliced chorizo to the paella for extra flavor.
You can find chorizo at most supermarkets nowadays.
The smoky taste of chorizo complemented the beans perfectly.

Etymology

From Spanish 'chorizo', meaning sausage.
