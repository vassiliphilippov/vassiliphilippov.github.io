Damascene

1. NOUN

/ ˌdæməˈsin / (AmE), / ˌdæməˈsiːn / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Damascus, the capital city of Syria.
native or resident of Damascus

Examples

As a proud Damascene, she often spoke of the city's rich history.
Many Damascenes celebrate traditional festivals with great enthusiasm.
He met a Damascene who offered to show him the ancient markets.

Etymology

From Latin Damascenus, from Greek Damaskēnos, from Damascus.
