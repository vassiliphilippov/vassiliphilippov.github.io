star-studded

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɑr ˌstʌdɪd / (AmE), / ˈstɑː ˌstʌdɪd / (BrE)
Featuring or containing many famous people, especially celebrities.
having many celebrities present

Examples

The movie premiere was a star-studded affair with actors from all over the world.
We attended a star-studded charity event that featured several famous musicians.
Her wedding turned into a star-studded celebration when multiple celebrities arrived.

Synset Examples

He felt like a nonebrity at the star-studded gala.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celebrity-packed - filled with celebrities
  • Antonym: low-profile - not attracting attention; lacking famous people

Etymology

Formed from 'star' (celebrity) + 'studded' (covered or decorated with small objects).
