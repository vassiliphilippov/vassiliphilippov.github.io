inexpensive

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnɛkˈspɛnsɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪnɪkˈspensɪv / (BrE)
costing relatively little; not expensive
not expensive; low in price

Examples

They found an inexpensive hotel near the station.
This smartphone is surprisingly inexpensive considering its features.
We had lunch at an inexpensive little café on the corner.

Synset Examples

it would have been cheap at twice the price
inexpensive family restaurants

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheap - costing little money; low-priced
  • Antonym: expensive - costing a lot of money
  • Synonym: affordable - reasonably priced; able to be afforded

Etymology

From prefix in- (not) + expensive, from Latin *expensivus* (costly).
