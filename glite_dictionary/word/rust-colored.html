rust-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌstˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈrʌstˌkʌləd / (BrE)
Having the brownish-red or orange-brown color of rust.
having the color of rust

Examples

The old barn's rust-colored roof stood out against the green fields.
She wore a rust-colored scarf that matched the autumn leaves perfectly.
The artist used a rust-colored paint to give the bridge a weathered appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reddish-brown - of a color slightly more red than brown
  • Synonym: orange-brown - of a brown color tinged with orange
