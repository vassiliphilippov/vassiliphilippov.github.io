underprivileged

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndərˈprɪvəlɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈprɪvəlɪdʒd / (BrE)
lacking the rights, advantages, or opportunities enjoyed by others in society; disadvantaged
disadvantaged; lacking advantages in society

Examples

The new program aims to help underprivileged families access healthcare and education.
He grew up in an underprivileged neighborhood but went on to attend university.
Volunteers work with underprivileged children, providing them with meals and tutoring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disadvantaged - lacking basic necessities or privileges in society
  • Antonym: privileged - having special rights or advantages

Etymology

From 'under-' (below, less) + 'privileged', from Latin 'privilegium' (law affecting an individual).
