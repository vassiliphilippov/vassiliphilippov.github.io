uninebriated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪˈniː.bri.eɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈniː.bri.eɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
not affected by alcohol; sober.
not under the influence of alcohol

Examples

She was one of the few uninebriated guests at the party, quietly sipping sparkling water.
Despite the late hour, the driver remained uninebriated and fully alert.
The police only questioned those who appeared uninebriated after the incident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sober - not affected by alcohol; not drunk
  • Antonym: inebriated - affected by alcohol; drunk

Etymology

Formed from prefix un- (not) + inebriated (from Latin inebriatus, drunk).
