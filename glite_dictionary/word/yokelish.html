yokelish

1. ADJ

/ ˈjoʊkəlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈjəʊkəlɪʃ / (BrE)
(of a person) lacking in refinement or sophistication; resembling or characteristic of a yokel.
lacking refinement; unsophisticated

Examples

He found their yokelish manners both endearing and frustrating at the formal dinner.
Her yokelish laughter stood out among the polished city crowd.
The director wanted someone with a slightly yokelish charm for the comedic role.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yokel - an unrefined or unsophisticated country person
  • Antonym: sophisticated - having or showing refinement and worldly experience

Etymology

From 'yokel' (country bumpkin) + suffix '-ish' (having the qualities of).
