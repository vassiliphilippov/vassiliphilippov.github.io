braceface

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪs.feɪs / (AmE), / ˈbreɪs.feɪs / (BrE)
a person, especially a child or teenager, who wears dental braces, often used in a teasing or playful way
person with dental braces (informal, teasing)
informal slang

Examples

She felt like a braceface during high school.
Kids called him a braceface, but he laughed it off.
When I got braces, my brother started calling me braceface every day.

Synset Examples

She felt like a total braceface during high school.
