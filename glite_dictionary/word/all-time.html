all-time

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔlˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈɔːlˌtaɪm / (BrE)
(used before a noun) unsurpassed or unmatched in a particular respect up to the present time; the best, worst, highest, etc., that has ever been recorded.
unsurpassed up to the present

Examples

This year the company reported all-time high profits despite the economic downturn.
Attendance at the festival reached an all-time record last weekend.
Her performance is considered one of the all-time greats in tennis history.

Synset Examples

prices at an all-time high
morale at an all-time low
among the all-time great lefthanders
