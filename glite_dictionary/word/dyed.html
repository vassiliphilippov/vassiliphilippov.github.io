dyed

1. ADJ

/ daɪd / (AmE), / daɪd / (BrE)
(of hair, fabric, etc.) artificially colored, not natural.
artificially colored, not natural

Examples

She has dyed her hair a deep red shade.
Many fabrics in the store are dyed using natural ingredients.
He prefers dyed leather for its rich, even color.

Synset Examples

a bleached blonde

Related Terms

  • Antonym: natural - existing in or caused by nature; not artificial
