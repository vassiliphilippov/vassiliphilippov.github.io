Albuquerque

1. PROPN

/ ˈæl.bəˌkɝ.ki / (AmE), / ˈæl.bəˌkɜː.ki / (BrE)
a city in central New Mexico, USA, the largest city in the state, located on the Rio Grande river
largest city in New Mexico, USA

Examples

Albuquerque is known for its annual International Balloon Fiesta, attracting visitors from around the world.
After moving to Albuquerque, she quickly grew to love the beautiful desert scenery.
The television series was filmed on location in Albuquerque, showcasing the city's vibrant neighborhoods.

Etymology

From the Spanish city name 'Alburquerque', likely from Arabic and Latin roots.
