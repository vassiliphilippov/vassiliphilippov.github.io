thirty-fourth

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɝːti ˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / ˌθɜːti ˈfɔːθ / (BrE)
ordinal number matching the number 34 in a series; position 34th in a sequence
the ordinal number 34th

Examples

She finished in thirty-fourth place out of a hundred runners.
This is the thirty-fourth edition of the annual conference.
Their office is on the thirty-fourth floor of the building.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirty-third - the ordinal number 33rd
  • Degree Higher: thirty-fifth - the ordinal number 35th
