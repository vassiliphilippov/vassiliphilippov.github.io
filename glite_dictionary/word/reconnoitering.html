reconnoitering

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈkɑnəˌtɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˌkɒnɪˈtɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of exploring or surveying an area, especially for military or strategic purposes, to gather information
exploring to gather information (military)

Examples

The platoon was tasked with reconnoitering the area before the main force advanced.
Reconnoitering enemy positions required caution and stealth in the dense forest.
Extensive reconnoitering helped the team map out a safe route through hostile territory.

Synset Examples

scouting in enemy territory is very dangerous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scouting - the act of exploring an area to gather information

Etymology

From the verb 'reconnoiter,' itself from French 'reconnaître' (to recognize), from Latin 'recognoscere' (to examine, recognize).
