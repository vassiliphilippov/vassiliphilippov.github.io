expandable

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈspændəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪkˈspændəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be made larger or capable of being expanded.
able to be expanded

Examples

The expandable foam fills all the gaps when applied.
This suitcase has an expandable compartment for extra storage.
Gases are highly expandable under the right conditions.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: compressible - able to be compressed; capable of reduction in volume

Etymology

From 'expand' + '-able', from Latin expandere (to spread out) + Latin suffix '-abilis'.

2. ADJ

/ ɪkˈspændəbəl / (AmE), / ɪkˈspændəbəl / (BrE)
able to be expanded or capable of expanding in size, range, or amount.
able to be expanded

Examples

The expandable suitcase allows you to pack more belongings for your trip.
This sofa has an expandable section that turns it into a bed.
Many tech companies offer expandable storage in their smartphones.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: contractible - able to contract or be contracted

Etymology

From 'expand' + '-able', meaning able to be expanded.
