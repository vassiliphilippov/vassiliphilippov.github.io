maltster

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔlt.stɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɔːlt.stə / (BrE)
a person who makes malt, especially for use in brewing or distilling
person who makes malt

Examples

The maltster delivered a fresh batch of malted barley to the brewery.
As a skilled maltster, he took pride in producing the finest malt in the region.
During the tour, we met a retired maltster who explained the malting process to us.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malter - a person who makes malt

Etymology

From 'malt' + '-ster' (an agent noun suffix denoting a person engaged in an activity).
