dealfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈdiːlˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdiːlˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
A long, slender deep-sea fish with a ribbon-like body, found in various oceans.
a long, slender deep-sea ribbonfish

Examples

Scientists recently discovered a rare dealfish washed up on the shore.
The dealfish can grow to impressive lengths and is rarely seen by divers.
Fishermen occasionally encounter a dealfish caught accidentally in deep-sea nets.
