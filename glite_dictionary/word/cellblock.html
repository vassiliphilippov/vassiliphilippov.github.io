cellblock

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛlˌblɑk / (AmE), / ˈselˌblɒk / (BrE)
a section of a prison containing a number of cells for inmates
prison section with inmate cells

Examples

The prisoner's cellblock was placed under strict lockdown after the incident.
Guards patrol the cellblock every hour to ensure security.
He spent several years in the maximum-security cellblock of the prison.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cell - a small room in a prison where a prisoner is kept
