explication

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛkspləˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛksplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of clarifying or explaining the meaning of something, especially a word, text, or symbol
clarification or explanation

Examples

The professor's explication of the poem helped students understand its deeper meaning.
Her detailed explication of the legal text clarified several ambiguous points.
During the seminar, he offered an explication of complex philosophical concepts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: explanation - a statement that makes something clear
  • Antonym: obfuscation - the act of making something unclear or obscure

Etymology

From Latin explicatio (an unfolding, explanation), from explicare (to unfold, explain).

2. NOUN

/ ˌɛkspləˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛksplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a detailed explanation of the meaning or interpretation of something, especially a text or concept
detailed explanation or interpretation

Examples

The professor's explication of the poem revealed hidden layers of meaning.
We found his detailed explication of the legal terms extremely helpful.
Without a clear explication, the technical report was difficult to understand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: explanation - a statement that makes something clear
  • Synonym: interpretation - the act of explaining the meaning of something

Etymology

From Latin explicatio (unfolding, explanation), from explicare (to unfold, explain).
