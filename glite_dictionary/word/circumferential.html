circumferential

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɜr.kəm.fəˈrɛn.ʃəl / (AmE), / ˌsɜː.kəm.fəˈrɛn.ʃəl / (BrE)
lying around or near the outer edge or periphery of something; surrounding the circumference
around the outer edge; peripheral

Examples

A circumferential road was built to ease traffic congestion in the city center.
The surveyors measured the circumferential distance around the property line.
Some cities have circumferential highways to redirect vehicles away from downtown.

Synset Examples

circumferential highways around cities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peripheral - relating to or situated on the edge or periphery of something

Etymology

From Latin circumferentia (a carrying around) + English -al.
