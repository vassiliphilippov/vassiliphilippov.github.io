Bantu-speaking

1. ADJ

/ ˈbæn.tuˌspi.kɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈban.tuˌspiː.kɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to people who speak Bantu languages or to the Bantu languages themselves
relating to Bantu language speakers

Examples

The Bantu-speaking communities in eastern Africa have diverse traditions and customs.
Researchers have studied the migration of Bantu-speaking groups across the continent.
Many Bantu-speaking peoples have influenced the cultural landscape of southern Africa.

Synset Examples

the Bantu-speaking people of Africa

Etymology

From 'Bantu' (a group of languages in Africa) + 'speaking'.
