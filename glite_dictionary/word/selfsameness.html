selfsameness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛlfˌseɪmnɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɛlfˌseɪmnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being identical with itself; sameness or identity over time
quality of being identical with itself

Examples

Philosophers often debate the concept of selfsameness in discussions about personal identity.
The selfsameness of the artifact was questioned after years of replacement parts.
He argued that true change disrupts the selfsameness of an object over time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: identity - the fact of being who or what a person or thing is

Etymology

From 'self' + 'sameness', modeled after German philosophical usage (Selbigkeit).
