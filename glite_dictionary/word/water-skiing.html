water-skiing

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˌskiɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌskiːɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport of gliding across water on skis while being pulled by a speedboat
sport of skiing on water while towed by a boat

Examples

She learned water-skiing during her summer vacation at the lake.
Water-skiing requires balance and strong leg muscles.
Many resorts offer lessons in water-skiing for beginners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waterski - a ski used for water-skiing

2. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ɚ ˌskiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌskiːɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of skiing on water while being pulled by a motorboat
sport of skiing on water while towed by a boat

Examples

He tried water-skiing for the first time during his summer vacation.
Many resorts offer water-skiing lessons for beginners.
Water-skiing requires a good sense of balance and strong leg muscles.
