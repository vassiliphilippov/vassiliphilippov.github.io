Vali

1. PROPN

/ ˈvɑːli / (AmE), / ˈvɑːli / (BrE)
In Norse mythology, one of the Aesir gods, son of Odin, and avenger of Balder.
Norse god, avenger of Balder

Examples

Vali was born to Odin specifically to avenge the death of Balder.
In many Norse legends, Vali is depicted as a young and powerful god.
The story of Vali highlights the theme of vengeance in Norse mythology.

Etymology

From Old Norse Váli, a figure in Norse mythology.
