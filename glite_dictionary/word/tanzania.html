Tanzania

1. PROPN

/ tænˈzeɪniə / (AmE), / tanˈzɑːnɪə / (BrE)
A country in eastern Africa, officially known as the United Republic of Tanzania.
country in eastern Africa

Examples

She is planning to travel to Tanzania for a wildlife safari next summer.
Tanzania is known for its rich cultural heritage and diverse landscapes.
The highest mountain in Africa, Kilimanjaro, is located in Tanzania.

Etymology

Formed in 1964 from the union of Tanganyika and Zanzibar; the name combines letters from both.
