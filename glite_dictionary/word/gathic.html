Gathic

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæθɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡæθɪk / (BrE)
an ancient Iranian language found in the most sacred hymns (the Gathas) of Zoroastrian scripture
ancient Iranian language of Zoroastrian sacred texts

Examples

Scholars have studied the Gathic language to better understand the origins of Zoroastrianism.
The Gathic hymns are considered the oldest part of the Avesta.
Few people today can read the original Gathic texts without specialized training.

Etymology

From the Gathas, sacred hymns of the Zoroastrian Avesta, and the suffix -ic.
