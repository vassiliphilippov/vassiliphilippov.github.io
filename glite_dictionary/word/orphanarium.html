orphanarium

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔːrfəˈnɛriəm / (AmE), / ˌɔːfəˈneəriəm / (BrE)
an institution where orphans are cared for and educated
institution for caring for orphans

Examples

The local government decided to fund the orphanarium after visiting its facilities.
Many children found new families while living at the orphanarium.
Volunteers spent every Saturday helping out at the orphanarium.

Synset Examples

The orphanarium was known for its loving environment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orphanage - an institution for orphans

Etymology

From 'orphan' and the Latin suffix '-arium' (indicating a place).
