battle-axe

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæt̬l ˌæks / (AmE), / ˈbætl ˌæks / (BrE)
an aggressive, domineering woman, especially a sharp-tongued wife or older woman
aggressive, domineering woman
informal slang

Examples

Everyone was afraid of their new boss, who was such a battle-axe in the office.
He described his mother-in-law as a real battle-axe, always telling people what to do.
Despite her reputation as a battle-axe, she really cared for her family.

Related Terms
