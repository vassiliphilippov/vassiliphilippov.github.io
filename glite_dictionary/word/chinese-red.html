Chinese-red

1. ADJ

/ ˌtʃaɪˈniːz ˈrɛd / (AmE), / ˌtʃaɪˈniːz ˈrɛd / (BrE)
Of a vivid red to reddish-orange color, typically associated with Chinese art and lacquerwork.
vivid red or reddish-orange color

Examples

The antique vase was painted in a brilliant Chinese-red lacquer.
She chose Chinese-red wallpaper for a bold accent wall in her living room.
Artists often use Chinese-red pigments to replicate traditional Asian designs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vermilion - a bright red to reddish-orange color

Etymology

Refers to the color traditionally used in Chinese lacquerware and art.
