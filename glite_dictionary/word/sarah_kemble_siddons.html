Sarah Kemble Siddons

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɛrə ˈkɛmbəl ˈsɪdənz / (AmE), / ˈseərə ˈkɛmbəl ˈsɪdənz / (BrE)
(1755–1831) An English actress famous for her performances in leading Shakespearean roles, particularly noted for her portrayal of Lady Macbeth.
famous 18th-century English Shakespearean actress

Examples

Many critics consider Sarah Kemble Siddons to be the greatest tragic actress of her era.
