drum

1. NOUN

/ drʌm / (AmE), / drʌm / (BrE)
a large cylindrical container, usually made of metal, used for storing or transporting liquids or powders
large cylindrical storage container

Examples

The oil was stored in a massive drum behind the factory.
They delivered several drums of chemicals to the warehouse.
After the rainstorm, the empty drum collected water near the construction site.
