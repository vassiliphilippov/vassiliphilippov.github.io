Gesneria

1. NOUN

/ ɡɛzˈnɪəriə / (AmE), / ɡɛzˈnɪəriə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical American herbs with showy tubular flowers, belonging to the family Gesneriaceae.
genus of tropical American herbs

Examples

Many collectors specialize in cultivating rare Gesneria species for their vibrant blossoms.
The Gesneria genus is native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.
Researchers discovered a new variety of Gesneria growing in the mountains of Puerto Rico.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gesneriaceae - the botanical family containing the genus Gesneria

Etymology

Named after Swiss naturalist Conrad Gessner (1516–1565).
