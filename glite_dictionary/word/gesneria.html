gesneria

1. NOUN

/ ɡɛzˈnɪəriə / (AmE), / ɡɛzˈnɪəriə / (BrE)
Any plant of the genus Gesneria, a group of tropical flowering plants in the family Gesneriaceae.
a genus of tropical flowering plants

Examples

The vibrant flowers of the gesneria add a splash of color to tropical gardens.
Many collectors prize rare species of gesneria for their unique blossoms.
She studied the growth habits of gesnerias in their native Caribbean habitat.

Etymology

Named after Swiss botanist Conrad Gessner (1516–1565).
