Princess Grace of Monaco

1. PROPN

/ ˈprɪnsɪs ɡreɪs əv məˈnɑːkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈprɪnsɪs ɡreɪs əv məˈnækəʊ / (BrE)
(1929–1982) The title of Grace Kelly, an American film actress who became the Princess of Monaco after marrying Prince Rainier III.
the title of Grace Kelly, American actress and Princess of Monaco

Many people still admire Princess Grace of Monaco for her elegance and charitable work.

Princess Grace of Monaco transitioned from Hollywood stardom to a royal role with dignity and grace.
