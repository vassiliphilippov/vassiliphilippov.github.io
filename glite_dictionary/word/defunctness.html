defunctness

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfʌŋkt.nəs / (AmE), / dɪˈfʌŋkt.nəs / (BrE)
The state or condition of no longer existing or being in use.
state of no longer existing or functioning

Examples

The defunctness of the old railway station made it a haunting sight in the town.
With the rise of digital streaming, the defunctness of video rental stores has become obvious.
The committee discussed the defunctness of certain outdated policies that no longer served their purpose.

Synset Examples

the extinction of a species

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extinction - the state of being extinct
  • Synonym: obsolescence - the process of becoming obsolete or outdated
  • Antonym: existence - the state of existing, being

Etymology

Derived from 'defunct' (from Latin 'defunctus', meaning 'dead, finished') with the suffix '-ness' denoting state or quality.
