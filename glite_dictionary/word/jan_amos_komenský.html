Jan Amos Komenský

1. PROPN

/ ˈjɑːn ˈɑːməs koʊˈmɛnski / (AmE), / ˈjan ˈamɔs kɒˈmɛnskiː / (BrE)
(1592–1670) Czech educational reformer and philosopher, renowned for his innovative ideas in modern education and pedagogy.
Czech educational reformer and philosopher

Examples

Jan Amos Komenský is often called the father of modern education for his pioneering theories.
In his writings, Jan Amos Komenský advocated universal education and lifelong learning.
Many schools and institutions across Europe bear the name of Jan Amos Komenský in his honor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Comenius - Latinized name of Jan Amos Komenský, the same historical figure

Etymology

Czech form of his name; known in Latin as Comenius.
