unbefitting

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnbɪˈfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnbɪˈfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
not suitable or appropriate for a particular person, situation, or purpose; inappropriate
not suitable; inappropriate

Examples

His rude remarks were unbefitting a guest at the ceremony.
It would be unbefitting of a leader to act selfishly in times of crisis.
The committee agreed that such behavior was unbefitting for a professional environment.

Synset Examples

behavior unbefitting a father

Related Terms

  • Antonym: befitting - appropriate or suitable for a particular person, situation, or purpose

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'befitting' (suitable, appropriate).
