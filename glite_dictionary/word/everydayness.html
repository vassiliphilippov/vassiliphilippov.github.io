everydayness

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛvriˈdeɪnəs / (AmE), / ˌɛvrɪˈdeɪnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being ordinary or commonplace through frequent occurrence.
the state of being ordinary or commonplace

Examples

The everydayness of their routine made their holiday feel even more special.
She found inspiration in the everydayness of city life.
Many poets write about the everydayness of human experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ordinariness - the quality of being ordinary
  • Synonym: commonness - the state of being common
