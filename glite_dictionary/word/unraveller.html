unraveller

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈrævələr / (AmE), / ʌnˈravələ / (BrE)
a person who untangles or removes something from a tangled or confused state
person who untangles things

Examples

She became the chief unraveller of the family conflicts, always smoothing things over.
The unraveller worked patiently on the mass of knotted necklaces until each one was free.
He prided himself on being an excellent unraveller of mysteries, both big and small.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to unravel - to undo tangled or confused threads or situations

Etymology

From 'unravel' + '-er', denoting a person who unravels.
