ambitiousness

1. NOUN

/ æmˈbɪʃəsnəs / (AmE), / æmˈbɪʃəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having a strong desire to achieve success or reach goals
quality of being ambitious

Examples

Her ambitiousness impressed her colleagues and quickly led to a promotion.
Many entrepreneurs attribute their success to sheer ambitiousness and hard work.
While his ambitiousness was admirable, it sometimes made him impatient with slower progress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ambition - a strong desire to achieve something
  • Antonym: unambitiousness - lack of ambition

Etymology

Formed from 'ambitious' (from Latin 'ambitiosus') + suffix '-ness'.
