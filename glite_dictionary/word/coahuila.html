Coahuila

1. PROPN

/ ko.ɑːˈwi.lə / (AmE), / kəʊ.əˈwiː.lə / (BrE)
a state in northern Mexico, the third-largest in the country, consisting mostly of high plateau terrain
Mexican state; high plateau region

Examples

Coahuila is known for its vast deserts and stunning mountain ranges.
The capital city of Coahuila is Saltillo, a major industrial center.
Many dinosaur fossils have been discovered in the region of Coahuila.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Coahuila', possibly of indigenous origin.
