warmly

1. ADV

/ ˈwɔrmli / (AmE), / ˈwɔːmli / (BrE)
In a warm manner; with warmth or heat.
in a warm manner

Examples

The children were warmly dressed for the snowy day.
She welcomed us warmly into her home.
The house was warmly lit and felt very inviting.

Synset Examples

warmly dressed
warm-clad skiers

Related Terms

  • Antonym: coldly - in a cold manner; without warmth or emotion

2. ADV

/ ˈwɔrmli / (AmE), / ˈwɔːmli / (BrE)
In a hearty, affectionate, or enthusiastic manner.
in a hearty or affectionate way

Examples

She smiled warmly at everyone who entered the room.
He was warmly welcomed by his old friends after years abroad.
The host thanked the guests warmly for attending the event.

Synset Examples

‘Yes,’ the children chorused heartily
We welcomed her warmly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cordially - in a warm and friendly way
  • Antonym: coldly - in an unfriendly or unemotional manner

Etymology

From "warm" (Middle English, from Old English *wearm*) + -ly.
