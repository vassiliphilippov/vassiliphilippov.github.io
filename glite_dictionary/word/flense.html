to flense

1. VERB

/ flɛns / (AmE), / flɛns / (BrE)
to strip the blubber or skin from a whale or seal
strip blubber or skin from whale or seal

Examples

After the whale was brought ashore, the crew began to flense it with long knives.
Specialized tools are required to flense a seal efficiently.
He has flensed dozens of whales during his long career as a whaler.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skin - to remove the skin from an animal or object

Etymology

From Dutch 'vlensen' (to strip the blubber from a whale).
