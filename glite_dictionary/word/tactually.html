tactually

1. ADV

/ ˈtæk.tʃu.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈtak.tʃu.ə.li / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or by means of the sense of touch
by touch; using the sense of touch

Examples

The surface texture is best examined tactually rather than visually.
Blind individuals often learn to read Braille tactually.
The artifact was identified tactually by running fingers along its grooves.

Synset Examples

he perceives shapes tactually

Related Terms

  • Antonym: visually - by means of sight
  • Synonym: tactilely - in a tactile manner; by means of touch

Etymology

From "tactual" (from Latin tactus, 'touch') + the adverbial suffix -ly.
