woodcarver

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdˌkɑrvər / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌkɑːvə / (BrE)
a person who makes decorative objects or panels out of wood by carving
person who carves decorative wood

Examples

The skilled woodcarver produced intricate designs for the old church doors.
Each woodcarver in the village uses unique techniques passed down through generations.
Visitors admired the work of a celebrated woodcarver at the local art festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sculptor - an artist who makes sculptures
  • Synonym: woodworker - a person who works with wood to make things
