unclothed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkloʊðd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkləʊðd / (BrE)
not wearing any clothing; naked or bare.
not wearing any clothing

Examples

He found the lost child unclothed and shivering in the woods.
The statue depicted a completely unclothed figure, which surprised many visitors.
After falling into the river, he was left unclothed and embarrassed on the shore.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naked - having no clothes on
  • Antonym: clothed - wearing clothing

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' to 'clothed'.
