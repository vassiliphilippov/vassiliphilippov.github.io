wiener

1. NOUN

/ ˈwiːnər / (AmE), / ˈviːnə / (BrE)
A smooth-textured sausage made of minced beef or pork, usually smoked and often served in a bread roll.
type of sausage; frankfurter

Examples

She ordered a wiener with mustard at the baseball game.
The butcher sells a variety of wieners made fresh every morning.
He put ketchup and relish on his wiener before eating it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frankfurter - a type of smoked sausage similar to a wiener
  • Synonym: hot dog - a cooked sausage served in a sliced bun

Etymology

From German 'Wiener', meaning 'of Vienna', referring to Vienna, Austria, where this style of sausage originated.
