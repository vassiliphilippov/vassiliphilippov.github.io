presswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛsˌwʊm.ən / (AmE), / ˈprɛsˌwʊm.ən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is printing, especially in a print shop or newspaper office
female printer (printing profession)

Examples

She was the first presswoman hired by the leading newspaper in her city.
The presswoman operated the machinery with remarkable skill and precision.
A veteran presswoman shared her experiences at the national printers' conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pressman - a person whose job is printing

Etymology

Formed from 'press' (printing press) + 'woman', in parallel to 'pressman'.
