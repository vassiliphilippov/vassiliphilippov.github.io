steroidal

1. ADJ

/ stəˈrɔɪdəl / (AmE), / stəˈrɔɪdəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of steroids, especially steroid hormones or their effects.
relating to steroids or their effects
medical

Examples

Some medications have steroidal components that affect hormone levels in the body.
The doctor prescribed a steroidal cream to reduce the inflammation.
Athletes are often tested for steroidal substances before major competitions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steroid - a large class of organic compounds with a characteristic molecular structure, many of which are hormones

Etymology

Formed from 'steroid' + '-al', denoting relating to steroids.
