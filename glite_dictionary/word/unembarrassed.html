unembarrassed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnəmˈbɛrəst / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪmˈbærəst / (BrE)
not feeling or showing embarrassment; calm and confident in situations where others might feel awkward or self-conscious.
not feeling embarrassment

Examples

Despite the awkward silence, she remained unembarrassed and quickly changed the subject.
He gave an unembarrassed explanation of his mistake, refusing to blush or stammer.
The children danced with unembarrassed joy at the end-of-year party.

Synset Examples

a tinseled charm and unabashed sentimentality
an unembarrassed greeting as if nothing untoward had happened

Related Terms

  • Antonym: embarrassed - feeling self-conscious or awkward
  • Synonym: unabashed - not embarrassed, disconcerted, or ashamed

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'embarrassed', from French 'embarrasser'.
