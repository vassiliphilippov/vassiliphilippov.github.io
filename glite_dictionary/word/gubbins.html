gubbins

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌbɪnz / (AmE), / ˈɡʌbɪnz / (BrE)
An object or collection of objects whose names are unknown, forgotten, or regarded as unimportant; miscellaneous small items.
unspecified item(s); miscellaneous bits
informal british

Examples

Can you hand me that gubbins over there on the shelf?
I've got a drawer full of wires, tools, and other gubbins in the garage.
He spent the afternoon fixing the machine with a handful of odd gubbins.

Synset Examples

she eased the ball-shaped doodad back into its socket
there may be some great new gizmo around the corner that you will want to use

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gizmo - a small mechanical or electronic device whose name is unknown or forgotten
  • Synonym: thingamajig - an object whose name is unknown or temporarily forgotten

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from English dialect meaning 'odds and ends'.
