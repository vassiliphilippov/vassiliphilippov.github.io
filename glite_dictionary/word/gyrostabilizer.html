gyrostabilizer

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒaɪ.roʊˈsteɪ.bɪ.laɪ.zɚ / (AmE), / ˌdʒaɪ.rəʊˈsteɪ.bɪ.laɪ.zə / (BrE)
a device using a spinning gyroscope to reduce unwanted rolling motions in a ship, plane, or similar vehicle
gyro device reducing roll in vehicles

Examples

The ship was equipped with a gyrostabilizer to ensure a smoother voyage even in rough seas.
Recent aircraft designs incorporate a gyrostabilizer to improve stability during turbulence.
The gyrostabilizer automatically adjusts to counteract rolling and keep the vessel level.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gyroscope - a spinning wheel used to maintain orientation
  • Synonym: stabilizer - a device for making something steady

Etymology

Formed from 'gyro-' (from Greek 'gyros', circle) and 'stabilizer'.
