usualness

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuːʒuəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈjuːʒuəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being usual; the condition of being ordinary or customary
the quality of being usual

Examples

The usualness of the routine made the days blend together for her.
Despite the excitement around him, he maintained a sense of usualness in his work.
There was a comforting usualness about the family's Sunday dinners.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unusualness - the quality of being uncommon or not usual
  • Synonym: ordinariness - the quality of being ordinary

Etymology

From 'usual' + '-ness', modeled on similar noun constructions in English.
