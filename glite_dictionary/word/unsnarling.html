unsnarling

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈsnɑrlɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsnɑːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of untangling or releasing something from a tangled or snarled condition
act of untangling or untwisting

Examples

The unsnarling of the fishing nets took the crew several hours.
She focused her attention on the unsnarling of the knotted yarn.
After much patient unsnarling, the gold chain finally hung straight again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: untangling - the act of undoing a tangle
  • Antonym: snarling - the act of tangling or becoming tangled

Etymology

From un- (reverse) + snarl (tangle).
