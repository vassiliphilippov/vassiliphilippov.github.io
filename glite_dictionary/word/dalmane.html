Dalmane

1. NOUN

/ ˈdælmeɪn / (AmE), / ˈdælmeɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked name for a tranquilizer used to treat insomnia.
trade name for insomnia medication

Examples

My doctor prescribed Dalmane to help with my chronic insomnia.
The sedative effects of Dalmane make it useful for short-term sleep problems.
Patients should not take Dalmane without following their physician's instructions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flurazepam - the generic drug known as Dalmane
