Susa

1. PROPN

/ ˈsuːsə / (AmE), / ˈsuːsə / (BrE)
a port city in eastern Tunisia on the Mediterranean Sea, known for its historic sites and as a tourist destination
port city in eastern Tunisia

Examples

Susa is famous for its beautiful beaches and Roman ruins.
The old medina of Susa is a UNESCO World Heritage site.
Many cruise ships stop at Susa during tours of the Mediterranean.
