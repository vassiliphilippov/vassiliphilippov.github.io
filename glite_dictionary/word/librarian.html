librarian

1. NOUN

/ laɪˈbrɛriən / (AmE), / laɪˈbrɛəriən / (BrE)
a person who is trained in library science and works in a library, managing its resources and assisting users
person managing a library

Examples

The librarian helped me find the history book I needed for my research project.
After earning her degree, she became the head librarian at the city library.
Many children look forward to story time led by the librarian every Friday afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: library assistant - a person who assists in library duties, often without professional qualifications

Etymology

From Latin 'librarius' (of books), from 'liber' (book).
