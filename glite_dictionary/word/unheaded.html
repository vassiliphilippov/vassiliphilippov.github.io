unheaded

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛdɪd / (BrE)
not having a heading or caption; lacking a title at the beginning
lacking a heading or title

Examples

The report included several unheaded tables, making it hard to understand the data.
She submitted an unheaded essay, which caused confusion about its author.
In textbooks, unheaded sections are often overlooked by students.

Synset Examples

unheaded sections

Related Terms

  • Synonym: untitled - not having a title
  • Antonym: headed - having a heading or title
