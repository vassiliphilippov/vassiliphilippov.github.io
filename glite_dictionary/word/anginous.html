anginous

1. ADJ

/ ænˈdʒaɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ænˈdʒɪn.əs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the pain or symptoms of angina pectoris
relating to angina pectoris symptoms
medical

Examples

The patient complained of anginous pain radiating across his chest.
Doctors noted several anginous episodes during the course of the stress test.
She was immediately treated after experiencing anginous discomfort while exercising.

Etymology

From Latin 'angina' (quinsy, strangling, sore throat) + English '-ous'.
