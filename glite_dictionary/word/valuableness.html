valuableness

1. NOUN

/ ˈvæljuəblnɪs / (AmE), / ˈvaljuːəblnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being valuable, precious, or of great worth.
quality of being valuable or precious

Examples

The valuableness of old family heirlooms is often sentimental as well as financial.
During the appraisal, the antiques' valuableness became clear to everyone present.
She spoke about the valuableness of integrity in both personal and professional life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: value - the importance, worth, or usefulness of something
  • Synonym: worth - the value or importance of something
  • Antonym: valuelessness - the lack of value or importance

Etymology

From 'valuable' + '-ness', forming a noun denoting the state or quality.
