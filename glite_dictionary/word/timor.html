Timor

1. PROPN

/ ˈtiːmɔr / (AmE), / ˈtiːmɔː / (BrE)
an island in southeastern Asia, lying in the Malay Archipelago; the largest and easternmost of the Lesser Sunda Islands, divided between Timor-Leste (East Timor) and Indonesia.
island in Indonesia/Malay Archipelago

Examples

The island of Timor is divided between Indonesia and the independent nation of Timor-Leste.
After visiting Bali, she continued her travels eastward to Timor.
Many unique animal species can be found on Timor due to its isolation.

Etymology

From Malay 'Timor' meaning 'east', as it is the easternmost of the Lesser Sunda Islands.
