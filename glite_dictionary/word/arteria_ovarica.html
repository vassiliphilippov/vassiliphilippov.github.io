arteria ovarica

1. NOUN

/ ɑːrˈtɪəriə oʊˈvɛrɪkə / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə əʊˈvɑːrɪkə / (BrE)
the artery that branches from the aorta and supplies blood to the ovaries
artery supplying blood to the ovaries
medical

Examples

The arteria ovarica is crucial for providing oxygenated blood to the ovaries.
During surgery, care must be taken not to damage the arteria ovarica.
Blockage of the arteria ovarica can lead to ovarian ischemia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ovarian artery - artery supplying blood to the ovaries

Etymology

From Latin: 'arteria' (artery) and 'ovarica' (of the ovary).
