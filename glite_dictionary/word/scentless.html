scentless

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛntləs / (AmE), / ˈsɛntləs / (BrE)
lacking the sense of smell; unable to perceive odors.
lacking the sense of smell

Examples

After the accident, she became completely scentless and could no longer detect any odors.
The scentless patient found it difficult to notice warning smells like smoke or gas leaks.
Being scentless made him oblivious to the aroma of fresh flowers in the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anosmic - lacking the sense of smell

Etymology

From 'scent' + suffix '-less' meaning 'without'.

2. ADJ

/ ˈsɛntləs / (AmE), / ˈsɛntləs / (BrE)
having no smell; emitting or holding no odor.
having no smell

Examples

The laboratory was filled with scentless gases used for experiments.
Some flowers are beautiful but completely scentless to the human nose.
He searched the forest for edible mushrooms, preferring the scentless varieties.

Synset Examples

scentless wisps of straw
a scentless stretch of rocky ground

Related Terms

  • Synonym: odorless - having no odor or smell
  • Antonym: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell

Etymology

from 'scent' + '-less' (meaning 'without')

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01061728-s
Synset POS: s
