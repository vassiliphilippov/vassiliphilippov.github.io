prognathic

1. ADJ

/ prəgˈnæθɪk / (AmE), / prɒgˈnæθɪk / (BrE)
(of the jaw or face) having a projecting lower jaw or jaws that protrude forward.
having a projecting lower jaw
medical

Examples

The fossil skull was noted to be distinctly prognathic, with the lower jaw jutting forward.
A prognathic profile is commonly seen in certain genetic conditions.
Her prognathic jaw was corrected with reconstructive surgery.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: retrognathic - having a receding jaw

Etymology

From Greek pro- (forward) + gnathos (jaw).
