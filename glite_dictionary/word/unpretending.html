unpretending

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.prɪˈtɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prɪˈtɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
not showy, ostentatious, or trying to attract attention; modest and simple
modest, not showy

Examples

Despite her wealth, she lived in an unpretending cottage by the sea.
The restaurant has an unpretending charm that makes everyone feel comfortable.
His unpretending attitude put the nervous guests at ease.

Synset Examples

his unostentatious office
unostentatious elegance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: modest - not showing excessive pride or boastfulness; humble
  • Antonym: ostentatious - characterized by pretentious or showy display; designed to impress

Etymology

From un- (not) + pretend (from Latin praetendere, to stretch forth, show) + -ing.
