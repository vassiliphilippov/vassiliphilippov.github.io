ER

1. NOUN

/ i ɑr / (AmE), / iː ɑː / (BrE)
a hospital room equipped and staffed to provide emergency medical care to patients requiring immediate attention
hospital emergency treatment room
medical american

Examples

She was rushed to the ER after the accident.
The ER was crowded on Saturday night with patients needing urgent care.
Doctors in the ER treated him for a broken arm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emergency room - a hospital room for emergency treatment

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'emergency room'.
