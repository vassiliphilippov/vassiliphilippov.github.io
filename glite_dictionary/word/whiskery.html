whiskery

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪs.kə.ri / (AmE), / ˈwɪs.kəri / (BrE)
having whiskers or hair on the cheeks and chin, especially resembling those of an animal or an unshaven man
having hair on the cheeks and chin

Examples

The old man had a whiskery face that made him look wise and gentle.
The cat rubbed its whiskery muzzle against her hand, purring loudly.
He gave a whiskery grin as he greeted his friends at the café.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bearded - having a beard; hair growing on the chin and cheeks

Etymology

From 'whisker' + '-y', indicating possession or presence of whiskers.
