Dresden

1. PROPN

/ ˈdrɛzdən / (AmE), / ˈdrɛzdən / (BrE)
a city in southeastern Germany on the Elbe River, noted for its cultural heritage and the extensive destruction it suffered in World War II.
city in southeastern Germany

Examples

The historic center of Dresden has been beautifully restored since World War II.
She visited several museums in Dresden during her trip to Germany.
The bombing of Dresden in 1945 remains a controversial event in history.
