grimly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡrɪmli / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪmli / (BrE)
in a stern, forbidding, or relentless manner; with grim determination or severity
in a stern or forbidding manner

Examples

She nodded grimly as she prepared to face the challenge ahead.
He grimly accepted the bad news, showing no emotion.
They marched grimly through the storm, determined to reach safety.

Synset Examples

he was grimly satisfied

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sternly - in a firm or strict manner
  • Antonym: cheerfully - in a happy and positive manner

Etymology

From 'grim' + '-ly', where 'grim' comes from Old English 'grim' (fierce, cruel).
