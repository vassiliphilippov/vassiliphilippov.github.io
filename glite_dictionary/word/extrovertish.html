extrovertish

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛkstroʊˈvɜrtɪʃ / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəʊˈvɜːtɪʃ / (BrE)
Having some characteristics of an extrovert; somewhat outgoing or sociable but not fully extroverted.
somewhat extroverted
informal

Examples

Although she's usually reserved, she can be quite extrovertish at parties.
His extrovertish nature makes it easy for him to meet new people, but he also enjoys quiet evenings alone.
I'm not fully outgoing, but people sometimes describe me as extrovertish.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: extroverted - outgoing and socially confident
  • Antonym: introvertish - somewhat introverted

Etymology

From 'extrovert' + '-ish', a suffix meaning 'having the qualities of'.
