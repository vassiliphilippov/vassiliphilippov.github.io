Velban

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛlˌbæn / (AmE), / ˈvɛlˌbæn / (BrE)
A trade name for vinblastine, a drug derived from the periwinkle plant and used in chemotherapy to disrupt cell division.
trade name for vinblastine (chemotherapy drug)
medical

Examples

The oncologist prescribed Velban as part of her treatment regimen.
Patients receiving Velban may experience side effects such as hair loss and nausea.
Velban is administered intravenously under medical supervision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vinblastine - an alkaloid drug used in chemotherapy
