Inderal

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndəˌræl / (AmE), / ˈɪndəræl / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for propranolol, the first beta blocker used to treat hypertension, angina pectoris, and essential tremor.
brand name for propranolol (beta blocker drug)
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Inderal to help manage my high blood pressure.
Some performers use Inderal to control symptoms of stage fright or anxiety.
After starting Inderal, his episodes of essential tremor became less frequent.

Related Terms
