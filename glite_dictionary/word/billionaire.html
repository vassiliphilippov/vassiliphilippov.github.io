billionaire

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɪljəˈnɛr / (AmE), / ˌbɪljəˈneə / (BrE)
A person who has assets worth at least one billion dollars or other currency units.
person with wealth of at least one billion

Examples

The technology billionaire donated millions to charity last year.
Few people ever become a billionaire through hard work alone.
He was named the youngest billionaire in the country after his recent business success.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: millionaire - person with wealth of at least one million

Etymology

From 'billion' + '-aire', patterned after 'millionaire'.
