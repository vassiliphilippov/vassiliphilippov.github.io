inescapable

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnɪˈskeɪpəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnɪˈskeɪpəbl / (BrE)
impossible to avoid or escape; certain to happen or be experienced.
impossible to avoid or escape

Examples

The team's defeat seemed inescapable after the early goal by their opponents.
There is an inescapable truth that we must all face at some point in our lives.
The film creates an inescapable sense of tension from beginning to end.

Synset Examples

inescapable conclusion
an ineluctable destiny
an unavoidable accident

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unavoidable - impossible to prevent or evade
  • Synonym: inevitable - certain to happen; unavoidable

Etymology

From in- (not) + escapable, based on escape from Old French escaper.
