unitard

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuːnɪˌtɑrd / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪˌtɑːd / (BrE)
a tight-fitting, stretchy one-piece garment covering the body from the shoulders to the thighs or ankles, sometimes with long sleeves; commonly worn by dancers and acrobats
one-piece, tight-fitting garment for dancers

Examples

The ballet company ordered custom unitards for their modern dance performance.
She wore a sparkly unitard during her gymnastics routine at the competition.
Many acrobats prefer wearing a unitard for both comfort and flexibility on stage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leotard - a close-fitting one-piece garment covering the torso but not the legs

Etymology

Blend of 'uni-' (meaning one) and 'leotard'.
