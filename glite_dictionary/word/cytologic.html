cytologic

1. ADJ

/ saɪtəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / saɪtəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study of cells and their structure, function, and chemistry
relating to cytology (study of cells)
medical

Examples

The cytologic findings pointed to a benign condition.
Doctors recommended a cytologic examination of the fluid sample.
A cytologic analysis of the tissue was performed in the laboratory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cytological - alternative adjective form meaning relating to cytology
  • Synonym: histologic - relating to the study of tissues (histology)

Etymology

From 'cytology' (the study of cells), which is from Greek 'kytos' (container, cell) + '-logy'.
