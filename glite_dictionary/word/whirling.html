whirling

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of spinning quickly in a circle or spiral motion
spinning motion

Examples

The whirling of the dancers mesmerized the audience.
She grew dizzy from the constant whirling of the carousel.
Leaves caught in the wind began their chaotic whirling along the path.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spinning - the act of turning around rapidly
