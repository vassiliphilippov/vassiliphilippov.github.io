Lutra lutra

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːtrə ˈluːtrə / (AmE), / ˈluːtrə ˈluːtrə / (BrE)
a species of otter native to Europe and Asia, commonly known as the European otter.
European otter species

Examples

The Lutra lutra population has been steadily recovering across many European rivers.
Conservation efforts have helped protect habitats crucial for Lutra lutra.
Researchers have tracked the movements of Lutra lutra in both rural and urban waterways.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: otter - semi-aquatic mammal with webbed feet and a long tail

Etymology

From Latin; genus name *Lutra* meaning 'otter'.
