Gorilla gorilla

1. NOUN

/ gəˈrɪlə gəˈrɪlə / (AmE), / gəˈrɪlə gəˈrɪlə / (BrE)
the scientific name for the western gorilla, the largest anthropoid ape, found in the forests of central west Africa
scientific name for the western gorilla

Examples

The endangered Gorilla gorilla inhabits the lowland forests of western Africa.
Researchers are studying Gorilla gorilla to better understand primate behavior.
Wildlife documentaries often feature Gorilla gorilla in their natural habitat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gorilla - a large great ape native to Africa

Etymology

From the genus name 'Gorilla', based on Greek 'Gorillai' (a tribe of hairy women) and the practice of doubling the species name in binomial nomenclature.
