making

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or act of producing, creating, or causing something to exist or happen
process of producing or creating something

Examples

Her passion for pottery began with the making of simple clay cups.
The project was years in the making before it was finally completed.
He documented the making of the documentary in a behind-the-scenes film.

Synset Examples

the devising of plans
the fashioning of pots and pans
the making of measurements
it was already in the making

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creation - the act of bringing something into existence
  • Synonym: production - the act of making or manufacturing something

Etymology

From Old English *macian* (to make) + -ing.
