to pee-pee

1. VERB

/ ˈpiˌpi / (AmE), / ˈpiːˌpiː / (BrE)
to urinate, especially used by or when speaking to children; to pass urine
urinate (informal, esp. for children)
informal

Examples

My son said he needed to pee-pee before we left the house.
The kitten pee-peed on the carpet again last night.
She asked the babysitter if she could pee-pee before nap time.

Synset Examples

Again, the cat had made on the expensive rug

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to urinate - to discharge urine from the body; to pee
  • Synonym: piss - to urinate (vulgar)

Etymology

Reduplicative form of 'pee', itself a childish or informal shortening of 'piss'.
