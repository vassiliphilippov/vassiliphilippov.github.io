necrophilia

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɛk.rəˈfɪl.i.ə / (AmE), / ˌnɛk.rəˈfɪl.ɪ.ə / (BrE)
a sexual attraction or sexual act involving corpses
sexual attraction to corpses
medical

Examples

The criminal was charged with necrophilia after the police investigation.
Psychiatrists study necrophilia to understand rare and abnormal sexual behaviors.
Laws regarding necrophilia vary considerably between different countries.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: paraphilia - abnormal sexual desires, typically involving extreme or dangerous activities

Etymology

From Greek nekros (corpse) + -philia (fondness for).
