Aarhus

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑr.huːs / (AmE), / ˈɑː.huːs / (BrE)
a port city of Denmark in eastern Jutland, known for its vibrant cultural scene and as a commercial center
port city in Denmark

Examples

She spent a semester studying in Aarhus, exploring its historic architecture and modern art museums.
The conference is being held in Aarhus this year, attracting visitors from around the world.
After docking at the port in Aarhus, the tourists took a guided tour of the city.

Etymology

From Danish 'Aarhus', from Old Danish 'Árós', meaning 'river mouth'.
