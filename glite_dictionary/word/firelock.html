firelock

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪərlɑk / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəlɒk / (BrE)
a historical firearm; a muzzle-loading gun fitted with a flintlock mechanism
historical firearm with flintlock

Examples

The soldier carefully loaded his firelock before marching into battle.
During the demonstration, the old firelock was fired, astonishing the crowd with its loud report.
Collectors often seek out rare examples of the 17th-century firelock for their historical value.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flintlock - a firearm with a flint striking ignition mechanism
