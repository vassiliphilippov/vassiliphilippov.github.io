flexibly

1. ADV

/ ˈflɛksəbli / (AmE), / ˈflɛksəbli / (BrE)
In a way that shows flexibility; able to change or adapt easily to different conditions or situations.
in a flexible manner

Examples

The company allows employees to work flexibly from home or in the office.
She always handles unexpected changes flexibly and calmly.
The project timeline can be adjusted flexibly if necessary.

Synset Examples

‘Come whenever you are free,’ he said flexibly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rigidly - in a rigid or inflexible manner
  • Synonym: flexible - able to bend or change easily

Etymology

From "flexible" (from Latin flexibilis), plus adverbial suffix -ly.
