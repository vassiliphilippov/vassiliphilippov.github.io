misreading

1. NOUN

/ mɪsˈriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / mɪsˈriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
an incorrect or mistaken interpretation of written or spoken words; a misinterpretation resulting from reading inaccurately
incorrect interpretation from reading inaccurately

Examples

Her misreading of the instructions caused the experiment to fail.
There was a serious misreading of the politician’s intentions in the media.
A misreading of the data led to an incorrect conclusion in the report.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: misinterpretation - the act of interpreting something wrongly
  • Antonym: reading - the act of interpreting written or printed matter

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'mis-' (meaning 'wrongly') + 'reading' (from 'read').
