Nefud

1. PROPN

/ nɛˈfuːd / (AmE), / nɛˈfuːd / (BrE)
a desert region in northern Saudi Arabia, known for its red sand and violent winds
desert in northern Saudi Arabia

Examples

The Nefud is famous for its striking red sand dunes stretching across the horizon.
Archaeologists have studied ancient tools discovered in the Nefud desert.
Strong winds in the Nefud can create dangerous sandstorms.
