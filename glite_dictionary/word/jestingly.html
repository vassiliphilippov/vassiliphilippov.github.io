jestingly

1. ADV

/ ˈdʒɛstɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛstɪŋli / (BrE)
in a joking or playful manner; not meant to be taken seriously
in a joking manner

Examples

She jestingly suggested we all quit our jobs and move to the beach.
He jestingly claimed he could eat a hundred hot dogs in one sitting.
Anna jestingly challenged her friend to a race up the stairs.

Synset Examples

I asked him jokingly whether he thought he could drive the Calcutta-Peshawar express

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jokingly - in a humorous or playful manner
  • Antonym: seriously - in a solemn or earnest manner

Etymology

From 'jest' (Middle English, from Old French *geste*, from Latin *gesta* meaning deeds or exploits) + -ly.
