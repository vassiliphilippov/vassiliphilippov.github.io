corned

1. ADJ

/ kɔrnd / (AmE), / kɔːnd / (BrE)
(of meat) preserved or cured in brine, often with added salt and spices.
meat cured in brine

Examples

We ordered a corned beef sandwich from the deli.
The corned brisket needs to simmer for several hours to become tender.
She prefers corned meat over roasted for its unique flavor.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fresh - not preserved or cured; opposite of 'corned' in the context of meat

Etymology

From the verb 'corn' meaning 'to preserve or season with grains of salt.'
