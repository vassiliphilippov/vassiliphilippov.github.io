weightlifter

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪtˌlɪftər / (AmE), / ˈweɪtˌlɪftə / (BrE)
an athlete who competes in the sport of lifting heavy weights, especially barbells
athlete who lifts weights competitively

Examples

As a professional weightlifter, she trains six days a week to improve her technique and strength.
The weightlifter broke the national record with a clean and jerk of 200 kilograms.
Many aspiring athletes look up to the Olympic weightlifter for inspiration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bodybuilder - a person who strengthens and enlarges the muscles of their body by strenuous exercise
  • Synonym: powerlifter - an athlete who competes in the sport of powerlifting

Etymology

Formed from 'weight' and 'lifter', indicating a person who lifts weights.
