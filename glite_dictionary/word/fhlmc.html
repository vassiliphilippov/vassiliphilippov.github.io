FHLMC

1. NOUN

/ ɛf eɪtʃ ɛl ɛm si / (AmE), / ɛf eɪtʃ ɛl ɛm si / (BrE)
a US government-sponsored corporation that provides a secondary market for residential mortgages, commonly known as Freddie Mac
US mortgage financing corporation ('Freddie Mac')
american

Examples

The FHLMC plays a crucial role in stabilizing the US housing market.
Many lenders sell their loans to the FHLMC to free up funds for new mortgages.
Investors closely watch the securities issued by the FHLMC.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Freddie Mac - US government-backed mortgage finance company, nickname for FHLMC

Etymology

Acronym from Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corporation.
