Friulian

1. NOUN

/ friˈuliən / (AmE), / frɪˈuːlɪən / (BrE)
A Rhaeto-Romance language spoken in the Friuli region of northeastern Italy.
Rhaeto-Romance language of northeastern Italy

Examples

Many inhabitants of Udine still speak Friulian in their daily lives.
The Friulian language has its own distinct grammar and vocabulary.
She is studying Friulian to connect with her family’s heritage.

Etymology

From Italian 'friulano', from the region name 'Friuli'.
