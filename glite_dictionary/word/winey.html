winey

1. ADJ

/ ˈwaɪni / (AmE), / ˈwaɪni / (BrE)
having the taste or aroma characteristic of wine; reminiscent of wine in flavor or scent
having a taste or smell of wine

Examples

The sauce had a winey aroma that added depth to the dish.
I prefer chocolate with a slightly winey aftertaste.
The grapes produced a juice that was rich and winey.

Synset Examples

a rich winy taste

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vinous - having the qualities or characteristics of wine

Etymology

Formed from 'wine' + '-y' (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'characteristic of').
