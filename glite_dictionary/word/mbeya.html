Mbeya

1. PROPN

/ əmˈbeɪ.ə / (AmE), / əmˈbeɪ.ə / (BrE)
A city in southwestern Tanzania, known as a regional and economic center in the southern highlands.
city in southwestern Tanzania

Examples

She grew up in Mbeya before moving to Dar es Salaam for university.
The climate of Mbeya is cooler than many other Tanzanian cities, making it unique.
A new road has improved travel between Mbeya and the neighboring regions.
