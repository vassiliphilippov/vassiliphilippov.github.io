tableware

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəlˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəlˌweə / (BrE)
the dishes, cutlery, glassware, and other articles used at a dining table for serving and eating food
dishes and utensils used at a dining table

Examples

We keep our best tableware in a special cabinet for holiday dinners.
The restaurant uses elegant tableware to enhance the dining experience.
She bought new tableware for her apartment, including plates, glasses, and forks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cutlery - knives, forks, and spoons used for eating or serving food
  • Synonym: silverware - dishes, eating utensils, and similar articles made of or plated with silver

Etymology

Formed from 'table' + 'ware' in English, modeling after similar compounds.
