gazebo

1. NOUN

/ ɡəˈziːboʊ / (AmE), / ɡəˈziːbəʊ / (BrE)
a small, open-sided, roofed structure in a garden or park, designed to provide shade, shelter, and a place to rest
small roofed garden structure

Examples

They held the wedding ceremony under a beautifully decorated gazebo in the park.
We sat in the gazebo and enjoyed the view of the sunset.
The old gazebo in their backyard became a favorite spot for summer picnics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pavilion - a light, often ornamental building used for shelter or events in a park or garden
  • Synonym: pergola - a garden structure with a shaded walkway or sitting area with vertical posts and cross-beams

Etymology

Originally a humorous pseudo-Latin formation from 'gaze' + Latin suffix '-ebo' (as in 'videbo' meaning 'I shall see').
