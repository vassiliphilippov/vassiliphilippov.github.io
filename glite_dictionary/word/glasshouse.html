glasshouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlæsˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A building with glass walls and a roof, used for growing and displaying plants in controlled conditions.
building of glass for growing plants

Examples

The botanical garden built a new glasshouse to showcase rare tropical species.
Tom grows his tomatoes in a glasshouse to protect them from the cold.
During the school trip, the children explored the glasshouse filled with exotic plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greenhouse - A glass building for cultivating plants; commonly used synonym

Etymology

From 'glass' + 'house', referring to a house-like structure made primarily of glass.
