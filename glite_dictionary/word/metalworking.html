metalworking

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛt̬lˌwɝːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɛtlˌwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or craft of shaping and making things from metal, especially using specialized tools and skills
the craft of making things from metal

Examples

He has spent his entire career in metalworking, crafting intricate sculptures from steel.
The museum features an exhibit on traditional metalworking techniques used by ancient artisans.
Many students in the technical college take courses in metalworking to learn valuable manufacturing skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blacksmithing - the craft of forging objects from iron or steel

Etymology

From 'metal' + 'working', referring to the process of working with metals.
