gazoo

1. NOUN

/ ɡəˈzu / (AmE), / ɡəˈzuː / (BrE)
A whimsical or playful term for a car or automobile.
whimsical word for car
informal

Examples

After work, Jake hopped into his gazoo and sped off to the beach.
The old gazoo finally broke down after so many years of faithful service.
Whenever we pass by, the neighbors wave at us from their shiny new gazoo.

Synset Examples

We took the gazoo on a road trip last summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car - a road vehicle with four wheels, powered by an engine, that carries a small number of passengers
  • Synonym: automobile - a wheeled motor vehicle used for transportation
