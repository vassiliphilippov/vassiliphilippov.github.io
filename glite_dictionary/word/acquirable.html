acquirable

1. ADJ

/ əˈkwaɪrəbəl / (AmE), / əˈkwaɪərəbl / (BrE)
capable of being acquired or obtained.
able to be acquired

Examples

Many rare books are not easily acquirable through ordinary bookstores.
Knowledge about the local market is acquirable with enough research and time.
Some skills are acquirable only through hands-on experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obtainable - able to be obtained
  • Antonym: inacquirable - not able to be acquired

Etymology

From 'acquire' + '-able', denoting capability.
