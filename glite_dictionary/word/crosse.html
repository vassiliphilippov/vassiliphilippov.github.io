crosse

1. NOUN

/ krɑs / (AmE), / krɒs / (BrE)
a long stick with a triangular frame at one end, used in playing lacrosse to catch, carry, and throw the ball
lacrosse stick with triangular head

Examples

He held his crosse tightly as he ran down the field.
Players must use their crosse to carry and pass the ball.
She bought a new crosse before the tournament started.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lacrosse stick - the stick used in lacrosse, synonymous with 'crosse'

Etymology

From French 'crosse', meaning 'stick' or 'crozier'.
