zoology

1. NOUN

/ zoʊˈɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / zəʊˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of animals, including their biology, behavior, and classification
study of animals in biology

Examples

She decided to study zoology at university because of her passion for animals.
The museum’s new exhibit was curated by a professor of zoology.
Advances in zoology have helped scientists better understand endangered species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biology - the science of life and living organisms
  • Antonym: botany - the scientific study of plants

Etymology

From Greek zōion (animal) + -logia (study, science).
