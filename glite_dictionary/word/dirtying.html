dirtying

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɜrtiɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɜːtiɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making something dirty or soiled
act of making dirty

Examples

The children were scolded for dirtying their clothes during the picnic.
The dirtying of the carpets meant extra work for the cleaning staff.
He was accused of dirtying the public restroom walls with graffiti.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cleaning - the act of making something clean, the opposite process
