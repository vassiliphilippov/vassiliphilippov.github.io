braces

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪsɪz / (AmE), / ˈbreɪsɪz / (BrE)
a device consisting of wires and brackets fitted to the teeth, used to correct dental irregularities
orthodontic dental device

Examples

My dentist said I need braces to straighten my teeth.
She wore braces for two years during high school.
Getting braces can improve both the appearance and health of your smile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orthodontic appliance - device used in orthodontics to align teeth and jaws
