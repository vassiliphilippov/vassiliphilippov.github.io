hardfisted

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑrdˌfɪstɪd / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌfɪstɪd / (BrE)
unwilling to spend or give away money; stingy or miserly
unwilling to spend money

Examples

My grandfather was known to be quite hardfisted when it came to spending money.
Despite his wealth, the businessman remained hardfisted and rarely donated to charity.
Her hardfisted attitude made it difficult to raise funds for the community project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stingy - unwilling to give or spend; not generous
  • Antonym: generous - willing to give more of something, especially money, than is strictly necessary or expected

Etymology

Formed by combining 'hard' and 'fisted', referring metaphorically to holding tightly onto one's money.
