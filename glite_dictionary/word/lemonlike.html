lemonlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛmənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈlɛmənˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a taste, smell, or appearance similar to that of a lemon, especially being sour and citrusy.
resembling a lemon in taste, smell, or appearance

Examples

The salad dressing had a lemonlike tang that brightened up the greens.
She described the drink as refreshing and lemonlike in flavor.
A lemonlike scent filled the kitchen when he cut into the fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lemony - having the taste or aroma of lemon
