Tashmit

1. PROPN

/ tæʃˈmiːt / (AmE), / tæʃˈmiːt / (BrE)
A goddess in Mesopotamian mythology, known as the consort of Nabu.
Mesopotamian goddess, consort of Nabu

Examples

According to legend, Tashmit was revered as the wife of the god Nabu.
Ancient temples were dedicated to both Tashmit and her consort.
The role of Tashmit highlights the importance of female deities in Mesopotamian religion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nabu - Mesopotamian god of wisdom and writing
