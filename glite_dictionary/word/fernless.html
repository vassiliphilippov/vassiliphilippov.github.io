fernless

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɝːn.ləs / (AmE), / ˈfɜːn.ləs / (BrE)
Lacking or having no ferns.
without ferns

Examples

After the drought, the once lush garden became fernless and dry.
The botanist was disappointed to find the area almost completely fernless.
She preferred a fernless landscape because it made spotting wildflowers easier.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fern - a type of non-flowering plant with feathery leaves

Etymology

Formed from 'fern' + suffix '-less' (without).
