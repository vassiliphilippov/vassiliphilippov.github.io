plainie

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪni / (AmE), / ˈpleɪni / (BrE)
A simple, unpretentious way of living, dressing, or presenting oneself.
simple and unpretentious style or approach
informal

Examples

She admired her colleague's plainie attitude and down-to-earth demeanor.
He prefers a plainie look, avoiding flashy clothes and accessories.
Their home has a plainie feel with minimal decoration and simple furnishings.

Synset Examples
