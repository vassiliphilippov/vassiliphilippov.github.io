2. NOUN

/ məˈnɪs.i.əm / (AmE), / mɪˈnɪs.si.əm / (BrE)
A genus of terrestrial ferns native to the tropical regions of the Americas.
genus of tropical American ferns

Examples

The Meniscium genus contains several ferns valued for their ornamental fronds.
Researchers discovered a new species within the Meniscium genus in the Amazon rainforest.
Collectors often seek out rare Meniscium ferns for botanical gardens.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin, based on Greek 'meniskos' meaning 'crescent', referring to the shape of some fern parts.
