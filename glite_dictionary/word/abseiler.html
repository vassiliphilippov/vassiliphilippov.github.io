abseiler

1. NOUN

/ ˈæb.saɪ.lər / (AmE), / ˈæb.saɪ.lə / (BrE)
a person who descends a near-vertical surface using a rope in the manner of abseiling
person descending by rope (abseiling)

Examples

The experienced abseiler made his way swiftly down the cliff face.
Each abseiler must check their equipment before starting the descent.
A team of abseilers volunteered to clean the tall building’s windows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abseil - descend a surface using a rope technique

Etymology

From German 'abseilen' (to rope down), with '-er' denoting agent noun.
