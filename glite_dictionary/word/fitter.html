fitter

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɪtə / (BrE)
in better health or physical condition than before
comparative of 'fit'; more healthy or in better shape

Examples

Since starting her new exercise routine, she feels much fitter and more energetic every day.
After a few weeks of healthy eating and walking, he became noticeably fitter.
The team was fitter this season, which helped them win more matches.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fit - in good health or physical condition
  • Degree Higher: fittest - in the best health or physical condition

2. NOUN

/ ˈfɪtər / (AmE), / ˈfɪtə / (BrE)
a person who adjusts and tailors garments to fit an individual wearer
person who tailors garments to fit

Examples

The bridal fitter took her measurements before adjusting the dress.
He worked for years as a fitter in a high-end boutique.
A skilled fitter can alter a suit to perfection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tailor - person who makes or alters clothing

Etymology

From 'fit' (to make suitable) + agent noun suffix '-er'.
