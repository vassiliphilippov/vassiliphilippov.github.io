unsuspected

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnsəˈspɛktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnsəˈspɛktɪd / (BrE)
not thought likely or not suspected to exist, be true, or be present
not suspected; not thought likely

Examples

The police discovered an unsuspected connection between the two crimes.
An unsuspected leak in the system caused the engine to fail.
She faced unsuspected challenges during her first year at college.

Synset Examples

remained unsuspected as the head of the spy ring
he was able to get into the building unspotted and unsuspected
unsuspected difficulties arose
unsuspected turnings in the road

Related Terms

  • Antonym: suspected - regarded with suspicion or doubted

Etymology

From Latin 'suspectus' (suspected), with prefix 'un-' meaning 'not'.
