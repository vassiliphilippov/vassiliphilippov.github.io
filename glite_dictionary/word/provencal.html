Provencal

1. ADJ

/ ˌproʊ.vɑːnˈsɑl / (AmE), / ˌprɒ.vɒ̃ˈsɑːl / (BrE)
relating to Provence in southeastern France, its people, or their culture
of Provence, its people, or culture

Examples

The Provencal countryside is famous for its lavender fields and picturesque villages.
She prepared a Provencal dish using fresh herbs and olive oil.
Their home was decorated in a distinctive Provencal style, full of warm colors and rustic furniture.

Etymology

From French Provençal, relating to Provence.
