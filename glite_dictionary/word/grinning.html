grinning

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
A broad smile in which the corners of the mouth are turned up, often showing amusement or pleasure.
broad smile; expression of amusement

Examples

Her constant grinning made everyone around her feel more relaxed and welcome.
Despite his nervousness, his grinning revealed how excited he was about the opportunity.
The photographer captured the child's grinning as she received her birthday present.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grin - A broad smile typically showing the teeth
  • Antonym: frown - A facial expression of dislike or displeasure
