drugstore

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrʌɡˌstɔr / (AmE), / ˈdrʌɡˌstɔː / (BrE)
a shop where medicines and miscellaneous items are sold, especially one with a pharmacy
a shop selling medicines and other items
american

Examples

I stopped by the drugstore to pick up a prescription and a bottle of shampoo.
Most American drugstores are open late and offer a wide variety of products.
She found a birthday card and some snacks at the local drugstore.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pharmacy - a store or part of a store where medicinal drugs are dispensed and sold

Etymology

American English, from 'drug' + 'store', first used in the 19th century.
