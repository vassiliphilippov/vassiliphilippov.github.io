vaginocele

1. NOUN

/ vəˈdʒɪnəˌsil / (AmE), / vəˈdʒɪnəsiːl / (BrE)
a hernia in which tissue projects into the vagina
hernia projecting into the vagina
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed a vaginocele based on her symptoms and examination.
Surgical treatment was recommended to repair the vaginocele and relieve discomfort.
A vaginocele can cause a noticeable bulge in the vaginal wall.

Etymology

From Latin 'vagina' (sheath, vagina) + Greek 'kēlē' (tumor, hernia).
