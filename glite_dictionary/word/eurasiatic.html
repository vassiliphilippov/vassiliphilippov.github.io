Eurasiatic

1. ADJ

/ jʊˌreɪʒiˈætɪk / (AmE), / jʊəˌreɪzɪˈætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or originating from both Europe and Asia.
of or from Europe and Asia

Examples

The Eurasiatic landmass stretches from the Atlantic to the Pacific Ocean.
Several animal species are found across the Eurasiatic region.
The researcher specialized in Eurasiatic cultural studies.

Synset Examples

His mother was Eurasian, and his father Chinese
the Eurasian landmass is the largest in the world

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Eurasian - relating to or originating from both Europe and Asia

Etymology

Formed from 'Eurasia' (Europe + Asia) with the suffix -tic.
