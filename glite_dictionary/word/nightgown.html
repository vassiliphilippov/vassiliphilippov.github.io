nightgown

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪtˌɡaʊn / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtɡaʊn / (BrE)
a loose dress worn by women or girls for sleeping
loose dress for sleeping (women/girls)

Examples

She changed into her favorite nightgown before going to bed.
The store window displayed a variety of silk nightgowns for sale.
As a birthday present, Mary received a soft cotton nightgown from her grandmother.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nightdress - a loose dress worn for sleeping, especially by women or girls
  • Antonym: pajamas - loose-fitting clothes worn for sleeping, typically consisting of trousers and a top

Etymology

Formed from 'night' + 'gown', reflecting its use and garment type.
