moderatorship

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑdərəˌtɔrʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈmɒdərəˌtɔːʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or role of being a moderator, especially in meetings, discussions, or online communities.
position of being a moderator

Examples

After years of dedicated service, she was finally granted the moderatorship of the council.
His moderatorship of the online forum brought about many positive changes.
The responsibilities of moderatorship can be demanding during heated debates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moderator - a person who presides over a discussion or forum

Etymology

Formed from 'moderator' + '-ship', a suffix denoting status or condition.
