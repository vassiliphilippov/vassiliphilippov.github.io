lorchel

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔrʃəl / (AmE), / ˈlɔːʃəl / (BrE)
A large fungus of the family Helvellaceae, typically with a saddle-shaped or convoluted cap.
large fungus of the Helvellaceae family

Examples

The lorchel is notable for its distinctive, wrinkled appearance in woodland habitats.
Some species of lorchel are considered poisonous if not prepared correctly.
During their hike, the biologists encountered a rare lorchel growing by the stream.

Etymology

From German 'Lorchel', referring to fungi of this genus.
