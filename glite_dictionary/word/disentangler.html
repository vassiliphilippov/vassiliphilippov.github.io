disentangler

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪsɪnˈtæŋɡlər / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˈtæŋɡlə / (BrE)
a person who removes tangles or makes something untangled
person who untangles things

Examples

The expert disentangler managed to free the delicate chains without breaking them.
She acted as the disentangler of office rumors, clarifying misunderstandings for everyone.
A skilled disentangler is needed when fishing lines become severely knotted.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: untangler - a person or thing that untangles

Etymology

From 'disentangle' (to free from tangles) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
