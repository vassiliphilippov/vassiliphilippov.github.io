Burundi

1. PROPN

/ bəˈrʊndi / (AmE), / bəˈrʊndi / (BrE)
A landlocked country in east-central Africa, located on the northeastern shore of Lake Tanganyika.
country in east-central Africa

Examples

She traveled to Burundi to conduct research on local agriculture.
International aid organizations operate health clinics throughout Burundi.
The culture of Burundi is known for its traditional drumming and dance.
