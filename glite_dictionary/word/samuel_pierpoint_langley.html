Samuel Pierpoint Langley

1. PROPN

/ ˈsæmjəl ˈpɪrˌpɔɪnt ˈlæŋli / (AmE), / ˈsamjʊl ˈpɪəpɔɪnt ˈlæŋli / (BrE)
(1834–1906) A United States astronomer and aviation pioneer who invented the bolometer and contributed to the design of early aircraft.
