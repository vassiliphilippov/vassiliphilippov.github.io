Stan Laurel

1. PROPN

/ stæn ˈlɔɹəl / (AmE), / stan ˈlɒrəl / (BrE)
(1890–1965) English-born American comedian and actor, famous as the scatterbrained member of the Laurel and Hardy duo in classic slapstick films.
English-American film comedian; half of Laurel and Hardy

Examples

Stan Laurel perfected the art of physical comedy alongside Oliver Hardy during Hollywood’s golden age.
Many film historians credit Stan Laurel with influencing generations of comedians.
A documentary aired last night about the life and legacy of Stan Laurel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Oliver Hardy - the other member of the comic duo Laurel and Hardy
