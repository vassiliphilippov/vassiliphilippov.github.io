indie

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndi / (AmE), / ˈɪndi / (BrE)
a small independent film company not affiliated with a major studio
independent film company

Examples

Many acclaimed movies this year were produced by a small indie.
After working for years at a big studio, she started her own indie.
Big studios are starting to invest in indies to diversify their portfolios.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: independent - not controlled by others or by a central organization

Etymology

Short for 'independent' (as in 'independent label' or 'independent film').

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08019748-n
Synset POS: n

2. ADJ

/ ˈɪndi / (AmE), / ˈɪndi / (BrE)
(of music, film, or related artists) produced independently of major commercial organizations, especially major recording companies or studios.
independent of major commercial organizations

Examples

The band's latest album features a distinctly indie sound, free from commercial influences.
She prefers watching indie films rather than summer blockbusters.
Many indie artists distribute their music online to avoid traditional record labels.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mainstream - conforming to the dominant commercial music or film industry
  • Synonym: alternative - relating to music or art that is outside the commercial mainstream

Etymology

Short for 'independent'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00732577-s
Synset POS: s

3. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.di / (AmE), / ˈɪn.di / (BrE)
a pop or rock music group that is not signed to a major record company, often characterized by an independent, alternative style
independent music group or band
informal

Examples

The festival lineup this year includes several local indie bands.
She prefers listening to indie groups rather than mainstream pop acts.
Many successful artists began their careers on the indie music scene.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mainstream - music or artists associated with major commercial record labels

Etymology

Shortened form of 'independent', first used in the music industry in the 1980s.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08266775-n
Synset POS: n
