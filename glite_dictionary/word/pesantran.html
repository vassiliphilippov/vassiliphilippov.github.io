pesantran

1. NOUN

/ pɛˈsan.tran / (AmE), / pɛˈsan.tran / (BrE)
A Muslim boarding school in Indonesia operated by religious leaders, focused on religious education.
Indonesian Islamic boarding school

Examples

Many students in rural areas choose to attend a pesantran to receive religious instruction.
The pesantran plays a vital role in the local community's social and educational life.
International organizations have studied the curriculum provided at several pesantrans across Java.

Etymology

From Indonesian 'pesantren', ultimately from 'santri' (student) with the prefix 'pe-' and suffix '-an'.
