Autodefensas Unidas de Colombia

1. PROPN

/ aʊtoʊdeɪˈfɛnsɑs ˈunidɑs deɪ koʊˈlʌmbiə / (AmE), / ˌaʊtəʊdeɪˈfɛnsæs ˈjuːnɪdæs deɪ kəˈlʌmbiə / (BrE)
a paramilitary and terrorist organization in Colombia, formed in 1997 as an umbrella group for regional militias and financed largely by narcotics trafficking
Colombian paramilitary and terrorist group

Examples

The Autodefensas Unidas de Colombia were known for their violent campaign against leftist guerrilla groups.
International observers have condemned the human rights abuses committed by the Autodefensas Unidas de Colombia.
In peace negotiations, the government demanded the Autodefensas Unidas de Colombia lay down their arms.

Synset Examples

the AUC conducted over 800 assassinations in one year

Related Terms

  • Synonym: AUC - abreviation for Autodefensas Unidas de Colombia

Etymology

Spanish, meaning 'United Self-Defenders of Colombia'.
