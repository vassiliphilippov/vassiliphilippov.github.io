masturbation

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæstərˈbeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmæstəˈbeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of touching or stimulating your own or another person's genital organs for sexual pleasure, usually to the point of orgasm
manual sexual stimulation of genitals

Examples

Many people consider masturbation to be a normal and healthy part of sexuality.
Sexual education classes often address topics such as consent and masturbation.
He felt embarrassed when the subject of masturbation came up in conversation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: autoeroticism - sexual gratification obtained solely through self-stimulation
  • Antonym: coitus - sexual intercourse between individuals

Etymology

From Latin 'masturbari', meaning to handle oneself.
