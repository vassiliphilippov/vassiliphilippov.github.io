lyricist

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪr.ɪ.sɪst / (AmE), / ˈlɪr.ɪ.sɪst / (BrE)
a person who writes the words for songs, especially popular songs or musicals.
a person who writes song lyrics

Examples

She is a talented lyricist who has written songs for many famous artists.
The musical's lyricist worked closely with the composer to create memorable tunes.
He became a lyricist after discovering his passion for poetry and music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: songwriter - a person who writes the music and/or lyrics for songs
  • Synonym: composer - a person who writes music, especially classical music

Etymology

From 'lyric' and the suffix '-ist', indicating someone who practices or is concerned with something.
