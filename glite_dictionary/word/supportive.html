supportive

1. ADJ

/ səˈpɔːrtɪv / (AmE), / səˈpɔːtɪv / (BrE)
giving help, encouragement, or emotional support to someone
giving help or encouragement

Examples

Her parents were always supportive during her difficult career change.
The teacher's supportive attitude made all the difference for the students.
Having a supportive group of friends can help you overcome tough times.

Synset Examples

a supportive family network
his family was supportive of his attempts to be a writer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: encouraging - giving someone support or confidence
  • Antonym: unsupportive - not providing help or encouragement

Etymology

From 'support' + '-ive', where 'support' is from Old French 'suport', from Latin 'supportare' (to carry, bring up).
