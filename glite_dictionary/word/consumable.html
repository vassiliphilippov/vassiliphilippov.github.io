consumable

1. ADJ

/ kənˈsuːməbl̩ / (AmE), / kənˈsjuːməbl̩ / (BrE)
(of a resource or item) able to be used up or consumed, especially in the process of use.
able to be used up; expendable

Examples

The printer requires consumable supplies such as ink cartridges and paper.
Batteries are consumable items that need regular replacement in some devices.
The chef takes note of all consumable goods to ensure the kitchen is always stocked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expendable - Able to be used up and then discarded or replaced

Etymology

From Latin 'consumere' (to consume) + English '-able'.
