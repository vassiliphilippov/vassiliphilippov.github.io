hospitalisation

1. NOUN

/ hɑːˌspɪtəlɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / hɒˌspɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of admitting someone to a hospital for medical treatment or care
admission to hospital for medical care
medical british

Examples

The hospitalisation of the patient lasted three weeks due to complications.
Rising cases of flu have led to an increase in hospitalisation rates this winter.
Her insurance covers full hospitalisation in both public and private facilities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hospitalization - placing in medical care in a hospital (American spelling)

Etymology

From 'hospital' + '-isation'; chiefly British spelling, compare American 'hospitalization.'
