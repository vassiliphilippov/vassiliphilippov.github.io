Soho

1. PROPN

/ ˈsoʊˌhoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.həʊ / (BrE)
a district in central London famous for its lively nightlife, restaurants, and entertainment venues
district in central London known for nightlife

Examples

We spent the evening exploring the bars and clubs in Soho.
Soho attracts both tourists and locals with its vibrant atmosphere and diverse cuisine.
Many famous artists and writers once frequented the cafés of Soho.

Etymology

Name of an area in London, likely from an old hunting cry.
