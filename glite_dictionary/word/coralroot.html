coralroot

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔːrəlˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəlˌruːt / (BrE)
A European bittercress plant with a knotted white rootstock, belonging to the genus Dentaria.
a plant with a white knotted rootstock

Examples

The coralroot thrives in damp woodland areas across Europe.
We identified several coralroot plants by their distinctive knotted white roots.
Wild garlic and coralroot can often be found growing near each other.
