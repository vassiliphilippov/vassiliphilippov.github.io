Ask.com

1. PROPN

/ ˈæsk dɑt kɑm / (AmE), / ˈɑːsk dɒt kɒm / (BrE)
A search engine that allows users to enter questions, phrases, or terms in plain English to find information on the internet.
an internet search engine

Examples

You can use Ask.com to find answers to your questions online.
She preferred Ask.com because it understood natural language queries.
In the early 2000s, Ask.com was one of the most popular search engines.
