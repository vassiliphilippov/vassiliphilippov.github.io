arrivederci

1. NOUN

/ ɑˌrivəˈdɛrtʃi / (AmE), / əˌriːvəˈdɛːtʃi / (BrE)
Used to say goodbye; an exclamation meaning 'farewell,' borrowed from Italian.
a way to say goodbye (from Italian)

Examples

With a wave and a smile, she said arrivederci and stepped onto the train.
He ended the email with a cheerful arrivederci instead of the usual goodbye.
At the party, guests exchanged hugs and arrivederci as they prepared to leave.

Synset Examples

they said their good-byes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - a conventional expression used to bid farewell

Etymology

From Italian *arrivederci* ('until we see each other again'), from *a* ('to'), *rivedere* ('see again').
