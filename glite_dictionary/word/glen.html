glen

1. NOUN

/ ɡlɛn / (AmE), / ɡlɛn / (BrE)
A narrow, secluded valley, especially in a mountainous area.
narrow valley, especially in mountains

Examples

We hiked through a beautiful glen surrounded by misty mountains.
The village was nestled in a quiet glen far from the main road.
After crossing the ridge, they descended into a scenic glen filled with wildflowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: valley - a low area of land between hills or mountains, often with a river running through it

Etymology

From Scottish Gaelic 'gleann' (valley), also related to Irish 'gleann'.
