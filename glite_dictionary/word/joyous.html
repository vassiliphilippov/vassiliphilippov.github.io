joyous

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɔɪəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːjəs / (BrE)
full of happiness or joy; showing or causing joy.
full of joy; happy

Examples

The holiday parade was a joyous celebration for the entire community.
She had a joyous smile on her face when she received the good news.
The children's joyous laughter filled the playground on a sunny afternoon.

Synset Examples

felt a joyous abandon
joyous laughter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: happy - feeling or showing pleasure or contentment
  • Synonym: joyful - feeling, expressing, or causing great pleasure and happiness
  • Antonym: sad - feeling or showing sorrow; unhappy

Etymology

From Old French *joios*, from Late Latin *gaudiosus*, from Latin *gaudium* (joy).
