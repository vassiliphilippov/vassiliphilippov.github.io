Dizzy Gillespie

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɪzi gɪˈlɛspi / (AmE), / ˈdɪzi gɪˈlɛspi / (BrE)
(1917–1993) an American jazz trumpeter, bandleader, and key figure in the bebop movement.
American jazz trumpeter and bandleader

Examples

Dizzy Gillespie was a pioneer of modern jazz and played a crucial role in the development of bebop.
The trumpet style of Dizzy Gillespie continues to influence musicians around the world.
Many jazz enthusiasts consider Dizzy Gillespie one of the greatest improvisers in the history of music.
