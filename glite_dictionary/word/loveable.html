loveable

1. ADJ

/ ˈlʌvəbəl / (AmE), / ˈlʌvəbəl / (BrE)
having qualities that inspire love or affection; easy to love.
inspiring love or affection

Examples

Everyone found her loveable personality absolutely charming.
The movie features a loveable dog who helps his owner solve mysteries.
Despite his clumsy mistakes, he remained a loveable character throughout the series.

Synset Examples

a mischievous but lovable child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adorable - inspiring great affection or delight

Etymology

Formed from 'love' + '-able', meaning 'able to be loved'.
