insalubriousness

1. NOUN

/ ɪn.səˈluː.bri.əs.nəs / (AmE), / ɪn.səˈljuː.bri.əs.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being unhealthy or detrimental to health.
unhealthiness; state of being insalubrious

Examples

The insalubriousness of the factory conditions led to widespread illness among the workers.
She was concerned about the insalubriousness of the neighborhood after noticing the rundown buildings and litter.
Years of neglect contributed to the insalubriousness of the river, affecting both wildlife and residents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unhealthiness - the state of being unhealthy
  • Antonym: salubriousness - the quality of being health-giving or healthful

Etymology

From insalubrious + -ness; ultimately from Latin salubris (healthful).
