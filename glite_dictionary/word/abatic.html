abatic

1. ADJ

/ əˈbætɪk / (AmE), / əˈbætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by abasia (inability to walk)
relating to abasia (inability to walk)
medical

Examples

The neurologist described the patient's condition as abatic due to complete loss of voluntary movement in the legs.
After the accident, Mark developed abatic symptoms and required physical therapy.
The report included several cases of abatic paralysis in elderly patients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abasic - relating to abasia (inability to walk)

Etymology

From 'abasia' (inability to walk), ultimately from Greek 'a-' (not) + 'basis' (step).
