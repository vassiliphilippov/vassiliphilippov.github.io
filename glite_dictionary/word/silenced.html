silenced

1. ADJ

/ ˈsaɪlənst / (AmE), / ˈsaɪlənst / (BrE)
having been made quiet or unable to speak; reduced to silence, often suddenly or by external means
made quiet; reduced to silence

Examples

The silenced telephone echoed in the empty office after the power cut.
She gave a silenced nod when asked if she understood the rules.
The silenced protesters stood in defiance, forbidden from speaking out.

Synset Examples

the silenced crowd waited expectantly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quiet - making little or no noise; calm or still
  • Antonym: noisy - making a lot of noise
