escolar

1. NOUN

/ ɛˈskoʊlɑr / (AmE), / ɛˈskɒlɑː / (BrE)
a large deep-sea fish of the mackerel family, Lepidocybium flavobrunneum, noted for rings around its eyes like spectacles
a large deep-sea fish of the mackerel family

Examples

The chef recommended grilled escolar as the fish of the day.
Caution is advised when consuming escolar due to its natural oil content.
The marine biologist studied the migration patterns of escolar in tropical waters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snake mackerel - a type of predatory marine fish in the family Gempylidae

Etymology

From Spanish *escolar* (literally 'scholar'), originally used for several fish species.
