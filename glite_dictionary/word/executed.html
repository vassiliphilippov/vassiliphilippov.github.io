executed

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛksɪˌkjutɪd / (AmE), / ˈɛksɪˌkjuːtɪd / (BrE)
Put to death as a legal punishment, especially following a court sentence.
put to death as punishment

Examples

The government refused to return the belongings of the executed prisoners.
Families gathered at the cemetery to mourn their executed relatives.
The executed man was later proven to be innocent.

Synset Examples

claimed the body of the executed traitor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hanged - put to death by hanging as a form of execution
  • Antonym: pardoned - forgiven and released from punishment, especially a sentence of death
