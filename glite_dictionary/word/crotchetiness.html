crotchetiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɑːtʃətinəs / (AmE), / ˈkrɒtʃɪtɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being eccentric, fussy, or irritable, especially in temperament
eccentric or irritable temperament

Examples

His crotchetiness often made conversations at the dinner table unpredictable.
The old professor’s crotchetiness was well known among his students.
She accepted her grandfather’s crotchetiness as just another part of his charm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irascibility - tendency to become easily angered
  • Synonym: eccentricity - quality of being unconventional or slightly strange
  • Antonym: placidity - calmness or peacefulness of temperament

Etymology

From 'crotchety' + '-ness'. 'Crotchety' from 'crotchet', meaning a perverse or eccentric notion.
