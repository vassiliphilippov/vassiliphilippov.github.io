daughterly

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɔtərli / (AmE), / ˈdɔːtəli / (BrE)
characteristic of, or befitting, a daughter; showing affection, duty, or care as a daughter would
characteristic of a daughter

Examples

She cared for her aging mother with daughterly devotion.
He recognized the daughterly pride in her eyes as she accepted the award.
There was a daughterly tenderness in the way she spoke to her father.

Synset Examples

daughterly affection

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filial - of, relating to, or befitting a son or daughter

Etymology

From 'daughter' + suffix '-ly' forming adjectives meaning 'like' or 'characteristic of.'
