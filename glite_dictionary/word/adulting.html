adulting

1. NOUN

/ əˈdʌltɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈdʌltɪŋ / (BrE)
The practice of behaving in a way characteristic of a responsible adult, especially the accomplishment of mundane but necessary tasks.
acting responsibly as an adult
informal

Examples

Paying my own bills and filing taxes are just part of adulting.
Sometimes adulting feels overwhelming, especially when it comes to doing laundry and managing finances.
She joked that buying groceries instead of fast food was her way of adulting for the week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adult - a person who is fully grown or developed

Etymology

From 'adult' + '-ing', an informal formation popularized in the 2010s via social media.
