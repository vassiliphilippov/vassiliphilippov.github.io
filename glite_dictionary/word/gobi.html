Gobi

1. PROPN

/ ˈgoʊ.bi / (AmE), / ˈgəʊ.bi / (BrE)
a large desert region in northern China and southern Mongolia, known for its arid climate and harsh landscape
desert in northern China and Mongolia

Examples

The Gobi is famous for its rugged beauty and extreme temperature changes.
Scientists have discovered dinosaur fossils in the Gobi Desert.
Few plants can survive in the harsh conditions of the Gobi.

Etymology

From Mongolian 'gov' meaning 'waterless place'.
