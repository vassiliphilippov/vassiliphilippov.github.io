psychopathic

1. ADJ

/ ˌsaɪkəˈpæθɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəˈpæθɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or affected by psychopathy; showing or characteristic of a chronic mental disorder with abnormal or violent social behavior.
relating to psychopathy or mental disorder
medical

Examples

The suspect displayed psychopathic tendencies that alarmed the investigators.
Her psychopathic behavior made it difficult for her to maintain relationships.
Experts debated whether his crimes were driven by a psychopathic personality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sociopathic - relating to or characteristic of sociopathy
  • Antonym: normal - not affected by any mental disorder

Etymology

From Greek psykho- (mind, soul) and -pathic (suffering, disease), from pathos (suffering).
