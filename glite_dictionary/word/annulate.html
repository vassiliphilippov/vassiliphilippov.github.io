annulate

1. ADJ

/ ˈæn.jə.lət / (AmE), / ˈæn.jʊ.lət / (BrE)
having ring-shaped or ring-like segments; shaped like a ring.
having ring-shaped segments

Examples

Some worms are annulate, with visible bands along their bodies.
The fossil showed an annulate pattern around the tube-shaped structure.
Under the microscope, the insect's annulate antennae were clearly segmented.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ring-shaped - having the shape of a ring

Etymology

From Latin 'annulatus' (provided with rings), from 'annulus' (ring).
