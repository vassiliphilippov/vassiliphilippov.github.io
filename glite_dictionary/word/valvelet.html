valvelet

1. NOUN

/ ˈvælv.lət / (AmE), / ˈvælv.lɪt / (BrE)
a small valve, especially in a technical or anatomical context
small valve

Examples

The intricate mechanism contained a valvelet that regulated the precise flow of air.
Each valvelet plays a crucial role in preventing backflow within the system.
The engineer replaced the worn valvelet to improve the pump's efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: valve - a device that controls the flow of a fluid

Etymology

From 'valve' combined with the diminutive suffix '-let'.
