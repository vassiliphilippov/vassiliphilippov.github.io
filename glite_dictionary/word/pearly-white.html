pearly-white

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɜrli waɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːli waɪt / (BrE)
having a white color that resembles the luster and shade of a pearl.
white resembling a pearl

Examples

Her pearly-white teeth gleamed as she smiled brightly at the camera.
The bride wore a pearly-white gown that shimmered under the lights.
They chose a pearly-white paint for the kitchen cabinets to give the room a fresh look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ivory - a hard, creamy-white substance forming the tusks of elephants and walruses; also a color resembling it
