Owensboro

1. PROPN

/ ˈoʊwənzˌbɝːoʊ / (AmE), / ˈəʊwɪnzbʌrəʊ / (BrE)
A city in northwestern Kentucky, United States, situated on the Ohio River and known historically as a tobacco market.
city in Kentucky, USA

Examples

The annual BBQ festival in Owensboro attracts visitors from across the region.
She grew up in Owensboro, a town rich in history and southern charm.
Tobacco farming has long been a part of Owensboro's local economy.
