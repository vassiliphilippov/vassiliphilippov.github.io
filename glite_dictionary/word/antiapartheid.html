antiapartheid

1. ADJ

/ ˌænti.əˈpɑrˌteɪd / (AmE), / ˌænti.əˈpɑːteɪd / (BrE)
Opposing the system of apartheid, especially in South Africa.
opposing apartheid (South Africa)

Examples

He was a prominent antiapartheid activist during the 1980s.
The antiapartheid movement gathered international support for its cause.
Many antiapartheid protests were held outside embassies around the world.

Synset Examples

an antiapartheid leader

Related Terms

  • Antonym: apartheid - a policy of racial segregation and discrimination in South Africa

Etymology

From anti- (against) + apartheid (Afrikaans for 'apartness').
