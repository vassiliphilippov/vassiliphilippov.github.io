Reykjavik

1. PROPN

/ ˈreɪkjəˌvɪk / (AmE), / ˈreɪkjəvɪk / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Iceland, located in the southwest of the country, known for its geothermal heating and coastal location
capital city of Iceland

Examples

Many tourists visit Reykjavik to see the northern lights and experience Icelandic culture.
Geothermal energy provides most of the heating for buildings in Reykjavik.
The conference will be held in Reykjavik next summer.

Etymology

From Icelandic "Reykjavík" meaning 'bay of smoke', referring to local hot springs.
