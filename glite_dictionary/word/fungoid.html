fungoid

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʌŋ.ɡɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋ.ɡɔɪd / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of fungi.
resembling fungi

Examples

The walls were covered in a fungoid growth after the flood.
She noticed a fungoid pattern spreading across the old bread.
The artist’s depiction of the forest included several fungoid shapes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fungal - of or relating to fungi

Etymology

From Latin 'fungus' (mushroom, fungus) + '-oid' (resembling).
