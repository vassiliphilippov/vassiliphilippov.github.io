LuGanda

1. NOUN

/ luˈɡɑːndə / (AmE), / luˈɡandə / (BrE)
the Bantu language spoken by the Buganda people of Uganda
language of the Buganda people

Examples

She studied LuGanda to communicate better with local communities in Uganda.
LuGanda is widely used on radio stations in central Uganda.
His grandmother taught him stories in LuGanda during his visits.

Etymology

From local term 'Oluganda', with 'lu-' denoting language in Bantu languages.
