Luganda

1. NOUN

/ luːˈɡɑːndə / (AmE), / luːˈɡandə / (BrE)
the Bantu language spoken by the Buganda people of central Uganda
Bantu language of the Buganda people (Uganda)

Examples

Many people in Uganda grow up speaking Luganda as their first language.
He is taking classes to learn the basics of Luganda before traveling to Kampala.
Translations between English and Luganda are in high demand among NGOs working in the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bantu - a family of closely related African languages

Etymology

From the native name Luganda, where 'Lu-' is a Bantu prefix for languages and 'Ganda' refers to the Buganda people.
