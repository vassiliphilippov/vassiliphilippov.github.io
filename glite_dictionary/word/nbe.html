NbE

1. NOUN

/ ɛn bi ˈi / (AmE), / ɛn bi ˈiː / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point east (clockwise) of due north, equal to 11°15′ on the compass.
compass point, one point east of north

Examples

The ship maintained a course of NbE through the stormy night.
On the chart, the bearing was marked as NbE from the lighthouse.
Experienced navigators can easily identify NbE among other compass points.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: north by east - The compass point one point east of due north
  • Degree Lower: north - Primary compass point at 0°
  • Degree Higher: north-northeast - Compass point one point further east from north by east

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'north by east', from nautical compass terminology.
