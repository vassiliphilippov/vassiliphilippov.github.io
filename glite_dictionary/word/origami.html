origami

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔːrɪˈɡɑːmi / (AmE), / ˌɒrɪˈɡɑːmi / (BrE)
the Japanese art of folding paper into decorative shapes and figures
Japanese art of paper folding

Examples

She learned how to make a paper crane with origami techniques.
The children were fascinated by the delicate origami animals on display.
He gave her an origami rose as a token of appreciation.

Etymology

From Japanese 'origami', from 'oru' (to fold) + 'kami' (paper).
