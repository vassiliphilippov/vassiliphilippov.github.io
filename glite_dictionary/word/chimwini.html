ChiMwini

1. NOUN

/ tʃiˈmwiːni / (AmE), / tʃɪˈmwiːni / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken in southern Somalia
Bantu language of southern Somalia

Examples

The ChiMwini language is used by communities along the southern coast of Somalia.
Linguists are working to document and preserve the ChiMwini language.
She is fluent in both Somali and ChiMwini after growing up in a multilingual family.
