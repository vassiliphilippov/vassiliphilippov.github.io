Tiziano Vecellio

1. PROPN

/ tiˈtsjɑːnoʊ veˈtʃɛljoʊ / (AmE), / tɪˈtsiːɑːnəʊ veˈtʃɛliəʊ / (BrE)
(c. 1490–1576) Italian Renaissance painter, one of the leading figures of the Venetian school, also known as Titian.
Renaissance Venetian painter, known as Titian

Examples

The works of Tiziano Vecellio display a mastery of color and form that influenced generations of artists.
Tiziano Vecellio was one of the most prominent members of the Venetian school during the Renaissance.
Art historians often refer to Tiziano Vecellio by his more familiar name, Titian.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Titian - Venetian painter; the anglicized name of Tiziano Vecellio

Etymology

Italian form of the painter’s birth name; known in English as Titian.
