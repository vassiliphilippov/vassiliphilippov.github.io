Afghani

1. ADJ

/ æfˈɡɑni / (AmE), / æfˈɡɑːni / (BrE)
of or relating to Afghanistan or its people
relating to Afghanistan or its people

Examples

The Afghani culture is rich in tradition and hospitality.
She wore a beautiful Afghani dress to the festival.
Many Afghani artists are gaining international recognition for their work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Afghan - of or relating to Afghanistan or its people

Etymology

From Persian 'Afghānī', meaning 'of the Afghans'.

2. NOUN

/ æfˈɡɑːni / (AmE), / æfˈɡɑːni / (BrE)
the Iranian language spoken in Afghanistan and Pakistan, also known as Pashto; the official language of Afghanistan
official language of Afghanistan (Pashto)

Examples

Many people in eastern Afghanistan speak Afghani as their first language.
He is learning Afghani to communicate with his relatives in Kabul.
The constitution was translated into Afghani for official use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pashto - an Iranian language spoken in Afghanistan and Pakistan

Etymology

From Persian Afghanī (native or pertaining to Afghanistan).

Synset POS: n
