afghani

1. NOUN

/ æfˈɡɑːni / (AmE), / æfˈɡɑːni / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Afghanistan, equal to 100 puls
currency of Afghanistan

Examples

The cost of the meal was two hundred afghanis.
He exchanged his dollars for afghanis at the airport.
The value of the afghani has fluctuated significantly in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rupee - basic unit of money in India and some other South Asian countries
  • Synonym: dollar - basic unit of money in the US and other countries

Etymology

From Dari Persian افغانستان‎ (Afġānistān), the country name.
