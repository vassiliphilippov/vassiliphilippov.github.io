Bishkek

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɪʃˌkɛk / (AmE), / ˈbɪʃˌkɛk / (BrE)
the capital city of Kyrgyzstan, formerly known as Frunze from 1926 to 1991
capital of Kyrgyzstan

Examples

She traveled to Bishkek for a conference on Central Asian culture.
After independence, Bishkek replaced its Soviet-era name, Frunze.
Many tourists begin their journey through Kyrgyzstan in Bishkek.

Etymology

From Kyrgyz Бишкек (Biškek), previously known as Frunze.

2. PROPN

/ bɪʃˈkɛk / (AmE), / bɪʃˈkɛk / (BrE)
the capital city of Kyrgyzstan, formerly known as Frunze from 1926 to 1991.
capital city of Kyrgyzstan

Examples

The summit was held in Bishkek, drawing leaders from across Central Asia.
After traveling through the mountains, we arrived in Bishkek late in the evening.
Bishkek has grown rapidly since gaining independence from the Soviet Union.

Etymology

From Kyrgyz Бишкек (Bishkek), formerly called Frunze after Soviet leader Mikhail Frunze.
