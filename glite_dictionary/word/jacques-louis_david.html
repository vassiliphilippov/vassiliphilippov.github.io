Jacques-Louis David

1. PROPN

/ ʒɑk luˈi daˈvid / (AmE), / ʒak lwi daˈviːd / (BrE)
(1748–1825) A French neoclassical painter known for his influential works and active support of the French Revolution.
French neoclassical painter (1748–1825)

Examples

Jacques-Louis David painted some of the most significant works of the French Revolution era.
The art museum features a special exhibition on Jacques-Louis David and his students.
Many consider Jacques-Louis David the leading figure of neoclassical painting in France.
