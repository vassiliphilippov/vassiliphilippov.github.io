War Admiral

1. PROPN

/ wɔr ˈæd.mɪ.rəl / (AmE), / wɔː ˈæd.mɪ.rəl / (BrE)
a celebrated American Thoroughbred racehorse (1934–1959) that won the Triple Crown in 1937.
famous American racehorse, Triple Crown winner

Examples

War Admiral was known for his incredible speed and determination on the track.
In 1938, War Admiral famously competed against Seabiscuit in a highly anticipated match race.
Many racing fans regard War Admiral as one of the greatest Thoroughbreds in history.
