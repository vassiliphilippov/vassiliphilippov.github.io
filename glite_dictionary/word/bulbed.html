bulbed

1. ADJ

/ bʌlbd / (AmE), / bʌlbd / (BrE)
having a bulb or bulbs, especially in reference to a part that is swollen or rounded like a bulb.
having a bulb (swollen or rounded)

Examples

The plant has a bulbed root system suited to storing water during dry periods.
He examined the bulbed end of the glass pipette before continuing the experiment.
Some snakes have a distinctly bulbed head, making them easy to identify.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulbous - swollen, shaped like a bulb; having bulbs
