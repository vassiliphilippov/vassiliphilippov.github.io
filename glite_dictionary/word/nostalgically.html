nostalgically

1. ADV

/ nəˈstældʒɪkli / (AmE), / nɒˈstaldʒɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows or expresses nostalgia; in a manner that is sentimental about the past.
in a nostalgic manner

Examples

He spoke nostalgically about his childhood home and the memories he made there.
The old friends reminisced nostalgically as they looked through the photo album.
She gazed nostalgically at the empty playground where she once spent her summers.

Synset Examples

they spent an hour together, talking nostalgically as they wandered slowly down the coast road

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nostalgic - having or showing a longing for the past

Etymology

Formed from 'nostalgic' + '-ally'; 'nostalgic' from Greek 'nostos' (return home) + 'algos' (pain).
