butyraceous

1. ADJ

/ ˌbjuːtəˈreɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌbjuːtɪˈreɪʃəs / (BrE)
having the qualities of butter; resembling or producing butter; buttery
having the qualities of butter

Examples

The butyraceous texture of the cheese made it incredibly smooth and rich.
Some tropical seeds produce a butyraceous fat used in cooking and cosmetics.
The sauce had a butyraceous sheen that hinted at generous amounts of butter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buttery - having the taste, smell, or consistency of butter

Etymology

From Latin 'butyraceus', from 'butyrum' (butter).
