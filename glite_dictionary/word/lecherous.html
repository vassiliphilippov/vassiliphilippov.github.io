lecherous

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛtʃ.ɚ.əs / (AmE), / ˈlɛtʃ.ər.əs / (BrE)
having or showing excessive or offensive sexual desire; lustful or lascivious
having excessive sexual desire

Examples

The manager was fired after several lecherous comments to his colleagues were reported.
She avoided the lecherous glances from the men at the party.
He wore a lecherous smile that made everyone uncomfortable.

Synset Examples

a lecherous gleam in his eye
a lecherous good-for-nothing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lustful - having or showing strong sexual desire
  • Antonym: chaste - abstaining from sexual activity; morally pure

Etymology

From Old French *lecheros* (lecherous), from *lecher* (lecher).
