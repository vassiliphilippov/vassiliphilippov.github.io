primarily

1. ADV

/ praɪˈmɛrəli / (AmE), / praɪˈmɛːrɪli / (BrE)
For the most part; mainly; chiefly.
mainly; chiefly

Examples

The group was formed primarily to provide support for local artists.
Our discussion will focus primarily on the first chapter of the book.
She chose the restaurant primarily for its convenient location.

Synset Examples

he is mainly interested in butterflies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mainly - for the most part; chiefly
  • Synonym: chiefly - mainly; for the most part
  • Antonym: secondarily - in a lesser or subordinate way

Etymology

From Latin 'primarius' (of the first rank) + '-ly'.
