WATS

1. NOUN

/ wæts / (AmE), / wæts / (BrE)
An acronym for wide area telephone service; a telephone line service offering long-distance calls at fixed rates for specific zones, typically used by businesses.
wide area telephone service (long-distance phone line service)

Examples

Our company relies on the WATS line to handle outgoing customer calls efficiently.
Many businesses used WATS services before the introduction of more advanced telecommunications systems.
She remembered the days when orders were placed by WATS from remote stores.

Etymology

Acronym formed from 'Wide Area Telephone Service'.
