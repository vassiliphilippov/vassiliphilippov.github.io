Heroica Puebla de Zaragoza

1. PROPN

/ eɪˈroʊ.ɪ.kə ˈpweɪ.blə deɪ sɑːˈraː.ɣə / (AmE), / eɪˈrəʊ.ɪ.kə ˈpwɛb.lə deɪ zəˈrɑː.ɣə / (BrE)
a city in south central Mexico, southeast of Mexico City, located on the edge of the central Mexican plateau; often referred to simply as Puebla.
city in south central Mexico

Examples

Many tourists visit Heroica Puebla de Zaragoza to experience its rich colonial architecture and vibrant cuisine.
The university in Heroica Puebla de Zaragoza is renowned for its research programs.
After leaving Mexico City, we drove southeast to spend a week in Heroica Puebla de Zaragoza.

Etymology

From Spanish, meaning 'Heroic Puebla of Zaragoza', honoring the city's resistance during the Battle of Puebla (1862).
