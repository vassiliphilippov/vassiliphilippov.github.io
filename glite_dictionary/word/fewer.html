fewer

1. ADJ

/ ˈfjuːər / (AmE), / ˈfjuːə / (BrE)
Comparative of 'few'; a quantifier used with countable nouns to indicate a smaller number of people or things.
smaller number (comparative of 'few')

Examples

There were fewer cars on the road during the lockdown.
Schools are closing because fewer students are enrolling each year.
Recent surveys show fewer people are interested in traditional newspapers.

Synset Examples

fewer birds came this year
the birds are fewer this year
fewer trains were late

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: few - small in number; not many people or things
