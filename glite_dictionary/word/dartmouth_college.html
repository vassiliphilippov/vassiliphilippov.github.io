Dartmouth College

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɑrtməθ ˈkɑlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdɑːtməθ ˈkɒlɪdʒ / (BrE)
A private Ivy League university in Hanover, New Hampshire, United States, known for its undergraduate focus and liberal arts education.
Ivy League university in New Hampshire

Examples

After graduating from Dartmouth College, she began her career in finance.
Dartmouth College is renowned for its picturesque campus and rigorous academic programs.
He applied to several universities but ultimately chose Dartmouth College for its close-knit community.

Etymology

Named after William Legge, 2nd Earl of Dartmouth, an early benefactor.
