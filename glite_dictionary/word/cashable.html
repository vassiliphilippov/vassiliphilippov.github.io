cashable

1. ADJ

/ ˈkæʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkæʃəbl / (BrE)
able to be converted into cash or ready money
convertible into cash

Examples

The voucher is only cashable at participating stores.
Gambling chips are cashable at the casino cashier's desk.
We only accept cashable checks for large purchases.

Synset Examples

a cashable check
cashable gambling chips
redeemable stocks and bonds
a redeemable coupon

Related Terms

  • Synonym: redeemable - able to be exchanged for money, goods, or services

Etymology

From 'cash' + '-able', meaning capable of being cashed.
