limo

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈlɪməʊ / (BrE)
a large, luxurious car, often driven by a chauffeur and used for special occasions
large, luxurious chauffeur-driven car
informal

Examples

The wedding guests arrived in a sleek white limo that drew everyone's attention.
After the concert, the band left in a black limo parked behind the venue.
He booked a limo for their anniversary to make the evening extra special.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: limousine - a large luxurious car, especially one driven by a chauffeur

Etymology

Shortened form of 'limousine'.
