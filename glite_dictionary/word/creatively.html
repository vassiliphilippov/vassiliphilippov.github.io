creatively

1. ADV

/ kriˈeɪtɪvli / (AmE), / kriˈeɪtɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that shows creativity or imagination; in a creative manner.
in a creative way

Examples

The marketing team approached the project creatively and came up with an innovative campaign.
She decorated her new apartment creatively, using colors and patterns in unexpected ways.
Problems are often solved more effectively when they are tackled creatively rather than following routines.

Synset Examples

she solved the problem creatively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imaginatively - in a way that shows imagination or inventiveness; synonym for creatively
