abscessed

1. ADJ

/ ˈæbˌsɛst / (AmE), / ˈabsɛst / (BrE)
(of tissue, especially a tooth) infected and filled with pus as a result of an abscess.
infected and filled with pus
medical

Examples

The dentist explained that my abscessed tooth needed to be treated right away.
She was in severe pain because of the abscessed area on her gums.
Antibiotics were prescribed to help clear the abscessed wound.

Synset Examples

an abscessed tooth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infected - contaminated with harmful organisms or substances
  • Antonym: healthy - free from infection or disease

Etymology

From 'abscess', from Latin 'abscessus' (a going away, an abscess).
