sultanate

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌl.tə.neɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌl.tə.neɪt / (BrE)
A country or territory ruled by a sultan.
country ruled by a sultan

Examples

The Brunei sultanate is known for its wealth and Islamic traditions.
Under the new sultanate, trade flourished in the region for centuries.
Several historic sultanates existed in the Malay Archipelago.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kingdom - a country ruled by a king or queen
  • Synonym: sultan - a Muslim sovereign or monarch

Etymology

From Arabic 'sulṭānah', from 'sulṭān' (ruler, sultan).
