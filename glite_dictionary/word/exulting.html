exulting

1. ADJ

/ ɪɡˈzʌltɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzʌltɪŋ / (BrE)
Feeling or showing great joy and triumph; rejoicing proudly, especially because of success.
showing great joy and triumph

Examples

His exulting teammates lifted the trophy above their heads after the final whistle.
She sent an exulting message to her friends when she got the news of her promotion.
The exulting crowd cheered wildly as the results were announced.

Synset Examples

rejoicing crowds filled the streets on VJ Day
a triumphal success
a triumphant shout

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rejoicing - showing or expressing great happiness or joy
  • Synonym: triumphant - feeling or expressing jubilation after a victory or achievement
  • Antonym: mournful - feeling or expressing sadness

Etymology

From Latin exsultare (to leap up), via Latin exultare.
