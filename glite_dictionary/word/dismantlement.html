dismantlement

1. NOUN

/ dɪsˈmæntlmənt / (AmE), / dɪsˈmæntlmənt / (BrE)
the act of taking apart a machine, system, or structure into its component parts
the process of taking something apart

Examples

The dismantlement of the old factory was completed ahead of schedule.
After the treaty, the dismantlement of nuclear arms became a top priority.
Her job involved the careful dismantlement of delicate mechanical devices.

Synset Examples

Russia and the United States discussed the dismantling of their nuclear weapons

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dismantling - the process of taking something apart
  • Antonym: assembly - the act of putting something together

Etymology

From dismantle + -ment, patterned after similar English formations.
