mastalgia

1. NOUN

/ mæsˈtældʒiə / (AmE), / mæsˈtældʒiə / (BrE)
pain in the breast, especially in women
pain in the breast
medical

Examples

The patient reported experiencing severe mastalgia during her menstrual cycle.
Many women seek medical advice for mastalgia, though it is often benign.
Persistent mastalgia can sometimes indicate an underlying health issue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breast pain - pain experienced in the breast; non-technical synonym

Etymology

From Greek 'mast-' (breast) + 'algia' (pain).
