cryptographic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkrɪptoʊˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌkrɪptəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving cryptography or the techniques of secure communication and code-breaking.
relating to cryptography or code-breaking

Examples

The team implemented a cryptographic algorithm to secure user data.
Her research focuses on cryptographic techniques for protecting information in digital systems.
A strong cryptographic key is essential for safe online transactions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cryptography - the study or practice of secure communication through encoding information

Etymology

From Greek 'kryptos' (hidden) and English '-graphic' (relating to writing or recording).
