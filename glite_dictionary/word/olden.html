olden

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊldən / (AmE), / ˈəʊldən / (BrE)
(used in set phrases) relating to a distant time in the past; belonging to long ago.
relating to a distant past

Examples

In the olden days, people traveled by horse and carriage.
Stories from olden times often tell of brave knights and dragons.
The castle was built in olden centuries and still stands today.

Synset Examples

olden days

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ancient - belonging to the very distant past
  • Antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times

Etymology

Formed from 'old' + the suffix '-en', used in Middle English to make adjectives.
