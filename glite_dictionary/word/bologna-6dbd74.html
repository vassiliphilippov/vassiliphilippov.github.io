bologna

1. NOUN

/ bəˈloʊni / (AmE), / bəˈləʊni / (BrE)
a large, smooth-textured smoked sausage made from beef, veal, and pork, often sliced for sandwiches
smoked sausage made from beef, veal, and pork

Examples

He made a sandwich with slices of bologna and cheese.
The deli offers several types of bologna, including beef and turkey varieties.
For lunch, she packed a bologna and mustard roll in her bag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mortadella - an Italian sausage similar to bologna, containing pieces of fat and spices

Etymology

From the Italian city Bologna, where a similar sausage originated.
