standoffishness

1. NOUN

/ ˌstændˈɑːfɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˌstandˈɒfɪʃnəs / (BrE)
a quality of being distant, unfriendly, or reserved in manner
quality of being distant or unfriendly

Examples

His standoffishness made it difficult for new colleagues to approach him.
Despite her standoffishness, she cared deeply about her friends.
Some interpreted his silence as standoffishness, but he was simply shy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aloofness - quality of being emotionally distant or detached
  • Antonym: friendliness - quality of being friendly and approachable

Etymology

From 'standoffish' (distant or reserved) + -ness (forming nouns of quality).
