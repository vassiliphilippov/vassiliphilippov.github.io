suck

1. NOUN

/ sʌk / (AmE), / sʌk / (BrE)
an act of drawing liquid, air, or another substance into the mouth by using the lips and creating a vacuum
the act of sucking

Examples

The baby needed a good suck on the bottle to calm down.
He took a quick suck of his drink before answering the question.
After his suck on the ice lolly, his lips turned blue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sip - to drink in small quantities
