ruefully

1. ADV

/ ˈruː.fə.li / (AmE), / ˈruː.fə.li / (BrE)
in a way that expresses regret, sorrow, or pity
with regret or sorrow

Examples

She smiled ruefully, acknowledging her mistake.
He glanced ruefully at the broken vase on the floor.
'I should have listened to you,' he admitted ruefully.

Synset Examples

‘I made a big mistake,’ he said ruefully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regretfully - in a way that shows regret

Etymology

From rueful + -ly; 'rueful' from the verb 'rue' (to regret), of Old English origin.
