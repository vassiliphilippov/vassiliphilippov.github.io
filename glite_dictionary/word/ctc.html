CTC

1. NOUN

/ si ti si / (AmE), / siː tiː siː / (BrE)
an acronym for the Counterterrorist Center, an agency responsible for coordinating counterterrorism activities, especially within U.S. intelligence services.
acronym: Counterterrorist Center

Examples

The CTC played a significant role in developing strategies to prevent terrorist attacks.
After joining the CTC, she specialized in international intelligence operations.
The report highlighted the cooperation between the CTC and other federal agencies.

Etymology

Acronym formed from Counterterrorist Center.
