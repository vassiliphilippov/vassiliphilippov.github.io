inhumane

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnhjuˈmeɪn / (AmE), / ˌɪnhjuˈmeɪn / (BrE)
lacking compassion or pity; cruel or brutal.
lacking compassion; cruel

Examples

The treatment of the prisoners was considered inhumane by international organizations.
It is inhumane to deny animals proper shelter and care.
Many people protested the inhumane working conditions in the factory.

Synset Examples

humans are innately inhumane; this explains much of the misery and suffering in the world
biological weapons are considered too inhumane to be used

Related Terms

  • Antonym: humane - showing compassion or kindness
  • Synonym: cruel - willfully causing pain or suffering to others

Etymology

From Medieval Latin inhumanus, from Latin in- (not) + humanus (human).
