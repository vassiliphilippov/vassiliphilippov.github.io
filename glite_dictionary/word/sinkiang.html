Sinkiang

1. PROPN

/ sɪnˈkjɑŋ / (AmE), / sɪnˈkjæŋ / (BrE)
an autonomous region in far northwestern China, officially known as Xinjiang, and the homeland of the Uighur people
autonomous region in northwestern China

Examples

Many Silk Road travelers passed through Sinkiang on their way to Central Asia.
The culture of Sinkiang reflects a unique blend of Chinese and Central Asian influences.
In the 20th century, Sinkiang became known internationally as Xinjiang.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Xinjiang - official name for Sinkiang, an autonomous region in China

Etymology

From Chinese 新疆 (Xīnjiāng), meaning 'new frontier'; 'Sinkiang' is a transliteration from older romanizations.
