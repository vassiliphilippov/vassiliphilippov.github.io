Brest

1. PROPN

/ brɛst / (AmE), / brɛst / (BrE)
a port city in northwestern France, located in Brittany, and the chief naval station of France.
port city in Brittany, France

Examples

The French navy has a major base in Brest on the Atlantic coast.
Many tourists visit Brest to explore its naval history and scenic harbor.
The international regatta departed from Brest and sailed towards Spain.
