Coracias garrulus

1. NOUN

/ kɔːˈreɪʃiəs ɡəˈruːləs / (AmE), / kɒˈreɪʃiæs ɡəˈruːləs / (BrE)
A species of brightly colored European bird, known as the European roller, with blue and green plumage and a reddish-brown back.
European blue-and-green roller bird

Examples

We spotted a Coracias garrulus perching on a telephone wire during our trip to Spain.
The conservation status of Coracias garrulus has raised concerns among ornithologists.
A Coracias garrulus was photographed feeding its young in the open countryside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: European roller - Common name for Coracias garrulus, a brightly colored bird native to Europe

Etymology

From Latin genus name 'Coracias' and species 'garrulus' meaning 'chattering'.
