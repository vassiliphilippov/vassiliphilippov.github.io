unregularity

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˌrɛɡjəˈlærɪti / (AmE), / ʌnˌrɛɡjʊˈlarɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of not following a fixed principle or occurring at irregular intervals; lack of regularity
lack of regularity or fixed pattern
archaic

Examples

The unregularity of his attendance caused some concern among the staff.
Doctors noted the unregularity of her heartbeat during the examination.
In the countryside, the unregularity of the train service often frustrates travelers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irregularity - state of being irregular; lack of regularity

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'regularity', from Latin 'regula' (rule).
