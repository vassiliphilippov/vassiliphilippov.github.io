commie

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmi / (AmE), / ˈkɒmi / (BrE)
(informal, often derogatory) A communist or a person who advocates communism.
communist (informal, often derogatory)
informal

Examples

Some politicians were accused of being commies during the Cold War era.
The book tells the story of a family labeled as commies in the 1950s.
He was called a commie because of his radical views.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communist - a person who supports or believes in the principles of communism

Etymology

Shortened form of 'communist'.
