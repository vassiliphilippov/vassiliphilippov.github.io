auriferous

1. ADJ

/ ɔːˈrɪfərəs / (AmE), / ɔːˈrɪfərəs / (BrE)
(of rocks, soil, or minerals) containing gold.
containing gold

Examples

The geologist identified several auriferous rock samples during the survey.
Prospectors are often drawn to regions with auriferous deposits in the soil.
Mining companies are interested in areas known for their auriferous quartz veins.

Synset Examples

auriferous quartz veins

Etymology

From Latin 'aurifer' (gold-bearing), from 'aurum' (gold) + 'ferre' (to bear).
