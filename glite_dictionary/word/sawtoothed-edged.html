sawtoothed-edged

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɔːˌtuːθt ˌɛdʒd / (AmE), / ˈsɔːˌtuːθt ˌɛdʒd / (BrE)
(of leaves) having an edge with pointed projections resembling the teeth of a saw.
having a sawtooth-like edge (esp. of leaves)

Examples

The sawtoothed-edged leaves of this plant make it easy to identify in the wild.
Many holly varieties are known for their sawtoothed-edged foliage.
Gardeners often prefer species with sawtoothed-edged leaves for their striking appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: serrated - having a notched or saw-like edge, especially used for leaves and knives
