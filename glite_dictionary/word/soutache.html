soutache

1. NOUN

/ suˈtæʃ / (AmE), / suˈtɑːʃ / (BrE)
a narrow, flat braid used as a decorative trimming on clothing or upholstery
narrow decorative braid

Examples

The jacket was adorned with intricate soutache along the collar and cuffs.
She chose black soutache to embellish the edges of the velvet dress.
Antique furniture often features soutache as a decorative element on fabric surfaces.

Etymology

From French 'soutache', possibly from Italian 'soutaccé', referring to a kind of braid.
