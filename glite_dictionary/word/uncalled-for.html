uncalled-for

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkɔld fɔr / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɔːld fɔː / (BrE)
not necessary or justified; inappropriate or unwarranted
not necessary or justified

Examples

His rude comments were completely uncalled-for during the meeting.
The punishment was severe and uncalled-for given the circumstances.
She apologized for her uncalled-for criticism.

Synset Examples

uncalled-for suggestions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unwarranted - not justified or authorized
  • Synonym: unnecessary - not needed or required
  • Antonym: justified - having a good or legitimate reason

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkɔld ˌfɔr / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɔːld fɔː / (BrE)
(of an action or remark) unnecessary and unwarranted; not justified or appropriate in the circumstances.
unnecessary and unwarranted

Examples

Her criticism was entirely uncalled-for and hurt his feelings.
The manager considered the comment uncalled-for during the staff meeting.
I apologize; my reaction was uncalled-for and I regret it.

Synset Examples

a strikers' tent camp … was burned with needless loss of life

  • Synonym: unnecessary - not needed; more than is required
