Siamese

1. ADJ

/ saɪəˈmiz / (AmE), / saɪəˈmiːz / (BrE)
of or relating to Siam (the former name of Thailand), or its people, language, or culture.
relating to Siam (Thailand) or its people
archaic

Examples

She wore a traditional Siamese silk dress for the cultural exhibition.
Many antique maps label the region as Siamese territory instead of Thai.
The Siamese language is now officially known as Thai.
