trumpetwood

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌmpɪtˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪtwʊd / (BrE)
a tropical American tree with large, shield-shaped leaves and hollow stems, often belonging to the genus Cecropia or similar genera
tropical American tree with large leaves and hollow stems

Examples

The dense foliage of the trumpetwood provides shelter for many rainforest animals.
Researchers studied the rapid growth of trumpetwood in disturbed areas of the forest.
Locals use the hollow stems of trumpetwood to make simple instruments and tools.
