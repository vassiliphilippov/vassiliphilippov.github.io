tsarist

1. ADJ

/ ˈzɑrɪst / (AmE), / ˈzɑːrɪst / (BrE)
relating to, characteristic of, or supporting a tsar or the system of government ruled by a tsar, especially in Russia before 1917.
relating to a tsar or tsarist system

Examples

The tsarist regime was overthrown during the Russian Revolution of 1917.
He specializes in the study of tsarist architecture and Russian history.
Many families fled the country to escape tsarist persecution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: czarist - relating to a czar or system of government under a czar

Etymology

From Russian tsar (czar) + English -ist.
