sore-eyed

1. ADJ

/ sɔr aɪd / (AmE), / sɔː aɪd / (BrE)
having sore or inflamed eyes, especially due to conjunctivitis or irritation
having sore or inflamed eyes

Examples

She was clearly sore-eyed after a night of crying and rubbing her face.
The doctor diagnosed him as sore-eyed and prescribed eye drops for relief.
After swimming in the chlorinated pool, many children became sore-eyed and uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conjunctivitis - inflammation of the conjunctiva of the eye
