Saul Steinberg

1. PROPN

/ sɔl ˈstaɪnˌbɜːrɡ / (AmE), / sɔːl ˈstaɪnbɜːɡ / (BrE)
(1914–1999) a United States cartoonist and illustrator, born in Romania, known for his satirical caricatures and contributions to The New Yorker magazine.
Romanian-born American cartoonist and illustrator

Examples

Saul Steinberg was renowned for his imaginative covers for The New Yorker.
An exhibition of Saul Steinberg's work attracted visitors from around the world.
Many artists have cited Saul Steinberg as an influence on their own approach to cartooning.
