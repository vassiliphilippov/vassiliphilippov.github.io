Nenets

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛnɛts / (AmE), / ˈnɛnɪts / (BrE)
the Uralic language spoken by the Nenets people of northern Siberia
Uralic language of northern Siberia

Examples

Linguists have studied the structure of Nenets to understand its relationship with other Uralic languages.
He can speak some Nenets, having worked with the indigenous people in the Arctic region.
A collection of folktales has been translated from Nenets into Russian and English.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Samoyedic - branch of Uralic languages to which Nenets belongs

Etymology

From Russian Ненец (Nenets), the self-designation of the people.
