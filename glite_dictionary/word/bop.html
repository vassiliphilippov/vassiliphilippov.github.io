BoP

1. NOUN

/ biː oʊ piː / (AmE), / biː əʊ piː / (BrE)
abbreviation for the Bureau of Prisons, the agency of the US Department of Justice responsible for operating federal prisons and detention facilities.
US federal prison agency (abbreviation)
legal american

Examples

After being convicted of a federal crime, he was transferred into the custody of the BoP.
The BoP oversees the operation of all federal prisons in the United States.
New reforms within the BoP aim to improve inmate rehabilitation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bureau of Prisons - the agency responsible for operating US federal prisons

Etymology

Acronym formed from Bureau of Prisons.
