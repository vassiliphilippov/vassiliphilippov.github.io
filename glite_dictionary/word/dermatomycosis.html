dermatomycosis

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɜːrmətoʊmaɪˈkoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌdɜːmətəʊmaɪˈkəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a fungal infection of the skin, especially in moist areas covered by clothing
fungal infection of the skin
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with dermatomycosis after developing a persistent rash in the folds of his skin.
Athletes are at higher risk of contracting dermatomycosis due to excessive sweating and tight clothing.
Treatment for dermatomycosis usually involves the application of topical antifungal creams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ringworm - a common fungal infection of the skin; synonym of dermatomycosis

Etymology

From Greek 'derma' (skin) + 'mykes' (fungus) + '-osis' (condition).
