hosta

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊstə / (AmE), / ˈhɒstə / (BrE)
A perennial herbaceous plant with broad leaves and spikes of flowers, native to East Asia and commonly grown as a garden ornamental; also known as plantain lily.
an ornamental perennial plant (plantain lily)

Examples

The shady border was lined with hosta in varying shades of green and blue.
She added a new variety of hosta to her perennial garden this spring.
The large leaves of the hosta provide excellent ground cover and suppress weeds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plantain lily - another name for hosta, an ornamental garden plant

Etymology

Modern Latin, from Austrian botanist Nicolaus Thomas Host (1761–1834).
