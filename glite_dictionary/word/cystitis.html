cystitis

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈstaɪtɪs / (AmE), / sɪsˈtaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the urinary bladder, often causing pain and frequent urination
inflammation of the bladder
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with cystitis after she complained of painful urination.
Symptoms of cystitis include frequent urges to urinate and discomfort in the lower abdomen.
Drinking plenty of water may help alleviate the symptoms of cystitis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bladder infection - infection of the urinary bladder, often resulting in cystitis
  • Degree Lower: urinary tract infection - infection in any part of the urinary system, of which cystitis is a subtype

Etymology

From Greek 'kystis' (bladder) + '-itis' (inflammation).
