coleus

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊ.li.əs / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.li.əs / (BrE)
a tropical plant of the genus Coleus, cultivated for its brightly colored decorative leaves and spikes of blue flowers
decorative tropical plant with colorful leaves

Examples

She planted a coleus in the garden to add color to the shady area.
The vibrant leaves of the coleus make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor displays.
During the summer, her coleus thrives on the porch with its brilliant foliage.

Etymology

From modern Latin Coleus, the genus name, from Greek koleos (sheath).
