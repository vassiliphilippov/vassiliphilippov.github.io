Mam

1. NOUN

/ mɑm / (AmE), / mɑːm / (BrE)
A member of a Mayan people living primarily in southwestern Guatemala and adjacent regions.
a Mayan people of Guatemala

Examples

Many Mam have preserved their language and traditional customs.
The Mam community celebrates several traditional festivals each year.
Researchers visited villages where the Mam people still maintain ancient agricultural practices.

2. NOUN

/ mɑm / (AmE), / mam / (BrE)
a Mayan language spoken by the Mam people in Guatemala and parts of Mexico.
a Mayan language of Guatemala and Mexico

Examples

Linguists are working on a project to preserve the Mam language among younger generations.
She speaks both Spanish and Mam fluently.
Radio programs are broadcast in Mam to reach indigenous communities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mayan - the language family to which Mam belongs

Etymology

From the name of the Mam people, an indigenous group in Central America.
