lipfern

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪpˌfɝn / (AmE), / ˈlɪpˌfəːn / (BrE)
Any of various terrestrial ferns of the genus Cheilanthes, found in arid and semiarid regions worldwide.
terrestrial fern of the genus Cheilanthes

Examples

The lipfern thrives on rocky slopes in arid landscapes.
Several species of lipfern can be found across North and South America.
She carefully identified the lipfern growing among the stones.
