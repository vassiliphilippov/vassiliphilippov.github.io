non-resinous

1. ADJ

/ nɑn ˈrɛzənəs / (AmE), / nɒn ˈrɛzɪnəs / (BrE)
Not containing or producing resin.
not having resin

Examples

Pine is a resinous wood, while oak is usually considered non-resinous.
For this experiment, use only non-resinous materials to avoid contamination.
The craftsman prefers non-resinous woods for fine furniture making.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: resinous - containing or producing resin

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) and 'resinous'.
