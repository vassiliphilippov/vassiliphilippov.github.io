kickshaw

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪkˌʃɔ / (AmE), / ˈkɪkʃɔː / (BrE)
a delicacy or fancy dish; something considered choice to eat
delicacy; fancy dish
archaic

Examples

At the banquet, the table was laden with every sort of kickshaw imaginable.
She rarely indulged in kickshaws, preferring plain and simple fare.
The chef prided himself on creating unique kickshaw for discerning guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delicacy - a choice or expensive food item

Etymology

From French 'quelque chose' (something), altered in English.
