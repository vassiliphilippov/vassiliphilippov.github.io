kittenish

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɪtənɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɪtənɪʃ / (BrE)
(of a person or their manner) playfully lively or flirtatious, like a kitten.
playfully lively or flirtatious

Examples

She gave him a kittenish grin before darting away.
His kittenish behavior at the party charmed all the guests.
The actress is known for her kittenish wit and playful personality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: playful - full of fun and high spirits
  • Degree Lower: frivolous - not serious; playful or light-hearted

Etymology

From 'kitten' + '-ish', formed by analogy with words like 'boyish' or 'puppyish'.
