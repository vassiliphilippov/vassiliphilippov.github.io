listlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈlɪstlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈlɪstlɪsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows little or no energy, interest, or enthusiasm.
without energy or enthusiasm

Examples

She stared listlessly out the window, barely noticing the rain.
After a long day, he spoke listlessly to his friends.
The students moved listlessly through the crowded hallway.

Synset Examples

they shook hands rather listlessly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: languidly - in a slow, relaxed, or weak manner
  • Antonym: energetically - in a lively or vigorous manner

Etymology

Formed from 'listless' + '-ly'. 'Listless' comes from 'list' (desire) + '-less'.
