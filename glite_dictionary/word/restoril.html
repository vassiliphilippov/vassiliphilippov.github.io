Restoril

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛstəˌrɪl / (AmE), / ˈrɛstəˌrɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked name for temazepam, a benzodiazepine medication used to treat insomnia, especially for people who wake frequently during the night.
trade name for a sleep medication (temazepam)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Restoril to help with her persistent insomnia.
After taking Restoril, he was able to sleep through the night without waking up.
Patients are advised to use Restoril only as directed due to its risk of dependence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: temazepam - a benzodiazepine drug used for treating insomnia
