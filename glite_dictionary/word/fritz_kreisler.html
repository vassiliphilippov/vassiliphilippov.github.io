Fritz Kreisler

1. PROPN

/ frɪts ˈkraɪslɚ / (AmE), / frɪts ˈkraɪslə / (BrE)
(1875–1962) An Austrian-born American violinist, regarded as one of the greatest virtuosos of the early 20th century.
Austrian-American violin virtuoso (1875–1962)

Examples

Many critics consider Fritz Kreisler one of the finest violinists of the twentieth century.
The music of Fritz Kreisler remains popular among violinists today.
Fritz Kreisler was celebrated for his expressive style and distinctive tone.
