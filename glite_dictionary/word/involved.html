involved

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈvɑlvd / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɒlvd / (BrE)
complicated, entangled, or hindered as if caught up in difficulties or confusion
complicated or entangled; hindered by difficulties

Examples

The legal dispute became more involved as new evidence emerged.
Her explanation was so involved that nobody could follow it clearly.
The plot of the novel grew increasingly involved with each new chapter.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: complicated - difficult to analyze or understand

Examples
