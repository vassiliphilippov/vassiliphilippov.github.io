spermatic

1. ADJ

/ spərˈmætɪk / (AmE), / spɜːˈmætɪk / (BrE)
relating to, consisting of, or resembling spermatozoa (sperm cells)
relating to spermatozoa (sperm cells)
medical

Examples

The doctor noted a spermatic structure inside the sample under the microscope.
Rare spermatic cysts can form in the male reproductive system.
The biologist studied spermatic movement for her research on fertility.

Etymology

From Greek spermatikos ('of seed, spermatic'), from sperma ('seed').
