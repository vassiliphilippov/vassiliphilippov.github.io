comprehensible

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛnsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnsəbl / (BrE)
able to be understood; intelligible to the mind
able to be understood

Examples

The professor explained the theory in a comprehensible way for all students.
His handwriting is hardly comprehensible to anyone except himself.
They used simple language to make the report comprehensible to a wider audience.

Synset Examples

an idea comprehensible to the average mind

Related Terms

  • Antonym: incomprehensible - not able to be understood; impossible to comprehend
  • Synonym: understandable - able to be understood; intelligible

Etymology

From Latin 'comprehensibilis', from 'comprehendere' (to grasp, understand).
