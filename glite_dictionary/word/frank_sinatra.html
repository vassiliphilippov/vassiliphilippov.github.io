Frank Sinatra

1. PROPN

/ fræŋk səˈnɑtrə / (AmE), / fræŋk sɪˈnɑːtrə / (BrE)
(1915–1998) An American singer and film actor known for his smooth voice and influential contributions to popular music and cinema.
famous American singer and actor

Examples

Frank Sinatra was often referred to as 'Ol' Blue Eyes' for his strikingly blue eyes.
Many jazz critics regard Frank Sinatra as one of the greatest American vocalists of the 20th century.
She played her favorite Frank Sinatra album during the dinner party.
