thermometer

1. NOUN

/ θərˈmɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / θəˈmɒmɪtə / (BrE)
An instrument for measuring and indicating temperature.
instrument for measuring temperature

Examples

He checked the patient's temperature with a thermometer.
The outdoor thermometer dropped below freezing last night.
Before baking, she used a thermometer to make sure the oven was hot enough.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hygrometer - instrument for measuring humidity
  • Synonym: barometer - instrument for measuring atmospheric pressure

Etymology

From Greek thermos (hot) + metron (measure).
