dosed

1. ADJ

/ doʊst / (AmE), / dəʊst / (BrE)
treated, covered, or impregnated with a particular substance or application.
treated or covered with a substance

Examples

The vegetables were dosed with a mild pesticide before being shipped.
Her handkerchief was dosed with essential oils to help her breathe better.
The fabric had been dosed with a special water-resistant coating.

Synset Examples

a mustache dosed with bear grease

Related Terms

  • Synonym: treated - having had a substance applied for a particular effect
  • Antonym: contaminated - impregnated with an unwanted substance

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'dose', from French *dose*, from Greek *dosis* (a giving, gift, dose).
