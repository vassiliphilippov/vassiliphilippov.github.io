CV

1. NOUN

/ siː ˈviː / (AmE), / siː ˈviː / (BrE)
a written summary of a person's education, qualifications, and previous jobs, often sent with a job application
summary of work and education history

Examples

Please attach your CV and cover letter to the job application.
She updated her CV before applying for the new position.
Most employers will ask for a CV to review your qualifications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: résumé - a similar document listing a person's work experience and skills, especially in US English

Etymology

Abbreviation of Latin 'curriculum vitae' (course of life).
