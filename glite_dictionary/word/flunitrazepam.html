flunitrazepam

1. NOUN

/ fluˌnɪˈtræzəˌpæm / (AmE), / fluˌnɪˈtræzəˌpam / (BrE)
A powerful sedative and tranquilizer, sometimes known by the trade name Rohypnol, used medically to treat insomnia but also misused illicitly.
powerful sedative and tranquilizer drug
medical

Examples

The police found traces of flunitrazepam in the suspect’s vehicle.
Doctors warn about the abuse potential of flunitrazepam outside medical supervision.
Flunitrazepam is banned in many countries due to its role in drug-facilitated crimes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Rohypnol - Trade name for flunitrazepam, a sedative and hypnotic drug

Etymology

From chemical nomenclature: 'fluor-' (fluorine), 'nitra-' (nitro group), '-zepam' (from benzodiazepine).
