currishly

1. ADV

/ ˈkɜr.ɪʃ.li / (AmE), / ˈkʌr.ɪʃ.li / (BrE)
In a mean-spirited or base manner; ignobly.
in a mean-spirited manner
archaic

Examples

He spoke currishly about his colleagues instead of showing respect.
The villain currishly betrayed his closest friend for money.
In the debate, she attacked her opponent currishly, with unwarranted insults.

Synset Examples

he behaved ignobly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ignobly - in a dishonorable or shameful manner
  • Antonym: nobly - in a virtuous, honorable manner

Etymology

From 'currish' + '-ly'. 'Currish' from 'cur' (a contemptible person or dog) + '-ish'.
