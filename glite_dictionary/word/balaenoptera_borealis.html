Balaenoptera borealis

1. NOUN

/ bəˌliːnəpˈtɛrə bɔːˈraɪəlɪs / (AmE), / bəˌliːnɒpˈtɛərə bɔːˈraɪəlɪs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the sei whale, a large marine mammal similar to but smaller than the finback whale.
scientific name for the sei whale

Examples

The Balaenoptera borealis is known for its speed and distinctive appearance.
Scientists observed a group of Balaenoptera borealis migrating along the coast.
Unlike the fin whale, the Balaenoptera borealis tends to travel alone or in small groups.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sei whale - A large species of baleen whale; common name for Balaenoptera borealis
  • Degree Higher: finback whale - A closely related but larger baleen whale

Etymology

From Latin: 'balaena' (whale) + 'ptera' (fin) and 'borealis' (northern).
