pea-chick

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːˌtʃɪk / (AmE), / ˈpiːˌtʃɪk / (BrE)
A young peafowl, especially a chick of either the Indian or green peafowl.
young peafowl

Examples

The zoo welcomed several adorable pea-chicks this spring.
A pea-chick relies on its mother for warmth and protection.
We watched the tiny pea-chick explore the grass for the first time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peafowl - a large bird, the peacock or peahen

Etymology

From 'pea-' (from 'peafowl') + 'chick'.
