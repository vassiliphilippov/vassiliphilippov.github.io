Tbilisi

1. PROPN

/ təˈbiːlisi / (AmE), / təˈbiːlisi / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Georgia, located on the Kura River in the southeastern part of the country
capital city of Georgia (country)

Examples

I visited Tbilisi last summer and was impressed by its unique architecture and vibrant culture.
The government of Georgia is based in Tbilisi.
Many international flights to Georgia land at Tbilisi International Airport.

Etymology

From Georgian თბილისი (Tbilisi), meaning 'warm place', referring to the area’s hot springs.
