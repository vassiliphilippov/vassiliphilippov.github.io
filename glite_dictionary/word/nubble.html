nubble

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌbəl / (AmE), / ˈnʌbəl / (BrE)
a small lump or protuberance; a small rounded projection or bump.
small lump or bump

Examples

The old sweater had a nubble on its sleeve.
She ran her fingers over the rough nubble on the wall.
A tiny nubble formed where the tree branch had been cut.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lump - a compact mass of a substance, often with no definite or regular shape
  • Synonym: protuberance - something that protrudes from a surface; a bulge or swelling

Etymology

Probably a diminutive of 'nub' (lump or knob).
