Palaemon

1. NOUN

/ pəˈliːmən / (AmE), / pəˈleɪmən / (BrE)
A genus of shrimps in the family Palaemonidae, widely distributed in marine and freshwater environments.
genus of shrimps (Palaemonidae)

Examples

Species in the genus Palaemon are found in both freshwater and marine habitats.
Researchers studied the population dynamics of Palaemon in the local estuary.
The Palaemon shrimp is important for ecological balance in its natural environment.

Etymology

From Latin Palaemon, a name in Greek mythology, used in zoological taxonomy.
