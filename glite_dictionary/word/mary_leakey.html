Mary Leakey

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɛri ˈliːki / (AmE), / ˈmɛəri ˈliːki / (BrE)
(1913–1996) English paleontologist famous for discovering the Zinjanthropus skull and making significant contributions to the study of early human evolution.
English paleontologist, discovered Zinjanthropus skull

Examples

Mary Leakey made one of the most important discoveries in the history of paleoanthropology.
The Zinjanthropus skull was unearthed by Mary Leakey at Olduvai Gorge in 1959.
As a pioneering paleontologist, Mary Leakey helped shape our understanding of early hominids.
