off

1. ADJ

/ ɔf / (AmE), / ɒf / (BrE)
(of a machine, device, or system) not operating, turned or switched to a non-functional state.
not operating; switched off

Examples

The air conditioner is off because no one is home.
She noticed the TV was off when she entered the room.
Please turn the computer off before leaving.

Synset Examples

the oven is off
the lights are off

Related Terms

  • Antonym: on - in operation or functioning (of a device, machine, etc.)
