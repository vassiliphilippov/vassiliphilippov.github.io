cursedly

1. ADV

/ ˈkɜrsɪdli / (AmE), / ˈkɜːsɪdli / (BrE)
In an extremely annoying, unpleasant, or damnable manner.
in a damnable or irritating way
archaic

Examples

He can be cursedly stubborn when he sets his mind to something.
The weather has been cursedly cold all week, making it hard to go outside.
She found the old house cursedly difficult to heat in winter.

Synset Examples

kindly Arthur — so damnably, politely, endlessly persistent!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: damnably - in a manner deserving condemnation or irritation

Etymology

From 'cursed' + '-ly', with 'cursed' from Middle English, originally past participle of 'curse'.
