booby

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuːbi / (AmE), / ˈbuːbi / (BrE)
A stupid, ignorant, or foolish person.
an ignorant or foolish person

Examples

Everyone thought he was a booby for believing such an obvious lie.
Don't be a booby; double-check your answers before submitting.
She laughed at her own mistake, calling herself a booby in jest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - a person lacking sense or judgment
  • Synonym: simpleton - a person lacking in common sense
  • Antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence or ability

Etymology

Perhaps from Spanish 'bobo' meaning simple or foolish.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbuːbi / (AmE), / ˈbuːbi / (BrE)
a large tropical seabird of the gannet family, noted for its brightly colored bill or feet, found chiefly in warm ocean regions
tropical seabird with bright bill or feet

Examples

The blue-footed booby is famous for its strikingly colored feet and humorous courtship dance.
We spotted a flock of boobies nesting on the rocky island during our boat tour.
The booby dived gracefully into the sea to catch fish for its chicks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gannet - a large seabird related to the booby, with predominantly white plumage

Etymology

From Spanish 'bobo' meaning 'stupid' or 'fool', likely from the bird's unwary nature.
