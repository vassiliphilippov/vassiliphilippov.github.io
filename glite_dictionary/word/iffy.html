iffy

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪfi / (AmE), / ˈɪfi / (BrE)
uncertain, unreliable, or dependent on chance; not definite or guaranteed
uncertain or unreliable
informal

Examples

Our picnic plans are iffy because of the unpredictable weather.
He gave me an iffy answer, so I'm not sure if he'll show up.
