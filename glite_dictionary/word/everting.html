everting

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈvɝtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈvɜːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of turning something inside out
turning inside out
medical

Examples

The surgeon explained that everting the eyelid helps to examine the inner surface.
In some animals, everting the stomach is used as a defense mechanism.

Related Terms

Etymology

From Latin 'evertere' (to turn out).
