Clitocybe nuda

1. NOUN

/ klɪtəˌsaɪbi ˈnudə / (AmE), / klɪtəˌsaɪbi ˈnjuːdə / (BrE)
An edible species of wild mushroom, typically pale lilac when young, with a smooth, moist cap, also known as the wood blewit.
a species of edible wild mushroom

Examples

The seasoned forager easily identified the Clitocybe nuda growing beneath the fallen leaves.
After sautéing the Clitocybe nuda, she served it with fresh herbs and garlic.
Always be cautious when harvesting wild mushrooms, even familiar ones like Clitocybe nuda.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wood blewit - common name for Clitocybe nuda, an edible wild mushroom

Etymology

Scientific name from Latin; 'Clitocybe' from Greek klitos (sloping) and kybe (head), 'nuda' meaning naked.
