Gloria Steinem

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡlɔːriə ˈstaɪnəm / (AmE), / ˈɡlɔːriə ˈstaɪnəm / (BrE)
(born 1934) A prominent American feminist, journalist, and social-political activist noted for her leadership in the women's liberation movement.
American feminist and activist

Examples

Gloria Steinem played a key role in the women's liberation movement in the 1970s.
Many young activists cite Gloria Steinem as a major inspiration for their work.
