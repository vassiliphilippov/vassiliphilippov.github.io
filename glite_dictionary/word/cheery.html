cheery

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɪri / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪəri / (BrE)
bright and pleasant, making people feel cheerful or happy
bright and pleasant; promoting cheerfulness

Examples

She always gives a cheery wave to her neighbors in the morning.
The room felt much more cheery after they painted it a bright yellow.
He tried to maintain a cheery tone throughout the difficult conversation.

Synset Examples

a cheery hello
a gay sunny room
a sunny smile

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheerful - feeling or showing happiness and optimism

Etymology

Formed from 'cheer' + '-y', 19th century.
