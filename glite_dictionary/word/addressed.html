addressed

1. ADJ

/ əˈdrɛst / (AmE), / əˈdrɛst / (BrE)
(of mail) marked with the name and place of the intended recipient.
of mail: marked with a recipient's details

Examples

Please ensure every envelope is addressed before putting it in the outbox.
Any addressed letters will be sent out this afternoon.
She discarded all addressed mail that was not meant for her.

Synset Examples

I throw away all mail addressed to ‘resident’

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unaddressed - (of mail) not marked with a destination

Etymology

From the past participle of the verb 'address', of French and Latin origin.
