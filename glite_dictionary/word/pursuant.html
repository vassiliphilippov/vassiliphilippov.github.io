pursuant

1. ADJ

/ pərˈsuːənt / (AmE), / pəˈsjʊənt / (BrE)
in accordance with or as a result of (especially in the phrase 'pursuant to')
in accordance with; following
legal

Examples

Pursuant to the new regulations, all applicants must provide proof of identity.
The contract was terminated pursuant to clause 17.
All amendments shall be made pursuant to this agreement.

Synset Examples

pursuant to our agreement
pursuant to the dictates of one's conscience

Related Terms

  • Synonym: according to - in agreement with; as stated by

Etymology

From Anglo-French 'pursuant', present participle of Old French 'poursuivre' (to pursue).
