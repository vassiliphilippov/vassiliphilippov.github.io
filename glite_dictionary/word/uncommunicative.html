uncommunicative

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.kəˈmjunɪˌkeɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kəˈmjuː.nɪ.kə.tɪv / (BrE)
not willing or inclined to talk, share information, or express opinions.
not willing to communicate

Examples

Although he was present at the meeting, Tom remained uncommunicative throughout.
After the accident, she became noticeably withdrawn and uncommunicative.
The witness appeared nervous and uncommunicative during questioning.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: talkative - willing and fond of talking

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'communicative'.
