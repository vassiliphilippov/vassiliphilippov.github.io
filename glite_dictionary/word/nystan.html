Nystan

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪstæn / (AmE), / ˈnaɪstæn / (BrE)
A trademarked antifungal and antibiotic medication containing nystatin, used to treat fungal infections.
antifungal and antibiotic drug (trade name)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Nystan for the patient's oral thrush infection.
After taking Nystan, her symptoms improved within a few days.
Nystan is often used to treat fungal infections in immunocompromised patients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nystatin - an antifungal medication; active ingredient in Nystan
  • Synonym: Mycostatin - another trade name for nystatin
