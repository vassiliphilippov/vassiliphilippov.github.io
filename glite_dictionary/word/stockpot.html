stockpot

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑkˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈstɒkˌpɒt / (BrE)
a large, deep pot used for preparing soup stock, stews, or boiling foods
large pot for making stock or soups

Examples

She simmered bones and vegetables in the stockpot to make a rich broth.
Every professional kitchen has a large stockpot for preparing soups and stews.
The recipe calls for all the ingredients to be placed together in a stockpot and slowly cooked for hours.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saucepan - a deep pan with a long handle, used for cooking
  • Degree Higher: cauldron - a large metal pot for cooking over an open fire
