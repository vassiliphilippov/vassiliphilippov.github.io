nut-bearing

1. ADJ

/ ˈnʌt ˌbɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnʌt ˌbeərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of shrubs or trees) producing nuts; having the ability to yield nuts as fruit.
producing nuts (of plants)

Examples

The nut-bearing trees in the orchard provide food for wildlife each autumn.
Many species of squirrels rely on nut-bearing shrubs and trees for survival.
Investing in nut-bearing plants can add value and biodiversity to your garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nut-producing - producing nuts; yielding nuts as fruit
