classroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæsˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsˌruːm / (BrE)
a room in a school or other educational institution where lessons are taught
room where lessons are taught

Examples

The students quietly filed into the classroom before the bell rang.
Each classroom is equipped with a smartboard and internet access.
She decorated her classroom with colorful posters for her new students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lecture hall - a large room used for lectures, especially in a university
  • Synonym: laboratory - a room equipped for scientific experiments or research in a school

Etymology

Formed from 'class' + 'room', first recorded in late 18th century.
