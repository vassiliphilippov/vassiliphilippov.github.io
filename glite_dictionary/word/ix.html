IX

1. NUM

/ aɪ ˈɛks / (AmE), / aɪ ˈɛks / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for the number nine
Roman numeral for nine

Examples

The clock struck IX just as the meeting began.
The king's reign ended in the year IX.
He wore a jersey with the Roman numeral IX on the back.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nine - the number 9

Etymology

From Latin "IX", combining "I" (one) placed before "X" (ten) to indicate one less than ten.
