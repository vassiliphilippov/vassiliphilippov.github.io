shutting

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃʌtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃʌtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of closing or causing something to close
the act of closing something

Examples

The shutting of the factory's heavy doors echoed throughout the building.
Sudden shutting of the window startled the cat on the sill.
Her shutting of the book signaled the end of the conversation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: closing - the act of making something shut
