paradisaical

1. ADJ

/ ˌpærəˈdaɪzɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌparəˈdʌɪsɪəkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Paradise; heavenly or idyllic.
relating to Paradise; heavenly

Examples

The garden was a paradisaical retreat, filled with rare flowers and the soothing sound of water.
They spent their honeymoon on a paradisaical tropical island far from the city.
Moments of paradisaical tranquility are rare in such a busy life.

Synset Examples

together in that paradisal place
paradisiacal innocence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paradisiacal - Relating to or resembling Paradise; alternate spelling.

Etymology

From Paradise + English adjectival suffix -ical.
