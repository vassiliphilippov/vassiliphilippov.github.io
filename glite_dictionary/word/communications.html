communications

1. NOUN

/ kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃənz / (AmE), / kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃənz / (BrE)
the study of the methods and principles involved in transmitting information, especially by devices such as print, radio, or television
study of information transmission methods

Examples

He decided to pursue a degree in communications to learn about media and information technology.
The university's communications department offers courses in journalism, broadcasting, and digital media.
Rapid advances in communications have transformed the way people share information worldwide.

Synset Examples

communications is his major field of study

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communication - the act of conveying information or expressing ideas
