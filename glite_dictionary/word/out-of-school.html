out-of-school

1. ADJ

/ ˌaʊt əv ˈskuːl / (AmE), / ˌaʊt əv ˈskuːl / (BrE)
not attending school, especially referring to children or young people who are no longer in or do not go to school
not attending school; free from school attendance

Examples

The charity provides training programs for out-of-school youth.
Many out-of-school children work to support their families.
Efforts are being made to reintegrate out-of-school students into the education system.

Synset Examples

opportunities for out-of-school youth

Related Terms

  • Antonym: in-school - currently attending school or enrolled in an educational institution
