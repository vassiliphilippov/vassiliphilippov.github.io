Sevastopol

1. PROPN

/ səˌvæstəˈpoʊl / (AmE), / sɪˌvæstəˈpɒl / (BrE)
a port city in southern Ukraine, located on the Black Sea, with strategic military and historical significance
port city in southern Ukraine on the Black Sea

Examples

The Russian navy has maintained a base in Sevastopol for many years.
The defense of Sevastopol during World War II is well known in military history.
Many tourists visit Sevastopol to see its historical monuments and beautiful coastline.

Etymology

From Russian Севастополь (Sevastopol').
