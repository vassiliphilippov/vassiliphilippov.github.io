allocatable

1. ADJ

/ əˈlɑːk.əˌteɪ.bəl / (AmE), / əˈlɒk.əˌteɪ.bəl / (BrE)
able to be allocated or distributed for a particular purpose
able to be allocated or distributed

Examples

The company must ensure that enough allocatable resources are available to support the new project.
All seats in the theater are allocatable according to the ticketing system.
In cloud computing, storage capacity is often allocatable on demand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distributable - able to be distributed

Etymology

From 'allocate' + '-able', with 'allocate' from Latin 'allocare' (to assign).
