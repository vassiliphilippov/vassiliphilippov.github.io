riverside

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪvərˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɪvəsaɪd / (BrE)
the land or area along the edge of a river
the bank or edge of a river

Examples

We enjoyed a picnic by the riverside on a sunny afternoon.
There is a beautiful path that follows the riverside for several miles.
Artists often gather at the riverside to paint the scenic views.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: riverbank - the sloping ground beside a river
