modiste

1. NOUN

/ moʊˈdiːst / (AmE), / məʊˈdiːst / (BrE)
a woman who makes or sells fashionable dresses and hats
woman who makes or sells dresses and hats

Examples

The famous Parisian modiste designed an exquisite gown for the queen.
After working as a modiste for years, she opened her own boutique.
The elegant hat was the creation of a skilled modiste in Milan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressmaker - a person who makes custom clothing for women
  • Synonym: milliner - a person who makes or sells women's hats

Etymology

From French 'modiste', from 'mode' meaning fashion.

2. NOUN

/ moʊˈdiːst / (AmE), / məʊˈdiːst / (BrE)
a person, especially a woman, who makes and sells fashionable hats or clothing, particularly for women
maker and seller of hats or fashionable clothes

Examples

The celebrated modiste designed hats for many of Paris's most fashionable women.
After apprenticing with a skilled modiste, she opened her own hat shop.
In the novel, the heroine consults a renowned modiste before the grand ball.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milliner - someone who makes or sells women's hats

Etymology

From French 'modiste', from 'mode' (fashion).
