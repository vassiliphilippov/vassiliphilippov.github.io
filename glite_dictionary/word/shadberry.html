shadberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃædˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈʃadˌbɛri / (BrE)
An edible purple or red berry, especially that of the serviceberry tree (genus Amelanchier).
edible berry of the serviceberry tree

Examples

The jam was made from fresh shadberries picked in the forest.
Every spring, the shadberry bushes bloom with small white flowers before fruiting.
Birds love to eat the shadberries that ripen in early summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: serviceberry - a North American tree or shrub bearing edible fruit, also known as 'shadberry'
