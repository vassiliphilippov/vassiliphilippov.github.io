cyclist

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪkləst / (AmE), / ˈsaɪklɪst / (BrE)
a person who rides a bicycle, especially as a sport or means of transport
person who rides a bicycle

Examples

The city has built special lanes for cyclists to improve road safety.
As an avid cyclist, she rides over 100 miles each week.
During the race, one cyclist broke away from the main group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bicyclist - a person who rides a bicycle
  • Antonym: driver - a person who drives a motor vehicle

Etymology

From cycle + -ist, formed in English in the late 19th century.
