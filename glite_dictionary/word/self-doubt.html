self-doubt

1. NOUN

/ sɛlf ˈdaʊt / (AmE), / sɛlf ˈdaʊt / (BrE)
Lack of confidence in oneself and one's abilities or decisions.
lack of confidence in oneself

Examples

Her self-doubt made her hesitate before speaking up in meetings.
Struggling with self-doubt is common when starting a new job.
Overcoming self-doubt is an important step in building self-confidence.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: confidence - the feeling or belief that one can rely on oneself or something; self-assurance

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'doubt'.
