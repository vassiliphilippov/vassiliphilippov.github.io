panchayet

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌn.tʃəˌjɛt / (AmE), / ˈpæn.tʃəˌjɛt / (BrE)
a local village council in India or southern Pakistan, responsible for administering village affairs and resolving disputes
village council in South Asia

Examples

The panchayet met to resolve a boundary dispute between neighboring villages.
In rural India, important decisions are often made by the panchayet.
She was elected as the first woman head of her village panchayet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: panchayat - alternative spelling for a village council in India or Pakistan

Etymology

From Hindi 'pañcāyat', from Sanskrit 'pañc' (five), originally referring to a council of five elders.
