sampan

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæmˌpæn / (AmE), / ˈsæm.pæn / (BrE)
a small, flat-bottomed Asian boat, typically with a shelter, propelled by oars or sometimes a sail
small Asian wooden boat

Examples

The fishermen set out early each morning in their sampan to catch fish in the river.
Tourists marveled at how gracefully the sampan moved through the busy harbor.
She watched as a man rowed his sampan across the misty lake at dawn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skiff - a small, light boat

Etymology

From Chinese (Cantonese) saam^1 baan^2, literally 'three boards'.
