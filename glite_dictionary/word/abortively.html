abortively

1. ADV

/ əˈbɔrtɪvli / (AmE), / əˈbɔːtɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner that fails to produce the intended result; unsuccessfully or fruitlessly.
in an unsuccessful manner

Examples

Their attempts to solve the problem ended abortively due to a lack of cooperation.
The expedition proceeded abortively, failing to reach its intended destination.
Negotiations collapsed abortively without any agreement being reached.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fruitlessly - without producing any result; in vain
  • Antonym: successfully - in a way that achieves the intended result

Etymology

From 'abortive' (from Latin abortivus, relating to an untimely birth) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
