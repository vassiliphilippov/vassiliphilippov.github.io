resupine

1. ADJ

/ ˌriːˈsuːpaɪn / (AmE), / ˌriːˈsjuːpaɪn / (BrE)
Lying face upward; positioned with the back down and face or front upward.
lying face upward

Examples

The patient was positioned resupine on the examination table before the procedure.
After fainting, he lay resupine beneath the tree, staring at the sky.
The yoga instructor demonstrated a resupine posture for relaxation at the end of class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supine - lying on the back, face upward

Etymology

From Latin resupinus, from re- (back) + supinus (lying on the back).
