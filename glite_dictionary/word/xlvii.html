xlvii

1. NUM

/ ɛks ɛl vɪ ˈaɪ / (AmE), / ɛks ɛl vɪ ˈaɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral representing the number 47
Roman numeral for 47

Examples

The page was marked with the Roman numeral xlvii at the bottom.
Chapter xlvii discusses the final steps in the experiment.
The inscription on the monument included the year as xlvii.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 47 - the cardinal number forty-seven
  • Degree Lower: xlvi - Roman numeral for 46
  • Degree Higher: xlviii - Roman numeral for 48

Etymology

From Latin, where 'XL' represents 40 (50 minus 10) and 'VII' represents 7.
