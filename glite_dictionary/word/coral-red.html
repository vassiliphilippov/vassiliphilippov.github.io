coral-red

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɔrəl ˌrɛd / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəl ˌrɛd / (BrE)
Of a red color tinged with the pink or orange hue of coral.
red tinged with coral pink or orange

Examples

She painted her nails a bold coral-red to match her summer dress.
The sunset cast a beautiful coral-red glow over the ocean.
She bought a lipstick in her favorite coral-red shade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crimson - a deep red color
  • Degree Higher: scarlet - a bright red color with a slightly orange tinge

Etymology

Named after the color of coral, a marine organism; 'coral' from Latin 'corallium', plus 'red'.
