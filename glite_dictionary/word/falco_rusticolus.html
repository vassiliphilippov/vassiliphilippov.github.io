Falco rusticolus

1. NOUN

/ ˌfælkoʊ ˌrʌstɪˈkoʊləs / (AmE), / ˌfæl.kəʊ ˌrʌs.tɪˈkəʊ.ləs / (BrE)
a large and rare falcon of the Arctic, known as the gyrfalcon, with both white and dark color phases
large Arctic falcon (gyrfalcon)

Examples

The Falco rusticolus is prized by falconers for its power and speed.
Researchers tracked the migration patterns of Falco rusticolus across the Arctic tundra.
A Falco rusticolus in its white color phase is a striking sight against the snow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gyrfalcon - another common name for Falco rusticolus, the largest falcon species

Etymology

From Latin: 'falco' (falcon) and 'rusticolus' (country-dwelling).
