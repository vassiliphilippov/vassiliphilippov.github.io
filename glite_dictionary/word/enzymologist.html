enzymologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛn.zaɪˈmɑː.lə.dʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɛn.zɪˈmɒl.ə.dʒɪst / (BrE)
A scientist who specializes in the study of enzymes and their properties, functions, and reactions.
scientist specializing in enzymes
medical

Examples

The research team included an expert enzymologist to analyze protein activity.
As an enzymologist, Dr. Patel investigates how enzymes facilitate metabolic reactions.
Many breakthroughs in drug development are thanks to the work of dedicated enzymologists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biochemist - a scientist who studies the chemical processes in living things

Etymology

From enzyme + -logist, modeled after analogous terms in biology.
