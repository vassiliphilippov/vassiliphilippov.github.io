saunterer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔn.tər.ər / (AmE), / ˈsɔːn.tər.ə / (BrE)
a person who walks at a slow, relaxed pace
person who walks leisurely

Examples

The saunterer enjoyed the blooming gardens as he moved along the path.
In the city park, a lone saunterer watched the world go by.
Unlike hurried commuters, a saunterer takes time to enjoy the scenery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stroller - a person who walks in a slow, relaxed manner
  • Synonym: walker - a person who walks, often at any pace

Etymology

From verb 'saunter' (to walk in a leisurely manner).
