phenotypical

1. ADJ

/ ˌfiːnəˈtɪpɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌfiːnəˈtɪpɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a phenotype; observable traits or characteristics of an organism as influenced by the environment and genetic makeup.
relating to a phenotype

Examples

Scientists analyzed the phenotypical differences between wild and domesticated animals.
The disease is associated with specific phenotypical traits in the affected population.
Researchers create phenotypical profiles to better understand genetic variations.

Synset Examples

phenotypical profile

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phenotypic - relating to or denoting the observable characteristics of an organism
  • Antonym: genotypical - relating to the genetic makeup of an organism

Etymology

From phenotype + -ical, patterning after 'phenotypic'. 'Phenotype' from German 'Phänotypus'.
