sisterlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪstərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəlaɪk / (BrE)
having qualities or showing affection typical of a sister; characteristic of, or appropriate to, a sister.
typical or characteristic of a sister

Examples

She always treated her friends with sisterlike affection, supporting them through every challenge.
There was a sisterlike bond between the two classmates, even though they had just met.
Her sisterlike concern for her neighbors made her a beloved member of the community.

Synset Examples

sisterly kindness
sororal concern

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sisterly - showing affection or care typical of a sister
  • Synonym: sororal - relating to or characteristic of a sister
