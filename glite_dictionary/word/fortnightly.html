fortnightly

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrtˌnaɪtli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtˌnaɪtli / (BrE)
Happening or appearing every two weeks; occurring once every fortnight.
every two weeks

Examples

Our team has fortnightly meetings to review the project’s progress.
The magazine is published fortnightly throughout the year.
He receives a fortnightly paycheck from his employer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biweekly - occurring every two weeks

Etymology

From 'fortnight' (fourteen nights) + '-ly'.
