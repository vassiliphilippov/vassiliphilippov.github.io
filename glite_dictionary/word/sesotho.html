Sesotho

1. NOUN

/ sɛˈsoʊtoʊ / (AmE), / sɛˈsəʊtəʊ / (BrE)
the language of the Basotho people, an official language of Lesotho and South Africa
a Bantu language spoken in Lesotho and South Africa

Examples

She learned to speak Sesotho during her stay in Lesotho.
The novel has been translated into both English and Sesotho.
Many schools in South Africa offer Sesotho as a language subject.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sotho - the language group to which Sesotho belongs

Etymology

From Sotho 'seSotho', where 'se-' is the language prefix.
