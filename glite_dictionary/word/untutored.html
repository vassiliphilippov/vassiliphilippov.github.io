untutored

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtuːtɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtjuːtəd / (BrE)
not having received formal education or instruction; lacking schooling or training.
lacking formal education or training

Examples

Despite being untutored in music, she composed beautiful melodies by ear.
Many untutored children struggle to catch up with their classmates in reading.
His untutored style brings a raw and honest quality to his artwork.

Synset Examples

untaught people whose verbal skills are grossly deficient
an untutored genius
uneducated children

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uneducated - lacking an education or formal schooling
  • Antonym: educated - having received education or schooling

Etymology

From un- (not) + tutored, from tutor (instructor or teacher).
