crazed

1. ADJ

/ kreɪzd / (AmE), / kreɪzd / (BrE)
behaving in a wild, uncontrolled, or insane way, as if driven mad
wildly insane or uncontrolled

Examples

A crazed look appeared in his eyes as he shouted at the crowd.
The crazed fan broke through the security barriers to reach the stage.
She ran through the streets like a crazed animal, terrified and confused.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insane - mentally ill; crazy
  • Antonym: sane - of sound mind; not insane

Etymology

From past participle of 'craze', Middle English 'crasen' (to shatter, break)
