chubbiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃʌbinəs / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌbɪnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being plump and round in body shape.
quality of being plump or round

Examples

The baby's chubbiness made everyone want to pinch her cheeks.
He worried that his chubbiness would make it harder to fit into his favorite jeans.
The artist captured the chubbiness of the puppy in the sculpture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plumpness - the quality of being pleasantly fat or rounded
  • Antonym: thinness - the quality of being thin or slim

Etymology

From 'chubby' + '-ness', formed in English.
