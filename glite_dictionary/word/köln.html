Köln

1. PROPN

/ kœln / (AmE), / kœln / (BrE)
A city in western Germany on the Rhine River, known in English as Cologne, noted for its historic architecture and as a major cultural and commercial center.
city in Germany; also called Cologne

Examples

Many tourists visit Köln to see its famous cathedral and vibrant old town.
The Rhine River flows through Köln, making it an important transportation hub.
She studied art history at a university in Köln.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cologne - English name for the German city Köln

Etymology

From German Köln, from Latin Colonia (Colonia Claudia Ara Agrippinensium).
