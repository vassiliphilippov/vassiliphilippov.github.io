Osmitrol

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑzmɪtrɔl / (AmE), / ˈɒzmɪtrɒl / (BrE)
A brand name for mannitol, a diuretic used to promote the excretion of urine.
brand of diuretic (mannitol)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Osmitrol to reduce the patient's intracranial pressure.
After receiving Osmitrol, the patient's urine output increased significantly.
Osmitrol is often used in hospitals to help the kidneys eliminate excess fluid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mannitol - a sugar alcohol used as a diuretic and sweetener
