sisterly

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪstɚli / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəli / (BrE)
showing affection, concern, or qualities characteristic of a sister
like or typical of a sister

Examples

Her sisterly advice helped me get through a tough time.
He showed sisterly concern for his younger classmates.
Maria gave Tom a warm, sisterly hug after the ceremony.

Synset Examples

sisterly kindness
sororal concern

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brotherly - showing affection or qualities typical of a brother

Etymology

Formed from 'sister' + '-ly', in analogy with similar adjectives (e.g., brotherly).
