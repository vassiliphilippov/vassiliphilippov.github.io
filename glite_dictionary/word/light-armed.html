light-armed

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪt ɑːrmd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt ɑːmd / (BrE)
(of soldiers or military units) equipped with light weapons and minimal protective gear, rather than heavy arms and armor.
equipped with light weapons

Examples

The scouts were light-armed to move swiftly across the mountainous terrain.
A light-armed cavalry division led the advance ahead of the main force.
During the skirmish, their light-armed units provided crucial reconnaissance.

Synset Examples

a light-armed brigade

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heavily armed - equipped with heavy weapons and extensive armor
