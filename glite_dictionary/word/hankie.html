hankie

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæŋki / (AmE), / ˈhæŋki / (BrE)
an informal term for a small square piece of cloth used for wiping the eyes or nose or as a fashion accessory
informal handkerchief
informal

Examples

She reached into her bag for a hankie to wipe away her tears.
He always carries a clean hankie in his pocket, just in case.
The little girl waved her pink hankie as the parade passed by.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handkerchief - a square piece of cloth for personal use

Etymology

Diminutive of 'handkerchief.'
