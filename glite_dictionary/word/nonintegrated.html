nonintegrated

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈɪntəˌɡreɪtəd / (AmE), / nɒnˈɪntɪˌɡreɪtɪd / (BrE)
Not combined or unified into a whole; not integrated.
not integrated or unified

Examples

The committee remained a nonintegrated group, with each member working independently.
As one of the nonintegrated schools in the area, it had different admission policies.
The region has several nonintegrated transport networks that make travel inconvenient.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: integrated - combined to form a whole

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'non-' meaning 'not' plus 'integrated'.
