raffish

1. ADJ

/ ˈræfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈræfɪʃ / (BrE)
slightly disreputable but attractive in a carefree or unconventional way
unconventionally and charmingly disreputable

Examples

She admired his raffish charm and effortless confidence.
The old bar had a certain raffish appeal that drew in artists and musicians.
His raffish lifestyle was both frowned upon and secretly envied by his peers.

Synset Examples

a cocktail party given by some … raffish bachelors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bohemian - unconventional, artistic, and nonconformist in lifestyle
  • Antonym: respectable - considered proper and socially acceptable

Etymology

From English 'raff' (persons of low character) + -ish.
