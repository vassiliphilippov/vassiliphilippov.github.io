homemaking

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊmˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the management and organization of household affairs and domestic activities
management of a household

Examples

She devoted much of her life to homemaking and raising her children.
Modern homemaking involves managing finances as well as cleaning and cooking.
He took pride in his homemaking skills after retiring from his job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: housekeeping - the management and routine tasks involved in running a home

Etymology

Formed from 'home' + 'making', meaning the act of making a home.
