technologically

1. ADV

/ ˌtɛk.nəˈlɑ.dʒɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌtɛk.nəˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kli / (BrE)
By means of or relating to technology; in a manner involving or based on technology.
by means of technology

Examples

The spacecraft was technologically advanced for its time, featuring many new innovations.
It is technologically possible to produce clean energy from multiple renewable sources.
The country has made great strides and is now technologically competitive on a global scale.

Synset Examples

technologically impossible

Related Terms

  • Synonym: technically - in a manner related to technique or technical detail

Etymology

From 'technology' + '-ally', from Greek 'tekhnē' (art, skill) and '-logia' (study).
