panelist

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæn.ə.lɪst / (AmE), / ˈpæn.əl.ɪst / (BrE)
a person who is a member of a panel, especially one who takes part in a discussion or debate before an audience
member of a panel

Examples

Each panelist offered a unique perspective during the televised debate.
The conference invited several industry experts to serve as panelists for the discussion.
As a panelist, she answered questions from both the moderator and the audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: panel - a group of people gathered for discussion or judging

Etymology

From 'panel' + '-ist', with '-ist' denoting a person associated with a specified thing.
