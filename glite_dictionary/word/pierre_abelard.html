Pierre Abelard

1. PROPN

/ pjɛər æbəˈlɑrd / (AmE), / pjɛər ˈabəlɑːd / (BrE)
(1079–1142) French philosopher and theologian, known for his work in scholastic philosophy and his romance with Heloise.
French philosopher and theologian (1079–1142)

Examples

Pierre Abelard was a prominent thinker in the early development of scholastic philosophy.
The tragic romance between Heloise and Pierre Abelard remains famous in medieval literature.
Many universities still study the works of Pierre Abelard and his contributions to logic and theology.
