uncommonly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈkɑmənli / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɒmənli / (BrE)
to an unusual or exceptional degree; remarkably
exceptionally; to an unusual degree

Examples

The weather this summer has been uncommonly hot for this region.
She was uncommonly talented at solving complex mathematical problems.
This vintage wine is uncommonly smooth and rich in flavor.

Synset Examples

a common remedy is uncommonly difficult to find

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exceptionally - to an unusually high degree
  • Synonym: remarkably - in a way that is unusual or surprising
  • Antonym: commonly - frequently; usually
