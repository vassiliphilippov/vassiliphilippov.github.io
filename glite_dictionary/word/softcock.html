softcock

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑftˌkɑk / (AmE), / ˈsɒftˌkɒk / (BrE)
a person who is weak, ineffective, or excessively timid, especially in challenging situations
a timid or ineffective person (slang, vulgar)
slang vulgar

Examples

Tom never stands up for himself—he's such a softcock at work.
The manager accused the team of being softcocks for not taking more risks.
Don't be a softcock; speak your mind in the meeting.

Synset Examples

Being a softcock in business can lead to missed opportunities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wimp - a weak and ineffectual person
  • Synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage to face danger or pain
