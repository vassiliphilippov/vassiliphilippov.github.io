unsubmissive

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.səbˈmɪs.ɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.səbˈmɪs.ɪv / (BrE)
not willing to submit or yield to authority or control; not submissive or servile.
not submissive; not yielding to authority

Examples

Despite the pressure from his superiors, her unsubmissive attitude remained unchanged.
She admired his unsubmissive nature, especially when faced with unjust authority.
The unsubmissive child refused to follow unreasonable orders without question.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: submissive - willing to yield or submit to authority
  • Antonym: servile - having or showing an excessive willingness to serve or please others

Etymology

Prefix un- (not) + submissive.
