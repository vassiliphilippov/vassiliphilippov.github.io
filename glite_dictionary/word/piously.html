piously

1. ADV

/ ˈpaɪəsli / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ.əsli / (BrE)
In a devout, religiously sincere, or reverent manner.
in a devout or religious way

Examples

She knelt piously by her bed every night to pray before sleeping.
He listened piously to the sermon, nodding in agreement at every word.
The villagers celebrated the festival piously, following every traditional ritual with devotion.

Synset Examples

she was devoutly Catholic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devoutly - in a deeply religious or earnest manner

Etymology

From 'pious' + '-ly', ultimately from Latin 'pius' (dutiful, devout).
