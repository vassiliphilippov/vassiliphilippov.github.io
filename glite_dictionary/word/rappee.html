rappee

1. NOUN

/ ræˈpeɪ / (AmE), / ræˈpeɪ / (BrE)
A strong kind of snuff made from dark, coarse tobacco.
strong, dark coarse tobacco snuff

Examples

He carried a small box of rappee in his coat pocket.
The old gentleman preferred rappee to milder forms of snuff.
The shopkeeper offered a fresh batch of rappee imported from France.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snuff - powdered tobacco inhaled through the nose

Etymology

From French 'rapé' (grated), from the process of grating tobacco.
