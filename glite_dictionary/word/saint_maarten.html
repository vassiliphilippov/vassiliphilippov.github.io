Saint Maarten

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt ˈmɑːrtən / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈmɑːtən / (BrE)
An island in the western Leeward Islands, administered jointly by France (Saint-Martin) and the Netherlands (Sint Maarten).
island in the Caribbean Sea, split between France and the Netherlands

Examples

Many tourists visit Saint Maarten for its beautiful beaches and vibrant nightlife.
The island of Saint Maarten is known for its unique blend of Dutch and French cultures.
Hurricane Irma caused significant damage to Saint Maarten in 2017.

Etymology

From Dutch and French, named after Saint Martin of Tours.
