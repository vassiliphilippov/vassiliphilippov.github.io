pissing

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
An act of urinating; the process of passing urine from the body, usually considered informal or vulgar.
(informal, vulgar) act of urinating
informal vulgar

Examples

He was fined for public pissing outside the nightclub.
She burst out laughing at his joke about pissing in the woods.
There's a law against pissing in public places.

Synset Examples

he took a pee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urination - the act of discharging urine from the body
  • Antonym: defecation - the act of passing solid waste from the body

Etymology

From the verb 'piss' (to urinate), which has Middle English and Old French origins.
