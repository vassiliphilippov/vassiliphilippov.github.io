panamica

1. NOUN

/ pəˈnæmɪkə / (AmE), / pəˈnamɪkə / (BrE)
A low, stingless nettle found in Central and South America, recognized by its velvety brownish-green toothed leaves and clusters of small green flowers.
a stingless nettle plant of Central and South America

Examples

The panamica thrives in the humid forests of Central America, where it forms dense ground cover.
Botanists are studying the medicinal properties of the panamica's leaves.
Clusters of green flowers distinguish the panamica from other nettle species in the region.
