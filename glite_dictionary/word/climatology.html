climatology

1. NOUN

/ ˌklaɪ.məˈtɑ.lə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌklaɪ.məˈtɒl.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
The scientific study of climates, including their phenomena and variations over time.
the study of climates

Examples

She decided to specialize in climatology after completing her degree in environmental science.
Research in climatology helps scientists predict long-term weather patterns and climate change.
The university is offering a new course in climatology starting next semester.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meteorology - the scientific study of the atmosphere and weather

Etymology

From French climatologie, from Greek klima (region, zone) + -logia (study of).
