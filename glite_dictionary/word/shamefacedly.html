shamefacedly

1. ADV

/ ʃeɪmˈfeɪsɪdli / (AmE), / ʃeɪmˈfeɪsɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows embarrassment or shame.
in an embarrassed or ashamed manner

Examples

She shamefacedly admitted that she had forgotten his birthday.
He shamefacedly avoided eye contact as he apologized.
After the incident, the student shamefacedly returned to his seat.

Synset Examples

quarrels and dissensions ensued among the cast, most of whom hurriedly and shamefacedly handed over their parts to understudies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ashamedly - in a manner showing shame or embarrassment

Etymology

From 'shamefaced' (embarrassed or ashamed) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
