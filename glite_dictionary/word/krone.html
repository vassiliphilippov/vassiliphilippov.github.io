krone

1. NOUN

/ ˈkroʊnə / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊnə / (BrE)
the official currency and basic unit of money in Norway, equal to 100 øre
Norwegian currency unit

Examples

I exchanged my dollars for Norwegian krone before traveling to Oslo.
The price of the sweater is 500 krone.
Tourists often compare the krone to other European currencies when shopping in Norway.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: krona - the basic unit of money in Sweden

Etymology

From Norwegian 'krone' (crown), related to the Latin 'corona' (crown).

2. NOUN

/ ˈkroʊ.nə / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊ.nə / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Denmark, equal to 100 øre
Danish unit of currency

Examples

The price was listed as 500 kroner on the Danish website.
She exchanged her euros for krone before flying to Copenhagen.
The Danish krone has remained stable against the euro over the past year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: krona - unit of currency in Sweden

Etymology

From Danish 'krone', meaning 'crown', ultimately from Latin 'corona'.
