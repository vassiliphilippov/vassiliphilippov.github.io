hardcore

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑrdˌkɔr / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌkɔː / (BrE)
(of entertainment or material) extremely explicit and graphic, especially in depicting sexual activity; not intended to be mild or censored.
extremely explicit (often sexual)

Examples

The film was classified as hardcore and could not be shown in mainstream cinemas.
He said the magazine only published hardcore images, not suitable for younger readers.
Many websites clearly label content as hardcore to warn viewers about explicit material.

Synset Examples

hard-core pornography

Related Terms

  • Antonym: softcore - entertainment or material with mild or less explicit sexual content

2. ADJ

/ ˈhɑrdˌkɔr / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌkɔː / (BrE)
extremely committed, dedicated, or loyal to a cause, group, or activity.
extremely committed or loyal

Examples

She has a group of hardcore fans who follow her to every concert.
Only the most hardcore gamers completed the final level without any help.
His hardcore supporters refused to back down even after the controversy.

Synset Examples

his hard-core supporters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: staunch - very loyal and committed in attitude
  • Degree Higher: fanatical - filled with excessive and single-minded zeal
  • Degree Lower: casual - not regular or committed

Etymology

From 'hard' (intense) + 'core' (central part), originally American slang from the mid-20th century.
