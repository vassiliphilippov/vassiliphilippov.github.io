pickelhaube

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪkəlˌhaʊbə / (AmE), / ˈpɪkəlˌhaʊbə / (BrE)
a spiked helmet worn by German soldiers, especially in the 19th and early 20th centuries
spiked German military helmet

Examples

The German officer's pickelhaube gleamed in the afternoon sunlight.
Collectors often seek authentic pickelhaubes as valuable pieces of military history.
He recognized the silhouette of a pickelhaube in the old wartime photograph.

Etymology

From German Pickelhaube (literally 'pointed bonnet'): Pickel (pike, spike) + Haube (hood, bonnet).
