famed

1. ADJ

/ feɪmd / (AmE), / feɪmd / (BrE)
widely known and respected; celebrated or renowned
widely known; celebrated

Examples

The famed chef opened a new restaurant downtown, attracting food lovers from across the city.
She traveled to the famed ruins, hoping to learn more about ancient history.
His famed discoveries changed the course of modern medicine.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: famous - known about by many people
  • Synonym: celebrated - greatly admired; renowned
  • Antonym: obscure - not well-known

Etymology

From Middle English 'famed', from Old French 'famer', from Latin 'fama' (report, fame).
