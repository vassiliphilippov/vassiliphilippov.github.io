Hypentelium

1. NOUN

/ haɪˌpɛnˈtɛliəm / (AmE), / haɪˌpɛnˈtɛliəm / (BrE)
A genus of freshwater sucker fish in the family Catostomidae, comprising several species native to North America.
genus of freshwater sucker fish

Examples

The Hypentelium genus includes the northern hog sucker found in many North American rivers.
Researchers studied the distribution of Hypentelium species in relation to water quality.
Young ichthyologists can easily identify a Hypentelium by its distinctive sucker mouth.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin, formed from hypo- (under) + entelium (possibly referencing lip structure), used in scientific taxonomy.
