unimpeachably

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.ɪmˈpiː.tʃəb.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪmˈpiː.tʃəb.li / (BrE)
in a manner that is beyond doubt or criticism; unquestionably or faultlessly
in a way that cannot be doubted or faulted

Examples

Her integrity was unimpeachably demonstrated throughout her long career.
The company has unimpeachably high ethical standards in all its operations.
He handled the situation unimpeachably, leaving no room for criticism.

Synset Examples

Fred Winter is unquestionably the jockey to follow
they hired unimpeachably first-rate faculty members

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unquestionably - without any doubt; certainly

Etymology

From 'unimpeachable' (impossible to discredit or call into question) + '-ly'.
