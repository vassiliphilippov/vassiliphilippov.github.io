powerfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊərfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈpaʊəfʊlnəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being powerful; possessing great strength, influence, or authority.
state of being powerful

Examples

The powerfulness of the storm left the entire town without electricity for days.
She was admired for the powerfulness of her convictions and her ability to inspire others.
The powerfulness of his arguments eventually won over his critics.

Synset Examples

the deterrent power of nuclear weapons
the power of his love saved her
his powerfulness was concealed by a gentle facade

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power - ability to control or influence
  • Antonym: weakness - state of being weak or lacking power

Etymology

From 'powerful' + '-ness', indicating the state or quality.
