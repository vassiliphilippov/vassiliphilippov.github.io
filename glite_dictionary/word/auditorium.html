auditorium

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔː.dəˈtɔː.ri.əm / (AmE), / ˌɔː.dɪˈtɔː.rɪ.əm / (BrE)
the part of a theater, concert hall, or other public building in which the audience sits to watch or listen to a performance or event
seating area for audience in a theater or hall

Examples

The auditorium was filled with excited students awaiting the start of the play.
Please find your seat in the auditorium before the concert begins.
Renovations to the auditorium improved both the seating and the acoustics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theater - a building or outdoor area where plays or other performances are given
  • Antonym: stage - the area of a theater where actors perform

Etymology

From Latin auditorium, from audīre (to hear).
