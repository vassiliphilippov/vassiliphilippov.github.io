Aberzombie

1. NOUN

/ ˈæbərˌzɑmbi / (AmE), / ˈabəˌzɒmbi / (BrE)
a person who is extremely tired or drained after extensive socializing, particularly in social or party environments
person exhausted from socializing
informal slang

Examples

After a long weekend of parties, she became an Aberzombie on Monday morning.
Don't ask me to go out tonight—I'm still recovering as a total Aberzombie from last night.
By midnight, half the party guests were already Aberzombies slumped on the couch.

Synset Examples

After the party, he felt like a total Aberzombie.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: zombie - a person appearing lifeless or mechanical

Etymology

Blend of 'Abercrombie' (from Abercrombie & Fitch, a fashion brand) and 'zombie'.
