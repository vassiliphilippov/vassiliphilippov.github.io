ill-used

1. ADJ

/ ɪlˈjuːzd / (AmE), / ɪlˈjuːzd / (BrE)
(of a person) treated unfairly; taken advantage of or subjected to abuse or neglect.
treated unfairly; taken advantage of

Examples

She felt ill-used after her hard work was ignored by her manager.
The ill-used employees finally decided to speak out against their boss.
He wrote a memoir about being ill-used by those he trusted.

Synset Examples

after going out of his way to help his friend get the job he felt not appreciated but used

Related Terms

  • Synonym: misused - treated in the wrong way or abused
  • Antonym: well-treated - having been treated kindly and with respect

Etymology

Formed from 'ill-' (badly) and 'used' (treated), meaning treated badly.
