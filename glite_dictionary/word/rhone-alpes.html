Rhone-Alpes

1. PROPN

/ roʊn ˈælpz / (AmE), / rəʊn ˈælpz / (BrE)
a former administrative region of eastern France, known for its mountainous terrain and major rivers including the Rhone, Saone, and Isere.
region of eastern France

Examples

The Rhone-Alpes region is famous for its stunning alpine scenery and winter sports resorts.
Many travelers visit Rhone-Alpes for its culinary traditions and beautiful landscapes.
Grenoble, a major city in Rhone-Alpes, is known as the 'Capital of the Alps.'

Etymology

From the names of the Rhône river and the Alps mountains.
