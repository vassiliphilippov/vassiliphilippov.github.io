praiseworthiness

1. NOUN

/ ˌpreɪzˈwɝːði.nəs / (AmE), / ˌpreɪzˈwɜːði.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of deserving praise or admiration
the quality of being worthy of praise

Examples

The artist's praiseworthiness was evident in the heartfelt reactions of the critics.
We should recognize the praiseworthiness of their efforts to improve the community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worthiness - the quality of being worthy or deserving
  • Antonym: blameworthiness - the quality of being deserving of blame

2. NOUN

/ ˌpreɪzˈwɜrðɪnəs / (AmE), / ˌpreɪzˈwɜːðɪnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being deserving of praise or commendation.
quality of deserving praise

Examples

Her praiseworthiness was acknowledged by everyone at the ceremony.
The committee discussed the praiseworthiness of his actions in great detail.
Acts of praiseworthiness often go unnoticed in everyday life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: merit - the quality of being particularly good or worthy
  • Antonym: blameworthiness - the quality of being deserving blame

Etymology
