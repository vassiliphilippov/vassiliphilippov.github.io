Tarabulus Ash-Sham

1. PROPN

/ tɑːˈrɑːbuːləs æʃ ʃɑːm / (AmE), / tæˈrɑːbuːləs æʃ ˈʃɑːm / (BrE)
The Arabic name for Tripoli, a major port city and commercial center in northwestern Lebanon on the Mediterranean Sea.
Tripoli, Lebanon's Arabic name

Examples

Many visitors travel to Tarabulus Ash-Sham to explore its historic souks and ancient mosques.
The port of Tarabulus Ash-Sham plays a vital role in Lebanon's economy.
In Arabic, Tripoli is known as Tarabulus Ash-Sham, distinguishing it from the Libyan city with the same name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tripoli - A major port city in Lebanon, known as Tarabulus Ash-Sham in Arabic

Etymology

From Arabic طرابلس الشام (Ṭarābulus al-Shām), meaning 'Tripoli of the Levant'.
