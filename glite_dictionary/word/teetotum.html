teetotum

1. NOUN

/ tiˈtoʊtəm / (AmE), / tiːˈtəʊtəm / (BrE)
a type of spinning top, often conical, used in games and especially associated with traditional children's play
a spinning top used in games

Examples

As a child, he loved watching the teetotum spin on the kitchen floor.
The old wooden teetotum was passed down through generations.
She explained the rules of the game that involved a teetotum to her friends.

Synset Examples

he got a bright red top and string for his birthday

Related Terms

  • Synonym: top - a toy that spins on an axis, usually standing on a point

Etymology

From Latin 'totum' (the whole), referring to the letters inscribed on its sides.
