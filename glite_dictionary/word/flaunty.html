flaunty

1. ADJ

/ ˈflɔnti / (AmE), / ˈflɔːnti / (BrE)
Tending to display oneself or one's possessions in an obvious or boastful way.
inclined to flaunt; showy

Examples

Her flaunty jewelry caught everyone's eye at the party.
The celebrity's flaunty style often made headlines in fashion magazines.
He adopted a flaunty attitude, eager to impress his new colleagues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: showy - attracting attention by being bright, ostentatious, or extravagant
  • Antonym: modest - not showing off or drawing attention to oneself
