hematologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌhiːməˈtɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˈtɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the blood and blood-forming organs
doctor specializing in blood diseases
medical

Examples

After several tests, the hematologist diagnosed her with a rare blood disorder.
The patient was referred to a hematologist for further evaluation of his anemia.
As a hematologist, Dr. Smith often collaborates with oncologists for treatment plans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oncologist - a doctor who specializes in the treatment of cancer

Etymology

From Greek haima (blood) + -logist (one who studies).
