chorally

1. ADV

/ ˈkɔːrəli / (AmE), / ˈkɔːrəli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or performed by a chorus.
in the manner of a chorus

Examples

The anthem was chorally performed at the opening ceremony.
Many of the hymns are chorally arranged for special services.
The music was chorally accompanied to enhance its grandeur.

Synset Examples

chorally accompanied

Related Terms

  • Synonym: choral - relating to a chorus or choir

Etymology

From 'choral' + '-ly', patterned after similar adverbs.
