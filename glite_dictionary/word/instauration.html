instauration

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.stəˈreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.stɔːˈreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of establishing or beginning something for the first time; the introduction of something new
the act of founding or instituting

Examples

The instauration of the university marked a turning point in the city's history.
Researchers celebrated the instauration of a new era in science after the discovery.
The instauration of democratic institutions brought hope to the nation.

Synset Examples

she looked forward to her initiation as an adult
the foundation of a new scientific society

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foundation - the act of establishing an institution or organization
  • Synonym: initiation - the act of beginning something or being introduced to something new
  • Antonym: abolition - the act of formally ending or abolishing something

Etymology

From Latin instauratio (restoration, renewal), from instaurare (to renew).
