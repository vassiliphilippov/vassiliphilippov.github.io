archipelagic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑrkəpəˈlædʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌɑːkɪpəˈlædʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or belonging to an archipelago; consisting of many islands.
relating to an archipelago

Examples

Indonesia is an archipelagic country made up of more than 17,000 islands.
The nation has established an archipelagic defense strategy to protect its vast maritime borders.
Her research focused on the unique ecosystems of archipelagic regions.

Synset Examples

an archipelagic war

Related Terms

  • Synonym: archipelago - a group of islands or sea containing many islands

Etymology

From 'archipelago' + '-ic', modeled on similar English adjectives.
