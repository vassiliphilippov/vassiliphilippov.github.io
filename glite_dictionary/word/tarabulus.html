Tarabulus

1. PROPN

/ tɑːˈrɑːbuˌlus / (AmE), / tɑːˈrɑːbʊlʊs / (BrE)
the Arabic name for Tripoli, a port city and commercial center in northwestern Lebanon on the Mediterranean Sea
Arabic name for Tripoli, Lebanon

Examples

Many Arabic speakers refer to the Lebanese city of Tripoli as Tarabulus.
The ancient markets of Tarabulus are famous for their architecture and history.
International organizations sometimes use both 'Tripoli' and Tarabulus to refer to the same Lebanese city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tripoli - a port city and commercial hub in northwestern Lebanon

Etymology

Arabic: طَرَابُلُس (Ṭarābulus), from Greek Tripolis (meaning 'three cities').
