Stanford

1. PROPN

/ ˈstæn.fərd / (AmE), / ˈstæn.fəd / (BrE)
A prestigious private university located in California, known for its academic excellence and research.
university in California, USA

Examples

She received her degree from Stanford and went on to become a successful entrepreneur.
Many tech startups have been launched by graduates of Stanford.
The research conducted at Stanford has had a global impact in many fields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Harvard - prestigious university in Massachusetts, USA
  • Synonym: Cambridge - prestigious university in England

Etymology

Named after Leland Stanford (1824–1893), American industrialist and founder.
