xii

1. NUM

/ ɛks aɪ aɪ / (AmE), / ɛks aɪ aɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for twelve (12)
Roman numeral for twelve

Examples

The clock struck xii at midnight, marking the start of a new day.
Chapter xii of the novel contains the story’s main turning point.
He was born on the xii day of December.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twelve - the number 12
  • Degree Lower: xi - Roman numeral for eleven
  • Degree Higher: xiii - Roman numeral for thirteen

Etymology

From Latin numeral system, with 'X' for 10 and 'II' for 2.
