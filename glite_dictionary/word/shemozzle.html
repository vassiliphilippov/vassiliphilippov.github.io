shemozzle

1. NOUN

/ ʃəˈmɑzəl / (AmE), / ʃəˈmɒzəl / (BrE)
a confused or chaotic situation; a mess
a chaotic or messy situation
informal

Examples

After the meeting turned into a shemozzle, nobody knew what had been decided.
The whole project became a financial shemozzle that cost thousands more than expected.
There was a shemozzle at the train station when all the departures were suddenly canceled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mess - a situation that is dirty or not organized
  • Degree Higher: fiasco - a complete and often humiliating failure

Etymology

From Yiddish shlomoze(l), probably from Hebrew shel mazal (of luck) or related sources.
