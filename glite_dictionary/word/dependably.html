dependably

1. ADV

/ dɪˈpɛndəbli / (AmE), / dɪˈpɛndəbli / (BrE)
In a way that can be relied upon; in a trustworthy and consistent manner.
in a reliable, trustworthy way

Examples

She handled every task dependably and never missed a deadline.
The train arrives dependably every morning at 8:00 a.m.
We need employees who can work dependably under pressure.

Synset Examples

it always came on, faithfully, like the radio

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reliably - in a trustworthy and consistent way
  • Antonym: unreliably - in a way that cannot be trusted

Etymology

Formed from 'dependable' (from 'depend') + -ly.
