noticeability

1. NOUN

/ ˌnoʊtɪsəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌnəʊtɪsəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being easy to notice, see, or understand
quality of being easy to notice

Examples

The noticeability of the warning signs prevented accidents in the area.
Designers often try to increase the noticeability of important information.
The bright colors contribute to the noticeability of the advertisement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visibility - the quality of being able to be seen
  • Antonym: invisibility - the state of not being able to be seen

Etymology

Formed from 'noticeable' + '-ity', patterned after other abstract nouns in English.
