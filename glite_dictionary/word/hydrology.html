hydrology

1. NOUN

/ haɪˈdrɑlədʒi / (AmE), / haɪˈdrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of the movement, distribution, and management of water on the Earth and in the atmosphere
the study of water in Earth's systems

Examples

She decided to specialize in hydrology to address water scarcity issues in her country.
Research in hydrology helps improve our understanding of floods and droughts.
The university offers advanced courses in hydrology and water resource management.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geology - the science that deals with the Earth's physical structure and substances

Etymology

From Greek ‘hydor’ (water) + ‘-logia’ (study of).
