divinity

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈvɪnəti / (AmE), / dɪˈvɪnəti / (BrE)
a god, goddess, or any supernatural being who is worshipped as controlling some aspect of the world or life
a supernatural being or deity

Examples

Ancient civilizations often believed in more than one divinity governing different parts of nature.
The temple was built in honor of a powerful divinity associated with fertility.
In mythology, each divinity has unique characteristics and stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deity - a god or goddess

Etymology

From Latin divinitas (divine nature), from divinus (divine).
