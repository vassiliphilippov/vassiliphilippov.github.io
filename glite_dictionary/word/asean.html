ASEAN

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑːsiˌɑːn / (AmE), / ˈæs.i.æn / (BrE)
an association of Southeast Asian nations formed to promote economic, political, and security cooperation among its member states
Association of Southeast Asian Nations (international organization)

Examples

ASEAN held its annual summit to discuss regional security and economic growth.
Many countries look to ASEAN as a model for regional cooperation and integration.
The expansion of ASEAN has strengthened ties among Southeast Asian nations.

Etymology

Acronym from Association of Southeast Asian Nations.
