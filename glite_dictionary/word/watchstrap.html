watchstrap

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑːtʃˌstræp / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃˌstræp / (BrE)
a band made of cloth, leather, or metal links, attached to a wristwatch and worn around the wrist
band for attaching a wristwatch to the wrist

Examples

He replaced the broken watchstrap with a new leather one.
Her silver watchstrap matched her bracelet perfectly.
After years of use, the watchstrap became worn and needed replacement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wristband - a band worn around the wrist, not necessarily for watches
