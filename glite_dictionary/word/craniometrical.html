craniometrical

1. ADJ

/ ˌkreɪniəˈmɛtrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌkreɪniəˈmɛtrɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the scientific measurement of the skull
relating to skull measurement
medical

Examples

The researcher used craniometrical techniques to analyze the ancient skulls.
Detailed craniometrical data were collected during the excavation.
Her study focused on the craniometrical differences between various populations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: craniometric - relating to craniometry (measurement of the skull)

Etymology

From craniometry + -ical, based on Greek kranion (skull) and metron (measure).
