pricing

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpraɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of determining or evaluating the price of a product or service
setting or evaluating prices

Examples

The company reviewed its pricing strategy to remain competitive in the market.
Before launching the product, the team discussed different pricing models.
Transparent pricing has become important for building consumer trust.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: valuation - the process of estimating the worth or value of something
  • Synonym: costing - the calculation of the cost of something

Etymology

Formed from 'price' + '-ing', denoting the process related to price.
