rhinoplasty

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪnəˌplæsti / (AmE), / raɪˈnɒpləsti / (BrE)
a surgical procedure to alter the shape or appearance of the nose, often for cosmetic or medical reasons
surgery to alter the nose's shape
medical

Examples

She decided to have a rhinoplasty to address the bump on her nose.
Many people undergo rhinoplasty for both cosmetic and medical reasons.
After his rhinoplasty, he felt much more confident about his appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nose job - informal term for rhinoplasty

Etymology

From Greek 'rhino-' (nose) + 'plasty' (molding, forming).
