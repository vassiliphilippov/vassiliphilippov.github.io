personalized

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝːsənəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəˌlaɪzd / (BrE)
Made or adapted for a particular person; tailored to an individual's needs or preferences.
made for a specific person

Examples

The company offers personalized mugs with your name printed on them.
She received personalized feedback that addressed her specific concerns.
Many apps now provide personalized recommendations based on user behavior.

Synset Examples

personalized luggage
personalized advice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: customized - modified or built to individual specifications

2. ADJ

/ ˈpɜrsənəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəlaɪzd / (BrE)
referring to or concerning a particular person's individual personality or private affairs, often in a way that is intrusive or offensive
concerning someone's individual or private matters

Examples

He made several personalized remarks that made the meeting uncomfortable.
Her comments became too personalized and hurt his feelings.
The debate was civil until one side started making personalized attacks.

Synset Examples

unnecessarily personalized remarks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personal - relating to an individual person rather than to anyone else

Etymology

From personalize (to make personal) + -ed, indicating an adjective.
