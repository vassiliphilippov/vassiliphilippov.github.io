hereness

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪr.nəs / (AmE), / ˈhɪə.nɪs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being here or present in a particular place.
state of being present here

Examples

Philosophers contemplate the "hereness" and "nowness" of conscious experience.
She felt the hereness of the moment as she stood on stage.
Meditation helps increase your sense of hereness and awareness.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: thereness - the state or quality of being there or present at another place

Etymology

Formed from 'here' + '-ness', denoting a state or quality.
