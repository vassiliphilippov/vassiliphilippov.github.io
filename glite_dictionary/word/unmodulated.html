unmodulated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈmɑːdʒəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈmɒdjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not varied or adjusted in pitch, tone, or volume; lacking modulation.
lacking variation in pitch or tone

Examples

Her explanation was delivered in an unmodulated tone that made it difficult to stay engaged.
The actor's lines sounded dull and unmodulated, lacking any emotional depth.
He gave his presentation in an unmodulated voice, which failed to capture the audience's attention.

Synset Examples

he lectured in an unmodulated voice edged with hysteria

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monotone - a continuing sound, especially of someone's voice, that is unchanging in pitch; without intonation or expressiveness

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) and 'modulated', from Latin modulatus (measured, set to music).
