bottlebrush

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːt̬lˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɒtlˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)
a cylindrical brush with a long handle, used for cleaning bottles and other narrow containers
brush for cleaning bottles

Examples

She used a bottlebrush to clean the inside of the narrow vase.
Make sure to rinse the bottlebrush thoroughly after each use to keep it sanitary.
The bottlebrush is perfect for cleaning baby bottles and other hard-to-reach places.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brush - an implement with bristles for cleaning or painting
