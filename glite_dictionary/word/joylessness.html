joylessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɔɪləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪləsnɪs / (BrE)
The state or quality of having no joy or happiness; cheerlessness.
lack of joy or happiness

Examples

The film's joylessness made it difficult for audiences to connect with the characters.
Despite the celebration, a sense of joylessness lingered in the room.
His joylessness was evident even during moments meant to inspire happiness.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: joy - A feeling of great happiness or pleasure
  • Synonym: cheerlessness - The state of being without cheer or happiness

Etymology

From 'joyless' + '-ness', formed in English.
