yore

1. NOUN

/ jɔr / (AmE), / jɔː / (BrE)
a time long ago, especially as remembered or referred to in stories and literature
time long ago
archaic

Examples

Legends of yore often speak of powerful wizards and brave knights.
She longed for the simple pleasures of days of yore.
In stories of yore, dragons guarded vast treasures deep within the mountains.

Etymology

From Middle English *yore*, from Old English *geara* (long ago).
