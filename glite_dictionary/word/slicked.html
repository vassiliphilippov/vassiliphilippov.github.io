slicked

1. ADJ

/ slɪkt / (AmE), / slɪkt / (BrE)
(of hair) made smooth and glossy by applying a sticky or glossy substance, such as pomade or gel.
hair made smooth and shiny by product

Examples

He always arrived at work with his hair slicked back for a polished appearance.
The movie star's slicked hairstyle became iconic in the 1950s.
After applying mousse, her slicked hair stayed perfectly in place all night.

Synset Examples

black hair plastered with pomade

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gelled - (of hair) styled and stiffened with gel
  • Antonym: unkempt - (of hair) not combed or tidy
