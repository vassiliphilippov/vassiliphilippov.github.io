trailhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪlˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈtreɪlˌhɛd / (BrE)
the place where a trail begins, especially one used for hiking or walking
the beginning of a trail

Examples

The trailhead is located just past the visitors' center near the parking lot.
We met at the trailhead early in the morning to begin our hike up the mountain.
A large map at the trailhead shows all the paths and points of interest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trail - a path or track, especially one used for walking or hiking

Etymology

Formed from 'trail' + 'head', meaning the head or starting point of a trail.
