nonattendance

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɑn.əˈtɛn.dəns / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.əˈtɛn.dəns / (BrE)
the fact of not being present at an event or occasion when expected or required to be there
failure to be present or attend

Examples

Her repeated nonattendance at meetings raised concerns among her colleagues.
The school's strict policy penalizes students for nonattendance without a valid excuse.
Due to his nonattendance, he missed several important announcements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absence - the state of being away from a place or event
  • Antonym: attendance - the act of being present at a place

Etymology

From prefix 'non-' (not) + 'attendance' (from attend).
