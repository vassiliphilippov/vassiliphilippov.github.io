El Misti

1. PROPN

/ ɛl ˈmisti / (AmE), / ɛl ˈmɪsti / (BrE)
a prominent active volcano located in the Andes of southern Peru, near the city of Arequipa
active volcano in southern Peru

Examples

Many climbers are drawn to El Misti for its breathtaking views and challenging terrain.
The city of Arequipa is overshadowed by the imposing peak of El Misti.
Ash from El Misti can occasionally be seen drifting over the nearby valleys.

Etymology

Spanish, from local Quechua for 'the gentleman' or 'lord', used as a respectful term.
