Russian

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌʃən / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃ.ən / (BrE)
the Slavic language that is the official language of Russia and widely spoken in neighboring countries
official language of Russia

Examples

He is fluent in Russian, French, and English.
The novel was originally written in Russian.
Learning Russian can be challenging but rewarding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ukrainian - the East Slavic language spoken in Ukraine
  • Synonym: Russian (adjective) - relating to Russia, its people, or their language

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'Russiānus', from 'Russia'.
