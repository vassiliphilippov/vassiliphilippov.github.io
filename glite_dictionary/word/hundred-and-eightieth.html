hundred-and-eightieth

1. ADJ

/ ˌhʌndrəd ənd ˈeɪtiəθ / (AmE), / ˌhʌndrəd ən ˈeɪtiəθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number corresponding to one hundred eighty; being number 180 in a sequence.
ordinal number: 180th

Examples

The library celebrated its hundred-and-eightieth anniversary last year.
She was the hundred-and-eightieth runner to finish the marathon.
They live on the hundred-and-eightieth floor of the tallest building in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one hundred and eightieth - the ordinal number equivalent to 180th
  • Degree Lower: hundred-and-seventieth - the ordinal number corresponding to 170th
  • Degree Higher: hundred-and-ninetieth - the ordinal number corresponding to 190th
