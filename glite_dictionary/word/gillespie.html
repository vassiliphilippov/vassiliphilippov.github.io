Gillespie

1. PROPN

/ ɡɪˈlɛspi / (AmE), / ɡɪˈlɛspi / (BrE)
(1917–1993) United States jazz trumpeter and composer, a leading figure in the development of bebop.
U.S. jazz trumpeter, pioneer of bebop
american
