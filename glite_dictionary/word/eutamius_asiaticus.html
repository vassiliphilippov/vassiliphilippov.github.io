Eutamius asiaticus

1. NOUN

/ juːˈteɪmi.əs æsiˈætɪkəs / (AmE), / juːˈteɪmi.əs æsiˈatɪkəs / (BrE)
A species of terrestrial squirrel found in Siberia, known commonly as the Siberian chipmunk.
a Siberian terrestrial squirrel species

Examples

The Eutamius asiaticus is often called the Siberian chipmunk in English.
Researchers are studying how Eutamius asiaticus adapts to the harsh Siberian winters.
Unlike other squirrels, Eutamius asiaticus stores food underground for the colder months.

Etymology

From New Latin, with Eutamius as the genus name and asiaticus meaning 'Asian'.
