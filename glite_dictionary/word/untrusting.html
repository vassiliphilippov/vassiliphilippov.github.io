untrusting

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtrʌstɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈtrʌstɪŋ / (BrE)
not inclined to trust others; openly distrustful and unwilling to confide in others.
not willing to trust others

Examples

After her betrayal, Maria became increasingly untrusting of new people in her life.
The dog was untrusting and kept its distance from strangers.
Their untrusting attitude made team collaboration difficult.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distrustful - feeling or showing distrust of someone or something
  • Antonym: trusting - inclined to believe or confide readily; opposite of untrusting
