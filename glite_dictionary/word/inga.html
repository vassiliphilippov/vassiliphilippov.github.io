Inga

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical trees and shrubs in the pea family, native to Central and South America, known for their pods and feathery leaves.
genus of tropical trees (pea family)

Examples

The Inga genus includes many species that are popular for their edible pods.
Local farmers planted rows of Inga trees along the riverbank to prevent soil erosion.
Scientists discovered a new species within the Inga genus in the Amazon rainforest.

Etymology

From the Tupi word *inga*, referring to the plant.
