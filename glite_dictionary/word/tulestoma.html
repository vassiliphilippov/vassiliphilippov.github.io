Tulestoma

1. NOUN

/ tuːˈlɛstəʊmə / (AmE), / tjuːˈlɛstəmə / (BrE)
A genus of fungi in the family Tulostomaceae, known as stalked puffballs.
a genus of stalked puffball fungi

Examples

Scientists discovered several Tulestoma species growing in sandy soils in the desert.
The characteristic stalk of Tulestoma distinguishes it from other puffball fungi.
Mycologists study Tulestoma to learn more about fungal diversity.
