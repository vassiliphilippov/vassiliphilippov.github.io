black-marked

1. ADJ

/ blæk mɑrkt / (AmE), / blæk mɑːkt / (BrE)
having black marks or spots on the surface
marked with black spots or marks

Examples

The black-marked leopard was easily distinguished from the others.
Her notebook was black-marked after spilling ink on it.
They found a black-marked egg among the white ones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotted - having spots or patches on the surface
