formalwear

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrməlˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈfɔːməlˌwɛə / (BrE)
clothing designed to be worn at formal events, especially in the evening, such as tuxedos and evening gowns.
clothing for formal events

Examples

He rented his formalwear for the charity gala last Saturday.
The invitation specified black-tie formalwear for all guests attending the banquet.
She chose an elegant gown from the formalwear boutique downtown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evening dress - formal attire worn in the evening, often used interchangeably with formalwear
  • Antonym: casualwear - clothing designed for informal, everyday wear
