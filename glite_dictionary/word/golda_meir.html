Golda Meir

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡoʊldə maɪˈɪər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldə maɪˈɪə / (BrE)
(1898–1978) Israeli stateswoman who served as the fourth Prime Minister of Israel, noted for her leadership during the Yom Kippur War.
Israeli Prime Minister, 1969–1974

Examples

Golda Meir was the first and only woman to serve as Prime Minister of Israel.
During her tenure, Golda Meir played a significant role in shaping Israeli foreign policy.
In many history books, Golda Meir is remembered for her strength and determination.
