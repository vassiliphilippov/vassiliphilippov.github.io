Babe Didrikson Zaharias

1. PROPN

/ beɪb dɪdɹɪksən zəˈhɑːriəs / (AmE), / beɪb dɪdɹɪksən zəˈhɑːriəs / (BrE)
(1911–1956) A celebrated American athlete who excelled in track and field, golf, and basketball, considered one of the greatest female athletes of the 20th century.
American multi-sport athlete (1911–1956)

Examples

Babe Didrikson Zaharias won two gold medals at the 1932 Olympics in Los Angeles.
Many consider Babe Didrikson Zaharias to be one of the most versatile athletes of all time.
In addition to golf and basketball, Babe Didrikson Zaharias set world records in track and field.
