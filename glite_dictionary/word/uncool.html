uncool

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈkuːl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkuːl / (BrE)
Not fashionable, attractive, or socially accepted; considered boring or out of style.
not fashionable; boring or out of style
informal slang

Examples

Wearing that old jacket to the party was really uncool.
His uncool jokes made everyone groan instead of laugh.
They think it's uncool to care about grades, but I disagree.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cool - fashionable, impressive, or socially accepted

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' to 'cool' (fashionable or impressive).
