dishrag

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪʃˌræɡ / (AmE), / ˈdɪʃˌræɡ / (BrE)
A small cloth used for washing dishes.
cloth for washing dishes

Examples

She wiped the plates with a clean dishrag after dinner.
Please toss the dirty dishrag into the laundry basket.
He reached for a fresh dishrag to wash the pots and pans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dishcloth - a cloth used for washing or drying dishes

Etymology

From 'dish' + 'rag', meaning a rag used for dishes.
