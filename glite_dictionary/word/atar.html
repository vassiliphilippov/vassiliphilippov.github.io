atar

1. NOUN

/ əˈtɑr / (AmE), / əˈtɑː / (BrE)
A fragrant essential oil or perfume distilled from flowers, especially roses.
essential oil made from flowers

Examples

The perfumer added a few drops of atar to the mixture, giving it a rich floral scent.
Traditional ceremonies often use atar to anoint guests and create a pleasant atmosphere.
She purchased a small bottle of rose atar from the local market during her travels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attar - another spelling for 'atar', meaning essential oil from flowers

Etymology

From Persian 'atar', meaning perfume or scent.
