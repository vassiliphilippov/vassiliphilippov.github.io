to skydive

1. VERB

/ ˈskaɪˌdaɪv / (AmE), / ˈskaɪˌdaɪv / (BrE)
to jump from an aircraft and perform maneuvers in the air before opening a parachute
jump from aircraft with parachute

Examples

I have always wanted to skydive over the mountains and experience the thrill of freefall.
He learned how to skydive before his big trip to Australia.
They plan to skydive together for their anniversary celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parachute - to jump from an aircraft using a parachute

Etymology

Blend of 'sky' and 'dive', first used in the 1950s.
