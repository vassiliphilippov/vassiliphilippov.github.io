compatriot

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpeɪtriət / (AmE), / kəmˈpatriət / (BrE)
A person from the same country as someone else; a fellow countryman or countrywoman.
a fellow citizen or countryman

Examples

She met a compatriot while studying abroad, and they quickly became friends.
Several compatriots gathered to celebrate their national holiday together.
He felt a special bond with his compatriot in the foreign city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: countryman - a person from the same country as another; fellow citizen

Etymology

From French 'compatriote', from Latin 'com-' (with) + 'patria' (fatherland).
