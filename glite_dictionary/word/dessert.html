dessert

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈzɝt / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːt / (BrE)
the sweet course eaten at the end of a meal
sweet course at end of meal

Examples

We ordered chocolate mousse for dessert after our dinner.
She skipped the main course and went straight to dessert.
Fresh fruit makes a healthy dessert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pudding - sweet dish following the main course (mainly in British English)
  • Antonym: main course - the principal dish of a meal

Etymology

From French *dessert*, from *desservir* (to clear the table).
