bicyclic

1. ADJ

/ baɪˈsaɪklɪk, ˌbaɪˈsɪklɪk / (AmE), / bʌɪˈsɪklɪk, baɪˈsɪklɪk / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) having molecules consisting of two fused rings.
having two fused molecular rings
medical

Examples

Naphthalene is a classic example of a bicyclic aromatic hydrocarbon.
Many alkaloids found in nature have a bicyclic structure at their core.
Researchers synthesized a new bicyclic compound for use in pharmaceuticals.

Etymology

From bi- (two) + cyclic (pertaining to a ring or cycle).
