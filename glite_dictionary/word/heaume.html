heaume

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm / (AmE), / həʊm / (BrE)
a large type of medieval helmet that covers the entire head and rests on the shoulders, used by knights and soldiers.
large medieval helmet covering the head and shoulders
archaic

Examples

The knight placed his heavy heaume over his head before riding into battle.
In the museum, a 14th-century heaume is displayed among other medieval armours.
During the reenactment, he wore a replica heaume as part of his costume.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helmet - a protective covering for the head, often used in battle or sports

Etymology

From Old French 'heaume', from Frankish *helm (helmet), related to German 'Helm'.
