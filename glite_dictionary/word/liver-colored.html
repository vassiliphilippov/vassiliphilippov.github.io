liver-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪvər ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈlɪvə ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having a reddish-brown color, resembling the color of a liver
reddish-brown in color

Examples

The dog had a beautiful liver-colored coat that shone in the sunlight.
She chose a liver-colored fabric for the curtains in the study.
Many spaniels are known for their distinctive liver-colored fur.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reddish-brown - having a brown color with a hint of red

Etymology

Formed from 'liver' (organ) + '-colored'.
