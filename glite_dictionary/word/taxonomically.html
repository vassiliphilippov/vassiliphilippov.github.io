taxonomically

1. ADV

/ tækˈsɑːnəmɪkli / (AmE), / tækˈsɒnəmɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to taxonomy or the classification of organisms.
in relation to taxonomy

Examples

The two species are taxonomically classified within the same family.
Birds and reptiles are taxonomically closer than many people realize.
The samples differ taxonomically but have similar ecological roles.

Synset Examples

closely related taxonomically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: systematically - in a methodical and organized way

Etymology

From taxonomy + -ically, ultimately from Greek taxis (arrangement) and -nomia (method).
