idiot-proof

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪdiət ˌpruːf / (AmE), / ˈɪdiət ˌpruːf / (BrE)
designed to be extremely easy to use or operate, so that anyone can do it successfully
very easy to use or operate
informal

Examples

The new coffee machine is idiot-proof—just press one button and it makes your drink.
They created an idiot-proof installation guide for people with no technical experience.
Her phone has an idiot-proof interface that anyone can master within minutes.

Synset Examples

This design is idiot-proof, ensuring it functions flawlessly.
The instructions are idiot-proof, making it easy for anyone.
The gadget claims to be idiot-proof, even for beginners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: user-friendly - easy for anyone to use or understand
  • Antonym: complicated - difficult to understand or use
