birdcage

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːdˌkeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdˌkeɪdʒ / (BrE)
A cage designed to keep and house a pet bird.
cage for keeping a bird

Examples

She hung a beautiful birdcage in the living room window.
The parrot spent most of its day inside its birdcage.
They cleaned the birdcage every morning to keep it fresh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cage - a structure of bars or wires in which animals are confined
