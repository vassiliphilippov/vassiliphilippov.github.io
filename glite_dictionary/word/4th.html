4th

1. ADJ

/ fɔrθ / (AmE), / fɔːθ / (BrE)
coming immediately after the third in position, order, or rank; ordinal form of four
ordinal number: number four in order

Examples

She finished 4th in the race, just missing the medals.
The meeting will take place on the 4th of July.
This is the 4th time I have asked you to be quiet.
