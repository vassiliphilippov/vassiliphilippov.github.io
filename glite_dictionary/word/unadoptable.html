unadoptable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈdɑp.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈdɒp.tə.bəl / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) considered unlikely or impossible to be placed for adoption.
not likely to be adopted

Examples

Many older animals at the shelter are deemed unadoptable because of medical or behavioral issues.
The agency worked tirelessly to find homes for children once considered unadoptable.
Shelter staff were heartbroken to label the dog as unadoptable after repeated unsuccessful placements.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: adoptable - able to be placed for adoption

Etymology

Formed from un- (not) + adoptable (suitable for adoption).
