Agelaius

1. NOUN

/ əˈɡɛliəs / (AmE), / əˈɡiːlɪəʊs / (BrE)
A genus of American blackbirds, including the red-winged blackbird and closely related species.
genus of American blackbirds

Examples

The genus Agelaius contains several species of North American blackbirds.
Scientists observed that members of Agelaius often nest in large colonies.
The distinctive coloration of Agelaius species makes them easily recognizable to birdwatchers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red-winged blackbird - a species of blackbird in the genus Agelaius

Etymology

From Greek *agelaios* (gregarious, pertaining to a herd).
