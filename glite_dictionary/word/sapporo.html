Sapporo

1. PROPN

/ səˈpɔːroʊ / (AmE), / səˈpɔːrəʊ / (BrE)
a large commercial city in northern Japan, located on the western part of Hokkaido island
city in northern Japan, on Hokkaido

Examples

The annual Snow Festival in Sapporo attracts thousands of visitors from around the world.
During my trip to Japan, I enjoyed fresh seafood in Sapporo.
Sapporo hosted the 1972 Winter Olympic Games, the first ever held in Asia.

Etymology

From Japanese 札幌 (Sapporo), the city's native name.
