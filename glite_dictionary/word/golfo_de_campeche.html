Golfo de Campeche

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡoʊlfoʊ deɪ kɑmˈpeɪtʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡɒlfəʊ deɪ kæmˈpeɪtʃeɪ / (BrE)
a region of the Gulf of Mexico located off the western coast of the Yucatán Peninsula.
gulf area west of Yucatán Peninsula

Examples

The oil industry is active in the Golfo de Campeche due to the region's rich reserves.
A powerful hurricane made landfall near the Golfo de Campeche last night.
Fishermen often travel to the Golfo de Campeche to take advantage of its abundant marine life.

Etymology

Spanish, meaning 'Bay of Campeche', named after the Mexican city of Campeche.
