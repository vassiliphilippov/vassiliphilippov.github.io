Shreveport

1. PROPN

/ ˈʃriːvpɔːrt / (AmE), / ˈʃriːvpɔːt / (BrE)
A city in northwest Louisiana, United States, situated on the Red River near the Texas border.
city in northwest Louisiana, US

Examples

She was born and raised in Shreveport, where her family still lives.
The conference will be held in Shreveport next summer.
Many travelers stop in Shreveport before continuing on to Texas.

Etymology

Named after Henry Miller Shreve, an American steamboat captain and river engineer.
