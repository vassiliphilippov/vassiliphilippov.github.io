fiftysomething

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪftiˌsʌmθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪftiˌsʌmθɪŋ / (BrE)
a person who is between the ages of 50 and 59; or describing someone or something in that age range
a person in their fifties

Examples

As a fiftysomething, he started a new career in teaching.
Many fiftysomethings are choosing to travel more during their free time.
The study focused on the health habits of fiftysomethings in urban areas.

Synset Examples

In her fiftysomething years, she's learned a lot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fortysomething - a person in their forties
  • Degree Higher: sixtysomething - a person in their sixties
  • Degree Lower: fortysomething - a person in their forties
