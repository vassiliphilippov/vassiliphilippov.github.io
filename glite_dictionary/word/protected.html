protected

1. ADJ

/ prəˈtɛktɪd / (AmE), / prəˈtɛktɪd / (BrE)
kept safe from harm, danger, or injury; guarded or shielded from potential threats or loss.
kept safe from harm or danger

Examples

Certain areas of the rainforest are now protected by government law.
A protected witness testified in the high-profile case.
The eggs were kept in a protected enclosure to prevent predators from reaching them.

Synset Examples

the most protected spot I could find

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unprotected - not protected; exposed to harm or danger

2. ADJ

/ prəˈtɛktɪd / (AmE), / prəˈtɛktɪd / (BrE)
kept safe from harm, injury, or destruction by being shielded or guarded
kept safe from harm or destruction

Examples

The rare plant is protected by a fence to prevent damage from animals.
Some species are protected by law to prevent them from becoming extinct.
He felt protected from the cold by his thick coat.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unprotected - not kept safe from harm or danger
  • Synonym: secure - free from danger or threat
  • Synonym: safe - not in danger; secure

Etymology

From the verb 'protect,' from Latin 'protectus,' past participle of 'protegere' (to cover in front).
