punctilious

1. ADJ

/ pʌŋkˈtɪliəs / (AmE), / pʌŋkˈtɪliəs / (BrE)
showing great attention to detail or correct behaviour; very careful and precise.
showing great attention to detail

Examples

She is punctilious about checking every detail in her reports before submitting them.
The punctilious waiter ensured that every aspect of our dinner was perfect.
His punctilious adherence to protocol impressed the visiting diplomats.

Synset Examples

meticulous research
punctilious in his attention to rules of etiquette

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meticulous - showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
  • Antonym: careless - not giving sufficient attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors

Etymology

From Italian 'puntiglioso' (fine point), from Latin 'punctum' (point).
