Espana

1. PROPN

/ esˈpɑːn.jə / (AmE), / esˈpa.nja / (BrE)
The Spanish name for Spain, a country in southwestern Europe on the Iberian Peninsula, known officially as the Kingdom of Spain.
Spanish name for Spain (country)

Examples

Many works of art from Espana are displayed in renowned museums around the world.
After traveling through Portugal, we crossed the border into Espana for a week-long tour.
The cuisine of Espana reflects its diverse regions and rich history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Spain - a country in southwestern Europe officially called the Kingdom of Spain

Etymology

Spanish form of Latin 'Hispania', the ancient Roman name for the Iberian Peninsula.
