unestablished

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪˈstæblɪʃt / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈstæblɪʃt / (BrE)
not firmly founded, recognized, or accepted; not established
not established or recognized

Examples

Her theory remained unestablished in the scientific community for several years.
The company is investing in an unestablished market that shows promise for growth.
He has an unestablished reputation in the field, so few people know his work.

Synset Examples

a reputation as yet unestablished

Related Terms

  • Antonym: established - having been in existence for a long time and therefore recognized and generally accepted
