blinded

1. ADJ

/ ˈblaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˈblaɪndɪd / (BrE)
Unable to see because of injury, disease, or other cause; deprived of sight.
unable to see; deprived of sight

Examples

After the accident, he was blinded in one eye and had to adjust to his new reality.
The explosion blinded the miner, leaving him unable to continue his work underground.
She was temporarily blinded by the bright light shining in her eyes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blind - unable to see; lacking sight
