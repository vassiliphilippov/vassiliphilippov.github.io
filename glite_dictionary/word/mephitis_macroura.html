Mephitis macroura

1. NOUN

/ məˈfaɪtɪs məˈkraʊrə / (AmE), / mɛˈfɪtɪs mæˈkrɔːrə / (BrE)
A species of skunk, commonly known as the hooded skunk, found in Mexico and the southernmost parts of the southwestern United States.
hooded skunk species

Examples

The Mephitis macroura is easily recognized by its long white hood of fur.
Researchers observed several Mephitis macroura in the arid valleys of northern Mexico.
Unlike other skunks, the Mephitis macroura often forages in open grasslands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hooded skunk - Common name for Mephitis macroura, a species of skunk

Etymology

From Latin: 'mephitis' (noxious vapor, skunk) and 'macroura' (long-tailed).
