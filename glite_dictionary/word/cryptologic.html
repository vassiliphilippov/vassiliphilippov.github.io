cryptologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkrɪptəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌkrɪptəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to cryptology or the study of codes and ciphers, especially in decoding secret messages.
relating to cryptology or code-breaking

Examples

The agency specializes in cryptologic research and secret communications.
She has a background in both mathematics and cryptologic analysis.
The cryptologic community works closely with intelligence organizations worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cryptographic - relating to cryptography (encoding/decoding information)

Etymology

From Greek kryptos (hidden) and logos (word, study), via 'cryptology'.
