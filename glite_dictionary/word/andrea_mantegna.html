Andrea Mantegna

1. PROPN

/ ænˈdreɪ.ə mænˈteɪn.jə / (AmE), / anˈdreː.a manˈteɲ.ɲa / (BrE)
(1431–1506) Italian painter and engraver known for his Renaissance frescoes and influence on perspective in art.
Italian Renaissance painter and engraver

Examples

Andrea Mantegna is celebrated for his masterful use of perspective in Renaissance painting.
The frescoes by Andrea Mantegna in the Camera degli Sposi are considered artistic masterpieces.
Art historians often study Andrea Mantegna when examining the development of Italian Renaissance art.
