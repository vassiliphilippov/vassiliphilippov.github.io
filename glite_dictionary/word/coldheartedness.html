coldheartedness

1. NOUN

/ ˌkoʊldˈhɑrtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌkəʊldˈhɑːtɪdnəs / (BrE)
a lack of compassion or concern for the welfare of others; emotional insensitivity or indifference
lack of compassion or concern for others

Examples

Her coldheartedness shocked everyone at the funeral.
Despite his reputation for coldheartedness, he secretly donates to charity.
The villain's coldheartedness made her feared by all.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: compassion - sympathetic pity and concern for the sufferings of others
  • Synonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern

Etymology

From 'coldhearted' (cold + hearted) + -ness, meaning the quality of being emotionally cold.
