Plectrophenax nivalis

1. NOUN

/ plɛk.trəˈfiː.næks naɪˈveɪ.lɪs / (AmE), / plɛk.trəˈfiː.næks nɪˈvɑː.lɪs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the snow bunting, a small, white and brown songbird found in Arctic and northern regions.
scientific name for snow bunting

Examples

The migration of Plectrophenax nivalis is closely monitored by ornithologists each year.
We spotted a flock of Plectrophenax nivalis feeding along the snowy shore.
Plectrophenax nivalis is easily recognized by its predominantly white plumage during winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snow bunting - a small, white and brown Arctic songbird

Etymology

From Greek 'plektron' (spur) and 'phenax' (imposter), with Latin 'nivalis' (snowy).
