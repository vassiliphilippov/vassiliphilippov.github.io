Yangon

1. PROPN

/ jæŋˈɡɒn / (AmE), / jæŋˈɡɒn / (BrE)
the largest city and former capital of Myanmar, located in the south near the Irrawaddy river delta
largest city in Myanmar

Examples

Many travelers start their journey through Myanmar in Yangon due to its international airport.
The Shwedagon Pagoda, located in Yangon, is one of the country's most sacred sites.
Business opportunities in Yangon have grown since Myanmar opened up to international trade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Rangoon - former name of Yangon, Myanmar's largest city

Etymology

Formerly known as Rangoon; from Burmese Yangon.
