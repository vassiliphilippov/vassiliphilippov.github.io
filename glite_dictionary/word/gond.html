Gond

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑnd / (AmE), / ɡɒnd / (BrE)
a member of an indigenous people of south central India, historically classified as a tribal group
member of an indigenous Indian people

Examples

The Gond people have a rich tradition of oral storytelling and art.
Anthropologists have studied the rituals and customs of the Gond community for decades.
Many Gond now live in urban areas while maintaining their cultural heritage.

Etymology

From Hindi 'गोण्ड' (Goṇḍ), referring to the ethnic group.
