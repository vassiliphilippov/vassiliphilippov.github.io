grievous

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡriːvəs / (AmE), / ˈɡriːvəs / (BrE)
causing great pain, suffering, or severe harm; very serious or severe.
very serious or severe; causing great harm

Examples

The accident left him with grievous injuries requiring immediate surgery.
They expressed deep regret over the grievous error made by the company.
A grievous loss struck the family, changing their lives forever.
