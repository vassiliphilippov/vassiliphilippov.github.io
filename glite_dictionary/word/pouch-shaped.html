pouch-shaped

1. ADJ

/ paʊtʃ ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / paʊtʃ ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of a pouch; resembling a pouch in form.
having the shape of a pouch

Examples

The flower has a distinctive pouch-shaped base that stores nectar.
Some marsupials have a pouch-shaped fold of skin for carrying their young.
The pouch-shaped cavity allows the animal to transport food easily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sac-like - resembling or shaped like a sac
