to bodypaint

1. VERB

/ ˈbɑːdiˌpeɪnt / (AmE), / ˈbɒdiˌpeɪnt / (BrE)
to decorate or cover the human body with paint, usually for artistic, cultural, or entertainment purposes.
decorate a body with paint

Examples

The artist will bodypaint the model for the festival tomorrow.
He loves to bodypaint abstract designs at music events.
They have bodypainted hundreds of athletes for the competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paint - to cover a surface with paint
