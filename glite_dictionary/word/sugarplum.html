sugarplum

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃʊɡɚˌplʌm / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡəˌplʌm / (BrE)
A small round or oval sweet, typically made of sugar and often including fruit or nuts.
a small sweet candy

Examples

For the holidays, the shop sold handmade sugarplums alongside other traditional treats.
She dreamed of sugarplums and gingerbread on Christmas Eve.
A bowl of colorful sugarplums sat on the table during the festivities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: candy - A sweet food made with sugar or syrup combined with fruit, chocolate, or nuts.

Etymology

From sugar + plum, originally referring to a comfit (nut or seed coated with sugar).
