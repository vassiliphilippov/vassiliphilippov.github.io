otherworldly

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌðɚˈwɝːld.li / (AmE), / ˌʌð.əˈwɜːld.li / (BrE)
seeming to belong to or come from another world; strange or unearthly, not in accordance with ordinary experience or nature.
unearthly; not of this world

Examples

The mountain was shrouded in an otherworldly mist that made it seem magical.
Her singing voice has an otherworldly quality that captivates audiences.
The sculpture's otherworldly appearance made it stand out in the gallery.

Synset Examples

find transcendental motives for sublunary action

Related Terms

  • Antonym: earthly - relating to the physical world or typical human experience
  • Synonym: supernatural - attributed to forces beyond scientific understanding or the laws of nature

Etymology

Formed from 'other' + 'worldly', literally meaning 'from another world'.
