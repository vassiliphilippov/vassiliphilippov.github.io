shortcut

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɔrtˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt.kʌt / (BrE)
a route that is shorter than the usual way, taken to save time or distance
a quicker or shorter route

Examples

We decided to take a shortcut through the park to get home faster.
He found a shortcut that cut ten minutes off his daily commute.
If you know a good shortcut, please show me.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: detour - a longer or indirect route taken to avoid something or because the usual route is blocked
