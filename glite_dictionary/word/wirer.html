wirer

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪərə / (BrE)
a person who sends a telegram
person who sends a telegram

Examples

The wirer waited anxiously at the counter for a response from the city office.
In the early 1900s, every wirer relied on skilled telegraph operators for quick service.
As a frequent wirer, he was well-known at the telegraph office.

Etymology

From 'wire', referring to the telegraph wire used for sending messages.

2. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪərə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to install or repair electrical wiring
person who installs or repairs wiring

Examples

The wirer skillfully connected all the new lights in the renovated office.
Before the inspection, a wirer was called to check the apartment's electrical system.
She trained as a wirer and found steady work on construction sites.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electrician - a person who installs, maintains, or repairs electrical equipment
