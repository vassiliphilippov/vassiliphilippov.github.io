terminal

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɜrmɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈtɜːmɪnəl / (BrE)
Relating to or occurring at the end of a fixed period or term.
at the end of a term or period

Examples

Final grades are often determined by terminal examinations at the end of the academic year.
Employees receive their terminal payments when their contract concludes.
The terminal assessment is designed to evaluate all that students have learned throughout the course.

Synset Examples

terminal examinations
terminal payments

Etymology

From Latin 'terminalis', from 'terminus' (end, boundary).
