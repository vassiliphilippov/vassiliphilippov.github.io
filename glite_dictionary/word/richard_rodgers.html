Richard Rodgers

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɪtʃərd ˈrɑːdʒərz / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃəd ˈrɒdʒəz / (BrE)
(1902–1979) An American composer known for his influential musicals, often in collaboration with Oscar Hammerstein II and Lorenz Hart.
American composer of musical comedies

Examples

The music of Richard Rodgers continues to inspire generations of theatre lovers.
They attended a performance of a classic Richard Rodgers and Hammerstein musical.
Many consider Richard Rodgers one of the greatest composers in Broadway history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Oscar Hammerstein II - American lyricist and librettist, Rodgers's longtime collaborator
  • Synonym: Lorenz Hart - American lyricist who collaborated with Rodgers
