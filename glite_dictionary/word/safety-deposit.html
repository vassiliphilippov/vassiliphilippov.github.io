safety-deposit

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪfti dɪˌpɑzɪt / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti dɪˌpɒzɪt / (BrE)
a fireproof metal box, usually in a bank, used for securely storing valuables such as documents or jewelry
secure box in a bank for valuables

Examples

The bank keeps their customers' jewels in a secure safety-deposit to protect them from theft and fire.
She opened her safety-deposit to retrieve important legal documents kept there for years.
After his grandmother passed away, he discovered old coins in a dusty safety-deposit at the local bank.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safe-deposit box - a small secure container in a bank for valuables
