do-si-do

1. NOUN

/ ˌdoʊ si ˈdoʊ / (AmE), / ˌdəʊ siː ˈdəʊ / (BrE)
a square dance move in which two dancers circle around each other back to back before returning to their places
square dance movement circling back to back

Examples

The caller instructed everyone to do-si-do during the lively square dance.
She learned how to do-si-do at her first barn dance.
Couples moved swiftly as they performed the do-si-do on the dance floor.

Etymology

From French 'dos-à-dos' (back to back).
