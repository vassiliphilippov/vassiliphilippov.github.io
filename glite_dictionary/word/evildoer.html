evildoer

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːvɪlˌduːər / (AmE), / ˈiːvɪlˌduːə / (BrE)
a person who does evil or wicked things; a wrongdoer
person who does evil or wrong

Examples

Many believed the mysterious figure stalking the town was an evildoer with sinister motives.
The fairy tale warns children to be wary of any evildoer disguising themselves as a friend.
He swore to bring the evildoer to justice for the harm he had caused.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrongdoer - a person who does wrong or illegal things
  • Synonym: villain - a wicked or criminal person
  • Antonym: saint - a person regarded as exceptionally virtuous or holy

Etymology

Formed from 'evil' + 'doer', meaning one who does evil.
