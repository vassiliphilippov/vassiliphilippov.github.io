pleaser

1. NOUN

/ ˈpliːzər / (AmE), / ˈpliːzə / (BrE)
a person or thing that entertains or delights others, especially an audience
entertainer; person who delights others

Examples

The magician was a real pleaser at the children's party, keeping everyone laughing for hours.
The band is known as a crowd pleaser because of their energetic performances.
That dish is always a pleaser at family gatherings, enjoyed by kids and adults alike.

Synset Examples

he is quite the crowd pleaser

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entertainer - someone whose job is to entertain others
  • Antonym: bore - someone or something that fails to interest or amuse an audience

Etymology

From the verb 'please' + -er suffix indicating a person or thing that performs an action.
