unended

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɛndɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɛndɪd / (BrE)
not ended; lacking an end or conclusion.
not ended or concluded

Examples

The story was left unended, leaving the audience in suspense.
His career remains unended as he continues to break records every year.
After years of research, the scientist declared the mystery remained unended.

Synset Examples

he did with the book still unended

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unfinished - not finished or completed
  • Antonym: concluded - brought to an end or finish

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'ended.'
