diddlysquat

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪdliˌskwɑt / (AmE), / ˈdɪdliˌskwɒt / (BrE)
A very small or worthless amount; nothing at all.
nothing; a worthless amount
informal american

Examples

After all that work, I got diddlysquat as a reward.
He claims to know a lot, but really he knows diddlysquat.
The settlement offered the workers diddlysquat compared to what they deserved.

Synset Examples

you don't know jack

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jack - nothing; a worthless amount (US slang)
  • Synonym: nothing - not anything; zero

Etymology

Possibly an alteration of 'diddly' (nothing) and 'squat' (nothing), both American slang terms.
