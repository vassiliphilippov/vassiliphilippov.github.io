Nowruz

1. NOUN

/ noʊˈruːz / (AmE), / naʊˈruːz / (BrE)
The Persian New Year, celebrated at the vernal equinox in Iran, Azerbaijan, Afghanistan, Pakistan, parts of India, and by Kurds.
Persian New Year festival at equinox

Examples

People gather with family and friends to celebrate Nowruz with special foods and rituals.
During Nowruz, it is traditional to clean the house and buy new clothes to welcome the new year.
Many cities host public festivities on Nowruz, featuring music, dance, and cultural performances.

Etymology

From Persian نوروز (nowruz), meaning 'new day'.
