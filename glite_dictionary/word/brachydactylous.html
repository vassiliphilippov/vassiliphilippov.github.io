brachydactylous

1. ADJ

/ ˌbrækiˈdæktɪləs / (AmE), / ˌbrækɪˈdæktɪləs / (BrE)
having abnormally short fingers or toes
having abnormally short fingers or toes
medical

Examples

The geneticist explained that the patient was brachydactylous due to a rare mutation.
Several members of the family are brachydactylous, having noticeably short fingers.
In some cases, brachydactylous traits can be inherited from one or both parents.

Etymology

From Greek 'brachys' (short) + 'daktylos' (finger).
