Lepidocybium

1. NOUN

/ lɛpɪdoʊˈsɪbiəm / (AmE), / lɛpɪdəˈsɪbiəm / (BrE)
A genus of predatory fish in the family Gempylidae, known especially for the species Lepidocybium flavobrunneum (escolar).
a fish genus in Gempylidae family

Examples

The Lepidocybium genus includes the escolar, a fish prized for its rich, oily flesh.
Biologists identified the specimen as belonging to the Lepidocybium genus during their deep-sea survey.
Research on Lepidocybium has provided insights into the diet and habitat of escolar.

Etymology

From Greek 'lepis' (scale) and 'kybion' (little cube), referring to fish scale characteristics.
