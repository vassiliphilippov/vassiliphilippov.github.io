silvery-white

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪlvəri ˈwaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəri ˈwaɪt / (BrE)
having a white color that resembles silver; shining with a lustrous or metallic white appearance.
white resembling silver in color

Examples

The metal had a silvery-white sheen that caught the sunlight beautifully.
Her dress sparkled in a silvery-white glow under the ballroom lights.
Sodium is a soft, silvery-white element that reacts violently with water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silver - a shiny grayish-white color or the metal itself
