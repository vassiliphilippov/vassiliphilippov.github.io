Weimar

1. PROPN

/ ˈwaɪmɑr / (AmE), / ˈvaɪmaː / (BrE)
a city in central Germany, known as the site of the adoption of the Weimar Republic's constitution in 1919
city in central Germany, site of 1919 constitution

Examples

Many tourists visit Weimar to explore its rich cultural and political history.
The constitution of the Weimar Republic was drafted in Weimar in 1919.
Weimar is famous for its association with writers like Goethe and Schiller.
