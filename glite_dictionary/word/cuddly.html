cuddly

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌd.li / (AmE), / ˈkʌd.li / (BrE)
pleasant to hug or hold close; inviting cuddling or affection.
inviting to be hugged or cuddled

Examples

The cuddly kitten curled up on my lap as soon as I sat down.
Children often love to sleep with a cuddly teddy bear at night.
His new sweater is so soft and cuddly that he wears it every day.

Synset Examples

a cuddlesome baby
a cuddly teddybear

Related Terms

  • Synonym: huggable - pleasant or suitable for hugging
