lanai

1. NOUN

/ ləˈnaɪ / (AmE), / ləˈnaɪ / (BrE)
a veranda or roofed patio, often furnished and used as an outdoor living room, especially in Hawaii
roofed patio or veranda (esp. Hawaii)

Examples

We ate breakfast every morning on the lanai overlooking the ocean.
The house featured a spacious lanai perfect for entertaining guests.
After work, she relaxed on the lanai with a good book.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: veranda - a roofed platform along the outside of a house

Etymology

From Hawaiian 'lānai' (porch, balcony).
