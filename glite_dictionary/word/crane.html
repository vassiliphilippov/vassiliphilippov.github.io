Crane

1. PROPN

/ kreɪn / (AmE), / kreɪn / (BrE)
a small constellation in the southern sky, located near the constellation Phoenix
constellation in the southern sky

Examples

The constellation Crane is not visible from most locations in the Northern Hemisphere.
Early astronomers mapped Crane near the brighter constellation Phoenix.
She enjoyed identifying Crane on clear southern nights with her telescope.

Etymology

Named after the bird 'crane', from Latin 'Grus', meaning 'crane'.
