horologer

1. NOUN

/ hɔˈrɑlədʒər / (AmE), / hɒˈrɒlədʒə / (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs watches and clocks
watch or clock maker and repairer

Examples

The antique horologer carefully restored the century-old pocket watch to working order.
As a skilled horologer, she opened her own shop specializing in both modern and vintage timepieces.
Many horologers dedicate years to mastering the intricate art of watchmaking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watchmaker - a person who makes or repairs watches

Etymology

From French 'horologer', from Latin 'horologium' (clock, timepiece).
