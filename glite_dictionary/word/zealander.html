Zealander

1. NOUN

/ ˈziː.lænd.ər / (AmE), / ˈziː.lənd.ə / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Zealand, especially the island in Denmark.
person from Zealand (usually Denmark)

Examples

As a proud Zealander, she often spoke about life on the island.
Many Zealanders celebrate local traditions that date back centuries.
The museum features artifacts donated by Zealander families.

Etymology

Formed from 'Zealand' (the Danish island name) + '-er' (demonym suffix).
