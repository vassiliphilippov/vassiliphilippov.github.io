intracranial

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.trəˈkreɪ.ni.əl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trəˈkreɪ.ni.əl / (BrE)
Situated or occurring within the skull.
within the skull
medical

Examples

The doctors discovered an intracranial tumor during the MRI scan.
He suffered from increased intracranial pressure after the head injury.
Certain infections can lead to severe intracranial complications.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: extracranial - outside the skull

Etymology

From Latin 'intra-' (within) + 'cranium' (skull).
