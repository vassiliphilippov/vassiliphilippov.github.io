roughdried

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌfˌdraɪd / (AmE), / ˈrʌfˌdraɪd / (BrE)
(of laundry) dried after washing but not ironed or finished.
laundry dried but not ironed

Examples

The clothes were roughdried and left in a basket until someone could iron them.
I prefer my shirts roughdried because they are easier to iron later.
She asked the laundry service to have the linens roughdried rather than fully pressed.

Etymology

Formed from 'rough' + past participle of 'dry.'
