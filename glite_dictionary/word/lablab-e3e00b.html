Lablab

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæb.læb / (AmE), / ˈlæb.læb / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the pea family, with one species, the hyacinth bean (Lablab purpureus), widely cultivated for food and ornamental purposes.
genus of flowering plants (hyacinth bean)

Examples

The Lablab genus includes the popular hyacinth bean, grown in tropical regions.
Botanists classify the hyacinth bean under the Lablab genus.
Farmers often plant Lablab as a cover crop to enrich soil fertility.

Etymology

From Latin, adopted from the Arabic 'lablab', referring to the hyacinth bean.
