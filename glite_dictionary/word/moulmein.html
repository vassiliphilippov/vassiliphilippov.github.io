Moulmein

1. PROPN

/ muːlˈmeɪn / (AmE), / muːlˈmeɪn / (BrE)
A city and port in southern Myanmar (Burma), located on the Gulf of Martaban.
city and port in southern Myanmar

Examples

George Orwell was stationed in Moulmein during his time in Burma.
The city of Moulmein is known for its scenic waterfront and colonial architecture.
Many traders in the 19th century passed through Moulmein on their way to other parts of Southeast Asia.

Etymology

From Burmese Mawlamyine, anglicized as Moulmein during British colonial rule.
