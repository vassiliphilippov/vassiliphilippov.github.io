ruttish

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌtɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrʌtɪʃ / (BrE)
feeling or expressing strong sexual desire
full of sexual desire

Examples

His ruttish behavior made everyone uncomfortable at the dinner party.
She tried to ignore his ruttish glances.
The novel describes the ruttish yearnings of its protagonist in vivid detail.

Synset Examples

feeling horny

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horny - sexually aroused
  • Synonym: lustful - showing strong sexual desire
  • Antonym: chaste - abstaining from sexual activity

Etymology

From Middle English rut, from Latin rūtus (past participle of ruere, to rush, used for 'rut' in animals) + -ish.
