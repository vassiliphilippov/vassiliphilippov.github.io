Antwerp

1. PROPN

/ ˈæntˌwɜrp / (AmE), / ˈæntwɜːp / (BrE)
a major city and port in northern Belgium, known for its diamond industry and historical financial significance
city and port in northern Belgium

Examples

The diamond district in Antwerp attracts traders from all over the world.
She traveled to Antwerp to study its remarkable architecture and museums.
Antwerp has been a vital European port for centuries.

Etymology

From Dutch Antwerpen, meaning 'to throw against', possibly referring to a legend about the river Scheldt.
