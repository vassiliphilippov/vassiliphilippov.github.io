chanted

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃæn.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːn.tɪd / (BrE)
spoken or sung rhythmically, often in a monotone, as in chanting lyrics or prayers.
sung or spoken rhythmically in monotone

Examples

The monk's chanted prayer echoed softly through the ancient cathedral.
The poem was chanted by the children during the ceremony.
Their chanted slogans filled the street with rhythmic energy.

Synset Examples

a chanted psalm

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intoned - uttered in a continuous or monotonous tone

Etymology

From Old French chanter, from Latin cantare (to sing).
