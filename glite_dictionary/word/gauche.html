gauche

1. ADJ

/ ɡoʊʃ / (AmE), / ɡəʊʃ / (BrE)
lacking social grace, sensitivity, or polish; socially awkward or tactless.
socially awkward or unpolished

Examples

He felt gauche at the elegant dinner party, unsure of which fork to use.
Her gauche remarks made the guests uncomfortable during the conversation.
Although he was intelligent, his gauche demeanor often led to misunderstandings.

Synset Examples

too gauche to leave the room when the conversation became intimate
their excellent manners always made me feel gauche

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awkward - lacking grace or ease in movement, behavior, or social situations
  • Antonym: polished - showing refinement and good manners

Etymology

From French 'gauche' (left, awkward).
