cucurbitaceous

1. ADJ

/ ˌkjuːkɚbɪˈteɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌkjuːkəˌbɪˈteɪʃəs / (BrE)
relating to or belonging to the plant family Cucurbitaceae, which includes gourds, cucumbers, squashes, and melons
of the gourd family Cucurbitaceae

Examples

The pumpkin is a cucurbitaceous vegetable widely cultivated around the world.
Many cucurbitaceous plants are grown for their edible fruits and seeds.
She specializes in the study of cucurbitaceous crops such as melons and squashes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gourd - plant belonging to Cucurbitaceae family, often with hard-rinded fruits

Etymology

From modern Latin Cucurbitaceae (the plant family) + -aceous (forming adjectives meaning 'of or pertaining to').
