performing

1. NOUN

/ pərˈfɔrmɪŋ / (AmE), / pəˈfɔːmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of acting a part or role in a drama or performance
theatrical acting or staging

Examples

The performing of Hamlet's role requires both skill and emotional depth.
Her passion for performing on stage was evident in every show.
Students studied different techniques of performing in classical theatre.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acting - the art or occupation of performing in plays or movies

Etymology

From verb 'perform' + suffix '-ing'.
