voguish

1. ADJ

/ ˈvoʊɡɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈvəʊɡɪʃ / (BrE)
fashionable or in accordance with the latest trend or style
fashionable, trendy

Examples

Her voguish haircut was admired by everyone at the party.
The boutique is known for its voguish selection of clothing each season.
He adopted voguish terms to impress his friends.

Synset Examples

trendy ideas
trendy clothes
voguish terminology

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trendy - very fashionable, up-to-date
  • Antonym: unfashionable - not in style or not popular

Etymology

From 'vogue' + '-ish', where 'vogue' comes from French 'vogue' meaning 'fashion'.

2. ADJ

/ ˈvoʊɡɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈvəʊɡɪʃ / (BrE)
following the current fashion or style; fashionable and elegant
fashionable; stylish

Examples

Her voguish new hairstyle turned heads wherever she went.
He always chooses voguish restaurants when taking clients out to dinner.
The magazine is known for featuring voguish designs by up-and-coming fashion houses.

Synset Examples

chic elegance
a smart new dress
a suit of voguish cut

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fashionable - following the latest trends in style or fashion

Etymology

From 'vogue' (fashion, popularity)+ '-ish' (adjective suffix); 'vogue' from French 'vogue' (fashion).
