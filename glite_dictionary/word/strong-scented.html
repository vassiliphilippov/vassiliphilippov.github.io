strong-scented

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrɔŋ ˌsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈstrɒŋ ˌsɛntɪd / (BrE)
having a powerful or intense smell
having a strong smell

Examples

The strong-scented flowers filled the entire room with their aroma.
Some cheeses are known for being particularly strong-scented.
She avoided the strong-scented perfume because it gave her a headache.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell
  • Degree Higher: pungent - having a sharply strong taste or smell
