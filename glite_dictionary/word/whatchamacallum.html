whatchamacallum

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌtʃəməˌkɔləm / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃəməˌkɔːləm / (BrE)
an object or thing whose name is forgotten or unknown
unspecified or forgotten thing
informal

Examples

Can you pass me that whatchamacallum on the shelf?
I need the whatchamacallum to fix my phone, but I can’t remember its real name.
She was looking for a whatchamacallum to open the can.

Synset Examples

she eased the ball-shaped doodad back into its socket
there may be some great new gizmo around the corner that you will want to use

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whatchamacallit - an object whose name is forgotten or not known
  • Synonym: thingamajig - an object or gadget whose name is not known or forgotten
  • Synonym: gizmo - a gadget, especially one whose name is unknown or forgotten

Etymology

Variant of 'whatchamacallit', from the phrase 'what you may call it'.
