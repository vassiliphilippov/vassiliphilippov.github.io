Yosemite

1. PROPN

/ joʊˈsɛmɪti / (AmE), / jəʊˈsɛmɪti / (BrE)
a series of waterfalls in Yosemite National Park, California, known for their height and seasonal flow.
famous waterfalls in Yosemite National Park, California

Examples

Tourists from around the world visit Yosemite to witness the breathtaking waterfalls.
In late summer, Yosemite often slows to a delicate trickle as the snowmelt fades.
The mist from Yosemite creates rainbows on sunny days, delighting park visitors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Yosemite National Park - a national park in the western Sierra Nevada of Central California, site of Yosemite Falls

Etymology

From Miwok (indigenous Californian language) meaning 'killer' or 'they are killers'.
