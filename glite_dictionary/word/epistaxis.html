epistaxis

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛpɪˈstæksɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈstæksɪs / (BrE)
bleeding from the nose; nosebleed
bleeding from the nose
medical

Examples

If epistaxis is persistent, medical attention may be necessary.
The doctor treated his patient for recurrent epistaxis.
Dry climates can increase the risk of epistaxis in some individuals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nosebleed - a common term for bleeding from the nose

Etymology

From Greek epistaxis (a dripping), from epistazein (to drip on).
