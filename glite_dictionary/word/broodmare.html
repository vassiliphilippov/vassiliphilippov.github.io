broodmare

1. NOUN

/ ˈbruːdˌmɛr / (AmE), / ˈbruːdˌmeə / (BrE)
a female horse kept for breeding purposes
female horse used for breeding

Examples

The farm purchased a new broodmare to improve their racing stock.
That broodmare has produced several champion foals over the years.
After her racing career ended, she became a valuable broodmare.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stallion - an adult male horse used for breeding

Etymology

From 'brood' (to breed, produce young) + 'mare' (female horse).
