wheaten

1. ADJ

/ ˈwiːtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈwiːtən / (BrE)
made from, containing, or relating to wheat.
made from or relating to wheat

Examples

The bakery offers fresh wheaten rolls every morning.
She prefers wheaten flour for baking bread because of its texture and flavor.
A traditional Irish dinner might include wheaten bread served with butter.

Synset Examples

wheaten bread

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wheat - a cereal plant that is cultivated for its grain

Etymology

From wheat + -en (adjective forming suffix).
