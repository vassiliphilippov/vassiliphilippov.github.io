Sandril

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæn.drɪl / (AmE), / ˈsæn.drɪl / (BrE)
A trade name for reserpine, an antihypertensive drug extracted from the plant Rauwolfia serpentina.
trade name for reserpine (antihypertensive drug)

Examples

The doctor prescribed Sandril to help control her high blood pressure.
Patients taking Sandril should be monitored regularly for side effects.
Sandril is less commonly used today, but it was once a major treatment for hypertension.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reserpine - a drug used to treat high blood pressure, extracted from Rauwolfia serpentina
