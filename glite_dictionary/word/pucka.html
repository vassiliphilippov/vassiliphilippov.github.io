pucka

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʌkə / (AmE), / ˈpʌkə / (BrE)
absolutely genuine and of the highest quality; authentic or first-class.
genuine; first-class
british

Examples

His reputation as a pucka gentleman was well known throughout the city.
She insisted on buying only pucka products from reputable stores.
The hotel offered a truly pucka afternoon tea experience, complete with fine china and silverware.

Related Terms

Etymology

From Hindi 'pakka' (ripe, cooked, permanent, substantial), adopted into English during the British colonial period in India.
