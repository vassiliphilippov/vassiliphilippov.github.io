goral

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɔrəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːrəl / (BrE)
A small goat-antelope with short, conical horns native to the mountainous regions of southern and eastern Asia.
small Asian goat-antelope

Examples

The goral is well adapted to climbing steep, rocky slopes.
Researchers observed several gorals grazing on the mountain ledge.
A goral can often be spotted early in the morning near dense shrubs.

Etymology

From Nepali 'goral' (गोरल) or Hindi 'goral' (गोरल), referring to the animal.

2. NOUN

/ ˈɡɔrəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːrəl / (BrE)
a small goat-antelope native to the mountains of southern and eastern Asia, distinguished by short, conical horns and a stocky build
small Asian goat-antelope

Examples

The goral inhabits rocky cliffs and steep slopes in the Himalayan region.
Researchers observed a goral foraging early in the morning near the edge of the forest.
Unlike other mountain goats, the goral is known for its agility and solitary nature.

Etymology

From Hindi 'goral', referring to this animal.
