fancywork

1. NOUN

/ ˈfænsiˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈfænsiˌwɜːk / (BrE)
decorative needlework done to embellish fabric or garments, such as embroidery or lacework
decorative needlework

Examples

She spent her evenings doing fancywork to decorate handkerchiefs and pillowcases.
The exhibit showcased beautiful fancywork from the Victorian era.
His grandmother taught him how to do delicate fancywork with colored threads.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embroidery - art of decorating fabric with needle and thread
