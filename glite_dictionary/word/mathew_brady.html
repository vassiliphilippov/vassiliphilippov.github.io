Mathew Brady

1. PROPN

/ ˈmæθju ˈbreɪdi / (AmE), / ˈmæθju ˈbreɪdi / (BrE)
(1823–1896) A pioneering American photographer best known for his portraits and as the official Union photographer during the American Civil War.
pioneer American Civil War photographer

Examples

Mathew Brady is renowned for his extensive photographic documentation of the American Civil War.
The striking images taken by Mathew Brady have shaped how we view 19th-century history.
A collection of Mathew Brady's portraits was exhibited at the national gallery.
