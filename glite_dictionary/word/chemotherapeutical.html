chemotherapeutical

1. ADJ

/ ˌkiːmoʊˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌkɛmɪəʊˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or involving chemotherapy, especially in the treatment of diseases such as cancer.
relating to chemotherapy
medical

Examples

The hospital developed new chemotherapeutical protocols to improve patient outcomes.
Research continues into more effective chemotherapeutical agents for resistant cancers.
Her response to the chemotherapeutical treatment was closely monitored by her doctors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chemotherapeutic - relating to chemotherapy; another common adjectival form

Etymology

From chemo- (chemical) + therapeutical (relating to treatment).
