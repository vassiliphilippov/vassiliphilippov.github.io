Sabah

1. PROPN

/ səˈbɑ / (AmE), / səˈbɑː / (BrE)
a state in Malaysia, situated on the northern part of the island of Borneo, known for its diverse culture and natural attractions
state in Malaysia on Borneo

Examples

Mount Kinabalu is the highest peak in Sabah and attracts many climbers every year.
Sabah is famous for its rich biodiversity and beautiful islands.
She spent her vacation exploring the rainforests of Sabah in Borneo.
