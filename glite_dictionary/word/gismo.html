gismo

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪzmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪzməʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a small device or gadget, especially one whose name is unknown or forgotten
informal term for gadget or device
informal

Examples

He handed me a gismo that was supposed to measure humidity.
There's always a new gismo in his toolbox that I've never seen before.
Can you figure out what this gismo does?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gizmo - informal term for a small device or gadget
  • Synonym: gadget - a small mechanical or electronic device

Etymology

Alteration of 'gizmo,' of unknown origin, first recorded in American English in the 1940s.

/ ˈɡɪzmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪzməʊ / (BrE)
an object or device whose name is forgotten, unknown, or unspecified; a gadget.
unspecified device or gadget
informal

Examples

Can you hand me that gismo so I can fix the shelf?
Every kitchen drawer seems to be full of mysterious gismos and tools.
He invented a clever little gismo to help keep his wires organized.
