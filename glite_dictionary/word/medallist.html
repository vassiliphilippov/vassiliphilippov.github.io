medallist

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəlɪst / (BrE)
(in golf) the player with the lowest score in a medal play tournament.
winner in golf medal play

Examples

After an impressive round, she was named the medallist of the tournament.
He took great pride in becoming the medallist at the county golf championship.
The medallist celebrated his victory with his teammates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: winner - a person who wins something

Etymology

From medal + -ist, denoting a person distinguished by winning a medal.

2. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who has won a medal, especially in a sporting or competitive event
person who has won a medal

Examples

The Olympic medallist was greeted with cheers on her return home.
He became a gold medallist after his outstanding performance in the finals.
Several former medallists attended the ceremony to present awards to this year's winners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: winner - a person who wins something
  • Antonym: loser - a person who does not win

Etymology

From medal + -ist, denoting a person associated with medals.
