smoothhound

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmuðˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈsmuːðhaʊnd / (BrE)
A small, slender shark of the genus Mustelus, found in European coastal waters and known for its smooth skin and dog-like snout.
a small shark of European coastal waters

Examples

A smoothhound was spotted near the harbor by local fishermen.
The smoothhound is often found swimming in shallow, sandy bays.
Marine biologists are studying the feeding habits of the smoothhound.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dogfish - a small shark of various species, often with a dog-like snout

Etymology

From 'smooth' + 'hound', referring to its smooth skin and dog-like snout.
