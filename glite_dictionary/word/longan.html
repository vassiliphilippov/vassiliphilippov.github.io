longan

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔŋən / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋən / (BrE)
A tropical tree of southeastern Asia and Australia, grown for its sweet edible fruit similar to the lychee.
tropical fruit tree, related to lychee

Examples

Fresh longan is often served as a dessert in Southeast Asian cuisine.
The longan tree thrives in warm, humid climates and produces clusters of round fruit.
He prefers longan over lychee because of its subtly sweet flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lychee - another tropical fruit with juicy, translucent flesh

Etymology

From Cantonese lùhng-ngáahn (龍眼), literally 'dragon eye', referring to the fruit's appearance.
