pothook

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːtˌhʊk / (AmE), / ˈpɒtˌhʊk / (BrE)
a metal hook, often S-shaped, used to suspend a pot over a fire
S-shaped hook for suspending pots over fires

Examples

The old kitchen had a large iron pothook hanging over the hearth.
She adjusted the pothook to raise the pot closer to the flames.
Antique pothooks are now collected as historical kitchenware.
