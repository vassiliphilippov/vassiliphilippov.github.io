unindustrialized

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪnˈdʌstri.əˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈdʌstri.ə.laɪzd / (BrE)
(of a country, region, etc.) not having undergone industrial development; lacking industry or large-scale manufacturing.
not developed through industry

Examples

Many rural areas remain unindustrialized, relying primarily on agriculture for income.
The government hopes to attract factories to unindustrialized regions to boost employment.
Several unindustrialized countries face challenges in improving infrastructure and living standards.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: industrialized - having undergone industrial development

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'industrialized'.
