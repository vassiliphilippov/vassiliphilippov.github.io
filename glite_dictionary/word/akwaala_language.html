Akwa'ala language

1. NOUN

/ ɑːˈkwɑː.ɑː.lə ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɑːˈkwɑː.ɑː.lə ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Yuman language spoken by the Akwa'ala people of northern Baja California, Mexico
Yuman language of the Akwa'ala people

Examples

Scholars are working to document the Akwa'ala language before it becomes extinct.
