pak choi

1. NOUN

/ pæk tʃɔɪ / (AmE), / pæk tʃɔɪ / (BrE)
A Chinese vegetable with crisp white stalks and dark green leaves, commonly used in Asian cooking.
Chinese leafy vegetable

Examples

Stir-fried pak choi is a popular side dish in Chinese cuisine.
I usually add chopped pak choi to my noodle soup for extra flavor.
The market sells fresh pak choi alongside other Asian vegetables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bok choy - Variant spelling for a similar Chinese cabbage

Etymology

From Cantonese 白菜 (baak choi, lit. 'white vegetable').
