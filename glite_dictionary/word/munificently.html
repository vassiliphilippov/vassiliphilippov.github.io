munificently

1. ADV

/ mjuˈnɪfəsəntli / (AmE), / mjuːˈnɪfɪsəntli / (BrE)
In a very generous or lavish manner.
in a generous or lavish manner

Examples

The foundation donated munificently to rebuild the city library.
He rewarded his employees munificently for their hard work.
She always tips munificently at restaurants, earning the admiration of the staff.

Synset Examples

he gave liberally to several charities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: generously - in a generous manner
  • Antonym: stingily - in a mean or miserly manner

Etymology

From Latin 'munificentia' (generosity), via 'munificus' (generous).
