house-proud

1. ADJ

/ ˈhaʊsˌpraʊd / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌpraʊd / (BrE)
Proud of the cleanliness, appearance, or orderliness of one's home and furnishings.
proud of a well-kept home

Examples

She is extremely house-proud and spends hours cleaning every day.
Their house-proud attitude is evident from the immaculate living room.
Although busy with work, Mark remains house-proud and always keeps his apartment tidy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proud - feeling deep pleasure or satisfaction as a result of achievements, qualities, or possessions

Etymology

Formed from 'house' and 'proud', first attested in the late 19th century.
