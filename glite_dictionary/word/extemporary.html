extemporary

1. ADJ

/ ɛkˈstɛm.pəˌrɛr.i / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛmpərəri / (BrE)
Done with little or no preparation or planning; improvised or spontaneous.
done without preparation; improvised

Examples

The committee held an extemporary meeting to address the urgent issue.
Her extemporary remarks impressed the audience with their wit and relevance.
He delivered an extemporary speech that captivated everyone despite being unprepared.

Etymology
