rose-purple

1. ADJ

/ ˈroʊz ˌpɝːpəl / (AmE), / ˈrəʊz ˌpɜːpl / (BrE)
of a purple color that has a hint or tinge of rose; purplish with a slight pinkish or reddish shade
purple tinged with rose

Examples

The rose-purple sunset painted the horizon in breathtaking colors.
She wore a rose-purple dress to match the spring flowers.
The artist mixed red and blue paints to achieve a perfect rose-purple hue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mauve - a pale purple color
  • Degree Lower: lavender - a pale purple color with a bluish tint

Etymology

Formed from 'rose' (the flower/color) and 'purple'.
