Sir Lancelot

1. PROPN

/ sɚ ˈlænsəlɑt / (AmE), / sə ˈlæn.sə.lɒt / (BrE)
In Arthurian legend, one of the most famous Knights of the Round Table; renowned for his bravery, chivalry, and his romance with Queen Guinevere.
legendary Knight of the Round Table

Examples

Many stories describe Sir Lancelot's courage and loyalty to King Arthur.
The forbidden love between Sir Lancelot and Queen Guinevere is central to the Arthurian legends.
Children were fascinated by tales of Sir Lancelot and his adventures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: King Arthur - legendary king of Britain in Arthurian legend
  • Synonym: Guinevere - Queen in Arthurian legend, wife of King Arthur
