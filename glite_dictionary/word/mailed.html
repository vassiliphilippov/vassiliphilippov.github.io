mailed

1. ADJ

/ meɪld / (AmE), / meɪld / (BrE)
wearing or covered with protective mail (a type of armor made of metal rings or plates)
wearing protective mail armor

Examples

The mailed knight advanced with confidence, his chain armor gleaming in the sunlight.
Her mailed fist clutched the sword tightly, ready for battle.
The ancient manuscript depicted a mailed warrior defending the castle gates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armored - protected by armor
  • Antonym: unarmored - not protected by armor

Etymology

From 'mail' (armor made of metal rings), ultimately from Old French 'maille' (mesh).
