overemotional

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvɚɪˈmoʊʃənl̩ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪˈməʊʃənəl / (BrE)
displaying emotion to an excessive or abnormal degree; too emotional.
excessively emotional

Examples

The movie's script made the characters seem overemotional in even the simplest situations.
He became overemotional during the meeting and couldn't finish his presentation.
Some people say she's overemotional, but I think she's just honest about her feelings.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: emotional - showing strong feelings or emotions
  • Antonym: stoic - enduring pain or hardship without showing feelings

Etymology

From 'over-' meaning excessively + 'emotional'.
