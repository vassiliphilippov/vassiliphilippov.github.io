amnionic

1. ADJ

/ æmˈniːɑnɪk / (AmE), / æmˈniːənɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the amnion or characterized by having an amnion.
relating to the amnion
medical

Examples

The amnionic membrane protects the developing fetus throughout pregnancy.
The doctor explained the function of the amnionic sac during the first trimester.
Researchers studied the amnionic fluid to diagnose genetic conditions.

Synset Examples

amniotic membrane

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amniotic - relating to the amnion

Etymology

From 'amnion', from Greek amnion (a membrane enclosing the fetus).
