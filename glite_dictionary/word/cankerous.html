cankerous

1. ADJ

/ ˈkæŋ.kɚ.əs / (AmE), / ˈkaŋ.kər.əs / (BrE)
affected by or resembling a canker or ulcer; ulcerous.
having an ulcer or canker

Examples

The cankerous lesions caused her significant discomfort and required constant medical attention.
Gardeners often avoid planting susceptible species to prevent cankerous diseases.
A cankerous spot appeared on the bark of the tree, threatening its health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ulcerous - having or consisting of ulcers

Etymology

From Middle English cankerous, from Anglo-Norman cancreus, from Latin cancer (crab, ulcer, sore).
