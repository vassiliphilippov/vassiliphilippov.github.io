Lemmus lemmus

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛm.əs ˈlɛm.əs / (AmE), / ˈlɛm.əs ˈlɛm.əs / (BrE)
a small rodent species, the Norway lemming, known for its periodic mass migrations, sometimes resulting in many individuals drowning
Norway lemming, a migratory rodent

Examples

The Lemmus lemmus population surged, leading to one of its famous mass migrations across the tundra.
Scientists track the movements of Lemmus lemmus to study their migration patterns.
Many Lemmus lemmus unfortunately drowned during their attempt to cross the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lemming - a small mouselike rodent known for population booms and mass migrations

Etymology

From New Latin, genus and species name given in scientific taxonomy.
