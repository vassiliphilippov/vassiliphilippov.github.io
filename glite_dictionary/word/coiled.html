coiled

1. ADJ

/ kɔɪld / (AmE), / kɔɪld / (BrE)
curled or wound into a series of rings or spirals, often in a compact way
curled or wound in rings or spirals

Examples

The garden hose was coiled neatly beside the shed.
She found a coiled wire under the desk.
A coiled serpent was carved into the ancient stone.

Synset Examples

a coiled snake ready to strike
the rope lay coiled on the deck

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curled - having the shape of a curl or spiral
  • Antonym: unwound - not wound or coiled; straightened out
