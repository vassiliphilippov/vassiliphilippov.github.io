activewear

1. NOUN

/ ˈæktɪvˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈæktɪvˌwɛə / (BrE)
Clothing designed for physical activity or exercise, often worn casually as well as for sport.
clothing for exercise or sport

Examples

She prefers to wear activewear even when she's not going to the gym.
Most brands offer a variety of activewear designed for comfort and style.
The store specializes in activewear for yoga, running, and other sports.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sportswear - clothing worn for sport or exercise

Etymology

From 'active' + 'wear', coined in late 20th century to refer to sports and leisure clothing.
