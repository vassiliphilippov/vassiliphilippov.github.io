hubby

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌbi / (AmE), / ˈhʌbi / (BrE)
informal term for a woman's husband
informal term for husband
informal

Examples

She always talks about her hubby when we meet for coffee.
My hubby surprised me with flowers for our anniversary.
After work, her hubby picks up the kids from school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: husband - the man to whom someone is married

Etymology

Diminutive of 'husband', imitating baby-talk formation.
