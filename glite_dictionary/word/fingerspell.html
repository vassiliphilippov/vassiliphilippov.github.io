to fingerspell

1. VERB

/ ˈfɪŋɡɚˌspɛl / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəˌspɛl / (BrE)
to communicate a word or words by spelling them out using specific hand shapes for each letter, especially as used in sign language
spell words using hand signs

Examples

In the classroom, the teacher sometimes fingerspells new vocabulary for deaf students.
When he didn't know the sign, he quickly fingerspelled the word to his friend.
Learning to fingerspell requires practice and attention to each hand movement.

Synset Examples

The Deaf often fingerspell

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sign - to communicate using sign language

Etymology

from 'finger' + 'spell', indicating the act of spelling with the fingers
