Arianrod

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛəriənˌrɒd / (AmE), / ˈærɪənrɒd / (BrE)
A Celtic goddess renowned for her beauty, known in Welsh mythology as the mother of Dylan.
Celtic goddess in Welsh mythology

Examples

In Welsh legends, Arianrod is often associated with the moon and the stars.
The myth of Arianrod was a central part of ancient Celtic beliefs.
Many modern works of fantasy reference Arianrod as a symbol of femininity and mystery.

Etymology

From Welsh *Arianrhod*, meaning 'silver wheel'.
