spurious

/ ˈspjʊriəs / (AmE), / ˈspjʊəriəs / (BrE)
(archaic) Born out of wedlock; illegitimate.
archaic: illegitimate (born out of wedlock)
archaic

Examples

2. ADJ

/ ˈspjʊriəs / (AmE), / ˈspjʊəriəs / (BrE)
Appearing to be genuine or valid, but actually false or not what it claims to be.
false or not genuine

Examples

He dismissed the rumors as spurious and provided evidence to the contrary.

Related Terms
