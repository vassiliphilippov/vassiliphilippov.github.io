wastewater

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪstˌwɔtɚ / (AmE), / ˈweɪstˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
water that has been used and contains waste products, such as sewage or other pollutants
water mixed with waste or pollutants

Examples

The factory must treat its wastewater before releasing it into the river.
Many cities are investing in advanced wastewater treatment plants to protect the environment.
Households produce large amounts of wastewater from showers, washing machines, and toilets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sewage - waste matter carried off by sewers
