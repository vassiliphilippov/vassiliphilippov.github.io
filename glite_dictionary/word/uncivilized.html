uncivilized

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɪvəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪvɪlaɪzd / (BrE)
lacking the social, cultural, or moral development considered characteristic of a civilized society; not socially or culturally advanced.
lacking cultural or social development

Examples

The explorers described the remote region as uncivilized and untouched by modern society.
His uncivilized behavior shocked everyone at the formal dinner.
Some ancient practices are now viewed as uncivilized by contemporary standards.

Synset Examples

barbarian invaders
barbaric practices
a savage people
fighting is crude and uncivilized especially if the weapons are efficient
wild tribes

Related Terms

  • Antonym: civilized - having an advanced stage of social and cultural development
  • Synonym: barbaric - primitive and uncultured, cruel
  • Synonym: savage - fierce, violent, and uncontrolled; primitive

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'civilized'; from Latin 'civilis' (relating to citizens).
