Madison

1. PROPN

/ ˈmædəsən / (AmE), / ˈmædɪsən / (BrE)
the capital city of Wisconsin, located in the southern part of the state, and home to the main campus of the University of Wisconsin
capital city of Wisconsin

Examples

The city of Madison is known for its beautiful lakes and vibrant community.
She decided to attend the University of Wisconsin in Madison.
Many visitors enjoy the farmers' market on the Capitol Square in Madison.

Etymology

Named after James Madison, the fourth President of the United States.
