high-backed

1. ADJ

/ haɪˈbækt / (AmE), / haɪˈbækt / (BrE)
(of a chair or similar piece of furniture) having a back that rises unusually high above the seat.
having a high back (esp. furniture)

Examples

She chose a high-backed armchair for extra support while reading.
The dining room boasted several elegant high-backed chairs around the long table.
He rested comfortably in a high-backed sofa by the fireplace.

Synset Examples

a high-backed sofa

Related Terms

  • Antonym: low-backed - (of furniture) having a back that is low in relation to the seat
