rasher

1. NOUN

/ ˈræʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈræʃə / (BrE)
A commercially important fish found along the Pacific coast of North America, especially valued as food.
Pacific coast food fish

Examples

Fishermen caught several rashers off the coast of California this season.
The restaurant serves grilled rasher as one of its local seafood specialties.
Due to overfishing, the population of rashers has declined in recent years.
