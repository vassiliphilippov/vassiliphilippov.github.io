to electrify

1. VERB

/ ɪˈlɛktrəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to excite someone suddenly and intensely; to inspire a strong feeling of enthusiasm or excitement
excite suddenly and intensely

Examples

The crowd was electrified by the band's performance.
Her passionate speech electrified the audience into action.
The unexpected announcement electrified the entire office.

Synset Examples

The news electrified us

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thrill - to cause someone to feel excitement and pleasure
  • Antonym: bore - to make someone feel uninterested or tired

Etymology

From electric + -fy, modeled after verbs like 'clarify'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01834740-v
Synset POS: v

2. VERB

/ ɪˈlɛktrəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to equip or supply (a device, building, system, etc.) with electricity, so that it can operate or function using electrical power
equip or supply with electricity

Examples

The company plans to electrify its entire fleet of delivery vehicles by next year.
Older houses often need to be electrified to support modern appliances and lighting.
They hired an engineer to electrify the new factory during its construction phase.

Synset Examples

electrify an appliance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power - to supply with energy, especially electricity

Etymology

From electric + -fy, modeled after similar verbs meaning 'to make electric' or 'to equip with electricity.'

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00506883-v
Synset POS: v

3. VERB

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to charge (a conductor) with electricity
charge with electricity

Examples

When you electrify a metal rod, it becomes capable of attracting small paper pieces.
The laboratory experiment required students to electrify several copper wires to observe the effect.
Technicians must electrify the fence to ensure the security system works properly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charge - to give an electric charge to

Etymology

From French électrifier, based on Greek ēlektron (amber).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00507121-v
Synset POS: v
