greenish-white

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡriːnɪʃ waɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnɪʃ waɪt / (BrE)
Of a white color tinged with green; having a pale, green-tinged white shade.
white tinged with green

Examples

The greenish-white petals gave the plant a delicate and unusual appearance.
She chose greenish-white flowers to complement the soft pastel decor.
Under the lamp, the walls appeared a subtle greenish-white hue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: off-white - a white color with a slight tint of another color
  • Degree Higher: green - the color between blue and yellow in the spectrum
  • Degree Lower: white - the color of pure snow, milk, or paper
