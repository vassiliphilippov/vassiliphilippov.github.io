functioning

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or manner in which something operates or works
operation or working process

Examples

The functioning of the machine was affected by the cold weather.
Proper functioning of the heart is vital to good health.
Routine maintenance is essential for the efficient functioning of any engine.

Synset Examples

the power of its engine determines its operation
the plane's operation in high winds
they compared the cooking performance of each oven
the jet's performance conformed to high standards

Related Terms

  • Synonym: operation - an act or manner of working or functioning
  • Synonym: performance - the manner in which something operates or functions

2. ADJ

/ ˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ / (BrE)
Able to perform its normal or intended function; working properly.
working or operational

Examples

The machine is fully functioning after the repairs.
We need a functioning internet connection to complete the project.
He replaced the batteries to make sure the flashlight was functioning again.

Synset Examples

a functioning flashlight

Related Terms

  • Antonym: broken - not working or not able to function
  • Synonym: operational - in operation; ready for use

Etymology

From the verb 'function' + present participle suffix '-ing'.
