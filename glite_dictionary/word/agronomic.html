agronomic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæɡ.rəˈnɑ.mɪk / (AmE), / ˌæɡ.rəˈnɒ.mɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the science or practice of agronomy, especially regarding crop and soil management.
relating to agronomy or agricultural science

Examples

The new fertilizer methods had a significant agronomic impact on crop yields.
He published several studies on agronomic practices in sustainable farming.
The conference focused on agronomic innovation to address global food security.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agricultural - relating to agriculture or farming

Etymology

From agronomy, which is from French agronomie, from Greek agronomos (overseer of land).
