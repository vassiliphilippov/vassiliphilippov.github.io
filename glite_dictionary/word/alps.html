Alps

1. PROPN

/ ælps / (AmE), / ælps / (BrE)
a range of high mountains in south-central Europe, known for their scenic beauty and as a center for winter sports
mountain range in Europe

Examples

The Alps attract thousands of skiers every year from all over the world.
We drove through picturesque villages at the foot of the Alps.
Mountaineers dream of climbing challenging peaks in the Alps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pyrenees - mountain range between France and Spain
  • Synonym: Himalayas - the highest mountain range in the world, in Asia

Etymology

From Latin 'Alpes', possibly of Celtic origin.
