umbo

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌm.boʊ / (AmE), / ˈʌm.bəʊ / (BrE)
a small, rounded elevation on the eardrum where the malleus bone attaches
rounded elevation on the eardrum
medical

Examples

The doctor pointed to the umbo on the diagram of the ear.
A normal tympanic membrane has a clearly visible umbo.
Damage to the umbo can affect hearing sensitivity.

Etymology

From Latin umbo (the boss of a shield), referring to a rounded elevation.
