purple-lilac

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɜr.pəl ˈlaɪ.læk / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.pəl ˈlaɪ.læk / (BrE)
of a lavender color tinged with purple; having the appearance of lavender shaded toward purple.
lavender color tinged with purple

Examples

The artist chose a purple-lilac paint to capture the soft hues of the evening sky.
She wore a beautiful dress in purple-lilac that complemented her complexion perfectly.
The garden was filled with purple-lilac blossoms, creating a calming and elegant atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: lavender - a pale purple color, named after the flower
  • Degree Higher: purple - a deep color between blue and red
