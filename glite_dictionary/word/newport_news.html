Newport News

1. PROPN

/ ˌnuːpɔrt ˈnuːz / (AmE), / ˌnjuːpɔːt ˈnjuːz / (BrE)
A port city in southeastern Virginia, USA, located at the mouth of the James River and noted for its large shipyards.
port city in Virginia, USA

Examples

She grew up in Newport News before moving to New York City for college.
The massive shipyards in Newport News are known for building aircraft carriers.
Tourists often visit Newport News to explore its maritime history and museums.

Etymology

Named after English settler Christopher Newport and reputedly an early settler named News; first used in the 17th century.
