goshawfully

1. ADV

/ ˈɡɑʃˌɔfəli / (AmE), / ˈɡɒʃˌɔːfəli / (BrE)
(informal) Extremely; used to emphasize the degree of an adjective, such as hot, late, or tired.
extremely, for emphasis
informal

Examples

It was goshawfully hot during our hike.
He was goshawfully late to the party.
She felt goshawfully tired after the long meeting.

Synset Examples

It was goshawfully hot during our hike.
He was goshawfully late to the party.
She felt goshawfully tired after the long meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awfully - To a very great degree (intensifier)
  • Synonym: terribly - To a very great or severe degree (intensifier)
  • Antonym: slightly - To a small degree (intensifier)

Etymology

From 'gosh', a mild euphemism for 'God', combined with 'awfully' as an intensifier.
