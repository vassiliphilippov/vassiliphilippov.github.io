Bergen

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɝːɡən / (AmE), / ˈbɜːɡən / (BrE)
a port city in southwestern Norway, known for its historic harbor, surrounding mountains, and role as a gateway to the fjords.
city in southwestern Norway

Examples

We spent two days exploring Bergen and its famous fish market.
The train journey from Oslo to Bergen offers breathtaking views of Norway's scenery.
Bergen is often called the gateway to the fjords because of its location.
