wanderlust

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑndərˌlʌst / (AmE), / ˈwɒndəlʌst / (BrE)
a strong or irresistible desire to travel or wander
strong desire to travel

Examples

After a year stuck in one place, her wanderlust grew stronger than ever.
His wanderlust has taken him to more than thirty countries across five continents.
The film follows a young man whose wanderlust leads him on a journey of self-discovery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: travel bug - an urge or enthusiasm for traveling
  • Antonym: homesickness - longing for home; opposite of desire to travel

Etymology

From German 'Wanderlust' (desire to wander), from 'wandern' (to hike, wander) + 'Lust' (desire).
