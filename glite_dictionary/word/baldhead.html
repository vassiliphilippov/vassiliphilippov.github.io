baldhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔldˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈbɔːldˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person whose head is bald or has been shaved
person with a bald or shaved head

Examples

The old baldhead nodded politely as he entered the shop.
Some children teased him by calling him a baldhead, but he didn't mind.
She embraced the look and became a proud baldhead after her treatment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baldy - informal term for a bald person
