ethnographical

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛθnəˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɛθnəˈɡræfɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to ethnography or the study of the characteristics of different peoples and the differences and relationships between them
relating to ethnography

Examples

The museum hosts an ethnographical collection from various indigenous cultures.
Her research is based on ethnographical observations made during fieldwork in Africa.
He published a series of ethnographical studies about rural communities.

Synset Examples

ethnographical data

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ethnographic - relating to ethnography or its methods

Etymology

From 'ethnography' (Greek ethnos 'nation' + -graphia 'writing') + -ical (adjectival suffix).
