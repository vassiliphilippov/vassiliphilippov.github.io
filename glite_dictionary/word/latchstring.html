latchstring

1. NOUN

/ ˈlætʃˌstrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlatʃ.strɪŋ / (BrE)
A string used to lift the latch of a door from the outside, especially in older or simpler doors.
string for opening a door latch from outside

Examples

The old farmhouse still had a latchstring hanging outside the kitchen door.
In pioneer days, people would leave the latchstring out as a sign of welcome.
He pulled the latchstring and quietly let himself into the cottage.
