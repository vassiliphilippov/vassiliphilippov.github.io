carhop

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrˌhɑp / (AmE), / ˈkɑːˌhɒp / (BrE)
a waiter or waitress who serves customers in their cars at a drive-in restaurant
waiter/waitress at a drive-in restaurant

Examples

In the 1950s, every drive-in diner had at least one carhop skating between the rows of parked cars.
The carhop brought our burgers and milkshakes right to the window of our convertible.
Dressed in a retro uniform, the carhop flashed a smile as she delivered the order to our vehicle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waiter - a person who serves food and drinks at a restaurant

Etymology

Formed from 'car' and 'hop', reflecting the worker hopping between cars.
