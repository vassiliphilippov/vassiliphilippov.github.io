mithan

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪθæn / (AmE), / ˈmɪθən / (BrE)
an ox of Southeast Asia, sometimes regarded as a domesticated breed of the gaur
domesticated ox of Southeast Asia

Examples

The villagers raise mithan as an important source of milk and meat.
A herd of mithan grazed peacefully along the edge of the forest.
Researchers studied the habits of domesticated mithan in the mountain valleys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gaur - a large wild ox of South and Southeast Asia
