Instagram

1. PROPN

/ ˈɪnstəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈɪnstəˌɡræm / (BrE)
A popular social networking service for sharing photos and videos online, especially via mobile devices.
photo and video sharing service

Examples

She posted a picture of her new puppy on Instagram this morning.
Many brands use Instagram to connect with younger audiences and promote their products.
After traveling to Italy, he shared dozens of scenic photos on Instagram.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Facebook - a major social networking website and Instagram's parent company
  • Synonym: Snapchat - a mobile application for sharing photos and short videos

Etymology

From 'instant' + 'telegram'; name created by founders in 2010.
