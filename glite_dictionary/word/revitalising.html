revitalising

1. ADJ

/ riˈvaɪtəˌlaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / riˈvɪtəlaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
giving new life, energy, or strength to something or someone; making something more active or successful again.
giving new life or energy

Examples

The revitalising effects of the spa treatments left her feeling renewed and energetic.
A revitalising walk in the crisp morning air can improve your mood for the whole day.
Many cities have benefited from revitalising investments in their downtown areas.

Synset Examples

the renewing warmth of the sunshine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refreshing - making someone feel less tired or more energetic
  • Synonym: invigorating - making someone feel more energetic or lively

Etymology

From 'revitalise' + -ing; 'revitalise' from re- (again) + vitalise (give life to).
