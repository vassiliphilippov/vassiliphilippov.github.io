homocentric

1. ADJ

/ ˌhoʊmoʊˈsɛntrɪk / (AmE), / ˌhəʊməʊˈsɛntrɪk / (BrE)
having a common center; sharing the same central point
sharing the same center

Examples

The scientist explained that the planet's orbits are homocentric, sharing a common center.
In the diagram, the circles are perfectly homocentric and do not overlap.
The castle was protected by three homocentric walls, each surrounding the central keep.

Synset Examples

concentric rings

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concentric - having a common center, often used to describe circles or spheres

Etymology

From Greek homo- (same) + kentron (center).
