to illumine

1. VERB

/ ɪˈluːmɪn / (AmE), / ɪˈluːmɪn / (BrE)
to light up or brighten something; to introduce light into
to light up; to brighten

Examples

The candles illumine the old cathedral, casting a soft golden glow on the walls.
A full moon illumined the path through the quiet forest last night.
As dawn broke, the sun's rays began to illumine the mountain peaks.

Synset Examples

This lamp lightens the room a bit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to illuminate - to light up, make brighter or clearer

Etymology

From Latin 'illuminare' meaning 'to light up.'
