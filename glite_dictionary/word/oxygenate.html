to oxygenate

1. VERB

/ ˈɑksɪdʒəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈɒksɪdʒəˌneɪt / (BrE)
to supply, combine, or enrich with oxygen
supply or enrich with oxygen

Examples

The lungs oxygenate the blood before it circulates through the body.
Aquariums often use pumps to oxygenate the water for the fish.
This process helps to oxygenate the wine, enhancing its flavor.

Synset Examples

oxygenate blood

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to deoxygenate - to remove oxygen from
  • Synonym: to oxidize - to combine or react with oxygen

Etymology

From oxygen + -ate, formed in scientific English in the 18th century.
