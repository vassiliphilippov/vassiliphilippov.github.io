hundred-and-forty-fifth

1. ADJ

/ ˌhʌndrəd ən ˌfɔːrti ˈfɪfθ / (AmE), / ˌhʌndrəd ən ˌfɔːti ˈfɪfθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number that corresponds to one hundred and forty-five in a sequence; 145th.
ordinal number: 145th

Examples

The parade took place on the hundred-and-forty-fifth anniversary of the city's founding.
She finished in hundred-and-forty-fifth place out of two hundred runners.
This is the hundred-and-forty-fifth time I have called about the same issue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one hundred forty-fifth - alternative spelling of ordinal for 145th
