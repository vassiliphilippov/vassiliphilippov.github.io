caravanning

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛrəˌvænɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkærəˌvænɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of spending holidays traveling or staying in a caravan, typically for leisure
holidaying in a caravan

Examples

They spent their summer caravanning along the coast of France.
Many people enjoy caravanning because it gives them the freedom to explore new places at their own pace.
After retiring, Jenny took up caravanning as her favorite way to travel around the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camping - the activity of spending a holiday living in a tent or camper

Etymology

From 'caravan' (vehicle for living/traveling), with suffix '-ing' forming a noun.
