outclassed

1. ADJ

/ aʊtˈklæst / (AmE), / aʊtˈklɑːst / (BrE)
decisively surpassed by someone or something, making one seem of inferior quality or ability
clearly surpassed in ability or quality

Examples

The visiting team was thoroughly outclassed by the league champions in every aspect of the game.
She felt completely outclassed by the other contestants' impressive performances.
Despite their hard work, the local candidates were outclassed by their more experienced rivals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inferior - of lower quality or rank
  • Synonym: surpassed - exceeded or outdone
  • Antonym: superior - higher in quality or ability

Etymology

From 'outclass' (verb) + '-ed', meaning to surpass in class or excellence.
