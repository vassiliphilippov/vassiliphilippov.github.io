finances

1. NOUN

/ fəˈnænsɪz, faɪˈnænsɪz / (AmE), / faɪˈnɑːnsɪz, fɪˈnænsɪz / (BrE)
the money and other assets that a person, company, or organization has and manages
money or assets held by someone

Examples

After losing his job, he struggled to keep his finances in order.
The company's finances improved significantly after the merger.
They met with an advisor to discuss their personal finances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: funds - available money for a particular purpose
  • Synonym: resources - assets that can be drawn on by a person or organization
  • Antonym: debt - money that is owed to others

Etymology

From French *finance* (a payment, revenue), ultimately from Latin *finis* (end, settlement).
