submenu

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌbˌmɛnju / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌmɛnjuː / (BrE)
A secondary menu that appears when you place the cursor over an item on the main menu, typically in computer software or websites.
secondary menu in a computer interface

Examples

Clicking the 'File' menu will display a submenu with additional options.
You can access the settings through the submenu under 'Preferences.'
A small arrow indicates that an item has a submenu with more choices.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: menu - a list of options or commands in a computer interface

Etymology

From 'sub-' (meaning 'under' or 'secondary') + 'menu' (from French 'menu', meaning 'list').
