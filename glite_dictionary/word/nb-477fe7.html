N.B.

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛn ˈbi / (AmE), / ˌɛn ˈbiː / (BrE)
(used to draw attention) note well; used to indicate that special attention should be paid to the following statement or information.
used to indicate special attention

Examples

Please remember to submit all forms by next Friday—N.B., late submissions will not be accepted.
The lecturer wrote 'N.B.: this will be on the exam' on the board to highlight the key concept.
Pay close attention to the instructions, especially the parts marked with an N.B. in the manual.

Synset Examples

the margins of his book were generously supplied with pencilled NBs

Etymology

From Latin *nota bene* meaning 'note well'.
