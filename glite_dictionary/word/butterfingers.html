butterfingers

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌtərˌfɪŋɡərz / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌfɪŋɡəz / (BrE)
(informal) a person who often drops things, especially in sport or when handling objects.
a person prone to dropping things
informal

Examples

He missed an easy catch again—what a total butterfingers!
She dropped her phone twice today; she's such a butterfingers.
Don’t let him carry the cake, he’s a real butterfingers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clumsy - lacking skill or coordination; awkward

Etymology

Formed from 'butter' (suggesting slipperiness) and 'fingers', first used in the 19th century.
