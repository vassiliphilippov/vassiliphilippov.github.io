cheesemonger

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃiːsˌmʌŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzˌmʌŋɡə / (BrE)
a person or shop that sells cheese
person or shop selling cheese

Examples

She visited the local cheesemonger to find some artisanal goat cheese for the party.
The cheesemonger recommended a strong blue cheese to accompany the wine.
Our city has a renowned cheesemonger who imports rare cheeses from Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fishmonger - person or shop that sells fish
  • Synonym: greengrocer - person or shop selling fruit and vegetables

Etymology

From cheese + monger (dealer or trader).
