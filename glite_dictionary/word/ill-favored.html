ill-favored

1. ADJ

/ ɪlˈfeɪvərd / (AmE), / ɪlˈfeɪvəd / (BrE)
Having an unattractive or unpleasant appearance, especially of the face.
unattractive in appearance (esp. of the face)

Examples

Despite his ill-favored appearance, he was kind and generous to everyone.
The villain in the play had an ill-favored face that frightened the children.
She disguised herself with an ill-favored mask to remain unrecognized at the party.

Synset Examples

an ill-favored countenance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unattractive - not pleasant to look at
  • Antonym: good-looking - attractive in appearance, especially of the face

Etymology

Formed from 'ill-' (bad) and 'favored' (having a certain appearance), meaning 'badly favored in looks.'
