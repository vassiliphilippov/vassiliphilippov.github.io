ovine

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊvaɪn / (AmE), / ˈəʊvaɪn / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of sheep.
relating to sheep

Examples

The farmer specialized in ovine breeding and managed a large flock of sheep.
The disease primarily affects ovine species and rarely occurs in other animals.
Scientists studied ovine behavior to compare it with that of goats and cattle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bovine - relating to cattle
  • Synonym: caprine - relating to goats

Etymology

From Latin ovīnus, from ovis (sheep).
