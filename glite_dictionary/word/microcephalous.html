microcephalous

1. ADJ

/ ˌmaɪkroʊˈsɛfələs / (AmE), / ˌmɪkrəʊˈsɛfələs / (BrE)
having an abnormally small head, typically associated with an underdeveloped brain
having an abnormally small head
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that the baby was microcephalous, which meant extra care would be needed.
Some genetic conditions can cause infants to be born microcephalous and with developmental challenges.
Researchers are investigating the causes of microcephalous disorders in certain populations.

Synset Examples

a nanocephalic dwarf

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microcephalic - having an abnormally small head

Etymology

From New Latin microcephalus, from Greek mikros (small) + kephalē (head).
