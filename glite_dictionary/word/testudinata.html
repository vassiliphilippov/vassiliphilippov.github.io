Testudinata

1. NOUN

/ tɛˌstjuːdɪˈneɪtə / (AmE), / tɛˌstjuːdɪˈneɪtə / (BrE)
The group of reptiles that includes all turtles and tortoises.
group of turtles and tortoises

Examples

The order Testudinata encompasses all living and extinct turtles and tortoises.
Paleontologists have discovered a new fossil in the Testudinata lineage.
Scientists debate when exactly Testudinata evolved during the Mesozoic Era.

Etymology

From Latin testudō (tortoise).
