pebble-grained

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛbəlˌɡreɪnd / (AmE), / ˈpɛbəlˌɡreɪnd / (BrE)
(of leather or similar material) having a rough, raised surface pattern resembling small pebbles, typically produced by treatment with a patterned roller.
having a rough, pebbled surface (esp. of leather)

Examples

The craftsman selected a pebble-grained leather for the handbag to add texture and durability.
Her shoes were made from pebble-grained material that gave them a distinctive look.
You can identify genuine pebble-grained leather by its unique, bumpy pattern.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smooth-grained - having a smooth, even surface texture
