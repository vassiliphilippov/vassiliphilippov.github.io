markka

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrkə / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkə / (BrE)
the former basic unit of currency in Finland, replaced by the euro in 2002
former Finnish currency unit

Examples

Before Finland joined the Eurozone, the markka was the official currency.
She found some old Finnish markka notes in a drawer.
The exchange rate between the euro and the markka was fixed in 2002.

Etymology

Finnish, from Swedish 'mark', an old unit of mass and currency.
