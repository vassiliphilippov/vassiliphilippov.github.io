sought-after

1. ADJ

/ sɔt ˈæftɚ / (AmE), / sɔːt ˈɑːftə / (BrE)
Desirable or in demand; much wanted or sought by many people.
much wanted; in demand

Examples

This neighborhood is a sought-after place to live because of its excellent schools.
Tickets to the concert are highly sought-after and sold out within minutes.
She owns a sought-after collection of vintage records.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desired - wanted or wished for as being attractive, useful, or necessary
  • Synonym: popular - liked or admired by many people
  • Antonym: unwanted - not desired or wished for

Etymology

Past participle of 'seek after,' from Old English 'sēcan' (to seek).
