utterance

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌt̬.ɚ.əns / (AmE), / ˈʌt.ər.əns / (BrE)
a spoken word, statement, or vocal sound; the act of expressing something aloud
spoken word or statement

Examples

Her final utterance was barely audible above the noise of the crowd.
Every utterance during the meeting was carefully recorded for review.
Children often learn new words by repeating the utterances they hear from adults.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: statement - something that is said, a declaration
  • Synonym: speech - the act of speaking; verbal communication

Etymology

From Middle English 'utteraunce', related to 'utter' (to speak), from Old English 'utteran'.
