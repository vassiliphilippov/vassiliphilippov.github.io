vexatious

1. ADJ

/ vɛkˈseɪʃəs / (AmE), / vɛkˈseɪʃəs / (BrE)
Causing irritation, annoyance, or distress.
causing annoyance or irritation

Examples

The vexatious delays at the airport left passengers feeling frustrated and tired.
His vexatious habit of interrupting others made meetings difficult for everyone.
She found the constant noise from the construction site especially vexatious during exam week.
