VDU

1. NOUN

/ ˌviː ˌdiː ˈjuː / (AmE), / ˌviː ˌdiː ˈjuː / (BrE)
(British) A video display unit; a device with a screen for displaying data from a computer.
video display unit (screen)
british

Examples

The student stared at the VDU for hours while analyzing the data.
Most modern offices have replaced their old VDUs with flat-screen monitors.
Training staff to use the new VDU system was a top priority for the IT department.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monitor - a device that displays information from a computer

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'video display unit'.
