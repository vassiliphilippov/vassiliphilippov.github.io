wine-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈwaɪn ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪn ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
Having a deep red color similar to that of red wine.
deep red, like red wine

Examples

Her dress was a stunning wine-colored silk that caught everyone's attention at the party.
They chose a wine-colored paint for the dining room walls to create a warm, elegant atmosphere.
He bought a wine-colored scarf to match his winter coat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burgundy - a dark red color, like Burgundy wine
  • Synonym: crimson - a rich deep red color with a hint of blue
