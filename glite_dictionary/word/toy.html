toy

1. NOUN

/ tɔɪ / (AmE), / tɔɪ / (BrE)
an object designed for children to play with
object for children to play with

Examples

The child picked up his favorite toy and started playing in the living room.
Many toys are designed to help children develop important motor skills.
She donated a box of toys to the local charity before moving to a new city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plaything - object intended for play

Etymology

From Middle English *toye* (amusement, plaything), of uncertain origin.
