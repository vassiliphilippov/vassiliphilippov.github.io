childless

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃaɪl(d)ləs / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪl(d)ləs / (BrE)
Having no children, either by choice or circumstance.
without children or offspring

Examples

After years of marriage, they remained childless by choice.
The king was concerned because his heir was childless.
She often wondered how her life would be different if she were not childless.

Synset Examples

in some societies a childless woman is rejected by her tribesmen

Related Terms

  • Antonym: parent - a person who has a child or children
  • Synonym: barren - unable to produce children or offspring

Etymology

From 'child' + suffix '-less' (without).
