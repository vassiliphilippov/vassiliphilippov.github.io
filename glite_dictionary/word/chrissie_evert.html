Chrissie Evert

1. PROPN

/ ˈkrɪsi ˈɛvərt / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsi ˈɛvət / (BrE)
(born 1954) An American tennis player noted for winning multiple women's singles titles in the United States and at Wimbledon.
American tennis player, multiple Grand Slam winner

Examples

Chrissie Evert dominated the world of women's tennis throughout the 1970s and early 1980s.
Many consider Chrissie Evert's rivalry with Martina Navratilova to be one of the greatest in sports history.
Growing up, she admired the composure and consistency that Chrissie Evert displayed on the court.
