loom

1. NOUN

/ lum / (AmE), / luːm / (BrE)
a machine used for weaving yarn or thread into cloth
machine used for weaving textiles

Examples

The artisan skillfully operated the loom to create a beautiful patterned rug.
Early industrial mills were filled with the repetitive sound of looms at work.
She admired the ancient wooden loom displayed in the museum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weaving - the process of forming fabric by interlacing threads

Etymology

From Old English *geloma* (tool).
