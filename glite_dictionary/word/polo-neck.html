polo-neck

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊ.loʊ.nɛk / (AmE), / ˈpəʊ.ləʊ.nɛk / (BrE)
a sweater or jersey with a high, close-fitting collar that folds over
sweater with high, folded collar
british

Examples

She wore a black polo-neck under her blazer for a stylish look.
Many fashion designers include the classic polo-neck in their winter collections.
A red polo-neck can be both warm and elegant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turtleneck - a sweater with a high, close-fitting, turned-over collar

Etymology

From 'polo', the sport, where similar collars were first popularized.
