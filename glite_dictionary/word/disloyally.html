disloyally

1. ADV

/ dɪsˈlɔɪ.ə.li / (AmE), / dɪsˈlɔɪ.ə.li / (BrE)
In a manner that shows a lack of loyalty; in a disloyal way.
in a disloyal manner

Examples

He was hurt when his trusted friend acted disloyally during the crisis.
The agent disloyally shared sensitive information with a rival company.
The knight was punished for serving his king disloyally.

Synset Examples

his men acted disloyally and betrayed him in the end

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loyally - in a loyal manner
  • Synonym: disloyal - showing a lack of loyalty
