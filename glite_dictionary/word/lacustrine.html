lacustrine

1. ADJ

/ ləˈkʌstriːn / (AmE), / ləˈkʌstriːn / (BrE)
relating to, formed in, or living near lakes
relating to lakes

Examples

The lacustrine ecosystem supports a wide variety of fish and plant species.
Geologists studied the lacustrine sediments left by the ancient lake.
Many lacustrine birds build their nests close to the water.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: marine - relating to the sea
  • Antonym: fluvial - relating to rivers

Etymology

From Latin 'lacus' meaning 'lake'.
