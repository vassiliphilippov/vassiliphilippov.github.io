thusly

1. ADV

/ ˈðʌsli / (AmE), / ˈðʌsli / (BrE)
in this way; as indicated or described
in this way; as indicated

Examples

He arranged the flowers thusly, following the instructions exactly.
The device must be calibrated thusly to ensure accuracy.
Please sign your name thusly at the bottom of the page.

Synset Examples

hold the brush so
set up the pieces thus

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thus - in this way; like this

Etymology

Formed humorously or redundantly from 'thus' + the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
