Irula

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪruːlə / (AmE), / ˈɪruːlə / (BrE)
a Dravidian language closely related to Tamil, spoken in a hilly region of southwestern India
a Dravidian language of southwestern India

Examples

The Irula language is spoken by an indigenous community in southern India.
Researchers are documenting the grammar of Irula to preserve linguistic diversity.
He is learning Irula to communicate with local villagers during his fieldwork.
