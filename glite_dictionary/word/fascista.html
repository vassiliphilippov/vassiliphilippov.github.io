fascista

1. NOUN

/ faˈsiːstə / (AmE), / faˈsiːstə / (BrE)
A member of the Italian Fascist Party under the regime of Mussolini.
Italian fascist under Mussolini

Examples

During the 1930s, the fascista enforced strict government policies in Rome.
His grandfather was a fascista who supported Mussolini during the war.
Many fascista leaders were tried for their actions after World War II.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fascist - A supporter of fascism or a member of a fascist movement

Etymology

From Italian 'fascista' (fascist), related to 'fascio' (bundle, group), referring to the political movement.
