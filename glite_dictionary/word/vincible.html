vincible

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɪnsəbəl / (AmE), / ˈvɪnsɪbəl / (BrE)
Capable of being defeated or overcome; not invincible.
capable of being defeated

Examples

Despite their confidence, the team proved to be vincible after all.
History has shown that even the mightiest empires are vincible.
Her belief that she was vincible led her to prepare thoroughly for every challenge.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: invincible - impossible to defeat or overcome

Etymology

From Latin vincibilis, from vincere (to conquer).
