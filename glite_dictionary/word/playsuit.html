playsuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪ.suːt / (AmE), / ˈpleɪ.sjuːt / (BrE)
A one-piece or coordinated outfit, usually for women or children, consisting of shorts and a top, designed for play or casual wear.
one-piece casual outfit; shorts and top

Examples

She wore a stylish playsuit to the beach party.
This summer's fashion trend includes floral playsuits for women of all ages.
The child's red playsuit made it easy for her mother to spot her in the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: romper - one-piece garment combining shorts and a shirt, often for children
