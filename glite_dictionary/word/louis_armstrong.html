Louis Armstrong

1. PROPN

/ ˌluːɪs ˈɑːrmstrɒŋ / (AmE), / ˌluːi ˈɑːmstrɒŋ / (BrE)
(1901–1971) a famous American jazz trumpeter, composer, and bandleader noted for his influential role in developing jazz music.
famous American jazz trumpeter and bandleader

Examples

Louis Armstrong transformed jazz with his innovative trumpet playing and distinctive voice.
Many regard Louis Armstrong as one of the greatest musicians of the 20th century.
The influence of Louis Armstrong can still be heard in modern jazz bands.
