Muslimah

1. NOUN

/ muːˈslɪmə / (AmE), / ˈmʊslɪmə / (BrE)
A Muslim woman; a female follower of Islam.
a Muslim woman

Examples

The conference focused on issues important to modern Muslimah communities around the world.
As a proud Muslimah, she wears her hijab with confidence every day.
The book offers advice and inspiration for every young Muslimah navigating university life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Muslim - a follower of the religion of Islam

Etymology

From Arabic مُسْلِمَة‎ (muslima), feminine form of مُسْلِم (muslim, meaning 'Muslim').
