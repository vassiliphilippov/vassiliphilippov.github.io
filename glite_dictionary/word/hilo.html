Hilo

1. PROPN

/ ˈhiːloʊ / (AmE), / ˈhiːləʊ / (BrE)
A town on the eastern coast of the island of Hawaii, known as the largest settlement on the island.
town on the island of Hawaii

Examples

Hilo is famous for its lush rainforests and scenic waterfalls.
We visited Hilo during our vacation to the Big Island.
Many tourists fly into Hilo International Airport when they arrive in Hawaii.
