Saskatoon

1. PROPN

/ ˌsæs.kəˈtun / (AmE), / ˌsæs.kəˈtuːn / (BrE)
A city in central Saskatchewan, Canada, and the largest city in the province.
largest city in Saskatchewan, Canada

Examples

She moved to Saskatoon to attend university and found the city very welcoming.
The concert tour included stops in Calgary, Edmonton, and Saskatoon.
During the winter, Saskatoon is known for its beautiful riverbank scenery.

Etymology

Named after the Cree word 'misâskwatômina', meaning 'Saskatoon berries'.
