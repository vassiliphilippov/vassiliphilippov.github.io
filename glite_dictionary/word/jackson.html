Jackson

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒæksən / (AmE), / ˈdʒaksən / (BrE)
The capital city of the state of Mississippi, located on the Pearl River in the southern United States.
capital of Mississippi, USA

Examples

I visited Jackson last summer for a music festival.
The governor gave a speech in downtown Jackson yesterday.
Many historic buildings can be found in Jackson, Mississippi.

Etymology

Named after Andrew Jackson, the seventh President of the United States.
