flavorlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfleɪ.vɚ.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfleɪ.və.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of having no flavor or taste
absence of flavor or taste

Examples

The flavorlessness of the soup disappointed everyone at the dinner table.
Some complain about the flavorlessness of certain health foods.
After boiling the vegetables too long, they ended up with nothing but flavorlessness left.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: flavor - distinctive taste, especially of food or drink
  • Synonym: flavorless - lacking flavor; having no taste
  • Synonym: tastelessness - the quality of lacking taste or flavor

Etymology

Formed from 'flavorless' + '-ness'.
