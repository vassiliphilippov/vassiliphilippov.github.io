seventy-two

1. NUM

/ ˌsɛv.ən.ti ˈtu / (AmE), / ˌsɛv.ən.ti ˈtjuː / (BrE)
the number equal to seventy plus two; 72
the cardinal number 72

Examples

She turned seventy-two last week and celebrated with her family.
There are seventy-two pages in the first section of the report.
The marathon attracted seventy-two participants from different countries.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: seventy-one - the cardinal number 71
  • Degree Higher: seventy-three - the cardinal number 73
