styler

1. NOUN

/ ˈstaɪlər / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlə / (BrE)
a person who cuts, shapes, or arranges hair, especially as a profession
person who styles or beautifies hair

Examples

The new styler at the salon is known for her creative haircuts.
He booked an appointment with a styler to prepare for the wedding.
Many stylers attend workshops to keep up with the latest trends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairdresser - a person whose job is to cut, wash, and shape hair
  • Synonym: barber - a person whose job is to cut men's hair and shave or trim beards

Etymology

From English 'style' + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
