lowbred

1. ADJ

/ ˈloʊˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌbrɛd / (BrE)
(of a person) lacking refinement, manners, or social grace; coarse or uncultured.
lacking refinement or social grace

Examples

His lowbred manners made a poor impression at the formal dinner.
She found his lowbred jokes offensive and out of place.
The lowbred crowd jeered at the speaker without any sense of decorum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uncouth - lacking good manners or refinement
  • Antonym: refined - having or showing well-bred feeling, taste, etc.

Etymology

From 'low' (of humble rank) + 'bred' (brought up, reared).
