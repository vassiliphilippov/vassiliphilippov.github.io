emphasized

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛmfəˌsaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈɛmfəsʌɪzd / (BrE)
(of a word or phrase) spoken or expressed with special emphasis to indicate importance or meaning.
spoken or expressed with emphasis

Examples

She repeated the emphasized syllable to help her students grasp the pronunciation.
In his speech, the emphasized words highlighted key points for the audience.
The teacher's instructions were clear, and certain steps were emphasized for safety.

Synset Examples

an emphatic word

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emphatic - (of speech or expression) showing or giving emphasis
