erroneousness

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈroʊniəsnəs / (AmE), / ɪˈrəʊniəsnəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being wrong or incorrect.
the quality of being incorrect

Examples

The erroneousness of his statement was soon revealed by the evidence.
She admitted the erroneousness of her assumptions after reviewing the data.
We discussed the erroneousness present in the report's calculations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: error - a mistake or inaccuracy
  • Antonym: correctness - the quality of being correct

Etymology

From Latin erroneus (wandering, mistaken) + -ness.
