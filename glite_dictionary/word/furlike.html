furlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɜr.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɜː.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having qualities similar to fur
resembling fur

Examples

The plant's leaves have a furlike texture that feels soft to the touch.
She wrapped herself in a furlike blanket for extra warmth during winter.
