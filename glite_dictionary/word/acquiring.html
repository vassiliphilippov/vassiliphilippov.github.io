acquiring

1. NOUN

/ əˈkwaɪɚɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈkwaɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of gaining possession or ownership of something
the act of obtaining

Examples

She has a natural talent for acquiring foreign languages quickly and accurately.
The company's strategy focuses on acquiring smaller businesses to expand its market share.
His obsession with acquiring rare coins became the talk of the collectors' community.

Synset Examples

I envied his talent for acquiring
he's much more interested in the getting than in the giving

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acquisition - the act of acquiring something (especially knowledge or assets)
