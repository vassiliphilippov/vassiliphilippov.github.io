Suriname

1. PROPN

/ ˌsɜrəˈnɑːm / (AmE), / ˌsʊərɪˈnɑːm / (BrE)
A country in northeastern South America, bordering the Atlantic Ocean, which gained independence from the Netherlands in 1975.
country in northeastern South America

Examples

She spent several years living in Suriname and studying its rich biodiversity.
The official language of Suriname is Dutch, a legacy of its colonial history.
Many tourists visit Suriname to explore its rainforests and cultural heritage.

Etymology

From Dutch 'Suriname', from the name of the Surinam River, probably from the indigenous Surinen people.
