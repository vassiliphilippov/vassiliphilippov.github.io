brownish-grey

1. ADJ

/ ˈbraʊnɪʃ-ɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnɪʃ-ɡreɪ / (BrE)
of a grey color with a tinge of brown; between brown and grey in appearance.
grey with a brown tinge

Examples

The walls were painted a brownish-grey that gave the room a warm, earthy feel.
He wore a brownish-grey coat that blended well with the autumn landscape.
The puppy's fur turned a soft brownish-grey as it grew older.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grayish-brown - of a brown color with a tinge of grey
