Trot

1. NOUN

/ trɑt / (AmE), / trɒt / (BrE)
a supporter of Trotsky's theory that socialism should be established worldwide through continuous revolution
supporter of Trotskyist socialism

Examples

At university, he was considered a Trot because of his radical views.
The political debate featured heated arguments between a Trot and a Stalinist.
Some newspapers accused the party's new leader of being a closet Trot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Trotskyist - a person who supports the political theories of Trotsky
  • Antonym: Stalinist - a supporter of the methods and policies of Joseph Stalin

Etymology

Shortened from Trotsky, the surname of Leon Trotsky, Russian revolutionary leader.
