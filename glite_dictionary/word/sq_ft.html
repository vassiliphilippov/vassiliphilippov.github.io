sq ft

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr fʊt / (AmE), / skweə fʊt / (BrE)
abbreviation for square foot, a unit of area equal to one foot by one foot square
abbreviation for square foot (unit of area)

Examples

The apartment is 800 sq ft and has two bedrooms.
They listed the office space as 1,200 sq ft on the website.
Our new kitchen will add nearly 200 sq ft to the house.

Related Terms
