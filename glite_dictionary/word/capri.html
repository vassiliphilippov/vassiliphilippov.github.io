Capri

1. PROPN

/ kəˈpri / (AmE), / ˈkæpri / (BrE)
an island off the southwest coast of Italy in the Bay of Naples, known for its scenic beauty and as a popular tourist destination.
Italian island in the Bay of Naples

Examples

The Blue Grotto is one of the most famous attractions on Capri.
Many celebrities visit Capri during the summer for its stunning views and luxury hotels.
We took a ferry from Naples to spend the day exploring Capri.

Etymology

From Italian Capri, of uncertain origin, possibly pre-Roman.
