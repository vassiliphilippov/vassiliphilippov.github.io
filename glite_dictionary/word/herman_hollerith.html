Herman Hollerith

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɝːmən ˈhɑːlərɪθ / (AmE), / ˈhɜːmən ˈhɒlərɪθ / (BrE)
(1860–1929) United States inventor who developed a system for recording and processing information on punched cards.
U.S. inventor of punched card system

Examples

Herman Hollerith revolutionized data processing with his punched card system.
The census of 1890 was processed using Herman Hollerith's invention.
Historians credit Herman Hollerith as a pioneer of computing technology.
