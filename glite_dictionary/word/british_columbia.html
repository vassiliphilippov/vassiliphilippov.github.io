British Columbia

1. PROPN

/ ˌbrɪtɪʃ kəˈlʌmbiə / (AmE), / ˌbrɪtɪʃ kəˈlʌmbiə / (BrE)
a province in western Canada, situated between the Pacific Ocean and the Rocky Mountains
a province in western Canada

Examples

Many people travel to British Columbia each year to enjoy its stunning natural scenery.
Vancouver is the largest city in British Columbia and a major port on the west coast of Canada.
She moved to British Columbia to study forestry at the University of British Columbia.

Etymology

Named for the British Crown and the Columbia River.
