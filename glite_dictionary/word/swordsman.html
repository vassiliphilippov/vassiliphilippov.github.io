swordsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔrdzmən / (AmE), / ˈsɔːdzmən / (BrE)
a man who is skilled in fighting with a sword, especially in fencing or swordsmanship
man skilled in sword fighting

Examples

The legendary swordsman defeated all of his challengers in the arena.
As a young man, he trained to become a skilled swordsman and protector of the realm.
The movie features a mysterious swordsman who seeks revenge against his enemies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fencer - a person skilled at using a sword, especially in the sport of fencing
  • Antonym: swordswoman - a woman skilled at fighting with a sword

Etymology

From sword + man, dating to the 16th century.
