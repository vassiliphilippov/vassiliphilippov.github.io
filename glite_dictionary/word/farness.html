farness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑrnəs / (AmE), / ˈfɑːnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being distant or remote
remoteness; being distant

Examples

The farness of the island made it an ideal place for meditation and solitude.
She could sense the farness in his gaze as he stared out the window.
Despite the farness of their homes, the friends kept in close contact.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: nearness - the state of being close or near
  • Synonym: remoteness - the state or quality of being remote

Etymology

Formed from "far" + suffix "-ness" (indicating a state or quality).
