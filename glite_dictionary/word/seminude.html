seminude

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛmiˈnud / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈnjuːd / (BrE)
partially clothed, with some but not all clothing removed or absent, especially so that more of the body is visible than usual.
partially clothed

Examples

The artist painted several seminude models during her career.
Tabloids published a seminude photograph of the celebrity on vacation.
Some magazines feature seminude images in their advertisements.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: nude - completely unclothed; naked

Etymology

From Latin prefix 'semi-' (half, partly) + 'nude' (bare, unclothed).
