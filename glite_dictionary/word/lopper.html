lopper

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑːpər / (AmE), / ˈlɒpə / (BrE)
a long-handled tool with a curved blade or clipper at the end, used for pruning small branches and trees
pruning tool for small branches

Examples

He used a pair of loppers to trim the overgrown branches of the apple tree.
Gardeners recommend sharp loppers for cutting through tough, woody stems with ease.
After sharpening her lopper, she was able to prune the bushes quickly and efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pruner - a tool used for cutting small branches; synonym of lopper

Etymology

From 'lop' (to cut off) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
