Elixophyllin

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɪksəˈfɪlɪn / (AmE), / ɪˌlɪksəˈfɪlɪn / (BrE)
A proprietary preparation of theophylline, used in medicine to dilate the bronchi and treat breathing disorders such as asthma.
theophylline-based bronchial dilator (trade name)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Elixophyllin to help control his asthma symptoms.
After taking Elixophyllin, he noticed an improvement in his breathing within a few hours.
Patients using Elixophyllin should be monitored for possible side effects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theophylline - a stimulant and bronchodilator found in tea and used to treat asthma
