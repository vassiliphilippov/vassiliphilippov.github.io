sunglasses

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌnˌɡlæsɪz / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌɡlɑːsɪz / (BrE)
Glasses with darkened or polarized lenses worn to protect the eyes from sunlight.
glasses to protect eyes from sunlight

Examples

I left my sunglasses in the car and now it's too bright to see clearly.
She put on her sunglasses before stepping out into the midday sun.
Her sunglasses had large, round lenses that covered most of her eyes.

Synset Examples

he was wearing a pair of mirrored shades

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shades - informal synonym for sunglasses
  • Synonym: spectacles - general term for glasses worn to aid vision or protect the eyes

Etymology

Formed from 'sun' + 'glasses'; 'glasses' from Middle English, from Old English 'glæs' (glass).
