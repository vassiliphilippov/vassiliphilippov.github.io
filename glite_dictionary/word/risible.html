risible

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ˈrɪzɪbəl / (BrE)
causing or capable of causing laughter; amusing or laughable
causing laughter; amusing

Examples

The politician's excuse was so risible that the entire room burst into laughter.
His risible antics on stage kept the audience thoroughly entertained.
Some critics found the film's plot risible rather than dramatic.

Synset Examples

an amusing film with a steady stream of pranks and pratfalls
an amusing fellow
a comic hat
a comical look of surprise
funny stories that made everybody laugh
a very funny writer
it would have been laughable if it hadn't hurt so much
a mirthful experience
risible courtroom antics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laughable - so ridiculous or absurd as to provoke laughter
  • Synonym: comical - funny, especially in a strange or unexpected way

Etymology

From Latin 'risibilis' (laughable), from 'ridere' (to laugh).
