Chari

1. PROPN

/ ˈʃɑːri / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːri / (BrE)
a river in central Africa that flows northwest into Lake Chad
river in central Africa

Examples

The Chari River provides water for many communities in Chad and the Central African Republic.
Fishermen rely on the Chari for their daily catch.
Recent flooding of the Chari has raised concern among environmentalists.
