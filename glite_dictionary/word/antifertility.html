antifertility

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.ti.fɚˈtɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌæn.ti.fəˈtɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
(of a drug, device, or substance) capable of preventing conception or impregnation.
preventing conception or impregnation
medical

Examples

The researchers studied the antifertility effects of the new drug on laboratory rats.
Many traditional herbs have been investigated for their antifertility properties.
The doctor discussed various antifertility options with the patient during the consultation.

Synset Examples

contraceptive devices and medications

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contraceptive - serving to prevent pregnancy

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'anti-' (against) and 'fertility'.
