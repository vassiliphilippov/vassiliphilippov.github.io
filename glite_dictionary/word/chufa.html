chufa

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃuːfə / (AmE), / ˈtʃuːfə / (BrE)
A European sedge plant (Cyperus esculentus) with small, edible, nut-like tubers.
plant with edible tubers

Examples

The farmer planted chufa in the field to harvest its nutritious tubers.
Many traditional Spanish sweets are made using chufa as a main ingredient.
We roasted chufa tubers and added them to our salad for extra crunch.

Etymology

From Spanish 'chufa', ultimately from Arabic 'ṣūfa' (nutlet).
