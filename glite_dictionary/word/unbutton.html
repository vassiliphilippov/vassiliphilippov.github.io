to unbutton

1. VERB

/ ʌnˈbʌtən / (AmE), / ʌnˈbʌtən / (BrE)
to undo the buttons of a piece of clothing
undo the buttons of clothing

Examples

He quickly unbuttoned his coat as he entered the warm house.
Please unbutton your cuffs before rolling up your sleeves.
She unbuttons her blouse to get more comfortable after work.

Synset Examples

unbutton the shirt

Related Terms

  • Antonym: button - to fasten with buttons

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (reversing an action) + 'button'.
