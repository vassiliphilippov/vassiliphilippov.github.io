unflustered

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈflʌstərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈflʌstəd / (BrE)
not agitated or upset; calm and composed, especially in a stressful situation.
calm and composed

Examples

Even when the discussion became heated, she remained unflustered and replied rationally.
Despite the tight deadline, the manager appeared unflustered and in control.
He stayed unflustered as reporters asked difficult questions.

Synset Examples

the waiters were unflurried and good natured
with contented mind and unruffled spirit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness, anger, or other strong emotions
  • Synonym: collected - calm and self-controlled
  • Antonym: flustered - agitated or confused

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + fluster (to agitate or confuse).
