oeuvre

1. NOUN

/ ˈɜːvrə / (AmE), / ˈɜːvrə / (BrE)
the complete works of an artist, writer, or composer
complete works of an artist or writer

Examples

Art critics have analyzed the oeuvre of Frida Kahlo in great detail.
The museum is hosting an exhibition encompassing the painter's entire oeuvre.
Her oeuvre reflects the major themes of twentieth-century literature.

Synset Examples

he studied the entire Wagnerian oeuvre
Picasso's work can be divided into periods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: work - the output or creations of an artist, writer, or composer

Etymology

From French 'œuvre' (work), from Latin 'opera' (work, effort).
