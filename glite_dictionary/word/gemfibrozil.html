gemfibrozil

1. NOUN

/ dʒɛmˈfaɪbrəzɪl / (AmE), / dʒɛmˈfɪbrəzɪl / (BrE)
a medication used to lower triglyceride levels in the blood, often prescribed to treat high cholesterol
medication to lower blood triglycerides
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed gemfibrozil to help reduce her high triglyceride levels.
Patients taking gemfibrozil should have their liver function monitored regularly.
Before starting gemfibrozil, inform your physician of any other medications you are taking.
