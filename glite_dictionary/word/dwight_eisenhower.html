Dwight Eisenhower

1. PROPN

/ dwaɪt ˈaɪzənˌhaʊər / (AmE), / dwaɪt ˈaɪzənhaʊə / (BrE)
(1890–1969) 34th President of the United States and Supreme Commander of Allied forces in Europe during World War II.
US President and WWII Allied commander

Examples

Dwight Eisenhower led the Allied forces to victory during the Normandy invasion in World War II.
The interstate highway system was established during Dwight Eisenhower's presidency.
Many historians admire Dwight Eisenhower for his leadership both in wartime and as President.
