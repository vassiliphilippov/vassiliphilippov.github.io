buttonlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʌtn̩ˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbʌtnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
small, round, and shiny in appearance, resembling a button or bead
resembling a small shiny button

Examples

The puppy looked at me with buttonlike eyes full of curiosity.
She wore a coat decorated with several buttonlike ornaments down the front.
The beetle's buttonlike shell glistened in the sunlight.

Synset Examples

bright beady eyes
black buttony eyes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beady - small, round, and gleaming like a bead
