birdnesting

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːdˌnɛstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdˌnɛstɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of searching for birds' nests, usually to collect eggs
hunting for birds' nests for eggs

Examples

During spring, some people engage in birdnesting to collect rare eggs.
The practice of birdnesting is discouraged in many nature reserves due to its impact on wild bird populations.
He wrote a book documenting his experiences with birdnesting in remote forests.
