sanguineness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæŋɡwɪn.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsaŋɡwɪn.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being optimistically cheerful and confident
optimistic cheerfulness and confidence

Examples

Her sanguineness in the face of adversity inspired everyone around her.
Despite the setbacks, his sanguineness never seemed to falter.
The team's sanguineness about their chances was contagious.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimism - hopefulness and confidence about the future
  • Antonym: pessimism - a tendency to see the worst aspect of things

Etymology

From Latin sanguineus (of blood), influenced by the ancient belief that a predominance of blood led to a cheerful temperament.
