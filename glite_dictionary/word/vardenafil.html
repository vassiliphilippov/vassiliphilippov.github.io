vardenafil

1. NOUN

/ vɑrˈdɛnəˌfɪl / (AmE), / vɑːˈdɛnəfɪl / (BrE)
A drug used to treat erectile dysfunction in men by increasing blood flow to the penis; marketed under the trade name Levitra.
drug for erectile dysfunction (Levitra)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed vardenafil to help with his erectile dysfunction.
After starting vardenafil, he noticed an improvement in his condition.
Vardenafil should be taken as directed, typically before sexual activity.

Related Terms
