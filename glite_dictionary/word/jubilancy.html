jubilancy

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuː.bə.lən.si / (AmE), / ˈdʒuː.bɪ.lən.si / (BrE)
a feeling of great joy and triumph
great joy; triumph

Examples

The jubilancy in the stadium was palpable after the home team scored the winning goal.
Graduates tossed their caps in jubilancy as they celebrated their achievements.
Her face shone with jubilancy when she received the good news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jubilation - a feeling or expression of great joy
  • Degree Lower: joy - a feeling of great pleasure and happiness
  • Degree Higher: ecstasy - an overwhelming feeling of great happiness or joyful excitement

Etymology

From Latin 'jubilare' (to shout for joy), via 'jubilant.'
