turdlicker

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɜrdˌlɪkər / (AmE), / ˈtɜːdˌlɪkə / (BrE)
A highly offensive and vulgar slang term for a person considered deceitful or insincere.
vulgar insult for a deceitful person
vulgar offensive slang

Examples

If you keep lying to your friends, they'll all think you're a turdlicker.
No one trusts a turdlicker in this community after what happened last year.
Calling someone a turdlicker is extremely offensive and should be avoided in any civil conversation.

Synset Examples

Don't be a turdlicker; just admit your mistake.
