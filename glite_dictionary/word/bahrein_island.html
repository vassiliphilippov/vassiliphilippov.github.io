Bahrein Island

1. PROPN

/ bɑːˈreɪn ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / bɑːˈreɪn ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
the largest island of Bahrain, located in the Persian Gulf, forming most of the country of Bahrain
largest island of Bahrain

Examples

Many expatriates live on Bahrein Island due to its modern infrastructure.
The capital city, Manama, is situated on Bahrein Island.
Oil was first discovered on Bahrein Island in the early twentieth century.

Etymology

From Arabic البحرين (al-Baḥrayn), meaning 'the two seas.'
