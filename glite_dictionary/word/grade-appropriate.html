grade-appropriate

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡreɪd əˈproʊpriət / (AmE), / ˌɡreɪd əˈprəʊpriət / (BrE)
suitable or fitting for the level of ability and knowledge expected in a particular school grade.
suitable for a specific school grade

Examples

The teacher designed grade-appropriate assignments for her third-grade students.
Please ensure that all reading materials are grade-appropriate for the class.
This math test is not grade-appropriate for fifth graders; it's too advanced.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: age-appropriate - suitable for a particular age group
