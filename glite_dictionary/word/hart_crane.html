Hart Crane

1. PROPN

/ hɑrt kreɪn / (AmE), / hɑːt kreɪn / (BrE)
(1899–1932) an influential American poet known for his innovative style and ambitious works, including 'The Bridge'.
American poet (1899–1932)

Examples

Hart Crane's poetry captures the complexity and promise of early twentieth-century America.
Many critics consider Hart Crane to be one of the greatest American poets of his generation.
The innovative structure of 'The Bridge' makes Hart Crane's work both challenging and rewarding to study.
