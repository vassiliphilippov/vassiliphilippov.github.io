math

1. NOUN

/ mæθ / (AmE), / mɑːθ / (BrE)
the science of numbers, quantity, and space, including arithmetic, algebra, geometry, and related subjects
the study of numbers and shapes
american

Examples

I always struggled with math in high school, especially algebra.
She decided to major in math at university because she enjoyed solving complex problems.
His job as an engineer requires strong math skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mathematics - the abstract science of number, quantity, and space
  • Degree Lower: arithmetic - the branch of mathematics dealing with numbers and calculations
  • Degree Higher: geometry - the branch of mathematics dealing with shapes and their properties

Etymology

Abbreviation of mathematics, from Greek mathematikos, relating to learning.
