Gaudi i Cornet

1. PROPN

/ gaʊˈdi i kɔrˈnɛt / (AmE), / gaʊˈdi i kɔːˈnɛt / (BrE)
Spanish architect who was a leading exponent of Art Nouveau in Europe, known for his distinctive and innovative buildings in Barcelona.
Spanish Art Nouveau architect (1852–1926)

Examples

Many tourists visit Barcelona to admire Gaudi i Cornet's imaginative architecture.
The style of Gaudi i Cornet can be seen in the curves and colors of the Sagrada Família.
Scholars often discuss how Gaudi i Cornet influenced modern architectural design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Antoni Gaudí - Spanish architect known for Sagrada Família and Art Nouveau works

Etymology

Catalan-Spanish name; commonly known as Antoni Gaudí.
