Michel de Notredame

1. PROPN

/ miːˈʃɛl də ˈnɒtrəˌdæm / (AmE), / miˈʃɛl də ˈnɒtrəˌdam / (BrE)
(1503–1566) A French astrologer and physician best known for his book of cryptic predictions, which continue to be widely discussed and interpreted.
French astrologer and author of prophecies

Examples

Many people attribute accurate predictions of historical events to Michel de Notredame.
The mysterious prophecies of Michel de Notredame have been interpreted in various ways over the centuries.
Historians continue to debate the intentions behind Michel de Notredame's famous writings.
