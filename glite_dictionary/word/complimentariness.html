complimentariness

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːmpləˌmɛn.təˈriː.nəs / (AmE), / ˌkɒmplɪˌmɛn.təˈrɪə.nɪs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being given to or characterized by expressing praise, congratulation, encouragement, or respect.
quality of being complimentary

Examples

Her complimentariness made everyone feel appreciated and motivated during the team meeting.
There was an unmistakable air of complimentariness in the speaker's remarks about her colleagues.
Critics questioned whether his complimentariness was genuine or merely intended to win favor.

Synset Examples

M. Bourget is highly complimentary to these maggots of literature; but he is ingeniously vague in his complimentariness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: complimentary - expressing praise, approval, or respect

Etymology

From 'complimentary' + '-ness'.
