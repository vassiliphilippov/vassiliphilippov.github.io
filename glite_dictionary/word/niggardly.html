niggardly

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɪɡərdli / (AmE), / ˈnɪɡədli / (BrE)
Unwilling to give, spend, or share; excessively mean or miserly.
unwilling to give or spend; miserly

Examples

Despite his wealth, he left only a niggardly tip for the waiter.
Her donation was so niggardly that it barely covered the cost of a sandwich.
The company’s niggardly holiday bonuses disappointed many employees.

Synset Examples

a niggardly tip

Related Terms

  • Antonym: generous - Willing to give or share; antonym of 'niggardly'.
  • Synonym: stingy - Unwilling to give or spend; synonym of 'niggardly'.

Etymology

From Middle English 'niggard' (mean person) + '-ly'.
