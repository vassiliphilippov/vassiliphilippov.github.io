malemute

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæl.ə.mjuːt / (AmE), / ˈmæl.ə.mjuːt / (BrE)
a large breed of sled dog developed in Alaska, known for its strength and endurance
large Alaskan sled dog breed

Examples

The malemute is renowned for its ability to pull heavy sleds across long distances.
Their malemute thrived in the snowy Alaskan wilderness.
Many explorers rely on the strength and stamina of the malemute during Arctic expeditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Alaskan Malamute - large Alaskan sled dog with a thick coat, also spelled 'Malamute'

Etymology

From the name of the Mahlemut, an Inuit tribe of northwestern Alaska.
