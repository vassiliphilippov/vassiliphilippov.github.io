cowskin

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊˌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈkaʊˌskɪn / (BrE)
leather made from the hide of a cow.
leather from a cowhide

Examples

The artisan crafted a sturdy bag from cowskin.
Vintage armchairs upholstered in cowskin remain highly sought after.
The company uses only genuine cowskin for its premium shoes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cowhide - the skin of a cow, especially when tanned
