hilariously

1. ADV

/ hɪˈlɛriəsli / (AmE), / hɪˈlarɪəsli / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely funny or causes laughter.
in a very funny manner

Examples

The movie was hilariously over-the-top, keeping the audience in stitches for hours.
He hilariously imitated his teacher, making everyone in class laugh.
Her attempt to bake a cake went hilariously wrong, ending in a kitchen disaster.

Synset Examples

hilariously funny

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hilarious - extremely funny

Etymology

From "hilarious" (Cheerful, extremely funny) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
