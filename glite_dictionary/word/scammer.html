scammer

1. NOUN

/ ˈskæmər / (AmE), / ˈskæmə / (BrE)
a person who deceives others in order to cheat them out of money or valuables
person who commits fraud

Examples

The police warned about a new scammer targeting elderly residents in the area.
She realized she had given her bank details to an online scammer.
Many scammers use fake phone numbers to trick people into revealing personal information.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fraudster - a person who commits fraud
  • Antonym: victim - a person who is deceived or cheated

Etymology

From 'scam' (a fraudulent scheme) + '-er' (agent suffix).
