Vedic

1. ADJ

/ ˈveɪdɪk / (AmE), / ˈviːdɪk / (BrE)
relating to the Vedas or to the ancient Sanskrit language in which they were composed
relating to the Vedas or ancient Sanskrit

Examples

The Vedic texts are among the oldest known religious scriptures.
Many rituals described in Vedic literature are still practiced today.
He is an expert in Vedic Sanskrit and its grammatical structure.

Synset Examples

the Vedic literature

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'veda' (knowledge), referring to ancient sacred texts of India.
