Capra aegagrus

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæprə iːˈɡæɡrəs / (AmE), / ˈkæprə iːˈɡæɡrəs / (BrE)
A species of wild goat native to Iran and adjacent regions, also considered to be the ancestor of the domestic goat.
wild goat species native to Iran

Examples

The Capra aegagrus thrives in rocky, mountainous terrain where few other animals can survive.
Scientists believe that Capra aegagrus is the wild ancestor of modern domestic goats.
Conservation efforts are underway to protect the remaining populations of Capra aegagrus in Iran.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wild goat - goat species found in mountainous regions, closely related to Capra aegagrus
  • Degree Lower: domestic goat - domesticated subspecies derived from Capra aegagrus

Etymology

From Latin 'capra' (goat) and Greek 'aegagrus' (wild goat).
