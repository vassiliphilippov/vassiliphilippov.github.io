pedagogue

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛdəˌɡɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈpɛdəɡɒɡ / (BrE)
a teacher, especially one who educates children or young people, sometimes with a connotation of being overly formal or didactic
teacher, especially of children

Examples

The old-fashioned pedagogue favored strict discipline in his classroom.
As a dedicated pedagogue, she inspired her students to enjoy learning.
In the novel, the pedagogue is portrayed as rigid but genuinely caring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially in a school

Etymology

From Greek paidagōgos (slave who led a boy to school), from pais (boy) + agein (to lead).
