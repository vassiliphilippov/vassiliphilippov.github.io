traveller

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævələr / (AmE), / ˈtrævələ / (BrE)
a person who is travelling or who frequently travels to different places
person who travels

Examples

The traveller arrived at the station just in time to catch the last train.
Experienced travellers always research their destinations before leaving home.
As a frequent traveller, she keeps a packed suitcase ready at all times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tourist - a person who travels for pleasure, especially sightseeing
  • Synonym: commuter - a person who travels regularly between home and work

Etymology

From Old French *travailler* (to toil or labor), later evolving into 'travel' in English.
