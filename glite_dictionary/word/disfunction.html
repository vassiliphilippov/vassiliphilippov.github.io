disfunction

1. NOUN

/ dɪsˈfʌŋkʃən / (AmE), / dɪsˈfʌŋkʃən / (BrE)
Impairment or abnormality in the functioning of a bodily organ, system, or social group.
abnormal functioning of body or social unit
medical

Examples

Chronic liver disfunction can lead to serious health complications.
Relationship disfunction often arises from poor communication and unresolved conflicts.
The medication was prescribed to treat sexual disfunction in older men.
