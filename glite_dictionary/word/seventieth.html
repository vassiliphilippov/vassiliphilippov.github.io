seventieth

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛvənˌtiəθ / (AmE), / ˈsɛv.ən.ti.ɪθ / (BrE)
being the ordinal number seventy in a sequence; 70th.
ordinal number for 70th

Examples

She finished in seventieth place out of a hundred competitors.
The seventieth anniversary of the school was celebrated with a large ceremony.
Only the seventieth page of the novel contains the missing clue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seventieth - the number 70th in order
  • Degree Lower: sixty-ninth - the ordinal number for 69th
  • Degree Higher: seventy-first - the ordinal number for 71st

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɛvənˌtiɪθ / (AmE), / ˈsɛv.ən.ti.ɪθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number matching the number seventy; being number 70 in a sequence.
ordinal number: 70th in sequence

Examples

He celebrated his seventieth birthday with all his family and friends.
The school finished in seventieth place in the national competition this year.
On the list, my name was the seventieth to be called.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sixty-ninth - ordinal number: 69th in sequence
  • Degree Higher: seventy-first - ordinal number: 71st in sequence

Etymology

Formed from 'seventy' (from Old English 'seofontig') plus the ordinal suffix '-th'.
