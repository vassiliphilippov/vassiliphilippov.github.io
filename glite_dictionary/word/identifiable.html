identifiable

1. ADJ

/ aɪˌdɛn.tɪˈfaɪ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / aɪˌdɛn.tɪˈfaɪ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be recognized or distinguished as a particular person or thing.
able to be recognized or identified

Examples

The suspect was easily identifiable because of his distinctive tattoo.
No identifiable fingerprints were found at the scene of the crime.
She wore a mask to make herself less identifiable in public.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unidentifiable - not able to be identified
  • Synonym: recognizable - able to be recognized

Etymology

From identify + -able, modelled on French 'identifiable'.
