sigmoidal

1. ADJ

/ sɪɡˈmɔɪdəl / (AmE), / sɪɡˈmɔɪdəl / (BrE)
relating to or shaped like the sigmoid flexure in the large intestine
relating to the sigmoid colon
medical

Examples

The doctor observed a sigmoidal shadow on the X-ray, indicating a potential issue in the sigmoid colon.
A sigmoidal curve can be seen in the structure of the large intestine.
During the surgery, the team carefully examined the sigmoidal region for any signs of disease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sigmoid - having an S-shaped curve; relating to the sigmoid colon

Etymology

From Greek 'sigmoeidēs' (sigmoid), from 'sigma' (the Greek letter S), referring to the S-shaped curve.
