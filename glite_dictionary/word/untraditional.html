untraditional

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌntrəˈdɪʃənl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌntrəˈdɪʃənl̩ / (BrE)
not following or conforming to traditional ideas, methods, or customs
not conforming to tradition

Examples

Her untraditional approach to teaching inspired her students to think creatively.
They decided to have an untraditional wedding ceremony in the forest.
The chef is known for using untraditional ingredients in classic dishes.

Synset Examples

nontraditional designs
nontraditional practices

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nontraditional - not following or conforming to tradition
  • Antonym: traditional - following or conforming to tradition
