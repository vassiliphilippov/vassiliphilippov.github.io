irresolutely

1. ADV

/ ˌɪrˈrɛzəˌlutli / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛzəluːtli / (BrE)
In a way that shows lack of determination or decisiveness.
in an undecided or hesitant way

Examples

She answered irresolutely, unable to make up her mind about the decision.
He irresolutely glanced at the door, unsure if he should leave or stay.
The committee acted irresolutely, delaying the important vote several times.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: resolutely - in a determined or unwavering way
  • Synonym: hesitantly - in a hesitant or uncertain manner

Etymology

From Latin in- (not) + resolutus (determined).
