Thespesia

1. NOUN

/ θɛsˈpiːziə / (AmE), / θɛsˈpiːziə / (BrE)
A small genus of tropical trees, including the portia tree, often found in coastal regions.
genus of tropical trees

Examples

Botanists have identified several species within the genus Thespesia.
The Thespesia populnea, commonly known as the portia tree, is valued for its timber.
Many coastal communities rely on Thespesia trees to prevent erosion.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin, derived from Greek 'Thespēsios', meaning 'divine' or 'marvelous'.
