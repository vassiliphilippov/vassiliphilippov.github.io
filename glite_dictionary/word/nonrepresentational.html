nonrepresentational

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃənəl / (BrE)
(of art) not representing or depicting objects as they appear in the natural world; abstract.
not representing real-world objects in art

Examples

The gallery features a collection of nonrepresentational paintings by modern artists.
She prefers nonrepresentational art that emphasizes color and form over realistic images.
His early works were realistic, but later he embraced a nonrepresentational style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abstract - (of art) depicting ideas rather than reality
  • Antonym: representational - (of art) depicting recognisable objects or scenes

Etymology

From non- (not) + representational, from Latin repraesentare (to present) + -al.
