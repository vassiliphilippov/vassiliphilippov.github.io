Croat

1. NOUN

/ ˈkroʊ.æt / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊ.at / (BrE)
a person belonging to the Slavic ethnic group native to Croatia
person from Croatia (Slavic ethnic group)

Examples

Many Croats celebrate national holidays with traditional music and dance.
The Croat community in Australia has grown significantly over the last decade.
He proudly identified himself as a Croat during the interview.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Croatian - of or relating to Croatia, its people, or language

Etymology

From Croatian 'Hrvat', the self-designation of the people.
