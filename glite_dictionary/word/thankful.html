thankful

1. ADJ

/ ˈθæŋkfəl / (AmE), / ˈθaŋkfəl / (BrE)
feeling or expressing gratitude; pleased and relieved about something
feeling or expressing gratitude

Examples

She felt truly thankful for all the support she received during her recovery.
I am thankful that the weather stayed clear for our outdoor wedding.
After finding his lost wallet, he was extremely thankful to the person who returned it.

Synset Examples

a grateful heart
grateful for the tree's shade
a thankful smile

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grateful - feeling or showing an appreciation of kindness; thankful
  • Antonym: ungrateful - not feeling or showing gratitude

Etymology

From Old English 'þancful', from 'þanc' (thought, gratitude) + '-ful' (full of).
