homey

1. ADJ

/ ˈhoʊmi / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmi / (BrE)
(of a place or atmosphere) cozy, comfortable, and welcoming, like home.
cozy and comfortable like home

Examples

The little café had a homey atmosphere that made everyone feel welcome.
Her apartment is so homey and inviting that guests never want to leave.
They decorated their cabin to give it a homey feel, complete with quilts and warm lighting.

Synset Examples

the homely everyday atmosphere
a homey little inn

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homely - (British) warm and comfortable in a way that makes you feel at home
  • Antonym: unwelcoming - not friendly or inviting, lacking comfort

Etymology

From 'home' + diminutive or descriptive suffix '-y', indicating resemblance.
