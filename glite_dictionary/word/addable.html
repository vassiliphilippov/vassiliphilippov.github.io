addable

1. ADJ

/ ˈædəbəl / (AmE), / ˈadəbəl / (BrE)
Able to be added or joined to something; capable of being added.
capable of being added

Examples

Is the new feature addable to the current software version?
Only addable items will appear in this menu for selection.
The mathematics problem requires you to find which numbers are addable to seventeen to make a prime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: combinable - able to be combined with something
  • Antonym: removable - able to be removed

Etymology

From 'add' + suffix '-able'.
