Cape Canaveral

1. PROPN

/ ˌkeɪp kəˈnævərəl / (AmE), / ˌkeɪp kəˈnævərəl / (BrE)
a sandy promontory on the eastern coast of Florida, United States, known as the site of a major NASA spaceflight center
U.S. site for NASA space launches

Examples

The rocket was launched from Cape Canaveral early in the morning.
She has always dreamed of witnessing a space launch at Cape Canaveral.
Many important missions in U.S. space history have started at Cape Canaveral.

Etymology

Named after the Spanish word 'cañaveral', meaning 'canebrake' or 'canefield'.
