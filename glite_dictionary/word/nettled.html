nettled

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɛt̬əld / (AmE), / ˈnɛtəld / (BrE)
annoyed, irritated, or made angry by something
annoyed or irritated

Examples

She was nettled by his repeated interruptions during the meeting.
He looked nettled after receiving unnecessary criticism from his boss.
I became increasingly nettled as the noise outside grew louder.

Synset Examples

made an irritated gesture
feeling nettled from the constant teasing
peeved about being left out
felt really pissed at her snootiness
riled no end by his lies
roiled by the delay

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irritated - feeling annoyed or bothered
  • Antonym: calm - not agitated or irritated; peaceful

Etymology

From the verb 'nettle', based on the stinging nature of the nettle plant.
