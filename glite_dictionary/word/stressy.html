stressy

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrɛsi / (AmE), / ˈstɹɛsi / (BrE)
feeling stressed or anxious; easily becoming tense or nervous, especially in informal contexts.
informal; feeling stressed or anxious
informal

Examples

I've been feeling really stressy about my exams this week.
Don't get too stressy over small mistakes at work.
Whenever there's a big deadline, she becomes quite stressy.

Synset Examples

She felt stressy before the big presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stressed - feeling mental strain or anxiety
  • Antonym: calm - free from agitation or anxiety
