Whitney

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪtni / (AmE), / ˈwɪtni / (BrE)
the highest peak in the Sierra Nevada range in California, United States, standing at 14,494 feet (4,418 meters).
highest peak in California's Sierra Nevada

Examples

Mount Whitney attracts thousands of hikers each year seeking to reach its summit.
From the top of Whitney, you can see for miles across the Sierra Nevada.
Climbing Whitney is considered one of the top hiking achievements in California.

Etymology

Named after Josiah Whitney, an American geologist.
