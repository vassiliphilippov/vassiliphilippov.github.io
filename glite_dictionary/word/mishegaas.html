mishegaas

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪʃəˈɡɑs / (AmE), / ˌmɪʃəˈɡɑːs / (BrE)
Craziness; senseless or irrational behavior or activity, typically used with a humorous or pejorative tone.
craziness; senseless behavior
informal american

Examples

Every family has its share of mishegaas, but somehow we all get along.
I can’t handle the mishegaas of holiday shopping at the mall.
Ignore his latest mishegaas—it’s just another one of his weird ideas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: madness - the state of being mentally ill or extremely foolish behavior
  • Antonym: sanity - the state of being sane; soundness of mind

Etymology

From Yiddish משׁיגאַס (*mishegas*), meaning 'madness', from Hebrew מְשׁוּגָע (*m'shuga*) 'crazy'.
