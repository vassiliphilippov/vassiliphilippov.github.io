Enneagram

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛniəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈɛniəɡram / (BrE)
A model of human personality that classifies people into nine interconnected personality types, often used in psychology and self-help.
personality typology of nine types

Examples

She studied the Enneagram to better understand her own personality type.
Many workshops use the Enneagram as a tool for self-discovery and relationship building.
He explained how the Enneagram divides people into nine main personality categories.

Etymology

From Greek 'ennea' (nine) + 'gramma' (something written or drawn).
