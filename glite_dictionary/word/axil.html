axil

1. NOUN

/ ˈæksɪl / (AmE), / ˈæksɪl / (BrE)
the upper angle between a stem and a branch or leafstalk in a plant
upper angle between stem and branch/leafstalk

Examples

The bud developed in the axil of the leaf.
Flowers often form in the axil between a branch and its main stem.
You can clearly see the tiny fruit forming in the axil of the plant.

Etymology

From Latin 'axilla' (armpit).
