nineteenth

1. NOUN

/ ˌnaɪnˈtinθ / (AmE), / ˌnaɪnˈtiːnθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number corresponding to the number 19 in a sequence; the position of someone or something that is number 19 in a series.
nineteenth in a sequence

Examples

Her nineteenth birthday was a memorable celebration for the whole family.
The runner finished in nineteenth place out of fifty participants.
He was the nineteenth person to sign the petition.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eighteenth - ordinal number for position 18
  • Degree Higher: twentieth - ordinal number for position 20

Etymology

Formed from 'nineteen' + suffix '-th' indicating an ordinal number.

2. ADJ

/ ˈnaɪnˌtinθ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪntiːnθ / (BrE)
Coming next after eighteenth in a sequence; being number nineteen in order or position.
coming after eighteenth; ordinal number 19th

Examples

She finished in nineteenth place at the marathon.
The nineteenth century saw tremendous changes in technology and society.
He is the nineteenth person on our list of speakers.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eighteenth - coming next after the seventeenth in position; 18th
  • Degree Higher: twentieth - coming next after the nineteenth in position; 20th

Etymology

Formed from "nineteen" + the ordinal suffix "-th."
