thoughtfully

1. ADV

/ ˈθɔt.fəli / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt.fəli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows consideration for others or careful thought.
in a considerate or thoughtful manner

Examples

She listened thoughtfully to his explanation before responding.
He thoughtfully paused before making his final decision.
The waiter thoughtfully brought extra napkins to the table.

Synset Examples

he had thoughtfully brought with him some food to share

Related Terms

  • Synonym: considerately - in a way that shows consideration for others
  • Antonym: thoughtless - lacking consideration for others
Source: WordNet

2. ADV

/ ˈθɔt.fəli / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt.fəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows careful consideration or deep thought.
in a careful or considerate manner

Examples

She listened thoughtfully to his explanation before responding.
He gazed thoughtfully at the painting, trying to understand its meaning.
After the meeting, Maria walked thoughtfully through the park, considering her options.

Synset Examples

he stared thoughtfully out the window

Related Terms

  • Antonym: thoughtless - showing lack of consideration or thought
