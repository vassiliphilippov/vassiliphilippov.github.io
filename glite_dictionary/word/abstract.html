abstract

1. NOUN

/ ˈæbˌstrækt / (AmE), / ˈab.strækt / (BrE)
A general idea or concept not associated with any specific instance; something that exists only as a thought or theory.
general idea or concept

Examples

Philosophy often deals with abstract rather than concrete realities.
She prefers to think in terms of practical outcomes, not in the abstract.
Beauty is an abstract that is hard to define precisely.

Synset Examples

he loved her only in the abstract — not in person

Related Terms

  • Antonym: concrete - actual thing or instance; the opposite of abstract

Etymology

From Latin abstractum, meaning 'a separated or removed thing.'

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-05862715-n
Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ ˈæbˌstrækt / (AmE), / ˈæbstrækt / (BrE)
a brief summary of the main points of a speech, argument, article, or academic paper
summary of main points

Examples

The journal requires an abstract summarizing your key findings.
Please read the abstract to get a quick overview of the article.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summary - a brief statement of the main points
  • Synonym: synopsis - a condensed statement or outline

Etymology

From Latin 'abstractum', meaning 'something drawn away'.

3. VERB

/ æbˈstrækt / (AmE), / æbˈstrækt / (BrE)
to consider something independently of its specific instance; to think about it in a general or theoretical manner
consider theoretically; remove from specifics

Examples

It's often useful to abstract from the details when developing a new theory.
When solving this problem, try to abstract away from the specifics of your own experience.
Researchers sometimes abstract general principles from individual cases.

Synset Examples

Let's abstract away from this particular example

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to generalize - to make a general or broad statement by inferring from specific cases
  • Antonym: to concretize - to make something more concrete or specific

Etymology

From Latin abstrahere (to draw away), from ab- (away) and trahere (to draw).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00736610-v
Synset POS: v
