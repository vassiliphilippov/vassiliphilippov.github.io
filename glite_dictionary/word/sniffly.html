sniffly

1. ADJ

/ ˈsnɪf.li / (AmE), / ˈsnɪf.li / (BrE)
Tending to sniffle, especially because of a cold or allergies.
tending to sniffle; runny-nosed

Examples

My son felt sniffly all day because of his spring allergies.
She sounded sniffly on the phone and said she was coming down with a cold.
After walking in the chilly rain, I got a bit sniffly myself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sniffling - making repeated sniffing sounds as from a cold or crying
