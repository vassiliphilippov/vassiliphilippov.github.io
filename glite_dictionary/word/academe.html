academe

1. NOUN

/ ˌækəˈdim / (AmE), / ˌakəˈdiːm / (BrE)
the world of academic institutions, scholars, and learning; academia
the academic world; academia

Examples

After twenty years in academe, she decided to transition to a corporate career.
Many believe that academe is disconnected from the needs of everyday people.
He spent much of his life publishing research and teaching in academe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: academia - the environment or community concerned with the pursuit of research, education, and scholarship

Etymology

From Greek 'Akadēmeia', the name of Plato’s school of philosophy.
