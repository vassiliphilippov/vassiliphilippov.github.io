hummingbird

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌmɪŋˌbɝd / (AmE), / ˈhʌmɪŋˌbɜːd / (BrE)
a very small, brightly colored bird native to the Americas, with a slender bill and wings adapted for rapid, hovering flight
tiny, colorful bird with rapid wings

Examples

A hummingbird hovered above the red flowers, drinking nectar with its long beak.
Many people hang feeders in their gardens to attract hummingbirds.
The iridescent feathers of the hummingbird sparkled in the morning sunlight.
