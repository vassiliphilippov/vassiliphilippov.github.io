tagliatelle

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɑːl.jəˈtɛl.i / (AmE), / ˌtæl.jəˈtɛl.eɪ / (BrE)
long, flat ribbons of pasta, typically served with a sauce
long flat pasta ribbons

Examples

She ordered tagliatelle with a rich meat ragu for dinner.
Fresh tagliatelle cooks quickly and pairs well with creamy sauces.
The chef demonstrated how to make homemade tagliatelle from scratch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fettuccine - pasta cut into flat, thick ribbons

Etymology

Italian, plural of tagliatella, from tagliare (to cut).
