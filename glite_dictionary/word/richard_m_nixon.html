Richard M. Nixon

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɪtʃərd ˈɛm ˈnɪksən / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃəd ˈɛm ˈnɪksən / (BrE)
(1913–1994) The 37th President of the United States (1969–1974), and Vice President under Eisenhower; resigned from the presidency after the Watergate scandal.
37th U.S. President (1969–1974); resigned after Watergate

Examples

In 1974, Richard M. Nixon resigned from the presidency amid the Watergate scandal.
