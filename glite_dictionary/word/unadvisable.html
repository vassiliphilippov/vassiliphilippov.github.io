unadvisable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ədˈvaɪ.zə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ədˈvaɪ.zə.bəl / (BrE)
not recommended; not wise or prudent
not recommended or wise

Examples

It is unadvisable to invest all your savings in one company.
Walking alone at night in this area is generally considered unadvisable.
Skipping meals before a long journey is unadvisable for most people.

Synset Examples

running on the ice is inadvisable

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inadvisable - not recommended; not wise or sensible
  • Antonym: advisable - recommended; wise or prudent

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + advisable (recommended, wise).
