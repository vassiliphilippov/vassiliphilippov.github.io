mincemeat

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪnsˌmiːt / (AmE), / ˈmɪnsmiːt / (BrE)
A mixture of chopped dried fruits, spices, and sometimes meat, often used as a filling for pies.
spiced fruit (and sometimes meat) pie filling

Examples

Traditional mince pies are filled with mincemeat made from dried fruit and spices.
She bought a jar of mincemeat to prepare for the holidays.
This recipe uses vegetarian mincemeat, with no actual meat included.

Etymology

From 'minced meat,' originally denoting a mixture containing finely chopped meat and fruit.
