almighty

1. ADJ

/ ɔlˈmaɪti / (AmE), / ɔːlˈmaɪti / (BrE)
having unlimited power; all-powerful.
having unlimited power

Examples

Many people pray to the almighty for guidance in difficult times.
With almighty strength, the mythic hero defeated his enemies.
The storm struck with almighty force, destroying everything in its path.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: omnipotent - having unlimited power; able to do anything
  • Antonym: powerless - lacking power or ability

Etymology

From Old English 'ælmīhta', meaning 'all-powerful'.
