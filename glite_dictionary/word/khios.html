Khios

1. PROPN

/ ˈki.oʊs / (AmE), / ˈkiː.ɒs / (BrE)
an island in the Aegean Sea, off the west coast of Turkey, belonging to Greece
Greek island in the Aegean Sea

Examples

The ancient city of Khios was known for its production of mastic gum.
Many tourists visit Khios every summer to enjoy its beaches and history.
Earthquakes have historically affected the island of Khios due to its location.

Etymology

From Greek Χίος (Khíos).
