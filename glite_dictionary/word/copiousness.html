copiousness

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊpiəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈkəʊpiəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being plentiful or having an abundant supply of something
abundant supply; plentifulness

Examples

The copiousness of her notes impressed the entire team during the meeting.
We were overwhelmed by the copiousness of the harvest this year.
Throughout history, periods of copiousness have often been followed by times of scarcity.

Synset Examples

an age of abundance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abundance - a very large quantity of something; great plenty
  • Antonym: scarcity - the state of being in short supply; shortage

Etymology

From Latin copiosus (plentiful, abundant), from copia (plenty).
