Mainer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪnər / (AmE), / ˈmeɪnə / (BrE)
a person who is a native or resident of the state of Maine, USA
person from Maine

Examples

She proudly calls herself a Mainer even after living away for decades.
Many Mainers are known for their strong community spirit.
As a lifelong Mainer, he cherishes the state’s natural beauty.

Etymology

Formed from the state name 'Maine' with the suffix '-er' denoting inhabitant.
