Julio Iglesias

1. PROPN

/ ˈhuːlioʊ ɪˈgleɪsiəs / (AmE), / ˈhuːliəʊ ɪˈgleɪsiəs / (BrE)
(born 1943) A Spanish singer renowned for his romantic ballads and love songs.
Spanish singer known for ballads

Examples

Julio Iglesias has sold millions of albums worldwide, making him one of the best-selling Latin artists ever.
