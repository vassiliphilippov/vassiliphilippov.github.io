Roget

1. PROPN

/ roʊˈʒeɪ / (AmE), / rəʊˈʒeɪ / (BrE)
Peter Mark Roget (1779–1869), English physician and lexicographer, best known for compiling Roget's Thesaurus.
English lexicographer; compiled Roget's Thesaurus

Examples

Roget is celebrated for creating the original version of the thesaurus that bears his name.
Many writers owe their expanded vocabularies to the meticulous work of Roget.
Students often consult Roget when searching for synonyms for their essays.
