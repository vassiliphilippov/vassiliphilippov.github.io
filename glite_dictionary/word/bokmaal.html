Bokmaal

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuːkˌmɔl / (AmE), / ˈbʊkmɑːl / (BrE)
One of the two official written standards of the Norwegian language, closely related to Danish.
official Norwegian written language

Examples

Many Norwegian books are published in Bokmaal.
She learned both Bokmaal and Nynorsk at school in Oslo.
Official documents in Norway are often available in Bokmaal and Nynorsk.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Nynorsk - the other official written standard of Norwegian

Etymology

From Norwegian 'Bokmål', meaning 'book language'.
