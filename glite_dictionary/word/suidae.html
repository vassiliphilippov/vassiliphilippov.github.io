Suidae

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈsjuːɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of mammals that includes pigs, hogs, and boars.
the pig family (mammals)

Examples

Modern farmers raise several species in the Suidae family for food and agriculture.
Wild members of the Suidae family can be found in forests and grasslands globally.
The zoologist gave a lecture on the evolutionary history of Suidae.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pig - a domesticated mammal from the family Suidae
  • Synonym: boar - a wild pig; a member of Suidae

Etymology

From modern Latin Suidae, based on Latin sus (pig).
