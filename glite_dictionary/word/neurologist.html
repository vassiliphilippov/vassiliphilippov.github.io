neurologist

1. NOUN

/ nuˈrɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / njʊəˈrɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system
doctor specializing in the nervous system
medical

Examples

After years of unexplained headaches, she consulted a neurologist for a thorough evaluation.
The neurologist ordered several tests to identify the cause of his numbness and weakness.
During her residency, she decided to specialize as a neurologist because she was fascinated by the brain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neurosurgeon - a doctor specializing in performing surgery on the nervous system
  • Synonym: psychiatrist - a doctor specializing in mental illness

Etymology

From French neurologiste, from Greek neuron (nerve) + -logist (specialist).
