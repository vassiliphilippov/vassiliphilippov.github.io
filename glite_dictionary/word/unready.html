unready

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈrɛdi / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɛdi / (BrE)
not prepared or in a state of readiness; slow to understand or react
not prepared; slow to respond

Examples

She felt unready for the challenge and asked for more time to prepare.
His unready answer betrayed his surprise at the unexpected question.
The team was unready for the sudden change in the weather conditions.

Synset Examples

she cursed her unready tongue

Related Terms

  • Antonym: prepared - ready and able to deal with something
  • Antonym: ready - in a suitable state for an activity, action, or situation

Etymology

From un- (not) + ready, formed in Middle English.
