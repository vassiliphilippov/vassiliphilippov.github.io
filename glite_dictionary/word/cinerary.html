cinerary

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪnəˌrɛri / (AmE), / ˈsɪnərəri / (BrE)
containing or used for the ashes of a cremated body, especially in reference to urns or vessels.
containing ashes of the cremated dead

Examples

The museum displayed a rare cinerary urn from ancient Rome.
Archaeologists uncovered several cinerary vases at the burial site.
She ordered a specially crafted cinerary box for her father's ashes.

Synset Examples

a cinerary urn

Etymology

From Latin cinis (ashes) + -ary (pertaining to).
