nelly

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛli / (AmE), / ˈnɛli / (BrE)
A person who is regarded as weak, cowardly, or overly sensitive, typically used in a disparaging or mocking way.
weak or cowardly person (disparaging)
informal offensive

Examples

Stop being such a nelly and try the roller coaster with us.
He's acting like a nelly about giving his presentation at work.
Don’t be a nelly—it’s just a little rain!

Synset Examples

He's acting like a nelly about the whole situation.
Stop being a nelly and join us for the hike.
Don't be such a nelly; just go for it!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wimp - a person who is weak and lacking in courage
  • Synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage
