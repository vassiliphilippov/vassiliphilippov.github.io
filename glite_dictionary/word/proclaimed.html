proclaimed

1. ADJ

/ proʊˈkleɪmd / (AmE), / prəˈkleɪmd / (BrE)
declared or announced publicly; made widely known
announced or declared publicly

Examples

The company's proclaimed goal is to achieve carbon neutrality by 2030.
He was known for his proclaimed support of environmental causes.
The city celebrated its proclaimed status as a center of the arts.

Synset Examples

their announced intentions
the newspaper's proclaimed adherence to the government's policy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: announced - stated publicly or made known officially
  • Antonym: secret - not known or meant to be known by others; kept hidden

Etymology

From Latin proclamare (to shout forth, announce) via Old French proclamer.
