appendant

1. ADJ

/ əˈpɛndənt / (AmE), / əˈpɛndənt / (BrE)
affixed or attached as an appendage; joined to something as a subordinate or supplementary part.
affixed as an appendage

Examples

A small medal was appendant to the ribbon.
Her signature appeared on an appendant sheet, not the main document.
The charm was appendant to the bracelet, giving it a personal touch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attached - joined or fastened to something
  • Antonym: detached - not connected or joined; separate

Etymology

From Latin appendens, present participle of appendere (to hang upon).
