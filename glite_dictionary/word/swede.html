Swede

1. NOUN

/ swiːd / (AmE), / swiːd / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Sweden
native or inhabitant of Sweden

Examples

She met a Swede on her trip who showed her around Stockholm.
After moving to London, he became friends with a group of Swedes.
The Swede was proud to share his country's traditions at the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Swedish - relating to Sweden, its people, or their language

Etymology

From Old English Swēoðeod, referring to the people of Sweden.
