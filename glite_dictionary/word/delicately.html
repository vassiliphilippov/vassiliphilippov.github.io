delicately

1. ADV

/ ˈdɛlɪkətli / (AmE), / ˈdɛlɪkətli / (BrE)
In a manner that is gentle, careful, or sensitive; with delicacy.
in a gentle or sensitive way

Examples

She placed the fragile vase delicately on the table to avoid breaking it.
He handled the newborn puppy delicately so as not to startle it.
The pastry chef delicately decorated the cake with intricate sugar flowers.

Synset Examples

finely shaped features
her fine drawn body

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gently - in a gentle manner
  • Antonym: roughly - in a rough or harsh manner

Etymology

Formed from 'delicate' (Middle English, from Old French, from Latin 'delicatus') + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
