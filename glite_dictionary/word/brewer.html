brewer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbruː.ər / (AmE), / ˈbruː.ə / (BrE)
a person who owns, manages, or operates a brewery where beer is made
owner or manager of a brewery

Examples

The local brewer introduced several new craft beers this year.
She became the first female brewer to win the national award for excellence.
As a successful brewer, he expanded his business across the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brewmaster - a person who supervises the brewing process in a brewery

Etymology

From Old English *breowan* (to brew).

2. NOUN

/ ˈbruːər / (AmE), / ˈbruːə / (BrE)
a person or company that makes beer, ale, or similar drinks by brewing
person or company that makes beer

Examples

The brewer introduced a new craft lager to much acclaim.
As an experienced brewer, Sarah knows how to balance hops and malt for the perfect flavor.
The local brewer hosts tours every Saturday afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brewery - a place where beer is made commercially
  • Antonym: distiller - a person or company that makes spirits by distillation

Etymology

From Old English 'breowan' (to brew).
