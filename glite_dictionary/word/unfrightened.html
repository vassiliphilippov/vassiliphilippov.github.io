unfrightened

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfraɪtnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfraɪtənd / (BrE)
not feeling fear; not frightened or scared.
not frightened or scared

Examples

She remained unfrightened even when the thunderstorm grew louder.
Despite the darkness, the children walked home unfrightened.
His unfrightened expression helped calm everyone around him.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: frightened - feeling fear or alarm

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + frightened.
