Belo Horizonte

1. PROPN

/ ˌbeɪloʊ ɔːˌɾiːˈzɔːnti / (AmE), / ˌbeɪləʊ ˌɒriːˈzɒnteɪ / (BrE)
A city in southeastern Brazil, north of Rio de Janeiro, known as the country's first planned city.
city in southeastern Brazil

Examples

The city of Belo Horizonte is renowned for its modern architecture and urban planning.
Many international firms have set up offices in Belo Horizonte due to its economic growth.
When traveling through Brazil, she spent two weeks exploring Belo Horizonte and its surrounding areas.

Etymology

From Portuguese, meaning 'Beautiful Horizon'.
