nontechnical

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈtɛknɪkəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈtɛknɪkəl / (BrE)
not involving or requiring technical knowledge, language, or skills; understandable or suitable for those without specialized training
not involving technical knowledge

Examples

The instructions were written in nontechnical language so everyone could understand them.
During the meeting, he focused on the nontechnical aspects of the project, such as teamwork and communication.
This book offers a nontechnical introduction to computer science for beginners.

Synset Examples

nontechnical aspects of the job
nontechnical training
an untechnical reader
in clear effective nontechnical language

Related Terms

  • Antonym: technical - relating to a particular subject, art, or craft, or its techniques

Etymology

From the prefix 'non-' (not) + 'technical' (from Greek *technikós*)
