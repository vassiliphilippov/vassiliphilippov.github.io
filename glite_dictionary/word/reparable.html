reparable

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛpərəbəl / (AmE), / ˈrɛpərəbəl / (BrE)
capable of being repaired or corrected
able to be repaired or rectified

Examples

The mechanic said the reparable damage could be fixed within a day.
Fortunately, the mistake was reparable and did not cause lasting harm.
All the parts were still intact, making the old clock completely reparable.

Synset Examples

reparable damage to the car
rectifiable wrongs

Related Terms

  • Antonym: irreparable - not able to be repaired or rectified

Etymology

From Latin reparabilis (that may be repaired), from reparare (to repair).
