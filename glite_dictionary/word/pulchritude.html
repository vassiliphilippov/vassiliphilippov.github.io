pulchritude

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌlkrɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈpʌlkrɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
physical beauty, especially that of a woman; attractiveness in appearance
physical beauty, especially of a woman

Examples

Her undeniable pulchritude captivated everyone at the gala.
The old poet wrote many verses in praise of youthful pulchritude.
Though admired for her intelligence, it was her rare pulchritude that was most often mentioned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty - the quality of being physically attractive

Etymology

From Latin 'pulchritudo', from 'pulcher' meaning 'beautiful'.
