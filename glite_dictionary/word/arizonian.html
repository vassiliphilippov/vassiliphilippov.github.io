Arizonian

1. NOUN

/ ɛrɪˈzoʊniən / (AmE), / ˌærɪˈzəʊniən / (BrE)
a native or resident of Arizona, a state in the southwestern United States
native or resident of Arizona

Examples

My neighbor is an Arizonian who moved to town last year.
Several Arizonians attended the cultural festival to share their traditions.
As an Arizonian, she loves hiking in the desert landscapes near her home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Arizonan - a native or resident of Arizona; alternative demonym

Etymology

From Arizona (name of the U.S. state) + -ian (demonym suffix from Latin *-ianus*).
