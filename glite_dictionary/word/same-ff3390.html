same

1. ADJ

/ seɪm / (AmE), / seɪm / (BrE)
identical to a previously mentioned or known person or thing; not different in identity
identical to something previously mentioned

Examples

We visited the same museum we went to last year.
She wore the same outfit to both interviews.
Are you talking about the same person I met yesterday?

Synset Examples

the same man I saw yesterday
never wore the same dress twice
this road is the same one we were on yesterday
on the same side of the street

Related Terms

  • Antonym: different - not the same; not identical
  • Synonym: identical - exactly the same in every detail

Etymology

From Old English 'same', from Proto-Germanic *samaz (same).
