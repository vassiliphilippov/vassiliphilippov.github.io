boy

1. NOUN

/ bɔɪ / (AmE), / bɔɪ / (BrE)
A male child or young male person.
male child; young male person

Examples

The boy eagerly opened his birthday presents.
She asked the boy to help her carry the books.
When he was a boy, he loved climbing trees.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: girl - female child; young female person
  • Degree Higher: man - adult male human

Etymology

From Middle English *boi*, possibly from Old French *embuié* (fettered, tied up), of uncertain origin.
