ear-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪr ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈɪə ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a form or outline resembling the shape of an ear
resembling the shape of an ear

Examples

The unusual leaf was ear-shaped, with a distinct curved edge.
They served the soup in charming, ear-shaped bowls at the restaurant.
The artist carved an ear-shaped pendant out of silver.
