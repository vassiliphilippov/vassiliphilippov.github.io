regulated

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛɡjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
controlled or governed according to rules, principles, or laws
controlled by rules or laws

Examples

These are regulated markets, so all companies must comply with strict guidelines.
The regulated flow of water prevents flooding in the valley.
Our school has a regulated schedule to maintain discipline.

Synset Examples

well regulated industries
houses with regulated temperature

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unregulated - not controlled or governed by rules or laws
  • Synonym: controlled - managed or guided according to rules or requirements

Etymology

From the verb 'regulate', from Latin 'regulare' (to rule, guide)
