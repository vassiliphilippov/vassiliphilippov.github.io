monarcho-fascist

1. ADJ

/ məˌnɑrkoʊˈfæʃɪst / (AmE), / məˌnɑːkəʊˈfæʃɪst / (BrE)
Relating to or supporting a political ideology or regime that combines elements of monarchy and fascism.
combining monarchy and fascism

Examples

The protesters rallied against the monarcho-fascist policies introduced by the government.
Several historians have analyzed the rise of monarcho-fascist movements in twentieth-century Europe.
His book provides a detailed critique of monarcho-fascist ideologies and their impact on society.

Synset Examples

They labeled the regime as monarcho-fascist in their protests.
The term 'monarcho-fascist' was used to describe their policies.
He wrote an article condemning the monarcho-fascist movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fascist - supporter of fascism
  • Synonym: monarchist - supporter of monarchy

Etymology

Formed from 'monarch-' (monarchy) and 'fascist.'
