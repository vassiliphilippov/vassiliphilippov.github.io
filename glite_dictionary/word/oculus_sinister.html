oculus sinister

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊ.kjəl.əs ˈsɪn.ɪ.stər / (AmE), / ˈɒk.jʊ.ləs ˈsɪn.ɪ.stər / (BrE)
the left eye, especially as referred to in medical contexts
the left eye (medical)
medical

Examples

The prescription for her glasses indicated a higher correction for her oculus sinister.
Doctors often use the abbreviation O.S. to refer to the oculus sinister in patient charts.
During the examination, he noticed increased pressure in the oculus sinister.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: oculus dexter - the right eye, especially as referred to in medical contexts

Etymology

From Latin 'oculus' (eye) and 'sinister' (left).
