rip-roaring

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪp ˌrɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪp ˌrɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely noisy, lively, or exciting.
noisy or lively; exciting

Examples

The concert turned into a rip-roaring party as the band played their final song.
They had a rip-roaring time at the amusement park last weekend.
The comedian's rip-roaring jokes kept the audience laughing nonstop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boisterous - noisy and lively
  • Synonym: rowdy - noisy and disorderly

Etymology

Formed from 'rip' and 'roaring', referencing great excitement or vigorous commotion.
