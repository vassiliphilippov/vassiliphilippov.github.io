verified

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɛr.əˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈvɛr.ɪ.faɪd / (BrE)
proven to be true, accurate, or authentic, often through examination or evidence
proven to be true or accurate

Examples

The documents he submitted were verified by the legal department before approval.
Only verified sources are accepted for academic research.
His verified account on the website reassured other users of his identity.

Synset Examples

a verified claim

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confirmed - shown to be true or definite, typically after checking
  • Antonym: unverified - not confirmed as true or accurate

Etymology

From Latin 'verus' (true) via Medieval Latin 'verificare' (to make true, verify).
