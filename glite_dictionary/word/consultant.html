consultant

1. NOUN

/ kənˈsʌltənt / (AmE), / kənˈsʌltənt / (BrE)
a person who provides expert advice professionally, especially in a particular area such as business, medicine, or education
professional expert advisor

Examples

The company hired a consultant to help improve its management structure.
After the merger, the hospital brought in a consultant to review medical protocols.
He works as a financial consultant for several start-up firms.

Synset Examples

an adviser helped students select their courses
the United States sent military advisors to Guatemala

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adviser - a person who gives advice, especially professionally
  • Synonym: expert - a person with comprehensive knowledge in a particular field

Etymology

From French 'consultant', from Latin 'consultare' (to consult).
