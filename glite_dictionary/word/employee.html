employee

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈplɔɪ.i / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔɪ.i / (BrE)
A person who is hired by an organization or individual to perform a job and is paid for their work.
a person hired to do a job

Examples

Each employee must complete the training before starting their new role.
The company hired five new employees this month to handle increased demand.
As an employee, you are entitled to paid leave and health insurance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: employer - a person or organization that hires people to work for them
  • Synonym: worker - a person who works, especially one who does manual or industrial labor

Etymology

From French 'employé', past participle of 'employer' (to employ).
