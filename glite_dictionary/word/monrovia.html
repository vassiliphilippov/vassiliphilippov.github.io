Monrovia

1. PROPN

/ mənˈroʊviə / (AmE), / mɒnˈrəʊviə / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Liberia, located on the Atlantic Coast.
capital of Liberia

Examples

Monrovia serves as the administrative and commercial center of Liberia.
Several international organizations have their offices in Monrovia.
The climate in Monrovia is tropical, with heavy rainfall during the wet season.

Etymology

Named after James Monroe, the fifth president of the United States.
