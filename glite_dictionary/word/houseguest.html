houseguest

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌɡɛst / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsɡɛst / (BrE)
a person who is staying in someone's house as a guest
person staying as a guest in a house

Examples

We have a houseguest staying with us for the weekend.
Please let me know if your houseguest has any dietary restrictions.
She enjoys being a houseguest and always helps out in the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guest - a person invited to stay in someone's home or attend an event
