Assouan

1. PROPN

/ əˈsuː.ɑːn / (AmE), / əˈsuː.ɑːn / (BrE)
an ancient city on the Nile River in southern Egypt, now commonly known as Aswan, famous for its two dams and historical significance
ancient city on the Nile in Egypt

Examples

The ancient city of Assouan is famous for its beautiful scenery and history.
Two major dams were constructed near Assouan to regulate the Nile's flow.
Tourists often visit Assouan to see the Temple of Philae and the Aswan High Dam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aswan - modern name for the same city in Egypt

Etymology

From Arabic 'Aswān', Latinized as 'Assouan'.
