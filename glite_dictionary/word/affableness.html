affableness

1. NOUN

/ ˈæfəblnəs / (AmE), / ˈafəblnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being friendly, pleasant, and easy to talk to
quality of being affable and approachable

Examples

Her affableness made guests feel welcome from the moment they arrived.
People are drawn to his affableness and positive attitude.
Despite his fame, the actor’s affableness never wavered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affability - the quality of being pleasant and easy to approach

Etymology

From Latin 'affābilis' (easy to converse with) via French 'affable' + '-ness'.
