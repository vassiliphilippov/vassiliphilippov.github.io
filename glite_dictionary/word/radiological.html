radiological

1. ADJ

/ ˌreɪdiəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving radiology, the use of radiation—such as X-rays—for medical examination or treatment
relating to radiology
medical

Examples

The radiological findings confirmed the doctor's initial diagnosis.
She specializes in radiological techniques for cancer treatment.
A detailed radiological examination was performed before surgery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiologic - relating to radiology (alternative spelling)

Etymology

From radiology + -ical, forming the adjective.
