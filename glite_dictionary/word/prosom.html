ProSom

1. NOUN

/ ˈproʊ sɑm / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ sɒm / (BrE)
a brand name for a frequently prescribed sleeping pill containing estazolam, used to treat insomnia
brand name for a sleeping pill
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed ProSom to help her manage her insomnia.
Some patients experience mild side effects when taking ProSom.
He asked his pharmacist if ProSom was suitable for short-term use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: estazolam - the active ingredient in ProSom, used as a hypnotic drug
  • Synonym: sleeping pill - a medicine taken to help induce sleep
