spiderflower

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪdərˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdəˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Cleome, noted for its showy, spider-like flowers and often strong scent.
a plant of the genus Cleome

Examples

The garden was filled with blooming spiderflowers of various colors.
Many gardeners appreciate the unique appearance of the spiderflower.
The strong scent of spiderflower attracts bees and butterflies.
