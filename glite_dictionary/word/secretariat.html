Secretariat

1. PROPN

/ ˌsɛkrəˈtɛriət / (AmE), / ˌsɛkrəˈtɛːriət / (BrE)
the American Thoroughbred racehorse that won the Triple Crown in 1973 and is widely regarded as one of the greatest racehorses of all time
famous Triple Crown-winning racehorse (1973)

Examples

Secretariat set a record time in the 1973 Belmont Stakes that still stands today.
Many consider Secretariat to be the greatest racehorse in the history of American horse racing.
The documentary highlighted Secretariat's remarkable Triple Crown victory.
