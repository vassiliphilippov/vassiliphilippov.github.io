unopened

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈoʊpənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈəʊpənd / (BrE)
(of something) not yet opened or unsealed; remaining in its original closed state.
not yet opened or unsealed

Examples

The package arrived yesterday, still unopened on the kitchen table.
He found an unopened bottle of wine in the cellar.
She was surprised to receive a gift with the tag attached and the box unopened.

Synset Examples

unopened Christmas presents

Related Terms

  • Antonym: opened - having been opened; not closed or sealed
