monometallic

1. ADJ

/ ˌmoʊnoʊmɪˈtælɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəʊmɪˈtælɪk / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) containing one atom of a metal in each molecule.
containing one metal atom per molecule

Examples

The researcher synthesized a monometallic complex for the experiment.
Unlike bimetallic species, monometallic catalysts have only one type of metal present.
Certain monometallic carbonyls have distinctive spectroscopic signatures.

Synset Examples

monometallic carbonyls

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bimetallic - containing two different metal atoms or ions

Etymology

From mono- (one) + metallic (relating to metal).
