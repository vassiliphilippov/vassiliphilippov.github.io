coordinately

1. ADV

/ koʊˈɔːrdənətli / (AmE), / kəʊˈɔːdɪnətli / (BrE)
In a coordinated or well-organized manner.
in a coordinated way

Examples

The team moved coordinately to complete the task efficiently.
She and her partner danced coordinately across the ballroom floor.
Multiple agencies worked coordinately to manage the response to the emergency.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: uncoordinatedly - in an uncoordinated manner

Etymology

Formed from 'coordinate' + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
