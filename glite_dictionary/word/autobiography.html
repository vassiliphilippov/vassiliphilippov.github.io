autobiography

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔtəbaɪˈɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəbaɪˈɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
an account of a person's life written by that person
a person's life story written by themself

Examples

She published her autobiography at the age of seventy, reflecting on her remarkable journey.
The autobiography reveals intimate details about the author's childhood and career.
Many famous people choose to write an autobiography to share their experiences with the world.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: biography - an account of someone's life written by someone else

Etymology

From Greek 'auto-' (self) + 'biographia' (biography).
