item

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.təm / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.təm / (BrE)
an individual object, part, or element that can be considered or dealt with separately
individual object or part

Examples

Each item on the list must be checked before we leave.
The museum displays every item with a detailed description.
She packed her suitcase, making sure not to forget a single item.

Synset Examples

it was perfect in all details

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detail - a small part of something; particular element

Etymology

From Latin 'item' (also, likewise, in the same manner).

/ ˈaɪ.təm / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.təm / (BrE)
an individual article or unit, especially as part of a list, collection, or group
individual unit or article

Examples

Related Terms
