billiard

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɪljərd / (AmE), / ˈbɪlɪəd / (BrE)
relating to or used in the game of billiards
related to billiards (the game)

Examples

He bought a new billiard cue for his home game room.
The green felt covering the billiard table looked brand new.
A standard billiard ball is heavier than a typical table tennis ball.

Synset Examples

a billiard ball
a billiard cue
a billiard table

Related Terms

  • Synonym: billiards - a cue sport played on a table with balls and a cue stick

Etymology

From French *billiard*, named after the game of billiards.
