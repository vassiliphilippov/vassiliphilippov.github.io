Duke Wayne

1. PROPN

/ duːk weɪn / (AmE), / djuːk weɪn / (BrE)
A nickname for John Wayne (1907–1979), a famous American film actor known for playing tough heroes.
nickname for actor John Wayne

Some people still refer to John Wayne as Duke Wayne out of respect for his legacy.
