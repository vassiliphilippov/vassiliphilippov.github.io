BPH

1. NOUN

/ bi pi eɪtʃ / (AmE), / biː piː eɪtʃ / (BrE)
an enlarged prostate gland, typically occurring as part of the natural aging process in men; abbreviation of benign prostatic hyperplasia
medical: enlarged prostate (benign prostatic hyperplasia)
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that my symptoms could be related to BPH.
Men over the age of fifty are commonly diagnosed with BPH.
Regular checkups are important for early detection of conditions like BPH.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: benign prostatic hyperplasia - medical term for noncancerous enlargement of the prostate

Etymology

Initialism of 'benign prostatic hyperplasia'.
