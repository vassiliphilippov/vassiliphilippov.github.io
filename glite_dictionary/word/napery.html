napery

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪpəri / (AmE), / ˈneɪpəri / (BrE)
table linens such as napkins, tablecloths, and placemats used for dining.
table linens for dining

Examples

The hotel’s collection of napery included elegant white tablecloths and embroidered napkins.
She inherited a set of fine Irish napery from her grandmother.
Caring for the household napery was one of the responsibilities of the head maid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: linen - cloth made from flax, commonly used for household items

Etymology

From Old French *naperie* (table linen), from *nappe* (tablecloth).
