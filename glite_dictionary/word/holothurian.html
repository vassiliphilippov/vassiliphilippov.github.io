holothurian

1. NOUN

/ hoʊ.ləˈθʊr.i.ən / (AmE), / hɒl.əˈθjʊə.ri.ən / (BrE)
A marine echinoderm with a soft, elongated body, tentacles around the mouth, and tube feet; commonly known as a sea cucumber.
marine animal (sea cucumber)

Examples

The ocean floor was dotted with large holothurians slowly moving through the sand.
Scientists study holothurians because of their important role in the marine ecosystem.
A curious diver watched a holothurian extend its tentacles to feed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea cucumber - a marine animal with an elongated body and leathery skin

Etymology

From New Latin Holothuria (genus name), from Greek holothourion (sea cucumber).
