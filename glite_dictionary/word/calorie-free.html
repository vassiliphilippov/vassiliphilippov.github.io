calorie-free

1. ADJ

/ ˌkæləriˈfriː / (AmE), / ˌkæləriˈfriː / (BrE)
(of food or drink) containing no calories or an insignificant amount of calories.
containing no calories

Examples

She prefers to drink calorie-free soda with her meals.
Many people choose calorie-free sweeteners to reduce their sugar intake.
The restaurant offers a variety of calorie-free beverages on the menu.

Synset Examples

light (or lite) beer
lite (or light) mayonnaise
a low-cal diet
cola light
