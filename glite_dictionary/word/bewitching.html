bewitching

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈwɪtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈwɪtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
so attractive or charming that it seems to cast a spell on people
very charming or enchanting

Examples

She smiled at him with a bewitching charm that made his heart race.
The garden was filled with the bewitching scent of blooming jasmine.
Her bewitching voice kept the audience spellbound throughout the entire performance.

Synset Examples

bewitching smile
Roosevelt was a captivating speaker
enchanting music
an enthralling book
antique papers of entrancing design
a fascinating woman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enchanting - delightfully charming or captivating
  • Synonym: captivating - holding interest or attraction in a charming way
  • Synonym: fascinating - extremely interesting or charming

Etymology

From verb 'bewitch' + -ing; 'bewitch' from Middle English 'bi-' (intensive prefix) + 'witch' (practice magic).
