Maha

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːhə / (AmE), / ˈmɑːhə / (BrE)
a member of a Siouan Native American people formerly living in the Missouri River valley in northeastern Nebraska
member of a Siouan tribe of Nebraska

Examples

The Maha were known for their intricate beadwork and detailed oral histories.
Several descendants of the Maha still reside in Nebraska today.
Anthropologists have studied the traditions of the Maha people extensively.
