dermatome

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɜrməˌtoʊm / (AmE), / ˈdɜːmətəʊm / (BrE)
a surgical instrument designed to cut thin slices of skin, typically for grafting purposes
instrument for cutting thin skin slices
medical

Examples

The surgeon used a dermatome to obtain a thin graft for the burn patient.
Proper calibration of the dermatome is essential for consistent skin thickness during procedures.
Cleaning the dermatome thoroughly after each use is crucial to prevent infection.

Etymology

From Greek derma (skin) + -tome (cutting instrument).
