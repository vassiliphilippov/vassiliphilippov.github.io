hibernal

1. ADJ

/ haɪˈbɜrnəl / (AmE), / haɪˈbɜːnəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of winter.
relating to winter

Examples

The hibernal landscape was covered with a blanket of snow.
Many animals enter a state of hibernal dormancy during the coldest months.
Her poems often evoke the quiet beauty of hibernal nights.

Synset Examples

bears in brumal sleep

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brumal - relating to winter
  • Antonym: vernal - relating to spring

Etymology

From Latin hibernalis, from hibernus (of winter).
