xcvi

1. NUM

/ ek.sɪv.aɪ / (AmE), / ek.sɪv.aɪ / (BrE)
The Roman numeral for ninety-six.
Roman numeral for 96

Examples

The book was published in the year xcvi of the reign, according to the inscription.
In Roman numerals, xcvi represents the number 96.
The championship's xcvi edition welcomed teams from across the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 96 - The Arabic numeral representation of ninety-six
  • Degree Lower: xcv - Roman numeral for ninety-five
  • Degree Higher: xcvii - Roman numeral for ninety-seven

Etymology

From Latin numerals: X (10), C (100), V (5), I (1), using the subtractive principle common in Roman numerals.
