itinerary

1. NOUN

/ aɪˈtɪnəˌrɛri / (AmE), / aɪˈtɪnərəri / (BrE)
a planned route or journey, or a written list of the places to be visited on a trip
planned route or journey

Examples

Our travel agent sent us a detailed itinerary for the entire trip.
The tour guide handed out the day's itinerary to all group members.
Please review the itinerary before we leave to make sure we visit all the planned sites.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: route - a way or course taken in getting from a starting point to a destination
  • Synonym: schedule - a plan for carrying out a process or procedure

Etymology

From Latin *itinerarium* (account of a journey), from *iter* (journey).

3. NOUN

/ aɪˈtɪnəˌrɛri / (AmE), / ɪˈtɪnərəri / (BrE)
a planned route or journey, or a schedule detailing proposed travel and destinations
planned route or travel schedule

Examples

Our travel agent sent us a detailed itinerary for the trip to Italy.
I printed the itinerary so we would know exactly where to go each day.
Her itinerary includes stops in Paris, Rome, and Barcelona.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: route - a way or course taken in getting from a starting point to a destination
  • Synonym: schedule - a plan for carrying out a process or procedure, giving lists of intended events and times

Etymology

From Latin 'itinerarium' (account of a journey), from 'iter', 'itineris' (journey, route).

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: n
