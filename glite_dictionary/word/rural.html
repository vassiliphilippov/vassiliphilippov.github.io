rural

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʊr.əl / (AmE), / ˈrʊə.rəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of the countryside rather than the town or city.
of the countryside, not urban

Examples

She prefers the quiet pace of rural life over the hustle of the city.
The rural landscape was dotted with small farms and open fields.
Government programs aim to improve healthcare access in rural communities.

Synset Examples

rural people
large rural households
unpaved rural roads
an economy that is basically rural
rural electrification
rural free delivery

Related Terms

  • Antonym: urban - relating to a city or town

Etymology

From Latin 'ruralis', from 'rus' meaning 'country, countryside'.
