correctional

1. ADJ

/ kəˈrɛkʃənl / (AmE), / kəˈrɛkʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the punishment, treatment, or rehabilitation of people who have committed crimes, especially in a prison or similar institution.
relating to punishment or rehabilitation of offenders

Examples

The government announced plans to improve correctional facilities across the state.
She works as a psychologist in the correctional system.
Correctional programs are designed to help rehabilitate offenders.

Synset Examples

a correctional institution

Related Terms

  • Synonym: penal - relating to punishment for crimes

Etymology

Formed from 'correction' + '-al', a suffix meaning 'of or relating to'.
