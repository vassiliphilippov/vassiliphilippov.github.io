sente

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛnteɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɛnteɪ / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Lesotho, equal to one hundredth of a loti.
Lesotho currency unit, 1/100 of a loti

Examples

When traveling in Lesotho, you may receive change in sente coins.
The price of a loaf of bread in Maseru is fifty sente.
She exchanged her rands for loti and collected several sente in the process.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: loti - main unit of currency in Lesotho
