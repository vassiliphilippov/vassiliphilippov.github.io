paraphernalia

1. NOUN

/ ˌpærəfərˈneɪliə / (AmE), / ˌpærəfəˈneɪliə / (BrE)
various objects or equipment used for a particular activity or purpose.
miscellaneous equipment for an activity

Examples

The photographer carefully packed all his paraphernalia before heading to the shoot.
Camping trips always require a surprising amount of paraphernalia.
Police found drug-related paraphernalia at the scene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equipment - the necessary items for a particular purpose
  • Synonym: gear - equipment needed for a specific activity

Etymology

From Medieval Latin paraphernalia bona (property apart from a dowry), from Greek parapherna (property beyond a dowry).
