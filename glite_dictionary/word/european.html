European

1. ADJ

/ jʊˈroʊpiən / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən / (BrE)
of or relating to Europe or its people, languages, or cultures.
relating to Europe or its people

Examples

The European Parliament met to discuss environmental policy.
Italian architecture is a fine example of European design.
They specialize in importing European wines and cheeses.

Synset Examples

European Community

Related Terms

  • Synonym: European - a person from Europe

Etymology

From Latin Europa (Europe) + English suffix -an.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02979870-a
Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ jʊˌroʊˈpiən / (AmE), / jʊəˌrəʊˈpiːən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Europe.
person from Europe

Examples

As a European, she enjoys traveling between countries without needing a visa.
The conference attracted Europeans from all over the continent.
Many Europeans speak more than one language fluently.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Asian - a native or inhabitant of Asia
  • Antonym: African - a native or inhabitant of Africa

Etymology

From Latin 'Europaeus' (of Europe), from 'Europa' (Europe).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09705914-n
Synset POS: n
