zoo

1. NOUN

/ zuː / (AmE), / zuː / (BrE)
An establishment where live wild animals are kept for public viewing, education, and conservation.
place where wild animals are exhibited

Examples

We took the children to the zoo to see the lions and elephants.
The local zoo runs several conservation programs for endangered species.
She volunteers at the zoo every weekend, helping to care for the animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zoological garden - A garden or park where live animals are kept for exhibition.

Etymology

Shortened from 'zoological garden', first used in London in the 19th century.
