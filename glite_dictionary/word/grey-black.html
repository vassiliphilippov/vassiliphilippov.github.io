grey-black

1. ADJ

/ ɡreɪ ˈblæk / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈblæk / (BrE)
Of a color that is black with a tinge of grey.
black tinged with grey

Examples

The cat had grey-black fur that shimmered in the sunlight.
She chose a grey-black dress for the evening gala.
The artist mixed paints to create a perfect grey-black shade for the background.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charcoal - a dark grey color resembling burnt wood
  • Degree Higher: jet-black - an intense pure black color
  • Degree Lower: grey - a color intermediate between black and white
