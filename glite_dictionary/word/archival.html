archival

1. ADJ

/ ɑːrˈkaɪvəl / (AmE), / ɑːˈkaɪvəl / (BrE)
Relating to or suitable for archives; concerning the collection, preservation, or storage of historical records.
relating to archives or historical records

Examples

The museum uses archival materials to restore ancient manuscripts.
Our library offers archival storage for rare photographs and documents.
She conducted archival research to gather primary sources for her thesis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: archive - a collection of historical documents or records

Etymology

From archive + -al, following the pattern of forming adjectives from nouns.
