blob

1. NOUN

/ blɑb / (AmE), / blɒb / (BrE)
a shapeless, indistinct mass or lump, often of a soft or thick substance
a shapeless mass or lump

Examples

A blob of paint landed on the floor, staining the wood.
We watched the blob of jelly slowly spread across the plate.
From far away, the island looked like a dark blob on the horizon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lump - a compact mass of a substance
  • Synonym: spot - a small round mark or stain
  • Antonym: shape - a clearly defined form
