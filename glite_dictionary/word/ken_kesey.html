Ken Kesey

1. PROPN

/ kɛn ˈkisi / (AmE), / kɛn ˈkiːzi / (BrE)
(1935–2001) American novelist best known for his book 'One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest', inspired by his experiences working in a mental hospital.
American novelist, author of 'One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest'

Examples

Many consider Ken Kesey to be one of the most influential authors of the 20th century.
In college, she wrote her thesis on the works of Ken Kesey and the Beat Generation.
Ken Kesey's experiences working in a psychiatric hospital inspired his famous novel.
