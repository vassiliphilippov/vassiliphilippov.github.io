Miwok

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiː.wɑːk / (AmE), / ˈmiː.wɒk / (BrE)
A member of a Native American people traditionally living in the central Sierra Nevada region of California.
a Native American people of central California

Examples

The Miwok built their villages along the rivers in what is now California.
She is studying the culture and traditions of the Miwok.
Many Miwok descendants still live in the region today.

Etymology

From Central Sierra Miwok *miw·yk*, meaning 'people'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈmiːˌwɒk / (AmE), / ˈmiːwɒk / (BrE)
a Penutian language spoken traditionally by the Miwok people of central California
a language of the Miwok people

Examples

The Miwok language has several distinct dialects spoken in different regions of California.
Many elders are working to preserve the Miwok language for future generations.
She is studying Miwok as part of her research on Native American languages.

Etymology

From Miwok, meaning 'people' in their own language.
