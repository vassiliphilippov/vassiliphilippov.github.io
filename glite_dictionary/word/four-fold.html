four-fold

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌfəʊld / (BrE)
Consisting of four parts, units, or elements; four times as great or as many.
consisting of four parts or times

Examples

The experiment resulted in a four-fold increase in production efficiency.
Each box was divided into a four-fold compartment for organization.
The four-fold solution addressed all aspects of the problem.

Synset Examples

quadruple rhythm has four beats per measure
quadruplex wire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quadruple - Consisting of four parts or elements; four times as much.

Etymology

Formed from 'four' + 'fold', following Old English model of multiplicative adjectives.
