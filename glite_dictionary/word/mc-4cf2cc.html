Mc

1. NOUN

/ ɛm si / (AmE), / ɛm si / (BrE)
An abbreviation for megacycle, a unit equal to one million cycles per second, previously used to measure frequency.
abbreviation for megacycle (one million cycles/second)

Examples

The radio operates at a frequency of 100 Mc.
Before the introduction of hertz, frequencies were often measured in Mc.
Early electronics textbooks frequently reference Mc as a unit of frequency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: MHz - abbreviation for megahertz, one million hertz

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'megacycle', from Greek 'mega-' (great) and 'cycle' (circle, complete rotation).
