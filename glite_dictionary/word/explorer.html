Explorer

1. PROPN

/ ɪkˈsplɔɚɚ / (AmE), / ɪkˈsplɔːrə / (BrE)
a web browser developed by Microsoft, officially known as Internet Explorer, widely used on Windows computers from 1995 to 2022
Microsoft web browser 1995–2022

Examples

Many people switched from Explorer to other browsers when support was discontinued.
I used Explorer to access websites before moving to Google Chrome.
Some old business applications only work properly in Explorer due to compatibility settings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: browser - a software program for accessing information on the Internet
