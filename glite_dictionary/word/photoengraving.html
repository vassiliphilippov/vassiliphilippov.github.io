photoengraving

1. NOUN

/ ˌfoʊtoʊɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
An engraving plate produced photographically and used to reproduce an illustration, especially in printing.
engraving plate made by photographic process

Examples

The book's illustrations were reproduced using photoengraving for precise detail.
Advancements in photoengraving revolutionized the printing industry in the 19th century.
He studied the process of photoengraving to create plates for high-quality prints.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: engraving - a print made from an engraved plate, block, or other surface

Etymology

Formed from 'photo-' (photograph) and 'engraving.'
