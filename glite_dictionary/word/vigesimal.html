vigesimal

1. ADJ

/ vaɪˈdʒɛsɪməl / (AmE), / vɪˈdʒɛsɪməl / (BrE)
relating to or based on the number twenty, especially as a counting system
based on the number twenty

Examples

The ancient Maya used a vigesimal numbering system in their mathematics.
Some languages, such as French, show traces of the vigesimal system in their counting words.
In a vigesimal scale, each position represents a power of twenty.

Etymology

From Latin vigesimus (twentieth), from viginti (twenty).
