tollman

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊlmən / (AmE), / ˈtəʊlmən / (BrE)
a man whose job is to collect tolls at a tollgate or similar place
man who collects tolls

Examples

The tollman waved as each car passed through the gate.
After midnight, the lonely tollman kept careful records of every vehicle.
The travelers stopped at the bridge to pay the tollman before continuing their journey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toll collector - person employed to collect tolls

Etymology

From 'toll' (a fee for passage) + 'man'.
