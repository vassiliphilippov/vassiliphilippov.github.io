unrealized

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈriːəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪəlaɪzd / (BrE)
(of a person or their qualities, ambitions, etc.) Not having achieved or developed their full potential.
not having achieved full potential

Examples

Many artists remain unrealized because they never share their work with the world.
She left the company with a sense of unrealized potential and unfinished projects.
His talents went unrealized due to a lack of opportunity and support.

Synset Examples

unfulfilled and uneasy men
unrealized dreams and ambitions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unfulfilled - not achieved or satisfied; lacking fulfillment

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' and 'realized', meaning not made real or not brought to fulfillment.
