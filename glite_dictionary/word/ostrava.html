Ostrava

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈstrɑːvə / (AmE), / ɒˈstrɑːvə / (BrE)
an industrial city in northeastern Czech Republic, located in the Moravian-Silesian region, known for its coal mining and steel industry
industrial city in northeastern Czech Republic

Examples

The international conference was held in Ostrava last year.
Many musicians from Ostrava have gained recognition in Europe.
The industrial heritage of Ostrava is visible throughout the city.
