armless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːrmləs / (AmE), / ˈɑːmlɪs / (BrE)
Having no arms.
without arms

Examples

The museum displays an armless statue from ancient Greece.
After the accident, he designed tools for people who are armless.
The shirt was tailored for an armless dress form.
