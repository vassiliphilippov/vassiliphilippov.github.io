rho

1. NOUN

/ roʊ / (AmE), / rəʊ / (BrE)
the seventeenth letter of the Greek alphabet (ρ, Ρ), often used as a symbol in mathematics and science
Greek letter; 17th of Greek alphabet

Examples

The symbol rho is frequently used to denote density in physics equations.
In the Greek alphabet, rho comes after pi and before sigma.
Mathematicians often use rho to represent variables or constants in formulas.

Etymology

From Greek ῥῶ (rhō), the name of the letter.
