severely

1. ADV

/ səˈvɪr.li / (AmE), / sɪˈvɪə.li / (BrE)
to a very great, serious, or extreme degree; in a way that is very severe or intense.
to a very serious or extreme degree

Examples

His eyesight was severely impaired after the accident.
The storm severely damaged several homes in the area.
Children who misbehaved were severely punished in the past.

Synset Examples

fingers so badly frozen they had to be amputated
badly injured
a severely impaired heart
is gravely ill
was seriously ill

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seriously - to a serious degree, not lightly or jokingly
  • Antonym: mildly - to a slight or minor degree

Etymology

From Latin 'severus' (serious, strict) via Old French 'severement'.

2. ADV

/ səˈvɪrli / (AmE), / sɪˈvɪəli / (BrE)
To a very great degree or in a way that causes serious harm, damage, or hardship.
to a great or harmful degree

Examples

The drought severely affected their food supply, leaving many families without enough to eat.
The company was severely damaged by the economic crisis and had to lay off workers.
He was severely criticized for his role in the incident.

Synset Examples

industries hit hard by the depression
she was severely affected by the bank's failure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harshly - in a way that is unkind or severe

Etymology

From Latin severus (serious, strict) via French sévère + English -ly.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00092328-r
Synset POS: r

3. ADV

/ səˈvɪrli / (AmE), / sɪˈvɪəli / (BrE)
In a severe, strict, or stern manner.
in a strict or stern way

Examples

The teacher spoke severely to the student after he broke the rules.
She looked severely at her son when he lied to her.
His actions were severely criticized by the press.

Synset Examples

‘No,’ she said sternly
peered severely over her glasses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sternly - in a strict or severe manner
  • Antonym: leniently - in a tolerant or permissive manner

Etymology

From severe + -ly, ultimately from Latin severus (serious, strict).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00242552-r
Synset POS: r
