unwedded

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈwɛdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛdɪd / (BrE)
(of a person) not married; never having entered into marriage.
not married; never married

Examples

She raised her child as an unwedded mother in a small village.
The novelist often explores the lives of unwedded women in early 20th-century society.
Despite being unwedded, he was regarded with respect by the local community.

Synset Examples

unwed mother

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unmarried - not married
  • Antonym: wedded - having entered into marriage
  • Synonym: single - not married or in a romantic relationship

Etymology

From un- (not) + wedded, past participle of wed, from Old English 'weddian' (to pledge, marry).
