to hygienize

1. VERB

/ ˈhaɪdʒiˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdʒiːnaɪz / (BrE)
to make something sanitary by cleaning or sterilizing it
make sanitary by cleaning or sterilizing

Examples

All equipment must be hygienized before it is used in the laboratory.
The staff worked late to hygienize the kitchen after the health inspection.
It is important to hygienize your hands frequently to prevent the spread of germs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sanitize - make clean and free from bacteria

Etymology

From hygiene + -ize, patterned after sanitize.
