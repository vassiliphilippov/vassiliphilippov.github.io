Antirrhinum majus

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.tiˈraɪ.nəm ˈmeɪ.dʒəs / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪˈraɪ.nəm ˈmæ.dʒəs / (BrE)
a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean, widely grown for its colorful purple or pink flowers; commonly known as the snapdragon
Mediterranean flowering plant; snapdragon

Examples

The garden was filled with vibrant Antirrhinum majus blooming along the walkway.
She chose Antirrhinum majus for her flower arrangements because of their striking colors.
Botanists often study Antirrhinum majus to better understand flower development.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snapdragon - common name for Antirrhinum majus, a popular garden flower

Etymology

From Greek anti- (like) + rhis (nose), referring to the flower shape; Latinized as Antirrhinum.
