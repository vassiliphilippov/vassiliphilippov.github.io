Argentine

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːrdʒənˌtaɪn / (AmE), / ˌɑːdʒənˈtiːn / (BrE)
relating to Argentina or its people.
of or relating to Argentina

Examples

The Argentine team played exceptionally well in the finals.
He loves listening to Argentine folk music whenever he visits South America.
Many tourists are drawn to the rich flavors of Argentine cuisine.

Synset Examples

Argentinian tango

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Argentinian - relating to Argentina or its people

Etymology

From French *argentin*, from Latin *argentum* (silver), referencing the country name Argentina.
