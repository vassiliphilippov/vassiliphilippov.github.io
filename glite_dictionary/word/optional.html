optional

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑpʃənl̩ / (AmE), / ˈɒpʃənəl / (BrE)
Not required; left to one's choice or discretion.
not required; left to choice

Examples

Participation in the workshop is optional, not mandatory for all employees.
Most of the advanced features on the app are optional and can be enabled as needed.
A jacket is optional at the dinner, but many guests choose to wear one.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: compulsory - required by law or rule; obligatory
  • Antonym: mandatory - required; not optional
  • Synonym: voluntary - done by choice, without obligation

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'optionalis', from Latin 'optio' (free choice).
