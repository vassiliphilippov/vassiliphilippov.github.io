undecorated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɪˈkɔːrətɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈkɒrətɪd / (BrE)
not having adornments, ornaments, or embellishments added; plain or unadorned.
not decorated; plain

Examples

The walls of the apartment were completely undecorated when they moved in.
She preferred an undecorated cake, believing simplicity was beautiful.
His office was surprisingly undecorated for someone in such a high position.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: decorated - having adornments or embellishments added
  • Synonym: plain - simple or lacking adornment
  • Synonym: unadorned - not adorned or decorated

Etymology

from un- (not) + decorated (from Latin decorare, to adorn)
