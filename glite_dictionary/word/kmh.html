km/h

1. NOUN

/ kiː ɛm pɚ aʊɚ / (AmE), / keɪ ɛm pə aʊə / (BrE)
kilometres per hour; a unit of speed expressing the number of kilometres traveled in one hour.
unit of speed (kilometres per hour)

Examples

The car was traveling at 90 km/h when it passed the speed camera.
Cyclists can reach speeds of up to 45 km/h in a sprint.
The speed limit on the highway is 120 km/h in many countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mph - miles per hour, a similar unit of speed in miles
  • Synonym: m/s - metres per second, SI unit of speed

Etymology

From 'kilometre' (SI unit of length) and 'hour' (unit of time); abbreviation formed from 'kilometres per hour'.
