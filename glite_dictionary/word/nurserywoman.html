nurserywoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɜrsəriˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈnɜːsəriˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who owns, manages, or works in a plant nursery, especially one involved in cultivating and selling plants.
woman working in a plant nursery

Examples

The experienced nurserywoman showed me how to pot the young seedlings correctly.
As a nurserywoman, she spent her days tending to thousands of plants.
The local nurserywoman offers valuable advice to amateur gardeners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nurseryman - a man who works in or owns a plant nursery

Etymology

From 'nursery' (a place where plants are cultivated) + 'woman'. Modeled after 'nurseryman'.
