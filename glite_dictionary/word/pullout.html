pullout

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊlˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈpʊl.aʊt / (BrE)
the act of withdrawing or disengaging from a military action, operation, or place
military withdrawal or disengagement

Examples

The government announced a complete pullout of troops from the conflict zone.
After a hasty pullout, tensions in the region quickly escalated.
Many soldiers felt conflicted about the abrupt pullout from the operation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: withdrawal - the act of removing forces from an area
  • Antonym: advance - the act of moving forward in a military action
