congressional

1. ADJ

/ kənˈɡrɛʃənəl / (AmE), / kənˈɡrɛʃənəɫ / (BrE)
relating to a congress, especially the United States Congress.
relating to a congress

Examples

The committee held a congressional hearing on the proposed legislation.
She is an expert in congressional procedures and rules.
Major congressional elections are held every two years in the United States.

Synset Examples

congressional hearing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parliamentary - relating to a parliament

Etymology

From congress + -ional, deriving from Latin 'congressus' (a meeting).
