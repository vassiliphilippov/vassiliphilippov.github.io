paederastic

1. ADJ

/ ˌpiːdəˈræstɪk / (AmE), / ˌpiːdəˈræstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or involving a sexual relationship between a man and a boy.
relating to male adult-boy sexual relationships

Examples

The author discusses the paederastic practices common in certain ancient societies.
Some historical texts contain references to paederastic relationships in Greek culture.
The museum's exhibition sparked debate due to its depiction of paederastic art from antiquity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pederasty - sexual relationship between a man and a boy

Etymology

From Greek *paiderastēs* (lover of boys), from *pais* (boy) + *erastēs* (lover).
