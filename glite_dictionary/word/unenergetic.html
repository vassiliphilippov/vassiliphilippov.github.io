unenergetic

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌɛnəˈdʒɛtɪk / (BrE)
lacking energy or enthusiasm; not inclined to be active or enterprising
lacking energy or enthusiasm

Examples

After a long week, Maria felt unenergetic and spent most of Saturday resting.
The team's unenergetic response showed little enthusiasm for the new project.
His unenergetic manner made it difficult for him to motivate others.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: energetic - having or showing great energy or enthusiasm

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + energetic.
