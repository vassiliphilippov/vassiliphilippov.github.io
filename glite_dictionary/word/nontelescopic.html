nontelescopic

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˌtɛləˈskɑpɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌtɛlɪˈskɒpɪk / (BrE)
Not capable of being extended or contracted like a telescope; not telescopic.
not capable of being extended or contracted

Examples

The engineer designed a nontelescopic arm to maintain structural rigidity.
Unlike many camera lenses, this model uses a nontelescopic design to prevent mechanical failure.
The nontelescopic pole was preferred for its simplicity and strength.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: telescopic - capable of being extended or contracted like a telescope

Etymology

Formed from the prefix non- (not) and telescopic.
