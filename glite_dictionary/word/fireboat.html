fireboat

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪrˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌbəʊt / (BrE)
A boat specially equipped for fighting fires on ships or along waterfronts.
boat equipped for firefighting

Examples

The city's fireboat arrived quickly to extinguish the blaze on the docked freighter.
A new fireboat was commissioned to protect the busy harbor area.
During the waterfront festival, the fireboat demonstrated its powerful water cannons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fire engine - a land vehicle equipped for firefighting
