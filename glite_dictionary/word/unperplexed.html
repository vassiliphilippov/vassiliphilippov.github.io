unperplexed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnpərˈplɛkst / (AmE), / ˌʌnpəˈplɛkst / (BrE)
Not feeling confused or bewildered; experiencing no difficulty in understanding.
not confused or bewildered

Examples

He remained unperplexed despite the complicated instructions given to him.
Her unperplexed demeanor reassured the rest of the team during the crisis.
The mathematician answered every question in an unperplexed and confident manner.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: perplexed - confused or bewildered
  • Synonym: unconfused - not confused, clear in mind

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (not) to 'perplexed.'
