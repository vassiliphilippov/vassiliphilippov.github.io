stylostixis

1. NOUN

/ staɪloʊˈstɪksɪs / (AmE), / staɪləʊˈstɪksɪs / (BrE)
A method of treating pain or disease by inserting needles at specific points on the skin; acupuncture.
insertion of needles for medical treatment
medical

Examples

Some patients seek relief from chronic pain through stylostixis performed by a certified practitioner.
The ancient art of stylostixis has been practiced in Eastern medicine for centuries.
Researchers are studying the effectiveness of stylostixis for treating migraines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acupuncture - A treatment involving insertion of needles into the skin at specific points, chiefly used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Etymology

From Greek 'stylos' (pillar, needle) + 'stixis' (pricking).
