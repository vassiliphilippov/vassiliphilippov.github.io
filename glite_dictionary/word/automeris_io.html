Automeris io

1. PROPN

/ ˌɔːtəˈmɛrɪs ˈaɪoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmɛrɪs ˈaɪəʊ / (BrE)
a large North American moth with bright yellow wings and a prominent eyespot on each hind wing; its caterpillars have stinging spines
a North American moth with yellow wings and eyespots

Examples

The brilliant eyespots on the hind wings of the Automeris io are used to startle predators.
Gardeners are wary of the caterpillars of Automeris io because of their stinging spines.
The Automeris io moth is often found in woodlands across eastern North America.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin; genus name 'Automeris' and species name 'io', named after the mythological figure Io.
