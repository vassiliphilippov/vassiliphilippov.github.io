homefolk

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊmˌfoʊk / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌfəʊk / (BrE)
the people from one's home area, especially one's own family
people from one's home region or family

Examples

Whenever he felt homesick, he would write long letters to his homefolk.
Her homefolk welcomed her back with a festive dinner after years abroad.
Many artists find inspiration in memories of their homefolk and childhood.

Synset Examples

he wrote his homefolk every day

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kin - one's family and relations
  • Synonym: kinsfolk - members of one's family
  • Antonym: stranger - a person whom one does not know
