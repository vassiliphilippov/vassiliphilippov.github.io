loggerheaded

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɑɡərˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɒɡəhɛdɪd / (BrE)
(informal) Stupid; lacking intelligence or common sense.
stupid or foolish
informal

Examples

Calling such a loggerheaded plan 'brilliant' was the height of irony.
She found the proposal so loggerheaded that she refused to participate.
Only a truly loggerheaded person would ignore warnings like that.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment
  • Synonym: stupid - showing a lack of intelligence

Etymology

Formed from 'loggerhead' (blockhead, fool) + '-ed.'
