extraordinaire

1. ADJ

/ ɛkˌstrɔrdəˈnɛr / (AmE), / ɛkˌstrɔːdɪˈneə / (BrE)
Outstanding or remarkable in a particular capacity or field; exceptional.
exceptional; outstanding (esp. in a certain role)

Examples

She is a chef extraordinaire who creates unforgettable dining experiences.
They call him a fundraiser extraordinaire because of his remarkable skills.
Our guide was a storyteller extraordinaire, captivating us throughout the trip.

Synset Examples

a woodworker extraordinaire
a self-starter extraordinaire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exceptional - unusually good; outstanding
  • Antonym: ordinary - normal; not remarkable

Etymology

From French 'extraordinaire' meaning 'extraordinary'.
