Malto

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæltoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmaltəʊ / (BrE)
a member of a Dravidian ethnic group living in northern Bengal in eastern India
a Dravidian ethnic group of northern Bengal, India

Examples

The Malto have a rich tradition of folk music and dance.
Researchers have conducted linguistic studies among the Malto community.
Many Malto still practice their traditional forms of agriculture.

2. NOUN

/ ˈmɑltoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmæl.təʊ / (BrE)
the Dravidian language spoken by the Malto people of eastern India.
Dravidian language of the Malto people

Examples

Linguists have studied Malto to understand its unique structure among Dravidian languages.
Many children in the region learn both Hindi and Malto at home.
The government is working to preserve Malto as it faces the risk of becoming endangered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dravidian - a family of languages spoken mainly in southern India and Sri Lanka

Etymology

From the name of the Malto people of eastern India.
