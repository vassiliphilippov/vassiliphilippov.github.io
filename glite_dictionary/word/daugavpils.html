Daugavpils

1. PROPN

/ ˈdaʊɡaˌpɪls / (AmE), / ˈdaʊɡəˌpɪls / (BrE)
A city in southeastern Latvia, situated on the Daugava River, known as an important regional center.
city in southeastern Latvia

Examples

She visited Daugavpils during her summer trip to explore Latvia's cultural heritage.
The university in Daugavpils attracts students from across the Baltic region.
Daugavpils is famous for its diverse architecture and rich history.

Etymology

From Latvian Daugavpils, meaning 'castle on the Daugava (river)'.
