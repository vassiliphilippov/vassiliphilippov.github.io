Xbone

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛks.boʊn / (AmE), / ˈɛks.bəʊn / (BrE)
A slang term for the Xbox One video game console developed by Microsoft.
slang for Xbox One console
slang

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Xbox One - a video game console developed by Microsoft

Etymology

A slang blending of 'Xbox' and 'One' as a humorous or informal shortening.
