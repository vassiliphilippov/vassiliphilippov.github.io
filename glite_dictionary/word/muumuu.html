muumuu

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːˌmuː / (AmE), / ˈmuːˌmuː / (BrE)
a woman's loose, brightly colored, unbelted dress of Hawaiian origin, typically worn for comfort or informal occasions
a loose, brightly colored dress

Examples

On hot summer days, she prefers to wear a comfortable muumuu around the house.
The shop features traditional Hawaiian shirts and colorful muumuus for tourists.
She bought a new muumuu before her vacation to Hawaii.

Etymology

From Hawaiian 'muʻumuʻu' meaning 'cut off', referring to the yoke-less dress style.
