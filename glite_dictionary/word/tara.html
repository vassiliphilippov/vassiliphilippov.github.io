Tara

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɑːrə / (AmE), / ˈtɑːrə / (BrE)
a historic hill and site in eastern Ireland, northwest of Dublin, formerly the seat of Irish kings until the 6th century
historic site in eastern Ireland

Examples

Ancient Irish rulers were crowned at Tara over a thousand years ago.
The Hill of Tara is a famous archaeological and tourist site in County Meath.
Legends say the kings of Ireland gathered at Tara for important ceremonies.
