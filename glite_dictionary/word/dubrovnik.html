Dubrovnik

1. PROPN

/ duˈbroʊvnɪk / (AmE), / djuːˈbrɒvnɪk / (BrE)
A city and port in southwestern Croatia on the Adriatic Sea, known as a popular tourist destination for its historic old town and scenic location.
city and port in Croatia

Examples

I visited Dubrovnik last summer and was amazed by its ancient city walls.
Dubrovnik attracts thousands of tourists every year for its breathtaking coastal views.
The television series filmed several episodes on location in Dubrovnik.

Etymology

From Croatian 'Dubrovnik', originally from 'dubrava' meaning oak grove.
