Labanotation

1. NOUN

/ ˌleɪbənoʊˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌlɑːbənəʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a system for recording and analyzing dance movements using symbols to represent positions, directions, tempo, and dynamics
symbolic system for dance notation

Examples

She studied Labanotation to help preserve traditional folk dances.
The choreography was carefully transcribed into Labanotation for future performers.
Using Labanotation, instructors can accurately teach complex movement patterns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dance notation - notation system for dances

Etymology

Named after Rudolf Laban, Hungarian dance theorist and choreographer, combined with 'notation'.
