nonproliferation

1. NOUN

/ nɑn.prə.lɪfəˈreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / nɒn.prə.lɪfəˈreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the prevention of the spread or increase of something, especially the prevention of more countries acquiring nuclear weapons
prevention of spreading, especially of nuclear weapons

Examples

International agreements on nonproliferation aim to stop the spread of nuclear weapons worldwide.
The policy of nonproliferation is essential for maintaining global security and stability.
Many nations have signed treaties promoting nonproliferation and disarmament.

Synset Examples

they protested that the nonproliferation treaty was just a plot to maintain the hegemony of those who already had nuclear weapons
nuclear disarmament and nonproliferation are closely related goals

Related Terms

  • Antonym: proliferation - rapid increase or spread, especially of nuclear weapons

Etymology

From non- (not) + proliferation (from Latin proliferare, to reproduce)
