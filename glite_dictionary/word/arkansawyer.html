Arkansawyer

1. NOUN

/ ɑːr.kənˌsɔː.jər / (AmE), / ɑː.kənˌsɔː.jə / (BrE)
a native or resident of Arkansas, a state in the southern United States.
native or resident of Arkansas

Examples

He proudly introduced himself as an Arkansawyer during the reunion.
As an Arkansawyer, she was familiar with the state's rich musical traditions.
The novelist, an Arkansawyer by birth, often wrote about life in the Ozarks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Arkansan - a native or resident of Arkansas

Etymology

Formed from Arkansas (the state) + -yer, modeled after forms like 'New Yorker'.
