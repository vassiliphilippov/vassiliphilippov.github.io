taping

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈteɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
a recording made on magnetic tape, especially of audio or video material
recording made on magnetic tape

Examples

The band released a limited edition taping of their rehearsal sessions.
He listened to the taping of last night's broadcast to check the audio quality.
Old tapings of family gatherings were stored in boxes in the attic.

Synset Examples

the several recordings were combined on a master tape

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recording - a process or result of capturing sound or images onto a medium

Etymology

From 'tape' + '-ing', referring to the process of recording onto magnetic tape.
