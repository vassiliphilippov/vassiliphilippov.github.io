Irishwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪrɪʃˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈaɪərɪʃˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who is a native or inhabitant of Ireland.
woman from Ireland

Examples

She is a proud Irishwoman who cherishes her heritage deeply.
As the first Irishwoman to win the award, she inspired many young women.
The story follows an Irishwoman who immigrated to America in the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Irishman - A man who is a native or inhabitant of Ireland

Etymology

Formed from 'Irish' + 'woman'.
