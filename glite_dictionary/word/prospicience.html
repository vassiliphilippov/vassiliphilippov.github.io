prospicience

1. NOUN

/ prɑːˈspɪʃiəns / (AmE), / prɒˈspɪsɪəns / (BrE)
the ability to foresee or anticipate future events; foresight.
foresight; ability to foresee

Examples

Her remarkable prospicience allowed the company to prepare for market changes before they occurred.
A leader with prospicience can guide a nation through uncertain times.
Historians admired his prospicience in predicting the social upheavals of the next decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foresight - the ability to predict what will happen or be needed in the future
  • Synonym: prescience - knowing something before it takes place; foreknowledge

Etymology

From Latin prospiciens (looking forward), from prospicere (to look forward).
