nanocephalic

1. ADJ

/ ˌnænoʊsɪˈfælɪk / (AmE), / ˌnanəʊsɪˈfalɪk / (BrE)
having an abnormally small head and underdeveloped brain, typically as a congenital condition
having an abnormally small head and brain
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed as nanocephalic at birth due to his extremely small skull.
Children with nanocephalic features often experience significant developmental challenges.
A nanocephalic condition can sometimes be detected during prenatal scans.

Synset Examples

a nanocephalic dwarf

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: microcephalic - having an abnormally small head; used for a related but less extreme condition

Etymology

From Greek 'nano-' (dwarf, very small) and 'kephalē' (head).
