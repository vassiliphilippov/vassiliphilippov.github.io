gunfire

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌnˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnˌfaɪə / (BrE)
the act of shooting guns, or the sound produced by guns being fired
the shooting or sound of guns

Examples

The sound of gunfire echoed through the deserted streets.
Heavy gunfire forced the soldiers to take cover behind the wall.
After a brief pause, gunfire resumed from both sides of the conflict.

Synset Examples

the gunfire endangered innocent bystanders
they retreated in the face of withering enemy fire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fire - shots fired from guns; the act of discharging firearms

Etymology

Formed from 'gun' + 'fire', denoting the firing (discharge) of guns.
