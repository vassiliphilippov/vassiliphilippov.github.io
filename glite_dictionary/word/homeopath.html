homeopath

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊ.mi.əˌpæθ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊ.mi.əˌpæθ / (BrE)
a person who practices homeopathy, a system of alternative medicine based on the use of highly diluted substances
practitioner of homeopathy

Examples

After seeing a homeopath, she began a new treatment regimen.
The homeopath recommended a remedy tailored to his symptoms.
Many people consult a homeopath for chronic health conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homeopathy - a system of alternative medicine using highly diluted substances

Etymology

Formed from 'homeo-' (similar) + '-path' (suffering/disease) on the model of 'homeopathy'.
