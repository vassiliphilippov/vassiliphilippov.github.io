savouring

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪvərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈseɪvərɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of tasting or enjoying a small amount of something to appreciate its flavor or quality.
the act of tasting or appreciating

Examples

The chef took pleasure in the careful savouring of each ingredient in his creation.
Her savouring of the chocolate was so intense that she closed her eyes to focus on the taste.
He lingered over dinner, prolonging the savouring of each delicious bite.

Synset Examples

cooking was fine but it was the savoring that he enjoyed most

Related Terms

  • Synonym: savoring - US spelling: the act of tasting or enjoying something

Etymology

From the verb 'savour', from Old French *savourer*, from Latin *sapōrāre* (to taste); -ing noun form.
