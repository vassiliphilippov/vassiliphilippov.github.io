nicely

1. ADV

/ ˈnaɪsli / (AmE), / ˈnaɪsli / (BrE)
In a pleasant, attractive, or satisfactory manner.
in a pleasant or satisfactory way

Examples

The chef arranged the vegetables nicely on the plate.
She spoke very nicely to the new student.
The room was nicely decorated for the party.

Synset Examples

a nicely painted house

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pleasantly - in a pleasant manner
  • Antonym: badly - in an unsatisfactory or unpleasant manner

Etymology

From 'nice' + '-ly', forming the adverb.
