tough-skinned

1. ADJ

/ tʌf skɪnd / (AmE), / tʌf skɪnd / (BrE)
not easily upset or affected by criticism or negative comments; emotionally resilient or insensitive to criticism
not sensitive to criticism

Examples

She has always been tough-skinned, never letting rude remarks bother her.
As a politician, you need to be tough-skinned to deal with constant public scrutiny.
The manager’s tough-skinned attitude made it easier for him to handle negative feedback.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thick-skinned - not easily hurt by criticism
  • Antonym: sensitive - quick to feel or be hurt by criticism
