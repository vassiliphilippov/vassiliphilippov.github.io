Dasht-e Lut

1. PROPN

/ dɑːʃt eɪ lʊt / (AmE), / dɑːʃt eɪ lʊt / (BrE)
a large desert in eastern Iran, known for its extreme heat and unique geological features
desert in eastern Iran

Examples

The Dasht-e Lut holds the record for some of the highest surface temperatures on Earth.
Scientists conducted research in the Dasht-e Lut to study its unique salt formations.
Tourists are often amazed by the striking landscapes of Iran's Dasht-e Lut.

Etymology

From Persian 'Dasht-e Lūt', meaning 'Lut Desert'.
