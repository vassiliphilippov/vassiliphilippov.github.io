Lermontov

1. PROPN

/ lɛrˈmɒnˌtɔv / (AmE), / lɛəˈmɒntɒf / (BrE)
(1814–1841) A Russian writer and poet known for his influential works in Romanticism and his contributions to Russian literature.
Russian poet and writer (1814–1841)

Examples

Mikhail Lermontov is often regarded as one of the greatest poets of Russian Romanticism.
The novel 'A Hero of Our Time' was written by Lermontov shortly before his death.
Many scholars have studied Lermontov's influence on later Russian literature.
