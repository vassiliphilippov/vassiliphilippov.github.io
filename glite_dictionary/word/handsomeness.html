handsomeness

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændsəmnəs / (AmE), / ˈhændsəmnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of having attractive and well-defined facial features, especially in a man
attractiveness, esp. in a man's features

Examples

His striking handsomeness made him stand out in the crowd.
Despite his handsomeness, he remained humble and approachable.
The actor was praised for both his talent and his undeniable handsomeness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty - the quality of being physically attractive
  • Antonym: ugliness - the quality of being unattractive

Etymology

From 'handsome' + '-ness'; 'handsome' from Middle English, originally 'easy to handle'.
