services

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝː.vɪs.ɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.vɪs.ɪz / (BrE)
helpful actions, work, or facilities provided for people or organizations, especially as an organized system by an authority or company.
work or facilities provided for others

Examples

The city is working to improve public services like transportation and sanitation.
Many residents rely on emergency services during natural disasters.
Tourists appreciate the wide range of services offered by the hotel.

Synset Examples

the mayor tried to maintain city services
the medical services are excellent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: service - an act of helping or doing work for someone

Etymology

From Old French 'servise', from Latin 'servitium' (slavery, servitude).
