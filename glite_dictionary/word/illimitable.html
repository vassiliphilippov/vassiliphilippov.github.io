illimitable

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈlɪmətəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪmɪtəbl̩ / (BrE)
without limits; impossible to measure or calculate in extent or amount
without limits; limitless

Examples

She gazed at the illimitable expanse of the night sky, filled with countless stars.
The poet described love as an illimitable force that knows no boundaries.
Scientists once believed the ocean's resources to be illimitable, but we now understand their true limits.

Synset Examples

limitless vastness of our solar system
The long unmeasured pulse of time moves everything. There is nothing hidden that it cannot bring to light, nothing once known that may not become unknown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: limitless - without any limit; infinite
  • Synonym: infinite - without limits or end
  • Antonym: limited - having a set or fixed limit

Etymology

From Latin 'illimitabilis' (not limited), from 'in-' (not) + 'limitare' (to limit).
