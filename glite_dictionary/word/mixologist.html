mixologist

1. NOUN

/ mɪkˈsɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / mɪkˈsɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who mixes and serves alcoholic drinks, especially as a skilled professional at a bar
professional bartender who creates cocktails

Examples

The mixologist crafted a unique cocktail for every customer at the event.
She trained as a mixologist to elevate her skills beyond standard bartending.
Many modern bars now have a resident mixologist specializing in new cocktail recipes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bartender - a person who mixes and serves drinks at a bar

Etymology

From English 'mix' + 'ologist', patterned after words for specialists (biologist, geologist).
