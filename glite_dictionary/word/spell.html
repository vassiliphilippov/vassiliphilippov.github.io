spell

1. VERB

/ spɛl / (AmE), / spɛl / (BrE)
to write or say the letters of a word in the correct order
write or say letters of a word

Examples

Can you spell your last name for me, please?
She spelled every word on the test correctly.
Children are taught how to spell new vocabulary in school.

Synset Examples

He spelled the word wrong in this letter

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to misspell - to spell a word incorrectly

Etymology

From Old French *espeller*, from Frankish *spellon* (to tell, relate), from Proto-Germanic *spellan* (to speak, recite).

Examples
