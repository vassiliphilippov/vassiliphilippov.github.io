sura

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʊrə / (AmE), / ˈsʊərə / (BrE)
the muscular back part of the lower leg, between the knee and the ankle; the calf
muscular back part of lower leg
medical

Examples

The athlete injured his sura during the race and had to withdraw.
The doctor palpated the sura to check for muscle tears.
Cramps in the sura are common after vigorous exercise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calf - the fleshy back part of the leg below the knee

Etymology

From Latin sura (calf of the leg).

2. NOUN

/ ˈsuːrə / (AmE), / ˈsʊərə / (BrE)
one of the chapters or sections into which the Koran is divided
chapter of the Koran

Examples

The first sura of the Koran is recited during daily prayers.
Many verses from a famous sura were quoted in the lecture.
Each sura has a specific theme and historical context.

Synset Examples

the Quran is divided in 114 suras

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chapter - a main division of a book, typically with a number or title

Etymology

From Arabic 'sūra', meaning 'chapter' or 'enclosure'.
