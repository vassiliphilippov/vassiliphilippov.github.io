fuelled

1. ADJ

/ ˈfjuld / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəld / (BrE)
heated, powered, or produced by the burning of fuel.
powered or produced by fuel

Examples

The ship was fuelled by diesel engines.
Most power stations in the region are fuelled by coal.
The debate was fuelled by recent environmental concerns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fueled - American English spelling of 'fuelled'
