single-bedded

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪŋɡəl ˈbɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl ˈbɛdɪd / (BrE)
(of a room or accommodation) having one or more single beds, typically intended for one person per bed.
having single beds (especially of rooms)

Examples

We requested a single-bedded room for our teenage son at the hotel.
The hostel offers both single-bedded and double-bedded options for travelers.
Every single-bedded cabin includes a small desk and private bathroom.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: double-bedded - having a double bed or double beds
