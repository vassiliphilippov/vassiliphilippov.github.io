to vasectomise

1. VERB

/ vəˈsɛk.tə.maɪz / (AmE), / vəˈsɛk.tə.maɪz / (BrE)
to perform a surgical operation to cut and seal the vas deferens, typically as a means of sterilization in men
perform male sterilization surgery
medical british

Examples

The doctor agreed to vasectomise the patient after a thorough consultation.
Several clinics now offer to vasectomise men as part of their family planning services.
He decided to vasectomise his patient upon request, making the procedure quick and safe.

Synset Examples

many men choose to be vasectomized as a form of safe birth control

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to vasectomize - to perform a vasectomy (US spelling)

Etymology

From 'vasectomy' + '-ise'; 'vasectomy' from Latin 'vas' (vessel) + Greek 'ektomē' (excision).
