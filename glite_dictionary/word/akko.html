Akko

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑːkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈækəʊ / (BrE)
A town and port in northwestern Israel on the Mediterranean coast, known for its historical and cultural significance.
port town in northwestern Israel

Examples

The ancient city of Akko attracts tourists with its historic architecture and vibrant markets.
She learned about the rich history of Akko on her trip to Israel.
Many cultures have left their mark on Akko over the centuries.

Etymology

From Hebrew עַכּוֹ (ʻAkko), ancient city name; also known as Acre in European languages.
