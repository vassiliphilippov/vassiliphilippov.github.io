obediently

1. ADV

/ oʊˈbiːdiəntli / (AmE), / əˈbiːdiəntli / (BrE)
in a way that shows willingness to obey or follow instructions or authority
in an obedient manner

Examples

The children sat obediently at their desks, waiting for the teacher's instructions.
She obediently handed over her phone when asked by her father.
The dog followed its owner's command obediently every time.

Synset Examples

obediently she slipped off her right shoe and stocking

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obedient - willing to do what one is told
  • Antonym: defiantly - in a manner that shows resistance or disobedience

Etymology

From 'obedient' + '-ly', ultimately from Latin 'oboedientem' (obedient).
