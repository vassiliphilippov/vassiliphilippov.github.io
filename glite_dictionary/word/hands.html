hands

1. NOUN

/ hændz / (AmE), / hændz / (BrE)
workers available to perform labor, especially in manual or industrial work
workers for manual or industrial labor

Examples

During harvest season, the farm hired extra hands to help gather the crops.
We need more hands on the assembly line to meet the production deadline.
All available hands were called in to clean up after the storm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laborers - people doing manual work
  • Synonym: staff - employees collectively, especially in an organization
