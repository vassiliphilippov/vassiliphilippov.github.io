skunkbush

1. NOUN

/ ˈskʌŋkˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈskʌŋkˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
A deciduous shrub native to California, with unpleasantly scented typically trifoliate leaves and edible fruit.
scented Californian shrub with edible fruit

Examples

The skunkbush is often planted in gardens for its attractive red berries.
Many animals use the skunkbush as a source of food and shelter.
She avoided the skunkbush because of the strong odor of its leaves.
