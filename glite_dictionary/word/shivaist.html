Shivaist

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃiːvaɪɪst / (AmE), / ˈʃɪvəɪɪst / (BrE)
A follower or devotee of the Hindu god Shiva.
worshipper of Shiva (Hindu deity)

Examples

The temple was visited daily by a devoted Shivaist who offered prayers at dawn.
As a practicing Shivaist, he observed festivals dedicated to Lord Shiva with great enthusiasm.
Anthropologists studied the rituals of the local Shivaist community during their fieldwork in India.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Shaivite - another term for a worshipper or follower of Shiva

Etymology

From 'Shiva', the Hindu deity, plus the suffix '-ist' denoting a follower or adherent.
