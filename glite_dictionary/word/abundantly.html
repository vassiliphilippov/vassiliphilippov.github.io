abundantly

1. ADV

/ əˈbʌndəntli / (AmE), / əˈbʌndəntli / (BrE)
In large quantities; in a way that is more than enough or plentiful.
in large or plentiful amounts

Examples

The garden was abundantly filled with colorful flowers in the spring.
She was abundantly clear about her expectations in the email.
This region is abundantly blessed with natural resources.

Synset Examples

they were abundantly supplied with food
he thanked her profusely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plentifully - in a plentiful manner; in great quantity

Etymology

From 'abundant' + '-ly', where 'abundant' is from Latin 'abundans' (overflowing).
