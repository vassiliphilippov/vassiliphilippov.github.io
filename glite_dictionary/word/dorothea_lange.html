Dorothea Lange

1. PROPN

/ ˌdɔːrəˈθiːə læŋ / (AmE), / ˌdɒrəˈθiːə læŋ / (BrE)
(1895–1965) a United States photographer known for her powerful portraits of rural workers during the Great Depression.
U.S. photographer of rural Depression-era workers

Examples

Dorothea Lange documented the struggles of American migrant workers through her evocative photographs.
An exhibition of Dorothea Lange's work drew attention to the realities of the Great Depression.
The name Dorothea Lange is often associated with the iconic image "Migrant Mother."
