off-roader

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔfˈroʊdər / (AmE), / ˌɒfˈrəʊdə / (BrE)
a bicycle with a strong frame and wide tires, designed for use on rough or mountainous terrain rather than paved roads
bicycle for rough terrain

Examples

He bought a new off-roader to tackle the rugged mountain trails.
Many cyclists prefer an off-roader when riding on dirt paths or rocky landscapes.
Her old off-roader handled the forest route much better than a standard road bike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mountain bike - bicycle designed for off-road cycling

2. NOUN

/ ˈɔf ˌroʊdər / (AmE), / ˈɒf ˌrəʊdə / (BrE)
a vehicle that is specially designed for driving on rough or uneven terrain rather than normal roads
vehicle for rough terrain

Examples

The off-roader handled the rocky mountain pass with no trouble at all.
She bought an off-roader for her cross-country expeditions.
Many outdoor enthusiasts choose an off-roader for camping in remote areas.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
