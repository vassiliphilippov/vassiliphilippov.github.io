roughrider

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌfˌraɪdər / (AmE), / ˈrʌfˌraɪdə / (BrE)
A horseman who is skilled at breaking wild horses to the saddle.
skilled horse-breaker or rider

Examples

Many western films feature a fearless roughrider taming wild stallions on the open plains.
Becoming a roughrider takes courage, patience, and a deep understanding of horses.
He earned a reputation as the best roughrider in the entire region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cowboy - a man who herds and tends cattle, often also a skilled rider

Etymology

From 'rough' + 'rider', originally referring to someone who rides unruly or untamed horses.
