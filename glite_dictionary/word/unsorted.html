unsorted

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɔrtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɔːtɪd / (BrE)
not arranged or organized according to a particular order, such as size, type, or sequence.
not arranged in order

Examples

The documents were left unsorted in a messy pile on the desk.
We received a box of unsorted buttons from the factory.
The files remain unsorted, making it difficult to find what we need.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sorted - arranged according to order or category

Etymology

From un- (not) + sort (organize) + -ed (adjective forming suffix).

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɔrtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɔːtɪd / (BrE)
not arranged or organized according to a particular system or order.
not arranged in order

Examples

The documents remained unsorted on the desk for weeks.
After the move, several boxes of clothes were still unsorted in the hallway.
The list appears unsorted, making it difficult to find specific names.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sorted - arranged or organized according to a system or order
