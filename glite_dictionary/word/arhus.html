Arhus

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑr.hʊs / (AmE), / ˈɑː.huːs / (BrE)
a port city in eastern Jutland, Denmark, known for its cultural institutions and harbor
city in eastern Jutland, Denmark

Examples

Many tourists visit Arhus to experience its vibrant arts scene and historic architecture.
The university in Arhus is renowned for its research in science and technology.
Cargo ships regularly arrive at the busy port of Arhus.

Etymology

Danish, possibly from Old Danish *Áros* meaning 'river mouth'.
