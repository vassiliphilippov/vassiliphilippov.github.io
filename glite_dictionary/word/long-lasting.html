long-lasting

1. ADJ

/ lɔŋ ˈlæstɪŋ / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈlɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
Continuing for a long period of time without significant change or deterioration.
continuing for a long time

Examples

Their long-lasting marriage is admired by everyone who knows them.
This battery is known for being exceptionally long-lasting in all conditions.
She values long-lasting friendships over fleeting acquaintances.

Synset Examples

hopes for a durable peace
a long-lasting friendship

Related Terms

  • Synonym: durable - able to last a long time without wearing out
  • Antonym: short-lived - lasting only a short time
