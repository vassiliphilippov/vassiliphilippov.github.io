New Zealand

1. PROPN

/ nuː ˈziːlənd / (AmE), / njuː ˈziːlənd / (BrE)
an island country in the South Pacific Ocean, comprising the North Island, South Island, and numerous smaller islands
country in the South Pacific

Examples

I have always wanted to visit New Zealand for its beautiful landscapes and unique wildlife.
She moved to New Zealand to pursue a new career opportunity in Wellington.
The film was shot on location in New Zealand, showcasing its stunning scenery.

Etymology

From Dutch 'Nieuw Zeeland', named by Dutch explorers after the Dutch province of Zeeland.
