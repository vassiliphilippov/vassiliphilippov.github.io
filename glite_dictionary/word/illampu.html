Illampu

1. PROPN

/ iˈjɑmpu / (AmE), / ɪˈlæmpuː / (BrE)
a mountain peak in the Andes, located in Bolivia, reaching 20,870 feet (6,368 meters) in elevation
mountain peak in Bolivia

Examples

The climbers planned an expedition to Illampu in hopes of reaching its summit.
From the village, you can see Illampu towering above the surrounding peaks.
Many trekkers consider Illampu one of the most challenging mountains in Bolivia.
