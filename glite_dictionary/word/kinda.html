kinda

1. ADV

/ ˈkaɪndə / (AmE), / ˈkaɪndə / (BrE)
(informal) To some extent; somewhat or rather; used as a casual form of 'kind of.'
somewhat; to some extent (informal)
informal

Examples

I'm kinda tired, so I think I'll go to bed early tonight.
The soup tastes kinda salty, but it's still good.
She was kinda upset after missing the meeting.

Synset Examples

it was rather cold
the party was rather nice
the knife is rather dull
I rather regret that I cannot attend
He's rather good at playing the cello
he is kind of shy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kind of - to some extent; somewhat
  • Synonym: rather - to a certain degree; somewhat

Etymology

Informal contraction of 'kind of'.
