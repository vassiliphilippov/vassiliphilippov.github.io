banefully

1. ADV

/ ˈbeɪnfəli / (AmE), / ˈbeɪnfəli / (BrE)
In a way that causes great harm or destruction; in a noxiously harmful manner.
in a noxiously harmful manner

Examples

The villain banefully influenced the course of events in the story.
Toxic substances spread banefully through the river, harming aquatic life.
His jealousy banefully affected their relationship over time.

Synset Examples

this banefully poisoned climate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baneful - causing great harm or destruction

Etymology

From 'baneful' + '-ly'; 'baneful' from Middle English 'bane' (a source of harm or ruin).
