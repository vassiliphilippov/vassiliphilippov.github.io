piste

1. NOUN

/ piːst / (AmE), / piːst / (BrE)
a flat, narrow, rectangular area marked out for fencing bouts
fencing competition area

Examples

The referee stepped onto the piste and signaled for the bout to begin.
Each fencer took their position at opposite ends of the piste.
Her quick movement along the piste impressed the judges during the competition.

Etymology

From French 'piste' meaning 'track' or 'trail'.

2. NOUN

/ pist / (AmE), / piːst / (BrE)
a marked ski run or path on a mountain, especially one with packed snow for skiing down
marked ski run or path

Examples

The red piste is suitable for intermediate skiers seeking a challenge.
After fresh snowfall, the piste was groomed and ready for the day's skiers.
They checked the map to choose the longest piste on the mountain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ski slope - an area of a mountain prepared for skiing

Etymology

From French *piste* (track, trail, path).
