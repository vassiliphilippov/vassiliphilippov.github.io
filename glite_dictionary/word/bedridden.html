bedridden

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛdˌrɪdən / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌrɪd.ən / (BrE)
Confined to bed because of illness or infirmity.
confined to bed by illness

Examples

She was bedridden for months after her accident.
The bedridden patient required constant care from the nurses.
He became bedridden as his illness progressed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedfast - confined to bed because of illness; synonym for bedridden

Etymology

From Middle English 'bedrid', from Old English 'bedreda', meaning 'bedridden'.
