homegrown

1. ADJ

/ ˈhoʊmˌɡroʊn / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌɡrəʊn / (BrE)
grown or produced in one's own region or locality, rather than imported or originating elsewhere
grown or produced locally

Examples

The market only sells homegrown tomatoes during the summer months.
She takes pride in serving homegrown vegetables at her restaurant.
The city has a number of talented homegrown musicians.

Synset Examples

stands selling homegrown fruits and vegetables

Related Terms

  • Synonym: local - relating to or occurring in a particular area
  • Antonym: imported - brought in from another place, especially from a foreign country

Etymology

From 'home' + 'grown', dating to the late 19th century.
