kindliness

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaɪndlɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈkaɪndlɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate toward others
friendly and considerate disposition

Examples

Her kindliness towards strangers made her beloved in the community.
He treated me with great kindliness during my difficult time.
The teacher’s kindliness created a warm and welcoming classroom atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kindness - the quality of being kind; benevolence
  • Antonym: cruelty - the quality of being cruel; lack of compassion

Etymology

From Middle English kindlinesse, from kind (nature, friendly) + -liness (state or quality).
