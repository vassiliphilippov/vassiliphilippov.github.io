authorities

1. NOUN

/ əˈθɔrɪtiz / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɒrɪtiz / (BrE)
official organizations or people with power to make decisions and enforce laws, especially in a political or governmental context
official governing organizations or officials

Examples

Local authorities are investigating the cause of the fire.
The authorities have imposed new regulations to improve public safety.
She reported the theft to the authorities immediately.

Synset Examples

the government reduced taxes
the matter was referred to higher authorities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: government - the group of people with the authority to govern a country or state

Etymology

Plural of 'authority', from Old French 'autorité', from Latin 'auctoritas'.
