Evenki

1. NOUN

/ ˌiːˈvɛnki / (AmE), / ˌiːˈvɛnki / (BrE)
a member of an indigenous people inhabiting regions of northern Mongolia and eastern Siberia
member of Siberian/Mongolian indigenous people

Examples

The Evenki have a rich tradition of reindeer herding and shamanism.
Researchers visited an Evenki community deep in the Siberian taiga.
Many Evenki today are working to preserve their native language and customs.

Etymology

From Russian Эвeнки (Evenki), the name of the people.

2. NOUN

/ iːˈvɛnki / (AmE), / iːˈvɛnki / (BrE)
the Tungusic language spoken by the Evenki people in eastern Siberia
Tungusic language of the Evenki people

Examples

She is conducting research on the Evenki language and its unique grammatical features.
Many words in Evenki have no direct equivalent in Russian or English.
Linguists have published a new Evenki dictionary to help preserve the language.

Etymology

From Russian 'эвенкий' (Evenki), from the self-designation of the Evenki people.
