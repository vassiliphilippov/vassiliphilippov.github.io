ezed

1. NOUN

/ izd / (AmE), / iːzɛd / (BrE)
(Scottish) the name of the letter Z in the Roman alphabet.
Scottish name for letter Z
british

Examples

In some Scottish schools, children are taught to pronounce the letter ezed instead of zed or zee.
His grandfather always insisted on calling it ezed when reciting the alphabet.
The history teacher explained that ezed was once common in Scotland.

Etymology

Variant of "zed" (from Late Latin zēta), influenced by Scottish English spelling.
