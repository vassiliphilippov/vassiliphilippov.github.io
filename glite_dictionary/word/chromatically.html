chromatically

1. ADV

/ kroʊˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / krəʊˈmatɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to color; with respect to color.
in relation to color

Examples

The design is chromatically balanced, combining warm and cool tones effectively.
The artist chose to arrange the tiles chromatically, creating a gradient effect across the wall.
In this experiment, pigments were separated chromatically to analyze their components.

Synset Examples

chromatically pure

Etymology

From Late Latin 'chromaticus' (relating to color) + English adverbial suffix '-ally'.
