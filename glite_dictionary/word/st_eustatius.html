St. Eustatius

1. PROPN

/ ˌseɪnt juːˈsteɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌseɪnt juːˈsteɪʃəs / (BrE)
an island in the Caribbean, part of the Netherlands Antilles, known for its historical significance and natural beauty
island in the Netherlands Antilles

Examples

The cruise ship stopped at St. Eustatius during its tour of the Caribbean islands.
Researchers traveled to St. Eustatius to study its diverse wildlife.
The history of St. Eustatius includes important events from colonial times.

Etymology

Named after Saint Eustace, a Christian saint.
