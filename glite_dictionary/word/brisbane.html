Brisbane

1. PROPN

/ ˈbrɪzˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈbrɪzbən / (BrE)
The capital and largest city of Queensland, Australia, located in the southeastern corner of the state on the Pacific coast.
capital city of Queensland, Australia

Examples

The weather in Brisbane is subtropical, making it a popular destination year-round.
She moved from Melbourne to Brisbane for a new job opportunity.
Many international students choose to study in Brisbane due to its vibrant lifestyle and universities.

Etymology

Named after Sir Thomas Brisbane, Governor of New South Wales (1821–1825).
