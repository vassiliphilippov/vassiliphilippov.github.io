vitaliser

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪtəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈvɪtəˌlaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who imparts energy, vitality, and enthusiasm to others
person who imparts energy to others

Examples

As a team leader, he was a true vitaliser who kept everyone's spirits high during tough times.
The new coach quickly became the vitaliser of the squad, inspiring them to achieve new goals.
Her enthusiasm made her a natural vitaliser at company gatherings and social events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: energizer - a person who gives energy or vitality to others

Etymology

From Latin 'vita' (life) + English '-iser', via French 'vitaliser' (to give life to).
