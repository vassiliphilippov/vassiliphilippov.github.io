rhinolaryngologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌraɪnoʊˌlærɪnˈɡɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌraɪnəʊˌlærɪŋˈɡɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a medical doctor who specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders of the ear, nose, or throat
ear, nose, and throat specialist
medical

Examples

After experiencing chronic sinus issues, she consulted a rhinolaryngologist for specialized treatment.
The rhinolaryngologist performed a thorough examination of my throat and nasal passages.
Many hospitals have a rhinolaryngologist available for complex ear, nose, and throat cases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: otolaryngologist - a medical doctor specializing in ear, nose, and throat disorders

Etymology

From Greek 'rhino-' (nose) + 'laryngo-' (throat) + '-logist' (one who studies).
