hatmaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈhætˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈhætˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes and sells hats
person who makes and sells hats

Examples

The skilled hatmaker crafted a beautiful velvet hat for the spring collection.
She apprenticed under a renowned hatmaker to learn the art of millinery.
At the city fair, a local hatmaker displayed her creative and colorful designs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milliner - a person who makes or sells women's hats

Etymology

From 'hat' + 'maker', denoting someone who makes hats.
