amoristic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæməˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌaməˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by romantic love.
of or relating to romantic love

Examples

Her poetry often explored amoristic themes and the complexities of falling in love.
The film had a distinctly amoristic atmosphere, focusing on youthful passion.
Some critics praised the novel's amoristic undertones for their subtlety and depth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: romantic - characterized by or relating to romance or love

Etymology

From Latin 'amor' (love) plus the English suffix '-istic'.
