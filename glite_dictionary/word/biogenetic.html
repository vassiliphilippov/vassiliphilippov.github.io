biogenetic

1. ADJ

/ ˌbaɪoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪəʊdʒɪˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the production or origination of living organisms from other living organisms.
of living organisms producing other organisms

Examples

The biogenetic processes responsible for cell growth were closely studied by the research team.
Many diseases can be understood better through biogenetic analysis of inherited traits.
The company specializes in biogenetic engineering to develop new strains of crops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biogenic - produced by living organisms

Etymology

From bio- (life) + genetic, relating to genesis or origin.
