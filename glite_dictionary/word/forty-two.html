forty-two

1. NUM

/ ˌfɔr.ti ˈtu / (AmE), / ˌfɔː.ti ˈtuː / (BrE)
the number equal to forty plus two; 42
the number 42

Examples

She celebrated her forty-second birthday with a big party.
The answer to the ultimate question in the novel was forty-two.
There are forty-two students enrolled in the class this semester.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty-one - the number 41
  • Degree Higher: forty-three - the number 43

2. NUM

/ ˌfɔr.ti ˈtu / (AmE), / ˌfɔː.ti ˈtjuː / (BrE)
the number 42; two more than forty
the number 42

Examples

There are forty-two students in the class this year.
The answer to the puzzle was forty-two, surprising everyone.
She bought forty-two balloons for the party.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty-one - the number 41; one less than forty-two
  • Degree Higher: forty-three - the number 43; one more than forty-two

Etymology

Composed of 'forty' and 'two', indicating a compound number.
