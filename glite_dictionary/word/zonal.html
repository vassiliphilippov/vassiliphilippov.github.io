zonal

1. ADJ

/ ˈzoʊ.nəl / (AmE), / ˈzəʊ.nəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a zone or zones, especially in geography, climatology, or astronomy.
relating to a zone

Examples

The meteorologists studied the zonal patterns of wind flow across the continent.
Certain plant species are found only in specific zonal regions of the forest.
The zonal frontier marks the boundary between two distinct climate areas.

Synset Examples

the zonal frontier

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zoned - divided into zones or sections

Etymology

From zone + -al, from Latin 'zona' (belt, girdle) and the adjectival suffix '-al'.

2. ADJ

/ ˈzoʊ.nəl / (AmE), / ˈzəʊ.nəl / (BrE)
related to or divided into zones; arranged in or characteristic of zones.
relating to zones or divisions

Examples

The scientist described the zonal distribution of the plant species across the region.
Many cities have adopted zonal fare systems for public transportation.
The climate in this area exhibits distinct zonal changes from the coast to the mountains.

Synset Examples

a zonal pattern of cell structure
zonal division

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zoned - divided into or marked by zones

Etymology

From Latin zona (belt, zone) + -al (forming adjectives).
