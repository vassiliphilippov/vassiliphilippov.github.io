Eight

1. PROPN

/ eɪt / (AmE), / eɪt / (BrE)
A group of American painters founded in 1907, known for their realistic portrayals of urban life, often focusing on gritty or unglamorous themes.
group of American painters founded in 1907

The exhibition featured works by members of Eight, highlighting their impact on American art.
Many critics praised Eight for challenging traditional artistic conventions of the early 20th century.
