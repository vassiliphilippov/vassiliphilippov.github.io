hematogenic

1. ADJ

/ ˌhiːmətoʊˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌhiːmətəʊˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
relating to the formation of blood or blood cells.
relating to blood formation
medical

Examples

The bone marrow is the primary hematogenic tissue in the human body.
Researchers study hematogenic processes to understand blood disorders.
Certain drugs can affect hematogenic activity in the spleen and liver.

Synset Examples

hemopoietic stem cells in bone marrow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hematopoietic - relating to the formation of blood cells

Etymology

From Greek haima (blood) + -genic (producing).
