disorganized

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈɔrɡəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / dɪsˈɔːɡənaɪzd / (BrE)
lacking order, method, or organization; not arranged or planned in a systematic way
lacking order or organization

Examples

Her desk was always disorganized, with papers scattered everywhere.
Because the event was disorganized, many guests missed important activities.
A disorganized approach to work can lead to mistakes and missed deadlines.

Synset Examples

a disorganized enterprise
a thousand pages of muddy and disorganized prose
she was too disorganized to be an agreeable roommate

Related Terms

  • Antonym: organized - arranged in a systematic and orderly way
  • Synonym: chaotic - in a complete state of disorder and confusion

Etymology

From 'dis-' (expressing reversal) + 'organized'.
