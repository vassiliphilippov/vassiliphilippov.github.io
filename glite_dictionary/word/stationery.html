stationery

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈsteɪʃənəri / (BrE)
writing paper, especially with matching envelopes, used for letters and other correspondence
writing paper and matching envelopes

Examples

She picked out elegant stationery to write thank-you notes to her friends.
The invitation arrived on cream-colored stationery with a gold-embossed border.
He keeps a drawer full of stationery for personal correspondence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: writing paper - paper used for writing letters or notes

Etymology

From stationary (not moving), by confusion with stationer (a dealer in writing materials), 18th century.
