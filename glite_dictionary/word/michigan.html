Michigan

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɪʃɪɡən / (AmE), / ˈmɪʃɪɡən / (BrE)
a state in the north-central United States, in the Great Lakes region, with Lansing as its capital.
U.S. state in the Great Lakes region

Examples

She grew up in Michigan before moving to New York for college.
The winters in Michigan can be very harsh, with heavy snowfall near the lakes.
Automotive manufacturing has long been a major industry in Michigan.

Etymology

From the Ojibwa (Chippewa) word *mishigamaa*, meaning 'large water' or 'large lake'.
