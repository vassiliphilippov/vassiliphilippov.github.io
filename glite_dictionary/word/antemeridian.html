antemeridian

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.ti.məˈrɪd.i.ən / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪ.məˈrɪd.i.ən / (BrE)
Relating to the time before noon; occurring before midday.
before noon; prior to midday

Examples

The antemeridian hours are often quieter in the office.
Sunrise occurs during the antemeridian period of the day.
Doctors recommend eating a healthy breakfast during the antemeridian timeframe.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: postmeridian - relating to the time after noon

Etymology

From Latin ante meridiem, meaning 'before midday'.
