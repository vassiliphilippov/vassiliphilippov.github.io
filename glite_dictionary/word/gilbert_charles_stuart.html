Gilbert Charles Stuart

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡɪlbɚt ˈtʃɑrlz ˈstuːɚt / (AmE), / ˈɡɪlbət ˈtʃɑːlz ˈstjʊət / (BrE)
(1755–1828) An American painter best known for his portraits, particularly of George Washington.
American portrait painter (1755–1828)

Examples

The famous portrait of George Washington on the one-dollar bill was painted by Gilbert Charles Stuart.
Gilbert Charles Stuart is often regarded as one of America's foremost portrait artists.
Museums across the United States display several works by Gilbert Charles Stuart.
