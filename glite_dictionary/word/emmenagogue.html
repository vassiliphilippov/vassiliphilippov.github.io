emmenagogue

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛmɪˈnæɡəɡ / (AmE), / ˌɛmɪˈnæɡəɡ / (BrE)
a substance that stimulates or increases menstrual flow
substance that increases menstrual flow
medical

Examples

The herbalist recommended an emmenagogue to treat menstrual irregularities.
Certain plants have long been used as natural emmenagogues in traditional medicine.
Excessive use of an emmenagogue without medical advice can be harmful.

Etymology

From Greek emmenagogos, from emmena (menses) + agōgos (leading).
