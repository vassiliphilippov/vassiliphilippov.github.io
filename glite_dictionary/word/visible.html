visible

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ˈvɪzɪbəl / (BrE)
(especially of resources or supplies) present and easily available.
present and easily available

Examples

After the storm, there was only a visible supply of fresh water left for the townspeople.
The organization managed its visible resources carefully to ensure continued support for its activities.
There must always be a visible amount of cash on hand for emergencies.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: available - able to be used or obtained
  • Antonym: scarce - insufficient or hard to find; not available

Etymology
