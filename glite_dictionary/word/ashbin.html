ashbin

1. NOUN

/ ˈæʃˌbɪn / (AmE), / ˈæʃbɪn / (BrE)
a container used for holding rubbish or ashes until it is collected or disposed of
container for rubbish or ashes

Examples

He threw the burnt papers into the ashbin by the door.
Please empty the ashbin before it overflows.
The city provides each household with an ashbin for waste collection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dustbin - a bin for household waste
  • Synonym: trash can - a container for household rubbish (American English)
  • Degree Higher: recycling bin - a container for items to be recycled

Etymology

Formed from 'ash' + 'bin', referring originally to a bin for ashes from fires.
