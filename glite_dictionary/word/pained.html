pained

1. ADJ

/ peɪnd / (AmE), / peɪnd / (BrE)
showing or expressing discomfort, emotional hurt, or annoyance
showing discomfort or emotional hurt

Examples

She gave him a pained smile when she heard the news.
His pained expression made it clear he was hurt by her words.
The lecture was met with pained silence from the audience.

Synset Examples

she looked offended
face had a pained and puzzled expression
injured feelings

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hurt - emotionally wounded or distressed
  • Synonym: offended - feeling upset or annoyed, often due to a slight
  • Antonym: comforted - feeling reassured or soothed emotionally

Etymology

From the verb 'pain' + -ed, meaning affected with pain or distress.
