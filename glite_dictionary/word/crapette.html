crapette

1. NOUN

/ kræˈpɛt / (AmE), / kræˈpɛt / (BrE)
A form of solitaire for two players, each using a separate pack of cards.
two-player solitaire card game

Examples

We spent the evening playing crapette and chatting by the fire.
Crapette requires both strategy and quick thinking to win.
It's unusual to find someone who knows how to play crapette these days.

Etymology

From French 'crapette', the name of the game.
