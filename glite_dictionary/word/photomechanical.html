photomechanical

1. ADJ

/ ˌfoʊtoʊməˈkænɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊməˈkænɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving methods of using photography to produce printing plates or create prints
relating to photographic printing techniques

Examples

The book features several photomechanical reproductions of famous paintings.
Early newspapers relied on photomechanical processes for image reproduction.
Advances in photomechanical technology revolutionized the printing industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photographic - relating to photography

Etymology

From photo- (relating to light or photography) and mechanical (relating to machinery).
