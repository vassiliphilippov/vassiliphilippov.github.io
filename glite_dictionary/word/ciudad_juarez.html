Ciudad Juarez

1. PROPN

/ siˌudɑd ˈhwɑrɛz / (AmE), / siˌuːdad ˈhwɑːrɛz / (BrE)
A city in northern Mexico located on the Rio Grande, directly across the border from El Paso, Texas.
city in northern Mexico, opposite El Paso

Examples

Many visitors cross from El Paso into Ciudad Juarez for shopping and dining.
Ciudad Juarez has grown rapidly over the past few decades, becoming a major manufacturing center.
The border between Ciudad Juarez and El Paso is one of the busiest international crossings in the world.

Etymology

Spanish for 'Juarez City,' named after Benito Juárez, a former president of Mexico.
