playtime

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈpleɪ.taɪm / (BrE)
the period of time set aside for play or recreation, especially for children
time set aside for play or recreation

Examples

The children ran outside excitedly when playtime began.
Rainy weather forced the school to hold playtime indoors today.
She always looks forward to playtime after her lessons are finished.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recess - a break between classes for children to play
