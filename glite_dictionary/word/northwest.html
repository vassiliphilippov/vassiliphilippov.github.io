northwest

1. NOUN

/ nɔrθˈwɛst / (AmE), / nɔːθˈwɛst / (BrE)
the northwestern part of a country, region, or city
the northwestern area of a place

Examples

Many tech companies are headquartered in the northwest of the country.
The northwest experienced heavy rainfall throughout the summer.
Their new office is in the northwest of the city, near the university.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: northeast - the northeastern part of a country, region, or city
  • Antonym: southwest - the southwestern part of a country, region, or city

Etymology

From Old English 'norð' (north) + 'west'.

3. NOUN

/ nɔrθˈwɛst / (AmE), / nɔːθˈwɛst / (BrE)
the direction on the compass halfway between north and west, or the area towards this direction
direction halfway between north and west

Examples

The sun set in the northwest, casting a golden glow on the mountains.
We drove in a northwest direction for three hours before reaching the coast.
Storm clouds gathered in the northwest, signaling that rain was on the way.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: southwest - direction between south and west
  • Antonym: northeast - direction between north and east
  • Degree Higher: north - direction toward the top of a map or compass
  • Degree Lower: west - direction toward the left on a standard map

Etymology

From Old English 'norð' (north) + 'west' (west).

direction between north and west

Examples

The city lies to the northwest of the capital.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: west-northwest - the compass point between west and northwest; 292.5 degrees
  • Antonym: southeast - the direction halfway between south and east; opposite of northwest
