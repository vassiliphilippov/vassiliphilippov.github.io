Northwest

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɔrθˈwɛst / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˈwɛst / (BrE)
the northwestern region of the United States, typically including states such as Washington, Oregon, and sometimes Idaho and Montana
northwestern U.S. region
american

Examples

She moved to the Northwest last year to take a job in Seattle.
Heavy rain is typical in the Northwest during the winter months.
Many tech companies are based in the Northwest of the United States.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pacific Northwest - region of the Pacific coast of the northwestern United States
