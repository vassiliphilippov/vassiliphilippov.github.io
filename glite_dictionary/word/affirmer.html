affirmer

1. NOUN

/ əˈfɜrmər / (AmE), / əˈfɜːmə / (BrE)
a person who asserts or declares something to be true with confidence
person who asserts something as true

Examples

As an outspoken affirmer of her beliefs, she never hesitated to speak her mind in public.
The affirmer of the statement faced questions from skeptical journalists at the press conference.
Many religious affirmers attended the event to express their support for traditional values.

Synset Examples

a bold asserter
a declarer of his intentions
affirmers of traditional doctrine
an asseverator of strong convictions
an avower of his own great intelligence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assertor - a person who asserts something
  • Antonym: denier - a person who denies something

Etymology

From French 'affirmer' (to affirm); see also Latin 'affirmare'.
