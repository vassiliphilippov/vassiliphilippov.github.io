Agrostis

1. NOUN

/ əˈɡrɑstɪs / (AmE), / əˈɡrɒstɪs / (BrE)
A genus of annual or perennial grasses, commonly known as bent grass, found mainly in the Northern Hemisphere.
a genus of grasses (bent grass)

Examples

The golf course used Agrostis on its greens for a smooth playing surface.
Botanists identified several species of Agrostis along the riverbank.
Many lawns in temperate climates are seeded with Agrostis for its fine texture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bent grass - a common name for grasses of the genus Agrostis

Etymology

From Greek agrostis, meaning 'a kind of grass'.
