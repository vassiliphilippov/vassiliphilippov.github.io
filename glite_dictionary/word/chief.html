chief

1. NOUN

/ tʃif / (AmE), / tʃiːf / (BrE)
the leader or head of a tribe, clan, or other group; a person with the highest rank or authority within a community or organization
leader of a tribe or group

Examples

The tribe gathered to hear the chief speak about their future.
After the old chief passed away, his son was chosen as the new leader.
The chief made important decisions for the well-being of the entire clan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group, organization, or country
  • Antonym: subordinate - a person under the authority or control of another

Etymology

From Old French 'chef' (head, leader), from Latin 'caput' (head).

Examples
