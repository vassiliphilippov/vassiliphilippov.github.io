nominee

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɑː.məˈniː / (AmE), / ˌnɒm.ɪˈniː / (BrE)
a person who has been formally chosen or proposed for a position, especially as a candidate for public office
person formally chosen for a position or office

Examples

The party announced their official nominee for president last night.
Every nominee must register before the election deadline.
She became the first female nominee of her party for governor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: candidate - a person who is considered for a position or office

Etymology

From French *nominer*, based on Latin *nominare* (to name).
