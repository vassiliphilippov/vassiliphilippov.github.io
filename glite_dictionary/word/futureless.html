futureless

1. ADJ

/ ˈfjuːtʃərləs / (AmE), / ˈfjuːtʃələs / (BrE)
having no prospect or hope of a future; lacking possibilities for progress or success
having no prospect for the future

Examples

After the factory closed, many workers faced a futureless existence in the struggling town.
He described his situation as futureless, unable to see any way forward in his career.
The character's futureless prospects added to the novel's sense of despair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hopeless - having no hope; despairing
  • Antonym: promising - showing signs of future success or good results

Etymology

From 'future' + '-less', meaning 'without a future'.
