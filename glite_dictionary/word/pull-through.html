pull-through

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊl ˌθru / (AmE), / ˈpʊl ˌθruː / (BrE)
a cleaning implement consisting of an oily rag attached by a cord to a weight, which is pulled through the barrel of a rifle or handgun to clean it
firearm barrel cleaning tool

Examples

The soldier used a pull-through to clean the barrel of his rifle after shooting practice.
Always run a pull-through through your handgun to remove any residue.
He packed a cloth, oil, and a pull-through in his gun-cleaning kit.
