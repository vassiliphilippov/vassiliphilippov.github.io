Ziziphus jujuba

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɪzɪˌfəs dʒuːˈdʒuːbə / (AmE), / ˈzɪzɪfəs dʒuːˈdʒuːbə / (BrE)
A spiny tree species native to Asia, known for its dark red, edible fruits commonly called jujubes.
spiny Asian tree with edible fruits

Examples

The Ziziphus jujuba is widely cultivated in China for its sweet, nutritious fruit.
Many herbal remedies use extracts from the Ziziphus jujuba tree for their calming effects.
She planted a Ziziphus jujuba in her backyard to enjoy fresh jujubes every autumn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jujube - the edible fruit of Ziziphus jujuba

Etymology

From Latinized form of Persian 'zizafūn' (the jujube tree) and species name from Latin 'jujuba' (the fruit).
