madderwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈmædərˌwɔrt / (AmE), / ˈmædəwəːt / (BrE)
any of various trees, shrubs, or vines of the family Rubiaceae, especially those with roots used as a source of dye.
plant of the Rubiaceae family

Examples

The roots of the madderwort were used to produce a red dye for textiles.
Botanists discovered a rare species of madderwort in the rainforest.
Several herbal remedies contain extracts from madderwort plants.

Etymology

From 'madder', a plant used as a dye, and 'wort' (Old English for 'plant').
