feebleminded

1. ADJ

/ ˈfiːbəlˌmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˈfiːblˌmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
having significantly reduced intellectual ability; mentally deficient. Considered archaic and offensive when describing people.
having reduced intellectual ability (archaic, offensive)
archaic offensive

Examples

The old laws discriminated against those considered feebleminded, preventing them from marrying.
Some early 20th-century institutions were established to care for the feebleminded.
The term feebleminded is now viewed as outdated and inappropriate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simple-minded - having or showing very little intelligence or judgment; lacking in mental ability
  • Antonym: intelligent - having or showing intelligence, especially of a high level
