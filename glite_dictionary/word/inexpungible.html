inexpungible

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnɪkˈspʌndʒəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnɛkˈspʌndʒɪbəl / (BrE)
not able to be removed, erased, or wiped out; indelible.
impossible to erase or remove

Examples

The inexpungible scent of her perfume lingered in the room long after she had left.
Some childhood memories are inexpungible and continue to shape our adult lives.
His confession had left an inexpungible mark on their friendship.

Synset Examples

the inexpungible scent of a bottle of perfume he had broken

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indelible - impossible to remove or forget
  • Antonym: expungible - capable of being expunged or erased

Etymology

From Latin in- (not) + expungere (to expunge) + -ible (able to be).
