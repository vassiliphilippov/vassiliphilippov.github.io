volary

1. NOUN

/ ˈvoʊləri / (AmE), / ˈvɒləri / (BrE)
A building or large enclosure in which birds are kept; an aviary.
building for keeping birds

Examples

The ancient castle featured a beautiful volary filled with rare and exotic birds.
As birdsong echoed through the volary, children pressed their faces against the wire mesh in wonder.
The estate's volary required daily maintenance to ensure the birds remained healthy and comfortable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aviary - a large enclosure for keeping birds

Etymology

From Medieval Latin *volarium* (birdcage), from Latin *volare* (to fly).
