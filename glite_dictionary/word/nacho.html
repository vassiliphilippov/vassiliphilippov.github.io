nacho

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑː.tʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈnɑː.tʃəʊ / (BrE)
A crisp tortilla chip, often topped with melted cheese and other savory toppings such as jalapeños, beans, or meat.
tortilla chip with savory toppings

Examples

Could you pass me a nacho from the tray?
We ordered a plate of spicy nachos to share at the restaurant.
She topped each nacho with cheese, salsa, and slices of jalapeño.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tortilla chip - a thin, crisp chip made from a tortilla

Etymology

From the Mexican Spanish personal name Nacho, a nickname for Ignacio; the dish is named after Ignacio Anaya, who invented it.
