seedcake

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːdˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈsiːdkɛɪk / (BrE)
a sweet cake flavored with seeds such as caraway or sesame and often lemon
sweet cake with seeds (usually caraway or sesame)

Examples

She offered me a slice of her homemade seedcake with afternoon tea.
The seedcake was flecked with caraway seeds and had a delicate lemon flavor.
Traditional English recipes for seedcake date back for centuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pound cake - a rich cake containing a pound each of butter, sugar, eggs, and flour
