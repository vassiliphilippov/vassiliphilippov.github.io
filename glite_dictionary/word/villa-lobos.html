Villa-Lobos

1. PROPN

/ ˌviː.jɑː loʊˈboʊs / (AmE), / ˌviː.lə ˈləʊ.bɒs / (BrE)
(1887–1959) a Brazilian classical composer known for blending Brazilian folk music with European classical traditions.
Brazilian composer (1887–1959)

Examples

Many musicians consider Villa-Lobos to be one of Brazil's most influential composers.
The works of Villa-Lobos often fuse classical forms with Brazilian folk melodies.
