leaner

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːnər / (AmE), / ˈliːnə / (BrE)
(in horseshoes) a throw in which the horseshoe lands leaning against the stake without encircling it.
horseshoes: shoe leaning against stake

Examples

She scored extra points by making a leaner in her last turn.
A leaner is often worth more than a horseshoe lying flat on the ground.
Winning the match depended on whether his last throw would be a leaner or a ringer.
