L.A.

1. PROPN

/ ˌɛl ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛl ˈeɪ / (BrE)
A common abbreviation for Los Angeles, a city in southern California known as the center of the American film industry and the second most populous city in the United States.
Abbreviation for Los Angeles, California city
american

Examples

Many celebrities choose to live in L.A. because of its proximity to Hollywood.
The weather in L.A. is usually warm and sunny throughout the year.
After graduating, she moved to L.A. to pursue a career in acting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Los Angeles - A city in southern California, known as the motion picture capital.

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'Los Angeles,' from Spanish meaning 'The Angels.'
