Fringilla montifringilla

1. NOUN

/ frɪnˈdʒɪlə ˌmɒntɪfrɪnˈdʒɪlə / (AmE), / frɪnˈdʒɪlə ˌmɒntɪfrɪnˈdʒɪlə / (BrE)
A small Eurasian finch, commonly known as the brambling, with distinctive orange breast and white rump.
Eurasian finch species (brambling)

Examples

Many birdwatchers travel to see the rare Fringilla montifringilla during its winter migration.
The Fringilla montifringilla is easily recognized by its orange breast and white rump.
In northern Europe, flocks of Fringilla montifringilla gather in forests during autumn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brambling - Common name for Fringilla montifringilla, a Eurasian finch

Etymology

From Latin: 'Fringilla' (finch) and 'montifringilla' (mountain finch).
