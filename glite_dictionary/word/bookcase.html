bookcase

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊkˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌkeɪs / (BrE)
a piece of furniture with shelves for storing books
furniture with shelves for books

Examples

He placed his favorite novels on the top shelf of the bookcase.
The living room felt cozier after they assembled the new bookcase.
All the reference materials are neatly organized in the bookcase by the window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bookshelf - a shelf for holding books, typically part of a bookcase

Etymology

From 'book' + 'case', indicating a case for books.
