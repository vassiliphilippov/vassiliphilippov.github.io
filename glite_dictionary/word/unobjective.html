unobjective

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əbˈdʒɛk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əbˈdʒɛk.tɪv / (BrE)
not based on facts or impartial judgment; influenced by personal feelings or opinions rather than being objective.
not impartial or based on facts

Examples

The report is criticized for being unobjective and lacking factual evidence.
Her unobjective opinions made it hard to take her arguments seriously.
A judge must avoid being unobjective when deciding a case.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: objective - not influenced by personal feelings; based on facts
  • Synonym: biased - unfairly prejudiced for or against something

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (meaning 'not') to 'objective'.
