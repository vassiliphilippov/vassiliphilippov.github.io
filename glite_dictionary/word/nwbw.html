NWbW

1. NOUN

/ ɛn dʌbljuː bi dʌbljuː / (AmE), / ɛn dʌbljuː bi dʌbljuː / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point west of northwest, or at 303.75° on the compass.
compass point west of northwest

Examples

The ship changed course to NWbW to avoid the storm.
He checked the bearing, which was exactly NWbW on the compass card.
Sailing at NWbW will take us along the edge of the bay.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: northwest - compass point between north and west, at 315°
  • Degree Lower: west-northwest - compass point one point west of northwest by west, at 292.5°

Etymology

Abbreviation for 'northwest by west', used in navigational contexts.
