Mustela nivalis

1. NOUN

/ mʌsˈtɛl.ə naɪˈveɪ.lɪs / (AmE), / mʌsˈtiː.lə nɪˈvɑː.lɪs / (BrE)
the scientific name for the least weasel, a small carnivorous mammal native to Europe, Asia, and North America
the least weasel (animal species)

Examples

The Mustela nivalis is the smallest carnivorous mammal in the world.
In scientific texts, the least weasel is often referred to as Mustela nivalis.
British wildlife enthusiasts sometimes spot Mustela nivalis in rural hedgerows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: least weasel - the common name for Mustela nivalis

Etymology

From Latin: 'mustela' meaning 'weasel' and 'nivalis' meaning 'of snow' or 'snowy'.
