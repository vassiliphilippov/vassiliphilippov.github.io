knobby

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɑːbi / (AmE), / ˈnɒbi / (BrE)
having knobs or rounded lumps; covered with small bumps or protuberances
having knobs or lumps

Examples

She admired the knobby branches of the old oak tree as they twisted skyward.
His knobby knees stuck out from his shorts during the summer hike.
The potato was knobby and misshapen, but still perfectly edible.
