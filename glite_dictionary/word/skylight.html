skylight

1. NOUN

/ ˈskaɪˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈskaɪlaɪt / (BrE)
a window set in a roof or ceiling to admit natural daylight
roof window for daylight

Examples

The living room felt brighter after they installed a large skylight above the couch.
Rain pattered soothingly against the glass of the skylight during the storm.
She lay in bed watching the stars through the skylight at night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roof window - a window installed in a roof for ventilation and light

Etymology

Formed from 'sky' + 'light', referring to light from the sky entering through a roof opening.
