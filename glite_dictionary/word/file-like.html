file-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈfaɪlˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfaɪl.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the characteristics of a file, especially in shape, appearance, or function.
resembling a file

Examples

The insect's file-like wings were rough and serrated.
He used a file-like tool to smooth the edges of the wood.
The leaves had a file-like texture, feeling coarse to the touch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: file-shaped - having the shape of a file
