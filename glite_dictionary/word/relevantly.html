relevantly

1. ADV

/ ˈrɛl.ə.vənt.li / (AmE), / ˈrel.ə.vənt.li / (BrE)
In a way that is connected with or appropriate to the matter at hand.
in a relevant manner

Examples

She spoke relevantly to the topic, ensuring everyone understood the key points.
All comments should be made relevantly to avoid confusion during the meeting.
His contributions were always relevantly tied to the discussion at hand.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: irrelevantly - in an irrelevant manner
