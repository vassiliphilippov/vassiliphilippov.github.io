hangout

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæŋˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈhæŋaʊt / (BrE)
a place where someone regularly spends time or meets friends
a regularly visited place

Examples

The coffee shop has become my favorite hangout after work.
Teenagers often choose the mall as their weekend hangout.
We spent hours at our old hangout chatting and laughing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: haunt - a place frequently visited by someone
  • Synonym: retreat - a place of refuge or seclusion

Etymology

From the verb phrase 'hang out' (to spend time idly), later nominalized.
