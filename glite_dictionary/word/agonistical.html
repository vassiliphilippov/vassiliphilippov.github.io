agonistical

1. ADJ

/ ˌæɡəˈnɪstɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌaɡəˈnɪstɪkəl / (BrE)
engaged in or relating to struggle, contest, or striving to overcome in argument or debate
striving to overcome in argument

Examples

The presidential debate quickly took on an agonistical tone as the candidates clashed over key policies.
His agonistical style of discussion often leads to heated arguments with his colleagues.
The philosopher adopted an agonistical approach to challenge prevailing assumptions in his field.

Synset Examples

a dialectical and agonistic approach

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agonistic - involving or characterized by conflict or opposition

Etymology

From Greek agōnistikos (fond of struggle, combative), formed from agōn (contest, struggle) + -istic + -al.
