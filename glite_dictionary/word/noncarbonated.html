noncarbonated

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈkɑrbəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / nɒnˈkɑːbəneɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a beverage or liquid) not containing dissolved carbon dioxide; not fizzy.
not containing carbonation

Examples

She always prefers noncarbonated water to soda with her lunch.
Many juices are noncarbonated, making them a good option for children.
The doctor advised me to stick to noncarbonated drinks for a while.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: carbonated - (of a beverage) containing dissolved carbon dioxide; fizzy

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'non-' to 'carbonated'.
