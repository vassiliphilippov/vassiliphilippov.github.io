racial

1. ADJ

/ ˈreɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˈreɪʃəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of people belonging to different races; concerning differences or issues between races
relating to race or races

Examples

The new law aims to prevent racial discrimination in employment practices.
There was a racial diversity among the students at the university.
The report highlighted racial tensions in the community.

Synset Examples

racial groups

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ethnic - relating to a population subgroup with a common national or cultural tradition

Etymology

From French 'racial', from 'race' (race) + '-al' (adjectival suffix).

WordNet ID: oewn-03153277-a

2. ADJ

/ ˈreɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˈreɪʃəl / (BrE)
relating to or arising from differences between human races, or characteristic of a particular race.
relating to human races or differences between them

Examples

The company implemented policies to prevent racial discrimination in the workplace.
There has been widespread debate about racial differences in access to education.
His research focuses on the impact of racial bias in the legal system.

Synset Examples

racial differences
racial discrimination

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ethnic - relating to a population subgroup with a common national or cultural tradition

Etymology

From race (in the sense of kind or lineage) + -al (adjective suffix).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01934053-a
Synset POS: a
