soulless

1. ADJ

/ ˈsoʊl.ləs / (AmE), / ˈsəʊl.ləs / (BrE)
lacking sensitivity, warmth, or the capacity for deep feeling; cold or devoid of emotion.
lacking sensitivity or deep feeling

Examples

The soulless office building made everyone feel unwelcome and uninspired.
His soulless response to the tragedy shocked those around him.
Some critics called the new pop song catchy but ultimately soulless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartless - showing a lack of compassion or kindness
  • Synonym: cold - lacking affection or warmth of feeling
  • Antonym: compassionate - showing sympathy and concern for others

Etymology

From soul + -less, meaning 'without a soul' or 'without spirit or feeling.'
