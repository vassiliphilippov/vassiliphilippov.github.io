Oaxaca

1. PROPN

/ wɑːˈhɑːkə / (AmE), / wəˈhækə / (BrE)
a city in southeastern Mexico, known for its colonial architecture, vibrant culture, and regional cuisine
city in southeastern Mexico

Examples

The vibrant markets of Oaxaca are famous for their traditional crafts and local food.
During our trip to Mexico, we spent several days exploring historic sites in Oaxaca.
Many travelers visit Oaxaca to experience its colorful Day of the Dead celebrations.

Etymology

From Nahuatl *Huāxyacac*, meaning 'at the nose of the huaje trees'.
