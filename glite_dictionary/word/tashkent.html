Tashkent

1. PROPN

/ tɑːʃˈkɛnt / (AmE), / tæʃˈkɛnt / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Uzbekistan, located in the eastern part of the country
capital of Uzbekistan

Examples

She traveled to Tashkent to attend an international conference on urban development.
Tashkent is known for its mix of modern architecture and historic sites.
The direct flight from Istanbul to Tashkent takes about five hours.

Etymology

From Uzbek Toşkent, from Turkic tosh (stone) + kent (city, town).
