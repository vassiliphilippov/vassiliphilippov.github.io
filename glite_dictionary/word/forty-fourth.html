forty-fourth

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɔrti ˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / ˌfɔːti ˈfɔːθ / (BrE)
Coming after forty-three others in a sequence; being number 44 in a list or ordered set.
ordinal number: number 44th

Examples

She finished in forty-fourth place out of a hundred competitors.
This is the forty-fourth anniversary of their founding.
He lives on the forty-fourth floor of the skyscraper.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty-third - number 43rd in order
  • Degree Higher: forty-fifth - number 45th in order
