William James

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪljəm ˈdʒeɪmz / (AmE), / ˈwɪljəm ˈdʒeɪmz / (BrE)
(1842–1910) United States philosopher and psychologist, one of the founders of pragmatism and modern psychology.
U.S. philosopher and psychologist (1842–1910)
