butty

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌti / (AmE), / ˈbʌti / (BrE)
(British) A sandwich, especially one with a filling such as bacon, sausage, or chips.
British term for sandwich
british

Examples

He ordered a butty and a cup of tea for breakfast.
After a night out, a bacon butty really hits the spot.
You can't beat a good chip butty with salt and vinegar.

Synset Examples

a bacon butty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sandwich - two slices of bread with a filling in between

Etymology

Possibly from 'butter' with the diminutive '-y' suffix, originally referring to a slice of bread and butter.
