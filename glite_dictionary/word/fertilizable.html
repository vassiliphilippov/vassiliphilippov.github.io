fertilizable

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɜːr.tɪˈlaɪ.zə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌfɜː.tɪˈlaɪ.zə.bəl / (BrE)
Capable of being fertilized, especially by pollen, sperm, or another fertilizing agent.
able to be fertilized

Examples

During ovulation, the egg becomes fertilizable and ready for conception.
Many seeds remain fertilizable for only a short period after being released.
Researchers studied the conditions under which the oocytes were most fertilizable.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: infertile - not able to be fertilized

Etymology

From 'fertilize' + '-able', following standard English formation of adjectives meaning 'capable of being ...'.
