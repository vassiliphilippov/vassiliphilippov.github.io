al-Qur'an

1. NOUN

/ æl kʊˈrɑːn / (AmE), / æl kʊˈrɑːn / (BrE)
the holy book of Islam, believed by Muslims to be the word of God as revealed to the Prophet Muhammad.
the holy book of Islam

Examples

Many Muslims recite verses from the al-Qur'an during daily prayers.
Scholars have translated the al-Qur'an into numerous languages to share its teachings with the world.
She attended a class to learn how to read the al-Qur'an in Arabic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Quran - Another form of the name for the holy book of Islam

Etymology

From Arabic 'al-Qur'ān', meaning 'the recitation'.
