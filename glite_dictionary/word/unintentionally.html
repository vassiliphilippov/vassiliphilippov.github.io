unintentionally

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnɪnˈtɛnʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈtɛnʃənəli / (BrE)
Without intention; in a way that is not deliberate or planned.
not deliberately or on purpose

Examples

He unintentionally offended his friend with a careless remark.
I unintentionally left my phone at home this morning.
She broke the vase unintentionally while cleaning the shelf.

Synset Examples

she hit him unintentionally

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intentionally - deliberately; done on purpose

Etymology

From 'unintentional' + '-ly', where 'unintentional' derives from Latin 'intentio' (intention) with the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not'.
