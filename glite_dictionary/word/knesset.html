Knesset

1. PROPN

/ kəˈnɛsɛt / (AmE), / kəˈnɛsɛt / (BrE)
the unicameral national parliament of Israel, responsible for enacting laws and overseeing the government.
parliament of Israel

Examples

The Knesset passed a new law regarding health care reform.
During their trip to Jerusalem, the students toured the Knesset building.
Debates in the Knesset are often broadcast live on national television.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parliament - the legislative body of government in a country

Etymology

From Hebrew *Knesset* (כנסת‎), meaning 'assembly' or 'gathering.'
