fantan

1. NOUN

/ ˈfænˌtæn / (AmE), / ˈfæn.tæn / (BrE)
A card game in which players lay down sevens and then other cards in sequence in the same suit, aiming to be the first to use all their cards.
a card game played by laying sevens and sequences

Examples

Every Friday night, the family would gather around the table to play fantan.
He taught his friends how to play fantan with a standard deck of cards.
At the party, they started a lively game of fantan that lasted for hours.

Etymology

From Cantonese 番攤 (faān tāan), originally referring to a Chinese gambling game.

2. NOUN

/ ˈfænˌtæn / (AmE), / ˈfæn.tæn / (BrE)
a traditional Chinese gambling game in which players bet on the remainder when a random number of counters are divided by four
Chinese gambling game with counters

Examples

The group spent all night playing fantan at the crowded gambling house.
He learned to play fantan during his travels in Macau.
Tourists watched locals gather around a table to place bets on fantan.

Etymology

From Cantonese 番攤 (fāan tāan) meaning 'repeatedly spread out'.
