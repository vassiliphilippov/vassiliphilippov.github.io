readying

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdiɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdiɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of making something or someone ready; preparation for an upcoming event or purpose
the act of preparing or making ready

Examples

The readying of the house for guests took the entire afternoon.
Final readying for the tournament began days before the first match.
Despite all our readying, last-minute issues still caught us by surprise.

Synset Examples

preparations for the ceremony had begun

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preparation - the action of making ready or being made ready for use or consideration

Etymology

From the verb 'ready' + -ing, forming a verbal noun.
