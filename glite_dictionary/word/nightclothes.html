nightclothes

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪtˌkloʊðz / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtˌkləʊðz / (BrE)
Garments, such as pajamas or nightgowns, worn in bed while sleeping.
bedtime garments

Examples

She changed into her nightclothes before going to bed.
Warm nightclothes are essential during cold winter nights.
He forgot to pack his nightclothes for the overnight stay.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pajamas - loose-fitting clothes worn for sleeping
  • Synonym: nightgown - a loose dress worn in bed by women or children
  • Antonym: daywear - clothes worn during the day
