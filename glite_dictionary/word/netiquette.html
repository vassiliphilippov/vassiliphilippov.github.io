netiquette

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛt.ɪˌkɛt / (AmE), / ˈnɛt.ɪ.kət / (BrE)
The code of good behavior and manners expected when communicating over the internet or via electronic communication.
online communication etiquette

Examples

It's important to follow proper netiquette when posting on forums or social media.
Many online courses begin with a lesson on netiquette to foster respectful discussions.
Poor netiquette can quickly lead to misunderstandings and conflicts online.

Synset Examples

I know there is a UK political blog war going on at the moment about ‘netiquette’ and lies and spin and deceit, and I have not wanted to join in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: etiquette - conventional rules of polite behavior

Etymology

Blend of 'network' and 'etiquette.'
