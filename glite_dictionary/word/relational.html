relational

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈleɪʃənəl / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪʃənəl / (BrE)
connected with or concerning the way in which two or more things or people are related.
connected with relationships

Examples

The relational structure of the database allows users to connect different pieces of information.
Family therapy often explores relational patterns between parents and children.
Her research focuses on the relational aspects of social behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: related - connected; having a relationship

Etymology

From Latin relatio (a bringing back) + -al.
