Nicosia

1. PROPN

/ ˌnɪkoʊˈsiːə / (AmE), / ˌnɪkəʊˈsiːə / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Cyprus, situated near the center of the island
capital city of Cyprus

Examples

Cyprus's capital, Nicosia, is divided by the Green Line into northern and southern sections.
The conference was held in Nicosia to promote peace and cooperation between communities.
She moved to Nicosia for university and quickly adapted to life in the vibrant city.

Etymology

From Italian 'Nicosia', from Medieval Greek 'Λευκωσία' (Lefkosía).
