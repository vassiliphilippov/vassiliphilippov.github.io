unfastened

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfæstənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɑːsənd / (BrE)
not tied, closed, or secured; left open or undone
not tied or secured

Examples

She noticed that her shoelaces were unfastened and quickly bent down to tie them.
The door swung open because it had been left unfastened by mistake.
His jacket remained unfastened despite the cold weather.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fastened - securely tied, closed, or fixed
  • Synonym: untied - not fastened or tied

Etymology

From un- (not) + fasten (to make secure).

not buttoned, buckled, tied, or otherwise secured; left open or undone.
not secured; left open or undone

Examples

Always check that the child's seatbelt is not unfastened before starting the car.
The door swung open because it was left unfastened by mistake.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fastened - secured, closed, or attached

3. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfæsn̩d / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɑːsn̩d / (BrE)
not closed, attached, or secured; loose or undone.
not secured; undone

Examples

She realized her shoelaces were unfastened as she started to walk.
The baby’s bib came unfastened during lunch.
Check that your helmet strap is not unfastened before riding.

Synset Examples

the car door was unfastened
unfastened seatbelts

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fastened - closed or securely attached
  • Synonym: loose - not firmly or tightly fixed in place

Etymology

un- (not) + fastened (from fasten, meaning to attach or secure).
