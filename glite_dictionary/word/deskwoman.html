deskwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛskˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈdɛskˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female officer who serves as a desk sergeant, typically in a police station, handling administrative duties and coordinating communications
female desk sergeant

Examples

After her promotion, Maria became the first deskwoman at the precinct.
The deskwoman efficiently handled all the reports coming into the station.
Citizens approached the deskwoman for assistance with their paperwork.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: deskman - a male desk sergeant or officer assigned administrative duties at a desk
  • Synonym: desk sergeant - a police officer in charge of administrative duties at a station's front desk

Etymology

Formed from 'desk' and 'woman,' following the pattern of occupational gendered nouns.
