Chelyabinsk

1. PROPN

/ tʃɛl.jəˈbɪnsk / (AmE), / tʃɛl.jəˈbɪnsk / (BrE)
a city in the Ural region of Russia, east of the Ural Mountains, known as a major industrial center
city in Russia, Ural region

Examples

The meteor that exploded over Chelyabinsk in 2013 drew worldwide attention.
She was born in Chelyabinsk and later moved to Moscow for her studies.
Many industrial goods are produced in Chelyabinsk, making it a vital economic hub.

Etymology

Named after the fortress of Chelyaba, on whose site the city was founded.
