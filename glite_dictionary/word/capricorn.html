Capricorn

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæp.rɪˌkɔrn / (AmE), / ˈkæp.rɪ.kɔːn / (BrE)
A faint zodiacal constellation in the Southern Hemisphere, situated between Sagittarius and Aquarius.
zodiacal constellation between Sagittarius and Aquarius

Examples

The constellation Capricorn is best seen in the night sky during early autumn.
Ancient astronomers identified Capricorn as a goat with a fish’s tail.
Between Sagittarius and Aquarius lies the faint constellation of Capricorn.

Etymology

From Latin 'Capricornus', meaning 'horned goat', due to its representation as a goat in astronomy and astrology.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkæprɪˌkɔrn / (AmE), / ˈkæp.rɪ.kɔːn / (BrE)
A person born when the sun is in the zodiac sign Capricorn, typically between December 22 and January 19.
person born under Capricorn zodiac sign

Examples

Many people believe that a Capricorn is ambitious and determined by nature.
As a Capricorn, she takes pride in being organized and practical.
My best friend and I are both Capricorns, born just a few days apart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sagittarius - person born under the Sagittarius zodiac sign
  • Synonym: Aquarius - person born under the Aquarius zodiac sign

Etymology

From Latin Capricornus (horned goat), referring to the zodiac sign.

3. NOUN

/ ˈkæprɪˌkɔrn / (AmE), / ˈkæprɪkɔːn / (BrE)
the tenth sign of the zodiac, covering the period from about December 22 to January 19, symbolized by the Goat
zodiac sign (December 22–January 19)

Examples

People born under Capricorn are said to be disciplined and responsible.
Her birthday falls in Capricorn, making her a natural leader according to astrologers.
The symbol for Capricorn is the Goat.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Sagittarius - the ninth sign of the zodiac
  • Degree Higher: Aquarius - the eleventh sign of the zodiac

Etymology

From Latin 'Capricornus', meaning 'horned goat'.
