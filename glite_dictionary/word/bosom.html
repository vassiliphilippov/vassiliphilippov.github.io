bosom

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊzəm / (AmE), / ˈbʊz.əm / (BrE)
the part of a garment that covers the chest or breasts
part of clothing covering the chest

Examples

She pinned a delicate brooch to her bosom before leaving the house.
The lace on the bosom of her dress was beautifully embroidered.
He tucked the letter into the bosom of his shirt for safekeeping.

Etymology

From Old English 'bosm' (breast, chest), of Germanic origin.
