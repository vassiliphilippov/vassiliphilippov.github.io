Seanad Éireann

1. PROPN

/ ˈʃænəd ɛˈrɑːn / (AmE), / ˈʃanəd ɛˈɪərən / (BrE)
the upper house of the parliament of Ireland, responsible for revising legislation and representing various interests
upper house of Ireland's parliament

Examples

The Seanad Éireann debated the bill before returning it to the lower house.
She was nominated to the Seanad Éireann by the Taoiseach in 2016.
Elections for the Seanad Éireann take place after the general election for the Dáil.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Oireachtas - the national parliament of Ireland, comprising the President, Dáil Éireann, and Seanad Éireann
  • Degree Lower: Dáil Éireann - the lower house of the parliament of Ireland

Etymology

From Irish, meaning 'Senate of Ireland' (Seanad: senate, Éireann: of Ireland).
