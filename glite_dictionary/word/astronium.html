Astronium

1. NOUN

/ æˈstroʊniəm / (AmE), / æˈstrəʊniəm / (BrE)
A genus of tropical trees in the family Anacardiaceae, native to Central and South America.
genus of tropical trees (Anacardiaceae)

Examples

The wood of Astronium species is highly valued for its hardness and beauty.
Botanists have identified several new Astronium species in the Amazon rainforest.
Many furniture makers prefer using Astronium for its rich color and fine grain.

Etymology

From New Latin, from Greek 'astron' (star), possibly referencing the star-shaped structure of some plant parts.
