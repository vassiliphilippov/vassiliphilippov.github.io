to deserve

1. VERB

/ dɪˈzɜrv / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːv / (BrE)
to be worthy of something, typically a reward or punishment, based on your actions or qualities
be worthy of (reward or punishment)

Examples

After all the effort she put in, she really deserves this recognition.
No one should deserve such harsh criticism for a minor mistake.
If you work hard, you deserve to succeed.

Synset Examples

You deserve a promotion after all the hard work you have done

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to earn - to receive as return for effort or service
  • Synonym: merit - to deserve or be worthy of

Etymology

From Old French 'deservir', from Latin 'deservire' (to serve well, be deserving).
