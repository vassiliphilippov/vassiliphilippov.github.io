putrid

1. ADJ

/ ˈpjuː.trɪd / (AmE), / ˈpjuː.trɪd / (BrE)
Morally corrupt, evil, or thoroughly rotten in character or morals.
morally corrupt or evil

Examples

The putrid atmosphere of the boardroom made honest employees uncomfortable.
Many critics denounced the regime’s putrid disregard for justice and decency.
A putrid culture of bribery thrived within the organization for decades.

Synset Examples

the putrid atmosphere of the court

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corrupt - lacking in integrity; dishonest or immoral
  • Antonym: virtuous - having or showing high moral standards

Etymology

From Latin putridus (rotten), from putrēre (to rot).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00625010-s
Synset POS: s

2. ADJ

/ ˈpjuː.trɪd / (AmE), / ˈpjuː.trɪd / (BrE)
Decaying or rotting and emitting a very unpleasant, foul smell.
decaying with a foul odor

Examples

The putrid smell coming from the garbage bin made her gag.
They found a putrid carcass in the woods, swarming with flies.
His socks had a putrid odor after being left in a gym bag for days.

Synset Examples

horrible like raw and putrid flesh

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rotten - decayed or decomposed, usually emitting a bad smell
  • Antonym: fresh - recently made or obtained; not decayed

Etymology

From Latin putridus, meaning 'rotten'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01074514-s
Synset POS: s

3. ADJ

/ ˈpjutrɪd / (AmE), / ˈpjuːtrɪd / (BrE)
Decaying or rotting and emitting a fetid smell; characteristic of or relating to putrefaction.
decaying and foul-smelling

Examples

The smell from the garbage was absolutely putrid after sitting out in the sun for days.
She recoiled at the sight of the putrid meat in the abandoned refrigerator.
The cellar was filled with a putrid odor that made it impossible to enter.

Synset Examples

putrid decomposition

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rotten - decayed, decomposed or spoiled
  • Antonym: fresh - recently made or obtained; not decayed

Etymology

From Latin putridus (rotten, decayed), from putrēre (to rot).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02797121-a
Synset POS: a
