Winslow Homer

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪnzloʊ ˈhoʊmər / (AmE), / ˈwɪnzləʊ ˈhəʊmə / (BrE)
(1836–1910) an American painter and printmaker best known for his seascapes and depictions of outdoor life.
American painter famous for seascapes

Examples

Winslow Homer's paintings often capture the rugged beauty of the American coastline.
The exhibition featured several works by Winslow Homer, highlighting his mastery of watercolor.
Many art historians regard Winslow Homer as one of the greatest American painters of the 19th century.
