customer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌs.tə.mɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌs.tə.mə / (BrE)
A person or organization that buys goods or services from a business.
person who buys goods or services

Examples

Every customer is required to show their receipt before leaving the store.
The restaurant always tries to make every customer feel welcome.
A satisfied customer is more likely to return and recommend the service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: client - person who receives professional services
  • Antonym: vendor - person or company selling goods or services

Etymology

From Anglo-Norman and Old French 'costumier' (customs officer), from 'costume' (custom, dues), ultimately from Latin 'consuetudo' (custom).
