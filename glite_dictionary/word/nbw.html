NbW

1. NOUN

/ ɛn bi dʌbəlˈjuː / (AmE), / ɛn bi dʌbəlˈjuː / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point west of due north, lying at 348°45′.
compass point, one point west of north

Examples

The ship was sailing on a course of NbW to avoid the rocky coastline.
The wind shifted from north to NbW during the night.
Plot your heading carefully if you want to travel exactly NbW across the bay.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: north - compass direction at 0° (due north)
  • Degree Higher: north-northwest - compass direction between north and northwest (337°30′)

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'north by west.'
