buttercrunch

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ɚˌkrʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌkrʌntʃ / (BrE)
a variety of lettuce with tender, relatively crunchy leaves, often used in salads
variety of lettuce with crunchy leaves

Examples

The salad featured crisp buttercrunch lettuce tossed with cherry tomatoes and a light vinaigrette.
I prefer buttercrunch in my sandwiches because of its tender, crunchy leaves.
We planted buttercrunch in the garden this year because it thrives in cool weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butterhead - a type of lettuce with soft, buttery leaves
