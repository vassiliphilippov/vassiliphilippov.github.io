grassfire

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡræsˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːsˌfaɪə / (BrE)
An uncontrolled fire that burns in an area covered with grass.
uncontrolled fire in grassland

Examples

The firefighters were called to put out a grassfire near the highway.
Strong winds made the grassfire spread quickly across the open field.
Last summer, a large grassfire threatened several homes at the edge of town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wildfire - a large destructive fire that spreads rapidly
