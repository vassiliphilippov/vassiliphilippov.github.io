tendinitis

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛn.dɪˈnaɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˌtɛn.dɪˈnaɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of a tendon, usually causing pain and swelling.
inflammation of a tendon
medical

Examples

The athlete was sidelined for weeks due to tendinitis in her shoulder.
He visited a specialist after noticing the symptoms of tendinitis in his elbow.
Proper stretching can help prevent tendinitis in active individuals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tendonitis - alternative spelling for tendinitis

Etymology

From Latin 'tendo' (tendon) and suffix '-itis' (inflammation).
