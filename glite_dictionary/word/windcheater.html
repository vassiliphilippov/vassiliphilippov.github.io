windcheater

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪndˌtʃitər / (AmE), / ˈwɪndˌtʃiːtə / (BrE)
A thin, weather-resistant jacket designed to protect against wind and light rain.
lightweight windproof jacket
british

Examples

She put on her windcheater before heading out into the chilly morning.
The windcheater kept him dry during the sudden drizzle.
Hikers often pack a windcheater for unpredictable weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: windbreaker - A similar lightweight jacket protecting against wind

Etymology

From 'wind' + 'cheat', meaning a garment that 'cheats' or protects against the wind.
