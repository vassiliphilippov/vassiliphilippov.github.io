nectarous

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɛk.tɚ.əs / (AmE), / ˈnɛk.tə.rəs / (BrE)
Extremely pleasing to the taste, especially sweet and fragrant.
extremely pleasing (sweet, fragrant) to the taste

Examples

The chef served a nectarous dessert that left everyone wanting more.
Her homemade lemonade was so nectarous that it became the highlight of the picnic.
The aroma from the orchard was nectarous, filling the air with a sweet fragrance.

Synset Examples

a nectarous drink
ambrosial food

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ambrosial - having a delicious taste or smell
  • Synonym: delicious - highly pleasant to the taste
  • Antonym: unpalatable - not pleasant to taste

Etymology

From Latin 'nectar' (the drink of the gods in classical mythology) + '-ous' (a suffix forming adjectives).
