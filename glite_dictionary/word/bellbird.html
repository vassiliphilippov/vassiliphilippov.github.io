bellbird

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛlˌbɝd / (AmE), / ˈbɛlˌbɜːd / (BrE)
A tropical American bird of the genus Procnias, known for its bell-like call.
a tropical bird with a bell-like call

Examples

The distinctive call of the bellbird can be heard echoing through the rainforest.
Researchers studied the mating behavior of the bellbird during the breeding season.
The clear, metallic notes of the bellbird delighted birdwatchers in Central America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bell - a hollow object that makes a ringing sound when struck
