skittishly

1. ADV

/ ˈskɪtɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈskɪtɪʃli / (BrE)
In a skittish manner; with nervousness or unpredictability.
in a nervous or unpredictable way

Examples

The colt darted skittishly away from the sound of footsteps.
She glanced skittishly around the crowded room, searching for a familiar face.
Leaves blew skittishly across the pavement in the autumn wind.

Synset Examples

the horse pranced around skittishly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skittish - nervous or easily frightened; unpredictable
