half-hearted

1. ADJ

/ ˌhæfˈhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˌhɑːfˈhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
Showing little enthusiasm, energy, or interest; unenthusiastic or lacking commitment.
lacking enthusiasm or commitment

Examples

She gave a half-hearted apology for her mistake, barely meeting his eyes.
His half-hearted attempt at cleaning the garage left most of the work unfinished.
The team's half-hearted performance disappointed the fans who expected more passion.

Synset Examples

a halfhearted effort
gave only lukewarm support to the candidate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unenthusiastic - not showing much interest or excitement
  • Antonym: enthusiastic - showing intense and eager enjoyment or approval

Etymology

From 'half' + 'hearted', meaning having only half the heart or enthusiasm for something.
