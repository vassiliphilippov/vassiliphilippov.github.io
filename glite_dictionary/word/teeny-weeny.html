teeny-weeny

1. ADJ

/ ˌtini ˈwini / (AmE), / ˌtiːni ˈwiːni / (BrE)
Very small; extremely tiny (used informally).
very small; extremely tiny (informal)
informal

Examples

The dollhouse furniture was so teeny-weeny that it fit in the palm of my hand.
She gave me a teeny-weeny piece of chocolate after dinner.
Even a teeny-weeny scratch can ruin the surface of the glass.

Synset Examples

a wee tot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiny - very small in size
  • Antonym: huge - extremely large in size

Etymology

Reduplication of 'teeny' (diminutive of 'tiny'), playful informal formation.
