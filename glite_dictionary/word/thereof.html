thereof

1. ADV

/ ðɛrˈʌv / (AmE), / ðeəˈrɒv / (BrE)
of or concerning the thing just mentioned; of that or of it.
of or concerning that or it

Examples

The parties agree to the terms and conditions thereof.
He referred to the contract and the obligations thereof.
The court evaluated the evidence and the merits thereof.

Synset Examples

a problem and the solution thereof

Etymology

From Middle English 'therof', from Old English 'þǣrof', composed of 'þǣr' (there) + 'of' (of).
