self-starter

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlfˈstɑrtər / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈstɑːtə / (BrE)
a motor that automatically starts an internal-combustion engine, especially in cars or other vehicles
motor that starts an engine automatically

Examples

The mechanic replaced the faulty self-starter in my car last week.
Without a functioning self-starter, the engine would not turn over.
Most modern vehicles are equipped with a self-starter to simplify ignition.

2. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlfˈstɑrtɚ / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈstɑːtə / (BrE)
a person who is energetic and able to start things independently without needing prompting from others
a proactive, independent person

Examples

Jessica is a real self-starter who never waits for instructions before getting to work.
Companies value employees who are self-starters and can manage their own tasks.
As a self-starter, he quickly identified problems and proposed effective solutions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go-getter - an aggressively enterprising person
  • Synonym: initiative - the ability to assess and start things independently

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'starter', referring to one who starts by themselves.
