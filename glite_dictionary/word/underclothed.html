underclothed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɚˈkloʊðd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈkləʊðd / (BrE)
wearing clothing that is insufficient to protect or cover the body adequately
wearing too little clothing

Examples

The refugees arrived underclothed for the harsh winter conditions.
She was clearly underclothed for the chilly evening event.
Many children in the camp remain underclothed despite relief efforts.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: overclothed - wearing more clothing than needed

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'under-' (meaning 'insufficiently') and 'clothed'.
