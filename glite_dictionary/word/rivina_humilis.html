Rivina humilis

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈvaɪ.nə ˈhjuː.mɪlɪs / (AmE), / rɪˈviː.nə ˈhjuː.mɪlɪs / (BrE)
a bushy tropical American plant with white to pale pink flowers followed by clusters of scarlet berries, often grown as a houseplant
tropical plant with red berries

Examples

The gardener added a Rivina humilis to his greenhouse collection for its striking red berries.
Many people grow Rivina humilis indoors because of its attractive foliage and delicate flowers.
Wildlife is often attracted to the bright berries produced by Rivina humilis.
