vena testicularis

1. NOUN

/ ˈviːnə tɛˌstɪˈkjʊlɛrɪs / (AmE), / ˈviːnə tɛsˌtɪkjuːˈlæːrɪs / (BrE)
the vein that drains blood from the testicle, carrying it towards the main venous system
vein from the testicles
medical

Examples

The vena testicularis drains blood from the testis into the abdominal vein.
In cases of varicocele, the vena testicularis may become enlarged or twisted.
Damage to the vena testicularis during surgery can lead to complications with blood flow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: testicular vein - the English name for vena testicularis, vein draining the testicle

Etymology

From Latin 'vena' (vein) + 'testicularis' (relating to the testicles).
