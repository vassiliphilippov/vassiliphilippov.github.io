arachnophobia

1. NOUN

/ əˌræk.nəˈfoʊ.bi.ə / (AmE), / əˌræk.nəˈfəʊ.bi.ə / (BrE)
An extreme or irrational fear of spiders.
extreme fear of spiders

Examples

He suffers from arachnophobia and panics at the sight of even small spiders.
Many people with arachnophobia are unable to enter rooms where they believe spiders might be present.
Her arachnophobia made camping in the woods a terrifying experience.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: phobia - an extreme or irrational fear of or aversion to something
  • Degree Lower: fear - an unpleasant emotion caused by the threat of danger, pain, or harm

Etymology

From Greek 'arachne' (spider) + '-phobia' (fear).
