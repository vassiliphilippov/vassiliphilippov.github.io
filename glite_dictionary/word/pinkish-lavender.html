pinkish-lavender

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɪŋkɪʃ ˈlævəndɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋkɪʃ ˈlævəndə / (BrE)
A color of lavender with a noticeable pink tinge.
lavender color tinged with pink

Examples

The dress was a soft shade of pinkish-lavender that caught the light beautifully.
She painted her bedroom walls a warm pinkish-lavender to create a calming atmosphere.
Blushes of pinkish-lavender appeared in the evening sky as the sun set.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: lavender - a pale purple color
  • Degree Lower: pinkish-purple - a purple color tinged with pink
