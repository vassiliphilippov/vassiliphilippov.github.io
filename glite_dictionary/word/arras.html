arras

1. NOUN

/ ˈær.əs / (AmE), / ˈær.əs / (BrE)
a wall hanging made of heavy woven fabric, often decorated with pictorial designs, used especially as a tapestry for covering walls
decorative wall tapestry or hanging

Examples

The grand hall was adorned with an exquisite arras depicting scenes from ancient mythology.
A faded arras hung on the castle wall, adding to its medieval charm.
They uncovered a hidden doorway behind the heavy arras during the renovation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tapestry - a piece of thick textile fabric with pictures or designs used as a wall hanging

Etymology

From the French town of Arras, known for its tapestry production.
