agriculture

1. NOUN

/ ˈæɡ.rɪˌkʌl.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈæɡ.rɪˌkʌl.tʃə / (BrE)
The science or practice of cultivating land and raising crops or livestock.
the science of farming

Examples

Many countries depend heavily on agriculture for their economic growth.
Advances in technology have revolutionized agriculture in the past century.
Sustainable agriculture aims to preserve the environment while meeting food needs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farming - the activity or business of growing crops and raising animals
  • Synonym: horticulture - the cultivation of gardens or orchards

Etymology

From Latin 'agricultura', from 'ager' (field) + 'cultura' (cultivation).
