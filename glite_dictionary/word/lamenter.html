lamenter

1. NOUN

/ ləˈmɛntɚ / (AmE), / ləˈmɛntə / (BrE)
a person who expresses grief or sorrow, especially over someone's death
person expressing grief or sorrow

Examples

The lamenter wept quietly in the corner during the funeral service.
Many lamenters gathered around the grave to pay their last respects.
A professional lamenter was hired to sing songs of sorrow at the memorial.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mourner - a person who attends a funeral or expresses grief over a death

Etymology

From Latin 'lamentari' (to wail, mourn).
