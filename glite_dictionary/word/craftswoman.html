craftswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkræftsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈkrɑːftsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who is highly skilled in a craft, especially the manual arts.
a skilled female artisan

Examples

The craftswoman displayed her handwoven baskets at the local market.
As a talented craftswoman, she spent years perfecting her skills in pottery.
The museum featured an exhibit by a renowned craftswoman specializing in stained glass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: craftsman - a man who is highly skilled in a craft, especially the manual arts

Etymology

Formed from 'craftsman' by replacing 'man' with 'woman'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkræftsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈkrɑːftsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who is skilled in a particular craft or handicraft.
female skilled craft worker

Examples

The craftswoman spent years perfecting her pottery techniques.
Each piece in the gallery was handcrafted by a skilled craftswoman.
She is a talented craftswoman known for her exquisite embroidery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: craftsman - a skilled male worker in a particular trade or handicraft

Etymology

From 'craftsman' and 'woman'. 'Craftsman' from Old English 'cræft' (skill, strength) + 'man' (person).
