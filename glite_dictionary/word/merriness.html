merriness

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛr.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmɛr.i.nɪs / (BrE)
a cheerful and lively quality; the state or trait of being full of joy, humor, and high spirits
cheerful and lively quality

Examples

Her merriness made every holiday gathering much more enjoyable for everyone.
Despite the rainy weather, his merriness brightened the whole trip.
The children’s merriness was contagious at the birthday party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheerfulness - the quality of being happy and optimistic
  • Antonym: gloominess - the quality of being sad or depressed

Etymology

From the adjective 'merry' + the noun-forming suffix '-ness'.
