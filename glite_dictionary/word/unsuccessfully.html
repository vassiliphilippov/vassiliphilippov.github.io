unsuccessfully

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.səkˈsɛs.fə.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.səkˈsɛs.fəli / (BrE)
In a way that does not achieve the desired result or success.
in a way that is not successful

Examples

She tried unsuccessfully to open the locked door.
He unsuccessfully attempted to fix the computer before calling for help.
The team unsuccessfully appealed the referee's decision.

Synset Examples

she tried unsuccessfully to persuade him to buy a new car

Related Terms

  • Antonym: successfully - in a way that achieves the desired result or success

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'successful' + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
