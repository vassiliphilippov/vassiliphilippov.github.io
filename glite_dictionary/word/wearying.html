wearying

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪriɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪəriɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing tiredness or exhaustion; making someone feel weary.
causing tiredness or exhaustion

Examples

The long, wearying journey left the hikers desperate for rest.
She found the endless meetings both wearying and frustrating.
It was a wearying experience to argue with him over every detail.

Synset Examples

an exhausting march
the visit was especially wearing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiring - causing someone to feel tired
  • Synonym: exhausting - making someone feel extremely tired

Etymology

From 'weary' (tired) + '-ing' (forming adjectives indicating causing an effect).
