Fornax

1. PROPN

/ ˈfɔr.næks / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.næks / (BrE)
a faint constellation in the Southern Hemisphere, located near Cetus and Phoenix.
constellation in the Southern Hemisphere

Examples

Fornax is not easily visible to the naked eye due to its dim stars.
The telescope was positioned to observe galaxies within the Fornax constellation.
Astronomers discovered a new dwarf galaxy in Fornax last year.

Etymology

From Latin 'fornax' meaning 'furnace', named by the French astronomer Nicolas Louis de Lacaille in the 18th century.
