ambitionless

1. ADJ

/ æmˈbɪʃənləs / (AmE), / æmˈbɪʃənləs / (BrE)
having little or no desire to achieve success or advance; lacking ambition
lacking ambition or drive

Examples

The ambitionless employee showed no interest in pursuing promotions or new responsibilities.
She worried that her ambitionless attitude would prevent her from achieving her goals.
His parents were concerned about his ambitionless lifestyle after graduation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unambitious - having or showing little or no ambition
  • Antonym: ambitious - having a strong desire and determination to succeed
