Mr

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪstər / (AmE), / ˈmɪstə / (BrE)
A title used before a man's surname or full name as a polite or formal form of address.
title for a man

Examples

Please hand these forms to Mr Smith at the front desk.
At the meeting, Mr Johnson introduced the new project manager.
The letter was addressed to Mr Robert Wilson.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Mrs - title for a married woman
  • Antonym: Ms - title for a woman, regardless of marital status
  • Antonym: Miss - title for an unmarried woman

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'Master' as a title of courtesy for men.
