danish

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪ.nɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ.nɪʃ / (BrE)
a light, sweet, yeast-raised pastry, often filled with fruits or cheese
sweet pastry with filling

Examples

She bought a cherry danish for breakfast at the bakery.
The café's cheese danish is especially popular with customers.
Would you like a danish with your coffee this morning?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pastry - a baked food made of dough, usually with a filling

Etymology

From Danish 'dansk' (Danish), because the pastry originated in Denmark.
