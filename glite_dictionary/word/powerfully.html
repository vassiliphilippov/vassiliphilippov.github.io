powerfully

1. ADV

/ ˈpaʊ.ɚ.fəli / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə.fəli / (BrE)
In a powerful or forceful manner; with great strength, influence, or effect.
in a strong or forceful way

Examples

The orchestra performed the finale powerfully, leaving the audience in awe.
The new policy was powerfully advocated by several influential senators.
The waves crashed powerfully against the rocky shoreline during the storm.

Synset Examples

the federal government replaced the powerfully pro-settler Sir Godfrey Huggins with the even tougher and more determined ex-trade unionist

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strongly - in a strong manner
  • Antonym: weakly - in a weak manner
