indecently

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈdisəntli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdiːsəntli / (BrE)
in a way that does not conform to accepted standards of morality or decency; in an improper or offensive manner
in an improper or offensive manner

Examples

She was indecently dressed at the party, attracting a lot of attention.
The comedian's jokes were considered indecently vulgar for a family show.
He behaved indecently in public, shocking the other bystanders.

Synset Examples

she was rather indecently dressed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: decently - in a decent or proper manner
  • Synonym: improperly - in an improper manner

Etymology

Formed from 'indecent' + '-ly', indicating manner.
