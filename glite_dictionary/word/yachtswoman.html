yachtswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɑtsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈjɒtsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who owns or sails a yacht, especially as a sport or hobby
female yacht owner or sailor

Examples

The yachtswoman skillfully navigated her boat through the rough coastal waters.
As an accomplished yachtswoman, she has won several international regattas.
The club welcomed its first female yachtswoman as a member this year.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: yachtsman - a man who owns or sails a yacht

Etymology

Formed from 'yacht' (from Dutch 'jacht') + 'woman'.
