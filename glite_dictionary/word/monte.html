monte

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːn.teɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɒn.teɪ / (BrE)
A gambling card game of Spanish origin in which players bet that one of three face-up cards will be matched before the others as cards are dealt from the pack.
a Spanish card gambling game

Examples

The travelers spent the evening playing monte around the campfire.
He lost his savings after a reckless night of monte.
Stories of old Western saloons often mention games of monte and poker.

Etymology

From Spanish 'monte' (mountain), the name of the game.
