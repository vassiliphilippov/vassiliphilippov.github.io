innovator

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnəˌveɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɪnəveɪtə / (BrE)
a person who introduces new ideas, methods, or inventions
person who introduces new ideas or inventions

Examples

Elon Musk is widely regarded as an innovator in the fields of technology and transportation.
The company's success was largely due to its team of brilliant innovators.
She saw herself as an innovator, always seeking new solutions to complex problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pioneer - a person who is among the first to explore or develop a new area
  • Antonym: traditionalist - a person who adheres to tradition and resists change

Etymology

From Latin 'innovare' (to renew).
