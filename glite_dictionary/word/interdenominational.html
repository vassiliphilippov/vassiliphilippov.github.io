interdenominational

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərdɪˌnɑməˈneɪʃənl / (AmE), / ˌɪntədɪˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃənəl / (BrE)
involving, relating to, or shared by different religious denominations or churches
involving different religious denominations

Examples

The conference was an interdenominational event attended by leaders from several Christian traditions.
An interdenominational choir performed at the service, representing various churches in the community.
They launched an interdenominational charity to support families regardless of religious affiliation.

Synset Examples

interchurch aid
interdenominational cooperation between Methodists and Presbyterians

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecumenical - representing or promoting unity among the world's Christian churches

Etymology

From inter- (between) + denominational, referring to religious denominations.
