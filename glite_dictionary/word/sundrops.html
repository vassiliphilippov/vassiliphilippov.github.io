sundrops

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌnˌdrɑps / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌdrɒps / (BrE)
A day-flowering biennial or perennial plant of the genus Oenothera, noted for its bright yellow blossoms.
a yellow-flowered plant of the genus Oenothera

Examples

The vibrant sundrops in her garden bloom early each morning.
We planted several sundrops along the pathway for their bright yellow color.
Native bees are especially attracted to the flowers of the sundrops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evening primrose - a related plant in the genus Oenothera, typically opening in the evening

Etymology

From 'sun' + 'drops', referring to the plant's bright, sunny yellow flowers.
