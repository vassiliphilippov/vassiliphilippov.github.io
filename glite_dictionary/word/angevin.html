Angevin

1. NOUN

/ ˈændʒəvɪn / (AmE), / ˈan(d)ʒɪvɪn / (BrE)
a person who is a native or resident of Anjou, a historical region in western France
native or resident of Anjou, France

Examples

The medieval castle was once home to an influential Angevin noble family.
Several Angevins contributed to the cultural development of the French court.
As an Angevin, she often shared stories about her hometown's unique traditions.

Etymology

From French Angevin, from Anjou + -in (denoting origin or association).
