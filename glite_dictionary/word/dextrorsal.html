dextrorsal

1. ADJ

/ ˌdɛksˈtroʊr.səl / (AmE), / ˌdɛksˈtrɔː.səl / (BrE)
(of a spiral or curve, especially in a plant) spiraling upward or outward from left to right.
spiraling from left to right (botany)

Examples

The dextrorsal arrangement of the leaves is characteristic of this vine species.
Botanists observed a dextrorsal twist in the plant's tendrils as they climbed the trellis.
The shell displays a clearly marked dextrorsal spiral pattern, easily distinguishable from its relatives.

Synset Examples

dextrorse vines

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sinistrorsal - spiraling upward from right to left (opposite direction in botany)

Etymology

From Latin 'dexter' (right) and '-al', via 'dextrorse'.
