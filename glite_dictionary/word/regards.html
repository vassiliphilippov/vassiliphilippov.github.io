regards

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈɡɑrdz / (AmE), / rɪˈɡɑːdz / (BrE)
(usually plural) a polite expression conveying best wishes to someone, often sent through another person or included in correspondence.
polite wishes or greetings (in plural)

Examples

Please give my regards to your family when you see them.
He ended the letter with his warmest regards.
Send my regards to everyone at the office.

Synset Examples

give him my kind regards
my best wishes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: best wishes - an expression of good intentions or hopes for someone's welfare
  • Synonym: greetings - a polite word or sign of welcome or recognition
