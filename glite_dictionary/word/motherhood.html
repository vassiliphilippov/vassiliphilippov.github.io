motherhood

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌðɚˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈmʌðəhʊd / (BrE)
the state or experience of being a mother, especially in relation to her child or children
the state of being a mother

Examples

She found great joy in motherhood after the birth of her first child.
The challenges of motherhood are different for every woman.
For many, motherhood is a life-changing experience.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fatherhood - the state of being a father
  • Synonym: parenthood - the state of being a parent

Etymology

From Middle English 'moderhod', from 'moder' (mother) + '-hood' (state, condition).
