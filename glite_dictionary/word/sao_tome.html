Sao Tome

1. PROPN

/ saʊ toʊˈmeɪ / (AmE), / saʊ təʊˈmeɪ / (BrE)
the capital city of Sao Tome and Principe, located on the island of Sao Tome in the Gulf of Guinea, West Africa
capital city of Sao Tome and Principe

Examples

We spent a week exploring the markets and colonial architecture of Sao Tome.
Trade agreements have boosted economic growth in Sao Tome in recent years.
Flights to Sao Tome are available from several major African cities.

Etymology

From Portuguese São Tomé, meaning 'Saint Thomas'.
