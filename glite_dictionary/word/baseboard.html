baseboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːd / (BrE)
A narrow wooden or plastic board covering the joint between an interior wall and the floor.
molding covering wall-floor joint

Examples

The new house had white baseboards that contrasted with the dark wooden floors.
He accidentally scratched the baseboard while moving the furniture into the living room.
Dust tends to gather along the baseboard and needs to be cleaned regularly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skirting board - a similar molding covering the joint between floor and wall, chiefly British term
