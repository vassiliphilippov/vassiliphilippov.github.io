unappreciatively

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.əˈpriː.ʃətɪv.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈpriː.ʃətɪv.li / (BrE)
In an ungrateful or unthankful manner; without showing appreciation.
in an ungrateful manner

Examples

He accepted their help unappreciatively, never once saying thank you.
She glanced unappreciatively at the gifts, making everyone feel awkward.
The employees felt demotivated when their efforts were acknowledged so unappreciatively.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: gratefully - in a thankful manner
