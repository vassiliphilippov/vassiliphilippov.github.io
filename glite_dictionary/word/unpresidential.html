unpresidential

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.prɛz.ɪˈdɛn.ʃəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prɛz.ɪˈdɛn.ʃəl / (BrE)
(of behavior or actions) not characteristic of or suitable for a president; lacking the dignity, decorum, or appropriateness expected of a president.
not suitable for a president

Examples

His outburst during the debate was considered highly unpresidential by many observers.
Critics argued that the candidate's tweets were often unpresidential in tone and content.
Such unpresidential remarks led to public concern about his leadership style.

Synset Examples

very unpresidential behavior

Related Terms

  • Antonym: presidential - characteristic of or suitable for a president

Etymology

Formed by combining the prefix 'un-' (not) with 'presidential' (relating to a president).
