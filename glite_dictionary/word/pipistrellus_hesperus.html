Pipistrellus hesperus

1. PROPN

/ pɪpɪˈstrɛləs hɛˈspɪrəs / (AmE), / pɪpɪˈstrɛləs hɛˈspɪərəs / (BrE)
A small vesper bat species native to western North America; also known as the western pipistrelle.
species of bat native to western North America

Examples

Scientists discovered a colony of Pipistrellus hesperus living near the canyon walls.
The Pipistrellus hesperus is notable for its ability to hunt insects at dusk.
During their field trip, students observed several Pipistrellus hesperus in flight at sundown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: western pipistrelle - common name for Pipistrellus hesperus

Etymology

From Latin 'Pipistrellus' (a genus of bats) and 'hesperus' meaning 'western' or 'evening'.
