toughly

1. ADV

/ ˈtʌf.li / (AmE), / ˈtʌf.li / (BrE)
In a manner that is strong, determined, or showing toughness.
in a tough or forceful manner

Examples

She toughly defended her position during the heated debate.
The coach responded toughly to criticism from the media.
The novel is toughly realistic in its portrayal of hardship.

Synset Examples

toughly vigorous story-telling

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tough - strong and able to withstand hardship
  • Antonym: gently - in a mild or soft manner
