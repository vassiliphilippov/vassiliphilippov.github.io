Czechness

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛk.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛk.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being Czech, especially in terms of national identity, culture, or characteristics.
quality of being Czech

Examples

Many people in the diaspora try to preserve their Czechness through traditional customs and language.
The festival celebrated the unique Czechness of the community, from music to cuisine.
She struggled with her Czechness while adapting to life in a new country.

Synset Examples

There's more Czechness in Brno than in Prague, says American academic now living in the Czech Republic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Czech - A person from the Czech Republic or relating to the Czech people or language

Etymology

Formed from Czech (relating to the Czech Republic or its people) + -ness (forming nouns indicating a state or quality).
