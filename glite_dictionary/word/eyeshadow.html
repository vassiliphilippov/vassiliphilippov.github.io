eyeshadow

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪˌʃædoʊ / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌʃædəʊ / (BrE)
a colored cosmetic applied to the eyelids or under the eyes to enhance or alter their appearance
cosmetic for coloring eyelids

Examples

She applied a shimmering eyeshadow to highlight her eyes for the evening party.
The new palette features eyeshadow shades perfect for any skin tone.
Many makeup artists recommend blending eyeshadow carefully for a natural look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mascara - cosmetic for darkening and thickening eyelashes
  • Synonym: blush - cosmetic used to color the cheeks

Etymology

From 'eye' + 'shadow', indicating a substance that shades the eyes.
