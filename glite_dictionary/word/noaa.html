NOAA

1. PROPN

/ ˈnoʊ.ə / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ.ə / (BrE)
The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration, a US government agency responsible for monitoring the oceans and atmosphere, predicting weather and natural disasters, and conserving marine resources.
US agency for weather and ocean monitoring
american

Examples

The NOAA has issued a severe weather warning for the coastal region.
Scientists from NOAA track hurricanes to help ensure public safety.
Latest climate data were published by NOAA on their official website.

Etymology

Acronym of National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration.
