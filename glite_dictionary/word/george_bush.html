George Bush

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɔrdʒ bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒ bʊʃ / (BrE)
(1924–2018) The 41st President of the United States (1989–1993) and Vice President under Ronald Reagan (1981–1989).
41st U.S. President (1989–1993); Vice President under Reagan

Examples

George Bush served as the 41st President of the United States from 1989 to 1993.
As Vice President, George Bush played a significant role in foreign policy decisions during the Reagan administration.
Many historians evaluate the Gulf War as a defining moment in George Bush's presidency.
