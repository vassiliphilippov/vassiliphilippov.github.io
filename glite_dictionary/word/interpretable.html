interpretable

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈtɜːrprətəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːprɪtəbəl / (BrE)
that can be understood or explained; capable of being interpreted
able to be understood or explained

Examples

The results from the experiment were interpretable by existing theories.
Her handwriting is barely interpretable, making it difficult to read her notes.
The legal document was written in clear, interpretable language.

Synset Examples

explainable phenomena

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unintelligible - impossible to understand or interpret
  • Synonym: comprehensible - able to be understood; intelligible

Etymology

From interpret + -able, dating to the mid 19th century.
