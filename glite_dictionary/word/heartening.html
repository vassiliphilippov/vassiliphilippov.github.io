heartening

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑːr.tən.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɑː.tən.ɪŋ / (BrE)
making you feel more cheerful or hopeful; providing encouragement.
encouraging or uplifting

Examples

The news of the rescue was truly heartening to everyone involved.
It is heartening to see the community come together in support of those in need.
Her heartening words gave me the confidence to keep going.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: discouraging - causing someone to feel less confident or hopeful
  • Synonym: encouraging - giving someone support or confidence

Etymology

From the verb 'hearten', itself from Middle English 'herten', meaning 'to encourage'.
