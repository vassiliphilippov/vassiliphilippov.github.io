definiteness

1. NOUN

/ ˈdəˈfɪnɪtnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈfɪnɪtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being definite, certain, or clearly defined; the state of being predictable with great confidence
quality of being definite or certain

Examples

The definiteness of his answer left no room for doubt among the audience.
Scientists value definiteness in experimental results to support their hypotheses.
Her instructions were marked by their definiteness, making the process easy to follow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certainty - the quality of being reliably true or certain
  • Antonym: indefiniteness - the quality of being not clearly defined or certain

Etymology

From definite + -ness, after Middle English definit (definite) and -ness (state, quality).
