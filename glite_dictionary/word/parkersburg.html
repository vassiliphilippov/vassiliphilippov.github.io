Parkersburg

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɑrkərzˌbɝɡ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkəzˌbɜːɡ / (BrE)
a city in northwestern West Virginia, situated on the Ohio River
city in West Virginia, USA

Examples

I visited Parkersburg last summer and enjoyed walking along the Ohio River.
Parkersburg has a rich history dating back to the 19th century.
Many people travel through Parkersburg on their way to other parts of West Virginia.

Etymology

Named after Alexander Parker, an early settler.
