spool

1. VERB

/ spuːl / (AmE), / spuːl / (BrE)
to wind thread, wire, tape, or similar material onto a spool or reel
wind onto a spool or reel

Examples

The machine automatically spools the thread onto the bobbin.
He carefully spooled the fishing line to avoid tangles.
Before playback, the audio tape must be spooled onto the reel.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unspool - to unwind or remove from a spool or reel

Etymology

From Middle English 'spole', from Middle Dutch 'spoele', relating to the noun 'spool'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01525666-v
Synset POS: v

2. NOUN

/ spuːl / (AmE), / spuːl / (BrE)
a cylindrical object around which thread, tape, film, or other flexible material can be wound for storage or use
cylindrical holder for flexible material

Examples

She wound the leftover thread onto a spool to keep it neat.
A film spool was jammed inside the old camera.
The sewing kit includes a spool of white cotton.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reel - a cylinder on which film, wire, thread, or other flexible material can be wound
  • Synonym: bobbin - a cylinder or cone holding thread, yarn, or wire, used especially in sewing machines

Etymology

From Middle English 'spole', from Middle Dutch 'spoel' (reel, bobbin).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02863755-n
Synset POS: n

3. VERB

/ spuːl / (AmE), / spuːl / (BrE)
(computing) to transfer data that is to be sent to a peripheral device, especially a printer, into temporary storage so it can be processed sequentially at the appropriate time.
transfer data to temporary storage (computing)

Examples

The system will spool your print job until the printer is ready to process it.
When too many users spool files at once, the print queue can become overloaded.
The print server automatically spools documents before sending them to the device.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: queue - to arrange data or tasks in a sequence to be processed

Etymology

From abbreviation of 'Simultaneous Peripheral Operations On-Line', used in computing.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02238315-v
Synset POS: v
