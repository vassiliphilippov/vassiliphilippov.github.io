Honoré-Gabriel Victor Riqueti

1. PROPN

/ ɔnɔːˈreɪ ɡæˈbriːəl vɪkˈtɔr rɪˈkɛti / (AmE), / ɔnɔːˈreɪ ɡaːˈbriːəl vɪkˈtɔː rɪˈkɛti / (BrE)
A French statesman and orator prominent in the early days of the French Revolution (1749-1791).
French revolutionary statesman (1749-1791)

Examples

Honoré-Gabriel Victor Riqueti played a crucial role in the formation of the National Assembly during the French Revolution.
Many historians credit Honoré-Gabriel Victor Riqueti with influencing the early direction of the revolutionary movement.
Books about the French Revolution often include detailed accounts of Honoré-Gabriel Victor Riqueti's speeches and actions.
