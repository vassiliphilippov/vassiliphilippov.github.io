Charlestown

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʃɑrlztaʊn / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːlztaʊn / (BrE)
a historic neighborhood in Boston, Massachusetts, formerly a separate town established in 1629
neighborhood of Boston, Massachusetts

Examples

Many tourists visit Charlestown to see the Bunker Hill Monument and other historical sites.
They bought a house in Charlestown because of its charming streets and rich history.
During the annual parade, the streets of Charlestown are filled with local residents and visitors.
