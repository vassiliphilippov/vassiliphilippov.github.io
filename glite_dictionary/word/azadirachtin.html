azadirachtin

1. NOUN

/ əzəˈdɪrəktɪn / (AmE), / əzəˈdɪərəktɪn / (BrE)
a natural compound found in the neem tree used as an insecticide to control pests
natural insecticidal compound
medical

Examples

Azadirachtin is extracted from neem seeds and is widely recognized for its effectiveness against agricultural pests.
Farmers often use azadirachtin as a safer, more environmentally friendly alternative to synthetic insecticides.
Research continues into how azadirachtin disrupts the life cycle of harmful insects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insecticide - a substance used for killing insects

Etymology

From the scientific name of the neem tree, Azadirachta indica, from which it is derived.
