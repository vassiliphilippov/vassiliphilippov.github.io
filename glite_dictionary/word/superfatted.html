superfatted

1. ADJ

/ ˌsuːpərˈfætɪd / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈfætɪd / (BrE)
(of soap or cosmetic products) containing extra unreacted fat above the amount required for saponification, making it gentler or more moisturizing.
containing extra unreacted fat (esp. in soap)

Examples

Dermatologists often recommend superfatted soaps for people with sensitive or dry skin.
The superfatted lotion helped keep my hands moisturized during winter.
She chose a superfatted bar of soap because it was less drying than regular soap.

Synset Examples

superfatted toilet soaps

Etymology

Formed from 'super-' (above, extra) and 'fatted', relating to added fat.
