kennel

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛnəl / (AmE), / ˈkɛnəl / (BrE)
a small building or shelter where a dog is kept
dog shelter or house

Examples

The dog slept peacefully in his kennel every night.
We built a new kennel in the backyard for our puppy.
Some shelters have a separate kennel for each animal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doghouse - a small house for a dog, usually outside

Etymology

From Old French *kenil*, from Medieval Latin *canile* (doghouse), from Latin *canis* (dog).

2. VERB

/ ˈkɛnəl / (AmE), / ˈkɛnəl / (BrE)
to put (a dog or other animal) in a kennel, especially temporarily
to put an animal in a kennel

Examples

We decided to kennel our dog while we were on holiday.
The shelter will kennel abandoned animals until they find homes.
If you need to travel, it's important to kennel your pets in a reputable facility.

Synset Examples

kennel a dog

Related Terms

  • Synonym: house - to provide accommodation or shelter for an animal
