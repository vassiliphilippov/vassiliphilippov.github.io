Mariehamn

1. PROPN

/ məˈriː.əˌhæm / (AmE), / məˈriː.əˌham / (BrE)
the capital and chief port of the Åland Islands, an autonomous region of Finland, located in the Baltic Sea
capital and port of the Åland Islands

Examples

The ferry docks daily at Mariehamn, bringing tourists to the Åland Islands.
Mariehamn is known for its maritime heritage and beautiful archipelago.
During our trip, we spent two nights exploring the sights of Mariehamn.

Etymology

From Swedish, meaning 'Mary's harbour' (Maria + hamn).
