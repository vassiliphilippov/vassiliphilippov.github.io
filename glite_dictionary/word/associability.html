associability

1. NOUN

/ əˌsoʊsiəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / əˌsəʊsiəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or condition of being easily associated, joined, or connected in thought or meaning
quality of being easily associated

Examples

The associability of these concepts makes them easy to remember as a group.
Color can affect the associability between different ideas in branding.
Researchers measured the associability of different sensory cues in their experiment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: association - the process of linking things together in thought or experience

Etymology

From associate + -ability, patterned after similar formations like probability, compatibility.
