circumscribed

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɜrkəmˌskraɪbd / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkəmskraɪbd / (BrE)
Restricted or limited within boundaries or to a particular area or scope.
restricted; limited within boundaries

Examples

The patient's movement was circumscribed by the cast on his leg.
Their authority was strictly circumscribed by the new regulations.
Her responsibilities in the company are circumscribed to financial matters only.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restricted - Limited in extent or scope
  • Antonym: unlimited - Without limits or boundaries

Etymology

From Latin circumscribere, meaning 'to draw around, limit'.
