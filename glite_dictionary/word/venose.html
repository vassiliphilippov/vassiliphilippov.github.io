venose

1. ADJ

/ ˈviːnoʊs / (AmE), / ˈviːnəʊs / (BrE)
marked with or having markings that resemble veins, especially as seen in leaves or insect wings
having vein-like markings

Examples

The leaf's venose pattern made it easy to identify the species.
She admired the venose texture on the butterfly's wings under the microscope.
The painting featured a venose network running across the marble surface.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: veined - having veins or markings like veins

Etymology

From Latin 'venosus', meaning full of veins.
