rending

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a sound) resembling or suggestive of violent tearing or splitting.
sound of violent tearing or splitting

Examples

The rending crash of thunder echoed through the valley after the lightning struck.
She was startled by the rending noise of the metal door being forced open.
A rending scream pierced the night, chilling everyone standing nearby.

Synset Examples

the tree split with a great ripping sound
heard a rending roar as the crowd surged forward

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tearing - making a loud or violent ripping sound
  • Synonym: splitting - breaking apart forcefully, especially with a loud sound

Etymology

Present participle of 'rend', from Old English *rendan* (to tear).
