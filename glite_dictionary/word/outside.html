outside

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈaʊtsaɪd / (BrE)
the external side or surface of something; the outer part or area that is beyond or away from the inside
outer part or surface

Examples

She painted the outside of the house a bright yellow.
Please leave your shoes on the outside before entering.
From the outside, the building looks quite ordinary.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inside - the inner part or surface of something

Etymology

From Old English 'ūt(e)', meaning 'out' + 'side'.
