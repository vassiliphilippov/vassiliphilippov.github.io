assiduous

1. ADJ

/ əˈsɪdʒuəs / (AmE), / əˈsɪdjʊəs / (BrE)
showing great care, attention, and persistent effort; hardworking and diligent
showing persistent care and effort

Examples

Her assiduous research resulted in ground-breaking discoveries in her field.
He is known for his assiduous attention to detail in every project he undertakes.
With assiduous study, she mastered the complexities of the new language.

Synset Examples

her assiduous attempts to learn French
assiduous research
sedulous pursuit of legal and moral principles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diligent - showing careful and persistent effort or work
  • Synonym: industrious - constantly, regularly, or habitually active or occupied
  • Antonym: lazy - unwilling to work or use energy

Etymology

From Latin assiduus (constant, persistent), from assidēre (to attend to).
