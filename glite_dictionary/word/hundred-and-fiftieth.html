hundred-and-fiftieth

1. ADJ

/ ˌhʌndrəd ənd ˈfɪf.ti.əθ / (AmE), / ˌhʌndrɪd ən ˈfɪf.ti.əθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number corresponding to one hundred fifty, used to indicate position in a sequence.
ordinal number for 150th

Examples

This year marks the hundred-and-fiftieth anniversary of the city's founding.
He finished in hundred-and-fiftieth place in the marathon, much to his surprise.
The museum held a special exhibition for its hundred-and-fiftieth year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one hundred fiftieth - alternative form of 'hundred-and-fiftieth', ordinal number for 150th
