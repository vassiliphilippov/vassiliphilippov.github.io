bighearted

1. ADJ

/ ˌbɪɡˈhɑːrtɪd / (AmE), / ˌbɪɡˈhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
Kind and generous; willing to give freely and help others.
generous; kind and giving

Examples

Her bighearted nature made her popular among her friends and neighbors.
The company was praised for its bighearted donations to local charities.
He acted in a bighearted way, always ready to help those in need.

Synset Examples

was a big tipper
the bounteous goodness of God
bountiful compliments
a freehanded host
a handsome allowance
Saturday's child is loving and giving
a liberal backer of the arts
a munificent gift
her fond and openhanded grandfather

Related Terms

  • Synonym: generous - willing to give or share unselfishly
  • Antonym: stingy - unwilling to give or spend; ungenerous

Etymology

Formed from 'big' + 'hearted', reflecting the idea of having a 'large heart' in a figurative sense.
