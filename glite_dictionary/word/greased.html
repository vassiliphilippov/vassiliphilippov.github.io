greased

1. ADJ

/ ɡrist / (AmE), / ɡriːst / (BrE)
covered with oil or grease, typically to reduce friction or allow smoother movement.
covered with oil or grease

Examples

The mechanic made sure all the gears were greased before reassembling the engine.
Her hair looked greased back for the play's 1950s scene.
To slide smoothly through the pipe, the cable was heavily greased.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lubricated - made slippery or smooth by the application of a substance like oil or grease
