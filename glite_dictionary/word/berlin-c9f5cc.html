berlin

1. NOUN

/ bərˈlɪn / (AmE), / bəˈlɪn / (BrE)
a type of limousine or carriage with a glass partition between the front and back seats, originally designed for comfort and privacy
limousine or carriage with a glass partition

Examples

The dignitaries arrived at the palace in a luxurious berlin that gleamed in the sunlight.
The museum's collection includes an antique berlin used by European royalty.
He admired the craftsmanship of the berlin, especially its elegant glass partition.

Etymology

From French 'berline', named after Berlin, the capital of Germany, where this style was first made.
