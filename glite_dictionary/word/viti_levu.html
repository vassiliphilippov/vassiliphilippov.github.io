Viti Levu

1. PROPN

/ ˌviːti ˈleɪvuː / (AmE), / ˌviːti ˈlɛvuː / (BrE)
the largest volcanic island in Fiji, situated in the South Pacific Ocean.
largest island of Fiji

Examples

The city of Suva, located on Viti Levu, is the capital of Fiji.
Tourists flock to Viti Levu for its beautiful beaches and coral reefs.
After the storm, several villages on Viti Levu needed assistance with rebuilding.
