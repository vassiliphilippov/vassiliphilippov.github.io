vii

1. NUM

/ viːˈaɪ ˈaɪ / (AmE), / viːˈaɪ ˈaɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for seven (7)
Roman numeral for seven

Examples

The clock struck vii as the ceremony began.
His office is on floor vii of the building.
The document is divided into sections I to vii.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seven - the number 7

Etymology

From Latin, where 'VII' is formed by combining 'V' (5) and 'II' (2) to represent 7.
