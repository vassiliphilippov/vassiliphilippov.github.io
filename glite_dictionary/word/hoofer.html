hoofer

1. NOUN

/ ˈhuːfər / (AmE), / ˈhuːfə / (BrE)
A professional dancer, especially one who specializes in tap dancing.
professional dancer, often in tap
informal

Examples

As a hoofer, she captivated audiences with her energetic tap routines.
Many Broadway stars started out as hoofers in small nightclubs.
He dreamed of becoming a famous hoofer like Fred Astaire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dancer - a person who dances or performs dances

Etymology

From the verb 'hoof' (to dance), originally slang for the act of dancing with one's feet (hoofing).
