Melanesian

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɛləˈniʒən / (AmE), / ˌmɛləˈniːʒən / (BrE)
relating to Melanesia, its people, or its culture
of Melanesia or its people/culture

Examples

The Melanesian languages are spoken across several Pacific islands.
She is fascinated by Melanesian art and traditions.
A Melanesian mask was displayed in the museum's Pacific collection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Polynesian - relating to Polynesia, its people, or its culture
  • Synonym: Micronesian - relating to Micronesia, its people, or its culture

Etymology

From 'Melanesia', itself from Greek 'melas' (black) and 'nēsos' (island).
