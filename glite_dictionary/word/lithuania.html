Lithuania

1. PROPN

/ lɪˌθuːˈeɪniə / (AmE), / lɪˌθjuːˈeɪniə / (BrE)
a country in northeastern Europe, situated on the Baltic Sea, with Vilnius as its capital
country in northeastern Europe, capital Vilnius

Examples

She traveled through Lithuania last summer and visited several historic towns.
The architecture in Lithuania reflects its complex history and cultural influences.
After gaining independence in 1990, Lithuania joined the European Union in 2004.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Latvia - a neighboring country to Lithuania, also on the Baltic Sea
  • Synonym: Estonia - a neighboring Baltic state north of Latvia
  • Synonym: Vilnius - the capital city of Lithuania

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'Lituania', from Lithuanian 'Lietuva'.
