interracially

1. ADV

/ ˌɪntəˈreɪʃəli / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈreɪʃəli / (BrE)
In a manner involving or relating to people of different racial backgrounds.
involving different races (adverb)

Examples

The couple decided to marry interracially, despite societal pressure.
New policies helped schools operate interracially for the first time.
The play explores families living interracially in urban neighborhoods.

Synset Examples

interracially restrictive

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interracial - involving or related to people of different races

Etymology

From 'interracial' (from Latin 'inter-' meaning 'between' + 'racial') + '-ly' adverbial suffix.
