Sibelius

1. PROPN

/ sɪˈbeɪliəs / (AmE), / sɪˈbeɪliəs / (BrE)
(1865–1957) A Finnish composer known for his symphonies and contributions to 20th-century classical music.
Finnish composer (1865–1957)

Examples

Many orchestras still perform Sibelius's Symphony No. 2 to sold-out crowds.
The music of Sibelius reflects elements of Finnish nationalism and nature.
She wrote her dissertation on the influence of Sibelius on modern composers.
