defectively

1. ADV

/ dɪˈfɛktɪvli / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛktɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that has defects; in a faulty or imperfect manner.
in a faulty or imperfect manner

Examples

The system was installed defectively, causing frequent malfunctions.
The packaging was sealed defectively, allowing the contents to spill out.
The printer operates defectively and often jams when in use.

Synset Examples

this machine functions only defectively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faultily - in a faulty manner

Etymology

From 'defective' + '-ly', where 'defective' comes from Latin 'defectivus' (lacking, failing).
