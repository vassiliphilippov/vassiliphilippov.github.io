xvii

1. NUM

/ ɛks vi aɪ aɪ / (AmE), / ɛks vi aɪ aɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for seventeen (17)
Roman numeral for 17

Examples

The book's chapter is labeled xvii in Roman numerals.
He was born in the year xvii of the reign of the king.
The answer to question xvii was particularly challenging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seventeen - the number 17
  • Degree Lower: xvi - the Roman numeral for sixteen (16)
  • Degree Higher: xviii - the Roman numeral for eighteen (18)

Etymology

From Latin numerals: X (10) + V (5) + II (2), making 17.
