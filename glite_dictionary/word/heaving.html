heaving

1. NOUN

/ ˈhivɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhiːvɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of throwing or lifting something heavy with great effort
the act of throwing something heavy

Examples

With one powerful heaving, the sailors launched the lifeboat into the sea.
Each heaving of the stone required all their strength.
The contest measured the distance achieved in a single heaving of the heavy sack.

Synset Examples

he gave it a mighty heave
he was not good at heaving passes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heave - an act of lifting or throwing with effort

Etymology

From the verb 'heave', meaning to lift or throw with effort; Old English 'hebban' (to lift).

2. NOUN

/ ˈhiːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhiːvɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of lifting or pulling something with great effort
lifting with great effort

Examples

After much heaving, they finally managed to move the sofa upstairs.
The box was so heavy that it required some serious heaving to lift it.
His face turned red from the heaving of the loaded cart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lifting - raising something to a higher position
  • Synonym: hauling - pulling or dragging something with effort

Etymology

From the verb 'heave', which comes from Old English 'hebban' (to lift).

3. NOUN

/ ˈhiːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhiːvɪŋ / (BrE)
an upward or rhythmical rising and falling movement, especially of waves or the chest when breathing deeply
rhythmical rising and falling movement

Examples

The heaving of the ocean made the boat rock back and forth all night.
After the long run, his chest was in constant heaving.
The heaving of the crowd grew louder as excitement filled the stadium.

Synset Examples

the heaving of waves on a rough sea

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swell - a slow, regular movement up and down, especially in waves

Etymology

From the verb 'heave', meaning to lift or raise, of Old English origin.

4. NOUN

/ ˈhiːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhiːvɪŋ / (BrE)
heavy or labored breathing, especially after physical exertion or emotional stress.
heavy or labored breathing

Examples

She noticed the heaving of his chest as he tried to catch his breath.
After sprinting up the hill, his heaving was loud in the silent night.
The only sound in the room was her heaving after the intense workout.

Related Terms
