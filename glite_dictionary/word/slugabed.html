slugabed

1. NOUN

/ ˈslʌɡəˌbɛd / (AmE), / ˈslʌɡəbɛd / (BrE)
a person who stays in bed later than is usual or expected, especially in the morning
person who stays in bed late
archaic

Examples

My brother is a notorious slugabed, never rising before ten on weekends.
Don’t be such a slugabed; there is so much to do today.
His reputation as a slugabed makes him the butt of family jokes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: late riser - person who habitually gets up late

Etymology

From 'slug', meaning lazy person, and 'abed', meaning in bed.
