granulator

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡræn.jəˌleɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡran.jə.leɪ.tə / (BrE)
a machine that reduces materials such as plastics or organic matter into small granules or particles
machine that makes materials into granules

Examples

The factory installed a new granulator to process plastic waste efficiently.
After shredding, the material passes through a granulator for further size reduction.
The granulator quickly turned the discarded bottles into small, reusable pellets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crusher - a machine that breaks something into pieces

Etymology

From Latin 'granulum' (small grain) + English '-ator' (agent noun suffix).
