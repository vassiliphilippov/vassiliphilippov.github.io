stylishness

1. NOUN

/ ˈstaɪlɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlɪʃnəs / (BrE)
elegance or attractiveness resulting from being fashionable or following the latest styles.
fashionable elegance or style

Examples

The stylishness of her outfit caught everyone's attention at the party.
He redecorated his apartment with a stylishness that impressed his friends.
Her writing has a stylishness that sets it apart from others in the genre.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elegance - graceful and stylish appearance or manner
  • Synonym: tastefulness - showing good aesthetic judgment and appreciation of beauty
  • Antonym: drabness - the quality of being dull and lacking brightness

Etymology

From 'stylish' + '-ness', denoting the state or quality of being stylish.
