Winston-Salem

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪnstən ˈseɪləm / (AmE), / ˈwɪnstən ˈseɪləm / (BrE)
A city in north central North Carolina, United States, known for its historical role in tobacco and textile industries.
city in North Carolina, USA

Examples

The Wake Forest University campus is located in Winston-Salem.
She moved to Winston-Salem to start a new job in the tech sector.
Winston-Salem is famous for its rich history and vibrant arts community.

Etymology

Resulting from the merger of the towns of Winston and Salem in 1913.
