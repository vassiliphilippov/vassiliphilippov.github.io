Saphar

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːfɑr / (AmE), / ˈsɑːfɑː / (BrE)
The second month of the Islamic calendar.
second month of the Islamic calendar

Examples

Muslims observe significant events during Saphar, the second month of their calendar.
The Islamic month of Saphar often falls at a different time each Gregorian year.
Many traditions and customs are associated with Saphar in various Muslim communities.

Etymology

From Arabic Ṣafar (صَفَر‎), the name of the month in the Islamic calendar.
