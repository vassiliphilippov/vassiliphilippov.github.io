Peter Carl Fabergé

1. PROPN

/ ˈfiːbərˌʒeɪ / (AmE), / ˈfæbəʒeɪ / (BrE)
(1846–1920) A Russian goldsmith and jeweler famous for creating exquisite jeweled and enameled Easter eggs for European royalty.
Russian jeweler famous for Fabergé eggs

Examples

The famous jeweler Peter Carl Fabergé created luxurious Easter eggs for the Russian imperial family.
Many collectors still seek works by Peter Carl Fabergé for their exquisite craftsmanship.
The House of Peter Carl Fabergé became renowned for its elegant and intricate jewelry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Fabergé egg - ornate jeweled eggs created by Peter Carl Fabergé
