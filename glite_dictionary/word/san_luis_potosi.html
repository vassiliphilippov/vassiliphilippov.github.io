San Luis Potosi

1. PROPN

/ sæn lwiːs poʊtoʊˈsiː / (AmE), / san lwiː poʊtəʊˈsiː / (BrE)
a city in central Mexico, which is the capital of the state of San Luis Potosi
capital city in central Mexico

Examples

I spent a week exploring San Luis Potosi and its beautiful colonial architecture.
The university in San Luis Potosi attracts students from across Latin America.
She was born and raised in San Luis Potosi, Mexico.

Etymology

Spanish, named after Saint Louis (San Luis) and the silver mines of Potosí (after the city in Bolivia).
