lividly

1. ADV

/ ˈlɪv.ɪd.li / (AmE), / ˈlɪv.ɪd.li / (BrE)
In a manner showing extreme anger or a bluish-gray coloration.
in an extremely angry or discolored manner

Examples

She glared lividly at her coworker after hearing the unfair accusation.
The sky turned lividly dark before the storm began.
His face flushed lividly when he realized the mistake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angrily - in an angry manner

Etymology

From 'livid' + '-ly'. 'Livid' from Latin 'lividus' (bluish, lead-colored).
