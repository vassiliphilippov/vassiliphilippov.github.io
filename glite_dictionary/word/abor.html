Abor

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːbɔr / (AmE), / ˈɑːbɔː / (BrE)
the name given to a group of little-known Kamarupan languages of northeastern India, spoken by the Abor people.
group of little-known Kamarupan languages

Examples

Linguists have studied the Abor languages to better understand the history of the region.
The Abor languages are spoken by small communities in northeastern India.
Efforts are being made to document the Abor languages before they become extinct.

Etymology

From the name of the Abor people of northeastern India.
