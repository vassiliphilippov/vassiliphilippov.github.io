loti

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊti / (AmE), / ˈləʊti / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Lesotho, equal to 100 lisente
currency unit of Lesotho

Examples

The price of the souvenir was fifty loti.
She exchanged her dollars for loti at the airport.
The loti has remained relatively stable against the US dollar this year.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: lisente - a subunit of the loti, currency of Lesotho
  • Synonym: rand - currency of South Africa, commonly exchanged with the loti

Etymology

From Sesotho 'loti', meaning 'mountain'.
