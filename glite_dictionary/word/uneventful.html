uneventful

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪˈvɛntfəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈvɛntfʊl / (BrE)
(of a period of time or an event) having nothing unusual, exciting, or significant happen.
without noteworthy events

Examples

The journey was uneventful and passed quickly.
After an uneventful year, she decided to travel and seek adventure.
His stay in the hospital was relatively uneventful, with no complications.

Synset Examples

an uneventful life
the voyage was pleasant and uneventful
recovery was uneventful

Related Terms

  • Antonym: eventful - marked by interesting or significant happenings

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'eventful'.
