offensive

1. ADJ

/ əˈfɛnsɪv / (AmE), / əˈfɛnsɪv / (BrE)
intended for use in attacking rather than defending.
intended for attack, not defense

Examples

The army launched an offensive campaign early in the morning.
Nations sometimes develop offensive weapons to deter potential threats.
The coach focused on building a stronger offensive strategy for the team.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: defensive - intended to defend rather than attack

Etymology

From Latin offendēre (to attack, strike against), via French offensif.
