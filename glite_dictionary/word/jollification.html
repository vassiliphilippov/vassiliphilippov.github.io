jollification

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒɑːlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdʒɒlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a lively and cheerful celebration or festivity
merry celebration or festivity

Examples

The unexpected arrival of old friends turned the quiet evening into a real jollification.
Every New Year's Eve, the city square is filled with people enjoying the jollification.
After the exam, the students planned a jollification to celebrate the end of term.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: festivity - a celebration or joyful event
  • Antonym: solemnity - a formal, serious ceremony or observance

Etymology

From jollity (cheerfulness) + -fication (forming nouns of action or process).
