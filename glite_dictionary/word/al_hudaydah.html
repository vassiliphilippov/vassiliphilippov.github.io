Al Hudaydah

1. PROPN

/ æl huˈdaɪdə / (AmE), / æl huˈdaɪdə / (BrE)
A major port city in western Yemen situated on the Red Sea.
port city in Yemen

Examples

The port of Al Hudaydah plays a vital role in Yemen's economy and humanitarian aid supply.
Fierce fighting broke out in the outskirts of Al Hudaydah last week.
Many international organizations monitor the situation in Al Hudaydah due to its strategic importance.

Etymology

From Arabic الحديدة (al-Ḥudaydah), literally 'the small iron [place]'.
