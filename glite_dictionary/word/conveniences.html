conveniences

1. NOUN

/ kənˈvin.jəns.ɪz / (AmE), / kənˈviː.ni.əns.ɪz / (BrE)
things that make life more comfortable, easy, or pleasant, especially such as appliances, facilities, or services
things that provide comfort or ease

Examples

The apartment includes all modern conveniences, such as a dishwasher and central heating.
Travelers expect basic conveniences like clean bathrooms and Wi-Fi in hotels.
With so many conveniences available, daily chores have become much easier.

Synset Examples

all the comforts of home

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amenities - features that provide comfort and convenience
  • Degree Higher: luxuries - things adding comfort or pleasure not essential to life
  • Degree Lower: necessities - essential things needed for daily life

Etymology

From 'convenience', from Middle French 'convenience', from Latin 'convenientia' (agreement, fitness).
