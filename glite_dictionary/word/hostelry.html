hostelry

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊstəlri / (AmE), / ˈhɒstəlri / (BrE)
A hotel or inn that provides overnight accommodation for travelers.
hotel or inn for travelers

Examples

The ancient hostelry welcomed weary travelers from across the region.
We decided to stay at a charming hostelry in the countryside during our trip.
Each hostelry along the route offered its own unique hospitality and comforts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inn - a small hotel, often in the country
  • Synonym: hotel - an establishment providing accommodation, meals, and other guest services

Etymology

From Old French hostelerie, from hostel (inn, lodging for travelers).
