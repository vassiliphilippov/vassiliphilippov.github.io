anywhere

1. ADV

/ ˈɛniˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈeniweə / (BrE)
At, in, or to any place, used to refer to an unspecified or unlimited location.
in, at, or to any place

Examples

You can find delicious restaurants anywhere in the city.
I couldn't see him anywhere after the concert ended.
Is there anywhere nearby we can buy some water?

Synset Examples

you can find this food anywhere

Related Terms

  • Antonym: somewhere - at, in, or to a place which is not specified
  • Antonym: nowhere - not in or to any place
  • Antonym: everywhere - in all places

Etymology

From 'any' + 'where', first recorded in Middle English.
