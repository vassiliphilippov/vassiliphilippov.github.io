cognizable

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑːɡ.nə.zə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒɡ.nɪ.zə.bəl / (BrE)
capable of being known, recognized, or perceived; able to be noticed or understood
capable of being known or recognized

Examples

The difference was easily cognizable to even the least experienced observer.
Certain legal claims are only cognizable by federal courts.
The effects of climate change are becoming increasingly cognizable around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recognizable - able to be recognized or identified
  • Antonym: unknowable - not able to be known or understood

Etymology

From Latin cognoscere (to know), via Medieval Latin cognizabilis.
