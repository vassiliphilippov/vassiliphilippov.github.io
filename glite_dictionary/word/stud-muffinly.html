stud-muffinly

1. ADV

/ stʌd ˈmʌfɪnli / (AmE), / stʌd ˈmʌfɪnli / (BrE)
Displaying attractive and charming self-assurance, especially in a way that draws romantic attention.
in an attractive and charming manner
informal slang

Examples

He carried himself in a stud-muffinly way that turned heads everywhere he went.
The actor's stud-muffinly charm was impossible to ignore at the premiere.
She laughed at his stud-muffinly attempts to impress the crowd.

Synset Examples

His stud-muffinly demeanor attracted many admirers.
He walked in with a stud-muffinly confidence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stud muffin - an attractive man; a man with sex appeal

Etymology

From 'stud muffin' (slang for an attractive man) + '-ly' (forming adjectives/adverbs).
