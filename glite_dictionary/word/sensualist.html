sensualist

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛnʃuəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɛnʃʊəlɪst / (BrE)
A person who is devoted to or pursues sensual pleasures and physical enjoyment.
person devoted to sensual pleasures

Examples

He considered himself a sensualist, always seeking new pleasures and delights.
Throughout history, artists have often been accused of being sensualists for their devotion to beauty and sensation.
The novel's protagonist was portrayed as a reckless sensualist indulging in every physical pleasure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hedonist - a person who believes that pleasure is the most important thing in life

Etymology

From Latin 'sensualis' (of the senses) + '-ist'.
