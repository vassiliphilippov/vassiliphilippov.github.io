doubled

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʌbəld / (AmE), / ˈdʌbld / (BrE)
twice as great or as many as before; increased to two times the original amount
twice as much or many; twofold

Examples

The recipe calls for a doubled amount of sugar to make the cake sweeter.
Their profits have doubled since last year, thanks to the new marketing strategy.
After the update, the computer's speed was effectively doubled compared to before.

Synset Examples

ate a double portion
the dose is doubled
a twofold increase

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double - twice as much or as many as usual
  • Antonym: halved - reduced to half its former amount
