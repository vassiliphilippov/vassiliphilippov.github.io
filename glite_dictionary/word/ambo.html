ambo

1. NOUN

/ ˈæm.boʊ / (AmE), / ˈam.bəʊ / (BrE)
a raised platform in a church, especially from which scriptures or sermons are read
raised church platform for readings

Examples

The priest climbed the ambo to deliver the Sunday homily.
Ancient cathedrals often contain a beautifully carved marble ambo.
The gospel was read from the ambo during the service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pulpit - a raised platform in a church where the preacher delivers a sermon

Etymology

From Late Latin *ambō*, from Greek *ambōn* (edge, raised rim, pulpit).
