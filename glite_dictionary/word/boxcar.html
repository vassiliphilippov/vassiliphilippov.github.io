boxcar

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑksˌkɑr / (AmE), / ˈbɒksˌkɑː / (BrE)
a railway freight car with a roof and sliding doors on the sides, used for transporting goods
railway freight car with sliding doors

Examples

The old boxcar was converted into a storage shed on the farm.
Several boxcars carrying grain parked on the siding overnight.
Workers loaded the boxcar with crates bound for the next city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freight car - a vehicle used for carrying goods on a railway
