bar-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑr ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈbɑː ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape of a rigid, straight strip, typically resembling a bar of metal or wood.
having the shape of a straight bar

Examples

The engineer selected a bar-shaped magnet for the experiment.
The sculpture features several bar-shaped elements made of bronze.
A bar-shaped piece of chocolate rested on the plate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rod-shaped - shaped like a straight, slender rod
