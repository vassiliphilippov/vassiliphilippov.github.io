suntrap

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌnˌtræp / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌtræp / (BrE)
A place, such as a terrace or garden, that receives a lot of sunshine and is sheltered from cold winds.
a sheltered, sunny place

Examples

The patio is a real suntrap and perfect for relaxing in the afternoon.
They designed their balcony as a suntrap to enjoy breakfast outdoors even in spring.
This little corner of the garden is a natural suntrap sheltered from the wind.

Etymology

Formed from 'sun' + 'trap', meaning a place that 'traps' or collects sunlight.
