uneventfully

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnɪˈvɛntfəli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈvɛntfəli / (BrE)
In a way that is not marked by any unusual or interesting events; without incident or excitement.
without incident or excitement

Examples

The meeting ended uneventfully, with no major decisions taken.
Despite concerns about the weather, the flight proceeded uneventfully.
The operation was completed uneventfully and without complications.

Synset Examples

the space shuttle landed uneventfully

Related Terms

  • Antonym: eventfully - in a way that is full of interesting or important events
