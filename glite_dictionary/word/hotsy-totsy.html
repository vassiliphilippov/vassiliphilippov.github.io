hotsy-totsy

1. ADJ

/ ˌhɑtsiˈtɑtsi / (AmE), / ˌhɒtsiˈtɒtsi / (BrE)
(informal) Fashionable, stylish, or impressive in appearance or manner.
fashionable; stylish; impressive
informal american

Examples

Her new haircut looks absolutely hotsy-totsy for the big party tonight.
They chose a hotsy-totsy venue for their anniversary dinner celebration.
Every detail of the show was hotsy-totsy, impressing all the fashionable guests.

Synset Examples

Her outfit looked hotsy-totsy for the event.
This restaurant is hotsy-totsy, perfect for special occasions.
Everything was hotsy-totsy at the gala last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stylish - fashionable and well-designed
  • Synonym: trendy - very fashionable or up to date
  • Antonym: old-fashioned - not in style; out of date

Etymology

Originally American slang, possibly a reduplication based on 'hot' meaning fashionable or attractive.
