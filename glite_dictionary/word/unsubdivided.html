unsubdivided

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˌsʌb.dɪˈvaɪ.dɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˌsʌb.dɪˈvaɪ.dɪd / (BrE)
(of a leaf) having no divisions or subdivisions; entire.
leaf with no divisions

Examples

The plant is characterized by its unsubdivided leaves, which are broad and smooth.
Unlike its relatives, this species has unsubdivided foliage, making identification easier.
The unsubdivided structure of the leaf simplifies its classification in botany.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entire - (of a leaf margin) having a smooth edge, not divided or toothed

Etymology

From un- (not) + subdivided.
