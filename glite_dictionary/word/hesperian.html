Hesperian

1. ADJ

/ hɛˈspɪriən / (AmE), / hɛˈspɪəriən / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of the countries of Europe and the western hemisphere; Western.
of the Western world; Western

Examples

The poet described the city as a shining jewel of Hesperian culture.
Art in Hesperian societies was heavily influenced by Enlightenment values.
She studied the contrasts between Hesperian and Eastern philosophies in her thesis.

Synset Examples

occidental civilization
Hesperian culture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: occidental - of or relating to the West (Europe and the Americas)
  • Antonym: oriental - of or relating to the East (Asia)

Etymology

From Latin 'Hesperia' (the West), from Greek 'Hesperos' meaning 'western' or 'evening.'
