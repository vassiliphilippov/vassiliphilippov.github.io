amusive

1. ADJ

/ əˈmjuːsɪv / (AmE), / əˈmjuːsɪv / (BrE)
providing amusement or enjoyment; pleasantly entertaining
pleasantly entertaining

Examples

The children's play was highly amusive, keeping the audience engaged from start to finish.
An amusive conversation can make a long journey seem much shorter.
His collection of amusive stories brought joy to many readers.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entertaining - pleasantly amusing or enjoyable

Etymology

From Latin 'amūsus', past participle of 'amūsāre' (to amuse), influenced by 'amuse'.
