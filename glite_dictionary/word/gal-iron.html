gal-iron

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæl ˌaɪərn / (AmE), / ˈɡæl ˌaɪən / (BrE)
galvanized iron, especially as used in construction for its durability
galvanized iron used in construction

Examples

The roof was covered with sheets of gal-iron to withstand the harsh weather conditions.
They replaced the rusted panels with new gal-iron for extra protection.
Many rural sheds are built from gal-iron because it is cost-effective and long-lasting.

Synset Examples

The old barn was made from sturdy gal-iron sheets.
He built a fence using gal-iron for durability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: galvanized iron - iron or steel coated with zinc to prevent rusting

Etymology

Contraction of 'galvanized iron'.
