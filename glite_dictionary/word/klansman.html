Klansman

1. NOUN

/ ˈklænzmən / (AmE), / ˈklænzmən / (BrE)
A member of the Ku Klux Klan, a white supremacist organization in the United States.
member of the Ku Klux Klan
offensive

Examples

A former Klansman testified during the trial, revealing details about the group's activities.
The novel explores the conflicted conscience of a young Klansman in the 1960s American South.
Many Klansmen marched openly in parades during the height of the group's influence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ku Klux Klan - A white supremacist hate group in the United States
  • Synonym: Klanswoman - A female member of the Ku Klux Klan
