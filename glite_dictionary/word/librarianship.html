librarianship

1. NOUN

/ laɪˈbrɛri.ənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / laɪˈbrɛərɪənˌʃɪp / (BrE)
the profession or duties of a librarian
profession of being a librarian

Examples

She decided to pursue a career in librarianship after earning her master's degree.
The conference focused on emerging trends in librarianship and information science.
His dedication to librarianship was evident throughout his decades of service at the university library.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: librarian - a person who works in or is in charge of a library

Etymology

From librarian +‎ -ship, a suffix denoting state or condition.
