Rabat

1. PROPN

/ rəˈbɑt / (AmE), / ræˈbæt / (BrE)
The capital city of Morocco, situated in the northwest of the country on the Atlantic coast.
capital city of Morocco

Examples

She traveled to Rabat for a business conference last spring.
The parliament of Morocco is located in Rabat.
Many embassies in Morocco are based in Rabat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Casablanca - largest city in Morocco
