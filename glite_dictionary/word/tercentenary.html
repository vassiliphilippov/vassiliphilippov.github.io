tercentenary

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɝː.sɛnˈtiː.nɛr.i / (AmE), / ˌtɜː.senˈtiː.nər.i / (BrE)
the 300th anniversary of a significant event, or the celebration of such an anniversary
300th anniversary or its celebration

Examples

The university held a grand banquet to mark its tercentenary this year.
Several commemorative events are planned for the city's tercentenary in 2025.
The museum's exhibition celebrates the tercentenary of the famous composer’s birth.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: bicentenary - the 200th anniversary or its celebration
  • Degree Lower: centenary - the 100th anniversary or its celebration

Etymology

From Latin 'ter' (three) + 'centenary', from 'centum' (hundred).
