Noel

1. NOUN

/ noʊˈɛl / (AmE), / nəʊˈɛl / (BrE)
the Christmas season, traditionally regarded as the period from December 24 to January 6
the Christmas season (Dec. 24–Jan. 6)

Examples

We always decorate the house beautifully for Noel each year.
Many families exchange gifts during Noel as part of their holiday traditions.
Carols and festive meals are important parts of the Noel celebrations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Christmas - the annual Christian festival celebrating Christ's birth

Etymology

From Old French *noel*, from Latin *natalis* (birth, especially the birth of Christ).
