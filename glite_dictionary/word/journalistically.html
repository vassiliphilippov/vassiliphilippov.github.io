journalistically

1. ADV

/ ˌdʒɝː.nəˈlɪs.tɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌdʒɜː.nəˈlɪs.tɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a manner characteristic of journalism or journalists; in connection with the methods or style typical of reporters or news media.
in the manner of journalists

Examples

The event was covered journalistically by several national newspapers.
She tries to approach her reports journalistically, staying objective and fact-based.
He criticized the movie for being journalistically inaccurate.

Synset Examples

he is being journalistically crucified in his own country

Related Terms

  • Synonym: journalistic - relating to journalism or journalists

Etymology

From 'journalistic' + '-ally', on the model of adverbs from adjectives ending in -ic.
