achromous

1. ADJ

/ əˈkroʊ.məs / (AmE), / əˈkrəʊ.məs / (BrE)
lacking color; colorless.
having no color

Examples

The solution appeared achromous after the chemical reaction was complete.
The artist searched for an achromous pigment to achieve a pristine white background.
Under the microscope, the cells seemed achromous in the unstained sample.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colorless - without color
  • Antonym: chromatic - having or producing color

Etymology

From Greek 'a-' (without) + 'chrōma' (color).
