constipated

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑn.stəˌpeɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.stɪˌpeɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
Unable to evacuate the bowels easily or as often as usual; affected by constipation.
unable to pass stools easily
medical

Examples

After surgery, some patients may feel constipated for several days.
If you are constipated, increasing your fiber intake can often help.
He complained that he had been constipated since starting his new medication.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: constipation - a condition in which bowel movements are infrequent or difficult
  • Antonym: regular - having normal, healthy bowel movements

Etymology

From Latin constipatus, past participle of constipare ‘to press together’.
