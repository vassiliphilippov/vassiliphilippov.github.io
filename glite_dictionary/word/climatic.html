climatic

1. ADJ

/ klaɪˈmætɪk / (AmE), / klaɪˈmatɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a climate or the climate of a particular place or period.
relating to climate

Examples

The region has experienced significant climatic changes over the past century.
Some plants cannot survive in extreme climatic conditions.
Researchers study the climatic impact of greenhouse gas emissions.

Synset Examples

climatic changes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: climatic - pertaining to climate
  • Synonym: climate - the general weather conditions of a region

Etymology

From Greek klimatikos, from klima meaning 'region, zone'.
