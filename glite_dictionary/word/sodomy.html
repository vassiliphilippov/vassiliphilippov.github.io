sodomy

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːdəmi / (AmE), / ˈsɒdəmi / (BrE)
sexual activity involving anal intercourse or intercourse with an animal, historically regarded as a criminal offense in some societies
anal or animal sexual acts, often illegal

Examples

The law in some countries still prohibits sodomy, including acts with animals.
He was prosecuted for sodomy, a charge that carried severe penalties at the time.
Debates about the definition of sodomy have varied across different legal and cultural frameworks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bestiality - sexual activity between a human and an animal

Etymology

From Old French sodomie, based on Latin Sodoma (Sodom), a city destroyed for its inhabitants' wickedness in the Bible.

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːdəmi / (AmE), / ˈsɒdəmi / (BrE)
sexual intercourse involving anal penetration, especially between men; historically, any form of sexual activity considered unnatural by law or custom.
anal intercourse; historically, unnatural sexual acts
legal

Examples

Some countries still have laws prohibiting sodomy.
He was arrested and charged with sodomy under outdated legislation.
Historical texts often mention sodomy as a serious offense.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anal intercourse - sexual activity involving penetration of the anus
  • Antonym: bestiality - sexual relations between a person and an animal

Etymology

From Old French *sodomie*, from Late Latin *Sodomia*, after the biblical city of Sodom.
