compassionately

1. ADV

/ kəmˈpæʃ.ən.ət.li / (AmE), / kəmˈpæʃ.ən.ət.li / (BrE)
in a manner showing sympathy and concern for the suffering or misfortune of others; with compassion
in a compassionate manner

Examples

She spoke compassionately to the frightened child, offering comfort and reassurance.
The doctor listened compassionately to his patient's concerns before explaining the treatment options.
Volunteers worked compassionately with the homeless, providing food and support.

Synset Examples

the nurse looked at him pityingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sympathetically - in a way that shows sympathy

Etymology

From "compassionate" + "-ly", with "compassionate" derived from Latin "compassio" (fellow feeling, pity)
