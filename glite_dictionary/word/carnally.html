carnally

1. ADV

/ ˈkɑrnəli / (AmE), / ˈkɑːnəli / (BrE)
In a carnal manner; relating to or involving physical, especially sexual, desires or activities.
in a sexual or physical manner

Examples

He was accused of having acted carnally with his neighbor's wife.
The law forbade them from knowing each other carnally.
Ancient texts often describe how the gods interacted carnally with mortals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexually - in a sexual manner

Etymology

From Latin 'carnalis' (fleshly), from 'caro' (flesh).
