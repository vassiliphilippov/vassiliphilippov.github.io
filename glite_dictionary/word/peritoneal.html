peritoneal

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɛr.ɪ.tənˈiː.əl / (AmE), / ˌpɛr.ɪ.təˈniː.əl / (BrE)
of or relating to the peritoneum, the serous membrane lining the abdominal cavity and covering the abdominal organs
relating to the peritoneum (abdominal membrane)
medical

Examples

The patient developed peritoneal inflammation after the surgery.
A peritoneal infection can be life-threatening if not treated promptly.
Doctors decided to use peritoneal dialysis to treat her kidney failure.

Synset Examples

peritoneal cancer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peritoneum - the serous membrane lining the abdominal cavity

Etymology

From New Latin peritoneum (the membrane) + -al (adjective suffix).
