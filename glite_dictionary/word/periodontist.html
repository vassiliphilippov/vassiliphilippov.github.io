periodontist

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɪr.i.oʊˈdɑːn.tɪst / (AmE), / ˌpɪə.ri.əˈdɒn.tɪst / (BrE)
a dentist who specializes in the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of diseases affecting the gums and other structures supporting the teeth
dentist specializing in gum diseases
medical

Examples

After experiencing gum problems, she made an appointment with a periodontist for specialized treatment.
The periodontist explained how to prevent further bone loss around my teeth.
He was referred to a periodontist when routine dental care was not enough to manage his condition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dentist - a person qualified to treat the diseases and conditions that affect the teeth and gums

Etymology

From 'periodont-' (relating to the tissues surrounding the teeth) + '-ist' (specialist suffix). Ultimately from Greek 'peri-' (around) + 'odous' (tooth).
