waltzer

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔltsər / (AmE), / ˈwɒltsə / (BrE)
a person who dances the waltz
person who dances the waltz

Examples

As an experienced waltzer, she glided gracefully across the ballroom floor.
Every waltzer must listen carefully to the rhythm of the music.
The competition awarded a prize to the best waltzer of the evening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waltz - a ballroom dance in triple time

Etymology

Formed from 'waltz' + '-er', denoting a person who waltzes.
