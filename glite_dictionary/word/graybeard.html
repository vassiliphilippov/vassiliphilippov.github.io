graybeard

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪˌbɪrd / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪˌbɪəd / (BrE)
an old man, especially one with a gray beard
an old man (often with a gray beard)

Examples

The village graybeard told tales of times long past.
At the meeting, a respected graybeard offered his opinion.
He was a wise graybeard who had seen many generations come and go.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: old man - an elderly male person

Etymology

From 'gray' (the color) + 'beard', referring to the appearance of aging men.
