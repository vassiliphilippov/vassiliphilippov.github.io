socialistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌsoʊʃəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌsəʊʃəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
advocating or following the principles of socialism
relating to socialism

Examples

The new policies introduced by the party are considered socialistic by many critics.
His views on property and income distribution are highly socialistic.
They established a socialistic government after the revolution.

Synset Examples

socialistic government

Related Terms

  • Synonym: socialist - supporting or relating to socialism
  • Antonym: capitalistic - relating to capitalism

Etymology

From 'socialist' + '-ic', meaning relating to socialism.
