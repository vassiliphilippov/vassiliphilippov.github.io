brownish-gray

1. ADJ

/ ˈbraʊnɪʃˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnɪʃˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a grey color tinged with brown.
grey with a brown tint

Examples

The walls were painted a subtle brownish-gray that complemented the wooden floors.
Her coat was a soft brownish-gray, blending in perfectly with the autumn landscape.
We chose a brownish-gray fabric for the sofa because it hides stains well.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taupe - a dark gray-brown color
  • Degree Lower: gray - a neutral color between black and white
  • Degree Higher: brown - a color produced by mixing red, yellow, and blue, darker than orange and lighter than black
