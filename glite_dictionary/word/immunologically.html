immunologically

1. ADV

/ ˌɪmjəˌnoʊˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɪmjʊnəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that relates to immunology or the immune system.
from the viewpoint of immunology
medical

Examples

The two groups were immunologically distinct, showing different antibody responses.
Patients with autoimmune disorders often react immunologically to substances harmless to others.
The treatment was classified as immunologically safe for most trial participants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immunology - the branch of medicine and biology concerned with immunity

Etymology

From immunology + -ally (adverbial suffix).
