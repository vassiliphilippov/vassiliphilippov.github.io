Thuringia

1. PROPN

/ θʊˈrɪndʒiə / (AmE), / θjʊəˈrɪndʒɪə / (BrE)
a region in central Germany, historically significant and currently a federal state (Land) of Germany
region and state in central Germany

Examples

She spent a summer researching medieval castles throughout Thuringia.
The dense forests of Thuringia are famous among hikers and nature lovers.
During the reunification of Germany, Thuringia regained its status as a federal state.

Etymology

From Latin Thuringia, derived from the name of the Germanic tribe Thuringii.
