Torquato Tasso

1. PROPN

/ tɔrˈkwɑtoʊ ˈtɑsoʊ / (AmE), / tɔːˈkwɑːtəʊ ˈtæsəʊ / (BrE)
(1544–1595) Italian poet known for his epic poem 'Jerusalem Delivered', which recounts the capture of Jerusalem during the First Crusade.
Italian epic poet (1544–1595)

Examples

Many literary critics have analyzed the influence of Torquato Tasso on English poetry.
The epic 'Jerusalem Delivered' was written by Torquato Tasso in the late sixteenth century.
Students of Renaissance literature often study the works of Torquato Tasso.
