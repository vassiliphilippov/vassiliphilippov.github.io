pita

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːtə / (AmE), / ˈpiːtə / (BrE)
A type of round, flat bread that can be split open to form a pocket for holding a filling.
round flat bread with a pocket

Examples

She filled the pita with falafel and fresh vegetables for lunch.
The restaurant serves warm pitas with hummus as a starter.
You can use a pita pocket to make a quick and healthy sandwich.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flatbread - bread made with flour, water, and salt, flattened and baked

Etymology

From Modern Greek πίτα (píta), meaning 'pie, cake.'
