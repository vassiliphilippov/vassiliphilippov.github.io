ravigotte

1. NOUN

/ ˌræviˈɡɒt / (AmE), / ˌravɪˈɡɒt / (BrE)
A velouté sauce seasoned with chopped chervil, chives, tarragon, shallots, and capers, commonly used as a condiment in French cuisine.
herb-flavored French velouté sauce

Examples

The grilled fish was served with a tangy ravigotte sauce for extra flavor.
He garnished the vegetables with ravigotte to add a fresh, herbal taste.
In classic French cooking, ravigotte is often paired with cold meats or eggs.

Etymology

French, diminutive of 'ravigoter' (to reinvigorate).
