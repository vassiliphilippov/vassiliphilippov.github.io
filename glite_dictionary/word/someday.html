someday

1. ADV

/ ˈsʌmˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈsʌm.deɪ / (BrE)
At some unspecified time in the future.
at an unspecified future time

Examples

Someday I hope to travel to Japan and explore Tokyo.
She promised me that someday things would get better.
You might regret your decision someday, even if you can't see it now.

Synset Examples

someday you will understand my actions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eventually - in the end, after a long time or some effort
  • Degree Lower: soon - in a short time from now
