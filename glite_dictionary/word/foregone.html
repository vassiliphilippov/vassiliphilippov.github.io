foregone

1. ADJ

/ fɔːrˈɡɔn / (AmE), / fɔːˈɡɒn / (BrE)
belonging to an earlier time; former or in the past
from an earlier time; former

Examples

The museum displayed artifacts from a foregone era.
He often reminisced about foregone days with his childhood friends.
Poets wrote about the beauty of foregone times.

Synset Examples

bygone days
dreams of foregone times
sweet memories of gone summers
relics of a departed era

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bygone - from an earlier time; former

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'forego,' meaning to go before.
