stablegirl

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪ.bəlˌɡɝl / (AmE), / ˈsteɪ.bəlˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
a girl or woman employed in a stable to look after the horses
girl/woman working in a stable

Examples

The stablegirl brushed down the horses every morning before breakfast.
As a stablegirl, her duties included feeding and exercising the young ponies.
The owner praised the stablegirl for her dedication and care of the animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stableboy - a boy employed in a stable to look after horses
