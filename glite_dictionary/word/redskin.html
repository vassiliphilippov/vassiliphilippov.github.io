redskin

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdˌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌskɪn / (BrE)
(offensive slang) A derogatory term for a Native American.
offensive term for a Native American
slang offensive

Examples

He was criticized for using the word redskin during the discussion.
The team's name, formerly known as the Redskins, was changed due to its offensive nature.
Many Native Americans find the term redskin deeply disrespectful.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Native American - a member of any of the indigenous peoples of North and South America
  • Synonym: Indian - dated term for Native American; now considered inaccurate and sometimes offensive

Etymology

Originated in North America in the 18th century, referring to the skin colour of Native Americans; now considered offensive.
