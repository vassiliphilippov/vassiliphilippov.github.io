no-life

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊˌlaɪf / (AmE), / ˈnəʊˌlaɪf / (BrE)
a person who spends most of their time alone or engaged in solitary activities, and has little or no social life
person with little or no social life
slang

Examples

Because he spends all his free time online, some people call him a no-life.
Her parents worried she would become a no-life if she didn't make more friends at school.
It's unfair to judge someone as a no-life just because they have different hobbies.

Synset Examples

He spends all day gaming; what a no-life!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loner - a person who prefers to be alone
