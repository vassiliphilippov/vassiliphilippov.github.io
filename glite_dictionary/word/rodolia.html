Rodolia

1. NOUN

/ roʊˈdoʊliə / (AmE), / rəˈdəʊliə / (BrE)
A genus of Australian ladybugs known for their role in the biological control of pests.
genus of Australian ladybugs

Examples

The Rodolia genus is well known for containing beetles used in pest control.
Researchers released Rodolia into the orchards to curb the scale insect population.
Several species within the Rodolia genus are native to Australia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ladybug - A small beetle often brightly colored with spots, also known as ladybird.
