threatened

1. ADJ

/ ˈθrɛtənd / (AmE), / ˈθrɛtənd / (BrE)
(of a plant or animal) likely to become endangered in the near future.
likely to become endangered (of species)

Examples

The polar bear is a threatened species due to habitat loss in the Arctic.
Many threatened animals are protected by law to prevent extinction.
Conservation programs aim to help threatened plants and animals recover their populations.

Synset Examples

the spotted owl is a threatened species, not yet an endangered one

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: endangered - (of a species) at serious risk of extinction
