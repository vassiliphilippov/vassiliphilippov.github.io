operatively

1. ADV

/ ˈɑː.pɚ.ə.tɪv.li / (AmE), / ˈɒp.ər.ə.tɪv.li / (BrE)
In a way that is effective in producing a desired result or effect.
in an effective or functional way

Examples

The plan must be implemented operatively to achieve the desired outcome.
The new regulations will become operatively effective from next month.
Medication was administered operatively to ensure rapid recovery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: effectively - in a way that produces the intended result

Etymology

From 'operative' + '-ly'; 'operative' from Latin 'operativus' (active, effective).
