crystallizing

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstəlaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the process through which a substance forms crystals or is converted into a crystalline form
the process of forming crystals

Examples

The sample was cooled slowly to encourage crystallizing of the salt.
Crystallizing is an essential step in the purification of many compounds.
Scientists observed the crystallizing of sugar under controlled conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crystallization - the act or process of forming crystals

Etymology

From 'crystallize' + -ing; 'crystallize' from Greek *krustallos* (ice, crystal) via Latin *crystallum*.
