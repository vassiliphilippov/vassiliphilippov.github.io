pallette

1. NOUN

/ pælˈɛt / (AmE), / pælˈɛt / (BrE)
one of the small, rounded armor plates that protect the armpits in a suit of armor.
armor plate at the armpits

Examples

The knight's pallette was dented during the fierce tournament.
Careful craftsmanship went into every pallette to ensure maximum protection and flexibility.
A missing pallette left a vulnerable gap at the warrior's arm joint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pauldron - a plate of armor covering the shoulder

Etymology

From Middle French *palette*, diminutive of *pale* (shovel, blade), referring to the plate's shape.
