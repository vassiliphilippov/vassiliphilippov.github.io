tip-top

1. ADV

/ ˈtɪp ˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈtɪp ˌtɒp / (BrE)
to the highest or best possible degree; extremely well.
to the highest extent; extremely well
informal

Examples

Everything was arranged tip-top for the ceremony.
Her performance in the interview went tip-top, landing her the job.
He keeps his car tip-top so it runs smoothly all year round.

Synset Examples

the shoes fit me tip-top

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfectly - in a perfect manner; completely or flawlessly
  • Synonym: excellently - in an excellent manner; extremely well

Etymology

From the reduplicated form of 'tip', likely meaning the utmost point, first recorded in the 18th century.
