Naples

1. PROPN

/ ˈneɪpəlz / (AmE), / ˈneɪpəlz / (BrE)
a city and major port in southwestern Italy, serving as the capital of the Campania region
city and port in southwestern Italy

Examples

During our trip to Italy, we spent two days exploring Naples and its vibrant markets.
Many tourists visit Naples for its stunning views of the Bay and delicious pizza.
The ancient ruins of Pompeii are just a short drive from Naples.

Etymology

From Italian Napoli, from Latin Neapolis, from Ancient Greek Neápolis (new city).
