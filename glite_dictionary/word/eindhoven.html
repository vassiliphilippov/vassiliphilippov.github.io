Eindhoven

1. PROPN

/ ˈaɪnˌdoʊvən / (AmE), / ˈɛɪntˌhoʊvən / (BrE)
A city in southeastern Netherlands noted for its electrical industry and role as a technology and design center.
city in the Netherlands, tech hub

She moved to Eindhoven to start a new job with a tech company.
Eindhoven is famous for its innovation and modern architecture.
