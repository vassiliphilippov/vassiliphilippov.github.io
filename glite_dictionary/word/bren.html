Bren

1. NOUN

/ brɛn / (AmE), / brɛn / (BrE)
a light, gas-operated submachine gun used by British forces during World War II
British WWII submachine gun

Examples

The infantryman carried a Bren as part of his squad's firepower.
During the demonstration, the instructor explained how to operate a Bren.
Collectors value the Bren for its historical significance and reliable design.

Etymology

Named after Brno (in Czechoslovakia), where the original design was made, and Enfield (in England), where it was produced.
