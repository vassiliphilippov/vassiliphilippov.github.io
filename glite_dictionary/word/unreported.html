unreported

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈpɔr.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈpɔː.tɪd / (BrE)
not mentioned, disclosed, or made known to the public or relevant authorities.
not disclosed or made public

Examples

The company was fined for unreported income.
Many crimes in the city go unreported each year.
There are risks associated with taking unreported medication.

Synset Examples

unreported results

Related Terms

  • Antonym: reported - described or made known officially

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'reported' (past participle of report).
