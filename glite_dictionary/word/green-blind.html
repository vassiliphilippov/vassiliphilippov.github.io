green-blind

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡrin ˌblaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈɡriːn ˌblaɪnd / (BrE)
Unable to see the color green or to distinguish green from purplish-red, typically due to a form of color blindness.
unable to see the color green
medical

Examples

People who are green-blind may struggle to tell the difference between traffic lights.
As a result of his green-blind vision, he couldn't see the green leaves clearly.
The study focused on individuals who were specifically green-blind rather than generally color-blind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: color-blind - unable to distinguish certain colors, esp. red and green
