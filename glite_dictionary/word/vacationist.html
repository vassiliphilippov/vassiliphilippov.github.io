vacationist

1. NOUN

/ veɪˈkeɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / vəˈkeɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who is on vacation, engaging in leisure and relaxation rather than work
a person on vacation

Examples

During the summer months, the beach was crowded with vacationists from all over the country.
The hotel offers a variety of activities for every vacationist seeking relaxation or adventure.
As a frequent vacationist, Martin has visited more than twenty countries in the last decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holidaymaker - a person who is on holiday, especially in British English

Etymology

Derived from 'vacation' + '-ist', denoting a person associated with vacations.
