contestant

1. NOUN

/ kənˈtɛstənt / (AmE), / kənˈtɛstənt / (BrE)
a person who takes part in a competition, especially one involving skill or knowledge
person competing in a contest

Examples

Each contestant was given two minutes to answer the question.
The reality show eliminated one contestant every week.
As a contestant in the spelling bee, she practiced words for hours.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: competitor - a person who competes with others
  • Synonym: participant - a person who takes part in something

Etymology

From 'contest' + '-ant', modeled after similar agent nouns in French and Latin.
