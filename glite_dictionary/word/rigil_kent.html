Rigil Kent

1. PROPN

/ ˈraɪdʒɪl ˈkɛnt / (AmE), / ˈraɪdʒɪl ˈkɛnt / (BrE)
the traditional name for Alpha Centauri, the brightest star in the constellation Centaurus and the second closest star system to the Sun.
brightest star in Centaurus; also Alpha Centauri

Examples

Astronomers have studied Rigil Kent as a possible destination for interstellar probes.
Rigil Kent is visible to the naked eye from the southern hemisphere.
Historical records often refer to Alpha Centauri as Rigil Kent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Alpha Centauri - the star system comprising the stars Alpha Centauri A, Alpha Centauri B, and Proxima Centauri

Etymology

From Arabic 'Rijl al-Qanṭūris' meaning 'the foot of the Centaur'.
