danseur

1. NOUN

/ dɑnˈsɜr / (AmE), / dɒnˈsɜː / (BrE)
A male ballet dancer, especially one who partners a ballerina in performances.
male ballet dancer

Examples

The leading danseur impressed audiences with his strength and elegance at the gala.
He was the first American danseur to join the prestigious Paris Opera Ballet.
The choreographer praised the danseur for his flawless technique and expressive movements.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ballerina - a female ballet dancer

Etymology

From French 'danseur' (dancer).
