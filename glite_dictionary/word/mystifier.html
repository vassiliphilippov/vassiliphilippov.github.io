mystifier

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪstəˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈmɪstɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a baffling or perplexing problem, especially one with a solution to be found; a puzzle
a baffling problem or puzzle

Examples

The crossword in today's paper is a real mystifier for even the best solvers.
That riddle was such a mystifier that we spent hours debating its answer.
She enjoys tackling any mystifier that comes her way, whether it's a puzzle or a complex mystery.

Synset Examples

he loved to solve chessmate puzzles
that's a real puzzler

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puzzle - a problem that tests ingenuity or knowledge
  • Synonym: conundrum - a confusing or difficult problem

Etymology

From French mystifier (to mystify), from mystère (mystery) + -ifier (to make).
