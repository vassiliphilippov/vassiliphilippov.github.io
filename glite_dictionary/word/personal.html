personal

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝːsənəl / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəl / (BrE)
(of qualities or effects) relating to or arising from an individual's personality, rather than external or impersonal factors.
relating to personality or character

Examples

The actor's personal charm won over the audience.
She attributes her success to her personal drive and ambition.
His personal magnetism made him a natural leader.

Synset Examples

personal magnetism

Etymology

From Latin personalis (pertaining to a person), from persona (person).
