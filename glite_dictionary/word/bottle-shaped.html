bottle-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑːt̬l ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈbɒtl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of a bottle; shaped like a bottle.
having the shape of a bottle

Examples

She bought a bottle-shaped vase for the dining room table.
The architect designed a bottle-shaped tower to make it stand out.
