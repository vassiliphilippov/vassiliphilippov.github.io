.22 caliber

1. ADJ

/ ˌtwɛntiˈtuː ˈkæl.ə.bɚ / (AmE), / ˌtwentiˈtjuː ˈkæl.ɪ.bə / (BrE)
(of a firearm or ammunition) having a bore or bullet diameter of 0.22 inches (about 5.6 millimeters).
having a 0.22-inch bore (firearms/ammunition)

Examples

The hunter chose a .22 caliber rifle for small game hunting.
She learned to shoot with a lightweight .22 caliber pistol at the range.
A .22 caliber bullet is commonly used for target practice due to its low recoil.

Synset Examples

a .22 caliber pistol
