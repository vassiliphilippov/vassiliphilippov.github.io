flibbertigibbet

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɪbɚtɪˌdʒɪbɪt / (AmE), / ˈflɪbətɪˌdʒɪbɪt / (BrE)
a frivolous, flighty, or excessively talkative person, especially a woman
frivolous or flighty person
archaic

Examples

My aunt often calls me a flibbertigibbet because I can't seem to focus on anything for long.
The character in the play was a charming flibbertigibbet who entertained everyone with her endless chatter.
He dismissed her concerns, thinking she was just a flibbertigibbet with nothing serious to say.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scatterbrain - a person who is forgetful or disorganized
  • Synonym: airhead - a silly or foolish person

Etymology

Imitative; origin possibly from Middle English, representing the sound of meaningless chatter.
