rima pudendi

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪmə pjuːˈdɛndaɪ / (AmE), / ˈraɪmə pjuːˈdendi / (BrE)
the slit or fissure between the labia majora in females
fissure between the labia majora
medical

Examples

The rima pudendi can be observed during a routine gynecological examination.
Doctors examine the rima pudendi for signs of infection or abnormality.
Certain conditions may cause swelling or changes to the rima pudendi.

Etymology

From Latin rima (fissure) and pudendi ('of the genitals').
