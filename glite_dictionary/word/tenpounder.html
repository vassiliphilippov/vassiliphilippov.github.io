tenpounder

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnˌpaʊndər / (AmE), / ˈtɛnˌpaʊndə / (BrE)
a game fish resembling the tarpon but smaller, typically found in warm coastal waters
small game fish similar to tarpon

Examples

The fisherman was thrilled to catch a tenpounder near the mouth of the river.
Several tenpounders were spotted swimming alongside juvenile tarpons in the lagoon.
Anglers prize the tenpounder for its energetic fight despite its small size.
