spiritedness

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪrɪtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being lively, energetic, or full of spirit
liveliness; energetic quality

Examples

Her spiritedness brightened the atmosphere at every gathering she attended.
The team's spiritedness helped them overcome many challenges in the competition.
Despite the setback, his spiritedness remained undiminished throughout the ordeal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liveliness - the quality of being lively and full of energy
  • Antonym: listlessness - the quality of lacking energy or enthusiasm

Etymology

From 'spirited' + '-ness', with 'spirit' from Latin 'spiritus' (breath, energy).
