moonlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈmuːnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling the moon in shape or appearance.
resembling the moon

Examples

The dancer's face had a moonlike pallor under the spotlight.
They built a garden pond with a moonlike curve along its edge.
The strange, moonlike object in the sky caught everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunar - relating to the moon; resembling the moon
