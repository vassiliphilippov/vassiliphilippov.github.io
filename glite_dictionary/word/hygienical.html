hygienical

1. ADJ

/ haɪˈdʒinɪkəl / (AmE), / haɪˈdʒiːnɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or promoting practices that preserve health and cleanliness (rare, chiefly historical)
relating to promoting health (rare)
archaic

Examples

Doctors recommended several hygienical measures to prevent the spread of disease.
Early twentieth-century schools were designed with hygienical concerns in mind, such as increased ventilation.
The pamphlet outlined hygienical habits for children to follow at home.

Synset Examples

hygienic habits like using disposable tissues
hygienic surroundings with plenty of fresh air

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hygienic - promoting health through cleanliness

Etymology

From hygiene + -ical, modeled after 'hygienic'; rare variant form.
