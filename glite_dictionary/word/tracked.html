tracked

1. ADJ

/ trækt / (AmE), / trækt / (BrE)
having tracks, especially a vehicle or surface marked by tracks or equipped with continuous tracks instead of wheels
having tracks or continuous tracks

Examples

The army deployed tracked vehicles to navigate the muddy terrain.
She noticed the tracked paw prints in the fresh snow.
Bulldozers are often tracked to improve their stability on rough ground.

Synset Examples

new snow tracked by rabbits
tracked vehicles

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wheeled - having wheels rather than tracks
