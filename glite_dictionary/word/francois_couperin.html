Francois Couperin

1. PROPN

/ fʀɑ̃ˈswɑ kuːpəˈræn / (AmE), / fʀɑ̃ˈswɑː kuːpəˈræn / (BrE)
(1668–1733) a French composer and organist, renowned for his baroque keyboard music and as a member of a distinguished family of musicians.
French baroque composer and organist

Examples

Many consider Francois Couperin a master of French baroque music.
The organ pieces of Francois Couperin are still regularly performed today.
She cited Francois Couperin as a major influence on her interpretation of early music.
