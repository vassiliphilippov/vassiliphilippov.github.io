ethnical

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛθ.nɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈɛθ.nɪ.kəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of a particular ethnic group or their cultural traditions.
relating to an ethnic group

Examples

The museum features a diverse collection of ethnical artifacts from various cultures.
Debates often arise over the ethnical composition of certain regions.
She researches the ethnical traditions of indigenous peoples around the world.

Synset Examples

influenced by ethnic and cultural ties
ethnic food

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ethnic - relating to a large group of people who have the same national, racial, or cultural origins

Etymology

From Greek ethnikos (national, foreign, gentile), from ethnos (nation, people).
