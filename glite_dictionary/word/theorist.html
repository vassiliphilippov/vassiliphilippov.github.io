theorist

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɪrɪst / (AmE), / ˈθɪərɪst / (BrE)
a person who develops or advocates theories, especially in science or art
person who formulates theories

Examples

As a leading theorist in quantum physics, she has proposed several groundbreaking ideas.
The art theorist presented an analysis of modern painting techniques.
Many political theorists debate the origins of democracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theoretician - a person who is concerned with the theoretical aspects of a subject

Etymology

From theory + -ist, modelled on similar agent nouns.
