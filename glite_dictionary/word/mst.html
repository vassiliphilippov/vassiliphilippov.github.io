MST

1. NOUN

/ ɛm ɛs ti / (AmE), / ɛm ɛs ti / (BrE)
Abbreviation for Mountain Standard Time; the standard time in the Mountain Time Zone of North America, seven hours behind UTC.
Mountain Standard Time (US/Canada time zone)
american

Examples

Flights to Denver operate according to MST, so check your tickets carefully.
During the winter, most states in the Mountain Time Zone switch to MST.
The conference call is scheduled for 2:00 p.m. MST.

Etymology

Initialism of Mountain Standard Time.
