Hungary

1. PROPN

/ ˈhʌŋˌɡɛri / (AmE), / ˈhʌŋɡəri / (BrE)
A country in central Europe, officially known as the Republic of Hungary.
country in central Europe

Examples

She traveled to Hungary to visit family in Budapest.
The Danube River flows through several countries, including Hungary.
Hungarian cuisine is a highlight for visitors to Hungary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Budapest - the capital city of Hungary

Etymology

From Medieval Latin Hungaria, from the ethnonym 'Hungarus'.
