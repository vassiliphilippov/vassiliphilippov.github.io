mack

1. NOUN

/ mæk / (AmE), / mæk / (BrE)
a waterproof raincoat made of rubberized fabric.
waterproof raincoat
british

Examples

He put on his mack before heading out into the rain.
Don't forget your mack—it looks like it might pour later.
I left my mack at home and got completely soaked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mackintosh - a waterproof raincoat, especially one made of rubberized cloth
  • Synonym: raincoat - a coat worn to protect from rain

Etymology

Shortened from 'mackintosh', named after Charles Macintosh (1766–1843), the Scottish inventor.
