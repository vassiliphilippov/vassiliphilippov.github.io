floriculture

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɔr.ɪˌkʌl.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈflɒr.ɪˌkʌl.tʃə / (BrE)
The cultivation and management of flowering and ornamental plants for gardens, floral arrangements, and the floral industry.
cultivation of flowering plants

Examples

She decided to study floriculture to learn more about growing ornamental plants.
The local college offers courses in floriculture as part of its horticulture program.
Advances in floriculture have led to the development of new varieties of roses and lilies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horticulture - the science and art of cultivating plants, including fruits, vegetables, and ornamental plants

Etymology

From Latin 'flos' (flower) + 'culture' (from Latin 'cultura', cultivation).
