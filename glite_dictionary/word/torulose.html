torulose

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɔr.jəˌloʊs / (AmE), / ˈtɔː.rjʊˌləʊs / (BrE)
(of a cylindrical or ellipsoid body) swollen and constricted at intervals along its length.
swollen and constricted at intervals (biology)

Examples

The fungus has torulose hyphae that alternate between swollen and narrow sections.
The botanist observed a torulose stem on the unusual plant specimen.
Under the microscope, the bacteria appeared torulose, with periodic enlargements along their length.

Etymology

From Latin 'torulosus', from 'torulus' (a small protuberance).
