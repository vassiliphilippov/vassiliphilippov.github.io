Marie Jeanne Bécu

1. PROPN

/ maˈʁi ʒan beˈky / (AmE), / maˈʁi ʒan beˈky / (BrE)
(1743–1793) French courtier and the last mistress of King Louis XV, executed during the French Revolution.
mistress of Louis XV, executed in Revolution

Examples

Marie Jeanne Bécu became widely known at the French court under the title Comtesse du Barry.
During the Revolution, Marie Jeanne Bécu was arrested and eventually sent to the guillotine.
Historians often cite Marie Jeanne Bécu as a symbol of the opulence and decline of the Ancien Régime.
