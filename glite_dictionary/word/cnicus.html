Cnicus

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪ.kəs / (AmE), / ˈsiː.nɪ.kəs / (BrE)
A genus of thistle-like flowering plants in the family Asteraceae, of which blessed thistle is a notable species.
genus of thistle-like plants

Examples

The herbalist recommended Cnicus for its traditional medicinal properties.
Botanists classify blessed thistle under the genus Cnicus.
Some species previously in Cnicus have been reclassified into other genera.

Etymology

From Greek knikos (safflower, thistle).
