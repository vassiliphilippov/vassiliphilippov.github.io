preferably

1. ADV

/ ˈprɛfɚəbli / (AmE), / ˈprɛf.ər.ə.bli / (BrE)
Ideally; if possible or if one could choose, in a way that is more desirable or suitable.
in a preferred or ideal manner

Examples

You should arrive early, preferably before 9 a.m.
All applications must be submitted in English, preferably typed rather than handwritten.
Store the medication at room temperature, preferably in a dry place.

Synset Examples

clean it well, preferably with warm water
I'd rather be in Philadelphia
I'd sooner die than give up

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ideally - in the most suitable or perfect way
  • Degree Lower: optionally - if possible but not necessary

Etymology

Formed from 'prefer' (from Latin 'praeferre') + '-ably', meaning 'in an able or fit manner'.
