practitioner

1. NOUN

/ prækˈtɪʃənər / (AmE), / prækˈtɪʃənə / (BrE)
a person who works in a professional field, especially one requiring specialized knowledge or training
qualified professional worker

Examples

The practitioner carefully reviewed her client's medical history before beginning treatment.
He is a skilled practitioner of corporate law, known for his expertise and professionalism.
As a mindfulness practitioner, she meditates daily and attends workshops regularly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: professional - a person engaged in a profession; similar meaning

Etymology

From practice +‎ -itioner, modeled after 16th-century French praticien (practitioner).
