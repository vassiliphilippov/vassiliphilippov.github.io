bionomic

1. ADJ

/ ˌbaɪəˈnɑːmɪk / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪəˈnɒmɪk / (BrE)
relating to the science of ecology or the relationships of organisms to their environment
relating to ecology or environmental relationships

Examples

Recent bionomic studies have revealed new insights into rainforest ecosystems.
The bionomic impact of pollution on marine life is a serious concern.
Her research focuses on the bionomic interactions between plants and soil microbes.

Synset Examples

ecological research

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecological - relating to ecology or the environment

Etymology

From Greek 'bios' (life) + 'nomos' (law).
