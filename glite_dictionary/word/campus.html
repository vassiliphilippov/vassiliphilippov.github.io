campus

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæm.pəs / (AmE), / ˈkæm.pəs / (BrE)
the grounds and buildings of a university, college, or school
university or college grounds

Examples

The university campus was bustling with students during orientation week.
She lives in a dormitory on campus to be close to her classes.
They enjoyed walking around the beautifully landscaped campus in spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: university - an institution of higher learning

Etymology

From Latin 'campus' meaning 'field'.
