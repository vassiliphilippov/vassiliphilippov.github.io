outmoded

1. ADJ

/ aʊtˈmoʊdɪd / (AmE), / aʊtˈməʊdɪd / (BrE)
No longer fashionable or useful; having gone out of style.
no longer in fashion or use

Examples

Many people believe that physical newspapers are becoming outmoded in the digital age.
She replaced her outmoded phone with the latest model.
The school's outmoded teaching methods were finally updated.

Synset Examples

a suit of rather antique appearance
demode (or outmoded) attire
outmoded ideas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obsolete - no longer produced or used; out of date
  • Antonym: fashionable - popular or approved of at a particular time; in fashion

Etymology

From prefix 'out-' + 'mode', meaning something that is no longer the current fashion.
