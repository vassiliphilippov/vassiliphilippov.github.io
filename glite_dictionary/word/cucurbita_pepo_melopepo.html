Cucurbita pepo melopepo

1. NOUN

/ kuːˈkɝːbɪtə ˈpiːpoʊ ˌmɛlɪˈpoʊ / (AmE), / kjuːˈkɜːbɪtə ˈpiːpəʊ ˌmiːlɪˈpəʊ / (BrE)
a group of bushy plants in the squash family, such as zucchini or pattypan squash, grown for edible fruit picked when immature
bushy squash plant (e.g., zucchini) picked young for food

Examples

Farmers often grow Cucurbita pepo melopepo varieties like zucchini and pattypan squash for summer harvest.
The nutritionist recommended including Cucurbita pepo melopepo in the diet because of its low calorie content.
Young chefs are learning new ways to prepare dishes with Cucurbita pepo melopepo for a healthy menu.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zucchini - a type of summer squash, commonly eaten as a vegetable
  • Synonym: pattypan squash - a variety of summer squash with a round, scalloped shape

Etymology

From Latin: Cucurbita (gourd) + pepo (melon, pumpkin) + melopepo (a kind of melon).
