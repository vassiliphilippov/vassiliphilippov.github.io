Turk

1. NOUN

/ tɝk / (AmE), / tɜːk / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Turkey.
native or inhabitant of Turkey

Examples

He met a Turk who gladly shared stories about his homeland.
Several Turks moved to the city in the 1990s.
As a proud Turk, she celebrates her cultural heritage every year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Turkish person - a person from Turkey

Etymology

From Old French Turc, from Medieval Latin Turcus, from Byzantine Greek Tourkos, from Persian Türk.
