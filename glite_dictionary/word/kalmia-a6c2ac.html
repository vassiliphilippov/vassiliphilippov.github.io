kalmia

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæl.mi.ə / (AmE), / ˈkæl.mi.ə / (BrE)
A genus of evergreen shrubs with showy flowers, commonly known as mountain laurel, native to North America.
genus of evergreen shrubs (mountain laurels)

Examples

The garden features several kalmia bushes in full bloom during the spring.
Many hikers stopped to admire the wild kalmia growing along the mountain trail.
Because of its hardy nature, kalmia is popular in ornamental landscaping.

Etymology

Named after Peter Kalm, a Swedish botanist.
