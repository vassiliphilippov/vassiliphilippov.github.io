dining

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of eating a meal, especially dinner, usually in a formal or social setting
the act of eating dinner or a meal

Examples

He enjoys dining at fine restaurants when he travels.
Elegant dining is available at the city's top hotels.
We had an evening of delightful dining and conversation with friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to dine - to eat dinner or the main meal of the day

Etymology

From the verb 'dine,' originating from Old French *disner* (to dine).
