upholder

1. NOUN

/ ʌpˈhoʊldər / (AmE), / ʌpˈhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person who supports or maintains a principle, cause, or tradition
person who supports or maintains something

Examples

She was a passionate upholder of environmental causes throughout her life.
As an upholder of the law, he always acted with integrity.
Many see the monarchy as an upholder of national tradition.

Synset Examples

firm upholders of tradition
they are sustainers of the idea of democracy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supporter - a person who gives support to something
  • Synonym: defender - someone who defends or protects

Etymology

From 'uphold' (to support) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
