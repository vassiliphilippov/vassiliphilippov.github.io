procuress

1. NOUN

/ proʊˈkjʊr.ɪs / (AmE), / prəˈkjʊə.rɪs / (BrE)
a woman who procures clients for prostitutes; a female pimp
female who procures clients for prostitutes

Examples

The notorious procuress was known for recruiting young women into the trade.
In the novel, Madame Rouge acts as a cunning procuress for high-society clientele.
The police arrested the procuress for her involvement in the illegal operation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pimp - a person who procures clients for prostitutes, typically male

Etymology

From procure + -ess (feminine suffix), after French 'procureuse'.
