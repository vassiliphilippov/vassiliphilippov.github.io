unscathed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈskeɪðd / (AmE), / ʌnˈskeɪðd / (BrE)
not injured or harmed; unaffected by damage or injury.
not injured or harmed

Examples

She emerged from the accident unscathed and in good spirits.
The company survived the economic crisis unscathed.
Miraculously, the puppy was found unscathed under the rubble.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: injured - harmed or wounded
  • Synonym: unharmed - not hurt or damaged

Etymology

From un- (not) + scathe (to harm), with 'scathe' from Old Norse 'skaði' (harm, injury).
