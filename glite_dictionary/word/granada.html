Granada

1. PROPN

/ ɡrəˈnɑdə / (AmE), / ɡrəˈnɑːdə / (BrE)
a city in southeastern Spain, formerly the capital of the Moorish kingdom until 1492, famous for the Alhambra palace and fortress.
city in southeastern Spain, famous for the Alhambra

Examples

Many tourists travel to Granada to visit the historic Alhambra palace.
The rich cultural heritage of Granada reflects its Moorish past.
After visiting Madrid, they took a train south to Granada for a weekend getaway.
