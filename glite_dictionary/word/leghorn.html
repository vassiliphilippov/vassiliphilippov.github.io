leghorn

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛɡˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡ.hɔːn / (BrE)
a hat made of braided straw, typically with a flat crown and stiff brim, originally made from Italian straw
straw hat with flat crown

Examples

She wore a delicate leghorn trimmed with blue ribbons to the garden party.
The vintage shop had an elegant leghorn displayed in the window, recalling the styles of the 19th century.
As the sun grew stronger, he reached for his old leghorn to shield his eyes.

Etymology

From Italian Livorno, known as Leghorn in English, where such straw hats were originally exported.
