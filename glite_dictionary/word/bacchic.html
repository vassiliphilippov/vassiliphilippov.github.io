bacchic

1. ADJ

/ ˈbæk.ɪk / (AmE), / ˈbak.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to riotously drunken revelry or festivities, especially those reminiscent of Bacchus, the Roman god of wine
relating to drunken revelry

Examples

The party quickly transformed into a bacchic celebration that lasted until dawn.
The artist's paintings are full of bacchic scenes, reveling in excess and joy.
The festival's bacchic atmosphere made it famous for wild, spirited dancing and singing.

Synset Examples

a night of bacchanalian revelry
carousing bands of drunken soldiers
orgiastic festivity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bacchanalian - characterized by or involving drunken revelry; riotously drunken
  • Synonym: orgiastic - relating to wild, unrestrained revelry or festivity

Etymology

From Latin Bacchicus, from Bacchus, the Roman god of wine.
