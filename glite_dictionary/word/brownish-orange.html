brownish-orange

1. ADJ

/ ˈbraʊnɪʃ ɔːˌrændʒ / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnɪʃ ɒːrɪndʒ / (BrE)
having a light brown color tinged with orange
light brown color with orange tint

Examples

The autumn leaves glowed with a brownish-orange hue in the afternoon light.
She painted the living room walls a warm brownish-orange to create a cozy atmosphere.
The kitten had a soft, brownish-orange patch on its back.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tan - a pale tone of brown
  • Degree Higher: orange - a bright reddish-yellow color
  • Degree Lower: brown - a color produced by mixing red, yellow, and blue, as of dark wood or rich soil
