Mandelshtam

1. PROPN

/ ˈmændɛlʃtæm / (AmE), / ˈmændɛlʃtæm / (BrE)
Osip Mandelshtam (1891–1938), a Russian poet renowned for his significant contributions to 20th-century literature, who died in a Soviet prison camp.
Russian poet (1891–1938)

Examples

Many scholars have studied Mandelshtam's poetry for its unique style and historical importance.
During the Soviet era, the works of Mandelshtam were banned and circulated only underground.
Her thesis focused on the political themes present in Mandelshtam's later poems.

Etymology

Transliteration of Russian Мандельштам, a surname of Jewish (Yiddish) origin.
