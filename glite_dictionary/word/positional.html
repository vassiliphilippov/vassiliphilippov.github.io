positional

1. ADJ

/ pəˈzɪʃənl̩ / (AmE), / pəˈzɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or determined by the position of something or someone
relating to position

Examples

The positional advantage of the hill made a difference during the battle.
The coach stressed the importance of positional awareness on the field.
Certain chess strategies rely heavily on positional play rather than tactics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spatial - relating to space or position

Etymology

From 'position' + '-al', the suffix forming adjectives.
