stripy

1. ADJ

/ ˈstraɪpi / (AmE), / ˈstraɪpi / (BrE)
marked or decorated with stripes
having stripes

Examples

She wore a stripy shirt with bright blue and white lines.
The child's toy was covered in stripy patterns of red and yellow.
I bought a stripy rug to add color to the living room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: striped - having one or more stripes
