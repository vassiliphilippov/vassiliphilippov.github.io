arcuate

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːrkjuˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɑːkjʊeɪt / (BrE)
Shaped like an arch; curved.
shaped like an arch

Examples

The arcuate shape of the bridge made it both elegant and strong.
Doctors examined the arcuate arteries supplying the kidney.
A series of arcuate windows lined the old cathedral.

Synset Examples

an arched ceiling

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arched - having a shape or structure like an arch
  • Antonym: straight - having no curves or bends

Etymology

From Latin arcuatus, from arcus (arch, bow).
