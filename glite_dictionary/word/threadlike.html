threadlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈθrɛdˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈθrɛd.laɪk / (BrE)
thin and resembling a thread in shape or appearance
thin and resembling a thread

Examples

The fungus grows long, threadlike hyphae under the microscope.
Her hair was so fine and threadlike it slipped through my fingers.
Tiny threadlike roots spread throughout the soil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filamentous - composed of or resembling filaments or threads
