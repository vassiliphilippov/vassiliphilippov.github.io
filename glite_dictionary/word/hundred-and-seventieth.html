hundred-and-seventieth

1. ADJ

/ ˌhʌndrəd ən ˈsɛvən ti ɪθ / (AmE), / ˌhʌndrɪd ən ˈsɛv(ə)n ti ɪθ / (BrE)
Ordinal number corresponding to one hundred seventy in a sequence; 170th.
ordinal number: 170th

Examples

Her office was on the hundred-and-seventieth floor of the new skyscraper.
This is the hundred-and-seventieth anniversary of his birth, marked by special events worldwide.
He finished in hundred-and-seventieth place out of over two hundred competitors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hundred-and-seventieth - the 170th ordinal number
  • Degree Lower: hundred-and-sixty-ninth - the 169th ordinal number, one before hundred-and-seventieth
  • Degree Higher: hundred-and-seventy-first - the 171st ordinal number, one after hundred-and-seventieth
