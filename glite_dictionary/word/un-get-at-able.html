un-get-at-able

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌɡɛtˈætəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌɡɛtˈætəbəl / (BrE)
Very difficult or impossible to reach or attain.
hard to reach or obtain
archaic

Examples

The mysterious island remained un-get-at-able to all but the bravest explorers.
For years, her dreams seemed un-get-at-able, no matter how hard she worked.
The treasure was locked away in an un-get-at-able place deep beneath the castle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inaccessible - not able to be reached or entered
  • Antonym: reachable - able to be reached or accessed

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-', the phrase 'get at', and the suffix '-able'.
