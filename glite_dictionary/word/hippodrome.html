hippodrome

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪpəˌdroʊm / (AmE), / ˈhɪpədrəʊm / (BrE)
a stadium or arena for horse shows, horse races, or chariot races
stadium for horse or chariot races

Examples

The annual derby was held at the ancient hippodrome just outside the city.
The architect based the design of the new stadium on a Roman hippodrome.
Tourists visited the remains of the Byzantine hippodrome during their stay in Istanbul.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racecourse - a track for horse racing
  • Synonym: arena - an enclosed area for public events

Etymology

From Greek 'hippodromos', from 'hippos' (horse) and 'dromos' (racecourse, running).
