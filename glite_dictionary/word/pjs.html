pj's

1. NOUN

/ piː ˈdʒeɪz / (AmE), / piː ˈdʒeɪz / (BrE)
Loose-fitting clothes, typically consisting of a top and trousers, worn for sleeping or lounging; pajamas.
informal nightclothes; pajamas
informal

Examples

After dinner, the children changed into their pj's and got ready for bed.
She spent the entire Saturday morning relaxing in her favorite pj's.
Don’t forget to pack your pj's for the sleepover.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pajamas - loose-fitting clothes worn for sleeping
  • Synonym: pyjamas - British spelling of 'pajamas'

Etymology

Informal shortening of 'pajamas' or 'pyjamas'.
