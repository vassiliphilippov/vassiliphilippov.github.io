basketmaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskɪtˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes baskets by weaving flexible materials such as willow, cane, or reeds
person who weaves baskets

Examples

She apprenticed with a renowned basketmaker to learn traditional weaving techniques.
At the fair, a local basketmaker demonstrated how to create intricate patterns from willow branches.
The museum features a display about the life of a 19th-century basketmaker in rural England.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weaver - a person who weaves fabric or other materials
