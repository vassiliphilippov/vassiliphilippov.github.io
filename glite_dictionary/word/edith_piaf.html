Edith Piaf

1. PROPN

/ ˈiːdɪθ piˈɑf / (AmE), / ˈiːdɪθ piˈaf / (BrE)
(1915–1963) A famous French cabaret singer known for her powerful voice and emotional performances.
famous French cabaret singer

Examples

The music of Edith Piaf evokes strong emotions in listeners around the world.
Many consider Edith Piaf to be one of France's greatest vocalists.
The film about Edith Piaf's life introduced her work to a new generation.
