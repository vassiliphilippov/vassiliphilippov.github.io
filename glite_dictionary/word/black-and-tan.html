black-and-tan

1. ADJ

/ ˌblæk ən ˈtæn / (AmE), / ˌblæk ən ˈtæn / (BrE)
having a pattern or combination of black and tan colors, especially in the coat of an animal such as a dog.
having black and tan coloration

Examples

The black-and-tan puppy stood out among its littermates.
Her new boots had a stylish black-and-tan pattern on the sides.
The beagle's black-and-tan markings are characteristic of the breed.
