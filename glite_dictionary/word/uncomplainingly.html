uncomplainingly

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.kəmˈpleɪ.nɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kəmˈpleɪ.nɪŋ.li / (BrE)
in a way that shows no sign of complaining, even under difficulty or hardship
without complaining; showing acceptance

Examples

She uncomplainingly accepted the extra responsibilities at work without protest.
He uncomplainingly endured the long delays during their journey.
Despite her illness, she cared for her family uncomplainingly every day.

Synset Examples

an equally gallant little wife and mother uncomplainingly keeping up the production of tasty and nourishing meals

Related Terms

  • Antonym: complainingly - in a complaining manner

Etymology

From 'uncomplaining' + '-ly', where 'un-' denotes 'not' and 'complain' from Old French 'complaindre'.
