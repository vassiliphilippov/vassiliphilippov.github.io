spliff

1. NOUN

/ splɪf / (AmE), / splɪf / (BrE)
A cigarette made by rolling marijuana leaves, sometimes mixed with tobacco, in paper for smoking.
marijuana cigarette
slang

Examples

After work, he relaxed outside with a spliff and some music.
She rolled a spliff to share with her friends at the party.
It's illegal to carry a spliff in most countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joint - a hand-rolled cigarette containing marijuana

Etymology

Probably from Jamaican English, of uncertain origin.
