Seyhan

1. PROPN

/ seɪˈhɑn / (AmE), / seɪˈhæn / (BrE)
a river in southern Turkey flowing south-southwest into the Mediterranean Sea
river in southern Turkey

Examples

The Seyhan provides vital irrigation for the agricultural lands of the region.
They took a boat trip along the Seyhan to enjoy the scenery.
Many towns and villages are located on the banks of the Seyhan river.

Etymology

From Turkish Seyhan, the local name of the river.
