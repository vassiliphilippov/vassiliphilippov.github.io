stableman

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪ.bəlˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈsteɪ.bəl.mæn / (BrE)
a man employed in a stable to take care of the horses
man who cares for horses in a stable

Examples

The stableman fed the horses every morning before sunrise.
As a stableman, he knew each horse by name and temperament.
She asked the stableman to prepare her horse for the afternoon ride.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stableboy - a boy employed in a stable to care for horses
  • Synonym: groom - a person employed to take care of horses

Etymology

Formed from 'stable' (building for horses) + 'man' (male person).
