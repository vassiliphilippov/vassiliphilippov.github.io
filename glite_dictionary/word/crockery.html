crockery

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɑk.ə.ri / (AmE), / ˈkrɒk.əri / (BrE)
Plates, dishes, cups, and other items made of earthenware or china used for serving or eating food.
plates and dishes for eating/serving

Examples

She bought new crockery for the kitchen before the holidays.
Please handle the crockery carefully to avoid breaking any plates.
The hotel dining room was set with fine white crockery and silver cutlery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tableware - utensils and dishes used for setting a table, serving food, and dining

Etymology

From crock + -ery, where 'crock' means earthenware pot.
