trend-setter

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɛndˌsɛtər / (AmE), / ˈtrɛndˌsɛtə / (BrE)
a person who leads the way in fashion or ideas, making new styles or trends popular
person who initiates new fashions or trends

Examples

The fashion industry eagerly watches what each new trend-setter wears.
She was considered a trend-setter for introducing bold colors in office attire.
Many designers look to social media trend-setters for inspiration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: innovator - a person who introduces new ideas or methods
  • Antonym: follower - a person who adopts a trend after it becomes popular

Etymology

Formed from 'trend' (current style) and 'setter' (one who establishes something), popularized in the mid-20th century.
