galago

1. NOUN

/ ɡəˈleɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / ɡəˈleɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
A small, agile, long-tailed nocturnal African primate with dense woolly fur, large eyes, and large ears, also known as a bush baby.
small nocturnal African primate (bush baby)

Examples

The galago leapt gracefully from one branch to another under the cover of night.
Researchers studied the large eyes of the galago to understand nocturnal vision.
Children were fascinated by the galago at the wildlife sanctuary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bush baby - another common name for galago, a small nocturnal primate

Etymology

From French 'galago', of African origin.
