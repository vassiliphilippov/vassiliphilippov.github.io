SSA

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛs ˌɛs ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛs ˌɛs ˈeɪ / (BrE)
an independent United States government agency responsible for administering the Social Security system, including retirement, disability, and survivors' benefits
US Social Security Administration agency
american

Examples

You can contact the SSA for information about your retirement benefits.
The SSA issued a statement regarding changes to Social Security payments.
To replace your lost Social Security card, visit your local SSA office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Social Security Administration - the full name of the agency referred to by SSA

Etymology

Abbreviation of Social Security Administration.
