hinderingly

1. ADV

/ ˈhɪndərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈhɪndərɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that obstructs or impedes progress; in an obstructive way.
in an obstructive or impeding manner

Examples

She spoke hinderingly during the meeting, making it difficult to reach a consensus.
The outdated rules operated hinderingly against rapid innovation within the company.
If the machinery works hinderingly, production will slow down considerably.

Synset Examples

he acted very obstructively when we tried to carry out our project

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obstructively - in a way that creates obstacles or blocks progress
  • Antonym: helpfully - in a way that assists or makes things easier

Etymology

Formed from 'hinder' + '-ing' + '-ly', based on hinder (to obstruct or delay).
