tastefully

1. ADV

/ ˈteɪst.fəli / (AmE), / ˈteɪst.fəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows good taste, especially in style, decoration, or design.
in a tasteful manner

Examples

The living room was tastefully furnished with elegant antiques.
Her wedding dress was tastefully simple, without any excessive decoration.
The restaurant is tastefully designed, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

Synset Examples

the house was tastefully decorated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tastefully - in a tasteful manner
  • Antonym: tastelessly - in a tasteless manner; lacking good taste

Etymology

From "tasteful" + "-ly"; "tasteful" from "taste" + "-ful".
