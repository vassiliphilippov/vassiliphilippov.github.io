voyeuristical

1. ADJ

/ vɔɪˈjɪrɪstɪkəl / (AmE), / vɔɪˌjʊərɪˈstɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of a voyeur or voyeurism, especially having or showing an excessive interest in observing others engaged in private or intimate activities.
relating to voyeurs or voyeurism

Examples

Many found the documentary too voyeuristical in its portrayal of the family's struggles.
There is a fine line between healthy curiosity and voyeuristical interest in others' private lives.
The artist's work was criticized for its voyeuristical fascination with subjects in vulnerable moments.

Synset Examples

his voyeuristic pleasures

Related Terms

  • Synonym: voyeuristic - relating to or characteristic of a voyeur or voyeurism

Etymology

From 'voyeur' (from French, 'one who sees') + '-istic' + '-al'.
