to zoop

1. VERB

/ zuːp / (AmE), / zuːp / (BrE)
to move quickly and energetically or to travel rapidly to a place
move quickly or energetically
informal slang

Examples

He loves to zoop through the park on his bike.
The kids would zoop around the playground until they were tired.
She zooped up the stairs to answer the phone before it stopped ringing.

Synset Examples
