tra-la-la

1. NOUN

/ trɑː lɑː lɑː / (AmE), / trɑː lɑː lɑː / (BrE)
a set of nonsensical syllables, often sung or hummed as a refrain or to represent singing without actual words
nonsensical sung syllables

Examples

She walked through the park, singing tra-la-la as the sun shone down.
You can just hum tra-la-la if you forget the words to the song.
The children danced in a circle, chanting tra-la-la and laughing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: la-la-la - nonsensical sung syllables used in singing refrains

Etymology

Imitative of the sound of singing or humming a tune without words.
