spewer

1. NOUN

/ ˈspjuːər / (AmE), / ˈspjuːə / (BrE)
a person who vomits; someone who ejects the contents of their stomach through the mouth
a person who vomits

Examples

After the roller coaster ride, Tom became a notorious spewer among his friends.
The nurse had to attend to another spewer during the outbreak of food poisoning.
He was embarrassed to be called the party's main spewer after feeling ill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vomiter - a person who vomits

Etymology

From Middle English 'spewen', from Old English 'spīwan', meaning 'to spit, vomit'.
