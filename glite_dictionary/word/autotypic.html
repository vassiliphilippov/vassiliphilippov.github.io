autotypic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔː.təˈtɪp.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔː.təˈtɪp.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of autotypy, especially in printing or photoengraving processes.
relating to autotypy (printing)

Examples

The autotypic process revolutionized image reproduction in the publishing industry.
Modern digital techniques have replaced many autotypic printing methods.
The clarity of autotypic engravings made them popular in 19th-century books.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: autotypy - the method of reproducing photographs by means of a halftone process

Etymology

From 'autotypy', modeled after 'autotype' + '-ic'.
