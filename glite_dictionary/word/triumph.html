triumph

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraɪ.əmf / (AmE), / ˈtraɪ.əmf / (BrE)
a great victory or achievement, especially after difficulty or struggle
a great victory or success

Examples

Winning the championship was a remarkable triumph for the inexperienced team.
Her research represented a personal triumph over adversity.
The new bridge is hailed as an engineering triumph across the region.
