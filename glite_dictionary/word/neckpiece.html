neckpiece

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛkˌpis / (AmE), / ˈnɛkˌpiːs / (BrE)
An item of clothing or adornment worn around the neck, such as a collar, scarf, or piece of jewelry.
item worn around the neck

Examples

She wore a beautiful neckpiece encrusted with emeralds to the gala.
The designer created a unique neckpiece out of recycled materials for the runway show.
Traditional dancers often adorn themselves with a decorative neckpiece during performances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collar - the part of a garment that fits around the neck
  • Synonym: necklace - a piece of jewelry worn around the neck
