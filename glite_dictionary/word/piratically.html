piratically

1. ADV

/ pəˈrætɪkli / (AmE), / pɪˈratɪkəli / (BrE)
In a manner characteristic of a pirate; in a way that is illegal or unscrupulous, especially in publishing or reproduction.
in a pirate-like or illegal manner

Examples

The unauthorized copies were distributed piratically across several countries.
She acted piratically when she took credit for someone else's work.
The novel was published piratically without the author's knowledge or consent.

Synset Examples

the piratically published edition of his book

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illegally - in an illegal manner
  • Antonym: lawfully - in a lawful or legal manner

Etymology

From 'piratical' (pirate-like) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
