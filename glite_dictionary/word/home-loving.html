home-loving

1. ADJ

/ ˈhoʊm ˌlʌvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊm ˌlʌvɪŋ / (BrE)
Enjoying and devoted to home life and its pleasures.
devoted to home life

Examples

She is a home-loving person who prefers quiet evenings with her family.
Their home-loving lifestyle centers around cooking and gardening on weekends.
Although he travels often for work, he remains deeply home-loving at heart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: domestic - fond of family life or home activities
  • Antonym: adventurous - willing to try new or exciting experiences
