snoek

1. NOUN

/ snʊk / (AmE), / snʊk / (BrE)
a large marine food fish common along the coasts of Australia, New Zealand, and southern Africa
large marine food fish of the southern oceans

Examples

Fishermen in South Africa often catch snoek during the winter season.
The recipe called for smoked snoek served with potatoes and onions.
Markets in Cape Town are famous for their fresh snoek.

Etymology

From Afrikaans 'snoek', from Dutch 'snoek' (pike).
