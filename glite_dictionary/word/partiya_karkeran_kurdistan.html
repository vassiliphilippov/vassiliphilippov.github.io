Partiya Karkeran Kurdistan

1. PROPN

/ pɑːrˈtiːjə ˌkɑːrkəˈrɑːn kɜːrˈdɪstɑːn / (AmE), / pɑːˈtiːjə ˌkɑːkəˈrɑːn kɜːˈdɪstɑːn / (BrE)
a Kurdish militant and political organization seeking an independent Kurdish state in eastern Turkey, also known as the PKK
Kurdish militant group (PKK) in Turkey

Examples

The Turkish government has accused Partiya Karkeran Kurdistan of involvement in several attacks.
Many countries consider Partiya Karkeran Kurdistan a terrorist organization, while others recognize its political goals.
Talks between the state and Partiya Karkeran Kurdistan have sometimes aimed at peaceful resolution of the conflict.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kurdistan Workers' Party - Common English name for the same organization (PKK)

Etymology

From Kurdish 'Partiya Karkerên Kurdistan' meaning 'Kurdistan Workers' Party'.
