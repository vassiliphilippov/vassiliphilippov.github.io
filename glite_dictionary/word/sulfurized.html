sulfurized

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌlfəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ˈsʌlfəraɪzd / (BrE)
Treated or impregnated with sulfur, often to alter properties or prevent corrosion.
treated with sulfur

Examples

The sulfurized oil helps reduce wear and tear on industrial machinery.
Some types of steel are sulfurized to improve their machinability and performance.
The chemist explained how sulfurized compounds prevent the oxidation of the materials.

Synset Examples

sulfuretted hydrogen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sulfur - a yellow chemical element, symbol S

Etymology

From 'sulfur' + suffix '-ize' (to treat or combine with), with past participle ending.
