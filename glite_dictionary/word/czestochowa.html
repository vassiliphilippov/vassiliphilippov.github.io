Czestochowa

1. PROPN

/ ˌtʃɛstəˈkɔvə / (AmE), / ˌtʃɛstɔˈxɔːvə / (BrE)
A city in southern Poland, renowned for its church housing the statue of the Black Madonna, an important pilgrimage site.
city in southern Poland; site of Black Madonna

Examples

Every year, thousands of pilgrims travel to Czestochowa to visit the iconic shrine of the Black Madonna.
The city of Czestochowa is considered one of the spiritual capitals of Poland.
While touring southern Poland, we spent a memorable day in Czestochowa exploring its historic monastery.
