bitumenoid

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈtuːməˌnɔɪd / (AmE), / bɪˈtjuːməˌnɔɪd / (BrE)
resembling or having the characteristics of bitumen.
resembling bitumen

Examples

The sediment contained bitumenoid materials that indicated the presence of organic matter.
Some shales are notable for their bitumenoid composition, giving them a dark, glossy appearance.
Scientists studied the bitumenoid substances to understand ancient biological processes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bituminous - containing or resembling bitumen

Etymology

From 'bitumen' (a black, sticky substance) + '-oid' (resembling).
