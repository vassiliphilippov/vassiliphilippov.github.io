berk

1. NOUN

/ bɜrk / (AmE), / bɜːk / (BrE)
A stupid or foolish person, especially someone who is easily deceived or taken advantage of.
a stupid or foolish person
slang british

Examples

Don't be such a berk; of course that's not how it works.
He acted like a complete berk at the meeting yesterday.
They tricked the berk into giving away his tickets for free.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid or foolish person
  • Antonym: genius - an exceptionally intelligent person

Etymology

British slang, probably from rhyming slang 'Berkshire Hunt' (a euphemism for a vulgar term).
