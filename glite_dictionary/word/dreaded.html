dreaded

1. ADJ

/ ˈdrɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈdrɛdɪd / (BrE)
regarded with great fear or apprehension; causing dread or terror
causing fear or apprehension

Examples

The students waited nervously for the dreaded exam results to be posted.
