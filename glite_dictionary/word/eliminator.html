eliminator

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɪməˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪmɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that eliminates or removes something
one who or that which eliminates

Examples

The water eliminator removes moisture from the air in the ventilation system.
She was the top eliminator in the first round of the competition.
This new pesticide is an effective eliminator of garden pests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remover - a person or thing that removes
  • Synonym: to eliminate - to completely remove or get rid of something

Etymology

From 'eliminate' + '-or', modeled after Latin agent nouns.
