Libritabs

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪbrɪˌtæbz / (AmE), / ˈlɪbrɪˌtæbz / (BrE)
A trademarked tranquilizer medication (trade name) used in the treatment of alcoholism and anxiety, containing chlordiazepoxide.
tranquilizer trade name (chlordiazepoxide)

Examples

The doctor prescribed Libritabs to help manage his withdrawal symptoms.
She found that Libritabs helped reduce her anxiety during treatment.
Some patients report mild side effects when taking Libritabs for short periods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Librium - a tranquilizer (trade name) used to treat anxiety and alcoholism

Etymology

Trade name from 'Librium' (a related brand) and 'tablets'.
