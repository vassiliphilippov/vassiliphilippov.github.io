jugale

1. NOUN

/ dʒuˈɡeɪli / (AmE), / dʒuˈɡɑːli / (BrE)
a craniometric point where the frontal and temporal processes of the zygomatic bone meet
craniometric point on the cheekbone
medical

Examples

During the skull measurement, the technician located the jugale for reference.
The anthropologist noted a fracture near the jugale on the specimen.
Precise placement of the caliper on the jugale is essential for repeatable results.

Etymology

From Latin 'jugalis', meaning 'of the yoke' or 'pertaining to a joining'.
