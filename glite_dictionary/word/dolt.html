dolt

1. NOUN

/ doʊlt / (AmE), / dəʊlt / (BrE)
a person who is stupid or foolish
stupid or foolish person

Examples

Don't be such a dolt—read the instructions carefully before starting.
She dismissed him as a dolt who would never learn from his mistakes.
Despite his reputation as a dolt, he sometimes came up with clever solutions.

Synset Examples

The economy, stupid!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment; a fool
  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - a person who is exceptionally intelligent or creative

Etymology

From Middle English *dolt* (dull, foolish person), of uncertain origin.
