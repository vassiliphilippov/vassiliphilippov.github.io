apocynaceous

1. ADJ

/ ˌæpəˌsaɪˈneɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌapəsɪˈneɪʃəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of plants belonging to the family Apocynaceae, a large family of mostly tropical flowering plants.
relating to the Apocynaceae plant family

Examples

The frangipani tree is an apocynaceous plant prized for its fragrant blossoms.
Several apocynaceous species contain compounds that are toxic to animals and humans.
Botanists classified the specimen as apocynaceous based on its distinctive milky sap and floral structure.

Etymology

From modern Latin Apocynaceae (the plant family) + -ous.
