bandyleg

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæn.di.lɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈban.dɪ.lɛɡ / (BrE)
A leg that curves outward at or below the knee, causing the knees to be set widely apart; a bowleg.
a leg bowed outward at or below the knee

Examples

The doctor noted that the child had a pronounced bandyleg on the right side.
Years of riding horses gave him a characteristic bandyleg walk.
She became self-conscious about her bandyleg as she grew older.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowleg - a leg that curves outward at or below the knee

Etymology

From 'bandy', meaning to bend outward, plus 'leg'.
