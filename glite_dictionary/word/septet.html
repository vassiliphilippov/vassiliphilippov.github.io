septet

1. NOUN

/ sɛpˈtɛt / (AmE), / sɛpˈtɛt / (BrE)
A musical composition written for seven performers.
music piece for seven performers

Examples

The chamber group performed a septet by Beethoven at last night's concert.
She composed a jazz septet that highlighted every instrument in the ensemble.
Their new album features a septet with piano, strings, and wind instruments.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: quintet - a composition for five performers
  • Degree Higher: octet - a composition for eight performers

Etymology

From French 'septet', modeled after 'quartet', based on Latin 'septem' (seven).
