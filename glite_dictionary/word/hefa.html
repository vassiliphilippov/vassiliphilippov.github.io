Hefa

1. PROPN

/ ˈhaɪfə / (AmE), / ˈhaɪfə / (BrE)
a major port city in northwestern Israel, located on the Mediterranean coast
major port city in northwestern Israel

Examples

She traveled to Hefa to visit friends and explore the city's famous gardens.
The port of Hefa is one of the busiest in Israel, handling significant international trade.
Many tourists are drawn to Hefa for its beautiful beaches and vibrant cultural scene.

Etymology

From Hebrew חֵיפָה (Ḥefa or Ḥaifa).
