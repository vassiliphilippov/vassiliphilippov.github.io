Olimbos

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈlɪmbɒs / (AmE), / əʊˈlɪmbɒs / (BrE)
a mountain in northeast Greece near the Aegean coast, traditionally believed by ancient Greeks to be the home of the gods
mountain in Greece, mythological home of Greek gods

Examples

According to Greek mythology, Olimbos was the residence of the gods.
Climbers from all over the world travel to ascend Olimbos' highest peak.
The legends surrounding Olimbos have inspired countless works of art and literature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Olympus - another name for the same mountain in Greece, mythological home of the Greek gods

Etymology

Variant of Olympus, from Greek Όλυμπος (Ólympos).
