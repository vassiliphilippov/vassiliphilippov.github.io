quadrennium

1. NOUN

/ kwɑːˈdrɛniəm / (AmE), / kwɒˈdrɛniəm / (BrE)
A period of four years.
period of four years

Examples

The Olympic Games are held every quadrennium, bringing together athletes from across the world.
The organization's strategy plan extends for one quadrennium before a major review is conducted.
A new set of policies will be presented at the start of each quadrennium by the committee.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: biennium - a period of two years
  • Degree Higher: decade - a period of ten years

Etymology

From Latin 'quadrennium', from 'quattuor' (four) + 'annus' (year).
