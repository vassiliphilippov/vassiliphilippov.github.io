meow

1. NOUN

/ miˈaʊ / (AmE), / mɪˈaʊ / (BrE)
the sound that a cat makes, or any sound resembling it
cat's vocal sound

Examples

The loud meow from the kitchen told me the cat was hungry.
Every time I open a can, I hear an insistent meow at my feet.
Her ringtone is a realistic meow that always confuses her friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mew - a soft crying sound made by a cat or kitten
  • Synonym: purr - a low continuous vibrating sound made by a cat

Etymology

Imitative origin, representing the natural sound made by cats.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07401214-n
Synset POS: n

2. VERB

/ miˈaʊ / (AmE), / miˈaʊ / (BrE)
(of a cat) to make a characteristic crying sound; to utter a meow.
make a sound like a cat

Examples

The kitten meowed loudly when it was hungry.
She could hear her cat meowing outside the door.
My neighbor’s cat meows every morning to be let inside.

Synset Examples

the cat meowed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mew - to make a soft crying sound, especially as a cat or gull

Etymology

Imitative of the natural sound made by a cat.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01054654-v
Synset POS: v
