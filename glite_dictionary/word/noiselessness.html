noiselessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔɪzləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈnɔɪzləsnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being without noise; complete silence.
the state of being without noise

Examples

The noiselessness of the empty library made every footstep seem loud.
She admired the noiselessness of the new electric car as it glided down the street.
After the storm, an eerie noiselessness settled over the town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silence - absence of sound or noise
  • Synonym: quietness - state of being quiet; little or no noise

Etymology

From 'noiseless' + '-ness', forming a noun indicating the state or quality.
