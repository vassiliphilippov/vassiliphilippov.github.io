teenaged

1. ADJ

/ ˈtiːnˌeɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˈtiːnˌeɪdʒd / (BrE)
being between the ages of 13 and 19; relating to someone in their teenage years.
being between 13 and 19 years old

Examples

The teenaged boy helped his little sister with her homework every evening.
Many teenaged students struggle with balancing school and part-time jobs.
She started her first business when she was just teenaged.

Synset Examples

teenage mothers
the teen years

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teenage - of or relating to people aged 13–19
  • Antonym: adult - a person aged 18 or older; mature person

Etymology

Formed from 'teenager' (from 'teen' meaning thirteen to nineteen) + adjectival suffix '-ed'.
