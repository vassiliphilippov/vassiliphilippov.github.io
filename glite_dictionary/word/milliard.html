milliard

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪliɑrd / (AmE), / ˈmɪliɑːd / (BrE)
A number equal to one thousand million (1,000,000,000), especially as formerly used in British English.
one thousand million (1,000,000,000)
british

Examples

In some European countries, a milliard refers to what Americans call a billion.
The company was valued at over five milliard dollars.
Older British texts sometimes use the term milliard instead of billion.

Synset Examples

in England they call one thousand million a ‘milliard’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: billion - the number 1,000,000,000, especially in American English

Etymology

From French 'milliard', from 'mille' (thousand).
