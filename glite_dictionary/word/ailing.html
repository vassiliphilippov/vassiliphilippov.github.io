ailing

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈeɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
in poor health; slightly ill or prone to illness
in poor health; ill

Examples

The company has provided special care for its ailing employees.
His ailing mother required constant support and attention.
They donated money to help the ailing hospital in their town.

Synset Examples

my poor ailing grandmother

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sick - affected by illness; not well
  • Antonym: healthy - in good health; not ill

Etymology

From the verb 'ail', which comes from Old English eglan (to trouble, pain).
