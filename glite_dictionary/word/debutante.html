debutante

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛb.jəˌtɑnt / (AmE), / ˈdɛb.juːˌtɑːnt / (BrE)
a young woman from an upper-class family who is introduced to society at a formal event for the first time
young woman making first social appearance

Examples

The family's eldest daughter attended the ball as a debutante this year.
In the novel, the debutante nervously greeted guests at her grand introduction.
A lavish party was held to celebrate her coming out as a debutante.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: debut - the first public appearance of a person or thing

Etymology

From French 'débutante', feminine of 'débutant', from 'débuter' (to make a first appearance).
