agonised

/ ˈæɡəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈæɡənʌɪzd / (BrE)

british

Examples

She let out an agonised cry when she heard the tragic news.

Related Terms

Etymology

From agonise + -ed; 'agonise' from Greek agōnizesthai (to contend). Variant of American 'agonized.'
