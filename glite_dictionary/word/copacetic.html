copacetic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkoʊ.pəˈsɛ.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.pəˈsɛ.tɪk / (BrE)
in excellent order or completely satisfactory; fine
in excellent order; satisfactory
informal

Examples

After checking the report, the manager confirmed everything was copacetic.
The two friends agreed that the arrangements were copacetic for their needs.
Please let me know if everything looks copacetic before we finalize the order.

Synset Examples

his smile said that everything was copacetic
You had to be a good judge of what a man was like, and the English was copacetic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: satisfactory - meeting requirements or expectations; acceptable
  • Antonym: unsatisfactory - not meeting necessary requirements; not good enough

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from early 20th-century American slang, sometimes linked to Southern US African-American speech.
