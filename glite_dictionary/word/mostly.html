mostly

1. ADV

/ ˈmoʊstli / (AmE), / ˈməʊstli / (BrE)
For the most part; mainly or chiefly.
mainly; chiefly

Examples

The weather here is mostly sunny throughout the year.
Her diet consists mostly of vegetables and fruits.
Students were mostly satisfied with the changes to the curriculum.

Synset Examples

These accounts are largely inactive

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mainly - chiefly; for the most part
  • Synonym: chiefly - primarily; principally
  • Antonym: partly - to some extent; not completely

Etymology

From 'most' + '-ly'.
