Xmas

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛksməs / (AmE), / ˈɛksməs / (BrE)
An abbreviation of Christmas, a Christian holiday celebrating the birth of Christ, observed on December 25th.
abbreviation for Christmas

Examples

We always exchange gifts on Xmas morning with the whole family.
The store will be closed on Xmas Day and reopen on December 26th.
She sent out Xmas cards to all her friends at the start of December.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Christmas - Christian holiday celebrating the birth of Christ

Etymology

From the Greek letter chi (Χ), the first letter of 'Christos' (Χριστός), combined with 'mas' from 'mass'.
