Samuel Langhorne Clemens

1. PROPN

/ ˈsæmjuəl læŋˌhɔrn ˈklemənz / (AmE), / ˈsamjʊəl læŋˌhɔːn ˈklɛmənz / (BrE)
The real name of Mark Twain, an American writer and humorist best known for his novels about Tom Sawyer and Huckleberry Finn (1835-1910).
real name of Mark Twain, American author

Examples

Samuel Langhorne Clemens is better known under his pen name, Mark Twain.
Many consider Samuel Langhorne Clemens one of the greatest American authors of all time.
Samuel Langhorne Clemens wrote several novels that remain classics of American literature.
