wifely

1. ADJ

/ ˈwaɪfli / (AmE), / ˈwaɪfli / (BrE)
Typical of or suitable for a wife; befitting the character or duties of a wife.
typical of or suitable for a wife

Examples

She cared for her family with a wifely devotion that impressed everyone.
The novel describes her wifely duties in great detail.
He appreciated her calm, wifely advice during difficult times.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: husbandly - befitting or characteristic of a husband

Etymology

From 'wife' + '-ly', formed in Middle English.
