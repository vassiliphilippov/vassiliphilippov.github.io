fuckoid

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌkˌɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈfʌkˌɔɪd / (BrE)
a contemptible or obnoxious person, especially in a social setting such as a party (slang, derogatory)
slang term for an obnoxious person
slang offensive vulgar

Examples

Nobody wanted to talk to the fuckoid at parties because he was so loud and obnoxious.
She avoided inviting that fuckoid to future parties after his rude comments last time.
At every gathering, there's always one fuckoid who ruins the mood for everyone.

Synset Examples

That guy is such a fuckoid at parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - an obnoxious or contemptible person
  • Synonym: asshole - a very rude or unpleasant person (vulgar slang)

Etymology

Formed from 'fuck' (vulgar insult) + '-oid' (suffix meaning 'like' or 'resembling').
