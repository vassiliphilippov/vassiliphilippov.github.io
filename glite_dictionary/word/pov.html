pov

1. NOUN

/ pi oʊ vi / (AmE), / piː əʊ viː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for 'point of view'; used to express a person's perspective or opinion, especially in informal contexts or social media.
abbreviation for 'point of view'
informal

Examples

We should consider his pov before making a choice.
From her pov, the decision made perfect sense.
Online arguments often center on conflicting povs.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: point of view - a particular attitude or perspective

Etymology

Abbreviation of the English phrase 'point of view'.
