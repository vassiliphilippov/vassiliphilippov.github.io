calligraphical

1. ADJ

/ kəˌlɪɡrəˈfɪkəl / (AmE), / kəˌlɪɡrəˈfɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of calligraphy; expressed in a calligraphic style.
relating to calligraphy

Examples

The invitation was written in a calligraphical style that impressed everyone.
Her calligraphical skills set her wedding cards apart from all the others.
The artist is known for his calligraphical flourishes in every piece.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calligraphic - relating to or written in calligraphy

Etymology

From calligraphy + -ical, modeled after words like 'graphical'. Ultimately from Greek kalligraphia ('beautiful writing').
