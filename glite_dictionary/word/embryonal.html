embryonal

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛm.briˈoʊ.nəl / (AmE), / ˌɛm.brɪˈəʊ.nəl / (BrE)
relating to an organism at the stage before birth or hatching; pertaining to embryos or early development.
relating to embryos or early development
medical

Examples

The researcher studied the embryonal development of fish in the laboratory.
Certain cancers are classified as embryonal because they resemble tissues found in early development.
These defects arise during the embryonal period of human growth.

Synset Examples

in the embryonic stage
embryologic development

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embryonic - relating to an embryo or to the early stages of development

Etymology

From New Latin 'embryonalis', from 'embryo' + '-al'.
