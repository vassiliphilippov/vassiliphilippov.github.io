fall-board

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔlˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːlˌbɔːd / (BrE)
the hinged cover that protects the keyboard of a piano when it is not being played
piano keyboard's protective cover

Examples

She closed the fall-board before leaving the room to protect the piano keys from dust.
The old piano's fall-board was beautifully carved with floral patterns.
Please be careful when lowering the fall-board so you don't damage the keys.
