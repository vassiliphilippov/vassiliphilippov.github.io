déjà vu

1. NOUN

/ ˌdeɪʒɑ ˈvuː / (AmE), / ˌdeɪʒɑː ˈvuː / (BrE)
the feeling that a situation you are experiencing has happened before, even though it is actually new
feeling of having experienced something before

Examples

Walking into the old house, she felt a strong sense of déjà vu.
Sometimes I get déjà vu when I meet someone for the first time.
As he listened to the song, a wave of déjà vu washed over him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: familiarity - a feeling of recognizing something

Etymology

From French 'déjà vu' meaning 'already seen'.
