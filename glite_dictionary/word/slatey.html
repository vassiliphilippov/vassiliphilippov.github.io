slatey

1. ADJ

/ ˈsleɪti / (AmE), / ˈsleɪti / (BrE)
Of a bluish-gray color, like that of slate.
bluish-gray in color, like slate

Examples

The slatey sky of dawn hinted at an approaching storm.
Her dress was a soft slatey gray that complemented the winter mood.
Clouds gathered in a slatey mass above the city as evening fell.

Synset Examples

the slaty sky of dawn

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slaty - of or resembling the color of slate
  • Degree Lower: gray - of a color between black and white
  • Degree Higher: blue-gray - a bluish shade of gray

Etymology

From slate (the rock) + -y (adjective-forming suffix).
