tortious

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɔrʃəs / (AmE), / ˈtɔːʃəs / (BrE)
Relating to or constituting a tort; wrongful and subject to civil legal liability.
relating to a tort (legal wrong)
legal

Examples

The company was found liable for its tortious interference with the contract.
Such tortious conduct can result in a lawsuit for damages.
His actions were deemed both negligent and tortious by the court.

Synset Examples

tortious acts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tort - a wrongful act leading to civil legal liability

Etymology

From Anglo-Norman *tortius*, from Latin *tortus* meaning 'twisted' or 'wrong'.
