swordplay

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔrdˌpleɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɔːdˌpleɪ / (BrE)
the skill or activity of fighting with swords, especially in a vigorous or skillful way
skillful or vigorous fighting with swords

Examples

The movie featured impressive swordplay during the climactic battle scene.
His mastery of swordplay earned him respect among his peers.
She was fascinated by the elegance and precision of medieval swordplay.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fencing - sport or skill of fighting with swords

Etymology

From 'sword' + 'play', referring to fighting or fencing with swords.
