Bessemer

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɛsəmər / (AmE), / ˈbɛsɪmə / (BrE)
(1813–1898) British inventor and metallurgist best known for developing the Bessemer process for the mass production of steel.
British inventor and metallurgist

Examples

Sir Henry Bessemer revolutionized the steel industry with his innovative process.
Many modern bridges rely on steel produced using the Bessemer process.
The achievements of Bessemer greatly impacted industrial manufacturing in the nineteenth century.
