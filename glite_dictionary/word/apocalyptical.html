apocalyptical

1. ADJ

/ əˌpɑːkəˈlɪptɪkəl / (AmE), / əˌpɒkəˈlɪptɪkəl / (BrE)
describing or predicting events involving widespread destruction or the end of the world; prophetic of devastation or ultimate doom
prophetic of great destruction or doom

Examples

The movie portrays a bleak, apocalyptical vision of the future.
During the storm, the sky turned an apocalyptical shade of red.
Some religious texts feature apocalyptical warnings about the end times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apocalyptic - describing or prophesying the complete destruction of the world

Etymology

From 'apocalypse' (Greek apokálypsis 'revelation') plus -ical.
