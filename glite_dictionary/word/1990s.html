1990s

1. NOUN

/ naɪnˈtiːn ˈnaɪntiz / (AmE), / naɪnˈtiːn ˈnaɪntiz / (BrE)
the decade from the year 1990 to the year 1999
the decade 1990–1999

Examples

Many popular trends of the 1990s are making a comeback among today's youth.
The internet became widely accessible during the 1990s.
She grew up listening to music from the 1990s.
