cannery

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæn.ə.ri / (AmE), / ˈkæn.əri / (BrE)
a factory where food is preserved and sealed in cans
factory that cans food

Examples

The old cannery by the river has been converted into a museum.
Hundreds of workers were employed at the salmon cannery during peak season.
They took a tour of the local cannery to learn about the canning process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: factory - a building or group of buildings where goods are manufactured

Etymology

From 'can' (the container) + '-ery' (denoting a place of business, as in 'bakery').
