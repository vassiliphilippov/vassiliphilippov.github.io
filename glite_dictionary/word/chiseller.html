chiseller

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪzələr / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪzələ / (BrE)
a person who cheats or swindles others through deception or fraud
swindler; person who cheats others

Examples

The company went bankrupt after trusting a notorious chiseller with their finances.
Don't be a chiseller—honesty in business pays off in the long run.
She discovered that the contractor she hired was a chiseller who took money without completing the work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swindler - a person who cheats or defrauds others
  • Synonym: fraudster - someone who commits fraud
  • Synonym: cheat - a person who acts dishonestly or unfairly to gain an advantage

Etymology

From 'chisel' (to cheat or defraud), originally from the tool 'chisel', by association with cutting or taking away.
