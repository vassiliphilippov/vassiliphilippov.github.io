ohmmeter

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊmˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈəʊmˌmiːtə / (BrE)
an instrument for measuring electrical resistance in ohms
device that measures electrical resistance

Examples

The technician used an ohmmeter to check the resistance of the circuit.
Always disconnect power before measuring resistance with an ohmmeter.
Modern multimeters often include an ohmmeter function for versatile testing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multimeter - an instrument that can measure voltage, current, and resistance
  • Synonym: ammeter - an instrument for measuring electric current
  • Synonym: voltmeter - an instrument for measuring electric potential difference

Etymology

From 'ohm', the unit of electrical resistance (named after German physicist Georg Ohm) + 'meter', from Greek 'metron' (measure).
