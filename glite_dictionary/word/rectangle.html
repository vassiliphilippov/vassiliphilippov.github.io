rectangle

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛkˌtæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈrɛktæŋɡl / (BrE)
A four-sided flat shape with opposite sides equal in length and all angles right angles (90 degrees).
four-sided shape with right angles

Examples

The teacher drew a rectangle on the blackboard to illustrate the problem.
If you measure the sides of a rectangle, you can easily find its area.
A business card is typically shaped like a rectangle.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: square - a rectangle with all four sides equal in length
  • Degree Lower: parallelogram - a four-sided shape with opposite sides parallel

Etymology

From Latin 'rectangulus,' from 'rectus' (right) + 'angulus' (angle).
