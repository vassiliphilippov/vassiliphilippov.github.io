John Lennon

1. PROPN

/ dʒɑn ˈlɛnən / (AmE), / dʒɒn ˈlɛnən / (BrE)
(1940–1980) English musician, songwriter, and member of the Beatles, noted for his influence on popular music.
English musician and member of the Beatles

Examples

Many consider John Lennon to be one of the most influential musicians of the twentieth century.
I grew up listening to songs written by John Lennon and Paul McCartney.
A statue of John Lennon stands in Liverpool to honor his legacy.
