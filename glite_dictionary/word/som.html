som

1. NOUN

/ sɔm / (AmE), / sɒm / (BrE)
the official unit of currency in Kyrgyzstan, equal to 100 tyiyns.
currency of Kyrgyzstan

Examples

You can exchange dollars for som at the airport upon arrival in Bishkek.
The restaurant only accepted payment in som, not foreign currency.
The price of a taxi ride was 300 soms across town.

Etymology

From Kyrgyz сом (som), meaning 'pure'.

2. NOUN

/ sɑm / (AmE), / sɒm / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Uzbekistan, equal to 100 tiyin
currency of Uzbekistan

Examples

You need to exchange your dollars for som when you arrive in Uzbekistan.
The price of admission was 20,000 Uzbek soms per person.
How many soms do I need for a taxi ride from the airport?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tenge - currency of Kazakhstan
  • Degree Lower: tiyin - a subunit of the Uzbek som, equal to 1/100th

Etymology

From Uzbek 'soʻm', meaning 'pure' or 'legitimate', referring to pure money.
