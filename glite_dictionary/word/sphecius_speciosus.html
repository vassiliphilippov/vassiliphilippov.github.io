Sphecius speciosus

1. NOUN

/ ˈsfiːʃi.əs spɪˈsiː.oʊs / (AmE), / ˈsfiːsɪ.əs spɪˈsɪəʊs / (BrE)
a large North American wasp with black or rust-colored markings that preys on cicadas
large wasp species, preys on cicadas

Examples

The Sphecius speciosus is often seen digging burrows in sandy soil.
I was startled by a Sphecius speciosus hunting in my backyard.
Scientists study Sphecius speciosus to learn about the relationship between wasps and cicadas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cicada killer - common name for Sphecius speciosus, a wasp preying on cicadas

Etymology

From Latin: 'Sphecius' is a genus name, and 'speciosus' means 'showy' or 'handsome'.
