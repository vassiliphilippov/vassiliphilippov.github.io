penknife

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛnˌnaɪf / (AmE), / ˈpɛnˌnaɪf / (BrE)
A small folding knife with a blade that fits into the handle, originally used for sharpening quill pens and now commonly used for small cutting tasks.
small folding pocketknife

Examples

He always carries a penknife for emergencies.
She used a penknife to open the package neatly.
The old penknife belonged to his grandfather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pocketknife - a small knife that folds into the handle and is carried in the pocket

Etymology

From 'pen' (as in 'quill pen') and 'knife'; originally used to shape or sharpen quill pens.
