mighty

1. ADJ

/ ˈmaɪti / (AmE), / ˈmaɪti / (BrE)
Having or showing great strength, power, or intensity.
having great strength or power

Examples

The boxer delivered a mighty punch that knocked out his opponent.
A mighty river cut through the valley, shaping the landscape over centuries.
With a mighty effort, she lifted the heavy beam off the trapped animal.

Synset Examples

struck a mighty blow
the mighty logger Paul Bunyan
the pen is mightier than the sword

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powerful - having great power or strength
  • Antonym: weak - lacking physical strength or power

Etymology

From Old English *mihtig*, from *miht* (might, power).
