Bharat

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɑːrət / (AmE), / ˈbɑːrət / (BrE)
The official Hindi name for the Republic of India, a country in South Asia.
official Hindi name for India

Examples

The President of Bharat addressed the nation during the Independence Day celebrations.
Many official documents in India refer to the country as Bharat as well as India.
The constitution recognizes Bharat as the official name of the country in Hindi.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: India - the Republic of India, country in South Asia

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Bharata', name of an ancient Indian king and the country in Hindu epics.
