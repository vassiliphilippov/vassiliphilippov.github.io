hesitantly

1. ADV

/ ˈhɛzɪtəntli / (AmE), / ˈhɛzɪtəntli / (BrE)
In a hesitant manner; with hesitation or uncertainty.
with hesitation or uncertainty

Examples

She hesitantly approached the podium to deliver her speech.
He hesitantly agreed to try the new dish his friend recommended.
The child hesitantly answered the teacher's question, unsure if she was correct.

Synset Examples

he finally accepted hesitantly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: confidently - in a confident manner; without hesitation
  • Synonym: uncertainly - in an uncertain manner

Etymology

From hesitant + -ly, forming an adverb.
