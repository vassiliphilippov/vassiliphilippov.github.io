hardener

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrdənər / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdənə / (BrE)
a substance added to another material, such as paint or varnish, to make it harder or give it a tougher finish.
substance that causes hardening

Examples

Always add the hardener to the resin and mix thoroughly before applying.
The new paint formula includes a hardener for extra durability.
Workers must wear gloves when handling the chemical hardener.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catalyst - a substance that increases the rate of a chemical reaction
