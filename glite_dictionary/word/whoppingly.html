whoppingly

1. ADV

/ ˈwɑːpɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈwɒpɪŋli / (BrE)
To a very great or extraordinary extent; extremely.
extremely; to a great extent
informal

Examples

She was whoppingly surprised by the news.
Sales figures were whoppingly higher than expected this quarter.
The dessert was whoppingly delicious, with layers of rich chocolate.

Synset Examples

She was whoppingly surprised by the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extremely - to a very great degree; very
  • Antonym: slightly - to a small degree; not much

Etymology

From 'whopping' (very large or impressive) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
