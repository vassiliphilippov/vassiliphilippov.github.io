Erechtites

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈrɛk.taɪtiːz / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛk.tɪtiːz / (BrE)
A genus of coarse herbs with whitish, discoid flower heads and silky pappus, belonging to the family Asteraceae.
genus of coarse herbs

Examples

Several species of Erechtites are commonly found in disturbed areas and open woodlands.
The coarse leaves and silky seeds help identify the Erechtites among other wildflowers.
Botanists debated the classification of Erechtites within the Asteraceae family.

Etymology

From Greek 'erechthites', referring to a plant name in classical sources.
