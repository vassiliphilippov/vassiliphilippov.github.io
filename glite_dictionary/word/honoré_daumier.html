Honoré Daumier

1. PROPN

/ ɔˈnɔːreɪ doʊˈmjɛr / (AmE), / ɔːˈnɔːreɪ doʊˈmjɛə / (BrE)
(1808–1879) A French painter, sculptor, and printmaker known especially for his satirical lithographs commenting on 19th-century bourgeois society and politics.
French painter and satirical printmaker

Examples

Many art historians regard Honoré Daumier as a master of satirical illustration.
The museum featured an exhibition dedicated to the works of Honoré Daumier.
Honoré Daumier's lithographs provide a sharp critique of 19th-century Parisian society.
