nondriver

1. NOUN

/ nɑnˈdraɪvər / (AmE), / nɒnˈdraɪvə / (BrE)
a person who does not drive a motor vehicle
person who does not drive

Examples

The application process is the same for a nondriver ID card as it is for a driver's license.
As a nondriver, she relies on public transportation to commute to work.
There are special parking areas designated for nondriver visitors at the event.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: driver - a person who drives a motor vehicle

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'non-' (not) + 'driver'.
