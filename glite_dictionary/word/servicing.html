servicing

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɜːrvɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of mating by a male animal, especially for breeding purposes
the mating of a male animal

Examples

The stud's servicing resulted in several prize-winning foals that year.
They paid a premium for the bull's servicing due to his excellent lineage.
Accurate records are kept for each servicing to ensure proper breeding management.

Synset Examples

the bull was worth good money in servicing fees

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mating - the process by which animals come together to reproduce
  • Synonym: breeding - the process of producing offspring, especially in animals
