idolized

1. ADJ

/ ˈaɪdəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈaɪdəlaɪzd / (BrE)
regarded with deep admiration, love, or reverence, often to an excessive degree
deeply admired or revered

Examples

As a child, she idolized her older brother and followed him everywhere.
The movie star was idolized by fans around the world.
His idolized status made it hard for him to live a normal life.

Synset Examples

adored grandchildren
an idolized wife

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adored - regarded with deep love and respect
  • Antonym: despised - regarded with intense dislike or contempt

Etymology

From idolize + -ed; based on Latin idolum (idol) via Old French.
