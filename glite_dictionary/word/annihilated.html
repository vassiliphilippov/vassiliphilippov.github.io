annihilated

1. ADJ

/ əˈnaɪ.əˌleɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / əˈnaɪ.ɪ.leɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
completely destroyed; wiped out or reduced to nothing
completely destroyed

Examples

The entire town was annihilated by the earthquake and left in ruins.
When the enemy attacked at dawn, their defenses were quickly annihilated.
Centuries of cultural heritage were annihilated during the war.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: destroyed - completely ruined or put an end to
  • Synonym: devastated - caused severe and overwhelming damage
  • Antonym: preserved - kept safe or maintained without damage

Etymology

From Latin annīhilātus (to reduce to nothing), past participle of annīhilāre.
