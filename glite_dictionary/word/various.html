various

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɛr.i.əs / (AmE), / ˈveə.ri.əs / (BrE)
several different; having a great deal of diversity or variety
diverse; of different kinds

Examples

She has published papers in various scientific journals.
The company offers various services to meet customer needs.
The museum displays various artifacts from ancient civilizations.

Synset Examples

his various achievements are impressive
his vast and versatile erudition

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diverse - showing a great deal of variety; very different
  • Antonym: similar - resembling without being identical; alike

Etymology

From Latin 'varius' (variegated, diverse).
