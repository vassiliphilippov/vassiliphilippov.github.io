designing

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈzaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of planning and creating the form or structure of something, often by making sketches or detailed plans
the act of planning or creating something

Examples

The designing of the building took several months before construction began.
She studied designing in college, focusing on furniture and interiors.
Modern technology has greatly influenced the designing of new products.

Synset Examples

he contributed to the design of a new instrument

Related Terms

  • Synonym: design - a plan or drawing produced to show the look and function or workings of something

Etymology

Present participle of 'design', from Latin 'designare' (to mark out, devise).

/ dɪˈzaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
having secret or crafty plans, especially for advancing one's own interests in a deceptive way
crafty or scheming for personal gain

Examples

The detective soon realized that the seemingly helpful neighbor was actually designing and manipulative.
She warned her brother to stay away from the designing stranger who seemed too eager to help.
Throughout history, many rulers have fallen prey to the plots of designing courtiers.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scheming - having a tendency to make secret plans for personal gain
