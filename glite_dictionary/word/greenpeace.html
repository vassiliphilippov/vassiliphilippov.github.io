Greenpeace

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡrinˌpis / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnˌpiːs / (BrE)
an international organization dedicated to environmental conservation and protecting endangered species through campaigning and advocacy
environmental conservation organization

Examples

Greenpeace launched a new campaign to reduce plastic pollution in the oceans.
Many volunteers joined Greenpeace to support reforestation projects around the world.
Greenpeace activists staged a protest at the summit to draw attention to climate change.

Etymology

Formed from 'green' (environmental) + 'peace' (advocating nonviolence), established in Canada in 1971.
