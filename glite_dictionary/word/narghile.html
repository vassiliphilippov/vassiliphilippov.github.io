narghile

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑrɡɪleɪ / (AmE), / ˈnɑːɡɪleɪ / (BrE)
an oriental tobacco pipe with a long flexible tube, in which smoke is passed through water before being inhaled; also known as a hookah or water pipe.
type of oriental water pipe for smoking tobacco

Examples

The group sat together, sharing a narghile and talking late into the night.
He ordered apple-flavored tobacco for his narghile at the café.
A beautifully decorated narghile stood in the corner as part of the restaurant's authentic décor.

Synset Examples

a bipolar world with the hookah and Turkish coffee versus hamburgers and Coca Cola

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hookah - an oriental water pipe for smoking, similar to a narghile
  • Synonym: shisha - another term for hookah or narghile, especially in Middle Eastern contexts

Etymology

From Turkish *nargile*, from Persian *nārgīleh* (water pipe, literally 'coconut'), reflecting the traditional use of a coconut shell as a base.
