arame

1. NOUN

/ əˈrɑː.meɪ / (AmE), / əˈrɑː.meɪ / (BrE)
a type of edible brown seaweed with a mild flavor, commonly used in Japanese cuisine
an edible brown seaweed

Examples

She tossed some arame into the salad for an extra nutritional boost.
Dried arame can be rehydrated and added to soups or stir-fries.
The chef recommended using arame for its delicate taste and subtle texture.

Etymology

From Japanese アラメ (arame), a kind of seaweed.
