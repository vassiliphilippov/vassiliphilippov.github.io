vegan

1. NOUN

/ ˈviː.ɡən / (AmE), / ˈviː.ɡən / (BrE)
A person who does not eat or use any animal products, including meat, dairy, and often leather or other animal-derived materials.
someone who avoids all animal products

Examples

He became a vegan after learning about animal welfare issues.
The restaurant offers several delicious vegan options on its menu.
As a vegan, she avoids wearing leather or wool clothing.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: vegetarian - a person who does not eat meat, and sometimes other animal products

Etymology

Coined in 1944 by Donald Watson, from the first and last letters of 'vegetarian'.

2. ADJ

/ ˈviːɡən / (AmE), / ˈviːɡən / (BrE)
(of food, products, or a diet) not containing any animal products.
not containing animal products (adjective)

Examples

Many restaurants now offer vegan options for customers with dietary restrictions.
This chocolate cake is entirely vegan, so it contains no eggs or dairy.
You can find vegan shampoos that are free from animal-derived ingredients.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: vegetarian - not containing meat, sometimes including other animal products

Etymology

From 'vegan', a shortening of 'vegetarian', coined in English in 1944.
