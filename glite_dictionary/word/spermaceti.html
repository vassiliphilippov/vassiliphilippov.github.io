spermaceti

1. NOUN

/ spɜr.məˈsiː.ti / (AmE), / spɜː.məˈsiː.ti / (BrE)
a white, waxy substance found in the head cavities of the sperm whale, formerly used in making candles, ointments, and cosmetics
waxy substance from sperm whale

Examples

Early candles were often made from spermaceti collected from whaling expeditions.
The ointment contained spermaceti as one of its main moisturizing ingredients.
Trade in spermaceti declined sharply as alternative materials became available.

Etymology

From Latin sperma (seed) + ceti (of a whale), referring to the substance's appearance.
