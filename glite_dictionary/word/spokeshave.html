spokeshave

1. NOUN

/ ˈspoʊkˌʃeɪv / (AmE), / ˈspəʊkʃeɪv / (BrE)
a small woodworking tool with a blade and a handle on each side, used for shaping or smoothing rounded or curved wooden surfaces
tool for shaping curved wood surfaces

Examples

The craftsman used a spokeshave to smooth the handle of the chair.
Restoring antique furniture often requires careful work with a spokeshave.
She reached for her spokeshave to refine the curve of the bannister.

Etymology

From 'spoke' (a rod of a wheel) and 'shave' (to cut in thin slices or layers).
