estranged

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈstreɪndʒd / (AmE), / ɪˈstreɪndʒd / (BrE)
no longer close or affectionate to someone; alienated from someone once loved or with whom one was once close
no longer close; alienated

Examples

After years apart, he felt estranged from his family.
The novel tells the story of an estranged wife seeking reconciliation.
She became estranged from her best friend after the argument.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alienated - experiencing or causing a feeling of separation or isolation

Etymology

From Old French 'estranger', from Latin 'extraneare' (to treat as a stranger).
