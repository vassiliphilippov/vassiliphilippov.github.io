to dingdong

1. VERB

/ ˈdɪŋˌdɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɪŋˌdɒŋ / (BrE)
(of a bell or similar object) to make a repeated ringing sound resembling 'ding dong'.
make a repeated bell-like sound

Examples

The church bells dingdong through the quiet village every Sunday morning.
Suddenly, the old grandfather clock began to dingdong at midnight.
Wind made the chimes on the porch dingdong softly throughout the afternoon.

Etymology

Imitative; from the sound of a ringing bell.
