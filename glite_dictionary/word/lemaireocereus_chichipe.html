Lemaireocereus chichipe

1. NOUN

/ lɛˌmɛr.i.oʊˈsɪəri.əs tʃiˈtʃiːpeɪ / (AmE), / lɛˌmɛəri.əʊˈsɪəri.əs tʃɪˈtʃiːpeɪ / (BrE)
A tall, tree-like cactus native to Mexico, known for its edible red fruit.
Mexican cactus with edible red fruit

Examples

The Lemaireocereus chichipe is prized for its sweet edible fruit in many parts of Mexico.
Botanists studied the unique flowering patterns of Lemaireocereus chichipe in desert ecosystems.
Farmers sometimes cultivate Lemaireocereus chichipe for its drought resistance and nutritional fruits.

Etymology

Genus name from French botanist Charles Lemaire; species name of Mexican origin.
