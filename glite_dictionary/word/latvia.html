Latvia

1. PROPN

/ ˈlæt.vi.ə / (AmE), / ˈlat.vi.ə / (BrE)
A country in northeastern Europe on the eastern coast of the Baltic Sea.
country in northeastern Europe

Examples

She spent her summer vacation exploring the beautiful cities of Latvia.
Riga, the capital of Latvia, is known for its stunning architecture.
Many bird species migrate through Latvia during the spring and autumn.

Etymology

From the native name *Latvija*, adopted in the early 20th century.
