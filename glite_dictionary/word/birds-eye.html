bird's-eye

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɝːdz aɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdz aɪ / (BrE)
(of a view or perspective) seen from above, as if observed by a bird in flight.
seen from above

Examples

The architect presented a bird's-eye view of the proposed development.
From the rooftop, we could see a bird's-eye panorama of the entire city.
The map offers a bird's-eye perspective, showing the layout of roads and buildings.

Synset Examples

a bird's-eye survey
a panoramic view
