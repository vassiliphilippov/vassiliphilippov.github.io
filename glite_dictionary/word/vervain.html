vervain

1. NOUN

/ vərˈveɪn / (AmE), / vəˈveɪn / (BrE)
any of various plants of the genus Verbena, often with showy, colorful spike-like flowers, native to tropical and subtropical Americas
flowering plant of the genus Verbena

Examples

A cluster of vervain bloomed at the edge of the garden, attracting butterflies and bees.
She pressed dried vervain between the pages of her sketchbook as a keepsake.
Many herbal remedies use vervain for its purported calming effects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: verbena - any plant of the genus Verbena or its flowers

Etymology

From Old French verveine, from Latin verbēna (sacred bough or foliage).
