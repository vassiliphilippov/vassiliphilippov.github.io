namer

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪmər / (AmE), / ˈneɪmə / (BrE)
a person who gives a name or names to someone or something
person who gives names

Examples

As the namer of our new puppy, you get to choose whatever you like.
The namer of the park wanted to honor a local hero.
In many cultures, the namer carries great responsibility and respect.

Synset Examples

the owner is usually the namer of a boat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nominator - a person who nominates someone or something

Etymology

From the verb 'name' + -er, denoting a person who performs an action.
