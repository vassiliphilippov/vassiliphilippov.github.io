Proverbs

1. PROPN

/ ˈproʊvɚbz / (AmE), / ˈprəʊvɜːbz / (BrE)
A book of the Old Testament consisting of wise sayings and teachings, traditionally attributed to Solomon and other Israeli sages.
book of wise sayings in the Old Testament

Examples

I have been reading the Book of Proverbs for daily inspiration.
Many people find guidance from the wise sayings in Proverbs.
The pastor quoted a verse from Proverbs during his sermon.

Etymology

From Latin *Proverbia*, the book title, plural of proverbium (proverb).
