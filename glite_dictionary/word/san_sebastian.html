San Sebastian

1. PROPN

/ sæn səˈbæs.tʃən / (AmE), / san səˈbas.ti.ən / (BrE)
a city in northern Spain on the Bay of Biscay near the French border, known as a fashionable seaside resort
city and seaside resort in northern Spain

Examples

Every summer, tourists flock to San Sebastian for its renowned beaches and gourmet cuisine.
During our trip to northern Spain, we spent three days exploring San Sebastian.
The International Film Festival held in San Sebastian attracts filmmakers from around the world.

Etymology

From Spanish 'San Sebastián', named after Saint Sebastian.
