to sulphurette

1. VERB

/ sʌl.fjʊˈrɛt / (AmE), / sʌl.fjəˈrɛt / (BrE)
to combine or treat with sulfur, especially in a chemical process
combine or treat with sulfur

Examples

The chemist decided to sulphurette the compound in order to examine its changed properties.
Some industrial processes sulphurette metals to produce more durable alloys.
They sulphuretted the mixture as part of the purification procedure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sulphurize - to treat or combine with sulfur

Etymology

From 'sulphur' + '-ette', modeled after verbs like 'silicify', used in the 19th-century chemical vocabulary.
