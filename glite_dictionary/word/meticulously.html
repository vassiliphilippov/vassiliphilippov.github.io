meticulously

1. ADV

/ məˈtɪkjələsli / (AmE), / məˈtɪkjʊləsli / (BrE)
In a careful and precise manner, paying great attention to detail.
with great attention to detail

Examples

He meticulously organized his notes before the meeting.
The artist meticulously painted every detail of the landscape.
The report was meticulously checked for errors before submission.

Synset Examples

the set was meticulously authentic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carefully - with care and attention
  • Antonym: sloppily - in a careless or messy manner

Etymology

From 'meticulous' + '-ly'. 'Meticulous' comes from Latin *meticulosus* (fearful).
