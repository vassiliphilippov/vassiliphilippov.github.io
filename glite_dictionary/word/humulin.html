Humulin

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmjʊlɪn / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmjʊlɪn / (BrE)
A brand name for human insulin made using recombinant DNA technology, used to treat diabetes.
brand of human insulin for diabetes
medical

Examples

Her doctor prescribed Humulin to help control her blood sugar levels.
Some patients who are allergic to animal insulin may tolerate Humulin better.
The nurse explained how to store and inject Humulin safely at home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insulin - A hormone regulating blood sugar; used as a medication for diabetes

Etymology

Formed from 'human' and 'insulin'; trademark of Eli Lilly.
