Cornishwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrnɪʃˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnɪʃˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is a native or resident of Cornwall, England
woman from Cornwall, England

Examples

Her grandmother was a proud Cornishwoman who cherished the traditions of her homeland.
The novelist, a Cornishwoman by birth, often set her stories in the rugged landscapes of Cornwall.
As a Cornishwoman, she spoke with a distinct regional accent and loved to prepare traditional Cornish pasties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cornishman - a man who is a native or resident of Cornwall
  • Synonym: Cornish - of, from, or relating to Cornwall

Etymology

From Cornish + woman, denoting a female inhabitant of Cornwall.
