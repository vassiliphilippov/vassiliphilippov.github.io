twistwood

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɪstwʊd / (AmE), / ˈtwɪstwʊd / (BrE)
a vigorous deciduous European shrub common along waysides, bearing red berries that turn black
European shrub with berries

Examples

The hedge was lined with twistwood, its berries ripening in the sun.
Birds frequently feed on the black berries of twistwood in late summer.
After the leaves fell, the twistwood stood bare except for a few lingering berries.
