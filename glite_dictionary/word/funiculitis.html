funiculitis

1. NOUN

/ fəˌnɪkjəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / fjʊˌnɪkjʊˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of a funiculus, especially of the spermatic cord
inflammation of a funiculus
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed the patient with funiculitis after examining his symptoms and medical history.
Chronic funiculitis can cause pain and swelling in the groin area.
Treatment for funiculitis typically involves antibiotics and supportive care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchitis - inflammation of the testes
  • Synonym: epididymitis - inflammation of the epididymis

Etymology

From Latin funiculus (small rope, cord) + -itis (indicating inflammation).
