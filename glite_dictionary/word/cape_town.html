Cape Town

1. PROPN

/ keɪp taʊn / (AmE), / keɪp taʊn / (BrE)
A port city in southwestern South Africa; the country's legislative capital.
port city and legislative capital of South Africa

Examples

Many tourists visit Cape Town to experience its stunning landscapes and vibrant culture.
The South African Parliament meets in Cape Town during its legislative sessions.
Cape Town is renowned for its harbor and proximity to Table Mountain.

Etymology

Named after the Cape of Good Hope, which it is situated near.
