10th

1. ADJ

/ tɛnθ / (AmE), / tɛnθ / (BrE)
coming next after the ninth and before the eleventh in order; ordinal form of ten
ordinal number; position after ninth

Examples

James finished in 10th place in the marathon, just missing the top ten.
Her office is on the 10th floor, overlooking the city skyline.
Our reunion is scheduled for the 10th of August every year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tenth - ordinal number between ninth and eleventh
  • Degree Lower: 9th - ordinal number immediately before tenth
  • Degree Higher: 11th - ordinal number immediately after tenth

Etymology

From Middle English tenth, from ten with the ordinal suffix -th.
