epee

1. NOUN

/ eˈpeɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛpeɪ / (BrE)
A fencing sword similar to a foil but with a heavier, stiffer blade and a large hand guard, used in the sport of fencing.
type of fencing sword

Examples

In the championship match, he chose to compete with his favorite epee.
Her training focused on mastering both the epee and the foil.
Each fencer inspected their epee before stepping onto the piste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foil - a light fencing sword with a flexible blade

Etymology

From French épée, meaning 'sword'.
