machete

1. NOUN

/ məˈʃɛti / (AmE), / məˈʃɛti / (BrE)
a large, heavy knife with a broad blade, used as a weapon or for cutting vegetation
large heavy knife

Examples

He cleared a path through the jungle with his machete.
The farmer used a machete to cut sugarcane in the fields.
She swung her machete carefully to avoid damaging the plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knife - an instrument composed of a blade fixed into a handle, used for cutting

Etymology

From Spanish 'machete', diminutive of 'macho' (sledgehammer).
