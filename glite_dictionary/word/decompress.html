to decompress

1. VERB

/ ˌdiːkəmˈprɛs / (AmE), / ˌdiːkəmˈprɛs / (BrE)
to restore something, especially digital data or a file, to its original, uncompressed form
restore data or files to original form

Examples

The program can easily decompress any zipped archive you download.
After downloading the file, you need to decompress it before you can open it.
The software automatically decompresses the data for faster access.

Synset Examples

decompress data

Related Terms

  • Antonym: compress - reduce the size of data or a file

Etymology

From de- (reverse) + compress (to press together); modeled after the use in data processing.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01392261-v
Synset POS: v

2. VERB

/ diːkəmˈprɛs / (AmE), / diːkəmˈprɛs / (BrE)
to become less tense or stressed; to relax, rest, or take it easy after tension or work
to relax or unwind

Examples

After a stressful week at work, she likes to decompress by reading a good book.
He went for a long walk to decompress and clear his mind.
Spending time with friends helps me decompress after tough days.

Synset Examples

He relaxed in the hot tub
Let's all relax after a hard day's work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to relax - to make or become less tense or anxious
  • Synonym: to unwind - to relax after a period of work or tension
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00026378-v
Synset POS: v

3. VERB

/ ˌdiːkəmˈprɛs / (AmE), / ˌdiːkəmˈprɛs / (BrE)
to reduce or release the pressure in something, especially air or gas pressure.
reduce or release pressure

Examples

Engineers needed to decompress the chamber before entering it safely.
Scuba divers must decompress gradually to avoid getting the bends.
The pilot warned the passengers that he had to decompress the cabin due to a technical issue.

Synset Examples

depressurize the cabin in the air plane

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to pressurize - to increase the pressure of something

Etymology

from de- (remove) + compress, meaning to reverse the process of compression.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00404874-v
Synset POS: v
