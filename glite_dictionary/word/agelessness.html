agelessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪdʒlɪsnəs / (AmE), / ˈeɪdʒləsnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being unaffected by age or the passage of time; timelessness or eternity.
the quality of being timeless or eternal

Examples

The agelessness of the sculpture gave it a sense of mystery and grandeur.
Many admired the actress for her apparent agelessness throughout her long career.
In literature, vampires are often depicted with a striking agelessness that fascinates humans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: timelessness - the quality of being timeless or eternal
  • Synonym: eternity - endless or infinite time

Etymology

From 'ageless' + suffix '-ness', forming a noun meaning the state or quality of being ageless.
