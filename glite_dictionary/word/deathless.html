deathless

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɛθləs / (AmE), / ˈdɛθləs / (BrE)
Never dying; lasting forever; immortal.
never dying; immortal

Examples

His memory achieved a deathless fame in the hearts of his people.
The poet’s deathless words inspired generations.
Legends tell of a deathless spirit haunting the mountain.

Synset Examples

his undying fame

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immortal - living forever; never dying or decaying
  • Antonym: mortal - subject to death; not living forever

Etymology

From 'death' + '-less' (without).
