Texas

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɛk.səs / (AmE), / ˈtɛk.səs / (BrE)
A state in the southwestern United States, the second largest in area, on the Gulf of Mexico.
U.S. state in the southwest

Examples

Many people dream of visiting Texas to experience its unique culture and cuisine.
Oil production has shaped the economy and landscape of Texas for over a century.
The city of Austin, located in Texas, is known for its vibrant music scene.

Etymology

From Caddo (Hasinai) word 'taysha', meaning 'friends' or 'allies'.
