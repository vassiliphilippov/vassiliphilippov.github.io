impuissant

1. ADJ

/ ɪmˈpwɪsənt / (AmE), / ɪmˈpwɪsənt / (BrE)
Lacking physical strength or power; weak or powerless.
lacking strength or power

Examples

Feeling impuissant after his illness, he struggled to climb the stairs.
The protesters considered themselves impuissant against the authority of the government.
Her impuissant efforts to change the system were met with indifference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powerless - without power or strength
  • Synonym: weak - lacking physical strength or energy
  • Antonym: powerful - having great power or strength

Etymology

From French 'impuissant', from Latin 'im-' (not) + 'potentia' (power).
