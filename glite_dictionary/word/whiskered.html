whiskered

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪskɚd / (AmE), / ˈwɪskəd / (BrE)
having whiskers or prominent hair on the cheeks and chin
having whiskers (hair on cheeks/chin)

Examples

The old fisherman was whiskered and tanned from years at sea.
Many species of seal are easily identified by their whiskered faces.
A whiskered cat sat purring on the windowsill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bearded - having a beard; hair growing on the lower part of the face

Etymology

From 'whisker' + '-ed', denoting possession of whiskers.
