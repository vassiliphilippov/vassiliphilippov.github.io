pasted

1. ADJ

/ ˈpeɪstɪd / (AmE), / ˈpeɪstɪd / (BrE)
attached or affixed with paste or glue, or as if by paste or glue.
affixed with paste or glue

Examples

The pasted label started peeling off the jar after a few days.
Several pasted sheets of colored paper decorated the classroom walls.
She found an old book filled with pasted photographs and clippings.

Synset Examples

he stayed glued to one spot
pieces of pasted paper

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glued - stuck fast as if with glue
  • Antonym: unstuck - not affixed or glued, detached
