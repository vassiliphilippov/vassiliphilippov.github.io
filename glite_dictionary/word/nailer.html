nailer

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪlər / (AmE), / ˈneɪlə / (BrE)
a worker whose job is to fasten things with nails
worker who fastens things with nails

Examples

The nailer carefully attached the wooden boards to the frame.
In the shipyard, a skilled nailer was an essential part of the construction crew.
Historically, a nailer would be responsible for making and using nails in various trades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpenter - a person who makes and repairs wooden objects and structures

Etymology

From 'nail' (to fasten with a nail) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
