alky

1. NOUN

/ ˈælki / (AmE), / ˈælki / (BrE)
a person who habitually drinks alcohol to excess; an alcoholic (informal, often derogatory)
habitual excessive drinker; alcoholic
informal slang

Examples

After losing his job, he started hanging out with other alkies at the bar.
Some people call him an alky, but he’s trying to get help.
The movie depicts the life of an alky struggling to rebuild relationships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alcoholic - a person addicted to or habitually drinking alcohol

Etymology

Informal shortening of 'alcoholic'.
