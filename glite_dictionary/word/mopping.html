mopping

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of cleaning a floor or surface with a mop
cleaning with a mop

Examples

After the party, the kitchen needed a thorough mopping to get rid of the sticky mess.
A quick mopping of the hallway kept the floors looking clean and shiny.
The janitor finishes his evening rounds with one final mopping of the lobby.

Synset Examples

he gave it a good mopping

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mop - a tool for cleaning floors, typically with absorbent material
  • Synonym: scrubbing - vigorous cleaning, often with a brush
