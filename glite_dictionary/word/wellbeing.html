wellbeing

1. NOUN

/ wɛlˈbiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌwɛlˈbiːɪŋ / (BrE)
A state of being comfortable, healthy, or happy.
state of being healthy and happy

Examples

Physical exercise is essential for your overall wellbeing.
The government is responsible for the social wellbeing of its citizens.
Meditation can greatly improve a person's sense of wellbeing.

Synset Examples

the town was finally on the upbeat after our recent troubles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welfare - health, happiness, and fortune of a person or group
  • Antonym: ill-being - a state of being unwell or unhappy

Etymology

From 'well' + 'being', recorded since the 16th century.
