penlight

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛnˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɛnˌlaɪt / (BrE)
A small, slender flashlight shaped like a fountain pen, typically used for medical or inspection purposes.
small flashlight shaped like a pen

Examples

The doctor used a penlight to check the patient's pupils.
I always carry a penlight in my bag for emergencies.
She bought a new penlight for her night shifts at the hospital.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flashlight - a portable electric light
  • Synonym: torch - British term for a portable electric light

Etymology

From 'pen' + 'light', reflecting its size and shape.
