nakedly

1. ADV

/ ˈneɪkɪdli / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is not wearing any clothes; without clothing
without clothing

Examples

He stood nakedly at the window, unashamed of his bare form.
The actors performed nakedly for the unconventional scene in the play.
A child ran nakedly through the garden on a hot summer day.

Synset Examples

Henriette saw the weaving figure of an Apache warrior reel nakedly on a pony and rush by with a rifle raised

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naked - without clothes

2. ADV

/ ˈneɪkɪdli / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that leaves someone or something exposed, unprotected, or without concealment
in an exposed or unprotected manner

Examples

The prisoners stood nakedly before their captors, shivering in the cold.
He nakedly admitted his mistakes during the interview.
The truth was nakedly revealed to everyone in the courtroom.

Synset Examples

they were attacked as they huddled nakedly on the bare hill

Related Terms

  • Synonym: openly - in a way that is not hidden or secret
  • Antonym: covertly - in a hidden or secret manner

Etymology

From 'naked' + '-ly', formed in Middle English.
