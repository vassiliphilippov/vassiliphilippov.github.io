Anton Rubinstein

1. PROPN

/ ˈæn.tɑn ruːˈbɪn.stiːn / (AmE), / ˈæn.tɒn ˈruː.bɪn.staɪn / (BrE)
(1829–1894) Russian composer and pianist, noted for his contributions to classical music and for founding the Saint Petersburg Conservatory.
Russian composer and pianist (1829–1894)

Examples

Anton Rubinstein was instrumental in shaping Russian classical music education.
