better

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛtər / (AmE), / ˈbɛtə / (BrE)
in a more satisfactory state, especially in health or fitness, compared to a previous condition

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: worse - in a less good or poorer state or condition
  • Degree Lower: good - of high quality or in a satisfactory state
  • Degree Higher: best - the most satisfactory or excellent
