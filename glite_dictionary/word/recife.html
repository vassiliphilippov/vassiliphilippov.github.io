Recife

1. PROPN

/ heɪˈsiːfeɪ / (AmE), / heɪˈsiːfeɪ / (BrE)
a port city and the capital of Pernambuco state in northeastern Brazil, located on the Atlantic coast
port city in northeastern Brazil

Examples

We spent two beautiful weeks in Recife exploring its beaches and historic sites.
Flights to Recife are often cheaper in the off-season.
Recife has a rich cultural history and vibrant festivals.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'recife' meaning 'reef', referring to the coastal reefs near the city.
