moonlit

1. ADJ

/ ˈmuːn.lɪt / (AmE), / ˈmuːn.lɪt / (BrE)
illuminated by the light of the moon
lit by the moon

Examples

We enjoyed a peaceful walk along the moonlit beach.
The garden looked magical under the moonlit sky.
A moonlit path led the way through the quiet forest.

Synset Examples

the moonlit landscape

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moonlight - light from the moon

Etymology

From 'moon' + 'lit', past participle of 'light.'
