HUM

1. PROPN

/ eɪtʃ juː ɛm / (AmE), / eɪtʃ juː ɛm / (BrE)
An Islamic fundamentalist group formed in Pakistan, formerly active against Soviet forces in Afghanistan and now operating as a terrorist organization mainly in Kashmir.
Islamic group, terrorist organization based in Pakistan/Kashmir

Examples

The militant group known as HUM has been designated a terrorist organization by several countries.
International agencies closely monitor the activities of HUM in the Kashmir region.
Reports suggest that HUM continues to seek political change through violence and insurgency.

Etymology

Acronym for Harkat-ul-Mujahideen, from Arabic and Urdu: 'Movement of the Mujahideen'.
