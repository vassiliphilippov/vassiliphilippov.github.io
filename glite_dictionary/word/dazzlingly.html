dazzlingly

1. ADV

/ ˈdæz.lɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈdæz.lɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that impresses or astonishes greatly, especially by being extremely bright, beautiful, or skillful.
in an impressively bright or skillful way

Examples

The stage was dazzlingly lit for the grand finale of the show.
She was dazzlingly dressed in a shimmering silver gown.
The chef's dazzlingly creative dish impressed the toughest critics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brilliantly - in an impressive or dazzling manner
  • Antonym: modestly - in a way that is not showy or dazzling
