IE

1. NOUN

/ aɪ i / (AmE), / aɪ iː / (BrE)
abbreviation for Internet Explorer, a web browser developed by Microsoft.
Microsoft web browser (abbreviation)
american

Examples

Many websites no longer support IE due to its outdated technology.
Please do not use IE for secure transactions, as it is less safe than other browsers.
The company recommended switching from IE to a more modern browser.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Internet Explorer - Microsoft's web browser, originally bundled with Windows
