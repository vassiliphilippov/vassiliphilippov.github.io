caller-out

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔlər aʊt / (AmE), / ˈkɔːlə aʊt / (BrE)
a person who announces or calls out the changes of steps during a dance, especially in country or folk dance.
person who calls out dance steps

Examples

For the square dance, the caller-out stood at the front and guided everyone through each move.
Without a skilled caller-out, the dancers quickly lost their place during the reel.
The community hired a local caller-out to help teach newcomers the traditional dances.

Synset Examples

you need a fiddler and a caller for country dancing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caller - person who calls out dance steps in folk dancing
