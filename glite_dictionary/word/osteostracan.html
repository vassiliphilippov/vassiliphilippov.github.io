osteostracan

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑstioʊˈstrækən / (AmE), / ˌɒstiəʊˈstrækən / (BrE)
an extinct genus or group of jawless fish from the Devonian period, characterized by an armored head
extinct jawless fish with armored head
medical

Examples

Fossils of the osteostracan reveal a heavy shield covering its head and forebody.
The osteostracan thrived in the shallow seas of the Devonian period.
Some scientists believe that the osteostracan is an evolutionary link to early jawed vertebrates.

Etymology

From Greek osteon (bone) + ostrakon (shell).
