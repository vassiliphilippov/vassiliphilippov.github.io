XXVII

1. NUM

/ ɛks ɛks v aɪ aɪ / (AmE), / ɛks ɛks v aɪ aɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral representing the number 27
Roman numeral for 27

Examples

The pages were numbered with Roman numerals up to XXVII.
Chapter XXVII of the novel reveals the main character's secret.
The monument was built in the year XXVII of the Roman era.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 27 - the number equal to twenty-seven
  • Degree Lower: XXVI - Roman numeral for 26
  • Degree Higher: XXVIII - Roman numeral for 28

Etymology

From Latin numerals: X (10) + X (10) + V (5) + II (2).
