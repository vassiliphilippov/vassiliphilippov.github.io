Harpullia cupanioides

1. NOUN

/ hɑrˈpʊliə kuːˌpæniˈoʊdiːz / (AmE), / hɑːˈpʊliə kjuːˌpæniˈəʊdiːz / (BrE)
A fast-growing tree native to India and the East Indies, valued for its wood especially used in building.
tree of India/East Indies used for timber

Examples

The durable wood of Harpullia cupanioides is widely used in local construction projects.
Botanists documented the growth patterns of Harpullia cupanioides in its native habitat.
Villagers planted Harpullia cupanioides for both shade and timber supply.
