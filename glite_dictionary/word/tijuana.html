Tijuana

1. PROPN

/ tiˈwɑːnə / (AmE), / tiˈwɑːnə / (BrE)
A city in northwestern Mexico, just south of San Diego, known for its vibrant culture and proximity to the US border.
city in northwestern Mexico, near US border

Examples

Many Americans visit Tijuana for its lively nightlife and authentic cuisine.
The border crossing between San Diego and Tijuana is one of the busiest in the world.
Tijuana has experienced rapid growth in recent decades, becoming a major economic center.
