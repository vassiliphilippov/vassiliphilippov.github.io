two-wheel

1. ADJ

/ tuː ˈwiːl / (AmE), / tuː ˈwiːl / (BrE)
(of a vehicle) having or relating to two wheels.
having or relating to two wheels (vehicle)

Examples

She prefers to ride a two-wheel scooter through the city.
The mechanic repaired a classic two-wheel motorcycle from the 1960s.
Learning to balance on a two-wheel bicycle is a rite of passage for many children.

Synset Examples

a two-wheeled cart

Related Terms

  • Antonym: four-wheel - having or relating to four wheels (vehicle)
  • Antonym: three-wheel - having or relating to three wheels (vehicle)
