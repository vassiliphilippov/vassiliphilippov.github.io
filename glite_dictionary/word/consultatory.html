consultatory

1. ADJ

/ kənˈsʌl.təˌtɔr.i / (AmE), / kənˈsʌl.tə.təri / (BrE)
providing or intended to provide advice or guidance; advisory
providing advice; advisory

Examples

The board formed a consultatory committee to assist in the review process.
Her consultatory role involved making recommendations to management rather than enforcing policies.
We appreciated the consultatory tone of the meeting, which encouraged open discussion.

Synset Examples

an advisory memorandum
his function was purely consultative

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advisory - having the characteristic of giving advice
  • Synonym: consultative - having the function of giving advice or discussing; advisory

Etymology

From Latin 'consultare' (to consult) plus English suffix '-ory', indicating 'related to'.
