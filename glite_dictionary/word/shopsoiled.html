shopsoiled

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɑpˌsɔɪld / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpˌsɔɪld / (BrE)
(of goods) having become slightly damaged, faded, or dirty from being displayed or handled in a shop, and therefore considered less valuable.
worn from shop display

Examples

The store offered a discount on all shopsoiled clothing items.
Some of the books were shopsoiled after being handled by many customers.
She noticed a shopsoiled box at the back of the shelf and asked for a lower price.

Synset Examples

shopworn merchandise at half price

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shopworn - (of goods) slightly damaged or faded from long display in a shop

Etymology

From 'shop' + 'soiled', meaning dirtied or tarnished.
