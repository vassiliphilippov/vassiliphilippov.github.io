wych hazel

1. NOUN

/ wɪtʃ ˈheɪzəl / (AmE), / wɪtʃ ˈheɪzəl / (BrE)
a lotion made from an astringent alcoholic solution containing an extract of the witch hazel plant, used chiefly for skin care
astringent skin lotion from witch hazel

Examples

After shaving, he applied wych hazel to soothe his skin.
The pharmacist recommended wych hazel for minor skin irritations and cuts.
She always keeps a bottle of wych hazel in her medicine cabinet for first aid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: witch hazel - shrub from which the lotion is made

Etymology

From the resemblance to the word 'witch hazel'; 'wych' is a variant of 'witch' in this context.
