Acapulco

1. PROPN

/ ˌækəˈpʊlkoʊ / (AmE), / ˌakəˈpʊlkəʊ / (BrE)
a port and resort city on the Pacific coast of southern Mexico, famous for its beaches and water sports
Mexican resort city on the Pacific coast

Examples

We spent our honeymoon in Acapulco, enjoying the famous beaches and lively nightlife.
Many tourists visit Acapulco every year for its warm climate and luxury resorts.
The cliff divers of Acapulco attract crowds with their daring performances.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Acapulco', from Nahuatl 'Aka-pol-co' meaning 'place of reeds'.
