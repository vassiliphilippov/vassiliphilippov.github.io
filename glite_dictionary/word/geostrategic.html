geostrategic

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʒiː.oʊ.strəˈtiː.dʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː.əʊ.strəˈtiː.dʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the strategic importance of a geographic area, especially in politics or military affairs
relating to geographic strategic importance

Examples

The region holds significant geostrategic value for several world powers.
Leaders discussed the geostrategic consequences of the new trade route.
Her analysis highlighted the geostrategic risks facing small island nations.

Synset Examples

Pakistan became a country of paramount geostrategic importance to the United States

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geopolitical - relating to politics influenced by geographical factors

Etymology

From geo- (earth) + strategic, formed on analogy with 'geopolitical'.
