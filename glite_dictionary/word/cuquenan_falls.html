Cuquenan Falls

1. PROPN

/ kuːkəˈnæn fɔlz / (AmE), / kuːkəˈnan fɔːlz / (BrE)
a large and famous waterfall located in southeastern Venezuela, known as one of the highest waterfalls in the world
waterfall in southeastern Venezuela

Examples

Tourists often visit Cuquenan Falls to admire its breathtaking height and scenic surroundings.
The water at Cuquenan Falls drops nearly 2,200 feet from the edge of a table-top mountain.
Photographers are drawn to the dramatic beauty of Cuquenan Falls in Venezuela.
