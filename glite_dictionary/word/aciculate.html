aciculate

1. ADJ

/ əˈsɪkjʊlət / (AmE), / əˈsɪkjʊlət / (BrE)
Having needle-shaped parts or structures, especially referring to leaves, crystals, or animal features.
needle-shaped or having needlelike parts

Examples

The pine tree has aciculate leaves that help reduce water loss.
Many minerals display aciculate crystal formations growing from a central point.
The botanist collected specimens with aciculate stems for further study.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: needle-shaped - shaped like a needle

Etymology

From Latin acicula (small needle), diminutive of acus (needle).
