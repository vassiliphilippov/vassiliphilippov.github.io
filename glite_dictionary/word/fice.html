fice

1. NOUN

/ faɪs / (AmE), / faɪs / (BrE)
A small, nervous, and aggressive mongrel dog, often used to describe an excitable or yapping mixed-breed.
small, aggressive mongrel dog
archaic

Examples

The old man shouted at the fice that barked at every passerby.
Stories of the hunting days often mentioned a courageous little fice.
She was startled by a fice nipping at her heels as she walked through the yard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mongrel - a dog of no definable type or breed
  • Synonym: cur - an aggressive or unkempt dog, especially a mongrel

Etymology

From dialectal English, alteration of 'foist' (mongrel dog), possibly of unknown origin.
