frequenter

1. NOUN

/ ˈfriːkwəntɚ / (AmE), / ˈfriːkwəntə / (BrE)
a person who regularly visits a particular place, especially as a customer or patron
regular visitor or patron

Examples

As a frequenter of the local café, she was greeted by name every morning.
The bookstore gave discounts to every frequenter who joined their loyalty program.
Many frequenters of the park know each other by sight from daily walks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regular - a person who frequents a place regularly
  • Synonym: patron - a customer, especially a regular one, at a store, restaurant, or other establishment

Etymology

From Middle French 'frequenter', from Latin 'frequentator' (one who frequents).
