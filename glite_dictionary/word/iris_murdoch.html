Iris Murdoch

1. PROPN

/ ˈaɪrɪs ˈmɜrdɑk / (AmE), / ˈaɪrɪs ˈmɜːdək / (BrE)
(1919–1999) British novelist and philosopher, known for her psychologically complex characters and exploration of moral themes.
British novelist and philosopher (1919–1999)

Iris Murdoch is celebrated for her philosophically rich and psychologically acute novels.
