PST

1. NOUN

/ pi ɛs ti / (AmE), / piː ɛs tiː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for Pacific Standard Time, the standard time zone 8 hours behind Greenwich Mean Time, used in the far western United States and parts of Canada and Mexico
Pacific Standard Time zone (GMT-8)
american

Examples

The meeting is scheduled for 3:00 PST, which is 6:00 Eastern Time.
Our office hours are 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. PST Monday through Friday.
Broadcasts will begin at 7:00 PST, so please adjust your clocks accordingly.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: PDT - Pacific Daylight Time, the daylight saving time variant for the Pacific time zone
  • Degree Higher: GMT - Greenwich Mean Time, the time standard from which time zones are calculated
  • Degree Higher: MST - Mountain Standard Time, the time zone immediately east of PST

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'Pacific Standard Time'.
