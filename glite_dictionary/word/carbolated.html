carbolated

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑrbəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbəˌleɪtɪd / (BrE)
Containing or treated with carbolic acid.
containing carbolic acid

Examples

The surgeon washed his hands with carbolated soap before the procedure.
Hospitals once used carbolated dressings to prevent infection in wounds.
The laboratory cleaned equipment with a carbolated solution for disinfection.

Etymology

From 'carbolic (acid)' + '-ated', forming a chemical adjective.
