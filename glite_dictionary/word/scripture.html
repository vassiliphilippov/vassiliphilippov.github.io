Scripture

1. NOUN

/ ˈskrɪptʃər / (AmE), / ˈskrɪptʃə / (BrE)
The sacred writings of the Christian religions, especially the Bible.
Christian sacred writings; the Bible

Examples

Many people turn to Scripture for guidance during difficult times.
He reads passages from Scripture every morning as part of his routine.
In the sermon, the priest quoted from various parts of Scripture to illustrate his message.

Synset Examples

he went to carry the Word to the heathen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bible - The Christian sacred scriptures
  • Degree Higher: scriptures - The sacred writings of a religion

Etymology

From Latin 'scriptura' (writing), from 'scribere' (to write).
