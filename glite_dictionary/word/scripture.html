scripture

1. NOUN

/ ˈskrɪptʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈskrɪptʃə / (BrE)
any sacred writing or text regarded as authoritative by a religious group
sacred religious text

Examples

Many people find comfort and guidance in their religious scripture.
The teacher read a passage from the scripture during the ceremony.
Each faith community interprets its scripture in a unique way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holy writ - sacred scriptures, especially of Christianity
  • Synonym: canon - the collection of books accepted as genuine and sacred in a religion

Etymology

From Latin 'scriptura' (writing, scripture), from 'scribere' (to write).
