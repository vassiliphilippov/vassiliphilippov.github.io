frier

1. NOUN

/ ˈfraɪ.ər / (AmE), / ˈfraɪ.ə / (BrE)
A kitchen appliance used for deep frying food in hot oil.
appliance for deep frying food

Examples

The chef carefully placed the potatoes in the frier to make crispy fries.
Our restaurant just bought a new frier for faster food preparation.
Be sure to clean the frier regularly to maintain the quality of the oil.

Synset Examples

Deep fryers are used for cooking many fast foods, and making them crisp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deep fryer - appliance designed for deep frying

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'fryer', from 'fry' (to cook in hot fat or oil) + '-er'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈfraɪər / (AmE), / ˈfraɪə / (BrE)
a young chicken of medium size, especially one suitable for cooking by frying
young chicken for frying

Examples

The chef selected a fresh frier for the special dinner menu.
Supermarkets often sell friers labeled as ideal for pan-frying or deep-frying.
She bought a whole frier to make her grandmother's favorite fried chicken recipe.

Related Terms
