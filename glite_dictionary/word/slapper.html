slapper

1. NOUN

/ ˈslæpɚ / (AmE), / ˈslapə / (BrE)
A person who slaps someone or something, especially with an open hand.
person who slaps with an open hand

Examples

The slapper was quickly identified by the people in the crowd.
During the game, the slapper was penalized for unsportsmanlike conduct.
She confessed to being the mysterious slapper who pranked her friends.

Synset Examples

someone slapped me on the back and I turned to see who the slapper was
my father was the designated spanker in our family

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spanker - a person who spanks (hits with an open hand, especially on the buttocks)

Etymology

From slap (verb) + -er (agent noun suffix).
