winnings

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪnɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈwɪnɪŋz / (BrE)
money or prizes that are won, especially in gambling or competitions.
money or prizes won

Examples

Her casino winnings totaled over a thousand dollars last weekend.
He donated a portion of his lottery winnings to charity.
Most contestants spent their winnings on vacations or new cars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prize - an award given as a result of winning a contest
  • Antonym: losses - money or items lost, especially in gambling or competitions

Etymology

From 'win' + plural suffix '-ing(s)'.
