mucosal

1. ADJ

/ mjuˈkoʊzl̩ / (AmE), / mjuˈkəʊzl̩ / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the mucous membranes of the body
relating to mucous membranes
medical

Examples

The doctor observed some mucosal inflammation inside the patient’s mouth.
Certain drugs are absorbed through the mucosal lining of the intestines.
Mucosal surfaces act as barriers to many pathogens entering the body.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mucous - producing or covered with mucus
  • Antonym: serous - relating to, producing, or resembling serum

Etymology

From 'mucosa' (Latin for mucous membrane) plus the suffix '-al' meaning 'relating to'.
