Bogota

1. PROPN

/ boʊɡəˈtɑː / (AmE), / bəʊɡəˈtɑː / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Colombia, situated in central Colombia on a high fertile plateau
capital city of Colombia

Examples

She traveled to Bogota for an international conference on education.
The climate in Bogota is cooler than many other Colombian cities due to its elevation.
Many startups have set up offices in Bogota to tap into Colombia's growing tech sector.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Bogotá', originally from the name of the indigenous Muisca settlement 'Bacatá'.
