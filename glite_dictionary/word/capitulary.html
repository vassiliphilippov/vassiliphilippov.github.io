capitulary

1. ADJ

/ kəˈpɪtʃəˌlɛri / (AmE), / kəˈpɪtʃələri / (BrE)
relating to an ecclesiastical chapter or its members, duties, or property
of an ecclesiastical chapter

Examples

The capitulary statutes determined the rights of the canons.
He examined the capitulary records held by the chapter.
Many medieval churches were funded by capitulary estates.

Synset Examples

capitular estates

Etymology

From Medieval Latin capitularis ('of a chapter'), from Latin capitulum ('chapter').
