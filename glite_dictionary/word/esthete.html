esthete

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛsˌθit / (AmE), / ˈiːsθiːt / (BrE)
a person who has or professes to have a special appreciation of art and beauty
one who appreciates art and beauty

Examples

As an esthete, she spent her weekends visiting art galleries and museums.
The poet was known as an esthete with a passion for beauty in all its forms.
He was criticized for being an esthete who valued style over substance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aesthete - alternative (esp. British) spelling of 'esthete', same meaning

Etymology

From Greek aisthetēs (one who perceives), from aisthanesthai (to perceive).
