seance

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪ.ɒns / (AmE), / ˈseɪ.ɒns / (BrE)
a meeting at which people try to communicate with the spirits of the dead, often through a medium
meeting to communicate with spirits

Examples

The group gathered for a seance in hopes of reaching their late friend.
During the seance, the medium claimed to receive messages from the other side.
Some people were skeptical about the results of the seance.

Synset Examples

the seance was held in the medium's parlor

Etymology

From French séance (a sitting, session), from Latin sessio (a sitting).
