Krakatau

1. PROPN

/ ˌkrɑːkəˈtaʊ / (AmE), / ˌkrækəˈtaʊ / (BrE)
a volcanic island in Indonesia between Java and Sumatra, famous for its catastrophic eruption in 1883
volcanic island in Indonesia

Examples

The eruption of Krakatau in 1883 had devastating global effects.
Scientists still study the geological changes caused by Krakatau.
Tourists visit the remains of Krakatau to witness its powerful legacy.

Etymology

From Indonesian 'Krakatau'.
