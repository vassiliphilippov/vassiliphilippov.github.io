to Simonize

1. VERB

/ ˈsaɪməˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪmənaɪz / (BrE)
to polish and treat (a motor vehicle's surface) with wax, especially using a commercial product, to achieve a shiny, protective finish
polish a vehicle with wax

Examples

Every spring, Tom makes time to Simonize his vintage car until it gleams.
The dealership promised to Simonize the car as part of the purchase agreement.
After she Simonized the motorcycle, the paintwork looked brand new.

Synset Examples

The motorcycle has been Simonized

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polish - to make the surface of something smooth and shiny by rubbing it

Etymology

Trademark of Simoniz, a brand of automobile wax, from early 20th-century United States.
