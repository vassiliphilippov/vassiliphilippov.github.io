lessening

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛsənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛsənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of becoming smaller or less in amount, degree, or intensity; a reduction or decrease
reduction or decrease

Examples

The doctor noticed a lessening of symptoms after the new medication was prescribed.
There has been a significant lessening in public interest since last year.
The company reported a lessening of demand for its services in the fourth quarter.

Synset Examples

there was a decrease in his temperature as the fever subsided
there was a sharp drop-off in sales

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decrease - the process of becoming less
  • Synonym: reduction - the process of making something smaller or less
  • Antonym: increase - the process of becoming larger or greater

Etymology

From Middle English lessen (to lessen) + -ing (forming nouns of action).
