Microgramma

1. NOUN

/ ˌmaɪkroʊˈɡræmə / (AmE), / ˌmʌɪkrəʊˈɡramə / (BrE)
A genus of small epiphytic ferns native to tropical regions of America and Africa.
genus of small epiphytic ferns

Examples

Microgramma thrives on tree trunks in the humid forests of South America.
Some species within the genus Microgramma are popular among fern enthusiasts for their unusual leaves.
Researchers discovered a new Microgramma species in the forests of West Africa.

Etymology

From Greek 'mikros' (small) + 'gramma' (letter, line), referring to the small sporangia or sori.
