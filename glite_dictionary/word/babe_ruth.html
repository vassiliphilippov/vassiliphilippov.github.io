Babe Ruth

1. PROPN

/ ˌbeɪb ˈruːθ / (AmE), / ˌbeɪb ˈruːθ / (BrE)
(1895–1948) United States professional baseball player, widely regarded as one of the greatest hitters in the history of the sport, famous for his home run record.
legendary American baseball player

Examples

Many consider Babe Ruth the greatest baseball player of all time.
The records set by Babe Ruth stood for decades in Major League Baseball.
A statue of Babe Ruth stands outside the stadium in Baltimore.
