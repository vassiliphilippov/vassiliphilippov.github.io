unfenced

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfɛnst / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɛnst / (BrE)
(of land or an area) not enclosed or surrounded by a fence.
not enclosed by a fence

Examples

The cattle wandered freely across the unfenced fields.
An unfenced garden is more vulnerable to animals and trespassers.
Many wild animals roam in unfenced reserves.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fenced - enclosed or surrounded by a fence
