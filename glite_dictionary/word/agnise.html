to agnise

1. VERB

/ æɡˈnaɪz / (AmE), / aɡˈnaɪz / (BrE)
to recognize, acknowledge, or admit something as true; to be aware of something
recognize or acknowledge
archaic

Examples

She could not agnise the painful truth even when it was clear.
The old manuscript required careful study to agnise its authorship.
He quietly agnised his mistakes during the conversation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to recognize - to perceive or show awareness of something; acknowledge
  • Antonym: to ignore - to refuse to take notice of; disregard

Etymology

From Latin agnoscere (to recognize, acknowledge).
