volcanically

1. ADV

/ vɑlˈkænɪkli / (AmE), / vɒlˈkænɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to, characteristic of, or caused by volcanoes.
in a volcanic manner or by volcanoes

Examples

The island was formed volcanically millions of years ago.
The region is volcanically active, with several eruptions each decade.
This unique terrain developed volcanically rather than through erosion.

Synset Examples

volcanically created landscape

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volcanic - relating to or produced by a volcano

Etymology

From 'volcanic' + '-ally', modeled on adverbial forms.
