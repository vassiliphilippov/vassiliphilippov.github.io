Philippines

1. PROPN

/ ˌfɪlɪˈpinz / (AmE), / ˌfɪlɪˈpiːnz / (BrE)
a country consisting of an archipelago of over 7,000 islands in the southwestern Pacific Ocean, southeast of Asia
country in Southeast Asia, Pacific archipelago

Examples

The Philippines is known for its beautiful beaches and rich biodiversity.
She traveled to the Philippines last summer to visit her relatives.
Many festivals are celebrated throughout the Philippines each year.

Etymology

From Spanish *Filipinas*, named after King Philip II of Spain.

2. PROPN

/ fɪˈlɪpiːnz / (AmE), / ˌfɪlɪˈpiːnz / (BrE)
a country in Southeast Asia consisting of an archipelago of over 7,000 islands; achieved independence from the United States in 1946
country in Southeast Asia, island nation

Examples

The Philippines is known for its beautiful beaches and rich cultural heritage.
She spent a year teaching English in the Philippines.
Typhoons frequently affect the Philippines during the rainy season.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Filipinas', named in honor of King Philip II of Spain.
