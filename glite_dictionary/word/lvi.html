LVI

1. NUM

/ ɛl vi aɪ / (AmE), / ɛl vi aɪ / (BrE)
The Roman numeral representing the number 56.
Roman numeral for 56

Examples

The page was marked with LVI in the bottom corner, following the tradition of using Roman numerals.
Super Bowl LVI was held in Los Angeles in 2022.
The document cited law number LVI in its references.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: LV - Roman numeral for 55
  • Degree Higher: LVII - Roman numeral for 57

Etymology

From Latin Roman numerals: L (50) + V (5) + I (1).
