nimblewill

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪmbəlˌwɪl / (AmE), / ˈnɪmbəlˌwɪl / (BrE)
A slender, branching American grass (Muhlenbergia schreberi) found chiefly in central United States, sometimes used for grazing.
a slender North American grass

Examples

The pasture was filled with nimblewill and other native grasses.
Some farmers allow cattle to graze on nimblewill when better forage is scarce.
Identifying nimblewill can be challenging due to its fine, wiry stems.
