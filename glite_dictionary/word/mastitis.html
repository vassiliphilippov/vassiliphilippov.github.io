mastitis

1. NOUN

/ mæˈstaɪdɪs / (AmE), / maˈstaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the breast or udder, typically due to infection
breast or udder inflammation
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with mastitis after noticing swelling and redness in her breast.
Dairy farmers must watch for signs of mastitis in their cows to maintain healthy milk production.
Prompt treatment is important to prevent complications from mastitis.

Etymology

From Greek 'mastos' (breast) + '-itis' (inflammation).
