El Paso

1. PROPN

/ ɛl ˈpæsoʊ / (AmE), / ɛl ˈpɑːsəʊ / (BrE)
A city in western Texas, United States, situated on the northern bank of the Rio Grande, across from Ciudad Juárez, Mexico.
city in western Texas, USA, on the Mexican border

Examples

After moving to El Paso, she quickly adapted to the desert climate.
The border between El Paso and Juárez is one of the busiest in the world.
Many people travel through El Paso on their way to other parts of Texas.

Etymology

Spanish for 'the pass', referring to a mountain pass near the city.
