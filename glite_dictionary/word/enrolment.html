enrolment

1. NOUN

/ ənˈroʊlmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈrəʊlmənt / (BrE)
The process of officially joining or registering for a course, institution, or organization.
the act of registering or joining

Examples

University enrolment numbers have increased significantly this year.
You must complete your enrolment before attending classes.
After her successful enrolment, she received a welcome letter from the school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: registration - the act of officially recording something or enrolling
  • Antonym: withdrawal - the act of removing oneself from a course, institution, or organization

Etymology

From enroll + -ment, ultimately from Old French enroller (to inscribe in a list).
