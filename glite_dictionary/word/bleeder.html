bleeder

1. NOUN

/ ˈbliːdər / (AmE), / ˈbliːdə / (BrE)
a person who suffers from hemophilia and is prone to uncontrollable bleeding
person with hemophilia
medical

Examples

Doctors must take extra care when treating a bleeder to prevent severe blood loss.
As a bleeder, she must avoid situations where she could get injured.
Special medications are available to help a bleeder manage their condition safely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hemophiliac - a person with hemophilia
