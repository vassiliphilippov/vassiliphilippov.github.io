Hindustan

1. PROPN

/ ˌhɪnduˈstɑn / (AmE), / ˌhɪndʊˈstɑːn / (BrE)
A historical and cultural region in northern India, traditionally where Hinduism predominates.
northern region of India

Examples

Many classical poets described Hindustan as a land of great rivers and plains.
The Mughal Empire once extended over much of Hindustan.
To some, the term Hindustan serves as a symbol of India's cultural heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: India - a country in South Asia

Etymology

From Persian 'Hindūstān' (land of the Hindus), from 'Hind' (India) + '-stān' (land).
