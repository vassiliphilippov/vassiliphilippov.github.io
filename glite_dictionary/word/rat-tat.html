rat-tat

1. NOUN

/ ˈrætˌtæt / (AmE), / ˈrætˌtæt / (BrE)
a series of short, sharp taps or knocks, especially as made on a door or a drum
series of sharp taps or knocks

Examples

The rat-tat at the door startled everyone in the room.
We could hear the rat-tat of drums echoing down the street.
A quick rat-tat was her secret signal to let me know she had arrived.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rapping - a series of quick, sharp knocks
  • Degree Higher: bang - a sudden loud noise, often from striking

Etymology

Imitative of the sound of knocking or tapping.
