musclebuilder

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsəlˌbɪldər / (AmE), / ˈmʌsəlˌbɪldə / (BrE)
a person who performs specific exercises to develop large and strong muscles, especially for appearance or competition
person who builds large muscles through exercise

Examples

He spent years training as a musclebuilder to compete in national championships.
A dedicated musclebuilder follows a strict workout and diet routine.
The gym was full of musclebuilders lifting heavy weights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bodybuilder - a person who strengthens and enlarges their muscles through exercise, often for competition

Etymology

From 'muscle' + 'builder', indicating one who builds muscle.
