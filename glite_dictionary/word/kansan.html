Kansan

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæn.sən / (AmE), / ˈkan.sən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the state of Kansas in the United States
person from Kansas, USA

Examples

As a proud Kansan, she always celebrates her state's heritage.
The Kansan delegation arrived early for the conference in Washington, D.C.
He grew up as a Kansan before moving to New York for college.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kansas - a state in the central United States

Etymology

Formed from Kansas + -an, a common English suffix for demonyms.
