sulphuretted

1. ADJ

/ sʌlˈfjʊrɛtɪd / (AmE), / sʌlˈfjʊərɛtɪd / (BrE)
treated, impregnated, or combined with sulfur, especially in a chemical process.
treated or combined with sulfur
archaic

Examples

The chemist analyzed the sulphuretted solution for traces of other elements.
Exposure to sulphuretted compounds in the mine caused a distinct odor in the air.
19th-century laboratories often produced sulphuretted water for experiments.

Synset Examples

sulfuretted hydrogen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sulfurated - treated or impregnated with sulfur

Etymology

From 'sulphur' + past participle ending '-etted', used in 18th–19th century chemical terms.
