fifth

1. ADJ

/ fɪfθ / (AmE), / fɪfθ / (BrE)
Coming next after the fourth in a sequence; ordinal number for five.
ordinal number for five

Examples

She finished in fifth place in the race, just missing out on a medal.
Today is the fifth anniversary of our moving into this house.
His office is on the fifth floor of the building.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fourth - coming next after the third; ordinal for four
  • Degree Higher: sixth - coming next after the fifth; ordinal for six

Etymology

From Old English 'fīfta', from 'fīf' (five).
