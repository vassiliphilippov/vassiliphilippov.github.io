serious-mindedness

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪriəsˈmaɪndɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌsɪəriəsˈmaɪndɪdnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being serious, earnest, or thoughtful in attitude or behavior.
quality of being serious or earnest

Examples

Her serious-mindedness made her a respected leader in the organization.
He approached the task with a level of serious-mindedness that impressed everyone.
Despite her youth, her serious-mindedness was evident in how she handled difficult situations.

Synset Examples

a lack of solemnity is not necessarily a lack of seriousness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seriousness - the quality of being serious
  • Antonym: lightheartedness - the quality of being cheerful and carefree
