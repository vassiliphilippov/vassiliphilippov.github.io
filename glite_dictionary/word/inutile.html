inutile

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈnjuːtəl / (AmE), / ɪˈnjuːtaɪl / (BrE)
Having no use or value; useless.
useless; of no value or use
archaic

Examples

The device was deemed inutile after multiple failed attempts to repair it.
He found their suggestions inutile in solving the problem at hand.
The old manual typewriter sat inutile in the attic for years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: useless - Having no use, purpose, or value

Etymology

From Latin 'īnutilis' meaning 'useless', from 'in-' (not) + 'utilis' (useful).
