9th

1. ADJ

/ naɪnθ / (AmE), / naɪnθ / (BrE)
coming next after the eighth and just before the tenth in position; ordinal form of nine
ordinal number, position after eighth

Examples

She celebrated her 9th birthday with a big party and all her friends.
The conference will be held on the 9th floor of the building.
He finished in 9th place in the marathon, just ahead of his teammate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ninth - spelled-out form of 9th
  • Degree Lower: 8th - ordinal number before 9th
  • Degree Higher: 10th - ordinal number after 9th

Etymology

From Old English nigoþa, based on 'nine' plus the ordinal suffix '-th'.
