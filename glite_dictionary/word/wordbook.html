wordbook

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːdˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈwɜːdˌbʊk / (BrE)
a reference book listing words, often with their meanings; a dictionary or vocabulary book
reference book listing words or vocabulary

Examples

He found the unfamiliar term in a dusty old wordbook on the shelf.
Each student in the class was given a wordbook to build their vocabulary.
The early English wordbook included not only definitions but also pronunciation guides.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dictionary - a book listing words of a language with their meanings
