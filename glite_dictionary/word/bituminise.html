to bituminise

1. VERB

/ bɪˈtjuːməˌnaɪz / (AmE), / bɪˈtjuːmɪnaɪz / (BrE)
to treat or coat (a surface) with bitumen
treat or coat with bitumen

Examples

The old road was bituminised to improve its durability and reduce maintenance costs.
Engineers decided to bituminise the car park to prevent dust and erosion.
After they bituminised the playground, it became safer for children to play during wet weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to bituminize - to treat or coat with bitumen (US spelling)

Etymology

From 'bitumen', with the verb-forming suffix '-ise'.
