unadventurous

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ədˈvɛn.t̓ʃə.rəs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ədˈvɛn.tʃə.rəs / (BrE)
not willing to take risks or try new or exciting things; lacking in boldness.
not willing to take risks

Examples

Her unadventurous nature kept her from traveling to unfamiliar places.
He stuck to his usual order at restaurants, being rather unadventurous with food.
The film was criticized for its unadventurous plot and predictable ending.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: adventurous - willing to take risks or try new things
  • Synonym: timid - lacking courage or confidence

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'adventurous'.
