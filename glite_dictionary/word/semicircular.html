semicircular

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛm.iˈsɪr.kjə.lɚ / (AmE), / ˌsɛm.iˈsɜː.kjʊ.lə / (BrE)
having the shape or form of a half circle; curved like a semicircle.
shaped like a half circle

Examples

The ancient theater had a semicircular arrangement of seats around the stage.
We sat around the table in a semicircular formation to face the speaker.
The architect designed a semicircular window that overlooked the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circular - having the shape of a circle

Etymology

From Latin 'semi-' (half) + 'circulus' (circle).
