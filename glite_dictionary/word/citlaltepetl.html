Citlaltepetl

1. PROPN

/ sit.lalˈte.pɛtəl / (AmE), / sit.lalˈtɛ.pɛtl / (BrE)
an extinct volcano in southern Mexico, also known as Pico de Orizaba; the highest peak in Mexico, standing at 18,695 feet (5,636 meters)
highest peak and volcano in Mexico

Examples

Climbers from around the world attempt to reach the summit of Citlaltepetl every year.
The snow-capped peak of Citlaltepetl can often be seen from many miles away.
Citlaltepetl is an important landmark in Mexican geography and culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pico de Orizaba - alternative name for Citlaltepetl, the highest peak in Mexico

Etymology

From Nahuatl *Citlāltepetl*, meaning 'Star Mountain' (*citlalli* 'star' + *tepetl* 'mountain').
