Strasbourg

1. PROPN

/ ˈstræz.bɜrɡ / (AmE), / ˈstræz.bɜːɡ / (BrE)
a city on the Rhine in eastern France, near the German border, known as an important port and for its historical and cultural significance
city in eastern France, near Germany

Examples

The European Parliament regularly meets in Strasbourg.
After studying in Paris, she moved to Strasbourg for her new job.
Strasbourg is famous for its beautiful medieval city center.

Etymology

From French Strasbourg, derived from Germanic roots meaning 'town at the (Roman) roads'.
