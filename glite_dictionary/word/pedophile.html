pedophile

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛdəˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈpiːdəʊfaɪl / (BrE)
A person, typically an adult, who is sexually attracted to children.
adult sexually attracted to children

Examples

The documentary explored the mindset of a convicted pedophile serving a life sentence.
Laws are in place to protect children from pedophiles who seek to exploit them.
The court ordered a psychological evaluation for the accused pedophile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: child molester - person who sexually abuses children
  • Synonym: hebephile - adult sexually attracted to early pubescent children

Etymology

From Greek pais (child) + philia (love, friendship).
