opaquely

1. ADV

/ oʊˈpeɪkli / (AmE), / əʊˈpeɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that is difficult to see through or understand; obscurely or unclearly.
in an obscure or unclear manner

Examples

The government spokesperson answered opaquely when questioned about the issue.
Her motives remained opaquely hidden throughout the investigation.
The instructions were written opaquely, making them hard to follow.

Synset Examples

he referred opaquely to her recent past

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obscurely - in an unclear or difficult-to-understand manner

Etymology

From "opaque" (from Latin opacus ‘shaded’) + -ly.
