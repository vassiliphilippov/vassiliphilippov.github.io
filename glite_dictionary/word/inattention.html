inattention

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.əˈtɛn.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.əˈtɛn.ʃən / (BrE)
the failure to notice or pay attention to something; the state of being inattentive
lack of attention

Examples

His accident was caused by his inattention while driving.
Students' inattention often leads to mistakes on exams.
The teacher was frustrated by the class's inattention during the lesson.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: attention - notice taken of someone or something
  • Synonym: heedlessness - carelessness; lack of paying attention

Etymology

Formed from 'in-' (not) and 'attention', from Latin 'attendere' (to attend).
