Allium cepa

1. NOUN

/ ˈæliəm ˈsiːpə / (AmE), / ˈælɪəm ˈsiːpə / (BrE)
the scientific name for the common onion, a bulbous plant with hollow leaves widely cultivated for its edible spherical bulb
the scientific name for onion

Examples

The scientific name for the common onion is Allium cepa.
Farmers cultivate Allium cepa around the world for its edible bulbs.
During botany class, we studied the life cycle of Allium cepa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: onion - a round vegetable with a layer of papery skin and a pungent aroma, used in cooking

Etymology

From Latin: 'allium' (garlic) and 'cepa' (onion).
