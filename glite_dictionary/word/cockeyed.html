cockeyed

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑk.aɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.aɪd / (BrE)
(informal) Very drunk; intoxicated to a great degree.
very drunk; intoxicated
informal slang

Examples

After too many beers at the party, he was completely cockeyed.
She stumbled down the street, obviously cockeyed from the celebration.
You can't believe anything he says when he's cockeyed like that.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drunk - affected by alcohol to the point of losing control of one's faculties

absurd or wildly unreasonable

Examples
