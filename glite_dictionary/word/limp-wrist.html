limp-wrist

1. ADJ

/ lɪmp rɪst / (AmE), / lɪmp rɪst / (BrE)
(of a man) displaying behavior or mannerisms regarded as weak, effeminate, or stereotypically associated with homosexuality (often considered offensive).
weak or effeminate in manner (offensive)
offensive

Examples

He was mocked for his limp-wrist gestures in the office.
The comedian used a limp-wrist walk to parody certain stereotypes.
Using a limp-wrist portrayal on stage is now widely criticized as disrespectful.

Synset Examples

The actor portrayed a limp-wrist character to great comedic effect.
He was teased for his limp-wristed way of speaking.
