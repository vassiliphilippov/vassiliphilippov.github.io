sixty-fourth

/ ˌsɪksti ˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / ˌsɪksti ˈfɔːθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number corresponding to sixty-four; being number 64 in a sequence
the ordinal number for 64th

Examples

Today marks the sixty-fourth anniversary of the founding of the school.
He celebrated his sixty-fourth birthday with his family and friends.

/ ˌsɪksti ˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / ˌsɪksti ˈfɔːθ / (BrE)

Examples
