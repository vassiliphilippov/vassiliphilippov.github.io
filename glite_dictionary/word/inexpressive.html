inexpressive

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnɪkˈsprɛsɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪnɪkˈsprɛsɪv / (BrE)
(of a person's face or manner) showing little or no expression or emotion.
showing little or no emotion

Examples

Despite the shocking news, his inexpressive face revealed nothing.
She maintained an inexpressive look throughout the entire performance.
His inexpressive eyes gave no hint of his true feelings.

Synset Examples

an inexpressive face

Related Terms

  • Antonym: expressive - showing emotions or thoughts clearly and openly

Etymology

From Latin in- (not) + expressive (from express, meaning to communicate or show).
