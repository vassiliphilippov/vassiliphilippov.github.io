lockbox

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑːk.bɑks / (AmE), / ˈlɒk.bɒks / (BrE)
a strong, usually fireproof metal box, often used in banks, for securely storing valuables
secure strongbox for valuables

Examples

She kept her most important documents in a lockbox at the bank.
The jeweler stored the diamonds overnight in a secure lockbox.
Each employee was given a personal lockbox for confidential materials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safe - a strong, secure box for storing money or valuables
  • Synonym: deposit box - a secure box in a bank used to store valuables
