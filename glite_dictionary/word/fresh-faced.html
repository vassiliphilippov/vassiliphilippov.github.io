fresh-faced

1. ADJ

/ ˈfrɛʃ ˌfeɪst / (AmE), / ˈfrɛʃ ˌfeɪst / (BrE)
(of a person) having a youthful, healthy, and innocent appearance.
having a youthful and innocent appearance

Examples

The fresh-faced intern surprised everyone with her competence and energy.
After a good night's sleep, he appeared fresh-faced at the breakfast table.
The campaign used fresh-faced models to promote a sense of vitality and innocence.

Synset Examples

The fresh-faced actor charmed everyone at the premiere.
She looked fresh-faced after her weekend getaway.
His fresh-faced demeanor made him popular among classmates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: youthful - looking or feeling young
  • Synonym: innocent - lacking experience or worldly knowledge; pure
