hemispheric

1. ADJ

/ ˌhɛmɪˈsfɪrɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɛmɪˈsfɛrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the cerebral hemispheres of the brain.
relating to the brain's hemispheres
medical

Examples

The patient exhibited hemispheric differences in motor function after the stroke.
Scientists observed increased hemispheric activity during the memory task.
A hemispheric lesion was detected in the left side of the brain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cerebral - of or relating to the brain

Etymology

From hemisphere + -ic, formed after French hémisphérique.
