Lilium

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪliəm / (AmE), / ˈlɪliəm / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants known as true lilies, belonging to the family Liliaceae.
genus of true lilies

Examples

The garden was filled with several species of Lilium, each displaying vibrant blooms.
Botanists classify many ornamental lilies under the genus Lilium.
Some members of Lilium are cultivated for their striking flowers and pleasant scent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lily - common name for flowering plants of the genus Lilium

Etymology

From Latin *lilium* (lily).
