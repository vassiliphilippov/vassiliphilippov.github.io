ump

1. NOUN

/ ʌmp / (AmE), / ʌmp / (BrE)
an official who watches a baseball game closely and enforces the rules; short for umpire
baseball official; short for umpire
informal

Examples

The ump made a controversial call that upset the home team.
Kids shouted at the ump from the stands after he called strike three.
Every little league game needs an experienced ump to keep things fair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: umpire - an official who watches a game and enforces the rules

Etymology

Shortened form of 'umpire,' first used in the late 19th century.
