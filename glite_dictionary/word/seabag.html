seabag

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌbæɡ / (BrE)
a cylindrical bag with a drawstring, typically used by sailors to carry clothing and personal belongings
bag used by sailors for belongings

Examples

He packed all his clothes into the seabag before boarding the ship.
Each sailor must keep his seabag organized during the voyage.
The old seabag was faded from years of travel at sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: duffel bag - a large cylindrical bag for carrying personal items, especially by travelers or soldiers

Etymology

From 'sea' + 'bag,' referring to its use by sailors.
