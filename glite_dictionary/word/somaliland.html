Somaliland

1. PROPN

/ soʊˈmɑːlɪˌlænd / (AmE), / səʊˈmɑːlɪˌlænd / (BrE)
a region in the Horn of Africa that declared independence from Somalia in 1991 but is internationally recognized as an autonomous region of Somalia
unrecognized state in the Horn of Africa

Examples

The government of Somaliland has pursued stability and democratic elections despite international non-recognition.
Many investors see Somaliland as a safe haven compared to the rest of Somalia.
She traveled through Somaliland to study its unique political system.

Etymology

From 'Somali' (referring to the Somali people) + 'land'.
