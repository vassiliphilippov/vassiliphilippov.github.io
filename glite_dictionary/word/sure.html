sure

1. ADJ

/ ʃʊr / (AmE), / ʃɔː / (BrE)
certain to happen or to be true; infallible or unfailing.

Examples

A quick response is a sure indication of good customer service.
Her victory seemed sure after she gained a large lead early in the race.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certain - known for sure; established beyond doubt
  • Synonym: unfailing - always working or reliable; without fail
  • Antonym: doubtful - not known with certainty; open to doubt
