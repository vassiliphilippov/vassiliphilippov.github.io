NB

1. NOUN

/ ˌenˈbi / (AmE), / ˌenˈbiː / (BrE)
used in writing to indicate that something is particularly important or should be given special attention; note well.
used to mark important notes

Examples

He wrote NB in the margin to highlight the key point.
Please NB: the deadline for applications has changed.
Her notes often included an NB to draw attention to crucial details.

Synset Examples

the margins of his book were generously supplied with pencilled NBs

Etymology

Abbreviation of Latin 'nota bene' meaning 'note well'.
