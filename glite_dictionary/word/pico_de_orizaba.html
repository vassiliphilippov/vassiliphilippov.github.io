Pico de Orizaba

1. PROPN

/ ˈpiːkoʊ deɪ ɔːˈriːzəbə / (AmE), / ˈpiːkəʊ deɪ ɒˈrɪzəbə / (BrE)
an extinct volcano in southern Mexico, situated between Mexico City and Veracruz; it is the highest peak in Mexico at 18,695 feet (5,636 meters).
the highest mountain in Mexico

Examples

Many climbers attempt to summit Pico de Orizaba each year due to its challenging routes.
From the top of Pico de Orizaba, you can see for miles across the Mexican landscape.
Snow covers the upper slopes of Pico de Orizaba even during the hottest months.

Etymology

Spanish for 'Peak of Orizaba'.
