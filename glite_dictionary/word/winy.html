winy

1. ADJ

/ ˈwaɪni / (AmE), / ˈwaɪni / (BrE)
Having the taste, smell, or qualities of wine.
having the taste or character of wine

Examples

The sauce had a winy aroma that complemented the beef.
Some cheeses develop a winy flavor as they age.
I prefer red wines with a pleasantly winy aftertaste.

Synset Examples

a rich winy taste

Etymology

From wine + -y, forming adjectives meaning 'having the qualities of'.
