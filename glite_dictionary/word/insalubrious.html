insalubrious

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnsəˈluːbriəs / (AmE), / ˌɪnsəˈluːbriəs / (BrE)
(of a place or environment) unhealthy; making you likely to become ill; detrimental to health.
unhealthy; harmful to health

Examples

Many people avoid the insalubrious parts of the city after dark.
Living in such insalubrious conditions can seriously affect your health.
The restaurant was shut down due to its insalubrious environment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unhealthy - not beneficial to health; harmful
  • Antonym: salubrious - health-giving; healthy

Etymology

From Latin 'insalubris', from 'in-' (not) + 'salubris' (healthful).
