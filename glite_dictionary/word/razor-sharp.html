2. ADJ

/ ˈreɪzər ˌʃɑrp / (AmE), / ˈreɪzə ˌʃɑːp / (BrE)
Extremely sharp, as sharp as a razor; used both literally to describe an edge or blade, or figuratively to mean very keen or acute.
extremely sharp or keen

Examples

The chef's knife was razor-sharp and sliced through the tomato effortlessly.
Her razor-sharp wit made her a formidable debater.
After hours of honing, the blade became razor-sharp.
