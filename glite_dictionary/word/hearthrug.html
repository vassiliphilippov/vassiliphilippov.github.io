hearthrug

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrθˌrʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːθˌrʌɡ / (BrE)
A rug placed in front of a fireplace to protect the floor and provide comfort.
rug in front of a fireplace

Examples

She placed a new hearthrug in front of the fire for extra warmth.
The puppy curled up on the hearthrug as the fire crackled nearby.
Years of use had left the hearthrug faded, but it was still comfortable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rug - a piece of thick material used as a floor covering

Etymology

From 'hearth' (the floor of a fireplace) + 'rug'.
