workbook

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːkˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌbʊk / (BrE)
a book containing exercises and problems, with spaces provided for students to write their answers
book with exercises for students

Examples

Please complete page 15 in your workbook for homework tonight.
The math workbook includes practice problems for every lesson.
She bought a new workbook to help her prepare for the exam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exercise book - a book for students to write lessons or practice exercises
