qibla

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪblə / (AmE), / ˈkɪblə / (BrE)
the direction that Muslims face when praying, towards the Kaaba in Mecca
direction of prayer in Islam

Examples

Muslims determine the qibla before they pray, ensuring they face Mecca.
The hotel room had a marking on the ceiling indicating the direction of the qibla.
He used a compass app to find the qibla while traveling.

Etymology

From Arabic *qibla* (direction).
