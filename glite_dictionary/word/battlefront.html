battlefront

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæt̬.əl.frʌnt / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əl.frʌnt / (BrE)
the line or area where opposing armies face each other and fighting occurs during a battle.
line or area where armies face each other in battle

Examples

The soldiers advanced toward the battlefront at dawn.
News from the battlefront was often slow to reach the capital.
After months on the battlefront, the troops were finally rotated out for rest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: front line - the foremost line or position of an armed force
