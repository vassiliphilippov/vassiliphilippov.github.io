snotty-nosed

1. ADJ

/ ˈsnɑtiˌnoʊzd / (AmE), / ˈsnɒtiˌnəʊzd / (BrE)
(of a person, especially a child) showing a lack of respect or behaving in a rude, arrogant manner.
impudently disrespectful or arrogant (esp. of a child)

Examples

She found his snotty-nosed attitude hard to ignore during the meeting.
The coach refused to put up with any snotty-nosed remarks from the young players.
Don't be so snotty-nosed—try to treat your classmates with respect.

Synset Examples

a flip answer to serious question
the student was kept in for impudent behavior

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impudent - lacking respect or showing rudeness
  • Antonym: respectful - showing respect or courtesy

Etymology

From 'snotty' (covered with nasal mucus) + 'nosed'; figuratively extended to mean 'disrespectful' by association with childishness.
