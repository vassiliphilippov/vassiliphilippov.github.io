restlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈrɛst.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈrɛst.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a restless manner; in a way that shows inability to relax or stay still, often due to anxiety or impatience.
in a restless way

Examples

She paced restlessly up and down the hallway, waiting for news.
The children tossed restlessly in their beds during the thunderstorm.
He tapped his foot restlessly while waiting for the meeting to start.

Synset Examples

he cracked his knuckles restlessly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restless - unable to relax or be still
