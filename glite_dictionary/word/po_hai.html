Po Hai

1. PROPN

/ poʊ haɪ / (AmE), / pəʊ haɪ / (BrE)
an inlet of the Yellow Sea along the coast of eastern China, forming a major body of water near Beijing and Tianjin
inlet of the Yellow Sea in eastern China

Examples

Many cargo ships travel through the Po Hai to reach ports in northern China.
The region around the Po Hai is known for its heavy industry and economic importance.
Environmental concerns have increased due to pollution in the Po Hai.
