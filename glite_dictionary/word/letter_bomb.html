to letter bomb

1. VERB

/ ˈlɛt̬.ɚ bɑm / (AmE), / ˈlɛt.ə bɒm / (BrE)
to send an explosive device concealed in a letter or package to a person or organization, typically with intent to harm
send an explosive device via mail

Examples

The suspected terrorist letter bombed several government offices last year.
Authorities believe someone may try to letter bomb the company after recent threats.
He was convicted of letter bombing politicians during the campaign.

Synset Examples

The Unabomber letter bombed a number of individuals and institutions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bomb - to attack with or as if with bombs
