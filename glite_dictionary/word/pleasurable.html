pleasurable

1. ADJ

/ ˈplɛʒərəbəl / (AmE), / ˈplɛʒərəbl / (BrE)
Giving or capable of giving pleasure or satisfaction.
giving pleasure or satisfaction

Examples

The walk through the garden was a truly pleasurable experience for everyone involved.
She finds reading novels to be one of the most pleasurable ways to relax after work.
Their vacation in Italy turned out to be more pleasurable than they had ever imagined.

Synset Examples

the company was enjoyable
found her praise gratifying
full of happiness and pleasurable excitement
good printing makes a book more pleasurable to read

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enjoyable - giving pleasure or satisfaction
  • Antonym: painful - causing pain or discomfort

Etymology

From Middle English 'plesur' (pleasure) + '-able', from Old French 'plaisir'.
