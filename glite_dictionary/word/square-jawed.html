square-jawed

1. ADJ

/ skwɛr dʒɒd / (AmE), / skwɛə dʒɔːd / (BrE)
having a jaw that is broad and angular, giving the face a square appearance
having a broad, angular jaw

Examples

He was a handsome, square-jawed hero straight out of an old movie.
The detective’s square-jawed profile made him look determined and tough.
Many comic book characters are drawn with a square-jawed appearance to emphasize strength.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: weak-chinned - having a receding or less prominent jaw
