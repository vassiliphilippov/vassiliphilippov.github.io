dacitic

1. ADJ

/ dəˈsɪtɪk / (AmE), / dəˈsɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of dacite, a light-colored volcanic rock consisting mainly of plagioclase and quartz.
relating to dacite rock

Examples

The region is characterized by extensive dacitic lava flows dating back millions of years.
Researchers analyzed the dacitic composition of the volcanic ash to determine its origin.
The mountain's summit is formed by a dome of dacitic rock, making it unique among nearby peaks.

Synset Examples

dacitic magma is highly viscous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dacite - a light-colored volcanic rock

Etymology

From 'dacite' + -ic, denoting relation or belonging.
