silva

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvə / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə / (BrE)
The collective forest trees of a particular country or region.
the forest trees of a region

Examples

The Amazon silva is home to countless plant species.
The ancient silva of Europe once stretched across vast areas.
Researchers studied the changes in the local silva over several decades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forest - a large area covered chiefly with trees and undergrowth

Etymology

From Latin 'silva' meaning 'forest, woodland'.
