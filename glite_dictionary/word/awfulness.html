awfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔf.əl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɔːf.əl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely unpleasant or disagreeable
extreme unpleasantness

Examples

The awfulness of the meal left everyone disappointed.
She struggled to find words to describe the awfulness of the situation.
Despite the awfulness of the weather, the event was not canceled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dreadfulness - the quality of being very bad or unpleasant
  • Antonym: wonderfulness - the quality of being extremely good, pleasant, or admirable

Etymology

From 'awful' + '-ness', denoting the state or quality.
