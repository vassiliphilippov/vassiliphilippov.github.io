jade-green

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒeɪd ˌɡrin / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪd ˌɡriːn / (BrE)
having a color resembling that of jade, typically varying from bluish green to yellowish green
color between bluish green and yellowish green

Examples

She wore a stunning jade-green dress to the gala.
The river glimmered with a mysterious jade-green hue under the morning sun.
He painted the walls jade-green to bring a sense of calm to the room.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: emerald-green - having a bright, vivid green color similar to that of an emerald
  • Degree Lower: olive-green - having a yellowish-green color resembling that of olive skin or leaves

Etymology

From 'jade', the name of the gemstone, combined with 'green'.
