orange-coloured

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having the color of ripe oranges; being of an orange hue.
of the color of ripe oranges

Examples

She wore an orange-coloured dress to the summer party.
The kitten played with an orange-coloured ball of yarn.
An orange-coloured sunset lit up the sky above the mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orange - a color between red and yellow in the spectrum; the color of ripe oranges
