23

1. NUM

/ twɛnti θri / (AmE), / twɛnti θri / (BrE)
the number representing three more than twenty; twenty-three.
the number twenty-three

Examples

There are 23 students in the classroom today.
She celebrated her 23rd birthday last week with her friends.
The book is divided into 23 chapters, each covering a different topic.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-two - the number representing two less than twenty-four
  • Degree Higher: twenty-four - the number representing one more than twenty-three
