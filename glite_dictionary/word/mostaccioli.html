mostaccioli

1. NOUN

/ moʊˌstɑː.tʃiˈoʊ.li / (AmE), / mə(ʊ)ˌstæ.tʃiˈəʊ.li / (BrE)
A type of tubular pasta similar to penne, typically cut diagonally and often used in baked pasta dishes.
type of tubular pasta

Examples

She served the baked mostaccioli with a rich tomato sauce and melted cheese.
The restaurant offers several dishes featuring mostaccioli, including vegetarian and meat options.
For tonight's dinner, I decided to cook mostaccioli instead of spaghetti.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: penne - a type of short, diagonally cut tubular pasta

Etymology

From Italian mostaccioli, plural of mostacciolo, from Latin mustaceum (a type of cake or must bread).
