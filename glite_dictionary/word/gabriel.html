Gabriel

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡeɪbriəl / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪbriəl / (BrE)
In the Bible and other religious texts, the archangel who serves as God's messenger.
archangel; God's messenger in the Bible

Examples

According to the Bible, Gabriel delivered messages from God to several important figures.
Many works of art depict Gabriel announcing the birth of Jesus to Mary.
In both Christianity and Islam, Gabriel plays a vital role as a messenger of God.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: archangel - an angel of high rank; chief angel

Etymology

From Hebrew 'Gavri'el', meaning 'God is my strength'.
