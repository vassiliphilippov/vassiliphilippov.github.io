Orudis

1. NOUN

/ ɔːˈruːdɪs / (AmE), / ɔːˈruːdɪs / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for the nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug ketoprofen, used to reduce pain and inflammation.
brand name for ketoprofen (pain reliever)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Orudis to help relieve her joint pain.
Some patients prefer Orudis over other anti-inflammatory medications due to fewer side effects.
You should not take Orudis if you have a history of stomach ulcers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ketoprofen - the generic drug known as Orudis
