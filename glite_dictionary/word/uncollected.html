uncollected

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.kəˈlɛk.tɪd / (AmE), / ʌn.kəˈlɛk.tɪd / (BrE)
not having been gathered or brought together into one place; not collected.
not gathered or collected

Examples

The project was delayed due to uncollected data from several sources.
After the festival, uncollected litter covered the entire park.
He grew anxious about the uncollected mail piling up in his mailbox.

Synset Examples

uncollected garbage in the streets

Related Terms

  • Antonym: collected - having been gathered together

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' to 'collected'.
