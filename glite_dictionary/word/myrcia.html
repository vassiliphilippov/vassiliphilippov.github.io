Myrcia

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪrsiə / (AmE), / ˈmɪəsɪə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical American trees and shrubs of the myrtle family (Myrtaceae)
genus of tropical American trees and shrubs

Examples

Several species of Myrcia are prized for their aromatic leaves.
Researchers discovered a new Myrcia species in the Amazon rainforest.
The Myrcia genus is important for its ecological role in tropical forests.

Etymology

From New Latin, based on Greek 'myrke' (myrtle).
