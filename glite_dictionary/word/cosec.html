cosec

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊˌsɛk / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌsɛk / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'cosecant'; the ratio of the hypotenuse to the opposite side in a right-angled triangle.
abbreviation of cosecant (trigonometric ratio)

Examples

The formula for cosec theta is the reciprocal of sine theta.
You often see cosec in trigonometric identities and equations.
The graph of cosec x has vertical asymptotes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cosecant - the trigonometric function; ratio of hypotenuse to opposite side

Etymology

Shortened from 'cosecant', which is from Latin co- (complementary) + secans (cutting).
