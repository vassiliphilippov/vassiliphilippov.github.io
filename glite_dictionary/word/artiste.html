artiste

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈtist / (AmE), / ɑːˈtiːst / (BrE)
a professional performer, especially a singer, dancer, or stage entertainer.
professional stage performer

Examples

The famous artiste captivated the audience with her powerful voice.
Several artistes performed at the charity concert last night.
He developed his skills as an artiste early in his career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: performer - a person who entertains an audience
  • Synonym: entertainer - someone whose job is to entertain people
  • Synonym: artist - a person who creates art, especially fine art, but can also mean performer

Etymology

From French 'artiste' (artist, performer), from Latin 'artista'.
