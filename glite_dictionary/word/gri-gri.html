gri-gri

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡri ˌɡri / (AmE), / ˈɡriː ˌɡriː / (BrE)
A tropical American feather palm with a swollen, spiny trunk and edible nuts.
a tropical American feather palm

Examples

The gri-gri palm thrives in the humid forests of the Caribbean.
Local villagers gather the nuts from the gri-gri tree to eat or sell.
The swollen trunk of the gri-gri makes it easy to recognize among other palms.
