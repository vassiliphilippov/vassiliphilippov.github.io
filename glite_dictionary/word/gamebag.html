gamebag

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪmˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmˌbaɡ / (BrE)
a bag made of canvas or leather for carrying game, especially birds, killed by a hunter
bag for carrying hunted game

Examples

The hunter slung his gamebag over his shoulder before setting out at dawn.
After a successful shoot, she emptied the partridges from her gamebag onto the table.
His old leather gamebag had seen many seasons in the field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: haversack - a small sturdy bag carried on the back or over the shoulder
