George Armstrong Custer

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɔrdʒ ˈɑrmstrɔŋ ˈkʌstər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒ ˈɑːmstrɒŋ ˈkʌstə / (BrE)
(1839–1876) United States Army general, best known for his defeat and death at the Battle of the Little Bighorn against the Sioux and Cheyenne.
American general killed at Little Bighorn

Examples

The defeat of George Armstrong Custer at the Battle of the Little Bighorn became legendary in American history.
A biography of George Armstrong Custer explores his controversial military career and personal life.
Many tourists visit the site where George Armstrong Custer made his last stand.
