clipboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɪpˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈklɪpˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a small flat board with a clip at the top for holding papers in place for writing or reading
board with a clip for holding papers

Examples

The nurse wrote down the patient's information on her clipboard as she walked between rooms.
Please attach your resume to the clipboard at the front desk when you arrive.
The coach carried a clipboard to make notes during the game.

Etymology

From 'clip' + 'board', indicating its function as a board with a clip.
