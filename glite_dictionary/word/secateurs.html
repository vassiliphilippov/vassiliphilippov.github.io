secateurs

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛkəˈtɝz / (AmE), / ˌsɛkəˈtɜːz / (BrE)
a pair of small pruning shears with a spring mechanism, used for cutting plants or branches in gardening.
small pruning shears for gardening

Examples

She trimmed the roses with her favorite secateurs.
Be sure to clean your secateurs after each use to prevent disease.
A good pair of secateurs is essential for any gardener.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pruning shears - gardening scissors for cutting plants

Etymology

From French 'sécateur' (pruning shears), from Latin 'secare' (to cut).
