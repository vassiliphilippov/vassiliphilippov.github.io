to astound

1. VERB

/ əˈstaʊnd / (AmE), / əˈstaʊnd / (BrE)
to surprise or shock someone very much; to fill with wonder or amazement
to surprise greatly; amaze

Examples

Her creativity never fails to astound me.
The magician's final trick astounded the entire audience.
It may astound you to learn how much technology has changed in just a decade.

Synset Examples

Your ability to speak six languages amazes me!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to amaze - to fill with great surprise or wonder
  • Synonym: to stun - to shock or surprise greatly
  • Antonym: bore - to make uninterested or weary

Etymology

From Latin *extonare* (to thunder out), via obsolete English *astone*.
