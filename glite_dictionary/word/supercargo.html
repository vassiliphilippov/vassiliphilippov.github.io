supercargo

1. NOUN

/ ˌsuːpərˈkɑːrɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈkɑːɡəʊ / (BrE)
an officer on a merchant ship who is responsible for the cargo and its sale and purchase during the voyage
officer in charge of ship's cargo

Examples

The supercargo was responsible for negotiating trade deals at every port.
After inspecting the hold, the supercargo updated the inventory list.
Every merchant ship relied on a skilled supercargo to maximize profits on its journey.

Etymology

From Spanish *sobrecargo*, meaning 'overcharge' or 'person in charge of freight'.
