thermometric

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɜrməˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of temperature or to thermometers.
relating to thermometry or thermometers

Examples

The thermometric properties of mercury make it ideal for use in thermometers.
Changes in thermometric readings can indicate alterations in a machine's performance.
Researchers used a thermometric method to monitor the experiment's temperature fluctuations.

Synset Examples

helium gas was the thermometric fluid

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermal - relating to heat
  • Synonym: thermometry - the science of measuring temperature

Etymology

From Greek thermos (warm) + metron (measure) through French thermométrique.
