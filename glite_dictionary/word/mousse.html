mousse

1. NOUN

/ mus / (AmE), / muːs / (BrE)
A light, foamy substance dispensed from an aerosol can, used to style and give volume to hair.
foamy hair styling product

Examples

She applied some mousse to her damp hair before blow-drying it.
Use a volumizing mousse for a fuller hairstyle that lasts all day.
This mousse holds my curls in place without making them stiff.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gel - a thick, jelly-like hair styling product

Etymology

From French 'mousse' meaning 'froth' or 'foam'.

/ mus / (AmE), / muːs / (BrE)

light creamy dish set with gelatin

Examples

For special occasions, she often serves a savory seafood mousse in elegant molds.

3. VERB

/ mus / (AmE), / muːs / (BrE)
to apply a foamy styling product to (hair), especially to add volume or shape
apply styling mousse to hair

Examples

She always mousses her hair before blow-drying it for extra volume.
After washing his hair, he moussed it to help keep his curls defined.
If you want a sleek style, try moussing your hair while it's still damp.
