alhamdulilah

1. NOUN

/ ælˌhæmduˈlilɑː / (AmE), / ælˌhæmdʊˈlɪlɑː / (BrE)
An exclamation used by Muslims meaning 'praise be to God'; often said to express gratitude or relief.
praise be to God (Muslim exclamation)

Examples

After passing her exams, she whispered softly, alhamdulilah, to express her gratitude.
Whenever he finished a difficult task successfully, he would say alhamdulilah.
She smiled and said alhamdulilah when asked about her family's good health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hallelujah - an exclamation of praise to God, chiefly in the Jewish and Christian tradition

Etymology

From Arabic 'al-ḥamdu li-llāh' meaning 'praise be to God'.
