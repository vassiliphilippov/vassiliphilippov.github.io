Potos

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊtoʊs / (AmE), / ˈpəʊtɒs / (BrE)
A genus of mammals in the family Procyonidae, including the kinkajou.
genus of mammals in the raccoon family

Examples

The kinkajou is the only living species in the genus Potos.
Researchers studied the genetic diversity within Potos to better understand its evolutionary history.
Unlike raccoons, members of Potos have prehensile tails perfect for their arboreal lifestyle.

Etymology

From Latinized Greek Ποτός, used in taxonomic nomenclature.
