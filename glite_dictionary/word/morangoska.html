morangoska

1. NOUN

/ moʊˌrɑːŋˈɡoʊskə / (AmE), / məˌræŋˈɡɒskə / (BrE)
A Portuguese cocktail made with strawberries, vodka, sugar, and ice, similar to a caipirinha but substituting strawberries for lime.
strawberry-based Portuguese cocktail

Examples

I ordered a morangoska at the bar to try something new and refreshing.
The morangoska was garnished with fresh strawberries and a sprig of mint.
She learned to make a morangoska after visiting Portugal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caipirinha - A Brazilian cocktail made with lime, sugar, and cachaça

Etymology

From Portuguese 'morango' (strawberry) + Russian-influenced 'caipiroska' (caipirinha with vodka).
