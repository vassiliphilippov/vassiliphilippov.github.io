laundryman

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔːndriˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈlɔːndriˌman / (BrE)
a man whose job is to operate an industrial washing machine or to work in a laundry
man who works in a laundry

Examples

The laundryman sorted the linens before putting them in the machine.
After years as a laundryman, he became supervisor of the laundry department.
The hotel hired an experienced laundryman to handle their increased workload.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laundress - a woman employed to wash and iron clothes

Etymology

Formed from 'laundry' + 'man', similar in construction to other occupational nouns.
