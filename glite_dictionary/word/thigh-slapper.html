thigh-slapper

1. NOUN

/ ˈθaɪ ˌslæpər / (AmE), / ˈθaɪ ˌslæpə / (BrE)
a joke or story that is extremely funny and causes great amusement
extremely funny joke
informal

Examples

That comedian always tells at least one thigh-slapper during his routine.
The room erupted in laughter at his latest thigh-slapper.
It takes real talent to come up with a genuine thigh-slapper every time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knee-slapper - a very funny joke
  • Antonym: groaner - a joke that is so bad it causes people to groan

Etymology

From the gesture of slapping one's thigh as a physical reaction to something very funny.
