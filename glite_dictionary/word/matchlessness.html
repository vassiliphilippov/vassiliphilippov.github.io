matchlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈmætʃləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈmatʃləsnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being without equal; having no rival or peer.
state of having no equal

Examples

The sheer matchlessness of her voice captivated audiences across the world.
Historians often remark on the matchlessness of the ancient city's architecture.
Their matchlessness in the field set a new standard for all future competitors.

Synset Examples

The negative opinions that the tenor received in the roles that turned him into a legend might come as a surprise, in view of all the claims of his matchlessness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incomparability - the quality of being beyond comparison
  • Synonym: superiority - the state of being superior to others
  • Antonym: mediocrity - the quality of being average or ordinary

Etymology

From 'matchless' + '-ness', indicating the state or quality.
