Qiang

1. NOUN

/ tʃjɑːŋ / (AmE), / tʃjɑːŋ / (BrE)
the Tibeto-Burman language spoken by the Qiang people of Sichuan Province, China
language of the Qiang people in China

Examples

Linguists have studied the grammatical structure of Qiang for decades.
She is fluent in both Mandarin and Qiang.
There are several dialects within the Qiang language.

Etymology

From Mandarin 羌 (Qiāng), the name of the Qiang ethnic group.
