applicator

1. NOUN

/ ˈæplɪˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈæplɪˌkeɪtə / (BrE)
a device used for putting a substance onto a surface or into something
device for putting a substance on/in

Examples

She used the applicator to spread the ointment evenly over her skin.
The mascara comes with a special applicator that helps separate your lashes.
A disposable applicator is included in the package for easy use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: applicator brush - a brush used as an applicator, often for cosmetics or paint

Etymology

From Latin applicare (to attach, apply) + -or (agent noun suffix).
