boo-boo

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuːˌbuː / (AmE), / ˈbuːˌbuː / (BrE)
An embarrassing or silly mistake.
an embarrassing mistake
informal

Examples

I made a boo-boo on the presentation and gave the wrong figures.
Forgetting her name was a major boo-boo at the meeting.
He admitted his boo-boo and apologized to the whole team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blunder - a careless or stupid mistake
  • Synonym: gaffe - an unintentional act causing embarrassment
  • Synonym: howler - a very embarrassing mistake

2. NOUN

/ ˈbuː buː / (AmE), / ˈbuː buː / (BrE)
a minor mistake or a small injury, especially one considered unimportant or treated in a light-hearted way
small mistake or minor injury
informal

Examples

Lila ran to her mother after she got a boo-boo on her knee from falling off her bike.
Don't worry, everyone makes a boo-boo at work sometimes.
He laughed off the boo-boo he made while giving his speech.

Synset Examples

She laughed at her son's boo-boo after tripping.
He made a little boo-boo on his report.
