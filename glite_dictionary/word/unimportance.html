unimportance

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.ɪmˈpɔːr.təns / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪmˈpɔː.təns / (BrE)
the quality of not being important or worthy of attention
lack of importance

Examples

He was annoyed by the unimportance of his role in the project.
Sometimes we worry too much about things that are of utter unimportance.
She pointed out the unimportance of minor details during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: importance - the quality of being important or significant
  • Synonym: insignificance - the quality of being unimportant or trivial

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'importance'.

2. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.ɪmˈpɔr.təns / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪmˈpɔː.təns / (BrE)
the state or quality of having little or no significance or influence
lack of importance or significance

Examples

He realised the unimportance of material possessions after the accident.
Their complaints faded into unimportance in the face of the emergency.
She spoke with genuine humility about her own unimportance in the success of the project.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: importance - the state of being important or significant
  • Synonym: insignificance - the state of having no importance or worth

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'importance' (from Latin 'importare', to bring in).
