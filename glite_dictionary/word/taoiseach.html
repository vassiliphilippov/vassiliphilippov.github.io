taoiseach

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiːʃəx / (AmE), / ˈtiːʃək / (BrE)
the prime minister of Ireland
prime minister of Ireland

Examples

The taoiseach addressed the nation during the crisis.
After the election, she became the first female taoiseach in Irish history.
The taoiseach will meet with the US president next week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prime minister - the head of government in parliamentary systems; often the chief executive

Etymology

From Irish *taoiseach* meaning 'chief' or 'leader'.
