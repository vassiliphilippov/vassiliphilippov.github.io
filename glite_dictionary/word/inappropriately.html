inappropriately

1. ADV

/ ˌɪnəˈproʊpriətli / (AmE), / ˌɪnəˈprəʊpriətli / (BrE)
In a way that is not suitable or proper for the situation.
in an unsuitable or improper way

Examples

The employee behaved inappropriately during the meeting, making everyone uncomfortable.
Children who are dressed inappropriately for the weather can easily catch a cold.
He was criticized for responding inappropriately to the serious situation.

Synset Examples

he was inappropriately dressed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: appropriately - in a suitable or proper way
  • Synonym: improperly - in a way that is not correct or not following accepted standards

Etymology

Formed from 'inappropriate' + '-ly'; 'inappropriate' from Latin 'in-' (not) + 'appropriate'.
