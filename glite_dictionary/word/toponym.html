toponym

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑː.pə.nɪm / (AmE), / ˈtɒp.ə.nɪm / (BrE)
a name or term given to a geographical place or location
name of a geographical place

Examples

The toponym 'New York' was chosen during the colonial era.
She studied the origins of the city's toponym for her thesis on urban history.
Many Native American toponyms are still used in the United States today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: place name - common term for the name of a place

Etymology

From Greek topos (place) + onoma (name).
