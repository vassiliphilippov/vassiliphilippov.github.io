quelled

1. ADJ

/ kwɛld / (AmE), / kwɛld / (BrE)
subdued or suppressed, especially by force; having been brought under control
subdued or brought under control

Examples

The soldiers celebrated their victory over the quelled uprising in the city.
Her quelled anger was evident in her strained expression.
After negotiations, the region remained peaceful and quelled for years.

Synset Examples

the quelled rebellion
an uprising quenched almost before it started
a squelched rumor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: subdued - restrained or brought under control
  • Synonym: suppressed - forcibly put an end to; subdued

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'quell', from Old English 'cwellan' (to kill).
