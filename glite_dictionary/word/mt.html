MT

1. NOUN

/ ɛm ti / (AmE), / ɛm ti / (BrE)
abbreviation for 'machine translation': the use of computers to translate text or speech from one language to another.
abbreviation for machine translation
informal

Examples

Many companies now rely on MT to quickly localize their websites for global audiences.
While MT has improved, human translators are still needed for nuanced documents.
The software uses advanced MT algorithms to provide real-time subtitles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: machine translation - the use of computers to translate text or speech between languages

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'machine translation'.

3. NOUN

/ ɛm ti / (AmE), / ɛm ti / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'metric ton', a unit of weight equal to 1,000 kilograms
metric ton (1,000 kg)

Examples

The shipment weighed 20 MT and was delivered on schedule.
Last year, our factory produced over 5,000 MT of steel.
The country aims to reduce its CO₂ emissions by 10 MT annually.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tonne - unit of weight equal to 1,000 kilograms
  • Antonym: ton - unit of weight; in the US, commonly 2,000 pounds

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'metric ton'.

Source: WordNet
