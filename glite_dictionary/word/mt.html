Mt

1. NOUN

/ ɛm ti / (AmE), / ɛm ti / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for meitnerium, a radioactive transuranic element with atomic number 109
symbol for meitnerium (element 109)

Examples

The element Mt was first synthesized in 1982.
Researchers study the properties of Mt using particle accelerators.
Due to its instability, Mt has no practical applications outside of scientific research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meitnerium - a radioactive transuranic element with atomic number 109

Etymology

From the name 'meitnerium', which was named after Lise Meitner, an Austrian-Swedish physicist.
