churchgoer

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɝːtʃˌɡoʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːtʃˌɡəʊə / (BrE)
a person who regularly attends church services
person who regularly attends church

Examples

As a devoted churchgoer, she rarely missed a Sunday service.
The community of churchgoers organized a charity event for the homeless.
He became a regular churchgoer after moving to the new town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worshipper - a person who takes part in religious worship

Etymology

From 'church' + 'goer', pattern after words like 'moviegoer'.
