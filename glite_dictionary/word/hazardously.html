hazardously

1. ADV

/ ˈhæzərdəsli / (AmE), / ˈhazədəsli / (BrE)
In a manner that is dangerous or involves risk.
in a dangerous or risky manner

Examples

The chemicals were stored hazardously close to heat sources, which posed a serious threat.
He drove hazardously down the mountain road in heavy fog.
The scaffolding was hazardously assembled without proper safety measures.

Synset Examples

he came dangerously close to falling off the ledge

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dangerously - in a dangerous manner

Etymology

From 'hazardous' + '-ly'; 'hazardous' from Old French *hasarde* (game of chance).
