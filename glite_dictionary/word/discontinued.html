discontinued

1. ADJ

/ ˌdɪs.kənˈtɪn.juːd / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.kənˈtɪn.juːd / (BrE)
no longer produced, supplied, or continued; having been stopped permanently or for an indefinite period
no longer produced or continued

Examples

That model has been discontinued due to low demand.
The discontinued service left many customers looking for alternatives.
Collectors search for rare, discontinued products online.

Synset Examples

discontinued models
a discontinued magazine
a discontinued conversation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obsolete - no longer produced or used; out of date
  • Antonym: current - still in use or production

Etymology

From 'discontinue' + -ed; 'discontinue' from Latin 'dis-' (reversal) + 'continuare' (to continue).
