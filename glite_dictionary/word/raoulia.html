Raoulia

1. NOUN

/ raʊˈuːliə / (AmE), / raʊˈuːliə / (BrE)
A genus of low-growing, mat-forming plants native to New Zealand, some species of which are sometimes included in the genus Haastia.
genus of mat-forming New Zealand plants

Examples

The alpine garden features several species of Raoulia growing between the rocks.
Scientists collected samples of Raoulia to study its adaptation to harsh climates.
Raoulia is sometimes called 'vegetable sheep' because of its woolly appearance.

Etymology

Named after Étienne Raoul, a French botanist active in New Zealand.
