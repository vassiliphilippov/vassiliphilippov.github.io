creamy-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈkriːmi ˌkʌlərd / (AmE), / ˈkriːmi ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having the color of fresh cream; pale yellowish-white in color
pale yellowish-white color

Examples

A creamy-colored cat lounged in the afternoon sunlight.
