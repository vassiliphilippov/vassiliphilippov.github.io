photographically

1. ADV

/ ˌfoʊtəˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
by means of photography; using or relating to the process of taking or producing photographs
using photographic means

Examples

The museum's artifacts were photographically cataloged for record-keeping.
