gastralgia

1. NOUN

/ ɡæsˈtræl.dʒə / (AmE), / ɡæsˈtræl.dʒi.ə / (BrE)
Pain or ache localized in the stomach or abdominal region.
stomach or abdominal pain
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with gastralgia after she complained of persistent stomach pain.
Patients experiencing gastralgia should avoid spicy foods until symptoms subside.
Severe gastralgia can be a sign of an underlying medical condition that requires prompt attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stomachache - pain in the stomach area

Etymology

From Greek gastēr (stomach) + -algia (pain).
