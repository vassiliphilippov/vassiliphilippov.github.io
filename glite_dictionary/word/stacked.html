stacked

1. ADJ

/ stækt / (AmE), / stækt / (BrE)
arranged in a neat pile or stack, one on top of another
arranged in a pile or stack

Examples

The magazines were stacked neatly on the coffee table.
Boxes stacked against the wall made it hard to find space.
The plates are stacked in the cupboard after being washed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piled - arranged in a pile

/ stækt / (AmE), / stækt / (BrE)
(of a woman) having a large bosom and an attractively curvaceous figure, especially as considered sexually desirable.
curvaceous, with a large bosom (of a woman)
slang

Examples

The lead actress in the film was described as stacked by several magazines.
He was embarrassed when his friends pointed out the stacked woman at the bar.
Many fashion magazines in the 1950s frequently featured stacked models in glamorous photoshoots.
