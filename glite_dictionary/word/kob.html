kob

1. NOUN

/ kɑb / (AmE), / kɒb / (BrE)
a medium-sized antelope of southeast Africa, with an orange-brown coat and white underparts
African orange-brown antelope

Examples

A solitary kob grazed quietly on the savanna, blending into the golden grass.
During our safari, we spotted several kobs near the riverbank at dawn.
The distinctive horns of the male kob make it easy to identify amongst other antelopes.

Etymology

From Wolof *kobu* (antelope).
