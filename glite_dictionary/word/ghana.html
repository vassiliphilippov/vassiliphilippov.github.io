Ghana

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡɑnə / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːnə / (BrE)
A country in West Africa on the Gulf of Guinea, known for its diverse cultures and rich history.
country in West Africa

Examples

I have always wanted to visit Ghana to experience its vibrant culture and history.
After gaining independence from Britain in 1957, Ghana became the first sub-Saharan African country to do so.
Cocoa is one of Ghana's most important exports.

Synset Examples

Ghana was colonized as the Gold Coast by the British

Etymology

Named after the ancient Ghana Empire, which flourished in West Africa between the 6th and 13th centuries.
