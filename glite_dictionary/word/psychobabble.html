psychobabble

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪkoʊˌbæbəl / (AmE), / ˈsaɪkəʊˌbæbəl / (BrE)
language that uses psychological jargon or concepts, often superficially or without real meaning
superficial psychological jargon
informal

Examples

Her explanation was full of psychobabble that confused everyone.
I'm tired of all this psychobabble on talk shows these days.
The self-help book was criticized for its meaningless psychobabble.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jargon - special words or expressions used by a particular profession or group

Etymology

Blend of 'psychology' and 'babble' (meaning foolish or meaningless talk); recorded from the 1970s.
