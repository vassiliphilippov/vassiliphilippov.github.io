czaristic

1. ADJ

/ zɑˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / zɑːˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a czar or the system of government by a czar
relating to a czar or czarist system

Examples

The country was ruled with a czaristic approach for decades.
Historians often debate the impact of czaristic policies on Russian society.
Her czaristic style of leadership was criticized for being too authoritarian.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: czarist - relating to the system or supporters of a czar

Etymology

From 'czar' (title of Russian monarch) plus the adjective-forming suffix '-istic'.
