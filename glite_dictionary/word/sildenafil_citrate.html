sildenafil citrate

1. NOUN

/ sɪlˈdɛnəfɪl ˈsaɪtreɪt / (AmE), / sɪlˈdɛlɪfɪl ˈsʌɪtreɪt / (BrE)
a medication, marketed especially under the trade name Viagra, used to treat erectile dysfunction in men
medication for erectile dysfunction
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed sildenafil citrate to help treat his erectile dysfunction.
Studies have shown that sildenafil citrate is effective for many men with circulation problems.
He asked the pharmacist about potential side effects of sildenafil citrate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Viagra - trade name for sildenafil citrate, a drug for erectile dysfunction

Etymology

From chemical name: sildenafil (name assigned by pharmaceutical company), combined with citrate (the salt form).
