footsure

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʊtˌʃʊr / (AmE), / ˈfʊtʃʊə / (BrE)
Certain in movement; unlikely to stumble or make a misstep, either physically or figuratively.
confident and unlikely to stumble

Examples

The climber was so footsure on the rocky path that she never hesitated.
Only a footsure goat could manage those narrow ledges in the mountains.
He walked across the wet floor with a footsure confidence that impressed everyone.

Synset Examples

on surefooted donkeys

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surefooted - unlikely to stumble or slip; stable on one's feet

Etymology

Formed from 'foot' + 'sure', indicating confidence in footing.
