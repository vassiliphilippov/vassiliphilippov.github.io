humanness

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmənnəs / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmənnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being human, especially as distinguished by human nature, emotions, and vulnerabilities
the quality of being human

Examples

The novel explores the humanness of its characters through their struggles and relationships.
In his speech, he emphasized the importance of preserving our humanness in the face of technological progress.
Her compassion and empathy are clear signs of her deep humanness.

Synset Examples

he feared the speedy decline of all manhood

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humanity - the condition of being human; human nature

Etymology

From 'human' + '-ness', denoting state or quality.
