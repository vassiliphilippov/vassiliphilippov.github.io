friendless

1. ADJ

/ ˈfrɛndləs / (AmE), / ˈfrɛndləs / (BrE)
having no friends or feeling excluded from society; without companionship or social support.
having no friends; without companionship

Examples

After moving to a new city, she often felt friendless and isolated.
The friendless boy sat alone at the lunch table every day.
He wrote poems about being friendless in a bustling world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lonely - unhappy because of being alone or without company

Etymology

Formed from 'friend' + '-less' (meaning 'without').
