Piranga rubra

1. NOUN

/ piˈræŋɡə ˈruːbrə / (AmE), / pɪˈræŋɡə ˈruːbrə / (BrE)
The summer tanager, a North American songbird species in which the male is bright red and the female mostly yellow.
species of North American songbird

Examples

The bright red male Piranga rubra is easy to spot among the green foliage.
Many birdwatchers hope to catch a glimpse of the elusive Piranga rubra during spring migration.
Unlike the male, the female Piranga rubra is mostly yellow in color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summer tanager - common name for Piranga rubra, a North American songbird

Etymology

From Latin: Piranga, genus name; rubra, meaning 'red'.
