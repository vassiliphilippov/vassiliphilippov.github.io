sixpack

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪksˌpæk / (AmE), / ˈsɪksˌpak / (BrE)
A carton or package containing six bottles or cans of a beverage, typically beer or soft drinks.
carton of six bottles or cans

Examples

I picked up a sixpack of soda for the party.
He carried a sixpack of beer to the picnic table.
She bought a sixpack at the corner store on her way home.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: twelve-pack - carton containing twelve bottles or cans
  • Degree Higher: case - large box containing multiple bottles or cans

Etymology

From 'six' (the number) + 'pack' (a set of items packaged together).
