bistred

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɪstɹəd / (AmE), / ˈbɪstɹɛd / (BrE)
Having a brownish or grayish-brown color, as if stained with bister.
colored brownish like bister

Examples

The artist used a bistred wash to give the landscape a moody atmosphere.
Her old notebook was filled with bistred sketches and faded ink.
A bistred light filtered through the dusty window, giving the room an antique look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bister - a grayish to yellowish brown pigment

Etymology

From bister +‎ -ed, meaning 'colored with bister'.
