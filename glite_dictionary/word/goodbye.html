goodbye

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊdˈbaɪ / (AmE), / ɡʊdˈbaɪ / (BrE)
A word or remark used to express good wishes when parting or at the end of a conversation.
parting remark; farewell

Examples

She waved and said goodbye as the train pulled away.
It was difficult for him to say goodbye to his childhood home.
After a long conversation, they finally exchanged goodbyes and hung up the phone.

Synset Examples

they said their good-byes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farewell - an expression of good wishes on leaving
  • Antonym: hello - a greeting or expression of welcome

Etymology

From 'God be with ye,' an old expression of parting; later contracted to 'goodbye.'
