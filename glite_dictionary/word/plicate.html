to plicate

1. VERB

/ ˈplaɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈplaɪˌkeɪt / (BrE)
to fold or arrange into pleats
to fold into pleats

Examples

The designer decided to plicate the fabric for a more structured look.
You need to plicate the edges carefully to achieve a neat finish.
Certain origami models require you to plicate the paper in precise ways.

Synset Examples

Pleat the cloth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pleat - to fold fabric into pleats

Etymology

From Latin 'plicatus' (folded), past participle of 'plicare' (to fold).
