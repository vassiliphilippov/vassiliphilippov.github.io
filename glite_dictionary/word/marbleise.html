to marbleise

1. VERB

/ ˈmɑːrbəlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɑːbɪlaɪz / (BrE)
to make (an object or surface) look like marble, especially by applying a finish or paint that imitates the appearance of marble
make something look like marble
british

Examples

They decided to marbleise the columns to match the grand entryway.
The artist used special paints to marbleise the old fireplace surround.
You can marbleise wooden furniture to give it a luxurious finish.

Synset Examples

marbleize the fireplace

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to marbleize - make something look like marble (American spelling)

Etymology

From 'marble' + '-ise' (British spelling of the verb-forming suffix).
