campstool

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæmpˌstuːl / (AmE), / ˈkampˌstuːl / (BrE)
a portable folding stool, typically used outdoors or while camping
folding portable stool for camping

Examples

We packed a campstool for each person before heading to the lake.
He unfolded his campstool and sat down by the fire.
A lightweight campstool is useful for long hikes and picnics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stool - a seat without a back or arms, typically for one person
