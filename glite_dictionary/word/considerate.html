considerate

1. ADJ

/ kənˈsɪd.ɚ.ət / (AmE), / kənˈsɪd.ər.ət / (BrE)
thoughtful of the needs, rights, or feelings of others; showing careful regard for others
thoughtful of others' feelings or needs

Examples

He is always considerate of other people's needs.
It was very considerate of her to call and check on me.
Please be considerate and turn off your phone during the performance.

Synset Examples

friends considerate enough to leave us alone

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtful - showing consideration for the needs of others
  • Antonym: inconsiderate - not showing concern for the feelings or rights of others

Etymology

From Latin 'consideratus', past participle of 'considerare' (to consider).
