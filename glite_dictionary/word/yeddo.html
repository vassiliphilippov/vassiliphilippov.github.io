Yeddo

1. PROPN

/ ˈjɛdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈjɛdəʊ / (BrE)
an archaic English name for Tokyo, the capital and largest city of Japan
archaic name for Tokyo, Japan
archaic

Examples

The treaty was signed in Yeddo in the late nineteenth century.
Merchants from all over the world visited Yeddo during its time as Japan's principal city.
In old travelogues, Yeddo is often described as bustling and vibrant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tokyo - the modern name for the capital of Japan

Etymology

From Japanese Edo, the former name for Tokyo.
