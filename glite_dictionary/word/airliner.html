airliner

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌlaɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈeəˌlaɪnə / (BrE)
A large commercial airplane designed to carry passengers.
commercial passenger airplane

Examples

The airliner took off smoothly despite the rainy weather.
An international airliner can carry hundreds of passengers across continents.
We saw a new airliner displayed at the aviation expo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airplane - a powered flying vehicle with fixed wings

Etymology

Formed from 'air' and 'liner', modeled after 'ocean liner'.
