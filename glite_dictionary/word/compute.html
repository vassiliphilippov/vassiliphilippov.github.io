to compute

1. VERB

/ kəmˈpjuːt / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːt / (BrE)
to calculate something, especially using a machine or by following a method
to perform a calculation

Examples

The program can compute the total cost of your purchases in seconds.
Scientists computed the probability of the event occurring using advanced software.
Can you compute the answer without a calculator?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to calculate - to determine mathematically
  • Antonym: estimate - to roughly calculate or judge the value of something

Etymology

From Latin 'computare' (to count, calculate), from 'com-' (together) + 'putare' (to reckon).
