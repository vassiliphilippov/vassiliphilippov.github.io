asterismal

1. ADJ

/ əˈstɛr.ɪz.məl / (AmE), / əˈstɛr.ɪz.məl / (BrE)
relating to asterisms or constellations, especially in astronomy.
relating to asterisms or constellations

Examples

The sky was filled with asterismal patterns easily recognizable to experienced stargazers.
Researchers discussed the asterismal significance of certain ancient carvings.
She delivered a lecture on the asterismal groupings visible in the southern hemisphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astronomical - relating to astronomy

Etymology

From 'asterism' (from Greek 'asterismos', marking with stars) + '-al' (adjectival suffix).
