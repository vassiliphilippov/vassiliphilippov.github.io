zymoid

1. ADJ

/ ˈzaɪ.mɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈzaɪ.mɔɪd / (BrE)
resembling or having characteristics of an enzyme.
resembling an enzyme

Examples

The zymoid properties of the compound were tested in the laboratory.
Researchers described the protein as zymoid due to its enzyme-like activity.
Her thesis explores several zymoid substances found in plants.

Etymology

From Greek 'zymē' (leaven, yeast) + '-oid' (resembling, like).
