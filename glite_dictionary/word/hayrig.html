hayrig

1. NOUN

/ ˈheɪ.rɪg / (AmE), / ˈheɪ.rɪg / (BrE)
A frame attached to a wagon to increase the amount of hay it can carry.
wagon frame for carrying more hay

Examples

The farmer used a hayrig to transport a larger load of hay from the field.
They rebuilt the old wagon with a sturdy new hayrig for the harvest season.
By attaching a hayrig, the cart could handle double the usual capacity.
