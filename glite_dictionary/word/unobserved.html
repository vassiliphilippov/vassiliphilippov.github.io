unobserved

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnəbˈzɜrvd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəbˈzɜːvd / (BrE)
not seen, noticed, or watched by anyone; happening without being detected
not noticed or seen

Examples

The thief managed to escape unobserved through a side door.
Many important details went unobserved during the hurried inspection.
The animal lived its life mostly unobserved by humans in the dense forest.

Synset Examples

managed to slip away unobserved
unseen forces at work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unnoticed - not seen or paid attention to
  • Synonym: unseen - not seen or perceived
  • Antonym: noticed - having been seen or observed
  • Antonym: observed - having been watched or noticed

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'observed', past participle of 'observe'.
