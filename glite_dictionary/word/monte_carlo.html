Monte Carlo

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɑnti ˈkɑrloʊ / (AmE), / ˌmɒnti ˈkɑːləʊ / (BrE)
A town and popular resort in Monaco, renowned for its casino and luxurious lifestyle.
famous resort town in Monaco

Examples

The glamorous atmosphere of Monte Carlo attracts celebrities from around the world.
She visited Monte Carlo to experience its famous casino and luxurious hotels.
Racing fans gather in Monte Carlo every year for the prestigious Grand Prix.

Etymology

Named after Prince Charles III of Monaco (Monte Carlo meaning 'Mount Charles' in Italian).
