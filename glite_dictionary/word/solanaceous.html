solanaceous

1. ADJ

/ ˌsoʊ.ləˈneɪ.ʃəs / (AmE), / ˌsəʊ.ləˈneɪ.ʃəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of plants belonging to the family Solanaceae, which includes potatoes, tomatoes, and nightshades
relating to the plant family Solanaceae

Examples

Several important crops, including tomatoes and eggplants, are solanaceous plants.
The potato blight mainly affects solanaceous species in the garden.
She specialized in the study of solanaceous weeds and their impact on agriculture.

Etymology

From Latin 'Solanaceae', the scientific name for the nightshade family.
