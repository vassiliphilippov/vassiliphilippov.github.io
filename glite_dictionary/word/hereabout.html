hereabout

1. ADV

/ ˌhɪrəˈbaʊt / (AmE), / ˌhɪərəˈbaʊt / (BrE)
in or near this place; in this vicinity or area.
in this vicinity; nearby

Examples

There isn't much traffic hereabout at this time of day.
We found few restaurants hereabout that serve vegetarian food.
The climate hereabout is generally mild throughout the year.

Synset Examples

the people are friendly hereabouts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hereabouts - in this vicinity; nearby (more common plural form)
  • Antonym: thereabout - in or near that place; in that vicinity

Etymology

From 'here' + 'about', modeled after analogous constructions like 'thereabout'.
