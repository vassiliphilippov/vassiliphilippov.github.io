paragrapher

1. NOUN

/ ˈpærəˌɡræfər / (AmE), / ˈpærəˌɡrɑːfə / (BrE)
a writer of short articles or paragraphs, especially for publication in newspapers or periodicals
a writer of newspaper paragraphs

Examples

The paragrapher contributed witty observations to the editorial page each week.
As a young paragrapher, she quickly learned how to capture readers’ attention in just a few lines.
His reputation as a skilled paragrapher grew after several of his pieces were widely circulated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: journalist - a person who writes for newspapers or magazines

Etymology

From 'paragraph' + '-er', denoting an agent noun.
