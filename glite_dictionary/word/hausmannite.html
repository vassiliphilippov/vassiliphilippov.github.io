hausmannite

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌmænaɪt / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌman.aɪt / (BrE)
A mineral consisting of manganese tetroxide (Mn3O4), important as a source of manganese.
a manganese mineral; Mn3O4

Examples

The geologist identified the black crystal as hausmannite during the mineral survey.
Significant deposits of hausmannite are an important industrial source of manganese.
Under the microscope, hausmannite displays distinctive brownish-black coloration and tetragonal crystals.

Etymology

Named after German mineralogist Johann Friedrich Ludwig Hausmann (1782–1859).
