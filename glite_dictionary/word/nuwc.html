NUWC

1. NOUN

/ ˈnjuː wɪk / (AmE), / ˈnjuː wɪk / (BrE)
abbreviation for Naval Undersea Warfare Center, the U.S. agency providing scientific, engineering, and technical support for submarine and undersea warfare systems.
