green-minded

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡrin ˌmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡriːn ˌmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
concerned with or supportive of environmental protection and sustainability; environmentally conscious.
environmentally conscious; supportive of sustainability

Examples

Our company prefers to work with green-minded suppliers who prioritize sustainability.
She is very green-minded and always recycles her waste.
The festival was organized by a group of green-minded students eager to promote environmental awareness.

Synset Examples

Her green-minded approach made her a leader in environmental initiatives.
Being green-minded, he chose to bike instead of drive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eco-friendly - not harmful to the environment
  • Synonym: green - concerned with or supporting protection of the environment
  • Antonym: polluting - causing environmental pollution
