clearcutness

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɪrˌkʌtnəs / (AmE), / ˈklɪəˌkʌtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being clear, definite, and unmistakable
quality of being clear or definite

Examples

The clearcutness of her argument left no room for doubt.
We admired the clearcutness of the design, which made the instructions easy to follow.
Legal documents require a degree of clearcutness to avoid misinterpretation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clarity - the quality of being clear
