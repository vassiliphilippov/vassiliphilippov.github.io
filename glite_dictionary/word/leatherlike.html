leatherlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛðərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈlɛðəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling leather in texture or appearance; tough but flexible like leather
resembling leather in texture or look

Examples

The sofa was covered in a leatherlike material that felt almost as soft as real leather.
She wore a jacket made of leatherlike fabric that was both stylish and durable.
The plant has leatherlike leaves that help it conserve water in its dry environment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leathery - having a tough, leather-like texture
