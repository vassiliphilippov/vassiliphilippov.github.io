copper-bottomed

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑː.pɚˈbɑː.t̬əmd / (AmE), / ˌkɒp.əˈbɒt.ənd / (BrE)
(of a plan, investment, or claim) certain or guaranteed; completely reliable or trustworthy.
completely reliable; guaranteed

Examples

The company only invests in copper-bottomed projects to minimize risk.
His promise was as copper-bottomed as any she had ever heard.
Investors are always looking for copper-bottomed opportunities that guarantee good returns.

Synset Examples

The investment was copper-bottomed, guaranteeing returns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dependable - able to be relied on; trustworthy
  • Antonym: unreliable - not able to be trusted or depended on

Etymology

Originally referred to ships having a copper lining on the bottom for protection and reliability.
