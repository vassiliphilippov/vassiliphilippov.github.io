optimist

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːptəmɪst / (AmE), / ˈɒptɪmɪst / (BrE)
a person who tends to expect the best possible outcome or looks on the bright side of things
a person who expects the best

Examples

Even in difficult times, she remained an optimist and encouraged others to keep hoping.
Being an optimist helps him see new opportunities in every challenge.
The group needs at least one optimist to balance out everyone's doubts.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pessimist - a person who tends to expect the worst
  • Synonym: optimistic - believing that good things will happen

Etymology

From French *optimiste*, from Latin *optimus* (best).
