extrovertive

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛkstrəˈvɜrtɪv / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˈvɜːtɪv / (BrE)
concerned with or oriented toward the social and physical environment rather than inward thought; outgoing or sociable in nature
outwardly focused; sociable

Examples

Being extrovertive helped her quickly make friends at her new school.
People with extrovertive personalities enjoy social gatherings and teamwork.
The company sought employees who were extrovertive and enjoyed meeting new clients.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: introvertive - concerned with internal thoughts and feelings rather than the external world
  • Synonym: extroverted - outgoing; focused on the external world

Etymology

From extrovert + -ive, modeled on introvertive.
