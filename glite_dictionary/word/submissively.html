submissively

1. ADV

/ səbˈmɪsɪvli / (AmE), / səbˈmɪsɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows a willingness to obey or yield to others; humbly or obediently
in an obedient or yielding manner

Examples

She bowed submissively before the king, waiting for his command.
He submissively accepted the criticism, without attempting to defend himself.
The dog submissively lowered its head as its owner approached.

Synset Examples

he always acts so deferentially around his supervisor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obediently - in an obedient manner
  • Antonym: defiantly - in a manner showing resistance or disobedience

Etymology

From 'submissive' + '-ly'; 'submissive' from Latin 'submittere' (to submit).
