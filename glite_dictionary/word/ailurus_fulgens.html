Ailurus fulgens

1. NOUN

/ aɪˈlʊərəs ˈfʊlˌdʒɛnz / (AmE), / aɪˈlʊərəs ˈfʊl.dʒɛnz / (BrE)
the scientific name for the red panda, a small reddish-brown mammal native to the eastern Himalayas and southwestern China
scientific name for the red panda

Examples

The Ailurus fulgens is often confused with the giant panda, though they belong to different families.
Researchers observed the feeding habits of Ailurus fulgens in its native Himalayan habitat.
Many zoos participate in conservation programs to protect Ailurus fulgens from extinction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red panda - a small, reddish-brown carnivorous mammal native to the Himalayas and China

Etymology

From Greek 'ailouros' (cat) and Latin 'fulgens' (shining), referring to its appearance.
