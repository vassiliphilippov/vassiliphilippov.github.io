Moldova

1. PROPN

/ moʊlˈdoʊvə / (AmE), / mɒlˈdəʊvə / (BrE)
a landlocked country in eastern Europe, formerly part of the Soviet Union, which became independent in 1991; official name: Republic of Moldova.
country in eastern Europe

Examples

She traveled through Moldova on her way to Ukraine.
The population of Moldova has experienced significant changes since independence.
Wine production is a key industry in Moldova.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Romania - country bordering Moldova to the west
  • Synonym: Chisinau - the capital city of Moldova

Etymology

From Romanian 'Moldova', the name of the historical region.
