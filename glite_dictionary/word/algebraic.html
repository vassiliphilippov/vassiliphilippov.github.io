algebraic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæl.dʒəˈbreɪ.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌæl.dʒəˈbreɪ.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to, involving, or using algebra.
relating to algebra

Examples

She found the algebraic solution to the problem quite challenging.
The algebraic expression simplifies to a single variable.
He specializes in algebraic geometry for his research.

Synset Examples

algebraic geometry

Related Terms

  • Antonym: arithmetic - relating to arithmetic (as distinct from algebra)

Etymology

From Medieval Latin algebraicus, from algebra + -ic.
