Galwegian

1. ADJ

/ ɡælˈwiː.dʒi.ən / (AmE), / ɡælˈwiː.dʒi.ən / (BrE)
of or relating to the Scottish district of Galloway or its people
of or from Galloway, Scotland

Examples

The Galwegian landscape is known for its rugged beauty and historic sites.
Many traditions unique to Galwegian culture have been preserved for centuries.
She spoke proudly of her Galwegian ancestry during the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Galloway - a region in southwestern Scotland

Etymology

From Galloway, a region in southwest Scotland, with English adjectival suffix -an.
