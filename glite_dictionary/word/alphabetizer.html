alphabetizer

1. NOUN

/ ˈæl.fə.bəˌtaɪ.zɚ / (AmE), / ˈæl.fə.bɪˌtaɪ.zə / (BrE)
a person who arranges items in alphabetical order
person who arranges items alphabetically

Examples

The librarian acted as the alphabetizer, ensuring all the books were in order.
She worked as an alphabetizer for a large database of client names.
Every contest entry was checked by an alphabetizer before the results were announced.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sorter - person or device that sorts things

Etymology

From 'alphabet' + '-izer', indicating one who arranges by the alphabet.
