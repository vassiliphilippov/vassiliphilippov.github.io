restauranter

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛstəˈræntər / (AmE), / ˌrɛstəˈræntə / (BrE)
a person who owns or manages a restaurant; a restaurant proprietor
restaurant owner or manager

Examples

The successful restauranter opened three more bistros in the city.
She dreamed of becoming a restauranter and opening her own café.
As a seasoned restauranter, Marcus paid close attention to every detail in his dining room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restaurateur - a person who owns or manages a restaurant

Etymology

Modeled on 'restaurant' with agent suffix '-er', by analogy with 'restaurateur'.
