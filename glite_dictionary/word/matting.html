matting

1. NOUN

/ ˈmætɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmætɪŋ / (BrE)
material or a layer used as a border or background for a picture, especially in framing to provide contrast or protection.
border or background for a picture

Examples

The gallery used a white matting to highlight the photograph’s colors.
He chose a black wood frame and cream matting for his diploma.
Custom matting can make artwork look more professional and preserve it longer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mount - a backing or setting for a picture or photograph
Source: WordNet

2. NOUN

/ ˈmæ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmæt.ɪŋ / (BrE)
a coarse fabric, often made from straw or hemp, used as a covering for floors or other surfaces
coarse fabric used as covering

Examples

The floor of the hut was covered with matting made from woven reeds.
We rolled out the straw matting to keep the room cool during summer.
The museum displayed antique hemp matting as part of its exhibit on traditional crafts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mat - a piece of material placed on a surface to protect it or for decoration
