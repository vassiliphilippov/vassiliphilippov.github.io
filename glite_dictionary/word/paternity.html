paternity

1. NOUN

/ pəˈtɝː.nə.ti / (AmE), / pəˈtɜː.nə.ti / (BrE)
the state of being a father or the relationship of a father to his child
the state or relationship of fatherhood

Examples

The results of the DNA test established paternity beyond any doubt.
Questions about paternity can lead to complex legal and emotional issues.
He applied for paternity leave after the birth of his son.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: maternity - the state of being a mother

Etymology

From Latin 'paternitas' (fatherhood), from 'pater' (father).
