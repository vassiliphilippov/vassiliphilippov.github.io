Strand

1. PROPN

/ strænd / (AmE), / strænd / (BrE)
a main street in central London, known for its theaters, hotels, and historic significance
notable street in central London

Examples

She took a stroll down the Strand to see the famous theaters.
Many luxury hotels can be found along the Strand in London.
The bustling atmosphere of the Strand attracts tourists year-round.

Etymology

From Old English 'strand' (shore), originally referring to the street's riverside location.
