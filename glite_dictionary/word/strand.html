strand

1. NOUN

/ strænd / (AmE), / strænd / (BrE)
a necklace made by stringing together beads, pearls, or similar objects in a single line
a single-string necklace (e.g. of pearls or beads)

Examples

She wore a beautiful strand of pearls around her neck.
My grandmother left me her favorite strand of amber beads.
The jeweler displayed a delicate strand with tiny silver charms.

Synset Examples

a strand of pearls

Related Terms

  • Synonym: string - a necklace made by stringing beads or similar objects together

2. VERB

/ strænd / (AmE), / strænd / (BrE)
to leave someone in a place from which they cannot escape, especially without help or resources
to leave someone helpless or isolated

Examples

The heavy snowstorm stranded hundreds of drivers on the highway overnight.
If we miss the last ferry, we'll be stranded on the island until tomorrow.
Her car broke down, stranding her in the middle of nowhere.
