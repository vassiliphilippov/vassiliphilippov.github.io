colpocele

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑlpəˌsil / (AmE), / ˈkɒlpəsiːl / (BrE)
A hernia in which a portion of tissue or organ protrudes into the vagina.
hernia projecting into the vagina
medical

Examples

The surgeon diagnosed the patient with a colpocele after examining her symptoms.
Treatment for a colpocele may involve surgical repair depending on its severity.
A colpocele can cause discomfort and pressure in the pelvic region.

Etymology

From Greek kolpos (vagina) + -cele (hernia, tumor).
