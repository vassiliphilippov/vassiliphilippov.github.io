backyard

1. NOUN

/ ˈbækˌjɑrd / (AmE), / ˌbækˈjɑːd / (BrE)
an area of land behind a house, typically used for recreation or gardening
land behind a house

Examples

They spent the afternoon playing soccer in the backyard.
My father built a small shed in our backyard for his tools.
The children love to camp out in the backyard during summer nights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garden - a piece of ground for growing flowers, fruits, or vegetables, especially behind a house (British English sense)
