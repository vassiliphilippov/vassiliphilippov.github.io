Marie Henri Beyle

1. PROPN

/ maˈri ɑnˈri beɪl / (AmE), / maˈri ɑ̃ˈʁi bɛl / (BrE)
(1783–1842) French novelist notable for pioneering psychological analysis in fiction; best known under the pen name Stendhal.
French novelist; pioneer of psychological fiction

Examples

Marie Henri Beyle, under the pen name Stendhal, wrote 'The Red and the Black.'
Literary critics regard Marie Henri Beyle as a pioneer of psychological analysis in the novel.
The life of Marie Henri Beyle reflects the tumultuous era of early 19th-century France.
