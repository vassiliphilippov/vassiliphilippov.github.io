Mohammed Reza Pahlevi

1. PROPN

/ moʊˈhɑːməd ˈrɛzə pɑːˈlɛvi / (AmE), / məˈhæmɪd ˈreɪzə pæˈleɪvi / (BrE)
(1919–1980) The Shah of Iran from 1941 until he was deposed in 1979 by Islamic revolutionaries.
Shah of Iran (1919–1980)

Examples

Mohammed Reza Pahlevi ruled Iran for nearly four decades before the 1979 revolution.
Many Iranians emigrated after Mohammed Reza Pahlevi was overthrown.
History books detail the reign and exile of Mohammed Reza Pahlevi.
