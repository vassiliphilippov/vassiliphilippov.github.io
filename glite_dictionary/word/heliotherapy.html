heliotherapy

1. NOUN

/ ˌhiːlioʊˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˌhiːliəʊˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
The therapeutic use of sunlight in the treatment of certain medical conditions.
treatment using sunlight
medical

Examples

Many dermatologists once recommended heliotherapy for patients with certain skin conditions.
He traveled to a sanitarium that specialized in heliotherapy to ease his symptoms.
Modern medicine sometimes still explores the benefits of heliotherapy for specific illnesses.

Etymology

From Greek hēlios (sun) + therapeia (healing, treatment).
