tasselled

1. ADJ

/ ˈtæsəld / (AmE), / ˈtæsəld / (BrE)
decorated or trimmed with tassels, often as a form of ornamentation
decorated with tassels

Examples

She wore a stunning tasselled shawl to the party.
The tasselled curtains added an elegant touch to the room.
His graduation cap was adorned with a gold tasselled cord.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fringed - having a decorative border of hanging threads or strips

Etymology

From 'tassel' (from Old French 'tassel' meaning a clasp or ornament), with the suffix '-ed' forming the adjective.
