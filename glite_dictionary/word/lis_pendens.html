lis pendens

1. NOUN

/ lɪs ˈpɛndɛnz / (AmE), / lɪs ˈpɛndɛnz / (BrE)
A legal notice that a lawsuit has been filed concerning real estate, which may affect the property's title or ownership.
legal notice of pending lawsuit on property
legal

Examples

The buyer discovered there was a lis pendens on the property and decided not to proceed with the purchase.
Before completing the transfer, the lawyer checked the registry for any lis pendens affecting the title.
A lis pendens serves as public notice that legal action may impact the real estate involved.

Etymology

From Latin, meaning 'pending lawsuit.'
