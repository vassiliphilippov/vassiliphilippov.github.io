hardwood

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrdˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌwʊd / (BrE)
The wood from broad-leaved trees, especially angiosperms such as oak or maple, as distinguished from the wood of conifers.
wood from broad-leaved trees

Examples

The furniture was made from solid hardwood, which made it durable and long-lasting.
Installing hardwood floors can increase the value of your home.
Maple is a popular hardwood used in the construction of musical instruments.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: softwood - wood from coniferous trees, typically softer than hardwood
