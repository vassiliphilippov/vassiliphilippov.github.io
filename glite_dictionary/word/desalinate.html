to desalinate

1. VERB

/ diˈsælɪˌneɪt / (AmE), / diˈsælɪneɪt / (BrE)
to remove salt or other minerals from something, especially water, to make it suitable for drinking or irrigation
remove salt from (water)

Examples

Many modern plants are built to desalinate seawater for human consumption.
New technologies have made it more affordable to desalinate brackish water in arid regions.
Engineers are seeking better ways to desalinate ocean water with less energy use.

Synset Examples

desalinate water

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to desalt - remove salt from (something)

Etymology

From Latin prefix 'de-' (removal) + 'sal' (salt).
