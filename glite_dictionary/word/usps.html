USPS

1. NOUN

/ ˌjuː ɛs piː ˈɛs / (AmE), / ˌjuː ɛs piː ˈɛs / (BrE)
the United States Postal Service; the independent federal agency responsible for mail processing and delivery in the United States
U.S. mail delivery agency
american

Examples

Your package will be shipped via USPS and should arrive in three days.
I went to the USPS office to buy stamps and send a letter.
The USPS has updated its delivery times due to the holiday season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: postal service - national or regional mail delivery system

Etymology

Initialism of United States Postal Service.
