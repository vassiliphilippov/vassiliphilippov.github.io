nonadhesive

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.ædˈhiː.sɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.ədˈhiː.sɪv / (BrE)
Not tending or able to stick or adhere to a surface.
not able to stick or adhere

Examples

Medical dressings labeled as nonadhesive are designed to be gentle on sensitive skin.
He chose a nonadhesive tape for temporary labeling so it would be easy to remove.
The label was nonadhesive and kept falling off the plastic container.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: adhesive - able to stick or adhere

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) and 'adhesive' (able to stick).
