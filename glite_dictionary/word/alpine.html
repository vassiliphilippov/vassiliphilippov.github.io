Alpine

1. ADJ

/ ˈælpaɪn / (AmE), / ˈælpaɪn / (BrE)
relating to the Alps or their inhabitants.
of or relating to the Alps

Examples

He spent a week exploring the Alpine villages of Switzerland.
The Alpine climate can be very harsh during the winter months.
Many tourists visit the Alpine regions for skiing and hiking adventures.

Synset Examples

Alpine countries, Switzerland, Italy, France, and Germany

Etymology

From Latin Alpes (the Alps), the European mountain range.
