Strindberg

1. PROPN

/ ˈstrɪnd.bɛrɡ / (AmE), / ˈstrɪnd.bɜːɡ / (BrE)
(1849–1912), Swedish dramatist and novelist, known for works such as 'Miss Julie' and 'The Father.'
