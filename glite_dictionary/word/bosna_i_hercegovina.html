Bosna i Hercegovina

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɔs.nɑ i hɛr.t͡se.ɡɔˈvi.na / (AmE), / ˈbɒs.nə i ˌhɛː.t͡sɛ.ɡɒˈviː.nə / (BrE)
The official Bosnian, Croatian, and Serbian name for Bosnia and Herzegovina, a country in southeastern Europe.
official name for Bosnia and Herzegovina

Examples

Many international organizations recognize Bosna i Hercegovina as a sovereign state.
Tourists are increasingly drawn to the natural beauty of Bosna i Hercegovina.
He studied the recent history of Bosna i Hercegovina in his political science class.

Related Terms

Etymology

Bosnian, Croatian, and Serbian for 'Bosnia and Herzegovina.'
