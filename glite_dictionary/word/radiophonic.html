radiophonic

1. ADJ

/ ˌreɪdi.oʊˈfɑːnɪk / (AmE), / ˌreɪdi.əʊˈfɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or produced by radio, especially radio broadcasting or the transmission of sound by radio waves.
relating to radio transmission or broadcasting

Examples

The radiophonic broadcasts brought cutting-edge sounds to a wide audience.
The composer experimented with radiophonic techniques to create an immersive atmosphere.
She became fascinated by radiophonic art after listening to experimental radio shows.

Etymology

From radio + -phonic, the latter from Greek phōnē (sound, voice).
