take-home

1. ADJ

/ ˈteɪk hoʊm / (AmE), / ˈteɪk həʊm / (BrE)
(of salary or wages) remaining after all deductions, such as taxes, have been made; net.
net salary or wages after deductions

Examples

My take-home pay is lower than expected because of the extra taxes.
They offered a decent salary, but the take-home amount was disappointing.
Your take-home earnings will depend largely on your tax bracket.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: gross - (of salary or wages) before any deductions or taxes are taken out
