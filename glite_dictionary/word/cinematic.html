cinematic

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɪnəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɪnəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
relating to the cinema or having qualities characteristic of motion pictures
relating to the cinema

Examples

The director's use of lighting and camera angles creates a highly cinematic experience.
Many video games today strive for a cinematic presentation in their storytelling.
The lush landscapes and sweeping shots made the documentary feel truly cinematic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filmic - relating to motion pictures or their style

Etymology

From French cinématique, derived from cinéma (cinema).
