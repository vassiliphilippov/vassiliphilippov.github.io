BNS

1. NOUN

/ biː ɛn ɛs / (AmE), / biː en es / (BrE)
an academic degree awarded for completing undergraduate studies in naval science
bachelor's degree in naval science

Examples

After four years of rigorous study, she earned her BNS and joined the Navy as an officer.
Many universities offer a BNS to students interested in naval leadership and maritime operations.
The requirements for a BNS include courses in navigation, engineering, and military history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: BSN - Bachelor of Science in Nursing, an undergraduate nursing degree

Etymology

Acronym formed from 'Bachelor of Naval Science.'
