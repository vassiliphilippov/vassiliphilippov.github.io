mocha

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊ.kə / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.kə / (BrE)
a dark brown color, often with a slight reddish or coffee-like undertone
dark brown color

Examples

The designer chose a mocha shade for the living room walls.
She wore a mocha dress to match her brown shoes.
This sofa is available in cream, grey, and mocha colors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffee - a medium to dark brown color

Etymology

Named after Mocha, a port city in Yemen known for its coffee, from which the color reference derives.

2. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊkə / (AmE), / ˈməʊkə / (BrE)
soft suede leather made from goatskin, used especially for gloves and other fine leather items
soft goatskin suede leather

Examples

The manufacturer specializes in fine mocha gloves for luxury retailers.
She admired the supple texture of the mocha used in the designer’s latest handbag collection.
Traditional shoemakers often select mocha for its softness and durability in premium products.

Etymology

Named after Mocha, a port in Yemen associated with exporting such leather.

3. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊkə / (AmE), / ˈmɒkə / (BrE)
a flavoring or beverage combining coffee and chocolate, often used for drinks or desserts
coffee and chocolate flavoring

Examples

I ordered a mocha with extra whipped cream from the café downstairs.
The rich mocha frosting on the cake was absolutely delicious.
She prefers the taste of mocha to regular coffee because of its chocolatey flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cappuccino - coffee drink with espresso and frothed milk
  • Synonym: latte - coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk

Etymology

From Mocha, a port city in Yemen, once known for its coffee exports.

4. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊ.kə / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.kə / (BrE)
A superior, dark coffee made from beans originally grown in Arabia, typically noted for its rich flavor and aroma.
superior dark coffee from Arabia

Examples

She always orders a mocha when visiting her favorite café.
The rich aroma of freshly brewed mocha filled the kitchen.
He prefers mocha over other types of coffee for its bold taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: espresso - a strong coffee brewed by forcing steam through ground coffee beans
  • Degree Lower: latte - a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk

Etymology

From the Yemeni port of Mocha, a historical coffee trade center.
