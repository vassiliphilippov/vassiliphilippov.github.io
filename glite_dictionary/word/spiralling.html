spiralling

1. ADJ

/ ˈspaɪrəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspaɪərəlɪŋ / (BrE)
Shaped like a coil or forming a spiral; winding continuously around a central point or axis.
shaped like a coil; winding in a spiral

Examples

The staircase had a spiralling design that gave the building a unique look.
A spiralling vine climbed up the old brick wall, adding charm to the garden.
Smoke rose from the chimney in a spiralling column toward the grey sky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiral - curve or object winding in a continuous curve around a central point

Etymology

From 'spiral' (from Latin 'spiralis') + English participle suffix '-ing'.
