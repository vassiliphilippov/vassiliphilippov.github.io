novelette

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɑvəˈlɛt / (AmE), / ˌnɒvəˈlɛt / (BrE)
a short novel, typically longer than a short story but shorter than a full-length novel
short novel

Examples

Her latest novelette was praised for its vivid characters and concise storytelling.
The magazine features a new science fiction novelette in every issue.
He is working on a novelette because he finds the shorter form more manageable than a full-length novel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: novella - a short novel or long short story
  • Degree Higher: novel - a long work of fiction
  • Degree Lower: short story - a brief fictional prose narrative

Etymology

Diminutive of "novel", from Italian *novella* (story, news).
