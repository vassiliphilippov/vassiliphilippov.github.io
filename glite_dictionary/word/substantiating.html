substantiating

1. ADJ

/ səbˈstænʃiˌeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / səbˈstænʃieɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
providing support or evidence that confirms or corroborates something
providing supporting evidence

Examples

The lawyer presented substantiating documents to support her argument in court.
We need more substantiating evidence before making such a serious accusation.
His claims were dismissed due to a lack of substantiating data.

Synset Examples

collateral evidence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corroborating - providing additional support or confirmation

Etymology

From Latin 'substantiatus' (to substantiate), via the verb 'substantiāre'.
