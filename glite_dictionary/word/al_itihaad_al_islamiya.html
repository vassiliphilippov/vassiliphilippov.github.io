al Itihaad al Islamiya

1. PROPN

/ æl ɪtiˈhɑd æl ɪslɑːˈmiːjɑ / (AmE), / æl ɪtiˈhɑːd æl ɪslɑːˈmiːjə / (BrE)
a Somali fundamentalist Islamic group associated with militant and terrorist activities, including attacks in Somalia and Ethiopia, and links to al-Qaeda.
Somali Islamist militant group

Examples

The United Nations has condemned the actions of al Itihaad al Islamiya in Somalia.
Intelligence reports suggest al Itihaad al Islamiya has connections to other extremist groups in the region.
In the 1990s, al Itihaad al Islamiya was considered one of the most dangerous organizations in East Africa.

Etymology

From Arabic, meaning 'The Islamic Union.'
