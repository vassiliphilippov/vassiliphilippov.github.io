preparedness

1. NOUN

/ prɪˈpɛrdnəs / (AmE), / prɪˈpeədnɪs / (BrE)
the state of being ready or prepared for something, especially for action or an emergency
state of being ready

Examples

Emergency drills improve a school’s preparedness for natural disasters.
The military increased its level of preparedness after receiving intelligence of a possible attack.
Her preparedness for the interview clearly impressed the hiring manager.

Synset Examples

putting them in readiness
their preparation was more than adequate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: readiness - the state of being fully prepared for something
  • Antonym: unpreparedness - the state of not being ready or not prepared

Etymology

Formed from 'prepared' + '-ness', indicating the state or condition of being prepared.
