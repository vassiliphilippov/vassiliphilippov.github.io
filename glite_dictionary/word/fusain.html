fusain

1. NOUN

/ fuːˈzeɪn / (AmE), / fjuːˈzeɪn / (BrE)
A stick of black carbon material used for drawing, especially in artistic sketching and rendering.
a black carbon drawing stick

Examples

The artist used a fusain to create bold, expressive lines in her sketch.
He prefers drawing with fusain rather than pencils for its rich, dark texture.
Many art students experiment with fusain during their first year of classes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charcoal - Black carbon material used in art for drawing or sketching

Etymology

From French 'fusain' (charcoal pencil), from Latin 'fusus' (spindle), referring to the spindle shape of the stick.
