angered

1. ADJ

/ ˈæŋɡərd / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡəd / (BrE)
Feeling or showing strong anger; very angry or enraged.
filled with strong anger

Examples

She was clearly angered by his rude remarks during the meeting.
The new policy angered many employees who felt it was unfair.
He tried to remain calm, but the unjust accusation angered him deeply.

Synset Examples

the enraged bull attached
furious about the accident
a furious scowl
infuriated onlookers charged the police who were beating the boy
could not control the maddened crowd

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enraged - extremely angry, filled with rage
  • Synonym: furious - extremely angry, with intense emotion
  • Antonym: calm - not angry, peaceful
