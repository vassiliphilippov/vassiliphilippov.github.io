mm

1. NOUN

/ ɛm ɛm / (AmE), / ɛm ɛm / (BrE)
a metric unit of length equal to one thousandth of a meter
metric unit of length (millimeter)

Examples

The width of the gap is just 2 mm, making it barely visible.
She measured the screw and found it was 10 mm long.
Rainfall last night amounted to 15 mm in total.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: millimeter - metric unit of length, same as mm

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'millimeter', from French 'millimètre', from Latin 'mille' (thousand) + Greek 'metron' (measure).
