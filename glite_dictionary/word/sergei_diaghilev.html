Sergei Diaghilev

1. PROPN

/ sərˈɡeɪ diˈɑːɡəlɛv / (AmE), / səˈɡeɪ dɪˈɑːɡɪlɛv / (BrE)
(1872–1929) Russian ballet impresario best known for founding the Ballets Russes and introducing Russian ballet to Western Europe.
Russian ballet impresario, founder of the Ballets Russes

Examples

Sergei Diaghilev transformed the world of ballet through his innovative and daring productions.
Many famous artists and composers collaborated with Sergei Diaghilev during the early 20th century.
The Ballets Russes, founded by Sergei Diaghilev, had a lasting impact on Western art and culture.
