centimetre

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛn.tɪˌmiː.tər / (AmE), / ˈsɛntɪmiːtə / (BrE)
a unit of length in the metric system equal to one hundredth of a meter (0.01 meter)
metric unit of length (0.01 meter)

Examples

The width of the notebook is about 15 centimetres.
She grew five centimetres taller in a year.
Please measure the fabric in centimetres for accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: millimetre - a metric unit of length equal to one thousandth of a meter
  • Degree Higher: meter - the base unit of length in the metric system
  • Synonym: inch - a unit of length in the imperial system, approximately 2.54 centimetres

Etymology

From French 'centimètre', from 'centi-' (hundredth) + 'mètre' (meter, measure)
