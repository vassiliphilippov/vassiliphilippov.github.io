propwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑpˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈprɒpˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female member of a theater stage crew responsible for managing and arranging props during a production
female props manager in theater

Examples

The propwoman hurried backstage to make sure every item was set for the next scene.
As a skilled propwoman, she could find or create any prop the director requested.
During rehearsals, the actors often thanked the propwoman for her attention to detail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: propman - male member of the stage crew in charge of props

Etymology

Formed by combining 'prop' (short for 'property,' meaning stage objects) and 'woman.'
