counseling

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊn.səl.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.səl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of giving professional advice or guidance to help someone make decisions or deal with problems, especially of a personal or emotional nature
professional advice or guidance

Examples

She sought counseling to help her cope with the loss of her job.
The university offers free counseling services for students experiencing stress or anxiety.
After the accident, the family attended counseling sessions together to work through their emotions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advice - an opinion or recommendation offered as a guide to action or conduct
  • Degree Higher: therapy - treatment intended to relieve or heal a disorder

Etymology

From 'counsel' (advice), ultimately from Latin 'consilium' (deliberation, advice).
