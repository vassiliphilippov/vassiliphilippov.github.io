nonphysical

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈfɪzɪkəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈfɪzɪkəl / (BrE)
not having physical substance; not capable of being touched, seen, or measured by physical means.
lacking physical substance or reality

Examples

Some philosophers debate whether consciousness is a nonphysical phenomenon.
Her influence on the team went beyond the nonphysical realm of morale.
The ghost was believed to be a nonphysical entity that could move through walls.

Synset Examples

that intangible thing — the soul

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intangible - unable to be touched or grasped; not having physical presence
  • Antonym: physical - having material existence; perceptible by the senses

Etymology

Formed from 'non-' (not) + 'physical'.
