activistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæktɪˈvɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌaktɪˈvɪstɪk / (BrE)
advocating or engaged in activism; actively supporting or promoting a cause, especially a political or social one.
advocating or engaged in activism

Examples

Her activistic approach inspired many people to join the environmental movement.
The organization adopted a more activistic role in addressing social justice issues.
He is known for his activistic stance on human rights throughout his career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: activist - a person who campaigns for social or political change

Etymology

From activist + -ic, forming an adjective.
