cigaret

1. NOUN

/ sɪgəˈrɛt / (AmE), / sɪgəˈrɛt / (BrE)
A thin roll of finely ground tobacco wrapped in paper for smoking.
thin tobacco roll for smoking

Examples

He pulled a cigaret from the pack and lit it with a match.
The doctor advised him to quit smoking cigarets for his health.
She offered her last cigaret to a stranger at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cigarette - a thin roll of tobacco for smoking

Etymology

Variant of 'cigarette', from French 'cigarette', diminutive of 'cigare'.
