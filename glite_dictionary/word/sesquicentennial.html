sesquicentennial

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛskwi.sɛnˈtɛn.i.əl / (AmE), / ˌsɛskwi.sɛnˈtɛn.i.əl / (BrE)
The 150th anniversary of an event or the celebration marking it.
150th anniversary or celebration

Examples

The city held a grand parade to mark its sesquicentennial.
Many alumni returned for the university’s sesquicentennial celebrations.
The museum is planning special exhibits for the sesquicentennial of its founding.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: centennial - 100th anniversary or its celebration
  • Degree Higher: bicentennial - 200th anniversary or its celebration

Etymology

Formed from Latin 'sesqui-' (one and a half times) + 'centennial' (hundredth anniversary).
