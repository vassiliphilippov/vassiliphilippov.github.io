Laconia

1. PROPN

/ ləˈkoʊniə / (AmE), / ləˈkəʊniə / (BrE)
an ancient region of southern Greece in the southeastern Peloponnesus, historically dominated by Sparta
ancient Greek region, home to Sparta

Examples

The warriors of Laconia were renowned for their discipline and austerity.
Many archaeological sites in Laconia reveal the area's rich history dating back to ancient times.
In classical literature, Laconia is often mentioned in connection with the legendary city of Sparta.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sparta - an ancient Greek city-state, dominant power in Laconia

Etymology

From Latin Laconia, from Ancient Greek Λακωνία (Lakōnía).
