demander

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈmændər / (AmE), / dɪˈmɑːndə / (BrE)
a person who makes demands or requests, especially insistently or formally
person who makes demands

Examples

The demander insisted on immediate payment for the work completed.
In a competitive market, the demander sets the tone for negotiations.
The contract was reviewed after the demander raised several concerns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plaintiff - the party who brings a case against another in a court of law
  • Synonym: requester - a person who asks for something

Etymology

From demand + -er, denoting 'one who demands'.
