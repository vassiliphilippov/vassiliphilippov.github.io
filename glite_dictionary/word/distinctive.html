distinctive

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈstɪŋktɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪŋktɪv / (BrE)
having a quality or feature that makes something noticeably different from others; characteristic and distinguishing.
having a distinguishing feature or quality

Examples

The artist's use of bold colors gave her paintings a distinctive style.
That building is very distinctive because of its unusual shape.
He has a distinctive accent that sets him apart from other speakers.

Synset Examples

Jerusalem has a distinctive Middle East flavor
that is typical of you!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: characteristic - typical or representative feature that serves to identify something
  • Synonym: typical - having the usual qualities of a particular type or group

Etymology

From Latin 'distinctivus', from 'distinguere' (to separate, distinguish).
