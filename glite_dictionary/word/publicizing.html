publicizing

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪˌsaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪˌsaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or business of drawing public attention to goods or services, especially in order to promote them
the act of promoting goods or services

Examples

The company's new publicizing strategy boosted sales significantly this quarter.
Effective publicizing is essential for attracting customers to a new product.
She specializes in publicizing for small startups and local businesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertising - the activity or industry of producing advertisements
  • Synonym: marketing - the action or business of promoting and selling products or services

Etymology

From the verb 'publicize', formed from 'public' and the suffix '-ize'.
