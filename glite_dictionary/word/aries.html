Aries

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr.iˌiz / (AmE), / ˈɛəriːz / (BrE)
A person born between March 21 and April 19, when the sun is in the astrological sign Aries.
person born under zodiac sign Aries

Examples

My friend is an Aries and is known for her energetic personality.
As an Aries, he often takes the lead in group activities.
Many Aries are said to enjoy adventure and excitement.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Pisces - person born under zodiac sign Pisces
  • Antonym: Taurus - person born under zodiac sign Taurus

Etymology

From Latin Aries, meaning 'ram', associated with the zodiac sign.

2. NOUN

/ ˈɛə.riˌiz / (AmE), / ˈeə.riːz / (BrE)
the first sign of the zodiac, which the sun enters at the vernal equinox, typically from March 21 to April 19
the first sign of the zodiac

Examples

People born under Aries are said to be energetic and adventurous.
The sun enters Aries at the vernal equinox, marking the start of spring.
Her zodiac sign is Aries, which she believes influences her personality.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Taurus - the second sign of the zodiac

Etymology

From Latin 'Aries' meaning 'ram', referring to the constellation.

3. NOUN

/ ˈɛriˌiz / (AmE), / ˈɛːriːz / (BrE)
a small constellation of the zodiac, located in the Northern Hemisphere between Pisces and Taurus
zodiacal constellation between Pisces and Taurus

Examples

The constellation Aries is best visible in the night sky during autumn and early winter.
Many astronomers use Aries as a reference point when mapping stars in the northern sky.
Aries is located between Pisces and Taurus in the zodiac.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pisces - a zodiacal constellation next to Aries
  • Synonym: Taurus - a zodiacal constellation adjacent to Aries

Etymology

From Latin 'Aries' meaning 'ram'.
