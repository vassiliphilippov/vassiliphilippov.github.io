Richea

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪkiə / (AmE), / ˈrɪkiə / (BrE)
a genus of evergreen trees or shrubs native to the mountains of Australia and Tasmania
genus of evergreen trees/shrubs (Australia, Tasmania)

Examples

The alpine slopes were dotted with clusters of Richea in full bloom.
Some species of Richea are highly valued by botanists for their unique adaptation to cold climates.
While hiking in Tasmania, we saw several Richea shrubs along the path.
