rearwards

1. ADV

/ ˈrɪrwərdz / (AmE), / ˈrɪəwədz / (BrE)
towards the back or rear; in the direction behind.
towards the back or rear

Examples

The car rolled rearwards down the slight incline.
She glanced rearwards to check if anyone was following.
The horse stumbled as it stepped rearwards from the trailer.

Synset Examples

he moved back
tripped when he stepped backward
she looked rearward out the window of the car

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backwards - towards the back or rear
  • Antonym: forwards - towards the front

Etymology

From 'rear' (the back part) + suffix '-wards' (in the direction of).
