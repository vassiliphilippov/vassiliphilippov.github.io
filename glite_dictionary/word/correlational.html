correlational

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɔːrəˈleɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌkɒrəˈleɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving correlation, especially as a method of analysis in statistics or research.
relating to correlation (esp. in research)

Examples

The researchers used a correlational study to examine the relationship between stress and productivity.
This paper explores the correlational patterns between dietary habits and heart disease.
A correlational analysis was conducted to assess the association between age and memory performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: correlation - a mutual relationship or connection between two or more things

Etymology

Formed from 'correlation' + '-al'.
