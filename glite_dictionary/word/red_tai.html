Red Tai

1. NOUN

/ rɛd taɪ / (AmE), / rɛd taɪ / (BrE)
a branch of the Tai languages spoken in parts of northern Myanmar, particularly in Kachin State and Sagaing Region
Tai language variety of Myanmar

Examples

Linguists have conducted extensive research on the grammar of Red Tai.
Many people in Homalin township speak Red Tai as their first language.
The Red Tai language is known for its rich vowel system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tai languages - a family of related languages spoken in Southeast Asia
