bed-wetting

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛdˌwɛtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbedˌwɛtɪŋ / (BrE)
Involuntary urination during sleep; especially common in children who usually outgrow it.
involuntary urination during sleep

Examples

Bed-wetting can sometimes persist into adolescence if not addressed with proper support.
Many parents worry when their child experiences bed-wetting, but it is usually a phase.
Doctors recommend reassurance and patience when helping a child who struggles with bed-wetting.
