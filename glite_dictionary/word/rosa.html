Rosa

1. PROPN

/ ˈroʊzə / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzə / (BrE)
The genus of flowering plants within the rose family (Rosaceae), including all cultivated and wild roses.
genus of flowering plants (roses)

Examples

The genus Rosa includes both wild and cultivated species of rose.
Many garden varieties belong to Rosa, prized for their beauty and fragrance.
Botanists have identified over a hundred species within the Rosa genus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rose - a flowering plant of the genus Rosa

Etymology

From Latin 'rosa' (rose), used in botanical Latin.
