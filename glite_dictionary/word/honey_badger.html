honey badger

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌni ˌbædʒər / (AmE), / ˈhʌni ˌbadʒə / (BrE)
a person or animal who is extremely tough, fearless, or persistent, often regardless of danger
fearless or tough individual (slang)
informal slang

Examples

After seeing her handle the crisis, it was clear she was a real honey badger in the office.
Despite his small size, the running back played like a honey badger and never gave up.
Journalists described the activist as a honey badger for her relentless pursuit of justice.

Synset Examples

That player is a honey badger on the field.
The honey badger doesn't back down from fights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: badass - a tough, uncompromising, or intimidating person

Etymology

From the reputation of the honey badger (Mellivora capensis), an animal known for its fearless nature.
