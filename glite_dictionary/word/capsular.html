capsular

1. ADJ

/ ˈkæpsjələr / (AmE), / ˈkapsjʊlə / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling a capsule, especially in anatomical, botanical, or biological contexts.
relating to a capsule
medical

Examples

The patient suffered a capsular tear in his knee requiring surgery.
A capsular structure surrounds the spores of some plants.
They discovered a capsular swelling near the joint during the examination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: capsule - a small case, sack, or shell, especially in biology or medicine

Etymology

From Latin 'capsula' (a small box or case) + '-ar' (forming adjectives).
