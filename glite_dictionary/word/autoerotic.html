autoerotic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔːtoʊ.ɪˈrɑːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊ.ɪˈrɒtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to sexual arousal or satisfaction obtained by stimulating oneself, especially by masturbation.
sexually self-stimulating

Examples

Some psychologists study autoerotic behaviors to understand patterns of sexual development.
People may feel uncomfortable discussing autoerotic activities due to social stigma.
The book examines various autoerotic practices across different cultures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masturbatory - relating to or involving masturbation
  • Antonym: heteroerotic - sexual attraction directed toward the opposite sex

Etymology

From Greek 'auto-' (self) + 'erōtikos' (sexual, amorous).
