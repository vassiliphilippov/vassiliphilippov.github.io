baric

1. ADJ

/ ˈbærɪk / (AmE), / ˈbærɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing barium, especially in chemical compounds.
relating to or containing barium

Examples

The compound formed a baric oxide during the reaction.
Researchers analyzed the baric chloride found in the mineral sample.
In barite, the baric content plays a crucial role in its properties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barium - a chemical element with symbol Ba; a soft, silvery alkaline earth metal

Etymology

From modern Latin 'barium' + '-ic', denoting a relationship or presence.
