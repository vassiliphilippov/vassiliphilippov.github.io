Xizang

1. PROPN

/ ɕi˥˩ tsɑːŋ˧˥ / (AmE), / ɕiːˈzæŋ / (BrE)
an autonomous region of the People's Republic of China, located in the Himalayas and also known as Tibet.
Chinese region in the Himalayas; Tibet

Examples

Many tourists visit Xizang each year to experience its unique culture and breathtaking landscapes.
The capital of Xizang is Lhasa, a city known for its historic monasteries.
China has invested in infrastructure projects across Xizang to boost connectivity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tibet - autonomous region in the Himalayas, also referred to as Xizang

Etymology

Chinese name for Tibet; from Mandarin 西藏 (Xīzàng), meaning 'Western Storage.'
