BVD

1. NOUN

/ bi vi ˈdi / (AmE), / biː viː ˈdiː / (BrE)
a trademark for a brand of men's underwear, especially undershirts and briefs
brand of men's underwear

Examples

He bought several pairs of BVD undershirts for the summer.
The store carries a variety of BVD briefs in different colors.
Many men prefer BVD because of its comfortable fit.

Etymology

From the initials of the founders Bradley, Voorhees & Day.
