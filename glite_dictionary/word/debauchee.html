debauchee

1. NOUN

/ ˌdiːˌbɔːˈtʃiː / (AmE), / ˌdiːbɔːˈtʃiː / (BrE)
A person, typically a man, who indulges excessively in sensual pleasures and lacks moral restraint.
a person morally unrestrained; pleasure-seeker

Examples

The infamous debauchee was the subject of gossip throughout the town.
After years of wild living, the old debauchee finally sought redemption.
She avoided the company of any known debauchee at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: libertine - a person who behaves without moral principles, especially in sexual matters
  • Synonym: sinner - a person who transgresses divine law by committing sin
  • Antonym: ascetic - a person who practices severe self-discipline and abstention from all forms of indulgence

Etymology

From French 'débauché' (debauched person), past participle of 'débaucher' (to debauch).
