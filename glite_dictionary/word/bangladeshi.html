Bangladeshi

1. ADJ

/ ˌbæŋɡləˈdɛʃi / (AmE), / ˌbaŋɡləˈdɛʃi / (BrE)
relating to Bangladesh, its people, or its language
of or relating to Bangladesh

Examples

He loves Bangladeshi cuisine, especially spicy curries.
The Bangladeshi community in London has grown significantly over the years.
She wore a traditional Bangladeshi sari to the festival.

Synset Examples

Bangladeshi dialects

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bangladesh - a country in South Asia, east of India

Etymology

From Bangladesh + English suffix -i, denoting nationality or origin.

2. NOUN

/ ˌbæŋɡləˈdɛʃi / (AmE), / ˌbaŋɡləˈdɛʃi / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Bangladesh
native or inhabitant of Bangladesh

Examples

Many Bangladeshis living abroad maintain strong ties to their homeland.
The city has a vibrant Bangladeshi community with its own shops and restaurants.
He is a proud Bangladeshi who frequently visits Dhaka.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bangladesh - country in South Asia

Etymology

From Bangladesh + -i, denoting association with the country.
