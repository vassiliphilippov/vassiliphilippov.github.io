viewless

1. ADJ

/ ˈvjuːləs / (AmE), / ˈvjuːləs / (BrE)
Lacking or not expressing opinions or personal views.
lacking opinions or views

Examples

The committee remained viewless on the controversial issue.
He was surprisingly viewless in the debate, offering no clear stance.
A viewless neutrality often frustrates those seeking decisive leadership.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: opinionated - having and expressing strong opinions

Etymology

Formed from 'view' + '-less', meaning 'without views'.
