Goeteborg

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡɜːtəˌbɜːrɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːtəˌbɜːɡ / (BrE)
a port city in southwestern Sweden; the second largest city in Sweden, also known as Gothenburg
city in southwestern Sweden

Examples

She visited Goeteborg to attend an international conference on marine biology.
Exports from Goeteborg account for a significant portion of Sweden's trade.
Many travelers enjoy the museums and vibrant culture of Goeteborg.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gothenburg - alternative English name for the city of Goeteborg

Etymology

From Swedish 'Göteborg', named after the Göta River and the Swedish word 'borg' (fortress).
