auto

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊ / (BrE)
a motor vehicle, typically with four wheels, powered by an internal combustion engine; a car
motor vehicle; car

Examples

He bought his first auto when he was twenty years old.
Many people rely on their auto to commute to work every day.
The family decided to rent an auto for their road trip across the country.

Synset Examples

he needs a car to get to work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car - an automobile; a road vehicle with four wheels

Etymology

From French 'auto', short for 'automobile', from Ancient Greek 'autós' (self).
