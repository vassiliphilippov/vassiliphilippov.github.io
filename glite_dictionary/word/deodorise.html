to deodorise

1. VERB

/ diˈoʊdəˌraɪz / (AmE), / diˈəʊdəraɪz / (BrE)
to remove or conceal unpleasant smells from something
remove unpleasant smells
british

Examples

I always deodorise my shoes after a long run to keep them fresh.
The cleaning staff deodorised the restroom thoroughly before guests arrived.
You can use baking soda to deodorise your refrigerator naturally.

Synset Examples

This stick will deodorize your armpits

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to deodorize - American English spelling of 'deodorise'

Etymology

From 'de-' (removal) + 'odor' + '-ise' (verb-forming suffix); modeled on French 'déodoriser'.
