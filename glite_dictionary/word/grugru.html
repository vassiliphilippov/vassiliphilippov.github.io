grugru

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡruːˌɡruː / (AmE), / ˈɡruːˌɡruː / (BrE)
A tropical American feather palm with a swollen, spiny trunk and edible nuts.
tropical palm with spiny trunk and edible nuts

Examples

The grugru palm is easily recognized by its distinctive spiny trunk.
Locals often collect the edible nuts from the grugru for making snacks.
A grugru can provide shade and food in tropical American forests.

Etymology

From a South American indigenous language, possibly Tupi-Guarani.
