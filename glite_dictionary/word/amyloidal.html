amyloidal

1. ADJ

/ ˌæm.iˈlɔɪ.dl̩ / (AmE), / ˌæm.ɪˈlɔɪ.dl̩ / (BrE)
resembling or having characteristics of starch, especially in appearance or texture.
resembling starch

Examples

The tissue exhibited an amyloidal appearance when stained and examined under the microscope.
The scientist noted an amyloidal deposit in the sample, suggesting the presence of abnormal proteins.
These structures have an amyloidal quality, making them difficult to distinguish from true starch grains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: starchy - containing or resembling starch

Etymology

From New Latin 'amyloideus', from Greek 'amylon' (starch).
