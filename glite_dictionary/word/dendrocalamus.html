Dendrocalamus

1. NOUN

/ dɛnˌdroʊˈkæləməs / (AmE), / dɛndrəʊˈkæləməs / (BrE)
a genus of giant, clumping bamboos native to Asia, known for their large woody stems
genus of giant clumping bamboos

Examples

Dendrocalamus species are often used in construction due to their strong, large stems.
The botanist collected samples of Dendrocalamus from the tropical forest for research.
Farmers planted Dendrocalamus to create a natural windbreak around their fields.
