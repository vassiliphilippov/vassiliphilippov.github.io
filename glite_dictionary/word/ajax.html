Ajax

1. PROPN

/ ˈeɪˌdʒæks / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.dʒæks / (BrE)
A Greek hero in mythology, renowned for his strength and bravery in the Trojan War, especially as described in the Iliad.
Greek hero of the Trojan War

Examples

The bravery of Ajax was praised by both his allies and enemies during the war.
Many artists have depicted Ajax in scenes from the Iliad.
The legend of Ajax has inspired countless stories and plays throughout history.

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Αἴας (Aías), the name of the hero.
