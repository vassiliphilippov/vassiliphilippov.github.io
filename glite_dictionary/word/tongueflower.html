tongueflower

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌŋˌflaʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈtʌŋˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
An orchid with blue to purple flowers, distinguished by tongue-shaped or strap-shaped protuberances (calli) at the base of the lip.
orchid with tongue-shaped calli on the lip

Examples

The tongueflower is admired for its striking blue petals and unusual lip structure.
She added a rare tongueflower to her orchid collection last spring.
Botanists studied the tongueflower to better understand its unique pollination mechanism.

Etymology

From 'tongue' (referring to shape) and 'flower'.
