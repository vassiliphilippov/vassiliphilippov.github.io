snowplow

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnoʊˌplaʊ / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌplaʊ / (BrE)
A vehicle or device with a blade used to remove snow from roads or other surfaces.
vehicle for clearing snow from roads

Examples

The city dispatched a snowplow to clear the main streets after the heavy storm.
We had to wait for the snowplow to pass before we could leave the driveway.
During winter, a snowplow is essential for keeping the roads safe and accessible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snowplough - British spelling for a vehicle or device used to remove snow from roads

Etymology

Formed from 'snow' and 'plow', referring to its function.
