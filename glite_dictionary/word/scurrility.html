scurrility

1. NOUN

/ skəˈrɪlɪti / (AmE), / skəˈrɪlɪti / (BrE)
the use of coarse, abusive, or obscene language or behavior
coarse or abusive language

Examples

The politician's speech descended into scurrility, shocking many in the audience.
She was offended by the scurrility directed at her by the online trolls.
Such scurrility has no place in civil discourse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abuse - cruel or violent treatment or language
  • Synonym: vulgarity - the quality of being crude or coarse, especially in speech
  • Antonym: politeness - behavior that is respectful and considerate of other people

Etymology

From Latin 'scurrilitas', from 'scurrilis' (coarse, buffoonish).
