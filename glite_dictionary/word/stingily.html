stingily

1. ADV

/ ˈstɪndʒɪli / (AmE), / ˈstɪndʒɪli / (BrE)
In a stingy manner; unwillingly giving or spending, or with extreme reluctance to part with resources.
in an ungenerous or tightfisted manner

Examples

He tipped the waiter stingily, despite the excellent service.
They donated stingily to the fundraiser, giving only a token amount.
Her praise was given stingily, making her approval feel rare and hard-earned.

Synset Examples

their rich uncle treated them rather chintzily

Related Terms

  • Antonym: generously - in a generous manner

Etymology

From 'stingy' + '-ly', forming an adverb from the adjective.
