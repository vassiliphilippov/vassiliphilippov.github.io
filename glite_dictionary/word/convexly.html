convexly

1. ADV

/ kənˈvɛksli / (AmE), / kɒnˈvɛksli / (BrE)
In a way that has a surface or outline that curves outward like the exterior of a sphere or circle.
in a convex manner

Examples

The drop of water rested convexly on the leaf's surface, forming a perfect dome.
The lens is shaped convexly to better focus the light.
The shell expanded convexly outward as the animal matured.

Synset Examples

bulging out convexly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: concavely - in a concave way; curving inward

Etymology

From 'convex' + '-ly'. 'Convex' from Latin 'convexus' (vaulted, arched).
