obligational

1. ADJ

/ əˌblɪɡəˈʃənl̩ / (AmE), / əˌblɪɡəˈʃənl / (BrE)
relating to or creating a legal or financial obligation.
relating to legal/financial obligations
legal

Examples

The contract sets out all the obligational duties of both parties involved.
This type of bond carries certain obligational risks for the issuer.
The committee discussed the obligational authority required to approve the expenditure.

Synset Examples

obligational authority

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obligatory - required by law, rule, or custom

Etymology

From 'obligation' + '-al', modeled on other legal adjectives.
