delayed

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈleɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈleɪd / (BrE)
slower or later than expected, especially in development or progress.
slower than expected in development

Examples

The child was found to be delayed in language skills compared to his peers.
Because of chronic illness, some children may become delayed in their physical development.
The program provides extra help for students who are delayed in learning to read.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: advanced - ahead in development or progress
