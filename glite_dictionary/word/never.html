never

1. ADV

/ ˈnɛvɚ / (AmE), / ˈnɛvə / (BrE)
At no time in the past or future; not ever; not at all.
not ever; at no time

Examples

She has never traveled outside her home country.
I will never forget the kindness you showed me.
They said they would never allow smoking in the building.

Synset Examples

I have never been to China
I shall never forget this day
had never seen a circus
never on Sunday
I will never marry you!
never fear

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ever - at any time
  • Antonym: always - at all times; on all occasions

Etymology

From Old English *nǣfre* ('not ever'), from *ne* ('not') + *ǣfre* ('ever').
