Entsi

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛntsi / (AmE), / ˈɛntsi / (BrE)
the Uralic language spoken by the Enets people, primarily in northern Siberia.
Uralic language of the Enets people

Examples

The Entsi language is considered critically endangered due to its declining number of speakers.
Linguists have published several studies about the grammar of Entsi.
Only a few elders in the community still speak fluent Entsi.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Enets - the indigenous people who speak Entsi

Etymology

From Russian Энцы (Entsy), referring to the Enets people.
