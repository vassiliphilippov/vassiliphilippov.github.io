wafer-thin

1. ADJ

/ ˈweɪfər θɪn / (AmE), / ˈweɪfə θɪn / (BrE)
Extremely thin; as thin as a wafer.
extremely thin

Examples

The chef sliced the vegetables into wafer-thin strips for the salad.
They bought a new television that was wafer-thin compared to their old set.
His patience wore to a wafer-thin margin as the meeting dragged on.

Synset Examples

wafer-thin sheets of metal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thin - having little thickness or depth
  • Synonym: paper-thin - extremely thin, like paper

Etymology

Formed from 'wafer', a thin crisp cake, and 'thin'.
