Pope Urban II

1. PROPN

/ poʊp ˈɜrbən ðə ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / pəʊp ˈɜːbən ðə ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
(c. 1042–1099) The Pope from 1088 to 1099, known for initiating the First Crusade through his call at the Council of Clermont.
French pope who launched the First Crusade

Examples

Pope Urban II called for the First Crusade in his famous speech at Clermont in 1095.
Many historians credit Pope Urban II with uniting European Christians for a common cause.
The legacy of Pope Urban II has been widely discussed by scholars of medieval history.
