deboned

1. ADJ

/ diˈboʊnd / (AmE), / diːˈbəʊnd / (BrE)
(of meat or fish) having had the bones removed.
meat or fish with bones removed

Examples

The recipe recommends using deboned chicken breasts for easier preparation.
She ordered a deboned fish fillet at the restaurant.
For safety, always choose deboned cuts of meat when cooking for children.

Synset Examples

a boneless rib roast
a boned (or deboned) fish

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boneless - having no bones or having had the bones removed (especially of meat or fish)
