Surinam River

1. PROPN

/ ˈsʊrɪnæm ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈsjʊərɪnæm ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in Suriname that flows northward to the Atlantic Ocean.
river flowing through Suriname

Examples

The Surinam River is an important waterway for transportation in the country.
Many villages are located along the banks of the Surinam River.
Tourists enjoy boat tours on the scenic Surinam River each year.

Etymology

Named after the country Suriname, whose name comes from the indigenous Surinen people.
