tenting

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɛntɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of encamping and living in tents, especially as part of an outing or trip
the act of camping in tents

Examples

We spent the weekend tenting in the national park and enjoyed the fresh air.
Many families enjoy tenting during the summer holidays as an affordable vacation.
The scouts organized a tenting trip to learn outdoor survival skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camping - the activity of spending a holiday living in a tent or camper
