accouterment

1. NOUN

/ əˈkuːtɚmənt / (AmE), / əˈkuːtəmənt / (BrE)
an additional item of dress or equipment, not part of a person's main clothing; an accessory or piece of equipment
additional item of dress or equipment

Examples

The knight's armor was decorated with various accouterments that showed his family crest.
Her handbag, scarf, and sunglasses were stylish accouterments to her outfit.
The explorers packed all the necessary accouterments for the long journey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accessory - an additional item worn or carried for convenience or decoration

Etymology

From French *accoutrement*, from accoutrer (to equip, dress).
