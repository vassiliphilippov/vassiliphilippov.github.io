doubtfully

1. ADV

/ ˈdaʊtfəli / (AmE), / ˈdaʊtfəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows uncertainty or lack of confidence.
in an uncertain or hesitant manner

Examples

She looked at the instructions doubtfully before trying to assemble the furniture.
He answered doubtfully, unsure if his information was correct.
The child glanced doubtfully at the plate of unfamiliar food.

Synset Examples

Gerald shook his head doubtfully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uncertainly - in an uncertain manner
  • Antonym: confidently - in a confident manner

Etymology

From 'doubtful' + '-ly'.
