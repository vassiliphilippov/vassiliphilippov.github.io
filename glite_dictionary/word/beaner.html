beaner

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːnər / (AmE), / ˈbiːnə / (BrE)
(Baseball) a pitch deliberately thrown at the batter's head.
baseball pitch thrown at the batter's head

Examples

The pitcher was ejected after throwing a beaner that narrowly missed the batter.
Throwing a beaner is considered dangerous and can lead to suspension.
Fans were outraged when the star player was hit by a beaner during the final inning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beanball - a baseball pitch thrown at or near the batter's head

Etymology

From baseball slang 'bean' meaning 'head', combined with the suffix '-er'.
