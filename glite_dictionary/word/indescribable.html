indescribable

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪndɪˈskraɪbəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈskraɪbəbl̩ / (BrE)
Too unusual, extreme, or wonderful to be described adequately in words.
impossible to describe in words

Examples

The sunset painted the sky in indescribable colors that left everyone speechless.
Her joy at seeing her family again was simply indescribable.
He experienced an indescribable sense of peace while walking through the quiet forest.

Synset Examples

indefinable yearnings
indescribable beauty
ineffable ecstasy
unspeakable happiness
unutterable contempt
a thing of untellable splendor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indefinable - impossible to define or describe
  • Synonym: ineffable - too great or extreme to be expressed in words
  • Synonym: unspeakable - too bad or shocking to be expressed in words
  • Antonym: describable - able to be described

Etymology

From Latin prefix in- (not) + describable, from describere (to describe).
