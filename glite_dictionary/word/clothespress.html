clothespress

1. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊðzˌprɛs / (AmE), / ˈkləʊðzˌprɛs / (BrE)
a cupboard or closet for storing clothes
a cupboard for storing clothes

Examples

She opened the clothespress to choose a dress for the evening.
The old clothespress in the corner was made of oak and beautifully carved.
He kept his winter coats neatly arranged in the clothespress by the window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wardrobe - a large cupboard for storing clothes
  • Synonym: closet - a small room or compartment for storing clothes

Etymology

From 'clothes' + 'press', with 'press' meaning a cupboard or chest (Middle English, from Old French 'presse').
