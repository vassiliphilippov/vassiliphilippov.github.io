Vitaceae

1. NOUN

/ vaɪˈteɪsiˌi / (AmE), / vɪˈteɪsiːiː / (BrE)
the botanical family of climbing plants, including grapevines and their relatives, belonging to the order Rhamnales
the grape family of plants

Examples

The Vitaceae family includes economically important plants such as grapes and currants.
Researchers study the Vitaceae for their diversity and adaptation in various climates.
Several species within the Vitaceae are valued for their ornamental and edible qualities.

Etymology

From Latin 'vitis' (vine) + suffix '-aceae' denoting plant families.
