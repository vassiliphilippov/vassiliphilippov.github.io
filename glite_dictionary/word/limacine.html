limacine

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪməˌsaɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɪməsiːn / (BrE)
resembling or relating to a slug; slug-like in appearance or characteristics.
slug-like; resembling a slug

Examples

The shell-less, limacine mollusk glided silently across the wet garden path.
Its movement was slow and limacine, leaving a slimy trail behind.
The artist's sculpture had a limacine shape, soft and elongated like a slug.

Etymology

From Latin limax (slug) + -ine (adjective suffix).
