Zimmer

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɪmɚ / (AmE), / ˈzɪmə / (BrE)
(British) a light, wheeled or castored frame with handles, used by elderly or disabled people to help them walk; walking frame.
wheeled walking frame for mobility support
british

Examples

After her hip surgery, she relied on a Zimmer to get around the house.
The hospital provided several Zimmers for patients in the rehabilitation unit.
Many elderly people find a Zimmer essential for maintaining their independence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: walking frame - a frame to support walking for the infirm or elderly

Etymology

From the trade name of the Zimmer company, which manufactures medical equipment.
