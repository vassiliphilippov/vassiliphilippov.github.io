beautification

1. NOUN

/ ˌbjuː.t̬ɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌbjuː.tɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something more beautiful or attractive
the act of making more beautiful

Examples

The city council approved a beautification program to plant more trees along the main streets.
Many community members volunteered for the park beautification project last spring.
The beautification of the historic district has attracted more visitors and businesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to beautify - to make beautiful; to improve the appearance of
  • Antonym: uglification - the process of making something uglier

Etymology

From 'beautify' + '-ation', with 'beautify' from Old French 'beauté' and Latin 'bellus' (pretty).
