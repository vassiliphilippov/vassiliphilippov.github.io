Mahratti

1. NOUN

/ məˈrɑːti / (AmE), / məˈrɑːti / (BrE)
an Indic language spoken in the state of Maharashtra in west-central India, written in the Devanagari script. Also known as Marathi.
an Indic language of Maharashtra, India

Examples

She is fluent in both Hindi and Mahratti.
The official documents were translated into Mahratti for the local population.
Learning Mahratti helped him better understand the culture of Maharashtra.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Marathi - the principal Indic language of Maharashtra, India

Etymology

From Hindi marāṭhī, relating to Maharashtra, a region in India.
