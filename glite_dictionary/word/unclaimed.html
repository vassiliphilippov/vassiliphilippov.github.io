unclaimed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkleɪmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkleɪmd / (BrE)
not claimed or taken by its owner or rightful person
not claimed by an owner

Examples

The lost and found was filled with unclaimed jackets and umbrellas.
After 90 days, any unclaimed property is donated to charity.
There was an auction for the unclaimed luggage at the airport.

Synset Examples

unclaimed luggage

Related Terms

  • Antonym: claimed - declared as belonging to someone

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'claimed' (past participle of 'claim').
