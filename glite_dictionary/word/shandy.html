shandy

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃændi / (AmE), / ˈʃændi / (BrE)
a drink consisting of beer mixed with lemonade, ginger beer, or a similar non-alcoholic beverage
beer mixed with lemonade or another soft drink

Examples

On a hot day, I like to relax in the garden with a cold shandy.
She ordered a shandy because she wanted something lighter than beer.
At the barbecue, they served homemade shandies to the guests.
