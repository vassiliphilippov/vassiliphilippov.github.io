acinose

1. ADJ

/ ˈæsɪˌnoʊs / (AmE), / ˈæsɪnəʊs / (BrE)
Relating to or composed of small sac-like structures, especially in compound glands.
relating to sac-like glands or structures
medical

Examples

The pancreas is an acinose gland composed of many small lobular sacs.
Microscopic examination revealed acinose clusters typical of salivary tissue.
Many exocrine glands in the body exhibit an acinose structure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acinar - relating to acini or small sacs in glands

Etymology

From Latin acinus (grape, berry) + -ose (having the quality of).
