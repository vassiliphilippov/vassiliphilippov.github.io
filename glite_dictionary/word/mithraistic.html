mithraistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɪθrəˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɪθrəˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to Mithraism, an ancient mystery religion centered on the god Mithras
relating to Mithraism or its god

Examples

Archaeologists found several mithraistic symbols carved into the stones of the underground temple.
The rituals of the mithraistic cult often took place in secluded caves or crypts.
Several Roman soldiers were followers of mithraistic traditions during the empire's peak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mithraic - relating to Mithraism or the god Mithras

Etymology

From Mithraism, the name of the cult of Mithras, with the suffix -istic denoting relation.
