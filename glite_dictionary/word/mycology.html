mycology

1. NOUN

/ maɪˈkɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / maɪˈkɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of fungi, including their genetic and biochemical properties and their uses and diseases caused by them
the study of fungi
medical

Examples

She decided to specialize in mycology after developing a fascination with mushrooms.
The university offers several advanced courses in mycology and fungal diseases.
His groundbreaking research in mycology led to new treatments for fungal infections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fungology - the scientific study of fungi

Etymology

From Greek 'mykēs' (fungus) and '-logy' (study of).
