Lillie Langtry

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪli ˈlæŋtri / (AmE), / ˈlɪli ˈlæŋtri / (BrE)
(1853–1929) British actress known for her beauty, stage career, and as the mistress of the future King Edward VII.
British actress and royal mistress

Examples

Lillie Langtry gained fame in Victorian society both for her acting and her high-profile relationships.
The play depicted the glamorous life of Lillie Langtry in 19th-century London.
Many artists painted portraits of Lillie Langtry during her celebrated career.
