wheezing

1. ADJ

/ ˈwiːzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwiːzɪŋ / (BrE)
making a whistling or rattling sound while breathing, usually as a result of difficulty in breathing.
making a whistling sound when breathing

Examples

The patient reported wheezing throughout the night due to his asthma.
She heard a wheezing noise coming from the next room and went to investigate.
After running in the cold air, he was left wheezing and short of breath.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wheeze - to breathe with a whistling or rattling sound in the chest

Etymology

From the verb 'wheeze,' which is of imitative origin.
