unattainable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈteɪ.nə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈteɪ.nə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to achieve or reach; not able to be attained.
impossible to achieve or reach

Examples

For many, financial independence seems like an unattainable dream.
The mountain's summit was declared unattainable due to dangerous weather conditions.
He set an unattainable standard that no one else could hope to meet.

Synset Examples

an unattainable goal

Related Terms

  • Antonym: achievable - able to be achieved or reached
  • Antonym: reachable - able to be reached or attained
  • Synonym: impossible - not able to occur, exist, or be done

Etymology

From un- (not) + attainable, from Old French atenir (to attain).
