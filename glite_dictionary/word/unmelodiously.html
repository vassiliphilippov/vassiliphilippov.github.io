unmelodiously

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.məˈloʊ.di.əs.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.məˈləʊ.di.əs.li / (BrE)
In a way that is not melodious; lacking melody or pleasant sound.
in an unmelodious way

Examples

The parrot screeched unmelodiously every morning, disturbing the whole street.
The amateur singer performed unmelodiously, making the audience cringe.
Even though the band played unmelodiously, their energy was infectious.

Synset Examples

she sings rather unmelodiously

Related Terms

  • Antonym: melodiously - in a melodious, pleasant-sounding manner

Etymology

From unmelodious + -ly. 'Unmelodious' from un- (not) + melodious.
