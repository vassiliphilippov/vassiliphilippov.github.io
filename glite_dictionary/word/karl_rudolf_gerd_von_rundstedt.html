Karl Rudolf Gerd von Rundstedt

1. PROPN

/ kɑrl ˈruːdɑlf ˈɡɛrd fɔn ˈrʊnʃtɛt / (AmE), / kɑːl ˈruːdɑlf ˈɡeːd fɒn ˈrʊnʃtɛt / (BrE)
(1875–1953) A German field marshal during World War II, noted for directing the conquest of Poland and leading the Ardennes counteroffensive.
