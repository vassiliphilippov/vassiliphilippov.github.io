Peoria

1. PROPN

/ piˈɔriə / (AmE), / piˈɔːriə / (BrE)
A city in central Illinois, located on the Illinois River.
city in central Illinois, USA

Examples

Many major companies have their headquarters in Peoria.
She grew up in Peoria before moving to Chicago for college.
The Illinois River runs through Peoria, offering beautiful waterfront views.

Etymology

Named after the Peoria people, a Native American tribe.
