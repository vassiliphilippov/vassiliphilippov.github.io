Friuli

1. NOUN

/ friˈuːli / (AmE), / friˈuːli / (BrE)
a Rhaeto-Romance dialect spoken in the Friuli region of northeastern Italy
Rhaeto-Romance dialect of northeastern Italy

Examples

The Friuli dialect is recognized as a minority language in Italy.
Many residents of Udine still speak Friuli at home with their families.
She studied Friuli to better understand the region's cultural history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Friulian - the Rhaeto-Romance language/dialect spoken in Friuli

Etymology

From the name of the Friuli region in Italy, ultimately from Latin 'Forum Iulii'.
