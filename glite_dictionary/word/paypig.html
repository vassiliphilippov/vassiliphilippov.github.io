paypig

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪˌpɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪˌpɪɡ / (BrE)
A man who derives sexual gratification from giving money or gifts to a dominant person, typically in the context of financial domination.
submissive man who pays as part of a fetish
slang

Examples

Julia has several paypigs who regularly send her money just for her attention.
He identified as a paypig in various online financial domination communities.
The documentary explored the secretive world of paypigs and their relationships with dominatrixes.

Etymology

Blend of 'pay' and 'pig', where 'pig' is a slang term for a submissive or someone greedy.
