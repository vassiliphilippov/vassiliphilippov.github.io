hunchbacked

1. ADJ

/ ˈhʌntʃˌbækt / (AmE), / ˈhʌntʃˌbækt / (BrE)
having a humped or stooped back, especially due to abnormal curvature of the spine (kyphosis)
having a hump or stooped back

Examples

The old man walked slowly, his hunchbacked figure silhouetted against the evening sky.
She was hunchbacked from years of working at a sewing machine.
The novel features a hunchbacked character shunned by society.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kyphotic - having an abnormal outward curvature of the spine

Etymology

From 'hunchback' (person with a humped back) + '-ed' (adjectival suffix).
