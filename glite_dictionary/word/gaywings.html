gaywings

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪˌwɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪˌwɪŋz / (BrE)
a perennial plant (Polygala paucifolia) native to eastern North America, with wintergreen-like leaves and typically rosy-purple flowers with winged sepals
a North American flowering plant with winged sepals

Examples

The delicate gaywings blossomed along the forest path in early spring.
She bent down to admire the rosy-purple flowers of the gaywings nestled among the ferns.
Botanists noted that gaywings prefer shady, moist woodlands for optimal growth.
