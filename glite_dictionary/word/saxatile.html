saxatile

1. ADJ

/ sækˈsætəl / (AmE), / sækˈsætɪl / (BrE)
(of a plant or animal) growing on or living among rocks.
growing among rocks (biology)

Examples

Alpine plants with saxatile habits are often found in areas with abundant rocky outcrops.
The saxatile lizard is commonly seen basking on sun-warmed stones.
Many saxatile species display adaptations for clinging to rough, uneven surfaces.

Etymology

From Latin 'saxatilis' meaning 'of rocks', from 'saxum' (rock).
