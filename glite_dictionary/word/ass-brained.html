ass-brained

1. ADJ

/ æs breɪnd / (AmE), / æs breɪnd / (BrE)
Very foolish or showing a lack of intelligence; acting in a silly or thoughtless way.
very foolish; lacking intelligence
slang vulgar

Examples

His ass-brained plan was doomed to fail from the start.
I couldn't believe how ass-brained their excuse sounded in court.
Don't be so ass-brained—think before you act next time.

Synset Examples

His ass-brained comments made everyone roll their eyes.
She realized she was being ass-brained and changed her mind.
Stop being so ass-brained about your decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment
  • Antonym: sensible - showing good sense or judgment
