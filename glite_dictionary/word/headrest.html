headrest

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdˌrɛst / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌrɛst / (BrE)
A padded support attached to the top of a seat, especially in vehicles, designed to support the head and prevent neck injuries.
support for the head on a seat

Examples

The new car model comes with adjustable headrests for added comfort and safety.
She leaned her head back against the headrest and closed her eyes for a moment.
Proper use of the headrest can help prevent whiplash injuries during an accident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head support - a device or cushion to support the head

Etymology

Formed from 'head' + 'rest', meaning a support for the head.

2. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdˌrɛst / (AmE), / ˈhɛd.rɛst / (BrE)
A support attached to a seat or chair, designed to rest the head and provide comfort or protection.
support for the head on a seat or chair

Examples

I adjusted the headrest in my car to make the drive more comfortable.
The dentist’s chair had a soft headrest to help patients relax during treatment.
For long flights, a good headrest can make a big difference in comfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head support - a device or part providing support for the head

Etymology

Formed from 'head' + 'rest' (support for the head).
