brostep

1. NOUN

/ ˈbroʊ.stɛp / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊ.stɛp / (BrE)
a subgenre of electronic dance music characterized by heavy bass drops, aggressive rhythms, and intense sound design, often associated with dubstep's evolution in North America
electronic dance music subgenre with heavy bass
slang

Examples

Many clubs now feature DJs who specialize in brostep sets.
She discovered brostep through online music forums and instantly loved the heavy bass sound.
Brostep rose to mainstream popularity in the early 2010s, especially among young festival-goers.

Synset Examples

His playlist is full of the latest brostep hits.
The party was filled with brostep music all night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dubstep - a genre of electronic dance music with syncopated rhythms and heavy bass

Etymology

Blend of 'bro' (slang for a young man) and 'dubstep' (electronic music genre).
