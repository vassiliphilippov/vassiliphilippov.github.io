Trilisa odoratissima

1. NOUN

/ trɪˈlaɪsə oʊdəˌrætɪˈsɪmə / (AmE), / trɪˈlaɪsə əʊdəˌrætɪˈsɪmə / (BrE)
A perennial plant native to the southeastern United States, known for its vanilla-scented leaves.
