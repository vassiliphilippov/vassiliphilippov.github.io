calorific

1. ADJ

/ ˌkæl.əˈrɪf.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌkæləˈrɪfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the production of heat; generating or capable of generating heat.
generating or relating to heat

Examples

The engineer calculated the calorific value of each type of fuel.
This food has a high calorific content, so it provides a lot of energy.
Coal is often preferred for its strong calorific properties in industrial applications.

Synset Examples

the calorific properties of fuels

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermal - relating to heat

Etymology

From Latin 'calorificus', from 'calor' (heat) + 'facere' (to make).
