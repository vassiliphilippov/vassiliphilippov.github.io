Kirkuk

1. PROPN

/ kɜrˈkuk / (AmE), / kɜːˈkuːk / (BrE)
a city in northeastern Iraq, known for its rich oilfields and strategic pipelines to the Mediterranean.
city in northeastern Iraq

Examples

Oil production has made Kirkuk one of the most important cities in Iraq.
The pipeline from Kirkuk to the Mediterranean has played a key role in regional trade.
Many families in Kirkuk have ties to both Kurdish and Arab communities.
