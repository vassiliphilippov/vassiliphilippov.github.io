isaw

1. NOUN

/ iˈsaʊ / (AmE), / iˈsaʊ / (BrE)
A Filipino street food made from skewered and barbecued pig or chicken intestines, usually grilled over charcoal.
Filipino street food: grilled animal intestines

Examples

Many people enjoy eating isaw from street vendors in Manila.
The market was filled with the aroma of grilled isaw and other delicacies.
He tried isaw for the first time and was surprised by its flavor.

Etymology

From Filipino 'isaw', referring to intestines used as food.
