unobstructed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnəbˈstrʌktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəbˈstrʌktɪd / (BrE)
not blocked or hindered; free from any impediment or obstruction.
not blocked or hindered

Examples

We had an unobstructed view of the mountains from our hotel room.
The path was unobstructed, making it easy for the runners to keep a steady pace.
Light streamed through the unobstructed window into the quiet library.

Synset Examples

an unobstructed view

Related Terms

  • Antonym: blocked - obstructed or stopped up
  • Synonym: clear - free from obstruction or unwanted material

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'obstructed', from Latin 'obstruere' (to block up).
