eight-membered

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪt ˌmɛmbɚd / (AmE), / ˈeɪt ˌmɛmbəd / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) having a ring structure consisting of eight atoms or groups.
having a ring of eight atoms (chemistry)

Examples

The compound contains an eight-membered ring which is rare in natural products.
Researchers synthesized an eight-membered lactone as part of the experiment.
The stability of eight-membered rings differs significantly from that of six-membered rings.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: six-membered - having a ring of six atoms (chemistry)
  • Degree Higher: ten-membered - having a ring of ten atoms (chemistry)
