pinko

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪŋkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋkəʊ / (BrE)
(informal, often derogatory) A person with mildly leftist or socialist political views.
person with mildly leftist political views
informal slang american

Examples

Some critics called the new mayor a pinko because of her support for public healthcare.
Back in the 1950s, being labeled a pinko could ruin your career in politics.
His uncle often rants about so-called pinkos in government.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leftist - person with left-wing political views
  • Antonym: right-winger - person with conservative or right-wing political views

Etymology

From the color 'pink' (suggesting a weak version of 'red', the color associated with communism), plus '-o' as a slang suffix; popularized in 20th-century American politics.
