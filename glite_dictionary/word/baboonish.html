baboonish

1. ADJ

/ bəˈbuːnɪʃ / (AmE), / bəˈbuːnɪʃ / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of a baboon.
resembling a baboon

Examples

His baboonish features gave him a wild, untamed look.
She made a baboonish face at her friend to make her laugh.
The statues had a strangely baboonish appearance that unsettled visitors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baboon - a large Old World monkey

Etymology

From 'baboon' + '-ish', with '-ish' forming adjectives meaning 'like' or 'resembling'.
