nearer

1. ADV

/ ˈnɪrər / (AmE), / ˈnɪərə / (BrE)
at or to a shorter distance in space or time; more close or almost at a point than before, comparative of 'near'.
comparative of 'near', at a shorter distance

Examples

She moved nearer to the window to get a better view.
The deadline is drawing nearer every day.
As we approached the mountain, it seemed much nearer than before.

Synset Examples

come closer, my dear!
they drew nearer
getting nearer to the true explanation

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: near - at or to a short distance
  • Degree Higher: nearest - superlative of 'near', at the smallest distance
