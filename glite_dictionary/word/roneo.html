Roneo

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊnioʊ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊniəʊ / (BrE)
a rotary duplicating machine that presses ink through a stencil, formerly common in offices and schools
rotary duplicating machine (stencil-based)

Examples

The school secretary produced copies of the newsletter using the Roneo.
Old examination papers were often duplicated with a Roneo machine before photocopiers became widespread.
He learned to operate the Roneo as part of his office duties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mimeograph - a duplicating machine using a stencil, similar to Roneo

Etymology

Trade name, from Roneo Ltd, originally Register Once Ltd, a British office machinery company (early 20th century).
