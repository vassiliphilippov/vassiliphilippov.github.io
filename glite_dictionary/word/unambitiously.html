unambitiously

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.æmˈbɪʃ.əs.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.æmˈbɪʃ.əs.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of ambition or a desire to achieve more.
in a manner lacking ambition

Examples

Despite his talent, Mark approached his career unambitiously and avoided taking on challenging projects.
The company expanded unambitiously, preferring steady progress over rapid growth.
She lived unambitiously, content with a simple, predictable routine.

Synset Examples

he does his job, but he works unambitiously

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ambitiously - in an ambitious manner

Etymology

Formed from 'unambitious' (prefix 'un-' + 'ambitious') + '-ly'.
