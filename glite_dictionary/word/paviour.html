paviour

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪviər / (AmE), / ˈpeɪvɪə / (BrE)
a machine used for laying pavement or paving surfaces
machine for laying pavement

Examples

The city invested in a new paviour to speed up road construction.
Workers operated a large paviour to lay fresh asphalt on the highway.
Using a modern paviour reduces the time needed to finish paving projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paver - machine that lays paving material

Etymology

From earlier sense of 'pavior', meaning one who paves, later extended to paving machines.
