Xanax

1. NOUN

/ ˈzænˌæks / (AmE), / ˈzæn.æks / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for alprazolam, a prescription drug of the benzodiazepine class used to treat anxiety and panic disorders.
brand of anti-anxiety medication
medical

Examples

Her doctor prescribed Xanax to help manage her anxiety attacks.
After taking Xanax, he felt noticeably calmer within an hour.
It is important not to mix Xanax with alcohol due to potential side effects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alprazolam - the generic name for Xanax, a medication for anxiety
