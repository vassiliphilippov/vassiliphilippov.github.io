cardiovascular

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑːrdioʊˈvæskjələr / (AmE), / ˌkɑːdiəʊˈvæskjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to the heart and blood vessels
of the heart and blood vessels
medical

Examples

Regular exercise can improve cardiovascular health and reduce the risk of heart disease.
Smoking has many negative effects on the cardiovascular system.
Doctors recommend cardiovascular training to help maintain healthy blood pressure.

Synset Examples

cardiovascular conditioning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circulatory - relating to the circulation of the blood or lymph
  • Antonym: respiratory - relating to breathing or the organs of respiration

Etymology

From Latin 'cardia' (heart) and 'vasculum' (small vessel).
