Ayr

1. PROPN

/ ɛr / (AmE), / ɛə / (BrE)
a town and port in southwestern Scotland, situated on the Firth of Clyde
port town in southwestern Scotland

Examples

Ayr is known for its picturesque seafront and historic buildings.
She traveled from Glasgow to Ayr by train for the day.
The annual horse races in Ayr attract visitors from across Scotland.

Etymology

From the River Ayr, possibly of pre-Celtic origin.
