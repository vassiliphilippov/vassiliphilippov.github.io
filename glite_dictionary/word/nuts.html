nuts

1. ADJ

/ nʌts / (AmE), / nʌts / (BrE)
(informal, slang) Crazy or insane; mentally unstable.
crazy; insane (informal)
informal slang

Examples

Everyone thought his plan was nuts, but he proved them wrong.
I must be nuts to agree to such a risky mission.
After working 18-hour days, she felt she was going nuts.

Synset Examples

it used to drive my husband balmy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged, insane
  • Antonym: sane - of sound mind, not mentally ill

Etymology

Originated as American slang in the early 20th century; likely influenced by 'nut' as a slang term for the head or brain.
