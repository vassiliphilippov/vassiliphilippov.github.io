apportioned

1. ADJ

/ əˈpɔɹʃənd / (AmE), / əˈpɔːʃənd / (BrE)
divided and given out in portions, especially according to a plan or formula
divided and distributed in parts

Examples

The annual budget is carefully apportioned among the different departments.
Seats in the assembly are apportioned according to population size.
Profits from the project were apportioned equally between the partners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: allocated - set apart or distributed for a particular purpose
  • Synonym: divided - separated into parts
  • Antonym: collected - gathered together, not given out

Etymology

Past participle of 'apportion', from Old French 'apportioner', from Latin 'ad-' (to) + 'portionem' (a part, share).
