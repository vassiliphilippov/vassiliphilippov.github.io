tidily

1. ADV

/ ˈtaɪdɪli / (AmE), / ˈtaɪdɪli / (BrE)
In a neat, orderly, or well-organized manner.
in a neat or orderly way

Examples

She arranged the books tidily on the shelf before leaving.
He always dresses tidily for important meetings.
The kitchen was tidily cleaned after the party.

Synset Examples

the door gave access to a tidily furnished sitting-room where chintz and oak predominated

Related Terms

  • Antonym: untidily - in a messy or disorderly manner
  • Synonym: neatly - in a neat or efficient manner

Etymology

From "tidy" + "-ly," forming the adverb from the adjective.
