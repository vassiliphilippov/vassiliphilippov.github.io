evergreen

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛvərˌɡrin / (AmE), / ˈɛvəɡriːn / (BrE)
a plant that retains green leaves throughout the year, rather than losing them seasonally
plant that stays green all year

Examples

The forest was filled with towering evergreen trees that provided shade even in winter.
We planted an evergreen in the yard so it would stay lush all year round.
Many people choose evergreen shrubs for their gardens to enjoy greenery during cold months.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: deciduous - plant that sheds its leaves annually

Etymology

From ever (always) + green, referring to lasting green foliage.

2. ADJ

/ ˈɛvɚˌɡrin / (AmE), / ˈɛvəˌɡriːn / (BrE)
(of a plant) retaining green leaves throughout the year, not shedding leaves seasonally.
retaining leaves year-round (plants)

Examples

The evergreen pine trees looked vibrant even during the winter months.
Unlike many deciduous varieties, evergreen shrubs provide privacy and color all year long.
We planted a row of evergreen bushes to keep the garden looking lively through every season.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: deciduous - (of plants) shedding leaves annually

Etymology

From ever (always) + green, referring to the year-round foliage.
