supraocular

1. ADJ

/ suːprəˈɑːkjələr / (AmE), / suːprəˈɒkjʊlə / (BrE)
situated or occurring above the eye or eye socket, especially referring to anatomical features
located above the eye socket

Examples

The lizard is distinguished by a supraocular scale above each eye.
Researchers observed a prominent supraocular ridge on the bird's skull.
Certain snakes have supraocular plates that help protect their eyes.

Etymology

From Latin 'supra' (above) + 'ocular' (of the eye).
