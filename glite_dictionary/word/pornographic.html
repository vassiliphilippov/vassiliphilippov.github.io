pornographic

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɔrnəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɔːnəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
intended to arouse sexual desire, especially by explicit depiction of sexual activity.
sexually explicit; intended to arouse sexual desire

Examples

The museum faced criticism for displaying pornographic artwork in a public space.
He argued that the novel contained pornographic passages rather than genuine literary content.
Some countries strictly regulate pornographic magazines and online content.

Synset Examples

pornographic films and magazines
adult movies

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: erotic - sexually arousing but usually less explicit than 'pornographic'

Etymology

From Greek 'pornographos' (writing about prostitutes), via French 'pornographique'.
