typographical

1. ADJ

/ ˌtaɪpəˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌtɪpəˈɡræfɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to typography or the style, layout, or appearance of printed matter.
relating to typography

Examples

The designer paid close attention to every typographical detail in the brochure.
A simple typographical mistake completely changed the meaning of the sentence.
Her job involves correcting typographical errors before the book goes to print.

Synset Examples

the typographic art
a typographical error

Related Terms

  • Synonym: typographic - relating to typography or printed appearance; variant of 'typographical'

Etymology

From Greek typos (impression, form) + -graphia (writing), via French typographique.
