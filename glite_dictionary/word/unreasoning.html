unreasoning

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈriːzənɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈriːzənɪŋ / (BrE)
(of emotion or behavior) not based on reason, evidence, or logical thinking; irrational.
irrational; not based on reason

Examples

Her unreasoning fear of the dark made it hard for her to sleep alone.
The crowd was swept by an unreasoning panic during the emergency.
He showed an unreasoning loyalty to his boss, regardless of the facts.

Synset Examples

blind hatred
blind faith
unreasoning panic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irrational - not logical or reasonable
  • Antonym: reasonable - based on or using good sense and logic

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'reasoning' (thinking logically or rationally).
