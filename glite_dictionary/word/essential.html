essential

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈsɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ɪˈsɛnʃl / (BrE)
(of oils or substances) containing or relating to the concentrated essence of a plant, often as extracted for fragrance or flavor.
relating to plant essence or concentrated extract

Examples

Lavender essential oil is widely used in aromatherapy for its calming effects.
The chef added a drop of orange essential oil to enhance the dessert's flavor.
Many perfumes are made with essential oils distilled from flowers and herbs.

Synset Examples

essential oil

Etymology

From Latin 'essentia' (being, essence).
