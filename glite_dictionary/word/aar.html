Aar

1. PROPN

/ ɑr / (AmE), / ɑː / (BrE)
a river in north central Switzerland that flows northeast into the Rhine
Swiss river flowing into the Rhine

Examples

The Aar flows through several Swiss towns before joining the Rhine.
Many tourists enjoy hiking along the Aar in the summer.
The city of Bern is situated on a peninsula formed by a bend in the Aar.

Etymology

From Swiss German 'Aare', the native name of the river.
