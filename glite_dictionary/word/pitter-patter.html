pitter-patter

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪtərˌpætər / (AmE), / ˈpɪtəˌpætə / (BrE)
A series of rapid, light tapping sounds, such as the sound made by raindrops or small footsteps.
rapid, light tapping sounds

Examples

The pitter-patter of rain on the window soothed her to sleep.
He smiled when he heard the pitter-patter of his dog's paws racing across the floor.
The pitter-patter of children running through the hallway filled the house with joy.

Synset Examples

she missed the pitter-patter of little feet around the house

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tap - a light, quick sound made by a slight force

2. ADV

/ ˈpɪt̬ɚˌpæt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɪtəˌpætə / (BrE)
(adverb) With a light, rhythmic, rapid succession of beats or taps, like falling raindrops or a quickened heartbeat.
with a light, rhythmic, rapid beat

Examples

The rain fell pitter-patter on the rooftops all night long.
She tiptoed pitter-patter down the hallway to avoid waking anyone.
He could feel his heart go pitter-patter as he waited for the results.

Synset Examples

his heart went pit-a-pat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pit-a-pat - with a light, quick beating sound

3. ADV

/ ˈpɪtərˌpætər / (AmE), / ˈpɪtəˌpætə / (BrE)
With a quick, light, and repeated sound, especially of small or hurried footsteps or raindrops.
with a rapid, light repetitive sound

Examples

The toddler's feet made a gentle pitter-patter as she ran across the wooden floor.
Rain began to pitter-patter on the windowpane late in the evening.
I woke up to the soothing pitter-patter of our cat's paws moving through the living room.

Synset Examples

he came running pit-a-pat down the hall

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pit-a-pat - with a rapid, light, rhythmic sound, especially of footsteps or heartbeat

Etymology

Imitative of the sound produced by quick, light steps or drops.

to make light, rapid, and repeated sounds, often resembling gentle raindrops or quick footsteps
make light repeated sounds

Examples

Raindrops began to pitter-patter softly against the windowpane as evening fell.
I could hear her tiny feet pitter-pattering across the wooden floor upstairs.
The squirrel’s paws pitter-pattered through the leaves as it searched for acorns.

Related Terms
