4. NOUN

/ wɛst / (AmE), / wɛst / (BrE)
the cardinal compass point corresponding to 270 degrees on a compass, indicating west
cardinal compass point; west

Examples

The storm was approaching from the W and moving rapidly eastward.
Sailors adjusted their course to head directly into the W.
If you look at the weather map, the W is clearly marked near the coast.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: E - the cardinal compass point at 90 degrees (east)
  • Antonym: N - the cardinal compass point at 0/360 degrees (north)
  • Antonym: S - the cardinal compass point at 180 degrees (south)

From the first letter of 'west.'
