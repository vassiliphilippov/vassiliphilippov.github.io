shipside

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪp.saɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃɪp.saɪd / (BrE)
the edge or part of a wharf adjacent to a ship for loading or unloading
edge of wharf next to ship

Examples

Cargo was transferred directly from the warehouse to the shipside for loading.
Security checks were performed at the shipside before the crew could board.
Workers waited at the shipside as the vessel docked early in the morning.
