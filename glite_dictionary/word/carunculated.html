carunculated

1. ADJ

/ kəˈrʌŋkjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / kəˈrʌŋkjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
Having a caruncle; possessing a small, fleshy protuberance, often found on animals.
having a caruncle

Examples

Many vulture species are carunculated, displaying prominent fleshy growths on their heads.
The bird's carunculated neck helped distinguish it from similar species.
Some reptiles exhibit carunculated skin as a form of ornamentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caruncle - a small, fleshy growth, especially as found in animals

Etymology

From Latin 'caruncula' (small piece of flesh) + '-ated' (having).
