dissatisfied

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈsætɪsˌfaɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈsætɪsfaɪd / (BrE)
not pleased or content with something; feeling that expectations or desires have not been met.
not pleased or content

Examples

She was dissatisfied with the service at the restaurant and decided not to leave a tip.
Many employees remained dissatisfied despite recent changes in company policy.
His dissatisfied expression made it clear that he expected more.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: satisfied - pleased or content with what has been received
  • Antonym: content - feeling satisfied and at ease
  • Synonym: discontented - not happy or satisfied with something

Etymology

From prefix dis- (expressing reversal) + 'satisfied', from Latin satisfacere (to satisfy).
