achievability

1. NOUN

/ əˌtʃiː.vəˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / əˌtʃiː.vəˈbɪl.ə.ti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being possible to achieve or accomplish
the state of being achievable

Examples

The achievability of our project goals must be assessed carefully before we begin.
She questioned the achievability of such ambitious plans within the limited time frame.
We debated the achievability of reaching a carbon-neutral status by 2050.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: achievable - able to be achieved

Etymology

From 'achievable' + '-ity', indicating state or condition.
