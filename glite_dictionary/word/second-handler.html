second-handler

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd ˌhændər / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd ˌhændə / (BrE)
an item that has been previously owned or used by someone else
item previously owned or used

Examples

The shop is known for rare second-handlers that collectors treasure.
She decorated her apartment with stylish second-handlers she found at the market.
I've always preferred second-handlers to brand new items for their history and charm.

Synset Examples

She prefers second-handers for their unique character.
The bookstore has a great selection of second-handers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: second-hand - previously owned or used, especially goods

Etymology

From 'second-hand' (previously owned) + '-er' noun-forming suffix.
