mislabeled

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɪsˈleɪbəld / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈleɪbəld / (BrE)
branded or labeled incorrectly or falsely, often in violation of statutory requirements
branded or labeled incorrectly

Examples

The shipment was seized because the products were mislabeled as organic.
She returned the medication after discovering it was mislabeled with the wrong dosage.
Importers face penalties if their goods are mislabeled under trade regulations.

Synset Examples

confiscated the misbranded drugs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: misbranded - branded or labeled falsely, especially in a legal context

Etymology

Formed from 'mis-' (wrong, badly) and 'labeled'; from label (via Old French label) + prefix mis-.
