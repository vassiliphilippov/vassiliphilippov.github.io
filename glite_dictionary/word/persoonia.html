Persoonia

1. NOUN

/ pərˈsuːniə / (AmE), / pəˈsɔːniə / (BrE)
a genus of Australian shrubs or small trees in the heath family, known as geebungs, with yellow flowers and edible fruits
genus of Australian shrubs (geebungs)

Examples

Several species of Persoonia are prized for their unusual yellow flowers in Australian gardens.
The fruit of the Persoonia is sometimes collected and eaten by Indigenous Australians.
Botanists conducted fieldwork on Persoonia populations in New South Wales to study plant diversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geebung - common name for species of Persoonia

Etymology

Named after Dutch botanist Christiaan Hendrik Persoon (1761–1836).
