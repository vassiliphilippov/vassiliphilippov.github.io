Morone interrupta

1. NOUN

/ məˈroʊni ˌɪntəˈrʌptə / (AmE), / məˈrəʊni ˌɪntəˈrʌptə / (BrE)
A North American freshwater bass that closely resembles the larger marine striped bass; commonly known as white bass.
North American freshwater bass species

Etymology

From Latin: 'Morone' is a genus name; 'interrupta' means 'interrupted', referring to physical characteristics.
