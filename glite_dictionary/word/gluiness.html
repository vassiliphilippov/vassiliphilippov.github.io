gluiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡluːi.nɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡluːɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being sticky or glue-like; stickiness or adhesiveness.
the state of being sticky

Examples

The gluiness of the syrup made it difficult to clean off the table.
He disliked the gluiness of the adhesive after removing the bandage.
Warm weather increased the gluiness of the candy, making it hard to unwrap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stickiness - the quality of being sticky

Etymology

Formed from 'gluey' (sticky like glue) + '-ness' (noun suffix).
