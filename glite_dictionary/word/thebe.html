thebe

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbi / (AmE), / ˈteɪbi / (BrE)
a monetary unit of Botswana, equal to one hundredth of a pula.
a unit of currency in Botswana

Examples

I exchanged my dollars for thebe and pula at the bank in Gaborone.
The item costs 50 thebe at the local market.
Tourists often collect a few thebe coins as souvenirs from Botswana.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: pula - the main unit of currency in Botswana

Etymology

From Tswana thebé (shield), reflecting protection and value.
