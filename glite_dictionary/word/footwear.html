footwear

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌweə / (BrE)
things that people wear on their feet, such as shoes, boots, and sandals.
items worn on the feet

Examples

The shop offers a wide selection of footwear for all occasions.
He forgot to bring appropriate footwear for hiking in the mountains.
Proper footwear is required to enter the laboratory for safety reasons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoes - a covering for the foot, typically made of leather, with a sturdy sole and not reaching above the ankle

2. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌwɛə / (BrE)
things such as shoes, boots, or sandals that are worn on the feet.
covering for the feet, such as shoes or boots

Examples

It's important to choose the right footwear for hiking in rough terrain.
The museum has an exhibit on traditional footwear from around the world.
His job requires him to wear protective footwear at all times.

Etymology

Formed from 'foot' + 'wear'.
