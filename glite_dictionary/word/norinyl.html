Norinyl

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔːrɪˌnɪl / (AmE), / ˈnɔːrɪˌnɪl / (BrE)
A trade name for an oral contraceptive containing norethindrone and mestranol, used to prevent pregnancy.
a brand of oral contraceptive
medical

Examples

Her doctor prescribed Norinyl to help regulate her menstrual cycles.
Many women rely on Norinyl as their chosen method of birth control.
If you miss a dose of Norinyl, consult your healthcare provider for advice.
