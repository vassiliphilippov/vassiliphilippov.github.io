thirty-first

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɜːrti ˈfɜːrst / (AmE), / ˌθɜːti ˈfɜːst / (BrE)
the ordinal number corresponding to thirty-one; coming after thirtieth in order or position
ordinal number for 31st

Examples

She was the thirty-first person to arrive at the party.
The thirty-first of December is celebrated as New Year's Eve.
His name appeared thirty-first on the list of participants.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirtieth - ordinal number for 30th; coming before thirty-first
  • Degree Higher: thirty-second - ordinal number for 32nd; coming after thirty-first
