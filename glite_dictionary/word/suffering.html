suffering

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌfərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌfərɪŋ / (BrE)
the state of experiencing intense physical or mental pain
acute pain or distress

Examples

The refugees endured unimaginable suffering during the war.
Her illness brought years of suffering and hardship to the family.
He dedicated his life to easing the suffering of others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agony - extreme physical or mental pain
  • Antonym: comfort - freedom from pain or distress

Etymology

From Old French *sufferir* (to endure), from Latin *sufferre* (to bear, endure).
