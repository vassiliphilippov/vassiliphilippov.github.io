endearingly

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈdɪrɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪə.rɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a manner that inspires affection, fondness, or love.
in a lovable or charming way

Examples

She smiled endearingly at her grandmother, melting everyone's heart in the room.
The puppy tilted its head endearingly every time it heard a strange sound.
He endearingly called her by a nickname only the two of them understood.

Synset Examples

the toddler behaved adorably

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adorably - in a cute or charming way
  • Synonym: charmingly - in a pleasing or delightful manner

Etymology

From "endearing" + "-ly"; "endearing" from "endear" (to make dear).
