rattler

1. NOUN

/ ˈræt.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈræt.lə / (BrE)
A rattlesnake, a venomous pit viper with horny segments at the end of its tail that make a rattling sound when shaken.
a type of rattlesnake

Examples

The hiker stepped back quickly when he saw a rattler coiled near the trail.
Late at night, we could hear the rattler shaking its tail from behind the rocks.
Farmers in the region are careful to avoid disturbing a rattler while working in the fields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rattlesnake - a venomous American snake with a rattling tail
