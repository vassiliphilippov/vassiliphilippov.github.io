anthropical

1. ADJ

/ ænˈθrɑːpɪkəl / (AmE), / ænˈθrɒpɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to humankind or to the period of humankind's existence
relating to humankind or its era

Examples

The anthropical age refers to the era marked by human activity.
Some scientists debate when the anthropical period truly began.
Debates about climate change often focus on anthropical impact on the environment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anthropic - relating to human beings

Etymology

From Greek anthrōpikos, from anthrōpos (human, man).
