uprightly

1. ADV

/ ˈʌpˌraɪtli / (AmE), / ˈʌp.raɪt.li / (BrE)
In an upright or vertical position.
in a vertical or upright position

Examples

The bookshelf stood uprightly in the corner, despite the uneven floor.
She balanced the vase uprightly on the narrow shelf, hoping it wouldn’t fall.
Please sit uprightly during the interview to make a good impression.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vertically - in a direction or position at right angles to the horizon
  • Antonym: horizontally - in a flat or level position parallel to the horizon

2. ADV

/ ˈʌpˌraɪtli / (AmE), / ˈʌp.raɪt.li / (BrE)
In an honest or honorable way; with integrity.
in an honest or honorable way

Examples

The judge conducted himself uprightly throughout the entire trial.
She believed that living uprightly was more important than material success.
Despite the pressure to cheat, he acted uprightly and told the truth.

Synset Examples

he acted honorably

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honorably - in an honorable way
  • Antonym: dishonestly - in a dishonest way

Etymology

Formed from the adjective "upright" + adverbial suffix "-ly".
