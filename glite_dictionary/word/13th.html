13th

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɜrˌtinθ / (AmE), / ˈθɜːˌtiːnθ / (BrE)
coming next after the twelfth in a sequence; ordinal form of thirteen
ordinal number: after twelfth

Examples

He finished in 13th place out of thirty competitors.
Their anniversary will be on the 13th of June.
The company occupies the 13th floor of the building.
