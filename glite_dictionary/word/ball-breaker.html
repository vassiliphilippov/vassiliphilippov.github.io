ball-breaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔlˌbreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlˌbreɪkə / (BrE)
a job or situation that is extremely demanding, arduous, or punishing
demanding or punishing job or situation
informal vulgar

Examples

Finishing the project by tomorrow is going to be a real ball-breaker.
Training for the marathon in this heat is a total ball-breaker.
That exam was a ball-breaker; hardly anyone finished on time.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbɔlˌbreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlˌbreɪkə / (BrE)
(slang) A woman who is excessively demanding or intimidating, often perceived as undermining men's confidence or authority.
demanding/intimidating woman (slang)
slang offensive

Examples

Some colleagues think the new manager is a total ball-breaker, but I just see her as assertive.
He complained that his ex-wife was a ball-breaker who never gave him a moment's peace.
The movie villain is portrayed as a ruthless ball-breaker who terrifies her staff.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: battle-axe - a forceful or aggressive woman
  • Antonym: shrinking violet - an extremely shy or submissive person

Etymology

From slang use of 'break balls' meaning to cause distress or difficulty, combined with 'ball' as a vulgar slang for testicle.
