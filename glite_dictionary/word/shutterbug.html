shutterbug

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃʌt̬ɚˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈʃʌtəbʌɡ / (BrE)
an enthusiastic amateur photographer; someone who loves taking photographs
photography enthusiast
informal

Examples

Ever since he bought his first camera, David has become quite the shutterbug.
Do you have any tips for a beginner shutterbug like me?
Every trip turns into a photo session with my shutterbug friend around.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photographer - a person who takes photographs

Etymology

From 'shutter' (the part of a camera) + 'bug' (enthusiast or craze).
