to counterstrike

1. VERB

/ ˈkaʊntərˌstraɪk / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəˌstraɪk / (BrE)
to attack in response to an attack; to make a counterattack
to make a counterattack

Examples

The army decided to counterstrike after suffering heavy losses in the first assault.
When provoked on the field, she waited for the right moment to counterstrike.
After taking a defensive position, they planned to counterstrike at dawn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: counterattack - to attack in response to an attack

Etymology

From the prefix 'counter-' (in opposition to) + 'strike' (to attack).
