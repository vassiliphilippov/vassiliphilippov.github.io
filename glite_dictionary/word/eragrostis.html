Eragrostis

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛrəˈɡrɑːstɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛrəˈɡrɒstɪs / (BrE)
A genus of annual or perennial grasses found in tropical and subtropical regions, commonly known as lovegrass.
genus of tropical and subtropical grasses

Examples

Several species of Eragrostis are used for forage and soil conservation in tropical regions.
The delicate flowers of Eragrostis sway gently in the summer breeze.
Researchers are studying Eragrostis as a potential crop for arid environments.

Etymology

From Greek 'erā' (love) + 'grostis' (grass).
