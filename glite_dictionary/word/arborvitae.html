arborvitae

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːrbərˈvaɪti / (AmE), / ˌɑːbəˈvaɪti / (BrE)
Any of several evergreen coniferous trees or shrubs of the genera Thuja and Thujopsis, native to Asia and North America, often grown as ornamental plants.
evergreen coniferous tree of Thuja/Thujopsis

Examples

The arborvitae hedge provided privacy while adding greenery to their backyard.
Several species of arborvitae are popular choices for landscaping because of their dense foliage.
We planted an arborvitae near the front entrance to enhance the garden's appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thuja - a genus of evergreen coniferous trees; member of the cypress family

Etymology

From New Latin arborvitae, meaning 'tree of life', from Latin arbor (tree) + vitae (of life).
