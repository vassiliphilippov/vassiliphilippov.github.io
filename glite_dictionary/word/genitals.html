genitals

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛnɪtlz / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnɪtəlz / (BrE)
the external sexual organs, especially those of humans
external sexual organs

Examples

The doctor examined the patient's genitals for any signs of infection.
Some animals display their genitals as a mating signal.
Always ensure privacy and respect when discussing or examining genitals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genitalia - the sexual organs, especially the external ones

Etymology

From Latin genitalis (relating to birth or reproduction).
