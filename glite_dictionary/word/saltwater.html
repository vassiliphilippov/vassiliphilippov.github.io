saltwater

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔltˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈsɔːltˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
water that contains a significant amount of salt, especially sea water
water containing salt, usually seawater

Examples

The aquarium uses a special tank to hold saltwater fish.
After swimming in the saltwater, I always take a shower to rinse off the salt.
Desalination plants turn saltwater into clean drinking water.

Synset Examples

the water in the ocean is all saltwater

Related Terms

  • Antonym: freshwater - water that does not contain a significant amount of salt
