1960s

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪnˌtiːn ˈsɪkstiz / (AmE), / ˌnaɪnˈtiːn ˈsɪkstiz / (BrE)

the decade from 1960 to 1969

Fashion in the 1960s was marked by bold colors and unique styles.
