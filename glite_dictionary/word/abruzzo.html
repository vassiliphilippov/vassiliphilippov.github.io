Abruzzo

1. PROPN

/ əˈbrutsoʊ / (AmE), / əˈbrʊtsəʊ / (BrE)
a mountainous region of central Italy on the Adriatic Sea, known for its national parks, medieval towns, and coastline
region of central Italy

Examples

We spent a week hiking through Abruzzo and exploring its charming villages.
The cuisine of Abruzzo is famous for its rustic flavors and fresh ingredients.
Many tourists visit Abruzzo for its beautiful national parks and unspoiled landscapes.
