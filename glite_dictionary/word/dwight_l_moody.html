Dwight L. Moody

1. PROPN

/ dwaɪt ɛl ˈmuːdi / (AmE), / dwaɪt ɛl ˈmuːdi / (BrE)
(1837–1899) American evangelist and publisher who was a prominent figure in the revivalist movement of the late 19th century.
American evangelist and revivalist (1837–1899)

Examples

Dwight L. Moody was a leading figure in the American revivalist movement of the nineteenth century.
The teachings of Dwight L. Moody influenced many modern evangelical leaders.
Many schools and institutions were named after Dwight L. Moody in recognition of his work.
