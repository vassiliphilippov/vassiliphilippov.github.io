hectometer

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛk.təˌmiː.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɛk.təˌmiː.tə / (BrE)
a metric unit of length equal to 100 meters
unit of length (100 meters)

Examples

The race track measured exactly one hectometer in length.
A hectometer is equivalent to one hundred meters in the metric system.
Engineers sometimes use the hectometer to measure large distances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meter - the basic unit of length in the metric system

Etymology

From French 'hectomètre', from Greek 'hekaton' (hundred) + French 'mètre' (meter).
