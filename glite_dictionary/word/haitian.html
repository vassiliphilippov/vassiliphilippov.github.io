Haitian

1. ADJ

/ ˈheɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈheɪtɪən / (BrE)
of or relating to Haiti or its people or culture.
relating to Haiti or its people

Examples

The festival featured traditional Haitian music and dance.
Many Haitian artists have gained international recognition for their work.
She prepared a delicious Haitian meal for her guests.

Synset Examples

Haitian shantytowns

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Haitian - a person from Haiti
  • Degree Lower: Haiti - the country in the Caribbean

Etymology

From Haiti + -an, denoting origin or association.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03083079-a
Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ heɪˈʃən / (AmE), / heɪˈtɪən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Haiti.
person from Haiti

Examples

She met a Haitian at the conference who shared her interest in art.
Many Haitians have migrated to other countries in search of better opportunities.
The festival celebrated Haitian culture through music and dance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Haiti - a country in the Caribbean

Etymology

From Haiti, the country name, with English demonymic suffix -an.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09731844-n
Synset POS: n
