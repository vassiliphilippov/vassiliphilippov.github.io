beauteous

1. ADJ

/ ˈbjuːtiəs / (AmE), / ˈbjuːtɪəs / (BrE)
(poetic or literary) beautiful, especially to the eye; visually attractive.
poetic/literary word for beautiful
archaic

Examples

The princess stood in the garden, surrounded by beauteous roses in full bloom.
A beauteous landscape stretched before them, bathed in golden morning light.
He wrote a poem celebrating the beauteous wonders of nature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beautiful - pleasing the senses or mind aesthetically
  • Antonym: ugly - unpleasant or repulsive in appearance

Etymology

From Middle English, based on 'beauty' + -ous.
