gimcrackery

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪmˌkræk.ɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈɡɪmˌkræk.əri / (BrE)
Cheap, showy, and ornamental objects of little or no real value.
cheap ornamental objects

Examples

The shop was filled with gimcrackery meant to attract tourists.
She dusted the shelves cluttered with gimcrackery inherited from relatives.
Despite all the gimcrackery on display, nothing in that stall was truly valuable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bauble - a small, showy trinket or decoration
  • Synonym: junk - old or discarded objects of little value

Etymology

From 'gimcrack' (a showy but useless object) + '-ery'.
