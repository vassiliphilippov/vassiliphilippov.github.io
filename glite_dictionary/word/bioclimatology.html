bioclimatology

1. NOUN

/ ˌbaɪ.oʊˌklaɪ.məˈtɑl.ə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əʊˌklʌɪ.məˈtɒl.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of the effects of climate on living organisms, including plants, animals, and humans
study of climate effects on organisms
medical

Examples

Advances in bioclimatology have helped researchers understand how temperature changes affect disease patterns.
The conference brought together experts in bioclimatology from around the world.
Her doctoral thesis was focused on the role of bioclimatology in managing agricultural crops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: climatology - the science of climate and its phenomena

Etymology

From bio- (life) + climatology (study of climate)
