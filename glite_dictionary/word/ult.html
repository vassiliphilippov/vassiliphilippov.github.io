ult

1. ADJ

/ ʌlt / (AmE), / ʌlt / (BrE)
of or relating to the month preceding the present one (used chiefly in formal correspondence, now rare).
of last month (archaic, formal)
archaic

Examples

We received your payment on the 15th ult.
His reply to my letter dated the 30th ult has not yet arrived.
The invoice sent on the 22nd ult remains unpaid.

Synset Examples

your letter received on the 29th ult

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inst - of the present month (used in correspondence)
  • Antonym: prox - of the next month (used in correspondence)

Etymology

Abbreviation of Latin ultimo mense, meaning 'in the last month'.
