issuance

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪʃuəns / (AmE), / ˈɪʃuəns / (BrE)
the act of officially supplying or distributing something, especially in quantity or for official use
official act of supplying or distributing

Examples

The government announced the issuance of new ID cards nationwide starting next month.
Tim was responsible for the issuance of permits to the construction crews.
After the issuance of the revised safety guidelines, all employees needed to complete new training.

Synset Examples

a new issue of stamps
the last issue of penicillin was over a month ago

Related Terms

  • Synonym: issue - the act of supplying or distributing something officially

Etymology

From 'issue' + '-ance', formed in English.
