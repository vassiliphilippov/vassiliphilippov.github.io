xxiii

1. NUM

/ ɛks ɛks iː aɪ aɪ aɪ / (AmE), / ɛks ɛks ɪː ɪː ɪː / (BrE)
the Roman numeral representing the number 23
Roman numeral for 23

Examples

The chapter is labeled as xxiii in the old book.
She wrote the number xxiii using Roman numerals.
The inscription bore the date in Roman numerals: xxiii.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 23 - the number twenty-three
  • Degree Lower: xxii - the Roman numeral representing the number 22
  • Degree Higher: xxiv - the Roman numeral representing the number 24

Etymology

From Latin Roman numerals: X (10) + X (10) + III (3).
