ensiform

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛn.səˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈɛn.sɪ.fɔːm / (BrE)
Shaped like a sword blade; long, narrow, and pointed.
sword-shaped; long and narrow

Examples

The plant's ensiform leaves help distinguish it from other species.
She studied the fossil's ensiform structure under the microscope for further classification.
Botanists often describe iris blades as ensiform due to their sharp, elongated shape.

Synset Examples

the iris has an ensiform leaf

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sword-shaped - shaped like a sword

Etymology

From Latin 'ensis' (sword) + '-form' (shape).
