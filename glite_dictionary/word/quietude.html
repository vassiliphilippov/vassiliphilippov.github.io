quietude

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwaɪ.əˌtuːd / (AmE), / ˈkwaɪ.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
a state of calm and peacefulness; tranquillity
state of calm and peacefulness

Examples

She found quietude in the peaceful garden, far from the city noise.
Meditation often helps people achieve a sense of quietude in their busy lives.
After the storm, a profound quietude settled over the village.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tranquillity - a state of being calm and peaceful
  • Synonym: peacefulness - the state of being peaceful
  • Antonym: turmoil - a state of great disturbance or confusion

Etymology

From Latin "quietudo" (rest, calm), from "quietus" (quiet, at rest).
