Jerez de la Frontera

1. PROPN

/ həˈrɛz deɪ lɑ fronˈtɛrə / (AmE), / hɛˈrɛθ de la frɒnˈtɛrə / (BrE)
A city in southwestern Spain, renowned for the production of sherry.
city in Spain known for sherry

Examples

She visited Jerez de la Frontera to tour the famous sherry bodegas.
The cuisine of Jerez de la Frontera is influenced by Andalusian traditions.
Many world-class flamenco shows are held annually in Jerez de la Frontera.

Etymology

From Spanish, literally 'Jerez of the Frontier,' reflecting its historical border status.
