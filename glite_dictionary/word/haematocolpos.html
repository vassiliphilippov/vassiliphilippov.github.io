haematocolpos

1. NOUN

/ ˌhiːmətəˈkɑːlpəs / (AmE), / ˌhiːmətəˈkɒlpɒs / (BrE)
A medical condition in which menstrual blood accumulates in the vagina, usually due to an imperforate hymen.
accumulation of menstrual blood in the vagina
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with haematocolpos following an examination for abdominal pain.
Ultrasound imaging revealed a significant haematocolpos due to an imperforate hymen.
Prompt treatment of haematocolpos is essential to prevent complications.

Etymology

From Greek haima (blood) + kolpos (vagina).
