meatloaf

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːtˌloʊf / (AmE), / ˈmiːtˌləʊf / (BrE)
A dish consisting of ground meat formed into a loaf shape and baked, often with additional ingredients such as breadcrumbs, eggs, or seasonings.
baked loaf of ground meat

Examples

My grandmother makes delicious meatloaf every Sunday for dinner.
The restaurant serves their meatloaf with mashed potatoes and green beans.
Leftover meatloaf makes a great sandwich for lunch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meatball - small ball of ground meat, often cooked in sauce
