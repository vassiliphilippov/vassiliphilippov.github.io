sheepherder

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃiˌhɜrdər / (AmE), / ˈʃiːˌhɜːdə / (BrE)
a person who tends, herds, or manages sheep, especially on open land
person who herds sheep

Examples

The sheepherder guided his flock over the hillside every morning.
During the storm, the sheepherder worked late to keep the sheep safe.
We met a retired sheepherder who shared stories about life on the range.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shepherd - a person who takes care of sheep, especially as a job
