curvilinear

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɜrvəˈlɪniɚ / (AmE), / ˌkɜːvɪˈlɪniə / (BrE)
Having or consisting of curved lines; characterized by or following a curved line.
curved or consisting of curved lines

Examples

The architect designed a curvilinear structure that stood out among the city's rectangular buildings.
The path through the garden follows a gentle, curvilinear route.
Some artists favor curvilinear forms over straight lines to create a sense of movement and grace.

Synset Examples

curvilinear tracery
curvilinear motion

Related Terms

  • Antonym: linear - consisting of straight lines
  • Synonym: curved - having a smooth, rounded shape, not straight

Etymology

From Latin 'curvus' (curved) + 'linea' (line).
