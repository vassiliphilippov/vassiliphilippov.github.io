bichrome

1. ADJ

/ ˈbaɪkroʊm / (AmE), / ˈbʌɪkrəʊm / (BrE)
having two colors; made or decorated using only two colors.
having two colors

Examples

The artist created a striking bichrome painting using only red and black.
The vase features a bichrome design that highlights both elegance and simplicity.
The bichrome tiles in the hallway alternate between blue and white.

Synset Examples

a bicolor flower

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bicolor - having two colors

Etymology

From Greek bi- (two) and chrōma (color).
