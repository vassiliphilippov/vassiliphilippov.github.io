mouth-watering

1. ADJ

/ ˈmaʊθˌwɔtɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmaʊθˌwɔːtərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of food) having an appearance, smell, or taste that makes you want to eat it very much; extremely appetizing.
extremely appetizing (food)

Examples

The bakery’s mouth-watering pastries can be smelled from down the street.
She prepared a mouth-watering feast for her guests.
The aroma of mouth-watering barbecue filled the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appetizing - stimulating one's appetite, especially by the appearance or smell of food
  • Antonym: unappetizing - not appealing to the appetite
