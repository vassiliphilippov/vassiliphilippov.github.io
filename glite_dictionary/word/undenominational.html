undenominational

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɪˌnɑməˈneɪʃənl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃənl̩ / (BrE)
Not restricted or devoted to a particular religious denomination; not affiliated with or promoting any single denomination.
not affiliated with a specific denomination

Examples

The school offers undenominational religious education, focusing on general spiritual principles.
She prefers to attend an undenominational service that welcomes people of all faiths.
The committee encouraged undenominational discussions to ensure everyone's beliefs were respected.

Synset Examples

undenominational religious instruction

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nondenominational - not affiliated with any religious denomination

Etymology

Formed from un- (not) + denominational, itself from denomination.
