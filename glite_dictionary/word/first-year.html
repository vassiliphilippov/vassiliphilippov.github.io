first-year

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɝst ˌjɪr / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˌjɪə / (BrE)
(of a person) in the first year of an experience, such as high school, college, or a job.
in the first year of an experience

Examples

As a first-year student, she found college life both exciting and overwhelming.
The company offers a special orientation program for first-year employees.
All first-year players must attend the rookie training camp before the season begins.

Synset Examples

a freshman senator
freshman year in high school or college

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freshman - a student in the first year of high school or college (especially US)
