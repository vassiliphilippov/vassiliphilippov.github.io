percentage

1. NOUN

/ pərˈsɛntɪdʒ / (AmE), / pəˈsɛntɪdʒ / (BrE)
a proportion or share expressed as a fraction of 100
proportion per hundred

Examples

Only a small percentage of the population agreed with the new policy.
He calculated the percentage of students who passed the exam.
The company achieved a higher profit percentage compared to last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: percent - one part in every hundred

Etymology

From Latin 'per centum' (by the hundred) with the English suffix '-age'.
