slenderly

1. ADV

/ ˈslɛn.dɚ.li / (AmE), / ˈslɛn.də.li / (BrE)
in a way that is small in amount, degree, or extent; in a meager manner
in a meager or slight way

Examples

Funding for the project was slenderly provided by the local government.
The region is slenderly populated compared to major cities.
He is slenderly built, with long limbs and narrow shoulders.

Synset Examples

these voices are meagerly represented at the conference
the area is slenderly endowed with natural resources

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meagerly - in a small or insufficient amount; scantily

Etymology

From 'slender' (thin, slight) + '-ly' (forming adverbs from adjectives).

2. ADV

/ ˈslɛn.dɚ.li / (AmE), / ˈslɛn.də.li / (BrE)
In a slim or slender manner; in a way that is thin or gracefully narrow.
in a slim or slender manner

Examples

The model walked slenderly down the runway in her elegant dress.
The old tower rose slenderly above the trees, visible for miles around.
Her arms were slenderly built, giving her a delicate appearance.

Synset Examples

a slenderly built woman
slightly built

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slender - gracefully thin or slim (adjective)
