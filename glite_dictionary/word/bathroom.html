bathroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæθˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈbɑːθruːm / (BrE)
a room in a house or other building containing a bathtub or shower, usually also a washbasin and toilet
room with bath/shower and toilet

Examples

Please make sure to clean the bathroom before our guests arrive.
The hotel room has a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.
She went to the bathroom to wash her hands before dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restroom - a public room with toilets and sinks
  • Synonym: toilet - a room or compartment with a toilet
  • Antonym: kitchen - a room for preparing and cooking food

2. NOUN

/ ˈbæθ.ruːm / (AmE), / ˈbɑːθ.ruːm / (BrE)
A room containing a toilet and often a sink and a bath or shower, used for personal hygiene.
room with a toilet and usually a bath or shower

Examples

She hurried to the bathroom to wash her hands before dinner.
Every hotel room has its own private bathroom.
The public bathroom on the first floor is being renovated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restroom - a public room with toilets, mainly US usage
  • Synonym: toilet - a room or fixture for urination and defecation

Etymology

Formed from 'bath' + 'room', meaning a room for bathing.
