dispensary

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈspɛnsəri / (AmE), / dɪˈspɛnsəri / (BrE)
a place, often in a hospital or clinic, where medicines and medical supplies are prepared and given out to patients
place where medicines are dispensed

Examples

The hospital's new dispensary provides free medication to low-income patients.
After registering at the clinic, she was directed to the dispensary for her prescription.
Many rural areas rely on a local dispensary for essential drugs and basic healthcare.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin dispensarium, from Latin dispensare (to distribute).
