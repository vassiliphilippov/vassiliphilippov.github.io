dexter

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɛkstər / (AmE), / ˈdɛkstə / (BrE)
(in heraldry) on or relating to the right side of a shield or coat of arms (the left for an observer).
heraldic term for right side

Examples

The family crest displayed a dexter lion holding a sword.
In heraldic terms, the dexter side is to the shield bearer's right.
A red stripe appears on the dexter side of the emblem.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sinister - relating to the left side in heraldry

Etymology

From Latin 'dexter' meaning 'on the right side'.
