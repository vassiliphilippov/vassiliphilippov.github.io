unnotched

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈnɑtʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈnɒtʃt / (BrE)
not having any notches; smooth or uninterrupted by notches.
without notches

Examples

The carpenter selected an unnotched piece of wood for the visible surface.
Ensure the edges remain unnotched to maintain structural integrity.
An unnotched blade reduces stress concentrations during use.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: notched - having one or more notches

Etymology

From the prefix un- (not) + notched.
