uncommonness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈkɑmənnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɒmənnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being rare or not often encountered.
quality of being uncommon or rare

Examples

The uncommonness of that species makes it a prized find for birdwatchers.
He was fascinated by the uncommonness of ancient manuscripts in the collection.
The uncommonness of such kindness is what made it truly memorable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rarity - the state of being rare
  • Antonym: commonness - the state of being common or frequently encountered

Etymology

Formed from 'uncommon' + '-ness', indicating state or quality.
