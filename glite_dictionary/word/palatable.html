palatable

1. ADJ

/ ˈpæləˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈpalətəbəl / (BrE)
pleasant or acceptable to the taste; or acceptable or satisfactory to the mind or feelings.
pleasant-tasting or acceptable

Examples

The chef created a palatable dish using only a few fresh ingredients.
She found his proposal to be a palatable compromise given the circumstances.
The restaurant is known for its palatable desserts that appeal to all ages.

Synset Examples

palatable food
a palatable solution to the problem

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tasty - having a pleasant flavor
  • Antonym: unpalatable - not pleasant to the taste or mind

Etymology

From Latin 'palatum' (palate) + '-able'.
