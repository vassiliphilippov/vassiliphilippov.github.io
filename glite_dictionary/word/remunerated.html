remunerated

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈmjuːnəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈmjuːnərɛɪtɪd / (BrE)
receiving payment for work or services
receiving payment for work

Examples

All remunerated staff must submit their time sheets by Friday.
The position is remunerated and includes benefits such as paid leave and health insurance.
Volunteers are not remunerated for their service, but may receive other forms of recognition.

Synset Examples

salaried workers
a stipendiary magistrate

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unpaid - not receiving payment for work or services
  • Synonym: compensated - having received money in return for work or loss

Etymology

From Latin 'remuneratus', past participle of 'remunerari' (to reward).
