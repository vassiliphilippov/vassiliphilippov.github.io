Kinshasa

1. PROPN

/ kɪnˈʃɑsə / (AmE), / kɪnˈʃɑːsə / (BrE)
the capital city of the Democratic Republic of the Congo, located on the Congo River opposite Brazzaville
capital of the Democratic Republic of the Congo

Examples

The population of Kinshasa has grown rapidly over the past three decades.
She traveled from Kinshasa to Brazzaville by ferry across the Congo River.
Many international organizations have their regional offices in Kinshasa.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Brazzaville - the capital of the Republic of the Congo, across the river from Kinshasa

Etymology

Named after a village called Kinchassa (or Kinshassa) that existed before the city.
