repetitively

1. ADV

/ rɪˈpɛt̬.ə.tɪv.li / (AmE), / rɪˈpet.ɪ.tɪv.li / (BrE)
In a manner that involves repetition, especially frequent or monotonous repetition.
in a repetitive manner

Examples

The alarm sounded repetitively throughout the night, disturbing everyone's sleep.
She tapped her pen repetitively on the desk, making it hard to concentrate.
The instructions were given repetitively to ensure no one forgot any steps.

Synset Examples

this type of border display is used repetitively in advertising

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repeatedly - again and again; many times
