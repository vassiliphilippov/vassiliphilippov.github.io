empty-bellied

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛmpti ˌbɛlid / (AmE), / ˈɛmpti ˌbɛlid / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) needing food; hungry.
needing food; hungry

Examples

The aid workers were moved by the sight of so many empty-bellied children in the village.
After two days without food, he felt truly empty-bellied and weak.
The documentary showed a line of empty-bellied refugees waiting for a meal.

Synset Examples

after skipped lunch the men were empty by suppertime
empty-bellied children

Related Terms
