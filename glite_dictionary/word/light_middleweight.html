light middleweight

1. NOUN

/ laɪt ˈmɪdəlˌweɪt / (AmE), / laɪt ˈmɪdəlweɪt / (BrE)
an amateur boxing weight class for fighters weighing no more than 156 pounds (71 kg)
boxing weight class (amateur, ≤156 lbs)

Examples

The light middleweight division has produced many talented amateur boxers over the years.
She trained hard to stay within the light middleweight limit before the tournament.
He won gold in the light middleweight category at the national championships.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: middleweight - a boxing class heavier than welterweight but lighter than light heavyweight
  • Degree Lower: welterweight - a boxing class lighter than middleweight
