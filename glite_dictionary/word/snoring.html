snoring

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnɔːrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsnɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or sound of breathing noisily while sleeping.
noisy breathing during sleep

Examples

His snoring kept me awake all night.
She apologized for her snoring, saying she didn’t realize it was so loud.
The gentle snoring of the baby was oddly comforting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snore - a single instance of snoring; to breathe noisily in sleep (verb and noun)
