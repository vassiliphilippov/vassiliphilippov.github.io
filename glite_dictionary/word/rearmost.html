rearmost

1. ADJ

/ ˈriˌɑrˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈrɪəˌməʊst / (BrE)
furthest towards the back or rear; situated at the very back of something
furthest at the back

Examples

The luggage was stored in the rearmost compartment of the bus.
She sat quietly in the rearmost row of the theater, away from the crowd.
The emergency exit is located at the rearmost section of the plane.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to rearm - to equip with new weapons
  • Antonym: foremost - closest to the front or most important

Etymology

From 'rear' + 'most', formed in analogy with 'foremost' and other superlatives.
