short-headed

1. ADJ

/ ʃɔrt ˈhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ʃɔːt ˈhɛdɪd / (BrE)
Having a head that is broad and short, typically referring to a brachycephalic shape.
having a broad, short head

Examples

The bulldog is known for its short-headed appearance and compact skull.
Certain sheep breeds are classified as short-headed due to the shape of their skulls.
The study focused on the genetic basis for short-headed features in different dog breeds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brachycephalic - having a short, broad head
