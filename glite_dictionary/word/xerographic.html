xerographic

1. ADJ

/ ˌzɪrəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌzɪərəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or using xerography, a dry photocopying process.
relating to xerography (dry photocopying)

Examples

The office uses a xerographic copier for all its document duplication needs.
Advancements in xerographic printing have improved image quality significantly.
She studied the xerographic process as part of her training in document reproduction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: xerography - a dry photocopying technique using electrostatic charges

Etymology

Formed from 'xerography' + '-ic', referencing the dry (Greek 'xeros') copying process.
