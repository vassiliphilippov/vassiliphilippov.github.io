pinguecula

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋˈɡwɛkjʊlə / (AmE), / pɪŋˈɡwɛkjʊlə / (BrE)
a slightly raised, yellowish deposit of lipid in the conjunctiva near the cornea, usually not covering the cornea itself
yellowish eye conjunctival growth
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that the small yellow spot on her eye was a pinguecula and not a serious problem.
People who spend a lot of time in the sun are more likely to develop a pinguecula.
A pinguecula may cause mild irritation but usually does not affect vision.

Etymology

From Latin 'pinguecula' (diminutive of 'pinguis', meaning 'fat').
