Zhu Jiang

1. PROPN

/ dʒu ˈdʒjɑŋ / (AmE), / dʒuː ˈdʒiːɑŋ / (BrE)
a major river in southeastern China, also known as the Pearl River, that flows into the South China Sea
river in southeastern China

Examples

The Zhu Jiang delta is one of the most densely urbanized regions in the world.
Many factories are located along the Zhu Jiang due to its access to international shipping.
Tourists enjoy taking a night cruise along the illuminated Zhu Jiang in Guangzhou.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pearl River - another name for Zhu Jiang, river in southern China

Etymology

From Mandarin Chinese 珠江 (Zhū Jiāng), meaning 'Pearl River'.
