Basseterre

1. PROPN

/ bæsəˈtɛr / (AmE), / bæsəˈtɛə / (BrE)
the capital city of Saint Kitts and Nevis, located on the island of Saint Kitts (Saint Christopher) in the West Indies
capital city of Saint Kitts and Nevis

Examples

The Prime Minister gave a speech in Basseterre to celebrate Independence Day.
Many tourists arrive in Basseterre by cruise ship each year.
After exploring Basseterre, we visited other towns on Saint Kitts.

Etymology

From French 'basse terre' meaning 'low land' or 'low ground'.
