kiddie

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪdi / (AmE), / ˈkɪdi / (BrE)
an informal term for a young child
young child (informal)
informal

Examples

The playground was full of kiddies laughing and playing.
That movie is only suitable for kiddies under seven years old.
He bought some colorful balloons for the kiddies at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kid - a child (informal)

Etymology

Diminutive of 'kid', meaning child, with the affectionate -ie ending.
