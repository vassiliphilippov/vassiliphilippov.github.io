artificiality

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːrtɪfɪʃiˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌɑːtɪfɪʃiˈælɪti / (BrE)
The quality or state of being made by people rather than occurring naturally; the condition of being artificial.
the quality of being artificial

Examples

The artificiality of the display was obvious compared to authentic artifacts.
Critics complained about the artificiality in her mannerisms during the performance.
Some people resent the artificiality of modern processed foods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: artificial - made by humans rather than occurring naturally

Etymology

From Latin artificialis (made by art) + -ity.
