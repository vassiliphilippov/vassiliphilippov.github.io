newsvendor

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuzˌvɛndər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌvɛndə / (BrE)
a person who sells newspapers, typically in public places or on the street
person who sells newspapers

Examples

The newsvendor on the corner greeted his regular customers each morning.
A young newsvendor shouted the latest headlines as people hurried by.
Many newsvendors have switched to selling snacks alongside newspapers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newsagent - person who sells newspapers and magazines from a shop

Etymology

From 'news' + 'vendor', meaning someone who sells news (newspapers).
