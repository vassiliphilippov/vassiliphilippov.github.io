musclebuilding

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsəlˌbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmʌsəlˌbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of exercising to increase the size and strength of muscles
exercise to increase muscle size and strength

Examples

He dedicated himself to musclebuilding at the gym over the summer.
The magazine featured tips on nutrition and musclebuilding routines for beginners.
Proper rest is just as important as musclebuilding exercises for muscle growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bodybuilding - the activity of developing muscles through exercise; often for competition

Etymology

Formed from 'muscle' and 'building', referring to the act of building up muscles.
