arccos

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˌkɑs / (AmE), / ɑːˌkɒs / (BrE)
the inverse function of the cosine; the angle whose cosine is a given number
inverse cosine function

Examples

To find the angle, take the arccos of the ratio of the adjacent side to the hypotenuse.
The value of arccos(0.5) is 60 degrees or π/3 radians.
Many calculators have a dedicated button for the arccos function.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arccosine - the full term for arccos, the inverse function of cosine
  • Antonym: cosine - the trigonometric function relating angles to side ratios
  • Synonym: arcsin - inverse sine function
  • Synonym: arctan - inverse tangent function

Etymology

Shortened form of 'arc cosine', from 'arc' (as in arcus, a Latin root) and 'cosine'.
