municipally

1. ADV

/ məˈnɪs.ə.pəli / (AmE), / mjʊˈnɪs.ɪ.pəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or is administered by a municipality or local government.
by or through a municipality

Examples

The new community center was municipally operated and open to all residents.
Many parks are municipally maintained to ensure cleanliness and safety.
Garbage collection is often municipally funded in urban areas.

Synset Examples

municipally funded

Related Terms

  • Synonym: municipal - relating to a city or town or its governing body

Etymology

From 'municipal' (relating to a municipality) + '-ly' (adverb suffix). 'Municipal' is from Latin 'municipalis'.
