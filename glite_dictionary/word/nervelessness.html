nervelessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɝːv.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈnɜːv.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
calm and confident self-possession, especially in dangerous or stressful situations; lack of nervousness or fear
fearless composure, lack of nervousness

Examples

Her nervelessness in a crisis impressed everyone in the team.
The pilot's nervelessness saved many lives during the emergency landing.
Despite the chaos, he handled the situation with remarkable nervelessness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: composure - calmness and self-control; emotional stability
  • Antonym: nervousness - the state of being nervous or anxious

Etymology

From 'nerveless' + '-ness', formed in English.
