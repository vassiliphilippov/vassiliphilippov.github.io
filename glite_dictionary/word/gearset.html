gearset

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪrˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈɡɪəsɛt / (BrE)
a group of gears arranged to work together in a machine, such as a gearbox or transmission
set of gears in a machine

Examples

The mechanic replaced the damaged gearset in the bicycle's transmission.
Modern cars often feature a multi-speed gearset for improved efficiency.
A well-designed gearset can provide smooth and reliable shifting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gearbox - a casing containing a set of gears in a vehicle
  • Synonym: transmission - system of gears transmitting power in vehicles

Etymology

From 'gear' (mechanism for transmitting motion) + 'set' (collection of things).
