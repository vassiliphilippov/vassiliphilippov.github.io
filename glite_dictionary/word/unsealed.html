unsealed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsiːld / (AmE), / ʌnˈsiːld / (BrE)
not closed or made secure with a seal; open or not fastened shut
not closed or secured with a seal

Examples

The package arrived unsealed, so I checked the contents immediately.
He read the unsealed envelope to see who it was from.
Unattended unsealed containers can lead to safety hazards in the laboratory.

Synset Examples

unsealed goods
the letter arrived unsealed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sealed - closed or fastened with a seal

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'sealed'.
