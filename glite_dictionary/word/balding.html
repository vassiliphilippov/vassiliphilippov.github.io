balding

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɔldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɔːldɪŋ / (BrE)
Beginning to lose hair on the scalp; becoming bald.
losing hair, becoming bald

Examples

His balding head glistened under the bright lights of the stage.
She noticed her father was balding and started wearing hats more often.
The balding man smiled warmly at the children in the park.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: bald - lacking hair on the scalp

Etymology

From 'bald' + '-ing', denoting the process of becoming bald.
