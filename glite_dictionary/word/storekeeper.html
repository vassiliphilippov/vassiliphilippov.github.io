storekeeper

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɔrˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈstɔːˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person who owns or manages a shop or store, often responsible for its day-to-day operations
person who manages or owns a shop

Examples

The storekeeper greeted customers as they entered the shop.
After working for years, she became the storekeeper of her local grocery store.
The storekeeper is responsible for keeping track of inventory and ordering new stock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shopkeeper - a person who owns or manages a small shop

Etymology

From 'store' + 'keeper', meaning someone who keeps (manages) a store.
