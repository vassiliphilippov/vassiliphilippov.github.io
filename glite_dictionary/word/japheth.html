Japheth

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒeɪfɛθ / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪfɪθ / (BrE)
(in the Bible) the youngest son of Noah, traditionally regarded as the ancestor of various peoples after the flood.
biblical son of Noah

Examples

According to the Old Testament, Japheth was one of Noah's three sons who survived the flood.
Many ancient genealogies trace the origins of certain nations back to Japheth.
Bible scholars often discuss the descendants of Japheth when studying early human history.

Etymology

From Hebrew *Yepheth*, a name in the Old Testament.
