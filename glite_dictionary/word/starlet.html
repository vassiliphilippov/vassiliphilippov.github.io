starlet

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑr.lət / (AmE), / ˈstɑː.lɪt / (BrE)
a young actress who is promoted as likely to become a film star
young aspiring film actress

Examples

The young starlet quickly rose to fame after her breakout role in the romantic comedy.
Every Hollywood studio was searching for the next big starlet to feature in their upcoming blockbuster.
Despite being labeled a starlet, she was determined to be taken seriously as an actress.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: star - a famous and successful actor, singer, etc.
  • Synonym: actress - a female actor

Etymology

Formed from 'star' + diminutive suffix '-let', meaning a small or young star.
