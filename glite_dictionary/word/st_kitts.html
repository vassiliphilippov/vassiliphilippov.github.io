St. Kitts

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt kɪts / (AmE), / sənt kɪts / (BrE)
an island in the West Indies; the largest of the islands forming the nation of Saint Kitts and Nevis
Caribbean island; part of Saint Kitts and Nevis

Examples

We're planning our honeymoon on St. Kitts because of its beautiful beaches.
The capital city of St. Kitts is Basseterre.
Many cruise ships make a stop at St. Kitts during their Caribbean itineraries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nevis - the smaller island paired with St. Kitts to form the nation Saint Kitts and Nevis

Etymology

Contraction of Saint Christopher, named by Christopher Columbus for Saint Christopher, the patron saint of travelers.
