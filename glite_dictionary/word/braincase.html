braincase

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪnˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈbreɪnkeɪs / (BrE)
the part of the skull that encloses and protects the brain
part of skull enclosing brain

Examples

The fossil's braincase was unusually large for its species.
During surgery, the doctor carefully avoided damaging the braincase.
Some mammals have a more rounded braincase than others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cranium - the portion of the skull enclosing the brain
