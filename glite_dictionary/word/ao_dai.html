ao dai

1. NOUN

/ aʊ ˈdaɪ / (AmE), / aʊ ˈdaɪ / (BrE)
A traditional Vietnamese women's dress consisting of a long-sleeved tunic with front and back panels, worn over trousers.
traditional Vietnamese women's dress

Examples

She looked stunning in a bright red ao dai at the wedding ceremony.
Many performers wore the traditional ao dai during the cultural festival.
Her mother helped her choose a silk ao dai for the celebration.

Etymology

From Vietnamese 'áo dài', meaning 'long shirt'.
