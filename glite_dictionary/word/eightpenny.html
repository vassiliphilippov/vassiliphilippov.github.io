eightpenny

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪtpɛni / (AmE), / ˈeɪtpəni / (BrE)
(of a nail) measuring 2 1/2 inches (6.4 cm) in length; designated as 'eightpenny' in standard nail size classification.
nail size: 2 1/2 inches long

Examples

The carpenter requested a box of eightpenny nails for the framing project.
He used eightpenny nails because they were the correct length for the boards.
Make sure to buy eightpenny nails if you're building a standard wall frame.

Etymology

From the price of 100 nails in old British currency: 'penny' indicating price per hundred; 'eightpenny' meaning 'eight pennies for 100 nails'.
