weldment

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛldmənt / (AmE), / ˈwɛldmənt / (BrE)
an assembly of metal components joined together by welding
assembly joined by welding

Examples

The steel frame is a weldment designed to support heavy machinery.
Inspectors examined each weldment for structural integrity and defects.
This custom weldment was fabricated according to precise engineering specifications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weld - to join pieces of metal by melting the parts together

Etymology

From 'weld' (to join by welding) + '-ment' (forming nouns of action or resulting state).
