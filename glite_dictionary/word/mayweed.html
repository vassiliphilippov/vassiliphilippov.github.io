mayweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈmeɪwiːd / (BrE)
a widespread, strong-smelling weed with white, daisy-like flowers and yellow centers
a strong-smelling weed with white and yellow flowers

Examples

The field was covered in mayweed, its pungent scent drifting on the breeze.
Gardeners often remove mayweed because it can take over flowerbeds quickly.
The bright white petals of mayweed stand out against the green grass.
