Louisianan

1. NOUN

/ luˌiːziˈænən / (AmE), / luːˌɪziˈænən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the state of Louisiana in the United States.
person from Louisiana, USA

Examples

As a proud Louisianan, she celebrates Mardi Gras every year with her family.
He met a fellow Louisianan while studying abroad in France.
Many Louisianans are passionate about their unique cuisine and culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Louisianian - another demonym for a person from Louisiana

Etymology

Formed from 'Louisiana' (the U.S. state) + '-an', a common English suffix for demonyms.
