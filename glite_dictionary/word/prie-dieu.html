prie-dieu

1. NOUN

/ priːˈdjuː / (AmE), / priːˈdjɜː / (BrE)
a low piece of furniture with a kneeling bench and a sloping shelf, used for private prayer.
kneeling bench for prayer

Examples

The chapel features a beautifully carved prie-dieu near the altar.
She knelt on the prie-dieu each morning for her prayers.
A prie-dieu stood in the corner, inviting moments of quiet reflection.

Etymology

From French 'prie-Dieu', meaning 'pray [to] God'.
