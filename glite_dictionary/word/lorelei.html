Lorelei

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɔrəˌlaɪ / (AmE), / ˈlɔːrəlaɪ / (BrE)
a legendary siren of German folklore said to sing from a rock on the Rhine River and lure sailors to their doom
legendary siren from German folklore

Examples

The legend of the Lorelei continues to fascinate visitors to the Rhine Valley.
He compared her alluring voice to that of the mythical Lorelei.
Many German poems and songs recount the tragic story of the Lorelei.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: siren - a mythological female creature who lures sailors with her singing

Etymology

From German Lorelei, the name of a rock on the Rhine associated with the legend.
