wreckfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛkˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɛk.fɪʃ / (BrE)
a large, brown fish (Polyprion americanus) of the Atlantic and Mediterranean, often found near rocks and shipwrecks
a large Atlantic and Mediterranean fish

Examples

Divers frequently spot wreckfish hiding in the shadows of sunken ships.
The chef prepared a delicious meal using fresh wreckfish from the Mediterranean.
Wreckfish are known for living around rocky reefs and underwater structures.
