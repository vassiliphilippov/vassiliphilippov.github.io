Caenolestes

1. NOUN

/ keɪ.nəˈlɛstiːz / (AmE), / keɪ.nəˈlɛstiːz / (BrE)
A genus of small South American marsupials, serving as the type genus of the family Caenolestidae.
genus of small South American marsupials

Examples

The Caenolestes is native to the forests and grasslands of South America.
Recent studies on Caenolestes have provided insight into the evolution of marsupials.
Unlike most marsupials, Caenolestes species have elongated heads and small eyes.

Etymology

From Greek 'kainos' (new) and 'lestes' (robber).
