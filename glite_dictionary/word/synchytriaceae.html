Synchytriaceae

1. NOUN

/ sɪnˌkaɪtriaˈsiːiː / (AmE), / sɪnˌkɪtrɪˈeɪsiː / (BrE)
A family of fungi in the order Chytridiales.
family of fungi in Chytridiales

Examples

The Synchytriaceae family includes several important plant pathogens.
Scientists are studying Synchytriaceae to better understand the evolution of fungi.
Members of Synchytriaceae can be found in various aquatic environments.

Etymology

From New Latin, based on the type genus Synchytrium + family suffix -aceae.
