pilosebaceous

1. ADJ

/ ˌpaɪloʊsɪˈbeɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌpɪləʊsɪˈbeɪʃəs / (BrE)
relating to a hair follicle and its associated sebaceous gland
relating to a hair follicle and sebaceous gland
medical

Examples

Acne commonly affects the pilosebaceous units of the skin.
Scientists studied the structure of the pilosebaceous gland under a microscope.
Inflammation of the pilosebaceous tissue can lead to various skin disorders.

Etymology

From Latin 'pilus' (hair) and 'sebaceus' (of fat, oily).
