constructivist

1. NOUN

/ kənˈstrʌktɪvɪst / (AmE), / kənˈstrʌktɪvɪst / (BrE)
an artist who belongs to or practices constructivism, an abstract art movement emphasizing geometric forms and modern materials
artist of the constructivism art movement

Examples

Kazimir Malevich influenced many early constructivist artists in Russia.
The museum's new exhibition features works by a prominent constructivist from the 1920s.
As a constructivist, she believed art should serve a social purpose.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: constructivism - an abstract art movement emphasizing geometric shapes and construction

Etymology

From 'constructivism' + '-ist', indicating a follower or practitioner.
