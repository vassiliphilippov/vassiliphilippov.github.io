reusable

1. ADJ

/ riˈjuːzəbəl / (AmE), / riːˈjuːzəbl / (BrE)
able to be used more than once; designed or intended for repeated use.
able to be used again

Examples

The company offers reusable shopping bags to reduce waste.
Please bring your own reusable water bottle to the event.
Reusable containers are better for the environment than single-use plastics.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: disposable - intended to be used once and then thrown away
  • Synonym: recyclable - able to be processed and used again, especially referring to materials

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 're-' (again) and 'usable' (able to be used).
