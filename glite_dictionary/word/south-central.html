south-central

1. ADJ

/ saʊθ ˈsɛntrəl / (AmE), / saʊθ ˈsɛntrəl / (BrE)
Located in or relating to the southern part of the central area within a geographic region.
situated in southern central area

Examples

The company recently opened a new office in south-central Chicago.
Many species unique to south-central Africa face threats from habitat loss.
Urban development is expanding rapidly in south-central London.

2. ADJ

/ saʊθ ˈsɛntrəl / (AmE), / saʊθ ˈsɛntrəl / (BrE)
relating to or situated in the south-central region of a country, especially referring to the area of the United States that includes the lower Mississippi Valley and areas east of the Rio Grande.
of the south-central region (especially US)

Examples

Many people moved to the south-central United States during the Dust Bowl era.
The south-central region is known for its distinctive cuisine and music.
A tornado warning was issued for several south-central counties last night.

  • Degree Lower: southeastern - relating to or situated in the southeast region
  • Degree Lower: southwestern - relating to or situated in the southwest region
