algometric

1. ADJ

/ ˌælɡoʊˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌalɡəʊˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to algometry, or the measurement of pain sensitivity
relating to the measurement of pain sensitivity
medical

Examples

The researchers used an algometric test to assess participants' pain thresholds.
Recent advances in algometric techniques have improved our understanding of chronic pain conditions.
The study analyzed the algometric data obtained from clinical trials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: algometry - the measurement of pain sensitivity

Etymology

From 'algometry' + '-ic', with 'algometry' from Greek 'algos' (pain) and 'metron' (measure).
