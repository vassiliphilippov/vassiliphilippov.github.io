centare

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛnˌteɪr / (AmE), / ˈsɛntɑː / (BrE)
a metric unit of area equal to one square metre or one hundredth of an are
metric unit of area, 1 square metre

Examples

The garden occupies an area of exactly one centare.
Although rarely used today, the centare is equivalent to one square metre.
The plot of land was measured to be fifty centares in size.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: are - a metric unit of area, equal to 100 square metres

Etymology

From French 'centiare', from 'centi-' (hundredth) + 'are' (unit of area).
