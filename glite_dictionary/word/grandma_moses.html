Grandma Moses

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡrænˌmɑ ˈmoʊzɪz / (AmE), / ˈɡran.mɑː ˈməʊzɪz / (BrE)
(1860–1961) An American folk artist known for her colorful, naive paintings depicting rural life.
famous American folk artist

Examples

Many art collectors admire Grandma Moses for her vibrant portrayals of country life.
A painting by Grandma Moses recently sold at auction for a significant sum.
As a self-taught artist, Grandma Moses began her career in her seventies.

Etymology

Nickname of Anna Mary Robertson Moses, an American painter.
