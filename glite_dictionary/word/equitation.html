equitation

1. NOUN

/ ˌekwɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌiːkwɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the art or practice of riding horses, especially as a sport
the art or sport of horse riding

Examples

She has been taking equitation lessons to improve her riding skills.
The judges awarded him first place in the equitation competition.
Proper equitation requires both balance and control over the horse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horsemanship - skill or art of riding horses

Etymology

From Latin 'equitatio', from 'equitare' (to ride on horseback), from 'equus' (horse).
