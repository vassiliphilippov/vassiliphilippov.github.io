ninetieth

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪn.i.əθ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪn.i.ɪθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number matching the number ninety; being number 90 in a sequence; the item in the ninetieth position
position number ninety in a sequence

Examples

She came in ninetieth place in the marathon, but was still proud of her effort.
Today marks the ninetieth anniversary of the school's founding.
He was the ninetieth person to cross the finish line.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eighty-ninth - the ordinal number for eighty-nine (89th)
  • Degree Higher: ninety-first - the ordinal number for ninety-one (91st)

Etymology

Formed from "ninety" + "-th" (ordinal suffix).

2. ADJ

/ ˈnaɪn.i.əθ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪn.ti.əθ / (BrE)
constituting number ninety in a sequence; 90th in order
ordinal number: 90th

Examples

He finished in ninetieth place out of a hundred runners.
This is the ninetieth anniversary of the school's founding.
Her office is on the ninetieth floor of the skyscraper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ninetieth - 90th fractional part; one of ninety equal parts
  • Degree Lower: eighty-ninth - ordinal number: 89th
  • Degree Higher: ninety-first - ordinal number: 91st

Etymology

Formed from 'ninety' + '-th', denoting an ordinal number.

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: s
