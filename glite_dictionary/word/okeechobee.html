Okeechobee

1. PROPN

/ oʊkiˈtʃoʊbi / (AmE), / əʊkiˈtʃəʊbi / (BrE)
A large lake in southeast Florida, situated north of the Everglades.
lake in southeast Florida

Examples

The water levels in Okeechobee are closely monitored during hurricane season.
Many species of birds can be found around Okeechobee throughout the year.
He spent his vacation fishing on Okeechobee with his family.

Etymology

From Hitchiti (Muskogean) oki (water) + chubi (big), meaning 'big water'.
