nephritic

1. ADJ

/ nɛˈfrɪtɪk / (AmE), / nɪˈfrɪtɪk / (BrE)
Affected by or relating to nephritis (inflammation of the kidneys).
affected by nephritis; kidney-related
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with nephritic syndrome after presenting symptoms of kidney inflammation.
Doctors observed nephritic changes in his laboratory tests.
Her recovery from the nephritic condition was closely monitored by specialists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nephritis - inflammation of the kidneys

Etymology

From Greek nephritikos, from nephros (kidney).

2. ADJ

/ nɛˈfrɪtɪk / (AmE), / nɪˈfrɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the kidneys or kidney disease.
relating to the kidneys
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with a nephritic condition after showing signs of severe kidney inflammation.
A nephritic syndrome can cause a range of symptoms including high blood pressure and swelling.
Doctors prescribed medication to manage her nephritic symptoms and reduce kidney damage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: renal - relating to the kidneys

Etymology

From Greek 'nephritikos', from 'nephros' meaning 'kidney'.
