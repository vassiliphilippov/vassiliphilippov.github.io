Lepidothamnus fonkii

1. NOUN

/ lɛpɪdoʊˈθæmnəs ˈfɒŋki / (AmE), / lɛpɪdəʊˈθamnəs ˈfɒŋki / (BrE)
A species of hardy, prostrate spreading shrub in the family Podocarpaceae, found in mountainous regions of southern Chile.
hardy spreading shrub native to southern Chile

Examples

Lepidothamnus fonkii thrives in the harsh climates of southern Chilean mountains.
Botanists studied how Lepidothamnus fonkii adapts to cold temperatures compared to other Podocarpaceae.
The garden's rare plant collection includes a specimen of Lepidothamnus fonkii.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mountain rimu - A related species of prostrate shrub in the same family

Etymology

Genus name from Greek lepis (scale) and thamnos (shrub); species after botanist Fonk.
