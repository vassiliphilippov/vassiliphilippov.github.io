Glaucomys

1. NOUN

/ ɡloʊˈkoʊmɪs / (AmE), / ɡlaʊˈkəʊmɪs / (BrE)
A genus of New World flying squirrels that includes the northern and southern flying squirrels.
genus of North American flying squirrels

Examples

The northern flying squirrel belongs to the genus Glaucomys.
Researchers have observed habitat changes affecting Glaucomys populations in recent decades.
Glaucomys species can glide between trees using a membrane called the patagium.

Etymology

From Greek 'glaukos' (gleaming, blue-gray) and 'mys' (mouse).
