Franz Liszt

1. PROPN

/ frɑnts lɪst / (AmE), / frants lɪst / (BrE)
(1811–1886) A Hungarian composer and piano virtuoso noted for his innovative compositions and remarkable skill as a performer.
Hungarian composer and pianist (1811–1886)

Examples

Franz Liszt revolutionized piano technique with his innovative and demanding musical compositions.
Many pianists aspire to master the challenging works of Franz Liszt.
The 19th-century composer Franz Liszt remains an iconic figure in the world of classical music.
