Durango

1. PROPN

/ duˈræŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / djʊəˈræŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
a city in north-central Mexico known as a mining center and the capital of Durango state.
city in north-central Mexico

Examples

She plans to visit Durango during her upcoming trip to Mexico.
Many people in Durango rely on the mining industry for employment.
The historic architecture of Durango attracts tourists from around the world.

Etymology

From the Basque place name Durango, exported to Mexico by Spanish settlers.
