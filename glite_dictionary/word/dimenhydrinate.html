dimenhydrinate

1. NOUN

/ ˌdaɪmənˈhaɪdrɪˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˌdɪmɛnˈhʌɪdrɪneɪt / (BrE)
an antihistamine and antiemetic drug, used to treat motion sickness, often sold under the brand name Dramamine.
antihistamine drug for motion sickness
medical

Examples

My doctor recommended dimenhydrinate to help prevent nausea during the cruise.
Dimenhydrinate is commonly found in over-the-counter motion sickness treatments.
Some people feel drowsy after taking dimenhydrinate before long car journeys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dramamine - brand name for dimenhydrinate, a motion sickness medication

Etymology

From 'dimethyl' and 'diphenhydramine' with additional elements; the name reflects its chemical structure.
