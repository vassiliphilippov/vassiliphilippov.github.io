mouse-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈmaʊs ˌkʌlərd / (AmE), / ˈmaʊs ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having a dull, pale brownish-grey color resembling that of a mouse
dull brownish-grey color

Examples

Her mouse-colored hair framed her gentle face.
The old coat was a faded, mouse-colored shade after years of use.
He stared at the mouse-colored walls, lost in thought.

Synset Examples

a mousy brownish-grey color
mousy hair
mouse-colored hair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mousy - having a color like that of a mouse; dull brownish-grey

Etymology

Formed from 'mouse' (the rodent, referring to typical fur color) + 'colored'.
