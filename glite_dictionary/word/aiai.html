AIAI

1. PROPN

/ ˌeɪ.aɪ.eɪˈaɪ / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.aɪ.eɪˈaɪ / (BrE)
a fundamentalist Islamic militant group originating in Somalia, known for terrorist activities and connections with al-Qaeda, with operations in multiple countries
Somali Islamic militant group

Examples

AIAI was involved in several attacks against government and foreign forces in Somalia.
Intelligence reports indicated that AIAI had ties to international terrorist organizations.
The activities of AIAI were condemned by multiple countries at the United Nations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: al-Qaeda - a militant Islamist organization responsible for terrorist attacks

Etymology

Acronym of 'Al-Itihaad al-Islamiya', Arabic for 'Islamic Union'.
