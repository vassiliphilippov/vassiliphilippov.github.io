degustation

1. NOUN

/ diˌɡʌˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / diːˌɡʌsˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of carefully tasting food or drink, especially in small amounts, to appreciate its flavors
careful tasting of food or drink

Examples

The chef offered a degustation menu featuring a variety of small, expertly prepared dishes.
He attended a wine degustation to learn more about different grape varieties.
During the degustation, guests took time to savor and discuss each course.

Synset Examples

cooking was fine but it was the savoring that he enjoyed most

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tasting - the act of sampling food or drink

Etymology

From French dégustation, from Latin degustatio (a tasting).
