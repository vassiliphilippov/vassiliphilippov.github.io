shufti

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃʌfti / (AmE), / ˈʃʌfti / (BrE)
a quick look or glance, especially in a situation requiring alertness (originally military slang)
quick look; glance (military slang)
slang british

Examples

He took a shufti through the window before entering the building.
Give that report a quick shufti and let me know what you think.
The patrol had a shufti around the corner to check for danger.

Synset Examples

take a shufti while you're out there

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glance - a quick or brief look
  • Synonym: look - the act of directing one's gaze

Etymology

From Arabic شُوفْتِ (šufti), meaning 'have you seen?'; adopted into British military slang during World War II.
