Ibadan

1. PROPN

/ iˈbɑːdən / (AmE), / ɪˈbɑːdən / (BrE)
A large city in southwestern Nigeria, an important center for the Yoruba people and home to a major university.
large city in southwestern Nigeria

Examples

The University of Ibadan is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in Nigeria.
He grew up in Ibadan before moving abroad for his master's degree.
Traffic in Ibadan can be quite heavy during rush hour.
