Doryopteris

1. PROPN

/ ˌdɔːriˈɑːptərɪs / (AmE), / ˌdɔːriˈɒptərɪs / (BrE)
A genus of small to medium tropical tufted ferns, sometimes classified in the family Adiantaceae.
genus of tropical tufted ferns

Examples

The delicate fronds of Doryopteris make it a popular choice for shaded conservatories.
Several species of Doryopteris are cultivated by fern enthusiasts for their unique foliage.
Botanists have noted that Doryopteris sometimes resembles ferns in the Adiantaceae family.

Etymology

From Greek *dory* (spear) and *pteris* (fern), referring to the spear-shaped segments of the fronds.
