toothy

1. ADJ

/ ˈtuːθi / (AmE), / ˈtuːθi / (BrE)
having or showing prominent or large teeth, especially in a noticeable smile or expression
showing prominent teeth

Examples

She gave me a toothy grin when she saw the surprise.
A toothy smile can make someone appear more approachable and cheerful.
The actor is known for his distinctive toothy expression in every film.

Synset Examples

a toothy smile

Related Terms

  • Antonym: toothless - lacking teeth

Etymology

From 'tooth' + '-y', a suffix forming adjectives meaning 'having the quality of'.
