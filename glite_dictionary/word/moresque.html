Moresque

1. ADJ

/ mɔˈrɛsk / (AmE), / mɔːˈrɛsk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of the Moors, especially in reference to art or architecture influenced by Moorish styles.
relating to the Moors or Moorish style

Examples

The elaborate tilework in the old palace is distinctly Moresque in style.
He admired the Moresque arches and intricate patterns of the courtyard.
The museum's collection includes several Moresque artifacts from the 15th century.

Synset Examples

Moorish courtyard

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Moorish - Relating to the Moors or their architecture

Etymology

From French 'moresque', from Italian 'moresco', from 'moro' meaning 'Moor'.
