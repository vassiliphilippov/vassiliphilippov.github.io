singalong

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋ.əˌlɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋ.əˌlɒŋ / (BrE)
an informal gathering or event where people sing popular songs together, often accompanied by music
group singing event or activity
informal

Examples

We had a lively singalong around the campfire last night.
The musical ended with a singalong, encouraging the whole audience to join in.
Grandma always loves a good singalong at family gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: karaoke - a form of entertainment in which people sing along to recorded music

Etymology

Formed from "sing" + "along" (early 20th century), reflecting the participatory nature of the activity.
