Stagira

1. PROPN

/ stəˈdʒaɪrə / (AmE), / stəˈdʒaɪrə / (BrE)
an ancient town in northeastern Greece, known as the birthplace of the philosopher Aristotle
ancient Greek town, birthplace of Aristotle

Examples

Many tourists visit Stagira to see the birthplace of Aristotle.
The ruins of Stagira offer a glimpse into ancient Greek civilization.
Aristotle spent his early years in Stagira before moving to Athens.

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Στάγειρα (Stágeira), the original Greek name of the town.
