Capparis

1. NOUN

/ kəˈpɛərɪs / (AmE), / kəˈpæːrɪs / (BrE)
a genus of tropical and subtropical evergreen shrubs or small trees, including the caper bush
genus of tropical evergreen shrubs or small trees

Examples

The genus Capparis includes the plant commonly known as the caper bush.
Botanists have identified several medicinal compounds in species of Capparis.
Some varieties of Capparis are cultivated for their edible flower buds.

Etymology

From Latin Capparis, from Greek kápparis (caper bush).
