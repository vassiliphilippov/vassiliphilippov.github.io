antimonial

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.tɪˈmoʊ.ni.əl / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪˈməʊ.ni.əl / (BrE)
containing or relating to antimony, especially as an ingredient in alloys or compounds.
containing or relating to antimony

Examples

The chemist examined an antimonial alloy used in battery manufacturing.
Certain types of antimonial glass have unique optical properties due to the presence of antimony.
They replaced the lead pipes with antimonial lead to improve corrosion resistance.

Synset Examples

antimonial lead

Etymology

From Medieval Latin antimonium (antimony) + -al (forming adjectives).
