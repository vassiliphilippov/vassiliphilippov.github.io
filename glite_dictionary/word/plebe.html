plebe

1. NOUN

/ plib / (AmE), / pliÀêb / (BrE)
a first-year student or trainee at a military academy, especially in the United States
first-year military academy trainee

Examples

The incoming plebes were required to uphold strict discipline during their first year.
As a plebe, she quickly learned the importance of teamwork and resilience.
Upperclassmen often mentor the plebes through the challenges of military training.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freshman - a first-year student, especially in a college or university
  • Antonym: upperclassman - a student in the later years of a military academy or college

Etymology

Shortened form of 'plebeian', from Latin 'plebeius' (of the common people).
