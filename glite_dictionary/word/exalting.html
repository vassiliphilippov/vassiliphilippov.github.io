exalting

1. ADJ

/ ɪɡˈzɑltɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːltɪŋ / (BrE)
making someone or something feel uplifted, inspired, or elevated; tending to exalt.
making one feel uplifted or inspired

Examples

His speech gave the crowd an exalting sense of hope for the future.
The choir's exalting performance moved the audience to tears.
Art can have an exalting effect on the human spirit.

Synset Examples

an exalting eulogy
ennobling thoughts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ennobling - inspiring moral or emotional upliftment
  • Antonym: depressing - making someone feel sad or less confident
