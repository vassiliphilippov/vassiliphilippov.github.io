commodious

1. ADJ

/ kəˈmoʊdiəs / (AmE), / kəˈməʊdiəs / (BrE)
(of a room, building, etc.) spacious and comfortable; having plenty of space.
spacious and comfortable

Examples

The apartment was surprisingly commodious, offering plenty of space for furniture and guests.
Their new office building is far more commodious than the previous cramped workspace.
We were delighted to find a commodious suite for our family at the hotel.

Synset Examples

a commodious harbor
a commodious building suitable for conventions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spacious - having ample space

Etymology

From Latin 'commodus' (convenient, suitable, comfortable).
