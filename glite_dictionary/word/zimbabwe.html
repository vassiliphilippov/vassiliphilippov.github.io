Zimbabwe

1. PROPN

/ zɪmˈbɑːbweɪ / (AmE), / zɪmˈbɑːbweɪ / (BrE)
A landlocked country in south central Africa, formerly known as Rhodesia, which became independent from the United Kingdom in 1980.
a country in south central Africa

Examples

Tourists travel to Zimbabwe to visit Victoria Falls and the ruins of Great Zimbabwe.
Since gaining independence, Zimbabwe has undergone significant political and economic changes.
The official currency of Zimbabwe has changed several times over the past decades.

Etymology

From Shona, meaning 'houses of stone,' referring to Great Zimbabwe.
