unsightly

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsaɪtli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsaɪtli / (BrE)
unpleasant or unattractive to look at; ugly or displeasing in appearance
unpleasant to look at

Examples

The abandoned building had an unsightly appearance that bothered the neighbors.
Please remove that unsightly stain from the carpet before the guests arrive.
Several unsightly weeds grew rapidly along the edges of the sidewalk.

Synset Examples

unsightly billboards

Related Terms

  • Antonym: attractive - pleasing or appealing in appearance
  • Synonym: ugly - unpleasant or repulsive in appearance

Etymology

From un- (not) + sightly (pleasing in appearance, from 'sight')
