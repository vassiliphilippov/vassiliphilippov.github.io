Somalia

1. PROPN

/ səˈmɑːliə / (AmE), / səˈmɑːliə / (BrE)
a country in eastern Africa, located on the Horn of Africa, officially known as the Federal Republic of Somalia
country in eastern Africa

Examples

Somalia has a long coastline along the Indian Ocean.
Political unrest has affected the stability of Somalia for decades.
Aid organizations have been active in Somalia during times of drought.

Etymology

From the Somali ethnic group and region, ultimately from the Somali language.
