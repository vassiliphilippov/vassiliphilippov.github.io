nineties

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪn.tiz / (AmE), / ˈnaɪn.tiz / (BrE)
The period of a person's life between the ages of 90 and 100.
ages 90 to 100 in life

Examples

She remained active well into her nineties, gardening and volunteering in her community.
Many people today are living healthier lives into their nineties.
Despite being in his nineties, he still enjoyed daily walks around the park.

2. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪntiːz / (AmE), / ˈnaɪntiz / (BrE)
the decade from 1990 to 1999.
decade 1990–1999

Examples

Many popular TV shows from the nineties are still watched today.
Fashion trends that were popular in the nineties have made a comeback.
He grew up during the nineties, so he remembers cassette tapes and dial-up internet.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eighties - the decade from 1980 to 1989
  • Degree Higher: two thousands - the decade from 2000 to 2009
