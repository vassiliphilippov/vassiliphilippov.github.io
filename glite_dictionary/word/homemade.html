homemade

1. ADJ

/ ˌhoʊmˈmeɪd / (AmE), / ˌhəʊmˈmeɪd / (BrE)
made or produced at home rather than bought from a store or prepared commercially.
made at home, not bought

Examples

She prefers to bake homemade bread for her family every weekend.
The festival featured a variety of homemade crafts and treats.
Nothing tastes quite like homemade soup on a cold day.

Synset Examples

homemade bread

Related Terms

  • Antonym: store-bought - purchased from a store rather than made at home
