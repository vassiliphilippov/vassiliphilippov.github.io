toaster

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊstɚ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊstə / (BrE)
a kitchen appliance, usually electric, used for toasting slices of bread
kitchen appliance for toasting bread

Examples

She bought a new toaster to make breakfast easier.
The toaster automatically pops up the bread when it's ready.
Please don't put metal utensils in the toaster for safety reasons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grill - a device for cooking food with direct heat
  • Synonym: oven - a kitchen appliance used for baking or roasting food

Etymology

From 'toast' (to brown or cook bread) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
