Kalamazoo

1. PROPN

/ ˌkæləməˈzu / (AmE), / ˌkæləməˈzuː / (BrE)
A city in southwest Michigan, United States, known for its universities and history as a manufacturing center.
city in southwest Michigan, USA

Examples

She moved to Kalamazoo to attend Western Michigan University.
The band played a concert in Kalamazoo last summer.
He grew up in Kalamazoo before relocating to Chicago.

Etymology

Thought to be derived from a Potawatomi word, possibly meaning 'boiling water' or 'mirage'.
