electromotive

1. ADJ

/ ɪˌlɛktroʊˈmoʊtɪv / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈməʊtɪv / (BrE)
relating to or producing electric current
relating to producing electric current

Examples

The battery generates an electromotive force that drives current through the circuit.
The electromotive properties of the material make it useful for scientific experiments.
Her research focuses on developing new electromotive devices for renewable energy applications.

Etymology

From electro- (relating to electricity) + motive (causing motion or movement).
