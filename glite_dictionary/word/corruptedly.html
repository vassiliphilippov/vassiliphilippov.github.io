corruptedly

1. ADV

/ kəˈrʌptɪdli / (AmE), / kəˈrʌptɪdli / (BrE)
In a corrupt or dishonest manner.
in a corrupt or dishonest manner

Examples

The official acted corruptedly to secure personal gain.
It was proven in court that he had behaved corruptedly during the investigation.
The contract was awarded corruptedly, ignoring all legal procedures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dishonestly - in a dishonest manner
  • Synonym: corruptly - in a corrupt manner
  • Antonym: honestly - in an honest manner

Etymology

Formed from 'corrupted' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
