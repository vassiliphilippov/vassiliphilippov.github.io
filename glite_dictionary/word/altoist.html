altoist

1. NOUN

/ ˈæl.toʊ.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈæl.təʊ.ɪst / (BrE)
a musician who plays the alto saxophone
alto saxophone player

Examples

The talented altoist captivated the audience with a soulful solo.
As an aspiring altoist, she practiced scales every day.
Many famous jazz bands feature a skilled altoist in their lineup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saxophonist - a person who plays the saxophone

Etymology

From 'alto' + '-ist', denoting a performer on an alto-range instrument.
