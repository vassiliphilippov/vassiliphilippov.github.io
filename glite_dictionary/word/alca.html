Alca

1. NOUN

/ ˈæl.kə / (AmE), / ˈæl.kə / (BrE)
a genus of seabirds in the Alcidae family, comprising solely the razorbill
genus of seabirds (razorbill)

Examples

The genus Alca contains only the razorbill species found in the North Atlantic.
Ornithologists recently published a study on the behavior of Alca torda.
Members of the family Alcidae, such as Alca, are expert divers.

Etymology

From New Latin Alca, adopted from Swedish 'alka' meaning 'auk'.
