Houyhnhnm

1. NOUN

/ huːˈhɪnəm / (AmE), / huːˈɪnəm / (BrE)
a member of a fictional race of intelligent horses who govern the Yahoos, in Jonathan Swift's novel 'Gulliver's Travels'
intelligent horse in Swift's 'Gulliver's Travels'

Examples

The Houyhnhnm regarded Gulliver as a curious creature, somewhere between a Yahoo and themselves.
In literature classes, we discussed the philosophy of the Houyhnhnms as depicted by Swift.
He admired the society of the Houyhnhnms for its rationality and order.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Yahoo - a brutish, uncouth human in 'Gulliver's Travels'

Etymology

Coined by Jonathan Swift in 'Gulliver's Travels' (1726) as the name of a fictional species.
