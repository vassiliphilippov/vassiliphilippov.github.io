paperhanging

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpɚˌhæŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəˌhæŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of applying wallpaper to walls
applying wallpaper to walls

Examples

He learned paperhanging as an apprentice before opening his own decorating business.
The room looked brand new after a day of paperhanging and painting.
She hired a professional because she found paperhanging too difficult to do herself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wallpapering - the process of covering walls with wallpaper

Etymology

From 'paper' and 'hanging', referring to the practice of hanging paper (wallpaper) on walls.
