toothsomeness

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːθ.səm.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtuːθ.səm.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being delicious, appetizing, or pleasing to the taste
the quality of being delicious or appetizing

Examples

The toothsomeness of the dessert left everyone craving a second helping.
Critics praised the toothsomeness of the chef's signature dishes.
She admired the toothsomeness of the freshly baked bread as soon as she tasted it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deliciousness - the quality of being delicious

Etymology

From 'toothsome' (pleasing to the taste), from 'tooth' + 'some'.
