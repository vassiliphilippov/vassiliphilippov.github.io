Mossad

1. PROPN

/ moʊˈsɑd / (AmE), / mɒˈsæd / (BrE)
the national foreign intelligence agency of Israel, responsible for intelligence gathering, covert operations, and counterterrorism outside Israel
Israel's foreign intelligence agency

Examples

The Mossad played a crucial role in gathering intelligence during the operation.
Many novels feature agents from the Mossad involved in international espionage.
She claimed to have met a former Mossad operative while traveling in Europe.

Synset Examples

the primary focus of the Mossad is on Arab nations

Etymology

From Modern Hebrew מוֹסָד ‎(mosád, 'institute' or 'foundation')
