windburn

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪnd.bɝːn / (AmE), / ˈwɪnd.bɜːn / (BrE)
Redness, irritation, and soreness of the skin caused by prolonged exposure to strong wind.
skin irritation from strong wind

Examples

After hiking all day in the cold, he came home with a painful windburn on his cheeks.
The strong gusts on the mountaintop left her skin red from windburn.
Remember to cover your face to protect against windburn when skiing in harsh weather.
