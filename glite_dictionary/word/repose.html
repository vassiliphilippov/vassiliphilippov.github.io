repose

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈpoʊz / (AmE), / rɪˈpəʊz / (BrE)
a state of restfulness or calm, especially freedom from worry or anxiety
a state of calm or peace of mind

Examples

After a long day, she enjoyed a moment of repose in her favorite chair.
Meditation helped him find repose in the midst of chaos.
The garden's quiet beauty filled her mind with repose.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tranquillity - a state of calm and peacefulness
  • Antonym: agitation - a state of nervousness or unrest

Etymology

From Old French *reposer*, from Latin *repausare* (to rest).
