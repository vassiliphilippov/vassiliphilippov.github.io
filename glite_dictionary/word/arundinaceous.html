arundinaceous

1. ADJ

/ əˌrʌndəˈneɪʃəs / (AmE), / əˌrʌndɪˈneɪʃəs / (BrE)
of, relating to, or resembling reed-like plants of the genus Arundinaria or similar grasses
resembling or related to reed-like plants

Examples

The scientist described the arundinaceous features of the rare grass species.
Her garden is filled with arundinaceous plants that resemble bamboo and reeds.
The landscaper recommended arundinaceous varieties to achieve a lush, grass-like effect.

Etymology

From Latin arundinaceus (of reeds), from arundo, arundinis (reed).
