applemint

1. NOUN

/ ˈæp.əlˌmɪnt / (AmE), / ˈæp.əl.mɪnt / (BrE)
a type of mint plant with apple-scented leaves, native to southern and western Europe and naturalized in the United States
mint plant with apple-scented leaves

Examples

Fresh applemint adds a subtle fruity flavor to salads and beverages.
She grew applemint in her garden alongside spearmint and peppermint.
The leaves of applemint are often used for herbal teas and potpourri.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mint - an aromatic herb of the genus Mentha
