moviemaking

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuː.viˌmeɪ.kɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmuː.viˌmeɪ.kɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of making movies, from conception to production and editing
the process of making movies

Examples

The rise of digital technology has transformed moviemaking in profound ways.
She moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in moviemaking.
Documentaries have become an important part of contemporary moviemaking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filmmaking - the process of making films

Etymology

Formed from 'movie' + 'making', modeled after 'filmmaking'.
