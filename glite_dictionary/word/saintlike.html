saintlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈseɪntˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈseɪnt.laɪk / (BrE)
Having the qualities, virtues, or goodness associated with a saint; exceptionally kind, patient, or virtuous.
having qualities of a saint

Examples

Her saintlike patience with the children was truly remarkable.
He offered saintlike forgiveness, even after being wronged.
The nurse's saintlike compassion brought comfort to every patient.

Synset Examples

angelic beneficence
a beatific smile
a saintly concern for his fellow men
my sainted mother

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saintly - having the moral qualities of a saint
  • Synonym: angelic - possessing the goodness or purity of an angel
  • Antonym: devilish - very wicked or cruel

Etymology

From "saint" + "-like", meaning resembling a saint.
