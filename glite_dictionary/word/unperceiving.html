unperceiving

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnpərˈsivɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnpəˈsiːvɪŋ / (BrE)
not having or showing the ability to perceive; lacking perception or awareness.
lacking perception or awareness

Examples

He remained unperceiving of the subtle changes in her mood throughout the evening.
The painting's beauty was lost on his unperceiving eyes.
She walked into the room, unperceiving of the tension in the air.

Synset Examples

as unperceptive as a boulder

Related Terms

  • Antonym: perceptive - having or showing keenness of perception
  • Synonym: unperceptive - lacking perception or insight

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'perceiving', from Latin 'percipere' (to seize, understand).
