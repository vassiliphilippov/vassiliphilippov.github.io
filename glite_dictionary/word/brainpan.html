brainpan

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪnˌpæn / (AmE), / ˈbreɪnˌpæn / (BrE)
the part of the skull that encloses and protects the brain
skull part enclosing the brain

Examples

He suffered a blow to the brainpan during the accident.
Ancient skulls show that the brainpan has changed shape over time.
The surgeon carefully examined the brainpan for signs of injury.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cranium - the part of the skull enclosing the brain

Etymology

From Middle English 'brenepanne', literally 'brain pan', referring to the protective container for the brain.
