Ile St-Louis

1. PROPN

/ il sɛnt ˈluːi / (AmE), / il sɛnt ˈluːi / (BrE)
an island in the River Seine in Paris, known for its historic architecture and residential character
island in central Paris

Examples

Many tourists enjoy walking across Ile St-Louis to admire its beautiful 17th-century mansions.
The ice cream shop on Ile St-Louis is famous among Parisians and visitors alike.
Apartments on Ile St-Louis are some of the most sought-after in Paris.

Etymology

French, meaning 'Island of Saint Louis,' after King Louis IX of France.
