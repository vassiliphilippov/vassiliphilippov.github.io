panatela

1. NOUN

/ ˌpænəˈtɛlə / (AmE), / ˌpanəˈtɛlə / (BrE)
A long, slender cigar with straight sides, thinner than most other types.
long, thin cigar

Examples

He enjoyed a panatela after dinner, savoring its mild flavor.
The box contained a dozen panatelas, each wrapped in gold foil.
She bought a panatela as a gift for her grandfather's birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cigar - A rolled bundle of dried and fermented tobacco leaves for smoking

Etymology

From Spanish 'panetela', meaning 'ladyfinger' or a kind of biscuit, likely referring to the cigar's shape.
