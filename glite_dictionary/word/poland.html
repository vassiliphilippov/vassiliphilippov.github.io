Poland

1. PROPN

/ ˈpoʊ.lənd / (AmE), / ˈpəʊ.lənd / (BrE)
A country in central Europe, officially known as the Republic of Poland.
country in central Europe

Examples

Many tourists visit Poland to explore its rich history and vibrant cities.
The invasion of Poland in 1939 marked the beginning of World War II.
Warsaw, the capital of Poland, is known for its beautiful architecture.

Etymology

From Polish 'Polska', meaning 'the land of fields'.
