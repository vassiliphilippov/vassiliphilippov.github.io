rustproofed

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌstˌpruːft / (AmE), / ˈrʌstˌpruːft / (BrE)
Treated so as to be protected against rusting.
treated to prevent rusting

Examples

The car's chassis was rustproofed before it left the factory.
They had their metal patio furniture rustproofed to make it last longer outdoors.
A rustproofed appliance tends to have a longer lifespan in humid environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rustproof - made so as not to rust
