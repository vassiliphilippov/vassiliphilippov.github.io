frogbit

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɔɡˌbɪt / (AmE), / ˈfrɒɡˌbɪt / (BrE)
A European aquatic plant with floating, heart-shaped leaves and small white flowers.
aquatic plant with floating leaves

A cluster of frogbit floated near the edge of the pond, its bright white flowers in bloom.
