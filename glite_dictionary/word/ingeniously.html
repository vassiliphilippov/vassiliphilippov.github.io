ingeniously

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈdʒiːniəsli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒiːniəsli / (BrE)
In a clever, inventive, and resourceful manner; using new or original ideas to solve problems.
in a clever and inventive way

Examples

The puzzle was solved ingeniously by the young scientist.
She hid the key ingeniously in a hollow book on the shelf.
The architect ingeniously designed the house to use as much natural light as possible.

Synset Examples

a Hampshire farmer had fowls of different breeds, including Dorkings, and he discriminated ingeniously between the ‘dark ones’ and the ‘white ones’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleverly - in a clever manner
  • Antonym: stupidly - in a stupid or foolish manner

Etymology

From 'ingenious' + '-ly'; 'ingenious' from Latin 'ingeniosus' (full of talent).
