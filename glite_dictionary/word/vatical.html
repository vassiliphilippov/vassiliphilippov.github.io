vatical

1. ADJ

/ ˈvætɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈvatɪkəl / (BrE)
Having the qualities of a prophet or prophecy; prophetic.
prophetic; relating to prophecy

Examples

Many believed the poet's vatical verses predicted great changes to come.
The priest spoke in a vatical tone, warning of the kingdom's downfall.
She claimed her dreams were vatical, filled with omens and hidden messages.

Synset Examples

the high priest's divinatory pronouncement
mantic powers
a kind of sibylline book with ready and infallible answers to questions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prophetic - predicting what will happen in the future
  • Synonym: mantic - relating to divination or prophecy

Etymology

From Latin vaticinus, from vates (prophet).
