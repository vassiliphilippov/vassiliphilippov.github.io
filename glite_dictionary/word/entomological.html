entomological

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛntəˈmɑlədʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɛntəməˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the scientific study of insects (entomology)
relating to the study of insects

Examples

She published several entomological papers on the behavior of ants.
The museum has an impressive entomological collection featuring rare beetles and butterflies.
Their entomological expertise helped control the spread of crop pests in the region.

Synset Examples

entomological research

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entomology - the scientific study of insects

Etymology

From entomology + -ical; entomology from Greek entomon (insect) + -logia (study).
