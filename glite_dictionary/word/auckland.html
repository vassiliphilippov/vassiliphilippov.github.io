Auckland

1. PROPN

/ ˈɔk.lənd / (AmE), / ˈɔːk.lənd / (BrE)
the largest city and main port of New Zealand, located on the North Island
city in New Zealand

Examples

Many tourists visit Auckland to enjoy its vibrant culture and beautiful harbors.
She moved from Wellington to Auckland for a new job opportunity.
The flight to Auckland was delayed due to severe weather conditions.

Etymology

Named after George Eden, Earl of Auckland, who was First Lord of the Admiralty at the time of the city's founding.
