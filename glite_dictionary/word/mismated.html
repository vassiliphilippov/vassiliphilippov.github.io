mismated

1. ADJ

/ mɪsˈmeɪtɪd / (AmE), / mɪsˈmeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not well matched or not easy to combine harmoniously
not well matched; incompatible

Examples

Their mismated personalities made collaboration difficult in the workplace.
The colors in the living room were deliberately mismated for a dramatic effect.
Wearing mismated socks became his signature style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incompatible - unable to exist or work together without conflict
  • Antonym: matched - suited or made to go well together

Etymology

From the prefix 'mis-' (badly, wrongly) + 'mated' (past participle of 'mate', to pair).
