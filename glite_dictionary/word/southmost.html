southmost

1. ADJ

/ ˈsaʊθˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈsaʊθməʊst / (BrE)
Situated farthest to the south.
farthest to the south

Examples

The research team set up camp at the southmost point of the peninsula.
Our journey took us to the southmost edge of the national park.
This lighthouse marks the southmost tip of the island.

Synset Examples

Key West is the southernmost city in the continental United States

Related Terms

  • Antonym: northernmost - Situated farthest to the north
  • Degree Higher: easternmost - Situated farthest to the east
  • Synonym: southernmost - Situated farthest south (variant, more common)

Etymology

From 'south' + 'most,' modeled after 'northmost' and similar compounds.
