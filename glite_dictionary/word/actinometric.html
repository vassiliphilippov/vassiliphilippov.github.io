actinometric

1. ADJ

/ ˌæktɪnəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌæktɪnəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to actinometry or to the measurement of the intensity of radiant energy, especially sunlight.
relating to measurement of radiant energy

Examples

The scientist used an actinometric device to measure solar radiation during the experiment.
Careful actinometric analyses are essential for studying the effects of sunlight on atmospheric chemistry.
The results were processed using actinometric methods to ensure accuracy in the energy readings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: actinometry - the measurement of the intensity of radiant energy, especially sunlight

Etymology

From actinometry (from Greek 'aktis' meaning 'ray' + '-metry' meaning 'measurement') + -ic.
