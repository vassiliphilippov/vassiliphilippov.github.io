stumblebum

1. NOUN

/ ˈstʌm.bəlˌbʌm / (AmE), / ˈstʌm.bəl.bʌm / (BrE)
A person who is clumsy, awkward, or incompetent, especially in a foolish or bumbling way.
clumsy or incompetent person
slang

Examples

The new player looked like a stumblebum on the soccer field, tripping over his own feet.
Some critics called the comedian a lovable stumblebum with a knack for slapstick humor.
After three failed attempts to fix the leak, he admitted he was a bit of a stumblebum around the house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or action
  • Synonym: bumbling - acting in a confused or incompetent way
  • Antonym: competent - having the necessary ability or skill

Etymology

Blend of 'stumble' and 'bum' (slang for a worthless person).

2. NOUN

/ ˈstʌm.bəlˌbʌm / (AmE), / ˈstʌm.bəl.bʌm / (BrE)
an incompetent or second-rate prizefighter, especially a clumsy or awkward one
second-rate, clumsy prizefighter
informal american

Examples

After a string of losses, critics began calling him a stumblebum in the boxing magazines.
The manager refused to sign another stumblebum for the upcoming fight.
Many fans felt the bout was a mismatch, with a champion facing a stumblebum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pugilist - a professional boxer, especially one who fights with skill

Etymology

Formed from 'stumble' (to trip or lose balance) + 'bum' (slang for a vagrant or incompetent person) in American English.
