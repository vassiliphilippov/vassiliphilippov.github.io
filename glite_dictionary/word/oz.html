oz.

1. NOUN

/ oʊ zɪs / (AmE), / əʊ zɪz / (BrE)
a unit of weight equal to one sixteenth of a pound, or 28.349 grams
unit of weight (ounce)

Examples

The recipe calls for 8 oz. of chocolate chips.
Each can contains 12 oz. of soda.
He bought a bar weighing 16 oz. at the store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ounce - a unit of weight equal to one sixteenth of a pound

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'ounce', from Latin 'uncia' (a twelfth part).
