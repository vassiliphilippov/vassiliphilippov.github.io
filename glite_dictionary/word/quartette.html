quartette

1. NOUN

/ kwɔrˈtɛt / (AmE), / kwɔːˈtɛt / (BrE)
a musical composition written for four performers or instruments
composition for four performers

Examples

The string quartette is one of Beethoven's most celebrated compositions.
She composed a beautiful quartette for cello, violin, viola, and piano.
Tonight's concert features a quartette performed by talented young musicians.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quartet - a musical composition for four performers; standard spelling variant

Etymology

From Italian 'quartetto', from 'quattro' meaning 'four'.
