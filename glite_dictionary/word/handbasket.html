handbasket

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændˌbæskɪt / (AmE), / ˈhændˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
A small basket with handles, typically made of woven materials, carried in the hand.
small basket with handles

Examples

She carried her groceries home in a handbasket.
The flowers were arranged beautifully inside a woven handbasket.
He set down the handbasket filled with fresh apples on the table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basket - a container made of interwoven material
