West Berliner

1. NOUN

/ wɛst bɚˈlɪnɚ / (AmE), / wɛst bəˈlɪnə / (BrE)
a person who is or was an inhabitant of West Berlin, especially during the period before German reunification
inhabitant of West Berlin

Examples

During the Cold War, life for a West Berliner was very different from that in the East of the city.
My grandmother was a West Berliner who witnessed the fall of the Berlin Wall.
The unique culture of the West Berliner community still influences the city's identity today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Berliner - an inhabitant of Berlin

Etymology

Formed from 'West Berlin' and the suffix '-er' denoting an inhabitant.
