drawnwork

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɔnˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːnˌwɜːk / (BrE)
ornamental needlework in which threads are drawn out of the fabric to create lacelike patterns
lace-like ornamental needlework

Examples

Her grandmother taught her how to create drawnwork on fine linen napkins.
The intricate drawnwork on the tablecloth was admired by all the guests.
She spent hours perfecting her drawnwork technique for the new exhibit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: needlework - the craft of sewing or embroidery using a needle
