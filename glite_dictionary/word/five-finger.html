five-finger

1. NOUN

/ faɪv ˈfɪŋɡɚ / (AmE), / faɪv ˈfɪŋɡə / (BrE)
any of various plants, especially in temperate regions, with five-petaled flowers; sometimes believed to have medicinal properties
plant with five-petaled flowers

Examples

The field was dotted with five-finger and other wildflowers.
Her grandmother used five-finger in herbal remedies for colds.
The five-finger is easy to identify by its distinctive five-petaled blossom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cinquefoil - a plant of the genus Potentilla, also called five-finger
