fraternally

1. ADV

/ frəˈtɜrnəli / (AmE), / frəˈtɜːnəli / (BrE)
in a brotherly or friendly manner; like a brother
in a brotherly way

Examples

Despite their differences, the two leaders greeted each other fraternally at the summit.
Delegates from different countries worked together fraternally to find a solution.
He extended his hand fraternally to welcome the new member.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brotherly - showing affection as a brother would

Etymology

From Latin fraternalis (brotherly), from frater (brother).
