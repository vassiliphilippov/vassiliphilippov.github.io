furthermost

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɝːðərˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈfɜːðəməʊst / (BrE)
at the greatest distance in space, time, or order; most remote or farthest.
most distant; farthest

Examples

The explorers reached the furthermost point of the island before turning back.
She stored her old letters in the furthermost drawer of the desk.
At the furthermost edge of the forest, the trees thinned out and revealed open fields.

Synset Examples

had traveled to the farthest frontier
don't go beyond the farthermost (or furthermost) tree
explored the furthest reaches of space
the utmost tip of the peninsula

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farthest - at the greatest distance; most remote
  • Synonym: furthest - at the greatest distance; most distant

Etymology

Formed from 'further' (comparative of 'far') + 'most' (superlative suffix).
