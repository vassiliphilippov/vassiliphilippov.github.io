Neckar

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɛkɑr / (AmE), / ˈnɛkaː / (BrE)
a river in southwestern Germany that rises in the Black Forest and flows north to join the Rhine
river in Germany, tributary of the Rhine

Examples

The Neckar winds its way through picturesque towns such as Heidelberg.
Boats sailed up the Neckar carrying goods to nearby cities.
The Black Forest is the source of the Neckar river.
