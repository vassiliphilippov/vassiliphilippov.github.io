cliquishness

1. NOUN

/ ˈkliːkɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈklɪkɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
the tendency to associate exclusively with a small, select group and avoid others
tendency to form exclusive groups

Examples

The cliquishness of the group made it difficult for new members to fit in.
Many criticized the corporate culture for its cliquishness and lack of diversity.
Her cliquishness was apparent at every social event she attended.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exclusiveness - the state or quality of being limited to a select group
  • Antonym: openness - willingness to include others or be accessible

Etymology

From 'cliquish' + '-ness'; 'cliquish' from French 'clique' (a small exclusive group).
