dilly-dallier

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪli ˌdæliər / (AmE), / ˈdɪli ˌdæliə / (BrE)
a person who wastes time or delays because of indecision or lack of urgency
person who wastes time or delays
informal

Examples

Stop being such a dilly-dallier and make a decision already.
There's no room for dilly-dalliers on a busy construction site.
As a notorious dilly-dallier, he often missed important deadlines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: procrastinator - someone who delays or postpones tasks
  • Synonym: time-waster - someone who wastes time
  • Antonym: go-getter - an energetic, determined person

Etymology

From 'dilly-dally' (to waste time), a reduplication of 'dally'.
