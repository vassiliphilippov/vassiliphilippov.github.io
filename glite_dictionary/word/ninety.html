ninety

1. NUM

/ ˈnaɪnti / (AmE), / ˈnaɪnti / (BrE)
the number 90; the number equal to ten times nine
the number 90

Examples

There are ninety students enrolled in the course this semester.
He celebrated his ninety birthday with his family and friends.
The temperature reached ninety degrees Fahrenheit by noon.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eighty - the number 80
  • Degree Higher: hundred - the number 100

Etymology

From Old English nigontig, from nigon (nine) + -tig (group of ten).
