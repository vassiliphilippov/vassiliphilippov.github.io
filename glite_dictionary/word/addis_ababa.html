Addis Ababa

1. PROPN

/ ˌædɪs əˈbɑbə / (AmE), / ˌædɪs əˈbɑːbə / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Ethiopia, located in the central part of the country
capital city of Ethiopia

Examples

She spent two years living in Addis Ababa for her job at the embassy.
The conference on African development was held in Addis Ababa last spring.
Many visitors are fascinated by the vibrant culture and history of Addis Ababa.

Etymology

From Amharic 'Addis Abäba' meaning 'new flower'.
