lymphatic

1. ADJ

/ lɪmˈfætɪk / (AmE), / lɪmˈfætɪk / (BrE)
relating to, containing, or produced by lymph or the lymphatic system
relating to lymph or lymphatic system
medical

Examples

The lymphatic system plays a crucial role in defending the body against infections.
Swelling can occur due to lymphatic obstruction or malfunction.
Specialists examined the lymphatic tissue for signs of disease.

Etymology

From Latin lympha (water, clear fluid) + -atic (forming adjectives).
