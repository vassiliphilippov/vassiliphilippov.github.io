bismuthal

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɪzməθəl / (AmE), / ˈbɪzmʊθəl / (BrE)
relating to or containing bismuth, a chemical element
relating to bismuth (element)
medical

Examples

The chemist analyzed the bismuthal alloy for impurities.
Patients were given a bismuthal compound to treat their infection.
A bismuthal coating was applied to the metal to prevent corrosion.

Related Terms

Etymology

From bismuth (the element) + -al, a suffix forming adjectives.
