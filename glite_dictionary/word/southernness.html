southernness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌðərnnəs / (AmE), / ˈsʌðənnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being situated in the south or having southern characteristics
quality of being southern

Examples

The southernness of the city makes it warmer throughout the year.
You can sense a certain southernness in her accent and hospitality.
The region's southernness is reflected in its food and traditions.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: northerliness - the quality or state of being northern
  • Degree Lower: easternness - the quality or state of being eastern
  • Synonym: southern - situated in or relating to the south
