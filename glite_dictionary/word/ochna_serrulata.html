Ochna serrulata

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑk.nə ˌsɛr.jʊˈleɪ.tə / (AmE), / ˈɒk.nə ˌsɛr.jʊˈlɑː.tə / (BrE)
A species of evergreen shrub native to southern Africa, with narrow, glossy leaves and yellow flowers, commonly known as the Mickey Mouse plant.
evergreen shrub with yellow flowers; Mickey Mouse plant

Examples

The Ochna serrulata in our garden attracts many butterflies with its bright yellow flowers.
Gardeners often plant Ochna serrulata for its ornamental value and glossy foliage.
During spring, the Ochna serrulata bursts into distinctive yellow blooms followed by red and black fruits.

Etymology

From Latin genus name 'Ochna' and species name 'serrulata' meaning 'finely serrated', referring to the leaf margins.
