Chimariko

1. NOUN

/ tʃɪˈmɑːrɪkoʊ / (AmE), / tʃɪˈmɑːrɪkəʊ / (BrE)
the extinct Hokan language formerly spoken by the Chimariko people of northern California.
extinct Hokan language of northern California

Examples

She studied the grammar of Chimariko for her dissertation on Native American languages.
Recordings of Chimariko are rare, making language revitalization difficult.
Linguists have compared Chimariko with other Hokan languages to reconstruct elements of the language family.

Etymology

From the name of the Chimariko people of northern California.

Examples
