Canara

1. NOUN

/ kəˈnɑrə / (AmE), / kəˈnɑːrə / (BrE)
a historical region on the southwestern coast of India, known for its distinct cultural and linguistic heritage.
historical region of southwestern India

Examples

The coastal cuisine of Canara is famous for its use of seafood and coconut.
Many ancient temples in Canara reflect the region's unique architectural style.
He spent several years researching traditional music in Canara for his thesis.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Canara', derived from local Kannada language 'Karāvalli' meaning 'coast'.
