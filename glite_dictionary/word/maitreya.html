Maitreya

1. PROPN

/ maɪˈtreɪ.ə / (AmE), / maɪˈtreɪ.ə / (BrE)
In Buddhist tradition, the future Buddha to appear on Earth, expected to achieve complete enlightenment and teach the pure Dharma.
the future Buddha in Buddhist tradition

Examples

Many Buddhists believe that Maitreya will descend to earth in the distant future to renew the Dharma.
Statues of Maitreya are commonly found in temples across East Asia.
The prophecy of Maitreya inspires hope for an era of peace and enlightenment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Buddha - enlightened being in Buddhism

Etymology

From Sanskrit मैत्रेय (Maitreya), meaning 'friendly' or 'the compassionate one'.
