pearlescent

1. ADJ

/ pɜrˈlɛsənt / (AmE), / pɜːˈlɛsənt / (BrE)
having a lustrous, iridescent appearance like that of a pearl, showing changing rainbow-like colors when viewed from different angles
having a pearl-like, iridescent sheen

Examples

The car's pearlescent paint shimmered beautifully under the sunlight.
She wore a dress with a subtle pearlescent finish that caught the eye.
The artist used a pearlescent glaze to add depth and shine to the ceramic vase.

Synset Examples

an iridescent oil slick
nacreous (or pearlescent) clouds looking like mother-of-pearl
a milky opalescent (or opaline) luster

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iridescent - showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles
  • Synonym: opalescent - showing varying colors like an opal
  • Antonym: matte - having a dull, non-reflective surface

Etymology

From 'pearl' + '-escent', modeled after words like 'iridescent'.
