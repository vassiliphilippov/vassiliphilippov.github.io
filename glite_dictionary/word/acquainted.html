acquainted

1. ADJ

/ əˈkweɪntɪd / (AmE), / əˈkweɪntɪd / (BrE)
having some knowledge or experience of someone or something; familiar through experience or association
having knowledge of; familiar with

Examples

She is well acquainted with local customs after living there for a year.
I'm only acquainted with the basics of the software, not an expert.
Through their mutual friend, they became acquainted and eventually started working together.

Synset Examples

they were acquainted
fully acquainted with the facts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: familiar - well-known from long or close association; well acquainted
  • Antonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness in general; unacquainted with

Etymology

Past participle of 'acquaint,' from Old French 'acointer', from Latin 'accognitare' (to make known).
