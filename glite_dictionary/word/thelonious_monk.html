Thelonious Monk

1. PROPN

/ θəˈloʊniəs mʌŋk / (AmE), / θəˈləʊniəs mʌŋk / (BrE)
(1917–1982) A prominent American jazz pianist and composer known for his influential role in the development of the bebop style.
American jazz pianist and composer

Examples

Thelonious Monk was renowned for his unique improvisational style and innovative compositions.
Jazz enthusiasts often cite Thelonious Monk as a major influence on modern jazz pianists.
The album 'Brilliant Corners' showcases the genius of Thelonious Monk at the height of his career.
