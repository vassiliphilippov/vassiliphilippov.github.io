OH

1. NOUN

/ oʊ eɪtʃ / (AmE), / əʊ eɪtʃ / (BrE)
The official postal abbreviation for the U.S. state of Ohio, used especially in mailing addresses and official documents.
postal abbreviation for Ohio (U.S. state)

Examples

Her new address is in Cleveland, OH, and she loves living near Lake Erie.
Please send all forms to the main office in Columbus, OH.
The weather in OH can change quickly, especially in the spring.
