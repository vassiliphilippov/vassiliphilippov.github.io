vigorously

1. ADV

/ ˈvɪgərəsli / (AmE), / ˈvɪɡərəsli / (BrE)
In a way that involves physical strength, effort, or energy; forcefully or strongly.
with energy or force

Examples

She scrubbed the floor vigorously until it sparkled.
The lawyer argued vigorously on behalf of her client.
The trees were shaken vigorously by the strong wind.

Synset Examples

he defended his ideas vigorously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: energetically - in an energetic manner

Etymology

From 'vigorous', which ultimately derives from Latin 'vigorem' (liveliness, activity).
