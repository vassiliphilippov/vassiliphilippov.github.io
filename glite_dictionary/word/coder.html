coder

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊdər / (AmE), / ˈkəʊdə / (BrE)
a person who writes and develops computer programs or software code
someone who writes computer code

Examples

The company is looking to hire a skilled coder to develop their new app.
As a freelance coder, she works on various programming projects from home.
He started as a coder but quickly advanced to lead software architect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: programmer - a person who writes computer programs

Etymology

From 'code' (as in computer code) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
