eightieth

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪtiəθ / (AmE), / ˈeɪtiɪθ / (BrE)
being number eighty in a sequence; 80th.
ordinal number for 80

Examples

She finished in eightieth place in the marathon.
This is the eightieth anniversary of the school's foundation.
He celebrated his eightieth birthday surrounded by family and friends.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: seventieth - the ordinal number for 70
  • Degree Higher: ninetieth - the ordinal number for 90

Etymology

From 'eight' + '-ieth', ordinal suffix.

2. NOUN

/ ˈeɪtiəθ / (AmE), / ˈeɪtiəθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number corresponding to eighty; being number 80 in a sequence
80th in a sequence; ordinal number

Examples

She finished in eightieth place out of over a thousand runners.
The eightieth anniversary of the founding of the school was celebrated with a special ceremony.
My grandfather’s eightieth birthday was a huge family event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eighty - the cardinal number 80
  • Degree Lower: seventieth - the ordinal number for 70th position
  • Degree Higher: ninetieth - the ordinal number for 90th position

Etymology

Formed from 'eighty' with the ordinal suffix '-th'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13871709-n
Synset POS: n
