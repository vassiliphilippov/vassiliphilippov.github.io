guardswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdzˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is a member of a military unit known as 'the guard' or 'guards'
female member of a military guard unit

Examples

The first guardswoman completed her training and joined the royal ceremony.
Every guardswoman in the regiment must meet the same fitness standards as her male colleagues.
She took great pride in serving as a guardswoman at the national memorial.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guardsman - a male or generic member of a military guard unit

Etymology

From 'guard' + 'woman', modeled after 'guardsman'.
