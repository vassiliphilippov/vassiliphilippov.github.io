frenziedly

1. ADV

/ ˈfrɛn.zid.li / (AmE), / ˈfrɛn.zɪd.li / (BrE)
In a wild, frantic, or violently agitated manner.
in a wild or frantic manner

Examples

They packed their suitcases frenziedly as the storm approached.
The chef worked frenziedly to prepare meals for all the guests.
Fans cheered frenziedly when their team scored the winning goal.

Synset Examples

we rehearsed frenziedly the last few days before the premiere

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frantically - in a hurried, panicked, or desperate way
  • Antonym: calmly - in a relaxed or peaceful way

Etymology

From 'frenzied' (from Old French 'frenesie', from Latin 'phrenesis') + -ly.
