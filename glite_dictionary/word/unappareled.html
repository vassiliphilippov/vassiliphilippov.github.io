unappareled

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈpær.əld / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈpær.əld / (BrE)
not wearing any clothing; naked or unclothed.
not wearing clothing; unclothed

Examples

In the painting, the goddess stands unappareled amidst blooming flowers.
She felt a sense of freedom as she walked unappareled into the cool water.
The statue depicted a figure unappareled and unashamed, celebrating the human form.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clothed - wearing clothes
  • Synonym: naked - not wearing any clothes; bare

Etymology

From un- (not) + appareled (dressed, clothed), deriving from 'apparel'.
