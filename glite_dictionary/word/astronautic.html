astronautic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæstrəˈnɔtɪk / (AmE), / ˌæstrəˈnɔːtɪk / (BrE)
relating to astronauts or the science and technology of space travel.
pertaining to astronauts or space travel

Examples

The astronautic program requires highly trained engineers and scientists.
She specializes in astronautic engineering, focusing on spacecraft design.
Recent advances in astronautic research have made deep space missions more feasible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astronautical - relating to astronauts or astronautics

Etymology

From 'astronaut' + '-ic', patterned after 'aeronautic'.
