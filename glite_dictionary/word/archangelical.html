archangelical

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑːrˌkeɪnˈdʒɛlɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɑːkˌeɪnˈdʒɛlɪkəl / (BrE)
of or relating to archangels; resembling or characteristic of an archangel.
relating to archangels

Examples

Her painting possessed an archangelical beauty that captivated everyone in the chapel.
He spoke with an archangelical authority, inspiring awe among his listeners.
The choir's voices soared in archangelical harmony throughout the cathedral.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: angelic - resembling or characteristic of angels

Etymology

From archangel + -ical, derived from Greek 'archangelos' (chief angel) and Latin '-icalis'.
