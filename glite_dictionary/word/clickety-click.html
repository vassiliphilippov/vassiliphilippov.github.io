clickety-click

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɪk.ə.ti ˌklɪk / (AmE), / ˈklɪk.ɪ.ti ˌklɪk / (BrE)
A fast and rhythmic series of clicking or clacking sounds, often produced by machinery or repetitive actions.
fast, rhythmic clicking sound

Examples

The old train rumbled down the tracks with a constant clickety-click as its wheels spun.
She loved the comforting clickety-click of knitting needles late at night.
The clickety-click from the typewriter filled the quiet office.

Synset Examples

the clickety-clack of the typewriters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clickety-clack - a fast, rhythmic clacking sound

Etymology

Reduplicative formation from 'click' imitating the sound.
