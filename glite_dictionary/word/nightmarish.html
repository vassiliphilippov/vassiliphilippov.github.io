nightmarish

1. ADJ

/ ˈnaɪtˌmɛrɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtˌmeərɪʃ / (BrE)
extremely frightening or unpleasant, like a nightmare; terrifying or distressing in nature
extremely frightening or unpleasant

Examples

The movie's nightmarish scenes left the audience too frightened to sleep.
She described her experience in the hospital as a nightmarish ordeal she would never forget.
The city was plunged into a nightmarish chaos after the storm hit without warning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrifying - causing intense fear
  • Antonym: pleasant - giving a sense of happy satisfaction

Etymology

Formed from 'nightmare' + suffix '-ish', meaning 'like a nightmare'.
