constitutionalist

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːn.stɪˈtuː.ʃə.nə.lɪst / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.stɪˈtjuː.ʃə.nə.lɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates constitutional government
supporter of constitutional government

Examples

As a proud constitutionalist, she believes in the limits of government power defined by law.
The new party leader is known as a staunch constitutionalist who values legal frameworks.
Historically, constitutionalists challenged absolute monarchies throughout Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: democrat - a person who supports democracy or democratic government
  • Antonym: authoritarian - a person supporting or enforcing strict obedience to authority

Etymology

From constitution + -alist, indicating one who supports a system or principle.
