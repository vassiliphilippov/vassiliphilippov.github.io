enlivening

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈlaɪvənɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈlaɪvənɪŋ / (BrE)
Making something more lively, spirited, or full of energy.
making lively or spirited

Examples

The enlivening music filled the room with energy and excitement.
She found the fresh mountain air deeply enlivening after days spent in the city.
His jokes had an enlivening effect on the otherwise serious meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invigorating - making one feel stronger or more active
  • Antonym: dull - lacking interest or excitement

Etymology

From the verb 'enliven' + -ing, derived from 'enliven' (to make lively).
