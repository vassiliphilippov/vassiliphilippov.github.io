Falco sparverius

1. NOUN

/ ˈfælkoʊ spɑrˈvɪəriəs / (AmE), / ˈfalkəʊ spɑːˈvɪəriəs / (BrE)
the scientific name for the American kestrel, a small North American falcon
scientific name for the American kestrel

Examples

The Falco sparverius is the smallest falcon found in North America.
Researchers tracked the migration patterns of Falco sparverius across several states.
Birdwatchers were excited to spot a Falco sparverius nesting on the old barn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: American kestrel - a small North American falcon known for its colorful plumage

Etymology

From Latin: 'falco' (falcon), 'sparverius' (relating to sparrows, referencing the bird's prey).
