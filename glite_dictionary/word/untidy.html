untidy

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtaɪdi / (AmE), / ʌnˈtaɪdi / (BrE)
not neat, orderly, or well arranged; messy or disorganized.
not neat or orderly

Examples

His desk was always untidy, with papers scattered everywhere.
She wore her hair in an untidy bun, not caring about appearances.
The children left their toys in an untidy pile on the floor.

Synset Examples

careless and untidy in her personal habits
an untidy living room
untidy and casual about money

Related Terms

  • Synonym: messy - untidy or disorganized
  • Antonym: tidy - neat and well arranged

Etymology

Prefix 'un-' (not) + 'tidy' (Neat, orderly).
