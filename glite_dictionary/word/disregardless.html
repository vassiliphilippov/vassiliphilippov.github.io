disregardless

1. ADV

/ ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑrdləs / (AmE), / ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːdləs / (BrE)
(informal, nonstandard) Without paying attention to drawbacks or opposition; regardless.
nonstandard form of 'regardless', without consideration of drawbacks
informal

Examples

She continued with her plans disregardless of her friends’ opinions.
He made the announcement disregardless of the looming controversy.
The team pressed on disregardless of the bad weather conditions.

Synset Examples

he carried on regardless of the difficulties

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regardless - without paying attention to difficulties or obstacles

Etymology

Blend of 'dis-' (a prefix) + 'regardless', probably modeled after 'irregardless'.
