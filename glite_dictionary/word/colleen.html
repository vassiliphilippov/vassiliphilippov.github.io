colleen

1. NOUN

/ kəˈliːn / (AmE), / kɒˈliːn / (BrE)
An Irish girl or young woman.
Irish girl or young woman

Examples

The old storyteller spoke fondly of the days when he courted a beautiful colleen from Galway.
She danced with the grace and laughter of a true Irish colleen.
Many songs and poems celebrate the charm of the Irish colleen.

Etymology

From Irish Gaelic 'cailín' meaning 'girl' or 'young woman'.
