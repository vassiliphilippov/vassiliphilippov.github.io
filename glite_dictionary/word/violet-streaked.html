violet-streaked

1. ADJ

/ ˈvaɪələt striːkt / (AmE), / ˈvaɪələt striːkt / (BrE)
Marked with streaks or lines of violet color.
marked with violet-colored streaks

Examples

The artist painted a violet-streaked sky to capture the beauty of sunset.
She wore a dress with a violet-streaked pattern that stood out in the crowd.
The violet-streaked petals of the flower added a touch of elegance to the bouquet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: striped - having stripes or bands of color
