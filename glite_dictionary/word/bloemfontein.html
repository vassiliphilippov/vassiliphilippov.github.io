Bloemfontein

1. PROPN

/ bloʊmˈfɒnteɪn / (AmE), / bluːmˈfɒnteɪn / (BrE)
the judicial capital of South Africa and the seat of the country's Supreme Court.
judicial capital of South Africa

Examples

The Supreme Court of Appeal is located in Bloemfontein, making it a key legal center.
During her trip to South Africa, she visited Bloemfontein to learn about the country's judicial system.
Bloemfontein is also known for its beautiful gardens and historical architecture.

Etymology

From Afrikaans Bloemfontein, literally 'fountain of flowers'.
