aptitudinal

1. ADJ

/ ˌæp.tɪˈtjuː.dɪn.əl / (AmE), / ˌæp.tɪˈtjuː.dɪ.nəl / (BrE)
relating to or based on aptitude; concerned with natural ability or skill, especially as measured by tests.
relating to aptitude or natural ability

Examples

The school administered several aptitudinal tests to assess the students' strengths.
Researchers found a strong correlation between aptitudinal scores and academic achievement.
The company uses aptitudinal assessments during the hiring process to identify potential talent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aptitude - natural ability or talent

Etymology

From 'aptitude' + '-al', formed in analogy with other adjectives.
