Berliner

1. NOUN

/ bɛrˈlɪnər / (AmE), / bɜːˈlɪːnə / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from Berlin, the capital city of Germany.
inhabitant of Berlin

Examples

She met a friendly Berliner who showed her around the city.
Many Berliners are proud of their city's vibrant culture.
As a Berliner, he loves cycling through the Tiergarten.

Etymology

From German 'Berliner', meaning a person from Berlin.
