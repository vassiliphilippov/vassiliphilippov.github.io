langbeinite

1. NOUN

/ ˌlæŋˈbaɪnaɪt / (AmE), / ˌlæŋˈbaɪnaɪt / (BrE)
a mineral consisting of potassium magnesium double sulphate, used especially as a fertilizer
potassium magnesium sulphate mineral

Examples

The soil was enriched with langbeinite to improve magnesium and potassium content.
Farmers often prefer langbeinite as a balanced mineral fertilizer for certain crops.
Deposits of langbeinite are found in evaporite formations worldwide.

Etymology

Named after German chemist A. Langbein + -ite, used for mineral names.
