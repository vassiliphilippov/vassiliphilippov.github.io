pogonion

1. NOUN

/ poʊˈɡoʊniən / (AmE), / pɒˈɡəʊniən / (BrE)
the craniometric point that is the most forward-projecting point on the anterior surface of the chin
forward-most point on the chin
medical

Examples

In orthodontics, the pogonion is used as a reference point for analyzing facial structure.
The surgeon marked the location of the pogonion before the procedure.
On the X-ray, the pogonion appeared slightly asymmetrical.

Etymology

From Greek 'pōgōnion', diminutive of 'pōgōn' meaning 'beard'.
