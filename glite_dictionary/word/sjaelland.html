Sjaelland

1. PROPN

/ ˈsjɛˌlɑnd / (AmE), / ˈsjɛˌlænd / (BrE)
the largest island of Denmark, located in the east and home to the capital city, Copenhagen
the largest island of Denmark

Examples

The train journey across Sjaelland offers stunning views of the Danish countryside.
Tourists often visit Sjaelland to experience Copenhagen's vibrant culture.
After leaving Jutland, they traveled to Sjaelland for a weekend getaway.

Etymology

Danish, from Old Danish ‘Siâland’, probably meaning ‘sea land’.
