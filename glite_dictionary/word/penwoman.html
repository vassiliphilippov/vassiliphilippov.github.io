penwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛnˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈpɛnˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A female journalist or writer, especially one who writes professionally for newspapers or magazines.
female journalist or writer
informal

Examples

The magazine hired a well-known penwoman to cover the arts section.
As a talented penwoman, she quickly gained a loyal readership.
The award recognized the achievements of an outstanding penwoman in investigative journalism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: penman - a male journalist or writer

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'penman', from 'pen' + 'woman'.
