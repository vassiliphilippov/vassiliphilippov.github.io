surtout

1. NOUN

/ sɚˈtu / (AmE), / sɜːˈtuː / (BrE)
a man's overcoat, particularly in the style of a frock coat, popular in the 18th and 19th centuries
man's overcoat in frock coat style

Examples

He wore a dark surtout over his suit as he walked through the snow.
In Victorian novels, a surtout often signified a sense of refinement and style.
The tailor specialized in recreating period garments, including the classic surtout.

Etymology

From French 'surtout' (literally 'above all'), referring to an outer garment.
