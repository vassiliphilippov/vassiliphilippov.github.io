unwomanly

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈwʌmənli / (AmE), / ʌnˈwʌmənli / (BrE)
not characteristic of or appropriate to a woman; lacking qualities traditionally associated with women
not characteristic of a woman

Examples

The press considered her outspoken views to be rather unwomanly for her time.
He criticized her for behaving in what he called an unwomanly manner.
Her refusal to conform to traditional roles was described as unwomanly by some critics.

Synset Examples

the logical clearness of her arguments … condemned her as eccentric and unwomanly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: womanly - having qualities traditionally associated with women

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'womanly'.
