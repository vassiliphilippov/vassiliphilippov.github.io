Vancocin

1. NOUN

/ ˈvæŋkoʊsɪn / (AmE), / ˈvæŋkəʊsɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand of the antibiotic vancomycin, used to treat certain serious bacterial infections.
brand name antibiotic (vancomycin)
medical

The doctor prescribed Vancocin to treat the patient's severe infection.
