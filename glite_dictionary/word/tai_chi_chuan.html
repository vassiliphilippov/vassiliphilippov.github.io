t'ai chi chuan

1. NOUN

/ taɪ ˈtʃi tʃwɑn / (AmE), / taɪ ˈtʃi tʃwæn / (BrE)
a Chinese system of gentle, meditative physical exercise and martial art designed to promote relaxation, balance, and health
Chinese meditative exercise system

Examples

Every morning, a group gathers in the park to practice t'ai chi chuan together.
She credits t'ai chi chuan for helping her improve her balance and mental calm.
Many people start learning t'ai chi chuan as a gentle way to exercise and reduce stress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tai chi - a simplified form or alternative spelling of t'ai chi chuan

Etymology

From Chinese 太極拳 (tàijí quán), literally ‘supreme ultimate fist’.
