thornbill

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɔrnˌbɪl / (AmE), / ˈθɔːnˌbɪl / (BrE)
a small South American hummingbird with a slender, sharp-pointed bill
a South American hummingbird with a sharp bill

Examples

The thornbill hovered delicately as it fed from the vibrant flowers.
Several species of thornbill can be found in the forests of South America.
Birdwatchers were excited to spot a rare thornbill near the riverbank.
