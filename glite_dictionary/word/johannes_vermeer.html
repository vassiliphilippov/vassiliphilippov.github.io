Johannes Vermeer

1. PROPN

/ joʊˈhænəs vərˈmɪr / (AmE), / jəʊˈhanəs vəˈmɪə / (BrE)
A Dutch painter (1632–1675), celebrated for his masterful use of light and detailed domestic interior scenes.
Dutch painter renowned for light and interior scenes

Examples

Many art historians consider Johannes Vermeer a master of capturing light in his paintings.
The exhibition featured several works attributed to Johannes Vermeer, including 'The Milkmaid.'
Students studied Johannes Vermeer to understand the techniques of 17th-century Dutch art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Rembrandt - Another renowned Dutch painter of the 17th century
  • Synonym: Girl with a Pearl Earring - A famous painting by Vermeer
