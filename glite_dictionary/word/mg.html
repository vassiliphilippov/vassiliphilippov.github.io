Mg

1. NOUN

/ ɛm dʒi / (AmE), / ɛm dʒi / (BrE)
the symbol for magnesium, a light, silver-white metallic element that burns with a brilliant white flame and is found in minerals such as magnesite and dolomite
chemical symbol for magnesium

Examples

The periodic table lists Mg as the symbol for magnesium.
Doctors may prescribe supplements that contain Mg to treat deficiencies.
The presence of Mg in mineral water contributes to its taste and health benefits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magnesium - a light, silver-white metallic element, symbol Mg

Etymology

Chemical symbol formed from the first two letters of 'magnesium'.
