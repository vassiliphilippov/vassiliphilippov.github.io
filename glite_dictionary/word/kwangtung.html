Kwangtung

1. PROPN

/ kwɑŋˈtuŋ / (AmE), / kwæŋˈtʊŋ / (BrE)
An older English name for Guangdong, a province in southern China.
historical name for Guangdong province, China

Examples

In the early 1900s, many immigrants from Kwangtung settled in California.
The cuisine of Kwangtung is renowned for its subtle flavors and diverse ingredients.
His ancestors hailed from Kwangtung province in southern China.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Guangdong - a province in southern China; the modern name of Kwangtung

Etymology

From Chinese 廣東 (Guǎngdōng), formerly romanized as 'Kwangtung' under the Wade-Giles system.
