banqueting

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæŋkwətɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbæŋkwɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of eating an elaborate meal, often accompanied by entertainment
eating an elaborate, festive meal

Examples

The grand hall was filled with nobles banqueting into the night.
They spent the weekend banqueting with family and friends at the retreat.
During the festival, the villagers enjoy banqueting and music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feasting - the act of eating and celebrating with a large meal
  • Antonym: fasting - the act of abstaining from food

Etymology

From Middle English 'banquet', from Old French 'banquet' (feast), diminutive of 'banque' (bench, table).
