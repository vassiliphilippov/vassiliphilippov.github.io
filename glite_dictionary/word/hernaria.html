Hernaria

1. NOUN

/ hərˈnɛriə / (AmE), / həːˈnɛːriə / (BrE)
A genus of low-growing Old World herbs with small bright green leaves, commonly found in dry habitats.
genus of low-growing Old World herbs

Examples

Hernaria is often used as ground cover in dry, sandy soils.
Some herbalists use extracts from Hernaria for traditional remedies.
Botanists observed that Hernaria thrives in Mediterranean climates.

Etymology

From Latin 'Hernaria', the name of the plant genus.
