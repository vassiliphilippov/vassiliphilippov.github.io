crownbeard

1. NOUN

/ ˈkraʊnˌbɪrd / (AmE), / ˈkraʊnˌbɪəd / (BrE)
Any plant of the genus Verbesina, characterized by clustered white or yellow flower heads.
plant of the genus Verbesina

Examples

The fields in late summer were dotted with blooming crownbeard and goldenrod.
Botanists identified the unfamiliar plant as a species of crownbeard.
Many pollinators are attracted to the yellow flowers of crownbeard.
