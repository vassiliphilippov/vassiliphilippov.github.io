San Francisco

1. PROPN

/ sæn frənˈsɪskoʊ / (AmE), / sæn frənˈsɪskəʊ / (BrE)
A major city and port in western California, known for its Golden Gate Bridge and fine natural harbor.
major city in western California, USA

Examples

Many tourists visit San Francisco each year to see the Golden Gate Bridge and ride the famous cable cars.
After moving to San Francisco, she quickly adapted to the city's vibrant culture.
The fog rolling over San Francisco Bay creates a beautiful but chilly atmosphere.

Etymology

Spanish for 'Saint Francis,' named after St. Francis of Assisi.
