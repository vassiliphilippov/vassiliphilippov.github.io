red-gray

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛdˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a grey color tinged or mixed with red.
grey tinged with red

Examples

The artist chose a red-gray background to complement the warm tones in the painting.
Her hair had turned a unique red-gray shade as she aged.
The walls were painted in a soothing red-gray hue that made the room feel cozy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gray - the color between black and white
  • Synonym: reddish - tinged with red; slightly red
