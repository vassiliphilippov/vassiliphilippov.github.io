nonfat

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɑnˌfæt / (AmE), / ˈnɒnˌfæt / (BrE)
Containing no fat or fat solids; with the fat removed.
containing no fat

Examples

She prefers nonfat yogurt with her breakfast every morning.
The cafeteria now offers nonfat milk as a healthier option for students.
He ordered a nonfat latte to reduce his calorie intake.

Synset Examples

nonfat or fat-free milk

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fat-free - Containing no fat; with all fat removed

Etymology

Formed from 'non-' (not) + 'fat'.
