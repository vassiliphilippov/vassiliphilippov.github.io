enlivener

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈlaɪvənɚ / (AmE), / ɪnˈlaɪvənə / (BrE)
a person or thing that gives energy, life, or increased spirit to something
something that gives energy or life

Examples

Music was the real enlivener of the otherwise dull gathering.
The early morning sun acted as an enlivener for the sleepy town.
Her enthusiastic speech was an enlivener for the entire team.

Synset Examples

the soul is the quickener of the body

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invigorator - a person or thing that makes something stronger or more lively

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'enliven' (from 'en-' + 'liven') with the agentive suffix '-er'.
