fiddle-faddle

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪdəl ˌfædəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪd(ə)l ˌfæd(ə)l / (BrE)
silly talk or activity; trivial nonsense
trivial nonsense or silliness

Examples

Stop worrying about all that fiddle-faddle and focus on what's important.
Her explanation was just a lot of fiddle-faddle designed to distract us.
He dismissed their concerns as mere fiddle-faddle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - words or ideas that are silly or have no meaning
  • Synonym: rubbish - foolish or worthless talk or ideas

Etymology

Reduplicative formation based on 'fiddle' (possibly from the instrument) and 'faddle' (meaning trifle or nonsense).
