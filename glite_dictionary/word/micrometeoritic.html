micrometeoritic

1. ADJ

/ ˌmaɪ.kroʊˌmiː.ti.əˈrɪ.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.krəʊˌmiː.ti.əˈrɪ.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of micrometeoroids, especially their effects or presence
relating to micrometeoroids

Examples

The lunar soil contains a significant amount of micrometeoritic material deposited over billions of years.
Scientists studied the micrometeoritic impact on spacecraft surfaces to design better shielding.
The micrometeoritic flux in near-Earth orbit poses a risk to satellites and astronauts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: micrometeoroid - a tiny meteoroid, typically smaller than a grain of sand

Etymology

From micro- (small) + meteoritic (relating to meteorites)
