multiversity

1. NOUN

/ ˌmʌltɪˈvɜrsəti / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈvɜːsəti / (BrE)
A university system consisting of several campuses, colleges, and research centers, often serving a large and diverse student body.
large university system with multiple campuses

Examples

The state has invested heavily in its new multiversity to accommodate the growing student population.
As a multiversity, the institution supports collaboration between various research centers and colleges.
Many modern universities are adopting a multiversity model to offer a wider range of programs and services.

Etymology

Blend of 'multi-' (many) and 'university'.
