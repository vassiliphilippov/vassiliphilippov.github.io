ninepin

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪnpɪn / (AmE), / ˈnaɪnpɪn / (BrE)
a bowling pin used in the game of ninepins, or in skittles (especially in England)
a pin used in ninepins or skittles

Examples

He knocked over every ninepin with his first throw.
The old English inn still keeps a set of wooden ninepins for guests to play skittles.
A single ninepin remained standing after the ball rolled past.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skittle - a pin used in the game of skittles
