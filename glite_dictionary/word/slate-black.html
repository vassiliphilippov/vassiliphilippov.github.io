slate-black

1. ADJ

/ sleɪt blæk / (AmE), / sleɪt blak / (BrE)
Of a black color tinged with slate-grey; black with a hint of blue-grey.
black tinged with slate-grey

Examples

His coat was a beautiful slate-black that shimmered in the light.
The artist chose a slate-black background to make the bright colors pop.
A slate-black bird perched quietly on the windowsill.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: jet-black - completely black; pure black color
  • Synonym: slate-grey - grey with a bluish tint, resembling slate
