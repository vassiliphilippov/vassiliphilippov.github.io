self-heal

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛlfˌhil / (AmE), / ˈsɛlfˌhiːl / (BrE)
a low-growing perennial plant (Prunella vulgaris) with blue-violet flowers, traditionally believed to have medicinal properties
a medicinal perennial plant (Prunella vulgaris)

Examples

Many herbalists use self-heal in remedies for sore throats and wounds.
The self-heal covered the meadow with its delicate purple flowers in early summer.
She identified the wildflower as self-heal, known for its supposed healing powers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prunella - another name for the self-heal plant
