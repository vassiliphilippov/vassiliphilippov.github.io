quarreler

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɔrələr / (AmE), / ˈkwɒrələ / (BrE)
A person who quarrels or frequently engages in arguments or disputes.
person who quarrels or argues

Examples

The meeting quickly devolved into chaos thanks to one persistent quarreler in the group.
He was known as a habitual quarreler, always looking for faults in others' opinions.
Unlike his brother, John was never a quarreler and preferred calm discussions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arguer - a person who argues or reasons
  • Antonym: peacemaker - a person who attempts to make peace

Etymology

From 'quarrel' (to argue), from Old French 'quarrel', from Latin 'querela' (complaint).
