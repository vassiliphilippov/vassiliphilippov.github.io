sedgy

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛdʒi / (AmE), / ˈsɛdʒi / (BrE)
(of land) covered with or full of sedges, grasslike marsh plants.
covered with sedge plants

Examples

The hikers trudged through the sedgy meadows alongside the river.
The pond was surrounded by sedgy banks teeming with wildlife.
After heavy rains, the field became particularly sedgy and difficult to cross.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sedge - a grasslike plant of wet ground or marshes

Etymology

From 'sedge' + '-y', denoting 'full of or characterized by'.
