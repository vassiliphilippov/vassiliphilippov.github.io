Antoine Laurent de Jussieu

1. PROPN

/ ɑ̃ˈtwan loʁɑ̃ də ʒysjø / (AmE), / ɒ̃ˈtwan loˈʁɑ̃ də ʒyˈsjɶ / (BrE)
(1748–1836) A French botanist renowned for categorizing plants into families and developing an influential system of plant classification.
French botanist and plant classifier

Examples

Antoine Laurent de Jussieu revolutionized plant taxonomy with his classification system.
The work of Antoine Laurent de Jussieu laid the foundation for modern botanical nomenclature.
Many botanists still reference Antoine Laurent de Jussieu when discussing plant family groupings.
