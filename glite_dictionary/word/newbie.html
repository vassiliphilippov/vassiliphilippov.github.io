newbie

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːbi / (AmE), / ˈnjuːbi / (BrE)
a person who is new to a particular activity, job, or place and lacks experience in it
a newcomer with little experience
informal

Examples

When I started my new job, I felt like a total newbie during the first week.
Online forums can be intimidating places for a newbie unfamiliar with the rules.
Even as a newbie, she quickly picked up the basics of coding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: novice - a person new to a field or activity
  • Synonym: beginner - a person starting to learn a skill or activity
  • Antonym: expert - a person with extensive knowledge or ability in a field

Etymology

Informal term, originally from new + -bie, possibly influenced by earlier slang 'newie' or 'new boy'.
