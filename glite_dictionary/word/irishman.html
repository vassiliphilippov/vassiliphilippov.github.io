Irishman

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪrɪʃˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈaɪrɪʃmən / (BrE)
a man who is a native or inhabitant of Ireland
a man from Ireland

Examples

The Irishman told us stories about growing up in Dublin.
She married an Irishman she met while studying abroad.
The film follows the journey of an Irishman searching for his family history.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Irishwoman - a woman who is a native or inhabitant of Ireland
  • Synonym: Irish - a person from Ireland (gender-neutral) or anything relating to Ireland

Etymology

Formed from 'Irish' + 'man'.
