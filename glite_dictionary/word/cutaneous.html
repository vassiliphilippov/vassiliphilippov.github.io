cutaneous

1. ADJ

/ kjuˈteɪniəs / (AmE), / kjuːˈteɪnɪəs / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the skin.
of or relating to the skin
medical

Examples

The doctor examined the patient for any cutaneous reactions to the medication.
Many diseases can present with cutaneous symptoms such as rashes or blisters.
Researchers studied the effects of UV light on cutaneous tissue.

Synset Examples

cutaneous nerves
a cutaneous infection

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dermal - relating to the skin (especially the dermis)
  • Degree Lower: subcutaneous - situated or applied under the skin

Etymology

From Latin cutaneus, from cutis (skin).
