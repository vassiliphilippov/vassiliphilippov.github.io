overawed

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈɔd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərˈɔːd / (BrE)
Extremely impressed, intimidated, or subdued by a feeling of awe.
extremely impressed or intimidated by awe

Examples

The students were overawed by the size of the ancient cathedral.
She felt both excited and overawed during the award ceremony.
The children stood overawed in front of the famous actor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awed - filled with awe
  • Antonym: unimpressed - not feeling impressed or awed

Etymology

From 'overawe' (to fill with awe), 17th century; 'over-' (excessively) + 'awe'.
