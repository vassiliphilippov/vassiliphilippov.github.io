stenographic

1. ADJ

/ ˌstɛnoʊˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌstɛnəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to, using, or characteristic of stenography (shorthand writing).
relating to shorthand writing

Examples

The court reporter produced a stenographic transcript of the entire trial.
She took stenographic notes during the meeting to capture every detail.
Many official proceedings require stenographic records for accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stenography - the practice or process of writing in shorthand

Etymology

From Greek 'stenos' (narrow) + 'graphein' (to write), via 'stenography'.
