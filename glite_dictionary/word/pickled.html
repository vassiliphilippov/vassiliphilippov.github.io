pickled

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɪkəld / (AmE), / ˈpɪkld / (BrE)
(of food) preserved in vinegar, brine, or another pickling liquid.
preserved in pickling liquid

Examples

She served a platter of pickled cucumbers at the party.
The recipe calls for fresh or pickled ginger as a garnish.
I enjoy the sharp taste of pickled onions with my sandwich.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preserved - kept from spoiling, especially by chemical means
