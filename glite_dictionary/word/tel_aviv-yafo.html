Tel Aviv-Yafo

1. PROPN

/ tɛl əˌviv jɑːˈfoʊ / (AmE), / tɛl əˌviːv ˈjɑːfəʊ / (BrE)
the largest city and a major financial center of Israel, situated on the Mediterranean coast in western Israel
largest city of Israel, financial center

Examples

Many international companies have offices in Tel Aviv-Yafo due to its economic importance.
The coastline of Tel Aviv-Yafo is famous for its beautiful beaches and vibrant nightlife.
She is flying to Tel Aviv-Yafo next week to attend a business conference.

Etymology

Hebrew for 'Hill of Spring' (Tel Aviv) and the ancient port city of Jaffa (Yafo) joined in 1950.
