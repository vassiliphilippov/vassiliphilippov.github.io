wavelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈweɪvˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈweɪv.laɪk / (BrE)
having the appearance or movement of waves; resembling a wave in form or motion, often used to describe something as uneven or undulating.
resembling a wave in form or motion

Examples

The dancer's arms moved in a wavelike motion across the stage.
We admired the wavelike dunes stretching across the desert horizon.
As the wind blew, the field of grass took on a wavelike appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undulating - moving or shaped like gentle waves; having a smooth, wavy form
  • Antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface or motion
