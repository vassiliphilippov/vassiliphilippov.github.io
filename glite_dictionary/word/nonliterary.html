nonliterary

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˈlɪt̬əˌri / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈlɪtərəri / (BrE)
Not related to literature or literary style; characterized by a straightforward or factual manner, lacking literary qualities, affectation, or pedantry.
not related to literature; straightforward, factual

Examples

His style is refreshingly nonliterary, making complex ideas accessible to everyone.
The report was written in a clear, nonliterary fashion, focusing on facts and figures.
She prefers nonliterary magazines that discuss science and current events.

Synset Examples

her talk was very unliterary

Related Terms

  • Antonym: literary - relating to literature or written works with artistic qualities

Etymology

Formed by prefixing non- (not) to literary.
