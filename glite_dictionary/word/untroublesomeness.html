untroublesomeness

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌnˈtrʌb.əl.səm.nəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈtrʌb.əl.səm.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of not causing trouble, difficulty, or anxiety.
absence of trouble or anxiety

Examples

We appreciated the untroublesomeness of the new software update, which ran smoothly without any issues.
Her untroublesomeness made her a preferred roommate among her peers.
The untroublesomeness of the quiet countryside brought him much-needed relief from city life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peacefulness - the state of being peaceful or calm
  • Antonym: troublesomeness - the quality of causing difficulty or worry
