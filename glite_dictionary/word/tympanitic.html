tympanitic

1. ADJ

/ ˌtɪmpəˈnɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɪmpəˈnɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of tympanites; marked by a drumlike distension of the abdomen due to gas
relating to drumlike abdominal distension
medical

Examples

The patient's abdomen was noted to be tympanitic on physical examination.
A tympanitic sound was heard when the doctor percussed the swollen belly.
Severe bloating and a tympanitic abdomen may indicate the presence of intestinal obstruction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tympanites - swelling or distension of the abdomen due to gas

Etymology

From Greek tympanitēs (suffering from swelling like a drum).
