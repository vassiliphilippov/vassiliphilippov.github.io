DoI

1. PROPN

/ di oʊ aɪ / (AmE), / diː əʊ aɪ / (BrE)
abbreviation for the United States Department of the Interior, the federal department responsible for conservation and management of natural resources, established in 1849.
US Department of the Interior (abbreviation)
american

Examples

The DoI announced new regulations to protect endangered species across the country.
Many parks and public lands are managed by the DoI.
