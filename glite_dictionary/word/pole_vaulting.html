pole vaulting

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊl ˌvɔltɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊl ˌvɔːltɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of jumping over a high bar using a long pole as an aid
jumping over a bar using a pole (sport)

Examples

He has been training for years to compete in pole vaulting at the national level.
The track team introduced several students to pole vaulting this season.
Her favorite sport is pole vaulting, which she practices three times a week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high jump - athletics event involving jumping over a high bar, but without a pole
