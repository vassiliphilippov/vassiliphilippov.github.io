carefree

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɛrˌfri / (AmE), / ˈkeəˌfriː / (BrE)
free from anxiety or responsibility; unconcerned and without worries
free from worry or responsibility

Examples

Despite the looming deadline, she remained carefree and relaxed.
He spent his childhood in a carefree village, running through the fields.
Their carefree laughter echoed down the beach as the sun set.

Synset Examples

carefree with his money
freewheeling urban youths
had a harum-scarum youth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: careless - not giving sufficient attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors
  • Antonym: responsible - having an obligation to do something or having control over someone

Etymology

From 'care' + 'free', first recorded in late 16th century.

Examples
