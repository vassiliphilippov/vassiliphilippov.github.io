lance-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈlæns ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈlɑːns ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
narrow and tapering to a point at the apex, like the head of a lance; especially used to describe leaves or objects with this shape.
narrow and tapering to a point; lance-like

Examples

The plant’s lance-shaped leaves help it shed water efficiently.
She admired the lance-shaped petals of the exotic flower.
The fish has a long, lance-shaped body ideal for swift swimming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lanceolate - narrow and tapering to a point, especially of leaves
