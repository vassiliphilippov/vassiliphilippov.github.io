nonglutinous

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈɡluː.tɪ.nəs / (AmE), / nɒnˈɡluː.tɪ.nəs / (BrE)
Not sticky or glue-like in texture; not having the properties of glue.
not sticky; not glue-like

Examples

We always use nonglutinous rice to keep the grains separate and fluffy.
The paint creates a nonglutinous surface that doesn’t attract dust.
She prefers nonglutinous adhesives for crafts, as they are easier to remove.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: glutinous - having the sticky properties of glue

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'non-' (not) to 'glutinous' (from Latin 'glutinosus', sticky).
