Wisconsinite

1. NOUN

/ wɪˈskɑnsənaɪt / (AmE), / wɪˈskɒnsɪnaɪt / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the state of Wisconsin in the United States
person from Wisconsin

Examples

As a proud Wisconsinite, she loves cheese and football.
Many Wisconsinites enjoy outdoor activities during the summer months.
He met another Wisconsinite while traveling abroad and they quickly became friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Wisconsin - a state in the north-central United States

Etymology

From Wisconsin + -ite, a suffix for denoting inhabitants.
