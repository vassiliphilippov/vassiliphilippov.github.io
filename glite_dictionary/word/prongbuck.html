prongbuck

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɔŋˌbʌk / (AmE), / ˈprɒŋˌbʌk / (BrE)
a swift, antelope-like animal native to the western plains of North America, notable for its small, branched horns
North American antelope-like mammal

Examples

A herd of prongbuck grazed quietly on the open prairie beneath the vast sky.
The prongbuck can reach impressive speeds when running from predators.
We spotted several prongbucks near the watering hole during our trip to Wyoming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pronghorn - the North American mammal known for its forked horns and great speed
  • Synonym: antelope - a swift-running, horned mammal of Africa and Eurasia, similar in appearance to the prongbuck
