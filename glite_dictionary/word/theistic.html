theistic

1. ADJ

/ θiˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / θiˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of theism or the belief in the existence of a god or gods.
relating to belief in a god or gods

Examples

Her views are strictly theistic, emphasizing the presence of a higher power in the universe.
The debate contrasted theistic and atheistic worldviews at the philosophy conference.
Some people hold theistic beliefs without adhering to any specific religion.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: atheistic - relating to atheism or the belief that there is no god
  • Synonym: theism - belief in the existence of a god or gods

Etymology

Formed from 'theism' (from Greek theos meaning 'god') with the suffix -ic, denoting relating to.
