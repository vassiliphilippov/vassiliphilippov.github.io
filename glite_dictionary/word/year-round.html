year-round

1. ADJ

/ ˈjɪr ˌraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈjɪə ˌraʊnd / (BrE)
operating, occurring, or available throughout the entire year; not restricted to a particular season or time of year.
all year long; throughout the year

Examples

They offer year-round classes at the community center for all ages.
The hotel has a year-round heated pool for guests to enjoy.
Because of the mild climate, the market remains year-round and never closes.

Synset Examples

a year-round resort
a year-round job

Related Terms

  • Antonym: seasonal - operating or available only during a particular season
