aerobatics

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛroʊˈbætɪks / (AmE), / ˌeərəʊˈbætɪks / (BrE)
the practice of performing stunts and complex maneuvers with an aircraft while flying, often as entertainment or sport
aircraft stunt flying

Examples

The pilot amazed the crowd with his daring aerobatics during the airshow.
She is training to compete in aerobatics at an international level.
Many aviation festivals feature spectacular aerobatics performed by skilled stunt pilots.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acrobatic - performing extraordinary physical feats or stunts

Etymology

From 'aerobatic', a blend of 'aeronautics' and 'acrobatics'.
