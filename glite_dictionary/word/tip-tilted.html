tip-tilted

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɪpˌtɪltɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɪpˌtɪltɪd / (BrE)
(of the nose) having the tip turned upward; upturned at the end.
with the tip turned upward (of a nose)

Examples

She had a small tip-tilted nose that gave her face a playful look.
The child’s tip-tilted nose reminded her grandmother of her youth.
He admired the actress’s delicate features, especially her tip-tilted nose.

Synset Examples

a retrousse nose
a small upturned nose

Related Terms

  • Synonym: upturned - (of the nose) directed or turned upward at the tip
  • Synonym: retroussé - (of the nose) turned up at the tip

Etymology

Formed from 'tip' and the past participle of 'tilt', meaning to cause to slope or turn in a direction.
