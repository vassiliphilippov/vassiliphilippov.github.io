grungy

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡrʌndʒi / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌndʒi / (BrE)
dirty or grimy in appearance; covered with ingrained dirt or soot.
dirty or grimy in appearance

Examples

After a long day hiking, my boots were so grungy I had to leave them outside.
The abandoned factory had a grungy floor covered in black soot.
My brother's room always looks grungy no matter how often he tries to clean it.

Synset Examples

a miner's begrimed face
dingy linen
grimy hands
grubby little fingers
a grungy kitchen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grimy - covered with or resembling grime; dirty
  • Synonym: dirty - not clean; covered or marked with an unclean substance
  • Antonym: clean - free from dirt, marks, or stains

Etymology

From 'grunge' (dirt, grime), possibly from US slang in the 20th century, with -y suffix forming adjectives.
