Pipturus

1. NOUN

/ pɪpˈtʊrəs / (AmE), / pɪpˈtjʊərəs / (BrE)
A genus of woody plants in the nettle family (Urticaceae), native to Australia and other regions.
genus of woody plants in Urticaceae family

Examples

Pipturus is commonly found in the rainforests of northern Australia.
Researchers studied the medicinal properties of several Pipturus species.
The fibres from Pipturus plants have traditional uses among indigenous communities.

Etymology

From modern Latin, genus name, possibly based on Greek 'piptein' (to fall) or similar roots.

Examples

Researchers discovered a new species of Pipturus in the Australian outback.
