Alfred de Musset

1. PROPN

/ ˈælfrəd də myˈsɛt / (AmE), / ˈælfrɪd də myˈseɪ / (BrE)
(1810–1857) A French poet, playwright, and novelist, known for his Romantic works and contributions to 19th-century French literature.
French Romantic poet and writer

Examples

Alfred de Musset is celebrated for his influential works during the Romantic period in France.
Many literature students analyze Alfred de Musset's poetry for its emotional intensity and lyrical beauty.
The play reading featured excerpts from Alfred de Musset's most renowned dramas.
