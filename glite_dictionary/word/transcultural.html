transcultural

1. ADJ

/ ˌtrænzˈkʌltʃɚəl / (AmE), / ˌtrænzˈkʌltʃərəl / (BrE)
relating to or extending across multiple human cultures; involving or encompassing more than one culture.
extending across multiple cultures

Examples

The museum's new exhibit explores transcultural influences in modern art.
She researches transcultural communication and the ways traditions merge across societies.
Their friendship is a powerful example of transcultural understanding.

Synset Examples

a transcultural ideal of freedom embracing all the peoples of the world

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multicultural - relating to or including several different cultures
  • Antonym: monocultural - relating to a single culture

Etymology

From Latin prefix 'trans-' (across, beyond) + 'cultural'.
