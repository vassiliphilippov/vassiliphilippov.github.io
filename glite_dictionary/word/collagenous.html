collagenous

1. ADJ

/ kəˈlɑː.dʒə.nəs / (AmE), / kəˈlædʒ.ɪ.nəs / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of collagen, the main structural protein in connective tissue.
consisting of or related to collagen
medical

Examples

Certain diseases can affect the collagenous tissue in joints and skin.
The collagenous fibers provide strength and support to connective tissue.
Scientists studied the collagenous structure of the scar to understand how it healed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collagen - the main structural protein found in skin and other connective tissues

Etymology

From 'collagen' + '-ous', indicating 'pertaining to or possessing the qualities of'.
