supranormal

1. ADJ

/ ˌsuːprəˈnɔrməl / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˈnɔːməl / (BrE)
beyond what is normal or outside the scope of scientific explanation; supernatural or extraordinary
beyond normal or explainable limits

Examples

The scientist dismissed the claims of supranormal abilities as lacking empirical evidence.
He attributed the mysterious events to supranormal forces.
Researchers have investigated whether some people have supranormal perception beyond the five senses.

Synset Examples

supernormal intimations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paranormal - phenomena beyond scientific explanation
  • Antonym: normal - conforming to the standard or usual

Etymology

From Latin 'supra-' meaning 'above' and English 'normal'.
