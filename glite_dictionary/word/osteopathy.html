osteopathy

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːstioʊˈæpəθi / (AmE), / ˌɒstiˈɒpəθi / (BrE)
A system of medical treatment based on manipulating the skeleton and muscles to restore health.
medical therapy involving manipulation of bones and muscles
medical

Examples

She sought osteopathy to relieve her persistent back pain.
Many people believe osteopathy can complement traditional medical treatments.
He decided to study osteopathy after seeing its benefits firsthand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chiropractic - system of therapy focused on spinal adjustment

Etymology

From Greek osteon (bone) + -pathy (suffering or treatment).
