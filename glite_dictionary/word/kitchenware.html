kitchenware

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtʃənˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃənˌweə / (BrE)
Utensils, dishes, and other equipment used in a kitchen for cooking or serving food.
utensils and equipment for kitchen use

Examples

The store sells a wide variety of kitchenware, from pans to blenders.
She bought new kitchenware before moving into her apartment.
All the kitchenware should be washed after use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cookware - pots, pans, and containers used for cooking food
  • Synonym: tableware - dishes and utensils used at the table for eating and serving food

Etymology

Formed from 'kitchen' + 'ware', following the pattern of other household goods like 'tableware'.
