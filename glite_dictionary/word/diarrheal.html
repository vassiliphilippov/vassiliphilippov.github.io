diarrheal

1. ADJ

/ daɪˈæriəl / (AmE), / daɪəˈrɪəəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of diarrhea
relating to diarrhea
medical

Examples

The patient exhibited diarrheal symptoms after eating contaminated food.
There was a significant outbreak of diarrheal disease in the village.
Doctors are researching new treatments for diarrheal infections in children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diarrhea - frequent and watery bowel movements

Etymology

From English 'diarrhea' + -al (forming adjectives).
