reliability

1. NOUN

/ rɪˌlaɪ.əˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / rɪˌlaɪ.əˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
The quality of being trustworthy or consistently performing well; dependability.
the quality of being dependable

Examples

The company's success is largely due to the reliability of its products.
You can always count on her reliability when you need help.
Engineers are testing the reliability of the new software before its release.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dependability - the quality of being dependable or trustworthy
  • Antonym: unreliability - the quality of not being reliable or dependable

Etymology

From reliable + -ity, patterned after ability.
