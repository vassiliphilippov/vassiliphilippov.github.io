unmotorised

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈmoʊtɚˌaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈməʊtəraɪzd / (BrE)
Not equipped with a motor; lacking a motor.
without a motor

Examples

Bicycles are a common form of unmotorised transport in many cities.
Only unmotorised boats are allowed on this lake to protect wildlife.
The park has dedicated trails for unmotorised vehicles such as skateboards and scooters.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: motorised - equipped with a motor

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'motorised'.
