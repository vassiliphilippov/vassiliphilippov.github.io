Charlie Parker

1. PROPN

/ ˈʧɑrli ˈpɑrkər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːli ˈpɑːkə / (BrE)
(1920–1955) An American jazz saxophonist and composer, influential leader of the bebop style of jazz.
American jazz saxophonist and bebop pioneer

Examples

Many jazz enthusiasts regard Charlie Parker as one of the most innovative musicians of the 20th century.
The music of Charlie Parker continues to inspire saxophonists around the world.
A documentary about Charlie Parker explored his influence on the bebop movement.
