programmer

1. NOUN

/ ˈproʊˌɡræmər / (AmE), / ˈprəʊˌɡræmə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to design, write, and test computer programs
person who writes computer programs

Examples

She works as a skilled programmer for a major tech company.
The programmer spent all night debugging the code.
Many programmers start learning to code at a young age.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: developer - person who creates software applications

Etymology

From 'program' + '-er', indicating a person who programs.
