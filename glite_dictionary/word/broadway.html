Broadway

1. PROPN

/ ˈbrɔdˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːd.weɪ / (BrE)
A street in Manhattan, New York City, noted for its concentration of professional theaters and regarded as the center of the American theatrical industry.
famous theater district in Manhattan

Examples

Seeing a play on Broadway was at the top of her New York itinerary.
Many aspiring actors dream of performing on Broadway one day.
Tickets for popular shows on Broadway often sell out months in advance.
