twenty-second

1. ADJ

/ ˌtwɛntiˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˌtwɛntiˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
Coming next after the twenty-first in a sequence; 22nd.
ordinal number: 22nd

Examples

He finished in twenty-second place in the marathon.
Her birthday falls on the twenty-second of March.
This is the twenty-second time they've visited London.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-first - coming next after the twentieth; 21st
  • Degree Higher: twenty-third - coming next after the twenty-second; 23rd
