triangularity

1. NOUN

/ traɪˌæŋɡjuˈæɹɪti / (AmE), / traɪˌæŋɡjuˈælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being shaped like a triangle
the state of being triangular

Examples

The triangularity of the roof gave the house a distinctive appearance.
Mathematicians often discuss the triangularity of certain shapes or patterns.
There is a pleasing triangularity to the composition of this painting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triangular - having three sides and three angles; shaped like a triangle

Etymology

From triangle + -arity, modeled after 'singularity'.
