patriot

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪtriət / (AmE), / ˈpeɪtriət / (BrE)
A person who vigorously supports and defends their country and is prepared to serve its interests.
a person devoted to their country

Examples

Many consider him a patriot because he served his country in the army.
The speech inspired citizens to act like true patriots and defend their homeland.
She was recognized as a patriot for her lifetime of service to her country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nationalist - a person strongly identifying with their own nation
  • Antonym: traitor - a person who betrays their country

Etymology

From Greek 'patriōtēs' (fellow countryman), from 'patris' (fatherland).
