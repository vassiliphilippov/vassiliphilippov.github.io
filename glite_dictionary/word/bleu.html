bleu

1. NOUN

/ bluː / (AmE), / bluː / (BrE)
A type of cheese that contains blue-green mold, giving it a distinct flavor and appearance; blue cheese.
cheese containing blue mold

Examples

I ordered a salad topped with crumbled bleu for extra flavor.
The restaurant serves a steak with bleu cheese sauce.
She prefers bleu over other types of cheese because of its strong taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blue cheese - cheese with blue-green mold veins

Etymology

From French 'bleu' meaning 'blue', referring to the color of the mold.
