chairperson

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛrˌpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈtʃeəˌpɜːsən / (BrE)
the person who presides over a meeting, committee, or organization
person presiding over a meeting

Examples

As the chairperson, she opened the annual meeting with a short welcome speech.
All questions should be directed to the chairperson during the discussion.
The chairperson called for a vote on the proposal.

Synset Examples

address your remarks to the chairperson

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chairman - male or gender-specific presiding officer
  • Synonym: chairwoman - female presiding officer
  • Antonym: secretary - officer responsible for administrative work in meetings

Etymology

Formed from 'chair' + 'person' to provide a gender-neutral alternative to 'chairman' or 'chairwoman'.
