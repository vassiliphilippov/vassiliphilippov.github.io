euphonical

1. ADJ

/ juˈfɑnɪkəl / (AmE), / juˈfɒnɪkəl / (BrE)
pleasing in sound; characterized by euphony
pleasing in sound

Examples

The poet chose euphonical words to evoke a sense of tranquility.
Her euphonical voice captivated everyone in the hall.
The composer sought a euphonical arrangement for the closing passage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: euphonious - pleasing to the ear; harmonious in sound
  • Antonym: cacophonous - having a harsh or discordant sound

Etymology

From Greek euphōnia (sweetness of sound), via 'euphony' + -ical.
