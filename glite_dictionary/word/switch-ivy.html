switch-ivy

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪtʃ ˌaɪ.vi / (AmE), / ˈswɪtʃ ˌaɪ.vi / (BrE)
A fast-growing evergreen shrub of the southeastern United States, characterized by arching, interlaced branches and clusters of white flowers.
fast-growing evergreen shrub of southeastern US

Examples

The garden’s border was lined with switch-ivy, providing rich green cover all year round.
She admired the delicate white flowers that bloomed on the switch-ivy in late spring.
Even without much attention, switch-ivy quickly spread across the fence.
