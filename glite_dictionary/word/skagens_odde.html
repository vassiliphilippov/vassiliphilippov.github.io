Skagens Odde

1. PROPN

/ ˈskɑːgənz ˈɒdə / (AmE), / ˈskɑːɡenz ˈɒdə / (BrE)
the cape forming the northernmost point of Jutland in Denmark, lying between the Skagerrak and the Kattegat seas
cape at the tip of Jutland, Denmark

Examples

Many tourists visit Skagens Odde to see where the two seas meet.
The shifting sands of Skagens Odde have formed unique landscapes over centuries.
Birdwatchers gather at Skagens Odde to observe migratory species crossing between Scandinavia and continental Europe.

Etymology

Danish, meaning 'Skagen's spit' or 'Skagen's point'.
