stoneroot

1. NOUN

/ ˈstoʊnˌrut / (AmE), / ˈstəʊnˌruːt / (BrE)
a perennial herb (Collinsonia canadensis) native to eastern North America, with serrated pointed leaves and loose clusters of yellowish flowers
a North American perennial herb

Examples

The woodland was filled with stoneroot blooming in late summer.
Herbalists often use extracts from stoneroot for traditional remedies.
We identified the plant as stoneroot by its strong scent and yellowish flowers.
