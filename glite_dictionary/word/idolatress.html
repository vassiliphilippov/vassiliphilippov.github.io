idolatress

1. NOUN

/ aɪˈdɑːlətrɪs / (AmE), / aɪˈdɒlətrɪs / (BrE)
a woman who worships idols or practices idolatry
female worshipper of idols

Examples

In the ancient tale, the idolatress was banished from the village for her beliefs.
The preacher denounced the city as a haven for every idolatress and idolater.
She was accused of being an idolatress because of her devotion to the foreign statue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idolater - a person who worships idols or practices idolatry

Etymology

From idolater + -ess (feminine suffix).
