Icelander

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪs.lænd.ər / (AmE), / ˈaɪs.lənd.ə / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Iceland
native or inhabitant of Iceland

Examples

My friend is an Icelander who grew up in Reykjavik.
The Icelander explained their country's traditions to us.
Many Icelanders speak excellent English as a second language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Icelandic - relating to Iceland, its people, or language

Etymology

From Iceland + -er, denoting a person from Iceland.
