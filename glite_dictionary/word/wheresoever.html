wheresoever

1. ADV

/ wɛr.soʊˈɛv.ɚ / (AmE), / wɛə.səʊˈɛv.ə / (BrE)
in whatever place; wherever.
in whatever place; wherever
archaic

Examples

Wheresoever you go, may happiness follow you.
He sought peace wheresoever it could be found, even in the most unlikely places.
They are committed to justice wheresoever injustice appears in the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wherever - in any place; regardless of where

Etymology

Formed from 'where' + archaic intensive 'soever'.
