billboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪlˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbɪlˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a large outdoor board for displaying advertisements or public information, typically found beside roads or on buildings
large outdoor advertising board

Examples

A huge billboard advertised the new phone on the side of the highway.
The company rented a billboard in Times Square to attract more customers.
She designed a creative billboard for the upcoming music festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poster - a printed picture or notice put on a wall, for advertising or decoration

Etymology

Formed from 'bill' (a posted notice) + 'board'.
