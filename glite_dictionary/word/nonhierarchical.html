nonhierarchical

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˌhaɪəˈrɑrkɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌhaɪəˈrɑːkɪkəl / (BrE)
Not arranged or classified according to a hierarchy.
not involving a hierarchy

Examples

The company replaced its traditional management system with a nonhierarchical structure to encourage collaboration.
Some animal species organize themselves in nonhierarchical groups, where no member is dominant over the others.
A nonhierarchical network allows all participants to contribute equally without a chain of command.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hierarchical - arranged in order of rank or authority

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'non-' to 'hierarchical', from Greek 'hierarkhēs' (leader of sacred rites).
