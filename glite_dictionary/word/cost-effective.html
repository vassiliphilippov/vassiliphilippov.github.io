cost-effective

1. ADJ

/ kɑst ɪˈfɛktɪv / (AmE), / kɒst ɪˈfɛktɪv / (BrE)
giving good results without costing a lot of money; producing good results relative to the amount of money spent
productive relative to cost

Examples

The company implemented a cost-effective solution that reduced its annual expenses by 20%.
Solar panels can be a cost-effective way to lower your electricity bills in the long run.
We are always searching for the most cost-effective methods to deliver quality products to our customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: economical - using no more of something than is necessary; not wasteful
  • Antonym: expensive - costing a lot of money; not cost-effective
