senatorial

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛn.əˈtɔr.i.əl / (AmE), / ˌsɛn.əˈtɔː.ri.əl / (BrE)
relating to a senator or a senate, especially in government or politics.
relating to a senator or senate

Examples

The country will hold a senatorial election next month to choose new representatives.
Her senatorial duties require frequent travel to the capital.
He gave a speech with a distinctly senatorial tone, emphasizing dignity and tradition.

Synset Examples

senatorial election

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legislative - relating to the making or enacting of laws

Etymology

From Latin 'senatorius', from 'senator' (member of a senate).
