sightly

1. ADJ

/ ˈsaɪtli / (AmE), / ˈsaɪtli / (BrE)
attractive and pleasing to look at; visually appealing
pleasant or attractive in appearance

Examples

The garden was filled with sightly flowers that caught everyone's attention.
She arranged the books in a sightly manner on the shelf, making the room look inviting.
Although the village is small, its sightly countryside attracts many tourists each year.

Synset Examples

my bonny lass
there's a bonny bay beyond
a comely face
young fair maidens

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comely - pleasant to look at; attractive
  • Antonym: unattractive - not pleasing or appealing to look at

Etymology

From Middle English, derived from 'sight' + '-ly', meaning 'liking for appearance'.
