newsmonger

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌmʌŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌmʌŋɡə / (BrE)
a person who habitually spreads gossip or divulges personal or sensational news about others
someone who spreads gossip or news

Examples

The newsmonger couldn't wait to share the latest scandal with the entire office.
Every village seems to have at least one newsmonger who knows everyone's business.
She was labeled a newsmonger after repeatedly spreading rumors about her neighbors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gossip - a person who habitually reveals personal or sensational facts about others

Etymology

From 'news' + 'monger' (dealer or trader), from Old English 'mangere' (merchant).
