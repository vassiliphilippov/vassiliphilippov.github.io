gastroduodenal

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡæs.troʊˌduː.əˈdiː.nəl / (AmE), / ˌɡæstrəʊˌdjuːəˈdiːnəl / (BrE)
relating to the stomach and the duodenum (the first section of the small intestine)
relating to the stomach and duodenum
medical

Examples

She was diagnosed with a gastroduodenal ulcer that required immediate treatment.
The surgeon discussed the risks of bleeding from gastroduodenal arteries during the procedure.
A gastroduodenal endoscopy was performed to examine the upper digestive tract.

Etymology

From Greek 'gastēr' (stomach) + 'duodenum' + '-al' (relating to).
