to singularize

1. VERB

/ ˈsɪŋɡjələˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡjʊləraɪz / (BrE)
to regard or treat as singular; to make distinct from others as singular
to make or regard as singular

Examples

The teacher asked the students to singularize the noun in each sentence.
In linguistics, certain rules singularize abstract concepts for clarity.
The author tends to singularize characters by giving them unique traits.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to pluralize - to make or regard as plural

Etymology

From Latin 'singularis' (single, unique) + English '-ize'.
