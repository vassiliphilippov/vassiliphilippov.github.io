repulse

/ rɪˈpʌls / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌls / (BrE)
an act of driving back or warding off something or someone, especially an enemy or attack
an act of driving back or warding off

Examples

Their repulse of the invaders boosted morale throughout the nation.
Despite a fierce repulse, the attackers regrouped for another assault.

Related Terms
