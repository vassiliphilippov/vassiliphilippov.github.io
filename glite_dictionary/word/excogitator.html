excogitator

1. NOUN

/ ɛkˈskɑdʒɪˌteɪtər / (AmE), / ɛkˈskɒdʒɪˌteɪtə / (BrE)
a person who thinks carefully and thoroughly about things; a deep and careful thinker
a person who thinks carefully and thoroughly

Examples

The philosopher was regarded as an excogitator whose insights shaped modern thought.
As an excogitator, she spent hours analyzing every angle of a problem before making a decision.
In history, many inventors were also excogitators, pondering solutions long before acting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thinker - a person who thinks deeply and seriously

Etymology

From Latin excogitator, from excogitare meaning 'to think out'.
