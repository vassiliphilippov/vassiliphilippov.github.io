lienal

1. ADJ

/ ˈliːnəl / (AmE), / ˈlaɪənəl / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the spleen
relating to the spleen
medical

Examples

During the surgery, the doctor noted several lienal arteries branching off near the spleen.
A blockage in the lienal vein can lead to spleen-related complications.
The researcher compared lienal tissue samples from healthy and diseased subjects.

Related Terms

Etymology

From Latin 'lien' (spleen) + '-al' (adjective suffix).
