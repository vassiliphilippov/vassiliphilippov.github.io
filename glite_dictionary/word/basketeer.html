basketeer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskəˌtir / (AmE), / ˌbɑːskəˈtɪə / (BrE)
an athlete who plays basketball
basketball player

Examples

He dreamed of becoming a professional basketeer and playing in the major leagues.
The young basketeer scored thirty points in last night's game.
Many talented basketeers start competing in national tournaments before finishing high school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basketball player - an athlete who plays basketball

Etymology

Formed by analogy to sports terms like 'cricketeer,' from 'basket' (as in basketball) + '-eer' (denoting someone engaged in an activity).
