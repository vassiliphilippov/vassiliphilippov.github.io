Tunguz

1. NOUN

/ tʊnˈɡuːz / (AmE), / tʊŋˈɡuːz / (BrE)
the Tungusic language spoken by the Evenki people of eastern Siberia
the language of the Evenki people (Siberia)

Examples

Linguists have studied the grammar and phonetics of Tunguz for decades.
She is fluent in both Russian and Tunguz, enabling her to work as a translator.
The Tunguz language is integral to the cultural identity of the Evenki people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Evenki - the people who speak the Tunguz language

Etymology

From Russian ‘тунгуз’ (Tunguz), possibly from an East Turkic word for 'swamp dwellers'.
