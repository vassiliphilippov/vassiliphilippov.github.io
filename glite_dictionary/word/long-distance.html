long-distance

1. ADJ

/ lɔŋ ˈdɪstəns / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈdɪstəns / (BrE)
relating to telephone calls made to distant locations, often involving additional charges beyond local calls.
relating to non-local telephone calls

Examples

I need to make a long-distance call to my parents in another state.
Back in the 1980s, making a long-distance call could be quite expensive.
The old hotel offered a long-distance operator service for its guests.

Synset Examples

a long-distance call
a long-distance transmission line
a long-distance operator

Related Terms

  • Antonym: local call - a telephone call within a local area
