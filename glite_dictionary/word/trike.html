trike

1. NOUN

/ traɪk / (AmE), / traɪk / (BrE)
a vehicle with three wheels, typically powered by foot pedals and used as a child's toy or for recreation
three-wheeled pedal vehicle

Examples

My daughter loves riding her trike around the park on sunny days.
He bought a new red trike for his son's third birthday.
After learning to pedal a trike, children are often ready for a bicycle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tricycle - a three-wheeled vehicle, especially one for children
  • Antonym: bicycle - a vehicle with two wheels that is powered by pedals

Etymology

Shortened form of 'tricycle'.
