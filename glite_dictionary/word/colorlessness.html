colorlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɚlɪsnəs / (AmE), / ˈkʌləlɪsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being without color or hue; lacking chromatic color
lack of color

Examples

The colorlessness of the sky suggested an overcast and gloomy afternoon.
Doctors checked the patient for signs such as colorlessness of the lips or fingers.
He admired the colorlessness of the crystal, which made it seem pure and flawless.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: colorful - full of color; having much or varied color
