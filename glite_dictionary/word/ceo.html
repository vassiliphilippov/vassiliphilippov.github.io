CEO

1. NOUN

/ si i oʊ / (AmE), / siː iː əʊ / (BrE)
the highest-ranking executive in a company or organization, responsible for its overall management and performance
chief executive of a company

Examples

The CEO announced a new strategy to improve company profits at the annual meeting.
After ten years with the company, she was promoted to CEO.
Many employees look to the CEO for guidance during challenging times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chief executive officer - the highest-ranking executive in a company
  • Synonym: managing director - executive head of a company, especially in British English

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'Chief Executive Officer'.
