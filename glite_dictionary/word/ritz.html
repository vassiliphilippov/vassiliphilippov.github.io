Ritz

1. PROPN

/ rɪts / (AmE), / rɪts / (BrE)
a famous chain of luxury hotels founded by César Ritz, known for elegance and high standards of service
luxury hotel chain founded by César Ritz

Examples

They celebrated their anniversary at the Ritz, enjoying exceptional service and dining.
The Ritz epitomizes sophisticated luxury in the heart of London.
Many celebrities choose to stay at the Ritz during their visits to Paris.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ritz-Carlton - an international luxury hotel chain, part of the Ritz tradition

Etymology

Named after Swiss hotelier César Ritz (1850–1918), founder of the original Ritz Hotel.
