Dhaka

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɑːkə / (AmE), / ˈdɑːkə / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Bangladesh, located in the central part of the country on the Buriganga River
capital city of Bangladesh

Examples

She was born and raised in Dhaka, where she attended university.
Many international flights arrive at Dhaka Airport every day.
The textile industry is a significant part of Dhaka's economy.

Etymology

From Bengali ঢাকা (Ḍhākā), the name of the city.
