selling

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
the action or business of exchanging goods or services for money
the act of exchanging goods for money

Examples

The selling of fresh produce at the farmers' market begins early in the morning.
Online selling has become more popular during the past decade.
Successful selling often depends on strong communication skills.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: buying - the act of acquiring goods by paying money

Etymology

From sell + -ing; 'sell' from Old English 'sellan' (to give, deliver).
