absorbing

1. ADJ

/ əbˈzɔrbɪŋ / (AmE), / əbˈzɔːbɪŋ / (BrE)
so interesting or engaging that it fully captures your attention
very interesting; fully engaging

Examples

The documentary was so absorbing that I forgot to check the time.
She finished the absorbing novel in just one day.
We found the museum's exhibit on ancient Egypt truly absorbing.

Synset Examples

a fascinating story

Related Terms

  • Synonym: engrossing - so interesting and absorbing as to take up all one's attention
  • Antonym: boring - not interesting; failing to hold attention

Etymology

Present participle of 'absorb', from Latin 'absorbere' (to swallow up).
