overmuchness

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊvɚˈmʌtʃnəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈmʌtʃnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being excessive or more than is appropriate
excessiveness; too much

Examples

The overmuchness of praise made her uncomfortable during the ceremony.
Some people criticize the overmuchness of regulations in the industry.
He regretted the overmuchness of his comments, admitting they went too far.

Synset Examples

four-year-olds have an overabundance of energy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excess - an amount that is more than necessary or permitted
  • Antonym: moderation - the avoidance of excess

Etymology

Formed from 'overmuch' (excessive) + '-ness' (state or quality).
