dishpan

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪʃˌpæn / (AmE), / ˈdɪʃ.pan / (BrE)
A large, deep bowl or pan used for washing dishes by hand.
large pan for washing dishes

Examples

She filled the dishpan with soapy water before starting to wash the plates.
After dinner, he dried the cutlery while she scrubbed pots in the dishpan.
The old enamel dishpan was chipped but still perfectly usable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basin - a round, wide container used for washing
  • Synonym: sink - a fixed basin with a water supply and drainage, used for washing

Etymology

Formed from 'dish' and 'pan'; refers to a pan used for dishes.
