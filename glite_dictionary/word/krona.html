krona

1. NOUN

/ ˈkroʊ.nə / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊ.nə / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Sweden, equal to 100 öre.
Swedish unit of currency

Examples

The price of the book was 120 kronor in the Stockholm store.
She exchanged dollars for krona before her trip to Sweden.
The Swedish krona has fluctuated in value against the euro this year.

Etymology

From Swedish 'krona', meaning 'crown'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkroʊnə / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊnə / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Iceland, equal to 100 aurar.
Icelandic unit of currency

Examples

The price of the sweater was 10,000 krona in Reykjavik.
She exchanged her euros for krona at the airport.
The krona fluctuated against the US dollar last year.

Etymology

From Icelandic 'króna', meaning ‘crown’, related to Latin 'corona'.
