to empurple

1. VERB

/ ɪmˈpɜr.pəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɜː.pəl / (BrE)
to make or become purple in color
make or become purple

Examples

The evening sky began to empurple as the sun set behind the mountains.
His cheeks would empurple whenever he spoke in public.
The artist used a wash to empurple the background of her painting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purple - to color or become purple

Etymology

From em- (variant of en-, meaning 'make') + purple, modeled on similar English formations from French and Latin roots.
