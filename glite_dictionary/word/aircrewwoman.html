aircrewwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌkruːˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈeəˌkruːˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is a member of an aircrew
female member of an aircrew

Examples

The aircrewwoman completed her preflight checks before boarding the helicopter.
As the only aircrewwoman in the squadron, she felt a special sense of pride.
Several experienced aircrewwomen participated in the training exercise alongside their male colleagues.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: aircrewman - male member of an aircrew

Etymology

Formed from 'aircrew' + 'woman' by analogy with 'aircrewman'.
