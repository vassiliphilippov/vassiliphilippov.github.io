Numbers

1. PROPN

/ ˈnʌmbərz / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbəz / (BrE)
the fourth book of the Old Testament, describing the numbering of the Israelites and their journey through the wilderness after the Exodus from Egypt
the fourth book of the Old Testament

Examples

The Book of Numbers describes the Israelites' wanderings in the desert.
She is studying Numbers for her theology class.
There are many laws and stories recorded in Numbers.

Etymology

From the Latin 'Numeri', which is the title of the book in the Vulgate, reflecting its emphasis on census in Hebrew 'Bemidbar' (in the wilderness).
