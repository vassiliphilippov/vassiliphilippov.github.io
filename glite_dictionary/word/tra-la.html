tra-la

1. NOUN

/ trɑˈlɑ / (AmE), / trɑˈlɑ / (BrE)
a set of nonsensical syllables, often used when singing or humming a cheerful tune or refrain
nonsensical syllables in singing

Examples

She danced around the room singing tra-la without a care in the world.
As he washed the dishes, he hummed a cheerful tra-la to himself.
Children skipped down the road, chanting tra-la and laughing.
