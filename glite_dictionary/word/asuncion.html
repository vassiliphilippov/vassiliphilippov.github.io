Asuncion

1. PROPN

/ əˌsʊnˈsjɔn / (AmE), / əˌsʊnˈsjɒn / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Paraguay, situated on the Paraguay River
capital city of Paraguay

Examples

The president gave a speech in Asuncion to address the nation.
Many tourists visit Asuncion for its vibrant culture and historical landmarks.
She moved to Asuncion after accepting a new job opportunity.

Etymology

From Spanish Asunción, meaning 'Assumption' (referring to the Assumption of Mary).
