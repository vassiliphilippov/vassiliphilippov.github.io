meteoritic

1. ADJ

/ ˌmiːtiəˈrɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌmiːtɪəˈrɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or caused by meteorites
relating to meteorites

Examples

Scientists discovered a layer of meteoritic dust buried deep beneath the Antarctic ice.
The chemical composition of the rocks suggests a meteoritic origin.
Researchers found meteoritic particles scattered across the desert surface after the meteor shower.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meteoritical - relating to meteorites (alternative, less common form)

Etymology

From meteorite + -ic (adjective forming suffix).
