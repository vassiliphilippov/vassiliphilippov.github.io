Wallace Hume Carothers

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɑːlɪs hjuːm kəˈrʌðərz / (AmE), / ˈwɒlɪs hjuːm kəˈrʌðəz / (BrE)
(1896–1937) United States chemist known for developing nylon and pioneering work in polymer chemistry.
American chemist, developed nylon

Examples

Wallace Hume Carothers made groundbreaking discoveries in the field of synthetic polymers.
The invention of nylon by Wallace Hume Carothers revolutionized the textile industry.
