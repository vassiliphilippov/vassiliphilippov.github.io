potty

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑti / (AmE), / ˈpɒti / (BrE)

children's training toilet

Examples

The toddler proudly used the potty for the first time today.
After her nap, she sat on the potty and read a picture book.
We keep a potty in the bathroom for our youngest child.

3. NOUN

/ ˈpɑti / (AmE), / ˈpɒti / (BrE)
A small bowl or chair used as a toilet by young children, especially during toilet training.
child's toilet for training

Examples

The toddler proudly used her potty for the first time today.
We keep a potty in the bedroom for our son during the night.
Training a child to use the potty can take time and patience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chamber pot - a bowl kept in a bedroom and used as a toilet, especially in the past

Etymology

Diminutive of 'pot' with the suffix '-y', meaning 'small or related to'.
