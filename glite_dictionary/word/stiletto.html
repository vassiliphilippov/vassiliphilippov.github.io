stiletto

1. NOUN

/ stəˈlɛtoʊ / (AmE), / stɪˈlɛtəʊ / (BrE)
a small dagger with a slender, tapering blade, often used for stabbing
small dagger with a tapered blade

Examples

The thief carried a stiletto hidden in his boot.
She admired the antique stiletto displayed at the museum.
The detective found a stiletto at the crime scene, its blade stained.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dagger - a short knife with a pointed and edged blade, used as a weapon

Etymology

From Italian 'stiletto', diminutive of 'stilo' meaning 'dagger', from Latin 'stilus' (stake, pointed instrument).
