Klaipeda

1. PROPN

/ klaɪˈpeɪdə / (AmE), / klaɪˈpeːdə / (BrE)
A city in western Lithuania on the Baltic Sea, formerly an important trading town of the Hanseatic League.
city in western Lithuania on the Baltic Sea

Examples

The port of Klaipeda plays a key role in Lithuania's economy.
We visited Klaipeda during our Baltic Sea cruise last summer.
Klaipeda boasts a blend of German and Lithuanian architectural styles.

Etymology

From Lithuanian Klaipėda, formerly known by its German name, Memel.
