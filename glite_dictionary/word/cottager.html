cottager

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑt̬ɪdʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒtɪdʒə / (BrE)
a person who lives in a cottage, especially in a rural area
person living in a cottage

Examples

The old cottager welcomed us in with a warm smile and a hot cup of tea.
Many cottagers participate in the annual village fair to sell their handmade crafts.
The novel describes the simple lifestyle of a Scottish cottager in the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: villager - a person who lives in a village

Etymology

From 'cottage' + '-er', denoting a person associated with a cottage.
