Sierra Leone

1. PROPN

/ siˌɛrə liˈoʊn, siˈɛrə liˌoʊn / (AmE), / siˌɛərə lɪˈəʊn, siˈɛərə liˌəʊn / (BrE)
a country in West Africa, bordered by Guinea and Liberia, which became independent from the United Kingdom in 1961
country in West Africa

Examples

She traveled to Sierra Leone to volunteer in local health clinics.
The capital city of Sierra Leone is Freetown, located on the Atlantic coast.
Sierra Leone gained independence from British colonial rule in 1961.

Etymology

From Spanish 'sierra leona', literally 'lioness mountain', so named by Portuguese explorer Pedro de Sintra in the 15th century.
