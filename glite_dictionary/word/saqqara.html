Saqqara

1. PROPN

/ sɑːˈkɑːrə / (AmE), / səˈkɑːrə / (BrE)
an ancient site in northern Egypt, known for being the location of the oldest Egyptian pyramids, including the Step Pyramid of Djoser.
ancient pyramid site in Egypt

Examples

Archaeologists discovered new tombs near Saqqara during their latest excavation.
The Step Pyramid at Saqqara is considered one of the earliest monumental stone structures in Egypt.
Tourists often visit Saqqara to explore its ancient burial sites and pyramids.
