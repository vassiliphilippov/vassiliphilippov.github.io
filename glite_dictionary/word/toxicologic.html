toxicologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌtɑksɪkəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɒksɪkəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the scientific study of poisons and their effects
relating to toxicology
medical

Examples

The researchers conducted a toxicologic analysis of the contaminated water supply.
She is specializing in toxicologic medicine, focusing on the effects of environmental toxins.
A toxicologic report confirmed the presence of several harmful chemicals in the sample.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toxicological - relating to toxicology (alternative, more common form)

Etymology

Formed from 'toxicology' (from Greek toxikon 'poison' + -logia 'study of') plus the adjective suffix -ic.
