piety

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪ.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ.əti / (BrE)
the quality of being religious or reverent; devotion to religious duties and practices.
religious devotion or reverence

Examples

Her piety was evident in her daily prayers and acts of charity.
Throughout history, piety has inspired countless works of art and literature.
He was respected for his piety and commitment to helping those in need.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devotion - strong love or loyalty, especially for a cause, activity, or religion
  • Antonym: impiety - lack of piety or reverence

Etymology

From Latin 'pietas' (duty, religiousness, devotion).
