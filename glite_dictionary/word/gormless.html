gormless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɔrmləs / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːmləs / (BrE)
(British, informal) Lacking intelligence or drive; stupid or dull-witted.
lacking intelligence or energy
informal british

Examples

I can't believe you forgot your own birthday—you can be so gormless sometimes.
Everyone thought his gormless comments in the meeting were rather amusing.
The gormless look on his face made it clear he hadn't understood the question.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dim - person who is not intelligent; slow-witted
  • Synonym: clueless - lacking knowledge or understanding
  • Antonym: bright - intelligent; quick-witted

Etymology

From 'gorm', an old dialect word meaning sense; + '-less'.
