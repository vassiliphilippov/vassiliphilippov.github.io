gayness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪ.nəs / (BrE)
sexual attraction to or sexual activity with people of the same sex; homosexuality
sexual attraction to the same sex

Examples

His friends always supported him as he embraced his gayness and lived openly.
She wrote a powerful essay on the importance of accepting one's gayness.
In some societies, gayness was once considered taboo, but attitudes have changed over time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homosexuality - sexual attraction to people of one's own sex
  • Antonym: heterosexuality - sexual attraction to people of the opposite sex

Etymology

From 'gay' (homosexual) + '-ness' (state or quality).
