to whop

1. VERB

/ wɑp / (AmE), / wɒp / (BrE)
to hit or strike someone or something very hard
hit something or someone hard
informal

Examples

He threatened to whop me if I didn't tell the truth.
She whopped the punching bag with surprising force during her workout.
If you aren't careful, you'll whop your head on that low ceiling.

Synset Examples

The teacher whacked the boy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whack - to strike with a sharp blow
  • Synonym: smack - to hit something with force
  • Antonym: caress - to touch gently

2. VERB

/ wɑp / (AmE), / wɒp / (BrE)
to strike or hit someone or something with great force
hit hard with force

Examples

He managed to whop the punching bag so hard it flew across the room.
The boxer whopped his opponent with a powerful right hook.
If you aren't careful, you'll get whopped with that heavy book.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wallop - to strike with a heavy blow
  • Synonym: smack - to hit someone or something with a sharp slap
