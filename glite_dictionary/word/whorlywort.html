whorlywort

1. NOUN

/ ˈhwɝːliˌwɝːt / (AmE), / ˈwɜːliˌwɜːt / (BrE)
A tall perennial herb native to eastern North America, with spikes of small white or purple flowers.
tall perennial herb with spikes of flowers

Examples

The whorlywort is often found along shady woodland edges in eastern North America.
After learning to identify whorlywort, she noticed it growing throughout the preserve.
Clusters of delicate white blooms marked the patch of whorlywort beneath the trees.

Etymology

From 'whorl', describing the arrangement of leaves or flowers, and 'wort', an old word for plant or herb.
