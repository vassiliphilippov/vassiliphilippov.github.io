microprocessor

1. NOUN

/ ˌmaɪkroʊˈproʊˌsɛsər / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈprəʊsɛsə / (BrE)
an integrated circuit that contains the central processing unit (CPU) of a computer and performs most of its processing functions
central processing unit chip of a computer

Examples

The new laptop is powered by a faster microprocessor than its predecessor.
A microprocessor interprets and executes instructions in most modern electronic devices.
Many household appliances now contain a small microprocessor to control their functions.

Synset Examples

a microprocessor functions as the central processing unit of a microcomputer
a disk drive contains a microprocessor to handle the internal functions of the drive

Related Terms

  • Synonym: CPU - central processing unit of a computer

Etymology

From 'micro-' meaning small + 'processor' (from process + -or).
