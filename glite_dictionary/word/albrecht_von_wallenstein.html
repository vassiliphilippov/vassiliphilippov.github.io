Albrecht von Wallenstein

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑlbrɛkt fɔn ˈvaːlənʃtaɪn / (AmE), / ˈalbrɛkt fɒn ˈvalənstaɪn / (BrE)
(1583–1634) An Austrian general who served the Hapsburgs and was a leading commander during the Thirty Years' War.
Austrian general during the Thirty Years' War

Examples

Albrecht von Wallenstein played a crucial role in shaping the course of the Thirty Years' War.
The military strategies of Albrecht von Wallenstein are still studied by historians today.
Emperor Ferdinand II relied heavily on Albrecht von Wallenstein for his military campaigns.
