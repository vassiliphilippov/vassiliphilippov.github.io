kopek

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊˌpɛk / (AmE), / ˈkəʊpɛk / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Russia, equal to one hundredth of a ruble.
Russian currency unit (1/100 ruble)

Examples

She found an old kopek at the bottom of the drawer.
The museum displayed coins from the Soviet era, including the kopek.
It used to cost only a few kopeks to buy bread in those days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kopeck - alternative spelling of kopek

Etymology

From Russian копейка (kopeyka), diminutive of копьё (kop'yo, 'spear'), referring to the image on the coin.
