favoured

1. ADJ

/ ˈfeɪvərd / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvəd / (BrE)
Regarded or treated with special preference or partiality; preferred above others.
preferred or treated with special preference

Examples

She was always the favoured candidate for promotion among her colleagues.
The sunny side of the hill is the favoured spot for picnics.
Certain students seem to get favoured treatment from the teacher.

Synset Examples

the favored child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preferred - regarded with particular favor or choice
  • Antonym: disfavoured - regarded or treated with less or no favor

Etymology

From the verb 'favour', from Old French 'favor', from Latin 'favorem' (goodwill, support).
