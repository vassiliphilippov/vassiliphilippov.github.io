self-awareness

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlf əˈwɛrnəs / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf əˈweənəs / (BrE)
conscious knowledge and understanding of your own character, feelings, motives, and individuality.
conscious knowledge of one's own individuality

Examples

Increased self-awareness can help you make better decisions and improve your relationships.
She displayed remarkable self-awareness during the interview, openly discussing her strengths and weaknesses.
Meditation is often practiced to enhance self-awareness and reduce stress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-consciousness - awareness of oneself, sometimes with discomfort

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'awareness', both of Old English origin.
