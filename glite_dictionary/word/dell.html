dell

1. NOUN

/ dɛl / (AmE), / dɛl / (BrE)
a small valley, usually among trees or woodland
small wooded valley or hollow

Examples

A flock of sheep grazed peacefully in the sunlit dell.
They enjoyed a picnic on the grass at the heart of the quiet dell.
The path disappeared into a shady dell surrounded by tall trees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vale - a valley, especially a broad one
  • Synonym: glen - a narrow valley, especially in Scotland or Ireland

Etymology

Old English *del* (valley), related to Dutch *dal* and German *Tal* (valley).
