theism

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiːɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈθiːɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief in the existence of a god or gods, especially as a creator and ruler of the universe
belief in a god or gods

Examples

His study explored the philosophical arguments for theism in contrast to atheism.
Many world religions are based on some form of theism.
The debate between theism and secularism continues in modern society.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: atheism - disbelief or lack of belief in the existence of a god or gods
  • Degree Lower: deism - belief in the existence of a supreme being who does not intervene in the universe

Etymology

From Greek 'theos' (god) + '-ism'.
