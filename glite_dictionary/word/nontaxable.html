nontaxable

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈtæksəbəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈtæksəbəl / (BrE)
(of goods, income, or funds) not subject to taxation by law.
not subject to taxation

Examples

Donations made to registered charities are often considered nontaxable by the government.
The company provided a breakdown of their taxable and nontaxable income for the year.
Interest earned from municipal bonds is typically nontaxable at the federal level.

Synset Examples

the funds of nonprofit organizations are nontaxable
income exempt from taxation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: taxable - subject to taxation
  • Synonym: tax-exempt - exempt from taxation, especially by law

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) and 'taxable' (subject to tax).
