unprepossessing

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌpriːpəˈzɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌpriːpəˈzɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
not attractive or impressive in appearance, making a neutral or unfavorable first impression.
not attractive or impressive

Examples

Despite its unprepossessing exterior, the restaurant serves excellent food.
She found the unprepossessing house surprisingly cozy on the inside.
His unprepossessing manner hid a sharp wit and a kind heart.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: prepossessing - attractive; making a good impression
  • Synonym: plain - ordinary or lacking adornment; not beautiful

Etymology

Formed by adding the negative prefix 'un-' to 'prepossessing', which derives from 'prepossess' meaning to influence beforehand.
