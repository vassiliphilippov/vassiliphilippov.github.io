unaided

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈeɪdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈeɪdɪd / (BrE)
carried out without help, support, or assistance from others
without help or assistance

Examples

He solved the complex puzzle unaided, relying solely on his own skills.
The climber reached the summit unaided after months of training.
Children should not swim unaided in deep water.

Synset Examples

his first unaided walk through the park

Related Terms

  • Antonym: assisted - with help or support
  • Synonym: independent - not relying on others; self-reliant

Etymology

From un- (not) + aided (helped).
