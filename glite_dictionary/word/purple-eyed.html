purple-eyed

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝːpəl aɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl aɪd / (BrE)
(of flowers) having a purple, eye-like marking at or near the center.
having a purple eye-like marking (in flowers)

Examples

The purple-eyed pansies stood out vividly among the other blooms in the garden.
Several wildflowers display purple-eyed centers that attract pollinators.
Her favorite variety was the purple-eyed daisy because of its unique coloration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eyed - having a marked or colored eye or eye-like spot
