twenty-seven

1. NUM

/ ˌtwɛntiˈsɛvən / (AmE), / ˌtwɛntiˈsɛvən / (BrE)
the number 27; the cardinal number equalling seven more than twenty
the cardinal number 27

Examples

She turned twenty-seven this year and decided to travel abroad.
There are twenty-seven students in the science club.
He scored twenty-seven points in the basketball game.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-six - the cardinal number 26, one less than twenty-seven
  • Degree Higher: twenty-eight - the cardinal number 28, one more than twenty-seven
