George V

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɔrdʒ ðə fɪfθ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒ ðə fɪfθ / (BrE)
(1865–1936) King of the United Kingdom and Emperor of India from 1910 to 1936, who changed the royal family's German name during World War I.
King of the United Kingdom, 1910–1936

Examples

George V ruled during a significant period that included the First World War.
In 1917, George V changed the royal family’s name to Windsor.
The reign of George V saw social change and the growth of the British Empire.
