least

1. ADV

/ list / (AmE), / liːst / (BrE)
to the smallest extent or degree; used to form the superlative of 'little'.
to the smallest extent or degree

Examples

Of all the candidates, she was the least experienced for the job.
You should worry least about what others think and focus on your goals.
He was the least affected by the news among his friends.

Synset Examples

The garter snake is the least dangerous snake

Related Terms

  • Antonym: most - to the greatest extent or degree, superlative of 'much' or 'many'

Etymology

From Old English læst (smallest, least), superlative of lȳtel (little).
