myalgic

1. ADJ

/ maɪˈældʒɪk / (AmE), / maɪˈaldʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by muscle pain.
relating to muscle pain
medical

Examples

The patient reported myalgic symptoms after the intense workout.

Related Terms

Etymology

From Greek 'myo-' (muscle) + 'algos' (pain).
