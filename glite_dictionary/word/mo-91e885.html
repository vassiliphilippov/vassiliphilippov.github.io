Mo

1. NOUN

/ moʊ / (AmE), / məʊ / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for molybdenum, a metallic element used to strengthen steel
chemical symbol for molybdenum

Examples

The alloy contains a small percentage of Mo to increase its hardness.
Engineers often refer to Mo when discussing steel additives.
High-strength steel typically includes elements like chromium and Mo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: molybdenum - a chemical element, symbol Mo, used to strengthen steel

Etymology

Abbreviation of molybdenum, from Neo-Latin molybdenum, based on Greek molybdos (lead).
