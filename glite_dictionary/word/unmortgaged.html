unmortgaged

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmɔrɡɪdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɔːɡɪdʒd / (BrE)
(of property or a title) not mortgaged; free from any legal claim or encumbrance that may affect ownership.
not subject to a mortgage or encumbrance

Examples

The previous owner sold the house as an unmortgaged property, making the transaction easier.
They were relieved to have an unmortgaged title after years of paying off their loan.
Buying land that is unmortgaged eliminates many potential legal complications.

Synset Examples

I have clear title to this property

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mortgaged - (of property) pledged as security for a loan
  • Synonym: clear - (of a title) free from any encumbrance or doubt

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not', and 'mortgaged', from Old French 'mort gage' meaning 'dead pledge'.
