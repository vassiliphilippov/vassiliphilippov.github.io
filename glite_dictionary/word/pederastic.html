pederastic

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɛdəˈræstɪk / (AmE), / ˌpiːdəˈræstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to sexual or romantic activity between a man and a boy, especially as a historical or cultural practice.
of homosexual relations between men and boys

Examples

The ancient Greeks are often discussed in relation to pederastic practices.
Some scholars have written about pederastic relationships in historical contexts.
The novel examines the consequences of a pederastic attraction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pederasty - sexual relationship between a man and a boy

Etymology

From Greek paiderastēs (a lover of boys), from pais (boy) + erastēs (lover).
