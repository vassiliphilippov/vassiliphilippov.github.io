killingly

1. ADV

/ ˈkɪlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkɪlɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that is extremely funny or causes great amusement.
in a very humorous way

Examples

She told a killingly funny story that left the whole room in stitches.
His killingly sharp wit made every dinner party unforgettable.
The film was so killingly clever that even the critics were laughing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hilariously - in an extremely amusing or funny way
  • Synonym: wittily - in a clever and humorous way
