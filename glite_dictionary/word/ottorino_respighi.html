Ottorino Respighi

1. PROPN

/ ˌoʊtoʊˈrinoʊ rɛˈspiːgi / (AmE), / ˌɒtəˈriːnoʊ rɛˈspiːgi / (BrE)
An Italian composer known for his symphonic poems and orchestral works, active in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.
Italian composer (1879–1936)
