exultingly

1. ADV

/ ɪɡˈzʌltɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzʌltɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows great joy or triumph; in an exultant way.
in a jubilant or triumphant way

Examples

She exultingly announced her victory to the cheering crowd.
The team exultingly celebrated their hard-earned win after the final whistle.
He exultingly shared the good news with his friends.

Synset Examples

it was exultingly easy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joyfully - in a joyful manner
  • Synonym: triumphantly - in a triumphant manner

Etymology

From 'exult' + '-ing' + '-ly'; ultimately from Latin 'exsultare' (to leap up).
