passado

1. NOUN

/ pəˈsɑːdoʊ / (AmE), / pəˈsɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
(in fencing) an attacking thrust made by stepping forward with one foot, keeping the back leg straight, and extending the sword arm forward.
fencing move: forward attacking thrust

Examples

The fencer surprised his opponent with a swift passado and scored a point.
In the lesson, the coach demonstrated how to execute a proper passado.
Elizabeth practiced the passado to improve her attack during tournaments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunge - a sudden forward movement in fencing to attack with the sword

Etymology

From Italian passato (past participle of passare ‘to pass’), referring to a passing step.
