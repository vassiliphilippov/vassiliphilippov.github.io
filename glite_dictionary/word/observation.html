observation

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːb.zɚˈveɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɒb.zəˈveɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a remark or statement based on careful thought or notice of details
carefully considered remark

Examples

Her insightful observation about the project led to major improvements in its design.
During the discussion, he made an observation that everyone found thought-provoking.
I have just one observation regarding your proposal: the timeline seems too optimistic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comment - a verbal or written remark
  • Synonym: statement - a definite or clear expression of something in speech or writing

Etymology

From Latin observatio (watching, noting), from observare (to watch, to observe).
