Pritzelago

1. NOUN

/ prɪtˈsɛləˌɡoʊ / (AmE), / prɪtˈzɛləˌɡəʊ / (BrE)
a genus of small herbaceous plants in the mustard family, commonly known as chamois cress
genus of plants; chamois cress

Examples

Many alpine gardens feature Pritzelago for its delicate white flowers.
The botanist collected specimens of Pritzelago during her expedition in the mountains.
Chamois cress, or Pritzelago, thrives in rocky terrain.

Etymology

Named after German botanist Georg August Pritzel.
