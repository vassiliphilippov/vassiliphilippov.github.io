price

1. NOUN

/ praɪs / (AmE), / praɪs / (BrE)
a reward of money offered for the capture of a criminal
reward for capturing a criminal

Examples

The outlaw was hunted far and wide because there was a price on his head.
She feared someone would betray him for the price being offered by the authorities.
News spread quickly that a considerable price awaited anyone who turned in the fugitive.

Synset Examples

the cattle thief has a price on his head

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bounty - a sum paid for capturing or killing a criminal or animal

/ praɪs / (AmE), / praɪs / (BrE)
to decide the amount of money that should be charged for something; to set a price for.
set the price for something

Examples

Some luxury brands deliberately price their products higher to create an image of exclusivity.
Make sure you price your services competitively to attract more customers.

Related Terms
