affixed

1. ADJ

/ əˈfɪkst / (AmE), / əˈfɪkst / (BrE)
firmly attached to something; fastened or fixed in place.
firmly attached or fastened

Examples

The certificate was affixed to the wall beside the entrance.
Each package had a barcode label affixed to the top.
An official seal was affixed to the envelope to ensure its authenticity.

Synset Examples

the affixed labels

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attached - joined or fastened to something
  • Antonym: detached - not connected or separated

Etymology

Past participle of 'affix', from Latin 'affixus', past participle of 'affigere' (to fasten to).
