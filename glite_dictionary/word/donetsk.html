Donetsk

1. PROPN

/ doʊˈnjɛtsk / (AmE), / dɒˈnjɛtsk / (BrE)
A major industrial city in the Donets Basin, eastern Ukraine.
industrial city in eastern Ukraine

Examples

Many factories in Donetsk contribute significantly to Ukraine's economy.
Fierce fighting was reported near Donetsk during the conflict.
The football club from Donetsk has achieved international recognition.

Etymology

From Russian Донецк (Donetsk), named after the Donets River.
