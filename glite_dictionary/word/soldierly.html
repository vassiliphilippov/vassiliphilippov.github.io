soldierly

1. ADJ

/ ˈsoʊl.dʒɚ.li / (AmE), / ˈsəʊl.dʒə.li / (BrE)
having or showing qualities that are characteristic of a soldier, such as discipline, bravery, or military bearing.
like or befitting a soldier

Examples

The captain's soldierly conduct earned him the respect of his troops.
Her soldierly attitude impressed everyone during the rigorous training.
He displayed a soldierly sense of discipline even under pressure.

Synset Examples

spirit, pluck, and fine soldierly qualities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: martial - related to war or soldiers
  • Synonym: military - relating to armed forces or soldiers

Etymology

From 'soldier' + suffix '-ly', meaning 'like a soldier'.
