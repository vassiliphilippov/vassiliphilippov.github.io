SK-Ampicillin

1. NOUN

/ ɛs keɪ æmˈpɪsɪlɪn / (AmE), / ɛs keɪ æmˈpɪsɪlɪn / (BrE)
A brand name for ampicillin, a semisynthetic penicillin antibiotic used to treat various bacterial infections.
trade name for ampicillin antibiotic
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed SK-Ampicillin for the patient's bacterial infection.
Some pharmacies no longer carry SK-Ampicillin as newer antibiotics are available.
Allergic reactions can sometimes occur with SK-Ampicillin in sensitive individuals.
