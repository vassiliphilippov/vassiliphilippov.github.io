USACIL

1. NOUN

/ ˌjuː ˌɛs eɪ ˈsiː aɪ ˈɛl / (AmE), / ˌjuː ˌɛs eɪ ˈsiː aɪ ˈɛl / (BrE)
The United States Army Criminal Investigation Laboratory, the primary forensic laboratory supporting criminal investigations for the U.S. Army.
U.S. Army forensic laboratory (acronym)
american

Examples

After collecting evidence from the crime scene, the investigators sent it to USACIL for forensic analysis.
Test results from USACIL helped solve the high-profile military case.
Many forensic scientists at USACIL have extensive experience with criminal investigations.

Etymology

Acronym for United States Army Criminal Investigation Laboratory.
