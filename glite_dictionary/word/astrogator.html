astrogator

1. NOUN

/ ˈæstroʊˌɡeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈastrəʊˌɡeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who navigates a spacecraft
navigator of a spacecraft

Examples

The astrogator plotted a safe course through the asteroid field.
As the ship's astrogator, she constantly monitored star charts and trajectory data.
Every successful mission relied on a skilled astrogator to guide the crew across the galaxy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: navigator - person who navigates a ship, aircraft, or vehicle

Etymology

From 'astro-' (relating to stars or space) and 'navigator'.
