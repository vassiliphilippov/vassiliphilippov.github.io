paddle-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈpæd.əl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈpæd.əl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a shape similar to that of a paddle; broad and flat in form.
broad and flat like a paddle

Examples

The turtle has paddle-shaped limbs adapted for swimming.
The scientist observed the plant's paddle-shaped leaves under the microscope.
Their paddle-shaped fins help them move quickly through the water.
