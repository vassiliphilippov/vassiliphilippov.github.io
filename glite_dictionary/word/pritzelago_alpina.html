Pritzelago alpina

1. NOUN

/ ˌprɪtsəˈlɑːɡoʊ ælˈpiːnə / (AmE), / ˌprɪtsɪˈlɑːɡəʊ ælˈpiːnə / (BrE)
A small, tufted perennial herb found in central and southern European mountains, with very small flowers on usually leafless stems; sometimes classified in the genus Lepidium.
mountain herb with tiny flowers (Europe)

Examples

The delicate Pritzelago alpina blooms high in the alpine meadows each spring.
Botanists discovered a patch of Pritzelago alpina growing among the rocky slopes.
Hikers admired the clusters of Pritzelago alpina during their trek through the European mountains.
