under-appreciated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndər əˈpriʃiˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndə əˈpriːʃieɪtɪd / (BrE)
Not valued or recognized as much as deserved.
not sufficiently valued or recognized

Examples

Many artists are under-appreciated during their lifetimes but celebrated after their deaths.
She felt under-appreciated at work despite her significant contributions to the team.
Volunteers often go under-appreciated even though their efforts are vital to the community.

Synset Examples

She felt under-appreciated at her job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unappreciated - not recognized or valued
