villainous

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɪlɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈvɪlɪnəs / (BrE)
Extremely wicked or criminal; characteristic of a villain.
extremely wicked; like a villain

Examples

The villainous character plotted to take over the entire city.
Her villainous behavior shocked everyone at the meeting.
The film depicted a villainous mastermind who stopped at nothing to achieve his goals.

Synset Examples

nefarious schemes
a villainous plot
a villainous band of thieves

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wicked - Morally wrong or evil
  • Antonym: virtuous - Having or showing high moral standards

Etymology

From Old French *villainos* (of or pertaining to a villein), from Latin *villanus* (farmhand).
