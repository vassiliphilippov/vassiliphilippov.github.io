Kwell

1. NOUN

/ kwɛl / (AmE), / kwɛl / (BrE)
a proprietary preparation of lindane, available as a cream or shampoo, used to kill lice and itch mites
brand name for a lindane-based lice treatment
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Kwell shampoo to treat the child's head lice.
Some people experience skin irritation after using Kwell cream for scabies.
You should carefully follow the instructions when applying Kwell to avoid potential side effects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lindane - an insecticide and medication used to treat lice and scabies
