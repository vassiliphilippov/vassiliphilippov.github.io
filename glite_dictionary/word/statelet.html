statelet

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪtlət / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtlɪt / (BrE)
a very small independent or semi-independent state, especially one of minor influence
a very small independent or semi-independent state

Examples

The tiny statelet was recognized by only a handful of other countries.
After years of negotiations, the statelet secured limited autonomy within the federation.
Several ethnic groups sought to form their own statelets following the collapse of the empire.

Synset Examples

The small statelet struggled for independence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microstate - a very small independent country or sovereign state

Etymology

Formed from 'state' with diminutive suffix '-let'.
