centered

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛn.tɚd / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.təd / (BrE)
Being in or placed at the center; having something at or as a central point.
being or placed in the center

Examples

The dining table is centered in the room to allow for easy movement.
Make sure the title is centered at the top of the page.
A perfectly centered circle is essential for this graphic design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: central - of, at, or forming the center
  • Antonym: off-center - not in or at the center
