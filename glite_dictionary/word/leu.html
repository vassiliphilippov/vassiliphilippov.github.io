leu

1. NOUN

/ leɪˈuː / (AmE), / leɪˈuː / (BrE)
the basic unit of money in Moldova, equal to 100 bani.
basic unit of Moldovan currency

Examples

The price of the meal was 150 leu, which is quite affordable in Moldova.
She exchanged her dollars for leu before traveling to Chişinău.
Banks in Moldova offer competitive rates for converting euros into leu.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leu - the basic unit of money in Romania

Etymology

From Romanian 'leu' meaning 'lion', originally appearing on the Dutch thaler used in Eastern Europe.

2. NOUN

/ leɪˈuː / (AmE), / leɪˈuː / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Romania, equal to 100 bani.
Romanian currency unit

Examples

She exchanged her euros for Romanian leu at the airport.
The price of lunch was 30 leu at the restaurant.
Foreign investors are watching the value of the leu closely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lei - plural of 'leu', the Romanian currency

Etymology

From Romanian 'leu', meaning 'lion', referring to a lion that appeared on earlier Dutch thalers used in the region.
