go-to-meeting

1. ADJ

/ ˌgoʊ tʊ ˈmiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌgəʊ tə ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of clothes) suitable for wearing to church or for formal occasions; best or most formal clothing.
suitable for church or formal occasions
american

Examples

She wore her go-to-meeting dress to the family wedding.
He keeps a pair of go-to-meeting shoes just for special events.
Their go-to-meeting outfits were set aside for Sunday mornings.

Synset Examples

her Sunday-go-to-meeting clothes

Etymology

From the phrase 'go to meeting', referring originally to attending church services, especially in American English.
