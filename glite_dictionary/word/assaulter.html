assaulter

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɔltər / (AmE), / əˈsɔːltə / (BrE)
A person who attacks someone physically or violently.
person who attacks

Examples

The police were able to identify the assaulter from security camera footage.
Victims of the attack described the assaulter as tall and wearing a black jacket.
The assaulter was quickly apprehended after the incident in the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attacker - A person who attacks someone or something
  • Antonym: defender - A person who defends or protects against attack

Etymology

From 'assault' (Middle English), from Old French 'assaillir', from Vulgar Latin *assaltāre.
