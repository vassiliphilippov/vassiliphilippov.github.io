lawfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔf(ə)lnəs / (AmE), / ˈlɔːf(ə)lnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being in accordance with the law; legality
quality of conforming to law

Examples

The lawyer emphasized the lawfulness of her client's actions in court.
Public trust in institutions is strengthened by the lawfulness of government decisions.
We must question the lawfulness of such surveillance measures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legality - the quality or state of being legal
  • Antonym: illegality - the state of being illegal or against the law

Etymology

Formed from 'lawful' + '-ness', following regular English word formation.
