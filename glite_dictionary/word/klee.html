Klee

1. PROPN

/ kliː / (AmE), / kliː / (BrE)
Paul Klee (1879–1940), a Swiss painter known for his highly individual style influenced by movements such as Expressionism, Cubism, and Surrealism.
Swiss painter (1879–1940) known for innovative style
