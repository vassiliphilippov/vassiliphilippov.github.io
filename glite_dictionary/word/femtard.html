femtard

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛm.tɑrd / (AmE), / ˈfɛm.tɑːd / (BrE)
A highly derogatory and offensive slang term used mainly online to insult or demean feminist individuals or women.
offensive slang insult for feminists or women
offensive slang

Examples

Using the word femtard in arguments is disrespectful and will not be tolerated here.
The online forum has strict rules against calling anyone a femtard or using similar hate speech.
Several users reported the comment after someone referred to a participant as a femtard.

Synset Examples

The group condemned the use of the word femtard in discussions.
