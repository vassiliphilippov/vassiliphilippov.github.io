humbled

1. ADJ

/ ˈhʌmbəld / (AmE), / ˈhʌmbəld / (BrE)
Brought low in dignity, status, or condition; subdued or reduced in pride or importance.
brought low; subdued in status or spirit

Examples

She felt humbled by the generosity of the local community after the disaster.
After losing everything, he emerged a humbled man, wiser and more compassionate.
The defeat left the once-confident team genuinely humbled and determined to work harder.

Synset Examples

brought low
a broken man
his broken spirit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broken - subdued or crushed by grief, hardship, or humiliation
  • Antonym: exalted - raised in rank, status, or character
