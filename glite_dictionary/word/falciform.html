falciform

1. ADJ

/ ˈfæl.sɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈfæl.sɪˌfɔːm / (BrE)
Curved or shaped like a sickle; sickle-shaped.
shaped like a sickle

Examples

The liver's falciform ligament separates its left and right lobes.
Certain algae have falciform cells with gracefully curved tips.
The radiologist noted a falciform shadow on the abdominal X-ray.

Synset Examples

a falcate leaf
falcate claws
the falcate moon

Related Terms

  • Synonym: falcate - curved like a sickle

Etymology

From Latin 'falx, falcis' (sickle) + '-form' (form, shape).
