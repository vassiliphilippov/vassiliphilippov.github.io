Nyiragongo

1. PROPN

/ niːˌɪrəˈɡɒŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / niːˌɪərəˈɡɒŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
an active volcano in eastern Democratic Republic of the Congo, known for its large, persistent lava lake
active volcano in eastern Congo

Examples

Mount Nyiragongo is famous for its spectacular lava lake, attracting volcanologists from around the world.
In 2002, Nyiragongo erupted, causing widespread destruction in the nearby city of Goma.
The steep slopes of Nyiragongo make its lava flows especially fast and hazardous.
