unworkmanlike

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈwɜːrkmənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɜːkmənlaɪk / (BrE)
not showing the skill or qualities expected of a good workman; lacking professional workmanship or quality
lacking skill or professional quality

Examples

The repairs to the wall were done in an unworkmanlike manner and had to be redone.
His unworkmanlike approach to carpentry resulted in a shaky table.
The painting had an unworkmanlike appearance, with uneven brush strokes and sloppy lines.

Synset Examples

an unworkmanlike result
an unworkmanlike tool

Related Terms

  • Antonym: workmanlike - showing the skill or quality of a skilled worker

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'workmanlike', meaning not like that of a skilled workman.
