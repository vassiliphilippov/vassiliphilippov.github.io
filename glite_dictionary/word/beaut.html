beaut

1. NOUN

/ bjut / (AmE), / bjuːt / (BrE)
an outstanding or remarkable example of its kind, often used informally for emphasis
an outstanding or remarkable example
informal

Examples

That was a real beaut of a goal he scored last night.
I made a mistake, and it was a beaut this time.
Check out this old car—it’s a beaut!

Synset Examples

his roses were beauties
when I make a mistake it's a beaut

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty - something of great beauty or excellence

Etymology

Shortened from 'beauty', originally from Old French 'beaute' and Latin 'bellus' (pretty).
