amateurishness

1. NOUN

/ ˌæmətərˈɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˌæmətəˈrɪʃnəs / (BrE)
the quality of lacking professional skill or competence; showing inexperience or ineptitude in manner or execution
lack of professional skill or competence

Examples

The amateurishness of the performance was obvious to everyone in the audience.
Critics complained about the amateurishness of the film's direction and editing.
Despite his enthusiasm, his work was marred by a certain amateurishness.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: professionalism - the competence or skill expected of a professional
  • Synonym: amateurish - lacking professional skill or competence

Etymology

From 'amateurish' + '-ness', formed on 'amateur' from French 'amateur' (lover of).
