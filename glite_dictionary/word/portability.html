portability

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɔrtəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌpɔːtəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being light and easy to carry or move
quality of being easily carried

Examples

The portability of the laptop made it ideal for my frequent business trips.
One of the main advantages of this speaker is its portability, allowing you to take it anywhere.
They chose camping equipment with a focus on portability and ease of setup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mobility - the ability to be moved easily and freely
  • Antonym: heaviness - the quality of being heavy

Etymology

From portable + -ity, after Middle French portabilité, from Latin portare (to carry).
