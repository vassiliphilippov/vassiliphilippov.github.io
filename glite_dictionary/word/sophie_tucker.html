Sophie Tucker

1. PROPN

/ ˌsoʊfi ˈtʌkər / (AmE), / ˌsəʊfi ˈtʌkə / (BrE)
(1884–1966) A United States vaudeville singer and entertainer, known for her powerful voice and flamboyant performances.
U.S. vaudeville singer and entertainer

Examples

Many consider Sophie Tucker one of the greatest female entertainers in vaudeville history.
Sophie Tucker was famous for her bold personality and lively stage presence.
The museum held a special exhibition about the life and music of Sophie Tucker.
