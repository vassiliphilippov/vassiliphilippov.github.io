70th

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛv.ən.ti.əθ / (AmE), / ˈsɛv.ən.ti.ɪθ / (BrE)
coming after sixty-ninth in a sequence; the ordinal number of seventy.
ordinal number for seventy

Examples

She finished in 70th place in the marathon out of hundreds of runners.
His grandfather recently celebrated his 70th birthday with a big family reunion.
This marks the company's 70th anniversary since it was founded in 1954.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seventieth - the ordinal number corresponding to seventy
