roadside

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊd.saɪd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊd.saɪd / (BrE)
the edge or border of a road or path; the strip of land immediately beside a road
the edge of a road or path

Examples

The car broke down on the roadside just outside town.
Wildflowers grew along the roadside during the spring.
He sat by the roadside to rest and watch the passing traffic.

Synset Examples

flowers along the wayside

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wayside - the side or edge of a road or path

Etymology

From 'road' + 'side', combining the terms for the traveled way and its edge.
