nimiety

1. NOUN

/ nɪˈmaɪ.ə.ti / (AmE), / nɪˈmʌɪ.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
an excessive amount; a quantity much larger than is needed
an excessive amount

Examples

The report highlighted a nimiety of regulations that hindered business growth.
Decorating the room with such a nimiety of flowers made it look overcrowded.
There was a nimiety of suggestions, making it hard to choose the best one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excess - an amount that is more than necessary
  • Synonym: surplus - an extra amount or quantity
  • Antonym: deficiency - a lack or shortage

Etymology

From Latin nimietas (excess, too great a quantity), from nimius (too much, excessive).
