Bh

1. NOUN

/ biː eɪtʃ / (AmE), / biː eɪtʃ / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for bohrium, a synthetic transuranic element with atomic number 107
chemical symbol for bohrium (element 107)

Examples

The element with the symbol Bh is extremely rare and radioactive.
Scientists synthesized Bh for the first time in 1981.
All known isotopes of Bh are highly unstable and have short half-lives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bohrium - a synthetic transuranic element with atomic number 107

Etymology

From the name bohrium, itself named after Danish physicist Niels Bohr.
