apsidal

1. ADJ

/ ˈæp.sɪ.dəl / (AmE), / ˈæp.sɪ.dəl / (BrE)
relating to or resembling an apse, especially in architecture
relating to an apse (architecture)

Examples

The cathedral's apsidal chapel was decorated with intricate mosaics.
Architects studied the apsidal end of the basilica for restoration.
A row of columns supports the apsidal vault at the rear of the church.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apse - a domed or vaulted recess, especially in a church

Etymology

From Latin 'apsidalis', from 'apsis' (arch, vault).
