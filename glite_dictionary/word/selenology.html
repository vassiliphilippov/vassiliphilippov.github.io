selenology

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛləˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌsɛlɪˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of science concerned with the study of the Moon
study of the Moon

Examples

She decided to focus her research on selenology after becoming fascinated by lunar geology.
Thanks to advances in selenology, we now understand much more about the Moon's surface.
The conference featured leading experts in selenology presenting their latest findings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astronomy - the scientific study of celestial objects, space, and the universe as a whole

Etymology

From Greek 'selēnē' (Moon) + 'logy' (study of).
