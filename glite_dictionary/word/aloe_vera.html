Aloe vera

1. NOUN

/ ˌæloʊ ˈvɛrə / (AmE), / ˌaləʊ ˈvɪərə / (BrE)
a succulent plant with thick fleshy leaves that produce a soothing, gelatinous substance, often used for medicinal and cosmetic purposes.
succulent plant used for soothing gel

Examples

She applied Aloe vera gel to her sunburned skin for relief.
Many skincare products contain extracts from Aloe vera leaves.
Aloe vera is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and health benefits.

Etymology

From Latin, meaning 'true aloe' (aloe = the plant, vera = true).
