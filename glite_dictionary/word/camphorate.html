to camphorate

1. VERB

/ ˈkæmfəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈkamfərət / (BrE)
to treat or impregnate (something) with camphor
treat with camphor

Examples

To preserve the fabric, they decided to camphorate it before storage.
Pharmacists sometimes camphorate ointments to provide a soothing effect.
The tincture was camphorated to enhance its medicinal properties.

Etymology

From camphor + -ate (as a verb-forming suffix).
