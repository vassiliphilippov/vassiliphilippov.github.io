winemaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪnˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈwaɪnˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes wine, especially as a profession
person who makes wine

Examples

The winemaker carefully monitored the fermentation process to ensure the best flavor.
Every year, the winemaker introduces a new vintage at the spring festival.
She apprenticed with a renowned winemaker in France before starting her own vineyard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vintner - someone who makes or sells wine

2. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪnˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈwaɪnˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person or company involved in making wine, especially as a professional occupation
a person who makes wine

Examples

The winemaker carefully monitored the fermentation process to ensure a high-quality vintage.
Local winemakers often experiment with different grape varieties to create unique wines.
As a renowned winemaker, she has won several awards for her exceptional reds and whites.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vintner - a person who makes or sells wine
  • Antonym: brewer - a person who makes beer
