Edvard Munch

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛdˌvɑrd ˈmʊŋk, sometimes ˈmʌŋk / (AmE), / ˈɛdvɑːd ˈmʊŋk, sometimes ˈmʌŋk / (BrE)
(1863–1944) A Norwegian painter best known for his expressionist works, especially 'The Scream'.
Norwegian painter, created 'The Scream'

Examples

The famous painting 'The Scream' was created by Edvard Munch in the late 19th century.
Edvard Munch's art had a profound influence on the development of German Expressionism.
Many people recognize Edvard Munch as one of the most important Scandinavian artists.
