lean

1. ADJ

/ liːn / (AmE), / liːn / (BrE)
(of an amount, supply, or resource) containing little or no excess; sparing or meagre.
containing little or no excess

Examples

After years of plenty, the company had to adjust to a lean budget with very few luxuries.
Their lean spending habits helped them save for emergencies.
The organization operated efficiently even during lean times when resources were scarce.

Synset Examples

a lean budget
a skimpy allowance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meagre - lacking in quantity or quality
  • Antonym: generous - (of an amount) more than adequate; ample
