entryphone

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛntriˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɛntriˌfəʊn / (BrE)
a telephone system at the entrance of a building that allows communication with visitors before granting them access
entrance intercom system

Examples

Many modern apartment buildings are equipped with an entryphone for residents' security.
She buzzed him in using the entryphone without leaving her desk.
Please speak clearly into the entryphone so we can hear you inside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intercom - a communications system enabling conversation between rooms or areas
