diagrammatic

1. ADJ

/ ˌdaɪə.ɡrəˈmæt̬.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌdaɪ.ə.ɡrəˈmæt.ɪk / (BrE)
shown or represented using diagrams; relating to or resembling a diagram.
represented or explained by diagrams

Examples

The process is shown in a diagrammatic form for simplicity.
Her explanation was brief and diagrammatic, focusing on the main points.
This textbook uses lots of diagrammatic illustrations to clarify concepts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schematic - represented in the form of a diagram or schema

Etymology

From Greek diagrammatikos, from diagramma (figure marked out by lines).
