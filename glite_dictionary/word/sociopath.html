sociopath

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊ.si.oʊˌpæθ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.si.əʊˌpæθ / (BrE)
A person with a personality disorder manifesting itself in extreme antisocial attitudes and behavior and a lack of conscience.
person with antisocial personality disorder

Examples

The main character in the film is a charming sociopath who manipulates everyone around him.
Experts debated whether the criminal's actions stemmed from being a sociopath or from other mental health issues.
Some people mistakenly use the terms sociopath and psychopath interchangeably.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychopath - a person with a personality disorder characterized by persistent antisocial behavior, but often distinguished from sociopaths by aspects of personality and causation

Etymology

From 'socio-' (relating to society) + '-path' (suffering, disease) as in 'psychopath'; developed in mid-20th century.
