sweatpants

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɛtˌpænts / (AmE), / ˈswɛtˌpænts / (BrE)
Loose-fitting casual trousers with elastic cuffs at the ankles, often worn for exercise or lounging.
loose casual trousers with elastic cuffs

Examples

He changed into his comfortable sweatpants after getting home from work.
The gym requires that all students wear sweatpants during physical education class.
With winter approaching, she bought a new pair of fleece-lined sweatpants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tracksuit bottoms - trousers worn as part of a tracksuit

Etymology

Formed from 'sweat' and 'pants'; originally referring to pants for sweating/exercising.
