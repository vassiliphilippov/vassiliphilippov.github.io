barometrical

1. ADJ

/ ˌbæ.rəˈmɛt.rɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌbæ.rəˈmɛt.rɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to or measured by a barometer, or involving atmospheric pressure.
relating to barometers or atmospheric pressure

Examples

The barometrical readings indicated a significant drop in atmospheric pressure before the storm.
Scientists use barometrical data to track weather patterns and predict changes in climate.
A sudden barometrical change can often herald the arrival of a cold front.

Synset Examples

barometric pressure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barometric - relating to atmospheric pressure or barometers

Etymology

From barometer + -ical, following the pattern of scientific adjectives.
