pasang

1. NOUN

/ pɑːˈsɑːŋ / (AmE), / pəˈsæŋ / (BrE)
A wild goat native to Iran and nearby regions, known for its large curved horns.
wild goat of Iran and nearby regions

Examples

The pasang is admired for its impressive horns and agility.
Researchers studied the behavior of the pasang in its natural habitat.
Local legends often feature the elusive pasang high in the mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ibex - wild mountain goat with large curved horns

Etymology

From Persian 'pāzang', meaning wild goat.

2. NOUN

/ pɑːˈsɑːŋ / (AmE), / pəˈsæŋ / (BrE)
a large African antelope with long, straight, nearly upright horns, found in open and lightly wooded regions
large African antelope with upright horns

Examples

The pasang roamed the African savanna, grazing peacefully among the tall grasses.
Researchers observed a herd of pasangs moving cautiously near the waterhole.
With its impressive upright horns, the pasang stands out among other antelopes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: addax - a large antelope of the Sahara with spiral horns
