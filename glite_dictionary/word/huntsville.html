Huntsville

1. PROPN

/ ˈhʌntsvɪl / (AmE), / ˈhʌntsvɪl / (BrE)
A city in northern Alabama, United States, known as a center for space research and technology.
city in northern Alabama, USA

Examples

Many engineers move to Huntsville to work at NASA's Marshall Space Flight Center.
The annual science festival in Huntsville attracts visitors from all over the country.
During our road trip through Alabama, we spent two days exploring Huntsville's museums.
