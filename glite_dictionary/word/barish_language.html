Barish language

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːrɪʃ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːrɪʃ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a group of Kamarupan languages spoken in Assam in northeastern India
Kamarupan languages of Assam, India

Examples

The Barish language is mostly spoken in Assam by various indigenous communities.
Researchers are studying the grammar of the Barish language to understand its relation to other Kamarupan languages.
Educational materials in the Barish language are being developed for schools in northeastern India.
