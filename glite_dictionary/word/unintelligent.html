unintelligent

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪnˈtɛlədʒənt / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənt / (BrE)
lacking intelligence; not smart or clever.
lacking intelligence

Examples

He dismissed the idea as unintelligent and poorly thought out.
It would be unintelligent to ignore the risks involved in the project.
The film portrays the villain as cruel and unintelligent.

Synset Examples

a dull job with lazy and unintelligent co-workers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stupid - lacking intelligence or common sense
  • Antonym: clever - quick to understand or learn; intelligent
  • Synonym: dull - lacking intelligence or quickness of mind

Etymology

From Latin prefix un- (not) + intelligent (from Latin intelligens, present participle of intelligere, to understand).
