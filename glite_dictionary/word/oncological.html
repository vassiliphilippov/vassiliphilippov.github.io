oncological

1. ADJ

/ ɑn.kəˈlɑ.dʒɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ɒŋ.kəˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to the study, diagnosis, or treatment of tumors and cancer (oncology)
pertaining to oncology or cancer treatment
medical

Examples

She was referred to an oncological specialist after her diagnosis.
The hospital established a new oncological center for cancer research.
Her interest in oncological nursing led her to pursue further training.

Synset Examples

oncological nurse

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oncology - the study and treatment of tumors and cancer

Etymology

From oncology + -ical; oncology from Greek 'onkos' (mass, tumor) + '-logy' (study).
