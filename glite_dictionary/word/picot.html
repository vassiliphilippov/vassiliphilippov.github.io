picot

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpiːkəʊ / (BrE)
a small loop or series of loops used as an ornamental edging on lace or ribbon
small decorative loop on lace or ribbon

Examples

The antique handkerchief was edged with a delicate picot trim.
She learned how to create a picot border in her crochet class.
The ribbon has a picot edge, making it perfect for gift wrapping.

Etymology

From French picot, diminutive of pic (point or prickle).
