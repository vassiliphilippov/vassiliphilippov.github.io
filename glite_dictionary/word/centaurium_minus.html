Centaurium minus

1. NOUN

/ sɛnˈtɔriəm ˈmaɪnəs / (AmE), / sɛnˈtɔːrɪəm ˈmaɪnəs / (BrE)
A common European annual herb of the centaury family, with smooth stems and small, pink flowers in dense clusters.
a European annual herb with pink clustered flowers

Examples

The fields were dotted with the tiny pink flowers of Centaurium minus in full bloom.
Botanists often study Centaurium minus for its role in local ecosystems.
A herbalist might use Centaurium minus as an ingredient in traditional remedies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: centaury - any plant of the genus Centaurium, with small pink or red flowers

Etymology

From New Latin, combining the genus name 'Centaurium' and the Latin 'minus' meaning 'lesser'.
