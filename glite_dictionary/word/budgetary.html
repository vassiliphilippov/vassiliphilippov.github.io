budgetary

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʌdʒɪˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˈbʌdʒɪtəri / (BrE)
relating to or involving a budget or financial planning
relating to a budget

Examples

The committee discussed budgetary constraints when planning the new project.
We need to consider budgetary factors before approving any increase in spending.
The school's budgetary policies ensure that funds are allocated efficiently.

Synset Examples

budgetary considerations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: financial - relating to finance or money matters

Etymology

Formed from 'budget' + '-ary' (Latin suffix meaning 'of or relating to').
