Mancunian

1. NOUN

/ mænˈkjuːniən / (AmE), / mæŋˈkjuːniən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Manchester, a city in northwestern England.
native or resident of Manchester

Examples

As a proud Mancunian, she always cheers for the city's football teams.
Many famous musicians are Mancunian by birth.
The Mancunian accent is distinctive and easily recognizable throughout the UK.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'Mancuniensis', meaning 'of Manchester'.

relating to the English city of Manchester or its residents
