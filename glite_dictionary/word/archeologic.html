archeologic

1. ADJ

/ ɑrkɪˈɑlədʒɪk / (AmE), / ɑːkɪˈɒlədʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with archaeology; dealing with the study of human history and prehistory through the excavation and analysis of artifacts and other physical remains.
relating to archaeology

Examples

The team conducted archeologic research at the remote cave site.
She specializes in archeologic fieldwork related to ancient civilizations.
New archeologic evidence suggested the city was much older than previously believed.

Synset Examples

an archaeological dig
a dramatic archaeological discovery

Related Terms

  • Synonym: archaeological - relating to archaeology (variant, more common spelling)

Etymology

From Greek 'arkhaiologikos', via Latin 'archaeologicus'; see also 'archaeology'.
