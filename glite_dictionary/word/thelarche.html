thelarche

1. NOUN

/ θiˈlɑrki / (AmE), / θiˈlɑːki / (BrE)
the beginning of breast development in girls, marking the onset of puberty
onset of breast development in puberty
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that thelarche typically occurs between the ages of 8 and 13.
Early thelarche can be an indicator of precocious puberty in some girls.
During thelarche, girls may experience tenderness as their breasts begin to develop.

Etymology

From Greek 'thēlē' (nipple, teat) + 'archē' (beginning, origin).
