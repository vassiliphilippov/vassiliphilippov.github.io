Annona muricata

1. NOUN

/ əˈnoʊnə ˌmjʊrɪˈkɑːtə / (AmE), / əˈnəʊnə ˌmjʊərɪˈkɑːtə / (BrE)
A small tropical American tree bearing large, succulent, slightly acidic fruit; also known as soursop.
tropical fruit tree (soursop)

Examples

The leaves of Annona muricata are sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
Farmers in tropical regions cultivate Annona muricata for its unique and flavorful fruit.
Scientists are studying compounds found in Annona muricata for their potential health benefits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soursop - Large, spiny tropical fruit from Annona muricata

Etymology

From Latin: genus name Annona, species muricata meaning 'spiny' or 'prickly'.
