interpretive

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈtɜːrprətɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːprətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or providing an explanation or interpretation of something, especially artistic or cultural works.
providing or relating to interpretation

Examples

The exhibition included many interpretive materials to help visitors understand the artworks.
Her interpretive reading of the poem brought out new layers of meaning.
The park features an interpretive center with displays about local wildlife and history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: explanatory - giving an explanation; intended to explain

Etymology

From Latin interpretari (to explain, translate) via Old French and late Middle English.
