chiocciole

1. NOUN

/ kiˈoʊ.tʃoʊ.leɪ / (AmE), / kiˈɒt.tʃə.leɪ / (BrE)
A type of pasta that is shaped like a snail shell or elbow, often used in various Italian dishes.
elbow-shaped pasta

Examples

I made a creamy cheese sauce to go with the chiocciole for dinner.
The restaurant serves homemade chiocciole tossed with fresh pesto and cherry tomatoes.
She prefers using chiocciole instead of penne in her baked pasta recipes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elbow macaroni - short, curved, hollow pasta; common type of elbow-shaped pasta

Etymology

From Italian 'chiocciole', plural of 'chiocciola' meaning 'snail'.
