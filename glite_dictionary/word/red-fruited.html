red-fruited

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛd ˌfruːtɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɛd ˌfruːtɪd / (BrE)
(of a plant) bearing red fruit.
plant having red fruit

Examples

The red-fruited variety of the bush attracts more birds in autumn.
Botanists identified the plant as a red-fruited hawthorn species.
The red-fruited trees stood out vividly against the green forest background.
