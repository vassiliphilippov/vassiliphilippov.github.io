tilling

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
The preparation and cultivation of land for growing crops.
cultivation of land for crops

Examples

Modern farmers use advanced machinery for tilling before planting their seeds.
Proper tilling helps to aerate the soil and improve crop yields.
The ancient method of tilling relied heavily on manual labor and simple tools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cultivation - the act of preparing land for crops

Etymology

From Old English 'tilian' (to strive, get, prepare land).
