adventurism

1. NOUN

/ ədˈvɛn.tʃəˌrɪz.əm / (AmE), / ədˈvɛn.tʃə.rɪ.zəm / (BrE)
reckless or irresponsible behavior, especially in politics or foreign affairs, involving risky or unconsidered actions
reckless political or foreign policy behavior

Examples

Critics accused the government of adventurism in its aggressive foreign policy maneuvers.
His adventurism led to international tensions and uncertain diplomatic consequences.
The region suffered instability as a result of political adventurism by nearby powers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recklessness - lack of regard for danger or consequences
  • Antonym: caution - careful avoidance of risk or danger

Etymology

Formed from 'adventure' + '-ism', indicating a tendency toward adventurous or risk-taking behavior.
