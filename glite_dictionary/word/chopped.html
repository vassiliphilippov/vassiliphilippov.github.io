chopped

1. ADJ

/ tʃɑpt / (AmE), / tʃɒpt / (BrE)
(of food or other material) prepared by cutting into small pieces.
cut into small pieces

Examples

The recipe calls for two cups of chopped onions.
She added some chopped parsley to garnish the dish.
The soup is full of fresh, chopped vegetables.

Synset Examples

sliced tomatoes
sliced ham
chopped clams
chopped meat
shredded cabbage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sliced - cut into thin pieces
  • Synonym: diced - cut into small cubes

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'chop', which comes from Middle English *choppen* (to strike, cut).
