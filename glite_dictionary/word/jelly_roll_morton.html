Jelly Roll Morton

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɛli roʊl ˈmɔrtən / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛli rəʊl ˈmɔːtən / (BrE)
(1885–1941) An American jazz pianist, composer, and bandleader considered a pioneer in the development of New Orleans jazz.
American jazz pianist and composer

Examples

Jelly Roll Morton played a key role in shaping the sound of early jazz music.
Many musicians attribute the composition 'King Porter Stomp' to Jelly Roll Morton.
The influence of Jelly Roll Morton can still be heard in modern jazz performances.
