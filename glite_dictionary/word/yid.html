yid

1. NOUN

/ jɪd / (AmE), / jɪd / (BrE)
An extremely offensive and derogatory term for a Jewish person.
offensive term for a Jewish person
offensive

Examples

The word yid is an offensive slur and should never be used.
He was shocked to overhear someone yell yid during the match.
Many organizations actively campaign against the use of words like yid in public.

Etymology

From Yiddish *Yid* meaning 'Jew', ultimately from Middle High German *jüde* via Hebrew *yəhūdhī* (Jew).
