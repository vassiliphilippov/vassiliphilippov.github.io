tegular

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛɡjələr / (AmE), / ˈtɛɡjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or resembling tiles, especially in arrangement or structure
resembling or relating to tiles

Examples

The building’s roof had a distinctive tegular pattern that caught the afternoon sun.
The lizard’s scales formed a regular, tegular arrangement along its back.
Her design features a tegular layout, making the floor easy to clean and visually striking.

Etymology

From Latin 'tegula' (tile).
