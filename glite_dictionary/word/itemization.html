itemization

1. NOUN

/ ˌaɪtəmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌaɪtɪmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of listing individual items, especially in a detailed or systematic way
the process of listing items

Examples

The detailed itemization of expenses helped her track her monthly budget more accurately.
Please ensure you include an itemization of all products on the invoice.
This report offers a thorough itemization of the committee's findings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: list - an itemized record or series of names, numbers, or other items

Etymology

From item + -ization, modeled on similar English formations.
