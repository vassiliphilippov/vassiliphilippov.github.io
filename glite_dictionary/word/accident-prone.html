accident-prone

1. ADJ

/ ˈæk.sɪ.dənt proʊn / (AmE), / ˈæk.sɪ.dənt prəʊn / (BrE)
liable to have accidents more often than is typical; tending to be frequently involved in accidents or mishaps.
often involved in accidents

Examples

He's so accident-prone that he once tripped over his own shoelaces and broke his arm.
The company took extra safety precautions for staff members who seemed particularly accident-prone.
His accident-prone nature meant he was always coming home with scrapes and bruises.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clumsy - lacking physical coordination, often leading to accidents or mistakes
