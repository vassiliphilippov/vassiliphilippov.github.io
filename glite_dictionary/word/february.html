February

1. PROPN

/ ˈfɛb.juˌɛri / (AmE), / ˈfɛb.ru.əri / (BrE)
The second month of the year, coming after January and before March.
second month of the year

Examples

Valentine's Day is celebrated in February every year.
The weather in February can be quite cold in many countries.
She scheduled her vacation for early February to avoid the crowds.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: March - the third month of the year, following February
  • Degree Lower: January - the first month of the year, preceding February

Etymology

From Latin 'Februarius', named after 'Februa', a Roman purification festival.
