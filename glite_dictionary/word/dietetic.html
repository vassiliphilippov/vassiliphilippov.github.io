dietetic

1. ADJ

/ ˌdaɪəˈtɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈtɛtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to diet, especially as it affects health.
relating to diet or nutrition
medical

Examples

The hospital employs several dietetic specialists to plan patient meals.
She follows a strict dietetic regime to manage her diabetes.
Some products in the store are labeled as dietetic foods for those needing special diets.

Synset Examples

dietary restrictions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dietary - relating to diet or prescribed diet

Etymology

From Greek *diaitētikos* (pertaining to diet), from *diaita* (way of life, diet).
