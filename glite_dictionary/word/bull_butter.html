bull butter

1. NOUN

/ bʊl ˈbʌtɚ / (AmE), / bʊl ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
(slang) Nonsense or insincere talk; something not to be believed, usually to avoid using stronger language.
nonsense; insincere talk (slang)
slang

Examples

I don't have time for your bull butter, so just answer the question honestly.
He gave me some bull butter excuse about why he was late again.
Everyone knew her story was just a load of bull butter from the start.

Synset Examples

Don't give me that bull butter; just tell the truth.
I can't believe you fell for that bull butter story.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullshit - crude slang for nonsense or lies

Etymology

Euphemism for 'bullshit', using 'butter' to soften the expression.
