needle-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈnidl̩ ˌʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈniːdl ˌʃeɪpt / (BrE)
narrow, long, and pointed in shape, resembling a needle, often used to describe leaves or other slender objects.
narrow, long, and pointed in shape

Examples

Many pine trees have needle-shaped leaves that are adapted to dry environments.
The crystals formed needle-shaped structures under the microscope.
Certain cacti have needle-shaped spines to protect themselves from herbivores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: needle-like - having the shape or appearance of a needle
