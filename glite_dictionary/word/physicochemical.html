physicochemical

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɪzɪkoʊˈkɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌfɪzɪkəʊˈkɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or concerned with both physical and chemical properties, processes, or phenomena.
pertaining to physical and chemical properties

Examples

The physicochemical properties of a substance influence its behavior in the environment.
Researchers measured the physicochemical parameters of the water samples.
This study investigates the physicochemical changes during the reaction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chemical - relating to chemistry or chemicals
  • Synonym: physical - relating to physics or the properties of matter

Etymology

Formed from 'physico-' (physical) + 'chemical'.
