generational

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənl̩ / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənəl / (BrE)
Relating to, or characteristic of, a generation or the differences between generations.
relating to a generation

Examples

There is a clear generational gap in attitudes toward technology.
The novel explores generational conflict within a single family.
Many traditions are passed down through generational lines.

Etymology

From 'generation' + '-al', patterned on other adjectives for nouns ending in -tion.
