orangish-red

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔːrɪn(d)ʒɪʃ rɛd / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪn(d)ʒɪʃ rɛd / (BrE)
Of a color that is primarily red with a noticeable tint of orange.
reddish color with orange tint

Examples

The sky glowed an orangish-red at sunset.
She painted her nails a bright orangish-red for the party.
The artist mixed the paints to create an orangish-red shade for the autumn leaves.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: red - the primary color at the end of the visible spectrum next to orange
  • Synonym: orange-red - a color between orange and red
  • Degree Higher: orange - the color between red and yellow in the visible spectrum
