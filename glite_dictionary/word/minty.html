minty

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɪn.ti / (AmE), / ˈmɪn.ti / (BrE)
Having the taste or smell of mint, especially peppermint.
having a mint flavor or aroma

Examples

The toothpaste left a minty freshness in my mouth.
I love the minty flavor of these candies.
Her breath smelled pleasantly minty after chewing gum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: herbal - having the taste or smell of herbs
  • Synonym: pepperminty - having the flavor or aroma of peppermint
  • Antonym: spicy - having a strong, pungent flavor or aroma (sometimes contrasted as non-minty)

Etymology

Formed from 'mint' + '-y', denoting possession of the qualities of mint.

2. ADJ

/ ˈmɪnti / (AmE), / ˈmɪnti / (BrE)
Tasting or smelling of mint; resembling or suggestive of mint.
smelling or tasting of mint

Examples

The toothpaste left a minty freshness in my mouth after brushing.
She offered me a piece of minty chewing gum before the meeting.
I love the minty aroma that fills the kitchen when I make herbal tea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pepperminty - having the strong, sharp flavor or smell of peppermint

Synset POS: a
