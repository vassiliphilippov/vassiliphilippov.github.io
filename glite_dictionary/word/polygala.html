Polygala

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪɡələ / (AmE), / pɒˈlɪɡələ / (BrE)
a genus of flowering plants in the milkwort family (Polygalaceae), which includes species such as milkworts and senega.
genus of flowering plants (milkworts)

Examples

Polygala species are known for their brightly colored flowers and wide distribution across temperate regions.
The herbal remedy was prepared from the roots of Polygala senega.
Researchers collected several samples of Polygala during their field study in South America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milkwort - common name for various species in the genus Polygala
  • Degree Higher: Polygalaceae - the botanical family to which Polygala belongs

Etymology

From Greek *polygala* meaning 'much milk', referring to a belief that some species increase milk production in cows.
