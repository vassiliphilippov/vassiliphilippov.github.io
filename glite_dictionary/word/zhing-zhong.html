zhing-zhong

1. ADJ

/ ʒɪŋ ˈʒɒŋ / (AmE), / ʒɪŋ ˈʒɒŋ / (BrE)
(informal) Whimsical or chaotic in style, especially used to describe decorations that are garish, mismatched, or exuberantly playful.
whimsical or chaotic decoration style
informal slang

Examples

The living room was decorated in a zhing-zhong style for the festival.
His desk is cluttered with zhing-zhong souvenirs from around the world.
She loves the zhing-zhong look of colorful streamers and mismatched lights.
