Peshmerga

1. NOUN

/ pɛʃˈmɛrɡə / (AmE), / pɛʃˈmɛːɡə / (BrE)
a member of the Kurdish military forces operating primarily in Iraqi Kurdistan
member of Kurdish military in Iraqi Kurdistan

Examples

The Peshmerga played a key role in liberating villages from ISIS control.
Many Peshmerga have been trained to handle advanced weaponry.
International aid has supported the Peshmerga in their fight against terrorism.

Etymology

From Kurdish 'pêşmerge', literally 'one who faces death' (pêş 'before' + merg 'death').
