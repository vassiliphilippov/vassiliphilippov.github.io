peshmerga

1. NOUN

/ pɛʃˈmɛrɡə / (AmE), / pɛʃˈmɛːɡə / (BrE)
a member of the Kurdish armed forces, particularly those fighting for an independent Kurdish state
Kurdish armed forces member

Examples

The peshmerga played a crucial role in combating ISIS forces in northern Iraq.
Many peshmerga soldiers are highly respected in their communities for their bravery.
A group of peshmerga advanced into the region to secure the border.

Etymology

From Kurdish 'pêşmerge', meaning 'one who faces death' (pêş 'before, in front of' + merg 'death').
