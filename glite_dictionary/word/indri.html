Indri

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndri / (AmE), / ˈɪndri / (BrE)
a large, short-tailed lemur of Madagascar with thick, silky fur in black, white, and fawn colors
large Madagascan short-tailed lemur

Examples

The Indri is known for its haunting calls that echo through the Madagascan rainforest.
Unlike other lemurs, the Indri has only a small stub of a tail.
Tourists often visit Madagascar in hopes of catching a glimpse of the rare Indri in its natural habitat.

Etymology

From Malagasy indri, the local name for the animal.
