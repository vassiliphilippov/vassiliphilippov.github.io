backache

1. NOUN

/ ˈbækˌeɪk / (AmE), / ˈbæk.eɪk / (BrE)
A continuous or repeated pain felt in the back.
pain or ache in the back

Examples

She missed work yesterday because of a severe backache.
After sleeping on the old mattress, he woke up with a terrible backache.
Regular exercise and good posture can help prevent backache.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: back pain - pain experienced in the back
