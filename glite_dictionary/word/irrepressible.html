irrepressible

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪrəˈprɛsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪrəˈprɛsəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to control, restrain, or keep down; lively and enthusiastic and difficult to suppress.
impossible to control or restrain

Examples

Her irrepressible energy made her the life of the party.
The comedian's irrepressible sense of humor kept the audience laughing.
Despite the setbacks, his optimism remained irrepressible.

Synset Examples

an irrepressible chatterbox
uncontrollable laughter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uncontrollable - not able to be controlled
  • Antonym: restrainable - able to be restrained or controlled

Etymology

From Latin in- (not) + reprimere (to press back, restrain).
