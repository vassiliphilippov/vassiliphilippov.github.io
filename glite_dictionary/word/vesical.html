vesical

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɛsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈvɛsɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the bladder, especially the urinary bladder.
relating to the bladder
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed a vesical infection after reviewing the patient's symptoms.
She underwent surgery for a vesical tumor that was causing frequent discomfort.
Vesical pressure was measured to assess the function of his bladder.

Etymology

From Latin vesica (bladder) with English -al suffix.
