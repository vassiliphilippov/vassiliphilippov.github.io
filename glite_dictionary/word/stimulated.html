stimulated

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɪmjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈstɪmjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
feeling excited or emotionally aroused, often due to external influences or events
emotionally aroused or excited

Examples

She felt stimulated by the lively discussion at the conference.
After listening to the inspiring talk, he was mentally stimulated for hours.
The energetic music left everyone feeling stimulated and uplifted.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excited - feeling or showing excitement or enthusiasm
  • Antonym: calm - not showing or feeling strong emotions

Etymology

Past participle of 'stimulate', from Latin *stimulatus*, past participle of *stimulare* (to goad, incite).
