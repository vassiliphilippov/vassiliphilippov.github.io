expressively

1. ADV

/ ɪkˈsprɛsɪvli / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that shows emotion, feeling, or thought clearly and vividly; in an expressive manner.
in an expressive manner

Examples

The actor spoke expressively, capturing the audience's attention with every word.
She used her hands expressively to describe what had happened.
The pianist played expressively, conveying the emotion behind each note.

Synset Examples

she gave the order to the waiter, using her hands very expressively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emotionally - in a way that shows strong emotions
  • Antonym: flatly - in a way that lacks emotion or expression

Etymology

From 'expressive' + '-ly'. 'Expressive' from Latin 'expressivus'.
