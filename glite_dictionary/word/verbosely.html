verbosely

1. ADV

/ vərˈboʊsli / (AmE), / vəːˈbəʊsli / (BrE)
In a manner that uses more words than necessary; with excessive wordiness.
in an overly wordy manner

Examples

The professor explained the theory verbosely, making it hard for students to follow.
Please avoid writing verbosely in your essay; be concise instead.
He tends to speak verbosely in meetings, often going off on tangents.

Synset Examples

she explained her ideas verbosely

Related Terms

  • Antonym: succinctly - in a brief and clearly expressed manner
  • Synonym: wordily - in a wordy or verbose manner

Etymology

From 'verbose' + '-ly'. 'Verbose' from Latin 'verbosus' (wordy).
