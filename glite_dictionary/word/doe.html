DOE

1. NOUN

/ diː oʊ iː / (AmE), / diː əʊ iː / (BrE)
abbreviation for the Department of Energy, the federal department responsible for the national energy policy of the United States.
US Department of Energy (abbreviation)
american

Examples

The DOE announced a new initiative to invest in renewable energy technologies this year.
Many scientists apply for grants from the DOE to fund their energy research projects.
Regulations set by the DOE affect how energy is produced and consumed across the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Department of Energy - the United States federal executive department for energy policy

Etymology

Acronym formed from the initial letters of 'Department of Energy'.
