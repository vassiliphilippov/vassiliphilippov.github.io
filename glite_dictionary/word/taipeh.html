Taipeh

1. PROPN

/ taɪˈpeɪ / (AmE), / taɪˈpeɪ / (BrE)
a variant spelling of Taipei, the capital city of Taiwan, located in the north of the island
variant of Taipei, Taiwan's capital

Examples

He travelled to Taipeh on business several times last year.
The climate in Taipeh is subtropical and humid for much of the year.
Many international companies have their regional offices in Taipeh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Taipei - the capital city of Taiwan

Etymology

From Chinese 臺北 (Táiběi), meaning 'Northern Taiwan'. 'Taipeh' is an older romanization; 'Taipei' is now standard.
