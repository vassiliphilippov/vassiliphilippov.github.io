gemmed

1. ADJ

/ dʒɛmd / (AmE), / dʒɛmd / (BrE)
decorated or covered with jewels, beads, or sequins
decorated with jewels or beads

Examples

The queen wore a magnificent, gemmed crown to the ceremony.
Her dress was gemmed with sequins that sparkled under the lights.
The ornate box was gemmed along its lid and sides, making it a true treasure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bejewelled - decorated with jewels
  • Antonym: plain - not decorated or ornamented

Etymology

From 'gem' (precious stone) + -ed suffix (forming adjectives).
