nobber

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑːbər / (AmE), / ˈnɒbə / (BrE)
an annoying or foolish person, often used in a teasing or disparaging way
annoying or foolish person
informal slang

Examples

Stop being a nobber and listen to what I'm telling you.
Everyone thinks he's a bit of a nobber for acting that way at the party.
If you keep interrupting, people will just see you as a nobber.

Synset Examples

Don't be such a nobber; just join us!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid or foolish person
  • Synonym: jerk - a contemptibly obnoxious or annoying person
  • Antonym: genius - an exceptionally intelligent person
