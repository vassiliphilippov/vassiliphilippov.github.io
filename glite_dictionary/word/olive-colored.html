olive-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑː.lɪv ˌkʌl.ɚd / (AmE), / ˈɒl.ɪv ˌkʌl.əd / (BrE)
having a yellowish-green or grayish-green color resembling that of green olives
yellowish-green color like olives

Examples

The bird's feathers were an olive-colored shade, blending perfectly with the foliage.
She wore an olive-colored dress that highlighted her hazel eyes.
The army uniform is traditionally olive-colored to provide camouflage in wooded areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: olive-green - having the muted yellowish-green color of olives
