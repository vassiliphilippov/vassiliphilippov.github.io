Geomys pinetis

1. NOUN

/ dʒiˈoʊmɪs paɪˈniːtɪs / (AmE), / dʒiːˈəʊmɪs paɪˈniːtɪs / (BrE)
A species of pocket gopher found in Alabama, Georgia, and Florida.
a gopher species of southeastern US

Examples

The Geomys pinetis is commonly found in sandy soils of the southeastern United States.
Biologists observed a Geomys pinetis burrow near the edge of the forest preserve.
The diet of Geomys pinetis consists mainly of roots and tubers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gopher - a small burrowing rodent

Etymology

From scientific Latin: genus Geomys, species pinetis, referring to pine woods.
