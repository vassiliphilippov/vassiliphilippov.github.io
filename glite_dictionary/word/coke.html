Coke

1. NOUN

/ koʊk / (AmE), / kəʊk / (BrE)
A trademarked carbonated soft drink with a cola flavor, produced by The Coca-Cola Company; commonly used to refer to any cola-flavored soft drink.
trademarked cola soft drink

Examples

I'll have a Coke with my burger, please.
She prefers Coke over other soft drinks when eating out.
They served diet Coke at the party as a low-calorie option.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cola - a type of carbonated soft drink flavored with cola nuts or similar flavorings

Etymology

Shortened form of 'Coca-Cola,' the brand name created in 1886.
