purse

1. NOUN

/ pɝːs / (AmE), / pɜːs / (BrE)
a small bag used for carrying money, cards, and personal items, typically carried by women
small bag for money and personal items

Examples

She searched her purse for her house keys.
He bought his mother a new leather purse for her birthday.
The thief snatched the woman's purse and ran away.

Synset Examples

she reached into her bag and found a comb

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wallet - a flat folding case for carrying paper money and cards

Etymology

From Old English 'purs' (bag, purse), from Medieval Latin 'bursa' (bag, purse).

Source: WordNet

/ pɝs / (AmE), / pɜːs / (BrE)

Examples
