Toyonaka

1. PROPN

/ toʊˈjoʊ.nɑː.kə / (AmE), / təʊˈjəʊ.nə.kə / (BrE)
A city in southern Honshu, Japan, serving as a main residential suburb of Osaka.
a city in southern Honshu, Japan
