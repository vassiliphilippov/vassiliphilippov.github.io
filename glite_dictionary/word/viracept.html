Viracept

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪrəˌsɛpt / (AmE), / ˈvɪərəˌsɛpt / (BrE)
A trademarked protease inhibitor drug (nelfinavir) used in combination with other medications to treat HIV infection.
HIV medication (nelfinavir)
medical

Examples

Her doctor prescribed Viracept as part of her antiretroviral therapy for HIV.
Combination therapy with Viracept has been shown to reduce viral load in patients.
The patient asked about possible side effects of taking Viracept with other HIV drugs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nelfinavir - the generic name for the drug Viracept, an HIV protease inhibitor
