unionist

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuːniənɪst / (AmE), / ˈjuːniənɪst / (BrE)
a person who belongs to and supports a trade union, especially a worker who is a member of such an organization
member of a trade union

Examples

As a proud unionist, Maria participated in the strike to demand better working conditions.
Many unionists attended the annual conference to discuss labor rights.
The company met with unionist representatives to negotiate the new contract.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trade unionist - a member of a trade union

Etymology

From 'union' + '-ist', indicating a person who supports or belongs to a union.
