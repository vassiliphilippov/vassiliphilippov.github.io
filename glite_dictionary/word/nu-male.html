nu-male

1. NOUN

/ nuːˈmeɪl / (AmE), / njuːˈmeɪl / (BrE)
A modern man who embraces progressive, often non-traditional forms of masculinity, typically associated with liberal or progressive values.
man embracing progressive masculinity
slang

Examples

Some critics argue that the nu-male challenges traditional gender roles in beneficial ways.
He was described as a nu-male because of his progressive views on masculinity.
The documentary explored the popularity of the nu-male identity among young urban men.

Synset Examples

Some view the nu-male as a positive change in culture.
He identifies as a nu-male, embracing modern masculinity.
The article discussed the rise of the nu-male in society.

Etymology

Formed from 'nu', a respelling of 'new', and 'male', suggesting a 'new kind of male'.
