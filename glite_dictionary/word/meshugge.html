meshugge

1. ADJ

/ məˈʃʊɡə / (AmE), / məˈʃʊɡə / (BrE)
senseless or crazy; behaving in a foolish or irrational way.
crazy or senseless (informal)
informal

Examples

Everyone thought he was a bit meshugge for quitting his job to travel the world with no plans.
Her meshugge idea to build a rollercoaster in the backyard actually worked!
That whole scheme sounds completely meshugge to me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mad, not sane; not thinking or behaving in a logical way
  • Antonym: sane - of sound mind; not crazy

Etymology

From Yiddish *meshugge* (mad, crazy), from Hebrew *m'shuggah* (insane).
