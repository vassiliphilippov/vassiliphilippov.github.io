Saintpaulia ionantha

1. NOUN

/ seɪntˈpɔːliə ˌaɪ.əˈnænθə / (AmE), / seɪntˈpɔːliə ˌaɪ.əˈnanθə / (BrE)
A tropical African plant widely grown as a houseplant for its violet, white, or pink flowers; also known as African violet.
flowering houseplant (African violet)

Examples

The Saintpaulia ionantha thrives in indirect sunlight and moist soil in most homes.
My grandmother has several pots of Saintpaulia ionantha decorating her windowsills.
Botanists prize the Saintpaulia ionantha for its beautiful, long-lasting flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: African violet - Common name for Saintpaulia ionantha, a popular flowering houseplant

Etymology

From the Latinized name of German botanist Walter von Saint Paul-Illaire, who discovered the plant, plus Latin 'ionantha' meaning 'violet-flowered'.
