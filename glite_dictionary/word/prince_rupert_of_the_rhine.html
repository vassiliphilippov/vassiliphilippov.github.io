Prince Rupert of the Rhine

1. PROPN

/ prɪns ˈruːpərt əv ðə raɪn / (AmE), / prɪns ˈruːpət əv ðə raɪn / (BrE)
(1619–1682) A German-born English military leader and nephew of King Charles I who commanded Royalist forces during the English Civil War.
English Royalist commander (1619–1682)

Examples

Prince Rupert of the Rhine was one of the most prominent Royalist commanders during the Civil War.
Historians debate the military strategies employed by Prince Rupert of the Rhine.
Artifacts belonging to Prince Rupert of the Rhine can be found in several British museums.
