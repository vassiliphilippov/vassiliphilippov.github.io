bimonthly

1. ADJ

/ baɪˈmʌnθli / (AmE), / baɪˈmʌnθli / (BrE)
Occurring or produced twice each month; semi-monthly.
occurring twice a month

Examples

The committee holds bimonthly meetings on the 1st and 15th of each month.
We receive a bimonthly report from the project manager.
The magazine’s bimonthly issues always feature the latest trends.

Synset Examples

a semimonthly publication

Related Terms

  • Synonym: semimonthly - occurring twice a month

Etymology

From bi- (meaning 'two' or 'twice') + monthly.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01977079-s
Synset POS: s

2. ADV

/ baɪˈmʌnθli / (AmE), / baɪˈmʌnθli / (BrE)
occurring or done twice a month
twice a month

Examples

Her bills are paid bimonthly to ensure she never misses a deadline.
The club meets bimonthly for workshops and discussions.
Employees receive their salaries bimonthly rather than on a monthly schedule.

Synset Examples

salaries are paid semimonthly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: semimonthly - occurring twice a month

Etymology

From bi- (meaning 'two') + monthly, modeled after terms such as biweekly.

Source: WordNet

3. ADV

/ baɪˈmʌnθli / (AmE), / baɪˈmʌnθli / (BrE)
Occurring or done every two months.
every two months

Examples

The magazine is published bimonthly, so subscribers receive an issue every other month.
Utility bills are often sent out bimonthly to reduce administrative costs.
He schedules his medical check-ups bimonthly to closely monitor his health.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: semimonthly - occurring twice a month

Etymology

From bi- (two) + monthly; following the pattern of biannual, biennial.

occurring every two months

Examples

5. NOUN

/ ˌbaɪˈmʌnθli / (AmE), / ˌbaɪˈmʌnθli / (BrE)
a magazine or periodical that is published either every two months or twice each month
a periodical published twice a month or every two months

Examples

I subscribe to a bimonthly that focuses on environmental issues.
The new bimonthly offers insights into the latest art trends.
Back issues of the bimonthly are available online for readers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monthly - a periodical published every month
  • Synonym: weekly - a periodical published every week

Etymology

From Latin 'bi-' (two) + 'monthly' (from 'month').
