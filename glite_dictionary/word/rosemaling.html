rosemaling

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊzəˌmɑːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzəmɑːlɪŋ / (BrE)
a traditional Scandinavian style of decorative painting or carving featuring floral designs, typically used on furniture, walls, or dinnerware
Scandinavian floral decorative art style

Examples

The ornate chest was adorned with intricate rosemaling in vibrant colors.
She studied the rosemaling techniques passed down through generations in Norway.
Many traditional Scandinavian homes feature rosemaling on their furniture and walls.

Etymology

From Norwegian 'rosemaling', meaning 'rose painting'.
