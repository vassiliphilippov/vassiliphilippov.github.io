four-footed

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɔːrˈfʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˌfɔːˈfʊtɪd / (BrE)
Having four feet, especially referring to animals.
having four feet

Examples

The four-footed mammals roamed freely across the open plains.
My daughter loves drawing pictures of four-footed creatures like dogs and horses.
Many reptiles are four-footed, using all their limbs to walk or run.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quadruped - an animal with four feet
