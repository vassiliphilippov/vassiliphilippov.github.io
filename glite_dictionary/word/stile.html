stile

1. NOUN

/ staɪl / (AmE), / staɪl / (BrE)
An upright piece in the frame of a door or window, supporting panels or glass.
upright part of door or window frame

Examples

The carpenter replaced a damaged stile in the old wooden door.
Make sure each stile is securely joined to the rails for stability.
The window's left stile was warped after years of exposure to moisture.

Etymology

From Middle English *styl*, from Old French *estile*, influenced by Latin *stilus* (stake, post).
