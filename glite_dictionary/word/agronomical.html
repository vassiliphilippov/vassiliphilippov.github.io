agronomical

1. ADJ

/ ˌæɡrəˈnɑːmɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌæɡrəˈnɒmɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the science of soil management and crop production; pertaining to agronomy.
relating to agronomy

Examples

The farmer adopted agronomical techniques to improve soil fertility and crop yields.
Recent agronomical studies have shown the benefits of crop rotation in sustainable agriculture.
Her agronomical expertise helped develop more efficient irrigation systems for arid regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agronomic - relating to agronomy

Etymology

From agronomy + -ical, modeled on similar scientific adjectives.
