specifier

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛs.əˌfaɪ.ər / (AmE), / ˈspɛs.ɪ.faɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who writes or sets out detailed requirements or instructions, especially for a project, product, or process
person who sets specifications

Examples

The specifier outlined every technical detail needed for the construction project.
As a professional specifier, she ensures all standards are clearly documented for engineering teams.
Patent applications often require a specifier to define the invention's unique attributes.

Etymology

From 'specify' + '-er', formed from Latin 'specificare' (to mention particularly).
