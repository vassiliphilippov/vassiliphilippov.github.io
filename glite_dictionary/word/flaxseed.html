flaxseed

1. NOUN

/ ˈflækˌsid / (AmE), / ˈflæksiːd / (BrE)
the small seed of the flax plant, used as a source of oil and as a food ingredient
seed of the flax plant

Examples

Many people add flaxseed to smoothies for its nutritional benefits.
Flaxseed oil is rich in omega-3 fatty acids.
She sprinkled ground flaxseed on her cereal every morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: linseed - the seed of the flax plant, especially when used for its oil
