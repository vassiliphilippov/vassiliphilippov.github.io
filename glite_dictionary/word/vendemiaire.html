Vendemiaire

1. NOUN

/ vɑnˈdeɪm.iˌɛr / (AmE), / vɒnˈdeɪm.iˌɛə / (BrE)
the first month of the French Revolutionary calendar, corresponding roughly to September and October, named after the grape harvest
first month of the French Revolutionary calendar

Examples

The uprising took place in Vendemiaire, during the early stages of the new calendar.
Many significant events of the Revolution were marked by their occurrence in Vendemiaire.
The grape harvest season inspired the naming of Vendemiaire in the Revolutionary calendar.

Etymology

French Vendémiaire, from Latin vendemia (grape harvest)
