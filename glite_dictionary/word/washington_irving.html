Washington Irving

1. PROPN

/ ˌwɑʃɪŋtən ˈɝːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌwɒʃɪŋtən ˈɜːvɪŋ / (BrE)
(1783–1859) An American writer best known for his short stories such as 'Rip Van Winkle' and 'The Legend of Sleepy Hollow'.
American writer (1783–1859)

Examples

Washington Irving's stories have captivated readers for generations.
