Verbesina alternifolia

1. NOUN

/ vərˈbiːsɪnə ˌɔːltɚnɪˈfoʊliə / (AmE), / vəːˈbiːsɪnə ˌɔːltəˈnɪfəʊliə / (BrE)
A perennial herb native to the eastern United States, with showy yellow flowers; commonly known as wingstem or yellow crownbeard.
perennial herb with yellow flowers (wingstem)

Examples

The Verbesina alternifolia brightened the riverbank with its vibrant yellow blooms.
Botanists surveyed populations of Verbesina alternifolia across several states to study pollinator activity.
Gardeners appreciate Verbesina alternifolia for attracting butterflies to native plant gardens.

Etymology

From Latin: 'Verbesina' (a genus of plants) + 'alternifolia' (having alternate leaves).
