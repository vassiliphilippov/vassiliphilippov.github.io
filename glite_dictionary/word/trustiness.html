trustiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌstinəs / (AmE), / ˈtrʌstinɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being trustworthy or worthy of confidence
the quality of being trustworthy

Examples

Her trustiness made her an invaluable member of the team.
The trustiness of the brand had been established over decades of reliability.
I admire your trustiness in all situations, even under pressure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trustworthiness - the quality of being reliable and deserving of trust

Etymology

From 'trusty' + '-ness', ultimately from Old Norse 'traust' (trust).
