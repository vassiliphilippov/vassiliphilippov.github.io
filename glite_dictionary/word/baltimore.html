Baltimore

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɔltɪˌmɔr / (AmE), / ˈbɔːltɪˌmɔː / (BrE)
the largest city in Maryland, USA, known for its major seaport and as an industrial and cultural center
largest city in Maryland, USA

Examples

Many historic landmarks can be found throughout Baltimore, reflecting its rich maritime heritage.
After moving to Baltimore, she quickly became involved in the local art scene.
The Inner Harbor is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Baltimore.

Etymology

Named after Lord Baltimore, the founding proprietor of the Maryland Colony.
