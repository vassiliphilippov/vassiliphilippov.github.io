writhen

1. ADJ

/ ˈraɪðən / (AmE), / ˈraɪðən / (BrE)
twisted or contorted, especially as if in pain or struggle; having a twisted form
twisted or contorted
archaic

Examples

The ancient tree's writhen branches sprawled against the stormy sky.
A writhen expression crossed her face as the pain intensified.
The sculptor captured the anguish of the moment in his statue's writhen features.

Synset Examples

his mad contorted smile
writhed lips
my writhen features

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twisted - bent or distorted in shape; not straight
  • Synonym: contorted - twisted or bent out of normal shape

Etymology

From Old English wrīthen, past participle of wrīthan (to twist, wind, writhe).
