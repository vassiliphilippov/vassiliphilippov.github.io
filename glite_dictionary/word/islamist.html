Islamist

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈslɑːmɪst / (AmE), / ɪˈslɑːmɪst / (BrE)
a scholar who is knowledgeable in Islamic studies
scholar of Islamic studies

Examples

The conference was attended by a renowned Islamist who delivered a keynote speech.
As an Islamist, she has published extensively on the history of Islamic law.
Many young students look up to the local Islamist for religious guidance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Islamic scholar - a person with advanced knowledge of Islamic theology and law

Etymology

From Islam + -ist, indicating a person associated with Islam.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10237005-n
Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ ɪˈslɑːmɪst / (AmE), / ɪzˈlɑːmɪst / (BrE)
A person who supports or advocates political Islam, often seeking to implement Islamic principles in government and society.
supporter of political Islam

Examples

The group was formed by Islamists seeking to establish an Islamic state.
The government's crackdown on Islamist movements intensified after the elections.
Debates about the influence of Islamist parties have emerged across the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Islamism - belief in or support of political Islam and the application of Islamic principles to government

Etymology

From Islam + -ist (denoting a person who practices or is concerned with something).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09702199-n
Synset POS: n
