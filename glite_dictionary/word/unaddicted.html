unaddicted

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnəˈdɪktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈdɪktɪd / (BrE)
Not addicted; not dependent on a substance or habit.
not addicted to something

Examples

She was proud to be unaddicted to caffeine after months of trying to quit coffee.
Many people live unaddicted lives by making healthy lifestyle choices.
After rehab, he remained unaddicted and found new ways to cope with stress.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: addicted - physically or mentally dependent on a substance or habit

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'addicted.'
