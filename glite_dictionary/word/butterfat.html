butterfat

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ərˌfæt / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌfat / (BrE)
The natural fat present in milk, and the main constituent from which butter is made.
fatty substance in milk

Examples

Premium ice cream contains higher amounts of butterfat, giving it a rich and creamy texture.
Researchers measured the butterfat content in samples from several dairy farms.
Reducing butterfat in the diet may help lower cholesterol levels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milk fat - another term for the fat content in milk
