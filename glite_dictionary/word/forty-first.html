forty-first

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɔrtiˈfɜrst / (AmE), / ˌfɔːtiˈfɜːst / (BrE)
Coming after the fortieth in a series; 41st.
ordinal number: 41st

Examples

She celebrated her forty-first birthday with friends and family.
This is the forty-first chapter in the book, where the plot takes a new turn.
He finished in forty-first place in the marathon, just a few spots ahead of his last year's result.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fortieth - ordinal number: 40th
  • Degree Higher: forty-second - ordinal number: 42nd
