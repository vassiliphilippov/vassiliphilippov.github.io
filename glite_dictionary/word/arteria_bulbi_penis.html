arteria bulbi penis

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈtɪriə ˈbʊlbaɪ ˈpiːnɪs / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə ˈbʊlbaɪ ˈpiːnɪs / (BrE)
A branch of the internal pudendal artery in males that supplies blood to the bulb of the penis.
artery branch supplying bulb of penis
medical

Examples

The arteria bulbi penis is an important vessel in male pelvic anatomy.
Damage to the arteria bulbi penis can restrict blood supply to the bulb of the penis.
Surgeons must carefully avoid the arteria bulbi penis during certain urological procedures.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: internal pudendal artery - main artery supplying the perineum and external genitalia

Etymology

From Latin: 'arteria' (artery), 'bulbi' (of the bulb), and 'penis' (penis).
