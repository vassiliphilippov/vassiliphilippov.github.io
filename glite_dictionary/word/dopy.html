dopy

1. ADJ

/ ˈdoʊpi / (AmE), / ˈdəʊpi / (BrE)
Foolish, silly, or showing a lack of intelligence.
foolish or silly
informal

Examples

He made a dopy mistake on his math test.
She felt dopy after staying up all night watching movies.
That was a really dopy idea, and it didn't work at all.

Synset Examples

ridiculous anserine behavior

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dopey - foolish or sluggish, often due to sleepiness or confusion
  • Synonym: stupid - lacking intelligence or common sense
  • Antonym: sensible - showing good sense or judgment

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'dopey', from 'dope' (a stupid person).
