Bali

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɑːli / (AmE), / ˈbɑːli / (BrE)
an island province of Indonesia east of Java, known for its volcanic landscapes, rich culture, traditional dances, and handicrafts
Indonesian island known for culture and scenery

Examples

She spent two weeks exploring the beautiful beaches and temples of Bali.
The traditional dances of Bali are famous throughout the world for their beauty and complexity.
Bali attracts tourists from around the globe thanks to its vibrant culture and natural scenery.
