PLF

1. NOUN

/ pi ɛl ɛf / (AmE), / piː ɛl ɛf / (BrE)
an abbreviation for the Palestine Liberation Front, a Palestinian militant organization formed in 1977 following a split from the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine.
Palestinian militant organization (abbreviation)

Examples

The PLF was responsible for several attacks during the 1980s.
After the split, members loyal to the PLF established new operations.
Reports indicate the PLF became closely aligned with al-Fatah over time.
