Microstomus kitt

1. NOUN

/ ˌmaɪ.kroʊˈstoʊ.məs kɪt / (AmE), / ˌmʌɪ.krəʊˈstəʊ.məs kɪt / (BrE)
A species of European flatfish, commonly known as the lemon sole, highly valued as food.
a European flatfish (lemon sole)

Examples

The delicate flavor of Microstomus kitt makes it a favorite in European seafood cuisine.
Researchers studied the population decline of Microstomus kitt in the North Sea.
Many chefs prefer to use Microstomus kitt for its tender, white flesh in gourmet dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lemon sole - common name for Microstomus kitt, a European flatfish

Etymology

From Neo-Latin; genus name Microstomus from Greek mikros (small) + stoma (mouth); species name kitt, traditional in ichthyology.
