sericulturist

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛrɪˈkʌltʃərɪst / (AmE), / ˌsɛrɪˈkʌltʃərɪst / (BrE)
a person who raises silkworms for the production of raw silk
person who produces raw silk

Examples

The sericulturist carefully tended to the silkworms in his greenhouse every morning.
As a skilled sericulturist, she supplies raw silk to several textile factories.
Modern sericulturists use advanced techniques to improve the quality and yield of silk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silkworm farmer - person who farms silkworms for silk production

Etymology

From Latin 'sericum' (silk) + 'culture' + '-ist'.
