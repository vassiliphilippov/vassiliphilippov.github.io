governable

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡʌvərnəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvənəbl̩ / (BrE)
capable of being controlled, managed, or directed; able to be governed
capable of being controlled or governed

Examples

The manager found the team highly governable and easy to lead.
With clear rules, even young children can be governable.
The small country was considered governable due to its cooperative population.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ungovernable - impossible to control or manage

Etymology

From Old French gouvernable, from Latin gubernabilis.
