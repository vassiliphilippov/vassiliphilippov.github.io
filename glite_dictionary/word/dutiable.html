dutiable

1. ADJ

/ ˈduːtiəbəl / (AmE), / ˈdjuːtiəbəl / (BrE)
liable to payment of customs duty or tax, especially on imports
subject to import tax or customs duty

Examples

All dutiable goods must be declared at customs on arrival.
The shipment included several dutiable items that required additional paperwork.
Some travelers wrongly assume that certain products are not dutiable when entering the country.

Synset Examples

dutiable imports

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxable - subject to taxation
  • Antonym: duty-free - exempt from import duty or tax

Etymology

From 'duty' + '-able', formed on the model of adjectives indicating liability.
