400th

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔr hʌndrɪθ / (AmE), / ˈfɔː hʌndrɪθ / (BrE)
being the ordinal number corresponding to four hundred in a sequence; coming after the three hundred ninety-ninth.
ordinal number for four hundredth

Examples

The city will celebrate its 400th anniversary with a grand festival next year.
He finished in 400th place out of over a thousand participants.
This is the 400th time the meeting has been held since the club was founded.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: four hundred - the cardinal number 400
  • Degree Lower: 399th - ordinal number for three hundred ninety-ninth
  • Degree Higher: 401st - ordinal number for four hundred first
