Idahoan

1. NOUN

/ ˌaɪ.dəˈhoʊ.ən / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.dəˈhəʊ.ən / (BrE)
A person who is a native or resident of the state of Idaho in the United States.
native or resident of Idaho

Examples

As an Idahoan, she takes pride in the state's natural beauty.
Many Idahoans enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking and fishing.
The festival attracted both visitors and local Idahoans.

Etymology

Formed from Idaho, the name of the U.S. state, + -an (denoting origin or relation).
