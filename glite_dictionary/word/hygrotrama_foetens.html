Hygrotrama foetens

1. NOUN

/ haɪɡrəˌtrɑmə ˈfiːətənz / (AmE), / haɪɡrəˌtrɑːmə ˈfiːətənz / (BrE)
a small grey-brown fungus notable for its unpleasant odor resembling mothballs
a small fungus with a mothball odor

Examples

Hygrotrama foetens is often identified by its distinctive mothball-like scent in damp forests.
While foraging, the strong odor helped her distinguish Hygrotrama foetens from other similar mushrooms.
The grey-brown cap of Hygrotrama foetens makes it easy to overlook among fallen leaves.

Etymology

From Greek 'hygros' (wet) and 'trama' (framework), with Latin 'foetens' meaning 'smelling'.
