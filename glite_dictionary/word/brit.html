Brit

1. NOUN

/ brɪt / (AmE), / brɪt / (BrE)
an informal term for a native or inhabitant of Great Britain
a native of Great Britain (informal)
informal

Examples

There were several Brits among the participants at the conference.
My friend is a Brit, but he's lived in New York for years.
The Brits are known for their distinctive sense of humor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Briton - a native or inhabitant of Great Britain

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'Briton' or 'British.'
