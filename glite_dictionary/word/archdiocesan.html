archdiocesan

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑːrtʃ.daɪ.əˈsiː.zən / (AmE), / ˌɑːtʃ.daɪ.əˈsiː.zən / (BrE)
Relating to an archdiocese or its affairs.
of or relating to an archdiocese

Examples

The archdiocesan offices are located next to the cathedral.
She attended an archdiocesan school throughout her childhood.
The archdiocesan guidelines were revised last year to address new issues.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: diocesan - relating to a diocese

Etymology

From 'archdiocese', itself from Greek 'arkhi-' (chief) + 'diocese'.
