interactional

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntəˈrækʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntərˈakʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving communication or mutual influence between people, systems, or things
relating to mutual influence or communication

Examples

The study focused on the interactional dynamics between teachers and students.
Effective therapy often depends on strong interactional skills.
There are various interactional patterns in group discussions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interactive - allowing a two-way flow of information or communication

Etymology

Formed from the noun 'interaction' plus the suffix '-al'.
