fifty-six

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪfti sɪks / (AmE), / ˈfɪfti sɪks / (BrE)
equal to the number 56; being six more than fifty.
the number 56

Examples

She bought fifty-six balloons for the party.
The class consists of fifty-six students this year.
He ran fifty-six miles in less than a week.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fifty-five - equal to the number 55; being one less than fifty-six
  • Degree Higher: fifty-seven - equal to the number 57; being one more than fifty-six

2. ADJ

/ ˈfɪfti sɪks / (AmE), / ˈfɪfti sɪks / (BrE)
equal to the number six more than fifty; represented by the numeral 56.
the number 56

Examples

She celebrated her fifty-sixth birthday with friends and family.
There are fifty-six students enrolled in the class this year.
The museum displayed a collection of fifty-six ancient coins.

Related Terms
