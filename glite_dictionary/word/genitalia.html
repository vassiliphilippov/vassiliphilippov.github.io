genitalia

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒɛnɪˈteɪliə / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛnɪˈteɪlɪə / (BrE)
the external sex organs, especially the reproductive organs of males and females
external sex organs

Examples

During the examination, the doctor checked the patient's genitalia for any signs of infection.
Male and female genitalia are distinctly different in structure and function.
Proper hygiene is important to prevent infections in the genitalia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genitals - sex organs, especially external reproductive organs

Etymology

From Latin 'genitalia', plural of 'genitalis' (pertaining to birth or reproduction).
