sadomasochistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌseɪdoʊˌmæsəˈkɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌseɪdəʊˌmæsəˈkɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by sadomasochism, especially in sexual practices or psychological behavior.
relating to sadomasochism

Examples

Some novels explore the sadomasochistic aspects of human relationships in explicit detail.
The film was criticized for its depiction of sadomasochistic fantasies.
They discussed sadomasochistic behaviors in the context of alternative sexualities.

Synset Examples

sadomasochistic sexual practices

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sadistic - deriving pleasure from inflicting pain
  • Synonym: masochistic - deriving pleasure from one's own pain or humiliation

Etymology

Formed from 'sadism' and 'masochism', combining French 'sadisme' and German 'Masochismus'.
