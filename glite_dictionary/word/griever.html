griever

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡriːvər / (AmE), / ˈɡriːvə / (BrE)
a person who is experiencing grief, especially due to the death of someone close
person experiencing grief

Examples

The support group was formed to help each griever share their feelings and experiences.
As a recent griever, he found comfort in writing letters to his late wife.
The counselor offered guidance to each griever coping with their loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mourner - a person who attends a funeral or feels or shows sorrow for someone who has died

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'grieve' + the agentive suffix '-er'.
