nodular

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɑːdʒələr / (AmE), / ˈnɒdjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by the presence of nodules; having a lumpy or knotty appearance.
having nodules or lumps
medical

Examples

The doctor noted that the patient's liver was nodular on palpation.
A nodular rash can indicate certain underlying conditions.
Chronic infection led to nodular changes in the tissue.

Synset Examples

Nodular vaginitis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noduled - having small knots or lumps (nodules)
  • Antonym: smooth - having an even, regular surface; not lumpy or nodular

Etymology

From Latin 'nodulus', diminutive of 'nodus' (knot).

having nodules or lumps
