Gascony

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡæskəni / (AmE), / ˈɡæskəni / (BrE)
a historical region in southwestern France, known for its distinct culture and history
region of southwestern France

Examples

She spent her summer traveling around Gascony and fell in love with its landscapes.
The wines produced in Gascony are renowned for their quality and distinctive flavor.
Many medieval tales are set in the hills and villages of Gascony.

Etymology

From French 'Gascogne', from Latin 'Vasconia', land of the Basques.
