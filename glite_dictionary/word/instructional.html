instructional

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈstrʌkʃənəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈstrʌkʃənəl / (BrE)
intended to teach; relating to teaching or the process of instruction
related to teaching or instruction

Examples

She created an instructional video to help students understand the concept.
The teacher distributed several instructional materials before the class began.
Our library offers many instructional resources for self-study.

Synset Examples

instructional aids

Related Terms

  • Synonym: educational - relating to education or the provision of education

Etymology

From 'instruction' + '-al', meaning 'pertaining to instruction'.
