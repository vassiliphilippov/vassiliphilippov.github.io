overserious

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvɚˈsɪriəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈsɪəriəs / (BrE)
excessively serious, often to a degree that is inappropriate or lacking in humor
excessively serious

Examples

He tends to become overserious during group discussions, missing the lighthearted moments.
Her overserious attitude made the meeting feel tense and uncomfortable.
We laughed at his overserious attempt to explain a simple joke.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: serious - not joking or frivolous; grave in manner or speech
  • Synonym: earnest - showing sincere and intense conviction

Etymology

From over- (excessively) + serious.
