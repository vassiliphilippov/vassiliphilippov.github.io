dg

1. NOUN

/ diː ˈdʒiː / (AmE), / diː ˈdʒiː / (BrE)
An abbreviation for decigram, a unit of mass equal to one tenth of a gram.
abbreviation for decigram (1/10 gram)

Examples

The package is labeled as containing 50 dg of sugar.
For accuracy, the pharmacist weighed the powder to the nearest dg.
Each tablet contains 2 dg of active ingredient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decigram - Unit of mass equal to one tenth of a gram

Etymology

Abbreviation of decigram, from French décigramme, based on Greek 'deci-' meaning 'tenth'.
