cheesecake

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃiːzˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːz.keɪk / (BrE)
a photograph or picture of an attractive woman, usually scantily clad, intended to be sexually appealing
sexy photo of a woman

Examples

The magazine was criticized for publishing too much cheesecake on its cover.
During the 1940s, cheesecake photos were common in men's magazines.
She dismissed the idea of posing for cheesecake shots to promote her movie.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: beefcake - a photograph of a muscular man in minimal attire, intended to be sexually appealing

Etymology

Originally from the food term; in this sense, possibly from 1930s American slang referencing the appeal of 'eye candy'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈtʃizˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzkeɪk / (BrE)
A sweet dessert consisting of a creamy mixture of cream cheese, eggs, and sugar on a crumbly base, typically made from crushed biscuits or pastry.
rich dessert made with cream cheese

Examples

She baked a delicious cheesecake for the party.
New York cheesecake is famous for its rich and creamy texture.
Would you like a slice of cheesecake for dessert?

Etymology

From 'cheese' + 'cake', indicating a cake made with cheese.
