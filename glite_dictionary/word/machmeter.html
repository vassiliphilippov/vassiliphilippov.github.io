machmeter

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑkˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈmækˌmiːtə / (BrE)
an instrument in an aircraft that measures the speed relative to the speed of sound (Mach number)
instrument measuring aircraft speed as Mach number

Examples

The pilot checked the machmeter as the aircraft approached supersonic speed.
A malfunctioning machmeter can pose serious risks during high-speed flight.
The machmeter indicated they were flying close to Mach 1.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airspeed indicator - an instrument showing the speed of an aircraft relative to the air

Etymology

From 'Mach' (after physicist Ernst Mach), combined with 'meter' (measuring device).
