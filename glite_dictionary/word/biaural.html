biaural

1. ADJ

/ baɪˈɔrəl / (AmE), / baɪˈɔːrəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving both ears or hearing with two ears.
involving both ears or hearing with two ears

Examples

Bats rely on biaural hearing to locate prey in complete darkness.
Some headphones are designed specifically for biaural sound reproduction.
The patient's biaural ability was tested using sound delivered separately to each ear.

Synset Examples

binaural hearing

Related Terms

  • Antonym: monaural - relating to or involving one ear or hearing with one ear
  • Synonym: binaural - relating to or involving both ears; another form of the same concept

Etymology

From Latin 'bi-' (two) + 'auris' (ear).
