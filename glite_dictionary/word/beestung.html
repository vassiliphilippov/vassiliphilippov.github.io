beestung

1. ADJ

/ ˈbiˌstʌŋ / (AmE), / ˈbiːstʌŋ / (BrE)
swollen or puffy in appearance, as if resulting from a bee sting, especially used to describe lips or facial features
swollen, as if stung by a bee

Examples

The model's beestung lips were the highlight of the fashion show.
After crying all night, her eyes appeared beestung and red.
The allergic reaction left his face looking slightly beestung.

Synset Examples

She looked beestung after getting stung by a bee.

Etymology

Formed from 'bee' and past participle of 'sting', indicating the effect of a bee's sting.
