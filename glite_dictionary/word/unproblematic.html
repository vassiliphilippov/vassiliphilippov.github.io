unproblematic

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌprɑːbləˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌprɒbləˈmætɪk / (BrE)
not involving difficulties, complications, or problems; easy to deal with or understand
not causing difficulties or problems

Examples

The plan seemed completely unproblematic to everyone involved.
She chose an unproblematic topic for her presentation to avoid any controversy.
Most of the process was unproblematic, but there were a few unexpected delays.

Synset Examples

an elementary problem in statistics
elementary, my dear Watson
a simple game
found an uncomplicated solution to the problem

Related Terms

  • Synonym: easy - not difficult; requiring little effort
  • Antonym: problematic - posing problems or difficulties

Etymology

From un- (not) + problematic, patterned after similar negative adjectives.
