dogy

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊ ɡi / (AmE), / ˈdəʊ ɡi / (BrE)
A motherless calf in a range herd of cattle, often raised by hand or with special care.
motherless calf in a cattle herd

Examples

After the storm, the ranch hands found a dogy wandering near the creek.
The cowboys took turns feeding the weak dogy with a bottle.
It's common practice to give extra attention to a dogy in the early weeks.
