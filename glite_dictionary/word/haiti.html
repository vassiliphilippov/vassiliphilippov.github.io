Haiti

1. PROPN

/ ˈheɪti / (AmE), / ˈheɪti / (BrE)
A country in the Caribbean occupying the western part of the island of Hispaniola; it became independent from France in 1804.
country in the Caribbean, on Hispaniola

Examples

Haiti suffered a devastating earthquake in 2010.
Many humanitarian organizations operate in Haiti to assist with healthcare and education.
French and Haitian Creole are the official languages of Haiti.

Etymology

From the Taíno word 'Ayiti', meaning 'land of high mountains'.
