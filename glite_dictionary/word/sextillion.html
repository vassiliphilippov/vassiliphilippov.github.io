sextillion

1. NOUN

/ sɛkˈstɪljən / (AmE), / sɛkˈstɪljən / (BrE)
the number represented as 1 followed by 21 zeros in the American system (10^21), or 1 followed by 36 zeros in the British system (10^36)
a number: 1 followed by 21 (US) or 36 (UK) zeros

Examples

The Milky Way contains more than a sextillion grains of dust and countless stars.
Economists predict that global data storage will reach several sextillions of bytes in the near future.
A sextillion is written as the digit one followed by twenty-one zeros in the American system.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: trillion - the number 1 with 18 zeros (US system); 1 with 18 zeros (UK system)
  • Degree Higher: septillion - the number 1 with 24 zeros (US system); 1 with 42 zeros (UK system)

Etymology

From Latin 'sextus' (sixth) + '-illion' (patterned after million, billion, etc.).
