hemline

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛmˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɛm.laɪn / (BrE)
the lower edge of a skirt, dress, or coat, considered in terms of its height or shape
lower edge of a skirt or coat

Examples

The designer chose a higher hemline to create a more modern silhouette.
She adjusted the hemline of her dress before leaving for the event.
Fashion trends often dictate whether the hemline should be above or below the knee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hem - the edge of a piece of cloth that is folded over and sewn

Etymology

Formed from 'hem' + 'line', indicating the line where the hem is sewn.
