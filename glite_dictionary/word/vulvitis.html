vulvitis

1. NOUN

/ vʌlˈvaɪtɪs / (AmE), / vʌlˈvaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the vulva, the external female genital organs.
inflammation of the vulva
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with vulvitis after evaluating her symptoms and performing an examination.
Persistent itching and redness can be signs of vulvitis in some women.
Proper hygiene and prompt treatment can help prevent recurring vulvitis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vaginitis - inflammation of the vagina

Etymology

From Latin 'vulva' (womb, covering, vulva) + '-itis' (Greek suffix meaning inflammation).
