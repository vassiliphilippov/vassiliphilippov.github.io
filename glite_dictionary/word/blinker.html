blinker

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈblɪŋkə / (BrE)
A leather flap attached to a horse's bridle to limit its vision to the front, preventing it from seeing to the sides.
device limiting a horse's side vision

Examples

The racehorse wore blinkers to stay focused during the race.
Farmers often put blinkers on their horses to prevent them from getting distracted.
He removed the blinkers after the long day at the stables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blinder - another term for blinker in this context
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04600956-n
Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ ˈblɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈblɪŋkə / (BrE)
a light that flashes on and off and is used as a signal, especially on a vehicle to indicate direction
flashing signal light, esp. in vehicles

Examples

She turned on her blinker before making a left turn at the intersection.
The car's blinker was flashing, warning other drivers of the upcoming lane change.
Remember to use your blinker when merging onto the highway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indicator - a device that shows direction on a vehicle

Etymology

From blink + -er, indicating something that blinks or causes blinking.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02855432-n

3. VERB

/ ˈblɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈblɪŋkə / (BrE)
to put blinders on (a horse) to prevent it from seeing to the side and becoming distracted or frightened
put blinders on a horse

Examples

The trainer decided to blinker the nervous horse before the race.
Some jockeys prefer not to blinker their mounts, believing it keeps them more alert.
When you blinker a horse, its field of vision narrows to help it focus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blinder - a pair of leather flaps attached to a horse’s bridle to limit vision to the side

4. NOUN

/ ˈblɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈblɪŋkə / (BrE)
a flashing light on a motor vehicle that signals the direction the driver intends to turn
flashing vehicle turn indicator
american

Examples

She forgot to turn on her blinker before changing lanes.
The law requires drivers to use their blinkers when making a turn.
He repaired the left blinker because it wasn't flashing properly.

Related Terms
