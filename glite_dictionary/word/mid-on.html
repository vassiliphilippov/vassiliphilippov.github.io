mid-on

1. NOUN

/ mɪd ˈɑn / (AmE), / mɪd ˈɒn / (BrE)
In cricket, the fielding position close to the bowler on the on side of the pitch.
cricket fielding position near bowler (on side)

Examples

He was caught at mid-on after mistiming his shot.
The captain moved the fielder closer to mid-on to catch the batsman off guard.
There was a sharp catch taken at mid-on in the first over.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mid-off - the fielding position on the opposite side of the pitch from mid-on

Etymology

From 'mid' (middle) and 'on' (the on side in cricket fielding terms).
