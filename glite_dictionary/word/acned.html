acned

1. ADJ

/ ˈæk.nɪd / (AmE), / ˈæk.nɪd / (BrE)
(of skin) affected by or showing signs of acne; having pimples or blemishes.
having acne or blemishes

Examples

The teenager felt self-conscious about his acned face during the school photo session.
Regular cleansing can help manage an acned complexion.
She covered her acned cheeks with a touch of makeup before leaving the house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acne - an inflammatory skin condition resulting in pimples

Etymology

From 'acne' + '-ed' suffix, forming an adjective from the noun.
