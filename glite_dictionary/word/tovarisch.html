tovarisch

1. NOUN

/ təˈvɑːrɪʃ / (AmE), / təˈvɒrɪʃ / (BrE)
(in Russian or Soviet contexts) a comrade or companion, especially as a term of address among communists.
Russian term for comrade

Examples

The Soviet officer addressed his fellow soldiers as tovarisch during the meeting.
In the novel, the character would often cry out, 'Tovarisch! We must stand together.'
Even now, some people jokingly call each other tovarisch when discussing politics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comrade - a companion who shares one's activities or is a fellow member of an organization

Etymology

From Russian товарищ (tovarishch), meaning 'comrade' or 'companion'.
