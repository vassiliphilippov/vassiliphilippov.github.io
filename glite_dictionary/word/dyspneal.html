dyspneal

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈniːəl / (AmE), / dɪsˈniːəl / (BrE)
Suffering from or pertaining to difficulty in breathing; experiencing shortness of breath.
having difficulty breathing
medical

Examples

The patient became dyspneal after climbing only a few stairs.
Emergency staff are trained to recognize dyspneal symptoms in incoming patients.
He described himself as dyspneal during mild exertion due to his chronic illness.

Synset Examples

breathless at thought of what I had done
breathless from running
followed the match with breathless interest

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dyspneic - relating to or suffering from dyspnea (difficulty breathing)
  • Synonym: breathless - temporarily unable to breathe easily; gasping for air

Etymology

From Ancient Greek 'dys-' (bad, difficult) + 'pnoia' (breath).
