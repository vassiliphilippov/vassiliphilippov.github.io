cherimolla

1. NOUN

/ ˌtʃɛrɪˈmoʊjə / (AmE), / ˌtʃɛrɪˈmɒljə / (BrE)
A large tropical fruit with leathery skin and sweet, soft flesh, related to custard apples.
tropical fruit related to custard apple

Examples

The market had a fresh shipment of cherimollas imported from South America.
Many people enjoy the creamy texture and unique flavor of a ripe cherimolla.
She used cherimolla in the smoothie for its sweet, tropical taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: custard apple - a tropical fruit with sweet, soft flesh related to cherimolla

Etymology

From Spanish 'chirimoya', derived from Quechua 'chirimuya' (cold seeds).
