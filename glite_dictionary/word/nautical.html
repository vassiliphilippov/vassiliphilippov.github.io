nautical

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɔtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈnɔːtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to ships, sailors, or navigation on water
relating to ships or navigation

Examples

The captain examined the nautical charts before setting sail.
She studied nautical navigation in preparation for the voyage.
His collection includes antique nautical instruments from the nineteenth century.

Synset Examples

nautical charts
maritime law
marine insurance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maritime - relating to the sea, shipping, or navigation

Etymology

From Latin 'nauticus', from Greek 'nautikos' (of ships or sailors).
