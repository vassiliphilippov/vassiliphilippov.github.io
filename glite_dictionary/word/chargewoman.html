chargewoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑrdʒˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːdʒˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female worker whose job is to blast with explosives, especially in mining or construction.
female worker who blasts with explosives

Examples

As the only chargewoman on the site, she was responsible for handling all the explosives.
The mining company hired a skilled chargewoman to oversee the blasting operations.
Each chargewoman must follow strict safety protocols during demolition projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chargeman - a worker employed to blast with explosives

Etymology

Formed from 'charge' + 'woman', modelled on 'chargeman'.
