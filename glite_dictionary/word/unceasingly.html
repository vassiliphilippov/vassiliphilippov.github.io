unceasingly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈsiːsɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsiːsɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that does not stop or relent; continuously and persistently.
continuously; without stopping

Examples

She worked unceasingly to improve conditions for her community.
The rain fell unceasingly throughout the night, drumming on the rooftop until morning.
His passion for learning drives him unceasingly toward new discoveries.

Synset Examples

dance inspires him ceaselessly to strive higher and higher toward the shining pinnacle of perfection that is the goal of every artiste

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ceaselessly - continuously; without stopping
  • Synonym: constantly - happening all the time; always
  • Antonym: intermittently - not continuous; starting and stopping at intervals

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'ceasing' from 'cease' (to stop) + suffix '-ly'.
