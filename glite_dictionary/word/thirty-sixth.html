thirty-sixth

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɜrtiˈsɪksθ / (AmE), / ˌθɜːtiˈsɪksθ / (BrE)
Coming after the thirty-fifth in a sequence; 36th.
ordinal number for 36th in sequence

Examples

She finished in thirty-sixth place out of hundreds of runners.
This is the thirty-sixth chapter in the book, so we are almost done.
Their wedding anniversary marks their thirty-sixth year together.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirty-fifth - the ordinal number for 35th in sequence
  • Degree Higher: thirty-seventh - the ordinal number for 37th in sequence
