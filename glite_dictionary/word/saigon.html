Saigon

1. PROPN

/ saɪˈɡɒn / (AmE), / saɪˈɡɒn / (BrE)
the former name of Ho Chi Minh City, a major city in southern Vietnam and the capital of French Indochina until 1954
former name of Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam

Examples

Many people still refer to Ho Chi Minh City as Saigon in everyday conversation.
The historical architecture of Saigon reflects its colonial past under French rule.
During the Vietnam War, Saigon was a critical center of political and military activity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ho Chi Minh City - current official name of the city formerly called Saigon
