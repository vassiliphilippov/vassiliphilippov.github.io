Equus hemionus

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛk.wəs hɛˈmaɪ.oʊ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɛk.wəs hɛˈmɪ.ə.nəs / (BrE)
The Asiatic wild ass, a species of wild equid native to Asia, known for its stamina and speed.
Asiatic wild ass species

Examples

The Equus hemionus once roamed widely across the Asian steppes but is now restricted to a few regions.
Researchers are studying the habitat preferences of Equus hemionus to aid in conservation efforts.
Unlike domesticated donkeys, Equus hemionus is known for its elusive nature and swift running abilities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: onager - Another name for the Asiatic wild ass (Equus hemionus), especially referring to some subspecies

Etymology

From Latin: 'Equus' (horse) + 'hemionus' (half-ass, mule), referencing its intermediate features between horses and asses.
