gray-blue

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡreɪˈbluː / (AmE), / ˌɡreɪˈbluː / (BrE)
Of a shade of blue with a tinge of grey.
bluish color tinged with grey

Examples

The sky turned a gray-blue as the evening approached.
She wore a delicate dress in a soft gray-blue hue.
The artist mixed white with blue and a little black to achieve the perfect gray-blue shade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bluish-gray - gray with a tint of blue
  • Synonym: blue-gray - a color intermediate between blue and grey
