millrace

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlˌreɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɪlˌreɪs / (BrE)
a channel that directs the flow of water to drive a millwheel
water channel for a millwheel

Examples

The old millrace behind the factory is now overgrown with grass.
Engineers repaired the millrace to restore water flow to the historic mill.
Trout like to hide in the cool currents of the millrace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: millstream - the stream of water that supplies a mill

Etymology

From 'mill' + 'race', where 'race' means a channel for water.
