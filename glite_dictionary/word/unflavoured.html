unflavoured

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfleɪvɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfleɪvəd / (BrE)
Not having any added flavoring; plain or without taste enhancement.
without added flavoring
british

Examples

The recipe calls for unflavoured gelatin to avoid altering the dessert's taste.
He prefers unflavoured yogurt so he can add his own fruit.
A cup of unflavoured tea can be surprisingly refreshing.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: flavoured - having flavoring added

Etymology

From 'un-' meaning 'not' + 'flavoured', past participle of 'flavour'.
