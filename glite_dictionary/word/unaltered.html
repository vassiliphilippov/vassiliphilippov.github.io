unaltered

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɔltɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɔːltəd / (BrE)
remaining in its original state; not changed or modified
not changed; in original state

Examples

The document has remained unaltered since it was first published.
His opinion about the issue has stayed unaltered despite all the arguments.
The painting was discovered unaltered after decades in storage.

Synset Examples

persisting unaltered through time

Related Terms

  • Antonym: changed - having been made different
  • Antonym: modified - having been altered or adjusted
  • Synonym: pristine - in its original condition; unspoiled

Etymology

From the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'altered', past participle of 'alter'.
