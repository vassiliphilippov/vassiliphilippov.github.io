strongwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɔŋˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈstrɒŋˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female political leader who rules or exercises power by force or violence
female authoritarian political ruler

Examples

The country has been ruled by a strongwoman who tolerates no dissent.
Despite international criticism, the strongwoman tightened her grip on power.
Some compare her leadership style to past strongwoman rulers in history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strongman - a male authoritarian political leader who rules by force or violence

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'strongman', from 'strong' + 'woman'.
