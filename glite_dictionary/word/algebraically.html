algebraically

1. ADV

/ ˌæl.dʒəˈbreɪ.ɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌæl.dʒɪˈbreɪ.ɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or involves algebra.
in an algebraic way

Examples

The equation can be solved algebraically or numerically, depending on the desired approach.
She demonstrated how to represent the pattern algebraically for easier manipulation.
The problem was algebraically determined to have two valid solutions.

Synset Examples

algebraically determined

Related Terms

  • Synonym: algebraic - relating to or involving algebra

Etymology

Formed from 'algebraic' + '-ally'. 'Algebraic' is from Medieval Latin 'algebraicus', from 'algebra'.
