terrifically

1. ADV

/ təˈrɪfɪkli / (AmE), / təˈrɪfɪkli / (BrE)
To an excellent or remarkable degree; extremely or exceedingly.
to an excellent or extreme degree

Examples

The new restaurant is terrifically popular with young people in the city.
She performed terrifically well at the piano recital last night.
The weather was terrifically hot during our entire vacation.

Synset Examples

her voice is superbly disciplined
the colors changed wondrously slowly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extremely - to a very great degree
  • Synonym: wonderfully - in a way that causes wonder or delight
  • Antonym: poorly - in a poor or inadequate manner

Etymology

From 'terrific' + '-ally', adverbial suffix.
