airworthiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrwɚðɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈeəwɜːðɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or condition of being fit and safe for flight
fitness for safe flight

Examples

The airline must regularly check each aircraft's airworthiness before it can carry passengers.
After several repairs, inspectors certified the jet's airworthiness once again.
New regulations require stricter tests to ensure the airworthiness of all commercial airplanes.

Synset Examples

the plane received a certificate of airworthiness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airworthy - fit to fly; safe for flight

Etymology

From 'airworthy' (fit to fly) + -ness (state or quality).
