hankey

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæŋki / (AmE), / ˈhæŋki / (BrE)
A square piece of cloth used for wiping the eyes or nose, or as a costume accessory; variant of handkerchief or hanky.
square cloth for wiping nose or eyes

Examples

He pulled a clean hankey from his pocket and handed it to her.
She always carries a floral hankey in her handbag.
After sneezing, he quickly reached for his hankey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hanky - informal variant of handkerchief
  • Synonym: handkerchief - a square cloth used for wiping the face or nose

Etymology

Variant spelling of "hanky," which is a diminutive of "handkerchief."
