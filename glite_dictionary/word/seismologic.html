seismologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌsaɪzməˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪzməˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with seismology, the scientific study of earthquakes and the movements of the Earth's crust.
relating to seismology

Examples

The seismologic data indicated a strong earthquake had occurred nearby.
Researchers used seismologic methods to study the Earth's interior structure.
Improvements in seismologic technology have led to more accurate earthquake predictions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seismological - relating to seismology

Etymology

From seismology + -ic.
