cervical

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɝː.vɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.vɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to the cervix of the uterus.
relating to the cervix (uterus)
medical

Examples

A routine screening can help detect cervical abnormalities early.
Many women are vaccinated to prevent cervical cancer caused by HPV.
She scheduled an appointment with her doctor for a cervical smear test.

Synset Examples

cervical cancer

Etymology

From Latin 'cervix' meaning 'neck', referring here to the 'neck' of the uterus.
