Srbija

1. PROPN

/ ˈsər.bi.ja / (AmE), / ˈsɛər.bɪ.ja / (BrE)
The Serbian name for Serbia, a historical and geographical region in central and northern Yugoslavia, now a sovereign state in southeastern Europe.
Serbia (Serbian name); Balkan country/region

Examples

The capital city of Srbija is Beograd, also known as Belgrade.
Many historic monasteries can be found throughout Srbija.
The Danube River runs through the northern part of Srbija.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Serbia - A country in Southeast Europe, official name for the region

Etymology

From Serbian Србја (Srbija), the native name for Serbia.
