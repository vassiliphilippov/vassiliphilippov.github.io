Milton

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɪltən / (AmE), / ˈmɪltən / (BrE)
(1608–1674) An English poet best known for his epic poem 'Paradise Lost', exploring themes of humanity's fall from grace.
English poet, author of 'Paradise Lost'

Examples

Many consider Milton one of the greatest English poets of all time.
'Paradise Lost' is Milton's most famous work, admired for its rich language and themes.
