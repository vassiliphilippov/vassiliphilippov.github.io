high-keyed

1. ADJ

/ haɪˈkid / (AmE), / haɪˈkiːd / (BrE)
(of a person) very excitable or easily agitated; highly strung.
excitable or highly strung (person)

Examples

His high-keyed temperament made him prone to anxiety in stressful situations.
The team was full of high-keyed individuals who thrived on competition.
She is naturally high-keyed, reacting strongly to even small changes in her routine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excitable - easily excited; quick to respond emotionally
  • Antonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness or other strong emotions
