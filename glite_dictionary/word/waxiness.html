waxiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈwæksi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈwæksi.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being made of or resembling wax, or being covered with wax
the quality of being waxy or covered with wax

Examples

The waxiness of the candle's surface made it smooth to the touch.
Doctors noted an unusual waxiness in the patient's skin, which needed further investigation.
Over time, the fruit's waxiness helped protect it from moisture loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waxy - having the appearance or texture of wax

Etymology

From waxy + -ness, indicating the state or quality of being waxy.
