shouldered

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃoʊldərd / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldəd / (BrE)
(in combination) having shoulders of the kind specified; used in adjectives to indicate shoulder characteristics.
having shoulders as specified (in compounds)

Examples

The tall, broad-shouldered man entered the room with quiet confidence.
She admired the elegant, narrow-shouldered silhouette of the dress.
A stoop-shouldered figure sat quietly in the corner, lost in thought.

Synset Examples

stoop-shouldered
broad-shouldered
