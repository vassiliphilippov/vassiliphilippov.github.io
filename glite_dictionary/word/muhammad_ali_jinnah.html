Muhammad Ali Jinnah

1. PROPN

/ mʊˈhɑːməd ɑːˈli dʒɪˈnɑː / (AmE), / mʊˈhæməd ɑːˈliː dʒɪˈnɑː / (BrE)
(1876–1948) Indian statesman and founder of Pakistan as a Muslim state.
founder of Pakistan; Indian statesman

Examples

Muhammad Ali Jinnah played a leading role in the creation of Pakistan in 1947.
Many consider Muhammad Ali Jinnah the father of the nation in Pakistan.
Muhammad Ali Jinnah's vision shaped the country's founding principles.
