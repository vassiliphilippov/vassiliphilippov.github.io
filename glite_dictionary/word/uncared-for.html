uncared-for

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkɛrd fɔr / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɛəd fɔː / (BrE)
not receiving the necessary care, attention, or maintenance; neglected.
not given care or attention; neglected

Examples

The uncared-for garden was overgrown with weeds and brambles.
After years of being uncared-for, the old house began to fall apart.
Some pets become ill if they are left uncared-for for long periods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neglected - not receiving proper care or attention
