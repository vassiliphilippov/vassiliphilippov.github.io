ideation

1. NOUN

/ ˌaɪdiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌaɪdiːˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of forming and developing ideas, especially in a creative or problem-solving context
the process of forming ideas

Examples

The marketing team held a session devoted to ideation for their next advertising campaign.
Creative ideation plays a crucial role in the development of innovative products.
During therapy, patients are sometimes encouraged to engage in positive ideation to improve their outlook.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brainstorming - a group problem-solving technique involving the spontaneous contribution of ideas

Etymology

From Late Latin 'ideatio', from Latin 'idea' (idea) + '-tion' (forming nouns of action).
