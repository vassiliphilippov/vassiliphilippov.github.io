opportunely

1. ADV

/ əˈpɚ.tuːn.li / (AmE), / ɒˈpɔː.tʃuːn.li / (BrE)
At a suitable or convenient time; in a way that is well-timed or advantageous for a particular purpose.
at a suitable or convenient time

Examples

The courier delivered the package opportunely, just before the office closed for the weekend.
Her offer to help arrived opportunely when I was overwhelmed with work.
The rain stopped opportunely, allowing the parade to continue as planned.

Synset Examples

he arrived rather opportunely just when we needed a new butler

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conveniently - in a way that is suitable or comfortable for a particular purpose
  • Antonym: inopportunely - at an inconvenient or unsuitable time

Etymology

Formed from 'opportune' + '-ly'; 'opportune' from Latin opportunus (fitting, favorable).
