ex

1. NOUN

/ ɛks / (AmE), / ɛks / (BrE)
a woman who was formerly married to a particular man; a former wife
former wife

Examples

After the divorce, he occasionally ran into his ex at the supermarket.
She didn't want to invite her ex to the wedding.
All his exes live in Texas, according to the song.

Synset Examples

all his exes live in Texas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ex-wife - a woman who is no longer someone's wife
  • Antonym: ex-husband - a man who is no longer someone's husband

Etymology

Shortened from 'ex-wife', from Latin ex- (out of, from).

2. NOUN

/ ɛks / (AmE), / ɛks / (BrE)
a man who was formerly a particular woman's husband, boyfriend, or partner
former husband or male partner
informal

Examples

After the divorce, she rarely spoke to her ex.
Her ex sent her a message on her birthday.
People often compare their new partners to their ex.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ex-wife - a woman who was formerly married to someone
  • Antonym: current husband - a man who is currently married to someone

Etymology

Shortened from 'ex-husband' or 'ex-boyfriend', from Latin 'ex-' meaning 'out of, from'.

Related Terms
