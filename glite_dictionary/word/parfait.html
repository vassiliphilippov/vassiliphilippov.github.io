parfait

1. NOUN

/ pɑːrˈfeɪ / (AmE), / pɑːˈfeɪ / (BrE)
A dessert consisting of layers of ice cream, syrup, and whipped cream, typically served in a tall glass.
layered dessert with ice cream and syrup

Examples

She ordered a strawberry parfait for dessert after dinner.
The restaurant serves a delicious chocolate parfait layered with nuts and caramel.
You can easily make a fruit parfait at home using yogurt and berries.

Etymology

From French 'parfait' (meaning 'perfect'), originally used for a kind of frozen dessert.
