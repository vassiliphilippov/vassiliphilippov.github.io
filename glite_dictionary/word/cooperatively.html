cooperatively

1. ADV

/ koʊˈɑːpərətɪvli / (AmE), / kəʊˈɒpərətɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that involves working together with others for a common purpose
in cooperation with others

Examples

The students worked cooperatively to complete the group project on time.
All departments must function cooperatively if we want the company to succeed.
The team members solved the problem cooperatively rather than competing against each other.

Synset Examples

they work hand in glove

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collaboratively - in a collaborative manner; working together with others
  • Antonym: independently - in a way that does not rely on others; acting alone

Etymology

From 'cooperative' + '-ly'; ultimately from Latin 'cooperari' (to work together).
