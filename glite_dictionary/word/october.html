October

1. PROPN

/ ɑkˈtoʊbɚ / (AmE), / ɒkˈtəʊbə / (BrE)
The tenth month of the year, following September and preceding November.
the tenth month of the year

Examples

The leaves begin to change color in October as autumn sets in.
Her birthday is in October, and she always celebrates with a big party.
School usually starts a new term in early October in some countries.

Etymology

From Latin 'October', from 'octo' meaning 'eight', as it was the eighth month in the Roman calendar.
