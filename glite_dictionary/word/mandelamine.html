Mandelamine

1. NOUN

/ ˌmændl̩əˈmiːn / (AmE), / ˌmændl̩əˈmiːn / (BrE)
A trademarked antibacterial agent used in the treatment of urinary tract infections.
antibacterial agent (trademark), for urinary infections
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Mandelamine to help prevent further urinary tract infections.
Patients with recurring UTIs may benefit from Mandelamine as part of their treatment plan.
Before taking Mandelamine, inform your physician about any allergies you may have.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: methenamine - antibacterial drug used for urinary tract infections; Mandelamine is a trade name for this
