perineum

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɛrəˈniəm / (AmE), / ˌpɛrɪˈniːəm / (BrE)
the area of the body between the anus and the genitals
area between anus and genitals
medical

Examples

During the examination, the doctor checked the perineum for any signs of injury.
Childbirth can sometimes cause tears in the perineum.
Good hygiene is important to maintain the health of the perineum.

Etymology

From Late Latin perinaeum, from Greek perinaion (the space between the anus and the scrotum or vulva).
