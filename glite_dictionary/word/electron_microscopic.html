electron microscopic

1. ADJ

/ ɪˌlɛktrɑn ˌmaɪkrəˈskɑpɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrɒn ˌmaɪkrəˈskɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving an electron microscope; visible or measurable only by means of an electron microscope.
relating to an electron microscope

Examples

The electron microscopic examination revealed tiny structures undetectable by light microscopy.
Researchers analyzed the samples using electron microscopic techniques to identify cellular abnormalities.
Detailed electron microscopic images were crucial in understanding the virus's structure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microscopic - relating to or visible only with a microscope
