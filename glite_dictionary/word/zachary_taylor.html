Zachary Taylor

1. PROPN

/ ˈzækəri ˈteɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈzækəri ˈteɪlə / (BrE)
(1784–1850) The 12th President of the United States, who served from 1849 until his death in 1850.
12th President of the United States (1849–1850)

Examples

Many historians debate whether Zachary Taylor's death changed the course of American history.
The presidency of Zachary Taylor was marked by tensions over the expansion of slavery.
Tourists often visit the site of Zachary Taylor's tomb in Kentucky.
