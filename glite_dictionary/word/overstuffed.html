overstuffed

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈstʌft / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈstʌft / (BrE)
(of furniture) upholstered with an excessive or thick amount of padding, making it very soft and full.
thickly padded (furniture)

Examples

The living room featured an overstuffed armchair that was perfect for relaxing after work.
She sank into the overstuffed sofa and let out a sigh of relief.
His apartment was filled with overstuffed furniture that looked incredibly comfortable.

Synset Examples

an overstuffed sofa

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: stuffed - upholstered or filled with material to make soft or full

Etymology

From 'over-' (excessive) + 'stuffed' (filled with material), indicating extra stuffing or padding.
