handmade

1. ADJ

/ hændˈmeɪd / (AmE), / hændˈmeɪd / (BrE)
made by hand rather than by machine, typically with individual care and attention to detail
made by hand, not machine

Examples

She wore a beautiful handmade scarf that her grandmother knitted for her.
The pottery shop specializes in handmade bowls and plates crafted by local artists.
Every handmade card in that collection is unique and full of character.

Synset Examples

delicate handmade baby dresses

Related Terms

  • Antonym: machine-made - made by machine, not by hand
