stoutheartedness

1. NOUN

/ staʊtˈhɑːrtɪdnəs / (AmE), / staʊtˈhɑːtɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being brave and determined in difficult situations
courageous and determined character

Examples

Her stoutheartedness inspired the rest of the team to face the challenge.
In times of crisis, stoutheartedness can make all the difference.
People admired his stoutheartedness in speaking out against injustice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: courage - the ability to do something that frightens one; bravery
  • Antonym: cowardice - lack of bravery
  • Synonym: bravery - courageous behavior or character

Etymology

From 'stouthearted' (stout + hearted) with suffix '-ness' forming a noun.
