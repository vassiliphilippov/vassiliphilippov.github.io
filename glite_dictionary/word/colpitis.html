colpitis

1. NOUN

/ kɑlˈpaɪtɪs / (AmE), / kɒlˈpaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the vagina, usually caused by infection or irritation.
inflammation of the vagina
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with colpitis after complaining of persistent vaginal discomfort.
Certain types of bacteria and fungi can lead to colpitis if not properly managed.
Proper hygiene and prompt treatment reduce the risk of colpitis in women.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vaginitis - inflammation of the vagina; common synonym in medical usage

Etymology

From Greek kolpos (vagina) + -itis (inflammation).
