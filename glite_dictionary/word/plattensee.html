Plattensee

1. PROPN

/ ˈplɑːtənseɪ / (AmE), / ˈplatənzeɪ / (BrE)
the German name for Lake Balaton, a large shallow lake in western Hungary, popular as a tourist destination.
German name for Lake Balaton, a Hungarian lake

Examples

Many tourists visit the Plattensee each summer for its beautiful scenery and mild waters.
Sailing competitions are regularly held on the Plattensee in Hungary.
She fondly remembers family holidays by the shores of the Plattensee during her childhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lake Balaton - a large shallow lake in western Hungary, also known as Plattensee

Etymology

From German Platten (meaning 'plates' or 'flat') and See (meaning 'lake').
