everywoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛvriˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈɛvriˌwʊmən / (BrE)
an ordinary or typical woman who represents all women
an ordinary or typical woman

Examples

The novel's heroine is an everywoman facing everyday challenges and triumphs.
She became an icon for the everywoman, showing that ordinary people can achieve great things.
Many viewers connected with the everywoman character, seeing themselves in her experiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: everyman - an ordinary or typical man who represents all men

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'Everyman', combining 'every' and 'woman'.
