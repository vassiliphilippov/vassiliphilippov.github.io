ketorolac

1. NOUN

/ kəˈtoʊrəˌlæk / (AmE), / kɛˈtɒrəlæk / (BrE)
A nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to relieve moderate to severe pain, often prescribed for short-term management.
nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug for pain relief
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed ketorolac to manage her postoperative pain.
Patients taking ketorolac should be monitored for potential side effects like stomach ulcers.
You should not use ketorolac for more than five days without medical advice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: NSAID - nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug
