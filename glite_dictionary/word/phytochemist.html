phytochemist

1. NOUN

/ ˌfaɪtoʊˈkɛmɪst / (AmE), / ˌfʌɪtəʊˈkɛmɪst / (BrE)
A chemist who specializes in the chemistry of plants.
chemist specializing in plant chemistry

Examples

The phytochemist analyzed the leaves to identify active plant compounds.
She became a leading phytochemist after years of research on medicinal herbs.
His work as a phytochemist contributed to the discovery of new plant-based medicines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: botanist - a scientist who studies plants

Etymology

From Greek 'phyto-' (plant) and 'chemist'.
