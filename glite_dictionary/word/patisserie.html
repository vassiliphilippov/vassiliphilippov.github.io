patisserie

1. NOUN

/ pəˈtɪsəri / (AmE), / pæˈtiːsəri / (BrE)
a bakery where French pastries and cakes are made and sold
French pastry bakery

Examples

We stopped at a patisserie to try their famous éclairs and tarts.
Her dream is to open a patisserie that serves authentic French desserts.
The smell of fresh croissants wafted from the busy patisserie on the corner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bakery - a shop where bread, cakes, and pastries are made or sold

Etymology

From French 'pâtisserie', from 'pâtissier' (pastry cook).
