Chongqing

1. PROPN

/ tʃʊŋˈtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / tʃɒŋˈtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
A large city in south-central China, located on the Yangtze River, known as a major commercial and industrial center for western China.
major city in southwest China

Examples

The booming economy of Chongqing has attracted many foreign investors in recent years.
Many tourists visit Chongqing for its spicy cuisine and dramatic hillside scenery.
The river cruise from Chongqing down the Yangtze is a popular way to see the Three Gorges.

Etymology

From Mandarin 重庆 (Chóngqìng), meaning 'double celebration' or 'double happiness'.
