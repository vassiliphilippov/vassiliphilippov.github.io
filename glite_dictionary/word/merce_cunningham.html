Merce Cunningham

1. PROPN

/ mɝs ˈkʌnɪŋhæm / (AmE), / mɜːs ˈkʌnɪŋham / (BrE)
(1919–2009) An American dancer and choreographer known for his innovative work in modern dance and collaborations with leading artists.
American modern dancer and choreographer
