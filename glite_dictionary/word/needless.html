needless

1. ADJ

/ ˈniːdləs / (AmE), / ˈniːdləs / (BrE)
Not necessary; not required or justified.
not necessary; unwarranted

Examples

The manager apologized for the needless delays in processing our application.
She suffered a needless injury because the safety rules were ignored.
Let's avoid any needless arguments and focus on solving the problem.

Synset Examples

a strikers' tent camp … was burned with needless loss of life

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unnecessary - not needed; not required
  • Antonym: essential - absolutely necessary; extremely important

Etymology

Formed from 'need' + '-less' (meaning 'without need').
