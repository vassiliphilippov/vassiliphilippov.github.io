Baisakh

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪ.sɑːk / (AmE), / bʌɪˈsɑːk / (BrE)
The second month of the Hindu calendar, usually corresponding to April–May in the Gregorian calendar.
second month of the Hindu calendar

Examples

The festival of Vaisakhi is celebrated during Baisakh every year in northern India.
Many farmers begin sowing their crops at the start of Baisakh.
According to the Hindu calendar, his birthday falls in Baisakh.

Etymology

From Sanskrit *Vaiśākha*, name of a month in the Hindu calendar.
