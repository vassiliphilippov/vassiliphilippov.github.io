donation

1. NOUN

/ doʊˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / dəʊˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
An act of giving money, goods, or time to help a person or organization, especially a charity.
act of giving to a cause or charity

Examples

She made a generous donation to the local animal shelter.
All donations will be used to support community projects.
The charity relies on donation from the public to continue its work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contribution - a gift or payment to a common fund or collection
  • Synonym: gift - something that is given to someone without payment

Etymology

From Latin donatio, donare (to give).

2. NOUN

/ doʊˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / dəʊˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
Something, especially money, that is given to a person or organization such as a charity in order to help them.
something given to help, especially money or goods

Examples

Several companies made a generous donation to the disaster relief fund.
She decided to make a donation to the local animal shelter.
The museum is funded entirely by donation from the public.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contribution - something that is given to help achieve or provide something, especially money
  • Synonym: gift - something voluntarily given to another person or group

Etymology

From Latin 'donatio' (a giving, gift), from 'donare' (to give).

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: n
