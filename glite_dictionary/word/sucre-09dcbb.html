sucre

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːkreɪ / (AmE), / ˈsuːkreɪ / (BrE)
the former basic unit of currency in Ecuador, subdivided into 100 centavos
former Ecuadorian currency unit

Examples

During my visit to Quito, I paid for souvenirs in sucre.
The value of the sucre declined sharply before Ecuador switched to the US dollar.
Old coins marked in sucres are now collectors' items.

Etymology

Named after Antonio José de Sucre, a leader in Latin American independence.
