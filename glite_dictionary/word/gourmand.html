gourmand

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊrˈmɑnd / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːmənd / (BrE)
a person who takes great pleasure in eating and drinking, often to excess
person who enjoys eating and drinking excessively

Examples

As a true gourmand, he never missed the chance to sample a new restaurant.
The annual food festival attracts every gourmand in the city.
She was a gourmand who enjoyed indulging in lavish meals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glutton - a person who eats to excess
  • Degree Higher: gourmet - a person who has a refined taste in food and drink

Etymology

From French gourmand (one who is fond of eating and drinking), related to 'gourmet' but with a focus on quantity.
