funniness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌn.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfʌn.i.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being amusing or causing laughter; a humorous incident or series of incidents
quality of being amusing or comic incident

Examples

The movie's funniness kept the audience laughing throughout the night.
He relied on his natural funniness to lighten tense situations at work.
We all appreciated the unexpected funniness of the conversation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humor - the quality of being amusing or comic
  • Antonym: seriousness - the quality of being solemn or earnest

Etymology

From 'funny' + '-ness', forming a noun from the adjective.
