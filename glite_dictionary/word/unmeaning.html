unmeaning

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmiːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈmiːnɪŋ / (BrE)
lacking meaning, sense, or significance; meaningless
lacking meaning or sense

Examples

His apology felt completely unmeaning, as if he just said it out of obligation.
She gave him an unmeaning nod and turned away without interest.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

Etymology

From un- (not) + meaning.
