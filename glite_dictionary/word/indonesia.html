Indonesia

1. PROPN

/ ˌɪn.doʊˈniː.ʒə / (AmE), / ˌɪn.dəˈniː.zi.ə / (BrE)
a large country in Southeast Asia consisting of an archipelago of more than 13,000 islands, officially the Republic of Indonesia.
country in Southeast Asia, archipelago nation

Examples

I have always wanted to visit Indonesia because of its beautiful islands and rich culture.
Bali is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Indonesia.
Indonesia is known for its diverse languages and unique wildlife.

Etymology

From Latin 'Indus' (India) + Greek 'nēsos' (island); named for its location and island geography.
