Combretum bracteosum

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈbriːtəm ˌbræk.tiˈoʊ.səm / (AmE), / kɒmˈbriːtəm ˌbræk.tiˈəʊ.səm / (BrE)
An ornamental African shrub or climbing plant with red flowers, belonging to the Combretum genus.
ornamental African shrub with red flowers

Examples

The Combretum bracteosum brightened the garden with its clusters of red flowers.
Botanists often recommend Combretum bracteosum for ornamental landscaping in warm climates.
I noticed a hummingbird feeding from the blossoms of the Combretum bracteosum near the fence.

Etymology

From Latin, used in botanical nomenclature: Combretum (the genus), bracteosum (with conspicuous bracts).
