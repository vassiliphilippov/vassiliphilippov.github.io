to boat-race

1. VERB

/ ˈboʊt ˌreɪs / (AmE), / ˈbəʊt ˌreɪs / (BrE)
to take part in a race using boats, usually as a sport or competition
take part in a race with boats

Examples

Every summer, our team boat-races on the river against rival schools.
They boat-raced across the lake, determined to win the trophy.
As the sun rose, dozens of crews gathered to boat-race in the regatta.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: race - to compete in speed against others; to participate in a race
