Billy Graham

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɪli ˈɡreɪəm / (AmE), / ˈbɪli ˈɡreɪəm / (BrE)
(1918–2018) A prominent American evangelical Christian preacher, known for his mass evangelism and large-scale revival meetings.
American evangelical preacher and mass evangelist

Examples

Billy Graham held crusades around the world, drawing thousands to his sermons.
Many leaders sought advice from Billy Graham during his long career.
The legacy of Billy Graham continues to influence evangelical Christianity today.
