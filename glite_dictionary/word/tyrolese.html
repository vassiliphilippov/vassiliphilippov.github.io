Tyrolese

1. ADJ

/ ˌtaɪ.rəˈliːz / (AmE), / ˌtɪə.rəˈliːz / (BrE)
relating to the Tyrol region or its people, culture, or language.
of Tyrol or its people/culture

Examples

The Tyrolese costume is famous for its distinctive hats and embroidery.
She performed traditional Tyrolese songs during the cultural festival.
A Tyrolese village nestled in the Alps attracts tourists every year.

Synset Examples

Tyrolean yodeling

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tyrolean - relating to Tyrol or its people

Etymology

From Tyrol, a region in the Alps, plus the suffix -ese indicating origin.
