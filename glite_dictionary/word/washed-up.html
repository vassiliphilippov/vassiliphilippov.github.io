washed-up

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɑʃtˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌwɒʃtˈʌp / (BrE)
No longer effective, successful, or popular; having lost former fame or influence.
no longer successful or popular

Examples

The critics said the actor was washed-up, but he made a dramatic comeback.
Many believed the band was washed-up, yet their new album topped the charts.
Some athletes refuse to admit they're washed-up and continue to compete.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finished - no longer capable of success or effectiveness
  • Antonym: thriving - successful, prosperous, or growing strongly
