alphabetized

1. ADJ

/ ˈæl.fə.bəˌtaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈæl.fə.bə.taɪzd / (BrE)
(of items) arranged in alphabetical order.
arranged in alphabetical order

Examples

All the files in the cabinet are alphabetized by last name.
She quickly alphabetized the list of students to find the name she needed.
The books on the shelf are alphabetized by author for easy access.

Synset Examples

the cards are all alphabetized, as you requested

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alphabetical - relating to or arranged according to the alphabet

Etymology

From the verb 'alphabetize' + -ed.
