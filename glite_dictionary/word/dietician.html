dietician

1. NOUN

/ ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
A person who is an expert in diet and nutrition, advising on healthy eating and creating diets for individuals or groups.
expert in diet and nutrition

Examples

The hospital employed a dietician to help patients plan healthy meals.
You should consult a dietician before making significant changes to your diet.
Her work as a dietician involves creating personalized nutrition plans for clients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutritionist - an expert in nutrition

Etymology

From diet + -ician (patterned after physician).
