left over

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛft ˌoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɛft ˌəʊvə / (BrE)
remaining after the rest has been used or consumed; not used up.
remaining; not used up

Examples

There was some pizza left over from last night's dinner.
After paying all the bills, I had a little money left over.
She put the left over fabric in a drawer for future projects.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surplus - an amount that is more than needed; excess
  • Synonym: remaining - still left after other parts have been used or taken
  • Antonym: spent - used up; expended
