financial

1. ADJ

/ faɪˈnænʃəl / (AmE), / faɪˈnænʃəl / (BrE)
relating to money, banking, or investments, or the management of money.
relating to finances or money matters

Examples

The company is facing serious financial difficulties this year.
She received expert financial advice before buying the house.
We need to plan carefully to avoid future financial problems.

Synset Examples

fiscal responsibility

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fiscal - relating to government revenue, especially taxes

Etymology

From French 'financier', from finance (money affairs), originally from Latin 'finis' (end, settlement).
