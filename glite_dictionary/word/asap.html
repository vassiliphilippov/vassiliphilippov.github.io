ASAP

1. ADV

/ ˌeɪ ˌɛs ˌeɪ ˈpi / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˌɛs ˌeɪ ˈpi / (BrE)
abbreviation for 'as soon as possible'
abbreviation: as soon as possible
informal

Examples

Please reply to my email ASAP, as this is urgent.
The manager asked for the report ASAP so we can meet the deadline.
Let me know your decision ASAP so we can move forward with the project.

Etymology

Acronym formed from the initial letters of 'as soon as possible'.
