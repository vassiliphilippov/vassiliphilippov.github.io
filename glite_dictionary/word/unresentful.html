unresentful

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnrɪˈzɛntfəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈzɛntfəl / (BrE)
Not feeling or showing resentment; free from feelings of bitterness or ill will.
not feeling resentment

Examples

She remained unresentful despite the unfair criticism from her colleagues.
His unresentful attitude helped mend broken relationships in the family.
Even after losing the promotion, she was remarkably unresentful toward her boss.

Synset Examples

completely unresentful and forgiving

Related Terms

  • Antonym: resentful - feeling or expressing bitterness or indignation

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' and 'resentful.'
