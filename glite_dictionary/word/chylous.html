chylous

1. ADJ

/ ˈkaɪ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈkaɪ.ləs / (BrE)
Consisting of or resembling chyle; having the properties of chyle, a milky bodily fluid formed during digestion.
relating to or resembling chyle
medical

Examples

The surgeon noted a chylous effusion during the operation.
A chylous fistula can sometimes occur after neck surgery.
The fluid was identified as chylous based on its milky appearance and high triglyceride content.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chyle - a milky bodily fluid formed during digestion

Etymology

From chyle + -ous; chyle from Greek 'khylos' (juice).
