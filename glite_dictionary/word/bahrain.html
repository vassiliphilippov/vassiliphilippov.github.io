Bahrain

1. PROPN

/ bəˈreɪn / (AmE), / bəˈreɪn / (BrE)
an island country in the Persian Gulf, off the coast of Saudi Arabia, known for its oil resources and modern development
island country in the Persian Gulf

Examples

The Formula 1 Grand Prix is held annually in Bahrain, attracting fans from around the world.
She moved to Bahrain to work for an international bank based in Manama.
Oil revenues transformed Bahrain into one of the most modern states in the region.

Etymology

From Arabic البحرين (al-Baḥrayn), meaning 'the two seas'.
