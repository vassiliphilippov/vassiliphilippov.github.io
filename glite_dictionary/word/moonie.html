Moonie

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːni / (AmE), / ˈmuːni / (BrE)
(often derogatory) A member of the Unification Church, a religious movement founded by Sun Myung Moon.
member of the Unification Church
offensive

Examples

Many former Moonies have shared their experiences after leaving the movement.
The media often used the term Moonie to refer to followers of Sun Myung Moon.
He grew up in a family of Moonies and later chose a different path.

Etymology

From the name of Sun Myung Moon, founder of the Unification Church, with the suffix -ie.
