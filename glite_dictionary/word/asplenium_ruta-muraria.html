Asplenium ruta-muraria

1. NOUN

/ æsˈpliːniəm ˌruːtə mjʊˈrɛriə / (AmE), / æsˈpliːniəm ˌruːtə mjʊəˈreərɪə / (BrE)
a small, delicate species of spleenwort fern, commonly found on walls and cliffs in Eurasia and North America
a type of spleenwort fern

Examples

The old stone bridge was covered with Asplenium ruta-muraria growing in the cracks.
Botanists discovered a rare patch of Asplenium ruta-muraria on the northern cliffs.
You can often spot Asplenium ruta-muraria thriving on ancient castle walls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wall rue - common English name for Asplenium ruta-muraria

Etymology

From Latin: Asplenium (genus name for spleenwort) and ruta-muraria, meaning 'wall rue'.
