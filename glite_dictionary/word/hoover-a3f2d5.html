to hoover

1. VERB

/ ˈhuːvər / (AmE), / ˈhuːvə / (BrE)
to clean a floor, carpet, etc., using a vacuum cleaner
clean with a vacuum cleaner
british

Examples

I need to hoover the living room before our guests arrive.
She hoovered the carpets yesterday and now everything looks spotless.
He spends Sunday mornings hoovering the entire house.

Synset Examples

vacuum the carpets

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vacuum - to clean with a vacuum cleaner

Etymology

From the brand name Hoover, a major manufacturer of vacuum cleaners.
