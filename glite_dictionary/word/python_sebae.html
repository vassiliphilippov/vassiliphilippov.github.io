Python sebae

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪθɑn sɪˈbeɪ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪθən sɪˈbiː / (BrE)
A very large species of python native to tropical and southern Africa, also known as the African rock python.
large python species from Africa

Examples

The Python sebae can reach lengths of over six meters, making it one of the world's largest snakes.
Wildlife photographers spent months tracking the elusive Python sebae in the grasslands of southern Africa.
Despite its size, the Python sebae is known for its powerful constricting abilities when subduing prey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: African rock python - Large python species native to Africa

Etymology

From New Latin, combining the genus name Python and species epithet sebae, named after Dutch naturalist Albertus Seba.
