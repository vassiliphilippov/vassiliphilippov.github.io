militarized

1. ADJ

/ mɪˈlɪtəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / mɪˈlɪtəraɪzd / (BrE)
Equipped with military arms or organized for military purposes.
equipped with military arms

Examples

The government has created a militarized zone along the border to increase security.
Many local police departments have become increasingly militarized in recent years, with new weapons and vehicles.
The region became militarized after the outbreak of conflict, restricting civilian access.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: demilitarized - removed from military control or stripped of military arms

Etymology

From 'militarize' (verb), formed from military + -ize, meaning to make military in character.
