glittering

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡlɪt̬ɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪtərɪŋ / (BrE)
shining with many bright, flashing points of light; sparkling or brilliantly illuminated.
shining with bright flashes of light

Examples

The city skyline looked glittering at night, reflecting off the river below.
She wore a glittering necklace that caught everyone's attention at the party.
A glittering layer of frost covered the field at dawn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sparkling - shining with many small flashes of light; similar meaning
  • Antonym: dull - lacking brightness or shine; opposite meaning

Etymology

Present participle of 'glitter', from Middle English 'gliteren', probably of Germanic origin.
