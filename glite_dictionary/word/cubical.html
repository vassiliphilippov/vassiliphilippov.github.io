cubical

1. ADJ

/ ˈkjuːbɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈkjuːbɪkəl / (BrE)
having the shape of a cube; shaped like a cube.
shaped like a cube

Examples

The architect designed a cubical building that stood out among the nearby towers.
Sugar is often sold in cubical packets at cafés in France.
He marveled at the perfectly cubical crystal he found in the rock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cube-shaped - having the shape of a cube
  • Antonym: spherical - having the shape of a sphere

Etymology

From Latin cubiculum (bedroom), from cubare (to lie), with later sense shaped by analogy to "cube".
