spelaeologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌspiːliˈɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌspiːliˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who explores caves, especially as a scientific or recreational activity
person who explores caves

Examples

The spelaeologist carefully mapped the underground tunnels for future expeditions.
As an experienced spelaeologist, she has discovered several previously uncharted caves.
The conference attracted spelaeologists from around the world to discuss safe exploration techniques.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caver - a person who explores caves, typically as a hobby
  • Synonym: speleologist - a person who explores or studies caves

Etymology

From Latin 'spelaeum' (cave) + Greek '-logia' (study of).
