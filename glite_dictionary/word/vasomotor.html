vasomotor

1. ADJ

/ ˌveɪ.soʊˈmoʊ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˌveɪ.səʊˈməʊ.tə / (BrE)
relating to the nerves or muscles that influence the constriction or dilation of blood vessels
relating to regulation of blood vessel diameter
medical

Examples

The vasomotor nerves regulate blood flow by adjusting the diameter of arteries.
She experienced vasomotor symptoms, such as hot flashes, during menopause.
A disruption in vasomotor function can lead to problems with blood pressure.

Etymology

From Latin 'vas' (vessel) + 'motor' (mover).
