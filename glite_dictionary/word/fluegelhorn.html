fluegelhorn

1. NOUN

/ ˈfluːɡəlˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈflʊɡəlˌhɔːn / (BrE)
a brass musical instrument similar to a cornet but with a wider, conical bore, producing a mellower tone
brass instrument like a cornet, mellower tone

Examples

The jazz band featured a fluegelhorn solo during their final piece.
She prefers the warm sound of the fluegelhorn over the sharper tone of the trumpet.
A brass ensemble usually includes at least one fluegelhorn among its instruments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cornet - a brass instrument similar in shape to the trumpet but shorter and with a mellower tone
  • Synonym: trumpet - a brass wind instrument with a bright, penetrating tone

Etymology

From German Flugelhorn, literally 'wing horn', originally used for signaling at flanks (wings) of an army.
