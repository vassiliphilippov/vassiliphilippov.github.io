Busan

1. PROPN

/ buːˈsɑːn / (AmE), / buːˈsæn / (BrE)
a large city and major port in southeastern South Korea, located on the Korean Strait; the country's second largest city
city and port in South Korea

Examples

Many international conferences have been hosted in Busan due to its excellent facilities.
She traveled from Seoul to Busan by high-speed train last summer.
The bustling markets in Busan are famous for fresh seafood and vibrant street food.

Etymology

From Korean 부산 (Busan).
