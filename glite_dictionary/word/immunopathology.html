immunopathology

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪm.jə.noʊ.pəˈθɑl.ə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌɪm.juː.nəʊ.pəˈθɒl.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
the branch of medicine that studies diseases related to abnormalities or dysfunction of the immune system
study of diseases caused by immune system disorders
medical

Examples

She decided to specialize in immunopathology after her interest in autoimmune diseases grew.
Recent advances in immunopathology have improved our understanding of allergies and immune responses.
The conference covered numerous topics in immunopathology, including the mechanisms of transplant rejection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immunology - the study of the immune system and its functions

Etymology

From immuno- (relating to the immune system) + pathology (study of disease).
