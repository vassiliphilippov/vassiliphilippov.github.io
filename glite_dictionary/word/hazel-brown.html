hazel-brown

1. ADJ

/ ˈheɪzəl ˈbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈheɪzəl ˈbraʊn / (BrE)
Of a shade of brown tinged with hazel, typically used to describe eye or hair color.
brown with a hazel tinge (color)

Examples

Her hazel-brown eyes sparkled in the afternoon sunlight.
The puppy had a soft, hazel-brown coat that gleamed after its bath.
We painted the fence a warm, hazel-brown to match the autumn leaves.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: hazel - light brownish-yellow or greenish-brown color
  • Degree Lower: brown - dark color between red and yellow; typical of earth or wood
