criminological

1. ADJ

/ ˌkrɪmɪnəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌkrɪmɪnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the scientific study of crime and criminals
relating to criminology

Examples

This report provides a criminological analysis of urban violence.
The university offers several criminological research opportunities for graduate students.
Her paper explores the criminological theories that explain why people commit crimes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: criminology - the scientific study of crime and criminals

Etymology

From criminology + -ical, ultimately from Latin 'crimen' (crime).
