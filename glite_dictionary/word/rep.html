rep

1. NOUN

/ rɛp / (AmE), / rɛp / (BrE)
A fabric with prominent, rounded crosswise ribs, often used for upholstery or clothing.
fabric with rounded, crosswise ribs

Examples

The curtains were made of a thick rep that blocked out the sunlight.
He chose a dark blue rep for reupholstering the antique sofa.
The designer recommended rep for its durability and elegant texture.

Etymology

From French 'reps', perhaps from English 'rib(s)'.

2. NOUN

/ rɛp / (AmE), / rɛp / (BrE)
An informal abbreviation of 'representative,' used especially for a person who represents a company, organization, or group.
informal abbreviation for 'representative'
informal

Examples

The company's rep visited our office yesterday to discuss the new products.
If you have any issues with your order, please contact your sales rep.
She works as a rep for a pharmaceutical company.
