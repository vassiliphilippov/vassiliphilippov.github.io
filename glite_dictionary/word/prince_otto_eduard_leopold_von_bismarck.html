Prince Otto Eduard Leopold von Bismarck

1. PROPN

/ ˈoʊtoʊ ˈɛdˌwɑrd liˈoʊpəld vɑn ˈbɪzˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈɒtəʊ ˈɛdwɑːd liˈəʊpɒlt fɒn ˈbɪzmɑːk / (BrE)
(1815–1898) German statesman and the first Chancellor of the German Empire, known for uniting Germany and his influential role in European affairs.
German statesman; first Chancellor of Germany

Examples

Prince Otto Eduard Leopold von Bismarck played a crucial role in the unification of Germany.
Many historians regard Prince Otto Eduard Leopold von Bismarck as one of the great statesmen of the nineteenth century.
The policies introduced by Prince Otto Eduard Leopold von Bismarck greatly influenced European politics for decades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bismarck - shortened name; German Chancellor who united Germany
  • Synonym: Otto von Bismarck - shortened but standard form of his full name
