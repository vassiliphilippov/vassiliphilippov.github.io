operations

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃənz / (AmE), / ˌɒpəˈreɪʃənz / (BrE)

brokerage financial activities

Examples

Recent upgrades in technology have improved the operations involved in transferring client funds.
He decided to pursue a career in operations within the investment banking sector.
