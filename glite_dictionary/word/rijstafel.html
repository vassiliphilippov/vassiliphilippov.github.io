rijstafel

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪstɑːfəl / (AmE), / ˈrɪstəfəl / (BrE)
A meal of Indonesian origin consisting of a wide variety of foods and sauces served with rice.
Indonesian meal of assorted dishes with rice

Examples

The restaurant offers an authentic rijstafel with over a dozen traditional Indonesian dishes.
We enjoyed a delicious rijstafel during our trip to Amsterdam.
He prepared a homemade rijstafel for his dinner party guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rice table - A literal translation and synonym for rijstafel, referring to the same style of meal

Etymology

From Dutch 'rijsttafel', meaning 'rice table'.
