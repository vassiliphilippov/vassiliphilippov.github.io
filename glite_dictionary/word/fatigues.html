fatigues

1. NOUN

/ fəˈtiɡz / (AmE), / fəˈtiːɡz / (BrE)
loose clothing worn by military personnel while performing manual or non-ceremonial duties; military work uniform
military work uniform

Examples

The soldiers changed into their fatigues before starting maintenance duty.
She wore her army fatigues during the training exercises.
His fatigues were stained with mud after a long day in the field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uniform - standardized clothing worn by members of an organization

Etymology

Derived from 'fatigue' (exhaustion from work), referencing clothing worn during laborious tasks.
