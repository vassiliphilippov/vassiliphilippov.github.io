St. Vincent

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt ˈvɪnsənt / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈvɪnsənt / (BrE)
an island in the Caribbean, the largest of the islands comprising Saint Vincent and the Grenadines
Caribbean island; largest in Saint Vincent and the Grenadines

Examples

We spent our vacation relaxing on St. Vincent and exploring its volcanic landscapes.
The capital city of St. Vincent is Kingstown, known for its colonial architecture.
Many tourists visit St. Vincent for its beautiful beaches and vibrant culture.

Etymology

From Saint Vincent, named by Christopher Columbus after Saint Vincent of Saragossa.
