Cuban

1. ADJ

/ ˈkjuːbən / (AmE), / ˈkjuːbən / (BrE)
of or relating to Cuba or its people, language, or culture.
of or relating to Cuba or its people

Examples

The restaurant is famous for its Cuban sandwiches and strong coffee.
She studied Cuban history while traveling in Havana.
The festival featured traditional Cuban music and dancing.

Synset Examples

Cuban rum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cuban - a person from Cuba

Etymology

From Cuba + -an suffix, denoting origin or association.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkjuː.bən / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.bən / (BrE)
A person who is a native or inhabitant of Cuba.
a person from Cuba

Examples

Several Cubans settled in Miami after leaving their homeland.
He met a group of Cubans while traveling through Havana.
The Cuban greeted us warmly and offered to show us around the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cuban - of or relating to Cuba, its people, or their language or culture (adjective)
  • Antonym: foreigner - a person not native to a particular place; an outsider

Etymology

From Spanish 'cubano', from Cuba + '-ano'.
