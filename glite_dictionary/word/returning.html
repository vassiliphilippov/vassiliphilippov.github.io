returning

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈtɝnɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈtɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a condition or state) tending to reappear or revert to an earlier occurrence or form.
tending to reappear or recur

Examples

After months of treatment, his returning symptoms began to worry the doctors again.
The returning pattern in fashion often brings back styles from decades ago.
Many chronic illnesses have returning issues that patients must learn to manage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recurrent - happening repeatedly or periodically
  • Synonym: persistent - continuing to exist or endure over a prolonged period
