sweatband

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɛtˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈswɛtˌbænd / (BrE)
A band of fabric or leather sewn inside the crown of a hat to absorb sweat.
band inside a hat to absorb sweat

Examples

The sweatband in my favorite hat keeps the sweat from dripping down my face.
After years of use, the sweatband inside his cap had become discolored.
This hat's sweatband is made of soft leather for extra comfort.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hatband - a decorative band on the outside of a hat

2. NOUN

/ ˈswɛtˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈswɛtˌbænd / (BrE)
A strip of absorbent material worn around the forehead or wrist to absorb sweat, especially during physical activity.
band to absorb sweat

Examples

She wore a sweatband during her run to keep perspiration out of her eyes.
The tennis player adjusted his sweatband before serving the ball.
He always brings an extra sweatband to practice in case one gets soaked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headband - band worn around the head
  • Synonym: wristband - band worn around the wrist
