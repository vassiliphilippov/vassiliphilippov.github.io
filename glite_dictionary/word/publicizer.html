publicizer

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪˌsaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪˌsaɪzə / (BrE)
a person or organization that makes something widely known or brings it to public attention
someone who brings things to public attention

Examples

The publicizer ensured that the charity event received media coverage across the city.
As a skilled publicizer, she brought many important issues to public attention.
The book was successful thanks in part to its energetic publicizer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promoter - a person who promotes something, especially events
  • Synonym: publicist - a person responsible for publicizing a product, person, or company

Etymology

From English 'publicize' + '-er'.
