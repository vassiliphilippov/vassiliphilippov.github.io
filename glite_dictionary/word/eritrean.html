2. NOUN

/ ɪˌrɪˈtriːən / (AmE), / ɪˌrɪˈtriːən / (BrE)
a person from Eritrea or of Eritrean descent
person from Eritrea

Examples

Many Eritreans celebrate their Independence Day with parades and festivities.
She met a group of Eritrean students at the international conference.
The restaurant is owned by a local Eritrean who moved here ten years ago.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Eritrea - country in northeastern Africa

Etymology

From Eritrea, the name of the East African country, from the Latin 'Mare Erythraeum' (Erythraean Sea).
