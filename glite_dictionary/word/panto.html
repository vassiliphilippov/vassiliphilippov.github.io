panto

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæn.toʊ / (AmE), / ˈpæn.təʊ / (BrE)
An informal abbreviation for pantomime, especially a Christmas theatrical entertainment popular in Britain.
informal abbreviation for pantomime
informal british

Examples

The children are looking forward to this year's panto at the local theatre.
She landed the leading role in the Christmas panto.
Going to a panto is a tradition for many British families during the holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pantomime - a type of musical comedy stage production, traditionally performed at Christmas in Britain

Etymology

Abbreviation of the word 'pantomime'.
