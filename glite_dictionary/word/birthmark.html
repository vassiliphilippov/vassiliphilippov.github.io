birthmark

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝθˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθˌmɑːk / (BrE)
a mark on the skin present from birth, often differing in color or texture from surrounding skin
mark on skin present from birth

Examples

She has a small birthmark on her left shoulder.
The doctor said the birthmark was completely harmless.
His unique birthmark made him easy to identify.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mole - a small dark spot or raised area on the skin, sometimes present from birth

Etymology

From 'birth' + 'mark', indicating a mark present from birth.
