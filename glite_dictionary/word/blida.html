Blida

1. PROPN

/ ˈbliːdə / (AmE), / ˈbliːdə / (BrE)
A city in northern Algeria located at the foot of the Atlas Mountains, southwest of Algiers.
city in northern Algeria

Examples

The city of Blida is famous for its beautiful gardens and proximity to the Atlas Mountains.
After their trip to Algiers, the group decided to visit Blida for its unique blend of culture and history.
Many citrus fruits exported from Algeria are grown in the fertile lands around Blida.
