smallish

1. ADJ

/ ˈsmɔːlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːlɪʃ / (BrE)
fairly small in size; somewhat small
rather small in size

Examples

The apartment is smallish, but it's perfect for one person.
She wore a smallish hat decorated with a single flower.
Despite its smallish size, the car felt surprisingly spacious inside.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: small - of a size that is less than normal or usual
  • Degree Higher: medium - of average or moderate size

Etymology

Formed by combining 'small' with the suffix '-ish', which means 'somewhat' or 'rather'.
