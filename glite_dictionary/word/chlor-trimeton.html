Chlor-Trimeton

1. NOUN

/ klɔr ˈtraɪmətɑn / (AmE), / klɔː ˈtraɪmətɒn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for an antihistamine (chlorpheniramine) used to relieve symptoms of allergy, hay fever, and the common cold.
antihistamine brand name for allergy relief
medical

Examples

My doctor recommended Chlor-Trimeton to manage my seasonal allergies.
You can purchase Chlor-Trimeton over the counter at most pharmacies.
If you are allergic to pollen, Chlor-Trimeton may help relieve your symptoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chlorpheniramine - antihistamine used to treat allergy symptoms

Etymology

Brand name, formed from 'chloro' (referring to chlorine) and 'trimeton' (unclear origin, possibly invented for branding).
