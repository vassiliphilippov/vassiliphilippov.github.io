advisement

1. NOUN

/ ədˈvaɪzmənt / (AmE), / ədˈvaɪzmənt / (BrE)
careful consideration or thoughtful deliberation, especially before making a decision
careful consideration

Examples

The proposal was taken under advisement before any decision was made.
After due advisement, the committee announced their final recommendation.
The judge held the case under advisement until all evidence was reviewed.

Synset Examples

a little deliberation would have deterred them

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deliberation - careful thought or discussion before making a decision
  • Antonym: haste - excessive speed or urgency of action; lack of deliberation

Etymology

From Middle English, influenced by Old French *avisement* (consideration, reflection), from *aviser* (to consider).
