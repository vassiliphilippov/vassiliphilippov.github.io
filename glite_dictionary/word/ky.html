KY

1. NOUN

/ ˌkeɪ ˈwaɪ / (AmE), / ˌkeɪ ˈwaɪ / (BrE)
The official postal abbreviation for Kentucky, a state in the east central United States, known for horse breeding and its Civil War history.
abbreviation for Kentucky (U.S. state)
american

Examples

Letters addressed to KY may be sent to any city in Kentucky.
The famous Kentucky Derby takes place annually in KY.
Many beautiful horse farms are located throughout KY.
