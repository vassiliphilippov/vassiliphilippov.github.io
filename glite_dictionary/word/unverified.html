unverified

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈvɛrɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈvɛrɪˌfaɪd / (BrE)
not confirmed as true, accurate, or authentic by evidence or investigation.
not confirmed by evidence

Examples

The news article was based on unverified information and was later corrected.
Her claim remained unverified due to a lack of supporting evidence.
It is dangerous to spread unverified rumors on social media.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: verified - confirmed as true or accurate by evidence
  • Synonym: confirmed - shown to be definitely true or correct

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'verified', from Latin 'verificare' (to make true).
