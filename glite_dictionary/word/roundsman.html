roundsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈraʊndzmən / (AmE), / ˈraʊndzmən / (BrE)
a male worker who makes regular rounds to deliver goods or conduct inspections, typically on a set route
male worker making regular deliveries or inspections

Examples

The roundsman visited every store on his route before noon each day.
As a roundsman, he was responsible for checking each building’s fire safety systems.
The milk roundsman delivered fresh bottles to our doorstep every morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deliveryman - a man who delivers goods to customers
