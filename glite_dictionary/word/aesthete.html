aesthete

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛsˌθit / (AmE), / ˈiːsθiːt / (BrE)
a person who has or professes a special appreciation of art and beauty, especially in nature or the arts
person sensitive to art and beauty

Examples

As an aesthete, he spends his weekends visiting art galleries and botanical gardens.
She was often described as an aesthete for her passion for classical music and sculpture.
The dinner party attracted every aesthete in the city, all eager to discuss paintings and poetry.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: philistine - a person who is indifferent or hostile to art and culture

Etymology

From Greek aisthētēs (one who perceives), related to aisthanesthai (to perceive, to feel).
