Primus

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪməs / (AmE), / ˈprʌɪməs / (BrE)
a portable paraffin (kerosene) stove, typically used for cooking outdoors or by campers
portable paraffin camping stove

Examples

The hikers prepared dinner on their Primus as the sun set behind the mountains.
Make sure to bring your Primus if you're camping in remote areas without electricity.
Her vintage Primus still works perfectly after all these years.

Etymology

Trademark, originally of Swedish origin; from Latin 'primus' (first), used as a brand name for the first such stoves.
