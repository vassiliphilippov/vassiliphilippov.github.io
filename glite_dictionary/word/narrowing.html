narrowing

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæroʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
an instance or process of becoming narrow or more restricted in width or scope
instance of becoming narrow

Examples

There is a significant narrowing of the road ahead, so be cautious when driving.
Doctors detected a narrowing of the arteries during the examination.
The narrowing of his interests alarmed his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: constriction - the action or process of making something narrower or tighter
  • Antonym: widening - the process of becoming wider
