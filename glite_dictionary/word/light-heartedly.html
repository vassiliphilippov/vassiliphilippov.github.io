light-heartedly

1. ADV

/ ˌlaɪtˈhɑrtɪdli / (AmE), / ˌlaɪtˈhɑːtɪdli / (BrE)
In a cheerful and carefree manner; without seriousness or anxiety.
in a cheerful, carefree manner

Examples

She spoke light-heartedly about her adventures abroad, making everyone laugh.
He answered the difficult question light-heartedly, refusing to worry about the consequences.
The team discussed their setbacks light-heartedly, showing their positive attitude.

Synset Examples

he light-heartedly overlooks some of the basic facts of life

Related Terms

  • Antonym: seriously - in a serious or earnest manner
