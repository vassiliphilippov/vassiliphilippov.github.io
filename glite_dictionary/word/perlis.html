Perlis

1. PROPN

/ pɜrˈliːs / (AmE), / pəˈliːs / (BrE)
the smallest state in Malaysia, located in the northwestern part of Peninsular Malaysia, bordering Thailand and the state of Kedah.
the smallest Malaysian state

Examples

I spent a week exploring the natural beauty of Perlis.
The border between Thailand and Malaysia runs through the state of Perlis.
Many tourists visit Perlis for its peaceful landscapes and unique cuisine.
