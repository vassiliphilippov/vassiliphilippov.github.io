28th

1. ADJ

/ ˈtwɛnti ˈeɪtθ / (AmE), / ˈtwɛnti ˈeɪtθ / (BrE)
coming after the twenty-seventh in order; being the ordinal number for twenty-eight
ordinal number for twenty-eight

Examples

Her birthday falls on the 28th of February every leap year.
He finished 28th in the marathon, just behind his teammate.
This is the 28th time I have visited this museum.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 27th - coming after the twenty-sixth in order
  • Degree Higher: 29th - coming after the twenty-eighth in order
