lithoglyptics

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɪθəˈɡlɪptɪks / (AmE), / ˌlɪθəˈɡlɪptɪks / (BrE)
the art or practice of engraving designs or inscriptions onto precious stones
engraving on precious stones

Examples

She studied lithoglyptics to master the ancient techniques used in gemstone engraving.
The museum's collection includes several outstanding examples of lithoglyptics from the Roman Empire.
He wrote a detailed book on the history and methods of lithoglyptics.

Etymology

From Greek lithos (stone) + glyptikos (of carving).
