Alupent

1. NOUN

/ ˈæluˌpɛnt / (AmE), / ˈæljuˌpɛnt / (BrE)
A trademarked bronchodilator medication used to treat asthma, emphysema, and other lung conditions.
trademarked bronchodilator drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Alupent to help control her asthma symptoms.
If you experience shakiness after taking Alupent, inform your physician immediately.
Alupent is available in both inhalant and tablet forms for respiratory relief.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metaproterenol - A bronchodilator medication; the generic name of Alupent.
