tetragon

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛtrəˌɡɑn / (AmE), / ˈtɛtrəɡən / (BrE)
A polygon with four sides and four angles; a quadrilateral.
four-sided polygon

Examples

The shape of the garden was a perfect tetragon with equal sides.
In geometry class, we studied the properties of a tetragon and compared it to other polygons.
Each window was designed as a tetragon, giving the building a unique look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quadrilateral - polygon with four sides

Etymology

From Greek 'tetragōnos' (having four angles), from 'tetra-' (four) + 'gōnia' (angle).
