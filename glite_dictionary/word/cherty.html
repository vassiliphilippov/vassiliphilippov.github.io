cherty

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɜrti / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːti / (BrE)
Containing or resembling chert; made up of or characterized by the presence of chert.
containing or resembling chert

Examples

The geologist noted that the outcrop was composed of cherty limestone.
Several cherty layers could be seen running parallel to the rest of the formation.
This region is known for its cherty soils, which affect local vegetation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chert - a hard, fine-grained sedimentary rock composed of microcrystalline quartz

Etymology

From 'chert', a hard, fine-grained sedimentary rock, with -y forming an adjective.
