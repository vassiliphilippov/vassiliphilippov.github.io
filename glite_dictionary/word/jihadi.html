jihadi

1. ADJ

/ dʒɪˈhɑːdi / (AmE), / dʒɪˈhɑːdi / (BrE)
relating to jihad or to individuals who participate in jihad, especially in the context of militant Islamic activity.
relating to jihad or jihadists

Examples

Extremist groups spread jihadi propaganda through various online platforms.
The authorities intensified their efforts to counter jihadi networks.
They uncovered a plan with suspected jihadi connections during the investigation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jihadist - a person who participates in or advocates jihad, especially in a militant form

Etymology

From Arabic 'jihādī' (relating to jihad).
