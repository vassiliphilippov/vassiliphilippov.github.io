Bogartian

1. ADJ

/ boʊˈɡɑːrtiən / (AmE), / bəʊˈɡɑːtɪən / (BrE)
Of, relating to, or in the style of Humphrey Bogart, especially in reference to his manner or roles in film.
in the style of Humphrey Bogart

Examples

The detective's Bogartian demeanor made the film feel like a classic noir.
She wore a trench coat and hat in true Bogartian fashion.
His voice had a Bogartian rasp that brought old Hollywood to mind.

Etymology

Formed from the name Humphrey Bogart (1899–1957), American film actor, with the adjectival suffix -ian.
