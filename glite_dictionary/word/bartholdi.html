Bartholdi

1. PROPN

/ bɑːrˈtoʊldi / (AmE), / bɑːˈtɒldi / (BrE)
(1834–1904) French sculptor best known for designing the Statue of Liberty in New York Harbor.
French sculptor; creator of the Statue of Liberty

Examples

Bartholdi is celebrated as the artist who designed the iconic Statue of Liberty.
Many art historians study the works of Bartholdi for their monumental style.
Bartholdi's legacy lives on through his contributions to public art.

Etymology

From the French surname Bartholdi; full name Frédéric Auguste Bartholdi.
