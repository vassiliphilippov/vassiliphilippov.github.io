Linz

1. PROPN

/ lɪnts / (AmE), / lɪnts / (BrE)
A city in northern Austria, located on the Danube River, known as a cultural centre.
city in northern Austria

Examples

She plans to visit Linz during her trip across Austria.
Linz hosts several renowned art festivals each year.
The Danube flows through Linz, adding to its scenic beauty.

Etymology

From German Linz, the name of the city.
