Muzzer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌzər / (AmE), / ˈmʌzə / (BrE)
a person who curates or selects music tracks, especially for playlists, events, or digital platforms
person who curates music tracks
informal

Examples

She’s known as the Muzzer who selects all the tracks for the station.
As a Muzzer, he is always searching for new artists to feature on his playlists.
The club hired a Muzzer to create a unique vibe with every setlist.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curator - a person who selects and organizes content, such as in a museum or for media
  • Synonym: DJ - a person who selects and plays recorded music for an audience
