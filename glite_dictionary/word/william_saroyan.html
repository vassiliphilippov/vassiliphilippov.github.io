William Saroyan

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪljəm səˈrɔɪən / (AmE), / ˈwɪljəm səˈrɔɪən / (BrE)
(1908–1981) Armenian-American writer known for his plays and short stories depicting the immigrant experience and everyday life.

William Saroyan won the Pulitzer Prize for Drama in 1940 for his play "The Time of Your Life."
