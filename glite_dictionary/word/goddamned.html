goddamned

1. ADV

/ ˈɡɑdˌdæmd / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌdæmd / (BrE)
Used to express strong anger, annoyance, or emphasis, often considered vulgar and offensive.
vulgar intensifier; expresses anger or annoyance
vulgar offensive

Examples

I don't want to hear another goddamned excuse from you.
Can you please turn off that goddamned alarm?
It's about time you fixed the goddamned car.

Synset Examples

you are goddamn right!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: damned - used for emphasis; expressing annoyance or anger

Etymology

From 'God' and 'damn', historically used as a curse.

2. ADJ

/ ˈɡɑdˌdæmd / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌdamd / (BrE)
(Offensive, vulgar, informal) Used to express anger or emphasis, especially to intensify a statement or refer disparagingly to a person or thing.
used for emphasis; offensive intensifier
informal offensive vulgar

Examples

I don't want to hear another goddamned word about it.
The goddamned car broke down again on my way to work.
Why is this goddamned paperwork never finished?

Synset Examples
