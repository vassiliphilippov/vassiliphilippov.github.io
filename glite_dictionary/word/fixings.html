fixings

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪksɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈfɪksɪŋz / (BrE)
the accessories or additional items that are typically served with or used to accompany something, especially food or a special activity
accompaniments or accessories, especially for food

Examples

Thanksgiving dinner was delicious, with turkey, stuffing, and all the fixings.
The barbecue comes with all the fixings, including baked beans and coleslaw.
She prepared burgers and set out a table with all the fixings so everyone could make their own.

Synset Examples

an elaborate formal dinner with all the fixings
he bought a Christmas tree and trimmings to decorate it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trimmings - additional items or accompaniments served with a main dish

2. NOUN

/ ˈfɪksɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈfɪksɪŋz / (BrE)
various ingredients or accompaniments served with a main dish, especially as part of a meal or recipe
ingredients or accompaniments for a dish
american

Examples

We served the turkey with all the fixings, including stuffing, cranberry sauce, and mashed potatoes.
The barbecue included burgers, hot dogs, and all the fixings you could want.
She bought the fixings for a hearty salad, such as lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.

Synset Examples

the recipe lists all the fixings for a salad

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trimmings - additional items served with a meal, e.g., roast and all the trimmings
