advertent

1. ADJ

/ ædˈvɝːtənt / (AmE), / ədˈvɜːtənt / (BrE)
paying careful attention; attentive; deliberately observant
attentive; giving attention

Examples

The judge was advertent to every detail of the testimony.
She made an advertent effort to listen to each speaker's concerns.
His advertent attention to the safety protocols prevented any accidents.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inadvertent - unintentional; not done on purpose
  • Synonym: attentive - paying close attention; alert and observant

Etymology

From Latin 'advertere' (to turn toward), root of 'adverse', 'advert', and 'inadvertent'.
