Evelyn Waugh

1. PROPN

/ ˈivlɪn wɔ / (AmE), / ˈiːvlɪn wɔː / (BrE)
(1903–1966) English author known for satirical novels, including 'Brideshead Revisited' and 'Scoop'.
English satirical novelist (1903–1966)

Examples

Evelyn Waugh is renowned for his sharp wit and satirical portrayal of British society.
Many consider Evelyn Waugh's 'Decline and Fall' a classic of 20th-century English literature.
The works of Evelyn Waugh continue to influence modern novelists around the world.
