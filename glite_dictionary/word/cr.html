Cr

1. NOUN

/ si ɑr / (AmE), / si: ɑː / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for chromium, a hard, brittle metallic element that resists corrosion and tarnishing
chemical symbol for chromium

Examples

Stainless steel contains a significant amount of Cr to prevent rusting.
Lab results showed elevated Cr levels in the sample.
Adding Cr improves the hardness of the alloy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chromium - a hard, metallic element with symbol Cr

Etymology

Abbreviation of Latin 'chromium', from Greek 'chroma' (color), referring to its colorful compounds.
