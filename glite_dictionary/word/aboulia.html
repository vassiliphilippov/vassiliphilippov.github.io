aboulia

1. NOUN

/ əˈbuːliə / (AmE), / əˈbuːlɪə / (BrE)
A medical condition characterized by a loss of willpower or the ability to make decisions.
loss of willpower; decision-making impairment
medical

Examples

Patients with aboulia may struggle to initiate even simple daily activities.
The psychiatrist recognized signs of aboulia in the patient's marked indecisiveness.
After the stroke, she developed aboulia and found it hard to make any choices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apathy - lack of emotion or interest

Etymology

From Greek a- (without) + boulē (will, determination).
