LAN

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛl eɪ ˈɛn / (AmE), / ˌɛl eɪ ˈɛn / (BrE)
a local area network; a system of computers and other devices connected within a limited area, such as an office or building, to share resources and information
local area computer network

Examples

All the computers in our office are connected to a LAN for easy file sharing.
The IT department upgraded the LAN to improve network speed and reliability.
Printing documents is quicker now that every workstation uses the same LAN.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: WAN - a wide area network; a computer network that covers a broad area
  • Synonym: network - an interconnected group or system, especially of computers

Etymology

Acronym of 'local area network'
