rectangularity

1. NOUN

/ rɛkˌtæŋɡjəˈlærəti / (AmE), / rɛkˌtæŋɡjʊˈlærɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being shaped like a rectangle
the quality of being rectangular

Examples

The rectangularity of the design gives the building a modern appearance.
She admired the precise rectangularity of the neatly arranged tiles.
The artist broke the strict rectangularity of the frame with sweeping curves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rectangular - shaped like a rectangle

Etymology

From rectangular + -ity, patterned after Latin formations for abstract nouns.
