plink

1. NOUN

/ plɪŋk / (AmE), / plɪŋk / (BrE)
a short, high-pitched, metallic sound, typically produced by a small object striking a hard surface
short, high-pitched metallic sound

Examples

The marble hit the glass bowl with a gentle plink.
A single raindrop landed on the tin roof with a sharp plink.
Every time he plucks the string, it produces a distinct plink.

Synset Examples

She dropped the coin, and it made a plink sound.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ping - a short, sharp, high-pitched sound
  • Synonym: clink - a light, sharp, ringing sound, like glass striking glass
