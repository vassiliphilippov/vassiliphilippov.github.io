nonmoving

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˈmuːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈmuːvɪŋ / (BrE)
not moving; not in motion or not changing position.
not moving; stationary

Examples

The car remained nonmoving at the traffic light for several minutes.
All nonmoving parts of the machine should be inspected for dust buildup.
The security camera detected only nonmoving objects in the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stationary - not moving or not intended to be moved
  • Antonym: moving - in motion; changing position

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'non-' (not) and 'moving'.
