unqualifiedly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈkwɑlɪfaɪdli / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwɒlɪfaɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that is complete and without any reservations or limitations.
completely; without limitation

Examples

She unqualifiedly supports the new policy, believing it is the best approach.
The board unqualifiedly approved the proposal after a brief discussion.
He unqualifiedly denies all the accusations made against him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: completely - totally; with nothing left out
  • Antonym: partially - in part; not totally or completely

Etymology

Formed from 'unqualified' + adverbial '-ly'.
