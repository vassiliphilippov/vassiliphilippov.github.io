odalisque

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊdəlɪsk / (AmE), / ˈəʊdəlɪsk / (BrE)
(historical) A female slave or concubine, especially in a Turkish harem.
female slave in a Turkish harem

Examples

The artist painted the reclining odalisque in vibrant colors.
In Ottoman palaces, an odalisque often served the sultan’s wives.
The novel tells the story of an odalisque seeking freedom from the harem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concubine - a woman who lives with a man but has lower status than his wife or wives

Etymology

From French *odalisque*, from Turkish *odalık* (chambermaid), from *oda* (room).
