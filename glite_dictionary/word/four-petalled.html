four-petalled

1. ADJ

/ fɔrˈpɛtəld / (AmE), / fɔːˈpɛtəld / (BrE)
(of a flower) having four petals.
having four petals (of flowers)

Examples

The four-petalled flowers stood out among the others on the forest floor.
Botanists are particularly interested in plants with four-petalled blossoms for classification.
Each four-petalled bloom added a unique symmetry to the garden arrangement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: four-lobed - having four lobes (botanical term)
