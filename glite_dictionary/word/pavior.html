pavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪviər / (AmE), / ˈpeɪviə / (BrE)
a machine used for laying pavement or paving roads
machine for laying pavement

Examples

The construction crew used a pavior to efficiently lay the new road surface.
Modern paviors are designed to handle various types of pavement materials.
After the old asphalt was removed, the pavior smoothed a fresh layer in just a few hours.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paver - machine for laying pavement or one who paves

Etymology

From the noun 'pavior' meaning a person who lays pavements, ultimately from 'pave'.
