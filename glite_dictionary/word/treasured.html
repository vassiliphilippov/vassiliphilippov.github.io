treasured

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrɛʒərd / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒəd / (BrE)
regarded with deep affection and valued highly; cherished
greatly valued or cherished

Examples

The ring was a treasured gift from her grandmother.
She keeps all her treasured memories in a journal.
That old photograph is one of his most treasured possessions.

Synset Examples

a cherished friend
children are precious
a treasured heirloom
so good to feel wanted

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cherished - regarded with affection; cared for deeply
  • Synonym: prized - valued highly; esteemed
  • Antonym: neglected - not given proper care or attention

Etymology

From the verb 'treasure', ultimately from Old French *tresor*, from Latin *thesaurus* (treasure, storehouse).
