authorized

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔːθəraɪzd / (AmE), / ˈɔːθəraɪzd / (BrE)
Having official permission or approval to do something; sanctioned by authority.
having official permission or approval

Examples

Only authorized personnel are allowed beyond this point.
This is the authorized version of the software, fully licensed and supported.
He wrote the authorized biography of the famous scientist.

Synset Examples

an authoritative communique
the authorized biography

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unauthorized - not having official permission or approval
  • Synonym: approved - officially agreed or accepted

Etymology

Formed from 'authorize' (from Old French autoriser, based on Latin auctoritas 'authority') + -ed.
