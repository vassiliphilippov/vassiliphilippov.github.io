Monday

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌn.deɪ / (AmE), / ˈmʌn.deɪ / (BrE)
The day of the week following Sunday and preceding Tuesday, typically considered the first working day.
second day of the week

Examples

She always feels tired on Monday after a relaxing weekend.
The meeting has been scheduled for next Monday at 10 a.m.
Many people dread going back to work on Monday mornings.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Sunday - the day before Monday and the first day of the week in some calendars
  • Degree Higher: Tuesday - the day after Monday, the third day of the week

Etymology

From Old English 'Monandæg', meaning 'day of the Moon'.
