afresh

1. ADV

/ əˈfrɛʃ / (AmE), / əˈfrɛʃ / (BrE)
again, but in a new or different way; from a new beginning.
in a new or different way; anew

Examples

After losing his job, he decided to start afresh in a new city.
She wiped the board clean and began the exercise afresh.
Sometimes, it helps to look at a problem afresh to find a solution.

Synset Examples

start afresh
wanted to write the story anew
starting life anew in a fresh place

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anew - again, but in a new or different way

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'a-' (meaning 'in' or 'on') and 'fresh', meaning new.
