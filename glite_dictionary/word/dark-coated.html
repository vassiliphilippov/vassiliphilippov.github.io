dark-coated

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɑrkˌkoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɑːkˌkəʊtɪd / (BrE)
(of an animal) having a coat of fur or hair that is dark in color.
covered with dark hair or fur

Examples

The dark-coated dog blended into the shadows at dusk.
Several dark-coated horses galloped across the field.
She adopted a dark-coated cat from the animal shelter.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: light-coated - having a coat of fur or hair that is light in color
