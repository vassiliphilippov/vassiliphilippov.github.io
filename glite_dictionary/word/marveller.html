marveller

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrvələr / (AmE), / ˈmɑːvələ / (BrE)
a person who feels or expresses admiration and wonder about something
person who marvels or is filled with awe

Examples

The tourists stood as marvellers before the ancient ruins, unable to conceal their awe.
As a lifelong marveller of nature, Jane spent hours walking in the forest.
He was a quiet marveller, often found gazing up at the night sky in silent wonder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admirer - a person who admires or is attracted to something or someone

Etymology

From marvel + -er. 'Marvel' ultimately from Old French 'merveille'.
