Brummie

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrʌmi / (AmE), / ˈbrʌmi / (BrE)
a native or resident of Birmingham, England
native or resident of Birmingham, England
british

Examples

My grandmother is a proud Brummie who has lived in Birmingham her entire life.
Although he moved to London, he still considers himself a true Brummie.
Many Brummies are known for their distinctive regional accent.

Etymology

From Brummagem, a colloquial name for Birmingham.
