Luluabourg

1. PROPN

/ luːˈluː.ə.bɔːrɡ / (AmE), / luːˈluː.ə.bɔːɡ / (BrE)
the former name (used until 1966) of the city now known as Kananga, in southwestern Democratic Republic of the Congo.
former name of Kananga, Congo

Examples

The city once known as Luluabourg was renamed Kananga in 1966.
During the colonial era, Luluabourg was an important administrative center.
Many historical documents refer to Kananga by its old name, Luluabourg.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kananga - modern name of the city formerly known as Luluabourg
