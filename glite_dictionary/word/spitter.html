spitter

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪtər / (AmE), / ˈspɪtə / (BrE)
a person who spits saliva or phlegm from their mouth
person who spits saliva or phlegm

Examples

The baseball coach warned that any spitter would be penalized for unsportsmanlike conduct.
During the parade, officials asked the crowd not to be a spitter for hygiene reasons.
He gained a reputation as a spitter who had little regard for public spaces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spit - to eject saliva or other substances from the mouth

2. NOUN

/ ˈspɪtər / (AmE), / ˈspɪtə / (BrE)
(Baseball) An illegal pitch in which the pitcher applies a foreign substance such as saliva or Vaseline to the ball to alter its movement.
illegal baseball pitch with a doctored ball

Examples

The umpire suspected the pitcher of throwing a spitter during the crucial inning.
Using a spitter is against the rules and can result in suspension.
Legend has it that some old-time pitchers relied on a spitter to win games.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fastball - a pitch thrown with maximum velocity in baseball
  • Synonym: curveball - a pitch that curves as it approaches the batter

Etymology

From 'spit' + '-er', referring to spitting on the ball.
