risen

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪzən / (AmE), / ˈrɪzən / (BrE)
(of celestial bodies) positioned or appearing above the horizon.
above the horizon (of celestial bodies)

Examples

We watched as the risen sun cast golden light over the mountains.
The risen moon illuminated the quiet fields at night.
With the risen star, sailors could finally determine their bearings.

Synset Examples

the risen sun

Related Terms

  • Antonym: set - (of celestial bodies) having dipped below the horizon

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'rise', from Old English 'rīsan'.
