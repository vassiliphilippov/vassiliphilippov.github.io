Kotar

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊtɑːr / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtɑː / (BrE)
A Dravidian language spoken by the Kota people of southern India.
a Dravidian language of the Kota people

Examples

Few people outside the Nilgiri hills are familiar with the Kotar language.
Linguists study Kotar to understand its unique features among Dravidian languages.
Only a small community of Kota people still speaks Kotar today.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊ.tɑr / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.tɑː / (BrE)
A member of a Dravidian people living in the Nilgiri Hills of southern India.
member of a Dravidian people of southern India

Examples

The Kotar have a rich tradition of craftsmanship and music.
Anthropologists have studied the social structure of the Kotar community in the Nilgiri Hills.
Some Kotar still maintain traditional rituals and ceremonies unique to their culture.
