BR

1. NOUN

/ bi ɑr / (AmE), / biː ɑː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for the Red Brigades, a Marxist-Leninist militant organization active in Italy in the late 20th century.
Marxist-Leninist militant group (Italy)

Examples

The Italian government intensified its crackdown on the BR after a series of high-profile kidnappings.
During the 1970s, the BR was involved in several violent attacks against state officials.
Despite being mostly inactive now, the legacy of the BR still influences discussions on terrorism in Italy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Red Brigades - Italian Marxist-Leninist militant organization active from the 1970s-1980s

Etymology

Abbreviation of Italian 'Brigate Rosse' (Red Brigades).
