evenly

1. ADV

/ ˈiːvənli / (AmE), / ˈiːvənli / (BrE)
in equal amounts or shares; in a balanced or impartial way
in equal or balanced way

Examples

The cake should be cut evenly so everyone gets the same amount.
She distributed the workload evenly among her team members.
The paint dried evenly across the entire surface.

Synset Examples

a class evenly divided between girls and boys
they split their winnings equally
deal equally with rich and poor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equally - in equal amounts or degree
  • Antonym: unevenly - in an unequal or unbalanced way

Etymology

From 'even' + '-ly', Old English 'efen' (level, equal).

2. ADV

/ ˈiːvənli / (AmE), / ˈiːvənli / (BrE)
In a level, equal, or regular way, without variations or irregularities.
in a level or regular way

Examples

Spread the butter evenly over the toast for best flavor.
The players were evenly matched, making the game exciting until the last minute.
Make sure to mix the ingredients evenly so the cake bakes properly.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unevenly - in a way that is not level, equal, or regular

Etymology

Formed from 'even' + '-ly'; 'even' from Old English 'efen' (level, flat).
