Sciadopitys

1. NOUN

/ saɪˌædəˈpaɪtɪs / (AmE), / saɪˌadəˈpɪtɪs / (BrE)
A genus of evergreen coniferous trees known as Japanese umbrella pines, native to Japan and the sole genus of the family Sciadopityaceae.
genus of Japanese umbrella pines

Examples

The Sciadopitys is often cultivated as an ornamental tree in gardens outside its native Japan.
Botanists studied the unique leaf arrangement of Sciadopitys for clues about conifer evolution.
Unlike most conifers, Sciadopitys has long, flattened needles arranged in umbrella-like whorls.

Etymology

From Greek 'skiados' (umbrella) and 'pitys' (pine tree), referring to the tree's umbrella-shaped whorls of leaves.
