ten thousand

1. NUM

/ tɛn ˈθaʊzənd / (AmE), / tɛn ˈθaʊzənd / (BrE)
the number 10,000; the cardinal number that is the product of ten and one thousand
the number 10,000

Examples

There were ten thousand people at the concert last night.
The prize was increased to ten thousand dollars this year.
During migration, flocks as large as ten thousand birds have been observed.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thousand - the number 1,000
  • Degree Higher: hundred thousand - the number 100,000
