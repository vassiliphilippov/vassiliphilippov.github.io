damply

1. ADV

/ ˈdæmpli / (AmE), / ˈdæmpli / (BrE)
In a manner that is slightly wet or moist.
in a damp or moist way

Examples

The leaves stuck damply to his shoes after the rainstorm.
Her hair clung damply to her forehead as she jogged.
The towel felt damply cool against his sunburned skin.

Synset Examples

a scarf was tied round her head but the rebellious curl had escaped and hung damply over her left eye

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moistly - in a moist manner
