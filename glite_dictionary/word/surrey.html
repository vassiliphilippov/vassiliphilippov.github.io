Surrey

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɜri / (AmE), / ˈsʌri / (BrE)
a county in southeastern England, located south of the River Thames, known for its historic towns and countryside.
county in southeastern England

Examples

She grew up in Surrey before moving to London for university.
The gardens of Surrey are famous for their beauty in spring.
Many commuters live in Surrey and travel to London for work.

Etymology

From Old English *Sūþrige* (‘southern region’).
