Mali

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑːli / (AmE), / ˈmɑːli / (BrE)
a landlocked country in West Africa, formerly a French colony, known for its rich history and cultural heritage
country in West Africa

Examples

Mali declared its independence from France in 1960.
Timbuktu, a historic city in Mali, was once a major center of learning and trade.
The Niger River is a vital resource for agriculture in Mali.

Etymology

From the name of the Mali Empire, a powerful West African state from the 13th to 16th centuries.
