taboret

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæbəˌrɛt / (AmE), / ˈtæbərɪt / (BrE)
a low stool, typically shaped like a drum and often used as a small seat or stand
a low drum-shaped stool

Examples

She placed the vase on the antique taboret in the corner of the room.
The museum displayed a beautifully painted taboret from the 18th century.
He used a small taboret as an extra seat during the crowded party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stool - a seat without a back or arms, typically for one person

Etymology

From French tabouret, diminutive of 'tabour' (drum).
