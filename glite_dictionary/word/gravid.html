gravid

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡrævɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡrævɪd / (BrE)
(of a female or her abdomen) carrying eggs or young; pregnant, especially at an advanced stage.
pregnant; carrying eggs or young

Examples

The veterinarian confirmed that the snake was gravid with eggs.
In medical terms, a gravid uterus refers to one that is carrying a fetus.
She was described in the old records as 'big and gravid with child.'

Synset Examples

was big with child
was great with child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pregnant - having a child or young developing in the uterus
  • Synonym: parturient - about to give birth, especially in animals

Etymology

From Latin gravidus (pregnant, heavy).
