BM

1. NOUN

/ biː ˈɛm / (AmE), / biː ˈɛm / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'bowel movement'; the solid waste matter expelled from the bowels
abbreviation for bowel movement (excrement)
medical informal

Examples

The nurse asked if he had a BM today.
A regular BM is a sign of good digestive health.
He felt uncomfortable because he hadn't had a BM in two days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowel movement - the act of passing solid waste from the bowels
  • Synonym: feces - solid bodily waste discharged from the intestines

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'bowel movement'.
