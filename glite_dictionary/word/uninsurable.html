uninsurable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪnˈʃʊr.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈʃɔː.rə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be insured or ineligible for insurance coverage
not eligible for insurance

Examples

Because of its faulty wiring, the building was deemed uninsurable by every major company.
Flood-prone homes are often considered uninsurable due to the high risk involved.
His medical condition made him uninsurable under most health insurance policies.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: insurable - able to be insured or eligible for insurance
  • Synonym: uninsured - not covered by insurance

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' and 'insurable' (able to be insured).
