titillated

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɪtəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɪtɪleɪtɪd / (BrE)
Experiencing mild pleasurable excitement or stimulation, often in a slightly provocative or teasing way.
feeling mild excitement or stimulation

Examples

She felt titillated by the whispered secrets shared during the party.
The comedian's jokes left the audience titillated and wanting more.
Even a brief glance at the gossip column was enough to make her feel titillated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excited - feeling or showing excitement; thrilled
  • Antonym: bored - feeling weary and uninterested

Etymology

From Latin titillatus, past participle of titillare (to tickle).
