forthcomingness

1. NOUN

/ fɔrθˈkʌmɪŋnəs / (AmE), / fɔːθˈkʌmɪŋnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being imminent or likely to occur soon
state of being imminent

Examples

The forthcomingness of the storm made the residents prepare for evacuation.
We discussed the forthcomingness of new regulations in the upcoming months.
The tension in the room reflected the forthcomingness of an important announcement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imminence - the state of being about to happen
  • Antonym: remoteness - the quality of being distant in time or unlikely to happen soon

Etymology

From 'forthcoming' + '-ness', with 'forthcoming' likely from Middle English 'forthcomen' (to come forth).
