schoolfellow

1. NOUN

/ ˈskuːlˌfɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈskuːlˌfɛləʊ / (BrE)
a person who attends the same school as someone else; a schoolmate
person you attend school with

Examples

He was reunited with his old schoolfellow at the college graduation.
Many of his closest friends today were once his schoolfellows in primary school.
She met her future business partner, a former schoolfellow, at a networking event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schoolmate - a person who attends the same school as another
  • Synonym: classmate - a fellow member of a class at school, college, or university

Etymology

Formed from 'school' and 'fellow' in English, 16th century onward.
