Kolkata

1. PROPN

/ koʊlˈkɑːtə / (AmE), / kɒlˈkɑːtə / (BrE)
A major city in eastern India, formerly known as Calcutta, and the capital of the state of West Bengal.
large city in eastern India

Examples

I plan to visit Kolkata next year to experience its rich culture and history.
She grew up in Kolkata before moving abroad for her studies.
Kolkata is famous for its vibrant festivals and colonial architecture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Calcutta - former English name for Kolkata

Etymology

From Bengali কলকাতা (Kôlkata), name of the city, formerly anglicized as Calcutta.
