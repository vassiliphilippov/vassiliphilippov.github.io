Nablus

1. PROPN

/ ˈnæbləs / (AmE), / ˈnæbləs / (BrE)
An ancient city in the northern West Bank, north of Jerusalem, with historical significance dating back to biblical times.
ancient city in the West Bank

Examples

The historic old city of Nablus is famous for its bustling markets and ancient architecture.
Pilgrims visited Nablus as it is associated with biblical figures.
Nablus has long been known as a center of culture and learning in the region.
