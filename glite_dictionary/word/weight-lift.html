to weight-lift

1. VERB

/ ˈweɪtˌlɪft / (AmE), / ˈweɪtˌlɪft / (BrE)
to lift heavy weights as a form of exercise or sport
lift weights for exercise or sport

Examples

She likes to weight-lift at the gym every morning before work.
After his injury, he wasn't able to weight-lift for several months.
Many athletes weight-lift to increase their strength and endurance.

Synset Examples

This guy can press 300 pounds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weightlifting - the sport or activity of lifting weights
