Adolf Loos

1. PROPN

/ ˈædɑlf loʊs / (AmE), / ˈadɒlf ləʊs / (BrE)
(1870–1933) An Austrian architect known for his influential modernist designs and strong criticism of ornamentation in architecture.
Austrian modernist architect (1870–1933)

Examples

Adolf Loos was famous for rejecting excessive decoration in his architectural works.
Many of the essays by Adolf Loos continue to influence design theory today.
The radical ideas of Adolf Loos marked a major shift in twentieth-century architecture.
