conversation

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːn.vərˈseɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.vəˈseɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a talk, especially an informal one, between two or more people, in which news and ideas are exchanged
informal talk between people

Examples

We had a long conversation about our plans for the future.
The conversation soon turned to their upcoming holiday.
She joined the group and listened quietly to their conversation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dialogue - a conversation between two or more people as a feature of a book, play, or movie
  • Synonym: discussion - the action or process of talking about something to reach a decision or exchange ideas
  • Antonym: monologue - a long speech by one person during a conversation

Etymology

From Latin 'conversatio', from 'conversari' (to associate with), from 'con-' (with) + 'versari' (to turn, move about).
