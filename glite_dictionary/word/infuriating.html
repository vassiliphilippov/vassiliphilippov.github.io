infuriating

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈfjʊriˌeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfjʊəriːeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely annoying or causing intense anger.
causing intense annoyance or anger

Examples

Waiting in that endless line was absolutely infuriating.
Her infuriating habit of interrupting made meetings difficult.
It is infuriating to lose your keys right before leaving the house.

Synset Examples

his cavalier curtness of manner was exasperating
I've had an exasperating day
her infuriating indifference
the ceaseless tumult of the jukebox was maddening

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exasperating - extremely annoying or irritating
  • Synonym: maddening - extremely annoying or upsetting
  • Antonym: soothing - having a calming or comforting effect

Etymology

From 'infuriate' + '-ing'; 'infuriate' from Italian infuriare, from Latin furia ('fury').
