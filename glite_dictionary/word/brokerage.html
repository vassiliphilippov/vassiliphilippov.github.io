brokerage

1. NOUN

/ ˈbroʊkərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkərɪdʒ / (BrE)
the business or service of acting as a broker, especially in arranging transactions between buyers and sellers, typically for a fee
the business or services of a broker

Examples

The brokerage earned a substantial fee for facilitating the merger.
He works for a major brokerage specializing in stock investments.
Online brokerage services have made investing more accessible to individuals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commission - a fee paid for services, especially to a broker or agent
  • Synonym: agency - the business or function of acting on another's behalf, often for a fee

Etymology

From Anglo-Norman *brokage*, from *broker* (one who negotiates), with Old French roots.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbroʊkərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkərɪdʒ / (BrE)
A place where a broker conducts their business.
place where a broker works

Examples

She visited the brokerage downtown to discuss her investment options.
After graduating, he found a job at a large real estate brokerage.
The new brokerage offers both stock and insurance services.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broker's office - an office where a broker works

Etymology

From broker + -age, after French 'courtage' (from 'courtier' meaning 'broker').

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02908613-n
Synset POS: n

3. NOUN

/ ˈbroʊ.kɚ.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊ.kər.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
A company or business that acts as an intermediary between buyers and sellers, especially in stock or commodity markets, usually charging a fee or commission for its services.
business acting as financial intermediary

Examples

She opened an account with a reputable brokerage to start investing in stocks.
The brokerage earns a commission for every transaction made on its platform.
Many investors compare brokerage fees before choosing where to trade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broker - a person or firm that arranges transactions between a buyer and a seller

Etymology

From broker + -age, denoting an activity or business related to brokering.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08086714-n
Synset POS: n
