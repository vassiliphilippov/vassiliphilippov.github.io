armpit

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrmˌpɪt / (AmE), / ˈɑːmpɪt / (BrE)
the hollow area under the arm at the shoulder
hollow area under the arm

Examples

She applied deodorant to her armpit before leaving the house.
A rash had developed in his left armpit after the hike.
The doctor examined the swollen lymph nodes in her armpit.

Synset Examples

they were up to their armpits in water

Related Terms

  • Synonym: axilla - the anatomical term for the area under the arm at the shoulder

Etymology

Formed from 'arm' + 'pit', referring to the recess or hollow at the shoulder.
