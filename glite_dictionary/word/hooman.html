hooman

1. NOUN

/ ˈhuːmən / (AmE), / ˈhuːmən / (BrE)
A humorous or playful misspelling of 'human,' often used to represent the way animals might refer to people in internet memes and social media.
comic misspelling of 'human'
informal slang

Examples

My cat stares at me as if to say, "Silly hooman, feed me now!"
On the internet, dogs often call their owners 'hooman' in funny captions.
This meme shows a dog judging its hooman for working late again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: human - a person, a member of the species Homo sapiens

Etymology

Playful alteration of 'human,' popularized by internet memes.
