happy-go-lucky

1. ADJ

/ ˌhæpi ɡoʊ ˈlʌki / (AmE), / ˌhæpi ɡəʊ ˈlʌki / (BrE)
cheerfully unconcerned about the future or possible consequences; carefree and irresponsible.
cheerfully carefree; unconcerned

Examples

Despite his happy-go-lucky attitude, he managed to get all his work done on time.
She has always been happy-go-lucky, never worrying too much about life's problems.
His happy-go-lucky behavior sometimes gets him into trouble with his boss.

Synset Examples

carefree with his money
freewheeling urban youths
had a harum-scarum youth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carefree - free from anxiety or responsibility; unconcerned
  • Synonym: irresponsible - not showing a proper sense of responsibility

Etymology

Compound of 'happy' + 'go' + 'lucky', originally (mid-19th century) meaning 'as luck will have it'.
