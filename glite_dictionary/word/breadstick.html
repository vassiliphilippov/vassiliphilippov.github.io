breadstick

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛdˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈbrɛd.stɪk / (BrE)
a long, thin, crisp stick of bread, often served as an accompaniment to soup or salads
long, crisp stick of bread

Examples

The waiter brought a basket of breadsticks to the table before our meal.
I love dipping breadsticks into tomato soup on a cold day.
She baked homemade breadsticks seasoned with garlic and herbs.
