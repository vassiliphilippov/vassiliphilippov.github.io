Estonia

1. PROPN

/ ɛˈstoʊniə / (AmE), / ɛˈstəʊniə / (BrE)
A country in northeastern Europe on the Baltic Sea, with Tallinn as its capital.
country in northeastern Europe

Examples

She traveled through Estonia last summer and loved the medieval architecture in Tallinn.
Estonia became an independent nation after the collapse of the Soviet Union.
The winters in Estonia are known for being particularly cold and snowy.

Etymology

From Estonian Eesti, the native name for the country.
