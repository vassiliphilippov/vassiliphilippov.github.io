sweetly

1. ADV

/ ˈswitli / (AmE), / ˈswiːtli / (BrE)
In an affectionate, gentle, or pleasing manner.
in an affectionate or pleasant manner

Examples

She smiled sweetly at the child, putting him at ease.
The songbird sang sweetly in the early morning.
He spoke sweetly to his grandmother, hoping to cheer her up.

Synset Examples

Susan Hayward plays the wife sharply and sweetly
how sweet the moonlight sleeps upon this bank
talking sweet to each other

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gently - in a mild and kind manner
  • Antonym: harshly - in a rough or unpleasant manner

Etymology

From Old English swēte (sweet) + -ly (adverbial suffix).
