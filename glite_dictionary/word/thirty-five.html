thirty-five

1. NUM

/ ˌθɜr.tiˈfaɪv / (AmE), / ˌθɜː.tiˈfaɪv / (BrE)
The number that is five more than thirty; 35.
the number 35

Examples

She turned thirty-five years old last week.
There are thirty-five chairs in the conference room.
The book costs thirty-five dollars at the bookstore.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirty-four - the number 34, one less than thirty-five
  • Degree Higher: thirty-six - the number 36, one more than thirty-five
