bookmark

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊkˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈbʊk.mɑːk / (BrE)
a piece of paper, ribbon, or similar material placed between the pages of a book to mark a reader's place
marker for keeping one's place in a book

Examples

She slipped a bookmark between the pages before putting the novel down for the night.
The library was giving away free bookmarks designed by local artists.
I couldn't find my bookmark, so I used a receipt instead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: placeholder - something used to keep a position or place for something else
