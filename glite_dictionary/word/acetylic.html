acetylic

1. ADJ

/ əˌsiːˈtɪlɪk / (AmE), / əˌsiːˈtɪlɪk / (BrE)
relating to or derived from acetic acid.
of or relating to acetic acid

Examples

The compound was identified as an acetylic derivative due to its chemical structure.
Researchers observed significant reactivity in acetylic compounds under alkaline conditions.
The formation of acetylic acid is an essential step in this industrial process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acetyl - the acyl group CH3CO- derived from acetic acid

Etymology

From 'acetyl', referring to the acyl group derived from acetic acid, plus the adjective suffix '-ic'.
