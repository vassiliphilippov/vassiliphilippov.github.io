asset

1. NOUN

/ ˈæsɛt / (AmE), / ˈæset / (BrE)
a useful or valuable quality, skill, or person that helps someone achieve success
useful or valuable quality or skill

Examples

Her communication skills are a real asset to the team.
Honesty is a valuable asset in any profession.
Fluency in several languages can be a significant asset when working abroad.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: liability - a disadvantage or drawback
  • Synonym: advantage - something that helps to make someone or something better or more likely to succeed

Etymology

From Old French ‘aset’ (enough, possession), from Latin ‘ad satis’ (to enough).
