Diu lei

1. INTJ

/ diu leɪ / (AmE), / diu leɪ / (BrE)
An exclamation showing strong frustration or annoyance, often used as a mild curse.
exclamation of frustration (Hong Kong English)
vulgar

Examples

He slammed the door and muttered Diu lei under his breath.
When the computer crashed again, she couldn't help but shout Diu lei in exasperation.
You could tell how angry he was just by the way he spat out Diu lei.

Synset Examples

'Diu lei' perfectly captured her frustration in that moment.

Etymology

From Cantonese 『屌你』 (*diu nei*), a vulgar phrase meaning 'f*** you.' Now sometimes used in English contexts in Hong Kong.
