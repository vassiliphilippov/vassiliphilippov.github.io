diu lei

1. INTJ

/ djuː leɪ / (AmE), / djuː leɪ / (BrE)
(Cantonese) An exclamation expressing surprise, excitement, or strong emotion, often used informally.
Cantonese exclamation of surprise or excitement
informal slang

Examples

He shouted diu lei when the ball hit the window unexpectedly.
She couldn't help but exclaim diu lei when she saw the results.
People often say diu lei in Cantonese when they're taken by surprise.

Synset Examples

He shouted 'diu lei' when he saw the surprise party.
Everyone laughed when he exclaimed 'diu lei' during the game.

Etymology

Cantonese phrase, literally 'fuck you', used as a strong exclamation.
