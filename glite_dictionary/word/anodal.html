anodal

1. ADJ

/ əˈnoʊdəl / (AmE), / əˈnəʊdəl / (BrE)
relating to, occurring at, or involving an anode.
relating to an anode

Examples

The anodal region of the cell attracted positive ions during the experiment.
Researchers observed increased activity near the anodal electrode.
An anodal current is essential for this type of electrochemical reaction.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cathodal - relating to or occurring at a cathode

Etymology

From 'anode' (Greek 'anodos', way up) + '-al' suffix forming adjectives.
