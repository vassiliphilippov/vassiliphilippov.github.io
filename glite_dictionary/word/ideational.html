ideational

1. ADJ

/ ˌaɪdiˈeɪʃənl / (AmE), / ˌaɪdiːˈeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
Relating to or concerned with ideas, concepts, or mental representations rather than physical objects.
related to ideas or concepts

Examples

His ideational abilities made him excel in philosophy and theory-based disciplines.
The study focused on the ideational aspects of language development in children.
Her ideational framework helped structure the discussion around abstract concepts.

Synset Examples

a plan abstract and conceptional
to improve notional comprehension
a notional response to the question

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conceptual - relating to or based on mental concepts
  • Synonym: notional - existing as or based on a notion or idea; theoretical
  • Antonym: practical - concerned with actual use or practice rather than ideas

Etymology

From idea + -tion + -al, formed on the basis of 'idea.'
