gymnastics

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪmˈnæstɪks / (AmE), / dʒɪmˈnæstɪks / (BrE)
A sport involving exercises that demonstrate strength, balance, agility, and coordination, typically performed on specialized equipment.
sport involving strength and agility displays

Examples

She began gymnastics when she was six and now competes at a national level.
Watching the Olympic gymnastics finals was truly inspiring for young athletes.
The school offers gymnastics classes for children of all skill levels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acrobatic - involving spectacular gymnastic feats
  • Synonym: athletics - various sports or physical exercises, esp. track and field

Etymology

From Greek 'gymnastikos' (fond of exercise), via French 'gymnastique' and Latin 'gymnastica'.
