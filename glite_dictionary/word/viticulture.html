viticulture

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪt̬.ɪˌkʌl.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɪt.ɪˌkʌl.tʃə / (BrE)
the science or practice of cultivating grapevines, especially for winemaking
the cultivation of grapevines

Examples

Many regions in France are famous for their viticulture and high-quality wines.
Advances in viticulture have improved the resistance of grapevines to disease.
He studied viticulture at university before starting his own vineyard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oenology - the science and study of wine and winemaking

Etymology

From Latin 'vitis' (vine) + 'culture' (cultivation).
