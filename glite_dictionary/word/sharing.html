sharing

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɛːrɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of using or enjoying something jointly with others
using or enjoying something with others

Examples

The sharing of resources helped the community thrive.
Good sharing habits are taught to children at an early age.
Teamwork relies on the sharing of ideas and responsibilities.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: division - the act of separating something into parts

2. NOUN

/ ˈʃɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɛːrɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of distributing or dividing something into shares for multiple people or groups
distribution into shares

Examples

The sharing of profits among company employees encouraged teamwork and motivation.
Proper sharing of resources is essential during an economic crisis.
The treaty called for the fair sharing of water from the river among neighboring countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: division - the act of separating something into parts or groups
  • Synonym: allocation - the process of giving or distributing something

Etymology

From the verb 'share' + -ing.
