ashram

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːʃ.rəm / (AmE), / ˈæʃ.rəm / (BrE)
a place for spiritual retreat or religious study, especially one modeled after those in India
religious retreat or spiritual community

Examples

After quitting her stressful job, she spent a month at an ashram in the Himalayas.
The ashram welcomes visitors of all faiths who seek meditation and spiritual guidance.
He wrote a memoir about his transformative experiences living in an ashram in southern India.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retreat - a quiet or secluded place for reflection or prayer
  • Synonym: monastery - a building or community where monks live and worship

Etymology

From Sanskrit āśrama (hermitage, religious retreat).

2. NOUN

/ ˈɑːʃ.rɑːm / (AmE), / ˈaʃ.ræm / (BrE)
(in India) a place of religious retreat or spiritual hermitage, especially for Hindus.
Hindu religious retreat or hermitage

Examples

After a stressful year, she decided to spend a month at an ashram in India to find peace.
Many Westerners travel to an ashram to practice yoga and meditation under expert guidance.
The ashram provided a serene environment for spiritual growth and reflection.

Etymology

From Sanskrit āśrama (hermitage, place of spiritual retreat).
