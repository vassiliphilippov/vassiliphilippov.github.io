Marcel Breuer

1. PROPN

/ mɑrˌsɛl ˈbrɔɪ.ər / (AmE), / mɑːˌsɛl ˈbrɔɪ.ə / (BrE)
(1902–1981), Hungarian-born American architect associated with the Bauhaus movement, known for his contributions to modernist design.
Hungarian-American Bauhaus architect

The furniture designs of Marcel Breuer are displayed in many leading museums.
