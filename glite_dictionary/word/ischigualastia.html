Ischigualastia

1. NOUN

/ ˌiʃiɡwaˈlastiə / (AmE), / ˌɪʃɪɡwaˈlæstɪə / (BrE)
an extinct genus of large herbivorous dicynodont reptiles from the Late Triassic period, known from fossils discovered in Argentina.
extinct dicynodont reptile genus from Argentina

Examples

The fossils of Ischigualastia provide important information about prehistoric ecosystems in South America.
Paleontologists discovered a nearly complete skeleton of Ischigualastia in the Ischigualasto Formation.
Unlike many dicynodonts, Ischigualastia reached a massive size and had a distinctive broad skull.

Etymology

From the Ischigualasto Formation in Argentina, where the fossils were discovered.
