ahorseback

1. ADV

/ əˈhɔrsˌbæk / (AmE), / əˈhɔːsˌbæk / (BrE)
on horseback; riding on the back of a horse.
on a horse; riding horseback
archaic

Examples

The soldiers rode ahorseback through the valley at dawn.
He preferred to travel ahorseback rather than by carriage.
Villagers watched as the messenger arrived ahorseback with urgent news.

Synset Examples

he rode horseback to town
managed to escape ahorse
policeman patrolled the streets ahorseback

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horseback - on the back of a horse

Etymology

From the prefix 'a-' (on, in, at) + 'horseback' (the back of a horse).
