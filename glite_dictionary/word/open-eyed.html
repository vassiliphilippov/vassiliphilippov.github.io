open-eyed

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊpən aɪd / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən aɪd / (BrE)
carefully observant or attentive; alert to possible danger or developments.
carefully observant; alert

Examples

She remained open-eyed during the meeting, ready to catch any sign of disagreement.
An open-eyed approach to travel helps you notice dangers and opportunities alike.
The new manager was open-eyed about the challenges facing the team.

Synset Examples

a policy of open-eyed awareness
the vigilant eye of the town watch
there was a watchful dignity in the room
a watchful parent with a toddler in tow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vigilant - being alert to possible danger or trouble
  • Synonym: watchful - paying close attention to possible danger or trouble
  • Antonym: unaware - not aware or observant
