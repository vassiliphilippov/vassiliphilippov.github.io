Sao Bernardo do Campo

1. PROPN

/ saʊ bɛrˈnɑrdoʊ doʊ ˈkæmpoʊ / (AmE), / saʊ bəːˈnɑːdəʊ dəʊ ˈkampəʊ / (BrE)
a city in southeastern Brazil, known as an industrial suburb of São Paulo
city in southeastern Brazil

Examples

Sao Bernardo do Campo is recognized as a major center for automobile manufacturing in Brazil.
Many commuters travel from Sao Bernardo do Campo to work in central São Paulo each day.
The population of Sao Bernardo do Campo has grown rapidly due to its expanding industries.

Etymology

From Portuguese, meaning 'Saint Bernard of the Field'.
