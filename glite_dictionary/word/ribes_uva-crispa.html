Ribes uva-crispa

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪbiːz ˌjuːvə ˈkrɪspə / (AmE), / ˈraɪbiːz ˌjuːvə ˈkrɪspə / (BrE)
A spiny Eurasian shrub with greenish purple-tinged flowers and ovoid yellow-green or red-purple berries, commonly known as the gooseberry.
species of gooseberry shrub

Examples

The Ribes uva-crispa in her garden produced a bumper crop of tart berries this year.
Researchers studied the disease resistance of Ribes uva-crispa in various climates.
The unique flavor of Ribes uva-crispa makes it popular for making jams and desserts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gooseberry - common name for Ribes uva-crispa; a tart edible berry

Etymology

From Latin: 'Ribes' (currant) + 'uva-crispa' (curly grape, i.e., gooseberry).
