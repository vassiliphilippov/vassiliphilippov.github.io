genu

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnjuː / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnjuː / (BrE)
Anatomical term for the knee joint, especially the hinge joint connecting the femur, tibia, and fibula, protected in front by the patella.
anatomical term for the knee joint
medical

Examples

The surgeon carefully examined the genu to assess the extent of the injury.
The patella serves as a shield for the anterior surface of the genu.
Abnormal swelling around the genu may indicate internal damage to the joint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knee - joint connecting the thigh and lower leg in humans

Etymology

From Latin genu (knee).
