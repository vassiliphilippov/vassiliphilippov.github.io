shoplifter

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑːpˌlɪftər / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpˌlɪftə / (BrE)
A person who steals goods from a shop while pretending to be a customer.
person who steals goods from a shop

Examples

The shoplifter tried to leave the store without paying for the items hidden in his bag.
Security cameras caught the shoplifter in the act of stealing cosmetics.
The store manager called the police after apprehending a suspected shoplifter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thief - a person who steals, especially secretly or without using force
  • Synonym: burglar - a person who illegally enters buildings and steals things
  • Synonym: shoplifting - the act of stealing goods from a shop

Etymology

From 'shoplift' (verb) + '-er'.
