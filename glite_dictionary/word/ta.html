Ta

1. NOUN

/ ti eɪ / (AmE), / tiː eɪ / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for tantalum, a hard, grey, lustrous metallic element highly resistant to corrosion
chemical symbol for tantalum (element)

Examples

The corrosion resistance of Ta makes it ideal for use in chemical processing equipment.
In the periodic table, Ta falls under the transition metals group.
Scientists use Ta electrodes in the manufacture of certain electronic components.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tantalum - a hard, grey, corrosion-resistant metallic element (chemical element, symbol Ta)

Etymology

From New Latin *tantalum*, named after Tantalus of Greek mythology.
