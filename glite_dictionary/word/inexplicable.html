inexplicable

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈɛksplɪkəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈɛksplɪkəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to explain or account for; mysterious.
impossible to explain

Examples

The scientist was puzzled by the inexplicable disappearance of the evidence.
For some inexplicable reason, she declined the prestigious award.
Sudden mood swings can be inexplicable and hard to manage.

Synset Examples

inexplicable errors
left the house at three in the morning for inexplicable reasons

Related Terms

  • Antonym: explainable - able to be explained or accounted for
  • Synonym: incomprehensible - impossible or very difficult to understand

Etymology

From Latin inexplicabilis, from in- (not) + explicabilis (explainable).

2. ADJ

impossible to explain or account for
impossible to explain

Examples

The sudden drop in temperature was completely inexplicable to the scientists.
She felt an inexplicable sadness as she walked through the old house.
His inexplicable absence at the meeting concerned his colleagues.

Synset Examples

inexplicable errors
left the house at three in the morning for inexplicable reasons

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unexplainable - impossible to explain
  • Synonym: mysterious - difficult or impossible to understand; puzzling
  • Antonym: understandable - able to be understood or explained

Etymology

From Latin inexplicabilis, from in- (not) + explicabilis (capable of being explained).
