Sorex

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔrɛks / (AmE), / ˈsɔːrɛks / (BrE)
A genus of small, insectivorous mammals known as shrews, belonging to the family Soricidae.
genus of shrews (mammals)

Examples

The common European shrew belongs to the genus Sorex.
Researchers observed several Sorex species in the dense undergrowth of the forest.
The taxonomy text explained the distinctive dental features of Sorex.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shrew - Small, mouse-like insectivorous mammal

Etymology

From Latin *sorex* (shrew).
