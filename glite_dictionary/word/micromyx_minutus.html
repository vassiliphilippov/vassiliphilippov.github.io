Micromyx minutus

1. NOUN

/ ˌmaɪkroʊˌmɪks mɪˈnuːtəs / (AmE), / ˌmɪkrəʊˌmɪks mɪˈnjuːtəs / (BrE)
a small reddish-brown Eurasian mouse typically found in habitats such as cornfields
species of small Eurasian mouse

Examples

The tiny Micromyx minutus can often be seen darting among the stalks in European cornfields.
Researchers studied the habitat preferences of Micromyx minutus throughout eastern Europe.
Unlike other mice, the Micromyx minutus builds intricate nests high in tall grasses.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin genus Micromyx and species epithet minutus, meaning 'small' in Latin.
