Pictor

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɪktər / (AmE), / ˈpɪktə / (BrE)
a constellation in the Southern Hemisphere, situated near Dorado and Columba
Southern Hemisphere constellation

Examples

The telescope was pointed toward Pictor to observe a newly discovered star.
Many faint galaxies can be found within the boundaries of Pictor.
Pictor is best seen in the evenings during the Southern Hemisphere's summer.

Etymology

From Latin 'pictor', meaning 'painter', as the constellation represents an easel.
