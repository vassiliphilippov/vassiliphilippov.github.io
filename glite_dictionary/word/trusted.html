trusted

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrʌstɪd / (AmE), / ˈtrʌstɪd / (BrE)
Regarded as reliable or deserving of trust or confidence.
regarded as reliable; deserving trust

Examples

She is the company's most trusted advisor.
We left our dog with a trusted friend while we were on vacation.
A trusted colleague will always support you at work.

Synset Examples

a sure (or trusted) friend

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reliable - can be depended on
  • Antonym: distrusted - regarded with suspicion or lack of trust

Etymology

From the verb 'trust' + -ed, forming the past participle/adjective.
