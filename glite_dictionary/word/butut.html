butut

1. NOUN

/ buːˈtʊt / (AmE), / buːˈtʊt / (BrE)
A monetary unit of The Gambia, equal to one hundredth of a dalasi.
Gambian monetary unit

Examples

The coin was worth only one butut, the smallest denomination in Gambian currency.
Prices have risen so much that a butut has almost no purchasing power left.
He exchanged all his bututs for dalasis at the local market.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: dalasi - basic monetary unit of The Gambia
