cosily

1. ADV

/ ˈkoʊzəli / (AmE), / ˈkəʊzɪli / (BrE)
In a comfortable, warm, and pleasant manner; in a way that suggests coziness or contentment.
in a comfortable, warm manner

Examples

She wrapped herself cosily in the thick blanket during the winter storm.
The two friends sat cosily near the fireplace, sipping hot chocolate and chatting.
The cat was sleeping cosily on the windowsill, purring softly in the afternoon sun.

Synset Examples

nestled cozily by the fire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comfortably - in a comfortable manner
  • Antonym: uncomfortably - in an uncomfortable manner

Etymology

From 'cosy' (British spelling of 'cozy') + '-ly' adverbial suffix.
