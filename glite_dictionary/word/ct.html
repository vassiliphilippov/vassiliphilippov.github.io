CT

1. NOUN

/ si ti / (AmE), / siː tiː / (BrE)
abbreviation for Connecticut, a state in the northeastern United States and one of the original thirteen colonies.
abbreviation for Connecticut (U.S. state)
american

Examples

The package was sent to Hartford, CT, last week.
She grew up in New Haven, CT, before moving to New York.
The conference will be held in Stamford, CT, this September.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Connecticut - a state in the northeastern United States

2. NOUN

/ si ti / (AmE), / siː tiː / (BrE)
a medical imaging method that uses X-rays and computer processing to create cross-sectional images of the body
computed tomography scan (medical imaging)
medical

Examples

The doctor ordered a CT to get more detailed images of her abdomen.
After the accident, a CT was performed to check for internal injuries.
A CT can help detect tumors that might not be visible on standard X-rays.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: MRI - magnetic resonance imaging, a different medical imaging technique

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'computed tomography'.
