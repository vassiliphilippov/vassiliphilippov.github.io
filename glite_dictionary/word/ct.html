Ct.

1. NOUN

/ si ti / (AmE), / siː tiː / (BrE)
abbreviation for Connecticut, a state in the northeastern United States that was one of the original 13 colonies.
abbreviation for Connecticut (US state)
american

Examples

The company has its headquarters in Hartford, Ct., where it was founded in 1820.
She drove from New York to Greenwich, Ct., for the meeting.
The university in New Haven, Ct., is internationally renowned.
