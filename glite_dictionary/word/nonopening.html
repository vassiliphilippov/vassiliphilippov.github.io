nonopening

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈoʊpənɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˈəʊpənɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an object or structure) not opening or not capable of being opened.
not opening or not capable of opening

Examples

The apartment has a nonopening window for safety reasons.
Due to fire regulations, certain stairwell doors must remain nonopening at all times.
A nonopening skylight lets in light but cannot be used for ventilation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fixed - (of a window, door, etc.) not able to be opened

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'non-' (not) to 'opening'.
