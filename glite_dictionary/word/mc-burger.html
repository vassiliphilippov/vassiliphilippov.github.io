Mc-burger

1. NOUN

/ mək ˈbɝːɡər / (AmE), / mək ˈbɜːɡə / (BrE)
a hamburger served at McDonald's fast food restaurants
a McDonald's hamburger
informal

Examples

After a long road trip, we stopped for a Mc-burger and fries at the nearest rest area.
He craved a classic Mc-burger more than anything after his workout.
The Mc-burger is known for its signature taste and simplicity.

Synset Examples

I grabbed a Mc-burger for lunch today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hamburger - a sandwich consisting of a cooked patty of ground meat in a bun

Etymology

Formed from 'Mc' (from McDonald's) and 'burger' (shortening of 'hamburger').
