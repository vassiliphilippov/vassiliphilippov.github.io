bliss

1. NOUN

/ blɪs / (AmE), / blɪs / (BrE)
A state of perfect happiness or joy.
perfect happiness; joy

Examples

She was in a state of bliss on her wedding day.
The holiday brought us a few weeks of pure bliss.
He lay on the beach, lost in bliss as the sun warmed his face.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecstasy - an overwhelming feeling of great happiness or joyful excitement
  • Antonym: misery - a state of great suffering and unhappiness

Etymology

From Old English *blīths* (joy, cheer, happiness).
