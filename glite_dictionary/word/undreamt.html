undreamt

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdɹɛmpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈdriːmt / (BrE)
not imagined or thought possible, even in one's wildest dreams
not imagined; beyond belief

Examples

The explorer spoke of undreamt wonders hidden deep within the jungle.
She achieved success undreamt of by her family and friends.
The technology advanced at a pace once undreamt by earlier generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unimaginable - impossible to imagine; inconceivable
  • Antonym: expected - regarded as likely to happen; anticipated

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'dreamt' (past participle of dream).
