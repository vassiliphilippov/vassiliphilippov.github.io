motoring

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊtərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈməʊtərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or practice of driving a motor vehicle, especially as a leisure activity
the act of driving a car
british

Examples

He has always loved motoring through the countryside on sunny weekends.
The magazine is full of articles about classic cars and the joys of motoring.
With the rise in fuel prices, the cost of motoring has increased significantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: driving - the act of operating and controlling a motor vehicle

Etymology

From 'motor' + '-ing'; 'motor' from Latin 'motor' (mover).
