ash-blonde

1. ADJ

/ æʃ ˈblɑnd / (AmE), / æʃ ˈblɒnd / (BrE)
(of hair) having a very light, pale blond color with a slightly grayish or silvery tint.
very light, grayish-blond hair color

Examples

She dyed her hair ash-blonde to achieve a cooler, more natural look.
His mother's ash-blonde curls sparkled in the sunlight.
Many celebrities have opted for an ash-blonde shade this season.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: blonde - hair color that is yellowish or pale gold
