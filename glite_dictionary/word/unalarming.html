unalarming

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈlɑːrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈlɑː.mɪŋ / (BrE)
not causing fear, worry, or alarm; reassuring or calming.
not causing alarm or worry

Examples

The test results were surprisingly unalarming, to everyone's great relief.
Her tone was brisk yet unalarming, putting the nervous child at ease.
Despite the loud noise, the doctor's explanation was clear and unalarming.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: alarming - causing fear, worry, or alarm
  • Synonym: reassuring - making someone feel less worried or afraid

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'alarming'.
