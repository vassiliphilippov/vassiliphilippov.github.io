staplegun

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪ.pəlˌɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈsteɪ.pəlˌɡʌn / (BrE)
A hand-held tool for driving staples into materials such as wood, plastic, or fabric.
hand tool for driving staples

Examples

The carpenter used a staplegun to attach the fabric to the frame quickly.
Make sure your staplegun is loaded before starting the project.
She bought a heavy-duty staplegun for her upholstery work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stapler - A device for fastening sheets of paper together with staples

Etymology

From 'staple' (the metal fastener) + 'gun', referring to its mechanism and appearance.
