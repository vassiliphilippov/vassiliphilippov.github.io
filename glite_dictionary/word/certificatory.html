certificatory

1. ADJ

/ sərˈtɪfɪkəˌtɔri / (AmE), / səˈtɪfɪkət(ə)ri / (BrE)
serving to certify or officially endorse something as valid or genuine
serving to certify officially

Examples

The board issued a certificatory letter to validate his qualifications.
A certificatory document is required to prove your eligibility for the program.
Their audit report serves a certificatory function within the organization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certifying - confirming or attesting as true or genuine

Etymology

From 'certificate' + '-ory', indicating relation or function.
