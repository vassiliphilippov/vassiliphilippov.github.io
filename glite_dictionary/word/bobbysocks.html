bobbysocks

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑbiˌsɑks / (AmE), / ˈbɒbiˌsɒks / (BrE)
A sock that reaches just above the ankle, typically worn by girls and young women.
ankle-high sock, usually for girls

Examples

She wore her favorite dress with a pair of crisp white bobbysocks and saddle shoes.
In the 1950s, bobbysocks became a symbol of youthful fashion in America.
My grandmother still has a picture of herself as a teenager in bobbysocks and a poodle skirt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ankle sock - a sock that covers the ankle but not higher

Etymology

Diminutive form of 'sock', popularized in mid-20th century America in reference to 'bobby soxers'.
