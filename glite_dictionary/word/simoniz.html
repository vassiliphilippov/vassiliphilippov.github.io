Simoniz

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪmənaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪmənaɪz / (BrE)
A brand of car polish used to clean, shine, and protect automotive paint.
a brand of car polish

Examples

After waxing his car with Simoniz, the paint looked as good as new.
He went to the store to buy some Simoniz for his detailing project.
Many professionals recommend using Simoniz for a lasting shine on your vehicle.

Etymology

From the name of the Simoniz Company, founded in the United States in 1910.
