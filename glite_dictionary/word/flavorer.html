flavorer

1. NOUN

/ ˈfleɪvərər / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvərə / (BrE)
a substance added to food or drink to give it a particular taste or enhance its flavor
substance added to give flavor

Examples

A pinch of cumin is a common flavorer in many curries.
Vanilla extract is a popular flavorer for cakes and desserts.
Some artificial flavorers are used to mimic the taste of real fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seasoning - a substance used to add flavor to food
  • Synonym: flavoring - something used to impart flavor to food or drink

Etymology

From 'flavor' + '-er', indicating an agent noun.
