V-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈviˌʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈviːˌʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape or form of the letter 'V'.
shaped like the letter V

Examples

They followed a flock of V-shaped geese flying south for the winter.
The mountain range forms a distinctive V-shaped valley.
He described her face as having a sharp, V-shaped chin.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: U-shaped - having the shape of the letter 'U'

Etymology

Formed from 'V', the letter, and 'shaped'.
