one-sixtieth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ˈsɪkstiəθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈsɪkstiɪθ / (BrE)
One part out of sixty equal parts; the fraction 1/60.
one part in sixty; fraction 1/60

Examples

The recipe calls for one-sixtieth of a teaspoon of saffron for subtle flavor.
Each degree is subdivided into one-sixtieth of a minute on the protractor.
The error margin was just one-sixtieth of the total measurement, so it was acceptable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sixtieth - Ordinal number for 60th; also a fraction (1/60)
