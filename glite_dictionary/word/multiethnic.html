multiethnic

1. ADJ

/ ˌmʌltiˈɛθnɪk / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈɛθnɪk / (BrE)
including or involving people from several different ethnic groups.
involving several ethnic groups

Examples

The city’s multiethnic neighborhoods are known for their diverse cultures and cuisines.
Our school celebrates its multiethnic student body with an annual cultural festival.
The multiethnic composition of the team brought unique perspectives to the project.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: monolithic - formed of a single, uniform whole, especially lacking diversity
  • Synonym: multiethnic - composed of or involving multiple ethnic groups

Etymology

From multi- (many) + ethnic.
