uptime

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌpˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌtaɪm / (BrE)
the period during which a machine, computer system, or service is functioning and available for use
period a system is functioning

Examples

The server maintained an impressive uptime of 99.9% throughout the year.
Regular maintenance is scheduled to maximize machine uptime and reduce unexpected failures.
High uptime is crucial for online businesses that rely on constant access to their services.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: downtime - period when a machine or service is not functioning
  • Synonym: availability - state of being usable or accessible

Etymology

Formed from 'up' + 'time', modeled after 'downtime' in mid-20th-century technical contexts.
