Pretoria

1. PROPN

/ prɪˈtɔriə / (AmE), / prɪˈtɔːriə / (BrE)
the administrative capital of South Africa, located in the province of Gauteng
capital city of South Africa

Examples

Pretoria serves as the seat of government for South Africa, housing many official buildings.
The University of Pretoria is one of the country's leading educational institutions.
They traveled from Cape Town to Pretoria by train.

Etymology

Named after Andries Pretorius, a leader of the Voortrekkers.
