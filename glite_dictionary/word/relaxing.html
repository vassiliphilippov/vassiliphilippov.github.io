relaxing

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈlæksɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈlæksɪŋ / (BrE)
Making you feel calm and comfortable, reducing stress or tension.
making you feel calm or at ease

Examples

The sound of the ocean waves was so relaxing that she fell asleep on the beach.
A cup of herbal tea can be relaxing after a long day at work.
They played soft music to create a more relaxing atmosphere in the spa.

Synset Examples

she spent a restful night at home

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stressful - causing stress or tension
  • Synonym: restful - peaceful and promoting rest; quiet

Etymology

From 'relax' + '-ing', originally from Latin 'relaxare' (to loosen).
