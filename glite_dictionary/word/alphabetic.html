alphabetic

1. ADJ

/ ˌælfəˈbɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌælfəˈbɛtɪk / (BrE)
arranged in the order of the letters of the alphabet; relating to the alphabet
arranged by the alphabet

Examples

The books are organized in an alphabetic sequence by author.
Please sort the files into alphabetic order before submitting them.
An alphabetic list makes it easier to locate a specific name.

Synset Examples

an alphabetic arrangement
dictionaries list words in alphabetical order

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alphabetical - relating to or arranged according to the alphabet

Etymology

From Greek 'alphabetikos', from 'alphabetos' (alphabet).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00101395-a
Synset POS: a

2. ADJ

/ ˌæl.fəˈbɛ.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌæl.fəˈbɛ.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to or expressed by an alphabet or writing system that uses an alphabet.
relating to alphabet or alphabet systems

Examples

The English language is written in an alphabetic script.
Sanskrit used an alphabetic system called Devanagari.
Researchers compared alphabetic and pictographic forms of writing.

Synset Examples

alphabetical writing system

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alphabetical - arranged in the order of the letters of the alphabet

Etymology

From Late Latin alphabeticus, from Greek alphabētikos.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02624611-a
Synset POS: a
