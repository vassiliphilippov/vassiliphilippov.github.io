Cebuan

1. NOUN

/ sɛˈbuːən / (AmE), / sɛˈbuːən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of the island of Cebu in the Philippines, or a member of the Visayan ethnic group originating from Cebu.
native or inhabitant of Cebu, Philippines

Examples

Many Cebuans celebrate Sinulog, a major cultural festival held in Cebu City each January.
Several Cebuans have achieved national prominence in the fields of politics and the arts.
The cuisine of Cebuans is known for its distinct use of spices and fresh seafood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cebuano - a person or language of Cebu, Philippines
  • Degree Higher: Filipino - a native or citizen of the Philippines

Etymology

From Cebu, the name of the island in the Philippines, with the English suffix -an denoting inhabitants.
