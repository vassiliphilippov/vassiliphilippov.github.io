boloney

1. NOUN

/ bəˈloʊni / (AmE), / bəˈləʊni / (BrE)
pretentious or foolish talk; nonsense
pretentious or silly talk; nonsense
informal

Examples

Everything he said about the project was just boloney.
Don't believe that story—it's pure boloney.
She dismissed the rumors as boloney.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - spoken or written words that have no meaning or make no sense
  • Synonym: drivel - silly or senseless talk
  • Antonym: sense - something that is reasonable or makes good logical meaning

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'baloney', likely influenced by the sausage 'bologna'.
