stairway

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɛrˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈsteəˌweɪ / (BrE)
a set of steps that allows people to move between different levels of a building or structure
a set of steps between levels

Examples

She cautiously walked up the stairway to the second floor.
The old mansion's stairway creaked with every step.
They admired the grand marble stairway in the hotel lobby.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: staircase - a set of stairs and its surrounding structure
  • Antonym: ladder - a structure for climbing, usually with rungs

Etymology

Formed from 'stair' + 'way', meaning a way or passage of stairs.
