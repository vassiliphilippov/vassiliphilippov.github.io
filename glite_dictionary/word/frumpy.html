frumpy

1. ADJ

/ ˈfrʌm.pi / (AmE), / ˈfrʌm.pi / (BrE)
(of a person or their clothes) old-fashioned, unattractive, and lacking in style or neatness; dowdy.
dowdy or old-fashioned, lacking style

Examples

She felt frumpy in her old sweater and skirt at the glamorous party.
The frumpy dress she wore was terribly out of fashion.
Despite her intelligence, some colleagues judged her by her frumpy appearance.

Synset Examples

nothing so frumpish as last year's gambling game

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dowdy - unfashionable and without style
  • Antonym: stylish - having elegance or fashion

Etymology

Probably from "frump", meaning a dowdy or drab person, origin uncertain.
