gup-shup

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌp ʃʌp / (AmE), / ɡʌp ʃʌp / (BrE)
an informal conversation or a casual chat, often among friends or acquaintances
casual or informal conversation
informal

Examples

Every evening, the neighbors gather for some gup-shup on the porch.
A bit of gup-shup during lunch breaks helps everyone relax at work.
She enjoys the friendly gup-shup whenever old friends get together.

Synset Examples

We had a lovely gup-shup over coffee yesterday.
The office gup-shup was filled with laughter and stories.
Their evening gup-shup turned into a late-night debate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chit-chat - light informal conversation about trivial matters
  • Synonym: conversation - a talk, especially an informal one, between two or more people

Etymology

From Hindi-Urdu 'gup-shup' (गप-शप / گپ شپ), meaning chit-chat.
