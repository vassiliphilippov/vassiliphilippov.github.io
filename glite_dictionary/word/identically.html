identically

1. ADV

/ aɪˈdɛntɪkli / (AmE), / aɪˈdɛntɪkəli / (BrE)
In exactly the same way; with complete sameness.
in exactly the same way

Examples

The twins were dressed identically for the birthday party.
The machines operate identically despite being made by different manufacturers.
Each copy of the document was reproduced identically to the original.

Synset Examples

he is fitted with an identically similar one

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: similarly - in a similar way; resembling

Etymology

From 'identical' (from Late Latin 'identicus') + '-ly'.
