PS

1. NOUN

/ ˌpiː ˈɛs / (AmE), / ˌpiː ˈɛs / (BrE)
a note added at the end of a letter or email after the signature, providing additional information
note added after a letter's signature

Examples

She added a PS to the end of her letter to mention the party on Saturday.
In his email, he included a PS with an important reminder.
I forgot to answer your question, so here's a quick PS.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: postscript - a note added to a letter after the signature

Etymology

Abbreviation of Latin post scriptum (written after).
