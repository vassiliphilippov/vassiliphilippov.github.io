jocularly

1. ADV

/ ˈdʒɑː.kjə.lɚ.li / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒk.jʊ.lə.li / (BrE)
in a humorous or joking manner; with humor.
in a humorous manner

Examples

He answered the unexpected question jocularly, making the audience laugh.
Despite the tense situation, she spoke jocularly to break the ice.
The manager jocularly commented on the team's messy workspace.

Synset Examples

they tried to deal with this painful subject jocularly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humorously - in a funny or amusing manner
  • Antonym: seriously - in a serious manner, opposite of humorously

Etymology

From Latin *jocularis* (joking), from *jocus* (joke).
