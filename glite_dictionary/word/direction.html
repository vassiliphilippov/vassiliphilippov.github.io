direction

1. NOUN

/ dəˈrɛkʃən, daɪˈrɛkʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛkʃən, daɪˈrɛkʃən / (BrE)
an official or formal instruction or order given by someone in authority, especially in a legal or organizational setting
formal instruction or order

Examples

Under the board's direction, all staff must complete the new training module.
The court's direction must be followed exactly to avoid penalties.
