Schwarzwald

1. PROPN

/ ˈʃvɑrtsˌvalt / (AmE), / ˈʃvɑːtsˌvalt / (BrE)
a hilly, forested region in southwestern Germany, also known as the Black Forest
forest region in southwestern Germany

Examples

The Schwarzwald is famous for its dense woods and picturesque villages.
Tourists often visit the Schwarzwald for hiking and sampling the local Black Forest cake.
She spent her summer break exploring the scenic trails of the Schwarzwald.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Black Forest - alternative English name for Schwarzwald, a forested region in Germany

Etymology

German, from 'schwarz' (black) + 'Wald' (forest).
