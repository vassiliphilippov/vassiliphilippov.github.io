hearts

1. NOUN

/ hɑrts / (AmE), / hɑːts / (BrE)
A card game for four players in which each tries to avoid taking tricks containing hearts or the queen of spades.
card game avoiding hearts and queen of spades

Examples

We played hearts all evening at the cabin.
She lost badly in hearts because she took the queen of spades twice.
Do you know the rules for hearts, or should I explain them?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spades - another suit in a deck of cards and a card game
