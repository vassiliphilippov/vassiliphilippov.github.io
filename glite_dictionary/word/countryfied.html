countryfied

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌntrifaɪd / (AmE), / ˈkʌntrɪfaɪd / (BrE)
having qualities or characteristics typical of rural life; rustic or unsophisticated.
characteristic of rural life; rustic

Examples

The bed and breakfast had a countryfied charm that made guests feel relaxed and at home.
She preferred wearing countryfied dresses with floral patterns and simple fabrics.
His manners were kind but a bit countryfied, showing his roots in a small farming community.

Synset Examples

countrified clothes
rustic awkwardness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rustic - characteristic of the countryside; simple and unsophisticated
  • Antonym: urban - relating to a city or town

Etymology

Formed from 'country' + '-fied', a variant of '-fied' meaning 'made' or 'become'.
