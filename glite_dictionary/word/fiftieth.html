fiftieth

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪf.ti.əθ / (AmE), / ˈfɪf.ti.ɪθ / (BrE)
the position in a sequence that comes after the forty-ninth and before the fifty-first; ordinal number 50th.
50th in a sequence

Examples

He finished the race in fiftieth place, just making it into the top fifty.
This is the fiftieth time I've told you to be careful with that vase.
The city is celebrating its fiftieth anniversary with a big parade.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty-ninth - 49th in a sequence; the previous ordinal
  • Degree Higher: fifty-first - 51st in a sequence; the next ordinal

Etymology

From Middle English 'fifty' + '-th', forming ordinals.

2. ADJ

/ ˈfɪftiəθ / (AmE), / ˈfɪftiɪθ / (BrE)
ordinal number matching the position or sequence number 50th in a list or series
the ordinal number 50th

Examples

She celebrated her fiftieth birthday with a big party.
The runner finished in fiftieth place out of a hundred competitors.
This is the fiftieth anniversary of the founding of the school.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fifty - the number 50
  • Degree Lower: forty-ninth - ordinal number for 49th position
  • Degree Higher: fifty-first - ordinal number for 51st position

Etymology

Formed from 'fifty', from Old English 'fīftig', plus the ordinal suffix '-th'.

Synset POS: s
