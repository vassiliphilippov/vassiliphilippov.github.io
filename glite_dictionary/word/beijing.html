Beijing

1. PROPN

/ beɪˈdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / beɪˈdʒiːŋ / (BrE)
The capital city of the People's Republic of China, located in the northeast of the country.
capital city of China

Examples

The 2008 Olympic Games were held in Beijing and attracted athletes from around the world.
Many international companies have offices in Beijing due to its economic importance.
He moved to Beijing to study at one of its top universities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Shanghai - the largest city in China

Etymology

From the Mandarin 北京 (Běijīng), meaning 'northern capital'.
