bumptious

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʌmpʃəs / (AmE), / ˈbʌmpʃəs / (BrE)
Self-assertive in an arrogant or offensively confident way.
arrogantly self-assertive

Examples

I found his bumptious attitude in the meeting quite irritating.
The bumptious new intern quickly got on everyone's nerves with her constant boasting.
Despite his bumptious claims, he failed to impress the interviewers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arrogant - having an exaggerated sense of one's importance or abilities
  • Antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low estimation of one's importance

Etymology

Apparently formed in English as a blend of 'bump' and 'fractious,' imitating a self-important manner (early 19th century).
