bookshop

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊkˌʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈbʊkʃɒp / (BrE)
A shop where books are sold to the public.
shop selling books

Examples

I spent the afternoon browsing in the bookshop near my office.
The new bookshop opened last week and already has many customers.
She dreams of owning her own bookshop someday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bookstore - a store where books are sold; American English equivalent

Etymology

Formed from 'book' + 'shop', modeled after other compounds.
