offer

1. VERB

/ ˈɔːfər / (AmE), / ˈɒfə / (BrE)
to make something available for sale, purchase, or use
make available for sale

Examples

The supermarket is offering fresh strawberries at a reduced price this week.
Many online retailers offer free shipping during the holiday season.
They offered the limited-edition poster for sale at the concert.

Synset Examples

The stores are offering specials on sweaters this week

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sell - to give something to someone else in exchange for money

Etymology

From Latin 'offerre' (to present, bring before), from ob- (toward) + ferre (to bring).
