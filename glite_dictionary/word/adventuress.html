adventuress

1. NOUN

/ ədˈvɛntʃərɪs / (AmE), / ədˈvɛntʃərɪs / (BrE)
A woman who seeks adventure or is daring and enterprising, especially in pursuing personal advantage.
a woman who seeks adventure

Examples

The fearless adventuress trekked across the jungle in pursuit of lost treasures.
In nineteenth-century novels, an adventuress was often portrayed as both daring and mysterious.
They described her as an adventuress because of her bold travels around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adventurer - a person who seeks adventure

Etymology

From 'adventure' + '-ess', a suffix forming female nouns.
