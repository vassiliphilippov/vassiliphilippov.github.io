Stokowski

1. PROPN

/ stoʊˈkaʊski / (AmE), / stəʊˈkaʊski / (BrE)
Leopold Stokowski (1882–1977), British-born American conductor renowned for his long and influential career, particularly with the Philadelphia Orchestra.
renowned British-born American conductor

Examples

Stokowski was famous for his innovative orchestral arrangements and conducting style.
Many music lovers admire the recordings made by Stokowski with the Philadelphia Orchestra.
Disney's "Fantasia" featured Stokowski as the conductor in several segments.
