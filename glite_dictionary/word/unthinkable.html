unthinkable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈθɪŋkəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɪŋkəbl / (BrE)
Impossible to imagine or accept; incapable of being considered as a possible option.
impossible to imagine or accept

Examples

The idea of quitting my job is simply unthinkable at the moment.
It was once unthinkable for women to vote in many countries, but not anymore.
Such a disaster was previously considered unthinkable by most experts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inconceivable - impossible to imagine or believe
  • Synonym: impossible - not able to occur or be done
  • Antonym: thinkable - capable of being thought of or considered

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'thinkable'.
