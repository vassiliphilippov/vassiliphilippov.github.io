asker

1. NOUN

/ ˈæskər / (AmE), / ˈɑːskə / (BrE)
a person who asks a question or requests information
person who asks a question

Examples

The asker waited patiently for a response to his question.
Each asker in the meeting received a thoughtful answer from the panel.
Online forums often show the username of the original asker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: questioner - someone who asks questions

Etymology

From 'ask' + '-er', forming an agent noun.
