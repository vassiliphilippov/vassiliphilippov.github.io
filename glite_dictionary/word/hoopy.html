hoopy

1. ADJ

/ ˈhuːpi / (AmE), / ˈhuːpi / (BrE)
(slang) Very cool, accomplished, or together; knowledgeable and well-prepared.
very cool or accomplished (slang)
slang

Examples

All the hoopy froods know where their towels are.
She seemed pretty hoopy after handling that crisis.
If you want to be hoopy, you’d better prepare for anything.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cool - very good, fashionable, or impressive

Etymology

Popularized by Douglas Adams in 'The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy' (1979).
