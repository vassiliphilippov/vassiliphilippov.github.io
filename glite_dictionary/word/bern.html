Bern

1. PROPN

/ bɛrn / (AmE), / bɜːn / (BrE)
the capital city of Switzerland, located in the western part of the country
capital city of Switzerland

Examples

Many tourists visit Bern to admire its medieval old town and historic clock tower.
The Swiss Parliament is located in Bern, making it the political center of the country.
After moving to Bern, she quickly learned to appreciate Swiss cuisine and culture.

Etymology

German name for the city of Bern, first mentioned in 1191 as 'Bern.'
