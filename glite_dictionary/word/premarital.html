premarital

1. ADJ

/ priˈmærɪt̬əl / (AmE), / priˈmærɪtəl / (BrE)
occurring or existing before marriage, especially in reference to activities, conditions, or agreements
before marriage

Examples

They attended premarital counseling to prepare for their life together.
Many couples sign a premarital agreement to clarify financial matters.
Her parents were concerned about premarital relationships and their implications.

Synset Examples

prenuptial agreement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prenuptial - existing or occurring before marriage

Etymology

Formed from Latin prefix 'pre-' (before) + 'marital' (from Latin 'maritalis', relating to marriage).
