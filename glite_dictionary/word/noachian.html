Noachian

1. ADJ

/ noʊˈeɪkiən / (AmE), / nəʊˈeɪkɪən / (BrE)
Relating to Noah or the time of Noah, especially as described in the biblical account.
relating to Noah or his era

Examples

The ancient text describes a Noachian flood that reshaped the landscape.
Many scholars debate the historical basis of the Noachian period.
The myth has inspired countless works that reference Noachian events and catastrophes.

Synset Examples

Noachian deluge

Etymology

From Latin Noachus (Noah), from Hebrew Noach.
