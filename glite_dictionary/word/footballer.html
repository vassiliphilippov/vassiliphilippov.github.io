footballer

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌbɔlɚ / (AmE), / ˈfʊtbɔːlə / (BrE)
An athlete who plays American football, typically at a professional or collegiate level.
athlete in American football

Examples

The talented footballer signed a contract with the New York Giants.
As a college footballer, he trained six days a week to stay in top shape.
Many young athletes dream of becoming a professional footballer in the NFL.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: football player - a player of American football
