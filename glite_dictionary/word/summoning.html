summoning

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌmənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of calling forth supposed supernatural forces by using spells or incantations
calling up supernatural forces with spells

Examples

Many stories feature the summoning of demons through ancient rituals.
The wizard specialized in the summoning of mystical creatures to aid him in battle.
Legends warn that reckless summoning can bring great danger to those unprepared.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conjuration - the act of summoning or invoking a spirit, demon, or deity by magical means
  • Antonym: banishing - the act of driving out or dismissing a supernatural entity

Etymology

From the verb 'summon', from Old French 'sumoner', from Latin 'summonere' (to call, summon).
