geta

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɛtə / (AmE), / ˈɡɛtə / (BrE)
a traditional Japanese footwear with a wooden sole, often raised on two blocks and worn with kimono or other Japanese attire
Japanese wooden sandals

Examples

She wore a pair of geta with her kimono during the festival.
Traditional Japanese geta make a distinctive clacking sound as you walk.
Tourists sometimes buy geta as souvenirs because of their unique design.

Etymology

From Japanese 下駄 (geta).
