bullfighter

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlˌfaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌfaɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose profession is fighting bulls, especially as part of a public spectacle in countries such as Spain or Mexico
person who fights bulls in bullfighting events

Examples

The famous bullfighter was cheered by thousands of spectators in the arena.
She studied the art and tradition before becoming a bullfighter in Spain.
Many people admire the courage and skill it takes to be a bullfighter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: matador - the main bullfighter who kills the bull in a bullfight
  • Degree Lower: bull - the animal fought in bullfighting
