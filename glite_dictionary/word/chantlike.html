chantlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃæntˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːntˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Having a monotonous, rhythmic, or musical quality similar to chanting.
resembling chanting in sound or rhythm

Examples

The monks' voices had a chantlike rhythm as they recited their prayers.
She spoke in a slow, chantlike tone that calmed the children.
The leader's chantlike instructions echoed through the hall.

Synset Examples

their chantlike intoned prayers
a singsong manner of speaking

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monotonous - lacking in variety and pitch; dull and repetitive
  • Synonym: singsong - characterized by a repetitive and musical tone, like singing
