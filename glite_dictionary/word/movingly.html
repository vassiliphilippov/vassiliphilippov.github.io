movingly

1. ADV

/ ˈmuːvɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈmuːvɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that arouses strong emotion, especially sadness or sympathy
in an emotionally affecting way

Examples

She spoke movingly about her experiences during the war.
The film’s ending was movingly portrayed by the actors.
He wrote movingly of his childhood hardships in his autobiography.

Synset Examples

she sang movingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emotionally - in a way that relates to or expresses emotion
