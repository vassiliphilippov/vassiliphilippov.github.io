adenitis

1. NOUN

/ ˌædəˈnaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌadɪˈnʌɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of a gland or a lymph node.
inflammation of a gland or lymph node
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed the swelling in his neck as adenitis.
Treatment for adenitis often involves addressing the underlying infection.
Persistent adenitis may require further medical investigation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lymphadenitis - inflammation of the lymph nodes

Etymology

From Greek 'aden', meaning 'gland', and '-itis', meaning 'inflammation'.
