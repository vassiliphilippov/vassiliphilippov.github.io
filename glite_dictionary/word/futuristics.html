futuristics

1. NOUN

/ fjuːˈtʃɚˌɪstɪks / (AmE), / fjʊˈtʃʊərɪstɪks / (BrE)
the study or prediction of future trends or developments based on current conditions
study of future trends or developments

Examples

He is an expert in futuristics and predicts major shifts in global technology.
Universities sometimes offer courses in futuristics to help students anticipate future challenges.
Her interest in futuristics began with a fascination for science fiction novels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: futurology - the systematic study of the future, especially as an academic discipline

Etymology

From 'future' + '-istics', modeled after scientific fields ending in '-istics' or '-ics'.
