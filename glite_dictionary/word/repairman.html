repairman

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈpɛrˌmæn / (AmE), / rɪˈpeəˌman / (BrE)
a man whose job is to fix or mend machines, equipment, or other things that are broken or not working properly
man who repairs things as a job

Examples

The repairman fixed our washing machine in less than an hour.
When the air conditioner broke, we called a repairman to take a look at it.
A repairman must carry a variety of tools to handle different types of jobs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repairperson - a person whose job is to repair things, gender-neutral term
