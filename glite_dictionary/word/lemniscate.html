lemniscate

1. NOUN

/ lɛmˈnɪskət / (AmE), / lɛmˈnɪskət / (BrE)
A plane algebraic curve shaped like a figure eight, especially as represented in mathematical equations.
figure-eight mathematical curve

Examples

The mathematician drew a perfect lemniscate on the board to illustrate the concept.
In calculus, a lemniscate can be described by a specific polar equation.
The artist incorporated the elegant shape of a lemniscate into her abstract painting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infinity symbol - mathematical symbol (∞), which has the shape of a lemniscate

Etymology

From Latin lemniscatus (decorated with ribbons), from Greek lēmniskos (ribbon).
