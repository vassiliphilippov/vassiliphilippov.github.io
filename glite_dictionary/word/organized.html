organized

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔrɡəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡənaɪzd / (BrE)
arranged or structured in a systematic and efficient way; methodical and orderly
methodical and systematic; well arranged

Examples

She kept all her files organized in clearly labeled folders.
His organized approach to studying helped him achieve excellent grades.
The organized layout of the store made shopping quick and easy.

Synset Examples

how well organized she is
his life was almost too organized

Related Terms

  • Antonym: disorganized - not properly arranged or lacking order
  • Synonym: systematic - done or acting according to a plan or system; methodical

Etymology

From organize + -ed, related to the French 'organiser' and Medieval Latin 'organizare'.

Source: WordNet

2. ADJ

/ ˈɔrɡəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡənaɪzd / (BrE)
arranged or structured in a systematic, orderly, or coherent way, especially on a large scale
structured or systematic; well arranged

Examples

She keeps an organized desk so she can always find what she needs.
The conference was highly organized and ran smoothly from start to finish.
They joined an organized sports league to play soccer every weekend.

Synset Examples

organized medicine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: systematic - done according to a fixed plan or system; methodical
  • Antonym: disorganized - lacking order or structure

Etymology

From organize + -ed, with organize from French 'organiser' and Medieval Latin 'organizare'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01674474-a
Synset POS: a

3. ADJ

/ ˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡənaɪzd / (BrE)
(of a worker, group, or workplace) belonging to or formed into a labor union.
belonging to a labor union

Examples

The company has both organized and non-organized employees on its staff.
Many organized workers participated in the strike to demand better wages.
An organized shop must follow the rules agreed upon by the labor union.

Synset Examples

organized labor
unionized workers
a unionized shop

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unionized - belonging to or formed into a labor union
