headset

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌsɛt / (BrE)
A device worn over the head that consists of a pair of headphones, typically also including a microphone, used for listening to audio and/or speaking.
pair of headphones, often with microphone

Examples

She put on her headset and joined the online meeting.
The pilot wore a special headset to communicate with air traffic control.
Gamers often use a headset with a built-in microphone to talk to teammates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headphones - A pair of small loudspeakers worn on or around the head over a user's ears.

Etymology

From 'head' + 'set', referring to a set of audio receivers worn on the head.
