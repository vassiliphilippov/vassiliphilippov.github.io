dealings

1. NOUN

/ ˈdiːlɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈdiːlɪŋz / (BrE)
the activities or conduct involved in transacting business or maintaining relations, especially commercial or professional
transactions or relations, especially business

Examples

His business dealings with foreign companies were always conducted with integrity.
The police are investigating her dealings with the suspect over the past year.
They assured the public all their dealings had been transparent and above board.

Synset Examples

no transactions are possible without him
he has always been honest in his dealings with me

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transactions - instances of buying or selling; business arrangements
  • Synonym: relations - the way in which two or more people or organizations are connected

Etymology

From 'deal', meaning to conduct transactions or actions, with the suffix '-ing' and plural form.

2. NOUN

/ ˈdiːlɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈdiːlɪŋz / (BrE)
interactions or relationships between people, groups, or organizations, especially in business or official contexts
interactions or relationships between parties

Examples

His business dealings with the company have always been honest and straightforward.
She avoided any personal dealings with her former colleagues after leaving the firm.
International dealings can be complicated by differences in law and culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relations - the way in which two or more people or groups feel and behave towards each other
  • Synonym: transactions - instances of buying or selling; business conducted

Etymology

From 'deal' (verb: to do business, to trade), plus the suffix '-ing' and plural '-s'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13802661-n
Synset POS: n

3. NOUN

/ ˈdiːlɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈdiːlɪŋz / (BrE)
interactions or communications, especially in the context of business, social, or verbal exchanges (usually followed by 'with')
interactions or communications with others

Examples

He always preferred honest dealings with his clients.
Her dealings with the company were brief but professional.
The report outlined the senator's dealings with foreign officials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relationship - the way in which two or more people or things are connected
  • Synonym: transactions - instances of buying, selling, or exchanging

Etymology

From Middle English deleyng, from 'deal' + -ing, meaning action or conduct.

Source: WordNet
