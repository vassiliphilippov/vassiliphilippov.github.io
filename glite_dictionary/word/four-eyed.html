four-eyed

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔr ˌaɪd / (AmE), / ˈfɔː ˌaɪd / (BrE)
(slang, derogatory) Used to describe a person who wears glasses, often as an insult.
slang term for someone with glasses
slang offensive

Examples

The kids in his class used to call him four-eyed because of his thick glasses.
She was unfairly labeled as four-eyed by some of her peers.
He overcame the hurtful four-eyed nickname he was given in school.

Synset Examples

He was often teased for being four-eyed in school.
The term four-eyes used to be an insult in school.
She embraced her identity as a four-eyes geek.
They called him four-eyes because of his glasses.
