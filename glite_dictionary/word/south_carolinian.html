South Carolinian

1. NOUN

/ saʊθ ˌkæ.rəˈlɪn.i.ən / (AmE), / saʊθ ˌkær.əˈlɪn.i.ən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of South Carolina, a state in the southeastern United States
native or resident of South Carolina, US

Examples

Many South Carolinians are proud of their state's rich history and cultural heritage.
The festival drew visitors from all over, but most attendees were local South Carolinians.
As a South Carolinian, she grew up enjoying Lowcountry cuisine and Southern hospitality.
