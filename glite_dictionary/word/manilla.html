manilla

1. NOUN

/ məˈnɪlə / (AmE), / məˈnɪlə / (BrE)
A strong paper or thin cardboard with a smooth light brown finish, originally made from Manila hemp.
strong, light brown paper or cardboard

Examples

The documents were stored in a manilla envelope for safekeeping.
She bought a pack of manilla folders to organize her papers.
Each student received a manilla tag for their name.

Etymology

From Manila, the capital of the Philippines, where Manila hemp was produced.
