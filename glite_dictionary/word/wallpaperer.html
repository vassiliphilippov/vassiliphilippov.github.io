wallpaperer

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔːlˌpeɪpɚɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːlˌpeɪpərə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to hang wallpaper on walls
person who hangs wallpaper

Examples

We hired a professional wallpaperer to redecorate the living room.
The wallpaperer brought several sample books for us to choose from.
After years as a wallpaperer, he developed an expert eye for patterns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decorator - a person whose job is to decorate interior spaces, including walls

Etymology

From 'wallpaper' + '-er', indicating someone who does an action.
