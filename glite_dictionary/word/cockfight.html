cockfight

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑkˌfaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒkˌfaɪt / (BrE)
A contest in which two roosters, specially bred and trained for fighting, are placed in a ring to fight each other, often with metal spurs attached.
contest between two fighting roosters

Examples

Spectators crowded around to watch the illegal cockfight in the rural village.
Police raided the warehouse where a cockfight was taking place.
He was arrested for organizing a cockfight and training the birds for combat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cockfighting - the practice or sport of organizing cockfights

Etymology

From 'cock' (rooster) + 'fight'.
