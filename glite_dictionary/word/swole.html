swole

1. ADJ

/ swoʊl / (AmE), / swəʊl / (BrE)
(slang) Extremely muscular or having well-developed muscles.
extremely muscular (slang)
slang informal

Examples

Ever since he started lifting weights, he's gotten really swole.
The actor trained for months to look swole for the superhero role.
Everyone at the gym was impressed by how swole she looked after her transformation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muscular - having well-developed muscles
  • Synonym: ripped - having a muscular and well-defined physique (slang)
  • Antonym: skinny - very thin, lacking muscle or fat

Etymology

Altered form of "swollen," influenced by African American Vernacular English.
