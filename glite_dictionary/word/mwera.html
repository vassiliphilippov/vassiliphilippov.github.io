Mwera

1. NOUN

/ ˈmwɛrə / (AmE), / ˈmwɛərə / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken primarily along the southern coast of Tanzania
Bantu language of southern Tanzania

Examples

The Mwera language is used in education in some parts of southern Tanzania.
He is fluent in both Swahili and Mwera.
Researchers documented local folktales spoken in Mwera.
