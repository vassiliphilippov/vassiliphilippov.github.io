pisanosaur

1. NOUN

/ ˌpaɪsəˈnɔːr / (AmE), / ˌpiːsəˈnɔː / (BrE)
a primitive dinosaur genus found in Argentina, considered one of the earliest known ornithischians
primitive dinosaur from Argentina

Examples

The pisanosaur is one of the oldest known dinosaurs discovered in South America.
Fossils of the pisanosaur provide insights into the early evolution of dinosaurs.
A team of paleontologists unearthed a nearly complete pisanosaur skeleton in Argentina.

Etymology

From Pisanosaurus, named after Argentine paleontologist Juan Pisano, plus Greek 'sauros' (lizard).
