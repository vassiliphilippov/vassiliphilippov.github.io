miaow

1. NOUN

/ miˈaʊ / (AmE), / miˈaʊ / (BrE)
the cry or sound made by a cat, or any noise resembling this
sound made by a cat

Examples

The kitten's miaow woke me up early in the morning.
We heard a soft miaow coming from behind the door.
Her ringtone is the sound of a cat's miaow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meow - alternative form of the cry of a cat
  • Degree Lower: purr - low continuous vibrating sound made by a cat
  • Degree Higher: yowl - loud, wailing cry made by a cat

Etymology

Imitative of the sound made by a cat.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07401214-n
Synset POS: n

2. VERB

/ miˈaʊ / (AmE), / miˈaʊ / (BrE)
(of a cat) to make the characteristic crying sound; to mew or meow.
(of a cat) make its crying sound

Examples

The kitten miaowed softly at the back door, waiting to be let in.
As soon as she saw her owner, the hungry cat began to miaow loudly.
All night, strays miaowed beneath the window, keeping the neighbors awake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meow - (of a cat) make the characteristic crying sound (American spelling)

Etymology

Imitative of the sound a cat makes.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00943751-v
Synset POS: v
