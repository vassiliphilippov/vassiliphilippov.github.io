Ponte 25 de Abril

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɒnteɪ ˌventi ˈsɪŋku dɪ əˈbril / (AmE), / ˈpɒnteɪ ˌvɛnti ˈsɪŋku deɪ əˈbriːl / (BrE)
a large suspension bridge across the Tagus River in Lisbon, Portugal, named to commemorate the Carnation Revolution of 25 April 1974
suspension bridge in Lisbon, Portugal

Examples

The Ponte 25 de Abril is an iconic landmark visible from many parts of Lisbon.
Traffic was backed up for miles on the Ponte 25 de Abril during the rush hour commute.
Many tourists enjoy photographing the Ponte 25 de Abril with the city skyline in the background.

Etymology

Portuguese, meaning '25th of April Bridge', named after the date of the 1974 revolution.
