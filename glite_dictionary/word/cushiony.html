cushiony

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʊʃəni / (AmE), / ˈkʊʃəni / (BrE)
soft and comfortable, like or filled with cushions or padding
soft and padded; like a cushion

Examples

I love the cushiony seats in this theater—they make long movies much more enjoyable.
The new slippers have a cushiony feel that makes walking around the house a pleasure.
She rested her head on a cushiony pillow and quickly fell asleep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soft - not hard or firm; yielding easily to pressure

Etymology

From "cushion" + English adjective suffix "-y".
