albuca

1. NOUN

/ ælˈbuːkə / (AmE), / ælˈbjuːkə / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Albuca, native to South Africa, with large clusters of pale yellow flowers
plant with pale yellow flowers, native to South Africa

Examples

The albuca in her garden blossomed with delicate pale yellow flowers in the spring.
Many collectors prize the unique curly leaves of the albuca plant.
Botanists travelled to South Africa to study different species of albuca in the wild.

Etymology

From Latin Albuca, the genus name, of uncertain origin.
