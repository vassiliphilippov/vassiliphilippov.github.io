irretrievable

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪ.rɪˈtriv.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌɪ.rɪˈtriː.və.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to recover, regain, or repair; unable to be restored or remedied.
impossible to recover or restore

Examples

The documents were destroyed in a fire, making their loss completely irretrievable.
After the scandal, her reputation suffered irretrievable damage.
He committed an irretrievable mistake that changed the course of his career.

Synset Examples

an irretrievable loss
irretrievable errors in judgment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irreversible - impossible to reverse or undo
  • Antonym: recoverable - able to be recovered or regained

Etymology

From Latin 'irretrievabilis', from in- ‘not’ + retrievabilis ‘recoverable’.
