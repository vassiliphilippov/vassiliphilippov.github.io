literacy

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬.ɚ.ə.si / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.ər.ə.si / (BrE)
the ability to read and write.
ability to read and write

Examples

Literacy rates have improved significantly over the past decade.
Promoting literacy is essential for economic and social development.
The government launched a new literacy program for adults in rural areas.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: illiteracy - the inability to read and write

Etymology

From Latin 'literatus' (learned, lettered), from 'litera' (letter).
