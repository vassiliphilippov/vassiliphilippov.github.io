forty-second

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɔrtiˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˌfɔːtiˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
coming after the forty-first in a series; 42nd.
ordinal number: 42nd

Examples

She finished in forty-second place in the marathon.
This is the forty-second time I’ve asked you to clean your room.
The company is celebrating its forty-second anniversary this year.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty-first - ordinal number: 41st
  • Degree Higher: forty-third - ordinal number: 43rd
