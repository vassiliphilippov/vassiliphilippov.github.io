Latin American

1. NOUN

/ ˌlætn əˈmɛrɪkən / (AmE), / ˌlatɪn əˈmɛrɪkən / (BrE)
a person who was born in, or comes from, Latin America.
a person from Latin America

Examples

There has been a rise in the number of Latin Americans living in the city.
The festival celebrates the culture and traditions of Latin Americans.
As a Latin American, she is proud of her heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Latino - a person of Latin American origin or descent
  • Antonym: North American - a person from North America

Etymology

From Latin (referring to Romance-language heritage) + American (from America).
