eisteddfod

1. NOUN

/ aɪˈstɛd.vɑd / (AmE), / aɪˈstɛd.vɒd / (BrE)
An annual Welsh festival featuring artistic competitions such as poetry, music, and especially singing.
Welsh festival of artistic competitions

Examples

The local community center is hosting its first eisteddfod next month.
She won several awards for her poetry at the national eisteddfod.
Children from different schools take part in the eisteddfod every spring.

Etymology

From Welsh eisteddfod (session, sitting), from eistedd (to sit) + bod (to be).
