Eumenes

1. PROPN

/ juːˈmiːniːz / (AmE), / juːˈmiːniːz / (BrE)
A genus of mason wasps known for constructing nests from mud; these insects are found in various regions worldwide.
genus of mason wasps

Examples

The Eumenes wasp builds pot-shaped nests using small pellets of mud.
Researchers observed that Eumenes are effective at controlling caterpillar populations.
Several species within the genus Eumenes are commonly found in temperate climates.

Etymology

From Latin Eumenes, derived from Greek Eumenēs, meaning 'well-disposed'; used as a taxonomic name in zoology.
