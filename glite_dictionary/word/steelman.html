steelman

1. NOUN

/ ˈstiːlˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈstiːlˌman / (BrE)
a man who works in the production or manufacturing of steel
male steel worker

Examples

My grandfather was a proud steelman in the mills of Pittsburgh.
During the industrial boom, the life of a steelman was demanding and dangerous.
They honored every retired steelman at the factory's centennial celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steelworker - a person who works in the production of steel

Etymology

From 'steel' (the metal) + 'man' (worker).
