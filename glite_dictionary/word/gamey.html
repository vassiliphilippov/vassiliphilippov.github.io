gamey

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡeɪmi / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmi / (BrE)
(of language, stories, etc.) slightly shocking, often because of sexual content or suggestiveness.
sexually suggestive, slightly indecent

Examples

Some of the jokes in his routine were a bit gamey for the audience.
The novel was considered too gamey for publication in the 1950s.
Her stories always have a gamey sense of humor that some people might find inappropriate.

2. ADJ

/ ˈɡeɪmi / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmi / (BrE)
(of the smell or flavor of meat) having a strong, distinctive, and sometimes slightly spoiled odor or taste, often considered unpleasant.
meat tasting or smelling strong or tainted

Examples

The leftover venison had a slightly gamey aroma that made it unappetizing.
Some people enjoy the strong, gamey flavor of wild boar, but others find it off-putting.
After several days without refrigeration, the meat grew progressively more gamey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spoiled - having become unfit for eating or use; turned bad
  • Antonym: fresh - not spoiled or tainted, recently prepared

Etymology

From 'game' (wild animals hunted for food), influenced by the strong flavor often associated with their meat, plus '-y'.
