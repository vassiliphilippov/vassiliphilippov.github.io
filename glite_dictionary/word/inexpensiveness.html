inexpensiveness

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.ɪkˈspɛn.sɪv.nəs / (AmE), / ˌɪn.ɪkˈspen.sɪv.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of costing little or being affordable
the quality of being affordable

Examples

The inexpensiveness of the new smartphone makes it popular among students.
We chose that hotel for its inexpensiveness as well as its location.
Many people appreciate the inexpensiveness of public transportation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheapness - the quality of being low in price
  • Antonym: expensiveness - the quality of being high in price

Etymology

From 'inexpensive' (not costing a lot) + '-ness' (state or quality).
