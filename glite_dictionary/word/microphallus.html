microphallus

1. NOUN

/ ˌmaɪkroʊˈfæləs / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈfaləs / (BrE)
A penis that is abnormally small, especially as defined by medical criteria.
abnormally small penis (medical)
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that microphallus can sometimes be a symptom of hormonal disorders.
A newborn diagnosed with microphallus may require further medical evaluation.
Researchers are studying the genetic factors that can cause microphallus in infants.

Etymology

From Greek 'mikros' (small) + 'phallos' (penis).
