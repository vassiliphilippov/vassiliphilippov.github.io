chinless

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɪnləs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪn.ləs / (BrE)
having or characteristic of a receding or very small chin
having a receding chin

Examples

The portrait featured a chinless man with delicate features.
He was described as a shy, chinless aristocrat by his peers.
Some people feel self-conscious about appearing chinless in photographs.
