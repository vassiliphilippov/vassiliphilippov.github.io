unreportable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈpɔːrt.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈpɔː.tə.bəl / (BrE)
(of income or information) not required by law to be reported; not reported.
not required to be reported

Examples

Certain foreign assets may be considered unreportable under current tax regulations.
The accountant explained that the amount in question was unreportable and did not need to appear on the form.
Some types of compensation are unreportable, making them exempt from disclosure requirements.

Synset Examples

very little income is unreportable

Related Terms

  • Antonym: reportable - required by law to be reported

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'reportable'.
