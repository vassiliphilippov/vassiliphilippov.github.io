imbecile

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪmbəˌsɪl / (AmE), / ˈɪmbəsɪl / (BrE)
an offensive term for a person with extremely low intelligence or mental capacity; also used as an insult meaning a very foolish person
a person with very low intelligence (offensive)
offensive archaic

Examples

Calling your classmates an imbecile is disrespectful and offensive.
In the early 20th century, the word imbecile was used as a medical classification, but it is now outdated and inappropriate.
He dismissed the suggestion, muttering that only an imbecile would propose such an idea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a person of extremely low intelligence (offensive, old-fashioned)
  • Synonym: moron - a person with very low intelligence (offensive, old-fashioned)
  • Antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence or ability

Etymology

From Latin imbecillus (weak, feeble).
