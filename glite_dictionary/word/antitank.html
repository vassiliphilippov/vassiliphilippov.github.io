antitank

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.tiˈtæŋk / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈtæŋk / (BrE)
(of weapons, equipment, or measures) designed for defense against tanks and other armored vehicles.
designed to combat tanks

Examples

The army deployed several antitank missiles along the front line.
Modern antitank weapons can penetrate even the thickest armor.
They reinforced their defenses with antitank mines to prevent enemy advances.

Etymology

From 'anti-' (against) + 'tank' (armored military vehicle).
