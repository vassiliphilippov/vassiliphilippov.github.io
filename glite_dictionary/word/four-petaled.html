four-petaled

1. ADJ

/ fɔr ˈpɛtld / (AmE), / fɔː ˈpɛtld / (BrE)
(of a flower) having four petals.
having four petals (of a flower)

Examples

The plant produces beautiful four-petaled flowers every spring.
She sketched a four-petaled blossom in her notebook during biology class.
Many members of the mustard family have four-petaled flowers arranged in a cross shape.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: five-petaled - having five petals (of a flower)
  • Degree Lower: three-petaled - having three petals (of a flower)
