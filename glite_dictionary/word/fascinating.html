fascinating

1. ADJ

/ ˈfæsəˌneɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfæsɪneɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely interesting or charming, capturing and holding attention.
captivating; very interesting

Examples

The museum has a fascinating display of ancient artifacts.
She told us a fascinating tale about her travels in Africa.
I found the documentary absolutely fascinating and couldn't look away.

Synset Examples

a fascinating story

Related Terms

  • Synonym: captivating - very interesting or charming
  • Antonym: boring - not interesting; dull

Etymology

From Latin 'fascinatus', past participle of 'fascinare' (to bewitch, enchant).
