sharp-nosed

1. ADJ

/ ʃɑrp noʊzd / (AmE), / ʃɑːp nəʊzd / (BrE)
having a nose or front end that is sharply pointed
having a sharply pointed nose

Examples

The sharp-nosed aircraft was designed to reduce air resistance.
A sharp-nosed dog is often able to sniff out scents more easily.
The sharp-nosed shovel made digging through the hard soil much simpler.
