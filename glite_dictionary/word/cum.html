cum

1. NOUN

/ kʌm / (AmE), / kʌm / (BrE)
A slang term for the thick, white fluid containing sperm that is ejaculated from the male genital tract; semen.
slang for semen (male ejaculate)
slang vulgar

Examples

He quickly cleaned up the cum from the bedsheets.
The explicit scene showed cum on the table.
She was embarrassed when she accidentally stepped into a puddle of cum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: semen - the fluid containing sperm produced by the male reproductive organs

Etymology

Altered spelling of 'come', referring to sexual climax (to come).
