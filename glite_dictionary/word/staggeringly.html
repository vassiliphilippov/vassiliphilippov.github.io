staggeringly

1. ADV

/ ˈstæɡɚɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈstæɡərɪŋli / (BrE)
To a degree that is extremely surprising, impressive, or hard to believe.
extremely surprisingly or greatly

Examples

The company's new product was staggeringly successful, exceeding all expectations.
She finished the marathon in a staggeringly fast time, shocking everyone.
The cost of living in that city is staggeringly high compared to the national average.

Synset Examples

he was enormously popular

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enormously - to a very great degree; extremely
  • Synonym: astonishingly - in a way that causes great surprise

Etymology

From 'staggering' (causing shock or astonishment) + adverbial suffix -ly.
