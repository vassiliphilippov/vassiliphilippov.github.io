skillfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪlfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈskɪlf(ʊ)lnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having or showing skill; proficiency or competence in a particular area
the state of being skillful

Examples

Her skillfulness in negotiations helped the company secure a better deal.
The skillfulness of the surgeon was praised by all her colleagues.
He completed the task with remarkable skillfulness, impressing his supervisor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: competence - the ability to do something successfully or efficiently
  • Antonym: ineptitude - lack of skill or ability

Etymology

From 'skillful' + '-ness', indicating the state or quality.
