El Muerto

1. PROPN

/ el ˈmwɛrtoʊ / (AmE), / el ˈmwɛətəʊ / (BrE)
a mountain peak in the Andes on the border between Argentina and Chile, rising to 21,457 feet (6,544 meters)
Andean mountain on Argentina–Chile border

Examples

Climbers from around the world attempt to reach the summit of El Muerto each year.
The border between Argentina and Chile runs across El Muerto in the Andes.
Weather conditions on El Muerto can be extremely harsh due to its high altitude.

Etymology

Spanish, meaning 'the dead man'
