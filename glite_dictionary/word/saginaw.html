Saginaw

1. PROPN

/ ˈsæɡɪˌnɔ / (AmE), / ˈsæɡɪnɔː / (BrE)
A city in east central Michigan, located on the Saginaw River near an arm of Lake Huron.
city in east central Michigan, USA

Examples

I grew up in Saginaw and still visit my family there every summer.
The economy of Saginaw was once heavily reliant on manufacturing.
Many visitors enjoy boating on the river that runs through Saginaw.

Etymology

From Ojibwe *Saginaw* meaning 'where the Sauk were', from the history of the area.
