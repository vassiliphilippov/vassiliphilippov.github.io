Panama City

1. PROPN

/ ˌpænəˈmɑ ˈsɪti / (AmE), / ˌpænəmə ˈsɪti / (BrE)
A city and resort area in northwest Florida on the Gulf of Mexico, popular for its beaches and fishing.
city in northwest Florida, USA

Examples

Every summer, tourists flock to Panama City for its beautiful white-sand beaches.
The fishing industry is a major part of Panama City's local economy.
Hurricane Michael caused significant damage to Panama City in 2018.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Panama City Beach - a neighboring Gulf Coast resort town in Florida
