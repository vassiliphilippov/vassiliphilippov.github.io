planless

1. ADJ

/ ˈplæn.ləs / (AmE), / ˈplæn.ləs / (BrE)
Without a plan, aim, or direction; lacking a method or purpose.
without a plan or direction

Examples

His planless approach to work often led to confusion and delays.
They wandered planless through the city streets, enjoying the day as it unfolded.
A planless investment strategy can be risky for beginners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aimless - without purpose or direction
  • Antonym: purposeful - having a clear aim or purpose

Etymology

Formed from 'plan' + '-less', a common English suffix meaning 'without'.
