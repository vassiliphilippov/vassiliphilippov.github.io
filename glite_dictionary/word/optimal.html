optimal

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːptɪməl / (AmE), / ˈɒptɪməl / (BrE)
Best or most favorable; most desirable or effective under specific conditions or restrictions.
most favorable or effective

Examples

The medicine must be stored at an optimal temperature to remain effective.
She adjusted the lighting to achieve optimal conditions for reading.
Regular exercise is important for optimal health.

Synset Examples

an optimum return on capital
optimal concentration of a drug

Related Terms

  • Antonym: suboptimal - less than the best or most desirable possible
  • Synonym: optimum - the best or most favorable condition, degree, or amount for a particular situation

Etymology

From Latin 'optimus' (best).
