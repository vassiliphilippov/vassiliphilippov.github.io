magniloquently

1. ADV

/ mægˈnɪləkwəntli / (AmE), / mæɡˈnɪləkwəntli / (BrE)
In a grand or lofty manner, especially in speech or writing; using high-flown or pompous language.
in a grandiose or lofty manner

Examples

The politician spoke magniloquently about his vision for the country, captivating the audience with lofty phrases.
The author writes magniloquently, favoring elaborate and ornate sentences to emphasize his points.
She delivered her acceptance speech magniloquently, choosing words that elevated the occasion.

Synset Examples

the orator spoke magniloquently

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandiloquently - in a lofty or pompous manner
  • Antonym: modestly - in a humble or unassuming manner

Etymology

From Latin 'magniloquus' (speaking grandly) + English adverbial suffix '-ly'.
