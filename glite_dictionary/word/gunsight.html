gunsight

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌnˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌn.saɪt / (BrE)
a device attached to a gun to help the user aim accurately
device for aiming a gun

Examples

The marksman adjusted the gunsight to account for the wind.
Her rifle was equipped with a high-precision gunsight for long-distance shots.
The pilot aligned his target using the plane's gunsight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sight - a device on a firearm for aiming
