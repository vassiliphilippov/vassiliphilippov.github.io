oven

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌvən / (AmE), / ˈʌv(ə)n / (BrE)
A chamber or enclosed compartment, usually part of a kitchen appliance, used for baking, roasting, or heating food.
kitchen appliance for baking or roasting

Examples

Please preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius before baking the cake.
She took the roast chicken out of the oven and let it cool on the counter.
The smell of fresh bread filled the kitchen as it baked in the oven.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stove - appliance used for heating or cooking food

Etymology

Old English 'ofen', from Proto-Germanic '*ubnaz' and related to Dutch 'oven' and German 'Ofen'.
