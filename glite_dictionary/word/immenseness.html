immenseness

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmɛnsnəs / (AmE), / ɪˈmɛnsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely large in size, extent, or number; vastness
vast size or extent

Examples

The immenseness of the ocean filled her with awe.
They underestimated the immenseness of the task ahead of them.
Looking up, we were struck by the immenseness of the night sky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vastness - the quality of being very great in size or extent
  • Antonym: smallness - the quality of being small in size, extent, or number

Etymology

From Latin 'immensus' (immeasurable, vast).
