Idesia

1. NOUN

/ aɪˈdiːziə / (AmE), / aɪˈdiːziə / (BrE)
A genus of deciduous trees in the willow family, native to East Asia, containing the species Idesia polycarpa.
genus of East Asian trees (Idesia polycarpa)

Examples

The Idesia tree is known for its ornamental value and distinctive round fruits.
Botanists have classified only one species under the genus Idesia.
In East Asian gardens, Idesia is appreciated for its broad leaves and attractive appearance.

Etymology

Named after Dutch botanist Pieter van Idsinga.
