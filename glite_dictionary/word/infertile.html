infertile

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈfɜrtəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɜːtaɪl / (BrE)
Unable to produce offspring or reproduce; not fertile.
unable to reproduce

Examples

The doctor explained that she was infertile and discussed treatment options.
In some cases, environmental factors can make animals infertile.
The couple struggled for years before learning they were infertile.

Synset Examples

an infertile couple

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fertile - capable of reproducing or producing offspring

Etymology

From Latin 'infertilis', from 'in-' (not) + 'fertilis' (fruitful).
