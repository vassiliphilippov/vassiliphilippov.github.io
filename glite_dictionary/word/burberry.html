Burberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːbəri / (AmE), / ˈbɜːbəri / (BrE)
A lightweight belted raincoat, typically made of tan gabardine with a distinctive tartan lining; originally manufactured by the Burberry company.
type of belted raincoat with tartan lining

Examples

She wore her classic Burberry to the meeting on a rainy morning.
His grandfather's old Burberry is still stylish and practical after many years.
Many celebrities are photographed in a Burberry during the London fashion season.

Etymology

Named after the British company Burberry, founded by Thomas Burberry in the 19th century.
