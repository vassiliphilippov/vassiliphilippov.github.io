undependably

1. ADV

/ ˌʌndɪˈpɛndəbli / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈpɛndəbli / (BrE)
In a manner that is unreliable, untrustworthy, or lacking dependability.
in an unreliable manner

Examples

The equipment performed undependably during the crucial test phase.
He has handled his assignments undependably in the past, which concerns his supervisor.
The software update causes the application to behave undependably on several devices.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: reliably - in a dependable or trustworthy manner
