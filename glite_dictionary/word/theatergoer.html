theatergoer

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiət̬ɚˌɡoʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈθɪətəˌɡəʊ.ə / (BrE)
a person who attends the theater, especially regularly or as a habit
person who attends the theater

Examples

As an avid theatergoer, she never misses a new stage production in town.
The city is a paradise for the theatergoer seeking both classic and modern performances.
Being a theatergoer has introduced him to a wonderful community of drama enthusiasts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: audience member - a person who is part of the audience

Etymology

Formed from 'theater' + 'goer', indicating a person who goes to the theater.
