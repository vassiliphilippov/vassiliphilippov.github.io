issuing

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪʃuɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪʃuːɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of supplying or distributing something, especially in quantity or for official purposes
the act of distributing something officially

Examples

The issuing of new passports will begin next week.
Due to shortages, the issuing of medication was delayed at the hospital.
Her job involves the issuing of student ID cards every semester.

Synset Examples

a new issue of stamps
the last issue of penicillin was over a month ago

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distribution - the act of giving or delivering something to people

Etymology

From the verb 'issue', which comes from Old French 'essuir', from Latin 'exire' (to go out).
