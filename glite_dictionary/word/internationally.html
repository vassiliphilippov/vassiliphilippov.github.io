internationally

1. ADV

/ ɪn.tɚˈnæʃ.ə.nəl.i / (AmE), / ɪn.təˈnæʃ.ə.nəl.i / (BrE)
In or to many countries; in a way that involves more than one nation or is recognized across nations.
involving many countries

Examples

She is internationally known for her contributions to science.
The movie was released internationally after its success in the United States.
The company operates internationally, with offices in over thirty countries.

Synset Examples

She is internationally known
this is globally significant

Related Terms

  • Antonym: nationally - in a way that relates to one nation
  • Synonym: globally - in or to all parts of the world

Etymology

Formed from 'international' (from inter- + national) plus adverbial suffix -ly.
