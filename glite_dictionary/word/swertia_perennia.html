Swertia perennia

1. NOUN

/ swɜːrtiə pəˈrɛniə / (AmE), / ˈswɜːtɪə pəˈrɛnɪə / (BrE)
A perennial plant found in damp mountainous areas of Eurasia and North America, with dull blue or violet flowers.
mountain perennial plant with blue or violet flowers

Examples

We discovered several clusters of Swertia perennia growing along the damp mountain trail.
The Swertia perennia blooms late in the summer, displaying its characteristic dull violet flowers.
Botanists visited the region to study populations of Swertia perennia in their natural habitat.

Etymology

From the genus name Swertia (named for the Swiss botanist Emanuel Sweerts) and Latin perennis (perennial).
