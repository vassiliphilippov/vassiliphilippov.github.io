caimitillo

1. NOUN

/ kaɪ.miˈti.joʊ / (AmE), / kaɪ.mɪˈtiː.jəʊ / (BrE)
A tropical American tree with dark, hard, heavy wood and small, plum-like purple fruit.
tropical American tree with purple fruit

Examples

We tasted the sweet, juicy fruit of the caimitillo during our trip to Puerto Rico.
The dark, dense wood from the caimitillo is highly valued by local carpenters.
Several caimitillo trees stood at the edge of the rainforest, heavy with purple fruit.

Etymology

From Spanish 'caimitillo', diminutive of 'caimito', a regional name for related tropical trees.
