fatty

1. ADJ

/ ˈfæti / (AmE), / ˈfæti / (BrE)
Containing or composed of fat; having a high fat content.
containing a lot of fat

Examples

Eating too much fatty food can increase your cholesterol levels.
She avoids fatty meats for health reasons.
Fish like salmon are considered fatty, but they contain healthy fats.

Synset Examples

fatty food
fat tissue

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lean - having little or no fat (used especially of meat)
  • Synonym: fat - containing a lot of fat; having fat content (as an adjective)

2. NOUN

/ ˈfæti / (AmE), / ˈfæti / (BrE)
(offensive, informal) A person, especially a child, who is overweight.
overweight person (offensive, informal)
informal offensive

Examples

The other kids used to call him fatty on the playground, which really upset him.
Some people think it's funny to shout 'fatty' at strangers, but it's just cruel.
She hated being referred to as a fatty behind her back.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fatso - offensive term for a fat person
  • Antonym: slim - person who is thin
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10101915-n
Synset POS: n
