student

1. NOUN

/ ˈstuːdənt / (AmE), / ˈstjuːdənt / (BrE)
a person who is studying at a school, college, or university
person studying at an educational institution

Examples

The new student quickly made friends in her class.
Each student is required to submit their assignment by Friday.
The university offers scholarships to every international student.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pupil - a young person who is taught at school
  • Antonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially in a school

Etymology

From Latin 'studens', present participle of 'studere' (to study).

2. NOUN

/ ˈstuːdənt / (AmE), / ˈstjuːdənt / (BrE)
A person who is studying at a school, college, or university, or is learning from a teacher.
person who is learning, especially in school

Examples

The student raised her hand to ask a question during the lesson.
Many students find mathematics challenging but rewarding.
As a university student, he attended lectures and worked on research projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pupil - a person, especially a child, who is taught by a teacher, usually in school
  • Antonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially in a school

Etymology

From Latin 'studens', present participle of 'studere' (to study).
