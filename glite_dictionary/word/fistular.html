fistular

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪs.tjələr / (AmE), / ˈfɪs.tjʊ.lə / (BrE)
hollow and tube-shaped like a reed
hollow and tube-shaped; reedlike

Examples

The fistular structure of these stems allows for efficient water transport.
Some aquatic plants have a fistular morphology, aiding in buoyancy.
The scientist described the worm's body as fistular, resembling a slender reed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tubular - having the form of a tube

Etymology

From Latin fistula (reed, pipe) + -ar.
