oilstone

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔɪlˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɔɪl.stəʊn / (BrE)
A stone used with oil for sharpening the edges of cutting tools.
stone for sharpening tools (used with oil)

Examples

He used an oilstone to keep his kitchen knives razor sharp.
After years of use, the oilstone developed a smooth, even surface.
Sharpen your chisel on an oilstone before starting woodworking projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whetstone - a stone used for sharpening cutting tools
