libidinous

1. ADJ

/ lɪˈbɪdənəs / (AmE), / lɪˈbɪdɪnəs / (BrE)
having or showing excessive sexual desire; lustful.
having excessive sexual desire

Examples

The novelist was known for his libidinous descriptions of romance and desire.
During the scandal, many were shocked by his libidinous behavior at the party.
Some critics argued that the film was too libidinous for a mainstream audience.

Synset Examples

libidinous orgies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lustful - having or showing strong sexual desire
  • Antonym: chaste - abstaining from sexual activity, pure

Etymology

From Latin 'libidinosus' (full of desire), from 'libido' (desire).
