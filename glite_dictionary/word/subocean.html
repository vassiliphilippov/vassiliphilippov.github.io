subocean

1. ADJ

/ sʌbˈoʊʃən / (AmE), / sʌbˈəʊʃən / (BrE)
Located, formed, or occurring beneath the ocean or ocean bed.
situated beneath the ocean

Examples

Geologists studied the subocean rock layers to better understand tectonic movements.
New drilling technology allows access to subocean oil reserves.
The research vessel deployed sensors to monitor subocean earthquakes.

Synset Examples

suboceanic oil resources

Related Terms

  • Synonym: submarine - existing or occurring under the surface of the sea

Etymology

From Latin 'sub-' (under) + 'oceanus' (ocean).
