oddball

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑdˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈɒdˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a person who behaves in a strange or unconventional way
person with unusual behavior
informal

Examples

He was considered the oddball of the group, always coming up with bizarre ideas.
Her oddball sense of humor made her stand out at every party.
Despite being an oddball, she was well-liked for her originality and kindness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - a person with unconventional and slightly strange views or behavior
  • Synonym: weirdo - a person whose behavior seems strange or eccentric
  • Antonym: conformist - a person who conforms to accepted behavior or established practices

Etymology

Formed from 'odd' + 'ball', meaning someone odd like a stray ball; US slang from early 20th century.
