balanitis

1. NOUN

/ ˌbæləˈnaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌbaləˈnʌɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the head (glans) of the penis, usually caused by infection or irritation.
inflammation of the head of the penis
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with balanitis after presenting with redness and swelling.
Good hygiene is important to prevent balanitis in uncircumcised men.
Symptoms of balanitis may include itching, pain, and discharge.

Etymology

From Greek balanos (acorn, glans penis) + -itis (inflammation).
