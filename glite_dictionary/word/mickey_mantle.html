Mickey Mantle

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɪki ˈmæntl̩ / (AmE), / ˈmɪki ˈmæntl / (BrE)
(1931–1995) An American professional baseball player, regarded as one of the greatest hitters in Major League Baseball and a legendary center fielder for the New York Yankees.
American baseball player, Yankees legend
american

Examples

Growing up, my grandfather idolized Mickey Mantle and watched every Yankees game he played.
Sports fans still debate whether Mickey Mantle was the greatest switch hitter of all time.
A signed Mickey Mantle baseball card was recently sold for a record-breaking price.
