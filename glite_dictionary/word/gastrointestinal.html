gastrointestinal

Relating to the stomach and intestines.
of the stomach and intestines
medical

Examples

The patient was admitted to the hospital with a severe gastrointestinal infection.
Doctors suspect that gastrointestinal bleeding may be the cause of her symptoms.

Synset Examples

Etymology

From Greek 'gaster' (stomach) and Latin 'intestinum' (intestine).
