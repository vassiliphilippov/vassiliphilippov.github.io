Lescol

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛskoʊl / (AmE), / ˈlɛskɒl / (BrE)
A trademarked name for a statin drug (fluvastatin) used to lower cholesterol, typically taken orally at bedtime.
brand name statin drug (fluvastatin)

Examples

My doctor prescribed Lescol to help lower my cholesterol.
Patients taking Lescol are usually advised to do so at bedtime.
Generic fluvastatin may be used by those who cannot afford Lescol.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fluvastatin - the generic name for the drug sold as Lescol
