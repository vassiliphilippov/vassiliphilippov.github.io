wrong 'un

1. NOUN

/ rɔŋ ˈʌn / (AmE), / rɒŋ ˈʌn / (BrE)
(in cricket) a delivery bowled by a leg-spin bowler that appears to be a leg break but spins in the opposite direction, like a googly.
cricket ball spinning opposite to expected

Examples

The bowler surprised the batsman with a wrong 'un that clipped the off stump.
He struggled to pick the wrong 'un from the bowler's usual deliveries.
Commentators praised her skill in bowling a perfect wrong 'un during the final over.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: googly - a leg-spin delivery that spins in the opposite direction from a normal leg break

Etymology

Colloquial contraction of 'wrong one', referring to a ball that deceives the batsman by turning the 'wrong' way.
