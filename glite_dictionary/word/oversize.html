oversize

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊvərˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəsaɪz / (BrE)
larger than the usual or standard size for its type or kind
larger than normal size

Examples

The airline charged extra for my oversize luggage.
He wore an oversize jacket that nearly reached his knees.
The package is oversize and won't fit in the mailbox.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: undersize - smaller than the usual or standard size for its type
