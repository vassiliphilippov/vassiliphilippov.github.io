southland

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaʊθ.lænd / (AmE), / ˈsaʊθ.lənd / (BrE)
A region or area located in or toward the south, often used to describe the southern part of a country or territory.
region in the south

Examples

They dreamed of moving to the southland where the winters are mild and the sun always shines.
The birds migrate to the southland as the weather turns colder in the autumn.
Legends from the southland have shaped the culture of the entire nation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: northland - region located in or toward the north
