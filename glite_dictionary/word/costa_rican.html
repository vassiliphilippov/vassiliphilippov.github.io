Costa Rican

1. ADJ

/ ˌkoʊstə ˈriːkən / (AmE), / ˌkɒstə ˈriːkən / (BrE)
of or relating to Costa Rica or its people or culture
relating to Costa Rica or its people

Examples

The Costa Rican cuisine is known for its fresh ingredients and vibrant flavors.
Many Costa Rican artists have gained international recognition for their work.
The company imports coffee beans from Costa Rican farms.

Synset Examples

the Costa Rican rain forest
our Costa Rican neighbors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Costa Rica - a country in Central America

2. NOUN

/ ˌkoʊstə ˈriːkən / (AmE), / ˌkɒstə ˈriːkən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Costa Rica
person from Costa Rica

Examples

She met a Costa Rican on her trip to Central America.
Many Costa Ricans celebrate their Independence Day with parades and music.
The Costa Rican explained the traditions of his hometown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Costa Rican - of or relating to Costa Rica or its people (adjective)

Etymology

From Costa Rica, the name of the country, and English suffix -an indicating nationality.
