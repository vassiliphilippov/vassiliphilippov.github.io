sauciness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔːsinəs / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsɪnɪs / (BrE)
playful or disrespectful boldness, often regarded as slightly rude or cheeky
playful or cheeky boldness

Examples

Her sauciness often got her into trouble at school for talking back to teachers.
There was a certain sauciness in his grin that made everyone laugh.
The comedian's sauciness kept the audience entertained throughout the show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impudence - impertinent or disrespectful behavior
  • Synonym: cheekiness - behavior that is annoyingly bold or disrespectful
  • Antonym: politeness - behavior showing good manners and respect

Etymology

From 'saucy' + '-ness'; 'saucy' from Middle English, from 'sauce'.
