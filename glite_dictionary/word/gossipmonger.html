gossipmonger

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑːsɪpˌmʌŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈɡɒsɪpˌmʌŋɡə / (BrE)
a person who habitually spreads gossip and personal information about others
person who spreads gossip

Examples

The office gossipmonger couldn’t wait to share the latest scandal with everyone.
Being labeled a gossipmonger can hurt your reputation at work.
She avoided the neighborhood gossipmonger to keep her secrets safe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gossiper - a person who gossips; spreads rumors or private information
  • Synonym: rumormonger - a person who spreads rumors

Etymology

Formed from 'gossip' (from Old English 'godsibb', meaning godparent, later 'one who gossips') and 'monger' (dealer or trader, from Old English 'mangere').
