nonslippery

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈslɪpɚi / (AmE), / nɒnˈslɪpəri / (BrE)
Not slippery; providing a surface or condition that reduces the risk of slipping or skidding.
not slippery; safe from slipping

Examples

Make sure the bathroom floor is nonslippery to prevent accidents.
We bought nonslippery mats for the stairs for extra safety.
The walkway is paved with nonslippery tiles ideal for rainy weather.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: slippery - likely to cause slipping or sliding
