NSC

1. NOUN

/ ɛn ɛs si / (AmE), / ɛn ɛs si / (BrE)
an abbreviation for National Security Council, a committee in the executive branch of government that advises the president on foreign, military, and national security matters
abbreviation for National Security Council (US government committee)
american

Examples

The NSC met to discuss potential responses to the international crisis.
In times of national threat, the president often relies heavily on advice from the NSC.
The NSC plays a key role in shaping U.S. foreign policy decisions.

Etymology

Abbreviation of National Security Council (US).
