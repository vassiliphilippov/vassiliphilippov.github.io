surprising

1. ADJ

/ sərˈpraɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / səˈpraɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
causing someone to feel surprised; unexpected or astonishing
causing surprise; unexpected

Examples

The results of the election were surprising to many observers.
It's surprising how quickly children learn new languages.
Her surprising answer left everyone speechless.

Synset Examples

the report shows a surprising lack of hard factual data
leaped up with surprising agility
she earned a surprising amount of money

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unexpected - not expected; unforeseen
  • Antonym: predictable - able to be predicted; not surprising

Etymology

From the verb 'surprise', from Old French 'surprendre' (to overtake, surprise).
