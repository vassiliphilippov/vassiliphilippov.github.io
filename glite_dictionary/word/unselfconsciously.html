unselfconsciously

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnˌsɛlfˈkɑn.ʃəs.li / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌsɛlfˈkɒn.ʃəs.li / (BrE)
in a manner showing no self-consciousness; acting naturally and comfortably without concern for how one is perceived by others
in a natural, unconcerned manner

Examples

She laughed unselfconsciously, enjoying the moment without any embarrassment.
The children played unselfconsciously in the garden, unaware of anyone watching them.
He danced unselfconsciously, moving freely to the music with no concern for others' opinions.

Synset Examples

they were naked, unshy, and unselfconsciously beautiful

Related Terms

  • Antonym: self-consciously - in a way that is overly aware of how one is perceived by others
