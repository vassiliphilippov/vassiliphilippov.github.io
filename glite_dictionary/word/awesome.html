awesome

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔsəm / (AmE), / ˈɔːsəm / (BrE)
Extremely impressive or daunting; inspiring awe, admiration, or wonder.
inspiring awe or admiration

Examples

The fireworks display was truly awesome and left the crowd speechless.
Standing at the edge of the Grand Canyon, he marveled at the awesome view.
The film's awesome special effects amazed even the harshest critics.

Synset Examples

New York is an amazing city
the Grand Canyon is an awe-inspiring sight
the awesome complexity of the universe
this sea, whose gently awful stirrings seem to speak of some hidden soul beneath
Westminster Hall's awing majesty, so vast, so high, so silent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amazing - causing great surprise or wonder
  • Degree Lower: awful - causing fear or dread; originally inspiring awe

Etymology

From awe + –some; 'awe' from Old Norse 'agi' (fear, terror).
