enthusiastic

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪk / (AmE), / ɪnˌθjuːziˈæstɪk / (BrE)
showing or characterized by intense excitement or keen interest in something
showing great excitement or interest

Examples

The team received an enthusiastic round of applause after their performance.
She is always enthusiastic about trying new recipes in the kitchen.
His enthusiastic attitude inspired everyone to work harder on the project.

Synset Examples

enthusiastic crowds filled the streets
an enthusiastic response
was enthusiastic about taking ballet lessons

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passionate - having or showing strong emotions
  • Antonym: indifferent - having no particular interest or sympathy

Etymology

From Greek enthousiastikos, from enthousiasmos (inspiration, enthusiasm).
