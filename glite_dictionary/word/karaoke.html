karaoke

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɛriˈoʊki / (AmE), / ˌkæriˈəʊki / (BrE)
a form of entertainment in which people sing popular songs to recorded music, usually in bars or nightclubs
singing with recorded music as entertainment

Examples

We enjoyed a night of karaoke at the local bar with friends.
Her favorite activity is singing karaoke after work on Fridays.
They installed a karaoke machine in the living room for parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sing-along - group singing along with recorded or performed music

Etymology

Japanese, from 'kara' (empty) + 'oke' (orchestra, short for ōkesutora).
