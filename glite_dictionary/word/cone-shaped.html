cone-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈkoʊn ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈkəʊn ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of a cone; shaped like a cone.
resembling a cone in shape

Examples

The Christmas tree was decorated in a cone-shaped arrangement of lights.
They served ice cream in cone-shaped pastry shells at the party.
The ancient temple has a distinctive cone-shaped roof.

Synset Examples

conical mountains
conelike fruit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conical - having the shape of a cone
