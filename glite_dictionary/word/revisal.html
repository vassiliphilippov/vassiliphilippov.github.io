revisal

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈvaɪzəl / (AmE), / rɪˈvaɪzəl / (BrE)
the act of revising or rewriting something
act of revising or rewriting

Examples

The editor requested a revisal before the article could be published.
After several revisals, the proposal was finally approved by the committee.
Her thorough revisal of the report improved its clarity and accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revision - the process of revising or changing something

Etymology

From 'revise' + '-al', a noun-forming suffix, modeled on similar Latin-derived patterns.
