ill-defined

1. ADJ

/ ɪl dɪˈfaɪnd / (AmE), / ɪl dɪˈfaɪnd / (BrE)
not clearly or exactly stated, described, or determined; vague or unclear.
not clearly described or determined

Examples

The committee's goals were ill-defined, which led to confusion among the members.
The diagnosis remained ill-defined due to the lack of clear symptoms.
Many of the rules are ill-defined, making them difficult to enforce.

Synset Examples

he confuses the reader with ill-defined terms and concepts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vague - not clear or definite in meaning or intention
  • Antonym: well-defined - clearly stated or described
