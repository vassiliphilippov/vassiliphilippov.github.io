highflying

1. ADJ

/ ˈhaɪˌflaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌflaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
moving upward or at a considerable height above the ground
moving at a great height

Examples

The highflying eagle soared effortlessly over the valley.
Rescue teams struggled to reach the highflying balloon that had drifted out of sight.
The highflying jet left a trail of white vapor across the sky.

Synset Examples

some highflying fighter pilot fired a cannon shell into it

/ ˈhaɪˌflaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌflaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person, organization, or ambition) very successful, extravagant, or extremely ambitious.
very ambitious or successful

Examples

She was once a highflying executive in a major corporation.
Many highflying startups struggled during the economic downturn.
His highflying ambitions were admired by some and resented by others.

Synset Examples
