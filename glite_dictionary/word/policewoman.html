policewoman

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlisˌwʊmən / (AmE), / pəˈliːswʊmən / (BrE)
A female police officer; a woman who is a member of a police force.
female police officer

Examples

The policewoman directed traffic efficiently during the busy morning rush hour.
After years of service, the policewoman was promoted to detective.
A policewoman visited the school to talk about community safety.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: policeman - a male member of a police force
  • Synonym: police officer - a member of a police force, gender-neutral

Etymology

Formed from 'police' + 'woman', analogous to 'policeman'.
