Coumarouna

1. NOUN

/ kuːməˈruːnə / (AmE), / kuːməˈruːnə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical American trees, including species that yield tonka beans.
genus of tropical trees yielding tonka beans

Examples

The seeds of Coumarouna are widely known as tonka beans.
Botanists are studying several species within the Coumarouna genus for their aromatic properties.
Harvesting Coumarouna is important for local farmers in South America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tonka bean - the fragrant seed produced by some Coumarouna species

2. NOUN

/ kuːˌmɑːrəˈnuːnə / (AmE), / ˌkuːmərəˈnuːnə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical American trees in the legume family, including species that produce tonka beans.
genus of trees yielding tonka beans

Examples

Seeds from the Coumarouna tree are commonly known as tonka beans.
The wood of Coumarouna species is sometimes used in carpentry.
Botanists studied the Coumarouna genus for its aromatic properties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tonka bean - the fragrant seed produced by trees of this genus

Etymology

From New Latin 'Coumarouna', based on Tupi 'cumaru' (tonka bean).
