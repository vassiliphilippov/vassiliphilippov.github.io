electrologist

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person trained to remove hair roots, moles, or warts using electrical devices, especially for permanent hair removal
person skilled in electrical hair removal

Examples

After struggling with unwanted facial hair, Jenna decided to visit an electrologist for a consultation.
The electrologist explained the procedure and answered all my questions before starting the treatment.
Many people seek the expertise of an electrologist for long-term hair removal solutions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electrolysis - the process of using electricity to remove hair permanently

Etymology

From electro- (relating to electricity) + -logist (one who studies or practices).
