sold

1. ADJ

/ soʊld / (AmE), / səʊld / (BrE)
(of goods or property) having been purchased or no longer available for sale.
no longer available for sale; purchased

Examples

The house was sold within two days of being listed.
Sorry, these tickets are already sold.
Once the painting is sold, it cannot be returned.

Synset Examples

this merchandise is sold

Related Terms

  • Antonym: available - able to be bought or obtained; not yet sold
