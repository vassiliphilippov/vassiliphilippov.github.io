neurologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌnʊrəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the nervous system or to the scientific study of its diseases and functions (neurology).
relating to neurology or the nervous system
medical

Examples

The patient displayed several neurologic symptoms, including dizziness and muscle weakness.
A neurologic examination can help doctors determine the cause of seizures.
She specializes in neurologic research, focusing on diseases of the brain and spinal cord.

Synset Examples

neurological evidence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neurological - relating to the nervous system or neurology

Etymology

From neurology + -ic, following the pattern of scientific adjectives.
