Leadbelly

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɛdˌbɛli / (AmE), / ˈlɛdˌbɛli / (BrE)
The nickname of Huddie Ledbetter (1885–1949), a United States folk and blues singer and composer known for his powerful vocals and vast repertoire.
famous American folk and blues singer
american

Examples

Many of the songs popularized by Leadbelly have become standards in American folk music.
The influence of Leadbelly can be heard in countless blues and rock musicians.
A documentary about Leadbelly explores both his musical legacy and his turbulent life.

Etymology

Stage name of Huddie Ledbetter; 'Lead Belly' refers to his reputed strength and toughness.
