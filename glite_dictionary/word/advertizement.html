advertizement

1. NOUN

/ ˌædvɚˈtaɪzmənt / (AmE), / ˌædvəˈtaɪzmənt / (BrE)
A public notice, announcement, or display that promotes a product, service, event, or job opportunity.
public promotion or announcement

Examples

The company spent thousands on a full-page advertizement in the Sunday paper.
She saw an online advertizement for a new smartphone model.
They released a creative advertizement to attract younger customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertisement - A public promotion of some product or service (standard spelling)

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'advertisement', from advertise + -ment.
