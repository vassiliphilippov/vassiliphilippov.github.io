banner

1. NOUN

/ ˈbænər / (AmE), / ˈbænə / (BrE)
a newspaper headline that runs across the full width of a page, often used for important or dramatic stories
full-width newspaper headline

Examples

The front page featured a banner announcing the historic peace agreement.
Readers were drawn in by the striking banner at the top of the newspaper.
The editor decided to use a large banner for the major story of the day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headline - the main heading that appears at the top of a newspaper article
Source: WordNet

Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ ˈbænɚ / (AmE), / ˈbænə / (BrE)
a long strip of cloth or paper bearing a sign, slogan, or design, used for decoration or advertising, often displayed in a prominent place
long decorative or advertising sign

Examples

Volunteers held a large banner above the crowd during the demonstration.
The shop displayed a bright red banner to announce its grand opening.
Colorful banners decorated the streets for the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flag - piece of cloth with a symbol or design, used as a symbol of a country or institution

Etymology

From Old French 'baniere', from Late Latin 'bandum' (standard, flag).

3. NOUN

/ ˈbænər / (AmE), / ˈbænə / (BrE)
a long strip of cloth bearing a slogan, design, or symbol, typically used as a distinctive flag or standard for identification or decoration
a distinctive flag or cloth sign

Examples

The soldiers marched beneath the banner of their regiment.
A colorful banner hung over the main street during the festival.
He proudly waved his team’s banner after they won the championship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flag - a piece of cloth used as a symbol, signaling device, or decoration
  • Synonym: pennant - a long, narrow flag, often used for signaling or identification

Etymology

From Old French 'baniere', from Late Latin 'bandum' (standard, banner).

4. ADJ

/ ˈbænər / (AmE), / ˈbænə / (BrE)
Unusually good; outstanding or excellent.
exceptionally good; outstanding

Examples

Last year was a banner year for our sales department.
The festival enjoyed banner attendance, with record crowds every day.
This has been a banner season for the team, with many unexpected victories.

Synset Examples

a banner year for the company

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outstanding - Exceptionally good; standing out from others
  • Synonym: excellent - Extremely good; of the highest quality
  • Antonym: mediocre - Of only average quality; not very good
