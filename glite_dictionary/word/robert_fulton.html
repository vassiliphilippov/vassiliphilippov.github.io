Robert Fulton

1. PROPN

/ ˈroʊbərt ˈfʊltən / (AmE), / ˈrəʊbət ˈfʊltən / (BrE)
(1765–1815) An American inventor known for designing the first commercially successful steamboat and the first steam-powered warship.
American inventor; steamboat pioneer

Examples

Robert Fulton is credited with revolutionizing river transportation in the early 19th century.
The success of Robert Fulton's steamboat greatly impacted commerce and travel.
Many history books highlight Robert Fulton and his achievements in steam navigation.
