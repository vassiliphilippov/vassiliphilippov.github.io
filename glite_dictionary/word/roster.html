roster

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑː.stɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɒs.tə / (BrE)
a list of people's names, especially of those available for a particular job, team, or duty
list of names for a group or duty

Examples

The coach posted the new roster on the locker room door.
Our department maintains a roster of volunteers for weekend events.
The annual employee roster includes everyone hired by the company.

Synset Examples

his name was struck off the rolls

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roll - an official list or register of names

Etymology

From Dutch 'rooster' (table, list), from Middle Dutch, literally 'gridiron', referring to the pattern of a list.
