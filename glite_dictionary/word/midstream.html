midstream

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪdˌstriːm / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.striːm / (BrE)
the middle part of a stream or river
the middle of a stream or river

Examples

The boat was caught in midstream and struggled to make progress.
A swimmer paused in midstream to rest before heading for the shore.
The bridge crosses the river at midstream, offering a clear view of both banks.
