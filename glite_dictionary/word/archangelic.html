archangelic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑːrkeɪnˈdʒɛlɪk / (AmE), / ˌɑːkeɪnˈdʒɛlɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling archangels; of or characteristic of an archangel.
of or relating to archangels

Examples

The painting depicted an archangelic figure watching over the city.
Her demeanor had an archangelic quality, both powerful and serene.
The choir sang with an archangelic intensity that moved the congregation.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: angelic - of or relating to angels

Etymology

From 'archangel' + '-ic', from Greek 'archangelos' (chief angel).
