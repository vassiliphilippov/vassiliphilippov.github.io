isothermic

1. ADJ

/ ˌaɪsəˈθɜrmɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəˈθɜːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or indicating equal or constant temperature, especially as shown by an isotherm.
relating to equal or constant temperature

Examples

The weather map displayed several isothermic lines across the continent.
In an isothermic process, the temperature remains constant throughout the reaction.
Scientists study isothermic curves to analyze changes in atmospheric temperature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: isothermal - having equal or constant temperature
  • Synonym: isotherm - a line on a map connecting points of equal temperature

Etymology

From Greek 'isos' (equal) and 'thermē' (heat).
