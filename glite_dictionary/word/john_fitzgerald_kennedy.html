John Fitzgerald Kennedy

1. PROPN

/ dʒɑn fɪt͡sˈdʒɛrəld ˈkɛnədi / (AmE), / dʒɒn fɪt͡sˈdʒɛrəld ˈkɛnədi / (BrE)
(1917–1963) The 35th President of the United States, known for initiating the Peace Corps and being assassinated in Dallas.
