ungodly

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɡɑdli / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡɒdli / (BrE)
morally wrong or wicked, especially regarded as sinful or irreligious
wicked, sinful, or irreligious

Examples

They were shocked by his ungodly behavior at the meeting.
The novel describes the ungodly actions of a corrupt regime.
She considered gambling an ungodly pastime.

Synset Examples

iniquitous deeds
he said it was sinful to wear lipstick
ungodly acts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wicked - morally wrong; evil
  • Synonym: sinful - full of sin; wicked
  • Antonym: godly - devout and religious; pious

Etymology

From un- + godly, meaning 'not godly'.
