journeyer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɝːniɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːniə / (BrE)
A person who travels from one place to another, especially on a journey or trip.
person traveling on a journey

Examples

The journeyer paused to rest beside the river after a long day of travel.
Each journeyer brought back a different story from their adventures abroad.
The old novel describes the trials of a lone journeyer crossing the mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traveler - someone who travels from place to place

Etymology

Formed from 'journey' + '-er', where 'journey' derives from Old French 'jornee' meaning 'a day's travel'.
