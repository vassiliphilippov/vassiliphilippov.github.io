Bartlesville

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɑːrtəlzˌvɪl / (AmE), / ˈbɑːtəlzˌvɪl / (BrE)
A city in northeastern Oklahoma, known for its history in the oil industry.
city in Oklahoma, USA

Examples

The headquarters of Phillips Petroleum were once located in Bartlesville.
During our road trip through Oklahoma, we spent a night in Bartlesville.
Bartlesville is home to several historic buildings and an annual music festival.

Etymology

Named after Jacob Bartles, a prominent local businessman in the 19th century.
