ovaritis

1. NOUN

/ oʊ.vəˈraɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / əʊ.vəˈraɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the ovaries.
inflammation of the ovaries
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with ovaritis after experiencing severe pelvic pain.
Antibiotics are often prescribed to treat ovaritis.
Untreated ovaritis can sometimes lead to complications affecting fertility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oophoritis - inflammation of an ovary or both ovaries

Etymology

From ovary + -itis (Greek, denoting inflammation).
