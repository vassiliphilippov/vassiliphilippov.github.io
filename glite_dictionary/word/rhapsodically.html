rhapsodically

1. ADV

/ ræpˈsɒdɪkli / (AmE), / ræpˈsɒdɪkli / (BrE)
In an ecstatic, enthusiastic, or highly emotional manner.
in an ecstatic or enthusiastic manner

Examples

She spoke rhapsodically about her experiences traveling the world.
The audience cheered rhapsodically after the final performance.
He described the sunset rhapsodically, unable to hide his excitement.

Synset Examples

he reacted ecstatically to my plan to travel to Africa

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecstatically - in a state of ecstasy or great delight
  • Antonym: calmly - in a relaxed or tranquil manner

Etymology

From rhapsodic (from Greek rhapsōidikos) + -ally.
