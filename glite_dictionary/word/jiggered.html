jiggered

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɪɡɚd / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪɡəd / (BrE)
(British, informal) Used to express surprise or astonishment.
(Brit. informal) surprised or astonished
informal british

Examples

Jiggered if I know what he's talking about!
Well, I'll be jiggered, I never expected them to win.
She was completely jiggered when she saw the surprise party.

Synset Examples
