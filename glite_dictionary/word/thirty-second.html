thirty-second

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɜːrti ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈθɜːti ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
Ordinal number coming after thirty-first and before thirty-third, representing the position of 32nd in a sequence.
ordinal number; 32nd in order

Examples

She finished in thirty-second place in the marathon.
This is the thirty-second chapter of the book, right before the conclusion.
They celebrated their thirty-second wedding anniversary last month.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirty-first - ordinal number; 31st in order
  • Degree Higher: thirty-third - ordinal number; 33rd in order

2. NOUN

/ ˌθɜrtiˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˌθɜːtiˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
Each of the thirty-two equal parts into which something can be divided; one thirty-second part.
one of thirty-two equal parts

Examples

The mechanic needed a thirty-second of an inch adjustment to align the parts.
The recipe required a thirty-second of a teaspoon of spice for the right flavor.
Only a thirty-second of the original painting remains after so many restorations.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sixty-fourth - one part in sixty-four equal parts
  • Degree Higher: sixteenth - one part in sixteen equal parts
