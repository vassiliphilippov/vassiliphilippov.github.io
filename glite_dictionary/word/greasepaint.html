greasepaint

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrisˌpeɪnt / (AmE), / ˈɡriːsˌpeɪnt / (BrE)
a thick, oily substance used as makeup by actors, especially in the theater
theatrical makeup for actors

Examples

The actor applied greasepaint before going on stage.
It's difficult to remove greasepaint after a long performance under the lights.
She bought a new set of greasepaint for her role in the play.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: makeup - cosmetic substances applied to the face or body
