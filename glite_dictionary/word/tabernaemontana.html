Tabernaemontana

1. NOUN

/ təˌbɝːniˌmɑnˈteɪnə / (AmE), / təˌbɜːnɪˌmɒnˈteɪnə / (BrE)
A genus of evergreen tropical trees and shrubs with milky sap, belonging to the dogbane family (Apocynaceae).
genus of tropical evergreen trees and shrubs

Examples

The fragrant flowers of Tabernaemontana are popular in tropical gardens.
Several species of Tabernaemontana are prized for their ornamental leaves and blooms.
Botanists discovered a new species in the Tabernaemontana genus in Southeast Asia.

Etymology

Named after Jakob Theodor von Bergzabern (Tabernaemontanus), a German botanist.
