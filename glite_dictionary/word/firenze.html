Firenze

1. PROPN

/ fiˈrɛntsɛ / (AmE), / fiˈrɛntse / (BrE)
the Italian name for Florence, a city in central Italy on the Arno River, known as the center of the Renaissance and the capital of Tuscany
Italian name for Florence, Italy

Examples

After studying art history, Julia dreamed of visiting Firenze to see Michelangelo’s masterpieces.
The Uffizi Gallery is one of the main attractions in Firenze.
Many people refer to Florence as Firenze, its original Italian name, especially in travel guides.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Florence - capital city of Tuscany, Italy, known for Renaissance art and architecture

Etymology

Italian form of Latin 'Florentia', the city's historical name.
