tranquilly

1. ADV

/ ˈtræŋ.kwɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈtræŋ.kwɪ.li / (BrE)
In a calm and peaceful manner; without disturbance or emotional agitation.
in a calm and peaceful manner

Examples

She sat tranquilly by the lake, listening to the gentle sounds of nature.
Despite the chaos around him, he continued to work tranquilly.
After the storm passed, the village returned to its usual, tranquilly pace.

Synset Examples

tranquilly she went on with her work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calmly - in a calm manner
  • Synonym: peacefully - in a peaceful manner
  • Antonym: nervously - in an anxious or agitated manner

Etymology

From the adjective 'tranquil' (Latin tranquillus, meaning calm, quiet) + adverbial suffix -ly.
