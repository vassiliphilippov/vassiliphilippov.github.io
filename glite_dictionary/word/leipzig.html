Leipzig

1. PROPN

/ ˈlaɪpsɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪpsɪɡ / (BrE)
a city in eastern Germany, known for its long history as a center of trade fairs, music, and publishing
city in eastern Germany, cultural and trade center

Examples

The Bach Museum in Leipzig attracts classical music enthusiasts from around the world.
This year, the international trade fair took place in Leipzig as usual.
She spent a semester studying German language and culture in Leipzig.

Etymology

From German Leipzig, after the Slavic name Lipsk, meaning 'settlement by the lime trees'.
