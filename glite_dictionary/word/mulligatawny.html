mulligatawny

1. NOUN

/ ˌmʌlɪɡəˈtɔni / (AmE), / ˌmʌlɪɡəˈtɔːni / (BrE)
A soup originally from India, typically made with meat or chicken and flavored with curry and other spices.
spicy Indian soup, often with meat

Examples

We ordered mulligatawny as a starter at the Indian restaurant last night.
Her homemade mulligatawny is rich with spices and tender chicken pieces.
The chef added extra curry powder to the mulligatawny for an intense flavor.

Etymology

From Tamil *milagu-tanni* (pepper-water).
