proctology

1. NOUN

/ prɑkˈtɑlədʒi / (AmE), / prɒkˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the colon, rectum, and anus
medical specialty for colon, rectum, and anus
medical

Examples

He decided to specialize in proctology after developing an interest in digestive health.
The patient was referred to the hospital’s proctology department for further examination.
Advances in proctology have greatly improved treatments for colorectal diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gastroenterology - the branch of medicine focused on the digestive system and its disorders

Etymology

From Greek proktos (anus) + -logy (study).
