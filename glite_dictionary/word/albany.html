Albany

1. PROPN

/ ˈɔl.bə.ni / (AmE), / ˈɔːl.bə.ni / (BrE)
The capital city of the state of New York, situated on the west bank of the Hudson River.
capital city of New York State

Examples

She was born and raised in Albany, where her family still lives.
The state government of New York is headquartered in Albany.
On their road trip, they visited Albany to see the historic capitol building.

Etymology

Named after the Duke of Albany, a title in the Scottish peerage.
