soft-haired

1. ADJ

/ sɔft hɛrd / (AmE), / sɒft heəd / (BrE)
having hair that is soft to the touch
having soft hair

Examples

The soft-haired puppy curled up in my lap and fell asleep.
She bred a new line of soft-haired rabbits for showing at the fair.
His soft-haired arms reminded her of velvet as she held his hand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silky - having a smooth, soft texture like silk
  • Antonym: coarse-haired - having hair that is rough or not soft to the touch
