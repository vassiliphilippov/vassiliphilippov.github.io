vendition

1. NOUN

/ vɛnˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / vɛnˈdɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the act of selling goods, especially as a commercial activity or occupation
the act of selling goods

Examples

The merchant made his livelihood through the vendition of imported fabrics.
Their license permitted the vendition of agricultural products at the local market.
He built his fortune on the vendition of rare jewels and antiques.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sale - exchange of goods or services for money
  • Antonym: purchase - the act of buying something

Etymology

From Latin venditio (a selling), from vendere (to sell).
