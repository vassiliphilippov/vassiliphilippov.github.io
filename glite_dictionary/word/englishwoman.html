Englishwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who is a native or inhabitant of England.
woman from England

Examples

She is an accomplished Englishwoman who has represented her country in international sports.
Many tourists are fascinated by the stories of this famous Englishwoman from the Victorian era.
As an Englishwoman living abroad, she often misses traditional English tea.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Englishman - A man who is a native or inhabitant of England

Etymology

Formed from English + woman, denoting gender and national origin.
