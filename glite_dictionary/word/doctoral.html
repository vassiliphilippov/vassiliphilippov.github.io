doctoral

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɑktɚəl / (AmE), / ˈdɒktərəl / (BrE)
Relating to a doctorate or to a person who is pursuing or has received a doctorate.
relating to a doctorate degree

Examples

She is currently writing her doctoral thesis in chemistry.
The university offers several doctoral programs in engineering and science.
He was awarded a doctoral fellowship to conduct his research abroad.

Synset Examples

doctoral dissertation
doctorial candidates

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doctorate - a university degree of the highest level

Etymology

From Medieval Latin doctoralis, from doctor (teacher).
