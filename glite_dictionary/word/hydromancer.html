hydromancer

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪdrəˌmænsər / (AmE), / ˈhʌɪdrəˌmansə / (BrE)
a person who practices hydromancy, a form of divination involving water
practitioner of water-based divination

Examples

The hydromancer gazed into the rippling pool to predict the year's harvest.
Legends speak of a powerful hydromancer who could control the tides.
As a renowned hydromancer, she was consulted before every major voyage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hydromancy - divination by water or other liquids

Etymology

From Greek 'hydro-' (water) + 'manteia' (divination).
