dreamlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈdriːmˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdriːmˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a quality or atmosphere that is strange or unreal, similar to a dream
resembling a dream; surreal or unreal

Examples

The misty landscape had a dreamlike quality that made it feel otherworldly.
Her memories of childhood often seem vague and dreamlike to her now.
The movie creates a dreamlike atmosphere through its use of soft lighting and slow motion.

Synset Examples

night invested the lake with a dreamlike quality
as irrational and surreal as a dream

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surreal - having the quality of being bizarre or dreamlike
  • Antonym: realistic - having a quality true to real life, not dreamlike
