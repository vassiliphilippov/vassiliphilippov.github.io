unprocurable

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.prəˈkjʊr.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌn.prəˈkjʊə.rə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be obtained or acquired; impossible to procure.
not able to be obtained

Examples

With demand skyrocketing, the medication quickly became unprocurable in local pharmacies.
Rare antique coins like this one are now almost unprocurable at any price.
The document was deemed lost and considered unprocurable by historians.

Synset Examples

a rare work, today almost inaccessible
timber is virtually unobtainable in the islands
untouchable resources buried deep within the earth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unattainable - not able to be reached or achieved
  • Antonym: obtainable - able to be acquired or procured

Etymology

From un- (not) + procurable (from Latin procūrāre 'to take care of').
