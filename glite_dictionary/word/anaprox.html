Anaprox

1. NOUN

/ ˈænəˌprɑks / (AmE), / ˈænəˌprɒks / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for naproxen sodium, a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used to relieve pain and reduce inflammation.
trademarked pain-relief medication

Examples

My doctor prescribed Anaprox to help with my arthritis pain.
After spraining her ankle, she took Anaprox for the swelling and discomfort.
Some people experience fewer stomach issues when taking Anaprox compared to other painkillers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naproxen - a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used to relieve pain and inflammation
