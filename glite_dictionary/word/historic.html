historic

1. ADJ

/ hɪˈstɔrɪk / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒrɪk / (BrE)
famous or important in history, or having great and lasting significance.
famous or important in history

Examples

The city hosted several historic meetings that changed the course of world politics.
Visiting the historic castle helped the students appreciate their country’s heritage.
That was a historic moment for the team when they won the championship.

Synset Examples

historic victories
historical (or historic) times
a historical character

Related Terms

  • Synonym: historical - relating to the past or to history, especially as opposed to 'historic'

Etymology

From Latin 'historicus' (historical) via Old French 'historique'.

2. ADJ

/ hɪˈstɔrɪk / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒrɪk / (BrE)
famous or important in history, or likely to be remembered as significant
important in history

Examples

The signing of the peace treaty was a historic event that changed the course of the nation.
She still has the historic medal her grandfather won in World War II.
This building is considered historic because it witnessed several key moments in our city's past.

Synset Examples

the historic first voyage to outer space

Related Terms

  • Synonym: historical - relating to history or past events, but not necessarily important

Etymology

From Greek historikos (historical), via Latin historicus.
