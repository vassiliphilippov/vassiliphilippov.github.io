hygienically

1. ADV

/ haɪˈdʒiːnɪkli / (AmE), / haɪˈdʒiːnɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that promotes or maintains cleanliness and good health.
in a clean and healthy way

Examples

All medical instruments must be handled hygienically to prevent infections.
The kitchen was cleaned hygienically after every meal.
Food should be prepared hygienically to ensure it is safe for consumption.

Synset Examples

the body must be cared for hygienically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hygienic - promoting health or cleanliness
  • Antonym: unsanitarily - in an unclean or unhealthy way

Etymology

From "hygienic" + "-ally" (adverbial suffix), related to French "hygiénique" and ultimately from Greek "hygieinos" (healthful).
