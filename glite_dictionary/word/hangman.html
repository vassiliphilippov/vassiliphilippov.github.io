hangman

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæŋˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈhæŋmən / (BrE)
a man whose job is to execute condemned people by hanging
man who executes by hanging

Examples

The infamous hangman carried out dozens of executions during his career.
Crowds would gather to watch the hangman perform his grim duty at dawn.
The last official hangman in England became a reluctant celebrity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: executioner - person who carries out a death sentence

Etymology

From Middle English hangman, literally 'man who hangs', from hang + man.
