guib

1. NOUN

/ ɡiːb / (AmE), / ɡiːb / (BrE)
an antelope found in Africa, noted for its twisted horns and white markings resembling a harness
African antelope with twisted horns

Examples

The guib is easily recognized by its striking white bands across its body.
Wildlife photographers often seek out the guib for its unique twisted horns.
Several guibs were seen grazing near the river at dawn.
