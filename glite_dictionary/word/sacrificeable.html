sacrificeable

1. ADJ

/ ˈsækrɪˌfaɪs(ə)bəl / (AmE), / ˈsakrɪˌfʌɪsəbəl / (BrE)
able to be sacrificed; capable of being deliberately given up or destroyed to achieve a goal
able to be sacrificed

Examples

In military strategy, supplies considered sacrificeable are often left behind to speed up a retreat.
The king viewed the outpost as sacrificeable if it meant securing the capital.
Some data is marked as sacrificeable in the event of a system breach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dispensable - able to be done without; not essential

Etymology

From 'sacrifice' + '-able'.
