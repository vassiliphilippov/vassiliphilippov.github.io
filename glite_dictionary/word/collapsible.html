collapsible

1. ADJ

/ kəˈlæpsəbəl / (AmE), / kəˈlæpsəbl / (BrE)
able to be folded or collapsed into a smaller form, typically for storage or transport.
able to be folded down

Examples

The picnic table is collapsible and fits easily in the trunk of a car.
She bought a collapsible water bottle for hiking trips.
A collapsible tripod is handy for photographers who travel often.

Synset Examples

a collapsible boat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foldable - able to be folded

Etymology

From collapse + -ible, modeled after Latin forms indicating ability.
