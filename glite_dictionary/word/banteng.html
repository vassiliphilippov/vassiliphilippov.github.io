banteng

1. NOUN

/ ˈbænˌtɛŋ / (AmE), / ˈbæn.teŋ / (BrE)
a wild ox native to Southeast Asia, especially the Malay Archipelago
wild ox of Southeast Asia

Examples

The banteng is listed as endangered due to habitat loss and hunting.
A herd of bantengs was seen grazing in the forest reserve.
Unlike domestic cattle, the banteng is rarely encountered in captivity.

Etymology

From Malay 'banteng' (wild ox).
