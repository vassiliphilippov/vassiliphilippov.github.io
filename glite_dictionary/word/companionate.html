companionate

1. ADJ

/ kəmˈpænjənət / (AmE), / kəmˈpænjənət / (BrE)
characterized by companionship or friendship, especially describing relationships based on mutual affection and equality rather than passion or duty.
characterized by companionship or friendship

Examples

They preferred a companionate marriage, valuing friendship as much as romance.
The dog was known for its companionate nature and loyalty to its owners.
They shared a companionate bond that was evident to all their friends.

Synset Examples

companionate marriage
a companionate dog

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friendly - kind and pleasant; disposed to companionship
  • Degree Higher: loving - feeling or showing love or affection

Etymology

From 'companion' + '-ate' (adjectival suffix).
