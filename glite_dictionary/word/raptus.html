raptus

1. NOUN

/ ˈræp.təs / (AmE), / ˈ ræp.təs / (BrE)
a state of being carried away by overwhelming emotion or ecstasy; a condition of intense emotional absorption
a state of emotional ecstasy
archaic

Examples

She spoke of her experience in a sudden raptus of joy.
The painting was inspired by a raptus that overtook the artist.
In the poem, the lovers are described as being seized by raptus.

Synset Examples

listening to sweet music in a perfect rapture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecstasy - an overwhelming feeling of great happiness or joyful excitement
  • Synonym: rapture - a feeling of intense pleasure or joy

Etymology

From Latin *raptus* (a carrying off, a seizure, ecstasy), from *rapere* (to seize).
