sociocultural

1. ADJ

/ ˌsoʊsi.oʊˈkʌl.tʃɚ.əl / (AmE), / ˌsəʊ.si.əʊˈkʌl.tʃər.əl / (BrE)
relating to the interaction between social and cultural factors or aspects.
concerning social and cultural factors

Examples

The company's policies address a wide range of sociocultural issues in the workplace.
Researchers are examining the sociocultural impact of technology on young people.
His novel explores sociocultural changes in rural communities over the past century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cultural - relating to culture or cultivation
  • Synonym: social - relating to society or its organization

Etymology

From Latin 'socius' (companion, associate) + 'cultural,' from Latin 'cultura' (cultivation).
