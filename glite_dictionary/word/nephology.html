nephology

1. NOUN

/ nəˈfɑlədʒi / (AmE), / nɪˈfɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of meteorology that deals with the study of clouds and cloud formation
the study of clouds in meteorology
medical

Examples

He specialized in nephology, focusing on how different clouds affected local weather patterns.
Advances in nephology have improved our ability to predict rain and storms.
The conference on nephology attracted meteorologists from around the world.

Etymology

From Greek nephos (cloud) + -logy (study of).
