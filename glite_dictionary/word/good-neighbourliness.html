good-neighbourliness

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈneɪbɚliːnəs / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈneɪbəlɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being friendly, helpful, and considerate towards one's neighbors
friendliness and helpfulness toward neighbors

Examples

Her good-neighbourliness made the new residents feel instantly welcome in the community.
The festival promoted a spirit of good-neighbourliness among people from different backgrounds.
The city council praised his good-neighbourliness after he helped several seniors during the snowstorm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neighborliness - the quality of being friendly and helpful toward one's neighbors
