metrology

1. NOUN

/ məˈtrɑlədʒi / (AmE), / mɪˈtrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The scientific study of measurement, including the development of protocols and systems for precise and accurate measurement in science and industry.
scientific study of measurement
medical

Examples

Advances in metrology have improved the accuracy of scientific experiments.
The company hired a specialist in metrology to ensure quality control in production.
Understanding metrology is essential for anyone working with precision instruments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: measurement - the process of measuring something

Etymology

From French métrologie, from Greek metron (measure) + -logia (study, science).
