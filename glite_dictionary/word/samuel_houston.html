Samuel Houston

1. PROPN

/ ˈsæmjuəl ˈhjuːstən / (AmE), / ˈsæmjuːəl ˈhjuːstən / (BrE)
(1793–1863) United States politician and military leader known for his role in securing Texas's independence from Mexico and for making Texas part of the United States.
U.S. leader in Texas independence, 1793–1863

Examples

Samuel Houston was a key figure in the history of Texas's independence from Mexico.
Many schools and cities in Texas are named after Samuel Houston.
Samuel Houston served as the first president of the Republic of Texas.
