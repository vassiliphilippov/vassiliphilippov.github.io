eyeshot

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈaɪʃɒt / (BrE)
the range or distance over which one can see; the field of vision
the visible range; field of vision

Examples

The boat disappeared from eyeshot as it sailed behind the island.
She tried to stay within eyeshot of her hiking partner at all times.
Once out of eyeshot, the children began their secret game.

Synset Examples

they were soon out of view

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sight - the ability to see or the range of vision
  • Synonym: view - the range seen from a particular point

Etymology

Formed from 'eye' + 'shot', meaning the distance something can be seen.
