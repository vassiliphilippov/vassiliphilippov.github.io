hottish

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑt̬ɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɒtɪʃ / (BrE)
Somewhat hot; slightly hot or warm.
somewhat hot

Examples

The soup was hottish, but not too warm to eat right away.
It was a hottish afternoon, so we decided to stay indoors.
She prefers her coffee hottish rather than piping hot.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: hot - having a high degree of heat or temperature
  • Degree Lower: warm - having or producing a moderate degree of heat
