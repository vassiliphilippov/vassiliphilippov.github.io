fling

1. NOUN

/ flɪŋ / (AmE), / flɪŋ / (BrE)
an act of throwing or casting something forcefully
act of throwing forcefully

Examples

With a sudden fling, he hurled the ball across the field.
She gave the letter a quick fling into the wastebasket.
The wrestler sent his opponent flying with a powerful fling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toss - an act of throwing something lightly or carelessly
  • Synonym: throw - an act of propelling something with force through the air

Etymology

From Old Norse *flengja* (to flog, strike), related to Old Icelandic *flengja*.
