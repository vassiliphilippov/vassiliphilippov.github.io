reiteration

1. NOUN

/ ˌriː.ɪˈteɪ.ɾɚˌeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌriː.ɪˈteə.rə.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of repeating something again, often for emphasis or clarity; an instance of repetition
the act of repeating something

Examples

The manager's constant reiteration of the rules made everyone pay attention.
Her reiteration of the main point helped clarify the argument for the audience.
Despite the reiteration of warnings, some employees still ignored the safety procedures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repetition - the action of doing or saying something again
  • Synonym: iteration - the repetition of a process or utterance

Etymology

From Latin 'reiteratio', from 'reiterare' (to repeat).
