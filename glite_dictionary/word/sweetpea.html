sweetpea

1. NOUN

/ ˈswitˌpi / (AmE), / ˈswiːtˌpiː / (BrE)
a climbing garden plant with fragrant pastel-colored flowers, widely grown for ornament; usually referring to Lathyrus odoratus.
climbing garden plant with fragrant flowers

Examples

The sweetpea added a splash of color and fragrance to her garden.
He planted several sweetpeas along the fence to climb and bloom in spring.
Cutting fresh sweetpea blossoms brightened up the table during summer.
