cupful

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌpˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈkʌp.fʊl / (BrE)
An amount that fills a cup; the quantity a cup can hold, used as a standard measure.
the quantity a cup can hold

Examples

Add one cupful of flour to the mixing bowl and stir thoroughly.
She poured a cupful of tea and handed it to her guest.
The recipe calls for a cupful of chopped nuts for extra flavor.

Synset Examples

he drank a cup of coffee
he borrowed a cup of sugar

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teaspoonful - the amount a teaspoon can hold
  • Synonym: tablespoonful - the amount a tablespoon can hold
