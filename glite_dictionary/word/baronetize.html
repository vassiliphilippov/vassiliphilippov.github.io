to baronetize

1. VERB

/ ˈbærənɪˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈbærənɪˌtaɪz / (BrE)
to confer the title of baronet on someone
confer title of baronet

Examples

In recognition of his achievements, the king decided to baronetize the distinguished scientist.
The parliament voted to baronetize several war heroes after the conflict ended.
He was baronetized by the monarch as a reward for his loyalty.

Synset Examples

He was baronetized for his loyalty to the country

Etymology

From 'baronet' (a hereditary title), with the verb-forming suffix '-ize'.
