airline

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈeəˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a flexible hose or tube used to convey air under pressure, typically in pneumatic systems or aquariums
tube for carrying pressurized air

Examples

The aquarium pump is connected to the filter by a clear airline.
Be sure to check each airline for leaks before operating the machinery.
He replaced the damaged airline to restore proper airflow to the compressor.

2. NOUN

/ ˈer.laɪn / (AmE), / ˈeə.laɪn / (BrE)
A company that provides regular flights carrying passengers or goods by aircraft.
company providing scheduled flights

Examples

The national airline announced new routes to several international destinations.
After the delay, the airline provided meal vouchers to all passengers.
Many people prefer to fly with a budget airline to save money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carrier - an organization that transports goods or passengers
  • Antonym: railway - a company operating trains for passengers or freight

Etymology

From 'air' + 'line', referring to a line or company operating air routes.
