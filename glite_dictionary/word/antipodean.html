antipodean

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.təˈpoʊ.di.ən / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪˈpəʊ.di.ən / (BrE)
relating to the antipodes, or situated on opposite sides of the Earth; sometimes used to refer to Australia and New Zealand.
relating to opposite sides of the Earth

Examples

Scientists studied the antipodean regions to understand climate variations across the globe.
He marveled at the idea of drilling through the earth to reach an antipodean point.
In literature, Australia is sometimes described as an antipodean land, far removed from Europe.

Synset Examples

antipodean latitudes
antipodal regions of the earth
antipodal points on a sphere

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antipodal - relating to or situated at the opposite side of the Earth

Etymology

From Greek 'antipodes' (those with the feet opposite), via Latin 'antipoda' + English suffix '-an'.
