global

1. ADJ

/ ˈgloʊ.bəl / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊ.bəl / (BrE)
Relating to or involving the whole world; not limited to a particular area or group.
relating to the whole world

Examples

The company launched a global marketing campaign to reach customers in every country.
Climate change is a serious global issue that requires international cooperation.
Their research focuses on global economic trends rather than local markets.

Synset Examples

global war
global monetary policy
neither national nor continental but planetary
a world crisis
of worldwide significance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worldwide - extending or reaching throughout the world
  • Antonym: local - relating to a particular area or region

Etymology

From Latin globalis, from globus ‘sphere’.
