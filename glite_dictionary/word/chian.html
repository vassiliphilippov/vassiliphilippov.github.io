Chian

1. ADJ

/ ˈkaɪ.ən / (AmE), / ˈkiː.ən / (BrE)
relating to or originating from the Greek island of Chios.
of or from Chios, a Greek island

Examples

The ancient Chian wine was famous throughout the Mediterranean.
She studied Chian architecture as part of her research on Greek islands.
A group of Chian merchants settled in the port city centuries ago.

Etymology

From Chios, the name of the Greek island, with the adjectival suffix -an.
