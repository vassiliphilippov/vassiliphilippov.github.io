ta

1. INTJ

/ tɑ / (AmE), / tɑː / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly British) An informal way of saying 'thank you.'
informal thank you (British)
informal british

Examples

She smiled at the driver and said ta as she got off the bus.
Just a quick ta for helping me with my homework yesterday!
He handed her the parcel and she replied, "ta very much."

Synset Examples

He waved and said ta before leaving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thanks - expression of gratitude
  • Synonym: cheers - (British informal) used to mean 'thank you' or 'goodbye'
  • Synonym: thank you - polite expression of gratitude

Etymology

Probably from baby talk in English, imitating 'thank you.' Common in British English since the mid-19th century.
