weapons-grade

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛpənzˌɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɛpənzˌɡreɪd / (BrE)
(informal) Extremely strong, potent, or concentrated; of a level or intensity comparable to that required for making weapons.
extremely strong or potent (informal)
informal

Examples

That was some weapons-grade coffee—you could smell it down the hall.
Be careful with this weapons-grade glue; it bonds instantly.
The comedian delivered a weapons-grade roast that left everyone in stitches.

Synset Examples

industrial-strength detergent
weapons-grade salsa

Related Terms

  • Synonym: industrial-strength - extremely strong, concentrated, or powerful; usu. not for home use

Etymology

From 'weapons' + 'grade', originally describing material pure enough for use in weapons, especially nuclear weapons.
