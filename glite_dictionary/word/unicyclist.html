unicyclist

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuːnɪˌsaɪklɪst / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪˌsaɪklɪst / (BrE)
a person who rides or performs on a unicycle
a person who rides a unicycle

Examples

The unicyclist amazed the crowd with his impressive balancing acts.
My cousin is an experienced unicyclist who performs in local parades.
During the festival, a unicyclist rode past us juggling three balls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cyclist - someone who rides a bicycle or similar vehicle

Etymology

From unicycle + -ist (person engaged in an activity).
