165th

1. ADJ

/ wʌn hʌndrəd sɪksti fɪfθ / (AmE), / wʌn hʌndrəd sɪksti fɪfθ / (BrE)
being the ordinal number corresponding to one hundred and sixty-five; in position 165 in a sequence.
ordinal number for 165th place

Examples

She finished in 165th place in the marathon, but was proud of her achievement.
This is the 165th anniversary of the school's founding.
My seat was in the 165th row, so I couldn't see the stage very well.
