namelessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪmləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈneɪmləsnəs / (BrE)
the state or condition of having no name or of being anonymous
state of having no name

Examples

The author's namelessness protected her from unwanted attention.
He felt a sense of namelessness in the crowded city, lost among strangers.
Throughout history, the namelessness of certain artists has puzzled scholars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anonymity - the condition of being anonymous

Etymology

From 'nameless' + '-ness', forming a noun indicating the state or quality.
