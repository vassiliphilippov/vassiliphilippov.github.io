third-dimensional

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɜrd dɪˈmɛnʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌθɜːd dɪˈmɛnʃənl / (BrE)
involving or relating to three dimensions or aspects, especially having depth or giving the illusion of depth
relating to three dimensions or depth

Examples

The artist used light and shadow to make the scene appear truly third-dimensional.
Modern video games often feature third-dimensional graphics that simulate depth and realism.
The new display technology creates a third-dimensional effect, making images seem to pop out of the screen.
