regaining

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈɡeɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈɡeɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of getting something back after it has been lost or given away
the act of getting back something lost

Examples

The athlete's regaining of his championship title inspired many fans.
After years of dispute, the museum celebrated the regaining of its stolen artworks.
She focused on the regaining of her confidence through therapy and hard work.

Synset Examples

upon the restitution of the book to its rightful owner the child was given a tongue lashing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recovery - the process of becoming well again after an illness or loss
  • Antonym: loss - the act or process of losing something

Etymology

From the verb 'regain' + -ing, ultimately from Old French 'rega(i)gner'.
