calendrical

1. ADJ

/ kəˈlɛn.drɪ.kəl / (AmE), / kəˈlɛn.drɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a calendar or the measurement of time
relating to a calendar or time measurement

Examples

The festival has great calendrical significance for the local community.
They studied the calendrical systems developed by ancient civilizations.
The solstice holds calendrical importance in many cultures.

Synset Examples

calendric systems
solstice is a time of calendric importance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calendar - a system for organizing days for social, religious, commercial, or administrative purposes

Etymology

From 'calendar' + '-ical', following the pattern of adjectives denoting relation.
