poenology

1. NOUN

/ ˌpiːəˈnɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌpiːɒˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study of the management of prisons and the rehabilitation of prisoners, especially as a branch of criminology
study of prison management and rehabilitation

Examples

She wrote her dissertation on poenology and criminal justice reform.
Many experts in poenology advocate for more humane treatment of prisoners.
The university offers advanced courses in criminology and poenology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: penology - study of prison management and rehabilitation (standard contemporary spelling)

Etymology

From Latin 'poena' (punishment) + '-logy' (study of).
