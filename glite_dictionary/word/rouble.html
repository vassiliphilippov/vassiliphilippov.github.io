rouble

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːbəl / (AmE), / ˈruːbəl / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Russia
Russian unit of currency

Examples

The price of petrol has risen to 60 roubles per liter.
She exchanged her dollars for roubles at the airport.
The rouble fell sharply against the dollar after the announcement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ruble - alternative spelling for the same Russian currency unit

Etymology

From Russian рубль (rublʹ), meaning 'ruble'.
