cobblers

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑblərz / (AmE), / ˈkɒbləz / (BrE)
(slang) A man's testicles.
slang term for testicles
slang british

Examples

He took a kick right in the cobblers during the football match.
The comedian made a joke about getting your cobblers trapped in a door.
He doubled over in pain, clutching his cobblers after the mishap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: balls - slang for a man's testicles
  • Synonym: testicles - male reproductive glands in the scrotum

Etymology

Cockney rhyming slang: 'cobblers' from 'cobblers' awls', rhyming with 'balls'.
