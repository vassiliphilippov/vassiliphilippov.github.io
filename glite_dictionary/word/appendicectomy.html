appendicectomy

1. NOUN

/ əˌpɛndəˈsɛktəmi / (AmE), / əˌpɛndɪˈsɛktəmi / (BrE)
a surgical operation to remove the vermiform appendix
surgical removal of the appendix
medical british

Examples

The patient underwent an appendicectomy after being diagnosed with acute appendicitis.
Complications following appendicectomy are relatively rare when performed promptly.
She recovered quickly after her emergency appendicectomy last month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appendectomy - surgical removal of the appendix (American English)

Etymology

From Latin appendix (an addition) and Greek -ektomē (excision).
