burgomaster

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːɡəˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈbɜːɡəˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
the mayor of a town or municipality in Germany, the Netherlands, Flanders, or Austria
mayor in some European countries

Examples

The city's burgomaster welcomed the visiting diplomats with a formal ceremony.
After years of public service, she was elected burgomaster of her hometown in Flanders.
During the festival, the burgomaster gave a speech to the gathered crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mayor - the elected head of a city, town, or municipality

Etymology

From Dutch 'burgemeester', from 'burger' (citizen) + 'meester' (master).
