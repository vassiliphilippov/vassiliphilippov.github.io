Jubbulpore

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒʌbəlˌpɔr / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌb(ə)lpɔː / (BrE)
a former English name for the city of Jabalpur, an industrial city in central India, southeast of Delhi
former name for Jabalpur, India

Examples

During the British Raj, the city was commonly known as Jubbulpore.
The old railway station still displays the name Jubbulpore above its entrance.
Historical documents frequently refer to Jabalpur by its former name, Jubbulpore.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jabalpur - modern name of the city formerly called Jubbulpore

Etymology

Anglicized form of the Hindi name 'Jabalpur.'
