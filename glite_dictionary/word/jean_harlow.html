Jean Harlow

1. PROPN

/ dʒin ˈhɑrloʊ / (AmE), / dʒiːn ˈhɑːləʊ / (BrE)
(1911–1937) An American film actress known for her roles in several films with Clark Gable and as one of the most popular movie stars of the 1930s.
famous American film actress (1911–1937)

Examples

Many consider Jean Harlow to be one of the greatest stars of Hollywood's Golden Age.
The platinum blonde look was popularized by Jean Harlow in the 1930s.
Film historians often discuss the on-screen chemistry between Clark Gable and Jean Harlow.
