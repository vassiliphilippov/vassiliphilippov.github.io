Australianness

1. NOUN

/ əˈstreɪliənnəs / (AmE), / ɒˌstreɪliˈæn.nəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being Australian; the distinctive cultural or national attributes associated with Australia or its people
the quality of being Australian

Examples

Debates about Australianness often focus on cultural identity and heritage.
The novelist explores themes of Australianness in her latest book.
Critics questioned whether the film truly captured the spirit of Australianness.

Synset Examples

The point of departure is the notion that Australianness has been constructed as an identity caught between empires, between the old (British) empire and the new (American) empire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Britishness - the state or quality of being British
  • Synonym: Australianness - the concept of what it means to be Australian
