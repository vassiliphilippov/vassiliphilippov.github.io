ladder-back

1. NOUN

/ ˈlædɚ ˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈlædə ˌbak / (BrE)
A chair with a backrest formed of two upright posts connected by two or more horizontal slats, resembling the rungs of a ladder.
chair with horizontal slatted backrest

Examples

The antique shop had a beautiful ladder-back for sale in the corner.
Our dining room is furnished with old oak ladder-backs.
She admired the craftsmanship of the ladder-back, especially the smoothly curved slats.
