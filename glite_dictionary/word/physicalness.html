physicalness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪzɪkəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪk(ə)lnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being physical or consisting of matter
the state of being physical

Examples

The physicalness of the sculpture gave it a striking presence in the gallery.
She discussed the physicalness of pain compared to emotional suffering.
Children often explore the world through the physicalness of touch and movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physicality - the quality of being physical, especially in a noticeable or strong way
