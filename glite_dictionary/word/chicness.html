chicness

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃiːknəs / (AmE), / ˈʃiːknəs / (BrE)
the quality of being elegantly fashionable or stylish
elegant, fashionable quality

Examples

The chicness of her outfit caught everyone's attention at the party.
They admired the effortless chicness of the Parisian café interior.
His sense of chicness set him apart in the world of fashion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elegance - the quality of being graceful and stylish in appearance or manner
  • Synonym: chic - stylishly elegant, fashionable
  • Antonym: sloppiness - lack of neatness or style, the opposite of chicness

Etymology

From French 'chic' (stylish, smart) + English suffix '-ness' (indicating a state or quality).
