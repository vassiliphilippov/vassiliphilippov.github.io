saphead

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæpˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈsapˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person considered foolish or lacking in good judgment
foolish or unintelligent person

Examples

Only a saphead would fall for such an obvious scam.
He acted like a complete saphead during the meeting.
Don't be a saphead; think before you make decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment
  • Synonym: simpleton - a person lacking in common sense
  • Antonym: wise - having or showing experience, knowledge, and good judgment

Etymology

Formed from 'sap' (slang for fool) + 'head'.
