eye-popping

1. ADJ

/ ˈaɪ ˌpɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaɪ ˌpɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
Amazingly impressive or startling; extremely surprising or visually striking.
extremely impressive or visually striking
informal

Examples

The movie was full of eye-popping special effects that left the audience amazed.
She wore an eye-popping dress that turned heads as soon as she entered the room.
The company's profits showed an eye-popping increase last quarter, surprising investors.

Synset Examples

the skater's dazzling virtuosic leaps
these great best canvases still look as astonishing and as invitingly new as they did … when … his fulgurant popularity was in full growth
adventures related … in a style both vivid and fulgurous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astonishing - extremely surprising or impressive
  • Antonym: dull - not interesting or impressive

Etymology

Formed in English from "eye" + present participle of "pop," suggesting the eyes "popping" open in surprise.
