Rasht

1. PROPN

/ ræʃt / (AmE), / ræʃt / (BrE)
a city in northwestern Iran, near the Caspian Sea, known as an important regional center for trade and agriculture.
city in northwestern Iran

Examples

She was born in Rasht and later moved to Tehran for university.
The climate of Rasht is much milder than many other Iranian cities.
Tourists often visit Rasht to explore the nearby Caspian coast and local markets.
