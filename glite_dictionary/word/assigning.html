assigning

1. NOUN

/ əˈsaɪ.nɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈsaɪ.nɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of distributing or designating something to a specific person or place
distributing or designating to someone/somewhere

Examples

The assigning of roles was completed before the meeting started.
Her job involves the assigning of duties among team members.
Efficient assigning of resources can improve project outcomes.

Synset Examples

the first task is the assignment of an address to each datum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assignment - a task given to someone or the act of assigning
