character

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛrɪktər / (AmE), / ˈkærɪktə / (BrE)
the set of qualities or features that make something or someone distinctive; the individual nature of a person, place, or thing
distinctive quality or nature

Examples

The character of the old building has been carefully preserved during renovations.
Her paintings have a unique character that sets them apart from other artists' work.
The city's character comes from centuries of cultural influences and historical events.

Synset Examples

each town has a quality all its own
the radical character of our demands

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quality - a distinctive attribute or characteristic possessed by someone or something
  • Synonym: nature - the inherent or essential qualities of something or someone

Etymology

From Greek 'kharaktēr' (engraved mark, distinctive quality) via Latin 'character'.
