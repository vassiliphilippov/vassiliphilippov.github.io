pancreatic

1. ADJ

/ ˌpæŋ.kriˈæt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌpaŋ.krɪˈat.ɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or involving the pancreas.
relating to the pancreas
medical

Examples

The doctor is a specialist in pancreatic diseases and treatments.
They are researching new drugs to fight pancreatic cancer.
The symptoms of pancreatic disorders can sometimes be difficult to detect.

Synset Examples

pancreatic cancer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pancreas - a large gland behind the stomach that helps with digestion and blood sugar regulation

Etymology

From pancreas + -ic, indicating 'of or relating to the pancreas.'
