topcoat

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑpˌkoʊt / (AmE), / ˈtɒpˌkəʊt / (BrE)
a heavy outer coat worn over other clothes, especially in cold weather or winter
a heavy outer winter coat

Examples

She pulled her topcoat tighter around her as the wind picked up.
He hung his topcoat on the rack before entering the room.
During winter, a warm topcoat is an essential part of my wardrobe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overcoat - a long heavy coat worn over other clothes, especially outdoors in cold weather

Etymology

Formed from 'top' + 'coat' to indicate its use as a coat worn over other clothing.
