contrasting

1. ADJ

/ kənˈtræstɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈtrɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
strikingly different from each other in a way that emphasizes their differences
strikingly different; sharply distinct

Examples

The interior features contrasting colors of black and white for a modern look.
She wore a contrasting scarf to make her outfit stand out.
Their personalities are contrasting, which makes their friendship interesting.

Synset Examples

contrasting (or contrastive) colors

Related Terms

  • Antonym: similar - having a resemblance in appearance or nature, not very different
  • Synonym: different - not the same as another or each other
