alarmingly

1. ADV

/ əˈlɑrmɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈlɑːmɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that causes worry, fear, or concern; in an alarming manner.
in a way that causes alarm or concern

Examples

The city's population has been increasing alarmingly over the past decade.
Wildfires are becoming alarmingly frequent in the region.
Pollution levels have risen alarmingly since last year.

Synset Examples

It grew alarmingly fast

Related Terms

  • Antonym: reassuringly - in a way that makes someone feel less worried
