befitting

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
appropriate or suitable for a particular person, situation, or occasion.
appropriate; suitable; proper

Examples

The ceremony had a befitting sense of dignity and respect.
He wore attire befitting his new position as director.
It was a befitting end to her remarkable career.

Synset Examples

behavior befitting a father

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appropriate - suitable or proper in the circumstances
  • Antonym: unsuitable - not appropriate or fitting

Etymology

Present participle of "befit" (from be- + fit).
