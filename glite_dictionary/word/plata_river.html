Plata River

1. PROPN

/ ˈplɑtə ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈplɑːtə ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
an estuary between Argentina and Uruguay formed by the confluence of the Paraná and Uruguay rivers, flowing into the Atlantic Ocean
estuary between Argentina and Uruguay

Examples

The Plata River serves as a natural border between Argentina and Uruguay.
Many ships travel through the Plata River on their way to Buenos Aires.
The ecosystem of the Plata River supports a wide range of marine life.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Río de la Plata' meaning 'River of Silver'.
