nonracist

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˈreɪ.sɪst / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈreɪ.sɪst / (BrE)
Not showing or holding prejudice or discrimination based on race.
not prejudiced about race

Examples

The company is committed to a nonracist workplace culture.
She admired his nonracist attitude toward people from different backgrounds.
The organization promotes nonracist values in all of its programs.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: racist - showing or holding prejudice or discrimination based on race

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' meaning 'not', and 'racist'.
