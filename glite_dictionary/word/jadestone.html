jadestone

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒeɪdˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪdˌstəʊn / (BrE)
a semiprecious gemstone, usually green but sometimes whitish, consisting of jadeite or nephrite and taking a high polish
type of semiprecious gemstone (jadeite or nephrite)

Examples

She wore a beautiful necklace featuring a single jadestone pendant.
The ancient sculpture was carved from a solid piece of jadestone.
Collectors value the deep green color often seen in high-quality jadestone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jade - a hard, typically green gemstone used in jewelry and art

Etymology

From 'jade' (a green gemstone) + 'stone' (referring to its mineral form).
