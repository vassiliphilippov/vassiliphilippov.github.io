trilingual

1. ADJ

/ traɪˈlɪŋɡwəl / (AmE), / traɪˈlɪŋɡwəl / (BrE)
knowing or using three languages fluently
knowing three languages

Examples

She is trilingual, speaking English, Spanish, and French fluently.
The company's trilingual staff can assist clients in English, Mandarin, and Japanese.
Growing up in Montreal, he became trilingual at an early age.

Synset Examples

trilingual speakers

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: bilingual - knowing or using two languages fluently
  • Degree Higher: multilingual - knowing or using several languages fluently

Etymology

From Latin 'tri-' (three) + 'lingua' (tongue, language).
