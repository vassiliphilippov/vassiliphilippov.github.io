psephologist

1. NOUN

/ siːˈfɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / siːˈfɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
an expert who studies and analyzes elections and voting patterns
expert in election analysis

Examples

The psephologist accurately predicted the outcome of the national election.
Many journalists interviewed a psephologist to understand the swing in voter preferences.
As a renowned psephologist, she advised several political parties during the campaign.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: political scientist - an expert in the study of politics and government

Etymology

From Greek 'psephos' (pebble, used for voting) + '-logist' (one who studies).
