represented

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntɪd / (BrE)
Accurately or precisely depicted, described, or portrayed.
depicted or portrayed accurately

Examples

The data in the chart is represented precisely, making trends easy to identify.
She felt her views were not adequately represented in the final report.
Animals are often represented in art to symbolize certain human traits.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: misrepresented - not depicted accurately or truthfully
