lido

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈliːdəʊ / (BrE)
a public outdoor swimming pool or bathing beach, often used for recreation and water sports
public outdoor swimming pool or bathing beach
british

Examples

The city council plans to renovate the old lido to attract more visitors in the summer.
They spent every sunny weekend swimming at the open-air lido near their home.
Tickets for the popular lido sold out within hours on the hottest day of the year.

Etymology

From Lido, the name of a bathing place in Venice, Italy, ultimately from Latin 'litus' (shore).
