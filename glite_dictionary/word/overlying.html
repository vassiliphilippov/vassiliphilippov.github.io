overlying

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈlaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈlaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
lying or placed on or over something else; covering or situated above another object or layer.
placed on or over something else

Examples

The geologist examined the overlying rock layers to understand the region's history.
An overlying sheet of ice covered the lake during winter.
The new paint was applied to the overlying surface of the wall.

Synset Examples

an overlying image

Related Terms

  • Antonym: underlying - situated beneath or below something else
