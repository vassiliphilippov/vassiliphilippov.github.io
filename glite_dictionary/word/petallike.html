petallike

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛtəlˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɛtəlˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the characteristics of a petal, especially in shape, texture, or appearance.
resembling a petal

Examples

The flower's petallike leaves added a delicate beauty to the garden.
Some fungi produce petallike structures that attract pollinators.
The artist painted petallike patterns in soft pastel shades on the canvas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petaloid - resembling a petal; having petal-like characteristics

Etymology

From 'petal' + 'like', meaning similar to a petal.
