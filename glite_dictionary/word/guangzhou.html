Guangzhou

1. PROPN

/ ɡwɑŋˈdʒoʊ / (AmE), / ɡwæŋˈdʒəʊ / (BrE)
a city in southern China, the capital of Guangdong province, located on the Zhu Jiang (Pearl River) delta and known as a major port and commercial center
Chinese port city and Guangdong capital

Examples

Many international companies have their Asia-Pacific headquarters in Guangzhou.
The annual Canton Fair is held in Guangzhou, attracting buyers from around the world.
After landing in Guangzhou, we set out to explore the city's modern and historic districts.

Etymology

From Mandarin Chinese Guangzhou (广州), from 'Guǎng' (broad, wide) and 'zhōu' (prefecture).
