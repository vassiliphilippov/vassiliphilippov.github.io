Emmental

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛmənˌtɑl / (AmE), / ˈɛməntɑːl / (BrE)
A type of Swiss cheese characterized by its large holes and mild, nutty flavor.
Swiss cheese with large holes

Examples

He ordered a sandwich topped with slices of Emmental and ham.
The recipe calls for grated Emmental to be melted over the vegetables.
She picked up a block of Emmental at the local cheese shop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Swiss cheese - A generic term for cheeses with holes, notably Emmental

Etymology

From Emmental, a region in Switzerland where the cheese originated.
