Henry Clay Frick

1. PROPN

/ ˌhɛnri kleɪ ˈfrɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɛnri kleɪ ˈfrɪk / (BrE)
(1849–1919) An American industrialist who amassed a fortune in the steel industry and played a key role in the development of the United States steel sector.
American steel industry industrialist (1849–1919)

Examples

The museum was once the home of Henry Clay Frick, a leading figure in the steel industry.
Henry Clay Frick was instrumental in founding the Frick Collection in New York.
As a partner of Andrew Carnegie, Henry Clay Frick played a pivotal role in shaping American industry.
