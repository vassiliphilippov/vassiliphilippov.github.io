der-brain

1. NOUN

/ dɜr breɪn / (AmE), / dɜː breɪn / (BrE)
A person who is foolish or forgetful; someone who does or says silly things.
foolish or forgetful person (slang)
slang

Examples

I was such a der-brain for losing my car keys again.
Don't be a der-brain—remember to submit your assignment.
She felt like a der-brain when she realized she had missed her stop.

Synset Examples

She called herself a der-brain for forgetting the presentation.
Stop acting like a der-brain and think before you speak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airhead - a person who is not very intelligent or is forgetful
  • Synonym: scatterbrain - a person who is disorganized and forgetful

Etymology

Blend of 'der' (from 'derp', an exclamation of foolishness) and 'brain'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈdɝːˌbreɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɜːˌbreɪn / (BrE)
A person who acts in a foolish, clumsy, or inept way; an idiot.
a foolish or inept person (slang)
slang informal

Examples

He forgot his own birthday—what a der-brain!
She acted like a der-brain during the presentation and mixed up all the facts.
If you keep losing your keys, people will start calling you a der-brain.

Synset Examples

After that last meeting, I felt like a total der-brain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - an exceptionally intelligent person

Etymology

Formed from 'der' (shortened and altered from 'derp' or 'deranged', both slang) + 'brain'.
