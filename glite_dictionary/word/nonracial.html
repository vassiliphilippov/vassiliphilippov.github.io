nonracial

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈreɪʃəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈreɪʃəl / (BrE)
Not related to race or races.
not related to race

Examples

The committee worked to ensure all decisions were based on nonracial criteria.
The selection process is designed to be completely nonracial and fair.
His remarks focused on economic factors and were entirely nonracial.

Synset Examples

his remarks were intended to be completely nonracial
a nonracial reason for the peremptory challenge of the juror

Related Terms

  • Antonym: racial - relating to race or ethnicity

Etymology

From prefix 'non-' (not) + 'racial' (relating to race).
