nongranular

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈɡræn.jə.lɚ / (AmE), / nɒnˈɡræn.jʊ.lə / (BrE)
not containing or composed of granules; lacking a granular structure.
not containing granules

Examples

The sample showed a nongranular texture under the microscope.
Certain cells display a nongranular cytoplasm, distinguishing them from granular cells.
The powder was specifically refined to create a nongranular material.

Synset Examples

clear nongranular cytoplasm

Related Terms

  • Antonym: granular - containing or resembling granules

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) and 'granular' (having granules).
