gymkhana

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪmˈkɑnə / (AmE), / ɡɪmˈkɑːnə / (BrE)
an event, typically involving horses, where riders compete in various games and skill competitions
equestrian event with games and competitions

Examples

The annual gymkhana at the local riding club attracts competitors from across the region.
She won first place in the barrel racing event at yesterday's gymkhana.
Children participated enthusiastically in their first gymkhana, learning basic riding skills through fun games.

Etymology

From Hindi-Urdu 'gymkhāna', originally meaning an assembly or place of assembly.
