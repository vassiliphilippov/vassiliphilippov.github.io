melange

1. NOUN

/ meɪˈlɑːnʒ / (AmE), / meɪˈlɒ̃ʒ / (BrE)
a mixture or assortment of different things, often forming a varied or incongruous whole
a mixture or assortment

Examples

The novel offers a melange of styles, combining mystery, romance, and science fiction.
On the buffet table was a colorful melange of fruits, cheeses, and cured meats.
The city is a fascinating melange of cultures, languages, and traditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mixture - a combination of different things blended together
  • Synonym: blend - a mixture of different things or substances

Etymology

From French 'mélange' (mixture), from Old French 'mesler' (to mix).
