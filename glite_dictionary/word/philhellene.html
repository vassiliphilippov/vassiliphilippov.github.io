philhellene

1. NOUN

/ fɪlˈhɛlin / (AmE), / fɪlˈhɛliːn / (BrE)
A person who admires Greece, Greek people, or Greek culture.
admirer of Greece or Greek culture

Examples

During his travels, he became a noted philhellene and collected Greek art and manuscripts.
The philhellene devoted years to studying ancient Greek history and language.
Many philhellenes supported Greece during its war of independence in the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hellenophile - a person who admires Greek culture or Greeks

Etymology

From Greek 'philos' (loving) + 'Hellēn' (Greek).

/ fɪlˈhɛˌlin / (AmE), / fɪlˈhɛliːn / (BrE)

admiring Greek culture or Greece

Examples
