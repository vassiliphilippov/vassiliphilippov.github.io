gravidation

/ ˌɡrævɪˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɡravɪˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
the condition or process of being pregnant; pregnancy (technical or scientific use)
the state of being pregnant (technical)
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that early gravidation can sometimes be difficult to confirm.
