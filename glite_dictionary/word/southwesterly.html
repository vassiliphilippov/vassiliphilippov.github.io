southwesterly

1. ADJ

/ ˌsaʊθˈwɛstərli / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈwɛstəli / (BrE)
Situated in or oriented toward the southwest.
facing or toward the southwest

Examples

The old cottage had a southwesterly aspect, allowing it to catch the afternoon sun.
They decided to hike in a southwesterly direction to reach the next village.
Winds with a southwesterly orientation often bring warmer air from the ocean.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: northeasterly - situated in or oriented toward the northeast

2. ADJ

/ ˌsaʊθˈwɛstərli / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈwɛstəli / (BrE)
(of wind or weather) coming from the southwest.
coming from the southwest

Examples

A southwesterly wind brought warm air to the region.
They predicted southwesterly breezes in the afternoon.
The storm arrived quickly, driven by a strong southwesterly gale.

Synset Examples

the winds are southwesterly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: northwesterly - coming from the northwest
  • Antonym: southeasterly - coming from the southeast

Etymology

Formed from 'southwest' + suffix '-erly' meaning 'in the direction of'.
