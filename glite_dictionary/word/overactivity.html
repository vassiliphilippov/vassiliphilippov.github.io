overactivity

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊvɚækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərækˈtɪvəti / (BrE)
excessive or abnormally increased activity, especially in a bodily organ or process.
excessive activity

Examples

The overactivity of his immune system led to frequent allergic reactions.
Scientists are studying how overactivity in the brain may be linked to anxiety disorders.
Doctors observed overactivity in the child's thyroid gland, causing rapid growth.

Synset Examples

overactivity of the sebaceous glands causes the skin to become oily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hyperactivity - abnormally or excessively active behavior or function

Etymology

From 'over-' (excessive) + 'activity'.
