nightie

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪti / (AmE), / ˈnaɪti / (BrE)
A loose, comfortable dress worn by women as nightwear.
women's loose nightdress

Examples

She slipped into her favorite nightie before going to bed.
The shop had a beautiful selection of silk nighties in various colors.
He bought his wife a new nightie for her birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nightgown - a long, loose garment worn in bed by women or children
  • Degree Lower: pyjamas - cloths worn for sleeping or lounging

Etymology

Diminutive form of 'night', referring to nightdress.
