Zeppo Marx

1. PROPN

/ ˈzɛpoʊ ˈmɑrks / (AmE), / ˈzɛpəʊ ˈmɑːks / (BrE)
(1901–1979) American comedian and actor; the youngest of the Marx Brothers, known for appearing in their early films.
American comedian, one of the Marx Brothers

Examples

Many people remember Zeppo Marx for his straight-man roles in the Marx Brothers' early films.
Unlike his brothers, Zeppo Marx often played the romantic lead in their movies.
Fans of classic comedy still debate whether Zeppo Marx was an underrated performer.
