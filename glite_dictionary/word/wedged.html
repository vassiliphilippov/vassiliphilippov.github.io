wedged

1. ADJ

/ wɛdʒd / (AmE), / wɛdʒd / (BrE)
tightly packed or fitted into a space so that movement is difficult or impossible
tightly packed or fitted in

Examples

The door was wedged shut by a pile of books behind it.
Several cars were wedged together after the accident, making it hard to pull them apart.
Her suitcase was wedged tightly under the bed and wouldn't move.

Synset Examples

an impacted tooth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impacted - firmly wedged in place and difficult to remove
