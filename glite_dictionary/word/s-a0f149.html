2. NOUN

/ ɛs / (AmE), / ɛs / (BrE)
The symbol for second, the SI base unit of time equal to 1/60 of a minute.
second (unit of time) symbol

Examples

The experiment lasted exactly 10 s.
The athlete ran 100 meters in under 12 s.
A delay of just a few s can make a difference in a race.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: second - the SI unit of time, equal to 1/60 of a minute

Etymology

International symbol established by the International System of Units (SI) from Latin 'secunda', meaning 'second' (as in the 'second division' of an hour).
