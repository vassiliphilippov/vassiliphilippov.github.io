piazza

1. NOUN

/ piˈɑːtsə / (AmE), / piˈætsə / (BrE)
a public square, especially in an Italian town, open area or marketplace for people to gather
public square or open marketplace

Examples

The musicians played lively tunes in the piazza as people stopped to watch.
We enjoyed our morning coffee at a little café on the main piazza.
Many local festivals are held in the historic piazza every summer.

Synset Examples

they met at Elm Plaza
Grosvenor Place

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plaza - an open public square or marketplace, especially in a Spanish-speaking country
  • Synonym: square - an open area in a town, often where people gather

Etymology

From Italian 'piazza' (public square), from Latin 'platea' (broad street).
