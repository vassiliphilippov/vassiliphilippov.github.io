minutes

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪnɪts / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪts / (BrE)
An official written record of what was discussed and decided at a meeting.
official written record of a meeting

Examples

The secretary distributed the minutes of last week's meeting to all members.
Please review the minutes before the next board meeting.
Accurate minutes help ensure that everyone's contributions are recorded.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: record - an account of something kept in writing or another form
  • Synonym: transcript - a written or printed version of material originally presented in another medium

Etymology

Plural of 'minute', from Latin 'minuta scriptura' (small writing, short notes).
