alimentative

1. ADJ

/ ˌælɪˈmɛntətɪv / (AmE), / ˌælɪˈmɛntətɪv / (BrE)
relating to the provision or supply of nourishment or food.
supplying nourishment or food

Examples

A diet rich in vitamins has great alimentative value for children.
The plant's alimentative properties make it important in traditional remedies.
Scientists studied the alimentative functions of digestive enzymes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutritive - relating to nutrition or nourishing; providing nourishment

Etymology

From Latin 'alimentum' (nourishment) with the suffix '-ive'.
