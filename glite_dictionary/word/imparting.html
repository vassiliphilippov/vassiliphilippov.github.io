imparting

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈpɑrtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɑːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of conveying or communicating information, knowledge, or skills to others
the act of transmitting information

Examples

The imparting of knowledge is a central goal of education.
His lectures focused on the clear imparting of complex scientific concepts.
Effective imparting of information can inspire learners to explore further.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transmission - the act of passing something from one person or place to another

Etymology

From the verb 'impart', from Latin 'impartire' (to share, bestow).
