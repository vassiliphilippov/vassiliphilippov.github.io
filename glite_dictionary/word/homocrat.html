Homocrat

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊməˌkræt / (AmE), / ˈhəʊməˌkrat / (BrE)
A politician who advocates for LGBTQ+ rights, often used in political contexts.
politician supporting LGBTQ+ rights

Examples

The Homocrat introduced new legislation to protect transgender rights.
During the debate, one Homocrat was challenged on their support for same-sex marriage.
The party's Homocrats often collaborate with advocacy groups to promote equality.

Synset Examples

The Homocrat represents LGBTQ+ interests in the party.

Etymology

Blend of 'homo-' (from homosexual, relating to LGBTQ+) and 'Democrat'.
