light-haired

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪt hɛrd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt heəd / (BrE)
Having light-colored hair, especially blond or fair hair.
having light-colored hair

Examples

She has always been the only light-haired person in her family.
The group of children included both dark-haired and light-haired siblings.
His light-haired appearance made him easy to spot in the crowd.

Synset Examples
