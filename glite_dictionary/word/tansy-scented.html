tansy-scented

1. ADJ

/ ˈtænzi sɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈtænzi sɛntɪd / (BrE)
having the smell of tansy, a strong-scented herb
smelling of tansy

Examples

The garden was filled with tansy-scented air after the rain.
She wore a tansy-scented perfume that reminded me of wild meadows.
The herbal pillow had a distinct, tansy-scented aroma that helped me sleep.
