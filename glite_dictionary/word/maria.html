maria

1. NOUN

/ məˈriːə / (AmE), / məˈriːə / (BrE)
A valuable timber tree native to Panama, known for its durable wood.
timber tree of Panama

Examples

The dense forests of Panama are home to the maria, prized for its wood.
Local craftsmen prefer using maria for high-quality furniture due to its strength.
Exports of maria have contributed to Panama's forestry industry.

2. NOUN

/ ˈmɛəriə / (AmE), / ˈmɑːrɪə / (BrE)
dark, extensive regions on the surface of the moon, formed by ancient volcanic eruptions, and appearing as large, flat plains
dark plains on the moon's surface

Examples

Astronomers have mapped the maria on the lunar surface in detail.
The Apollo missions landed near some of the largest maria on the Moon.
Unlike the brighter highlands, maria are rich in basalt and appear darker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mare - a large, dark plain on the surface of the moon

Etymology

From Latin 'maria', plural of 'mare' (sea), due to their appearance resembling seas to early astronomers.
