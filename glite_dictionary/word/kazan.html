Kazan

1. PROPN

/ kəˈzɑːn / (AmE), / kəˈzæn / (BrE)
an industrial city in western Russia, the capital and largest city of the Republic of Tatarstan.
a city in western Russia, capital of Tatarstan

Examples

Many tourists visit Kazan for its rich Tatar culture and historic architecture.
The University of Kazan is one of the oldest and most prestigious in Russia.
After a short flight, they arrived in Kazan for an international conference.

Etymology

From Russian Казань (Kazánʹ).
