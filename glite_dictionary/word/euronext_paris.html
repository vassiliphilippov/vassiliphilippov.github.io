Euronext Paris

1. PROPN

/ ˌjʊrəˌnɛkst ˈpærɪs / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəˌnɛkst ˈpærɪs / (BrE)
the principal stock exchange located in Paris, France, and part of the pan-European Euronext group
the main Parisian stock exchange

Examples

Shares of the French company were first listed on Euronext Paris in 2001.
Euronext Paris closed up 2% after a day of heavy trading.
International investors watch Euronext Paris for trends in European markets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stock exchange - a market for buying and selling stocks and other securities

Etymology

Formed from 'Euronext', the name of the pan-European exchange, and 'Paris', indicating its location.
