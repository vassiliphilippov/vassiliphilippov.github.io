Isak Dinesen

1. PROPN

/ ˈaɪsæk dɪˈneɪsən / (AmE), / ˈiːzak ˈdiːnəsən / (BrE)
the pen name of Karen Blixen, a Danish writer known for her books about her life in Kenya, including 'Out of Africa'
Danish writer (pen name of Karen Blixen)

Examples

Isak Dinesen wrote evocatively about her years spent in colonial Kenya.
Many readers know Isak Dinesen as the author of 'Out of Africa.'
In literature classes, Isak Dinesen is celebrated for her unique narrative style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Karen Blixen - real name of Danish writer Isak Dinesen

Etymology

Pen name adopted by Karen Blixen (1885–1962), Danish author.
