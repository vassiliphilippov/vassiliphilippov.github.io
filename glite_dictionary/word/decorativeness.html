decorativeness

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈkɔrəˌtɪvnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈkɒrətɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being decorative or serving to make something more attractive
quality of being decorative

Examples

The decorativeness of the room made it perfect for hosting elegant parties.
Some people value function over decorativeness in home furnishings.
She admired the decorativeness of the handmade pottery on display.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ornamentation - the act or process of decorating or adorning

Etymology

From 'decorative' + '-ness' (noun suffix).
