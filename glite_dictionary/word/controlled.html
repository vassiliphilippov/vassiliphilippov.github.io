controlled

1. ADJ

/ kənˈtroʊld / (AmE), / kənˈtrəʊld / (BrE)
restrained, managed, or kept within certain limits; under regulation or supervision.
restrained or managed; regulated

Examples

The pilot executed a controlled landing despite the bad weather.
The doctor recommended a controlled diet to manage her condition.
There was a controlled release of information to the public.

Synset Examples

controlled emotions
the controlled release of water from reservoirs

Related Terms

  • Antonym: uncontrolled - not restrained or managed; out of control
