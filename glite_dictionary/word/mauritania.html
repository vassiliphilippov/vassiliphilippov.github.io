Mauritania

1. PROPN

/ mɔːˈrɪtəniə / (AmE), / mɒːˈrɪtəniə / (BrE)
A country in northwestern Africa, mostly covered by the western Sahara Desert, which gained independence from France in 1960.
country in northwestern Africa

Examples

She traveled across Mauritania to study the unique ecosystems of the Sahara Desert.
Mauritania declared independence from France in 1960.
The majority of Mauritania consists of arid desert landscapes.

Etymology

From French 'Mauritanie', from Latin 'Mauretania', an ancient Berber kingdom in North Africa.
