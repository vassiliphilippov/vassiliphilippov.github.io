gaskin

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæskɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡæskɪn / (BrE)
the lower part of a horse's thigh, located between the hock and the stifle
lower part of a horse's thigh

Examples

The veterinarian examined the horse's gaskin for signs of muscle strain.
A strong gaskin is important for a horse's speed and jumping ability.
The injury was located just above the right gaskin, near the stifle joint.
