compote

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑm.poʊt / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.pəʊt / (BrE)
A dessert consisting of stewed or baked fruit, often served chilled and sometimes sweetened or flavored.
dessert of stewed or baked fruit

Examples

She served a delicious compote of apples and berries for dessert.
I like to eat compote with yogurt for breakfast.
The restaurant offered a pear compote with vanilla ice cream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stewed fruit - fruit cooked slowly in liquid, usually with sugar

Etymology

From French 'compote', from Latin 'compositus' (arranged, prepared).
