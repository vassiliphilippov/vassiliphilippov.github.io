longstanding

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɔŋˌstændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌstændɪŋ / (BrE)
having existed or continued for a long time
existing for a long time

Examples

Their longstanding friendship endured through many challenges.
She finally resolved the longstanding dispute with her neighbor.
The town is known for its longstanding traditions and festivals.

Synset Examples

a long-standing friendship
the longstanding conflict

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: ancient - belonging to the very distant past
  • Antonym: recent - having happened or started not long ago
