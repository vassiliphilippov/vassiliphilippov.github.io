effeminate

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈfɛmənət / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛmɪnət / (BrE)
(of a man or boy) having or showing qualities, appearance, or behavior more often associated with women or girls; unmanly.
having traditionally feminine qualities (of a man or boy)

Examples

Many critics described the actor's performance as too effeminate for the role of a traditional hero.
His effeminate mannerisms set him apart from the other boys at school.
The old stereotypes associated long hair and jewelry with effeminate men.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: masculine - having qualities traditionally associated with men
  • Degree Higher: feminine - having qualities traditionally associated with women

Etymology

From Latin *effeminatus*, meaning 'made feminine', from *ex-* (out of) + *femina* (woman).
