bint

1. NOUN

/ bɪnt / (AmE), / bɪnt / (BrE)
a derogatory slang term for a woman
offensive slang term for a woman
slang offensive british

Examples

He was reprimanded for calling his colleague a bint during the meeting.
That sort of language, especially the word bint, is considered highly offensive.
Some British TV shows in the past used the word bint, but it's unacceptable today.

Synset Examples

He called her a bint in jest at the party.

Etymology

From Arabic 'bint' meaning 'daughter', entered British English slang through military contact in the Middle East.
