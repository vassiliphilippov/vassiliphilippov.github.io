to pistol-whip

1. VERB

/ ˈpɪstəl wɪp / (AmE), / ˈpɪstəl wɪp / (BrE)
to strike or beat someone with a pistol, typically using the handle or barrel as a blunt weapon
strike with a pistol

Examples

The guard was pistol-whipped by one of the robbers during the heist.
He threatened to pistol-whip anyone who tried to interfere with his escape.
After being pistol-whipped, the victim was left with a cut above his eye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bludgeon - to strike with a heavy object
