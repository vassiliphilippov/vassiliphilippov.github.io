vapourisable

1. ADJ

/ ˈveɪpəˌraɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ˈveɪpərʌɪzəbəl / (BrE)
(of a substance) capable of being changed into vapor; able to be volatilized.
able to be turned into vapor
british

Examples

This chemical is highly vapourisable at room temperature and must be stored in sealed containers.
Researchers studied the vapourisable compounds released during the reaction.
Ensure that all vapourisable substances are handled in a well-ventilated laboratory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volatilizable - can be converted to vapor or gas

Etymology

From 'vapour' (British spelling of 'vapor') + '-isable', from Latin 'vapor' (steam) and '-ize' (to make).
