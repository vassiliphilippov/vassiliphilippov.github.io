inartistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnɑrˈtɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪnɑːˈtɪstɪk / (BrE)
Lacking aesthetic sensibility or appreciation; not showing artistic quality or taste.
lacking artistic sensibility or taste

Examples

The critic dismissed the film as inartistic and emotionally flat.
Her inartistic approach failed to capture the beauty of the landscape.
Some found the architecture appealing, while others considered it harsh and inartistic.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: artistic - showing creative skill or taste; having aesthetic sensibility

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'in-' meaning 'not' + 'artistic.'
