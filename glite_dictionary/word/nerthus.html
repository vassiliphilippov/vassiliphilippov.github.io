Nerthus

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɜrθəs / (AmE), / ˈnɜːθəs / (BrE)
A Teutonic goddess of fertility, later identified with the Norse god Njord.
Teutonic fertility goddess

Examples

The tribe worshipped Nerthus as their principal fertility goddess.
Tacitus described rituals dedicated to Nerthus in his historical writings.
Some scholars believe Nerthus was later syncretized with the Norse god Njord.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Njord - Norse god of the sea and winds, associated with fertility

Etymology

From Old High German *Nerthus*, recorded by the Roman historian Tacitus.
