curiousness

1. NOUN

/ ˈkjʊriəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈkjʊəriəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being eager to know or learn something; curiosity
quality of being curious

Examples

Her curiousness led her to explore every corner of the old library.
There was a childlike curiousness in the way he approached new ideas.
Despite her curiousness, she knew when to respect other people's privacy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curiosity - desire to know or learn things

Etymology

From 'curious' + '-ness', following standard pattern for forming abstract nouns in English.
