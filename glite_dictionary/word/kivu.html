Kivu

1. PROPN

/ ˈkiːvuː / (AmE), / ˈkiːvuː / (BrE)
a lake in the mountains of central Africa, located between the Democratic Republic of the Congo and Rwanda
lake between Congo and Rwanda in central Africa

There are several small fishing communities along the shores of Kivu.
