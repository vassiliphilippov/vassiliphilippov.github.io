flea bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈfli bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈfliː bæɡ / (BrE)
an old, shabby, or dirty sofa
old, shabby sofa
informal

Examples

After years of use, the living room's flea bag was sorely in need of replacement.
He slumped down on the flea bag with a weary sigh after work.
They tried to disguise the flea bag with a colorful blanket, but it still looked worn out.

Synset Examples

That old sofa is just a flea bag.
