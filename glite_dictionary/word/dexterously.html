dexterously

1. ADV

/ ˈdɛk.stɚ.əs.li / (AmE), / ˈdɛk.stər.əs.li / (BrE)
In a dexterous manner; with skill, especially in using the hands or body.
with skill or dexterity

Examples

She dexterously wove the delicate threads into an intricate pattern.
The magician dexterously shuffled the cards, amazing the audience with his sleight of hand.
He dexterously repaired the watch without leaving a single scratch on its surface.

Synset Examples

dextrously he untied the knots

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skillfully - in a skillful manner
  • Antonym: clumsily - in a clumsy manner, lacking skill

Etymology

From 'dexterous' + '-ly', ultimately from Latin 'dexter' (on the right, skillful).
