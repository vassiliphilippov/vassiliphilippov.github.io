murderess

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɝːdɚ.ɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɜː.dər.ɪs / (BrE)
A woman who has committed murder.
woman who commits murder

Examples

The notorious murderess was finally apprehended after a nationwide search.
Her reputation as a murderess haunted her for the rest of her life.
The novel tells the chilling story of a nineteenth-century murderess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: murderer - a person who commits murder

Etymology

From Old French *murderesse*, feminine form of *murderer*.
