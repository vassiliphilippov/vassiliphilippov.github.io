well-done

1. ADJ

/ wɛl ˈdʌn / (AmE), / wɛl ˈdʌn / (BrE)
(of meat) cooked thoroughly so that there is no pink part left inside.
meat cooked with no pink inside

Examples

He prefers his steak well-done, with no pink in the middle.
The burger was cooked well-done, just how she liked it.
Please make sure the chicken is well-done before serving.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rare - (of meat) cooked very briefly, still red inside
  • Degree Lower: medium - (of meat) cooked so the inside is pink, not red or raw
  • Synonym: overcooked - (of food) cooked too long, often dry or tough
