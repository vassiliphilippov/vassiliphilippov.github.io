hopped-up

1. ADJ

/ hɑpt ʌp / (AmE), / hɒpt ʌp / (BrE)
(slang) Under the influence of narcotics, especially cannabis; high or intoxicated due to drug use.
under the influence of drugs
slang

Examples

He acted strangely all night, like he was hopped-up on something.
The police stopped a driver who seemed hopped-up behind the wheel.
You could tell from his behavior that he was completely hopped-up again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high - under the influence of drugs or alcohol
  • Antonym: sober - not under the influence of any substances; not intoxicated

Etymology

Derived from 'hop', a slang term for narcotic drugs, especially opiates, in early 20th-century American English.
