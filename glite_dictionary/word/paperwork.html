paperwork

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpɚˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəwɜːk / (BrE)
documents, forms, or written work that are part of official or administrative tasks, often regarded as routine or tedious.
administrative work with documents

Examples

Most new employees spend the first day filling out paperwork for human resources.
She stayed late at the office to finish the paperwork for the upcoming audit.
Dealing with all the paperwork for the loan application took several hours.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: documentation - the materials that provide official information or evidence; documents collectively

Etymology

Formed from 'paper' + 'work', first recorded in the early 20th century.
