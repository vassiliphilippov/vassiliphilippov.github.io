Commiphora meccanensis

1. NOUN

/ kəˌmɪfərə ˌmekəˈnɛnsɪs / (AmE), / kəˌmɪfərə ˌmekəˈnensɪs / (BrE)
A small evergreen tree native to Africa and Asia, whose bruised leaves emit a strong aromatic odor.
aromatic evergreen tree of Africa and Asia

Examples

The leaves of the Commiphora meccanensis give off a spicy scent when crushed.
Scientists are studying Commiphora meccanensis for its potential medicinal properties.
In traditional medicine, extracts from Commiphora meccanensis trees are sometimes used to treat wounds.

Etymology

From the botanical genus Commiphora and species epithet meccanensis, indicating association with Mecca.
