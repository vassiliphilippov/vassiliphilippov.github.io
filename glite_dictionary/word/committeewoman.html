committeewoman

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɪtiˌwʊmən / (AmE), / kəˈmɪtiˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is a member of a committee
female committee member

Examples

She served as a committeewoman for several years on the school board.
The committeewoman proposed a new initiative to support local businesses.
Elected as a committeewoman, she represented her district at the annual conference.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: committeeman - a man who is a member of a committee

Etymology

Formed from 'committee' + 'woman', following the pattern of 'committee' + 'man'.
