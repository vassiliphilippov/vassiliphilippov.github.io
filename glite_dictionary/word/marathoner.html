marathoner

1. NOUN

/ ˈmærəˌθɑnər / (AmE), / ˈmærəˌθɒnə / (BrE)
a person who participates in a marathon or long-distance running race
person who runs in marathons

Examples

She trained for months to become a dedicated marathoner.
Many marathoners travel around the world to compete in famous races.
The marathoner crossed the finish line with a look of relief and pride.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: runner - a person who runs, especially in races

Etymology

From 'marathon' (the long-distance running race) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
