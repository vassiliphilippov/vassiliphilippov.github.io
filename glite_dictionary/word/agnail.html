agnail

1. NOUN

/ ˈæɡˌneɪl / (AmE), / ˈaɡneɪl / (BrE)
A small piece of torn skin near the base or side of a fingernail that can be painful and prone to infection.
torn skin near fingernail

Examples

She winced as she pulled at the agnail on her thumb.
Biting an agnail can make your finger sore and lead to infection.
Washing your hands gently helps prevent agnails from forming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hangnail - a torn piece of skin near a fingernail

Etymology

Old English *angnægl* (painful nail or corn).
