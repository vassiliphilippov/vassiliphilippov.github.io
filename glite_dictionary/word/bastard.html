bastard

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæstərd / (AmE), / ˈbɑːstəd / (BrE)
An offensive term for a person who is stupid, irritating, or ridiculous.
offensive term for a stupid or irritating person
offensive vulgar

Examples

He's such a bastard for tricking everyone at work.
Don't be a bastard—just tell me the truth.
Some bastard scratched my new car while I was inside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Synonym: jerk - a contemptibly obnoxious person
  • Antonym: gentleman - a polite or honorable man

Etymology

From Old French 'bastard', of uncertain origin, possibly from Medieval Latin 'bastardus'.
