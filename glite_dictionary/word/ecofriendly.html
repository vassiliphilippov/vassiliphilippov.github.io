ecofriendly

1. ADJ

/ ˌikoʊˈfrɛndli / (AmE), / ˌiːkəʊˈfrendli / (BrE)
Not harmful to the environment; designed to have minimal impact on the natural world.
not harmful to the environment

Examples

The company uses ecofriendly packaging to reduce waste.
Many consumers now prefer ecofriendly cleaning products for their homes.
Switching to ecofriendly transportation options can greatly reduce carbon emissions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: environmentally friendly - not harmful to the environment
  • Synonym: green - environmentally conscious or beneficial
  • Antonym: polluting - causing harm to the environment

Etymology

Formed from 'eco-' (from 'ecology') and 'friendly'.
