motional

1. ADJ

/ ˈmoʊʃənəl / (AmE), / ˈməʊʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by motion; pertaining to movement.
relating to motion or movement

Examples

The scientist studied the motional properties of particles in the experiment.
The earthquake produced motional effects that were felt throughout the city.
Modern technology allows us to observe motional changes at microscopic levels.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: motionless - without motion; not moving

Etymology

From 'motion' + '-al', patterned after similar formations like 'national', from Latin 'motio' (movement).
