tearfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪr.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtɪə.fəl.nəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being full of tears or prone to weeping, often as a sign of sadness
state of being prone to tears

Examples

Her tearfulness during the farewell speech touched everyone in the audience.
Some medications may have side effects such as unexplained tearfulness or mood swings.
His tearfulness revealed just how much he cared about the outcome.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sadness - the state of being sad
  • Antonym: joyfulness - the state of being joyful

Etymology

From 'tearful' + '-ness', denoting a state or condition.
