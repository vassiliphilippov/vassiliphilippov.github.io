handsewn

1. ADJ

/ ˈhændˌsoʊn / (AmE), / ˈhændˌsəʊn / (BrE)
sewn by hand rather than by machine.
sewn by hand, not machine

Examples

This dress is completely handsewn and took weeks to finish.
She admired the handsewn details on the quilt.
Each handsewn button was perfectly aligned on the jacket.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: machine-sewn - sewn by machine rather than by hand
  • Synonym: handmade - made by hand rather than by machine

Etymology

From 'hand' + 'sewn', with 'sewn' past participle of 'sew'.
