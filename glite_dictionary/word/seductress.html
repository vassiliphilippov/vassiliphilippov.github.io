seductress

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈdʌktrɪs / (AmE), / sɪˈdʌk.trɪs / (BrE)
a woman who entices someone into sexual activity or temptation, especially by using charm or attraction
woman who seduces others sexually

Examples

The film portrayed her as a cunning seductress who manipulated everyone around her.
Legends often tell of a seductress who leads heroes astray with her charms.
Despite her reputation as a seductress, she longed for genuine affection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seducer - a man who seduces others sexually
  • Synonym: temptress - a woman who tempts or entices someone, often sexually

Etymology

Formed from "seduce" and the feminine suffix "-ess".
