operagoer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑː.p(ə).rəˌɡoʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈɒp.ərəˌɡəʊ.ə / (BrE)
a person who regularly attends performances at the opera
person who attends opera performances

Examples

As a dedicated operagoer, she never misses a new production at the Metropolitan Opera.
Many operagoers dress elegantly for evening performances.
The seasoned operagoer recognized the famous aria immediately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theatregoer - person who attends the theatre regularly
  • Synonym: concertgoer - person who attends concerts regularly

Etymology

From 'opera' (Italian, from Latin) + '-goer' (person who goes).
