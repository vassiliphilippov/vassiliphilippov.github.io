curving

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɜr.vɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɜː.vɪŋ / (BrE)
having or forming a smoothly rounded bend or arc; not straight
having a rounded bend or arc

Examples

The curving road wound through the hills, offering beautiful views of the valley below.
The artist admired the curving lines of the sculpture's form.
A curving staircase led guests up to the grand entrance hall.

Synset Examples

the curved tusks of a walrus
his curved lips suggested a smile but his eyes were hard

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curved - having or marked by a curve or arc
  • Antonym: straight - without bends or curves; not curving

Etymology

From the verb 'curve', ultimately from Latin 'curvus' (bent, curved).
