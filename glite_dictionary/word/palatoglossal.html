palatoglossal

1. ADJ

/ ˌpælətəˈɡlɑsəl / (AmE), / ˌpalətəˈɡlɒsəl / (BrE)
Relating to the palate and the tongue, especially to the arch connecting them.
of the palate and tongue
medical

Examples

The palatoglossal arch separates the mouth from the throat.
Doctors examined the palatoglossal folds to assess inflammation.
Damage to palatoglossal structures can affect swallowing.

Etymology

From Latin 'palatum' (palate) and Greek 'glōssa' (tongue).
