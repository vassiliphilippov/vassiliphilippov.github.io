NH

1. NOUN

/ ɛn eɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɛn eɪtʃ / (BrE)
abbreviation for New Hampshire, a state in the northeastern United States, with the capital Concord; one of the original 13 American colonies.
abbreviation for New Hampshire, US state

Examples

The presidential candidates spent several weeks campaigning in NH before the primary.
She grew up in NH and always missed the White Mountains.
The license plate design is unique to NH.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: New Hampshire - a state in the northeastern United States
