unavoidable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈvɔɪ.də.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈvɔɪ.də.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to avoid or prevent.
impossible to avoid

Examples

The cancellation of the event was unavoidable due to the severe weather conditions.
Delays are sometimes unavoidable in construction projects.
It is unavoidable that mistakes will happen when learning something new.

Synset Examples

inescapable conclusion
an ineluctable destiny
an unavoidable accident

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inevitable - certain to happen; unavoidable
  • Antonym: avoidable - able to be prevented or avoided

Etymology

From un- (not) + avoidable, from Old French 'evitier' (to shun or avoid).
