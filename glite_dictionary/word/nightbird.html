nightbird

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪt.bɝːd / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt.bɜːd / (BrE)
a person who prefers to be awake and active late at night
person who is active late at night

Examples

He's always been a nightbird, staying up until the early hours to write his stories.
As a nightbird, she finds it easy to work late into the night and sleep in the morning.
Most nightbirds struggle with 9-to-5 jobs that require early mornings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: night owl - a person who is habitually active late at night
