limbed

1. ADJ

/ lɪmd / (AmE), / lɪmd / (BrE)
Having limbs, or having limbs of a particular kind; used especially in combination.
having limbs or limbs of a type

Examples

The artist depicted a limbed creature emerging from the depths.
The tree appeared strangely limbed after the storm.
He was a powerful, long-limbed swimmer.

Synset Examples

strong-limbed

Etymology

From 'limb' (body part) + -ed, forming adjectives.
