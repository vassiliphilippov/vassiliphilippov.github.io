affably

1. ADV

/ ˈæfəbli / (AmE), / ˈafəbli / (BrE)
In a friendly and pleasant manner; showing warmth and kindness.
in a friendly, pleasant way

Examples

She greeted everyone affably, instantly putting the guests at ease.
The manager affably answered all our questions during the interview.
Despite the tense situation, he smiled affably at his opponents.

Synset Examples

‘Come and visit me,’ he said amiably

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amiably - in a friendly or pleasant manner
  • Antonym: gruffly - in a rough or unfriendly manner

Etymology

From 'affable' + '-ly'; 'affable' from Latin 'affabilis' (easy to speak to).
