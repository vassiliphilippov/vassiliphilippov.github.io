Tadzhikistan

1. PROPN

/ tɑːdˈʒik ɪˌstɑːn / (AmE), / tædˈʒiːk ɪstɑːn / (BrE)
A landlocked mountainous country in southeast Central Asia, north of Afghanistan, formerly part of the Soviet Union.
country in Central Asia, north of Afghanistan

Examples

The capital city of Tadzhikistan is Dushanbe.
Many travelers are drawn to the stunning landscapes of Tadzhikistan.
After the dissolution of the Soviet Union, Tadzhikistan became an independent nation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tajikistan - standard modern spelling of the country in Central Asia

Etymology

Variant transliteration of 'Tajikistan', from Tajik 'tojik' (a Persian ethnic group) + Persian '-stan' (land).
