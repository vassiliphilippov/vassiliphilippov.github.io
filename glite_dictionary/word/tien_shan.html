Tien Shan

1. PROPN

/ tiːˈɛn ʃɑːn / (AmE), / tiːˈɛn ʃæn / (BrE)
a major mountain range in Central Asia, extending about 1,500 miles across several countries.
mountain range in Central Asia

Examples

The Tien Shan mountains are known for their dramatic peaks and glaciers.
Snow leopards are sometimes found in the remote areas of the Tien Shan.
The expedition set out to cross the entire length of the Tien Shan range.

Etymology

From Chinese 天山 (Tiānshān) meaning 'Celestial Mountains'.
