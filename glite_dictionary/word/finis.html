finis

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪnɪs / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪs / (BrE)
the end or concluding part of a performance, book, or other work
the end of a work or performance

Examples

After the last note faded, the word finis appeared on the screen, signaling the film's end.
The author wrote 'finis' at the bottom of the manuscript to mark its completion.
A simple 'finis' was all that was needed to conclude the performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: end - the final part of something
  • Synonym: conclusion - the ending or last part of something

Etymology

From Latin finis (end, boundary).
