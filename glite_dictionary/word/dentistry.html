dentistry

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛn.tɪ.stri / (AmE), / ˈdɛn.tɪ.stri / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with the study, diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of diseases and conditions of the teeth and gums
medical study and care of teeth
medical

Examples

She decided to pursue a career in dentistry after shadowing a local dentist.
Advances in dentistry have improved dental health for millions of people.
Preventive dentistry focuses on keeping teeth healthy and preventing cavities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: odontology - the scientific study of the structure and diseases of teeth

Etymology

From French 'dentisterie', from 'dent' (tooth), from Latin 'dens, dentis'.
