Hovea

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊ.vi.ə / (AmE), / ˈhəʊ.vi.ə / (BrE)
A genus of Australian evergreen shrubs in the pea family (Fabaceae), known for their purple or blue flowers.
genus of Australian evergreen shrubs

Examples

Several species in the Hovea genus are popular in Australian native gardens for their attractive flowers.
The leaves of Hovea are typically narrow and dark green.
Botanists recently discovered a new species within the Hovea genus in Western Australia.
