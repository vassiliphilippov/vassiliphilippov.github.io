hovea

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈviːə / (AmE), / həʊˈviːə / (BrE)
a genus of evergreen shrubs native to Australia, known for glossy green foliage and vibrant blue or violet flowers
Australian shrub genus with vivid flowers

Examples

The hovea in her garden bloomed with brilliant violet flowers each spring.
Many Australian gardens feature hovea for their lush foliage and striking colours.
The botanist studied the distribution of the hovea species across different regions.

Etymology

Named after botanist Anton Pantaleon Hove.
