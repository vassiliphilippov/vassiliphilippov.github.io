lamentable

1. ADJ

/ ləˈmɛntəbəl, ˈlæmənˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ləˈmɛntəbl, ˈlæmənteɪbl / (BrE)
bad or deserving regret; very unfortunate or deplorable
bad or regrettable

Examples

The company's failure to address the safety issue was truly lamentable.
It was a lamentable waste of resources that could have been avoided.
His ignorance of the facts resulted in a lamentable decision.

Synset Examples

my finances were in a deplorable state
a lamentable decision
her clothes were in sad shape
a sorry state of affairs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deplorable - deserving strong condemnation; very bad
  • Synonym: regrettable - causing regret; unfortunate
  • Antonym: fortunate - bringing good luck or favorable results

Etymology

From Latin lamentabilis (lamentable, to be lamented), from lamentari (to wail or lament).
