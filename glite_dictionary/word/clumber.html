clumber

1. NOUN

/ ˈklʌmbər / (AmE), / ˈklʌmbə / (BrE)
a breed of thickset spaniel with a long, silky coat, known for its gentle temperament and sporting abilities
breed of spaniel with long, silky hair

Examples

The clumber is known for its calm demeanor and loyalty to its family.
She entered her prize clumber in the national dog show.
Unlike other spaniels, the clumber tends to move slowly and methodically when hunting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Clumber Spaniel - a breed of spaniel dog originating in England

Etymology

Named after Clumber Park in Nottinghamshire, England, where the breed was developed.
