wordfinder

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːdˌfaɪndɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːdˌfaɪndə / (BrE)
a reference book or online tool designed to help users find a word they cannot recall, typically by searching definitions, meanings, or related terms
reference tool to find forgotten words

Examples

She kept a wordfinder on her desk for those moments when she couldn't recall the right expression.
Many writers use a wordfinder to overcome writer's block and find precise terms.
The online wordfinder makes it easy to search for words by meaning or description.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thesaurus - a reference book listing synonyms and antonyms
