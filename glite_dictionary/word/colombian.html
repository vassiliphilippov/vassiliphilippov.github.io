Colombian

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlʌmbiən / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmbiən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Colombia in South America
native or inhabitant of Colombia

Examples

Many Colombians celebrate their Independence Day with music and dancing.
She met a group of Colombians while traveling in South America.
The restaurant is owned by a proud Colombian who shares traditional recipes from his homeland.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Colombia - country in northwestern South America

Etymology

From Colombia + -an, denoting association with the country Colombia.

2. ADJ

/ kəˈlʌmbiən / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmbiən / (BrE)
relating to Colombia or its people, culture, or language
of or from Colombia

Examples

She loves Colombian cuisine, especially the traditional dishes from Bogotá.
Many Colombian artists have gained international recognition for their work.
The company imports Colombian coffee, which is famous for its rich aroma.

Synset Examples

Colombian coffee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Colombia - a country in South America

Etymology
