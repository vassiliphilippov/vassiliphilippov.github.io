logistical

1. ADJ

/ ləˈdʒɪstɪkəl / (AmE), / ləˈdʒɪstɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the organization and planning of a complex operation, such as the movement of people or supplies
relating to logistics or organization

Examples

They encountered many logistical challenges while planning the international conference.
The logistical details of the project will be handled by the operations team.
Due to logistical issues, the shipment was delayed by several days.

Synset Examples

logistic requirements

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organizational - relating to the arrangement or coordination of activities or structures
  • Synonym: operational - relating to the running of a business or system

Etymology

From 'logistics' + '-al'. 'Logistics' from French 'logistique', from Greek 'logistikē' (art of calculation).
