unalloyed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈælɔɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈalɔɪd / (BrE)
(of a substance or feeling) pure; not mixed with anything else.
pure, not mixed with anything else

Examples

She felt unalloyed joy when she heard the good news.
The sculpture was made from unalloyed gold, giving it a brilliant shine.
His apology was accepted with unalloyed pleasure by everyone involved.

Synset Examples

unalloyed metal
unalloyed pleasure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pure - free from any contamination
  • Antonym: mixed - combined with other elements or substances

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'alloyed' (from Old French 'aloi', from Latin 'alligare', to bind).
