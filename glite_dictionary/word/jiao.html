jiao

1. NOUN

/ dʒaʊ / (AmE), / dʒaʊ / (BrE)
a unit of currency in China, equal to one-tenth of a yuan
Chinese monetary unit (1/10 yuan)

Examples

The price of the bun was only five jiao this morning.
He handed me three jiao coins to pay for the bottle of water.
Ten jiao make up one yuan in Chinese currency.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: yuan - the basic unit of currency in China

Etymology

From Chinese 角 (jiǎo), meaning 'horn' and used for a unit of money.
