Jeremy Bentham

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɛrəmi ˈbɛnθəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛərəmi ˈbɛnθəm / (BrE)
(1748–1832) An English philosopher and jurist, regarded as the founder of utilitarianism.
English philosopher and founder of utilitarianism

Examples

Jeremy Bentham is widely considered the founder of modern utilitarian philosophy.
The legal reforms inspired by Jeremy Bentham had a profound impact on English law.
Many philosophers have debated the principles first formulated by Jeremy Bentham.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utilitarianism - the ethical theory founded by Jeremy Bentham
