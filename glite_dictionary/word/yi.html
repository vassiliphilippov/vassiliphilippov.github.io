Yi

1. NOUN

/ ji / (AmE), / ji / (BrE)
A Loloish language spoken by the Yi (or Lolo) people in China, Vietnam, and Thailand.
language of the Yi people (Loloish)

Examples

Many children in the region learn Yi before they start studying Mandarin in school.
Researchers have published a new dictionary to help preserve the Yi language.
He is fluent in both Yi and Chinese, which helps him connect with his heritage.
