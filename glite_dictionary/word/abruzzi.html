Abruzzi

1. PROPN

/ əˈbruːtsi / (AmE), / əˈbruːtsi / (BrE)
a mountainous region in central Italy, located on the Adriatic coast and known for its natural beauty and historic towns
mountainous region in central Italy

Examples

The Abruzzi region is famous for its national parks and medieval villages.
He spent his summer hiking in the mountains of Abruzzi.
Many traditional Italian dishes originate from Abruzzi.

Etymology

From Italian 'Abruzzi,' plural of 'Abruzzo,' the name of the region.
