Casablanca

1. PROPN

/ ˌkæsəˈblæŋkə / (AmE), / ˌkæsəˈblaŋkə / (BrE)
the largest city and principal Atlantic port of Morocco, located in the northwest of the country
largest city and port of Morocco

Examples

He was born in Casablanca and moved to France as a child.
Many international companies have offices in Casablanca due to its economic importance.
The movie revived interest in the city of Casablanca among tourists.

Etymology

From Spanish 'casa blanca' meaning 'white house', referring to a whitewashed building there.
