eponymous

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈpɑːnɪməs / (AmE), / ɪˈpɒnɪməs / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) giving their name to something, or bearing the name of a person; relating to an eponym.
giving or bearing the name; relating to an eponym

Examples

The band's eponymous album was released to critical acclaim.
She played the eponymous heroine in the popular television series.
The restaurant's eponymous founder still oversees the kitchen every night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eponym - a person after whom something is named

Etymology

From Greek 'epōnymos' (giving name), from 'epi-' (upon) + 'onyma' (name).
