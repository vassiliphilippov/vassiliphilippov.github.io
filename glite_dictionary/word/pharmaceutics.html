pharmaceutics

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɑːrməˈsuːtɪks / (AmE), / ˌfɑːməˈsjuːtɪks / (BrE)
the branch of science concerned with the preparation, formulation, and dispensing of drugs and medicines
science of preparing and dispensing drugs
medical

Examples

She decided to pursue a career in pharmaceutics after completing her degree in chemistry.
Advances in pharmaceutics have led to more effective treatments for many diseases.
The conference covered new trends and research in the field of pharmaceutics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pharmacy - the science or practice of preparing and dispensing medicinal drugs

Etymology

From pharmaceutical, based on Greek pharmakeutikos (of drugs/medicine)
