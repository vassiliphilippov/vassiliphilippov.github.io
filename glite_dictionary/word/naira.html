naira

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪrə / (AmE), / ˈnaɪərə / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Nigeria, equal to 100 kobo
currency unit of Nigeria

Examples

You can exchange US dollars for naira at the airport bureau de change.
The cost of living in Lagos is rising as the value of the naira declines.
She paid 500 naira for a taxi ride across the city.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: kobo - a monetary subunit of the naira, 1/100 of a naira

Etymology

Introduced in 1973; derived from 'Nigeria'.
