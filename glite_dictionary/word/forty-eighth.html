forty-eighth

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɔːrtiˈeɪθ / (AmE), / ˌfɔːtiˈeɪθ / (BrE)
coming after the forty-seventh in a sequence; 48th.
ordinal number: 48th

Examples

She finished in forty-eighth place in the marathon.
This is the forty-eighth episode of the series.
The class celebrated their forty-eighth reunion last week.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty-seventh - coming after the forty-sixth in a sequence; 47th
  • Degree Higher: forty-ninth - coming after the forty-eighth in a sequence; 49th
