Diacalpa

1. NOUN

/ daɪˈælkəpə / (AmE), / daɪˈalkəpə / (BrE)
A genus of delicate ferns native to the foothills of the Himalayas, comprising a single known species.
genus of delicate Himalayan ferns

Examples

Botanists discovered a rare specimen of Diacalpa during their expedition in the Himalayan foothills.
The delicate fronds of Diacalpa make it a prized addition to fern collections.
Researchers are studying the sole species of Diacalpa to understand its ecological role in its native habitat.
