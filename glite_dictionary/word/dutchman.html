Dutchman

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌtʃ.mən / (AmE), / ˈdʌtʃ.mən / (BrE)
a male native or inhabitant of the Netherlands
male native of the Netherlands

Examples

A proud Dutchman waved his country's flag at the World Cup.
He met an old Dutchman who shared fascinating stories about Amsterdam.
As a Dutchman, he enjoyed cycling through the countryside.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Dutchwoman - a female native or inhabitant of the Netherlands

Etymology

From Dutch 'Duitsman' (German man), later referring to people from the Netherlands in English.
