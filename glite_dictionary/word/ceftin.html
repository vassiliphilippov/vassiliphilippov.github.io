Ceftin

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɛf.tɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɛf.tɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked name for an antibiotic (cefuroxime axetil) used to treat various bacterial infections, including those of the lungs, throat, ears, and urinary tract.
antibiotic medication (brand name)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Ceftin to treat her sinus infection.
Some patients experience nausea as a side effect of Ceftin.
It's important to finish your course of Ceftin, even if you feel better.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cefuroxime - an antibiotic of the cephalosporin class
