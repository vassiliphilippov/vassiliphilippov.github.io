to ozonise

1. VERB

/ ˈoʊzəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈəʊzənaɪz / (BrE)
to convert oxygen into ozone, typically by passing an electric current through it
convert oxygen into ozone

Examples

Some laboratories ozonise air to purify water supplies efficiently.
Engineers ozonised the oxygen to test the new filtration process.
The company plans to ozonise their waste gases to reduce emissions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ozonize - convert oxygen into ozone

Etymology

From ozone + -ise, following the pattern of scientific verbs.
