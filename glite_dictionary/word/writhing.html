writhing

1. ADJ

/ ˈraɪðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraɪðɪŋ / (BrE)
moving in a twisting, contorted, or snake-like fashion
twisting or contorted in motion

Examples

The snake was writhing on the forest floor as it tried to escape.
Under the microscope, the bacteria appeared writhing and alive.
He gave her a jar full of writhing worms for bait.

Synset Examples

wiggly worms

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twisting - moving or contorting with a twisting motion

Etymology

Present participle of the verb 'writhe', from Old English 'wrīthan' (to twist).
