moonbat

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːnˌbæt / (AmE), / ˈmuːn.bæt / (BrE)
A person with extreme or irrational political views, especially on the far left; used disparagingly.
person with extreme political views (slang)
slang

Examples

The senator dismissed her critics as a bunch of moonbats making wild accusations.
He was labeled a moonbat by political opponents after proposing radical reforms.
Online forums sometimes become echo chambers for moonbats from across the political spectrum.

Synset Examples

The moonbat crowd gathered, sharing wild ideas about the government.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extremist - a person who holds extreme political or religious views
  • Antonym: moderate - a person with moderate, middle-of-the-road political views

Etymology

Blend of 'moon' (suggesting lunacy) and 'bat' (as in 'batty', meaning crazy).
