muser

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuzər / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzə / (BrE)
a person who thinks deeply and meditatively, especially in a quiet and reflective manner
person who thinks deeply

Examples

She was always a quiet muser, lost in her thoughts during long walks in the countryside.
The muser spent hours contemplating the mysteries of life in his journal.
Unlike others who chatted incessantly, he preferred being a muser, enjoying solitude and reflection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thinker - a person who thinks deeply about subjects
  • Antonym: superficial person - a person who does not engage in deep thought

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'muse' (to think deeply), itself from Old French 'muser'.
