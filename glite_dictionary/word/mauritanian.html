Mauritanian

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɔːrɪˈteɪniən / (AmE), / ˌmɔːrɪˈteɪniən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Mauritania, a country in northwest Africa
native or inhabitant of Mauritania

Examples

Many Mauritanians live and work in the capital city, Nouakchott.
A group of Mauritanians participated in the international cultural festival.
He became friends with several Mauritanians while studying abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mauritanian - of or relating to Mauritania or its people

Etymology

From Mauritania, the country name, with the suffix -an forming demonyms.
