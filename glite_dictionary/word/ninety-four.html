/ ˈnaɪnti fɔr / (AmE), / ˈnaɪnti fɔː / (BrE)
The number 94; the cardinal number that is four more than ninety.
the number 94

Examples

There are ninety-four students enrolled in this course.
The team scored ninety-four points in the final game.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: ninety-three - the number 93

Etymology

From Old English 'nigontīene' (nineteen) and 'feower' (four), by compounding 'ninety' and 'four'.
