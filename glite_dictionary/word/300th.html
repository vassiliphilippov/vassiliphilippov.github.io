300th

1. ADJ

/ ˈθriː ˈhʌndrədθ / (AmE), / ˈθriː ˈhʌndrədθ / (BrE)
being number three hundred in a sequence; the ordinal form of 300.
ordinal form of 300

Examples

Today marks the 300th anniversary of the village's founding.
The museum welcomed its 300th visitor of the day just before closing time.
