WTC

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʌbəl.juː tiː ˈsiː / (AmE), / ˌdʌbəl.juː tiː ˈsiː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for World Trade Center, the twin skyscrapers in New York City that were destroyed during the terrorist attacks of September 11, 2001.
abbreviation for World Trade Center towers, NYC

Examples

Many people around the world remember exactly where they were when the WTC was attacked.
The memorial was built on the original site of the WTC in Lower Manhattan.
The skyline of New York City changed forever after the loss of the WTC.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: World Trade Center - the twin towers destroyed in the 2001 terrorist attacks in New York City

Etymology

Abbreviation of World Trade Center.
