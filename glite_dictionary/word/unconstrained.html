unconstrained

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkənˈstreɪnd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈstreɪnd / (BrE)
not restricted, limited, or controlled by anything; free from constraint or restraint
not restricted or limited

Examples

The discussion was lively and unconstrained, with everyone free to share their ideas.
Her unconstrained laughter filled the entire room with joy.
Engineers prefer unconstrained designs that allow for more creativity and innovation.

Synset Examples

he was unconstrained by any of the sanctions of polite society
the dog was unconstrained
idle, unconstrained gossip

Related Terms

  • Synonym: free - not under the control or power of another; able to act or be as one wishes
  • Antonym: restricted - limited in extent, number, scope, or action

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) and 'constrained' (from Latin 'constringere', to bind tightly).
