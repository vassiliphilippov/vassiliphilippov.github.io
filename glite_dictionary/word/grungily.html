grungily

1. ADV

/ ˈɡrʌndʒɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌndʒɪli / (BrE)
In a dirty, shabby, or run-down manner.
in a dirty or shabby way

Examples

The abandoned house looked grungily neglected from the outside.
He dressed grungily for the concert, with ripped jeans and a stained T-shirt.
Old posters clung grungily to the faded walls of the alley.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grungy - dirty, shabby, or run-down in appearance
