pyrotechnical

1. ADJ

/ paɪ.roʊˈtɛk.nɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌpaɪ.rəʊˈtɛk.nɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to the manufacture or art of making fireworks or similar explosive displays
relating to fireworks or pyrotechnics

Examples

The festival organizers hired a pyrotechnical expert to design the fireworks display.
She developed a keen interest in pyrotechnical engineering after watching a fireworks show.
Strict safety measures are essential in any pyrotechnical production facility.

Synset Examples

pyrotechnic smokes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pyrotechnic - relating to fireworks or the art of making fireworks

Etymology

From Greek 'pyr' (fire) + 'techne' (art, skill), via French 'pyrotechnique'.
