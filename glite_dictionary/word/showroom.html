showroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌruːm / (BrE)
a room or area where goods, especially vehicles, are displayed for sale to customers
room for displaying goods for sale

Examples

The new car was displayed in the brightly lit showroom at the dealership.
They spent an hour browsing furniture in the company's spacious showroom.
Many customers visit the showroom before making a final purchase decision.

Synset Examples

in Britain a showroom is called a ‘salesroom’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salesroom - a room for displaying goods for sale (used in British English)

Etymology

From 'show' + 'room', first used in the early 20th century for rooms where goods were displayed.
