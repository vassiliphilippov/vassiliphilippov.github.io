Martha Jane Burke

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑːrθə dʒeɪn bɜrk / (AmE), / ˈmɑːθə dʒeɪn bɜːk / (BrE)
(1852–1903) A United States frontierswoman and legendary figure of the Wild West, noted for her marksmanship and adventurous life; also known as Calamity Jane.
legendary Wild West frontierswoman

Examples

Stories of Martha Jane Burke have become part of American frontier folklore.
Many historians debate which exploits of Martha Jane Burke are fact and which are legend.
A new biography explores the life and adventures of Martha Jane Burke in the Wild West.
