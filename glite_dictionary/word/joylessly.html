joylessly

1. ADV

/ ˈdʒɔɪ.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪ.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a manner that lacks joy or happiness; without showing pleasure or delight.
without joy or happiness

Examples

He handed over the report joylessly, barely looking up from his desk.
She joylessly prepared dinner, lost in her worries.
The children cleaned their room joylessly after being scolded.

Synset Examples

she greeted her guests joylessly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: joyfully - in a joyful manner; with joy
  • Synonym: glumly - in a sad or gloomy manner
