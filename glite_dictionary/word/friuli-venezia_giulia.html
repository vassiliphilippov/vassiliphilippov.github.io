Friuli-Venezia Giulia

1. PROPN

/ friˈuli vəˈnɛtsiə ˈdʒuːliə / (AmE), / friˈuːli vɛˈnɛtsiə ˈdʒuːliə / (BrE)
an autonomous region in northeastern Italy, bordered by Austria, Slovenia, and the Adriatic Sea, known for its diverse culture and landscape
region of northeastern Italy

Examples

My family spent a memorable summer in Friuli-Venezia Giulia exploring its mountains and coast.
The cuisine of Friuli-Venezia Giulia reflects both Italian and Central European influences.
Friuli-Venezia Giulia is famous for its white wines and beautiful scenery.

Etymology

Italian, named after the historical regions of Friuli and Venezia Giulia.
