tropics

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɑːpɪks / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪks / (BrE)
the region of the Earth between the Tropic of Cancer and the Tropic of Capricorn, known for its hot climate
region between the Tropics of Cancer and Capricorn

Examples

Many exotic fruits grow best in the tropics due to the warm, humid climate.
She spent her vacation exploring the rainforests of the tropics.
Scientists study biodiversity in the tropics to understand Earth's ecosystems.

Etymology

From Greek 'tropikos' (of a turn or solstice), referencing the points on the celestial sphere where the sun appears to turn back in its apparent path.
