sands

1. NOUN

/ sændz / (AmE), / sændz / (BrE)
stretches of sandy ground along the edge of a sea, lake, or ocean; shore covered with sand
sandy shore along water

Examples

We walked for miles along the golden sands of the bay.
The sands were crowded with families enjoying the sunny weather.
Turtles come ashore to lay their eggs on the warm sands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beach - an area of sand or pebbles along the edge of a sea or lake
