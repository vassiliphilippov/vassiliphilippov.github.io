smelter

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmɛltɚ / (AmE), / ˈsmɛltə / (BrE)
a plant or building where metal is extracted from its ore by heating and melting
industrial plant for extracting metal from ore

Examples

The mining company built a new smelter to process the extracted copper ore.
Toxic fumes from the smelter forced nearby residents to relocate.
Aluminum is produced at a smelter using electricity to separate it from its ore.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foundry - a workshop or factory for casting metal

Etymology

From Middle English smeltren, related to 'smelt' (to melt or fuse).
