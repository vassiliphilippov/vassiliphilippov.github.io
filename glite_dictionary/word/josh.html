to josh

1. VERB

/ dʒɑʃ / (AmE), / dʒɒʃ / (BrE)
to tease or joke with someone in a playful, good-natured way
tease or joke playfully

Examples

The friends josh each other about their favorite sports teams.
He likes to josh his coworkers to keep things light at the office.
They were just joshing when they said your tie was outrageous.

Synset Examples

After we relaxed, we just kidded around

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tease - to make fun of someone in a playful or unkind way
  • Synonym: kid - to joke or tease playfully

Etymology

Origin unknown; probably American English, attested from the mid-19th century.
