computable

1. ADJ

/ kəmˈpjuːtəbl̩ / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be calculated, computed, or estimated by mathematical means
able to be calculated or estimated

Examples

The total cost of the project is computable using this formula.
Most algorithmic problems are computable by standard computers.
The effect of the policy is difficult to measure directly but should be computable in theory.

Synset Examples

a calculable risk
computable odds
estimable assets

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calculable - able to be calculated or estimated

Etymology

From compute + -able; first recorded in mid-17th century.
