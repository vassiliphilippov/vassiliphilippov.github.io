lifelessly

1. ADV

/ ˈlaɪfləslɪ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪflɪslɪ / (BrE)
In a way that lacks energy, animation, or vitality.
without energy or vitality

Examples

She stared lifelessly out of the window, lost in thought.
The flowers drooped lifelessly in the summer heat, clearly needing water.
He repeated the speech lifelessly, showing no interest in the subject.

Synset Examples

lifelessly he performed the song

Related Terms

  • Synonym: listlessly - without energy or enthusiasm
  • Antonym: vigorously - with energy and enthusiasm

Etymology

From 'lifeless' + '-ly'.
