ungummed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɡʌmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡʌmd / (BrE)
not treated with adhesive gum or coating, especially referring to paper or envelopes.
not treated with adhesive gum

Examples

The ungummed labels require an additional adhesive to stick properly.
We ordered a batch of ungummed envelopes by mistake, so they don’t seal automatically.
For certain art projects, ungummed paper is preferred because it won’t stick or curl.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: gummed - treated with an adhesive gum

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not', and 'gummed', from 'gum' (adhesive substance).
