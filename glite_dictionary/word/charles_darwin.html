Charles Darwin

1. PROPN

/ ˈʧɑrlz ˈdɑrwɪn / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːlz ˈdɑːwɪn / (BrE)
(1809–1882) English naturalist who formulated the theory of evolution by natural selection.
English scientist, evolution theorist

Examples

Charles Darwin revolutionized biology with his theory of evolution by natural selection.
The work of Charles Darwin continues to influence scientists around the world.
Many books have been written about the life and discoveries of Charles Darwin.
