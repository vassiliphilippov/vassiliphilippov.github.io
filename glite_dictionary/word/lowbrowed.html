lowbrowed

1. ADJ

/ ˈloʊˌbraʊd / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌbraʊd / (BrE)
(of a person or their tastes) lacking intellectual, cultural, or artistic sophistication; not cultivated.
not intellectual or cultured

Examples

The critic dismissed the movie as too lowbrowed to warrant serious discussion.
He always preferred lowbrowed entertainment over high art and literature.
Her lowbrowed sense of humor made her popular at parties.

Synset Examples

lowbrow tastes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lowbrow - not highly intellectual or cultured
  • Antonym: highbrowed - having intellectual or cultural tastes

Etymology

Formed from 'lowbrow', which originated in early 20th-century English, referring metaphorically to the physical shape of the forehead as indicating intelligence.
