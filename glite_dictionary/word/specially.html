specially

1. ADV

/ ˈspɛʃəli / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃəli / (BrE)
in a special or particular manner; for a special purpose or reason
in a special or particular manner

Examples

The chef prepared a specially designed menu for the event.
These shoes were specially made to fit her feet.
The room was specially decorated for the celebration.

Synset Examples

a specially arranged dinner

Related Terms

  • Synonym: especially - to a great extent; very much; particularly

Etymology

From special + -ly.

2. ADV

/ ˈspɛʃəli / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃəli / (BrE)
to a distinctly greater extent or degree than is common; particularly or especially.
to a greater degree than usual

Examples

He became specially nervous before exams, much more than his classmates.
This policy is specially designed to help low-income families.
The recipe calls for specially selected ingredients to enhance the flavor.

Synset Examples

he was particularly fussy about spelling
a particularly gruesome attack
under peculiarly tragic circumstances
an especially (or specially) cautious approach to the danger

Related Terms

  • Synonym: especially - to a greater extent than usual; particularly
  • Synonym: particularly - to a high degree; especially

Etymology

From special + -ly, ultimately from Latin specialis (particular, special).
