brewing

1. NOUN

/ ˈbruːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbruːɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of making beer or other malt beverages by steeping, boiling, and fermenting ingredients such as malt and hops with yeast
process of making beer or ale

Examples

The art of brewing has been passed down through generations in his family.
She studied the science behind brewing to open her own craft brewery.
Advancements in technology have transformed the traditional brewing process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beer - an alcoholic drink made from fermented malt and hops
  • Antonym: distillation - the process of purifying a liquid by heating and cooling

Etymology

From Old English *brēowan* (to brew), related to German *brauen* (to brew).
