abstentious

1. ADJ

/ əbˈstɛnʃəs / (AmE), / əbˈstɛnʃəs / (BrE)
self-restraining; refraining from indulging an appetite, especially for food or drink.
self-restraining, especially with food or drink

Examples

She is remarkably abstentious even when surrounded by delicious food at parties.
His abstentious habits contributed to his healthy lifestyle and disciplined mindset.
Although not completely abstinent, he remained abstentious with alcohol throughout his life.

Synset Examples

not totally abstinent but abstemious

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abstemious - restrained in consumption, especially of food or alcohol
  • Antonym: indulgent - allowing oneself to enjoy pleasures freely

Etymology

From Latin 'abstentus' (held back), from 'abstinēre' (to refrain).
