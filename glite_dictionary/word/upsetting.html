upsetting

1. ADJ

/ ʌpˈsɛtɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌpˈsɛtɪŋ / (BrE)
causing distress, anxiety, or emotional disturbance; making someone feel unhappy or worried.
causing distress or emotional disturbance

Examples

The news of the accident was extremely upsetting to everyone involved.
She found his comments upsetting and left the room in tears.
It can be upsetting to see animals treated poorly in documentaries.

Synset Examples

his disconcerting habit of greeting friends ferociously and strangers charmingly
an upsetting experience

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disturbing - causing worry or anxiety; unsettling
  • Antonym: comforting - making one feel less anxious or distressed
