Stheno

1. PROPN

/ ˈstɛnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈstɛnəʊ / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, the eldest of the three Gorgons, who were monstrous sisters with snakes for hair.
eldest of the Gorgons in Greek mythology

Examples

In some myths, Stheno was more ferocious than her sisters.
Few heroes dared to face Stheno and her deadly gaze.
Unlike Medusa, Stheno was said to be immortal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Medusa - the most famous of the three Gorgons
  • Synonym: Euryale - one of the three Gorgons, sister of Stheno and Medusa

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Σθενώ (Sthenṓ), meaning 'strong'.
