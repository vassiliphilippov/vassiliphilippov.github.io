bella sombra

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɛl.ə ˈsɒm.brə / (AmE), / ˌbɛl.ə ˈsɒm.brə / (BrE)
A fast-growing evergreen tree native to South America, noted for its broad trunk with high water content and dark green oval leaves.
South American evergreen tree with broad trunk

Examples

The bella sombra provides welcome shade in the hot, dry summers of Argentina.
Gardeners admire the bella sombra for its rapid growth and unique trunk structure.
A row of bella sombras lines the boulevard, creating a lush green canopy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ombú - alternative name for bella sombra tree

Etymology

From Spanish 'bella sombra' meaning 'beautiful shade'.
