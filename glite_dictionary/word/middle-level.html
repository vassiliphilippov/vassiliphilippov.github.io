middle-level

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɪdəl ˌlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.əl ˌlɛv.əl / (BrE)
Intermediate in rank, status, or position, especially within an organization.
intermediate in rank or position

Examples

She accepted a middle-level management position at the new company.
Most middle-level employees attend both staff and department meetings.
The middle-level supervisors serve as a bridge between workers and upper management.
