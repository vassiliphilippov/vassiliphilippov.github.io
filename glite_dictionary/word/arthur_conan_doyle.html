Arthur Conan Doyle

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑːrθɚ ˈkoʊnən ˈdɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈɑːθə ˈkəʊnən ˈdɔɪl / (BrE)
(1859–1930) a British author best known for creating the detective Sherlock Holmes.
creator of Sherlock Holmes

Examples

Arthur Conan Doyle is celebrated worldwide for his compelling stories featuring Sherlock Holmes.
Many literary scholars consider Arthur Conan Doyle one of the greatest crime writers of all time.
Few people know that Arthur Conan Doyle also wrote historical novels and science fiction.
