OPCW

1. PROPN

/ ˌoʊ pi si ˈdʌbəl.ju / (AmE), / ˌəʊ piː siː ˈdʌb.l.juː / (BrE)
An international organization that oversees the implementation of the Chemical Weapons Convention and seeks to eliminate chemical weapons.
Organisation for the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons

Examples

The OPCW has played a key role in the destruction of declared chemical weapon stockpiles around the world.
Several countries work closely with the OPCW to ensure compliance with international chemical disarmament agreements.
The annual report of the OPCW highlights progress made in chemical weapons elimination.

Etymology

Acronym for Organisation for the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons.
