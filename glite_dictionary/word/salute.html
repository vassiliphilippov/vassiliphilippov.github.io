salute

1. NOUN

/ səˈlut / (AmE), / səˈluːt / (BrE)
an act of showing respect or honoring someone or something, often in a formal or ceremonial way
act of honoring or showing respect

Examples

The mayor's speech was a heartfelt salute to the retiring police chief.
The parade ended with a musical salute celebrating the country's independence.
Her book is a loving salute to her late mentor.

Synset Examples

a musical salute to the composer on his birthday

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tribute - an act, statement, or gift intended to show gratitude, respect, or admiration
  • Antonym: disregard - lack of consideration or respect; ignoring something deserving attention

Etymology

From Latin 'salutare' (to greet), from 'salus' (health, welfare).
