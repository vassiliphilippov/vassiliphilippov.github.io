utterable

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌt̬.ɚ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈʌt.ər.ə.bəl / (BrE)
(of words or thoughts) able to be spoken or expressed in speech; capable of being uttered.
capable of being spoken or expressed

Examples

The complexity of her ideas made them barely utterable in a single conversation.
He struggled to transform his emotions into any utterable form.
Some truths are so painful they hardly seem utterable.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inutterable - impossible to express in words, incapable of being spoken

Etymology

From 'utter' + '-able', based on Latin '-abilis' meaning 'able to be'.
