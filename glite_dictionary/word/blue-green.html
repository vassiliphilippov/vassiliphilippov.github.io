blue-green

1. ADJ

/ bluːˈɡriːn / (AmE), / bluːˈɡriːn / (BrE)
Having a color intermediate between blue and green; of a bluish shade of green.
bluish shade of green

Examples

The walls were painted a soft blue-green to create a calming effect.
She wore a stunning blue-green dress to the party last night.
The lake appeared blue-green under the bright midday sun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teal - a dark greenish-blue color
  • Synonym: turquoise - a greenish-blue color
