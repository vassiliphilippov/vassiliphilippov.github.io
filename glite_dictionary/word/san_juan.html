San Juan

1. PROPN

/ sæn ˈwɑn / (AmE), / san ˈwæn / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Puerto Rico, an island in the Caribbean Sea
capital of Puerto Rico

Examples

We spent our vacation exploring the colorful streets of San Juan.
The cruise ship docked in San Juan before continuing its journey around the Caribbean.
Many tourists visit San Juan for its beautiful beaches and historical sites.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: San Juan River - a major river in Central America

Etymology

From Spanish 'San Juan', meaning 'Saint John'.
