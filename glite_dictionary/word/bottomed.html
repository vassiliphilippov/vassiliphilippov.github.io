bottomed

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑːt̬əmd / (AmE), / ˈbɒtənd / (BrE)
(of an object) having a bottom of a specified kind or shape.
having a specified kind of bottom

Examples

The laboratory used several flat-bottomed flasks for their experiments.
They sailed across the calm lake in a shallow-bottomed boat.
The restaurant featured unique, round-bottomed bowls for their signature noodle soup.
