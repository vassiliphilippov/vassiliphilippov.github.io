superintendence

1. NOUN

/ ˌsuːpərɪnˈdɛndəns / (AmE), / ˌsjuːpərɪnˈdɛndəns / (BrE)
the act of overseeing or supervising the performance or operation of a person, group, or activity
act of supervising or overseeing

Examples

The project was completed under the superintendence of an experienced engineer.
Library operations came directly under the superintendence of the university board.
He was responsible for the superintendence of all financial matters in the company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supervision - the action or process of supervising someone or something
  • Synonym: management - the process of dealing with or controlling things or people

Etymology

From Latin 'superintendens', present participle of 'superintendere' (to oversee).
