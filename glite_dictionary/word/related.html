related

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈleɪtɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪtɪd / (BrE)
connected by blood, marriage, or a common ancestor; belonging to the same family or origin.
connected by family or origin

Examples

He explained that they were related through their grandmother on their father's side.
Many of the guests at the reunion were related by marriage rather than by blood.
Are you related to the famous scientist who shares your last name?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unrelated - not connected by kinship, origin, or marriage
  • Synonym: connected - joined or linked together, especially by relationship

2. ADJ

/ ɹɪˈleɪtɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪtɪd / (BrE)
connected by logic, cause, or shared characteristics; associated or linked in some way
connected or associated

Examples

Many school policies apply only to related events and activities.
These documents are all related to the company’s annual report.
Experts discussed art and the related field of design.

Synset Examples

painting and the related arts
school-related activities
related to micelle formation is the … ability of detergent actives to congregate at oil-water interfaces

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unrelated - not connected or associated
  • Synonym: connected - joined or associated in some way
  • Synonym: associated - closely connected in some way
