sclerite

1. NOUN

/ ˈsklɛr.aɪt / (AmE), / ˈsklɪə.raɪt / (BrE)
a hard plate or component of the exoskeleton in some arthropods
hard exoskeletal plate in arthropods

Examples

The beetle's wing covers are reinforced by thick sclerites.
Scientists studied the arrangement of sclerites to identify the insect species.
Crab exoskeletons are composed of numerous sclerite plates.

Etymology

From Greek 'sklēros' (hard) and '-ite' (denoting part or component).
