salaah

1. NOUN

/ sɑːˈlɑː / (AmE), / sɑːˈlɑː / (BrE)
the ritual prayer of Muslims, performed five times daily facing Mecca, as required by Islamic law
Muslim ritual prayer, performed five times daily

Examples

Muslims gather at the mosque several times a day to perform salaah.
He learned the proper way to carry out salaah during his religious studies.
Missing a salaah is considered a serious matter in many Islamic communities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salat - Muslim ritual prayer, performed five times daily (alternate spelling)

Etymology

From Arabic ṣalāh (prayer, supplication).
