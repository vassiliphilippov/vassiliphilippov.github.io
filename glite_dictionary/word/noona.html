noona

1. NOUN

/ nuː.nɑː / (AmE), / ˈnuː.nɑː / (BrE)
In Korean culture, a word used by males to address an older sister or an older female friend.
Korean term for older sister or woman addressed by a male

Examples

After he moved to Seoul, he often called his friend's sister noona as a sign of respect.
She smiled warmly when her little brother's friend greeted her with a cheerful 'noona!'
K-dramas often feature male characters affectionately calling an older female character noona.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: oppa - term used by females to address an older male in Korean culture

Etymology

From Korean 누나 (nuna), meaning 'older sister' as used by males.
