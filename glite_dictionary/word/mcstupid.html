McStupid

1. NOUN

/ məkˈstuːpɪd / (AmE), / məkˈstjuːpɪd / (BrE)
a slang term used to describe someone who feels or acts foolish or silly
slang for someone foolish or silly
slang

Examples

After tripping over his own feet, he muttered, 'Nice going, McStupid.'
She called herself McStupid for leaving the report at home.
Don't be a McStupid—check your work before handing it in.

Synset Examples

He felt like a McStupid for forgetting his keys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very foolish or stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - an exceptionally intelligent person

Etymology

Formed by combining the prefix 'Mc-' (as in McDonald's, used humorously to create pejoratives) with 'stupid'.
