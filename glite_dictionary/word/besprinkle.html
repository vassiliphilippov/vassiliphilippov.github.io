to besprinkle

1. VERB

/ bɪˈsprɪŋkəl / (AmE), / bɪˈsprɪŋkl̩ / (BrE)
To scatter drops of liquid lightly over something; to sprinkle lightly.
to sprinkle lightly with liquid

Examples

The baker besprinkled the cake with a dusting of powdered sugar.
A gentle rain besprinkled the windows and made the garden shine.
She besprinkles her favorite perfume on her wrists before leaving the house.

Synset Examples

Sprinkle the lawn

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sprinkle - to scatter drops or particles lightly on a surface

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'be-' and 'sprinkle,' following the pattern of similar English verbs.
