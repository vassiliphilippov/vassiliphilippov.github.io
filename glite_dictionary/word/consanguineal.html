consanguineal

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑn.sæŋˈɡwɪn.i.əl / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.sæŋˈɡwɪn.i.əl / (BrE)
Related by blood; having a common ancestor.
related by blood

Examples

Inheritance is often determined by consanguineal relationships in traditional societies.
They studied the consanguineal ties within royal families.
A consanguineal relative may be eligible to donate blood in many countries.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: affinal - related by marriage, not by blood
  • Synonym: consanguineous - related by blood; sharing a common ancestor

Etymology

From Latin 'consanguineus' (of the same blood).
