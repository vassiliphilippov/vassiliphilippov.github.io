ROI

1. NOUN

/ ɑr oʊ aɪ / (AmE), / ɑːr əʊ aɪ / (BrE)
abbreviation for 'Return on Investment'; a measure, usually expressed as a percentage, used to evaluate the efficiency or profitability of an investment, calculated by dividing earnings before interest and taxes by total capital invested.
return on investment; profitability measure

Examples

The company's ROI improved significantly after the restructuring last quarter.
Investors often compare the ROI of different projects before making a decision.
Understanding your ROI can help you allocate resources more effectively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: return - the profit or yield from an investment

Etymology

Acronym for 'Return on Investment'.
