B. B. King

1. PROPN

/ ˌbiː ˌbiː ˈkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌbiː ˌbiː ˈkɪŋ / (BrE)
(1925–2015) an American guitarist and singer, known as one of the most influential blues musicians.
influential American blues guitarist and singer

Examples

Many musicians credit B. B. King as a major influence on their sound.
The soulful guitar solos of B. B. King have inspired generations of blues artists.
I once saw B. B. King perform live, and it was an unforgettable experience.

Etymology

Stage name derived from his initials; real name Riley B. King.
