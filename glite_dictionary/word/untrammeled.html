untrammeled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtræməld / (AmE), / ʌnˈtræməld / (BrE)
not restricted or limited in any way; free and unrestricted.
not restricted or limited; free

Examples

The new law allows for untrammeled access to the national park by the public.
Her writing is characterized by untrammeled creativity and boldness.
He longed for an untrammeled life, free from routine and expectation.

Synset Examples

the gift of a fresh eye and an untrammeled curiosity
the untrammeled rush that the snows had shown in the first spring sun

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unrestricted - not limited or controlled; free
  • Antonym: confined - restricted in space, size, amount, or extent

Etymology

From un- (not) + trammel (to confine), from Old French tramail (a kind of net for catching birds), from Late Latin tremaculum.
