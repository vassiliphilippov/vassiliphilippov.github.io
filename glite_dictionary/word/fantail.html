fantail

1. NOUN

/ ˈfænˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈfanˌteɪl / (BrE)
the overhanging, fan-shaped part of a ship's deck that extends aft of the sternpost
fan-shaped stern deck on a ship

Examples

The crew gathered at the fantail to watch the sunset over the ocean.
Maintenance was scheduled for the fantail due to increased wear from recent storms.
During the ceremony, the captain addressed the sailors assembled on the fantail.
