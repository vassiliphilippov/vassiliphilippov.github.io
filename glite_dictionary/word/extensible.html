extensible

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈstɛnsəbəl / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnsɪbəl / (BrE)
capable of being extended, stretched, or opened out
capable of being extended or stretched

Examples

Some measuring tapes are extensible to make measuring long distances easier.
A frog's extensible tongue helps it catch insects quickly.
The designer chose an extensible table that can seat more people when needed.

Synset Examples

an extensile tongue
an extensible measuring rule

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extensile - capable of being extended or stretched
  • Antonym: inextensible - not capable of being extended or stretched

Etymology

From Latin 'extendere' (to stretch out) + English '-ible' (capable of).
