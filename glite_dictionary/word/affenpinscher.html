affenpinscher

1. NOUN

/ ˈæfənˌpɪnʃər / (AmE), / ˈafənˌpɪnʃə / (BrE)
A small European dog breed with dark, wiry hair and a tufted muzzle, resembling a terrier.
a small wire-haired dog breed

Examples

The affenpinscher is known for its playful personality and unique monkey-like face.
She brought her affenpinscher to the dog show, where it impressed the judges with its spirited attitude.
Many owners appreciate how little affenpinschers shed compared to other breeds.

Etymology

From German Affe (ape, monkey) + Pinscher (terrier).
