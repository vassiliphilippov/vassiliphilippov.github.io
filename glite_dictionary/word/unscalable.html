unscalable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈskeɪləbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈskeɪləbl / (BrE)
impossible to climb or ascend; unable to be scaled
impossible to climb

Examples

The sheer rock face was unscalable without special climbing equipment.
For centuries, the fortress was protected by its unscalable walls.
The cliff appeared unscalable, discouraging any attempt to reach the summit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insurmountable - too great to be overcome or defeated
  • Antonym: scalable - able to be climbed or scaled

Etymology

From un- (not) + scalable (able to be climbed), from scale (to climb) + -able.
