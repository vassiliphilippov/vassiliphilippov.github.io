unimpaired

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪmˈpɛrd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪmˈpeəd / (BrE)
not damaged, affected, or diminished in any way; remaining complete or whole.
not damaged or diminished

Examples

Despite the accident, her memory was unimpaired and she could recall every detail clearly.
He was able to continue his duties with unimpaired judgment throughout the crisis.
The ancient artifact has remained remarkably unimpaired after centuries of exposure to the elements.

Synset Examples

his speech remained unimpaired

Related Terms

  • Antonym: impaired - diminished or damaged function or ability

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'impaired', from Latin 'impedire' (to hinder), via Old French 'empairer' (to impair).
