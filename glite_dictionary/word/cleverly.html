cleverly

1. ADV

/ ˈklɛvərli / (AmE), / ˈklɛvəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows intelligence, ingenuity, or skill.
in a clever or skillful manner

Examples

She solved the difficult puzzle cleverly, impressing everyone in the room.
The architect cleverly designed the house to maximize sunlight.
They cleverly avoided traffic by taking a different route.

Synset Examples

they were cleverly arranged
a smartly managed business

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skillfully - in a skillful manner
  • Antonym: stupidly - in a stupid manner

Etymology

From 'clever' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
