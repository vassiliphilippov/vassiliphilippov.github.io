poultryman

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊl.tri.mæn / (AmE), / ˈpəʊl.tri.mæn / (BrE)
A man who sells or deals in poultry and poultry products.
man who sells poultry

Examples

The poultryman at the market offered fresh eggs and chickens for sale.
As a third-generation poultryman, he knew everything about raising turkeys.
The restaurant sources its chicken from a trusted local poultryman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poultry dealer - person who sells poultry and related products

Etymology

From 'poultry' + 'man', denoting a person engaged with poultry.
