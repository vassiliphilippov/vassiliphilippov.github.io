sledder

1. NOUN

/ ˈslɛdər / (AmE), / ˈslɛdə / (BrE)
a person who rides on a sled, especially for recreation or sport
person who rides a sled

Examples

The hill was crowded and every sledder was laughing as they raced down the slope.
As a child, she was a fearless sledder during the snowy winters.
Helmets are recommended for every young sledder to ensure safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sled - a vehicle for sliding over snow or ice

Etymology

From 'sled' (a vehicle for sliding over snow) + '-er', an agent noun suffix.
