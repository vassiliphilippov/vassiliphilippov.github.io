uncheerful

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtʃɪrfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɪəfəl / (BrE)
not cheerful; causing or characterized by feelings of gloom or sadness.
not cheerful; gloomy or sad

Examples

The weather was cold and uncheerful, making everyone want to stay indoors.
Her uncheerful demeanor made it hard to lighten the mood at the meeting.
They redecorated the uncheerful lobby to create a warmer, more inviting atmosphere.

Synset Examples

the economic outlook is depressing
something cheerless about the room
a moody and uncheerful person
an uncheerful place

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheerless - lacking cheer or happiness; gloomy
  • Synonym: depressing - making someone feel sad or without hope
  • Antonym: cheerful - happy and positive

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'cheerful'.
