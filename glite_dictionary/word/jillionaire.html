jillionaire

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒɪl.jəˈnɛr / (AmE), / ˌdʒɪl.jəˈneə / (BrE)
an extremely wealthy person (used humorously or exaggeratedly)
an extremely wealthy person
informal

Examples

He wished he could be a jillionaire and travel the world in luxury.
In the movie, the main character goes from broke to jillionaire overnight.
People joked that whoever solved the problem would become a jillionaire.

Synset Examples

With his latest invention, he dreams of being a jillionaire.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: millionaire - a person whose assets are worth a million or more units of currency
  • Degree Higher: billionaire - a person whose assets are worth a billion or more units of currency

Etymology

Formed from 'jillion' (an indefinitely large number) + '-aire' (as in millionaire).
