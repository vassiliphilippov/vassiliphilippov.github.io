Smyrna

1. PROPN

/ smɝː.nə / (AmE), / smɜː.nə / (BrE)
a former name for the port city of Izmir in western Turkey, known for its historical and cultural significance
former name of Izmir, Turkish port city

Examples

In ancient times, Smyrna was one of the most prosperous cities in Asia Minor.
The ruins of Smyrna attract archaeologists and tourists from around the world.
She researched her family's origins in Smyrna before it became Izmir.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Izmir - modern name of the same Turkish city formerly known as Smyrna

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Σμύρνα (Smýrna), the original name of the city.
