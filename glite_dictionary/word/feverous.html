feverous

1. ADJ

/ ˈfiːvɚəs / (AmE), / ˈfiːvərəs / (BrE)
having or affected by a fever; characterized by or resulting from fever
having or affected by a fever

Examples

She lay in bed, her body feverous and trembling despite the heavy blankets.
The feverous child was quickly taken to see a doctor.
His feverous state made him delirious and restless throughout the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: febrile - having or showing the symptoms of a fever

Etymology

From Middle English *feverous*, from Old French *fieverous*, from Latin *febris* (fever).
