can-do

1. ADJ

/ ˈkæn ˌdu / (AmE), / ˈkan ˌduː / (BrE)
having a positive attitude and a willingness to tackle difficult tasks or problems
willing and able to get things done

Examples

Her can-do attitude helped the team overcome many obstacles.
We need someone with a can-do spirit to lead this project.
His can-do approach impressed everyone at the interview.

Synset Examples

a can-do kind of person
the city's indomitable optimism and can-do spirit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimistic - hopeful and confident about the future
  • Antonym: pessimistic - expecting the worst or the most negative outcome
