Kotoko

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈtoʊkoʊ / (AmE), / kəʊˈtəʊkəʊ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken south of Lake Chad, mainly along the lower Chari and Logone rivers
Chadic language of Lake Chad region

Examples

The Kotoko language is spoken in communities near the border of Cameroon and Chad.
Linguists have documented many oral traditions in Kotoko.
She is fluent in both French and Kotoko.
