Luba

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːbə / (AmE), / ˈluːbə / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken in southeastern Congo
Bantu language of southeastern Congo

Examples

Many communities in southeastern Congo communicate using Luba.
She is studying Luba as part of her African languages course.
Books in Luba are available at the university library.

2. NOUN

/ ˈluːbə / (AmE), / ˈluːbə / (BrE)
a member of a Bantu ethnic group living in southeastern Congo
member of a Bantu ethnic group in Congo

Examples

The Luba have a rich artistic heritage, especially in wood carving and sculpture.
Many members of the Luba migrated to urban centers during the 20th century.
A traditional Luba ceremony celebrates the beginning of the planting season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bantu - a member of a large group of peoples living in central and southern Africa
