Bull

1. NOUN

/ bʊl / (AmE), / bʊl / (BrE)
(Astrology) A person born while the sun is in the zodiac sign Taurus.
person born under Taurus (astrology)

Examples

As a proud Bull, she loves stability and comfort in her life.
Many Bulls are believed to be patient but stubborn.
My best friend is a Bull, born in May.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Taurus - the second sign of the zodiac, symbolized by the bull
