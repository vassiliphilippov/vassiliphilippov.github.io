unforgettable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.fɚˈɡɛt.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.fəˈɡɛt.ə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to forget; leaving a lasting impression in one's memory.
impossible to forget

Examples

The view from the mountain peak at sunrise was truly unforgettable.
Their wedding day was an unforgettable experience for everyone involved.
She gave an unforgettable performance that moved the entire audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: memorable - worth remembering; easily remembered
  • Antonym: forgettable - easily forgotten; not memorable

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'forgettable' (able to be forgotten).
