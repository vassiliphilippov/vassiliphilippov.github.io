hooved

1. ADJ

/ huːvd / (AmE), / huːvd / (BrE)
having hoofs or resembling hoofs
having hoofs; hoofed

Examples

Wild deer are hooved animals that inhabit forests and grasslands.
Many farm animals, such as cows and goats, are hooved.
Fossil evidence suggests that some extinct species were also hooved.

Synset Examples

horses and other hoofed animals

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hoofed - having hoofs
