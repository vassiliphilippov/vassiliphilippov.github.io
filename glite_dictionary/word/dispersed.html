dispersed

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈspɝst / (AmE), / dɪˈspɜːst / (BrE)
spread or distributed over a wide area; scattered
spread or scattered widely

Examples

The remains of the ancient city are dispersed throughout the valley.
After the festival, the crowd dispersed quickly across the streets.
Many research institutions are dispersed across several countries.

Synset Examples

has ties with many widely dispersed friends
eleven million Jews are spread throughout Europe

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scattered - spread over an area in a disorganized way

Etymology

From Latin dispersus, past participle of dispergere (to scatter).
