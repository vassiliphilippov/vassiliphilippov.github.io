hyperboloidal

1. ADJ

/ ˌhaɪ.pɚ.bəˈlɔɪ.dəl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪ.pə.bəˈlɔɪ.dəl / (BrE)

having the shape of a hyperboloid

Examples

The cooling tower's hyperboloidal structure is both elegant and efficient.

Related Terms
