Alfred Bernhard Nobel

1. PROPN

/ ˈælfrəd bɛrnˈɑrd noʊˈbɛl / (AmE), / ˈælfrɪd ˌbɜːnˈhɑːd nəʊˈbɛl / (BrE)
(1833–1896) Swedish chemist, engineer, and inventor, best known for inventing dynamite and establishing the Nobel Prizes.
Swedish chemist, inventor of dynamite, founder of Nobel Prizes

Examples

The Nobel Prizes were established according to the will of Alfred Bernhard Nobel.
Alfred Bernhard Nobel is credited with inventing dynamite in the 19th century.
Many biographies have been written about Alfred Bernhard Nobel and his contributions to science.
