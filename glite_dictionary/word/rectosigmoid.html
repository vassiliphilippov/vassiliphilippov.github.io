rectosigmoid

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɛk.toʊˈsaɪ.ɡmɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌrɛk.təʊˈsɪɡ.mɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to the sigmoid colon and the upper part of the rectum.
of the sigmoid colon and upper rectum
medical

Examples

The doctor found a polyp in the rectosigmoid region during the colonoscopy.
A rectosigmoid tumor may require a different surgical approach than other colorectal tumors.
Chronic inflammation can develop in the rectosigmoid area of the colon.

Etymology

From Latin 'rectum' (straight, i.e., rectum) and Greek 'sigmoeidēs' (sigma-shaped, i.e., sigmoid).
