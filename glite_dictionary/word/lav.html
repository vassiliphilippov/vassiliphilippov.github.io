lav

1. NOUN

/ læv / (AmE), / læv / (BrE)
an informal term for a room or building containing one or more toilets; a bathroom or lavatory
informal term for a toilet or bathroom
informal british

Examples

Excuse me, can you tell me where the nearest lav is?
The pub's lav was surprisingly clean for a Friday night.
He dashed off to the lav between acts at the theater.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lavatory - a room or building containing one or more toilets or a fixture for washing
  • Synonym: toilet - a room or fixture for urination and defecation
  • Synonym: bathroom - a room containing a bath or a toilet

Etymology

Shortened form of 'lavatory', from Latin lavatorium (place for washing), from lavare (to wash).
