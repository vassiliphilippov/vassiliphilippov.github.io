trimly

1. ADV

/ ˈtrɪm.li / (AmE), / ˈtrɪm.li / (BrE)
In a neat, tidy, or smart manner.
in a neat or tidy way

Examples

He was trimly attired in a well-fitted suit and polished shoes.
The garden was trimly maintained, with every bush evenly shaped.
Her trimly organized desk impressed the new supervisor.

Synset Examples

he was trimly attired

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neatly - in a neat manner
  • Antonym: messily - in a messy, untidy manner

Etymology

From 'trim' (neat; smart in appearance) + '-ly' (adverb forming suffix).
