freshener

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛʃənər / (AmE), / ˈfrɛʃənə / (BrE)
a substance or device that makes something smell or feel fresher, especially the air in a room or car
something that makes something smell fresher

Examples

I bought a new freshener for my car because it was starting to smell musty.
Many people use a lavender freshener to keep their homes smelling pleasant.
The bathroom freshener needed to be replaced after a few weeks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air freshener - a product that makes indoor air smell fresher

Etymology

From 'freshen' + '-er', denoting an agent noun.
