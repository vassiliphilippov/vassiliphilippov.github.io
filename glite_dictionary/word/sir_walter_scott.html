Sir Walter Scott

1. PROPN

/ sɜr ˈwɔltər skɑt / (AmE), / sə ˈwɔːltə skɒt / (BrE)
(1771–1832) A British author famous for his historical novels and ballads, including 'Ivanhoe' and 'Rob Roy'.
British historical novelist and poet

Examples

Many scholars consider Sir Walter Scott the father of the historical novel genre.
I recently finished reading 'Ivanhoe' by Sir Walter Scott.
