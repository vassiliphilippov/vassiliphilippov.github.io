Manila

1. PROPN

/ məˈnɪlə / (AmE), / məˈnɪlə / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of the Philippines, situated on the island of Luzon
capital city of the Philippines

Examples

Manila is known for its vibrant culture and historical landmarks.
The conference was held in Manila last summer.
She traveled from Manila to other parts of the Philippines.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Manila,' derived from the Tagalog 'Maynilà,' meaning 'where indigo is found.'
