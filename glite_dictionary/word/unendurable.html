unendurable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪnˈdʊrəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈdjʊərəbəl / (BrE)
So unpleasant or difficult that it is impossible to tolerate or endure.
impossible to tolerate or endure

Examples

The pain became unendurable and she had to seek medical help.
After hours in the unendurable heat, the players finally took a break.
His arrogance made working with him completely unendurable.

Synset Examples

an intolerable degree of sentimentality

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intolerable - too bad or difficult to endure
  • Antonym: tolerable - able to be tolerated or endured

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'endurable' (from Latin *indurare* ‘to harden’).
