pentecostal

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɛn.tɪˈkɑː.stəl / (AmE), / ˌpɛn.tɪˈkɒs.təl / (BrE)
of or relating to the festival of Pentecost or to events occurring at Pentecost.
relating to Pentecost festival

Examples

The pentecostal feast is celebrated fifty days after Easter Sunday.
Many churches hold special pentecostal services to commemorate the descent of the Holy Spirit.
The story has pentecostal significance in Christian tradition.

Etymology

From Pentecost, with the adjectival suffix -al; Pentecost from Greek *pentēkostē* (fiftieth day).
