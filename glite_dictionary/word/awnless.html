awnless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔnləs / (AmE), / ˈɔːnləs / (BrE)
(of a plant, especially a grass) lacking awns or having only very short awns.
without awns (botanical)

Examples

The new cultivar is an awnless variety favored for easier harvesting.
Some awnless bromegrass species are cultivated for forage.
Researchers compared the yields of awnless and awned wheats in different climates.

Synset Examples

awnless bromegrass

Related Terms

  • Antonym: awned - having awns; with projecting bristles

Etymology

Formed from "awn" + suffix "-less" (without).
