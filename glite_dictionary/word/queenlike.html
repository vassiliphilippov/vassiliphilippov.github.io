queenlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈkwiːnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈkwiːnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Having the qualities, appearance, or rank of a queen; resembling or suitable for a queen.
resembling or befitting a queen

Examples

Her queenlike manner impressed everyone at the gala.
The statue captured her queenlike grace and dignity.
He treated her with queenlike respect throughout the ceremony.

Synset Examples

queenly propriety
clad in her queenly raiment
Rosetti's queenly portraits of women

Related Terms

  • Synonym: queenly - having the qualities or dignity of a queen

Etymology

Formed from 'queen' + 'like', meaning having the likeness of a queen.
