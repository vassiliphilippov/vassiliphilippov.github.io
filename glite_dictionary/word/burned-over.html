burned-over

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɝːnd ˌoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːnd ˌəʊvə / (BrE)
(of an area) destroyed or severely damaged by fire.
destroyed by fire

Examples

The firefighters surveyed the burned-over land after the wildfire had passed.
Researchers are studying how quickly vegetation returns to a burned-over forest.
The area was declared unsafe due to unstable trees in the burned-over zone.

Synset Examples

a row of burned houses
a charred bit of burnt wood
a burned-over site in the forest
barricaded the street with burnt-out cars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charred - burned and blackened by fire
  • Synonym: scorched - damaged or marked by burning
  • Antonym: intact - not damaged or destroyed
