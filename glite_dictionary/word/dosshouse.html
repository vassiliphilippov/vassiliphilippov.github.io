dosshouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑsˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈdɒs.haʊs / (BrE)
A cheap lodging house, often offering very basic accommodation for people with little money, especially overnight.
cheap lodging house for people with little money
british informal

Examples

He spent the night in a dosshouse after losing his job.
Many people without permanent homes turn to a dosshouse for shelter.
The old dosshouse was eventually shut down by the council.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flophouse - a cheap hotel or lodging house, especially for homeless people

Etymology

From British slang 'doss' (to sleep, especially rough) + 'house'.
