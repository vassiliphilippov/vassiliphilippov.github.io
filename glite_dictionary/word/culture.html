culture

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
The ideas, customs, and social behavior of a particular people or society.
shared ideas and customs in a society

Examples

Respect for elders is an important part of Japanese culture.
The company's culture encourages innovation and collaboration among employees.
Studying abroad exposed her to a completely different culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: civilization - an advanced stage of social development and organization
  • Degree Lower: subculture - a cultural group within a larger culture
  • Synonym: ethos - the characteristic spirit and beliefs of a community

Etymology

From Latin cultura (cultivation, tending), from colere (to till, cultivate).
