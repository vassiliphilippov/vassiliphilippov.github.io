Asio

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ.si.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.sɪ.əʊ / (BrE)
A genus of owls found in Europe and other parts of the world, including species such as the long-eared owl.
genus of owls

Examples

The Asio genus includes several well-known species such as the long-eared and short-eared owls.
Unlike other owls, members of the Asio genus have prominent ear tufts.
Research on Asio has contributed to our understanding of nocturnal bird adaptation.

Etymology

From Latin Asio (a type of horned owl), used in scientific taxonomy.
