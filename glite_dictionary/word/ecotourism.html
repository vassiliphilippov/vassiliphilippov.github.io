ecotourism

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːkoʊˌtʊrɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈiːkəʊˌtʊərɪzəm / (BrE)
Tourism directed towards exotic, often threatened, natural environments, intended to support conservation efforts and observe wildlife.
tourism focused on nature and conservation

Examples

Many travelers choose ecotourism to experience untouched wilderness and help protect endangered species.
The company specializes in ecotourism packages to the Amazon rainforest.
Growing interest in conservation has led to a rise in ecotourism around the globe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sustainable tourism - tourism that minimizes environmental impact and supports local communities

Etymology

Formed from 'eco-', referring to ecology or the environment, and 'tourism'.
