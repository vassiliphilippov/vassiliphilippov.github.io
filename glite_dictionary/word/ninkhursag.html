Ninkhursag

1. PROPN

/ nɪnˈkʊr.sɑg / (AmE), / nɪŋˈkʊː.sæɡ / (BrE)
an ancient Sumerian mother goddess associated with fertility, creation, and mountains, also known as Aruru, Mama, or Nintu
ancient Sumerian mother goddess

Examples

Ancient texts describe Ninkhursag as one of the creators of humanity in Sumerian myth.
Many temples were dedicated to Ninkhursag as a symbol of fertility and protection.
Scholars often compare Ninkhursag to other mother goddesses from early civilizations.

Etymology

From Sumerian *Nin* (lady) + *ḫur.sag* (mountain), meaning 'Lady of the Mountain.'
