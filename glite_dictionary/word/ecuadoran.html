Ecuadoran

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛkwəˈdɔrən / (AmE), / ˌɛkwəˈdɔːrən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Ecuador.
native or inhabitant of Ecuador

Examples

An Ecuadoran was interviewed about life in Quito.
The team included both locals and an Ecuadoran living abroad.
Many Ecuadorans celebrate their cultural heritage with pride.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ecuadorian - native or inhabitant of Ecuador

Etymology

From Ecuador + -an, a suffix used to form demonyms.
