handwoven

1. ADJ

/ ˌhændˈwoʊ.vən / (AmE), / ˌhændˈwəʊ.vən / (BrE)
(of fabric or textiles) made by hand on a loom rather than by machine.
made by hand on a loom

Examples

She proudly displayed the handwoven rug she bought at the market.
The artist specializes in handwoven scarves made from natural fibers.
A handwoven basket can add a rustic touch to your home decor.

Synset Examples

handwoven tablecloth

Related Terms

  • Antonym: machine-made - made using a machine, especially as opposed to by hand

Etymology

Formed from 'hand' + 'woven', indicating a textile woven by hand.
