tachygraphy

1. NOUN

/ təˈkɪɡrəfi / (AmE), / takˈɪɡrəfi / (BrE)
A method of writing rapidly by means of abbreviated symbols or shorthand systems.
rapid writing by abbreviation or shorthand

Examples

Historians study ancient tachygraphy to decipher shorthand manuscripts from past centuries.
Legal proceedings in some countries were once recorded using tachygraphy to keep up with rapid speech.
He mastered tachygraphy so he could take lecture notes more efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shorthand - method of rapid writing with symbols or abbreviations

Etymology

From Greek takhys (swift) + -graphy (writing).
