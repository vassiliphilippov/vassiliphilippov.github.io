Phoxinus phoxinus

1. NOUN

/ foʊˈksaɪnəs foʊˈksaɪnəs / (AmE), / fɒkˈsaɪnəs fɒkˈsaɪnəs / (BrE)
A very small European freshwater fish, commonly found in gravelly streams; also known as the common minnow.
small European freshwater fish (common minnow)

Examples

The population of Phoxinus phoxinus thrives in cool, gravel-bottomed European streams.
