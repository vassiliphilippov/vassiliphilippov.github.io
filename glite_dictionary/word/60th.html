60th

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪkstiəθ / (AmE), / ˈsɪkstiɪθ / (BrE)
being number sixty in a sequence; ordinal form of sixty
ordinal number for sixty

Examples

She finished in 60th place in the marathon.
This year marks the company's 60th anniversary.
The conference will be held on the 60th floor of the building.

Related Terms
