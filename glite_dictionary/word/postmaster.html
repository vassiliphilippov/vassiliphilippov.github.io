postmaster

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
the person who is in charge of a post office
person in charge of a post office

Examples

The postmaster greeted everyone warmly at the small town post office.
She applied for the position of postmaster after years of working as a clerk.
The retirement of the local postmaster was marked with a community celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: postmistress - female person in charge of a post office

Etymology

From post (mail) + master, first used in the 17th century.
