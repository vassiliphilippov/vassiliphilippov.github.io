prodromic

1. ADJ

/ proʊˈdroʊmɪk / (AmE), / prəʊˈdrəʊmɪk / (BrE)
relating to symptoms that indicate the beginning of a disease or an attack
relating to onset symptoms of illness
medical

Examples

The patient experienced prodromic headaches before the migraine began.
Certain rashes can be prodromic signs of viral infections.
Doctors pay close attention to prodromic symptoms to prevent complications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prodromal - relating to the early symptoms indicating the onset of a disease

Etymology

From Greek prodromikos, from prodromos 'forerunner'.
