twenty-five

1. NUM

/ ˈtwɛnti faɪv / (AmE), / ˈtwɛnti faɪv / (BrE)
the number 25; the number equal to twenty-four plus one
the number 25

Examples

He received twenty-five votes in the election.
There are twenty-five students in the class this semester.
Her parents have been married for twenty-five years.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-four - the number 24
  • Degree Higher: twenty-six - the number 26

Etymology

From Old English 'twēntig' (twenty) and 'fīf' (five).
