locule

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.kjuːl / (AmE), / ˈlɒ.kjuːl / (BrE)
a small cavity or compartment, especially within an organ in a plant or animal
small cavity in a plant or animal organ
medical

Examples

The seed is contained within a locule of the fruit.
Each ovary consists of several locules filled with developing seeds.
Scientists examined the locule structure to determine the species of the plant.

Etymology

From Latin *loculus* (a small compartment or place).
