NEbN

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛn iː bɪ ˈɛn / (AmE), / ˌɛn iː bɪ ˈɛn / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point north of northeast, equal to 33.75° on the compass.
compass point between NE and NNE

Examples

The ship changed course to NEbN to avoid the storm and stay on schedule.
He marked their route at NEbN on the navigational chart.
Sailors must know how to distinguish NEbN from similar compass points in poor visibility.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: northeast - the compass point midway between north and east
  • Degree Higher: north-northeast - the compass point between north and northeast

Etymology

Abbreviation for 'northeast by north'.
