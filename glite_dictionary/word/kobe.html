Kobe

1. PROPN

/ ˈkoʊbeɪ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊbeɪ / (BrE)
a city and port on Osaka Bay in southern Honshu, Japan, known for its vibrant economy and severely damaged by an earthquake in 1995
Japanese city and port on Osaka Bay

Examples

The city of Kobe is famous for its scenic harbor and cosmopolitan atmosphere.
After the 1995 earthquake, Kobe underwent significant reconstruction to restore its infrastructure.
Many tourists visit Kobe to enjoy its acclaimed beef and waterfront sights.
