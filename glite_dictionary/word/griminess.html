griminess

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡraɪmi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪmi.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being covered with dirt or grime; dirtiness.
state of being dirty or grimy

Examples

The griminess of the abandoned building made it uninviting to visitors.
After hours of scrubbing, the griminess finally disappeared from the kitchen tiles.
Despite its griminess, the old workshop was still full of character.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grimy - covered with or characterized by grime; dirty
  • Antonym: cleanliness - the state of being clean, free from dirt or impurities
  • Synonym: dirtiness - the state of being dirty

Etymology

From 'grimy' (dirty) with the suffix '-ness' forming a noun.
