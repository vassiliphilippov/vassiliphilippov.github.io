aquicultural

1. ADJ

/ ˌækwɪˈkʌltʃərəl / (AmE), / ˌakwɪˈkʌltʃərəl / (BrE)
Relating to the cultivation of aquatic plants or the farming of fish and other aquatic organisms.
relating to aquaculture or aquatic farming

Examples

The new regulations require all aquicultural facilities to monitor their waste output carefully.
Advancements in aquicultural technology have increased fish yields around the world.
She studied aquicultural practices during her internship at the marine research center.

Synset Examples

aquacultural methods
hydroponic lettuce

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aquacultural - pertaining to aquaculture; alternate spelling
  • Degree Lower: agricultural - relating to agriculture

Etymology

From Latin 'aqua' (water) + 'cultural', from 'culture' (cultivation).
