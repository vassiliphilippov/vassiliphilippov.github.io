Burma

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɝːmə / (AmE), / ˈbɜːmə / (BrE)
the former official name of Myanmar, a mountainous country in southeast Asia on the Bay of Bengal
former name of Myanmar (country in southeast Asia)

Examples

He traveled through Burma while it was still closed to most tourists.
Tea is an important crop in Burma's hill regions.
Many people still refer to Myanmar as Burma.

Synset Examples

much opium is grown in Myanmar

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Myanmar - current official name of the country formerly known as Burma

Etymology

From the Burmese name 'Bamar', referring to the dominant ethnic group.
