vlogger

1. NOUN

/ ˈvlɑːɡər / (AmE), / ˈvlɒɡə / (BrE)
a person who creates and regularly posts video blogs (vlogs) to share on the internet
person who creates video blogs

Examples

The popular vlogger shared her latest travel adventures on her YouTube channel.
Many aspiring filmmakers start out as vloggers before moving on to larger projects.
He became a full-time vlogger after his first video went viral.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blogger - a person who writes and manages a blog, usually text-based
  • Synonym: vlog - a video blog; a blog in video format

Etymology

Blend of 'video' and 'blogger'.
