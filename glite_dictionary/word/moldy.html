moldy

1. ADJ

/ ˈmoʊl.di / (AmE), / ˈməʊl.di / (BrE)
covered with or smelling of mold, typically showing signs of decay or mildew
covered with or smelling of mold

Examples

The cheese in the fridge turned moldy after being left out for weeks.
She threw away the moldy bread before anyone could eat it.
A moldy smell drifted from the damp basement.

Synset Examples

moldy bread
a moldy (or musty) odor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musty - having a stale, moldy, or damp smell

Etymology

From English "mold" (fungus) + "-y" (adjective suffix).
