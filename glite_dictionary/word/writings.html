Writings

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪŋz / (BrE)
the third of the three main divisions of the Hebrew Scriptures, comprising Psalms, Proverbs, Job, and other books; also called the Ketuvim.
third division of the Hebrew Scriptures (Ketuvim)

Examples

Many of the poetic books of the Hebrew Bible are included in the Writings.
Scholars analyze the Writings alongside the Torah and the Prophets to understand biblical themes.
The Book of Psalms is part of the Writings section in the Jewish Tanakh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ketuvim - the Hebrew name for the Writings section of the Hebrew Scriptures
  • Degree Lower: Prophets - the second division of the Hebrew Scriptures
  • Degree Lower: Torah - the first division of the Hebrew Scriptures
