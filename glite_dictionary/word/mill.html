mill

1. VERB

/ mɪl / (AmE), / mɪl / (BrE)
to shape or finish metal by passing it through a rolling machine
shape metal with a rolling machine

Examples

The steel plates are milled to achieve the desired thickness.
After casting, the metal is milled for a smooth finish.
This factory mills aluminum sheets for use in the automotive industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roll - to shape metal or other material by passing it through rollers

Etymology

From Middle English 'milne' (mill for grinding), later applied to industrial processes involving machines.
