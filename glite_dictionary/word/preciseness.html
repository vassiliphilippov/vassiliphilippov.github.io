preciseness

1. NOUN

/ prɪˈsaɪsnəs / (AmE), / prɪˈsaɪsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being exact, accurate, or clearly defined
the quality of being precise or exact

Examples

The preciseness of her calculations impressed the entire team.
He admired the preciseness with which the craftsman built the model ship.
Clear communication relies on the preciseness of the information shared.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: precision - the quality of being exact and accurate

Etymology

From the adjective 'precise' + the suffix '-ness'.
