squeezable

1. ADJ

/ ˈskwizəbəl / (AmE), / ˈskwiːzəbl / (BrE)
Able to be easily compressed, pressed, or shaped by squeezing.
able to be easily compressed

Examples

This bottle is squeezable, making it easy to dispense ketchup quickly.
The child picked up a soft, squeezable toy from the playroom floor.
Look for squeezable containers if you want to avoid spills in the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compressible - Able to be pressed into a smaller space; capable of compression.

Etymology

From squeeze + -able, patterned after similar English formations.
