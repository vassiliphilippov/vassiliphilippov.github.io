pedaler

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛdələr / (AmE), / ˈpɛdlə / (BrE)
A person who rides a bicycle or other pedal-driven vehicle.
person who rides a bicycle

Examples

The lead pedaler broke away from the group during the final lap.
Every experienced pedaler knows the importance of a well-maintained bike.
As a daily pedaler, she enjoys her morning commute through the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cyclist - person who rides a bicycle

Etymology

Formed from 'pedal' + '-er', denoting someone who pedals.
