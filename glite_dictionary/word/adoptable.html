adoptable

1. ADJ

/ əˈdɑptəbəl / (AmE), / əˈdɒptəbl / (BrE)
suitable or eligible to be adopted, especially by law or under official standards.
suitable for adoption

Examples

The shelter had several adoptable puppies looking for new homes.
Due to her friendly temperament, she was quickly deemed adoptable by the agency.
There is a growing list of children classified as adoptable by social services.

Synset Examples

a shortage of adoptable babies

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unadoptable - not suitable or eligible for adoption

Etymology

From 'adopt' + suffix '-able', indicating capability or suitability.
