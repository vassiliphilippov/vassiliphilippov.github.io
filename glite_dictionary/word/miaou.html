miaou

1. NOUN

/ miˈaʊ / (AmE), / miˈaʊ / (BrE)
the characteristic sound made by a cat, or any sound similar to this
sound made by a cat

Examples

The little kitten gave a plaintive miaou as it waited by the door.
A soft miaou woke me from my nap.
He found the stray cat by following its constant miaou.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meow - sound made by a cat; standard English form
  • Synonym: mew - high-pitched cry of a cat or kitten

Etymology

From French 'miaou', imitative of a cat's cry.

WordNet ID: oewn-07401214-n

2. VERB

/ miˈaʊ / (AmE), / miˈaʊ / (BrE)
(of a cat) to make a sound characteristic of a cat; to meow.
make a cat-like sound

Examples

The little kitten began to miaou softly at the door.
All night long, stray cats would miaou under my window.
She could hear her pet miaou for attention from the hallway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meow - make a cat-like sound

Etymology

From French 'miaou', imitative of a cat's cry.
