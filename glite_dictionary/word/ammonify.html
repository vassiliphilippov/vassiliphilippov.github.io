to ammonify

1. VERB

/ əˈmɑːnəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / əˈmɒnɪˌfaɪ / (BrE)
to treat with ammonia or cause to undergo ammonification
treat with ammonia; undergo ammonification

Examples

The laboratory decided to ammonify the soil sample before further analysis.
Certain bacteria have the ability to ammonify organic nitrogen compounds as part of the nitrogen cycle.
We must ammonify the solution carefully to avoid unwanted reactions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to nitrify - to treat with or convert into nitrates or nitrites

Etymology

From ammonia + -ify (to make or become).
