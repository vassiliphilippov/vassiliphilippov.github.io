Wolverine

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊlvəriːn / (AmE), / ˈwʊlvəriːn / (BrE)
a native or resident of the U.S. state of Michigan, especially as a nickname or informal reference
person from Michigan (U.S.)
informal american

Examples

Many people in the crowd were proud to call themselves Wolverines.
She's a Wolverine by birth, having grown up in Detroit.
During college football season, you can always spot a Wolverine fan by their gear.

Etymology

Refers to the nickname for Michigan, the 'Wolverine State'.
