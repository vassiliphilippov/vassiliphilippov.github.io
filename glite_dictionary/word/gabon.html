Gabon

1. PROPN

/ ɡəˈbɑn / (AmE), / ɡəˈbɒn / (BrE)
a country located on the west coast of Central Africa, known officially as the Gabonese Republic
country in Central Africa

Examples

She traveled to Gabon to study rainforest conservation.
Oil exports are a major contributor to Gabon's economy.
Gabon gained independence from France in 1960.

Etymology

From French 'Gabon', derived from the Portuguese 'Gabão' (meaning 'cloak'), referring to the shape of the Komo River estuary.
