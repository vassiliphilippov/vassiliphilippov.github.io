rhymeless

1. ADJ

/ ˈraɪm.lɪs / (AmE), / ˈraɪm.lɪs / (BrE)
(of verse or poetry) not having rhyme; lacking rhyming lines or sound patterns.
lacking rhyme (in poetry)

Examples

She prefers to write rhymeless poetry because it feels more natural to her.
Many modern poets experiment with rhymeless verse to explore new forms of expression.
While some criticize rhymeless poems for lacking structure, others praise their freedom.

Synset Examples

writing unrhymed blank verse is like playing tennis without a net

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unrhymed - (of verse or poetry) not having rhyme
  • Antonym: rhymed - (of verse) having lines which end in rhyme

Etymology

From 'rhyme' + '-less' (without).
