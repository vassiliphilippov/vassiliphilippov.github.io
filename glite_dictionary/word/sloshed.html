sloshed

1. ADJ

/ slɑʃt / (AmE), / slɒʃt / (BrE)
very drunk; heavily intoxicated with alcohol (informal)
very drunk (informal)
informal

Examples

He was completely sloshed by the end of the party.
Don't drive if you're sloshed, it's dangerous and illegal.
We found him sloshed and singing karaoke at the bar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drunk - slightly or very intoxicated with alcohol
  • Antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol; not drunk

Etymology

Probably from the verb 'slosh' (to splash about), suggesting being unsteady as if sloshing liquid.
