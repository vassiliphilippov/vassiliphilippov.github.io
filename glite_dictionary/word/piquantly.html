piquantly

1. ADV

/ ˈpiːkwəntli / (AmE), / ˈpiːkwəntli / (BrE)
In a way that is pleasantly spicy or stimulating to the taste, or interesting and exciting.
in a pleasantly spicy or stimulating manner

Examples

The chef seasoned the dish piquantly, adding just the right amount of pepper.
Her comments were piquantly delivered, keeping everyone amused and attentive.
The salsa was piquantly flavored, making it a favorite at the party.

Synset Examples

the soup was spicily flavored

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spicily - in a spicy or piquant manner

Etymology

From the adjective 'piquant', from French 'piquant' (pricking, stimulating).
