brotula

1. NOUN

/ broʊˈtuːlə / (AmE), / brəʊˈtjuːlə / (BrE)
a deep-sea fish belonging to the family Ophidiidae, typically found in tropical and subtropical waters
a type of deep-sea fish

Examples

The brotula is often found at great depths in the Atlantic Ocean.
Scientists discovered a new species of brotula off the coast of Indonesia.
The elongated body of the brotula helps it navigate through narrow crevices.

Etymology

From New Latin 'Brotula', the genus name.
