antler-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈæntlər laɪk / (AmE), / ˈæntlə laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having characteristics of antlers, especially in shape or appearance
resembling antlers

Examples

The coral's antler-like branches provided shelter for many fish.
He wore an antler-like headdress for the festival parade.
Some fungi display antler-like growths that make them easy to identify.
