Citrus limetta

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪtrəs lɪˈmɛtə / (AmE), / ˈsɪtrəs lɪˈmɛtə / (BrE)
a species of small tree in the citrus family, known for its sweet or mildly flavored fruit commonly called sweet lime
a species of citrus tree (sweet lime)

Examples

Citrus limetta is widely cultivated in India and the Middle East for its refreshing fruit.
Juice extracted from Citrus limetta is often used in beverages and traditional remedies.
Unlike other limes, the fruit of Citrus limetta is sweet and low in acidity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweet lime - common name for Citrus limetta fruit
  • Degree Higher: lemon - a related citrus fruit but typically sour

Etymology

From Latin genus name Citrus and specific epithet limetta, from Italian 'limetta' (little lime).
