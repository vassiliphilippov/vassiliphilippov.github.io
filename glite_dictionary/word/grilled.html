grilled

1. ADJ

/ ɡrɪld / (AmE), / ɡrɪld / (BrE)
(of food) cooked over direct heat on a grill, usually outdoors.
cooked over a grill

Examples

She ordered the grilled chicken sandwich with a side salad.
The chef recommended the grilled vegetables as a healthy option.
We enjoyed grilled steaks at the family barbecue last weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broiled - cooked with direct heat, often in an oven or under a grill (AmE)

2. ADJ

/ ɡrɪld / (AmE), / ɡrɪld / (BrE)
(of food) cooked over direct radiant heat, especially on a grill.
cooked over direct heat

Examples

She ordered a grilled chicken sandwich with a side of fries.
The vegetables tasted delicious after being grilled over an open flame.
Last night, we enjoyed a perfectly grilled steak at the restaurant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roasted - (of food) cooked by dry heat, especially in an oven
  • Synonym: baked - (of food) cooked by dry heat in an oven
  • Antonym: fried - (of food) cooked in hot fat or oil

Etymology

From the verb 'grill', ultimately from Old French 'grille' (grating, grill).
