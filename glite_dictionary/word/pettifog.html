to pettifog

1. VERB

/ ˈpɛtiˌfɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈpɛtɪfɒɡ / (BrE)
to argue or quibble over trivial or petty matters, especially in a petty or dishonest way
argue over trivial matters

Examples

The committee wasted time by pettifogging over minor details in the proposal.
Please don't pettifog when there are more important issues to discuss.
Instead of making progress, the teams pettifogged about inconsequential rules.

Synset Examples

Let's not quibble over pennies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quibble - to argue or raise objections about a trivial matter

Etymology

From petty (small, trivial) + fog (to bicker quarrelsomely); originally referring to unscrupulous lawyers handling petty cases.
