gunsmith

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌnˌsmɪθ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌn.smɪθ / (BrE)
a person who makes, modifies, or repairs firearms
a person who makes or repairs guns

Examples

The old gunsmith repaired my grandfather's hunting rifle with great care.
After years as a gunsmith, she opened her own workshop in the city center.
You should consult a professional gunsmith before attempting to modify your firearm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armorer - a person who makes or repairs arms or armor
  • Synonym: gunsmithing - the activity of making or repairing firearms

Etymology

From 'gun' + 'smith', modeled after 'blacksmith', first recorded in the 17th century.
