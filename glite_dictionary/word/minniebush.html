minniebush

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪniˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɪniˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
a low shrub native to the eastern United States, known for its downy twigs
a low shrub with downy twigs

Examples

The minniebush is often found growing on rocky hillsides in the Appalachian region.
Gardeners appreciate the minniebush for its attractive foliage and hardy nature.
Hikers sometimes notice the soft, velvety twigs of the minniebush along trails in the eastern forests.
