to spang

1. VERB

/ spæŋ / (AmE), / spæŋ / (BrE)
(of a projectile) to strike or ricochet with a sudden sharp sound or movement.
to hit or ricochet with a sharp noise

Examples

The bullet spanged off the metal post, leaving a visible dent.
Rocks spanged across the frozen lake as we skipped them in the chilly air.
A shot spanged against the hull, echoing throughout the valley.

Synset Examples

Bullets spanged into the trees

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bang - to make a sudden loud noise; to strike loudly
  • Synonym: ricochet - to bounce off a surface at an angle
