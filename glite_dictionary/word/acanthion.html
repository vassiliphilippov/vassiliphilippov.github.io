acanthion

1. NOUN

/ əˈkænθiən / (AmE), / əˈkanθɪən / (BrE)
the anatomical point at the tip of the anterior nasal spine, located at the front end of the intermaxillary suture
craniometric point at tip of anterior nasal spine
medical

Examples

During the facial measurement, the technician identified the acanthion as a key reference point.
Accurate localization of the acanthion is essential in orthodontic and craniofacial surgery.
On the X-ray image, the surgeon marked the acanthion to guide the procedure.

Etymology

From Greek akanthion (little thorn), diminutive of akantha (thorn, spine).
