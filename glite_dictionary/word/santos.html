Santos

1. PROPN

/ ˈsænˌtoʊs / (AmE), / ˈsan.tɒs / (BrE)
a port city in southeastern Brazil, located on an offshore island near São Paulo, known for its major port and beachfront.
city and major port in southeastern Brazil

Examples

The coffee arrived at the docks in Santos before being shipped worldwide.
Tourists flock to Santos for its beautiful beaches and vibrant culture.
Many goods exported from Brazil pass through the port of Santos.
