schmeer

1. NOUN

/ ʃmɪr / (AmE), / ʃmɪə / (BrE)
(informal) A collection or assortment of things, especially those that go together or are considered as a whole.
collection or assortment of things
informal

Examples

He ended up buying the whole schmeer at the market.
When planning the party, she decided to order the full schmeer of decorations, food, and entertainment.
They brought the entire schmeer of tools needed for the job.

Synset Examples

he bought the whole schmeer

Etymology

From Yiddish שמיר (shmir), meaning 'spread' or 'smear', later extended to mean 'the whole lot'.
