Pashtu

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæʃtuː / (AmE), / ˈpæʃtuː / (BrE)
an Iranian language spoken mainly in Afghanistan and Pakistan, and the official language of Afghanistan
an Iranian language of Afghanistan and Pakistan

Examples

She is fluent in both Dari and Pashtu.
Many official documents in Afghanistan are written in Pashtu.
He decided to study Pashtu to understand Afghan culture better.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pashto - an Iranian language spoken in Afghanistan and Pakistan

Etymology

From Pashto پښتو (paʂˈto), the native name for the language.

2. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːʃtuː / (AmE), / ˈpæʃtuː / (BrE)
the Iranian language spoken mainly in Afghanistan and Pakistan, and the official language of Afghanistan
official language of Afghanistan

Examples

She learned to speak Pashtu during her time in Afghanistan.
Many government documents in Afghanistan are published in both Dari and Pashtu.
The poet recited his work in Pashtu at the cultural festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dari - the other official language of Afghanistan

Etymology

From Pashto پښتو (paṣto), the native name of the language.
