pinwheel

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪnˌwil / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌwiːl / (BrE)
A toy made of colored paper or plastic vanes attached to a stick, designed to spin when held in the wind.
spinning toy with colored vanes

Examples

The child laughed as the pinwheel spun quickly in the summer breeze.
Each guest received a colorful pinwheel at the outdoor party.
She bought a pinwheel at the fair to remind her of her childhood days.
