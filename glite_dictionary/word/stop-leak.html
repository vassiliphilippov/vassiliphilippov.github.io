stop-leak

1. NOUN

/ stɑpˌlik / (AmE), / stɒpˌliːk / (BrE)
a liquid product added to a radiator to seal minor leaks temporarily
liquid sealer for radiator leaks

Examples

He poured some stop-leak into the radiator to fix the leak temporarily.
Many mechanics advise against relying on stop-leak for serious cooling system problems.
Always follow the instructions when using stop-leak products in your car.

Synset Examples

Using stop leak is a quick solution to the problem, not a permanent repair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sealant - substance used to block the passage of fluids through the surface or joints
