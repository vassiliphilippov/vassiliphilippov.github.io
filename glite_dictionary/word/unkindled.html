unkindled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkɪndəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɪndld / (BrE)
Not set on fire; not ignited or made to burn.
not set on fire

Examples

The fireplace was filled with unkindled logs, awaiting the touch of a match.
Her hope remained unkindled despite the encouragement of her friends.
Rows of unkindled candles lined the shelves during the daytime.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: kindled - set on fire; ignited

Etymology

From un- (not) + kindle (to set alight).
