marina

1. NOUN

/ məˈɹiːnə / (AmE), / məˈriːnə / (BrE)
A specially designed harbor with moorings and facilities for small boats and yachts.
harbor for yachts and small boats

Examples

We spent the night on our boat at the marina before setting sail.
A new marina has recently opened along the river, attracting many boating enthusiasts.
They keep their yacht docked at a private marina near the city center.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harbor - place on the coast where vessels may find shelter

Etymology

From Italian *marina* meaning 'seacoast', from Latin *marinus* ('of the sea').
