Athens

1. PROPN

/ ˈæθənz / (AmE), / ˈæθənz / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Greece, known for its ancient history and as the center of classical civilization
capital city of Greece

Examples

She studied classical architecture while living in Athens for two years.
The ancient ruins in Athens attract millions of tourists every year.
The Olympic Games were held in Athens in 2004.

Synset Examples

in the 5th century BC ancient Athens was the world's most powerful and civilized city

Etymology

Named after Athena, the patron goddess of the city in Greek mythology.
