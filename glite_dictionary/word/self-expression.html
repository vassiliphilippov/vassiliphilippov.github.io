self-expression

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlf ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
the expression of one's thoughts, feelings, or individuality, especially through creative activities such as art, music, or writing
expression of one's individuality

Examples

Art often provides a powerful avenue for self-expression.
Children need opportunities for self-expression at school.
Writing poetry became his main form of self-expression.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expression - the act of conveying thoughts or feelings
