exterritorial

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛkstɛˌrɪˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / ˌɛkstɛˌrɪˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
Situated or occurring outside the territorial limits or jurisdiction of a particular country or authority.
outside territorial limits or jurisdiction
legal

Examples

The embassy staff were granted exterritorial status under international law.
Certain areas may be considered exterritorial for diplomatic reasons.
He studied the impacts of exterritorial rights on maritime boundaries.

Synset Examples

fishing in extraterritorial waters
enjoying exterritorial privileges and rights

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extraterritorial - existing or taking place outside the territory or jurisdiction of a state

Etymology

From Latin ex- (out of) + territorial (of territory).
