unfermented

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.fərˈmɛn.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.fəˈmɛn.tɪd / (BrE)
(of food or drink) not having undergone fermentation; not soured or preserved by fermentation.
not having undergone fermentation

Examples

The grapes were pressed to make unfermented juice for children.
This tea is classified as unfermented, unlike black or oolong teas.
Fresh, unfermented milk has a sweeter taste and a shorter shelf life.

Synset Examples

sweet milk

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fermented - having undergone fermentation

Etymology

Formed from the prefix un- (not) and the past participle of ferment.
