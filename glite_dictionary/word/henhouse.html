henhouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛnˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈhɛnˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A small building on a farm where chickens or other poultry are kept.
small building for poultry

Examples

The farmer built a new henhouse to protect his chickens from predators.
Every morning, she collects eggs from the henhouse.
The old henhouse needed repairs before any poultry could be kept inside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken coop - a small enclosure for keeping chickens
  • Degree Higher: barn - a large farm building for storing grain or animals
