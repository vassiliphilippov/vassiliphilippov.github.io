goateed

1. ADJ

/ ɡoʊˈtid / (AmE), / ɡəʊˈtiːd / (BrE)
(of a person) having a small, pointed beard on the chin, resembling a goat's beard.
having a small, pointed chin beard

Examples

The goateed professor stood at the front of the lecture hall, gesturing enthusiastically.
She recognized him immediately by his goateed chin and warm smile.
The film's villain was goateed, adding to his sinister appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bearded - having a beard
  • Antonym: clean-shaven - having no beard or facial hair

Etymology

From 'goatee' (a small, pointed beard) + '-ed' suffix indicating possession.
