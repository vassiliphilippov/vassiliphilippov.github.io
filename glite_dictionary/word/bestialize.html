to bestialize

1. VERB

/ ˈbɛstiəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈbɛstɪəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make someone or something brutal, depraved, or resembling a beast; to deprive of human qualities
make brutal or animal-like

Examples

The prolonged conflict seemed to bestialize the soldiers, stripping them of empathy.
Centuries of oppression can bestialize entire communities, making compassion scarce.
This horror film attempts to bestialize its characters in the eyes of the audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to dehumanize - to deprive of human qualities or personality
  • Synonym: to brutalize - to make brutal or cruel

Etymology

From Latin 'bestia' (beast) + English '-ize'.
