Teresa of Avila

1. PROPN

/ təˈreɪzə əv ˈævɪlə / (AmE), / təˈreɪzə əv ˈævɪlə / (BrE)
(1515–1582) Spanish mystic, religious reformer, and author of spiritual classics; recognized as a saint in the Roman Catholic Church.
Spanish mystic and Christian saint (1515–1582)

Examples

Many pilgrims visit the birthplace of Teresa of Avila each year in Spain.
The writings of Teresa of Avila have influenced Christian mysticism for centuries.
She studied the life and teachings of Teresa of Avila as part of her theology course.
