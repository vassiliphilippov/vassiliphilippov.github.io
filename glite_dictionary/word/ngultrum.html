ngultrum

1. NOUN

/ ŋʊlˈtruːm / (AmE), / ŋʊlˈtruːm / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Bhutan
Bhutan's unit of currency

Examples

Travelers are advised to exchange their dollars for ngultrum upon arrival in Bhutan.
The shopkeeper quoted the price as 250 ngultrum for the souvenir.
Bhutan's economy relies on the ngultrum as its official currency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rupee - unit of currency in India and other countries

Etymology

From Dzongkha དངུལ་ཀྲམ་ (dngul-kram), Dzongkha for 'money' and 'silver'.
