Makarios III

1. PROPN

/ məˈkɑriˌoʊs ðə θɜrd / (AmE), / məˈkɑːriɒs ðə θɜːd / (BrE)
(1913–1977) Greek Orthodox bishop who became Archbishop of Cyprus and was the first president of independent Cyprus.
first president of independent Cyprus, 1913–1977

Examples

Makarios III played a crucial role in leading Cyprus to independence from British rule.
The presidency of Makarios III was marked by significant political and religious influence.
Many streets and schools in Cyprus are named after Makarios III in recognition of his contributions.
