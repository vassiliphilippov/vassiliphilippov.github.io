intersexual

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
Having sexual characteristics that are intermediate between those typically associated with male and female.
having intermediate male and female sexual characteristics
medical

Examples

Some plants are classified as intersexual because they display both male and female traits.
The researchers studied the genetics of intersexual fish in polluted rivers.
Medical practitioners are trained to understand intersexual conditions and provide appropriate care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hermaphroditic - having both male and female reproductive organs

Etymology

From Latin 'inter-' (between) and 'sexualis' (sexual).

2. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
Existing or occurring between the sexes.
existing or occurring between the sexes

Examples

The study focused on intersexual competition among different species.
Researchers observed intersexual behavior that was critical to the birds’ mating patterns.
Certain intersexual differences can influence disease susceptibility.

Etymology

From Latin 'inter-' (between) + 'sexual' (relating to sex).
