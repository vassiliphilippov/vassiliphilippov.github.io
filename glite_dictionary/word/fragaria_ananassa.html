Fragaria ananassa

1. NOUN

/ frəˈɡɛri.ə ˌæn.əˈnæs.ə / (AmE), / frəˈɡaː.ri.ə ˌæn.əˈnæs.ə / (BrE)
the cultivated species of strawberry widely grown for its edible fruit; commonly known as the garden strawberry
the cultivated garden strawberry

Examples

Fragaria ananassa is the species most commonly grown for commercial strawberry production worldwide.
The fruit of Fragaria ananassa is prized for its sweetness and bright red color.
Gardeners often select Fragaria ananassa varieties for their large, juicy strawberries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strawberry - the sweet, red, edible fruit of Fragaria plants

Etymology

From Latin; 'Fragaria' for strawberry genus, and 'ananassa' from Tupi 'ananas' (pineapple) due to its pineapple-like flavor note.
