sleepyhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈsliːpiˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈsliːpiˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person who is sleepy, especially one who tends to be slow to get up in the morning
a person who is sleepy

Examples

Come on, sleepyhead, it's time to get out of bed!
She called him a sleepyhead because he was always the last to wake up.
Don't be such a sleepyhead; we're going to be late.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sluggard - a lazy or sluggish person
  • Antonym: early bird - a person who rises early in the morning

Etymology

Formed from "sleepy" + "head," first recorded in the 19th century.
