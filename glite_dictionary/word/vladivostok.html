Vladivostok

1. PROPN

/ ˌvlɑːdɪˈvɑːstɑk / (AmE), / ˌvlædɪˈvɒstɒk / (BrE)
a major seaport and city in the Asian part of Russia, located near the Sea of Japan
Russian port city in Asia

Examples

The train journey on the Trans-Siberian Railway ends in Vladivostok.
Many ships dock at Vladivostok every day due to its strategic location.
The port of Vladivostok plays a key role in Russia's maritime trade.

Etymology

From Russian Владивосток (Vladivostok), meaning 'rule the east' (владеть, to rule + восток, east).
