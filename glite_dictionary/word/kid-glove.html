kid-glove

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɪd ɡlʌv / (AmE), / ˈkɪd ɡlʌv / (BrE)
Displaying carefulness, tact, or sensitivity, especially in dealing with people or delicate matters.
tactful or sensitive handling

Examples

The coach chose a kid-glove approach when giving feedback to the new players.
The issue was so sensitive that the committee handled it with kid-glove care.
The company received kid-glove treatment from the regulators compared to other firms.

Synset Examples

by diplomatic conduct he avoided antagonizing anyone
a tactful way of correcting someone
the agency got the kid-glove treatment on Capitol Hill

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tactful - showing sensitivity in dealing with others
  • Synonym: diplomatic - having or showing an ability to deal with people in a sensitive way
  • Antonym: harsh - cruel or severe in tone or treatment

Etymology

Originally referring to gloves made of kid leather, prized for their softness and delicacy, later used metaphorically for gentle handling.
