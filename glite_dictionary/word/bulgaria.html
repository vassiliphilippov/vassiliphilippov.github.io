Bulgaria

1. PROPN

/ bʌlˈɡɛriə / (AmE), / bʌlˈɡɛːrɪə / (BrE)
a country in southeastern Europe, occupying the eastern part of the Balkan Peninsula
country in southeastern Europe

Examples

She spent her summer vacation traveling across Bulgaria with friends.
Bulgaria joined the European Union in 2007.
The capital city of Bulgaria is Sofia.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'Bulgaria', from the name of the Bulgar people.
