penniless

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛnɪləs / (AmE), / ˈpɛnɪlɪs / (BrE)
Having no money; very poor.
having no money

Examples

After losing his job, Tom found himself penniless and unable to pay rent.
The once-successful actor died penniless in a small, rented room.
She gave away her inheritance and lived penniless for many years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: destitute - without the basic necessities of life
  • Synonym: impoverished - poor, lacking money or resources
  • Antonym: wealthy - having a great deal of money, resources, or assets

Etymology

From 'penny' + '-less', meaning 'without a penny'.
