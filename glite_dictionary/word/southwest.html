Southwest

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaʊθˈwɛst / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈwɛst / (BrE)
the southwestern region of the United States, typically including New Mexico, Arizona, Texas, California, and sometimes Utah and Colorado
southwestern region of the US
american

Examples

Many artists are inspired by the vast desert landscapes of the Southwest.
She moved to the Southwest for its warm climate and rich cultural heritage.
The cuisine of the Southwest features distinctive flavors influenced by Native American and Mexican traditions.
