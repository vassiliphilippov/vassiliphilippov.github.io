southwest

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaʊθˈwɛst / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈwɛst / (BrE)
The direction midway between south and west on the compass; 225° clockwise from north.
direction between south and west

Examples

The storm was moving rapidly toward the southwest.
They built their house facing the southwest to catch the evening sun.
We set out on our journey in the direction of the southwest.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: northeast - the direction between north and east on the compass
  • Degree Lower: southeast - the direction between south and east on the compass
  • Degree Higher: west - the cardinal direction at 270° on the compass
