triskaidekaphobia

1. NOUN

/ ˌtrɪskaɪˌdɛkəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌtrɪskaɪˌdiːkəˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
An irrational or extreme fear of the number 13.
fear of the number 13
medical

Examples

Some people with triskaidekaphobia refuse to live on the 13th floor of a building.
His triskaidekaphobia caused him to avoid making any important decisions on the 13th of the month.
The hotel skipped room number 13 to accommodate guests with triskaidekaphobia.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: phobia - an extreme or irrational fear of or aversion to something

Etymology

From Greek 'tris' (three) + 'deka' (ten) + 'phobia' (fear).
