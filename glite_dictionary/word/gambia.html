Gambia

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡæmbiə / (AmE), / ˈɡæmbiə / (BrE)
a West African country, a narrow republic almost entirely surrounded by Senegal, with a short Atlantic coastline
country in West Africa, surrounded by Senegal

Examples

The Gambia declared its independence from Britain in 1965.
She traveled to the Gambia to volunteer in a local community project.
The River Gambia flows through the heart of the country.
