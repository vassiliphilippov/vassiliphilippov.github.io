to mudwrestle

1. VERB

/ ˈmʌdˌrɛsəl / (AmE), / ˈmʌdˌrɛsəl / (BrE)
to wrestle in mud, typically as a spectacle or form of entertainment
to wrestle in mud for entertainment

Examples

Every summer, the festival features a group of professionals who mudwrestle for the crowd's enjoyment.
She never imagined she would one day mudwrestle on live television.
Two of the competitors agreed to mudwrestle for charity at the event.

Synset Examples

some people enjoy watching people who mudwrestle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrestle - to fight by holding and trying to force someone to the ground
