wrinkleproof

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪŋkəlˌpruːf / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋ.kl̩.pruːf / (BrE)
(of fabric or clothing) treated or manufactured so as to resist wrinkling and maintain a smooth appearance.
not easily wrinkled (of fabric)

Examples

This wrinkleproof fabric is perfect for travelers who want to pack light.
He bought a wrinkleproof shirt for his business trip.
Many school uniforms are made from wrinkleproof materials for easy care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrinkle-resistant - (of fabric) resistant to wrinkling

Etymology

Formed from 'wrinkle' + 'proof,' following the pattern of compounds meaning 'resistant to.'
