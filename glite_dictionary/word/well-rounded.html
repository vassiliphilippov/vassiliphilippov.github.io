well-rounded

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɛlˈraʊndɪd / (AmE), / ˌwɛlˈraʊndɪd / (BrE)
having a variety of experiences, abilities, or skills; balanced and comprehensive in development or character
having a range of skills or qualities

Examples

The university aims to produce well-rounded graduates who are prepared for all aspects of life.
She is a well-rounded athlete, excelling in several different sports.
His well-rounded background makes him an excellent candidate for the role.

Synset Examples

an all-around athlete
a well-rounded curriculum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: versatile - able to do many different things well
  • Antonym: specialized - concentrated on a specific skill or area
