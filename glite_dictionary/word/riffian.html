Riffian

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪfiən / (AmE), / ˈrɪfiən / (BrE)
a member of a Berber ethnic group living in the Rif region of northern Morocco
a Berber from northern Morocco

Examples

Many Riffians speak the Tamazight language as their mother tongue.
The Riffian revolt was a significant event in Moroccan history.
As a Riffian, she maintains traditional customs passed down through generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Berber - a member of the indigenous peoples of North Africa

Etymology

From 'Rif', the northern region of Morocco, with the English suffix '-ian'.
