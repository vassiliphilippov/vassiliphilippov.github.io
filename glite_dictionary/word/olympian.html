Olympian

1. ADJ

/ əˈlɪmpiən / (AmE), / əˈlɪmpiən / (BrE)
relating to the greater gods of ancient Greece, especially those believed to reside on Mount Olympus.
relating to the gods of Mount Olympus

Examples

The ancient Greeks honored the Olympian gods with elaborate temples and rituals.
She was fascinated by the myths surrounding the Olympian deities.
Statues of the Olympian pantheon adorned the entrance to the museum.

Synset Examples

Olympian deities

Related Terms

  • Antonym: chthonic - relating to gods or spirits of the underworld in ancient Greek mythology

Etymology

From Latin Olympianus, from Greek Olympios (of Olympus), referring to Mount Olympus, mythological abode of the gods.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03104642-a
Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ oʊˈlɪmpiən / (AmE), / əˈlɪmpiən / (BrE)
one of the twelve principal gods of ancient Greek mythology, who resided on Mount Olympus after defeating the Titans
Greek god dwelling on Mount Olympus

Examples

In Greek mythology, Zeus is considered an Olympian who rules the sky and thunder.
The ancient temple was dedicated to an Olympian revered by all the local people.
Many heroes and mortals sought the favor of an Olympian to aid them in their quests.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Titan - a member of the race of giant deities overthrown by the Olympian gods

Etymology

From Mount Olympus, the mythical home of the gods in Greek mythology.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09576476-n
Synset POS: n

3. NOUN

/ əˈlɪmpiən / (AmE), / əˈlɪmpiən / (BrE)
an athlete who competes in the Olympic Games
athlete in the Olympic Games

Examples

She trained for years to become an Olympian and represent her country.
The city welcomed the returning Olympian with a grand parade.
Every Olympian dreams of winning a gold medal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Paralympian - an athlete who competes in the Paralympic Games

Etymology

From Olympus, the site in ancient Greece; via French/Olympique, ultimately from Greek 'Olympos'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10396990-n
Synset POS: n

4. ADJ

/ oʊˈlɪmpiən / (AmE), / əˈlɪmpiən / (BrE)
of or relating to Olympia in Greece or its inhabitants; originating from the region of Olympia.
of Olympia (Greece) or its people

Examples

The famous Olympian plain is mentioned in many ancient texts.
Archaeologists discovered artifacts from the Olympian region.
They studied the Olympian customs that shaped early Greek civilization.

Synset Examples

Olympian plain

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Olympia - ancient Greek city, site of the Olympic Games

Etymology

From Olympia, a region in ancient Greece, site of the original Olympic Games.
