billystick

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪliˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɪliˌstɪk / (BrE)
a short, heavy club carried and used as a weapon by police officers
policeman's short, stout club

Examples

The officer gripped his billystick tightly as he approached the suspect.
Several billysticks were found in the abandoned police locker.
He was struck on the arm with a billystick during the altercation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: billy club - a short, heavy club used by police
  • Synonym: truncheon - a baton or club carried by police, especially in Britain

Etymology

From 'billy', of uncertain origin, possibly after the English slang for a policeman's club, and 'stick'.
