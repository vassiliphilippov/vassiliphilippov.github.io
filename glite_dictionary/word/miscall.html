to miscall

1. VERB

/ ˌmɪsˈkɔl / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈkɔːl / (BrE)
to call someone or something by an incorrect or inappropriate name
call by an incorrect name

Examples

He did not mind if people miscall him out of ignorance.
The island is often miscalled 'Treasure Island' by tourists.
They miscalled the tradition, giving it a name that holds a different meaning.

Synset Examples

These misnamed philanthropists

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to misname - to give a wrong or incorrect name to

Etymology

From Old English 'mis-' (wrong) + 'ceallian' (to call).
