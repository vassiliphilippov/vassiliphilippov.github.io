iron-grey

1. ADJ

/ ˈaɪərn ˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈaɪən ˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a dark grey color resembling that of iron.
dark grey color like iron

Examples

Her hair had turned iron-grey with age, giving her a distinguished look.
The overcast sky was an unbroken sheet of iron-grey for days.
He wore an iron-grey suit to the formal dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gray - of the color between black and white

2. NOUN

/ ˈaɪərn ɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈaɪən ɡreɪ / (BrE)
a dark shade of grey resembling the color of freshly broken cast iron
a dark grey color like cast iron

Examples

The suit was an elegant iron-grey that matched his reserved style.
Her hair had turned a distinguished iron-grey over the years.
The puppy's coat was a soft iron-grey flecked with white.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gray - the color between black and white; grey
  • Degree Higher: charcoal - a dark grey color
  • Degree Lower: slate - a medium-dark gray color
