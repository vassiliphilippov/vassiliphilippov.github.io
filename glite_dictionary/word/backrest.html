backrest

1. NOUN

/ ˈbækˌrɛst / (AmE), / ˈbæk.rɛst / (BrE)
a support at the back of a chair or seat for resting one's back while sitting
support for the back on a chair or seat

Examples

The new office chair has an adjustable backrest for extra comfort.
She leaned against the backrest and closed her eyes for a moment.
Most car seats feature a padded backrest to support your posture during long drives.

Synset Examples

the back of the dental chair was adjustable

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armrest - a support for the arms on a chair or seat

Etymology

Formed from 'back' + 'rest', denoting a rest for the back.
