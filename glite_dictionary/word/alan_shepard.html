Alan Shepard

1. PROPN

/ ˈælən ˈʃɛpərd / (AmE), / ˈælən ˈʃɛpəd / (BrE)
(1923–1998) American astronaut, naval officer, and test pilot who became the first American in space with his suborbital flight in 1961.
first American astronaut in space

Examples

Alan Shepard made history as the first American to travel into space.
The achievements of Alan Shepard inspired generations of astronauts.
A memorial was dedicated to Alan Shepard at the space museum.
