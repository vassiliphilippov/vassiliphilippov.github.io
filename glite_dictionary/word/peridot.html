peridot

1. NOUN

/ ˈper.ɪˌdɑt / (AmE), / ˈper.ɪ.dɒt / (BrE)
a transparent, pale green variety of olivine used as a gemstone
pale green gemstone

Examples

She wore a necklace with a peridot pendant that sparkled in the sunlight.
The pale green color of peridot makes it a popular choice for summer jewelry.
Geologists identified the stone as peridot, prized for its clarity and hue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: olivine - a mineral of which peridot is a gem-quality variety

Etymology

From French 'péridot', probably from Arabic 'farīdah' meaning 'gem'.
