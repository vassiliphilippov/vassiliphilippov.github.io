appro

1. NOUN

/ ˈæp.roʊ / (AmE), / ˈæp.rəʊ / (BrE)
an informal British abbreviation for approval, especially when goods are provided for approval before purchase
British abbreviation for approval (of goods)
informal british

Examples

The jeweler let her take the necklace on appro for the weekend.
We ordered the samples on appro before making a final decision.
The artwork was delivered on appro, so payment wasn’t required until we approved it.

Synset Examples

he accepted it on appro

Related Terms

  • Synonym: approval - the act of agreeing to or accepting something

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'approval'.
