instalike

1. VERB

/ ˈɪnstəˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɪnstəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
to instantly show approval for something on social media, especially by clicking a 'like' button soon after it is posted
instantly like something on social media
informal

Examples

She always instalikes her friends' photos within seconds of them being posted.
It's flattering when someone instalikes your story the moment it goes live.
He instaliked the announcement and then commented to show his support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: like - to express approval by clicking a 'like' button on social media

Etymology

Blend of 'instant' and 'like', modeled after social media terms such as Instagram 'like'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈɪnstəˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɪnstəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
something viewed as so good or appealing that it deserves an instant 'like' on social media platforms.
thing deserving instant like (social media)
informal

Examples

Her creative cake design was a total instalike among her friends on Instagram.
The puppy video was so adorable, it was an obvious instalike for everyone who watched it.
That travel photo is an instalike—the composition and colors are just perfect.

Etymology

Formed from 'instant' and 'like', referring to an immediate positive reaction on social media.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-90009142-n
Synset POS: n
