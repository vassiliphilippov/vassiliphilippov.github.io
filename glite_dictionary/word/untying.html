untying

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈtaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈtaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of loosening or removing a tie, knot, or fastening.
the act of loosening a tie or knot

Examples

He had trouble with the untying of his shoelaces after the long walk.
The magician amazed the crowd with his swift untying of complex knots.
Instructions for the package included careful steps for both tying and untying the ribbons.

Synset Examples

the tying of bow ties is an art; the untying is easy

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tying - the act of fastening or binding with a tie or knot
