husbandly

1. ADJ

/ ˈhʌzbəndli / (AmE), / ˈhʌzbəndli / (BrE)
Characteristic of or appropriate for a husband.
relating to a husband

Examples

He took on several husbandly responsibilities after they bought the house.
She admired his husbandly devotion and care for the family.
He approached his husbandly duties with diligence and patience.

Synset Examples

assumed husbandly duties like mowing the lawn

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wifely - characteristic of or appropriate for a wife

Etymology

From husband + -ly (adjectival suffix).

2. ADJ

/ ˈhʌzbəndli / (AmE), / ˈhʌzbəndli / (BrE)
befitting or characteristic of a husband; showing qualities typically associated with a husband
befitting or characteristic of a husband

Examples

He fulfilled his duties in a husbandly manner, always caring for his partner.
His husbandly affection was evident in the little things he did for his wife.
The novel describes his gentle yet firm husbandly guidance in their household.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wifely - befitting or characteristic of a wife

Etymology

From 'husband' + '-ly', following a regular English adjective formation.
