plush-like

1. ADJ

/ plʌʃˌlaɪk / (AmE), / plʌʃˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Having a texture or appearance that resembles plush fabric; soft and luxurious.
resembling plush fabric in texture

Examples

The plush-like material of the sofa made it incredibly comfortable to sit on.
She bought a plush-like rug to add warmth and softness to her bedroom.
The hotel’s plush-like towels gave guests a feeling of luxury.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plush - a rich fabric with a soft, thick, even pile
