Urga

1. PROPN

/ ˈɜrɡə / (AmE), / ˈɜːɡə / (BrE)
the historical name for Ulaanbaatar, the capital and largest city of Mongolia
historical name for Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia's capital

Examples

In the early 20th century, Urga was a crucial trading post in Mongolia.
The city now called Ulaanbaatar was once known as Urga.
Many historical records refer to Urga as the center of Mongolian politics and religion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ulaanbaatar - capital and largest city of Mongolia (modern name for Urga)

Etymology

From Mongolian 'ᠦᠷᠭ᠎ᠡ' (ürge/örge), meaning 'palace' or 'residence'.
