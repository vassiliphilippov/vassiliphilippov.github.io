Mexican

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛksɪkən / (AmE), / ˈmɛksɪkən / (BrE)
of or relating to Mexico, its people, or their language and culture.
of or relating to Mexico or its people

Examples

Many Americans enjoy Mexican cuisine for its vibrant flavors and spices.
She bought some Mexican pottery while visiting Oaxaca last year.
The festival featured traditional Mexican music and dance performances.

Synset Examples

Mexican food is hot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mexican - a person from Mexico

Etymology

From Mexico + -an, denoting origin or association.

/ ˈmɛksɪkən / (AmE), / ˈmɛksɪkən / (BrE)

Examples

My friend is a proud Mexican who was born and raised in Guadalajara.

Many Mexicans celebrate their country's independence on September 16th.

Related Terms
