vitalizing

1. ADJ

/ ˈvaɪtəˌlaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvaɪtəlaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
Giving or having the power to give life, energy, or spirit.
giving life or energy

Examples

The vitalizing effects of a morning run can last throughout the day.
Her speech had a vitalizing influence on the team’s morale.
Sunlight has a vitalizing effect on most living organisms.

Synset Examples

returning the life-giving humus to the land
life-giving love and praise
the vitalizing rays of the warming sun

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invigorating - giving strength or energy

Etymology

From Latin 'vita' (life) via 'vitalize' + '-ing'.
