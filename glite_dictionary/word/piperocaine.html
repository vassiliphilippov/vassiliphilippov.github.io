piperocaine

1. NOUN

/ ˌpaɪpəroʊˈkeɪn / (AmE), / ˌpaɪpərəˈkeɪn / (BrE)
a compound used as a local or spinal anesthetic, especially in the form of its hydrochloride
local or spinal anesthetic compound
medical

Examples

The surgeon selected piperocaine for the spinal anesthesia during the operation.
Some patients may have an allergic reaction to piperocaine.
The use of piperocaine is less common today due to the development of newer anesthetics.
