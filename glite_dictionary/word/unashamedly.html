unashamedly

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.əˈʃeɪ.mɪd.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈʃeɪ.mɪd.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows no shame or embarrassment about one's actions or feelings.
in a shameless or unapologetic manner

Examples

She unashamedly voiced her unpopular opinion at the meeting.
He unashamedly took all the credit for the team's success.
They unashamedly ignored the rules to achieve their goal.

Synset Examples

he unashamedly abandoned the project when he realized he would not gain from it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shamelessly - in a shameless manner

Etymology

From 'unashamed' + '-ly', where 'unashamed' is from un- (not) + ashamed.
