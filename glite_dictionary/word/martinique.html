Martinique

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɑrtəˈnik / (AmE), / ˌmɑːtɪˈniːk / (BrE)
an island in the eastern Caribbean Sea, part of the Windward Islands, and an overseas region of France
Caribbean island; French overseas region

Examples

She spent her honeymoon on Martinique and loved the island's vibrant culture.
Hurricanes sometimes threaten Martinique during the Caribbean storm season.
Many people on Martinique speak both French and Creole.

Etymology

French, from Spanish 'Martinica', likely named after Saint Martin.
