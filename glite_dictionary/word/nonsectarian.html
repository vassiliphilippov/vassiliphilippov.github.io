nonsectarian

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˌsɛkˈtɛəriən / (AmE), / nɒnˌsɛkˈtɛəriən / (BrE)
Not restricted to or associated with a particular religious sect, political party, or school of thought.
not limited to a particular sect or group

Examples

The university prides itself on maintaining a nonsectarian environment where students of all backgrounds feel welcome.
She enrolled her child in a nonsectarian school to avoid religious divisions.
The charity was praised for its nonsectarian approach to providing help, serving anyone in need regardless of belief.

Synset Examples

religious training in a nonsectarian atmosphere
nonsectarian colleges
a wide and unsectarian interest in religion

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sectarian - restricted to or associated with a specific religious sect or group

Etymology

From non- (not) + sectarian, which derives from 'sect' (a religious denomination or group).
