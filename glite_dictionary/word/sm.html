Sm

1. NOUN

/ ɛs ɛm / (AmE), / ɛs ɛm / (BrE)
The chemical symbol for samarium, a grey lustrous metallic element of the rare earth group used in special alloys and found in monazite and bastnasite.
chemical symbol for samarium

Examples

The magnet contained a small amount of Sm to enhance its performance.
Scientists isolated pure Sm from monazite sands for further study.
The periodic table lists Sm as element 62.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: samarium - A grey metallic chemical element with the symbol Sm

Etymology

From New Latin 'samarium', named for the mineral 'samarskite'.
