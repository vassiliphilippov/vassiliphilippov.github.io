lapidist

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæpɪdɪst / (AmE), / ˈlæpɪdɪst / (BrE)
a person skilled in cutting, engraving, or polishing precious stones
a cutter or engraver of precious stones

Examples

The lapidist carefully shaped the sapphire into a brilliant oval for the ring.
As a professional lapidist, she spent hours perfecting the details on each gemstone.
The museum displayed tools used by an expert lapidist from the nineteenth century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lapidary - a person who cuts, polishes, or engraves gems

Etymology

From Latin 'lapis' (stone) + -ist.
