admired

1. ADJ

/ ədˈmaɪɚd / (AmE), / ədˈmaɪəd / (BrE)
regarded with respect and approval; highly esteemed.
regarded with respect and approval

Examples

She is one of the most admired scientists of her generation.
His admired leadership style made a lasting impact on the company.
The artist's work is universally admired for its originality and technique.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respected - held in high regard
  • Antonym: despised - regarded with strong dislike or disrespect

Etymology

From the verb 'admire', ultimately from Latin *admirari* (to wonder at).
