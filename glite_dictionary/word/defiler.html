defiler

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfaɪlər / (AmE), / dɪˈfaɪlə / (BrE)
a person or organization that causes pollution or damage to the environment
someone who pollutes or damages the environment

Examples

Many activists protested against the defiler responsible for dumping chemicals into the river.
Environmental laws are designed to punish any defiler who harms local ecosystems.
The company was labeled a defiler of nature after the oil spill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polluter - a person or thing that pollutes, especially the environment

Etymology

From Latin 'defilare', meaning to pollute or make foul.
