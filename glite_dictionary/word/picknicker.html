picknicker

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪkˌnɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpɪk.nɪk.ə / (BrE)
a person who is having or attending a picnic
person having a picnic

Examples

The picknicker spread a blanket under the large oak tree and unpacked lunch.
Dozens of picknickers enjoyed the sunny afternoon by the lake.
A sudden rainstorm sent every picknicker running for cover.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: picnicker - person attending a picnic (standard spelling)

Etymology

Formed from 'picnic' + '-er', meaning someone who picnics.
