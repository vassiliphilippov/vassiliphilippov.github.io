hi-tech

1. ADJ

/ haɪ ˈtɛk / (AmE), / haɪ ˈtɛk / (BrE)
using or involving the most advanced technology available.
involving advanced technology

Examples

Her new apartment is fully equipped with hi-tech appliances and smart home features.
The company specializes in developing hi-tech medical devices for hospitals worldwide.
He enjoys reading about the latest hi-tech gadgets released each year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high-tech - involving highly advanced technology
  • Antonym: low-tech - using simple or traditional technology

Etymology

Shortened form of 'high technology.'
