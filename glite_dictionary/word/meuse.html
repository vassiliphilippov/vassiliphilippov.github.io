Meuse

1. PROPN

/ mjuz / (AmE), / mjuz / (BrE)
a major river in Western Europe, rising in France and flowing through Belgium and the Netherlands to the North Sea
a river in Western Europe

Examples

The Meuse is an important waterway for transport and trade in the region.
We followed the banks of the Meuse during our cycling trip across Belgium.
Numerous towns and cities have grown along the Meuse over the centuries.

Etymology

From French 'Meuse', derived from Latin 'Mosa', the ancient name of the river.
