merit badging

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛrət ˌbædʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɛrɪt ˌbadʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or system of awarding badges to recognize achievements, especially in educational or youth organization contexts
system of awarding badges for achievement

Examples

Scouts are encouraged to pursue merit badging in areas such as science, art, and community service.
The school has introduced a merit badging program to motivate students to excel.
Through merit badging, participants receive visible recognition for their individual accomplishments.

Synset Examples

The program uses merit badging to recognize achievements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: badge - a small emblem worn to show achievement or membership
