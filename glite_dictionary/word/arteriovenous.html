arteriovenous

1. ADJ

/ ɑrˌtɪri.oʊˈvinəs / (AmE), / ɑːˌtɪəri.əʊˈviːnəs / (BrE)
Relating to, or connecting, both an artery and a vein.
connecting an artery and a vein
medical

Examples

The surgeon created an arteriovenous fistula to provide access for dialysis.
An arteriovenous malformation can lead to serious health problems if left untreated.
Radiologists detected an arteriovenous shunt on the patient's brain scan.

Synset Examples

an arteriovenous fistula

Etymology

From Latin 'arteria' (artery) + 'vena' (vein).
