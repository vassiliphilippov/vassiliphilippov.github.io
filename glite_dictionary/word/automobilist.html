automobilist

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔːtəˈmoʊbɪlɪst / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈməʊbɪlɪst / (BrE)
a person who drives or travels in an automobile
someone who drives or rides in a car

Examples

The automobilist navigated the winding country roads with ease.
During the early 20th century, being an automobilist was considered quite adventurous.
Every responsible automobilist should ensure their vehicle is safely maintained.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motorist - a person who drives a car

Etymology

From French 'automobiliste', from 'automobile' + '-ist'.
