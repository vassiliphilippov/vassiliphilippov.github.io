to metricize

1. VERB

/ ˈmɛtrɪˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrɪˌsaɪz / (BrE)
to convert from a non-metric measurement system to the metric system
convert to the metric system

Examples

The government decided to metricize all official road signs throughout the country.
It can be challenging to metricize recipes that use traditional units like cups and ounces.
The laboratory started to metricize their measurements for consistency with international standards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to metricate - to convert to the metric system

Etymology

Formed from 'metric', after French 'métrique', and the verb-forming suffix '-ize'.

2. VERB

/ ˈmɛtrɪˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrɪˌsaɪz / (BrE)
to express or convert something in terms of the metric system
express in the metric system

Examples

The laboratory decided to metricize all its scientific measurements for consistency.
Many countries chose to metricize their road signs to align with international standards.
It can be challenging to metricize recipes that use traditional units of measurement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: convert - to change from one system, form, or unit to another

Etymology

From 'metric' + '-ize', indicating the process of making or converting to the metric system.
