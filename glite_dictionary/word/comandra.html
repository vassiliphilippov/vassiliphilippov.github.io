Comandra

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈmændrə / (AmE), / kəʊˈmændrə / (BrE)
a small genus of chiefly North American parasitic plants
genus of North American parasitic plants

Examples

Comandra is a genus that includes the commonly seen bastard toadflax.
Botanists have studied the root systems of Comandra to understand its parasitic behavior.
Several North American prairies are known for their populations of Comandra species.
