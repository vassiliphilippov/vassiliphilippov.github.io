to spiritize

1. VERB

/ ˈspɪrɪˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtaɪz / (BrE)
to imbue with a spirit or spiritual quality
imbue with a spirit

Examples

The ritual was meant to spiritize the empty hall with ancestral energy.
Through music and prayer, the ceremony seeks to spiritize the community.
He hoped to spiritize his writing to reach a more profound truth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to spiritualize - to make spiritual, imbue with spiritual qualities

Etymology

From 'spirit' + '-ize', modeled on similar verb formations.
