corky

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɔːrki / (AmE), / ˈkɔːki / (BrE)
(of wine) having an unpleasant musty taste caused by a faulty cork.
having a musty, tainted flavor (wine)

Examples

The sommelier recognized that the wine was corky and immediately brought a new bottle.
A corky aroma completely ruined what should have been an excellent vintage.
She sent her glass back, complaining it tasted corky and unpleasant.

Synset Examples

a corked port

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corked - (of wine) spoiled by a faulty cork; musty taste
