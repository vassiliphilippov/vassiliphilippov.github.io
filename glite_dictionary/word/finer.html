finer

1. ADJ

/ ˈfaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnə / (BrE)
of higher quality or greater excellence than something else; comparative form of 'fine'
comparative of 'fine'; higher quality

Examples

This is a finer example of craftsmanship than the others on display.
She was recognized as one of the finer artists of her generation.
Today’s weather is much finer than it was yesterday.

Synset Examples

a finer wine
a finer musician

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fine - of high quality; good or excellent
  • Degree Higher: finest - superlative of 'fine'; of the highest quality or excellence
