perpetration

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɜːrpəˈtreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɜːpɪˈtreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of committing a crime or other wrongful deed
the act of committing a crime

Examples

The perpetration of the robbery was captured on the store’s security cameras.
There is still no evidence linking her to the perpetration of the crime.
The new laws aim to prevent the perpetration of fraud and corruption.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commission - the act of committing a crime (formal synonym)

Etymology

From Latin perpetrare (to accomplish, perform).
