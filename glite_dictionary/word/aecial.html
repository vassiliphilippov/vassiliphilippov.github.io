aecial

1. ADJ

/ iːˈsiːəl / (AmE), / iːˈsiːəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of an aecium, a specialized spore-producing structure in certain fungi.
pertaining to an aecium in fungi
medical

Examples

Rust fungi have a complex life cycle, with an aecial stage that produces aeciospores.
The aecial cup appears on the leaves during the spring months.
Researchers examined the aecial clusters under the microscope to study spore development.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aecium - a cup-like structure in rust fungi where aeciospores are produced

Etymology

From 'aecium', a structure in rust fungi, plus the suffix '-al' meaning 'pertaining to'.
