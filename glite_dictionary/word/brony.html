brony

1. NOUN

/ ˈbroʊni / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊni / (BrE)
An adult male fan of the animated television series My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic, often participating in the associated fan community.
adult male fan of My Little Pony
informal

Examples

Jake is proud to be a brony and often attends My Little Pony conventions.
The bronies created a supportive online community centered around their shared interests.
Despite some criticism, many bronies openly discuss their love for the show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fanboy - an obsessive male fan, often of comics or TV series

Etymology

Blend of 'bro' (slang for brother) and 'pony'.
