Bruxelles

1. PROPN

/ bʁyˈsɛl / (AmE), / bʁyˈsɛl / (BrE)
The French name for Brussels, the capital and largest city of Belgium; seat of the European Union and NATO.
French name for Brussels, Belgium's capital

Examples

Many diplomats work in Bruxelles because of its international institutions.
She moved to Bruxelles to study at the Université libre de Bruxelles.
The meeting will be held in Bruxelles next spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Brussels - the capital of Belgium; also known as Bruxelles in French

Etymology

French form of 'Brussels', from Old Dutch 'Broekzele' meaning 'settlement in the marsh'.
