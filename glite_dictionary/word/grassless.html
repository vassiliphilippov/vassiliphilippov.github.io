grassless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡræs.ləs / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːs.ləs / (BrE)
(of land or a surface) having no grass growing on it; lacking grass.
lacking grass

Examples

After the drought, the field was completely grassless and barren.
The children played soccer on a grassless patch of dirt behind the school.
They struggled to find a spot to picnic, as much of the park was grassless by late summer.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: grassy - covered with or abundant in grass
