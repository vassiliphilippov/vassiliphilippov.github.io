pichiciego

1. NOUN

/ ˌpiːtʃiˈsiːɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˌpiːtʃɪˈsiːɡəʊ / (BrE)
A very small armadillo native to Argentina, characterized by pale silky hair and pink bony plates on its head and neck.
small Argentine armadillo species

Examples

The pichiciego is rarely seen due to its burrowing habits and nocturnal lifestyle.
Researchers in Argentina are working to protect the habitat of the endangered pichiciego.
With its pink armor and tiny size, the pichiciego is a unique and fascinating mammal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pink fairy armadillo - another name for the species 'Chlamyphorus truncatus', a small Argentine armadillo

Etymology

From Spanish, diminutive of 'pichi', a local word for armadillo.
