sodalist

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊ.də.lɪst / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.də.lɪst / (BrE)
a member of a sodality, especially a religious or charitable association
member of a sodality

Examples

As a devout sodalist, she participated in many charitable activities organized by her group.
The sodalist attended regular meetings to strengthen the community's sense of fellowship.
Many sodalists dedicated their time to supporting the local parish and its outreach programs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sodality - a fellowship or association, especially for religious or charitable purposes

Etymology

From Latin 'sodalis' (companion, associate).
