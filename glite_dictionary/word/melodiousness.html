melodiousness

1. NOUN

/ məˈloʊdiəsnəs / (AmE), / mɪˈləʊdiəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having a pleasant melody or musical sound
quality of being melodious

Examples

The melodiousness of the song captivated the entire audience.
Critics praised the melodiousness of her voice in the new recording.
He admired the melodiousness in the birdsong at dawn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: melody - a sequence of musical notes; a tune
  • Synonym: tunefulness - the quality of being tuneful; musical
  • Antonym: cacophony - a harsh, discordant mixture of sounds

Etymology

From 'melodious' + '-ness', after French *mélodiosité*.
