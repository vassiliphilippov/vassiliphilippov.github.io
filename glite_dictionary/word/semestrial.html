semestrial

1. ADJ

/ səˈmɛstriəl / (AmE), / sɪˈmɛstriəl / (BrE)
occurring every six months or relating to a period of six months
occurring every six months

Examples

The board holds semestrial meetings to evaluate company performance.
Many contracts require semestrial reviews and adjustments.
Our school operates on a semestrial schedule, with major exams every six months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biannual - occurring twice a year
  • Degree Lower: annual - occurring once a year

Etymology

From Latin 'semestris' (of six months), from 'sex' (six) + 'mensis' (month).
