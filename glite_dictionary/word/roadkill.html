roadkill

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊdˌkɪl / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdˌkɪl / (BrE)
An animal that has been killed by a vehicle on a road, or the dead body of such an animal.
animal killed by a vehicle on the road

Examples

The side of the highway was littered with roadkill after the stormy night.
Many scavenger species rely on roadkill as a significant food source.
She swerved to avoid adding another animal to the roadkill statistics.

Synset Examples

vultures usually feed on carrion or roadkill

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carrion - the decaying flesh of dead animals

Etymology

Blend of "road" and "kill," first recorded in the United States in the mid-20th century.
