Stellaria

1. NOUN

/ stəˈlɛriə / (AmE), / stɛˈlɑːrɪə / (BrE)
A genus of small flowering plants in the pink family (Caryophyllaceae), commonly known as chickweed or stitchwort.
genus of flowering plants (chickweed, stitchwort)

Examples

Stellaria is often found growing in gardens and open fields across Europe and North America.
The delicate white flowers of Stellaria make it easily recognizable in the wild.
Many herbalists value Stellaria for its traditional medicinal uses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chickweed - common name for plants in the Stellaria genus
  • Synonym: stitchwort - common name for several species in the Stellaria genus

Etymology

From Latin *stellaria*, meaning 'star-like', referring to the star-shaped flowers.
