unconventionally

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnkənˈvɛnʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈvɛnʃənəli / (BrE)
In a way that is not based on or conforming to what is generally done or believed; in an unconventional manner.
in an unconventional manner

Examples

She dresses unconventionally and enjoys expressing her individuality.
The architect designed the house unconventionally, using recycled materials and bold shapes.
He solved the problem unconventionally, surprising everyone with his creative approach.

Synset Examples

she always behaves rather unconventionally

Related Terms

  • Antonym: conventionally - in a conventional or usual manner

Etymology

From 'unconventional' + '-ly'.
