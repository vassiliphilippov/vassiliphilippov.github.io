Shevchenko

1. PROPN

/ ʃɛvˈtʃɛŋkoʊ / (AmE), / ʃɛvˈtʃɛŋkəʊ / (BrE)
Taras Shevchenko (1814–1861), renowned Ukrainian poet, writer, artist, and national figure.
Ukrainian poet and national figure

Examples

Shevchenko is widely regarded as the founder of modern Ukrainian literature.
Many schools and streets in Ukraine are named after Shevchenko.
I read a collection of Shevchenko's poems in translation last year.
