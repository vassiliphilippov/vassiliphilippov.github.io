malodour

1. NOUN

/ ˌmælˈoʊdər / (AmE), / ˈmaləʊdə / (BrE)
An unpleasant or offensive smell; a bad odor.
unpleasant or offensive smell

Examples

The malodour coming from the garbage bin made the whole room unbearable.
After days of rain, a persistent malodour developed in the basement.
She opened the window to get rid of the malodour lingering in the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stench - a strong and very unpleasant smell
  • Antonym: fragrance - a pleasant, sweet smell

Etymology

From Latin malus (bad) + odōr (smell).
