Bond

1. PROPN

/ bɑnd / (AmE), / bɒnd / (BrE)
A fictional British secret agent, also known as 007, created by writer Ian Fleming and featured in novels and films.
fictional British spy character

Examples

Many actors have played Bond in the famous film series based on the novels.
The latest Bond movie broke box office records worldwide.
He dreams of living the adventurous life of Bond, complete with gadgets and fast cars.
