Satureja hortensis

1. NOUN

/ sæˌtʃuːriə hɔːrˈtɛnsɪs / (AmE), / sæˌtjʊərɪə hɔːˈtɛnsɪs / (BrE)
A species of annual herb known as summer savory, with oval leaves and pink flowers, used for flavoring meats, soups, or salads.
herb used in cooking (summer savory)

Examples

Many recipes for bean soup call for fresh Satureja hortensis to enhance the flavor.
In her herb garden, she grew Satureja hortensis alongside basil and thyme.
Cooks often add dried Satureja hortensis to sausage mixtures for a spicy aroma.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summer savory - common name for Satureja hortensis, a culinary herb

Etymology

From Latin, where 'Satureja' refers to savory and 'hortensis' means 'of gardens'.
