to nictate

1. VERB

/ ˈnɪk.teɪt / (AmE), / ˈnɪk.teɪt / (BrE)
to close and open the eyes quickly; to blink
to blink; close and open eyes quickly

Examples

The bright camera flash made her nictate involuntarily.
Owls nictate to keep their eyes moist and protected.
Humans often nictate more frequently in dry or smoky environments.

Synset Examples

The TV announcer never seems to blink

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blink - to shut and open the eyes quickly

Etymology

From Latin 'nictare' (to wink).
