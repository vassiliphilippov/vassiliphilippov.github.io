jobless

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɑb.ləs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒb.ləs / (BrE)
not having a job; unemployed.
unemployed; not having a job

Examples

After losing his position at the factory, he was jobless for almost a year.
The city's jobless rate has risen significantly in recent months.
Many jobless workers attended the job fair hoping to find new opportunities.

Synset Examples

idle carpenters
jobless transients
many people in the area were out of work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unemployed - without a paid job but available to work

Etymology

From 'job' + '-less', meaning 'without a job'.
