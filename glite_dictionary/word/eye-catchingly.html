eye-catchingly

1. ADV

/ ˈaɪ ˌkætʃ.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈaɪ ˌkætʃ.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a manner that is striking and attracts attention.
in an attention-grabbing way

Examples

The building was decorated eye-catchingly with bold geometric patterns.
Her dress was eye-catchingly bright, drawing everyone's attention at the party.
The campaign posters were designed eye-catchingly to stand out on busy streets.

Synset Examples

The new logo was eye-catchingly vibrant and modern.
She arranged the flowers eye-catchingly on the table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strikingly - in a way that attracts attention by being unusual or noticeable
  • Antonym: unremarkably - in a way that does not attract attention; not striking or unusual

Etymology

Formed from 'eye-catching' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
