Panamanian

1. NOUN

/ ˌpænəˈmeɪniən / (AmE), / ˌpanəˈmeɪnɪən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Panama.
native or inhabitant of Panama

Examples

Many Panamanians are proud of their country's rich cultural heritage.
The festival attracted visitors and Panamanians from all over the country.
She became friends with a group of Panamanians during her semester abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Panamanian - of or relating to Panama, its people, or language (adjective)

Etymology

From Panama + English suffix -ian, forming demonyms.
