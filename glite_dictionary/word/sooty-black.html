sooty-black

1. ADJ

/ ˈsuːti blæk / (AmE), / ˈsuːti blæk / (BrE)
Having the deep black color of soot.
deep black like soot

Examples

The fireplace was coated in a sooty-black residue from years of burning wood.
She wore a dress of sooty-black velvet that shimmered in the light.
Ravens have sooty-black feathers that absorb sunlight efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jet-black - intensely black color, like jet
  • Synonym: ebony - deep, dark black color
