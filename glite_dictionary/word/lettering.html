lettering

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛt̬ərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛtərɪŋ / (BrE)
letters or words that are inscribed, carved, or painted on a surface, especially as decorative or identifying text
inscribed or carved letters/words

Examples

The ancient stone bore lettering that was almost unreadable.
The artist chose a classic script for the shop's gold lettering.
Restorers carefully cleaned the engraved lettering on the monument.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inscription - words or letters written or engraved on something
  • Synonym: engraving - a design or text cut into a hard surface
