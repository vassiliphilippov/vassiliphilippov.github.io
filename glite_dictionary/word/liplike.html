liplike

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪpˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈlɪp.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the appearance of lips, especially in shape or form
resembling lips in shape or form

Examples

The flower's liplike petals attract certain species of insects for pollination.
Her wound had a liplike appearance along the edge, making it distinctive.
The structure of the cave included a narrow, liplike opening at the entrance.
