Pyongyang

1. PROPN

/ pjoʊŋˈjɑːŋ / (AmE), / pjɒŋˈjæŋ / (BrE)
the capital city of North Korea, known as an industrial and political center
capital city of North Korea

Examples

After arriving in Pyongyang, the delegation was taken on a guided tour of the city.
Much of Pyongyang was rebuilt after the Korean War, giving it a unique architectural style.
Flights to Pyongyang are limited, with only a few international connections available.

Synset Examples

Pyongyang is Korea's oldest city but little of its history has been preserved

Etymology

From Korean 평양 (Pyeongyang), meaning 'flat land'.
