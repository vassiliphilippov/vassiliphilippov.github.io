Sakkara

1. PROPN

/ səˈkɑːrə / (AmE), / səˈkɑːrə / (BrE)
a town in northern Egypt, famed as the site of the oldest Egyptian pyramids
ancient Egyptian town with early pyramids

Examples

The Step Pyramid at Sakkara is considered one of the earliest large-scale cut stone constructions.
Many archaeologists travel to Sakkara to study its ancient tombs and monuments.
Historical records often mention Sakkara as the necropolis for the ancient city of Memphis.
