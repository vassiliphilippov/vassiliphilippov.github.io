Nephelium litchi

1. NOUN

/ nəˈfiliəm ˈliːtʃi / (AmE), / nɛˈfɛlɪəm ˈliːtʃi / (BrE)
a tropical tree native to China, cultivated for its edible fruit; also known as the lychee tree
the lychee tree, a fruit-bearing plant

Examples

The Nephelium litchi is widely grown for its delicious and aromatic fruit.
Farmers in India have recently planted more Nephelium litchi to meet increasing demand for lychees.
Studies show that Nephelium litchi prefers warm, humid climates to thrive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lychee - the fruit produced by Nephelium litchi, edible and sweet

Etymology

From the genus name 'Nephelium' (from Greek 'nephelion', little cloud) and 'litchi' (from the Cantonese lychee).
