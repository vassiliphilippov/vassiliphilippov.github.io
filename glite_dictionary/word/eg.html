1. ADV

/ i ˈdʒi / (AmE), / iː ˈdʒiː / (BrE)
used to introduce one or more examples of something just mentioned; for example
for example (abbreviation)

Examples

There are many ways to solve the problem, e.g., using a calculator or computer.
Certain fruits, e.g., apples and oranges, are good sources of vitamins.
Some European countries, e.g., France and Italy, have strict food regulations.

Synset Examples

take ribbon snakes, for example

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for example - used to introduce an example
  • Antonym: i.e. - used to clarify or restate previous information (that is)

Etymology

From Latin 'exempli gratia' meaning 'for the sake of example'.
