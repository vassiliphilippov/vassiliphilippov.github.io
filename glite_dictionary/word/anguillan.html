Anguillan

1. NOUN

/ ænˈɡwɪlən / (AmE), / ænˈɡwɪlən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Anguilla in the West Indies
native or inhabitant of Anguilla

Examples

The Anguillan greeted us warmly when we arrived on the island.
As an Anguillan, she takes great pride in her country's culture.
Many Anguillans celebrate their heritage with vibrant festivals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Anguillan - of or relating to Anguilla

Etymology

From Anguilla, the name of the Caribbean island, with the suffix -an forming the demonym.
