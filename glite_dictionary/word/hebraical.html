Hebraical

1. ADJ

/ hiˈbreɪɪkəl / (AmE), / hɛˈbreɪɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to the Hebrews, especially their language, culture, or customs.
of or relating to the Hebrews

Examples

The museum displayed several Hebraical manuscripts from the Middle Ages.
Her research focuses on Hebraical influences in medieval philosophy.
The professor gave a lecture on Hebraical traditions in religious texts.

Synset Examples

the old Hebrew prophets

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hebrew - relating to the Hebrew people or language

Etymology

From Late Latin 'Hebraicus' (Hebrew) + English '-al'.
