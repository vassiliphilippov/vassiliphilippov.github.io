sis

1. NOUN

/ sɪs / (AmE), / sɪs / (BrE)
an informal word for a sister; a female sibling
informal word for sister
informal

Examples

My sis is coming home for the holidays.
Don't mess with my sis if you want to stay friends with me.
Her sis helped her move into her new apartment.

Synset Examples

my sister married a musician

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sister - a female person who has the same parents as another person

Etymology

Shortened form of 'sister', first recorded in the 19th century.
