Guantanamo

1. PROPN

/ ɡwɑnˈtɑːnəˌmoʊ / (AmE), / ɡwænˈtænəməʊ / (BrE)
A city in southeastern Cuba, known as the site of a United States naval base.
city in southeastern Cuba; site of US naval base

Examples

The United States maintains a naval base at Guantanamo in Cuba.
News reports about Guantanamo focus on both the city and the detention camp.
Tourists rarely visit Guantanamo compared to other Cuban cities.
