shandygaff

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃændiˌɡæf / (AmE), / ˈʃandiɡaf / (BrE)
A drink made by mixing beer with lemonade.
beer mixed with lemonade

Examples

He ordered a shandygaff at the pub to enjoy a lighter drink.
During the summer picnic, many guests preferred a cold shandygaff over stronger beverages.
The bartender recommended a shandygaff to pair with the grilled fish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shandy - A drink of beer mixed with lemonade or another non-alcoholic beverage

Etymology

From 'shandy' (from 19th-century English slang, shortened from 'shandygaff') and 'gaff', possibly of unknown origin.
