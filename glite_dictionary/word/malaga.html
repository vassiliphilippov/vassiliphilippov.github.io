Malaga

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑləɡə / (AmE), / ˈmælɪɡə / (BrE)
A port city and popular resort in Andalusia, southern Spain, located on the Mediterranean coast.
port city in southern Spain

Examples

Malaga is famous for its beautiful beaches and rich cultural history.
They spent their summer holiday exploring the historic streets of Malaga.
The Picasso Museum in Malaga attracts thousands of visitors each year.

Etymology

From Spanish Málaga, derived from Latin Malaca, from Ancient Greek Μάλακα (Málaka).
