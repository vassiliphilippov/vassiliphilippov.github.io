Ningirsu

1. PROPN

/ nɪŋˈɡɪər.su / (AmE), / nɪŋˈɡɪə.suː / (BrE)
a Sumerian solar deity, regarded as the firstborn of Bel and the consort of Gula, and associated with war, hunting, and agriculture, sometimes identified with the biblical Nimrod.
Sumerian solar and war deity

Examples

Ancient texts describe how Ningirsu was worshipped as a powerful war god in Lagash.
Archaeologists uncovered a temple dedicated to Ningirsu in southern Mesopotamia.
Legends say that Ningirsu helped his followers in battles and ensured abundant harvests.

Etymology

From Sumerian 'Nin-gir-su', meaning 'Lord of Girsu' (the Sumerian city).
