bobsledding

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːbˌslɛdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɒbˌslɛdɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of riding on a bobsled, typically down an ice track
the sport of riding a bobsled

Examples

The winter resort offers bobsledding for thrill-seekers every Saturday.
Olympic athletes train year-round to compete in bobsledding events.
She tried bobsledding for the first time and found it both exhilarating and terrifying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bobsleigh - a vehicle for racing down an ice track, also the name of the sport

Etymology

From 'bobsled' + '-ing', where 'bobsled' originated in the late 19th century from 'bob' + 'sled'.
