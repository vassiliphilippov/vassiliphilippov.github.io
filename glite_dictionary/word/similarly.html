similarly

1. ADV

/ ˈsɪm.ə.lɚ.li / (AmE), / ˈsɪm.ɪ.lə.li / (BrE)
in a similar or like manner; in the same way as something previously mentioned
in a similar manner

Examples

All our offices are decorated similarly to create a unified look throughout the company.
If you exercise regularly, your energy will increase; similarly, your mood may also improve.
The northern region experienced heavy rainfall last week, and similarly, the south was affected yesterday.

Synset Examples

He was similarly affected
some people have little power to do good, and have likewise little strength to resist evil

Related Terms

  • Synonym: likewise - in the same way; also
  • Antonym: differently - in a different way

Etymology

From similar + -ly, modeled after the pattern of English adverbs.
