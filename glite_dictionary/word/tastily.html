tastily

1. ADV

/ ˈteɪstɪli / (AmE), / ˈteɪstɪli / (BrE)
In a way that is tasty or full of good flavor.
in a tasty manner

Examples

The pasta was tastily seasoned with fresh herbs and garlic.
She baked a pie that was tastily spiced and perfectly sweet.
The chef served the chicken tastily garnished with a citrus sauce.

Synset Examples

the house was tastefully decorated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deliciously - in a delicious manner; very pleasantly to the taste

Etymology

From 'tasty' + '-ly'.

2. ADV

/ ˈteɪstɪli / (AmE), / ˈteɪstɪli / (BrE)
In a way that is tasty; with a pleasant flavor.
in a tasty manner

Examples

The vegetables were always prepared tastily in her kitchen.
He arranged the platter tastily, making it both appetizing and attractive.
Their homemade bread is tastily flavored with herbs and spices.

Synset Examples

the meal was tastily cooked

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deliciously - in a way that is very pleasing to the taste or smell

Etymology

Formed from 'tasty' + '-ly' (adverb suffix).
