Pengo

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɛŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken in south central India, particularly in Odisha
Dravidian language of Odisha, India

Examples

The Pengo language is primarily spoken in Odisha, India.
Linguists have studied Pengo to better understand Dravidian languages.
He learned basic phrases in Pengo to communicate with the local community.
