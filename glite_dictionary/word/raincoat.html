raincoat

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪnˌkoʊt / (AmE), / ˈreɪnkəʊt / (BrE)
A coat made from water-resistant material, worn to protect the body from rain.
waterproof coat for rain

Examples

She wore her raincoat and carried an umbrella to stay dry during the storm.
Don't forget your raincoat if the forecast calls for showers.
His bright yellow raincoat made him easy to spot in the crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mackintosh - a type of waterproof coat, especially one made from rubberized fabric

Etymology

From 'rain' + 'coat', first recorded in the 19th century.
