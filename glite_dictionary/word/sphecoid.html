sphecoid

1. NOUN

/ ˈsfiː.kɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈsfiː.kɔɪd / (BrE)
any of various solitary wasps, especially those resembling or belonging to the superfamily Sphecoidea.
a kind of solitary wasp

Examples

The sphecoid builds its nest in sandy soils near riverbanks.
Scientists discovered a rare sphecoid species in the tropical forest canopy.
Many sphecoids are known for their remarkable hunting techniques.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: wasp - a winged insect of the order Hymenoptera known for its sting

Etymology

From Greek 'sphex' (wasp) + English '-oid' (like, resembling).
