North American

1. ADJ

/ nɔrθ əˈmɛrɪkən / (AmE), / nɔːθ əˈmerɪkən / (BrE)
relating to North America or its people, countries, or cultures
relating to North America or its people

Examples

Many North American cities have diverse populations.
The North American climate varies greatly from north to south.
She studied the history of North American indigenous peoples in college.

2. NOUN

/ nɔrθ əˈmɛrɪkən / (AmE), / nɔːθ əˈmɛrɪkən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of North America, including Canada, the United States, and Mexico
person from North America

Examples

Many North Americans travel abroad during the summer months.
The city is popular among North Americans seeking cultural experiences in Europe.
As a North American, he was used to diverse weather conditions.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: South American - a person from South America
  • Antonym: Central American - a person from Central America
