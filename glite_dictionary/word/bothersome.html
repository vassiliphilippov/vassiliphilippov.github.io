bothersome

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑːðərsəm / (AmE), / ˈbɒðəsəm / (BrE)
causing annoyance, trouble, or irritation
causing annoyance or irritation

Examples

I find the paperwork for this process extremely bothersome.
