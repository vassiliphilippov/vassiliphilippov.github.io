dottily

1. ADV

/ ˈdɑː.ti.li / (AmE), / ˈdɒ.tɪ.li / (BrE)
In a mildly insane or eccentric manner; behaving as if slightly crazy or odd.
in an eccentric or mildly insane way

Examples

After retirement, he started dressing dottily in mismatched clothes and bright hats.
She laughed dottily at jokes only she seemed to understand.
The professor sometimes speaks dottily about his theories to amuse his students.

Synset Examples

the old lady is beginning to behave quite dottily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentrically - in an unconventional or odd manner

Etymology

From 'dotty' (mildly insane or eccentric) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
