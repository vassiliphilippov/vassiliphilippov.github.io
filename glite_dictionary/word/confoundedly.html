confoundedly

1. ADV

/ kənˈfaʊndɪdli / (AmE), / kənˈfaʊndɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that expresses annoyance, bewilderment, or surprise; extremely or perplexedly.
in a perplexed or intense manner

Examples

He was confoundedly frustrated by the sudden change in plans.
The instructions were confoundedly difficult to follow for a beginner.
She confoundedly forgot her keys again, much to her own annoyance.

Synset Examples

he looked at his professor perplexedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perplexedly - in a puzzled or confused manner

Etymology

From 'confounded' + '-ly'; 'confounded' from past participle of 'confound', from Latin 'confundere' (to mix together).
