rigout

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪɡaʊt / (AmE), / ˈrɪɡaʊt / (BrE)
an outfit or costume, especially one that is unusual or striking
unusual or striking outfit
informal british

Examples

She turned up to the party in a bizarre rigout that caught everyone's eye.
His friends burst out laughing at his clownish rigout for the costume contest.
You can't go to a wedding in that rigout; it's far too casual!

Synset Examples

What a queer rigout!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outfit - a set of clothes worn together, especially for a particular occasion or purpose

Etymology

Possibly from 'rig' (to dress) + 'out', originally British and Irish English slang.
