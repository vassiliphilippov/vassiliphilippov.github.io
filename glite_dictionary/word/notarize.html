to notarize

1. VERB

/ ˈnoʊtəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtəraɪz / (BrE)
to have a document officially certified by a notary public as authentic or legitimate
certify by a notary public

Examples

The lawyer will notarize your contract for a small fee.
We need to notarize these documents before submitting them to the court.
She quickly notarized the letter while we waited in her office.

Synset Examples

We had to have the signature notarized

Etymology

From notary + -ize
