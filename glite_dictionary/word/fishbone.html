fishbone

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃ.bəʊn / (BrE)
a bone from a fish, especially a small and thin one
a bone from a fish

Examples

Be careful when eating this dish; it may contain a fishbone.
He choked on a fishbone while having dinner last night.
Surprisingly, the X-ray revealed a small fishbone lodged in her throat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bone - the hard structural tissue making up the skeleton of most vertebrates
