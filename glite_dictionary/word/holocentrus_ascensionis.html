Holocentrus ascensionis

1. NOUN

/ ˌhoʊləˈsɛntrəs æsˌɛnʃiˈoʊnɪs / (AmE), / ˌhɒləsɛnˈtrʌs æsˌɛnsiˈəʊnɪs / (BrE)
a bright red tropical fish found in the West Indies and Bermuda
bright red fish of the West Indies and Bermuda

Examples

The Holocentrus ascensionis is easily recognized by its vivid red color in the coral reefs.
During our dive, we spotted several Holocentrus ascensionis swimming near the shipwreck.
Marine biologists often study Holocentrus ascensionis to learn about reef fish populations.

Etymology

From Modern Latin, combining genus Holocentrus and species name ascensionis referring to Ascension Island.
