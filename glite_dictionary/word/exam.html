exam

1. NOUN

/ ɪgˈzæm / (AmE), / ɪgˈzæm / (BrE)
a formal test of a person's knowledge or proficiency in a subject or skill
formal test of knowledge or skill

Examples

She studied hard all week for her chemistry exam.
He felt nervous as he handed in his final exam of the semester.
All students must pass the entrance exam to be accepted into the program.

Synset Examples

when the test was stolen the professor had to make a new set of questions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: test - a procedure intended to establish the quality, performance, or reliability of something, especially before use

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'examination', from Latin 'examinatio' (a weighing, examination).
