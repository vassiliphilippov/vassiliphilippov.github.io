Nazi

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑtsi / (AmE), / ˈnɑːtsi / (BrE)
a member of the National Socialist German Workers' Party, particularly in Germany under Adolf Hitler
member of Hitler's Nazi Party

Examples

The Nazi regime came to power in Germany in 1933.
Her grandfather was a staunch Nazi during World War II.
Many Nazis were tried for war crimes after the war.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fascist - member or supporter of a fascist regime or ideology

Etymology

Shortened from German Nationalsozialist (National Socialist).

2. ADJ

/ ˈnɑːtsi / (AmE), / ˈnɑːtsi / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of the ideology, practices, or people associated with Nazism or the Nazis.
relating to Nazism or Nazis

Examples

The Nazi regime implemented oppressive laws and policies across Germany.
His speech reflected deeply Nazi ideas that alarmed historians and critics alike.
Documentaries often explore the Nazi approach to propaganda in the 1930s.

Synset Examples

the total Nazi crime
the Nazi interpretation of history

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fascist - relating to or supporting fascism or fascists
  • Antonym: anti-Nazi - opposed to Nazis or Nazism

Etymology

Borrowed from German 'Nazi', a shortened form of 'Nationalsozialist' (National Socialist).

3. ADJ

/ ˈnɑtsi / (AmE), / ˈnɑːtsi / (BrE)
Relating to the National Socialist (Nazi) Party, its ideology, or policies, especially as practised in Germany under Adolf Hitler. Sometimes, used to describe things connected with a form of extreme right-wing, authoritarian socialism promoted by the Nazi Party.
relating to the Nazi Party or its ideology

Examples

The city was under Nazi control during World War II.
Propaganda reflecting Nazi ideals was widespread in Germany in the 1930s.
The museum displayed artifacts from the Nazi era.

Synset Examples

the national socialist party came to power in Germany in 1933

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fascist - relating to an authoritarian, nationalistic right-wing system of government and social organization

Etymology

From German 'Nazi', a shortening of 'Nationalsozialist' (National Socialist).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03011226-a
Synset POS: a
