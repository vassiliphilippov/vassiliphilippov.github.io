130th

1. ADJ

/ wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈθɜrti ɛθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈθɜːti ɪθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number corresponding to one hundred thirty in a series; coming after the 129th and before the 131st.
ordinal number: one hundred thirty

Examples

Our company is celebrating its 130th anniversary this year.
She finished in 130th place in the marathon, beating her personal record.
This is the 130th time the festival has been held since its inception.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 129th - the ordinal number corresponding to one hundred twenty-nine
  • Degree Higher: 131st - the ordinal number corresponding to one hundred thirty-one
