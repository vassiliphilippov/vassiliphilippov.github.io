Vladimir Putin

1. PROPN

/ vlɑːˈdɪmɪr ˈpuːtɪn / (AmE), / ˈvlædɪmɪə ˈpuːtɪn / (BrE)
(born 1952) Russian statesman who has served as president and prime minister of the Russian Federation.
Russian president and political leader

Examples

Vladimir Putin has played a pivotal role in Russian politics since the late 1990s.
During his presidency, Vladimir Putin implemented significant economic and political reforms in Russia.
Many world leaders have met with Vladimir Putin to discuss international relations.
