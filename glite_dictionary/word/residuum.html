residuum

1. NOUN

/ rəˈzɪd.ju.əm / (AmE), / rɪˈzɪd.juː.əm / (BrE)
the part that is left after the main or otherwise important parts have been removed or used; a remainder or residue
something left after other parts removed

Examples

After the solution was filtered, only a fine residuum remained at the bottom of the beaker.
The residuum of the estate was divided among the heirs after all debts were paid.
Years of research yielded a substantial residuum of unanswered questions.

Synset Examples

there was no remainder
he threw away the rest
he took what he wanted and I got the balance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: residue - a small amount of something that remains after the main part has gone or been taken
  • Synonym: remainder - the part that is left over or remains
  • Antonym: main part - the most important or largest part

Etymology

From Latin 'residuum' (that which is left, residue), from 'residere' (to remain).
