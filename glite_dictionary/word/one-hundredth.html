one-hundredth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ˈhʌndrədθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈhʌndrɪdθ / (BrE)
One part out of one hundred equal parts; 1/100.
one part in a hundred (1/100)

Examples

Only a one-hundredth of the population lives in rural areas now.
She received one-hundredth of the prize money for her efforts.
A one-hundredth of a meter is equal to one centimeter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: percent - one part in a hundred; per hundred
