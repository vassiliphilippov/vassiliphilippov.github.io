asa dulcis

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪ.sə ˈdʌl.sɪs / (AmE), / ˌɑː.sə ˈdʊl.sɪs / (BrE)
a gum resin used especially for treating skin irritation
gum resin used to treat skin irritation
medical

Examples

Some traditional remedies for eczema include the use of asa dulcis on affected areas.
The herbalist recommended asa dulcis to soothe my skin irritation.
Researchers studied the effectiveness of asa dulcis in treating various dermatological conditions.

Etymology

From Latin 'asa' (resin) and 'dulcis' (sweet).
