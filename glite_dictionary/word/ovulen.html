Ovulen

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊ.vjʊ.lən / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.vjʊ.lən / (BrE)
A trade name for an oral contraceptive containing mestranol and a progestin compound.
oral contraceptive (brand name)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Ovulen as a birth control option for her patient.
Some women experienced side effects while taking Ovulen in the 1970s.
