dapperness

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæpərnəs / (AmE), / ˈdæpənəs / (BrE)
the quality of being neat, stylish, and smart in appearance
stylish or neat appearance

Examples

His dapperness stood out at the office party, drawing compliments from everyone.
The actor was known for his dapperness both on and off the screen.
With a natural dapperness, she carried herself with confidence and grace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elegance - the quality of being graceful and stylish
  • Antonym: shabbiness - the quality of being untidy or worn out in appearance

Etymology

From 'dapper' (neat, trim, stylish) + '-ness' (forming a noun of quality).
