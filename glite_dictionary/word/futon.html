futon

1. NOUN

/ ˈfuː.tɑn / (AmE), / ˈfjuː.tɒn / (BrE)
a thin mattress, usually filled with cotton, that can be used on the floor or on a frame for sleeping or sitting, originally from Japan
thin Japanese-style mattress

Examples

He unfolded the futon and laid it out on the living room floor for his guest.
They decided to buy a futon for their small apartment because it doubles as a bed and a sofa.
After a long day, she enjoyed relaxing on her futon while reading a book.

Etymology

From Japanese 'futon' (布団), meaning 'bedding'.
