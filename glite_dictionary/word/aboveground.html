aboveground

1. ADJ

/ əˈbʌvˌɡraʊnd / (AmE), / əˈbʌvˌɡraʊnd / (BrE)
Situated, occurring, or done on or above the surface of the ground.
on or above the ground; not underground

Examples

The city’s new metro line features an aboveground stretch along the river.
Many plants store energy in their aboveground parts during the summer.
During the 1950s, several countries conducted aboveground nuclear tests.

Synset Examples

aboveground nuclear testing

Related Terms

  • Antonym: underground - situated, occurring, or done below the surface of the ground
