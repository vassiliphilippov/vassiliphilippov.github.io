topographic

1. ADJ

/ ˌtɑːpəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɒpəˈɡrafɪk / (BrE)
relating to the physical features of a place or region, especially as represented on maps
relating to the features of land or maps

Examples

The scientist used topographic data to analyze changes in the landscape.
She specializes in creating topographic maps for hikers.
A topographic survey revealed new details about the mountain's surface.

Synset Examples

a topographical engineer
a topographical survey
topographic maps

Related Terms

  • Synonym: topographical - alternative form, same meaning as 'topographic'
  • Synonym: geographical - relating to geography or the arrangement of places and physical features

Etymology

From Greek topos (place) and -graphia (description or writing).
