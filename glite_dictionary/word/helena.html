Helena

1. PROPN

/ həˈleɪnə / (AmE), / həˈlɛːnə / (BrE)
the capital city of the state of Montana, located in western Montana, United States
capital city of Montana, USA

Examples

The state legislature meets in Helena every year.
She grew up in Helena before moving to Seattle.
Tourists often visit the historic downtown area of Helena.

Etymology

Named after Saint Helena or possibly a mining camp in Minnesota called Saint Helena.
