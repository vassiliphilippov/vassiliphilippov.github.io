puissant

1. ADJ

/ ˈpwiːsənt / (AmE), / ˈpwiːsənt / (BrE)
having great power or influence; powerful.
powerful; influential

Examples

The puissant king ruled the land with wisdom and strength.
In its heyday, the company was a puissant force in the tech industry.
Her puissant advocacy changed the course of the debate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powerful - having great power or strength
  • Antonym: weak - lacking power or strength

Etymology

From Old French puissant, from Latin posse (to be able).
