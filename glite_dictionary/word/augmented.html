augmented

1. ADJ

/ ɔgˈmɛntɪd / (AmE), / ɔːgˈmɛntɪd / (BrE)
increased in amount, number, or strength; made greater by addition
increased or made greater

Examples

The museum unveiled an augmented reality exhibit for visitors.
Her responsibilities at work have been augmented since the promotion.
They enjoyed an augmented income after taking on a second job.

Synset Examples

his augmented renown
a greatly augmented collection of books

Related Terms

  • Synonym: increased - made greater in amount, number, or degree
  • Antonym: diminished - made smaller or less

Etymology

From Latin 'augmentare' (to increase), from 'augere' (to increase).
