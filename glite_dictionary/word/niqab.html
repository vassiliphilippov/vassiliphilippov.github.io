niqab

1. NOUN

/ niˈkɑb / (AmE), / nɪˈkɑːb / (BrE)
A veil worn by some Muslim women that covers the lower part of the face, leaving only the eyes visible.
veil covering lower face (Islamic)

Examples

The young woman wore a niqab that concealed everything except her eyes.
Debate about the niqab has intensified in several European countries recently.
Her sister chose not to wear a niqab, preferring only a simple headscarf.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: burqa - a garment covering the body and face worn by some Muslim women
  • Degree Lower: hijab - a headscarf covering the hair and neck but leaving the face clear

Etymology

From Arabic نِقَاب (niqāb), meaning 'veil, mask'.
