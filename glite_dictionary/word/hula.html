hula

1. NOUN

/ ˈhuːlə / (AmE), / ˈhuːlə / (BrE)
A traditional Polynesian dance, originally from Hawaii, characterized by rhythmic movement and gestures that tell a story, often performed to chant or music.
Hawaiian dance with rhythmic movements

Examples

She performed a graceful hula at the cultural festival.
Tourists enjoy watching the traditional hula dancers in Hawaii.
He learned the basic steps of the hula during his vacation.

Etymology

From Hawaiian hula (dance).
