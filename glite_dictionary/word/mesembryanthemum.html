Mesembryanthemum

1. NOUN

/ məˌzɛm.briˈæn.θə.məm / (AmE), / mɛˌzɛm.brɪˈæn.θɪ.məm / (BrE)
a genus of South African annual or biennial plants with flowers that open only in bright sunlight
a genus of South African sun-loving plants

Examples

The Mesembryanthemum bloomed spectacularly as soon as the midday sun hit the garden.
Botanists often study the Mesembryanthemum for its unique flowering habits.
She planted several Mesembryanthemums to add color to her sunniest flower bed.

Etymology

From Greek 'mesembria' (midday) and 'anthemon' (flower), meaning 'midday flower.'
