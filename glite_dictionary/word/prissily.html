prissily

1. ADV

/ ˈprɪsɪli / (AmE), / ˈprɪsɪli / (BrE)
In an excessively proper, prim, or fussy manner.
in an overly prim or fussy way

Examples

She arranged the napkins prissily, making sure every corner was perfectly aligned.
He answered prissily, offended by the slightest breach of etiquette.
The children rolled their eyes as their mother prissily corrected their posture at the table.

Synset Examples

the new teacher alienates the children by behaving prissily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prissy - excessively proper or prim
