tadalafil

1. NOUN

/ təˈdæl.əˌfɪl / (AmE), / təˈdal.ə.fɪl / (BrE)
A drug used to treat erectile dysfunction and sometimes benign prostatic hyperplasia in men.
drug for erectile dysfunction
medical

After discussing his symptoms with his doctor, John was prescribed tadalafil for his erectile dysfunction.

Some patients take tadalafil daily, while others use it as needed before sexual activity.
