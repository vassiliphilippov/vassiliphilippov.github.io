Obama

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈbɑːmə / (AmE), / əʊˈbɑːmə / (BrE)
The 44th President of the United States (2009–2017), and the first African-American President, Barack Obama (born 1961).
44th U.S. President, Barack Obama

Examples

Obama delivered a memorable speech at the 2008 Democratic National Convention.
Many historians believe Obama brought a new era of diplomacy to the White House.
Obama was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 2009.

Etymology

From the surname of Barack Obama, of Luo (Kenyan) origin.
