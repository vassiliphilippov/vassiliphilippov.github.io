surpassingly

1. ADV

/ sərˈpæsɪŋli / (AmE), / səˈpɑːsɪŋli / (BrE)
To an extraordinary or exceptional degree; extremely.
to an extraordinary degree

Examples

The novel was surpassingly well written and captivated readers from the first page.
Her kindness is surpassingly rare in the industry today.
He found the view from the mountain peak to be surpassingly beautiful.

Synset Examples

she was a surpassingly beautiful woman
I will mention only one particular aspect of the current mess because … this one is surely something new and passing strange

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extremely - to a very great degree
  • Synonym: exceptionally - to a much greater degree than usual

Etymology

From 'surpass' (to exceed) + '-ingly' (adverbial suffix).
