popularly

1. ADV

/ ˈpɑːpjʊlərli / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊləli / (BrE)
by most people; in a way that is commonly accepted or believed by the general public.
in a manner accepted by most people

Examples

The city is popularly known as the 'Big Apple'.
The belief is popularly held, despite scientific evidence to the contrary.
This author is popularly regarded as one of the best writers of the century.

Synset Examples

this topic was popularly discussed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commonly - frequently or generally occurring

Etymology

From 'popular' + '-ly', with 'popular' from Latin 'populus' (people).
