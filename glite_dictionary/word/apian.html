apian

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪ.pi.ən / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.pi.ən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of bees
relating to bees

Examples

The scientist published an article on apian behavior in wild honey bees.
She decorated her garden with apian motifs inspired by the local bees.
The decline in apian populations has caused concern among ecologists.

Etymology

From Latin 'apius', from 'apis' (bee).
