Upupa

1. NOUN

/ juˈpjuːpə / (AmE), / juːˈpjuːpə / (BrE)
The genus of birds comprising the hoopoes, the type genus of the family Upupidae.
genus of birds (hoopoes)

Examples

The bird observed in the field belonged to the genus Upupa.
Upupa is characterized by its distinctive crown of feathers.
Many birdwatchers hope to spot a member of the Upupa genus during migration season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hoopoe - a colorful bird of the family Upupidae

Etymology

From Latin 'upupa', the word for hoopoe.
