ruthlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈruːθləsli / (AmE), / ˈruːθləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no pity or compassion for others; in a harsh or merciless manner.
in a harsh or merciless way

Examples

The company ruthlessly eliminated hundreds of jobs to cut costs.
The dictator ruthlessly suppressed any opposition to his regime.
Competition in the tech industry can be ruthlessly intense.

Synset Examples

the government has been urged to take immediate action to deal ruthlessly with the strikers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mercilessly - in a way that shows no mercy
  • Antonym: compassionately - in a way that shows compassion

Etymology

From 'ruthless' + '-ly'. 'Ruthless' from 'ruth' (pity, compassion) + '-less'.
