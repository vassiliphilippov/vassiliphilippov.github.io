Tessin

1. PROPN

/ tɛˈsiːn / (AmE), / tɛˈsiːn / (BrE)
an Italian-speaking canton (region) in southern Switzerland, known in Italian as Ticino.
Italian-speaking region of Switzerland

Examples

The picturesque landscapes of Tessin attract tourists from around the world.
Many Swiss Italians live in the canton of Tessin.
We spent our summer hiking through the mountains of Tessin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ticino - Italian name for the same Swiss canton

Etymology

German name for the canton of Ticino; from the river Ticino.
