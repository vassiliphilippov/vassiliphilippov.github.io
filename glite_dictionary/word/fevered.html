fevered

1. ADJ

/ ˈfiːvərd / (AmE), / ˈfiːvəd / (BrE)
highly excited, agitated, or influenced by intense emotion
highly excited or agitated

Examples

After hearing the news, his fevered mind could not rest for hours.
The team worked through the night in a fevered rush to meet the deadline.
Her fevered imagination led her to dream up elaborate stories.

Synset Examples

a fevered imagination

Related Terms

  • Antonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness or agitation
  • Synonym: excited - showing or feeling excitement; emotionally aroused

Etymology

From 'fever' + '-ed', originally describing a state similar to or caused by fever.
