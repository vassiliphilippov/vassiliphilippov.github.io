demitasse

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛmɪˌtæs / (AmE), / ˈdɛmɪˌtæs / (BrE)
A small cup, typically used for serving black coffee or espresso.
small cup for black coffee

Examples

She sipped her espresso from a delicate demitasse after dinner.
The set included a porcelain demitasse with an intricate floral design.
They served strong black coffee in tiny demitasses at the café.

Etymology

From French 'demi-tasse', literally meaning 'half cup'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈdɛm.iˌtæs / (AmE), / ˈdɛ.mɪ.tæs / (BrE)
A small cup used to serve strong black coffee, especially espresso, without milk or cream.
small cup for strong black coffee

Examples

He sipped his demitasse of espresso after dinner.
The delicate demitasse was part of her grandmother's antique china set.
At the café, they served Turkish coffee in a tiny demitasse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: espresso - strong black coffee brewed by forcing steam through ground coffee beans
  • Degree Higher: coffee cup - a cup designed for serving coffee

Etymology

From French 'demi-tasse' meaning 'half-cup' (demi 'half' + tasse 'cup').
