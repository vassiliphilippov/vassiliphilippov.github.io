excitingly

1. ADV

/ ɪkˈsaɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪkˈsaɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that causes excitement or strong interest; in an exciting manner.
in an exciting manner

Examples

The story unfolds excitingly, keeping the audience on the edge of their seats.
The match ended excitingly with a last-minute goal.
Each chapter begins excitingly, drawing readers deeper into the book.

Synset Examples

at the time of its appearance, the movie must have seemed excitingly new, even revolutionary

Related Terms

  • Antonym: boringly - in a boring manner
