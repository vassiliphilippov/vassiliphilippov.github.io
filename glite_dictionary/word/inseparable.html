inseparable

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈsɛprəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛpərəbəl / (BrE)
unable to be separated; impossible to divide or part
unable to be separated

Examples

The two friends have been inseparable since childhood.
The components are fused together and are virtually inseparable.
After the merger, the companies became inseparable in their operations.

Synset Examples

inseparable pieces of rock

Related Terms

  • Antonym: separable - able to be separated or divided

Etymology

From Medieval Latin inseparabilis, from Latin in- (not) + separabilis (separable).
