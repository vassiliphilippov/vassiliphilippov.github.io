unraised

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈreɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈreɪzd / (BrE)
(of bread or other baked food) made without leavening; not allowed to rise.
made without leavening

Examples

Matzo is a traditional Jewish bread that is unraised.
Some cuisines feature unraised flatbreads made from simple ingredients.
Rice flour pancakes that are unraised have a dense texture.

Synset Examples

unleavened bread is often simply flour mixed with water

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unleavened - (of bread or cake) made without leavening agents; not risen
