esports

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːˌspɔrts / (AmE), / ˈiːˌspɔːts / (BrE)
Organized, competitive video gaming played by professionals, often in tournaments for spectators.
competitive professional video gaming

Examples

The popularity of esports has grown rapidly over the past decade.
Many universities now offer scholarships for esports players.
She dreams of turning her passion for gaming into a career in esports.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: video game - an electronic game played by means of images on a screen
  • Synonym: sport - an activity involving physical exertion and skill in which individuals or teams compete

Etymology

Blend of 'electronic' and 'sports'.
