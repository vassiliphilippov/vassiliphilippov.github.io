Campanulaceae

1. NOUN

/ kæm.pə.njuˈleɪ.si.i / (AmE), / kam.pə.njʊˈleɪ.si.iː / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants in the order Campanulales, typically including bellflowers and sometimes lobelias
family of bellflower plants
medical

Examples

The Campanulaceae family includes a wide range of ornamental garden plants.
Many species in the Campanulaceae are prized for their striking bell-shaped flowers.
The classification of Campanulaceae sometimes overlaps with that of the Lobeliaceae family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bellflower - a common name for plants in the Campanulaceae family
  • Synonym: Lobeliaceae - a closely related plant family, sometimes included within Campanulaceae

Etymology

From the genus name Campanula (Latin for 'little bell') + -aceae, the standard suffix for plant family names.
