Peshawar

1. PROPN

/ pɛˈʃɑːwər / (AmE), / pɛˈʃɑːwə / (BrE)
A city in northern Pakistan, located at the eastern end of the Khyber Pass.
city in northern Pakistan

Examples

The historic city of Peshawar has long been a gateway between Central and South Asia.
Trade routes passing through Peshawar have connected empires for centuries.
She traveled to Peshawar to study its rich cultural and architectural heritage.
