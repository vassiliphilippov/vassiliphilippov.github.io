abstracter

1. NOUN

/ æbˈstræktər / (AmE), / əbˈstræktə / (BrE)
a person who makes abstracts or summarizes information, especially for records or documents
person who makes abstracts or summaries

Examples

The abstracter prepared a concise summary of the lengthy legal document.
As an abstracter, she reads and summarizes scientific papers for publication.
A skilled abstracter can condense complex data into a brief, understandable overview.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summarizer - a person who summarizes something
  • Synonym: abstractor - an alternative spelling of 'abstracter', especially common in North America

Etymology

Formed by adding the agent suffix '-er' to 'abstract'.
