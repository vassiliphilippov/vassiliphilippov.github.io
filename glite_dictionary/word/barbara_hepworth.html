Barbara Hepworth

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɑrbərə ˈhɛpwɜrθ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbərə ˈhɛpwəθ / (BrE)
(1902–1975) a British sculptor known for her modern abstract works, often in stone or wood.
British modernist sculptor (1902–1975)

Examples

The museum hosted an exhibition of Barbara Hepworth's iconic sculptures.
Many art students study the works of Barbara Hepworth for inspiration.
Barbara Hepworth played a significant role in the development of British modernist art.
