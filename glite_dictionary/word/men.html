men

1. NOUN

/ m…õn / (AmE), / men / (BrE)
the workers available to carry out a job, especially used collectively in contexts like military or organizations
workforce; group of workers

Examples

The general inspected his men before the operation began.
We need more men to finish this project on time.
The captain thanked his men for their hard work and dedication.

Etymology

Plural of 'man', from Old English 'mann' (person, human being).
