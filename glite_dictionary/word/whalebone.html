whalebone

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪlˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈweɪl.bəʊn / (BrE)
A tough, flexible material from the upper jaws of certain whales, historically used in corsets, umbrella ribs, and fan ribs.
material from whales used in corsets and fans

Examples

The corset was stiffened with whalebone to provide support.
In the Victorian era, whalebone was commonly used in making fashion accessories.
He marveled at the strength and flexibility of whalebone in old umbrella frames.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baleen - the filtering plates in the mouths of some whales, also known as whalebone
