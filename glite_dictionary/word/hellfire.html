hellfire

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɛlˌfaɪə / (BrE)
(in religion) a place or state of eternal fire, often depicted as a punishment for sinners after death.
eternal fire as punishment for sinners

Examples

Many preachers warned their congregation about hellfire and damnation.
The painting depicted sinners suffering in hellfire for their misdeeds.
Stories of hellfire were often used to instill fear and encourage moral behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hell - (in religion) the place of eternal punishment for the wicked after death
