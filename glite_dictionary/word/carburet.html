to carburet

1. VERB

/ ˈkɑːr.bjəˌrɛt / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.bjʊˌrɛt / (BrE)
To combine or treat with carbon, especially in chemical processing.
treat or combine with carbon

Examples

The process will carburet the gas to improve its energy content.
Early chemists attempted to carburet iron to create steel.
Certain oils can carburet fuels for more efficient combustion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to carbonize - to convert into carbon, as by heating or burning

Etymology

From French 'carburer', from 'carbone' (carbon), ultimately from Latin 'carbo'.
