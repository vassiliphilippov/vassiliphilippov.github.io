amour

1. NOUN

/ əˈmʊr / (AmE), / əˈmʊə / (BrE)
A usually secret or illicit love affair.
secret or illicit love affair

Examples

He had an amour with a married woman, which he tried desperately to keep hidden.
The novel explores the consequences of a forbidden amour between two colleagues.
Her past amour was the talk of the town for years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affair - a sexual relationship, often secret or extramarital
  • Synonym: tryst - a secret meeting, especially between lovers

Etymology

From French 'amour' (love), from Latin 'amor'.
