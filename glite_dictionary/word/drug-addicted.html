drug-addicted

1. ADJ

/ drʌɡ əˈdɪktɪd / (AmE), / drʌɡ əˈdɪktɪd / (BrE)
Unable to stop taking drugs because of a dependence on them.
addicted to drugs

Examples

The organization provides support for drug-addicted individuals seeking recovery.
He grew up in a neighborhood where many people were drug-addicted.
After years of struggle, she was finally able to help her drug-addicted brother get treatment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: addicted - physically or mentally dependent on a substance or activity
