ascendible

1. ADJ

/ əˈsɛndəbəl / (AmE), / əˈsɛndɪbəl / (BrE)
able to be ascended or climbed; capable of being gone up.
capable of being ascended

Examples

The steep hill was barely ascendible after the heavy snowfall.
Only the most experienced climbers found the rocky cliff ascendible.
The ancient staircase remained safe and ascendible despite its age.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: climbable - able to be climbed

Etymology

From Latin ascendere (to climb up) + -ible (able to be).
