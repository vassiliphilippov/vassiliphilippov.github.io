pull-off

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊlˌɑf / (AmE), / ˈpʊlˌɒf / (BrE)
a designated paved area beside a main road where vehicles can stop temporarily
designated roadside stopping area
american

Examples

We stopped at a pull-off to take pictures of the mountains.
There's a pull-off just ahead where you can make a phone call safely.
The scenic pull-off offers a beautiful view of the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lay-by - a place at the side of a road where vehicles may pull off and stop safely
