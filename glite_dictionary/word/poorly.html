poorly

1. ADV

/ ˈpɔrli / (AmE), / ˈpɔːli / (BrE)
in an unsatisfactory or inadequate way; not well
in an unsatisfactory way

Examples

He managed the project poorly and missed several important deadlines.
If you sleep poorly, you might feel tired the next day.
