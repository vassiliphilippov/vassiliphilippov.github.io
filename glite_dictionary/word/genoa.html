Genoa

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɛnoʊə / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnəʊə / (BrE)
a major seaport in northwestern Italy; the capital of the Liguria region
Italian seaport and regional capital

Examples

Many cruise ships dock at Genoa before touring the Mediterranean.
The historical center of Genoa is a UNESCO World Heritage site.
She spent a semester studying architecture in Genoa and exploring its ancient port.

Etymology

From Italian 'Genova' (the native name).
