stan

1. NOUN

/ stæn / (AmE), / stæn / (BrE)
an extremely devoted or obsessive fan of a particular celebrity, genre, or work
devoted or obsessive fan
informal slang

Examples

She has been a stan of that singer since her debut album.
The online community is filled with stans who defend their favorite show passionately.
He proudly admitted to being a stan for the comic book series.

Synset Examples

He's a real stan for that pop star, attending every concert.
She would proudly call herself a stan of the series.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fan - a person who has a strong interest in or admiration for something or someone

Etymology

Originated from Eminem's song 'Stan' (2000), about an obsessive fan named Stan.
