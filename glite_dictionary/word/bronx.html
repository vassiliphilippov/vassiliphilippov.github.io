Bronx

1. PROPN

/ brɑŋks / (AmE), / brɒŋks / (BrE)
the northernmost of the five boroughs of New York City, known for its diverse culture and history
borough of New York City

Examples

She grew up in the Bronx and often talks about its vibrant neighborhoods.
Many famous hip-hop artists started their careers in the Bronx.
The Bronx Zoo is one of the largest metropolitan zoos in the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Manhattan - an island borough of New York City, known as a financial and cultural center
  • Synonym: Brooklyn - a borough of New York City, located on western Long Island
  • Synonym: Queens - a borough of New York City, the largest in area and second in population
  • Synonym: Staten Island - the southernmost borough of New York City, primarily residential

Etymology

Named after Jonas Bronck, an early Dutch settler.
