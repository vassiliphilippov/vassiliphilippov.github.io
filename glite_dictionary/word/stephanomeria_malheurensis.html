Stephanomeria malheurensis

1. NOUN

/ stɛf.ə.noʊˈmɪr.i.ə ˌmæl.hjuːˈrɛn.sɪs / (AmE), / stɛf.ə.nəʊˈmɪə.ri.ə ˌmæl.hjʊəˈrɛn.sɪs / (BrE)
A rare, small flowering plant native to Oregon, resembling mustard and classified as a threatened species.
a rare Oregon plant species

Examples

Stephanomeria malheurensis is listed as a threatened species due to its extremely limited range in Oregon.
Botanists are working to preserve the natural habitat of Stephanomeria malheurensis.
Unlike other local flora, Stephanomeria malheurensis closely resembles certain types of mustard plants.

Etymology

Genus name from Greek 'stephanos' (crown) and 'meros' (part); species named after Malheur County, Oregon.
