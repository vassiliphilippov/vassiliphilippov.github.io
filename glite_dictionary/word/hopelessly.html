hopelessly

1. ADV

/ ˈhoʊplɪsli / (AmE), / ˈhəʊpləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows or expresses a lack of hope; in a dispirited manner.
without hope; in a despairing manner

Examples

She looked hopelessly at the mess in her room, unsure where to start cleaning.
After hearing the bad news, he sighed hopelessly and sat down in silence.
The team tried hopelessly to win the game, but their efforts were in vain.

Synset Examples

the first Mozartian opera to be subjected to this curious treatment ran dispiritedly for five performances

Related Terms

  • Synonym: despairingly - in a way that shows despair

Etymology

From 'hopeless' + '-ly', forming the adverb from the adjective.

2. ADV

/ ˈhoʊpləsli / (AmE), / ˈhəʊpləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows or involves no hope; without any possibility of success or improvement.
without hope; with no chance of success

Examples

She was hopelessly lost in the unfamiliar city streets after dark.
The instructions were hopelessly confusing to everyone who read them.
He is hopelessly in love with his childhood friend.

Synset Examples

the papers were hopelessly jumbled
he is hopelessly romantic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desperately - in a way that shows despair, often similar in context to hopelessly

Etymology

Formed from 'hopeless' + '-ly' (adjective + adverb suffix).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00202043-r
Synset POS: r

3. ADV

/ ˈhoʊplɪsli / (AmE), / ˈhəʊpləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows or feels a loss of hope; in a desperate or despairing manner.
in a hopeless or desperate way

Examples

She looked hopelessly at the broken computer, unsure how to fix it.
After searching for hours, they wandered hopelessly through the thick fog.
He fell hopelessly in love with her the moment they met.

Synset Examples

he hung his head hopelessly
‘I must die,’ he said hopelessly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desperately - in a way that shows despair or great need
  • Antonym: confidently - in a way that shows certainty and hope

Etymology

Formed from 'hopeless' + '-ly', with 'hopeless' from 'hope' + '-less'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00201821-r
Synset POS: r
