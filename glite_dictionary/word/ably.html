ably

1. ADV

/ ˈeɪbli / (AmE), / ˈeɪbli / (BrE)
In a competent, skillful, or effective manner.
in a competent or skillful way

Examples

She managed the team ably throughout the busy season.
The lawyer ably defended his client in court.
All tasks assigned to her were ably completed ahead of schedule.

Synset Examples

they worked competently

Related Terms

  • Synonym: competently - in a competent manner
  • Antonym: ineptly - in an incompetent or clumsy manner

Etymology

From "able" + "-ly", based on the Latin "habilis" (able, handy).
