Chaga

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑːɡə / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːɡə / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken by the Chaga people in northern Tanzania
Bantu language of northern Tanzania

Examples

The Chaga language is used in daily communication among the Chaga communities on Mount Kilimanjaro.
Linguists have studied Chaga as part of their research on Bantu languages.
She learned basic phrases in Chaga before traveling to Tanzania.
