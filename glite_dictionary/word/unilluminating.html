unilluminating

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪˈluːməˌneɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈljuːmɪneɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
not making something clearer or easier to understand; failing to shed light on a subject
not clarifying or informative

Examples

The professor's explanation was thoroughly unilluminating, leaving the students even more confused.
His comments on the issue were disappointingly unilluminating and added nothing to the discussion.
Many readers found the report unilluminating and lacking in useful details.

Synset Examples

an unenlightening comment

Related Terms

  • Antonym: illuminating - making something clearer or easier to understand
  • Synonym: unenlightening - not providing useful information or insight

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) and 'illuminating' (from Latin *illuminare*, to light up).
