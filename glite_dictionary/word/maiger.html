maiger

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈmeɪgə / (BrE)
A large marine food fish (Argyrosomus regius) found in European waters, prized for its firm, white flesh.
large European marine food fish

Examples

The fisherman was pleased with the size of the maiger he caught off the coast of France.
Grilled maiger is considered a delicacy in many European seaside towns.
Researchers are studying the migration patterns of the maiger in the Mediterranean Sea.
