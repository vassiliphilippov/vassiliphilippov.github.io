twenty-sixth

1. ADJ

/ ˌtwɛntiˈsɪksθ / (AmE), / ˌtwentiˈsɪksθ / (BrE)
Coming next after the twenty-fifth in a sequence; being number 26 in order.
ordinal number, 26th in a sequence

Examples

Her birthday falls on the twenty-sixth of June each year.
This is the twenty-sixth time he has visited the museum.
He finished in twenty-sixth place in the race.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-fifth - ordinal number, 25th in a sequence
  • Degree Higher: twenty-seventh - ordinal number, 27th in a sequence
