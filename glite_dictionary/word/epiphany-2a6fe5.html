epiphany

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈpɪf.ə.ni / (AmE), / ɪˈpɪf.əni / (BrE)
A moment when a divine being reveals itself to humans; a manifestation of a god or the divine.
divine manifestation

Examples

The prophets spoke of an epiphany in which the divine would become visible to all.
In ancient myths, an epiphany is often described as a god appearing before mortals.
The festival celebrates the epiphany of Christ to the Magi.

Etymology

From Greek epiphaneia (manifestation, striking appearance), from epiphainesthai (to appear).
