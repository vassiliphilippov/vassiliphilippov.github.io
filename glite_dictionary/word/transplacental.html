transplacental

1. ADJ

/ trænspləˈsɛntəl / (AmE), / trænzpləˈsɛntəl / (BrE)
Occurring through or by way of the placenta.
through the placenta
medical

Examples

The virus can be transmitted via transplacental transfer from mother to fetus.
Transplacental drug exposure may impact fetal development.
The doctor discussed possible transplacental antibody passage with the expecting parents.

Synset Examples

transplacental passage of nutrients

Etymology

From Latin 'trans-' (across) and 'placenta'.
