Hayastan

1. PROPN

/ haɪ.əˈstɑn / (AmE), / haɪ.əˈstɑːn / (BrE)
the native (Armenian) name for Armenia, a country in southwestern Asia
Armenia (native name)

Examples

Many Armenians refer to their homeland as Hayastan rather than Armenia.
The diaspora community often feels a strong connection to Hayastan.
He traveled from Paris to Hayastan to explore his ancestral roots.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Armenia - A country in southwestern Asia; also known by its native name, Hayastan

Etymology

From Armenian Հայաստանի Հանրապետություն (Hayastani Hanrapetut’yun), meaning 'Republic of Armenia'; 'Hayastan' itself is from 'Hayk', a legendary ancestor of Armenians, and Iranian suffix '-stan' meaning 'land'.
