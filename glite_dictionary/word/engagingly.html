engagingly

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that attracts and holds attention or interest; charmingly or appealingly.
in an appealing or charming manner

Examples

She spoke engagingly about her travels, captivating the entire audience.
The book is written so engagingly that readers find it hard to put down.
He smiled engagingly, making everyone feel instantly welcome.

Synset Examples

she played the role engagingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charmingly - in a charming or delightful way
  • Antonym: uninterestingly - in a way that does not attract interest or attention

Etymology

From 'engaging' + '-ly.' 'Engaging' from Old French 'engagier' (to pledge) via 'engage.'
