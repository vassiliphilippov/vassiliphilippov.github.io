bustier

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌsti.eɪ / (AmE), / ˈbʌsti.eɪ / (BrE)
a close-fitting, strapless top worn by women, often as lingerie or as part of evening wear
strapless women's top or lingerie

Examples

She wore a black bustier under her jacket for a chic evening look.
The designer's latest collection features an elegant lace bustier.
Many brides choose a strapless bustier to wear under their wedding dresses.

Etymology

From French 'bustier', from 'buste' (bust, chest).
