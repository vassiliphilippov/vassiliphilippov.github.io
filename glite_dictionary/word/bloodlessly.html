bloodlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈblʌd.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈblʌd.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a manner that does not involve bloodshed or violence; without causing blood to be shed.
without causing bloodshed

Examples

The revolution ended bloodlessly, with hardly a shot fired.
He took control of the company bloodlessly, avoiding any major conflicts.
Political power shifted bloodlessly after weeks of peaceful negotiation.

Synset Examples

the coup disposed of the dictator bloodlessly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bloodless - without bloodshed; without violence

Etymology

From 'bloodless' + '-ly'.
