Las Cruces

1. PROPN

/ lɑs ˈkruːsɪz / (AmE), / lɑːs ˈkruːsɪz / (BrE)
a city in southern New Mexico, United States, situated on the Rio Grande.
city in New Mexico, USA

Examples

Many retirees have chosen Las Cruces for its warm climate and scenic landscapes.

Etymology
