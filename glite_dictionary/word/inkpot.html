inkpot

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋkˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈɪŋkˌpɒt / (BrE)
A small container or bottle used to hold ink, especially for writing with a pen.
small container for ink

Examples

The old wooden desk still had an inkpot in the corner.
He dipped his quill into the inkpot before writing each line.
Antique collectors sometimes search for Victorian inkpots at markets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ink bottle - a bottle containing ink for refilling pens

Etymology

Formed from 'ink' + 'pot', referring to a pot (container) for ink.
