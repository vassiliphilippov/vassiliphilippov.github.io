unconscientious

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌkɑːnʃiˈɛnʃəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌkɒnʃiˈɛnʃəs / (BrE)
Not guided by or showing a sense of right and wrong; not conscientious or careful about doing what is right.
not careful or principled

Examples

The manager was fired for being unconscientious in handling sensitive documents.
His unconscientious attitude led to frequent mistakes at work.
It is risky to trust important tasks to someone so unconscientious.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: conscientious - wishing to do what is right, especially to do one's work well and thoroughly

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix un- to conscientious.
