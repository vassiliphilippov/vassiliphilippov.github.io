Nevadan

1. NOUN

/ nəˈvædən / (AmE), / nɪˈvɑːdən / (BrE)
a person who was born in or is a resident of Nevada, a state in the western United States
a person from Nevada (U.S.)

Examples

She is a proud Nevadan who celebrates her state's history every year.
The Nevadan delegation traveled to Washington for the annual conference.
As a lifelong Nevadan, he has seen significant changes in Las Vegas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nevada - a state in the western United States

Etymology

From Nevada, the U.S. state, with the suffix -an forming demonyms.
