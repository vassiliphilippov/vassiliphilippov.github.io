uninhibited

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪnˈhɪbɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈhɪbɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a person or their behavior) expressing feelings or thoughts freely and openly without restraint or self-consciousness.
free and open; not restrained or self-conscious

Examples

Her uninhibited laughter filled the room and made everyone smile.
The dancer's uninhibited movements captivated the entire audience.
He admired her uninhibited approach to expressing her opinions.

Synset Examples

uninhibited exuberance

Related Terms

  • Antonym: restrained - characterized by reserve or moderation; kept in check
  • Synonym: uninhibitedly - in an uninhibited manner; without restraint

Etymology

From un- (not) + inhibited, from Latin inhibitus (held in check) via inhibit.
