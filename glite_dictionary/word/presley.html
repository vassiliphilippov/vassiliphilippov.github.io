Presley

1. PROPN

/ ˈprɛzli / (AmE), / ˈprɛzli / (BrE)
(1935–1977), United States rock singer whose many hit records and flamboyant style greatly influenced American popular music; chiefly refers to Elvis Presley.
famous American rock singer, Elvis Presley

Examples

Many fans still celebrate Presley's birthday every year.
The influence of Presley on modern music cannot be overstated.
She owns a rare autograph from Presley that she treasures deeply.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Elvis - United States singer and actor, known as the 'King of Rock and Roll'
