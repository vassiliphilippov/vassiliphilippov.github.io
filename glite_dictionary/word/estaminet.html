estaminet

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛstəmiˈneɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛstamɪˈneɪ / (BrE)
A small, typically shabby cafe or bar, especially in France or Belgium, selling wine, beer, and coffee.
small shabby French or Belgian cafe

Examples

During their travels, they stopped at a quaint estaminet in a small Belgian village.
The novel's protagonist spends his evenings playing cards at the local estaminet.
Tourists were delighted by the authentic atmosphere of the old estaminet on the corner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cafe - small restaurant serving coffee and light meals

Etymology

From French 'estaminet' (small cafe or bar).
