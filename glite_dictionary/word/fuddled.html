fuddled

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʌd.əld / (AmE), / ˈfʌd.əld / (BrE)
Very drunk; in a confused or stupefied state due to alcohol.
very drunk; confused by alcohol

Examples

After drinking several glasses of wine, he appeared quite fuddled at the dinner table.
Her fuddled speech made it clear she wasn’t thinking straight anymore.
The fuddled driver struggled to find his car keys in the parking lot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inebriated - drunk; intoxicated with alcohol
  • Antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol; not drunk

Etymology

From the verb 'fuddle' (to confuse or make drunk), probably from a dialectal alteration of 'fozzle' or 'fuzzle'.
