alarming

1. ADJ

/ əˈlɑrmɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈlɑːmɪŋ / (BrE)
causing worry, fear, or concern because of a sense of danger or threat
causing fear or concern

Examples

The news of the sudden outbreak was truly alarming.
Her alarming behavior worried her friends and family.
The number of accidents last year is quite alarming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frightening - causing fear or alarm
  • Antonym: comforting - making people feel less worried or afraid

Etymology

From 'alarm' + '-ing'; 'alarm' from Old Italian 'all'arme!' (to arms).
