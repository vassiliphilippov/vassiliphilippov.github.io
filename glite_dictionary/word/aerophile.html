aerophile

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrəˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈɛərəˌfaɪl / (BrE)
A person who is fond of aviation or enjoys flying.
lover of aviation

Examples

As a lifelong aerophile, he attends every local airshow he can find.
The museum attracts aerophiles from all over the country.
Her father is something of an aerophile, collecting airplane models and pilot magazines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aviation enthusiast - a person who is interested in or passionate about aviation

Etymology

From Greek 'aēr' (air) + '-phile' (lover of).
