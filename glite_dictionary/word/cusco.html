Cusco

1. PROPN

/ ˈkʊs.koʊ / (AmE), / ˈkʊz.kəʊ / (BrE)
a city in southern Peru, located in the Andes, known as the former capital of the Inca Empire
city in Peru; former Inca capital

Examples

Many tourists visit Cusco before heading to Machu Picchu.
Cusco was once the flourishing capital of the vast Inca Empire.
The historic center of Cusco is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Etymology

From Quechua *Qusqu* or *Qosqo*, the indigenous name of the city.
