unseaworthy

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈsiːˌwɝːði / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈsiːˌwɜːði / (BrE)
Not fit or safe to travel by sea.
not safe or fit for sea travel

Examples

The ship was declared unseaworthy after a thorough inspection by the authorities.
Sailing in such an unseaworthy boat put everyone's life at risk.
The fishermen refused to board the unseaworthy vessel.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: seaworthy - fit or safe for a voyage at sea

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'seaworthy' (fit for sailing at sea).
