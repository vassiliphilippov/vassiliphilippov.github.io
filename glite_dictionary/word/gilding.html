gilding

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
a thin layer or coating of gold or a material imitating gold applied to a surface for decoration
a coating of gold or gold-like material

Examples

The ornate frame was finished with a layer of gilding to enhance its beauty.
Much of the original gilding on the statue had faded over the years.
The restoration process included repairing damaged gilding on the ceiling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gold leaf - very thin sheets of gold used for decoration
  • Synonym: plating - the process of coating a metal with a thin layer of another metal

Etymology

From Middle English gildyng, from gild (to cover with gold) + -ing.
