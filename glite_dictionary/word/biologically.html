biologically

1. ADV

/ ˌbaɪ.əˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to biology or living organisms.
in terms of biology

Examples

These twins are biologically identical, but their personalities are different.
The food is biologically grown without synthetic chemicals.
Humans and chimpanzees are biologically similar in many ways.

Synset Examples

biologically related

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biological - relating to biology or living organisms

Etymology

From 'biological' + '-ly', where 'biological' derives from Greek 'bios' (life) and 'logia' (study).
