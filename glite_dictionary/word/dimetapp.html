Dimetapp

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪməˌtæp / (AmE), / ˈdɪmɪˌtæp / (BrE)
A trademarked over-the-counter medication containing an antihistamine and a decongestant, used to relieve nasal congestion and treat rhinitis.
brand of cold and allergy medication

Examples

My doctor recommended Dimetapp to help with my allergy symptoms.
You can buy Dimetapp over the counter at most pharmacies.
Children's Dimetapp is commonly used for relief of nasal congestion.
