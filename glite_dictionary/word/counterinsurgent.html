counterinsurgent

1. ADJ

/ ˌkaʊntərɪnˈsɜrdʒənt / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntərɪnˈsɜːdʒənt / (BrE)
relating to actions, strategies, or forces aimed at combating or defeating an insurgency, especially by a government
relating to combating insurgency

Examples

The government implemented counterinsurgent strategies to restore order in the conflict zone.
Specialized counterinsurgent units were deployed to suppress the rebel uprising.
Her research focuses on counterinsurgent tactics in modern warfare.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: insurgent - a person who rises in active revolt against authority
  • Synonym: counterinsurgency - military or political action taken to defeat insurgency

Etymology

From 'counter-' (against) + 'insurgent' (a person who rises in revolt).
