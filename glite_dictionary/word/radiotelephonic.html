radiotelephonic

1. ADJ

/ ˌreɪdioʊˌtɛləˈfɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌreɪdɪəʊˌtɛlɪˈfɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or carried out by means of radiotelephony (the transmission of voice by radio waves)
relating to radiotelephony

Examples

The pilot maintained radiotelephonic contact with air traffic control throughout the flight.
A radiotelephonic system was installed on the ship for emergency communication.
Early expeditions relied on radiotelephonic equipment to keep in touch with their teams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiotelephony - the transmission of speech by radio waves

Etymology

From radio- + telephonic, patterned after radiotelephony.
