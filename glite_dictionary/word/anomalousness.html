anomalousness

1. NOUN

/ əˈnɑː.mə.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / əˈnɒm.ə.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being different from what is usual, normal, or expected; deviation from the common order, form, or rule.
quality of being abnormal or irregular

Examples

The anomalousness of his results prompted scientists to question existing theories.
Many were puzzled by the anomalousness in weather patterns this year.
She was fascinated by the anomalousness of certain animal behaviors.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: normality - the state of being normal or typical
  • Synonym: abnormality - the state of being abnormal or deviant

Etymology

From 'anomalous' + '-ness'; 'anomalous' from Greek anōmalos (uneven, irregular).
