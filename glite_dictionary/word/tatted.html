tatted

1. ADJ

/ ˈtætɪd / (AmE), / ˈtætɪd / (BrE)
having many tattoos on the body
covered in tattoos
informal

Examples

He showed off his tatted arms at the concert.
The tatted bartender has a story behind every design.
Many athletes these days are heavily tatted.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tattooed - having one or more tattoos

Etymology

Formed from the past participle of 'tat' (short for 'tattoo').
