hauncher

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔntʃər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːntʃə / (BrE)
a machine used to make shaped cuts on the end or edge of a board, especially in woodworking.
machine for shaping board ends or edges

Examples

The carpenter used the hauncher to shape the tenon joints quickly and precisely.
Each hauncher in the workshop is maintained regularly to ensure accurate cutting.
Training on the hauncher is required before employees can use it for door frame production.
