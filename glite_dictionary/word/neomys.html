Neomys

1. NOUN

/ ˈniːəˌmɪs / (AmE), / ˈniːəʊmɪs / (BrE)
A genus of aquatic shrews in the family Soricidae, found in Europe and parts of Asia.
genus of aquatic shrews

Examples

The water shrew, belonging to the genus Neomys, is well adapted for swimming.
Researchers observed several Neomys species along the riverbank, noting their distinct foraging behavior.
Unlike other shrews, Neomys has stiff hairs on its feet to aid in swimming.

Etymology

From Neo- (new) and Greek mys (mouse), referring to its distinction from other shrews.
