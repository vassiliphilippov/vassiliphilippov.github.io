1. ADJ

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌnmɪˈsteɪkəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnmɪˈsteɪkəbl / (BrE)
so clear or obvious that it cannot be mistaken for anything else; easily recognized.
impossible to mistake; obvious

Examples

The manager's disappointment was unmistakable in his tone of voice.
There was an unmistakable sense of excitement in the air before the concert began.
The smell of freshly baked bread is unmistakable and always reminds me of home.

Synset Examples

his opposition to slavery was unmistakable

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obvious - easily perceived or understood; clear, self-evident
  • Antonym: ambiguous - open to more than one interpretation; not having one obvious meaning

Etymology

From un- (not) + mistakable, based on mistake (to misunderstand or misinterpret), from Old French *mestake*, from Old Norse *mistaka* (to take in error).
