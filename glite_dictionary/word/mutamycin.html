Mutamycin

1. NOUN

/ ˌmjuːtəˈmaɪsɪn / (AmE), / ˌmjuːtəˈmaɪsɪn / (BrE)
the trade name for mitomycin, an antibiotic and anticancer drug derived from Streptomyces bacteria, used in chemotherapy
anticancer antibiotic drug (trade name)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Mutamycin as part of her cancer treatment regimen.
Researchers are studying the effectiveness of Mutamycin in treating various types of tumors.
Because of its potency, Mutamycin is administered only under careful medical supervision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mitomycin - an antibiotic compound used in cancer treatment
