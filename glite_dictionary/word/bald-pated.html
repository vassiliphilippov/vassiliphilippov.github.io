bald-pated

1. ADJ

/ ˌbɔldˈpeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌbɔːldˈpeɪtɪd / (BrE)
Having little or no hair on the top of the head; bald.
lacking hair on the scalp

Examples

The bald-pated professor gave an inspiring lecture to the students.
A bald-pated man waited patiently at the bus stop.
His bald-pated appearance made him easily recognizable in the crowd.

Synset Examples

a bald pate
a bald-headed gentleman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bald - having no hair on the scalp

Etymology

From 'bald' (lacking hair) and 'pate' (top of the head).
