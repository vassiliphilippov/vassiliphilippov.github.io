scented

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɛntɪd / (BrE)
having a pleasant smell, especially because perfume or a similar substance has been added.
having a pleasant or added fragrance

Examples

She placed a scented candle on the table to create a relaxing atmosphere.
He bought scented soap as a gift for his mother.
The scented letter reminded her of spring gardens.

Synset Examples

perfumed boudoir
perfumed stationery
scented soap

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell
  • Antonym: unscented - having no added scent or fragrance

Etymology

From the verb 'scent' + -ed, indicating something provided with a scent.

Examples

/ ˈsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɛntɪd / (BrE)

having a noticeable fragrance

Examples

The garden was filled with scented roses in full bloom.
He loves using scented soap because of its refreshing fragrance.

Synset Examples

scented flowers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell
  • Antonym: unscented - not having a noticeable scent or fragrance
