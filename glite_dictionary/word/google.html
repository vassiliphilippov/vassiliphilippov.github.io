to google

1. VERB

/ ˈɡuɡəl / (AmE), / ˈɡuːɡl / (BrE)
to search for information about someone or something on the internet using the Google search engine
search online using Google

Examples

If you don't know the answer, just google it.
She googled her favorite author to find recent news.
I spent an hour googling cheap flight deals last night.

Synset Examples

He googled the woman he had met at the party
My children are googling all day

Related Terms

  • Synonym: search - to look for information, especially on the internet
  • Synonym: to look up - to seek information, especially in reference books or databases

Etymology

Trademark of Google, the internet search company, from 1998; ultimately from 'googol' (a large number, 10¹⁰⁰).
