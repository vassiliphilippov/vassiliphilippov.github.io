appetence

1. NOUN

/ ˈæpɪtəns / (AmE), / ˈapɪtəns / (BrE)
a strong desire or craving for something; longing or appetite
strong desire or craving

Examples

She felt an unmistakable appetence for adventure that urged her to travel.
His appetence for knowledge led him to read constantly.
The appetence for success drove her to work late every night.

Synset Examples

an appetite for life
the object of life is to satisfy as many appetencies as possible

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appetite - a natural desire for food or other desires
  • Synonym: craving - an intense urge for something
  • Antonym: aversion - a strong dislike or disinclination

Etymology

From French 'appétence', from Latin 'appetentia' (desire), from 'appetere' (to desire).
