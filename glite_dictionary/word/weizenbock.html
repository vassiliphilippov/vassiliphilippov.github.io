Weizenbock

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪtsənˌbɑk / (AmE), / ˈvaɪtsənˌbɒk / (BrE)
a German wheat beer of strong strength, combining characteristics of bock and wheat beers
strong German wheat beer

Examples

The brewery released a new Weizenbock for their winter seasonal lineup.
A traditional Weizenbock features rich malt flavors with hints of banana and clove.
He paired the roasted pork with a chilled glass of Weizenbock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bock - a strong German lager

Etymology

German, from 'Weizen' (wheat) + 'Bock' (bock beer, a strong lager).
