nontoxicity

1. NOUN

/ nɑn.tɑkˈsɪɾ.i / (AmE), / nɒn.tɒkˈsɪt.i / (BrE)
The quality or state of not being toxic or poisonous.
state of not being toxic

Examples

The nontoxicity of the cleaning product makes it safe for use around children and pets.
Researchers emphasized the nontoxicity of the new chemical compound in their study results.
Regulations require manufacturers to demonstrate the nontoxicity of materials used in food packaging.

Synset Examples

The nontoxicity of AlSO4 has also been indicated by soil culture experiments.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: toxicity - the quality of being toxic or poisonous

Etymology

From non- (not) + toxicity.
