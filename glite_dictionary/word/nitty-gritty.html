nitty-gritty

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɪtiˈɡrɪti / (AmE), / ˌnɪtiˈɡrɪti / (BrE)
the most important or practical details of a subject or situation; the essentials
essential details; the basics
informal

Examples

Let's skip the introductions and get down to the nitty-gritty of the proposal.
The manager wanted to discuss the nitty-gritty of the new marketing strategy with the team.
When you look at the nitty-gritty of the plan, some problems become apparent.

Synset Examples

We need to address the nitty-gritty of the budget.
Understanding the nitty-gritty helps avoid mistakes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: essentials - fundamental elements or core aspects
  • Synonym: details - individual features, facts, or items
  • Antonym: superficial - concerned only with the surface or obvious; not thorough
