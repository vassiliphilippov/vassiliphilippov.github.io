above-named

1. ADJ

/ əˈbʌv neɪmd / (AmE), / əˈbʌv neɪmd / (BrE)
mentioned or named earlier in the same text, typically in formal or legal contexts
named earlier in the text
legal

Examples

All claims should be submitted by the above-named individual.
The above-named company is responsible for the payment.
If you have questions, please contact the above-named representative.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aforementioned - mentioned previously in the same context
  • Antonym: below-named - named later in the text
