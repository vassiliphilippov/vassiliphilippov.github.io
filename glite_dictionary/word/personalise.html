to personalise

1. VERB

/ ˈpɜːrsənəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make something personal or tailored to the needs or preferences of an individual
make personal or individual
british

Examples

We can personalise your birthday cake with any message you like.
The company offers personalised training plans for each client.
Many websites now personalise content based on your browsing history.

Synset Examples

personalized service

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to personalize - to make something personal or more personal (US spelling)

Etymology

From personal + -ise, modelled on French personnaliser.
