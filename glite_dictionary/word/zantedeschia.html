Zantedeschia

1. NOUN

/ ˌzæn.təˈdɛs.ki.ə / (AmE), / ˌzantɪˈdɛskiə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the arum family, native to southern Africa, commonly known as calla lilies and cultivated for their distinctive trumpet-shaped flowers.
genus of plants, calla lilies

Examples

Many gardeners choose to plant Zantedeschia for their elegant white or colored blooms.
The Zantedeschia thrives in well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight.
Botanists have identified several species of Zantedeschia across southern Africa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calla lily - common name for Zantedeschia species

Etymology

Named after Italian botanist Giovanni Zantedeschi (1773–1846).
