scarily

1. ADV

/ ˈskɛrəli / (AmE), / ˈskeərəli / (BrE)
In a way that causes fear or is frightening.
in a frightening manner

Examples

The forest was scarily quiet as night fell.
He drove scarily fast on the icy road, making everyone nervous.
Her predictions about the future turned out to be scarily accurate.

Synset Examples

the disturbing thing about the Minister's behavior is that far from being artificial, it too often rings frighteningly true

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frighteningly - in a way that causes fear
  • Antonym: comfortingly - in a way that brings comfort

Etymology

From "scary" (frightening) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
