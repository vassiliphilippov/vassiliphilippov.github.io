borsht

1. NOUN

/ bɔrʃt / (AmE), / bɔːʃt / (BrE)
A soup of Eastern European origin, typically made with beets and often served with sour cream.
beet soup of Eastern European origin

Examples

We started our meal with a steaming bowl of borsht topped with sour cream.
Traditional borsht gets its vibrant color from fresh beets.
Many Eastern European families have their own secret recipe for borsht.

Etymology

From Yiddish 'borsht', from Russian 'borshch' (борщ).
