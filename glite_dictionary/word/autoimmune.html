autoimmune

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔːtoʊɪˈmjun / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊɪˈmjuːn / (BrE)
relating to or caused by an immune response of the body against its own healthy cells or tissues.
relating to immune response against oneself
medical

Examples

Some diseases, like lupus and rheumatoid arthritis, are classified as autoimmune disorders.
Researchers are exploring new ways to treat autoimmune conditions by regulating the immune system.
An autoimmune reaction occurs when the body's immune system mistakenly attacks its own tissues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immunity - the state of being protected from disease

Etymology

From auto- (self) + immune, indicating immunity against the self.
