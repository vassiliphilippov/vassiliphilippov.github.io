Saint Christopher-Nevis

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt ˈkrɪstəfər ˈniːvɪs / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈkrɪstəfə ˈniːvɪs / (BrE)
A country in the Caribbean Sea, consisting of the islands of Saint Kitts (Christopher) and Nevis; it gained independence from the United Kingdom in 1983.
Caribbean country (Saint Kitts and Nevis)

Examples

The economy of Saint Christopher-Nevis largely depends on tourism and agriculture.
After achieving independence, Saint Christopher-Nevis joined the Commonwealth of Nations.
Many visitors are drawn to the beaches of Saint Christopher-Nevis each year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Saint Kitts and Nevis - Caribbean country comprising the islands of Saint Kitts and Nevis

Etymology

The islands are named after Saint Christopher (now known as Saint Kitts) and Nevis; 'Christopher' referring to the patron saint of travelers.
