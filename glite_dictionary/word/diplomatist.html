diplomatist

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈplɑːmətɪst / (AmE), / dɪˈpləʊmətɪst / (BrE)
an official who is skilled in or engaged in international negotiations or diplomacy
official in international negotiations

Examples

The experienced diplomatist helped negotiate the peace treaty between the two countries.
As a skilled diplomatist, she handled delicate international discussions with great tact.
The conference room was filled with renowned diplomatists from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diplomat - official representing a country abroad

Etymology

From French 'diplomatiste', from 'diplomate' (diplomat).
