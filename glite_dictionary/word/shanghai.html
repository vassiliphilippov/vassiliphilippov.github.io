Shanghai

1. PROPN

/ ʃæŋˈhaɪ / (AmE), / ʃæŋˈhaɪ / (BrE)
A major city and seaport in eastern China, one of the largest cities in the world.
city in eastern China

Examples

After graduating from college, she moved to Shanghai to pursue a career in finance.
Millions of tourists visit Shanghai each year to see its urban skyline and cultural sites.
The port of Shanghai handles more shipping volume than any other city in the world.

Etymology

From Mandarin Chinese 上海 (Shànghǎi), meaning 'upon-the-sea.'
