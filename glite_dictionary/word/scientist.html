scientist

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / ˈsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
a person who is trained in and works in one or more fields of science, especially someone whose job is to discover new knowledge or facts through research
person with advanced knowledge of science

Examples

Dr. Harris is a leading scientist in the field of genetics.
The scientist carefully recorded the results of each experiment.
Many scientists believe climate change is accelerating due to human activity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: researcher - person who carries out research, especially in science

Etymology

From Latin scientia (knowledge) + English suffix -ist (denoting a person who practices or is concerned with something).
