outlawed

1. ADJ

/ ˈaʊtˌlɔd / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌlɑːd / (BrE)
forbidden by law; made illegal by official authority
forbidden by law

Examples

The organization was declared outlawed and its activities were banned by the government.
Possession of that substance is outlawed in several countries due to its harmful effects.
Many considered the group heroes, even though their actions were officially outlawed.

Synset Examples

an illegitimate seizure of power
illicit trade
an outlaw strike
unlawful measures

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illegal - forbidden by law or statute
  • Antonym: lawful - permitted by law

Etymology

From Middle English ‘outlawe’ (a person declared outside the protection of the law), past participle of ‘to outlaw’.
