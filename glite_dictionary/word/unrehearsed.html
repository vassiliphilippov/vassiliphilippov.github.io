unrehearsed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈhɜrst / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈhɜːst / (BrE)
not planned or practiced in advance; done without preparation
not planned or practiced; spontaneous

Examples

Her unrehearsed response impressed the interviewers with its honesty and clarity.
The actor delivered an unrehearsed speech after receiving his award, charming the audience.
They preferred an unrehearsed performance to capture the genuine energy of the band.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extemporaneous - spoken or done without preparation

Etymology

From the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'rehearse' (to practice), meaning not practiced.
