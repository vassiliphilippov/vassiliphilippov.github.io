Nuytsia

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːtsiə / (AmE), / ˈnʌɪtsiə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants consisting of the species Nuytsia floribunda, commonly known as the Western Australian Christmas tree.
genus of flowering plant (Nuytsia floribunda)

Examples

The botanist was fascinated by the unique characteristics of Nuytsia floribunda.
Nuytsia is well known in Western Australia for its bright orange flowers.
Researchers studied the parasitic nature of Nuytsia during the flowering season.

Etymology

Named after Dutch navigator Pieter Nuyts (1598–1655).

2. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪtsiə / (AmE), / ˈnʌɪtsɪə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants native to Western Australia, containing the species Nuytsia floribunda, commonly known as the Western Australian Christmas tree.
genus of plants native to Western Australia

Examples

Nuytsia floribunda is a well-known tree in Western Australia, often called the Christmas tree.
The brightly colored flowers of the Nuytsia attract many pollinators in summer.
Scientists studied the root system of Nuytsia to understand its unique hemiparasitic nature.

Etymology

Named after Dutch explorer Pieter Nuyts (1598–1655).
