arithmancy

1. NOUN

/ əˈrɪθ.mæn.si / (AmE), / əˈrɪθ.mən.si / (BrE)
the practice of divination by interpreting numbers
divination using numbers

Examples

In ancient times, practitioners believed that arithmancy could reveal one's destiny.
Some claim that arithmancy offers insight into the future based on names and dates of birth.
She studied arithmancy to understand the hidden meanings behind numbers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: numerology - the belief in the mystical significance of numbers

Etymology

From Greek arithmos (number) + manteia (divination).
