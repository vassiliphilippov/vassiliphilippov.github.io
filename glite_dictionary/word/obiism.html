obiism

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊ.biˌɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.bi.ɪz.əm / (BrE)
a belief system centered around sorcery and spiritual practices that originated in Africa and is practiced in the West Indies
West Indian sorcery belief system

Examples

The practice of obiism has deep roots in some Caribbean communities.
Researchers have studied the influence of obiism on local beliefs and customs.
He wrote a book detailing the rituals associated with obiism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obeah - West Indian folk magic or sorcery

Etymology

From 'Obeah', a West Indian term for folk magic, of African origin, plus the suffix '-ism'.
