rougeberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːʒˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈruːʒˌbɛri / (BrE)
A bushy tropical houseplant with white to pale pink flowers that develop into clusters of scarlet berries, native to the Americas.
tropical houseplant with scarlet berries

Examples

The rougeberry is popular for its decorative scarlet berries during the winter months.
She placed a potted rougeberry by the window to brighten the room.
The rougeberry blooms with delicate pink flowers before producing its vibrant fruit.
