purplish

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝːplɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːplɪʃ / (BrE)
Having a color somewhat like purple; intermediate between red and blue.
somewhat purple in color

Examples

She wore a purplish dress that shimmered in the light.
The bruise had turned a dark purplish color by the next day.
Some flowers display a subtle purplish hue in early spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purple - a color intermediate between red and blue

Etymology

Formed from 'purple' + '-ish' (adjective-forming suffix indicating likeness).
