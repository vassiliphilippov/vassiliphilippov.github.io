Mirish language

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪrɪʃ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɪrɪʃ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A little-known language of the Kamarupan group, spoken in northeastern India.
a Kamarupan language of northeastern India

Examples

Linguists have documented only a few texts in the Mirish language.
The Mirish language is considered endangered due to its small number of speakers.
Efforts are underway to preserve the Mirish language and its unique cultural heritage.
