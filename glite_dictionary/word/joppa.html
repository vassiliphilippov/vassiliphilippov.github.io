Joppa

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɑːpə / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒpə / (BrE)
a historic port city in western Israel on the Mediterranean Sea, now incorporated into Tel Aviv
historic port in western Israel

Examples

According to tradition, Jonah set sail from Joppa before being swallowed by the great fish.
Many ancient texts mention Joppa as an important trade hub.
Today, the area that was once Joppa is a vibrant district within Tel Aviv.

Etymology

From Hebrew Yafo (also Yaffa, Jaffa), known as Joppa in ancient Greek and Latin sources.
