overland

1. ADV

/ ˈoʊvɚˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəlænd / (BrE)
across or by land rather than by sea or air
by land, not by sea or air

Examples

They traveled overland from Istanbul to Beijing.
The supplies arrived overland after the storm made sea transport impossible.
It was once common for traders to journey overland across the deserts.

Synset Examples

an overland journey
the overland route used by Marco Polo

Related Terms

  • Antonym: oversea - across the sea
