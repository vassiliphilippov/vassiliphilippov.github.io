Calabria

1. PROPN

/ kəˈleɪbriə / (AmE), / kəˈleɪbriə / (BrE)
a region of southern Italy, forming the toe of the Italian 'boot'
region in southern Italy

Examples

She spent her summer exploring the beautiful beaches of Calabria in southern Italy.
Many traditional dishes from Calabria are famous for their use of spicy peppers.
After moving to Calabria, he fell in love with the region's rugged landscapes.

Etymology

From Latin Calabria, the name for the region in ancient times.
