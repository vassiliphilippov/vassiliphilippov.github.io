Pusan

1. PROPN

/ puːˈsɑːn / (AmE), / puːˈsæn / (BrE)
A port city in southeastern South Korea on the Korean Strait; it is the country's chief port and second largest city.
major port city in South Korea

Examples

After her job transfer, she moved to Pusan and quickly adapted to city life.
Many tourists visit Pusan for its beautiful beaches and lively festivals.
The port of Pusan handles a significant amount of international trade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Busan - alternate romanization of Pusan, same city
