hereby

1. ADV

/ hɪrˈbaɪ / (AmE), / hɪəˈbaɪ / (BrE)
(formal) As a result of this statement or action; by means of this.
by means of this statement or action

Examples

I hereby sentence you to five years in prison.
By signing below, I hereby agree to the terms and conditions.
The company hereby announces a new policy effective immediately.

Synset Examples

I hereby declare you man and wife

Etymology

From Middle English 'here' (in this place) + 'by' (by, near).
