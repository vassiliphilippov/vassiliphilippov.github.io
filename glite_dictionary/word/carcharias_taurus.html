Carcharias taurus

1. NOUN

/ kɑrˌkɛəriəs ˈtɔːrəs / (AmE), / kɑːˌkɛəriəs ˈtɔːrəs / (BrE)
A species of large, predatory shark found in shallow coastal waters on both sides of the Atlantic, sometimes known as the sand tiger shark and known for its sharp, jagged teeth.
large shallow-water shark species, sand tiger shark

Examples

Divers often spot the Carcharias taurus lurking near rocky reefs and wrecks.
The Carcharias taurus is sometimes mistakenly believed to be very aggressive.
Scientists track the migration patterns of Carcharias taurus to better understand their behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sand tiger shark - common name for Carcharias taurus

Etymology

From Greek *karcharías* (shark) and Latin *taurus* (bull).
