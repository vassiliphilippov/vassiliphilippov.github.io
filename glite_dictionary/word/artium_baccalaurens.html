Artium Baccalaurens

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑr.ti.əm bəˈkæl.jəˌlɜrnz / (AmE), / ˌɑː.ti.əm bəˈkæl.jəˌlɜːnz / (BrE)
The Latin designation for the academic degree of Bachelor of Arts (B.A.), awarded for undergraduate studies in arts and sciences.
Latin term for Bachelor of Arts degree

Examples

He proudly listed Artium Baccalaurens among his academic achievements.
The university awarded her the degree of Artium Baccalaurens in 1920.
On his diploma, the title Artium Baccalaurens was inscribed in bold Latin letters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bachelor of Arts - undergraduate degree in the arts or sciences

Etymology

Latin: 'artium' (of the arts) + 'baccalaureus' (bachelor).
