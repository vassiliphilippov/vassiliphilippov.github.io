Bostonian

1. NOUN

/ bɑːˈstoʊniən / (AmE), / bɒˈstəʊniən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Boston, especially Boston, Massachusetts.
native or resident of Boston

Examples

As a proud Bostonian, she loves the city’s rich history and vibrant culture.
Many Bostonians gather each year to celebrate Patriots' Day with a big parade.
He became a Bostonian after moving from Chicago and living there for over a decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: New Yorker - a native or resident of New York City
  • Synonym: Londoner - a native or resident of London

Etymology

From Boston, the name of the city, plus the suffix -ian denoting origin or association.
