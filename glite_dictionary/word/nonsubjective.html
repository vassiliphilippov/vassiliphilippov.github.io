nonsubjective

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnsəbˈdʒɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnsəbˈdʒɛktɪv / (BrE)
not influenced by personal feelings or opinions; based on facts or observable evidence
not influenced by personal feelings

Examples

His analysis was refreshingly nonsubjective, relying solely on the available data.
Scientific research should strive to be as nonsubjective as possible to avoid bias.
The judge sought a nonsubjective interpretation of the law, free from personal opinions.

Synset Examples

an objective appraisal
objective evidence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: objective - not influenced by personal feelings; unbiased, fact-based
  • Antonym: subjective - based on or influenced by personal feelings or opinions

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' meaning 'not', and 'subjective'.
