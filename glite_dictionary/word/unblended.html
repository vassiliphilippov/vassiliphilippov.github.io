unblended

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈblɛndɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈblɛndɪd / (BrE)
Not blended or mixed together; remaining in a pure or separate state.
not mixed or blended

Examples

The artist preferred to use unblended colors for a more striking effect.
He enjoyed the rich, unblended flavor of single malt whiskey.
The ingredients were left unblended to highlight their individual textures.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: blended - mixed together so the separate parts are not distinguishable

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'blend' (to mix).
