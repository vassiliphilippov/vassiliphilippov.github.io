Johann Wolfgang von Goethe

1. PROPN

/ ˈjoʊˌhɑn ˈvɔlfɡaŋ fɔn ˈɡɜːtə / (AmE), / ˈjoːhan ˈvɔlfɡaŋ fɔn ˈɡøːtə / (BrE)
(1749–1832) German poet, playwright, and novelist, widely regarded as one of the greatest literary figures in Western literature.
German poet and writer (1749–1832)

Examples

Johann Wolfgang von Goethe is best known for his tragic play "Faust."
The works of Johann Wolfgang von Goethe have been translated into numerous languages.
Many scholars consider Johann Wolfgang von Goethe a central figure in German literature.
