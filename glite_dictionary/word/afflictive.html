afflictive

1. ADJ

/ əˈflɪktɪv / (AmE), / əˈflɪktɪv / (BrE)
Causing pain, suffering, or distress.
causing pain or distress

Examples

The patient endured several afflictive treatments in hopes of a cure.
The economic downturn had afflictive consequences for many families in the region.
She tried to escape the afflictive memories of her childhood.

Synset Examples

it was a sore trial to him

Related Terms

  • Antonym: soothing - having a calming or relieving effect

Etymology

From Latin afflictivus, from affligere (to afflict).
