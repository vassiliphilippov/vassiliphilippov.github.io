informatively

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈfɔrmətɪvli / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɔːmətɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that provides useful or interesting information.
in an informative way

Examples

The professor spoke informatively about the history of the region, including many fascinating details.
She always answers questions informatively, making sure to include all the relevant facts.
The article was written informatively, making complex topics clear and accessible to the reader.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: informative - providing useful or interesting information
  • Antonym: unhelpfully - in a way that is not helpful or informative
