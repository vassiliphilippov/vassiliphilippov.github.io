genic

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒiː.nɪk / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nɪk / (BrE)
Relating to, produced by, or originating from a gene or genes.
relating to genes
medical

Examples

The scientist studied genic mutations responsible for the disease.
Certain genic regions influence eye color in humans.
The research focused on genic differences between the two plant species.

Synset Examples

genic combinations
genetic code

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genetic - relating to genes or heredity

Etymology

From gene + -ic, forming an adjective meaning 'of or relating to genes'.
