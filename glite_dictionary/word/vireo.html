Vireo

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪrioʊ / (AmE), / ˈvɪəriəʊ / (BrE)
A genus of small to medium-sized passerine birds in the family Vireonidae, native to the Americas.
a genus of birds in the Vireonidae family

Examples

The Vireo is commonly found in North and South America, inhabiting woodlands and forests.
Scientists have discovered a new species within the Vireo genus in Central America.
Members of the Vireo genus are known for their persistent singing during the breeding season.

Etymology

From Latin *vireo* (a green migratory bird).
