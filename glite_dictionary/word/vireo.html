vireo

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪrioʊ / (AmE), / ˈvɪəriəʊ / (BrE)
A small insect-eating American songbird, typically olive-grey in color, belonging to the Vireonidae family.
small insectivorous American songbird

Examples

We spotted a vireo singing in the branches above the trail.
Several species of vireo migrate south for the winter.
The vireo's distinctive song made it easy to identify among the other birds.

Etymology

From Latin *vireo* (meaning green bird or sprout, green plant).
