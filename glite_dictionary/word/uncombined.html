uncombined

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.kəmˈbaɪnd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kəmˈbaɪnd / (BrE)
not joined or united; separate or distinct from others
not joined or united; separate

Examples

The chemicals should remain uncombined until the final stage of the experiment.
Many gases exist in nature in an uncombined state.
The report analyzes both individual and uncombined company figures.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: combined - joined or united into one

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'combined' (from Latin 'combinare', to unite).
