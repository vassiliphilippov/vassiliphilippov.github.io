shingler

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪŋɡlər / (AmE), / ˈʃɪŋɡlə / (BrE)
a worker who installs shingles on roofs.
worker who installs roof shingles

Examples

The shingler completed the new roof just before it started to rain.
Each shingler on the team had years of experience with different types of roof shingles.
Hiring a professional shingler ensures the roof is properly protected from leaks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roofer - a person whose job is installing or repairing roofs
