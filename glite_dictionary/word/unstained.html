unstained

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsteɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsteɪnd / (BrE)
not having been coated with stain or varnish; not stained.
not treated with stain or varnish

Examples

The furniture in the room was left unstained to preserve the wood's natural color.
She preferred unstained wood for her table to maintain a rustic look.
Always use gloves when handling unstained surfaces to avoid leaving fingerprints.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stained - having been coated with stain or varnish
Source: WordNet

Synset POS: s

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsteɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsteɪnd / (BrE)
not marked or soiled by stains; free from spots or dirt.
not stained or marked

Examples

After hours of cleaning, the white tablecloth remained unstained for the entire evening.
The scientist required unstained glass slides for the experiment to avoid contamination.
Her reputation was unstained by any hint of scandal.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stained - marked or discolored by stains
  • Synonym: spotless - completely clean or free from dirt or marks
Source: WordNet

3. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsteɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsteɪnd / (BrE)
not marked or discolored by stains; clean or pure in appearance or condition.
not marked by stains; clean or pure

Examples

Despite hours of painting, her shirt remained unstained at the end of the day.
The museum showcased an unstained ancient manuscript in a glass case.
He prided himself on keeping his car's interior perfectly unstained.

Synset Examples

An apron keeps his clothing unstained

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stained - marked or discolored by stains

Etymology

From un- (not) + stained (past participle of 'stain').

4. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsteɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsteɪnd / (BrE)
(of reputation or character) not spoiled, blemished, or discredited; pure or untarnished.
not spoiled or tarnished (esp. of reputation)

Examples

He maintained an unstained reputation throughout his long legal career.
The judge was known for her unstained integrity and fairness.
Despite many accusations, his honor remained unstained in the eyes of the community.

Synset Examples

his unsullied name
an untarnished reputation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unsullied - not spoiled or made impure (of reputation, character)
  • Synonym: untarnished - not made less valuable or respected; not damaged (esp. reputation)
  • Antonym: stained - marked or tainted (esp. of reputation)
  • Antonym: tarnished - spoiled or made less good (esp. reputation)

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'stained' (soiled, marked); 'stain' from Old French 'esteindre', from Latin 'tingere' (to dye).
