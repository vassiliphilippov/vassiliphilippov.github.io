governorship

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌvərnərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvənəˌʃɪp / (BrE)
the position, rank, or office of a governor
office or position of a governor

Examples

His governorship was marked by economic reforms and increased foreign investment.
She was praised for her achievements during her governorship of the province.
The governorship changed hands after the closely contested election.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: governor - an official in charge of a region or state

Etymology

Formed from 'governor' + '-ship' (denoting office or position).
