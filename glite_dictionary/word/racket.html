racket

1. NOUN

/ ˈræk.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈræk.ɪt / (BrE)
a loud, unpleasant noise; a disturbance of sound that is harsh or annoying and lacks musical quality
loud, unpleasant noise

Examples

The children made such a racket playing upstairs that I couldn't concentrate on my work.
When the garbage trucks come early in the morning, the racket wakes up the whole neighborhood.
We had to shout over the racket at the construction site just to be heard.

Synset Examples

modern music is just noise to me

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noise - a sound, especially one that is loud or unpleasant
  • Synonym: din - a loud, unpleasant, and prolonged noise
  • Antonym: silence - the complete absence of sound

2. VERB

/ ˈrækɪt / (AmE), / ˈrækɪt / (BrE)
to hit a ball with a racket, especially in sports such as tennis or badminton.
hit a ball with a racket

Examples

He racketed the ball across the net with surprising force.
Players racket serves at high speeds during professional matches.
She racketed a winning shot to claim the last point.

3. NOUN

/ ˈrækɪt / (AmE), / ˈrækɪt / (BrE)
an illegal business or scheme, especially one that makes money through fraud, extortion, or other criminal means
illegal enterprise or criminal scheme

Examples

The police uncovered a racket involving fake lottery tickets sold online.
He was arrested for running a protection racket in the city.
The government vowed to put an end to the drug racket operating in the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scam - a dishonest scheme or fraud, usually for financial gain
  • Synonym: fraud - wrongful or criminal deception for financial or personal gain
  • Antonym: legitimate business - a legal and authorized business enterprise

Etymology

Possibly from Italian 'raquette', meaning 'a fraudulent scheme'.

4. NOUN

/ ˈrækɪt / (AmE), / ˈrækɪt / (BrE)
a sports implement with a handle and an oval frame strung with tightly interlaced strings, used to strike a ball or shuttlecock in games such as tennis or badminton
sports implement with strings for hitting a ball or shuttlecock

Examples

She bought a new racket for her tennis lessons.
The strings on his racket snapped during the match.
A good badminton racket should feel light and well-balanced.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bat - a solid implement used to hit a ball in games such as baseball or cricket
  • Synonym: racquet - alternate spelling of racket, used especially in sports contexts
