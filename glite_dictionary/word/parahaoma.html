parahaoma

1. NOUN

/ pɑːr.əˈhoʊ.mə / (AmE), / pɑː.rəˈhəʊ.mə / (BrE)
a ritual drink prepared from the Haoma plant, traditionally used in Zoroastrian religious ceremonies
Zoroastrian ritual drink from Haoma plant

Examples

During the ceremony, the priest offered parahaoma to the fire as part of the ritual.
The recipe for preparing parahaoma has been passed down through generations of Zoroastrian priests.
Scholars have studied the role of parahaoma in ancient Persian religious texts.

Synset Examples

The plant Haoma yielded the essential ingredient for the ritual drink, parahaoma.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: haoma - a sacred plant and its extract used in Zoroastrian rituals

Etymology

From Avestan 'para-haoma', referring to a preliminary Haoma preparation in religious rituals.
