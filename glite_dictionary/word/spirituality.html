spirituality

1. NOUN

/ ˌspɪrɪtʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌspɪrɪtjuˈæləti / (BrE)
the quality of being concerned with the human spirit or soul as opposed to material or physical things
concern with spiritual or religious matters

Examples

Many people find spirituality through meditation, prayer, or time spent in nature.
Her search for spirituality led her to study various world religions.
The retreat focused on developing inner peace and spirituality rather than religious rituals.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: materialism - a focus on material or physical things over spiritual matters

Etymology

From Late Latin spiritualitas (spiritual nature), from Latin spiritualis (spiritual).
