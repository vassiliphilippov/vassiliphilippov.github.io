bon vivant

1. NOUN

/ bɔ̃ viːˈvɑːnt / (AmE), / bɒ̃ viːˈvɒ̃ / (BrE)
A person who enjoys good food, drink, and luxurious living; someone devoted to refined sensuous enjoyment.
person who enjoys luxurious living

Examples

He is a true bon vivant, never missing an opportunity to try a new restaurant or vintage wine.
Known as a bon vivant, she hosted lavish dinner parties for all her friends.
Despite his reputation as a bon vivant, he always managed to stay in excellent health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: epicurean - person devoted to luxurious and sensuous pleasures, especially food and drink
  • Synonym: gourmand - person who enjoys eating and often eats to excess
  • Antonym: ascetic - person who practices severe self-discipline and abstention

Etymology

From French, literally ‘good liver’, from bon (‘good’) and vivant (‘living’).
