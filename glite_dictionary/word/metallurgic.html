metallurgic

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɛt̬əˈlɝːdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɛt.əlˈlɜː.dʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving metallurgy, the science and technology of metals
relating to metals or metallurgy

Examples

The new plant specializes in metallurgic processes for refining rare earth elements.
She pursued a career in metallurgic research after university.
Advances in metallurgic techniques have improved the strength of modern alloys.

Synset Examples

metallurgical engineer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metallurgical - relating to the science and technology of metals

Etymology

From French métallurgique, from metallurgy + -ic.
