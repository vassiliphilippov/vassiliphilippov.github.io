domestic

1. ADJ

/ dəˈmɛstɪk / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk / (BrE)
produced, made, or originating within a particular country; not foreign
produced in a particular country

Examples

The government imposed tariffs on domestic steel to protect local producers.
This company specializes in both imported and domestic furniture.
The restaurant only serves domestic wines from local vineyards.

Synset Examples

domestic wine
domestic oil

Related Terms

  • Antonym: foreign - produced outside one's own country

Etymology

From Latin domesticus (of the home, belonging to the household), from domus (house).

Synset POS: s

/ dəˈmɛstɪk / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk / (BrE)
