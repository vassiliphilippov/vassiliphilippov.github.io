unwontedly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈwɑntɪdli / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɒntɪdli / (BrE)
In an unusual or unaccustomed manner; not habitually.
in an unusual manner

Examples

The air was unwontedly silent after the storm had passed.
She responded unwontedly warmly to his greeting, surprising everyone in the room.
He smiled unwontedly, a rare sight that brightened the atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unusually - in a way that is not usual or common
  • Antonym: normally - in a usual or expected manner

Etymology

From unwonted + -ly, where 'unwonted' means 'not customary' (from 'un-' + archaic 'wont' meaning 'accustomed').
