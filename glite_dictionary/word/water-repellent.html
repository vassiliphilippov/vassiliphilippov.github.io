water-repellent

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɔtər rɪˈpɛlənt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə rɪˈpelənt / (BrE)
Not easily penetrated by water; resistant to the absorption or passage of water.
resistant to water penetration

Examples

She wore a water-repellent jacket to stay dry during the rainstorm.
The tent is made from water-repellent fabric, making it suitable for camping in wet conditions.
After applying the water-repellent spray, the shoes easily shed splashes and stains.

Synset Examples

a water-repellent coat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waterproof - impervious to water
  • Antonym: absorbent - able to soak up liquids easily
