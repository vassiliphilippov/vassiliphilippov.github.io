cattitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈkætɪˌtuːd / (AmE), / ˈkatɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
a confident, stylish, or sassy attitude, often likened to the mannerisms associated with cats
confident, cat-like attitude
informal

Examples

She walked into the meeting with pure cattitude, unfazed by anyone's opinion.
Her fashion sense is all about bold colors and cattitude.
The way he replied to the criticism was full of cattitude.

Synset Examples

He loved her sass; it was pure cattitude.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attitude - a manner, disposition, or feeling

Etymology

Blend of 'cat' and 'attitude', modeled after the confident or sassy nature attributed to cats.
