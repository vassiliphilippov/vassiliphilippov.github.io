porn

1. NOUN

/ pɔrn / (AmE), / pɔːn / (BrE)
Creative works such as writing, pictures, or films intended chiefly to stimulate sexual desire and regarded as having little or no artistic or literary value.
sexually explicit material

Examples

He was caught watching porn on his laptop at work.
Many websites restrict access to porn to protect minors.
The debate over the impact of porn on society continues among academics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pornography - sexually explicit material intended to arouse sexual desire

Etymology

Abbreviated form of 'pornography', from Greek *pornographos*, meaning 'writing about prostitutes'.
