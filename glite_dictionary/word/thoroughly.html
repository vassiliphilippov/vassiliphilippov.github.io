thoroughly

1. ADV

/ ˈθɝːəli / (AmE), / ˈθʌrəli / (BrE)
In a complete and detailed way; completely and absolutely.
completely; in great detail

Examples

The room was thoroughly cleaned before the guests arrived.
She read the report thoroughly to understand every detail.
The evidence was thoroughly examined by the police.

Synset Examples

he was soundly defeated
we beat him good

Related Terms

  • Synonym: completely - entirely; to the fullest extent
  • Antonym: partially - in part; not completely

Etymology

From 'thorough' + '-ly', formed in Middle English.

2. ADV

/ ˈθɜːroʊli / (AmE), / ˈθʌrəli / (BrE)
In a very detailed and complete way; with great attention to detail, so that nothing is missed.
completely; in a detailed manner

Examples

She checked the report thoroughly before submitting it to her boss.
The house was thoroughly cleaned after the renovation work was finished.
Please make sure to thoroughly explain the procedure to the team.

Synset Examples

we searched the files thoroughly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: completely - to the greatest degree possible, fully
  • Antonym: partially - to some extent, but not completely

Etymology

From 'thorough' + '-ly', with 'thorough' from Old English 'thurh' (through).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00057795-r
Synset POS: r
