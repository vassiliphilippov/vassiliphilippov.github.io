chaffweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃæfˌwid / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːfˌwiːd / (BrE)
A small weedy plant with short, dry, chaff-like leaves, often found in moist places.
small weedy plant with chaff-like leaves

Examples

The botanist identified the small plant as chaffweed growing along the pond's edge.
In wet meadows, you can often spot patches of chaffweed among the grasses.
She pressed a specimen of chaffweed between the pages of her field journal.

Etymology

From 'chaff' (the husks of grain) + 'weed'.
