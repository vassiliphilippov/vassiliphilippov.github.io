garnet-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɑrnət ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːnɪt ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
Having a deep red color resembling that of garnet.
deep red like garnet

Examples

She wore a striking garnet-colored dress to the gala.
The wine had a rich, garnet-colored hue under the candlelight.
They chose garnet-colored paint to give the dining room a warm atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ruby-red - having a red color like that of a ruby

Etymology

From 'garnet', the gemstone, referencing its typical color + 'colored'.
