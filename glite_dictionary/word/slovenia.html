Slovenia

1. PROPN

/ sloʊˈviːniə / (AmE), / sləʊˈviːniə / (BrE)
a country in central Europe, known for its mountains, lakes, and as a former part of Yugoslavia; became independent in 1991.
country in central Europe

Examples

After gaining independence in 1991, Slovenia quickly became a member of the European Union.
The Julian Alps are a popular tourist destination in Slovenia.
Many people visit Slovenia for its picturesque lakes and medieval towns.

Etymology

From Slovenian 'Slovenija', referring to the Slavic people inhabiting the region.
