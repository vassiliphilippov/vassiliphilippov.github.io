Stenotus

1. NOUN

/ stɛˈnoʊtəs / (AmE), / stɛˈnəʊtəs / (BrE)
A genus of low evergreen shrubs native to western North America, typically growing in dense tufts.
genus of low evergreen shrubs

Examples

The botanist collected several samples of Stenotus during his expedition in the Rocky Mountains.
Stenotus thrives in arid, rocky habitats where few other shrubs can survive.
The dense tufts of Stenotus provided excellent ground cover in the native garden.

Etymology

From Greek 'stenos' (narrow), referring to a characteristic of the plant.
