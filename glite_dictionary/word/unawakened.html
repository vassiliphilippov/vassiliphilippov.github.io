unawakened

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈweɪ.kənd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈweɪ.kənd / (BrE)
Not aroused, activated, or brought into awareness; dormant or inactive.
not aroused or activated; dormant

Examples

Her unawakened interest in art soon blossomed after visiting the museum.
The novel explores the theme of unawakened desires lying deep within the human heart.
Many talents remain unawakened until the right opportunity presents itself.

Synset Examples

unawakened emotions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dormant - temporarily inactive or asleep
  • Antonym: active - engaged in action or functioning

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌnəˈweɪkənd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈweɪkənd / (BrE)
not yet awake; still asleep.
not yet awake

Examples

He lay there, unawakened, while the rest of the house bustled with activity.
The city was unawakened under the soft light of dawn.
Dreams lingered in her unawakened mind as sunlight crept through the window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asleep - in a state of sleep
  • Antonym: awake - no longer asleep; conscious

Etymology

From un- (not) + awakened (past participle of 'awaken', from Old English 'awæcnan').
