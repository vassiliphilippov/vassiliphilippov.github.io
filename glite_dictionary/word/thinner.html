thinner

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈθɪnə / (BrE)
a liquid used to make paint or other substances less thick and easier to apply or clean up
liquid for diluting paint

Examples

Use a small amount of thinner to clean your brushes after painting.
The paint was too thick, so he added some thinner to achieve the right consistency.
Store thinner in a tightly closed container away from heat sources.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solvent - a substance that dissolves another to form a solution

Etymology

From 'thin' + '-er', indicating something that makes another substance thinner.
