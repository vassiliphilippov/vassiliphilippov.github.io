sobersided

1. ADJ

/ ˈsoʊbərˌsaɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˈsəʊbəˌsaɪdɪd / (BrE)
serious and lacking in humor or lightness; solemn or grave in manner or character.
serious; lacking humor or lightness

Examples

Her sobersided attitude made it difficult for her to relax at parties.
The sobersided tone of the documentary suited its serious subject matter.
Although respected for his sobersided demeanor, some colleagues found him unapproachable.

Synset Examples

choreography that was sobersided and sententious
a play with a sobersided social message

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solemn - dignified and serious in manner or character
  • Synonym: grave - serious or solemn in manner
  • Antonym: playful - full of fun and humor; light in manner

Etymology

Formed from 'sober' + 'sided', patterned after similar adjectives like 'two-sided'.
