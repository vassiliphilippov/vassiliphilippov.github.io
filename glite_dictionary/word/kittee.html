kittee

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪti / (AmE), / ˈkɪti / (BrE)
A method of torture, reportedly used by some American Indians, in which sensitive parts of the body were squeezed between two boards until the victim could no longer endure the pain.
historical torture method involving crushing between boards

Examples

Historical records describe the use of the kittee as an extreme form of punishment.
The kittee caused excruciating pain by crushing sensitive areas of the body.
References to the kittee are rare and mostly found in early colonial accounts.
