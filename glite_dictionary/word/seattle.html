Seattle

1. PROPN

/ siˈætəl / (AmE), / siˈætəl / (BrE)
a major city and port in the northwest United States, the largest city in Washington State, known for its technology industry and scenic surroundings.
largest city in Washington State, USA
american

Examples

The tech industry has grown rapidly in Seattle over the past two decades.
She moved to Seattle to attend the University of Washington.
The skyline of Seattle is famous for the iconic Space Needle.

Etymology

Named after Chief Seattle (c. 1786–1866), a local native leader.
