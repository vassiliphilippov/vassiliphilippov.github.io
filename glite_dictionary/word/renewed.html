renewed

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈnud / (AmE), / rɪˈnjuːd / (BrE)
having been restored to a new or original condition or made fresh or strong again.
restored or made new again

Examples

After a short nap, she felt renewed and ready to continue the hike.
The treaty was signed with renewed hope for peace in the region.
A renewed interest in classical music has emerged among young people.

Synset Examples

felt renewed strength

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refreshed - feeling less tired and more energetic
  • Antonym: worn - damaged or impaired from use

Etymology

From the verb 'renew', from Old French 'renouveler', from Latin 'renovare' (to make new again).
