Xishuangbanna Dai

1. NOUN

/ ʃiːˌʃwɑŋbəˈnɑː daɪ / (AmE), / ʃiːˌʃwæŋbəˈnɑː daɪ / (BrE)
a branch of the Tai languages, spoken mainly in Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Prefecture in Southern Yunnan Province, China, and neighboring areas
Tai language spoken in Southern Yunnan, China

Examples

The Xishuangbanna Dai language is widely spoken in Jinghong and surrounding areas of Yunnan Province.
Linguists are documenting Xishuangbanna Dai to preserve its unique dialects and oral traditions.
He is fluent in both Mandarin and Xishuangbanna Dai, allowing him to communicate with local communities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tai language - a group of related languages spoken in Southeast Asia and southern China
