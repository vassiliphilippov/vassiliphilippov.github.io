Arthur Rubinstein

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑrθər ruːˈbiːnstaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɑːθə ruːˈbɪnstaɪn / (BrE)
(1887–1982) Polish-born American pianist acclaimed for his expressive interpretations of Chopin and other composers' works.
Polish-American pianist (1887–1982)

Examples

Arthur Rubinstein was widely regarded as one of the greatest pianists of the twentieth century.
The concert featured rare recordings by Arthur Rubinstein playing Chopin’s nocturnes.
Many critics praised Arthur Rubinstein for his passionate and lyrical performances.
