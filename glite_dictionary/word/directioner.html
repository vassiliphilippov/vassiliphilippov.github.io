directioner

1. NOUN

/ dəˈrɛkʃənər / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛkʃənə / (BrE)
a fan of the British-Irish boy band One Direction
fan of One Direction (band)
informal

Examples

As a proud directioner, I have been to every One Direction concert in my city.
The internet exploded when thousands of directioners tweeted about the band's latest announcement.
She decorated her room with posters because she's a huge directioner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: belieber - fan of pop singer Justin Bieber

Etymology

Formed from the band name One Direction + -er (denoting a person associated with something).
