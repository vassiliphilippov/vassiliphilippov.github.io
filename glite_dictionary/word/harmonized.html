harmonized

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑrməˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈhɑːmənaɪzd / (BrE)
involving or characterized by harmony; arranged or combined in a pleasing or coordinated way
involving harmony or coordination

Examples

The choir sang a harmonized version of the classic hymn at the concert.
The countries adopted a harmonized set of regulations to streamline trade.
She enjoys music that features beautifully harmonized vocals.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: discordant - lacking harmony or agreement
  • Synonym: harmonious - forming a pleasing or consistent whole

Etymology

From the verb 'harmonize', from Greek 'harmonia' (agreement, harmony).
