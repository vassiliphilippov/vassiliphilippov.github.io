neglect

1. VERB

/ nɪˈɡlɛkt / (AmE), / nɪˈɡlɛkt / (BrE)
to fail to give proper care or attention to someone or something
fail to care for properly

Examples

If parents neglect their responsibilities, children can suffer serious consequences.
She often neglected her plants, so most of them wilted and died.
He was accused of neglecting his duties at work.

Synset Examples

he neglects his children

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ignore - to deliberately pay no attention to
  • Antonym: to attend - to pay attention to or care for

Etymology

From Latin 'neglegere' (to disregard).
