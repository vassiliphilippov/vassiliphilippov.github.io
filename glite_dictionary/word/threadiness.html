threadiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈθrɛdi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈθrɛdɪ.nɪs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being like a thread or made up of thin fibers or filaments.
quality of being threadlike or fibrous

Examples

The threadiness of the yarn made it perfect for weaving delicate patterns.
The soup had an unusual threadiness because of the shredded chicken.
Artists often look for paper with the right threadiness to add texture to their work.

Synset Examples

The dropping is going better than I'd anticipated due to the threadiness of the yarn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fibrousness - The quality of being composed of or resembling fibers
  • Synonym: threadlike - Resembling or having the characteristics of a thread

Etymology

From 'thread' (Old English 'thrǣd', a thin strand) + '-iness' (forming nouns denoting a quality or state).
