unlovely

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlʌv.li / (AmE), / ʌnˈlʌv.li / (BrE)
not attractive or lacking in beauty or charm; unpleasant to look at or experience
not attractive; lacking charm or beauty

Examples

The abandoned building looked particularly unlovely in the gray winter light.
Despite its unlovely appearance, the dish tasted surprisingly good.
She feared that her unlovely words had hurt his feelings.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lovely - very attractive or beautiful

Etymology

From un- (not) + lovely, meaning not lovely.
