aircrewman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌkruːmən / (AmE), / ˈeəˌkruːmən / (BrE)
a male member of an aircrew, especially in the military or aviation industry
male member of an aircrew

Examples

The aircrewman checked the cabin equipment before takeoff.
As an experienced aircrewman, he participated in many rescue missions.
The aircrewman wore a special flight suit for the high-altitude operation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aircrew - all personnel operating an aircraft
  • Synonym: airman - a person who serves in an air force or operates an aircraft

Etymology

From 'aircrew' + 'man', modeled after similar occupational compounds.
