tetranychid

1. NOUN

/ tɛˈtrænɪkɪd / (AmE), / tɛˈtrænɪkɪd / (BrE)
a small web-spinning mite of the family Tetranychidae that attacks garden plants and fruit trees
web-spinning plant pest mite

Examples

The leaves were covered in fine silk threads, a sign of a tetranychid infestation.
Orchard owners often have to control tetranychids to protect their fruit trees from damage.
A magnifying glass revealed several red tetranychids moving across the underside of the leaf.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spider mite - a common name for mites of the family Tetranychidae

Etymology

From New Latin Tetranychidae, the family name, based on Greek 'tetra-' (four) and 'onyx, onychos' (claw).
