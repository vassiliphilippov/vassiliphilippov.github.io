Glaux

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlɔks / (AmE), / ˈɡlɔːks / (BrE)
A small genus of coastal flowering plants in the primrose family, commonly known as sea milkwort.
genus of coastal flowering plant

Examples

The botanist studied the unique habitat requirements of Glaux along the salt marshes.
Glaux is often found in coastal areas with saline conditions.
The delicate pink flowers of Glaux bloom during the summer months.

Etymology

From Greek 'glaux' (owl), possibly referring to the shape or color of the flower.
