stoppable

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɑːpəbəl / (AmE), / ˈstɒpəbəl / (BrE)
Able to be stopped or prevented from continuing.
able to be stopped

Examples

The team appeared invincible last season, but this year they seem stoppable.
With the new security measures, the threat is finally stoppable.
Is the flow of misinformation online truly stoppable with current technology?

Synset Examples

if we pick up our pace he may be stoppable

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unstoppable - impossible to stop or prevent

Etymology

From 'stop' + '-able', meaning able to be stopped.
