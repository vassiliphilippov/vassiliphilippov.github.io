Ticino

1. PROPN

/ tɪˈtʃiːnoʊ / (AmE), / tɪˈtʃiːnəʊ / (BrE)
A region in southern Switzerland where Italian is the main language spoken.
Italian-speaking region of Switzerland

Examples

The beautiful landscapes of Ticino attract tourists from all over the world.
She spent a semester studying architecture in Ticino, improving her Italian along the way.
Although Ticino is in Switzerland, its culture is closely linked to northern Italy.

Etymology

From Italian 'Ticino', the name of the region and river.
