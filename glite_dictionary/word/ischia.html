Ischia

1. PROPN

/ ˈɪskiə / (AmE), / ˈɪskiə / (BrE)
a volcanic island in the Tyrrhenian Sea at the northern end of the Bay of Naples, part of Campania, Italy
volcanic island in Campania, Italy

Examples

Tourists from around the world visit Ischia for its natural hot springs and stunning beaches.
The picturesque island of Ischia lies just off the coast of Naples.
We spent a week on Ischia, enjoying local cuisine and exploring its ancient castles.

Etymology

From Italian Ischia, possibly from Latin Aenaria or Ischia.
