self-important

1. ADJ

/ sɛlf ɪmˈpɔɹtənt / (AmE), / sɛlf ɪmˈpɔːtənt / (BrE)
having or showing an exaggerated sense of one's own value or importance
having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance

Examples

The manager's self-important attitude made it difficult for his employees to communicate honestly.
She walked into the room with a self-important air, ignoring everyone around her.
He gave a self-important speech that left the audience unimpressed.

Synset Examples

an arrogant official
arrogant claims
chesty as a peacock

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arrogant - having an exaggerated sense of one's own abilities or importance
  • Antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low estimate of one's importance

Etymology

From 'self' + 'important'; modeled as the state of considering oneself important.
