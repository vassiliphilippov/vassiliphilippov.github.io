nutcase

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌtˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈnʌtkeɪs / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is crazy or behaves in a very strange way, sometimes considered dangerous
informal term for a crazy or strange person
informal slang

Examples

Everyone thinks he's a nutcase after that wild outburst at the meeting.
The police warned us that the escaped nutcase could be dangerous.
You'd have to be a real nutcase to try skydiving without any training.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunatic - a person who is mentally ill; an extremely foolish person
  • Synonym: madman - a man who behaves in a wild or uncontrolled way
  • Antonym: sane - mentally healthy, reasonable

Etymology

From 'nut' (slang for head or brain) + 'case' (person), 20th-century American slang.
