off-color

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔfˈkʌlər / (AmE), / ˌɒfˈkʌlə / (BrE)
slightly indecent or in poor taste, especially in reference to jokes or remarks
slightly indecent; in poor taste

Examples

He was reprimanded at the office for making an off-color joke during the meeting.
Some comedians use off-color humor that pushes the boundaries of what is acceptable.
His remarks at dinner were a bit off-color for such a formal occasion.

Synset Examples

an indelicate remark
an off-color joke

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indelicate - having or showing a lack of sensitive understanding or tact; somewhat improper

2. ADJ

/ ˈɒf ˌkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈɒf ˌkɒlə / (BrE)
(of humor, jokes, or remarks) slightly indecent or risqué, often in a way intended to be funny.
slightly indecent; risqué (humor, jokes)
vulgar

Examples

Some comedians rely heavily on off-color jokes to get laughs from their audience.
The movie contained a few off-color remarks that were not suitable for children.
He apologized after making an off-color comment during the meeting.

Synset Examples

bawdy songs
off-color jokes
ribald language

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bawdy - humorously indecent
  • Synonym: risqué - slightly indecent or liable to shock
  • Antonym: clean - not indecent or improper (of jokes, language)
