autism

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːtɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈɔːtɪzəm / (BrE)
A developmental condition characterized by difficulties with social interaction and communication, and by restricted or repetitive patterns of thought and behavior.
developmental condition marked by social and communication difficulties
medical

Examples

Early diagnosis of autism can make a significant difference in a child's development.
He advocates for greater awareness and understanding of autism in the workplace.
Some children with autism may have exceptional abilities in specific areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: autistic - relating to or affected by autism

Etymology

From Greek autos (self) + -ism.
