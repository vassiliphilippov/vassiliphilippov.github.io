corpse

1. NOUN

/ kɔrps / (AmE), / kɔːps / (BrE)
the dead body of a human being
a dead human body

Examples

The police discovered a corpse hidden in the abandoned warehouse.
Medical students practice on a corpse to learn about human anatomy.
An eerie silence fell over the room as the corpse was carried away.

Etymology

From Latin 'corpus' (body).
