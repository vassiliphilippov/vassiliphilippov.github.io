speediness

1. NOUN

/ ˈspiːdi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈspiːdi.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being fast or quick; rapidity of action or movement
the quality of being rapid or quick

Examples

His speediness on the track surprised everyone and helped him win the race.
The speediness of the response impressed the customers.
We were grateful for the speediness with which the repairs were completed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speed - quickness of movement or action
  • Antonym: slowness - the quality of lacking speed; slow movement

Etymology

Formed from 'speedy' + '-ness', with 'speedy' from Old English 'spēd' (success, prosperity, quickness).
