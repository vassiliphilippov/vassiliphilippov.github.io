Zu

1. PROPN

/ zuː / (AmE), / zuː / (BrE)
a demon or storm god in Mesopotamian mythology, often depicted as a giant black bird
Mesopotamian storm god, black bird

Examples

In ancient myths, Zu is said to have stolen the Tablet of Destinies from the gods.
Scholars have found references to Zu in cuneiform texts from Mesopotamia.
The depiction of Zu as a giant bird symbolizes chaos and rebellion in the myth.

Etymology

From Akkadian 'Anzû', a demon or divine storm-bird in ancient Mesopotamian mythology.
