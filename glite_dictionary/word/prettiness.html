prettiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛt̬i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈprɛt.i.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being visually attractive, especially in a delicate, graceful, or charming way.
delicate or graceful attractiveness

Examples

Her prettiness was enhanced by the delicate floral dress she wore.
He was captivated by the prettiness of the young dancer on stage.
The prettiness of the garden made it a peaceful place to relax.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty - the quality of being physically attractive or pleasing
  • Antonym: ugliness - the quality of being unpleasant to look at

Etymology

From 'pretty' + '-ness', forming a noun indicating the state or quality of being pretty.
