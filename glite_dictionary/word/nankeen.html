nankeen

1. NOUN

/ nænˈkin / (AmE), / nænˈkiːn / (BrE)
A durable, yellowish cotton fabric originally handwoven in China.
yellowish cotton fabric

Examples

He wore trousers made of nankeen during the summer months.
The merchant imported fine nankeen fabrics from China.
Her dress was sewn from pale yellow nankeen cloth.

Etymology

From Nanking (now Nanjing), China, where the fabric was first produced.
