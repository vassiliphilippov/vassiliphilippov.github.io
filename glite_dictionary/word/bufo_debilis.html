Bufo debilis

1. NOUN

/ ˌbjuːfoʊ dɛˈbaɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˌbjuːfəʊ dɛˈbiːlɪs / (BrE)
A small green or yellow-green toad marked with small black bars and stripes, native to parts of North America.
a species of small greenish toad

Examples

The Bufo debilis is commonly found in arid regions of the southwestern United States.
Scientists studied the habitat preferences of Bufo debilis during the rainy season.
Unlike other toads, the Bufo debilis has distinctive black stripes on its back.

Etymology

From Latin: 'Bufo' meaning 'toad' and 'debilis' meaning 'weak' or 'feeble'.
