Dioon

1. NOUN

/ diˈoʊ.ɒn / (AmE), / dɪˈəʊ.ɒn / (BrE)
A genus of arborescent cycads native to Mexico and Central America, sometimes included in the family Cycadaceae.
genus of cycads from Mexico and Central America

Examples

Several species in the Dioon genus are cultivated for ornamental purposes in botanical gardens.
The ancient lineage of Dioon makes it a living fossil among cycads.
Botanists discovered a new Dioon species in the mountains of Mexico.

Etymology

From Greek 'dyo' (two) and 'oon' (egg), referring to the paired seeds.
