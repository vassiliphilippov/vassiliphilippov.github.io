180th

1. ADJ

/ wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈeɪtiəθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈeɪtiəθ / (BrE)
being the ordinal number corresponding to one hundred eighty in a series; one coming after the one hundred seventy-ninth.
ordinal number for one hundred eighty

Examples

The parade began with the 180th regiment marching down the avenue.
She was the 180th person to cross the finish line in the marathon.
He celebrated his 180th day at the company with a small party.
