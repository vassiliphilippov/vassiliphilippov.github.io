idealized

1. ADJ

/ aɪˈdiəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / aɪˈdɪəlaɪzd / (BrE)
represented as being perfect or better than reality, often to an unrealistic degree.
represented as perfect or better than real

Examples

His childhood memories were viewed through an idealized lens, making everything seem perfect.
The film presents an idealized version of family life that rarely exists in reality.
Many historical figures are idealized in stories long after their deaths.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: realistic - representing things as they really are

Etymology

From 'ideal' + '-ize' (to make), with '-ed' forming the past participle.
