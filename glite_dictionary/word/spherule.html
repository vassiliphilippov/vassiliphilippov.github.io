spherule

1. NOUN

/ ˈsfɪr.juːl / (AmE), / ˈsfɪə.rjuːl / (BrE)
a small, roughly spherical object or particle
small spherical object

Examples

The volcanic ash contained many tiny spherules formed by rapid cooling.
Microscopists observed several spherules scattered across the sample.
A single spherule was lodged inside the filter, visible under magnification.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: sphere - a perfectly round three-dimensional object, like a ball

Etymology

From Latin 'sphaerula', diminutive of 'sphaera' (sphere).
