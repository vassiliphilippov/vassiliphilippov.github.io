Ovocon

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊvoʊˌkɑn / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəʊˌkɒn / (BrE)
A trade name for an oral contraceptive pill containing 30 mcg ethinylestradiol and 1.5 mg norethindrone acetate.
brand of oral contraceptive
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Ovocon to help regulate my menstrual cycles.
Some women experience mild side effects when they start taking Ovocon.
It is important to take Ovocon at the same time every day for maximum effectiveness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birth control pill - a pill taken orally to prevent pregnancy
  • Synonym: ethinylestradiol - a synthetic estrogen used in oral contraceptives
  • Synonym: norethindrone acetate - a synthetic progestin used in contraceptives
