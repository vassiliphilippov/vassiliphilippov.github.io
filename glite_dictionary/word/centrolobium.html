Centrolobium

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛn.trəˈloʊ.bi.əm / (AmE), / ˌsɛn.trəˈləʊ.bi.əm / (BrE)
A genus of tropical South American trees in the legume family, known for their hardwood and sometimes used as ornamental plants.
genus of tropical South American trees

Examples

The wood of the Centrolobium tree is prized for its durability and color.
Several species within the Centrolobium genus are cultivated for ornamental purposes.
Botanists discovered a new variety of Centrolobium in the Amazon rainforest.

Etymology

From Greek 'kentron' (spike) + 'lobos' (pod/lobe), referring to aspects of the plant's fruit.
