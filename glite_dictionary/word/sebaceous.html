sebaceous

1. ADJ

/ səˈbeɪʃəs / (AmE), / sɪˈbeɪʃəs / (BrE)
containing, producing, or resembling fat or oil; greasy or oily, especially in reference to glands or substances secreted by them.
oily; relating to oil or grease
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that sebaceous glands produce oil to protect our skin.
A sebaceous cyst can develop when a gland becomes blocked.
The animal's fur appeared sebaceous due to a buildup of natural oils.

Synset Examples

greasy hamburgers
oily fried potatoes
oleaginous seeds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greasy - Covered with or containing grease; oily
  • Synonym: oily - Containing or resembling oil; greasy

Etymology

From Latin 'sebaceus' (of tallow, oily), from 'sebum' (tallow, grease).
