unpopularity

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌnˌpɑːpjuˈlærəti / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌpɒpjʊˈlærəti / (BrE)
The state or quality of not being liked or approved by many people.
state of not being popular

Examples

The president's growing unpopularity led to protests throughout the country.
His unpopularity at work made team projects difficult for everyone involved.
Despite her unpopularity, she persisted in pursuing her goals.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: popularity - state of being liked or admired by many people

Etymology

From un- (not) + popularity, from Latin popularitas (favour of the people).
