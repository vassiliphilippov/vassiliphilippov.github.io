fanlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈfæn.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈfæn.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or shaped like a fan, especially in having a spread or radiating form
resembling or shaped like a fan

Examples

Palm leaves often have a distinctive fanlike appearance.
The peacock strutted proudly, showing off its fanlike train.
