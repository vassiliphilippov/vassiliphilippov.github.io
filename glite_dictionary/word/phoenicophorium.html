Phoenicophorium

1. NOUN

/ foʊˌnɪkəˈfɔːriəm / (AmE), / fəʊˌnɪkəˈfɔːriəm / (BrE)
A genus of palm trees native to the Seychelles, commonly known as the latanier palm.
genus of palms native to Seychelles

Examples

The Phoenicophorium genus is unique to the islands of the Seychelles.
Botanists are working to protect Phoenicophorium from the threat of habitat loss.
The large leaves of Phoenicophorium are used locally for thatching roofs.

Etymology

From Greek 'phoinix' (palm) + 'phorion' (bearing).
