phoenicophorium

1. NOUN

/ fəˌnɪkəˈfɔːriəm / (AmE), / fəˌnɪkəˈfɔːriəm / (BrE)
A genus of palm trees native to the Seychelles, commonly known as the latanier palm.
a genus of Seychelles palm trees

Examples

The phoenicophorium is recognized for its large, fan-shaped leaves.
Conservation efforts are underway to protect the endangered phoenicophorium in the Seychelles.
Botanists studied the growth patterns of phoenicophorium in its native habitat.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin, combining Greek 'phoinix' (palm) and 'phorion' (bearing).
