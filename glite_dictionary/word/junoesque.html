Junoesque

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʒuː.nəˈɛsk / (AmE), / ˌdʒuː.nəʊˈɛsk / (BrE)
(of a woman) tall and stately, often with an imposing and dignified appearance, suggestive of the goddess Juno.
tall, stately (of a woman)

Examples

The actress walked down the red carpet with a Junoesque elegance that turned every head.
Her Junoesque figure and graceful posture made her stand out in the crowd.
He admired her Junoesque beauty, reminiscent of classical sculpture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: statuesque - tall, graceful, and dignified like a statue

Etymology

From Juno, the Roman goddess of marriage, combined with '-esque' meaning 'in the style of'.
