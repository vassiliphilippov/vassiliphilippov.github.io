sauteed

1. ADJ

/ sɔːˈteɪd / (AmE), / sɔːˈteɪd / (BrE)
(of food) fried quickly in a small amount of fat.
fried quickly in a little fat

Examples

The chef served chicken breasts sauteed with garlic and fresh herbs.
I love eating mushrooms sauteed in olive oil for breakfast.
Green beans sauteed with lemon and almonds make a tasty side dish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fried - (of food) cooked in hot fat or oil

Etymology

From French 'sauté,' past participle of 'sauter' (to jump), referring to the tossing motion in cooking.
