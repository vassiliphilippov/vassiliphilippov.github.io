sharpened

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɑr.pənd / (AmE), / ˈʃɑː.pənd / (BrE)
having been made sharp or given a pointed edge or tip
made sharp or pointed

Examples

He reached for a sharpened knife to cut the vegetables.
The sharpened stakes were placed around the camp as a defense.
After using a sharpened pencil, she began sketching her portrait.

Synset Examples

a sharpened pencil

Related Terms

  • Antonym: blunt - not sharp; having a rounded or worn edge or point
