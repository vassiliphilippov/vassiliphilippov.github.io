Hector Berlioz

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɛktər ˈbɛrliˌoʊz / (AmE), / ˈhɛktə ˈbɛəliˌəʊz / (BrE)
(1803–1869) a French composer known for his influential Romantic music, including 'Symphonie fantastique.'
French Romantic composer (1803–1869)

Examples

The music of Hector Berlioz inspired many later composers in the Romantic era.
We studied Hector Berlioz and his innovative orchestration in our music history class.
A concert featuring works by Hector Berlioz drew a large audience at the symphony hall.
