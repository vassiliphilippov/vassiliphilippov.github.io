dishwater

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪʃˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈdɪʃˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
water that has been used for washing dishes and cooking utensils
water used for washing dishes

Examples

After dinner, she poured the dishwater down the sink.
The dishwater was cloudy from all the soap and food scraps.
He used old dishwater to water the plants in the garden.
