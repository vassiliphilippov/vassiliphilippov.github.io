flightiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaɪ.ti.nəs / (AmE), / ˈflaɪ.ti.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being fickle, unpredictable, or prone to acting on sudden impulses or whims rather than reason or judgment
tendency to be unpredictable or impulsive

Examples

Her flightiness made it hard for her friends to rely on her plans.
The manager worried that flightiness was affecting the team's productivity.
Despite her flightiness, she was admired for her creativity and enthusiasm.

Synset Examples

I despair at the flightiness and whimsicality of my memory

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whimsicality - the quality of being whimsical or capricious
  • Antonym: steadiness - the quality of being steady and reliable

Etymology

From 'flighty' (whimsical, capricious), from 'flight' (sense of fleeting movement) + -y + -ness.
