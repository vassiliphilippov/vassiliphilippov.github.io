Wallis Simpson

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɔlɪs ˈsɪmp.sən / (AmE), / ˈwɒlɪs ˈsɪmp.sən / (BrE)
(1896–1986) An American socialite whose marriage to Edward VIII led to his abdication as King of the United Kingdom in 1936.
American socialite; wife of Edward VIII

Examples

The romance between Edward VIII and Wallis Simpson was a major scandal in 1930s Britain.
Wallis Simpson was the first woman to become Duchess of Windsor.
Many biographies have been written about the life of Wallis Simpson.
