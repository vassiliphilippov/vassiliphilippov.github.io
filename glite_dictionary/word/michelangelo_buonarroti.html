Michelangelo Buonarroti

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɪkəˈlændʒəloʊ ˌbwɑːnəˈrɔːti / (AmE), / ˌmɪkəlˈændʒələʊ ˌbwɒnəˈrɒti / (BrE)
(1475–1564) An Italian sculptor, painter, and architect, renowned as one of the greatest figures of the Renaissance for works such as the Sistine Chapel ceiling and the statue of David.
Italian Renaissance artist and sculptor

Examples

The ceiling of the Sistine Chapel was painted by Michelangelo Buonarroti.
Many consider Michelangelo Buonarroti to be the greatest artist of the Italian Renaissance.
The statue of David is a masterpiece created by Michelangelo Buonarroti.
