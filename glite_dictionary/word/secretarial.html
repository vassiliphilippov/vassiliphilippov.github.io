secretarial

1. ADJ

/ ˌsikrɪˈtɛriəl / (AmE), / ˌsiːkrəˈteəriəl / (BrE)
relating to the duties or work of a secretary
relating to secretaries or their work

Examples

She completed a secretarial course before starting her job at the law firm.
His secretarial duties include organizing files and scheduling appointments.
The company is seeking someone with strong secretarial skills for the position.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secretary - a person employed to assist with correspondence and administrative tasks

Etymology

From secretary + -al, denoting relating to.
