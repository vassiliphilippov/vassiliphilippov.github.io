adaptability

1. NOUN

/ əˌdæptəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / əˌdaptəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being able to adjust to new conditions or changes
ability to adjust to change

Examples

Her adaptability helped her succeed in the fast-changing technology sector.
The adaptability of wild animals enables them to survive in difficult environments.
Employers often look for candidates with strong adaptability and problem-solving skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flexibility - willingness or ability to change easily
  • Antonym: rigidity - inability to adapt or change; stiffness

Etymology

From 'adapt' (to adjust) + '-ability' (capacity, fitness).
