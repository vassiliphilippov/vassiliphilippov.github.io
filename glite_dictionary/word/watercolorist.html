watercolorist

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚˌkʌlɚɪst / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌkʌlərɪst / (BrE)
an artist who paints using watercolors
artist who uses watercolors

Examples

The renowned watercolorist exhibited her latest landscapes at the gallery.
He began his career as a watercolorist before experimenting with oils.
Many watercolorists prefer to paint outdoors to capture natural light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: painter - an artist who paints pictures

Etymology

Formed from 'watercolor' + '-ist' (person who practices or is concerned with something).
