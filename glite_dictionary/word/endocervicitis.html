endocervicitis

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛndoʊsɜːrˌvɪˈsaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛndəʊsɜːvɪˈsaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the mucous lining of the uterine cervix.
inflammation of cervical mucous membrane
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with endocervicitis after observing inflammation during the examination.
Untreated endocervicitis can sometimes lead to further reproductive health problems.
Symptoms of endocervicitis may include abnormal vaginal discharge and discomfort.

Etymology

From endo- (inside) + cervix (neck, especially of the uterus) + -itis (inflammation).
