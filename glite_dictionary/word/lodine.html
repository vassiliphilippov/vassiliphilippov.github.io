Lodine

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊˌdaɪn / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌdiːn / (BrE)
A trademarked name for etodolac, a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used to relieve pain and inflammation.
brand of anti-inflammatory drug (etodolac)
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Lodine to manage my arthritis pain.
After switching to Lodine, she noticed a reduction in joint inflammation.
You should not take Lodine on an empty stomach to avoid gastrointestinal issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: etodolac - the generic name for the same drug as Lodine
