statehouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪtˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A government building where a state legislature meets.
government building for state legislature

Examples

The governor delivered her annual address at the statehouse on Monday.
Protesters gathered outside the statehouse to demand legislative action.
Each state's statehouse reflects unique architectural traditions and regional history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: capitol - a building where a legislative body meets, especially in the US
