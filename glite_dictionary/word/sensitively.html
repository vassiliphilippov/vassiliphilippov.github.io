sensitively

1. ADV

/ ˈsɛnsɪtɪvli / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsɪtɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that shows awareness of and consideration for the feelings and needs of others.
in a sensitive manner

Examples

The therapist spoke sensitively about the child's experiences.
She handled the delicate negotiations sensitively and respectfully.
The documentary explores complex issues sensitively, avoiding sensationalism.

Synset Examples

she questioned the rape victim very sensitively about the attack

Related Terms

  • Antonym: insensitively - in a way lacking sensitivity or consideration

Etymology

From 'sensitive' + '-ly'.
