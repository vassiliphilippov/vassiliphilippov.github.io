bellwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛlˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈbɛlˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman employed to carry luggage and run errands for guests at a hotel
female hotel luggage carrier or errand runner

Examples

The hotel recently hired a bellwoman to assist guests with their luggage.
As the only bellwoman on staff, she took pride in her work and greeted every guest with a smile.
Guests praised the bellwoman for her prompt and friendly service during their stay.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bellboy - a male hotel employee who carries luggage and does errands
  • Synonym: bellman - a male hotel porter or attendant

Etymology

Patterned after 'bellboy' or 'bellman', from 'bell' (as in service bell) + 'woman'.
