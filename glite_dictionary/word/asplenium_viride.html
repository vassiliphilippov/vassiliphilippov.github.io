Asplenium viride

1. NOUN

/ æˈspliːniəm vəˈraɪdiː / (AmE), / æsˈpliːniəm vɪˈriːdiː / (BrE)
A small fern with slender green fronds, commonly found in cool regions of the Northern Hemisphere.
a small green-leaved fern species

Examples

We spotted an Asplenium viride growing between the rocks during our hike.
The delicate fronds of Asplenium viride add a vibrant touch to shaded gardens.
Botanists often study Asplenium viride because of its wide distribution across the Northern Hemisphere.

Etymology

From Latin: genus 'Asplenium' (a group of ferns) and 'viride' (green).
