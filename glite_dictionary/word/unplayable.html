unplayable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpleɪ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈpleɪ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be played or not suitable for being played, either because of difficulty or poor conditions
not able to be played

Examples

After the storm, the football pitch became unplayable due to flooding.
Some pieces by this composer are so complex they're almost unplayable.
The software bug made the video game completely unplayable.

Synset Examples

the golf ball was in an unplayable lie
the field was unplayable
some music seems almost unplayable

Related Terms

  • Antonym: playable - able to be played or suitable for playing
