reaching

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈriːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of achieving or attaining a particular goal or objective
achievement of a goal or objective

Examples

The reaching of a consensus was praised by all parties involved.
Her reaching of the summit marked a milestone in her climbing career.
Persistent work is necessary for the reaching of your professional aspirations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: achievement - something accomplished successfully, especially by effort, courage, or skill
  • Antonym: failure - lack of success in achieving a goal

2. NOUN

/ ˈriːtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈriːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of stretching out an arm or hand toward something
the act of stretching out to reach

Examples

The reaching of her hand toward the shelf was cautious and deliberate.
His sudden reaching made the cat jump away from the table.
They applauded the child's persistent reaching for the cookies on the counter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reach - to stretch out an arm in order to touch or grasp something

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'reach' + '-ing', used as a noun.
