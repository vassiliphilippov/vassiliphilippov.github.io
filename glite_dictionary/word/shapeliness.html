shapeliness

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃeɪpli.nəs / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪp.lɪ.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of having an attractive, well-proportioned, and pleasing shape, especially of the body
quality of being attractively shaped

Examples

The shapeliness of the sculpture made it stand out among the other works.
He admired the shapeliness of her figure without being disrespectful.
Regular exercise contributed to the shapeliness of her body.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty - the quality of being pleasing to the senses or mind
  • Antonym: ugliness - the quality of being unpleasant to look at

Etymology

From 'shapely' (well-formed) + '-ness' (forming nouns denoting a state or quality).
