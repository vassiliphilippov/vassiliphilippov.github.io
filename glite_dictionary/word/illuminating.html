illuminating

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈluːməˌneɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈluːmɪneɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
helping to increase understanding or knowledge; making something clearer or easier to understand
making something clearer or more understandable

Examples

The professor's illuminating explanation helped everyone grasp the complex concept.
Her biography provides an illuminating account of an often misunderstood era.
Our discussion was truly illuminating and changed the way I think about the topic.

Synset Examples

an enlightening glimpse of government in action
an illuminating lecture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enlightening - providing new insight or understanding

Etymology

From Latin 'illuminare' (to light up, enlighten) via Middle English.
