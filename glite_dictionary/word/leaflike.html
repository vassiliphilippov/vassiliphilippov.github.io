leaflike

1. ADJ

/ ˈliːfˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈliːfˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having characteristics of a leaf, especially in shape or appearance
resembling a leaf

Examples

Some insects have leaflike wings that help them blend into their environment.
The leaflike patterns on the rock resembled ancient fossils.
Her hat was decorated with a delicate, leaflike ornament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foliaceous - having the form or nature of a leaf
