Nessie

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛsi / (AmE), / ˈnɛsi / (BrE)
a large mythical aquatic creature said to inhabit Loch Ness in Scotland, often described as resembling a serpent or plesiosaur
legendary monster of Loch Ness

Examples

Many tourists visit Scotland hoping to catch a glimpse of Nessie in Loch Ness.
Some believe that Nessie is just a myth, while others claim to have seen her.
Photographs claiming to show Nessie often spark debates about their authenticity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Loch Ness Monster - another name for Nessie, a legendary creature reported in Loch Ness

Etymology

Diminutive of 'Loch Ness', the Scottish lake associated with the legend.
