Pyrola minor

1. NOUN

/ paɪˈroʊlə ˈmaɪnɚ / (AmE), / paɪˈrəʊlə ˈmɪnə / (BrE)
A species of small evergreen herb with clusters of pink-tinged white flowers, native to Europe and North America, commonly known as lesser wintergreen.
a small evergreen plant; lesser wintergreen

Examples

Pyrola minor is often found growing in moist woodlands across northern Europe and North America.
The pink-tinged blooms of Pyrola minor appear in clusters during early summer.
Botanists documented Pyrola minor alongside other wintergreen species in the reserve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wintergreen - common name for plants of the genus Pyrola

Etymology

From New Latin, combining the genus name Pyrola and the Latin minor (smaller or lesser).
