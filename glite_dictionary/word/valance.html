valance

1. NOUN

/ ˈvæl.əns / (AmE), / ˈvæl.əns / (BrE)
a short piece of decorative fabric or a framework fixed across the top of a window to conceal the curtain fittings and give a finished look
decorative cover for curtain fixtures

Examples

The living room windows were finished with a beautiful floral valance to match the drapes.
She chose a lace valance to add a touch of elegance above the window.
A wooden valance was custom-built to conceal the curtain rods in the master bedroom.

Etymology

From Old French 'valence', from Old Provençal 'valença' (hanging, covering).
