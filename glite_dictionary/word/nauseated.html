nauseated

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɔziˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈnɔːsiˌeɪtɪd / (BrE)
feeling as if you are about to vomit; affected with nausea.
feeling sick or about to vomit

Examples

She felt nauseated after riding the roller coaster several times in a row.
The smell of rotten eggs made him instantly nauseated.
Many people become nauseated during long car journeys if they read while traveling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: queasy - feeling sick or uneasy in the stomach
  • Synonym: nauseous - causing or feeling nausea

Etymology

From Latin 'nauseare' (to feel seasick), from Greek 'nausia' (ship sickness).
