aoudad

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊˌdæd / (AmE), / ˈaʊˌdæd / (BrE)
a wild sheep of northern Africa, noted for its curved horns and shaggy coat, also called Barbary sheep
wild sheep of northern Africa

Examples

The aoudad is well adapted to the rocky mountains of the Sahara Desert.
Zookeepers were excited to welcome a new baby aoudad to their herd.
Hunters sometimes travel to Africa in hopes of spotting a wild aoudad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Barbary sheep - another name for aoudad, a wild sheep of North Africa

Etymology

From French 'aoudad', from Berber 'awdad'.
