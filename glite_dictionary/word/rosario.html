Rosario

1. PROPN

/ roʊˈsɑːrioʊ / (AmE), / rəʊˈsɑːriəʊ / (BrE)
a port city on the Paraná River in eastern central Argentina, known for its industrial and economic importance
port city in eastern central Argentina

Examples

The city of Rosario is a major industrial center in Argentina.
Many tourists visit Rosario to see its beautiful riverfront parks.
After moving to Rosario, she quickly adapted to life in the bustling port city.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Rosario', meaning 'rosary', named after the city's original chapel dedicated to Our Lady of the Rosary.
