bewildered

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈwɪldərd / (AmE), / bɪˈwɪldəd / (BrE)
confused and unable to understand what is happening or what to do
confused; unable to understand

Examples

She looked bewildered as she tried to follow the complicated instructions.
The sudden changes in policy left many employees feeling bewildered and anxious.
After hearing the unexpected news, he stood bewildered in the hallway, unsure of what to do next.

Synset Examples

obviously bemused by his questions
bewildered and confused
a cloudy and confounded philosopher
just a mixed-up kid
she felt lost on the first day of school

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confused - unable to think clearly or to understand something
  • Synonym: perplexed - completely baffled; very puzzled
  • Antonym: certain - completely sure and confident; having no doubt

Etymology

From Middle English 'bewilder', from be- (intensive prefix) + 'wilder' (to lead astray).
