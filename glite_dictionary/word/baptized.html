baptized

1. ADJ

/ bæpˈtaɪzd / (AmE), / bæpˈtaɪzd / (BrE)
Having undergone the Christian ritual of baptism.
having received baptism

Examples

She was baptized as an infant in the local church.
Only baptized members are eligible to participate in the ceremony.
The priest asked whether the child was baptized before proceeding with the service.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unbaptized - not having undergone baptism

Etymology

Past participle of 'baptize', from Greek 'baptizein' (to immerse, to dip).
