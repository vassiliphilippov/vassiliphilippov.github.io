lop-eared

1. ADJ

/ lɑp ɪrd / (AmE), / lɒp ɪəd / (BrE)
(of an animal) having ears that hang down or droop.
having drooping ears (of animals)

Examples

The farmer kept a lop-eared rabbit as a pet in his garden.
That lop-eared puppy always looked a bit sad but was full of energy.
A distinctive feature of the breed is its lop-eared appearance.

Synset Examples

a lop-eared hound

Etymology

From 'lop' (to hang loosely or droop) + 'eared' (having ears).
