turnspit

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɜrnˌspɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnˌspɪt / (BrE)
A mechanical device or apparatus for turning a roasting spit.
device for turning a roasting spit
archaic

Examples

The old kitchen still contains a turnspit next to the fireplace.
In medieval times, a turnspit was used to rotate the meat evenly over the fire.
They displayed a restored turnspit at the museum to demonstrate historical cooking methods.

Etymology

From 'turn' + 'spit', named for its function of turning a roasting spit.
