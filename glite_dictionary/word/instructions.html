instructions

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈstrʌkʃənz / (AmE), / ɪnˈstrʌkʃənz / (BrE)
a booklet or document that provides guidance on how to install or operate a device or system
manual explaining how to use something

Examples

Before assembling the furniture, make sure to read the instructions carefully.
The washing machine came with detailed instructions to guide new users.
If you follow the instructions step by step, you won't have any trouble installing the software.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manual - a book giving practical instructions or information

Etymology

From Latin 'instructio' (arrangement, teaching), from 'instruere' (to build, teach).
