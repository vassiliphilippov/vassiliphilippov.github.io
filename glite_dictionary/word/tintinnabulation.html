tintinnabulation

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɪntɪˌnæbjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtɪntɪˌnæbjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a ringing or tinkling sound, especially that made by bells
ringing sound of bells

Examples

The tintinnabulation of the cathedral bells echoed across the quiet town square.
She woke each morning to the gentle tintinnabulation of wind chimes outside her window.
Poets have long been inspired by the tintinnabulation that fills the air during festive celebrations.

Synset Examples

the distinctive ring of the church bell
the ringing of the telephone
the tintinnabulation that so voluminously swells from the ringing and the dinging of the bells

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ringing - the sound produced by a bell or similar object

Etymology

From Latin tintinnabulum (bell), from tintinnare (to ring).
