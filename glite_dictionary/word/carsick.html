carsick

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑrˌsɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɑːˌsɪk / (BrE)
feeling sick or nauseous because of motion while traveling in a car
feeling nauseous while riding in a car

Examples

After an hour of winding roads, Kelly started to feel carsick and asked the driver to stop.
Children are more likely to become carsick during long journeys.
If you know you get carsick, it's best to sit near the front and look out the window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seasick - feeling nauseous due to motion while traveling by boat
  • Synonym: airsick - feeling nauseous due to motion while traveling by aircraft

Etymology

Formed from 'car' and 'sick', modeled on 'seasick'.
