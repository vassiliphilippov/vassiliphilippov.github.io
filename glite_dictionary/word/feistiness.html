feistiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪstinəs / (AmE), / ˈfaɪstinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being lively, energetic, and determined, especially in a spirited or combative way
lively and determined spirit

Examples

Her feistiness impressed the interviewers and set her apart from the other candidates.
Despite his small stature, his feistiness made him a formidable opponent on the soccer field.
The author's feistiness shone through in her witty responses during the debate.

Synset Examples

Sarabi was the smallest of the litter but made up for her lack of size by her feistiness!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spirit - courage or liveliness of temperament
  • Antonym: timidity - lack of courage or confidence

Etymology

From 'feisty' (spirited), itself from English dialect 'feist' (small dog).
