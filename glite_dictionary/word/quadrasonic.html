quadrasonic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkwɑː.drəˈsɑː.nɪk / (AmE), / ˌkwɒ.drəˈsɒ.nɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving quadraphony, a system of sound reproduction using four channels.
relating to quadraphonic sound

Examples

The band recorded their latest album using a quadrasonic system for a richer sound experience.
He invested in a new quadrasonic speaker setup to enhance concerts in his studio.
Some vintage records were originally mixed in quadrasonic formats, requiring special playback equipment.

Synset Examples

his quadraphonic sound system

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quadraphonic - relating to sound reproduction using four channels

Etymology

Formed from Latin 'quadri-' (four) and 'sonic' (relating to sound).
