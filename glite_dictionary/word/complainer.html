complainer

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpleɪnɚ / (AmE), / kəmˈpleɪnə / (BrE)
a person who often complains, especially in a persistent or excessive way
person who complains a lot

Examples

Maggie was known as the office complainer, always finding fault with every new policy.
Even after everything improved, he continued to be a complainer about the smallest inconveniences.
Nobody likes a constant complainer at parties; it can spoil the mood for everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whiner - a person who complains in an annoying, high-pitched way
  • Synonym: grumbler - a person who complains with muttering or grumbling
  • Antonym: optimist - a person who tends to be hopeful and confident; not a complainer

Etymology

From the verb 'complain' + agent suffix '-er'.
