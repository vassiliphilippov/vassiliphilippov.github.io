stomper

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑːmpər / (AmE), / ˈstɒmpə / (BrE)
a person who walks with heavy, noisy steps or stamps on the ground
person who walks or stamps noisily

Examples

You could hear the stomper coming down the hallway from a mile away.
The upstairs neighbor is a notorious stomper, especially late at night.
At concerts, some fans are real stompers, dancing and thudding their feet to the beat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stomp - to tread or walk heavily and noisily
