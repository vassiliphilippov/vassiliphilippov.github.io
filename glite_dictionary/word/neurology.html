neurology

1. NOUN

/ nuˈrɑlədʒi / (AmE), / njʊəˈrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The branch of medicine that deals with the anatomy, functions, and disorders of the nervous system.
medical study of the nervous system
medical

Examples

She decided to specialize in neurology after discovering her interest in brain disorders.
The hospital's neurology department treats patients with conditions affecting the brain and nerves.
Recent advances in neurology have improved the diagnosis of neurological diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neuroscience - the scientific study of the nervous system

Etymology

From Greek neuron (nerve) + -logia (study).

2. NOUN

the branch of medicine that deals with the nervous system and its disorders
medical study of the nervous system
medical

Examples

She decided to specialize in neurology after completing her medical degree.
Advances in neurology have improved our understanding of diseases like Parkinson's and Alzheimer's.
The hospital's neurology department is famous for its research into the brain.

Related Terms
