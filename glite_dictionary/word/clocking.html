clocking

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɒkɪŋ / (BrE)
the time taken to traverse a measured course, especially as recorded in a race or timed event
recorded time for a race or event

Examples

Her clocking in the marathon was just under three hours.
He broke the school record with a new clocking of 10.2 seconds in the 100 meters.
The sprinter was disappointed with his clocking, missing the qualifying time by a fraction of a second.

Synset Examples

it was a world record clocking

Related Terms

  • Synonym: time - the period measured by a clock in a race or event
