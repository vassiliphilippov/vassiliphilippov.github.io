pipefitting

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪpˌfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪpˌfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a component, usually a threaded piece, used to join sections of pipe together
component for joining pipes

Examples

The plumber replaced the old pipefitting to stop the leak.
Stainless steel pipefittings are often used in food processing plants for durability.
You need the correct size of pipefitting to ensure a tight seal between the pipes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coupling - a device used to connect two pipes together

Etymology

From 'pipe' + 'fitting', indicating a part used to connect pipes.
