killable

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɪləbəl / (AmE), / ˈkɪləbəl / (BrE)
able to be killed; fit or suitable for killing, especially for food
able to be killed, especially for food

Examples

Only mature deer are considered killable during hunting season.
In some fisheries, only killable fish above a certain size may be kept.
The farmer identified which animals were killable for market this year.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unkillable - unable to be killed

Etymology

Formed from 'kill' + '-able', following a common English pattern.
