tummy

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌmi / (AmE), / ˈtʌmi / (BrE)
an informal word for the stomach, the main organ of digestion, or the front part of the lower trunk
informal word for stomach or belly
informal

Examples

Her tummy started to ache after she ate too much candy.
The child pointed to his tummy when the doctor asked where it hurt.
She laid on her back and watched the clouds, her hands resting on her tummy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stomach - the principal organ of digestion in the body
  • Synonym: belly - the front part of the human trunk below the ribs

Etymology

Childish alteration of 'stomach'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈtʌmi / (AmE), / ˈtʌmi / (BrE)
(slang) A rounded or protruding belly; a paunch.
rounded or protruding belly (slang)
slang

Examples

Ever since he stopped exercising, he's developed quite a tummy.
She patted her tummy after finishing the big meal, laughing at how full she felt.
His shirt was a bit tight around his tummy these days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paunch - a large or protruding belly
  • Synonym: stomach - abdomen; belly area

Etymology

Diminutive form of 'stomach', influenced by baby talk.
