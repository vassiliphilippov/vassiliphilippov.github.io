Lepechinia

1. NOUN

/ lɛpəˈkɪniə / (AmE), / lɛpɛˈkɪnɪə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the mint family (Lamiaceae), native to the Americas and characterized by aromatic leaves and tubular flowers.
a genus of mint-family flowering plants

Examples

Several species of Lepechinia are valued for their aromatic leaves.
The botanic garden features a rare Lepechinia shrub in its native plant section.
Researchers studied the medicinal properties of Lepechinia extracts in traditional remedies.

Etymology

Named after Ivan Ivanovich Lepechin, Russian naturalist (1740–1802).
