ellipsoidal

1. ADJ

/ ɪˌlɪpˈsɔɪ.dl̩ / (AmE), / ɪˌlɪpˈsɔɪ.dl̩ / (BrE)
Having the shape or characteristics of an ellipsoid.
having the shape of an ellipsoid

Examples

The planet has an ellipsoidal shape due to its rapid rotation.
The crystal's ellipsoidal form makes it unique among minerals.
Medical images sometimes reveal ellipsoidal organs within the body.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elliptical - having the shape of an ellipse

Etymology

From ellipsoid + -al, indicating form or relation.
