vending

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or business of selling goods, especially in a public place or through machines
selling goods as a business

Examples

She makes a living through vending handmade jewelry at local markets.
Street vending is an important source of income in many cities around the world.
The park recently banned vending to maintain cleanliness and order.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retailing - the activity of selling goods to consumers
  • Antonym: buying - the act of purchasing goods

Etymology

From the verb 'vend', from Latin 'vendere' (to sell).
