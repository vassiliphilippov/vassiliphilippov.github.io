bling bling

1. NOUN

/ blɪŋ blɪŋ / (AmE), / blɪŋ blɪŋ / (BrE)
expensive, flashy, and ostentatious jewelry or accessories, especially as worn to attract attention
flashy, ostentatious jewelry
slang

Examples

His gold chain and diamond watch were pure bling bling.
Many celebrities are famous for showing off their bling bling on the red carpet.
The shop specializes in custom bling bling for musicians and athletes.

Related Terms

Popularized in the 1990s in hip-hop culture, imitating the sound of light reflecting off jewelry.
