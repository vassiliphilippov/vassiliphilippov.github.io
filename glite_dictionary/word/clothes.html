clothes

1. NOUN

/ kloʊðz / (AmE), / kləʊðz / (BrE)
items worn to cover the body; garments or dress in general.
garments; items worn on the body

Examples

She packed all her clothes into a suitcase before leaving for college.
You should wear warm clothes if you're going outside in winter.
His clothes were soaked after walking through the rain.

Synset Examples

she was refined in her choice of apparel
he always bought his clothes at the same store
fastidious about his dress

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clothing - garments collectively; clothes
  • Synonym: attire - clothes, especially fine or formal ones
  • Antonym: naked - without clothes; bare

Etymology

From Old English 'clāþas', plural of 'clāþ' (cloth).
