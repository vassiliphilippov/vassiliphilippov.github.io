staggering

1. ADJ

/ ˈstæɡərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstæɡərɪŋ / (BrE)
So great, impressive, or surprising as to shock or overwhelm.
extremely impressive or overwhelming

Examples

The final cost of the new stadium was staggering and far exceeded initial estimates.
She received a staggering amount of support from her friends and family.
The statistics about global poverty rates are simply staggering.

Synset Examples

such an enormous response was astonishing
an astounding achievement
the amount of money required was staggering
suffered a staggering defeat
the figure inside the boucle dress was stupefying

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astonishing - Extremely surprising or impressive; amazing
  • Synonym: astounding - Very surprising; difficult to believe
  • Antonym: underwhelming - Failing to impress or cause excitement

Etymology

From the verb 'stagger' (to reel or sway), likely by extension to something figuratively causing one to reel from surprise.
