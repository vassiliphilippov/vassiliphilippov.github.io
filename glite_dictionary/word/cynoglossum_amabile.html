Cynoglossum amabile

1. NOUN

/ saɪnəˌɡlɒsəm əˈmæbəl / (AmE), / sɪnəˌɡlɒsəm əˈmɑːbɪli / (BrE)
A biennial plant native to East Asia, grown for its striking bright blue flowers; also known as Chinese forget-me-not.
East Asian plant with blue flowers

Examples

The garden was full of Cynoglossum amabile in full bloom, their blue petals bright under the sun.
She planted Cynoglossum amabile along the fence to add color to the spring landscape.
Botanists often recommend Cynoglossum amabile as a hardy and attractive addition to flower beds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chinese forget-me-not - Common name for Cynoglossum amabile

Etymology

From Latin 'cynoglossum' (hound's tongue) and 'amabile' (lovely, pleasing).
