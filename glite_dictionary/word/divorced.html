divorced

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈvɔrst / (AmE), / dɪˈvɔːst / (BrE)
Having had one's marriage legally dissolved; no longer married to one's former spouse.
no longer married after legal dissolution

Examples

After ten years together, they are now divorced and live in separate cities.
She felt uncertain about dating again after becoming divorced last year.
He is divorced with two young children from his previous marriage.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: married - having a husband or wife
  • Antonym: widowed - having lost one's spouse by death
  • Antonym: single - not married or in a romantic relationship
