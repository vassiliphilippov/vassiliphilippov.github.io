Tenormin

1. NOUN

/ təˈnɔːrmɪn / (AmE), / tɪˈnɔːmɪn / (BrE)
a trademarked brand of atenolol, an oral beta blocker used to treat high blood pressure and angina
beta blocker drug (atenolol) brand

Examples

The doctor prescribed Tenormin to help control her blood pressure.
Some patients taking Tenormin report experiencing fatigue or dizziness.
Before switching to a generic, he had been using Tenormin for several years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atenolol - the generic name of the medication sold as Tenormin
