angledozer

1. NOUN

/ ˈæŋ.ɡəlˌdoʊ.zər / (AmE), / ˈæŋ.ɡəlˌdəʊ.zə / (BrE)
A bulldozer equipped with a moldboard angled to one side so as to push earth to one side rather than directly ahead.
bulldozer with angled blade

Examples

The construction crew used an angledozer to clear debris from the roadside.
With its angled blade, the angledozer efficiently pushed the soil to one side of the trench.
An angledozer is ideal for creating windrows in earthmoving projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulldozer - a powerful tractor with a large blade in front for moving earth

Etymology

From 'angle' and 'dozer' (short for bulldozer), referring to the angled blade.
