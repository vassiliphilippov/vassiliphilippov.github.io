broadwise

1. ADV

/ ˈbrɔdˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdˌwaɪz / (BrE)
in the direction across the breadth of something; breadthwise.
across the breadth

Examples

For this project, fold the fabric broadwise to create a wider panel.
Please arrange the planks broadwise for better support across the frame.
He decided to cut the tape broadwise rather than lengthwise.

Synset Examples

cut the cloth breadthwise

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breadthwise - in the direction of the breadth
  • Antonym: lengthwise - in the direction of the length
