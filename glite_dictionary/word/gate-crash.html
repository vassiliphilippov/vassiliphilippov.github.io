to gate-crash

1. VERB

/ ˈɡeɪtˌkræʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪtˌkræʃ / (BrE)
to enter or attend a social event without an invitation
attend without invitation
informal

Examples

They tried to gate-crash the exclusive gala, but security caught them.
It's risky to gate-crash a wedding, but some people find it exciting.
We decided to gate-crash the neighbors' barbecue when we smelled the food.

Synset Examples

let's crash the party!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crash - to attend a party or event without an invitation

Etymology

Formed from 'gate' (entry) + 'crash' (to enter forcefully); first recorded in the early 20th century.
