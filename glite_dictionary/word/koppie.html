koppie

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːpi / (AmE), / ˈkɒpi / (BrE)
A small, rounded hill rising abruptly from the surrounding plain, especially in southern Africa.
small hill on the African plain

Examples

A lone koppie stood out against the flat expanse of the veld.
We climbed to the top of the koppie to watch the sunset over the savannah.
The expedition camped near a koppie for shelter from the wind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hill - a naturally raised area of land, not as high or craggy as a mountain

Etymology

From Afrikaans 'koppie', diminutive of 'kop' (head, hill), from Dutch.
