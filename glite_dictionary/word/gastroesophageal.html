gastroesophageal

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡæstroʊɪˌsɑfəˈdʒiəl / (AmE), / ˌɡæstrəʊɪˌsɒfəˈdʒiːəl / (BrE)
Relating to or involving the stomach and the esophagus.
relating to stomach and esophagus
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed him with gastroesophageal reflux disease after noting his symptoms.
Chronic heartburn is often a sign of a gastroesophageal problem.
She had surgery to correct a gastroesophageal abnormality.

Etymology

From Greek 'gaster' (stomach) and 'esophagus' (gullet).
