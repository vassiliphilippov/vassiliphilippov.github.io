all-round

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔlˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ˌɔːlˈraʊnd / (BrE)
Having many skills or qualities; able to perform well in a variety of tasks or activities.
having many skills or qualities

Examples

She is an all-round musician, comfortable playing several instruments and composing her own songs.
The company is looking for an all-round employee who can handle both administrative and technical tasks.
His all-round knowledge made him an invaluable member of the quiz team.

Synset Examples

an all-around athlete
a well-rounded curriculum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: versatile - able to adapt or be adapted to many different functions or activities
  • Antonym: specialized - having or involving detailed knowledge or training in a particular subject
