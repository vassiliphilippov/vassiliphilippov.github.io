supplies

1. NOUN

/ səˈplaɪz / (AmE), / səˈplaɪz / (BrE)
the items or materials needed to carry out a particular activity or task
materials or items needed for a task

Examples

We packed all the supplies we would need for the camping trip.
The teacher asked students to bring their own supplies for the art class.
After the storm, volunteers distributed food and other essential supplies to affected families.

Synset Examples

I've got all the supplies we need for the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: provisions - supplies of food, drink, or equipment, especially for a journey
  • Synonym: equipment - the necessary items for a particular purpose

Etymology

Plural of 'supply', from Old French 'soupleer', from Latin 'supplementum' (something that fills up or completes).
