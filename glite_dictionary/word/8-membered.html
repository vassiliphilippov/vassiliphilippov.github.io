8-membered

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪt ˈmɛm.bərd / (AmE), / ˈeɪt ˈmɛmbəd / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) having a ring structure composed of eight atoms or members.
having a ring of eight members (in chemistry)

Examples

The compound features an 8-membered ring, which is unusually stable.
Researchers synthesized an 8-membered lactam for pharmaceutical studies.
Macrocycles with 8-membered rings often have interesting chemical properties.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 7-membered - having a ring of seven members (in chemistry)
  • Degree Higher: 9-membered - having a ring of nine members (in chemistry)
