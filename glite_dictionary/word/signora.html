Signora

1. NOUN

/ siˈnjɔɾə / (AmE), / siˈnjɔːrə / (BrE)
an Italian title or form of address for a married woman, equivalent to 'Mrs.'
Italian title for a married woman

Examples

The waiter greeted the Signora with a warm smile as she entered the restaurant.
Please tell the Signora that her table is ready.
During our trip to Rome, we stayed with a gracious Signora who owned a charming bed and breakfast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Señora - Spanish title for a married woman
  • Antonym: Signorina - Italian title for an unmarried woman

Etymology

From Italian 'signora' (lady, mistress), feminine of 'signore' (lord, mister).
