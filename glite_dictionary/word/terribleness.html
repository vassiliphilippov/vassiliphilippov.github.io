terribleness

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛr.ə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtɛr.ɪ.bəl.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being extremely unpleasant, dreadful, or severe.
quality of being extremely unpleasant

Examples

The terribleness of the situation left everyone in shock and disbelief.
She could not forget the terribleness of the accident she had witnessed.
He spoke about the terribleness of war and its lasting effects on families.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awfulness - quality of being extremely bad or unpleasant
  • Antonym: splendor - magnificent and splendid appearance; grandeur (as an antonym in positive sense)

Etymology

Formed from 'terrible' + '-ness', following a standard English noun-forming pattern.
