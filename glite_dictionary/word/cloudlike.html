cloudlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈklaʊdˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈklaʊdˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling a cloud in appearance, form, or quality
resembling a cloud

Examples

The steam rose in cloudlike billows above the boiling pot.
A cloudlike mist settled quietly over the valley at dawn.
Her dress was made from a soft, cloudlike fabric that shimmered in the light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cloudy - full of or resembling clouds
