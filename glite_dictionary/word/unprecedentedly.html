unprecedentedly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈprɛsɪˌdɛntɪdli / (AmE), / ʌnˈprɛsɪdɛntɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that has never been done, known, or experienced before.
in an unprecedented manner

Examples

The event was organized unprecedentedly quickly, surprising everyone involved.
Profits have risen unprecedentedly in the last quarter, breaking all previous records.
Public interest in the project grew unprecedentedly after the announcement.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: unusually - in a way that is not typical or usual
  • Synonym: unprecedented - never done or known before
