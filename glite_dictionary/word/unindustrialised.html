unindustrialised

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪnˈdʌstr.i.ə.laɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈdʌs.tri.ə.laɪzd / (BrE)
(of a country, region, or area) not having developed industries or not having undergone industrialization.
not having developed industries

Examples

Many rural regions remain unindustrialised due to limited access to infrastructure and investment.
The government aims to bring economic growth to unindustrialised areas.
Wildlife is more abundant in unindustrialised landscapes untouched by factories and urban expansion.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: industrialised - having undergone industrialization
  • Synonym: undeveloped - not developed, especially economically or industrially

Etymology

Formed by the prefix 'un-' plus 'industrialised'.
