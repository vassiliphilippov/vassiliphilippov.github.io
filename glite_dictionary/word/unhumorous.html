unhumorous

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhjumərəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈhjuːmərəs / (BrE)
Not having or showing a sense of humor; lacking amusement or wit.
lacking humor

Examples

Despite his reputation, the professor was often quite unhumorous during lectures.
Her unhumorous response made it clear she was not in the mood for jokes.
The meeting was remarkably unhumorous, with everyone focused solely on business.

Synset Examples

it was a humorless wink; a wink of warning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humorless - lacking any sense of humor

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'humorous', from Latin 'umor' (moisture, humor).
