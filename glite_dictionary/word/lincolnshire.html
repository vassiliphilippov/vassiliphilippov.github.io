Lincolnshire

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪŋkənˌʃɪr / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋkənʃə / (BrE)
a county in eastern England, noted for its agriculture, located on the coast of the North Sea
county in eastern England

Examples

She grew up in Lincolnshire before moving to London for university.
The flat landscapes of Lincolnshire are ideal for farming.
Many tourists visit Lincolnshire to explore its historic towns and countryside.

Etymology

From Old English Lindcylene + scir, meaning 'district of Lincoln'.
