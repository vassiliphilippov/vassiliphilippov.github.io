to handwrite

1. VERB

/ ˈhændˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈhændˌraɪt / (BrE)
to write something by hand rather than typing or printing it
write by hand

Examples

Please handwrite your answers on the exam sheet.
She handwrote all the wedding invitations herself.
Instead of emailing, I decided to handwrite a letter to my friend.

Synset Examples

You should handwrite the note to your guests

Related Terms

  • Antonym: type - to write using a typewriter or computer keyboard
  • Synonym: to write - to produce words on a surface, especially for communication

Etymology

from 'hand' + 'write', indicating writing performed by hand
