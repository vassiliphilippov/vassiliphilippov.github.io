pavonia

1. NOUN

/ pəˈvoʊniə / (AmE), / pəˈvəʊniə / (BrE)
Any of various evergreen plants of the genus Pavonia, known for their white, yellow, or purple flowers.
genus of evergreen flowering plants

Examples

The greenhouse features several rare specimens of pavonia.
Gardeners admire the colorful blooms of pavonia throughout the summer months.
Scientists studied how pavonia adapts to different soil conditions.

Etymology

Named after Spanish botanist José Antonio Pavón (1754–1844).
