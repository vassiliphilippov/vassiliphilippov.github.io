fugly

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʌɡ.li / (AmE), / ˈfʌɡ.li / (BrE)
(slang) extremely unattractive or ugly.
extremely ugly (slang)
slang offensive

Examples

That new building is absolutely fugly and ruins the skyline.
He wore a fugly sweater to the holiday party as a joke.
Honestly, the shoes are comfortable but pretty fugly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ugly - unpleasant to look at
  • Antonym: attractive - pleasing in appearance

Etymology

Formed from a blend of 'fucking' and 'ugly'; originated in US military slang.
