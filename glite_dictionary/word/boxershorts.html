boxershorts

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑksərˌʃɔrts / (AmE), / ˈbɒksəˌʃɔːts / (BrE)
Loose-fitting men's underpants that extend to the thighs, typically with an elastic waistband.
men's loose underpants

Examples

He prefers wearing boxershorts because they are more comfortable than briefs.
The store sells a wide variety of boxershorts in different colors and patterns.
After doing laundry, he realized he was out of clean boxershorts.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: briefs - tight-fitting men's underpants
  • Synonym: boxer briefs - men's underpants combining features of boxers and briefs

Etymology

Named after the shorts worn by boxers in the sport of boxing, combined with 'shorts'.
