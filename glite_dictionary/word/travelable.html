travelable

1. ADJ

/ ˈtræv.ə.lə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈtræv.əl.ə.bəl / (BrE)
(of a road, path, etc.) able to be traversed or suitable for travel.
able to be traversed or suitable for travel

Examples

After the storm, only a few roads remained travelable by car.
During the winter, the mountain pass is rarely travelable due to snow.
They worked through the night to make the runway travelable for emergency aircraft.

Synset Examples

highway crews soon had the roads travelable

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passable - able to be traveled over or through
  • Antonym: impassable - not able to be traveled over or through

Etymology

Formed from 'travel' + '-able', meaning capable of being traveled.
