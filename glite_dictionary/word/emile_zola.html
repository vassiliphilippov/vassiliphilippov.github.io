Emile Zola

1. PROPN

/ eɪˈmil ˈzoʊlə / (AmE), / eɪˈmiːl ˈzəʊlə / (BrE)
(1840–1902) A French novelist and critic, known for his role as a defender of Dreyfus and leader of the naturalist literary movement.
French novelist and critic (1840–1902)

Examples

Emile Zola wrote the famous open letter 'J'accuse' in defense of Alfred Dreyfus.
Many regard Emile Zola as one of the leading figures in the naturalist movement.
The political courage of Emile Zola has inspired activists around the world.
