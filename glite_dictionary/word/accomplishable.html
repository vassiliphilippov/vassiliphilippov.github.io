accomplishable

1. ADJ

/ əˈkɑːmplɪʃəbəl / (AmE), / əˈkɒmplɪʃəbəl / (BrE)
able to be achieved or brought to completion; possible to accomplish.
able to be accomplished or achieved

Examples

Finishing the project in two weeks is certainly accomplishable if we all work together.
She set realistic, accomplishable goals for her team to keep them motivated.
Although the task seemed difficult at first, with proper planning it became accomplishable.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unachievable - impossible to accomplish or achieve
  • Synonym: attainable - able to be attained or achieved

Etymology

From accomplish + -able, formed on the model of existing English adjectives.
