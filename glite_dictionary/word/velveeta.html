Velveeta

1. NOUN

/ vɛlˈviːtə / (AmE), / vɛlˈviːtə / (BrE)
a brand of soft processed American cheese, known for its smooth texture and mild flavor
brand of soft processed American cheese
american british

Examples

She melted some Velveeta to make a creamy cheese sauce for her nachos.
The recipe calls for a block of Velveeta and a can of diced tomatoes.
Many classic American casseroles feature Velveeta as a key ingredient.
