wiseacre

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪzˌeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈwaɪzˌeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who speaks or behaves as if they know everything, often in a sarcastic or conceited manner
know-it-all; sarcastic smart aleck

Examples

Don't be such a wiseacre when others are trying to have a serious discussion.
He always plays the wiseacre in class, offering sarcastic remarks to the teacher.
People soon grew tired of the wiseacre's constant jokes and clever comments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smart aleck - a person who is irritating because they behave as if they know everything
  • Synonym: know-it-all - a person who acts as if they know everything

Etymology

From Middle Dutch wijssegger (soothsayer), from wijs (wise) + segger (sayer).
