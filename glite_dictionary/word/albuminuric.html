albuminuric

1. ADJ

/ ˌæl.bjəˈnu.rɪk / (AmE), / ˌal.bjʊˈnjʊə.rɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by albuminuria, the condition of having albumin in the urine
relating to albuminuria
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed as albuminuric due to persistent protein in the urine.
Albuminuric complications can be an early sign of kidney disease in diabetics.
Researchers studied the effects of diet on albuminuric individuals to understand disease progression.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: albuminuria - the presence of albumin in the urine

Etymology

From albuminuria + -ic, after albumin (a type of protein) and -uric (relating to urine).
