Leonardesque

1. ADJ

/ ˌliːəˌnɑːrˈdɛsk / (AmE), / ˌliːəˌnɑːˈdɛsk / (BrE)
In the style or manner of Leonardo da Vinci; reminiscent of Leonardo da Vinci's techniques, artistry, or ideas.
in the manner of Leonardo da Vinci

Examples

The artist's use of sfumato technique gives his paintings a distinctly Leonardesque quality.
Her Leonardesque sketches displayed mastery of both anatomy and mechanical invention.
Critics praised the exhibition for its Leonardesque blend of artistry and scientific curiosity.

Etymology

From the name Leonardo (da Vinci) + -esque, from French -esque (in the style of).
