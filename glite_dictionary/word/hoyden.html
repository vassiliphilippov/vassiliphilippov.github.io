hoyden

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔɪdən / (AmE), / ˈhɔɪdən / (BrE)
A boisterous, bold, or tomboyish girl who behaves in a manner considered typically boyish or unruly.
boisterous or tomboyish girl
archaic

Examples

As a child, she was quite a hoyden, always climbing trees and playing rough games with the boys.
The strict schoolmistress struggled to control the energetic hoyden in her class.
Though her manners were unrefined, the village admired the young hoyden for her fearless spirit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tomboy - a girl who behaves in a boyish or spirited way

Etymology

From Dutch 'heiden' (heathen), reflecting unruly or wild behavior.
