correspondingly

1. ADV

/ ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌkɒrɪˈspɒndɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that matches or is directly related to something else.
in a matching or related way

Examples

Many companies are expanding, and their workforces are growing correspondingly.
If prices rise, wages should increase correspondingly.
The rules apply to adults and correspondingly to children.

Synset Examples

the temperature decreases correspondingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: similarly - in a similar manner
  • Antonym: therefore - for that reason

Etymology

Formed from 'corresponding' + '-ly', with 'correspond' from Latin 'correspondere' (to answer together).
