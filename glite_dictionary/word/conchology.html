conchology

1. NOUN

/ kɑnˈkɑlədʒi / (AmE), / kɒŋˈkɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study and collection of mollusc shells
study of mollusc shells

Examples

She developed an interest in conchology after finding an unusual shell on the beach.
The museum's collection is a valuable resource for anyone studying conchology.
Many enthusiasts pursue conchology as a hobby, traveling to coastal areas to collect rare shells.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malacology - the branch of zoology that deals with molluscs

Etymology

From Greek 'konkhē' (shell) and '-logy' (study of).
