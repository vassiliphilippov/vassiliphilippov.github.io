gap-toothed

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡæpˌtuːθt / (AmE), / ˈɡapˌtuːθt / (BrE)
having widely spaced teeth, especially noticeable between the front teeth
having widely spaced teeth

Examples

She flashed a gap-toothed smile at her friends.
The gap-toothed boy laughed as he played in the park.
His gap-toothed grin was both endearing and memorable.

Synset Examples

his gap-toothed grin

Etymology

Formed from 'gap' (an opening or space) and 'toothed' (having teeth).
