uncomplicated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkəmˈplɛkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəmˈplɪkətɪd / (BrE)
not difficult to understand or deal with; lacking complexity or elaboration.
lacking complexity; simple

Examples

Her instructions were uncomplicated and easy to follow.
They chose an uncomplicated design for the new kitchen.
The procedure is surprisingly uncomplicated and requires only a few steps.

Synset Examples

small and uncomplicated cars for those really interested in motoring
an unsophisticated machine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simple - easy to understand or do; not complicated
  • Antonym: complex - consisting of many different and connected parts; not simple

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'complicated', from Latin 'complicatus' (folded together).
