epicyclical

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛpɪˈsɪklɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈsɪklɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or resembling an epicycle, especially in mechanics or astronomy.
relating to an epicycle

Examples

The epicyclical motion of the planets fascinated early astronomers.
Engineers improved the efficiency of the epicyclical gearing system in the transmission.
Her thesis focused on the epicyclical models used in ancient astronomy to predict planetary paths.

Synset Examples

epicyclic gear train

Related Terms

  • Synonym: epicyclic - relating to or resembling an epicycle
  • Synonym: epicycle - a small circle whose center moves around the circumference of a larger one

Etymology

From epicycle + -al, from Greek 'epi' (upon) and 'kyklos' (circle).
