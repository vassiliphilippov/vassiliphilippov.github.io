Roseau

1. PROPN

/ roʊˈzoʊ / (AmE), / rəʊˈzəʊ / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Dominica, located on the island's southwest coast
capital of Dominica

Examples

We traveled to Roseau during our Caribbean cruise last year.
Roseau is known for its vibrant markets and colonial architecture.
After landing in Dominica, they drove straight to their hotel in Roseau.

Etymology

From French 'roseau' meaning 'reed', referring to the local river's vegetation.
