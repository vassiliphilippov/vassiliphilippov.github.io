Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences

1. PROPN

/ əˈkædəmi əv ˈmoʊʃən ˈpɪktʃɚ ɑrts ənd ˈsaɪənsɪz / (AmE), / əˈkædəmi ɒv ˈməʊʃən ˈpɪktʃə ɑːts ənd ˈsaɪənsɪz / (BrE)
an American organization that annually presents awards, including the Oscars, for achievements in motion picture production and performance
U.S. film award organization; presents the Oscars

Examples

The Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences announced this year’s Oscar nominees yesterday.
Membership in the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences is considered a great honor in the film industry.
The Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences was founded in 1927 to advance the arts and sciences of movies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Oscar - annual award for achievements in film awarded by the Academy
