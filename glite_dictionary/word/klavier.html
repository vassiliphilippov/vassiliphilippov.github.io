Klavier

1. NOUN

/ klɑˈviːɚ / (AmE), / klɑˈviːə / (BrE)
A keyboard musical instrument, especially a piano, used chiefly in musical contexts and borrowed from German.
keyboard instrument, especially piano

Examples

He performed a sonata for Klavier and violin at the concert.
The composer preferred writing music for the Klavier over the harpsichord.
Her collection included an antique Klavier from the nineteenth century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piano - A large keyboard musical instrument with strings struck by hammers

Etymology

From German 'Klavier', from French 'clavier' meaning 'keyboard'.
