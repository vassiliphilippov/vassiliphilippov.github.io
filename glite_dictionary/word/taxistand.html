taxistand

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæksi stænd / (AmE), / ˈtæksi stænd / (BrE)
a designated place where taxis wait for passengers to hire them
place where taxis wait for passengers

Examples

We found a taxistand outside the main train station, so it was easy to get a cab.
Please drop me off at the nearest taxistand, as I need a taxi to the airport.
During rush hour, there was a long line of taxis waiting at the taxistand.

Synset Examples

in England the place where taxis wait to be hired is called a ‘taxi rank’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxi rank - place where taxis wait to be hired, chiefly British English
