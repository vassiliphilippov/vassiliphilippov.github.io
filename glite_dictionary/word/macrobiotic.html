macrobiotic

1. ADJ

/ ˌmæk.roʊ.baɪˈɑː.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌmæk.rəʊ.baɪˈɒ.tɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of macrobiotics, especially a diet emphasizing whole grains and natural foods to promote health and longevity.
relating to macrobiotics or its diet

Examples

Many people who follow a macrobiotic lifestyle believe it leads to better physical and mental health.
She prepared a macrobiotic meal consisting of brown rice, vegetables, and tofu.
The chef specializes in macrobiotic dishes that are both nutritious and flavorful.

Synset Examples

macrobiotic diet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: macrobiotics - the theory or practice of promoting health through a natural diet

Etymology

From macrobiotics, itself from Greek 'makros' (long) + 'bios' (life).
