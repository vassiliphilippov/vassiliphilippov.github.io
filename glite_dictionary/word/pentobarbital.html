pentobarbital

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɛntoʊˈbɑːrbɪˌtɒl / (AmE), / ˌpɛntəʊˈbɑːbɪtəl / (BrE)
a barbiturate drug used as a sedative, hypnotic, and antispasmodic, often under the trade name Nembutal
barbiturate drug used as a sedative and hypnotic
medical

Examples

The veterinarian administered pentobarbital to euthanize the animal painlessly.
After ingesting pentobarbital, the patient quickly became unconscious.
Doctors sometimes use pentobarbital to control severe seizures in emergency situations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barbital - a barbiturate drug used as a sedative and hypnotic
  • Synonym: phenobarbital - a barbiturate used as a sedative and anticonvulsant

Etymology

From 'penta-' (five) and 'barbital', based on chemical structure.
