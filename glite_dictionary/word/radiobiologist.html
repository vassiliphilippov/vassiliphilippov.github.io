radiobiologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌreɪdioʊbaɪˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌreɪdɪəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies the effects of radiation on living organisms
scientist studying radiation effects on organisms
medical

Examples

The radiobiologist presented her findings at the international conference on nuclear medicine.
A team led by a radiobiologist is investigating how radiation exposure affects plant growth.
As a radiobiologist, he focuses on minimizing the harmful effects of medical imaging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biologist - a scientist who studies living organisms

Etymology

From radio- (relating to radiation) + biologist (a specialist in biology).
