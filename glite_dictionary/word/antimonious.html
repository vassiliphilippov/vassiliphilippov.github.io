antimonious

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.təˈmoʊ.ni.əs / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪˈməʊ.ni.əs / (BrE)
relating to or derived from antimony, especially in which antimony has a lower valence (typically +3).
containing or relating to antimony (lower valence)

Examples

The chemist analyzed the antimonious oxide sample to determine its composition.
Certain antimonious compounds are used in the production of flame retardants.
An antimonious solution must be handled carefully in the laboratory.

Synset Examples

antimonious oxide

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: antimonic - relating to antimony, especially with higher valence (+5)

Etymology

From antimony + -ous suffix, indicating a lower oxidation state in chemistry.
