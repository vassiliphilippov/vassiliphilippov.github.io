arteria ethmoidalis

1. NOUN

/ ɑːrˈtɪəriə ɛθˌmɔɪˈdeɪlɪs / (AmE), / ɑːˌtɪə.ri.ə ɛθˌmɔɪˈdeɪ.lɪs / (BrE)
a branch of the ophthalmic artery that supplies blood to structures in and around the nasal capsule
branch of the ophthalmic artery in the nasal area
medical

Examples

The arteria ethmoidalis is often discussed in surgical approaches to the skull base.
Damage to the arteria ethmoidalis can lead to significant bleeding during nasal surgery.
The ophthalmic artery branches into the arteria ethmoidalis to supply the nasal region.

Etymology

From Latin, meaning 'ethmoidal artery'.
