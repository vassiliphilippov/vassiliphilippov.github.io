distributer

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈstrɪbjutər / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɪbjʊtə / (BrE)
an electrical device in a gasoline engine that distributes voltage to the spark plugs in the correct firing order
device distributing voltage to spark plugs

Examples

The mechanic replaced the faulty distributer to restore proper engine timing.
Without a functioning distributer, the engine cannot fire its spark plugs in the correct sequence.
Older cars often require periodic maintenance on the distributer to ensure reliable ignition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distributor cap - the cover that protects and insulates the contacts in a distributor

Etymology

From Latin 'distribuere' (to divide, distribute).

2. NOUN

/ dɪˈstrɪbjutər / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɪbjuːtə / (BrE)
a person or company that supplies goods to shops and other businesses
person or company supplying goods

Examples

The regional distributer ensured that all stores received the latest products on time.
As a distributer, she coordinated shipments between manufacturers and retailers.
The company partnered with a new distributer to expand its market presence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distributor - a person or company that supplies goods to shops and other businesses

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'distributor', from Latin 'distributus', past participle of 'distribuere' (to divide, distribute).
