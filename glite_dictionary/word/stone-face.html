stone-face

1. NOUN

/ stoʊn feɪs / (AmE), / stəʊn feɪs / (BrE)
A succulent plant of the genus Lithops, native to Africa, with thick leaves resembling stones and solitary yellow or white flowers.
African Lithops plant resembling a stone

Examples

The stone-face blends perfectly into its rocky habitat, making it difficult for animals to spot.
She has an impressive collection of stone-faces in her greenhouse, each with unique patterns.
The stone-face blooms with a bright yellow flower every autumn.
