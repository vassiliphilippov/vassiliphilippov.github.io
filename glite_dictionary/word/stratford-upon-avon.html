Stratford-upon-Avon

1. PROPN

/ ˌstræt.fərd əˌpɑn ˈeɪ.vən / (AmE), / ˌstrætfəd əpɒn ˈeɪ.vən / (BrE)
a town in central England on the River Avon, known as the birthplace and burial place of William Shakespeare
town in England; Shakespeare's birthplace

Examples

Many tourists visit Stratford-upon-Avon each year to see Shakespeare's birthplace.
She spent her childhood in Stratford-upon-Avon and often visited the local theaters.
The historical charm of Stratford-upon-Avon attracts both locals and visitors alike.

Etymology

From 'Stratford' (street ford) and its location on the River Avon, England.
