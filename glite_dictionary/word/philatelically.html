philatelically

1. ADV

/ fɪˌlæt̬əˈlɪkli / (AmE), / fɪˌlætəˈlɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that relates to philately or stamp collecting.
in a way relating to stamp collecting

Examples

The event was celebrated philatelically with the release of a commemorative stamp.
She approached the topic philatelically, focusing on rare postage stamps.
Many countries mark historical events philatelically through special stamp issues.

Synset Examples

the Post Office honors great Americans philatelically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: philatelic - relating to stamp collecting or philately

Etymology

From philatelic + -ally, after French philatéliquement.
