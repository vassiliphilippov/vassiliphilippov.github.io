Arnhem

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑrn.hɛm / (AmE), / ˈɑːn.hɛm / (BrE)
a city in the central Netherlands on the lower Rhine River, notable as the site of a major World War II battle in 1944.
city in the Netherlands; World War II battle site

Examples

The Allied forces suffered heavy losses during the Battle of Arnhem in 1944.
Many tourists visit Arnhem to learn about its World War II history.
Arnhem lies on the banks of the lower Rhine in the Netherlands.
