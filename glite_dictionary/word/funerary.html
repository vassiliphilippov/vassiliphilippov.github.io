funerary

1. ADJ

/ ˈfjuːnəˌrɛri / (AmE), / ˈfjuːnərəri / (BrE)
relating to or intended for a funeral or burial rites
relating to a funeral or burial

Examples

The museum displayed a collection of funerary artifacts from ancient Egypt.
She specializes in the study of funerary architecture from the Roman Empire.
A funerary inscription revealed details about the burial site's occupants.

Synset Examples

funerary urn

Related Terms

  • Synonym: funeral - ceremony honoring a deceased person
  • Synonym: burial - the act of placing a dead body in the ground

Etymology

From Latin 'funerarius', from 'funus' meaning 'funeral'.
