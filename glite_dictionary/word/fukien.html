Fukien

1. NOUN

/ fuːˈkiːɛn / (AmE), / fuːˈkiːɛn / (BrE)
any of the forms of the Chinese language spoken in the Fukien (Fujian) province of China, also known as Hokkien.
Chinese dialects from Fukien/Fujian province

Examples

Many overseas Chinese communities communicate in Fukien as their common language.
The Fukien dialect differs significantly from Mandarin in pronunciation and vocabulary.
My grandmother grew up speaking Fukien at home in the Fujian province.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hokkien - a major dialect group spoken in southern Fujian and throughout Southeast Asia

Etymology

From Fukien, older romanization of modern Fujian, a province in southeastern China.
