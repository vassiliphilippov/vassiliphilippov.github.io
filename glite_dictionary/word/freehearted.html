freehearted

1. ADJ

/ ˈfriˌhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
Generous and open in giving or helping others; unselfishly kind or charitable.
generous; openhanded

Examples

Her freehearted nature made her beloved by everyone in the community.
They gave freehearted support to the cause, donating both money and time.
He welcomed us with a freehearted smile and invited us in for dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: generous - Willing to give more of something, especially money or time, than is strictly necessary or expected
  • Antonym: stingy - Unwilling to give or spend; ungenerous

Etymology

Formed from 'free' + 'hearted', suggesting a heart that gives freely.
