schoolboy

1. NOUN

/ ˈskuːlˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈskuːl.bɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy who attends school, especially a boy of school age
boy attending school

Examples

The schoolboy ran down the hallway to catch his bus.
As a schoolboy, he was always curious and eager to learn.
A group of schoolboys played soccer after their classes ended.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: schoolgirl - a girl attending school

Etymology

Formed from 'school' + 'boy', appearing in English since the 16th century.
