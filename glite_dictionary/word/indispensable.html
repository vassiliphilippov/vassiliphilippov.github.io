indispensable

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.dɪˈspɛn.sə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.dɪˈspɛn.sə.bəl / (BrE)
absolutely necessary; essential and not to be done without
absolutely necessary; essential

Examples

Her advice proved to be indispensable during the crisis.
A good dictionary is indispensable for language learners.
He is considered an indispensable member of the team.

Synset Examples

essential tools and materials
funds essential to the completion of the project
an indispensable worker

Related Terms

  • Synonym: essential - absolutely necessary, extremely important
  • Antonym: dispensable - able to be spared or done without; not necessary

Etymology

From French 'indispensable', from Latin 'indispensabilis': in- (not) + dispensabilis (able to be spared).

2. ADJ

/ ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbəl / (BrE)
(formal) Impossible to avoid or do without; absolutely unavoidable or requisite for something to happen or be completed.
impossible to avoid or do without

Examples

The ceremony is an indispensable part of the annual tradition.
Attendance at this training session is indispensable for all new employees.
Certain formal greetings are indispensable in diplomatic meetings.

Synset Examples

the routine but indispensable ceremonies of state

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unavoidable - impossible to avoid
  • Antonym: dispensable - able to be omitted or done without

Etymology

From French ‘indispensable’, from Latin ‘indispensabilis’ (not able to be dispensed with).
