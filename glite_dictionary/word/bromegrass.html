bromegrass

1. NOUN

/ ˈbroʊmˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊmˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
Any of various grasses of the genus Bromus, growing in woodlands and meadows in temperate regions.
type of grass; genus Bromus

Examples

The field was covered with bromegrass after the summer rains.
Farmers often plant bromegrass as a forage crop for livestock.
We identified several species of bromegrass in the nature reserve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bromus - the genus of bromegrasses

Etymology

From genus name Bromus plus 'grass'.
