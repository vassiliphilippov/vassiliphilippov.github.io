Djakarta

1. PROPN

/ dʒəˈkɑrtə / (AmE), / dʒəˈkɑːtə / (BrE)
An older spelling of Jakarta, the capital and largest city of Indonesia located on the island of Java.
former spelling of Jakarta, Indonesian capital

Examples

The international flight landed in Djakarta late at night.
Historical maps often refer to the Indonesian capital as Djakarta.
He spent several years working in Djakarta during the 1960s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jakarta - the capital and largest city of Indonesia

Etymology

Variant Dutch-based spelling of Jakarta, influenced by Dutch colonial usage.
