bellyacher

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛliˌeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbɛliˌeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who complains or whines excessively
a habitual complainer
informal

Examples

No one wants to listen to a bellyacher during a difficult situation.
She quickly gained a reputation as the office bellyacher, always finding something to grumble about.
Instead of being a bellyacher, try to suggest solutions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: complainer - someone who complains frequently
  • Synonym: grumbler - a person who grumbles or complains
  • Antonym: optimist - a person who tends to be hopeful and confident about the future

Etymology

Formed from 'bellyache' (to complain excessively), with '-er' agent noun suffix.
