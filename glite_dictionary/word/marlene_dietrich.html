Marlene Dietrich

1. PROPN

/ mɑrˈleɪnə ˈdiˌtrɪk / (AmE), / mɑːˈleɪnə ˈdiːtrɪx / (BrE)
(1901–1992) A German-American actress and singer, known for her roles in classic Hollywood films and as a cabaret performer.
German-American film actress and singer

Examples

Marlene Dietrich captivated audiences with her iconic performance in "The Blue Angel."
Many critics consider Marlene Dietrich one of the most influential actresses of the twentieth century.
After her Hollywood career, Marlene Dietrich found success as an international cabaret star.
