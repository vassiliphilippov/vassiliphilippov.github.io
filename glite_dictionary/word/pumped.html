pumped

1. ADJ

/ pʌmpt / (AmE), / pʌmpt / (BrE)
very excited and full of energy, usually because of anticipation or adrenaline; extremely enthusiastic
very excited or enthusiastic
informal

Examples

Everyone on the team was pumped before the championship game.
She felt pumped after getting the good news about her job offer.
I'm so pumped for tonight's concert that I can't sit still.

Synset Examples

we were really pumped up for the race
he was so pumped he couldn't sleep

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excited - feeling or showing excitement
  • Antonym: calm - not showing or feeling excitement; relaxed or peaceful
