110th

1. ADJ

/ wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈtɛnθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈtɛnθ / (BrE)
being the ordinal number corresponding to one hundred ten in a sequence; coming after the 109th and before the 111th
one hundred tenth (ordinal number)

Examples

He finished in 110th place out of more than 500 runners.
The 110th anniversary of the school will be celebrated next year.
She lives on the 110th floor of the skyscraper.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 109th - the ordinal number of one hundred nine
  • Degree Higher: 111th - the ordinal number of one hundred eleven
