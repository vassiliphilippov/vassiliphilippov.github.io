Francisco Villa

1. PROPN

/ frænˈsiskoʊ ˈviːjə / (AmE), / franˈsiːskəʊ ˈviːjə / (BrE)
(1878–1923) Mexican revolutionary leader and general, prominent during the Mexican Revolution.
Mexican revolutionary leader (1878–1923)

Examples

Many historians consider Francisco Villa a pivotal figure in the Mexican Revolution.
The troops followed Francisco Villa during the famous raid on Columbus, New Mexico.
Movies and novels have depicted the dramatic life of Francisco Villa.
