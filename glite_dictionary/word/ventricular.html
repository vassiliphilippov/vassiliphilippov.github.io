ventricular

1. ADJ

/ vɛnˈtrɪkjələr / (AmE), / vɛnˈtrɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to a ventricle, especially one of the chambers of the heart or brain.
relating to a heart or brain ventricle
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed a ventricular arrhythmia after analyzing the patient's ECG results.
Swelling in the ventricular region of the brain can indicate increased intracranial pressure.
Researchers studied the effects of the new drug on ventricular function in heart disease patients.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: atrial - relating to an atrium, especially of the heart

Etymology

From Latin 'ventriculus' meaning 'little belly' or 'ventricle'.
