Arabist

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrəbɪst / (AmE), / ˈærəbɪst / (BrE)
a scholar who specializes in Arabic language, literature, or culture
scholar of Arabic language or culture

Examples

She is an eminent Arabist who has translated classical Arabic poetry into English.
The museum consulted an Arabist to ensure the accuracy of the historical exhibit.
As an Arabist, he has spent many years living and researching in the Middle East.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orientalist - a scholar of Eastern (often Middle Eastern or Asian) cultures or languages

Etymology

From Arab + -ist (a suffix indicating a specialist or adherent).
