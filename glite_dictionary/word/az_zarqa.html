Az Zarqa

1. PROPN

/ æz zɑrˈkɑ / (AmE), / æz zɑːˈkɑː / (BrE)
A city in northwestern Jordan, northeast of Amman, known as an industrial center.
city in northwestern Jordan

Examples

Many factories are located in Az Zarqa, making it a hub of Jordan's industry.
He was born in Az Zarqa and later moved to Amman for university.
Tourists often pass through Az Zarqa on their way to northern Jordan.

Etymology

From Arabic الزرقاء (az-Zarqāʾ), meaning 'the blue (city/one)'.
