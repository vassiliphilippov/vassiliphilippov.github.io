AMusD

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ ˌmjuːz ˈdiː / (AmE), / ˈeɪ ˌmjuːz ˈdiː / (BrE)
a doctorate degree in musical arts; Doctor of Musical Arts.
Doctor of Musical Arts degree

Examples

After years of study, she was awarded her AMusD in composition.
He pursued an AMusD to advance his career as a conductor.
Many university faculty members in music hold an AMusD or DMA degree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: DMA - Doctor of Musical Arts degree

Etymology

Abbreviation of Latin 'Artium Musicarum Doctor' (Doctor of Musical Arts).
