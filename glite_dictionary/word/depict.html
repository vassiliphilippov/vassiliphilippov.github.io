to depict

1. VERB

/ dɪˈpɪkt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɪkt / (BrE)
to represent or describe someone or something in words, images, or art
represent or describe in words, images, or art

Examples

The artist chose to depict the historical scene in vivid colors.
The novel depicts life in a small coastal town with remarkable detail.
The documentary depicts the challenges faced by refugees around the world.

Synset Examples

He drew an elaborate plan of attack

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to portray - to represent or describe in a particular way
  • Synonym: to describe - to give an account of something in words

Etymology

From Latin 'depictus', past participle of 'depingere' (to paint).

WordNet ID: oewn-00989103-v
Synset POS: v

2. VERB

/ dɪˈpɪkt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɪkt / (BrE)
to show or represent something in a picture, painting, or other form of art; to describe in words vividly.
to represent or show in art or words

Examples

The novel vividly depicts life in a small coastal village.
The mural depicts scenes from the town’s history.
Her latest film depicts the struggles faced by refugees.

Synset Examples

This scene depicts country life
the face of the child is rendered with much tenderness in this painting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to portray - describe or show in a work of art or words
  • Synonym: to illustrate - to provide a picture or example to explain or decorate

Etymology

From Latin depictus, past participle of depingere (to paint, portray), from de- (down) + pingere (to paint).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01690851-v
Synset POS: v

3. VERB

/ dɪˈpɪkt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɪkt / (BrE)
to represent someone by drawing, painting, or otherwise creating their portrait
represent by drawing or painting

Examples

The artist spent months trying to depict the king in a way that captured his true character.
Many Renaissance painters sought to depict religious figures with as much realism as possible.
She was commissioned to depict the mayor in a formal portrait for the city hall.

Synset Examples

Goya wanted to portray his mistress, the Duchess of Alba

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to portray - to make a likeness or representation of, especially in art
  • Synonym: paint - to create an image with paints, especially a portrait

Etymology

From Latin 'depictus', past participle of 'depingere' (to paint).
