nonskid

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɑnˌskɪd / (AmE), / ˈnɒnˌskɪd / (BrE)
(of a surface or material) designed to reduce or prevent slipping or skidding.
prevents slipping or skidding

Examples

Be sure to use a nonskid rug in the bathroom to prevent accidents.
These nonskid shoes are perfect for working in kitchens.
The stairs were covered with a nonskid tape for added safety.

Synset Examples

nonskid tires

Related Terms

  • Antonym: slippery - difficult to hold or stand on because smooth, wet, or slimy

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'non-' (not) + 'skid' (to slide uncontrollably).
