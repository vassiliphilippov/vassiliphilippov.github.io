coarse-furred

1. ADJ

/ kɔrs ˈfɜrd / (AmE), / kɔːs ˈfɜːd / (BrE)
Having hair or fur that is rough or harsh to the touch.
having rough or harsh fur

Examples

The wolf was coarse-furred, its pelt bristling in the cold wind.
Some breeds of dogs are naturally coarse-furred and require special grooming.
The coarse-furred cat preferred to stay outdoors, regardless of the weather.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smooth-furred - having hair or fur that is smooth to the touch
