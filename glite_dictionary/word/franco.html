Franco

1. PROPN

/ ˈfrɑːŋkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈfraŋkəʊ / (BrE)
(1892–1975) Spanish general and statesman who ruled Spain as a dictator from 1939 until his death.
Spanish dictator (1892–1975)

Examples

Franco established an authoritarian regime in Spain that lasted for decades.
Historians debate the long-term effects of Franco's rule on Spanish society.
Many families were divided during the civil war led by Franco.
