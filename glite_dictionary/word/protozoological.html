protozoological

1. ADJ

/ ˌproʊtəˌzoʊəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəˌzəʊəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the branch of zoology that studies protozoans
relating to protozoan zoology
medical

Examples

The research team published several protozoological papers on newly discovered species.
Her protozoological expertise proved invaluable during the outbreak investigation.
This laboratory specializes in protozoological studies, focusing on parasites affecting humans and animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protozoology - the branch of zoology dealing with protozoans

Etymology

From 'protozoology' (study of protozoans) + '-ical', ultimately from Greek 'prōto-' (first) and 'zōon' (animal).
