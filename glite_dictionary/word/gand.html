Gand

1. PROPN

/ ɡɑ̃ / (AmE), / ɡɑ̃ / (BrE)
the French name for Ghent, a port city in northwestern Belgium known for its industry and historic cloth trade
French name for Ghent, city in Belgium

Examples

The medieval city center of Gand attracts tourists from all over the world.
She studied textile history in Gand due to its renowned cloth industry.
Many documents from the 19th century refer to Ghent as Gand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ghent - a city in Belgium, known in Dutch as Gent

Etymology

French name for the city known in Dutch as Gent and in English as Ghent.
