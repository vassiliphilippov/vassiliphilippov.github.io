Bhaga

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɑːɡə / (AmE), / ˈbɑːɡə / (BrE)
a Hindu god associated with wealth and love
Hindu god of wealth and love

Examples

In Hindu mythology, Bhaga is worshipped as a deity of wealth and fortune.
People often pray to Bhaga for prosperity and harmony in their homes.
The story of Bhaga appears in several ancient Sanskrit texts.

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Bhaga', meaning 'lord' or 'dispenser'.
