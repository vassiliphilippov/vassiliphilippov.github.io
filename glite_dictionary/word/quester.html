quester

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɛstər / (AmE), / ˈkwɛstə / (BrE)
a person who is searching for something, especially knowledge, truth, or a particular object
someone engaged in a quest

Examples

As a lifelong quester after knowledge, she traveled the world to study ancient languages.
The novel's main character is a quester in search of a legendary artifact.
Each quester faced a different challenge on the journey to enlightenment.

Synset Examples

they are seekers after truth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seeker - a person who tries to find or obtain something
  • Synonym: searcher - someone who searches for something

Etymology

From 'quest' (from Latin quaerere 'to seek') + '-er', denoting an agent noun.
