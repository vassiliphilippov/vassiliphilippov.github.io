impress

1. VERB

/ ɪmˈprɛs / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛs / (BrE)
to reproduce (a design, image, or text) by printing, stamping, or pressing onto a surface
reproduce by printing or stamping

Examples

Each coin has its own design impressed upon it during minting.
The printer impressed the letterhead onto the paper with great precision.
A company seal was impressed at the bottom of the certificate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: print - to produce text or images on paper
  • Synonym: stamp - to imprint a mark or design using pressure

Etymology

From Latin imprimere, from in- (into) + premere (to press).
