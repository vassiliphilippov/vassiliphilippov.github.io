Alexander Bell

1. PROPN

/ ˈælɪgˌzændər bɛl / (AmE), / ˈælɪksˌændə bɛl / (BrE)
The Scottish-born American inventor (1847–1922) best known for inventing the telephone.
inventor of the telephone, 1847–1922
