Pyrenees

1. PROPN

/ ˌpɪrəˈniz / (AmE), / ˌpɪərəˈniːz / (BrE)
a mountain range forming a natural border between France and Spain, extending from the Atlantic Ocean to the Mediterranean Sea
mountain range between France and Spain

Examples

The Pyrenees are known for their stunning landscapes and diverse wildlife.
We spent our summer vacation hiking in the Pyrenees.
Snowfall in the Pyrenees can be heavy during the winter months.

Etymology

From Latin 'Pyrenaei montes', from Greek 'Πυρηναῖα ὄρη' (Pyrēnaîa órē).
