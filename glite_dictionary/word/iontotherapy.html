iontotherapy

1. NOUN

/ aɪˌɑntoʊˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / aɪˌɒntəˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
a medical treatment that uses an electric current to introduce medicinal ions into bodily tissues through the skin
therapy using electric current to deliver ions
medical

Examples

The dermatologist recommended iontotherapy to treat her persistent skin condition.
Recent studies have shown that iontotherapy can enhance the effectiveness of drug delivery.
After several sessions of iontotherapy, the patient reported noticeable improvement in symptoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iontophoresis - the introduction of ions into the body by applying a local electric current

Etymology

From ion + therapy; modeled after 'iontophoresis'.
