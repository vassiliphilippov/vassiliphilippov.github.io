Greta Garbo

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡritə ˈɡɑrboʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡriːtə ˈɡɑːbəʊ / (BrE)
(1905–1990) A Swedish-born American film actress noted for her striking looks, enigmatic persona, and reclusive lifestyle.
Swedish-born American film actress (1905–1990)

Examples

Greta Garbo was famous for her captivating screen presence and mysterious private life.
Film historians consider Greta Garbo one of the greatest actresses of the silent and classic Hollywood eras.
Many fans were intrigued by Greta Garbo's decision to retire from acting at the height of her fame.

Etymology

Born Greta Lovisa Gustafsson in Sweden; 'Garbo' adopted as a stage name.
