Azeri

1. NOUN

/ əˈzɛri / (AmE), / əˈzɪəri / (BrE)
a member of an ethnic group native to Azerbaijan and parts of neighboring countries
member of the Azeri ethnic group

Examples

Many Azeris live in the capital city of Baku.
The Azeri community has preserved its cultural traditions for centuries.
He is proud of his Azeri heritage and language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Azerbaijani - a person from Azerbaijan; relating to Azerbaijan or its people

Etymology

From Azerbaijani 'Azəri', relating to Azerbaijan.
