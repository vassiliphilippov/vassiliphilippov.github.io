two-footed

1. ADJ

/ tuˈfʊtɪd / (AmE), / tuˈfʊtɪd / (BrE)
Having two feet; characterized by having or using two feet.
having two feet

Examples

Most birds are naturally two-footed creatures.
The gymnast balanced gracefully on her two-footed landing.
He designed a robot with a two-footed structure for better balance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bipedal - walking on two feet or using two feet for locomotion
