wide-ranging

1. ADJ

/ ˈwaɪdˌreɪndʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪdˌreɪndʒɪŋ / (BrE)
covering or including a large variety of subjects, ideas, or areas; extensive in scope or effect.
covering a large area or variety

Examples

The committee conducted a wide-ranging investigation into the company’s financial practices.
Her interests are wide-ranging, from classical literature to quantum physics.
The government announced a wide-ranging series of reforms affecting many sectors.

Synset Examples

the pianist's wide-ranging repertoire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extensive - covering a large area or scope
  • Antonym: narrow - limited in extent or scope

2. ADJ

/ waɪd ˈreɪndʒɪŋ / (AmE), / waɪd ˈreɪndʒɪŋ / (BrE)
including or dealing with a large variety of subjects, things, or people; comprehensive or diverse in scope.
covering a large variety; comprehensive

Examples

The government is introducing wide-ranging reforms affecting nearly every sector.
Her interests are wide-ranging, from classical music to mountain climbing.
The report offered wide-ranging solutions to the company’s financial problems.

Synset Examples

varied motives prompt people to join a political party
varied ethnic traditions of the immigrants

Related Terms

  • Synonym: varied - showing variety; different or diverse
  • Synonym: comprehensive - including all or nearly all elements or aspects
  • Antonym: narrow - limited in extent, amount, or scope
