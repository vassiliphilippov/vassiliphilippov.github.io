benedictive

1. ADJ

/ ˌbɛnɪˈdɪktɪv / (AmE), / ˌbɛnɪˈdɪktɪv / (BrE)
expressing or conveying a blessing or benediction
expressing a blessing

Examples

The priest raised his hands in a benedictive gesture at the end of the ceremony.
Her tone took on a benedictive quality as she wished everyone well before leaving.
A benedictive prayer echoed through the hall, invoking peace and prosperity for all present.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: benedictory - expressing or containing a blessing

Etymology

From Latin 'benedictus', meaning 'blessed', via the English noun 'benediction'.
