habitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæbɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈhæbɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
a habitual way of behaving or a customary pattern of conduct
habitual way of behaving

Examples

He quickly fell back into his old habitude of taking a walk after dinner.
The artist's creative habitude led to a steady output of paintings each month.
Her morning habitude includes reading the news over coffee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: habit - a regular tendency or practice

Etymology

From Latin 'habitudo' (condition, habit), via Old French 'habitude'.
