theoriser

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiəraɪzər / (AmE), / ˈθɪəraɪzə / (BrE)
a person who forms theories, especially in science or art
person who forms theories
british

Examples

She is a respected theoriser in the field of quantum physics.
Many artists dislike being labelled as mere theorisers rather than creators.
The panel included both practitioners and theorisers of contemporary dance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theorist - a person concerned with the theoretical aspects of a subject
  • Antonym: practitioner - a person actively engaged in an art, discipline, or profession

Etymology

From 'theorise' (British spelling of 'theorize') + '-er'.
