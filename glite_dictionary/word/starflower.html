starflower

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑrˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌflaʊə / (BrE)
a small plant with narrow leaves and clusters of star-shaped white flowers, originally from Europe and now also found in eastern North America
plant with star-shaped white flowers

Examples

The woods were filled with blooming starflowers in early spring.
A single starflower stood out among the green undergrowth.
Gardeners appreciate the delicate beauty of the starflower for shady borders.

Etymology

Named for its star-shaped flowers; 'star' + 'flower'.
