pipistrel

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪpɪˌstrɛl / (AmE), / ˈpɪpɪˌstrɛl / (BrE)
a small brown bat of Europe, typically referring to a species of the genus Pipistrellus
a small European brown bat

Examples

We spotted a pipistrel fluttering near the garden at dusk.
The pipistrel feeds on insects it catches during its nightly flights.
Researchers observed the pipistrel roosting in an old stone building.

Etymology

From French *pipistrelle*, derived from Italian *pipistrello*, from Latin *vespertilio* (bat).
