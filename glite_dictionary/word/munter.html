munter

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌntər / (AmE), / ˈmʌntə / (BrE)
(slang, British) An unattractive person, especially one considered to be very unattractive.
unattractive person (slang, British)
slang british

Examples

She was cruelly called a munter by some classmates at school.
He felt hurt after being called a munter behind his back.
In some circles, being called a munter can seriously damage your confidence.

Synset Examples

He was called a munter for his unusual style.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: beauty - A person who is very attractive
  • Antonym: hottie - Attractive person, especially sexually attractive
