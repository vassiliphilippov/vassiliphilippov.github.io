holder

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊldɚ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊldə / (BrE)
a device or container designed to hold or support something
device or container for holding something

Examples

She put her keys in the holder by the door so she wouldn't lose them.
The office was equipped with a holder for pens and pencils on each desk.
A wine glass holder is built into the picnic basket for convenience.

Synset Examples

a towel holder
a cigarette holder
an umbrella holder

Related Terms

  • Synonym: container - an object that can be used to hold or transport something

Etymology

From Middle English 'holden' (to hold) + '-er', meaning one who or that which holds.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03530634-n
Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person who possesses or has ownership of something, such as a document, title, or object
person possessing something

Examples

Only the holder of a valid ticket may enter the venue.
She is the proud holder of a United States passport.
Each holder must show identification before receiving their certificate.

Synset Examples

he holds the trophy
she holds a United States passport
Permit holders are also required to have a valid business license

Related Terms

  • Synonym: owner - a person who owns something
  • Synonym: possessor - someone who possesses something
  • Synonym: occupant - a person who resides in or holds a position

Etymology

From Old English 'healdan' (to hold) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10199809-n
Synset POS: n

3. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person who possesses a cheque, note, bond, or document of title, especially one that is made payable to them or to the bearer
legal possessor of a negotiable document
legal

Examples

The holder of the check must endorse it before it can be deposited.
Only the holder of the bond is entitled to receive the payment.
The ticket is valid for use by the holder only and is non-transferable.

Synset Examples

the bond was marked ‘payable to bearer’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bearer - a person in possession of a negotiable document; entitled to payment or rights under it
