to wildin'

1. VERB

/ ˈwaɪldɪn / (AmE), / ˈwaɪldɪn / (BrE)
behaving in a crazy, reckless, or out-of-control manner, often for fun or as a display of excitement
acting crazily or recklessly (slang)
informal slang

Examples

The crowd was wildin' at the concert, dancing and shouting all night.
You're really wildin' today—calm down before you get in trouble.
She told her friends they were wildin' when they started jumping on the tables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wilding - acting wildly or causing chaos
  • Synonym: behaving - to act in a specified way

Etymology

Contraction of 'wilding', based on 'wild' meaning unruly or out of control, popularized in African American Vernacular English.
