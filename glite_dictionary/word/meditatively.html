meditatively

1. ADV

/ ˌmɛdəˈteɪtɪvli / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪˈteɪtɪvli / (BrE)
in a thoughtful or reflective manner, as if engaged in meditation
in a meditative or reflective way

Examples

She stared meditatively out the window, lost in her thoughts.
He spoke meditatively, choosing each word with care.
The artist painted meditatively, fully absorbed in the process.

Synset Examples

round and round, while meditatively, as a cow chewing the cud, he let his eyes rest on the flat water ahead of him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meditative - thoughtful; deeply reflective

Etymology

From 'meditative' + '-ly', forming an adverb.
