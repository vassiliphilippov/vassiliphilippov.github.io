roofing

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈruːfɪŋ / (BrE)
material used for covering and constructing a roof, such as tiles, shingles, or metal sheets
material used to cover a roof

Examples

The contractor recommended metal roofing for durability in harsh weather conditions.
They stored rolls of roofing in the garage before starting the renovation.
Shingles are a popular type of roofing for residential homes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roof - the structure forming the upper covering of a building

2. NOUN

/ ˈruːfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈruːfɪŋ / (BrE)
the job or activity of constructing or repairing roofs
the craft of making or repairing roofs

Examples

He has been working in roofing for over twenty years.
The roofing on the new house took several days to complete.
She is learning the basics of roofing as part of her apprenticeship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpentry - the trade of working with wood
  • Synonym: plastering - the craft of applying plaster to walls and ceilings

Etymology

From 'roof' + '-ing', indicating the act or process related to roofs.
