HHS

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪtʃ eɪtʃ ˈɛs / (AmE), / ˌeɪtʃ eɪtʃ ˈɛs / (BrE)
abbreviation for the United States Department of Health and Human Services, the federal department responsible for public health and welfare programs.
US Department of Health and Human Services (abbreviation)
american

Examples

The HHS announced new guidelines for public health agencies this morning.
Funding for the program was provided by the HHS.
Several research studies were commissioned by HHS to improve healthcare access.
