finable

1. ADJ

/ ˈfaɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnəbəl / (BrE)
Liable to be fined or subject to a financial penalty.
liable to a fine
legal

Examples

Deliberate littering is a finable offense in this city.
The company was found guilty of a finable violation of safety regulations.
Not all traffic violations are finable; some result only in warnings.

Etymology

From fine (to impose a penalty) + -able (capable of).
