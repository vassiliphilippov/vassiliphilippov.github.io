yap

1. NOUN

/ jæp / (AmE), / jæp / (BrE)
(informal) The mouth, especially when considered as a source of talking or noise.
mouth (informal)
informal slang

Examples

If you don’t keep your yap shut, you’ll get us all in trouble.
He opened his yap to complain again during the meeting.
She told him to close his yap and listen for once.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trap - (informal) mouth, especially as a source of noise or talk

2. VERB

/ jæp / (AmE), / jæp / (BrE)
(of a dog) to bark in a sharp, high-pitched tone, typically in a repetitive or shrill manner.
bark in a high-pitched tone

Examples

The small dog began to yap loudly every time someone walked by the window.
At night, the puppies would yap until their mother returned to the kennel.
The terrier yapped excitedly as it chased its tail in the backyard.

Synset Examples

the puppies yelped

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yelp - to give a sharp, high-pitched bark or cry

Etymology

Imitative of the sound; related to Middle English *yappe* and akin to 'yelp'.
