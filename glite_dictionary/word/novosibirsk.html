Novosibirsk

1. PROPN

/ ˌnoʊvoʊsɪˈbɪrsk / (AmE), / ˌnəʊvəsɪˈbɪəsk / (BrE)
A city in the Asian part of Russia, located on the Ob River; it is the largest city in Siberia.
largest city in Siberia, Russia

Examples

The Trans-Siberian Railway passes through Novosibirsk, connecting Europe and Asia.
She moved to Novosibirsk to study engineering at the renowned university there.
Many scientific institutes are located in Novosibirsk, making it a center of research in Russia.

Etymology

From Russian Новосибирск (Novosibirsk), meaning 'New Siberia City'.
