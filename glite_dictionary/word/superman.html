superman

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːpərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəmæn / (BrE)
(slang) A street name for lysergic acid diethylamide (LSD), a powerful hallucinogenic drug.
slang name for LSD
slang

Examples

He said he could get some superman for the party this weekend.
The police found several tabs of superman hidden in his backpack.
In the 1970s, superman was a common slang term for LSD among young people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: LSD - lysergic acid diethylamide, a hallucinogenic drug

2. NOUN

/ ˈsuːpərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəmæn / (BrE)
a person who is exceptionally powerful or capable, far beyond ordinary human ability
exceptionally powerful or capable person

Examples

He trained so hard that his teammates started calling him a superman on the field.
The novel tells the story of a superman who tries to save the world from disaster.
After a full day of work and caring for three children, she felt like a superman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hero - a person admired for courage or achievements
  • Antonym: mortal - a human being who is not a god or superhuman

Etymology

From German *Übermensch* (overman, superman), popularized by philosopher Friedrich Nietzsche.
