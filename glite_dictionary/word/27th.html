27th

1. ADJ

/ ˈtwɛn.ti ˈsɛv.ənθ / (AmE), / ˈtwɛn.ti ˈsɛv.ənθ / (BrE)
coming next after the twenty-sixth in position in a sequence; ordinal form of twenty-seven
ordinal number: twenty-seventh

Examples

She was born on the 27th of April.
This year marks the 27th anniversary of the foundation of the company.
Our meeting is scheduled for the 27th floor of the building.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 26th - coming next after the twenty-fifth in position
  • Degree Higher: 28th - coming next after the twenty-seventh in position
