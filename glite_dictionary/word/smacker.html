smacker

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmækər / (AmE), / ˈsmækə / (BrE)
a very powerful blow delivered with the fist
a hard punch with the fist
informal

Examples

He landed a smacker right on his opponent's jaw.
After taking a smacker to the face, he stumbled backwards.
She threatened to give him a smacker if he didn't apologize.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punch - a blow with the fist
  • Degree Lower: slap - a blow with the open hand

2. NOUN

/ ˈsmækər / (AmE), / ˈsmækə / (BrE)
a loud or noisy kiss
a loud kiss

Examples

She gave him a big smacker on the cheek before leaving.
He was surprised when his aunt planted a smacker right on his forehead.
The little boy ran to his mother and landed a loud smacker on her lips.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: peck - a light, quick kiss
