to snapchat

1. VERB

/ ˈsnæpˌtʃæt / (AmE), / ˈsnæpˌtʃæt / (BrE)
to have a conversation or exchange messages or images using the Snapchat application
chat or message via Snapchat app

Examples

We snapchatted all night after the concert, sending silly photos and messages to each other.
She prefers to snapchat her friends rather than call them on the phone.
He's always snapchatting during class, even though it's not allowed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: message - to send a written or electronic communication to someone

Etymology

From the name of the messaging application Snapchat, created 2011.
