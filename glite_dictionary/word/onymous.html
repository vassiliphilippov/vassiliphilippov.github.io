onymous

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑː.nə.məs / (AmE), / ˈɒn.ɪ.məs / (BrE)
Having the name of the author or creator made known; not anonymous.
bearing a name; not anonymous

Examples

The editor prefers that all submissions are onymous rather than anonymous.
The museum acquired several onymous paintings by contemporary artists.
Legal complaints must be filed in an onymous manner to be considered valid.

Synset Examples

articles in magazines are usually onymous

Related Terms

  • Antonym: anonymous - not bearing a name; of unknown authorship

Etymology

From Greek 'onyma' or 'onoma' meaning 'name'.
