Matabele

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɑːtəˈbiːli / (AmE), / ˌmatəˈbiːli / (BrE)
a Bantu language of Zimbabwe, closely related to Zulu and sometimes considered a dialect of Zulu
Bantu language related to Zulu

Examples

Linguists have noted that Matabele shares many characteristics with Zulu.
She is studying Matabele as part of her research on southern African languages.
Some historians document oral traditions in both Matabele and English.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Zulu - a Bantu language of South Africa, closely related to Matabele

Etymology

From the name of the Ndebele people (Matabele), adapted from Zulu 'amaNdebele'.
