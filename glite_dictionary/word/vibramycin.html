Vibramycin

1. NOUN

/ ˌvaɪbrəˈmaɪsɪn / (AmE), / ˌvɪbrəˈmaɪsɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked trade name for doxycycline, an antibiotic used to treat a variety of bacterial infections.
trademarked trade name for doxycycline
medical

Examples

Some people experience mild side effects when taking Vibramycin.
Vibramycin is effective against a wide range of bacterial infections.
