doucheness

1. NOUN

/ ˈduːʃ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈduːʃ.nɪs / (BrE)
Offensive or obnoxious behavior or attitude, especially that which is irritating or contemptible.
offensive or obnoxious behavior
informal slang

Examples

Her constant doucheness made it hard for anyone to work with her.
He was called out for his doucheness during the meeting by several colleagues.
People get tired of that kind of doucheness really quickly.

Synset Examples

I can’t stand his doucheness; it’s exhausting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: douchebag - an obnoxious or contemptible person (slang)

Etymology

From 'douche' (slang insult) + suffix '-ness' forming a noun denoting a quality.
