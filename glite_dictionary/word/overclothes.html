overclothes

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊvərˌkloʊðz / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌkləʊðz / (BrE)
clothes worn over ordinary garments, especially for outdoor use or protection against weather.
clothes for wearing outdoors

Examples

He hung his overclothes by the door before stepping into the warm house.
In winter, children need extra overclothes to stay warm at recess.
She brushed the snow from her overclothes after coming inside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outerwear - clothing worn outdoors, such as coats, jackets, and hats

Etymology

Formed from 'over-' (meaning above or outer) and 'clothes'.
