nutbrown

1. ADJ

/ ˈnʌtˌbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈnʌtˌbraʊn / (BrE)
Of a rich, warm brown color similar to that of nuts.
warm brown color like nuts

Examples

Her nutbrown hair gleamed in the sunlight as she walked through the garden.
The artist chose a nutbrown hue for the horse in his painting.
He admired the nutbrown warmth of the old wooden table.

Synset Examples

nutbrown hair

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: brown - a color produced by mixing red, yellow, and blue, similar to wood or earth

Etymology

Formed from 'nut' (the seed of some plants) and 'brown' (the color), denoting the shade of brown characteristic of certain nuts.
