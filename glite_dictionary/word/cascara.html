cascara

1. NOUN

/ kæsˈkɑːrə / (AmE), / kæˈskɑːrə / (BrE)
the dried bark of the cascara buckthorn, used as a laxative
dried bark used as a laxative
medical

Examples

The herbalist recommended cascara for its gentle laxative properties.
Many over-the-counter remedies contain cascara as an active ingredient.
Regular use of cascara should be monitored by a healthcare professional.

Etymology

From Spanish 'cáscara' meaning 'bark' or 'husk'.
