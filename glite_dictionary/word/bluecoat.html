bluecoat

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluˌkoʊt / (AmE), / ˈbluː.kəʊt / (BrE)
A person, especially a soldier or sailor, who wears a blue uniform.
person wearing a blue uniform, esp. soldier or sailor

Examples

During the parade, a group of bluecoats marched down the main street in perfect formation.
The young historian was fascinated by the uniforms worn by bluecoats during the 19th-century wars.
A fleet of bluecoat sailors prepared the ship for departure early in the morning.
