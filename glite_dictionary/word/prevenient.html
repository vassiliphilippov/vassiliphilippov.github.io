prevenient

1. ADJ

/ prɪˈviːniənt / (AmE), / prɪˈviːniənt / (BrE)
occurring or existing before or in anticipation of something; preceding
anticipating or preceding

Examples

His prevenient caution saved the team from a costly mistake.
Some theologians argue for the concept of prevenient grace in salvation.
A prevenient analysis can often reveal potential issues before they arise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preceding - coming before in time or order
  • Antonym: subsequent - coming after or later

Etymology

From Latin praevenient-, present participle of praevenire (to come before).
