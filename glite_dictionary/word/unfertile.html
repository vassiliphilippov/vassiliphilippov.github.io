unfertile

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfɝːtəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɜːtaɪl / (BrE)
Unable to produce offspring or incapable of reproduction.
incapable of reproduction

Examples

The couple discovered that the cause of their difficulty was that one partner was unfertile.
Some plants are naturally unfertile and do not produce seeds.
Scientists studied the unfertile mice to understand the genetic basis of infertility.

Synset Examples

an infertile couple

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infertile - unable to reproduce; sterile
  • Antonym: fertile - able to reproduce; capable of producing offspring

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'fertile' (able to reproduce), the latter from Latin 'fertilis' (fruitful).
