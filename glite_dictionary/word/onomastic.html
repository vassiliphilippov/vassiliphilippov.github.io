onomastic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑː.nəˈmæs.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒn.əˈmæs.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study of the origin, history, and use of proper names.
relating to the study of names

Examples

The professor gave an onomastic analysis of family surnames.
He wrote several onomastic articles about the history of place names.
Her onomastic interests led her to study the meanings of first names.

Synset Examples

he published a collection of his onomastic essays

Related Terms

  • Synonym: onomastics - the study of the origin and forms of proper names

Etymology

From Greek onomastikos (of or belonging to naming), from onoma (name).
