osteopath

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːstioʊˌpæθ / (AmE), / ˈɒstiəˌpæθ / (BrE)
A practitioner of osteopathy who treats medical conditions by manipulating bones and muscles.
practitioner of osteopathy

Examples

After injuring his back, he visited an osteopath for treatment.
My sister is training to become an osteopath at a medical college.
Many patients report relief after seeing an osteopath for muscle pain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chiropractor - a practitioner who treats disorders by manipulating the spine and other body parts

Etymology

From Greek 'osteon' (bone) + 'pathos' (suffering, disease).
