photographer

1. NOUN

/ fəˈtɑːɡrəfər / (AmE), / fəˈtɒɡrəfə / (BrE)
a person who takes photographs, especially as a profession
person who takes photographs professionally

Examples

The photographer captured the couple’s wedding day with a series of beautiful images.
She hired a professional photographer for her fashion magazine shoot.
As a wildlife photographer, he spends months in remote locations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cameraman - a person who operates a camera, especially in film or television
  • Antonym: amateur - a person who engages in a pursuit as a pastime rather than professionally

Etymology

From Greek 'phōtographos' (written with light), from 'phōs' (light) + 'graphos' (writing).
