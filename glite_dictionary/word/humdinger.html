humdinger

1. NOUN

/ hʌmˈdɪŋər / (AmE), / hʌmˈdɪŋə / (BrE)
A person or thing that is exceptionally impressive or excellent.
remarkably impressive person or thing
informal

Examples

That was a humdinger of a speech at the conference last night.
Everyone agreed that the new recruit is a real humdinger when it comes to solving problems.
The team played a humdinger of a match and won easily.

Synset Examples

a humdinger of a secretary

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crackerjack - a person or thing of exceptional quality or ability
  • Antonym: dud - something that fails or is of poor quality
