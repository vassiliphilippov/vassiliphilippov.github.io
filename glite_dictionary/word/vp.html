V.P.

1. NOUN

/ viː piː / (AmE), / viː piː / (BrE)
An abbreviation for Vice President, an executive officer ranking immediately below a president.
Abbreviation for Vice President

Examples

The company promoted her to V.P. of sales after just two years.
As V.P., he was responsible for key strategic decisions within the organization.
If the president is unavailable, the V.P. will chair the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Vice President - An executive officer immediately below a president
