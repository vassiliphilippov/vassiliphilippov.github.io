immensity

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmɛnsəti / (AmE), / ɪˈmɛnsəti / (BrE)
the extremely large size, extent, or degree of something
vastness; extreme largeness

Examples

The immensity of the mountain range took their breath away.
She struggled to grasp the immensity of the universe.
The project will require resources of great immensity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vastness - great extent or size
  • Synonym: enormity - extremely large size or scale

Etymology

From Latin 'immensitas', from 'immensus' (immense, vast).
