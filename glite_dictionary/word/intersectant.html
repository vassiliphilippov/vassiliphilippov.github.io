intersectant

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.tərˈsɛk.tənt / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈsɛk.tənt / (BrE)
crossed or intersected so as to form an X or a cross
crossed to form an X

Examples

The two beams were positioned in an intersectant pattern above the stage.
Scientists observed intersectant lines appearing under the microscope.
The architect designed the roof using intersectant supports for added stability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crossed - placed or lying across each other

Etymology

From Latin intersectus (intersected) + English -ant.
