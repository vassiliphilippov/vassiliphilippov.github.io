Silvia

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪl.vi.ə / (AmE), / ˈsɪl.vi.ə / (BrE)
the type genus of the Sylviidae family, comprising various species of warblers
genus of warblers (birds)

Examples

The genus Silvia includes several small insectivorous birds known as warblers.
Some ornithologists specialize in the study of Silvia and its related genera.
The habitat preferences of Silvia species are diverse, ranging from dense forests to open woodlands.

Etymology

From Latin 'silvia', meaning 'of the forest' or 'forest-dweller'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɪl.vi.ə / (AmE), / ˈsɪl.vi.ə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Sylviidae, comprising various species of warblers.
type genus of the Sylviidae (warblers)

Examples

The ornithologist described several species within the genus Silvia.
Many warblers formerly classified under Silvia have been reclassified as new research emerges.
Silvia plays an important role in the study of Old World warblers.

Etymology

From Latin *Silvia*, feminine form of *Silvius*, a Roman name.
