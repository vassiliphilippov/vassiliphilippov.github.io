comprehendible

1. ADJ

/ kəmˈprɛhəndəbəl / (AmE), / kəmˈprɛhəndɪbəl / (BrE)
Able to be understood; capable of being comprehended.
able to be understood

Examples

The instructions should be comprehendible to people with no technical background.
She spoke in clear, comprehendible language that everyone could follow.
The report is written in a style that makes complex data easily comprehendible.

Synset Examples

an idea comprehensible to the average mind

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comprehensible - able to be understood
  • Antonym: incomprehensible - impossible or extremely difficult to understand

Etymology

From Latin 'comprehendere' (to understand, grasp) + English '-ible'.
