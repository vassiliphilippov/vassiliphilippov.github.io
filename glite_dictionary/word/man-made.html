man-made

1. ADJ

/ ˈmæn ˌmeɪd / (AmE), / ˈmæn ˌmeɪd / (BrE)
Made or produced by people rather than occurring naturally; artificial.
artificial; produced by humans

Examples

Most man-made lakes are created by damming rivers.
The chair is upholstered with man-made materials instead of leather.
Scientists are studying the effects of man-made chemicals on the environment.

Synset Examples

man-made fibers
synthetic leather

Related Terms

  • Antonym: natural - existing or caused by nature; not made or caused by humankind
  • Synonym: artificial - made or produced by human beings rather than occurring naturally

Etymology

Formed from 'man' (meaning human) and 'made', indicating produced by people.
