niqaabi

1. NOUN

/ niːˈkɑːbi / (AmE), / niːˈkɑːbi / (BrE)
An observant Muslim woman who covers her face and hands in public or in the presence of non-family men, typically by wearing a niqab.
Muslim woman who wears a niqab

Examples

The niqaabi felt comfortable expressing her identity despite being in a non-Muslim majority country.
At the mosque, several niqaabi women gathered for the celebration.
During her travels, the niqaabi respected local customs while maintaining her religious practices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: niqab - A veil worn by some Muslim women that covers the face except for the eyes

Etymology

From Arabic نِقَابِيّ‎ (niqābiyy), derived from 'niqab', the facial veil.
