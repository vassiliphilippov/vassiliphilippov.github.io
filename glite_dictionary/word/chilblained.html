chilblained

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɪlˌbleɪnd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪlˌbleɪnd / (BrE)
(of a part of the body) affected by or having chilblains (painful, itchy swellings on the skin caused by cold).
affected by chilblains

Examples

She rubbed her chilblained fingers together, trying to warm them by the fire.
His chilblained toes made walking painful during the harsh winter.
The nurse applied ointment to the child's chilblained hands.

Synset Examples

her poor chilblained hands

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chilblain - a painful swelling of the skin caused by exposure to cold

Etymology

From 'chilblain' + '-ed', the regular participial adjective formation.
