queenly

1. ADJ

/ ˈkwiːnli / (AmE), / ˈkwiːnli / (BrE)
having the rank, qualities, or appearance of a queen; regal or dignified
having qualities or appearance of a queen

Examples

She walked into the room with a queenly grace that caught everyone's attention.
The actress wore a queenly gown at the award ceremony.
His mother always handled family matters in a wise and queenly manner.

Synset Examples

queenly propriety
clad in her queenly raiment
Rosetti's queenly portraits of women

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regal - resembling or fit for a monarch, especially a king or queen
  • Synonym: kingly - having the rank or qualities of a king

Etymology

From 'queen' + '-ly', meaning like or characteristic of a queen.
