unstirred

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈstɝd / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɜːd / (BrE)
(of a liquid or mixture) not agitated by stirring; left without being mixed.
not mixed by stirring

Examples

The sauce remained unstirred on the stove, developing a thick skin on top.
After adding the powder, he left the mixture unstirred for several minutes.
Her instructions said to let the batter sit unstirred before pouring it into the pan.

Synset Examples

the ingredients sat in the bowl unstirred while she buttered the pan

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stirred - mixed or agitated by stirring
