jird

1. NOUN

/ dʒɜrd / (AmE), / dʒɜːd / (BrE)
A species of gerbil found in northern Africa, characterized by its burrowing habits and long hind legs.
African gerbil species

Examples

The Sahara jird has adapted well to life in arid desert conditions.
A colony of jirds was observed digging complex burrows near the oasis.
Unlike the common gerbil, the jird prefers open, sandy plains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gerbil - small burrowing rodent often kept as a pet
