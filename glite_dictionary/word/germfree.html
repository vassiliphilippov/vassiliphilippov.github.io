germfree

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʒɜrmˈfri / (AmE), / ˌdʒɜːmˈfriː / (BrE)
Free from germs or pathogenic organisms; completely sterile.
free from germs; sterile

Examples

All surgical tools must be germfree before any operation.
Scientists conducted experiments in a germfree laboratory to avoid contamination.
Raising germfree animals helps researchers study the effects of microbes on health.

Synset Examples

a germfree environment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sterile - free from living microorganisms; completely clean
