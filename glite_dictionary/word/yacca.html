yacca

1. NOUN

/ ˈjækə / (AmE), / ˈjakə / (BrE)
A West Indian evergreen tree or shrub, characterized by medium to long leaves.
West Indian evergreen plant

Examples

The yacca thrives in the dry, rocky hills of Jamaica.
Botanists studied the unique leaves of the yacca found throughout the West Indies.
Locals use parts of the yacca for traditional remedies and crafts.
