Lincolnesque

1. ADJ

/ ˌlɪŋ.kənˈɛsk / (AmE), / ˌlɪŋ.kəlˈɛsk / (BrE)
Of or relating to Abraham Lincoln or characteristic of his style, principles, or manner.
in the style or manner of Abraham Lincoln

Examples

His Lincolnesque honesty and humility inspired everyone on the team.
The candidate's speech had a Lincolnesque tone, focusing on unity and moral values.
The commemorative statue captured a Lincolnesque dignity that moved many visitors.

Etymology

Formed from Lincoln (Abraham Lincoln) + -esque (French, meaning 'in the style of').
