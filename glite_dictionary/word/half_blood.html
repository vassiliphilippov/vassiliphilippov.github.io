half blood

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæf ˌblʌd / (AmE), / ˈhɑːf ˌblʌd / (BrE)
a sibling who shares only one parent with another sibling
sibling sharing one parent

Examples

His half blood visited him every summer after their parents divorced.
As half bloods, they inherited different traditions from their separate families.
She grew up with her half blood but always felt a strong connection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: half-brother - a brother with whom one shares only one parent
  • Synonym: half-sister - a sister with whom one shares only one parent
