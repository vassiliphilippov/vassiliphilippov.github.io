to dulcify

1. VERB

/ ˈdʌlsɪfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈdʌlsɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to make sweeter in taste; to sweeten
make sweeter in taste

Examples

The chef used honey to dulcify the tart flavor of the sauce.
Adding some sugar will dulcify your morning coffee if you find it too bitter.
They dulcified the lemonade with a generous splash of maple syrup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sweeten - make or become sweet or sweeter

Etymology

From Latin dulcis (sweet) + -fy (make).
