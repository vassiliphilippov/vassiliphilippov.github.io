unequalized

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪˈkwəlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈkwəlaɪzd / (BrE)
not made equal; lacking adjustment to achieve equality or balance.
not made equal or balanced

Examples

The team lost due to unequalized resources among the players.
The pressure remained unequalized after the valve failed to open.
He noticed an unequalized distribution of tasks in the project.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: equalized - made equal or balanced; adjusted to be the same
