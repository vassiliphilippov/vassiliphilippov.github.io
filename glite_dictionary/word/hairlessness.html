hairlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛrləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈheələsnɪs / (BrE)
The condition of being without hair.
state of being without hair

Examples

The doctor explained that hairlessness can result from genetics or medical treatments.
Certain breeds of cats are famous for their hairlessness and smooth skin.
He accepted his hairlessness with confidence and a sense of humor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baldness - the condition of having no hair on the scalp

2. NOUN

/ ˈhɛr·ləs·nəs / (AmE), / ˈheə·ləs·nəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of having no hair
absence of hair

Examples

Some people embrace their hairlessness and choose not to wear wigs or hats.
The hairlessness of certain dog breeds can reduce allergic reactions in owners.
He was self-conscious about his hairlessness after undergoing chemotherapy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baldness - the condition of having no hair on the scalp

Etymology

Formed from 'hairless' (from Old English 'hǣr') + '-ness', a suffix indicating state or quality.
