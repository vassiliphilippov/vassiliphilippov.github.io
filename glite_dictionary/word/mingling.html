mingling

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of mixing socially with others, especially by moving around and talking with different people
the act of mixing socially with others

Examples

She spent the evening mingling with guests at the reception.
After some mingling, the participants sat down for dinner.
He found the constant mingling at networking events exhausting.

Synset Examples

all the random mingling and idle talk made him hate literary parties

Related Terms

  • Synonym: socializing - the activity of mixing socially with others

Etymology

From the verb 'mingle,' which comes from Middle English 'menglen,' a frequentative of 'mengen' (to mix), from Old English.
