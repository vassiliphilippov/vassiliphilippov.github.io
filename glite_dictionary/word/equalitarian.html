equalitarian

1. NOUN

/ ˌiː.kwəˌlɛˈtɛr.i.ən / (AmE), / ˌiː.kwə.lɪˈteə.ri.ən / (BrE)
a person who believes in the equality of all people, especially in social, political, or economic life
person who believes in equality for all

Examples

As an equalitarian, she advocated for equal rights regardless of gender or background.
The organization was founded by a group of passionate equalitarians.
He was known as an equalitarian during the early civil rights movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: egalitarian - a person who believes in the equality of all people

Etymology

From equal + -itarian (patterned after humanitarian or utilitarian).
