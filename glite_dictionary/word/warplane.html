warplane

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔrˌpleɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːˌpleɪn / (BrE)
an aircraft designed and equipped for use in warfare
military combat aircraft

Examples

The museum displays a restored warplane from World War II.
Pilots were trained to operate the new warplane in combat situations.
Enemy radar detected an approaching warplane over the border.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fighter - a fast warplane designed to destroy enemy aircraft
  • Synonym: bomber - a warplane designed to carry and drop bombs
  • Antonym: airliner - a large passenger aircraft (civilian, not for combat)

Etymology

From 'war' + 'plane', a shortening of 'airplane'.
