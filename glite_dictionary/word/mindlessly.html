mindlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈmaɪndlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈmaɪndləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no thought, attention, or intelligence.
without thought or attention

Examples

He mindlessly scrolled through social media, barely noticing what he was seeing.
Workers sometimes perform repetitive tasks mindlessly when they become too routine.
She mindlessly repeated the instructions without understanding their purpose.

Synset Examples

all day long, he mindlessly filled out forms

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtlessly - without consideration or attention
  • Synonym: carelessly - without care or attention
  • Antonym: attentively - with attention or concentration
