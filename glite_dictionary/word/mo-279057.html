Mo.

1. NOUN

/ moʊ / (AmE), / məʊ / (BrE)
Abbreviation for Missouri, a state in the central United States.
abbreviation for Missouri (U.S. state)
american

Examples

The package was sent from St. Louis, Mo., last week.
She grew up in Kansas City, Mo., before moving east.
The conference will be held in Columbia, Mo., this summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Missouri - A state in the central United States

Etymology

Abbreviation of Missouri, named from a Siouan language meaning 'town of the large canoes'.
