shrublet

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃrʌb.lət / (AmE), / ˈʃrʌb.lɪt / (BrE)
a small or dwarf shrub, typically a woody plant smaller than a typical shrub
a small or dwarf shrub

Examples

The delicate shrublet grew close to the rocky hillside, barely reaching a foot in height.
Many alpine gardens feature at least one type of hardy shrublet among the larger plants.
Botanists discovered a rare shrublet species thriving in the arid valley.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: shrub - a woody plant smaller than a tree, usually with several stems
  • Synonym: bush - a low dense woody plant, typically smaller than a tree

Etymology

Diminutive of 'shrub'.
