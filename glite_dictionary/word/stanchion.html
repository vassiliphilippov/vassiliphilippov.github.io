stanchion

1. NOUN

/ ˈstæn.tʃən / (AmE), / ˈstan.tʃən / (BrE)
a vertical post or rod used as a support, especially in buildings, fences, or railings
vertical support post or rod

Examples

A metal stanchion holds up the handrail along the staircase.
The workers installed new stanchions to reinforce the safety barrier.
Each cow was tied to a stanchion in the barn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: post - an upright support in a structure
  • Synonym: column - an upright pillar supporting a structure

Etymology

From Old French 'estanchon' (post, prop), related to 'estanchier' (to prop up).
