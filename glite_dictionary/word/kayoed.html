kayoed

1. ADJ

/ ˈkeɪˌoʊd / (AmE), / ˈkeɪˌəʊd / (BrE)
(slang) knocked unconscious by a heavy blow, especially in boxing or a fight.
knocked out by a blow (slang)
slang

Examples

He was kayoed by a single punch in the third round.
The player lay kayoed on the field after the collision.
She felt as if she'd been kayoed by the shocking news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knocked out - rendered unconscious by a blow

Etymology

Variant of 'K.O.', abbreviation for 'knock out'.
