crinkly

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɪŋkli / (AmE), / ˈkrɪŋkli / (BrE)
having many small wrinkles, waves, or twists on the surface; uneven or wrinkled in appearance.
having small wrinkles or waves

Examples

Her crinkly hair caught the sunlight as she walked through the garden.
The package was wrapped in crinkly silver paper that made a soft sound when touched.
He smiled, showing his crinkly eyes and friendly dimples.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrinkled - having wrinkles; creased or folded surface

Etymology

From 'crinkle' + '-y', formed in English.
