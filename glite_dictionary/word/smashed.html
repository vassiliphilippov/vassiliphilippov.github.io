smashed

1. ADJ

/ smæʃt / (AmE), / smæʃt / (BrE)
Extremely intoxicated by alcohol; very drunk.
very drunk (informal)
informal slang

Examples

He was completely smashed after the party last night.
By midnight, most of the guests were already smashed.
Don't drive if you're smashed; call a taxi instead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drunk - intoxicated by alcohol
  • Antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol; not drunk
