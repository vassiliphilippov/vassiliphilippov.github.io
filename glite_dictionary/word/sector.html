sector

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛktɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɛktə / (BrE)
a plane figure formed by two radii of a circle and the arc between them
plane figure between two radii and an arc

Examples

In geometry class, we learned how to calculate the area of a sector of a circle.
The cake was cut into several sectors for each guest.
A quarter-circle is an example of a sector with a 90-degree angle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: segment - part of a circle bounded by a chord and the arc it subtends

Etymology

From Latin 'sector', from 'secare' (to cut).

/ ˈsɛktɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɛktə / (BrE)

Examples

Formatting the disk creates a new sector structure for data storage.
