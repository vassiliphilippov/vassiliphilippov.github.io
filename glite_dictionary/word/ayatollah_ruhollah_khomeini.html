Ayatollah Ruhollah Khomeini

1. PROPN

/ aɪ.jəˌtoʊ.lə ruːˈhoʊ.lɑː xoʊˈmeɪ.ni / (AmE), / aɪ.jəˌtɒl.ə ruːˈhɒl.ə xəʊˈmeɪ.ni / (BrE)
(1900–1989) Iranian religious leader and political figure who led the 1979 Iranian Revolution, becoming the country's Supreme Leader and establishing an Islamic republic.
Iranian religious leader and revolutionary

Examples

Ayatollah Ruhollah Khomeini returned from exile to lead Iran after the 1979 revolution.
Many consider Ayatollah Ruhollah Khomeini one of the most influential leaders in recent Middle Eastern history.
The policies introduced by Ayatollah Ruhollah Khomeini shaped the direction of the Islamic Republic.
