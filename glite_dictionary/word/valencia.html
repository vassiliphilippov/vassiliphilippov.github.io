Valencia

1. PROPN

/ vəˈlɛnsiə / (AmE), / vəˈlɛnsɪə / (BrE)
a city and port in eastern Spain, situated on the Mediterranean coast and known for its history, architecture, and culture
city in eastern Spain

Examples

Thousands of tourists visit Valencia every year to enjoy its beaches and festivals.
The climate in Valencia is mild, with warm summers and mild winters.
Valencia is famous for its delicious paella and stunning City of Arts and Sciences.

Synset Examples

Valencia is the third largest city in Spain

Etymology

From Latin Valentia, meaning 'strength' or 'valor', the ancient Roman name for the city.
