fizzing

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
making a hissing or bubbling sound as a result of producing gas, especially in liquids.
making a hissing or bubbling sound

Examples

The soda was fizzing as soon as I opened the bottle.
She listened to the fizzing of the bath bomb dissolving in the tub.
A fizzing sound came from the laboratory flask during the experiment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hissing - making a sharp sibilant sound like that of an 's'
  • Synonym: bubbling - producing bubbles or a boiling sound

Etymology

from the verb "fizz", likely imitative of the sound, plus the present participle suffix -ing.
