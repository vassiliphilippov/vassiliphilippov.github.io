R.V.

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑr ˈvi / (AmE), / ˌɑː ˈviː / (BrE)
a large motor vehicle equipped with living space and amenities, used for camping or recreational travel
motor vehicle for camping/travel
american

Examples

They spent the summer traveling across the country in their R.V..
The campground has special parking spaces for large R.V.s.
We rented an R.V. for our road trip through the national parks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camper - a motor vehicle equipped for living in, used for camping
  • Synonym: caravan - a vehicle equipped for living in, typically towed by a car, used mostly in British English

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'recreational vehicle', first used in the US in the mid-20th century.
