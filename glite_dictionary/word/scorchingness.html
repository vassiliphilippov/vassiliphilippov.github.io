scorchingness

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɔrtʃɪŋ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈskɔːtʃɪŋ.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being extremely hot; intense heat.
extreme heat; intense hotness

Examples

The scorchingness of the desert at noon made it almost impossible to travel.
They sought shelter from the scorchingness of the afternoon sun under a large tree.
No one could ignore the scorchingness that blanketed the city during the record-breaking heatwave.

Synset Examples

Today was our first over 100 degree day for the season of scorchingness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heat - high temperature; the quality of being hot
  • Antonym: coolness - the quality of being cool; absence of heat

Etymology

From 'scorching' + '-ness', forming a noun denoting the state or quality of being scorching.
