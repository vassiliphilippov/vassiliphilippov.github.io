Gujerat

1. PROPN

/ ɡuˈdʒɛrɑt / (AmE), / ɡuːˈdʒɑːrət / (BrE)
a state in western India, recognized for its industry and coastal cities; officially spelled 'Gujarat'.
industrialized state in western India

Examples

Many textile factories are based in Gujerat, making it an important center for trade.
Tourists often visit Gujerat to see its historic temples and vibrant culture.
After moving to Gujerat, she quickly adapted to the dynamic urban lifestyle.

Etymology

From Gujarati, ultimately from Sanskrit 'Gurjarā'.
