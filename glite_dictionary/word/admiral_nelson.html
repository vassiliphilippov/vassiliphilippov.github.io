Admiral Nelson

1. PROPN

/ ˈæd.mɚ.əl ˈnɛl.sən / (AmE), / ˈæd.mər.əl ˈnɛl.sən / (BrE)
(1758–1805) English admiral who defeated the French fleets of Napoleon and was mortally wounded at the Battle of Trafalgar.
famous English admiral, defeated French at Trafalgar

Examples

Admiral Nelson is celebrated as one of Britain's greatest naval heroes.
The statue of Admiral Nelson in Trafalgar Square honors his victory and sacrifice.
Many books have been written about the life and battles of Admiral Nelson.
