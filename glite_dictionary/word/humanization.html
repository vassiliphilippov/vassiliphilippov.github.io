humanization

1. NOUN

/ hjuːmənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / hjuːmənʌɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the process of making something more human or giving it human qualities
process of making more human

Examples

The humanization of technology has made devices more intuitive and relatable.
Her humanization of the villain allowed readers to understand his motives.
The film focuses on the humanization of artificial intelligence.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dehumanization - the process of depriving of human qualities

Etymology

From human + -ization, modeled after French humanisation.
