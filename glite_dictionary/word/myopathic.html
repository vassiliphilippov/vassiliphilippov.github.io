myopathic

1. ADJ

/ ˌmaɪ.oʊˈpæθ.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.əʊˈpæθ.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by any disease of the muscles not caused by nerve dysfunction.
related to muscle disease (not caused by nerves)
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with a myopathic disorder after extensive muscle testing.
A myopathic condition can lead to muscle weakness and wasting.
Her symptoms were consistent with a myopathic process rather than a problem with the nerves.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: neuropathic - relating to nerve disease
  • Synonym: muscular - relating to muscles

Etymology

From Greek 'mys' (muscle) and 'pathos' (suffering, disease), via medical Latin.
