preadolescent

1. ADJ

/ ˌpriːˌædəˈlɛsənt / (AmE), / ˌpriːˌadəˈlɛsənt / (BrE)
relating to or typical of children between the ages of 9 and 12, especially before the onset of puberty.
relating to children aged 9–12

Examples

The research focused on the emotional development of preadolescent children.
She teaches a class designed specifically for preadolescent students.
Some preadolescent boys may begin to show early signs of puberty.

Synset Examples

a preteen party
preteen clothing

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: adolescent - relating to teenagers (between childhood and adulthood)

Etymology

From Latin 'prae-' (before) + 'adolescent' (growing up).
