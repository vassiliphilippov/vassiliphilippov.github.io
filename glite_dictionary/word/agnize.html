to agnize

1. VERB

/ æɡˈnaɪz / (AmE), / æɡˈnaɪz / (BrE)
to recognize or acknowledge; to be fully aware or cognizant of (now rare or archaic)
recognize or acknowledge (archaic)
archaic

Examples

He did not agnize his friend at first, so changed was his appearance.
The judge must agnize both the guilt and the remorse of the accused.
She slowly agnized the truth of her situation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to recognize - to identify as already known or to acknowledge
  • Synonym: to acknowledge - to accept or admit the existence of something

Etymology

From Latin 'agnoscere' (to recognize).
