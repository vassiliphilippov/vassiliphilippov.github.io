whatever

1. PRON

/ wʌˈtɛvɚ / (AmE), / wɒˈtɛvə / (BrE)
used to refer to anything or everything of a particular kind, no matter which; any, regardless of which one.
any, no matter which (pronoun/determiner)

Examples

You can choose whatever book you like from the shelf.
Feel free to eat whatever is left in the fridge.
Take whatever action you think is necessary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: any - used to refer to one or some of a thing or number of things, no matter how much or how many

Etymology

From Middle English 'what ever', merging 'what' and 'ever'.
