to retick

1. VERB

/ riˈtɪk / (AmE), / riˈtɪk / (BrE)
to sew a new covering onto (a mattress or similar object)
sew a new cover onto a mattress

Examples

We decided to retick our old mattress instead of buying a new one.
The upholsterer will retick the cushions so they look fresh again.
After years of use, the mattress finally needed to be reticked for comfort and cleanliness.

Synset Examples

tick a mattress

Etymology

From the prefix re- (again) + tick (cloth covering of a mattress).
