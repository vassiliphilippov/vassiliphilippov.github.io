bedless

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛdləs / (AmE), / ˈbɛdlɪs / (BrE)
without a bed; lacking a bed for sleeping or resting.
without a bed

Examples

After moving into the new apartment, we spent a bedless night on the floor.
The shelter was overcrowded, leaving some residents bedless for the night.
Prisoners complained about the bedless conditions in their cells.

Synset Examples

the cell was bedless

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bed - a piece of furniture for sleeping on

Etymology

From 'bed' + suffix '-less', meaning 'without'.
