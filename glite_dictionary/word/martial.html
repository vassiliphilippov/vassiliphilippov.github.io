martial

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɑrʃəl / (AmE), / ˈmɑːʃəl / (BrE)
relating to soldiers, war, or the armed forces
of or relating to the armed forces

Examples

The government declared martial law during the crisis to restore order.
The parade featured several martial bands playing triumphant music.
His interests include martial history and military tactics.

Synset Examples

martial law

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military - relating to or characteristic of soldiers or armed forces

Etymology

From Latin 'martialis', meaning 'of Mars (the god of war)'.

Her speech was filled with martial rhetoric, calling for strength and discipline.
Ancient Roman society was heavily influenced by martial traditions and values.
