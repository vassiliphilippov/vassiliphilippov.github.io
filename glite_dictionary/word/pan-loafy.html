pan-loafy

1. ADJ

/ pæn ˈloʊ.fi / (AmE), / pæn ˈləʊ.fi / (BrE)
having a texture similar to that of bread baked in a pan, typically soft and even
having texture like pan-baked bread

Examples

This cake is wonderfully pan-loafy, with a soft and uniform crumb.
I prefer my sandwiches made with pan-loafy bread, rather than rustic loaves.
The new bakery is famous for its pan-loafy texture in all its loaves.

Synset Examples

This bread has a delightful, pan-loafy texture.
The cake turned out beautifully, all pan-loafy and soft.
I love how this sandwich feels so pan-loafy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pan loaf - bread baked in a loaf pan, yielding a soft, even texture

Etymology

From 'pan loaf' (bread baked in a pan) + '-y' forming an adjective.
