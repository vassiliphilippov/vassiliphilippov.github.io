litas

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːtɑs / (AmE), / ˈliːtæs / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Lithuania before the adoption of the euro in 2015.
former currency of Lithuania

Examples

Before Lithuania adopted the euro, prices were listed in litas.
She exchanged her euros for litas at the Vilnius airport.
Many souvenirs from Lithuania still show prices in litas from previous years.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: euro - the official currency used in Lithuania since 2015

Etymology

From Lithuanian 'litas', derived from 'Lietuva' (Lithuania).
