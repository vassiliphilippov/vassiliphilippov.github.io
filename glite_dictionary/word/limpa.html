limpa

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪmpə / (AmE), / ˈlɪmpə / (BrE)
A type of rye bread from Scandinavia, typically sweetened with molasses or brown sugar.
sweet Scandinavian rye bread

Examples

The bakery sells a delicious limpa flavored with orange peel and anise.
She learned to bake limpa from her Swedish grandmother's recipe.
Freshly baked limpa pairs well with strong cheeses and smoked fish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rye bread - bread made with rye flour

Etymology

From Swedish 'limpa' meaning 'loaf'.
