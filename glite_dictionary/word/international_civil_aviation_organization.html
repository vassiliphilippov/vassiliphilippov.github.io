International Civil Aviation Organization

1. PROPN

/ ˌɪntəɹˈnæʃənəl ˌsɪvəl ˌeɪviˈeɪʃən ˌɔɹgənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəl ˌsɪvəl ˌeɪvɪˈeɪʃən ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
a specialized agency of the United Nations responsible for establishing global standards and regulations for civil aviation.
UN agency regulating civil aviation

Examples

The International Civil Aviation Organization sets international standards for air traffic safety.
Canada is the headquarters of the International Civil Aviation Organization.
The International Civil Aviation Organization was created to promote safe and orderly development of international civil aviation.
