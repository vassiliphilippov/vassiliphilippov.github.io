unbeatable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbiːdəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈbiːtəbl̩ / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to defeat or surpass
impossible or very difficult to defeat

Examples

After years of practice, their partnership became unbeatable on the dance floor.
The team has an unbeatable strategy for winning the championship.
Her confidence made her feel unbeatable in any challenge she faced.

Synset Examples

an unbeatable ball team

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invincible - too powerful to be defeated or overcome
  • Antonym: defeatable - able to be defeated

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'beatable' (able to be beaten).
