seldom

1. ADV

/ ˈsɛl.dəm / (AmE), / ˈsɛl.dəm / (BrE)
Not often; rarely.
not often; rarely

Examples

He seldom goes out during the week because he's busy with work.
Such opportunities seldom arise, so you should seize them when they do.
We seldom see deer in this area these days.

Synset Examples

we rarely met

Related Terms

  • Antonym: often - frequently; many times
  • Synonym: rarely - not often; infrequently

Etymology

From Old English seldum (rarely), related to Old Norse sjaldan.
