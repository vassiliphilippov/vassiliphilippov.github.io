atelier

1. NOUN

/ ˌæt̬.əlˈjɛr / (AmE), / ˌat.əlˈjeə / (BrE)
A studio or workshop, especially one used by an artist or designer.
artist's or designer's studio

Examples

The painter invited us into his atelier to view his latest work.
Each atelier in the district has its own unique character.
She spent the summer studying sculpture at a renowned atelier in Paris.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: studio - a room or place where an artist, photographer, or designer works

Etymology

From French atelier (workshop, studio), from Old French astelier (carpenter's shop).
