fine-toothed

1. ADJ

/ ˈfaɪnˌtuθt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnˌtuːθt / (BrE)
having very thin and closely spaced teeth, especially on a comb or similar tool
having fine, closely spaced teeth

Examples

She used a fine-toothed comb to remove the lint from her sweater.
Inspect the documents with a fine-toothed approach to catch every error.
The detective examined the crime scene with fine-toothed precision.

Synset Examples

a fine-toothed comb

Related Terms

  • Antonym: coarse-toothed - having relatively large and widely spaced teeth
