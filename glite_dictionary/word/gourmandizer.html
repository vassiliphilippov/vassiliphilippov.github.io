gourmandizer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʊrˌmɑːndaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈɡʊə.mənd.aɪ.zə / (BrE)
A person who eats and drinks to excess; someone with a great appetite for food and drink.
person who eats and drinks to excess

Examples

Everyone knew Henry was a true gourmandizer, never missing a chance for a lavish meal.
The critic dismissed the chef as simply a gourmandizer with no real sense of cuisine.
After the banquet, he was accused of being a gourmandizer by those who saw his overflowing plate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glutton - a person who eats to excess
  • Antonym: epicure - a person with refined taste in food and wine (without excess)

Etymology

From 'gourmand' (French, meaning glutton or one fond of eating) plus English '-izer'.
