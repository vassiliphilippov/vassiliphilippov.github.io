Culiacán

1. PROPN

/ kuːliɑːˈkɑːn / (AmE), / kuːliːɑːˈkɑːn / (BrE)
A city in northwestern Mexico, capital of the state of Sinaloa.
city in northwestern Mexico

Examples

Tourists often visit Culiacán for its vibrant culture and cuisine.
The university in Culiacán is known for its research programs.
Heavy rains caused flooding in several neighborhoods of Culiacán last summer.

Etymology

From Nahuatl *Colhuacán*, the name of a pre-Hispanic village in the region.
