hyperopic

1. ADJ

/ haɪpɚˈɑpɪk / (AmE), / haɪpəˈrɒpɪk / (BrE)
Able to see distant objects more clearly than near ones; relating to or affected by hyperopia (farsightedness).
able to see distant objects clearly (farsighted)
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that being hyperopic means she can see distant objects more clearly than those up close.
Children who are hyperopic may have trouble reading small print without corrective lenses.
He was diagnosed as hyperopic during his routine eye examination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farsighted - able to see distant objects clearly; synonym for 'hyperopic'
  • Antonym: myopic - able to see nearby objects clearly but not distant ones; nearsighted

Etymology

From Greek 'hyperōps' (farsighted), from 'hyper-' (over) + 'ōps' (eye).
