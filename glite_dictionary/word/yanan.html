Yanan

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɑːnɑːn / (AmE), / ˈjɑːnən / (BrE)
a language group of the Hokan family formerly spoken by the Yana people of northern California
language group of the Hokan family

Examples

Linguists have studied the extinct Yanan languages to learn more about the Hokan family.
The Yanan language group was spoken by the indigenous Yana people of California.
Only a few written records of Yanan exist due to the early decline of its speakers.

Etymology

From the name of the Yana people, Indigenous to northern California.
