septillion

1. NOUN

/ sɛpˈtɪljən / (AmE), / sɛpˈtɪljən / (BrE)
The number represented by a one followed by twenty-four zeros (1,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000) in the United States.
the number 1 followed by 24 zeros (US)

Examples

Scientists estimate there are about a septillion stars in the observable universe.
A septillion is written as the digit one followed by twenty-four zeros in American English.
The national debt is nowhere near a septillion dollars, but the word puts things in perspective.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: quadrillion - the number 1 followed by 15 zeros (US)
  • Degree Higher: octillion - the number 1 followed by 27 zeros (US)

Etymology

From French 'septillion', from Latin 'septem' (seven) + '-illion', modeled on 'million'.
