Malabo

1. PROPN

/ məˈlɑːboʊ / (AmE), / məˈlɑːbəʊ / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Equatorial Guinea, located on the island of Bioko in the Gulf of Guinea
capital of Equatorial Guinea

Examples

She moved to Malabo to start her new job at the embassy.
The climate in Malabo is tropical, with frequent rainfall throughout the year.
Malabo serves as a cultural and economic hub for Equatorial Guinea.
