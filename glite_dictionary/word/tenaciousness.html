tenaciousness

1. NOUN

/ təˈneɪʃəsnəs / (AmE), / tɪˈneɪʃəsnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being persistent, determined, and unwilling to give up.
persistent determination

Examples

Her tenaciousness helped her overcome every obstacle she encountered in her career.
Without tenaciousness, it's hard to achieve long-term goals in challenging situations.
He admired her tenaciousness in pursuing just and difficult causes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tenacity - the quality of being very determined
  • Synonym: determination - the quality of being firm in purpose

Etymology

From tenacious + -ness, ultimately from Latin 'tenax' (holding fast).
