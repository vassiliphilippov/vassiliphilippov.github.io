carelessly

1. ADV

/ ˈkɛr.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈkeə.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of care, attention, or concern.
without taking care or attention

Examples

She carelessly left her keys on the bus and couldn't get into her house.
The document was carelessly edited, resulting in several embarrassing mistakes.
He carelessly tossed his jacket on the chair without thinking.

Synset Examples

one unfortunately sees historic features carelessly lost when estates fall into unsympathetic hands

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recklessly - without regard to danger or consequences
  • Antonym: carefully - with attention and caution

Etymology

From 'careless' + '-ly'; 'careless' from Middle English, influenced by Old English 'caru' (care).

3. ADV

/ ˈkɛr.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈkeə.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of care, attention, or concern.
without care or attention

Examples

She carelessly left her phone on the bus and never got it back.
The artist carelessly splashed paint across the canvas, creating a chaotic effect.
He carelessly tossed the documents onto his desk without reading them.

Synset Examples

carelessly raised the children's hopes without thinking of their possible disappointment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recklessly - without regard to danger or consequences
  • Antonym: carefully - with attention or caution

Etymology

From 'careless' + '-ly', modeled after other English adverbs.
