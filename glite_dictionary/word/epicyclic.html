epicyclic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛpɪˈsaɪklɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈsɪklɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resembling an epicycle, especially as used in astronomy or mechanical systems such as gear trains
relating to an epicycle or epicycles

Examples

The engineer explained how the epicyclic gear train optimizes power transmission in the gearbox.
Historically, astronomers used epicyclic models to explain the movements of planets.
This watch features an epicyclic mechanism to improve its accuracy.

Synset Examples

epicyclic gear train

Related Terms

  • Synonym: epicycle - a small circle whose center moves around the circumference of a larger one; used in astronomy and mechanics

Etymology

From Greek epikyklikos, from epi (upon) + kyklos (circle).
