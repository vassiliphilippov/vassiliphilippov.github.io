biz

1. NOUN

/ bɪz / (AmE), / bɪz / (BrE)
(informal) a particular area of business or professional activity, especially one’s occupation or industry.
a particular business or industry (informal)
informal

Examples

After years in the restaurant biz, he decided to open his own café.
Many people want to break into the film biz, but it's a tough industry to succeed in.
She left the tech biz to pursue a career in art.

Synset Examples

he's in the plumbing game
she's in show biz

Related Terms

  • Synonym: business - the activity of making, buying, or selling goods or services for profit

Etymology

Shortened form of 'business'.
