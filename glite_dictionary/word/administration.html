administration

1. NOUN

/ ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or activity of managing and supervising the affairs of a government, organization, or institution, especially with respect to making and enforcing rules or policies
the act of governing or managing

Examples

The new mayor implemented several reforms during her administration.
Efficient administration is essential for the success of any large institution.
The administration of public health policies requires careful planning and oversight.

Synset Examples

regulations for the governing of state prisons
he had considerable experience of government

Related Terms

  • Synonym: management - the process of dealing with or controlling things or people
  • Synonym: governance - the action or manner of governing a state or organization
  • Antonym: mismanagement - the process of managing something badly or wrongly

Etymology

From Latin administratio (management, direction), from administrare (to manage, administer).
