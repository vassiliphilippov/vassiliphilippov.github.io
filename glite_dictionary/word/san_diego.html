San Diego

1. PROPN

/ sæn diˈeɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / san dɪˈeɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
a city in southern California, located on San Diego Bay near the Mexican border, known for its beaches, parks, and naval base.
city in southern California, USA

Examples

Many tourists visit San Diego for its beautiful beaches and vibrant nightlife.
The naval base in San Diego is one of the largest in the United States.
After moving to San Diego, she enjoyed the city's mild climate and scenic views.

Etymology

From Spanish 'San Diego', meaning 'Saint Didacus', named after the Catholic saint Didacus of Alcalá.
