agronomy

1. NOUN

/ əˈɡrɑːnəmi / (AmE), / əˈɡrɒnəmi / (BrE)
the science of soil management and crop production
science of soil management and crops

Examples

He studied agronomy to help improve crop yields in his country.
Advancements in agronomy have led to more sustainable farming practices.
The university offers a degree program specializing in agronomy and soil science.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agriculture - the practice of farming, including cultivation of the soil and growing of crops

Etymology

From French agronomie, from Greek agros (field) + nomos (law, management).
