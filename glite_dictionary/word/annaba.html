Annaba

1. PROPN

/ ɑˈnɑːbə / (AmE), / æˈnɑːbə / (BrE)
a port city in northeastern Algeria, near the border with Tunisia
port city in northeastern Algeria

Examples

Many ships dock at Annaba because of its strategic location on the Mediterranean Sea.
During our travels through Algeria, we spent two days exploring Annaba.
The university in Annaba is known for its engineering department.

Etymology

From Arabic عنّابة (ʿAnnāba), meaning 'place of jujube trees'.
