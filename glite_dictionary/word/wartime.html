wartime

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔr.taɪm / (AmE), / ˈwɔː.taɪm / (BrE)
A period during which a country is engaged in war.
period during which war occurs

Examples

During wartime, many goods were rationed to conserve resources.
She grew up in wartime Britain and remembers the blackouts vividly.
The city was transformed by the challenges of wartime life.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: peacetime - period during which a country is not at war
