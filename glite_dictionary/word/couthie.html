couthie

1. ADJ

/ ˈkuːθi / (AmE), / ˈkuːθi / (BrE)
(chiefly Scottish) Warm, friendly, and approachable; genial and good-natured.
friendly and genial (Scottish)
british

Examples

His couthie manner put all the guests at ease during the gathering.
She gave us a couthie welcome when we visited her cottage in the Highlands.
The old innkeeper was known for his couthie hospitality and generous spirit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friendly - kind and pleasant
  • Synonym: genial - cheerful and friendly
  • Antonym: unfriendly - not friendly or kind

Etymology

Scottish variant of 'couth', which comes from Old English 'cūth' (known, familiar).
