grassy-leaved

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡræs.i liːvd / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑː.si liːvd / (BrE)
(of a plant) having leaves that resemble grass, typically long, narrow, and blade-like in shape.
having leaves like grass

Examples

Many irises are known for their grassy-leaved appearance.
The grassy-leaved sedge blended seamlessly into the surrounding lawn.
Selecting grassy-leaved perennials can give a garden a more natural look.
