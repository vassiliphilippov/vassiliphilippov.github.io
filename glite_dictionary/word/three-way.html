three-way

1. ADJ

/ ˈθriˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈθriːˌweɪ / (BrE)
Involving or carried out by three parties, people, or elements.
involving three parties or elements

Examples

The peace agreement was a three-way negotiation between the countries involved.
A three-way tie in the competition led to an extra playoff round.
She installed a three-way switch to control the lights from multiple locations.

Synset Examples

a tripartite treaty
a tripartite division
a three-way playoff

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tripartite - involving or made up of three parts or parties
