bass-ackwards

1. ADV

/ ˌbæsˈæk.wɚdz / (AmE), / ˌbæsˈæk.wədz / (BrE)
In a backward or completely wrong way; the reverse of what is normal or expected, often used humorously or derisively.
in a backward or wrong way
informal vulgar

Examples

He set up the entire system bass-ackwards, so nothing worked as intended.
They handled the scheduling bass-ackwards, causing needless confusion.
Whenever she tries to fix something, she seems to do it bass-ackwards.

Synset Examples

Her bass-ackwards approach surprised her colleagues.
He did everything bass-ackwards and still succeeded.
Completing the project bass-ackwards confused everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ass-backwards - in a totally wrong or backward way
  • Synonym: backwards - the opposite way to normal
  • Antonym: correctly - in the right or proper way

Etymology

Alteration and play on 'ass-backwards', with inversion of the initial sounds for humorous effect.
