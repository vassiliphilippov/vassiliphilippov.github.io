Taif

1. PROPN

/ taɪˈiːf / (AmE), / taɪˈiːf / (BrE)
A city in western Saudi Arabia, located east of Mecca, known for its cool climate and agricultural significance.
city in western Saudi Arabia

Examples

Many tourists visit Taif in the summer to enjoy its milder weather compared to other Saudi cities.
Roses from Taif are famous for their fragrance and are used to make traditional perfumes.
The annual fruit festival in Taif attracts vendors and visitors from across the region.
