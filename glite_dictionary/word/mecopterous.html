mecopterous

1. ADJ

/ məˈkɑptərəs / (AmE), / mɪˈkɒptərəs / (BrE)
Relating to or belonging to the order Mecoptera, a group of insects known as scorpionflies and their relatives.
relating to mecopteran insects
medical

Examples

The mecopterous wings of this specimen distinguish it from other insect orders.
Scientists study mecopterous species to understand evolutionary relationships among insects.
The forest habitat supports a diverse range of mecopterous insects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mecopteran - an insect of the order Mecoptera

Etymology

From New Latin *Mecoptera* (the insect order) + -ous (forming adjectives).
