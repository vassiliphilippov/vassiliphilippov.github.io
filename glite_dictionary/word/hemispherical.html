hemispherical

1. ADJ

/ ˌhɛmɪˈsfɪrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌhɛmɪˈsfɪərɪkəl / (BrE)
having the shape of half a sphere; relating to or characteristic of a hemisphere.
shaped like half a sphere

Examples

The planetarium has a hemispherical dome that displays images of the night sky.
The turtle's shell has a slightly hemispherical shape.
Scientists used a hemispherical mirror to study the reflection of light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spherical - having the shape of a sphere; like a ball

Etymology

From hemisphere + -ical; ultimately from Greek hēmisphairion (half-sphere).
