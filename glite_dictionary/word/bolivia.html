Bolivia

1. PROPN

/ boʊˈlɪviə / (AmE), / bəˈlɪvɪə / (BrE)
a landlocked country in central South America, founded in 1825 after gaining independence from Spain.
country in central South America

Examples

The president of Bolivia addressed the United Nations last week.
We learned about the rich cultural history of Bolivia in geography class.
La Paz is one of the capital cities of Bolivia.

Etymology

Named after Simón Bolívar, a leader in the struggle for independence in South America.
