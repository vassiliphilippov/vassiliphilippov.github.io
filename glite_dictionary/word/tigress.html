tigress

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪɡrəs / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡrəs / (BrE)
a female tiger
female tiger

Examples

The tigress paced quietly through the shadows of the forest.
We watched as the protective tigress cared for her cubs.
A tigress can be fiercely territorial, especially during mating season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiger - a large wild cat with a striped coat
  • Synonym: lioness - female lion

Etymology

From tiger + -ess (feminine suffix).
