matman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmætˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmatˌman / (BrE)
a male wrestler, especially one who competes in matches on a mat
male wrestler

Examples

The matman prepared for his final match of the season with intense focus.
After an exhausting bout, the younger matman emerged victorious.
The school honored their best matman during the sports banquet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrestler - a person who takes part in wrestling
