appetitive

1. ADJ

/ əˈpɛtəˌtɪv / (AmE), / əˈpɛtɪtɪv / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by appetite or a desire to satisfy bodily needs or wants.
relating to appetite or desire

Examples

The researchers examined the appetitive behaviors of the animals in response to food cues.
Some disorders involve abnormal appetitive drives that affect eating habits.
The drug was shown to reduce appetitive responses to high-calorie foods.

Synset Examples

appetitive needs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appetite - a natural desire to satisfy a bodily need, especially for food

Etymology

From Latin appetitivus (relating to desire), from appetitus ‘desire’.
