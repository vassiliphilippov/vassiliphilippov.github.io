bodybuilder

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdiˌbɪldər / (AmE), / ˈbɒdiˌbɪldə / (BrE)
a person who does intensive physical training to develop large and well-defined muscles, often for competition or display
person who trains to build large muscles

Examples

The bodybuilder spent hours each day lifting weights at the gym.
A famous bodybuilder gave a motivational speech at the fitness expo.
She trained like a bodybuilder, focusing on strength and muscle growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weightlifter - a person who lifts weights as a sport or for exercise

Etymology

From 'body' + 'builder', referring to one who builds up the body through exercise.
