androgenic

1. ADJ

/ ˌændrəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌandrəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or denoting the development of male characteristics due to the action of male sex hormones (androgens)
relating to male hormones or their effects
medical

Examples

The drug was found to have strong androgenic effects in laboratory animals.
Some athletes misuse androgenic steroids to enhance muscle growth and performance.
Male pattern baldness has been linked to androgenic activity in the scalp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: androgen - a male sex hormone, such as testosterone

Etymology

From androgen (male sex hormone) + -ic (adjective-forming suffix).
