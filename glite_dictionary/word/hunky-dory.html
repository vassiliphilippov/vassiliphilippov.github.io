hunky-dory

1. ADJ

/ ˌhʌŋkiˈdɔri / (AmE), / ˌhʌŋkiˈdɔːri / (BrE)
informal: satisfactory; going well; fine or okay.
fine; satisfactory; okay
informal

Examples

Despite the earlier confusion, everything was hunky-dory by the time the guests arrived.
She told me that her new job was hunky-dory, and she had nothing to complain about.
Don't worry, everything is hunky-dory now that we've sorted out the problem.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fine - satisfactory; acceptable; in good condition
  • Synonym: okay - satisfactory; in a good enough state
  • Antonym: bad - not good; unsatisfactory

Etymology

Probably of American origin, popularized in the mid-19th century; exact origin uncertain, possibly from Dutch.
