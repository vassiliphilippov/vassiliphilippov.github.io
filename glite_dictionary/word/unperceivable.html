unperceivable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.pɚˈsiː.və.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.pəˈsiː.və.bəl / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to perceive by the mind or senses.
impossible to perceive

Examples

The movement of the glacier was so slow that it was unperceivable to the naked eye.
There was an unperceivable change in the tone of his voice during the conversation.
For some animals, ultrasonic sounds are audible, but to humans they are unperceivable.

Synset Examples

an imperceptible drop in temperature
an imperceptible nod
color is unperceivable to the touch

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imperceptible - impossible or very difficult to see, hear, or notice
  • Antonym: perceivable - able to be perceived; detectable

Etymology

Formed from prefix un- (not) and perceivable (able to be perceived).
