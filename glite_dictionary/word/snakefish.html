snakefish

1. NOUN

/ ˈsneɪkˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsneɪk.fɪʃ / (BrE)
a tropical fish with a large mouth and a head resembling a lizard, found in various regions worldwide
tropical fish with lizard-like head

Examples

The snakefish is known for its voracious appetite and elongated body.
Researchers discovered a new species of snakefish in the Amazon basin.
Some aquarists keep snakefish in large, well-decorated tanks due to their unique appearance.
