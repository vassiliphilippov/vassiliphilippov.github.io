Kauai

1. PROPN

/ kaʊˈɑː.i / (AmE), / kaʊˈɑː.i / (BrE)
an island in northwest Hawaii, known for its lush landscapes and beaches, and the fourth largest of the Hawaiian Islands.
Hawaiian island; fourth largest in Hawaii

Examples

We spent two weeks exploring the natural beauty of Kauai.
The beaches on Kauai are among the most beautiful in the world.
Filmmakers often choose Kauai as a location for its stunning scenery.

Etymology

From Hawaiian Kauaʻi, the name of the island.
