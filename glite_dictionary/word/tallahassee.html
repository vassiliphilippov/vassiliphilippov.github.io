Tallahassee

1. PROPN

/ ˌtæl.əˈhæs.i / (AmE), / ˌtæl.əˈhæs.i / (BrE)
the capital city of the state of Florida, located in the northern part of the state
capital city of Florida, USA

Examples

The state's legislature meets in Tallahassee each year.
She moved to Tallahassee to attend Florida State University.
Tallahassee is known for its rolling hills and canopy roads.

Etymology

From Muskogean (Creek) *talwa ahassee*, meaning 'old town'.
