surfperch

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɜrfˌpɝtʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfˌpɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a small to medium-sized shallow-water fish found along the Pacific coast of North America, belonging to the family Embiotocidae
a Pacific coast shallow-water fish

Examples

Many anglers enjoy fishing for surfperch from California beaches in the summer.
The biologist conducted a survey of surfperch populations near coastal reefs.
We grilled the fresh surfperch we caught earlier that morning.
