freeing

1. NOUN

/ ˈfriːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfriːɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of setting someone or something free; liberation.
the act of liberating

Examples

The freeing of the hostages was celebrated by the entire community.
Environmentalists are working toward the freeing of captive dolphins into the wild.
Many novels explore the emotional impact of the sudden freeing of oppressed peoples.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liberation - the act of setting someone free from imprisonment, slavery, or oppression
  • Antonym: captivity - the condition of being imprisoned or confined

Etymology

From the verb 'free' + '-ing', forming a noun indicating the action of freeing.
