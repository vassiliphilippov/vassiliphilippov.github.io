bletia

1. NOUN

/ ˈbliːti.ə / (AmE), / ˈbliːti.ə / (BrE)
a genus of orchids with pseudobulbs and erect leafless racemes bearing large purple or pink flowers, native to the Americas
a genus of orchids with purple or pink flowers

Examples

The garden's collection includes a rare bletia in full bloom this season.
Many bletias thrive in humid, tropical climates and produce striking floral displays.
Experts identified the orchid as a bletia due to its distinctive pseudobulbs.

Etymology

Named after Spanish botanist Luis Blet (1737–1805).
