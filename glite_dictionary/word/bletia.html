Bletia

1. NOUN

/ ˈbliːtiə / (AmE), / ˈbliːtɪə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical American terrestrial orchids with large purple or pink flowers.
genus of tropical American orchids

Examples

The Bletia genus is valued by horticulturists for its brightly colored blossoms.
Several species of Bletia thrive in the warm climates of Central and South America.
Botanists recently discovered a new variety belonging to the Bletia genus.

Etymology

Named after Spanish botanist Don Luis Blet.
