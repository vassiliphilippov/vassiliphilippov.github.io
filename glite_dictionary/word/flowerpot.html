flowerpot

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊ.ɚˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.əˌpɒt / (BrE)
a container, usually made of clay, plastic, or ceramic, used for growing plants or flowers indoors or outdoors
container for growing plants

Examples

She placed the tulips in a bright red flowerpot on the windowsill.
The ceramic flowerpot cracked when it fell off the shelf.
My grandmother decorates each flowerpot with hand-painted designs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plant pot - a container in which plants are cultivated
