marinara

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɑː.rɪˈnɑː.rə / (AmE), / ˌmær.ɪˈnɑː.rə / (BrE)
a sauce for pasta made with tomatoes, garlic, and herbs
tomato-based pasta sauce

Examples

She prepared a fresh marinara for the spaghetti dinner.
The pizza was topped with marinara, mozzarella, and basil.
He prefers his meatballs served with extra marinara sauce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tomato sauce - a sauce made primarily from tomatoes

Etymology

From Italian 'marinara', meaning 'mariner's (sauce)', from 'marinaio' (sailor).
