psychopathological

1. ADJ

/ ˌsaɪ.kəˌpæθ.əˈlɑ.dʒɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌsaɪ.kəʊˌpæθ.əˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
Relating to or affected by mental disorders; involving or characteristic of psychopathology.
relating to mental disorders
medical

Examples

The patient exhibited several psychopathological symptoms that concerned the doctor.
Researchers are seeking better ways to classify psychopathological conditions in children.
Her psychopathological behavior indicated the need for further psychiatric evaluation.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: psychotic - relating to or affected by psychosis
  • Degree Lower: neurotic - relating to, or affected by neurosis

Etymology

From psycho- (mind) + pathological (relating to disease), ultimately from Greek.
