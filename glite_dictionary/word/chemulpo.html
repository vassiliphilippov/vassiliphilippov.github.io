Chemulpo

1. PROPN

/ tʃɛˈmʊlpoʊ / (AmE), / tʃɛˈmʊlpəʊ / (BrE)
a former name for Incheon, a port city in western South Korea on the Yellow Sea
former name for Incheon, South Korea

Examples

The early 20th-century trade routes often stopped at Chemulpo before heading to China.
During the Russo-Japanese War, the Battle of Chemulpo Bay made headlines worldwide.
Many historical documents refer to Incheon by its old name, Chemulpo, especially in the context of colonial Korea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Incheon - major port city in western South Korea, formerly called Chemulpo

Etymology

From Korean, formerly used as the name for Incheon.
