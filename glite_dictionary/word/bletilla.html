Bletilla

1. NOUN

/ bləˈtɪlə / (AmE), / blɪˈtɪlə / (BrE)
A small genus of hardy terrestrial orchids native chiefly to East Asia, similar to the genus Bletia.
genus of East Asian terrestrial orchids

Examples

The Bletilla thrives best in well-drained soil with partial shade.
Botanists admire the resilience of Bletilla in temperate climates.
My garden features several Bletilla species that bloom beautifully each spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bletia - a related genus of orchids

Etymology

From genus name, based on similar orchid genus Bletia.
