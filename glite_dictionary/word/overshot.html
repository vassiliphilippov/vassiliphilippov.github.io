overshot

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊvərˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəʃɒt / (BrE)
(of an upper part) projecting beyond or extending over a lower part.
having upper part projecting over lower part

Examples

The horse was born with an overshot jaw, which sometimes made eating difficult.
Some old watermills used an overshot wheel for greater efficiency.
He consulted a dentist about his slightly overshot upper teeth.

Synset Examples

an overshot jaw

Etymology

From 'over-' (above) + 'shot' (past participle of 'shoot'), originally used in technical senses such as 'overshot wheel'.
