Weigela florida

1. NOUN

/ waɪˈdʒiːlə ˈflɔːrɪdə / (AmE), / waɪˈdʒiːlə ˈflɒrɪdə / (BrE)
a deciduous shrub widely cultivated for its white, pink, or red flowers, also known for its arching branches and ornamental value
deciduous shrub with ornamental flowers

Examples

The Weigela florida in her garden bloomed with a profusion of deep pink flowers in spring.
Many gardeners favor Weigela florida for its ability to attract hummingbirds and butterflies.
Pruning your Weigela florida after flowering will help encourage more blossoms next year.

Etymology

From the genus name 'Weigela' (after German botanist Christian Ehrenfried Weigel) and Latin 'florida', meaning 'flowery'.
