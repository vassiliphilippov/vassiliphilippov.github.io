foreignness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrɪnnəs / (AmE), / ˈfɒrɪnnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being foreign, unfamiliar, or alien
the state of being foreign or unfamiliar

Examples

She felt a sense of foreignness when she first moved to the new country.
The foreignness of the customs made her feel out of place.
Despite its foreignness, the dish became popular among locals.

Synset Examples

the strangeness of a foreigner

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strangeness - the quality of being strange or unfamiliar
  • Antonym: familiarity - the state of being familiar or well known

Etymology

From 'foreign' + '-ness', forming a noun indicating the state or quality.
