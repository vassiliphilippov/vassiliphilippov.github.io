unfinished

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfɪnɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪnɪʃt / (BrE)
not completed; not brought to the desired final state.
not completed or finished

Examples

The building remained unfinished for over a decade because of funding issues.
He left the table covered with unfinished work when he went home.
Her novel was published posthumously, although it was unfinished.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: finished - completely done or brought to the desired final state
  • Synonym: incomplete - not complete; lacking some part

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' + 'finished' (from Middle English 'finisshen' from Old French 'finir', from Latin 'finire').

Synset POS: a

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfɪnɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪnɪʃt / (BrE)
not having a protective or decorative surface finish such as paint, varnish, or polish, especially referring to wood or furniture.
not having a surface finish

Examples

The carpenter delivered the table in an unfinished state so we could choose our own stain.
Many people prefer unfinished wood because it has a natural appearance.
Please keep unfinished furniture away from moisture to prevent damage.

Synset Examples

bare wood
unfinished furniture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bare - lacking a covering or finish

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'finished'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01718610-s
Synset POS: s

3. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfɪnɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪnɪʃt / (BrE)
not completed; not brought to an end or finished state
not completed; incomplete

Examples

The renovations remain unfinished after several months of work.
She turned in an unfinished painting for the competition.
There is still some unfinished homework on the table.

Synset Examples

unfinished business
the building is still unfinished

Related Terms

  • Antonym: completed - having been finished or brought to an end
  • Synonym: incomplete - not complete; not finished

Etymology

From un- (not) + finished, from Middle English 'finisshen', from Old French 'finir', from Latin 'finire' (to end, finish).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01007907-a
Synset POS: a
