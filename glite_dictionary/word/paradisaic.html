paradisaic

1. ADJ

/ ˌpærəˈdaɪzɪk / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈdeɪzɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling Paradise; idyllic or blissful.
of or like Paradise

Examples

The secluded garden had a paradisaic tranquility that soothed her mind.
Artists often depict the afterlife as a paradisaic realm filled with light and beauty.
He described his childhood home as a paradisaic haven where happiness seemed endless.

Synset Examples

together in that paradisal place
paradisiacal innocence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paradisiacal - relating to or characteristic of Paradise

Etymology

From Latin paradisus (paradise) + -ic (adjective-forming suffix).
