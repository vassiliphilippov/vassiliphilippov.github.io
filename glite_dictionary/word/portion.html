portion

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔrʃən / (AmE), / ˈpɔːʃən / (BrE)
money or property given by a woman's family to her husband at marriage; a dowry.
money or property given as a dowry

Examples

She brought a substantial portion to her marriage, securing her husband's financial future.
In many cultures, a daughter's portion was considered essential for a good match.
His family depended on his wife's portion to improve their estate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dowry - money or property that a woman brings to her husband in marriage

Etymology

From Latin 'portio' (a share, part).
