unperceived

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnpərˈsivd / (AmE), / ˌʌnpəˈsiːvd / (BrE)
not noticed, detected, or remarked upon; not perceived.
not noticed or detected

Examples

She moved through the room unperceived by the guests, her footsteps utterly silent.
The error went unperceived until it caused a serious problem in the data analysis.
Much of his hard work remained unperceived by the management, though it was crucial to the team's success.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: perceived - noticed; detected; recognized

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'perceived' (from Latin percipere, to seize entirely, understand).
