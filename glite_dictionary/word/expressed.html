expressed

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈsprɛst / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛst / (BrE)
clearly stated, communicated, or made known in words or by actions.
clearly stated or communicated

Examples

The opinions expressed in this article are solely those of the author.
She clearly expressed her disappointment during the meeting.
His gratitude was expressed in a heartfelt letter.

Synset Examples

frequently uttered sentiments

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unexpressed - not stated or communicated

Etymology

Past participle of 'express', from Latin 'expressus' (pressed out).
