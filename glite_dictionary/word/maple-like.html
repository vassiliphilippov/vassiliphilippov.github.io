maple-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈmeɪpəl laɪk / (AmE), / ˈmeɪpəl laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a maple, especially in shape or appearance.
resembling a maple

Examples

The tree in the garden has maple-like leaves that turn red in autumn.
The tabletops were made of a maple-like wood, giving the café a warm feel.
Her sketch featured a flower with strikingly maple-like petals.
