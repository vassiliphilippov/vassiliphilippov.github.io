Mirish

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪrɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɪrɪʃ / (BrE)
A group of little-known Kamarupan languages spoken in northeast India.
group of Kamarupan languages

Examples

Researchers have documented several endangered languages within the Mirish group.
The Mirish languages are primarily spoken in Arunachal Pradesh.
Linguists debate the classification of Mirish among other Kamarupan languages.
