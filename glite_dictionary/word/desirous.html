desirous

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈzaɪɚəs / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪərəs / (BrE)
having a strong wish or longing for something; expressing desire for something
having or showing desire for something

Examples

He is desirous of making a good impression on his first day.
Many investors are desirous of new opportunities in the tech industry.
She looked at the cake with a desirous expression on her face.

Synset Examples

desirous of high office
desirous of finding a quick solution to the problem

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eager - having keen or enthusiastic desire
  • Antonym: apathetic - showing or feeling no interest or desire

Etymology

From Old French 'desireus', from Latin 'desiderosus', from 'desiderare' (to desire).
