unreconciled

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈrɛk.ən.saɪld / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈrɛk.ən.saɪld / (BrE)
(of things or people) not made consistent, compatible, or brought into agreement.
not made compatible or consistent

Examples

The two reports remained unreconciled despite several rounds of revisions.
Her unreconciled feelings about the decision made it hard for her to move on.
There are still unreconciled differences between the two political parties.

Synset Examples

two unreconciled accountings

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incompatible - unable to exist or work together without conflict
  • Antonym: reconciled - made consistent or brought into agreement

Etymology

From un- (not) + reconciled, from Latin conciliāre (to bring together).
