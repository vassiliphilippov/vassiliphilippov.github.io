Pediamycin

1. NOUN

/ ˌpiːdiəˈmaɪsɪn / (AmE), / ˌpiːdɪəˈmaɪsɪn / (BrE)
A trade name for erythromycin, an antibiotic used to treat infections caused by bacteria.
trade name for erythromycin antibiotic
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Pediamycin to treat the child's throat infection.
After taking Pediamycin for a week, her symptoms significantly improved.
Some patients experience mild stomach upset when using Pediamycin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: erythromycin - an antibiotic obtained from Streptomyces erythreus
