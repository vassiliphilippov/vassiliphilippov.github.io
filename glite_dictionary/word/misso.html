misso

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɪsəʊ / (BrE)
(informal) A term of endearment for a woman, especially used colloquially or affectionately.
colloquial term of endearment for a woman
informal slang

Examples

Everyone liked to call her misso because she always looked after the group.
He sent a postcard home to his misso while traveling abroad.
The guys at the pub joked about bringing their misso to the football game.

Synset Examples

The locals affectionately referred to her as 'misso' during her stay.
