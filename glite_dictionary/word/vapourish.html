vapourish

1. ADJ

/ ˈveɪpərɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈveɪpərɪʃ / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of vapor; having a thin, misty, or insubstantial quality
resembling vapor; misty or insubstantial

Examples

The mountain tops were shrouded in vapourish clouds that softened the landscape.
Her memory of that day remained vapourish, elusive and hard to grasp.
A vapourish mist drifted across the fields early in the morning.

Synset Examples

vaporous clouds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vaporous - having the form or nature of vapor; misty or insubstantial

Etymology

From English 'vapour' (mist, steam) + '-ish'.
