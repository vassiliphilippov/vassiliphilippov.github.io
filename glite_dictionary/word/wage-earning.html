wage-earning

1. ADJ

/ ˈweɪdʒ ˌɜrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈweɪdʒ ˌɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or group) earning a regular income through paid work, especially manual or industrial labor.
earning money through paid work

Examples

Many wage-earning families struggle to make ends meet each month.
His parents are both wage-earning workers in a local factory.
The government introduced new policies to support the wage-earning population.

Synset Examples

party of the propertyless proletariat

Related Terms

  • Antonym: salaried - receiving a fixed regular payment, typically not based on manual labor
