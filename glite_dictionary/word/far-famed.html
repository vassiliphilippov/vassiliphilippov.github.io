far-famed

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɑr ˌfeɪmd / (AmE), / ˈfɑː ˌfeɪmd / (BrE)
widely known and much admired; famous over a large area or among many people
widely known and admired

Examples

The far-famed city attracted travelers from every corner of the globe.
Her far-famed generosity inspired countless acts of kindness throughout the region.
The chef’s far-famed pastries brought visitors from thousands of miles away.

Synset Examples

a famous actor
a celebrated musician
a famed scientist
an illustrious judge
a notable historian
a renowned painter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: famous - known about by many people
  • Antonym: obscure - not well known; not famous

Etymology

Formed from 'far' (distant) and 'famed' (celebrated).
