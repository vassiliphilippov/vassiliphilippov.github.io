arbour

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrbər / (AmE), / ˈɑːbə / (BrE)
a shelter in a garden made of a framework that supports climbing plants, often used as a shady resting place
garden shelter formed by climbing plants
british

Examples

They sat together beneath the arbour, enjoying the cool shade and the scent of roses.
The old wooden arbour at the end of the path was covered in wisteria and ivy.
She dreamed of building a beautiful arbour in her garden where she could read in peace.

Synset Examples

the arbor provided a shady resting place in the park

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arbor - a shelter in a garden made by climbing plants (American spelling)
  • Synonym: gazebo - a freestanding, open garden structure

Etymology

From Old French *erbier* (grassplot), from Latin *herba* (grass, herb).
