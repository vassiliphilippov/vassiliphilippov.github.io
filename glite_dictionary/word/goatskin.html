goatskin

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊtˌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊtˌskɪn / (BrE)
the skin or leather obtained from a goat, used especially to make leather goods
leather from a goat's hide

Examples

The traditional drum was covered with goatskin for a rich, resonant sound.
He bought a pair of fine goatskin gloves at the market.
Ancient manuscripts were sometimes written on goatskin parchment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sheepskin - the skin of a sheep, often used for leather
