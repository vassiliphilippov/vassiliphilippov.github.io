ade

1. NOUN

/ eɪd / (AmE), / eɪd / (BrE)
A sweetened drink made from diluted fruit juice, such as lemonade or limeade.
sweet fruit-flavored beverage

Examples

I served cold ade at the summer picnic.
She prefers grape ade to soda as a refreshing drink.
After playing outside, the kids drank a glass of ade to cool off.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lemonade - a sweetened lemon-flavored beverage

Etymology

From French 'ade', as in 'limonade' (lemonade), referring to sweetened drinks.
