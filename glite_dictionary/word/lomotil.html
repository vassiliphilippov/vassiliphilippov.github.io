Lomotil

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊmoʊˌtɪl / (AmE), / ˈləʊmətɪl / (BrE)
A proprietary name for a medication used to treat diarrhea, containing diphenoxylate and atropine.
brand of antidiarrheal medication
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Lomotil to control the patient's severe diarrhea.
After taking Lomotil, she noticed her symptoms improved within a few hours.
Lomotil is often used when other treatments for diarrhea are not effective.
