to halve

1. VERB

/ hæv / (AmE), / hɑːv / (BrE)
to divide something into two equal parts
divide into two equal parts

Examples

To prepare the recipe, you must halve the tomatoes before adding them to the bowl.
She decided to halve her portion to save some for later.
Carefully halve the cake so that everyone gets an equal share.

Synset Examples

Halve the cake

Related Terms

  • Synonym: divide - to separate into parts or groups
  • Antonym: double - to make twice as much or as many

Etymology

From Old English 'healfian' (to make half), from 'healf' (half).
