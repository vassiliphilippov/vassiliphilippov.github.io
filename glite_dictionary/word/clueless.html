clueless

1. ADJ

/ ˈkluːləs / (AmE), / ˈkluːlɪs / (BrE)
having no knowledge, understanding, or ability regarding a particular subject or situation; completely uninformed or unaware.
completely uninformed or unaware

Examples

Despite several hints, he remained clueless about the surprise party.
She felt completely clueless when it came to fixing her car.
Many new students are clueless about the campus layout during their first week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness in general or about something specific
  • Antonym: informed - having or showing knowledge of a particular subject or situation

Etymology

Formed from 'clue' + '-less'.
