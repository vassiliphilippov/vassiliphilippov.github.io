rheologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌrioʊˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of rheology, the study of the flow and deformation of matter.
relating to rheology (study of flow/deformation)
medical

Examples

The rheologic properties of the material were measured using advanced equipment.
Engineers considered the rheologic behavior of fluids when designing the pipeline.
The mixture's rheologic response was crucial for its intended industrial application.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rheological - relating to rheology, variant adjectival form
  • Antonym: nonrheologic - not relating to rheology

Etymology

From rheology + -ic, after Greek 'rheos' (flow).
