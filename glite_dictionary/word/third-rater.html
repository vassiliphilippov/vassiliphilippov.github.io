third-rater

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɝd ˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈθɜːd ˌreɪtə / (BrE)
A person or thing of distinctly inferior or poor quality; someone or something third-rate.
inferior person or thing

Examples

The critics dismissed him as a third-rater with little original talent.
Despite his efforts, he remained a third-rater among accomplished writers.
No one wanted to hire a third-rater for such an important project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: third-rate - of inferior or poor quality
  • Antonym: first-rater - person or thing of the highest quality
  • Degree Lower: second-rater - person or thing of average or mediocre quality

Etymology

From the ordinal 'third' and 'rate', patterned after 'first-rate', 'second-rate', etc.
