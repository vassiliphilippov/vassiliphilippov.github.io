bulgy

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʌldʒi / (AmE), / ˈbʌldʒi / (BrE)
curving or swelling outward; protruding in a rounded way
curving or swelling outward

Examples

The suitcase was so full that its sides became bulgy and hard to close.
He noticed his new glasses left a slightly bulgy mark on his nose.
The frog's bulgy eyes gave it a comical appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulging - swelling or sticking out in a rounded shape
  • Antonym: sunken - curved inward or hollowed, the opposite of protruding

Etymology

Formed from 'bulge' + '-y', indicating having the quality of bulging.
