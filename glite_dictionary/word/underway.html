underway

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndərˈweɪ / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈweɪ / (BrE)
currently in progress; happening or being done now
in progress; happening now

Examples

The investigation is underway and police are collecting evidence from the scene.
A major construction project is underway in the city center this summer.
Preparations for the ceremony are already underway despite the uncertainty.

Synset Examples

there is mischief afoot
plans are afoot
preparations for the trial are underway

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ongoing - continuing to exist or develop; in progress
  • Synonym: pending - awaiting completion or resolution

Etymology

From the nautical phrase 'under way', originally referring to a vessel that has started moving.
