Sno-cat

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnoʊˌkæt / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌkat / (BrE)
A type of motorized vehicle designed for travel on snow, typically with tracks and sometimes skis, used especially for transport or work in snowy environments.
tracked vehicle for travel on snow

Examples

The research team used a Sno-cat to transport their equipment across the Antarctic ice.
After the heavy snowfall, a Sno-cat cleared the trail for the hikers.
Our resort offers guided tours in a Sno-cat to explore the mountain slopes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snowmobile - small motor vehicle for traveling on snow

Etymology

Coined in the mid-20th century, altered from 'snow cat', short for 'snow caterpillar' (referring to caterpillar tracks).
