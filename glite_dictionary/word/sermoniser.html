sermoniser

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɜːrməˌnaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsɜːmənʌɪzə / (BrE)
a person who delivers sermons, especially as an occupation; a preacher
person who delivers sermons

Examples

The sermoniser spoke passionately about forgiveness and compassion during the Sunday service.
As a traveling sermoniser, she visited many small communities to share her message.
Some found the sermoniser's style inspiring, while others thought it was too moralistic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preacher - a person who gives sermons, especially as a member of the clergy
  • Synonym: to sermonize - to deliver a sermon or moral speech

Etymology

From 'sermon' (from Latin 'sermo' meaning 'discourse, speech') + '-iser' (agent suffix).
