abysmally

1. ADV

/ əˈbɪzməli / (AmE), / əˈbɪzməli / (BrE)
In an extremely bad or terrible manner.
in an extremely bad way

Examples

Our team played abysmally in the championship final and lost by a wide margin.
The service at the restaurant was abysmally slow, which made the experience quite unpleasant.
He managed the project abysmally, causing significant delays and confusion.

Synset Examples

she sings terribly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terribly - in a very bad way; extremely poorly
  • Antonym: excellently - in an extremely good or outstanding manner

Etymology

From 'abysmal' (from abyss), meaning 'like an abyss' or 'extremely bad', plus the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
