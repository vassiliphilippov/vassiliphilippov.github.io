to tenderise

1. VERB

/ ˈtɛndəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈtɛndəraɪz / (BrE)
to make (meat or other food) tender or more tender, especially by marinating, pounding, or adding a tenderizer
make meat or food more tender
british

Examples

She always tenderises the steak before cooking it for dinner.
You can tenderise chicken by marinating it in lemon juice overnight.
Many chefs tenderise tough cuts of beef by pounding them with a mallet.

Synset Examples

tenderize meat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to tenderize - make meat tender (American spelling of 'tenderise')

Etymology

From 'tender' + '-ise' (chiefly British spelling); see 'tenderize' for American spelling.
