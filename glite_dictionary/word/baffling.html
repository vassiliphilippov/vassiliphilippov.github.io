baffling

1. ADJ

/ ˈbæf.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbæf.lɪŋ / (BrE)
impossible to understand or explain; confusing or puzzling
confusing or hard to understand

Examples

The instructions were so baffling that even experts struggled to follow them.
It was a baffling disappearance that left police searching for answers for years.
Her sudden anger was truly baffling to everyone in the room.

Synset Examples

a baffling problem
I faced the knotty problem of what to have for breakfast
a problematic situation at home

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puzzling - difficult to understand or solve
  • Synonym: confusing - hard to understand; causing confusion
  • Antonym: straightforward - easy to understand; uncomplicated
  • Synonym: perplexing - causing confusion or difficulty

Etymology

Present participle of 'baffle', which likely originated from 18th-century Scots, meaning 'to disgrace' or 'to perplex'.
