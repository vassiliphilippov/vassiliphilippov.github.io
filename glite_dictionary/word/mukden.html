Mukden

1. PROPN

/ ˈmʌk.dən / (AmE), / ˈmʌk.dən / (BrE)
the former name for Shenyang, a major city in northeastern China
former name of a Chinese city (Shenyang)

Examples

In 1905, the Battle of Mukden marked a turning point in the Russo-Japanese War.
Many historical texts refer to Shenyang by its old name, Mukden.
The ancient palaces of Mukden attract many tourists interested in China's history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Shenyang - current name of the city formerly known as Mukden

Etymology

From Manchu "Mukden" (meaning "rising prosperity"), historical name used during Qing dynasty.
