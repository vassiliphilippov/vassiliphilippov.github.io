psychometrika

1. NOUN

/ saɪˌkɑməˈtrɪkə / (AmE), / saɪˌkɒməˈtrɪkə / (BrE)
any branch of psychology concerned with the measurement of psychological variables, such as intelligence, personality, or abilities.
branch of psychology for measuring psychological traits

Examples

Recent advances in psychometrika have improved our understanding of intelligence testing.
Many universities offer graduate courses in psychometrika to train students in psychological measurement.
Her research in psychometrika focused on developing reliable assessments for cognitive abilities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychometrics - the science of measuring mental capacities and processes

Etymology

From 'psycho-' (mind) and Greek 'metron' (measure).
