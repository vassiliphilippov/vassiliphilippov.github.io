exactness

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˈzæktnəs / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzæktnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being accurate and precise; the state of being free from error or deviation
the quality of being accurate and precise

Examples

The engineer's exactness was evident in every measurement she took.
He approached each task with remarkable exactness, leaving no detail overlooked.
In scientific experiments, exactness is essential for reliable results.

Synset Examples

he demanded exactness in all details
a man of great exactitude

Related Terms

  • Synonym: precision - the quality of being exact and accurate
  • Synonym: accuracy - the quality of being correct or true
  • Antonym: inexactness - the quality of not being exact

Etymology

From exact + -ness, modeled after Middle French exactnesse.
