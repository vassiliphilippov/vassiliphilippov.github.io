uniqueness

1. NOUN

/ juˈniknəs / (AmE), / juˈniːknɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being the only one of its kind; distinctiveness
the quality of being one of a kind

Examples

Her uniqueness made her stand out in the crowd.
The uniqueness of the painting attracts many visitors to the gallery.
Scientists study the uniqueness of each individual's genetic code.

Synset Examples

that singularity distinguished him from all his companions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: singularity - the state or quality of being singular or unique
  • Antonym: commonness - the state of being common or ordinary

Etymology

From unique + -ness, where 'unique' is from French 'unique', from Latin 'unicus' (only, sole, single).
