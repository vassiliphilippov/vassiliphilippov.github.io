first basewoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɜrst ˈbeɪswʊmən / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˈbeɪswʊmən / (BrE)
(in baseball) a woman who plays the position of first base on a team.
female baseball first baseman

Examples

After a strong season, the team's first basewoman was nominated for most valuable player.
The first basewoman caught every throw with impressive skill during the game.
They recruited a new first basewoman to strengthen their infield defense.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first baseman - a player who plays first base (neutral or male)
