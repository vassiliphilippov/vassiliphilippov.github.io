Vatican

1. PROPN

/ ˈvæt̬.ɪ.kən / (AmE), / ˈvæt.ɪ.kən / (BrE)
the official residence of the Pope in Vatican City, serving as the administrative and spiritual center of the Roman Catholic Church
residence of the Pope in Vatican City

Examples

She has always dreamed of visiting the Vatican to see the famous artworks.
The new policy was announced by officials at the Vatican.
Many people gather in Saint Peter's Square to hear the Vatican's Christmas message.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Vatican City - an independent city-state enclaved within Rome, headquarters of the Roman Catholic Church

Etymology

From Latin 'Vaticanus', after Mons Vaticanus (Vatican Hill) in Rome.
