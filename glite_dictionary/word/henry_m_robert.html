Henry M. Robert

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɛnri ɛm ˈrɑbərt / (AmE), / ˈhɛnri əm ˈrɒbət / (BrE)
(1837–1923) United States Army officer and author of Robert's Rules of Order, a widely used manual of parliamentary procedure.
author of Robert's Rules of Order
