sizable

1. ADJ

/ ˈsaɪzəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪzəbl̩ / (BrE)
fairly large in size or amount; considerable
fairly large; considerable

Examples

She donated a sizable amount to the charity last year.
The company reported a sizable increase in profits for the quarter.
They live in a sizable house on the outskirts of the city.

Synset Examples

a sizable fortune
an ample waistline
of ample proportions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ample - large or plentiful in amount, extent, or capacity
  • Antonym: modest - not large in size or amount; moderate

Etymology

From English 'size' + '-able'.
