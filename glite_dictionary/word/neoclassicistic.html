neoclassicistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌniː.oʊˌklæs.əˈsɪs.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌniː.əʊˌklæs.ɪˈsɪs.tɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or advocating neoclassicism, especially in art, architecture, literature, or music.
relating to neoclassicism

Examples

Her paintings often display a strong neoclassicistic influence in their structure and composition.
The building's neoclassicistic features include balanced proportions and classical columns.
Critics praised his neoclassicistic approach to modern sculpture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neoclassical - relating to neoclassicism

Etymology

From 'neo-' (new) + 'classic' + '-istic', patterning after 'neoclassicism'.
