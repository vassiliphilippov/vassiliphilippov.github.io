spatula-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈspætʃələ ˌʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈspatjʊlə ˌʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having a broad, rounded end and a narrow base, especially used to describe leaves.
broad-rounded end with narrow base (leaf shape)

Examples

The plant can be identified by its spatula-shaped leaves that taper at the base.
Some insects lay eggs on spatula-shaped pads for better attachment.
The artist praised the unique spatula-shaped petals of the rare flower.

Etymology

Formed from 'spatula' (Latin 'spatula', a small flat tool) and '-shaped'.
