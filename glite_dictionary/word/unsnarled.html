unsnarled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsnɑrld / (AmE), / ʌnˈsnɑːld / (BrE)
Having been disentangled or straightened out from a tangled or knotted state.
disentangled; straightened out

Examples

After hours of patient work, the knotted cords were finally unsnarled.
The dispute was complex, but the mediator unsnarled the conflicting claims.
She unsnarled her necklace chain with remarkable dexterity.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tangled - twisted together in a confusing or complicated way
  • Synonym: disentangled - having become free from entanglement

Etymology

From 'un-' (not, opposite of) and 'snarl' (to tangle).
