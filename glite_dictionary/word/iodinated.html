iodinated

1. ADJ

/ ˈaɪ.əˌdeɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.əʊˌdeɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
Treated or combined with iodine, especially as a chemical process.
treated with iodine

Examples

Some contrast agents used in medical imaging are iodinated compounds.
Using iodinated salt helps prevent iodine deficiency in populations.
The laboratory stored several iodinated chemicals for research purposes.

Synset Examples

iodized salt

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iodized - Treated with iodine, especially referring to salt

Etymology

From iodine + -ated, indicating treatment or combination with iodine.
