sheeny

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃini / (AmE), / ˈʃiːni / (BrE)
An extremely offensive ethnic slur for a Jewish person. Strongly offensive; do not use.
offensive ethnic slur for a Jew
offensive

Examples

The use of the word sheeny is widely recognized as deeply offensive and unacceptable.
He was appalled when he heard someone refer to his friend with the slur sheeny.
Scholars study hateful terms like sheeny to trace the history of anti-Semitism in language.

Etymology

Probably from Yiddish "shayn" (beautiful) or German "schön", used contemptuously in English.

2. ADJ

/ ˈʃini / (AmE), / ˈʃiːni / (BrE)
Having a shiny, glossy, or lustrous surface; reflecting light.
shiny; glossy; lustrous

Examples

The actress wore a sheeny satin dress that caught every bit of light.
Each leaf appeared sheeny with dew in the early morning sunlight.
The sheeny finish on the car made it look freshly waxed.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shiny - having a surface that reflects light easily
  • Antonym: dull - not shiny or glossy; lacking luster

Etymology

From 'sheen' (brightness, luster) + '-y' (adjectival suffix).
