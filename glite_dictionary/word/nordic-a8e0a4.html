nordic

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɔːrdɪk / (AmE), / ˈnɔːdɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of the peoples of Scandinavia, especially in appearance or cultural traits.
resembling Scandinavian peoples

Examples

The character's tall stature and fair hair gave him a distinctly nordic appearance.
She admired the minimalist designs inspired by nordic aesthetics.
Anthropologists once classified certain physical traits as nordic features.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Scandinavian - relating to Scandinavia or its peoples

Etymology

From French nordique, from nord (north); originally applied to northern European peoples.
