Vanessa Bell

1. PROPN

/ vəˈnɛsə bɛl / (AmE), / vəˈnɛsə bɛl / (BrE)
(1879–1961) An English painter and interior designer, sister of Virginia Woolf, and prominent member of the Bloomsbury Group.
English painter and Bloomsbury Group member

Examples

Vanessa Bell played a key role in shaping the aesthetic of the Bloomsbury Group.
Many art historians have studied the paintings of Vanessa Bell for their bold colors and innovative designs.
An exhibition of Vanessa Bell's work drew visitors from across the country.
