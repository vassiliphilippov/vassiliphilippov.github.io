buffeting

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌf.ɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʌf.ɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
the action of striking repeatedly and forcefully, especially with strong blows
repeated heavy blows

Examples

The ship endured constant buffeting from the stormy sea all night.
The buffeting of the wind made it difficult to walk down the street.
After hours of buffeting, the old barn finally collapsed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beating - the act of hitting repeatedly
  • Synonym: pounding - the act of striking with repeated force

Etymology

From Middle English 'buffeten' (to strike), related to Old French 'buffet' (a blow, slap).
