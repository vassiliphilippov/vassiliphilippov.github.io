gray-haired

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡreɪˈhɛrd / (AmE), / ˌɡreɪˈheəd / (BrE)
(of a person) having hair that is gray or white, typically as a sign of aging.
having gray or white hair

Examples

My grandfather is gray-haired and wise, always ready to offer advice.
She admired the gray-haired professor for his knowledge and kindness.
At the reunion, the once youthful team was now mostly gray-haired men and women reminiscing about old times.

Synset Examples

whose beard with age is hoar
nodded his hoary head

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hoary - (of a person) having gray or white hair due to age
