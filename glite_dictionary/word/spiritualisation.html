spiritualisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌspɪrɪtʃuələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌspɪrɪtjʊəlʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something spiritual or infusing it with spiritual qualities or content
making something spiritual

Examples

The spiritualisation of art is a recurring theme in many philosophical discussions.
Some religious movements seek the spiritualisation of everyday life.
His teachings focus on the spiritualisation of human relationships and society.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiritualization - the act or process of making something spiritual (US spelling)

Etymology

From spiritual + -isation, modeled after French spiritualisation.
