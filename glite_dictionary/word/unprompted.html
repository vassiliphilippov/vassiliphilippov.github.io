unprompted

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈprɑmptɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈprɒmptɪd / (BrE)
done spontaneously or without being encouraged or requested by someone else
done spontaneously, without encouragement

Examples

Her apology was completely unprompted, which made it even more sincere.
The child offered unprompted help to his teacher during class.
He gave an unprompted explanation for his absence, even though no one had asked.

Synset Examples

an impulsive gesture of affection

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spontaneous - done naturally, without external cause
  • Antonym: prompted - done as a result of encouragement or request
