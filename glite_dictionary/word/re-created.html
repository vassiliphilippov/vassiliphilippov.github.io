re-created

1. ADJ

/ ˌriˈkreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌriːˈkriːeɪtɪd / (BrE)
made again or created anew, especially after being destroyed or lost; reconstructed or reproduced.
created again; reconstructed

Examples

The ancient statue was carefully re-created using digital technology and historical records.
In the film, a lost medieval village was re-created in stunning detail by set designers.
Her research focused on how extinct plants have been re-created in botanical gardens.

Synset Examples

this re-created literalness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rebuilt - built again after destruction
  • Synonym: restored - returned to a former condition
  • Antonym: original - existing from the beginning; not a copy or reproduction

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 're-' (again) and 'created'.
