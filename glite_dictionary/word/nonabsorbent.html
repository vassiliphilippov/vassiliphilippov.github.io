nonabsorbent

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.əbˈzɔr.bənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.əbˈzɔː.bənt / (BrE)
Not capable of absorbing or soaking up liquids.
not able to absorb liquids

Examples

We used nonabsorbent material to prevent the liquid from seeping through.
The lab requires nonabsorbent gloves when handling chemicals.
Ceramic tiles are typically nonabsorbent and easy to clean.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: absorbent - able to soak up liquids easily

Etymology

From 'non-' (not) + 'absorbent'.
