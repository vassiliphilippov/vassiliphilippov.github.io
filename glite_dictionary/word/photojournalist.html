photojournalist

1. NOUN

/ ˌfoʊ.t̬oʊˈdʒɝː.nə.lɪst / (AmE), / ˌfəʊ.təʊˈdʒɜː.nə.lɪst / (BrE)
a journalist who reports news stories mainly by taking and presenting photographs, often with accompanying text
journalist who reports news with photographs

Examples

The photojournalist captured powerful images of the protest for the front page.
As a photojournalist, she traveled to conflict zones around the world.
The magazine hired a photojournalist to document the effects of climate change on coastal communities.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: journalist - a person who writes for newspapers, magazines, or news websites
  • Degree Lower: photographer - a person who takes photographs, especially as a profession

Etymology

Formed from 'photo-' (photograph) and 'journalist'.
