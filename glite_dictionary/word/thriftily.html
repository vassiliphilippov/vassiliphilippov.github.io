thriftily

1. ADV

/ ˈθrɪftɪli / (AmE), / ˈθrɪftɪli / (BrE)
in a way that shows careful use of money or resources; in a thrifty manner.
in a thrifty manner

Examples

She always shops thriftily to make her paycheck last longer.
The family lived thriftily, saving a little bit of money each month.
He decorated his apartment thriftily, using secondhand furniture and homemade art.

Synset Examples

a used towel that he had used and had left thriftily on the ledge below the mirror rather than consign to the linen basket

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thrifty - showing careful use of money or resources
  • Antonym: extravagantly - in a way that spends resources or money wastefully

Etymology

From 'thrifty' + '-ly', forming the adverb from the adjective.
