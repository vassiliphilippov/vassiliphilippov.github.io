tonsured

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɑːnʃərd / (AmE), / ˈtɒnʃəd / (BrE)
having part of the head, especially the crown, shaved or left naturally bald, often as a religious practice
having the crown shaved or bald

Examples

The tonsured monk knelt silently in the chapel.
Several tonsured figures walked through the ancient cloisters at dawn.
The painting depicts a tonsured priest amid the congregation.

Synset Examples

tonsured monks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bald - lacking hair on the scalp

Etymology

From the Latin 'tonsura' (clipping, shaving) via Old French 'tonsure'.
