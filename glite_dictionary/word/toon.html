toon

1. NOUN

/ tun / (AmE), / tuÀên / (BrE)
an informal term for a cartoon or animated film, especially one made by photographing drawings to create the illusion of movement
informal term for cartoon or animated film
informal

Examples

My kids love watching a toon every Saturday morning.
The studio released a new toon featuring its famous mouse character.
She collects merchandise from classic toons from the 90s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cartoon - a film made by photographing a series of drawings for animation

Etymology

Abbreviated form of 'cartoon'.
