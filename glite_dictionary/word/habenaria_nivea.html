Habenaria nivea

1. NOUN

/ həˌbɛˈnɛriə ˈnaɪviə / (AmE), / həˌbɛˈnɑːriə ˈniːviə / (BrE)
A species of slender fringed orchid found in eastern North America, known for its delicate white flowers.
slender fringed orchid with white flowers

Examples

The delicate white flowers of the Habenaria nivea are a rare sight in eastern forests.
Botanists study the habitats of Habenaria nivea to understand its environmental needs.
I was thrilled to spot a blooming Habenaria nivea during my hike in the wetlands.

Etymology

From Latin; 'Habenaria' is a genus of orchids, and 'nivea' means 'snowy' or 'white'.
