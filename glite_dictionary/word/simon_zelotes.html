Simon Zelotes

1. PROPN

/ ˈsaɪmən zɛˈloʊtiːz / (AmE), / ˈsaɪmən zɛˈləʊtiːz / (BrE)
one of the twelve Apostles of Jesus, sometimes identified as Simon the Zealot in the New Testament.
one of the twelve Apostles of Jesus

Examples

Simon Zelotes is sometimes referred to as Simon the Zealot in various translations of the Bible.
Some scholars debate whether Simon Zelotes and Simon the Cananaean are the same person.
The feast day of Simon Zelotes is celebrated in the Western Christian tradition.

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Σίμων ὁ Ζηλωτής (Simōn ho Zēlōtēs), meaning 'Simon the Zealot'.
