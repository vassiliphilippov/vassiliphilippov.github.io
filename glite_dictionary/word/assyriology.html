Assyriology

1. NOUN

/ əˌsɪriˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / əˌsɪəriˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The academic study of the history, language, and culture of the ancient Assyrians and related civilizations of Mesopotamia.
study of ancient Assyrians and Mesopotamia

Examples

She decided to specialize in Assyriology after taking a course on ancient Mesopotamian civilizations.
The university's department of Assyriology houses one of the largest collections of cuneiform tablets in the world.
His groundbreaking research in Assyriology earned him international recognition among scholars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: archaeology - the study of human history through excavation and analysis of artifacts

Etymology

From Latin 'Assyrius' (Assyrian) and Greek '-logia' (study of).
