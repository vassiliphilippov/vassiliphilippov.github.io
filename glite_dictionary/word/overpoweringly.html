overpoweringly

1. ADV

/ ˌoʊvɚˈpaʊɚɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈpaʊərɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely strong or intense, to the point of being impossible to resist.
in an extremely intense or irresistible way

Examples

The smell of fresh bread was overpoweringly tempting as she walked past the bakery.
He found the crowd's enthusiasm overpoweringly infectious, making him want to join in.
The perfume was overpoweringly strong, filling the entire room within minutes.

Synset Examples

the candy looked overwhelmingly desirable to the dieting man

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overwhelmingly - in a way that is very great or intense

Etymology

Formed from 'overpowering' + '-ly'.
