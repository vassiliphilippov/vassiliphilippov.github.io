Daba

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑːbə / (AmE), / ˈdɑːbə / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in regions south of Lake Chad, particularly in parts of northern Cameroon
a Chadic language of northern Cameroon

Examples

The Daba language is spoken by several communities in northern Cameroon.
Linguists are studying word formation in Daba to understand its structure.
He grew up bilingual, learning both French and Daba as a child.
