single-minded

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɪŋɡəlˈmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋɡəlˈmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
Focusing all your attention and efforts on one particular purpose or goal; determined and unwavering.
firmly focused on one goal or purpose

Examples

Her single-minded pursuit of excellence inspired everyone on the team.
Despite the obstacles, he remained single-minded in achieving his goals.
She approached every challenge with a single-minded determination to succeed.

Synset Examples

she was firmly resolved to be a doctor
single-minded in his determination to stop smoking

Related Terms

  • Synonym: determined - having made a firm decision and being resolved not to change it
  • Antonym: indecisive - not able to make decisions quickly or effectively

Etymology

Formed from 'single' + 'minded,' meaning having a single (one) mind toward something.

2. ADJ

/ ˈsɪŋɡəlˌmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl ˌmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
having or showing a single aim or purpose and being very determined to achieve it; focused and unwavering
focused and determined on one goal

Examples

Her single-minded dedication to environmental causes has inspired many people.
It takes a single-minded approach to train for a marathon successfully.
Despite criticism, he remained single-minded in his pursuit of his dreams.

Synset Examples

She is single-minded in her pursuit of success.
To win, you must be single-minded in your efforts.
His single-minded focus on the project impressed everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: determined - having made a firm decision and being resolved not to change it
  • Synonym: focussed - having or showing concentration on a particular task or goal
  • Antonym: distracted - unable to concentrate because one's mind is preoccupied

Etymology

Formed from 'single' (meaning one) + 'minded' (having a specified kind of mind or disposition); first recorded in the 17th century.
