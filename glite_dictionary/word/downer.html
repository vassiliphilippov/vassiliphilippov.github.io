downer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaʊnɚ / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnə / (BrE)
A drug that has a sedative effect, reducing excitement or anxiety.
sedative drug
slang

Examples

He took a downer to help him relax after a stressful day.
The doctor warned her about the dangers of mixing downers with alcohol.
Back in the 1970s, downers were commonly prescribed for anxiety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sedative - A drug promoting calm or sleep
  • Antonym: upper - A drug that increases energy and alertness
