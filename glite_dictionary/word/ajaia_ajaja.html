Ajaia ajaja

1. NOUN

/ əˈdʒaɪ.ə əˈdʒaɪ.ə / (AmE), / əˈdʒaɪ.ə əˈdʒaɪ.ə / (BrE)
A tropical American spoonbill with rose-colored plumage, known for its distinctive spatula-shaped bill.
roseate spoonbill species

Examples

The Ajaia ajaja can often be seen wading in shallow waters throughout South and Central America.
Due to habitat loss, conservationists closely monitor populations of Ajaia ajaja in the wild.
The distinctive pink feathers make Ajaia ajaja a favorite among birdwatchers and photographers alike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roseate spoonbill - A tropical American wading bird with pink plumage and a spatula-shaped bill

Etymology

From Latin, established as the scientific binomial for the roseate spoonbill.
