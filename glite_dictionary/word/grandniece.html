grandniece

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrændˌnis / (AmE), / ˈɡrændˌniːs / (BrE)
The daughter of one's niece or nephew.
niece's or nephew's daughter

Examples

My grandniece will be visiting us for the holidays next week.
She often sends birthday cards to her grandniece and grandnephew every year.
After her nephew had a daughter, she loved being called a grandniece by the family.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: niece - a daughter of one's brother or sister
  • Synonym: great-niece - a daughter of one's niece or nephew (slightly less common term for grandniece)
  • Synonym: grandnephew - son of one's niece or nephew

Etymology

Formed from 'grand-' (denoting a generation removed) and 'niece', following the pattern of words like 'granddaughter'.
