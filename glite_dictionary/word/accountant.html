accountant

1. NOUN

/ əˈkaʊn.tənt / (AmE), / əˈkaʊn.tənt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to keep or inspect financial accounts for individuals, companies, or organizations
person who manages financial accounts

Examples

She hired an accountant to prepare her annual tax returns.
As a certified accountant, he managed the company's financial statements.
The accountant discovered a discrepancy in the balance sheet during the audit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bookkeeper - a person who records the financial affairs of a business

Etymology

From Old French *acompte* (account), influenced by 'account' + '-ant' (agent noun suffix).
