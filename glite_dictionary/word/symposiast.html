symposiast

1. NOUN

/ sɪmˈpoʊziˌæst / (AmE), / sɪmˈpəʊziːæst / (BrE)
a person who participates in a symposium, especially as a speaker or discussant
participant in a symposium

Examples

Each symposiast contributed unique insights during the academic conference.
As a seasoned symposiast, she was invited to speak at international events.
The discussion grew lively as every symposiast shared their perspective on the topic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: panelist - a person who takes part in a panel discussion

Etymology

From symposium + -ist; ultimately from Greek 'symposion' (drinking party, discussion) and '-ist' (person associated with).
