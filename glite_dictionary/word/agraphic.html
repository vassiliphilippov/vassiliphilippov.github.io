agraphic

1. ADJ

/ əˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / əˈɡrafɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or affected by agraphia; unable to write due to brain injury or illness.
relating to inability to write
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed as agraphic following his stroke.

Her sudden agraphic state made communication with her family difficult.

Related Terms

Etymology

From a- (not, without) + Greek 'graphē' (writing).
