mathematician

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæθəməˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmæθəməˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who is highly skilled in or studies mathematics, especially as a profession
person skilled in mathematics

Examples

She became a renowned mathematician after publishing several ground-breaking theories.
The young mathematician presented his research at the international conference.
Many mathematicians collaborate across the world to solve complex problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mathematics - the abstract science of number, quantity, and space

Etymology

From French *mathématicien*, based on *mathématique* (mathematics), from Latin *mathematicus*.
