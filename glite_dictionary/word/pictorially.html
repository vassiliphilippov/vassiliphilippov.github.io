pictorially

1. ADV

/ pɪkˈtɔriəli / (AmE), / pɪkˈtɔːriəli / (BrE)
in a way that is related to or represented by pictures; in a visual or illustrative manner
in a pictorial or visual manner

Examples

The artist explained her concept pictorially rather than with words.
The process can be described pictorially using diagrams in the manual.
Complex data is often presented pictorially to make it easier to understand.

Synset Examples

depth is established pictorially

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visually - in a way that relates to seeing or sight
  • Antonym: verbally - in a way that uses words, not images

Etymology

From 'pictorial' + '-ly', formed from Latin 'pictorius' (of painting).
