strapless

1. ADJ

/ ˈstræpləs / (AmE), / ˈstræpləs / (BrE)
(of an item of clothing) having no straps over the shoulders.
having no straps (esp. of clothing)

Examples

She wore a beautiful strapless dress to the gala.
The boutique featured a wide selection of strapless gowns for the summer season.
A strapless top is perfect for hot weather.

Synset Examples

a strapless evening gown

Etymology

Formed from 'strap' + '-less' (without).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02361028-s

2. NOUN

/ ˈstræpləs / (AmE), / ˈstræpləs / (BrE)
a woman's garment, especially a dress or top, that is designed to be worn without shoulder straps and leaves the shoulders uncovered.
shoulderless woman's garment

Examples

She looked stunning in a black strapless at the gala last night.
The boutique sells a wide variety of summer straplesses and sundresses.
Many brides choose a strapless for their wedding ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tube top - a strapless, tight-fitting women's top
