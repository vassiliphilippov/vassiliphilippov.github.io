lawmaking

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔːˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɔːˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of making or enacting laws, especially by a legislative body
the process of creating laws

Examples

Effective lawmaking requires careful debate and public input.
The senator played a major role in the lawmaking process.
Many challenges arise during lawmaking, including political disagreements and lobbying efforts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legislation - the process of making or enacting laws
