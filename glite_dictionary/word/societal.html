societal

1. ADJ

/ səˈsaɪətl̩ / (AmE), / səˈsaɪətl / (BrE)
relating to human society or its organization and members
relating to human society

Examples

The conference focused on societal challenges such as inequality and climate change.
Her research investigates societal attitudes toward mental health.
Rapid societal change can cause stress and uncertainty among citizens.

Synset Examples

social institutions
societal evolution
societal forces
social legislation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: social - relating to society or organization of society; often broader or less formal than 'societal'

Etymology

From society + -al, formed in the 19th century, modeled after similar adjectival forms.
