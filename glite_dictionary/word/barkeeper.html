barkeeper

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːrˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to mix and serve alcoholic drinks at a bar
person who serves drinks at a bar

Examples

The barkeeper wiped down the counter and greeted the customers with a friendly smile.
After the last call, the barkeeper began to close up the bar for the night.
She asked the barkeeper for a recommendation on a local craft beer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bartender - a person who serves drinks at a bar

Etymology

From 'bar' (a place where drinks are served) and 'keeper' (one who manages or tends).
