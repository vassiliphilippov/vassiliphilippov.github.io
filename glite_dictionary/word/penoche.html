penoche

1. NOUN

/ pəˈnoʊtʃi / (AmE), / pəˈnəʊtʃi / (BrE)
A type of fudge made with brown sugar, butter, milk, and often nuts.
a kind of brown sugar fudge with nuts
american

Examples

My grandmother always made penoche for the holidays, using her secret recipe.
Would you like some penoche with walnuts for dessert tonight?
They sold homemade penoche at the church bake sale.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: penuche - fudge made with brown sugar, similar to penoche

Etymology

From Mexican Spanish *panocha* (a kind of raw sugar, or coarse sugar).
