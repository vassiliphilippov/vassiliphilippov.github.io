Satanic

1. ADJ

/ seɪˈtænɪk / (AmE), / səˈtanɪk / (BrE)
of, relating to, or characteristic of Satan or evil inspired by Satan
relating to Satan or characteristic of evil

Examples

The novel was accused of containing Satanic symbols that outraged some religious groups.
He believed the old mansion was the site of Satanic rituals in the past.
Some claimed that music with Satanic messages could influence impressionable listeners.

Synset Examples

Satanic verses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diabolic - of or pertaining to the devil; extremely evil
  • Antonym: angelic - of or relating to angels; exceptionally good or innocent

Etymology

From Medieval Latin Satanicus, from Satan (the adversary) in Hebrew.
