small-townish

1. ADJ

/ ˈsmɔlˌtaʊnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːlˌtaʊnɪʃ / (BrE)
having the qualities or atmosphere typical of a small town, especially in being friendly, close-knit, or old-fashioned
characteristic of a small town; friendly or close-knit

Examples

The town had a small-townish charm that made visitors feel at home.
Her small-townish manners were refreshing in the crowded city.
The restaurant's decor created a small-townish and inviting atmosphere.

Synset Examples

The community felt small-townish, friendly and welcoming.
They appreciated the small-townish atmosphere of the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: provincial - having the characteristics of people from the provinces; narrow-minded or unsophisticated
