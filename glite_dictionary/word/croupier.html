croupier

1. NOUN

/ ˈkruːpiər / (AmE), / ˈkruːpiə / (BrE)
a person in a casino who collects and pays bets at a gaming table
casino dealer at gaming tables

Examples

The croupier spun the roulette wheel and announced the winning number.
As a skilled croupier, she handled the cards with remarkable speed and accuracy.
The croupier paid out the chips to the lucky winner at the blackjack table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dealer - person who deals cards at a gambling table

Etymology

From French *croupier* (one who stands behind another’s chair at a gaming table).
