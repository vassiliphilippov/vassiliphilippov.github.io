langlaufer

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑŋˌlaʊfər / (AmE), / ˈlaŋˌlaufə / (BrE)
A person who engages in cross-country skiing.
cross-country skier

Examples

The experienced langlaufer navigated the snowy trails with ease and confidence.
Each langlaufer wore specialized skis designed for speed and endurance over flat terrain.
After training for months, she became a skilled langlaufer and competed in several local races.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cross-country skier - a person who skis over long distances on level ground or gentle slopes

Etymology

From German 'Langläufer', meaning 'long runner' (from 'lang' meaning 'long' and 'laufen' meaning 'to run').
