unpermed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpɜrmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɜːmd / (BrE)
(of hair) not having been given a permanent wave or curl by chemical treatment.
not having had a permanent wave (hair)

Examples

She preferred to keep her hair unpermed and natural-looking.
The salon offers styles for both unpermed and permed hair types.
Her unpermed hair shone in the sunlight.

Synset Examples

smooth glossy unpermed hair

Related Terms

  • Antonym: permed - (of hair) having been given a permanent wave or curl by chemical treatment

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'permed', from 'perm', a chemical treatment for hair.
