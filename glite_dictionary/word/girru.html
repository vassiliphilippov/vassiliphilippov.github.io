Girru

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡɪru / (AmE), / ˈɡɪru / (BrE)
the Babylonian god of fire, often invoked in incantations against sorcery
Babylonian god of fire

Examples

Ancient priests called upon Girru to protect them from evil magic.
In Babylonian mythology, Girru is closely associated with fire and purification.
The tablet contained a prayer addressed to Girru for deliverance from curses.
