cristal

1. NOUN

/ krɪˈstɑl / (AmE), / krɪˈstɑːl / (BrE)
A street name for methylenedioxymethamphetamine (MDMA), especially when referring to the crystalline form of the drug.
slang for MDMA (crystal form)
slang

Examples

The dealer offered several party drugs, including cristal and ecstasy tablets.
Police seized a large amount of cristal during the nightclub raid.
Some people mistakenly believe cristal is purer than other forms of MDMA.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecstasy - another street name for MDMA
  • Synonym: molly - another slang term for MDMA

Etymology

From Spanish 'cristal' (crystal), referring to the drug's appearance.
