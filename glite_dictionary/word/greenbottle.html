greenbottle

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrinˌbɑtəl / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnbɒtəl / (BrE)
a common blowfly with a metallic, coppery green body, often found around decaying matter
a metallic green blowfly

Examples

A greenbottle buzzed loudly as it landed on the windowsill.
The garden was swarming with greenbottles attracted by the rubbish bin.
You can easily spot a greenbottle by its shiny, metallic green coloring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blowfly - any of several flies, typically metallic in color, that lay eggs in meat or carrion
