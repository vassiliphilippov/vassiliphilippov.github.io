stingless

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɪŋləs / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋləs / (BrE)
(of an insect or animal) lacking a sting or not capable of stinging.
lacking a sting

Examples

The stingless bee produces honey and is harmless to humans.
Researchers observed stingless wasps pollinating several plant species in the rainforest.
He prefers keeping stingless insects in his collection due to their gentle nature.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stinging - having a sting or able to sting
