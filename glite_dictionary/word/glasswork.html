glasswork

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlæsˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsˌwɜːk / (BrE)
an object or item made of glass, especially used as tableware such as dishes, goblets, or bowls
glass tableware or glass item

Examples

The cabinet was filled with delicate glasswork collected from different countries.
She admired the fine glasswork set out for the dinner party.
Please handle the antique glasswork with care while washing it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glassware - objects made of glass, especially tableware and ornaments

Etymology

From 'glass' + 'work', denoting something made or crafted from glass.
