aphorism

1. NOUN

/ ˈæf.əˌrɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈæf.ər.ɪ.zəm / (BrE)
A short, clever saying that expresses a general truth or principle.
short saying expressing a general truth

Examples

The book is filled with memorable aphorisms about life and happiness.
One famous aphorism states, "Actions speak louder than words."
Oscar Wilde was known for his witty aphorisms that still resonate today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maxim - a short statement expressing a general rule of conduct
  • Synonym: adage - a traditional proverb or wise saying
  • Synonym: proverb - a simple, traditional saying expressing a perceived truth

Etymology

From Greek 'aphorismos' (definition, distinction).
