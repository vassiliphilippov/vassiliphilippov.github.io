Mayakovski

1. PROPN

/ maɪəˈkɔvski / (AmE), / mʌɪəˈkɒfski / (BrE)
Vladimir Mayakovski (1893–1930), Soviet poet and leading figure of Russian futurism.
Soviet futurist poet (1893–1930)

Examples

Many regard Mayakovski as one of the most influential poets of the Russian avant-garde.
The play featured works by Mayakovski and his contemporaries.
During his studies, he became fascinated by the poetry of Mayakovski.

Etymology

Russian surname, from Владимир Маяковский (Vladimir Mayakovsky).
