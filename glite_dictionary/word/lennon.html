Lennon

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɛnən / (AmE), / ˈlɛnən / (BrE)
John Lennon (1940–1980), English musician, songwriter, and co-founder of the Beatles, renowned for his influential work in music and culture.
English musician and member of the Beatles

Examples

Many fans consider Lennon's solo work to be as impactful as his time with the Beatles.
The partnership between McCartney and Lennon produced some of the greatest songs in pop history.
A peace activist, Lennon is remembered for both his music and his influence on culture.
