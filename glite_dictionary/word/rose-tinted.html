rose-tinted

1. ADJ

/ roʊz ˈtɪntɪd / (AmE), / rəʊz ˈtɪntɪd / (BrE)
Having a color tinged with rose; slightly pink in hue.
slightly pink in color

Examples

The evening sky had a soft, rose-tinted glow just before sunset.
She wore rose-tinted glasses that matched her cheerful mood.
The artist chose a rose-tinted palette for her latest series of portraits.
