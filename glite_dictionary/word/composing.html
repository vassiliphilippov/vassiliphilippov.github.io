composing

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpoʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / kəmˈpəʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of creating music, especially original pieces
musical creation process

Examples

She dedicated most of her life to composing and teaching aspiring musicians.
The art of composing requires both creativity and discipline.
Many great works of classical music were the result of years spent composing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: composition - a musical work or the process of composing music
  • Antonym: performance - the act of presenting music to an audience
