annunciatory

1. ADJ

/ əˈnʌn.si.əˌtɔr.i / (AmE), / əˈnʌn.si.ə.tər.i / (BrE)
Relating to announcing or the act of making an announcement.
relating to announcing

Examples

The trumpet's sound had an annunciatory tone, signaling the beginning of the ceremony.
She entered with an annunciatory air, as if about to proclaim some important news.
The press release was written in an annunciatory style, full of excitement and expectation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: announcement - a formal public statement
  • Synonym: declaratory - serving to declare or make known
  • Antonym: unannounced - not made public or declared

Etymology

From Latin 'annuntiare' (to announce), via Late Latin 'annuntiatorius'.
