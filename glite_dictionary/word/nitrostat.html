Nitrostat

1. PROPN

/ ˈnaɪtroʊˌstæt / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtrəʊˌstæt / (BrE)
A trade name for nitroglycerin, used as a coronary vasodilator in the treatment of angina pectoris.
brand of nitroglycerin for angina treatment
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Nitrostat tablets to manage his angina attacks.
She always carries her Nitrostat in case of sudden chest pain.
After taking Nitrostat, he felt relief from the tightness in his chest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nitroglycerin - a drug used to treat angina pectoris

2. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪtroʊˌstæt / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtrəʊˌstæt / (BrE)
A proprietary name for nitroglycerin, especially used as a medication to treat heart conditions such as angina; also used in explosives manufacturing.
brand of nitroglycerin (medication and explosive)

Examples

The doctor prescribed Nitrostat tablets to help control his angina attacks.
Paramedics quickly administered Nitrostat during the emergency to relieve chest pain.
If you experience chest discomfort, place a Nitrostat tablet under your tongue immediately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nitroglycerin - a heavy yellow poisonous oily explosive liquid used in explosives and as a medication
