Goteborg

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡuːtəˌbɜːrɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːtəˌbɔːɡ / (BrE)
a port city in southwestern Sweden, the country's second largest city and a major maritime and industrial center.
city in southwestern Sweden

Examples

During their trip to Sweden, they visited Goteborg to explore its famous canals and vibrant food scene.
The headquarters of several major companies are located in Goteborg.
Goteborg is known for its lively cultural festivals and maritime heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gothenburg - alternative spelling/anglicized form of Goteborg, used in English

Etymology

From Swedish Göteborg, named after the Göta River and borg meaning 'fortress'.
