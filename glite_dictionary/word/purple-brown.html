purple-brown

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝːpəlˌbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈpɜːplˌbraʊn / (BrE)
Of a brown color tinged with purple; having a purplish-brown hue.
brown with a hint of purple

Examples

The artist used a purple-brown paint to give depth to the forest shadows.
She wore a purple-brown scarf that complemented her autumn coat.
The leaves turned a distinctive purple-brown shade as the season changed.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: brown - a color midway between black and yellow; having a dark color like earth or wood
  • Degree Higher: purple - a color intermediate between red and blue
