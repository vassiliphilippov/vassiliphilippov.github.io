Ignacy Jan Paderewski

1. PROPN

/ ɪgˈnɑtsi jɑn ˌpædəˈrɛfski / (AmE), / ɪgˈnɑːtsi jan ˌpadəˈrɛfski / (BrE)
(1860–1941) A Polish pianist, composer, and politician who served as the first Prime Minister of independent Poland in 1919.
Polish pianist and first Prime Minister of Poland

Examples

Ignacy Jan Paderewski was celebrated for both his musical genius and his political achievements.
The concert hall displayed a portrait of Ignacy Jan Paderewski in honor of his visit a century ago.
Many people in Poland regard Ignacy Jan Paderewski as a national hero.
