Bureau of Intelligence and Research

1. PROPN

/ bjʊˈroʊ əv ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns ənd rɪˈsɜrtʃ / (AmE), / bjʊəˈrəʊ əv ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns ənd rɪˈsɜːtʃ / (BrE)
an agency within the U.S. Department of State that provides analyses of global developments and acts as a focal point for intelligence policy and activities
U.S. State Department intelligence agency

Examples

The Bureau of Intelligence and Research provided the State Department with updated analysis on security threats.
Diplomats often consult reports from the Bureau of Intelligence and Research before major international negotiations.
The director of the Bureau of Intelligence and Research testified before Congress on recent foreign developments.
