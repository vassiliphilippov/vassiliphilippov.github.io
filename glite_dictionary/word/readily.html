readily

1. ADV

/ ˈrɛdəli / (AmE), / ˈrɛdəli / (BrE)
without difficulty or delay; easily and quickly
easily, without difficulty

Examples

The instructions were readily understood by everyone in the class.
Many ingredients for this recipe are readily available at your local supermarket.
She readily agreed to help organize the charity event.

Synset Examples

these snakes can be identified readily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: easily - without difficulty; with little effort
  • Synonym: quickly - at a fast speed; rapidly

Etymology

From Middle English redily, from Old English rǣd (ready) + -ly.
