loadstar

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊdˌstɑr / (AmE), / ˈləʊdˌstɑː / (BrE)
a guiding star; a star used as a reference point in navigation or astronomy
guiding star for navigation

Examples

Sailors in ancient times relied on the loadstar to steer their ships across vast oceans.
The North Star has long been considered the most important loadstar for navigators.
He spoke poetically of his mentor as the loadstar guiding his career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lodestar - a star that leads or guides, especially the North Star

Etymology

Variant of 'lodestar,' from Middle English 'lode sterre' (leading star).
