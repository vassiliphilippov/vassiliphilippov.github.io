hematocolpometra

1. NOUN

/ ˌhiːməˌtɑːlpoʊˈmiːtrə / (AmE), / ˌhiːmətəʊlpəʊˈmiːtrə / (BrE)
accumulation of blood in both the vagina and the uterus, usually due to an obstruction
accumulation of blood in vagina and uterus
medical

Examples

The ultrasound revealed hematocolpometra caused by an imperforate hymen.
Treatment of hematocolpometra often involves surgical intervention to relieve the obstruction.
Doctors diagnosed the adolescent girl with hematocolpometra after noting her symptoms and medical history.

Etymology

From Greek *haima* (blood), *kolpos* (vagina), and *metra* (uterus).
