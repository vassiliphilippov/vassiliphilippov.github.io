bridal-wreath

1. NOUN

/ ˈbraɪdəl ˌriθ / (AmE), / ˈbraɪdl ˌriːθ / (BrE)
A Chilean evergreen shrub with delicate spikes of small white flowers, often grown as an ornamental plant.
Chilean evergreen shrub with white flowers

Examples

The bridal-wreath in our garden blooms beautifully every spring.
She chose bridal-wreath branches for her wedding decorations.
A mature bridal-wreath can add elegance to any landscape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiraea - A genus of shrubs, some of which are called bridal-wreath because of their white, cascading flowers
