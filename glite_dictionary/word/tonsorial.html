tonsorial

1. ADJ

/ tɑnˈsɔriəl / (AmE), / tɒnˈsɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to barbers or the work of cutting hair.
relating to barbers or haircutting

Examples

The shop’s elegant décor reflected its long tonsorial tradition.
He takes great pride in his tonsorial skills as a barber.
The town’s oldest tonsorial establishment closed after a century in business.

Synset Examples

tonsorial work
tonsorial parlor

Etymology

From Latin tonsorius (of or relating to shearing or shaving), from tondere (to shear, to shave).
