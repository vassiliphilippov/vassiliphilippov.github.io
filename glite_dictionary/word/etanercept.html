etanercept

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈtænərˌsɛpt / (AmE), / ɪˈtanəsɛpt / (BrE)
a genetically engineered drug that blocks tumor necrosis factor (TNF), used by injection to treat autoimmune conditions such as rheumatoid arthritis
a biologic drug used to treat autoimmune diseases
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed etanercept to help manage my rheumatoid arthritis symptoms.
The clinical trial showed that etanercept significantly reduced joint inflammation.
Patients receiving etanercept should be monitored for possible infections due to its immune-suppressing effects.
