overproud

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvɚˈpraʊd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈpraʊd / (BrE)
Excessively proud; having or showing too much pride.
excessively proud

Examples

Her overproud attitude made it hard for her to accept advice from others.
The family was so overproud that they looked down on their neighbors.
Being overproud can blind you to your own faults.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: proud - feeling deep pleasure or satisfaction as a result of one's achievements
  • Degree Higher: arrogant - having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities

Etymology

Formed from 'over-' meaning 'excessive' + 'proud'.
