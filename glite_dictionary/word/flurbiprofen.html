flurbiprofen

1. NOUN

/ flɝːbɪˈproʊfən / (AmE), / flɜːbɪˈprəʊfən / (BrE)
A nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used to reduce inflammation and pain, typically administered orally; also known by the trade name Ansaid.
a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug
medical

The doctor prescribed flurbiprofen to help manage his arthritis pain.
