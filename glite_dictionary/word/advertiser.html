advertiser

1. NOUN

/ ˈæd.vər.taɪ.zər / (AmE), / ədˈvɜː.tɪ.zə / (BrE)
a person or organization whose business is to advertise products, services, or events, often in various media
person or company in the advertising business

Examples

The advertiser launched a nationwide campaign to promote the new product.
Many advertisers use social media platforms to reach younger audiences.
As an advertiser, she always seeks creative ways to engage customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marketer - a person or company that markets or promotes products
  • Degree Lower: publisher - a person or company that prepares and issues material for sale, such as books or magazines

Etymology

From advertise + -er, formed in English.

2. NOUN

/ ˈæd.vɚˌtaɪ.zɚ / (AmE), / ˈad.və.taɪ.zə / (BrE)
a person or organization whose business is to promote products, services, or events to the public, especially through various media
person or company that advertises

Examples

The company is a leading advertiser in national newspapers and magazines.
Major advertisers are always looking for new ways to reach their audience.
As an advertiser, she manages several high-profile marketing campaigns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marketer - a person or company that promotes or sells products or services
  • Synonym: publisher - a person or company that prepares and issues material for public distribution, often including advertisements

Etymology

From advertise + -er, meaning 'one who advertises'.

Synset POS: n
