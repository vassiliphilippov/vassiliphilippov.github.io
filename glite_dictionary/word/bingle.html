bingle

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈbɪŋɡəl / (BrE)
(Baseball) A base hit on which the batter stops safely at first base; a single.
base hit (single) in baseball

Examples

The rookie hit a bingle to left field in his first major league at-bat.
With two outs, she managed to slap a bingle past the shortstop.
His third bingle of the night helped his team take the lead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: single - A base hit allowing the batter to reach first base
