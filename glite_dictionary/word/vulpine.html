vulpine

1. ADJ

/ ˈvʌl.paɪn / (AmE), / ˈvʌl.paɪn / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a fox, especially in cunning or slyness
fox-like; cunning or sly

Examples

Her vulpine smile suggested she was plotting something mischievous.
The company's vulpine tactics helped them outsmart their competitors.
His vulpine features and quick movements reminded everyone of a sly fox.

Synset Examples

vulpine cunning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foxy - resembling a fox; cunning or sly

Etymology

From Latin 'vulpinus', meaning 'of a fox', from 'vulpes' (fox).
