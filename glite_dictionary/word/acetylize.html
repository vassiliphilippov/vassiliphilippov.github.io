to acetylize

1. VERB

/ əˈsiːtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / əˈsiːtɪlaɪz / (BrE)
to introduce an acetyl group into (a chemical compound)
add an acetyl group (in chemistry)
medical

Examples

Researchers acetylized the compound to observe changes in its biological activity.
The laboratory technician plans to acetylize the substance during the next phase of the experiment.
Through repeated tests, the team successfully acetylized various organic molecules.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to acetylate - to introduce an acetyl group into (a chemical compound)

Etymology

From acetyl + -ize, modeled after similar chemical terminology.
