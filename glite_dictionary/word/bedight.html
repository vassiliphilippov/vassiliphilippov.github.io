to bedight

1. VERB

/ bɪˈdaɪt / (AmE), / bɪˈdaɪt / (BrE)
(archaic) To decorate or adorn; to dress up with ornaments.
decorate; adorn (archaic)
archaic

Examples

The hall was bedight with garlands for the king's arrival.
She appeared at the festival, bedight in silks and jewels.
The ancient tales speak of knights bedight in shining armor.

Synset Examples

deck the halls with holly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to decorate - to make something look more attractive by adding items or color
  • Synonym: to adorn - to make something or someone look more attractive

Etymology

From Middle English 'bedighten', from be- (intensive prefix) + dight (to set in order, adorn).
