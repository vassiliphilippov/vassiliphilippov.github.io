to tabularize

1. VERB

/ ˈtæbjələrˌaɪz / (AmE), / ˈtabjʊləˌrʌɪz / (BrE)
to arrange or enter information in tabular (table) form
arrange in table form

Examples

She decided to tabularize the survey responses for easier analysis.
The data must be tabularized before you can import it into the database.
He spent the morning tabularizing the results of the experiment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to tabulate - to arrange data in a table or list

Etymology

From 'tabular' + '-ize', indicating to make into a table format.
