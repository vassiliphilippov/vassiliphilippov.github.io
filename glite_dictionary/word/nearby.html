nearby

1. ADV

/ ˌnɪrˈbaɪ / (AmE), / ˌnɪəˈbaɪ / (BrE)
not far away; at or to a short distance from the place referred to.
not far away; close by

Examples

She found a nice café nearby after work.
If you need anything, I’ll be nearby all day.
There’s a park nearby where we can have a picnic.

Synset Examples

she works nearby
the planets orbiting nearby are Venus and Mars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: close - at or within a short distance
Source: WordNet

Synset POS: r

2. ADJ

/ ˈnɪrˌbaɪ / (AmE), / ˈnɪəbaɪ / (BrE)
Not far away; close in distance or position.
close in distance

Examples

There is a nearby café where we can have lunch.
She heard a strange noise coming from a nearby building.
Many students who attend the university live in nearby neighborhoods.

Synset Examples

the nearby towns
concentrated his study on the nearby planet Venus

Related Terms

  • Antonym: distant - far away in space or time
  • Synonym: close - near in distance or relationship
