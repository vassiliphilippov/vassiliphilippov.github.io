motionlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈmoʊʃənləsli / (AmE), / ˈməʊʃənləsli / (BrE)
In a way that does not involve any movement; without moving.
without movement; without moving

Examples

The deer stood motionlessly as the hikers passed by.
She waited motionlessly for her name to be called, barely even breathing.
The cat watched the bird motionlessly from its hiding place.

Synset Examples

he saw the black-haired man waiting motionlessly behind the opposite side of the platform

Related Terms

  • Synonym: still - not moving; motionless
  • Synonym: frozen - not able to move; motionless

Etymology

From 'motionless' (without movement) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
