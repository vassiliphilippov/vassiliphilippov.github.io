mayhap

1. ADV

/ meɪˈhæp / (AmE), / meɪˈhap / (BrE)
Perhaps; possibly; by chance (used especially in archaic or literary contexts).
perhaps; possibly (archaic)
archaic

Examples

Mayhap he will return before nightfall, but no one can be certain.
She looked out the window, thinking mayhap it would rain before morning.
The old book whispered secrets, mayhap remembered only by the wind.

Synset Examples

perhaps she will call tomorrow
we may possibly run into them at the concert
it may peradventure be thought that there never was such a time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perhaps - possibly; maybe
  • Synonym: maybe - possibly; perhaps

Etymology

Formed from 'may' + 'hap' (chance, luck); used from the 16th century.
