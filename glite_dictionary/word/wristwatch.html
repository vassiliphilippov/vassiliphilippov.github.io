wristwatch

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪstˌwɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɪstˌwɒtʃ / (BrE)
A small watch worn on a strap around the wrist.
watch worn on the wrist

Examples

He glanced at his wristwatch to check the time before leaving.
The wristwatch she received as a gift was both stylish and practical.
During the meeting, Tom kept looking at his wristwatch impatiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watch - a small timepiece worn on the person

Etymology

Formed from 'wrist' + 'watch', meaning a watch for the wrist.
