Dana

1. PROPN

/ ˈdeɪ.nə / (AmE), / ˈdɑː.nə / (BrE)
An ancient Celtic goddess regarded as the mother of the Tuatha De Danann, sometimes identified with the Welsh goddess Don.
Celtic mother goddess

Examples

Many legends speak of Dana as the mother of an entire divine race.
Some scholars believe Dana and the Welsh goddess Don are the same figure.
The Tuatha De Danann, children of Dana, are central to Irish mythology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Don - Welsh mother goddess, counterpart of Dana
