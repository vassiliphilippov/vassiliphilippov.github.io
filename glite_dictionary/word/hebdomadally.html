hebdomadally

1. ADV

/ hɛbˈdɑː.mə.dəli / (AmE), / hɛbˈdɒm.ə.dəli / (BrE)
Occurring every week; on a weekly basis.
every week; weekly

Examples

The team meets hebdomadally to discuss ongoing projects and progress.
Reports are required to be submitted hebdomadally by all staff members.
A newsletter is published hebdomadally and sent to all subscribers.

Synset Examples

she visited her aunt weekly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weekly - occurring once each week

Etymology

From Late Latin hebdomadalis (weekly), from Greek hebdomas (week).
