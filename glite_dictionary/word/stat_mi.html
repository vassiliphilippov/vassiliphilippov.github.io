stat mi

1. NOUN

/ stæt ˈmaɪl / (AmE), / stæt ˈmaɪl / (BrE)
an abbreviation for statute mile, a unit of length equal to 1,760 yards or 5,280 feet (exactly 1,609.344 meters)
abbreviation for statute mile; length unit (1,609.344 meters)
