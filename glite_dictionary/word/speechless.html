speechless

1. ADJ

/ ˈspiːtʃ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈspiːtʃ.ləs / (BrE)
unable to speak, especially temporarily, as a result of shock, surprise, or strong emotion
temporarily unable to speak

Examples

She was so surprised by the news that she was speechless for several minutes.
The breathtaking scenery left everyone in the group completely speechless.
He stood speechless with anger, unable to find the words to respond.

Synset Examples

struck dumb
speechless with shock

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dumb - unable to speak, often temporarily due to shock or emotion
  • Antonym: vocal - expressing oneself readily and clearly

Etymology

From Middle English specheles, from speche (speech) + -less (without).
