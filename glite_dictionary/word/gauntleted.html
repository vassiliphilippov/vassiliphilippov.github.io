gauntleted

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɔntlɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːntlɪtɪd / (BrE)
wearing or covered with a protective glove, especially one made of metal or leather
wearing a protective glove

Examples

The knight raised his gauntleted fist in a salute before the crowd.
She carefully examined the artifact with her gauntleted hands to avoid damaging it.
The guard signaled with a gauntleted hand for the visitors to stop.

Synset Examples

gestured with his gauntleted hand

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unclad - not wearing or covered with any clothing or protection

Etymology

From 'gauntlet' (protective glove) + '-ed', forming an adjective.
