crafter

1. NOUN

/ ˈkræftər / (AmE), / ˈkrɑːftə / (BrE)
a person who creates objects with skill in manual arts or crafts
skilled creator in manual arts

Examples

The handmade quilt was sewn by an experienced crafter.
At the fair, local crafters displayed their pottery and jewelry.
She is a talented crafter who specializes in woodwork and stained glass.

Synset Examples

the jewelry was made by internationally famous craftsmen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: craftsperson - a person who is skilled in a particular craft
  • Synonym: artisan - a worker skilled in a trade, especially one that involves making things by hand

Etymology

From 'craft' + agent suffix '-er'.
