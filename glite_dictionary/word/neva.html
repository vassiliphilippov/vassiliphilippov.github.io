Neva

1. PROPN

/ ˈneɪvə / (AmE), / ˈneɪvə / (BrE)
a river in northwestern Russia that flows west into the Gulf of Finland, passing through St. Petersburg.
river in northwestern Russia

Examples

The city of St. Petersburg is situated on the banks of the Neva.
During the summer, many tourists take boat tours on the Neva.
The Neva River is known for its impressive bridges and scenic views.
