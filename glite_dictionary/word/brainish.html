brainish

1. ADJ

/ ˈbreɪ.nɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbreɪ.nɪʃ / (BrE)
Acting hastily or impulsively without careful thought; rash or impetuous.
acting impulsively or rashly
archaic

Examples

His brainish reaction to criticism often landed him in trouble.
It's unwise to make such brainish investments without proper research.
The brainish prince charged into battle without a plan.

Synset Examples

a hotheaded decision
liable to such impulsive acts as hugging strangers
an impetuous display of spending and gambling
madcap escapade

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impetuous - acting or done quickly and without thought or care
  • Synonym: impulsive - acting or done without forethought
  • Antonym: deliberate - done consciously and intentionally; careful

Etymology

From 'brain' + '-ish', patterned after similar adjectives meaning prone to the qualities of the root noun.
