Windhoek

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪndˌhʊk, ˈvɪntˌhʊk / (AmE), / ˈwɪndˌhʊk, ˈvɪntˌhʊk / (BrE)
the capital city of Namibia, located in the central part of the country
capital city of Namibia

Examples

After living in Windhoek for a year, she fell in love with its vibrant culture.
Many international flights to Namibia arrive at Windhoek's main airport.
The conference will be held in Windhoek next month.

Etymology

From Afrikaans and Dutch, meaning 'wind corner' or 'windy corner'.
