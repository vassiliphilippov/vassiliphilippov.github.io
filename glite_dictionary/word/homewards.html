homewards

1. ADV

/ ˈhoʊm.wɚdz / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmwədz / (BrE)
towards home; in the direction of one's home.
towards home

Examples

After a long day at work, she walked homewards through the quiet streets.
The birds began their journey homewards as the sun started to set.
He glanced back once before heading homewards along the familiar path.

Synset Examples

fought his way homeward through the deep snow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homeward - toward home; in the direction of home (variant form)

Etymology

From 'homeward' + -s, following the pattern of directional adverbs like 'towards.'
