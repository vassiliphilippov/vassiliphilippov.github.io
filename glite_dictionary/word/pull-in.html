pull-in

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊl ɪn / (AmE), / ˈpʊl ɪn / (BrE)
a roadside café or rest stop, especially one serving lorry drivers
roadside café for lorry drivers
british

Examples

We stopped at a pull-in to grab some breakfast on the way north.
Most truck drivers know the best pull-in for a hot meal and a rest.
The old pull-in by the motorway has been turned into a convenience store.
