postmodernist

1. ADJ

/ ˌpoʊstˈmɑːdərnɪst / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈmɒdənɪst / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of postmodernism in art, architecture, literature, or philosophy
relating to postmodernism

Examples

The new museum's design is an example of postmodernist architecture.
Her theories reflect a distinctly postmodernist approach to literature.
Many critics debate the value of postmodernist art in contemporary culture.

Synset Examples

postmodernist architecture

Related Terms

  • Antonym: modernist - relating to modernism

Etymology

From postmodern + -ist, referring to those embracing or relating to postmodernism.
