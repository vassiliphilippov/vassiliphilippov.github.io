folderol

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊldəˌrɑl / (AmE), / ˈfɒldəˌrɒl / (BrE)
foolish or nonsensical talk or ideas; trivial or meaningless fuss
foolish or nonsensical talk

Examples

His explanation was dismissed as folderol by the skeptical audience.
The meeting was filled with so much folderol that nothing important was accomplished.
Let's skip the folderol and get straight to the point.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - speech or writing that has no meaning or makes no sense
  • Antonym: sense - logical or meaningful talk or writing

Etymology

Originally a nonsense refrain in 18th-century songs.
