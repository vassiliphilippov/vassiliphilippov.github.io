essentialness

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈsɛnʃəlnɪs / (AmE), / ɪˈsɛnʃəlnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being absolutely necessary or of fundamental importance
the quality of being essential

Examples

The essentialness of clean water cannot be overstated in public health discussions.
Many people overlook the essentialness of taking regular breaks while working.
His research revealed the essentialness of honesty in building lasting relationships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: essentiality - the quality of being essential
  • Antonym: inessentiality - the quality of not being essential

Etymology

Formed from 'essential' + '-ness', where 'essential' is from Latin 'essentialis' (pertaining to essence).
