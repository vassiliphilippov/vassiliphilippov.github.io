Versace

1. PROPN

/ vərˈsɑː.tʃeɪ / (AmE), / vɛəˈsɑː.tʃeɪ / (BrE)
Gianni Versace (1946–1997), an Italian fashion designer known for founding the Versace fashion house and influencing modern fashion with his bold designs.
Italian fashion designer (1946–1997)

Examples

Many celebrities have worn Versace on the red carpet at major award shows.
The legacy of Versace has greatly influenced luxury fashion worldwide.
She visited an exhibition dedicated to the life and work of Versace.
