familiarisation

1. NOUN

/ fəˌmɪljɚəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / fəˌmɪlɪəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or experience of becoming familiar with something
the process of becoming familiar
british

Examples

The training program included a familiarisation session with the new software.
Her quick familiarisation with the procedures impressed her supervisor.
The museum offers familiarisation tours for teachers before school visits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: familiarization - the American English spelling of 'familiarisation'

Etymology

From 'familiar' plus the verb-forming suffix '-ise' and the noun-forming suffix '-ation'.
