grillroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrɪlˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪlruːm / (BrE)
A restaurant or room in a restaurant where food, especially meat, is cooked on a grill and served.
restaurant where food is grilled

Examples

The hotel’s grillroom is famous for its perfectly cooked steaks and lively atmosphere.
We reserved a table in the grillroom for our anniversary dinner.
The new grillroom downtown offers a variety of meat and seafood dishes cooked over an open flame.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steakhouse - restaurant specializing in steak and grilled meats
  • Synonym: restaurant - a place where meals are served to customers

Etymology

From 'grill' (to cook over direct heat) + 'room'.
