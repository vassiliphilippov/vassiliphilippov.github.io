wine-coloured

1. ADJ

/ waɪnˈkʌlɚd / (AmE), / waɪnˈkʌləd / (BrE)
Of a deep red colour similar to that of red wine.
deep red like red wine

Examples

She wore a stunning wine-coloured dress to the party.
The living room was decorated with wine-coloured cushions and drapes.
He chose a wine-coloured paint for the accent wall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burgundy - a dark red color, named after Burgundy wine
