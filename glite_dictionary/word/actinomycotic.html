actinomycotic

relating to actinomycosis infection
medical

Examples

The patient presented with an actinomycotic lesion on the jaw.
Treatment of actinomycotic infections typically involves long-term antibiotics.
Researchers studied the spread of actinomycotic bacteria in cattle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: actinomycosis - a chronic bacterial infection caused by Actinomyces species

Etymology

From 'actinomycosis' (a type of infection) + '-ic' (forming adjectives).
