dweeb

1. NOUN

/ dwiːb / (AmE), / dwiːb / (BrE)
a person, especially a student, who is regarded as socially awkward, boringly studious, or unfashionable
a socially awkward or studious person (slang)
slang

Examples

Some people thought Alex was a dweeb because he spent so much time studying in the library.
Jenny used to be shy and a bit of a dweeb, but now she's much more confident.
The football players would tease the dweebs in their class, but many of them went on to great careers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nerd - a person who is socially awkward or very intellectual
  • Synonym: geek - a person who is enthusiastic about academic or technical pursuits, often lacking social skills
  • Antonym: cool - fashionable, impressive, or socially accepted

2. NOUN

/ dwiːb / (AmE), / dwiːb / (BrE)
a person regarded as socially inept, foolish, or awkward, often lacking in social skills or popularity
socially awkward or foolish person
informal slang

Examples

He always felt like a dweeb in high school because he was so shy.
Don't worry about making mistakes—no one will think you're a dweeb.
The movie hero started out as a total dweeb before transforming into someone confident.

Synset Examples

He felt like a dweeb for forgetting his friend's birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geek - a socially awkward or unattractive person
  • Synonym: nerd - someone who is very interested in technical subjects or intellectual pursuits, often seen as socially awkward
  • Antonym: cool - popular and admired, especially among peers
