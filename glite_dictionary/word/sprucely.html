sprucely

1. ADV

/ ˈspruːsli / (AmE), / ˈspruːsli / (BrE)
in a neat, tidy, and fashionable manner
in a neat or fashionable way

Examples

He always arrives sprucely dressed for the meeting.
The guests were sprucely attired for the formal dinner.
She decorated her office sprucely, giving it a modern and organized feel.

Synset Examples

He was smartly dressed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smartly - in a stylish or elegant manner
  • Antonym: shabbily - in a manner that is untidy or poorly dressed

Etymology

From "spruce" (neat, smart) + adverbial suffix -ly.
