stated

1. ADJ

/ ˈsteɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtɪd / (BrE)
expressed clearly or declared formally, often in writing or speech
clearly expressed or declared

Examples

His stated goal was to improve public education.
The stated rules of the competition were clear to everyone involved.
Despite her stated intentions, she did not follow through with her plans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: explicit - clearly and directly expressed, leaving no room for confusion
  • Antonym: implied - suggested but not directly stated

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'state', from Latin 'status' (standing, position).
