cognitive

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑːɡnətɪv / (AmE), / ˈkɒɡnɪtɪv / (BrE)
relating to the mental processes of perception, memory, judgment, and reasoning; connected with cognition.
relating to mental processes

Examples

Children go through important cognitive development stages as they grow.
The test was designed to assess cognitive abilities like memory and reasoning.
Researchers study cognitive processes to understand how people learn and solve problems.

Synset Examples

cognitive psychology
cognitive style

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intellectual - relating to the intellect or mental capacity

Etymology

From Latin cognitivus (pertaining to getting to know), from cognoscere (to know).
