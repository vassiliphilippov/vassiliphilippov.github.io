slantingly

1. ADV

/ ˈslæn.tɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈslɑːn.tɪŋ.li / (BrE)
in a manner that is slanted or not perpendicular; diagonally or at an incline
in a slanted or diagonal manner

Examples

The sunlight fell slantingly through the half-closed blinds, casting long shadows across the room.
He wrote his notes slantingly in the margin for emphasis.
Raindrops hit the window slantingly as the wind picked up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diagonally - in a diagonal direction or position
  • Antonym: perpendicularly - at a right angle to a surface or line
