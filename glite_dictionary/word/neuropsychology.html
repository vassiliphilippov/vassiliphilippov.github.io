neuropsychology

1. NOUN

/ ˌnʊroʊsaɪˈkɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊsaɪˈkɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of how the structure and function of the brain relate to psychological processes and behavior
study of brain-behavior relationships
medical

Examples

She decided to pursue a career in neuropsychology after studying the effects of brain injuries.
Recent advances in neuropsychology have improved our understanding of memory and learning disorders.
The conference featured experts in neuropsychology from universities around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychology - the scientific study of the mind and behavior
  • Synonym: neurology - the branch of medicine or biology dealing with the nervous system

Etymology

From Greek 'neuron' (nerve) + 'psychē' (mind, soul) + 'logia' (study).
