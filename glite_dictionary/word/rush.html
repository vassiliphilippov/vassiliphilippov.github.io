rush

1. NOUN

/ rʌʃ / (AmE), / rʌʃ / (BrE)
a sudden and forceful flow of something, especially of liquid, air, or emotion
sudden strong flow

Examples

She felt a rush of adrenaline as she stepped onto the stage.
There was a rush of cold air when he opened the door.

  • Synonym: surge - a sudden powerful forward or upward movement
