outspread

1. ADJ

/ aʊtˈsprɛd / (AmE), / aʊtˈsprɛd / (BrE)
fully extended or spread out, especially in width or area
fully extended or spread out

Examples

The eagle soared across the sky with its outspread wings.
She lay on the grass, arms outspread and eyes closed to the sun.
Maps were outspread across the desk as they planned their route.

Synset Examples

outspread wings
with arms spread wide

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spread - opened or extended over a surface

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'out-' and 'spread', meaning to extend outward.
