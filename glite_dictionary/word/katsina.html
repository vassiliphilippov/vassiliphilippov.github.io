Katsina

1. PROPN

/ kætˈsinə / (AmE), / kætˈsiːnə / (BrE)
a city in northern Nigeria, known as a major center of the Hausa people
city in northern Nigeria

Examples

Katsina has been a significant cultural and commercial hub for centuries.
During our travels in northern Nigeria, we spent several days in Katsina.
Many traditional festivals and markets take place in Katsina every year.
