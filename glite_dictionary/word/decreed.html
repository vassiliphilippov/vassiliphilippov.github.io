decreed

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈkriːd / (AmE), / dɪˈkriːd / (BrE)
fixed or established by an official order or command
established by order or command

Examples

The board met at the decreed hour to discuss the company's future.
The ritual was performed on the decreed date every year without exception.
His punishment was severe, as decreed by the council.

Synset Examples

at the time appointed (or the appointed time)

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appointed - fixed or set officially
  • Synonym: prescribed - set down as a rule or guide

Etymology

From the verb 'decree', from Old French 'decreer', from Latin 'decretum' (something decided).
