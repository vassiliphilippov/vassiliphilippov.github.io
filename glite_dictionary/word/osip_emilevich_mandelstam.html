Osip Emilevich Mandelstam

1. PROPN

/ ˈoʊsɪp ˌɛmɪˈleɪvɪtʃ ˈmændəlstɑm / (AmE), / ˈəʊsɪp ˌɛmɪˈleɪvɪtʃ ˈmændəlstam / (BrE)
(1891–1938) Russian poet and essayist, known for his influential contributions to 20th-century Russian literature and his persecution under Stalinist repression.
Russian poet and essayist (1891–1938)

Examples

Many literary critics praise Osip Emilevich Mandelstam for the depth and originality of his poetry.
Several of Osip Emilevich Mandelstam's poems subtly criticized the Soviet regime.
The tragic life and fate of Osip Emilevich Mandelstam have inspired generations of writers.
