gynecological

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡaɪnəˈkɑlədʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɡaɪnɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the branch of medicine dealing with the health of the female reproductive system.
relating to gynecology or women's reproductive health
medical

Examples

She scheduled her annual gynecological exam with the specialist.
Many hospitals offer gynecological services for women of all ages.
The gynecological conference presented new research on reproductive health.

Synset Examples

gynecological examination

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gynecology - the medical practice dealing with the health of the female reproductive system

Etymology

From gynecology + -ical; 'gynecology' from Greek gunē (woman) and -logia (study).
