departmental

1. ADJ

/ dɪˌpɑrtˈmɛntl̩ / (AmE), / dɪˌpɑːtˈmɛntl̩ / (BrE)
relating to or concerning a department within an organization, company, or institution.
relating to a department

Examples

All staff must follow the new departmental safety guidelines.
The university held a departmental meeting to discuss curriculum changes.
She was praised for her exceptional departmental leadership.

Synset Examples

departmental policy

Etymology

From department + -al, a suffix forming adjectives meaning 'relating to'.
