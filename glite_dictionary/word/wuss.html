wuss

1. NOUN

/ wʊs / (AmE), / wʊs / (BrE)
An informal term for a person who is weak, ineffectual, or lacking in courage.
weak or ineffectual person (informal)
informal slang

Examples

He didn't want to seem like a wuss by refusing to try the roller coaster.
She called him a wuss for not speaking up during the meeting.
Don't be such a wuss—just jump into the cold water!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wimp - a weak and cowardly or ineffectual person
  • Synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage
  • Antonym: brave - showing courage

Etymology

Probably a blend of 'wimp' and 'pussy' (slang); origin uncertain.
