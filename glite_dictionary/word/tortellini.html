tortellini

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɔrtəˈlini / (AmE), / ˌtɔːtəˈliːni / (BrE)
Small rings of pasta stuffed with a mixture, typically of meat, cheese, or vegetables.
small ring-shaped stuffed pasta

Examples

The restaurant's homemade tortellini is filled with spinach and ricotta cheese.
He ordered a bowl of tortellini in brodo for his starter.
You can find fresh tortellini in the refrigerated section of most supermarkets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ravioli - square or round pasta stuffed with filling

Etymology

Italian, plural of tortellino, diminutive of tortello (small cake), from torta (cake).
