impertinence

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈpɝːtənəns / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɜːtɪnəns / (BrE)
rude or disrespectful behavior; lack of respect or proper manners.
rude or disrespectful behavior

Examples

Her impertinence shocked everyone at the dinner table.
The student's impertinence towards the teacher was taken very seriously.
He was dismissed for his repeated acts of impertinence in the workplace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insolence - rude or disrespectful behavior
  • Antonym: politeness - showing good manners or respect for others

Etymology

From Latin 'impertinentia' (irrelevance, inappropriateness), from 'im-' (not) + 'pertinens' (pertinent).

playful or cheeky behavior that is considered inappropriate or disrespectful
inappropriate playfulness; cheeky behavior

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insolence - rude and disrespectful behavior
  • Antonym: respect - a feeling of deep admiration or regard for someone or something

Etymology

From Latin 'impertinentia' (irrelevance, unsuitable conduct), from 'im-' (not) + 'pertinens' (pertinent, relevant)

Related Terms
