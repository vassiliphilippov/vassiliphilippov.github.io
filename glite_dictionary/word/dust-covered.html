dust-covered

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʌstˌkʌvɚd / (AmE), / ˈdʌstˌkʌvəd / (BrE)
covered with a layer of dust.
covered with dust

Examples

The old attic was filled with dust-covered furniture and forgotten boxes.
She picked up a dust-covered photo frame and wiped it clean.
A dust-covered book lay unopened on the shelf for years.

Synset Examples

a dusty pile of books

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dusty - covered with or resembling dust
