Enrico Caruso

1. PROPN

/ ɛnˈrikoʊ kəˈruːsoʊ / (AmE), / ɛnˈriːkəʊ kəˈruːsəʊ / (BrE)
(1873–1921) A renowned Italian operatic tenor, celebrated as one of the greatest singers in the history of opera.
famous Italian operatic tenor

Examples

Enrico Caruso gained international fame for his powerful and expressive voice.
Many of Enrico Caruso's recordings are still admired by opera enthusiasts today.
The Metropolitan Opera considers Enrico Caruso one of its most legendary performers.
