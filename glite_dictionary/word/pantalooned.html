pantalooned

1. ADJ

/ ˌpæn.təˈlund / (AmE), / ˌpæn.təˈluːnd / (BrE)
Wearing or dressed in trousers.
wearing trousers

Examples

The old actor appeared pantalooned and ready for his role in the play.
She arrived at the masquerade extravagantly pantalooned in bright velvet fabric.
In the portrait, he is pantalooned and wearing a ruffled shirt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trousered - wearing trousers

Etymology

From 'pantaloon' (trousers), itself from Italian 'pantalone'.
