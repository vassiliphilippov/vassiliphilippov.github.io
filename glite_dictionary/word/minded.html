minded

1. ADJ

/ ˈmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˈmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
inclined or disposed to do something, especially when used with 'to' (e.g., 'not minded to reply').
inclined or disposed to do something

Examples

She was not minded to accept their offer, despite their insistence.
He is minded to leave early if the meeting runs too long.
I'm not minded to discuss this any further.

Synset Examples

he is apt to ignore matters he considers unimportant
I am not minded to answer any questions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inclined - having a tendency or preference to do something
  • Antonym: disinclined - not willing or disposed to do something
