ceremony

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / ˈsɛrɪməni / (BrE)
an act or series of acts performed in a formal, solemn, or elaborate way, often according to prescribed rules or customs
formal or elaborate act or procedure

Examples

He made a ceremony of tasting the wine before serving it to the guests.
The awards were presented with great ceremony and tradition at the annual banquet.
She signed the document without any ceremony or fuss.

Synset Examples

the ceremony of smelling the cork and tasting the wine
he makes a ceremony of addressing his golf ball
he disposed of it without ceremony

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ritual - an established or prescribed procedure for a ceremony
  • Synonym: formality - something done only for form's sake or according to custom
  • Antonym: informality - lack of ceremony or formality; casualness

Etymology

From Latin caerimonia (religious rite or ceremony).

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɛrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / ˈsɛrɪməni / (BrE)
formal acts or prescribed procedures performed on a solemn or important occasion, often following traditional rules or customs
formal acts for solemn occasion

Examples

The graduation ceremony was attended by students, families, and faculty.
He bowed during the award ceremony to show respect.
A traditional tea ceremony involves a series of precise steps and gestures.

Synset Examples

an inaugural ceremony

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ritual - a set of fixed actions and sometimes words performed regularly, especially as part of a ceremony

Etymology

From Latin caerimonia (religious rite, sacredness).

3. NOUN

/ ˈsɛrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / ˈsɛrɪməni / (BrE)
a formal event or act performed on a special occasion, often following a set of customs or traditions
formal event for a special occasion

Examples

The graduation ceremony was held outdoors this year due to the large number of attendees.
She wore traditional clothing to the wedding ceremony to honor her family's heritage.
A special ceremony was organized to welcome the new citizens to the country.

Synset Examples

a ceremony commemorating Pearl Harbor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ritual - a series of actions regularly and invariably followed, especially in ceremony
  • Synonym: celebration - a special event marking an important occasion

Etymology

From Latin 'caerimonia' (religious rite, ritual observance).
