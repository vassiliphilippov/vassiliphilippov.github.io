Morocco

1. PROPN

/ məˈrɑːkoʊ / (AmE), / məˈrɒkəʊ / (BrE)
A country in northwestern Africa, officially the Kingdom of Morocco, with a largely Muslim population; achieved independence from France in 1956.
country in northwestern Africa

Examples

She spent a summer exploring Morocco and its vibrant markets.
The capital of Morocco is Rabat, not Casablanca as many people think.
After gaining independence, Morocco rapidly modernized its cities and infrastructure.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Marruecos', from Arabic 'al-Maġrib' (the west).
