Loretta Young

1. PROPN

/ ləˈrɛtə jʌŋ / (AmE), / ləˈrɛtə jʌŋ / (BrE)
(1913–2000) A celebrated American film and television actress known for her roles in Hollywood films and her award-winning television work.
American actress (1913–2000)

Examples

Loretta Young won an Academy Award for Best Actress in 1948 for her role in 'The Farmer's Daughter'.
The television series 'The Loretta Young Show' helped Loretta Young reach a new generation of audiences.
Many film historians admire Loretta Young for her elegance and enduring presence in Hollywood's golden age.
