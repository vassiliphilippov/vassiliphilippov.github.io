Hanuman

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɑːnuˌmɑːn / (AmE), / ˈhænʊmɑːn / (BrE)
In Hinduism, a monkey god celebrated for his strength, loyalty, and devotion to Rama; a symbol of courage and faith.
Hindu monkey god, symbol of devotion and courage

Examples

Many people pray to Hanuman for strength and protection during difficult times.
Stories about the bravery of Hanuman are widely told in Hindu culture.
The festival of Hanuman Jayanti celebrates the birth of Hanuman with prayers and rituals.

Etymology

From Sanskrit हनुमान् (Hanumān), the name of the deity.
