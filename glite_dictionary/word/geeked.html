geeked

1. ADJ

/ ɡikt / (AmE), / ɡiːkt / (BrE)
(slang) Under the influence of a stimulant drug, especially cocaine; high.
slang: high on a stimulant drug
slang american

Examples

He was so geeked after the party that he couldn't stop talking.
They looked geeked out of their minds at the concert.
Rumors spread that some players were geeked during the championship game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high - affected by drugs or alcohol; intoxicated
