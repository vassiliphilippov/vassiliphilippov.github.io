Victoria Nyanza

1. PROPN

/ vɪkˈtɔriə niˈænzə / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔːriə nɪˈænzə / (BrE)
the largest lake in Africa, also known as Lake Victoria, and the second-largest freshwater lake in the world; a main source of the Nile River
largest lake in Africa; Lake Victoria

Examples

The explorers reached Victoria Nyanza after several weeks of trekking through dense forest.
Lake Tanganyika and Victoria Nyanza are among the Great Lakes of Africa.
Many communities along Victoria Nyanza rely on fishing for their livelihoods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lake Victoria - alternative and more common name for Victoria Nyanza

Etymology

Victoria, after Queen Victoria, and Nyanza, from a Bantu word meaning 'lake.'
