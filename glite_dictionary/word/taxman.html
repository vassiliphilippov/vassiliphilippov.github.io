taxman

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæksˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈtæksˌman / (BrE)
a person, especially a government official, whose job is to collect taxes
tax collector or official

Examples

The taxman came to audit their accounts last week.
Every business must keep its records ready for the taxman.
People often complain when the taxman takes a larger share of their income.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tax collector - an official who collects taxes

Etymology

Formed from "tax" + "man," meaning a collector or agent of taxes.
