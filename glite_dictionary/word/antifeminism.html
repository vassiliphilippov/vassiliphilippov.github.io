antifeminism

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.tiˈfɛm.əˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈfɛm.ɪ.nɪz.əm / (BrE)
opposition to or hostility toward feminism and the advocacy of women's rights
opposition to feminism

Examples

The politician faced criticism for his outspoken antifeminism during the debate.
Many historians have studied the rise of antifeminism in the early twentieth century.
Online forums sometimes become breeding grounds for antifeminism and misogynistic views.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: feminism - advocacy of women's rights and equality

Etymology

From anti- (against) + feminism.
