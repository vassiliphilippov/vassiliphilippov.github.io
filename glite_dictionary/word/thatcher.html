Thatcher

1. PROPN

/ ˈθætʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈθatʃə / (BrE)
(1925–2013) The first woman to serve as Prime Minister of the United Kingdom, known for her conservative policies and strong leadership style.
first female Prime Minister of the UK
british american

Examples

Thatcher served as Prime Minister of the United Kingdom from 1979 to 1990.
Many analysts have debated the long-term impact of Thatcher's economic reforms.
The leadership style of Thatcher influenced politicians across the world.
