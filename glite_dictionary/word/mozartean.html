Mozartean

1. ADJ

/ moʊˈzɑrtiən / (AmE), / məʊˈzɑːtiən / (BrE)
of or relating to Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, his music, or his style
relating to Mozart or his style

Examples

The orchestra delivered a Mozartean performance, full of clarity and grace.
Her interpretation of the sonata was distinctly Mozartean in its elegance and precision.
Many composers have aspired to create a Mozartean balance of melody and harmony in their works.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mozartian - of or relating to Mozart or his style (variant spelling)

Etymology

From the name of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart (1756–1791), Austrian composer, with the suffix -ean meaning 'relating to'.
