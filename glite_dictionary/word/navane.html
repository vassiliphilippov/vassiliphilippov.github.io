Navane

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæˌveɪn / (AmE), / ˈnæˌveɪn / (BrE)
A proprietary name for the antipsychotic drug thiothixene, used to treat schizophrenia and other mental health conditions.
antipsychotic drug (trade name)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Navane to help manage his schizophrenia.
Patients taking Navane should be monitored for potential side effects.
She had been taking Navane for several months under her psychiatrist's supervision.

Related Terms
