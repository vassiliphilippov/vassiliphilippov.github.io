angling

1. NOUN

/ ˈæŋɡlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡlɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or practice of fishing with a rod and line, typically using a hook
fishing with a rod and line

Examples

He spent the afternoon angling by the riverbank, hoping to catch some trout.
Many people take up angling as a relaxing weekend hobby in the countryside.
The club organizes regular angling competitions for both beginners and experts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fishing - the activity of catching fish, especially for food or as a sport

Etymology

From Middle English anglen (to fish with a hook), from angle (hook).
