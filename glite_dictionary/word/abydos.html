Abydos

1. PROPN

/ əˈbaɪdɒs / (AmE), / əˈbaɪdɒs / (BrE)
an ancient Greek city on the Asian side of the Dardanelles, known as the setting of the legend of Hero and Leander
ancient Greek city near the Dardanelles

Examples

According to legend, Leander swam nightly across the Hellespont from Sestos to Abydos to be with Hero.
The ruins of Abydos are located opposite the ancient city of Sestos.
In her lecture, the historian described the strategic importance of Abydos in the ancient world.

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Ἄβυδος (Ábūdos), name of the city.
