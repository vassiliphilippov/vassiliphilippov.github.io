pinkness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪŋknəs / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋknəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being pink in color
the quality of being pink

Examples

The pinkness of the sky at sunrise was breathtaking.
She admired the delicate pinkness of the roses in the garden.
Over time, the pinkness of the paint on the walls faded.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: redness - the quality or state of being red in color
  • Degree Lower: paleness - the quality or state of being pale in color
