pyramidic

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɪrəˈmɪdɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɪrəˈmɪdɪk / (BrE)
having the shape or characteristics of a pyramid; resembling a pyramid.
resembling a pyramid in shape

Examples

The structure has a pyramidic appearance that dominates the skyline.
Her painting includes several pyramidic forms that create a sense of balance.
The crystals grew in a pyramidic fashion, tapering to a fine point.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pyramidal - having the form of a pyramid

Etymology

Formed from 'pyramid' + '-ic', from Greek 'pyramis' (pyramid).
