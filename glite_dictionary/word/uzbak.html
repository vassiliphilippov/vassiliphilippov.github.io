Uzbak

1. NOUN

/ ˈʊz.bæk / (AmE), / ˈʊz.bæk / (BrE)
A member of a Turkic ethnic group native to Uzbekistan and neighboring regions.
a member of a Turkic ethnic group in Central Asia

Examples

He met an Uzbak merchant who shared stories of his homeland.
Many Uzbaks continue to uphold their traditional customs and language.
The festival celebrated the diverse cultures of the region, including the Uzbak people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Uzbek - a member of a Turkic ethnic group in Central Asia

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'Uzbek', from Uzbek (self-designation: Oʻzbek).
