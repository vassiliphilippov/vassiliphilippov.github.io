kismet

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪz.mət / (AmE), / ˈkɪz.mət / (BrE)
the belief that events are predetermined by fate or destiny, especially as understood in Islamic tradition
fate or destiny (esp. in Islam)

Examples

Some people believe that meeting your soulmate is a matter of kismet.
He survived the accident, claiming it was kismet that saved him.
In the film, the characters' paths cross repeatedly, suggesting the influence of kismet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fate - the development of events beyond a person's control, regarded as determined by a supernatural power
  • Synonym: destiny - the events that will necessarily happen to a particular person or thing in the future

Etymology

From Turkish *kısmet*, from Arabic *qismah* (lot, portion, fate).
