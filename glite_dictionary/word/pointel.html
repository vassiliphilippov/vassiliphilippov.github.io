pointel

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔɪn.təl / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪn.tɛl / (BrE)
a small, pointed tool used by an engraver for incising fine lines or details
engraver's pointed tool

Examples

The engraver selected a fine pointel to finish the intricate patterns.
To achieve delicate shading, she switched to a smaller pointel.
A skilled artisan knows the difference between a chisel and a pointel when engraving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graver - an engraver's cutting tool

Etymology

From French 'pointel', diminutive of 'pointe' (point), from Latin 'puncta' (point).
