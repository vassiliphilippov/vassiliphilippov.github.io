Batswana

1. NOUN

/ bɑːtˈswɑːnə / (AmE), / batsˈwɑːnə / (BrE)
A member of the Bantu ethnic group native mainly to Botswana and western South Africa.
member of a Bantu group in Botswana

Examples

Many Batswana take pride in their rich cultural heritage and traditions.
The government of Botswana works closely with the Batswana to promote sustainable development.
She studied the art of storytelling among the Batswana during her visit to southern Africa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tswana - the language spoken by the Batswana people

Etymology

From Tswana 'Batswana', plural of 'Motswana' (a Tswana person).
