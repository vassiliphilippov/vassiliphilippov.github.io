noctambulist

1. NOUN

/ nɑkˈtæmbjəlɪst / (AmE), / nɒkˈtæmbjʊlɪst / (BrE)
A person who walks about while asleep; a sleepwalker.
person who sleepwalks

Examples

The doctor explained that her son was a noctambulist and that his sleepwalking was not uncommon in children.
As a child, Daniel was a frequent noctambulist, often found wandering the house late at night.
Because she is a noctambulist, special precautions were taken to ensure her safety during sleep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleepwalker - a person who walks in their sleep

Etymology

From Latin 'noct-' (night) + 'ambulare' (to walk).
