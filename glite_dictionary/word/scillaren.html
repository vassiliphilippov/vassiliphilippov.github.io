scillaren

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈlɛrən / (AmE), / sɪˈlærən / (BrE)
a crystalline steroid glycoside obtained from squill (Drimia maritima), used in medicine as a cardiac stimulant.
a glycoside from squill used as medicine
medical

Examples

Scillaren is used in some countries as a treatment for certain heart conditions.
Researchers isolated scillaren from the bulbs of Drimia maritima.
The effectiveness of scillaren as a cardiac stimulant has been studied extensively.

Synset Examples

Scillaren is used in the treatment of ischaemic heart disease.

Etymology

From modern Latin 'scilla' (the plant genus name) + '-aren' (chemical suffix).
