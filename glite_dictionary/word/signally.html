signally

1. ADV

/ ˈsɪɡnəli / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡ.nə.li / (BrE)
in a significant, noticeable, or remarkable way; strikingly.
in a notable or remarkable manner

Examples

His plan was signally successful, yielding much better results than expected.
The company has signally failed to address the main issues raised by employees.
She was signally honored for her contributions to the organization.

Synset Examples

signally inappropriate methods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: notably - in a particularly noticeable or remarkable manner
  • Synonym: remarkably - in a way that is worthy of attention; strikingly
  • Antonym: insignificantly - in an unimportant or trivial way

Etymology

From 'signal' + '-ly', with 'signal' in the sense of notable or remarkable (17th century).
