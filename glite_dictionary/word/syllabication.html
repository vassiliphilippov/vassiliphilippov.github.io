syllabication

1. NOUN

/ sɪˌlæbɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / sɪˌlæbɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of dividing words into syllables
division of words into syllables

Examples

Proper syllabication helps students improve their reading skills.
The teacher explained the rules of syllabication to the class.
Learning syllabication is essential for understanding how to break long words into parts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to syllabify - to divide into syllables
  • Synonym: syllabication - division of words into syllables

Etymology

From Medieval Latin syllabicatio, from Latin syllaba (syllable).
