premenstrual

1. ADJ

/ priːˈmɛnstruəl / (AmE), / priːˈmɛnstruəl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring in the time just before menstruation begins
before menstruation; relating to pre-period time
medical

Examples

She often gets headaches and mood swings in her premenstrual phase.
Some women experience premenstrual symptoms such as bloating or irritability.
The doctor explained that premenstrual changes are common before each period.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: menstrual - relating to menstruation or a menstrual period
  • Antonym: postmenstrual - relating to or occurring after menstruation

Etymology

From Latin 'prae-' (before) + 'menstruus' (monthly, relating to menstruation).
