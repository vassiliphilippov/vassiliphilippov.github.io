fanboy

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæn.bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈfan.bɔɪ / (BrE)
A male fan who is obsessively enthusiastic about a particular subject, especially in popular entertainment media.
male obsessed fan, especially in entertainment
informal

Examples

He is such a fanboy when it comes to superhero movies, knowing every detail about the characters.
Online forums are full of fanboys passionately defending their favorite video games.
The author laughed, saying she still gets nervous around fanboys at conventions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fangirl - A female fan who is obsessively enthusiastic about a particular subject, especially in entertainment

Etymology

Blend of 'fan' (from 'fanatic') and 'boy' (male person).
