materiel

1. NOUN

/ ˌmætəˈriːl / (AmE), / ˌmatəˈriːl / (BrE)
the equipment and supplies used by a military force
military equipment and supplies

Examples

The army's success depended on the timely arrival of materiel at the front lines.
Without sufficient materiel, the mission could not proceed as planned.
They transported medical materiel to the disaster area after the earthquake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: material - substance or matter from which things are made

Etymology

From French 'matériel' meaning equipment or material.
