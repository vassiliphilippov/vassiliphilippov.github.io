methylenedioxymethamphetamine

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛθɪliˌnaɪˌɒksiˌmɛθəmˈfɛtəmiːn / (AmE), / ˌmiːθɪˌliːniˌdɪɒksiˌmɛθəmˈfɛtəmiːn / (BrE)
a synthetic stimulant and psychoactive drug, known for producing feelings of increased energy, pleasure, and emotional closeness; it is used recreationally and was formerly used in psychotherapy
a synthetic stimulant and psychoactive drug
medical

Examples

The effects of methylenedioxymethamphetamine can include increased heart rate and feelings of euphoria.
In the 1980s, methylenedioxymethamphetamine was researched as a potential therapeutic aid in psychotherapy.
Possession of methylenedioxymethamphetamine is illegal in many countries due to its psychoactive properties.

Synset Examples

MDMA is often used at parties because it enables partygoers to remain active for long periods of time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: MDMA - common abbreviation of methylenedioxymethamphetamine

Etymology

Formed from the chemical names methylene, dioxy, and methamphetamine.
