aroma

1. NOUN

/ əˈroʊmə / (AmE), / əˈrəʊmə / (BrE)
a distinctive, usually pleasant smell often associated with food, drink, or plants
pleasant or distinctive smell

Examples

The aroma of freshly baked bread filled the kitchen.
She loves the rich aroma of freshly brewed coffee in the morning.
The garden was alive with the sweet aroma of blooming roses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scent - a distinctive smell, especially a pleasant one
  • Synonym: odor - a smell, especially one that is distinctive
  • Antonym: stench - a very unpleasant smell

Etymology

From Latin 'arōma' (fragrant spice or herb), from Greek 'arōma'.

2. NOUN

/ əˈroʊmə / (AmE), / əˈrəʊmə / (BrE)
a distinctive, pleasant smell, often especially noticeable or enjoyable
pleasant, distinctive smell

Examples

The aroma of freshly baked bread filled the entire house.
He chose coffee for its rich flavor and strong aroma.
A delicate aroma of jasmine lingered in the evening air.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragrance - a pleasant, sweet smell
  • Synonym: scent - a distinctive smell, especially one that is pleasant
  • Antonym: stench - a strong and unpleasant smell

Etymology

From Latin 'arōma', from Ancient Greek ἄρωμα (arōma, 'spice, sweet herb').
