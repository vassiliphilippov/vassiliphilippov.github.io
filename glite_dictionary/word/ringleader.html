ringleader

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪŋˌliːdər / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋˌliːdə / (BrE)
the leader of a group, especially one involved in illegal or disreputable activities
leader of a group, often illicit

Examples

The police finally arrested the ringleader of the smuggling operation.
Sam was thought to be the ringleader behind the school prank.
During the trial, witnesses identified her as the ringleader of the gang.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leader - a person who leads a group or organization
  • Antonym: follower - a person who follows or is led by another

Etymology

From 'ring' + 'leader', originally referring to the leader in a group gathered in a circle for illicit activity (17th century English).
