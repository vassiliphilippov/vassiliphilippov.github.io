jokester

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒoʊk.stɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊk.stə / (BrE)
a person who enjoys telling or playing jokes, often to amuse others
person who tells jokes

Examples

At every party, Mark is the jokester who keeps everyone laughing.
Sometimes being a jokester in meetings can lighten the mood.
The class jokester pulled another harmless prank today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joker - a person who makes jokes or acts playfully
