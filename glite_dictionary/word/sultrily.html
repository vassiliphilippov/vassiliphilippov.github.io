sultrily

1. ADV

/ ˈsʌl.trə.li / (AmE), / ˈsʌl.trɪ.li / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely attractive in a sensual or sexual manner; in a hot and humid manner.
in a sensual or hot manner

Examples

She smiled sultrily across the dimly lit room, capturing everyone's attention.
The actress spoke sultrily, her voice lingering on every word.
He glanced over sultrily, making his intentions clear without saying anything.

Synset Examples

the belly dancer moved sensually among the tables

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensually - in a way that relates to or affects the senses, especially sexually
  • Antonym: coldly - in a way that lacks emotion or warmth

Etymology

From "sultry" + "-ly".
