parented

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛr.ənt.ɪd / (AmE), / ˈpeər.ənt.ɪd / (BrE)
Having parents or having been cared for by parent surrogates.
having parents or parental care

Examples

The well-parented children showed confidence and emotional security.
Every child deserves to be parented in a loving and supportive environment.
Orphanages often try to ensure that children are parented by caring adults whenever possible.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: orphaned - having lost parents through death
