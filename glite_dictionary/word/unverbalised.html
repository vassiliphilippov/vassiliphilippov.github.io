unverbalised

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈvɜrbəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈvɜːbəlaɪzd / (BrE)
Not expressed or put into words; not stated explicitly.
not put into words

Examples

There was an unverbalised understanding between the partners that was never written into the contract.
Her unverbalised fears influenced her decision to leave the city.
Many agreements in families remain unverbalised but still strongly shape behavior.

Synset Examples

the unexpressed terms of the agreement
things left unsaid
some kind of unspoken agreement
his action is clear but his reason remains unstated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unexpressed - not overtly stated or shown; not expressed
  • Synonym: unspoken - not said aloud or explicitly stated
  • Antonym: expressed - put into words; stated explicitly

Etymology

From un- (not) + verbalise (to express in words) + -ed.
