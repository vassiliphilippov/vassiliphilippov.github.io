self-acceptance

1. NOUN

/ sɛlf əkˈsɛptəns / (AmE), / sɛlf əkˈsɛptəns / (BrE)
the recognition and acceptance of one's own abilities and limitations; accepting yourself as you are
acceptance of oneself as one is

Examples

Practicing self-acceptance helped her develop a more positive outlook on life.
True self-acceptance means embracing both your strengths and weaknesses.
He struggled with self-acceptance after experiencing several setbacks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-esteem - confidence in one's own worth or abilities
  • Antonym: self-rejection - refusal to accept oneself or aspects of oneself

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'acceptance' (from Latin acceptare, to receive).
