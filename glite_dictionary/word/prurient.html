prurient

1. ADJ

/ ˈprʊriənt / (AmE), / ˈprʊəriənt / (BrE)
Having or showing an excessive interest in sexual matters.
excessively interested in sex

Examples

The judge condemned the magazine's prurient content as being unsuitable for minors.
He was known for making prurient remarks that made people uncomfortable at parties.
The film was criticized for its prurient depiction of relationships rather than for its artistic merits.

Synset Examples

eluding lubricious embraces
prurient literature
prurient thoughts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lewd - crude and offensive in a sexual way
  • Synonym: lascivious - feeling or revealing an overt sexual interest or desire
  • Antonym: chaste - not having any sexual nature or intention

Etymology

From Latin prurient-, pruriens, present participle of prurire (to itch, to long for).
