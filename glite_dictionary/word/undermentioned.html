undermentioned

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɚˈmɛnʃənd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈmɛnʃənd / (BrE)
(formal) mentioned or specified later in the same document or text.
mentioned later in a document

Examples

The undermentioned individuals are required to attend the hearing at 10 a.m.
Please ensure the undermentioned documents are included in your application.
The funds will be distributed among the undermentioned charities.

Synset Examples

the following items

Related Terms

  • Antonym: aforementioned - mentioned previously in the same document or text

Etymology

Formed from 'under-' (meaning 'below') and 'mentioned'.
