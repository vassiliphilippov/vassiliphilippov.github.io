avellan

1. ADJ

/ əˈvɛlən / (AmE), / əˈvɛlən / (BrE)
Relating to filberts or hazelnuts.
of or pertaining to hazelnuts

Examples

The chef created a unique avellan sauce using crushed hazelnuts for a rich flavor.
Several avellan varieties were cultivated in the orchard for their abundant nut production.
Her research focused on the avellan characteristics of shrubs found in the Mediterranean region.

Etymology

From Latin 'avellana' (hazelnut), named after Avella in Italy.
