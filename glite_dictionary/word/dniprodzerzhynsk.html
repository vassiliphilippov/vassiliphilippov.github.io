Dniprodzerzhynsk

1. PROPN

/ dniˌproʊdzərˈʒɪnsk / (AmE), / dniˌprɒdzəˈʒɪnsk / (BrE)
a port city and industrial center in east central Ukraine, located on the Dnieper River; now known as Kamianske.
industrial city in east central Ukraine

Examples

My grandparents grew up in Dniprodzerzhynsk before moving to Kyiv.
The steel industry was a major employer in Dniprodzerzhynsk during the Soviet era.
After visiting the Dnieper River, we stopped in Dniprodzerzhynsk to explore its historic sites.

Etymology

Named after Felix Dzerzhinsky, a Soviet statesman; now officially called Kamianske.
