periodontal

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɪriəˈdɑntl̩ / (AmE), / ˌpɪəriəˈdɒntl̩ / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the structures surrounding and supporting the teeth
relating to the tissues around the teeth
medical

Examples

Regular brushing and flossing can help prevent periodontal disease.
The dentist recommended a periodontal examination to check the health of my gums.
He specializes in periodontal surgery to treat advanced gum disease.

Synset Examples

periodontal disease

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gingival - relating to the gums
  • Degree Lower: dental - relating to teeth

Etymology

From Greek 'peri-' (around) + 'odous, odont-' (tooth).
