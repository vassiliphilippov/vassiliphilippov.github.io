archaeologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑr.ki.əˈlɑ.dʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌɑː.ki.əˈlɒ.dʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to archaeology or the study of ancient societies through their material remains.
relating to archaeology

Examples

The archaeologic evidence suggests the city was inhabited over 3,000 years ago.
She joined an archaeologic expedition to explore ancient ruins in Turkey.
New archaeologic methods have improved our understanding of prehistoric cultures.

Synset Examples

an archaeological dig
a dramatic archaeological discovery

Related Terms

  • Synonym: archaeological - relating to archaeology, especially commonly used form

Etymology

From Greek arkhaiologikos, from arkhaiologia (study of ancient things).
