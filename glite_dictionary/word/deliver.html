to deliver

1. VERB

/ dɪˈlɪvɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪvə / (BrE)
to formally hand over or surrender someone or something to another person or authority
formally hand over or surrender

Examples

The guard delivered the prisoner to the authorities after his arrest.
She refused to deliver the confidential documents to her competitor.
Following the trial, the judge ordered the officials to deliver the evidence to the court.

Synset Examples

the guard delivered the criminal to the police
render up the prisoners
render the town to the enemy
fork over the money

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surrender - to give up something or someone to another
  • Synonym: transfer - to move someone or something from one place or person to another

Etymology

From Latin *deliberare*, meaning 'to set free, deliver'.
