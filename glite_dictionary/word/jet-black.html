jet-black

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɛtˌblæk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛtˌblæk / (BrE)
Intensely black; as black as jet or coal.
intensely black color

Examples

She wore a jet-black dress that stood out against the white walls.
The cat's jet-black fur shone in the sunlight.
His jet-black hair was perfectly styled for the occasion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitch-black - completely black; very dark
  • Synonym: coal-black - as black as coal; deep black
  • Degree Lower: black - of the color black

Etymology

From 'jet', a shiny black mineral used in jewelry, plus 'black'.
