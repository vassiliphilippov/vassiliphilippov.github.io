Xiphosura

1. NOUN

/ zaɪfəˈsjʊrə / (AmE), / zɪfəˈsjʊərə / (BrE)
an order of arthropods comprising horseshoe crabs and their extinct relatives
order of arthropods (horseshoe crabs and relatives)

Examples

Scientists have discovered new fossil species belonging to the Xiphosura.
The modern horseshoe crab is a surviving member of the ancient order Xiphosura.
The Xiphosura have changed little in form over millions of years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horseshoe crab - marine arthropod of the family Limulidae

Etymology

From Greek xiphos (sword) + oura (tail), referring to the sword-like tail of these animals.

2. NOUN

/ zaɪfəˈsjʊrə / (AmE), / zaɪfəˈsjʊərə / (BrE)
An order of arthropods including the horseshoe crabs and related extinct forms.
order of arthropods: horseshoe crabs

Examples

The order Xiphosura includes both living horseshoe crabs and many extinct relatives.
Scientists study fossils of Xiphosura to understand ancient marine ecosystems.
Only a few species within Xiphosura have survived from prehistoric times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horseshoe crab - marine arthropod belonging to Xiphosura

Etymology

From Greek xiphos (sword) + oura (tail), referring to the sword-like tail of these animals.
