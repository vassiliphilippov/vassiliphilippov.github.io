technophilic

1. ADJ

/ ˌtɛk.nəˈfɪl.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɛk.nəʊˈfɪl.ɪk / (BrE)
Having a strong enthusiasm for or affinity with technology.
enthusiastic about technology

Examples

As a technophilic company, they quickly adopted the latest artificial intelligence tools.
His technophilic attitude often leads him to try every new gadget on the market.
The school nurtures a technophilic environment, encouraging students to explore robotics and coding.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: technophobic - having a fear or dislike of technology

Etymology

From 'techno-' (technology) + Greek 'philos' (loving).
