cybernaut

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪbərˌnɔt / (AmE), / ˈsʌɪbənɔːt / (BrE)
a person who frequently uses and explores the internet or cyberspace
frequent internet user

Examples

As a seasoned cybernaut, she spends hours each day exploring new websites and forums.
The conference attracted cybernauts from all over the world to discuss online privacy.
Every budding cybernaut should learn basic internet safety tips.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: netizen - a citizen or frequent user of the internet

Etymology

Formed from 'cyber-' (related to computers or the internet) and '-naut' (from Greek 'nautēs', meaning sailor).
