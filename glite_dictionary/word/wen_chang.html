Wen Ch'ang

1. PROPN

/ wɛn tʃɑŋ / (AmE), / wɛn tʃɑːŋ / (BrE)
In Chinese tradition, the god of literature, scholarship, and examinations.
Chinese god of literature and scholarship

Examples

Many students would make offerings to Wen Ch'ang before important exams.
The temple dedicated to Wen Ch'ang is visited by scholars seeking inspiration.
In Chinese folklore, Wen Ch'ang is revered as the patron of writers and academics.

Etymology

From Chinese 文昌 (*Wénchāng*), meaning 'flourishing culture' or 'literary prosperity'.
