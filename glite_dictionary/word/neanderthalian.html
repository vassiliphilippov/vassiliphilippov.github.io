Neanderthalian

1. ADJ

/ niˌæn.dərˈθeɪ.li.ən / (AmE), / niˌæn.dəˈθɑː.li.ən / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Neanderthal man.
of or like Neanderthal man

Examples

The archaeologist presented a Neanderthalian skull recovered from the cave.
Some scholars study Neanderthalian tools to learn about early human behavior.
The discovery shed light on Neanderthalian adaptations to cold climates.

Synset Examples

Neanderthal skull

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Neanderthal - an extinct species or subspecies of archaic humans

Etymology

From Neanderthal, a valley in Germany where remains of the early human species were first discovered, combined with the adjective-forming suffix '-ian'.
