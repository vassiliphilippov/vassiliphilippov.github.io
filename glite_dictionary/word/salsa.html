salsa

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑl.sə / (AmE), / ˈsæl.sə / (BrE)
A spicy sauce made from tomatoes, onions, and chili peppers, typically served with Mexican dishes.
spicy Mexican sauce

Examples

He added a spoonful of salsa to his tacos for extra flavor.
Would you like mild or spicy salsa with your chips?
The homemade salsa was the highlight of the meal.

Etymology

From Spanish 'salsa' (sauce), from Latin 'salsa' (salty, salted).
