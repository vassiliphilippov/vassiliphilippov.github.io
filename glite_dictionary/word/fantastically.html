fantastically

1. ADV

/ fænˈtæstɪkli / (AmE), / fanˈtastɪkli / (BrE)
To an exceptional or extremely high degree; exceedingly.
extremely; exceedingly

Examples

The view from the mountain was fantastically beautiful and took everyone's breath away.
He managed to solve the problem fantastically quickly, impressing the entire team.
Her performance was fantastically well received by both critics and audiences.

Synset Examples

she plays fabulously well
behind you the coastal hills plunge to the incredibly blue sea backed by the Turkish mountains

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extremely - to a very high degree
  • Synonym: exceedingly - to a very great extent

Etymology

From 'fantastic' + '-ally'; ultimately from Greek 'phantastikos' (able to imagine or display).
