diapensia

1. NOUN

/ ˌdaɪ.əˈpɛn.si.ə / (AmE), / ˌdaɪ.əˈpɛn.si.ə / (BrE)
A low-growing evergreen plant of the genus Diapensia, found in boreal regions.
boreal evergreen plant genus

Examples

During our hike in the Arctic region, we spotted a patch of diapensia growing on the rocky slopes.
The tiny white flowers of diapensia make it a favorite among alpine plant enthusiasts.
Researchers studied the resilience of diapensia to extreme cold in its natural habitat.

Etymology

From the genus name Diapensia, derived from modern Latin.
