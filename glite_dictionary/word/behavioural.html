behavioural

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈheɪvjərəl / (AmE), / bɪˈheɪvjərəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving behavior
relating to behavior
british

Examples

The school offers support for children with behavioural difficulties.
Researchers examined the behavioural patterns of wild chimpanzees.
Policy changes led to positive behavioural shifts in the workplace.

Synset Examples

behavioral sciences

Related Terms

  • Synonym: behavioral - relating to or involving behavior (US spelling)

Etymology

From 'behaviour' + '-al', with 'behaviour' from Old French 'behaveour', ultimately from Latin 'habere' (to have, to hold).
