nourishing

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɝːrɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnʌrɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
(of food or drink) providing the substances necessary for health and growth; promoting nourishment.
providing nourishment or sustenance

Examples

A bowl of nourishing soup is perfect on a cold day.
She prepared a nourishing meal for her family every evening.
Children need nourishing foods to help them grow healthy and strong.

Synset Examples

good nourishing stew

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutritious - food that is very good for your health or provides necessary nutrients
  • Antonym: fattening - causing weight gain, often by providing excess calories

Etymology

From nourish + -ing, ultimately from Latin *nutrire* (to feed, foster).
