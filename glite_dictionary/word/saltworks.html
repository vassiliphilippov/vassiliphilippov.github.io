saltworks

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔltˌwɜrks / (AmE), / ˈsɔːltˌwɜːks / (BrE)
A place or facility where salt is produced on a commercial scale, typically through evaporation or mining processes.
industrial salt production facility

Examples

The ancient saltworks along the coast played a crucial role in the region's economy.
Modern saltworks use advanced techniques to increase efficiency and yield.
Tourists visited the historic saltworks to learn about traditional salt extraction methods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salt mine - an excavation where salt is extracted from the ground
