Caviidae

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæv.i.ɪˌdi / (AmE), / ˈkavi.ɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a family of rodents that includes guinea pigs and cavies, native to South America
family of rodents including guinea pigs

Examples

The family Caviidae encompasses animals such as guinea pigs and maras.
Members of the Caviidae are native primarily to South America.
Many pet owners are familiar with Caviidae because of their popular domesticated guinea pigs.

Etymology

From modern Latin Caviidae, based on the genus Cavia.
