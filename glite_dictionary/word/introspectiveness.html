introspectiveness

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.trəˈspɛk.tɪv.nəs / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trəˈspɛk.tɪv.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of examining or thinking carefully about your own thoughts and feelings
the quality of self-examination of thoughts and feelings

Examples

Her introspectiveness led her to keep a detailed journal of her everyday life.
Writers are often praised for the introspectiveness they bring to their characters.
His introspectiveness helped him understand the roots of his anxiety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: introspection - the examination of one's own mental and emotional processes

Etymology

From introspective + -ness, ultimately from Latin intrōspicere (to look within).
