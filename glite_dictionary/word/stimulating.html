stimulating

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɪmjəˌleɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɪmjʊˌleɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Encouraging interest, enthusiasm, or activity; making someone feel more active or alert.
encouraging activity or alertness

Examples

The professor gave a stimulating lecture that kept everyone engaged.
We had a stimulating conversation that inspired new ideas.
Fresh air and exercise can have a stimulating effect on the mind.

Synset Examples

a stimulating discussion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exciting - causing strong feelings of enthusiasm and eagerness
  • Antonym: dull - not interesting or lively; lacking stimulation

Etymology

From 'stimulate' (Latin stimulare, from stimulus 'goad').
