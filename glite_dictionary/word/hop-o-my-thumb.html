hop-o'-my-thumb

1. NOUN

/ hɑp oʊ maɪ θʌm / (AmE), / hɒp əʊ maɪ θʌm / (BrE)
a character from folklore, typically depicted as a very small boy who uses his wit to overcome challenges
a tiny boy in folklore tales

The fairy tale of hop-o'-my-thumb has been retold for generations.
He wore a costume as hop-o'-my-thumb in the school play.
Many European cultures have their own version of hop-o'-my-thumb legends.

Etymology

From the phrase 'hop on my thumb,' referring to a person of very small stature.
