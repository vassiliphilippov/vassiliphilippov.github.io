cresson

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɛs ən / (AmE), / ˈkrɛs ən / (BrE)
A moderate yellow-green color that is greener and deeper than moss green and yellower and darker than pea green.
moderate yellow-green color

Examples

She chose a cresson dress for the spring wedding, which complemented the garden setting perfectly.
The artist mixed several pigments to create the ideal cresson shade for the meadow.
The walls of their new kitchen were painted in a soft cresson tone, giving the room a natural, earthy feel.

Etymology

From French 'cresson' meaning 'watercress', referencing the plant's color.
