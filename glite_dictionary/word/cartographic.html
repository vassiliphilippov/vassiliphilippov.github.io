cartographic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑːrtəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɑːtəˈɡrafɪk / (BrE)
relating to the making or study of maps and charts.
relating to maps or map-making

Examples

The explorer's journey contributed valuable cartographic information to the maps of the region.
Advances in cartographic technology have greatly improved our understanding of the ocean floor.
The book includes a detailed cartographic analysis of historical trade routes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cartography - the science or practice of drawing maps

Etymology

From French cartographique, from carte (map) + -graphique (-graphic).
