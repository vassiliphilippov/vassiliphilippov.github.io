frankfurter

1. NOUN

/ ˈfræŋkˌfɝːtər / (AmE), / ˈfraŋkfɜːtə / (BrE)
A smooth-textured sausage made of minced beef or pork, usually smoked and often served in a bread roll.
type of smoked sausage

Examples

She ordered a frankfurter with mustard and onions at the food stand.
The children enjoyed grilled frankfurters at the picnic.
A traditional frankfurter is usually made with pork or beef and has a smoky flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot dog - A cooked sausage served in a sliced bun

Etymology

From German 'Frankfurter', meaning 'of Frankfurt', the city in Germany where the sausage originated.
