cm

1. NOUN

/ si ɛm / (AmE), / siː ɛm / (BrE)
a metric unit of length equal to one hundredth of a meter; abbreviation for centimeter
metric unit of length (centimeter)
american british

Examples

The fabric is 50 cm wide, which is perfect for my project.
He grew 5 cm taller in just one year.
Please measure the board and mark 20 cm from the edge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: centimeter - a metric unit of length equal to one hundredth of a meter

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'centimeter', from French 'centimètre': Latin 'centum' (hundred) + Greek 'metron' (measure).
