nethermost

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɛðərˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈnɛðəməʊst / (BrE)
Situated at the lowest or deepest position; farthest down.
lowest in position; farthest down

Examples

She reached for the nethermost book on the crowded shelf.
The treasure was hidden in the nethermost chamber of the castle.
The roots extend deep into the nethermost layers of the soil.

Synset Examples

bottommost shelf

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bottommost - lowest or farthest down in position
  • Antonym: topmost - highest in position

Etymology

From Middle English 'nether' (lower) + '-most' (superlative suffix).
