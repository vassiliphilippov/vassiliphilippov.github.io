au revoir

1. NOUN

/ oʊ rəˈvwɑr / (AmE), / əʊ rəˈvwɑː / (BrE)
used to say goodbye when leaving, especially temporarily; farewell.
French farewell expression

Examples

She waved and said au revoir as she left the party.
After our meeting, he whispered a quiet au revoir before stepping out into the night.
They exchanged a warm au revoir at the airport, promising to meet again soon.

Synset Examples

they said their good-byes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - used to express farewell at parting

Etymology

From French 'au revoir' (literally 'until seeing again').
