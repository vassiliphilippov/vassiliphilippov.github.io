grower

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡroʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊ.ə / (BrE)
a person who cultivates plants or crops, especially as a profession
person who cultivates plants or crops

Examples

The region is known for its apple growers who supply fruit nationwide.
She is a skilled grower of rare orchids in her greenhouse.
Many coffee growers have adapted new sustainable practices to protect the environment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm
