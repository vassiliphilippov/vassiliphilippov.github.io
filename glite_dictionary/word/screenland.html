screenland

1. NOUN

/ ˈskrinˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈskriːnlænd / (BrE)
the world or community of people involved in the film industry
film industry community

Examples

Her incredible talent quickly made her a legend in screenland.
News from screenland always fascinates fans who want to know more about celebrities and directors.
The awards ceremony gathered the brightest stars of screenland together for one glamorous evening.

Synset Examples

a star of stage and screen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hollywood - the American film industry, especially the major studios and personnel in Los Angeles

Etymology

Formed from 'screen' (referring to the cinema or movie screen) and 'land' (used in sense of world or domain).
