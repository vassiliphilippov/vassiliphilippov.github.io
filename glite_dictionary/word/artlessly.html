artlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈɑrtləsli / (AmE), / ˈɑːtləsli / (BrE)
in a natural, ingenuous, or simple manner, without cunning or pretense
in a natural or ingenuous way

Examples

She artlessly confessed her feelings, surprising everyone with her honesty.
The child spoke artlessly, unaware of the impact of her words.
He dealt with the criticism artlessly, accepting it without defensiveness or guile.

Synset Examples

she answered the judge's questions artlessly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guilelessly - without guile; innocently
  • Synonym: innocently - in a harmless or unknowing manner
  • Antonym: deviously - in a cunning or indirect way
  • Antonym: artfully - in a crafty or skillful manner

2. ADV

/ ˈɑrtlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈɑːtləsli / (BrE)
in a crude, unskilled, or lacking sophistication; without skill or finesse
in a crude or unskilled manner

Examples

She painted the wall artlessly, leaving streaks and uneven patches everywhere.
The story unfolds artlessly, lacking the polish of a seasoned writer's hand.
He artlessly attempted to repair the clock, but only made things worse.

Synset Examples

an inexpertly constructed lean-to

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inexpertly - in an unskilled or unprofessional way

Etymology

From 'artless' (without skill), formed by adding the adverb suffix '-ly'.
