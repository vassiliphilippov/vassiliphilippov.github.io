starlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɑrˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling a star in shape, appearance, or qualities.
resembling a star

Examples

A faint, starlike glow could be seen through the telescope.
The flower's petals formed a beautiful, starlike pattern.
He wore a pin with a small, starlike design on his lapel.

Synset Examples

they saw a starlike object in the sky

Related Terms

  • Synonym: star-shaped - Having the shape of a star

Etymology

Formed from 'star' + '-like', using the English suffix '-like' meaning 'resembling'.
