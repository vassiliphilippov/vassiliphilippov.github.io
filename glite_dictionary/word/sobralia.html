Sobralia

1. NOUN

/ soʊˈbrɑːliə / (AmE), / səʊˈbrɑːliə / (BrE)
a genus of about 125 tropical American orchids, known for their showy and short-lived flowers
a genus of tropical American orchids

Examples

The Sobralia species are prized for their large and colorful orchid flowers.
Botanists have discovered several new Sobralia varieties in the rainforests of Central America.
Growing a Sobralia requires careful attention to temperature and humidity.

Etymology

Named after Francisco Sobral, Spanish botanist.
