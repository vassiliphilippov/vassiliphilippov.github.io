New Britain

1. PROPN

/ nuː ˈbrɪt.ən / (AmE), / njuː ˈbrɪt.ən / (BrE)
the largest island in the Bismarck Archipelago, located in Papua New Guinea
largest island in the Bismarck Archipelago, Papua New Guinea

Examples

The indigenous peoples of New Britain have a rich cultural heritage.
After the volcanic eruption, relief supplies were flown to New Britain.
Tropical rainforests cover much of New Britain, providing habitat for unique wildlife.

Etymology

Named by English explorer William Dampier in the early 18th century after Great Britain.
