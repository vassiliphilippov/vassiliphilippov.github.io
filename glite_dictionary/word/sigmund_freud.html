Sigmund Freud

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪɡmənd ˈfrɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡmənd ˈfrɔɪd / (BrE)
Austrian neurologist and founder of psychoanalysis, influential in the development of psychology and psychiatry.
Austrian founder of psychoanalysis

Examples

Sigmund Freud developed the theory of the unconscious mind and its influence on behavior.
Many concepts in modern psychology trace their roots back to Sigmund Freud.
The book provides an excellent overview of Sigmund Freud's theories and their impact on psychiatry.

Related Terms
