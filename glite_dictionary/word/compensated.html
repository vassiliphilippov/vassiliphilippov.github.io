compensated

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑːm.pənˌseɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.pənˌseɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
receiving or eligible to receive payment, especially as an employee or for a service performed
receiving or eligible for compensation

Examples

All compensated employees must submit their timesheets by Friday afternoon.
The company offers benefits to both compensated and volunteer staff members.
He took a compensated position as a consultant for the nonprofit organization.

Synset Examples

salaried workers
a stipendiary magistrate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salaried - receiving a regular salary for work
  • Antonym: unpaid - not receiving payment for work done

Etymology

From the verb 'compensate,' from Latin 'compensatus' (to weigh one thing against another, counterbalance).
