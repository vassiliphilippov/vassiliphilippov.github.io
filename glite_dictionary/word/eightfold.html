eightfold

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪtˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈeɪt.fəʊld / (BrE)
having eight parts, units, or components; made up of eight parts or elements
having eight parts

Examples

The company reported an eightfold increase in revenue over the last decade.
This molecule is eightfold symmetric, giving it unique chemical properties.
The organization expanded its staff to an eightfold number over two years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: octuple - consisting of eight parts or eight times as much

Etymology

From Old English 'eahta' (eight) + 'fold' (multiplied by, times).
