Lagos

1. PROPN

/ ˈleɪɡoʊs / (AmE), / ˈleɪɡɒs / (BrE)
the largest city and major port of Nigeria, located in the southwest on the Gulf of Guinea; formerly the capital of Nigeria
the largest city in Nigeria

Examples

Lagos is known for its vibrant nightlife and bustling markets.
She moved to Lagos to pursue a career in finance.
The port of Lagos handles a large portion of Nigeria's imports and exports.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Lagos', named by Portuguese explorers after the town of Lagos in Portugal.
