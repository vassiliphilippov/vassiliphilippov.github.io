claw-shaped

1. ADJ

/ klɔː ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / klɔː ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a shape that resembles a claw, often curved and pointed.
resembling the shape of a claw

Examples

The plant has claw-shaped petals that curl inward at the tips.
He drew a claw-shaped hook to hang his coat on.
Many birds have claw-shaped talons for catching prey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clawed - having claws or equipped with claws
