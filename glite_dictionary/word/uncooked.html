uncooked

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkʊkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʊkt / (BrE)
(of food) not prepared by cooking; raw.
not cooked; raw (esp. food)

Examples

You should never eat uncooked chicken because it may contain harmful bacteria.
The salad was topped with uncooked vegetables for extra crunch.
Sushi often uses uncooked fish as a main ingredient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raw - (of food) not cooked
  • Antonym: cooked - (of food) prepared by cooking

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'cooked', formed in English.
