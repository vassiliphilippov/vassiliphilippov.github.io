penetralium

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɛnɪˈtreɪliəm / (AmE), / ˌpɛnɪˈtreɪliəm / (BrE)
the innermost or most secret part of a place or thing, especially a building or sanctuary
innermost or most secret part

Examples

The archaeologists ventured into the penetralium of the ancient temple to uncover its mysteries.
Few ever saw the penetralium of the great mansion, where its owner spent his days in solitude.
Hidden in the penetralium of the forest lies a tranquil, untouched garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sanctum - a private or sacred place
  • Synonym: core - the central or most important part of something

Etymology

From Latin 'penetralia', plural of 'penetrale' (inner place), from 'penetrare' (to penetrate).
