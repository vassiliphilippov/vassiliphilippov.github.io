chestnut-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɛs.nət ˌkʌ.lɚd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛs.nət ˌkʌl.əd / (BrE)
Having a rich brown color resembling that of a chestnut.
of a rich chestnut brown color

Examples

The horse had a beautiful chestnut-colored coat that gleamed in the sun.
She wore a chestnut-colored sweater that brought out her eyes.
Autumn leaves often turn a striking chestnut-colored hue before falling.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: brown - the color between red and yellow, resembling the color of wood or earth
