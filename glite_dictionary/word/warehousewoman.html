warehousewoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛr.haʊsˌwʊm.ən / (AmE), / ˈweə.haʊsˌwʊm.ən / (BrE)
a woman who manages or works in a warehouse
female warehouse worker or manager

Examples

She was the only warehousewoman working the night shift at the distribution center.
The company honored its longest-serving warehousewoman at the annual staff meeting.
As a skilled warehousewoman, she quickly organized the incoming shipments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warehouseman - a person (usually a man) who manages or works in a warehouse

Etymology

From 'warehouse' + 'woman', patterned after 'warehouseman'.
