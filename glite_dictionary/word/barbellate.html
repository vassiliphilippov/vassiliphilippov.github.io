barbellate

1. ADJ

/ bɑrˈbɛleɪt / (AmE), / bɑːˈbɛleɪt / (BrE)
having or covered with barbs or bristle-like projections, such as spines, thorns, or setae.
having barbs or bristle-like projections

Examples

The cactus's barbellate spines help protect it from herbivores.
Some insect legs are barbellate, aiding in grasping or climbing surfaces.
The barbellate fruits easily attach to animal fur for dispersal.

Synset Examples

a horse with a short bristly mane
bristly shrubs
burred fruits
setaceous whiskers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: setaceous - bristle-like in shape or covered with bristles
  • Synonym: bristly - having or covered with bristles

Etymology

From Latin 'barbella' (diminutive of 'barba', meaning beard).
