Monaco

1. PROPN

/ məˈnɑːkoʊ / (AmE), / məˈnɑːkəʊ / (BrE)
A small, independent principality on the French Riviera, governed as a constitutional monarchy.
principality on the French Riviera

Examples

She spent her summer holiday in Monaco, enjoying the beaches and casinos.
Monaco is famous for its Grand Prix and luxurious lifestyle.
The Prince of Monaco attended the international summit on climate change.

Etymology

From Italian 'Monaco', from Latin 'Monachus' meaning 'monk', referring to the original settlement by monks.
