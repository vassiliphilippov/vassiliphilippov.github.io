antecubital

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.tiˈkjuː.bə.təl / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪˈkjuː.bɪ.təl / (BrE)
Relating to the area of the arm situated in front of the elbow.
of the inner elbow region
medical

Examples

The nurse located the antecubital vein to take the patient's blood sample.
Discomfort in the antecubital region may indicate an issue with the veins or nerves.
He had a small bruise on his antecubital fossa after the injection.

Synset Examples

blood was drawn from the antecubital region

Etymology

From Latin 'ante-' (before) and 'cubitum' (elbow).
