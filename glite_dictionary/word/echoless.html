echoless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛkoʊˌlɛs / (AmE), / ˈɛkəʊləs / (BrE)
(of a place or sound) having or producing no echo.
having or producing no echo

Examples

As the door closed, an echoless silence filled the abandoned hall.
The cave seemed oddly echoless, absorbing every sound we made.
Sound engineers designed the studio to be completely echoless for accurate recordings.

Synset Examples

the echoless darkness

Related Terms

  • Antonym: echoic - having the quality of an echo
  • Antonym: resonant - echoing or full of sound
