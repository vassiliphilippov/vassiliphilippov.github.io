Tuesday

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːz.deɪ / (AmE), / ˈtjuːz.deɪ / (BrE)
The day of the week between Monday and Wednesday; the third day of the week in international standards.
the day after Monday

Examples

She has a meeting every Tuesday morning before work.
They went out for dinner on Tuesday to celebrate her birthday.
Tuesdays are usually quieter at the museum than weekends.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Monday - the day before Tuesday
  • Degree Higher: Wednesday - the day after Tuesday

Etymology

From Old English 'Tiwesdæg', meaning 'Tiw's day', after the Germanic god Tiw or Tyr.
