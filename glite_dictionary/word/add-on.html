add-on

1. NOUN

/ ˈæd ˌɑn / (AmE), / ˈæd ɒn / (BrE)
a part or feature that is added to something to improve or enhance it
something added to improve something else

Examples

The new security add-on protects your computer from the latest threats.
Many cars offer a GPS navigation system as an add-on.
She installed a weather add-on to her browser for convenience.

Synset Examples

the addition of a bathroom was a major improvement
the addition of cinnamon improved the flavor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accessory - an additional item that is useful but not essential
  • Synonym: improvement - an addition or change that makes something better
