blue-eyed

1. ADJ

/ blu aɪd / (AmE), / bluː aɪd / (BrE)
having blue-colored eyes.
having blue eyes

Examples

She was a blue-eyed child with a bright smile.
Many people in Northern Europe are naturally blue-eyed.
The painting depicts a blue-eyed woman in a summer dress.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: brown-eyed - having brown eyes
  • Antonym: green-eyed - having green eyes

2. ADJ

/ bluː aɪd / (AmE), / bluː aɪd / (BrE)
Regarded with special favor or as a favorite, often by someone in authority or power.
regarded as a favorite

Examples

The manager clearly sees Tom as his blue-eyed employee who can do no wrong.
As the blue-eyed girl of her department, she often received the best assignments.
He became the blue-eyed candidate for the promotion after impressing the CEO.

Synset Examples

the fair-haired boy of the literary set

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fair-haired - regarded as a favorite, especially in a figurative sense
