DiMaggio

1. PROPN

/ dɪˈmɑʒioʊ / (AmE), / dɪˈmadʒiəʊ / (BrE)
Joe DiMaggio (1914–1999), United States professional baseball player renowned for his exceptional batting ability.
noted American baseball player (1914–1999)

Examples

Joe DiMaggio was famous for his 56-game hitting streak in Major League Baseball.
Many Yankees fans consider DiMaggio one of the greatest players of all time.
The legendary DiMaggio inspired generations with his remarkable performance on the field.
