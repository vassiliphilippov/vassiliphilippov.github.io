jinrikisha

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪnˈrɪkʃə / (AmE), / dʒɪnˈrɪkʃə / (BrE)
a small, two-wheeled cart for one passenger, traditionally pulled by a person, especially in East Asia
two-wheeled passenger cart pulled by a person

Examples

During his visit to Tokyo, he enjoyed a ride in a jinrikisha through the historic neighborhoods.
The museum had a restored jinrikisha on display as part of its transportation exhibit.
In the late nineteenth century, travelers often used a jinrikisha to get around Asian cities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rickshaw - a small covered vehicle with two wheels, pulled by a person

Etymology

From Japanese *jinrikisha* (人力車), meaning 'human-powered vehicle'.
