righthander

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtˌhændər / (AmE), / ˈraɪtˌhændə / (BrE)
a person who prefers to use their right hand for tasks requiring skill, such as writing or throwing
person who uses right hand more skillfully

Examples

She is a natural righthander and writes quickly with her right hand.
Most tools are designed with the righthander in mind, making them tricky for left-handed users.
As a pitcher, the righthander had an impressive record against left-handed batters.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: left-hander - a person who uses the left hand more skillfully than the right
  • Synonym: right-handed - using the right hand more skillfully or comfortably than the left

Etymology

Formed from 'right hand' + '-er' (agent suffix).
