39. VERB

/ ɡɪv / (AmE), / ɡɪv / (BrE)
to dedicate or direct (attention, thought, consideration, etc.) toward a particular thing or person
direct or dedicate attention or thought

Examples

You should give careful thought to your future career plans.
It's important to give priority to your health above all else.
Please give your full attention to the following instructions.

Synset Examples

give thought to
give priority to
pay attention to

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to devote - to give all or a large part of one's time or resources to something
  • Synonym: to bestow - to present or confer something, often formally or thoughtfully
  • Antonym: to withhold - to refuse to give or dedicate something

Etymology

Old English *giefan*, of Germanic origin, related to Dutch *geven* and German *geben*.
