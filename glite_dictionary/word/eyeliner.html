eyeliner

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪˌlaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌlaɪnə / (BrE)
a cosmetic applied along the edge of the eyelids to accentuate or define the eyes
cosmetic for lining eyelids

Examples

She carefully applied eyeliner to make her eyes look bigger.
My favorite eyeliner is waterproof and lasts all day.
He forgot his eyeliner in the bathroom before the performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mascara - cosmetic for coloring and thickening eyelashes

Etymology

From 'eye' + 'liner', indicating something that lines the eyes.
