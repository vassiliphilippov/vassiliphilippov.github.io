neck

1. NOUN

/ nɛk / (AmE), / nɛk / (BrE)
the part of a garment that surrounds or is close to the base of the wearer's neck, or the opening for the neck
part/opening of a garment near the neck

Examples

The neck of this sweater is a little too tight for me.
She prefers dresses with a V-shaped neck for a more flattering look.
The shirt has a high neck to keep you warm in winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collar - the part of a shirt, coat, or dress that fits around the neck
