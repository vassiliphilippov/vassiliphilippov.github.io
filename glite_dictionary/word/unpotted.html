unpotted

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpɑtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɒtɪd / (BrE)
(of a plant) not planted in a pot or removed from a pot.
not planted in a pot

Examples

The gardener left several unpotted seedlings on the table to be sorted later.
You should never leave delicate plants unpotted for too long, as their roots may dry out.
She bought a few unpotted herbs from the market and transferred them to her garden.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: potted - (of a plant) planted or grown in a pot
