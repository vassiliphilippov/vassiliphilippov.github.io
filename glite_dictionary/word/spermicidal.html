spermicidal

1. ADJ

/ spɝː.mɪˈsaɪ.dəl / (AmE), / spɜː.mɪˈsaɪ.dəl / (BrE)
Capable of destroying sperm cells or preventing their activity.
destroying sperm cells
medical

Examples

Many condoms are coated with a spermicidal lubricant for additional protection.
Some people experience irritation when using products with spermicidal agents.
Doctors may recommend alternatives if you are sensitive to spermicidal creams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contraceptive - a device or drug serving to prevent pregnancy

Etymology

From sperm (from Greek 'sperma', seed) + -cidal (from Latin 'caedere', to kill).
