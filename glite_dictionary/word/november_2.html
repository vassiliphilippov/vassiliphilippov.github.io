November 2

1. PROPN

/ noʊˈvɛmbɚ səˈkənd / (AmE), / nəʊˈvɛmbə səˈkənd / (BrE)
A day in the Christian calendar, also known as All Souls' Day, dedicated to prayer and remembrance for all the faithful departed, especially souls in purgatory.
All Souls' Day; Christian day of prayer for the dead

Examples

Many Catholics attend Mass on November 2 to pray for their departed loved ones.
In some countries, people visit cemeteries and light candles on November 2.
November 2, known as All Souls' Day, is an important date in the liturgical calendar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: All Souls' Day - Christian day of prayer and remembrance for the dead, especially in purgatory
