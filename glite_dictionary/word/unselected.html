unselected

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.səˈlɛk.tɪd / (AmE), / ʌn.sɪˈlɛk.tɪd / (BrE)
not chosen or picked out from a group or set.
not chosen or picked

Examples

Many candidates remained unselected after the first round of interviews.
Please review the list of unselected options before finalizing your decision.
Some players felt disappointed to be unselected for the starting line-up.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: selected - chosen from a group or set

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' and 'selected', from Latin 'selectus', past participle of 'seligere' (to choose).
