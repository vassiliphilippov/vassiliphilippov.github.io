to cannulize

1. VERB

/ ˈkæn.juˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkæn.juˌlaɪz / (BrE)
to insert a cannula or tube into a body part, especially a blood vessel
insert a cannula or tube
medical

Examples

The nurse expertly cannulized the patient's vein in preparation for surgery.
In emergency situations, doctors may need to quickly cannulize a large blood vessel.
Students practiced how to cannulize arteries safely during their clinical rotations.

Synset Examples

Cannulate the blood vessel in the neck

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cannulate - to insert a cannula into a body part

Etymology

From cannula (a small tube) + -ize (forming verbs meaning to cause or become).
