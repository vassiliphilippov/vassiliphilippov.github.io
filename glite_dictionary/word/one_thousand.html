one thousand

1. NUM

/ wʌn ˈθaʊzənd / (AmE), / wʌn ˈθaʊzənd / (BrE)
the cardinal number equal to 1,000; denoting a quantity consisting of a thousand items or units
the cardinal number 1,000

Examples

There were one thousand people attending the concert.
She saved one thousand dollars for her vacation.
The collection includes over one thousand rare books.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thousand - the number 1,000
  • Degree Lower: one hundred - the number 100
  • Degree Higher: ten thousand - the number 10,000

Etymology

From Old English 'ān' (one) and 'þūsend' (thousand).
