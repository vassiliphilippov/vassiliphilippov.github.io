to urinate

1. VERB

/ ˈjʊrəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈjʊərɪneɪt / (BrE)
to discharge urine from the body; to pass water
discharge urine from the body

Examples

Dogs are trained to urinate outside in the yard.
He had to urinate several times during the night.
Some medical tests require you to urinate into a special container.

Synset Examples

The sick men urinated blood

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pee - to pass urine (informal)
  • Antonym: to defecate - to discharge feces from the body

Etymology

From Latin 'urinari' (to urinate), from 'urina' (urine).
