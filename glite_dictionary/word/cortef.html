Cortef

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔr.tɛf / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.tɛf / (BrE)
A trademarked medicine containing hydrocortisone, used especially for its anti-inflammatory and immunosuppressive effects.
hydrocortisone medication (trade name)
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Cortef to help reduce the inflammation caused by my allergy.
Patients taking Cortef should be monitored for possible side effects like immune suppression.
The pharmacist explained that Cortef is a brand name for hydrocortisone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hydrocortisone - a steroid hormone used as a medication to reduce inflammation
