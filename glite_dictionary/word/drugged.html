drugged

1. ADJ

/ drʌɡd / (AmE), / drʌɡd / (BrE)
having taken or been given drugs that affect one's mind or behavior, especially narcotics or sedatives.
under the influence of drugs

Examples

He looked drugged and barely aware of his surroundings.
The kidnappers kept their victim drugged for several days.
She fell into a drugged sleep after taking the medication.

Synset Examples

knocked out by doped wine
a drugged sleep
were under the effect of the drugged sweets
in a stuperous narcotized state

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol or drugs
  • Synonym: intoxicated - affected by alcohol or drugs
