Telanthera

1. NOUN

/ tɛˈlænθərə / (AmE), / tɛˈlænθərə / (BrE)
a former genus of tropical plants in the amaranth family, now included in the genus Alternanthera
former plant genus (see Alternanthera)

Examples

The bright foliage of Telanthera was once popular in Victorian gardens.
Many species formerly known as Telanthera are now classified as Alternanthera.
Gardeners sometimes still refer to Alternanthera plants by their old name, Telanthera.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Alternanthera - genus of flowering plants in the amaranth family
