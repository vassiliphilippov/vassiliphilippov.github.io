skydiving

1. NOUN

/ ˈskaɪˌdaɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskaɪˌdaɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of jumping from an aircraft and performing acrobatics during free fall before opening a parachute
jumping from an aircraft and free falling before using a parachute

Examples

Many people find skydiving exhilarating and do it as an extreme sport.
She tried skydiving for the first time during her holiday in New Zealand.
Courses in skydiving are available at several training centers worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parachuting - the activity of descending to earth with a parachute

Etymology

From 'sky' + 'diving', first attested in the 1950s.
