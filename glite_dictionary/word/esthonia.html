Esthonia

1. PROPN

/ ɛsˈtoʊniə / (AmE), / ɛsˈtəʊniə / (BrE)
a former name for the Republic of Estonia, a country in northeastern Europe bordering the Baltic Sea, with capital Tallinn
former name for Estonia (country)
archaic

Examples

The independence of Esthonia was recognized after the end of World War I.
Many historical documents refer to the Baltic nation as Esthonia.
Trade routes through Esthonia were significant in medieval European history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Estonia - modern name for the country in northeastern Europe

Etymology

Latinized form of the native name; Esthonia was formerly used in English for 'Estonia'.
