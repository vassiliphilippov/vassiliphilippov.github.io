sq in

1. NOUN

/ skw ɛr ɪn / (AmE), / skw ɛər ɪn / (BrE)
abbreviation for square inch, a unit of area equal to one inch by one inch
abbreviation for square inch (area unit)

Examples

The kitchen measures about 100 sq in in total area.
Please cut a piece of paper exactly five sq in.
The label should not exceed 2 sq in for this product.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: square inch - a unit of area measuring one inch by one inch square
