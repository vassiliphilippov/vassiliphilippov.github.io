timetable

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪmˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmˌteɪbəl / (BrE)
a schedule listing the times of arrivals, departures, or other planned events
schedule of planned times or events

Examples

The train timetable shows departures and arrivals for the entire week.
Please check the bus timetable before planning your trip.
She pinned the exam timetable on the notice board for everyone to see.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schedule - a plan that lists when tasks or events are supposed to happen

Etymology

From 'time' + 'table', originally referring to a table or chart of times.

2. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪmˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmˌteɪbl̩ / (BrE)
a plan or list showing the times at which particular events will take place
schedule of events with times

Examples

The school timetable was changed to accommodate new classes.
I checked the train timetable to see when the next train arrives.
Please send me your weekly timetable so we can arrange a meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schedule - a plan of things to be done and the times they will happen
