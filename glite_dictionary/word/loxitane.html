Loxitane

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑksɪˌteɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɒksɪˌteɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked antipsychotic drug (loxapine) used to treat schizophrenia and other mental disorders.

medical
