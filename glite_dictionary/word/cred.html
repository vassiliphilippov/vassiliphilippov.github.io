cred

1. NOUN

/ krɛd / (AmE), / krɛd / (BrE)
Respect or credibility, especially among a particular group such as young or fashionable urban individuals.
respect or credibility in a group
informal

Examples

He's got a lot of cred in the music scene.
Wearing vintage sneakers only adds to her cred among her friends.
The new brand is trying to gain some cred with young consumers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: credibility - the quality of being trusted or believed in
  • Synonym: street cred - respect or credibility in urban culture

Etymology

Shortened form of 'credibility.'
