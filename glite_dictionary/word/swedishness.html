Swedishness

1. NOUN

/ ˈswidɪʃ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈswiːdɪʃ.nɪs / (BrE)
The characteristic qualities, attitudes, or cultural identity associated with being Swedish.
the quality of being Swedish

Examples

Many immigrants find Swedishness difficult to define when they first arrive in Sweden.
The quiet reserve often associated with Swedishness can be both charming and puzzling to outsiders.
Debates about national identity often center around the concept of Swedishness.

Synset Examples

What is Swedishness? Well, it's a very broad concept, with social codes that take years to master.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Swede - a native or inhabitant of Sweden
  • Synonym: Swedish - relating to Sweden, its people, language, or culture

Etymology

From Swedish + -ness (noun-forming suffix indicating a state or quality).
