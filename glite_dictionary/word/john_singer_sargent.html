John Singer Sargent

1. PROPN

/ dʒɑn ˈsɪŋər ˈsɑrdʒənt / (AmE), / dʒɒn ˈsɪŋə ˈsɑːdʒənt / (BrE)
(1856–1925) a prominent American painter, born in Italy, renowned for his elegant portraits of society figures.
American portrait painter (1856–1925)

Examples

The exhibition featured several masterpieces by John Singer Sargent.
Art historians praise John Singer Sargent for his innovative brushwork and striking likenesses.
Among his most famous works, John Singer Sargent's 'Portrait of Madame X' stands out.
