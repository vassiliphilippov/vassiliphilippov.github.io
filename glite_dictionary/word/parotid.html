parotid

1. ADJ

/ pəˈrɑː.tɪd / (AmE), / pəˈrɒ.tɪd / (BrE)
relating to or situated near the parotid gland, a major salivary gland located near the ear.
relating to the parotid gland
medical

Examples

He underwent surgery to remove a tumor from his parotid gland.
Swelling in the parotid region can indicate an infection or inflammation.
The doctor examined the patient's parotid area for any signs of tenderness or lumps.

Etymology

From Greek 'parotidēs', from 'para' (beside) + 'ous, ōt-' (ear).
