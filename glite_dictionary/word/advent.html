advent

1. NOUN

/ ˈædˌvɛnt / (AmE), / ˈæd.vɛnt / (BrE)
the arrival of a notable person, event, or thing, especially one that has been awaited
arrival of something important

Examples

The advent of the smartphone revolutionized how people communicate around the world.
Many historians note the advent of the printing press as a turning point in human knowledge.
With the advent of spring, the days grew warmer and flowers began to bloom.

Synset Examples

the advent of the computer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arrival - the act of arriving at a place
  • Antonym: departure - the act of leaving, especially to start a journey

Etymology

From Latin adventus (arrival, approach), from advenire (to arrive).
