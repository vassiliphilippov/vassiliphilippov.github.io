despairing

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈspɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈspɛːrɪŋ / (BrE)
feeling or showing a loss of hope; marked by despair.
feeling or showing despair

Examples

She gave a despairing sigh when she saw the damage to her car.
His despairing letters revealed how hopeless he felt about the situation.
Despite the despairing mood, her friends tried to cheer her up.

Synset Examples

a despairing view of the world situation
the last despairing plea of the condemned criminal
a desperate cry for help
helpless and desperate — as if at the end of his tether
her desperate screams

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desperate - feeling or showing a hopeless sense that a situation is so bad as to be impossible to deal with
  • Antonym: hopeful - feeling or inspiring optimism or a positive outlook

Etymology

From despair + -ing, formed from Anglo-Norman and Old French "desperer" (to lose hope).
