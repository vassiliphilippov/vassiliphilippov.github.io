Buenos Aires

1. PROPN

/ ˌbweɪnəs ˈaɪriːz / (AmE), / ˌbweɪnɒs ˈɛːriːz / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Argentina, an important port and cultural center located in eastern Argentina near the border with Uruguay
capital and largest city of Argentina

Examples

They spent two weeks exploring Buenos Aires and its historic neighborhoods.
The tango originated in Buenos Aires and is still popular throughout the city.
Flights to Buenos Aires are often full during the summer tourist season.

Etymology

Spanish, meaning 'fair winds'.
