Maalox

1. PROPN

/ ˈmæˌlɑks / (AmE), / ˈmɑːlɒks / (BrE)
A trademarked antacid medicine used to relieve heartburn and indigestion.
brand of antacid medicine

Examples

After eating spicy food, she took some Maalox to soothe her stomach.
The doctor recommended Maalox for occasional indigestion.
There was a bottle of Maalox in the medicine cabinet for heartburn relief.
