matrimonial

1. ADJ

/ ˌmætrɪˈmoʊniəl / (AmE), / ˌmætrɪˈməʊniəl / (BrE)
relating to marriage or the state of being married.
relating to marriage

Examples

They attended a matrimonial counseling session to resolve their differences.
The couple celebrated their tenth matrimonial anniversary with close friends and family.
Certain countries have strict laws regarding matrimonial property and divorce.

Synset Examples

marital status
marital fidelity
married bliss

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marital - relating to marriage; connected with married life

Etymology

From Latin matrimonialis, from matrimonium (marriage).
