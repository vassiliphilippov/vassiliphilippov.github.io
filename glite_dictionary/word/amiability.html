amiability

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪmiəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌeɪmɪəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of having a friendly and pleasant manner; cheerfulness and agreeableness
friendliness; pleasant manner

Examples

Everyone was charmed by her amiability and warm smile at the party.
His amiability made him popular among colleagues and friends alike.
Despite the stressful situation, she maintained her amiability throughout the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly
  • Antonym: hostility - unfriendliness or opposition

Etymology

From Latin 'amabilis' (lovable), from 'amare' (to love).

2. NOUN

/ ˌeɪmiəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌæmiəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
A friendly and pleasant manner or disposition that makes one easy to approach or talk to.
friendliness; affability

Examples

Her amiability quickly put everyone at ease during the meeting.
Despite the stressful situation, his amiability never faltered.
Guests were charmed by the amiability of the hosts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly
  • Antonym: hostility - unfriendliness or opposition

Etymology

From Latin 'amicabilis' (friendly) via French 'amabilité'.
