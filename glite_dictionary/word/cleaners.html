cleaners

1. NOUN

/ ˈkliːnərz / (AmE), / ˈkliːnəz / (BrE)
a shop or business where clothes and other textiles are professionally cleaned, especially by dry cleaning
shop for dry cleaning services

Examples

I need to take my suit to the cleaners before the wedding this weekend.
She picked up the freshly pressed shirts from the cleaners on her way home.
There is a new cleaners that just opened near the train station.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dry cleaners - shop where clothes are dry cleaned
