Communist

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmjənɪst / (AmE), / ˈkɒmjʊnɪst / (BrE)
a member of a Communist party, especially one supporting or advocating communism
member of a Communist party

Examples

She was a lifelong Communist who campaigned actively for workers' rights.
During the 1950s, being labeled a Communist could damage your reputation in America.
Several prominent Communists attended the international congress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: socialist - a member of a socialist party or supporter of socialism
  • Antonym: capitalist - a person who supports capitalism or is a member of a capitalist party

Etymology

From French communiste, from communisme (communism).
