Handies Peak

1. PROPN

/ ˈhændiz piːk / (AmE), / ˈhændiz piːk / (BrE)
a mountain peak in the San Juan Mountains of southwestern Colorado, rising to 14,048 feet (4,287 meters)
mountain peak in southwestern Colorado

Examples

Many hikers aspire to climb Handies Peak because it is one of Colorado's famous fourteeners.
The view from the summit of Handies Peak stretches across the entire San Juan Mountain range.
During the summer, wildflowers decorate the trails leading up to Handies Peak.
