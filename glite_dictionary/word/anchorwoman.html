anchorwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈæŋkɚˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈæŋkəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A female television or radio journalist who presents and coordinates a broadcast, often linking contributions from other correspondents.
female television news presenter

Examples

The anchorwoman introduced the latest breaking news with confidence and clarity.
Every evening, viewers tune in to watch their favorite anchorwoman on the national news channel.
As the anchorwoman, she seamlessly coordinated reports from correspondents around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anchorman - a male television or radio news presenter
  • Synonym: anchor - a person who presents and coordinates a news broadcast

Etymology

Formed from 'anchor' (as in news anchor) + 'woman', paralleling 'anchorman'.
