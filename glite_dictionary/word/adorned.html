adorned

1. ADJ

/ əˈdɔrnd / (AmE), / əˈdɔːnd / (BrE)
decorated or embellished with something to enhance beauty or distinction
decorated; embellished

Examples

The hall was adorned with colorful banners for the festival.
She wore a crown adorned with sparkling jewels at the ceremony.
Ancient temples are often adorned with intricate carvings and statues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decorated - having decorations added for ornamentation
  • Antonym: plain - not decorated or adorned; simple

Etymology

Past participle of 'adorn', from Latin 'adornare' (to equip, decorate).
