sumptuous

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌmp.tʃu.əs / (AmE), / ˈsʌmp.tju.əs / (BrE)
splendid and expensive-looking; marked by lavishness and rich quality
rich and luxurious in appearance

Examples

The banquet was served in a sumptuous hall adorned with crystal chandeliers and velvet drapes.
She wore a sumptuous silk gown that caught everyone's attention at the party.
We enjoyed a sumptuous breakfast at the luxury hotel, with freshly baked pastries and exotic fruit.

Synset Examples

a princely sum
gilded dining rooms
these architecture magazines are full of the lush interiors of the rich and famous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luxurious - extremely comfortable or elegant, especially in a way that involves great expense
  • Synonym: lavish - rich, elaborate, or luxurious

Etymology

From Latin 'sumptuosus', from 'sumptus' (expense).
