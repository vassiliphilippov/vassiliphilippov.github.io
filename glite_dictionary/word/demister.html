demister

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈmɪstər / (AmE), / dɪˈmɪstə / (BrE)
a device or heater in a vehicle that removes mist or condensation from the windshield
heater that removes mist from a car windshield

Examples

I turned on the demister to clear the fog from the windshield.
The car's demister worked quickly on a cold winter morning.
Please make sure the demister is functioning before the weather gets cold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defroster - device that removes frost or mist from a vehicle's windshield

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'de-' (removal) and 'mister' (something that produces or is affected by mist).
