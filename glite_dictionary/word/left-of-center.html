left-of-center

1. ADJ

/ ˌlɛft əv ˈsɛn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˌlɛft əv ˈsɛn.tə / (BrE)
having moderately left-wing political views or supporting policies that are slightly to the left of the political center.
moderately left-wing in politics

Examples

His policies are generally considered left-of-center in comparison to other candidates.
The party's platform shifted slightly left-of-center after the recent elections.
Many students hold left-of-center views on social and economic issues.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: right-of-center - having moderately right-wing political views
  • Degree Higher: left-wing - having strongly leftist or progressive political views
