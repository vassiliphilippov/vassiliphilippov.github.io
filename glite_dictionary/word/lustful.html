lustful

1. ADJ

/ ˈlʌst.fəl / (AmE), / ˈlʌst.fəl / (BrE)
Having or showing strong sexual desire.
filled with sexual desire

Examples

He cast a lustful glance in her direction, making her uncomfortable.
The novel was criticized for its lustful descriptions and explicit scenes.
She tried to ignore his lustful advances during the evening.

Synset Examples

eluding lubricious embraces
prurient literature
prurient thoughts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lecherous - having or showing excessive sexual desire
  • Synonym: prurient - having or encouraging an excessive interest in sexual matters
  • Antonym: chaste - abstaining from sexual activity or not having sexual nature

Etymology

From Middle English *lustful*, from Old English *lust* (desire, pleasure) + *-ful* (full of).
