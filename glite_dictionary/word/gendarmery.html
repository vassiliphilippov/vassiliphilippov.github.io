gendarmery

1. NOUN

/ ʒɑnˈdɑrməri / (AmE), / ʒɑːnˈdɑːməri / (BrE)
A police force, especially a military-style force of gendarmes, or the body of gendarmes collectively, particularly in France or countries with similar systems.
military-style police force; body of gendarmes

Examples

The French gendarmery played a crucial role in maintaining order during the festival.
International observers praised the professionalism of the local gendarmery.
The villagers called the gendarmery when they noticed suspicious activity near the border.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gendarmerie - military police force in France and some other countries

Etymology

From French 'gendarmerie', from 'gendarme' (armed man).
