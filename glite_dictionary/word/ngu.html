NGU

1. NOUN

/ ɛn dʒiː juː / (AmE), / ɛn dʒiː juː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for nongonococcal urethritis, a sexually transmitted infection of the urethra usually caused by chlamydia.
nongonococcal urethritis (STD)
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed him with NGU after reviewing the test results.
Treatment for NGU typically involves a course of antibiotics.
Many cases of NGU are linked to chlamydia infection in men.

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'nongonococcal urethritis.'
