Ononis

1. NOUN

/ əˈnoʊnɪs / (AmE), / əˈnəʊnɪs / (BrE)
A genus of European subshrubs or herbs, commonly known as restharrow, with pink, purple, or yellow flowers, often solitary or clustered.
a genus of flowering plants (restharrow)

Examples

Several species of Ononis are found in European meadows and roadsides.
Her research focused on the medicinal properties of the Ononis genus.
The clustered pink flowers make Ononis easy to spot among other wild plants.

Etymology

From Greek onōnis (a kind of plant, restharrow).
