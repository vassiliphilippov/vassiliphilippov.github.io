sadhe

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːdɛ / (AmE), / ˈsɑːdɛ / (BrE)
The eighteenth letter of the Hebrew alphabet (צ), transliterated as 'ts' or 'ṣ'.
eighteenth letter of the Hebrew alphabet

Examples

The Hebrew letter sadhe is sometimes transliterated as 'ts' or 'ṣ' in English.
In learning Hebrew, Anna struggled to pronounce the unique sound of sadhe.
On the chart, sadhe appears between pe and qoph in the Hebrew alphabet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zayin - seventh letter of the Hebrew alphabet
  • Synonym: aleph - first letter of the Hebrew alphabet
  • Synonym: tav - last letter of the Hebrew alphabet

Etymology

From Hebrew צָדֵי‎ (tsadé), the name of the letter.
