Saint Lucia

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt ˈluːʃə / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈluːʃə / (BrE)
an island country in the eastern Caribbean, part of the Windward Islands, known for its volcanic landscape and beaches.
Caribbean island country

Examples

We spent our honeymoon in Saint Lucia and enjoyed the beautiful beaches.
The Pitons are famous volcanic peaks located in Saint Lucia.
Many tourists visit Saint Lucia for its lush rainforests and vibrant culture.

Etymology

Named after Saint Lucy of Syracuse by French colonizers.
