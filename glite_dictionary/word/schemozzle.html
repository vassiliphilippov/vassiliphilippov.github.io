schemozzle

1. NOUN

/ ʃəˈmɑzəl / (AmE), / ʃəˈmɒzəl / (BrE)
an episode or situation that is confused, disordered, or chaotic; a mess
a confused or chaotic situation

Examples

After the power failure, the meeting descended into a schemozzle and nothing was resolved.
The new regulations created a bureaucratic schemozzle that frustrated everyone involved.
It turned into a complete schemozzle when the teams both claimed victory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shambles - a state of complete disorder or ruin
  • Synonym: mess - a state of confusion or untidiness
  • Antonym: order - a condition of logical or consistent arrangement

Etymology

From Yiddish *shlimazl* (bad luck, unlucky person), itself from German *schlim* (bad) + Hebrew *mazal* (luck).
