dimension

1. NOUN

/ dəˈmɛnʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈmɛnʃən / (BrE)
a measurable aspect or feature of something that distinguishes it from others; a way in which something can vary or be classified
measurable aspect or distinguishing feature

Examples

The experiment examined each dimension of personality to understand how people differ.
In psychology, intelligence is often measured across different dimensions such as memory and reasoning.
Social status is just one dimension along which members of society can be classified.

Synset Examples

self-confidence is not an endearing property

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aspect - a particular part or feature of something
  • Synonym: property - an attribute or characteristic of something

Etymology

From Latin 'dimensio' (a measuring), from 'dimetiri' (to measure out).
