thermohydrometer

1. NOUN

/ ˌθɜːrmoʊhaɪˈdrɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊhaɪˈdrɒmɪtə / (BrE)
A hydrometer fitted with a thermometer, used to measure both the density and the temperature of a liquid.
hydrometer with a thermometer

Examples

The laboratory technician used a thermohydrometer to check the density and temperature of the solution.
You should always read the thermohydrometer at eye level for accurate results.
A thermohydrometer is essential when brewing to monitor both sugar content and temperature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hydrometer - device for measuring the density of liquids

Etymology

From thermo- (relating to heat/temperature) and hydrometer (fluid density meter).
