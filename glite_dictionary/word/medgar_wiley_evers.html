Medgar Wiley Evers

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɛdɡɚ ˈwaɪli ˈɛvɚz / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɡə ˈwaɪli ˈɛvəz / (BrE)
(1925–1963) an American civil rights activist in Mississippi, known for his leadership in the movement to end racial segregation, and assassinated in 1963.
