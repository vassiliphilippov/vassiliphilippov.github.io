locker-room

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɑkɚ ˌrum / (AmE), / ˈlɒkə ˌruːm / (BrE)
(of language, humor, or behavior) characteristic of or suitable for a locker room, typically coarse or crude.
characteristic of a locker room; coarse or crude

Examples

Some comedians are known for their locker-room humor, which may offend some audiences.
His speech was full of locker-room talk that others found inappropriate for work.
The team's locker-room banter could sometimes be a bit much for newcomers.
