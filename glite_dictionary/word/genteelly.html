genteelly

1. ADV

/ dʒənˈtili / (AmE), / dʒɛnˈtiːli / (BrE)
In a refined, polite, or elegant manner.
in a refined or elegant way

Examples

He always spoke genteelly, even when he was annoyed.
The couple dined genteelly at the country mansion.
Despite their limited means, they tried to live genteelly.

Synset Examples

the English lived genteelly in India

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genteel - showing refined manners or elegance
  • Antonym: coarsely - in a rough or crude manner

Etymology

From "genteel" + adverbial suffix "-ly"; "genteel" from French "gentil" (well-born, noble).
