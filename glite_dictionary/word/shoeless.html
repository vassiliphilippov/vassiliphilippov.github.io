shoeless

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃoʊləs / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊləs / (BrE)
Not wearing shoes.
not wearing shoes

Examples

The children ran shoeless across the grassy field all summer long.
After losing his luggage, he arrived at the hotel shoeless and tired.
She found a small, shoeless puppy shivering beside the road.

Synset Examples

the barefoot boy
shoeless Joe Jackson

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barefoot - without any covering on the feet

Etymology

Formed by combining 'shoe' and the suffix '-less', meaning 'without'.
