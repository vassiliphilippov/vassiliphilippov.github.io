untufted

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtʌftɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʌftɪd / (BrE)
lacking tufts; not decorated or covered with tufts.
not having tufts

Examples

The wildcat was easily identified by its untufted ears and spotted coat.
Unlike its tufted cousins, this plant produces untufted leaves that look remarkably smooth.
Collectors prefer the untufted variety for textile samples due to its even appearance.

Synset Examples

untufted ears

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tufted - having or decorated with tufts

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'tufted'.
