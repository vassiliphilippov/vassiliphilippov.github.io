undisputedly

1. ADV

/ ˌʌndɪˈspjuːtɪdli / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈspjuːtɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that is not disputed; unquestionably or without challenge.
in a way that is not disputed

Examples

She was undisputedly the best athlete on the team that year.
The restaurant is undisputedly famous for its traditional cuisine.
His talent was undisputedly recognized by critics and fans alike.

Synset Examples

you write as if this fact, whilst inarguably forever condemning me to the ranks of Bohemianism, nevertheless earned for me the right of entry into any company

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indisputably - in a way that cannot be disputed; unquestionably

Etymology

From 'undisputed' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
