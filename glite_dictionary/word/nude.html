nude

1. NOUN

/ nud / (AmE), / njuːd / (BrE)
the state of not wearing any clothes; nakedness, especially as expressed by the phrase 'in the nude'.
the state of being naked

Examples

He posed for the painting in the nude.
They preferred to swim in the nude at the secluded beach.
The artist specialized in studies of the human nude.

Synset Examples

they swam in the nude

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naked - without clothes; not covered

Etymology

From Latin 'nudus' meaning 'naked'.
