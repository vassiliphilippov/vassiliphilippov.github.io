creamery

1. NOUN

/ ˈkriːməri / (AmE), / ˈkriːməri / (BrE)
a place where dairy products such as butter and cheese are made or sold
place making or selling dairy products

Examples

The local creamery produces award-winning cheeses and fresh butter.
We visited a creamery to see how traditional butter is made.
Their family has owned the same creamery for generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dairy - a place where milk is processed and products are made

Etymology

From 'cream' + '-ery', patterned after terms like 'brewery'.
