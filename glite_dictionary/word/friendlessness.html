friendlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛndləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈfrɛn(d)ləsnəs / (BrE)
The state of having no friends.
the state of having no friends

Examples

Despite his friendlessness, he remained optimistic and cheerful.
The novel explores the pain and friendlessness of its main character.
She struggled with feelings of friendlessness after moving to a new city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loneliness - the state of being alone or isolated
  • Antonym: companionship - the state of having company or friends

Etymology

From 'friendless' (without friends) + '-ness' (state or quality).
