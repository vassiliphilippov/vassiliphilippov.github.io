unimpregnated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪmˈprɛɡ.nə.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪmˈprɛɡ.nɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
(of an egg or female animal) not fertilized by a male; not impregnated.
not fertilized; not impregnated

Examples

The scientist examined the unimpregnated eggs under a microscope.
Most of the collected ova remained unimpregnated after the procedure.
Only the unimpregnated embryos were suitable for this type of research.

Synset Examples

an unfertilized egg

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fertilized - (of an egg or ovum) having undergone fertilization
  • Antonym: impregnated - having become pregnant or made pregnant

Etymology

Formed from the prefix un- (not) + impregnated (from Latin impraegnatus, made pregnant).
