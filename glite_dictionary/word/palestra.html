palestra

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɛstrə / (AmE), / pəˈlɛstrə / (BrE)
In ancient Greece or Rome, a public place devoted to the training of wrestlers and other athletes.
ancient Greek or Roman athletic training place

Examples

The ruins of the palestra reveal how important athletic training was in ancient society.
In his studies of ancient architecture, he described the typical design of a Greek palestra.
Wrestlers would gather in the palestra each morning for rigorous exercise and instruction.

Etymology

From Latin 'palaestra', from Greek 'palaistra' meaning 'wrestling school'.
