dawah

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑːwɑ / (AmE), / ˈdɑːwɑː / (BrE)
the act of inviting or calling people to embrace Islam, especially through missionary work and religious outreach
Islamic missionary work or religious outreach

Examples

Many organizations are dedicated to dawah in both Muslim and non-Muslim communities.
He traveled across Africa engaging in dawah and promoting interfaith dialogue.
Social media has become an important tool for dawah among younger generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: missionary work - the organized activity of spreading a religious faith

Etymology

From Arabic daʿwah (invitation, call).
