ONI

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊ ˌɛn ˈaɪ / (AmE), / ˌəʊ ˌɛn ˈaɪ / (BrE)
the military intelligence agency responsible for intelligence, counterintelligence, and security for the United States Navy
U.S. Navy intelligence agency

Examples

The ONI is responsible for gathering naval intelligence to support United States naval operations.
After analyzing the data, ONI issued a warning about potential threats to maritime security.
Many declassified ONI reports are now available to the public for historical research.

Etymology

Acronym of Office of Naval Intelligence.
