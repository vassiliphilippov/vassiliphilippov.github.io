Lansing

1. PROPN

/ ˈlænsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
the capital city of the state of Michigan, located in southern Michigan on the Grand River
capital city of Michigan, USA
american

Examples

The state government of Michigan is based in Lansing.
During our road trip, we spent a night in Lansing and toured the capitol building.
Many students in Lansing attend Michigan State University.

Etymology

Named after Lansing, New York, which was itself named for John Lansing, an American jurist and politician.
