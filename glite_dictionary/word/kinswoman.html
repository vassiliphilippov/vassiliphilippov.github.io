kinswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪnzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈkɪnzwʊmən / (BrE)
a female relative
female relative

Examples

She invited her closest kinswoman to join the family celebration.
The estate was inherited by a distant kinswoman of the late owner.
He found comfort in the support of his only surviving kinswoman.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: kinsman - a male relative
  • Synonym: relative - a person connected by blood or marriage

Etymology

From 'kin' (Old English 'cynn', family or race) + 'woman'.
