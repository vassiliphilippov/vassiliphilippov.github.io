Negress

1. NOUN

/ ˈniːɡrəs / (AmE), / ˈniːɡrəs / (BrE)
(offensive, archaic) A Black woman or girl.
offensive term for a Black woman
offensive archaic

Examples

The author described the central character as a proud and dignified Negress.
Historical texts sometimes used the word Negress to refer to Black women.
She rejected the label Negress as offensive and outdated in modern society.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Negro - A person of Black African descent (offensive, archaic)
  • Synonym: Black woman - A woman of Black African descent

Etymology

Formed from 'Negro' (from Spanish or Portuguese 'negro', meaning 'black') with the feminine suffix '-ess'.
