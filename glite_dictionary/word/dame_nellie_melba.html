Dame Nellie Melba

1. PROPN

/ deɪm ˈnɛli ˈmɛlbə / (AmE), / deɪm ˈnɛli ˈmɛlbə / (BrE)
(1861–1931) An Australian operatic soprano renowned for her international career and influence on the world of opera.
famous Australian operatic soprano

Examples

Dame Nellie Melba performed to sold-out audiences across Europe and the United States.
The legacy of Dame Nellie Melba lives on in the world of opera and music education.
Many musicians admire Dame Nellie Melba for her remarkable vocal technique and stage presence.

Etymology

Stage name 'Melba' is from her native city, Melbourne, Australia.
