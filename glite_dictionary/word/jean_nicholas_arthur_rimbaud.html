Jean Nicholas Arthur Rimbaud

1. PROPN

/ ʒɑn nɪˈkɑləs ɑrˈtuːʁ ræmˈboʊ / (AmE), / ʒɑ̃ nikɔla aʁtyʁ ʁɛ̃bo / (BrE)
(1854–1891) A French poet whose innovative and influential works helped shape modern poetry and inspired the surrealists.
French poet (1854–1891), influenced surrealism

Examples

Jean Nicholas Arthur Rimbaud wrote many of his most important poems before the age of twenty.
The surrealist movement drew great inspiration from Jean Nicholas Arthur Rimbaud's revolutionary style.
Many critics regard Jean Nicholas Arthur Rimbaud as a major influence on twentieth-century poetry.
