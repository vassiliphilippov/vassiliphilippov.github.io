puckishness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌk.ɪʃ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈpʌk.ɪʃ.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being playfully mischievous or impish
playful or impish mischievousness

Examples

His puckishness charmed everyone at the party, making even the shyest guests laugh.
There's a certain puckishness in her smile that hints at hidden jokes.
Critics admired the author's puckishness in weaving subtle pranks into the narrative.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mischievousness - the quality of causing or fondness for causing trouble in a playful way

Etymology

From 'puckish' (impish, mischievous), derived from Puck, a mischievous sprite in English folklore.
