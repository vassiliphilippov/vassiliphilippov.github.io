Woody Guthrie

1. PROPN

/ ˈwʊdi ˈɡʌθri / (AmE), / ˈwʊdi ˈɡʌθri / (BrE)
(1912–1967) United States folk singer and songwriter, known for his influential political and social songs.
American folk singer and songwriter

Examples

Many modern musicians have cited Woody Guthrie as a major influence on their work.
The songs of Woody Guthrie often addressed the struggles of working-class Americans.
A mural honoring Woody Guthrie was unveiled in his hometown last year.
