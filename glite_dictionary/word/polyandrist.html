polyandrist

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɑːliˈændrɪst / (AmE), / ˌpɒliˈændrɪst / (BrE)
a woman who has more than one husband at the same time
woman with multiple husbands

Examples

In certain Himalayan communities, a polyandrist is not uncommon due to cultural and economic factors.
The sociology lecture discussed the rare case of a polyandrist family structure.
As a polyandrist, she was married to three brothers who shared household responsibilities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polygamist - person with more than one spouse at the same time
  • Synonym: polyandry - the practice of a woman having more than one husband
  • Antonym: polygynist - a man with two or more wives

Etymology

From Greek 'poly-' (many) and 'anēr' (man, husband), formed by analogy with 'polyandry'.
