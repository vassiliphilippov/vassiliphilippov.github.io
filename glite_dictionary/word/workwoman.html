workwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɜrkˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman employed to do manual or industrial work
female manual or industrial worker

Examples

She was the only workwoman in the entire factory floor.
The skilled workwoman completed the repairs with remarkable speed.
Each workwoman received new safety equipment after the policy update.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workman - a man employed to do manual or industrial work
  • Synonym: worker - a person who works, especially one engaged in manual labor

Etymology

Formed from 'work' + 'woman', on the pattern of 'workman'.
