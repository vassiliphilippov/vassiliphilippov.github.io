riant

1. ADJ

/ ˈraɪənt / (AmE), / ˈraɪənt / (BrE)
smiling, cheerful, or showing happiness; expressing pleasure or mirth
smiling or cheerful

Examples

The landscape appeared particularly riant in the golden afternoon light.
Her riant smile made everyone feel instantly at ease.

Related Terms

Etymology
