Bubalus bubalis

1. NOUN

/ buˈbeɪləs buˈbɑːlɪs / (AmE), / bjuːˈbeɪləs bjuːˈbɑːlɪs / (BrE)
a large domesticated Asian buffalo, commonly used as a draft animal and for its milk and meat
domesticated Asian water buffalo

Examples

The Bubalus bubalis is widely used in Southeast Asia for plowing flooded rice fields.
Many villages depend on the Bubalus bubalis for milk, meat, and as a working animal.
Researchers are studying the genetic traits of Bubalus bubalis to improve disease resistance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water buffalo - common term for Bubalus bubalis, a domesticated Asian buffalo

Etymology

From Latin scientific name, where 'Bubalus' means 'buffalo' and 'bubalis' means 'like an ox or buffalo'.
