to versify

1. VERB

/ ˈvɜrsɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈvɜːsɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to compose verses or to express something in the form of verse
compose or put into verse

Examples

She decided to versify her experiences from the trip into a series of poems.
The author skillfully versified the legend, making it accessible to modern readers.
He spent hours each evening versifying stories he had heard in his childhood.

Synset Examples

He versified the ancient saga

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to poetize - to write poetry or compose in verse

Etymology

From Latin versificare, from versus (verse) + facere (to make).
