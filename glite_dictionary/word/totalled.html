totalled

1. ADJ

/ ˈtoʊt̬əld / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəld / (BrE)
(of a vehicle) so badly damaged in an accident that it is considered beyond repair and not worth fixing.
vehicle damaged beyond repair

Examples

After the crash, the car was totalled and had to be scrapped.
The mechanic told me the vehicle was totalled and not worth repairing.
Her insurance company paid out the value of the totalled SUV.

Synset Examples

the insurance adjuster declared the automobile totaled

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrecked - damaged or destroyed, especially in an accident
  • Antonym: intact - not damaged or impaired in any way; complete
