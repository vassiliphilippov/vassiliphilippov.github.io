unschooled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈskuːld / (AmE), / ʌnˈskuːld / (BrE)
not having received formal education or schooling; lacking education or training.
lacking formal education or training

Examples

Many unschooled workers struggle to find stable employment in a changing economy.
Her unschooled approach to painting gave her artwork a raw and original quality.
The organization provides lessons for unschooled children in rural villages.

Synset Examples

untaught people whose verbal skills are grossly deficient
an untutored genius
uneducated children

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uneducated - not having received much education
  • Antonym: educated - having received education, especially formal schooling

Etymology

From un- (not) + schooled (educated; trained).
