olivenite

1. NOUN

/ əˈlɪvəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ɒˈlɪvɪnaɪt / (BrE)
A rare green to black mineral consisting of hydrated copper arsenate, typically found in copper deposits.
rare green to black mineral

Examples

The bright green crystals were identified as olivenite by the mineralogist.
Samples of olivenite are often sought after by collectors.
Olivenite forms in the oxidized zones of copper ore deposits.

Etymology

From German 'Olivin', relating to its olive-green color, combined with '-ite' for minerals.
