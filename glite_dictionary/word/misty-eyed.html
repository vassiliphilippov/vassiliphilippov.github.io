misty-eyed

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɪstiˌaɪd / (AmE), / ˌmɪstiˈaɪd / (BrE)
having eyes blurred with tears or appearing as if about to cry, often due to emotion
eyes blurred with tears; about to cry

Examples

She grew misty-eyed as she remembered her childhood home.
The movie's ending left everyone feeling misty-eyed and nostalgic.
He tried to hide his misty-eyed expression when he received the award.

Synset Examples

sad and misty-eyed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tearful - showing or accompanied by tears
  • Antonym: dry-eyed - not showing tears; without tears

Etymology

From 'misty' (resembling mist) + 'eyed' (having eyes).
