idyllically

1. ADV

/ aɪˈdɪlɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˈdɪlɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely pleasant, peaceful, or picturesque, like an idyll.
in an extremely pleasant or picturesque way

Examples

The small cottage was idyllically situated beside a crystal-clear lake.
They spent an idyllically peaceful afternoon in the countryside.
The novel describes village life idyllically, focusing on its charm and tranquility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idyllic - extremely pleasant, peaceful, or picturesque

Etymology

From 'idyllic' + '-ally', ultimately from Greek eidullion (short poem).
