Charles Edward Ives

1. PROPN

/ tʃɑːrlz ˈɛd.wɚd aɪvz / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːlz ˈɛd.wəd aɪvz / (BrE)
(1874–1954) An American composer known for his innovative use of polytonality and modernist techniques.
