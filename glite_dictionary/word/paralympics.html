Paralympics

1. PROPN

/ ˌpærəˈlɪmpɪks / (AmE), / ˌparəˈlɪmpɪks / (BrE)
An international multi-sport event for athletes with disabilities, held after the Olympic Games and organized in parallel with them.
international sports event for athletes with disabilities

Examples

She won three medals at the Paralympics in Tokyo last year.
The Paralympics showcase the incredible talents of athletes with disabilities from around the world.
After training for years, he finally qualified to compete in the Paralympics.

Synset Examples

One of them is the channels that NBC as the broadcasting rights owner for the United States will use to air the Paralympic Games on.
McKenzie, a silver and bronze Paralympic medalist in wheelchair basketball, retired from the game after the 2012 Summer Paralympics in London
Wikinews interviews academic Simon Ličen about attitudes towards US Paralympics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Olympics - the Olympic Games, a leading international sporting event

Etymology

Blend of 'paraplegic' and 'Olympics'; officially used since 1960.
