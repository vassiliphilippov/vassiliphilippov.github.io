undergarment

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌndərˌɡɑrmənt / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌɡɑːmənt / (BrE)
A piece of clothing worn under outer clothes, typically next to the skin.
piece of clothing worn underneath

Examples

She bought a new undergarment to wear under her dress for the wedding.
All visitors must wear an undergarment beneath safety uniforms for hygiene reasons.
His suitcase was full of socks, shirts, and every type of undergarment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: underwear - clothing worn under outer clothes

Etymology

From 'under' + 'garment', first recorded in the mid-19th century.
