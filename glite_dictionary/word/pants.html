pants

1. NOUN

/ pænts / (AmE), / pɑːnts / (BrE)
Underpants worn by women; a garment covering the lower part of the body and hips, usually with separate coverings for each leg, primarily referring to women's underwear in British English.
women's underpants (British)
british

Examples

She was embarrassed when she realized her pants were visible under her dress.
In the UK, it's common for women to refer to their underwear as pants.
She bought several pairs of comfortable cotton pants for everyday wear.

Synset Examples

she was afraid that her bloomers might have been showing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knickers - women's underwear covering the hips and buttocks
  • Synonym: underpants - an undergarment covering the lower part of the body and each leg separately

Etymology

From American English 'pants', short for 'pantaloons'; meaning 'underpants' is chiefly British.
