horrifying

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɔːrɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɒrɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
causing or likely to cause a feeling of horror; extremely shocking or frightening
causing horror; extremely shocking

Examples

The documentary showed horrifying scenes of destruction after the hurricane.
Witnesses described the accident as a horrifying experience they will never forget.
She awoke from a horrifying nightmare, shaking and drenched in sweat.

Synset Examples

an atrocious automobile accident
a frightful crime of decapitation
an alarming, even horrifying, picture
war is beyond all words horrible
an ugly wound

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrifying - causing great fear or terror
  • Synonym: shocking - causing a strong feeling of surprise, horror, or disgust
  • Antonym: soothing - having a calming effect; relieving anxiety

Etymology

From Latin 'horrificus' (causing terror), via French 'horrifier' (to horrify).
