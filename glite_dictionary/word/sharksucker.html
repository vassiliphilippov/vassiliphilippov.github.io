sharksucker

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑrkˌsʌkər / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːkˌsʌkə / (BrE)
a remora, a marine fish that attaches itself to sharks or other large fish using a sucker on top of its head
a fish that attaches to sharks

Examples

The sharksucker clung tightly to the underside of the giant shark as it swam.
Divers observed a sharksucker hitching a ride on a passing hammerhead.
Sharksuckers use their suction discs to attach themselves to various large marine animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remora - a type of fish known for attaching to larger marine animals
