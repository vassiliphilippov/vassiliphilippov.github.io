showjumping

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊˌdʒʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌdʒʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport of riding horses in competitions over set courses with obstacles that must be jumped, testing horse and rider skill.
equestrian jumping competition

Examples

She has been training intensely for the showjumping competition next weekend.
The Olympic Games will include several equestrian sports, such as showjumping and dressage.
Many riders believe that showjumping requires a unique bond between horse and rider.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressage - horse training and performance competition emphasizing precision and skill
  • Synonym: eventing - an equestrian competition combining dressage, cross-country, and jumping

Etymology

From 'show' + 'jumping', referring to the exhibition of jumping skill.
