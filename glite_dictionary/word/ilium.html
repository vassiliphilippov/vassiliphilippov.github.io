ilium

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪliəm / (AmE), / ˈɪliəm / (BrE)
the uppermost and widest of the three bones that form each side of the pelvis
upper part of the hip bone
medical

Examples

The fractured ilium required immediate medical attention.
During the operation, the surgeon made an incision above the ilium.
The ilium supports the weight of the upper body when sitting or standing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ischium - the lower, rear part of the hip bone
  • Synonym: pubis - the lower, front part of the hip bone

Etymology

From Latin *ilium* or *ile* (groin, flank).
