papillose

1. ADJ

/ pəˈpɪloʊs / (AmE), / pəˈpɪləʊs / (BrE)
having or resembling small, nipple-like projections (papillae); covered with or composed of papillae.
having small, nipple-like projections

Examples

The surface of the leaf is distinctly papillose, giving it a rough texture to the touch.
Under the microscope, the mushroom cap appeared papillose with tiny, rounded projections.
The papillose skin of the frog helps it to absorb moisture more effectively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: papillary - of or relating to papillae; having papillae

Etymology

From Latin 'papilla' (nipple) + '-ose' (full of, having the qualities of).
