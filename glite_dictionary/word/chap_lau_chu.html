chap lau chu

1. NOUN

/ tʃæp laʊ tʃu / (AmE), / tʃap laʊ tʃu / (BrE)
A person with a carefree and fun-loving attitude, often used derogatorily to describe someone perceived as lacking style or polish.
carefree, unfashionable person
slang

Examples

Everyone loves inviting a chap lau chu to parties because they're always so relaxed.
His mother scolded him, calling him a chap lau chu for wearing pajamas to the store.
She took pride in her chap lau chu approach to life, ignoring the latest fashion trends.

Synset Examples

Her chap lau chu attitude made her the life of the party.
He joked about being a chap lau chu with his messy hair.
Everyone knows he's a chap lau chu when it comes to fashion.

Etymology

Of Cantonese origin, literally meaning 'brush up and go' or 'casual attitude.'
