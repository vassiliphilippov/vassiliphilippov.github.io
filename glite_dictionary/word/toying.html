toying

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
playful or teasing behavior intended to arouse sexual interest
sexual teasing or playful provocation

Examples

Her flirtatious toying left him confused about her true intentions.
Some people enjoy a bit of toying in their romantic relationships to keep things exciting.
His constant toying with her emotions eventually grew tiresome.

Etymology

From the verb 'toy', meaning to act playfully.
