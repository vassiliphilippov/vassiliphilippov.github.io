coastland

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊstˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊstˌlænd / (BrE)
land that is situated along or near a coast, especially the area near the sea.
land near or along a coast

Examples

The region's coastland is famous for its stunning cliffs and sandy beaches.
Tropical storms often bring heavy rain to the coastland areas.
Many bird species can be found nesting along the coastland during the spring months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coastline - the outline or shape of a coast, especially as seen from the sea or air

Etymology

Formed from 'coast' + 'land', meaning land by the coast.
