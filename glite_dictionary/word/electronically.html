electronically

1. ADV

/ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪkli / (BrE)
by means of electronic devices, processes, or systems; using electronics rather than mechanical or manual means.
by electronic means

Examples

The payment can be made electronically through the online banking system.
The security system is controlled electronically rather than with a traditional key.
She submitted her application electronically, avoiding the need for paperwork.

Synset Examples

the door opens electronically

Related Terms

  • Antonym: manually - by hand or by non-electronic means

Etymology

From 'electronic' + '-ally', forming an adverb to indicate action by electronic means.
