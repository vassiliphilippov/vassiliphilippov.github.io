Bahamian

1. ADJ

/ bəˈheɪ.mi.ən / (AmE), / bəˈheɪ.mi.ən / (BrE)
relating to the Bahamas, its people, or their culture
of or relating to the Bahamas

Examples

The Bahamian culture is known for its vibrant music and colorful festivals.
Many tourists are drawn to the stunning Bahamian beaches each year.
She prepared a traditional Bahamian dish for the international food fair.

Synset Examples

Bahamian population

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bahamian - a person from the Bahamas

Etymology

From Bahamas + -ian (denoting origin or affiliation).

2. NOUN

/ bəˈheɪ.mi.ən / (AmE), / bəˈheɪ.mi.ən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of the Bahamas
a native or inhabitant of the Bahamas

Examples

The museum exhibit featured art created by a talented Bahamian.
As a proud Bahamian, she celebrates Independence Day every year.
Several Bahamians competed in the international athletics event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bahamian - of or relating to the Bahamas (adjective)

Etymology

From Bahamas + English suffix -ian, meaning belonging to or relating to.
