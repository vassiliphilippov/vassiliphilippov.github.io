lomustine

1. NOUN

/ loʊˈmʌstin / (AmE), / ləʊˈmʌstiːn / (BrE)
an antineoplastic drug used in chemotherapy, especially to treat brain tumors and Hodgkin's lymphoma
antineoplastic chemotherapy drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed lomustine as part of her cancer treatment regimen.
Patients with recurring brain tumors may benefit from lomustine therapy.
Because lomustine can cause side effects, regular blood tests are essential during treatment.
