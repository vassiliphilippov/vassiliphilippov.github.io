Uttar Pradesh

1. PROPN

/ ˌuːtɑr prəˈdeɪʃ / (AmE), / ˌuːtɑː prəˈdeɪʃ / (BrE)
A state in northern India, known for its large population and rich historical significance.
state in northern India

Examples

The Taj Mahal, one of the most famous monuments in the world, is located in Uttar Pradesh.
Many festivals are celebrated with great enthusiasm across Uttar Pradesh.
She visited several historical cities during her trip to Uttar Pradesh.

Etymology

From Hindi उत्तर प्रदेश (Uttar Pradesh), meaning 'Northern Province'.
