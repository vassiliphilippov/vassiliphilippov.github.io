uncomplaining

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.kəmˈpleɪ.nɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kəmˈpleɪ.nɪŋ / (BrE)
accepting difficulties or discomfort without complaining or showing dissatisfaction
accepting without complaining

Examples

Even during long hours of hard work, he remained uncomplaining and cheerful.
The patient's uncomplaining attitude impressed everyone in the hospital.
She tackled every challenge with uncomplaining determination.

Synset Examples

uncomplaining courage

Related Terms

  • Antonym: complaining - expressing dissatisfaction or annoyance
  • Synonym: stoic - enduring pain or hardship without showing feelings or complaining
