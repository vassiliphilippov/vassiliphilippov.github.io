explainable

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈspleɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ɪkˈspleɪnəbl / (BrE)
Able to be explained or made clear; capable of being understood or accounted for.
able to be explained

Examples

Many scientific mysteries are now explainable thanks to new research methods.
Her sudden mood changes were quite explainable given the stressful situation.
The professor gave an explainable answer to every complex question the students asked.

Synset Examples

explainable phenomena

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inexplicable - incapable of being explained or accounted for
  • Synonym: understandable - able to be understood

Etymology

Formed from 'explain' + '-able', indicating capability.
