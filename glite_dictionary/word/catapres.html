Catapres

1. NOUN

/ ˈkætəˌprɛs / (AmE), / ˈkætəprɛs / (BrE)
A brand name for clonidine, a medication used to treat high blood pressure, available in oral or transdermal form.
antihypertensive medication (trade name)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Catapres to help control her high blood pressure.
Some patients prefer Catapres patches for more consistent blood pressure management.
Due to side effects, he asked his physician about alternatives to Catapres.

Related Terms
