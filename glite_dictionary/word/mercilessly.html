mercilessly

1. ADV

/ ˈmɝː.sɪ.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈmɜː.sɪ.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows no mercy or pity; without compassion.
without mercy or compassion

Examples

The soldiers advanced mercilessly, showing no regard for their opponents' pleas.
She was criticized mercilessly by the press after the scandal.
The sun beat down mercilessly on the hikers as they crossed the desert.

Synset Examples

he was mercilessly trounced by his opponent in the House

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ruthlessly - in a cruel and relentless way

Etymology

From 'merciless' (without mercy) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
