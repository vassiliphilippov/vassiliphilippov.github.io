labeled

1. ADJ

/ ˈleɪbəld / (AmE), / ˈleɪbəld / (BrE)
bearing or marked with a label or tag; having a label attached, identifying its contents, owner, or purpose
marked with a label or tag

Examples

The containers were neatly labeled with their contents to avoid confusion.
Always check if your prescription medication is clearly labeled before taking it.
All hazardous materials must be properly labeled according to safety regulations.

Synset Examples

properly labeled luggage

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unlabeled - not bearing a label or tag

Etymology

From the verb 'label' + -ed.
