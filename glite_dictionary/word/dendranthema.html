Dendranthema

1. NOUN

/ dɛnˈdrænθəmə / (AmE), / dɛnˈdrænθɪmə / (BrE)
a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae, often included in Chrysanthemum, that includes economically important florist's chrysanthemums
a genus of flowering plants including florist's chrysanthemums

Examples

Many florist shops sell Dendranthema varieties as decorative potted plants.
Taxonomists often debate whether Dendranthema should be separated from Chrysanthemum.
The bright flowers of Dendranthema are popular in autumn gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chrysanthemum - a genus of flowering plants closely related to Dendranthema

Etymology

From Greek dendron (tree) + anthemon (flower), referring to the plant's floral characteristics.
