histologic

1. ADJ

/ hɪstəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / hɪstəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the microscopic structure of tissues or pertaining to histology.
relating to tissue structure (histology)
medical

Examples

The histologic examination revealed tissue abnormalities not visible to the naked eye.
A histologic analysis was necessary to confirm the type of tumor.
The treatment was evaluated based on histologic changes in the affected organs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: histological - relating to the microscopic structure of tissues (alternative form)

Etymology

From Greek 'histos' (tissue) + 'logos' (study), via 'histology'.
