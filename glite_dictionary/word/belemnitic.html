belemnitic

1. ADJ

/ bɛlˈɛm.nɪ.tɪk / (AmE), / bɪˌlɛmˈnɪt.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of belemnites, an extinct group of marine cephalopods
relating to belemnites (extinct cephalopods)

Examples

The fossil bed contained a remarkable array of belemnitic remains.
Researchers examined the belemnitic structure to understand its evolutionary significance.
She specialized in the study of belemnitic fossils from the Jurassic period.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: belemnite - an extinct group of squid-like marine cephalopods

Etymology

From New Latin Belemnites (the genus name) plus the suffix -ic.
