Veneto

1. PROPN

/ vəˈneɪtoʊ / (AmE), / veˈniːtəʊ / (BrE)
a region of northeastern Italy bordering the Adriatic Sea, known for cities such as Venice and Verona
region of northeastern Italy

Examples

Many tourists visit Veneto to experience the beauty of Venice and its canals.
The cuisine of Veneto features delicious seafood and risotto dishes.
Winemakers in Veneto produce some of Italy's most famous Prosecco.

Etymology

From Italian 'Veneto', from Latin 'Venetus', referring to the ancient Veneti people.
