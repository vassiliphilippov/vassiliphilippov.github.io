schnozzle

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃnɑːzəl / (AmE), / ˈʃnɒzəl / (BrE)
(informal) a person's nose, especially one that is large or prominent.
slang term for the nose
informal slang

Examples

He broke his schnozzle during a basketball game last week.
She wiped her schnozzle with a tissue after sneezing.
People always joked about the size of his schnozzle, but he embraced it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schnoz - slang term for the nose
  • Synonym: nose - the part of the face bearing the nostrils

Etymology

Alteration of 'schnoz', itself from Yiddish 'shnoz' (nose), from German and Polish roots.
