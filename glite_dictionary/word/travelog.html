travelog

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəˌlɔg / (AmE), / ˈtrævəlɒg / (BrE)
A film or illustrated lecture that depicts the experiences and observations of traveling to different places.
film or lecture about travel experiences

Examples

The documentary channel aired an inspiring travelog about the ancient sites of Egypt.
She attended a travelog featuring stunning footage of South American wildlife.
He spent months editing his footage for a travelog to show at the library.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: travelogue - an alternative spelling and synonym for 'travelog'

Etymology

Formed from 'travel' + 'log' (short for 'logbook').
