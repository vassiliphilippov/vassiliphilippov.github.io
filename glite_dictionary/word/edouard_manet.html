Edouard Manet

1. PROPN

/ eɪdˈwɑr mæˈneɪ / (AmE), / eɪˈdwɑːr maˈneɪ / (BrE)
(1832–1883) A French painter whose innovative works greatly influenced the development of Impressionism.
French Impressionist-influencing painter

Examples

Edouard Manet is often regarded as a pivotal figure in the transition from realism to impressionism.
The painting 'Olympia' by Edouard Manet caused considerable controversy when first exhibited.
Many art students study Edouard Manet's technique to better understand modern European painting.
