soigne

1. ADJ

/ swɑːnˈjeɪ / (AmE), / swɒnˈjeɪ / (BrE)
Polished and well-groomed; having an appearance of sophisticated elegance.
polished; well-groomed; elegant

Examples

His soigne attire turned heads at the art gallery opening.
The soigne guests mingled in the candlelit ballroom, exuding effortless style.
Every detail in the hotel lobby was soigne, from the flowers to the polished marble floors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elegant - pleasingly graceful and stylish in appearance or manner
  • Antonym: slovenly - messy and careless in appearance or habits

Etymology

From French 'soigné', meaning 'well-groomed, neat', past participle of 'soigner' (to take care of).
