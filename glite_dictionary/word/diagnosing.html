diagnosing

1. NOUN

/ daɪˈæɡˌnoʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / daɪˈɑːɡnəʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
The process of identifying the nature or cause of a problem, especially in medical or technical contexts.
process of identifying a problem or cause

Examples

Accurate diagnosing of engine problems requires a good understanding of automotive systems.
Early diagnosing of the disease can greatly increase the chances of recovery.
Doctors spend years developing skills in diagnosing complex medical cases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diagnosis - the identification of the nature of an illness or problem

Etymology

From the verb 'diagnose', from Greek diagnōsis (discernment, distinguishing).
