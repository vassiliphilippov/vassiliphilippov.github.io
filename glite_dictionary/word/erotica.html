erotica

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈrɑːtɪkə / (AmE), / ɪˈrɒtɪkə / (BrE)
works of art, literature, photographs, or films intended to stimulate sexual desire, often with little or no other literary or artistic purpose
sexually stimulating works or literature

Examples

The bookstore had a special section for erotica tucked away in the back.
Some people collect vintage erotica for its historical value.
She published a series of erotica that quickly gained popularity online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pornography - explicit material intended to arouse sexual excitement

Etymology

From Greek *erōtiká* (matters of love), from *erōs* (sexual love).
