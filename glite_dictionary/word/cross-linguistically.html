cross-linguistically

1. ADV

/ krɔs lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪkli / (AmE), / krɒs lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that involves or compares multiple languages; across languages.
involving comparison across languages

Examples

The researchers analyzed the data cross-linguistically to identify universal patterns.
This theory has been confirmed cross-linguistically in numerous unrelated languages.
She is known for examining grammatical structures cross-linguistically in her publications.

Synset Examples

she studied the phenomenon cross-linguistically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comparatively - involving comparison between things

Etymology

Formed from 'cross-' (across) and 'linguistic' (relating to language), with adverbial suffix '-ally'.
