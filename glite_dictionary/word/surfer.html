surfer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːfər / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfə / (BrE)
a person who rides on waves using a surfboard, especially as a sport or hobby
person who rides waves on a surfboard

Examples

The surfer caught a huge wave and rode it all the way to the shore.
Every summer, skilled surfers travel to Hawaii for the best waves.
She became a passionate surfer after moving to the coast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surfboarder - a person who rides a surfboard

Etymology

From 'surf' (the mass or line of foam formed by waves breaking on a shore) + '-er', denoting a person who performs an action.
