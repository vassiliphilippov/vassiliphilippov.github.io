unmethodical

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.məˈθɑ.dɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.məˈθɒd.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
not done according to a method, system, or plan; lacking order or organization
lacking method or organization

Examples

His unmethodical approach made the task take much longer than expected.
The scientist was criticized for her unmethodical procedures during the experiment.
Because the team was unmethodical in its planning, the event was chaotic.

Synset Examples

the project failed through unmethodical planning

Related Terms

  • Antonym: methodical - done according to a method or system

Etymology

From un- (not) + methodical (from Greek methodikos, from methodos meaning 'pursuit, method').
