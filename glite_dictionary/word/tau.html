tau

1. NOUN

/ taʊ / (AmE), / taʊ / (BrE)
the nineteenth letter of the Greek alphabet, written as Τ (uppercase) or τ (lowercase)
Greek letter (Τ, τ), nineteenth in alphabet

Examples

In physics, the symbol tau is often used to represent time constants.
The Greek letter tau appears after sigma in the alphabet.
He learned to write both the uppercase and lowercase forms of tau.

Etymology

From Greek ταῦ (tau), the name of the letter.
