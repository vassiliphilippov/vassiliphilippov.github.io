vena bulbi penis

1. NOUN

/ ˈveɪ.nə ˈbʊl.baɪ ˈpiː.nɪs / (AmE), / ˈviː.nə ˈbʊl.baɪ ˈpiː.nɪs / (BrE)
A vein located in the head of the penis that is a tributary of the internal pudendal vein and drains blood from the perineum.
vein in the head of the penis
medical

Examples

The vena bulbi penis plays an important part in draining blood from the perineal region.
During the operation, the surgeon identified the vena bulbi penis to avoid accidental injury.
A blockage in the vena bulbi penis can lead to complications affecting penile blood flow.

Etymology

From Latin; 'vena' meaning vein, 'bulbi' referring to the bulb, and 'penis' meaning penis.
