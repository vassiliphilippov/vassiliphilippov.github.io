Valletta

1. PROPN

/ vəˈlɛtə / (AmE), / vəˈlɛtə / (BrE)
the capital city of Malta, located on the northeastern coast of the island.
capital city of Malta

Examples

My flight to Valletta arrives early in the morning.
Many tourists visit Valletta to explore its historic architecture and lively markets.
The annual festival in Valletta attracts visitors from around the world.

Etymology

Named after Jean de Valette, Grand Master of the Order of St. John, who founded the city in 1566.
