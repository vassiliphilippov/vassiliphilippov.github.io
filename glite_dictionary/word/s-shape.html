S-shape

1. NOUN

/ ɛs ʃeɪp / (AmE), / ɛs ʃeɪp / (BrE)
a double curve or form that resembles the letter S
shape resembling the letter S

Examples

The winding road formed an S-shape as it descended the mountainside.
The artist added an elegant S-shape to the metal sculpture.
The river bent in an S-shape through the green valley.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zigzag - a pattern or path that proceeds with sharp turns in alternating directions
