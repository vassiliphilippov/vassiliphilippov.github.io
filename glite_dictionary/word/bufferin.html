Bufferin

1. PROPN

/ ˈbʌfərɪn / (AmE), / ˈbʌfərɪn / (BrE)
Trademarked brand name for aspirin coated with a substance to neutralize acid, intended to reduce stomach irritation.
brand of buffered aspirin

Examples

I took Bufferin to help with my headache because it's easier on the stomach.
The doctor recommended Bufferin instead of regular aspirin to minimize stomach upset.
Some people prefer Bufferin for pain relief due to its buffered coating.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aspirin - medication used to reduce pain, fever, or inflammation

Etymology

Blend of 'buffer' + 'aspirin', trademarked brand name.
