Hitlerian

1. ADJ

/ hɪtˈlɪəriən / (AmE), / hɪtˈlɪəriən / (BrE)
Of or relating to Adolf Hitler or his Nazi regime, especially suggesting their authoritarian, dictatorial, or fascist qualities.
relating to Adolf Hitler or his regime

Examples

The dictator ruled with a Hitlerian sense of absolute authority.
His Hitlerian speeches stirred nationalistic fervor among his followers.
The museum exhibit explores Hitlerian propaganda during the 1930s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nazi - relating to the German National Socialist party or its ideology

Etymology

Formed from the name Hitler + -ian (adjectival suffix).
