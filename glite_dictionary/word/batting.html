batting

1. NOUN

/ ˈbætɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbætɪŋ / (BrE)
soft material made of layers of cotton, wool, or synthetic fiber, used as filling in quilts, upholstery, and similar items
soft filling material for quilts or upholstery

Examples

The quilt was filled with thick batting to keep it warm during winter.
She bought a roll of polyester batting for her craft project.
Cotton batting gives pillows a soft and fluffy feel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wadding - soft, thick material used for padding or stuffing

2. NOUN

/ ˈbætɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbætɪŋ / (BrE)
In baseball, the act of attempting to hit the ball in order to get on base.
attempt to hit the ball in baseball

Examples

He spent hours practicing his batting before the big game.
The team's batting improved significantly after hiring a new coach.
Good batting requires quick reflexes and strong hand-eye coordination.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pitching - throwing the ball to the batter in baseball
  • Synonym: hitting - striking the ball with the bat in baseball
