roping

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊ.pɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.pɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of capturing cattle or horses by throwing a lasso around them
capturing cattle or horses with a lasso

Examples

Learning the art of roping is essential for working on a cattle ranch.
The rodeo featured a roping competition that drew participants from across the state.
He practiced roping in the field until he could catch a running calf with ease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lassoing - the act of throwing a lasso to catch something
