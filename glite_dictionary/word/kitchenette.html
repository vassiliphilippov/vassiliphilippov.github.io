kitchenette

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɪtʃəˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˌkɪtʃɪˈnɛt / (BrE)
A small kitchen or a part of another room equipped for preparing light meals.
small kitchen

Examples

The studio apartment includes a compact kitchenette with a refrigerator and microwave.
Guests can prepare breakfast in the kitchenette before heading out for the day.
She set up a small table next to the kitchenette for dining.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: kitchen - a room where food is prepared and cooked

Etymology

From kitchen + French diminutive suffix -ette, meaning 'small'.
