hairnet

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛr.nɛt / (AmE), / ˈheə.nɛt / (BrE)
a light, netted covering worn over the hair to hold it in place, often for hygiene or styling purposes
netted hair covering

Examples

The chef wore a hairnet in the kitchen to keep stray hairs out of the food.
She tucked her hair neatly into a hairnet before putting on her ballet costume.
Factory workers are often required to wear a hairnet for reasons of cleanliness and safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snood - a type of fabric hair covering or net
