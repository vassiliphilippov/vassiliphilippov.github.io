Umbria

1. PROPN

/ ˈʌm.bri.ə / (AmE), / ˈʌm.brɪ.ə / (BrE)
a region in central Italy, known for its mountainous landscape and historic towns
mountainous region in central Italy

Examples

Many visitors travel to Umbria to experience its medieval architecture and beautiful hills.
The cuisine in Umbria is famous for its use of truffles and olive oil.
She spent a summer in Umbria learning Italian and exploring the countryside.

Etymology

From Latin 'Umbria', the name of the region.
