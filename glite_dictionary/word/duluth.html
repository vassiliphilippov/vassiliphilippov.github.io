Duluth

1. PROPN

/ dəˈluːθ / (AmE), / dəˈluːθ / (BrE)
a city in northeast Minnesota, United States, located on the western shore of Lake Superior
city in Minnesota on Lake Superior

Examples

Duluth is known for its scenic views of Lake Superior and its historic waterfront district.
Heavy snowfall is common during the winter months in Duluth.
The University of Minnesota has a campus in Duluth that attracts students from across the country.

Etymology

Named after Daniel Greysolon, Sieur du Lhut, a French explorer.
