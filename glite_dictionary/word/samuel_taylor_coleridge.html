Samuel Taylor Coleridge

1. PROPN

/ ˈsæmjəl ˈteɪlər ˈkoʊləˌrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsæmjʊəl ˈteɪlə ˈkəʊlɪrɪdʒ / (BrE)
(1772–1834) An English Romantic poet, literary critic, and philosopher, known for works such as 'The Rime of the Ancient Mariner' and 'Kubla Khan'.
English Romantic poet and critic (1772–1834)

Examples

Samuel Taylor Coleridge was a major influence on the Romantic movement in English literature.
Many scholars have analyzed the symbolism in Samuel Taylor Coleridge's 'The Rime of the Ancient Mariner'.
The collaboration between William Wordsworth and Samuel Taylor Coleridge produced the famous 'Lyrical Ballads'.
