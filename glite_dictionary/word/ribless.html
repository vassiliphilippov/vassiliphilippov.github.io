ribless

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪbləs / (AmE), / ˈrɪbləs / (BrE)
having no ribs or lacking visible ribs, either literally or figuratively
having no ribs or visible ribs

Examples

The snake is a ribless vertebrate, unlike most animals in its class.
The chef served a special ribless steak for customers who preferred leaner cuts.
Some styles of vests are ribless for a smoother, more elegant appearance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ribbed - having ribs or rib-like structures

Etymology

Formed from 'rib' + suffix '-less', meaning 'without'.
