forty-nine

1. NUM

/ ˌfɔr.tiˈnaɪn / (AmE), / ˌfɔː.tiˈnaɪn / (BrE)

the number 49

Examples

The team scored forty-nine points in the final game.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty-eight - the number 48; one less than forty-nine
  • Degree Higher: fifty - the number 50; one more than forty-nine

Etymology

From 'forty' (four times ten) and 'nine'.
