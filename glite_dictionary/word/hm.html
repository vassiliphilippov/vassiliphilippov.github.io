hm

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛm / (AmE), / ˈhɛm / (BrE)
a metric unit of length equal to 100 meters
metric unit of 100 meters

Examples

The field measured exactly one hm in length according to the surveyor.
This map uses hm as the distance unit for accuracy.
A marathon is just over four hm in length.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hectometre - one hundred meters

Etymology

Abbreviation of hectometre, from French 'hectomètre', from Greek 'hekaton' (hundred) + 'metron' (measure).
