frozen

1. ADJ

/ ˈfroʊzən / (AmE), / ˈfrəʊzən / (BrE)
Turned into ice or affected by freezing or severe cold.
turned into ice; affected by severe cold

Examples

We had to wait until the frozen ground began to thaw in spring.
The frozen lake was safe enough for skating this morning.
My car wouldn't start because the fuel line was frozen.

Synset Examples

the frozen North
frozen pipes
children skating on a frozen brook

Related Terms

  • Antonym: thawed - no longer frozen; having become unfrozen

Etymology

Past participle of 'freeze', from Old English 'frēosan'.
