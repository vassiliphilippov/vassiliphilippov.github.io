dog-tired

1. ADJ

/ dɑg ˈtaɪɚd / (AmE), / dɒg ˈtaɪəd / (BrE)
Extremely tired; completely exhausted.
extremely tired

Examples

After working a double shift, she came home dog-tired and went straight to bed.
He was dog-tired after hiking up the mountain all day.
By midnight everyone at the party was dog-tired but happy.

Synset Examples

the day's shopping left her exhausted
he went to bed dog-tired
was fagged and sweaty
the trembling of his played out limbs
felt completely washed-out
only worn-out horses and cattle
you look worn out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhausted - very tired, drained of energy
  • Synonym: worn out - extremely tired or used up
  • Antonym: refreshed - full of energy and feeling rested

Etymology

From the image of a dog made weary by exertion; attested in English since the mid-19th century.
