example

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˈzæmpəl / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɑːmpəl / (BrE)
a person or thing that is regarded as a model to be imitated.
person or thing to be imitated

Examples

She is an example of dedication and hard work for her colleagues.
He set a good example for his younger siblings by sharing his toys.
Teachers should strive to be a positive example for their students.

Synset Examples

an exemplar of success
a model of clarity
he is the very model of a modern major general

Related Terms

  • Synonym: model - a thing or person that serves as a pattern to be copied
  • Synonym: exemplar - a person or thing serving as a typical example or excellent model
  • Antonym: counterexample - an instance or case that refutes a proposition or theory

Etymology

From Latin exemplum (sample, specimen, model).
