Baruch ben Neriah

1. PROPN

/ bəˈruːk bɛn nɛˈraɪ.ə / (AmE), / bəˈruːk bɛn nɛˈraɪ.ə / (BrE)
A scribe and disciple of the prophet Jeremiah, mentioned in the Hebrew Bible.
scribe and disciple of Jeremiah in the Bible

Examples

According to tradition, Baruch ben Neriah assisted Jeremiah in recording his prophecies.
The book of Baruch is attributed to Baruch ben Neriah in some biblical traditions.
Historians discuss whether Baruch ben Neriah wrote other texts in the Hebrew Bible.

Etymology

From Hebrew בָּרוּךְ בֶּן-נֵרִיָּה‎ (Barūkh ben Nērīyā), meaning 'Baruch son of Neriah'.
