to officialize

1. VERB

/ əˈfɪʃəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / əˈfɪʃəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make (something) official or give it formal approval or recognition
make officially approved

Examples

The university decided to officialize the annual picnic as an institution-wide event.
After years of partnership, they finally officialized their business arrangement with a contract.
The government plans to officialize the new regulations next month.

Synset Examples

We officialized our relationship

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to authorize - to give official permission for something
  • Synonym: to validate - to make something officially acceptable or approved
  • Antonym: unofficial - not officially approved or recognized

Etymology

Formed from 'official' + '-ize', following standard English word formation.
