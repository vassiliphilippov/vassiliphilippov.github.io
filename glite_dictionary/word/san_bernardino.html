San Bernardino

1. PROPN

/ sæn ˌbɜːrnərˈdiːnoʊ / (AmE), / san ˌbɜːnɑːˈdiːnəʊ / (BrE)
a city in southern California, located east of Los Angeles
city in southern California, USA

Examples

The summer temperatures in San Bernardino often reach well above 90 degrees Fahrenheit.
She moved to San Bernardino for a new job opportunity last year.
The mountains surrounding San Bernardino offer excellent hiking trails for outdoor enthusiasts.

Etymology

From Spanish, named after Saint Bernardino of Siena.
