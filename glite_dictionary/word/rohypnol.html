Rohypnol

1. NOUN

/ roʊˈhɪpnɒl / (AmE), / rəʊˈhɪpnɒl / (BrE)
A powerful depressant and tranquilizer drug, sometimes associated with illegal use in sexual assaults; its medical name is flunitrazepam.
a strong tranquilizer drug; flunitrazepam
medical

Examples

Authorities warn that Rohypnol is sometimes used illegally to facilitate sexual assault.
The importation of Rohypnol was banned in several countries due to its misuse.
She had never heard of Rohypnol before the incident was reported on the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flunitrazepam - the generic name for Rohypnol

Etymology

Trademark, from ‘Ro-’ (Roche, the manufacturer) and ‘hypnol’ (from ‘hypnotic’).
