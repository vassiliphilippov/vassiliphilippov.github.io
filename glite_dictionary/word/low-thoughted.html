low-thoughted

1. ADJ

/ ˌloʊ ˈθɔtɪd / (AmE), / ˌləʊ ˈθɔːtɪd / (BrE)
Lacking intelligence, insight, or depth of thought; superficial or unintelligent.
lacking intelligence or depth of thought

Examples

His low-thoughted remarks annoyed the audience during the debate.
The book was dismissed as low-thoughted by literary critics for its lack of originality.
She refused to engage with low-thoughted gossip and preferred meaningful conversations.

Synset Examples

His low-thoughted comments revealed his lack of insight.
The article was criticized for being low-thoughted and superficial.
She avoided conversations with low-thoughted individuals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shallow - lacking depth of thought, superficial
  • Antonym: thoughtful - showing careful consideration or depth of thought
