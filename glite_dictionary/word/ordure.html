ordure

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdʒər / (AmE), / ˈɔːdjʊə / (BrE)
solid waste matter discharged from the bowels; excrement or dung
excrement; solid bowel waste

Examples

The streets were fouled with ordure from both humans and animals after the festival.
A foul smell of ordure drifted up from the cellar.
The manuscript contained insults and bitter ordure directed at his former colleagues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feces - solid bodily waste discharged from the intestine
  • Synonym: dung - excrement of animals, especially domesticated ones

Etymology

From Old French 'ordure' (dirt, filth), from 'ord' (dirty, foul).
