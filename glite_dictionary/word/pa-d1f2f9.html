P.A.

1. NOUN

/ ˌpiːˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌpiːˈeɪ / (BrE)
An electronic amplification system used to broadcast speech, music, or other sounds to a large public area.
public address system for amplification

Examples

The school announced the fire drill over the P.A. system.
The concert organizers used a powerful P.A. to ensure everyone could hear the music.
Static noises suddenly blared from the P.A. before the speaker started talking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public address system - system for amplifying and distributing sound in public places

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'public address.'
