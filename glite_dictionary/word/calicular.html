calicular

1. ADJ

/ kəˈlɪkjələr / (AmE), / kəˈlɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling a calyculus, typically referring to a cup-shaped structure in biology or medicine.
relating to a cup-shaped structure
medical

Examples

The coral displayed a distinct calicular structure under microscopic examination.
Certain glands have a calicular appearance, resembling small cups.
The doctor noted calicular changes in the tissue during the biopsy.

Etymology

From Latin caliculus (small cup, diminutive of calix ‘cup’).
