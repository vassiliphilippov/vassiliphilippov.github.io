shoot-'em-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃuːt əm ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈʃuːt əm ˌʌp / (BrE)
A film or video game characterized by frequent shooting and violent action, typically involving gunfights.
movie or game with shooting and violence
informal

Examples

He enjoys classic shoot-'em-up movies with plenty of action and explosions.
The game is a fast-paced shoot-'em-up where you must defeat waves of enemies.
Last night's film was just another mindless shoot-'em-up with no real plot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: action movie - a movie with a lot of exciting action and stunts

Etymology

From 'shoot them up', a colloquial expression meaning to engage in gunfire, often used of Western or action films.
