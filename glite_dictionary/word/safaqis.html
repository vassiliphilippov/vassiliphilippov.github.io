Safaqis

1. PROPN

/ səˈfɑːkɪs / (AmE), / səˈfɑːkɪs / (BrE)
A city in eastern Tunisia, the country's second largest, near a phosphate mining region on the Mediterranean coast.
city in eastern Tunisia

Examples

Safaqis is known for its vibrant marketplace and rich history in the olive oil trade.
Many people visit Safaqis to experience its unique blend of modern industry and traditional Tunisian culture.
The growing phosphate industry has contributed significantly to the economy of Safaqis.
