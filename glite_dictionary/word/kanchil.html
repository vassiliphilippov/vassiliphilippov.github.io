kanchil

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑn.tʃɪl / (AmE), / ˈkan.tʃɪl / (BrE)
A small chevrotain (mouse deer) native to southeastern Asia, especially Indonesia and Malaysia.
small Southeast Asian chevrotain

Examples

The kanchil is renowned in folktales for its cleverness and agility.
We saw a kanchil dart through the undergrowth during our hike in Malaysia.
Unlike deer, the kanchil is much smaller and does not have antlers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chevrotain - a small, deer-like hoofed mammal of the family Tragulidae

Etymology

From Malay 'kancil' (small deer or mouse deer).
