blastomycotic

1. ADJ

/ ˌblæstoʊmaɪˈkɑːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌblæstəʊmaɪˈkɒtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of blastomycosis, a fungal infection caused by Blastomyces species.
relating to blastomycosis (fungal disease)
medical

Examples

The patient showed blastomycotic lesions on her skin and lungs.
A blastomycotic infection requires prompt antifungal treatment.
Microscopic examination revealed blastomycotic yeast cells in the tissue sample.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blastomycosis - a fungal infection caused by Blastomyces species

Etymology

Formed from 'blastomycosis' (the fungal disease) + '-tic' (adjectival suffix).
