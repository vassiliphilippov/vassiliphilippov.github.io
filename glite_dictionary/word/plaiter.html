plaiter

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈpleɪtə / (BrE)
a person who braids or interlaces strands of hair, fabric, or other materials to form a plait
person who braids or plaits

Examples

The plaiter skillfully wove the ribbons into a colorful braid.
At the fair, a plaiter demonstrated how to interlace straw into decorative hats.
She hired a professional plaiter to prepare her hair for the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: braider - a person who braids hair, fabric or other materials

Etymology

From 'plait' (to braid or interlace) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
