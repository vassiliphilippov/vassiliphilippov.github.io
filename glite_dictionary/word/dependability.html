dependability

1. NOUN

/ dɪˌpɛndəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / dɪˌpɛndəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being trustworthy and reliable.
quality of being reliable

Examples

We value dependability in our employees above all other qualities.
Her dependability made her the obvious choice to lead the team.
The company's reputation for dependability sets it apart from its competitors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reliability - the quality of being reliable
  • Antonym: unreliability - the quality of being unreliable or not dependable

Etymology

From 'dependable' + '-ity', modeled after 'reliability' and related nouns.
