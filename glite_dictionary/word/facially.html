facially

1. ADV

/ ˈfeɪʃəli / (AmE), / ˈfeɪʃəli / (BrE)
in relation to the face; with respect to the face
in relation to the face

Examples

The patient was facially disfigured after the accident.
She is facially similar to her sister, but their personalities differ.
The actor was facially recognized by fans at the airport.

Etymology

From 'facial' (face-related) + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
