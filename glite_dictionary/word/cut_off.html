cut off

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌt ɑf / (AmE), / ˈkʌt ɒf / (BrE)
detached or removed by cutting, especially from a larger whole
detached by cutting

Examples

Her cut off sleeve revealed a jagged edge where the fabric had been removed.
