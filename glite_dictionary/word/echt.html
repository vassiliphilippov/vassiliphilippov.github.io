echt

1. ADJ

/ ɛxt / (AmE), / ɛxt / (BrE)
authentic; genuine; not fake or counterfeit
authentic; genuine

Examples

The antique dealer assured us that the vase was echt, not a modern reproduction.
Only an expert could determine whether the signature was echt or forged.
She takes pride in collecting echt works of art rather than imitations.

Synset Examples

a genuine Picasso
genuine leather

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genuine - authentic, real, not fake
  • Synonym: authentic - of undisputed origin; genuine
  • Antonym: fake - not genuine; counterfeit

Etymology

From German *echt* (genuine), used in English since the 19th century.
