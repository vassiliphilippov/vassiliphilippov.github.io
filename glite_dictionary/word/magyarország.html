Magyarország

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɒdʒ.ɑːrˌoʊr.sɑːɡ / (AmE), / ˈmæɡ.jə.rɔːˌsæɡ / (BrE)
the Hungarian name for Hungary, a republic in central Europe
Hungarian name for Hungary (country)

Examples

Tourists often visit Magyarország to explore its historic cities and famous thermal baths.
The river Danube flows through Magyarország, dividing Budapest into Buda and Pest.
He studied abroad in Magyarország to improve his knowledge of the Hungarian language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hungary - a country in central Europe; republic; capital Budapest

Etymology

Hungarian, from 'magyar' (Hungarian) + 'ország' (country).
