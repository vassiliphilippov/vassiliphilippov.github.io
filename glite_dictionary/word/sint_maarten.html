Sint Maarten

1. PROPN

/ sɪnt ˈmɑrtən / (AmE), / sɪnt ˈmɑːtən / (BrE)
an island and country in the northeastern Caribbean, part of the Kingdom of the Netherlands, occupying the southern part of the island of Saint Martin
Caribbean island country, part of Saint Martin

Examples

Many tourists visit Sint Maarten for its beautiful beaches and vibrant nightlife.
Sint Maarten celebrates its national day with parades and festivals every November.
There are direct flights to Sint Maarten from several international cities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Saint Martin - the entire island, administered by both France and the Netherlands
  • Degree Lower: Saint-Martin - the French-administered northern part of the island

Etymology

Dutch for 'Saint Martin', named after St. Martin of Tours.
