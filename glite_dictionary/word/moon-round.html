moon-round

1. ADJ

/ mun-raʊnd / (AmE), / muːn-raʊnd / (BrE)
Having a round shape resembling that of the moon.
round like the moon

Examples

Her moon-round face glowed softly in the candlelight.
The artist painted a moon-round window in the cottage wall.
The baby stared up with wide, moon-round eyes full of wonder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: round - having the shape of a circle or sphere
