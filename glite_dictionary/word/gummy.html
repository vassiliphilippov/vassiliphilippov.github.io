gummy

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡʌmi / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmi / (BrE)
covered with or resembling a sticky or adhesive gum.
coated with sticky gum

Examples

After spilling sap on his hands, they felt gummy all afternoon.
The tape left a gummy residue on the window after it was removed.
Be careful—the candy is gummy and will stick to your fingers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sticky - tending or designed to stick to things; adhesive

2. ADJ

/ ˈɡʌmi / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmi / (BrE)
sticky and resembling glue; having the adhesive qualities of a gum or glue.
sticky or glue-like in texture

Examples

The gummy residue on the label made it hard to peel off completely.
After the candy melted in the sun, it became gummy and stuck to the wrapper.
The tape left a gummy mark on the glass that took time to clean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sticky - having the property of adhering or sticking to a surface
  • Antonym: slippery - difficult to hold or stand on because of a smooth or wet surface

Etymology

From gum (resin or adhesive substance) + -y (forming adjectives).
