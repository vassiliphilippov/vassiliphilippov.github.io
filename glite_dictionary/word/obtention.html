obtention

1. NOUN

/ əbˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / əbˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of obtaining something
the act of obtaining

Examples

The obtention of accurate results requires careful planning and execution.
Her research focused on the obtention of new patents for the laboratory.
Successful obtention of a visa depends on submitting the correct documents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acquisition - the act of getting something, especially knowledge or skills

Etymology

From French 'obtention', from Latin 'obtentio', from 'obtinere' (to obtain).
