uprightness

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌpˌraɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈʌp.raɪt.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or condition of standing or sitting with the back straight and the body vertical
quality of being upright in posture

Examples

Her uprightness during yoga impressed the instructor.
Good uprightness can help prevent back injuries over time.
The statue's uprightness gave it a dignified appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: erectness - the quality of being in a vertical, upright position

2. NOUN

/ ˈʌpˌraɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈʌp.raɪt.nəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being in a vertical position at right angles to the horizon.
vertical position; being upright

Examples

The uprightness of the statue made it stand out in the center of the square.
After the storm, we checked the uprightness of the lampposts on our street.
Maintaining the uprightness of the mast was essential for the safety of the boat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: verticality - the quality of being vertical
  • Antonym: horizontality - the quality of being horizontal

Etymology

From upright + -ness.

3. NOUN

/ ˈʌpˌraɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈʌp.raɪt.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being honest, honorable, and morally correct.
state of being honest and honorable

Examples

Her uprightness earned her the respect of everyone in the community.
We admired his uprightness in all his professional dealings.
The company's reputation was built on the uprightness of its founders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: integrity - the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles
  • Antonym: dishonesty - lack of honesty or integrity

Etymology

From 'upright' + '-ness', deriving from Old English 'upryht' (vertical, just, honest) + '-ness' (state, quality).
