agreeably

1. ADV

/ əˈɡriəbli / (AmE), / əˈɡriːəbli / (BrE)
In a manner that is pleasant or enjoyable.
in a pleasant or enjoyable manner

Examples

The garden was agreeably cool on a hot afternoon.
We were agreeably surprised by the quality of the meal.
The meeting proceeded agreeably and finished ahead of schedule.

Synset Examples

we spent a pleasantly lazy afternoon

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pleasantly - in a pleasant manner

Etymology

From 'agreeable' + '-ly'.
