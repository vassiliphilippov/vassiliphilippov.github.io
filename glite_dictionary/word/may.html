May

1. NOUN

/ meɪ / (AmE), / meɪ / (BrE)
The fifth month of the year, after April and before June.
fifth month of the year

Examples

The flowers in May are especially beautiful this year.
We usually take our family vacation in May.
Her birthday falls at the end of May every year.

Etymology

From Latin 'Maius (mensis)', named after Maia, an ancient Roman goddess of spring and growth.
