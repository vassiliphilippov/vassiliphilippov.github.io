Rhodesian

1. ADJ

/ roʊˈdiːʒən / (AmE), / rəʊˈdiːʒən / (BrE)
relating to Rhodesia, the former name for what is now Zimbabwe
relating to former Rhodesia (now Zimbabwe)

Examples

Her grandfather was a Rhodesian farmer during the 1960s.
The old photographs showed men in Rhodesian military uniforms.
She studied the impact of Rhodesian history on modern Zimbabwe.

Etymology

From Rhodesia, the former name of Zimbabwe, itself named after Cecil Rhodes.
