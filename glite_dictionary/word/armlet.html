armlet

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrm.lət / (AmE), / ˈɑːm.lɪt / (BrE)
A band, often of metal or fabric, worn around the arm as a decoration or ornament.
decorative band worn on the arm

Examples

She wore a golden armlet on her upper arm during the ceremony.
The ancient sculpture depicts a warrior with a jeweled armlet on each arm.
He bought a silver armlet as a souvenir from the market in India.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bracelet - an ornamental band or chain worn around the wrist or arm

Etymology

From arm (body part) + -let, a diminutive suffix indicating smallness.
