spattered

1. ADJ

/ ˈspætɚd / (AmE), / ˈspætəd / (BrE)
marked or covered with spots or drops of a liquid or another substance, usually scattered or splashed irregularly
covered with scattered spots or drops

Examples

His white shirt was spattered with red paint after the art project.
The windshield became spattered with mud as we drove down the country road.
She entered the kitchen to find the floor spattered with sauce from the spilled pot.

Synset Examples

waves dabbled with moonlight
a blood-spattered room
gardens splashed with color
kitchen walls splattered with grease

Related Terms

  • Synonym: splattered - marked with splashes or blotches of a liquid or other material
  • Synonym: dabbled - marked with small patches or spots of color or light

Etymology

From the verb 'spatter', which derives from Middle Dutch 'spateren' (to splash).
