hallowed

1. ADJ

/ ˈhæloʊd / (AmE), / ˈhaləʊd / (BrE)
regarded as holy and deserving great respect, especially due to religious association
regarded as holy or sacred

Examples

They visited the hallowed grounds where the historic battle had taken place.
The church is built on hallowed land and is a site of pilgrimage.
The Constitution is treated as a hallowed document by many citizens.

Synset Examples

the sacred name of Jesus
Jerusalem's hallowed soil

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sacred - regarded with great respect and reverence, especially by a religious tradition
  • Antonym: profane - relating or devoted to that which is not sacred; showing disrespect toward sacred things

Etymology

From Old English *hālig* (holy) via the verb 'hallow' (to make holy).
