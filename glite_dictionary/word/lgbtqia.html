LGBTQIA+

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛlˌdʒiːˌbiːˌtiːˌkjuːˈaɪˈeɪ ˈplʌs / (AmE), / ˌɛl.dʒiːˌbiːˌtiːˌkjuːˈaɪˈeɪ ˈplʌs / (BrE)
the community of people who identify as lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer or questioning, intersex, asexual, or other non-normative gender or sexual identities
community of non-normative gender/sexual identities

Examples

The university offers resources and support specifically for the LGBTQIA+ community.
Pride Month celebrates the history and achievements of LGBTQIA+ individuals worldwide.
Many companies now provide training to create a more inclusive environment for LGBTQIA+ staff.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: LGBT - abbreviation for community of lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender people
  • Synonym: queer - term for non-normative gender or sexual identities

Etymology

Acronym formed from the initial letters of lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer or questioning, intersex, asexual, with plus sign representing additional identities.
