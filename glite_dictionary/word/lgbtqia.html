LGBTQIA

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛl.dʒiː.biː.tiː.kjuː.aɪˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛl.dʒiː.biː.tiː.kjuː.aɪˈeɪ / (BrE)
an acronym for lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer or questioning, intersex, and asexual people, referring to the community of individuals who have non-normative sexual or gender identities
the non-normative gender and sexual identity community

Examples

The university hosts workshops for the LGBTQIA community to promote inclusion and understanding.
Many organizations have started to introduce LGBTQIA-friendly policies in the workplace.
Resources for LGBTQIA youth are available through various community centers across the country.

Etymology

Formed as an acronym of the initial letters for lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer or questioning, intersex, and asexual.
