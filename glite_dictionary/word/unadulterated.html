unadulterated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈdʌl.təˌreɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈdʌl.tər.eɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
not mixed with any other substances; pure.
pure; not mixed with anything else

Examples

She insisted on using only unadulterated ingredients in her recipes.
The manufacturer guarantees that this is unadulterated olive oil.
His joy was unadulterated when he finally won the award.

Synset Examples

unadulterated maple syrup

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pure - free from any contamination or adulteration
  • Antonym: adulterated - mixed with inferior or impure substances

Etymology

From un- (not) + adulterated, from Latin adulterare (to corrupt).
