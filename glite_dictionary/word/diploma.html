diploma

1. NOUN

/ dəˈploʊmə / (AmE), / dɪˈpləʊmə / (BrE)
an official document certifying that someone has successfully completed a particular course of study
certificate for completing studies

Examples

She proudly displayed her diploma in a frame on her office wall.
After four years of study, he finally received his diploma.
Employers often ask to see a copy of your diploma during the hiring process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certificate - an official document attesting to a fact or qualification
  • Synonym: degree - the qualification awarded to students upon completion of a university course

Etymology

From Greek *diplōma* (folded paper, document), via Latin.
