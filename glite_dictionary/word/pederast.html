pederast

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛd.əˌræst / (AmE), / ˈpɛd.ərɑːst / (BrE)
a man who engages in sexual activity with a boy, especially as the passive partner
man who has sex with boys

Examples

The ancient Greeks had different attitudes toward the role of a pederast in society.
He was later prosecuted as a pederast under the laws of the time.
Discussions about historical figures sometimes mention whether they were considered a pederast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pedophile - an adult sexually attracted to children

Etymology

From Ancient Greek paiderastēs, from pais (boy) + erastēs (lover).
