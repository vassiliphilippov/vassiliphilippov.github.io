schmo

1. NOUN

/ ʃmoʊ / (AmE), / ʃməʊ / (BrE)
a foolish or contemptible person; a jerk
foolish or contemptible person
slang

Examples

He felt like a total schmo after forgetting her birthday.
Quit being such a schmo and listen to the instructions.
Only a schmo would wear sandals in the snow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - an irritating or contemptible person
  • Synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely

Etymology

From Yiddish שמאָ (shmó), related to 'schmuck'.
