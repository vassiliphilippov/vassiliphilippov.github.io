Harrisburg

1. PROPN

/ ˈhærɪsˌbɜrɡ / (AmE), / ˈhærɪsˌbɜːɡ / (BrE)
the capital city of Pennsylvania, located in the southern part of the state
capital of Pennsylvania, USA

Examples

The governor made an important announcement from Harrisburg yesterday.
After moving to Harrisburg, she started working for the state government.
The Susquehanna River runs through the heart of Harrisburg.

Etymology

Named after John Harris, Sr., an early settler and trader in the area.
