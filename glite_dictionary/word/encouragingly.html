encouragingly

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈkɝː.ɪ.dʒɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ɪnˈkʌr.ɪ.dʒɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that gives someone confidence or hope.
in an encouraging manner

Examples

She smiled encouragingly at her friend before his big performance.
The coach nodded encouragingly as the team prepared for the second half.
Parents often speak encouragingly to boost their children's confidence.

Synset Examples

‘Go on,’ he said encouragingly to his student

Related Terms

  • Antonym: discouragingly - in a discouraging manner

Etymology

From 'encouraging' + '-ly', based on Old French encouragier (to make strong, hearten).
