Vistula

1. PROPN

/ ˈvɪstʃələ / (AmE), / ˈvɪstjʊlə / (BrE)
the longest and largest river in Poland, flowing north into the Baltic Sea
longest river in Poland

Examples

The city of Warsaw is situated on the banks of the Vistula.
Many tourists enjoy taking boat trips along the Vistula during the summer months.
The Vistula plays a vital role in Poland's transportation and trade.

Etymology

From Polish Wisła, via Latin Vistula.
