hosiery

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊʒəri / (AmE), / ˈhəʊzəri / (BrE)
Stockings, socks, tights, and similar knitted or woven legwear, collectively; in British usage, may also include underwear.
stockings, socks, and similar legwear

Examples

The department store has a wide selection of hosiery in various colors and styles.
She bought a pair of patterned tights from the hosiery section.
Many professionals prefer quality hosiery for a polished appearance at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stockings - close-fitting coverings for the feet and legs, typically made of nylon or silk
  • Synonym: socks - garments for the feet, typically knitted from wool, cotton, or nylon
  • Degree Lower: underwear - clothing worn next to the skin under outer clothes

Etymology

From Middle English 'hosiery', from 'hose' (covering for the leg), ultimately from Old English 'hosa'.
