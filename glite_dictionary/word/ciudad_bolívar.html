Ciudad Bolívar

1. PROPN

/ siˌuːðɑd boʊˈliːvɑr / (AmE), / siˌuːðad bəʊˈliːvɑː / (BrE)
A city and port in eastern Venezuela on the Orinoco River, serving as the capital of Bolívar State.
city and port in eastern Venezuela

Examples

Many travelers begin their journey to Angel Falls from Ciudad Bolívar.
The Orinoco River passes through Ciudad Bolívar, making it a key transport hub.
Ciudad Bolívar is known for its colonial architecture and vibrant markets.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Ciudad Bolívar', named after Simón Bolívar.
