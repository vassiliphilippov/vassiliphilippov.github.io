painlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈpeɪnlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈpeɪnləsli / (BrE)
In a way that causes no pain or discomfort.
without pain; causing no pain

Examples

The dentist was able to remove the tooth painlessly, to my great relief.
She completed the marathon almost painlessly, thanks to her thorough training.
The procedure can be performed painlessly under local anesthesia.

Synset Examples

after the surgery, she could move her arms painlessly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: painful - causing physical or emotional pain
  • Synonym: painless - not causing pain
