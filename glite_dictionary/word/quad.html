quad

1. NOUN

/ kwɑd / (AmE), / kwɒd / (BrE)
(Printing) a block of type metal with a flat, plain face, used for spacing between words or lines in typesetting.
space block in printing

Examples

In traditional printing, a quad was inserted to create a wider gap between paragraphs.
The compositor reached for a quad to adjust the spacing on the page.
Various sizes of quads were kept in handy compartments at the typesetter's station.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quadrat - a piece of type metal used for spacing in printing

Etymology

Short for 'quadrat', from Latin 'quadratus' (square).

2. NOUN

/ kwɑd / (AmE), / kwɒd / (BrE)
the large muscle at the front of the thigh that is responsible for extending the leg at the knee; short for quadriceps
muscle at the front of the thigh
informal

Examples

After months of rehabilitation, his quad finally regained its strength.
Stretching your quads before running can help prevent injuries.
She felt a sharp pain in her quad after the uphill sprint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quadriceps - the large muscle at the front of the thigh

Etymology

Shortened form of 'quadriceps', from Latin 'quadri-' (four) and 'caput' (head).
