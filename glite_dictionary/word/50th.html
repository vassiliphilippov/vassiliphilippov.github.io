50th

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪftiəθ / (AmE), / ˈfɪftiəθ / (BrE)
being number fifty in a sequence; the ordinal number of fifty.
fiftieth in a sequence

Examples

This year marks the 50th anniversary of the historic event.
He finished in 50th place out of one hundred participants.
The 50th chapter of the book contains the key revelation.

Related Terms
