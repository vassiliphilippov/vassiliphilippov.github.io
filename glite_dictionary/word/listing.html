listing

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
a printed or electronic record showing an ordered set of items, such as names or topics, often for reference or selection
ordered record of items

Examples

The real estate agent sent us a listing of available properties in our price range.
You can find the full listing of today's television programs in the newspaper.
They updated the listing to include new restaurants that have opened recently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: list - a collection or series of names or items written or printed together, usually one below the other

Etymology

From the verb 'list' (to set down in order), plus the suffix -ing, dating to the late 16th century.

2. NOUN

/ ˈlɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of writing or forming a list of items
the act of making a list

Examples

The listing of names took over an hour to complete.
His job involved the listing of all incoming shipments.
Before packing, she made a listing of everything she needed to bring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: list - a series of items written or printed together

Etymology

From the verb 'list' + -ing, dating from the 17th century.
