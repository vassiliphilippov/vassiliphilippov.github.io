vambrace

1. NOUN

/ ˈvæmˌbreɪs / (AmE), / ˈvæm.breɪs / (BrE)
A piece of plate armor designed to protect the forearm.
forearm armor piece

Examples

The knight adjusted his vambrace before riding into battle.
Her costume included a replica vambrace made from hardened leather.
Archaeologists discovered a medieval vambrace buried alongside other pieces of armor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bracer - a protective covering for the forearm, especially in archery or armor

Etymology

From Old French 'avantbras', meaning 'forearm' (literally 'before-arm').
