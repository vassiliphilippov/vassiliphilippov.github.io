unicorn

1. NOUN

/ ˈjunɪˌkɔrn / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪˌkɔːn / (BrE)
A mythical animal typically depicted as a white horse with a single spiraling horn projecting from its forehead.
mythical horse-like animal with one horn

Examples

As a child, she loved stories about the magical unicorn and its adventures.
The tapestry depicted a majestic unicorn surrounded by forest animals.
Many believed the existence of the unicorn until it was proven to be a myth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mythical creature - an imaginary or legendary animal, often found in folklore or mythology

Etymology

From Latin 'unicornis', from 'uni-' (one) + 'cornu' (horn).
