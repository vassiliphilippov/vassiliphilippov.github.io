Bextra

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɛkstrə / (AmE), / ˈbɛkstrə / (BrE)
a trademark for valdecoxib, a Cox-2 inhibitor used to relieve pain and inflammation with less risk of gastrointestinal side effects than some other painkillers
trade name for anti-inflammatory drug valdecoxib
medical

Examples

Her doctor prescribed Bextra to help manage her arthritis symptoms.
Following concerns about side effects, Bextra was withdrawn from the market.
Many patients found Bextra effective in reducing pain without upsetting the stomach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: valdecoxib - the generic name of the drug sold as Bextra
