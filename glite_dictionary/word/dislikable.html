dislikable

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈlaɪk.ə.bəl / (AmE), / dɪsˈlaɪk.ə.bəl / (BrE)
Likely to be disliked; causing or deserving dislike.
causing or deserving dislike

Examples

His rude behavior made him a very dislikable person at the office.
The character in the novel was so dislikable that readers felt little sympathy for her.
Despite her talents, her arrogance often made her seem dislikable to her teammates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unpleasant - not enjoyable or attractive; causing discomfort or dislike
  • Antonym: likable - pleasant and easy to like

Etymology

From 'dislike' (to not like) + '-able' (capable of).
