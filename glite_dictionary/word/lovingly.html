lovingly

1. ADV

/ ˈlʌvɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈlʌvɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows love or great affection
in an affectionate way

Examples

She smiled lovingly at her newborn daughter.
He lovingly prepared her favorite meal for their anniversary.
The old letters were lovingly preserved in a special box.

Synset Examples

she spoke to her children fondly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fondly - with affection or tenderness
  • Antonym: coldly - in an unemotional or unfriendly way

Etymology

From 'loving' + '-ly', where 'loving' comes from 'love' (Old English 'lufu').
