hemophilic

1. ADJ

/ ˌhiːməˈfɪlɪk / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˈfɪlɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or having hemophilia, a medical condition where the blood does not clot properly.
relating to hemophilia; having hemophilia
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that a hemophilic patient requires special care during surgery.
Some families have a history of hemophilic disorders passed through generations.
Researchers are studying new treatments for hemophilic children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hemophilia - a medical condition in which the blood does not clot properly

Etymology

From hemophilia + -ic.
