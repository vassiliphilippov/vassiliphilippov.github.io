exquisite

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈskwɪzɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛkskwɪzɪt / (BrE)
extremely beautiful or delicate; extraordinarily pleasing in appearance or style.
extremely beautiful or delicate

Examples

She wore an exquisite gown that caught everyone's attention at the gala.
The museum showcased an exquisite piece of Renaissance jewelry.
His exquisite taste in art is admired by his friends.

Synset Examples

her exquisite face

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beautiful - pleasing to the senses or mind aesthetically
  • Antonym: ugly - unpleasant or repulsive in appearance

Etymology

From Latin 'exquisitus' (carefully sought out, rare, choice), past participle of 'exquirere' (to seek out).
