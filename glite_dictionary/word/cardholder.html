cardholder

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrdˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person who has and is authorized to use a credit card or debit card
person possessing a credit or debit card

Examples

Each cardholder must sign the back of their credit card as soon as they receive it.
The bank will contact the cardholder if any suspicious activity is detected.
Every cardholder is advised to keep their PIN confidential.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: account holder - a person who holds an account, especially at a bank

Etymology

From 'card' (as in credit or debit card) + 'holder' (person possessing something).

2. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrdˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person who holds a card or cards in a card game
a player holding cards

Examples

Each cardholder must keep their cards hidden from the other players.
The dealer asked every cardholder to show their hand at the end of the round.
In some games, a cardholder can choose to draw an additional card.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: player - someone who participates in a game or sport
