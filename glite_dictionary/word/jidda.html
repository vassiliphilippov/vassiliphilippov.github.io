Jidda

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɪdə / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪdə / (BrE)
A port city in western Saudi Arabia on the Red Sea, located near Mecca; also known as Jeddah.
city in western Saudi Arabia

Examples

Many pilgrims to Mecca arrive first in Jidda before completing their journey.
The port of Jidda is one of Saudi Arabia's major commercial hubs.
She spent a semester abroad studying marine biology in Jidda.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jeddah - alternative spelling of Jidda, city in Saudi Arabia

Etymology

From Arabic جدة (Jidda or Jeddah), meaning 'seashore' or 'grandmother'.
