scoffingly

1. ADV

/ ˈskɔf.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈskɒf.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a mocking or disrespectful manner; with derision or scorn.
in a mocking or scornful way

Examples

He replied scoffingly to every suggestion I made, making it clear he thought little of my ideas.
She laughed scoffingly at the proposal, dismissing it as completely unrealistic.
Whenever he loses, he scoffingly blames others instead of accepting responsibility.

Synset Examples

‘Sorry,’ she repeated derisively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: derisively - in a mocking or contemptuous manner
  • Antonym: respectfully - in a polite or respectful manner

Etymology

From 'scoff' + '-ingly'.
