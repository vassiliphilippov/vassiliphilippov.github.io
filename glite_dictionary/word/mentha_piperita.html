Mentha piperita

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛnθə paɪˈpɛrɪtə / (AmE), / ˌmɛnθə pɪˈpɛrɪtə / (BrE)
A herb with downy leaves and small purple or white flowers, known for producing a pungent oil used as a flavoring; commonly known as peppermint.
peppermint plant; aromatic herb

Examples

The essential oil extracted from Mentha piperita is widely used in candies and toothpaste.
Gardeners often plant Mentha piperita to enjoy its aromatic leaves and attractive flowers.
Research suggests that Mentha piperita tea may help soothe digestive discomfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peppermint - Common name for Mentha piperita, an aromatic herb used for flavoring
  • Synonym: spearmint - A related mint species, Mentha spicata, also used as a flavoring herb

Etymology

From Latin *Mentha piperita*, meaning 'peppermint'.
