yeastlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈjiːstˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈjiːstˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having characteristics of or resembling yeast; similar to or containing yeast.
resembling or containing yeast

Examples

The scientist noted a yeastlike odor coming from the culture dish.
Some infections are caused by yeastlike fungi rather than true yeasts.
During fermentation, a yeastlike foam formed on the liquid's surface.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yeasty - frothy or resembling yeast in nature
