fleapit

1. NOUN

/ ˈfliˌpæt / (AmE), / ˈfliːˌpæt / (BrE)
A small, old, and shabby cinema or theater, especially one considered dirty or poorly maintained.
shabby or rundown cinema/theater
informal british

Examples

The old fleapit down the road still shows classic films every weekend.
We watched the movie in a tiny fleapit that smelled of popcorn and dust.
Despite its shabby appearance, the fleapit was packed with loyal regulars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cinema - a theater where films are shown
  • Synonym: theater - a building used for the presentation of plays or other entertainment

Etymology

From 'flea' + 'pit', alluding humorously to the poor conditions.
