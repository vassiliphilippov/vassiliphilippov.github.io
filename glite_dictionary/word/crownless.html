crownless

1. ADJ

/ ˈkraʊn.ləs / (AmE), / ˈkraʊn.ləs / (BrE)
Lacking a crown; not provided with a crown, especially in reference to a ruler who has not yet been formally crowned.
without a crown

Examples

After the coup, the crownless prince wandered the countryside in disguise.
Shakespeare described the tragic figure as a crownless monarch, robbed of his title and throne.
Though crownless, she commanded respect from all who knew her.

Synset Examples

the uncrowned king

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uncrowned - not having or not yet given a crown or official status as king/queen

Etymology

From "crown" + the suffix "-less", meaning 'without'.
