combed

1. ADJ

/ koʊmd / (AmE), / kəʊmd / (BrE)
(of hair) made tidy by being passed through with a comb.
hair made tidy with a comb

Examples

Her combed hair glistened in the morning sunlight.
He always keeps his beard neatly combed for work.
The child's hair was perfectly combed before the family photo.

Synset Examples

with hair combed to the side

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unkempt - (of hair) not combed; untidy
  • Synonym: brushed - (of hair) made tidy by being brushed
