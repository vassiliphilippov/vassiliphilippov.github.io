Podalyria

/ pəˈdəˌlɪriə / (AmE), / pəˈdalɪriə / (BrE)

2. NOUN

/ poʊˈdælərɪə / (AmE), / pəʊˈdalɪrɪə / (BrE)
A genus of South African leguminous shrubs, often included in the genus Sophora.
genus of South African shrubs

Examples

The genus Podalyria is known for its attractive pink flowers and silver foliage.
Several species within the Podalyria genus are grown as ornamentals in South African gardens.
Botanists debated whether Podalyria should remain a distinct genus or be merged with Sophora.

Etymology

Named after Podalirius, a physician in Greek mythology.
