to greet

1. VERB

/ ɡrit / (AmE), / ɡriːt / (BrE)
(of a thing or sensation) to be the first thing noticed or experienced on arriving somewhere.
to be first perceived on arriving

Examples

A wave of cold air greeted us as we stepped into the room.
The smell of fresh bread greeted her when she opened the door.
A stunning view greeted them at the top of the mountain.

Synset Examples

Loud music greeted him when he entered the apartment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welcome - to receive in a friendly or glad manner
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02202019-v
Synset POS: v

2. VERB

/ ɡrit / (AmE), / ɡriːt / (BrE)
to say hello or welcome someone when you meet them
to say hello or welcome someone

Examples

She always greets her colleagues with a smile in the morning.
They stood at the door to greet their guests as they arrived.
He greeted me warmly after not seeing me for years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welcome - to greet someone in a friendly way upon arrival
  • Antonym: farewell - an expression of good wishes when parting

Etymology

From Old English grētan (to come in contact with, salute).

3. VERB

/ ɡrit / (AmE), / ɡriːt / (BrE)
to say hello to someone or express good wishes when meeting or communicating with them
say hello to; send greetings

Examples

As soon as I entered the room, she greeted me with a warm smile.
He always greets his neighbors when he sees them in the morning.
We were greeted at the door by a friendly receptionist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welcome - to greet someone in a warm and friendly way
  • Antonym: farewell - to say goodbye; opposite of greeting

Etymology

From Old English grētan (to approach, address, salute).

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: v

4. VERB

/ ɡrit / (AmE), / ɡriːt / (BrE)
to react or respond to someone or something in a particular way when you meet or encounter them
react or respond to upon meeting

Examples

The arrival of the new policy was greeted with skepticism by many employees.
The audience greeted the performer with thunderous applause as she walked on stage.
His proposal was greeted by silence, making him feel uneasy.

Synset Examples

The President was greeted with catcalls

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welcome - to greet someone in a warm and friendly way
  • Antonym: to ignore - to deliberately pay no attention to someone when encountered
