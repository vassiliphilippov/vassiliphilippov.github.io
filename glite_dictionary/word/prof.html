prof

1. NOUN

/ prɑf / (AmE), / prɒf / (BrE)
an informal term for a college or university professor; a faculty member at an institution of higher learning
informal for professor, faculty member
informal

Examples

The prof gave us an extra week to finish the assignment.
She always goes to her prof for advice about her research projects.
Our economics prof makes lectures engaging and thought-provoking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: professor - a teacher of the highest rank in a college or university

Etymology

Shortened from 'professor,' first recorded in the late 19th century.
