evaporated

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈvæpəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ɪˈvapəreɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a liquid) changed into vapor, especially by the action of heat.
changed into vapor by heat

Examples

Some of the water in the pond had already evaporated by midday.
They collected the evaporated salt left behind after the water boiled away.
The evaporated molecules left a thin mist in the air.

Synset Examples

evaporated molecules boil off

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vaporized - changed into vapor
