anatomic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.əˈtɑ.mɪk / (AmE), / ˌæn.əˈtɒm.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to the structure of the body or the science of anatomy
relating to body structure

Examples

The surgeon explained the anatomic differences between the two procedures.
Students studied the anatomic structures of the heart in detail.
Some diseases are classified based on their anatomic location in the body.

Synset Examples

anatomical features

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anatomical - relating to anatomy or body structure

Etymology

From Greek anatomikos, relating to dissection or anatomy.

2. ADJ

/ ˌænəˈtɑmɪk / (AmE), / ˌænəˈtɒmɪk / (BrE)
relating to anatomy or the structure of living organisms.
relating to anatomy

Examples

The surgeon explained the anatomic differences between the two organs.
Detailed anatomic studies were required to identify the new species.
She specialized in anatomic pathology during her medical training.
