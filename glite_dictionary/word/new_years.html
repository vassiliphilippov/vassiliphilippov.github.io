New Year's

1. NOUN

/ nuː ˈjɪrz / (AmE), / njuː ˈjɜːz / (BrE)
The first day of the year, celebrated as a holiday in many countries.
the first day of the year

Examples

We always visit my grandparents on New Year's to celebrate together.
The city holds a fireworks show every New Year's.
I made a resolution to exercise more beginning this New Year's.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: New Year's Day - the first day of the year
  • Antonym: New Year's Eve - the day before New Year's Day
