Agdestis

1. NOUN

/ æɡˈdɛstɪs / (AmE), / aɡˈdɛstɪs / (BrE)
A genus of rapidly growing climbing vine with tuberous roots, native to hot climates, with only one known species.
genus of climbing vine with tuberous roots

Examples

Agdestis is known for thriving in tropical and subtropical regions.
Botanists recently discovered a new location where Agdestis has spread rapidly.
The unique tuberous roots of Agdestis help it survive periods of drought.

Etymology

From Greek 'Agdestis', a mythological figure, used in botanical naming.

Examples
