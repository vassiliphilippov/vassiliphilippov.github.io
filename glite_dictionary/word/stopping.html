stopping

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
the technique of pressing the fingers on the strings of a stringed instrument to control the pitch
finger technique on stringed instruments

Examples

She praised the cellist's stopping for its clarity and precision.
Good stopping is essential for producing clean notes on the violin.
Mastering advanced stopping techniques takes years of practice.

Synset Examples

the violinist's stopping was excellent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fingering - specific way in which the fingers are placed on the strings or keys when playing an instrument
