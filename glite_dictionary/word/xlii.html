xlii

1. NUM

/ ɛks ɛl aɪ aɪ / (AmE), / ɛks ɛl aɪ aɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral representing the number 42
Roman numeral for 42

Examples

The inscription on the building reads xlii, marking the forty-second year of the century.
In the quiz, we learned that xlii stands for the number forty-two in Roman numerals.
The clock has the hour marked as xlii instead of 42.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 42 - the cardinal number after 41 and before 43

Etymology

From Latin numerals, where 'XL' represents 40 (10 before 50) and 'II' means two.
