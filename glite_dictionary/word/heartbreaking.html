heartbreaking

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑrtˌbreɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtˌbreɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
causing intense sorrow, distress, or grief
causing intense emotional pain

Examples

She received the heartbreaking news that her childhood home had burned down.
The film tells a heartbreaking story of loss and resilience.
It was heartbreaking to see the abandoned puppy waiting for its owner.

Synset Examples

a grievous loss
a grievous cry
her sigh was heartbreaking
the heartrending words of Rabin's granddaughter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartrending - causing deep sorrow or distress
  • Antonym: uplifting - making someone feel happier or more hopeful

Etymology

From 'heartbreak' + '-ing', formed in English.
