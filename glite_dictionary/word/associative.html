associative

1. ADJ

/ əˈsoʊsiˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / əˈsəʊsiətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or causing the connection of ideas, memories, or events in the mind.
pertaining to connecting ideas or memories

Examples

The researcher studied associative learning to understand how animals connect different experiences.
Memory can be enhanced through associative techniques such as linking new information to familiar concepts.
His associative thinking allowed him to make creative connections between seemingly unrelated ideas.

Synset Examples

associative learning

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dissociative - tending to separate ideas, memories, or identities

Etymology

From Latin 'associatus', past participle of 'associare' (to unite with).
