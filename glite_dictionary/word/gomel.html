Gomel

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡoʊ.mɛl / (AmE), / ˈɡɒ.mɛl / (BrE)
a city in southeastern Belarus, known as an industrial center
city in southeastern Belarus

Examples

She moved from Minsk to Gomel to start a new job.
The factories in Gomel provide employment for thousands of people.
Many tourists visit Gomel to explore its parks and cultural sites.

Etymology

From Russian and Belarusian Гомель (Homel’), the name of the city.
