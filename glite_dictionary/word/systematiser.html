systematiser

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪstəməˌtaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəmətaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who arranges things in a systematic and organized manner
person who organizes methodically
british

Examples

She is a true systematiser, able to bring order to the most chaotic projects.
As a systematiser, he developed a new framework for managing data efficiently.
The company's success was largely due to its founder, a natural systematiser of business processes.

Synset Examples

Aristotle was a great orderer of ideas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: systematizer - person who arranges things systematically; American spelling
  • Synonym: organizer - person who arranges or structures things

Etymology

From systematize + -er; chiefly a British spelling of 'systematizer'.
