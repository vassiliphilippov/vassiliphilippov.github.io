Paul Simon

1. PROPN

/ pɔl ˈsaɪmən / (AmE), / pɔːl ˈsaɪmən / (BrE)
(born 1942) An American singer and songwriter known for his work as part of the duo Simon & Garfunkel and for his solo career.
American singer-songwriter, part of Simon & Garfunkel

Examples

Paul Simon wrote many classic hits as both a solo artist and member of Simon & Garfunkel.
The Grammy Award was accepted by Paul Simon during the televised ceremony.
Music critics often praise the poetic lyrics of Paul Simon's songs.
