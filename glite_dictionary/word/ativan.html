Ativan

1. NOUN

/ ˈætɪvæn / (AmE), / ˈætɪvan / (BrE)
A trademarked medication (lorazepam) used as a tranquilizer to treat anxiety, tension, and insomnia.
trade name for a tranquilizer
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Ativan to help manage her anxiety attacks.
He found that Ativan was effective in treating short-term insomnia.
After taking Ativan, she felt calmer and was able to sleep through the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lorazepam - the generic name for Ativan, a type of benzodiazepine tranquilizer
