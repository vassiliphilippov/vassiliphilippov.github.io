to blow-dry

1. VERB

/ bloʊ ˌdraɪ / (AmE), / bləʊ ˌdraɪ / (BrE)
to dry hair using a hair dryer, often to style it
dry hair with a hair dryer

Examples

After washing her hair, she decided to blow-dry it for a smoother finish.
My stylist usually blow-dries my hair straight when I get a haircut.
He quickly blow-dried his hair before leaving for work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dry - to remove moisture from something
