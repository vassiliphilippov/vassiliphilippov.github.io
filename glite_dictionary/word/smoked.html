smoked

1. ADJ

/ smoʊkt / (AmE), / sməʊkt / (BrE)
(of food, especially meat or fish) dried and cured by exposure to smoke from burning wood or other materials.
dried and cured by smoke

Examples

He brought a platter of smoked salmon and cheeses to the table.
The shop specializes in smoked meats and traditional sausages.
I love the rich flavor of smoked fish in pasta dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salted - (of food) preserved or seasoned with salt

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'smoke'.
