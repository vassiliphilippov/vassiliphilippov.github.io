burrlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɜrˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɜːˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling a burr, especially by being prickly or covered in small hooks or bristles
resembling a burr; prickly

Examples

The surface of the plant was burrlike, sticking to my clothes as I brushed past.
She described the dog's fur as burrlike due to all the small tangles and debris.
The caterpillar's burrlike spines made it difficult for predators to handle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prickly - covered with prickles or sharp points

Etymology

Formed from 'burr' + 'like', where 'burr' refers to a prickly seed pod.
