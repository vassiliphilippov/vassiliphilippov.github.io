seashore

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiˌʃɔr / (AmE), / ˈsiːʃɔː / (BrE)
the land along the edge of a sea or ocean
shore of a sea or ocean

Examples

We collected seashells along the seashore during our vacation.
Children were running and playing on the seashore at sunset.
The house offers a beautiful view overlooking the seashore.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shore - the land along the edge of a body of water
  • Synonym: coast - the part of the land near the sea; the edge of the sea
