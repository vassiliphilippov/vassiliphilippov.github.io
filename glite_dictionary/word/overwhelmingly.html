overwhelmingly

1. ADV

/ ˌoʊvɚˈhwɛlmɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈwɛlmɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is very great in effect, power, or degree; to such an extent as to be difficult or impossible to resist
to a very great and irresistible degree

Examples

She found his offer overwhelmingly attractive and could not say no.
The evidence against the defendant was overwhelmingly convincing to the jury.
The party was overwhelmingly supported by young voters in the election.

Synset Examples

the candy looked overwhelmingly desirable to the dieting man

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irresistibly - in a way that cannot be resisted

Etymology

From 'overwhelming' (from overwhelm) + -ly.
