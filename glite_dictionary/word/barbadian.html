Barbadian

1. NOUN

/ bɑːrˈbeɪdiən / (AmE), / bɑːˈbeɪdiən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Barbados.
person from Barbados

Examples

The famous cricketer is a proud Barbadian who represents his country on the world stage.
Many Barbadians celebrate their independence day with music, food, and parades.
Her grandmother, a native Barbadian, often shares stories about life in Barbados.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bajan - an informal term for a Barbadian

Etymology

From Barbados + English suffix -ian, denoting origin or association.
