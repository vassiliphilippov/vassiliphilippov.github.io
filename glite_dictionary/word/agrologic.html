agrologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæɡrəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɡrəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
Relating to agrology, the scientific study of soils in relation to agriculture.
relating to the study of soils in agriculture

Examples

The agrologic properties of the region's soils determine which crops can be successfully cultivated.
Researchers presented their agrologic findings at the international conference on sustainable farming.
An agrologic survey is essential before starting any major agricultural project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agrological - relating to agrology
  • Synonym: agrology - the science of soils in relation to agriculture

Etymology

From 'agrology' (study of soils in relation to agriculture) + '-ic' (forming adjectives).
