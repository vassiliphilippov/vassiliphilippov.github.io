sari

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːri / (AmE), / ˈsɑːri / (BrE)
a garment worn by women in South Asia, consisting of a long piece of cloth wrapped around the body and draped over the shoulder
traditional South Asian women's dress

Examples

She wore a beautiful red sari to the wedding ceremony.
The intricate designs on her sari caught everyone's attention.
Many women in India prefer to wear a sari for special occasions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saree - alternative spelling of sari

Etymology

From Hindi साड़ी (sāṛī) meaning 'strip of cloth'.
