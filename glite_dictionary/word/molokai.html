Molokai

1. PROPN

/ ˌmoʊ.ləˈkaɪ / (AmE), / ˌməʊ.ləˈkaɪ / (BrE)
an island in central Hawaii, situated between Maui and Oahu.
Hawaiian island between Maui and Oahu

Examples

We spent a week hiking across Molokai and enjoyed its peaceful landscapes.
The island of Molokai is famous for its dramatic sea cliffs and historical sites.
She plans to visit Molokai during her trip to Hawaii next year.

Etymology

From Hawaiian 'Molokaʻi'.
