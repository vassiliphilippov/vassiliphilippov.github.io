1980s

1. NOUN

/ naɪnˈtiːn ˈeɪ.tiz / (AmE), / naɪnˈtiːn ˈeɪ.tiz / (BrE)
the decade beginning in 1980 and ending in 1989
the decade from 1980 to 1989

Examples

Music styles from the 1980s are making a comeback in today's pop culture.
Many iconic films were released during the 1980s, shaping an entire generation.
Fashion trends from the 1980s are often considered bold and colorful.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: 1990s - the decade from 1990 to 1999
  • Degree Lower: 1970s - the decade from 1970 to 1979
