sickroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪkˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈsɪk.rʊm / (BrE)
a room where a person who is ill is confined or cared for
room for a person who is ill

Examples

The nurse quietly entered the sickroom to check on her patient.
Fresh flowers were placed on the bedside table in the sickroom.
Because of the flu, he was confined to his sickroom for a week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ward - a large room in a hospital for patients
  • Synonym: infirmary - a place in a school, prison, or other institution for people who are ill

Etymology

Formed from 'sick' + 'room', meaning a room for the sick.
