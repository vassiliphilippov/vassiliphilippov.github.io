Papaverales

1. NOUN

/ pəˌpævəˈreɪliz / (AmE), / pəˌpævəˈreɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of dicotyledonous flowering plants that includes the poppy family (Papaveraceae) and several related families
order of flowering plants including poppies

Examples

The Papaverales order encompasses a variety of plants including the well-known poppy.
Researchers are studying the evolutionary history of the Papaverales in relation to other flowering plants.
Many species within the Papaverales are valued for their medicinal and ornamental properties.

Etymology

From Latin 'Papaver' (poppy) + '-ales' (suffix used in taxonomy for orders).
