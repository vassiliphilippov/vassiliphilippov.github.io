Fulvicin

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊl.vɪ.sɪn / (AmE), / ˈfʊl.vɪ.sɪn / (BrE)
A trade name for griseofulvin, a fungicidal antibiotic used especially to treat fungal infections of the skin, hair, and nails.
trade name for an antifungal antibiotic
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Fulvicin to treat the patient's ringworm infection.
Many patients respond well to Fulvicin for persistent fungal nail diseases.
Side effects of Fulvicin may include headaches or gastrointestinal discomfort.
