acromegalic

1. ADJ

/ ˌækrəˈmɛɡəlɪk / (AmE), / ˌakrəʊˈmɛɡəlɪk / (BrE)
having features or characteristics resulting from abnormal enlargement of the extremities or face, typically caused by acromegaly
having enlarged extremities or face (medical)
medical

Examples

The doctor noted the patient's acromegalic hands and jaw.
The sculpture depicted a figure with acromegalic features, including a pronounced brow and chin.
Certain hormonal disorders can result in an acromegalic appearance.

Synset Examples

a protruding acromegalic jaw

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acromegaly - a disorder marked by abnormal growth of the hands, feet, and face

Etymology

From Greek 'akron' (extremity) and 'megas' (large), after the disease acromegaly.
