supermarketer

1. NOUN

/ suːpərˌmɑrkətər / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈmɑːkɪtə / (BrE)
a person or organization that operates or manages a supermarket
person or company running a supermarket

Examples

The supermarketer decided to introduce a new range of organic products to attract more customers.
As a successful supermarketer, she managed several stores throughout the city.
The local newspaper interviewed a prominent supermarketer about changes in shopping habits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grocer - a person who owns or manages a grocery store
