to sculp

1. VERB

/ skʌlp / (AmE), / skʌlp / (BrE)
to create or form sculptures as an art or craft
to make sculptures

Examples

At the workshop, each student learned how to sculp a small animal from clay.
He prefers to sculp with wood rather than with stone.
Artists have sculped marble for centuries to create enduring masterpieces.

Synset Examples

She loves to sculp in her free time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sculpt - to create or shape a sculpture

Etymology

Back-formation from 'sculpture'.
