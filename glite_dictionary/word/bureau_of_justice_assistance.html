Bureau of Justice Assistance

1. PROPN

/ bjʊˈroʊ əv ˈdʒʌstɪs əˈsɪstəns / (AmE), / bjʊəˈrəʊ əv ˈdʒʌstɪs əˈsɪstəns / (BrE)
an agency within the U.S. Department of Justice that provides support to local and state criminal justice systems to reduce crime, violence, and drug abuse
U.S. Department of Justice agency supporting criminal justice systems
american

Examples

The Bureau of Justice Assistance provides funding to help local police departments combat drug-related crime.
Last year, the city received a grant from the Bureau of Justice Assistance to improve community policing strategies.
Training programs for correctional officers are often developed in collaboration with the Bureau of Justice Assistance.
