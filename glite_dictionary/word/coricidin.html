Coricidin

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɔːrəˈsɪdən / (AmE), / ˌkɒrɪˈsɪdɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for an over-the-counter antihistamine used to relieve allergy symptoms such as runny nose and sneezing.
trademarked antihistamine for allergies
medical

Examples

The doctor recommended Coricidin to help manage my allergy symptoms during spring.
If you have high blood pressure, Coricidin is sometimes considered a safer option for cold relief.
Some pharmacies keep Coricidin behind the counter due to potential misuse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chlor-Trimeton - a trade name for an antihistamine similar to Coricidin
