selected

1. ADJ

/ sɪˈlɛktɪd / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛktɪd / (BrE)
chosen from a group as the most suitable, preferred, or desirable
chosen as the most suitable

Examples

Only the selected candidates will be invited for an interview.
This exhibition features selected works from the artist's early career.
He ate only the selected pieces of fruit from the basket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chosen - picked or selected from a group
  • Antonym: rejected - not chosen or turned down

Etymology

Past participle of 'select', from Latin 'selectus' (chosen).
