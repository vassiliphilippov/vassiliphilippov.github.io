straitlaced

1. ADJ

/ ˈstreɪtˌleɪst / (AmE), / ˈstreɪtˌleɪst / (BrE)
Excessively strict in manners, morals, or opinion; having or showing very traditional or formal beliefs and disapproving of anything seen as improper.
excessively proper or morally strict

Examples

Her parents are so straitlaced that they disapprove of any form of casual dating.
The headmistress was known for her straitlaced views on discipline and decorum.
Despite his straitlaced upbringing, he grew to embrace more liberal ideas.

Synset Examples

my straitlaced Aunt Anna doesn't approve of my miniskirts

Related Terms

  • Antonym: permissive - allowing or characterized by great or excessive freedom of behavior
  • Synonym: upright - very honest, moral, and righteous in behavior

Etymology

From 'strait' (narrow, strict) + 'laced' (fastened with laces); originally referring to tightly laced corsets symbolizing strictness.
