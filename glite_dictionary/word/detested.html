detested

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈtɛstɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈtɛstɪd / (BrE)
regarded with intense dislike or hatred
strongly disliked or hated

Examples

The dictator was detested by his people for years of oppression.
She felt detested after the argument with her closest friends.
His detested views made him unpopular in social circles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hated - regarded with intense dislike or aversion
  • Antonym: loved - regarded with deep affection

Etymology

From Latin 'detestari' (to curse, to loathe), via the verb 'detest'.
