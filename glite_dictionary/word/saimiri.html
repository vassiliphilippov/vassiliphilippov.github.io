Saimiri

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈmiːri / (AmE), / saɪˈmiːri / (BrE)
A genus of small, agile New World monkeys known as squirrel monkeys, native to Central and South America.
a genus of squirrel monkeys

Examples

The genus Saimiri includes several species of squirrel monkeys found in the Amazon basin.
Researchers are studying the behavior of Saimiri in their natural habitat.
Many zoos now have breeding programs for Saimiri due to their popularity and ecological importance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squirrel monkey - a small, agile New World monkey of the genus Saimiri

Etymology

From Latinized form of a Tupi (indigenous Brazilian) word for squirrel monkey.
