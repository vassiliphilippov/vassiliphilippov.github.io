captain

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæp.tən / (AmE), / ˈkæp.tɪn / (BrE)
a senior dining-room attendant responsible for supervising waiters and managing the seating of customers
senior dining-room attendant

Examples

The captain greeted us at the entrance and showed us to our table.
When you need wine recommendations, the captain is the person to ask.
The captain ensured that all the guests were seated promptly and comfortably.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head waiter - the chief waiter in a restaurant, similar role to captain

Etymology

From Latin 'capitaneus' (chief), from 'caput' (head).
