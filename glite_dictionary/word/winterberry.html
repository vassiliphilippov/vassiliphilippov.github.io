winterberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪntɚˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈwɪntəbəri / (BrE)
a deciduous shrub of eastern North America, having bright red berries that persist into winter
deciduous shrub with red winter berries

Examples

The winterberry adds a splash of color to the garden with its bright red fruits.
Birds flock to the winterberry bushes in late autumn for the berries.
She planted a winterberry along the fence to provide winter interest.
