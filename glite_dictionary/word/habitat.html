habitat

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæb.əˌtæt / (AmE), / ˈhæb.ɪ.tæt / (BrE)
the natural environment in which a plant or animal normally lives or grows
natural environment an organism lives in

Examples

The polar bear's habitat is threatened by melting sea ice.
Tropical rainforests provide a habitat for countless plant and animal species.
The destruction of wetlands has reduced the natural habitat for many birds.

Synset Examples

a marine habitat
he felt safe on his home grounds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: environment - the surrounding conditions affecting an organism

Etymology

From Latin *habitare* (to dwell), frequentative of *habere* (to have, to hold).
