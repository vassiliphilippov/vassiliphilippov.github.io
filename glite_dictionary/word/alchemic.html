alchemic

1. ADJ

/ ælˈkɛmɪk / (AmE), / ælˈkɛmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of alchemy or alchemists.
relating to alchemy

Examples

The ancient manuscript contained alchemic symbols and obscure recipes.
Her interest in alchemic transformation influenced her approach to chemistry.
The novel explores an alchemic world of potions, metals, and secret knowledge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alchemical - relating to alchemy (alternative form)

Etymology

From Medieval Latin alchemicus, from alchimia (alchemy).
