Kopite

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɒpaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒpaɪt / (BrE)
A dedicated supporter of Liverpool Football Club, especially one who regularly attends matches at Anfield.
Liverpool FC supporter

Examples

Thousands of Kopites cheered on their team at the Champions League final.
As a lifelong Kopite, she rarely misses a home game at Anfield.
The chants from the Kopites could be heard all across the city.

Synset Examples

The Kopite fans filled the stadium with energy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Liverpool supporter - a person who supports Liverpool FC
  • Antonym: Evertonian - a fan of Everton FC (Liverpool's main rivals)

Etymology

From 'Kop', a famous stand at Liverpool's Anfield stadium, combined with the suffix '-ite' (denoting a follower).
