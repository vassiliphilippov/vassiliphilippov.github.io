spottedness

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑː.tɪd.nəs / (AmE), / ˈspɒt.ɪd.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being marked with spots
quality of being spotted

Examples

Giraffes are recognized for their tall stature and distinctive spottedness.
The artist tried to capture the spottedness of the animal's fur in his painting.
Some mushrooms are identifiable by the spottedness on their caps.

Synset Examples

They are often mistaken for leopards because of rough similarity in size and spottedness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spot - a small round mark or stain

Etymology

From 'spotted' + '-ness', where 'spotted' is derived from 'spot'.
