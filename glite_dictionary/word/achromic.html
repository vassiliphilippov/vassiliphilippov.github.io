achromic

1. ADJ

/ əˈkroʊ.mɪk / (AmE), / əˈkrəʊ.mɪk / (BrE)
lacking color; colorless.
lacking color; colorless

Examples

The scientist examined an achromic solution under the microscope.
Some forms of albinism leave the skin almost completely achromic.
The achromic tissues did not absorb the dye during the test.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colorless - having no color
  • Antonym: chromatic - relating to or produced by color

Etymology

From Greek a- (without) + chrōma (color).
