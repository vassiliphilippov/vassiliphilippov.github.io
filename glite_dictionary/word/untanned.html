untanned

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtænd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtand / (BrE)
(of a hide or skin) not converted into leather by treatment with a tanning agent.
not processed into leather

Examples

The craftsman used untanned animal skins to create traditional drums.
Because the hide was untanned, it remained flexible but was not as durable as leather.
A primitive shelter was covered with untanned hides for protection against the rain.

Synset Examples

a whip made of untanned hide

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tanned - (of a hide or skin) converted into leather by tanning
