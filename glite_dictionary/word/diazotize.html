to diazotize

1. VERB

/ daɪˈæzəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / daɪˈazətaɪz / (BrE)
to convert an amine into a diazo compound, typically by treatment with nitrous acid
convert an amine to a diazo compound
medical

Examples

In the experiment, we diazotized the aromatic amine to prepare an important intermediate.
The chemist diazotizes the sample as part of the testing protocol.
Once you diazotize the compound, you can proceed with the coupling reaction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diazotization - the process of converting an amine into a diazo compound

Etymology

From diazo- (referring to the diazo group) + -ize (forming verbs).
