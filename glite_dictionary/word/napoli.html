Napoli

1. PROPN

/ naˈpoʊli / (AmE), / naˈpɒli / (BrE)
The Italian name for Naples, a large port city and tourist center in southwestern Italy; the capital of the Campania region.
Italian name for Naples, Italy

Examples

I visited Napoli last summer and was amazed by its vibrant street life.
Many famous pizzas originated from Napoli, which is often called the birthplace of pizza.
The historic center of Napoli is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Naples - the English name for Napoli, city in southern Italy

Etymology

From Italian Napoli, from Latin Neapolis, meaning 'new city'.
