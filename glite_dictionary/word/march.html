MArch

1. NOUN

/ ɛm ɑrtʃ / (AmE), / ɛm ɑːtʃ / (BrE)
a postgraduate degree awarded for advanced study in architecture
degree in advanced architecture studies

Examples

She earned her MArch from a prestigious university before joining the firm.
Applicants with an MArch are eligible for more advanced architectural positions.
Completing an MArch program typically takes two to three years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Master of Architecture - a postgraduate degree in the field of architecture

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'Master of Architecture.'
