to sodomize

1. VERB

/ ˈsɑː.dəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɒd.ə.maɪz / (BrE)
to perform anal intercourse with (someone), especially forcibly or illegally
perform anal intercourse with
vulgar

Examples

The court heard evidence that the victim was forcibly sodomized during the attack.
He was arrested and charged after admitting to having sodomized his cellmate.
Prisoners are sometimes sodomized as a means of exerting control or punishment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bugger - to have anal intercourse with (vulgar slang)

Etymology

From Medieval Latin sodomizare, from Sodom (a city in the Bible destroyed for its depravity).

2. VERB

/ ˈsɑːdəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɒdəˌmaɪz / (BrE)
to perform anal or oral sex with someone or to have sexual intercourse with an animal; historically, to engage in sodomy
to perform anal or oral sex (often legal/medical/judicial)

Examples

The law strictly prohibits anyone from sodomizing animals under any circumstance.
He was prosecuted after reports that he had sodomized a farm animal.
Some medieval legal codes had severe punishments for people who sodomized livestock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bugger - to commit sodomy, especially with an animal

Etymology

From French 'sodomiser', from 'Sodome' (Sodom), referencing the biblical city; originally associated with 'sodomy'.

to subject someone to anal intercourse, especially forcibly or against their will
subject to anal intercourse
vulgar

Examples

The prisoner reported that he feared sodomization if left alone in the cell.
Laws protecting individuals from sodomization have evolved in many countries.
The novel recounts the trauma of being sodomised during childhood.

Synset Examples

He was horrified at the thought of being sodomised.

Related Terms
