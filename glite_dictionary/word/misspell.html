to misspell

1. VERB

/ mɪˈspɛl / (AmE), / mɪsˈspɛl / (BrE)
to spell (a word) incorrectly
spell a word incorrectly

Examples

If you misspell your name on the form, your application may be delayed.
She often misspells complicated technical terms in her emails.
He apologized for misspelling the company's name in the report.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: spell - to write or name the letters of a word
  • Synonym: to mispronounce - to pronounce a word incorrectly

Etymology

From mis- (wrongly) + spell, influenced by Old English misspellian.
