rollerblader

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊlɚˌbleɪdɚ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊləˌbleɪdə / (BrE)
a person who skates using Rollerblades or similar in-line skates
a person who uses Rollerblades

Examples

The park was filled with rollerbladers enjoying the sunny weather.
As a dedicated rollerblader, she trains every weekend along the river path.
You must wear protective gear if you want to become a skilled rollerblader.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skater - a person who skates on ice or wheels

Etymology

Formed from 'Rollerblade' (a trademarked brand of in-line skates) and the agentive suffix '-er'.
