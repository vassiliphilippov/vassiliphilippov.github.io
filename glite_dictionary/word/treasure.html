treasure

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɛʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒə / (BrE)
a collection of valuable objects such as gold, silver, jewels, or other precious things
collection of valuable or precious things

Examples

The pirates searched the island for hidden treasure.
She considers those old photographs her most prized treasure.
The museum displayed a remarkable treasure of ancient coins.

Synset Examples

the trunk held all her meager treasures

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hoard - a secret store of valuable objects or money

Etymology

From Old French *tresor*, from Latin *thēsaurus* (storehouse, treasure).
