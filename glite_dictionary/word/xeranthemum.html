Xeranthemum

1. NOUN

/ zɪˈrænθəməm / (AmE), / zɛˈranθɪməm / (BrE)
A genus of annual, densely hairy herbs native to the Mediterranean region and southwestern Asia.
genus of annual Mediterranean herbs

Examples

The Xeranthemum thrives in dry, sunny areas with well-drained soil.
Her bouquet included dried Xeranthemum flowers for a lasting display.
Botanists studied the unique adaptations of Xeranthemum to its arid environment.

Etymology

From Greek 'xēros' (dry) and 'anthemon' (flower).
