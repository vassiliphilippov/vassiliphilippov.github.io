sexy

/ ˈsɛk.si / (AmE), / ˈsɛk.si / (BrE)
Sexually attractive; stimulating sexual desire.
sexually attractive

Examples

He finds confidence to be the most sexy quality in a person.
That perfume has a sexy scent that turns heads.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unattractive - not pleasing in appearance or manner
