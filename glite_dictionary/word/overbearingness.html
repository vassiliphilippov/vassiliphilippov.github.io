overbearingness

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊvərˈberɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈbeərɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being domineering, arrogant, or excessively controlling in manner
domineering or arrogant quality

Examples

Her overbearingness made it difficult for her colleagues to work with her.
The overbearingness of the teacher stifled the students' creativity.
He tried to hide his overbearingness during the meeting, but it was still apparent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overbearing - domineering or excessively controlling in manner
  • Antonym: humility - the quality of being humble or modest

Etymology

Formed from 'overbearing' + '-ness' (state or quality suffix).
