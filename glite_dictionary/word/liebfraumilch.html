liebfraumilch

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːfˌfraʊˌmɪlk / (AmE), / ˈliːfˌfraʊˌmɪlç / (BrE)
A sweet, inexpensive white wine from the Rhine region of Germany, especially from Hesse.
sweet German white wine

Examples

She poured herself a glass of liebfraumilch to go with dinner.
The wine list included a variety of German options, including a nice liebfraumilch.
For those who prefer a sweeter wine, liebfraumilch is a popular choice.

Etymology

From German Liebfraumilch, literally ‘milk of the (Blessed) Lady’, named for the Liebfrauenkirche (Church of Our Lady) in Worms.
