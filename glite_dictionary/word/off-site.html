off-site

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔf.saɪt / (AmE), / ˈɒf.saɪt / (BrE)
Happening or located away from the main location or premises.
located away from the main site

Examples

The company held its annual retreat at an off-site location in the countryside.
All hazardous materials must be handled at specialized off-site facilities.
They scheduled an off-site meeting to encourage new ideas outside the usual work environment.

Synset Examples

an off-site waste treatment operation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: on-site - located or occurring at the main site or premises
