possibleness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑː.sə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈpɒs.ə.bəl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being possible; the capability of existing, happening, or being true.
the state of being possible

Examples

Many philosophers have debated the possibleness of free will in a deterministic universe.
The possibleness of life on other planets remains a topic of scientific inquiry.
He questioned the possibleness of achieving peace in the region within such a short time.

Synset Examples

there is a possibility that his sense of smell has been impaired

Related Terms

  • Synonym: possibility - the fact or state of being possible; a thing that may happen

Etymology

From 'possible' + '-ness', ultimately from Latin 'possibilis' (that can be done) + English suffix '-ness'.
