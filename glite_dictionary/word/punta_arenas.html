Punta Arenas

1. PROPN

/ ˌpun.tə ɑˈreɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˌpʊn.tə əˈreɪ.nəs / (BrE)
a city in southern Chile, located on the Strait of Magellan, known as the southernmost city in the world
city in southern Chile, Strait of Magellan

Examples

Many tourists visit Punta Arenas as a gateway to Patagonia and Antarctica.
The climate in Punta Arenas is often windy and cool due to its southern location.
Ships often stop at Punta Arenas when navigating around the tip of South America.

Etymology

Spanish for 'Sandy Point', from 'punta' (point) + 'arenas' (sands).
