acellular

1. ADJ

/ eɪˈsɛljələr / (AmE), / ˌeɪˈsɛljʊlə / (BrE)
not consisting of or divided into cells; lacking cellular structure.
not made up of cells
medical

Examples

The scientist studied an acellular slime mold under the microscope.
Some vaccines are described as acellular because they do not contain whole cells.
The tissue sample was found to be acellular, with no living cells present.

Etymology

From Latin prefix 'a-' (not) + 'cellular' (relating to cells).
