Belgian

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛl.dʒən / (AmE), / ˈbɛl.dʒən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Belgium
native or inhabitant of Belgium

Examples

The Belgian introduced himself with a warm smile.
Several Belgians competed in the international marathon.
As a Belgian, he is fluent in both French and Dutch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Belgium - a country in Western Europe

Etymology

From French 'Belge' + English suffix '-an', ultimately from Latin 'Belgae'.
