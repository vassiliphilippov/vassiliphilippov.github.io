Kashmir

1. PROPN

/ kæʃˈmɪr / (AmE), / kæʃˈmɪə / (BrE)
a region in southwestern Asia, the subject of territorial dispute between India and Pakistan, noted for its mountainous landscape
region in Asia disputed by India and Pakistan

Examples

The beautiful valleys of Kashmir attract tourists from all over the world.
The status of Kashmir remains a major issue between India and Pakistan.
He studied the complex history of Kashmir for his political science thesis.

Etymology

From Persian 'Kashmir', possibly derived from Sanskrit 'Kashmira'.
