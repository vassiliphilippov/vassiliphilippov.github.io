double-u

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌbəl ju / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl juː / (BrE)
the name of the letter W, the 23rd letter of the English alphabet
letter W of the alphabet

Examples

The word 'wonder' begins with a double-u.
She struggled to remember whether double-u comes before or after 'V' in the alphabet.
In cursive, writing a proper double-u can be tricky for beginners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: W - the letter W; the 23rd letter of the alphabet

Etymology

From the phrase 'double u', describing the letter's original shape as a doubled 'u'.
