sunscreen

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌnˌskrin / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌskriːn / (BrE)
A cream or lotion applied to the skin to protect it from the harmful effects of the sun's ultraviolet rays.
cream or lotion protecting skin from the sun

Examples

Don't forget to apply sunscreen before going to the beach.
She packed extra sunscreen for their hiking trip in the mountains.
Using sunscreen daily can help prevent premature skin aging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunblock - a lotion or cream that physically blocks sunlight from reaching the skin

Etymology

Blend of 'sun' and 'screen'.
