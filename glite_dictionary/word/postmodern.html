postmodern

1. ADJ

/ poʊstˈmoʊdərn / (AmE), / pəʊstˈməʊdən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of postmodernism, especially in art, literature, or architecture
relating to postmodernism

Examples

The museum features several postmodern paintings from the late twentieth century.
His writing style is often described as postmodern, blending reality and fiction in unusual ways.
The new city hall is a striking example of postmodern architecture.

Synset Examples

postmodernist architecture

Related Terms

  • Antonym: modern - relating to recent times or the present

Etymology

From 'post-' (after) + 'modern', originally referring to movements after modernism in art and culture.
