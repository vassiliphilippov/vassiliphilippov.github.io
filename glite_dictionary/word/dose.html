dose

1. NOUN

/ doʊs / (AmE), / dəʊs / (BrE)
an amount of a medicine, drug, or other substance that is taken at one time
amount of medicine or substance taken at once

Examples

The doctor prescribed a dose of antibiotics to be taken twice a day.
Be sure not to exceed the recommended dose on the label.
He received a high dose of radiation during the treatment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dosage - the administration of medicine in prescribed amounts

Etymology

From Greek 'dosis' (a giving), via Latin 'dosis'.
