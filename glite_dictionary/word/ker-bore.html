ker-bore

1. ADJ

/ kɚˈbɔɹ / (AmE), / kəˈbɔː / (BrE)
(informal, slang) To be extremely bored or uninterested, often to the point of exasperation or frustration.
extremely bored (slang/informal)
informal slang

Examples

I'm absolutely ker-bored after waiting in this queue for hours.
During the three-hour meeting, he found himself getting more and more ker-bored.
If you feel ker-bored in class, try taking better notes or asking questions.

Synset Examples

She was ker-bored during the long lecture.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: bored - feeling weary and impatient due to lack of interest
  • Synonym: bored stiff - extremely bored, synonyms in idiom form
  • Antonym: engaged - actively interested or involved

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'ker-' (a playful augmentative, as in 'kerfuffle') to 'bore'.
