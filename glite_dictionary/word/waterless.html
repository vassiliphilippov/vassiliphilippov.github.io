waterless

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɔt̬ɚləs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtələs / (BrE)
lacking water; dry or arid.
lacking water; dry

Examples

After hiking for hours across the waterless desert, they were desperate for a drink.
The village struggled through several waterless months during the worst drought in years.
Scientists study how plants survive in waterless environments like rocky plateaus.

Synset Examples

an arid climate
a waterless well
miles of waterless country to cross

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arid - extremely dry; having little or no rain
  • Synonym: dry - free from moisture; not wet or damp
  • Antonym: humid - marked by a relatively high level of moisture in the air
  • Antonym: wet - covered or saturated with water or another liquid
