Panthera leo

1. NOUN

/ pænˌθɪrə ˈliːoʊ / (AmE), / pænˌθɪərə ˈliːəʊ / (BrE)
the scientific name for the lion, a large carnivorous feline native to Africa and India with a tawny coat and, in males, a prominent mane
scientific name for the lion

Examples

The Panthera leo is one of the most iconic species of Africa's savannas.
Many conservationists are working to protect Panthera leo from habitat loss and hunting.
Male Panthera leo are distinguished by their majestic manes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lion - a large, muscular, wild cat found in Africa and India

Etymology

From Latin: 'Panthera' meaning panther, and 'leo' meaning lion.
