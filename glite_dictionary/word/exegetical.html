exegetical

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛksɪˈdʒɛtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɛksɪˈdʒɛtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the interpretation or explanation of a text, especially scriptural or literary works.
relating to interpretation of texts

Examples

The scholar published an exegetical analysis of the biblical parable.
Her exegetical approach clarified several obscure passages in the poem.
The article provides an exegetical summary of early church interpretations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interpretative - relating to interpretation or explanation
  • Synonym: explanatory - serving to explain something

Etymology

From Greek exēgētikos (interpretive), from exēgeisthai (to interpret, to explain).
