honeybee

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌniˌbi / (AmE), / ˈhʌniˌbiː / (BrE)
a social bee known for producing honey, often kept by humans for honey production
a bee that produces honey

Examples

The honeybee is essential for pollinating many crops around the world.
A swarm of honeybees settled in the old oak tree near our house.
Beekeepers take care of honeybees to harvest their honey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bee - an insect of the family Apidae that collects nectar and pollen
