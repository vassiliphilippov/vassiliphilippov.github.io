Federico García Lorca

1. PROPN

/ fəˌdeɪrikoʊ ɡɑrˈsiːə ˈlɔrkə / (AmE), / fəˌderiːkoʊ ɡɑːˈsiːə ˈlɔːkə / (BrE)
(1898–1936) Spanish poet and playwright known for his influential works and tragic death during the Spanish Civil War.
Spanish poet and dramatist (1898–1936)
