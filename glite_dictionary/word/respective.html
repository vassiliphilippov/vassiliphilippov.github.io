respective

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈspɛktɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈspɛktɪv / (BrE)
Relating or belonging to each one of the people or things previously mentioned, considered individually.
relating to each individually mentioned item

Examples

Each speaker answered questions about their respective areas of expertise.
Please return to your respective seats so we can continue the meeting.
Their opinions differed according to their respective backgrounds and experiences.

Synset Examples

the respective club members
specialists in their several fields
the various reports all agreed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: individual - single; relating to one person or thing rather than others
  • Antonym: collective - done by or characteristic of individuals acting together

Etymology

From Latin 'respectivus' (having regard to) via Late Middle English.
