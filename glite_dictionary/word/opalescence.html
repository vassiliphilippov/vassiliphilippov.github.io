opalescence

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊpəˈlɛsəns / (AmE), / ˌəʊpəˈlɛsəns / (BrE)
a milky or pearly iridescence that shows a play of colors, especially like that of an opal
milky iridescence; opal-like play of colors

Examples

The opalescence of the pearl gave it a mysterious, alluring glow.
She admired the opalescence of the morning clouds as the sun began to rise.
The artist tried to capture the opalescence seen on the surface of the lake at dusk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iridescence - a display of lustrous, rainbow-like colors

Etymology

From French 'opalescence', based on 'opal'.
