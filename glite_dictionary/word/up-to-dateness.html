up-to-dateness

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌp.tuːˈdeɪt.nəs / (AmE), / ˌʌp.təˈdeɪt.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being current or relevant to the present time
quality of being current

Examples

The up-to-dateness of the information makes the report highly valuable.
They pride themselves on the up-to-dateness of their technology and services.
You should check the up-to-dateness of those statistics before using them.

Synset Examples

the currency of a slang term

Related Terms

  • Synonym: currency - the state of being generally accepted or in use

Etymology

Formed from the phrase 'up to date' with the noun suffix '-ness.'
