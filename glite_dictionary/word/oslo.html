Oslo

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑzloʊ / (AmE), / ˈɒzləʊ / (BrE)
the capital city and largest urban area of Norway, situated at the head of the Oslofjord on the country's southern coast
capital city of Norway

Examples

She traveled to Oslo for an international conference last summer.
The Nobel Peace Prize ceremony is held annually in Oslo.
Many visitors are drawn to Oslo by its vibrant arts scene and beautiful fjord location.

Etymology

From Old Norse 'Ásló', possibly meaning 'the meadow beneath the ridge'.
