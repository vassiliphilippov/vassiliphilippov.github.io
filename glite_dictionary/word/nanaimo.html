Nanaimo

1. PROPN

/ nəˈnaɪmoʊ / (AmE), / nəˈnaɪməʊ / (BrE)
a city in southwestern British Columbia on Vancouver Island, west of Vancouver
city in British Columbia, Canada

Examples

We spent our summer vacation exploring Nanaimo and its beautiful harbor.
The ferry to Vancouver departs from Nanaimo several times a day.
Nanaimo is known for its scenic waterfront and vibrant arts community.

Etymology

From the name of the local Snuneymuxw First Nation, anglicized as 'Nanaimo'.
