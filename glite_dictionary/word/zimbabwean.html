Zimbabwean

1. ADJ

/ zɪmˈbɑːwiən / (AmE), / zɪmˈbɑːwiən / (BrE)
Relating to Zimbabwe or its people.
of or relating to Zimbabwe or its people

Examples

The Zimbabwean economy has faced many challenges in recent years.
She is proud of her Zimbabwean heritage and culture.
We enjoyed tasting traditional Zimbabwean dishes at the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Zimbabwean - A person from Zimbabwe or of Zimbabwean descent (noun use)

Etymology

From Zimbabwe, the country in southern Africa, plus the adjectival suffix -an.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03143418-a
Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ zɪmˈbɑːwiən / (AmE), / zɪmˈbɑːwiən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Zimbabwe
person from Zimbabwe

Examples

As a proud Zimbabwean, she celebrates her country's independence every year.
Many Zimbabweans speak Shona, Ndebele, or English as their native language.
The Zimbabwean community in London organizes cultural festivals each summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Zambian - person from Zambia (neighboring country)

Etymology

From Zimbabwe, the name of the country in southern Africa, plus the English suffix -an forming demonyms.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09771226-n
Synset POS: n
