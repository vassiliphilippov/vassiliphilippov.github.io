ankylotic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæŋ.kɪˈlɑː.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌæŋ.kɪˈlɒ.tɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of ankylosis, an abnormal stiffness or fusion of joints.
relating to joint stiffness (ankylosis)
medical

Examples

The patient exhibited ankylotic changes in her finger joints.
Chronic inflammation can lead to ankylotic stiffening of the spine.
The X-ray revealed an ankylotic fusion in the hip joint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ankylosis - abnormal stiffening and immobility of a joint

Etymology

From Greek ankylos (bent, crooked) + -tic (forming adjectives)
