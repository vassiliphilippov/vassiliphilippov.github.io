hereabouts

1. ADV

/ ˌhɪrəˈbaʊts / (AmE), / ˌhɪərəˈbaʊts / (BrE)
In this general area; near this place.
in or near this place

Examples

We haven't seen any deer hereabouts for several weeks.
Is there a good place to eat hereabouts?
Few large towns exist hereabouts; it's mostly countryside.

Synset Examples

the people are friendly hereabouts

Etymology

From 'here' + 'abouts', modeled after similar formations like 'thereabouts'.
