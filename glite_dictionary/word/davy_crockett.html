Davy Crockett

1. PROPN

/ ˈdeɪvi ˈkrɑkɪt / (AmE), / ˈdeɪvi ˈkrɒkɪt / (BrE)
(1786–1836) An American frontiersman, soldier, and politician who became a folk hero and died at the siege of the Alamo.
American frontiersman and folk hero

Examples

Many stories have been told about Davy Crockett and his adventures on the American frontier.
The legend of Davy Crockett continues to inspire books and movies to this day.
At the history museum, there was an exhibit dedicated to Davy Crockett and the Alamo.
