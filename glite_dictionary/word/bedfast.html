bedfast

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛdˌfæst / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌfɑːst / (BrE)
Confined to bed because of illness or infirmity.
confined to bed (by illness)
archaic medical

Examples

The patient remained bedfast for several weeks following her surgery.
His bedfast condition made regular physical therapy a necessity.
Grandfather was bedfast in his final months, requiring constant care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedridden - unable to get out of bed due to illness or weakness

Etymology

From 'bed' + archaic sense of 'fast' meaning fixed or stuck; literally 'stuck to bed'.
