Citellus parryi

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈtɛləs ˈpæri.aɪ / (AmE), / sɪˈtɛləs ˈparɪ.aɪ / (BrE)
a large ground squirrel native to the far northern regions of North America, also known as the Arctic ground squirrel
large ground squirrel of northern North America

The Citellus parryi is well adapted to the harsh climate of Alaska and northern Canada.
