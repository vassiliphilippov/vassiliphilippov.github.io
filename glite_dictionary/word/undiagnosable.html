undiagnosable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌdaɪəɡˈnoʊzəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌndaɪəɡˈnəʊzəbl / (BrE)
impossible to diagnose; not able to be identified as a particular disease or problem
not able to be diagnosed

Examples

After multiple tests, her condition remained undiagnosable by the medical team.
Some rare disorders are considered undiagnosable due to a lack of clear symptoms.
The source of the malfunction was undiagnosable, even after hours of analysis.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: diagnosable - able to be identified as a specific disease or problem

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' to 'diagnosable', meaning 'not diagnosable'.
