spic

1. NOUN

/ spɪk / (AmE), / spɪk / (BrE)
An extremely offensive and derogatory term for a person of Latin American or Spanish-speaking descent.
offensive term for a Hispanic/Latino person
offensive

Examples

The movie was criticized for its use of the word spic in several scenes.
He was deeply offended when someone called him a spic at school.
It is never acceptable to refer to anyone as a spic.

Etymology

Probably from a derogatory imitation of accented English "No spik English" (i.e., "speak English").
