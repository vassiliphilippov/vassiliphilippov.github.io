Rn

1. NOUN

/ ɑːn / (AmE), / ɑːn / (BrE)
The chemical symbol for radon, a radioactive, colorless, and odorless gaseous element that is the heaviest of the noble gases and poses health risks due to its radioactivity.
chemical symbol for radon (element)

Examples

Rn is measured in homes to assess the risk of radon exposure.
Scientists use Rn detectors when studying radioactive gases in the environment.
The element symbol Rn stands for radon on the periodic table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radon - a radioactive gaseous element, known as Rn

Etymology

Chemical symbol from the element name 'radon'.
