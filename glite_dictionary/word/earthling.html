earthling

1. NOUN

/ ˈɝθ.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɜːθ.lɪŋ / (BrE)
a person or being that lives on the planet Earth, often used in contrast to extraterrestrial beings
an inhabitant of Earth

Examples

In many science fiction stories, aliens refer to humans as earthlings.
The astronauts gazed back at the distant planet, feeling more like earthlings than ever before.
To Martians, all earthlings must seem very similar.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: alien - a being from another planet or world

Etymology

Formed from 'earth' + 'ling' (a suffix meaning 'person associated with'), used since the early 19th century.
