Benjamin Spock

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɛnʤəmɪn spɑk / (AmE), / ˈbɛnjəmɪn spɒk / (BrE)
(1903–1998) United States pediatrician and author whose influential books on child care shaped parenting practices worldwide.
American pediatrician and influential childcare author
