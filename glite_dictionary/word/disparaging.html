disparaging

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈspɛrɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈspærɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
expressing a low opinion or lack of respect for someone or something
expressing disapproval; showing low opinion

Examples

She made several disparaging remarks about her colleague's abilities.
The article was full of disparaging references to the new policy.
He responded to criticism with disparaging laughter, dismissing the complaints as unimportant.

Synset Examples

derogatory comments
disparaging remarks about the new house

Related Terms

  • Synonym: derogatory - showing a critical or disrespectful attitude

Etymology

From Middle French *desparagier* (to degrade), from Late Latin *disparagare* (to degrade in rank).
