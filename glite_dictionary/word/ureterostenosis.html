ureterostenosis

1. NOUN

/ jʊˌriːtərəstoʊˈnoʊsɪs / (AmE), / jʊˌriːtərəstəˈnəʊsɪs / (BrE)
abnormal narrowing of the ureter
narrowing of the ureter
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with ureterostenosis after complaining of severe abdominal pain and difficulty urinating.
Doctors treated the ureterostenosis by inserting a stent to open up the blocked section.
If left untreated, ureterostenosis can lead to kidney damage due to impaired urine flow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stenosis - abnormal narrowing of a bodily canal or passage

Etymology

From uretero- (relating to the ureter) + stenosis (from Greek stenos, narrow).
