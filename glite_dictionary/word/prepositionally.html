prepositionally

1. ADV

/ ˌprɛpəˈzɪʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌprɛpəˈzɪʃənəli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or as a preposition.
in a manner relating to prepositions

Examples

In this sentence, 'before' is used prepositionally rather than as an adverb.
Some words can function both prepositionally and adverbially, depending on the context.
The term was analyzed prepositionally to clarify its grammatical usage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prepositional - relating to or functioning as a preposition

Etymology

From 'prepositional' + '-ly', where 'prepositional' is from 'preposition' (from Latin 'praepositio', setting before).
