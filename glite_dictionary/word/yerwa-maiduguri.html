Yerwa-Maiduguri

1. PROPN

/ ˌjɛrwə maɪˈduːɡʊri / (AmE), / ˌjɛːwə maɪˈduːɡʊri / (BrE)
a city in northeastern Nigeria, serving as an agricultural trading center and the capital of Borno State.
city in northeastern Nigeria

Examples

The agricultural market in Yerwa-Maiduguri attracts traders from across the region.
During the rainy season, roads into Yerwa-Maiduguri can become difficult to traverse.
She grew up in Yerwa-Maiduguri before moving to the capital for university.

Etymology

Yerwa is the original name, while Maiduguri derives from the local Kanuri language; now often combined to refer to the city.
