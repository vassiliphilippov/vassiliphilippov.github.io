2. NOUN

/ bɛˈniːniz / (AmE), / bɛˈniːniːz / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Benin, or a person of Beninese descent.
person from Benin

Examples

The Beninese welcomed visitors with traditional music and dance.
She interviewed several Beninese about their cultural heritage for her documentary.
Many Beninese have moved to neighboring countries for educational opportunities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Benin - a country in West Africa

Etymology

From Benin, the name of the country, plus English suffix -ese (denoting nationality or origin).
