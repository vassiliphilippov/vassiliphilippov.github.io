shaggily

1. ADV

/ ˈʃæɡɪli / (AmE), / ˈʃæɡɪli / (BrE)
In a way that is shaggy; having an untidy, rough, or messy appearance.
in a shaggy manner

Examples

The dog looked shaggily adorable after it rolled in the mud.
His shaggily cut hair stuck out under his hat.
The sheep wandered shaggily across the hillside, their wool thick and long.

Synset Examples

shaggily unkempt mane

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shaggy - having a rough, messy, or untidy appearance, especially of hair or fur

Etymology

From 'shaggy' + '-ly', where 'shaggy' is from Middle English 'shagge', meaning 'rough, unkempt'.
