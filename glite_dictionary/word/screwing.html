screwing

1. NOUN

/ ˈskruɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskruːɪŋ / (BrE)
Slang term for sexual intercourse.
slang; sexual intercourse
slang vulgar

Examples

They were caught screwing in the office after hours.
He bragged about screwing his boss's daughter at the party.
She said she was tired of just screwing and wanted a real relationship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sex - sexual intercourse (neutral/standard term)
  • Synonym: making love - euphemism for sexual intercourse

Etymology

Derived from the verb 'screw' meaning to have sexual intercourse, likely from the twisting motion of a screw.
