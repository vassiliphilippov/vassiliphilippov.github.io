Islamic

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈslɑːmɪk / (AmE), / ɪˈslɑːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or supporting Islam, its people, or its culture
relating to Islam

Examples

The museum has an impressive collection of Islamic manuscripts and pottery.
Many countries in the Middle East are governed by Islamic law.
She studied Islamic history at university.

Synset Examples

Islamic art

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Muslim - relating to a follower of Islam or their practices

Etymology

From Arabic 'islāmī', relating to Islam.
