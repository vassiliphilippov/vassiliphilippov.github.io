slangily

1. ADV

/ ˈslæŋɪli / (AmE), / ˈslæŋɪli / (BrE)
in a manner characterized by the use of slang; using informal or colloquial language
in a slangy manner; with slang

Examples

The actor spoke slangily to fit the role of a streetwise teenager.
She answered slangily, confusing her elderly relatives with her choice of words.
His emails are often written slangily, full of abbreviations and informal expressions.

Synset Examples

he expresses himself slangily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slangy - resembling or characterized by slang

Etymology

Formed from 'slangy' + '-ly', following the standard English pattern for adverbs.
