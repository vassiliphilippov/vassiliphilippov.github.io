mizzen-topmast

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪzən ˌtɑpmæst / (AmE), / ˈmɪzən ˌtɒpmɑːst / (BrE)
the topmast that is situated directly above the mizzenmast on a sailing ship
topmast above the mizzenmast

Examples

The crew had to repair the mizzen-topmast after the storm damaged several spars.
From the deck, the mizzen-topmast soared high above the remaining sails.
They hoisted a new flag from the mizzen-topmast during the ceremony.

Synset Examples

The main topmast carries the upper end of the main-topmast-staysail; a mizzen-topmast may carry the equivalent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: main-topmast - the topmast above the mainmast

Etymology

From 'mizzen' (the aft-most mast on a ship, from Italian 'mezzana' meaning 'middle') + 'topmast' (the second section of a mast above the deck).
