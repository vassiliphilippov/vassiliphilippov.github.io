aimlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈeɪmlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈeɪmləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no purpose, direction, or plan.
without purpose or direction

Examples

She wandered aimlessly through the empty streets, unsure of where to go.
The leaves drifted aimlessly in the gentle afternoon breeze.
He scrolled aimlessly through his phone, looking for something to catch his attention.

Synset Examples

he wandered around aimlessly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: purposely - intentionally, with purpose

Etymology

From 'aimless' + '-ly', where 'aimless' is from 'aim' (to direct a course) and the suffix '-less' (without).
