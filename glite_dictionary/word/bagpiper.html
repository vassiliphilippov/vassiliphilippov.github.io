bagpiper

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæɡˌpaɪpər / (AmE), / ˈbæɡˌpaɪpə / (BrE)
a person who plays the bagpipe, a traditional musical instrument consisting of a bag and pipes
person who plays the bagpipe

Examples

The bagpiper marched at the front of the parade, filling the street with music.
They hired a talented bagpiper for the wedding ceremony.
Tourists gathered to listen to a bagpiper perform traditional Scottish tunes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bagpipe - a traditional musical instrument consisting of a bag, pipes, and reeds
