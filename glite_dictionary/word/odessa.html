Odessa

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈdɛsə / (AmE), / əʊˈdɛsə / (BrE)
A port city in south-central Ukraine, located on the northwestern shore of the Black Sea.
port city in Ukraine, Black Sea

Examples

Many tourists visit Odessa for its beautiful beaches and vibrant culture.
The port of Odessa is vital for Ukraine's maritime trade.
He was born in Odessa and moved to the capital as a young man.

Etymology

From Russian Одесса (Odesa), possibly after the ancient Greek city Odessos.

american

Many workers moved to Odessa during the oil boom.

Odessa has a rich history linked to the development of the Texas oil industry.
