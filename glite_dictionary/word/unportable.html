unportable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpɔːrtəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɔːtəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be easily moved or carried; not portable.
not easily moved or carried

Examples

The device was too bulky and unportable for regular travel.
Unlike its predecessor, the new model is completely unportable without special equipment.
I find desktop computers unportable compared to laptops.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: portable - able to be easily carried or moved

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'portable' (able to be carried).
