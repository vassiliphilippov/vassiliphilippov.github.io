towhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈtəʊˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person, especially a child, with very light blond hair
person with very light blond hair

Examples

The little towhead played happily in the sun.
She smiled down at the towhead standing by her side.
Growing up as a towhead made him easy to spot in school photos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blond - having fair or pale yellow hair

Etymology

From 'tow' (coarse flax or hemp fibers, pale in color) + 'head'.
