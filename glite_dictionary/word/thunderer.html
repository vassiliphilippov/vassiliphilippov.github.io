thunderer

1. NOUN

/ ˈθʌndərər / (AmE), / ˈθʌndərə / (BrE)
a device or noisemaker that produces a sound resembling thunder
noisemaker imitating thunder

Examples

The special effects team used a thunderer to recreate the sound of the approaching storm.
During the school play, the students hid a thunderer backstage for dramatic effect.
You can buy a thunderer at most novelty shops for parties and events.

Etymology

From 'thunder' + '-er', denoting something that produces thunder-like sounds.
