defencelessly

1. ADV

/ dɪˈfɛnsləsli / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛnsləsli / (BrE)
In a manner lacking protection or means of defense; without being able to resist danger or harm.
without protection or defense

Examples

She defencelessly accepted the criticism, unable to argue back.
The small animal lay defencelessly before its predator.
They found themselves defencelessly exposed to the harsh weather.

Synset Examples

the child was standing in the middle of the crossfire, defenselessly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defenselessly - In a manner lacking defense (US spelling)

Etymology

From 'defenceless' + '-ly', modelled as the adverbial form.
