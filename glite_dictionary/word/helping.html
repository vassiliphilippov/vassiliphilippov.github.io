helping

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɛlpɪŋ / (BrE)
an amount of food or drink served to one person at a meal
a portion of food or drink

Examples

She asked for a second helping of dessert because it was so delicious.
Could you pass me another helping of potatoes, please?
He politely declined a third helping even though he was still a bit hungry.

Synset Examples

the helpings were all small
his portion was larger than hers
there's enough for two servings each

Related Terms

  • Synonym: portion - an amount of food served to one person
  • Synonym: serving - an amount of food given to one person
