arsenal

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːr.sə.nəl / (AmE), / ˈɑː.sən.əl / (BrE)
A place where weapons and military equipment are made or stored.
place where weapons are made or stored

Examples

The old arsenal was converted into a museum showcasing military history.
Security at the arsenal was tightened following the attempted break-in.
New weapons were produced at the city’s main arsenal during the war.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armory - a place where arms are stored

Etymology

From Italian 'arsenale', from Arabic 'dār al-ṣināʿa' (house of manufacture).

2. NOUN

/ ˈɑːr.sən.əl / (AmE), / ˈɑː.sən.əl / (BrE)
a military establishment or building where weapons, ammunition, and military equipment are stored and where people are trained in the use of arms
military storehouse for arms and equipment

Examples

The city’s old arsenal now serves as a military history museum.
Soldiers were instructed in weapon maintenance at the arsenal.
A large quantity of ammunition was discovered hidden in the rebel arsenal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armory - a place where weapons are kept and maintained

Etymology

From Italian *arsenale*, from Arabic *dār al-ṣināʿa* (house of manufacture).

3. NOUN

/ ˈɑːr.sən.əl / (AmE), / ˈɑː.sən.əl / (BrE)
the complete collection of weapons and military equipment possessed by a country or group
collection of weapons and equipment

Examples

The country has a vast arsenal of nuclear weapons and advanced missiles.
New technologies are being added to the military's arsenal every year.
Negotiators discussed measures to reduce the overall size of each nation’s arsenal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armory - a place where weapons are kept or a supply of weapons; synonymous with arsenal in this sense

Etymology

From Italian *arsenale*, from Arabic *dār al-ṣināʕa* (house of manufacture).
