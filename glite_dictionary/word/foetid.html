foetid

1. ADJ

/ ˈfiːtɪd / (AmE), / ˈfiːtɪd / (BrE)
having an extremely unpleasant or offensive smell; foul-smelling.
having a foul odor

Examples

The air in the abandoned house was thick with a foetid stench.
A foetid pool of stagnant water stood behind the barn.
His boots were covered with foetid mud from the swamp.

Synset Examples

a foul odor
the kitchen smelled really funky

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fetid - having an extremely unpleasant or offensive smell

Etymology

From Latin 'foetidus' (having a bad smell, stinking).
