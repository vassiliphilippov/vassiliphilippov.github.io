birdbrain

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːdˌbreɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdˌbreɪn / (BrE)
an unintelligent or silly person; someone who is incapable of serious thought
a foolish or unintelligent person
informal

Examples

I can't believe you forgot your passport again, you birdbrain!
Even though she was called a birdbrain, she eventually solved the puzzle before everyone else.
They dismissed his wild suggestions as the ideas of a birdbrain, but he proved them wrong.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently
  • Antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intellectual ability

Etymology

Formed from 'bird' and 'brain', suggesting birds have small or simple brains.
