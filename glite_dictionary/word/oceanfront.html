oceanfront

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊʃənˌfrʌnt / (AmE), / ˈəʊʃənˌfrʌnt / (BrE)
a stretch of land or property directly adjoining or facing the ocean
land or property bordering the ocean

Examples

They bought a beautiful oceanfront home for their summer vacations.
The oceanfront is popular for its scenic views and walking trails.
Prices for oceanfront properties have risen sharply in the past decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seafront - land or property bordering a sea
