puke

1. NOUN

/ pjuːk / (AmE), / pjuːk / (BrE)
Vomit; the matter that is brought up from the stomach and ejected from the mouth.
matter ejected in vomiting
informal

Examples

There was puke all over the sidewalk after the carnival.
The smell of puke in the bathroom was overwhelming.
He had to clean up his friend's puke from the back seat of the car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vomit - matter ejected in vomiting (formal term)

Etymology

Possibly of imitative origin or an alteration of obsolete 'spewke', related to 'spew'.
