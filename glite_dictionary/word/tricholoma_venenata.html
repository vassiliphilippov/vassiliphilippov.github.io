Tricholoma venenata

1. NOUN

/ trɪˌkəloʊmə vəˈnɛnətə / (AmE), / trɪˌkɒləʊmə vɪˈnɛnətə / (BrE)
A species of poisonous white mushroom in the genus Tricholoma.
a poisonous white mushroom species

Examples

The hiker mistakenly collected Tricholoma venenata, not realizing it was dangerously poisonous.
Experts warn that Tricholoma venenata can be easily confused with edible mushrooms in the wild.
Microscopic examination helped to confirm the identification of Tricholoma venenata.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tricholoma - a genus of mushrooms

Etymology

From Latin: genus name 'Tricholoma', species name 'venenata' meaning 'poisoned' or 'poisonous'.
