hornless

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɔrn.ləs / (AmE), / ˈhɔːn.ləs / (BrE)
(of an animal) having no horns, either naturally or as a result of removal.
lacking horns (of an animal)

Examples

The farmer preferred hornless cattle for safety reasons.
Unlike most goats, this breed is completely hornless.
A hornless ram was grazing peacefully among the others.

Synset Examples

hornless cattle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polled - (of livestock) naturally without horns
  • Antonym: horned - having horns

Etymology

From English 'horn' + '-less' (meaning without).
