fathead

1. NOUN

/ ˈfætˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈfætˌhɛd / (BrE)
an unintelligent or foolish person; a stupid or incompetent man
a stupid or foolish person
informal

Examples

Don’t be such a fathead—you’ve completely misunderstood the problem.
He called his brother a fathead for forgetting their mother’s birthday again.
Sometimes, I act like a real fathead when I’m tired.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - a person of extraordinary intelligence

Etymology

From 'fat' + 'head', implying a head full of fat instead of brains. Recorded from the mid-19th century.
