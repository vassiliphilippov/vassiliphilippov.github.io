Biedermeier

1. ADJ

/ ˈbiːdərˌmaɪər / (AmE), / ˈbiːdəˌmaɪə / (BrE)
relating to a style of furniture and decorative arts that originated in Germany in the early 19th century, characterized by simplicity, elegance, and functionality
German 19th-century furniture style

Examples

The museum’s collection includes several beautiful Biedermeier chairs from the early nineteenth century.
She decorated her living room in a Biedermeier style to give it a classic and elegant look.
The auction featured rare Biedermeier furniture highly prized by collectors of European antiques.

Etymology

German, from the fictitious name 'Gottlieb Biedermeier,' symbolizing the conventional middle-class society of the era.
