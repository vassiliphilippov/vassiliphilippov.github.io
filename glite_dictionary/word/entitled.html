entitled

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈtaɪt̬əld / (AmE), / ɪnˈtaɪtld / (BrE)
having a right or claim to something; qualified to receive or do something by law or custom
having a right or claim to something

Examples

All citizens are entitled to freedom of expression.
She is entitled to receive compensation under the agreement.
After five years of service, employees are entitled to extra vacation days.

Synset Examples

we are all entitled to equal protection under the law

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eligible - having the right to do or obtain something; qualified

Etymology

From Old French *entituler*, from Latin *titulus* (title).
