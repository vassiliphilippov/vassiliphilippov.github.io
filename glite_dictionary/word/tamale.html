Tamale

1. PROPN

/ təˈmɑːleɪ / (AmE), / təˈmɑːleɪ / (BrE)
A city in northern Ghana, known as the capital of the Northern Region.
city in northern Ghana

Examples

She traveled to Tamale to attend a cultural festival last summer.
The climate in Tamale is typically hot and dry for much of the year.
Many NGOs have set up projects in Tamale to support education and health care.
