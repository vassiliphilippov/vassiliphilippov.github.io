meaningfully

1. ADV

/ ˈmiːnɪŋfəli / (AmE), / ˈmiːnɪŋfəli / (BrE)
In a way that expresses meaning or purpose; so as to convey significance or intention.
in a meaningful manner

Examples

She smiled meaningfully at her colleague during the meeting.
He paused meaningfully before answering the difficult question.
The teacher looked at the students meaningfully when discussing honesty.

Synset Examples

He glanced at her meaningfully

Related Terms

  • Antonym: meaningless - Having no meaning or significance
