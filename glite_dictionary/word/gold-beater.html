gold-beater

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldˌbiːtər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌbiːtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to hammer gold into thin sheets called gold leaf
artisan who makes gold leaf

Examples

The gold-beater skillfully hammered the metal until it became thin enough for gilding.
During the tour, we watched a gold-beater demonstrate his traditional techniques.
Few people today know how to become a master gold-beater.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldsmith - a person who makes or repairs things made of gold
