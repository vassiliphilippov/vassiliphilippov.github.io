ataraxic

1. ADJ

/ ˌætəˈræksɪk / (AmE), / ˌætəˈræksɪk / (BrE)
(of a substance or effect) tending to soothe or tranquilize; calming or reducing anxiety.
calming or tranquilizing

Examples

The doctor prescribed an ataraxic medication to help ease her anxiety.
After a stressful day, he was grateful for the ataraxic effects of the herbal tea.
Researchers studied the ataraxic properties of the new drug during clinical trials.

Synset Examples

valium has a tranquilizing effect
took a hot drink with sedative properties before going to bed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tranquilizing - having a calming or sedative effect
  • Synonym: sedative - having the property of calming or reducing excitement

Etymology

From Greek 'ataraxia' meaning 'impassiveness, calmness'.
