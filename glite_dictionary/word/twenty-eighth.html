twenty-eighth

1. ADJ

/ ˌtwɛntiˈeɪθ / (AmE), / ˌtwɛntiˈeɪtθ / (BrE)
next after the twenty-seventh in a sequence; ordinal number indicating position 28 in a series
ordinal number: 28th

Examples

Her birthday is on the twenty-eighth of February.
This is the twenty-eighth edition of the magazine.
He finished twenty-eighth in the annual marathon.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-seventh - next before the twenty-eighth in a sequence (27th)
  • Degree Higher: twenty-ninth - next after the twenty-eighth in a sequence (29th)
