to depute

1. VERB

/ dəˈpjut / (AmE), / dɪˈpjuːt / (BrE)
to appoint someone to act as a substitute or representative, especially for official duties
appoint as a substitute or representative

Examples

The manager decided to depute his assistant to oversee the project.
You may depute another officer to attend the meeting in your place.
They have deputed several staff members to organize the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delegate - to entrust a task or responsibility to another

Etymology

From Latin 'deputare' (to assign, allot), via Middle French 'députer'.

WordNet ID: oewn-02399587-v

2. VERB

/ dɪˈpjut / (AmE), / dɪˈpjuːt / (BrE)
to delegate authority, duty, or responsibility to another person, especially to represent oneself or perform a specific task
delegate or assign (a task or role)

Examples

The manager decided to depute several employees to oversee the new project.
It is common practice to depute tasks to trusted colleagues in large organizations.
She was deputed by the committee to lead the negotiations with the partner company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delegate - to entrust a task or responsibility to another person
  • Synonym: to assign - to allocate a job or duty to someone
  • Antonym: revoke - to take back or withdraw authority or responsibility

Etymology

From Latin deputare (to assign, appoint), via Old French deputer.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02396945-v
Synset POS: v

3. VERB

/ dɪˈpjut / (AmE), / dɪˈpjuːt / (BrE)
to appoint or delegate someone to perform a task or exercise power on one’s behalf
delegate authority or responsibility

Examples

The committee decided to depute its secretary to represent them at the conference.
She was deputed to handle the negotiations on behalf of the director.
Rather than attend the event himself, he will depute responsibility to his assistant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delegate - to entrust a task or responsibility to another person

Etymology

From Old French 'deputer', from Latin 'deputare' (to assign, consider).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02400537-v
Synset POS: v
