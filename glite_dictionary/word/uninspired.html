uninspired

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪnˈspaɪɚd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈspaɪəd / (BrE)
lacking originality, creativity, or enthusiasm; not exciting or imaginative
lacking originality or creativity

Examples

The movie was beautifully shot, but the plot felt uninspired and predictable.
Her speech was uninspired, lacking the passion and creativity the audience expected.
Despite the talent involved, the design seemed uninspired and failed to impress the judges.

Synset Examples

a sterile ideology lacking in originality
unimaginative development of a musical theme
uninspired writing

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inspired - showing creativity or enthusiasm
  • Synonym: unimaginative - not creative or original

Etymology

From un- (not) + inspired.

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪnˈspaɪɚd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈspaɪəd / (BrE)
lacking excitement, originality, or enthusiasm; not creative or stimulating.
lacking excitement or originality

Examples

Critics described the movie as visually stunning but ultimately uninspired.
Her speech was well-organized but rather uninspired, failing to move the audience.
The painting appeared technically perfect but somehow uninspired and lacking emotion.

Synset Examples

the production was professional but uninspired

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inspired - filled with creativity or enthusiasm
  • Synonym: dull - lacking interest or excitement

Etymology

From un- (not) + inspired, meaning not filled with inspiration.
