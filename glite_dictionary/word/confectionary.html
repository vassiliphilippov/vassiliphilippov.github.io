confectionary

1. NOUN

/ kənˈfɛkʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / kənˈfɛkʃənəri / (BrE)
A shop where sweets, chocolate, and other confections are sold.
a shop selling sweets and chocolates

Examples

There's a small confectionary near the station that sells handmade chocolates.
As a child, I loved visiting the confectionary every Saturday for a treat.
The oldest confectionary in town is famous for its traditional toffees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweet shop - a shop selling sweets and candies
  • Synonym: confectioner - a person or business that makes or sells sweets

Etymology

From confectioner + -y, modeled after the older 'confectionery' (sweets or sweet shop).
