omnipresence

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑmnɪˈprɛzəns / (AmE), / ˌɒmnɪˈprɛzəns / (BrE)
the state of being present everywhere at the same time; widespread presence
the state of being everywhere at once

Examples

The omnipresence of smartphones has changed how people interact with the world.
In many religions, God is described as possessing omnipresence.
Her omnipresence in the media made her a household name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ubiquity - the fact of appearing everywhere or of being very common

Etymology

From Latin omnis (all) + praesentia (presence).
