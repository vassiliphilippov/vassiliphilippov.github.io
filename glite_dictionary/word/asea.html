asea

1. ADV

/ əˈsiː / (AmE), / əˈsiː / (BrE)
In the direction of the sea; towards the sea or out at sea.
towards or out at sea

Examples

As the storm approached, the fishing boats drifted asea in the growing waves.
He gazed asea, searching for any sign of land on the horizon.
At dawn, the crew steered the ship asea for another long day of travel.

Synset Examples

the sailor looked seaward

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seaward - toward the sea; in the direction of the sea

Etymology

Formed from 'a-' (on, in) + 'sea'.
