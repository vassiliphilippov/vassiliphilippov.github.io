jauntiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɔntinəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːntinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being lively, cheerful, and stylish in appearance or manner
lively or stylish manner or look

Examples

The jauntiness of her hat added a playful touch to her outfit.
He walked with a jauntiness that made everyone smile.
The jauntiness in his dress signaled his confidence and joie de vivre.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sprightliness - the quality of being lively or spirited
  • Antonym: dullness - lack of liveliness or style

Etymology

From jaunty + -ness; 'jaunty' from French gentil (well-bred, elegant).

2. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɔntinəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːntinəs / (BrE)
a lively and carefree manner or appearance
lively, carefree manner

Examples

Her jauntiness cheered up everyone in the office on Monday mornings.
He walked into the room with a jauntiness that was impossible to ignore.
Despite the bad weather, her jauntiness never faded during the trip.

Synset Examples

a delightful breeziness of manner

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breeziness - a cheerful and relaxed quality or manner
  • Synonym: vivacity - the quality of being attractively lively and animated
  • Antonym: dullness - the quality of being without liveliness or excitement

Etymology

From jaunty (lively, carefree) + -ness (noun suffix).
