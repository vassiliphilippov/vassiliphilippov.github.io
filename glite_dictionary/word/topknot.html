topknot

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑpˌnɑt / (AmE), / ˈtɒp.nɒt / (BrE)
A decorative ribbon, bow, or knot worn on top of the head or in the hair as an ornament.
decorative knot or bow in the hair

Examples

She tied a bright red topknot into her hair for the celebration.
The little girl wore a blue ribbon topknot that matched her dress.
Victorian portraits often show women with elaborate topknots as part of their hairstyle.

Etymology

From 'top' + 'knot', literally meaning a knot at the top (of the head or hair).

2. NOUN

/ ˈtɑpˌnɑt / (AmE), / ˈtɒp.nɒt / (BrE)
a knot or tuft of hair or feathers arranged on the top of the head, often as a decorative feature
decorative knot or tuft on the head

Examples

The dancer wore a bright red ribbon around her topknot.
Some birds have a distinctive topknot of feathers on their heads.
He pulled his long hair into a topknot before heading to practice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bun - a hairstyle in which the hair is drawn back and twisted into a round coil at the back or top of the head

Etymology

From 'top' (the uppermost part) and 'knot' (an interlacing, fastening, or loop of hair or material).
