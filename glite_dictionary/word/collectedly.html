collectedly

1. ADV

/ kəˈlɛktɪdli / (AmE), / kəˈlɛktɪdli / (BrE)
In a calm and self-possessed manner; showing control over one's emotions or actions.
in a calm, self-possessed manner

Examples

She collectedly answered all the difficult questions during the interview.
Despite the emergency, he acted collectedly and guided everyone to safety.
The spokesperson spoke collectedly, even though the news was startling.

Synset Examples

he announced the death of his father collectedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calmly - in a peaceful or untroubled way
  • Antonym: nervously - in an anxious or agitated way

Etymology

From 'collected' (calm, composed) + '-ly'.
