three-fourths

1. NOUN

/ θri ˈfɔrθs / (AmE), / θriː ˈfɔːθs / (BrE)
An amount or fraction equal to three out of four equal parts of a whole; seventy-five percent.
three of four equal parts; 75%

Examples

The recipe calls for three-fourths of a cup of sugar.
She finished three-fourths of her homework before dinner.
About three-fourths of the students agreed with the proposal.

Synset Examples

three-fourths of a pound

Related Terms

  • Synonym: three-quarters - three out of four equal parts of a whole
