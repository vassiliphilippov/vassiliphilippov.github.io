metrological

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɛtrəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛtrəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to metrology, the scientific study of measurement.
pertaining to measurement science

Examples

The laboratory was accredited for its metrological competence in calibrating instruments.
A metrological standard ensures accuracy in scientific research and industry.
They discussed the metrological challenges involved in defining the kilogram.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metrology - the scientific study of measurement

Etymology

From metrology + -ical; 'metrology' from French métrologie, from Greek metron (measure) + -logia (study).
