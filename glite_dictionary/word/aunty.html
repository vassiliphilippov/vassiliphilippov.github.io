aunty

1. NOUN

/ ˈænti / (AmE), / ˈɑːnti / (BrE)
An informal term for the sister of one's father or mother, or the wife of one's uncle.
informal term for aunt
informal

Examples

I spent the weekend at my aunty's house in the countryside.
Whenever I feel sad, my aunty always knows how to cheer me up.
At the family gathering, everyone brought a gift for our favorite aunty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aunt - the sister of one's father or mother or the wife of one's uncle

Etymology

Diminutive form of 'aunt', with the '-y' suffix for familiarity or endearment.
