pluckily

1. ADV

/ ˈplʌkɪli / (AmE), / ˈplʌkɪli / (BrE)
In a way that shows courage and determination, especially in facing difficulties.
in a courageous and determined manner

Examples

Despite the odds against her, she pluckily completed the marathon.
He pluckily defended his position against harsh criticism from the audience.
The small dog pluckily stood its ground against the much larger animal.

Synset Examples

he was Brentford's defensive star in pluckily holding out the determined Reading raids for long periods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: courageously - in a brave or courageous manner
  • Synonym: fearlessly - in a manner without fear
  • Antonym: timidly - in a shy or lacking courage manner

Etymology

From 'plucky' (showing courage) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
