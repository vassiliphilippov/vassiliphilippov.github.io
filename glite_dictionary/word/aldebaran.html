Aldebaran

1. PROPN

/ ælˈdɛb.əˌræn / (AmE), / ælˈdɛb.ər.ən / (BrE)
the brightest star in the constellation Taurus, a prominent red giant visible from Earth
brightest star in Taurus constellation

Examples

Aldebaran can be easily spotted in the night sky due to its reddish hue.
Many ancient cultures identified Aldebaran as an important celestial marker.
The constellation Taurus is dominated by the bright presence of Aldebaran.

Etymology

From Arabic al-dabarān (the follower), referring to its position following the Pleiades.
