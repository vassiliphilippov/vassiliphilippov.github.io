conceptional

1. ADJ

/ kənˈsɛpʃənl̩ / (AmE), / kənˈsɛpʃənl / (BrE)
Relating to or of the nature of a concept or notion; theoretical rather than practical.
relating to a concept or notion

Examples

The research focused on the conceptional framework behind the proposed theory.
She raised several conceptional questions during the seminar.
This is a conceptional distinction, not a practical one.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

Etymology

Formed from 'conception' + '-al', following the pattern of forming adjectives indicating relation.
