delimitation

1. NOUN

/ dɪˌlɪməˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a line, border, or other indicator that shows the boundaries or limits of something
marking or setting of a boundary

Examples

The newly agreed delimitation of the two countries' territories was marked with stone posts.
There was a dispute regarding the delimitation of the fishing zone boundaries.
Surveyors worked to establish a clear delimitation between the plots of land.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boundary - a line that marks the limits of an area
  • Synonym: border - the edge or boundary of something

Etymology

From French délimitation, from Latin delimitare (to mark out the limits).
