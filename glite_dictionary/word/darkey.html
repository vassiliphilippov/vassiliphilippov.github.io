darkey

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑːrki / (AmE), / ˈdɑːki / (BrE)
An offensive and outdated term for a Black person.
offensive term for a Black person
offensive archaic

Examples

The novel contains the word darkey, reflecting the language of its time, but it is now considered extremely offensive.
She explained to students why darkey is inappropriate and hurtful in modern language.
Historians discuss the origins and usage of the slur darkey in 19th-century literature.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: black person - neutral term for a person of African descent
  • Synonym: darky - another offensive term for a Black person

Etymology

Diminutive form of 'darky', itself from 'dark' referring to skin color.
