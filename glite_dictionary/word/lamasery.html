lamasery

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑːməsɛri / (AmE), / ˈlɑːməsəri / (BrE)
a monastery for lamas, especially in Tibetan Buddhism
Buddhist monastery for lamas

Examples

The monks lived together in the ancient lamasery high in the mountains.
Tourists visited the remote lamasery to learn about Tibetan Buddhist traditions.
Each lamasery has its own rituals and way of life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monastery - a building where monks live and worship

Etymology

Formed from 'lama' (a Tibetan Buddhist monk or teacher) + '-sery', modeled after 'monastery.'
