Gilbert Stuart

1. PROPN

/ ˈgɪlbɚt ˈstuːɚt / (AmE), / ˈɡɪlbət ˈstjuːət / (BrE)
(1755–1828) An American painter best known for his portraits of George Washington and other prominent figures from the early United States.
American portrait painter (1755–1828)

Examples

Many art historians consider Gilbert Stuart one of America's greatest portraitists.
The famous unfinished portrait of George Washington was painted by Gilbert Stuart.
Gilbert Stuart's works are displayed in galleries across the United States.
