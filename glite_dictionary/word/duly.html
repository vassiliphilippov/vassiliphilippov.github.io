duly

1. ADV

/ ˈduːli / (AmE), / ˈdjuːli / (BrE)
in a proper or expected manner; as is appropriate or suitable for the circumstances
in a proper or appropriate way

Examples

The form was duly signed and submitted before the deadline.
She was duly warned about the risks involved in the project.
After his achievements were announced, he was duly congratulated by all present.

Synset Examples

he was appropriately dressed
If you don't behave properly, you'll have to leave!
I met the junior senator from Illinois and I was duly impressed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: properly - in a correct or suitable way
  • Synonym: appropriately - in a suitable or fitting manner
  • Antonym: improperly - in an unsuitable or incorrect way

Etymology

From Middle English, from Old French 'duelment', from 'deu' (due, owed).
