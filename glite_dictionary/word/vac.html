vac

1. NOUN

/ væk / (AmE), / væk / (BrE)
An informal term for vacation, especially a short break or holiday from work or study.
informal term for vacation or holiday
informal

Examples

We're heading to the beach for our summer vac next week.
I can't wait for vac to start so I can finally relax.
She spent her vac traveling through Europe with friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vacation - an extended period of leisure, especially away from home
  • Synonym: holiday - a period of time spent away from work or school for rest or recreation

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'vacation', also influenced by British English 'vac' for 'holiday'.
