flannelbush

1. NOUN

/ ˈflænəlˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈflænəlˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
any of several evergreen shrubs native to California and northern Mexico, with soft, downy, lobed leaves and showy yellow flowers
evergreen shrub with yellow flowers, native to California

Examples

The flannelbush in her garden bloomed with bright yellow flowers every spring.
Researchers studied how flannelbush adapts to California’s dry, rocky soils.
Hikers admired a hillside covered with vibrant flannelbushes on their trek.
