jeans

1. NOUN

/ dʒinz / (AmE), / dʒiːnz / (BrE)
Trousers made of heavy denim, typically worn as casual or workwear.
denim trousers for casual wear

Examples

He wore his favorite jeans to the party last night.
These jeans are comfortable and perfect for everyday use.
She bought a new pair of black jeans for the trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trousers - outer garment covering the lower body from the waist to the ankles, divided into two sections for the legs
  • Synonym: denim - a sturdy cotton twill fabric, typically blue, used for jeans

Etymology

From the French 'jean', referring to a type of twilled cotton cloth named after Genoa, Italy.
