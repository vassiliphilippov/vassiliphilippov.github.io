dyspnoeic

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈnoʊˌɪk / (AmE), / dɪsˈnəʊ.ɪk / (BrE)
Experiencing difficulty in breathing; short of breath.
having difficulty breathing
medical british

Examples

The patient appeared dyspnoeic and was struggling to speak in full sentences.
After climbing the stairs, she became noticeably dyspnoeic.
Doctors quickly administered oxygen to the dyspnoeic child in the emergency room.

Synset Examples

breathless at thought of what I had done
breathless from running
followed the match with breathless interest

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breathless - unable to breathe easily; short of breath

Etymology

From Greek *dys-* (bad, difficult) + *pnoia* (breath).
