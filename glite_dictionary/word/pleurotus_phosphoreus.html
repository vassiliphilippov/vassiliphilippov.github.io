Pleurotus phosphoreus

1. NOUN

/ pliːˈɹoʊtəs fɒsˈfɔːɹiːəs / (AmE), / plʊəˈrəʊtəs fɒsˈfɔːriəs / (BrE)
a species of red, luminescent mushroom found in Europe
red luminescent European mushroom

Examples

The forest path was aglow with the faint red light of Pleurotus phosphoreus.
Scientists study Pleurotus phosphoreus to learn more about natural luminescence in fungi.
While hiking in Europe, she spotted the unmistakable glow of Pleurotus phosphoreus under a decaying log.

Etymology

From Greek 'pleura' (side/rib) and 'phosphoros' (light-bearing), referring to the mushroom's appearance and its bioluminescence.
