Kuwait City

1. PROPN

/ kuˈweɪt ˈsɪti / (AmE), / kuːˈweɪt ˈsɪti / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Kuwait, located on the Persian Gulf and serving as its principal seaport
capital city of Kuwait

Examples

Large construction projects have transformed Kuwait City into a modern metropolis in recent decades.
The oil industry has greatly influenced the economy of Kuwait City.
Tourists often visit the historic souq in Kuwait City for local goods and souvenirs.
