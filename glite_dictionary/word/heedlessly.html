heedlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈhidləsli / (AmE), / ˈhiːdləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of care, attention, or consideration for possible consequences.
without care or attention; thoughtlessly

Examples

She heedlessly stepped into the busy street, ignoring the traffic signal.
The company heedlessly disregarded warnings about the environmental impact of their actions.
He heedlessly tossed important documents in the trash without reading them.

Synset Examples

carelessly raised the children's hopes without thinking of their possible disappointment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carelessly - without taking care; in a negligent manner
  • Synonym: thoughtlessly - without thinking about possible consequences; without consideration

Etymology

From 'heedless' + '-ly'; 'heedless' from Middle English 'heden' (to heed) + '-less'.
