Maria Montessori

1. PROPN

/ məˈriːə ˌmɒntəˈsɔːri / (AmE), / məˈriːə ˌmɒntɪˈsɔːri / (BrE)
(1870–1952) Italian educator who developed the Montessori method of education, emphasizing a child-centered, hands-on approach to learning.
Italian educator; developer of Montessori method

Examples

Many schools around the world have adopted the Maria Montessori approach to early childhood education.
As a pioneer in education, Maria Montessori believed in fostering independence in children.
The philosophy of Maria Montessori continues to influence modern educational practices globally.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Montessori method - an educational approach developed by Maria Montessori
