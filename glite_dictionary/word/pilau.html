pilau

1. NOUN

/ pɪˈlaʊ / (AmE), / pɪˈlaʊ / (BrE)
A dish of rice cooked in seasoned broth, often with onions, vegetables, poultry, game, or shellfish, and sometimes tomatoes.
rice dish cooked in seasoned broth

Examples

She served spicy pilau with chicken and vegetables at the dinner party.
This restaurant is famous for its traditional seafood pilau.
You can make pilau using lamb, beef, or just vegetables, depending on your preference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pilaf - rice cooked in seasoned broth; a variant spelling and synonym

Etymology

From Turkish 'pilav', ultimately from Persian 'polo' (rice dish).
