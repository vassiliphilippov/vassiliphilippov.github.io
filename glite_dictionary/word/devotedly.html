devotedly

1. ADV

/ dɪˈvoʊtɪdli / (AmE), / dɪˈvəʊtɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows great love, loyalty, or commitment
in a loyal or committed manner

Examples

She cared for her sick mother devotedly until the very end.
He worked devotedly to achieve the charity's goals.
The dog followed its owner devotedly everywhere she went.

Synset Examples

He served his master devotedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faithfully - in a loyal or steadfast manner

Etymology

From 'devoted' + '-ly'. 'Devoted' from Latin 'devotus', past participle of 'devovere' (to vow).
