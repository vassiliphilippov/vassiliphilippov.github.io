hl

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪtʃ ˈɛl / (AmE), / ˌeɪtʃ ˈɛl / (BrE)
A metric unit of volume equal to 100 liters.
metric unit of 100 liters

Examples

The winery produces over 200 hl of wine each season.
Water tanks with a capacity of 10 hl are used in the facility.
The shipment contained 15 hl of olive oil for export.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hectoliter - metric unit of volume equal to 100 liters

Etymology

Abbreviation of hectoliter, from Greek 'hekaton' (hundred) and French 'litre' (liter).
