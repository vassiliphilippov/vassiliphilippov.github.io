Leptinotarsa

1. NOUN

/ lɛp.tɪ.nəˈtɑːr.sə / (AmE), / lɛp.tɪ.nəˈtɑː.sə / (BrE)
a genus of beetles in the family Chrysomelidae, including the Colorado potato beetle and related species
genus of beetles including Colorado potato beetle

Examples

The Colorado potato beetle belongs to the genus Leptinotarsa, which is known for feeding on potato plants.
Several species within Leptinotarsa can be agricultural pests, not just the well-known Colorado potato beetle.
Entomologists often study Leptinotarsa to understand pesticide resistance in crop-damaging insects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Colorado potato beetle - common beetle species in the genus Leptinotarsa

Etymology

From New Latin, combining Greek leptos (slender) and tarsos (flat of the foot, sole).
