northernmost

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɔrðərnˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðənməʊst / (BrE)
situated at or relating to the point farthest north
farthest north; most northerly

Examples

Norway's North Cape is considered the northernmost point of mainland Europe.
The researchers set up camp in the northernmost region of the island.
Greenland is home to the world's northernmost permanent settlement.

Synset Examples

Alaska is our northernmost state

Related Terms

  • Antonym: southernmost - situated farthest south
  • Degree Lower: northerly - in a northern direction or situated toward the north

Etymology

Formed from 'northern' + 'most', following the pattern of superlative directional adjectives in English.
