Venezuela

1. PROPN

/ ˌvɛnəˈzweɪlə / (AmE), / ˌvɛnɪˈzweɪlə / (BrE)
a country in northern South America, bordering the Caribbean Sea, known for its oil resources and history of independence from Spain in 1811
country in northern South America

Examples

She traveled to Venezuela to explore the Angel Falls and vibrant local culture.
Venezuela is one of the world's leading oil-producing countries.
The cuisine of Venezuela features dishes like arepas and pabellón criollo.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Venezuela', meaning 'Little Venice', due to explorers' impressions of stilt houses reminiscent of Venice.
