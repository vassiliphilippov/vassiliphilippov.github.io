Moukden

1. PROPN

/ ˈmoʊk.dən / (AmE), / ˈməʊk.dən / (BrE)
a historical name for Shenyang, a major city in northeastern China
old name for Shenyang, China

Examples

The Battle of Moukden marked a turning point in the Russo-Japanese War.
Old maps often refer to Shenyang as Moukden.
In the early twentieth century, Moukden was an important industrial center.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Shenyang - current name for the city formerly known as Moukden

Etymology

From Manchu *Mukden*, the former name for Shenyang.
