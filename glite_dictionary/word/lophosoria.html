Lophosoria

1. NOUN

/ ˌloʊfəˈsɔːriə / (AmE), / ˌlɒfəˈsɔːrɪə / (BrE)
A genus of tree ferns native to Central and South America, consisting of a single species.
genus of tree ferns

Examples

Researchers discovered a new population of Lophosoria in the remote Andean cloud forests.
The distinctive fronds of Lophosoria make it easy to identify among other ferns.
Conservationists are studying Lophosoria to better understand its habitat requirements.

Etymology

From Greek 'lophos' (crest) + 'sōrós' (heap, in reference to sori of ferns).
