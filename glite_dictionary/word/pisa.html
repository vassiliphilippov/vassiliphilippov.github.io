Pisa

1. PROPN

/ ˈpiːzə / (AmE), / ˈpiːzə / (BrE)
A city in Tuscany, Italy, famous for its Leaning Tower and historic architecture.
Italian city noted for the Leaning Tower

Examples

We visited Pisa last summer to see the Leaning Tower and explore its historic center.
The university in Pisa is one of the oldest in Italy and has a distinguished reputation.
Photographs of Pisa's iconic tower attract millions of tourists from around the world each year.

Etymology

From Italian 'Pisa', the name of the city.
