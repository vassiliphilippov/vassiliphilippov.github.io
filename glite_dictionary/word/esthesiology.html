esthesiology

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛsθiˌsiˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌiːsθiːziˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of the sense organs and sensations.
study of sense organs and sensations
medical

Examples

A breakthrough in esthesiology helped scientists understand the mechanisms behind pain perception.
Many advancements in medical technology have roots in the field of esthesiology.

Etymology
