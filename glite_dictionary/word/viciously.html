viciously

1. ADV

/ ˈvɪʃ.əs.li / (AmE), / ˈvɪʃ.əs.li / (BrE)
In a cruel, violent, or savage manner.
in a cruel or violent way

Examples

The dog viciously defended its territory from intruders.
