high-ranking

1. ADJ

/ haɪˈræŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / haɪˈræŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
having a senior or important position within an organization or hierarchy.
holding a senior or important position

Examples

She was promoted to a high-ranking position within the company.
A high-ranking official from the ministry visited the disaster site.
Only high-ranking officers were allowed to attend the confidential briefing.

Synset Examples

a high-level official
a high-level corporate briefing
upper-level management

Related Terms

  • Synonym: senior - having a higher level or rank
  • Antonym: low-ranking - having a junior or less important position
