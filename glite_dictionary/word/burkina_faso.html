Burkina Faso

1. PROPN

/ bɝːˌkiːnə ˈfɑːsoʊ / (AmE), / bɜːˌkiːnə ˈfɑːsəʊ / (BrE)
a landlocked country in western Africa, formerly known as Upper Volta, which gained independence from France in 1960.
country in western Africa

Examples

She spent two years working in Burkina Faso with a humanitarian organization.
The capital city of Burkina Faso is Ouagadougou.
Many tourists visit Burkina Faso to experience its vibrant music and cultural festivals.

Etymology

From native Mossi languages, meaning 'land of upright people'; renamed in 1984 from French 'Haute-Volta' (Upper Volta).
