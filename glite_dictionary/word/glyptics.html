glyptics

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlɪptɪks / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪptɪks / (BrE)
the art of engraving or carving designs on precious stones
art of engraving on precious stones

Examples

He studied glyptics to master the art of gemstone engraving.
Ancient civilizations are renowned for their achievements in glyptics.
The museum's collection of glyptics includes hundreds of intricately carved gemstones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glyptic - relating to carving or engraving on gems

Etymology

From Greek glyptikē (the art of carving), from glyphein (to carve).
