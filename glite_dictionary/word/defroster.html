defroster

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfrɔstər / (AmE), / dɪˈfrɒstə / (BrE)
a device or system that removes ice or frost, especially from a vehicle's windshield, a refrigerator, or an airplane's wings
device/system to remove ice or frost

Examples

Please turn on the defroster so I can see out the windshield.
The refrigerator's automatic defroster makes maintenance much easier.
Ice on the plane's wings was quickly cleared by the defroster.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heater - a device that produces heat

Etymology

From de- + frost + -er, meaning 'one that removes frost'.
