Balkan

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæl.kən / (AmE), / ˈbæl.kən / (BrE)
a person from the Balkan Peninsula, especially in southeastern Europe
inhabitant of the Balkan Peninsula

Examples

After moving to London, he still felt proud of being a Balkan.
Many Balkans celebrate their diverse traditions and history.
The festival brought together Balkan musicians from different countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Southeasterner - a person from southeast Europe

Etymology

From Turkish 'Balkan', meaning 'mountain', originally used for a mountain range in Bulgaria.
