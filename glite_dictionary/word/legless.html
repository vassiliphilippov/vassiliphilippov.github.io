legless

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛɡ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡ.ləs / (BrE)
not having legs.
not having legs

Examples

The gecko is a legless lizard that moves by slithering along the ground.
After the accident, he became legless and had to adapt to a new way of living.
Some species of amphibians are legless, resembling large worms or snakes.
