Siqueiros

1. PROPN

/ siːˈkeɪ.ɾoʊs / (AmE), / siːˈkeɪ.ɾɒs / (BrE)
(1896–1974) Mexican painter renowned for his murals depicting protest and revolution.
Mexican muralist (1896–1974)

Examples

The bold murals of Siqueiros can be found across Mexico City.
Many artists cite Siqueiros as a major influence on their political art.
A retrospective of Siqueiros attracted large crowds at the art museum.
