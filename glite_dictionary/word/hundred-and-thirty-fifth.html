hundred-and-thirty-fifth

1. ADJ

/ ˌhʌndrəd ənd ˈθɜrti fɪfθ / (AmE), / ˌhʌndrɪd ənd ˈθɜːti fɪfθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number matching 135th in a sequence; coming after the one hundred and thirty-fourth and before the one hundred and thirty-sixth.
ordinal number for 135th

Examples

This is the hundred-and-thirty-fifth anniversary of the founding of the university.
She finished in hundred-and-thirty-fifth place in the marathon.
The hundred-and-thirty-fifth page contains a detailed map of the region.
