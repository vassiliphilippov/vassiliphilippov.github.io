punch-drunk

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʌntʃˌdrʌŋk / (AmE), / ˈpʌntʃˌdrʌŋk / (BrE)
dazed or confused, as if from repeated blows, often used figuratively to describe someone stunned or bewildered by an experience or emotion.
dazed or stunned as if from repeated blows

Examples

After working through the night, I stumbled out of the office feeling punch-drunk.
She was punch-drunk with excitement after hearing the news of her promotion.
He looked punch-drunk after running the marathon in the midday sun.

Synset Examples

knocked silly by the impact
slaphappy with exhaustion
punch-drunk with love

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dazed - unable to think clearly or act normally
  • Antonym: clearheaded - able to think and act normally; not confused or dazed

Etymology

Originally from boxing slang, referring to a boxer impaired by repeated blows to the head.
