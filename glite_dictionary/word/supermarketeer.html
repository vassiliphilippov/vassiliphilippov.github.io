supermarketeer

1. NOUN

/ ˌsuːpərˌmɑːrkəˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˌmɑːkɪˈtɪə / (BrE)
a person who operates or manages a supermarket
operator or manager of a supermarket

Examples

The new supermarketeer turned the struggling store into a thriving business.
As a successful supermarketeer, she led several supermarkets in the region.
The conference brought together leading supermarketeers to discuss industry trends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manager - person in charge of part or all of an organization

Etymology

Formed from 'supermarket' and agentive suffix '-eer', similar to 'market' + 'eer'.
