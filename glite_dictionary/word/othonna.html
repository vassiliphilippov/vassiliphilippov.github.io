Othonna

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈθɑnə / (AmE), / əʊˈθɒnə / (BrE)
A genus of herbs or shrubs native to western Africa, belonging to the daisy family (Asteraceae).
a genus of western African herbs or shrubs

Examples

The rare succulent Othonna thrives in the arid regions of western Africa.
Botanists have recently discovered a new species within the Othonna genus.
Some Othonna species are cultivated as ornamental plants for their unusual foliage.

Etymology

From New Latin, possibly formed from an African vernacular name.
