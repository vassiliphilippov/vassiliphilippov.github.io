low-priced

1. ADJ

/ ˌloʊ ˈpraɪst / (AmE), / ˌləʊ ˈpraɪst / (BrE)
costing a relatively small amount of money; inexpensive.
inexpensive; costing little money

Examples

The store offers a variety of low-priced electronic gadgets for budget-conscious shoppers.
We stayed in a low-priced hotel during our trip to save money.
The government is investing in low-priced housing to help low-income families.

Synset Examples

low-cost housing

Related Terms

  • Antonym: expensive - costing a lot of money
  • Synonym: cheap - inexpensive, costing little
