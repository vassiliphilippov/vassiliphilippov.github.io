insistently

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈsɪstəntli / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɪstəntli / (BrE)
In a persistent and forceful manner; repeatedly and with emphasis.
in a persistent and forceful manner

Examples

She insistently knocked on the door until someone answered.
The reporter insistently asked questions, refusing to give up.
He insistently reminded us about the approaching deadline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: persistently - in a continuous or repeated manner, especially despite difficulty or opposition
