barbershop

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑrbərˌʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbəʃɒp / (BrE)
A shop where men have their hair cut, beards trimmed, and other grooming services performed, typically by a barber.
shop for men's haircuts and grooming

Examples

He goes to the barbershop every month to get his hair trimmed.
The old barbershop downtown is famous for its friendly atmosphere and skilled barbers.
After work, he stopped by the barbershop for a quick shave.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hair salon - a shop where hairdressers work, often for women

Etymology

From 'barber' (one who cuts hair and shaves beards) + 'shop'.
