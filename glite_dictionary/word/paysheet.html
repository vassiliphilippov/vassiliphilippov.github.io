paysheet

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪˌʃit / (AmE), / ˈpeɪʃiːt / (BrE)
a list of employees of a company showing the amount each is to be paid
list of employees and their salaries

Examples

The accountant prepared the monthly paysheet for all staff members.
Each department submits its own paysheet at the end of the week.
Errors in the paysheet can delay employee payments.

Synset Examples

the company had a long payroll

Related Terms

  • Synonym: payroll - list of a company's employees and the wages due to each
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13433894-n
Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪˌʃiːt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪʃiːt / (BrE)
a list showing the total amount of money paid in wages by a company or organization
a record of total wages paid

Examples

At the end of each month, the accountant prepares the paysheet for all employees.
Due to company growth, the paysheet doubled compared to the previous year.
The manager checked the paysheet to verify that all salaries were processed correctly.

Synset Examples

the company had a large payroll

Related Terms

  • Synonym: payroll - the total amount paid in wages by a company

Synset POS: n
