Myanmar

1. PROPN

/ miˈɑːnˌmɑr / (AmE), / miˈæn.mɑː / (BrE)
A country in southeastern Asia, officially known as the Republic of the Union of Myanmar, formerly called Burma.
country in southeastern Asia

Examples

Exports from Myanmar have increased significantly over the past decade.
She spent two years teaching English in Myanmar after graduating from university.
The Irrawaddy River flows through the heart of Myanmar before reaching the sea.

Synset Examples

much opium is grown in Myanmar

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Burma - former official name of Myanmar, used historically and still in some contexts

Etymology

Officially adopted as the country's name in 1989, derived from the local short form 'Myanma'.
