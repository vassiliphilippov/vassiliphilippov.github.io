mailwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪlˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈmeɪlˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to deliver the mail to people's homes and businesses
female mail carrier

Examples

The mailwoman delivered a package to our house this afternoon.
Everyone on the street knows the friendly mailwoman by name.
The mailwoman wore a raincoat as she made her rounds in the storm.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mailman - a man who delivers the mail
  • Synonym: mail carrier - a person who delivers the mail

Etymology

Formed from 'mail' (post, letters) + 'woman' (female adult).
