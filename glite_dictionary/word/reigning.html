reigning

1. ADJ

/ ˈreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
currently holding and exercising power, authority, or a title, especially in a political or competitive context.
currently holding power or a title

Examples

The reigning champion will defend her title next weekend.
Many expected the reigning monarch to address the nation.
As the reigning president, he wields significant influence over policy decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incumbent - currently holding office or position
  • Antonym: former - having previously held a title or position, but not currently

Etymology

Present participle of the verb "reign."
