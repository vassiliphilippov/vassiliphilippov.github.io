Kiribati

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɪrɪˈbæs / (AmE), / ˌkɪrɪˈbɑːti / (BrE)
a republic in the central Pacific Ocean, consisting of 33 islands, south of the equator
island republic in the central Pacific

Examples

The president of Kiribati addressed the United Nations on climate change.
Tourists are drawn to the pristine beaches of Kiribati.
Rising sea levels pose significant risks to communities in Kiribati.

Etymology

From the local pronunciation of Gilbert (Islands), as the nation's name is derived from the former Gilbert Islands.
