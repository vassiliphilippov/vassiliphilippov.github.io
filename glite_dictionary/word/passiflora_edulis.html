Passiflora edulis

1. NOUN

/ ˌpæsɪˈflɔrə ˈɛdʒʊlɪs / (AmE), / ˌpæsɪˈflɔːrə ˈɛdjʊlɪs / (BrE)
A tropical vine native to Brazil, widely cultivated for its edible, aromatic purple fruit known as passion fruit.
tropical vine grown for passion fruit

Examples

The Passiflora edulis vine thrives in warm, humid climates and produces fragrant flowers.
Farmers in Brazil export large quantities of Passiflora edulis for its popular passion fruit.
Scientists are researching Passiflora edulis for its potential health benefits and unique flavors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passion fruit - the fruit produced by Passiflora edulis

Etymology

From Latin: 'passiflora' (passion flower) and 'edulis' (edible).
