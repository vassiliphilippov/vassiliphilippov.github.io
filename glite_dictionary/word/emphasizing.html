emphasizing

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛmfəˌsaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛmfəsaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving special importance or attention to something
the act of stressing importance

Examples

The teacher's emphasizing of key points helped the students understand the lesson better.
In her speech, she made her emphasizing of teamwork very clear.
He is known for his constant emphasizing of the need for innovation in the company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emphasis - special importance or value given to something
  • Antonym: downplaying - making something appear less important

Etymology

From the verb 'emphasize,' itself derived from Greek 'emphainein' (to present, exhibit).
