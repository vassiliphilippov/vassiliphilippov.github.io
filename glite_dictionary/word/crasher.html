crasher

1. NOUN

/ ˈkræʃər / (AmE), / ˈkræʃə / (BrE)
a person who attends a social event without an invitation or without paying
person who attends event uninvited

Examples

Several crashers managed to sneak into the exclusive party last night.
The security team quickly spotted a crasher near the entrance and escorted him out.
Every year, the gala attracts a few crashers hoping to mingle with celebrities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gatecrasher - a person who attends a party or event without an invitation

Etymology

From the verb 'crash' (to enter forcefully or abruptly), plus '-er' forming an agent noun.
