numismatics

1. NOUN

/ nuːmɪzˈmætɪks / (AmE), / njuːmɪzˈmætɪks / (BrE)
the study or collection of coins, paper money, and medals
study or collection of coins and money

Examples

She developed an interest in numismatics after inheriting her grandfather's coin collection.
The museum’s exhibit on numismatics attracted collectors from around the world.
Numismatics involves studying the history and design of currency as well as its production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coin collecting - the collection of coins as a hobby

Etymology

From French numismatique, from Latin numismatis (of coin), from Greek nomisma (coin, money).
