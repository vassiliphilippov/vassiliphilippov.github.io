transmitted

1. ADJ

/ trænzˈmɪtɪd / (AmE), / trænzˈmɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a characteristic or condition) passed from parent to offspring by heredity.
passed by heredity

Examples

The condition is transmitted from one generation to the next through genetic material.
Certain blood disorders are transmitted within families along hereditary lines.
Research has shown that eye color is transmitted genetically.

Synset Examples

an inherited disease
familial traits
genetically transmitted features

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inherited - (of a characteristic or disease) received from one's parents by genetic transmission
  • Antonym: acquired - developed after birth; not inherited

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'transmit', from Latin 'transmittere' (to send across).
