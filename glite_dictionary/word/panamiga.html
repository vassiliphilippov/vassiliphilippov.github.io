panamiga

1. NOUN

/ ˌpænəˈmiːɡə / (AmE), / ˌpanəˈmiːɡə / (BrE)
A low, stingless nettle native to Central and South America, characterized by velvety brownish-green toothed leaves and clusters of small green flowers.
Central/South American stingless nettle plant

Examples

The botanist collected samples of panamiga during her expedition in the Amazon rainforest.
Some traditional remedies in Central America use preparations made from the leaves of panamiga.
Due to its unusual texture, panamiga is easily distinguished from other nettles in the region.
