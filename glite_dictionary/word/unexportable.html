unexportable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪkˈspɔːr.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪkˈspɔː.tə.bəl / (BrE)
Not suitable or allowed for export to other countries.
not suitable for export

Examples

Some agricultural products are considered unexportable due to strict international regulations.
The company was left with a surplus of unexportable goods after the new trade ban was imposed.
Because of its perishable nature, fresh milk is largely unexportable to distant countries.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: exportable - suitable for export
