genus Arnoseris

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ɑːrˈnoʊ.sɛrɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ɑːˈnəʊ.sɛrɪs / (BrE)
A small genus of flowering plants in the daisy family, known commonly as lamb succory.
genus of flowering plants (lamb succory)

Examples

The genus Arnoseris contains only one known species, Arnoseris minima, commonly called lamb succory.
Botanists are interested in the distribution of genus Arnoseris across Europe.
In her research, she identified a rare specimen belonging to the genus Arnoseris.

Etymology

From New Latin, based on Greek 'arnos' (lamb) and 'seris' (chicory or salad plant).
