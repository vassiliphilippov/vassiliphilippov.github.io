poitrine d'agneau

1. NOUN

/ pwɑːˈtrɪn dænˈjoʊ / (AmE), / pwɑːˈtriːn danˈjəʊ / (BrE)
A cut of lamb that includes the breastbone and the attached muscles, prepared for cooking.
lamb breast cut (culinary)

Examples

The chef slow-roasted the poitrine d'agneau until the meat was tender and flavorful.
For tonight's menu, we are serving poitrine d'agneau with a garlic and herb crust.
Many French recipes call for poitrine d'agneau as the main ingredient in traditional stews.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lamb breast - cut of lamb from the breast section

Etymology

From French, literally meaning 'breast of lamb'.
