ten-thousandth

1. NOUN

/ tɛn ˈθaʊzəndθ / (AmE), / tɛn ˈθaʊzəndθ / (BrE)
one part of something that is divided into ten thousand equal parts
one part in ten thousand

Examples

Only a ten-thousandth of the original population survived the disaster.
The sample contains less than a ten-thousandth of a gram of the substance.
Even a ten-thousandth change in the measurements can affect the experiment's results.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thousandth - one part in a thousand equal parts
