quarreller

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɔrələr / (AmE), / ˈkwɒrələ / (BrE)
a person who frequently quarrels or engages in disputes; someone who argues in an angry or noisy way
person who quarrels or argues

Examples

Tom was well-known as a quarreller who often picked fights over the smallest things.
The group preferred to avoid inviting a quarreller to their meetings to keep the peace.
Throughout school, she gained a reputation as a quarreller among her classmates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disputant - person involved in a dispute or argument
  • Antonym: peacemaker - a person who tries to make peace, especially by settling disputes

Etymology

From 'quarrel' + '-er', meaning one who quarrels.
