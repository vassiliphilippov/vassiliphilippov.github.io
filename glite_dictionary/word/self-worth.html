self-worth

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlfˈwɜrθ / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈwɜːθ / (BrE)
a sense of one's own value or worth as a person; self-esteem or self-respect
one's sense of personal value or worth

Examples

A healthy level of self-worth is important for mental well-being.
She struggled with her self-worth after losing her job.
Therapists often help clients build their self-worth through counseling.

Synset Examples

it was beneath his dignity to cheat
showed his true dignity when under pressure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-esteem - confidence in one's own worth or abilities; self-respect
  • Synonym: self-respect - pride and confidence in oneself; a feeling that one is behaving with honor and dignity

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'worth', modeled after similar terms like 'self-esteem'.
