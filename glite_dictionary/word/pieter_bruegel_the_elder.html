Pieter Bruegel the Elder

1. PROPN

/ ˈpiːtər ˈbrɔɪɡəl ði ˈɛldər / (AmE), / ˈpiːtə ˈbrɔɪɡəl ði ˈɛldə / (BrE)
(1525–1569) A Flemish painter known for his detailed landscapes and scenes of peasant life.
Flemish painter noted for landscapes and peasant scenes

Examples

The paintings of Pieter Bruegel the Elder are studied for their vivid depiction of rural life.
