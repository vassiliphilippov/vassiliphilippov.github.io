undoubtedly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈdaʊtɪdli / (AmE), / ʌnˈdaʊtɪdli / (BrE)
Without doubt; certainly.
certainly; without doubt

Examples

There will undoubtedly be challenges ahead, but we are prepared to face them.
This painting is undoubtedly his finest work to date.
She is undoubtedly the most talented musician in her class.

Synset Examples

it's undoubtedly very beautiful

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certainly - without any doubt; surely
  • Antonym: doubtfully - with uncertainty; hesitantly

Etymology

From 'undoubted' + '-ly'; 'undoubted' from un- (not) + doubted.
