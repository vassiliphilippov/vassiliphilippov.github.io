DISA

1. NOUN

/ ˈdiːsə / (AmE), / ˈdiːsə / (BrE)
an agency within the US Department of Defense responsible for developing, operating, and supporting information systems for military and governmental use
US Defense information systems agency

Examples

The DISA provides secure communications for military operations worldwide.
After receiving instructions from DISA, the team updated all their network protocols.
DISA is responsible for ensuring the security of the Pentagon's information systems.

Etymology

Acronym of Defense Information Systems Agency.
