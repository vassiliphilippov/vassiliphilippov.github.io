Disa

1. NOUN

/ ˈdiːsə / (AmE), / ˈdiːsə / (BrE)
a genus of showy tropical African terrestrial orchids, known for their brightly colored flowers
a genus of tropical African orchids

Examples

The Disa genus includes some of the most striking orchids found in southern Africa.
Botanists study the unique pollination mechanisms present in Disa species.
Many collectors seek rare flowers from the Disa genus for their vibrant colors.

Etymology

Named after a character, Disa, in Swedish literature, as designated by botanist Carl Peter Thunberg.
