derring-do

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɛrɪŋˈdu / (AmE), / ˌdɛrɪŋˈduː / (BrE)
brave and adventurous actions; heroic deeds, especially those characterized by daring or boldness.
heroic daring deed or action

Examples

Tales of derring-do from the battlefield inspired the young soldiers.
The movie is full of derring-do and thrilling escapes.
Her grandfather's stories of derring-do fascinated the entire family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heroism - great bravery and courage
  • Antonym: cowardice - lack of bravery; fearfulness

Etymology

A misdivision of 'daring do' in Middle English, meaning 'daring to do'.
