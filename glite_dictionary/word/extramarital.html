extramarital

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛkstrəˈmærɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˈmarɪtəl / (BrE)
happening or existing outside of marriage, especially describing a sexual relationship involving at least one married person and someone who is not their spouse
occurring outside of marriage

Examples

He admitted to having several extramarital affairs during his marriage.
The scandal involved an extramarital relationship between a politician and their aide.
Rumors of extramarital activities spread quickly throughout the small town.

Synset Examples

an adulterous relationship
extramarital affairs
the extracurricular activities of a philandering husband

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adulterous - involving or characterized by adultery
  • Antonym: marital - relating to marriage or the relationship of a married couple

Etymology

From Latin extra- (outside) + maritalis (of marriage).
