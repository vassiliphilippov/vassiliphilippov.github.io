Pityrogramma calomelanos

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɪtioʊˈɡræmə ˌkæləˈmiːlænɒs / (AmE), / ˌpɪtɪəʊˈɡræmə ˌkæləməˈlanɒs / (BrE)
A species of tropical American fern known for its fronds with white undersides.
tropical fern with white undersides
