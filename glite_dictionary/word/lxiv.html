lxiv

1. NUM

/ ɛl ɛks aɪ vi / (AmE), / ɛl ɛks aɪ vi / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for sixty-four (64)
Roman numeral for 64

Examples

The clock above the church door was engraved with the Roman numeral lxiv.
Chapter lxiv of the novel reveals a crucial plot twist.
The stone was marked with the year MCMLXIV (1964), where lxiv stands for sixty-four.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sixty-four - the cardinal number following sixty-three and preceding sixty-five

Etymology

From Latin numerals L (50) + X (10) + IV (4).
