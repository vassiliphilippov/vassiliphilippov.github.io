Xestobium rufovillosum

1. NOUN

/ zɛsˈtoʊbiəm ˌruːfoʊvɪˈloʊsəm / (AmE), / zɛsˈtɒbiəm ˌruːfəʊvɪˈləʊsəm / (BrE)
a species of beetle known for boring through wood and producing a ticking sound, traditionally believed to foretell death
wood-boring beetle, 'deathwatch beetle'

Examples

The old manor was infested with Xestobium rufovillosum, and their ticking haunted the quiet nights.
Curators discovered signs of Xestobium rufovillosum damage in the museum's wooden beams.
Legend has it that the sound made by Xestobium rufovillosum foretells impending death.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deathwatch beetle - common name for Xestobium rufovillosum
