topicality

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɑpɪˈkæləti / (AmE), / ˌtɒpɪˈkælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being of current interest or relevance
state of being currently relevant

Examples

The topicality of the article made it widely discussed in the media.
Debaters are often judged on the topicality of their arguments.
Although interesting, the report's lack of topicality reduced its impact.

Synset Examples

the library had to discard books that had lost their topicality

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relevance - the quality of being closely connected or appropriate

Etymology

From 'topical' + '-ity', patterned after other abstract nouns in '-ity'.
