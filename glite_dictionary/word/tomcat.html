tomcat

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑmˌkæt / (AmE), / ˈtɒmˌkat / (BrE)
a male cat, especially one that is not neutered
male cat

Examples

The stray tomcat prowled the neighborhood at night.
Our old tomcat always fights with the neighbor's cats.
She adopted a rescue tomcat from the animal shelter.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: queen - a female cat, especially one used for breeding

Etymology

From 'Tom', a common male given name, plus 'cat'; popularized by the 1760 book 'The Life and Adventures of a Cat'.
