military

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of soldiers, armed forces, or warfare
relating to armed forces or soldiers

Examples

He wore a military uniform during the ceremony.
The country increased its military spending last year.
Strict military discipline was required at the training camp.

Synset Examples

military uniforms

Related Terms

  • Antonym: civilian - not in or related to the armed forces

Etymology

From Latin 'militarius', from 'miles' (soldier).

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri / (BrE)
the armed forces of a country, especially when regarded as an organized institution

Examples

The government has increased funding for the military to enhance national security.
After completing college, she decided to join the military and serve her country.
The military played a crucial role in providing aid after the natural disaster.
