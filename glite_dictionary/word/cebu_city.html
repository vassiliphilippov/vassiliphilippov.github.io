Cebu City

1. PROPN

/ sɛˈbu ˈsɪti / (AmE), / sɛˈbuː ˈsɪti / (BrE)
a major city and seaport located on Cebu Island in the Philippines
major city in the Philippines

Examples

Many tourists visit Cebu City for its historic landmarks and beautiful beaches.
The port of Cebu City plays a crucial role in the trade of the central Philippines.
She moved to Cebu City to attend university and start her career.

Etymology

From Cebu (island/province name), itself from the Old Cebuano 'sibu' meaning 'trade', combined with 'city'.
