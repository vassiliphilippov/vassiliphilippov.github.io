procreator

1. NOUN

/ ˈproʊ.kri.eɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.kri.eɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person who creates or produces something, especially one who brings another being into existence; a parent or originator
one who creates or produces; a parent

Examples

She was seen as the principal procreator of the project, shaping its direction from the start.
Some philosophical traditions regard the artist as a procreator of meaning and beauty.
As a procreator, he felt a deep responsibility for the well-being of those he had helped bring into the group.

Synset Examples

Her role as a procreator in the team was crucial.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creator - a person or thing that brings something into existence
  • Synonym: originator - a person who creates or initiates something

Etymology

From Latin procreare (to beget, produce).
