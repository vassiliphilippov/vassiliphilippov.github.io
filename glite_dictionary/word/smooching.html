smooching

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmuːtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsmuːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
kissing or cuddling affectionately, especially in a playful or romantic way
affectionate kissing or cuddling
informal

Examples

They were smooching on the park bench all afternoon.
The romantic movie ended with the couple smooching under the stars.
She caught her parents smooching in the kitchen and quickly looked away.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kissing - to touch with the lips as a sign of love, affection, or greeting
  • Synonym: making out - engaging in amorous kissing and caressing

Etymology

From 'smooch' (verb), of uncertain origin, possibly imitative.
