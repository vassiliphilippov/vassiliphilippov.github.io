collywobbles

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑliˌwɑbəlz / (AmE), / ˈkɒliˌwɒbəlz / (BrE)
a feeling of discomfort or pain in the stomach, often described as cramps or queasiness, usually caused by nervousness or indigestion
stomach pain or queasiness

Examples

I always get the collywobbles before speaking in public.
He blamed the bad seafood for his sudden attack of the collywobbles.
The thought of the final exam gave her the collywobbles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stomachache - pain in the stomach or abdomen

Etymology

Origin unknown; first recorded in 1800s, possibly as a fanciful or humorous formation influenced by dialect.
