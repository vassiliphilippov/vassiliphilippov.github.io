dourly

1. ADV

/ ˈdaʊrli / (AmE), / ˈdaʊəli / (BrE)
In a sullen, stern, or gloomy manner.
in a sullen or stern manner

Examples

She spoke dourly, making it clear she was not happy with the decision.
He dourly refused to join in the celebrations.
The manager glared dourly at the late employees.

Synset Examples

he sat in his chair dourly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dour - sullen, stern, or gloomy in manner or appearance
