baldie

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔl.di / (AmE), / ˈbɔːl.di / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is bald, especially one with very little or no hair on the scalp
person who is bald (informal)
informal

Examples

My uncle has been a proud baldie since his late twenties.
Some kids called him a baldie on the playground, but it didn't bother him.
She joined an online group for fellow baldies to share tips and stories.

Synset Examples

She teased him for being a baldie at such a young age.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bald - lacking hair on the scalp

Etymology

From 'bald' + diminutive or informal suffix '-ie'.
