Orissa

1. PROPN

/ ɔːˈrɪsə / (AmE), / ɔːˈrɪsə / (BrE)
a former name for the state of Odisha, a region in eastern India on the Bay of Bengal
former name of the Indian state Odisha

Examples

The ancient temples of Orissa attract thousands of visitors every year.
In 2011, the Indian government officially changed the state's name from Orissa to Odisha.
Many traditional dance forms in Orissa are still actively performed and taught.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Odisha - current official name of the Indian state formerly known as Orissa

Etymology

From Odia 'Oṛiśā', the name of the region and people.
