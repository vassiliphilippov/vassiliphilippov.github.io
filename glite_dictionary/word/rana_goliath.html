Rana goliath

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪnə ɡəˈlaɪ.əθ / (AmE), / ˈrɑːnə ɡəˈlaɪ.əθ / (BrE)
the largest living species of frog, native to West Africa, reaching up to a foot in length and weighing up to 10 lbs
largest living frog species

Examples

The Rana goliath can weigh as much as 10 pounds, making it a remarkable amphibian.
Wildlife documentaries often feature the Rana goliath to highlight diversity in African rainforests.
Scientists study the Rana goliath to understand how such a large frog thrives in its natural habitat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goliath frog - common name for Rana goliath, the world's largest frog species

Etymology

From Latin 'Rana' (frog) and Greek 'goliath' (giant), referencing the biblical giant.
