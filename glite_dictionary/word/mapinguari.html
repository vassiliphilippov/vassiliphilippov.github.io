mapinguari

1. NOUN

/ mæˌpiŋɡwəˈri / (AmE), / mæˌpɪŋɡwəˈri / (BrE)
a mythical creature said to inhabit the Amazon rainforest, described as a large, sloth-like beast in local folklore
mythical Amazonian beast

Examples

Legends tell of the mapinguari, a mysterious creature feared and respected by indigenous Amazonian peoples.
Some researchers believe that sightings of the mapinguari might be based on memories of extinct giant sloths.
Expeditions into the rainforest have been launched in search of the elusive mapinguari.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yeti - mythical ape-like creature from the Himalayas
  • Synonym: sasquatch - legendary hairy creature of North America
  • Synonym: cryptid - a creature whose existence is unsubstantiated or disputed

Etymology

From Portuguese, based on Tupi–Guarani words for 'roaring animal' or 'spirit.'
