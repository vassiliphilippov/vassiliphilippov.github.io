kempt

1. ADJ

/ kɛmpt / (AmE), / kɛmpt / (BrE)
(especially of hair or appearance) neatly kept; tidy and well-groomed.
neat and tidy (esp. hair, appearance)

Examples

Her kempt hair made a strong impression at the interview.
He always keeps his beard carefully kempt for a professional appearance.
The garden was kempt and inviting, with every flowerbed neatly in place.

Synset Examples

a nicely kempt beard

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unkempt - (especially of hair/appearance) untidy, disheveled
  • Synonym: tidy - neat and orderly, especially in appearance
  • Synonym: groomed - neatly maintained in appearance

Etymology

From Middle English, originally the past participle of 'kemb', meaning to comb.
