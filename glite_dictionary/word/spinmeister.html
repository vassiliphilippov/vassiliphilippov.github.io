spinmeister

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪnˌmaɪstər / (AmE), / ˈspɪnˌmaɪstə / (BrE)
a person, especially in politics or public relations, who tries to give a favorable interpretation of events or actions to influence public opinion; a spin doctor
person who puts a positive spin on news or events
informal

Examples

The politician's spinmeister tried to downplay the scandal in every interview.
Every company wants a skilled spinmeister to manage crisis communications.
As a seasoned spinmeister, she knew exactly how to turn the story to their advantage.

Synset Examples

his title is Director of Communications but he is just a spin doctor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spin doctor - a public relations expert skilled in presenting information favorably

Etymology

Blend of 'spin' (as in presenting information with a bias) and German 'meister' (master).
