NARA

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɑːrə / (AmE), / ˈnɑːrə / (BrE)
the National Archives and Records Administration; the independent U.S. agency responsible for preserving and overseeing government records, including presidential libraries and historic collections
US federal records management agency
american

Examples

Researchers often visit NARA to access archived government documents for their studies.
The NARA website provides digital versions of many important historical records.
A new exhibition was launched by NARA featuring original presidential documents.

Etymology

Acronym from National Archives and Records Administration.
