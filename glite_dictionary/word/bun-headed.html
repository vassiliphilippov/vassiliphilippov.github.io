bun-headed

1. ADJ

/ bʌn ˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / bʌn ˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
Having hair styled in a bun; (informal, sometimes derogatory) silly or foolish, especially as perceived by others.
having a bun hairstyle or being silly (informal)
informal

Examples

She walked into the room, her bun-headed look giving her an air of elegance.
He made a bun-headed remark that left everyone laughing.
The students joked about their bun-headed teacher who always wore her hair in a tight knot.

Synset Examples

I called her bun-headed for forgetting my birthday.
That bun-headed girl always loses her keys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bun - a hairstyle in which the hair is drawn back from the face and twisted or gathered into a round coil at the back of the head or top of the head
  • Synonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment
