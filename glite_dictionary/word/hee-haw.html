hee-haw

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiˌhɑ / (AmE), / ˈhiːˌhɑː / (BrE)
a loud, harsh laugh resembling the braying of a donkey or neighing of a horse
a loud, braying laugh

Examples

Her hee-haw echoed across the room, making everyone turn and smile.
The comedian's joke provoked a loud hee-haw from the audience.
He burst into a hee-haw that startled his friends.

Etymology

Imitative of the braying sound made by a donkey or horse.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07142619-n

2. VERB

/ ˈhiːˌhɔ / (AmE), / ˈhiːˌhɔː / (BrE)
(of a donkey) to make a loud, harsh braying sound.
donkey's braying sound

Examples

The donkey began to hee-haw loudly at dawn, waking everyone nearby.
We heard the animal hee-haw from the other side of the field.
As the children approached, the old mule hee-hawed in greeting.
