unmedical

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmɛdɪkəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɛdɪkəl / (BrE)
Not related to medicine; lacking a medicinal effect or not medically prescribed.
not relating to medicine or medicinal effects

Examples

The patient sought unmedical advice from friends rather than professionals.
He used herbal supplements for unmedical reasons, not to treat any illness.
Some substances have purely unmedical uses and are not intended for health purposes.

Synset Examples

he took mind-altering drugs for nonmedicinal reasons

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonmedicinal - not having a medicinal effect or purpose

Etymology

From the prefix un- (not) + medical.
