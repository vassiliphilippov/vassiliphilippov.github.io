Nivôse

1. NOUN

/ niˈvoʊz / (AmE), / niˈvəʊz / (BrE)
the fourth month of the French Revolutionary calendar, corresponding roughly to December and January, known as the snowy month
fourth month of the French Revolutionary calendar

Examples

The festival was scheduled to take place during Nivôse under the new French calendar.
Many records from the era are dated in Nivôse, which can confuse modern readers.
Winter in Nivôse was harsh, with heavy snowfall across most of the region.

Etymology

From French 'Nivôse', from Latin 'nix' (snow); coined during the French Revolution.
