tousled

1. ADJ

/ ˈtaʊzəld / (AmE), / ˈtaʊzəld / (BrE)
(of hair, clothing, etc.) untidy or disordered, often from being thrown into a state of confusion or carelessness.
untidy or disordered (esp. hair, clothing)

Examples

His tousled hair made it look like he had just rolled out of bed.
She wore a tousled sweater that gave her a carefree appearance.
The wind left my tousled locks impossible to comb.

Synset Examples

her clothing was disheveled
powder-smeared and frowzled
a rumpled unmade bed
a bed with tousled sheets
his brown hair was tousled, thick, and curly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disheveled - (of appearance) untidy or disordered, especially hair or clothing
  • Antonym: neat - arranged in an orderly, tidy way; the opposite of 'tousled'

Etymology

From the verb 'tousle', probably from Middle English *towsen* (to pull about, rumple).
