necessitous

1. ADJ

/ nəˈsɛsɪtəs / (AmE), / nɪˈsɛsɪtəs / (BrE)
lacking the basic necessities of life; very poor or needy
very poor; lacking necessities

Examples

Many charities exist to assist necessitous families in the community.
The necessitous child was grateful for a warm meal and a safe place to sleep.
In his will, he left a fund for the care of the necessitous in his hometown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: destitute - extremely poor; lacking basic resources
  • Antonym: affluent - having a great deal of money; wealthy
  • Synonym: impoverished - reduced to poverty; lacking resources

Etymology

From Latin 'necessitous' (in need), from 'necessitas' (necessity).
