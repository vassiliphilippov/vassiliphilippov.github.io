omnifarious

1. ADJ

/ ɑːm.nəˈfɛr.i.əs / (AmE), / ɒm.nɪˈfeə.ri.əs / (BrE)

of all varieties or kinds

Examples

His library contained books on omnifarious topics, from science to poetry.

Etymology
