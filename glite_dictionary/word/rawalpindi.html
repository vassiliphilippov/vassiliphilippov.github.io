Rawalpindi

1. PROPN

/ rɑːˌwɑːlˈpɪndi / (AmE), / ˌraʊ.əlˈpɪndi / (BrE)
an ancient city in northeastern Pakistan, which served as the capital of Pakistan while Islamabad was being built
city in northeastern Pakistan

Examples

Many tourists visit Rawalpindi to explore its historic sites and bustling bazaars.
After independence, Rawalpindi temporarily served as the capital of Pakistan.
The train from Lahore arrives at Rawalpindi in the early evening.
