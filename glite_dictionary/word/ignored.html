ignored

1. ADJ

/ ɪɡˈnɔrd / (AmE), / ɪɡˈnɔːd / (BrE)
not given attention or consideration; deliberately disregarded.
not given attention or consideration

Examples

She felt ignored by her colleagues during the meeting.
The warning signs were completely ignored before the incident occurred.
His suggestions were often ignored, despite their value.

Synset Examples

his cries were unheeded
Shaw's neglected one-act comedy, ‘A Village Wooing’
her ignored advice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unheeded - not noticed or given attention
  • Synonym: neglected - not given proper care or attention

Etymology

From the verb 'ignore' (from French 'ignorer', from Latin 'ignorare' meaning 'not know').
