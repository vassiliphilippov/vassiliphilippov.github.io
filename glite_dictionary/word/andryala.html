andryala

1. NOUN

/ ænˈdraɪələ / (AmE), / ænˈdraɪələ / (BrE)
any plant of the genus Andryala, characterized by milky sap and heads of bright yellow flowers, native to southern Europe and northern Africa.
a genus of yellow-flowered plants

Examples

The botanist discovered a rare species of andryala growing on the hillside.
Many andryala plants thrive in sunny, rocky soils along the Mediterranean coast.
The bright yellow flowers of andryala attract bees and other pollinators.

Etymology

From New Latin Andryala, the genus name in botanical Latin.
