to winterise

1. VERB

/ ˈwɪntəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈwɪntəraɪz / (BrE)
to prepare (a building, vehicle, or other object) for use or protection during the winter season
make ready for winter

Examples

We must winterise the cabin before the first snowfall.
She winterised her car by checking the antifreeze and installing snow tires.
Farmers often winterise their equipment to prevent damage from freezing temperatures.

Synset Examples

winterize cars
winterize your houses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to winterize - Alternate spelling, especially in American English

Etymology

Formed from 'winter' + '-ise' (verb-forming suffix).
