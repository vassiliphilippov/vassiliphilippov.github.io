self-defeating

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlf dɪˈfitɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌself dɪˈfiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing more problems or harm to yourself than the intended benefit; acting in a way that works against your own goals or interests.
acting against one's own interests

Examples

Criticizing yourself too much can be self-defeating and lower your motivation.
Skipping meals to lose weight is actually self-defeating and can harm your health.
His self-defeating behavior made it hard for him to achieve his goals.

Synset Examples

it is self-defeating … to ignore the progress of events

Related Terms

  • Synonym: counterproductive - having the opposite of the desired effect
  • Antonym: productive - achieving a positive result or benefit

Etymology

Formed by combining 'self' and 'defeating', meaning 'defeating oneself'.
