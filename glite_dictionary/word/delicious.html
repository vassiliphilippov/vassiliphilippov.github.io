Delicious

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈlɪʃəs / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪʃəs / (BrE)
A variety of sweet eating apple, known for its crisp texture and mild flavor.
type of sweet apple

Examples

He prefers Delicious apples to other varieties for his fruit salads.
The orchard grows several types of apples, including Delicious and Granny Smith.
She packed a ripe Delicious in her lunchbox every morning.
