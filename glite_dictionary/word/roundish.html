roundish

1. ADJ

/ ˈraʊndɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈraʊndɪʃ / (BrE)
somewhat round in shape or appearance; approximately round
somewhat round in shape

Examples

The old table had a roundish top that was worn smooth over time.
She selected a roundish stone from the riverbank for her collection.
His face was roundish, giving him a youthful appearance.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: round - having the shape or form of a circle or sphere
  • Degree Lower: oval - having an elongated round shape, like an egg

Etymology

Formed from 'round' with the suffix '-ish' meaning 'somewhat' or 'approximately'.
