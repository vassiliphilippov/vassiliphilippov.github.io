earthly

1. ADJ

/ ˈɜrθli / (AmE), / ˈɜːθli / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the physical world as opposed to heaven or the spiritual realm.
relating to the physical world

Examples

Many poets write about earthly pleasures as distinct from spiritual ones.
He was not interested in earthly possessions or material wealth.
The cathedral was designed to inspire awe for both earthly beauty and heavenly faith.

Synset Examples

earthly beings
believed that our earthly life is all that matters
earthly love
our earthly home

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heavenly - relating to heaven or the spiritual realm

Etymology

From Old English 'eorðlic' (of the earth), from 'eorðe' (earth).
