pansy

1. NOUN

/ ˈpænzi / (AmE), / ˈpænzi / (BrE)
a small garden plant with large, rounded, colorful velvety petals, often cultivated as an ornamental flower
garden plant with large, colorful velvety petals

Examples

The pansy blooms in early spring, adding splashes of color to the garden.
She grew a variety of pansies along the front walkway.
Vibrant pansy petals are often used in floral arrangements.

Etymology

From French 'pensée' (thought), referring to the flower's resemblance to a thoughtful face.
