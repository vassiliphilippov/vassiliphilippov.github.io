Marseillaise

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɑrseɪˈjɛz / (AmE), / ˌmɑːseɪˈjɛz / (BrE)
the national anthem of France, officially titled 'La Marseillaise'
French national anthem

Examples

The crowd stood as the orchestra played Marseillaise before the match began.
Hearing Marseillaise always reminds me of my trip to Paris.
He recognized the familiar tune of the Marseillaise on the radio.

Etymology

From French 'La Marseillaise', meaning 'the (song) of Marseille', because it was first sung by volunteers from Marseille during the French Revolution.
