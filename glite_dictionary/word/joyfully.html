joyfully

1. ADV

/ ˈdʒɔɪ.fə.li / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪ.fʊ.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows or expresses great happiness or delight.
in a very happy or delighted way

Examples

The children laughed joyfully as they played in the park.
She joyfully accepted the award on stage, beaming with pride.
After the final exam, the students joyfully celebrated their success.

Synset Examples

the old man had greeted her gleefully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gleefully - in a joyful or exuberant manner
  • Synonym: merrily - in a cheerful or lively way
  • Antonym: sadly - in a sorrowful or unhappy manner

Etymology

From 'joyful' + '-ly', with 'joyful' from Old French 'joie' (joy) and '-ful'.
