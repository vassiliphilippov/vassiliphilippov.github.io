glandular

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡlæn.dʒə.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡlæn.djʊ.lə / (BrE)
relating to, affecting, or functioning as a gland, especially in biology or medicine.
relating to glands
medical

Examples

The doctor's report mentioned a glandular disorder affecting her metabolism.
Some diseases can cause glandular swelling and discomfort.
A glandular structure in the neck became inflamed after infection.

Synset Examples

glandular malfunctions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gland - an organ in the body that produces a particular chemical substance

Etymology

From Latin 'glandula' (little acorn; gland).
