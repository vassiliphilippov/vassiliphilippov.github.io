Georges Braque

1. PROPN

/ ʒɔrʒ brɑk / (AmE), / ʒɔːʒ bʁak / (BrE)
(1882–1963) A French painter and sculptor who, along with Picasso, was a leading figure in the development of Cubism.
French painter, leader of Cubism

Examples

The works of Georges Braque were displayed alongside those of Picasso in the museum.
Art historians often cite Georges Braque as a co-founder of the Cubist movement.
Georges Braque's use of geometric forms changed the course of modern art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pablo Picasso - Spanish painter and co-founder of Cubism
