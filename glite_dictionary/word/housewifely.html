housewifely

1. ADJ

/ ˈhaʊsˌwaɪfli / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌwaɪfli / (BrE)
Relating to, characteristic of, or suited to a housewife; domestic or home-managing.
relating to a housewife or domestic management

Examples

She took pride in her housewifely skills, from cooking to sewing.
His grandmother's housewifely advice shaped his approach to keeping a tidy home.
The novel depicts the protagonist's housewifely virtues as both admirable and limiting.

Synset Examples

housewifely virtues

Related Terms

  • Synonym: domestic - relating to the running of a home

Etymology

From 'housewife' + '-ly', denoting qualities or characteristics related to a housewife.
