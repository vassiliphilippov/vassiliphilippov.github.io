bluish-lilac

1. ADJ

/ ˈbluːɪʃ ˈlaɪlæk / (AmE), / ˈbluːɪʃ ˈlaɪlæk / (BrE)
having a pale purple color with a noticeable tint of blue.
pale purple tinged with blue

Examples

The artist chose a bluish-lilac paint for the bedroom walls to create a calming atmosphere.
Her dress shimmered in a bluish-lilac shade under the evening lights.
We picked a bouquet of bluish-lilac flowers from the garden.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: lavender - a pale purple color, sometimes used for flowers
  • Synonym: lilac - a pale violet color; the color of lilac flowers
