ID

1. NOUN

/ aɪˈdi / (AmE), / aɪˈdiː / (BrE)
an official card or badge that proves who you are, especially for security or age verification purposes
identification card or badge

Examples

The security guard checked my ID before letting me enter the building.
She forgot her ID at home and couldn't buy the concert tickets.
You need to show a valid ID to pick up your package at the post office.

Synset Examples

you had to show your ID in order to get in

Related Terms

  • Synonym: identification - the document or card that proves a person's identity

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'identification' (from Latin 'identitas', meaning 'sameness').
