to hospitalise

1. VERB

/ ˈhɑːspɪtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪz / (BrE)
to admit someone to a hospital for medical care
admit to hospital for care
british

Examples

After the accident, three children were hospitalised with minor injuries.
The doctor decided to hospitalise him for observation overnight.
Many patients with severe COVID-19 symptoms are hospitalised for treatment.

Synset Examples

Mother had to be hospitalized because her blood pressure was too high

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to hospitalize - admit someone to a hospital (American spelling)

Etymology

From 'hospital' + '-ise', modeled after similar verbs.
