nitwit

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪtˌwɪt / (AmE), / ˈnɪt.wɪt / (BrE)
a person who is silly, foolish, or lacking in intelligence
a foolish or silly person
informal

Examples

Don't be such a nitwit; read the instructions first!
Everyone laughed when the nitwit forgot his own phone number.
She called her brother a nitwit for spilling juice on the carpet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simpleton - a person lacking in common sense or intelligence
  • Antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intellectual ability

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from 'nit' (meaning 'nothing') plus 'wit' (intelligence).
