Takilman

1. NOUN

/ təˈkɪlmən / (AmE), / təˈkɪlmən / (BrE)
a Penutian language formerly spoken by the Takelma people of southwestern Oregon.
Penutian language of the Takelma people

Examples

The Takilman language is now considered extinct, with no fluent speakers remaining.
Linguists study Takilman to understand the diversity of Native American languages.
Many myths and stories of the Takelma people were recorded in Takilman.
