taichichuan

1. NOUN

/ ˌtaɪ.tʃiˈtʃwɑːn / (AmE), / ˌtaɪ.tʃiˈtʃwæn / (BrE)
A Chinese system of slow, meditative physical exercises designed to promote relaxation, balance, and good health.
Chinese meditative exercise system

Examples

Many people practice taichichuan in the early morning at public parks.
Experts say that taichichuan can help reduce stress and improve balance.
She attended a taichichuan class to learn better breathing techniques and movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tai chi - Synonym; a system of Chinese meditative exercises

Etymology

From Chinese, combining 'tai' (great), 'chi' (ultimate), and 'chuan' (fist, boxing).
