light-blue

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪt ˌbluː / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt ˌbluː / (BrE)
pale or light in shade; a color that is a lighter version of blue
pale or light shade of blue

Examples

She painted her bedroom walls a soothing light-blue.
The dress was a delicate shade of light-blue that matched her eyes.
We watched clouds drift across the light-blue sky.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: blue - the color between green and violet in the visible spectrum
  • Synonym: sky-blue - a shade of blue similar to a clear sky
