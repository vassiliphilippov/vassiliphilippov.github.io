précis

1. NOUN

/ ˈpreɪsiː / (AmE), / ˈpreɪsiː / (BrE)
a concise summary or abstract of the main points of a text, speech, or argument
concise summary of main points

Examples

The professor asked the students to write a précis of each chapter.
Her précis captured the essence of the lengthy report.
Please include a précis at the beginning of your essay.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summary - a brief statement of the main points
  • Synonym: abstract - a summary of the contents of a book, article, or speech

Etymology

From French 'précis' (precise, condensed), from Latin 'praecisus' (cut short, abridged).
