bullnecked

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʊlˌnɛkt / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌnɛkt / (BrE)
having a thick, short, and powerful neck, resembling that of a bull
having a thick, powerful neck

Examples

The bullnecked wrestler intimidated his opponents with his imposing physique.
He had a bullnecked appearance, matching his reputation for brute strength.
The detective described the suspect as large and bullnecked with a shaved head.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thick-necked - having a thick neck

Etymology

From 'bull' (the animal, known for its thick neck) + 'necked' (having a neck of a specified kind).
