functionally

1. ADV

/ ˈfʌŋkʃənəli / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋkʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the purpose or function of something
with respect to function

Examples

The new kitchen is functionally designed to maximize workspace and efficiency.
Although the two devices look different, they are functionally identical.
This species cannot fly, so it is functionally flightless.

Synset Examples

the two units are functionally interdependent

Related Terms

  • Antonym: structurally - in a way that relates to the structure of something

Etymology

From 'functional', itself from Latin 'functionem' (performance, execution) plus adverbial suffix '-ly'.
