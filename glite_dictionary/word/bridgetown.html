Bridgetown

1. PROPN

/ ˈbrɪdʒˌtaʊn / (AmE), / ˈbrɪdʒ.taʊn / (BrE)
the capital city of Barbados, located on its southwestern coast, known for its port and historical significance
capital city of Barbados

Many tourists visit Bridgetown for its colonial architecture and beautiful beaches.
