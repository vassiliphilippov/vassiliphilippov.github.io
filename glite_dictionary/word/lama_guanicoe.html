Lama guanicoe

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑːmə ɡwɑːˈniːkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈlɑːmə ɡwɑːˈniːkəʊ / (BrE)
the scientific name for the guanaco, a wild South American mammal related to the llama
scientific name for the guanaco

Examples

The Lama guanicoe is well adapted to the arid regions of Patagonia.
Researchers tracked the movements of wild Lama guanicoe herds using GPS collars.
Unlike domesticated llamas, the Lama guanicoe lives entirely in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guanaco - a wild South American camelid closely related to the llama

Etymology

From Spanish, based on Quechua 'wanaku', adapted to standard zoological Latin naming.
