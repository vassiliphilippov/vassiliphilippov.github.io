incautiously

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈkɑʃəsli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɔːʃəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of caution or prudence; carelessly or heedlessly.
in a careless, unwise way

Examples

He stepped incautiously into the street, not noticing the oncoming traffic.
The documents were incautiously left on the desk for anyone to see.
She incautiously shared personal information online without considering the consequences.

Synset Examples

one unfortunately sees historic features carelessly lost when estates fall into unsympathetic hands

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cautiously - in a careful and prudent way
  • Synonym: carelessly - in a way that shows a lack of care or attention

Etymology

From in- (not) + cautiously, from Latin cautus (careful).
