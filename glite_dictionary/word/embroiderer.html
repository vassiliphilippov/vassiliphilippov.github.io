embroiderer

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈbrɔɪdərər / (AmE), / ɪmˈbrɔɪdərə / (BrE)
a person who decorates fabric or other materials with needlework
person who does decorative needlework

Examples

The embroiderer spent hours crafting intricate floral designs on the silk dress.
As a skilled embroiderer, she was often asked to create custom monograms for linens.
The queen hired a famous embroiderer to embellish her royal gowns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to embroider - to decorate by sewing patterns with thread

Etymology

From 'embroider' + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
