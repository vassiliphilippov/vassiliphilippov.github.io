bluehead

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluːˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌhɛd / (BrE)
a small Atlantic wrasse (Thalassoma bifasciatum), the male of which has a bright blue head
small Atlantic fish with blue-headed males

Examples

The male bluehead is easily recognized by its vibrant blue coloring.
Divers often spot schools of blueheads near coral reefs in the Caribbean.
A young marine biologist studied the mating behavior of the bluehead for her thesis.
