Trifolium stoloniferum

1. NOUN

/ traɪˈfoʊliəm stoʊˌlɒnɪˈfɛrəm / (AmE), / trɪˈfəʊliəm stɒləˈnɪfərəm / (BrE)
A rare species of clover native to the western United States, known as the running buffalo clover.
species of clover native to western US

Examples

The endangered Trifolium stoloniferum is carefully monitored by conservationists in several states.
Botanists were excited to discover a patch of Trifolium stoloniferum in the restored prairie.
Several efforts are underway to reintroduce Trifolium stoloniferum to its native habitats.

Etymology

From Latin: Trifolium meaning 'three-leaved plant' and stoloniferum meaning 'producing shoots or runners'.
