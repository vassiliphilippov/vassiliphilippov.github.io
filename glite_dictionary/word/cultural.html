cultural

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌltʃərəl / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃərəl / (BrE)
relating to the cultivation, raising, or breeding of plants or animals, especially for agricultural purposes.
relating to cultivation of plants or animals

Examples

This species is often used as a cultural variety in modern farming.
The cultural methods employed improve the quality of the produce.
Scientists are studying cultural techniques to boost yield in aquaculture.

Synset Examples

a cultural variety

Etymology

From Latin cultura (cultivation, tending).
