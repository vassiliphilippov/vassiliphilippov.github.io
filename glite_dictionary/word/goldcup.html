goldcup

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌkʌp / (BrE)
any of various plants of the genus Ranunculus, especially those with yellow cup-shaped flowers
type of buttercup plant

Examples

The meadow was dotted with bright goldcup flowers swaying gently in the breeze.
Among the grasses, she found several goldcup plants in full bloom.
Gardeners often plant goldcup for its cheerful yellow flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buttercup - a common plant of the genus Ranunculus with bright yellow flowers
