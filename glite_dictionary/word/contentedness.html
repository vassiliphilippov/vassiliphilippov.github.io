contentedness

1. NOUN

/ kənˈtɛntɪdnəs / (AmE), / kənˈtɛntɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the state of feeling satisfied and at ease with your situation or life
state of being contented or satisfied

Examples

After years of searching, she finally achieved a deep contentedness in her new home.
He radiated contentedness as he watched his grandchildren play in the garden.
Meditation helped him develop a lasting sense of contentedness despite life's challenges.

Synset Examples

he relaxed in sleepy contentedness
they could read to their heart's content

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contentment - a state of happiness and satisfaction
  • Antonym: dissatisfaction - the state of not being satisfied

Etymology

From 'contented' + '-ness', where 'contented' derives from Latin 'contentus' (satisfied).
