extensively

1. ADV

/ ɪkˈstɛnsɪvli / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that covers a large area or includes a great deal; widely or thoroughly.
widely; to a great extent

Examples

The policy was extensively discussed during the meeting before any decision was made.
This species has been extensively studied by researchers around the world.
Most of the land in this region is extensively farmed for crops.

Synset Examples

oxidation ponds are extensively used for sewage treatment in the Midwest

Related Terms

  • Synonym: widely - over or through a wide area
  • Antonym: narrowly - in a way that covers a limited area or scope

Etymology

Formed from 'extensive' (from Latin 'extensus', past participle of 'extendere', to stretch out) + '-ly'.
