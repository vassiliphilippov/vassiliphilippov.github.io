knifelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈnaɪfˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈnaɪf.laɪk / (BrE)
(of pain, sensations, or effects) extremely sharp or cutting, as if caused by a knife.
sharp or cutting, like a knife

Examples

He felt a sudden knifelike pain shooting through his chest.
The knifelike wind made walking outside almost unbearable.
Her words had a knifelike edge that cut deeper than she intended.

Related Terms

Etymology
