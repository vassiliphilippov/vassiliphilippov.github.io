Jerusalem

1. PROPN

/ dʒəˈruːsələm / (AmE), / dʒəˈruːsələm / (BrE)
The capital and largest city of Israel, considered a holy city by Jews, Christians, and Muslims, with a long and complex history.
capital city of Israel; holy city for three religions

Examples

Many people travel to Jerusalem each year to visit its religious sites.
After 1967, Jerusalem became the focal point of international debates.
The old city of Jerusalem contains important landmarks for several religions.

Etymology

From Hebrew *Yerushalayim*, meaning 'foundation of peace' or 'city of peace.'
