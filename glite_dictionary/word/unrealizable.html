unrealizable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌriːˈælaɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌrɪəˈlaɪzəbl / (BrE)
impossible to achieve or bring about; not capable of being made real or carried out.
impossible to achieve or carry out

Examples

Many critics regarded the plan as unrealizable due to financial constraints.
She was disappointed to learn that her dream was unrealizable in her current circumstances.
The project proved unrealizable after several failed attempts to secure funding.

Synset Examples

an unattainable goal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unattainable - impossible to reach or achieve
  • Antonym: achievable - able to be achieved or accomplished

Etymology

From un- (not) + realizable (from realize + -able).
