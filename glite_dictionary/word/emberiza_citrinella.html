Emberiza citrinella

1. NOUN

/ ɛm.bəˈraɪ.zə sɪ.trɪˈnɛl.ə / (AmE), / ɛm.bəˈraɪ.zə sɪ.trɪˈnɛl.ə / (BrE)
the scientific name for the yellowhammer, a European bunting bird whose male has bright yellow plumage
scientific name for the yellowhammer bird

Examples

The ornithologist spotted an Emberiza citrinella perched on the hedgerow.
During spring, male Emberiza citrinella are easily recognized by their vivid coloring.
Researchers studied the song patterns of Emberiza citrinella in various European habitats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellowhammer - common English name for Emberiza citrinella

Etymology

From Latin; 'Emberiza' is the bunting genus, 'citrinella' means 'yellowish'.
