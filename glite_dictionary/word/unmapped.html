unmapped

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmæpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈmæpt / (BrE)
(of an area or region) not yet surveyed, charted, or represented on a map.
not yet mapped or surveyed

Examples

Much of the forest remains unmapped due to its remote location.
The explorers set off into unmapped territory, unsure of what they would find.
Some of the ocean's deepest trenches are still unmapped by scientists.

Synset Examples

uncharted seas

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mapped - charted or surveyed; represented on a map
  • Synonym: uncharted - not yet mapped, surveyed, or explored
