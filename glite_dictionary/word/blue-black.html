blue-black

1. ADJ

/ bluˈblæk / (AmE), / bluːˈblæk / (BrE)
black in color with a noticeable hint or tinge of blue.
black with a blue tinge

Examples

The bird's feathers were a stunning blue-black in the sunlight.
She wore a coat made of blue-black velvet that shimmered as she moved.
The artist mixed pigments to achieve the exact blue-black shade she needed for the painting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jet-black - completely black; very dark black color
