Diuril

1. PROPN

/ ˈdaɪjʊrɪl / (AmE), / ˈdaɪjʊərɪl / (BrE)
A brand name for the diuretic drug chlorothiazide, used to treat edema and high blood pressure.
brand of diuretic medication
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Diuril to help manage her high blood pressure.
After taking Diuril, he noticed a significant reduction in swelling in his ankles.
Some patients may experience increased urination when they start Diuril.
