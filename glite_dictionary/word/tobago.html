Tobago

1. PROPN

/ təˈbeɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / təˈbeɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
An island in the southern Caribbean, forming part of the nation of Trinidad and Tobago.
island in the Caribbean

Examples

We spent our vacation relaxing on the beautiful beaches of Tobago.
The capital of Tobago is Scarborough, known for its historic fort.
Many tourists visit Tobago for its coral reefs and vibrant marine life.

Etymology

From the Spanish 'tabaco,' possibly referring to tobacco cultivated on the island.
