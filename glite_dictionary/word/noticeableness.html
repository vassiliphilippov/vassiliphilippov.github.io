noticeableness

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊ.tɪ.sə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ.tɪ.sə.bəl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being easily seen, recognized, or understood
quality of being noticeable

Examples

The noticeableness of the stain on his shirt made him feel self-conscious throughout the meeting.
What struck me most was the noticeableness of her absence at the event.
The changes were implemented with such noticeableness that everyone quickly recognized the improvements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prominence - the state of being important or easily noticed
  • Synonym: conspicuousness - the quality of being very easy to see or notice
  • Antonym: obscurity - the state of being difficult to see or understand

Etymology

From 'noticeable' + '-ness' (forming nouns denoting a state or quality).
