Umma

1. NOUN

/ ˈʊmə / (AmE), / ˈʊmə / (BrE)
The whole community of Muslims bound together by ties of religion, extending across nations.
the global Muslim community

Examples

The Umma represents a spiritual union among Muslims worldwide.
Debate exists within the Umma on matters of religious interpretation.
Leaders called on the Umma to work together for peace.

Synset Examples

moderate Muslims urge the Ummah to reject the terrorism of radical Muslims

Etymology

From Arabic *ummah* (community, nation, people).
