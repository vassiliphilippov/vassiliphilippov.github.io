airbus

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌbʌs / (AmE), / ˈeəˌbʌs / (BrE)
a large subsonic jet airliner designed for carrying passengers over short or medium distances
large short-haul jet airliner

Examples

The airline uses an airbus for its busy city-to-city routes.
We boarded the airbus for our flight to Madrid.
Modern airbuses are known for their fuel efficiency and comfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jetliner - a jet-propelled airliner
