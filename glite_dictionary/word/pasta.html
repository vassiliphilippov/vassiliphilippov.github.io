pasta

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːstə / (AmE), / ˈpɑːstə / (BrE)
A dish consisting mainly of noodles made from wheat flour and water, often served with a sauce.
Italian noodle-based dish

Examples

I cooked some pasta for dinner with tomato sauce and cheese.
Her favorite pasta is spaghetti with meatballs.
Many Italian restaurants offer a wide selection of pasta dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noodles - Long, thin strips of pasta or similar dough-based food

Etymology

From Italian 'pasta', from Latin 'pasta' (dough, paste).

2. NOUN

/ ˈpɑstə / (AmE), / ˈpæstə / (BrE)
A type of food made from unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, formed into various shapes and typically boiled.
Italian flour-based food, often in shapes

Examples

We made homemade pasta for dinner last night, and it tasted delicious.
She prefers whole wheat pasta because it's healthier than the regular kind.
The restaurant serves a wide variety of pasta dishes, including lasagna and ravioli.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noodle - Long, thin strip of pasta or similar dough

Etymology

From Italian 'pasta', from Late Latin 'pasta' (dough, pastry cake), from Greek 'pastē' (barley porridge).
