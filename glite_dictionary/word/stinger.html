Stinger

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋə / (BrE)
a portable surface-to-air missile system using infrared guidance and an impact fuse, designed to be fired from the shoulder at low-flying aircraft
portable shoulder-fired missile system

Examples

The soldiers were equipped with a Stinger to defend against enemy helicopters.
A single Stinger missile can pose a serious threat to low-flying aircraft.
After the demonstration, the trainees learned how to operate a Stinger safely.

Etymology

Named after the stinger of an insect, emphasizing its ability to 'sting' or attack with precision.
