statice

1. NOUN

/ ˈstætɪsi / (AmE), / ˈstatɪsi / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Limonium, found in temperate salt marshes and known for its spikes of white or mauve flowers.
plant of the Limonium genus

Examples

The florist included several stems of statice in the bouquet for added color and texture.
Statice thrives in salt marshes and coastal regions where few other flowers grow.
She dried a bunch of statice to use in her homemade floral arrangements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Limonium - the botanical genus including plants known as statice

Etymology

From New Latin genus name Statice, from Greek statikē (a kind of plant).
