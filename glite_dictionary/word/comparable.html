comparable

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑːmp(ə)rəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒmp(ə)rəb(ə)l / (BrE)
able to be compared or worthy of being compared, especially in value, amount, or quality
able to be compared

Examples

The two artists are hardly comparable in terms of style and influence.
This year's profits are comparable to those of last year.
Few athletes are comparable to her when it comes to determination and skill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: similar - resembling without being identical
  • Antonym: incomparable - so good or unusual as to be impossible to be compared; without equal

Etymology

From Late Latin comparabilis ‘that can be compared’, from Latin comparare ‘to compare’.
