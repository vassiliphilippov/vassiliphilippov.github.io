supermom

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuː.pɚ.mɑm / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pə.mɒm / (BrE)
an informal term for a mother who manages both childcare and a full-time job with apparent ease
a mother juggling job and childcare
informal american

Examples

My sister is a true supermom, balancing three kids, a full-time job, and volunteering every weekend.
People often wonder how a supermom manages to keep up with everything without breaking a sweat.
It's not easy being a supermom, but she makes it look effortless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: superwoman - a woman who manages several demanding roles exceptionally well

Etymology

Blend of 'super-' and 'mom', popularized in the late 20th century in North America.
