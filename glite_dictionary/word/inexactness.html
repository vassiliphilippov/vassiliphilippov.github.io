inexactness

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.ɪɡˈzækt.nəs / (AmE), / ˌɪn.ɪɡˈzæk.tnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being not exact; lack of precision or accuracy.
lack of exactness or precision

Examples

The inexactness of his measurements led to errors in the final calculation.
Critics pointed out the inexactness in her analysis of the data.
Because of the inexactness in the translation, some meaning was lost.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: precision - the quality of being exact
  • Synonym: inaccuracy - lack of accuracy or correctness

Etymology

From in- (not) + exact + -ness (noun-forming suffix).
