poophead

1. NOUN

/ ˈpuːpˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈpuːpˌhɛd / (BrE)
an insulting term, usually humorous or childish, for someone who is behaving foolishly or annoyingly
childish insult for a foolish person
informal slang

Examples

Don't be such a poophead and just apologize.
My little brother called me a poophead when I wouldn't share my toys.
Even though he acted like a poophead, we couldn't stay mad at him for long.

Synset Examples

Stop acting like a poohead and help us!
Stop acting like a poophead and just listen.

Etymology

Formed from 'poop' (slang for feces) + 'head'.
