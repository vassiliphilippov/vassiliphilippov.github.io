establishment

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈstæblɪʃmənt / (AmE), / ɪˈstæblɪʃmənt / (BrE)
the act of starting or founding something, such as an organization, system, or institution
the act of founding or creating

Examples

The establishment of the company transformed the local economy.
His career began with the establishment of his own medical practice.
The establishment of diplomatic relations took several months of negotiation.

Synset Examples

the constitution of a PTA group last year
it was the establishment of his reputation
he still remembers the organization of the club

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foundation - the act of founding, especially of an organization or institution
  • Antonym: dissolution - the act of officially ending or breaking up an organization

Etymology

From Old French *estableissement*, from *establir* (to establish).
