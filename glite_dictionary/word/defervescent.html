defervescent

1. ADJ

/ ˌdiːfərˈvɛsənt / (AmE), / ˌdiːfəˈvɛsənt / (BrE)
relating to the reduction or abatement of fever
pertaining to reducing fever
medical

Examples

The patient entered the defervescent phase after several days of high fever.
Doctors monitored his defervescent response to the medication closely.
A defervescent state suggests improvement in cases of viral infection.

Etymology

From Latin defervescere (to cease boiling or being hot).
