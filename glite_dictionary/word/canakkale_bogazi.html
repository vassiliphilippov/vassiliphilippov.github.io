Canakkale Bogazi

1. NOUN

/ tʃaˈnɑːkɑːleɪ boʊˈɑːzɪ / (AmE), / tʃaˈnækɑːleɪ bəˈɡɑːzi / (BrE)
the strait connecting the Aegean Sea and the Sea of Marmara, separating European and Asian Turkey; also known as the Dardanelles
strait separating European and Asian Turkey

Examples

Naval battles have taken place in the Canakkale Bogazi throughout history.
The Canakkale Bogazi is a vital waterway for international shipping routes.
Many travelers take ferries across the Canakkale Bogazi when visiting Turkey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dardanelles - the strait between the Aegean and the Sea of Marmara separating European and Asian Turkey

Etymology

Turkish, meaning 'Çanakkale Strait'.
