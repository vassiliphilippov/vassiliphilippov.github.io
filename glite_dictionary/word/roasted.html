roasted

1. ADJ

/ ˈroʊstɪd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊstɪd / (BrE)
(of food, especially meat) cooked by dry heat, typically in an oven or over a fire.
cooked by dry heat in an oven

Examples

We enjoyed a delicious roasted chicken for dinner last night.
The chef served roasted vegetables alongside the main course.
She prefers her coffee beans roasted to a dark finish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grilled - (of food) cooked over direct heat or on a grill
  • Antonym: boiled - (of food) cooked in hot water

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'roast', from Old French 'rostir', of Germanic origin.
