decade

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛˌkeɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɛk.eɪd / (BrE)
A period of ten years.
ten-year period

Examples

The city has changed dramatically over the past decade.
She spent a decade working on that novel before publishing it.
Trends in technology often shift rapidly from one decade to the next.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: century - a period of 100 years
  • Degree Lower: year - a period of 12 months or 365/366 days

Etymology

From Latin 'decas' (group of ten) via French 'décade'.
