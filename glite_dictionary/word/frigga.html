Frigga

1. PROPN

/ ˈfrɪɡə / (AmE), / ˈfrɪɡə / (BrE)
In Norse mythology, the goddess of the heavens and married love; wife of Odin.
Norse goddess, wife of Odin

Examples

According to legend, Frigga weaves the clouds in the sky.
Some historians believe that Friday is named after Frigga.
In many stories, Frigga is depicted as the motherly protector of families.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Odin - chief god in Norse mythology, husband of Frigga

Etymology

From Old Norse 'Frigg', meaning 'beloved'.
