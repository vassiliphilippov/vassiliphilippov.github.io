Orbignya

1. NOUN

/ ɔrˈbɪɡniə / (AmE), / ɔːˈbɪɡniə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical palms native to southern Mexico and northern South America, commonly known as the babassu palm
a genus of tropical palms (babassu palm)

Examples

The Orbignya genus provides essential resources for communities in Brazil.
Oil extracted from the nuts of Orbignya is widely used in cosmetics and cooking.
Researchers are studying Orbignya palms for their role in rainforest ecosystems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: babassu - a type of palm tree known for its edible nuts and oil

Etymology

Named after French naturalist Alcide d'Orbigny.
