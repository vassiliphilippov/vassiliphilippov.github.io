yellow-banded

1. ADJ

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˌbændɪd / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˌbændɪd / (BrE)
Having yellow bands or stripes, especially referring to animals or plants.
having yellow bands or stripes

Examples

The yellow-banded bee is easily identified by the bright stripes on its abdomen.
Some frogs are known for their distinctive yellow-banded legs.
We observed a rare yellow-banded butterfly in the tropical rainforest.
