skillet

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪlɪt / (AmE), / ˈskɪlɪt / (BrE)
a frying pan; a shallow pan with a long handle, used for frying or sautéing food
shallow pan for frying
american

Examples

She heated some oil in a skillet before adding the vegetables.
Use a non-stick skillet to make perfect pancakes every time.
He bought a new cast-iron skillet for camping trips.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frying pan - a pan used for frying food
  • Antonym: saucepan - a deep pan with a handle, used for cooking food in water or other liquids

Etymology

From Middle English *skelet*, from Old French *escuelette* (little dish), diminutive of *escuelle* (bowl).
