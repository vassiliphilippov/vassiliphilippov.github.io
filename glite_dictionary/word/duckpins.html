duckpins

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌkˌpɪnz / (AmE), / ˈdʌkˌpɪnz / (BrE)
A type of bowling game using smaller, squat pins that are shorter and wider than standard tenpins.
bowling game with small squat pins

Examples

He has played duckpins since he was a child growing up in Baltimore.
The alley offers both tenpins and duckpins for bowlers of all ages.
Unlike traditional bowling, duckpins require a different technique and a smaller ball.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tenpins - standard bowling game with larger, slimmer pins

Etymology

Probably from the resemblance of the small pins to ducks.
