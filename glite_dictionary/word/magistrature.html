magistrature

1. NOUN

/ ˈmædʒɪˌstreɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈmædʒɪˌstreɪtʃə / (BrE)

the office or authority of a magistrate

Examples

He aspired to enter the magistrature after completing his studies in law.

Related Terms

Etymology

From French 'magistrature', from Latin 'magistratus' (magistrate, office).
