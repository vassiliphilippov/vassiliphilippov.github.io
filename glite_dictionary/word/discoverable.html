discoverable

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈskʌv.ɚ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / dɪˈskʌv.ər.ə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be discovered, ascertained, or found out; possible to uncover or learn about
able to be discovered or found out

Examples

After months of research, the lost documents were finally discoverable in the archive.
The missing data might not be immediately discoverable, but advanced analysis could reveal it.
The court ruled that all relevant emails are discoverable by the opposing counsel.

Synset Examples

ascertainable facts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ascertainable - able to be found out or determined with certainty
  • Antonym: undiscoverable - impossible to be discovered or found out

Etymology

From 'discover' + '-able', from Old French 'descovrir' and Latin 'dis-' + 'cooperire' (to uncover).
