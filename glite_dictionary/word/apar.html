apar

1. NOUN

/ əˈpɑr / (AmE), / əˈpɑː / (BrE)
A South American armadillo species characterized by three bands of bony plates.
three-banded South American armadillo

Examples

The apar is known for its distinctive three-banded shell.
Researchers observed the apar in its natural habitat in the Amazon rainforest.
Unlike other armadillos, the apar can curl itself into a tight ball for protection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armadillo - a burrowing nocturnal mammal with a leathery armored shell
