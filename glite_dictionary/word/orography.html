orography

1. NOUN

/ ɔːˈrɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / ɒˈrɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the scientific study of the physical features, formation, and distribution of mountains
study of mountains' physical features

Examples

Her research in orography has contributed to our understanding of mountain formation.
The textbook includes a detailed section on the orography of the Himalayas.
Advancements in orography are important for studying climate patterns in mountainous regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geomorphology - the scientific study of landforms and the processes that shape them

Etymology

From Greek 'oros' (mountain) + '-graphy' (writing, description).
