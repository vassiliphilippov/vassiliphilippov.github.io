Clorox

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɔːrɑks / (AmE), / ˈklɔːrɒks / (BrE)
A trademarked brand of commercial bleach commonly used for cleaning and disinfecting.
brand of commercial bleach

Examples

She used Clorox to remove the stains from her white shirt.
Make sure to wear gloves when handling Clorox or any other strong bleach.
My mother always keeps a bottle of Clorox under the kitchen sink for cleaning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bleach - a chemical used to whiten or disinfect, especially in cleaning
