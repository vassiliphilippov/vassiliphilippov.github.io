unalike

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈlaɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈlaɪk / (BrE)
not alike; different from each other.
not alike; different

Examples

Although they are twins, their personalities are remarkably unalike.
The two paintings were so unalike that no one guessed they were by the same artist.
Their interests in music and art are completely unalike.

Synset Examples

as unalike as two people could be

Related Terms

  • Synonym: different - not the same as another or each other
  • Antonym: alike - similar to each other

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'alike'.
