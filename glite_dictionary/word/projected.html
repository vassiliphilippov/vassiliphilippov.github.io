projected

1. ADJ

/ prəˈdʒɛktɪd / (AmE), / prəˈdʒɛktɪd / (BrE)
extending out above or beyond a surface or boundary; protruding or jutting outward.
extending out beyond a surface

Examples

The projected roof provided shade for the entire patio.
Her projected cheekbones give her face a distinctive look.
The architect designed projected balconies for the new apartment complex.

Synset Examples

the jutting limb of a tree
massive projected buttresses
his protruding ribs
a pile of boards sticking over the end of his truck

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protruding - sticking out from a surface
  • Antonym: receding - moving or sloping back from a surface

Etymology

From Latin projectus, past participle of proicere (to throw forth).
