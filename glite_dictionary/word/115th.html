115th

1. ADJ

/ wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈfɪfti̩ŋθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈfɪftiːəθ / (BrE)
being the ordinal number corresponding to one hundred fifteen in a sequence; coming after the 114th and before the 116th.
ordinal number: one hundred fifteen

Examples

She finished in 115th place out of over five hundred participants.
The 115th anniversary of the school was celebrated with a grand ceremony.
After reading the 115th page, he closed the book for the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one hundred fifteenth - the ordinal number 115th, written in words
