diverting

1. ADJ

/ dəˈvɜrtɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈvɜːtɪŋ / (BrE)
providing amusement or entertainment; pleasantly engaging
pleasantly entertaining; amusing

Examples

The movie was surprisingly diverting and kept the audience engaged throughout.
She told a diverting tale that had everyone laughing at dinner.
I found the book both informative and diverting, making it an enjoyable read.

Synset Examples

an amusing speaker
a diverting story

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amusing - causing laughter or enjoyment
  • Synonym: entertaining - holding attention pleasantly or agreeably
  • Antonym: boring - not interesting; dull

Etymology

From the verb 'divert', itself from Latin 'divertere' (to turn aside).
