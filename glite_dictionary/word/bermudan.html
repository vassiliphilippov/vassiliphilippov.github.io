Bermudan

1. ADJ

/ bərˈmjudn̩ / (AmE), / bəˈmjuːdən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Bermuda or its people
from or relating to Bermuda

Examples

We spent a week relaxing on the beautiful Bermudan beaches.
The restaurant specializes in traditional Bermudan cuisine.
She admired the vibrant colors of the Bermudan houses during her visit.

Synset Examples

Bermudan beaches

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bermudian - relating to Bermuda or its inhabitants (variant spelling)

2. NOUN

/ bərˈmjuːdən / (AmE), / bəˈmjuːdən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Bermuda
a person from Bermuda

Examples

A famous Bermudan artist will be exhibiting her work in New York next month.
The team included a Bermudan among its international players.
She learned about traditional music from a local Bermudan during her visit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bermudian - a native or inhabitant of Bermuda; alternate form

Etymology

Formed from 'Bermuda' + English suffix '-an' (indicating nationality or origin).
