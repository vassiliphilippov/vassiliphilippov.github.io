Murmansk

1. PROPN

/ mʊrˈmænsk / (AmE), / mʊəˈmænsk / (BrE)
A port city in northwestern Russia on the Kola Peninsula, notable as the largest city north of the Arctic Circle and for its significance during the World Wars.
port city in northwestern Russia, above Arctic Circle

Examples

The port of Murmansk remains ice-free even during the harsh Arctic winters.
During World War II, Murmansk was a vital supply route for Allied aid to the Soviet Union.
She traveled above the Arctic Circle to visit Murmansk and witness the midnight sun.
