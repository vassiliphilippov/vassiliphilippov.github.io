Kiliwa

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiːliˌwɑ / (AmE), / ˈkiːliˌwɑ / (BrE)
the Yuman language spoken by the Kiliwa people of Baja California, Mexico
Yuman language of the Kiliwa people

Examples

She is studying the Kiliwa language to preserve her ancestral heritage.
Only a few elders still speak Kiliwa fluently in the community.
Linguists are working to document Kiliwa before it becomes extinct.

2. NOUN

/ kiˈliːwə / (AmE), / kiˈliːwə / (BrE)
A member of an indigenous people living in northern Baja California, Mexico.
member of an indigenous people of Baja California

Examples

Anthropologists have studied the traditions of the Kiliwa for decades.
The Kiliwa continue to preserve many aspects of their ancestral culture.
Several Kiliwa elders attended the cultural heritage conference last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kiliwa language - the language spoken by the Kiliwa people
