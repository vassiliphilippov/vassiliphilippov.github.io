Florida

1. PROPN

/ ˈflɔːrɪdə / (AmE), / ˈflɒrɪdə / (BrE)
a state in the southeastern United States, located between the Atlantic Ocean and the Gulf of Mexico
a state in the southeastern United States

Examples

We spent our summer vacation in Florida, enjoying the beaches and warm weather.
Many retirees choose to move to Florida for its mild winters and sunshine.
Florida is famous for its theme parks, including Walt Disney World and Universal Studios.

Etymology

From Spanish 'florida' meaning 'flowery'; named by explorer Juan Ponce de León in 1513.
