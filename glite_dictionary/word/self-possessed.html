self-possessed

1. ADJ

/ sɛlf pəˈzɛst / (AmE), / sɛlf pəˈzɛst / (BrE)
Calm, confident, and in control of one's feelings, especially in difficult situations.
calm and confident, in control of oneself

Examples

Despite the chaos around her, she remained self-possessed throughout the meeting.
The self-possessed manner of the manager helped calm the anxious staff.
He answered all the difficult questions in a self-possessed way.

Synset Examples

the witness remained collected throughout the cross-examination
perfectly poised and sure of himself
more self-contained and more dependable than many of the early frontiersmen
strong and self-possessed in the face of trouble

Related Terms

  • Synonym: composed - calm and in control of oneself
  • Antonym: nervous - easily agitated or distressed

Etymology

Formed from 'self-' + 'possessed' (having control).
