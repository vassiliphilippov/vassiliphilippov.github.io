Monet

1. PROPN

/ moʊˈneɪ / (AmE), / mɒˈneɪ / (BrE)
Claude Monet (1840–1926), a French impressionist painter known for his pioneering work in capturing light and atmosphere in landscape painting.
French impressionist painter

Examples

The paintings of Monet often depict beautiful gardens and water lilies.
Many art lovers visit museums to admire the works of Monet.
Monet's impressionist style transformed the course of modern art.

Etymology

From the French surname Monet.
