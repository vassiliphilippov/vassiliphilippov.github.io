ogdoad

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑɡ.doʊ.æd / (AmE), / ˈɒɡ.dəʊ.ad / (BrE)
The number eight; a group or set of eight.
the number eight or a group of eight
archaic

Examples

The ancient myth described an ogdoad of primordial deities.
An ogdoad of cells appeared after the eighth division in the embryo.
Each section of the artwork featured an ogdoad of identical patterns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: octad - a group or set of eight

Etymology

From Greek *ogdoas*, *ogdoados* (the number eight), from *ogdoos* (eighth).
