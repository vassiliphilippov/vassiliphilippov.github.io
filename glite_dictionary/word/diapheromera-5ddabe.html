Diapheromera

1. NOUN

/ daɪˌæfərˈoʊmərə / (AmE), / daɪˌafərˈɒmərə / (BrE)
a genus of stick insects in the family Phasmidae, native to North America, which includes the common walkingstick.
a genus of North American stick insects

Examples

The Diapheromera genus includes several species of walkingstick insects found in North America.
Researchers studied the behavior of Diapheromera in their natural woodland habitat.
The most common member of the Diapheromera genus is the Northern walkingstick.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin Diapheromera, coined for taxonomic classification.
