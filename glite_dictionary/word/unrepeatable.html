unrepeatable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈpiː.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈpiː.tə.bəl / (BrE)
not able or suitable to be repeated or quoted, often because it is inappropriate or unique
not able to be repeated or quoted

Examples

He told an unrepeatable joke that made everyone laugh.
There are unrepeatable remarks in the transcript that cannot be shared publicly.
Her reaction was so outrageous and unrepeatable that it stunned the audience.

Synset Examples

what he said was funny but unquotable

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unquotable - not suitable or able to be quoted
  • Antonym: repeatable - able or suitable to be repeated

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈpiː.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈpiː.tə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be repeated; unique or occurring only once.
not able to be repeated; unique

Examples

The artist’s performance was truly unrepeatable, making every show a special experience.
Each sunrise is unrepeatable, even if they seem the same at first glance.
Some moments are so extraordinary and unrepeatable that they stay with us forever.

Synset Examples

dogs and mice and flies are as unrepeatable as men are

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unique - being the only one of its kind; unlike anything else

Etymology

From un- (not) + repeat + -able.
