Acapulco de Juarez

1. PROPN

/ ˌɑːkəˈpʊlkoʊ deɪ ˈhwɑːreɪz / (AmE), / ˌakəˈpʊlkoʊ deɪ ˈhwɑːreɪz / (BrE)
a port and popular resort city on the Pacific coast of southern Mexico, famous for its beaches and water sports
Mexican port and resort city

Examples

Many travelers visit Acapulco de Juarez each year for its vibrant nightlife and sunny beaches.
We booked a week-long vacation in Acapulco de Juarez to relax by the ocean.
Acapulco de Juarez is renowned for its spectacular cliff divers and luxury resorts.

Etymology

From Nahuatl *Acapolco* ('place of reeds') and Spanish, named after Benito Juárez.
