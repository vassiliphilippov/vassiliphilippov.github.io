promptness

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑmpt.nəs / (AmE), / ˈprɒmpt.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of acting or arriving quickly and without delay
quality of acting quickly

Examples

Her promptness in responding to emails impressed her clients.
We appreciate your promptness in settling the bill.
The promptness of the emergency services saved many lives.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lateness - the state of being late or delayed
  • Synonym: punctuality - the quality of being on time

Etymology

From Latin promptus (ready, quick).

2. NOUN

/ ˈprɑmpt.nəs / (AmE), / ˈprɒmpt.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of doing something at the agreed or expected time; punctuality
the quality of being on time

Examples

His promptness in replying to emails impressed his employer.
The promptness of the delivery exceeded our expectations.
Customers appreciate the promptness with which complaints are handled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punctuality - the fact or quality of being on time
  • Antonym: tardiness - lateness in happening or arriving

Etymology

From Latin promptus (ready, quick), through Old French prompt and English -ness suffix.
