slender-bodied

1. ADJ

/ ˈslɛndɚ ˌbɑdid / (AmE), / ˈslɛndə ˌbɒdid / (BrE)
Having a slim or narrow body shape.
having a slim body

Examples

The slender-bodied snake moved swiftly through the grass.
Many fish in this region are known for their slender-bodied appearance.
She admired the slender-bodied dragonflies hovering above the pond.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slim-bodied - having a slim body shape
  • Antonym: stout-bodied - having a thick or heavy body
