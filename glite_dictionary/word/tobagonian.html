Tobagonian

1. NOUN

/ toʊˌbeɪˈɡoʊniən / (AmE), / təʊˌbeɪˈɡəʊniən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the island of Tobago in the West Indies
person from Tobago (West Indies)

Examples

Many Tobagonians celebrate the island’s history and culture with pride.
The Tobagonian welcomed us warmly to his village.
Several Tobagonians have achieved recognition in international sports.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Trinidadian - a native or inhabitant of the island of Trinidad

Etymology

From 'Tobago' (the island) + English suffix '-ian' denoting origin or association.
