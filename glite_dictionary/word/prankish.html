prankish

1. ADJ

/ ˈpræŋkɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpræŋkɪʃ / (BrE)
fond of playing tricks or pranks in a naughty or mischievous way; annoying or playful in a way that resembles a prank.
inclined to play pranks; mischievous

Examples

The twins were known for their prankish sense of humor, always pulling tricks on their classmates.
With a prankish grin, he replaced all the pens in the office with crayons.
Her prankish spirit made the team laugh even during stressful moments.

Synset Examples

teasing and worrying with impish laughter
a wicked prank

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mischievous - causing trouble in a playful way
  • Synonym: playful - full of fun and games; lighthearted

Etymology

From 'prank' + '-ish', denoting having qualities of pranks.
