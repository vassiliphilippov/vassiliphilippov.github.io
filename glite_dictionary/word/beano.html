beano

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbiːnəʊ / (BrE)
A game in which numbered balls are drawn at random and players cover the corresponding numbers on their cards, similar to bingo.
a game similar to bingo

Examples

Every Friday night, the local club hosts a beano for its members.
She shouted with excitement as she won the big prize at the beano.
The community center organized a charity beano to raise funds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bingo - a game where players mark off numbers on a card as they are called out, aiming to complete a specific pattern

Etymology

Probably a diminutive of 'bingo'.
