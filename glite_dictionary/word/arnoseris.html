Arnoseris

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈnoʊsərɪs / (AmE), / ɑːˈnɒsərɪs / (BrE)
A small genus of flowering plants in the daisy family, known commonly as lamb succory.
a genus of flowering plants (lamb succory)

Examples

Arnoseris is a rarely cultivated plant found in sandy soils across Europe.
The yellow flowers of Arnoseris resemble those of dandelions but are much smaller.
Many botanists are interested in the unique leaf structure of Arnoseris.

Etymology

From Greek 'arnos' meaning 'lamb' and 'seris' meaning 'chicory'.
