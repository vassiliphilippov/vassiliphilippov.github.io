nonreciprocal

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑːn.rɪˈsɪp.rə.kəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈsɪp.rɪ.kəl / (BrE)
not involving mutual exchange or action; not reciprocal.
not reciprocal; lacking mutual exchange

Examples

Their relationship was strictly nonreciprocal, with one person giving much more than the other.
Some electronic devices are designed to allow nonreciprocal signal flow in one direction only.
His nonreciprocal attitude made cooperation difficult.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: reciprocal - given, felt, or done in return; mutual

Etymology

Formed from 'non-' (not) + 'reciprocal' (from Latin reciprocus).
