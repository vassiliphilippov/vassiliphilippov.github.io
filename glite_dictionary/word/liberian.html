Liberian

1. ADJ

/ laɪˈbɪriən / (AmE), / laɪˈbɪəriən / (BrE)
relating to Liberia or its people, language, or culture
of or relating to Liberia or its people

Examples

The Liberian government issued a new statement regarding trade policies.
She enjoys learning about Liberian history and traditions.
Many Liberian students study abroad to pursue higher education.

Synset Examples

Liberian ship owners

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Liberia - a country in West Africa

Etymology

From Liberia (country in West Africa) + -an.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03092434-a
Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ lɪˈbɪriən / (AmE), / lɪˈbɪəriən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Liberia, a country in West Africa.
a person from Liberia

Examples

The new ambassador is a proud Liberian representing her country abroad.
Many Liberians celebrate Independence Day with parades and cultural events.
As a Liberian, he is eager to share his nation's history with visitors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Liberian - relating to Liberia or its people (adjective form)

Etymology

From Liberia + -an, denoting nationality or inhabitant.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09740238-n
Synset POS: n
