unlubricated

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈluː.brɪ.keɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈluː.brɪ.keɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
Not provided with or treated by lubrication; lacking lubricant.
not lubricated

Examples

Running the engine while it is unlubricated can cause serious damage to its components.
The machine made a loud noise because the gears were completely unlubricated.
Be sure not to operate any unlubricated parts to prevent unnecessary wear.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lubricated - coated or treated with lubricant

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'lubricated' (past participle of lubricate, from Latin lubricare).
