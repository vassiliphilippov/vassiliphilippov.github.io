to reawaken

1. VERB

/ ˌriːəˈweɪkən / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈweɪkən / (BrE)
to become awake or aware again; to arouse again from sleep, inactivity, or indifference.
become awake or aware again

Examples

The old memories began to reawaken as she returned to her childhood home.
A sense of hope can reawaken even after years of disappointment.
The spring sunlight helped the plants reawaken after the long, cold winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to awaken - to wake up or make someone wake up

Etymology

From re- (again) + awaken (to wake).
