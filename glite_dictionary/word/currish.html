/ ˈkɜr.ɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkʌr.ɪʃ / (BrE)
(of a person or their behavior) resembling a cur; snarling, rude, or contemptible.
snarling, rude, or contemptible
archaic

Examples

The baron's currish remarks offended everyone at the banquet.
She refused to engage with his currish behavior, maintaining her composure.
His response was so currish that it shocked even his closest friends.

Related Terms
