accepted

1. ADJ

/ əkˈsɛptɪd / (AmE), / əkˈsɛptɪd / (BrE)
widely approved or generally recognized as valid, suitable, or correct
widely approved or recognized

Examples

This is an accepted theory in the scientific community.
Her methods are now accepted in top medical journals.
It is the accepted practice to start the meeting at nine o'clock.
