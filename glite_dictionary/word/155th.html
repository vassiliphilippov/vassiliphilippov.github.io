155th

1. ADJ

/ wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈfɪfti fɪfθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈfɪfti fɪfθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number corresponding to one hundred and fifty-five in a sequence; coming after the 154th and before the 156th.
