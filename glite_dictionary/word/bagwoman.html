bagwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæɡˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈbæɡˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman, especially one involved in organized crime, responsible for collecting or distributing illicit payoff money
female collector/distributor of payoff money

Examples

The police arrested the bagwoman as she was delivering cash to the syndicate's leader.
Rumors spread that a new bagwoman had replaced the previous collector after the police raid.
The accused claimed she was just a messenger and not the notorious bagwoman the authorities sought.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bagman - a man who collects or distributes illicit payoff money

Etymology

Formed from 'bag' (referring to the money bag) + 'woman', modeled after 'bagman'.
