Branta bernicla

1. NOUN

/ ˈbræntə bərˈnaɪklə / (AmE), / ˈbræntə bəˈnɪklə / (BrE)
A species of small dark-bellied wild goose known as the brant or brant goose, found in artic and temperate coastal areas.
species of small wild goose (brant goose)

Examples

The Branta bernicla migrates vast distances between its arctic breeding grounds and temperate coastlines.
Birdwatchers were thrilled to spot a rare juvenile Branta bernicla among the flock.
Due to habitat loss, conservationists are monitoring the population of Branta bernicla closely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brant - a species of small dark-bellied wild goose

Etymology

From Latin: Branta (a genus of geese) + bernicla (barnacle goose).
