rotationally

1. ADV

/ roʊˈteɪʃənəli / (AmE), / rəʊˈteɪʃənəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or involves rotation.
in a rotational manner

Examples

The wheels must be aligned rotationally to ensure proper function.
The object can move both linearly and rotationally under the applied forces.
The platform spins rotationally at a constant speed during the experiment.

Synset Examples

the required influence lines are found by subjecting the model to small displacements horizontally, vertically and rotationally

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rotational - relating to rotation

Etymology

From 'rotational' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
