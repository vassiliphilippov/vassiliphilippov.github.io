Goniopteris

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡoʊ.niˈɑːp.tə.rɪs / (AmE), / ˌɡəʊ.niˈɒp.tər.ɪs / (BrE)
A genus of terrestrial ferns found in Florida, the West Indies, Central, and South America.
genus of terrestrial ferns

Examples

Botanists in Florida have documented several rare species of Goniopteris in local forests.
The delicate fronds of Goniopteris make it a favorite among tropical fern collectors.
Recent studies suggest that climate change may impact the distribution of Goniopteris in South America.

Etymology

From Greek 'gonio-' (angle) and 'pteris' (fern).
