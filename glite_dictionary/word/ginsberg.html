Ginsberg

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡɪnzˌbɜrɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪnzbɜːɡ / (BrE)
Allen Ginsberg (1926–1997), an influential American poet associated with the Beat Generation, known for works such as 'Howl'.
American poet of the Beat Generation

Examples

Allen Ginsberg was a leading figure in the American counterculture of the 1950s.
The poem 'Howl' by Ginsberg is considered a landmark in American literature.
Many students analyze the works of Ginsberg in modern poetry classes.
