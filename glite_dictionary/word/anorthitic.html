anorthitic

1. ADJ

/ əˌnɔrˈθɪtɪk / (AmE), / əˌnɔːˈθɪtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of anorthite, a type of feldspar mineral.
relating to anorthite (a feldspar mineral)

Examples

The rock sample displayed a distinctly anorthitic composition under microscopic examination.
Geologists often study anorthitic plagioclase to learn about Earth's crust formation.
The anorthitic luster of some lunar rocks suggests they contain calcium-rich feldspar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anorthite - a type of feldspar mineral, specifically a calcium aluminum silicate

Etymology

From anorthite + -ic, forming an adjective meaning 'of or relating to anorthite'.
