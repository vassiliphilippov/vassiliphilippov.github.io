choad

1. NOUN

/ tʃoʊd / (AmE), / tʃəʊd / (BrE)
a slang, derogatory term for a foolish or contemptible person
foolish or contemptible person (slang, derogatory)
slang offensive

Examples

Don't be a choad and mess up the plan for everyone.
Some people think calling someone a choad is just harmless teasing, but it can be really offensive.
He acted like a real choad during the entire meeting.

Synset Examples

He can be such a choad during meetings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person

  • Antonym: genius - a person of exceptional intelligence
