blinks

1. NOUN

/ blɪŋks / (AmE), / blɪŋks / (BrE)
A small annual plant (Montia fontana), also known as water chickweed or water blinks, found in northern regions and often eaten as a salad green.
small annual plant (Montia fontana)

Examples

In the Scottish Highlands, locals often forage for blinks to add a fresh touch to their salads.
After heavy rains, blinks thrive along the edges of streams and damp meadows.
Botanists studied how blinks adapted to the cooler climates of northern Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water chickweed - another name for blinks, the plant Montia fontana
