unoriginal

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnəˈrɪdʒənl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈrɪdʒənəl / (BrE)
Lacking originality; not new, fresh, or inventive.
not original or inventive

Examples

The critic found the novel disappointingly unoriginal and full of familiar tropes.
Her ideas for the project were rejected because they were considered too unoriginal.
The film was entertaining but ultimately felt unoriginal compared to similar movies in the genre.

Synset Examples

the manuscript contained unoriginal emendations
his life had been unoriginal, conforming completely to the given pattern

Related Terms

  • Antonym: original - new and creative; not derived from something else
  • Synonym: derivative - lacking originality; imitative of the work of others
