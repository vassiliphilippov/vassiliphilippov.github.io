uncrowded

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkraʊdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkraʊdɪd / (BrE)
having plenty of space; not filled with people or things
not crowded; roomy

Examples

The early morning bus was surprisingly uncrowded for a weekday.
With its uncrowded beaches, the island attracts those seeking peace and quiet.
They enjoyed an uncrowded restaurant where they could talk without distractions.

Synset Examples

an uncrowded train
an uncrowded view

Related Terms

  • Antonym: crowded - full of people or things; lacking space
