Bam

1. PROPN

/ bæm / (AmE), / bæm / (BrE)
an ancient city in southeastern Iran, known for its historical architecture and largely destroyed by an earthquake in 2003.
ancient city in Iran, destroyed in 2003 earthquake

Examples

The ancient city of Bam was famous for its mud-brick citadel.
After the devastating 2003 earthquake, Bam became a symbol of resilience and rebuilding.
Many tourists once visited Bam to admire its historic architecture.
