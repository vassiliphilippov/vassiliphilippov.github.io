personalised

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝː.sə.nə.laɪzd / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.sən.əl.aɪzd / (BrE)
designed or made to suit the needs, preferences, or identity of a particular person
made or adapted for a person
british

Examples

She received a personalised mug with her name printed on it.
The teacher gave each student personalised feedback on their assignments.
Many websites offer personalised recommendations based on your browsing history.

Synset Examples

personalized luggage
personalized advice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personalized - made for or directed or adjusted to a particular individual (American spelling)

Etymology

From 'personal' + '-ised', the British English variant; see also 'personalize' (American).
