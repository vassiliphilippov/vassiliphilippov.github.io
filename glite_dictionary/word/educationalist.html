educationalist

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛdʒuˈkeɪʃənəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃənəlɪst / (BrE)
A person who specializes in the theory and practice of education; an expert in educational methods and policy.
specialist in education theory and practice

Examples

The conference brought together leading educationalists from around the world to discuss new teaching methods.
As an educationalist, she has published several influential papers on curriculum development.
Many educationalists advocate for child-centered learning in early education.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: educator - someone who teaches or is involved in the practice of education

Etymology

From 'education' + '-alist', modeled after 'specialist'.
