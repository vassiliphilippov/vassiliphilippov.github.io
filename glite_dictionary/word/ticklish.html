ticklish

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɪklɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈtɪklɪʃ / (BrE)
requiring careful or delicate handling because of sensitivity or potential difficulty
requiring delicate or sensitive handling

Examples

Negotiating the merger was a ticklish process, given the competing interests involved.
She avoided addressing the ticklish issue during the meeting to prevent conflict.
It's always ticklish to discuss politics at family gatherings.

Synset Examples

delicate negotiations with the big powers
hesitates to be explicit on so ticklish a matter
a touchy subject

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delicate - requiring careful handling due to sensitivity
  • Synonym: touchy - easily upset or sensitive; requiring tact
