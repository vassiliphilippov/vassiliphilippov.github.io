spacewards

1. ADV

/ ˈspeɪs.wɚdz / (AmE), / ˈspeɪs.wədz / (BrE)
In the direction of outer space; towards or into outer space.
towards outer space

Examples

The rocket launched spacewards at dawn, leaving a fiery trail in the sky.
Satellites are routinely sent spacewards to study the universe far beyond our planet.
With each test flight, engineers hope to send their creations ever further spacewards.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: earthwards - towards the earth's surface

Etymology

Formed from 'space' + the directional suffix '-wards'.
