ratlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈrætˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈrætˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having characteristics of a rat, especially in appearance or behavior
resembling a rat

Examples

The thief's ratlike features made him instantly suspicious to the police.
Her ratlike behavior included sneaking through dark corridors and stealing food.
A ratlike shadow darted across the alley as the moonlight flickered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rodentlike - resembling a rodent
