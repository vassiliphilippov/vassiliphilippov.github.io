approach

1. NOUN

/ əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
an instance of being very similar to something, though not exactly the same; a close approximation
close approximation

Examples

This device offers the nearest approach to a perfect solution currently available.
Her response was an approach to what we expected, though not exact.
The project is an approach to achieving our sustainability goals.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: approximation - a thing that is similar to something else, but not exactly the same

Etymology

From Old French 'aprochier', from Late Latin 'appropiāre' (to come near).
