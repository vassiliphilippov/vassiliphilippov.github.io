pedagogically

1. ADV

/ ˌpɛdəˈɡɑdɪkli / (AmE), / ˌpɛdəˈɡɒdɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to the methods or principles of teaching.
in a manner relating to teaching methods

Examples

The curriculum was designed pedagogically to foster active student engagement.
She approaches every lesson pedagogically, carefully considering the learning outcomes.
The materials are organized pedagogically to guide students from basic to advanced concepts.

Synset Examples

this is a didactically sound method

Related Terms

  • Synonym: didactically - in a didactic manner; for instructional purposes

Etymology

From pedagogy, from Greek paidagōgikos (of teaching), ultimately from pais (child) + agōgos (guide).
