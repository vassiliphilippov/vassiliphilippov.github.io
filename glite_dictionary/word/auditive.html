auditive

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔː.dɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈɔː.dɪ.tɪv / (BrE)
relating to the sense or process of hearing.
relating to hearing

Examples

Researchers measured auditive responses to different frequencies of sound.
Her auditive perception has always been acute, making her an excellent musician.
The test is designed to evaluate children's auditive memory and comprehension skills.

Synset Examples

auditory processing
an audile person

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auditory - relating to the sense of hearing

Etymology

From Latin auditivus, from audire 'to hear'.
