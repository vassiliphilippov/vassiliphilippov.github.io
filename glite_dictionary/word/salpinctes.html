Salpinctes

1. NOUN

/ sælˈpɪŋtiːz / (AmE), / sælˈpɪŋtiːz / (BrE)
A genus of wrens (family Troglodytidae) that includes the rock wren, native to North and Central America.
genus of wrens (rock wren)

Examples

The rock wren is the sole member of the genus Salpinctes.
Many birdwatchers travel to the American Southwest to observe Salpinctes in its natural habitat.
The genus Salpinctes is distinguished by its unique song and foraging behavior.

Etymology

From Greek salpinktēs (trumpeter), likely referring to the bird's call.
