fanbase

1. NOUN

/ ˈfænˌbeɪs / (AmE), / ˈfanˌbeɪs / (BrE)
The collective group of enthusiastic supporters or devotees of a particular person, team, or thing.
group of devoted fans or supporters

Examples

The band has built an incredibly loyal fanbase over the past decade.
Her books have attracted a global fanbase that eagerly awaits each new release.
The football team's fanbase traveled from all over the country to see the final.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fans - people who enthusiastically support a person, team, or thing

Etymology

Formed from 'fan' (enthusiast or supporter) and 'base' (foundation or group).
