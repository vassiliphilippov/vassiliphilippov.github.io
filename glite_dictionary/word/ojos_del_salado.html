Ojos del Salado

1. PROPN

/ ˌoʊhɔs dɛl səˈlɑːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˌəʊhɒs dɛl səˈlɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
a volcano in the Andes on the Argentina-Chile border, noted as the world's highest active volcano
highest active volcano in the Andes

Examples

Climbers from around the world travel to Ojos del Salado to experience its extreme altitude.
At 6,893 meters, Ojos del Salado is renowned among mountaineers for its challenging conditions.
The border between Argentina and Chile runs through the summit of Ojos del Salado.

Etymology

Spanish for 'Eyes of the Salty [One]' (salado = salty), named for nearby salt lakes.
