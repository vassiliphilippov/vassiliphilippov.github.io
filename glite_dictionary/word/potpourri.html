potpourri

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɑːt.pʊˈriː / (AmE), / ˌpɒt.pʊˈriː / (BrE)
a mixture of dried flower petals and spices, usually placed in a decorative bowl or jar to perfume a room
mixture of dried flowers and spices for scent

Examples

I keep a bowl of potpourri on the table to freshen the room.
The scent of lavender potpourri filled the whole house.
She made her own potpourri from dried roses and spices.

Etymology

From French 'pot-pourri', literally 'rotten pot', referring to a mixture.

2. NOUN

/ ˌpoʊpʊˈri / (AmE), / ˌpɒpʊˈri / (BrE)
a musical composition made up of a collection of different songs or musical pieces, often taken from various sources and arranged as a continuous sequence
medley of musical pieces

Examples

The orchestra performed a potpourri of popular movie themes at the concert.
She arranged a potpourri for piano using melodies from different musicals.
Their latest album ends with a lively potpourri combining folk and jazz tunes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medley - a piece of music combining a number of different songs or tunes

Etymology

From French 'pot-pourri', literally 'rotten pot', in music describing a medley from the 18th century.

3. NOUN

/ ˌpoʊ.pʊˈri / (AmE), / ˌpɒ.pʊˈriː / (BrE)
A mixture or collection of various different items, often diverse or unrelated.
mixture of assorted items

Examples

The museum's new exhibit is a potpourri of art from various cultures and periods.
Her playlist offers a potpourri of music genres, from classical to hip-hop.
The festival featured a potpourri of activities, including games, food stalls, and performances.

Synset Examples

a great assortment of cars was on display
he had a variety of disorders
a veritable smorgasbord of religions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mixture - A combination of different things
  • Synonym: assortment - A collection of various things
  • Synonym: variety - The quality or state of being different or diverse

Etymology

From French 'pot-pourri' (literally 'rotten pot'), originally referring to a stew, later to a mixture of dried flowers and spices.
