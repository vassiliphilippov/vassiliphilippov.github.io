derriere

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɛriˈɛr / (AmE), / ˌdɛrɪˈɛə / (BrE)
the fleshy part of the human body that you sit on; buttocks
buttocks; rear part of the body

Examples

She slipped and landed right on her derriere in the middle of the sidewalk.
He spent the afternoon sitting on his derriere reading a book in the park.
The comedian made a joke about having a sore derriere after the long flight.
