carcinomatous

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑːrsəˈnoʊmətəs / (AmE), / ˌkɑːsɪnəˈməʊtəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of carcinoma (a type of cancer that begins in epithelial cells)
relating to carcinoma (a type of cancer)
medical

Examples

The biopsy revealed that the tissue was carcinomatous in nature.
Doctors monitored the carcinomatous changes in the patient's lungs over time.
A carcinomatous lesion requires immediate medical attention to prevent the spread of cancer.

Synset Examples

a carcinomatous lesion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carcinoma - a malignant tumor of epithelial origin

Etymology

From carcinoma and the suffix -ous, meaning 'pertaining to'.
