An Nafud

1. PROPN

/ æn nəˈfuːd / (AmE), / æn nəˈfuːd / (BrE)
a desert in northern Saudi Arabia, known for its red sand and strong winds
desert in northern Saudi Arabia

Examples

The An Nafud is famous for its vast stretches of red sand dunes.
Explorers have long been challenged by the harsh winds of the An Nafud.
Archaeological finds in the An Nafud indicate ancient human habitation in the region.

Etymology

From Arabic النفود‎ (an-nafūd), meaning 'the sandy desert'.
