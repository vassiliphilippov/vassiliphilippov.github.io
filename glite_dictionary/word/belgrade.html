Belgrade

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɛlˌɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɛlɡreɪd / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Serbia, located at the confluence of the Danube and Sava rivers
capital city of Serbia

Examples

During our trip to Serbia, we spent three days exploring Belgrade and its historic old town.
The Danube and Sava rivers meet at Belgrade, making it an important regional center for centuries.
Many cultural festivals are held annually in Belgrade, attracting visitors from across Europe.

Etymology

From Serbian 'Beograd', meaning 'white city', from 'beo' (white) + 'grad' (city).
