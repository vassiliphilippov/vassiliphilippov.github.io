snorer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnoʊrər / (AmE), / ˈsnɔːrə / (BrE)
a person who makes a loud breathing noise while sleeping
person who snores during sleep

Examples

My husband is such a snorer that I have to wear earplugs every night.
She complained to her roommate about being a loud snorer.
Doctors may recommend treatment if a snorer's breathing is interrupted during sleep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snore - to breathe noisily while sleeping

Etymology

Formed from 'snore' + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
