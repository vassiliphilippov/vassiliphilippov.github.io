earache

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪrˌeɪk / (AmE), / ˈɪəˌeɪk / (BrE)
pain or discomfort inside the ear, especially the middle or inner ear
pain or ache in the ear

Examples

She couldn't sleep because her earache was getting worse.
If your child has a persistent earache, you should consult a doctor.
After swimming, he developed an earache in his right ear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headache - pain in the head
  • Synonym: toothache - pain in or around a tooth

Etymology

From 'ear' + 'ache', used since the 16th century.
