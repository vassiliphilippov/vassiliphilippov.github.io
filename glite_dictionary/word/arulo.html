Arulo

1. PROPN

/ ɑːˈruːloʊ / (AmE), / ɑːˈruːləʊ / (BrE)
An artificial language designed for international use as an auxiliary language.
constructed international auxiliary language

Examples

Many linguists compare Arulo with Esperanto for its ease of learning and international focus.
The inventor of Arulo hoped it would overcome language barriers worldwide.
Members of the Arulo community gather annually to practice their language skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Esperanto - Most widely spoken constructed international auxiliary language
  • Synonym: Ido - Artificial language derived from Esperanto, intended for international use
