protanopic

1. ADJ

/ ˌproʊtəˈnɑːpɪk / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəˈnɒpɪk / (BrE)
Unable to see the color red or to distinguish red and bluish-green, due to a type of color blindness.
relating to red color blindness
medical

Examples

Because he is protanopic, he cannot tell the difference between red and certain shades of green.
The protanopic test results revealed a strong inability to perceive red hues.
Special design features help make websites more accessible for protanopic users.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: deuteranopic - unable to distinguish green from certain other colors due to green color blindness
  • Degree Higher: tritanopic - unable to distinguish blue from certain other colors due to blue color blindness

Etymology

From 'protanope' (a person with this condition) + '-ic', stemming from Greek 'protos' (first) referring to the first primary color, red.
