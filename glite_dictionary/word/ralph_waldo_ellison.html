Ralph Waldo Ellison

1. PROPN

/ rælf ˈwɔlˌdoʊ ˈɛlɪsən / (AmE), / rælf ˈwɔːldəʊ ˈɛlɪsən / (BrE)
(1914–1994) United States novelist best known for writing about the experiences of a young Black man in American society, most notably in his novel 'Invisible Man'.
American novelist, author of 'Invisible Man'

Examples

Ralph Waldo Ellison is celebrated for his groundbreaking novel 'Invisible Man'.
Many literary critics study the themes in Ralph Waldo Ellison's works.
The writings of Ralph Waldo Ellison continue to influence American literature.
