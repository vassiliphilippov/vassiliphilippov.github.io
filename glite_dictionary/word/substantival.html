substantival

1. ADJ

/ ˌsʌbstənˈtaɪvəl / (AmE), / ˌsʌbstənˈteɪvəl / (BrE)
relating to, having the nature of, or functioning as a substantive (noun or noun equivalent) in grammar or linguistics.
of or relating to a noun or substantive

Examples

The phrase acts as a substantival element within the sentence.
In some languages, adjectives may take on a substantival function.
His research focused on the substantival use of pronouns in Old English.

Synset Examples

a substantival constituent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: substantive - a noun or a word functioning as a noun

Etymology

From Medieval Latin substantivus (substantive) + -al.
