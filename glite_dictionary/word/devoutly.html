devoutly

1. ADV

/ dɪˈvaʊtli / (AmE), / dɪˈvaʊtli / (BrE)
In a way that shows deep religious feeling or commitment; with sincere devotion or earnestness.
in a deeply religious or earnest way

Examples

She prayed devoutly every morning before leaving her house.
He devoutly hoped that peace would soon return to the country.
Despite his busy schedule, he devoutly observed all the religious holidays.

Synset Examples

she was devoutly Catholic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piously - in a manner showing religious devotion
  • Antonym: irreverently - in a manner lacking proper respect, especially for something sacred

Etymology

From 'devout' (Middle English, from Old French *devot*, from Latin *devotus*, 'devoted').
