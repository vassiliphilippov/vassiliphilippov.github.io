tourist

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʊrɪst / (AmE), / ˈtʊərɪst / (BrE)
a person who travels to places for pleasure rather than for business or other reasons
person traveling for pleasure

Examples

The city is crowded with tourists during the summer months.
As a tourist, it's important to respect local customs and traditions.
Many tourists take guided tours to learn more about the history of the area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traveler - someone who travels, especially to distant or unfamiliar places
  • Antonym: local - a person who lives in a particular area

Etymology

From French *touriste*, from *tour* (a journey, tour).
