gigolo

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪɡəˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪɡələʊ / (BrE)
a man supported by a woman, especially in return for a sexual relationship
man financially supported by a woman, often sexually

Examples

He lived as a gigolo, relying on wealthy older women for support.
Many novels have portrayed the life of a gigolo in glamorous European cities.
He was rumored to be a gigolo, but he denied having relationships for money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: male escort - a man hired as a companion, sometimes with sexual implications

Etymology

From French gigolo (male companion, dancer), probably from gigue (dance).
