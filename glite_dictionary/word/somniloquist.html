somniloquist

1. NOUN

/ sɑmˈnɪləkwɪst / (AmE), / sɒmˈnɪləkwɪst / (BrE)
a person who talks while asleep
person who talks in their sleep

Examples

My brother is a somniloquist and often holds entire conversations while asleep.
The doctor explained that being a somniloquist is harmless but can be surprising for family members.
Have you ever met a somniloquist who reveals secrets in their sleep?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sleepwalker - a person who walks during sleep (somnambulist)

Etymology

From Latin 'somnus' (sleep) + 'loqui' (to speak), meaning 'sleep talker'.
