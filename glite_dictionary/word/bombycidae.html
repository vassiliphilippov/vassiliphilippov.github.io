Bombycidae

1. NOUN

/ bɑmˈbɪsɪˌdiː / (AmE), / bɒmˈbɪsɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of moths that includes the Chinese silkworm moth and related species.
family of moths including the silkworm

Examples

The Bombycidae family is best known for containing the Chinese silkworm moth.
Several species within the Bombycidae family are studied for their silk production.
Researchers identified a new species in the Bombycidae after sampling moths in the region.

Etymology

From New Latin Bombycidae, from Bombyx (the type genus) + -idae (family suffix).
