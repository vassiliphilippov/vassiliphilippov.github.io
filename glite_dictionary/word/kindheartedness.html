kindheartedness

1. NOUN

/ ˌkaɪndˈhɑrtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌkaɪndˈhɑːtɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being kind, sympathetic, and generous to others
quality of being kind and sympathetic

Examples

Her kindheartedness was evident in the way she comforted the lost child.
The community appreciated his kindheartedness during difficult times.
Acts of kindheartedness can make a real difference in people's lives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate

Etymology

Formed from 'kindhearted' + suffix '-ness' (state or quality).
