duplicatable

1. ADJ

/ ˌduːplɪˈkeɪtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌdjuːplɪˈkeɪtəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be duplicated; capable of being copied or reproduced exactly.
able to be duplicated

Examples

The process is fully duplicatable, ensuring consistent results every time.
Only duplicatable keys should be distributed to new staff members.
Making the experiment duplicatable is essential for scientific credibility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: replicable - able to be repeated or duplicated exactly
  • Antonym: unique - being the only one of its kind; not duplicatable

Etymology

From 'duplicate' + '-able'.
