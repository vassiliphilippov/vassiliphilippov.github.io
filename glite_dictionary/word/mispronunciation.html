mispronunciation

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪsprəˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsprəˌnʌnsɪˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or instance of pronouncing a word incorrectly
incorrect pronunciation

Examples

Her mispronunciation of 'quinoa' made everyone smile.
Repeated mispronunciation of common words can lead to misunderstandings.
He laughed at his own mispronunciation after hearing the correct form.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pronunciation - the way in which a word is pronounced

Etymology

Formed from 'mis-' (wrongly) + 'pronunciation', which is from Latin 'pronuntiatio'.
