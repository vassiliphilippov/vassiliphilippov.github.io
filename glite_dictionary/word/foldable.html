foldable

1. ADJ

/ ˈfoʊl.də.bəl / (AmE), / ˈfəʊl.də.bəl / (BrE)
(of an object) able to be folded up for easy storage or transport.
able to be folded

Examples

The foldable table fits perfectly in small apartments.
She bought a foldable bike for her daily commute.
Our new tent is lightweight and foldable, making it ideal for camping trips.

Synset Examples

a foldaway bed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collapsible - able to be folded or made smaller for storage

Etymology

From the verb 'fold' + suffix '-able', meaning able to be folded.
