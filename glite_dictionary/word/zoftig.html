zoftig

1. ADJ

/ ˈzɑf.tɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈzɒf.tɪɡ / (BrE)
(of a woman) having a full, rounded figure; healthily plump and attractive.
plump and attractive (of a woman)

Examples

The actress was celebrated for her zoftig charm and radiant smile.
He described the heroine as a zoftig beauty, both confident and graceful.
Vintage fashion often flatters a zoftig silhouette, highlighting voluptuous curves.

Synset Examples

a generation ago … buxom actresses were popular

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zaftig - (of a woman) having a full, rounded figure; healthily plump and attractive
  • Synonym: buxom - (of a woman) attractively plump or full-bosomed

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'zaftig,' from Yiddish 'zaftik' (juicy, succulent), from German 'saftig' (juicy).
