anatomist

1. NOUN

/ əˈnæt̬.ə.mɪst / (AmE), / əˈnæt.ə.mɪst / (BrE)
a person who is an expert in anatomy or studies the structure of living organisms
expert in anatomy

Examples

Leonardo da Vinci was not only an artist but also a renowned anatomist.
The anatomist carefully prepared the specimen for teaching purposes.
Many discoveries in medicine have been made by dedicated anatomists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biologist - a scientist who studies living organisms
  • Synonym: pathologist - a doctor who studies the causes and effects of diseases, especially by examining tissues

Etymology

From Late Latin anatomista, from Greek anatomē (dissection).
