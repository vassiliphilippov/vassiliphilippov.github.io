good-looking

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡʊd ˌlʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡʊd ˌlʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
physically attractive, especially because of pleasing features or proportions
physically attractive; pleasing in appearance

Examples

He was a good-looking young man with an easy smile.
The actress is not only talented but also incredibly good-looking.
Despite being very good-looking, she remains humble and approachable.

Synset Examples

a fine-looking woman
a good-looking man
better-looking than her sister
very pretty but not so extraordinarily handsome
our southern women are well-favored

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attractive - pleasing or appealing in appearance
  • Synonym: handsome - good-looking, especially used for men
  • Antonym: ugly - unpleasant or unattractive in appearance
