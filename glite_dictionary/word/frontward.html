frontward

1. ADV

/ ˈfrʌntwɚd / (AmE), / ˈfrʌntwəd / (BrE)
toward the front; in a forward direction.
toward the front

Examples

The dancers all moved frontward on the stage for the final scene.
She adjusted the mirror so it tilted slightly frontward.
The toy car rolled frontward after he gave it a gentle push.

Synset Examples

he faced forward
step forward
she practiced sewing backward as well as frontward on her new sewing machine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forward - toward the front or ahead
  • Antonym: backward - toward the back; in reverse direction

Etymology

Formed from 'front' + '-ward', analogous to 'forward' and 'backward'.
