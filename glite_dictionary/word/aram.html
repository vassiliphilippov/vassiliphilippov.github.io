Aram

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛər.əm, ˈɑː.rəm / (AmE), / ˈɑː.rəm / (BrE)
The biblical name for the region of ancient Syria.
biblical name for ancient Syria

Examples

In the Old Testament, Aram is frequently mentioned as a neighboring kingdom to Israel.
Historical records identify Aram with parts of present-day Syria.
Many ancient texts refer to Aram as a significant center of early Semitic civilization.

Etymology

From Hebrew 'Aram', the name of a region and people in the ancient Near East.
