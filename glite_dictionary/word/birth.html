birth

1. NOUN

/ bɜrθ / (AmE), / bɜːθ / (BrE)
the process of being born; the act or event of a baby or young animal coming out of its mother's body
the process of being born

Examples

After a long and difficult birth, the baby was finally delivered safely.
The doctor explained the stages of birth to the expectant parents.
She wrote a memoir about the traumatic birth of her first child.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delivery - the process of giving birth to a baby
  • Antonym: death - the end of life

Etymology

From Old Norse 'byrðr' (birth, childbearing).
