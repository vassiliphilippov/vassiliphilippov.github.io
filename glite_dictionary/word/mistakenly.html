mistakenly

1. ADV

/ mɪˈsteɪkənli / (AmE), / mɪˈsteɪkənli / (BrE)
In a mistaken or incorrect way; as a result of an error or misunderstanding.
in a mistaken or incorrect way

Examples

He mistakenly thought the meeting was at 3 p.m. instead of 2 p.m.
She mistakenly sent the email to the wrong address.
The article mistakenly reported that the company had gone bankrupt.

Synset Examples

he mistakenly believed it

Related Terms

  • Antonym: correctly - in a correct or accurate manner

Etymology

From 'mistaken' + '-ly', forming the adverb from the adjective.
