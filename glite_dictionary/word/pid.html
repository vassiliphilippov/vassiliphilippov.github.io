PID

1. NOUN

/ pi aɪ di / (AmE), / piː aɪ diː / (BrE)
abbreviation for 'pelvic inflammatory disease': inflammation of the female pelvic organs, often caused by infection, typically resulting in abdominal pain, fever, and abnormal vaginal discharge
inflammation of the female pelvic organs (medical abbreviation)
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with PID after she described her symptoms.
Proper treatment of PID is essential to prevent long-term complications such as infertility.
Antibiotics are typically prescribed to manage PID effectively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pelvic inflammatory disease - inflammation of the female pelvic organs, usually due to infection

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'pelvic inflammatory disease.'
