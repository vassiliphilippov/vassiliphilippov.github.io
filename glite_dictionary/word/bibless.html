bibless

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɪbləs / (AmE), / ˈbɪbləs / (BrE)
Not wearing or lacking a bib, especially referring to garments or in situations where a bib is commonly used.
without a bib

Examples

The toddler insisted on eating bibless, much to his mother's dismay.
Cyclists without race numbers were identified as bibless participants.
After forgetting his apron, the chef joked that he was going bibless for the evening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bib - a piece of cloth or plastic worn to protect clothing while eating
