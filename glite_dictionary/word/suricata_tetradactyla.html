Suricata tetradactyla

1. NOUN

/ sʊˌrɪkətə ˌtɛtrəˈdæktɪlə / (AmE), / sjuːˌrɪkətə ˌtɛtrəˈdæktɪlə / (BrE)
A burrowing, diurnal mammal of southern Africa, also known as the meerkat, often kept as a pet.
meerkat species of southern Africa

Examples

The Suricata tetradactyla lives in large social groups in arid regions of southern Africa.
Many zoos house a colony of Suricata tetradactyla for visitors to observe their playful behavior.
Researchers observed the foraging patterns of Suricata tetradactyla in the wild during daylight hours.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meerkat - a small burrowing mammal native to southern Africa

Etymology

From Latin; Suricata from French (meerkat), tetradactyla from Greek tetra- (four) + dactylos (finger).
