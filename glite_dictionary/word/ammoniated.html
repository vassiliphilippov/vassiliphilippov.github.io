ammoniated

1. ADJ

/ əˈmoʊniˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / əˈməʊniˌeɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a substance) combined or treated with ammonia.
treated with ammonia

Examples

The laboratory used ammoniated mercury as a disinfectant.
Farmers often apply ammoniated fertilizers to improve crop yields.
The textile was treated with an ammoniated solution to enhance its properties.

Synset Examples

ammoniated mercury

Etymology

From 'ammonia' + '-ated', indicating treatment with ammonia.
