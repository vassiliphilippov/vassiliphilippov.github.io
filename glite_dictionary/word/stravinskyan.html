Stravinskyan

1. ADJ

/ strəˈvɪnski.ən / (AmE), / strəˈvɪnski.ən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Igor Stravinsky or his musical style
relating to Stravinsky or his music

Examples

The composer's new ballet was praised for its Stravinskyan rhythms and inventive orchestration.
Her piano playing had a distinctly Stravinskyan energy and sharpness.
Critics noted the Stravinskyan dissonances in the modern symphony.

Etymology

From the name of composer Igor Stravinsky (1882–1971) plus the English adjectival suffix -an.
