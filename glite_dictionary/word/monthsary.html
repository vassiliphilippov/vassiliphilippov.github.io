monthsary

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌnθsɛri / (AmE), / ˈmʌnθsəri / (BrE)
The one-month anniversary of an event, especially a romantic relationship, often celebrated informally.
one-month anniversary (informal)
informal

Examples

They celebrated their first monthsary by going out for dinner together.
My friends reminded me not to forget our group's monthsary this Saturday.
It's our third monthsary, so I bought her flowers to mark the day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anniversary - the yearly recurrence of a date marking an event

Etymology

Blend of 'month' and 'anniversary'.
