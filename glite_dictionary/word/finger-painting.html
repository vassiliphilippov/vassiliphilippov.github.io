finger-painting

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪŋɡərˌpeɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəˌpeɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
A picture created by applying paint directly with the fingers instead of brushes or tools.
painting made with fingers

Examples

Her first finger-painting is proudly displayed on the refrigerator.
The children created colorful finger-paintings during art class.
At the art fair, there was an exhibit of abstract finger-painting by local kids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finger paint - paint designed to be applied with the fingers
