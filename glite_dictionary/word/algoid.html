algoid

1. ADJ

/ ˈæl.ɡɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈæl.ɡɔɪd / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling algae.
of or like algae

Examples

The pond was covered with an algoid film that shimmered in the sunlight.
Some microorganisms develop an algoid appearance under certain environmental conditions.
The biologist noted an algoid texture on the surface of the rock samples.

Etymology

From 'alga' (Latin for seaweed) + '-oid' (from Greek -oeidēs, meaning 'like').
