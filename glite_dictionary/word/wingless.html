wingless

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪŋləs / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋləs / (BrE)
not having wings; lacking wings.
lacking wings

Examples

The ostrich is a wingless bird, unable to fly but fast on land.
Many insects go through a wingless stage before developing wings.
The wingless queen ant was easy to distinguish from the others.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: winged - having wings

Etymology

Formed from 'wing' + '-less' (meaning 'without').
