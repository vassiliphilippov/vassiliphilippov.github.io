Pieter Brueghel

1. PROPN

/ ˈpiːtər ˈbrɔɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈpiːtə ˈbrɔɪɡəl / (BrE)
(1525–1569) A renowned Flemish painter known for his detailed landscapes and scenes of peasant life.
Flemish painter (1525–1569) noted for landscapes

Examples

Pieter Brueghel is celebrated for his vibrant depictions of peasant life in 16th-century Flanders.
The landscape paintings of Pieter Brueghel have influenced generations of artists.
A major exhibition showcasing works by Pieter Brueghel drew art lovers from around the world.
