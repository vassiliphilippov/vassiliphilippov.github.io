Al Tawhid

1. PROPN

/ æl tɑːˈwiːd / (AmE), / æl tɑːˈwiːd / (BrE)
an Islamic terrorist group that originated in Jordan and operates internationally, known for plotting attacks in Europe and Russia
Islamic terrorist group from Jordan

Examples

Security agencies increased surveillance of Al Tawhid after discovering their activities in Germany.
Reports indicate that Al Tawhid was planning attacks involving chemical weapons in Europe.
The international community has condemned the violent actions of Al Tawhid across several countries.

Etymology

From Arabic التوحيد (*at-tawḥīd*), meaning 'the unity' or 'monotheism'.
