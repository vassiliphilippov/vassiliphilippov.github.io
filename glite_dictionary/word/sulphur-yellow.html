sulphur-yellow

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌlfər ˈjɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsʌlfə ˈjɛləʊ / (BrE)
having the bright yellow color characteristic of sulfur.
bright yellow like sulfur

Examples

The bird's sulphur-yellow feathers made it easy to spot among the leaves.
She painted her room a soft shade of sulphur-yellow to brighten the space.
The sulphur-yellow blossoms stood out vividly in the spring sunshine.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: yellow - the color between green and orange in the spectrum

Etymology

From 'sulphur' (variant of 'sulfur', from Latin 'sulphur') + 'yellow', referring to the color of the element sulfur.
