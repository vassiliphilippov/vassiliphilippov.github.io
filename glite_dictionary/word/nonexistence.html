nonexistence

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɑːnɪɡˈzɪstəns / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɪstəns / (BrE)
the state of not existing or having no existence; absence of being or presence
state of not existing

Examples

His fear of nonexistence kept him searching for the meaning of life.
Many philosophers have debated the concept of nonexistence for centuries.
The abandoned village faded into complete nonexistence after the flood.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: existence - the state of existing
  • Synonym: absence - the state of being absent or nonexistent

Etymology

From non- (not) + existence, modeled after Latin roots.
