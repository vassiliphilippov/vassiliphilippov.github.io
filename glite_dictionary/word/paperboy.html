paperboy

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpərˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəbɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy or young man who delivers newspapers, typically as a part-time job
boy who delivers newspapers

Examples

When I was twelve, I worked as a paperboy every morning before school.
The paperboy tossed the newspaper onto the front porch and rode away on his bike.
Years ago, a paperboy was a familiar sight in every neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: papergirl - a girl who delivers newspapers
