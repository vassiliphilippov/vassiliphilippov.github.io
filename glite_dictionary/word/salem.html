Salem

1. PROPN

/ ˈseɪləm / (AmE), / ˈseɪləm / (BrE)
the capital city of the state of Oregon, located in the northwest on the Willamette River
capital city of Oregon, US

Examples

Salem is known for its beautiful parks and historic downtown area.
The state government of Oregon is headquartered in Salem.
After moving to Oregon, she found a new job in Salem.

Etymology

From Hebrew 'shalom' meaning 'peace'; several US towns are named Salem, often for biblical or peaceful association.

Examples
