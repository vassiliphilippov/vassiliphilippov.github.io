West-sider

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛst ˌsaɪdər / (AmE), / ˈwɛst ˌsaɪdə / (BrE)
a person who lives on the west side of Manhattan in New York City
resident of Manhattan's west side

Examples

As a West-sider, she prefers the quieter streets and the riverside parks.
Many West-siders take pride in their neighborhood's vibrant culture and food scene.
When he moved downtown, he still considered himself a true West-sider.
