scrapper

1. NOUN

/ ˈskræpər / (AmE), / ˈskrapə / (BrE)
a person who engages in fights, especially one who is combative or ready to fight; a fighter
a person who fights; fighter

Examples

Although small in stature, he was always a scrapper on the field.
She earned a reputation as a determined scrapper who never backed down from a challenge.
The club was full of scrappers looking for a quick brawl.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fighter - a person who fights

Etymology

From scrap (fight) + -er (agent suffix).
