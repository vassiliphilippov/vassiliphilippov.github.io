Eacles

1. NOUN

/ ˈiː.ə.kliz / (AmE), / ˈiː.ə.kliːz / (BrE)
A genus of large, colorful moths in the family Saturniidae, commonly known as imperial moths.
a genus of imperial moths

Examples

The caterpillar of the Eacles genus can consume large amounts of foliage.
Researchers recently discovered a new species within the Eacles group.
Eacles moths are prized for their striking coloration and impressive size.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imperial moth - a large yellow and purple moth found in the Americas

Etymology

From New Latin, used in zoological classification.
