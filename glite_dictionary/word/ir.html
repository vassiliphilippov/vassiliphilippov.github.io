IR

1. NOUN

/ aɪ ɑr / (AmE), / aɪ ɑː / (BrE)
abbreviation for 'Inland Revenue', the former British government department responsible for administering and collecting major direct taxes.
Inland Revenue (UK tax authority) abbreviation
british

Examples

The accountant submitted the tax returns to the IR before the deadline.
After the merger, many functions of the IR were transferred to HMRC.
She previously worked as a tax inspector for the IR in London.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Inland Revenue - the former department of the British government responsible for tax collection
  • Degree Higher: HMRC - Her Majesty's Revenue and Customs, UK government department that merged with Inland Revenue

Etymology

Abbreviation of Inland Revenue (UK).
