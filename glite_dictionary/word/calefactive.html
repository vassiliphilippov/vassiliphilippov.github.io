calefactive

1. ADJ

/ ˌkæləˈfæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌkælɪˈfæktɪv / (BrE)
serving to produce or provide heat; heating.
producing or providing heat

Examples

The calefactive effect of the lamp helped to keep the room warm on winter nights.
Certain ointments have a calefactive property, creating a warming sensation when applied to the skin.
A calefactive blanket can provide additional comfort in cold environments.

Synset Examples

a heating pad is calefactory

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heating - causing to become warm or hot

Etymology

From Latin calefactus (warmed), from calefacere (to make warm), from calere (to be warm) + facere (to make).
