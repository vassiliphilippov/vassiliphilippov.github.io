intragroup

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪn.trəˌɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈɪn.trəˌɡruːp / (BrE)
happening or existing within a particular group, institution, or community.
within a group or community

Examples

The manager tried to resolve the intragroup conflict that was affecting productivity.
Researchers studied the dynamics of intragroup communication in the team.
Frequent intragroup disagreements can hinder a department's progress.

Synset Examples

intragroup squabbling within the corporation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intergroup - occurring between two or more groups

Etymology

From Latin 'intra-' (within) + 'group'.
