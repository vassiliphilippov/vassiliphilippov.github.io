Lahu

1. NOUN

/ lɑːˈhuː / (AmE), / lɑːˈhuː / (BrE)
a Loloish language spoken by the Lahu people primarily in southwest Yunnan Province, China, and neighboring regions.
a Loloish language of southwest China

Examples

Many villagers in Yunnan speak Lahu as their first language.
The Lahu language has several distinct dialects within the region.
Researchers are documenting traditional stories told in Lahu for preservation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Loloish - a branch of the Sino-Tibetan language family

Etymology

From the autonym of the Lahu people.
