Saint Timothy

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt ˈtɪməθi / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈtɪməθi / (BrE)
A disciple of Saint Paul who became the leader of the early Christian community at Ephesus.
early Christian leader and disciple of Saint Paul

Examples

Many churches are named after Saint Timothy in honor of his dedication to the early Christian church.
According to tradition, Saint Timothy was martyred for his faith.
The letters to Saint Timothy are included in the New Testament as epistles from Paul.
