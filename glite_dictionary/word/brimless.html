brimless

1. ADJ

/ ˈbrɪmləs / (AmE), / ˈbrɪmləs / (BrE)
(of a hat or similar item) having no brim.
without a brim (e.g., a hat)

Examples

He wore a brimless cap that fit snugly on his head.
The museum displayed an ancient, brimless helmet from Greece.
For the ceremony, she chose a stylish brimless hat.

Synset Examples

a brimless hat

Related Terms

  • Antonym: brimmed - (of a hat) having a brim
