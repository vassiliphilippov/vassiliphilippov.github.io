supervisory

1. ADJ

/ suː.pɚˈvaɪ.zə.ri / (AmE), / suː.pəˈvaɪ.zər.i / (BrE)
relating to or involving the act of supervising people, activities, or work.
relating to supervision

Examples

She held a supervisory position at the company for over five years.
The new policy requires additional supervisory oversight during evening shifts.
He lacked the supervisory experience required for the higher-level job.

Synset Examples

in a supervisory capacity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: managerial - relating to management or managers
  • Antonym: subordinate - under the authority of a supervisor

Etymology

From supervise +‎ -ory, modelled after Latin origin meaning 'of or pertaining to overseeing.'
