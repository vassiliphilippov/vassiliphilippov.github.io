hallstand

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔlˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈhɔːlˌstænd / (BrE)
a piece of furniture, typically placed in a hall or entranceway, designed for storing coats, hats, umbrellas, and often featuring a mirror.
furniture for coats, hats, umbrellas in halls

Examples

The vintage hallstand in the foyer was perfect for hanging guests' coats and hats.
She placed her umbrella in the stand at the bottom of the hallstand before going inside.
Their new house had a spacious entryway, so they bought a wooden hallstand with a mirror.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hatstand - a stand for holding hats, especially in a hallway or entrance
