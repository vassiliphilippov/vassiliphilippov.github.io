epicanthic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛpɪˈkænθɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈkanθɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the epicanthus, especially referring to the skin fold of the upper eyelid covering the inner corner of the eye.
relating to the epicanthus fold
medical

Examples

The child was born with prominent epicanthic folds on his eyelids.
Researchers studied the prevalence of epicanthic features in different populations.
Doctors examined the baby's eyes and noted the presence of epicanthic folds.

Synset Examples

epicanthic folds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: epicanthus - a fold of skin of the upper eyelid covering the inner corner of the eye

Etymology

From Greek epi- (upon) + kanthos (corner of the eye).
