fuzz

1. NOUN

/ fʌz / (AmE), / fʌz / (BrE)
the fine, soft facial hair that first appears on an adolescent boy's chin and upper lip
first soft facial hair of adolescence

Examples

At fourteen, he started to notice a faint fuzz forming above his lip.
He joked about shaving the fuzz on his chin for the first time.
The boys compared the light fuzz they had grown over the summer.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: stubble - short, rough growth of beard after shaving

2. NOUN

/ fʌz / (AmE), / fʌz / (BrE)
a mass of fine, soft, hairlike threads, especially such growth found on the surface of some plants or fruits
fine hairlike growth on plants or fruit

Examples

The fuzz on a ripe peach makes its skin feel velvety to the touch.
A layer of fuzz covered the surface of the kiwi, giving it a slightly rough appearance.
She brushed the fuzz off the strawberries before adding them to the fruit salad.

Synset Examples

peach fuzz

Related Terms

  • Synonym: down - fine soft hair, especially on a young bird or on certain plants and fruits

3. NOUN

/ fʌz / (AmE), / fʌz / (BrE)
a state or quality of being indistinct, hazy, or blurred, either in appearance or mental clarity
hazy or indistinct state or representation

Examples

After a long night without sleep, everything around him was just a fuzz.
Memories of his childhood are shrouded in a fuzz and difficult to recall clearly.
The alcohol left a persistent fuzz in his mind, making it hard to concentrate.

Synset Examples

4. NOUN

/ fʌz / (AmE), / fʌz / (BrE)
a slang term for a policeman or police officer, often used in an uncomplimentary or contemptuous way
slang term for a policeman
slang

Examples

The fuzz showed up just as the party was getting loud.
He tried to outrun the fuzz, but they caught him on Main Street.
Some old movies always have characters referring to the police as the fuzz.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cop - informal term for a police officer
  • Synonym: police officer - official member of a police force

Etymology

Origin unknown; first cited in early 20th-century American slang.
