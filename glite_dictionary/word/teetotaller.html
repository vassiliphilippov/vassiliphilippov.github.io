teetotaller

1. NOUN

/ tiˈtoʊtələr / (AmE), / tiːˈtəʊtələ / (BrE)
a person who never drinks alcohol
person who abstains from alcohol

Examples

My grandfather was a strict teetotaller and never allowed alcohol in the house.
Even at parties, she remains a teetotaller and sips only soft drinks.
As a proud teetotaller, he always volunteers to drive his friends home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abstainer - a person who refrains from taking part, especially from alcohol
  • Antonym: drinker - a person who drinks alcohol

Etymology

From 'tee' (emphatic form of 'total') + 'total' + '-er', first used in the early 19th century.
