never-wozzer

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛvɚˌwɒzɚ / (AmE), / ˈnɛvəˌwɒzə / (BrE)
a person who feels out of place or awkward, often regarded as unsuccessful or not fitting in
an awkward, out-of-place person; unsuccessful
informal slang

Examples

I felt like a never-wozzer at the party, standing alone in the corner.
After being rejected for the job, he joked that he was just a never-wozzer anyway.
Growing up, she always worried about being seen as a never-wozzer by her classmates.

Synset Examples

I felt like a never-wozzer at the party.
He called himself a never-wozzer for missing the deadline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outsider - a person who does not belong to a particular group
  • Synonym: loser - someone considered unsuccessful or inadequate

Etymology

Formed from 'never was', with imitation of colloquial and regional spelling; refers to someone who never achieved status or belonging.
