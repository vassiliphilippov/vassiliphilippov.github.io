Raja batis

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪdʒə ˈbeɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˈreɪdʒə ˈbætɪs / (BrE)
a large European skate, valued as a food fish, found in the eastern North Atlantic and Mediterranean
a large European food skate

Examples

The wings of the Raja batis are prized in many European seafood recipes.
Fishermen often catch Raja batis off the coasts of Britain and France.
The population of Raja batis has declined due to overfishing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ray - a type of cartilaginous fish with flattened body
  • Synonym: skate - any of various flat-bodied cartilaginous fish of the family Rajidae

Etymology

From Latin: genus 'Raja' (ray, skate) and species 'batis' (ray, skate).
