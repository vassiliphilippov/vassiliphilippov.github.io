footsore

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʊtˌsɔr / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌsɔː / (BrE)
having sore or aching feet, typically from walking or standing for a long time
having sore or tired feet

Examples

After hiking all day, I was completely footsore by the evening.
The footsore travelers welcomed a chance to sit and rest.
She was footsore after spending hours on her feet at the exhibition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tired - feeling in need of rest or sleep

Etymology

Compound of 'foot' and 'sore', first recorded in Middle English.
