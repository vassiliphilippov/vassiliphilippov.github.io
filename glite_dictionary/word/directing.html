directing

1. ADJ

/ dəˈrɛktɪŋ, daɪˈrɛktɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛktɪŋ, daɪˈrɛktɪŋ / (BrE)
giving guidance, supervision, or direction to someone or something; leading or showing the way
giving guidance or showing the way

Examples

She placed her directing hand on his back and guided him forward.
The committee plays a significant directing role in the organization's activities.
Under his directing influence, the team achieved remarkable success.

Synset Examples

felt his mother's directing arm around him
the directional role of science on industrial progress

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guiding - providing direction or leadership
  • Synonym: leading - showing others the way; in charge

Etymology

Present participle of 'direct,' from Latin 'directus' (straight, guided).
