below

1. ADV

/ bɪˈloʊ / (AmE), / bɪˈləʊ / (BrE)
on a lower floor of a building.
on a lower floor

Examples

My neighbor lives below and often helps collect my packages.
We could hear music coming from the apartment below.
The office directly below ours is being renovated.

Synset Examples

the tenants live downstairs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: downstairs - on or to a lower floor of a building
  • Antonym: upstairs - on or to an upper floor of a building

2. ADV

/ bəˈloʊ / (AmE), / bɪˈləʊ / (BrE)
at a lower position or further down, especially in a text or on a page
at a lower position or further down

Examples

You will find the answer explained below in the next paragraph.
Please refer to the chart below for more information.
Her office is located two floors below mine.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: above - at a higher position or further up

Etymology

3. ADV

/ bɪˈloʊ / (AmE), / bɪˈləʊ / (BrE)
at or to a lower place or position; beneath.
at or to a lower position

Examples

The temperature dropped to freezing below during the night.
Her office is just below mine on the next floor.
They found the lost keys below the sofa cushions.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: above - at or to a higher place or position

Etymology

From Middle English 'bilowe', probably from Old English 'be-' (by) + 'hlāw' (mound, hill).
