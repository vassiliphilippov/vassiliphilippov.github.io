Han-Gook

1. PROPN

/ hanˈguːk / (AmE), / hanˈguːk / (BrE)
The Korean name for Korea, referring to the Asian peninsula separating the Yellow Sea and the Sea of Japan.
Korean name for Korea (peninsula)

Examples

Many Koreans refer to their homeland as Han-Gook rather than Korea.
In Han-Gook, traditional holidays like Chuseok are widely celebrated.
The cuisine of Han-Gook is renowned for its spicy flavors and fermented dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Korea - country and peninsula in East Asia

Etymology

From Korean 한국 (Han-guk), meaning 'Korea'.
