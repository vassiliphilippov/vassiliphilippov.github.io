Japaneseness

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈpæniznəs / (AmE), / dʒəˈpaniːznɪs / (BrE)
The distinctive qualities, character, or essence that are regarded as characteristic of Japanese people, culture, or things.
quality or character of being Japanese

Examples

The architect's latest project embodies a deep sense of Japaneseness in its design elements.
Scholars continue to debate what constitutes true Japaneseness in literature and art.
Her appreciation for Japaneseness grew during her years living in Tokyo.

Synset Examples

Japan's continuous obsession with the idea of the ‘Japaneseness’ in craft products was complicated by its effort to redeﬁne itself in terms of its ‘Orientalness’.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Japanese - of or relating to Japan or its people, language, or culture
