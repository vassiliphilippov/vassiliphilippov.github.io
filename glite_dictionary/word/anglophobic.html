Anglophobic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæŋɡləˈfoʊbɪk / (AmE), / ˌæŋɡləˈfəʊbɪk / (BrE)
Having or showing a dislike of or prejudice against England or English people.
showing dislike of England or English people

Examples

Many critics denounced the film's Anglophobic portrayal of British characters.
His comments were widely considered Anglophobic by the English community.
Some media outlets accused the politician of making Anglophobic statements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Anglophobia - strong dislike or fear of England or English people
  • Antonym: Anglophile - a person who admires England or English people

Etymology

Formed from 'Anglo-' (relating to England or English) and '-phobic' (from Greek 'phobos' meaning fear).
