Gustav IV Adolf of Sweden

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡʊstɑv fɔr ˈædɒlf əv ˈswidn̩ / (AmE), / ˈɡʊstɑːv ˌfɔːr ˈadɒlf əv ˈswiːdn̩ / (BrE)
(1778–1837) King of Sweden from 1792 to 1809, deposed after military losses to Napoleon I.
king of Sweden, deposed in 1809

Examples

During his reign, Gustav IV Adolf of Sweden struggled to maintain his kingdom’s independence against Napoleon.
The abdication of Gustav IV Adolf of Sweden marked a turning point in Swedish history.
Historians often debate whether Gustav IV Adolf of Sweden could have avoided defeat in the Napoleonic Wars.
