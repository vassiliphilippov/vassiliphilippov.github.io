leptorrhine

1. ADJ

/ ˌlɛp.təˈraɪn / (AmE), / ˌlɛp.təˈraɪn / (BrE)
(of a nose) long and narrow in shape, especially in reference to anatomical or anthropological classification.
having a long narrow nose
medical

Examples

Anthropologists classified the subject as leptorrhine due to her slender nasal structure.
Certain populations are described as leptorrhine based on the proportion of nose length to width.
The doctor noted his distinctly leptorrhine features during the examination.

Etymology

From Greek 'leptos' (slender) + 'rhis, rhinos' (nose).
