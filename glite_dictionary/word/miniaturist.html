miniaturist

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪniətʃəɹɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪətjʊərɪst / (BrE)
an artist who specializes in painting extremely small, detailed pictures
artist of tiny, detailed paintings

Examples

The museum features several works by a renowned miniaturist from the 18th century.
As a miniaturist, she spends hours perfecting the tiniest details with a fine brush.
Collectors prize the delicate paintings created by a skilled miniaturist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miniature - a very small detailed painting or work of art

Etymology

From French miniaturiste, based on 'miniature' from Italian 'miniatura' (small painting) and '-ist' (person).
