Symbion pandora

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪm.bi.ən pænˈdɔːr.ə / (AmE), / ˌsɪm.bi.ən pænˈdɔː.rə / (BrE)
a microscopic species of marine animal, the only known member of Cycliophora, living symbiotically on lobsters and having a complex life cycle with both asexual and sexual reproduction
microscopic animal living on lobsters

Examples

Symbion pandora was first discovered living on the mouthparts of Norwegian lobsters.
Scientists were fascinated by the unique reproductive cycle of Symbion pandora.
The lobster's lip is the exclusive home to Symbion pandora.
