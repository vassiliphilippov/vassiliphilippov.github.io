tchotchke

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɒtʃki / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒtʃki / (BrE)
A small decorative object or trinket, often inexpensive and considered to be of little value.
small decorative trinket

Examples

She bought a tchotchke from the souvenir shop as a keepsake of her trip.
His desk is cluttered with tchotchkes collected from various conferences.
They dusted the shelves filled with tchotchkes and family photos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trinket - a small ornament or item of jewelry of little value

Etymology

From Yiddish tshatshke (trinket), itself of Slavic origin.
