eighty

1. NUM

/ ˈeɪti / (AmE), / ˈeɪti / (BrE)
The number 80; ten more than seventy.
the number 80

Examples

There are eighty students enrolled in the class this semester.
He left his fortune to charity when he was in his eighties.
The speed limit on this highway is eighty kilometers per hour.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: seventy - the number 70
  • Degree Higher: ninety - the number 90

Etymology

From Old English 'eahtatig', from 'eahta' (eight) + '-tig' (group of ten).
