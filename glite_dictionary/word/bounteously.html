bounteously

1. ADV

/ ˈbaʊn.ti.əs.li / (AmE), / ˈbaʊn.ti.əs.li / (BrE)
In a generous and plentiful manner.
in a generous, plentiful way

Examples

The garden produced vegetables bounteously this summer, providing enough for the whole neighborhood.
She donated bounteously to the charity, helping many families in need.
The king rewarded his soldiers bounteously for their bravery in battle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bountifully - in a bountiful or generous manner

Etymology

From 'bounteous' + '-ly', with 'bounteous' from Middle English, based on 'bounty'.
