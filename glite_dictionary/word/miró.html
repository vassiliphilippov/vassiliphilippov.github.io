Miró

1. PROPN

/ miˈroʊ / (AmE), / mɪˈrəʊ / (BrE)
Joan Miró (1893–1983), a Spanish surrealist painter known for his abstract and colorful artworks.
Spanish surrealist painter (1893–1983)

Examples

The museum had a special exhibition of Miró's abstract paintings.
She wrote her art thesis on the influence of Miró in modern design.
A colorful Miró print hangs in their living room.

Etymology

Spanish surname; Joan Miró was from Catalonia, Spain.
