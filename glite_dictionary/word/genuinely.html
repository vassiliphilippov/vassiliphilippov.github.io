genuinely

1. ADV

/ ˈdʒɛnjuɪnli / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnjʊɪnli / (BrE)
In a way that is truly what it is said to be; sincerely and honestly; in accordance with truth or fact.
in a sincere or truthful way

Examples

She genuinely cares about the people she works with.
He was genuinely surprised by the unexpected party.
The documentary genuinely moved me to tears.

Synset Examples

she was now truly American
a genuinely open society
they don't really listen to us

Related Terms

  • Synonym: truly - in a truthful or sincere manner
  • Antonym: falsely - in a false or insincere manner

Etymology

From 'genuine' + '-ly'. 'Genuine' from Latin genuinus (innate, natural).
