threesome

1. NOUN

/ ˈθriˌsəm / (AmE), / ˈθriːsəm / (BrE)
a group of three people acting or considered together as a unit
group of three people

Examples

The band started as a threesome before adding a new member.
We played tennis as a threesome after our fourth friend cancelled.
A threesome can be more dynamic than a pair or a quartet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trio - a group of three people or things

Etymology

Formed from 'three' + '-some', meaning group.
