Shortia

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɔrtiə / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːtɪə / (BrE)
A genus of evergreen perennial herbs native to North America and eastern Asia, known as oconee bells.
genus of evergreen perennial herbs

Examples

The rare Shortia blooms in early spring along the forest floor.
Botanists traveled to study the habitat of Shortia in the southern Appalachians.
Several species within the genus Shortia display attractive, bell-shaped flowers.

Etymology

Named in honor of Charles Wilkins Short, an American botanist.
