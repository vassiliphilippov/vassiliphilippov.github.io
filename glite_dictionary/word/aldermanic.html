aldermanic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔːldərˈmænɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːldəˈmænɪk / (BrE)
of or relating to an alderman or the office of an alderman.
relating to an alderman

Examples

The new regulations addressed several aldermanic concerns regarding city budget allocations.
She attended the aldermanic meeting to present her proposal for the neighborhood park.
The aldermanic race drew candidates from diverse backgrounds this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alderman - a member of a municipal assembly or council

Etymology

From 'alderman' (member of a municipal council) + -ic (forming adjectives).
