sanatarium

1. NOUN

/ ˌsænəˈtɛriəm / (AmE), / ˌsanəˈteəriəm / (BrE)
a hospital or medical facility for patients needing extended care, especially for recovery from illness or treatment of chronic diseases
hospital for extended recovery or chronic illness
archaic

Examples

After his surgery, he spent several weeks recovering at a sanatarium in the mountains.
The old sanatarium on the hill has now been converted into a museum.
Many patients with tuberculosis were once sent to a sanatarium for long-term treatment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sanatorium - a hospital for long-term illness and rehabilitation
  • Synonym: hospital - an institution providing medical and surgical treatment and nursing care

Etymology

Variant of "sanatorium," from Latin 'sanare' (to heal).
