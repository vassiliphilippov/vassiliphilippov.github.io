to repoint

1. VERB

/ riˈpɔɪnt / (AmE), / riˈpɔɪnt / (BrE)
to repair or renew the mortar joints between bricks or stones in masonry
repair mortar joints in brickwork

Examples

It is necessary to repoint the old brick walls to prevent moisture damage.
The mason spent the day repointing the stone garden path.
They hired a professional to repoint the chimney before winter.

Synset Examples

point a chimney

Related Terms

  • Synonym: point - to fill or finish the joints between bricks with mortar

Etymology

From re- (again) + point (apply or fill in mortar joints).
