dreaminess

1. NOUN

/ ˈdriːminəs / (AmE), / ˈdriːminɪs / (BrE)
a relaxed and comfortable state, as if lost in pleasant thoughts or daydreams
a relaxed, comfortable feeling

Examples

A sense of dreaminess settled over the room as the afternoon sunlight streamed in.
He spoke with a dreaminess that made everyone feel at ease.
Her music creates an atmosphere of soft dreaminess perfect for relaxing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: serenity - the state of being calm and peaceful
  • Antonym: restlessness - the inability to relax or be still

Etymology

From "dreamy" (from "dream") + "-ness".
