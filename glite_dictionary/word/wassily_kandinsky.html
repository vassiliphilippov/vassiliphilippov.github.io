Wassily Kandinsky

1. PROPN

/ vəˈsiːli kænˈdɪnski / (AmE), / vəˈsiːli kənˈdɪnski / (BrE)
(1866–1944) A Russian painter and art theorist, recognized as a pioneer of abstract art.
Russian abstract art pioneer

Examples

Wassily Kandinsky revolutionized modern art with his abstract compositions.
Many museums feature works by Wassily Kandinsky in their permanent collections.
Art students often study Wassily Kandinsky for his theories on color and form.
