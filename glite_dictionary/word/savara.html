Savara

1. NOUN

/ səˈvɑːrə / (AmE), / səˈvɑːrə / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken by the Savara people in southeastern India, north of Chennai (Madras)
Dravidian language of southeastern India

Examples

The Savara language is spoken by many communities in southeastern India.
Linguists are studying the structure of Savara to document its unique features.
She is fluent in both Telugu and Savara.

/ səˈvɑːrə / (AmE), / səˈvɑːrə / (BrE)

member of a Dravidian ethnic group in India
