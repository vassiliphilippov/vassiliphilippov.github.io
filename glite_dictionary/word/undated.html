undated

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not bearing a date; lacking a written or printed date indicating when it was created or issued.
lacking a date

Examples

The undated letter left the recipient guessing about when it was sent.
Researchers struggled to verify the origin of the undated photographs in the collection.
We found several undated manuscripts stored in the old archive.

Synset Examples

a dateless letter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dateless - without a date; not bearing a date

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + dated (from 'date', meaning to mark with a date); first recorded in the 18th century.
