Oakland

1. PROPN

/ ˈoʊklənd / (AmE), / ˈəʊklənd / (BrE)
a city in western California, located on the eastern shore of San Francisco Bay, known as an industrial and cultural center
city in western California

Examples

He moved to Oakland for a new job opportunity.
The Warriors used to play their home games in Oakland.
The port of Oakland is one of the busiest in the United States.

Etymology

Named after the oak trees native to the area.
