telemetered

1. ADJ

/ təˈlɛmətɚd / (AmE), / təˈlɛmɪtəd / (BrE)
(of data or information) transmitted and received by means of telemetry.
transmitted via telemetry

Examples

The telemetered data allowed scientists to monitor the volcano's activity from a safe distance.
All wildlife movements were tracked using telemetered collars on the animals.
The spacecraft relayed telemetered signals back to mission control for analysis.

Synset Examples

the telemetered information was recorded and analyzed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telemetry - the process of recording and transmitting readings from a distance

Etymology

From 'telemeter' (device for measuring at a distance) + '-ed'. 'Telemeter' from Greek 'tēle' (far) + 'metron' (measure).
