totally

1. ADV

/ ˈtoʊtəli / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəli / (BrE)
Completely; to the full or entire extent; absolutely.
completely; absolutely

Examples

I totally forgot about our meeting this morning.
The movie was totally different from what I expected.
She totally agrees with your decision.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: completely - entirely; fully; in every way
  • Antonym: partly - to some extent but not completely

Etymology

From total + -ly, dating from late Middle English.
