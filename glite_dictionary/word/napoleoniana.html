napoleoniana

1. NOUN

/ nəˌpoʊliəˈnɑːnə / (AmE), / nəˌpəʊliəˈnɑːnə / (BrE)
objects, documents, or memorabilia related to the life, times, and legacy of Napoleon Bonaparte, such as engravings, paintings, and books
memorabilia related to Napoleon Bonaparte

Examples

The museum's collection of napoleoniana includes rare letters and original uniforms.
At auction, some napoleoniana can fetch remarkably high prices from collectors.
His study was filled with shelves devoted exclusively to napoleoniana.

Etymology

From Napoleon, the French military leader, and the suffix -iana, denoting a collection of items relating to a person.
