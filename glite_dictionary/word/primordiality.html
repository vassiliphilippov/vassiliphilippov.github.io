primordiality

1. NOUN

/ praɪˌmɔrdiˈæləti / (AmE), / praɪˌmɔːdiˈælɪti / (BrE)
The quality or state of being first, earliest, or original.
the quality of being original or first

Examples

The primordiality of the universe has long fascinated cosmologists and philosophers alike.
Her theory examines the primordiality of language development in human history.
They studied the primordiality of life forms found in extreme environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: originality - the quality of being novel or original
  • Synonym: antiquity - ancient times or the ancient past

Etymology

From primordial + -ity, ultimately from Latin primordium (origin, beginning).
