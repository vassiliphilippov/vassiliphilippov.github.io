photoelectrical

1. ADJ

/ ˌfoʊtoʊɪˈlɛktrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊɪˈlɛktrɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by the emission of electrons as a result of exposure to light (photoelectricity)
relating to photoelectricity

Examples

Many modern sensors use photoelectrical technology to detect changes in light intensity.
The photoelectrical properties of the material were analyzed in the laboratory.
He explained that the device works by converting sunlight into voltage through a photoelectrical process.

Synset Examples

the photoelectric effect

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photoelectric - relating to or caused by photoelectricity

Etymology

From photo- (relating to light) + electrical.
