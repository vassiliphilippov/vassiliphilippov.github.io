terazosin

1. NOUN

/ təˈræzəsɪn / (AmE), / təˈræzəsɪn / (BrE)
a drug used to treat high blood pressure and benign prostatic hyperplasia by relaxing blood vessels and muscles in the prostate
drug for high blood pressure and prostate
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed terazosin to help lower his blood pressure.
Some patients take terazosin for both hypertension and prostate issues.
Before starting terazosin, it's important to discuss potential side effects with your physician.
