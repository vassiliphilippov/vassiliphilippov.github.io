extraordinariness

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˌstrɔːr.dəˈner.i.nəs / (AmE), / ɪkˌstrɔː.dɪˈneə.ri.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being very unusual, remarkable, or exceptional.
the quality of being extraordinary

Examples

Her extraordinariness set her apart from everyone else in her class.
Few could deny the extraordinariness of the event they had just witnessed.
The artist's extraordinariness is evident in every piece of his work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exceptionality - the quality of being exceptional; uncommonly good
  • Antonym: normality - the state of being usual, typical, or expected

Etymology

From extraordinary + -ness, indicating a state or quality.
