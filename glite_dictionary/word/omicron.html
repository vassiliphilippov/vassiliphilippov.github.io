omicron

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːmɪˌkrɑn, oʊˈmaɪkrɒn / (AmE), / ˈəʊmɪkrɒn, ˈɒmɪkrɒn / (BrE)
the 15th letter of the Greek alphabet, represented as Ο (uppercase) or ο (lowercase)
Greek letter (15th in alphabet)

Examples

The Greek letter omicron often appears in mathematical and scientific equations.
In the Greek alphabet, omicron comes after xi and before pi.
Students had to memorize the order of the Greek letters, including omicron.

Etymology

From Greek ὂ μικρόν (o mikron), meaning 'small o', to distinguish it from omega ('large o').
