Android

1. PROPN

/ ˈænˌdrɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈæn.drɔɪd / (BrE)
A mobile operating system developed by Google, used on smartphones, tablets, and other devices.
mobile operating system by Google

Examples

Most smartphones today run on Android or iOS.
She prefers using an Android device because of its customization options.
The latest Android update includes several new security features.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: iOS - mobile operating system developed by Apple
  • Antonym: Windows Phone - a now-defunct mobile operating system from Microsoft

Etymology

From the earlier science-fiction term 'android' (a human-like robot), repurposed by the company Android Inc. and retained after acquisition by Google.
