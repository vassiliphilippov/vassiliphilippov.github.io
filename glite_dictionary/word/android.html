android

1. NOUN

/ ˈænˌdrɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈan.drɔɪd / (BrE)
a robot or automaton made to closely resemble a human being in appearance and behavior
robot resembling a human

Examples

The scientist designed an android that could mimic human emotions and speech.
In many science fiction films, an android serves as a companion to the main character.
Some people fear that advanced androids might one day replace humans in the workplace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: robot - a machine capable of carrying out complex tasks automatically, especially by being programmed by a computer
  • Degree Lower: gynoid - a robot shaped like a human female

Etymology

From Greek 'andr-', meaning 'man' and '-oid', meaning 'resembling'.
