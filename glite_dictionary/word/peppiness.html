peppiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛpinəs / (AmE), / ˈpepɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being lively, energetic, and full of enthusiasm
liveliness; energetic quality

Examples

The peppiness of the host lifted the mood of the entire party.
Her early morning peppiness made everyone else seem sluggish by comparison.
Some people drink coffee to add a bit of peppiness to their day.

Synset Examples

this tonic is guaranteed to give you more pep

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vivacity - the quality of being attractively lively and animated
  • Synonym: energy - the strength and vitality required for physical or mental activity
  • Antonym: lethargy - a lack of energy and enthusiasm

Etymology

From 'peppy' (full of pep) + '-ness'.
