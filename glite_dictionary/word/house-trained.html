house-trained

1. ADJ

/ ˈhaʊs treɪnd / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs treɪnd / (BrE)
(of a pet) trained to urinate and defecate outside or in a designated place rather than indoors.
(of a pet) trained not to soil indoors
british

Examples

We adopted a house-trained dog from the animal shelter.
All our puppies will be house-trained before you take them home.
The cat is not fully house-trained yet and sometimes has accidents indoors.

Synset Examples

housebroken pets
‘house-trained’ is chiefly British

Related Terms

  • Synonym: housebroken - (of a pet) trained not to defecate or urinate inside the house
