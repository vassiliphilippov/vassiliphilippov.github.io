soul-sucking

1. ADJ

/ ˈsoʊl ˌsʌkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊl ˌsʌkɪŋ / (BrE)
emotionally draining or unfulfilling, causing a feeling of exhaustion or emptiness
emotionally draining or exhausting
informal

Examples

After years in a soul-sucking job, he decided to pursue his passion for painting.
The endless meetings became so soul-sucking that employees dreaded coming to work.
She described the climate of constant criticism as completely soul-sucking.

Synset Examples

She quit her soul-sucking job for a new adventure.
His job was so soul-sucking that he felt drained.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: draining - causing someone to feel very tired or weak
  • Antonym: uplifting - making someone feel more cheerful or optimistic
