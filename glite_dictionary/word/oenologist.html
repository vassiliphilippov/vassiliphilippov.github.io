oenologist

1. NOUN

/ iˈnɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / iːˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who is an expert in the science and study of wine making
specialist in wine making

Examples

The oenologist carefully monitored the fermentation process to ensure the wine developed its best flavor.
As an oenologist, she travels to vineyards around the world to consult on wine production.
The winery hired an experienced oenologist to improve their overall wine quality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: viticulturist - an expert in the cultivation of grapevines

Etymology

From Greek oinos (wine) + -logist (one who studies), via French oenologue.
