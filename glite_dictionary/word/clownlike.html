clownlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈklaʊnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈklaʊn.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a clown, especially in appearance or behavior; amusing or foolish in a playful way.
resembling a clown

Examples

His clownlike smile made the children laugh during the party.
The performer adopted a clownlike outfit with oversized shoes and a red nose.
Whenever she made a clownlike gesture, the audience burst into laughter.

Synset Examples

a buffoonish walk
a clownish face
a zany sense of humor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clownish - acting in a silly or foolish manner like a clown
  • Synonym: buffoonish - ridiculously or foolishly amusing, like a buffoon
