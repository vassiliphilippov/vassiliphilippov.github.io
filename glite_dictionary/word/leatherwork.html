leatherwork

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛðɚˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈlɛðəˌwɜːk / (BrE)
objects made from leather, or the craft of making things out of leather.
objects or craft made from leather

Examples

She admired the detailed leatherwork on the cowboy's saddle.
Traditional leatherwork requires skill, patience, and quality materials.
The shop specialized in handmade leatherwork such as belts and wallets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leathercraft - the skill of making objects from leather

Etymology

From 'leather' + 'work', indicating objects or craftsmanship involving leather.
