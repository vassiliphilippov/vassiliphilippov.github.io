erogenous

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈrɑdʒənəs / (AmE), / ɪˈrɒdʒənəs / (BrE)
(of a part of the body) sensitive to sexual stimulation.
sexually sensitive (body part)

Examples

The neck is considered an erogenous zone for many people.
Some perfumes are designed to be applied to erogenous areas of the body.
He discovered that even a gentle touch on her arm was quite erogenous.

Etymology

From Greek erōgenēs, from eros (love, sexual desire) + -genēs (born of, produced by).
