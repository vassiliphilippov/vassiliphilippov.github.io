cameraman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæmərəˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈkamərəˌman / (BrE)
a person who operates a movie or television camera, especially one engaged in professional film or broadcast production
person who operates a movie or TV camera

Examples

The cameraman followed the actors closely during the action sequence.
She worked as a cameraman on several independent films before moving to Hollywood.
A skilled cameraman can capture stunning visuals even in difficult lighting conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camera operator - person who operates a camera, regardless of gender

Etymology

From 'camera' + 'man', formed in English in the early 20th century.
