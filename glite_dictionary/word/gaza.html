Gaza

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡɑzə / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːzə / (BrE)
a coastal region at the southeastern corner of the Mediterranean, bordering Israel and Egypt, forming part of the Palestinian territories
region bordering Israel and Egypt

Examples

A ceasefire was recently declared in Gaza after several days of fighting.
