damozel

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæm.ə.zɛl / (AmE), / ˈdæm.ə.zɛl / (BrE)
(Archaic, literary) a young unmarried woman; a maiden.
young unmarried woman (arch., literary)
archaic

Examples

The poet described the damozel as gazing down from heaven with longing in her eyes.
In the ancient ballad, a lone damozel awaited her knight's return by the riverbank.
They spoke of the mysterious damozel whose laughter echoed through the old castle halls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: damsel - a young, unmarried woman, especially in historical or literary use

Etymology

From Old French *damisele*, diminutive of *dame* (lady).
