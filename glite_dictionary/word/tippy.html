tippy

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɪpi / (AmE), / ˈtɪpi / (BrE)
(of a boat or similar object) unstable and likely to tip over easily.
unstable; likely to tip over

Examples

The canoe was so tippy that we were afraid it might capsize at any moment.
Small sailboats can feel quite tippy for beginners out on the water.
Because the raft was tippy, everyone had to sit still to avoid falling in.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stable - not likely to tip or overturn
