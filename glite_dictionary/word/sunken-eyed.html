sunken-eyed

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌŋkən aɪd / (AmE), / ˈsʌŋkən aɪd / (BrE)
(of a person or face) having eyes that appear deeply set or hollow, often due to illness, fatigue, or age.
having deeply set or hollow eyes

Examples

After weeks of illness, his sunken-eyed appearance worried his friends and family.
The sunken-eyed man trudged into the clinic, clearly exhausted and in poor health.
Photographs of famine victims often show sunken-eyed faces haunted by hunger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hollow-eyed - having eyes that appear hollow or deep-set
  • Antonym: bright-eyed - having lively or alert eyes
