Andrew Wyeth

1. PROPN

/ ˈændru ˈwaɪəθ / (AmE), / ˈændru ˈwaɪəθ / (BrE)
(1917–2009) An American painter known for his realistic depictions of rural life, particularly in Pennsylvania and Maine.
American realist painter (1917–2009)

Examples

Andrew Wyeth is best known for his painting "Christina's World," which has become an American icon.
An exhibition of Andrew Wyeth's watercolors attracted visitors from all over the world.
Many critics praise Andrew Wyeth for his meticulous attention to detail and atmospheric landscapes.
