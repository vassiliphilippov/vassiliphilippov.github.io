pillowcase

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪloʊˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɪləʊkeɪs / (BrE)
A removable cloth cover for a pillow, used to keep it clean and for decoration.
cloth cover for a pillow

Examples

She changed the pillowcase on her bed every week to keep it fresh.
The guests found an extra pillowcase in the closet when they needed a clean one.
He used a white pillowcase to carry his trick-or-treat candy on Halloween.

Synset Examples

the burglar carried his loot in a pillowcase

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pillow slip - another term for pillowcase
