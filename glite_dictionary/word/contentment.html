contentment

1. NOUN

/ kənˈtɛntmənt / (AmE), / kənˈtɛntmənt / (BrE)
a state of happiness and satisfaction with one's situation or life
state of being satisfied or happy

Examples

She found contentment in the quiet life of the countryside.
After years of searching, he finally achieved contentment with his career and family.
Many people believe contentment comes not from wealth, but from appreciating what you have.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: satisfaction - the feeling of pleasure from fulfilling a desire or need
  • Antonym: discontent - lack of satisfaction with one's circumstances
  • Synonym: happiness - the state of being happy

Etymology

From Old French 'contentement', from Latin 'contentus' (pleased).
