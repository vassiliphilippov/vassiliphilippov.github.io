short-haired

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɔrtˌhɛrd / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːtˌhɛəd / (BrE)
Having short hair, especially referring to animals or people.
having short hair

Examples

The shelter has several short-haired cats available for adoption.
Her new puppy is a short-haired breed that sheds very little.
He prefers short-haired styles because they are easier to maintain.

Synset Examples

a short-haired dog

Related Terms

  • Antonym: long-haired - having long hair
