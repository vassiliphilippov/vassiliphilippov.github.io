A genus of deciduous trees in the family Hamamelidaceae, native to the Himalayas.
genus of Himalayan deciduous trees

Examples

The Parrotiopsis genus is well known for its resilience to cold mountain climates.
Botanists discovered a rare species belonging to the Parrotiopsis genus in the Himalayas.
The flowers of Parrotiopsis trees are small and clustered, making them easily recognizable to plant enthusiasts.

Related Terms
