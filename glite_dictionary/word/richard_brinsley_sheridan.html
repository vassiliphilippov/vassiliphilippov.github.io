Richard Brinsley Sheridan

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɪtʃərd ˈbrɪnzli ˈʃɛrɪdən / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃəd ˈbrɪnzli ˈʃɛrɪdən / (BrE)
(1751–1816) An Irish playwright and politician celebrated for his satirical comedies of manners, including 'The School for Scandal' and 'The Rivals'.
Irish playwright and satirist (1751–1816)
