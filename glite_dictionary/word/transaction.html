transaction

1. NOUN

/ trænˈzækʃən / (AmE), / trænˈzækʃən / (BrE)
an instance of carrying out a business or commercial exchange; the act of transacting or conducting business between parties
an act of doing business or an exchange

Examples

The bank charges a small fee for each transaction you make.
Every transaction in the database must be recorded accurately.
After completing the transaction, she received a confirmation email.

Synset Examples

no transactions are possible without him
he has always been honest in his dealings with me

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deal - an agreement or arrangement, especially in business
  • Synonym: exchange - the act of giving one thing and receiving another
  • Synonym: purchase - the act of buying something

Etymology

From Latin transactio (agreement), from transigere (to drive through, accomplish).
