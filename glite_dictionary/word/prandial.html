prandial

1. ADJ

/ ˈprændiəl / (AmE), / ˈprændɪəl / (BrE)
relating to a meal, especially used in medical or formal contexts.
relating to a meal
medical

Examples

The doctor measured his blood sugar in the prandial state.
Some medications are best taken in relation to the prandial period.
Scientists study prandial changes in metabolism to understand digestion.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: postprandial - occurring after a meal
  • Degree Lower: preprandial - occurring before a meal

Etymology

From Latin prandium (a meal, especially breakfast or lunch) + -al.
