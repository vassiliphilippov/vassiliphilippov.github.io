Norwegian

1. NOUN

/ nɔːrˈwiːdʒən / (AmE), / nɔːˈwiːdʒən / (BrE)
a native or citizen of Norway
a person from Norway

Examples

She met a Norwegian who shared stories about his homeland.
Many Norwegians enjoy outdoor activities like skiing and hiking.
As a Norwegian, he is proud of his country's rich cultural traditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Norway - a country in northern Europe

Etymology

From Norway + -an, via Medieval Latin Norwegia

3. NOUN

/ nɔrˈwiːdʒən / (AmE), / nɔːˈwiːdʒən / (BrE)
the Scandinavian language spoken in Norway, belonging to the North Germanic language group
language of Norway

Examples

She is fluent in Norwegian as well as English.
The documentary was translated from Norwegian into several other languages.
Learning Norwegian can be challenging for English speakers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Danish - the North Germanic language spoken in Denmark
  • Synonym: Swedish - the North Germanic language spoken in Sweden

Etymology

From Medieval Latin Norwegia (Norway) + English -an.
