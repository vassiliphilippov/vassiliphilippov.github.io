greenish-gray

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡriːnɪʃ ˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnɪʃ ˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
of a grey color with a hint or tinge of green
grey tinged with green

Examples

The walls were painted a subtle greenish-gray that gave the room a calm feel.
She wore a greenish-gray scarf that matched her eyes perfectly.
Mist rose above the greenish-gray surface of the lake at dawn.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gray - a color between black and white
  • Degree Higher: green - a color between blue and yellow
