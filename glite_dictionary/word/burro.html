burro

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɜroʊ / (AmE), / ˈbʌrəʊ / (BrE)
A small donkey, especially one used as a pack animal.
small donkey used as a pack animal

Examples

The burro carried supplies up the steep mountain trail.
Farmers in the region often rely on the burro for transportation.
We rented a burro to help us carry our gear during the hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: donkey - Domesticated mammal related to the horse, used as a beast of burden

Etymology

From Spanish 'burro' (donkey).
