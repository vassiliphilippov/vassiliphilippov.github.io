scum bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈskʌm ˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈskʌm ˌbaɡ / (BrE)
an insult for a person regarded as despicable, contemptible, or morally reprehensible
insult for a despicable person
informal slang

Examples

I can't believe he lied to your face; what a scum bag!
The police finally arrested the scum bag behind the series of break-ins.
Don't waste your time arguing with a scum bag like him.

Synset Examples

only a rotter would do that

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rat - an insulting term for a contemptible person
  • Synonym: rotter - British term for a despicable or untrustworthy person
  • Synonym: git - British informal term for a contemptible person

Etymology

From 'scum' (refuse or filth) + 'bag'; originally US slang (1930s).
