individual

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪndəˈvɪdʒuəl / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈvɪdjʊəl / (BrE)
a single human being or organism, regarded separately from a group
single person or organism

Examples

Each individual in the study was given a unique identification number.
The zoo is careful to monitor the health of every individual animal.
No two individuals are exactly alike, even among twins.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: group - a number of individuals considered together

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'individualis', from Latin 'individuus' meaning undivided.
