recipient

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsɪpiənt / (AmE), / rɪˈsɪpiənt / (BrE)
the person or animate entity that receives something, especially as the person affected by an action in a sentence
person or entity that receives something

Examples

The recipient of the award was clearly moved by the honor.
In this sentence, the recipient is indicated by the word 'her'.
Donations will be distributed to every recipient equally.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: giver - the person who gives something

Etymology

From Latin 'recipiens', present participle of 'recipere' (to receive).

2. NOUN

/ rɪˈsɪpiənt / (AmE), / rɪˈsɪpiənt / (BrE)
a person who receives something, such as a gift, award, or message
person who receives something

Examples

The award was presented to the youngest recipient in the program's history.
Please check that you have entered the correct email address for each recipient.
The charity relies on the gratitude of every recipient of its services.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: giver - a person who gives something
  • Synonym: beneficiary - a person who benefits from something, especially a trust or will

Etymology

From Latin 'recipiens', present participle of 'recipere' (to receive).
