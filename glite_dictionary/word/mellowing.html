mellowing

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛloʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɛləʊɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of becoming mellow, especially softer or more relaxed in character, taste, or color
process of becoming mellow

Examples

The mellowing of the cheese gives it a smoother, richer flavor.
His mellowing over the years made him a more approachable leader.
The mellowing tones of the sunset colored the sky beautifully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: softening - the act of becoming softer
  • Antonym: hardening - the act of becoming harder or more severe
