unbacked

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbækt / (AmE), / ʌnˈbækt / (BrE)
Not supported or endorsed by others; lacking backing or support.
not supported by others

Examples

After submitting his proposal, he realized he was completely unbacked at the meeting.
The unbacked candidate struggled to gain public attention.
She felt unbacked when no colleagues agreed with her suggestion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unsupported - not given support or assistance
  • Antonym: supported - given support or backing

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'backed' (supported).
