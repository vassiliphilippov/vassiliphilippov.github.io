unexciting

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪkˈsaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈsaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
not interesting, stimulating, or thrilling; dull or lacking excitement.
not interesting or stimulating

Examples

The film was visually stunning but ultimately unexciting in terms of story.
I find his lectures rather unexciting compared to other professors.
Despite the hype, the event turned out to be surprisingly unexciting.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: exciting - causing great enthusiasm and eagerness
  • Synonym: dull - lacking interest or excitement

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + exciting.
