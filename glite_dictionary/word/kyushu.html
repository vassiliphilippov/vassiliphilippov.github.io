Kyushu

1. PROPN

/ ˈkjuˌʃu / (AmE), / ˈkjuːʃuː / (BrE)
the southernmost of the four main islands of Japan, known for its coal fields and active volcanoes
southern main island of Japan

Examples

Agriculture and industry both play major roles in Kyushu's economy.
She traveled through Kyushu to experience its famous hot springs and unique culture.
Kyushu is renowned for its beautiful landscapes and active volcanoes.

Etymology

From Japanese 九州 (Kyūshū), meaning 'nine provinces.'
