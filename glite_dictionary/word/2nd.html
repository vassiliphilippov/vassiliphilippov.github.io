2nd

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛk.ənd / (AmE), / ˈsɛk.ənd / (BrE)
coming next after the first in position in a sequence, in space, time, order, or rank; ordinal form of two
ordinal number for position after first

Examples

She finished 2nd in the race, just behind her teammate.
Today is the 2nd of May, so rent is due.
His office is on the 2nd floor of the building.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: first - coming before all others in time or order; ordinal for '1'
  • Degree Higher: third - coming next after the second; ordinal for '3'

Etymology

From Middle English, based on Latin 'secundus' meaning 'following' or 'second'.
