Relanium

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈleɪniəm / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪniəm / (BrE)
A proprietary drug containing diazepam, used as a tranquilizer, available mainly in Lithuania and Poland.
a brand of diazepam tranquilizer
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Relanium to help control her anxiety.
In Lithuania, Relanium is often used in hospitals as a sedative.
He was given Relanium after his surgery to help him relax.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diazepam - a drug used to treat anxiety and muscle spasms
