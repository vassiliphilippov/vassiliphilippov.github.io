ill-bred

1. ADJ

/ ɪlˈbrɛd / (AmE), / ɪlˈbrɛd / (BrE)
Lacking good manners or refinement; rude or impolite in behavior.
lacking good manners or refinement

Examples

The guests were offended by his ill-bred comments at dinner.
Many people found her ill-bred behavior shocking at such a formal event.
Teachers try to correct ill-bred habits in young children.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: well-bred - having or showing good manners or refinement
  • Synonym: rude - lacking manners, impolite

Etymology

Formed from 'ill' (badly) + 'bred' (brought up).
