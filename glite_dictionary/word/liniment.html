liniment

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪnəmənt / (AmE), / ˈlɪnɪmənt / (BrE)
A medicinal liquid that is rubbed into the skin to relieve muscular stiffness and pain.
medicinal liquid for muscle pain
medical

Examples

She applied the liniment to her sore shoulder after an intense workout.
The doctor recommended using a liniment to ease the pain in his aching back.
This herbal liniment is said to be effective for relieving muscle cramps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: balm - a fragrant ointment or preparation used to heal or soothe the skin
  • Synonym: ointment - a smooth oily substance applied to the skin for medicinal purposes

Etymology

From Latin 'linimentum', from 'linere' (to smear).
