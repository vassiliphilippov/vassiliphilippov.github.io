Cosimo de' Medici

1. PROPN

/ ˈkoʊzɪmoʊ deɪ ˈmɛdɪtʃi / (AmE), / ˈkɒzɪməʊ deɪ ˈmɛdɪtʃi / (BrE)
(1389–1464) Italian financier and statesman; the first of the Medici political dynasty, influential in Florence and a patron of the arts.
Italian statesman and Medici dynasty founder

Examples

Cosimo de' Medici established the Medici family as rulers of Florence through his political acumen and wealth.
Many artists and scholars flourished in Florence under the patronage of Cosimo de' Medici.
The legacy of Cosimo de' Medici can still be seen in the art and architecture of Renaissance Florence.
