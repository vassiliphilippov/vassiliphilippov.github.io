abode

1. NOUN

/ əˈboʊd / (AmE), / əˈbəʊd / (BrE)
a place where someone lives; a residence or dwelling
place where one lives

Examples

They invited us into their humble abode for tea and conversation.
He has called this old cottage his abode for over thirty years.
You must provide your current abode when filling out the registration form.

Synset Examples

a person can have several residences

Related Terms

  • Synonym: residence - a place where someone lives
  • Synonym: dwelling - a place of residence
  • Synonym: home - the place where one lives

Etymology

From Old English 'abidan' (to wait, remain), via Middle English 'abode', meaning act of waiting or staying.
