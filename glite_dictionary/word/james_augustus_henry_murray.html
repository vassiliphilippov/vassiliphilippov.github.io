James Augustus Henry Murray

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒeɪmz əˈɡʌstəs ˈhɛnri ˈmʌri / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪmz ɔːˈɡʌstəs ˈhɛnri ˈmʌri / (BrE)
(1837–1915) Scottish philologist and lexicographer, chief editor who shaped the first Oxford English Dictionary.
Scottish lexicographer; OED chief editor

Examples

James Augustus Henry Murray dedicated decades of his life to compiling the Oxford English Dictionary.
The editorial methods of James Augustus Henry Murray greatly influenced modern lexicography.
The legacy of James Augustus Henry Murray is celebrated by linguists worldwide.
