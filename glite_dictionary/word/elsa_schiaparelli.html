Elsa Schiaparelli

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛl.sə ˌskjɑː.pɑːˈrɛl.i / (AmE), / ˈɛl.sə ˌskiː.ə.pəˈrɛl.i / (BrE)
(1896–1973) an Italian-born fashion designer renowned for her innovative use of synthetic materials and bold colors.
Italian fashion designer (1896–1973)

Examples

The surrealist designs of Elsa Schiaparelli revolutionized women's fashion in the twentieth century.
Many celebrities admired the daring aesthetic of Elsa Schiaparelli in the 1930s and 1940s.
Modern runways often pay tribute to Elsa Schiaparelli by featuring vivid colors and unusual fabrics.
