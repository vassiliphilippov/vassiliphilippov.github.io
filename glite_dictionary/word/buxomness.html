buxomness

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌksəmnəs / (AmE), / ˈbʌksəmnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being attractively plump and full-bosomed, especially in women.
the state of being buxom

Examples

The actress was admired for her buxomness and vivacious personality.
Some consider buxomness a sign of traditional beauty in certain societies.
The artist's portrait celebrated the buxomness of the figure with graceful curves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buxom - attractively plump and full-bosomed, especially referring to women
