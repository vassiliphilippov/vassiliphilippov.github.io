kindled

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɪndəld / (AmE), / ˈkɪndld / (BrE)
(of something) set alight or ignited; made to burn or glow.
set alight; ignited

Examples

The kindled logs gave off a warm, welcoming glow.
She admired the kindled lanterns lining the garden path.
Only a small, kindled candle lit up the entire room.

Synset Examples

the ignited paper
a kindled fire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ignited - set on fire; kindled
  • Antonym: extinguished - put out (a fire); stopped from burning

Etymology

Past participle of 'kindle', from Middle English kindlen, perhaps from Old Norse 'kynda' (to kindle).
