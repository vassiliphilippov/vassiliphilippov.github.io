bulb-shaped

1. ADJ

/ bʌlb ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / bʌlb ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the round, swollen shape of a bulb; resembling a bulb in form.
resembling the shape of a bulb

Examples

The vase had a distinct bulb-shaped base that made it stand out on the shelf.
Many flowers have bulb-shaped roots that store nutrients underground.
The architect designed the building with a unique bulb-shaped dome.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulbous - having a rounded or swollen shape like a bulb
