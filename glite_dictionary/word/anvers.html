Anvers

1. PROPN

/ ɑ̃ˈvɛər / (AmE), / ɑ̃ˈvɛː / (BrE)
The French name for Antwerp, a busy port and financial center in northern Belgium, known for its diamond industry.
French name for Antwerp, Belgian city

Examples

Many European train timetables refer to Antwerp as Anvers.
She traveled from Paris to Anvers for a conference on the diamond trade.
The historic architecture of Anvers attracts visitors from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Antwerp - A major port city in northern Belgium

Etymology

French form of 'Antwerp'.
