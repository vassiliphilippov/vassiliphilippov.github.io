Gulo

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːloʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡuːləʊ / (BrE)
A genus of mammals in the weasel family (Mustelidae), including the wolverine.
genus of mammals including the wolverine

Examples

The wolverine is the only living species in the genus Gulo.
Biologists studied the distribution of Gulo across northern forests.
Fossil evidence suggests that extinct relatives of Gulo once inhabited wider regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wolverine - large carnivorous mammal of the genus Gulo

Etymology

From Latin gulo (glutton), used as the genus name by Linnaeus.
