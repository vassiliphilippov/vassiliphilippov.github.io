heatedly

1. ADV

/ ˈhiːtɪdli / (AmE), / ˈhiːtɪdli / (BrE)
in an intense or passionate manner, especially when showing strong emotion or anger
in a passionate or angry manner

Examples

She spoke heatedly during the debate, her passion clearly evident to everyone in the room.
They argued heatedly about the best way to solve the problem.
The negotiations became tense as both sides discussed their positions heatedly.

Synset Examples

‘To say I am behind the strike is so much nonsense,’ declared Mr Harvey heatedly
the children were arguing hotly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hotly - in an intense or passionate manner

Etymology

From 'heated' (past participle of 'heat') + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
