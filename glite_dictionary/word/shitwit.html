shitwit

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtwɪt / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtwɪt / (BrE)
a person regarded as stupid or annoying (slang, vulgar)
foolish or annoying person (slang, vulgar)
slang vulgar

Examples

He got himself banned from the group for being such a shitwit in every discussion.
Sometimes I wonder how a shitwit like him keeps his job.
Don't let that shitwit bother you; he's always trying to get a reaction.

Synset Examples

I can't believe he acted like such a shitwit yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a person of low intelligence or foolishness
  • Antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence
