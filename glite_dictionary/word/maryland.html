Maryland

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɛr.ə.lənd / (AmE), / ˈmɛə.lənd / (BrE)
a state in the eastern United States, one of the original 13 colonies, with Annapolis as its capital
US state in the Mid-Atlantic region

Examples

Tourists often visit Maryland for its historic landmarks and beautiful Chesapeake Bay shores.
She grew up in Maryland, attending school in Baltimore before moving to New York.
The capital of Maryland is Annapolis, which is famous for its naval academy.

Etymology

Named after Queen Henrietta Maria, wife of King Charles I of England.
