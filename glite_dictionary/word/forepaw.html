forepaw

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrˌpɔ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːpɔː / (BrE)
the front foot of a four-legged animal, especially one having claws or pads and analogous to the human hand
front foot of a four-legged animal

Examples

The cat licked its forepaw carefully after stepping outside.
A dog's forepaw is similar in structure to a human hand.
The raccoon grasped the food with its agile forepaw.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hindpaw - the back foot of a four-legged animal
  • Synonym: paw - the foot of an animal with claws or pads

Etymology

From 'fore-' (meaning 'front') + 'paw'.
