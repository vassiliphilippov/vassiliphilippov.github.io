loud-voiced

1. ADJ

/ laʊd vɔɪst / (AmE), / laʊd vɔɪst / (BrE)
having a voice that is unusually loud or powerful
having an unusually loud voice

Examples

The loud-voiced announcer could be heard clearly across the stadium.
She grew tired of her loud-voiced neighbor's constant shouting.
A loud-voiced child dominated the classroom conversation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: soft-spoken - having a gentle, quiet voice
