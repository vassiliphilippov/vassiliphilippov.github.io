Viagra

1. PROPN

/ vaɪˈæɡrə / (AmE), / vaɪˈaːɡrə / (BrE)
A trademarked drug used to treat erectile dysfunction in men by increasing blood flow to the penis.
drug for erectile dysfunction
medical

Examples

Many men have turned to Viagra as a solution for erectile dysfunction.
Doctors may prescribe Viagra to patients who experience difficulty maintaining an erection.
After taking Viagra, he noticed a significant improvement in his sexual performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sildenafil - the active ingredient in Viagra, a medication for erectile dysfunction

Etymology

Trademark; coined as a trade name by Pfizer in the 1990s.
