to oxygenise

1. VERB

/ ˈɑksɪdʒəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɒksɪdʒənaɪz / (BrE)
to impregnate, combine, or supply with oxygen; to treat or enrich with oxygen
supply or combine with oxygen

Examples

Doctors often oxygenise a patient's blood during surgery to ensure proper tissue function.
The green plants help to oxygenise the atmosphere through photosynthesis.
This machine is designed to oxygenise water in aquariums.

Synset Examples

oxygenate blood

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to oxygenate - to supply or enrich with oxygen

Etymology

Formed from 'oxygen' + suffix '-ise', after French 'oxygéner'.
