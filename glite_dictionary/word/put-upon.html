put-upon

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʊt əˌpɑn / (AmE), / ˈpʊt əˌpɒn / (BrE)
(of a person) taken advantage of or treated unfairly by others.
taken advantage of by others

Examples

She felt put-upon after doing her coworker's tasks without any thanks.
He always seems put-upon when his family relies on him too much.
After days of extra chores, the children complained about being put-upon.

Synset Examples

after going out of his way to help his friend get the job he felt not appreciated but used

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exploited - (of a person) used unfairly for someone else's benefit
  • Synonym: taken advantage of - used to describe someone who is unfairly used by others
