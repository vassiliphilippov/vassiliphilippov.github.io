sportively

1. ADV

/ ˈspɔrtɪvli / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtɪvli / (BrE)
in a playful or merry manner; with lively, lighthearted spirit
in a playful or merry way

Examples

The children danced sportively around the garden, laughing at each other's antics.
He sportively challenged his friends to a friendly game of chess after dinner.
The puppy sportively chased its tail, delighting everyone watching.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: playfully - in a playful or fun way

Etymology

From 'sportive' + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
