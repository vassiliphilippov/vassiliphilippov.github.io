pièce de résistance

1. NOUN

/ pjɛs də ɹeɪ.zɪˈstɑns / (AmE), / pjɛs də ɹe.zɪˈstɑ̃ːs / (BrE)
the outstanding or most important item in a collection, group, or series; the main highlight or attraction
main highlight or outstanding item

Examples

The art exhibit's pièce de résistance was a stunning sculpture by a local artist.
At the dinner, the chef unveiled his pièce de résistance, a beautifully roasted duck.
Every magician has a pièce de résistance that leaves the audience speechless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: highlight - the most important or memorable part
  • Synonym: centerpiece - the most important or central feature

Etymology

French, literally 'piece of resistance', meaning the chief dish of a meal or the main event.
