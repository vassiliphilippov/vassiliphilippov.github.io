worldly-minded

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɝːldli ˌmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldli ˌmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
concerned chiefly with material interests and practical affairs rather than spiritual or religious values
focused on material and practical affairs

Examples

Despite his religious upbringing, he became increasingly worldly-minded as he built his career.
Many critics argue that modern society is too worldly-minded and neglects spiritual values.
Her worldly-minded attitude led her to prioritize wealth over personal relationships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: materialistic - excessively concerned with material possessions

Etymology

Formed from 'worldly' and 'minded', indicating a mindset or inclination toward worldly matters.
