Rotarian

1. NOUN

/ roʊˈtɛriən / (AmE), / rəʊˈtɛəriən / (BrE)
a member of a Rotary Club, an international service organization
member of a Rotary Club

Examples

She became a proud Rotarian after participating in several community service projects.
Many Rotarians gather weekly to plan charitable activities.
As a Rotarian, he values the organization's commitment to ethical leadership.

Etymology

Formed from Rotary (Club) + -an (member of).
