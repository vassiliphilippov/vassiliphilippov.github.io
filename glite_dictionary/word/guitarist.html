guitarist

1. NOUN

/ ɡəˈtɑrɪst / (AmE), / ɡɪˈtɑːrɪst / (BrE)
a person who plays the guitar, especially professionally or as a performer
person who plays the guitar

Examples

The band's lead guitarist played an impressive solo during the concert.
She has been a classical guitarist for over twenty years.
Many famous guitarists have influenced the evolution of rock music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musician - person skilled in music, especially professional

Etymology

From guitar + -ist (a person who practices or is concerned with something).
