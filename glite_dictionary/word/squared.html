squared

1. ADJ

/ skwɛrd / (AmE), / skwɛəd / (BrE)
made square in shape; having equal sides and right angles.
made square in shape

Examples

The wooden frame was carefully squared before assembly.

All the tiles on the wall were perfectly squared for a neat appearance.
