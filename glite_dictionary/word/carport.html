carport

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrˌpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpɔːt / (BrE)
A shelter for one or more cars, usually consisting of a flat roof supported by posts, typically open at the sides.
shelter for cars with open sides

Examples

We decided to build a carport instead of a garage to save money.
The carport kept the car safe from hail during the storm.
Several bikes were parked alongside the carport near the house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garage - a building or shed for housing a motor vehicle

Etymology

Formed from 'car' + 'port', relating to sheltering or parking a car.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrˌpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈkɑːˌpɔːt / (BrE)
a shelter for one or more cars, typically consisting of a flat roof supported by posts and open at the sides
shelter for one or more cars, often open-sided

Examples

They decided to install a carport next to the house to protect their vehicles from the sun.
Our new apartment comes with a covered carport instead of a traditional garage.
The storm damaged the roof of the carport, but the cars were unharmed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garage - a building for housing a motor vehicle or vehicles

Etymology

Blend of 'car' and 'port' (as in 'harbor' or 'portal'), first used in the 1930s.
