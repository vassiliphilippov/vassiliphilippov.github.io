unconquerable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkɑŋkərəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɒŋkərəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to defeat or overcome
impossible to defeat

Examples

Despite all odds, her determination remained unconquerable throughout her life.
The fortress stood unconquerable for centuries, defying every invading army.
His unconquerable spirit inspired the entire team to persevere.

Synset Examples

insuperable odds
insuperable heroes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invincible - too powerful to be defeated or overcome
  • Synonym: insuperable - impossible to overcome; insurmountable
  • Antonym: vincible - able to be defeated or overcome

Etymology

From un- (not) + conquer + -able, meaning not able to be conquered.
