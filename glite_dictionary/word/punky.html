punky

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈpʌŋki / (BrE)
A very small, biting, two-winged insect that feeds on the blood of mammals, birds, or other insects; especially a biting midge or no-see-um.
small biting midge; no-see-um

Examples

We couldn't enjoy our picnic because the punkies kept biting our ankles.
The forest was thick with mosquitoes and punkies at dusk.
After hiking, I noticed several itchy bites from punkies on my arms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: midge - small two-winged biting insect
  • Synonym: no-see-um - tiny biting insect, especially biting midges
