measuredly

1. ADV

/ ˈmɛʒɚdli / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒədli / (BrE)
in a careful and deliberate way; without haste or impulsiveness
in a deliberate, unhurried manner

Examples

She spoke measuredly, choosing her words with great care.
He walked measuredly across the room, giving everyone time to notice him.
The judge responded measuredly to the heated arguments in court.

Synset Examples

she was working deliberately

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deliberately - in a careful and intentional way
  • Antonym: hurriedly - in a rushed or hasty manner

Etymology

From the adjective 'measured' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
