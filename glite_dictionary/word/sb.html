SB

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛs ˈbi / (AmE), / ˌɛs ˈbiː / (BrE)
a Bachelor of Science degree; an undergraduate academic degree awarded for completed courses in science or related fields
Bachelor of Science degree (abbreviation)

Examples

She graduated with an SB in chemical engineering from MIT last year.
Many employers look for candidates who hold an SB or higher qualification in the sciences.
After earning his SB, he decided to pursue a master's degree in biology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: BS - abbreviation for Bachelor of Science degree
  • Antonym: BA - abbreviation for Bachelor of Arts degree

Etymology

From Latin scientiae baccalaureus, meaning 'bachelor of science'.
