Sb

1. NOUN

/ ɛs ˈbi / (AmE), / ɛs ˈbiː / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for antimony, a metallic element used in alloys and found in stibnite
symbol for antimony (element)

Examples

Stibnite is the primary ore of Sb in the mining industry.
The chemist analyzed the sample for traces of Sb.
Alloys containing Sb are used to increase hardness and strength.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antimony - a brittle, silvery-white metallic element

Etymology

From Latin stibium, the origin of the chemical symbol for antimony.
