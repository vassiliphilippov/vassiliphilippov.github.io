Mustela nigripes

1. NOUN

/ mʌˈstelə ˈnaɪɡrəˌpiz / (AmE), / mʌˈstiːlə ˈnaɪɡriˌpiːz / (BrE)
A species of mustelid mammal, the black-footed ferret, native to prairie regions of the United States and now nearly extinct in the wild.
black-footed ferret species

Examples

The Mustela nigripes is considered one of the most endangered mammals in North America.
Conservationists have worked tirelessly to reintroduce Mustela nigripes into its natural habitat.
Few people have ever seen a wild Mustela nigripes due to its rarity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black-footed ferret - a common name for Mustela nigripes, a small North American mustelid

Etymology

From Latin: 'Mustela' meaning 'weasel'; 'nigripes' meaning 'black-footed'.
