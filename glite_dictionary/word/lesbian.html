Lesbian

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛz.bi.ən / (AmE), / ˈlɛz.bi.ən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of the island of Lesbos in Greece.
a resident of Lesbos, Greece

Examples

The ancient poet Sappho was a famous Lesbian from the island of Lesbos.
Several Lesbians participated in the island's cultural festival.
Historians have studied the role of prominent Lesbians in classical Greek society.

Etymology

From Lesbos, the Greek island. The English form derives from Latin 'Lesbius' and Greek 'Lesbios'.
