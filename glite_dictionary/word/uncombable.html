uncombable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkɑːməbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɒməbəl / (BrE)
(of hair or fibers) impossible or very difficult to comb into order.
impossible to comb (hair or fibers)

Examples

After sleeping on it, her hair was completely uncombable and stuck out in every direction.
The alpaca’s uncombable fleece made grooming a real challenge for the handlers.
His uncombable mop of curls gave him a wild, untamed look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tangled - (of hair, thread, etc.) twisted together in a messy way
  • Antonym: manageable - (of hair, material, etc.) able to be controlled or arranged easily

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'comb' + '-able'.
