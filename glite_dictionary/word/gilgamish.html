Gilgamish

1. PROPN

/ gɪlˈgɑːmɪʃ / (AmE), / gɪlˈɡæmɪʃ / (BrE)
a legendary king of Sumer and the central figure of a famous epic, known for his adventures and quests for immortality in ancient Mesopotamian mythology
legendary Sumerian king and epic hero

Examples

The ancient story of Gilgamish explores the themes of mortality and friendship.
Many scholars believe Gilgamish may have been based on a real king of Uruk.
The Epic of Gilgamish is considered one of the earliest works of literature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gilgamesh - legendary Sumerian king and epic hero (common alternate spelling)

Etymology

From Akkadian *Gilgameš*, the name of the hero in the ancient Mesopotamian epic.
