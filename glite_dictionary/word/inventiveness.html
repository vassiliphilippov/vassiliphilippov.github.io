inventiveness

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈvɛntɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɛntɪvnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being able to invent new things or think creatively
creative ability; originality

Examples

Her inventiveness impressed everyone at the company, especially when she presented her new product idea.
The film director is known for his remarkable inventiveness and unique visual style.
Children often display great inventiveness when given the freedom to play and create.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creativity - the use of imagination or original ideas
  • Synonym: originality - the ability to think independently and creatively

Etymology

From 'inventive' + '-ness', ultimately from Latin 'invenire' (to find, invent).
