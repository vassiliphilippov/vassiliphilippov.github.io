multicolored

1. ADJ

/ ˌmʌl.tiˈkʌl.ɚd / (AmE), / ˌmʌl.tiˈkʌl.əd / (BrE)
having many different colors or decorated with patches of various colors, often bright or contrasting
having many different colors

Examples

She wore a multicolored scarf that brightened up her whole outfit.
The parade featured a multicolored dragon winding through the crowded streets.
Children love to draw multicolored pictures with their crayons.

Synset Examples

a jester dressed in motley
the painted desert
a particolored dress
a piebald horse
pied daisies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colorful - having much or varied color; bright and varied in color

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'multi-' (meaning 'many') and 'colored'.
