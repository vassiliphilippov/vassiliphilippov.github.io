pastel-colored

1. ADJ

/ pæˈstɛl ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / pæˈstɛl ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having pale, soft, and delicate colors, often resembling those of artists' pastels
having pale, delicate colors

Examples

The living room was decorated with pastel-colored curtains and cushions, creating a calm and inviting space.
She wore a pastel-colored dress to the spring party, which matched the blooming flowers outside.
The bakery's display was full of pastel-colored cupcakes, perfect for the Easter celebration.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: vivid - having deep or bright colors
  • Synonym: pastel - a soft and delicate color or artist's crayon made from powdered pigment

Etymology

From 'pastel' (a crayon made of powdered pigment) + 'colored'.
