unrequested

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnrɪˈkwɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈkwɛstɪd / (BrE)
not asked for or requested; supplied or received without being sought or demanded.
not asked for or requested

Examples

The package contained several unrequested items that I did not order.
He received unrequested advice from his colleagues during the meeting.
The company apologized for sending unrequested promotional emails to its customers.

Synset Examples

the merchandise was unrequested

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unsolicited - not asked for or invited, especially in reference to offers or communications

Etymology

From the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' + 'requested' (past participle of 'request').
