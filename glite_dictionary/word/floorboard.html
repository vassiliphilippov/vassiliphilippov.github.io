floorboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɔrbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈflɔːbɔːd / (BrE)
the flat part of the floor in a vehicle, especially where passengers place their feet
floor of a vehicle interior

Examples

She accidentally dropped her phone onto the floorboard while getting out of the car.
Mud and leaves collected on the floorboard after our camping trip.
He found a coin wedged between the seat and the floorboard in the back of the van.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: floor - the lower surface of a room or vehicle on which one may walk

2. NOUN

/ ˈflɔrˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈflɔːbɔːd / (BrE)
A long, flat piece of wood used as part of the surface of a floor in a building.
board used in flooring

Examples

He replaced the broken floorboard in the hallway yesterday.
When you walk across the old house, a floorboard creaks under your feet.
She lifted a floorboard to hide the spare key underneath.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flooring - material used for covering a floor
