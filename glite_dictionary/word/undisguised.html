undisguised

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɪsˈɡaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪsˈɡaɪzd / (BrE)
Not concealed or hidden; clearly apparent or obvious.
not hidden or concealed

Examples

She looked at him with undisguised admiration, making her feelings perfectly clear.
His undisguised joy was evident when he received the news of his promotion.
The proposal was met with undisguised skepticism by several committee members.

Synset Examples

undisguised curiosity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obvious - easily seen or understood
  • Antonym: concealed - kept out of sight, hidden

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) and 'disguised' (altered in appearance).
