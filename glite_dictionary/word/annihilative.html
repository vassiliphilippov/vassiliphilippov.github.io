annihilative

1. ADJ

/ əˈnaɪ.ə.leɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / əˈnaɪ.ɪ.lə.tɪv / (BrE)
causing or capable of causing complete destruction; having the power to annihilate.
capable of causing complete destruction

Examples

The bomb was designed to have an annihilative impact on enemy targets.
Many feared the annihilative capabilities of modern weapons.
Her criticism was so annihilative that it left him speechless.

Synset Examples

possessing annihilative power
a devastating hurricane
the guns opened a withering fire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: destructive - causing great and irreparable damage

Etymology

From Latin 'annihilare' (to reduce to nothing) + '-ive' (forming adjectives).
