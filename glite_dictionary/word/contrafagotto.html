contrafagotto

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɒn.trə.fəˈɡɑː.toʊ / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.trə.fəˈɡɒt.əʊ / (BrE)
the contrabassoon; the largest and lowest-pitched instrument in the oboe family, a deep-sounding double-reed woodwind
large, low-pitched woodwind; contrabassoon

Examples

The orchestra featured a deep, resonant sound from the contrafagotto during the symphony's final movement.
He specialized in rare instruments, including the contrafagotto and the English horn.
The composer wrote a part for contrafagotto to give the piece additional depth and darkness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contrabassoon - a very large bassoon with a deep, low sound

Etymology

From Italian 'contrafagotto' (contrabassoon).
