hellgrammiate

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlˌɡræmi.eɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɛlˌɡræmi.eɪt / (BrE)
the large brown aquatic larva of the dobsonfly, commonly used as fishing bait
aquatic larva of the dobsonfly (bait)

Examples

The angler used a hellgrammiate as bait to catch trout in the fast-moving stream.
While exploring the riverbed, they discovered a wriggling hellgrammiate beneath a rock.
Fishermen often collect hellgrammiates for use in local fishing competitions.

Related Terms
