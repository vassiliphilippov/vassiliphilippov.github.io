scorpionweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɔr.pi.ənˌwid / (AmE), / ˈskɔː.pi.ənˌwiːd / (BrE)
any plant of the genus Phacelia, typically with coiled flower clusters and found in North America and elsewhere.
a plant of the genus Phacelia

Examples

The hillside was covered with blooming scorpionweed in the early spring.
Many pollinators are attracted to the distinctive blue flowers of scorpionweed.
Several species of scorpionweed are often used in wildflower gardens.
