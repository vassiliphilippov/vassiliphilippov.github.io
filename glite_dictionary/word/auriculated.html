auriculated

1. ADJ

/ ɔːˈrɪkjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ɔːˈrɪkjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
having ear-shaped appendages or lobes; possessing auricles (commonly used in biology and botany)
having ear-shaped lobes or auricles

Examples

The leaves of this plant are auriculated, giving them a distinctive appearance.
Some flowers have auriculated bases on their petals.
The shell's auriculated structure distinguishes it from other species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auriculate - having auricles; possessing ear-shaped appendages

Etymology

From Latin auriculatus, from auricula (external ear).
