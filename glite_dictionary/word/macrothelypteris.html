Macrothelypteris

1. NOUN

/ ˌmækroʊˌθɛlɪˈptɛrɪs / (AmE), / ˌmakrəʊˌθɛlɪˈptɛrɪs / (BrE)
A genus of medium to large terrestrial ferns native to tropical Asia, Polynesia, and Australia, now also naturalized in the Americas.
genus of large terrestrial ferns

Examples

The garden features a flourishing Macrothelypteris among its tropical fern collection.
Botanists identified the specimen as Macrothelypteris due to its distinctive frond structure.
Macrothelypteris has become naturalized in several regions outside its native range.

Etymology

From Greek 'makros' (large) and 'Thelypteris' (a genus name of ferns).
