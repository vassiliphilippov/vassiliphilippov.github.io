venerableness

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛnərəblnəs / (AmE), / ˈvɛnərəblnəs / (BrE)
The quality of deserving respect or reverence; being worthy of veneration.
the quality of deserving respect

Examples

The venerableness of the old professor was clear in every aspect of his life.
Many admired the venerableness of the ancient building, which had stood for centuries.
Her venerableness made her a respected figure in the community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: venerability - the quality or state of being venerable
  • Synonym: reverence - deep respect for someone or something
  • Antonym: disreputableness - the quality of not deserving respect

Etymology

Formed from 'venerable' + '-ness', ultimately from Latin 'venerabilis' (worthy of respect).
