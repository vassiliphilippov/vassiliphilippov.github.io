Don

1. NOUN

/ dɑn / (AmE), / dɒn / (BrE)
A Spanish courtesy title or form of address for men, prefixed to the forename.
Spanish courtesy title for men

Examples

In Spain, it is common to refer to respected older men as Don followed by their first name.
The locals greeted Don Carlos warmly when he entered the room.
She introduced him as Don Miguel, a prominent figure in the community.

Synset Examples

Don Roberto

Etymology

From Spanish 'don', from Latin 'dominus' meaning 'lord' or 'master'.
