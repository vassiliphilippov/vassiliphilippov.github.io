ill-scented

1. ADJ

/ ɪl ˈsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ɪl ˈsɛntɪd / (BrE)
having an unpleasant or offensive smell; malodorous.
having an unpleasant smell

Examples

The pond became ill-scented during the hot summer months.
An ill-scented breeze drifted in from the alley.
She wrinkled her nose at the ill-scented garbage bin.

Synset Examples

a foul odor
the kitchen smelled really funky

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malodorous - smelling very unpleasant
  • Antonym: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell

Etymology

From 'ill' (bad) and 'scented' (having a smell).
