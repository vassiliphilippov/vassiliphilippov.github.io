foxhound

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑksˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈfɒksˌhaʊnd / (BrE)
A medium-sized breed of dog with a glossy coat, developed specifically for hunting foxes.
breed of dog used for hunting foxes

Examples

The English foxhound is known for its stamina and keen sense of smell.
She raised three foxhounds on her country estate.
The foxhound chased the scent across fields and woods during the hunt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hound - a dog breed used for hunting

Etymology

From 'fox' + 'hound', indicating its purpose and function.
