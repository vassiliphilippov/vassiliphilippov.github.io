horned

1. ADJ

/ hɔrnd / (AmE), / hɔːnd / (BrE)
having one or more horns or hornlike parts, or having horns of a particular kind
having horns or hornlike parts

Examples

The horned viper is known for the protrusions above its eyes.
Long-horned beetles are found all over the world.
A horned owl perched silently on the branch, watching its surroundings.

Synset Examples

horned viper
great horned owl
the unicorn — a mythical horned beast
long-horned cattle

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hornless - lacking horns
