Demerol

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛmərˌɔl / (AmE), / ˈdɛmərɒl / (BrE)
A synthetic narcotic drug, used to treat moderate to severe pain, especially in medical settings; trade name for meperidine.
Synthetic narcotic painkiller (meperidine)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Demerol to manage her postoperative pain.
He experienced dizziness as a side effect after taking Demerol.
Nurses must record every dose of Demerol administered to patients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meperidine - the generic name for Demerol, a synthetic opioid painkiller
  • Degree Lower: morphine - a commonly used opioid painkiller
