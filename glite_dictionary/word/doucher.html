doucher

1. NOUN

/ ˈduːʃər / (AmE), / ˈduːʃə / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) An unpleasant or contemptible person; an insult similar to 'jerk' or 'asshole'.
(slang) contemptible person (offensive)
slang offensive vulgar

Examples

I can't believe doucher Dave ruined the meeting again with his rude comments.
Why does every group project seem to have at least one doucher in it?
Ignore that doucher over there—he's just trying to get attention.

Synset Examples

That doucher thinks he’s better than everyone else.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - an obnoxious or contemptible person (slang)
  • Synonym: asshole - offensive slang for a thoroughly unpleasant person

Etymology

Formed from slang 'douche' (an insult), + '-er'.
