surgical

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɝdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈsɜːdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
Done with exceptional precision and accuracy, especially in a manner resembling surgery.
done with great precision

Examples

The team carried out a surgical operation to remove the malware from their network.
The politician made a surgical critique of her opponent's policy proposal.
The military launched a surgical strike to minimize civilian casualties.

Synset Examples

a surgical air strike

Related Terms

  • Synonym: precise - marked by exactness and accuracy
  • Synonym: meticulous - showing great attention to detail

Etymology

From Latin 'chirurgicus', from Greek 'kheirourgikos', ultimately from 'kheirourgos' (working by hand).
