underlip

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌndɚˌlɪp / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌlɪp / (BrE)
the lower lip of the mouth
lower lip of the mouth

Examples

Her underlip trembled as she tried not to cry.
A small cut appeared on his underlip after the accident.
The actor's prominent underlip gave him a distinctive appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lower lip - the bottom lip of the mouth

Etymology

From under- (below) + lip.
