impeding

1. ADJ

/ ɪmˈpidɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪmˈpiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
preventing or slowing down movement or progress; obstructing.
obstructing or hindering movement

Examples

The impeding construction on Main Street caused traffic jams every morning.
Her progress was affected by several impeding obstacles along the path.
Fallen trees formed an impeding barrier after the storm.

Synset Examples

the clogging crowds of revelers overflowing into the street

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obstructing - blocking or hindering progress or movement
  • Antonym: facilitating - making an action or process easier

Etymology

Present participle of the verb 'impede', from Latin impedire (to shackle the feet, hinder).
