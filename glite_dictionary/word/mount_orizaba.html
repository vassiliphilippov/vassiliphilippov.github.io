Mount Orizaba

1. PROPN

/ maʊnt ɔːrɪˈzɑːbə / (AmE), / maʊnt ɒrɪˈzɑːbə / (BrE)
the highest peak in Mexico, an extinct volcano located between Mexico City and Veracruz in southern Mexico
highest mountain in Mexico

Examples

Climbers from around the world travel to Mount Orizaba to experience its challenging ascent.
Snow can be seen year-round on the summit of Mount Orizaba.
The view from Mount Orizaba extends for miles across the Mexican countryside.
