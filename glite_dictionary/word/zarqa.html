Zarqa

1. PROPN

/ zɑːrˈkɑː / (AmE), / zɑːˈkɑː / (BrE)
a city in northwestern Jordan, located northeast of Amman; known for its industrial importance
city in northwestern Jordan

Examples

She was born and raised in Zarqa, one of the largest cities in Jordan.
The factory in Zarqa provides jobs for thousands of residents.
On their trip to Jordan, they visited Amman, Petra, and Zarqa.

Etymology

From Arabic الزرقاء‎ (az-Zarqāʼ), meaning 'the blue (city or river)'.
