nitric

1. ADJ

/ ˈnaɪtrɪk / (AmE), / ˈnɪtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing nitrogen, especially as an oxidized compound
relating to nitrogen (chemistry)

Examples

The laboratory ordered a new batch of nitric acid for the experiment.
Exposure to nitric fumes can be hazardous without proper ventilation.
Some fertilizers release nitric compounds into the soil to increase nitrogen content.

Synset Examples

nitric acid

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: nitrous - relating to or containing nitrogen with lower oxidation state (as in nitrous oxide)

Etymology

From French *nitrique*, from nitre (niter) + -ique (-ic), relating to substances derived from niter.
