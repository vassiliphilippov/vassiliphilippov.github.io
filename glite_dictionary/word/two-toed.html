two-toed

1. ADJ

/ ˈtuːˌtoʊd / (AmE), / ˈtuːˌtəʊd / (BrE)
(of an animal) having two toes on each foot.
having two toes

Examples

The two-toed sloth moves slowly through the rainforest canopy.
Unlike its three-toed cousin, the ostrich is a two-toed bird.
Scientists discovered a fossil of a two-toed dinosaur in the region.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: three-toed - having three toes
  • Degree Higher: four-toed - having four toes
