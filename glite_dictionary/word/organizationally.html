organizationally

1. ADV

/ ˌɔːr.ɡə.nəˈzeɪ.ʃən.ə.li / (AmE), / ˌɔː.ɡə.naɪˈzeɪ.ʃən.əli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to the structure or arrangement of an organization or system.
in terms of organization

Examples

Organizationally, the company had to restructure to improve communication across departments.
He is organizationally skilled, which makes him an asset during large projects.
Organizationally, the event was handled with remarkable efficiency.

Synset Examples

organizationally, the conference was a disaster!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organizational - relating to an organization

Etymology

From 'organization' + '-ally' (adverbial suffix).
