blah

1. NOUN

/ blɑ / (AmE), / blɑː / (BrE)
Pompous or pretentious talk or writing, often regarded as meaningless or dull.
pretentious or meaningless talk

Examples

The speech was just a lot of blah without any real substance.
He filled his essay with blah to pad the word count.
She dismissed the meeting as nothing but corporate blah.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: balderdash - senseless or nonsensical talk
  • Synonym: drivel - silly, foolish, or meaningless talk
