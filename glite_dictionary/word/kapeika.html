kapeika

1. NOUN

/ kəˈpeɪkə / (AmE), / kəˈpeɪkə / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Belarus, equal to one hundredth of a rubel.
Belarusian monetary unit

Examples

He found a kapeika coin at the bottom of his pocket.
The smallest groceries sometimes list prices down to the kapeika.
Tourists exchanged their rubels for coins and found they had many kapeikas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kopeck - A monetary unit of Russia and some other countries, equal to one hundredth of a ruble

Etymology

From Russian копейка (kopeyka), also used in other Slavic languages.
