pedophilia

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɛdəˈfɪliə / (AmE), / ˌpiːdəˈfɪliə / (BrE)
A sexual attraction toward children, especially prepubescent children.
sexual attraction to children
medical

Examples

Some laws are designed specifically to target crimes associated with pedophilia.
The psychiatric community classifies pedophilia as a disorder.
There is ongoing research into the causes and treatments for pedophilia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hebephilia - sexual attraction to pubescent children
  • Synonym: ephebophilia - sexual attraction to teenagers

Etymology

From Greek pais, paid- (child) + philos (loving).
