cartographer

1. NOUN

/ kɑrˈtɑɡrəfər / (AmE), / kɑːˈtɒɡrəfə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to draw or produce maps
person who makes maps

Examples

After years of study, she became a renowned cartographer specializing in ancient world maps.
Every modern atlas requires the skills of a professional cartographer.
The conference brought together cartographers from around the world to discuss new mapping technologies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mapmaker - a person who makes maps

Etymology

From French cartographe, from carte (map) + -graphe (writer, recorder).
