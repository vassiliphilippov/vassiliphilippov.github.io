unachievably

1. ADV

/ ʌnəˈtʃivəbli / (AmE), / ʌnəˈtʃiːvəbli / (BrE)
In a way that cannot be achieved or reached; to an unattainable extent.
in an unattainable manner

Examples

After the deadline changed, finishing the project on time became unachievably difficult for the team.
The new height record is almost unachievably high for any athlete.
Many people find the ideal portrayed in the media to be unachievably perfect.

Synset Examples

this house is unattainably expensive

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unattainably - in an unattainable manner

Etymology

From unachievable + -ly, where 'unachievable' is from un- (not) + achievable.
