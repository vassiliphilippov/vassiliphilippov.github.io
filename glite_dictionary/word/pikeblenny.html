pikeblenny

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪkˌblɛni / (AmE), / ˈpaɪkˌblɛni / (BrE)
A small, tropical American fish known for the males' aggressive territorial defense.
tropical American fish species

Examples

The male pikeblenny fiercely guards its nesting site from other fish.
Marine biologists observed the behavior of pikeblennies along the coral reefs of Central America.
A pikeblenny uses its territorial instincts to keep rivals away during breeding season.
