Mokulu

1. NOUN

/ moʊˈkuːluː / (AmE), / məʊˈkuːluː / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in the Guera region of Chad
Chadic language of Chad

Examples

Linguists have conducted research on Mokulu to better understand Chadic language structures.
The people of several villages in Chad communicate using Mokulu.
He is fluent in French, Arabic, and Mokulu.
