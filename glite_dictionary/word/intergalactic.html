intergalactic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərɡəˈlæktɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪntəɡəˈlæktɪk / (BrE)
existing or occurring between galaxies
between galaxies

Examples

Scientists study intergalactic space to learn about the structure of the universe.
The movie featured an intergalactic battle between alien civilizations.
Astronomers discovered mysterious signals coming from an intergalactic origin.

Synset Examples

intergalactic space

Related Terms

  • Synonym: galactic - relating to a galaxy or galaxies

Etymology

From inter- (between) + galactic, relating to galaxies.
