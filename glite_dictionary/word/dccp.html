DCCP

1. NOUN

/ di si si pi / (AmE), / diː siː siː piː / (BrE)
an agency of the United Nations responsible for promoting drug control and crime prevention.
UN agency for drug control and crime prevention

Examples

The DCCP works with countries to develop effective anti-drug policies.
International conferences often include representatives from the DCCP.
Funding for the DCCP has increased in response to the rise in global crime.
