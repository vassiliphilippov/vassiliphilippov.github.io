North Yorkshire

1. PROPN

/ nɔrθ ˈjɔrkˌʃɪr / (AmE), / nɔːθ ˈjɔːkʃə / (BrE)
a county in the north of England, known for its rural landscapes and historic towns
county in northern England

Examples

Many tourists visit North Yorkshire to explore the Yorkshire Dales and its picturesque villages.
She grew up in North Yorkshire before moving to London for university.
The scenic landscapes of North Yorkshire attract hikers and cyclists throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Yorkshire - historic county in northern England

Etymology

Named from its geographical location in the historic county of Yorkshire, England.
