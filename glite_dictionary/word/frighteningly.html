frighteningly

1. ADV

/ ˈfraɪtənɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈfraɪtənɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that causes fear or terror; in a manner that is alarming.
in a terrifying or alarming way

Examples

The wind howled frighteningly outside as the storm intensified.
His face looked frighteningly pale after hearing the news.
The technology was frighteningly advanced for its time.

Synset Examples

the disturbing thing about the Minister's behavior is that far from being artificial, it too often rings frighteningly true

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrifyingly - in a manner that causes terror or extreme fear
  • Antonym: reassuringly - in a way that provides comfort or reassurance

Etymology

From 'frightening' + '-ly', with 'frighten' from Middle English 'frighten', from 'fright'.
