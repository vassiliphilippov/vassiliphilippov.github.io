seductively

1. ADV

/ sɪˈdʌktɪvli / (AmE), / sɪˈdʌktɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner that is intended to attract or tempt sexually or romantically.
in a tempting, attractive manner

Examples

She leaned closer and smiled seductively at him.
The actress spoke seductively in the advertisement for the new fragrance.
He looked at her seductively, hoping to catch her attention.

Synset Examples

she smiled at him seductively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: provocatively - in a way intended to arouse sexual interest
  • Antonym: innocently - in a way not intended to attract or tempt

Etymology

From 'seductive' + '-ly', ultimately from Latin 'seducere' (to lead astray).
