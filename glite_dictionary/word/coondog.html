coondog

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːnˌdɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈkuːnˌdɒɡ / (BrE)
A dog trained or bred to hunt raccoons.
dog trained to hunt raccoons
american

Examples

The coondog chased the raccoon up a tree during the night hunt.
He raised and trained coondogs for competitions across the state.
Their family owns a prize-winning coondog known for its keen tracking ability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coonhound - a breed of dog developed for raccoon hunting

Etymology

From 'coon', a colloquial abbreviation of 'raccoon', and 'dog'.
