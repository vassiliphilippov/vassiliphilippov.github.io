Islamabad

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈslɑːməˌbɑːd / (AmE), / ɪˈslɑːməbɑːd / (BrE)
the capital city of Pakistan, located in the northern part of the country on a plateau
capital city of Pakistan

Examples

Many tourists are attracted to Islamabad because of its beautiful parks and modern architecture.
The government of Pakistan relocated its capital to Islamabad in the 1960s.
She moved to Islamabad for her new job at the embassy.

Etymology

From Urdu اسلام آباد (Islām ābād), meaning 'abode of Islam'.
