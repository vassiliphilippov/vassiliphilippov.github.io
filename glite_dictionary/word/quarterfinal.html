quarterfinal

1. NOUN

/ ˌkwɔrtɚˈfaɪnəl / (AmE), / ˌkwɔːtəˈfaɪnəl / (BrE)
one of four matches or rounds in a tournament from which the winners progress to the semifinals
one of four rounds before semifinals

Examples

She reached the quarterfinal after a hard-fought victory in the previous round.
The team celebrated their advancement to the quarterfinals of the tournament.
Tomorrow's match will determine who secures the last spot in the quarterfinal.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: semifinal - a match or round before the final

Etymology

From 'quarter' (meaning one fourth) and 'final'.
