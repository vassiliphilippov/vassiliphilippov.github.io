tracking

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrækɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrækɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of pursuing a person or animal by following the tracks or marks they have left behind
following tracks to pursue

Examples

The ranger was skilled at tracking wildlife through the dense forest.
Their tracking of the missing child lasted for hours through difficult terrain.
He learned the art of tracking from his grandfather, who had been a hunter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trailing - the act of following someone or something, especially secretly
  • Synonym: tracking down - finding someone or something by following clues or traces
