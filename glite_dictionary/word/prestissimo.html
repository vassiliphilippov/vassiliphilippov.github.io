prestissimo

1. ADJ

/ prɛˈstɪsɪˌmoʊ / (AmE), / prɛˈstɪsɪməʊ / (BrE)
(in music) at a tempo as fast as possible; extremely rapid.
in music, as fast as possible

Examples

The final movement should be played prestissimo to capture the composer's intent.
The pianist finished the piece at a breathtaking prestissimo tempo.
The conductor signaled for an even faster pace, moving from presto to prestissimo.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: presto - (in music) very fast tempo

Etymology

From Italian 'prestissimo', superlative of 'presto' (very quickly).
