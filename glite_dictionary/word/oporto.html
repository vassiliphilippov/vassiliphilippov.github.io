Oporto

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈpɔrtoʊ / (AmE), / əʊˈpɔːtəʊ / (BrE)
A port city in northwest Portugal, famous for its production of port wine and historic architecture.
port city in northwest Portugal

Examples

Oporto is renowned worldwide for its rich port wines and beautiful riverside scenery.
Last summer, we explored the ancient streets of Oporto during our trip to Portugal.
Many tourists travel to Oporto to visit its historic cellars and taste the local wine.

Etymology

Portuguese name for the city, also known as Porto from Latin 'Portus'.
