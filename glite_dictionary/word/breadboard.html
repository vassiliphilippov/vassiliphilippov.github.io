breadboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛdˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbrɛdˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a wooden or plastic board on which dough is kneaded or bread is sliced
board for kneading dough or slicing bread

Examples

She placed the warm loaf on the breadboard before slicing it.
Baking is much easier when you have a sturdy breadboard to knead the dough on.
After breakfast, he cleaned the crumbs off the breadboard.
