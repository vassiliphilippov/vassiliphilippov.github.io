fittingness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪtɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˈfɪtɪŋnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being suitable or appropriate for a particular purpose or situation
quality of being suitable

Examples

The fittingness of her remarks was appreciated by everyone at the meeting.
He questioned the fittingness of awarding such an honor at that moment.
The play explores the fittingness of different responses to tragedy.

Synset Examples

they had to prove their fitness for the position

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suitability - the quality of being appropriate
  • Synonym: appropriateness - the quality of being right for a particular situation

Etymology

From 'fitting' + '-ness', formed by adding the noun-forming suffix to 'fitting'.
