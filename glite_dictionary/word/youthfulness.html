youthfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuθf(ə)lnəs / (AmE), / ˈjuːθf(ə)lnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being young, fresh, and energetic
the quality of being young and energetic

Examples

Her youthfulness made her stand out in the group of senior scientists.
He approached every challenge with remarkable youthfulness and optimism.
The actor's youthfulness is evident in both his appearance and his lively performances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: youth - the period of being young; young people collectively
  • Antonym: oldness - the quality of being old

Etymology

From 'youth' + '-fulness', denoting the state or quality of being young.
