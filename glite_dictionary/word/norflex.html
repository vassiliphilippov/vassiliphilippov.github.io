Norflex

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔr.flɛks / (AmE), / ˈnɔː.flɛks / (BrE)
A brand name for a skeletal muscle relaxant medication (orphenadrine), used to treat muscle spasms and certain conditions such as severe muscle strain and Parkinsonism.
brand name muscle relaxant medication
medical

Examples

After injuring his back, the doctor prescribed Norflex to help relieve the muscle spasms.
Some patients with Parkinsonism have found Norflex helpful in managing muscle stiffness.
She asked her pharmacist if Norflex would interact with her current medications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orphenadrine - the generic name of the drug sold as Norflex
