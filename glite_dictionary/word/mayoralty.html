mayoralty

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪ.əɹˌəl.ti / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.ə.əl.ti / (BrE)
the office, position, or term of a mayor
the office or term of a mayor

Examples

Her mayoralty was marked by significant urban development and innovation.
The city experienced rapid growth during his mayoralty.
Several challenges arose throughout the mayoralty of Jane Smith.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mayor - the elected head of a city, town, or municipality

Etymology

From Middle English, from Anglo-Norman 'mairalte', from 'maire' (mayor) + '-alty' (condition, office).
