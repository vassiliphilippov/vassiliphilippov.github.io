subpart

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌbˌpɑrt / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌpɑːt / (BrE)
a part that is itself contained within a larger part; a subdivision of a part
a subdivision of a part

Examples

Each chapter is divided into several subparts for easier reference.
The manual's subpart A deals with safety regulations.
A detailed examination of this subpart reveals critical issues.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: part - an element or portion of something

Etymology

From Latin prefix 'sub-' meaning 'under' or 'below' and 'part'.
