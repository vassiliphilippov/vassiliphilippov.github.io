handicraft

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændiˌkræft / (AmE), / ˈhændɪkrɑːft / (BrE)
An object made by hand, especially one that demonstrates skill or artistic ability.
object made by hand

Examples

The local market was filled with beautiful handicraft from the region.
She bought a handicraft basket made by an artisan.
Tourists often seek unique handicraft as souvenirs of their travels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: craft - an activity involving skill in making things by hand

Etymology

From Middle English handecrafte, from Old English handcræft.

2. NOUN

/ ˈhændiˌkræft / (AmE), / ˈhændɪkrɑːft / (BrE)
A craft or activity that involves making things skillfully by hand.
manual craft; hand-made work

Examples

She sells her beautiful handicraft items at the market every Saturday.
Learning a new handicraft can be both relaxing and rewarding.
These festivals often showcase traditional handicraft from local artisans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: craft - an activity involving skill in making things by hand
  • Synonym: artisanship - skill in a particular craft

Etymology

From Middle English, from 'handy' (of the hand) + 'craft' (skill).
