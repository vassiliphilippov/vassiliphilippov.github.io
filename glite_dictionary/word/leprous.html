leprous

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛpɹəs / (AmE), / ˈlɛpɹəs / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling leprosy; affected with leprosy.
affected by or resembling leprosy
medical

Examples

The villagers avoided the leprous man, fearing that his disease might be contagious.
The stone wall was covered with a leprous white mold that looked unhealthy.
He described the abandoned town as a leprous ruin, slowly being reclaimed by nature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leprotic - Relating to or resembling leprosy

Etymology

From Latin 'leprosus' (scaly, leprous), from 'lepra' (leprosy).
