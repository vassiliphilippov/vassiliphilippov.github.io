hoochie

1. NOUN

/ ˈhuːtʃi / (AmE), / ˈhuːtʃi / (BrE)
(slang, often offensive) A woman regarded as promiscuous or as dressing in a sexually provocative or flashy way.
slang: sexually provocative or promiscuous woman
slang offensive

Examples

Some people unfairly labeled her a hoochie because of her bold fashion choices.
The song's lyrics were criticized for calling women hoochies.
She disliked being judged or referred to as a hoochie based on her appearance.

Synset Examples

He called her a hoochie for her bold style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slut - a woman considered to be sexually promiscuous
  • Antonym: prude - a person who claims to be easily shocked by matters relating to sex or nudity

Etymology

Probably from 'hoochie coochie', a term for a sexually suggestive dance popular in the late 19th century.
