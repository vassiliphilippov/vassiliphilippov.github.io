burglarious

1. ADJ

/ bɜːrˈɡlɛriəs / (AmE), / bɜːˈɡlɑːrɪəs / (BrE)
relating to or involving burglary; characteristic of burglary or burglars.
relating to burglary

Examples

The police found a set of burglarious tools hidden under the suspect's bed.
He was charged with possessing items for burglarious purposes.
The burglarious entry into the warehouse was caught on security cameras.

Synset Examples

burglarious tools

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burglary - the act of breaking into a building, especially to commit theft

Etymology

From 'burglar' plus the suffix '-ious', patterned after Latin forms.
