Lesquerella

1. NOUN

/ lɛs.kwəˈrɛl.ə / (AmE), / lɛs.kwəˈrɛl.ə / (BrE)
A genus of low-growing, hairy herbs in the mustard family, commonly known as bladderpods.
genus of bladderpod plants

Examples

Lesquerella species are often found in arid and semi-arid regions of North America.
Researchers are studying the oil content of Lesquerella seeds for industrial applications.
The bright yellow flowers of Lesquerella make it easy to identify in the wild.

Etymology

Named after Leo Lesquereux, a Swiss-American botanist.
