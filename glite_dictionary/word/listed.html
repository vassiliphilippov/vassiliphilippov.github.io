listed

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪstɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪstɪd / (BrE)
(of an item, person, etc.) included on a particular list, especially an official or formal one.
included on a list

Examples

All listed companies must comply with strict financial regulations.
The medication is listed under controlled substances in the regulations.
Her name was not listed among the award recipients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: registered - officially recorded or entered on a list
  • Antonym: unlisted - not included on an official or formal list
