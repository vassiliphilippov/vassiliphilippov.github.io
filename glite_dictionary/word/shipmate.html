shipmate

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪpˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈʃɪp.meɪt / (BrE)
a person who serves or travels on the same ship as another
person on the same ship

Examples

During the long voyage, she became close friends with her shipmate.
Each shipmate played an important role in keeping the vessel running smoothly.
After years apart, he finally reunited with a former shipmate at the port.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crewmate - a fellow member of a ship's crew

Etymology

Formed from 'ship' + 'mate'.
