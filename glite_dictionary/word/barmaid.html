barmaid

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːrˌmeɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɑːmeɪd / (BrE)
A woman who serves drinks at a bar or pub.
female bartender

Examples

The barmaid greeted customers with a friendly smile as they entered the pub.
He waved at the barmaid to order another round of drinks for the group.
During the busy evening, the barmaid skillfully kept up with all the orders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bartender - a person who serves drinks at a bar
  • Synonym: barman - a male bartender

Etymology

Formed from 'bar' (drinking establishment) + 'maid' (young woman or female servant).
