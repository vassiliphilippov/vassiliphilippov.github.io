Gt

1. NOUN

/ dʒi ti / (AmE), / dʒiː tiː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for gigatonne, a unit of mass equal to one billion (10^9) metric tonnes.
abbreviation for gigatonne (1 billion tonnes)

Examples

The world's oceans absorb about 2 Gt of carbon annually.
Global emissions exceeded 35 Gt of CO₂ in 2022.
Scientists measure ice sheet loss in Gt per year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gigatonne - a unit of mass equal to one billion tonnes

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'giga-' (billion) and 'tonne'.
