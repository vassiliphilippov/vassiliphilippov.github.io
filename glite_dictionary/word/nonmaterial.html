nonmaterial

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.məˈtɪr.i.əl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.məˈtɪə.ri.əl / (BrE)
not consisting of matter; not having a physical or material existence.
not made of matter

Examples

Philosophers often debate whether the soul is a nonmaterial entity.
Some religions believe in nonmaterial spirits that exist beyond the physical world.
The artist tried to capture nonmaterial concepts such as love and hope in her paintings.

Synset Examples

immaterial apparitions
ghosts and other immaterial entities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immaterial - not consisting of matter; lacking physical substance

Etymology

Prefix non- (not) + material (from Latin *materialis*, relating to matter).
