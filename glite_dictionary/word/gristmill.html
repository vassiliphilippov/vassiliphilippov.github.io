gristmill

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrɪs(t)ˌmɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪs(t)ˌmɪl / (BrE)
a mill for grinding grain, especially grain brought by customers for their own use
mill for grinding grain

Examples

The old gristmill by the river has been restored as a historical site.
Farmers brought their wheat to the local gristmill to be ground into flour.
The sound of the gristmill turning was once common in rural communities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mill - a building equipped with machinery for grinding grain

Etymology

From Middle English *grist* (grain to be ground) + *mill* (grinding machine).
