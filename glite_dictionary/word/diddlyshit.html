diddlyshit

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪd.liˌʃɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɪd.liˌʃɪt / (BrE)
a very small or worthless amount; nothing at all
nothing; a worthless amount
slang vulgar

Examples

He doesn't know diddlyshit about fixing cars.
After all that work, I got diddlyshit for my efforts.
She said there was treasure, but we found diddlyshit.

Synset Examples

you don't know jack

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diddly-squat - a very small or worthless amount
  • Synonym: jack - nothing at all; a worthless amount (slang)

Etymology

Blend of "diddly" (slang for 'nothing') and "shit" (slang for 'worthless thing').
