Parji

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːrdʒi / (AmE), / ˈpɑːdʒi / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken in south central India, especially by the Dhurwa tribe in the districts of Koraput and Bastar in Chhattisgarh state
a Dravidian language of south central India

Examples

The Parji language is primarily used by the Dhurwa tribe in Chhattisgarh.
Linguists are working to document and preserve Parji, which is considered endangered.
He is fluent in Parji as well as several other local languages.
