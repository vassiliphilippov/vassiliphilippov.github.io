filly

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪli / (AmE), / ˈfɪli / (BrE)
a young female horse, especially one under the age of four
young female horse

Examples

The farmer introduced the playful filly to the rest of the herd.
At the show, her filly won first place for her age group.
They bought a spirited young filly for their daughter's riding lessons.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: colt - a young male horse
  • Degree Higher: mare - an adult female horse

Etymology

From Middle English 'fili', from Old Norse 'fylja' (young female horse).
