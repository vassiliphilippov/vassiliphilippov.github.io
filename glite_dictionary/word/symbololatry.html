symbololatry

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪmbəˈlɑlətri / (AmE), / ˌsɪmbəˈlɒlətri / (BrE)
the worship or excessive reverence of symbols
worship of symbols

Examples

Some critics accused the movement of symbololatry, claiming it valued images over principles.
Religious reformers warned against symbololatry within their communities.
His fascination with ancient icons teetered on the edge of symbololatry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idolatry - the worship of idols or physical objects as gods

Etymology

From symbol + -olatry, modeled after idolatry, with -olatry from Greek latreia (worship).
