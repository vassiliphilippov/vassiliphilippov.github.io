concessionaire

1. NOUN

/ kənˌsɛʃəˈnɛr / (AmE), / kənˌsɛʃəˈneə / (BrE)
a person or company that operates a business under a concession or holds the right to offer goods or services in a particular place
person or company operating a concession

Examples

The stadium concessionaire sells snacks and drinks during the game.
Each concessionaire must comply with strict health and safety regulations.
The park authority awarded a new contract to a concessionaire to manage the campgrounds.

Etymology

From French 'concessionnaire', from 'concession'.
