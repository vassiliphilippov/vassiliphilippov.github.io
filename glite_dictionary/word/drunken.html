drunken

1. ADJ

/ ˈdrʌŋkən / (AmE), / ˈdrʌŋkən / (BrE)
affected by alcohol; having consumed too much alcohol, or characterized by the presence of people who are drunk or by alcohol-fueled behavior.
affected by alcohol; intoxicated

Examples

He was arrested after causing a scene during a drunken argument outside the bar.
The party ended in a drunken celebration that lasted until sunrise.
They laughed loudly at his drunken stories from college.

Synset Examples

a bibulous fellow
a bibulous evening
his boozy drinking companions
thick boozy singing
a drunken binge
two drunken gentlemen holding each other up
sottish behavior

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol
  • Synonym: intoxicated - under the influence of alcohol; drunk
  • Synonym: inebriated - affected by drinking alcohol; drunk

Etymology

From Old English druncen, past participle of drincan (to drink).
