broadening

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɔdənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of making something wider or more extensive
the act of making wider

Examples

There has been a noticeable broadening of the river after the heavy rains.
The broadening of the highway helped reduce traffic congestion in the city.
Many educators advocate for the broadening of the curriculum to include more diverse subjects.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: narrowing - the act of making something narrower
  • Synonym: widening - the act of making something wider

Etymology

Formed from 'broaden' (to make broad) + -ing suffix.
