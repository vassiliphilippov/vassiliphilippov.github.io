unwell

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈwɛl / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛl / (BrE)
ill or not feeling well; suffering from a slight illness or discomfort.
feeling ill; not well

Examples

I'm feeling unwell and think I should rest today.
If you are unwell, please let your supervisor know before taking time off.
She looked slightly unwell during the meeting and left early.

Synset Examples

my poor ailing grandmother
feeling a bit indisposed today
you look a little peaked
feeling poorly
a sickly child
is unwell and can't come to work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ill - suffering from an illness; not well
  • Antonym: well - in good health
  • Synonym: sick - affected by illness; unwell

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'well' (healthy, in good health).
