duck

1. NOUN

/ dʌk / (AmE), / dʌk / (BrE)
(in cricket) a score of zero by a batsman in an innings.
score of zero in cricket

Examples

The opener was out for a duck in the very first over.
He has been dismissed for a duck three times this season.
Scoring a duck can be disappointing for any batsman.

Etymology

Possibly from 'duck's egg' for zero, by resemblance to the shape of a zero.
