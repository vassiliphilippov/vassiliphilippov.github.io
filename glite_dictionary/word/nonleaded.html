nonleaded

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈlɛdɪd / (AmE), / nɒnˈlɛdɪd / (BrE)
(of gasoline) not containing tetraethyl lead; free of added lead compounds.
not containing added lead (of gasoline)

Examples

Many cars today only use nonleaded gasoline for environmental reasons.
The pump was clearly marked for nonleaded fuel only.
After regulations changed, the refinery stopped producing anything but nonleaded gasoline.

Synset Examples

lead-free gasoline

Related Terms

  • Antonym: leaded - containing added lead (especially in fuel)

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' meaning 'not' + 'leaded' (containing lead).
