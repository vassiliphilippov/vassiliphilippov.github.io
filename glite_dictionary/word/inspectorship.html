inspectorship

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈspɛktərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ɪnˈspɛktəʃɪp / (BrE)
the official position, rank, or office of an inspector
the office or position of an inspector

Examples

He was appointed to the inspectorship of factories in the northern region.
After years of diligent work, she attained the inspectorship in the regulatory agency.
Several candidates are competing for the vacant inspectorship position.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspectorship - the state of being an inspector

Etymology

From 'inspector' + '-ship' (denoting state, office, or position).
