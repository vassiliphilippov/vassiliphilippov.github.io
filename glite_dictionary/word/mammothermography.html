mammothermography

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæməˌθɜːrməˈɡrɑːfi / (AmE), / ˌmaməʊˌθɜːməˈɡrɒfi / (BrE)
the use of thermography to detect breast tumors by identifying areas of higher temperature on the breast
thermographic method for detecting breast tumors
medical

Examples

Mammothermography is considered as a supplementary screening tool for early detection of breast cancer.
The clinic now offers mammothermography to patients who want a non-invasive breast examination.
Recent studies question the sensitivity of mammothermography compared to traditional mammography.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermography - recording body temperature by photographic or electronic means

Etymology

From Latin mamma (breast) + thermography (from Greek thermē 'heat' + -graphy 'writing')
