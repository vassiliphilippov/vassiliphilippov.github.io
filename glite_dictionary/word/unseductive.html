unseductive

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.sɪˈdʌk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.sɪˈdʌk.tɪv / (BrE)
not attractive or alluring; lacking the quality of being seductive
not alluring or attractive

Examples

The movie's lead character was surprisingly unseductive despite being written as a femme fatale.
He delivered an unseductive sales pitch that failed to catch the audience's interest.
The decor was clean but utterly unseductive, lacking any visual appeal.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: seductive - tending to seduce; alluring or tempting

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' and 'seductive.'
