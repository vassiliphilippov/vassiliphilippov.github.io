red-lavender

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɛd ˈlævəndɚ / (AmE), / ˌrɛd ˈlævəndə / (BrE)
Having a lavender color with a hint of red.
lavender tinged with red

Examples

The sunset painted the sky a beautiful shade of red-lavender.
She chose a red-lavender dress for the spring wedding.
The artist mixed paints to get just the right red-lavender hue for the portrait.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: lavender - a pale purple color, named after the lavender flower
  • Synonym: red-violet - a purplish red color
