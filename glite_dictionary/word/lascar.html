Lascar

1. PROPN

/ ˈlæskɑr / (AmE), / ˈlɑːskɑː / (BrE)
a volcano located in the Andes mountains of northern Chile, known for its frequent eruptions.
volcano in the Andes, Chile

Examples

The Lascar volcano erupted in 2015, sending ash high into the atmosphere.
Scientists monitor Lascar closely due to its active nature.
Trekking near Lascar offers breathtaking views of the surrounding Atacama Desert.
