billy

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪli / (AmE), / ˈbɪli / (BrE)
A male goat.
male goat

Examples

The billy strutted across the pasture, leading the rest of the herd.
Farmers often keep a billy for breeding purposes.
The children watched as the billy butted heads with another goat.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: nanny - female goat

2. NOUN

/ ˈbɪli / (AmE), / ˈbɪli / (BrE)
a heavy, short club carried and used by police officers as a weapon or for crowd control
policeman's heavy club

Examples

The officer gripped his billy tightly as he approached the suspect.
Police once carried a billy to help maintain order during large protests.
He found an old, worn billy in the attic among other memorabilia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: truncheon - a short thick stick carried as a weapon by a police officer
  • Synonym: baton - a stick used as a weapon or symbol of authority
