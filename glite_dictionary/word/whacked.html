whacked

1. ADJ

/ wækt / (AmE), / wækt / (BrE)
(British informal) Extremely tired or worn out; exhausted.
exhausted, worn out (British informal)
informal british

Examples

After running the marathon, I felt completely whacked and could barely move.
I stayed up all night studying and now I'm absolutely whacked.
By the end of the week, everyone at the office was pretty whacked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhausted - very tired, drained of energy
  • Antonym: refreshed - rested and full of energy
