Alfred the Great

1. PROPN

/ ˈælfrɪd ðə ɡreɪt / (AmE), / ˈælfrɪd ðə ɡreɪt / (BrE)
(849–899) King of Wessex noted for defending England against Viking invasions and promoting the use of English in writing.
King of Wessex renowned for defeating Vikings

Examples

Alfred the Great is remembered for uniting much of England and setting foundations for the English monarchy.
Many historians credit Alfred the Great with the revival of learning and literacy in Anglo-Saxon England.
The legacy of Alfred the Great continues to influence British history and identity.
