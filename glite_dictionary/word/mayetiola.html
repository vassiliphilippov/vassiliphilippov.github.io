Mayetiola

1. NOUN

/ maɪˌɛtiˈoʊlə / (AmE), / maɪˌɛtɪˈəʊlə / (BrE)
A genus of gall midges in the family Cecidomyiidae, including pest species such as the Hessian fly.
genus of gall midges (insects)

Examples

The Mayetiola genus includes insects that can cause significant damage to cereal crops.
Scientists studied several species within the Mayetiola genus to develop pest-resistant wheat varieties.
The most notorious member of the Mayetiola genus is the Hessian fly, a major agricultural pest.
