Chios

1. PROPN

/ ˈkaɪ.oʊs / (AmE), / ˈkiː.ɒs / (BrE)
an island in the Aegean Sea, off the west coast of Turkey, belonging to Greece
island in the Aegean Sea, Greece

Examples

Many tourists visit Chios to experience its stunning beaches and medieval villages.
The mastic trees found on Chios are famous for their unique resin.
Throughout history, Chios has played a significant role in Greek culture.

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Χίος (Khíos).
