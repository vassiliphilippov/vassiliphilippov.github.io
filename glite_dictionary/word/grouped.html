grouped

1. ADJ

/ ɡruːpt / (AmE), / ɡruːpt / (BrE)
arranged or organized into groups
arranged in groups

Examples

The students were grouped according to their interests for the project.
Books on the shelf were grouped by genre for easy access.
Similar data points were grouped together for analysis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organized - arranged in a structured way
  • Antonym: scattered - spread over a wide area or dispersed
