saved

1. ADJ

/ seɪvd / (AmE), / seɪvd / (BrE)
protected from harm, loss, or destruction
protected from harm or destruction

Examples

The old documents were saved from the fire by quick action.
Thanks to the new regulations, many endangered species are now saved from extinction.
All the data was saved on a backup drive to prevent loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safe - protected from or not exposed to danger or risk

2. ADJ

/ seɪvd / (AmE), / seɪvd / (BrE)
Having been rescued or delivered, especially from the spiritual consequences of sin.
rescued from sin; spiritually redeemed

Examples

She considered herself among the saved after embracing her new faith.
The preacher spoke of the blessings granted to the saved.
Many hymns celebrate the joy of being saved.

Synset Examples

a saved soul

Related Terms

  • Synonym: redeemed - freed from the consequences of sin
