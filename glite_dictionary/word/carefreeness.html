carefreeness

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛrˌfriːnəs / (AmE), / ˈkɛəˌfriːnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being free from worries or troubles; cheerful unconcern.
freedom from worry or trouble

Examples

Her carefreeness was evident as she danced in the summer rain.
I envy the carefreeness of children playing in the park without a worry in the world.
Traveling with friends brought a sense of carefreeness he hadn't felt in years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carefree - free from anxiety or responsibility
  • Antonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease

Etymology

Formed from 'carefree' + the noun suffix '-ness'.
