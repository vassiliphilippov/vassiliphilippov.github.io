EbN

1. NOUN

/ iː biː ˈɛn / (AmE), / iː biː ˈɛn / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point north of due east, equal to 78°45′.
compass point: east by north

Examples

The ship set its course to EbN to avoid the oncoming storm.
On the old nautical chart, EbN is clearly marked between east and east-northeast.
Sailors were instructed to steer EbN after passing the lighthouse.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: east - the cardinal compass point at 90 degrees
  • Degree Higher: east-northeast - the compass point one point north of east by north

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'east by north'.
