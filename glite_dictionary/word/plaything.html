plaything

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪˌθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌθɪŋ / (BrE)
an object or toy made for children or others to play with
object made for playing

Examples

The abandoned house was filled with old playthings left by the previous family.
She carefully selected a plaything for the toddler from the toy box.
These days, electronic gadgets have become popular playthings for children and adults alike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toy - an object for a child to play with

Etymology

From Old English 'plega' (play) + 'thing' (object).
