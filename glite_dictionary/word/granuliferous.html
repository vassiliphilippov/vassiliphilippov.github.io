granuliferous

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡræn.jəˈlɪf.ər.əs / (AmE), / ˌɡran.jʊˈlɪf.ər.əs / (BrE)
producing or containing granules.
producing/containing granules

Examples

Under the microscope, the scientist identified several granuliferous cells in the tissue sample.
Some fungi have a granuliferous surface due to the formation of spore granules.
The mineral was classified as granuliferous because it contained numerous tiny particles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: granular - consisting of or resembling small grains or granules

Etymology

From Latin granulum (small grain) + -ferous (bearing or producing).
