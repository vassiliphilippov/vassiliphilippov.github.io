prosperously

1. ADV

/ ˈprɑːspərəsli / (AmE), / ˈprɒspərəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows prosperity or success, especially with wealth or good fortune.
in a prosperous or successful way

Examples

After years of hard work, the company finally grew prosperously and expanded internationally.
The village has lived prosperously ever since the new factory opened.
He invested his savings wisely and now lives prosperously in retirement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: successfully - in a way that achieves a desired aim or result
  • Antonym: poorly - in an unsatisfactory or unsuccessful way

Etymology

From the adjective 'prosperous' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
