dull-purple

1. ADJ

/ dʌl ˈpɝːpəl / (AmE), / dʌl ˈpɜːpl̩ / (BrE)
having a muted or subdued shade of purple; not bright or vivid
muted shade of purple

Examples

She wore a dull-purple scarf that complemented her grey coat.
The walls of the room were painted a dull-purple to create a calm atmosphere.
A cluster of dull-purple flowers bloomed quietly by the roadside.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: purple - a color between red and blue
  • Degree Higher: violet - bluish-purple color; commonly brighter than dull purple
  • Degree Lower: lavender - pale purple color; lighter and softer than dull-purple
