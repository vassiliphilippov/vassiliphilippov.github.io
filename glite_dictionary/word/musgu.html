Musgu

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌs.gu / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.gu / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken mainly south of Lake Chad, especially in the Far North Region of Cameroon
Chadic language of Cameroon and Chad

Examples

The Musgu language is primarily spoken in the Mayo-Danay division of Cameroon.
Linguists are working to document the grammar of Musgu.
Few people outside the region have studied the Musgu language extensively.
