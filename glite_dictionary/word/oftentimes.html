oftentimes

1. ADV

/ ˈɔf(t)ənˌtaɪmz / (AmE), / ˈɒf(t)ənˌtaɪmz / (BrE)
on many occasions; frequently or repeatedly, especially over short intervals of time
frequently; on many occasions

Examples

Oftentimes, small errors can lead to big problems if they are overlooked.
Oftentimes, she visits her grandmother on weekends.
People oftentimes underestimate how challenging learning a new language can be.

Synset Examples

we often met over a cup of coffee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: often - frequently; many times

Etymology

From Middle English ofte tymes, combining 'oft' and 'times.'
