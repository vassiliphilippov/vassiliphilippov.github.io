Persea americana

1. PROPN

/ pərˌsiː.ə ˌæm.əˈrɪ.kə.nə / (AmE), / pɜːˌsiː.ə ˌæm.əˈrɪ.kə.nə / (BrE)
The scientific name for the avocado tree, a tropical American tree bearing large, pulpy green fruits.
scientific name for the avocado tree

Examples

The fruit of the Persea americana is prized for its creamy texture and nutritional value.
Cultivating Persea americana requires a warm climate with well-drained soil.
Researchers are studying the genetics of Persea americana to improve crop yields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avocado - common name for the fruit of Persea americana

Etymology

From Latinized forms of the genus name 'Persea' and species name 'americana', indicating its American origin.
