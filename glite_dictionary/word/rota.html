rota

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊ.tə / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.tə / (BrE)
a list of people and the order in which they are to perform certain duties or tasks
duty roster; sequence of assigned tasks
british

Examples

The weekly cleaning rota ensures everyone contributes to the household chores.
My name is next on the rota for night shifts at the hospital.
Is there a rota for who brings snacks to the meeting?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roster - a list of people's names, often for duty or attendance

Etymology

From Latin 'rota' (wheel), referring to rotation of turns.
