fridge

1. NOUN

/ frɪdʒ / (AmE), / frɪdʒ / (BrE)
A large electrical appliance kept in kitchens, used for storing food and drink at cool temperatures to prevent them from spoiling; a refrigerator.
electrical appliance for keeping food cold

Examples

The milk will go bad if you don't put it in the fridge.
She opened the fridge to look for a snack before dinner.
Our old fridge broke down, so we had to buy a new one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refrigerator - an appliance for keeping food and drinks cold

Etymology

Shortened form of 'refrigerator', influenced by the spelling in 'Frigidaire', a brand name.
