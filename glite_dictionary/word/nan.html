Nan

1. PROPN

/ næn / (AmE), / næn / (BrE)
a river in western Thailand that flows southward to join the Ping River, forming the Chao Phraya River
river in western Thailand

Examples

The Nan River is one of the main tributaries of the Chao Phraya River in Thailand.
Villages along the Nan have relied on its waters for centuries.
The annual floods of the Nan help fertilize the surrounding farmland.
