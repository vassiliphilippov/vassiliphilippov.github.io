Bartolome Esteban Murillo

1. PROPN

/ bɑːrˌtɔːloʊˈmeɪ ɛˈsteɪbən mʊˈriːjoʊ / (AmE), / bɑːˌtɒlɒˈmeɪ ɛˈstɛbən mʊˈriːjəʊ / (BrE)
(1617–1682) A Spanish Baroque painter known for his religious works and depictions of everyday life, noted for his soft, luminous style.
Spanish Baroque painter, 1617–1682

Examples

The museum features several works by Bartolome Esteban Murillo, renowned for his delicate portrayal of children.
Art historians often praise Bartolome Esteban Murillo for his masterful use of light and color.
During our trip to Seville, we saw a special exhibition dedicated to Bartolome Esteban Murillo.
