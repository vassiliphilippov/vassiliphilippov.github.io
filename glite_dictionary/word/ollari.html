Ollari

1. NOUN

/ əˈlɑːri / (AmE), / ɒˈlɑːri / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken in south central India, especially in the Koraput district of Odisha
Dravidian language of south central India

Examples

The Ollari language is primarily spoken in the Koraput district of Odisha.
Researchers have documented several folktales in Ollari over the years.
She learned to speak Ollari to communicate with the local community.
