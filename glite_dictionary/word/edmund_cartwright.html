Edmund Cartwright

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛdmənd ˈkɑrtˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛdmənd ˈkɑːtˌraɪt / (BrE)
(1743–1823) An English clergyman and inventor best known for inventing the power loom.
English inventor of the power loom

Examples

Edmund Cartwright revolutionized textile manufacturing with his invention of the power loom in the late 18th century.
The industrial revolution was significantly advanced by Edmund Cartwright's contributions to mechanized weaving.
Historians credit Edmund Cartwright as a key figure in the development of modern industrial machinery.
