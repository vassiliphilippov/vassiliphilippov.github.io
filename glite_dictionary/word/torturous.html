torturous

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɔrtʃərəs / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtʃərəs / (BrE)
causing extreme pain or suffering; very painful or agonizing
causing extreme pain or suffering

Examples

The recovery process after surgery was torturous and seemed never-ending.
He found waiting for the test results to be a torturous experience.
The hike through the desert was torturous in the scorching heat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agonizing - causing extreme physical or mental pain
  • Degree Lower: painful - causing pain
  • Degree Higher: excruciating - extremely painful; intensely painful

Etymology

From Latin 'tortura' (a twisting, torment), from 'torquere' (to twist).
