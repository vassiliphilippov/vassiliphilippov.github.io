Prima

1. NOUN

/ ˈpriːmə / (AmE), / ˈpriːmə / (BrE)
A variety of apple developed for eating, known for its firmness and sweet flavor.
variety of eating apple

Examples

The Prima apple is popular among growers for its disease resistance.
She prefers to pack a Prima in her lunch because of its crisp texture.
Many recipes call for tart apples, but the Prima is best enjoyed raw.
