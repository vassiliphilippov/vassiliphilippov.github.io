slanginess

1. NOUN

/ ˈslæŋi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈslæŋɪnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being slangy; the casual or informal use of slang in speech or writing.
quality of being slangy or informal

Examples

Her writing was criticized for its slanginess and lack of academic tone.
The comedian's slanginess helped him connect with the younger audience.
Teachers often discourage slanginess in formal essays.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: formality - the quality of being formal
  • Synonym: slangy - using or full of slang
