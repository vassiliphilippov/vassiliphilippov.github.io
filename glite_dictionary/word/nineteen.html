nineteen

1. NUM

/ ˌnaɪnˈtin / (AmE), / ˌnaɪnˈtiːn / (BrE)
the number 19; the cardinal number one greater than eighteen
the number 19

Examples

He scored nineteen points in the final game.
The house at number nineteen has a red door.
She celebrated her nineteenth birthday with her friends.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eighteen - the number 18
  • Degree Higher: twenty - the number 20

Etymology

From Old English nīgontīene, formed from 'nine' + 'ten'.

2. NUM

/ naɪnˈtin / (AmE), / naɪnˈtiːn / (BrE)
The cardinal number that is the sum of eighteen and one.
the number 19

Examples

She will turn nineteen next week and plans to celebrate with her friends.
The competition is open to students under nineteen years of age.
There are nineteen books in this series so far.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eighteen - the cardinal number 18
  • Degree Higher: twenty - the cardinal number 20

Etymology

From Old English nigontīene, from nigon (nine) + tīene (ten).
