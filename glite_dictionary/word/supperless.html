supperless

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌpərləs / (AmE), / ˈsʌpələs / (BrE)
Having had no supper; without having eaten supper.
without having eaten supper
archaic

Examples

He went to bed supperless after a long and tiring day.
Many poor villagers had to spend the night supperless during the harsh winter.
The soldier, left supperless, dreamed of a warm meal by the fire.

Synset Examples

went to bed supperless

Etymology

Formed from 'supper' + '-less' (meaning 'without').
