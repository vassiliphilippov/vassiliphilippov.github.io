Songhai

1. NOUN

/ sɔŋˈgaɪ / (AmE), / sɒŋˈgaɪ / (BrE)
a Nilo-Saharan language spoken chiefly by the Songhai people of Mali and Niger
a language of Mali and Niger

Examples

The ancient manuscripts are written in Songhai as well as Arabic.
She is fluent in both French and Songhai.
Researchers are documenting endangered dialects of Songhai in rural communities.
