Akron

1. PROPN

/ ˈæk.rən / (AmE), / ˈækrən / (BrE)
A city in northeastern Ohio, United States, known historically as the center of the American rubber industry.
city in Ohio, former rubber industry center
american american

Examples

He grew up in Akron before moving to Chicago for college.
Akron was once known as the "Rubber Capital of the World."
After visiting Cleveland, we drove down to Akron to explore its historic districts.

Etymology

Named after the Greek word 'akron' meaning 'summit' or 'high point'.
