unhomogenized

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.hoʊˈmɑː.dʒəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌn.hɒˈmɒdʒ.ɪ.naɪzd / (BrE)
(of a substance, especially milk) not having been homogenized; having not undergone the process of making a mixture uniform in composition.
not homogenized (esp. of milk)

Examples

If you buy unhomogenized milk, you may notice the cream rises to the top.
Some people prefer unhomogenized yogurt for its richer texture and more natural taste.
The label clearly states that the cheese is made from unhomogenized cream.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: homogenized - having undergone homogenization

Etymology

Prefix 'un-' meaning 'not,' added to 'homogenized'.
