short-stemmed

1. ADJ

/ ʃɔrt stɛmd / (AmE), / ʃɔːt stɛmd / (BrE)
having a short stem, especially in reference to plants or flowers
having a short stem

Examples

She placed a bouquet of short-stemmed roses in the small vase.
The garden was filled with short-stemmed plants that hugged the ground.
He prefers short-stemmed glassware when serving certain types of wine.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: long-stemmed - having a long stem
