obscenely

1. ADV

/ əbˈsinli / (AmE), / əbˈsiːnli / (BrE)
in a manner that is offensive to accepted standards of decency, especially in a sexual way; lewdly or indecently
in an obscene or lewd manner

Examples

He was fined for behaving obscenely in public.
The comedian's jokes were obscenely explicit, shocking some members of the audience.
The painting was removed from the gallery for being considered obscenely graphic.

Synset Examples

he had seen how in their dances the white men and women held one another obscenely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lewdly - in a lewd or indecent manner
  • Antonym: decently - in a way that conforms to accepted standards of morality or respectability

2. ADV

/ əbˈsinli / (AmE), / əbˈsiːnli / (BrE)
to a shockingly excessive or offensive degree
to an excessive or offensive degree

Examples

She was obscenely wealthy, owning multiple mansions around the world.
Some CEOs are paid obscenely high salaries compared to their employees.
The dessert was obscenely large, easily enough for four people.

Synset Examples

this man is obscenely rich

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excessively - to a greater than normal or acceptable degree
  • Antonym: moderately - to a moderate or reasonable degree
  • Synonym: outrageously - to an extremely shocking or unacceptable degree

Etymology

From obscene + -ly, with 'obscene' from Latin 'obscenus'.
