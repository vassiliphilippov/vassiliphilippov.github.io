annually

1. ADV

/ ˈæn.ju.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈæn.ju.ə.li / (BrE)
Once every year; yearly.
once every year

Examples

The company publishes its financial report annually in January.
We go on vacation to the same beach annually.
The awards ceremony is held annually to recognize outstanding achievements.

Synset Examples

they travel to China annually

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yearly - happening once every year
  • Degree Lower: monthly - happening once every month
  • Degree Higher: biennially - happening every two years

Etymology

From Latin annuālis (yearly), from annus (year).

2. ADV

/ ˈæn.ju.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈan.ju.ə.li / (BrE)
Once every year; each year.
every year; yearly

Examples

Interest is paid annually on the savings account.
The company reviews employees' performance annually.
Our membership must be renewed annually to remain active.

Synset Examples

he earned $100,000 per annum
we issue six volumes per annum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yearly - Happening or produced once a year
  • Degree Lower: monthly - Happening every month; once a month
  • Degree Higher: biennially - Happening every two years

Etymology

From Latin 'annuus' (yearly), from 'annus' (year) + English '-ly'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00252039-r
Synset POS: r
