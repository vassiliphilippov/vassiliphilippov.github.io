xi

1. NOUN

/ ksaɪ / (AmE), / ksaɪ / (BrE)
the fourteenth letter of the Greek alphabet (Ξ, ξ)
Greek alphabet letter (Ξ, ξ)

Examples

The mathematician used the Greek letter xi to represent an unknown variable.
In the sequence, xi comes after nu and before omicron.
Many fraternities and sororities include xi in their names.

Etymology

From Greek ξῖ (xî).
