uncommercialised

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.kəˈmɝː.ʃə.laɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kəˈmɜː.ʃə.laɪzd / (BrE)
not made commercial or developed for profit; not subject to commercialization.
not made commercial or for profit

Examples

The beach remains uncommercialised, attracting travelers seeking peace and natural beauty.
Many visitors appreciate that the town is still uncommercialised and retains its traditional character.
They chose an uncommercialised resort for a more authentic experience.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: commercialised - made commercial; developed for profit

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'commercialised'.
