to speed skate

1. VERB

/ spiːd skeɪt / (AmE), / spiːd skeɪt / (BrE)
to race on ice or roller skates, especially at high speed as a competitive sport
to race on skates competitively

Examples

Athletes from all over the world speed skate at the Winter Olympics.
She spent her weekends learning how to speed skate at the local arena.
The children loved to speed skate around the frozen pond each winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skate - to move or glide on skates
