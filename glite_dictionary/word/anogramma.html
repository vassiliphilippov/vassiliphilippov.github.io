Anogramma

1. NOUN

/ ˌænəˈɡræmə / (AmE), / ˌanəˈɡramə / (BrE)
A genus of small delicate ferns in the family Pteridaceae, found mainly in tropical and subtropical regions.
genus of small delicate ferns

Examples

The Anogramma genus includes species that thrive in moist, shaded environments.
Botanists recently discovered a new species within the Anogramma group in South America.
The delicate fronds of Anogramma make it an attractive addition to botanical gardens.

Etymology

From Greek 'an-' (without) + 'gramma' (line, letter), referring to characteristic features of the genus.
