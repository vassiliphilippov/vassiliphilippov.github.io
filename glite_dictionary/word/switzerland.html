Switzerland

1. PROPN

/ ˈswɪtsərlænd / (AmE), / ˈswɪtsələnd / (BrE)
a landlocked federal republic in central Europe, known for its neutrality, mountains, and multilingual culture
country in central Europe

Examples

Switzerland is famous for its scenic Alps and well-preserved medieval cities.
She traveled to Switzerland to experience winter sports and Swiss chocolate.
Diplomats often meet in Switzerland because of its long-standing neutrality.

Etymology

From the German 'Schweiz', after Schwyz, one of the founding cantons.
