lowerclassman

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.ɚˌklæs.mən / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.əˌklɑːs.mən / (BrE)
A student in a college or university who has not yet reached senior standing, typically a freshman or sophomore.
undergraduate not yet a senior
american

Examples

The orientation was designed specifically for lowerclassmen to help them adjust to campus life.
As a lowerclassman, she looked up to the seniors for advice about classes and extracurricular activities.
Many lowerclassmen are required to live in the on-campus dormitories during their first two years.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: upperclassman - a college or university student who is a junior or senior

Etymology

Formed in American English from 'lower' + 'class' + 'man', referring to lower academic classes.
