Dicentra spectabilis

1. NOUN

/ daɪˈsɛntrə spɛkˈteɪbɪlɪs / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛntrə spɛkˈteɪbɪlɪs / (BrE)
A garden plant with deep-pink, drooping, heart-shaped flowers, commonly known as 'bleeding heart'.
garden plant with heart-shaped flowers

Examples

The Dicentra spectabilis in her garden bloomed early this spring, adding vibrant color to the beds.
Many gardeners prize Dicentra spectabilis for its unique heart-shaped flowers and delicate foliage.
He planted several Dicentra spectabilis along the shaded path to create a romantic, old-fashioned look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bleeding heart - another common name for Dicentra spectabilis

Etymology

From modern Latin; genus name Dicentra from Greek di- (two) + kentron (spur), species epithet spectabilis meaning 'notable, showy' in Latin.
