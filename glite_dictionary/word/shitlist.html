shitlist

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈʃɪt.lɪst / (BrE)
A list of people who are out of favor or are disliked, especially for perceived wrongdoings.
list of people out of favor
slang vulgar

Examples

After missing the deadline, he found himself on the manager's shitlist for weeks.
She keeps a mental shitlist of people who have crossed her.
You'd better be careful, or you'll end up on her shitlist too.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blacklist - a list of people or things regarded as unacceptable or untrustworthy
