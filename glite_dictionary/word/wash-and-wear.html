wash-and-wear

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɑʃ ən ˈwɛr / (AmE), / ˌwɒʃ ən ˈweə / (BrE)
(of cloth or clothing) able to be washed and dried quickly and easily, without needing ironing.
requiring little or no ironing after washing

Examples

This wash-and-wear shirt is perfect for travel because it doesn't wrinkle easily.
The hotel provides wash-and-wear towels that dry quickly after laundering.
Most children's uniforms are made from wash-and-wear fabrics for convenience.

Synset Examples

a wash-and-wear shirt

Related Terms

  • Synonym: non-iron - (of fabric or clothing) requiring no ironing after washing
