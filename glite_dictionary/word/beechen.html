beechen

1. ADJ

/ ˈbiːtʃən / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃən / (BrE)
made of or relating to the wood of the beech tree.
made of beech wood

Examples

The mantelpiece was crafted from solid beechen timber, giving the room a warm appearance.
A thick carpet of leaves covered the beechen forest floor each autumn.
The old poem describes "the beechen boughs" shading the peaceful village.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beech - a tree of the genus Fagus, or its wood

Etymology

From Old English 'bēcen', from 'bēce' (beech).
