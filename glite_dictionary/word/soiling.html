soiling

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making something dirty or stained, especially with bodily waste or other substances
the act of making something dirty

Examples

The soiling of the carpet required professional cleaning.
Parents were concerned about the repeated soiling of their child’s clothes.
Food soiling is common in kitchens that are not regularly cleaned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soil - to make dirty or stain
