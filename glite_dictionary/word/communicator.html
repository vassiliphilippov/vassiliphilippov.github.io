communicator

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmjunəˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who is able to convey information or ideas to others effectively
person who communicates effectively

Examples

As a team leader, Sara is an excellent communicator who always keeps everyone informed.
Being a good communicator is crucial in customer service roles.
He was praised for his skills as a communicator during the conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speaker - a person who speaks, especially in public
  • Antonym: listener - a person who listens

Etymology

From Latin communicare (to share, to communicate) + -or (agent noun suffix).
