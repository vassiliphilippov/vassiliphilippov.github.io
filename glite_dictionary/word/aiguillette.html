aiguillette

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪɡwɪˈlɛt / (AmE), / ˌeɪɡwɪˈlɛt / (BrE)
an ornamental cord or braid, often with metal tips, worn on the shoulder of a uniform, especially in military or ceremonial contexts
ornamental cord on uniform shoulder

Examples

The officer's aiguillette gleamed in the sunlight as he led the parade.
Members of the royal guard wear a gold aiguillette as part of their ceremonial dress.
She examined the intricate braiding of his aiguillette with admiration.

Etymology

From French 'aiguillette', diminutive of 'aiguille' (needle), referring to the pointed metal tips.
