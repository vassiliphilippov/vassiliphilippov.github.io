maven

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪ.vən / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.vən / (BrE)
An expert or connoisseur; a person who is very knowledgeable about a particular subject.
expert; knowledgeable person

Examples

As a technology maven, she is always ahead of the latest trends.
The food maven recommended the best restaurant in town.
He became a fashion maven after years of working in the industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expert - a person with comprehensive knowledge or skill in a particular area
  • Antonym: novice - a person new to or inexperienced in a field

Etymology

Borrowed from Yiddish *meyvn* (one who understands), from Hebrew *mevin* (understander).
