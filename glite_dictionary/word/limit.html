limit

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪmɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪt / (BrE)
the greatest amount or number of something that is possible or allowed
maximum amount allowed

Examples

There is a strict limit to how much you can withdraw from your account each day.
The speed limit on this road is 60 miles per hour.
We have reached our limit for the number of registrations this year.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restriction - a rule or condition that limits something
  • Antonym: minimum - the least or smallest amount possible

Etymology

From Latin 'līmit-, limes' (boundary, border).
