al Sunna Wal Jamma

1. PROPN

/ æl ˈsunə wɑl dʒəˈmɑ / (AmE), / æl ˈsuːnə wæl dʒəˈmæ / (BrE)
a Nigerian Islamic fundamentalist organization believed to have engaged in or planned terrorist activities
Nigerian Islamic fundamentalist group

Examples

The Nigerian government has increased security due to threats from al Sunna Wal Jamma.
International agencies monitor the activities of al Sunna Wal Jamma for potential terrorist threats.
Several reports have linked al Sunna Wal Jamma to regional attacks in northern Nigeria.

Etymology

From Arabic: al-sunna (the tradition) wa al-jama'a (and the community).
