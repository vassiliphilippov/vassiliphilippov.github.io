independency

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪndɪˈpɛndənsi / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈpɛndənsi / (BrE)
the state of being free from the control or influence of others; independence
state of being independent

Examples

After years of struggle, the nation finally achieved its independency.
The community values its independency from external authorities highly.
Some historical documents refer to the Declaration of Independency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: independence - the state of being independent
  • Antonym: dependence - reliance on or being controlled by someone or something else

Etymology

From Latin 'independentia' via English 'independence'; used as a variant form since the 17th century.
