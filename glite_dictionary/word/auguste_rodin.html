Auguste Rodin

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈɡust roʊˈdæn / (AmE), / əʊˈɡuːst ˈrəʊ.dæ̃ / (BrE)
(1840–1917) A French sculptor renowned for his expressive renderings of the human form, best known for works such as The Thinker and The Kiss.
French sculptor, created The Thinker

Examples

The museum features several masterpieces by Auguste Rodin, including The Thinker.
Art students often study the techniques of Auguste Rodin to better understand modern sculpture.
Many consider Auguste Rodin the father of modern sculpture for his innovative approach to form.

Etymology

From the French name François-Auguste-René Rodin.
