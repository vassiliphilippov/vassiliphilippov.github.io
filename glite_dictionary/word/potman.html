potman

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑtmæn / (AmE), / ˈpɒtmən / (BrE)
a man employed in a pub or inn to collect empty glasses and perform other minor tasks
man in a pub collecting glasses
british

Examples

The potman skillfully collected empty glasses from the crowded tables throughout the night.
When the bar was especially busy, the potman was always quick to tidy up and assist the staff.
In old English pubs, a potman would take care of miscellaneous chores and help keep the place running smoothly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barman - man serving drinks in a bar
  • Synonym: bartender - person serving drinks in a bar
