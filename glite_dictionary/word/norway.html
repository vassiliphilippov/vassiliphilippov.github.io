Norway

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɔr.weɪ / (AmE), / ˈnɔː.weɪ / (BrE)
A country in northern Europe, occupying the western part of the Scandinavian Peninsula; a constitutional monarchy, independent since 1905.
country in northern Europe
american british

Examples

She traveled to Norway last summer to see the stunning fjords.
The Nobel Peace Prize is awarded annually in Norway.
Salmon from Norway is renowned throughout the world.

Etymology

From Old Norse 'Norðrvegr', meaning 'northern way' or 'way to the north'.
