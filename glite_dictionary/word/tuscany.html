Tuscany

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʌskəni / (AmE), / ˈtʌskəni / (BrE)
A region in central Italy known for its picturesque landscapes, rich history, and capital city, Florence.
region in central Italy

Examples

We spent last summer exploring the beautiful countryside of Tuscany.
Wines produced in Tuscany are renowned around the world for their quality.
The rolling hills of Tuscany are a favorite subject for artists and photographers.

Etymology

From Italian 'Toscana', from Latin 'Tuscia' (land of the Etruscans).
