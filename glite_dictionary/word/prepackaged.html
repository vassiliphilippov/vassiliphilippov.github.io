prepackaged

1. ADJ

/ priˈpækɪdʒd / (AmE), / priˈpækɪdʒd / (BrE)
(of goods, especially food) prepared and packaged before being offered for sale.
prepared and packaged in advance for sale

Examples

Many people rely on prepackaged meals for convenience during their busy workweeks.
The store carries a wide variety of prepackaged snacks and drinks.
She prefers fresh produce over prepackaged foods whenever possible.

Synset Examples

prepackaged foods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: packaged - prepared and wrapped in a package
