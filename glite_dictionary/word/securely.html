securely

1. ADV

/ sɪˈkjʊrli / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊəli / (BrE)
In a safe or firm manner; in a way that is protected from danger, loss, or risk.
in a safe or firm way

Examples

The ladder was securely fastened to the wall to prevent accidents.
She locked the documents securely in the safe each night.
He wrapped the package securely before mailing it.

Synset Examples

she held the child securely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safely - in a safe manner; without danger or risk

Etymology

Formed from 'secure' + '-ly'. 'Secure' from Latin 'securus' (free from care).
