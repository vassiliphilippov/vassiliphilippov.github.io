Ian Douglas Smith

1. PROPN

/ ˌiən ˈdʌɡləs smɪθ / (AmE), / ˌiːən ˈdʌɡləs smɪθ / (BrE)
(1919–2007) Rhodesian politician and Prime Minister who declared Rhodesia's independence from Great Britain in 1965.
Rhodesian prime minister (1919–2007)

Examples

Ian Douglas Smith led Rhodesia during a turbulent period of its history in the 1960s.
The government, under Ian Douglas Smith, issued a unilateral declaration of independence in 1965.
Many historians debate the legacy of Ian Douglas Smith and his leadership in southern Africa.
