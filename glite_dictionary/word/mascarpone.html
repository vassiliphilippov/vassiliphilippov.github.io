mascarpone

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæskɑːrˈpoʊneɪ / (AmE), / ˌmæskɑːˈpəʊneɪ / (BrE)
A soft, mild Italian cream cheese made from cream and used in desserts like tiramisu.
soft Italian cream cheese

Examples

The rich flavor of mascarpone makes it a key ingredient in traditional tiramisu.
She spread mascarpone on toast and topped it with fresh strawberries for breakfast.
We substituted mascarpone for regular cream cheese in the recipe to achieve a creamier texture.

Etymology

Italian, from Lombard dialect mascarpóni, a type of cheese.
