unachievable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnəˈtʃivəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈtʃiːvəbl / (BrE)
impossible to achieve or accomplish; not able to be reached or attained.
impossible to achieve or accomplish

Examples

For many, perfect happiness remains an unachievable dream.
Due to technical limitations, the original plan was simply unachievable.
She realized her expectations were unachievable given the limited time and resources.

Synset Examples

an unattainable goal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unattainable - impossible to achieve or reach
  • Antonym: achievable - able to be achieved or accomplished

Etymology

From the prefix un- (not) + achievable (able to be achieved).
