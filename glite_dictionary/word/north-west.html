north-west

1. ADV

/ nɔrθˈwɛst / (AmE), / nɔːθˈwɛst / (BrE)
To, toward, or in the direction of the northwest.
in or toward the northwest

Examples

The storm is moving north-west toward the coast.
They set off north-west across the open fields.
You should head north-west after the crossroads to reach the village.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: south-east - to, toward, or in the southeast
  • Degree Lower: north-east - to, toward, or in the northeast
  • Degree Lower: west - to, toward, or in the west

Etymology

From Old English 'norð' (north) + 'west'.
