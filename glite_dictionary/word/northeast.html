northeast

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɔrθˈist / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˈiːst / (BrE)
the direction or compass point halfway between north and east, at an angle of 45 degrees
compass direction between north and east

Examples

Snow is expected to move in from the northeast this evening.
The garden faces northeast, getting the morning sun.
A strong wind was blowing from the northeast all day.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: east - compass point at 90 degrees
  • Degree Higher: north - compass point at 0 or 360 degrees

Etymology

Formed from 'north' and 'east', indicating position between these directions.
