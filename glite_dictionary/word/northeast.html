Northeast

1. PROPN

/ ˌnɔrθˈist / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˈiːst / (BrE)
the northeastern region of the United States, often including states such as New York, Pennsylvania, Massachusetts, and New Jersey, among others
northeastern region of the U.S.

Examples

Many major cities in the Northeast experience cold, snowy winters.
He moved to the Northeast to attend a university in Boston.
The Northeast is famous for its autumn foliage and historical landmarks.
