waxmallow

1. NOUN

/ ˈwæksˌmæloʊ / (AmE), / ˈwæksˌmæləʊ / (BrE)
any of various plants of the genus Malvaviscus with bright, bell-shaped drooping flowers resembling unopened hibiscus blooms
plant of the genus Malvaviscus

Examples

The garden was filled with waxmallow, their vibrant red flowers attracting hummingbirds.
Botanists studied the unique shape of the waxmallow to compare it to hibiscus.
We planted a waxmallow beside the walkway to add some color to the entrance.
