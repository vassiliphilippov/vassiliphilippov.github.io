teriyaki

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛriˈjɑki / (AmE), / ˌtɛəriˈjɑːki / (BrE)
A Japanese dish of meat or fish that is marinated in a seasoned soy sauce and then grilled or broiled.
Japanese soy-marinated grilled dish

Examples

She ordered the teriyaki chicken with steamed rice for dinner.
The restaurant is famous for its beef teriyaki served with vegetables.
He enjoys making homemade teriyaki using salmon and a sweet soy glaze.

Etymology

Japanese, from 'teri' (glaze/shiny) + 'yaki' (grilling/broiling).
