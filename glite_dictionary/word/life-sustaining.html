life-sustaining

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪf səˌsteɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪf səˌsteɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
necessary to support or maintain life; essential for survival or continued existence
essential for supporting life

Examples

Many patients require life-sustaining treatments such as dialysis or ventilators.
Clean water is a life-sustaining resource for every human community.
The doctor's quick actions proved life-sustaining during the emergency.
