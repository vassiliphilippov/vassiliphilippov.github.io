winless

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪnləs / (AmE), / ˈwɪnləs / (BrE)
(of a person, team, or record) having no wins or victories.
having no wins or victories

Examples

After a winless season, the coach decided to make several changes to the team lineup.
The new chess player's winless streak finally ended with her first victory last week.
Despite their winless record, the players remained optimistic about improving next year.

Synset Examples

the team had a very disappointing winless season

Related Terms

  • Antonym: undefeated - not having lost any games or contests

Etymology

Formed from 'win' + '-less', meaning 'without a win'.
