granulated

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡræn.jəˌleɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡran.jʊ.leɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
made into small grains or particles; having a grainy texture or form
made granular or grainy

Examples

The recipe calls for one cup of granulated sugar.
After the snowstorm, the roads were spread with granulated salt for better traction.
The company sells granulated fertilizer that is easy to apply.

Synset Examples

granulated sugar

Related Terms

  • Antonym: powdered - made into a fine, soft, dry substance

Etymology

From the verb 'granulate', based on Latin 'granulum' (small grain).
