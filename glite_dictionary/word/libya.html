Libya

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪbiə / (AmE), / ˈlɪbɪə / (BrE)
A country in North Africa on the Mediterranean coast, largely desert and known for exporting petroleum.
country in North Africa

Examples

The civil war in Libya has drawn the attention of the international community.
My friend spent two years working in the oil fields of Libya.
The coastline of Libya stretches along the Mediterranean Sea.

Etymology

From Arabic 'Lībiyā', from ancient Latin and Greek 'Libya', the historical name for the region.
