pro-abort

1. ADJ

/ ˌproʊ əˈbɔrt / (AmE), / ˌprəʊ əˈbɔːt / (BrE)
(offensive) Describing a person or group who supports the legal right to abortion; favoring the availability of abortion.
supporting right to abortion (often offensive)
offensive

Examples

He accused his opponent of being a pro-abort after the debate became heated.
The newspaper published an article critical of pro-abort activists.
Despite the label, she insisted that her pro-abort views align with supporting women's rights.

Synset Examples

His views are clearly pro-abort, advocating for choice.
The organization is known for its pro-abort stance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pro-choice - supporting the legal right to abortion
  • Antonym: pro-life - opposing the legal right to abortion

Etymology

Formed by prefix 'pro-' (in favor of) and shortened 'abortion.'
