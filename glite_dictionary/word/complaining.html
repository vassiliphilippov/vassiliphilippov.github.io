complaining

1. ADJ

/ kəmˈpleɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / kəmˈpleɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
habitually or frequently expressing dissatisfaction or resentment; inclined to complain
habitually expressing dissatisfaction

Examples

The complaining employee was never satisfied with any decision made by management.
His complaining attitude made it difficult for him to get along with his teammates.
She grew tired of her neighbor's complaining remarks about the noise in the building.

Synset Examples

a complaining boss

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grumbling - habitually complaining or expressing dissatisfaction
  • Antonym: contented - feeling or showing satisfaction with one's situation
