nine-spot

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪn ˌspɑt / (AmE), / ˈnaɪn ˌspɒt / (BrE)
A playing card showing nine pips on its face, usually one of the four cards in a standard deck marked with the number nine.
playing card with nine pips

Examples

She drew the nine-spot of hearts and smiled confidently.
Do you have a nine-spot in your hand for this round?
The dealer turned over the nine-spot, changing the course of the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nine - the playing card with nine pips
