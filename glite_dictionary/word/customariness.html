customariness

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌs.tə.mɛr.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈkʌs.tə.mər.i.nəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being established by custom or common usage.
state of being customary

Examples

The customariness of the annual festival gives comfort to the community.
His actions lacked the customariness expected of someone in his position.
The customariness of the greeting made it feel warm and familiar.

Synset Examples

The customariness of Jesus' attendance at synagogue on Sabbath days shows him to be a pious Jew.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: customary - habitual or commonly done

Etymology

From 'customary' + '-ness', indicating the state or quality of being customary.
