taliped

1. ADJ

/ ˈtælɪˌpɛd / (AmE), / ˈtælɪˌpɛd / (BrE)
Having a deformed foot, especially one affected by clubfoot (talipes).
having a deformed foot (medical)
medical

Examples

The doctor examined the child's foot and confirmed he was a taliped.
Support groups exist for those born as a taliped to share their experiences.
Special shoes and therapy can greatly help a taliped improve mobility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talipes - a deformity of the foot, especially clubfoot

Etymology

From Latin 'talipes' (clubfoot), from 'talus' (ankle) + 'pes' (foot).
