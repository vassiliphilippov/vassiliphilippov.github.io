Antwerpen

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑntˌwɛrpən / (AmE), / ˈantˌʋɛrpə(n) / (BrE)
The Dutch name for Antwerp, a major port city in northern Belgium, known for its diamond industry and historical significance.
Dutch name for the city of Antwerp, Belgium

Examples

Many tourists visit Antwerpen each year to admire its impressive architecture and vibrant culture.
The diamond district in Antwerpen is world-famous for its trade and craftsmanship.
He traveled from Brussels to Antwerpen by train to attend an international conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Antwerp - Major port city in northern Belgium, also called Antwerpen

Etymology

Dutch name, from Middle Dutch *Antwerpen*, referring to the same city known as Antwerp in English.
