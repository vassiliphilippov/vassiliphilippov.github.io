Mauna Loa

1. PROPN

/ ˌmaʊnə ˈloʊə / (AmE), / ˌmaʊnə ˈləʊə / (BrE)
an active volcano in south central Hawaii, one of the largest in the world, located on the island of Hawaii.
active volcano in Hawaii

Examples

The slopes of Mauna Loa are covered with hardened lava flows from past eruptions.
Scientists monitor Mauna Loa closely for any signs of increased volcanic activity.
Hiking to the summit of Mauna Loa requires preparation and respect for changing weather conditions.

Etymology

Hawaiian, meaning 'Long Mountain'.
