salesman

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪlzmən / (AmE), / ˈseɪlzmən / (BrE)
A man whose job is to sell products or services, typically in a store or by visiting customers.
male salesperson; man who sells goods

Examples

The salesman explained the features of the new car to us.
As a salesman, he traveled across the country to meet clients.
The salesman greeted every customer with a friendly smile.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: saleswoman - a woman whose job is to sell products or services
  • Synonym: salesperson - a person whose job is to sell products or services (gender-neutral)

Etymology

From 'sale' + 'man', formed in English in the 19th century by analogy with other occupational terms.
