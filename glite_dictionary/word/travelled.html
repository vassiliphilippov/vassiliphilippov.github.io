travelled

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrævəld / (AmE), / ˈtrævəld / (BrE)
having visited many places, especially different countries or regions of the world
having visited many places

Examples

She is a travelled writer who has explored every continent.
His stories revealed how travelled he truly was.
Among the group, Carla was the most travelled and shared fascinating tales from around the world.

Synset Examples

a traveled, educated man
well-traveled people

Related Terms

  • Synonym: well-travelled - having travelled extensively
  • Synonym: widely travelled - having visited many far-off places
