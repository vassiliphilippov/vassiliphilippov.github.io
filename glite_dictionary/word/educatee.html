educatee

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛdʒəˈteɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪ / (BrE)
a person who is receiving education, especially at a school or university
person receiving education

Examples

Each educatee is expected to attend at least seventy-five percent of the classes.
The program is designed to give every educatee individual attention from teachers.
International educatees often require additional support adjusting to new academic systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: student - a person who is studying at an educational institution
  • Antonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially at a school

Etymology

Formed from 'educate' + '-ee' (denoting someone who is the recipient of an action).
