Cryptotermes brevis

1. NOUN

/ ˌkrɪptoʊˈtɜːrmiːz ˈbreɪvɪs / (AmE), / ˌkrɪptəʊˈtɜːmiːz ˈbriːvɪs / (BrE)
a species of extremely destructive drywood termite found in warm regions, known for infesting wood in buildings and furniture
species of destructive drywood termite

Examples

Cryptotermes brevis is notorious for causing severe damage to wooden structures in tropical and subtropical climates.
Professional pest control services often encounter Cryptotermes brevis when inspecting infested furniture.
The spread of Cryptotermes brevis has led to increased regulations on transporting untreated wood.

Etymology

From Greek 'kryptos' (hidden) + 'termes' (woodworm), and Latin 'brevis' (short).
