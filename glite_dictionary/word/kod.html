KO'd

1. ADJ

/ ˌkeɪˈoʊd / (AmE), / ˌkeɪˈəʊd / (BrE)
knocked unconscious, especially by a blow, typically in boxing or other combat sports
knocked unconscious (esp. in boxing)
informal

Examples

The champion was KO'd in the fifth round by a powerful uppercut.
After getting KO'd, the fighter needed several minutes to regain consciousness.
She watched her favorite boxer get KO'd on live television.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knockout - sudden defeat or unconsciousness in boxing or fighting

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'knocked out', from the initials 'K.O.' (knockout).
