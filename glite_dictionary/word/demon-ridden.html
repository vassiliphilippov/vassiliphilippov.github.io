demon-ridden

1. ADJ

/ ˈdiːmən ˌrɪdn̩ / (AmE), / ˈdiːmən ˌrɪdn / (BrE)
Seeming to be possessed, tormented, or dominated by demons; greatly troubled or haunted as if by evil spirits.
possessed or tormented by demons

Examples

Some believed the old mansion was demon-ridden, explaining its long history of strange happenings.
His nightmares left him looking demon-ridden and exhausted every morning.
The novel explores the demon-ridden fears of a superstitious village.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: possessed - controlled or influenced as if by a spirit or demon

Etymology

Formed from 'demon' and 'ridden' (from 'ride'), indicating being afflicted as if carried or controlled by demons.
