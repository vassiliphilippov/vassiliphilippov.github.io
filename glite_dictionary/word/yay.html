Yay

1. NOUN

/ jeɪ / (AmE), / jeɪ / (BrE)
a branch of the Tai language family spoken by the Bouyei people in southern Guizhou province, China.
Tai language of Bouyei people, China

Examples

The Yay language is an important part of Bouyei cultural identity.
Linguists have studied Yay to understand its relationship with other Tai languages.
Only a few villages in Guizhou still speak Yay as their primary language.
