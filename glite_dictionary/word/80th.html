80th

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪtiˌɛθ / (AmE), / ˈeɪtiɪθ / (BrE)
being the ordinal number corresponding to eighty in a sequence; in position number eighty.
ordinal number for eighty

Examples

She finished in 80th place in the marathon.
This is the 80th anniversary of the founding of the school.
The committee reviewed the 80th application yesterday.

Related Terms
