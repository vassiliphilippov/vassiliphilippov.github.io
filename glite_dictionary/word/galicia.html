Galicia

1. PROPN

/ ɡəˈliʃə / (AmE), / ɡəˈlɪsɪə / (BrE)
a region in northwestern Spain, formerly a kingdom, located on the Atlantic coast and the Bay of Biscay.
region in northwestern Spain

Examples

Every summer, tourists flock to Galicia for its beautiful beaches and unique culture.
The cuisine of Galicia is famous for its fresh seafood and hearty stews.
She traced her family roots back to Galicia, where her ancestors once lived.

Etymology

From Latin Gallaecia, the name of the ancient Roman province.
