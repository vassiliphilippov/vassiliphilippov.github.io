antediluvial

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.tɪ.dɪˈluː.vi.əl / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪ.dɪˈluː.vi.əl / (BrE)
of or relating to the period before the biblical flood; belonging to the time before the Flood described in the Bible.
before the biblical flood

Examples

Ancient texts sometimes reference antediluvial civilizations lost to history.
The scientist studied fossils from antediluvial strata to learn about prehistoric life.
Myths about antediluvial creatures abound in various cultures around the world.

Synset Examples

antediluvian man

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antediluvian - of or relating to the period before the biblical flood

Etymology

From Latin 'ante-' (before) + 'diluvium' (flood).
