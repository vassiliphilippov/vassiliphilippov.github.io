Maroc

1. PROPN

/ maˈrɑk / (AmE), / maˈrɒk / (BrE)
the French name for Morocco, a country in northwestern Africa with a largely Muslim population
Morocco; country in northwestern Africa

Examples

During his travels, Paul spent several months in Maroc experiencing the local culture.
The export of argan oil is a significant industry in Maroc.
She studied French colonial history, focusing on Maroc after independence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Morocco - a country in northwestern Africa

Etymology

From French 'Maroc', the French name for Morocco.
