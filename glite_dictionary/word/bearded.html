bearded

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɪrdɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɪədɪd / (BrE)
having a beard; with hair growing on the cheeks and chin
having a beard

Examples

The bearded man greeted us with a warm smile.
She prefers dating bearded men over those who are clean-shaven.
Many actors in the film sported a bearded look for their roles.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clean-shaven - having no beard; having a clean-shaven face
  • Antonym: beardless - without a beard

2. ADJ

/ ˈbɪrdɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɪədɪd / (BrE)
(of certain plants or grains) having long, hairlike awns or bristles.
having long, hairlike awns (in plants)

Examples

The field was full of bearded barley swaying in the breeze.
Certain types of wheat, such as bearded wheat, are known for their long bristles.
The botanist explained how bearded grasses protect their seeds with stiff hairlike structures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awned - having or bearing an awn or awns (botanical)
