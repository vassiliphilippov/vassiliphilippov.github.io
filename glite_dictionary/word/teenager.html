teenager

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiːnˌeɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈtiːnˌeɪdʒə / (BrE)
A person between the ages of 13 and 19; an adolescent.
person aged 13 to 19

Examples

The teenager spent hours studying for her exams.
Many teenagers enjoy socializing with their friends after school.
As a teenager, he developed a passion for music that lasted a lifetime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adolescent - a young person developing from a child into an adult

Etymology

Formed from 'teen' (as in thirteen, fourteen, etc.) + 'ager' (one of a specified age); popularized from the 1940s onward.
