underdrawers

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌndɚˌdrɔrz / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌdrɔːəz / (BrE)
Men's underpants, especially those of an older or more formal style.
men's underpants

Examples

He packed a few clean underdrawers for his trip to the countryside.
In the 19th century, gentlemen typically wore long cotton underdrawers beneath their trousers.
He discovered that his old-fashioned underdrawers were much warmer in winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: underpants - an undergarment covering the lower part of the torso, worn next to the skin under pants
  • Degree Lower: boxers - loose-fitting underpants for men
  • Degree Higher: briefs - close-fitting underpants for men

Etymology

From 'under-' (beneath) + 'drawers' (clothing drawn on the body), referring to garments worn under trousers.
