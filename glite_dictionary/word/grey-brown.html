grey-brown

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡreɪˈbraʊn / (AmE), / ˌɡreɪˈbraʊn / (BrE)
Of a brown color tinged with grey.
brown color tinged with grey

Examples

The grey-brown coat of the rabbit helped it blend into the forest floor.
She chose a grey-brown paint for the kitchen walls to create a warm, neutral atmosphere.
The grey-brown feathers of the sparrow make it difficult to spot among the tree branches.
