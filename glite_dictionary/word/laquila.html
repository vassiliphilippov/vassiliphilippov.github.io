L'Aquila

1. PROPN

/ lɑˈkwilə / (AmE), / laˈkwila / (BrE)
a city in central Italy, the capital of the Abruzzo region.
city and regional capital in central Italy

Examples

After the earthquake, many buildings in L'Aquila required extensive repairs.
He spent a semester at the university in L'Aquila while studying abroad.
Tourists visit L'Aquila to admire its medieval architecture and mountain scenery.

Etymology

From Italian 'L'Aquila', meaning 'the eagle'.
