viewable

1. ADJ

/ ˈvjuːəbəl / (AmE), / ˈvjuːəbl / (BrE)
able to be seen or capable of being viewed.
able to be seen

Examples

The mountains are viewable from the hotel balcony on a clear day.
Certain films are only viewable after creating an account on the website.
The document is now viewable online to all staff members.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visible - able to be seen
  • Antonym: invisible - unable to be seen

Etymology

From 'view' + '-able', indicating ability.
