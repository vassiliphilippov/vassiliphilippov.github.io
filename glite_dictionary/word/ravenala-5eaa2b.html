ravenala

1. NOUN

/ ˌrævəˈnɑːlə / (AmE), / ˌravəˈnɑːlə / (BrE)
a tall tropical plant of Madagascar, also known as the traveler's tree, with fan-shaped leaves and leafstalks that contain drinkable sap
tall Madagascan plant, traveler's tree

Examples

The ravenala is often called the traveler's tree due to its reservoir of water in the leafstalks.
In Madagascar, the ravenala is a prominent feature of the landscape and is used for its edible parts.
Explorers have long relied on the ravenala as a source of refreshment during their journeys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traveler's tree - common name for ravenala, known for storing water in its leafstalks

Etymology

From Malagasy ravenala, the local name of the plant.
