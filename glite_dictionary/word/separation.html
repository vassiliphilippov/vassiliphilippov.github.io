separation

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the space or distance between objects or parts
distance between things

Examples

The separation between the two shelves was large enough to fit a vase.
Engineers ensured a safe separation between the cables to prevent interference.
There must be some separation between electrical and gas lines for safety.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gap - an empty space or opening between two things
  • Synonym: interval - a space between two points or objects

Etymology

From Latin separatio (a parting, division), from separare (to separate).
