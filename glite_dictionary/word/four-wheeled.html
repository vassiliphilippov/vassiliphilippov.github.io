four-wheeled

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔr ˌwiːld / (AmE), / ˈfɔː ˌwiːld / (BrE)
Having four wheels, especially used to describe a vehicle.
having four wheels (vehicle)

Examples

The company introduced a new four-wheeled electric scooter this year.
Children rode their four-wheeled toy cars around the park.
Most passenger cars are four-wheeled vehicles.

Synset Examples

a four-wheel drive
