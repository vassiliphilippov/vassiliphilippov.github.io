Sarawak

1. PROPN

/ səˈrɑːwɑk / (AmE), / səˈrɑːwæk / (BrE)
a state in Malaysia, located on the northwest of the island of Borneo; the largest state in the country by area
state in Malaysia on Borneo

Examples

She traveled to Sarawak to experience the region's rich rainforest and unique wildlife.
Many indigenous communities in Sarawak maintain their traditional customs and ways of life.
The capital city of Sarawak, Kuching, is known for its vibrant food markets.

Etymology

From Malay 'Sarawak', of uncertain origin, possibly from a local river's name.
