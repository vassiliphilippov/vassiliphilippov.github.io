hitwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪtˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈhɪtˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A female professional killer who is paid to murder someone, especially using a gun.
female professional killer

Examples

The police arrested the hitwoman before she could carry out the contract.
Rumors spread quickly that a notorious hitwoman was in town for a job.
She had a reputation as the most efficient hitwoman in the criminal underworld.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hitman - a man who is paid to kill someone
  • Synonym: assassin - a person who murders an important person for political or monetary reasons

Etymology

Formed from 'hit' (as in 'hitman', slang for contract killer) + 'woman'.
