regrets

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈɡrɛts / (AmE), / rɪˈɡrɛts / (BrE)
a polite or formal message declining an invitation, often expressing sorrow for being unable to attend
formal polite refusal to an invitation

Examples

Please convey my regrets to the host as I cannot attend the party.
She sent her regrets in a formal letter declining the invitation.
He asked his assistant to express his regrets for missing the meeting.
