Transnistria

1. PROPN

/ trænzˈnɪstriə / (AmE), / tranzˈnɪstriə / (BrE)
A de facto unrecognized state in Eastern Europe that declared independence from Moldova; not internationally recognized as a sovereign country.
unrecognized breakaway state in Eastern Europe

Examples

Few countries officially recognize Transnistria as an independent nation.
The conflict between Moldova and Transnistria remains unresolved.
Trade restrictions complicate life in Transnistria.

Etymology

From Romanian 'Transnistria', meaning 'beyond the Dniester'.
