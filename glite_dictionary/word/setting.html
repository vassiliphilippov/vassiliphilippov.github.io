setting

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛt̬ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɛtɪŋ / (BrE)
a piece of metal in jewelry, such as a ring, that holds a gem in place

Examples

The diamond's setting was made of white gold and beautifully crafted.
She chose a sapphire ring with an intricate vintage setting.
A secure setting is crucial to keep the stone from falling out.

Synset Examples

  • Synonym: mount - a backing or frame that supports something, especially in jewelry
