sildenafil

1. NOUN

/ sɪlˈdɛnəfɪl / (AmE), / sɪlˈdɛlɪfɪl / (BrE)
A drug used to treat erectile dysfunction and pulmonary hypertension, often known by the trade name Viagra.
drug for erectile dysfunction (Viagra)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed sildenafil to help manage his patient's condition.
Many people recognize sildenafil by its brand name, Viagra.
Research continues into other potential applications for sildenafil beyond erectile dysfunction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Viagra - brand name for sildenafil, used to treat erectile dysfunction

Etymology

International Nonproprietary Name, combining chemical elements of the drug's structure.
