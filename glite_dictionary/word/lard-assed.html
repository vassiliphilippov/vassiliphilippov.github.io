lard-assed

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɑrdˌæst / (AmE), / ˈlɑːdˌɑːst / (BrE)
(informal, slang, often offensive) Overweight or habitually lazy, especially in a way seen as worthy of criticism or mockery.
overweight or habitually lazy (slang, offensive)
informal slang offensive

Examples

He was feeling so lard-assed after sitting on the couch all weekend.
If you keep skipping gym class, people might call you lard-assed.
She joked that she’d grown lard-assed after too many desserts.

Synset Examples

She was too lard-assed to join the hike.
They called him lard-assed for avoiding workouts.
He felt lard-assed after eating all the snacks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lazy - not willing to work or use effort
  • Synonym: fat - having a large amount of excess flesh

Etymology

Formed from 'lard-ass' (slang for a person with a large or fatty backside) and the adjectival suffix '-ed'.
