ichthyology

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪkθiˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌɪkθiˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The scientific study of fishes, forming a branch of zoology.
the scientific study of fishes
medical

Examples

Dr. Lee specializes in ichthyology, focusing on the behavior of deep-sea species.
Universities often offer courses in ichthyology as part of their biology programs.
His passion for marine life led him to pursue a career in ichthyology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zoology - the scientific study of animals

Etymology

From Greek ikhthys (fish) + -logia (study).
