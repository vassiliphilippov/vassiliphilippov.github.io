Jan Vermeer

1. PROPN

/ jɑn vɛrˈmɪr / (AmE), / jæn vəˈmɪə / (BrE)
(1632–1675) A Dutch painter renowned for his masterful use of light and color, best known for works such as 'Girl with a Pearl Earring.'
Dutch painter (1632–1675), master of light

Examples

The Louvre displayed several masterpieces by Jan Vermeer during the special exhibition.
Art historians continue to debate how Jan Vermeer achieved his extraordinary effects with light.
I visited Delft to walk in the footsteps of Jan Vermeer and see his birthplace.
