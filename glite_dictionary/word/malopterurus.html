Malopterurus

1. PROPN

/ ˌmæləpˈtɛrərəs / (AmE), / ˌmaləpˈtɛrərəs / (BrE)
A genus of African freshwater fish known as electric catfish, capable of producing electric shocks.
genus of electric catfish

Examples

Malopterurus is notable for its ability to generate electric shocks to stun prey.
The aquarium recently acquired a specimen of Malopterurus for its freshwater exhibit.
Researchers are studying the electric organs of Malopterurus to learn about bioelectricity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electric catfish - common name for fish of the genus Malopterurus

Etymology

From Greek 'malos' (soft) + 'pteron' (fin), referring to the fish's fin structure.
