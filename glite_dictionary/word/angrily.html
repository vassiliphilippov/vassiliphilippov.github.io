angrily

1. ADV

/ ˈæŋɡrəli / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡrɪli / (BrE)
In a manner showing anger or strong displeasure.
in an angry manner

Examples

She angrily slammed the door after the argument.
He angrily responded to the criticism, refusing to back down.
The teacher spoke angrily to the students who had broken the rules.

Synset Examples

he angrily denied the accusation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: calmly - in a calm or composed manner
  • Degree Higher: furiously - in a way that shows intense, often uncontrolled anger
