Tivoli

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɪvəli / (AmE), / ˈtɪvəli / (BrE)
a town about twenty miles east of Rome, famous since Roman times as a summer resort and known for its waterfalls and historic sites
historic town near Rome, Italy

Examples

We visited Tivoli to see the famous Villa d'Este and its beautiful gardens.
During the Roman Empire, Tivoli was a popular retreat for wealthy citizens.
The picturesque waterfalls in Tivoli attract tourists from around the world.

Etymology

From Latin Tibur (ancient name of the town).
