orthographic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔr.θəˈɡræf.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔː.θəˈɡræf.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to spelling or the conventional writing system of a language.
relating to spelling or writing systems

Examples

Her orthographic skills improved significantly after she started studying English spelling rules.
The dictionary included detailed orthographic information for each entry.
Different languages use different orthographic conventions to represent sounds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orthographical - alternative form of orthographic; relating to orthography
  • Antonym: phonetic - relating to speech sounds rather than written symbols

Etymology

From Greek orthographia ('correct writing'); 'ortho-' meaning 'correct' and 'graph-' meaning 'write'.
