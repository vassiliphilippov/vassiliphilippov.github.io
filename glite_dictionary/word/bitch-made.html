bitch-made

1. ADJ

/ bɪtʃˌmeɪd / (AmE), / bɪtʃˌmeɪd / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) Showing weakness, cowardice, or a lack of courage, often suggesting that someone is easily dominated or submissive.
weak, cowardly (slang, offensive)
slang offensive

Examples

He acted totally bitch-made when challenged by his friends.
Don't let anyone call you bitch-made—stand up for yourself.
The team lost because of their bitch-made attitude in the final moments.

Synset Examples

Stop being so bitch-made and take responsibility.
That bitch-made excuse didn't fool anyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cowardly - lacking courage
  • Antonym: brave - showing courage
