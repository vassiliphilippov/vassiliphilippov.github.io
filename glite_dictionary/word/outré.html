outré

1. ADJ

/ uːˈtreɪ / (AmE), / uːˈtreɪ / (BrE)
Unconventional and strikingly unusual; bizarre or eccentric.
strikingly unconventional; bizarre

Examples

Her outré sense of fashion drew attention wherever she went.
The artist became famous for his outré installations in urban spaces.
Critics described the novel's plot as both outré and imaginative.

Synset Examples

restaurants of bizarre design — one like a hat, another like a rabbit
famed for his eccentric spelling
a freakish combination of styles
his off-the-wall antics
the outlandish clothes of teenagers
outre and affected stage antics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - unusual in an appealing or interesting way
  • Synonym: outlandish - very strange or unusual

Etymology

From French outré (exaggerated, excessive), past participle of outrer (to overdo), from Latin ultra (beyond).
