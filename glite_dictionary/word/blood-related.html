blood-related

1. ADJ

/ ˈblʌd rɪˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈblʌd rɪˌleɪtɪd / (BrE)
having a relationship through biological descent rather than by marriage or adoption; related by birth.
having a biological family connection

Examples

He is my blood-related brother, not just a close family friend.
Only blood-related relatives are entitled to inherit under the new law.
While Sarah was adopted, her sister is her only blood-related sibling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: related - connected by family, marriage, or adoption; associated
