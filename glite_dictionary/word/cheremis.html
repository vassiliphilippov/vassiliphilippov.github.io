Cheremis

1. NOUN

/ ˌtʃɛrəˈmis / (AmE), / ˌtʃɛrəˈmiːs / (BrE)
an alternative name for the Mari language, a Finnic language spoken by the Mari people in Russia
Finnic language spoken in Russia

Examples

The Cheremis language has several distinctive dialects.
He conducted fieldwork with speakers of Cheremis in the Volga region.
Many traditional songs are preserved in the Cheremis language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mari - the Mari language, a Finnic language spoken in Russia

Etymology

From Russian Черемисы (Cheremisy), formerly used to refer to the Mari people and their language.

2. NOUN

/ ˌtʃɛrəˈmis / (AmE), / ˌtʃɛrəˈmiːs / (BrE)
a member of a rural Finnic people living in eastern Russia, also known as Mari
member of Finnic ethnic group in Russia

Examples

The Cheremis have preserved many of their traditional festivals and customs.
He wrote a book about the history of the Cheremis people.
Many anthropologists have traveled to study the Cheremis in their native region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mari - the self-designation of the Cheremis people

Etymology

Name used in Russian and English sources, historically referring to the Mari people.
