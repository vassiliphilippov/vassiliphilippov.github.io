fashionmonger

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃənˌmʌŋɚ / (AmE), / ˈfæʃ(ə)nˌmʌŋɡə / (BrE)
a person who is concerned with or advises others about fashionable clothing and trends
person concerned with fashion trends

Examples

He was a well-known fashionmonger, always advising his friends on the latest trends.
The celebrity hired a professional fashionmonger to revamp her wardrobe.
Some see her as a style icon, while others dismiss her as just another fashionmonger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trendsetter - someone who starts or popularizes new fashions

Etymology

From 'fashion' + 'monger' (a person who promotes or deals in something); 'monger' from Old English 'mangere'.
