blue-grey

1. ADJ

/ bluːˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / bluːˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a grey colour with a tinge of blue.
grey tinged with blue

Examples

The artist chose a blue-grey background for a subtle and calming effect.
Her eyes were a striking shade of blue-grey in the morning light.
He painted the walls blue-grey to match the modern decor.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: grey - of a colour intermediate between black and white
  • Degree Higher: blue - of the colour of the clear sky
