utterness

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌtərnəs / (AmE), / ˈʌtənəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being complete, absolute, or extreme
quality of being utter or complete

Examples

The utterness of her disappointment was clear from her expression.
He was stunned by the utterness of the silence in the empty house.
The decision was made with utterness and conviction, leaving no room for doubt.

Synset Examples

the starkness of his contrast between justice and fairness was open to many objections

Related Terms

  • Synonym: completeness - the state of being complete
  • Antonym: partiality - the state of being incomplete or only part of something

Etymology

From 'utter' (complete, absolute) plus the suffix '-ness', indicating a state or quality.
