to vasectomize

1. VERB

/ vəˈsɛktəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / vəˈsɛktəmaɪz / (BrE)
to perform a surgical procedure to cut or remove the vas deferens, usually to induce sterility in men
to surgically cut/remove the vas deferens
medical

Examples

Many men choose to vasectomize as a permanent method of contraception.
The doctor vasectomized four patients during the clinic session yesterday.
Some couples discuss whether or not to vasectomize as part of their family planning.

Synset Examples

many men choose to be vasectomized as a form of safe birth control

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vasectomy - surgical removal of part of the vas deferens

Etymology

Formed from 'vasectomy' (from Latin 'vas' meaning vessel + Greek 'ektomē' meaning excision) with the English verb suffix '-ize'.
