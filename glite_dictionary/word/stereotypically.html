stereotypically

1. ADV

/ ˌstɛriəˈtɪpɪkli / (AmE), / ˌstɪəriəˈtɪpɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is characteristic of a stereotype or conforms to a fixed, oversimplified idea or image.
in a stereotypical manner

Examples

He acted stereotypically masculine throughout the entire evening.
The movie portrays the villain stereotypically, relying on old clichés about criminals.
She was, stereotypically, cast as the caring nurse in the drama.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: typically - normally; in a way that is usual for a particular person or thing

Etymology

From 'stereotypical', which derives from 'stereotype', from French 'stéréotype'.
