ailurophobia

1. NOUN

/ aɪˌlʊrəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / aɪˌljuːrəˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
A morbid or intense fear of cats.
morbid fear of cats
medical

Examples

Her ailurophobia made it impossible for her to visit friends who owned cats.
Even the sight of a kitten triggered Mark's ailurophobia.
Cognitive therapy helped reduce his severe ailurophobia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zoophobia - an irrational fear of animals
  • Degree Lower: phobia - an extreme or irrational fear of something

Etymology

From Greek 'ailuros' (cat) + 'phobia' (fear).
