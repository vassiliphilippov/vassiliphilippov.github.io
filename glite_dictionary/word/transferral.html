transferral

1. NOUN

/ trænˈsfɜr.əl / (AmE), / trænsˈfɜː.rəl / (BrE)
the act or process of moving something from one place, position, or person to another
the act of transferring

Examples

The transferral of data to the new server was completed overnight.
Her transferral from the accounting department to marketing was a smooth process.
The committee discussed the transferral of funds to support the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transfer - the act of moving something from one person, place, or thing to another

Etymology

From 'transfer' + '-al', modeled after similar noun forms.
