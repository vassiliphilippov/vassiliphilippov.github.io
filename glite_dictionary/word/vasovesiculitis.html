vasovesiculitis

1. NOUN

/ ˌveɪsoʊvəˌsɪkjəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌveɪsəʊvəˌsɪkjʊˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the vas deferens and seminal vesicles, usually occurring with prostatitis.
inflammation of vas deferens and seminal vesicles
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with vasovesiculitis after experiencing pain and urinary symptoms.
Chronic vasovesiculitis can lead to complications in male fertility if left untreated.
His symptoms of fever and pelvic discomfort were attributed to an underlying vasovesiculitis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prostatitis - inflammation of the prostate gland

Etymology

From Latin 'vas' (vessel) + 'vesicula' (small bladder, vesicle) + '-itis' (inflammation).
