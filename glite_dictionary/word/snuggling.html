snuggling

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnʌɡlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsnʌɡlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of lying or sitting closely and affectionately with someone, often as a form of comfort or intimacy
affectionate, close cuddling

Examples

Their favorite activity on weekends was simply snuggling on the couch while watching movies.
A bit of snuggling before sleep helped them feel connected.
She missed the comfort of snuggling her pet cat after a long day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cuddling - the act of holding someone close in one's arms as a way of showing love or affection
