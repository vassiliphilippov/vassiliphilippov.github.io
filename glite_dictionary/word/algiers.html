Algiers

1. PROPN

/ ælˈdʒɪrz / (AmE), / ælˈdʒɪəz / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Algeria, an ancient port located on the Mediterranean coast
capital city of Algeria

Examples

The Casbah in Algiers is famous for its historic architecture.
She traveled to Algiers for a conference on urban development.
Trade flourished through the port of Algiers during the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Algeria - the country of which Algiers is the capital

Etymology

From French Alger, from Arabic al-Jazā’ir meaning 'the islands'.
