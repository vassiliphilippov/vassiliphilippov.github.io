to nutrify

1. VERB

/ ˈnuːtrəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtrɪˌfaɪ / (BrE)
to provide with nourishment; to supply with nutrients
to nourish or supply with nutrients

Examples

The soil must nutrify the plants for healthy growth.
Certain supplements are designed to nutrify the body at a cellular level.
A balanced diet will thoroughly nutrify all your vital organs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to nourish - to provide with the food or other substances necessary for growth, health, and good condition

Etymology

From Latin 'nutrire' (to nourish) + English '-fy' (to make).
