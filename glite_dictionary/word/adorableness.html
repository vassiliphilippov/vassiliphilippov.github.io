adorableness

1. NOUN

/ əˈdɔɹ.ə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / əˈdɔː.rə.bəl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely attractive or charming in a way that inspires affection
quality of being adorable

Examples

The adorableness of the puppy melted everyone's heart.
She couldn't resist commenting on the adorableness of her friend's baby.
The movie relies heavily on the adorableness of its animal characters to win over the audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adorable - inspiring great affection; charming
  • Synonym: cuteness - the quality of being cute

Etymology

From 'adorable' + noun suffix '-ness'.
