deerskin

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪrˌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɪəˌskɪn / (BrE)
leather made from the hide of a deer, valued for its softness and durability.
leather from a deer's hide

Examples

The jacket was made of supple deerskin and felt incredibly comfortable.
Some Native American moccasins are traditionally crafted from deerskin.
She bought a pair of deerskin gloves for the winter.

Etymology

Compound of 'deer' and 'skin'.
