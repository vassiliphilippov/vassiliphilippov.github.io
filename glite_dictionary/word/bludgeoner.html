bludgeoner

1. NOUN

/ ˈblʌdʒənər / (AmE), / ˈblʌdʒɪənə / (BrE)
a person who attacks or threatens with a bludgeon or heavy club
person who attacks with a bludgeon

Examples

The bludgeoner was apprehended by police shortly after the attack.
In the old novel, a mysterious bludgeoner roamed the foggy streets at night.
The judge sentenced the bludgeoner to ten years in prison for aggravated assault.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assailant - a person who attacks another

Etymology

From 'bludgeon', possibly of uncertain origin, plus '-er' denoting an agent.
