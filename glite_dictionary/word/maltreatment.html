maltreatment

1. NOUN

/ mælˈtritmənt / (AmE), / mælˈtriːtmənt / (BrE)
cruel or inhumane treatment, especially causing physical or emotional harm
cruel or inhumane treatment

Examples

The animal shelter investigates reports of maltreatment and neglect.
Victims of maltreatment often require both medical and psychological help.
Laws exist to prevent the maltreatment of children and vulnerable adults.

Synset Examples

the child showed signs of physical abuse

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abuse - cruel or violent treatment of a person or animal
  • Synonym: mistreatment - the action of treating someone badly
  • Antonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, or considerate

Etymology

Formed from 'mal-' (bad) and 'treatment', by analogy with 'mistreatment'.
