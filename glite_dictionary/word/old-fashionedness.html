old-fashionedness

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊldˈfæʃəndnəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊldˈfæʃəndnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being old-fashioned or no longer in fashion
the state of being old-fashioned

Examples

The old-fashionedness of her clothing made her stand out at the party.
Some people appreciate the old-fashionedness of traditional architecture.
Despite its old-fashionedness, the café was popular with young people.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: modernity - the quality of being modern
  • Synonym: outdatedness - the state of being outdated
