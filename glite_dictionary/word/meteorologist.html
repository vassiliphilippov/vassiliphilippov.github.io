meteorologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌmiːtiəˈrɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌmiːtiəˈrɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A scientist who studies and forecasts the weather by examining processes in the earth's atmosphere.
weather scientist

Examples

The meteorologist predicted heavy rainfall for the weekend.
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a meteorologist and studying hurricanes.
During the broadcast, the meteorologist explained the formation of the approaching storm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weather forecaster - a person who predicts the weather

Etymology

From Greek 'meteōrologia' (the study of atmospheric phenomena) + '-ist' (one who practices) via French 'météorologie'.
