déjeuner

1. NOUN

/ deɪˈʒuːneɪ / (AmE), / deɪˈʒɜːneɪ / (BrE)
A light meal taken at midday; lunch, especially in a French or formal context.
a midday meal; lunch (French term)

Examples

She enjoyed a delightful déjeuner in the gardens of the château.
The invitation was for a formal déjeuner at noon.
Many artists have depicted elegant déjeuner scenes in their paintings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunch - the meal eaten in the middle of the day

Etymology

From French 'déjeuner' (to breakfast, midday meal).
