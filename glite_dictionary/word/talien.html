Talien

1. PROPN

/ tɑːˈljen / (AmE), / tæˈliːən / (BrE)
A port and shipbuilding center in northeastern China on the Liaodong Peninsula, now known as Dalian and part of Luda.
port city in northeastern China

Examples

The city of Talien has a rich history as a port and shipbuilding center.
Many foreign powers were interested in controlling Talien due to its strategic location.
Today, Talien is better known as Dalian, an important Chinese economic hub.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dalian - modern name for the city formerly known as Talien
