addition

1. NOUN

/ əˈdɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / əˈdɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
an amount or quantity that is added to something else
an amount that is added

Examples

There was a significant addition to the company’s workforce this year.
The new regulations include an addition to the list of prohibited substances.
After the addition of extra rooms, the house became much more spacious.

Synset Examples

there was an addition to property taxes this year
they recorded the cattle's gain in weight over a period of weeks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: increase - an amount by which something becomes larger or greater

Etymology

From Latin additio, from addere (to add).
