aphrodisiacal

1. ADJ

/ ˌæf.roʊ.dɪˈzaɪ.ə.kəl / (AmE), / ˌæf.rəʊ.dɪˈzʌɪ.ə.kəl / (BrE)
(of a substance or quality) stimulating sexual desire.
stimulating sexual desire

Examples

The chef claimed that the dish had aphrodisiacal properties.
Some people believe in the aphrodisiacal effects of certain exotic spices.
She wore perfume with an aphrodisiacal scent to set the mood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aphrodisiac - a substance that stimulates sexual desire

Etymology

From aphrodisiac, which comes from Greek Aphrodisiakos 'pertaining to Aphrodite', the Greek goddess of love.
