fallal

1. NOUN

/ fəˈlæl / (AmE), / fəˈlæl / (BrE)
a piece of cheap, showy jewelry or an ornament used to decorate clothing
cheap showy ornament or jewelry

Examples

She adorned her hat with every fallal she could find at the market.
The dress was lovely, though perhaps weighed down by a few too many fallals.
He criticized the gown for its excessive fallals and gaudy ornaments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bauble - a small, showy trinket or decoration
  • Synonym: trinket - a small ornament, piece of jewelry, or item of little value

Etymology

Perhaps from French 'falbala' (showy trimming on a dress).
