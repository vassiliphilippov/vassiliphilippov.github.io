feetfirst

1. ADV

/ fiːtˈfɝst / (AmE), / fiːtˈfɜːst / (BrE)
(of movement) with the feet leading or foremost.
with feet leading or foremost

Examples

He slipped off the rock and landed feetfirst in the shallow water.
You should never jump feetfirst into an unknown body of water.
The worker descended the ladder feetfirst for safety.

Synset Examples

he jumped into the pool feetfirst

Related Terms

  • Antonym: headfirst - with the head leading or foremost
