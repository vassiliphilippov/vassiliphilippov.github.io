to metricate

1. VERB

/ ˈmɛtrɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrɪˌkeɪt / (BrE)
to convert from a non-metric system of measurement to the metric system
convert to the metric system

Examples

Several countries decided to metricate in the 1970s for consistency in trade.
The government is making efforts to metricate all official road signs and documents.
Many businesses had to purchase new equipment when they metricated their measurements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metrication - the process of adopting the metric system

Etymology

From metric + -ate, modeled after similar scientific verbs.
