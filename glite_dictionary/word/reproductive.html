reproductive

1. ADJ

/ ˌriːprəˈdʌktɪv / (AmE), / ˌriːprəˈdʌktɪv / (BrE)
relating to or capable of producing offspring or new life; concerned with reproduction.
relating to reproduction or producing offspring

Examples

The scientist studied the reproductive behavior of the endangered animal species.
Some chemicals are known to disrupt the reproductive systems of fish.
During puberty, the reproductive organs begin to mature.

Synset Examples

the reproductive potential of a species is its relative capacity to reproduce itself under optimal conditions
the reproductive or generative organs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: generative - relating to the power or function of producing offspring

Etymology

From Latin 'reproductivus', from 're-' (again) + 'producere' (to lead forth, produce).
