attractable

1. ADJ

/ əˈtræktəbəl / (AmE), / əˈtraktəbl̩ / (BrE)
capable of being attracted, especially by a magnet.
able to be attracted (esp. by magnet)

Examples

Only certain metals are attractable by a magnet, such as iron and steel.
The experiment showed that the particles were highly attractable under a strong magnetic field.
Before recycling, the technician separated all attractable materials using a powerful magnet.

Synset Examples

the magnetic chips of steel produced by a cutting tool are attractable by a magnet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magnetic - having the properties of a magnet; capable of being attracted to a magnet

Etymology

From 'attract' + '-able'.
