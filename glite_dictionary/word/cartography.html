cartography

1. NOUN

/ kɑrˈtɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / kɑːˈtɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
The science or practice of making maps and charts.
the making of maps

Examples

Advances in technology have revolutionized cartography in recent decades.
She specialized in cartography during her geography studies at university.
The museum's new exhibit explores the history of cartography from ancient times to today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mapmaking - the process or skill of making maps

Etymology

From French cartographie, from carte (map) and -graphie (writing, description).
