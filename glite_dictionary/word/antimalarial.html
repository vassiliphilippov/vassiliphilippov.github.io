antimalarial

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.ti.məˈlɛr.i.əl / (AmE), / ˌæn.ti.məˈleə.ri.əl / (BrE)
a drug used to prevent or treat malaria.
drug that prevents or treats malaria
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed an antimalarial for her trip to Africa.
Many antimalarials have side effects that need to be monitored.
He forgot to take his antimalarial tablets before entering the jungle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antimalarial drug - a drug used to prevent or cure malaria

Etymology

From anti- (against) + malaria.
