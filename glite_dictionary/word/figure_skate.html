to figure skate

1. VERB

/ ˈfɪɡjər skeɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡjə skeɪt / (BrE)
to perform artistic or dance movements on ice wearing skates, typically as a sport or display
perform dance movements on ice skates

Examples

She loves to figure skate at the local rink every winter.
The athletes will figure skate to classical music in tonight's show.
He started to figure skate competitively when he was just nine years old.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice skate - to move on ice using skates
