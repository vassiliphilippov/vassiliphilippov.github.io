Ronald Reagan

1. PROPN

/ ˈroʊnəld ˈreɪɡən / (AmE), / ˈrɒnəld ˈreɪɡən / (BrE)
(1911–2004) the 40th President of the United States, serving from 1981 to 1989, and previously an actor and Governor of California.
40th U.S. President (1981–1989)

Examples

Ronald Reagan was elected as the 40th President of the United States in 1980.
Many refer to the economic policies of Ronald Reagan as 'Reaganomics.'
Before entering politics, Ronald Reagan was a well-known film actor.
