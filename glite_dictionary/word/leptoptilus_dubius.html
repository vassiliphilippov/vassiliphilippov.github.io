Leptoptilus dubius

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɛptoʊpˈtaɪləs ˈduːbiəs / (AmE), / ˌlɛptəpˈtɪləs ˈdjuːbɪəs / (BrE)
a large stork native to India, noted for its upright, military gait
large Indian stork species

Examples

The Leptoptilus dubius is often seen wading through marshes in northern India.
Naturalists have studied the unique feeding habits of the Leptoptilus dubius for decades.
With its pronounced stride, the Leptoptilus dubius stands out among other storks.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin: genus name Leptoptilus, species from Latin dubius (doubtful), used in scientific classification.
