coaching

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊ.tʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.tʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the job or activity of a professional coach, especially in sports or business
the job of being a coach

Examples

After years of playing football, he made a successful transition into coaching.
She found her true passion in coaching and helping young athletes reach their potential.
Many executives seek coaching to improve their leadership and management skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: training - the process of teaching or learning skills

Etymology

From 'coach' (instructor or trainer) + '-ing' (denoting action or process).
