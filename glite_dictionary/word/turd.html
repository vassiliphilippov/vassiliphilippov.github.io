turd

1. NOUN

/ tɝd / (AmE), / tɜːd / (BrE)
A piece of solid waste passed from the bowels; feces (used as a vulgar or insulting term).
vulgar term for feces
vulgar

Examples

He accidentally stepped in a dog turd while walking in the park.
She called him a lazy turd after he refused to help.
There was a foul smell coming from the cat's turds in the litter box.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feces - solid bodily waste discharged from the bowels

Etymology

From Middle English *tord*, from Old English *tord* (dung, excrement), related to Proto-Germanic *turdam*.
