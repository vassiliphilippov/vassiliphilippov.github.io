chisel-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɪzəl laɪk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪzl laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the shape or qualities of a chisel, especially in being long, thin, and sharp-edged
resembling a chisel in shape or quality

Examples

The sculptor's nose was chisel-like, giving his face a striking profile.
The bird has a chisel-like bill, perfect for boring into wood.
His chisel-like features made him instantly recognizable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chisel - a hand tool with a sharp, beveled edge for cutting or shaping hard materials
