embroideress

1. NOUN

/ ɛmˈbrɔɪdərɛs / (AmE), / ɪmˈbrɔɪdərɪs / (BrE)
a woman who embroiders, especially one who does so professionally
female embroiderer

Examples

As an accomplished embroideress, she was often commissioned to create decorative garments for the royal family.
The exhibition featured works by a renowned embroideress from 19th-century France.
Her skills as an embroideress were evident in the delicate patterns she stitched on the fabric.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embroiderer - a person who embroiders

Etymology

From 'embroider' + '-ess', a suffix forming female agent nouns.
