la-di-da

1. ADJ

/ ˌlɑː dɪ ˈdɑː / (AmE), / ˌlɑː dɪ ˈdɑː / (BrE)
Pretending to be genteel or refined in an exaggerated, snobbish way.
affectedly genteel or snobbish
informal

Examples

She spoke in a la-di-da accent that annoyed everyone at the party.
His la-di-da attitude made him seem out of place in the casual office.
Stop acting so la-di-da—we all know you grew up here, too.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pretentious - attempting to impress by affecting greater importance or merit than is actually possessed
  • Antonym: down-to-earth - practical and unpretentious

Etymology

Imitative, apparently echoing affected speech patterns, first recorded in the late 19th century.
