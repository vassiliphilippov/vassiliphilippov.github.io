greyly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡreɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪli / (BrE)
In a manner having or displaying a grey appearance or quality.
in a grey manner or appearance

Examples

The landscape stretched greyly under the overcast sky, creating a somber mood.
Her eyes shone greyly in the fading evening light.
Mist drifted greyly across the quiet lake at dawn.

Synset Examples

lonely creeks are opal in the dawn, sword-blue in the sun, greyly silver under misty moons

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grayly - in a manner having a grey appearance (American spelling)
