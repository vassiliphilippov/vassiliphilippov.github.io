Anostraca

1. NOUN

/ əˈnɑːstrəkə / (AmE), / əˈnɒstrəkə / (BrE)
an order of small aquatic crustaceans that lack a carapace, including fairy shrimps and brine shrimps
order of crustaceans (fairy/brine shrimps)

Examples

Researchers collected several species of Anostraca from temporary ponds in the region.
The Anostraca are known for their ability to survive in highly saline environments.
Biology students examined live specimens of Anostraca under the microscope.

Etymology

From Greek an- (without) + ostrakon (shell).
