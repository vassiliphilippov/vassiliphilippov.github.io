towny

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaʊni / (AmE), / ˈtaʊni / (BrE)
a resident of a college town who is not associated with the college or university
person in a college town not affiliated with college

Examples

There was often tension between the students and the townies in that small college town.
The annual parade was attended mostly by townies rather than university students this year.
She grew up as a towny but always felt drawn to the academic scene across town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: townie - resident of a university town not connected to the university
