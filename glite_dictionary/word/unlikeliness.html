unlikeliness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈlaɪkli.nəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈlaɪkli.nɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being unlikely; improbability
improbability; unlikely quality or condition

Examples

The unlikeliness of her winning the competition did not deter her from trying.
Considering the weather forecast, the unlikeliness of a picnic was apparent to everyone.
They discussed the unlikeliness of finding a solution by tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: improbability - the state of being improbable
  • Antonym: likelihood - the probability or chance that something will happen
