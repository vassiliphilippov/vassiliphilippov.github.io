Lasthenia

1. NOUN

/ læˈsθɛniə / (AmE), / lasˈθiːnɪə / (BrE)
A small genus of flowering herbs native to the Pacific coasts of North and South America, known for their yellow flowers.
genus of Pacific coast flowering herbs

Examples

Several species of Lasthenia are important components of vernal pool ecosystems.
The fields were covered in bright yellow flowers belonging to the Lasthenia genus.
Botanists discovered a new population of Lasthenia on the coastal plains.

Etymology

From Greek Lastheneia, a proper name used in botanic nomenclature.
