frottage

1. NOUN

/ frəˈtɑʒ / (AmE), / frɒˈtɑːʒ / (BrE)
the act of achieving sexual stimulation or orgasm by rubbing against another person, especially in a crowd, often without the other person's consent
sexual stimulation by rubbing against another person

Examples

The police arrested a man for repeated acts of frottage on the subway.
Frottage is considered a form of sexual assault in many jurisdictions.
She learned about frottage as part of her psychology course on paraphilic disorders.

Etymology

From French 'frottage', from 'frotter' meaning 'to rub'.
