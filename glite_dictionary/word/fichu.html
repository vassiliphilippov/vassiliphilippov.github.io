fichu

1. NOUN

/ fiˈʃu / (AmE), / fiˈʃuː / (BrE)
A lightweight triangular scarf, often made of lace or muslin, worn by women around the neck and shoulders.
lightweight triangular scarf for women

Examples

She wore a delicate lace fichu over her dress for the evening.
The museum’s costume collection featured a silk fichu from the 18th century.
Her grandmother taught her how to fold and tie a fichu correctly.

Etymology

From French 'fichu' (negligently put on), from the past participle of 'ficher' (to fix or fasten).
