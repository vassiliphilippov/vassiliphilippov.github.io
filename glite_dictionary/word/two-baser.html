two-baser

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuː ˌbeɪsər / (AmE), / ˈtuː ˌbeɪsə / (BrE)
A base hit in baseball on which the batter stops safely at second base; a double.
baseball hit reaching second base

Examples

The rookie surprised everyone with a two-baser in his first at-bat.
She cheered loudly when her favorite player hit a two-baser in the eighth inning.
The crowd erupted as the batter smashed a two-baser down the right field line.

Synset Examples

he hit a double to deep centerfield

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double - a base hit on which the batter stops safely at second base
