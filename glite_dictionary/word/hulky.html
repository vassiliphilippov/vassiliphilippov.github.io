hulky

1. ADJ

/ ˈhʌl.ki / (AmE), / ˈhʌl.ki / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) large and heavy; having great size and bulk.
large and bulky

Examples

The wrestler's hulky physique intimidated his opponents before the match even started.
They hired a hulky moving van to transport all the furniture to their new home.
A hulky bouncer stood at the nightclub entrance, checking everyone's ID.

Synset Examples

a hulking figure of a man
three hulking battleships

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hulking - large and clumsy; massive and bulky
  • Antonym: slight - small in size, amount, or degree
  • Synonym: massive - large and heavy or solid

Etymology

From 'hulk' (a large, heavy person or object) + '-y'.
