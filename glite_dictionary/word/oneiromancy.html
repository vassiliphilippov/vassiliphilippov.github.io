oneiromancy

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈnaɪrəˌmænsi / (AmE), / əʊˈnaɪərəˌmænsi / (BrE)
divination or prediction of the future through the interpretation of dreams
divination by interpreting dreams

Examples

Some ancient cultures placed great importance on oneiromancy to guide their decisions.
In literature, characters often seek advice from those skilled in oneiromancy.
She studied oneiromancy, hoping to find hidden meaning in her recurring dreams.

Etymology

From Greek oneiros (dream) and manteia (divination).
