erythropoietic

1. ADJ

/ ɪˌrɪθ.roʊ.pɔɪˈɛt.ɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌrɪθ.rə.pɔɪˈɛt.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or promoting the formation of red blood cells.
relating to red blood cell formation
medical

Examples

The doctor ordered tests to evaluate her erythropoietic activity.
Diseases affecting erythropoietic function can lead to anemia.
Certain drugs can stimulate erythropoietic pathways in the bone marrow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hematopoietic - relating to the formation of blood or blood cells

Etymology

From Greek 'erythros' (red) + 'poiein' (to make).
