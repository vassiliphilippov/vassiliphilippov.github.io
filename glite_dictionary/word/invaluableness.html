invaluableness

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈvæljuəblnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈvaljuːəblnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being extremely valuable or priceless.
quality of being priceless

Examples

We often overlook the invaluableness of good health until we lose it.
The invaluableness of her advice became clear after the project succeeded.
Historians spoke about the invaluableness of primary sources for understanding the past.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invaluability - quality of being invaluable

Etymology

From 'invaluable' + '-ness', ultimately from Latin 'in-' (not) + 'valere' (to be worth).
