ambidexterity

1. NOUN

/ ˌæmbɪˌdɛkˈstɛrɪti / (AmE), / ˌæmbɪdɛkˈstɛrɪti / (BrE)
the ability to use both hands equally well
ability to use both hands equally well

Examples

Her ambidexterity made her a valuable player on the basketball team.
Artists with ambidexterity can create impressive works using both hands simultaneously.
Developing ambidexterity can enhance your coordination and cognitive flexibility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ambidextrous - able to use both hands equally well

Etymology

From Latin ambidexter (right-handed on both sides), from ambi- (both) + dexter (right-handed).
