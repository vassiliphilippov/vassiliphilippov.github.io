surrealistic

1. ADJ

/ səˌriəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / sjʊəˌrɪəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
having the qualities of surrealism, especially by featuring dream-like, unusual, or fantastic imagery and strange combinations; relating to or characteristic of surrealism.
having dream-like, fantastic imagery

Examples

The movie's surrealistic scenes made the plot feel like a dream.
His paintings are filled with surrealistic images that defy logical explanation.
The novel blends reality and fantasy in a surrealistic narrative style.

Synset Examples

a great concourse of phantasmagoric shadows
the incongruous imagery in surreal art and literature

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surreal - having the qualities of surrealism; bizarre or dream-like

Etymology

From French 'surréaliste' (surrealist), from 'sur-' (beyond) + 'réalisme' (realism).
