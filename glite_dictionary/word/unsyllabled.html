unsyllabled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɪləbəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪləbld / (BrE)
(of a word or sound) not spoken or divided into syllables.
not spoken in syllables

Examples

The poem ended with an unsyllabled sigh, lingering in the air.
He listened in silence, waiting for some unsyllabled response to the question.
Her lips moved, but the words remained unsyllabled and unspoken.

Etymology

Prefix un- (not) + syllabled (having syllables).
