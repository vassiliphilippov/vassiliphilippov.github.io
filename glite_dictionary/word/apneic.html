apneic

1. ADJ

/ ˈæpniɪk / (AmE), / ˈapniːɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or affected by apnea, a temporary cessation of breathing.
relating to apnea (breathing stop)
medical

Examples

The nurse closely monitored the apneic patient during the night.
Infants born prematurely are at higher risk of apneic episodes.
Prompt intervention is required when a person becomes apneic after surgery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apnea - temporary stopping of breathing

Etymology

From apnea, modeled on patterns of forming adjectives with '-ic'.
