odourless

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊdɚləs / (AmE), / ˈəʊdələs / (BrE)
having no smell; not possessing an odor
having no smell

Examples

Air is typically odourless and invisible to the naked eye.
The lab technician released an odourless gas into the chamber for the experiment.
She prefers her cleaning products to be odourless so they don't irritate her allergies.

Synset Examples

odorless gas
odorless flowers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: odorless - American spelling of 'odourless'
  • Antonym: scented - having a smell, often pleasant

Etymology

From 'odour' + '-less', meaning 'without odor'.
