Shirley Temple

1. PROPN

/ ˈʃɝːli ˈtɛmpəl / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːli ˈtɛmpəl / (BrE)
(1928–2014) An American child actress famous in the 1930s for her starring roles in numerous films, later a diplomat.
famous American child actress (1930s) and diplomat

Examples

As a child, Shirley Temple starred in many beloved Hollywood musicals.
Many people remember Shirley Temple for her curly hair and cheerful songs.
Later in life, Shirley Temple served as a U.S. ambassador.
