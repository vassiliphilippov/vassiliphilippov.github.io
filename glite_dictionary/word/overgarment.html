overgarment

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊvərˌɡɑrmənt / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌɡɑːmənt / (BrE)
an outer piece of clothing worn over other garments, especially for warmth or protection
outer garment worn over other clothes

Examples

She wore a thick overgarment to protect herself from the winter chill.
The museum displayed a medieval overgarment made of wool and fur.
Please remove your overgarment and hang it on the rack before entering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outerwear - clothing worn outdoors or over other clothes
  • Antonym: undergarment - a piece of clothing worn under other clothes

Etymology

Formed from 'over-' (above) and 'garment', from Old French 'garnement'.
