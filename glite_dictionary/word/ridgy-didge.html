ridgy-didge

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪdʒi dɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈrɪdʒi dɪdʒ / (BrE)
(Australian, informal) Genuine; authentic; real and not fake or imitation.
genuine; authentic (Australian slang)
informal slang

Etymology

Australian slang, from 'fair dinkum ridgy-didge', possibly an alteration of 'didgeridoo' (an Aboriginal instrument), but the precise origin is uncertain.
