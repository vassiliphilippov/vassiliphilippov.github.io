totalizator

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬əl.ɪˌzeɪ.t̬ɚ / (AmE), / təʊˈtæl.ɪ.zeɪ.tə / (BrE)
a machine or electronic system that records bets and calculates the total amount wagered, dividing winnings among successful bettors
betting machine that calculates and shares winnings

Examples

The totalizator automatically calculated the payouts for all race bets.
Many racecourses now use a computerized totalizator to handle betting pools.
The totalizator made it easier for winners to claim their share without disputes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tote - alternative term for a betting system that pools wagers

Etymology

From French 'totalisateur', from 'totaliser' meaning 'to total'.
