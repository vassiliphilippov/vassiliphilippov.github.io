uncheckable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtʃɛkəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɛkəbl̩ / (BrE)
Impossible to check, control, or restrain.
impossible to check or restrain

Examples

His temper became uncheckable after the incident, and nobody could calm him down.
The uncheckable spread of misinformation worried public health officials.
She displayed an uncheckable energy that inspired everyone around her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unchecked - not restrained or controlled
  • Antonym: controllable - capable of being controlled or restrained

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'checkable' (able to be checked).
