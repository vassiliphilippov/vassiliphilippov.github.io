overskirt

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊvɚˌskɝt / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌskɜːt / (BrE)
An outer skirt that is worn over another skirt, typically as a decorative garment or fashion layer.
outer skirt worn over another skirt

Examples

The bride wore a delicate lace overskirt that added elegance to her dress.
A brightly colored overskirt was popular in Victorian fashion.
Designers often layer an overskirt to create more volume and texture in evening gowns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skirt - a woman's outer garment fastened around the waist and hanging down around the legs

Etymology

Formed from 'over-' (meaning above or upon) + 'skirt'.
