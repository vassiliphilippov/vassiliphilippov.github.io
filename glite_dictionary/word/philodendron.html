philodendron

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɪləˈdɛndrən / (AmE), / ˌfɪləˈdɛndrən / (BrE)
A tropical American plant with large, typically heart-shaped leaves, often grown indoors as a houseplant.
tropical houseplant with large leaves

Examples

She placed a philodendron on the windowsill to brighten up her apartment.
The leaves of the philodendron are glossy and heart-shaped, making it a popular choice for interiors.
Many people choose a philodendron because it requires little maintenance and grows well indoors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monstera - another tropical houseplant with large, split leaves

Etymology

From Greek 'philo-' (loving) + 'dendron' (tree), referring to the plant's climbing habit.
