bluenose

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluˌnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈbluːnəʊz / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Nova Scotia, Canada
native or inhabitant of Nova Scotia

Examples

Many bluenoses are proud of their maritime heritage.
After moving to Halifax, she quickly made friends with several bluenoses.
As a bluenose, he has always celebrated Nova Scotia Day enthusiastically.

Etymology

Nickname derived from 18th-century usage; may refer to the bluish hue of a local potato or to cold weather affecting noses.
