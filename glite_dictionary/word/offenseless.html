offenseless

1. ADJ

/ əˈfɛnsləs / (AmE), / əˈfɛnslɪs / (BrE)
incapable of causing offense or harm; unable or unlikely to attack or offend
incapable of offending or attacking

Examples

The puppy's offenseless nature made it loved by everyone in the family.
She offered an offenseless reply, hoping not to escalate the disagreement.
With its soft eyes and offenseless stance, the animal posed no threat to anyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harmless - unable or unlikely to cause harm or injury
  • Synonym: inoffensive - not likely to offend or provoke; mild
  • Antonym: aggressive - ready or likely to attack or confront

Etymology

From 'offense' + '-less', indicating absence of offense.
