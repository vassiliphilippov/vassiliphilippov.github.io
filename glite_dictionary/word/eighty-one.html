eighty-one

1. NUM

/ ˌeɪti ˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˌeɪti ˈwʌn / (BrE)
the number 81; the cardinal number that is one more than eighty
the number 81

Examples

She scored eighty-one points on her math test, which was above average.
The building has eighty-one apartments spread across ten floors.
