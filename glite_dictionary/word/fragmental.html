fragmental

1. ADJ

/ fræɡˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / fræɡˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
Consisting of small, disconnected or incomplete parts; fragmentary.
consisting of small disconnected parts

Examples

Archaeologists uncovered fragmental remains of ancient pottery at the site.
The report was based on fragmental data rather than a complete analysis.
The old manuscript survived only in fragmental form, making it challenging to interpret.

Synset Examples

fragmentary remains

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragmentary - consisting of fragments; incomplete

Etymology

From Latin fragmentum (a fragment) plus the suffix -al.
