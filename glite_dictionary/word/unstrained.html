unstrained

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈstreɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈstreɪnd / (BrE)
not experiencing psychological or emotional stress; remaining calm or unaffected by pressure.
not under psychological stress

Examples

Despite the chaos in the office, her unstrained expression showed she was in control.
Their friendship remained unstrained even during difficult times.
He gave an unstrained laugh, showing no sign of worry or tension.

Synset Examples

the campaign would not leave party loyalties unstrained

Related Terms

  • Synonym: untroubled - not affected by worry or stress
  • Antonym: strained - showing signs of tension or stress

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈstreɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈstreɪnd / (BrE)
(especially of actions, speech, or music) natural, easy, and not showing signs of effort or being forced.
natural; not forced or strained

Examples

Her laughter had an unstrained charm that put everyone at ease.
He delivered the high notes in an unstrained manner throughout the performance.
Their conversation felt relaxed and unstrained, as if they had known each other for years.

Synset Examples

a voice with a pleasingly unforced quality
his playing is facile and unstrained

Related Terms

  • Antonym: strained - showing signs of tension or effort; forced
  • Synonym: unforced - not forced; natural; effortless

Etymology

From un- (not) + strained, past participle of strain (to force to make a strenuous effort).
