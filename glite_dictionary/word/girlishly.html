girlishly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡɜrlɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːlɪʃli / (BrE)
In a manner characteristic of a young girl, typically suggesting innocence, shyness, or youthfulness.
in a way characteristic of a young girl

Examples

She smiled girlishly at the compliment, her cheeks turning pink.
His excitement for the holiday was almost girlishly enthusiastic.
The actress laughed girlishly, bringing a sense of innocence to the role.

Synset Examples

she was girlishly shy

Related Terms

  • Antonym: boyishly - in a way characteristic of a boy

Etymology

Formed from 'girlish' + '-ly'.
