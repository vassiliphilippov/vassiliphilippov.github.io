vichyssoise

1. NOUN

/ ˌvɪʃiˈswɑz / (AmE), / ˌviːʃiːˈswɑːz / (BrE)
a creamy soup made of puréed potatoes, leeks, and onions, usually served cold
cold creamy potato and leek soup

Examples

For a refreshing summer appetizer, she served homemade vichyssoise garnished with fresh chives.
The restaurant’s signature dish is a chilled vichyssoise that delights potato lovers.
He learned how to make classic vichyssoise during his culinary training in Paris.

Etymology

French, from the name of the town Vichy in France.
