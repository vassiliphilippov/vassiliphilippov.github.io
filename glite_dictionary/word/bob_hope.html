Bob Hope

1. PROPN

/ bɑb hoʊp / (AmE), / bɒb həʊp / (BrE)
(1903–2003) United States comedian and actor, born in England, famous for his work in film, radio, and television, especially with Bing Crosby.
US comedian, actor, and entertainer (1903–2003)

Examples

Bob Hope entertained American troops for decades with his signature comedy shows.
The legendary comedian Bob Hope starred in many films alongside Bing Crosby.
Many people remember Bob Hope for his dedication to entertaining soldiers overseas.
