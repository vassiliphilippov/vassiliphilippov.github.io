John T. Scopes

1. PROPN

/ dʒɑn ti skoʊps / (AmE), / dʒɒn tiː skəʊps / (BrE)
(1900–1970) An American high school teacher in Tennessee, best known for being tried in 1925 for teaching evolution in violation of state law.
American teacher tried for teaching evolution

Examples

John T. Scopes became famous after being charged in the Scopes Trial of 1925.
The controversy surrounding John T. Scopes highlighted national debates over science and religion.
Many history textbooks discuss the significance of the case involving John T. Scopes.
