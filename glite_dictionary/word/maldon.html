Maldon

1. PROPN

/ ˈmældən / (AmE), / ˈmɔːldən / (BrE)
the site of a battle in 991 in which the Danes defeated the Saxons, commemorated in the Old English poem 'The Battle of Maldon'
battle site where Danes defeated Saxons in 991

Examples

The poem vividly describes the events that took place at Maldon in 991.
Historians still debate the strategy used by the Saxons at Maldon.
The legacy of Maldon is an important part of Anglo-Saxon literature.
