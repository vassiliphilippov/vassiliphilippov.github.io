Zoloft

1. NOUN

/ ˈzoʊlɑːft / (AmE), / ˈzəʊlɒft / (BrE)
a trademarked brand name for sertraline, a selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor (SSRI) used as an antidepressant
brand name for an SSRI antidepressant
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Zoloft to help manage my anxiety symptoms.
After starting Zoloft, she noticed an improvement in her mood within a few weeks.
It is important to take Zoloft exactly as directed by your healthcare provider.

Related Terms
