Yahoo!

1. PROPN

/ ˈjɑːhu / (AmE), / ˈjɑːhuː / (BrE)
A major web portal and search engine offering information, news, email services, finance, and more.
web portal and search engine

Examples

I use Yahoo! to check the latest news and weather updates every morning.
Many people still rely on Yahoo! for their email accounts and online searches.
The company's acquisition history made Yahoo! a frequent topic in business news.

Etymology

Trademark of Yahoo! Inc., originally an acronym for 'Yet Another Hierarchical Officious Oracle'.
