molester

1. NOUN

/ məˈlɛstər / (AmE), / məˈlɛstə / (BrE)
a person who subjects others to unwanted or improper sexual activity, especially of a sexual nature
person who commits sexual abuse or harassment

Examples

The police arrested the suspected molester after several complaints were filed by local residents.
Parents are concerned about the presence of a molester living in their neighborhood.
Victims of a molester often struggle with emotional trauma for many years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abuser - a person who abuses another, especially sexually or physically
  • Antonym: victim - a person who is harmed or abused

Etymology

From Latin molestare (to disturb), via French molester.
