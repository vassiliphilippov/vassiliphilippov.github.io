marvelously

1. ADV

/ ˈmɑrvələsli / (AmE), / ˈmɑːvələsli / (BrE)
Excellently; in a way that excites wonder or admiration.
excellently; in a wonderful manner

Examples

The cake was marvelously moist and flavorful, impressing everyone at the party.
She performed marvelously at the recital, earning a standing ovation from the crowd.
The museum was marvelously curated, with each exhibit thoughtfully arranged.

Synset Examples

her voice is superbly disciplined
the colors changed wondrously slowly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wonderfully - in a wonderful manner; excellently
  • Synonym: superbly - in an excellent or impressive way

Etymology

From marvelous + -ly, ultimately from Old French *merveille* and Latin *mirabilia* (wonderful things).
