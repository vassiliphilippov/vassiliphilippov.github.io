slumberer

1. NOUN

/ ˈslʌmbɚɚ / (AmE), / ˈslʌmbərə / (BrE)
a person who is sleeping
a person who is asleep

Examples

The slumberer did not stir, even as the thunderstorm raged outside.
With the softest steps, she crept past the slumberer on the couch.
A gentle smile lingered on the face of the slumberer in the morning light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleeper - a person who is sleeping

Etymology

From 'slumber' (to sleep), ultimately from Middle English 'slomren'.
