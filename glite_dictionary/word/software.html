software

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔftˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈsɒftˌweə / (BrE)
The programs and other operating information used by a computer.
programs and data for computers

Examples

Our company develops security software for international clients.
She upgraded her computer's software to the latest version last night.
Good software can improve your productivity and make tasks much easier.

Synset Examples

the market for software is expected to expand

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hardware - the physical components of a computer system

Etymology

Coined in the 1950s, using 'soft' (as opposed to hardware) and 'ware' (as in hardware), indicating intangible program instructions.
