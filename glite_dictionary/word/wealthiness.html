wealthiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛlθi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈwɛlθi.nɪs / (BrE)
The state of having a lot of money, property, or valuable possessions; richness or affluence.
state of being wealthy or affluent

Examples

Her wealthiness allowed her to travel the world in luxury.
The wealthiness of the region was evident in its beautiful homes and gardens.
He attributed his wealthiness to years of hard work and wise investments.

Synset Examples

great wealth is not a sign of great intelligence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wealth - abundance of valuable possessions or money
  • Antonym: poverty - the state of being extremely poor

Etymology

From 'wealthy' + '-ness', where 'wealthy' derives from 'wealth'.
