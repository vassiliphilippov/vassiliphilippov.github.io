DoL

1. NOUN

/ di oʊ ɛl / (AmE), / diː əʊ ɛl / (BrE)
abbreviation for the Department of Labor, the United States federal department responsible for promoting the welfare and working conditions of wage earners.
US Department of Labor (abbreviation)
american

Examples

The DoL released new statistics on employment this month.
Workers filed complaints with the DoL about unsafe factory conditions.
The policy change was announced by the DoL in a press briefing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Department of Labor - the federal department responsible for labor standards and welfare in the U.S.

Etymology

Abbreviation of Department of Labor.
