head-spinningly

1. ADV

/ ˈhɛdˌspɪnɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌspɪnɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely confusing, dizzying, or overwhelming, especially by being surprising or rapid.
in a confusing or overwhelming manner

Examples

The plot of the novel shifts head-spinningly between times and places, leaving the reader breathless.
The conference schedule changed head-spinningly, making it difficult for attendees to keep up.
Her career has advanced head-spinningly fast over the past two years.

Synset Examples

He spoke head-spinningly about his latest discoveries.
The head-spinningly fast ride made me dizzy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dizzily - in a way that makes you feel dizzy or confused
  • Synonym: confusingly - in a manner that causes confusion
  • Antonym: calmly - in a calm or tranquil manner

Etymology

Formed from the compound 'head-spinning' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
