dry-eyed

1. ADJ

/ ˈdraɪˌaɪd / (AmE), / ˈdraɪˌaɪd / (BrE)
not crying or showing tears; free from tears
free from tears; not crying

Examples

Despite the moving speech, she remained dry-eyed throughout the ceremony.
He walked away dry-eyed, refusing to show any emotion.
Some children stayed dry-eyed while others sobbed loudly at the end of the show.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tearful - showing or accompanied by tears
  • Synonym: unemotional - not showing emotion
