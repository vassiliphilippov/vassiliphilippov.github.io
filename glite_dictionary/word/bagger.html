bagger

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæɡər / (AmE), / ˈbaɡə / (BrE)
a machine for placing objects or substances into bags, especially in industrial or commercial settings
machine that puts things into bags

Examples

The factory installed a new bagger to improve packaging efficiency.
A bagger automatically fills each bag with the correct weight of flour.
They called the technician when the bagger malfunctioned during production.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbæɡər / (AmE), / ˈbaɡə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to put goods, especially groceries, into bags or containers for customers
person who packs items into bags

Examples

The supermarket bagger carefully placed the eggs on top of the other groceries.
After school, he worked as a bagger to earn some extra money.
Many customers appreciate when the bagger double-bags heavy items.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: packer - a person who packs goods for transport or sale
