rapidness

1. NOUN

/ ˈræp.ɪd.nəs / (AmE), / ˈrap.ɪd.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being rapid; swiftness or quickness.
the quality of being rapid or fast

Examples

The rapidness of her recovery amazed the doctors.
You can feel the rapidness of the river's current as you swim.
His response was notable for its rapidness and accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speed - the rate at which someone or something moves or operates
  • Antonym: slowness - the quality of being slow
  • Synonym: swiftness - the quality of moving very quickly

Etymology

From 'rapid' + '-ness', where 'rapid' is from Latin 'rapidus' (seizing, swift).
