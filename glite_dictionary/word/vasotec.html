Vasotec

1. NOUN

/ ˈvæsətɛk / (AmE), / ˈvæsətɛk / (BrE)
A trademarked name for enalapril, an ACE inhibitor used to treat high blood pressure and heart failure by relaxing blood vessels.
trade name for an ACE inhibitor (enalapril)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Vasotec to help control his high blood pressure.
After her heart attack, she began taking Vasotec as part of her daily medication.
Patients with congestive heart failure may benefit from treatment with Vasotec.
