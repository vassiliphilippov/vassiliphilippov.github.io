Charles the Bald

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʃɑrlz ðə bɔld / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːlz ðə bɔːld / (BrE)
(823–877) Frankish king and Holy Roman Emperor, known as Charles II as emperor and Charles I as king of France.
Frankish king and Holy Roman Emperor (823–877)

Examples

Charles the Bald was crowned Holy Roman Emperor in 875.
The reign of Charles the Bald saw significant political and cultural changes in Western Europe.
Historians often debate the effectiveness of Charles the Bald's rule over the Frankish kingdom.
