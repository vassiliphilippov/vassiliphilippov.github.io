North Carolinian

1. NOUN

/ nɔrθ ˌkɛrəˈlɪniən / (AmE), / nɔːθ ˌkærəˈlɪniən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the state of North Carolina, USA
person from North Carolina, USA

Examples

Jane is a proud North Carolinian who grew up in Raleigh.
As a North Carolinian, he loves barbecue and college basketball.
The festival was organized by a group of local North Carolinians.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tar Heel - informal term for a person from North Carolina

Etymology

Formed from 'North Carolina' + '-ian' (denoting association or origin).
