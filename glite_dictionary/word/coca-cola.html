Coca-Cola

1. NOUN

/ ˌkoʊkəˈkoʊlə / (AmE), / ˌkəʊkəˈkəʊlə / (BrE)
a trademarked carbonated soft drink with a cola flavor, produced by The Coca-Cola Company
a well-known cola-flavored soft drink

Examples

Would you like a Coca-Cola with your meal?
She ordered a Coca-Cola at the cafe.
The restaurant serves only Coca-Cola products.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Pepsi - a rival brand of cola-flavored soft drink

Etymology

From 'coca' (from the coca leaf) and 'cola' (from the kola nut), the original flavorings.
