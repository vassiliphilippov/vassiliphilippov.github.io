Peronospora hyoscyami

1. NOUN

/ pɛrəˌnɑspərə ˌhaɪəˈskaɪəmaɪ / (AmE), / pɛrəˌnɒspərə ˌhaɪəˈskaɪəmaɪ / (BrE)
a species of fungus that causes downy mildew disease on growing tobacco plants
fungus causing downy mildew on tobacco
medical

Examples

Peronospora hyoscyami can devastate tobacco crops if not properly managed.
Researchers have developed new treatments to protect plants from Peronospora hyoscyami.
Farmers monitor their fields closely for signs of Peronospora hyoscyami infection.

Etymology

Peronospora is derived from Greek 'peronē' (belt) and 'spora' (seed), indicating spore formation; 'hyoscyami' refers to its association with the Hyoscyamus plant genus.
