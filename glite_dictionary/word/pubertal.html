pubertal

1. ADJ

/ ˈpjuːbɚtəl / (AmE), / ˈpjuːbətəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of puberty.
relating to puberty

Examples

The doctor discussed pubertal development with the teenager during her checkup.
Early pubertal changes often include rapid growth and hormonal shifts.
Some children may experience pubertal onset earlier or later than their peers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pubescent - Relating to or occurring at puberty

Etymology

From puberty + -al (suffix forming adjectives).
