lingenberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪŋənˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋɡənˌbɛri / (BrE)
A small, evergreen shrub of northern regions in Europe, Asia, and North America that produces edible red berries.
northern shrub producing edible red berries

Examples

She picked fresh lingenberries from the forest to make jam.
The tart flavor of lingenberry sauce is popular in Scandinavian cuisine.
Wild lingenberries thrive in the cool climate of northern Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lingonberry - another spelling for the same shrub and its berries

Etymology

From Swedish 'lingon' (the fruit) + English 'berry.'
