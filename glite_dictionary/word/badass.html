badass

1. ADJ

/ ˈbædˌæs / (AmE), / ˈbædɑːs / (BrE)
Impressive or intimidating in appearance, attitude, or style; displaying confidence or toughness.
impressively tough or stylish
informal slang american

Examples

The movie's hero is a total badass who never backs down from a fight.
She looked especially badass in her leather jacket and boots.
His badass attitude inspired the whole team to push harder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tough - strong and able to deal with hardship
  • Synonym: intimidating - making others feel fear or awe
  • Antonym: timid - lacking courage or confidence

Etymology

From 'bad' + 'ass', originally American slang.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbædˌæs / (AmE), / ˈbadˌas / (BrE)
A person who is impressive because of their extreme confidence, toughness, or unconventional behavior.
impressively tough or confident person
informal slang american

Examples

Everyone at the gym knows Jenna is a total badass when it comes to lifting weights.
The movie's hero is a badass who never backs down from a fight.
After surviving the grueling training, she proved herself as a true badass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tough - person who is strong or resilient
  • Antonym: wimp - person who is weak or lacks confidence

Etymology

Blend of 'bad' and 'ass'; emerged as American slang in mid-20th century.

3. NOUN

/ ˈbædˌæs / (AmE), / ˈbædˌas / (BrE)
A person who is aggressive, intimidating, or tough, often in a way that commands respect or fear.
aggressive or tough person
informal slang

Examples

Everyone at school knew not to mess with the local badass who ran the block.
He tried to act like a badass, but most people saw through his tough exterior.
The movie's main villain is a real badass who intimidates everyone he meets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bully - a person who habitually seeks to harm or intimidate those whom they perceive as vulnerable
  • Antonym: pushover - a person who is easy to influence or overcome
