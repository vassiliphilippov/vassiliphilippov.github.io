cajolingly

1. ADV

/ kəˈdʒoʊlɪŋli / (AmE), / kəˈdʒəʊlɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that attempts to persuade someone gently or with flattery.
in a persuading, flattering manner

Examples

She smiled cajolingly at her friend, hoping to win his support.
He spoke cajolingly to the child, trying to persuade her to eat her vegetables.
The salesperson leaned in cajolingly, offering an extra discount if I purchased today.

Synset Examples

‘Come here,’ she said coaxingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coaxingly - in a gentle, persuasive manner

Etymology

From 'cajole' + '-ingly'. 'Cajole' from French 'cajoler' (to wheedle).
