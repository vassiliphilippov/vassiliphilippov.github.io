inaccessibly

1. ADV

/ ˌɪn.əkˈsɛs.ə.bli / (AmE), / ˌɪn.əkˈses.ə.bli / (BrE)
In a way that is difficult or impossible to reach, approach, or obtain.
in an inaccessible manner

Examples

The ancient temple was built so inaccessibly that only a few have ever visited it.
Rare birds nested inaccessibly high on the cliff face, far from human disturbance.
The files were stored inaccessibly within a secure database, making them hard to retrieve.

Synset Examples

located inaccessibly on the top of a mountain

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inaccessible - not able to be reached or entered
  • Antonym: accessibly - in an accessible manner

Etymology

From 'inaccessible' + '-ly'. 'Inaccessible' from Latin 'inaccessibilis'.
