hetero-fascism

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɛ.t̬ə.roʊˈfæʃ.ɪ.zəm / (AmE), / ˌhɛ.tə.rəʊˈfæʃ.ɪ.zəm / (BrE)
a political ideology or attitude that promotes heterosexual dominance and oppression or discrimination against LGBTQ+ individuals
ideology asserting heterosexual dominance and oppressing LGBTQ+

Examples

Many activists have spoken out against hetero-fascism in government policies.
Some critics accused the new legislation of encouraging hetero-fascism in schools.
The documentary explores the rise of hetero-fascism in various societies.

Synset Examples

Hetero-fascism is a serious concern in today's political climate.
The rally was criticized for promoting hetero-fascism and intolerance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homophobia - prejudice or discrimination against homosexual people
  • Synonym: fascism - authoritarian, nationalistic right-wing system of government and social organization

Etymology

From hetero- (meaning 'heterosexual') plus fascism, drawing analogy to the authoritarian and oppressive characteristics of fascism.
