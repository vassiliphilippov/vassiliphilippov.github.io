governmental

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡʌvərnˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌɡʌvənˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
relating to the affairs, activities, or structure of government or its institutions.
relating to government or its activities

Examples

The new law introduces several important governmental reforms to improve efficiency.
A governmental agency was established to oversee the project.
Public trust in governmental institutions has declined in recent years.

Synset Examples

governmental policy
public confidence and governmental morale

Related Terms

  • Synonym: political - relating to government or public affairs

Etymology

From government + -al (forming adjectives meaning 'relating to').
