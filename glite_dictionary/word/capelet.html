capelet

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪplət / (AmE), / ˈkeɪplət / (BrE)
a small cape covering the shoulders, typically worn by women as a garment or fashion accessory
small shoulder-covering cape (women's clothing)

Examples

She wore a delicate lace capelet over her evening gown.
The vintage capelet added a touch of elegance to her outfit.
Many brides choose a sheer capelet instead of a traditional veil.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: cape - a sleeveless outer garment fastened at the neck and hanging over the back and shoulders

Etymology

From French diminutive of 'cape'.
