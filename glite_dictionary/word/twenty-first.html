twenty-first

1. ADJ

/ ˌtwɛnti ˈfɝst / (AmE), / ˌtwɛnti ˈfɜːst / (BrE)
coming next after the twentieth in position; 21st in a sequence
ordinal number: 21st in order

Examples

He finished in twenty-first place in the race this year.
Her twenty-first birthday was a memorable celebration for everyone.
The meeting will be held on the twenty-first of November.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twentieth - coming next after the nineteenth; 20th in position
  • Degree Higher: twenty-second - coming next after the twenty-first; 22nd in position
