unclear

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈklɪr / (AmE), / ʌnˈklɪə / (BrE)
not stated or described in an explicit or easily understandable way; difficult to understand due to lack of clarity or detail.
not clearly stated or defined

Examples

The instructions were so unclear that no one knew what to do.
Her explanation was unclear, leaving the audience confused.
It remains unclear whether the proposal will be accepted.

Synset Examples

he confuses the reader with ill-defined terms and concepts

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clear - easy to perceive, understand, or interpret

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + clear, from Latin clarus (clear, bright).

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈklɪr / (AmE), / ʌnˈklɪə / (BrE)
not easily understood or deciphered; difficult to make out or interpret.
not easily understood or deciphered

Examples

Her handwriting was so unclear that even her teacher struggled to read it.
The instructions were unclear, so I wasn't sure how to assemble the furniture.
It was unclear whether the meeting would be held on Monday or Tuesday.

Synset Examples

indecipherable handwriting

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clear - easy to perceive, understand, or interpret
  • Synonym: indecipherable - impossible to read or interpret

Etymology

From un- (not) + clear, ultimately from Latin clarus (clear).

Source: WordNet

3. ADJ

/ ʌnˈklɪr / (AmE), / ʌnˈklɪə / (BrE)
not easy to understand, or confusing; not clear in meaning or intent
not clear; confusing or ambiguous

Examples

The instructions were so unclear that no one knew what to do.
It is still unclear whether the project will go ahead.
His explanation was unclear and left everyone confused.

Synset Examples

the law itself was unclear on that point
the reason for their actions is unclear to this day

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clear - easy to understand, unambiguous
  • Synonym: ambiguous - open to more than one interpretation; not having one obvious meaning

Etymology

Prefix 'un-' (not) + 'clear', ultimately from Latin 'clarus' (clear, bright).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00431687-a
Synset POS: a
