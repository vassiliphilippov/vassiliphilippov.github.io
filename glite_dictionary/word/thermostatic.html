thermostatic

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɜːrmoʊˈstætɪk / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməˈstætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or controlled by a thermostat, especially in regard to temperature regulation
relating to a thermostat

Examples

The thermostatic valve adjusts the radiator's temperature automatically.
Our heating system is equipped with a thermostatic control for better efficiency.
She replaced the old tap with a thermostatic mixer for safer water temperature.

Synset Examples

thermostatic control

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermostat - a device that automatically regulates temperature

Etymology

From 'thermostat' + '-ic', of Greek origin: 'thermos' (hot) + 'statēs' (stander).
