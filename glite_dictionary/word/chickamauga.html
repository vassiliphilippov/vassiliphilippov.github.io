Chickamauga

1. NOUN

/ ˌtʃɪkəˈmɔɡə / (AmE), / ˌtʃɪkəˈmɔːɡə / (BrE)
A major battle of the American Civil War fought in 1863 in northwestern Georgia, resulting in a Confederate victory.
Civil War battle, 1863, Confederate victory

Examples

The Battle of Chickamauga marked one of the bloodiest engagements of the American Civil War.
Many historians study Chickamauga to understand the tactics of Civil War generals.
Confederate forces celebrated their victory at Chickamauga despite heavy losses on both sides.

Etymology

From Chickamauga Creek; the name is of Cherokee origin.
