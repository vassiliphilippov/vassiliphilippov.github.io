empty-headed

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛmptiˈhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˌɛmptiˈhɛdɪd / (BrE)
lacking seriousness or intelligence; silly or frivolous
frivolous; lacking seriousness or intelligence

Examples

Everyone dismissed her as empty-headed, but she proved them wrong with her clever solution.
The movie is full of empty-headed characters who care only about parties and fashion.
He didn't want to seem empty-headed during the interview, so he prepared carefully.

Synset Examples

a dizzy blonde
light-headed teenagers
silly giggles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silly - showing a lack of good sense or judgment; absurd or foolish
  • Antonym: thoughtful - showing careful consideration or attention; serious-minded
