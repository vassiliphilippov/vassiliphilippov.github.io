hidden

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɪdən / (AmE), / ˈhɪdən / (BrE)
not easily seen or found; kept out of sight or concealed from view.
not easily seen or found

Examples

We discovered a hidden path that led through the woods.
She keeps a hidden stash of chocolates in her desk drawer.
The treasure was buried in a hidden compartment behind the bookshelf.

Synset Examples

hidden valleys
a hidden cave
an obscure retreat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concealed - kept out of sight; secret or disguised
  • Antonym: visible - able to be seen

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'hide,' from Old English *hydan* (to conceal).

2. ADJ

/ ˈhɪdn̩ / (AmE), / ˈhɪdn̩ / (BrE)
designed or constructed to escape notice or detection; concealed intentionally
designed to elude detection

Examples

He found a hidden passage under the old mansion.

Synset Examples
