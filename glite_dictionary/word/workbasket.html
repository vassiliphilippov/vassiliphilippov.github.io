workbasket

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːkˌbæskɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
A basket used for storing the tools and materials needed for needlework or other craftwork, especially sewing.
basket for sewing or craft materials

Examples

She kept her needles and threads neatly organized in a workbasket by her armchair.
My grandmother's old workbasket was filled with colorful spools of thread and fabric scraps.
He reached into the workbasket to find a pair of scissors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sewing basket - a basket for holding sewing implements and materials
