flat bone

1. NOUN

/ flæt boʊn / (AmE), / flæt bəʊn / (BrE)
A cut of beef from the sirloin, situated next to the wedge bone.
sirloin beef cut near the wedge bone

Examples

The butcher recommended a flat bone steak for grilling this weekend.
She ordered a flat bone cut because it is especially tender and flavorful.
The recipe suggests marinating the flat bone overnight for best results.
