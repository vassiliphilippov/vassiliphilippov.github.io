Salvadorian

1. NOUN

/ ˌsælvəˈdɔːriən / (AmE), / ˌsælvəˈdɔːriən / (BrE)
a person who was born in, or is a native or inhabitant of, El Salvador
native or inhabitant of El Salvador

Examples

The restaurant was run by a Salvadorian family who shared traditional recipes with their customers.
Several famous poets are Salvadorian and have received international recognition.
My best friend in college is a proud Salvadorian who visits El Salvador every summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Salvadoran - alternative form meaning native or inhabitant of El Salvador

Etymology

From El Salvador (country in Central America) + -ian (English suffix forming demonyms).
