Central American

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛntrəl əˈmɛrɪkən / (AmE), / ˌsɛntrəl əˈmɛrɪkən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Central America
person from Central America

Examples

As a proud Central American, she frequently spoke about her family's heritage.
Many Central Americans migrate north in search of better economic opportunities.
The museum features artwork from several Central American countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: South American - a native or inhabitant of South America
  • Synonym: North American - a native or inhabitant of North America

/ ˌsɛntrəl əˈmɛrɪkən / (AmE), / ˌsɛntrəl əˈmɛrɪkən / (BrE)
of or relating to Central America, its people, or its languages

Examples

The Central American coffee industry is known for its high-quality beans.
Many Central American countries share similar cultural traditions.
She is fluent in several Central American dialects.
