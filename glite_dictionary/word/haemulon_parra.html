Haemulon parra

1. NOUN

/ ˌhiːmjʊˈloʊn ˈpɑːrə / (AmE), / ˌhiːmjʊˈləʊn ˈparə / (BrE)
a species of marine fish in the grunt family, found from Florida to Brazil and in the Gulf of Mexico
a species of marine grunt fish

Examples

The Haemulon parra is commonly found near coral reefs along the western Atlantic coast.
Marine biologists studied the feeding habits of Haemulon parra in the Gulf of Mexico.
Fishermen often recognize Haemulon parra by its distinct coloring and shape.

Etymology

From scientific Latin: genus 'Haemulon' and species name 'parra'.
