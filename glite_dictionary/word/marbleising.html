marbleising

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrbəˌlaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːbəˌlaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
A texture or pattern that resembles the appearance of marble.
texture resembling marble

Examples

The artist used special paints to achieve a beautiful marbleising on the vase.
The marbleising on the paper made it look elegant and unique.
Decorative marbleising is often applied to walls to imitate expensive stone surfaces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marbling - the technique or result of creating a marble-like pattern

Etymology

From 'marble' + '-ise' (to make) + '-ing', referring to the process or result of giving something a marble-like appearance.
