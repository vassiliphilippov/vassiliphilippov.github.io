Orientalism

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔɹiˈɛn.tə.lɪ.zəm / (AmE), / ˌɔː.riˈen.tə.lɪ.zəm / (BrE)
the study or scholarly knowledge of Asian cultures, languages, and peoples, especially those of East and South Asia
study of Asian cultures and languages

Examples

He devoted his career to Orientalism, specializing in the ancient languages of China and Japan.
The museum's collection reflects the influence of Orientalism on European art in the nineteenth century.
Modern scholars often critique the assumptions underlying traditional Orientalism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Asian studies - academic field focused on Asian cultures and societies

Etymology

From French 'orientalisme', from 'oriental' (eastern), ultimately from Latin 'orientalis'.
