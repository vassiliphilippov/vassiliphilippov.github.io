country-bred

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌntriˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈkʌntriˌbrɛd / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) originating in the countryside, typically regarded as simple or unsophisticated due to rural upbringing.
originating from the countryside; unsophisticated

Examples

His country-bred manners made him seem out of place at the elegant dinner party.
She was a country-bred girl with a straightforward way of speaking.
The country-bred puppy was full of energy and unaffected by city noise.

Synset Examples

This childhood memory will take country-bred readers back to the days of their youth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rustic - of or relating to the countryside; simple or unsophisticated
  • Antonym: urbane - refined in manner or style, typically of a city dweller
