olive-gray

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːlɪv ˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv ˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a grey color tinged with olive; a grayish shade that has a hint of olive green.
gray tinged with olive

Examples

The artist chose an olive-gray backdrop to complement the warmer tones in her painting.
He wore a suit of olive-gray fabric, subtle yet stylish for the occasion.
The sky took on an olive-gray hue as the storm approached.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gray - a color between black and white
  • Degree Higher: olive - a dark yellowish-green color
