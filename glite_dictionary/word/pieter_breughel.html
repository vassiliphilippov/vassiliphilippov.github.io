Pieter Breughel

1. PROPN

/ ˈpitər ˈbrɔɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈpiːtə ˈbrɔɪɡəl / (BrE)
(1525–1569) A Flemish painter known for his detailed landscapes and peasant scenes, regarded as one of the greatest artists of the Dutch and Flemish Renaissance.
Flemish painter (1525–1569)

Examples

Pieter Breughel is celebrated for his vivid depictions of rural life in the sixteenth century.
Art historians often study the techniques of Pieter Breughel to understand Renaissance landscapes.
The museum acquired a rare painting by Pieter Breughel for its Flemish art collection.
