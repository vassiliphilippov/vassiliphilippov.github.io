washtub

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑːʃ.tʌb / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃ.tʌb / (BrE)
a large tub used for washing clothes or linens by hand
tub for washing clothes by hand

Examples

She filled the washtub with hot water to wash the dirty sheets.
In the past, many families used a washtub and a washboard to clean their clothes.
The old metal washtub now serves as a decorative planter in the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laundry tub - a tub used for doing laundry

Etymology

Formed from 'wash' + 'tub'.
