Storeria

1. NOUN

/ stəˈrɪəriə / (AmE), / stəˈrɪəriə / (BrE)
a genus of small, nonvenomous snakes in the family Colubridae, including brown snakes and the redbelly snake, native to North America
a genus of small North American snakes

Examples

The genus Storeria is commonly found in woodland habitats across eastern North America.
Naturalists observed a Storeria in the garden while collecting data on local wildlife.
Several species within Storeria are known for their secretive behavior and preference for moist environments.

Etymology

Named after American zoologist David Humphreys Storer.
