calefactory

1. ADJ

/ ˌkæləˈfæktəri / (AmE), / ˌkæləˈfæktəri / (BrE)
serving to produce or provide heat; related to heating.
serving to heat

Examples

The old monastery still had a calefactory where the monks could warm themselves in winter.
Many homes once had a calefactory chamber for drying clothes and storing fuel.
A calefactory device can help maintain consistent temperature in a greenhouse.

Synset Examples

a heating pad is calefactory

Etymology

From Latin 'calefactorium' (warming room), from 'calefacere' (to make warm).
