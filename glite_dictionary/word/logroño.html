Logroño

1. PROPN

/ loʊˈɡroʊnjoʊ / (AmE), / ləʊˈɡrɒnjəʊ / (BrE)
a city in northern Spain, situated on the Ebro River; the capital of the autonomous community of La Rioja
city in northern Spain, capital of La Rioja

Examples

The wine region surrounding Logroño is famous for its high-quality Rioja wines.
We spent a few days exploring the historic old quarter of Logroño.
Many pilgrims on the Camino de Santiago pass through Logroño on their journey.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Logroño', name of the city.
