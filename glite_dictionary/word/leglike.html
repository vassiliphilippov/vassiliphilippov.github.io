leglike

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛɡˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or functioning like a leg, especially in form or movement.
resembling a leg

Examples

The insect's leglike structures help it move efficiently across leaves.
Some sea creatures have leglike appendages instead of true legs.
In the model, wires were bent into leglike shapes to support the platform.

Synset Examples

leglike appendages

Etymology

From 'leg' + 'like'.
