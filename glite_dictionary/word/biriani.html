biriani

1. NOUN

/ bɪrˈjɑːni / (AmE), / bɪrˈjɑːni / (BrE)
an Indian dish of highly seasoned rice cooked with meat, fish, or vegetables
Indian rice dish with meat or vegetables

Examples

My favorite meal is biriani with lamb and plenty of spices.
She ordered vegetable biriani at the Indian restaurant last night.
The aroma of freshly cooked biriani filled the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biryani - variant spelling of biriani, Indian seasoned rice dish

Etymology

From Urdu بریانی (biryānī), from Persian بریان (biryān, roasted, fried).
