beading

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
decoration or ornamental edging made by stringing or attaching beads
decoration made with beads

Examples

She added intricate beading to the edge of her wedding dress.
The purse was covered in delicate beading that sparkled in the light.
Antique gowns often feature hand-sewn beading along the bodice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embroidery - decorative needlework similar to beading

Etymology

From 'bead' + '-ing', denoting the action or result of beading.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbiːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
a narrow decorative molding resembling a row of beads, used to edge or ornament furniture or other objects
decorative molding with bead-like pattern

Examples

The ornate table had beading along the edge for an elegant touch.
She carefully restored the beading on the antique cabinet.
Many Victorian fireplaces feature decorative beading around the mantel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: molding - a strip of material with a decorative profile used for ornament or finishing on furniture or walls

Etymology

From 'bead' + '-ing', originally referring to a row of rounded projections resembling beads.
