syllabically

1. ADV

/ sɪˈlæbɪkli / (AmE), / sɪˈlæbɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or using syllables.
in a way relating to syllables

Examples

The chant was sung syllabically rather than in a melismatic style.
The conlang uses words that are constructed syllabically instead of morphologically.
Each word in the list was syllabically pronounced for clarity.

Synset Examples

syllabically pronounced

Related Terms

  • Synonym: syllabic - relating to syllables

Etymology

From 'syllabic' + '-ally', ultimately from Greek 'syllabē' (syllable).
