fattism

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæt.ɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈfæt.ɪz.əm / (BrE)
prejudice or discrimination against people who are overweight or obese
discrimination against overweight people

Examples

Many activists are working to raise awareness about fattism in the workplace.
She spoke out against fattism after encountering discrimination at a doctor's office.
The documentary explores the effects of fattism on mental health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sizeism - prejudice or discrimination based on body size

Etymology

Formed from 'fat' + '-ism', modelled after words like 'sexism' or 'racism'.
