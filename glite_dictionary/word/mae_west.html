Mae West

1. PROPN

/ meɪ wɛst / (AmE), / meɪ wɛst / (BrE)
(1892–1980) a famous American film actress, playwright, and sex symbol known for her witty double entendres and stage presence.
famous American film actress and playwright

Few actresses matched the unique charm of Mae West in the early twentieth century.
