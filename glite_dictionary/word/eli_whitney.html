Eli Whitney

1. PROPN

/ ˈiːlaɪ ˈwɪtni / (AmE), / ˈiːlaɪ ˈwɪtni / (BrE)
(1765–1825) An American inventor best known for inventing the mechanical cotton gin.
American inventor; creator of the cotton gin

Examples

Historians credit Eli Whitney with revolutionizing cotton production in the United States.
The invention of the cotton gin by Eli Whitney had a profound impact on the Southern economy.
Many school textbooks mention Eli Whitney as a pivotal figure in American technological history.
