proofed

1. ADJ

/ ˈpruːft / (AmE), / ˈpruːft / (BrE)
treated so as to be resistant to a particular substance or effect, such as rust, water, or shrinking
treated to resist damage or effect

Examples

All the electronics are proofed against dust and moisture for outdoor use.
The manufacturer ensures the fabric is proofed to prevent shrinkage after washing.
These cables are proofed with a special coating to guard against corrosion.

Synset Examples

rust-proofed automobiles
shrink-proofed fabrics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proof - treated or made so as to resist something (e.g., waterproof)
