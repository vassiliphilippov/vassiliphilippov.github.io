La Spezia

1. PROPN

/ lɑ ˈspɛtsiə / (AmE), / la ˈspɛtsɪə / (BrE)
A port city and major seaport in the Liguria region of northwest Italy, located on the Ligurian Sea.
port city in northwest Italy

Examples

The train to La Spezia offers stunning views of the Italian Riviera along the way.
Many tourists use La Spezia as a gateway to the Cinque Terre villages.
The harbor of La Spezia is bustling with activity all year round.

Etymology

From Italian 'La Spezia', meaning 'The Spice'.
