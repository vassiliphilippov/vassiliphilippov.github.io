kurus

1. NOUN

/ kuˈruːʃ / (AmE), / kʊˈrʊʃ / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Turkey, equal to one hundredth of a lira.
Turkish currency unit, 1/100 lira

Examples

A loaf of bread in Turkey may cost just a few kurus.
She paid 50 kurus for a bottle of water at the store.
The coins in her pocket included both lira and kurus.

Etymology

From Ottoman Turkish 'قروش' (kurûş), itself from French 'gros', meaning large coin.
