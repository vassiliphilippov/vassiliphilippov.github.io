secondo

1. NOUN

/ sɛˈkɔndoʊ / (AmE), / sɛˈkɒn.dəʊ / (BrE)
the second or lower part in a duet, especially a piano duet; also, the performer playing this part.
lower part in a musical duet

Examples

She prefers to play the secondo when performing piano duets with her sister.
The secondo part often provides the harmonic foundation in a duet.
During rehearsal, he seamlessly switched from secondo to primo to help his partner.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: primo - the first or higher part in a duet (especially a piano duet)

Etymology

From Italian 'secondo' meaning 'second'.
