self-loader

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛlf ˈloʊdər / (AmE), / ˈsɛlf ˈləʊdə / (BrE)
a firearm that automatically reloads itself after each shot is fired.
firearm that reloads itself automatically

Examples

Many modern armies have adopted the self-loader as their standard rifle.
The self-loader reduces the time needed to fire successive shots.
Collectors often seek out rare models of the early self-loader.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automatic - a firearm that reloads and fires continuously as long as the trigger is pressed
