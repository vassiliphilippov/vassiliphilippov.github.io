pogey

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊɡi / (AmE), / ˈpəʊɡi / (BrE)
money paid by the government to people who are unemployed or in need, especially as welfare or unemployment benefit.
government welfare money; unemployment benefit
informal british

Examples

After losing his job, he had to rely on pogey to support his family.
Some people criticize the system, claiming too many are living on pogey.
He filed for pogey while searching for new employment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dole - government unemployment benefit
  • Synonym: welfare - financial support given by the state to people in need
  • Synonym: benefit - payment made by the state to unemployed people

Etymology

Canadian slang, possibly from 'pogie' (workhouse, poorhouse), of uncertain origin.
