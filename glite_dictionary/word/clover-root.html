clover-root

1. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊvər ˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈkləʊvə ˌruːt / (BrE)
A hairy Eurasian plant with small yellow flowers and an astringent root, formerly used in medicine.
Eurasian medicinal plant with yellow flowers

Examples

The herbalist recommended using clover-root for its traditional astringent properties.
In early spring, clover-root produces clusters of small yellow flowers.
Historically, clover-root was a common remedy in some European folk medicines.
