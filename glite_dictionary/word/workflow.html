workflow

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːkˌfloʊ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːk.fləʊ / (BrE)
The sequence or rate of progress by which work is organized and completed in an organization or project.
sequence or progress of work tasks

Examples

We need to improve our workflow to meet the new deadlines.
The software helps manage the workflow in large organizations.
Interruptions in the morning can disrupt my entire workflow for the day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: process - a series of actions or steps taken to achieve a particular end
  • Synonym: procedure - an established or official way of doing something

Etymology

From 'work' + 'flow', suggesting the movement or progression of work.
