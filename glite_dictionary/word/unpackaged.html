unpackaged

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpækɪdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpakɪdʒd / (BrE)
(of goods) not wrapped, boxed, or put into a package; sold or presented loose.
not wrapped or packaged

Examples

The store promotes environmentally friendly shopping by offering unpackaged fruits and vegetables.
Many health food shops sell unpackaged grains and nuts to reduce waste.
She prefers to buy unpackaged products whenever possible to avoid excess plastic.

Synset Examples

unpackaged nuts

Related Terms

  • Antonym: packaged - (of goods) wrapped or enclosed in a package

Etymology

Formed from the prefix un- (not) and the word 'packaged'.
