lubricated

1. ADJ

/ ˈluːbrɪˌkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈluːbrɪkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
covered or treated with oil, grease, or another substance to reduce friction.
treated with oil or grease

Examples

The machine must be kept lubricated to function properly.
Make sure the bike chain is well lubricated before your ride.
His hands were lubricated with oil after fixing the engine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greased - coated or smeared with grease

Etymology

From Latin lubricare (to make slippery), via lubricate + -ed.
