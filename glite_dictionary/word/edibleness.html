edibleness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛdəblnəs / (AmE), / ˈɛdɪblnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being fit or suitable to be eaten
fitness to be eaten

Examples

The edibleness of wild plants should always be verified before consuming them.
Many food scientists study the edibleness of newly discovered plant species.
Questions about the edibleness of the mushrooms made the hikers hesitant to cook them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: edibility - the quality of being edible; suitability for eating
  • Antonym: inedibility - the quality of being unfit for eating

Etymology

From edible +‎ -ness; 'edible' from Latin edibilis (fit to eat).
