grandnephew

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrændˌnɛfju / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːn(d)ˌnɛfjuː / (BrE)
The son of one's niece or nephew.
son of your niece or nephew

Examples

My grandnephew came to visit us for the holidays this year.
She knitted a sweater for her new grandnephew when he was born.
During the family reunion, he was introduced to his youngest grandnephew.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: great-nephew - son of your niece or nephew; alternative term for grandnephew
  • Antonym: grandniece - daughter of your niece or nephew

Etymology

Formed from 'grand-' (denoting next generation) and 'nephew'.
