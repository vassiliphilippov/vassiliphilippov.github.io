Browallia

1. NOUN

/ broʊˈæliə / (AmE), / brəʊˈalɪə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical South American annual plants in the nightshade family, known for their showy blue or violet flowers.
genus of tropical flowering plants

Examples

The garden featured several Browallia plants, attracting bees and butterflies to their vibrant blooms.
Some gardeners prefer to grow Browallia as annuals for a splash of summer color.
The delicate blue flowers of Browallia are popular in hanging baskets and window boxes.

Etymology

Named after Swedish botanist Johannes Browallius (1707–1755).
