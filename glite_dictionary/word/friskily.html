friskily

1. ADV

/ ˈfrɪs.kə.li / (AmE), / ˈfrɪs.kɪ.li / (BrE)
In a lively, energetic, and playful manner.
in a lively and playful way

Examples

The puppy jumped friskily around the garden, chasing butterflies.
She danced friskily across the lawn, her laughter echoing in the air.
Despite his age, the old man walked friskily down the street every morning.

Synset Examples

he moves about friskily despite his age

Related Terms

  • Synonym: playfully - in a fun or lighthearted way
  • Synonym: frisky - full of energy and playful (adjective)

Etymology

From 'frisky' (lively, playful) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
