Tricholoma flavovirens

1. NOUN

/ traɪˌkɒloʊmə flævoʊˈvaɪrɛnz / (AmE), / trɪˌkɒləmə flævəʊˈvɪərɛnz / (BrE)
An edible mushroom species with yellow gills and a sticky yellow cap marked by a brownish center.
a yellow-gilled edible mushroom species

Examples

We found several beautiful Tricholoma flavovirens while foraging in the pine forest.
The Tricholoma flavovirens is prized for its unique flavor in French cuisine.
Always be cautious when picking wild mushrooms like Tricholoma flavovirens, as some lookalikes can be toxic.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin: genus 'Tricholoma' (from Greek 'tricho-' meaning hair and 'loma' meaning fringe) and species 'flavovirens' meaning yellow-green in Latin.
