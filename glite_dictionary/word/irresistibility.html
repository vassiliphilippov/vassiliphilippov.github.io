irresistibility

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌrɪzɪstəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ɪˌrɪzɪstəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being impossible to resist or too attractive, powerful, or appealing to be refused
quality of being impossible to resist

Examples

The irresistibility of her smile left everyone charmed and unable to refuse her request.
Advertisers rely on the irresistibility of certain images to capture consumers' attention.
Despite his efforts, he could not deny the irresistibility of the freshly baked bread.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irresistible - impossible to resist
  • Antonym: resistibility - the ability to resist or withstand

Etymology

From irresistible + -ity, modeled after Latin -ibilitas.
