ragout

1. NOUN

/ ræˈɡu / (AmE), / ræˈɡuː / (BrE)
A highly seasoned stew of meat and vegetables, typically cooked slowly to blend the flavors.
well-seasoned meat and vegetable stew

Examples

The chef prepared a delicious ragout with lamb, carrots, and aromatic herbs.
For dinner, we enjoyed a hearty vegetable ragout served over rice.
Her cookbook features a classic French ragout that simmers for hours for maximum flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stew - dish of meat and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid

Etymology

From French 'ragout', from 'ragoûter' (to revive the taste).
