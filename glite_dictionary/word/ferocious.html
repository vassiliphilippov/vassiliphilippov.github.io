ferocious

1. ADJ

/ fəˈroʊʃəs / (AmE), / fəˈrəʊʃəs / (BrE)
Extremely aggressive, violent, or intense; showing fierce and savage energy.
fierce and violent; intense

Examples

The lion gave a ferocious roar that echoed through the jungle.
She launched a ferocious attack on her opponent's arguments during the debate.
A ferocious storm battered the coastline overnight, causing extensive damage.

Synset Examples

a ferocious beating
fierce fighting
a furious battle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fierce - having or displaying an intense or ferocious aggressiveness
  • Antonym: gentle - kind or mild in temperament or behavior; not violent or harsh

Etymology

From Latin ferox, feroc- (fierce, wild).
