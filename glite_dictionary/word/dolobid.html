Dolobid

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊləˌbɪd / (AmE), / ˈdəʊləˌbɪd / (BrE)
A trademarked nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID), used to treat arthritis and other inflammatory conditions.
brand of NSAID for arthritis and inflammation
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Dolobid to help manage the pain in my joints.
After starting Dolobid, she noticed a significant reduction in inflammation.
Some patients report mild stomach discomfort as a side effect of Dolobid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diflunisal - the generic drug sold under the brand name Dolobid
