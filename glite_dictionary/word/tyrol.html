Tyrol

1. PROPN

/ taɪˈroʊl / (AmE), / tɪˈrɒl / (BrE)
a picturesque mountainous region in western Austria and northern Italy, known for its alpine scenery and cultural heritage
region in Austria and northern Italy

Examples

Many travelers visit Tyrol to enjoy skiing and hiking in the Alps.
The villages of Tyrol are famous for their traditional architecture and festivals.
She spent her summer vacation exploring the castles and lakes of Tyrol.

Etymology

From German 'Tirol', the local name of the region.
