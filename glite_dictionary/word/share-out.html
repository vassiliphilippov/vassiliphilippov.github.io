share-out

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɛr aʊt / (AmE), / ˈʃeə aʊt / (BrE)
an act of dividing and distributing something in shares among several people
distribution in shares
british

Examples

The inheritance will undergo a share-out among the siblings next week.
After the project ended, there was a fair share-out of the proceeds.
A share-out of responsibilities was necessary to ensure the team's success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: division - the act or process of dividing something
  • Synonym: apportionment - the action or result of apportioning something
