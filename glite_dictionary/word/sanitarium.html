sanitarium

1. NOUN

/ ˌsænɪˈtɛriəm / (AmE), / ˌsænɪˈtɛːrɪəm / (BrE)
a hospital where people go to recover or receive treatment for chronic diseases, especially one for long-term care
hospital for recovery or chronic disease treatment

Examples

After months in the sanitarium, he finally regained his strength.
The remote mountain sanitarium specialized in treating patients with tuberculosis.
She was sent to a sanitarium to recover from her long illness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sanatorium - a medical facility for long-term illness or recovery
  • Synonym: hospital - an institution providing medical treatment and care

Etymology

From Latin 'sanitarius' (pertaining to health), patterned on 'sanatorium'
