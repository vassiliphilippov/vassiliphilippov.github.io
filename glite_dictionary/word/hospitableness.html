hospitableness

1. NOUN

/ hɑˈspɪtəblnəs / (AmE), / hɒˈspɪtəblnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being friendly and welcoming to guests or strangers.
friendliness and welcoming attitude to guests

Examples

Her hospitableness made everyone feel immediately at home during the party.
The hospitableness of the villagers impressed the foreign visitors.
Despite his wealth, he was most admired for his hospitableness toward strangers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hospitality - friendly reception and treatment of guests or strangers
  • Antonym: inhospitableness - the quality of being unfriendly or unwelcoming to guests

Etymology

From Latin hospitābilis (hospitable) + -ness.
