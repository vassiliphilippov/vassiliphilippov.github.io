silver-blue

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪlvɚˌblu / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəˌbluː / (BrE)
having a light, shiny blue color reminiscent of silver
light, shiny blue color

Examples

The car was painted a gorgeous silver-blue that sparkled in the sun.
Her dress shimmered in a silver-blue hue, catching everyone's eye at the party.
We admired the bird's silver-blue feathers as it perched on the fence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: azure - bright blue color, similar to the sky
  • Synonym: silver-gray - having a color that is silvery with gray tones
