latergram

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪ.tɚˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.təˌɡræm / (BrE)
a photo posted to social media, especially Instagram, some time after it was taken, not immediately
photo posted later on social media
informal

Examples

She finally uploaded a latergram from her vacation in Greece.
Yesterday's sunset was too beautiful not to share, so here's a latergram.
My feed is a mix of real-time shots and latergrams from old trips.

Etymology

Blend of 'later' and 'Instagram'.
