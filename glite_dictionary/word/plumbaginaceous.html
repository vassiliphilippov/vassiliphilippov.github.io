plumbaginaceous

1. ADJ

/ plʌmˌbæɡəˈneɪʃəs / (AmE), / plʌmˌbaɡɪˈneɪʃəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of plants belonging to the family Plumbaginaceae.
of the Plumbaginaceae plant family
medical

Examples

The plumbaginaceous species thrive in salty environments near the coast.
Wildflowers in the plumbaginaceous family are often used in ornamental gardening.
You can identify plumbaginaceous plants by their simple leaves and small clustered flowers.

Etymology

From Modern Latin Plumbaginaceae, the botanical family name, based on 'plumbago' (leadwort).
