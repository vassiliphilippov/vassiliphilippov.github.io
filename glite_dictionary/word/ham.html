Ham

1. PROPN

/ hæm / (AmE), / hæm / (BrE)
In the Old Testament, the second son of Noah, regarded as the ancestor of several peoples.
biblical son of Noah

Examples

According to the Bible, Ham was one of Noah's sons who survived the flood.
The descendants of Ham are mentioned in several ancient genealogies.
Many biblical scholars have explored the story of Ham and his brothers.
