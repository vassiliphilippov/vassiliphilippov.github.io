blissful

1. ADJ

/ ˈblɪs.fəl / (AmE), / ˈblɪs.fəl / (BrE)
extremely happy; showing or feeling complete happiness and contentment
extremely happy and contented

Examples

She had a blissful smile on her face as she walked through the garden.
They spent a blissful week relaxing by the lake, far from their worries.
He remained in blissful ignorance of the chaos unfolding around him.

Synset Examples

blissful young lovers
in blissful ignorance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: happy - feeling or showing pleasure or contentment
  • Synonym: joyful - feeling, expressing, or causing great pleasure and happiness
  • Antonym: miserable - very unhappy or uncomfortable

Etymology

From English 'bliss' + suffix '-ful'.
