uncarved

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkɑrvd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɑːvd / (BrE)
not shaped, decorated, or inscribed by carving; left in its natural or original form.
not shaped or decorated by carving

Examples

The statue was left uncarved, showcasing the raw beauty of the stone.
Our front door is made from a single uncarved slab of oak.
Many of the temple pillars remain uncarved after centuries.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: carved - shaped or decorated by carving
