to circumvolve

1. VERB

/ sɜrkəmˈvɑlv / (AmE), / sɜːkəmˈvɒlv / (BrE)
to cause to revolve or turn around an axis or center
cause to rotate or revolve

Examples

The engineer instructed us to circumvolve the steel rod around its axis slowly.
During the experiment, you must circumvolve the lens until the image comes into clear focus.
To generate electricity, the turbine blades circumvolve at a constant speed.

Synset Examples

Rotate the handle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to rotate - to turn or cause to turn around a central point
  • Synonym: to revolve - to move in a circle on a central axis

Etymology

From Latin circumvolvere, from circum- (around) + volvere (to roll).
