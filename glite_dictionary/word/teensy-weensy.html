teensy-weensy

1. ADJ

/ ˌtinsi ˈwinsi / (AmE), / ˌtiːnsi ˈwiːnsi / (BrE)
Very small; extremely tiny (informal).
very small; extremely tiny (informal)
informal

Examples

She gave me a teensy-weensy piece of cake.
There was a teensy-weensy scratch on the door, barely noticeable.
His handwriting is teensy-weensy, so I can hardly read it.

Synset Examples

a wee tot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiny - very small in size
  • Synonym: minute - extremely small

Etymology

Reduplication of 'teensy,' itself an alteration of 'tiny.'
