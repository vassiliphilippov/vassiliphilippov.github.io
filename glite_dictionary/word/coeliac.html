coeliac

1. ADJ

/ ˈsiːli.æk / (AmE), / ˈsiːli.æk / (BrE)
relating to the abdominal cavity, especially in anatomical or medical contexts.
relating to the abdominal cavity
medical british

Examples

The coeliac artery is a major blood vessel supplying the abdominal organs.
He was diagnosed with a rare condition affecting the coeliac plexus.
Researchers are studying the role of the coeliac lymph nodes in disease spread.

Etymology

From Greek koiliakos (pertaining to the belly), from koilia (belly, cavity).
