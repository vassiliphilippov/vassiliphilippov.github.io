pug-faced

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʌɡ feɪst / (AmE), / ˈpʌɡ feɪst / (BrE)
Having a short, flat, and wrinkled face like that of a pug dog.
having a flat, wrinkled face (like a pug)

Examples

The villain in the story was described as a pug-faced man with a sharp wit.
She always thought her pug-faced puppy was the cutest of them all.
A group of pug-faced statues lined the entrance to the historic building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snub-nosed - having a short, turned-up nose

Etymology

From 'pug', a breed of dog, and 'faced'.
