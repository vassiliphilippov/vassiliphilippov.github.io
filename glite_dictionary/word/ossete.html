Ossete

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɛt / (AmE), / ˈɒsɛt / (BrE)
a northeastern Iranian language spoken principally in the Ossetia region of Russia and Georgia
northeastern Iranian language of Ossetia

Examples

The Ossete language is primarily used by people living in the Caucasus region.
He studied both Russian and Ossete to better understand the area's history.
Linguists consider Ossete an important link to ancient Iranian languages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ossetian - variant name for the Ossete language or a person from Ossetia

Etymology

From Russian Осетин (Osetin), ultimately from Ossetic ир (ir, "Ossetian").
