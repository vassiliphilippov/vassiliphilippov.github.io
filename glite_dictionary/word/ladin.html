Ladin

1. NOUN

/ ləˈdiːn / (AmE), / ləˈdiːn / (BrE)
a Rhaeto-Romance language closely related to Romansh, spoken in southeastern Switzerland
a Rhaeto-Romance language of Switzerland

Examples

The Ladin language has preserved many old features lost in other Romance languages.
Several villages in southeastern Switzerland still actively speak Ladin.
He wrote his dissertation about the phonological system of Ladin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Romansh - a Rhaeto-Romance language spoken in Switzerland

Etymology

From Latin *latinus* (Latin).
