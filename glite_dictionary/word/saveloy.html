saveloy

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæv.ə.lɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈsav.ɪ.lɔɪ / (BrE)
a cooked and highly seasoned red pork sausage, traditionally served hot in certain British and Australian cuisines
a seasoned red pork sausage
british

Examples

The fish and chip shop always sells saveloy with a bright red casing.
He ordered a saveloy and chips for his lunch yesterday.
Is it true that a saveloy tastes best with plenty of mustard?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frankfurter - a cooked smoked sausage of beef or pork; hot dog
  • Synonym: sausage - highly seasoned minced meat shaped into cylindrical form

Etymology

From French *savoie* (Savoie region), possibly via the region's association with cured sausages.
