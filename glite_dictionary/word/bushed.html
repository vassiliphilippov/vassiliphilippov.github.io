bushed

1. ADJ

/ bʊʃt / (AmE), / bʊʃt / (BrE)
Extremely tired; exhausted.
very tired; exhausted
informal

Examples

By the end of the hike, I was completely bushed and ready for bed.
After working a double shift, she came home looking bushed.
He felt so bushed that he fell asleep on the sofa without even eating dinner.

Synset Examples

was all in at the end of the day
so beat I could flop down and go to sleep anywhere
bushed after all that exercise
I'm dead after that long trip

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhausted - very tired; drained of energy
  • Antonym: refreshed - feeling rested and energetic

Etymology

From Australian English, originally referring to being lost or fatigued from traveling in the bush.
