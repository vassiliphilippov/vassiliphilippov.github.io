cookery

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊkəri / (AmE), / ˈkʊkəri / (BrE)
the practice or skill of preparing food by combining, mixing, and heating ingredients
the preparation or study of cooking food

Examples

She enrolled in a cookery class to improve her culinary skills.
Many students find cookery both relaxing and rewarding.
His knowledge of cookery impressed everyone at the dinner party.

Synset Examples

cooking can be a great art
people are needed who have experience in cookery
he left the preparation of meals to his wife

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cooking - the act of preparing food by heating

Etymology

From Middle English cokery, from cook + -ery, after Anglo-Norman cokerye.
