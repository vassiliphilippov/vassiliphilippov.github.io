expansionist

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈspænʃənɪst / (AmE), / ɪkˈspænʃənɪst / (BrE)
Favoring or relating to a policy of territorial or economic expansion.
supporting expansionism

Examples

The country's expansionist policies led to conflicts with its neighbors.
Historians debate whether the emperor was truly expansionist in his ambitions.
Many citizens opposed the regime's expansionist military campaigns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expansionism - policy of expanding a state's territory or influence
  • Antonym: isolationist - favoring a policy of remaining apart from affairs of other groups

Etymology

From 'expansion' + '-ist'.
