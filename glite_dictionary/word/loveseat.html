loveseat

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌvˌsit / (AmE), / ˈlʌvˌsiːt / (BrE)
A small upholstered sofa designed to seat two people.
small sofa for two people

Examples

They bought a new loveseat to fit in their small living room.
She curled up on the loveseat with a good book and a blanket.
The hotel suite included a loveseat and a small coffee table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sofa - a long upholstered seat with a back and arms

Etymology

From 'love' and 'seat', originally referring to a seat for courting couples.
