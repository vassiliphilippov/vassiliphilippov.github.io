Berkshires

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɝːk.ʃɚz / (AmE), / ˈbɜːk.ʃɪəz / (BrE)
a low mountain range and popular resort region in western Massachusetts, known for scenic beauty, cultural events, and outdoor recreation
mountain range and resort region in Massachusetts

Examples

Every summer, many families vacation in the Berkshires to enjoy music festivals and hiking.
The autumn foliage in the Berkshires attracts visitors from all over the world.
She attended a writing retreat nestled in the scenic Berkshires mountains.

Etymology

Named after the English county of Berkshire.
