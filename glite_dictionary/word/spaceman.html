spaceman

1. NOUN

/ ˈspeɪsˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈspeɪsˌmæn / (BrE)
a man who is trained to travel and work in a spacecraft
male astronaut

Examples

The spaceman conducted experiments while orbiting Earth for several months.
Children often dream of becoming a spaceman and traveling to distant planets.
The spaceman waved at the camera before leaving the spacecraft.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astronaut - a person trained to travel in a spacecraft
  • Synonym: cosmonaut - a Russian astronaut
