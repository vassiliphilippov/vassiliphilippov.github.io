panetella

1. NOUN

/ pænəˈtɛlə / (AmE), / panɪˈtelə / (BrE)
a long, thin cigar with straight sides and rounded ends
a long, thin cigar

Examples

He offered me a panetella after dinner as a mark of hospitality.
The store has a wide selection of cigars, including the elegant panetella.
She bought her grandfather a box of his favorite panetellas for his birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cigar - a rolled bundle of dried and fermented tobacco leaves for smoking

Etymology

From Italian 'panatela', referring to its shape resembling a breadstick.
