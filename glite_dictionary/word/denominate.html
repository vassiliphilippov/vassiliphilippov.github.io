to denominate

1. VERB

/ dɪˈnɑmɪˌneɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈnɒmɪneɪt / (BrE)
to assign a specific name or title to someone or something
assign a name or title

Examples

The currency was denominated in euros after the country joined the European Union.
Several streets in the city are denominated after famous writers.
Official reports denominate the region as a protected wildlife area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: name - to give a name to someone or something

Etymology

From Latin denominare (to name), from de- (down, completely) + nominare (to name).
