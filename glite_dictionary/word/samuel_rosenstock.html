Samuel Rosenstock

1. PROPN

/ ˈsæmjuˌɛl ˈroʊzənstɑk / (AmE), / ˈsæmjʊəl ˈrəʊzənstɒk / (BrE)
(1896–1963) A French poet, born in Romania, who was one of the cofounders of the Dada movement; better known by the pseudonym Tristan Tzara.
French-Romanian Dadaist poet, founder of Dada movement

Examples

Samuel Rosenstock was a pivotal figure in early twentieth-century European art movements.
Few people realize that Samuel Rosenstock and Tristan Tzara are actually the same person.
The influence of Samuel Rosenstock on modern poetry cannot be understated.
