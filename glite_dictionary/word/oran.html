Oran

1. PROPN

/ ɔːˈrɑːn / (AmE), / ɔːˈrɑːn / (BrE)
A port city in northwestern Algeria, notable as the country's second largest city and a major Mediterranean port.
port city in northwestern Algeria

Examples

The city of Oran played a significant role during the colonial era.
Many traders pass through Oran due to its busy Mediterranean port.
She traveled from Algiers to Oran by train for her business meeting.
