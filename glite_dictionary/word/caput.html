caput

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪ.pət, ˈkæ.pət / (AmE), / ˈkæ.pʊt, ˈkeɪ.pʊt / (BrE)
A head-shaped protuberance or rounded end of a bone or organ, especially in anatomical contexts.
head-shaped anatomical protuberance
medical

Examples

The caput humeri fits into the glenoid cavity of the scapula.
A fracture at the caput femoris can disrupt blood supply to the hip joint.
During the lecture, the professor pointed to the caput of the radius on the diagram.

Synset Examples

the caput humeri is the head of the humerus which fits into a cavity in the scapula

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head - uppermost part of the body; anatomical 'head'

Etymology

From Latin caput (head).
