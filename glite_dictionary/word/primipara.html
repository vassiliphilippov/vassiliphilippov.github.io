primipara

1. NOUN

/ praɪˈmɪpərə / (AmE), / praɪˈmɪpərə / (BrE)
a woman who has given birth to a child for the first time
woman who has given birth once
medical

Examples

As a primipara, she was given extra support during labor.
The study compared outcomes between primipara and multipara women.
Admission was prioritized for the primipara due to her high-risk status.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: multipara - a woman who has given birth more than once

Etymology

From Latin 'primi-' (first) + 'parere' (to bring forth, give birth).
