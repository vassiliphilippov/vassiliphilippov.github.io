smooth-spoken

1. ADJ

/ ˌsmuðˈspoʊkən / (AmE), / ˌsmuːðˈspəʊkən / (BrE)
(of a person) able to speak in a fluent, persuasive, or charming manner.
speaking fluently and persuasively

Examples

He was so smooth-spoken that everyone quickly trusted his proposals.
The smooth-spoken politician easily swayed the undecided voters during the debate.
Don't let his smooth-spoken compliments fool you; he often says what people want to hear.

Synset Examples

able to dazzle with his facile tongue
silver speech

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silver-tongued - skilled at speaking in a way that persuades people
  • Synonym: facile - (of speech or writing) appearing neat and comprehensive but only by ignoring the true complexities
