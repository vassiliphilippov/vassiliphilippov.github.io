cu ft

1. NOUN

/ kjuː fʊt / (AmE), / kjuː fʊt / (BrE)
An abbreviation for 'cubic foot', a unit of volume equal to that of a cube with sides one foot in length.
abbreviation for cubic foot (unit of volume)

Examples

The refrigerator has a total capacity of 18 cu ft.
One cu ft of water weighs about 62 pounds.
The storage container holds up to 25 cu ft of material.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cubic foot - a unit of volume equal to a cube one foot on each side

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'cubic foot'.
