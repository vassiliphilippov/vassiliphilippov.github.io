magnifico

1. NOUN

/ mæɡˈnɪfɪkoʊ / (AmE), / mæɡˈnɪfɪkəʊ / (BrE)
A person of distinguished rank, importance, or appearance, especially one displaying grandeur or pomp.
person of distinguished rank or grandeur

Examples

The Venetian magnifico entered the hall clad in gold and crimson robes.
Many admired the magnifico for his generosity and stately presence.
The play featured a pompous magnifico as its main antagonist.

Etymology

From Italian 'magnifico' (noble, magnificent), from Latin 'magnificus' (great, noble).
