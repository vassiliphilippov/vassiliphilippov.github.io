wetback

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛtˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈwɛtˌbak / (BrE)
An extremely offensive term for a person of Mexican descent, particularly one who has entered the United States illegally.
offensive slang for a Mexican immigrant
offensive slang american

Examples

The movie was criticized for using the word wetback in its dialogue.
He was appalled when someone referred to his friend as a wetback.
The term wetback is widely regarded as deeply offensive and should not be used.

Etymology

Originally (mid-20th century) referring to Mexican immigrants who entered the US by swimming or wading across the Rio Grande.
