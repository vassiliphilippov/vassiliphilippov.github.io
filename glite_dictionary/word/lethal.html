lethal

1. ADJ

/ ˈliθəl / (AmE), / ˈliːθəl / (BrE)
Able to cause death; deadly or fatal, especially when referring to weapons, substances, or actions.
deadly; capable of causing death

Examples

The suspect was carrying a lethal weapon during the robbery.
Certain chemicals are lethal even in very small doses.
A lethal injection was administered as the method of execution.

Synset Examples

deadly poisons
lethal weapon
a lethal injection

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deadly - able or likely to cause death
  • Antonym: harmless - not able or likely to cause harm or death

Etymology

From Latin letalis (deadly), from letum (death).
