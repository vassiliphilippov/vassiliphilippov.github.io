Pansa

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌnsə / (AmE), / ˈpʌnsə / (BrE)
The tenth month in the Hindu lunar calendar, typically corresponding to parts of December and January in the Gregorian calendar.
tenth month of the Hindu calendar

Examples

The festival falls in Pansa, bringing families together for celebrations.
Many rituals are performed during Pansa according to the Hindu tradition.
The month of Pansa is considered auspicious for certain ceremonies.
