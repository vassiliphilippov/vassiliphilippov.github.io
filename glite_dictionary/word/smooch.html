smooch

1. NOUN

/ smutʃ / (AmE), / smuːtʃ / (BrE)
an enthusiastic or affectionate kiss
enthusiastic kiss
informal

Examples

He gave her a big smooch before leaving for work.
The couple shared a sweet smooch under the mistletoe.
She caught her parents in the kitchen sharing a quick smooch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kiss - touch or caress with the lips as a sign of love, affection, or greeting
