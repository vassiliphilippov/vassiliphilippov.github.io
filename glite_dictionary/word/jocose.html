jocose

1. ADJ

/ dʒoʊˈkoʊs / (AmE), / dʒəˈkəʊs / (BrE)
characterized by joking or playful good humor
playful; jokingly humorous

Examples

The author's jocose tone made the story enjoyable and lighthearted.
He delivered a jocose speech that had the audience laughing throughout the evening.
Despite her jocose remarks, her advice was both wise and sincere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humorous - funny; making people laugh
  • Antonym: serious - solemn, not joking or playful

Etymology

From Latin 'jocosus' (full of jest, joking).
