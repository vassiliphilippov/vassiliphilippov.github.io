classificatory

1. ADJ

/ ˌklæsɪfəˈkɛtɔri / (AmE), / ˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪtəri / (BrE)
relating to or involving the process of classification.
relating to classification

Examples

The researchers developed classificatory criteria for diagnosing the disease.
A classificatory system helps organize species according to shared traits.
Her paper focused on the classificatory distinctions used in linguistics.

Synset Examples

classificatory criteria

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classificational - relating to the process or result of classification

Etymology

From classification + -ory, indicating relating to or serving for.
