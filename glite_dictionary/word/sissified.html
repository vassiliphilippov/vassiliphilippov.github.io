sissified

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪsɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪsɪfaɪd / (BrE)
(of a person or behaviour) having characteristics considered stereotypically feminine, often used in a disparaging way to mean weak or effeminate.
having or showing stereotypically feminine traits
informal offensive

Examples

He was teased at school for his sissified haircut and gentle manners.
The character was portrayed in a sissified way, which some viewers found problematic.
She disliked any sissified decorations and preferred a more rugged style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: effeminate - having or showing characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly
  • Antonym: manly - having qualities traditionally associated with men, such as strength and courage

Etymology

From 'sissy' (slang for an effeminate boy) + '-fied' (variant of '-ified', forming adjectives).
