28

1. NUM

/ ˈtwɛnti eɪt / (AmE), / ˈtwɛnti eɪt / (BrE)
the cardinal number that is the sum of twenty-seven and one; represented as 28.
the number twenty-eight (28)

Examples

Sarah will turn 28 years old next month.
There are 28 days in February in a common year.
The class has exactly 28 students enrolled this semester.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twenty-eight - written form of 28
