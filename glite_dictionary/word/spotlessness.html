spotlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑt.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈspɒt.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being completely clean and free from spots or stains
state of being extremely clean

Examples

Her attention to spotlessness made the kitchen gleam every morning.
The spotlessness of the hotel room impressed all the guests.
He prided himself on the spotlessness of his white sneakers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleanliness - the state of being clean
  • Antonym: dirtiness - the state of being dirty or unclean

Etymology

From 'spotless' (without spots) + '-ness' (forming nouns denoting a quality or state).
