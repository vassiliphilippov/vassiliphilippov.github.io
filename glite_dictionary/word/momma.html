momma

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑmə / (AmE), / ˈmɒmə / (BrE)
An informal term for mother; used especially by children or in affectionate contexts.
informal term for mother
informal

Examples

Even though I'm grown, I still call my momma when I need advice.
Her momma always bakes the best cookies for the holidays.
Little Timmy ran to his momma after he scraped his knee.
