womanhood

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊm.ənˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈwʊm.ən.hʊd / (BrE)
The state or condition of being a woman; the qualities or experiences typical of women.
the state of being a woman

Examples

She felt a strong sense of womanhood as she entered adulthood.
The celebration recognized her transition into womanhood.
Throughout history, womanhood has been associated with various cultural roles and expectations.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: manhood - the state or period of being a man

Etymology

Formed from 'woman' + '-hood', the suffix denoting state or condition.
