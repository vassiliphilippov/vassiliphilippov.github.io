unexpectedness

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌnɪkˈspɛktɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈspɛktɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being unexpected or surprising
state of being unexpected

Examples

The unexpectedness of her visit caught everyone off guard.
He enjoyed the unexpectedness that travel often brings to life.
Sometimes, the unexpectedness of a small kindness can change someone's entire day.

Synset Examples

the unexpectedness of the warm welcome

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surprise - a feeling caused by something unexpected
  • Antonym: predictability - the quality of being expected or anticipated

Etymology

From 'unexpected' plus the noun suffix '-ness'.
