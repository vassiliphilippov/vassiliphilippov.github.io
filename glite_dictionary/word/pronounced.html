pronounced

1. ADJ

/ prəˈnaʊnst / (AmE), / prəˈnaʊnst / (BrE)
Very noticeable or strongly marked; easily perceived or obvious.
very noticeable; easily perceived

Examples

The gap between the two teams became more pronounced as the game went on.
She spoke with a pronounced French accent that was impossible to miss.
There was a pronounced difference in the quality of the fabrics.

Synset Examples

walked with a marked limp
a pronounced flavor of cinnamon

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marked - easy to notice; evident
  • Antonym: subtle - not obvious or easy to notice

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'pronounce', from Old French 'prononcer', from Latin 'pronuntiare'.
