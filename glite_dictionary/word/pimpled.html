pimpled

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɪmpəld / (AmE), / ˈpɪmpəld / (BrE)
(of skin or complexion) marked by small, raised spots or blemishes; covered with pimples.
covered with pimples

Examples

Ever since his teenage years, he has struggled with pimpled skin.
She self-consciously covered her pimpled cheeks with makeup before school.
The advertisement featured a before-and-after photo of a pimpled face that became clear.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clear - (of skin) free from spots or blemishes
  • Synonym: acned - affected with acne; having pimples
