peace-loving

1. ADJ

/ ˈpis ˌlʌvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpiːs ˌlʌvɪŋ / (BrE)
Preferring peace and avoiding conflict or violence.
preferring peace; avoiding conflict

Examples

The new leader promised to be a peace-loving president who values diplomacy over war.
Many peace-loving citizens attended the anti-war demonstration in the city center.
Historically, the region has been known for its peace-loving communities and strong sense of unity.

Synset Examples

they met in a peaceable spirit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peaceable - disposed to avoid conflict; peaceful
  • Antonym: warlike - disposed to war or aggression
