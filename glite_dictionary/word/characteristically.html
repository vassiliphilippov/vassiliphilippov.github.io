characteristically

1. ADV

/ ˌkɛrəktəˈrɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌkarɪktəˈrɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is typical of a particular person, thing, or group; in a manner showing the usual qualities.
in a typical or usual manner

Examples

He characteristically responded with good humor to the criticism.
The politician characteristically avoided giving a direct answer.
She characteristically arrives early to every meeting.

Synset Examples

he arrived characteristically late

Related Terms

  • Synonym: typically - in a way that is usual for a particular person, thing, or group

Etymology

From 'characteristic' + '-ally', modeled after the adjective 'characteristic.'
