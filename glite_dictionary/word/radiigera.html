Radiigera

1. NOUN

/ ˌreɪdiˈɡɛrə / (AmE), / ˌreɪdɪˈdʒɪərə / (BrE)
A genus of fungus in the family Geastraceae, known for species that produce earthstar-like fruiting bodies.
genus of earthstar-like fungi

Examples

The rare fungus Radiigera is sometimes found in arid regions around the world.
Scientists recently discovered a new species within the genus Radiigera.
The unique features of Radiigera help distinguish it from other genera in the Geastraceae family.
