papillate

1. ADJ

/ pəˈpɪleɪt / (AmE), / pəˈpɪleɪt / (BrE)
Having a surface that is covered with or resembles papillae (small, nipple-like projections).
covered with or resembling papillae
medical

Examples

The surface of the tongue appears papillate under a microscope.
Some leaves have a papillate texture due to tiny projections.
The biologist noted the papillate appearance of the cell membrane.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: papilla - small, nipple-like projection

Etymology

From Latin 'papilla' (nipple) + English '-ate'.
