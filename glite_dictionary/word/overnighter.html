overnighter

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊ.vɚˈnaɪ.t̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˌəʊ.vəˈnaɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person who stays somewhere overnight, especially as a guest
one who stays overnight as a guest

Examples

We have an overnighter staying with us this weekend, so the guest room needs to be prepared.
The camp can accommodate up to thirty overnighters during the summer months.
As an overnighter, she appreciated the early breakfast before leaving in the morning.

Etymology

Formed from 'overnight' + '-er' suffix indicating a person involved in the action.

2. NOUN

/ ˌoʊvərˈnaɪtər / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈnaɪtə / (BrE)
a small traveling bag designed to carry clothing and essentials for a one-night stay
small overnight travel bag

Examples

She packed her overnighter with just enough clothes for the weekend trip.
He bought a new overnighter for business travel, small enough to carry on the plane.
The lightweight overnighter fit easily in the overhead compartment.
