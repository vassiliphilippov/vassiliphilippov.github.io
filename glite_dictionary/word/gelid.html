gelid

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɛlɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛlɪd / (BrE)
Extremely cold; icy or frosty.
extremely cold; icy

Examples

A gelid wind swept across the empty fields, making everyone shiver.
They huddled together for warmth in the gelid air of the early morning.

Related Terms

Etymology

From Latin 'gelidus' meaning 'frozen, icy'.
