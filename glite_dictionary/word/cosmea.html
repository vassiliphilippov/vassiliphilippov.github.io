cosmea

1. NOUN

/ kɑzˈmiːə / (AmE), / kɒzˈmiːə / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Cosmos, especially cultivated varieties with radiate, brightly colored flowers and feathery leaves, popular as ornamental annuals.
ornamental plant; Cosmos genus

Examples

Every summer, her garden burst with pink and white cosmea swaying gently in the breeze.
He planted a row of cosmea seeds along the fence for a splash of autumn color.
The delicate petals of the cosmea stood out among the other wildflowers in the field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cosmos - plant of the genus Cosmos, including various ornamental species

Etymology

From New Latin Cosmea, variant of Cosmos, the genus name.
