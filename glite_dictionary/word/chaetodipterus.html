Chaetodipterus

1. PROPN

/ keɪˌtoʊdɪpˈtɛrəs / (AmE), / kiːˌtɒdɪpˈtɛərəs / (BrE)
A genus of marine fish in the family Ephippidae, commonly known as spadefish.
genus of marine spadefish

Examples

The Atlantic spadefish belongs to the genus Chaetodipterus.
Researchers observed several species within Chaetodipterus off the coast of Florida.
Members of Chaetodipterus are noted for their deep, laterally compressed bodies.

Etymology

From Greek 'chaite' (hair, bristle) + 'diphteros' (with two fins).

A genus of fish in the family Ephippidae, which includes species known as spadefish, found in Atlantic coastal waters.
genus of spadefish (Ephippidae)

Researchers identified the new specimen as a member of the genus Chaetodipterus.
