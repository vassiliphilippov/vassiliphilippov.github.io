comical

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑmɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɪkəl / (BrE)
causing laughter, especially because something is unusual, silly, or amusing
causing laughter; amusing

Examples

She wore a comical hat that made everyone at the party laugh.
His comical expressions always cheer up the office on Mondays.
The dog’s attempt to jump onto the couch was surprisingly comical.

Synset Examples

an amusing film with a steady stream of pranks and pratfalls

funny stories that made everybody laugh
a very funny writer
it would have been laughable if it hadn't hurt so much
a mirthful experience
risible courtroom antics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: funny - making you laugh or smile
  • Synonym: amusing - entertaining and funny
  • Antonym: serious - not funny; not intended to make people laugh

Etymology

From comic + -al; ultimately from Latin 'comicus' (comic).
