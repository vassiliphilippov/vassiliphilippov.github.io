funnily

1. ADV

/ ˈfʌnəli / (AmE), / ˈfʌnɪli / (BrE)
In a strange, odd, or peculiar manner.
in a strange or odd manner

Examples

Funnily enough, she arrived just after we had been talking about her.
The picture was hung funnily on the wall, tilted to one side.
He spoke funnily, using words that made little sense.

Related Terms
