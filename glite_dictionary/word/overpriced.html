overpriced

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈpraɪst / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈpraɪst / (BrE)
costing more than it is worth; excessively expensive for its value
costing more than it is worth

Examples

Many people avoid overpriced coffee shops in tourist areas.
The restaurant's food was good, but I thought it was overpriced.
He returned the overpriced jacket after finding a similar one for less.

Synset Examples

overpriced items at resort shops

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: expensive - costing a lot of money
  • Antonym: cheap - costing little money; low price

Etymology

From 'over-' (excessively) + 'priced' (from 'price').
