concept

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑn.sɛpt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.sɛpt / (BrE)
An abstract or general idea formed by considering specific instances or examples.
abstract idea; general notion

Examples

The concept of freedom is central to many philosophical debates.
She struggled to grasp the concept of quantum mechanics at first.
Before starting the project, let's clarify the main concept we want to explore.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: notion - A general understanding or idea about something; similar in meaning to concept.
  • Synonym: idea - A thought or suggestion as to a possible course of action or explanation; closely related to concept.
  • Degree Higher: theory - A system of ideas intended to explain something; a related but typically more developed form of concept.

Etymology

From Latin 'conceptum' (something conceived), from 'concipere' (to conceive).
