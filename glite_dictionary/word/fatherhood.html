fatherhood

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑðɚˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðəhʊd / (BrE)
The state or condition of being a father; the experience or responsibilities of being a father.
the state of being a father

Examples

He embraced fatherhood with enthusiasm and dedication.
Fatherhood has taught him patience and responsibility.
Balancing a career with fatherhood can be challenging for many men.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: motherhood - the state or experience of being a mother

Etymology

Formed from 'father' + '-hood', with '-hood' denoting state or condition.

2. NOUN

/ ˈfɑðɚˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðəhʊd / (BrE)
the state or role of being a father; the quality or responsibilities associated with being a father
state of being a father

Examples

He embraced fatherhood with joy and a sense of responsibility.
The challenges of fatherhood can be both rewarding and demanding.
Many books offer advice on navigating the early years of fatherhood.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: motherhood - the state or role of being a mother

Etymology

From 'father' + '-hood' (Old English *fæder* + *-hād*, state or condition).
