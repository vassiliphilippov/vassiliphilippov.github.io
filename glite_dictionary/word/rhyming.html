rhyming

1. ADJ

/ ˈraɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of words, syllables, or lines) having the same or corresponding sounds, especially at the end.
having corresponding ending sounds

Examples

The poet used several rhyming couplets in her sonnet.
Children love playing games with rhyming words.
He found it difficult to write rhyming lyrics for his song.

Synset Examples

rhymed verse
rhyming words

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rhyme - words with the same ending sounds

Etymology

From the verb 'rhyme' + -ing, meaning 'to have matching sounds, especially at the ends.'
