fives

1. NOUN

/ faɪvz / (AmE), / faɪvz / (BrE)
a ball game resembling handball, played on a court with a front wall and two side walls, in which the ball is struck with the hand.
handball-like court game

Examples

He has played fives competitively since his school days.
Many British public schools still have courts dedicated to fives.
They spent Saturday afternoon playing fives at the local club.

Etymology

The plural likely reflects the use of all five fingers to strike the ball.
