gorgeous

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɔrdʒəs / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːdʒəs / (BrE)
Extremely beautiful or attractive; dazzlingly beautiful.
extremely beautiful or attractive

Examples

She wore a gorgeous dress to the wedding and received many compliments.
The view from the mountain was absolutely gorgeous at sunset.
He brought home a gorgeous bouquet of flowers for her birthday.

Synset Examples

a gorgeous Victorian gown

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beautiful - pleasing the senses or mind aesthetically
  • Synonym: stunning - extremely impressive or attractive
  • Antonym: plain - not decorated or elaborate; simple or ordinary in appearance

Etymology

From Old French 'gorgias' meaning 'elegant, fashionable'.
