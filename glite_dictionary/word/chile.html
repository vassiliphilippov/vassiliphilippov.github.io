Chile

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʃiːleɪ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪleɪ / (BrE)
A country in southern South America, stretching along the Pacific coast and bordered by the Andes mountains.
country in South America

Examples

Traveling through Chile, we admired the stunning landscapes of Patagonia.
The capital of Chile, Santiago, is nestled at the foot of the Andes.
Exports of copper are a major part of Chile's economy.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Chile', of indigenous Mapuche origin.
