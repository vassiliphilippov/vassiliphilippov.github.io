weirdy

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪrdi / (AmE), / ˈwɪə.di / (BrE)
an unpleasantly strange or eccentric person
unpleasantly strange person
informal

Examples

Everyone at the party thought he was a total weirdy because of his odd behavior.
She used to get called a weirdy in school for her unusual fashion sense.
Don't be a weirdy and just join in with the group activity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weirdo - a person who is strange or eccentric
