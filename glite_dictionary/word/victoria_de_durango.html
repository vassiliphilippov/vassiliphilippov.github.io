Victoria de Durango

1. PROPN

/ vɪkˈtɔriə deɪ dʊˈræŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔːriə deɪ djʊˈræŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
A city in north central Mexico that serves as the capital of the state of Durango and is known as a mining center.
city in northern Mexico; capital of Durango state

Examples

The mining industry has played a key role in the economy of Victoria de Durango.
Many tourists visit the historic center of Victoria de Durango each year to admire its colonial architecture.
Victoria de Durango is known for its tranquil parks and vibrant festivals.

Etymology

Named after Guadalupe Victoria, the first president of Mexico, and the state of Durango.
