elasticized

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈlæstəˌsaɪzd / (AmE), / ɪˈlæstɪsʌɪzd / (BrE)
made with elastic strands or inserts, especially to provide stretch or a snug fit
containing elastic material for stretch

Examples

She prefers elasticized waistbands for extra comfort in her pants.
The fitted sheet has elasticized corners that keep it in place on the mattress.
Many modern jackets have elasticized cuffs to block out the wind.

Synset Examples

slacks with an elasticized waistband

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stretchy - able to stretch; elastic

Etymology

From "elastic" + suffix "-ized" meaning made or treated in a specified way.
