Yehudi Menuhin

1. PROPN

/ jəˈhuːdi ˈmɛnhuːɪn / (AmE), / jəˈhuːdi ˈmɛnhuːɪn / (BrE)
(1916–1999) A celebrated British-American violinist and conductor, known for his extraordinary technique, musicality, and humanitarian work.
famous British-American violinist and conductor

Examples

As a child, Yehudi Menuhin astonished audiences with his prodigious musical talent.
Yehudi Menuhin collaborated with many of the world's leading orchestras during his long career.
The recordings of Yehudi Menuhin remain influential among violinists and classical music enthusiasts.
