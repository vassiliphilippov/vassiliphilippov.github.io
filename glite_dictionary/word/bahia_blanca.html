Bahia Blanca

1. PROPN

/ baɪˌiː.ə ˈblæŋ.kə / (AmE), / baɪˌiː.ə ˈblaŋ.kə / (BrE)
a port city in eastern Argentina, located southwest of Buenos Aires on an inlet of the Atlantic Ocean
port city in eastern Argentina

Examples

Many cargo ships dock at Bahia Blanca before continuing their journey along the coast.
She traveled from Buenos Aires to Bahia Blanca to visit the historic port district.
Exports of agricultural goods pass through the busy harbor at Bahia Blanca each year.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Bahía Blanca' meaning 'White Bay'.
