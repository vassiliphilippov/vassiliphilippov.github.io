credenza

1. NOUN

/ krəˈdɛnzə / (AmE), / krɪˈdɛnzə / (BrE)
a long, low piece of furniture, often with cupboards, used for serving food or storing dining room items
a type of sideboard or buffet

Examples

The antique credenza in the dining room is beautifully carved and holds our best china.
She placed the dishes on the credenza before serving dinner to her guests.
They bought a modern credenza to match their new conference room table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sideboard - a piece of furniture for holding dining items

Etymology

From Italian 'credenza' (credence), originally referring to a side table for tasting food.
