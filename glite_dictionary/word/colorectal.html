colorectal

1. ADJ

/ ˌkoʊləˈrɛktəl / (AmE), / ˌkəʊləˈrɛktəl / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the colon and the rectum
relating to the colon and rectum
medical

Examples

The doctor specialized in colorectal surgery to treat his patients with bowel diseases.
Screening for colorectal cancer can help detect problems at an early stage.
Advances in colorectal research have improved the survival rates of many patients.

Synset Examples

most colorectal cancers arise from adenomatous polyps

Etymology

From Latin 'colon' (large intestine) and 'rectum' (straight intestine).
