unventilated

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈvɛntəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈvɛntɪleɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a space or room) not provided with ventilation; lacking fresh air circulation.
not provided with ventilation

Examples

The cellar was dark and unventilated, causing a musty smell to linger.
After hours in the unventilated office, everyone began to feel sleepy.
Sleeping in an unventilated room can be harmful to your health.

Synset Examples

stuffy unventilated rooms

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ventilated - provided with ventilation

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'ventilated' (from Latin ventilare, to fan or agitate).
