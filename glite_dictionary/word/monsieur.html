Monsieur

1. NOUN

/ məˈsjɝː / (AmE), / mɒnˈsjɜː / (BrE)
a French title of respect for a man, equivalent to ‘Mr’ in English
French title for a man; ‘Mr’

Examples

The maître d’ greeted us politely, saying, "Monsieur, your table is ready."
He addressed his letter to Monsieur Duval, following the customary French etiquette.
During our trip to Paris, every waiter respectfully called my father Monsieur.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mr - English title used before a man's name

Etymology

French, from Old French mon sieur (my lord)
