worthwhile

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɝːθˌwaɪl / (AmE), / ˈwɜːθˌwaɪl / (BrE)
sufficiently valuable or important to justify the effort, time, or cost involved
valuable enough to justify effort or time

Examples

Volunteering for the community is a worthwhile activity that benefits everyone.
She felt all the hard work was worthwhile when she saw the final result.
It's not worthwhile to fix the old car if the repairs cost more than a new one.

Synset Examples

a worthwhile book

Related Terms

  • Antonym: worthless - having no value or usefulness
  • Synonym: valuable - worth a lot or having high value

Etymology

Formed from 'worth' + 'while', literally meaning 'worth the while (time)'.
