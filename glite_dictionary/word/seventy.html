seventy

1. NUM

/ ˈsɛv.ən.ti / (AmE), / ˈsɛv.ən.ti / (BrE)
the number 70; ten more than sixty
the number 70

Examples

A total of seventy people attended the meeting last night.
The athlete will celebrate his seventy birthday next week.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sixty - the number 60
  • Degree Higher: eighty - the number 80
