purchaser

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝː.tʃə.sɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.tʃə.sə / (BrE)
a person who buys something, especially goods or property
person who buys something

Examples

The purchaser is responsible for paying any additional taxes on the property.
Please provide a receipt for the purchaser at the checkout counter.
The purchaser of this artwork wishes to remain anonymous.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buyer - someone who buys something
  • Antonym: seller - someone who sells something

Etymology

From Anglo-Norman French 'purchaser', from Old French 'purchacier' (to seek eagerly, procure), from 'pour-' (for) + 'chacier' (to chase).
