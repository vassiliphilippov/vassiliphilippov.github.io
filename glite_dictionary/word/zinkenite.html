zinkenite

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɪŋ.kəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈzɪŋ.kəˌnaɪt / (BrE)
A steel-grey metallic mineral consisting of a sulphide of lead and antimony.
steel-grey metallic lead-antimony mineral

Examples

The sample contained large crystals of zinkenite mixed with quartz.
Miners extracted zinkenite from the old deposit for scientific study.
He classified the shiny grey mineral as zinkenite due to its composition.

Etymology

Named after German mineralogist Johann Karl Ludwig Zinken.
