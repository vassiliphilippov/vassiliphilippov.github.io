soft-spoken

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɔftˈspoʊkən / (AmE), / ˌsɒftˈspəʊkən / (BrE)
speaking in a quiet and gentle voice
having a quiet, gentle speaking manner

Examples

He is a soft-spoken leader who rarely raises his voice.
Despite her soft-spoken nature, she commands respect in every meeting.
The soft-spoken teacher quickly put the anxious students at ease.

Synset Examples

she was always soft-spoken

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loud - speaking or producing sound at a high volume
  • Synonym: gentle - having or showing a mild, kind, or tender temperament or character
