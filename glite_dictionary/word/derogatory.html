derogatory

1. ADJ

/ dəˈrɑɡəˌtɔri / (AmE), / dɪˈrɒɡətri / (BrE)
showing a critical or disrespectful attitude; expressing a low opinion or lack of respect
disparaging; disrespectful

Examples

She was offended by the derogatory remarks made about her work.
The article contained several derogatory statements about the political candidate.
Avoid using derogatory language when discussing sensitive topics.

Synset Examples

derogatory comments
disparaging remarks about the new house

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disparaging - expressing the opinion that something is of little worth

Etymology

From Late Latin derogatorius, from Latin derogare (to detract, diminish).
