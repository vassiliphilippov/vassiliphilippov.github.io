cyclopean

1. ADJ

/ saɪ.kloʊˈpiː.ən / (AmE), / sɪ.kloʊˈpiː.ən / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a Cyclops, especially characterized by having a single large eye or by massive size or structure
relating to or resembling a Cyclops

Examples

The monster in the story had a single cyclopean eye that glowed in the darkness.
A massive, cyclopean boulder blocked the entrance to the cave.
The artist painted a cyclopean face with one staring eye.

Synset Examples

Cyclopean eye

Etymology

From Cyclops (Greek mythology) + -ean, meaning 'of or pertaining to'.
