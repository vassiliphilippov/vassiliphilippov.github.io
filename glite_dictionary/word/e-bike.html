e-bike

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːˌbaɪk / (AmE), / ˈiːˌbaɪk / (BrE)
a bicycle equipped with an electric motor that can assist with or replace pedalling
bicycle with an electric motor

Examples

She bought an e-bike to make her daily commute easier and faster.
Many cities have introduced e-bikes to their bike-sharing programs.
The battery on my e-bike lasts for up to 50 miles per charge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electric bicycle - a bicycle powered by an electric motor

Etymology

Formed from 'electric' and 'bike', a shortening of 'bicycle'.
