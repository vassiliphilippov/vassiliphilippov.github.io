Laredo

1. PROPN

/ ləˈreɪdoʊ / (AmE), / ləˈreɪdəʊ / (BrE)
A city in southern Texas, situated on the north bank of the Rio Grande, known for its role as a major port of entry between the United States and Mexico.
city in southern Texas

Examples

The border city of Laredo attracts visitors from both the United States and Mexico.
She grew up in Laredo, Texas, and still visits her family there every year.
Trade across the bridge at Laredo is essential for the regional economy.

Etymology

Likely from Spanish, named after Laredo in Cantabria, Spain.
