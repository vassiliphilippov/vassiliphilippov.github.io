Argonauts

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrɡəˌnɔts / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡənɔːts / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, the band of heroes who accompanied Jason on his quest to find the Golden Fleece.
heroes who sailed with Jason in mythology

Examples

The epic tale of the Argonauts has captivated readers for centuries.
Each of the Argonauts had unique skills that helped Jason overcome many challenges.
Many ancient works recount the adventures of Jason and the Argonauts in their search for the Golden Fleece.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jason - Leader of the Argonauts in Greek mythology

Etymology

From Greek ‘Argonautēs’, from ‘Argo’, the name of the ship, and ‘nautēs’ meaning sailor.
