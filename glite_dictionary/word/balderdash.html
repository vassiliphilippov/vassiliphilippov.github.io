balderdash

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔːldərˌdæʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɔːldədaʃ / (BrE)
silly or senseless talk; nonsense.
nonsense; silly talk

Examples

Don't believe his story—it's pure balderdash from start to finish.
The politician dismissed the rumors as complete balderdash.
Some people think astrology is scientific, but to me, it's just balderdash.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - words or ideas that have no meaning or make no sense

Etymology

Origin unknown; perhaps from an early sense meaning a muddled mixture.
