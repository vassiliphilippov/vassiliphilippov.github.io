cerebromeningitis

1. NOUN

/ səˌriːbroʊˌmɛnɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs / (AmE), / səˌriːbrəʊˌmɛnɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the brain, spinal cord, and their meninges.
inflammation of brain and spinal cord coverings
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with cerebromeningitis after presenting with severe neurological symptoms.
Early treatment is crucial in cases of cerebromeningitis to prevent lasting brain damage.
Research into vaccines aims to reduce the incidence of cerebromeningitis worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: meningitis - inflammation of the meninges
  • Degree Lower: encephalitis - inflammation of the brain

Etymology

From Latin 'cerebrum' (brain) and 'meningitis' (inflammation of the meninges).
