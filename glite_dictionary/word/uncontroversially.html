uncontroversially

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.kənˌtrɒ.vɚˈʃi.ə.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kənˌtrɒ.vəˈʃɪ.əli / (BrE)
In a manner that does not involve or cause controversy.
without causing controversy

Examples

The proposal passed uncontroversially, with unanimous support from all parties involved.
She was uncontroversially appointed to the role, as no one opposed her candidacy.
Most of his research findings were accepted uncontroversially within the scientific community.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: controversially - in a manner likely to cause public disagreement

Etymology

Formed from 'uncontroversial' with the suffix '-ly'.
