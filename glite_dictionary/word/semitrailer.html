semitrailer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsem.iˌtreɪ.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈsem.iˌtreɪ.lə / (BrE)
A trailer with wheels only at the rear, the front being supported by the vehicle that pulls it.
trailer with rear wheels only, front supported by towing vehicle

Examples

The truck driver expertly backed the semitrailer into the loading dock.
Federal regulations set standards for the length and weight of a semitrailer.
After unloading, the semitrailer was detached from the tractor unit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trailer - a vehicle drawn by another, often with wheels at both ends

Etymology

Formed from 'semi-' (half, partly) + 'trailer', indicating a partly self-supporting trailer.
