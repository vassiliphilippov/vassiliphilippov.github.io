arthropodous

1. ADJ

/ ɑːrˈθrɑːpədəs / (AmE), / ɑːˈθrɒpədəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of invertebrates of the phylum Arthropoda, such as insects, arachnids, and crustaceans.
relating to arthropods (invertebrates like insects, spiders)
medical

Examples

The exoskeleton is a distinctive feature of arthropodous animals.
Researchers observed several arthropodous species under the microscope.
The arthropodous structure of the specimen helped to classify it as an insect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arthropod - invertebrate animal of the phylum Arthropoda

Etymology

From New Latin Arthropoda (the phylum name) + English -ous.
