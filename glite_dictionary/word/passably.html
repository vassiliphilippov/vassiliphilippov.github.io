passably

1. ADV

/ ˈpæsəbli / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsəbli / (BrE)
to a moderate or acceptable degree; fairly well
to a moderate or acceptable degree

Examples

The hotel was passably clean, though not spotless.
He speaks French passably, enough to get by when traveling.
Her performance was passably good, but it lacked passion.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fairly - to a moderate extent; moderately
  • Synonym: reasonably - to a satisfactory or acceptable degree
  • Degree Higher: excellently - in an outstanding or extremely good way
  • Degree Lower: poorly - in a bad or inferior way

Etymology

From 'passable' + '-ly', with 'passable' from Late Latin 'passabilis' (that may be crossed).
