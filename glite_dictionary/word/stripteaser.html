stripteaser

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɪpˌtizər / (AmE), / ˈstrɪpˌtiːzə / (BrE)
a performer, typically in a nightclub or theater, who provides erotic entertainment by gradually undressing to music
performer who undresses erotically to music

Examples

The stripteaser captivated the audience with her elaborate routine.
During the 1950s, famous stripteasers performed in popular nightclubs across the country.
He didn't expect to see a professional stripteaser at the private event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stripper - a person who removes clothing as entertainment

Etymology

From 'striptease' (act of erotic dancing) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
