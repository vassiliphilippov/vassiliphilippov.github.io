crottal

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɑːtəl / (AmE), / ˈkrɒtəl / (BrE)
a lichen of the genus Parmelia, used especially in making reddish brown or purple dyes
Parmelia lichen used for dye

Examples

Traditional Scottish weavers often used crottal to create natural, lasting dyes for wool.
The hillside was covered in crottal, which the villagers collected for dyeing cloth.
Scientists identified the purple pigment in the sample as coming from crottal.
