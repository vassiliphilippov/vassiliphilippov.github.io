Tuxtla Gutierrez

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʊkstlə ɡuːˈtjɛrɛz / (AmE), / ˈtʊkstlə ɡɪˈtɛrɪɛz / (BrE)
the capital city of the state of Chiapas in southeastern Mexico
city; capital of Chiapas, Mexico

Examples

She traveled to Tuxtla Gutierrez to experience local culture and cuisine.
Flights to Tuxtla Gutierrez depart daily from Mexico City.
The zoo in Tuxtla Gutierrez is known for its collection of native animals.

Etymology

From Nahuatl ‘toxtla’ (place of rabbits) and Spanish ‘Gutiérrez’, after a 19th-century Mexican governor.
