Apocynum pumilum

1. NOUN

/ əˈpɑːsɪnəm ˈpjuːmɪləm / (AmE), / əˈpɒsɪnəm ˈpjuːmɪləm / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant similar to the common dogbane, belonging to the dogbane family (Apocynaceae).
North American plant, related to dogbane

Examples

Apocynum pumilum is often found in sandy soils across parts of North America.
Researchers studied the habitat preferences of Apocynum pumilum and its relation to other dogbane species.
Gardeners sometimes confuse Apocynum pumilum with the more common dogbane due to their similar appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dogbane - a common name for plants in the genus Apocynum, especially Apocynum cannabinum

Etymology

From Latin: 'Apocynum' (dogbane) + 'pumilum' (dwarf, small).
