homo

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊməʊ / (BrE)
An offensive and slang term for a homosexual person; someone who is sexually attracted to persons of the same sex.
offensive slang for homosexual person
slang offensive

Examples

The word homo is considered offensive and should be avoided.
Some people have reclaimed homo within the LGBTQ+ community, but it remains controversial.
Reports of bullying included name-calling such as homo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homosexual - a person attracted to members of the same sex
  • Antonym: heterosexual - a person attracted to members of the opposite sex

Etymology

Shortened form of 'homosexual'.
