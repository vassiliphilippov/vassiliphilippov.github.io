chapleted

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃæplɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈtʃæplɪtɪd / (BrE)
adorned with or provided with a chaplet (a garland or wreath, especially worn on the head).
adorned with a chaplet

Examples

The statue was chapleted with fresh flowers each morning by the villagers.
She entered the hall, chapleted in laurel as a symbol of her triumph.
In the old painting, a youth chapleted in wild roses gazed into the distance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garlanded - adorned with a garland
  • Synonym: crowned - adorned with a crown

Etymology

From chaplet (a garland or wreath) + -ed (past participle/adjectival suffix).
