meatball

1. NOUN

/ ˈmitˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈmiːtbɔːl / (BrE)
A small ball of ground meat, usually mixed with other ingredients and cooked by frying, baking, or simmering in sauce or broth.
ball of ground meat, cooked

Examples

We made homemade meatballs and served them with spaghetti for dinner.
He added the spicy meatballs to the simmering tomato sauce.
Each meatball was perfectly browned and full of flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meatloaf - a baked dish of ground meat shaped into a loaf
