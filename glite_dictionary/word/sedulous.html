sedulous

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛdʒʊləs / (AmE), / ˈsɛdjʊləs / (BrE)
showing great care and persistent effort; diligent and industrious.
showing diligent and persistent effort

Examples

She was a sedulous student, always completing her assignments ahead of time.
Through sedulous effort, the team achieved their ambitious goals.
His sedulous attention to detail made him an excellent editor.

Synset Examples

her assiduous attempts to learn French
assiduous research
sedulous pursuit of legal and moral principles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assiduous - showing great care and perseverance
  • Synonym: diligent - having or showing care and conscientiousness in one's work or duties

Etymology

From Latin 'sedulus' (diligent, industrious).
