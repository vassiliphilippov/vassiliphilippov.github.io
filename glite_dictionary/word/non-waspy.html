non-WASPy

1. ADJ

/ nɑn ˈwɑspi / (AmE), / nɒn ˈwɒspi / (BrE)
Not characteristic of White Anglo-Saxon Protestants; relating to cultural identity or practices outside traditional WASP norms.
not characteristic of WASPs; outside WASP norms
informal

Examples

The neighborhood is known for its non-WASPy mix of restaurants and shops.
He felt more comfortable in circles that were distinctly non-WASPy.
Her family’s non-WASPy traditions gave her a unique outlook on life.

Synset Examples

The festival highlighted its non-WASPy culture through music and food.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: WASPy - characteristic of White Anglo-Saxon Protestants; resembling WASP norms

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'non-' to 'WASPy', itself derived from 'WASP' (White Anglo-Saxon Protestant).
