mecca

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛkə / (AmE), / ˈmɛkə / (BrE)
A place that attracts many people who share a particular interest.
place attracting many visitors

Examples

The city has become a mecca for food lovers from around the world.
Silicon Valley is a mecca for tech entrepreneurs and investors.
Every year, the small town transforms into a mecca for music festival fans.

Synset Examples

New York is a mecca for young artists

Etymology

From Mecca, the holy city in Saudi Arabia, a destination for Muslim pilgrims.
