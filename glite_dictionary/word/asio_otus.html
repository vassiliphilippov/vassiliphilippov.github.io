Asio otus

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪsi.oʊ ˈoʊtəs / (AmE), / ˈæsɪəʊ ˈəʊtəs / (BrE)
A slender European owl with long ear tufts, found mainly in coniferous forests; also known as the long-eared owl.
species of owl with long ear tufts

Examples

The Asio otus is often seen roosting in dense spruce trees during winter.
Naturalists observed several Asio otus hunting quietly at dusk in the forest.
Due to habitat loss, Asio otus populations are under careful study across Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: long-eared owl - common name for Asio otus, a species of owl

Etymology

From Latin: 'Asio' (a kind of eared owl), 'otus' (large-eared).
