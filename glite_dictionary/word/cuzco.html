Cuzco

1. PROPN

/ ˈkuːzkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkuːzkəʊ / (BrE)
A city in the Andes in southern Peru, formerly the capital of the Inca Empire.
city in Peru; former Inca capital

Examples

Many tourists visit Cuzco before exploring Machu Picchu.
The ancient streets of Cuzco are filled with colonial architecture.
The festival in Cuzco attracted travelers from around the world.

Etymology

From Quechua 'Qusqu', name of the city.
