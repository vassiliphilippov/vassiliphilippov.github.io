vermicelli

1. NOUN

/ ˌvɜːrməˈtʃɛli / (AmE), / ˌvɜːmɪˈtʃɛli / (BrE)
a type of pasta formed into long, thin strings, thinner than spaghetti
thin pasta, thinner than spaghetti

Examples

She prepared a delicious soup with vermicelli and vegetables.
The recipe calls for vermicelli instead of regular spaghetti noodles.
After boiling the vermicelli, drain and toss it with olive oil and parsley.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: spaghetti - long, thin pasta thicker than vermicelli
  • Degree Lower: capellini - very thin pasta, thinner than vermicelli

Etymology

From Italian 'vermicelli', plural of 'vermicello' (little worm), diminutive of 'verme' (worm), from Latin 'vermis'.
