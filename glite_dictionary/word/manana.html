manana

1. NOUN

/ məˈnænə / (AmE), / məˈnɑːnə / (BrE)
an unspecified or indefinite time in the future, often implying a relaxed or procrastinatory attitude
an indefinite future time

Examples

He said he'd finish the project manana, but he still hasn't started.
In that town, everything seems to get done manana rather than today.
Whenever we ask when it will be ready, the answer is always manana.

Etymology

From Spanish 'mañana' meaning 'tomorrow'. Adopted into English for its cultural nuance.
