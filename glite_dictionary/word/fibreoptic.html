fibreoptic

1. ADJ

/ ˌfaɪbɚˈɑptɪk / (AmE), / ˌfaɪbəˈɒptɪk / (BrE)
relating to or using fiber optics, especially as a means of transmitting light or signals through thin, flexible fibers of glass or plastic
relating to fiber optics technology
british

Examples

The hospital installed a new fibreoptic cable network to improve data transmission speeds.
A fibreoptic camera allows surgeons to view inside the body during minimally invasive procedures.
The company offers fibreoptic broadband services to homes and businesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fiberoptic - relating to fiber optics (American spelling)

Etymology

From 'fibre' (British spelling of 'fiber') + 'optic', referring to the use of optical fibers.
