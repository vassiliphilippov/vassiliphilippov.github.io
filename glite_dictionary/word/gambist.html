gambist

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæmbɪst / (AmE), / ˈɡambɪst / (BrE)
A musician who plays the viola da gamba.
player of the viola da gamba

Examples

The renowned gambist captivated the audience with a solo performance on her historic instrument.
As a professional gambist, he specializes in early music ensembles.
Many Baroque composers wrote pieces specifically for the gambist in their courts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: violist - a musician who plays the viola

Etymology

From Italian 'gamba' (leg), referring to the shape of the viola da gamba, with '-ist' denoting a performer.
