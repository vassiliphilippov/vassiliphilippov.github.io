unsympathetically

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnˌsɪmpəˈθɛtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌsɪmpəˈθɛtɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of sympathy or compassion.
without sympathy; in an unsympathetic way

Examples

The manager reacted unsympathetically to the staff’s concerns about workload.
She listened unsympathetically to his explanation, unwilling to consider his point of view.
The proposal was rejected unsympathetically by the committee, who offered no encouragement.

Synset Examples

the judge listened to the accused unsympathetically

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sympathetically - in a sympathetic manner; showing sympathy
