postnuptial

1. ADJ

/ ˌpoʊstˈnʌp.tʃu.əl / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈnʌp.tʃu.əl / (BrE)
Occurring or existing after marriage.
after marriage

Examples

The couple signed a postnuptial agreement to clarify their financial arrangements.
Many changes took place in their relationship during the postnuptial period.
The postnuptial celebrations lasted for several days after the wedding.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: prenuptial - occurring or made before marriage

Etymology

From Latin 'post' (after) + 'nuptialis' (of marriage).
