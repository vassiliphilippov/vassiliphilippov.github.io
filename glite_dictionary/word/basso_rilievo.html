basso rilievo

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɑːsoʊ rɪˈljɛvoʊ / (AmE), / ˌbæsəʊ rɪˈljɛvəʊ / (BrE)
a type of sculptural relief in which the forms project only slightly from the background and none are completely undercut
shallow sculptural relief style

Examples

The museum displayed a beautiful basso rilievo depicting ancient mythological scenes.
In basso rilievo, the figures barely stand out from the background, creating subtle depth.
The artist carefully carved the marble to achieve the delicate effect of basso rilievo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bas-relief - sculptural relief with shallow depth; the form is only slightly raised from the background

Etymology

From Italian 'basso rilievo' meaning 'low relief'.
