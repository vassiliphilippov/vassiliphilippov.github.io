climatically

1. ADV

/ klaɪˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / klaɪˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
In relation to climate or climatic conditions.
with respect to climate

Examples

The two regions are climatically similar, despite being thousands of miles apart.
This plant can only grow in areas that are climatically suitable.
Their crops failed because the area is climatically unsuited for agriculture.

Synset Examples

they were used to a climatically different environment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: climatic - relating to climate

Etymology

From 'climatic' + '-ally', meaning pertaining to climate.
