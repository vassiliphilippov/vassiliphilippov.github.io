sandstorm

1. NOUN

/ ˈsændˌstɔrm / (AmE), / ˈsændˌstɔːm / (BrE)
A windstorm that blows clouds of sand through the air, especially in a desert.
windstorm with blowing sand

Examples

A sudden sandstorm forced the hikers to seek shelter behind a large rock.
Farmers struggled to protect their crops when a severe sandstorm swept across the fields.
The city was blanketed in dust after last night's unexpected sandstorm.

Synset Examples

it was the kind of duster not experienced in years

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dust storm - a windstorm that lifts up clouds of dust
