bilsted

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪl.stɛd / (AmE), / ˈbɪl.stɛd / (BrE)
A North American deciduous tree (Liquidambar styraciflua) of the genus Liquidambar, noted for its prickly spherical fruit clusters and fragrant sap; also known as sweetgum.
a North American tree with prickly fruit clusters

Examples

The bilsted is admired for its brilliant fall foliage and unique fruit clusters.
Farmers once collected the fragrant sap from the bilsted to make chewing gum.
Among the hardwoods in this region, the bilsted stands out with its star-shaped leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweetgum - Another common name for Liquidambar styraciflua
