hothouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːtˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈhɒtˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A heated greenhouse, often with plants arranged decoratively, used for growing tropical or delicate plants.
heated greenhouse with decorative plants

Examples

The rare orchids were kept in a hothouse to protect them from the cold.
She spent every morning tending to the plants in the hothouse at the botanical garden.
The hothouse was filled with colorful flowers arranged in artistic displays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greenhouse - a glass building in which plants are grown

Etymology

From 'hot' + 'house', referring to a heated enclosed space for plants.
