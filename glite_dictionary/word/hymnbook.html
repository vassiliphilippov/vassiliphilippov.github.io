hymnbook

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪmˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈhɪm.bʊk / (BrE)
A book containing a collection of hymns, usually for use in religious worship.
book of hymns for worship

Examples

The church distributed a new hymnbook to each member of the congregation.
She found her favorite song in the old hymnbook on the piano.
Many families keep a hymnbook at home for private devotion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hymnal - a book of hymns; sometimes used interchangeably with hymnbook
