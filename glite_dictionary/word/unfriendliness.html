unfriendliness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈfrɛndlɪnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈfrɛndlɪnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being unfriendly; lack of friendliness or warmth.
lack of friendliness; hostile quality

Examples

There was a sense of unfriendliness in her voice when she spoke to him.
The unfriendliness of the staff made customers feel unwelcome.
Despite his unfriendliness, she tried to start a conversation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly
  • Synonym: hostility - hostile or antagonistic attitude

Etymology

Formed from 'unfriendly' + -ness.

2. NOUN

/ ʌnˈfrɛndl̩inəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈfrɛndlɪnɪs / (BrE)
a lack of friendliness; a cold, hostile, or unsympathetic attitude or manner
absence of friendliness

Examples

She was surprised by the unfriendliness of the receptionist at the front desk.
Despite his unfriendliness, his colleagues still tried to include him in conversations.
The dog's unfriendliness made it difficult for new visitors to approach.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly
  • Synonym: hostility - hostile or antagonistic behavior or attitude

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' and 'friendliness'.
