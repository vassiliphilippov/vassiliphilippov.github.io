exerciser

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛksərˌsaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈɛksəsaɪzə / (BrE)
a piece of sports equipment used for gymnastic or fitness exercises
equipment used for exercise

Examples

The new gym has every type of exerciser imaginable, from treadmills to rowing machines.
She spends an hour each day on the elliptical exerciser to stay fit.
After injuring his leg, he bought a small pedal exerciser for physical therapy at home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fitness machine - equipment designed for physical exercise
