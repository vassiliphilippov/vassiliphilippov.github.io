boating

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of traveling or riding in boats for pleasure
traveling in boats for pleasure

Examples

We spent the afternoon boating on the lake and enjoying the sunshine.
Her family goes boating every summer during their vacation.
The park offers several activities, including fishing and boating.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sailing - the sport or activity of traveling in a boat with sails
  • Synonym: rowing - the activity of propelling a boat using oars

Etymology

From 'boat' + '-ing', denoting the action or activity.
