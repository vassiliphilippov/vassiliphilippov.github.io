painkiller

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪnˌkɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪnˌkɪlə / (BrE)
A medicine that is used to relieve or reduce pain.
medicine for relieving pain

Examples

If the headache persists, take a painkiller as directed.
She always keeps a bottle of painkillers in her bag for emergencies.
After his surgery, the doctor prescribed a strong painkiller to manage his discomfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: analgesic - a drug that relieves pain
  • Synonym: pain reliever - another term for a painkiller

Etymology

Formed from 'pain' + 'killer', meaning something that kills pain.
