pusillanimous

1. ADJ

/ ˌpjuːsəˈlænəməs / (AmE), / ˌpjuːsɪˈlænɪməs / (BrE)
lacking courage or determination; timid and easily frightened
lacking courage; timid

Examples

His pusillanimous refusal to stand up for his beliefs disappointed his friends.
The government was criticized as pusillanimous for not taking stronger action.
Unable to make a decision, she was often called pusillanimous by her colleagues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence; easily frightened
  • Synonym: cowardly - lacking courage; very fearful or timid
  • Antonym: brave - showing courage or determination

Etymology

From Latin pusillanimis, from pusillus (very small) + animus (mind, spirit).
