onetime

1. ADJ

/ ˈwʌn.taɪm / (AmE), / ˈwʌn.taɪm / (BrE)
formerly holding a particular position or status; belonging to a previous period.
former; previous

Examples

She sought advice from her onetime mentor when considering a career change.
The onetime world champion now coaches younger athletes.
Their onetime residence has since been renovated into luxury apartments.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: former - having previously filled a particular role or position
  • Synonym: erstwhile - former; of an earlier time
