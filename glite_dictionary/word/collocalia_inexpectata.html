Collocalia inexpectata

1. NOUN

/ kəˌloʊkəliə ɪnˌɛksˈpɛktətə / (AmE), / kɒˌlɒkəliə ɪnˌɛksˈpɛktətə / (BrE)
A species of swift native to eastern Asia, known for producing the edible bird's nest used in certain cuisines.
a swift species of eastern Asia producing edible nests

Examples

The nests built by Collocalia inexpectata are highly prized in gourmet cooking.
Researchers tracked the migration patterns of Collocalia inexpectata across Southeast Asia.
Local collectors carefully harvest the nests of Collocalia inexpectata for use in bird's nest soup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: edible-nest swiftlet - swift species producing edible nests; alternative common name
