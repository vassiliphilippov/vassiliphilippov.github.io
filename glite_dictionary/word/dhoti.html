dhoti

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊti / (AmE), / ˈdəʊti / (BrE)
a traditional long piece of cloth, worn wrapped around the waist and legs, by men in South Asia, especially in India and among Hindus
traditional South Asian men's garment

Examples

He wore a crisp white dhoti to the religious ceremony.
Many Indian politicians are often seen in a kurta and dhoti.
The dhoti is traditionally tied in a specific way, depending on the region.

Etymology

From Hindi धोटी (dhōṭī), from Sanskrit धोती (dhoti), meaning 'washed cloth'.
