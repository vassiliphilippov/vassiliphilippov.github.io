Buckeye

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌkˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈbʌk.aɪ / (BrE)
a native or resident of the state of Ohio in the United States.
person from Ohio, U.S.

Examples

Her father was a proud Buckeye who had lived in Ohio his whole life.
The university's football team is known as the Buckeyes.
Although he moved away years ago, he still considers himself a true Buckeye.

Etymology

Originally from the nickname of the Ohio buckeye tree (Aesculus glabra), symbolic of Ohio.
