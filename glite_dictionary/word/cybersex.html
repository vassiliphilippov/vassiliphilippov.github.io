cybersex

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪbərˌsɛks / (AmE), / ˈsaɪbəˌsɛks / (BrE)
Sexual activity or arousal involving online communication, typically through chat, video, or other internet platforms.
online sexual activity or arousal

Examples

Many couples engage in cybersex when they are in long-distance relationships.
He was caught having cybersex with strangers in online chat rooms.
The therapist discussed the implications of frequent cybersex on romantic partnerships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexting - sending sexually explicit messages or images, typically via mobile phone
  • Degree Lower: sex - sexual intercourse or sexual activity

Etymology

Blend of 'cyber-' (relating to computers or the internet) and 'sex'.
