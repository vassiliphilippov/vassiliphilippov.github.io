thrillful

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɹɪlfʊl / (AmE), / ˈθɹɪlfʊl / (BrE)
Full of excitement or intense pleasure; thrilled.
full of excitement; thrilled

Examples

The children were thrillful as they waited for the fireworks to start.
Her thrillful expression showed just how much she enjoyed the ride.
It was a thrillful experience to meet her favorite author in person.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thrilled - very excited or pleased
  • Antonym: bored - feeling uninterested or weary

Etymology

Formed by combining 'thrill' + '-ful' (full of).
