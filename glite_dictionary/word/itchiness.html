itchiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪtʃ.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɪtʃ.i.nəs / (BrE)
the uncomfortable sensation on the skin that causes a desire to scratch
uncomfortable, itchy skin sensation

Examples

The itchiness from the mosquito bites kept her awake all night.
Dry skin during winter can cause intense itchiness for many people.
He applied lotion to reduce the itchiness after swimming in the pool.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: itch - an uncomfortable skin sensation that causes a desire to scratch

Etymology

From 'itch' + '-iness', denoting the state or quality of itching.
