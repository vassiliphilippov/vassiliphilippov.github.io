limitless

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪmɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪtlɪs / (BrE)
without any limit in extent, size, or quantity; boundless
without limits; boundless

Examples

The possibilities before us seemed limitless and exciting.
Her limitless curiosity drove her to learn about many different subjects.
Space appears vast and limitless when you gaze up at the night sky.

Synset Examples

limitless vastness of our solar system
The long unmeasured pulse of time moves everything. There is nothing hidden that it cannot bring to light, nothing once known that may not become unknown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infinite - without end or limit; limitless
  • Antonym: limited - restricted in size, amount, or extent
  • Synonym: boundless - having no boundaries; unlimited

Etymology

From 'limit' + suffix '-less', meaning 'without limit'.

3. ADJ

/ ˈlɪmɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪtləs / (BrE)
without any end, limit, or boundary; having no restrictions on quantity, range, or extent
without limits; boundless

Examples

She gazed at the limitless expanse of the ocean, feeling humbled by its size.
Human imagination is often described as limitless in its capacity to create new worlds.
The company offered limitless opportunities for employees willing to work hard.

Synset Examples

to start with a theory of unlimited freedom is to end up with unlimited despotism
the limitless reaches of outer space

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boundless - without limits or boundaries
  • Synonym: infinite - having no limits or end
  • Antonym: restricted - having boundaries or limitations

Etymology

From 'limit' (from Latin 'līmitis', meaning 'boundary') + '-less' (a suffix meaning 'without').
