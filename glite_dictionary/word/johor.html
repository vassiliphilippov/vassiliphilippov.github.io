Johor

1. PROPN

/ dʒoʊˈhɔr / (AmE), / dʒəʊˈhɔː / (BrE)
A state in southern Malaysia, located on the Malay Peninsula, and formerly a sultanate.
state in southern Malaysia

Examples

Johor is known for its beautiful coastlines and historic buildings.
The capital of Johor is Johor Bahru, located near the border with Singapore.
Many tourists visit Johor for its theme parks and natural attractions.

2. PROPN

/ dʒoʊˈhɔr / (AmE), / dʒəʊˈhɔː / (BrE)
a sultanate and one of the 13 states in the Federation of Malaysia, located in the southern part of Peninsular Malaysia
state in southern Malaysia

Examples

The city of Johor Bahru is the capital of Johor.
Many tourists visit Johor for its beautiful beaches and rainforests.
Trade between Johor and Singapore has flourished for decades.

Etymology

From Malay Johor, derived from Arabic *jauhar* meaning 'jewel'.
