Gustavus III

1. PROPN

/ ɡʊˈstɑːvəs ðə ˈθɜrd / (AmE), / ɡʊˈstɑːvəs ðə ˈθɜːd / (BrE)
(1746–1792) King of Sweden known for strengthening royal authority and leading an unpopular war against Russia.
King of Sweden (1746–1792)

Examples

Gustavus III introduced important reforms that expanded the powers of the Swedish monarchy.
The war against Russia initiated by Gustavus III was deeply unpopular among his subjects.
Historians often debate the legacy of Gustavus III in the context of Swedish political history.

Etymology

Latinized form of the Swedish name Gustav; 'III' denotes his position as the third king of Sweden with this name.
