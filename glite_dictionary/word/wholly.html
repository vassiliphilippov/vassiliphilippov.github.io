wholly

1. ADV

/ ˈhoʊli / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli / (BrE)
completely; in every respect or to the full extent; entirely.
completely; entirely

Examples

She was wholly unaware of the changes made to her schedule.
The committee wholly supports the proposed legislation.
His explanation was wholly inadequate for the situation.
