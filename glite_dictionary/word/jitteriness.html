jitteriness

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪt̬ɚi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪtəri.nəs / (BrE)
A feeling of nervousness or anxiety, often accompanied by physical restlessness.
nervous or anxious feeling

Examples

She felt a wave of jitteriness before stepping onto the stage for her big speech.
After drinking too much coffee, a sense of jitteriness made it hard for him to concentrate.
His jitteriness was obvious as he tapped his foot and glanced nervously around the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nervousness - a state of being anxious or worried
  • Antonym: calmness - the state of being relaxed and not nervous
  • Synonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry or unease

Etymology

From 'jitter', probably of imitative origin, plus the suffix '-iness'.
