BSArch

1. NOUN

/ bi ɛs ɑr k / (AmE), / biː ɛs ɑːk / (BrE)
Bachelor of Science degree in Architecture; an undergraduate academic degree in the field of architecture.
bachelor's degree in architecture

Examples

She completed her BSArch before applying to graduate school in design.
The university offers a five-year BSArch program with a focus on sustainable construction.
To become a licensed architect, earning a BSArch is often the first step.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: BArch - Bachelor of Architecture, a similar undergraduate degree in architecture

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'Bachelor of Science in Architecture.'
