Aotus trivirgatus

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪətəs traɪˈvɜrgətəs / (AmE), / ˈeɪətəs traɪˈvɜːɡətəs / (BrE)
A species of nocturnal monkey native to Central and South America, noted for its large eyes and thick fur.
species of nocturnal monkey

Examples

The Aotus trivirgatus is often called the night monkey due to its nocturnal habits.
Researchers studied the sleeping patterns of Aotus trivirgatus in the Amazon rainforest.
Large eyes help Aotus trivirgatus see in the dark tropical forests where it lives.

Etymology

From Latin, genus name 'Aotus' (without ears) and species epithet 'trivirgatus' (three-striped).
