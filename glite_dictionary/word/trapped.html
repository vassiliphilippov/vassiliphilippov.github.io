trapped

1. ADJ

/ træpt / (AmE), / træpt / (BrE)
Unable to escape from a difficult or threatening situation, especially when forced to face an attacker or danger.
unable to escape from danger or threat

Examples

The prey felt trapped as the hunters closed in from all sides.
She looked like a trapped animal, unable to find a way out.
During the heated argument, he stood trapped in the corner, with everyone demanding answers.

Synset Examples

a stag at bay
she had me cornered between the porch and her car
like a trapped animal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cornered - Trapped with no way to escape, often by a pursuer or attacker
  • Synonym: at bay - Kept in a position where escape is impossible, especially by an enemy
