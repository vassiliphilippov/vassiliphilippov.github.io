Belem

1. PROPN

/ bəˈlɛm / (AmE), / bəˈlɛm / (BrE)
a port city in northern Brazil, located in the Amazon delta, and serving as the main port and commercial center for the Amazon River basin
port city in northern Brazil

Examples

Belem is known for its vibrant markets and colonial architecture.
Cargo ships regularly depart from Belem to destinations throughout the Amazon region.
We stopped in Belem before heading further into the rainforest.

Etymology

Portuguese spelling of 'Bethlehem', from Latin 'Bethlehem' via Greek and Hebrew origins.
