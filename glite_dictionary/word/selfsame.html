selfsame

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛlfˌseɪm / (AmE), / ˈsɛlfseɪm / (BrE)
being exactly the same person or thing; not another; identical.
exactly the same one

Examples

She realized it was the selfsame dress she had worn to the party last year.
He returned to the selfsame cottage where he had spent his childhood summers.
The scientist repeated the experiment under the selfsame conditions to verify the results.

Synset Examples

this is the identical room we stayed in before
saw the selfsame quotation in two newspapers
on this very spot
the very thing he said yesterday
the very man I want to see

Related Terms

  • Synonym: identical - being exactly alike or the same; indistinguishable

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'same', originating in Middle English.
