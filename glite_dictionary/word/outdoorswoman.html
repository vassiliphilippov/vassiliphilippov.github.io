outdoorswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˌaʊtˈdɔrzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˌaʊtˈdɔːzˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who enjoys or is skilled at outdoor activities such as hunting, fishing, hiking, and camping
a woman active in outdoor activities

Examples

As an avid outdoorswoman, she spends every weekend hiking and fishing in the mountains.
The magazine featured an inspiring interview with a famous outdoorswoman and her climbing achievements.
Many young girls look up to her as a role model for becoming an outdoorswoman and adventurer.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: outdoorsman - a man who enjoys or is skilled at outdoor activities

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'outdoorsman,' combining 'outdoors' + 'woman.'
