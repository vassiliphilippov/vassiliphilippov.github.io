outreach

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtˌriːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌriːtʃ / (BrE)
the act or process of reaching out or extending effort, especially to help or communicate with others or achieve a goal
the act of reaching out or extending effort

Examples

The organization's outreach to the local community made a significant impact.
His outreach to former colleagues was met with enthusiasm and gratitude.
The university launched an outreach program to encourage more students to study science.

Synset Examples

the outreach toward truth of the human spirit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outstretch - to stretch out or extend, especially the hand or arm

Etymology

From Middle English 'outen' (to put out) + 'rechen' (to reach).
