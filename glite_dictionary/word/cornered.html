cornered

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɔrnərd / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnəd / (BrE)
forced into a position where escape is impossible and confrontation is unavoidable, often by being surrounded or trapped
forced into a position with no escape

Examples

The suspect felt cornered by the police officers surrounding the building.
She looked like a cornered animal, ready to defend herself at any moment.
With no options left, he realized he was truly cornered.

Synset Examples

a stag at bay
she had me cornered between the porch and her car
like a trapped animal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trapped - unable to escape from a situation or place
  • Antonym: free - able to move or act without restriction; not confined
