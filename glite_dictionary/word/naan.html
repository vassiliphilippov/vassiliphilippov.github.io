naan

1. NOUN

/ nɑn / (AmE), / nɑːn / (BrE)
a leavened, oven-baked flatbread, typically teardrop-shaped, popular in Indian cuisine and often cooked in a clay tandoor oven
Indian leavened flatbread

Examples

I ordered butter chicken with a side of naan for dinner.
She loves to dip her naan in spicy curry sauces.
Freshly baked naan is soft, warm, and perfect with lamb kebabs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flatbread - a type of bread made with flour, water, and salt, then flattened and baked

Etymology

From Hindi nān, from Persian nān (bread).
