Neopolitan

1. NOUN

/ ˌniːəˈpɑːlətn̩ / (AmE), / ˌniːəˈpɒlətn̩ / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Naples, Italy
person from Naples, Italy

Examples

As a proud Neopolitan, she often shares stories about her city's history.
The museum was curated by a Neopolitan with deep knowledge of local art.
Many Neopolitans celebrate their traditions with lively festivals every summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Neapolitan - a native or resident of Naples, Italy; the standard spelling for the demonym

Etymology

From Italian 'napoletano', meaning 'of Naples' (Napoli).
