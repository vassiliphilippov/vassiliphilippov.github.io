one-seed

1. ADJ

/ wʌn siːd / (AmE), / wʌn siːd / (BrE)
(of a fruit or plant structure) having a single seed.
having a single seed

Examples

The one-seed fruit is characteristic of this species.
Some berries are one-seed while others contain multiple seeds.
Researchers studied the development of one-seed pods in the laboratory.
