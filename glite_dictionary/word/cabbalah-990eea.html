cabbalah

1. NOUN

/ kəˈbɑːlə / (AmE), / kəˈbɑːlə / (BrE)
A system of esoteric or mystical interpretation traditionally kept secret, closely associated with Jewish Kabbalah.
esoteric mystical system linked to Kabbalah

Examples

She spent years studying the cabbalah in search of deeper spiritual meaning.
Secrets of the cabbalah are said to be revealed only to the worthy.
The novel weaves elements of the cabbalah into its mysterious plot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kabbalah - Jewish mystical tradition of esoteric interpretation and doctrine

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'cabala', from Hebrew 'qabbālāh' (tradition, received lore).
