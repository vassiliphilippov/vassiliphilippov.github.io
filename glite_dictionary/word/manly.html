manly

1. ADJ

/ ˈmænli / (AmE), / ˈmanli / (BrE)
Having qualities such as strength, courage, or determination that are traditionally associated with men.
having qualities associated with men

Examples

He spoke in a calm and manly manner during the crisis.
Many admired her manly courage and sense of duty.
Writers once praised physical strength as a manly virtue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masculine - having qualities or appearance traditionally associated with men
  • Antonym: feminine - having qualities traditionally associated with women

Etymology

From Old English *mannlic* (having human qualities, male-like), from *mann* (man) + *-lic* (-ly).
