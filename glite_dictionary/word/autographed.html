autographed

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔtəˌɡræft / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəˌɡrɑːft / (BrE)
(of an object) bearing the signature of its author or a notable person.
bearing a signature; signed by an author or celebrity

Examples

I was thrilled to receive an autographed photo from my favorite actor.
He proudly displayed an autographed baseball in his office.
The library auctioned off an autographed first edition for charity.

Synset Examples

an autographed copy of his latest book

Related Terms

  • Synonym: signed - bearing a written signature

Etymology

Formed from 'autograph' (from Greek 'autographos' – 'written with one's own hand') and the suffix '-ed'.
