Callitris cupressiformis

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlaɪtrɪs ˌkjuːprəsɪˈfɔːrmɪs / (AmE), / kəˈlɪtrɪs ˌkjuːprɪsɪˈfɔːmɪs / (BrE)
An Australian cypress pine tree species characterized by globular cones and aromatic wood.
Australian cypress pine tree species

Examples

The dense timber of the Callitris cupressiformis makes it valuable for construction in Australia.
Seeds from Callitris cupressiformis are often spread by wind in dry woodlands.
Botanists study Callitris cupressiformis to understand its adaptation to arid environments.

Etymology

From Latin: genus name 'Callitris', species epithet 'cupressiformis' meaning 'cypress-like'.
