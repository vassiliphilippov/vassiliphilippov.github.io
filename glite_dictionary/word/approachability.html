approachability

1. NOUN

/ əˌproʊtʃəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / əˌprəʊtʃəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being friendly and easy to talk to or deal with.
quality of being easy to approach

Examples

Her approachability made her a favorite among the students.
The manager’s approachability encourages employees to share their concerns openly.
Many people admire his approachability and willingness to help others.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unapproachability - the quality of being difficult to approach or deal with
  • Synonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly

Etymology

From 'approachable' + '-ity', ultimately from Latin 'appropiare' (to draw near).
