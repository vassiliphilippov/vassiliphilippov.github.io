persisting

1. ADJ

/ pərˈsɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / pəˈsɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
continuing firmly or relentlessly in pursuit of something, especially in a determined or indefatigable way
continuing relentlessly or firmly

Examples

The persisting rumors about their breakup made headlines for weeks.
She was admired for her persisting efforts to find a cure for the disease.
Despite the difficulties, his persisting optimism inspired everyone around him.

Synset Examples

impossible to escape the dogging fears

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relentless - showing no signs of stopping or lessening in intensity
  • Synonym: indefatigable - persisting tirelessly in pursuit of something
  • Antonym: faltering - losing strength or momentum
