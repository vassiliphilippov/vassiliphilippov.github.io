unmarried

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmærid / (AmE), / ʌnˈmærid / (BrE)
Not married; not having a spouse.
not married; without a spouse

Examples

She remained unmarried throughout her life, focusing on her career instead.
The unmarried population in the city has increased over the past decade.
He bought a house as an unmarried man, planning for his future independence.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: married - having a husband or wife

Etymology

From the prefix un- (not) + married.
