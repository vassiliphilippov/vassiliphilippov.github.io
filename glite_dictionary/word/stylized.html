stylized

1. ADJ

/ ˈstaɪ.laɪzd / (AmE), / ˈstaɪ.laɪzd / (BrE)
characterized by following a particular artistic style or convention, rather than appearing natural or realistic.
following an artistic style or convention

Examples

The film's stylized visuals gave it a distinctly artistic atmosphere.
She prefers a more stylized approach to drawing landscapes rather than realistic depictions.
The stylized costumes in the play added to its dramatic flair.

Synset Examples

a stylized mode of theater production

Related Terms

  • Antonym: naturalistic - representing things in a natural or realistic way
  • Synonym: conventional - based on or in accordance with what is generally done or believed

Etymology

From 'style' + '-ized', meaning to make or give a certain style.
