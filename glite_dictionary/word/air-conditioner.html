air-conditioner

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr kənˌdɪʃənɚ / (AmE), / ˈeə kənˌdɪʃənə / (BrE)
a machine that cools and controls the humidity of the air in a room or building
device for cooling air indoors

Examples

We decided to install a new air-conditioner before summer arrived.
The hotel room was very comfortable thanks to its powerful air-conditioner.
Please remember to turn off the air-conditioner when you leave the office.

Synset Examples

we rarely turn on the air-conditioner

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air conditioning - the system or process of controlling air temperature and humidity indoors

Etymology

From 'air' and 'conditioner,' first used in the early 20th century alongside the development of climate-control technology.
