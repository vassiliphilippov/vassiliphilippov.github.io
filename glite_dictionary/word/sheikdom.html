sheikdom

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃeɪkdəm / (AmE), / ˈʃiːkdəm / (BrE)
A territory or state ruled by a sheik.
territory ruled by a sheik

Examples

The small sheikdom prospered after oil was discovered in its territory.
Several coastal sheikdoms formed alliances for mutual defense.
The tradition of hereditary rule is strong in the Persian Gulf sheikdoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emirate - the domain ruled by an emir
  • Synonym: kingdom - a country ruled by a king or queen

Etymology

From 'sheik' (Arabic 'shaykh', chief or elder) + '-dom' (denoting domain or condition).
