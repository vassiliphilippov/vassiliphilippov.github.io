Voltaren

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɔl.təˌrɛn / (AmE), / ˈvɒl.təˌrɛn / (BrE)
A proprietary name for a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) taken orally to relieve pain and reduce inflammation.
trademark: oral NSAID medication
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Voltaren to help manage my joint pain.
Some patients experience stomach upset when taking Voltaren for arthritis treatment.
You should not take Voltaren on an empty stomach to minimize side effects.
