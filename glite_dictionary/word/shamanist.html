shamanist

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɑːmə.nɪst / (AmE), / ˈʃæm.ən.ɪst / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of shamanism or its practices
relating to shamanism

Examples

The region is known for its strong shamanist influences among the indigenous people.
He conducted research into shamanist rituals in Central Asia.
Some modern artists draw inspiration from ancient shamanist traditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shamanic - relating to shamanism

Etymology

From shaman + -ist, patterned after shamanism.
