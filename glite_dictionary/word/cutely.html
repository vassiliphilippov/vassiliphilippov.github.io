cutely

1. ADV

/ ˈkjuːtli / (AmE), / ˈkjuːtli / (BrE)
In a way that is attractive, charming, or endearing.
in an attractive or charming way

Examples

The puppy wagged its tail cutely as it tried to get my attention.
She smiled cutely when she saw the surprise birthday cake.
The kitten cutely batted at the dangling ribbon.

Synset Examples

how cunningly the olive-green dress with its underskirt of rose-brocade fitted her perfect figure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adorably - in an adorable or lovable way
  • Antonym: awkwardly - in a clumsy or uncomfortable way
