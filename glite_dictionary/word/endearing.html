endearing

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈdɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
inspiring affection or fondness, especially through innocence or charm
inspiring affection; lovable or charming

Examples

Her endearing smile made everyone feel welcome.
The puppy's clumsy antics were incredibly endearing.
He has an endearing habit of laughing at his own jokes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lovable - easy to love; evoking affection
  • Synonym: charming - pleasant or attractive in a way that pleases others
  • Antonym: off-putting - unpleasant or repellent; discouraging affection

Etymology

From the verb 'endear', from Middle English 'enderen', from 'dear' (beloved, precious).
