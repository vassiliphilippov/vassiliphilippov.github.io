clinic

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɪnɪk / (AmE), / ˈklɪnɪk / (BrE)
a meeting at which people receive advice, training, or remedial instruction in a particular subject or activity
training or instruction session

Examples

The football team held a special clinic to help new players improve their skills.
She attended a writing clinic to get feedback on her short stories.
The music school offers a summer clinic for aspiring jazz musicians.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workshop - a meeting for intensive study or training in a subject

Etymology

From Greek klinikē (medical practice at the sickbed), later extended in English to specialized instruction meetings.

2. NOUN

/ ˈklɪnɪk / (AmE), / ˈklɪnɪk / (BrE)
A healthcare facility where people receive medical treatment or advice without staying overnight.
outpatient healthcare facility

Examples

The doctor told me to visit the clinic for my check-up.
After the accident, she received treatment at a local clinic.
Many communities rely on the free clinic for basic healthcare services.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: hospital - an institution providing medical and surgical treatment and nursing care

Etymology

From Greek 'klinikē' (practice at the sickbed), from 'klinikos' (of a bed, bedridden).

3. NOUN

/ ˈklɪnɪk / (AmE), / ˈklɪnɪk / (BrE)
A medical establishment where a group of doctors or specialists provide diagnosis and treatment to patients, often without overnight stay.
medical center for outpatient care

Examples

She went to the local clinic for a routine check-up.
The hospital runs a special clinic for diabetes patients every Wednesday.
Many people without health insurance rely on free neighborhood clinics for medical care.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: hospital - a large medical institution for inpatients and emergencies
  • Degree Lower: dispensary - a place where medicines are prepared and given out, usually for minor illnesses
  • Synonym: outpatient - a patient who visits a clinic for treatment without staying overnight

Etymology

From Greek 'klinē' (bed), via French 'clinique'.
