crural

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrʊrəl / (AmE), / ˈkrʊərəl / (BrE)
relating to the leg, especially the part between the knee and the foot.
relating to the lower leg
medical

Examples

The surgeon carefully examined the crural artery before proceeding with the operation.
Pain in the crural region can indicate a variety of musculoskeletal disorders.
An injury to the crural muscles may affect an athlete's ability to run.

Etymology

From Latin 'crus, cruris' (leg).
