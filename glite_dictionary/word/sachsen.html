Sachsen

1. PROPN

/ ˈzɑk.sən / (AmE), / ˈzak.sən / (BrE)
a region in eastern Germany, known in English as Saxony, historically the homeland of the Saxons and located around the upper Elbe river
region in eastern Germany; Saxony

Examples

Many tourists visit Sachsen for its castles, forests, and the beautiful city of Dresden.
The traditions of Sachsen have influenced German culture for centuries.
He moved to Sachsen in search of new job opportunities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Saxony - region in eastern Germany; English name for Sachsen

Etymology

From German 'Sachsen', referring to the Saxons, a Germanic people.
