abdominousness

1. NOUN

/ æbˈdoʊ.mə.nəs.nəs / (AmE), / æbˈdɒm.ɪ.nəs.nəs / (BrE)
the condition of having a large or protruding belly
condition of a protruding belly

Examples

His growing abdominousness was a source of gentle amusement among his friends.
Medical professionals warned that abdominousness can be linked to certain health risks.
Despite his abdominousness, he moved with surprising agility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obesity - the state of having excessive body fat

Etymology

From Latin 'abdomen' (belly) with the suffix '-ous' and '-ness' forming an abstract noun.
