aerolitic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛroʊˈlɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛərəʊˈlɪtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of certain stony meteorites.
relating to stony meteorites

Examples

Scientists examined the aerolitic fragments recovered from the impact site.
The aerolitic nature of the rock indicated it had descended from space.
Museums often display aerolitic specimens alongside other types of meteorites.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meteoritic - relating to meteorites

Etymology

From Greek 'aēr' (air) + 'lithos' (stone), referring to meteorites as 'stones from the air'.
