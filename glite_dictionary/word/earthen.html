earthen

1. ADJ

/ ˈɜrθən / (AmE), / ˈɜːθən / (BrE)
made of earth or baked clay, especially referring to materials or objects crafted from these substances
made of earth or baked clay

Examples

The villagers cooked their meals in earthen pots over an open fire.
She placed the bouquet in an earthen vase on the windowsill.
Many ancient temples feature earthen floors that stay cool in summer.

Synset Examples

an earthen pot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clay - a stiff, sticky fine-grained earth used to make pottery
  • Antonym: metal - made of metal (in contrast to earthen)

Etymology

From Old English 'eorthen', from 'eorðe' (earth).
