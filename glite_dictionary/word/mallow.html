mallow

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæloʊ / (AmE), / ˈmaləʊ / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Malva or the family Malvaceae, typically with pink or purple flowers and soft, hairy leaves.
plant of the Malvaceae family

Examples

The mallow in the garden attracts many butterflies with its colorful blooms.
She brewed a tea from dried mallow leaves to soothe her sore throat.
Several species of mallow grow wild along the hedgerows in the countryside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hibiscus - any plant of the genus Hibiscus, also in the Malvaceae family

Etymology

From Old English 'mealwe', from Latin 'malva'.
