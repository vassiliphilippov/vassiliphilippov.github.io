crippling

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɪplɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪplɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing severe damage or making someone or something unable to function normally; disabling or incapacitating.
causing severe damage or incapacity

Examples

The accident resulted in a crippling injury that ended his athletic career.
The company suffered a crippling financial loss during the recession.
A crippling disease spread rapidly through the livestock population.

Synset Examples

a crippling injury

Related Terms

  • Synonym: debilitating - making someone weak and infirm
  • Synonym: disabling - making unable to function or act; disabling

Etymology

From the verb 'cripple' + -ing.
