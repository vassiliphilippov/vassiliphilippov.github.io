believing

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈlivɪŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈliːvɪŋ / (BrE)
The process of accepting something as true or having conviction in the truth of a statement or claim.
process of accepting as true

Examples

For many, believing without evidence is difficult to accept.
In the world of science, believing often follows seeing reproducible results.
The phrase 'seeing is believing' reminds us to trust our own observations.

Synset Examples

seeing is believing

Related Terms

  • Antonym: doubting - the process of feeling uncertainty or lack of conviction
