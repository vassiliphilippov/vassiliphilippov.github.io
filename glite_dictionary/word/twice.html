twice

1. ADV

/ twaɪs / (AmE), / twaɪs / (BrE)
On two occasions; two times.
two times

Examples

I have visited Paris twice in my life.
The phone rang twice before she answered it.
He attempted the experiment twice, but both times he failed.

Synset Examples

I called her twice

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: once - one time; on one occasion
  • Degree Higher: thrice - three times; on three occasions

Etymology

From Old English 'twīwa' (twice), related to 'two.'
