bounderish

1. ADJ

/ ˈbaʊndərɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbaʊndərɪʃ / (BrE)
Lacking in refinement or grace; behaving in a coarse or socially inappropriate manner.
lacking grace or refinement

Examples

His bounderish remarks made everyone at the party uncomfortable.
She found his bounderish behavior unacceptable in polite society.
Despite his wealth, his bounderish manners revealed his lack of upbringing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uncouth - lacking good manners or refinement
  • Antonym: refined - elegant and cultured in appearance or manner

Etymology

From 'bounder,' meaning an ill-mannered person, with the suffix '-ish' meaning 'having the qualities of.'
