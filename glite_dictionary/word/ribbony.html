ribbony

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪbəni / (AmE), / ˈrɪbəni / (BrE)
long and narrow in shape, like a ribbon
resembling a ribbon in shape

Examples

The river stretched across the plain in a ribbony curve.
She decorated the cake with ribbony strands of chocolate.
Long, ribbony clouds drifted across the evening sky.

Synset Examples

ribbonlike noodles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ribbonlike - having the form or appearance of a ribbon
