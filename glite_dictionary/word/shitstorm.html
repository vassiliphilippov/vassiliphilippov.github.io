shitstorm

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪt.stɔrm / (AmE), / ˈʃɪt.stɔːm / (BrE)
A situation marked by intense controversy, anger, or public backlash, often involving chaotic or harsh criticism.
intense controversy or backlash
slang vulgar

Examples

The politician's remarks triggered a shitstorm on social media that lasted for days.
As soon as the news broke, a shitstorm erupted in the press and among the public.
Launching the policy without proper consultation caused a shitstorm within the company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uproar - a sudden loud or impassioned noise or disturbance
  • Synonym: controversy - public disagreement or heated discussion
