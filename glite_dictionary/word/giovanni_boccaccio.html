Giovanni Boccaccio

1. PROPN

/ dʒoʊˈvɑːni boʊˈkɑːtʃioʊ / (AmE), / dʒɒˈvɑːni bɒˈkætʃiəʊ / (BrE)
(1313–1375) An Italian poet, writer, and humanist best known for 'The Decameron.'
Italian poet and author (1313–1375)

Examples

Giovanni Boccaccio's 'The Decameron' is considered a masterpiece of Italian literature.
