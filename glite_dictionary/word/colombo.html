Colombo

1. PROPN

/ kəˈlʌmˌboʊ / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmbəʊ / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Sri Lanka, located on the western coast of the island; known for its major harbor and economic significance.
capital city of Sri Lanka

Examples

Colombo is a bustling city with a vibrant blend of modern life and colonial buildings.
She traveled from Colombo to the ancient city of Kandy by train.
The port of Colombo handles much of the country's international trade.
