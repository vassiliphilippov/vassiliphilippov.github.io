Depokene

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛpoʊˌkiːn / (AmE), / ˈdɛpəʊˌkiːn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand of valproic acid, used as an anticonvulsant to prevent certain types of seizures.
brand name anticonvulsant drug
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Depokene to help control my epilepsy.
Some patients experience side effects when taking Depokene for an extended period.
After switching to Depokene, his seizure frequency decreased significantly.
