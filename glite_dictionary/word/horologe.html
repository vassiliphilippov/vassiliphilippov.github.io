horologe

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrəˌloʊdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhɒrəlɒdʒ / (BrE)
an instrument or device for measuring or indicating time, such as a clock or watch
device for measuring time

Examples

The antique horologe in the hall has been keeping time for over two centuries.
Scholars studied the ancient horologe to understand medieval methods of time measurement.
She admired the intricate designs carved into the face of the old horologe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clock - an instrument for measuring and indicating time
  • Synonym: watch - a small timepiece worn typically on the wrist

Etymology

From Latin horologium, from Greek hōrológion (clock, timepiece).
