dotrel

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊtrəl / (AmE), / ˈdɒtrəl / (BrE)
a rare plover (Eudromias morinellus) found in upland regions of Eurasia, known for its distinctive plumage and tame behavior
rare upland Eurasian plover

Examples

The dotrel is seldom seen outside remote mountain areas during breeding season.
Birdwatchers travel long distances in hopes of spotting a dotrel among the heather.
The distinct markings of the dotrel make it easy for experts to identify.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dotterel - a small, tame plover of Eurasian uplands, alternate spelling

Etymology

Variant of 'dotterel', from Middle English, diminutive of 'dote' (meaning fool), due to its unwary nature.
