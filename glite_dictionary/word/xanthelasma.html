xanthelasma

1. NOUN

/ zænθəˈlæzmə / (AmE), / zanθɛˈlazmə / (BrE)
A yellowish, cholesterol-rich deposit of fat typically found on the eyelids, especially in elderly individuals.
yellowish fatty deposit on eyelids
medical

Examples

The dermatologist diagnosed the yellowish bumps on her eyelids as xanthelasma.
Treatment for xanthelasma may include removal for cosmetic reasons, but recurrence is common.
Xanthelasma is often associated with high cholesterol levels in the blood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: xanthoma - fatty growths under the skin, often cholesterol deposits

Etymology

From Greek xanthos (yellow) + elasma (plate).
