incognizable

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈkɑːɡnɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒɡnɪzəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to perceive or know; unable to be recognized or understood
impossible to perceive or know

Examples

The truth behind the mysterious phenomena remains incognizable to scientists.
Philosophers often debate whether the nature of reality is ultimately incognizable.
Some aspects of the universe might forever be incognizable by human minds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unknowable - impossible to know
  • Antonym: knowable - able to be known or understood

Etymology

From Latin in- (not) + cognoscere (to know) + -able.
