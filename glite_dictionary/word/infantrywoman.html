infantrywoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.fən.triˌwʊ.mən / (AmE), / ˈɪn.fən.triˌwʊ.mən / (BrE)
A female soldier who serves in an infantry unit and fights on foot with small arms.
female infantry soldier

Examples

The army recently trained its first female infantrywoman for frontline combat duty.
As an experienced infantrywoman, she led her squad through challenging terrain.
Many young women aspire to become an infantrywoman and serve their country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infantryman - a male or unspecified gender infantry soldier

Etymology

Formed from 'infantry' + 'woman', by analogy with 'infantryman'.
