portraitist

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔr.trə.tɪst / (AmE), / ˈpɔː.trət.ɪst / (BrE)
An artist who paints or draws portraits of people.
artist who paints or draws portraits

Examples

The portraitist captured the subtle smile of his subject on canvas.
She became a renowned portraitist, painting the faces of many famous figures.
As a portraitist, he traveled across Europe to find new models.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: portrait painter - an artist who specializes in painting portraits
  • Antonym: landscape painter - an artist who paints landscapes (not portraits)

Etymology

From 'portrait' + '-ist', modeled after 'artist'.
