Claforan

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæfəræn / (AmE), / ˈklæfəræn / (BrE)
A brand name for the antibiotic cefotaxime, used to treat severe infections of the lungs, throat, ears, or urinary tract.
brand name for cefotaxime antibiotic
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Claforan to treat my severe lung infection.
Some patients may experience side effects after receiving Claforan injections.
For complicated urinary tract infections, Claforan is often considered an effective option.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cefotaxime - an antibiotic belonging to the cephalosporin class
