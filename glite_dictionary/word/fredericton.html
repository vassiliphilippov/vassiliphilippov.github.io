Fredericton

1. PROPN

/ ˈfrɛdərɪktən / (AmE), / ˈfrɛdərɪktən / (BrE)
The capital city of the Canadian province of New Brunswick, located on the Saint John River.
capital of New Brunswick, Canada

Examples

Fredericton is known for its beautiful riverfront trails and historic architecture.
After moving to Fredericton, she quickly grew to love the local art scene.
The conference will take place in Fredericton next spring.

Etymology

Named after Prince Frederick, Duke of York (1763–1827).
