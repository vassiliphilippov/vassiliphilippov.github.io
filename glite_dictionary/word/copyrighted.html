copyrighted

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑːpiˌraɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɒpiˌraɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a literary, musical, artistic, or dramatic work) protected by copyright law, so that reproduction or use is restricted without permission.
protected by copyright law

Examples

It is illegal to distribute copyrighted material without the owner's consent.
The museum owns several copyrighted photographs from famous artists.
Make sure your song isn't a copyrighted work before uploading it online.

Synset Examples

permission to publish copyright material

Related Terms

  • Antonym: uncopyrighted - not protected by copyright law

Etymology

Formed from 'copyright' + '-ed', denoting possession of copyright protection.
