Rembrandtesque

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɛmˌbræntˈɛsk / (AmE), / ˌrɛmˌbræntˈɛsk / (BrE)
Having qualities or characteristics reminiscent of the style of Rembrandt, especially in regard to dramatic lighting or depth.
in the style of Rembrandt

Examples

The portrait's use of light and shadow is distinctly Rembrandtesque in its execution.
Many critics praised the film’s Rembrandtesque cinematography for its striking contrasts and rich textures.
The painter achieved a Rembrandtesque effect through deep browns and golden highlights.

Etymology

Formed from the name Rembrandt (the Dutch painter Rembrandt Harmenszoon van Rijn) and the suffix -esque, meaning 'in the style of'.
