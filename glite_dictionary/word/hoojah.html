hoojah

1. NOUN

/ ˈhuː.dʒɑː / (AmE), / ˈhuː.dʒɑː / (BrE)
a loud cheer or exclamation of excitement, often expressed in a group setting
loud cheer or exclamation

Examples

The students erupted in a hoojah as their school was announced the winner.
With a triumphant hoojah, the fans filled the stadium with noise.
She let out a hoojah when the final goal was scored.

Synset Examples

She gave a loud hoojah when her team won.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheer - a shout of encouragement, praise, or joy
  • Synonym: shout - a loud call or cry, especially as an expression of strong emotion
