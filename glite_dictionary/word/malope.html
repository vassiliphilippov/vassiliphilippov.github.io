Malope

1. NOUN

/ məˈloʊpi / (AmE), / məˈləʊpi / (BrE)
A small genus of mainly European herbs in the mallow family, cultivated for their attractive flowers.
genus of European flowering herbs

Examples

The Malope is prized in gardens for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers.
Several species within Malope are grown for ornamental purposes across Europe.
Botanists studied the distribution of Malope in Mediterranean habitats.

Etymology

From New Latin Malope, a genus name in botanical classification.
