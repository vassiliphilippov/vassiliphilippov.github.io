irritatingly

1. ADV

/ ˈɪr.ɪˌteɪ.tɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈɪr.ɪ.teɪ.tɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that causes annoyance or discomfort.
in an annoying manner

Examples

The alarm clock buzzed irritatingly early every morning, disturbing my sleep.
She irritatingly corrected every small mistake I made during the meeting.
The mosquito hovered irritatingly close to my ear all night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annoyingly - in an annoying manner
  • Antonym: soothingly - in a calming or comforting manner

Etymology

From 'irritating' + '-ly', where 'irritating' originates from Latin 'irritare' (to excite, provoke).
