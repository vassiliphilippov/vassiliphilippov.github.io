Kananga

1. PROPN

/ kəˈnɑŋɡə / (AmE), / kəˈnæŋɡə / (BrE)
A city in southwestern Democratic Republic of Congo, formerly known as Luluabourg until 1966.
city in southwestern Democratic Republic of Congo

Examples

Kananga is one of the largest cities in the Democratic Republic of Congo.
Many travelers pass through Kananga on their way to neighboring provinces.
The university in Kananga attracts students from across the country.
