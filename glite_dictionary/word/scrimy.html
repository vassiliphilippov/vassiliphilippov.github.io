scrimy

1. ADJ

/ ˈskraɪmi / (AmE), / ˈskraɪmi / (BrE)
petty or reluctant in giving or spending; stingy or miserly
stingy; reluctant to give or spend

Examples

Despite his wealth, the landlord remained scrimy when it came to repairs.
The charity was disappointed by the scrimy donations from the local businesses.
Her scrimy attitude made her unpopular among her more generous friends.

Synset Examples

a niggardly tip

Related Terms

  • Synonym: niggardly - unwilling to spend or give; miserly
  • Antonym: generous - willing to give money, help, or time freely
