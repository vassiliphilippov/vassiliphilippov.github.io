operatic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑːpəˈrætɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒpəˈrætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of opera, especially in style, content, or performance
relating to opera

Examples

Her operatic voice filled the concert hall, thrilling the audience.
The production was known for its operatic grandeur and elaborate costumes.
He delivered his lines in a dramatic, almost operatic manner, emphasizing every emotion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opera - a dramatic work set to music

Etymology

From Italian 'operatico', from 'opera' (work, musical drama).
