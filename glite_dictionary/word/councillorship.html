councillorship

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊnsələrˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈkaʊnsɪləˌʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or office of being a member of a council
the office of a council member

Examples

Her councillorship began after she won the city election last November.
He served two terms during his councillorship and introduced several important policies.
The responsibilities of councillorship include attending meetings and representing constituents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: councilor - a member of a council

Etymology

From 'council' + suffix '-ship' (denoting position or condition).
