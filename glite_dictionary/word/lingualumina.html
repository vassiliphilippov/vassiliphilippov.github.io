Lingualumina

1. NOUN

/ lɪŋˌɡwɑː.luːˈmiː.nə / (AmE), / lɪŋˌɡwɑː.luːˈmiː.nə / (BrE)
An artificial or constructed language, also known as the language of light.
constructed language; artificial language

Examples

Several science fiction authors have featured Lingualumina as a means of universal communication.
Enthusiasts gather annually to discuss grammar and vocabulary innovations in Lingualumina.
The ambassador delivered her message in fluent Lingualumina, impressing the delegates.

Etymology

From Latin 'lingua' (language) and 'lumina' (light), meaning 'language of light'.
