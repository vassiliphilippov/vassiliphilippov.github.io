rowing

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport or activity of propelling a boat using oars.
the sport of propelling a boat with oars

Examples

She took up rowing in college and quickly joined the varsity team.
Early mornings on the river are perfect for rowing practice.
The university has a strong tradition in competitive rowing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sculling - a form of rowing with two oars per rower

Etymology

From the verb 'row' (to move a boat with oars) + -ing.
