cetaceous

1. ADJ

/ sɪˈteɪʃəs / (AmE), / sɪˈteɪʃəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of whales, dolphins, and other members of the order Cetacea
relating to whales and dolphins

Examples

The blue whale is the largest living cetaceous animal on Earth.
Researchers conducted a study on the vocalizations of cetaceous species in the Pacific.
The museum's exhibit featured fossils from several ancient cetaceous creatures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marine mammal - an aquatic mammal such as a whale or dolphin

Etymology

From Latin 'cetus' (whale), via New Latin 'Cetacea' (order including whales and dolphins).
