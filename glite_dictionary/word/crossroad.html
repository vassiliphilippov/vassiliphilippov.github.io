crossroad

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɔsˌroʊd / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌrəʊd / (BrE)
a place where two or more roads intersect, forming a junction
a road junction

Examples

The accident occurred at the busy crossroad near the city center.
There's a new traffic light being installed at the crossroad to improve safety.
She waited patiently at the crossroad for the bus to arrive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intersection - a point where two or more things intersect, especially roads
  • Degree Higher: roundabout - a circular junction where roads meet

Etymology

Formed from 'cross' and 'road,' originally indicating a point where roads cross.
