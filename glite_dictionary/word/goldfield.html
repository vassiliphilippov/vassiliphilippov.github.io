goldfield

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldˌfiːld / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌfiːld / (BrE)
an area or district where gold is found and mined
district where gold is mined

Examples

Many prospectors flocked to the goldfield during the gold rush.
The remote town developed rapidly after a goldfield was discovered nearby.
Environmental concerns often arise around old goldfields due to leftover mining waste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gold mine - a mine from which gold is extracted
