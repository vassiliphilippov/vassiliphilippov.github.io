home ec

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm ɛk / (AmE), / həʊm ɛk / (BrE)
the theory and practice of homemaking, especially as a subject taught in school; home economics
abbreviation for home economics (homemaking subject)
informal

Examples

Many students took home ec to learn basic cooking and budgeting skills.
The school recently added sewing to its home ec curriculum.
My mother still uses some of the recipes she learned in home ec class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: home economics - the study of homemaking skills and household management

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'home economics'.
