stearic

1. ADJ

/ stiˈɛrɪk, ˈstɪərɪk / (AmE), / ˈstɪərɪk, stiːˈærɪk / (BrE)
relating to or composed of fat, especially stearic acid or its derivatives
relating to fat or stearic acid

Examples

The soap contains stearic acid, which helps to harden it.
Stearic compounds are often used in the manufacture of candles.
Her research focused on the stearic properties of various animal fats.

Etymology

From French stéarique, from Greek stear (genitive steatos) meaning 'tallow'.
