shmegegge

1. NOUN

/ ʃməˈɡɛɡi / (AmE), / ʃməˈɡɛɡi / (BrE)
Nonsense; foolish talk; something said that is silly or insincere.
nonsense; foolish talk
informal

Examples

Everything he said at the meeting was just shmegegge from start to finish.
Don’t take that shmegegge seriously—it’s his way of joking around.
They dismissed the rumor as pure shmegegge with no basis in fact.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baloney - silly or deceptive talk; nonsense
  • Synonym: nonsense - speech or writing that has no meaning or makes no sense

Etymology

From Yiddish שמעגעגי (shmegegge), meaning nonsense or foolish speech.
