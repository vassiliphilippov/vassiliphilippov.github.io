honest

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑnəst / (AmE), / ˈɒnɪst / (BrE)
earned or gained in a fair and morally upright way, without cheating or stealing
earned fairly or without cheating

Examples

He makes an honest living as a carpenter, never cutting corners or cheating anyone.
They insisted on paying him an honest wage for his hard work.
She was proud to have earned every penny through honest effort.

Synset Examples

an honest wage
a fair penny

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fair - treating people equally without cheating; just or honest

Etymology

From Old French honeste, from Latin honestus (honorable, decent).
