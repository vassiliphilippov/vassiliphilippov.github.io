interspecific

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərspəˈsɪfɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪntəspəˈsɪfɪk / (BrE)
Occurring or arising between different species, especially of animals or plants.
between different species

Examples

Scientists observed interspecific competition for food between foxes and badgers in the region.
Hybridization can sometimes result from interspecific breeding in plants.
The study focuses on interspecific communication among different bird species.

Synset Examples

an interspecific hybrid

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intraspecific - within a species

Etymology

From Latin inter- (between) + specificus (of a kind, species).
