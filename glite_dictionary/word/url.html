URL

1. NOUN

/ ˌjuː ɑr ˈɛl / (AmE), / ˌjuː ɑː ˈɛl / (BrE)
an address of a specific resource on the Internet, used to access web pages and other online content
address of a web page or online resource

Examples

Please enter the URL of the website you want to visit.
Each image on the internet has its own unique URL.
She sent me the URL for the conference registration page via email.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: web address - the address used to access a web page on the Internet

Etymology

Acronym for Uniform Resource Locator, coined in English in the early 1990s.
