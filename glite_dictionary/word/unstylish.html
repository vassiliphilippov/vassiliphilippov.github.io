unstylish

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈstaɪlɪʃ / (AmE), / ʌnˈstaɪlɪʃ / (BrE)
not fashionable or lacking in style or elegance
not fashionable; lacking style

Examples

She felt self-conscious about her unstylish shoes at the party.
Despite his talent, his unstylish haircut made him stand out in the photos.
The restaurant's unstylish décor didn't match its sophisticated menu.

Synset Examples

a styleless way of dressing
expensive but styleless country tweeds
wearing unstylish clothes

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stylish - fashionable and elegant

Etymology

From un- (not) + stylish.

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈstaɪlɪʃ / (AmE), / ʌnˈstaɪlɪʃ / (BrE)
not fashionable or not following current trends in style or appearance
not fashionable

Examples

His outfit was considered unstylish by most of his friends.
She felt self-conscious in her unstylish old coat at the party.
The restaurant's decor was charming but a bit unstylish compared to modern trends.

Synset Examples

unfashionable clothes
melodrama of a now unfashionable kind

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stylish - fashionable and elegant in appearance or manner
  • Synonym: unfashionable - not in accord with current fashion
