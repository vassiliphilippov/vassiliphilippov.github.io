New Jerseyite

1. NOUN

/ nu ˈdʒɝziˌaɪt / (AmE), / njuː ˈdʒɜːziˌaɪt / (BrE)
a person who is a native or resident of the state of New Jersey, USA
native or resident of New Jersey

Examples

Maria is a proud New Jerseyite who loves talking about her home state.
As a lifelong New Jerseyite, he knows the best places to get pizza.
Many New Jerseyites commute daily to New York City for work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: New Yorker - native or resident of New York

Etymology

Formed from 'New Jersey' + '-ite', a common suffix for denoting inhabitants.
