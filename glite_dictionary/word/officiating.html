officiating

1. NOUN

/ əˈfɪʃiˌeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈfɪʃieɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of serving as an official at a sports event, such as umpiring or refereeing
act of umpiring or refereeing

Examples

His performance in officiating was praised by both teams after the match.
The tournament committee is looking for volunteers interested in officiating.
Poor officiating can sometimes overshadow a well-played game.

Synset Examples

the officiating was excellent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: umpiring - the act of acting as an umpire
  • Synonym: refereeing - the act of acting as a referee

Etymology

From the verb 'officiate', which comes from Latin 'officium' (service, duty).
