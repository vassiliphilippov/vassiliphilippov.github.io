unassailably

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.əˈseɪ.ləb.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈseɪ.ləb.li / (BrE)
In a way that cannot be attacked, questioned, or doubted; indisputably.
in a way that cannot be doubted or attacked

Examples

The evidence is unassailably clear and convincing.
She argued her case unassailably, leaving no room for doubts.
Their right to the property was unassailably established in court.

Synset Examples

his views were unchangeably fixed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indisputably - in a way that cannot be disputed
  • Synonym: unquestionably - in a manner that cannot be questioned
  • Antonym: debatably - in a way that is open to debate or question

Etymology

From unassailable, from un- (not) + assail (attack) + -able + -ly.
