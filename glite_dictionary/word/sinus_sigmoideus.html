sinus sigmoideus

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaɪ.nəs sɪɡˈmɔɪ.di.əs / (AmE), / ˌsaɪ.nəs sɪɡˈmɔɪ.di.əs / (BrE)
A large, S-shaped venous channel (dural sinus) located on the temporal and occipital bones of the skull, draining blood from the brain.
S-shaped dural sinus in the skull
medical

Examples

The sinus sigmoideus plays a key role in draining blood from the brain's posterior regions.
During surgery, careful attention must be paid to avoid damaging the sinus sigmoideus.
Imaging revealed thrombosis in the patient's sinus sigmoideus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sigmoid sinus - S-shaped dural venous sinus in the brain

Etymology

From Latin, meaning 'sigmoid sinus' (S-shaped cavity).
