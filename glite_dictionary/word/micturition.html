micturition

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪktʃəˈrɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪktjʊˈrɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the process of discharging urine from the body
the act of urinating
medical

Examples

The doctor asked if any difficulties occurred during micturition.
Micturition is regulated by both voluntary and involuntary mechanisms.
In some medical conditions, frequent micturition can be a distressing symptom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urination - the act of discharging urine from the body
  • Antonym: defecation - the act of discharging feces from the body

Etymology

From Latin *micturire* (to want to urinate).
