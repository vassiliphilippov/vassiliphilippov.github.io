unintended

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪnˈtɛn.dɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈtɛn.dɪd / (BrE)
Not planned or done deliberately; not intended.
not deliberate or planned

Examples

His comments had unintended consequences that surprised everyone involved.
The spill was completely unintended, and he apologized right away.
Sometimes an unintended joke can make people laugh the most.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intentional - done on purpose; deliberate
  • Antonym: deliberate - done consciously and intentionally
  • Synonym: accidental - happening by chance, not planned

Etymology

From un- (not) + intended, from intend, from Latin intendere (to stretch out, intend).
