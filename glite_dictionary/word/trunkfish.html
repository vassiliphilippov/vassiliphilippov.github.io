trunkfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌŋkˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈtrʌŋkˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
A small tropical fish with its body and head encased in bony plates, belonging to the family Ostraciidae.
a tropical fish with a bony armored body

Examples

The trunkfish is easily recognized by its box-like armored body.
Scientists observed a school of trunkfish sheltering in the coral reef.
Aquariums often display the colorful trunkfish due to its unusual appearance.
