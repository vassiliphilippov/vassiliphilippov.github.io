to unstuff

1. VERB

/ ʌnˈstʌf / (AmE), / ʌnˈstʌf / (BrE)
to make something unblocked or clear, especially by removing something that is obstructing it
make unblocked or clear

Examples

This nasal spray will unstuff your nose quickly.
After drinking the hot tea, his sinuses began to unstuff.
The doctor promised that the treatment would unstuff my ears by tomorrow.

Synset Examples

The medicine unstuffed my nose in minutes!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clear - to make free from obstruction or blockage
  • Synonym: to unblock - to remove an obstruction from something

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'stuff', meaning to remove what is stuffed or blocking.
