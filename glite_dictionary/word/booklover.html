booklover

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊkˌlʌvər / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌlʌvə / (BrE)
A person who loves and often collects books.
someone fond of books

Examples

As a booklover, she spends most weekends browsing secondhand bookstores.
Every booklover dreams of having a personal library at home.
The club was formed by local booklovers who wanted to share their passion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bibliophile - a person who loves or collects books
