greenishness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡriːnɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being somewhat green in color
the state of being somewhat green

Examples

The greenishness of the paint gave the old house a strange appearance.
Some gemstones are valued for their unique greenishness rather than pure green color.
Over time, the statue acquired a subtle greenishness due to weathering.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: greenness - the quality of being green in color
  • Synonym: greenish - having a somewhat green color

Etymology

From 'greenish' + '-ness', denoting the quality of being greenish.
