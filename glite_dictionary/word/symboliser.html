symboliser

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪmbəlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsɪmbəlaɪzə / (BrE)
a person skilled in the interpretation or representation of symbols
expert in interpreting or representing symbols
british

Examples

The ancient text was so obscure that only a true symboliser could hope to understand its meaning.
As a professional symboliser, she was often consulted by museums to decipher old manuscripts.
Some people believe that every culture needs a symboliser to make sense of its rituals and signs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: symbolizer - Someone skilled in the interpretation or representation of symbols (American spelling)

Etymology

From symbol + -iser, patterned after 'symbolize' (British spelling; 'symbolizer' in American English).
