dry-cleaned

1. ADJ

/ ˈdraɪ ˌkliːnd / (AmE), / ˈdraɪ ˌkliːnd / (BrE)
cleaned using chemical solvents instead of water, especially by a professional dry-cleaning service
cleaned with chemical solvents

Examples

My suit had to be dry-cleaned after the wine spill.
These dry-cleaned curtains look as good as new.
The label says this coat should only be dry-cleaned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dry-clean - to clean clothes with chemical solvents
