asterisked

1. ADJ

/ ˈæstərɪskt / (AmE), / ˈastərɪskt / (BrE)
marked with an asterisk (*) to indicate importance, a note, or another distinction.
marked with an asterisk

Examples

Any asterisked item on this list requires your immediate attention.
The asterisked footnotes provide additional explanations for the complex terms.
Please review the asterisked sections before submitting your report.

Synset Examples

the starred items

Related Terms

  • Synonym: starred - marked with a star or asterisk

Etymology

From 'asterisk' (from Greek 'asteriskos' meaning little star) + '-ed', forming the past participle.
