oscheocoele

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːskiəsiːl / (AmE), / ˈɒskiəsiːl / (BrE)
swelling or hernial protrusion of the scrotum
swelling of the scrotum (medical)
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with an oscheocoele following a physical examination.
Ultrasound imaging confirmed the presence of an oscheocoele in the scrotal region.
Some forms of oscheocoele may require surgical intervention to prevent complications.

Etymology

From Greek 'oscheo-' (relating to the scrotum) and '-coele' (cavity, swelling).
