wheelless

1. ADJ

/ ˈwiːl.lɪs / (AmE), / ˈwiːl.ləs / (BrE)
Without wheels; not having wheels or not equipped with wheeled vehicles.
without wheels

Examples

The wheelless cart had to be carried instead of pulled.
Some remote villages rely on wheelless sleds during winter.
The museum displayed a wheelless chariot from ancient times.

Synset Examples

dragging a wheelless stoneboat filled with rocks
wheelless societies

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wheel - a circular object that rotates on an axle

Etymology

From 'wheel' + '-less', a suffix meaning 'without'.
