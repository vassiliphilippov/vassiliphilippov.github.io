Frédéric Auguste Bartholdi

1. PROPN

/ freɪˈdɪrɪk oʊˈɡʊst bɑrˈtoʊldi / (AmE), / freɪˈdɛrɪk əʊˈɡyːst bɑːˈtɒldi / (BrE)
(1834–1904) A French sculptor renowned for designing the Statue of Liberty, now located in New York Harbor.
French sculptor, creator of the Statue of Liberty

Examples

Frédéric Auguste Bartholdi was commissioned to design the iconic Statue of Liberty.
The works of Frédéric Auguste Bartholdi are celebrated for their monumental scale and artistry.
Many art historians consider Frédéric Auguste Bartholdi one of the great sculptors of the 19th century.
