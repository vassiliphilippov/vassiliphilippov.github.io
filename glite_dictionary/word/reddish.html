reddish

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛdɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdɪʃ / (BrE)
Slightly red in color; tinged with or resembling red.
slightly red in color

Examples

Her hair had a reddish tint in the sunlight.
The horizon glowed with a reddish hue as the sun set.
The apples in that basket are slightly reddish compared to the green ones.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: red - having the color at the end of the visible spectrum, like blood or rubies
  • Degree Lower: pink - a pale red color

Etymology

Formed from 'red' + '-ish' (a suffix meaning 'somewhat' or 'like').
