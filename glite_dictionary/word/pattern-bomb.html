to pattern-bomb

1. VERB

/ ˈpætərn bɑm / (AmE), / ˈpætən bɒm / (BrE)
to drop bombs over an area in a specific, predetermined pattern, usually as a military tactic
drop bombs in a specific pattern

Examples

The air force was ordered to pattern-bomb the enemy supply routes overnight.
During the conflict, military planes pattern-bombed large swathes of countryside to disrupt defenses.
They will likely pattern-bomb the target area to maximize damage and ensure effectiveness.
