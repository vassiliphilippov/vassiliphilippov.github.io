untainted

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈteɪntɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈteɪntɪd / (BrE)
not spoiled or damaged; not affected by anything bad, especially in regard to reputation or character.
not spoiled or damaged; pure

Examples

Despite the rumors, her reputation remained untainted by scandal.
He prided himself on an untainted record after years of service.
Their support was crucial because their names were untainted by controversy.

Synset Examples

his unsullied name
an untarnished reputation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tainted - spoiled or damaged; affected by something bad
  • Synonym: unsullied - not spoiled or made impure
  • Synonym: untarnished - not spoiled, damaged, or made less good, especially by anything affecting reputation

Etymology

Formed from the prefix un- (not) and tainted (spoiled or contaminated).
