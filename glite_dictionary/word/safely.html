safely

1. ADV

/ ˈseɪfli / (AmE), / ˈseɪfli / (BrE)
In a way that is free from harm or danger; in a safe manner.
in a safe manner

Examples

Please cross the street safely and watch out for cars.
She made it safely to her destination despite the storm.
The chemicals must be handled safely to prevent accidents.

Synset Examples

we are safely out of there

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dangerously - in a way that is likely to cause harm or danger
  • Synonym: securely - in a way that is safe from risk or harm

Etymology

From 'safe' + '-ly', forming the adverb from the adjective.
