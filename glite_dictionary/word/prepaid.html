prepaid

1. ADJ

/ ˌpriːˈpeɪd / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈpeɪd / (BrE)
(of an item or service) paid for in advance, especially before receiving or using it.
paid for in advance

Examples

The prepaid card can be used to purchase goods online or in stores.
Please send your application in the prepaid envelope provided with this letter.
Many mobile phone plans now offer prepaid options with no long-term contract.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: postpaid - paid after receiving goods or services

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'pre-' (before) + 'paid', past participle of 'pay'.
