streetwalker

1. NOUN

/ ˈstriːtˌwɔkər / (AmE), / ˈstriːtˌwɔːkə / (BrE)
a prostitute who solicits customers by walking the streets, rather than working from a brothel or other fixed location
prostitute who works on the street

Examples

The city increased police patrols in areas known for streetwalkers.
Many streetwalkers work at night in the downtown district.
The film tells the story of a young woman who becomes a streetwalker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prostitute - person who engages in sexual activity for payment
