comp

1. NOUN

/ kɑmp / (AmE), / kɒmp / (BrE)
an intensive examination testing a student's proficiency in a specific field, especially at the graduate level
comprehensive academic examination

Examples

After months of studying, she finally passed her comp in microbiology.
The graduate students were anxious about their upcoming comps.
He spent the entire weekend preparing for his comp in philosophy.

Synset Examples

she took her comps in English literature

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comprehensive exam - a full examination covering a broad field, especially in academics

Etymology

Shortened form of 'comprehensive (examination)'.
