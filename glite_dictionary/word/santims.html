santims

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæn.tɪmz / (AmE), / ˈsæn.tɪmz / (BrE)
a former monetary unit of Latvia, equal to one one-hundredth of a lats
former Latvian currency unit

Examples

Before Latvia adopted the euro, a cup of coffee cost around fifty santims.
He collected coins, including a rare one-lats and several santims from his travels.
The price was listed as 3 lats and 20 santims on the receipt.

Etymology

From Latvian 'santīms', itself from French 'centime' (hundredth part).
