Mogadishu

1. PROPN

/ ˌmoʊɡəˈdiːʃuː / (AmE), / ˌmɒɡəˈdiːʃuː / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Somalia, situated on the coast of the Indian Ocean
capital city of Somalia

Examples

She has lived in Mogadishu since childhood and knows the city well.
The peace talks were held in Mogadishu last year.
Piracy off the coast of Mogadishu has been a growing concern for international shipping.

Etymology

From Somali Muqdisho; influenced by Arabic مقديشو (Maqdashū).
