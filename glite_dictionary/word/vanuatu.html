Vanuatu

1. PROPN

/ vænəˈwɑtu / (AmE), / vænˈwɑːtuː / (BrE)
an island country in the South Pacific Ocean, forming a republic in Melanesia; independent since 1980
island nation in the Pacific Ocean

Examples

Tourists are often attracted to Vanuatu for its beautiful beaches and coral reefs.
After gaining independence in 1980, Vanuatu established itself as a sovereign nation.
Many languages are spoken across the islands of Vanuatu.

Etymology

From the local Austronesian languages, meaning 'Our Land'.
