Buddhist

1. ADJ

/ ˈbuːdɪst / (AmE), / ˈbʊdɪst / (BrE)
relating to Buddhism or its followers
relating to Buddhism

Examples

The temple was filled with beautiful Buddhist sculpture and artwork.
Many Buddhist monks gather here for meditation and study.
She is interested in studying Buddhist philosophy and teachings.

Synset Examples

Buddhist sculpture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Buddhism - the religion and philosophy based on the teachings of Buddha

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Buddha' (enlightened one) via Pali and English '-ist' suffix.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbuːdɪst / (AmE), / ˈbʊdɪst / (BrE)
a person who follows the teachings of Buddha or practices Buddhism
follower of Buddhism

Examples

Her grandmother is a devout Buddhist who meditates every morning.
Many Buddhists observe the practice of mindfulness in their daily lives.
As a Buddhist, he strives to follow the principles of compassion and nonviolence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Buddhism - a religion based on the teachings of Buddha

Etymology

From Sanskrit Buddha (enlightened, awakened), title of Siddhartha Gautama, combined with the English suffix -ist.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09703135-n
Synset POS: n
