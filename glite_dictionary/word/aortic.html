aortic

1. ADJ

/ eɪˈɔrtɪk / (AmE), / eɪˈɔːtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the aorta, the main artery carrying blood from the heart to the rest of the body
relating to the aorta
medical

Examples

The surgeon repaired the aortic valve during the operation.
He was diagnosed with an aortic aneurysm that required urgent attention.
The doctor listened for aortic murmurs using a stethoscope.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aorta - the main artery carrying blood from the heart to the rest of the body

Etymology

From aorta + -ic, from Greek 'aortē' (the aorta).
