fatherlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɑːðərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðəlaɪk / (BrE)
Having qualities resembling those of a father, especially being kind and protective.
resembling or characteristic of a father

Examples

His fatherlike advice helped guide me through some difficult decisions.
She appreciated his fatherlike concern for her well-being.
The teacher's fatherlike manner made the students feel safe and supported.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paternal - relating to or characteristic of a father
  • Antonym: motherly - having the qualities of a mother, especially being nurturing
