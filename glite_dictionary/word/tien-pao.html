Tien-pao

1. PROPN

/ tiːˈɛn paʊ / (AmE), / tiːˈɛn paʊ / (BrE)
In Taoist religion, the first of the three supreme deities collectively known as the Taoist Trinity.
first god of the Taoist Trinity

Examples

Many Taoists worship Tien-pao as part of their religious practice.
The temple was dedicated to Tien-pao and the other deities of the Taoist Trinity.
Scholars discussed the significance of Tien-pao within Taoist cosmology.

Etymology

From Chinese 天寶 (Tiānbǎo), meaning 'Heavenly Treasure.'
