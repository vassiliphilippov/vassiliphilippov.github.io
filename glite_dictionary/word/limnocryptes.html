Limnocryptes

1. NOUN

/ lɪm.noʊˈkrɪp.tiːz / (AmE), / lɪm.nəʊˈkrɪp.tiːz / (BrE)
A genus of small wading birds in the snipe family, including the jack snipe (Limnocryptes minimus).
a genus of small wading birds (snipes)

Examples

The Limnocryptes is distinguished by its smaller size compared to other snipes.
Ornithologists observed several Limnocryptes feeding along the marshy shoreline.
The jack snipe belongs to the genus Limnocryptes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snipe - a wading bird with a long straight bill, member of the family Scolopacidae

Etymology

From Greek 'limne' (marsh) and 'kryptos' (hidden).
