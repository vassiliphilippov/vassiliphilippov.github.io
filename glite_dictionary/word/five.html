five

1. NUM

/ faɪv / (AmE), / faɪv / (BrE)
the number 5; one more than four
the number 5

Examples

She counted to five before opening her eyes.
The meeting will start at five o'clock.
There are five apples on the table.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: six - the number 6; one more than five
  • Degree Lower: four - the number 4; one less than five
