foodless

1. ADJ

/ ˈfuːdləs / (AmE), / ˈfuːdləs / (BrE)
having no food; lacking food
having no food

Examples

After days stranded at sea, they were completely foodless and desperate.
The village was foodless following the storm that destroyed the crops.
The researcher described the cave as dark, damp, and utterly foodless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: starving - suffering or dying from lack of food
  • Degree Lower: hungry - feeling or showing the need for food

Etymology

Formed from 'food' + the suffix '-less'.
