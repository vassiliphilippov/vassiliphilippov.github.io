Santa Maria del Tule

1. PROPN

/ ˌsɑːntə məˈriːə dɛl ˈtuːleɪ / (AmE), / ˌsæntə məˈriːə dɛl ˈtuːleɪ / (BrE)
A town in southeastern Mexico near Oaxaca, known for the Ahuehuete, a giant Montezuma cypress tree, considered one of the largest and oldest trees in the world.
town in Mexico, famous for giant Montezuma cypress

Examples

Many tourists visit Santa Maria del Tule to see the famous Tule Tree.
The town of Santa Maria del Tule is located just outside Oaxaca City.
Local festivals in Santa Maria del Tule celebrate its cultural and natural heritage.

Etymology

Spanish, meaning 'Saint Mary of the Tule (cypress)'
