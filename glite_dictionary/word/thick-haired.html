thick-haired

1. ADJ

/ θɪk hɛrd / (AmE), / θɪk heəd / (BrE)
having dense or abundant hair.
having dense hair

Examples

Her thick-haired dog sheds a lot in the summer.
He was a thick-haired child who hated getting haircuts.
The salon offers special treatments for thick-haired clients.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: thin-haired - having sparse or scanty hair
