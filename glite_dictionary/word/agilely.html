agilely

1. ADV

/ ˈædʒɪlli / (AmE), / ˈædʒaɪlli / (BrE)
in a nimble, quick, and light manner; with agility
in a nimble and quick way

Examples

The gymnast moved agilely across the balance beam, impressing the judges.
He climbed agilely over the fence to retrieve the ball.
The cat darted agilely through the garden, chasing after a butterfly.

Synset Examples

nimbly scaling an iron gate
leaped agilely from roof to roof

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nimbly - in a quick and light way

Etymology

Formed from 'agile' + '-ly', from Latin 'agilis' (able to move quickly)
