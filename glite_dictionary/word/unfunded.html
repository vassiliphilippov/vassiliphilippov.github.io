unfunded

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfʌndɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfʌndɪd / (BrE)
not provided with financial support; lacking necessary funds or backing.
without financial support

Examples

The charity had to postpone the event because it was unfunded.
Many unfunded startups struggle to survive in a competitive market.
The architect submitted plans for an unfunded housing development.

Synset Examples

an unfunded project

Related Terms

  • Antonym: funded - provided with financial support

Etymology

From the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'funded' (provided with funds).
