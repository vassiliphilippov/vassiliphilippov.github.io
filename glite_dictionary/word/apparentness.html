apparentness

1. NOUN

/ əˈpɛr.ənt.nəs / (AmE), / əˈpær.ənt.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being apparent or clearly visible
the quality of being apparent

Examples

The apparentness of the error made it easy to correct.
She was surprised by the apparentness of his intentions.
The apparentness of the path through the woods faded as night fell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obviousness - the quality of being obvious
  • Antonym: invisibility - the state of not being visible or easily noticed

Etymology

From 'apparent' + '-ness', based on Latin 'apparentem' (appearing).
