London

1. PROPN

/ ˈlʌn.dən / (AmE), / ˈlʌn.dən / (BrE)
The capital city of England and the United Kingdom, situated on the River Thames in southeastern England; a major center for finance, industry, and culture.
the capital city of England

Examples

Many tourists visit London to see landmarks like Buckingham Palace and the Tower of London.
After moving to London, she enjoyed the vibrant theatre and art scene.
The financial district in London is home to many global banks and companies.

Etymology

From Old English 'Lunden' (recorded in Latin as 'Londinium'), possibly of pre-Roman origin.
