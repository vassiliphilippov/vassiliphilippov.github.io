Volcan de Colima

1. PROPN

/ voʊlˈkɑːn deɪ koʊˈliːmə / (AmE), / vɒlˈkæn də kɒˈliːmə / (BrE)
an active volcano located in southwestern Mexico, known for frequent eruptions
active volcano in southwestern Mexico

Examples

The Volcan de Colima erupted several times in the twentieth century, making it one of Mexico's most active volcanoes.
Scientists closely monitor the Volcan de Colima for any signs of increased volcanic activity.
Tourists visit the area near Volcan de Colima to witness its impressive landscape and frequent eruptions.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Volcán de Colima', meaning 'Colima Volcano', named after the nearby state of Colima.
