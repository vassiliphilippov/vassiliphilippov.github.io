Goethean

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡoʊtiən / (AmE), / ˈɡəːtiːən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Johann Wolfgang von Goethe or his works.
of or relating to Goethe

Examples

Her paintings have a distinctly Goethean approach to color and light.
The philosopher wrote a Goethean analysis of nature, emphasizing observation and intuition.
Many literary critics admire the Goethean spirit of universal curiosity in his essays.

Etymology

From the name of Johann Wolfgang von Goethe (1749–1832), German poet, novelist, and playwright, plus the suffix -an.
