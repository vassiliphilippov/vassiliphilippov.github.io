to dechlorinate

1. VERB

/ diˈklɔːrɪˌneɪt / (AmE), / diːˈklɔːrɪneɪt / (BrE)
to remove chlorine from (water or another substance)
remove chlorine from something

Examples

Many aquarium owners must dechlorinate tap water before adding it to their tanks.
The plant uses charcoal filters to dechlorinate drinking water.
Chemical additives can quickly dechlorinate pools, making the water safer for fish and plants.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to chlorinate - to treat or combine with chlorine

Etymology

From de- (removal of) + chlorinate (to treat with chlorine).
