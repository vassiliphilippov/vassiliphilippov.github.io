salmi

1. NOUN

/ sælˈmi / (AmE), / sælˈmiː / (BrE)
A dish of game meat stewed or braised in a rich sauce, often with wine.
game meat stewed in a rich sauce

Examples

The chef prepared a delicious salmi of pheasant for the special menu.
Traditional French cuisine often includes salmi when game birds are in season.
After the hunt, they enjoyed a hearty salmi served with red wine sauce.

Etymology

From French 'salmi', short for 'salmi de gibier' (ragout of game).
