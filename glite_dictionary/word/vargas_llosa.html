Vargas Llosa

1. PROPN

/ ˈvɑrɡəs ˈjoʊsə / (AmE), / ˈvɑːɡæs ˈjɒsə / (BrE)
Mario Vargas Llosa (born 1936), a prominent Peruvian writer and Nobel laureate known for his novels, essays, and political commentary.
Peruvian novelist and Nobel laureate

Examples

Many critics praise Vargas Llosa for his innovative storytelling and political insight.
The novels of Vargas Llosa have been translated into dozens of languages worldwide.
In 2010, Vargas Llosa was awarded the Nobel Prize in Literature.
