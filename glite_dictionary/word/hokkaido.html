Hokkaido

1. PROPN

/ hoʊˈkaɪdoʊ / (AmE), / həʊˈkaɪdəʊ / (BrE)
the second largest of Japan's four main islands, located north of Honshu
Japan's northernmost main island

Examples

Snow festivals attract thousands of visitors to Hokkaido each winter.
The climate in Hokkaido is much cooler than in other regions of Japan.
After visiting Tokyo, we took a train up to Hokkaido for hiking.

Etymology

From Japanese 北海道 (Hokkaidō), meaning "Northern Sea Circuit".
