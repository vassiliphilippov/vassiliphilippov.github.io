taxonomic

1. ADJ

/ tæk.səˈnɑ.mɪk / (AmE), / tæk.səˈnɒ.mɪk / (BrE)
relating to the classification of things, especially organisms, in an ordered system
relating to classification (taxonomy)

Examples

The taxonomic position of the newly discovered species is still being debated among scientists.
Her research focuses on the taxonomic classification of marine algae.
The museum exhibit provides a taxonomic overview of the region's birdlife.

Synset Examples

taxonomic relations
a taxonomic designation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxonomical - alternative adjectival form meaning 'relating to taxonomy'
  • Synonym: systematic - relating to a system or classification, especially in biology

Etymology

From taxonomy + -ic; taxonomy from Greek taxis (arrangement) + -nomia (method).
