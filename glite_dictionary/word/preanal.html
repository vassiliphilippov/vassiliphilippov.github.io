preanal

1. ADJ

/ priˈænəl / (AmE), / priˈanəl / (BrE)
situated in front of the anus or relating to the area located anterior to the anus, especially in anatomical or zoological contexts
situated in front of the anus
medical

Examples

Some fish species have a distinctive preanal fin located just before the anus.
During the examination, the doctor noticed swelling in the preanal region.
Certain worms have unusual preanal markings used for identification.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: anal - relating to the anus

Etymology

From Latin prefix 'pre-' (before) + 'anal' (relating to the anus).
