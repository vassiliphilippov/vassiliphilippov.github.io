steelwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈstiːlˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈstiːlˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who works in the production or manufacture of steel
female steel industry worker

Examples

During World War II, many steelwomen played a crucial role in the production of weapons and machinery.
The company featured a profile of a steelwoman who had worked in the mill for over twenty years.
Young girls were inspired by the stories of steelwomen breaking barriers in a traditionally male-dominated industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steelworker - a person who works in the steel industry
  • Synonym: steelman - a male worker engaged in making steel

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'steelworker', using 'woman' as the gender identifier.
