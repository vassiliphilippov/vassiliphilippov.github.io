functionality

1. NOUN

/ ˌfʌŋkʃəˈnæləti / (AmE), / ˌfʌŋkʃəˈnæləti / (BrE)
the range of operations or features that something, especially a device or software, can perform
the range of useful features of a device or program

Examples

The latest update adds new functionality to the app, including voice commands.
We chose this tool because its functionality meets all our business needs.
Users value both the design and the functionality of the new smartwatch.

Synset Examples

software with greater functionality

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feature - a distinctive attribute or aspect of something
  • Synonym: capability - the power or ability to do something

Etymology

From function + -ality, modeled after technical and scientific vocabulary.
