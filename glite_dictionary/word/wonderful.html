wonderful

1. ADJ

/ ˈwʌndɚfəl / (AmE), / ˈwʌndəfəl / (BrE)
Extremely good; causing delight, admiration, or pleasure.
extremely good; delightful or admirable

Examples

The view from the mountain top was wonderful, especially at sunset.
I had a wonderful time at the party last night.
She received some wonderful news about her new job.

Synset Examples

a fantastic trip to the Orient
the film was fantastic!
a howling success
a marvelous collection of rare books
had a rattling conversation about politics
a tremendous achievement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marvelous - Extremely good, wonderful
  • Synonym: fantastic - Remarkably good or wonderful
  • Antonym: terrible - Extremely bad, causing fear or terror

Etymology

From wonder + -ful, meaning 'full of wonder' (Old English wundor + full).
