Artocarpus odoratissima

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːrtəˈkɑːrpəs ˌoʊdəˌræˈtɪsɪmə / (AmE), / ˌɑːtəkɑːˈpʊs ˌəʊdəˌratɪˈsɪmə / (BrE)
A Philippine tree, related to the breadfruit, that bears edible fruit.
Philippine fruit tree related to breadfruit

Examples

The Artocarpus odoratissima produces a sweet fruit enjoyed by many in the Philippines.
Botanists classify Artocarpus odoratissima as closely related to breadfruit and jackfruit trees.
Farmers are cultivating more Artocarpus odoratissima trees due to increased demand for its fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breadfruit - tropical tree with large, starchy fruits

Etymology

From Latin: Artocarpus (genus name, from Greek 'artos' meaning bread and 'karpos' meaning fruit), and odoratissima, Latin for 'most fragrant'.
