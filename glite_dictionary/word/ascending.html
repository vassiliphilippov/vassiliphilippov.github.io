ascending

1. ADJ

/ əˈsɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈsɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
rising or moving upward; directed or progressing upward.
moving or rising upward

Examples

The hikers followed an ascending path up the mountain.
Smoke could be seen ascending from the chimney after the fire was lit.
The ascending escalator carried people to the next floor.

Synset Examples

the ascending plane
the ascending staircase
the ascending stems of chickweed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: descending - moving or going downward
  • Synonym: rise - to move upward or increase in height

Etymology

From Latin ascendere meaning 'to climb up', via Old French ascendre.
