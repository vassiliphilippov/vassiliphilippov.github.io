boyo

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊˌjoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪəʊ / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly Irish and Welsh English) A term of endearment or familiarity for a boy or man.
informal term for boy or man
informal british

Examples

Boyo, come over here and give me a hand with this.
I haven't seen that boyo around these parts in years.
You can trust him—he's a good boyo at heart.

Synset Examples

That boyo has quite the sense of humor!

Etymology

Probably from Welsh 'boi' (boy).
