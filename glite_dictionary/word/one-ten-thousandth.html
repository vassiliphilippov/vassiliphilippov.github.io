one-ten-thousandth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn tɛn θaʊzəndθ / (AmE), / wʌn tɛn ˈθaʊzəndθ / (BrE)
One part out of ten thousand equal parts; the fraction 1⁄10,000 or 0.0001.
1 in 10,000; 0.0001 fraction

Examples

A one-ten-thousandth decrease in interest rates can still have an impact on large investments.
The impurity comprised just one-ten-thousandth of the total mass of the solution.
He owned a one-ten-thousandth share of the company's stock.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: one-hundred-thousandth - one part in one hundred thousand equal parts
