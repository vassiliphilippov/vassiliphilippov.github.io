telescopically

1. ADV

/ təˌlɛˈskɑpɪkli / (AmE), / təˌlɛˈskɒpɪkli / (BrE)
In a telescopic manner; in a way that allows or involves extension or contraction like a telescope, especially in overlapping sections.
in a telescopic manner

Examples

The antenna can be extended telescopically for better reception.
The ladder unfolds telescopically, making it easy to store when not in use.
The camera lens moves telescopically to zoom in and out smoothly.

Synset Examples

each of the four legs contains a simple screw jack with a thrust bearing that is operated telescopically inside of two tubes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telescopic - having or using the ability to be extended or retracted in sections

Etymology

Formed from 'telescopic' + '-ally', based on the pattern of adverbs from adjectives.
