stickpin

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪkˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈstɪk.pɪn / (BrE)
a decorative pin worn on a necktie to fasten or adorn it
decorative pin worn in a necktie

Examples

He wore a gold stickpin in his tie at the wedding.
The antique stickpin was passed down through generations.
She admired the intricate design of his silver stickpin.

Etymology

From 'stick' (verb, to pierce) + 'pin' (pointed object), describing its use and form.
