manifest

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænəˌfɛst / (AmE), / ˈmanɪfɛst / (BrE)
an official list of the cargo, passengers, or contents carried by a ship, plane, or other vehicle, used especially for customs purposes
official list of cargo or passengers on transport

Examples

The customs officer checked the ship's manifest before allowing it to dock.
Each airplane must submit a complete manifest to authorities before departure.
The container's contents matched those listed on the manifest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cargo manifest - detailed list of goods on a ship or airplane

Etymology

From Italian *manifesto* (public declaration), from Latin *manifestus* (obvious, apparent).
