unlawful

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlɔfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɔːfəl / (BrE)
Not allowed by law; illegal.
not allowed by law

Examples

The company was fined for its unlawful business practices.
Carrying a weapon without a license is unlawful in many countries.
The new law aims to reduce unlawful immigration.

Synset Examples

unlawful measures
unlawful money
unlawful hunters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illegal - forbidden by law or statute
  • Antonym: lawful - permitted by law

Etymology

From un- (not) + lawful (from Old English lagu (law) + ful (full of)).
