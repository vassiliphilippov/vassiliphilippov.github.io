NGB

1. NOUN

/ ɛn dʒi bi / (AmE), / ɛn dʒiː biː / (BrE)
abbreviation for National Guard Bureau, the agency responsible for administering the Army National Guard and Air National Guard, and coordinating between the Army, Air Force, and National Guard units in the United States.
U.S. National Guard administrative agency (acronym)
american

Examples

The NGB issued new guidelines for state National Guard units this year.
He worked at the NGB as a liaison between the Army and Air Force.
Coordination between state and federal authorities often requires the involvement of the NGB.

Etymology

Acronym from National Guard Bureau.
