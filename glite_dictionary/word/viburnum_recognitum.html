Viburnum recognitum

1. NOUN

/ vaɪˈbɝːnəm rɪˈkɒɡnɪtəm / (AmE), / vɪˈbɜːnəm rɪˈkɒɡnɪtəm / (BrE)
A species of viburnum shrub native to the eastern United States, known as northern arrowwood.
species of viburnum shrub (northern arrowwood)

Examples

The Viburnum recognitum thrives in moist woodlands and along stream banks.
Gardeners appreciate Viburnum recognitum for its dense foliage and attractive white flowers.
Researchers compared the leaf structures of Viburnum recognitum and related species for identification.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Viburnum dentatum - closely related species known as southern arrowwood

Etymology

From Latin: 'Viburnum' (the genus name) and 'recognitum' meaning 'recognized' or 'distinguished.'
