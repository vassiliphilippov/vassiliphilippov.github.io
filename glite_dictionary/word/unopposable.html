unopposable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnəˈpoʊzəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈpəʊzəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to resist or oppose; not able to be opposed.
not able to be opposed

Examples

His authority in the organization was unopposable, and no one dared to challenge him.
The legal case was built on unopposable evidence that could not be disputed.
She made her point with such clarity that her argument became virtually unopposable in the debate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irresistible - impossible to resist; very appealing
  • Antonym: opposable - able to be opposed
