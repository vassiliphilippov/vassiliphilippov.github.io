knackwurst

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑkˌwɜrst / (AmE), / ˈnakˌwɜːst / (BrE)
a short, thick, highly seasoned German sausage, usually made of pork and beef
short, thick, highly seasoned sausage

Examples

She ordered a knackwurst with sauerkraut at the German deli.
The smoky flavor of the knackwurst distinguished it from other sausages.
At Oktoberfest, grilled knackwurst is a popular treat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sausage - a food made of ground meat, often seasoned and encased

Etymology

From German 'Knackwurst', from 'knacken' (to crack) + 'Wurst' (sausage), referring to its crisp skin.
