Hameln

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɑːmɛln / (AmE), / ˈhɑːmɛln / (BrE)
A town in northern Germany, near Hanover, noted as the setting for the legend of the Pied Piper.
town in Germany, site of Pied Piper legend

Examples

The town of Hameln attracts many tourists because of its association with the Pied Piper legend.
During their trip to Germany, they spent a day exploring the historic streets of Hameln.
Folk festivals in Hameln often reenact scenes from the famous tale of the Pied Piper.
