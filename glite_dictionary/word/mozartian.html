Mozartian

1. ADJ

/ moʊˈzɑːrtiən / (AmE), / məʊˈzɑːtiən / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart or his music.
of or relating to Mozart or his music

Examples

The pianist gave a Mozartian interpretation that emphasized clarity and grace.
Her music has a distinctly Mozartian elegance and charm.
The orchestra performed a Mozartian symphony with remarkable precision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Beethovenian - Relating to or characteristic of Beethoven or his music

Etymology

From the name of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart (1756–1791), Austrian composer, plus the suffix -ian.
