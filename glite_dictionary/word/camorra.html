Camorra

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɔrə / (AmE), / kəˈmɒrə / (BrE)
a secret criminal society originating in Naples, Italy, notorious for violence and blackmail.
Italian criminal society in Naples

Examples

The Camorra has been linked to numerous high-profile crimes in Naples.
Law enforcement agencies have struggled to dismantle the Camorra's complex network.
A new film explores the influence of the Camorra on local politics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mafia - an organized international body of criminals, originating in Sicily

Etymology

From Neapolitan Italian, possibly from 'camorra' (quarrel, fight).
