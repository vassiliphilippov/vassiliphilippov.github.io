Balistes vetula

1. NOUN

/ bəˈlaɪstiːz vəˈtuːlə / (AmE), / bəˈlaɪstiːz vɛˈtjʊlə / (BrE)
A species of brightly colored triggerfish found in the tropical Atlantic Ocean, commonly known as the queen triggerfish.
species of tropical Atlantic triggerfish

Examples

Divers were excited to spot a Balistes vetula swimming near the coral reef.
The vibrant colors of the Balistes vetula make it a favorite subject for underwater photographers.
Conservationists are monitoring Balistes vetula populations to study the health of reef ecosystems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: queen triggerfish - common name for Balistes vetula, a colorful reef fish

Etymology

From Latin; genus Balistes meaning 'one who throws' and species vetula meaning 'old woman'.
