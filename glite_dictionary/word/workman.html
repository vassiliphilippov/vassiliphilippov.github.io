workman

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːkmən / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkmən / (BrE)
a man employed to do manual or industrial work, especially in construction or manufacturing
male manual or industrial laborer

Examples

The workman repaired the broken pipe quickly and efficiently.
A workman climbed the ladder to fix the roof tiles during the storm.
After a long day, the workman returned home covered in dust and paint.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: workwoman - a woman employed to do manual or industrial work
  • Synonym: worker - a person who does a particular job, especially one that involves physical work

Etymology

From Old English 'weorcman', combining 'work' + 'man'.
