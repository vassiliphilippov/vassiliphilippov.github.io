to televise

1. VERB

/ ˈtɛlɪˌvaɪz / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪvʌɪz / (BrE)
to broadcast (a programme, event, etc.) by television
to broadcast by television

Examples

The Royal wedding was televised to millions of viewers around the world.
They decided to televise the championship game live on Sunday night.
Many historical events have been televised and are now part of the public record.

Synset Examples

The Royal wedding was televised

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broadcast - to transmit by radio or television

Etymology

From 'television' + '-ise' (verb-forming suffix).
