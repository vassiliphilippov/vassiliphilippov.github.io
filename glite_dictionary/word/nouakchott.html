Nouakchott

1. PROPN

/ nuːˈɑkʃɒt / (AmE), / nuːˈɑːkʃɒt / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Mauritania, situated in the western part of the country near the Atlantic coast
capital city of Mauritania

Examples

After years of living in Nouakchott, she became fluent in Hassaniya Arabic.
The new highway will connect Nouakchott to other major Mauritanian cities.
Climate change has made life in Nouakchott increasingly challenging due to rising sea levels.
