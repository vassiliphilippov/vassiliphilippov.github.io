tankful

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæŋkfʊl / (AmE), / ˈtaŋkfʊl / (BrE)
the amount that a tank will hold; a full tank
the amount a tank can hold

Examples

It takes less than a tankful of gas to drive across the entire state.
We stopped at the gas station to buy a tankful before heading out on the highway.
After three hours on the water, the boat had used up almost a full tankful of fuel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bucketful - the amount a bucket will hold
  • Synonym: spoonful - the amount a spoon will hold
