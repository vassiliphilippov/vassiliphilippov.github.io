cardhouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrdˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A structure built by balancing playing cards on top of each other, often unstable and easily collapsible.
a structure made by stacking playing cards

Examples

It took him an hour to finish his cardhouse, but it collapsed just as he placed the last card.
The children quietly competed to see who could build the tallest cardhouse before dinner.
Her hands were so steady that her cardhouse reached five levels high.

Synset Examples

he built three levels of his cardcastle before it collapsed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: house of cards - a structure made of playing cards; also a metaphor for something insecure
