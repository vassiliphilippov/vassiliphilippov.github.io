Bangla

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑŋɡlə / (AmE), / ˈbæŋɡlə / (BrE)
the official language of Bangladesh and West Bengal, also known as Bengali
the Bengali language

Examples

She is fluent in both Hindi and Bangla.
The national anthem of Bangladesh is written in Bangla.
He took a course to improve his Bangla reading skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bengali - the Indo-Aryan language of Bengal; synonym for Bangla

Etymology

From Bengali বাংলা (Bāṅlā), meaning 'Bengal'.
