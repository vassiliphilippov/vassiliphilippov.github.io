chancellery

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃæn.səl.ɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːn.sələri / (BrE)
a government building where the office of a chancellor is located
government building for a chancellor

Examples

The new chancellery was inaugurated by the prime minister last week.
Security is tight around the German chancellery during important diplomatic meetings.
He was given a tour of the historic chancellery in Vienna.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chancellor - a senior state or legal official, often the head of government or a university

Etymology

From Old French chancelerie, from medieval Latin cancellaria (office of a chancellor).
