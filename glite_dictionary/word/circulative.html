circulative

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɜrkjələˌtɪv / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkjʊlətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or involving circulation, especially of fluids such as blood or air
relating to circulation

Examples

The heart is a circulative organ responsible for pumping blood throughout the body.
Researchers studied the circulative properties of the solution in the vascular system.
Improved circulative efficiency can help boost cardiovascular health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circulatory - relating to circulation, especially of blood

Etymology

From Latin 'circulare' (to make round) + English suffix '-ive'.
