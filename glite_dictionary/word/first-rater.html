first-rater

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɝstˈreɪtər / (AmE), / ˌfɜːstˈreɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing of the highest quality or excellence
person or thing of the highest quality

Examples

Everyone agreed that the new chef was a first-rater in the kitchen.
Her performance in the debate proved she was a true first-rater.
Among all the candidates, he stood out as a first-rater thanks to his professionalism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first-rate - of the highest quality; excellent

Etymology

From 'first-rate' (of the highest class) + '-er' (denoting a person or thing).
