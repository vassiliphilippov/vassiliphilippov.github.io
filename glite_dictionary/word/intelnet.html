Intelnet

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.tɛl.nɛt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tɛl.nɛt / (BrE)
a secure computer network, separate from the internet, used for information sharing within the Intelligence Community
secure network for intelligence agencies

Examples

Access to the Intelnet is restricted to authorized intelligence officers.
Sensitive data was transmitted securely over the Intelnet.
The agency upgraded the Intelnet to enhance cybersecurity protocols.

Etymology

Blend of 'intelligence' and 'network', modeled on 'internet'.
