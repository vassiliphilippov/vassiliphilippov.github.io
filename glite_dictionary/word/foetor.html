foetor

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːtər / (AmE), / ˈfiːtə / (BrE)
An extremely unpleasant or offensive smell, especially noted in medical contexts.
offensively unpleasant smell
medical

Examples

The foetor of decay filled the abandoned building, making it impossible to breathe comfortably.
Doctors noted a distinct foetor on the patient's breath, indicating a possible infection.
Despite airing out the cellar for hours, the stubborn foetor lingered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stench - a strong and very unpleasant smell
  • Antonym: fragrance - a pleasant, sweet smell

Etymology

From Latin foetor (bad smell, stink).
