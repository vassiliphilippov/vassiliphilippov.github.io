Ac

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ ˈsiː / (AmE), / ˈeɪ ˈsiː / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for actinium, a radioactive element of the actinide series found in uranium ores
chemical symbol for actinium

Examples

The element Ac is used for research in radiation sources.
Scientists isolated Ac from uranium ore samples in the laboratory.
On the periodic table, Ac is listed among the actinides.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: actinium - a radioactive metallic element of the actinide series

Etymology

Abbreviation of actinium, from Greek 'aktinos' (ray).
