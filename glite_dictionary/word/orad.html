orad

1. ADV

/ ˈɔːræd / (AmE), / ˈɔːrad / (BrE)
In anatomy, toward the mouth or oral region.
toward the mouth (anatomy)
medical

Examples

The physician noted that the wound extended orad from the lower jaw.
During the procedure, the surgeon made an incision moving orad along the palate.
Certain nerves branch orad before reaching the buccal cavity.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: aborad - in the direction away from the mouth

Etymology

From Latin 'os, oris' (mouth) + '-ad' (toward).
