evanescent

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛvəˈnɛsənt / (AmE), / ˌiːvəˈnɛsənt / (BrE)
Quickly fading or disappearing; lasting for only a short time.
quickly fading or vanishing

Examples

The evanescent morning mist disappeared as the sun rose higher in the sky.
She was mesmerized by the evanescent colors that faded from the sunset in moments.
Fame can be evanescent, vanishing as quickly as it arrives.

Synset Examples

evanescent beauty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transient - lasting only for a short period of time
  • Antonym: permanent - lasting forever; never changing

Etymology

From Latin 'evanescens', present participle of 'evanescere' (to vanish).
