shameless

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃeɪmləs / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪmləs / (BrE)
Having or showing no sense of shame; not embarrassed or remorseful by something wrong or improper.
lacking shame; unashamed

Examples

He made a shameless attempt to take all the credit for the project.
Her shameless behavior at the party shocked everyone present.
The politician's shameless lies were exposed in the media.

Synset Examples

a shameless imposter
an unblushing apologist for fascism

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unashamed - not feeling or showing shame or embarrassment
  • Antonym: ashamed - feeling shame or guilt

Etymology

From Middle English 'shameles', from shame + -less.
