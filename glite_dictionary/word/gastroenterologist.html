gastroenterologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡæstroʊˌɛntəˈrɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɡæstrəʊˌɛntəˈrɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the stomach and intestines
specialist doctor for digestive tract diseases
medical

Examples

After months of stomach pain, Maria decided to see a gastroenterologist for a thorough evaluation.
The gastroenterologist recommended several tests to diagnose the cause of his digestive problems.
Finding a good gastroenterologist was essential for managing her chronic ulcerative colitis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doctor - a person licensed to practice medicine
  • Degree Lower: hepatologist - a doctor specializing in diseases of the liver
  • Degree Higher: colorectal surgeon - a surgeon specializing in the colon, rectum, and anus

Etymology

From Greek 'gaster' (stomach) and 'enteron' (intestine), plus '-logist' (specialist).
