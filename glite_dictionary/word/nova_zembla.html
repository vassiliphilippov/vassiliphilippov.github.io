Nova Zembla

1. PROPN

/ ˌnoʊvə ˈzɛmblə / (AmE), / ˌnəʊvə ˈzɛmblə / (BrE)
the name of two islands in the Arctic Ocean belonging to Russia, known as the site of a nuclear test center
Russian islands in the Arctic Ocean

Examples

The remote location of Nova Zembla made it suitable for nuclear testing.
Few people have ever visited the icy shores of Nova Zembla.
Researchers studied the impact of cold climate on wildlife in Nova Zembla.

Etymology

From Dutch 'Nova Zembla' meaning 'New Land', used by early European explorers.
