unsurprising

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnsɚˈpraɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnsəˈprʌɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
not causing surprise; expected or predictable.
not causing surprise

Examples

It is unsurprising that he got the job given his qualifications.
Their reaction to the news was entirely unsurprising.
Given the weather forecast, the flight delay was unsurprising.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: surprising - causing surprise; unexpected

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (not) to 'surprising'.
