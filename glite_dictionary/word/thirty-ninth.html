thirty-ninth

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɜrtiˈnaɪnθ / (AmE), / ˌθɜːtiˈnaɪnθ / (BrE)
constituting number thirty-nine in a sequence; 39th in order or position
ordinal number for thirty-nine (39th)

Examples

She finished in thirty-ninth place out of hundreds of competitors.
His office is on the thirty-ninth floor of the skyscraper.
The thirty-ninth chapter of the book discusses new scientific discoveries.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirty-eight - ordinal number for thirty-eight (38th)
  • Degree Higher: fortieth - ordinal number for forty (40th)
