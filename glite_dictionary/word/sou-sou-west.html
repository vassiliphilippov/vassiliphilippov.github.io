sou'-sou'-west

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaʊ sʊˈwɛst / (AmE), / ˌsaʊ sʊˈwɛst / (BrE)
the compass point that lies halfway between south and southwest, corresponding to a bearing of 202.5 degrees
compass point between south and southwest

Examples

The ship sailed towards sou'-sou'-west to avoid the storm.
We should set our course for sou'-sou'-west to reach the island.
The wind was coming from the sou'-sou'-west all afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: south-southwest - the compass point halfway between south and southwest

Etymology

Altered form of 'south-southwest', from combining 'south' and 'southwest'.

toward the south-southwest

Examples
