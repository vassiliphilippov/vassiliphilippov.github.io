Zagreb

1. PROPN

/ ˈzɑːˌɡrɛb / (AmE), / ˈzɑːɡrɛb / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Croatia, situated in the northwest of the country
capital city of Croatia

Examples

She spent a semester studying abroad in Zagreb and fell in love with its vibrant culture.
The international conference will be hosted in Zagreb next summer.
Flights from London to Zagreb have become more frequent in recent years.

Etymology

From Croatian Zagreb, first attested in the 11th century.
