Lego

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡəʊ / (BrE)
(trademark) a type of plastic construction toy consisting of interlocking bricks, used to build models or structures, especially by children.
trademark plastic construction toy set

Examples

My nephew loves building skyscrapers out of Lego.
The children spent the afternoon playing with their new Lego set.
Many adults collect rare Lego kits as a hobby.

Etymology

Coined by the Danish company; from Danish 'leg godt' meaning 'play well'.
