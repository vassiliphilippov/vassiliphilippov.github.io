unhardened

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhɑrdənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɑːd(ə)nd / (BrE)
not made hard or not brought to its final degree of hardness; not fully set or solidified.
not made hard; not fully solidified

Examples

The walls were constructed using unhardened mortar, which made them less stable during the storm.
Always store unhardened steel separately, as it is more susceptible to damage and corrosion.
Scientists examined the unhardened layer of lava left after the volcanic eruption.

Synset Examples

untempered mortar
untempered steel

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hardened - made hard or tough; has reached final hardness
