reclaimable

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈkleɪməbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈkleɪməbl / (BrE)
able to be recovered, reused, or restored to a useful condition
able to be recovered or reused

Examples

Much of the scrap metal from the demolition was reclaimable and sent to recycling plants.
The engineer designed the system to filter reclaimable water for irrigation purposes.
Some materials in the landfill are still reclaimable despite years of burial.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reusable - able to be used again

Etymology

From reclaim + -able, following the pattern of forming adjectives meaning 'capable of being'.
