thirty-seventh

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɝːtiˈsɛvənθ / (AmE), / ˌθɜːtiˈsɛvənθ / (BrE)
ordinal number corresponding to thirty-seven in a sequence; 37th
ordinal number: 37th

Examples

She finished in thirty-seventh place in the national spelling competition.
The thirty-seventh chapter of the book reveals the main twist.
His birthday falls on the thirty-seventh day of the year.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirty-sixth - the ordinal number 36th in a sequence
  • Degree Higher: thirty-eighth - the ordinal number 38th in a sequence
