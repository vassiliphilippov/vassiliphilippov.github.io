other

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌðɚ / (AmE), / ˈʌðə / (BrE)
(used with a time reference) referring to a recent period or event that has just passed; the one shortly before now.
recently past (with time reference)

Examples

I saw her other evening at the concert.
Do you remember what we discussed the other day?
The weather was lovely the other week when we went hiking.

2. ADJ

/ ˈʌðɚ / (AmE), / ˈʌðə / (BrE)
Very unusual; different in character or quality from what is normal or expected.
very unusual or different

Examples

Scientists theorize about other dimensions that could exist beyond our universe.
In her painting, she tried to capture a sense of something wholly other than everyday reality.
His experience was so other that he struggled to explain it to anyone.
