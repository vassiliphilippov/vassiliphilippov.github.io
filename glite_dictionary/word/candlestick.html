candlestick

1. NOUN

/ ˈkændl̩ˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈkændl̩ˌstɪk / (BrE)
a holder with one or more sockets for candles.
holder for candles

Examples

The elegant silver candlestick sat in the center of the dining table.
During the power outage, she lit a candlestick to illuminate the room.
He inherited an antique brass candlestick from his grandmother.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: candleholder - an object designed to hold a candle

Etymology

From candle + stick, first used in Middle English.
