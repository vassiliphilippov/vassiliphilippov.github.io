edgeless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛdʒ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈɛdʒ.ləs / (BrE)
having no sharp or cutting edge; not having a defined edge
without a sharp or defined edge

Examples

The edgeless knife was completely useless for cutting bread.
Her design featured edgeless tables to ensure safety in the playroom.
The sculpture's edgeless form gave it a smooth, modern appearance.

Related Terms
