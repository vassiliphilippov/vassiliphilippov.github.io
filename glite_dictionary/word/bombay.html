Bombay

1. PROPN

/ bɑmˈbeɪ / (AmE), / bɒmˈbeɪ / (BrE)
the former English name for Mumbai, a major city and port in western India on the Arabian Sea
former name for Mumbai, major Indian city

Examples

The colonial architecture in Bombay attracts tourists from all over the world.
After independence, Bombay became a center for commerce and industry.
His family moved from Bombay to London in the 1960s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mumbai - current official name for the city formerly known as Bombay

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Bombaim', itself from the local Marathi 'Mumbai'.
