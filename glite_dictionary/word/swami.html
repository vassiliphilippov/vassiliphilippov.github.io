swami

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɑːmi / (AmE), / ˈswɑːmi / (BrE)
a Hindu religious teacher, monk, or spiritual leader; used as a title of respect
Hindu religious teacher or monk

Examples

The swami delivered a sermon on compassion and nonviolence to his followers.
Many travelers visited the ashram to seek guidance from the swami.
He studied meditation for years under the guidance of a respected swami.

Etymology

From Sanskrit svāmī (owner, master, lord), adopted into English via Hindi.
