hula-hula

1. NOUN

/ ˌhuːləˈhuːlə / (AmE), / ˌhuːləˈhuːlə / (BrE)
a traditional Polynesian dance performed by a woman, characterized by rhythmic movements of the hips and hands, often as part of storytelling or ceremonial events
traditional Polynesian dance

Examples

The women performed a beautiful hula-hula at the cultural festival.
She learned the hula-hula during her trip to Hawaii.
The hula-hula tells a story through graceful movements and gestures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hula - Hawaiian dance with hip and hand movements

Etymology

From Hawaiian 'hula' meaning 'dance'.
