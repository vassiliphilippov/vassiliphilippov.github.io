grass-eating

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡræs ˌiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːs ˌiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an animal) feeding on grass; herbivorous.
feeding on grass

Examples

The grass-eating animals roamed the plains searching for fresh pastures.
She studied the digestive systems of grass-eating mammals like cows and zebras.
Pandas are not grass-eating animals, as their main diet consists of bamboo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: herbivorous - feeding on plants
