shitguzzler

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtˌɡʌzlər / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtˌɡʌzlə / (BrE)
(Vulgar slang) A person who drinks beer, or sometimes other beverages, to excess.
vulgar slang for heavy beer drinker
slang vulgar

Examples

When the pub opens, he's known as a shitguzzler among his friends.
The party was full of shitguzzlers trying to outdrink each other.
His reputation as a shitguzzler made him a legend in the local bar.

Synset Examples

He's a real shitguzzler when it comes to beer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drunkard - a person who drinks alcohol to excess
  • Antonym: teetotaler - a person who abstains from alcohol
