Gosainthan

1. PROPN

/ ˌgoʊsaɪnˈtɑn / (AmE), / ˌgəʊsaɪnˈtɑːn / (BrE)
a mountain in the Himalayas in Tibet, rising to 26,290 feet (8,013 meters)
a high Himalayan mountain in Tibet

Examples

The expedition team reached Gosainthan after weeks of trekking through difficult terrain.
Many climbers dream of summiting Gosainthan and witnessing its breathtaking views.
Gosainthan is also known as Shishapangma in Tibet.

Etymology

From Nepali 'Gosainthan', meaning 'Abode of God' or 'Holy place'.
