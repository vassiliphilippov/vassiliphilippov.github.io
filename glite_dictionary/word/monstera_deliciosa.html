Monstera deliciosa

1. NOUN

/ mɒnˈstɛrə dɛˌlɪʃiˈoʊsə / (AmE), / mɒnˈstɪərə dɪˌlɪsiˈəʊsə / (BrE)
A species of tropical American vine with large, perforated leaves and edible fruit that tastes like pineapple and banana.
tropical vine with edible fruit

Examples

The Monstera deliciosa is prized for its dramatic split leaves in stylish home decor.
Many people do not realize that the fruit of the Monstera deliciosa is actually edible when ripe.
She placed her new Monstera deliciosa near the window to ensure it got enough sunlight.

Etymology

From Latin 'monstera' (monster or abnormal, referencing the perforated leaves) and 'deliciosa' (delicious, referring to the fruit).
