papermaking

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpərˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəməɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the craft or process of making paper, especially as an industrial or artistic activity
the craft or process of making paper

Examples

The town's economy relied heavily on the art of papermaking.
She studied traditional papermaking techniques in Japan.
New advances in papermaking have improved the quality of recycled products.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paper mill - a factory where paper is made

Etymology

From 'paper' + 'making', first attested in the late 18th century.
