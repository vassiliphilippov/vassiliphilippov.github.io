apartment

1. NOUN

/ əˈpɑːrt.mənt / (AmE), / əˈpɑːt.mənt / (BrE)
A set of rooms forming a separate residence within a building, typically on one floor.
a set of rooms in a building; separate residence

Examples

She found a lovely apartment near the city center.
We moved into a new apartment last month.
His apartment has a beautiful view of the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flat - a British term for an apartment

Etymology

From French *appartement*, from Italian *appartamento* meaning 'a separated place.'
