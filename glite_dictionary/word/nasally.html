nasally

1. ADV

/ ˈneɪzəli / (AmE), / ˈneɪzəli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or through the nose.
in a nasal manner or through the nose

Examples

The medicine should be administered nasally rather than orally.
She tends to speak nasally when she has a cold.
Certain scents are more easily detected nasally than by taste.

Synset Examples

inhaled nasally

Etymology

From 'nasal' + '-ly', formed from the Latin 'nasus' (nose).
