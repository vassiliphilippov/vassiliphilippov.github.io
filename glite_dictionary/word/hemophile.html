hemophile

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiːməˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈhiːməˌfaɪl / (BrE)
a person who has hemophilia and is prone to uncontrollable bleeding
person with hemophilia
medical

Examples

Her nephew is a hemophile who needs regular medical treatment.
Many hemophiles participate in clinical trials for new therapies.
The camp offered special support for children who are hemophiles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hemophiliac - a person affected by hemophilia

Etymology

From hemophilia + -phile, after medical terminology.
