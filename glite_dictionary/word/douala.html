Douala

1. PROPN

/ duˈɑːlə / (AmE), / duˈɑːlə / (BrE)
the largest city and commercial capital of Cameroon, located on the country's Atlantic coast.
largest city of Cameroon

Examples

Many international companies have their African headquarters in Douala.
The port of Douala is vital for Cameroon's import and export trade.
During my travels, I spent three days exploring the bustling markets of Douala.
