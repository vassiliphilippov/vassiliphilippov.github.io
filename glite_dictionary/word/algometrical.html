algometrical

1. ADJ

/ ˌælɡəˈmɛtrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌalɡəˈmɛtrɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving algometry, especially in the measurement of pain sensitivity.
relating to algometry (pain measurement)
medical

Examples

The doctor used algometrical methods to assess the patient's pain threshold.
Recent studies have improved the accuracy of algometrical instruments in clinical trials.
His research focused on the algometrical evaluation of migraine sufferers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: algometric - relating to algometry

Etymology

From algometry + -ical.
