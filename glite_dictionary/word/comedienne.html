comedienne

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑː.məˈdiː.ɛn / (AmE), / ˌkɒ.məˈdiː.ɛn / (BrE)
a woman who performs in comedy as an actor or comedian
female comedian or comic actress

Examples

The famous comedienne captivated the audience with her witty jokes.
Over the years, she became known as a pioneering comedienne in television.
Many aspiring actresses admire the path she paved as a talented comedienne.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comedian - a person who performs in comedy

Etymology

From French 'comédienne' (female comedian), feminine of 'comédien'.

2. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːməˈdiːˌɛn / (AmE), / ˌkɒmɪˈdiːˌɛn / (BrE)
a woman who performs comedy, especially as a professional entertainer
female stand-up comedian or comic actor

Examples

The comedienne captivated the audience with her sharp wit and timing.
She became the first female comedienne to headline at the prestigious comedy club.
Awards were given to the best actor, actress, and comedienne of the year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comedian - a person who performs comedy

Etymology

From French 'comédienne', feminine of 'comédien' (comedian).
