expeditiousness

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛk.spəˈdɪʃ.əs.nəs / (AmE), / ˌɛk.spɪˈdɪʃ.əs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being prompt and efficient in action or movement
promptness and efficiency

Examples

The expeditiousness of the emergency response saved many lives after the disaster.
She admired the expeditiousness with which the team completed the project.
The CEO expects a certain level of expeditiousness in all company operations.

Synset Examples

it was done with dispatch

Related Terms

  • Synonym: efficiency - the state or quality of being efficient
  • Antonym: slowness - the quality of being slow

Etymology

From expeditious + -ness, expeditious from Latin expeditus (unimpeded, ready).
