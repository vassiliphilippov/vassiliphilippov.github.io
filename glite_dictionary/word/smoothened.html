smoothened

1. ADJ

/ ˈsmuːðənd / (AmE), / ˈsmuːðənd / (BrE)
made smooth, typically by ironing, rubbing, or pressing; rendered free of wrinkles or roughness.
made smooth, especially by ironing

Examples

The neatly smoothened fabric was ready to be sewn into a dress.
He admired the smoothened surface of the antique table after it was polished.
Every shirt was smoothened by the housekeeper before being placed in the closet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoothed - made smooth; having had its surface made even or uniform

Etymology

Formed from 'smoothen' (to make smooth), itself from 'smooth' + -en (verb-forming suffix).
