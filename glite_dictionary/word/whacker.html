whacker

1. NOUN

/ ˈwækɚ / (AmE), / ˈwækə / (BrE)
Something that is especially large or impressive of its kind.
something big or impressive
informal

Examples

He caught a whacker of a fish during his trip to the lake.
That new skyscraper downtown is a real whacker compared to the rest.
We saw a whacker of a storm roll in last night, with thunder shaking the windows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monster - an unusually large thing
  • Synonym: giant - something very large
