Angelo Giuseppe Roncalli

1. PROPN

/ ˈændʒəˌloʊ dʒuˈzɛp.eɪ rɒnˈkɑːli / (AmE), / ˈan.dʒə.ləʊ dʒuːˈzɛp.eɪ rɒnˈkɑː.li / (BrE)
(1881–1963) Italian prelate who served as Pope John XXIII from 1958 to 1963 and initiated the Second Vatican Council.
Pope John XXIII, Italian pope (1958–1963)
