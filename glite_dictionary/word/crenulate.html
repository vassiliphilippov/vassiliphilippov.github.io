crenulate

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɛnjələt / (AmE), / ˈkrɛnjʊlət / (BrE)
having a margin with small, rounded teeth or notches, especially referring to the edge of leaves or shells
having small, rounded notches on the edge

Examples

The crenulate leaves of the herb gave its edge a delicate, decorative appearance.
A shell with a crenulate margin can be distinguished from one with a smooth edge.
The species is identified by its crenulate petals, each lined with tiny, rounded lobes.

Synset Examples

a crenulate leaf

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crenate - having a scalloped or rounded-toothed edge

Etymology

From Latin 'crenulatus', diminutive of 'crena' (notch).
