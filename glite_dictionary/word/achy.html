achy

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪki / (AmE), / ˈeɪki / (BrE)
causing a dull, persistent pain; feeling sore or uncomfortable
having a dull, persistent pain

Examples

After hiking all day, my legs felt achy and tired.
She woke up with an achy back after sleeping on the old mattress.
Whenever a cold is coming on, he gets achy all over his body.

Synset Examples

my aching head
her old achy joints

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sore - painful or uncomfortable, especially when touched
  • Synonym: painful - causing or experiencing physical pain
