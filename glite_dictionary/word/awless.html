awless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔlɪs / (AmE), / ˈɔːlɪs / (BrE)
Lacking any sense of awe or reverence.
without awe or reverence
archaic

Examples

He approached the ancient ruins in an awless manner, showing no trace of respect.
Her awless attitude during the ceremony surprised the elders.
In their awless youth, they laughed at traditions others held sacred.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: reverent - showing deep respect or awe

2. ADJ

/ ˈɔlɪs / (AmE), / ˈɔːlɪs / (BrE)
lacking awe or respect; not feeling or showing reverence
lacking awe or respect

Examples

His awless attitude shocked the elders at the ceremony.
The critic's awless review spared no feelings and respected no reputation.
She spoke in an awless tone, unconcerned by the tradition she challenged.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: respectful - showing respect or reverence

Etymology

Formed from 'awe' + suffix '-less', meaning 'without awe'.
