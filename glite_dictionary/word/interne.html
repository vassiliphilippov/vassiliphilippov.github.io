interne

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnˌtɜrn / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌtɜːn / (BrE)
an advanced student or recent graduate in medicine who works in a hospital to gain supervised practical experience
medical trainee in a hospital
medical

Examples

As an interne, she rotated through different hospital departments to broaden her experience.
The interne was responsible for monitoring patients under the supervision of the resident physician.
During his year as an interne, he developed valuable clinical skills in emergency medicine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intern - a medical trainee working in a hospital

Etymology

From French interne, from Latin internus (inward, internal).
