macamba

1. NOUN

/ məˈkæmbə / (AmE), / məˈkæmbə / (BrE)
A tropical American feather palm with a swollen, spiny trunk and edible nuts.
tropical American feather palm

Examples

The trunk of the macamba is covered with sharp spines to deter animals.
Locals harvest the edible nuts from the macamba each season.
A row of macambas lined the edge of the tropical plantation.
