purplish-white

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝːplɪʃ waɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːplɪʃ waɪt / (BrE)
that is white with a slight hint or tinge of purple
white tinged with purple

Examples

Under the moonlight, the clouds took on a purplish-white hue.
She wore a dress made of soft purplish-white silk.
The rose petals faded from deep violet to a delicate purplish-white shade.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: lavender - a pale purple color
