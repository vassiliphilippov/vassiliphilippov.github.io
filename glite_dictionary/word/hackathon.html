hackathon

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæk.əˌθɑn / (AmE), / ˈhæk.əˌθɒn / (BrE)
An event, typically lasting several hours or days, in which a large number of people meet to engage in collaborative computer programming and software development.
event for collaborative programming

Examples

Our team developed an innovative app during the college hackathon last weekend.
She stayed up all night at the hackathon to finish her project before the deadline.
The company hosts an annual hackathon to encourage creative solutions and teamwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: codefest - An event where programmers collaborate intensively on software projects

Etymology

Blend of 'hack' (as in computer programming) and 'marathon'.
