emblematical

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛm.bləˈmæt.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌɛm.bləˈmæt.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
serving as a visible symbol for something abstract; symbolic.
serving as a symbol; symbolic

Examples

The olive branch is often regarded as emblematical of peace throughout Western culture.
Her speech was rich in emblematical references to hope and unity.
The painting featured an emblematical lion, representing strength and courage.

Synset Examples

a crown is emblematic of royalty
the spinning wheel was as symbolic of colonical Massachusetts as the codfish

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emblematic - serving as a visible symbol for something abstract
  • Synonym: symbolic - serving as a symbol or representation

Etymology

From Medieval Latin emblematicalis, from Greek *emblēmatikos* (pertaining to an ornament or inlaid work).
