Alkeran

1. NOUN

/ ˈæl.kəˌræn / (AmE), / ˈæl.kəˌran / (BrE)
a trademarked antineoplastic drug (melphalan) used to treat multiple myeloma and certain other cancers
trade name for a cancer chemotherapy drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Alkeran as part of her cancer treatment regimen.
Patients receiving Alkeran are closely monitored for side effects.
Long-term use of Alkeran requires regular blood tests to check for toxicity.
