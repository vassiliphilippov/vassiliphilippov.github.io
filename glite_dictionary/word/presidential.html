presidential

1. ADJ

/ ˌprɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌprɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl / (BrE)
Having qualities or a manner suitable for a president; befitting a president.
befitting a president

Examples

Many voters expect their leader to act in a presidential manner during a crisis.
His presidential demeanor impressed the members of the committee.
The candidate's speech was widely regarded as very presidential.

Synset Examples

criticized the candidate for not looking presidential

Related Terms

  • Synonym: president - the elected head of a republican state

Etymology

From president + -ial, modeled on similar Latin-based adjectives.

/ ˌprɛz.ɪˈdɛn.ʃəl / (AmE), / ˌprɛz.ɪˈdɛn.ʃəl / (BrE)
relating to a president or the presidency.
relating to a president or presidency

Examples

The country is preparing for the upcoming presidential election this year.
Several presidential candidates participated in the televised debate.
His experience as a presidential aide gave him insight into national politics.

Synset Examples

presidential aides
presidential veto

Related Terms

  • Synonym: president - the elected head of a republican state

Etymology

From French 'présidentiel', from 'président'.
