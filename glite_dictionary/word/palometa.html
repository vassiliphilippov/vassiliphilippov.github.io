Palometa

1. NOUN

/ ˌpæloʊˈmeɪtə / (AmE), / ˌpaləˈmiːtə / (BrE)
A genus of fish in the family Stromateidae, commonly known as butterfishes, found in tropical and subtropical waters.
genus of butterfish in Stromateidae

Examples

The Palometa genus includes several species commonly found in tropical oceans.
Marine biologists study the Palometa for its unique adaptations within the Stromateidae family.
The silvery appearance of many Palometa species helps them blend into open water environments.

Etymology

Latinized form of Spanish 'palometa', a term for certain types of fish.
