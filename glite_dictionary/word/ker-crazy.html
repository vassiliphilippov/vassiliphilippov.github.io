ker-crazy

1. ADJ

/ kɝˈkreɪzi / (AmE), / kəˈkreɪzi / (BrE)
Extremely hyperactive or excited, often in a wild or uncontrolled way.
extremely hyper or excited
informal slang

Examples

After winning the game, the whole team went ker-crazy in the locker room.
The kids acted ker-crazy every time they visited the amusement park.
He became ker-crazy when he heard the news about the surprise party.

Synset Examples

The kids were ker-crazy after too much candy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hyperactive - very active, unable to relax or be still
  • Synonym: excited - very enthusiastic and eager
