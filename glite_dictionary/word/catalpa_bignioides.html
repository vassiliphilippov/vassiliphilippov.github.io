Catalpa bignioides

1. NOUN

/ kəˈtæl.pə bɪɡˌni.oʊˈiː.diz / (AmE), / kəˈtal.pə bɪɡ.nɪ.əʊˈiː.diːz / (BrE)
A species of catalpa tree native to the southeastern United States, known for its large heart-shaped leaves and showy white flowers.
species of catalpa tree from the southeastern U.S.

Examples

The Catalpa bignioides is a popular ornamental tree due to its striking flowers.
In the southern United States, Catalpa bignioides is often planted for shade and decoration.
The wood of Catalpa bignioides is sometimes used in carpentry for its durability.

Etymology

From the genus name Catalpa, and species name from Latin, bignioides.
