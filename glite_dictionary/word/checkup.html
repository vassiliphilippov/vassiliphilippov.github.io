checkup

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛk.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛk.ʌp / (BrE)
a thorough medical examination to assess a person's general health
medical examination

Examples

You should schedule a yearly checkup with your doctor to monitor your health.
After his checkup, the physician said everything looked good.
Many schools require a physical checkup before students can join sports teams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: examination - the act of inspecting or testing something medically
  • Synonym: screening - testing for disease in people without symptoms

Etymology

From the verb 'check' (to examine) + 'up'; first used in early 20th-century American English.
