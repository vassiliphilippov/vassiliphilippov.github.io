to utilize

1. VERB

/ ˈjuːtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈjuːtɪlaɪz / (BrE)
to make practical or effective use of something for a specific purpose
make use of something for a purpose

Examples

The company plans to utilize new technology to improve efficiency.
Researchers utilized several methods to analyze the data.
We must utilize all available resources to solve the problem.

Related Terms

Etymology
