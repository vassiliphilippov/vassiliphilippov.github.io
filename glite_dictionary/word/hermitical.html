hermitical

1. ADJ

/ hɚˈmɪtɪkəl / (AmE), / həˈmɪtɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of a hermit or of living in solitude, especially for religious or ascetic reasons.
characteristic of a hermit or solitary living

Examples

She led a hermitical existence in the mountains, far from any town or city.
The hermitical traditions of the monks fascinated historians.
His hermitical lifestyle was marked by silence and meditation.

Synset Examples

the eremitic element in the life of a religious colony
his hermitic existence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eremitic - relating to the life of a hermit

Etymology

From hermit + -ical, based on Greek erēmitēs (desert-dweller).
