verbal

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɝː.bəl / (AmE), / ˈvɜː.bəl / (BrE)
expressed in spoken words rather than in writing
expressed in spoken words

Examples

They reached a verbal agreement without signing any documents.
A verbal promise is not always legally binding.
She gave him a verbal warning about his behavior.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: written - expressed in writing

Etymology

From Latin 'verbalis' (pertaining to words), from 'verbum' (word).
