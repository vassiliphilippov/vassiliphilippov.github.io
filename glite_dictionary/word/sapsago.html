sapsago

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæpˌseɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsæp.sə.ɡəʊ / (BrE)
a hard, green Swiss cheese made from skim-milk curd and flavored with dried clover leaves, particularly melilot
a hard, green Swiss cheese

Examples

The chef grated sapsago over the salad to give it a unique, tangy flavor.
Have you ever tried sapsago in traditional Swiss dishes?
She bought a block of sapsago during her trip to Switzerland.

Etymology

From Swiss German 'Schabziger', literally 'green cheese', via older English forms.
