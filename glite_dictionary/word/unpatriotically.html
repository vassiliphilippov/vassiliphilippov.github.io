unpatriotically

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnˌpeɪtriˈɑtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌpætriˈɒtɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of patriotism or loyalty to one's country.
in an unpatriotic manner

Examples

He was criticized for acting unpatriotically during the national crisis.
The article argued unpatriotically against supporting the country’s efforts.
Some accused her of speaking unpatriotically about government policies abroad.

Synset Examples

unpatriotically he contrived a way of avoiding military service

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unpatriotic - not having or showing love for one's country
