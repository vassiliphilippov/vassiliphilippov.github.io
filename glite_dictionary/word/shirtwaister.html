shirtwaister

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɜrtˌweɪstər / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːtˌweɪstə / (BrE)
A blouse or dress with buttons down the front, typically resembling a tailored shirt; chiefly British term for shirtwaist.
blouse or dress with front buttons (chiefly British)
british

Examples

She wore a crisp shirtwaister with pearl buttons to the interview.
Classic shirtwaister styles have remained popular in British fashion over the decades.
Do you think this blue shirtwaister would be suitable for the summer party?

Synset Examples

in Britain they call a shirtwaist a ‘shirtwaister’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shirtwaist - A woman's blouse or dress with buttons down the front and a tailored style

Etymology

Formed from 'shirtwaist' with the suffix '-er'; chiefly British adaptation.
