osteal

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːstiəl / (AmE), / ˈɒstiəl / (BrE)
relating to, consisting of, or containing bone; osseous.
relating to bone
medical

Examples

The surgeon noted several osteal fragments in the area of the fracture.
Some tumors exhibit osteal differentiation, producing bone-like tissue.
The dentist examined the osteal support of the patient's teeth using X-rays.

Synset Examples

osseous tissue

Related Terms

  • Synonym: osseous - consisting of or turned into bone; bony

Etymology

From Greek osteon (bone) + -al (forming adjectives).

/ ˈɑːstiəl / (AmE), / ˈɒstiəl / (BrE)

relating to bone; skeletal
medical

Examples

Osteal lesions are often visible on radiographs in patients with bone diseases.
