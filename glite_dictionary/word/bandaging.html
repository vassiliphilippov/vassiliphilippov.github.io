bandaging

1. NOUN

/ ˈbændɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbændɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of applying a bandage to a wound, injury, or part of the body
applying a bandage

Examples

She was trained in bandaging wounds during her first aid course.
Quick and careful bandaging can help prevent infection in minor injuries.
After the accident, the nurse spent several minutes bandaging his arm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressing - the act of cleaning and covering a wound
