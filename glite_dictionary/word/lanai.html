Lanai

1. PROPN

/ ləˈnaɪ / (AmE), / ləˈnaɪ / (BrE)
An island in central Hawaii, known for its pineapple plantations and scenic landscapes.
island of central Hawaii

Examples

We spent our vacation exploring Lanai and its beautiful beaches.
The island of Lanai is famous for its former pineapple plantations.
Wild deer roam freely across Lanai's quiet, scenic roads.

Etymology

From Hawaiian 'Lānaʻi', the name of the island.
