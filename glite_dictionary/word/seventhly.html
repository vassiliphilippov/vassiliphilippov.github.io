seventhly

1. ADV

/ ˈsɛvənθli / (AmE), / ˈsɛvənθli / (BrE)
Used to introduce the seventh point or item in a list or argument.
in the seventh place (enumeration)

Examples

Seventhly, you have no right to cancel the lease in mid-year.
He listed his arguments in detail: firstly, secondly, and finally seventhly.
Seventhly, we must consider the environmental impact of the proposal.

Synset Examples

seventhly, you have no right to cancel the lease in mid-year

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sixthly - used to introduce the sixth point in a series
  • Degree Higher: eighthly - used to introduce the eighth point in a series

Etymology

Formed by adding the adverbial -ly to 'seventh', patterned after sequence adverbs like 'firstly', 'secondly'.
