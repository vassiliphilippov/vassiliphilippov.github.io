alphanumeric

1. ADJ

/ ˌæl.fə.nuːˈmɛr.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌal.fə.njuːˈmɛr.ɪk / (BrE)
Consisting of or relating to both letters and numbers.
involving letters and numbers

Examples

Please use an alphanumeric password containing both letters and numbers.
Serial numbers typically consist of alphanumeric codes for better security.
The username must be alphanumeric and at least six characters long.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: numeric - consisting of or relating to numbers
  • Antonym: alphabetic - consisting of or relating to letters of the alphabet

Etymology

From Latin 'alphabetum' (alphabet) and 'numericus' (of numbers).
