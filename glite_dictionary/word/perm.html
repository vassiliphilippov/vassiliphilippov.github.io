Perm

1. PROPN

/ pɜrm / (AmE), / pɜːm / (BrE)
a city in western Russia, the administrative center of Perm Krai, located on the Kama River near the Ural Mountains
city in western Russia

Examples

Perm is situated on the western slopes of the Ural Mountains.
The train from Moscow to Perm takes about 24 hours.
Many industries in Perm benefit from the city's proximity to natural resources.
