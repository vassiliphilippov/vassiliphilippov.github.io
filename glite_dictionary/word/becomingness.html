becomingness

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈkʌmɪŋnəs / (AmE), / bɪˈkʌmɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being suitable, appropriate, or attractive in appearance or manner
the quality of being suitable or attractive

Examples

The becomingness of her attire impressed everyone at the event.
His speech had a becomingness that suited the formal occasion.
The artist admired the becomingness of the delicate arrangement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: becoming - suitable or attractive in appearance or manner

Etymology

Formed from 'becoming' (suitable, appropriate) + '-ness' (forming nouns denoting a state or quality).
