Tai Yuan

1. NOUN

/ taɪ ˈjuː.ən / (AmE), / taɪ ˈjʊə.n / (BrE)
A branch of the Tai languages spoken mainly in northern Thailand, specifically in regions such as Chiang Mai, Chiang Rai, Lamphun, Lampang, and others.
Tai language spoken in northern Thailand

Examples

Many people in Chiang Mai use Tai Yuan in their daily conversations alongside Thai.
She is studying Tai Yuan to better understand northern Thai culture.
The Tai Yuan language has unique tones and vocabulary distinct from standard Thai.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Northern Thai - another term for the Tai Yuan language
