godson

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑd.sʌn / (AmE), / ˈɡɒd.sʌn / (BrE)
A male child whose baptism is sponsored by a godparent.
male godchild

Examples

My nephew became my godson when I agreed to be his godparent.
She bought a special gift for her godson on his birthday.
After the christening, everyone welcomed their new godson into the family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: godchild - a child sponsored by a godparent at baptism
  • Antonym: goddaughter - a female child sponsored by a godparent

Etymology

From 'god' and 'son', indicating the relationship created by baptismal sponsorship.
