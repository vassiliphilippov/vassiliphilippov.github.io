Qizil Qum

1. PROPN

/ kɪˌzɪl ˈkuːm / (AmE), / kɪˌzɪl ˈkuːm / (BrE)
a large desert in Uzbekistan, lying southeast of the Aral Sea
desert in Uzbekistan

Examples

The Qizil Qum is known for its vast stretches of red sand dunes.
Scientists have studied the unique flora and fauna of the Qizil Qum.
Several ancient trade routes once passed through the Qizil Qum desert.

Etymology

From Uzbek 'Qizilqum', meaning 'Red Sand'.
