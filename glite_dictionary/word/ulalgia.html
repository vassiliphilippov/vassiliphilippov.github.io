ulalgia

1. NOUN

/ juːˈlældʒiə / (AmE), / juːˈlældʒiə / (BrE)
pain in the gums, especially as a medical condition
pain in the gums (medical)
medical

Examples

The dentist diagnosed her with ulalgia after examining her swollen gums.
Persistent ulalgia may be a sign of gum disease.
He experienced severe ulalgia whenever he brushed his teeth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gingivalgia - medical term for gum pain

Etymology

From Latin 'ulā' (gum) and Greek 'algos' (pain).
