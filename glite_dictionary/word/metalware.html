metalware

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛtəlˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəlˌweə / (BrE)
objects or utensils made of metal, especially household or table items such as cutlery, trays, or serving dishes.
household items made of metal

Examples

The antique store had a beautiful collection of metalware on display.
She polished the silver metalware before setting the table for dinner.
Most hotels use stainless steel metalware in their dining rooms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tableware - dishes or utensils used at the table

Etymology

From metal + ware, where 'ware' means manufactured goods or articles.
