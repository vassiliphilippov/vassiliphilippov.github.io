histologically

1. ADV

/ hɪstəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / hɪstəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to the microscopic structure of tissues or using histological methods
relating to tissue structure or histology
medical

Examples

The tumor was histologically assessed to determine its type and grade.
Some conditions are histologically distinct, even when clinically similar.
The tissue samples appeared normal histologically after the treatment.

Synset Examples

histologically identifiable structures

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microscopically - in a manner relating to using a microscope

Etymology

From 'histology' (the study of tissues) + '-ically' (forming adverbs).
