goatsbeard

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊtsˌbɪrd / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊtsˌbɪəd / (BrE)
a weedy European plant with yellow flowers, often found naturalized in the United States, belonging to the genus Tragopogon
a European plant with yellow flowers

Examples

The fields were dotted with goatsbeard, their yellow blossoms bright in the morning sun.
After a few weeks, the goatsbeard had taken over the abandoned garden.
She identified the wildflower as goatsbeard from its distinctive fluffy seed head.

Etymology

Named for the appearance of the plant's seed head, which resembles a goat's beard.
