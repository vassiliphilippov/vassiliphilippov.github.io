Denali

1. PROPN

/ dəˈnɑːli / (AmE), / dɪˈnɑːli / (BrE)
the highest mountain in North America, located in south-central Alaska, formerly known as Mount McKinley
highest mountain in North America

Examples

Many mountaineers dream of reaching the summit of Denali in Alaska.
The weather conditions on Denali can change rapidly, making climbing dangerous.
Denali National Park attracts thousands of visitors each year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mount McKinley - former name of Denali, the highest mountain in North America

Etymology

From Koyukon Athabaskan, meaning 'the high one' or 'the great one.'
