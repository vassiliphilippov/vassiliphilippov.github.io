irreplaceableness

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪrɪˈpleɪsəblnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈpleɪsəblnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being impossible to replace; uniqueness or indispensability.
quality of being impossible to replace

Examples

The irreplaceableness of historic artifacts makes their preservation essential.
She emphasized the irreplaceableness of her team's contribution to the project.
He realized the irreplaceableness of true friendship after moving to a new city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irreplaceability - the quality of being irreplaceable

Etymology

From irreplaceable + -ness (forming nouns indicating a state or quality).
