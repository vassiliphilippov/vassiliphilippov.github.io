Sarawakian

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɑːrəˈweɪkiən / (AmE), / ˌsærəˈweɪkiən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Sarawak, a state in Malaysia on the island of Borneo
person from Sarawak, Malaysia

Examples

The Sarawakian welcomed us warmly into her home.
Many Sarawakians celebrate the Gawai Dayak festival in June.
He is proud to be a Sarawakian, sharing his culture with visitors.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Malaysian - a native or inhabitant of Malaysia

/ səˌrɑːˈweɪ.ki.ən / (AmE), / səˌræˈweɪ.ki.ən / (BrE)
of or relating to Sarawak, its people, or their culture
of or relating to Sarawak or its people

Examples

The Sarawakian rainforest is renowned for its rich biodiversity.
Many Sarawakian dishes feature aromatic spices and fresh seafood.
He was fascinated by traditional Sarawakian crafts and textiles.

Synset Examples

Sarawakian beaches

Related Terms

Etymology
