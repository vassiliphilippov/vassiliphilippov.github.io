Dillenia

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈlɛniə / (AmE), / dɪˈlɛniə / (BrE)
A genus of East Indian and Australian shrubs and trees, known for their panicles of large white or yellow flowers.
a genus of tropical flowering trees or shrubs

Examples

Several species of Dillenia are cultivated in tropical gardens for their attractive flowers.
The fruit of the Dillenia tree is often used in traditional medicines in Asia.
Botanists classified the new specimen under the genus Dillenia after detailed analysis.

Etymology

Named after Johann Jacob Dillenius (1684–1747), a German botanist.
