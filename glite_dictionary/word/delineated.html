delineated

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈlɪniˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪniːeɪtɪd / (BrE)
accurately or precisely depicted, described, or represented
depicted or described accurately

Examples

The boundaries between the two countries are clearly delineated on the map.
The architect presented a delineated plan for the new museum.
Her research offers a well delineated explanation of the process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: described - depicted or explained in words
  • Degree Lower: sketched - represented in a rough or outline form
  • Degree Higher: detailed - represented with all the small particular features

Etymology

Past participle of 'delineate', from Latin 'delineare' (to sketch out).
