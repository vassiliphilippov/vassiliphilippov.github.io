aerogramme

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr.əˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈeə.rəˌɡræm / (BrE)
A lightweight letter sheet, often prepaid, designed for sending by airmail without an envelope.
an airmail letter sheet

Examples

He decided to send an aerogramme to his family while traveling abroad.
The aerogramme folded neatly into a self-sealing letter for international delivery.
In the past, writing an aerogramme was a popular way to keep in touch over long distances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airmail - system of sending letters by air transport

Etymology

From French 'aérogramme', combining 'aéro-' (air) and 'gramme' (written letter).
