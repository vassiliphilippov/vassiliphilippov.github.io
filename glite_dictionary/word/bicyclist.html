bicyclist

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪsɪklɪst / (AmE), / ˈbɪsɪklɪst / (BrE)
a person who rides a bicycle, especially as a hobby or sport
person who rides a bicycle

Examples

The bicyclist carefully navigated the busy intersection on her morning commute.
After the race, every bicyclist received a medal for participating.
A group of bicyclists gathered at the park for their weekly ride.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cyclist - a person who rides a bicycle or motorbike

Etymology

Formed from 'bicycle' and the agent suffix '-ist', meaning one who practices or is concerned with something.
