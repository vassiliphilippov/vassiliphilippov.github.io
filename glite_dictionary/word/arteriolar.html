arteriolar

1. ADJ

/ ɑrˈtɪriələr / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriələ / (BrE)
relating to, affecting, or involving arterioles (small branches of arteries)
relating to small arteries (arterioles)
medical

Examples

Arteriolar changes can be seen in patients with chronic hypertension.
The biopsy revealed arteriolar thickening in the affected tissue.
Researchers studied the arteriolar response to various medications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arteriole - a small branch of an artery leading into capillaries

Etymology

From arteriole, itself derived from Latin ‘arteriola’ (small artery), plus the suffix -ar.
