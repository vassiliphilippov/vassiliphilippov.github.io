Chimonanthus

1. NOUN

/ kaɪmoʊˈnænθəs / (AmE), / kɪməˈnænθəs / (BrE)
A small genus of Asian deciduous or evergreen shrubs with fragrant flowers, known as wintersweet.
genus of Asian fragrant shrubs (wintersweet)

Examples

The Chimonanthus blooms in midwinter, filling the garden with its sweet scent.
Gardeners often plant Chimonanthus for its beautiful and fragrant winter flowers.
A mature Chimonanthus shrub can be a centerpiece in a winter landscape.

Etymology

From Greek 'cheimon' (winter) + 'anthos' (flower), referring to its winter-blooming flowers.
