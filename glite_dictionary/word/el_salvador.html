El Salvador

1. PROPN

/ ɛl ˌsæl.vəˈdɔr / (AmE), / ɛl ˌsæl.vəˈdɔː / (BrE)
a country in Central America, located on the Pacific coast, known officially as the Republic of El Salvador
country in Central America

Examples

Tourists often visit El Salvador to experience its beautiful beaches and volcanic landscapes.
Coffee from El Salvador is highly regarded for its quality and flavor.
She grew up in El Salvador before moving to the United States.

Etymology

From Spanish, meaning 'The Savior'.
