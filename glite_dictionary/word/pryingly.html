pryingly

1. ADV

/ ˈpraɪ.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈpraɪ.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
in a way that shows excessive curiosity or intrusiveness about other people's private affairs
in an excessively curious manner

Examples

She glanced pryingly at the contents of his bag when he wasn't looking.
The neighbors listened pryingly to their conversation through the open window.
He asked pryingly about her financial situation, making her uncomfortable.

Synset Examples

‘Do you have a boyfriend?’ she asked her prospective tenant pryingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prying - excessively interested in other people's private affairs
  • Antonym: discreetly - in a careful and unobtrusive manner
