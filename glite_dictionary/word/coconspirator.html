coconspirator

1. NOUN

/ koʊ.kənˈspɪr.ə.tər / (AmE), / kəʊ.kənˈspɪr.ə.tə / (BrE)
A person who is involved with others in a conspiracy.
a fellow member in a conspiracy

Examples

The police arrested every coconspirator involved in the elaborate heist.
Prosecutors argued that he acted as a key coconspirator in the criminal plot.
Each coconspirator played a different role to avoid suspicion from authorities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conspirator - a person who takes part in a conspiracy

Etymology

From Latin 'con-' (together) + 'conspirator' (one who conspires).
