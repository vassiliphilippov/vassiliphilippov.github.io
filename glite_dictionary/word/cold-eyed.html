cold-eyed

1. ADJ

/ koʊld aɪd / (AmE), / kəʊld aɪd / (BrE)
not influenced by strong emotion or personal feelings; showing objectivity or detachment
unemotional; objective; detached

Examples

She gave him a cold-eyed assessment before delivering her verdict.
The team took a cold-eyed approach to evaluating the situation, avoiding any sentimentality.
His cold-eyed realism helped the company survive difficult times.

Synset Examples

a journalist should be a dispassionate reporter of fact

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dispassionate - not influenced by strong emotion, rational
  • Synonym: objective - not influenced by personal feelings or opinions
  • Synonym: impartial - treating all rivals fairly; unbiased
