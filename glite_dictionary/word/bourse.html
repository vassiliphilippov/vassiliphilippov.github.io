Bourse

1. NOUN

/ bʊrs / (AmE), / bʊəs / (BrE)
the stock exchange in Paris, or a stock exchange in general, especially in Europe
the Paris stock exchange; a European stock exchange

Examples

The Paris Bourse closed higher on Tuesday after three days of losses.
Many European investors keep a close eye on the Frankfurt Bourse and its daily performance.
He started his career as a broker on the Bourse in the late 1980s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stock exchange - an organized market for the buying and selling of stocks and shares

Etymology

From French 'bourse' (purse, stock exchange), ultimately from Medieval Latin 'bursa' (purse).
