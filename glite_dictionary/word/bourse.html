bourse

1. NOUN

/ bʊrs / (AmE), / bɔːs / (BrE)
A stock exchange, especially one in a European country; a financial market where securities are bought and sold.
European stock exchange or financial market

Examples

Many investors follow trends on the bourse to guide their decisions.
The Paris bourse is one of the major financial markets in Europe.
After the announcement, stock prices soared at the bourse.

Synset Examples

Investors gathered at the bourse to discuss the latest trends.
He studied the bourse to understand market fluctuations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stock exchange - an organized market for the buying and selling of stocks and shares

Etymology

From French 'bourse', ultimately from Medieval Latin 'bursa' (purse).
