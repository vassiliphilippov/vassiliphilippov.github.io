agonising

1. ADJ

/ ˈæɡ.əˌnaɪ.zɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈæɡ.ə.naɪ.zɪŋ / (BrE)
causing great physical or mental pain; extremely painful or distressing.
causing great pain or distress

Examples

The patient was in agonising pain after the surgery.
She faced an agonising decision between her career and her family.
He gave an agonising scream when he twisted his ankle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: painful - causing pain or discomfort
  • Synonym: excruciating - causing intense pain; extremely painful
  • Antonym: pleasant - giving a sense of happy satisfaction or enjoyment

Etymology

From 'agonise' + '-ing', ultimately from Greek 'agonia' (struggle).
