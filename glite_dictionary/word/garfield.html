Garfield

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡɑrˌfiːld / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːfiːld / (BrE)
James A. Garfield (1831–1881), the 20th President of the United States, who was assassinated in office.
20th U.S. President, assassinated in office

Examples

Garfield served as President of the United States until his assassination in 1881.
Historians often debate the significance of Garfield's brief time in office.
After Garfield was shot, the nation was plunged into mourning.
