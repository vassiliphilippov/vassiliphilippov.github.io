bargain-priced

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑrɡən ˌpraɪst / (AmE), / ˈbɑːɡən ˌpraɪst / (BrE)
Sold at a price lower than the standard or usual amount.
costing less than usual; cheaply priced

Examples

She found a bargain-priced sofa at the clearance sale.
The store is famous for its bargain-priced electronics and appliances.
Many shoppers wait for end-of-season sales to buy bargain-priced clothing.

Synset Examples

buying bargain-priced clothes for the children
cut-rate goods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cut-rate - offered for sale at a reduced price
  • Synonym: discounted - offered at a price lower than usual
