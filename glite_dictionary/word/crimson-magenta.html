crimson-magenta

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɪmzən məˈdʒɛntə / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmzən məˈdʒentə / (BrE)
A shade of magenta with a tinge of crimson, resulting in a deep purplish-red color.
deep purplish-red color

Examples

She wore a stunning scarf in crimson-magenta that caught everyone's attention.
The evening sky glowed with hues of crimson-magenta as the sun set.
He painted the flowers in a gentle crimson-magenta to give the artwork warmth and depth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magenta - a purplish-red color
  • Synonym: crimson - a deep red color with a hint of purple
