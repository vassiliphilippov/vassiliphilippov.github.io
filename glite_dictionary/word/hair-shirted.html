hair-shirted

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛr ˌʃɜrtɪd / (AmE), / ˈheə ˌʃɜːtɪd / (BrE)
Deliberately self-sacrificing or adopting an austere, penitent lifestyle.
self-sacrificing; austere

Examples

The governor adopted a hair-shirted approach to budgeting, slashing many popular programs.
Her hair-shirted attitude toward material comforts was admired by her colleagues.
Critics described the new policy as overly hair-shirted and unnecessarily harsh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ascetic - practicing severe self-discipline and abstention
  • Synonym: austere - severe or strict in manner, attitude, or appearance
  • Antonym: self-indulgent - characterized by doing exactly what one wants, especially when this involves pleasure or idleness

Etymology

From reference to the 'hair shirt,' a coarse garment worn by some religious ascetics as a form of self-punishment.
