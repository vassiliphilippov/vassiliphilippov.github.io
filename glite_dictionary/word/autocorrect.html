autocorrect

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔt̬oʊ.kəˌrekt / (AmE), / ˈɔː.təʊ.kəˌrekt / (BrE)
a software feature that automatically identifies and corrects spelling or typing errors in text as it is entered
automatic text correction feature

Examples

I rely on autocorrect to fix my typos when I send text messages.
Sometimes autocorrect changes my words to something completely different.
She turned off autocorrect because it kept making embarrassing mistakes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spellchecker - software tool that checks for spelling errors

Etymology

From 'auto-' (self, automatic) and 'correct'.
