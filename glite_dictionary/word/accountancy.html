accountancy

1. NOUN

/ əˈkaʊn.tən.si / (AmE), / əˈkaʊn.tən.si / (BrE)
the profession or activity of keeping and examining financial records and preparing financial reports for organizations
the profession of accounting

Examples

She decided to pursue a career in accountancy after graduating from university.
Accurate records are essential in the field of accountancy.
The firm specializes in accountancy services for small businesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accounting - the process or work of keeping financial accounts

Etymology

From 'accountant' + '-cy'; 'accountant' from Old French 'acompter', from Latin 'computare' (to calculate).
