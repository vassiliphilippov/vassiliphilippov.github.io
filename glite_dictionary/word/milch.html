milch

1. ADJ

/ mɪltʃ / (AmE), / mɪltʃ / (BrE)
(of an animal) giving milk or bred chiefly for milk production.
(of animals) giving or producing milk

Examples

The farmer keeps a herd of milch cows for the dairy business.
Milch goats are especially valued in regions where cow's milk is scarce.
In arid climates, milch camels are a vital source of nutrition.

Synset Examples

milch goats, milch camels

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dairy - (of animals) producing milk

Etymology

From Old English milce (milk), from Proto-Germanic *meluks.
