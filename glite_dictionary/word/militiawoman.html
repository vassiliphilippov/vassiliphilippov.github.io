militiawoman

1. NOUN

/ mɪˈlɪʃəˌwʊmən / (AmE), / mɪˈlɪʃəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A female member of a militia, typically serving only during emergencies.
female militia member

Examples

The militiawoman took command when her unit responded to the emergency.
During the civil unrest, a brave militiawoman helped protect her neighborhood.
She served as a militiawoman during the regional floods, assisting with rescue operations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: militiaman - a male member of a militia

Etymology

Modelled on 'militiaman', from 'militia' + 'woman'.
