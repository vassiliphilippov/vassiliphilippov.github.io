immurement

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmjʊrmənt / (AmE), / ɪˈmjʊəmənt / (BrE)
the state or act of being enclosed or imprisoned, especially by being shut within walls
imprisonment by being enclosed within walls

The ancient legend describes the immurement of the queen in the castle walls.
Many suffered immurement during the political turmoil, never to be seen again.
Stories of cruel immurement have inspired horror and fascination for centuries.

Etymology
