Edinburgh

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛd.ɪn.bɝː.ə / (AmE), / ˈɛd.ɪn.b(ə).rə / (BrE)
the capital city of Scotland, located in the Lothian Region on the southern shore of the Firth of Forth
capital city of Scotland

Examples

Every August, Edinburgh hosts the world's largest arts festival, attracting visitors from around the globe.
He plans to study at the University of Edinburgh next year.
The castle overlooks the city of Edinburgh from its rocky hilltop.

Etymology

From Middle English Edinburg, from Old English Edenburg, from Scottish Gaelic Dùn Èideann.
