pelvimeter

1. NOUN

/ pɛlˈvɪmɪtər / (AmE), / pɛlˈvɪmɪtə / (BrE)
A medical instrument used for measuring the dimensions of the pelvis, especially in obstetrics.
instrument for measuring the pelvis
medical

Examples

The obstetrician used a pelvimeter to assess the patient's pelvic dimensions before delivery.
A pelvimeter is an essential tool for evaluating the birth canal during pregnancy.
Older models of the pelvimeter were made of metal, but modern versions are often plastic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pelvimetry - the measurement of the pelvis

Etymology

From Latin pelvis (basin, pelvis) + Greek metron (measure).
