veg

1. NOUN

/ vɛdʒ / (AmE), / vɛdʒ / (BrE)
(informal) An edible plant or part of a plant other than a sweet fruit or seed, typically used as food.
informal term for vegetable
informal british

Examples

I'm trying to eat more veg with every meal.
She picked up some fresh veg at the farmers' market yesterday.
Roast meat and a side of veg make a perfect Sunday dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetable - an edible plant or its part, used as food

Etymology

Shortened form of 'vegetable.'
