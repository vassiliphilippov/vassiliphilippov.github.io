well-proportioned

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɛl pɚˈproʊrʃənd / (AmE), / ˌwɛl pəˈprɔːʃənd / (BrE)

having pleasing proportions

Examples

The statue has a well-proportioned figure that draws everyone's attention.
