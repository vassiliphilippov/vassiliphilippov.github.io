Kordofan

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɔːrdəˌfæn / (AmE), / ˌkɔːdəˈfæn / (BrE)
a mountainous region in central Sudan, known for its diverse ethnic groups and agriculture
mountainous region of central Sudan

Examples

The province of Kordofan is renowned for its agricultural production and cultural diversity.
Many humanitarian agencies are working in Kordofan to support local communities.
