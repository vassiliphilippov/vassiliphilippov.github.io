raftswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈræftsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈrɑːftsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who travels by raft, especially as an occupation or in a sporting context
woman traveling by raft

Examples

The experienced raftswoman guided her group safely through the rapids.
As a raftswoman, she has completed some of the most challenging river expeditions in South America.
Journalists interviewed the raftswoman about her recent solo journey down the Nile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raftsman - a man who travels by raft

Etymology

Formed by combining 'raft' and 'woman', modeled after 'raftsman'.
