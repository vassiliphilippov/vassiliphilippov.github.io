vulcanology

1. NOUN

/ ˌvʌlkəˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌvʌlkəˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of volcanoes and volcanic phenomena
the study of volcanoes

Examples

He decided to specialize in vulcanology after witnessing a volcanic eruption during his childhood.
Recent advances in vulcanology have improved our ability to predict eruptions.
Many universities offer courses in vulcanology as part of their earth sciences programs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volcanology - the scientific study of volcanoes and volcanic phenomena

Etymology

From Latin Vulcanus (the Roman god of fire) + -logy (study of).
