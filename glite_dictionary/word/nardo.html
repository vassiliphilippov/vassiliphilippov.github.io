nardo

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑːrdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈnɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
an Australian clover fern with soft, clover-like leaves, found in moist habitats
Australian clover fern

Examples

Botanists have observed the nardo thriving in the wet areas of southeastern Australia.
The delicate green leaves of the nardo resemble those of a clover.
Some gardeners value nardo for its ability to cover damp, shaded ground.
