Xylosma congestum

1. NOUN

/ zaɪˌlɒzmə kənˈdʒɛstəm / (AmE), / zaɪˌlɒzmə kənˈdʒɛstəm / (BrE)
A shrub or small tree with neat evergreen foliage and fragrant late-blooming flowers, grown as an ornamental plant in mild climates; native to China.
ornamental evergreen shrub or tree from China

Examples

Gardeners often choose Xylosma congestum for its attractive year-round foliage.
The glossy leaves of Xylosma congestum make it a popular choice in landscape design.
Our local nursery recommended Xylosma congestum for the mild coastal climate.

Etymology

From Greek 'xylon' (wood) + 'osma' (smell), and Latin 'congestum' (heaped together).
