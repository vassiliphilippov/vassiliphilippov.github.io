man-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈmænˌsaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈmænˌsaɪzd / (BrE)
(of an object or task) requiring the strength or effort typically expected of an adult man; large or challenging in size or scope.
requiring a man's strength or effort

Examples

He took on a man-sized project that took several months to complete.
The hikers were faced with a man-sized boulder blocking the trail.
Cooking for twelve people is a man-sized task.

Synset Examples

a man-sized job

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: large - of considerable or relatively great size
  • Degree Higher: enormous - very large in size
