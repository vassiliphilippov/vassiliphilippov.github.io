watercolourist

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑt̬ɚˌkʌlɚɪst / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəkʌlərɪst / (BrE)
an artist who paints using watercolours as their primary medium
a painter using watercolours

Examples

The exhibition featured works by a renowned watercolourist from the nineteenth century.
As a skilled watercolourist, she captured the delicate hues of nature with remarkable precision.
He began his career as a watercolourist before moving on to oil painting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: painter - a person who paints pictures

Etymology

From 'watercolour' + '-ist', indicating one who practices or is concerned with something.
