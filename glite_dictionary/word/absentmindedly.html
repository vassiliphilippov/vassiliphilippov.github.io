absentmindedly

1. ADV

/ ˌæb.səntˈmaɪn.dɪd.li / (AmE), / ˌæb.səntˈmaɪn.dɪd.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of attention or concentration because of preoccupation.
in a distracted or preoccupied way

Examples

He absentmindedly stirred his coffee while staring out the window.
She absentmindedly left her keys on the kitchen counter again.
He absentmindedly scrolled through his phone during the meeting.

Synset Examples

he read the letter absently

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absent-minded - having or showing a lack of attention; preoccupied

Etymology

From 'absent-minded' + '-ly'. 'Absent-minded' from absent (not present) + minded (having a mind of a specified kind).
