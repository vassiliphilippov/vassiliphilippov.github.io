lambency

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæmbənsi / (AmE), / ˈlæmbənsi / (BrE)
a soft, glowing, or gently flickering light or radiance
soft or glowing radiance

Examples

The lambency of the candlelight gave the room a warm, inviting feel.
Moonlight cast a gentle lambency over the quiet garden.
The painting captures the lambency of dawn as it touches the mountain tops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glow - a steady radiance of light or heat
  • Degree Higher: brightness - the quality of being bright or giving out light
  • Degree Lower: dimness - the quality of being not bright or clear

Etymology

From Latin 'lambere' (to lick), via 'lambent' (gently glowing or flickering).
