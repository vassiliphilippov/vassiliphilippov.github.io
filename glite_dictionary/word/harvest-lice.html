harvest-lice

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrvɪst ˌlaɪs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːvɪst ˌlaɪs / (BrE)
An erect perennial herb native to the Old World, typically found in dry, grassy habitats.
erect perennial Old World herb

Examples

During our walk, we spotted several harvest-lice growing along the dry hedgerow.
Many gardeners appreciate harvest-lice for their resilience in poor, grassy soils.
Wildlife in the area often use harvest-lice as shelter during the dry summer months.
