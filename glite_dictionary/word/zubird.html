Zubird

1. NOUN

/ ˈzuˌbɜrd / (AmE), / ˈzuːˌbɜːd / (BrE)
A mythological storm god associated with evil, traditionally represented as a black bird.
evil storm god in bird form (mythology)

Examples

In ancient tales, the villagers would pray to keep the wrath of Zubird away during the storm season.
The legend says that Zubird appears as a massive black bird that brings dark clouds and lightning.
Few dared to speak the name Zubird aloud, fearing they might attract the attention of the storm god.
