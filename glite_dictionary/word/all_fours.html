all fours

1. NOUN

/ ɔl ˈfɔɹz / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈfɔːz / (BrE)
a traditional card game in which players win points by capturing cards for 'high', 'low', 'jack', or 'game'
card game played for points on certain cards

Examples

They spent the evening playing all fours with their neighbors.
I learned the rules of all fours from my grandfather.
All fours is popular at family gatherings in the Caribbean.
