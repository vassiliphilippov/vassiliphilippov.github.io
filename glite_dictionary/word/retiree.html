retiree

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛˈtaɪˈri / (AmE), / ˌrɛˈtaɪˈriː / (BrE)
a person who has stopped working because they have reached a certain age or have chosen to retire
person who has retired from work

Examples

After 40 years at the company, John became a retiree and moved to Florida.
The community center offers special classes for retirees to help them stay active.
As a retiree, she finally had time to pursue her hobbies and travel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pensioner - a person who receives a pension, especially after retirement
  • Antonym: worker - a person who is employed or currently working

Etymology

From 'retire' + '-ee', patterned after 'employee'.
