vintager

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪn.tɪdʒ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɪn.tɪdʒ.ə / (BrE)
a person who harvests grapes for making wine
grape harvester for winemaking

Examples

The vintager carefully picked bunches of ripe grapes during the early morning harvest.
As a seasonal vintager, she traveled to different vineyards each autumn.
The winery hired dozens of vintagers to help with this year’s bumper crop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harvester - a person who gathers crops
  • Synonym: vintner - a person who makes or sells wine

Etymology

From 'vintage' (the wine harvest or its produce) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
