gobsmacked

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɑbˌsmækt / (AmE), / ˈɡɒbˌsmækt / (BrE)
Extremely surprised or shocked; utterly astounded.
utterly astounded; extremely surprised
informal british slang

Examples

I was gobsmacked when I heard the unexpected news.
She looked absolutely gobsmacked at the magician's final trick.
He stood there, gobsmacked, unable to say a word in response.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astonished - greatly surprised or amazed
  • Synonym: amazed - filled with great surprise or wonder
  • Antonym: unimpressed - not feeling admiration or interest

Etymology

British slang, from 'gob' (mouth) and 'smacked' (hit), meaning 'mouth hit' as in struck silent with surprise.
