spermicide

1. NOUN

/ spɚˈmɪsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈspɜːmɪsaɪd / (BrE)
a substance used as a contraceptive that kills sperm cells
substance that kills sperm (contraceptive)
medical

Examples

The doctor recommended using a spermicide in conjunction with other birth control methods.
Many people choose a spermicide gel for extra protection against pregnancy.
She asked the pharmacist about the effectiveness of spermicide products.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contraceptive - a device or substance used to prevent pregnancy

Etymology

From Latin 'sperma' (sperm, seed) and Latin 'cida' (killing), from 'caedere' (to kill).
