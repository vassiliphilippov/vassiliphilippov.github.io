Qum

1. PROPN

/ kum / (AmE), / kuÀêm / (BrE)
a city in northwestern Iran, known as a holy site and place of pilgrimage for Shiite Muslims.
city in northwestern Iran; holy site for Shiite Muslims

Examples

Every year, thousands of pilgrims visit Qum as part of their religious journey.
The shrine in Qum is an important site for Shiite Muslims.
She traveled to Qum to pursue her theological studies.
