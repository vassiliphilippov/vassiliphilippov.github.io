Antonius Stradivarius

1. PROPN

/ ænˈtoʊniəs ˌstrædəˈvɛriəs / (AmE), / ænˈtəʊniəs ˌstrædɪˈvɛəriəs / (BrE)
(1644?–1737) An Italian violin maker renowned for developing the modern violin and producing instruments of exceptional tonal quality.
Italian violin maker, developed the modern violin

Many musicians dream of owning a Antonius Stradivarius violin due to its legendary sound quality.

Historically, Antonius Stradivarius set the standard for excellence in violin making.
