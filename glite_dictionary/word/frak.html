frak

1. INTJ

/ fræk / (AmE), / fræk / (BrE)
used as an exclamation to express frustration, anger, or annoyance, often as a substitute for a stronger vulgar term
exclamation expressing frustration or anger
slang

Examples

He slammed the door, shouting, "frak!" when his computer crashed again.
"Frak this!" she muttered under her breath after reading the bad news.
After missing the train, he couldn't help but exclaim, "frak!" out of sheer annoyance.

Synset Examples

He cursed and said frak when he lost the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuck - vulgar expletive used to express anger or frustration

Etymology

Popularized by the television series Battlestar Galactica (1978; 2004) as a euphemism for a vulgar English word.

expletive expressing frustration (from sci-fi)
slang

Examples

You could hear him shout frak from across the room.

Related Terms
