Nd

1. NOUN

/ ɛn ˈdi / (AmE), / ɛn ˈdi / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for neodymium, a yellowish trivalent metallic element of the rare earth group
chemical symbol for neodymium (element)

Examples

The magnet was made with Nd to increase its strength.
Researchers analyzed the Nd content in the mineral samples.
Batteries using Nd alloys are more efficient than older models.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neodymium - a yellowish metallic element of the lanthanide series, symbol Nd

Etymology

Abbreviation of neodymium, from Greek neo- (new) + didymos (twin).
