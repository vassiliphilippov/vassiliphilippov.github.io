valuer

1. NOUN

/ ˈvæljuːər / (AmE), / ˈvæljuːə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to assess the monetary value of property, possessions, or assets
person who assesses monetary value

Examples

The bank sent a valuer to estimate the market price of the property.
A qualified valuer must assess the artwork before it can be insured.
Having the opinion of an independent valuer can be crucial in legal disputes over inheritance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appraiser - person who assesses the value of something

Etymology

From the verb 'value' + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
