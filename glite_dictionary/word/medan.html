Medan

1. PROPN

/ meɪˈdɑːn / (AmE), / meˈdæn / (BrE)
A city in northeastern Sumatra, Indonesia, known as the capital of North Sumatra province.
city in northeastern Sumatra, Indonesia

Examples

Medan is the largest city on the island of Sumatra.
