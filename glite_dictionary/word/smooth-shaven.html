smooth-shaven

1. ADJ

/ ˌsmuðˈʃeɪvən / (AmE), / ˌsmuːðˈʃeɪvən / (BrE)
(Of a man's face) having been very closely shaved so that the skin feels smooth.
closely shaved, smooth-faced

Examples

He appeared in court smooth-shaven and well-groomed.
His smooth-shaven chin gave him a youthful look.
After years of wearing a beard, he decided to go smooth-shaven for the new job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clean-shaven - having no facial hair, closely shaved
