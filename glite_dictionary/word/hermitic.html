hermitic

1. ADJ

/ hərˈmɪtɪk / (AmE), / hɜːˈmɪtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of a hermit or a life of solitude and asceticism.
relating to a hermit or solitary life

Examples

After years in the monastery, he adopted a truly hermitic way of life.
She found his hermitic existence both fascinating and sad.
Many religious figures have chosen a hermitic path to seek spiritual enlightenment.

Synset Examples

the eremitic element in the life of a religious colony
his hermitic existence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eremitic - relating to the life of a hermit, solitary and withdrawn

Etymology

From 'hermit' (from Old French, from Late Latin *heremita*, from Greek *erēmitēs* meaning 'person of the desert').
