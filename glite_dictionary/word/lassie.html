lassie

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæsi / (AmE), / ˈlasi / (BrE)
a girl or young woman, especially one who is unmarried or seen as youthful (mainly Scottish or northern English)
a young girl or unmarried woman (Scottish)

Examples

When he left, he promised the lassie he'd write every week.
The old man told a story about a brave lassie from his village.
A group of lassies danced at the festival in traditional Scottish dress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lass - a girl or young woman
  • Antonym: lad - a boy or young man

Etymology

Scottish diminutive of 'lass', meaning girl.
