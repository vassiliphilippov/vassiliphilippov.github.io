Torreon

1. PROPN

/ toʊˈreɪ.ən / (AmE), / tɒˈreɪ.ɒn / (BrE)
A city in northern Mexico, located west of Monterrey, known for its industry and commercial significance.
city in northern Mexico

Examples

Torreon is an important industrial hub in the state of Coahuila.
She was born and raised in Torreon before moving to the capital.
The train from Monterrey arrives in Torreon late in the afternoon.
