Trablous

1. PROPN

/ trɑːˈbluːs / (AmE), / trɑːˈbluːs / (BrE)
The Arabic name for Tripoli, a major port city and commercial center in northwestern Lebanon on the Mediterranean Sea.
Arabic name for Tripoli, Lebanon

Examples

Many people travel to Trablous to experience its famous souks and ancient architecture.
The port of Trablous plays an important role in Lebanon's economy.
Historians have written extensively about the diverse cultural heritage of Trablous.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tripoli - the largest city in northern Lebanon, capital of the North Governorate

Etymology

From Arabic Ṭarābulus, referring to the city of Tripoli in Lebanon.
