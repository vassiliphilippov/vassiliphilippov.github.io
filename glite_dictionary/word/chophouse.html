chophouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑpˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒpˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A restaurant that specializes in serving steaks and grilled meats.
restaurant specializing in steaks and grilled meats

Examples

We decided to celebrate at a famous chophouse in the city.
The chophouse is known for its perfectly grilled steaks and classic sides.
After work, they often meet at the local chophouse for dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steakhouse - a restaurant specializing in steaks

Etymology

From 'chop' (cut of meat) + 'house' (restaurant or establishment); dates to early 19th-century Britain.
