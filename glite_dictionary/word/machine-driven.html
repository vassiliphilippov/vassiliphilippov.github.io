machine-driven

1. ADJ

/ məˈʃin ˌdrɪvən / (AmE), / məˈʃiːn ˌdrɪvən / (BrE)
operated or controlled by a machine rather than by a person; automated.
operated by a machine

Examples

The factory installed several machine-driven assembly lines to boost productivity.
Her research focused on the ethics of machine-driven decision-making in healthcare.
They replaced the manual system with a fully machine-driven process to reduce human error.

Synset Examples

an automated stoker

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automated - operated by automation or machines, not manually
  • Antonym: manual - operated or done by hand rather than by machine
