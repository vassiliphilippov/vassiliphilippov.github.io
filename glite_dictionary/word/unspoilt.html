unspoilt

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈspɔɪlt / (AmE), / ʌnˈspɔɪlt / (BrE)
not spoiled; remaining in a pristine, undamaged, or original condition.
not spoiled or damaged

Examples

The countryside remained unspoilt by modern development, attracting many nature lovers.
Thanks to careful preservation, the ancient forest is still remarkably unspoilt.
He was relieved to discover that the milk was unspoilt despite being left out overnight.

Synset Examples

the meat is still good

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pristine - in its original condition; unspoiled
  • Antonym: spoiled - damaged, decayed, or no longer in a desirable state

Etymology

From un- (not) + spoiled, variant of 'spoil'.
