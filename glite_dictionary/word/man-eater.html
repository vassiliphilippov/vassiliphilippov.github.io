man-eater

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænˌitər / (AmE), / ˈmænˌiːtə / (BrE)
a large, aggressive shark that is known to attack humans
shark known to attack humans

Examples

The waters in this area are believed to be home to the infamous man-eater.
Many swimmers avoided the beach after reports of a man-eater sighting.
Some documentaries focus on the behavior of the great white, a notorious man-eater.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shark - large predatory fish often found in warm seas
