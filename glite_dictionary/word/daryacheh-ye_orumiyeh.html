Daryacheh-ye Orumiyeh

1. PROPN

/ dæɾiːˈɑːtʃeɪ jeɪ oːˈruːmiːjeɪ / (AmE), / ˌdɑːriːˈɑːtʃeɪ jeɪ ɔːˈruːmiːjeɪ / (BrE)
A shallow saline lake located in northwestern Iran, between Tabriz and the eastern border of Turkey.
a saline lake in northwestern Iran

Examples

The water level in Daryacheh-ye Orumiyeh has been declining rapidly in recent decades.
Birdwatchers visit Daryacheh-ye Orumiyeh to see rare species that nest there.
Environmentalists are concerned about pollution in Daryacheh-ye Orumiyeh.

Etymology

From Persian, meaning 'Lake of Urmia'.
