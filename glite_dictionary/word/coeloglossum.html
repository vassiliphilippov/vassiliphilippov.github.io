Coeloglossum

1. NOUN

/ siːəˈlɒɡləsəm / (AmE), / siːəˈlɒɡləsəm / (BrE)
A genus of terrestrial orchids found in cooler parts of North America and Europe, commonly known as the satyr orchid.
genus of terrestrial orchids

Examples

The rare Coeloglossum can be found in alpine meadows across Europe.
Botanists studied the distribution of Coeloglossum in the northern forests.
She photographed a blooming Coeloglossum during her field research.

Etymology

From Greek 'koilos' (hollow) and 'glossa' (tongue), referring to the hollowed tongue-shaped part of the flower.

2. NOUN

/ siːoʊˈɡlɒsəm / (AmE), / sɪəʊˈɡlɒsəm / (BrE)
A genus of terrestrial orchids found in cooler regions of North America and Europe, commonly known as the satyr orchid.
orchid genus of cooler regions

Examples

The Coeloglossum genus includes orchids adapted to cold climates.
Botanists discovered a rare Coeloglossum specimen in a northern woodland.
The satyr orchid, classified under Coeloglossum, is admired for its unique flower structure.

Etymology

From Greek 'koilos' (hollow) + 'glossa' (tongue), referring to the shape of the orchid's lip.
