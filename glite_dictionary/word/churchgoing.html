churchgoing

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɝtʃˌɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːtʃˌɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
regularly attending church or actively practicing one's religion, especially Christianity.
regularly attending church

Examples

His churchgoing family attended Sunday service every week without fail.
Despite being raised in a churchgoing household, he developed his own beliefs as an adult.
She leads a quiet, churchgoing life in the countryside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devout - deeply religious or pious
  • Antonym: secular - not connected with religious or spiritual matters

Etymology

Formed from 'church' + 'going', indicating the act of going to church.
