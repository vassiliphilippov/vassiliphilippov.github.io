unintelligently

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəntli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəntli / (BrE)
In a manner showing a lack of intelligence or good judgment.
in a way that lacks intelligence

Examples

The manager spoke unintelligently about the causes of the problem, confusing everyone further.
She reacted unintelligently to the warning, ignoring obvious signs of danger.
He planned his schedule unintelligently, leaving no time for rest or relaxation.

Synset Examples

he acted rather unintelligently in this crisis

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intelligently - in a manner showing intelligence; with good sense or judgment
