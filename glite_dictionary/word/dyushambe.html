Dyushambe

1. PROPN

/ djuːˈʃɑːmbi / (AmE), / djuːˈʃæmbɪ / (BrE)
the capital city of Tajikistan, known as Stalinabad from 1926 to 1991
capital city of Tajikistan

Examples

Many international conferences have been held in Dyushambe over the past decade.
She traveled from Moscow to Dyushambe when it was still called Stalinabad.
Dyushambe is situated in the Hissar Valley, surrounded by mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dushanbe - the current English spelling of the capital of Tajikistan
