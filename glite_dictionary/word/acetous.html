acetous

1. ADJ

/ ˈæsɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈæsɪtəs / (BrE)
Tasting or smelling like vinegar; having the sour quality of vinegar.
having a vinegar-like taste or smell

Examples

The salad had an acetous flavor that reminded me of pickled vegetables.
An acetous aroma wafted from the open bottle of wine.
The sauce became acetous after sitting out for several days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vinegary - having a sour, vinegar-like taste or smell

Etymology

From Latin 'acetosus' (sour, vinegary), from 'acetum' (vinegar).
