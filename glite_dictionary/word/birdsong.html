birdsong

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːdˌsɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdˌsɒŋ / (BrE)
the musical sounds or calls produced by birds, especially as a form of communication or attraction
the sound or singing of birds

Examples

The gentle birdsong in the morning makes waking up much more pleasant.
Scientists recorded the birdsong to study how birds communicate with each other.
The forest was alive with the melodious birdsong of robins and thrushes.

Synset Examples

a bird will not learn its song unless it hears it at an early age

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birdcall - a short, characteristic sound or cry made by a bird
