Bahreini

1. NOUN

/ bɑːˈreɪni / (AmE), / bɑːˈreɪni / (BrE)
a native or citizen of Bahrain.
person from Bahrain

Examples

Her friend is a Bahreini who grew up in Manama.
The Bahreini delegation arrived at the conference early.
Many Bahreinis celebrate their national day with parades and fireworks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bahraini - a native or inhabitant of Bahrain

Etymology

From Bahrain, the name of the country, with English demonymic suffix -i.
