32nd

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɜrti ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈθɜːti ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
The ordinal number matching the position thirty-two in a sequence.
ordinal number; thirty-second

Examples

This year marks the 32nd anniversary of the company's founding.
She finished in 32nd place in the marathon, which was her best result yet.
The 32nd chapter of the book contains some of the most important information.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thirty-second - the ordinal number of thirty-two in counting order
  • Degree Lower: 31st - the ordinal number of thirty-one; immediately before thirty-second
  • Degree Higher: 33rd - the ordinal number of thirty-three; immediately after thirty-second
