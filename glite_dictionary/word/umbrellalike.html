umbrellalike

1. ADJ

/ ʌmˈbɹɛl.əˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ʌmˈbrel.əˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the appearance of an umbrella, especially in shape or structure
resembling an umbrella

Examples

The mushroom had an umbrellalike cap, perfect for sheltering tiny insects during a rainstorm.
She admired the umbrellalike canopy of the ancient tree, which provided shade for the playground.
Some jellyfish drift gracefully, trailing their umbrellalike bodies in the ocean currents.

Etymology

Formed from 'umbrella' + '-like', a suffix meaning 'resembling'.
