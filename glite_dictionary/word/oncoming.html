oncoming

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑnˌkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɒnˌkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
moving toward or approaching from the opposite direction
approaching, moving toward

Examples

Drivers must yield to oncoming traffic when making a left turn.
She narrowly avoided the oncoming train by moving off the tracks in time.
The hikers took shelter from the oncoming storm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incoming - coming in or approaching from outside
  • Antonym: departing - leaving or moving away

Etymology

From the prefix 'on-' + present participle of 'come'; modelled after words like 'incoming'.
