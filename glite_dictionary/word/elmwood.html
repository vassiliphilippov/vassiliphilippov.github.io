elmwood

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛlmˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈɛlmˌwʊd / (BrE)
the hard, tough wood of an elm tree, often used for furniture and implements
wood from an elm tree

Examples

The cabinet was made from beautiful elmwood that shone under the light.
She preferred elmwood flooring for its durability and fine grain.
Antique desks crafted from elmwood are highly valued by collectors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elm - a type of deciduous tree with serrated leaves
