unpartitioned

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.pɑrˈtɪʃ.ənd / (AmE), / ʌn.pɑːˈtɪʃ.ənd / (BrE)
Not divided into parts or sections by partitions; left whole or undivided.
not divided into parts or partitions

Examples

The large room remained unpartitioned, giving it a spacious and open feel.
Some data disks are left unpartitioned during the initial system setup.
They preferred an unpartitioned workspace to encourage greater collaboration.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: partitioned - divided into parts or sections by partitions

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + partitioned (divided, from Latin partitio).
