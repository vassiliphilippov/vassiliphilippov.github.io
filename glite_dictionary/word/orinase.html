Orinase

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːrəˌneɪs / (AmE), / ˈɔːrɪneɪs / (BrE)
A trade name for tolbutamide, an oral medication used to treat type 2 diabetes by lowering blood sugar levels.
antidiabetic drug (trade name)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Orinase to help manage her type 2 diabetes.
Patients taking Orinase should monitor their blood sugar regularly.
Orinase was once a common medication for adult-onset diabetes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tolbutamide - an oral antidiabetic drug used to treat type 2 diabetes
