spiderwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪdərˌwɝt / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdəˌwɜːt / (BrE)
a herbaceous plant of the genus Tradescantia or related genera in the family Commelinaceae, with elongated leaves and usually blue, purple, or white three-petaled flowers
herbaceous plant of the Commelinaceae family

Examples

The spiderwort blooms in early summer, covering the garden with delicate blue flowers.
She identified the plant as a wild spiderwort growing by the roadside.
Many gardeners appreciate spiderwort for its long-lasting blossoms and easy care.

Etymology

From 'spider' + 'wort' (Middle English for plant or herb).
