Lanoxin

1. NOUN

/ ləˈnɑksɪn / (AmE), / ləˈnɒksɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for digoxin, a medication used to treat congestive heart failure and certain abnormal heart rhythms.
brand name for digoxin heart medication
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Lanoxin to help manage her heart arrhythmia.
Patients taking Lanoxin should be carefully monitored for side effects.
My grandfather has been on Lanoxin for his heart failure for several years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: digoxin - a medication used to treat certain heart conditions
