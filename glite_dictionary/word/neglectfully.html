neglectfully

1. ADV

/ nɪˈɡlɛktfəli / (AmE), / nɪˈɡlɛktfəli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows lack of care or attention; carelessly or negligently.
in a neglectful or careless manner

Examples

He handled the antique vase neglectfully, risking irreparable damage.
The paperwork was neglectfully filed, leading to serious delays in the project.
She neglectfully left the garden untended for weeks, and the plants began to wither.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carelessly - without taking care; inattentively
  • Antonym: attentively - with care or attention

Etymology

From 'neglectful' + '-ly' adverbial suffix.
