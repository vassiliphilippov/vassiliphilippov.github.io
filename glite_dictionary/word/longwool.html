longwool

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔŋˌwʊl / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌwʊl / (BrE)
a breed of domestic sheep known for its long, lustrous wool.
sheep breed with long wool

Examples

The farmer kept a flock of longwool for their highly valued fleece.
Longwool breeds are often chosen for their ability to produce high-quality yarn.
She learned to identify a longwool by the distinctive length and texture of its coat.
