Ernst Cassirer

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛrnst kɑˈsɪrər / (AmE), / ˈɜːnst kəˈsɪərə / (BrE)
(1874–1945) A German philosopher known for his work on concept formation and symbolic forms in human culture.
German philosopher (1874–1945)

Examples

Ernst Cassirer explored how people use symbols to shape their perception of reality.
Many students read Ernst Cassirer when studying the philosophy of language.
