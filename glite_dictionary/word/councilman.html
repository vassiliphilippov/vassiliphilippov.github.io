councilman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊnsəlˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈkaʊnsəlmən / (BrE)
A male member of a council, especially a local government council.
male council member

Examples

The councilman proposed a new initiative to improve public transportation in the city.
Several residents voiced their concerns directly to the councilman during the town hall meeting.
After years of service, the councilman decided not to run for re-election.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: councilwoman - a woman who is a council member
  • Synonym: councillor - member of a council, usually gender-neutral

Etymology

From 'council' + 'man'.
