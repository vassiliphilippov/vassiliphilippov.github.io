valorously

1. ADV

/ ˈvælɚəsli / (AmE), / ˈvælərəsli / (BrE)
In a manner showing great courage, especially in battle or in the face of danger.
in a courageous way

Examples

The soldiers defended their position valorously despite overwhelming odds.
She valorously rescued the child from the burning house.
He fought valorously for justice, undeterred by threats.

Synset Examples

he fought valiantly until the end

Related Terms

  • Synonym: valiantly - in a brave or valiant manner

Etymology

From 'valor' (meaning courage) + '-ous' (adjective suffix) + '-ly' (adverb suffix).
