eighteenth

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪˌtinθ / (AmE), / ˈeɪˌtiːnθ / (BrE)
coming after the seventeenth in position; 18th
ordinal number: 18th

Examples

She finished in eighteenth place in the race.
Today is his eighteenth birthday and he is celebrating with family.
The eighteenth chapter of the book is the most exciting one.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: seventeenth - coming after the sixteenth in position; 17th
  • Degree Higher: nineteenth - coming after the eighteenth in position; 19th

Etymology

Formed from 'eight' + 'teen' + ordinal suffix '-th'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈeɪˌtinθ / (AmE), / ˈeɪtiːnθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number matching the number eighteen in a sequence; the position of being number 18.
the number eighteen in order

Examples

She finished in eighteenth place at the marathon this year.
The meeting is scheduled for the eighteenth of July every year.
Only the eighteenth chapter remains for us to discuss.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: seventeenth - ordinal number for seventeen (17th)
  • Degree Higher: nineteenth - ordinal number for nineteen (19th)

Etymology

From 'eighteen' and the suffix '-th' indicating an ordinal number.
