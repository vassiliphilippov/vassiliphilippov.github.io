nubbin

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌbɪn / (AmE), / ˈnʌbɪn / (BrE)
a small lump or protuberance, especially an undeveloped fruit or ear of corn
a small lump; undeveloped fruit or corn

Examples

The farmer tossed aside the nubbin of corn that hadn't fully grown.
Only a nubbin remained after the squirrel finished eating the apple.
She found a nubbin of a carrot that never matured in the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nub - a small lump or protuberance
