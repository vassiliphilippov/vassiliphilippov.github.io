hated

1. ADJ

/ ˈheɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈheɪtɪd / (BrE)
Regarded with intense dislike or contempt; greatly detested.
regarded with intense dislike

Examples

The dictator was hated by the majority of the population.
She left that hated job to pursue her passion.
He avoided the hated vegetables on his plate.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loved - regarded with deep affection or fondness
  • Synonym: detested - strongly disliked; abhorred

Etymology

From the past participle of Old English 'hātian' (to hate).
