Nejd

1. PROPN

/ neɪdʒd / (AmE), / neɪdʒd / (BrE)
a central plateau region of the Arabian Peninsula, formerly an independent sultanate, now part of Saudi Arabia.
central plateau region in Arabia

Examples

The Nejd played a crucial role in the formation of modern Saudi Arabia.
Tribal traditions remain strong in the rural communities of the Nejd.
Arid landscapes and rocky plateaus characterize much of the Nejd region.

Etymology

From Arabic najd (highland, upland).
