frostbitten

1. ADJ

/ ˈfrɑstˌbɪtən / (AmE), / ˈfrɒstˌbɪtən / (BrE)
having tissue or surfaces injured or damaged by freezing or exposure to extreme cold
damaged by freezing

Examples

After hiking in the blizzard, he discovered his toes were frostbitten and needed medical attention.
The explorers returned with frostbitten cheeks after several days in subzero temperatures.
Many of the crops were frostbitten and could not be harvested.

Synset Examples

frostbitten fingers
frostbitten grapes unsalable as fresh fruit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frostbite - injury to body tissue caused by exposure to extreme cold

Etymology

Past participle of 'frostbite', formed from 'frost' (Middle English; from Old English 'forst') and 'bite' (Old English 'bītan').
