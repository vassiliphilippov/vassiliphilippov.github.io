muncher

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌntʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌntʃə / (BrE)
a person or thing that chews food with a repeated, audible action, often making a munching sound
person or thing that munches

Examples

The toddler was a noisy muncher at the dinner table, delighting everyone with his enthusiasm.
At the movies, every row had at least one popcorn muncher.
My dog is a voracious muncher when given a bone to chew on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chewer - a person or thing that chews

Etymology

From the verb 'munch' (to chew with a muffled, grinding sound) + -er (agent noun suffix).
