Capo Passero

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɑːpoʊ pəˈsɛroʊ / (AmE), / ˌkɑːpəʊ pəˈsɛərəʊ / (BrE)
a cape marking the southeastern corner of the island of Sicily, Italy
cape at southeast tip of Sicily

Examples

The lighthouse at Capo Passero is an important navigational landmark for sailors approaching Sicily.
Many migratory birds rest at Capo Passero during their seasonal journeys across the Mediterranean.
The waters near Capo Passero are popular with divers due to their rich marine life and underwater caves.

Etymology

Italian, meaning 'Passero Cape' or 'Cape Sparrow'.
