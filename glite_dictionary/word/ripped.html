ripped

1. ADJ

/ ɹɪpt / (AmE), / ɹɪpt / (BrE)
(slang) Stupefied, highly intoxicated, or excited due to the influence of alcohol or drugs.
intoxicated by alcohol or drugs
slang

Examples

He was so ripped at the party that he barely remembered getting home.
After three shots of tequila, she admitted she was completely ripped.
They laughed and joked together, clearly ripped after a long night out.

Synset Examples

a noisy crowd of intoxicated sailors
helplessly inebriated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wasted - Very drunk or under the influence of drugs
  • Antonym: sober - Not affected by alcohol or drugs
