eggfruit

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛɡˌfruːt / (AmE), / ˈɛɡˌfruːt / (BrE)
A tropical ovoid fruit with sweet, mealy orange-yellow flesh, native to Florida and the West Indies.
sweet tropical fruit with yellow flesh

Examples

The farmer harvested several ripe eggfruit from the tree for market.
I tasted eggfruit for the first time while vacationing in the Caribbean.
The eggfruit is prized for its sweet, custard-like flesh in local desserts.
