to concatenate

1. VERB

/ kənˈkætəˌneɪt / (AmE), / kɒnˈkatɪneɪt / (BrE)
to link or join together in a series or chain
link together in a series

Examples

The function will concatenate the two strings into one longer string.
To process these files, simply concatenate them in the desired order.
You can easily concatenate lists in most programming languages.

Synset Examples

concatenate terms
concatenate characters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: link - to connect or join things together

Etymology

From Latin concatenare, from con- (together) + catena (chain).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00190512-v
Synset POS: v
