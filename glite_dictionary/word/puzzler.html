puzzler

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌzlər / (AmE), / ˈpʌz.lə / (BrE)
a difficult or confusing problem or question, especially one designed to be solved as a game or challenge
difficult problem or puzzle

Examples

The detective was faced with a true puzzler when all the suspects had alibis.
Can you solve this mathematical puzzler before the time runs out?
That riddle was a real puzzler, but I finally figured it out.

Synset Examples

he loved to solve chessmate puzzles
that's a real puzzler

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puzzle - a game, problem, or toy that tests ingenuity or knowledge
  • Synonym: enigma - something that is mysterious, puzzling, or difficult to understand

Etymology

From 'puzzle' + '-er', denoting something or someone that puzzles.
