China

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʃaɪ.nə / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪ.nə / (BrE)
a large country in East Asia, officially known as the People's Republic of China, and the most populous nation in the world
country in East Asia

Examples

The economy of China has grown rapidly in recent decades.
Many products are manufactured in China before being exported worldwide.
I would like to visit the Great Wall of China someday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chinese - the people or language of China

Etymology

From Persian *Chīn*, ultimately from the Qin (Ch'in) dynasty.
