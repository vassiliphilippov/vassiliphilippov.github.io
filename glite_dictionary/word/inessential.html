inessential

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪnɪˈsɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnɪˈsɛnʃəl / (BrE)
a thing that is not essential; something unnecessary or unimportant
something unnecessary or unimportant

Examples

Before the move, we got rid of many inessentials from our home.
The company decided to cut back on all inessentials to save costs.
Traveling light means packing only the basics and leaving the inessentials behind.

Synset Examples

they discarded all their inessentials

Related Terms

  • Antonym: essential - a thing that is absolutely necessary
  • Synonym: nonessential - something not essential; not necessary

Etymology

From Latin in- (not) + essentialis (essential).
