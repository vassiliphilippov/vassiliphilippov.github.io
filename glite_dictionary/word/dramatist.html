dramatist

1. NOUN

/ ˈdræmətɪst / (AmE), / ˈdræmətɪst / (BrE)
a person who writes plays for the theater
person who writes plays

Examples

The famous dramatist premiered his play to rave reviews in London.
Many aspiring writers study the works of Shakespeare, the greatest dramatist of all time.
The dramatist spent years perfecting the dialogue for her latest tragedy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: playwright - a person who writes plays

Etymology

From French 'dramatiste', from Greek 'dramat-', stem of 'drāma' (drama).
