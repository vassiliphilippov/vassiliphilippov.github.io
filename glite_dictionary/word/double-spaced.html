double-spaced

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʌbəl speɪst / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl speɪst / (BrE)
(of text) having a blank line between each line, so that every other line is left empty.
(of text) with a blank line between each line

Examples

Essays for this class must be double-spaced and printed on one side only.
The editor asked her to submit a double-spaced copy of the manuscript.
He prefers to write reports in double-spaced format for clarity and ease of editing.

Synset Examples

manuscripts must be double-spaced

Related Terms

  • Antonym: single-spaced - (of text) with no blank lines between each line of type
