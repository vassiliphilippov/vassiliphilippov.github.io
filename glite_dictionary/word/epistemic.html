epistemic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛpɪˈstɛmɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈstiːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to knowledge or to the theory of knowledge (epistemology)
relating to knowledge or epistemology

Examples

The philosopher discussed various epistemic approaches to understanding reality.
In linguistics, an epistemic modal expresses the speaker’s degree of certainty about a statement.
Her research focused on the epistemic foundations of scientific inquiry.

Synset Examples

epistemic modal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: epistemological - relating to epistemology, the study of knowledge

Etymology

From Greek epistēmē (knowledge), from epistamai (to know).
