reunification

1. NOUN

/ ˌriːˌjuːnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌjuːnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of bringing together parts that were previously separated, especially referring to countries, organizations, or groups
the act of coming together again

Examples

The reunification of the country was celebrated with marches and fireworks.
Talks aimed at the reunification of the two companies resumed last month.
After years of separation, reunification brought new challenges as well as hope.

Synset Examples

monetary unification precipitated the reunification of the German state in October 1990

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unification - the process of being united or made into a whole

Etymology

From re- (again) + unification; based on unify, from Latin unificare ‘make one’.
