Ulaanbaatar

1. PROPN

/ uːˌlɑːnˈbɑːtɑːr / (AmE), / uːˌlɑːnˈbɑːtɑː / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Mongolia, located in the north-central part of the country
capital city of Mongolia

Examples

After traveling for days across the steppe, they finally arrived in Ulaanbaatar.
The winter temperatures in Ulaanbaatar are among the coldest of any capital city.
She moved from London to Ulaanbaatar to teach English.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ulan Bator - alternative spelling for the capital of Mongolia

Etymology

From Mongolian Улаанбаатар (Ulaanbaatar), meaning 'Red Hero'.
