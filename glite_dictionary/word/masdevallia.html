masdevallia

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæzdəˈvæliə / (AmE), / ˌmazdɪˈvalɪə / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Masdevallia, a group of small evergreen orchids with colorful flowers, usually having fused sepals forming a tubular shape.
small orchid plant of genus Masdevallia

Examples

The greenhouse contains a rare masdevallia blooming in the corner.
Botanists admire the unique flower shape of the masdevallia genus.
Several masdevallias require cool temperatures and high humidity to thrive.

Etymology

Named after Spanish botanist José Masdevall.
