nuptial

1. ADJ

/ ˈnʌp.tʃəl / (AmE), / ˈnʌp.tʃəl / (BrE)
relating to marriage or a wedding ceremony
relating to weddings or marriage

Examples

Their nuptial vows were exchanged in a beautiful outdoor ceremony.
The hotel offers special packages for couples celebrating their nuptial festivities.
During the nuptial procession, music filled the air and guests cheered.

Synset Examples

bridal procession
nuptial day
spousal rites

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bridal - relating to a bride or a wedding
  • Synonym: marital - relating to marriage or the relationship between married people

Etymology

From Latin nuptialis (pertaining to marriage), from nuptiae (wedding).
