Panadol

1. NOUN

/ ˈpænəˌdɔl / (AmE), / ˈpænədɒl / (BrE)
A trademarked brand of acetaminophen (paracetamol) used to relieve mild pain and reduce fever.
brand of acetaminophen (paracetamol)

Examples

If you have a headache, you can take Panadol to relieve the pain.
After her fever rose, the nurse gave her two Panadol tablets.
Many people prefer Panadol because it is gentle on the stomach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acetaminophen - a medicine used to relieve pain and reduce fever
  • Synonym: paracetamol - another common name for acetaminophen
