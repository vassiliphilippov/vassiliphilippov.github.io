pinky

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋki / (BrE)
the smallest finger on the hand, furthest from the thumb; little finger
smallest finger; little finger

Examples

She wore a tiny ring on her pinky.
Children sometimes link pinkies to make a promise.
His pinky was injured during the baseball game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: little finger - the smallest finger on the hand, farthest from the thumb

Etymology

Diminutive form of 'pink', possibly influenced by Dutch 'pink' (little finger).
