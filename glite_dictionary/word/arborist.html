arborist

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrbərɪst / (AmE), / ˈɑːbərɪst / (BrE)
a person trained in the care and maintenance of trees, especially damaged or diseased ones
tree care specialist

Examples

The city hired an arborist to assess the health of its oldest oak trees.
After the storm, an arborist was called to help save several damaged maples.
If you notice disease in your trees, consult an experienced arborist for advice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tree surgeon - a professional who treats damaged trees

Etymology

From Latin arbor (tree) + -ist (one who practices).
