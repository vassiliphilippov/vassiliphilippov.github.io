to rollerblade

1. VERB

/ ˈroʊlərˌbleɪd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊləˌbleɪd / (BrE)
to travel or move on inline skates with a single row of wheels
move on inline skates

Examples

Every weekend, she likes to rollerblade through the city park with her friends.
He rollerbladed down the waterfront, enjoying the breeze and sunshine.
They spent the afternoon rollerblading along the beach promenade.

Synset Examples

you'd better wear a helmet and knee protectors when you Rollerblade!
The muscular actor loves to Rollerblade

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skate - to move on skates

Etymology

From Rollerblade, the trademarked name of a popular brand of inline skates, used as a genericized verb.
