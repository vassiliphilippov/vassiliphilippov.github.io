Jap

1. NOUN

/ dʒæp / (AmE), / dʒap / (BrE)
An offensive slang term for a person of Japanese descent.
offensive slang for Japanese person
offensive slang

Examples

He was upset to hear someone use the word Jap in conversation.
The term Jap is widely recognized as offensive and should not be used.
During wartime, negative propaganda often included the slur Jap in its messages.

Etymology

Shortened from 'Japanese', first attested in early 20th-century English, especially during World War II.
