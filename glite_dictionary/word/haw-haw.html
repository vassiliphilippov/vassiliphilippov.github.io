haw-haw

1. NOUN

/ hɑːˈhɑː / (AmE), / hɔːˈhɔː / (BrE)
a loud, harsh laugh that resembles the neighing of a horse
loud, braying laugh

Examples

He let out a booming haw-haw that made everyone turn around.
Her distinctive haw-haw echoed across the room during the comedian's act.
You could hear his haw-haw from the other end of the hall.
