Li Bai

1. PROPN

/ liː baɪ / (AmE), / liː baɪ / (BrE)
a celebrated Chinese lyric poet of the Tang dynasty, known for his romantic and imaginative poetry
famous Chinese Tang dynasty poet

Examples

The poetry of Li Bai is revered for its beauty and emotion.
Scholars often compare Li Bai and Du Fu as the two great poets of the Tang dynasty.
Many of Li Bai's verses celebrate nature, friendship, and wine.
