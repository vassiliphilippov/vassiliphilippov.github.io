snidely

1. ADV

/ ˈsnaɪdli / (AmE), / ˈsnaɪdli / (BrE)
In a sneering, mocking, or contemptuous manner.
in a sneering or mocking manner

Examples

He responded snidely to every question, making the whole interview uncomfortable.
"Nice job on the project," she said snidely, clearly not meaning it as praise.
When asked about his opponent, the politician snidely dismissed his achievements.

Synset Examples

‘I don't believe in these customs,’ he said sneeringly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sneeringly - in a sneering manner
  • Antonym: respectfully - in a respectful manner

Etymology

From "snide" (sneering, derogatory) + "-ly" (adverb-forming suffix).
