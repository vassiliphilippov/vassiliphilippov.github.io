razmataz

1. NOUN

/ ˈræz.məˌtæz / (AmE), / ˈraz.məˌtaz / (BrE)
exciting or elaborate action designed to impress or confuse, especially in sports or performance
exciting, flashy display to impress or confuse

Examples

The quarterback surprised everyone with a bit of razmataz during the final play.
Their performance was full of razmataz, entertaining the crowd with every move.
The team's coach insisted they focus on basics instead of unnecessary razmataz.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: razzmatazz - flamboyant or elaborate activity or display
  • Synonym: razzle-dazzle - elaborate action done to impress or confuse, especially in sports

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'razzmatazz', origin uncertain; possibly imitative or from 'razzle-dazzle'.
