CIRA

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiː ˈaɪ ˈɑːr ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˈsiː ˈaɪ ˈɑːr ˈeɪ / (BrE)
an Irish republican paramilitary organization formed in 1994 as a clandestine, armed group opposed to the Northern Ireland peace process
Irish republican paramilitary group (est. 1994)

Examples

The CIRA claimed responsibility for several attacks after the 1998 Good Friday Agreement.
Authorities believe the CIRA continues to oppose political compromise in Northern Ireland.
Police in Belfast have increased security due to threats from the CIRA.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: IRA - Irish Republican Army, an earlier Irish paramilitary organization

Etymology

Acronym of Continuity Irish Republican Army.
