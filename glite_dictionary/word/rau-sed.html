Rau-Sed

1. NOUN

/ ˈraʊˌsɛd / (AmE), / ˈraʊˌsɛd / (BrE)
A brand name for an antihypertensive drug containing an alkaloid extracted from the plant Rauwolfia serpentina.
trade name for antihypertensive drug

Examples

The doctor prescribed Rau-Sed to help control her high blood pressure.
After switching to Rau-Sed, his blood pressure readings improved significantly.
Older antihypertensive treatments like Rau-Sed are used less frequently today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reserpine - an antihypertensive drug from Rauwolfia serpentina
