strikingly

1. ADV

/ ˈstraɪkɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈstraɪkɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that attracts attention or is very noticeable.
in a way that stands out or is noticeable

Examples

The painting was strikingly beautiful, capturing everyone's attention as soon as they entered the gallery.
Her voice is strikingly similar to her mother's when she sings.
The mountains rose strikingly against the clear blue sky.

Synset Examples

this was strikingly demonstrated
the evidence was strikingly absent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noticeably - in a way that can be easily noticed
  • Antonym: subtly - in a delicate or not obvious way

Etymology

Formed from 'striking' + '-ly'. 'Striking' from the verb 'strike' (from Old English 'strīcan', meaning 'to stroke').
