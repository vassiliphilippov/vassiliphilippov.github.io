boner

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊnər / (AmE), / ˈbəʊnə / (BrE)
a silly or embarrassing mistake
an embarrassing mistake

Examples

He realized too late that he'd made a huge boner during the presentation.
Calling her by the wrong name was a classic boner that left everyone chuckling.
The newspaper printed a boner in the headline that caused quite a stir.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blunder - a careless or stupid mistake
  • Synonym: error - a mistake

Etymology

Originally American slang, possibly from 'bonehead', referring to a blunder.
