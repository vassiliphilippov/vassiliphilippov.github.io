caducean

1. ADJ

/ kəˈduːsiən / (AmE), / kəˈdjuːsiən / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling a caduceus, especially as a symbol of medicine or healing.
relating to a caduceus (medical symbol)

Examples

The doctor's badge bore a caducean emblem, symbolizing his commitment to healing.
Many medical organizations use caducean imagery in their official logos.
The conference featured a large caducean staff at the entrance to welcome attendees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caduceus - a staff with two snakes wrapped around it, often used as a symbol of medicine

Etymology

From caduceus, with the suffix -an to form an adjective.
