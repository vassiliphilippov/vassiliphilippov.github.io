visitant

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪzɪtənt / (AmE), / ˈvɪzɪtənt / (BrE)
a person who visits a place; a visitor, especially in a formal or literary context
person who visits; visitor

Examples

The ancient castle was said to be frequented by a mysterious visitant each night.
During the festival, every visitant received a warm welcome from the villagers.
The poet described the ghost as a mournful visitant from another world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visitor - a person who visits a place

Etymology

From Latin 'visitant-', present participle of 'visitare' (to visit).
