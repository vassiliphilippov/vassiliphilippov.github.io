Snoopy

1. PROPN

/ ˈsnuːpi / (AmE), / ˈsnuːpi / (BrE)
A fictional beagle in the comic strip 'Peanuts' by Charles Schulz, known for his imaginative personality and adventures.
famous beagle from 'Peanuts' cartoon

Examples

Snoopy is often seen lying on the roof of his doghouse, dreaming of daring adventures.
Children and adults alike adore Snoopy for his wit and charm in the Peanuts comics.
In the Halloween special, Snoopy imagines himself as the World War I Flying Ace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Peanuts - comic strip featuring Snoopy
