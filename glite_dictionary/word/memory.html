memory

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛməri / (AmE), / ˈmɛməri / (BrE)
the branch of cognitive psychology that studies mental processes involved in storing and recalling information
area of psychology studying how memory works

Examples

She specialized in memory and learning during her doctoral research.
The seminar focused on recent advances in memory studies within psychology.
Understanding memory is essential for developing effective teaching methods.

Synset Examples

he taught a graduate course on learning and memory

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cognitive psychology - the scientific study of mental processes such as perception, memory, and reasoning
  • Antonym: amnesia - loss of memory

Etymology

From Latin memoria (remembrance, memory), from memor (mindful, remembering).
