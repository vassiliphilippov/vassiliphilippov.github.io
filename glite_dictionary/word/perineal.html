perineal

1. ADJ

/ pɛrɪˈniːəl / (AmE), / pɛrɪˈniːəl / (BrE)
relating to or situated in the region of the perineum
relating to the perineum
medical

Examples

The patient experienced perineal discomfort after the surgery.
A perineal tear is a common complication during childbirth.
Doctors recommend keeping the perineal area clean to prevent infections.

Synset Examples

perineal pains

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perineum - the area between the anus and genitals

Etymology

From perineum + -al, with perineum from Greek perinaion (space between anus and genitals).
