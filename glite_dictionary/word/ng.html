ng

1. NOUN

/ ɛn dʒi / (AmE), / ɛn dʒi / (BrE)
an abbreviation for nanogram; one billionth (1/1,000,000,000) of a gram
abbreviation for nanogram (one billionth of a gram)
medical

Examples

The laboratory found only 5 ng of the substance in the sample.
Hormone levels as low as 10 ng per milliliter can still affect your body.
Each pill contains 50 ng of the active ingredient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nanogram - one billionth of a gram
  • Degree Higher: mg - abbreviation for milligram (one thousandth of a gram)

Etymology

Abbreviation of nanogram, from 'nano-' (a factor of one billionth) and 'gram'.
