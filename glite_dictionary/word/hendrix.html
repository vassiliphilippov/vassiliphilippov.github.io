Hendrix

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɛn.drɪks / (AmE), / ˈhɛn.drɪks / (BrE)
Jimi Hendrix (1942–1970), American guitarist and singer-songwriter whose innovative electric guitar techniques greatly influenced rock music.
American rock guitarist and music icon
