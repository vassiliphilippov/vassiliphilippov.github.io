helical

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛlɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈhiːlɪkəl / (BrE)
having the shape or form of a helix; spiral or coiled
in the shape of a coil or spiral

Examples

The staircase wound upward in a helical pattern around the central column.
DNA molecules are famous for their helical structures.
The artist fashioned a helical sculpture out of polished metal for the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiral - winding in a continuous and gradually widening curve

Etymology

From Greek 'helix, helikos' meaning 'spiral' or 'twisted'.
