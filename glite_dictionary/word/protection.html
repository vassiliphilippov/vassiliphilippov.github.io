protection

1. NOUN

/ prəˈtɛkʃən / (AmE), / prəˈtɛkʃən / (BrE)
the use of duties or quotas on imports to safeguard domestic industries from foreign competition
trade policy to shield domestic industries

Examples

The government increased protection to help local manufacturers compete with imports.
Opponents argue that protection can lead to trade wars and higher consumer prices.
His economic plan called for more protection to defend domestic jobs.

Synset Examples

he made trade protection a plank in the party platform

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protectionism - the policy of protecting domestic industries through trade barriers
  • Antonym: free trade - policy of no restrictions on imports or exports
Source: WordNet

Synset POS: n
