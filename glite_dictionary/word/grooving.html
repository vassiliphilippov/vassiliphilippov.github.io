grooving

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡruːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡruːvɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of cutting spiral grooves into the inside of a gun barrel to improve bullet stability
spiral grooves cut inside a gun barrel

Examples

The technician examined the grooving in the barrel to ensure it met safety standards.
Proper grooving increases the accuracy of a firearm by causing the bullet to spin.
The grooving process is an essential step in the manufacture of modern rifles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rifling - the process of making spiral grooves inside a firearm barrel
