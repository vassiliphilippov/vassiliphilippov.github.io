lb

1. NOUN

/ ɛl biː / (AmE), / ɛl biː / (BrE)
A unit of weight equal to 16 ounces avoirdupois, used chiefly in the US and the UK; abbreviation for 'pound.'
unit of weight (pound)

Examples

The package weighs just under 2 lb and will ship easily.
She lost five lb on her new diet program.
The label states that each bag contains exactly one lb of coffee.

Synset Examples

he got a hernia when he tried to lift 100 pounds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pound - a unit of weight equal to 16 ounces avoirdupois
  • Degree Lower: ounce - a unit of weight equal to one sixteenth of a pound

Etymology

Abbreviation of Latin 'libra', meaning 'scales' or 'balance', the ancient Roman unit of weight.
