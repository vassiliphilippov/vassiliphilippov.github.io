stratification

1. NOUN

/ ˌstræt̬ɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌstrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
The arrangement of something into layers or strata.
arrangement in layers or strata

Examples

Geologists study the stratification of rock formations to understand Earth's history.
The stratification of society often leads to unequal access to resources.
Water in the lake shows clear stratification during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: layering - the process of forming or depositing layers

Etymology

From Medieval Latin stratificatio (layering).
