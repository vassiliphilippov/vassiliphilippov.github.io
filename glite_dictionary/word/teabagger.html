teabagger

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiːˌbæɡər / (AmE), / ˈtiːˌbaɡə / (BrE)
A derogatory term for a supporter of the Tea Party political movement in the United States.
derogatory term for Tea Party supporter
offensive american

Examples

He was labeled a teabagger after supporting the rally against government spending.
Many people found the term teabagger offensive and refused to use it in public discussions.
During the debate, she accused her opponent of being a typical teabagger.

Synset Examples

He was labeled a teabagger for his vocal protests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tea Partier - Supporter of the Tea Party movement

Etymology

Adopted in American political discourse around 2009 from 'Tea Party' (referring to the political movement) and 'teabag', with earlier vulgar slang connotations influencing its pejorative use.
