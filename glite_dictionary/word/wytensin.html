Wytensin

1. NOUN

/ waɪˈtɛnsɪn / (AmE), / waɪˈtɛnsɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for the antihypertensive drug guanabenz, used to lower blood pressure through its effect on the central nervous system.
