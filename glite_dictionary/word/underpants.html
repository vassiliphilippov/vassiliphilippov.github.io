underpants

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌn.dɚˌpænts / (AmE), / ˈʌn.dəˌpænts / (BrE)
An item of underwear covering the lower part of the body from the waist to the upper thighs, usually worn next to the skin.
garment worn under outer clothes covering lower body

Examples

He forgot to pack enough underpants for his vacation.
After the gym, she changed into a clean pair of underpants.
His mother always buys cotton underpants for comfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: briefs - undergarment covering the groin and buttocks
  • Synonym: panties - women's or girls' underpants
  • Synonym: boxers - men's loose-fitting underpants
  • Antonym: outerwear - clothing worn over underwear

Etymology

From 'under' + 'pants', originating in the mid 19th century.
