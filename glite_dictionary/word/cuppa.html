cuppa

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌpə / (AmE), / ˈkʌpə / (BrE)
(British, informal) a cup of tea.
cup of tea (British, informal)
informal british

Examples

Would you like a cuppa before you head out?
She always starts her morning with a strong cuppa.
After the long walk, we enjoyed a hot cuppa together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tea - hot drink made by infusing dried tea leaves in water

Etymology

Colloquial contraction of 'cup of' in British English.
