ardour

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrdɚ / (AmE), / ˈɑːdə / (BrE)
a strong and intense feeling of love or passion
intense feeling of love; passion
british

Examples

She confessed her ardour for him in a handwritten letter.
The ardour of their youthful romance gradually faded with time.
His poetry was inspired by an ardour that never seemed to diminish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passion - strong and barely controllable emotion; intense enthusiasm or love
  • Antonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern

Etymology

From Old French 'ardeur', from Latin 'ardor' (heat, passion).
