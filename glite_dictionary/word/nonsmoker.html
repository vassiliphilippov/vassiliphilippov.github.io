nonsmoker

1. NOUN

/ nɑnˈsmoʊkər / (AmE), / nɒnˈsməʊkə / (BrE)
a person who does not smoke tobacco products
person who does not smoke tobacco

Examples

The restaurant offers a special section for nonsmokers.
As a lifelong nonsmoker, she found the smell of cigarettes unpleasant.
The new policy protects the health of nonsmokers in public places.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smoker - person who smokes tobacco
