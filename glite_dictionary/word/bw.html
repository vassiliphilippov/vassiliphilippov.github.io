BW

1. NOUN

/ biː ˈdʌbəl.juː / (AmE), / biː ˈdʌbəl.juː / (BrE)
abbreviation for biological warfare: the use of bacteria, viruses, or toxins to cause harm or death to people, animals, or crops.
abbreviation: biological warfare

Examples

The international community has strict protocols against the use of BW in conflict.
Scientists are working to detect and counter potential BW threats worldwide.
Reports indicated that enemy forces might have prepared BW agents for deployment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biological warfare - the use of biological agents as weapons

Etymology

Abbreviation of biological warfare.
