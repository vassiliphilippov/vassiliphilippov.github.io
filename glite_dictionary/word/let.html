LET

1. PROPN

/ ɛl iː tiː / (AmE), / ɛl iː tiː / (BrE)
A militant Islamic organization based in Pakistan, known as Lashkar-e-Toiba, active in Kashmir and elsewhere, which seeks to restore Islamic rule in India, often through violent means.
militant Islamic group, Lashkar-e-Toiba
legal

Examples

The Indian government considers LET a significant security threat to the region.
Security forces claimed to have arrested a suspected LET operative near the border.
Reports linked the bombing incident to the LET network based in Pakistan.

Synset Examples

Lashkar-e-Toiba has committed mass murders of civilian Hindus

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lashkar-e-Toiba - A militant Islamist group, also known as LET

Etymology

Acronym of Lashkar-e-Toiba, from Urdu لشکر طیبہ‎ (the Army of the Good).
