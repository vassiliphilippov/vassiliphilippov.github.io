gimel

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪməl / (AmE), / ˈɡɪməl / (BrE)
the third letter of the Hebrew alphabet
third letter of the Hebrew alphabet

Examples

The letter gimel is often used as an abbreviation in Hebrew texts.
She learned to write gimel along with the other letters of the Hebrew alphabet.
On the dreidel, the symbol for gimel signifies that the player takes the entire pot.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: aleph - the first letter of the Hebrew alphabet
  • Degree Lower: beth - the second letter of the Hebrew alphabet

Etymology

From Hebrew גִּימֵל (gīmēl), meaning 'camel.'
