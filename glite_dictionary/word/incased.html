incased

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈkeɪst / (AmE), / ɪnˈkeɪst / (BrE)
covered or protected with or as if with a case
covered or protected by a case

Examples

The statue was carefully incased in glass to prevent any damage.
Her precious jewels were incased in velvet-lined boxes for safekeeping.
The ancient manuscript arrived incased in multiple protective layers.

Synset Examples

knights cased in steel
products encased in leatherette

Related Terms

  • Synonym: encased - covered or protected with or as if with a case

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'encased', from prefix 'en-' + 'case'.
