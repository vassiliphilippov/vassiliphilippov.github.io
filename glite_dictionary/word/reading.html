Reading

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɛd.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛd.ɪŋ / (BrE)
A city on the River Thames in Berkshire, southern England, known for its historic abbey and as a commercial centre.
city in Berkshire, England

Examples

Reading is famous for its annual music festival, attracting visitors from all over the UK.
He moved to Reading last year to start a new job in the city centre.
The train from London to Reading takes about twenty-five minutes.
