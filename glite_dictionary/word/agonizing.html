agonizing

1. ADJ

/ ˈæɡəˌnaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈæɡənʌɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
causing great physical or mental pain; extremely painful or distressing
extremely painful or distressing

Examples

The patient described the injury as agonizing, unable to move without intense pain.
Waiting for the test results was an agonizing experience for everyone involved.
She let out an agonizing scream when she twisted her ankle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: painful - causing pain
  • Degree Higher: unbearable - too painful or unpleasant to tolerate
  • Degree Lower: uncomfortable - causing slight pain or discomfort

Etymology

From 'agonize' (to suffer agony) + -ing, from Greek 'agōn' (contest).
