to eff

1. VERB

/ ɛf / (AmE), / ɛf / (BrE)
(informal, often euphemistic) To have sexual intercourse with someone; used as a less explicit substitute for 'fuck'.
have sexual intercourse (euphemistic)
informal

Examples

He claimed he had never effed anyone before marriage.
The comedian joked that he couldn't say 'fuck' on TV, so he just effed instead.
She whispered, 'Did you just eff her?' in disbelief.

Synset Examples

Adam knew Eve
Were you ever intimate with this man?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuck - to have sexual intercourse with (vulgar)
  • Synonym: to make love - to have sexual intercourse with someone

Etymology

Alteration of 'fuck', using the pronunciation of the letter 'F' as a euphemistic substitute.
