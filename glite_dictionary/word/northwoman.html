Northwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrθˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈnɔːθˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is a native or inhabitant of Scandinavia
female native of Scandinavia

Examples

The legendary Northwoman was known for her courage in battle.
In the ancient sagas, a Northwoman was often depicted as both fierce and wise.
She met a Northwoman during her travels in Scandinavia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Northman - a male inhabitant of Scandinavia

Etymology

Formed from 'North' (referring to northern Europe) + 'woman'.
