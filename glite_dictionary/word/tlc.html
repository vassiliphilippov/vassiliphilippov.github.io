TLC

1. NOUN

/ ti ɛl si / (AmE), / tiː ɛl siː / (BrE)
kind and thoughtful care or attention, especially when someone is unwell or something needs repair
kind, thoughtful care or attention

Examples

That old bike just needs a little TLC to look like new again.
After surgery, the puppy required lots of TLC.
The garden will bloom beautifully with some regular TLC.

Synset Examples

young children need lots of TLC

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tender loving care - kind and gentle attention or nurture

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'tender loving care', first used in the 20th century.
