margate

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːrˌɡeɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɑːɡeɪt / (BrE)
A tropical fish of the grunt family (Haemulon album), with a large mouth often tinged with red, found from Florida to Brazil.
a tropical grunt fish native to western Atlantic

Examples

Fishermen often catch margate near coral reefs in the Caribbean Sea.
The margate is recognized by its large mouth and silvery body.
Divers commonly spot margate swimming in schools along the Florida coast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grunt - marine fish of the family Haemulidae, known for grunting sound
