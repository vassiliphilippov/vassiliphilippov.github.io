underwear

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌndɚˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌwɛə / (BrE)
clothes worn next to the skin and under other clothes.
clothing worn under outer garments

Examples

She bought new underwear before her vacation.
In some cultures, it's considered rude to show your underwear in public.
He packed socks and underwear at the top of his suitcase.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undergarment - a piece of clothing worn under outer clothes

Etymology

Formed from 'under' + 'wear' in English, indicating clothing worn underneath.
