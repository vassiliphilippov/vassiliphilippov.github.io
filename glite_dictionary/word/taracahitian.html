Taracahitian

1. NOUN

/ ˌtærəkɑːˈhitiən / (AmE), / ˌtarəkəˈhɪtiən / (BrE)
a member of an indigenous group of peoples of Mexico
member of an indigenous Mexican group

Examples

The Taracahitian people have a rich tradition of storytelling and music.
Anthropologists studied the languages spoken by different Taracahitian communities.
Artifacts found at the site were linked to the Taracahitian culture.
