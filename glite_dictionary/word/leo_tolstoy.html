Leo Tolstoy

1. PROPN

/ liːoʊ tɔlˈstɔɪ / (AmE), / lɪəʊ tɒlˈstɔɪ / (BrE)
(1828–1910) Russian novelist and philosopher, renowned for major works such as 'War and Peace' and 'Anna Karenina'.
Russian novelist, author of 'War and Peace'

Examples

Many consider Leo Tolstoy's 'War and Peace' to be one of the greatest novels ever written.
The philosophy of Leo Tolstoy influenced leaders like Gandhi and Martin Luther King Jr.
She wrote her thesis on the moral vision of Leo Tolstoy in 19th-century Russian literature.

Etymology

From Russian Лев Толстой (Lev Tolstoy).
