nigher

1. ADV

/ ˈnaɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪ.ə / (BrE)
(Archaic or literary) comparative of ‘nigh’; within a shorter distance; nearer.
comparative of ‘nigh’; closer
archaic

Examples

As we approached the summit, the peak grew ever nigher above us.
The village lay nigher to the river than I had remembered.
With each step, the danger drew nigher.

Synset Examples

come closer, my dear!
they drew nearer
getting nearer to the true explanation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nearer - more near; at a shorter distance
  • Degree Lower: nigh - close in distance or time

Etymology

Formed from "nigh" (Old English ‘nēah’), with comparative suffix ‘-er’.
