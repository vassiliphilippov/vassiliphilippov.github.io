ex-servicewoman

1. NOUN

/ ɛk ˈsɜːrvɪswʊmən / (AmE), / ɛks ˈsɜːvɪswʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who has previously served in the armed forces, especially as a member of the military
woman formerly in the armed forces

Examples

As an ex-servicewoman, she helps other veterans adapt to civilian life.
The award was given to an ex-servicewoman for her dedication to charity work.
Many employers seek the skills that an ex-servicewoman can bring to the workplace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ex-serviceman - a man who has previously served in the armed forces
  • Synonym: veteran - someone who has served in the armed forces, especially in war

Etymology

Formed from 'ex-' (former) + 'servicewoman' (female member of the armed forces).
