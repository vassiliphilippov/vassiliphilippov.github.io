housekeeping

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌkipɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaʊskiːpɪŋ / (BrE)
the management and routine tasks involved in maintaining a house, such as cleaning, cooking, and general domestic work
management and cleaning of a house

Examples

Good housekeeping is essential for a comfortable and healthy living environment.
She found it challenging to balance housekeeping with her full-time job.
Some hotels offer daily housekeeping services for their guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: housework - regular work done to keep a house clean and tidy

Etymology

Formed from 'house' + 'keeping', where 'keeping' means care or maintenance.
