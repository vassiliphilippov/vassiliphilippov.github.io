Dramamine

1. NOUN

/ ˈdræməˌmiːn / (AmE), / ˈdræməmiːn / (BrE)
A trademarked antihistamine and antiemetic drug used to treat motion sickness and nausea.
motion sickness and nausea medication (trade name)

Examples

You should take Dramamine before the boat trip to prevent getting seasick.
My doctor recommended Dramamine for my long flight to avoid nausea.
She keeps Dramamine in her bag whenever she goes on road trips.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dimenhydrinate - the generic name for the drug sold as Dramamine
