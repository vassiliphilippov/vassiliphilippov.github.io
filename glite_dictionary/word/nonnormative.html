nonnormative

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈnɔrmətɪv / (AmE), / nɒnˈnɔːmətɪv / (BrE)
Not conforming to or based on a norm; not standard or typical.
not conforming to a norm

Examples

The study focused on nonnormative behaviors in adolescent groups.
Researchers investigated the impact of nonnormative life events on psychological development.
Her views on gender identity are considered nonnormative in some cultures.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: normative - conforming to a norm

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'non-' (not) + 'normative' (from Latin 'norma', a rule).
