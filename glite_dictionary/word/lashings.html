lashings

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæʃɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈlæʃɪŋz / (BrE)
a very large amount of something, especially used informally or humorously
a large quantity (informal)
informal british

Examples

We had lashings of delicious chocolate sauce on our ice cream.
The picnic was a feast with lashings of sandwiches, cakes, and tea.
There were lashings of praise for her outstanding performance.
