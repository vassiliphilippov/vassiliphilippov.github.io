Teheran

1. PROPN

/ tɛˈhɛːrɑn / (AmE), / tɛˈhɛːræn / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Iran, located in the northern part of the country
capital city of Iran

Examples

Many tourists visit Teheran to explore its historic sites and vibrant bazaars.
The negotiations took place in Teheran over several days.
She moved to Teheran for her university studies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tehran - alternative spelling of the capital city of Iran

Etymology

From Persian 'Tehrān' (تهران‎).
