Katharine Hepburn

1. PROPN

/ ˌkæθərɪn ˈhɛpˌbɝn / (AmE), / ˌkæθərɪn ˈhɛpˌbɜːn / (BrE)
(1907–2003) A prominent American film actress known for her distinctive voice, strong-willed characters, and a career spanning over six decades.
famous American film actress (1907–2003)

Examples

Many critics consider Katharine Hepburn one of the greatest actresses in film history.
Katharine Hepburn starred in 'The Philadelphia Story' and 'On Golden Pond.'
The partnership between Spencer Tracy and Katharine Hepburn became legendary in Hollywood.
