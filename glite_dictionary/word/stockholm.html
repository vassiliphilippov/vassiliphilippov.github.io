Stockholm

1. PROPN

/ ˈstɑkˌhoʊm / (AmE), / ˈstɒkhəʊm / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Sweden, situated in the southeast of the country on the Baltic Sea.
capital city of Sweden

Examples

The Nobel Prize ceremony takes place each year in Stockholm.
She moved from London to Stockholm for her new job.
Stockholm is famous for its beautiful archipelago and vibrant cultural scene.

Synset Examples

the Nobel Prize is awarded in Stockholm

Etymology

From Swedish 'Stockholm', from 'stock' (log) + 'holm' (islet), referring to the islets and logs protecting the city.
