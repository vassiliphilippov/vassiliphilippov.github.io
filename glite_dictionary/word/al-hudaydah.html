Al-Hudaydah

1. PROPN

/ æl huːˈdaɪdə / (AmE), / æl huːˈdaɪdə / (BrE)
A major port city in Yemen, located on the Red Sea coast.
port city in Yemen on the Red Sea

Examples

The busy port of Al-Hudaydah plays a crucial role in Yemen's economy.
Humanitarian aid ships regularly dock at Al-Hudaydah to deliver supplies.
Fierce fighting broke out near Al-Hudaydah as rival forces sought control of the city.

Etymology

From Arabic ٱلْحُدَيْدَة‎ (al-Ḥudaydah), meaning 'the iron', possibly referring to the iron-rich sand.
