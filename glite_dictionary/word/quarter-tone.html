quarter-tone

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɔrtɚ toʊn / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə təʊn / (BrE)
a musical interval equal to half a semitone, used in some music systems and compositions
musical interval half of a semitone

Examples

Some contemporary composers use quarter-tones to achieve unique musical effects.
The singer struggled to match the exact quarter-tone required by the piece.
In Middle Eastern music, melodies often include intervals of a quarter-tone between notes.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: semitone - a musical interval equivalent to a half step
