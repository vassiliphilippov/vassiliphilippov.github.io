celebrater

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛl.əˌbreɪ.tər / (AmE), / ˈsɛl.ɪˌbreɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person who is engaged in celebrating an occasion or event
person who is celebrating

Examples

The celebrater raised her glass for a toast at the party.
Every celebrater at the festival wore a colorful mask.
The awards ceremony was filled with laughter from each celebrater in the crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celebrant - a person who celebrates or takes part in a celebration, esp. a religious one
  • Synonym: celebrator - someone who celebrates, especially an occasion or event

Etymology

From celebrate + -er, denoting a person who celebrates.
