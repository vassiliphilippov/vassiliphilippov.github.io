explicable

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛksplɪkəbəl / (AmE), / ɪkˈsplɪkəbəl / (BrE)
able to be explained or understood; capable of being accounted for
able to be explained or understood

Examples

Her sudden anger was entirely explicable given the circumstances.
The strange noise was later found to be explicable by a loose window.
Many natural phenomena were once mysterious but are now explicable through science.

Synset Examples

explicable behavior

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inexplicable - impossible to explain or account for

Etymology

From Latin 'explicabilis' (capable of being explained), from 'explicare' (unfold, explain).
