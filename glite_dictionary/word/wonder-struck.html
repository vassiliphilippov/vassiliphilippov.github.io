wonder-struck

1. ADJ

/ ˈwʌndɚˌstrʌk / (AmE), / ˈwʌndəˌstrʌk / (BrE)
Experiencing a feeling of great wonder or amazement.
filled with wonder or amazement

Examples

She stood wonder-struck before the breathtaking mountain view.
The audience was wonder-struck by the magician's final trick.
As a child, he often felt wonder-struck during visits to the planetarium.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astonished - feeling great surprise or amazement
  • Antonym: unimpressed - not feeling admiration or respect

Etymology

Formed from 'wonder' (Old English *wundor*) and 'struck', meaning 'hit with wonder'.
