columbary

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlʌmbɛri / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmbəri / (BrE)
A structure or building designed to house pigeons or doves, typically with compartments for nesting.
building for housing pigeons or doves

Examples

The old estate still has a columbary where pigeons once nested.
We restored the columbary in the garden as a home for doves.
The roof of the columbary needed repair after the storm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dovecote - a small building for housing doves or pigeons

Etymology

From Latin 'columbarium' (dovecote), from 'columba' (dove, pigeon).
