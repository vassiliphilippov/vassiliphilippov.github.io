politicalness

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪtɪkəlnəs / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəlnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being political or related to politics.
the quality of being political

Examples

The politicalness of the decision was quickly recognized by members of both parties.
Some scholars debate the politicalness of art and whether it can ever be truly neutral.
She was uncomfortable with the increasing politicalness of their conversations.

Synset Examples

Maybe you could even define a topic as political in this context somehow, or at least build a test for the politicalness of a topic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: political - relating to the government or public affairs of a country

Etymology

Derived from 'political' + '-ness', forming a noun indicating a state or quality.
