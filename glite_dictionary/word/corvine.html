corvine

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɔːrˌvaɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɔːvaɪn / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a crow; of or pertaining to crows or the crow family.
relating to a crow

Examples

The bird's corvine features included its glossy black feathers and sharp beak.
With a corvine intelligence, the raven solved the puzzle quickly.
His costume had a distinctly corvine appearance, complete with faux feathers and a pointed beak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crow-like - resembling or characteristic of a crow

Etymology

From Latin 'corvinus', from 'corvus' (crow).
