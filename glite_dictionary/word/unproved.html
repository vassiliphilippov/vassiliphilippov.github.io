unproved

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpruvd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpruːvd / (BrE)
not shown to be true or correct; not verified or proven
not proven or verified

Examples

The accused was released due to the unproved nature of the allegations.
Many theories about ancient history remain unproved.
She refused to act on unproved rumors without more evidence.

Synset Examples

unproved allegations
unproved assumptions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unproven - not proven or verified; alternative form
  • Antonym: proven - shown to be true or correct

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'proved', past participle of 'prove'.
