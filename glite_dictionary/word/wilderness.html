Wilderness

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪldərnəs / (AmE), / ˈwɪldənəs / (BrE)
a wooded region in northeastern Virginia, USA, known as the site of major battles during the American Civil War in 1864.
Civil War battle site in Virginia

Examples

The Battle of the Wilderness marked the beginning of Grant's Overland Campaign in 1864.
Many soldiers were lost in the dense thickets of the Wilderness during the fierce fighting.
Historians often visit the Wilderness to study the tactics used during the Civil War battles.
