mealie

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːli / (AmE), / ˈmiːli / (BrE)
an ear of corn (maize), especially in South African English
an ear of corn (maize)

Examples

We roasted a mealie over the fire for lunch.
The farmer harvested dozens of fresh mealies from his field.
Children love to eat a hot mealie straight from the cob.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corn - a cereal plant (maize), especially in American English
  • Synonym: maize - a cereal plant grown for its large grains, also known as corn

Etymology

From Afrikaans 'mielie', from Portuguese 'milho' (maize).
