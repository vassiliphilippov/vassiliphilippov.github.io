trancelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrænzˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtrɑːnslʌɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a trance; appearing as if in a daze or deeply absorbed state
resembling a trance or daze

Examples

He moved through the crowded room in a trancelike state, hardly noticing anyone around him.
Her trancelike expression made it clear that she was lost in thought.
The repetitive music put the audience into a trancelike calm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hypnotic - causing a trance-like state or resembling hypnosis
  • Antonym: alert - fully aware and attentive
