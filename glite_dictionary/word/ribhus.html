Ribhus

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɪb.us / (AmE), / ˈrɪb.uːz / (BrE)
In Hindu mythology, any of three divine craftsmen who serve the gods as artisans.
artisans of Hindu gods in mythology

Examples

The Ribhus were famed for their skill and ingenuity in crafting divine objects.
Ancient texts often describe how the Ribhus aided the gods with their artistry.
Some hymns in the Rigveda are dedicated to the Ribhus and their accomplishments.

Etymology

From Sanskrit ऋभु (ṛbhu), meaning 'clever, skillful, inventive'.
