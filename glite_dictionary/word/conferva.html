Conferva

1. NOUN

/ kənˈfɜrvə / (AmE), / kənˈfɜːvə / (BrE)
A former genus of filamentous green algae, now regarded as obsolete and reclassified under other genera within the family Tribonemaceae.
obsolete genus of green algae

Examples

Early botanists classified many threadlike algae under the genus Conferva.
The name Conferva is now largely obsolete in modern taxonomic literature.
Samples previously labeled as Conferva have been reassigned to other genera.

Etymology

From Latin *conferva*, meaning a kind of water plant.
