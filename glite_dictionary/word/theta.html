theta

1. NOUN

/ ˈθeɪtə / (AmE), / ˈθiːtə / (BrE)
the eighth letter of the Greek alphabet (Θ, θ), used in mathematics, science, and linguistics.
Greek letter; used in maths and science

Examples

In geometry, the angle is labeled with the letter theta.
The pronunciation of theta varies between Greek and English.
Scientists often use theta to represent temperature change in physics.

Etymology

From Greek θῆτα (thēta).
