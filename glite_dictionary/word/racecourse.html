racecourse

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪsˌkɔrs / (AmE), / ˈreɪsˌkɔːs / (BrE)
an area of ground specially prepared and used for horse racing or other races
track for horse racing or other races

Examples

We spent the afternoon at the racecourse watching the horses compete.
The annual derby attracts thousands of visitors to the racecourse.
A new grandstand is being built at the city's main racecourse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racetrack - a track or course where races take place, especially horse races
