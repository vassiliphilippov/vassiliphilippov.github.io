tined

1. ADJ

/ taɪnd / (AmE), / taɪnd / (BrE)
having prongs or tines; used in combination to specify the number or type of prongs.
having prongs or tines

Examples

She used a three-tined fork to pick up the salad.
The farmer replaced the broken tined attachment on his pitchfork.
We found an old single-tined tool in the shed.

Synset Examples

a three-tined fork

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pronged - having prongs; similar in meaning to 'tined'

Etymology

From 'tine' (prong, forked projection), of Old English origin.
