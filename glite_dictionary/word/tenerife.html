Tenerife

1. PROPN

/ ˌtɛnəˈriːf / (AmE), / ˌtɛnəˈriːfə / (BrE)
an island belonging to Spain, located in the Atlantic Ocean off the northwestern coast of Africa; the largest of the Canary Islands
Spanish island; largest of the Canary Islands

Examples

They spent a wonderful week exploring Tenerife and its stunning volcanic landscapes.
The climate in Tenerife is mild year-round, attracting tourists from all over the world.
Many visitors hike up Mount Teide, the highest point in Tenerife.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Tenerife', possibly of Guanche origin.
