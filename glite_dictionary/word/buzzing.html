buzzing

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʌzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʌzɪŋ / (BrE)
full of continuous, low, humming noise, like the sound made by bees; lively and noisy with activity or excitement
full of humming noise or lively activity

Examples

The city streets were buzzing with excitement during the festival.
Her phone was buzzing all afternoon with messages from friends.
When I walked into the café, it was buzzing with cheerful conversation.

Synset Examples

the room was abuzz over the latest scandal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humming - making a low, steady continuous sound
  • Antonym: silent - without any sound or noise

Etymology

Present participle of 'buzz', imitating the sound made by bees or similar insects.
