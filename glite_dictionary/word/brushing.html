brushing

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrʌʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrʌʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of cleaning your teeth by using a brush, especially a toothbrush
cleaning teeth with a brush

Examples

A regular brushing routine helps prevent cavities and gum disease.
Children often need to be reminded about proper brushing techniques.
The dentist checked the child's teeth and asked about his brushing habits.

Synset Examples

the dentist recommended two brushes a day

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toothbrushing - the act of cleaning one's teeth with a toothbrush

2. NOUN

/ ˈbrʌʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrʌʃɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of moving a brush through something, especially to smooth or tidy hair.
the act of brushing, esp. hair

Examples

She gave her hair a quick brushing before leaving the house.
Regular brushing helps keep your hair healthy and shiny.
After his shower, he spent a few minutes brushing in front of the mirror.

Synset Examples

he gave his hair a quick brush

Related Terms

  • Synonym: combing - the act of using a comb to arrange or tidy hair

Etymology

From the verb 'brush' + -ing.
