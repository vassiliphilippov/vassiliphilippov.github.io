applier

1. NOUN

/ əˈplaɪɚ / (AmE), / əˈplaɪə / (BrE)
a device used to apply a substance to a surface or object
device for applying a substance

Examples

The paint applier made it easy to coat the wall evenly.
A mascara applier often comes with the product for convenience.
This handy glue applier ensures precise placement without a mess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: applicator - a device used for putting something on something else

Etymology

From apply + -er, denoting an agent or device that applies.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02733263-n
Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ əˈplaɪ.ər / (AmE), / əˈplaɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who requests or seeks something, such as a job, assistance, or admission, by submitting an application
person who submits an application

Examples

Each applier must submit their documents before the application deadline.
The company received more than a hundred appliers for the single position.
The applier should have relevant experience and strong communication skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: applicant - a person who applies, especially for a job or place at a college
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09630310-n
Synset POS: n
