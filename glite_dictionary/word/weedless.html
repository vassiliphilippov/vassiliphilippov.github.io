weedless

1. ADJ

/ ˈwiːdləs / (AmE), / ˈwiːdləs / (BrE)
(of land, a garden, etc.) free from weeds.
free from weeds

Examples

After hours of careful work, the lawn was finally weedless.
He preferred to grow vegetables in a weedless plot for better yields.
The park's weedless flowerbeds were admired by all the visitors.

Synset Examples

a weedless garden

Related Terms

  • Antonym: weedy - full of or covered with weeds

Etymology

Formed from 'weed' + the suffix '-less', meaning 'without weeds'.
