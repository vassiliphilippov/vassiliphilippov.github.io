vapourised

1. ADJ

/ ˈveɪpəraɪzd / (AmE), / ˈveɪpəraɪzd / (BrE)
(British spelling) Converted into a vapor or gas, especially by the application of heat.
converted into vapor or gas
british

Examples

The water was vapourised instantly when it touched the hot metal surface.
Chemicals vapourised during the experiment, filling the lab with a pungent smell.
Any spilled fuel vapourised quickly in the summer heat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vaporized - converted into vapor or gas (American spelling)
