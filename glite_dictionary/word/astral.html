astral

1. ADJ

/ ˈæstrəl / (AmE), / ˈæstrəl / (BrE)
relating to, or resembling stars; emanating from or connected with stars.
relating to stars

Examples

The scientist studied the astral patterns visible through the telescope.
Her paintings were inspired by the astral landscape of the night sky.
Mystics often describe out-of-body experiences as journeys taken by the astral self.

Synset Examples

an astral body
stellar light

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stellar - relating to stars

Etymology

From Latin 'astralis', from Greek 'astron' (star).
