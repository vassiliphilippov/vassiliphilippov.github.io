unitedly

1. ADV

/ juˈnaɪtɪdli / (AmE), / juˈnaɪtɪdli / (BrE)
In a united manner; with cooperation and mutual support.
in a united or cooperative manner

Examples

The team members acted unitedly to solve the problem efficiently.
We must stand unitedly if we want to achieve our common goal.
All nations responded unitedly to the international crisis.

Synset Examples

we worked together on the project

Related Terms

  • Synonym: together - with or in proximity to another person or people; in combination

Etymology

From 'united' + '-ly', formed as an adverb from the adjective 'united'.
