tail-shaped

1. ADJ

/ teɪl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / teɪl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of an animal's tail.
having the shape of a tail

Examples

The instrument has a distinctive tail-shaped handle that fits comfortably in the hand.
Some leaves are tail-shaped, making them easy to identify among other species.
The artist designed a tail-shaped pendant inspired by marine animals.
