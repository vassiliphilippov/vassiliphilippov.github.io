buying

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of purchasing something
the act of purchasing

Examples

Online buying has become increasingly popular over the last decade.
Smart buying often involves comparing prices from different retailers.
Her job involves the buying of goods for the store.

Synset Examples

buying and selling fill their days
shrewd purchasing requires considerable knowledge

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purchasing - the act of buying something
  • Antonym: selling - the act of offering something for sale

Etymology

From the verb 'buy' (Old English bycgan).
