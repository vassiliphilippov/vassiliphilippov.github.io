Akka

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑːkə / (AmE), / ˈækə / (BrE)
A town and port in northwestern Israel on the eastern Mediterranean, also known as Acre.
town and port in Israel

Examples

The ancient walls of Akka attract many tourists every year.
The port of Akka has been a strategic site for centuries.
She visited Akka during her trip to northern Israel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Acre - another name for Akka, referring to the same town in Israel

Etymology

From Hebrew עַכּוֹ (ʿAkko), known in English as Acre.
