Lester Young

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɛstər jʌŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛstə jʌŋ / (BrE)
(1909–1959) An American jazz tenor saxophonist celebrated for his smooth style and influential contributions to the development of modern jazz.
American jazz tenor saxophonist (1909–1959)

Examples

Many jazz enthusiasts regard Lester Young as one of the most innovative saxophonists of the 20th century.
The smooth phrasing of Lester Young influenced generations of saxophone players.
I listened to a classic recording featuring Lester Young with the Count Basie Orchestra.
