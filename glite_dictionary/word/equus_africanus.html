Equus africanus

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛk.wəs ˌæf.rɪˈkɑː.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɛk.wəs ˌæf.rɪˈkɑː.nəs / (BrE)
A species of wild ass native to Africa, known for its role as the ancestor of the domestic donkey.
wild African ass species

Examples

The rare Equus africanus is considered the ancestor of the modern domestic donkey.
Conservation efforts aim to protect the remaining wild populations of Equus africanus.
Scientists studied the habitat preferences of Equus africanus in northeastern Africa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wild ass - a wild member of the horse family (genus Equus)
  • Degree Lower: donkey - a domesticated descendant of Equus africanus

Etymology

From Latin: 'Equus' meaning 'horse' and 'africanus' meaning 'African'.
