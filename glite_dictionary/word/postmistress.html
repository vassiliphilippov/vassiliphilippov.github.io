postmistress

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstˌmɪstrəs / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstˌmɪstrɪs / (BrE)
a woman who is in charge of a post office
female head of a post office

Examples

The postmistress greeted everyone in the village by name as they collected their mail.
As the new postmistress, she quickly learned the routines of the small-town post office.
Local residents fondly remembered the postmistress who served the community for over thirty years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: postmaster - a person in charge of a post office

Etymology

Formed from 'postmaster' + feminine suffix '-ess'.
