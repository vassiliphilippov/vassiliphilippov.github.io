invigorating

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈvɪɡəˌreɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɪɡərətɪŋ / (BrE)
making you feel healthier, more energetic, or more alive; giving strength or vitality.
giving strength or energy

Examples

The invigorating sea breeze made our walk along the shore delightful.
She enjoys an invigorating morning run to start her day.
A cup of strong coffee can have an invigorating effect when you're feeling tired.

Synset Examples

the invigorating mountain air

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refreshing - making you feel refreshed or revived
  • Antonym: enervating - causing one to feel drained of energy

Etymology

From 'invigorate' + '-ing'; 'invigorate' from Latin 'in-' (in, into) + 'vigor' (liveliness).
