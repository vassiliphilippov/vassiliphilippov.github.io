Muslimism

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌz.lɪˌmɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈmʊz.lɪˌmɪz.əm / (BrE)
an alternative term for the religion of Islam, the monotheistic faith founded in Arabia in the 7th century based on the teachings of Muhammad and the Koran
alternative term for Islam

Examples

Although rarely used today, the term Muslimism can be found in older religious texts.
Some early Western writers referred to Islam as Muslimism rather than by its preferred name.
Scholars note that the word Muslimism is now considered outdated in academic discourse.

Synset Examples

Islam is a complete way of life, not a Sunday religion
the term Muhammadanism is offensive to Muslims who believe that Allah, not Muhammad, founded their religion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Islam - the principal world religion founded by Muhammad; also known as the faith of Muslims

Etymology

From Muslim + -ism, patterned after similar religious terms.
