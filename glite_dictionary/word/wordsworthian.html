Wordsworthian

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɝːdzˈwɝːθiən / (AmE), / ˌwɜːdzˈwɜːθiən / (BrE)
Characteristic of or relating to the English poet William Wordsworth or his works, especially in emphasis on nature and the imagination.
of or relating to William Wordsworth or his works

Examples

The novel adopts a distinctly Wordsworthian approach, focusing on the beauty and tranquility of the countryside.
Her poetry is filled with Wordsworthian reverence for nature and simple rural life.
Critics have described his philosophical ideas as having a Wordsworthian sensitivity to personal emotion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Romantic - relating to the Romantic movement in literature and art

Etymology

Formed from the name Wordsworth + -ian (belonging to, characteristic of).
