Ingerman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡərmən / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡəmən / (BrE)
A member of a western Finnish people who formerly lived in the region around the Baltic province where Saint Petersburg was built.
member of a western Finnish people

Examples

The Ingerman people maintained distinct cultural traditions despite Russian influence.
Archaeological findings reveal the lifestyle of the Ingerman community before the founding of Saint Petersburg.
Few descendants of the Ingerman population remain in the region today.
