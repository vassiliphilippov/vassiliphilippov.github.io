markedly

1. ADV

/ ˈmɑrkɪdli / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that is very noticeable or distinct.
in a noticeably distinct way

Examples

The temperature has increased markedly over the past decade.
Their performance improved markedly after the new coach arrived.
Interest in the subject declined markedly during the following years.

Synset Examples

sales of luxury cars dropped markedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distinctly - in a way that is clear and easy to notice
  • Antonym: slightly - to a small degree, not very noticeable

Etymology

From 'marked' + '-ly', where 'marked' comes from Old English 'mearcian' (to mark).
