meteoritical

1. ADJ

/ ˌmitiəˈrɪtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmiːtiəˈrɪtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by meteorites
relating to meteorites

Examples

The scientist presented her findings at the meteoritical society's annual conference.
They are conducting meteoritical research to analyze the composition of the specimens.
His interest in meteoritical phenomena began when he witnessed a meteor shower as a child.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meteoritic - relating to or caused by meteorites

Etymology

Formed from 'meteorite' + '-ical', modelled after 'meteorite'.
