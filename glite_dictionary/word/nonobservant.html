nonobservant

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnəbˈzɜrvənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəbˈzɜːvənt / (BrE)
Not practicing or following the customs or rules of a particular religion.
not practicing religious customs

Examples

She was raised in a Jewish family but now considers herself nonobservant.
Many nonobservant members of the community still celebrate major holidays for cultural reasons.
Although born into a Muslim family, he remained largely nonobservant throughout his life.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: observant - following and practicing religious customs and rules

Etymology

From non- (not) + observant (from Latin observare, to watch or attend to).
