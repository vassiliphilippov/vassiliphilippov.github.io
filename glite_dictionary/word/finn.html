Finn

1. NOUN

/ fɪn / (AmE), / fɪn / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Finland
person from Finland

Examples

Her grandfather was a proud Finn who moved to America in the 1950s.
The Finns celebrate their Independence Day on December 6 every year.
As a Finn, she enjoys the long summer days in Helsinki.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Finnish - the Finno-Ugric language spoken in Finland

Etymology

From Old Norse *Finnr*, referring to people of northern Scandinavia; modern usage denotes inhabitants of Finland.
