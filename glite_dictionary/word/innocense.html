innocense

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnəsɛns / (AmE), / ˈɪnəsəns / (BrE)
A Californian wildflower with white, lavender, or pale-blue flowers that grow in distinct rings around the stem.
Californian wildflower with ringed flowers

Examples

The meadow was dotted with innocense blooming in neat, colorful bands.
Botanists identified the unusual plant as a species of innocense native to California.
Each spring, hikers admire the pagoda-like rings of innocense along the hillside.
