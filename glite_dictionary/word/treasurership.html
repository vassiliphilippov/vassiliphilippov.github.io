treasurership

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɛʒərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒərəʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or office of a treasurer, especially in an organization or institution
the position of a treasurer

Examples

She was proud to accept the treasurership of the local charity.
During his treasurership, the club's finances improved significantly.
The treasurership comes with a great deal of responsibility and trust.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: treasurer - person managing funds in an organization

Etymology

Formed from 'treasurer' + '-ship' (denoting position, office).
