West Virginian

1. NOUN

/ wɛst vərˈdʒɪniən / (AmE), / wɛst vəˈdʒɪniən / (BrE)
a native or resident of West Virginia, a state in the eastern United States
a person from West Virginia

Examples

Her family has been West Virginian for generations.
As a proud West Virginian, he always celebrates the state's founding day.
Many West Virginians enjoy outdoor activities in the Appalachian Mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Virginian - a native or resident of Virginia
