chumminess

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃʌmi nəs / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌmɪ nɪs / (BrE)
friendly and informal behavior that creates a sense of easy familiarity and sociability
friendly, sociable manner

Examples

There was a surprising chumminess between the rival team captains at the press conference.
Her natural chumminess made everyone feel comfortable at the meeting.
He disliked the artificial chumminess at company parties and preferred genuine interactions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly
  • Antonym: aloofness - the quality of being distant or reserved

Etymology

From 'chummy' (friendly, like a chum) + '-ness' (state or quality).
