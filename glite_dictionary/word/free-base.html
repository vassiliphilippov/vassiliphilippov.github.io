to free-base

1. VERB

/ ˈfriˌbeɪs / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌbeɪs / (BrE)
to use purified cocaine by burning it and inhaling the fumes
use cocaine by burning and inhaling fumes

Examples

The addict admitted he would free-base cocaine late into the night.
Authorities warn of the dangers associated with free-basing illicit drugs.
He was caught attempting to free-base in the abandoned building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoke - inhale and exhale the smoke of a substance

Etymology

From 'free base,' referring to the chemically purified form of cocaine, plus the verb-forming use of 'base.'
