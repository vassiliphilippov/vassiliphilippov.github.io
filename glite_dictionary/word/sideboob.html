sideboob

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪdˌbuːb / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌbuːb / (BrE)
A portion of a woman's breast that is visible from the side, usually due to the style of clothing.
side view of exposed breast
informal slang

Examples

The dress she wore last night showed a hint of sideboob on the red carpet.
Some fashion styles are designed to highlight sideboob for a bold statement.
She adjusted her top to avoid any accidental sideboob while posing for photos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleavage - the hollow between a woman's breasts as revealed by a low-cut garment

Etymology

Formed from 'side' + 'boob' (slang for breast), popularized in late 20th century.
