Cashmere

1. PROPN

/ ˈkæʒmɪr / (AmE), / ˈkæʃmɪə / (BrE)
A region in southwestern Asia, whose control is contested between India and Pakistan.
disputed region in southwestern Asia

Examples

The situation in Cashmere remains a significant challenge for peace in South Asia.
Many travel writers have described the breathtaking beauty of Cashmere.
Tensions often arise due to the long-standing disagreement over Cashmere's sovereignty.

Etymology

From Persian *Kashmīr*, the name of the region.
