refluxy

1. ADJ

/ ˈriːˌflʌksi / (AmE), / ˈriːˌflʌksi / (BrE)
feeling symptoms of acid reflux or heartburn, such as discomfort or a burning sensation in the chest
feeling of acid reflux or heartburn
informal medical

Examples

After that big, greasy meal, I started to feel refluxy and uncomfortable.
If you feel refluxy, try drinking a glass of water to ease the sensation.
Some people feel refluxy at night if they eat too close to bedtime.

Synset Examples

Eating spicy food always makes me feel refluxy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartburn - a burning sensation in the chest due to acid reflux
  • Synonym: acid reflux - a condition where stomach acid flows back into the esophagus

Etymology

From 'reflux' (the backward flow of acid) + '-y' (forming adjectives).
