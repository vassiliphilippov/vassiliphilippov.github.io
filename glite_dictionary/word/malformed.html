malformed

1. ADJ

/ mælˈfɔrmd / (AmE), / mælˈfɔːmd / (BrE)
badly or abnormally shaped; so out of shape as to be unattractive or disfigured
badly or abnormally shaped

Examples

The puppy was born with a malformed paw that made it difficult to walk.
Radiologists discovered a malformed artery during the medical examination.
Many ancient coins are slightly malformed due to imperfect minting processes.

Synset Examples

deformed thalidomide babies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deformed - so badly formed or out of shape as to be unattractive
  • Synonym: misshapen - having an abnormal or irregular shape

Etymology

From Latin 'mal-' (bad) + 'formed', from 'forma' (shape, form).
