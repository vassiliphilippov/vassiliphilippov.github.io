hi-yah

1. INTJ

/ haɪˈjɑ / (AmE), / haɪˈjɑː / (BrE)
an exclamation used to accompany a sharp movement or strike, especially in martial arts
martial arts exclamation
informal

Examples

With a sudden hi-yah, the martial artist broke the board in half.
She leapt into the air, yelling hi-yah as she landed her kick.
The children shouted hi-yah while pretending to be karate masters in the backyard.

Synset Examples

With a loud 'hi-yah,' he launched himself into the air.
