ebulliently

1. ADV

/ ɪˈbʌliəntli / (AmE), / ɪˈbʌlɪəntli / (BrE)
In a lively, enthusiastic, or exuberant manner.
in a lively or enthusiastic way

Examples

She greeted her old friends ebulliently, her joy evident to everyone in the room.
The team captain ebulliently accepted the trophy, thanking the crowd for their support.
Despite the challenges, he spoke ebulliently about the future of the project.

Synset Examples

Khrushchev ebulliently promised to supply rockets for the protection of Cuba against American aggression

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enthusiastically - in a way that shows intense enjoyment or interest

Etymology

From 'ebullient' (from Latin ebullientem, present participle of ebullire 'to bubble out').
