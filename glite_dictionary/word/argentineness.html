Argentineness

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːrdʒənˈtiːnnəs / (AmE), / ˌɑːdʒənˈtiːnnəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being Argentine in origin, character, or style.
quality of being Argentine

Examples

Many immigrants embraced Argentineness as they settled into the country and adopted local customs.
Her music is infused with a strong sense of Argentineness, drawing on traditional styles and rhythms.
The film explores questions of identity and Argentineness in a globalized world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Argentine - a person from Argentina or relating to Argentina

Etymology

From Argentine (relating to Argentina) + -ness (forming nouns denoting quality or state).
