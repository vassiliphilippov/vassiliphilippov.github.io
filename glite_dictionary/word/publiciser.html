publiciser

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪˌsaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪˌsaɪzə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make something widely known, especially to promote a person, event, or product to the public
a person who publicizes something
british

Examples

The publiciser arranged interviews with several major newspapers.
As a freelance publiciser, she specializes in promoting small businesses online.
Every successful author needs a skilled publiciser to help build their reputation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: publicist - a person responsible for publicizing a person, event, or product

Etymology

From British English 'publicise' (to make public) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
