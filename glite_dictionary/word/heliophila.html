heliophila

1. NOUN

/ ˌhiːlioʊˈfɪlə / (AmE), / ˌhiːliəˈfɪlə / (BrE)
a genus of South African herbs and subshrubs cultivated for their long clusters of bright blue flowers with white centers
genus of South African flowering plants

Examples

Gardeners often choose heliophila for its striking blue flowers and ease of care.
The bright blooms of heliophila add vibrant color to spring gardens.
Several species of heliophila are popular among horticulturists for ornamental purposes.

Etymology

From Greek hēlios (sun) + philos (loving), meaning 'sun-loving'.
