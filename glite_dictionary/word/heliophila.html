Heliophila

1. NOUN

/ ˌhiːliˈɒfɪlə / (AmE), / ˌhiːlɪˈɒfɪlə / (BrE)
a genus of South African flowering herbs and subshrubs in the family Brassicaceae
genus of South African flowering plants

Examples

The South African plains are home to several species within the Heliophila genus.
Researchers have discovered a new member of the Heliophila in the Cape region.
Many gardeners admire the delicate blue flowers produced by Heliophila species.

Etymology

From Greek helios (sun) + philos (loving), referring to sun-loving nature of the plants.
