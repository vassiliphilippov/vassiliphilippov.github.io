/ ɛm aɪ / (AmE), / ɛm aɪ / (BrE)

An abbreviation for British government agencies responsible for intelligence and security, such as MI5 (domestic security) and MI6 (foreign intelligence).
UK intelligence service abbreviation
british

Examples

The existence of MI sections dates back to the early 20th century.
Several novels feature agents working for MI in covert operations.
After the breach, the government turned to MI to investigate the threat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: MI5 - the United Kingdom's domestic security agency
  • Synonym: MI6 - the United Kingdom's foreign intelligence service

4. NOUN

/ ˌɛmˈaɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛmˈaɪ / (BrE)
A medical abbreviation for myocardial infarction, the destruction of heart tissue resulting from obstruction of the blood supply to the heart muscle (commonly called a heart attack).
medical abbreviation for heart attack
medical

Examples

The doctor confirmed that the patient had suffered an MI during the night.
Prompt treatment can greatly improve the chances of recovery after an MI.
The hospital tracks all cases of MI in its cardiac care unit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: myocardial infarction - destruction of heart tissue caused by blocked blood supply
  • Synonym: heart attack - sudden loss of blood supply to part of the heart

Etymology

Abbreviation of myocardial infarction, from Greek mys (muscle) + kardia (heart) + Latin infarctus (stuffed, obstructed).
