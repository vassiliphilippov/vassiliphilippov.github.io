Moravia

1. PROPN

/ məˈreɪviə / (AmE), / məˈreɪviə / (BrE)
a historical region in the central and eastern part of the Czech Republic, situated east of Bohemia and west of the Carpathians.
region in the Czech Republic

Examples

She traveled across Moravia to explore its castles and vineyards.
The folklore and traditions of Moravia are an important part of Czech culture.
Brno is the largest city in Moravia and a major cultural center.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin Moravia, from the river Morava.
