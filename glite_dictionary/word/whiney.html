whiney

1. ADJ

/ ˈwaɪni / (AmE), / ˈwaɪni / (BrE)
often complaining in a childish or annoying way
habitually complaining or whining

Examples

The whiney toddler kept asking for snacks every five minutes.
Her whiney tone made it hard to sympathize with her complaints.
He grew tired of his friend's whiney attitude about schoolwork.

Synset Examples

a whiny child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whiny - habitually complaining in an irritating way
