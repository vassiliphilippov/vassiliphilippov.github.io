Ugandan

1. NOUN

/ juˈɡændən / (AmE), / juˈɡændən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Uganda
native or inhabitant of Uganda

Examples

The new ambassador is a prominent Ugandan with years of diplomatic experience.
Many Ugandans celebrated Independence Day with music and dance.
She is interviewing a Ugandan author for her latest article.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Uganda - a landlocked country in East Africa

2. ADJ

/ juːˈɡændən / (AmE), / juːˈɡændən / (BrE)
of or relating to Uganda or its people.
relating to Uganda or its people

Examples

The Ugandan government introduced new educational reforms last year.
She enjoyed learning about Ugandan culture and traditions during her travels.
Several Ugandan artists displayed their work at the international exhibition.

Synset Examples

Ugandan game parks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Uganda - a country in East Africa

Etymology

From Uganda + -an (suffix forming adjectives and demonyms).
