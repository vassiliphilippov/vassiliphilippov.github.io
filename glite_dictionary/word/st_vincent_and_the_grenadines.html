St. Vincent and the Grenadines

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt ˈvɪnsənt ənd ðə ˌɡrɛnəˈdiːnz / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈvɪnsənt ənd ðə ˌɡrɛnəˈdiːnz / (BrE)
An island country in the southeastern Caribbean, consisting of the island of Saint Vincent and a chain of smaller Grenadine islands; independent since 1979.
island country in the Caribbean

Examples

She traveled to St. Vincent and the Grenadines for her honeymoon last summer.
International aid was sent to St. Vincent and the Grenadines after the volcano eruption.
Cricket is a popular sport in St. Vincent and the Grenadines.
