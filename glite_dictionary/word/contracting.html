contracting

1. NOUN

/ kənˈtræktɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈtræktɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of becoming infected with a disease or illness
becoming infected with a disease

Examples

The contracting of malaria remains a significant risk in certain regions of the world.
He was hospitalized after the contracting of a bacterial infection.
Early detection can improve treatment outcomes after the contracting of a serious illness.

Synset Examples

catching cold is sometimes unavoidable
the contracting of a serious illness can be financially catastrophic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infection - the process of becoming infected by a disease

Etymology

From the verb 'contract', from Latin 'contractus' (drawn together, tightened), later meaning 'to acquire' (a disease).
