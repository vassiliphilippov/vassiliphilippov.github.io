barong

1. NOUN

/ bəˈrɔŋ / (AmE), / bəˈrɒŋ / (BrE)
a heavy, broad-bladed knife resembling a cleaver, traditionally used in the Philippines
Philippine broad-bladed knife

Examples

The chef admired the craftsmanship of the traditional barong displayed in the market.
Collectors often seek out a barong for its historical and cultural significance.
He used a barong to chop the wood with remarkable ease.

Etymology

From Filipino and Malay 'barong', denoting this specific knife.
