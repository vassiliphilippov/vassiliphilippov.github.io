Beantown

1. PROPN

/ ˈbinˌtaʊn / (AmE), / ˈbiːnˌtaʊn / (BrE)
An informal name for Boston, the capital and largest city of Massachusetts.
informal name for Boston
informal american

Examples

Many sports fans dream of visiting Beantown to watch a Celtics game at TD Garden.
She moved to Beantown for college and quickly fell in love with the city's history.
The marathon attracts thousands of runners to Beantown every spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Boston - the capital and largest city of Massachusetts

Etymology

An American English nickname for Boston, referring to the traditional dish of baked beans popular in the city.
