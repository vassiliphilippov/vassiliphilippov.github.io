Hel

1. PROPN

/ hɛl / (AmE), / hɛl / (BrE)
(in Norse mythology) the goddess of the dead and queen of the underworld.
Norse goddess of the underworld

Examples

In Norse legends, Hel rules over the souls of those who did not die in battle.
The goddess Hel is often depicted as half alive and half dead, symbolizing her domain.
Scholars study the myths surrounding Hel to understand ancient Norse beliefs about death.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hades - Greek god of the underworld

Etymology

From Old Norse Hel, the name of the underworld goddess.
