500

1. NUM

/ faɪv ˈhʌndrəd / (AmE), / faɪv ˈhʌndrəd / (BrE)
The number five hundred; a quantity or amount equal to five hundred.
the number five hundred (500)

Examples

There were exactly 500 people at the concert last night.
She needs to collect 500 signatures for the petition.
The company donated 500 blankets to the shelter after the storm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: five hundred - the number 500

Etymology

From Old English 'fīf hund' (five hundred), combining 'fīf' (five) and 'hund' (hundred).
