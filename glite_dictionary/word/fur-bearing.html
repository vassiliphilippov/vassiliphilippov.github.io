fur-bearing

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɝˌbɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɜːˌbɛrɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an animal) having fur, especially fur of a quality suitable for commercial use.
having fur, especially commercially valuable fur

Examples

Several fur-bearing animals, like mink and beavers, are trapped for their pelts.
Laws exist to regulate the hunting of fur-bearing mammals in this region.
The fur-bearing seal population has rebounded thanks to conservation efforts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: furred - covered with fur
