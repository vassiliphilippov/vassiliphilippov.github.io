sene

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪneɪ / (AmE), / ˈseːneɪ / (BrE)
a monetary unit of Samoa, equal to one-hundredth of a tala
Samoan monetary unit

Examples

In Samoa, ten sene coins are commonly used for small purchases.
The price was listed as five tala and twenty sene.
He exchanged his dollars for sene and tala at the airport.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: tala - the basic monetary unit of Samoa
  • Synonym: cent - a monetary unit equal to one-hundredth of a basic unit in many countries
