Lewiston

1. PROPN

/ ˈluːɪstən / (AmE), / ˈluːɪstən / (BrE)
A city in northwestern Idaho, United States, located at the confluence of the Snake and Clearwater Rivers.
city in northwestern Idaho, US

Examples

She moved to Lewiston last summer for a new job opportunity.
The Snake River Festival in Lewiston attracts visitors from all over the state.
While traveling through Idaho, we spent a night in Lewiston.

Etymology

Named after explorer Meriwether Lewis, of Lewis and Clark fame.
