articular

1. ADJ

/ ɑːrˈtɪkjələr / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
Relating to the joints of the body.
relating to body joints
medical

Examples

The doctor examined the articular cartilage for signs of damage.
Inflammation in any articular region can cause significant pain and limited movement.
Osteoarthritis often results in changes to the articular surfaces of the bones.

Synset Examples

the articular surfaces of bones
articular disease

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joint - a point at which two parts of the skeleton are fitted together

Etymology

From Latin 'articularis', from 'articulus' meaning 'joint'.
