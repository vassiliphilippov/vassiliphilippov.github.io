Oswald Spengler

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑzwɑld ˈspɛŋlər / (AmE), / ˈɒzwəld ˈspɛŋlə / (BrE)
(1880–1936) A German philosopher and historian known for proposing that civilizations grow and decline in cyclical patterns.
German philosopher; cyclical theory of cultures

Examples

Many scholars reference Oswald Spengler when discussing the rise and fall of civilizations.
The ideas of Oswald Spengler influenced twentieth-century theories of history.
She wrote her thesis on the works of Oswald Spengler.
