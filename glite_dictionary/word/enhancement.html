enhancement

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈhænsmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈhɑːnsmənt / (BrE)
an improvement that increases the quality, value, or attractiveness of something
an improvement or increase in value

Examples

The software update included a significant enhancement to the security features.
Adding fresh flowers was a simple enhancement to the room's decor.
The new policy is an enhancement of the previous rules.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: improvement - the act of making something better
  • Antonym: deterioration - the process of becoming worse

Etymology

From the verb 'enhance', from Middle English enhauncen, from Anglo-Norman enhaucer, ultimately from Latin 'altus' (high, tall).
