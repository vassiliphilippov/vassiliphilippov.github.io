Livingstone

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪvɪŋˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋstəʊn / (BrE)
David Livingstone (1813–1873), Scottish missionary, explorer, and physician, renowned for his expeditions in Africa and the discovery of Victoria Falls.
Scottish explorer and missionary (1813–1873)

Examples

Livingstone is credited with the discovery of Victoria Falls on the Zambezi River.
Many biographies have been written about the intrepid explorer Livingstone.
The name Livingstone is often associated with the spirit of exploration and adventure.
