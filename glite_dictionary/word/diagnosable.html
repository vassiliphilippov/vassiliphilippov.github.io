diagnosable

1. ADJ

/ ˌdaɪəɡˈnoʊzəbl / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəɡˈnəʊzəbl / (BrE)
Able to be identified or determined as a particular disease or condition by diagnosis.
able to be diagnosed

Examples

The condition is diagnosable with a simple blood test.
Early symptoms may not be diagnosable until the disease has progressed.
Thanks to new techniques, many rare disorders are now diagnosable.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: undiagnosable - not able to be diagnosed

Etymology

From diagnose + -able, meaning 'able to be diagnosed.'
