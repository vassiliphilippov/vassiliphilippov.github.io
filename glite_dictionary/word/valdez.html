Valdez

1. PROPN

/ vælˈdiz / (AmE), / vælˈdiːz / (BrE)
a port city on the southern coast of Alaska, known as an oil shipping hub
Alaskan port city and oil terminal

Examples

The tanker left Valdez loaded with crude oil bound for California.
After the earthquake, Valdez was largely rebuilt on more stable ground.
Valdez became widely known after the Exxon oil spill in 1989.
