enwrapped

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈræpt / (AmE), / ɪnˈræpt / (BrE)
completely absorbed or engrossed in something; giving full attention to something.
completely absorbed or engrossed

Examples

She sat enwrapped in her book, oblivious to the noises around her.
He listened to the music, enwrapped in its haunting melody.
The audience was enwrapped in the magician's performance, barely daring to breathe.

Synset Examples

that engrossed look of rapt delight
enwrapped in dreams
so intent on this fantastic … narrative that she hardly stirred
rapt with wonder
wrapped in thought

Related Terms

  • Synonym: engrossed - completely occupied or absorbed in something
  • Synonym: absorbed - fully focused or engaged in something
  • Antonym: distracted - having one's attention diverted

Etymology

From prefix 'en-' (to cause to be) + 'wrap'.
