recitalist

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsaɪ.t̬əl.ɪst / (AmE), / rɪˈsaɪ.təl.ɪst / (BrE)
A musician who performs recitals, especially as a soloist.
musician who gives recitals

Examples

She established her reputation as a talented recitalist touring across Europe.
The recitalist captivated the audience with her expressive interpretations of Chopin.
As a recitalist, he specializes in performing solo piano concerts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soloist - a musician who performs a solo piece or leads in a performance

Etymology

From 'recital' + suffix '-ist', indicating a person who practices.
