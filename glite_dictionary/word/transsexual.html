transsexual

1. NOUN

/ trænˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / trænˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
a person whose gender identity differs from the sex they were assigned at birth and who has undergone medical procedures to change their physical sex
person who has undergone sex reassignment

Examples

After years of struggle, Kim finally began living as a transsexual.
The clinic provides medical support for transsexual individuals seeking gender-affirming treatments.
Many transsexual people advocate for equal rights and acceptance in society.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transgender - a person whose gender identity does not match the sex assigned at birth, regardless of medical transition

Etymology

From Latin 'trans-' (across) and English 'sexual'.

2. ADJ

/ trænˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / trænˈsɛksjʊəl / (BrE)
(of a person) identifying as a gender different from the one assigned at birth, especially after undergoing medical procedures to change physical sex characteristics.
identifying as a different gender than assigned at birth

Examples

Many individuals who are transsexual choose to undergo gender-affirming treatments.
She identifies as transsexual and has found support within her community.
The policy was updated to ensure equal rights for transsexual employees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transgender - identifying as a different gender from that assigned at birth, regardless of medical transition

Etymology

From Latin 'trans-' (across) and 'sexus' (sex), first used in the 20th century.

3. NOUN

/ trænˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / trænˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
a person whose gender identity is different from their sex assigned at birth and who may seek to live and be recognized as a member of the opposite sex, sometimes undergoing medical procedures to change their body.
person identifying as the opposite sex

Examples

As a transsexual, she faced many challenges but found support in the community.
Some transsexual individuals choose to undergo medical procedures to align their bodies with their gender identity.
The film explores the life of a transsexual protagonist struggling for acceptance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transgender - a person whose gender identity differs from their sex assigned at birth

Etymology

From Latin 'trans-' (across) + 'sexual'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10745370-n
Synset POS: n
