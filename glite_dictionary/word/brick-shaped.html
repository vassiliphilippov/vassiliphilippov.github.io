brick-shaped

1. ADJ

/ brɪk ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / brɪk ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of a brick; rectangular and block-like in form.
shaped like a brick

Examples

The architect designed a brick-shaped building that stood out for its simplicity.
Several brick-shaped boxes were stacked neatly in the storage room.
She handed me a brick-shaped present, wrapped in glossy red paper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rectangular - having a shape like a rectangle
  • Degree Higher: cube-shaped - having the shape of a cube
