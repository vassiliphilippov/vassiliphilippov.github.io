hoya

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊ.jə / (AmE), / ˈhɔɪ.ə / (BrE)
A tropical climbing plant of the genus Hoya, having fleshy leaves and clusters of waxy, usually fragrant flowers.
tropical climbing plant with waxy flowers

Examples

The hoya in the living room has blossomed, displaying its signature star-shaped flowers.
She collects rare species of hoya for her greenhouse.
After months of care, my hoya finally produced fragrant blooms.

Etymology

Named after Thomas Hoy (c. 1750–1822), English gardener.
