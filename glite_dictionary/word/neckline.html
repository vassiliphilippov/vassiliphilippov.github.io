neckline

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛkˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈnɛk.laɪn / (BrE)
the edge or shape of a garment around or below the neck, especially as a feature of its style
the edge of a garment around the neck

Examples

The dress had a beautiful V-shaped neckline that highlighted her necklace.
He complimented her on the elegant neckline of her evening gown.
Square necklines have become increasingly popular in recent fashion trends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collar - the part of a shirt, coat, or dress that fits around the neck
