trademarked

1. ADJ

/ ˈtreɪdˌmɑrkt / (AmE), / ˈtreɪdˌmɑːkt / (BrE)
(of goods or names) protected by a legally registered trademark, indicating ownership and exclusive rights of use.
bearing a registered trademark

Examples

The company only sells trademarked clothing brands in its stores.
Using a trademarked logo without permission is illegal.
All trademarked products must display the appropriate symbol on their packaging.

Synset Examples

trademarked goods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: registered - officially recorded or listed; enrolled
  • Antonym: generic - not protected by a trademark; not specific to a brand

Etymology

From 'trademark' + past participle suffix '-ed'.
