bare-assed

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛrˌæst / (AmE), / ˈbeəˌɑːst / (BrE)
(informal, slang) Completely unclothed, especially with the buttocks exposed; naked.
completely unclothed; naked
informal slang vulgar

Examples

He ran bare-assed through the field as a dare from his friends.
The toddler escaped from the bath bare-assed and laughing.
She found herself accidentally locked out of her room, bare-assed and embarrassed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naked - without clothes; not covered
  • Antonym: clothed - wearing clothes; dressed
