yellowed

1. ADJ

/ ˈjɛloʊd / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊd / (BrE)
having become yellow or yellowish in color, typically as a result of aging or exposure.
discolored to yellow by age or exposure

Examples

The yellowed pages of the diary were fragile and brittle.
After years on the shelf, the white curtains had yellowed from the sunlight.
His yellowed teeth revealed years of heavy smoking.

Synset Examples

yellowed parchment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discolored - having lost or changed color

2. ADJ

/ ˈjɛl.oʊd / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊd / (BrE)
having turned yellow, especially as a result of age or exposure to air or light
turned yellow with age or exposure

Examples

The old documents were yellowed with age and difficult to read.
She treasured the yellowed letters her grandmother had written decades ago.
A stack of yellowed newspapers was discovered in the attic.
