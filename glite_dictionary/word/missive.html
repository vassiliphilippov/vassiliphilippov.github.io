missive

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪsɪv / (AmE), / ˈmɪsɪv / (BrE)
a formal or official letter or written message, often sent to communicate important information
formal written message or letter

Examples

He composed a lengthy missive detailing his concerns about the project.
The ambassador sent a secret missive to the head of state.
Her last missive was filled with heartfelt gratitude and advice.

Synset Examples

mailed an indignant letter to the editor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: letter - a written or printed message addressed to a person or organization
  • Synonym: message - a communication sent to someone

Etymology

From Latin 'missus', past participle of 'mittere' (to send).
