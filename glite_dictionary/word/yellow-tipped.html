yellow-tipped

1. ADJ

/ ˈjɛl.oʊ tɪpt / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊ tɪpt / (BrE)
(of flowers or objects) having the tips colored yellow.
having yellow-colored tips

Examples

The yellow-tipped petals stood out brightly against the green leaves.
Gardeners often prize yellow-tipped irises for their distinctive appearance.
I admired the yellow-tipped daisies blooming in the field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellow-edged - having edges that are yellow in color
