unbolted

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈboʊltɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbəʊltɪd / (BrE)
not fastened or secured with a bolt; left open or capable of being opened easily
not secured by a bolt

Examples

We found the garage door unbolted after the storm had passed.
She realized the gate was unbolted when it swung open in the wind.
The package was left inside an unbolted shed.

Synset Examples

an unbarred door
went through the unlatched gate into the street
an unlocked room

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unlocked - not locked; able to be opened without a key or restriction
  • Antonym: latched - fastened with a latch
  • Antonym: bolted - fastened or secured with a bolt

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'bolted' (secured with a bolt).
