sightlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪtləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈsaɪtləsnɪs / (BrE)
The state or condition of being blind; lack of sight.
the state of being blind

Examples

His sightlessness did not prevent him from achieving great success.
The artist depicted the world as experienced through sightlessness.
Medical advances offer hope to people affected by sightlessness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blindness - the condition of lacking sight

Etymology

From 'sightless' + '-ness'; 'sightless' from Middle English, meaning 'without sight'.
