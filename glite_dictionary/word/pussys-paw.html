pussy's-paw

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊsiz ˌpɔ / (AmE), / ˈpʊsiz ˌpɔː / (BrE)
a small perennial plant with clusters of pink, densely packed flowers on low stems, resembling the pads of a cat's feet, found in coniferous forests of western North America
a pink-flowered North American plant

Examples

A delicate pussy's-paw bloomed near the mossy forest trail, its pink flowers brightening the ground.
She pointed out a cluster of pussy's-paws nestled among the roots of a fallen pine tree.
During our hike in Oregon, we spotted several pussy's-paw plants along the shaded path.

Etymology

Named for its flowers' resemblance to a cat's paw.
