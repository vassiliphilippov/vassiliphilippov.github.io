King C. Gillette

1. PROPN

/ kɪŋ si dʒɪˈlɛt / (AmE), / kɪŋ si dʒɪˈlɛt / (BrE)
(1855–1932) United States inventor and manufacturer who developed the safety razor.
U.S. inventor of the safety razor

Examples

King C. Gillette revolutionized shaving by inventing the safety razor.
The company founded by King C. Gillette is now a household name worldwide.
Few people know that King C. Gillette originally worked as a traveling salesman before his fame as an inventor.
