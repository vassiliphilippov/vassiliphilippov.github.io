papers

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpɚz / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəz / (BrE)
official documents or written records that provide information, especially of an official or legal nature.
official documents or records

Examples

The immigration officer asked to see his papers before letting him enter the country.
She kept all her important papers in a locked drawer for safety.
The police searched the car and requested the driver's registration papers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: document - an official paper that gives information or proof of something
  • Synonym: certificate - an official document attesting to a fact
