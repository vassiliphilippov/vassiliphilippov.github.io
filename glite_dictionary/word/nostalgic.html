nostalgic

1. ADJ

/ nəˈstældʒɪk / (AmE), / nɒˈstældʒɪk / (BrE)
Feeling or expressing a longing for the past or for familiar people, places, or things, often with a mixture of sadness and affection.
longing for the past or for familiar things

Examples

Hearing that old song always makes me feel nostalgic for my childhood summers.
She grew nostalgic after looking through her high school yearbook.
There was a nostalgic atmosphere at the reunion as everyone shared memories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homesick - longing for one's home during absence
  • Synonym: sentimental - marked by feelings of tenderness or nostalgia

Etymology

From Greek *nostos* (return home) + *-algia* (pain); originally coined in the late 18th century.
