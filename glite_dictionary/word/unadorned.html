unadorned

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈdɔrnd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈdɔːnd / (BrE)
not decorated or embellished; plain or simple in appearance
plain; without decoration

Examples

The walls were unadorned, giving the room a stark, minimalist feel.
She preferred an unadorned wedding dress with no lace or jeweled embellishments.
His writing style is refreshingly unadorned and straightforward.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plain - not decorated or elaborate; simple
  • Antonym: decorated - having ornamentation or adornment

Etymology

From un- (not) + adorn, from Latin adornare (to decorate).
