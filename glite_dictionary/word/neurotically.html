neurotically

1. ADV

/ nʊˈrɑtɪkli / (AmE), / njʊəˈrɒtɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a tendency to be nervous, worried, or excessively anxious and emotional.
in an excessively anxious or nervous manner

Examples

She neurotically checked her phone every five minutes for new messages.
He arranged the books neurotically until everything was perfectly aligned.
Nina neurotically worried about missing the train, arriving an hour early.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neurotic - having or showing symptoms of neurosis

Etymology

Formed by adding the adverbial suffix -ly to 'neurotic', from Greek 'neuron' (nerve).
