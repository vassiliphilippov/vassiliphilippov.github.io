multicultist

1. NOUN

/ ˌmʌltiˈkʌltɪst / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈkʌltɪst / (BrE)
a person who adopts and values diverse cultural perspectives and promotes multiculturalism
person who supports multiculturalism

Examples

She considered herself a multicultist and enjoyed learning about new traditions.
As a proud multicultist, he advocated for cultural festivals in the city.
Some critics use the term multicultist to describe those who promote diversity in public policy.

Synset Examples

Being a multicultist means embracing different worldviews.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multiculturalist - a person who supports multiculturalism
  • Antonym: nationalist - a person who advocates for the interests of one nation or culture, often prioritizing it over others

Etymology

Blend of 'multicultural' and '-ist' (person who practices or supports a belief).
