webmaster

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛbˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈwɛbˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person responsible for designing, developing, or maintaining a website
person who manages a website

Examples

She works as the webmaster for a large university, keeping its site updated and secure.
The company hired a new webmaster to improve their online presence.
If you notice any broken links, please contact the webmaster immediately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: web designer - person who creates the visual aspects of websites
  • Synonym: administrator - person responsible for running a business or organization, or maintaining systems

Etymology

Blend of 'web' (from 'World Wide Web') and 'master'.
