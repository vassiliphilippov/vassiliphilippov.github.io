unsought

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɔt / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɔːt / (BrE)
not looked for or not wanted; not actively desired or requested
not sought or not wanted

Examples

The award was an unsought honor that came as a complete surprise to her.
He received unsought advice from colleagues despite not asking for help.
The discovery of the old letters was entirely unsought, found while cleaning the attic.

Synset Examples

an undesired result

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undesired - not wanted or wished for
  • Antonym: welcome - received with pleasure or approval

Etymology

from un- (not) + sought, past participle of 'seek' (from Old English sēcan)
