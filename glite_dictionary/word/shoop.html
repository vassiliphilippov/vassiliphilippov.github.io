to shoop

1. VERB

/ ʃuːp / (AmE), / ʃuːp / (BrE)
to move or switch between places in a casual or quick manner
move casually or quickly between places
informal

Examples

He likes to shoop from one friend's house to another on weekends.
We decided to shoop over to the beach after the movie.
The kids would shoop around the neighborhood on their bikes all afternoon.

Synset Examples

He loves to shoop between different cafes.
Let's shoop over to the other side of town.
