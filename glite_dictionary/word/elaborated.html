elaborated

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈlæb.əˌreɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ɪˈlæb.əˌreɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
developed or executed with careful attention and extensive detail
developed in great detail

Examples

The architect presented an elaborated design for the new museum.
Her research findings were based on an elaborated analysis of historical documents.
The report included an elaborated description of the company's future strategies.

Synset Examples

a detailed plan
the elaborate register of the inhabitants prevented tax evasion
the carefully elaborated theme

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elaborate - developed or executed with care and detail
  • Antonym: simple - easy to understand or not complicated

Etymology

Past participle of 'elaborate,' from Latin *elaboratus*, past participle of *elaborare* (work out, produce by labor).
