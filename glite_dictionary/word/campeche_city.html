Campeche City

1. PROPN

/ kæmˈpeɪtʃi ˈsɪti / (AmE), / kamˈpeɪtʃi ˈsɪti / (BrE)
A port city and the capital of the state of Campeche in southeastern Mexico, located on the Bay of Campeche.
port city in southeastern Mexico

Examples

We visited Campeche City last summer to explore its colonial architecture and vibrant markets.
The ancient walls of Campeche City are a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
Trade has historically flourished in Campeche City due to its strategic location on the Gulf of Mexico.

Etymology

Spanish 'Campeche', from the Maya 'Kan Pech', meaning 'place of snakes and ticks'.
