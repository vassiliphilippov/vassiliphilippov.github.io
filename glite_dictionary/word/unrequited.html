unrequited

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈkwaɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈkwaɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
(of a feeling, especially love) not returned or reciprocated by the other person.
not returned (especially love)

Examples

She suffered for years from unrequited love.
The poem explores the pain of unrequited affection.
Many songs are inspired by unrequited emotions.

Synset Examples

unrequited (unanswered) love

Related Terms

  • Antonym: requited - returned or reciprocated (especially of feelings)

Etymology

From Latin 'requitare' (to repay), with 'un-' meaning not.
