hammer-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈhæmɚ ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈhamə ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having a shape that resembles a hammer, typically with an enlarged or flattened end.
having the shape of a hammer

Examples

The orchid's hammer-shaped flower helps attract specific pollinators.
Some prehistoric tools were distinctly hammer-shaped for pounding food and breaking bones.
The unusual hammer-shaped head of the hammerhead shark is easily recognizable.
