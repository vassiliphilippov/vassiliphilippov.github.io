D. H. Lawrence

1. PROPN

/ di eɪtʃ ˈlɔːrəns / (AmE), / diː eɪtʃ ˈlɒrəns / (BrE)
(1885–1930) English novelist, poet, and essayist known for his works exploring industrial society and human sexuality.
