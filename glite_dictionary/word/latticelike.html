latticelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈlæt̬iˌslaɪk / (AmE), / ˈlætɪslaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having a pattern similar to a lattice or fretwork.
resembling a lattice pattern

Examples

The window had latticelike iron bars set in a geometric pattern.
A scientist observed that the mineral had a latticelike molecular structure.
The artist painted latticelike shadows on the wall from the woven bamboo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lattice - an interlaced structure or pattern resembling a network or grid
