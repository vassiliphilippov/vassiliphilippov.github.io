Interpol

1. PROPN

/ ˈɪntərˌpɔl / (AmE), / ˈɪntəˌpɒl / (BrE)
the International Criminal Police Organization, an international agency that facilitates cooperation between police forces from different countries to combat international crime
international police organization

Examples

The suspect was captured after Interpol issued an international alert for his arrest.
Interpol assists member countries in tracking down fugitives across borders.
Authorities contacted Interpol to help coordinate the investigation.

Etymology

Shortened form of 'International Criminal Police Organization' (French: Organisation internationale de police criminelle, OIPC).
