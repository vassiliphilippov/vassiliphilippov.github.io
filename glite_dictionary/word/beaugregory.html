beaugregory

1. NOUN

/ ˌboʊ.ɡrɪˈɡɔːri / (AmE), / ˌbəʊ.ɡrɪˈɡɔːri / (BrE)
a small blue and yellow damselfish found in the waters of Bermuda, Florida, and the West Indies
blue and yellow damselfish of the Caribbean region

Examples

Snorkelers often spot the colorful beaugregory swimming near coral reefs in the West Indies.
The beaugregory fiercely defends its territory from intruders.
Marine biologists studied the breeding patterns of the beaugregory along the coast of Florida.

Related Terms
