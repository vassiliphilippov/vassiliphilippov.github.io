shrimper

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃrɪmpɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃrɪmpə / (BrE)
A boat or vessel used for catching shrimp.
vessel used for catching shrimp

Examples

The fisherman repaired his shrimper before heading out to sea.
Several shrimpers were docked along the harbor after a long day’s haul.
Her family owned a shrimper and supplied local restaurants with fresh seafood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shrimp boat - a boat used for catching shrimp

Etymology

From 'shrimp' (the crustacean) + '-er' (denoting an agent or thing associated with an action).
