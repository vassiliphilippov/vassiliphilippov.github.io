Nicolas Poussin

1. PROPN

/ niˈkoʊlə pʊˈsæ̃ / (AmE), / ˈnɪkələs puːˈsæ̃ / (BrE)
(1594–1665) A French painter renowned for his classical style and influence on European art.
French classical painter (1594–1665)

Examples

Many art historians consider Nicolas Poussin a central figure in the development of French classical painting.
The museum's new exhibit includes several works by Nicolas Poussin.
Inspired by antiquity, Nicolas Poussin often depicted mythological and religious subjects in his paintings.
