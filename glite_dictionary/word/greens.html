greens

1. NOUN

/ ɡrinz / (AmE), / ɡriːnz / (BrE)
the leaves and stems of various plants, such as spinach, kale, or collards, eaten as vegetables
leafy vegetables eaten as food

Examples

She always includes fresh greens in her salad for extra nutrients.
Collard greens are a popular side dish in Southern cooking.
After harvesting, the greens need to be washed thoroughly to remove any dirt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetables - plants or parts of plants eaten as food
  • Degree Lower: salad - a cold dish of various mixtures of raw or cooked vegetables
