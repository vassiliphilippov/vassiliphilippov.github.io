anginose

1. ADJ

/ ˈændʒɪˌnoʊs / (AmE), / ˈandʒɪnəʊz / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the pain caused by angina pectoris
pertaining to angina pectoris pain
medical

Examples

The patient was admitted with anginose symptoms requiring immediate attention.
Doctors described the pain as distinctly anginose in its nature and severity.
His anginose discomfort subsided after he was given nitroglycerin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angina - chest pain or discomfort due to coronary heart disease

Etymology

From Latin 'angina' (quinsy, throat inflammation) + suffix '-ose' (full of, relating to).
