Accra

1. PROPN

/ əˈkrɑ / (AmE), / əˈkrɑː / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Ghana, located on the Atlantic coast and known for its deep-water port
capital city of Ghana

Examples

She traveled to Accra to attend a business conference last summer.
Many tourists visit Accra for its vibrant markets and cultural heritage.
Cargo ships dock regularly at the deep-water port in Accra.
