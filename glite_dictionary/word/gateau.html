gateau

1. NOUN

/ ɡæˈtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡætəʊ / (BrE)
A rich and elaborate cake, typically layered and decorated, often filled with cream, fruit, or icing.
rich, elaborate cake (often French)

Examples

The bakery's chocolate gateau was the highlight of the dessert menu.
She baked a strawberry gateau for her friend's birthday celebration.
Many French patisseries specialize in multi-layered gateau varieties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cake - a sweet baked dessert, usually made from flour, sugar, and eggs

Etymology

From French 'gâteau' (cake).
