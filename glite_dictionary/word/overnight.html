overnight

1. ADV

/ ˌoʊvərˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊvənaɪt / (BrE)
for the duration of a single night; during one night.
during one night

Examples

She left the soup to simmer overnight before serving it the next day.
The package was shipped overnight and arrived early the following morning.
He stayed at the hospital overnight for observation after the surgery.

Synset Examples

the fish marinates overnight

2. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊvənaɪt / (BrE)
lasting, open, or operating throughout the entire night
lasting or open all night

Examples

The hotel offers overnight accommodations for travelers passing through the city.
We took an overnight train from Paris to Venice.
They planned an overnight camping trip in the mountains.

very quickly; in a short time

You can't expect to see results overnight—it takes time.
