advanced

1. ADJ

/ ədˈvænst / (AmE), / ədˈvɑːnst / (BrE)
Highly developed or at a late stage of progress, especially in technology, industry, or social organization.
highly developed in technology or industry

Examples

Japan is considered an advanced country due to its cutting-edge technology and infrastructure.
Investment in education is essential for building an advanced society.
Many advanced economies have shifted from manufacturing to service industries.

Synset Examples

an advanced country technologically

  • Antonym: primitive - at an early stage of development; not advanced
