delightedly

1. ADV

/ dɪˈlaɪtɪdli / (AmE), / dɪˈlaɪtɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows great pleasure or joy.
in a joyful or pleased manner

Examples

She delightedly opened the gift, her face lighting up with excitement.
The children delightedly played in the snow all afternoon.
He delightedly agreed to join us for dinner after hearing the menu.

Synset Examples

delightedly, she accepted the invitation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: happily - in a happy or joyful way
  • Antonym: reluctantly - in an unwilling or hesitant way

Etymology

Formed from 'delighted' (from delight) and the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
