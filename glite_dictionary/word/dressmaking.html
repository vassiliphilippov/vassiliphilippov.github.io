dressmaking

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɛsˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the craft or activity of making dresses, typically by hand or as a profession
craft of making dresses

Examples

She learned dressmaking from her grandmother, who was a skilled seamstress.
The college offers evening courses in dressmaking for beginners.
After years of practice, he started his own dressmaking business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tailoring - the craft of making or altering clothing, especially men's suits

Etymology

From 'dress' + 'making', first recorded in the 18th century.
