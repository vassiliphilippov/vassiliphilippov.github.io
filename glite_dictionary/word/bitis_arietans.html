Bitis arietans

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪtɪs ˌæriˈeɪtænz / (AmE), / ˈbaɪtɪs ˌæriˈeɪtænz / (BrE)
A large venomous viper native to Africa, commonly known as the puff adder, which inflates its body when threatened.
African puff adder; large venomous viper

Examples

The Bitis arietans is responsible for more snakebite fatalities in Africa than any other species.
Herpetologists were excited to observe a wild Bitis arietans during their expedition.
When threatened, the Bitis arietans can inflate its body and hiss loudly to deter predators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puff adder - Common name for Bitis arietans, an African viper

Etymology

From the genus name Bitis and species name arietans, Latin for 'striking like a ram'.
