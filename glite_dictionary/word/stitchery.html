stitchery

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪtʃəri / (AmE), / ˈstɪtʃəri / (BrE)
needlework, especially decorative sewing done with needle and thread
decorative needlework

Examples

Her grandmother taught her how to create beautiful stitchery on pillowcases.
He admired the colorful stitchery displayed at the craft fair.
After finishing her stitchery, she hung it on the living room wall.

Synset Examples

she put her sewing back in the basket

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embroidery - art of decorating fabric with needle and thread
  • Synonym: needlework - work (such as sewing or embroidery) done with a needle

Etymology

Formed from 'stitch' + '-ery' (a suffix forming nouns denoting actions or associated things).
