hop-picker

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑp ˌpɪkər / (AmE), / ˈhɒp ˌpɪkə / (BrE)
a machine used to pick hops from the hop plant during harvest
machine for picking hops

Examples

The old brewery still uses a traditional hop-picker during the harvest season.
Advancements in technology have made the hop-picker more efficient and less labor-intensive.
Farmers gathered around the new hop-picker to see how it worked in the fields.
