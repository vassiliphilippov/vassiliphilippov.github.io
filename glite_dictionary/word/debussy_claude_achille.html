Debussy, Claude Achille

1. PROPN

/ dəˈbusi kleɪd əˈʃil / (AmE), / dəˈbjuːsi kləʊd aˈʃiːl / (BrE)
(1862–1918) A French composer who is regarded as one of the most influential figures in Impressionist music.
French composer, Impressionist music pioneer

Examples

Many pianists admire Debussy for his innovative approach to harmony and form.
The music of Debussy often evokes the imagery and atmosphere of Impressionist paintings.
During his studies, Thomas wrote an essay on the influence of Debussy on 20th-century composers.
