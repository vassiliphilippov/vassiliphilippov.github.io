Zalophus californianus

1. NOUN

/ zəˌloʊfəs ˌkælɪˌfɔrˈniːənəs / (AmE), / zæˌlɒfəs ˌkælɪˌfɔːˈniːənəs / (BrE)
the California sea lion, a species of sea lion native to western North America, often trained as a show animal
California sea lion species

Examples

The Zalophus californianus is commonly seen performing tricks at marine parks.
Researchers studied the migration patterns of Zalophus californianus along the Pacific coast.
Unlike other sea lions, Zalophus californianus is known for its intelligence and trainability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: California sea lion - species of sea lion native to the western coast of North America

Etymology

From Latin: genus name Zalophus and species name californianus, meaning 'of California'.
