Chico Marx

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʃiːkoʊ ˈmɑrks / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːkəʊ ˈmɑːks / (BrE)
(1891–1961) United States comedian and actor, one of the Marx Brothers who made popular motion pictures together.
American comedian, Marx Brothers member
