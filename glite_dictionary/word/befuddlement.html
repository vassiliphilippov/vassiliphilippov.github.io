befuddlement

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈfʌdəlmənt / (AmE), / bɪˈfʌdlmənt / (BrE)
A state of confusion or puzzlement, often resulting from being unable to understand something.
confused or puzzled state

Examples

The new policy left even the most experienced employees in a state of befuddlement.
Her befuddlement was obvious as she tried to solve the complex puzzle.
He stared at the foreign menu in complete befuddlement before ordering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confusion - a lack of understanding; uncertainty

Etymology

From the verb "befuddle" (from "be-" + "fuddle", meaning to make confused or muddled).
