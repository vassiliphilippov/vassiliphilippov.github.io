65th

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪksti fɪfθ / (AmE), / ˈsɪksti fɪfθ / (BrE)
Coming after the sixty-fourth in a sequence; ordinal number corresponding to sixty-five.
ordinal number for sixty-five

Examples

This year marks the 65th anniversary of the school's founding.
He finished in 65th place in the marathon, just a few minutes behind his friend.
The 65th chapter of the book discusses new medical advancements.

Related Terms
