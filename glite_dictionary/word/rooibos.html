rooibos

1. NOUN

/ ˈruː.bɑs / (AmE), / ˈruːɪ.bɒs / (BrE)
A South African shrub (Aspalathus linearis) whose dried leaves are used to make a popular herbal tea.
herbal tea plant from South Africa

Examples

She offered me a cup of rooibos tea, which had a naturally sweet flavor.
The health benefits of rooibos have made it increasingly popular around the world.
Farmers in South Africa cultivate rooibos for both local use and international export.

Etymology

From Afrikaans 'rooibos', meaning 'red bush' ('rooi' = red, 'bos' = bush').
