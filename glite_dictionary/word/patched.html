patched

1. ADJ

/ pætʃt / (AmE), / pæʧt / (BrE)
mended by covering a hole or tear with a patch, especially in a rough or visible way
mended by covering with a patch

Examples

He wore his old patched jacket to the workshop.
The patched quilt had been in her family for generations.
Her jeans were patched at the knees after years of use.

Synset Examples

2. ADJ

/ pætʃt / (AmE), / pætʃt / (BrE)
marked with spots or areas of contrasting color or texture; having patches.
marked with spots or patches

Examples

The old quilt was patched with scraps of different colored fabric.
After the fire, the ceiling looked patched and uneven.
In winter, the field was patched with snow and brown grass.
