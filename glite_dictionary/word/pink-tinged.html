pink-tinged

1. ADJ

/ pɪŋk tɪndʒd / (AmE), / pɪŋk tɪn(d)ʒd / (BrE)
Having a slight or moderate tint of pink.
slightly tinted with pink

Examples

The evening sky was pink-tinged after the sun set.
She wore a pink-tinged scarf that brightened her outfit.
The petals are mostly white, but some are pink-tinged at the edges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pinkish - having a somewhat pink color
