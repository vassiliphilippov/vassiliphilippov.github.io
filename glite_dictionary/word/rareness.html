rareness

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛr.nəs / (AmE), / ˈreə.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being uncommon or infrequent; noteworthy scarcity.
state of being rare or uncommon

Examples

The rareness of the artifact increased its value among collectors.
Meteor showers are admired for their rareness and fleeting beauty.
Because of its rareness, the bird is protected by environmental laws.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scarcity - the state of being scarce or in short supply
  • Antonym: abundance - a very large quantity of something; plentifulness

Etymology

From rare + -ness, forming a noun meaning the state or quality of being rare.
