greenish-grey

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡriːnɪʃˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnɪʃˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a grey color tinged with green.
grey with a hint of green

Examples

The walls were painted a subtle greenish-grey shade that gave the room a calm atmosphere.
Her eyes appeared greenish-grey in the dim light, reflecting hints of both colors.
The bird’s feathers had a distinct greenish-grey tone, blending perfectly with the mossy rocks.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: grey - a color between black and white
  • Degree Higher: green - color between blue and yellow; of grass
