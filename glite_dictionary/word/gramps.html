gramps

1. NOUN

/ ɡræmps / (AmE), / ɡræmps / (BrE)
an informal term for one's grandfather
informal word for grandfather
informal

Examples

My gramps used to tell the best stories after dinner.
We visited gramps every Sunday when I was a child.
Even at ninety, gramps still takes care of his garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandfather - the father of one's father or mother
  • Antonym: grandma - one's grandmother (the mother of one's father or mother)

Etymology

Informal alteration of 'grandpa'.
