unpretentious

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.prɪˈtɛn.ʃəs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prɪˈtɛn.ʃəs / (BrE)
not trying to impress others with an appearance of greater importance, talent, or culture than is actually possessed; modest and simple.
not showy; modest

Examples

Although the restaurant is very popular, it remains unpretentious and friendly.
She wore an unpretentious dress that suited the occasion perfectly.
The author’s unpretentious style makes the book accessible to a wide audience.

Synset Examples

his unostentatious office
unostentatious elegance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: modest - not boastful or showing off; having or showing a moderate opinion of oneself
  • Antonym: ostentatious - characterized by vulgar or pretentious display; designed to impress

Etymology

From Latin 'praetendere' (to pretend), with 'un-' as a negative prefix.

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.prɪˈtɛn.ʃəs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prɪˈtɛn.ʃəs / (BrE)
not trying to appear more important, special, or impressive than one really is; simple and genuine.
not trying to impress; simple and genuine

Examples

The restaurant has an unpretentious atmosphere and serves delicious home-cooked meals.
Despite his success, he remains friendly and unpretentious in all his interactions.
They chose an unpretentious wedding ceremony with only close family and friends.

Synset Examples

an unpretentious country church
her quiet unpretentious demeanor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: modest - not boasting or showing off; humble
  • Antonym: pretentious - trying to appear more important or impressive than one is

Etymology

From un- (not) + pretentious (from Latin praetendo, 'to pretend').

3. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.prɪˈtɛn.ʃəs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prɪˈtɛn.ʃəs / (BrE)
not attempting to impress others or show off; simple and showing restrained good taste.
not trying to impress; simple, modest

Examples

The restaurant has an unpretentious atmosphere, with simple decor and friendly staff.
Despite her success, she remains genuinely unpretentious and approachable.
Their unpretentious wedding focused on family, friends, and good food rather than lavish decorations.

Synset Examples

the room is pleasant and understated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: modest - not boastful or showy; humble
  • Antonym: pretentious - attempting to impress by showing greater importance or merit than is actually possessed

Etymology

From un- (not) + pretentious, from French prétentieux, from Latin praetendo (to pretend).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02401787-s
Synset POS: s
