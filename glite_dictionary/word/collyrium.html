collyrium

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlɪriəm / (AmE), / kɒˈlɪriəm / (BrE)
A medicinal solution used to cleanse or treat the eyes.
eye-cleansing medicinal solution
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed a collyrium to relieve her eye irritation.
Ancient physicians often prepared herbal collyria for patients with sore eyes.
He carefully applied the collyrium as instructed after washing his hands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eyewash - a liquid used to wash out the eyes

Etymology

From Latin 'collyrium', from Greek 'kollȳrion' (eye salve).
