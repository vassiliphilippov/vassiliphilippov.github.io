angelim

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.dʒəˈliːm / (AmE), / ˌan.dʒəˈliːm / (BrE)
Any of several tropical American trees of the genus Andira, valued for their hard wood.
a tropical American tree of the genus Andira

Examples

The dense wood of the angelim is prized for construction in South America.
Botanists discovered a new species of angelim in the Amazon rainforest.
Furniture made from angelim is known for its strength and durability.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'angelim', used in Brazil for various hardwood trees.
