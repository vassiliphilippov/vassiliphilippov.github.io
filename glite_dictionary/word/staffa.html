Staffa

1. PROPN

/ ˈstæfə / (AmE), / ˈstæfə / (BrE)
an island in western Scotland, part of the Inner Hebrides, famous for Fingal's Cave and its basalt columns.
island in western Scotland, Inner Hebrides

Examples

Tourists from all over the world visit Staffa to see the spectacular Fingal's Cave.
The unique basalt columns make Staffa one of the most recognizable islands in Scotland.
During their journey through the Inner Hebrides, they stopped at Staffa for a short expedition.
