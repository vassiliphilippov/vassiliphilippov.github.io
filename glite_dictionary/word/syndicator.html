syndicator

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪndɪˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsɪndɪkeɪtə / (BrE)
a person or company that organizes and manages a syndicate, especially to pool resources for investment or broadcasting.
person who organizes a syndicate

Examples

The syndicator brought together several investors to finance the real estate project.
A television syndicator distributes programs to local stations across the country.
As a successful syndicator, she manages partnerships for numerous commercial ventures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: syndicate - an association of individuals or organizations formed to promote a common interest

Etymology

From 'syndicate' + '-or', indicating a person who performs an action.
