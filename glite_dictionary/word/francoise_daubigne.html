Francoise d'Aubigne

1. PROPN

/ fɹɑ̃ˈswɑːz doʊˈbiːn / (AmE), / fʀɑ̃ˈswaz doˈbiɲ / (BrE)
(1635–1719) French noblewoman who became the second, secret wife of King Louis XIV after the death of his first wife, known as the Marquise de Maintenon.
French wife of Louis XIV, Marquise de Maintenon

Examples

Francoise d'Aubigne played a significant role in the court of Louis XIV as his wife and advisor.
Many letters from Francoise d'Aubigne provide insight into life at Versailles.
Historians debate how Francoise d'Aubigne influenced the king's policies.
