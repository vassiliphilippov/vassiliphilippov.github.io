doggie

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔːɡi / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡi / (BrE)
An informal or affectionate term for a dog, especially used by or to children.
informal, affectionate word for dog
informal

Examples

The little girl hugged her doggie tightly as they watched TV together.
Would you like to go outside and see the doggie in the yard?
He adopted a stray doggie from the shelter last week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog - a domesticated carnivorous mammal kept as a pet or for work
