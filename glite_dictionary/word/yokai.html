yokai

1. NOUN

/ joʊˈkaɪ / (AmE), / jəʊˈkaɪ / (BrE)
A class of supernatural creatures, spirits, or demons in Japanese folklore, often depicted as shapeshifters or mischievous beings.
supernatural being in Japanese folklore

Examples

Japanese legends are filled with tales of mysterious yokai that haunt the countryside.
In the anime, the main character befriends a mischievous yokai who can shapeshift.
Many yokai are depicted as having both human and animal features in traditional artwork.

Etymology

From Japanese 妖怪 (yōkai), meaning 'strange apparition' or 'mysterious phenomena.'
