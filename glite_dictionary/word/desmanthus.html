Desmanthus

1. NOUN

/ dɛsˈmænθəs / (AmE), / dɛsˈmanθəs / (BrE)
A genus of American herbs or shrubs with sensitive pinnate leaves and small whitish flowers, belonging to the pea family.
genus of American herbs or shrubs

Examples

The Desmanthus genus includes several plants native to North and South America.
Botanists studied the Desmanthus plants for their unique leaf movement.
Some species of Desmanthus are used in soil improvement projects.

Etymology

From Greek 'desmos' (bond) and 'anthos' (flower), referring to linked flowers.
