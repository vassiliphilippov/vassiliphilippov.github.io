serigraphy

1. NOUN

/ səˈrɪɡrəfi / (AmE), / sɪˈrɪɡrəfi / (BrE)
the process of creating prints using the silkscreen printing technique
silkscreen printing process

Examples

The gallery exhibited a collection of modern serigraphy prints by local artists.
He learned the basics of serigraphy in his university's printmaking course.
Using serigraphy, the artist produced vivid posters with intricate patterns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: screen printing - the technique of printing through a mesh or screen

Etymology

From Latin 'sericum' (silk) and Greek 'graphē' (writing), indicating printing with silk.
