/ əˈhɔrs / (AmE), / əˈhɔːs / (BrE)
On horseback; riding a horse.
on horseback
archaic

Examples

The soldiers rode ahorse through the village at dawn.
In the old painting, you can see the messenger ahorse, galloping across the fields.
Travelers ahorse arrived before those coming by carriage.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mounted - riding or seated on a horse
  • Antonym: on foot - traveling by walking

Etymology
