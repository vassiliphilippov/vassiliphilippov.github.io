tole

1. NOUN

/ toʊl / (AmE), / təʊl / (BrE)
Decorative enameled or lacquered metalware, often gilded and elaborately painted, especially popular in the 18th century.
decorative painted metalware

Examples

The antique shop displayed a beautiful tole tray with intricate floral designs.
She collects tole painted items from different countries for her kitchen.
A tole lamp added a charming vintage touch to the living room decor.

Synset Examples

the Pennsylvania Dutch tole watering can might be a reproduction but it looks convincing

Etymology

From French 'tôle', meaning 'sheet metal'.
