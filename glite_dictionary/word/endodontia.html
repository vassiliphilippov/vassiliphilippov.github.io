endodontia

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛn.doʊˈdɑn.ti.ə / (AmE), / ˌɛn.dəʊˈdɒn.ti.ə / (BrE)
the branch of dentistry concerned with the study and treatment of diseases of the dental pulp and tissues surrounding the root of a tooth
branch of dentistry for dental pulp diseases
medical

Examples

She decided to specialize in endodontia after developing an interest in root canal procedures.
Recent advances in endodontia have improved the success rate of saving natural teeth.
The seminar covered the latest research in endodontia and recommended treatment protocols.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endodontics - branch of dentistry dealing with dental pulp diseases (synonym)

Etymology

From Greek endo- (inside) + odont- (tooth) + -ia (condition).
