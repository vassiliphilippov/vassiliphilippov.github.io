raisable

1. ADJ

/ ˈreɪ.zə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.zə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be raised or lifted; capable of being moved to a higher position.
capable of being raised

Examples

The convertible comes with a raisable roof for added versatility in changing weather.
This desk has a raisable platform to accommodate standing work.
The theater features a raisable orchestra pit for various stage configurations.

Synset Examples

the roadster had a raisable sunroof

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liftable - able to be lifted

Etymology

From 'raise' + '-able', indicating capability.
