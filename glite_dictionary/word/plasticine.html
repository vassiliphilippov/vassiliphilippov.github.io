Plasticine

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæstəˌsin / (AmE), / ˈplæstɪsiːn / (BrE)
a soft, synthetic material similar to clay, used especially by children and artists for modeling and crafts
soft synthetic modeling material

Examples

The children molded animals out of Plasticine during art class.
He prefers using Plasticine for his sculptures because it never dries out.
A box of colorful Plasticine sat on the shelf in the classroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: modeling clay - soft material used for modeling shapes and figures

Etymology

Originally a trademark; from 'plastic' + the suffix '-ine'.
