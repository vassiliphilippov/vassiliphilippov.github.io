billionth

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɪljənθ / (AmE), / ˈbɪljənθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number corresponding to one billion in a sequence; position number one billion.
the ordinal number for one billionth place

Examples

This is the billionth time I've told you to clean your room.
He was the billionth customer to visit the store and won a prize.
If you count every grain of sand, you'd still be far from the billionth one.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: millionth - the ordinal number corresponding to one million
  • Degree Higher: trillionth - the ordinal number corresponding to one trillion

2. NOUN

/ ˈbɪljənθ / (AmE), / ˈbɪljənθ / (BrE)
The position in a series that is number one billion; 1,000,000,000th.
position number one billion in a sequence

Examples

He was the billionth customer to walk through the store's doors.
If you count steadily, reaching the billionth number would take many years.
NASA celebrated when their satellite orbited Earth for the billionth time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: billion - the number 1,000,000,000
  • Degree Lower: millionth - position 1,000,000 in a sequence

Etymology

From 'billion' plus the ordinal suffix '-th'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13872216-n
Synset POS: n

3. NOUN

/ ˈbɪljənθ / (AmE), / ˈbɪljənθ / (BrE)
One part of a quantity divided into a billion equal parts; the ordinal number matching the value one billion in a sequence.
one part in a billion

Examples

One billionth of a meter is called a nanometer.
The drug is effective at concentrations as low as a billionth of a gram.
She won the race by a margin of a billionth of a second.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: millionth - one part in a million

Etymology

From 'billion' plus the ordinal suffix '-th'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13761861-n
Synset POS: n
