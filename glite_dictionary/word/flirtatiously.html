flirtatiously

1. ADV

/ flɝːˈteɪʃəsli / (AmE), / flɜːˈteɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows playful or romantic interest in someone; in a way that attempts to attract attention in a playful, teasing, or suggestive way.
in a playful or teasing romantic manner

Examples

He glanced flirtatiously at her across the crowded room, hoping to catch her attention.
She laughed flirtatiously at his joke, making everyone notice their chemistry.
The waiter smiled flirtatiously, making the customers feel special and amused.

Synset Examples

she smiled coquettishly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coquettishly - in a flirtatious or teasing manner

Etymology

From 'flirtatious' + '-ly', with 'flirt' of uncertain origin, possibly from 16th-century English slang.
