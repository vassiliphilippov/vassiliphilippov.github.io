pyrotechny

1. NOUN

/ ˌpaɪrəˈtɛkni / (AmE), / ˌpʌɪrəˈtɛkni / (BrE)
the art or craft of making and using fireworks
the craft of making fireworks

Examples

The ancient Chinese were pioneers in the field of pyrotechny.
He spent years studying pyrotechny before designing his first fireworks show.
New Year's celebrations often showcase the brilliance of modern pyrotechny.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pyrotechnics - the art of making or using fireworks; skill with firework displays

Etymology

From French 'pyrotechnie', from Greek 'pyr' (fire) + 'techne' (art, craft).
