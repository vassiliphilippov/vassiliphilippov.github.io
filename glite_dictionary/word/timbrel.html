timbrel

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪmbrəl / (AmE), / ˈtɪmbrəl / (BrE)
a small hand drum similar to a tambourine, especially one used in ancient times
small ancient hand drum

Examples

In many ancient cultures, a timbrel was used during religious ceremonies.
She played the timbrel as the procession moved through the streets.
References to the timbrel can be found in several passages of the Bible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tambourine - small hand drum with jingles

Etymology

From Late Latin 'tymbalum', from Greek 'tympanon' (drum).
