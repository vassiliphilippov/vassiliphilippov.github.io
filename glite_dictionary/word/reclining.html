reclining

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈklaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈklaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of assuming or being in a position where the body is leaned or laid back
the act of leaning or lying back

Examples

Long hours of reclining in a chair can lead to back pain if you don't have proper support.
The spa is designed for comfort, encouraging reclining and relaxation alike.
She enjoyed reclining in her hammock on warm summer afternoons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to recline - to lean or lie back in a relaxed position
