Ponstel

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑnˌstɛl / (AmE), / ˈpɒnˌstɛl / (BrE)
A trademarked nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (mefenamic acid) used to relieve mild pain, especially menstrual cramps.
trademarked pain-relief drug (mefenamic acid)
medical american

Examples

The doctor prescribed Ponstel to help manage her menstrual pain.
Some patients experience fewer side effects with Ponstel than with other pain medications.
If you are allergic to NSAIDs, you should avoid taking Ponstel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mefenamic acid - a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used for pain and inflammation
