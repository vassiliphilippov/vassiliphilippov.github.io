friedcake

1. NOUN

/ ˈfraɪdˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈfraɪd.keɪk / (BrE)
A small cake, typically shaped as a ring, ball, twist, or strip, that is deep-fried in fat.
deep-fried small cake

Examples

My grandmother used to make homemade friedcake every Sunday morning.
He enjoyed a warm friedcake with his cup of coffee at the fair.
At the bakery, the friedcake was glazed with sugar and filled with jam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doughnut - a small, usually ring-shaped cake fried in deep fat
