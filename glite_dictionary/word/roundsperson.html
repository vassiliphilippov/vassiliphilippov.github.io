roundsperson

1. NOUN

/ ˈraʊndzˌpɜrsən / (AmE), / ˈraʊndzˌpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make regular rounds for delivering goods or carrying out inspections.
person who makes regular rounds for work

Examples

The new roundsperson covers the entire factory floor twice each shift.
As a roundsperson, she checks fire exits to ensure they are unobstructed.
The company hired a roundsperson to deliver mail to different departments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roundsman - a person who makes regular rounds for deliveries or inspections

Etymology

Formed by combining 'rounds' (as in regular circuit) and 'person', modeled after 'roundsman'.
