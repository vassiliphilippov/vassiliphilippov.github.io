hundred-and-fortieth

1. ADJ

/ ˌhʌndrəd ænd ˈfɔrtiəθ / (AmE), / ˌhʌndrəd ən ˈfɔːtiəθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number corresponding to one hundred and forty, used to indicate position in a sequence.
ordinal number: 140th

Examples

She finished in hundred-and-fortieth place out of several hundred runners.
This was the hundred-and-fortieth anniversary of the school's founding.
He correctly answered the hundred-and-fortieth question in the quiz contest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one hundred and fortieth - ordinal number: 140th (alternative form with 'one')
  • Degree Lower: hundred-and-thirty-ninth - ordinal number: 139th, coming just before 140th
  • Degree Higher: hundred-and-forty-first - ordinal number: 141st, coming just after 140th
