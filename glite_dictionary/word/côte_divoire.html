Côte d'Ivoire

1. PROPN

/ koʊt diˈvwɑːr / (AmE), / kəʊt diːˈvwɑː / (BrE)
a country in West Africa, located on the Gulf of Guinea; officially known as the Republic of Côte d'Ivoire, also called Ivory Coast.
country in West Africa (Ivory Coast)

Examples

I have always wanted to visit Côte d'Ivoire because of its beautiful beaches and vibrant culture.
The national football team of Côte d'Ivoire is famous for producing world-class players.
After gaining independence from France, Côte d'Ivoire rapidly developed its economy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ivory Coast - alternative English name for Côte d'Ivoire

Etymology

French for 'Ivory Coast', from côte (coast) and ivoire (ivory).
