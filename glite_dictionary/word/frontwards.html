frontwards

1. ADV

/ ˈfrʌntwɚdz / (AmE), / ˈfrʌntwədz / (BrE)
In the direction toward the front; forwards.
toward the front; forwards

Examples

The children walked frontwards in a neat line toward the classroom.
She can ride her skateboard both frontwards and backwards.
The sign should be facing frontwards so everyone can read it.

Synset Examples

he faced forward
step forward
she practiced sewing backward as well as frontward on her new sewing machine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forwards - toward the front; in the direction that one is facing or traveling
  • Antonym: backwards - in the direction behind; toward the rear
