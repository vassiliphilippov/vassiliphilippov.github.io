rete

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːti / (AmE), / ˈriːti / (BrE)
a network of intersecting blood vessels, nerves, or lymphatic vessels in the body
network of vessels or nerves
medical

Examples

The rete mirabile is an intricate vascular structure found in some animals.
Damage to the rete can disrupt normal blood supply to the organ.
The textbook included diagrams of the rete associated with the kidney.

Etymology

From Latin rete (net).
