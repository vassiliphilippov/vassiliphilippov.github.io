clocksmith

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɑːkˌsmɪθ / (AmE), / ˈklɒkˌsmɪθ / (BrE)
a person whose occupation is making or repairing clocks and watches
maker or repairer of clocks and watches

Examples

The antique shop hired a skilled clocksmith to restore its collection of old timepieces.
My grandfather was a renowned clocksmith who could fix even the most delicate mechanisms.
Finding a reliable clocksmith is essential when you want your heirloom clock repaired properly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watchmaker - a person who makes or repairs watches

Etymology

Formed from 'clock' + 'smith', following the pattern of 'blacksmith' and related crafts.
