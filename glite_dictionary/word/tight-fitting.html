tight-fitting

1. ADJ

/ ˈtaɪtˌfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtˌfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of clothing or a covering) fitting closely and firmly to the body or object, without being loose.
fitting closely to the body or object

Examples

She wore a tight-fitting dress to the party that accentuated her figure.
The tight-fitting lid kept the contents of the jar fresh for weeks.
For cycling, he prefers tight-fitting clothing to reduce wind resistance.

Synset Examples

a tightly-fitting cover
tight-fitting clothes

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loose-fitting - (of clothing) not fitting closely to the body
