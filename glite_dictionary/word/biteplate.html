biteplate

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪtˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈbaɪtˌpleɪt / (BrE)
A removable dental appliance worn against the palate to aid in diagnosis or treatment.
removable dental appliance for diagnosis or therapy
medical

Examples

The dentist recommended a biteplate to help correct her jaw alignment.
Wearing a biteplate at night reduced his teeth grinding symptoms.
Her orthodontist explained how to clean the biteplate properly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retainer - Dental appliance used to maintain teeth position
  • Synonym: mouthguard - Protective device worn in the mouth

Etymology

From 'bite' + 'plate', referring to its function and form.
