Scorpion

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɔr.pi.ən / (AmE), / ˈskɔː.pi.ən / (BrE)
the eighth sign of the zodiac, covering the period from about October 23 to November 21, represented by a scorpion
the eighth sign of the zodiac (October 23–November 21)

Examples

People born under the sign of Scorpion are said to be passionate and determined.
The sun enters Scorpion at the end of October each year.
My sister is a Scorpion, and she truly fits the typical description of the sign.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Scorpio - the eighth zodiac sign, represented by a scorpion

Etymology

From Latin 'Scorpio', meaning 'scorpion', used in astrology since Classical times.

2. NOUN

/ ˈskɔːrpiən / (AmE), / ˈskɔːpiən / (BrE)
(astrology) A person born under the zodiac sign Scorpio, which falls between October 23 and November 21.
person born under the zodiac sign Scorpio

Examples

Many Scorpions are said to have intense personalities and strong determination.
As a Scorpion, she often checks her horoscope for daily guidance.
My brother is a proud Scorpion who celebrates his birthday in early November.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Scorpio - the zodiac sign of the scorpion

Etymology

From Latin Scorpiō, the zodiac sign, from the Latin word for 'scorpion'.
