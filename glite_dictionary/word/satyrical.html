satyrical

1. ADJ

/ səˈtɪr.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / səˈtɪr.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
Relating to, or characteristic of a satyr, especially in having lascivious or lustful qualities.
relating to a satyr; lecherous
archaic

Examples

The old duke had a satyrical manner that made the court uneasy.
Ancient plays often featured satyrical characters with exaggerated appetites.
His satyrical behavior at the feast scandalized the more reserved guests.

Synset Examples

this satyric old man pursues young girls

Etymology

Formed from 'satyr' and the suffix '-ical'; see also 'satyr.'
