outdoorsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtˌdɔrzmən / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌdɔːzmən / (BrE)
a man who spends a lot of time doing outdoor activities such as hunting, fishing, or camping
man who enjoys outdoor activities

Examples

He is a true outdoorsman, spending his weekends hiking and fishing in the mountains.
As an experienced outdoorsman, he knew how to survive in the wilderness for weeks.
The magazine featured an interview with a renowned outdoorsman and wildlife expert.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: outdoorswoman - a woman who spends a lot of time doing outdoor activities
  • Synonym: hunter - a person who hunts wild animals
  • Synonym: angler - a person who fishes with a rod and line

Etymology

Formed from 'outdoors' + 'man', modeled after compounds such as 'sportsman'.
