muscleman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsəlˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmʌsəlˌman / (BrE)
A man whose strength is used to intimidate or protect others, often employed as a thug or bodyguard.
man hired as a thug or bodyguard

Examples

The club owner hired a muscleman to keep troublesome patrons in check.
Rumor has it the mob boss always travels with a muscleman beside him.
Seeing the muscleman at the door, the reporters kept their distance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thug - a violent person, especially a criminal
  • Synonym: bodyguard - a person employed to protect someone

2. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsəlˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmʌsəlˌman / (BrE)
a man with well-developed muscles, especially one who develops them through special exercises or bodybuilding
man with very developed muscles

Examples

The muscleman flexed his arms, impressing the crowd at the fitness expo.
After years of weightlifting, he became quite the muscleman in his local gym.
The movie features a heroic muscleman who saves the city from danger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bodybuilder - a person who strengthens and enlarges the muscles of their body through intensive exercise
  • Antonym: weakling - a person lacking physical strength
