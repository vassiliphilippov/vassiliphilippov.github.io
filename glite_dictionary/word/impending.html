impending

1. ADJ

/ ɪmˈpɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
(of something unpleasant or significant) about to happen soon; likely to occur in the near future.
about to happen soon

Examples

The country was preparing for the impending storm by stocking emergency supplies.
She felt anxious about her impending job interview the next morning.
Rumors about an impending merger caused uncertainty among the employees.

Synset Examples

retribution is at hand
some people believe the day of judgment is close at hand
in imminent danger
his impending retirement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imminent - about to happen; likely to occur very soon

Etymology

From Latin 'impendere' (to hang over).
