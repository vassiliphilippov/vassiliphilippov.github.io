glowstick

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡloʊˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊˌstɪk / (BrE)
a small plastic tube filled with chemicals that emit light when mixed, used as a source of temporary illumination, especially at parties and events
plastic tube that glows when activated

Examples

He cracked the glowstick and shook it until it glowed a bright green.
The children wore glowsticks around their wrists at the nighttime festival.
Each guest was given a glowstick to wear during the dance party.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
