fifthly

1. ADV

/ ˈfɪfθli / (AmE), / ˈfɪfθli / (BrE)
Used to introduce the fifth point or item in a series.
in the fifth place; as the fifth point

Examples

Fifthly, we must adhere to the rules set by the local government.
He listed his proposals one by one, concluding, 'fifthly, we need better communication.'
The committee discussed four issues already; fifthly, they focused on budget constraints.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: firstly - used to introduce the first point or argument in a series
  • Degree Lower: secondly - used to introduce the second point or argument in a series
  • Degree Lower: fourthly - used to introduce the fourth point or argument in a series
  • Degree Higher: sixthly - used to introduce the sixth point or argument in a series

Etymology

Formed from "fifth" + "-ly", following the pattern of ordinal adverbs.
