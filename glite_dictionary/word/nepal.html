Nepal

1. PROPN

/ nəˈpɑl / (AmE), / nɪˈpɑːl / (BrE)
A small, landlocked country in South Asia, located in the Himalayas between India and China.
country in the Himalayas, South Asia

Examples

She spent two weeks trekking in Nepal last year.
Mount Everest, the world's highest peak, is located on the border of Nepal and China.
Nepal is known for its diverse culture and breathtaking mountain landscapes.

Etymology

From Sanskrit नेपाल (Nepāla), traditional name of the country.
