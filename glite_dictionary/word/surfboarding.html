surfboarding

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːfˌbɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfˌbɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport of riding a surfboard on the crest of a wave toward the shore
the sport of riding waves on a surfboard

Examples

Many people travel to Hawaii specifically for the surfboarding opportunities.
He took up surfboarding during his summer vacation and quickly became skilled at the sport.
Competitions in surfboarding attract top athletes from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surfing - the sport of riding waves, typically on a surfboard

Etymology

From 'surfboard' (from 'surf' + 'board') + '-ing'.
