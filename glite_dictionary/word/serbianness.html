Serbianness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝː.bi.ə.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.bɪ.ə.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being Serbian, especially with regard to national identity, culture, or character.
the quality or state of being Serbian

Examples

Many traditions have been preserved to express a distinct sense of Serbianness.
Debates about Serbianness often arise in discussions of national identity.
Some feared that modern influences might dilute the core of Serbianness.

Synset Examples

The linking thread between the groups is the rejection of the EU, Nato and other western influences which could endanger ‘Serbianness’.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Serbian - relating to Serbia or its people, language, or culture

Etymology

From Serbian + -ness, denoting a state or quality.
