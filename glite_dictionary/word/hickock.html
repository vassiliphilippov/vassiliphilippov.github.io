Hickock

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɪkɑk / (AmE), / ˈhɪkɒk / (BrE)
(1837–1876) An American frontier marshal, scout, and gunfighter known for his legendary adventures in the Wild West.
famous American frontier marshal

Examples

Stories of Hickock and his exploits have been retold for generations.
Many television shows have portrayed the life of Hickock in the Old West.
Visitors to the small towns of Kansas can learn about Hickock at local museums.
