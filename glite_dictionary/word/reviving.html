reviving

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈvaɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈvaɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
making someone feel renewed energy, life, or strength; restoring vitality.
restoring energy or vitality

Examples

The reviving breeze from the ocean made us feel awake again.
A cup of reviving tea was just what she needed after a long walk.
He welcomed the reviving effects of a cold shower on a summer day.

Synset Examples

the renewing warmth of the sunshine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refreshing - making you feel less tired or hot or thirsty; invigorating

Etymology

Present participle of 'revive', from Latin revivere (to live again).
