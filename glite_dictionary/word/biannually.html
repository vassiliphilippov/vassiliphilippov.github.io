biannually

1. ADV

/ baɪˈæn.ju.ə.li / (AmE), / baɪˈæn.ju.ə.li / (BrE)
Twice a year; occurring every six months.
twice a year

Examples

The organization publishes its newsletter biannually to keep members informed of the latest developments.
We schedule health inspections biannually to ensure compliance with regulations.
The committee meets biannually to review the progress of ongoing projects.

Synset Examples

we hold our big sale biannually

Related Terms

  • Synonym: semiannually - occurring twice a year

Etymology

From Latin 'bi-' (two) + 'annual' (yearly).
