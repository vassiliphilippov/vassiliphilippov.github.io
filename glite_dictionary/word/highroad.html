highroad

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪˌroʊd / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌrəʊd / (BrE)
A main road or highway, especially one that is well-traveled or designated for public use.
main road; highway

Examples

We took the highroad through the mountains to reach the city faster.
The highroad was closed due to heavy snow, forcing drivers to find alternate routes.
Travelers preferred the highroad for its beautiful scenery and smooth pavement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: highway - a main road, especially for long distances

Etymology

Formed from 'high' and 'road'; 'high road' historically referred to a major public road.
