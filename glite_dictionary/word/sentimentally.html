sentimentally

1. ADV

/ ˌsɛntəˈmɛntəli / (AmE), / ˌsɛntɪˈmɛntəli / (BrE)
In a way that is full of or influenced by tender emotions or nostalgia.
in a sentimental or emotional manner

Examples

She looked sentimentally at the old photographs and sighed softly.
He sentimentally recalled the days of his youth during his retirement speech.
The movie sentimentally portrays the bond between a father and daughter.

Synset Examples

‘I miss the good old days,’ she added sentimentally

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sentimental - affectionately emotional or nostalgic

Etymology

From sentimental + -ly, forming the adverb from the adjective.
