adaptative

1. ADJ

/ əˈdæptəˌteɪtɪv / (AmE), / əˈdaptətɪv / (BrE)
having the ability or capacity to adapt or adjust to new conditions or environments
capable of adapting

Examples

Species that are adaptative tend to survive major environmental changes.
The adaptative mechanisms in the immune system help fight off new infections.
Her adaptative approach allowed her to succeed in many different jobs.

Synset Examples

the adaptive coloring of a chameleon

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adaptive - having a capacity for adaptation

Etymology

From French 'adaptatif', based on Latin 'adaptare' (to adapt).
