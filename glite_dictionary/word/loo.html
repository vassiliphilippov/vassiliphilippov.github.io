loo

1. NOUN

/ luː / (AmE), / luː / (BrE)
(British) A toilet or bathroom.
British toilet or bathroom
british informal

Examples

She asked where the loo was as soon as she entered the cafe.
They stopped at the service station for a quick visit to the loo.
The loo on this train is surprisingly clean.

Related Terms

Etymology

Probably from French "lieu" (place), with reference to "lieu d'aisance" (place of ease).
