sub-Saharan

1. ADJ

/ sʌb səˈhɛrən / (AmE), / sʌb səˈhɑːrən / (BrE)
Relating to or situated in the part of Africa south of the Sahara Desert.
of Africa south of the Sahara Desert

Examples

Many sub-Saharan countries have experienced rapid economic growth in recent years.
Malaria remains a major health issue in sub-Saharan Africa.
Research on sub-Saharan wildlife has revealed a rich biodiversity unique to the region.

Etymology

From 'sub-' (below) and 'Saharan' (of or relating to the Sahara Desert).
