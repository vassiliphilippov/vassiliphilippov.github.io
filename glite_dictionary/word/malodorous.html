malodorous

1. ADJ

/ ˌmælˈoʊdərəs / (AmE), / ˌmæləʊˈdɔːrəs / (BrE)
having an unpleasant or offensive smell
having an unpleasant smell

Examples

The malodorous garbage bin was finally emptied after days of neglect.
A malodorous cloud drifted from the polluted river, making it hard to breathe nearby.
His socks were so malodorous that no one wanted to sit beside him.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fragrant - having a pleasant smell
  • Synonym: smelly - having a strong or unpleasant odor

Etymology

From Latin malodorus, from malus (bad) + odor (smell).
