irreplaceable

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪrəˈpleɪsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪrəˈpleɪsəbl / (BrE)
Impossible to replace because of being unique, valuable, or having special qualities.
impossible to replace; unique or invaluable

Examples

The vase was irreplaceable, having been in the family for generations.
After the fire, many irreplaceable artifacts were lost forever.
Her guidance and support are irreplaceable to the success of the team.

Synset Examples

irreplaceable antiques

Related Terms

  • Antonym: replaceable - able to be replaced

Etymology

From prefix 'ir-' (not) + 'replaceable' (able to be replaced).
