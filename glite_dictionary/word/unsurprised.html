unsurprised

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnsərˈpraɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnsəˈprʌɪzd / (BrE)
not feeling or showing surprise; not surprised by something unexpected
not surprised; not expressing surprise

Examples

She seemed unsurprised when she heard the unexpected news.
His unsurprised reaction suggested he had anticipated the outcome.
They found her calm and unsurprised in the midst of the chaos.

Synset Examples

that unsurprised obstinate look on his face

Related Terms

  • Antonym: surprised - feeling or showing surprise

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + surprised.

2. ADJ

/ ʌn.sɚˈpraɪzd / (AmE), / ʌn.səˈpraɪzd / (BrE)
not feeling or showing surprise; not surprised.
not surprised; not expressing surprise

Examples

She seemed unsurprised by the news, as if she had expected it all along.
His unsurprised reaction told me that he already knew about the problem.
The manager remained unsurprised by the sudden changes in the schedule.

Synset Examples

that unsurprised obstinate look on his face

Related Terms

  • Antonym: surprised - feeling or showing surprise
  • Degree Higher: astonished - greatly surprised or impressed
