canaliculate

1. ADJ

/ kəˈnælɪkjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / kəˈnalɪkjʊleɪt / (BrE)
having narrow, parallel channels or grooves; channeled.
having narrow parallel channels

Examples

The botanist noted the canaliculate stems of the water plant in his report.
Some insects have canaliculate antennae that help them sense their environment more effectively.
The canaliculate structure of the shell is visible under a magnifying glass.

Synset Examples

canaliculate leafstalks of certain plants

Etymology

From Latin canaliculatus, from canaliculus ('small channel').
