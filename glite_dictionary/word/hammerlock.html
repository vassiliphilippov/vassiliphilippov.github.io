hammerlock

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæmɚˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈhæməlɒk / (BrE)
A wrestling hold in which an opponent's arm is twisted up behind their back, causing pain or immobilization.
arm-twisting wrestling hold

Examples

The wrestler put his opponent in a hammerlock and forced him to the ground.
She escaped the hammerlock by quickly twisting her body to the side.
In a surprising move, the coach demonstrated a perfect hammerlock during training.
