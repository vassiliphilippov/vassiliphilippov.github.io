pissed

1. ADJ

/ pɪst / (AmE), / pɪst / (BrE)
(informal) Very annoyed or angry.
very annoyed or angry (informal)
informal

Examples

She was so pissed when she found out about the mistake.
I get really pissed when people are late for meetings.
He seemed pissed after losing the game by just one point.

Related Terms

Etymology

Originally from past participle of 'piss', used figuratively in 20th-century English slang to mean 'angry'.
