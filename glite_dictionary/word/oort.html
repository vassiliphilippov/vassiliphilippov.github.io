Oort

1. PROPN

/ ɔrt / (AmE), / ɔːt / (BrE)
(Jan Hendrik) Oort (1900–1992), Dutch astronomer renowned for proving that the galaxy is rotating and for proposing the existence of the Oort cloud.
Dutch astronomer (1900–1992)

Examples

Many discoveries in astronomy are attributed to Oort and his pioneering research.
The Oort constant helps describe the rotation of our galaxy.
The renowned astronomer Oort proposed the idea of a distant cloud of comets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Oort cloud - theoretical cloud of icy bodies believed to surround the solar system

Etymology

Jan Hendrik Oort; Dutch surname.
