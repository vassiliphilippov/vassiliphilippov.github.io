uranalysis

1. NOUN

/ jʊəˈrænəlɪs / (AmE), / jʊəˈrænəlɪs / (BrE)
the chemical analysis of urine, especially for medical diagnosis
chemical analysis of urine
medical

Examples

The doctor ordered a uranalysis to help diagnose the patient's symptoms.
A routine uranalysis can reveal signs of infection or other health issues.
Accurate uranalysis is essential for detecting certain kidney disorders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urinalysis - examination of urine for medical diagnosis

Etymology

Formed from 'urine' and 'analysis'.
