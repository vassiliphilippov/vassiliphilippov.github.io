asphyxiated

1. ADJ

/ æsˈfɪksiˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / æsˈfɪksɪˌeɪtɪd / (BrE)
Unable to breathe because of a lack of oxygen; in a state of asphyxia.
deprived of oxygen; suffocated

Examples

The patient was found asphyxiated due to a gas leak in the apartment.
Emergency responders rescued the asphyxiated victims of the fire.
She became asphyxiated after being trapped in the small, airless room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suffocated - unable to breathe due to lack of air

Etymology

From Greek 'asphyxia' (stopping of the pulse) via modern Latin, plus the English suffix '-ated'.
