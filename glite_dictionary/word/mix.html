mix

1. NOUN

/ mɪks / (AmE), / mɪks / (BrE)
an event or situation in which different things or people are combined together
a combining of different things or people

Examples

The festival was a mix of music, food, and art from around the world.
There was an interesting mix of ages and backgrounds at the conference.
Their new neighborhood has a diverse mix of cultures and traditions.

Synset Examples

a gradual mixture of cultures

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mixture - something made by combining two or more things

Etymology

From Latin miscēre (to mix).

/ mɪks / (AmE), / mɪks / (BrE)
to combine two or more substances or elements together so that they become a single substance or mixture
