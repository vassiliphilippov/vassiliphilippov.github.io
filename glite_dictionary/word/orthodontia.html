orthodontia

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔrθəˈdɑnʃə / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəˈdɒnʃə / (BrE)
the branch of dentistry concerned with preventing and correcting irregularities of the teeth and jaws
dentistry branch for teeth alignment
medical

Examples

After her dentist recommended orthodontia, she scheduled a consultation with an orthodontist.
Advances in orthodontia now allow for less noticeable braces and faster results.
The field of orthodontia focuses on correcting teeth and jaw alignment issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orthodontics - the branch of dentistry dealing with the correction of teeth irregularities

Etymology

From Greek 'orthos' (straight) + 'odont-' (tooth).
