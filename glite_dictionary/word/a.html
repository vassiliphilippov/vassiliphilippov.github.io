a

1. NOUN

/ eɪ / (AmE), / eɪ / (BrE)
The first letter of the English alphabet.
the first letter of the alphabet

Examples

She taught her son to write the letter a before any other letters.
The word 'apple' begins with an a.
On the test, I circled answer choice a for question one.

Etymology

From Latin alphabet, ultimately from the Greek letter 'alpha (α)', derived from the Phoenician 'aleph'.
