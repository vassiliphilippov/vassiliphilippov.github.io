microtaggant

1. NOUN

/ ˌmaɪkroʊˈtæɡənt / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈtæɡənt / (BrE)
a microscopic identification particle used to trace explosives, hazardous materials, or prevent counterfeiting
microscopic identification particle

Examples

The explosives were embedded with microtaggants to allow authorities to trace their origin.
Manufacturers now add microtaggant particles to their products to help prevent counterfeiting.
Forensic scientists used a microtaggant to link the material back to its manufacturer.

Etymology

From 'micro-' (small) + 'taggant', a coined term based on 'tag'.
