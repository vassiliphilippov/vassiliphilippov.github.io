sending

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of causing something, especially a message, to be sent or delivered to another place or person
the act of dispatching something

Examples

The sending of the documents took longer than expected due to technical difficulties.
Efficient sending of messages is crucial for successful communication in business.
There is a delay in the sending of the invitation emails to the participants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transmission - the act of sending something from one place to another, especially electronically
  • Antonym: receiving - the act of getting or taking delivery of something
