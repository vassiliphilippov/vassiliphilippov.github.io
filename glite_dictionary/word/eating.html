eating

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of consuming food; the process of taking food into the body through the mouth
the act of consuming food

Examples

Healthy eating habits can improve your overall well-being.
There will be outdoor eating areas at the festival this year.
Binge eating is a serious health problem for some people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feeding - the act of giving food to a person or animal
  • Antonym: fasting - the act of abstaining from all or some types of food

Etymology

From the verb 'eat' + -ing, forming a noun indicating action or process.
