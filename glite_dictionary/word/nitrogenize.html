to nitrogenize

1. VERB

/ ˈnaɪtrədʒəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈnɪtrədʒənaɪz / (BrE)
to treat or combine with nitrogen or a nitrogen compound
treat with nitrogen

Examples

Modern fertilizers often nitrogenize the soil to promote better crop growth.
The researchers nitrogenized the samples before beginning their experiments.
To improve fuel quality, the refinery plans to nitrogenize the hydrocarbon mixture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nitrate - to treat with nitric acid or a nitrate

Etymology

Formed from 'nitrogen' + '-ize' (to make or treat with).
