pork-fish

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔrkˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɔːkˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a black and gold striped grunt fish (Anisotremus virginicus) found from Bermuda to the Caribbean to Brazil
a black and gold grunt fish species

Examples

The pork-fish is easily recognized by its striking black and gold stripes.
While snorkeling in the Caribbean, we spotted a school of pork-fish near the reef.
Marine biologists often study the pork-fish because of its unique coloration and habitat.
