quern

1. NOUN

/ kwɝn / (AmE), / kwɜːn / (BrE)
a simple hand-operated stone device used for grinding corn or grain into flour
hand-operated stone grain grinder

Examples

The archaeologists discovered a quern used by ancient farmers for grinding cereals.
She demonstrated how to use a traditional quern to produce flour from wheat.
Fragments of a stone quern were unearthed at the excavation site.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mill - a machine for grinding grain into flour

Etymology

From Old English 'cweorn', related to Old Norse 'kvern' and German 'Querne', all meaning 'mill'.
