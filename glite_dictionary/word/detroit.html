Detroit

1. PROPN

/ dəˈtrɔɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈtrɔɪt / (BrE)
A large city in southeastern Michigan, United States, positioned on the Detroit River and known as the center of the American automobile industry.
major city in Michigan, USA
american

Examples

My grandparents moved to Detroit in the 1950s to work in the automobile factories.
Many jazz musicians got their start performing in Detroit's vibrant music scene.
The skyline of Detroit is visible from across the river in Windsor, Canada.

Etymology

Named after the Detroit River, from French 'le détroit du Lac Érié', meaning 'the strait of Lake Erie'.
