hang glider

1. NOUN

/ hæŋ ˈɡlaɪdər / (AmE), / hæŋ ˈɡlaɪdə / (BrE)
a person who flies or rides a hang glider
rider or pilot of a hang glider

Examples

The hang glider soared gracefully above the valley during the competition.
As an experienced hang glider, she knew how to catch the best air currents.
Each hang glider must wear proper safety gear before taking off from the cliff.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pilot - a person who operates the flying controls of an aircraft
