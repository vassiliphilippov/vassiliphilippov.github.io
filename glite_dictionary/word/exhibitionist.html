exhibitionist

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who deliberately behaves in a way intended to attract attention or display their abilities
person who seeks to attract attention

Examples

The exhibitionist waved at strangers and cracked jokes loudly to draw a crowd.
Many thought the comedian was an exhibitionist because of his flamboyant stage presence.
Her exhibitionist tendencies were obvious at every party she attended.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: show-off - a person who acts pretentiously to impress others
  • Antonym: introvert - a person more focused on internal thoughts and less on external attention

Etymology

From exhibition + -ist, modeled on French exhibitionniste.

2. NOUN

/ ˌɛɡˌzɪbɪˈʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person with a compulsive desire to expose their genitals to others, usually in public, for sexual gratification
person who exposes genitals in public
medical

Examples

The police arrested the exhibitionist after several reports from park visitors.
A diagnosed exhibitionist may require psychological treatment for the underlying impulse.
Witnesses were shocked when the exhibitionist exposed himself in a crowded subway car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flasher - a person who exposes their genitals in public

Etymology

From Latin 'exhibere' (to display) + '-ist'.
