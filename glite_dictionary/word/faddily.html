faddily

1. ADV

/ ˈfæd.ɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈfæd.ɪ.li / (BrE)
In a manner that is characteristic of a fad; showing an excessive or superficial attachment to trends.
in a faddish manner

Examples

The new restaurant was decorated faddily, following every passing trend.
She dressed faddily to impress her fashion-conscious friends.
Critics remarked that the technology was marketed a bit too faddily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faddish - displaying concern for following fads

Etymology

Formed from 'faddish' + '-ly'.
