long-wearing

1. ADJ

/ lɔŋˈwɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / lɒŋˈwɛːrɪŋ / (BrE)
Able to withstand wear, tear, or decay for a long time; durable.
durable; lasting a long time

Examples

These long-wearing boots are perfect for hiking on rough terrain.
She prefers long-wearing lipstick that lasts all day without fading.
The long-wearing tiles in the kitchen have not shown any signs of damage after ten years.

Synset Examples

durable denim jeans

Related Terms

  • Synonym: durable - Able to withstand wear, pressure, or damage; hard-wearing.
  • Antonym: fragile - Easily broken or damaged; not durable.
