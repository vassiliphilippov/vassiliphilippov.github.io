suggester

1. NOUN

/ səˈdʒɛstər / (AmE), / səˈdʒɛstə / (BrE)
a person who makes a suggestion or proposal
person who makes a suggestion

Examples

She was the original suggester of the plan that changed our company.
The committee thanked the suggester for her valuable ideas.
If you disagree, please speak directly with the suggester rather than criticizing the proposal in public.

Synset Examples

the suggester of this absurd strategy was a fool

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proposer - a person who puts forward a proposal
  • Synonym: advisor - a person who gives advice

Etymology

From English verb 'suggest' + agent suffix '-er'.
