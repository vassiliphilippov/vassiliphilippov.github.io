mouthlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈmaʊθˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmaʊθˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the appearance of a mouth, especially used for openings or features.
resembling a mouth in appearance

Examples

The cave had a mouthlike entrance that seemed to beckon explorers inside.
The fish's mouthlike gills open and close rhythmically as it breathes.
A strange, mouthlike opening could be seen on the surface of the rock.
