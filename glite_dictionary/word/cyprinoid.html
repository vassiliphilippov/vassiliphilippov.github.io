cyprinoid

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪprɪˌnɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪprɪnɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the fish family Cyprinidae, which includes carps and minnows.
relating to cyprinid fishes

Examples

The cyprinoid fishes are widespread in freshwater habitats worldwide.
Researchers studied the feeding habits of several cyprinoid species in the lake.
The museum's collection includes many cyprinoid specimens, such as carps and minnows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cyprinid - a member of the fish family Cyprinidae

Etymology

From New Latin Cyprinus (the type genus of Cyprinidae) + -oid (meaning 'resembling').
