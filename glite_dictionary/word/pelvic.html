pelvic

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛlvɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɛlvɪk / (BrE)
relating to or located in the pelvis, the lower part of the torso between the abdomen and the legs
relating to the pelvis

Examples

The doctor recommended a pelvic exam during her annual checkup.
She experienced pain in the pelvic region after the accident.
Physical therapy can help strengthen pelvic floor muscles.

Synset Examples

pelvic exam
pelvic inflammation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pelvis - the bony structure at the base of the spine

Etymology

From Modern Latin pelvicus, from Latin pelvis (basin).
