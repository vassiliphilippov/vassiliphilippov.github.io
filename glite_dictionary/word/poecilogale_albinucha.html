Poecilogale albinucha

1. NOUN

/ poʊˌsɪlɪˈɡeɪl ælˌbɪˈnuːkə / (AmE), / pəʊˌsɪlɪˈɡɑːliː ælˌbɪˈnuːkə / (BrE)
A small, slender African weasel with a white patch on the top of its head, known for burrowing habits; also called the African striped weasel.
small African striped weasel

Examples

The Poecilogale albinucha is notable for its distinctive white marking on the head.
Researchers observed the burrowing behavior of Poecilogale albinucha in its natural habitat.
Farmers sometimes confuse Poecilogale albinucha with other small predators due to its slender build.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: African striped weasel - the common name for Poecilogale albinucha

Etymology

From Neo-Latin: 'Poecilogale' (genus) meaning 'varied weasel,' and 'albinucha' meaning 'white nape'.
