niner

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪnə / (BrE)
A word for the number nine, used especially in radio communication to avoid confusion with similar-sounding words.
the number nine, used in radio communication

Examples

The pilot read back the code as niner-one-seven to ensure clarity.
Please confirm, is your altitude five thousand niner hundred feet?
In military radio language, niner is always used instead of nine to avoid mistakes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nine - the number 9

Etymology

From 'nine' with an -er suffix, used in military and aviation communication.
