lxiii

1. NUM

/ ɛl ɛks sɪkstiː ˈθriː / (AmE), / ɛl ɛks sɪksˈtiː ˈθriː / (BrE)
the Roman numeral representing the number sixty-three (63)
Roman numeral for 63

Examples

The inscription read 'lxiii' to indicate the year of the emperor's reign.
She learned that 'lxiii' stands for sixty-three in Roman numerals during her history class.
The stone marker was labeled 'lxiii', showing it was the 63rd milestone on the road.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: lxii - Roman numeral for 62
  • Degree Higher: lxiv - Roman numeral for 64

Etymology

From Latin numerals: 'L' (50), 'X' (10), and 'III' (3).
