meshed

1. ADJ

/ mɛʃt / (AmE), / mɛʃt / (BrE)
(of an arrangement or structure) resembling a network; composed of interconnected parts.
resembling a network; interconnected

Examples

The city has a meshed system of alleyways and small streets.
Their meshed organization allowed for better communication across all departments.
A meshed pattern of veins is visible on the surface of the leaf.

Synset Examples

a meshed road system

Related Terms

  • Synonym: networked - connected in the form of a network
Source: WordNet

Synset POS: s

2. ADJ

/ mɛʃt / (AmE), / mɛʃt / (BrE)
(of toothed parts or gears) interlocked and engaged with each other so that their motion is transmitted together.
interlocked (of gears or toothed parts)

Examples

The two meshed gears ensure smooth power transmission in the machine.
Always check that the gears are properly meshed before starting the engine.
A loud noise may indicate that the gears are not fully meshed.

Synset Examples

the gears are engaged
meshed gears
intermeshed twin rotors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: engaged - (of gears) interlocked and working together
  • Antonym: disengaged - (of gears) not interlocked or not working together
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01104510-s
Synset POS: s
