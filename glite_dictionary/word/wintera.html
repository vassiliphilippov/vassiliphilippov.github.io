/ ˈwɪn.tə.rə / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə.rə / (BrE)
a genus of evergreen shrubs or small trees native to Australia and New Zealand, in the family Winteraceae

Examples

The Wintera species can be found in the temperate forests of New Zealand.
