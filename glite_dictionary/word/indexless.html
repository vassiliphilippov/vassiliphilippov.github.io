indexless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪndɛksləs / (AmE), / ˈɪndɛkslɪs / (BrE)
(of a book or document) lacking an index.
without an index

Examples

The indexless manual made it difficult to find relevant topics quickly.
Readers complained that the indexless textbook was inconvenient for research.
Many older publications are indexless, requiring readers to search through the entire text.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: indexed - having an index; provided with an index

Etymology

Formed from 'index' + suffix '-less', meaning 'without an index'.
