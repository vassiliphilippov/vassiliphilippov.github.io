residentially

1. ADV

/ ˌrɛz.ɪˈdɛn.tʃə.li / (AmE), / ˌrɛz.ɪˈdɛn.ʃəl.i / (BrE)
In a way that relates to residence or use as a dwelling.
in relation to residence or dwellings

Examples

The area is zoned residentially, so commercial development is limited.
She prefers to live residentially close to her workplace for convenience.
The building is residentially occupied throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: residential - relating to or suitable for living in; used as a residence

Etymology

From 'residential' (relating to residence) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
