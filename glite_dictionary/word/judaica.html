Judaica

1. NOUN

/ dʒuˈdeɪɪkə / (AmE), / dʒuːˈdeɪɪkə / (BrE)
objects, books, or other materials relating to Judaism, its culture, or its history.
materials relating to Judaism

Examples

The museum has an impressive collection of Judaica spanning several centuries.
She inherited rare pieces of Judaica from her grandparents.
A new volume of Judaica was recently published by the university press.

Etymology

From Latin 'Judaicus' (Jewish), from Greek 'Ioudaïkos'.
