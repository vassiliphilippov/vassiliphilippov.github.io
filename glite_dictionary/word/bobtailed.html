bobtailed

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑbˌteɪld / (AmE), / ˈbɒbˌteɪld / (BrE)
Having a short or cut tail, especially used of animals.
having a short or shortened tail

Examples

The bobtailed mare trotted across the field, her short tail flicking in the sun.
Many breeds of dogs have naturally bobtailed varieties, which some owners prefer.
We saw a bobtailed fox dashing through the underbrush, its tail barely visible.

Synset Examples

bobtail mare

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bobtail - an animal or thing with a short or docked tail

Etymology

Formed from 'bobtail' (a tail cut short) + -ed.
