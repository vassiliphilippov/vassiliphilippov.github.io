congregational

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑːŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving a congregation, especially in worship or group activity.
relating to a congregation

Examples

The church hosts regular congregational meetings to discuss community issues.
There was a powerful sense of unity during the congregational singing.
Their efforts have increased congregational participation in local service projects.

Synset Examples

congregational membership
congregational singing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: congregation - an assembled group, especially for religious worship

Etymology

From congregation + -al, formed in English.
