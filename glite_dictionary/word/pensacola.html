Pensacola

1. PROPN

/ ˌpɛnsəˈkoʊlə / (AmE), / ˌpɛnsəˈkəʊlə / (BrE)
A city in the extreme northwest of Florida, United States, near the Gulf of Mexico.
city in northwest Florida, USA

Examples

We spent our summer vacation in Pensacola enjoying the beautiful beaches.
Pensacola is known for its rich history and naval air station.
The drive from Tallahassee to Pensacola takes about three hours.

Etymology

From the Spanish adaptation of a Native American (possibly Choctaw or Pensacola tribe) name.
