numeric

1. ADJ

/ nuˈmɛrɪk / (AmE), / njuˈmɛrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or expressed as numbers.
relating to numbers

Examples

The software can perform complex numeric calculations in seconds.
She analyzed the data using various numeric methods.
There are numeric codes on the back of the product for identification.

Synset Examples

a numeral adjective
numerical order
a numeric symbol

Related Terms

  • Synonym: numerical - relating to numbers; often used interchangeably with 'numeric'

Etymology

From Latin numericus (of or belonging to numbers), from numerus (number).

2. ADJ

/ nuˈmɛrɪk / (AmE), / njuˈmɛrɪk / (BrE)
measured or expressed in numbers; relating to numbers or numerical values.
expressed in numbers; numerical

Examples

The software checks that only numeric values are entered in this field.

Synset Examples

the numerical superiority of the enemy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: numerical - relating to or expressed as numbers

From Latin numericus, from numerus (number).
