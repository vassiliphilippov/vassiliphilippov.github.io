anthropoidal

1. ADJ

/ ˌænθrəˈpɔɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌænθrəˈpɔɪdəl / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of an ape, especially in appearance or structure.
resembling an ape

Examples

The creature exhibited anthropoidal features that intrigued the scientists.
Her painting captured an anthropoidal figure lurking in the shadows of the forest.
Some robots are designed with anthropoidal limbs to mimic the movements of apes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anthropoid - resembling a human or ape; having human-like qualities

Etymology

Formed from 'anthropoid' (from Greek 'anthrōpos' meaning 'human being') + '-al', meaning 'pertaining to.'
