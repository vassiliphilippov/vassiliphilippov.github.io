tirolean

1. NOUN

/ tɪˈroʊliən / (AmE), / tɪˈrəʊliːən / (BrE)
A soft green felt hat, typically adorned with a feather or brush, traditionally associated with the Tyrol region of Austria.
a green felt hat with a feather or brush

Examples

He wore a tirolean to the Oktoberfest celebration, complete with a bright red feather.
The tirolean hat is a classic symbol of Alpine tradition and culture.
She bought a green tirolean as a souvenir during her trip to Austria.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fedora - soft felt hat with a brim and indented crown

Etymology

From Tyrolean, meaning 'of or related to Tyrol', a region in the Alps.
