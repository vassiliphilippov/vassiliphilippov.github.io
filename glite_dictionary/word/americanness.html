Americanness

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkənnəs / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkənnəs / (BrE)
the quality or characteristics that are typical of Americans or of the United States
quality of being American

Examples

The novel explores the complicated nature of Americanness through its diverse cast of characters.
She struggled with her Americanness after moving abroad, often feeling caught between cultures.
His speech celebrated the spirit of Americanness and the country's core values.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: American - a citizen or national of the United States

Etymology

Formed from 'American' + '-ness' (suffix forming nouns denoting quality or state).
