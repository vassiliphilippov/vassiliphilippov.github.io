stationer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪʃənər / (AmE), / ˈsteɪʃənə / (BrE)
a person or shop that sells writing materials, paper, and office supplies
seller of writing materials and office supplies

Examples

She stopped by the stationer to pick up some notebooks and pens.
The local stationer offers a wide selection of greeting cards and envelopes.
As a stationer, he enjoyed helping customers find the perfect writing paper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newsagent - a person or shop selling newspapers, magazines, and often stationery

Etymology

From Middle English 'stationer', originally referring to a bookseller with a fixed location (as opposed to itinerant sellers).
