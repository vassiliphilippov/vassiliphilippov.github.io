unnoted

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈnoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈnəʊtɪd / (BrE)
not noticed or not taken into account; overlooked or disregarded
not noticed or taken into account

Examples

The small error in the report went unnoted by the reviewers.
Many acts of kindness often remain unnoted by the public.
His quiet achievements did not go unnoted among his colleagues.

Synset Examples

his retirement was not allowed to go unmarked

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unnoticed - not seen or observed
  • Synonym: ignored - deliberately not considered or acknowledged

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'noted' (observed, remarked).
