nine-membered

1. ADJ

/ ˈnaɪn ˌmɛm.bɚd / (AmE), / ˈnaɪn ˌmɛm.bəd / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) having a ring structure consisting of nine atoms or members.
having a nine-atom ring (chemistry)

Examples

Many natural products contain a nine-membered ring as part of their molecular structure.
The synthesis of nine-membered heterocycles can be challenging due to ring strain.
A nine-membered aromatic ring is less common than its six-membered counterpart.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eight-membered - having a ring of eight members (chemistry)
  • Degree Higher: ten-membered - having a ring of ten members (chemistry)
