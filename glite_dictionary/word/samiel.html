samiel

1. NOUN

/ sæˈmiːl / (AmE), / sæˈmiːl / (BrE)
a violent, hot, sand-laden wind occurring in the deserts of Arabia and North Africa
hot desert wind in Arabia and North Africa

Examples

Travelers must take extra care when a samiel sweeps across the desert, as its force can be deadly.
The caravan halted as the ferocious samiel turned the sky yellow with blowing sand.
Legends speak of lost cities buried beneath the dunes after a sudden samiel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simoom - a hot, dry, suffocating wind in the deserts of Arabia and North Africa

Etymology

From Arabic ساميَل‎ (samiyal), referring to a hot desert wind.
