noteworthy

1. ADJ

/ ˈnoʊtˌwɜːrði / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtˌwɜːði / (BrE)
deserving attention or notice; significant or remarkable.
deserving attention; significant

Examples

The museum features several noteworthy paintings from the 19th century.
It is noteworthy that she completed the project in half the expected time.
His noteworthy contributions to science were recognized with a prestigious award.

Synset Examples

a noteworthy fact is that her students rarely complain
a noteworthy advance in cancer research
a remarkable achievement
a notable increase in the crime rate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: notable - worthy of notice or attention; remarkable
  • Synonym: remarkable - worthy of attention because it is unusual or special
  • Antonym: unremarkable - not particularly special or interesting

Etymology

Formed from 'note' + 'worthy', meaning worthy of being noted. Used since the 17th century.
