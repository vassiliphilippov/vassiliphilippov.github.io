domiciliary

1. ADJ

/ ˌdɑː.məˈsɪl.i.er.i / (AmE), / ˌdɒ.mɪˈsɪl.i.əri / (BrE)
relating to or taking place in a person's home, especially in reference to services or care provided at home rather than in an institution.
provided or occurring at home
medical

Examples

Many elderly patients prefer domiciliary care to staying in a hospital.
The government increased funding for domiciliary support services last year.
She works as a nurse providing domiciliary visits to housebound clients.

Synset Examples

domiciliary medical care
domiciliary caves

Related Terms

  • Synonym: domestic - relating to the home or household

Etymology

From Latin domicilium (dwelling, home).
