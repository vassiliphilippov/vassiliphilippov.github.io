peachy-coloured

1. ADJ

/ ˈpiːtʃi ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈpiːtʃi ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having a color resembling that of a peach; light pinkish-orange.
of a peach-like color

Examples

She wore a peachy-coloured scarf that brightened up her entire outfit.
The sky turned a peachy-coloured hue at sunset, stunning everyone on the beach.
I painted my bedroom walls a soft peachy-coloured shade to make the space feel warmer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peach - a light pinkish-orange color
  • Degree Lower: pink - a pale red color
  • Degree Higher: salmon - a pale pinkish-orange color
