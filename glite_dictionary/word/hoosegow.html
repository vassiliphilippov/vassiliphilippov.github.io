hoosegow

1. NOUN

/ ˈhuːzɡaʊ / (AmE), / ˈhuːzɡaʊ / (BrE)
(slang) A jail or prison.
slang for jail or prison
slang american

Examples

After the fight, he spent the night in the hoosegow.
They threatened to throw him in the hoosegow if he didn't pay the fine.
The old Western movies often showed outlaws ending up in the hoosegow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jail - a place for the confinement of people accused or convicted of a crime
  • Synonym: prison - a building where people are legally held as punishment for a crime

Etymology

From Mexican Spanish 'juzgado' (court), influenced by regional US English pronunciation.
