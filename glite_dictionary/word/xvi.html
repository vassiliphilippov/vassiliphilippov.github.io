XVI

1. NUM

/ ɛks vi aɪ / (AmE), / ɛks vɪ aɪ / (BrE)
The Roman numeral for the number sixteen.
Roman numeral for sixteen

Examples

The clock in the museum is marked with Roman numerals, so the number sixteen is shown as XVI.
The final chapter was labeled XVI in the old book.
On the monument, the year was inscribed as MDCCCLXXVI.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sixteen - the number sixteen, 16
  • Degree Lower: XV - Roman numeral for fifteen, 15
  • Degree Higher: XVII - Roman numeral for seventeen, 17

Etymology

From Latin numeral system, 'X' for ten, 'V' for five, 'I' for one, combined as sixteen.
