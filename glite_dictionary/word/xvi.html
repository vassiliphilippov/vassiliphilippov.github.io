xvi

1. NUM

/ ɛks vi aɪ / (AmE), / ɛks vi aɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for sixteen
Roman numeral for 16

Examples

The king ascended to the throne as Louis XVI of France.
In the outline, section XVI covers the final proposals for the project.
Chapter XVI of the novel describes the protagonist's escape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sixteen - the number 16
  • Degree Lower: xv - the Roman numeral for fifteen
  • Degree Higher: xvii - the Roman numeral for seventeen

Etymology

From Latin numerals, where 'X' = 10, 'V' = 5, and 'I' = 1.
