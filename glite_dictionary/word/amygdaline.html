amygdaline

1. ADJ

/ əˈmɪɡdəˌlaɪn / (AmE), / əˈmɪɡdəliːn / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling an almond, especially in shape or appearance.
relating to or resembling an almond

Examples

The artist painted her eyes with an amygdaline shape to convey delicacy.
Certain seeds possess an amygdaline appearance under the microscope.
The architect designed the windows with an amygdaline curve reminiscent of almond shells.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: almond-shaped - having the shape of an almond

Etymology

From Latin 'amygdalinus' (of or pertaining to the almond), from Greek 'amygdalē' (almond).
