watchmaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑtʃˌmeɪkɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make or repair watches and clocks
person who makes or repairs watches

Examples

My grandfather was a skilled watchmaker who could fix even the oldest pocket watches.
The watchmaker carefully adjusted the tiny gears inside the delicate timepiece.
Many vintage watches require a watchmaker who understands traditional craftsmanship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clockmaker - a person who makes or repairs clocks

Etymology

Formed from 'watch' (timepiece) + 'maker'.
