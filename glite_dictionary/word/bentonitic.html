bentonitic

1. ADJ

/ ˌbɛn.təˈnɪt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌbɛn.təˈnɪt.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to, containing, or characteristic of bentonite, a type of absorbent clay.
containing or relating to bentonite clay

Examples

The soil samples showed a bentonitic composition, making them suitable for landfill liners.
Engineers prefer bentonitic clays for their excellent sealing properties in construction projects.
The bentonitic layer acts as a natural barrier against groundwater contamination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bentonite - an absorbent clay formed by the breakdown of volcanic ash, often used industrially

Etymology

Formed from 'bentonite' (a type of clay) with the suffix '-ic' meaning 'relating to.'
