Hugueninia

1. NOUN

/ ˌhjuːɡəˈnɪniə / (AmE), / ˌhjuːɡɪˈniːniə / (BrE)
A genus of plants in the mustard family, including species such as the tansy-leaved rocket (Hugueninia tanacetifolia), native to parts of Europe.
genus of plants related to tansy-leaved rocket

Examples

The genus Hugueninia is known for its unique leaf shape resembling that of tansy.
Botanists recently documented a rare specimen of Hugueninia in the Alps.
Hugueninia typically grows in rocky mountainous regions of southern Europe.

Etymology

Named in honor of the French botanist Auguste Huguenin.
