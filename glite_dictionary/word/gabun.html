Gabun

1. PROPN

/ ɡəˈbuːn / (AmE), / ɡaˈbuːn / (BrE)
a republic on the west coast of Central Africa, known in English as Gabon
country in Central Africa (Gabon)

Examples

He traveled extensively throughout Gabun to study local wildlife.
The capital city of Gabun is Libreville, located on the Atlantic coast.
Many rare species can be found in the rainforests of Gabun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gabon - a republic on the west coast of Africa (English name)

Etymology

From the Portuguese word 'Gabão', referring to the estuary of the Komo River; 'Gabun' is the German and some European languages' form.
