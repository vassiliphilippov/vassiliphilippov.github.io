scrupulously

1. ADV

/ ˈskruːpjələsli / (AmE), / ˈskruːpjʊləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows great attention to detail and moral integrity; with extreme conscientiousness.
with great care or conscientiousness

Examples

She followed the scientist’s instructions scrupulously to ensure accurate results.
He always checks his expenses scrupulously before submitting a report.
They scrupulously cleaned the kitchen after every meal.

Synset Examples

he came religiously every morning at 8 o'clock

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carefully - with attention and caution
  • Antonym: negligently - in a careless or neglectful way

Etymology

From scrupulous + -ly; "scrupulous" from Latin scrupulus (uneasiness, small stone).
