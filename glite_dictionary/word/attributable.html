attributable

1. ADJ

/ əˈtrɪbjətəbəl / (AmE), / əˈtrɪbjʊtəbəl / (BrE)
(of something) regarded as being caused by or resulting from a particular person, event, or cause.
able to be ascribed to a cause

Examples

The error was attributable to a miscommunication between the teams.
Many believe that the decline in sales is attributable to changing consumer habits.
The work’s success is largely attributable to her leadership.

Synset Examples

the collapse of the movement was attributable to a lack of morale
an idea attributable to a Russian

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assignable - able to be assigned to something or someone
  • Antonym: unattributable - not able to be attributed to a particular cause or source

Etymology

From attribute + -able, meaning 'able to be attributed.'
