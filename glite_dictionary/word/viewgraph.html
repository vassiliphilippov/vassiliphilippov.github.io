viewgraph

1. NOUN

/ ˈvjuːˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈvjuːˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
a transparent sheet used on an overhead projector to display information for an audience
transparency for overhead projectors

Examples

The old professor used a viewgraph to present his research findings to the class.
Before digital projectors, every meeting room was equipped with an overhead projector and a stack of viewgraphs.
She hurried to prepare a new viewgraph after noticing an error in her original presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transparency - a clear sheet for projection purposes

Etymology

Formed from 'view' + 'graph', denoting something viewed with graphic information.
