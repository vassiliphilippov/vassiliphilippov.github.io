freemail

1. NOUN

/ ˈfriːˌmeɪl / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌmeɪl / (BrE)
an email service that is provided free of charge, usually in exchange for exposure to advertisements
free advertising-supported email service

Examples

Many people use freemail accounts for signing up to online services to avoid spam in their main inbox.
The popularity of freemail has increased as more users seek cost-effective communication solutions.
She switched to a freemail provider because she didn't want to pay for email hosting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: webmail - an email service accessed through a web browser
