sedulously

1. ADV

/ ˈsɛdʒələslɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɛdjʊləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows careful and persistent effort or attention; diligently and thoroughly.
in a diligent and thorough manner

Examples

She sedulously researched every detail of the project before presenting her findings.
The gardeners sedulously tended to the roses, ensuring they bloomed perfectly each year.
He sedulously avoided distractions in order to finish his thesis on time.

Synset Examples

this illusion has been sedulously fostered

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diligently - in a way that shows care and conscientiousness in one's work
  • Synonym: industriously - in a hardworking and energetic manner
  • Antonym: carelessly - in a way that shows lack of care or attention

Etymology

From Latin sedulus (zealous, diligent) + -ous + -ly.
