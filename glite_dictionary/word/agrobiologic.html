agrobiologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæɡroʊˌbaɪəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɡrəʊˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of agrobiology, the study of plant nutrition and growth in relation to soil and farming.
relating to agrobiology

Examples

The new fertilizer has significant agrobiologic benefits for wheat production.
Researchers analyzed the agrobiologic properties of the soil to optimize crop yield.
His thesis focused on agrobiologic factors affecting sustainable farming systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agrobiological - relating to agrobiology
  • Synonym: agrobiology - the study of plant nutrition and growth in relation to soil and farming

Etymology

From agrobiology, combining 'agro-' (field, soil) and 'biology.'
