embryologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛm.bri.əˈlɑ.dʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛm.brɪ.əˈlɒ.dʒɪk / (BrE)
Relating to embryos or the development of an organism before birth or hatching.
relating to embryos or pre-birth development
medical

Examples

The scientist focused on embryologic differences between mammals and birds.
Certain birth defects can be traced to embryologic abnormalities during early development.
The research explores embryologic processes that occur before organs are fully formed.

Synset Examples

in the embryonic stage
embryologic development

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embryological - relating to embryos or embryology (variant form)

Etymology

From embryo + -logic, influenced by Greek embryon (young one) and logos (study).
