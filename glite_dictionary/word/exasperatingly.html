exasperatingly

1. ADV

/ ɪɡˈzæspəˌreɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɑːspəˌreɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that causes irritation or annoyance; in an exasperating manner.
in an exasperating way

Examples

She exasperatingly refused to answer any of my questions.
The meeting dragged on exasperatingly slowly, testing everyone's patience.
He exasperatingly pointed out every small mistake I made.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annoyingly - in a way that causes annoyance or irritation
  • Antonym: calmly - in a calm manner, without irritation

Etymology

Formed from 'exasperating' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
