nonaligned

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑːn.əˈlaɪnd / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.əˈlaɪnd / (BrE)
Not affiliated with or formally aligned to any group, faction, party, or cause; neutral or independent.
not allied to any group or party

Examples

The country remained nonaligned during the Cold War, refusing to side with either superpower.
As a nonaligned voter, she prefers to evaluate candidates individually rather than along party lines.
Several nonaligned nations participated in the summit to discuss global cooperation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neutral - not taking sides in a conflict or dispute

Etymology

Formed from Latin prefix 'non-' (not) + 'aligned'.
