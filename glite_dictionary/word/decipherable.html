decipherable

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈsaɪfərəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈsaɪfərəbl / (BrE)
able to be understood or read; capable of being deciphered.
able to be deciphered or made out

Examples

Her handwriting was surprisingly decipherable despite the rush she was in.
The ancient code was only decipherable after years of careful research.
The instructions were written in plain, decipherable language for everyone to understand.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: illegible - not able to be read or deciphered
  • Antonym: indecipherable - impossible to decipher; cannot be understood or interpreted

Etymology

From decipher + -able, using Latin-based roots.
