institutionally

1. ADV

/ ˌɪn.stɪˈtuː.ʃən.əl.i / (AmE), / ˌɪn.stɪˈtjuː.ʃən.əl.i / (BrE)
In a manner related to, involving, or by means of an institution or institutions.
by means of an institution

Examples

Some decisions are institutionally mandated and must follow strict procedures.
The university is institutionally committed to diversity and inclusion.
Housing was institutionally segregated for decades in many cities.

Synset Examples

institutionally controlled

Related Terms

  • Synonym: institutional - relating to an institution

Etymology

From 'institutional' + '-ly'.
