schoolyard

1. NOUN

/ ˈskuːlˌjɑrd / (AmE), / ˈskuːlˌjɑːd / (BrE)
An outdoor area on the grounds of a school where children can play or gather.
yard or playground at a school

Examples

The children ran out to the schoolyard as soon as the lunch bell rang.
Teachers often supervise students in the schoolyard during recess.
She found a lost glove lying near the fence in the schoolyard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: playground - an outdoor area provided for children to play in, especially at a school
