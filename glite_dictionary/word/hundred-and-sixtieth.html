hundred-and-sixtieth

1. ADJ

/ ˌhʌndrəd ən ˈsɪkstiəθ / (AmE), / ˌhʌndrɪd ən ˈsɪkstiəθ / (BrE)
being the ordinal number of one hundred sixty in counting order; 160th.
ordinal number: 160th

Examples

She finished in hundred-and-sixtieth place out of nearly two thousand participants.
His office is on the hundred-and-sixtieth floor of the building.
This is the hundred-and-sixtieth anniversary of the town's founding.
