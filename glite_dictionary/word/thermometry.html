thermometry

1. NOUN

/ θɚˈmɑmətri / (AmE), / θəˈmɒmɪtri / (BrE)
the science or process of measuring temperature
the measurement of temperature
medical

Examples

Thermometry plays a crucial role in weather forecasting and environmental science.
Accurate thermometry is essential in many laboratory experiments.
The development of digital thermometry has improved health care and industrial processes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermometer - an instrument for measuring temperature

Etymology

From Greek thermos (hot) + metron (measure) + -y.
