cookie-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʊki saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈkʊki saɪzd / (BrE)
having approximately the same size as a cookie
about the size of a cookie

Examples

The scientist used a cookie-sized disk to collect samples from the site.
She handed me a cookie-sized patch to test the fabric's durability.
