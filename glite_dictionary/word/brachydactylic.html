brachydactylic

1. ADJ

/ ˌbrækiˌdækˈtɪlɪk / (AmE), / ˌbrækɪˌdækˈtɪlɪk / (BrE)
having abnormally short fingers or toes, especially as a congenital condition
having abnormally short fingers or toes
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that his brachydactylic condition was inherited from his mother.

Related Terms

Etymology
