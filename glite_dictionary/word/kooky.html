kooky

1. ADJ

/ ˈkuːki / (AmE), / ˈkuːki / (BrE)
strange or eccentric in an amusing or appealing way; slightly crazy or odd.
strange or eccentric
informal

Examples

Her kooky sense of humor always makes our meetings more fun.
That artist is known for his kooky ideas and wild costumes.
Wearing mismatched socks is just one of her many kooky habits.

Synset Examples

it used to drive my husband balmy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - unconventional and slightly strange
  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged or extremely strange

Etymology

Probably from 'kook' (slang for a strange or eccentric person), itself from 'cuckoo'.
