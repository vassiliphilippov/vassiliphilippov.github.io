ultimateness

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌltɪmətnəs / (AmE), / ˈʌltɪmətnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being ultimate; the final or most extreme degree, point, or nature of something
quality of being ultimate; extremity or finality

Examples

Philosophers have long debated the ultimateness of reality and existence.
In the contest, the ultimateness of her achievement was clear to everyone.
Some religions emphasize the ultimateness of spiritual truth above material concerns.

Synset Examples

the ultimacy of these social values

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ultimacy - the state or fact of being ultimate; finality
  • Synonym: finality - the quality of being final or complete

Etymology

From ultimate + -ness, forming a noun denoting a state or quality.
