rust-brown

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌstˌbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈrʌstˌbraʊn / (BrE)
having the brownish color of rust; reddish-brown.
reddish-brown like rust

Examples

The antique chair had a rust-brown patina that gave it character.
A rust-brown fox darted through the field at dusk.
Her dress was a deep, rust-brown shade perfect for autumn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reddish-brown - a brown color tinged with red
