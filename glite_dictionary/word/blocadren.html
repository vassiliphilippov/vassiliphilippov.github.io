Blocadren

1. NOUN

/ ˈbloʊkəˌdrɛn / (AmE), / ˈbləʊkəˌdrɛn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for timolol, a beta blocker used primarily to treat high blood pressure and to prevent complications after heart attacks.
beta blocker medication (brand name)
medical

Examples

After his heart attack, the doctor prescribed Blocadren to help reduce his risk of another episode.
Patients taking Blocadren should monitor their blood pressure regularly as advised by their physician.
The pharmacist explained that Blocadren is a brand name for the drug timolol.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: timolol - beta blocker medication used for hypertension and glaucoma
  • Synonym: beta blocker - type of drug that blocks beta-adrenergic substances, reducing blood pressure
