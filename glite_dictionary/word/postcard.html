postcard

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstˌkɑrd / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstkɑːd / (BrE)
a card, often with a picture on one side, used for sending a short message by mail without an envelope
card for mailing messages without envelope

Examples

I sent my grandmother a postcard from Paris to let her know I arrived safely.
She collects vintage postcards from different countries as a hobby.
Write your message on the back of the postcard and add the address before mailing it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greeting card - a card sent to express good wishes
