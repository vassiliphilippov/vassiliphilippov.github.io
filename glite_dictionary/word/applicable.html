applicable

1. ADJ

/ ˈæplɪkəbl / (AmE), / əˈplɪkəbl / (BrE)
relevant or appropriate to a particular situation or purpose; capable of being applied.
relevant or suitable

Examples

The discount is only applicable to selected products in the store.
These rules are not applicable in every situation.
Please include only the information applicable to your current address.

Synset Examples

gave applicable examples to support her argument

Related Terms

  • Antonym: irrelevant - not connected with or relevant to something
  • Synonym: relevant - closely connected or appropriate to what is being done or considered

Etymology

From Late Latin applicabilis, from Latin applicare ‘to apply’.
