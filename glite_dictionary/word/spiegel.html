spiegel

1. NOUN

/ ˈspiːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈspiːɡəl / (BrE)
a type of pig iron high in manganese, used as a deoxidizing agent and to raise the manganese content in steel production
pig iron high in manganese, used in steelmaking

Examples

The steel plant imported spiegel to improve the quality of its alloy.
Adding spiegel during the process enhances the steel's strength and durability.
Early steelmakers relied on spiegel as a source of manganese.

Etymology

From German 'Spiegeleisen' (mirror iron), referring to its shiny fracture surface.
