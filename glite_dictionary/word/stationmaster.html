stationmaster

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪʃənˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈsteɪʃənˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
the person who is in charge of a railway station
person in charge of a railway station

Examples

The stationmaster greeted passengers at the entrance to the platform.
As stationmaster, he was responsible for the safety of both staff and travelers.
The new stationmaster implemented several improvements at the old railway station.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: station manager - person who manages a railway station
  • Degree Lower: porter - person who helps passengers with luggage at a railway station

Etymology

From 'station' (railway station) + 'master' (person in charge), common in British English since the 19th century.
