June

1. NOUN

/ dʒun / (AmE), / dʒuːn / (BrE)
The sixth month of the year, following May and preceding July.
sixth month of the year

Examples

School usually ends in June for most students in the United States.
We are planning our wedding for June next year.
Roses typically bloom in June in this climate.

Etymology

From Latin 'Iunius', named after the Roman goddess Juno.
