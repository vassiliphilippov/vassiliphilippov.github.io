plumbous

1. ADJ

/ ˈplʌm.bəs / (AmE), / ˈplʌm.bəs / (BrE)
Relating to or containing lead, especially with a valence of +2 (as in lead(II) compounds).
of or relating to lead (II); lead with valence +2
medical

Examples

Compounds such as plumbous oxide contain lead in the +2 oxidation state.
Exposure to plumbous solutions can be hazardous to health in laboratory settings.
The chemist analyzed the sample and confirmed it contained a plumbous compound.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: plumbic - Relating to lead with a valence of +4 (lead(IV))

Etymology

From Latin 'plumbum' (lead), with the suffix '-ous' indicating a lower valence in chemistry.
