unpillared

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpɪlɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɪləd / (BrE)
(of a structure) having no pillars; not supported or adorned with pillars.
lacking pillars

Examples

The grand hall was distinctly unpillared, creating an uninterrupted, open space.
He admired the unpillared design that allowed for more natural light to enter the building.
Unlike most temples, this one remains completely unpillared and relies on other forms of structural support.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pillared - having or supported by pillars
