lite

1. ADJ

/ laɪt / (AmE), / laɪt / (BrE)
(of food or drink) containing fewer calories or less fat than the usual version; light.
lower-calorie or reduced-fat (food or drink)

Examples

She asked for a lite salad dressing with her meal.
Many people prefer lite beers because they have fewer calories.
This yogurt is labeled as lite, but still tastes creamy.

Synset Examples

light (or lite) beer
lite (or light) mayonnaise
a low-cal diet
cola light

Related Terms

  • Synonym: light - having less weight, calories, or intensity

Etymology

Altered spelling of 'light', adopted in marketing to emphasize reduced calories or fat.
