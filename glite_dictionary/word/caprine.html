caprine

1. ADJ

/ ˈkæpraɪn / (AmE), / ˈkaprɪn / (BrE)
relating to or resembling goats; characteristic of a goat or goats.
relating to or resembling goats

Examples

The disease is most commonly found in caprine animals such as goats.
She spoke in a caprine voice that was oddly high-pitched and nasal.
Researchers studied a caprine strain of the virus affecting livestock.

Synset Examples

caprine creatures
a caprine strain of virus
a caprine voice

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bovine - relating to cows or cattle
  • Antonym: ovine - relating to sheep

Etymology

From Latin caprinus (of a goat), from caper (goat).
