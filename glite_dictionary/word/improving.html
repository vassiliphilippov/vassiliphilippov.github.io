improving

1. ADJ

/ ɪmˈpruvɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪmˈpruːvɪŋ / (BrE)
showing progress or becoming better; getting higher in quality, amount, or strength
becoming better; showing progress

Examples

Analysts noted that the improving job market led to increased consumer confidence.
His health is steadily improving after the surgery.
The team's improving performance this season surprised many fans.
