tercentennial

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɜːrsɛnˈtɛniəl / (AmE), / ˌtɜːsɛnˈtɛnɪəl / (BrE)
The 300th anniversary of an event, or a celebration marking 300 years since it occurred.
300th anniversary or celebration

Examples

The university hosted a series of lectures to commemorate its tercentennial last year.
Boston’s tercentennial celebration attracted visitors from all over the world.
The tercentennial of the city’s founding was marked by a grand parade in the streets.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: bicentennial - the 200th anniversary or celebration thereof
  • Degree Higher: quadricentennial - the 400th anniversary or celebration thereof
  • Synonym: tricentennial - the 300th anniversary or celebration thereof (variant form)

Etymology

Formed from Latin roots: 'ter' (three) + 'centennial' (hundred years).
