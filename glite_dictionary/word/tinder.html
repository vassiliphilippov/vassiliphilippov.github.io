tinder

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪndɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɪndə / (BrE)
Dry, flammable material used for catching a spark and starting a fire.
material for starting a fire

Examples

We gathered dry tinder to help get the campfire going quickly.
The match failed to ignite the damp tinder in the fire pit.
Before lighting the fire, she placed some tinder beneath the logs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kindling - small sticks or twigs used for starting a fire

Etymology

From Old English 'tynder', related to 'tendan' (to kindle).
