inventively

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈvɛntɪvli / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɛntɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that shows creativity and originality; in an inventive manner.
in a creative or original way

Examples

The team solved the difficult problem very inventively, impressing their supervisor.
She decorates her home inventively, always finding new uses for old items.
The chef inventively combined ingredients to create a unique flavor.

Synset Examples

Picasso's liberated shapes and excitingly applied and inventively combined colours

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creatively - in a creative manner

Etymology

From 'inventive' + '-ly'. 'Inventive' from Latin 'inventivus', from 'invenire' (to come upon, find).
