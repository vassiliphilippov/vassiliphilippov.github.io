pipe-opener

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪp ˌoʊpənɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪp ˌəʊpənə / (BrE)
an action, comment, or activity that serves to initiate conversation or ease social interaction; an icebreaker
something that initiates conversation

Examples

Starting with a light-hearted question can be a good pipe-opener at a networking event.
Her clever anecdote worked as the perfect pipe-opener at the dinner table.
A group game served as an effective pipe-opener during the team retreat.

Synset Examples

He used a joke as a pipe-opener for the meeting.
The new coffee shop is a great pipe-opener for conversations.
A fun activity is the perfect pipe-opener for team bonding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: icebreaker - something that initiates conversation in a group
