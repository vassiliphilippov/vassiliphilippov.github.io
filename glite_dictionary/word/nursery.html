nursery

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɝː.sɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈnɜː.sər.i / (BrE)
a room in a house set aside for a baby or young child, often equipped for sleeping and play
a child's room for a baby

Examples

We painted the nursery a soft yellow before the baby arrived.
The baby sleeps in the nursery next to the parents' bedroom.
They decorated the nursery with animal-themed wallpaper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedroom - a room used for sleeping in

Etymology

From Middle English 'nurserie', from Old French 'nourrice' (nurse), from Latin 'nutricia'.
