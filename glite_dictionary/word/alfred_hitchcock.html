Alfred Hitchcock

1. PROPN

/ ˈælfrəd ˈhɪtʃkɒk / (AmE), / ˈælfrɪd ˈhɪtʃkɒk / (BrE)
(1899–1980) An English film director and producer famed for his skill in creating suspenseful movies, often called the 'Master of Suspense'.
English film director (1899–1980)

Examples

Alfred Hitchcock directed many classic thrillers, including 'Psycho' and 'Rear Window'.
Many film critics consider Alfred Hitchcock the master of suspense in cinema.
Alfred Hitchcock's unique storytelling style has influenced generations of filmmakers.
