Nentsy

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛntsi / (AmE), / ˈnɛntsi / (BrE)
a Uralic language spoken by the Nenets people of northern Siberia
a Uralic language of northern Siberia

Examples

The Nentsy language is mainly spoken by indigenous people living in the Arctic region.
Researchers are documenting the grammar of Nentsy to preserve it for future generations.
Few young people today are fluent in Nentsy, raising concerns about its survival.

Etymology

From Russian 'Ненцы' (Nentsy), the Russian ethnonym for the Nenets people.
