juice

1. NOUN

/ dʒus / (AmE), / dʒuːs / (BrE)
a liquid present in or produced by the body, such as digestive juices
liquid produced by the body

Examples

The stomach produces several types of juice that help with digestion.
Doctors studied the effects of different bodily juices on the immune system.
Enzymes are secreted into the intestinal juice during food processing.

Synset Examples

digestive juices

Etymology

From Old French jus (liquid, broth), from Latin jus (broth, sauce, juice).

2. NOUN

/ dʒus / (AmE), / dʒuːs / (BrE)

Examples

You can use lemon juice to add flavor to the dish.
