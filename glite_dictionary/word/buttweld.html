to buttweld

1. VERB

/ ˈbʌtˌwɛld / (AmE), / ˈbʌtˌwɛld / (BrE)
to join two pieces of metal or other materials together by a butt weld
join by a butt weld

Examples

The technician will buttweld the two metal pipes for a seamless joint.
They buttwelded several steel plates during the bridge construction.
Before painting, make sure all sections have been properly buttwelded.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weld - to join pieces of metal by heating and pressing; general term for metal joining

Etymology

From 'butt', meaning to join end to end, and 'weld', from Old English *wellan* (to boil, weld).
