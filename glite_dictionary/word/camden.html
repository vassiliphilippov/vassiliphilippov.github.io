Camden

1. PROPN

/ ˈkæmdən / (AmE), / ˈkæmdən / (BrE)
A city in southwestern New Jersey, United States, situated on the Delaware River near Philadelphia.
city in New Jersey, US

Examples

She grew up in Camden and often visited Philadelphia on weekends.
The museum in Camden attracts thousands of visitors each year.
After moving to Camden, he commuted daily to his job in the city center.

Etymology

Named after Charles Pratt, 1st Earl Camden, an 18th-century English nobleman.
