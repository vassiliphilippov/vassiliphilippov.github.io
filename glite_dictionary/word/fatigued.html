fatigued

1. ADJ

/ fəˈtiɡd / (AmE), / fəˈtiːɡd / (BrE)
Extremely tired; drained of energy or effectiveness.
extremely tired; exhausted

Examples

After the long hike, everyone felt completely fatigued and ready to sleep.
He looked fatigued after working on the project all night.
If you are feeling fatigued, it's important to take a break and rest.

Synset Examples

the day's shopping left her exhausted
he went to bed dog-tired
was fagged and sweaty
the trembling of his played out limbs
felt completely washed-out
only worn-out horses and cattle
you look worn out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhausted - extremely tired; completely drained of energy
  • Antonym: refreshed - feeling rested and full of energy again

Etymology

From French *fatigué*, past participle of *fatiguer* (to tire), from Latin *fatigare* (to weary).
