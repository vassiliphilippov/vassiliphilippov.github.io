unretrievable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈtriː.və.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈtriː.və.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to recover, regain, or get back; unable to be restored or reclaimed.
impossible to recover or regain

Examples

The stolen data was deemed unretrievable after a major cyberattack.
Once trust is broken, it can be unretrievable in some relationships.
The museum mourned the unretrievable artifacts lost in the fire.

Synset Examples

an irretrievable loss
irretrievable errors in judgment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irretrievable - impossible to recover or repair

Etymology

Formed from the prefix "un-" (not) + "retrievable".
