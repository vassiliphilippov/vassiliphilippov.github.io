Genova

1. PROPN

/ dʒəˈnoʊvə / (AmE), / dʒɛˈnɔːva / (BrE)
The Italian name for Genoa, a major seaport and the capital of Liguria in northwestern Italy.
Italian name for Genoa, Italian seaport city

Examples

The cruise set sail from Genova and traveled along the Mediterranean coast.
Many art lovers visit Genova for its stunning palaces and historic port.
He was born in Genova but has lived abroad for most of his life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Genoa - A city and major seaport in northwestern Italy

Etymology

Italian, from Latin Genua.
