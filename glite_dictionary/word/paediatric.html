paediatric

1. ADJ

/ piːdiˈætrɪk / (AmE), / piːdɪˈatrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the medical care or illnesses of children
relating to children's medical care
british medical

Examples

The hospital has a renowned paediatric department specializing in infants and young children.
She chose to become a paediatric surgeon to help children with serious illnesses.
Many paediatric medicines are flavored to make them more palatable for children.

Synset Examples

pediatric dentist

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pediatric - relating to children's medical care (American spelling)

Etymology

From Greek 'pais, paidos' (child) + 'iatrikos' (medical).
