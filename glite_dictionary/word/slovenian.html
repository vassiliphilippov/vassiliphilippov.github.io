Slovenian

1. NOUN

/ sloʊˈviːniən / (AmE), / sləˈviːniən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Slovenia.
person from Slovenia

Examples

My neighbor is a proud Slovenian who celebrates his heritage every year.
Many Slovenians speak more than one language fluently.
The Slovenian greeted us kindly when we visited Ljubljana.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Slovene - a native or inhabitant of Slovenia

Etymology

From Slovenia, the name of the country, plus the English suffix -an.
