headcanon

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdˌkænən / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌkanən / (BrE)
An idea or interpretation about a fictional world, character, or story that an individual regards as true, though it is not confirmed by official sources.
personal belief about fictional canon

Examples

According to my headcanon, the two characters are secretly siblings.
Her headcanon adds a whole backstory for that minor character.
Fans often debate whether a headcanon could one day become official.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: canon - the officially accepted story elements in a fictional universe

Etymology

Blend of 'head' and 'canon'; 'canon' here means the official storyline accepted in a fictional universe.
