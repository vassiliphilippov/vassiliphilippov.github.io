figured

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪɡjɚd / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡjəd / (BrE)
(of fabric or material) decorated with a pattern, especially one that is woven or printed into the surface.
decorated with a pattern (of fabric, etc.)

Examples

She wore a figured silk scarf with swirling designs.
The curtains were made from a richly figured brocade fabric.
I prefer plain shirts to those with figured patterns.

Synset Examples

my dress is richly figured

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patterned - decorated with a regular design or motif

Etymology

From the verb 'figure', from Old French 'figurer', from Latin 'figurare' (to form, shape).
