Corsican

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɔːrsɪkən / (AmE), / ˈkɔːsɪkən / (BrE)
relating to Corsica, its people, or their culture
of Corsica or its people

Examples

The Corsican language is spoken by many people on the island.
She cooked a delicious Corsican dish using local herbs and cheese.
He admired the rugged beauty of the Corsican landscape during his travels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Corsica - a French island in the Mediterranean Sea

Etymology

From French 'corse', from Latin 'Corsica' (the island in the Mediterranean).
