prenatal

1. ADJ

/ priˈneɪtəl / (AmE), / priːˈneɪtəl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring before birth, especially to the care of the mother and fetus during pregnancy
before birth; during pregnancy
medical

Examples

She attended regular prenatal checkups to ensure her baby's health.
The prenatal period is crucial for fetal development.
Doctors recommend vitamins and proper nutrition during the prenatal stage of pregnancy.

Synset Examples

the prenatal period
antenatal care

Related Terms

  • Antonym: postnatal - occurring or existing after birth
  • Synonym: antenatal - relating to the period before birth (chiefly British)

Etymology

From Latin 'prae-' (before) + 'natus' (born).
