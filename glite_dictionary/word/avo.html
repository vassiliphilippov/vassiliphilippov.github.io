avo

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːvoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɑːvəʊ / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Macao, equal to one hundredth of a pataca.
Macao monetary unit (one hundredth of a pataca)

Examples

The price was listed as 50 patacas and 30 avos on the receipt.
He exchanged dollars for avos while traveling in Macao.
Most coins in Macao are denominated in avos in everyday transactions.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: pataca - The basic monetary unit of Macao

Etymology

From Portuguese 'avo', meaning 'grandfather' or potentially 'fraction' in currency context.
