shelflike

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɛlfˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈʃɛlfˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the characteristics of a shelf, especially in shape or form
resembling a shelf

Examples

The rock formation had a distinct shelflike ledge protruding over the valley.
A shelflike table was built along the wall for extra counter space.
Clouds gathered in a shelflike pattern above the mountain ridge.
