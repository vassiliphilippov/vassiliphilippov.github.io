Leyden

1. PROPN

/ ˈlaɪdən / (AmE), / ˈlaɪdən / (BrE)
a city in the western Netherlands, historically significant as the residence of the Pilgrim Fathers before their journey to America in 1620
city in western Netherlands; historic residence of Pilgrim Fathers

Examples

The historic city of Leyden played a crucial role in the lives of the Pilgrim Fathers before their voyage to America.
On our trip to the Netherlands, we visited Leyden to see its famous old university.
Many tourists are drawn to Leyden for its beautiful canals and rich cultural heritage.
