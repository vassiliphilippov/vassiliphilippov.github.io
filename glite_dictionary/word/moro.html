Moro

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɔːrəʊ / (BrE)
a member of a predominantly Muslim ethnolinguistic group living chiefly in the southern Philippines
a Muslim group in the southern Philippines

Examples

Many Moros have advocated for greater autonomy in Mindanao.
The history of the Moro people is closely tied to the Islamic sultanates of the region.
Cultural traditions among the Moros differ significantly from other Filipino groups.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Moro', meaning 'Moor', historically used by Spanish colonizers to refer to Muslim inhabitants.
