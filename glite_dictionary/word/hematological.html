hematological

1. ADJ

/ ˌhiːməˈtɑːlədʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˈtɒlədʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of blood, blood-forming organs, or blood diseases; pertaining to hematology.
pertaining to blood or hematology
medical

Examples

The patient was referred to a specialist for further hematological evaluation.
Recent advancements in hematological research have improved treatment options for anemia.
Her hematological tests came back within the normal range.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hematologic - variant spelling; same meaning, chiefly American
  • Antonym: nonhematological - not relating to hematology

Etymology

From hematology + -ical; hematology from Greek haima (blood) + -logy (study).
