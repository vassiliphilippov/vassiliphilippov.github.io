unmechanized

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈmɛk.əˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈmiː.kə.naɪzd / (BrE)
(of a process, activity, or system) not operated or carried out by machinery.
not operated by machinery

Examples

Much of the farming in remote regions remains unmechanized to this day.
The workshop uses unmechanized tools and traditional craftsmanship for all their furniture.
Efforts to modernize the unmechanized industries have encountered both technical and cultural challenges.

Synset Examples

production of furniture remained largely unmechanized

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mechanized - operated by machinery
