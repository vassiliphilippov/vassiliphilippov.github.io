footlocker

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌlɑkər / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌlɒkə / (BrE)
a small trunk or chest kept at the foot of a bed, used for storing personal belongings, especially in barracks or dormitories
trunk for storing items at bed's foot

Examples

She kept her letters and photographs in a footlocker under her bed.
Each soldier was assigned a footlocker to store his personal gear.
During summer camp, I decorated my footlocker with stickers from home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trunk - a large, strong container for storage or transport

Etymology

From 'foot' (referring to the foot of a bed) + 'locker' (a chest or container for storage).
