hundred-and-twenty-fifth

1. ADJ

/ ˌhʌndrəd ənd ˌtwɛnti ˈfɪfθ / (AmE), / ˌhʌndrɪd ənd ˌtwɛnti ˈfɪfθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number corresponding to one hundred twenty-five in a sequence; 125th.
ordinal number: 125th

Examples

This is the hundred-and-twenty-fifth anniversary of the university's founding.
She finished in the hundred-and-twenty-fifth position in the marathon.
The hundred-and-twenty-fifth page contains the answer to your question.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one hundred twenty-fifth - alternative spelling of the same ordinal number
  • Degree Lower: hundred-and-twenty-fourth - 124th; the previous ordinal number
  • Degree Higher: hundred-and-twenty-sixth - 126th; the next ordinal number
