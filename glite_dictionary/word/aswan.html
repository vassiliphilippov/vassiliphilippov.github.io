Aswan

1. PROPN

/ æsˈwɑn / (AmE), / æsˈwɑːn / (BrE)
An ancient city on the Nile River in southern Egypt, noted for its historical significance and the nearby Aswan High Dam.
ancient city in Egypt on the Nile

Examples

Many tourists visit Aswan to explore the temples and monuments along the Nile.
The construction of the Aswan High Dam greatly impacted agriculture in Egypt.
Aswan is famous for its beautiful river scenery and significant historical sites.
