beanbag

1. NOUN

/ ˈbinˌbæg / (AmE), / ˈbiːnˌbæɡ / (BrE)
a small cloth bag filled with dried beans or similar material, used as a toy or in games
small cloth bag toy/game piece

Examples

The children played toss with a brightly colored beanbag during recess.
She sewed a homemade beanbag using scrap fabric and lentils.
Each player tried to land their beanbag on the target to score points.

Etymology

From 'bean' + 'bag', originally referring to bags filled with beans.
