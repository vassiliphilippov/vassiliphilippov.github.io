Kabolin

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæbəˌlɪn / (AmE), / ˈkabəlɪn / (BrE)
A trade name for nandrolone, an androgen medication used to treat testosterone deficiency, breast cancer, or osteoporosis.
trade name for an androgen medication
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Kabolin to help manage her osteoporosis.
Athletes are warned not to use substances like Kabolin without medical supervision.
Treatment with Kabolin can improve symptoms of testosterone deficiency in men.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Durabolin - another trade name for nandrolone, the same medication
