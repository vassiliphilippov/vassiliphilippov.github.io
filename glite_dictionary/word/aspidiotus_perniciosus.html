Aspidiotus perniciosus

1. NOUN

/ ˌæspɪˈdioʊtəs pərˈnɪʃəsəs / (AmE), / ˌæspɪˈdiːətəs pəˈnɪʃɪəsəs / (BrE)
A small scale insect native to East Asia but now widespread, known as a pest that damages fruit trees.
scale insect harmful to fruit trees

Examples

Farmers had to spray their orchards to control the spread of Aspidiotus perniciosus.
The presence of Aspidiotus perniciosus can lead to significant crop losses in apple and pear trees.
Researchers are studying ways to naturally combat Aspidiotus perniciosus infestations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: San Jose scale - common name for Aspidiotus perniciosus, a pest of fruit trees

Etymology

From Neo-Latin, genus Aspidiotus plus Latin perniciosus (destructive, harmful).
