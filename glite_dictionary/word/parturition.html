parturition

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɑːrtʃəˈrɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɑːtjuˈrɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of giving birth; childbirth, especially as a physiological process in mammals
the process of giving birth
medical

Examples

The veterinarian assisted with the cow's parturition to ensure the calf was healthy.
Difficulties during parturition can lead to complications for both mother and newborn.
Proper nutrition during pregnancy can ease the process of parturition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: childbirth - the act or process of giving birth to a baby
  • Synonym: labor - the process of childbirth, especially the period of contractions leading up to delivery

Etymology

From Latin 'parturitio', from 'parturire' (to be in labor, to desire to bring forth).
