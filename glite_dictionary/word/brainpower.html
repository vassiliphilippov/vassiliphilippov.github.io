brainpower

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪnˌpaʊər / (AmE), / ˈbreɪnˌpaʊə / (BrE)
the ability to think intelligently; intellectual capacity or mental ability
intellectual capacity; mental ability

Examples

Her brainpower impressed everyone at the science fair.
They hired extra staff to increase the company's brainpower.
Solving that puzzle took all my brainpower.

Synset Examples

he's got plenty of brains but no common sense

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intelligence - the ability to acquire and apply knowledge and skills
  • Synonym: wit - mental sharpness and inventiveness; keen intelligence
