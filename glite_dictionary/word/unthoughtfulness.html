unthoughtfulness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈθɔt.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɔːt.f(ə)l.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of not considering something carefully before acting or speaking
lack of careful consideration

Examples

His unthoughtfulness led to several mistakes in the final report.
She apologized for her unthoughtfulness after realizing her actions upset her friends.
The manager was frustrated by the team's constant unthoughtfulness during critical decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtlessness - the state of not thinking about others or about consequences
  • Antonym: thoughtfulness - the quality of being considerate and careful in thought

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'thoughtful' + '-ness' (noun suffix).
