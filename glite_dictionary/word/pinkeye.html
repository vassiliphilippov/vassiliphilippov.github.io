pinkeye

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪŋkˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋkˌaɪ / (BrE)
a contagious inflammation of the conjunctiva of the eye, causing redness and discharge
contagious eye inflammation
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with pinkeye after noticing redness and swelling in her eye.
Children with pinkeye should stay home from school to avoid spreading the infection.
He complained of itching and discharge, classic symptoms of pinkeye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conjunctivitis - inflammation of the conjunctiva (medical term for pinkeye)
