eighty-five

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪti faɪv / (AmE), / ˈeɪti faɪv / (BrE)
Being five more than eighty; the cardinal number 85.
the number 85

Examples

There were eighty-five students in the lecture hall this morning.
The marathon runner finished in eighty-fifth place out of hundreds of participants.
She celebrated her grandmother’s eighty-fifth birthday with a big family party.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eighty-four - the number 84; one less than eighty-five
  • Degree Higher: eighty-six - the number 86; one more than eighty-five

Etymology

From 'eighty' + 'five', following standard English formation of compound numbers.
