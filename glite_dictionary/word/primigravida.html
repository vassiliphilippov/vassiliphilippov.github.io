primigravida

1. NOUN

/ ˌpraɪ.mɪˈɡræv.ɪ.də / (AmE), / ˌprɪ.mɪˈɡræv.ɪ.də / (BrE)
A woman who is pregnant for the first time.
woman pregnant for the first time
medical

Examples

As a primigravida, she attended prenatal classes to prepare for her first child.
The doctor explained the risks that are more common in primigravida patients.
Being a primigravida, she was closely monitored throughout her pregnancy.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: multigravida - a woman who has been pregnant more than once

Etymology

From Latin 'primi-' (first) + 'gravida' (pregnant woman).
