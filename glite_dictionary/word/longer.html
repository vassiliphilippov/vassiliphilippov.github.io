longer

1. ADV

/ ˈlɔŋɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋə / (BrE)
For a greater amount of time; for more time than something or someone else.
for more time; for a greater duration

Examples

I decided to stay a bit longer even after most guests had left.
She wished she could sleep longer on weekends, but work called early.
Can you wait longer for the results, or do you need them now?

Synset Examples

can I stay bit longer?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: less - for a shorter amount of time
  • Degree Lower: long - for a large amount of time (base form)
  • Degree Higher: longest - for the greatest amount of time (superlative)
