effulgent

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈfʌldʒənt / (AmE), / ɪˈfʌldʒənt / (BrE)
Shining brightly; radiating light or splendour.
shining brightly; radiant

Examples

The effulgent moon bathed the landscape in silvery light.
Her effulgent smile lit up the entire room.
The effulgent sunrise marked the start of a beautiful day.

Synset Examples

the beaming sun
the effulgent daffodils
a radiant sunrise
a refulgent sunset

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiant - shining or glowing brightly
  • Synonym: refulgent - shining brightly; gleaming; radiant

Etymology

From Latin effulgent-, present participle of effulgere 'to shine out'.
