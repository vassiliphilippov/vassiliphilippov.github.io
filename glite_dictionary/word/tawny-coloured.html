tawny-coloured

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɔni ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈtɔːni ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having a brownish-yellow or light brown color; colored like tawny
of a brownish-yellow color

Examples

The lion's tawny-coloured mane shone in the sunlight.
She wore a tawny-coloured scarf that matched her eyes.
Their garden cat is a beautiful tawny-coloured creature that blends into the autumn leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tawny - of a brownish-yellow or light brown color

Etymology

From 'tawny' (from Old French tané, tanned) + 'coloured.'
