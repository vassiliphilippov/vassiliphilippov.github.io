Zhou En-lai

1. PROPN

/ dʒoʊ ˈɛnˌlaɪ / (AmE), / dʒəʊ ˈɛnˌlaɪ / (BrE)
(1898–1976) Chinese revolutionary and communist leader who served as the first Premier of the People's Republic of China from 1949 to 1976.
Chinese Premier and communist leader (1898–1976)

Examples

Zhou En-lai played a crucial role in the founding of the People's Republic of China.
As Premier, Zhou En-lai was known for his diplomatic skills and pragmatic approach to foreign policy.
Many consider Zhou En-lai one of the most influential leaders in modern Chinese history.
