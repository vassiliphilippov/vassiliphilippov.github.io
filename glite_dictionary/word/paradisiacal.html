paradisiacal

1. ADJ

/ ˌpær.ə.dɪˈsaɪ.ə.kəl / (AmE), / ˌpær.ə.dɪˈsaɪ.ə.kəl / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling Paradise; blissfully perfect or idyllic.
relating to or like Paradise

Examples

The island's paradisiacal beaches attract tourists from all over the world.
They described their honeymoon in Bali as a truly paradisiacal experience.
Her garden was so lush and colorful that it seemed almost paradisiacal.

Synset Examples

together in that paradisal place
paradisiacal innocence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heavenly - of heaven; blissful or divine
  • Antonym: earthly - related to the earth; mundane or non-spiritual

Etymology

From Latin paradisus (paradise) + -iacal (forming adjectives).
