Pynchon

/ ˈpɪn.tʃən / (AmE), / ˈpɪn.tʃən / (BrE)
(Thomas Ruggles Pynchon Jr.) United States novelist known for complex, pessimistic works about modern society and technology.
American novelist (born 1937)
