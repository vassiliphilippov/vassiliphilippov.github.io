myoid

1. ADJ

/ ˈmaɪˌɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈmaɪ.ɔɪd / (BrE)
resembling or having the appearance of muscle; muscle-like.
muscle-like; resembling muscle
medical

Examples

The tumor had a myoid appearance under microscopic examination.
Some cells exhibit myoid characteristics, suggesting muscular differentiation.
The myoid tissue was identified during the dissection of the specimen.

Etymology

From Greek myo- (muscle) + -oid (resembling, like).
