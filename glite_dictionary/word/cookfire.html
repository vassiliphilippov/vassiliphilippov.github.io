cookfire

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊkˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʊkˌfaɪə / (BrE)
A fire made and used for cooking food, typically outdoors or in primitive settings.
fire used for cooking

Examples

They gathered around the cookfire to share stories and roast vegetables.
A simple cookfire can be built using dry wood and stones for support.
Smoke from the cookfire drifted up into the clear night sky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: campfire - a fire built outdoors for warmth or cooking
