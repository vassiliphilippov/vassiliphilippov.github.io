ackee

1. NOUN

/ ˈæki / (AmE), / ˈæki / (BrE)
A red, pear-shaped tropical fruit with poisonous seeds; its flesh is edible only when properly ripe, otherwise poisonous.
tropical fruit with poisonous seeds

Examples

Jamaica's national dish is saltfish and ackee.
You must never eat ackee fruit unless it is fully ripe.
The seeds of the ackee are highly toxic and should be discarded.

Etymology

From West African Akan language (Twi) *akyɛ* (fruit).
