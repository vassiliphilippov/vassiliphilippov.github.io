years

1. NOUN

/ jɪrz / (AmE), / jɪəz / (BrE)
A very long time; an extended period that feels notably lengthy.
a long or indefinite period of time

Examples

I haven't seen her in years, so I barely recognized her at the reunion.
It feels like years since we last went on vacation together.
He waited for what seemed like years before receiving the important phone call.

Synset Examples

we've known each other for ages
I haven't been there for years and years

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ages - a very long time; a prolonged period
