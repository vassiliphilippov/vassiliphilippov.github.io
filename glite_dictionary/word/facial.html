facial

1. ADJ

/ ˈfeɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˈfeɪʃəl / (BrE)
of or relating to the face
of the face

Examples

He noticed the facial features that made her stand out in a crowd.
Some people are very expressive with their facial gestures.
The doctor recommended a facial cream for sensitive skin.

Synset Examples

a facial massage
facial hair
facial expression

Related Terms

  • Synonym: facial - a beauty treatment for the face

Etymology

From Medieval Latin facialis, from Latin facies (face, form).

2. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˈfeɪʃəl / (BrE)
A beauty treatment for the face that typically includes cleansing, massage, and the application of cosmetic creams.
beauty treatment for the face

Examples

She treated herself to a relaxing facial at the spa.
Regular facials can help improve the appearance of your skin.
The salon offers several different types of facial treatments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: massage - the act of rubbing and kneading muscles and joints of the body

Etymology

From Latin facialis (of the face), from facies (face).
