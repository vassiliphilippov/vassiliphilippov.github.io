hooray

1. NOUN

/ həˈreɪ / (AmE), / hʊˈreɪ / (BrE)
a shout of joy, encouragement, or triumph, often used as a cheer at celebrations or to express approval.
cheer of joy or triumph

Examples

The crowd erupted in a loud hooray when their team won the championship.
Children shouted hooray as the parade floats passed by.
With a final hooray, the group celebrated the successful end of the project.

Synset Examples

let's give the team a big hurrah

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hurrah - a shout of joy or approval; similar cheer

Etymology

Probably an alteration of 'hurrah', itself of uncertain origin but perhaps imitative or from German 'hurra'.
