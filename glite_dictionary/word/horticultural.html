horticultural

1. ADJ

/ ˌhɔːr.tɪˈkʌl.tʃər.əl / (AmE), / ˌhɔː.tɪˈkʌl.tʃər.əl / (BrE)
relating to the cultivation of gardens or the science and art of growing fruits, vegetables, flowers, or ornamental plants
relating to plant cultivation or gardening

Examples

She works at the university's horticultural research center focusing on new plant varieties.
The city sponsored a horticultural exhibition featuring rare and exotic plants.
His horticultural skills transformed the barren plot into a thriving garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agricultural - relating to farming and the cultivation of land

Etymology

From Latin hortus (garden) + cultura (cultivation).
