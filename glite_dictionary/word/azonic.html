azonic

1. ADJ

/ əˈzoʊnɪk / (AmE), / əˈzɒnɪk / (BrE)
Not restricted to any particular zone or region; not confined to a specific area.
not limited to a particular region or zone

Examples

The species is considered azonic because it can be found in diverse habitats across the globe.
That mineral's occurrence is azonic, appearing in various geographic regions without limitation.
Unlike endemic species, azonic forms are not confined by ecological borders.

Etymology

From Greek 'a-' (not) + 'zōnē' (belt, zone) + '-ic'.
