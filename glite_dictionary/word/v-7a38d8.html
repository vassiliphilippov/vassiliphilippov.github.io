v

1. NOUN

/ viː / (AmE), / viː / (BrE)
the 22nd letter of the Roman alphabet, representing the sound /v/ as in 'voice'.
the letter V; 22nd in the alphabet

Examples

My name begins with a v in the middle.
Please write the letter v clearly on your paper.
The symbol v is also used in mathematics and music.

Etymology

From Latin letter V, used for both 'u' and 'v' sounds; differentiated in English in the 17th century.
