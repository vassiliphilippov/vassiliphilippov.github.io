sneerer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnɪrər / (AmE), / ˈsnɪərə / (BrE)
a person who expresses contempt or disdain through mocking remarks or facial expressions
person who sneers; expresses contempt

Examples

It was clear from his expression that he was a habitual sneerer who rarely offered kind words.
The sneerer sat in the back row, smirking at every suggestion made during the meeting.
She tried to ignore the sneerer and focus on her presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sneer - to show contempt or scorn, especially by facial expression

Etymology

From 'sneer' (to express scorn), suffix '-er' indicating a person who does an action.
