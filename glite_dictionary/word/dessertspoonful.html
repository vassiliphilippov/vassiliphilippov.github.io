dessertspoonful

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈzɝtˌspunˌfʊl / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːtˌspuːnfʊl / (BrE)
An amount equal to what a dessert spoon can hold, used as a measure in cooking or serving.
amount held by a dessert spoon

Examples

Add one dessertspoonful of sugar to the mixture and stir well.
The recipe called for two dessertspoonfuls of olive oil.
He added a single dessertspoonful of honey to his tea.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: teaspoonful - an amount held by a teaspoon
  • Degree Higher: tablespoonful - an amount held by a tablespoon
