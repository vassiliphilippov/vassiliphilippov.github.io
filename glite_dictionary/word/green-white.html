green-white

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡrin ˌwaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡriːn ˌwaɪt / (BrE)
(of flowers) white with a tinge or shade of green.
white with a green tinge (esp. of flowers)

Examples

The orchid has beautiful green-white petals that look almost translucent in the sunlight.
Several species produce small green-white flowers in early spring.
The florist recommended a bouquet with delicate green-white roses for the arrangement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greenish - having a slight green hue
