Isabella Stewart Gardner

1. PROPN

/ ɪzəˈbɛlə ˈstuːərt ˈɡɑrnər / (AmE), / ɪzəˈbɛlə ˈstjuːət ˈɡɑːdnə / (BrE)
(1840–1924) An American art collector and philanthropist, founder of the Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum in Boston.
American art collector and museum founder

Examples

The art collection of Isabella Stewart Gardner remains one of Boston’s premier cultural treasures.
In 1903, Isabella Stewart Gardner opened her museum to the public, showcasing works from around the world.
Many artists and intellectuals of her time admired Isabella Stewart Gardner for her support of the arts.
