Dane

1. NOUN

/ deɪn / (AmE), / deɪn / (BrE)
A native or citizen of Denmark.
person from Denmark

Examples

Her grandfather was a proud Dane who often spoke of his homeland.
Many Danes celebrate Midsummer's Eve with bonfires and music.
The Dane was known for his hospitality and warmth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Norwegian - a native or inhabitant of Norway
  • Synonym: Swede - a native or inhabitant of Sweden

Etymology

From Old English Dene, from Old Norse Danir, meaning 'the Danes'.
