extended

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈstɛndɪd / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛndɪd / (BrE)
made longer or drawn out in space; having been lengthened or stretched.
made longer in space; lengthened

Examples

They built an extended patio that stretched across the whole backyard.
Her arm reached out in an extended position toward the shelf.
