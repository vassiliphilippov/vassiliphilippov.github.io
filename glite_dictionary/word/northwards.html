northwards

1. ADV

/ ˈnɔrθwɚdz / (AmE), / ˈnɔːθwədz / (BrE)
towards or in the direction of the north
towards the north

Examples

The birds migrate northwards in the spring to breed.
The expedition set out northwards across the tundra.
As we drove northwards, the weather became noticeably colder.

Synset Examples

they earn more up north
Let's go north!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: northward - towards the north

Etymology

Formed from 'north' + 'wards', with '-wards' denoting direction.
