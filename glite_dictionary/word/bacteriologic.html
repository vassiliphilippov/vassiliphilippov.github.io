bacteriologic

1. ADJ

/ bækˌtɪriəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / bækˌtɪəriəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the science of bacteriology or the study of bacteria
relating to bacteriology
medical

Examples

The laboratory performed bacteriologic tests to identify the infectious agent.
She specializes in bacteriologic research focused on antibiotic resistance.
Accurate bacteriologic methods are essential in diagnosing bacterial diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bacteriological - relating to bacteriology (variant form)

Etymology

From bacteriology, itself from French 'bactériologie', based on 'bacteria' + Greek '-logia' (study of).
