Morgan

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔːrɡən / (AmE), / ˈmɔːɡən / (BrE)
an American breed of small, compact, strong saddle horses, known for their endurance and versatility.
breed of American saddle horse

Examples

Her Morgan won first prize in the show for best gaited horse.
The Morgan is prized for its strength and friendly temperament.
He has owned several Morgans over the years for both riding and driving.

Etymology

Named after Justin Morgan (1747–1798), the breeder whose horse became the foundation sire of the breed.
