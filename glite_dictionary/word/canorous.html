canorous

1. ADJ

/ kəˈnɔrəs / (AmE), / kəˈnɔːrəs / (BrE)
Richly melodious; having a full and pleasant sound.
richly melodious; pleasant-sounding

Examples

The musician’s canorous voice filled the concert hall, captivating everyone in the audience.
As the wind blew through the trees, it created a canorous rustling that was soothing to hear.
Their choir was known for its canorous harmonies that resonated beautifully during performances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: melodious - having a pleasant musical sound

Etymology

From Latin 'canorus' (melodious), from 'canere' (to sing).
