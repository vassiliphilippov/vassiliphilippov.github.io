passionateness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæʃənətnəs / (AmE), / ˈpæʃənətnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having strong feelings or emotions; the state of being passionate
the quality of being passionate

Examples

Her passionateness inspired everyone in the room to take action.
The passionateness of his speech moved the audience to tears.
In her writing, there is a striking passionateness that sets her apart from others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passion - a strong emotion or feeling
  • Antonym: impassiveness - lack of emotion or passion

Etymology

From 'passionate' + '-ness', modeled after the structure of 'passionateness' in English.
