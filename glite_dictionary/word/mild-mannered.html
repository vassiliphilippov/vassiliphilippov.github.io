mild-mannered

1. ADJ

/ ˈmaɪld ˈmænərd / (AmE), / ˈmaɪld ˈmænəd / (BrE)
Having a gentle, calm, or unassertive manner.
gentle or unassertive in manner

Examples

Everyone described him as a mild-mannered gentleman, always polite and calm under pressure.
Despite her mild-mannered appearance, she could be surprisingly determined when necessary.
The mild-mannered teacher rarely raised his voice, even in difficult situations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gentle - having a mild and kind nature
  • Antonym: aggressive - behaving in a forceful or threatening way
