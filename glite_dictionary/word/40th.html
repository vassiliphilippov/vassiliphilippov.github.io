40th

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrtiəθ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtiɪθ / (BrE)
being number forty in a sequence; the ordinal form of forty.
ordinal number for forty

Examples

She celebrated her 40th birthday with friends and family.
The school is holding its 40th annual science fair this year.
He finished in 40th place in the marathon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fortieth - spelled word form of 40th (ordinal for forty)
  • Degree Lower: 39th - ordinal number for thirty-nine
  • Degree Higher: 41st - ordinal number for forty-one
