planchette

1. NOUN

/ plænˈʃɛt / (AmE), / plænˈʃɛt / (BrE)
a small, usually triangular board mounted on casters, used in séances for spelling out messages by supposedly supernatural means, typically by being lightly touched with the fingertips
triangular board used in séances

Examples

The group gathered around the Ouija board, gently resting their fingers on the planchette.
She claimed the planchette moved on its own, spelling out a mysterious message.
During the séance, the planchette slid across the board, pointing to different letters.

Etymology

From French, diminutive of 'planche' meaning 'board'.
