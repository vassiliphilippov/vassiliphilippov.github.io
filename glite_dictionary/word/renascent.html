renascent

1. ADJ

/ riˈnæsənt / (AmE), / rɪˈnæsənt / (BrE)
becoming active or vigorous again; experiencing renewal or revival.
experiencing renewal or revival

Examples

After decades of decline, the city's renascent arts scene is attracting attention worldwide.
The movement's renascent energy inspired new followers across the country.
We are witnessing a renascent interest in traditional crafts and techniques.

Synset Examples

resurgent nationalism

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resurgent - experiencing a renewed rise or growth

Etymology

From Latin renascent-, past participle of renasci (to be born again).
