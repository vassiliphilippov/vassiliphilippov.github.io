cloudless

1. ADJ

/ ˈklaʊd.ləs / (AmE), / ˈklaʊd.ləs / (BrE)
(of the sky) without any clouds; completely clear.
without clouds (sky)

Examples

We picnicked under a cloudless sky all afternoon.
The sunrise was beautiful on that cloudless morning.
A cloudless night made the stars easy to see.

Synset Examples

under a cloudless sky

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clear - (of the sky) free from clouds
