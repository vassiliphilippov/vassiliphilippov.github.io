alimentary

1. ADJ

/ ˌælɪˈmɛntəri / (AmE), / ˌælɪˈmɛntəri / (BrE)
relating to nourishment or the process of providing or receiving nutrition.
relating to nourishment

Examples

The doctor explained the importance of the alimentary canal in digestion.
Some plants possess special alimentary tissues to store nutrients.
The research focuses on alimentary supplements for patients recovering from surgery.

Synset Examples

good nourishing stew

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutritional - relating to nutrition or nourishment

Etymology

From Latin alimentarius, from alimentum (nourishment).
