west by south

1. NOUN

/ wɛst baɪ saʊθ / (AmE), / wɛst baɪ saʊθ / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point (11°15′) south of due west.
compass point one point south of west

Examples

Ships traveling west by south will skirt the southern coastline.
The compass needle pointed west by south as they navigated through the fog.
The storm was moving west by south at an alarming speed.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: west - the direction toward the point of the horizon where the sun sets
  • Degree Lower: west-southwest - the compass point between west by south and southwest
