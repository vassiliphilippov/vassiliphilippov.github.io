puerpera

1. NOUN

/ pjʊˈɜrpərə / (AmE), / pjʊəˈpɜːpərə / (BrE)
a woman who has just given birth or is in the immediate period after childbirth
woman after childbirth
medical

Examples

After delivery, the puerpera was carefully monitored for any signs of infection.
A healthy diet is essential for the well-being of the puerpera and her newborn.
The nurse checked the vital signs of the puerpera every four hours.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parturient - a woman about to give birth or in labor

Etymology

From Latin 'puerpera', from 'puer' (child) + 'parere' (to bring forth).
