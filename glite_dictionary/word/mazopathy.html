mazopathy

1. NOUN

/ ˌmeɪ.zəˈpæθ.i / (AmE), / ˌmæz.əˈpæθ.ɪ / (BrE)
any disease or pathological condition of the breast.
disease of the breast
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with a rare form of mazopathy.
Researchers are studying genetic factors that may contribute to mazopathy.
Symptoms of mazopathy can include pain, lumps, or changes in breast tissue.

Etymology

From Greek 'mastos' (breast) + '-pathy' (disease).
