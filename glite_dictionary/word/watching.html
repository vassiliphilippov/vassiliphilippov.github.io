watching

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of observing something carefully or attentively over a period of time
the act of observing

Examples

Bird-watching is a popular hobby among nature lovers.
After hours of watching, the guards finally spotted movement outside the fence.
The teacher spent the afternoon watching the children play in the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: observation - the action or process of closely observing or monitoring something
