Tel Aviv-Jaffa

1. PROPN

/ tɛl əˈviv ˈjɑːfə / (AmE), / tɛl əˈviːv ˈjæfə / (BrE)
A city in western Israel on the Mediterranean, known as the country's largest city and financial center.
largest city and financial center of Israel

Examples

Many international companies have offices in Tel Aviv-Jaffa due to its strong business environment.
I visited Tel Aviv-Jaffa and enjoyed its vibrant nightlife and beautiful beaches.
As Israel's largest metropolis, Tel Aviv-Jaffa is famous for its cultural diversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jerusalem - the capital city of Israel

Etymology

Formed from the merger of Tel Aviv (from Hebrew, 'Hill of Spring') and Jaffa (ancient port city) in 1950.
