propertyless

1. ADJ

/ ˈprɑːpərtɪləs / (AmE), / ˈprɒpətɪləs / (BrE)
not owning property; lacking material possessions or wealth
not owning property or wealth

Examples

In the early 20th century, many propertyless workers demanded land reforms.
The propertyless poor often faced significant social and economic challenges.
She fought for the rights of the propertyless in her community.

Synset Examples

party of the propertyless proletariat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impoverished - lacking money or financial resources
  • Antonym: wealthy - having a great deal of money, resources, or property

Etymology

Formed from 'property' + suffix '-less' (meaning 'without').
