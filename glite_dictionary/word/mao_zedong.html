Mao Zedong

1. PROPN

/ maʊ zəˈdʊŋ / (AmE), / maʊ zəˈdʊŋ / (BrE)
(1893–1976) Chinese communist leader and founding father of the People's Republic of China.
Chinese communist leader, 1893–1976

Examples

Mao Zedong led the Chinese Communist Party to victory in the Chinese Civil War.
Many historians debate the impact of Mao Zedong's policies on modern China.
The portrait of Mao Zedong still hangs prominently in Tiananmen Square.

Etymology

Transliteration from Modern Standard Chinese 毛泽东 (Máo Zédōng).
