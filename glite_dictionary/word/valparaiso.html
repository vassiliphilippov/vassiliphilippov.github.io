Valparaiso

1. PROPN

/ ˌvælpəˈraɪsoʊ / (AmE), / ˌvalpəˈraɪsəʊ / (BrE)
the chief port and the second largest city of Chile, located on a wide harbor along the central coast
major port city in central Chile

Examples

Many tourists visit Valparaiso for its vibrant street art and steep hills.
The shipping industry plays a vital role in Valparaiso's economy.
Pablo Neruda lived in Valparaiso for several years during his career.
