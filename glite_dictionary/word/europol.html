Europol

1. PROPN

/ ˈjʊrəˌpɑl / (AmE), / ˈjʊərəˌpɒl / (BrE)
the law enforcement agency of the European Union, responsible for supporting cooperation and coordination among EU member states' police forces to combat serious international crime and terrorism
EU law enforcement agency

Examples

Europol coordinated a major operation resulting in the arrest of several suspects across Europe.
According to Europol, cybercrime incidents have significantly increased in recent years.
National police forces often collaborate with Europol in investigations involving cross-border crime.

Etymology

Formed from 'Europe' and 'police'.
