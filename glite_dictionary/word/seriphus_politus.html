Seriphus politus

1. NOUN

/ səˈraɪfəs poʊˈlaɪtəs / (AmE), / sɛˈrɪfəs pɒˈliːtəs / (BrE)
A species of silvery and bluish drumfish found in shallow coastal waters of California, also known as queenfish.
a silvery-blue drumfish of California

Examples

Fishermen along the pier were hoping to catch Seriphus politus during the evening high tide.
The silvery scales of Seriphus politus glinted in the clear California water.
Biologists are studying the population decline of Seriphus politus off the Pacific coast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: queenfish - common name for Seriphus politus, a coastal drumfish
