drafter

1. NOUN

/ ˈdræftər / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːftə / (BrE)
a person who prepares or writes drafts, such as preliminary versions of documents, plans, or proposals.
person who writes drafts

Examples

The drafter carefully revised the document before submitting it to his supervisor.
As the main drafter of the proposal, she ensured all requirements were addressed.
Our company is looking for a skilled drafter to assist in preparing legal agreements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: draftsman - a person who draws sketches or plans, or prepares drafts of documents

Etymology

From draft + -er, meaning one who drafts.
