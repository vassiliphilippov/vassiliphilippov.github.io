Trotskyite

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɑtskiˌaɪt / (AmE), / ˈtrɒtskiˌaɪt / (BrE)
A supporter or follower of Trotsky's theory that socialism should be established worldwide through continuous revolution.
supporter of Trotsky's theory of world revolution

Examples

The party was accused of harboring Trotskyite elements who sought to push for worldwide revolution.
She was labeled a Trotskyite after publishing her criticism of Stalinist policies.
During the meeting, a group of young Trotskyites presented their vision for international socialism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Trotskyist - a supporter of Trotsky's theories; variant form/spelling

Etymology

From the name of Leon Trotsky (1879–1940), Russian revolutionary.
