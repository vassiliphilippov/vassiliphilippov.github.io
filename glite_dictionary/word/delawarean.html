Delawarean

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɛləˈwɛriən / (AmE), / ˌdɛləˈwɛːrɪən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the state of Delaware, USA
person from Delaware, USA

Examples

She is a proud Delawarean who volunteers in her community every week.
Many Delawareans enjoy the beautiful beaches along the coast.
As a Delawarean, he follows local politics closely and participates in state elections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Delaware - a state in the eastern United States

Etymology

From Delaware (U.S. state) + -an (denoting association or origin).
