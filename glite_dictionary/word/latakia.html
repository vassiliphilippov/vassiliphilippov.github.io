2. NOUN

/ ləˈteɪkiə / (AmE), / læˈtækɪə / (BrE)
An aromatic, smoky-flavored tobacco originally produced in Syria and Turkey, often used in pipe tobacco blends.
type of aromatic Turkish tobacco

Examples

Blenders often include Latakia to give their pipe tobaccos a distinctive smoky aroma.
He prefers tobacco with a generous amount of Latakia for its bold, rich flavor.
The unique curing process gives Latakia its characteristic taste and scent.

Etymology

Named after the port city of Latakia in Syria, where the tobacco was originally exported.
