semantic

1. ADJ

/ səˈmæntɪk / (AmE), / sɪˈmantɪk / (BrE)
Relating to meaning or the study of meaning, especially in language and logic.
relating to meaning or the study of meaning

Examples

The researcher conducted a semantic analysis of the text to understand its deeper meaning.
There are important semantic differences between these two words.
Her thesis explored the semantic development of language in children.

Synset Examples

semantic analysis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: semantical - alternative form of "semantic"
  • Antonym: syntactic - relating to the arrangement of words and phrases

Etymology

From French sémantique, from Greek sēmantikos (significant, having meaning), from sēma (sign).
