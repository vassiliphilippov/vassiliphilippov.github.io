short-tempered

1. ADJ

/ ʃɔrt ˈtɛmpɚd / (AmE), / ʃɔːt ˈtɛmpəd / (BrE)
Quick to become angry or easily irritated.
easily angered

Examples

The coach was known for being short-tempered when his team made mistakes.
My little brother is short-tempered and often shouts when things don’t go his way.
She grew more short-tempered as the stressful week went on.

Synset Examples

a hotheaded commander

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hotheaded - quick-tempered; easily angered
  • Antonym: even-tempered - consistently calm; not easily angered
