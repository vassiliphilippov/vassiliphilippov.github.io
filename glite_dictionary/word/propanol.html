propanol

1. NOUN

/ ˈproʊ.pəˌnɒl / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.pənɒl / (BrE)
a clear, colorless, volatile alcohol (C3H7OH) used as a solvent and antiseptic
a volatile alcohol used as solvent and antiseptic
medical

Examples

Laboratory technicians use propanol as a solvent in chemical analysis.
The wound was cleaned with propanol before being bandaged.
Storage of propanol requires proper ventilation due to its volatility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: isopropanol - another name for isopropyl alcohol, a structural isomer of propanol

Etymology

From propane (by adding '-ol' indicating an alcohol).
