apomictical

1. ADJ

/ ˌæp.əˈmɪk.tɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌap.əˈmɪk.tɪ.kəl / (BrE)
of or relating to a plant that reproduces by apomixis (asexual reproduction without fertilization)
relating to asexual plant reproduction
medical

Examples

Botanists studied the apomictical characteristics of the new grass species.
Some dandelions are apomictical, reproducing without sexual fertilization.
The apomictical nature of these plants makes them valuable for genetic research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apomictic - relating to or reproducing by apomixis

Etymology

From 'apomixis' (Greek apo- 'away from' + mixis 'mixing') plus the adjectival suffix '-ical'.
