hematopoietic

1. ADJ

/ ˌhiːmətoʊpaɪˈɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɛmətəʊpɔɪˈɛtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or involved in the formation of blood or blood cells.
relating to blood cell formation
medical

Examples

Bone marrow contains hematopoietic stem cells that give rise to all types of blood cells.
Researchers study hematopoietic disorders to better understand diseases like anemia and leukemia.
A hematopoietic growth factor stimulates the production of white blood cells after chemotherapy.

Synset Examples

hemopoietic stem cells in bone marrow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hemopoietic - Relating to the formation of blood or blood cells; alternate spelling

Etymology

From Greek 'haima' (blood) + 'poiein' (to make).
