unlipped

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlɪpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪpt / (BrE)
Having no lip or lips; lacking a projecting edge or rim that forms a lip.
without a lip or lips

Examples

The ancient jug was unlipped, making it difficult to pour liquids without spilling.
She noted that the flower was unlipped, differing from other species with prominent lips.
Certain musical instruments are designed with an unlipped mouthpiece for a different tonal quality.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lipped - having a lip or lips
