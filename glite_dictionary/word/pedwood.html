pedwood

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛd.wʊd / (AmE), / ˈpɛd.wʊd / (BrE)
A European deciduous shrub that turns red in autumn and has dull white flowers.
European shrub with red autumn leaves

Examples

The garden featured a beautiful pedwood, which turned crimson as autumn approached.
We planted pedwoods along the hedge for their vibrant fall color.
In spring, the pedwood produces clusters of dull white blossoms.
