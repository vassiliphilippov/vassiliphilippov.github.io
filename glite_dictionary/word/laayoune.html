Laayoune

1. PROPN

/ laˈjuːn / (AmE), / laˈjuːn / (BrE)
A town in western Sahara, Morocco, near the Atlantic coast, serving as the administrative center of Western Sahara.
town in Morocco, near the Atlantic coast

Examples

The city of Laayoune is known for its unique blend of cultures and rich history.
Many international delegations have visited Laayoune to discuss the Western Sahara conflict.
Flights to Laayoune are available from several major Moroccan cities.
