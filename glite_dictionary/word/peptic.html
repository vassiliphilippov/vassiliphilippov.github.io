peptic

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛptɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɛptɪk / (BrE)
relating to or promoting the process of digestion, especially by means of digestive enzymes or juices.
relating to digestion
medical

Examples

He was diagnosed with a peptic ulcer that required careful treatment.
The stomach secretes peptic juices to help break down food.
Certain medications can interfere with peptic processes in the digestive system.

Synset Examples

peptic juices

Related Terms

  • Synonym: digestive - relating to or aiding digestion

Etymology

From Greek *peptikos* (able to digest), from *peptos* (digested).
