campanulated

1. ADJ

/ kæmˈpæn.jə.leɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / kamˈpan.jʊ.leɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
Having a shape like a bell; bell-shaped, especially used of flowers.
shaped like a bell

Examples

The campanulated blossoms nodded gently in the morning breeze.
Many wildflowers in this region are campanulated, resembling tiny bells hanging from slender stems.
A botanist noted the campanulated form of the bluebell flower during her field study.

Synset Examples

campanulate flowers of the genus Campanula

Related Terms

  • Synonym: campanulate - having a bell-shaped form, especially in botany

Etymology

From Late Latin campanulatus (bell-shaped), from campana (bell).
