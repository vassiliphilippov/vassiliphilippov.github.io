squareness

1. NOUN

/ ˈskwɛrnəs / (AmE), / ˈskweənɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being square in shape or form
quality of being square in shape

Examples

The squareness of the room made it easy to arrange the furniture neatly.
Carpenters often use a tool to check the squareness of a corner.
The logo's squareness gives it a modern and balanced appearance.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: rectangularity - the quality of being rectangular in shape
  • Antonym: roundness - the quality of being circular or round in shape

Etymology

From 'square' + '-ness', forming a noun indicating the state or condition of being square.
