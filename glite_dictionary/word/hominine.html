hominine

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑːməˌnaɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɒmɪˌniːn / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of humans or members of the subfamily Homininae.
relating to humans or Homininae

Examples

The fossil displays several hominine features, such as upright posture and reduced canines.
Researchers debated whether the ancient bones truly belonged to a hominine species.
Unlike earlier apes, hominine ancestors began to walk habitually on two legs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: human - relating to or characteristic of people or humankind

Etymology

From Latin 'homin-' (man) + -ine, used in scientific classification.
