noodler

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːdlɚ / (AmE), / ˈnuːdlə / (BrE)
a person who experiments playfully, especially with cooking or ideas
person who experiments playfully

Examples

He's a noodler, always trying new ingredients in his dishes.
Every kitchen needs a noodler to keep things interesting.
Her reputation as a noodler grew as she experimented with fusion cuisine.

Synset Examples

He's a true noodler, always tinkering with his recipes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tinkerer - a person who enjoys repairing or improving things, especially as a hobby
  • Synonym: experimenter - a person who tries out new ideas or methods

Etymology

Formed from 'noodle' (to fiddle with or experiment idly) + '-er'.
