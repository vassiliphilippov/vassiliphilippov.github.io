Philippine

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪlɪˌpin / (AmE), / ˈfɪlɪˌpiːn / (BrE)
of or relating to the Philippines, its people, or their customs.
relating to the Philippines or its people

Examples

The Philippine archipelago is known for its beautiful beaches and diverse wildlife.
She is studying traditional Philippine dance for her cultural project.
The Philippine flag was raised during the Independence Day celebration.

Synset Examples

the Philippine President
our Filipino cook

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Filipino - relating to the people or culture of the Philippines

Etymology

From Spanish *Filipinas*, named for King Philip II of Spain.
