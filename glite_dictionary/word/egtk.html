EGTK

1. NOUN

/ i dʒi ti keɪ / (AmE), / iː dʒiː tiː keɪ / (BrE)
a Bolivian terrorist group, considered the remnants of insurgents trained by Che Guevara, that targets small and unprotected sites such as power pylons, pipelines, or government buildings.
Bolivian terrorist group, Che Guevara-linked

Examples

The Bolivian government increased security after the EGTK claimed responsibility for the attack on the power station.
News reports identified the EGTK as the group behind the recent sabotage of oil pipelines.
Authorities believe that members of the EGTK are remnants of guerrilla fighters trained decades ago.

Etymology

Acronym for Ejército Guerrillero Tupac Katari, Spanish for 'Tupac Katari Guerrilla Army'.
