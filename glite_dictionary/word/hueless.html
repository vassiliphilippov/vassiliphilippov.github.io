hueless

1. ADJ

/ ˈhjuːlɪs / (AmE), / ˈhjuːlɪs / (BrE)
entirely lacking in hue or color; having no saturation or colorfulness
lacking any hue or color

Examples

The landscape appeared hueless under the thick blanket of fog.
She painted a hueless world to convey a sense of emptiness.
After the storm, the hueless sky gave the city a somber mood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colorless - without color; lacking any distinct color or hue
  • Antonym: chromatic - relating to or produced by color, having a hue

Etymology

From 'hue' (color or shade) + '-less' (without).
