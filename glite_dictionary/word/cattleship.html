cattleship

1. NOUN

/ ˈkætəlˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈkætəlʃɪp / (BrE)
a cargo ship designed or used for transporting cattle or other livestock
ship for transporting livestock

Examples

The port was busy as a cattleship docked to unload hundreds of cows.
Strict regulations ensure animal welfare on board a cattleship.
The aging cattleship was retired due to updated maritime safety standards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: livestock carrier - a ship designed to carry live animals as cargo

Etymology

From 'cattle' + 'ship'.
