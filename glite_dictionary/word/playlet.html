playlet

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪ.lət / (AmE), / ˈpleɪ.lɪt / (BrE)
a short play, especially one that is brief and often performed as part of a larger program
a short play

Examples

The drama club staged a playlet about friendship as the opening act.
Her first script was a playlet performed at a community festival.
Each student wrote a playlet to demonstrate what they had learned about dialogue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skit - a short, humorous play or performance

Etymology

Formed from 'play' + diminutive suffix '-let'.
