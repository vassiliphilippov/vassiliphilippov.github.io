waterproofed

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɔt̬ɚˌpruːft / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌpruːft / (BrE)
treated so as not to permit the passage of water; made impermeable to water.
made impermeable to water

Examples

My jacket is waterproofed to keep me dry in heavy rain.
The tent has been waterproofed to withstand wet weather conditions.
These waterproofed boots are perfect for hiking through streams and puddles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waterproof - impermeable to water
