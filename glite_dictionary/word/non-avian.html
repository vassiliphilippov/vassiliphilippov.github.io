non-avian

1. ADJ

/ nɑn ˈeɪ.vi.ən / (AmE), / nɒn ˈeɪ.vi.ən / (BrE)
Not pertaining to or characteristic of birds; not related to birds.
not related to birds

Examples

Many scientists study non-avian dinosaurs to better understand evolution.
Recent fossils suggest that some non-avian reptiles shared features with birds.
The research focused on the anatomy of non-avian species in prehistoric ecosystems.

Synset Examples

We argue that our experimental approach, although not perfect, was effective in displacing the CoM and recreating locomotor patterns expected in non-avian theropods.
but the results reported here show that important aspects of non-avian theropod locomotion can be experimentally recreated in modern birds
In our experimental setup, we attempted to more closely mimic non-avian theropod tail morphology

Related Terms

  • Antonym: avian - pertaining to birds

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) and 'avian' (relating to birds, from Latin 'avis' meaning 'bird').
