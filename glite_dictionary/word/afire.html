afire

1. ADJ

/ əˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / əˈfaɪə / (BrE)
burning; on fire or set alight by fire or flame
burning; on fire

Examples

The old barn was suddenly afire after the lightning struck the roof.
Her imagination was afire with new ideas after reading the book.
The crowd watched as the fireworks set the night sky afire.

Synset Examples

forests set ablaze (or afire) by lightning
even the car's tires were aflame
a night aflare with fireworks
candles alight on the tables
houses on fire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ablaze - burning fiercely; on fire
  • Synonym: alight - on fire; burning
  • Synonym: aflame - burning; in flames

Etymology

From Old English 'on fȳre' (on fire).
