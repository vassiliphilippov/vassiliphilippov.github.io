shlemiel

1. NOUN

/ ʃləˈmiːl / (AmE), / ʃləˈmiːl / (BrE)
A clumsy or unlucky person who habitually makes mistakes or bungles things.
habitual bungler; clumsy, inept person

Examples

No matter what he tries, the shlemiel always manages to trip over his own feet.
Everyone laughed as the shlemiel spilled soup all over the table for the third time.
She's such a shlemiel that it's a wonder she gets through the day without a disaster.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bungler - a person who habitually makes mistakes
  • Synonym: klutz - a clumsy person; oaf

Etymology

From Yiddish *shlimazl* (an unlucky person), itself from German *schlim* (bad, evil) + *Mazel* (luck).
