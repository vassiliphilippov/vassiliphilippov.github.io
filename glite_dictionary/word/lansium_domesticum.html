Lansium domesticum

1. NOUN

/ ˌlæn.si.əm doʊˈmɛs.tɪ.kəm / (AmE), / ˌlan.si.əm dəʊˈmes.tɪ.kəm / (BrE)
A tropical tree native to Southeast Asia, bearing edible yellow berries known as langsat.
tropical fruit tree, also called langsat

Examples

The Lansium domesticum tree is widely cultivated for its sweet and tangy fruit.
Farmers in Indonesia export large quantities of Lansium domesticum every year.
I tasted Lansium domesticum for the first time during my trip to Malaysia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: langsat - edible fruit of Lansium domesticum

Etymology

From the genus name Lansium and species epithet domesticum, both from Latin.
