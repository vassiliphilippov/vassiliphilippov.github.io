medically

1. ADV

/ ˈmɛdɪkli / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to medicine or medical practice.
in a way relating to medicine

Examples

All of the patients are being medically monitored during the trial.
The building was declared medically safe after thorough inspection.
He is medically qualified to perform surgeries.

Synset Examples

medically trained nurses
medically correct treatment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clinically - in a way that relates to clinical practice or observation

Etymology

Formed from 'medical' + '-ly', from Latin 'medicus' (physician).
