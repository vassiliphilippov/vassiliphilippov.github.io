sulfuretted

1. ADJ

/ sʌlˈfjʊrɛtɪd / (AmE), / sʌlˈfjʊərɛtɪd / (BrE)
treated or impregnated with sulfur, especially referring to chemical compounds containing sulfur.
treated with or containing sulfur
archaic medical

Examples

The laboratory handled several sulfuretted compounds during the experiment.
Miners once reported a distinct smell from sulfuretted hydrogen in the tunnels.
Early chemists documented the effects of sulfuretted acid on different metals.

Synset Examples

sulfuretted hydrogen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sulfurized - treated or combined with sulfur

Etymology

From 'sulfur' + '-etted', an archaic English past participle ending; now largely replaced by 'sulfurized' or 'sulphurated'.
