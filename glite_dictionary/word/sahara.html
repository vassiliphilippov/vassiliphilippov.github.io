Sahara

1. PROPN

/ səˈhɑːrə / (AmE), / səˈhɑːrə / (BrE)
A vast desert region covering much of northern Africa, known as the world's largest hot desert.
the world's largest desert in northern Africa

Examples

The Sahara stretches across several countries, creating a challenging environment for travel and habitation.
Camels are often called the 'ships of the Sahara' because of their ability to cross the desert's vast sands.
Many explorers have tried to map the Sahara and study its unique climate.

Etymology

From Arabic ṣaḥrāʾ (desert).
