nonachiever

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɑn.əˈtʃi.vər / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.əˈtʃiː.və / (BrE)
a person, especially a student, who does not perform as well as expected based on their abilities or potential
a person who fails to achieve expected success

Examples

The teacher was concerned that Tom might be a nonachiever despite his high intelligence.
Some schools have programs to help nonachievers reach their full potential.
She was labeled a nonachiever in high school, but later became very successful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: underachiever - a person who performs below their potential or expectations
  • Antonym: overachiever - a person who performs better than expected based on their ability

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'non-' (not) + 'achiever'.
