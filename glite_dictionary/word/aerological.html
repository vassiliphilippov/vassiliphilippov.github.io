aerological

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛrəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɛərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to the scientific study of the atmosphere, especially its upper layers.
pertaining to atmospheric science

Examples

The meteorologist specializes in aerological observations to predict weather patterns.
During the expedition, aerological measurements were taken at various altitudes.
Her research in the aerological field contributed to advances in climate science.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aerology - the scientific study of the atmosphere

Etymology

From aerology + -ical; 'aerology' derives from Greek 'aēr' (air) + 'logia' (study).
