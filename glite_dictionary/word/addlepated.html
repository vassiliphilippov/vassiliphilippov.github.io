addlepated

1. ADJ

/ ˈædəlˌpeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈædəlˌpeɪtɪd / (BrE)
Confused and silly; having a muddled or scatterbrained mind.
confused and silly
archaic

Examples

After losing his keys for the third time this week, he wondered if he was becoming addlepated.
The addlepated professor forgot to bring any notes to the lecture.
Her addlepated comments often amused the rest of the group.

Synset Examples

blathering like the addlepated nincompoop that you are
a confused puddingheaded, muddleheaded fellow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scatterbrained - disorganized or forgetful in thought; silly
  • Antonym: clearheaded - mentally alert and not confused

Etymology

From 'addle' (confused, muddled) + 'pated' (having a specified kind of head).
