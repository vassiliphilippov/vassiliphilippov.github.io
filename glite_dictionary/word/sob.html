SOB

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛs oʊ ˈbi / (AmE), / ˌɛs əʊ ˈbiː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'shortness of breath'; a condition in which someone has difficulty breathing
shortness of breath (medical term)
medical

Examples

The patient reported experiencing SOB during mild physical activity.
Doctors noted that SOB was a significant symptom in his case.
If you develop sudden SOB, seek medical attention immediately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dyspnea - difficult or labored breathing

2. NOUN

/ ˌɛs.oʊˈbi / (AmE), / ˌɛs.əʊˈbiː / (BrE)
an insulting term for a person considered stupid, irritating, or ridiculous, used chiefly as an abbreviation of 'son of a bitch'
insulting term for a stupid or irritating person
slang offensive vulgar

Examples

After all he did, that SOB still had the nerve to show up at my door.
I can't believe the boss promoted that lazy SOB over me.
He's a real SOB when he doesn't get his way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - an irritating or contemptible person
  • Synonym: bastard - an unpleasant or despicable person (offensive)
  • Antonym: gentleman - a polite or honorable man

Etymology

Abbreviation of the vulgar phrase 'son of a bitch'.
