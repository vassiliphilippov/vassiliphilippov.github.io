to canoodle

1. VERB

/ kəˈnudl̩ / (AmE), / kəˈnuːdl / (BrE)
to kiss, cuddle, or caress affectionately, especially in a playful or loving way
kiss or cuddle affectionately

Examples

The couple loved to canoodle on the park bench, completely oblivious to the people passing by.
She caught her friends canoodling in the corner of the café, giggling and holding hands.
They were canoodling under the stars, enjoying the warmth of each other's embrace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cuddle - to hold close in an affectionate manner
  • Synonym: caress - to touch or stroke gently in a loving way
  • Synonym: to fondle - to touch or handle affectionately

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly an alteration of obsolete "canoodle" (to fool or coax), or related to "noodle" (simpleton).
