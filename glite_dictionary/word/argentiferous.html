argentiferous

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑrdʒənˈtɪfərəs / (AmE), / ˌɑːdʒənˈtɪfərəs / (BrE)
(of a substance or ore) containing or yielding silver.
containing or yielding silver

Examples

This region is known for its argentiferous deposits, which have been mined for centuries.

Etymology

From Latin argentum (silver) + -ferous (bearing).
