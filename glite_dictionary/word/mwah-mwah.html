mwah-mwah

1. INTJ

/ mwɑː mwɑː / (AmE), / mwɑː mwɑː / (BrE)
an onomatopoeic word imitating the sound of a kiss, used to express affection or as a playful greeting
sound imitating a kiss; affectionate greeting
informal

Examples

She waved goodbye to her grandmother and called out mwah-mwah from the car window.
At the party, friends exchanged air kisses and said mwah-mwah as they greeted each other.
Sending you a big mwah-mwah for your birthday!

Synset Examples

The kids shouted mwah-mwah as they ran to their parents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muah - another onomatopoeic representation of a kissing sound
