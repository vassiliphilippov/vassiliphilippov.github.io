Elli

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛli / (AmE), / ˈɛli / (BrE)
In Norse mythology, the goddess of old age who defeated Thor in a wrestling match.
goddess of old age in Norse mythology

Examples

In Norse legend, Elli symbolizes the inescapable power of old age, even over the gods themselves.
Mythology recounts how Elli easily defeated Thor in a test of strength by making him age rapidly.
Few people know that Elli is responsible for Thor's surprising wrestling defeat in the ancient tales.

Etymology

From Old Norse 'ellri', meaning 'old age'.
