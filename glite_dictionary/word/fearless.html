fearless

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪr.ləs / (AmE), / ˈfɪə.ləs / (BrE)
showing a lack of fear; not afraid of danger or difficulty
not afraid; brave

Examples

The fearless reporter entered the war zone to get the story firsthand.
Despite the risks, she was fearless in defending her beliefs.
The fearless climber reached the summit without hesitation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brave - showing courage and not being afraid
  • Synonym: courageous - having or showing courage
  • Antonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence; easily frightened

Etymology

From 'fear' + '-less', meaning 'without fear'.

2. ADJ

/ ˈfɪr.ləs / (AmE), / ˈfɪə.ləs / (BrE)
not afraid of anything; showing courage and lack of fear
not afraid; brave

Examples

The fearless firefighter rushed into the burning building without hesitation.
Her fearless attitude inspired everyone on the team to take more risks.
Despite the dangers, he remained fearless in pursuit of the truth.

Synset Examples

audacious explorers
fearless reporters and photographers
intrepid pioneers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brave - showing courage; ready to face danger or pain
  • Antonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence; easily frightened
  • Synonym: intrepid - very brave, fearless, unshakable; synonym for fearless

Etymology

From 'fear' + '-less' (without).

Synset POS: s
