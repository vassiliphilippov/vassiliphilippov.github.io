psychopathologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌsaɪkoʊpæθəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊpæθəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by mental disorder; suffering from a psychological abnormality, often undiagnosed
relating to mental disorder
medical

Examples

Some experts believe that his violent actions may be psychopathologic in origin.
The patient exhibited psychopathologic behaviors that concerned the medical staff.
Untreated trauma can lead to psychopathologic disturbances later in life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychopathological - relating to the study or manifestation of mental disorders
  • Degree Lower: psychological - relating to the mind or mental processes

Etymology

From psycho- (mind) + pathologic (relating to disease), modeled on pathology.
