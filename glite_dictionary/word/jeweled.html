jeweled

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒuːəld / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːəld / (BrE)
Decorated or covered with jewels, beads, or sequins, often to enhance beauty or value.
decorated with jewels or beads

Examples

Her gown was elaborately jeweled and shimmered under the lights.
He gave her a jeweled brooch for her birthday.
The princess wore a jeweled crown encrusted with emeralds and rubies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedazzled - decorated or adorned with sparkling objects
  • Antonym: plain - not decorated or ornamented

Etymology

From 'jewel' + '-ed' (past participle/adjectival suffix).
