illustrator

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪləˌstreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɪləstreɪtə / (BrE)
A person who creates drawings or pictures to explain or decorate books, magazines, advertisements, or other printed materials.
artist who creates illustrations

Examples

The children's book was brought to life by a talented illustrator.
She dreams of becoming an illustrator for a famous magazine someday.
Many advertising agencies hire a freelance illustrator to create engaging visuals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: artist - person who creates art, especially visual art
  • Antonym: author - person who writes books or other texts

Etymology

From Latin 'illustrator' (enlightener), from 'illustrare' meaning 'to make light or to illustrate'.
