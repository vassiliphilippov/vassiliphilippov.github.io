parking

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkɪŋ / (BrE)
an area or space where vehicles can be parked
space for parking vehicles

Examples

There is parking available for all customers at the rear of the building.
We couldn't find any parking near the concert venue, so we had to walk several blocks.
The parking fills up quickly during the morning rush hour.

Synset Examples

there is plenty of parking behind the store

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car park - a designated area or building for parking cars
  • Synonym: parking lot - an outdoor area where vehicles can be parked

2. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of positioning a vehicle in a place where it can be left for a period of time
the act of positioning a vehicle for temporary storage

Examples

The city introduced new rules for parking to reduce congestion downtown.
She received a ticket for illegal parking on the street.
He found parking difficult during rush hour near the office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: park - to bring a vehicle to a stop and leave it temporarily

Etymology

From the verb 'park' (to bring a vehicle to a halt and leave it temporarily) plus the suffix '-ing'.
