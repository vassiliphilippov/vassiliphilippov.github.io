coexisting

1. ADJ

/ ˌkoʊ.ɪɡˈzɪst.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.ɪɡˈzɪst.ɪŋ / (BrE)
existing at the same time as something else; occurring or living together simultaneously.
existing at the same time

Examples

The museum features coexisting art styles from different periods.
In the forest, several coexisting species share the same habitat.
The two cultures were known for their coexisting traditions and mutual respect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concurrent - existing or happening at the same time
