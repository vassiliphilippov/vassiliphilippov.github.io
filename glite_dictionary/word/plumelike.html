plumelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈpluːmlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpluːmlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the appearance of a plume (a large, feathery or fluffy structure)
resembling a plume

Examples

The parade float was decorated with plumelike streamers that danced in the breeze.
Her hat was adorned with a plumelike ornament that stood out in the crowd.
The plant’s plumelike leaves gave the garden a delicate, graceful look.

Synset Examples

the dog's plumy tail

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feathery - light and fluffy like feathers
  • Synonym: downy - covered with soft, fine feathers or hair
