pallone

1. NOUN

/ pɑˈloʊneɪ / (AmE), / paˈlɒneɪ / (BrE)
An Italian ball game similar to tennis, played with a large ball struck by the hand or a wooden bat, traditionally popular in some regions of Italy.
Italian ball game like tennis

Examples

The traditional sport of pallone is still played in some Italian villages today.
He learned the rules of pallone when he visited Tuscany last summer.
Unlike tennis, pallone is often played in the open air along narrow streets.

Etymology

From Italian 'pallone', meaning 'large ball', from 'palla' (ball).
