Linnaean

1. ADJ

/ lɪˈniən / (AmE), / lɪˈniːən / (BrE)
relating to Carl Linnaeus or to the system of taxonomic classification he developed
of or relating to Linnaeus or his classification system

Examples

The Linnaean system of classification is still widely used in biological sciences.
Many modern biologists have built upon Linnaean methods when categorizing new species.
She gave a lecture about the history and impact of the Linnaean taxonomy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxonomic - relating to classification, especially of organisms

Etymology

From the name of Carl Linnaeus (1707–1778), Swedish botanist.
