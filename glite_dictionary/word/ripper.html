ripper

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪpər / (AmE), / ˈrɪpə / (BrE)
a murderer who slashes or mutilates victims with a knife
knife-wielding murderer

Examples

Many believed the ripper would never be caught after the gruesome crimes in the city.
The infamous ripper terrified London during the late 19th century.
He wrote a novel inspired by the legend of the ripper.

Synset Examples

Jack the Ripper was probably a madman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: killer - a person who kills
  • Synonym: murderer - a person who commits murder

Etymology

From the verb 'rip' (to cut open) plus the agent suffix '-er'.
