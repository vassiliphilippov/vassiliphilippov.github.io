rubberlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌbərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈrʌbəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having an elastic, flexible, or tough texture similar to that of rubber
having rubber-like flexibility or toughness

Examples

The new phone case is made of a rubberlike material for extra protection.
The toy's rubberlike coating prevents it from breaking easily when dropped.
The mushroom's stem felt oddly rubberlike to the touch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elastic - able to resume its normal shape after stretching or compressing; flexible
  • Antonym: rigid - not able to be bent or changed easily; stiff
