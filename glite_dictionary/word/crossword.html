crossword

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɑsˌwɜrd / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌwɜːd / (BrE)
A puzzle in which words are filled into a grid according to numbered clues, with the words crossing vertically and horizontally.
word puzzle with intersecting words

Examples

She solves the crossword in the newspaper every morning with her coffee.
The Sunday edition had a particularly challenging crossword that took hours to finish.
He enjoys completing a crossword as a way to relax after work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puzzle - game or problem designed to test ingenuity or knowledge

Etymology

From 'cross' + 'word', reflecting the intersecting words in the puzzle grid. First used in the early 20th century.
