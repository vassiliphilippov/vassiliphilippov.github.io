boss

1. NOUN

/ bɑs / (AmE), / bɒs / (BrE)
a circular rounded projection or protuberance, especially one on a surface for strengthening, decoration, or functional purposes
rounded projection or protuberance

Examples

The shield was decorated with a central boss made of polished bronze.
A raised boss on the doorplate served as the handle.
Engineers designed the part with a boss to increase its rigidity.

Etymology

From Old French 'boce' (swelling, bump), of Germanic origin.
