batsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæts.mən / (AmE), / ˈbæts.mən / (BrE)
A player who is currently batting in a game, especially cricket or baseball.
player who is batting in a game

Examples

The batsman hit the ball for a six, thrilling the crowd.
After a long partnership, the next batsman took the crease.
The team's last batsman managed to score the winning run.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: batter - a player who is batting, especially in baseball

Etymology

Formed from 'bat' + 'man'; used since the 18th century in cricket.
