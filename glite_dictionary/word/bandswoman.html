bandswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈbændzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈbændzwʊmən / (BrE)
A female member of a band, especially a military band.
female band member (esp. military)

Examples

She was the first bandswoman to join the regiment's military band.
The bandswoman played the trumpet during the parade.
Many young girls were inspired by the bandswoman leading the drumline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bandsman - a male or generic member of a band (esp. military)

Etymology

Formed from 'band' + 'woman', by analogy with 'bandsman'.
