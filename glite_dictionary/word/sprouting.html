sprouting

1. NOUN

/ ˈspraʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspraʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
the process by which seeds or spores begin to grow and put out shoots
the initial growth of seeds or spores

Examples

The sprouting of seeds marks the beginning of the plant's life cycle.
Recent rains have led to the sprouting of wildflowers across the fields.
Proper temperature and moisture are crucial for successful sprouting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: germination - the process by which a seed develops into a new plant

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'sprout' + suffix '-ing'.
