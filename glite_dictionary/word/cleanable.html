cleanable

1. ADJ

/ ˈkliːnəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈkliːnəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be cleaned; capable of being made clean.
able to be cleaned

Examples

The kitchen floor is easily cleanable with a damp mop.
All the toys in the daycare are cleanable for safety reasons.
Choose furniture with cleanable fabric to make maintenance easier.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: washable - able to be washed without damage

Etymology

Formed by adding the suffix '-able' to 'clean'.
