formalised

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrməˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːməlʌɪzd / (BrE)
Given official status or recognition by following established procedures or making something official.
made official or recognized
british

Examples

The agreement was formalised through the signing of a written contract.
These traditions have been formalised over centuries into strict rituals.
The council formalised the policy at last week's meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: formalized - made official or given formal status (American spelling)
  • Antonym: unofficial - not officially recognized or sanctioned

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'formalise', which derives from Latin 'formalis' (relating to form).
