uncoupled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkʌpəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌpəld / (BrE)
not joined or connected; having been separated or detached, especially after previously being joined.
not joined or connected

Examples

The engineer noticed the uncoupled car drifting away from the rest of the train.
After repairs, the previously uncoupled machines worked together again.
The uncoupled sections of the pipeline required immediate attention.

Synset Examples

the uncoupled caboose rolled down the incline

Related Terms

  • Antonym: coupled - joined or connected together
  • Synonym: detached - separated, not attached

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'coupled' (joined).
