aptitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈæp.tɪ.tud / (AmE), / ˈæp.tɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
a natural ability or talent for learning or doing something well
natural ability or talent

Examples

Her aptitude for mathematics was evident from an early age.
The job requires an aptitude for problem-solving and creative thinking.
He demonstrated a remarkable aptitude in learning languages quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talent - natural aptitude or skill
  • Antonym: inability - lack of ability to do something

Etymology

From Latin aptitudo (fitness), from aptus (fit).
