yelping

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛlpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈjɛlpɪŋ / (BrE)
a sharp, high-pitched cry, especially made by a dog or animal
a sharp, high cry (especially by a dog)

Examples

The yelping of the puppy could be heard throughout the neighborhood.
She was awakened by the yelping of dogs outside her window.
The sudden yelping startled everyone in the quiet park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yelp - a sharp high-pitched bark or cry

Etymology

From Middle English 'yelpen', related to 'yelp' (to cry out sharply).
