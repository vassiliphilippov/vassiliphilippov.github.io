heart-whole

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑrt ˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈhɑːt ˌhəʊl / (BrE)
Showing unconditional and enthusiastic devotion; sincere and undivided in feeling or support.
unconditional and enthusiastic devotion

Examples

She gave her heart-whole support to the new initiative, believing deeply in its mission.
Their heart-whole friendship endured through years of challenges and success.
He pledged his heart-whole loyalty to the cause without a moment's hesitation.

Synset Examples

heart-whole friendship
gave wholehearted support to her candidacy
wholehearted commitment
demonstrated his whole-souled allegiance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wholehearted - completely sincere and committed

Etymology

From 'heart' (seat of emotion) + 'whole' (entire, undivided).
