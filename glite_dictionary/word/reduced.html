reduced

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈduːst / (AmE), / rɪˈdjuːst / (BrE)
Made smaller in size, amount, or degree.
made smaller in amount or degree

Examples

The company offered a reduced price for early orders.
He now works part-time and receives a reduced salary.
Processed foods often contain reduced amounts of salt for health reasons.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: increased - made greater in size, amount, or degree
  • Synonym: diminished - made smaller or less; decreased

2. ADJ

/ ɹɪˈduːst / (AmE), / rɪˈdjuːst / (BrE)
lower than the usual or original amount, especially in price or value
lower than usual, especially in price

Examples

Many items in the store are now being sold at reduced prices for the holiday sale.
She always looks for reduced merchandise when shopping for clothes.
The reduced fare is only available to senior citizens and students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discounted - sold at a reduced price
  • Antonym: full-price - sold at original or usual price
