acidulous

1. ADJ

/ əˈsɪdʒələs / (AmE), / əˈsɪdjʊləs / (BrE)
Having a somewhat sour or bitter taste.
somewhat sour or bitter in taste

Examples

The lemonade had an acidulous flavor that made everyone's lips pucker.
She preferred the acidulous tang of green apples over sweeter varieties.
His comments carried an acidulous tone that hinted at his displeasure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acidic - having the properties of an acid; sharp or sour
  • Antonym: sweet - having a taste characteristic of sugar or honey

Etymology

From Latin acidulus, diminutive of acidus (sour).
