umbrella-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ʌmˈbrɛl.ə ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ʌmˈbrɛl.ə ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
shaped in the form of an umbrella; resembling the shape of an umbrella.
having the shape of an umbrella

Examples

The mushroom had a distinctive umbrella-shaped cap that caught everyone's attention.
A large umbrella-shaped tree provided shade for the picnickers.
The chandelier was designed with umbrella-shaped glass panels that diffused the light beautifully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dome-shaped - having the shape of a dome
