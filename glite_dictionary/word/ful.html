Ful

1. NOUN

/ fuːl / (AmE), / fuːl / (BrE)
the language or group of closely related languages spoken by the Fulani people of West Africa, used as a lingua franca from Senegal to Chad
language of the Fulani people in West Africa

Examples

The Ful language is widely spoken throughout the Sahel region of Africa.
Many official documents are translated into Ful for better communication among locals.
She is fluent in Ful, Hausa, and French.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Fula - alternative name for the Ful language and ethnic group
  • Synonym: Fulani - another term for the people who speak Ful language

Etymology

From Hausa 'Fula' or directly from the endonym 'Fulɓe', the self-designation of the Fulani people.
