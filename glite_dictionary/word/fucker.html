fucker

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌkər / (AmE), / ˈfʌkə / (BrE)
an extremely offensive term for a contemptible, stupid, or detested man
vulgar insult for a despised man
offensive vulgar

Examples

I can't stand that fucker who keeps cutting in line.
Some fucker keyed my car last night and got away with it.
Don't be such a fucker, just help us out for once.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bastard - an unpleasant or despicable person (insult)
  • Synonym: jerk - an obnoxious or contemptible person
  • Antonym: gentleman - a polite or honorable man (opposite)

Etymology

From 'fuck' plus the agentive suffix '-er'; 'fuck' is of obscure Germanic origin.
