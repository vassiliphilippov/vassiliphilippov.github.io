dressmaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɛsˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make custom clothing, especially women's dresses, to order
person who makes custom clothing

Examples

She hired a talented dressmaker to create a bespoke gown for her wedding.
The dressmaker carefully measured the client before cutting the fabric.
Many famous designers began their careers as dressmakers in small ateliers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tailor - a person whose occupation is making fitted clothes, especially suits and jackets
