Paul McCartney

1. PROPN

/ pɔl məˈkɑrtni / (AmE), / pɔːl məˈkɑːtni / (BrE)
(born 1942) English musician, songwriter, and bass guitarist, best known as a member of the Beatles.
English musician and former Beatle

Examples

Paul McCartney played bass and sang on many classic Beatles tracks.
Few musicians have achieved the global recognition that Paul McCartney enjoys.
After the Beatles, Paul McCartney started the band Wings and continued his musical success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: John Lennon - English singer-songwriter, co-founder of the Beatles
  • Synonym: The Beatles - British rock band formed in the 1960s
