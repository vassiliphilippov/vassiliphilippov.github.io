New Ireland

1. PROPN

/ nu ˈaɪərlənd / (AmE), / njuː ˈaɪələnd / (BrE)
an island in the Bismarck Archipelago, located in Papua New Guinea, known for its diverse cultures and languages
island in Papua New Guinea

Examples

The traditional dances of New Ireland are a vibrant part of the island's culture.
Researchers are studying rare bird species unique to New Ireland.
Tourism in New Ireland has grown due to its beautiful beaches and coral reefs.

Etymology

Named by British explorer Philip Carteret in the 18th century after Ireland.
