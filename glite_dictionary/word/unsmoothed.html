unsmoothed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsmuðd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsmuːðd / (BrE)
not made smooth; having a rough or uneven surface that has not been smoothed
not smoothed; rough or uneven

Examples

The painter was unhappy with the unsmoothed surface of the wall before applying the final coat.
She ran her fingers over the unsmoothed wood, feeling every bump and groove.
The unsmoothed stone made the path difficult to walk on.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
  • Synonym: rough - having an uneven or irregular surface
