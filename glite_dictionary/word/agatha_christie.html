Agatha Christie

1. PROPN

/ ˈæɡəθə ˈkrɪsti / (AmE), / ˈæɡəθə ˈkrɪsti / (BrE)
(1890–1976) An English writer renowned for her many detective novels, particularly those featuring Hercule Poirot and Miss Marple.
famous English detective novelist (1890–1976)

Examples

Many consider Agatha Christie to be the greatest detective fiction writer of all time.
I spent the weekend reading several novels by Agatha Christie.
The works of Agatha Christie have been translated into numerous languages.
