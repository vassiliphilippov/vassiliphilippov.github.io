bibliothecarial

1. ADJ

/ ˌbɪbliəθəˈkɛriəl / (AmE), / ˌbɪbliəʊθɪˈkeəriəl / (BrE)
relating to a library, bibliotheca, or a librarian; pertaining to the administration or organization of libraries
relating to libraries or librarians

Examples

She is pursuing a degree in bibliothecarial studies to become a professional librarian.
The university hired a consultant with extensive bibliothecarial experience to redesign their library system.
Many bibliothecarial practices have evolved with the advent of digital technologies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: librarian - a person in charge of or working in a library

Etymology

From Latin bibliothecarius (of a library), from Greek bibliothēkē (library) + -al (adjectival suffix).
