Blackpool

1. PROPN

/ ˈblækˌpuːl / (AmE), / ˈblækpuːl / (BrE)
A resort town in Lancashire, northwestern England, on the Irish Sea, known for its seaside attractions and Blackpool Tower.
town in Lancashire, England, seaside resort

Examples

We spent a weekend in Blackpool enjoying the amusement parks and the pier.
Blackpool attracts millions of visitors each year to its famous illuminations.
The iconic tower in Blackpool is a major landmark of the town.
