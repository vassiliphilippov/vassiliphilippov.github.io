ornithology

1. NOUN

/ ɔrˌnɪˈθɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ɔːˌnɪˈθɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of birds, including their behavior, classification, and distribution
the study of birds

Examples

She developed a deep interest in ornithology after observing birds in her backyard.
The university offers advanced courses in ornithology for graduate students.
His lifelong passion for ornithology led him to discover several new bird species.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: zoology - the scientific study of animals

Etymology

From Greek ornithologia, from ornith- (bird) + -logia (study).
