nonoperational

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˌɑpəˈreɪʃənl / (AmE), / nɒnˌɒpəˈreɪʃənl / (BrE)
(military) Not participating or engaged in military operations or activities.
not participating in military operations

Examples

The old aircraft carrier is now nonoperational and serves as a museum ship.
Several units remained nonoperational during the conflict due to lack of equipment.
The base was declared nonoperational after the peace treaty was signed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: operational - actively engaged in military operations

Etymology

From prefix 'non-' (not) + 'operational'.
