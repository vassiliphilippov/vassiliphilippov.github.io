lab

1. NOUN

/ læb / (AmE), / læb / (BrE)
a room or building equipped for scientific experiments, research, or teaching, especially in science or medicine
room for scientific research

Examples

The students spent their afternoon working in the lab on their chemistry experiment.
Our new biology lab is equipped with the latest technology.
She prefers doing fieldwork to spending all day in the lab.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laboratory - a room or building for scientific experiments or research

Etymology

Shortened form of 'laboratory', from Medieval Latin 'laboratorium' (place of labor).
