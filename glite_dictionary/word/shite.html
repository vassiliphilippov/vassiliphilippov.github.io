shite

1. NOUN

/ ʃaɪt / (AmE), / ʃaɪt / (BrE)
Vulgar slang term for feces; used as an expletive or to refer to something considered worthless or contemptible.
vulgar slang for feces
slang vulgar british

Examples

He stepped right into a pile of shite on the street.
I’m sick of dealing with this shite every day at work.
The movie was utter shite, not worth the money at all.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shit - vulgar term for feces, also used as an expletive

Etymology

Variant of 'shit', from Old English *scītan* (to defecate).
