goo

1. NOUN

/ ɡuː / (AmE), / ɡuː / (BrE)
any thick, sticky, or viscous substance
thick, sticky substance

Examples

The toddler's hands were covered in goo after playing with the slime.
Some kind of green goo started oozing out of the machine.
She used a special cleaner to remove the sticky goo from the countertop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gunk - any unpleasant, sticky, or greasy substance
  • Synonym: slime - a thick, slippery, or sticky substance
  • Synonym: paste - a thick, soft, moist substance
