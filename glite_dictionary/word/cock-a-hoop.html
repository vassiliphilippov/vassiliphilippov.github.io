cock-a-hoop

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑːk.əˈhuːp / (AmE), / ˌkɒk.əˈhuːp / (BrE)
Feeling or showing excessive pride or self-importance; boastfully elated.
exhibiting self-importance; elated
archaic

Examples

After winning the award, he was cock-a-hoop about his success.
She walked into the meeting cock-a-hoop, eager to share her achievements.
Their cock-a-hoop attitude irritated those who preferred a more modest approach.

Synset Examples

big talk

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boastful - showing excessive pride and self-satisfaction
  • Antonym: modest - having a humble or low view of one's importance

Etymology

Probably from the phrase 'to set cock on hoop', meaning 'to drink festively'. Later associated with boastful elation.
