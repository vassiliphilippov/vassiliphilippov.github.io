uncurved

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkɝvd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɜːvd / (BrE)
not having any curves; straight or flat.
not curved; straight

Examples

The architect designed the ceiling with an uncurved surface for a modern look.
He admired the clean, uncurved lines of the sculpture.
Most of the roads here are uncurved, running perfectly straight through the countryside.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: curved - having a smooth, continuously bending shape
  • Synonym: straight - extending or moving uniformly in one direction only; without a curve or bend

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) and 'curved'.
