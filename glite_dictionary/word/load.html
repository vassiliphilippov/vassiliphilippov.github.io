load

1. NOUN

/ loʊd / (AmE), / ləʊd / (BrE)
the amount of power delivered by a generator or power plant at a given time
power output of a generator or plant

Examples

When the electricity load increases, the plant must generate more power to meet the demand.
The engineers monitor the load to ensure the generator is not overloaded during peak hours.
A sudden drop in load can cause instability in the power grid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: output - the amount of something produced by a person, machine, or industry
