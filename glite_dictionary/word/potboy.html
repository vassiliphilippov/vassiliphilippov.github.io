potboy

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑtˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɒtˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a male worker in an inn or pub who serves customers and performs various menial tasks
male inn or pub servant
archaic

Examples

In nineteenth-century London, the potboy was often the youngest employee at the tavern.
He worked as a potboy before finding a better job in the city.
The potboy spent his evenings clearing tables and fetching drinks for guests.
