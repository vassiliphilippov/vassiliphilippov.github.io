Duvalier

1. PROPN

/ duːˈvɑːljeɪ / (AmE), / djuːˈvalieɪ / (BrE)
Jean-Claude Duvalier (born 1951), known as 'Baby Doc', son and successor of François Duvalier as president of Haiti, who was overthrown in 1986.
former Haitian president ('Baby Doc')

Examples

Duvalier ruled Haiti with an authoritarian regime until his ouster in 1986.
After years of unrest, Duvalier finally fled the country, ending his family's reign.
Many Haitians still remember the harsh policies of Duvalier and his government.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: François Duvalier - former Haitian president, father of Jean-Claude Duvalier
