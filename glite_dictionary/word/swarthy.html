swarthy

1. ADJ

/ ˈswɔrði / (AmE), / ˈswɔːði / (BrE)
(of a person or complexion) having a naturally dark skin color.
having dark skin color

Examples

The sailor's swarthy face was weathered by years at sea.
Her swarthy complexion contrasted sharply with her sister's pale skin.
He was tall and swarthy, with deep brown eyes and black hair.

Synset Examples

a dark-skinned beauty
gold earrings gleamed against her dusky cheeks
a smile on his swarthy face
‘swart’ is archaic

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fair - having light skin color

Etymology

From Old English *sweart* (black, dark) via Old Norse *svartr*.
