to legitimatize

1. VERB

/ ləˈdʒɪt.ə.məˌtaɪz / (AmE), / lɪˈdʒɪt.ɪ.mə.taɪz / (BrE)
to make something legal or legitimate; to give legal status or authority to
make legal or legitimate

Examples

The government decided to legitimatize certain types of online gambling.
Activists are working to legitimatize same-sex marriage in their country.
By passing the new law, lawmakers hope to legitimatize the use of electric scooters in cities.

Synset Examples

Marijuana should be legalized

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to legalize - to make legal by official means

Etymology

From legitimate + -ize, modeled after 'legitimize'.
