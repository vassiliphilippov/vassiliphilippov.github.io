Helmut Schmidt

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɛlmuːt ˈʃmɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɛlmʊt ˈʃmɪt / (BrE)
(1918–2015) German statesman who served as chancellor of West Germany from 1974 to 1982.
German chancellor (1974–1982)

Examples

Helmut Schmidt played a crucial role in shaping European economic policy during the late 1970s.
Many historians study the leadership style of Helmut Schmidt during West Germany's political transition.
As chancellor, Helmut Schmidt dealt with major international crises and domestic reforms.
