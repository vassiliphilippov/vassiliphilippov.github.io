to shit-stir

1. VERB

/ ʃɪt stɜr / (AmE), / ʃɪt stɜː / (BrE)
To deliberately provoke, incite, or spread trouble or conflict, especially by causing arguments or stirring up negative emotions.
provoke trouble or conflict
informal slang vulgar

You shouldn't shit-stir just because you're bored; it creates unnecessary problems.
