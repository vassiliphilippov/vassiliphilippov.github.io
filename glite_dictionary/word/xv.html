XV

1. NUM

/ ɛks ˈviː / (AmE), / ɛks ˈviː / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for the number fifteen
Roman numeral for fifteen

Examples

The clock face showed the number XV at the top.
Super Bowl XV was played in New Orleans in 1981.
The inscription read 'Regina Elizabetha XV' on the monument.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fifteen - the number fifteen, one more than fourteen

Etymology

From Latin numeral system, where X represents ten and V represents five.
