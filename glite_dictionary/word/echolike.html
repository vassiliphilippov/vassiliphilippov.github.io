echolike

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛkoʊˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɛkəʊˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Having the qualities or characteristics of an echo, especially in sound or effect.
resembling an echo

Examples

Her voice had an echolike quality in the empty hallway.
An echolike repetition of sounds can be heard in the cave.
The stadium gave the singer’s applause an echolike resonance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: echoic - resembling or imitating an echo; onomatopoeic
