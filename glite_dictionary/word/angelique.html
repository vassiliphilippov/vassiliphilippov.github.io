angelique

1. NOUN

/ ænˈʤɛlɪk / (AmE), / anˈʤɛlɪk / (BrE)
any of several tall, robust plants of the genus Angelica, characterized by divided leaves and clusters of small white or greenish flowers.
a tall plant of the genus Angelica

Examples

The garden featured a bed of angelique beside the riverbank.
Herbalists often use dried angelique in traditional remedies.
The towering stalks of angelique provided a dramatic backdrop to the cottage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angelica - plant genus Angelica, tall herbs with divided leaves and umbels

Etymology

From Latin 'angelica' via French 'angélique', referring to the Angelica plant genus.
