Honolulu

1. PROPN

/ ˌhɑːnəˈluːluː / (AmE), / ˌhɒnəˈluːluː / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Hawaii, located on the island of Oahu
capital city of Hawaii

Examples

Many tourists visit Honolulu every year to enjoy its beautiful beaches and vibrant culture.
She moved to Honolulu for a new job opportunity and loves the island lifestyle.
The climate in Honolulu is warm and pleasant throughout the year.

Etymology

From Hawaiian 'Honolulu' meaning 'sheltered harbor'.
