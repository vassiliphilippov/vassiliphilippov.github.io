Chiwere

1. NOUN

/ tʃiˈwɛr.eɪ / (AmE), / tʃɪˈwɪə.reɪ / (BrE)
the Siouan language spoken by the Iowa, Oto, and Missouri peoples
a Siouan language of Iowa, Oto, and Missouri tribes

Examples

Linguists are working to document and revitalize the Chiwere language.
The Chiwere dialect differs somewhat among the Iowa, Oto, and Missouri tribes.
He presented research on the grammatical structure of Chiwere at the conference.

Etymology

From the name 'Jiwere,' meaning 'Iowa' in the native language.
