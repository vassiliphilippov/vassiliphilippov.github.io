Christmastime

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪs.məsˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.məsˌtaɪm / (BrE)
the period of time around Christmas, especially from December 24 to January 6
period around Christmas

Examples

Many families gather together during Christmastime to celebrate the holidays.
Shops are decorated beautifully at Christmastime.
He feels especially generous and kind at Christmastime, helping those in need.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Christmas - the annual Christian festival celebrating Christ's birth, held on December 25
