Victoria Land

1. PROPN

/ vɪkˈtɔri.ə lænd / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔːri.ə lænd / (BrE)
a mountainous region of Antarctica, located between the Ross Sea and Wilkes Land
a region of Antarctica

Examples

Scientists have discovered several new species in Victoria Land during their Antarctic expedition.
The breathtaking glaciers of Victoria Land make it an important area for climate research.
The first explorers to reach Victoria Land were amazed by its rugged mountain ranges.

Etymology

Named after Queen Victoria of the United Kingdom.
