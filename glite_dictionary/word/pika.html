pika

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪkə / (AmE), / ˈpiːkə / (BrE)
a small, short-eared mammal of rocky uplands in Asia and western North America, related to rabbits and hares
small mammal of rocky uplands

Examples

The alpine pika is known for its whistling calls among the rocks.
Climate change threatens the habitat of the pika in mountain regions.
We spotted a pika darting between the boulders on our hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rabbit - a small, burrowing mammal related to the pika
  • Synonym: hare - a fast-running, long-eared mammal related to the pika

Etymology

From Russian 'pika', referring to the animal.
