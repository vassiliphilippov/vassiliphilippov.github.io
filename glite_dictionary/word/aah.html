to aah

1. VERB

/ ɑː / (AmE), / ɑː / (BrE)
to express admiration, pleasure, or surprise by uttering a prolonged sound such as 'aah'
express pleasure or admiration aloud

Examples

The crowd began to aah at the spectacular fireworks display.
Children aahed as they saw the magician pull a rabbit from his hat.
We couldn't help but aah over the beautiful paintings at the gallery.

Synset Examples

They oohed and aahed when they unwrapped the presents

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ooh - to express surprise or admiration by saying 'ooh'
