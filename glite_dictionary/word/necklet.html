necklet

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛklət / (AmE), / ˈnɛklɪt / (BrE)
A piece of jewelry or ornament, such as a small necklace or a band of fur, worn closely around the neck.
small necklace or neck ornament

Examples

She wore a delicate necklet adorned with tiny pearls to the party.
The vintage fur necklet complemented her winter coat perfectly.
Antique dealers often display ornate silver necklets from the Victorian era.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: necklace - an ornamental chain or string of beads, jewels, or links worn around the neck

Etymology

From 'neck' + Old French diminutive suffix '-let', meaning 'little neck ornament'.
