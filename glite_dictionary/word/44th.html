44th

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrti ˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːti ˈfɔːθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number corresponding to forty-four, used to indicate position or order in a sequence
ordinal number for forty-four

Examples

The company will celebrate its 44th anniversary next year.
