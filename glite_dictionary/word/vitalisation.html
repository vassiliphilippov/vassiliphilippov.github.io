vitalisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌvaɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvaɪtələˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or state of being filled with life, energy, or vitality
state of being filled with life or energy

Examples

The yoga retreat focused on the vitalisation of both mind and body.
Community projects often lead to the vitalisation of neglected neighborhoods.
Exposure to sunlight contributes to the vitalisation of plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vitality - the state of being strong and active; energy

Etymology

From vital + -isation, patterned after the verb 'vitalize'.
