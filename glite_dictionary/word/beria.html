Beria

1. PROPN

/ ˈbeɪriə / (AmE), / ˈbɛːrɪə / (BrE)
(1899–1953) Soviet chief of secret police under Joseph Stalin, executed during a power struggle after Stalin's death.
Soviet chief of secret police (1899–1953)

Examples

Historians debate the extent of Beria's involvement in the purges during Stalin's regime.
After Stalin's death, Beria rapidly lost power and was ultimately executed.
The role of Beria in Soviet intelligence operations remains controversial to this day.
