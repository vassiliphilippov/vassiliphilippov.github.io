oodles

1. NOUN

/ ˈuː.dəlz / (AmE), / ˈuː.dəlz / (BrE)
a very large amount or number of something; lots
a very large amount or number
informal

Examples

We received oodles of support from our friends during the move.

  • Antonym: a few - a small number or amount

Etymology

Probably a playful alteration of 'oodles,' perhaps influenced by 'noodle' or similar-sounding nonsense words, American English, late 19th century.
