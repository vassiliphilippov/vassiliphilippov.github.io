Polemonium

1. NOUN

/ ˌpoʊləˈmoʊniəm / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈməʊniəm / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the family Polemoniaceae, commonly known as Jacob's ladder.
plant genus; Jacob's ladder

Examples

Many gardeners cultivate Polemonium for its attractive, ladder-like leaves and blue flowers.
Polemonium species are found in temperate regions across the Northern Hemisphere.
Botanists recently discovered a new Polemonium species in the mountains of China.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin, based on ancient Greek Polemonion, a plant name mentioned by Dioscorides.
