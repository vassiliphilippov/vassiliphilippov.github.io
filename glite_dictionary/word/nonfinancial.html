nonfinancial

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.faɪˈnæn.ʃəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.faɪˈnæn.ʃəl / (BrE)
Not involving or relating to financial matters; not pertaining to money or finance.
not involving financial matters

Examples

The company offers many nonfinancial benefits to its employees, such as flexible working hours and professional development programs.
They discussed several nonfinancial concerns, including employee morale and workplace safety.
Her support was purely nonfinancial, focusing on mentorship rather than funding.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: financial - involving money or finance

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'non-' (not) to 'financial'.
