accountantship

1. NOUN

/ əˈkaʊn.tənt.ʃɪp / (AmE), / əˈkaʊn.tənt.ʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or status of being an accountant
the role of an accountant

Examples

After years of dedicated study, she finally achieved accountantship at the prestigious firm.
His accountantship was marked by scrupulous honesty and professionalism.
The responsibilities of accountantship require strong analytical and ethical standards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accountant - a person whose job is to keep or inspect financial accounts

Etymology

From 'accountant' + '-ship', a suffix forming nouns denoting condition or status.
