whoremonger

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrˌmʌŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːˌmʌŋɡə / (BrE)
a man who habitually visits prostitutes or uses their services
man who visits prostitutes
archaic offensive

Examples

The judge was appalled by the testimony about the notorious whoremonger in the city.
In old novels, a whoremonger was often depicted as morally corrupt.
He was labeled a whoremonger after repeatedly being seen in the red-light district.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: john - a man who pays for the services of a prostitute

Etymology

From Middle English 'hore' (prostitute) + 'monger' (dealer or trader).

2. NOUN

/ ˈhɔːrˌmʌŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːmʌŋɡə / (BrE)
a man who procures prostitutes or has frequent dealings with prostitutes; a pimp or procurer
a man who procures prostitutes
archaic offensive

Examples

The notorious whoremonger was known for running several brothels in the city.
She despised the corrupt officials who acted as whoremongers behind the scenes.
In old literature, a whoremonger was considered morally reprehensible and shunned by society.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pimp - a person, especially a man, who controls prostitutes and arranges clients for them, taking part of their earnings
  • Synonym: procurer - a person who procures prostitutes for clients

Etymology

From Middle English 'horemongere', from 'whore' + 'monger' (dealer or trader).
