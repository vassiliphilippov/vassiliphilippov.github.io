fire-wheel

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪərˌwiːl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌwiːl / (BrE)
an annual flowering plant native to central United States, with long-stalked yellow flowers marked with scarlet or purple in the center
a brightly colored annual flower of the US

Examples

The fire-wheel brightened the garden with its vibrant red and yellow petals.
We saw fire-wheels growing wild alongside the country road.
She picked a bouquet of fire-wheel flowers for the table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blanket flower - another name for the fire-wheel plant
