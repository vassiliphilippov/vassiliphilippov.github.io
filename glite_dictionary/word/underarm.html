underarm

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌndərˌɑrm / (AmE), / ˈʌndərˌɑːm / (BrE)
(of a throw or stroke) performed with the arm or hand moving forward and up from below shoulder level.
with the arm below shoulder level

Examples

The softball was thrown underarm to ensure greater accuracy.
She served the ball underarm to surprise her opponent.
In cricket, an underarm delivery is now considered unusual.

Synset Examples

an underhand pitch
an underhand stroke

Related Terms

  • Antonym: overarm - performed with the arm above shoulder level
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02480006-a
Synset POS: a

2. ADV

/ ˈʌndərˌɑrm / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌɑːm / (BrE)
in a manner where the hand is swung below shoulder level, especially when throwing or bowling
with a hand swung below shoulder height

Examples

He tossed the ball underarm to his younger brother.
The cricket player preferred to bowl underarm instead of overarm.
You must throw underarm during this part of the game.

Synset Examples

throwing a ball underarm

Related Terms

  • Antonym: overarm - with the arm swinging above shoulder level when throwing or bowling

Synset POS: r
