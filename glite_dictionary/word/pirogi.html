pirogi

1. NOUN

/ pɪˈroʊɡi / (AmE), / pɪˈrɒɡi / (BrE)
A small pastry turnover, typically filled with fruit, cheese, or meat, and baked or fried.
filled pastry turnover

Examples

I ordered a plate of cherry pirogi for dessert at the restaurant.
She taught her children how to make traditional meat-filled pirogi.
The bakery offers both baked and fried pirogi filled with a variety of ingredients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pierogi - similar type of filled dumpling or turnover, Polish origin

Etymology

From Russian пирог (pirog), meaning 'pie' or 'pastry'.
