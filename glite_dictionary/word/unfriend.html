to unfriend

1. VERB

/ ʌnˈfrɛnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfrɛnd / (BrE)
to remove someone from your list of friends on a social networking website or app
remove from social network friends list

Examples

I decided to unfriend Mark after we had that big argument online.
She unfriended several people who were posting negative comments on her wall.
It can feel awkward to unfriend someone you know in real life.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to befriend - to act as a friend to someone
