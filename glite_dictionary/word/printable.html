printable

1. ADJ

/ ˈprɪn.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈprɪn.tə.bəl / (BrE)
(of content or language) suitable for publication because it does not contain material that is morally or legally objectionable.
suitable for publication; not offensive

Examples

The editor said that only printable language should be included in the report.
Please ensure that all comments on this forum are printable and appropriate for all readers.
Some of the jokes were not printable in a family newspaper.

Synset Examples

printable language

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unprintable - not suitable for publication because of offensive content

Etymology

From 'print' + '-able', formed in English.
