gastronomy

1. NOUN

/ ɡæˈstrɑnəmi / (AmE), / ɡəˈstrɒnəmi / (BrE)
the style of cooking characteristic of a particular region or culture
regional or cultural style of cooking

Examples

French gastronomy is renowned for its rich flavors and intricate techniques.
He spent years studying the gastronomy of Southeast Asia.
The restaurant showcases modern takes on traditional Italian gastronomy.

Synset Examples

New England gastronomy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cuisine - a style or method of cooking, especially as characteristic of a particular country, region, or establishment

Etymology

From French 'gastronomie', from Greek 'gastēr' (stomach) and 'nomos' (law).

2. NOUN

/ gæˈstrɑːnəmi / (AmE), / gæˈstrɒnəmi / (BrE)
the art and practice of preparing, choosing, and enjoying good food
the art of good food and eating

Examples

He has a deep interest in gastronomy and loves to experiment with new recipes.
The city is famous for its vibrant gastronomy and culinary culture.
Studying gastronomy opened her eyes to the history and science behind different foods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cuisine - a style or method of cooking, especially as characteristic of a particular country or region

Etymology

From French gastronomie, from Greek gastēr (stomach) and nomos (law, custom).
