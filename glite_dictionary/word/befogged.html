befogged

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈfɑgd / (AmE), / bɪˈfɒgd / (BrE)
unable to think clearly, especially as a result of alcohol, confusion, or doubt
mentally clouded, especially by drink or confusion

Examples

His mind was befogged after several glasses of wine.
She wandered through the day in a befogged state, unable to focus on her tasks.
The report was written in such befogged language that no one could understand it.

Synset Examples

the wino's poor befuddled mind
a mind befogged with drink

Related Terms

  • Synonym: befuddled - confused and unable to think clearly, often due to intoxication

Etymology

From be- (prefix) + fog (mist, confusion).
