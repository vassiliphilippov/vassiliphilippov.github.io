avionic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæviˈɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌæviˈɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to the electronic systems used in aircraft, spacecraft, and satellites.
relating to electronic systems in aircraft

Examples

The engineer specializes in avionic systems for commercial airplanes.
Upgrading the avionic equipment improved the aircraft's navigation and communication.
Recent advances in avionic technology have made space missions more reliable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avionics - the science and technology of the development and use of electronic systems in aircraft

Etymology

From 'avionics' (aviation + electronics).
