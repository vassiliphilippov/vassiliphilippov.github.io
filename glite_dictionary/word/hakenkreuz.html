Hakenkreuz

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑː.kənˌkrɔɪts / (AmE), / ˈhɑː.kənˌkrɔɪts / (BrE)
the official emblem of the Nazi Party and the Third Reich, consisting of a cross with its arms bent at right angles in a clockwise direction (swastika)
Nazi emblem of bent-armed cross

Examples

During the 1930s, the Hakenkreuz became a symbol of the Nazi regime.
Many artifacts from the era are marked with the infamous Hakenkreuz.
The museum exhibit included several items displaying the Hakenkreuz as part of historical documentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swastika - an ancient symbol in the form of a cross with bent arms; also used as the Nazi emblem

Etymology

German, from 'haken' (hook) + 'kreuz' (cross).
