bookbindery

1. NOUN

/ bʊkˈbaɪndəri / (AmE), / bʊkˈbaɪndəri / (BrE)
A workshop or establishment where books are bound.
workshop for binding books

Examples

The old bookbindery downtown still repairs rare volumes by hand.
She apprenticed at a bookbindery to learn traditional binding techniques.
The museum displayed equipment from a nineteenth-century bookbindery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bookbinder - a person who binds books as a profession

Etymology

From 'bookbinder' + '-y', denoting a place associated with a particular activity.
