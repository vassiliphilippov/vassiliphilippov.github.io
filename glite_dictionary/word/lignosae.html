lignosae

1. NOUN

/ lɪɡˈnoʊsi / (AmE), / lɪɡˈnəʊsi / (BrE)
a category of woody plants formerly used in some early plant taxonomies, especially in historical botanical literature
historical category of woody plants

Examples

The lignosae were contrasted with herbaceous plants in some older botanical classifications.
Early plant taxonomists placed oaks and maples in the lignosae.
References to lignosae can still be found in historical botany texts.

Etymology

From Latin 'lignosus' (woody), used in botanical Latin for plant groupings.
