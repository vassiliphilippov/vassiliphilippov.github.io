semidetached

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛmidɪˈtætʃt / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈdɪtʃt / (BrE)
(of a house) joined to another house on one side only.
adjoined on one side only (usually of a house)

Examples

They moved into a new semidetached house in the suburbs.
A semidetached home often provides more privacy than a terraced property.
Their semidetached house shares a wall with the neighbor's property.

Synset Examples

a semidetached house

Related Terms

  • Antonym: detached - a house that stands alone, not joined to another
  • Antonym: terraced - a house joined to others on both sides in a row

Etymology

From semi- (meaning 'half' or 'partial') + detached.
