Puebla City

1. PROPN

/ ˈpwɛb.lə ˈsɪ.ti / (AmE), / ˈpwɛb.lə ˈsɪ.ti / (BrE)
a city in south central Mexico, southeast of Mexico City, located on the edge of the central Mexican plateau
major city in south central Mexico

Examples

The food in Puebla City is famous for its unique blend of indigenous and colonial influences.
Many tourists visit Puebla City to admire its historic colonial architecture and vibrant culture.
From Puebla City, you can see the nearby Popocatépetl volcano on a clear day.

Etymology

From Spanish Puebla (full name: Puebla de Zaragoza), meaning 'settlement' or 'colony'.
