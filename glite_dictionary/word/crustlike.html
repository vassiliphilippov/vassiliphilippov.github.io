crustlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrʌst.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈkrʌst.laɪk / (BrE)
having a hard outer layer or coating resembling a crust
resembling a crust in appearance or texture

Examples

The bread had a crustlike surface after hours in the oven.
Some lichens form a crustlike growth on rocks and tree bark.
The mineral deposit had developed a crustlike layer over the years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crusty - having a hard outer layer or texture like a crust
