well-spoken

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛl ˌspoʊkən / (AmE), / ˈwɛl ˌspəʊkən / (BrE)
speaking in a fluent, articulate, and pleasing manner
articulate and pleasing in speech

Examples

She was so well-spoken that everyone listened intently to her ideas.
The job required a well-spoken individual to represent the company at public events.
Although young, he is remarkably well-spoken for his age.

Synset Examples

a well-spoken gentleman
a few well-spoken words on civic pride

Related Terms

  • Synonym: articulate - able to speak fluently and coherently
  • Antonym: inarticulate - unable to express oneself clearly
