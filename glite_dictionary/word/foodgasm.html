foodgasm

1. NOUN

/ ˈfuːdˌɡæzəm / (AmE), / ˈfuːdˌɡæzəm / (BrE)
An intense feeling of pleasure or delight triggered by eating or seeing delicious food.
extreme pleasure from food
informal slang

Examples

The chocolate lava cake gave me a serious foodgasm last night.
Her Instagram is full of drool-worthy dishes that could trigger a foodgasm in any foodie.
I think I just experienced a foodgasm after tasting that truffle pasta.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: orgasm - peak of sexual pleasure

Etymology

Blend of 'food' and 'orgasm', modelled on the sense of overwhelming pleasure.
