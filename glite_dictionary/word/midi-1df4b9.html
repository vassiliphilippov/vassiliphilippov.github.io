Midi

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːdi / (AmE), / ˈmiːdi / (BrE)
the southern part of France, especially as a distinct region known for its climate and culture
the southern region of France

Examples

Many tourists are drawn to the Midi for its warm weather and beautiful landscapes.
She spent her summer traveling through the picturesque villages of the Midi.
Cuisine in the Midi is closely tied to Mediterranean traditions.

Etymology

From French 'Midi', literally meaning 'midday' or 'south'.
