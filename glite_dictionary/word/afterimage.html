afterimage

1. NOUN

/ ˈæf.tɚˌɪ.mɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɑːf.təˌɪ.mɪdʒ / (BrE)
an image that continues to appear in the eyes after looking away from the original stimulus, often as a negative image
image that lingers after stimulus

Examples

Staring at the bright light left a green afterimage lingering in my vision.
Sometimes, a camera flash creates an afterimage that can last for several seconds.
When he looked away from the painting, he could still see its afterimage on the wall.

Etymology

Formed from 'after' + 'image'.
