nebule

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛb.jul / (AmE), / ˈnɛb.juːl / (BrE)
A small cloud or cloud-like formation, often used in poetic or literary contexts.
a small cloud

Examples

Early in the morning, a pale nebule drifted over the hills.
The painter added a delicate nebule to the sky in her landscape.
A single nebule lingered above the quiet valley as dusk approached.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cloud - a visible mass of condensed water vapor floating in the atmosphere

Etymology

From Latin 'nebula' meaning 'mist' or 'cloud'.
