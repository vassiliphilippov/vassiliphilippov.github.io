dissolvable

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈzɑːlvəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈzɒlvəbəl / (BrE)
able to be dissolved, especially in liquid
capable of being dissolved

Examples

The packaging is made from a dissolvable film that disappears in water.
She preferred dissolvable tablets because they are easier to swallow.
This type of glue is not dissolvable in oil, only in water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soluble - able to be dissolved, especially in water
  • Antonym: insoluble - impossible to dissolve

Etymology

From "dissolve" (originating from Latin dissolvere 'to loosen, break up') + -able suffix.
