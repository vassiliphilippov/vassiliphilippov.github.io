Lincocin

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪŋkəʊsɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋkəʊsɪn / (BrE)
A trade name for lincomycin, an antibiotic obtained from Streptomyces bacteria, used to treat infections resistant to penicillin.
trade name for lincomycin antibiotic
medical

Examples

The patient was prescribed Lincocin to treat their bacterial infection.
Some doctors choose Lincocin when patients are allergic to penicillin.
In severe cases, Lincocin is given by injection for effective results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lincomycin - the generic name for the antibiotic Lincocin
