mastoidal

1. ADJ

/ mæˈstɔɪ.dəl / (AmE), / mæˈstɔɪ.dəl / (BrE)
relating to or resembling the mastoid process (the nipple-shaped bone behind the ear), or having a nipple-like shape.
relating to the mastoid process or nipple-like shape
medical

Examples

The surgeon examined the mastoidal region for signs of infection.
Inflammation of the mastoidal cells often indicates mastoiditis.
The skull had a clearly visible mastoidal process at the base.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mastoid - the bone behind the ear with a nipple-like shape

Etymology

From Greek *mastos* (breast) + -oid (resembling) + -al (adjectival suffix).
