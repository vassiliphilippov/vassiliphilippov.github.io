jittering

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɪt̬.ɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪt.ər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
Undergoing small, rapid variations or movements.
small rapid variations or movements

Examples

The image on the screen was jittering due to a poor connection.
He noticed his hands were jittering after drinking too much coffee.
The data signal is jittering, causing interruptions in the video call.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jitter - small, rapid, irregular movements
