unraveler

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈrævələr / (AmE), / ʌnˈravələ / (BrE)
a person who untangles something or solves a complicated situation
person who untangles or solves problems

Examples

She quickly became the unraveler of complicated family disputes, always finding a peaceful solution.
The detective was known as the unraveler of mysteries in his small town.
In the old fairy tale, the unraveler freed the kingdom from the cursed knot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solver - person who finds solutions to problems
  • Antonym: tangler - person who tangles or makes something tangled

Etymology

From the verb 'unravel' (to untangle or solve), formed with the agent noun suffix '-er'.
