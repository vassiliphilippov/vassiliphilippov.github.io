spoopy

1. ADJ

/ ˈspuːpi / (AmE), / ˈspuːpi / (BrE)
Both funny and spooky at the same time; amusing in a way that is also mildly frightening.
funny and spooky at once (slang)
informal slang

Examples

That Halloween decoration is so spoopy—it has googly eyes and fake cobwebs everywhere!
The film mixed horror and comedy perfectly, making it more spoopy than scary.
Every October, people post their favorite spoopy memes on social media.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: spooky - frightening in a strange or unnatural way

Etymology

Blend of 'spooky' and 'silly' or 'goofy', popularized from a viral internet image in the early 2010s.
