mesomorphic

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɛzəˈmɔr.fɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɛzəˈmɔː.fɪk / (BrE)
having a robust, muscular physique with well-developed bone and muscle, typically associated with people whose body builds are classified as mesomorphs.
having a muscular or robust body type

Examples

He has a mesomorphic build, making him excel naturally in sports.
The mesomorphic physique is often idealized in body-building magazines.
Her mesomorphic body type allows her to gain muscle quickly with training.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ectomorphic - having a lean, thin body type
  • Antonym: endomorphic - having a soft, rounded body type

Etymology

From Greek 'mesos' (middle) + 'morphē' (form, shape).
