Artie Shaw

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑːrti ʃɔ / (AmE), / ˈɑːti ʃɔː / (BrE)
(1910–2004) American clarinetist and bandleader, prominent in the swing era of jazz music.
American swing clarinetist and bandleader

Examples

Artie Shaw was renowned for his skill on the clarinet during the swing era.
Many jazz historians consider Artie Shaw to be one of the greatest bandleaders of his time.
Songs recorded by Artie Shaw remain popular among jazz enthusiasts today.
