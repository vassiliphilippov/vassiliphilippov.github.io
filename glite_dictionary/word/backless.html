backless

1. ADJ

/ ˈbækləs / (AmE), / ˈbæklɪs / (BrE)
(of a chair, garment, etc.) lacking a back or a part that covers the back.
lacking a back

Examples

She wore a stunning backless dress to the party.
The cafe had only backless stools along the counter.
A backless bench can be uncomfortable if you need to sit for a long time.

Synset Examples

a stool is a backless and armless seat
