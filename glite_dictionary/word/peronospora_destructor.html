Peronospora destructor

1. NOUN

/ pɛˌroʊnəˈspɔrə dɪˈstrʌktər / (AmE), / pɛˌrəʊnəˈspɔːrə dɪˈstrʌktə / (BrE)
a fungus that causes downy mildew in onions, leading to plant disease and crop loss
fungus causing onion downy mildew
medical

Examples

The spread of Peronospora destructor can devastate entire onion crops if not promptly treated.
Researchers developed new resistant onion varieties to combat infections caused by Peronospora destructor.
Farmers monitor their fields closely for signs of Peronospora destructor during the growing season.

Etymology

From New Latin: genus name 'Peronospora' (from Greek 'peronē' meaning 'belt', and 'spora' meaning 'seed' or 'spore'), species epithet 'destructor' from Latin meaning 'destroyer.'
