Chateura

1. NOUN

/ tʃəˈtʊərə / (AmE), / tʃəˈtjʊərə / (BrE)
A genus of small swifts in the family Apodidae, including species such as the chimney swift.
genus of small swifts (birds)

Examples

The Chateura genus includes several swift species found throughout the Americas.
Scientists have studied the migratory habits of birds in the Chateura genus.
The chimney swift, a member of Chateura, is well known for nesting in chimneys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chimney swift - a species of swift found in the Americas, belonging to the genus Chateura

genus of swifts (birds)

Examples
