night-robe

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪtˌroʊb / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt.rəʊb / (BrE)
a loose dress worn by women in bed; a nightgown
a loose women's dress for sleeping

Examples

She wore a silk night-robe as she walked quietly through the hallway.
The old photograph showed her grandmother in a lace-trimmed night-robe.
After her bath, she slipped into a comfortable cotton night-robe before going to bed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nightgown - a woman's loose-fitting dress worn in bed
  • Synonym: negligee - a woman's light dressing gown, typically made of a soft, sheer fabric

Etymology

From 'night' + 'robe', both of Middle English origin.
