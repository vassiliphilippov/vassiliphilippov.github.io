Panthera tigris

1. NOUN

/ pænˈθɪrə ˈtaɪɡrɪs / (AmE), / panˈθɪərə ˈtaɪɡrɪs / (BrE)
the scientific name for the tiger, a large wild cat with a tawny coat and black stripes found in Asia
scientific name for the tiger

Examples

The Panthera tigris is listed as an endangered species due to habitat loss and poaching.
Researchers studied the habitat preferences of Panthera tigris in the Sundarbans region.
Conservation efforts for Panthera tigris have increased over the past decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiger - large Asian wild cat with black stripes

Etymology

From Latin; genus Panthera (panther) and specific epithet tigris (tiger).
