coonhound

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːnˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈkuːnˌhaʊnd / (BrE)
Any of several breeds of hound developed for hunting raccoons, known for their tracking ability and endurance.
breed of hound for hunting raccoons

Examples

The coonhound followed the raccoon's trail deep into the forest.
Many hunters value the coonhound for its keen sense of smell and stamina.
After the competition, her prize-winning coonhound was featured in the local newspaper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hound - a type of dog bred for hunting or tracking

Etymology

From 'coon', a shortened form of 'raccoon', and 'hound'.
