rubicund

1. ADJ

/ ˈruːbɪkʌnd / (AmE), / ˈruːbɪkənd / (BrE)
(of a person's face or complexion) having a healthy reddish color; rosy or ruddy.
having a healthy reddish color

Examples

The old sailor's rubicund face glowed as he laughed heartily.
Her grandfather's rubicund cheeks hinted at a life spent outdoors.
Santa Claus is often depicted with a rubicund complexion, symbolizing his merry nature.

Synset Examples

a ruddy complexion
Santa's rubicund cheeks
a fresh and sanguine complexion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ruddy - (of a person’s face) having a reddish color
  • Antonym: pale - lacking color in the face; not rosy

Etymology

From Latin 'rubicundus' (reddish), from 'ruber' (red).
