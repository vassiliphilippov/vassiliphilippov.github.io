groomsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡruːmzˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈɡruːmzmən / (BrE)
a male friend or relative who assists the bridegroom at a wedding ceremony
male attendant at a wedding

Examples

His best friend was chosen to be the groomsman at his wedding.
Each groomsman wore a matching navy blue suit for the ceremony.
As a groomsman, he helped organize the bachelor party and supported the groom.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bridesmaid - a female attendant of the bride at a wedding

Etymology

From 'groom' (bridegroom) + 'man'.
