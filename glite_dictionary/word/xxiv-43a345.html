xxiv

1. NUM

/ ɛks ɛks aɪ ˈviː / (AmE), / ɛks ɛks aɪ ˈviː / (BrE)
the Roman numeral representing the number twenty-four (24)
Roman numeral for 24

Examples

The book's chapter was labeled xxiv in Roman numerals.
He wore a jersey with xxiv on the back during the final match.
The monument was unveiled on May xxiv, 1913.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: xxiii - Roman numeral for 23
  • Degree Higher: xxv - Roman numeral for 25

Etymology

From Latin numerals: X (10) + X (10) + IV (4) = 24.
