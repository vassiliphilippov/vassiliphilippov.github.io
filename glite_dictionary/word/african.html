African

1. ADJ

/ ˈæf.rɪ.kən / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kən / (BrE)
Of or relating to Africa or its peoples or cultures.
related to Africa or its peoples

Examples

The museum features a rich collection of African art and historical artifacts.
She studied several African languages during her time at university.
The African continent is known for its diverse cultures and landscapes.

Synset Examples

African languages

Etymology

From Latin Africanus, meaning 'of Africa'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈæfrɪkən / (AmE), / ˈæfrɪkən / (BrE)
A person who is a native or inhabitant of Africa.
native or inhabitant of Africa

Examples

The museum's new exhibit features art from contemporary African artists.
As an African, she takes great pride in her cultural heritage.
Many Africans speak more than one language from a young age.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: African - of or relating to Africa or its peoples (adjective)

Etymology

From Latin 'Africanus', meaning 'of Africa'.
