inattentively

1. ADV

/ ˌɪn.əˈtɛn.tɪv.li / (AmE), / ˌɪn.əˈtɛn.tɪv.li / (BrE)
In a manner that shows a lack of attention or concentration; absentmindedly or heedlessly.
in a way that lacks attention

Examples

During the lecture, he inattentively doodled in his notebook instead of taking notes.
She listened inattentively to the instructions and made several mistakes later.
The student answered the question inattentively, barely looking up from her phone.

Synset Examples

he read the letter absently

Related Terms

  • Antonym: attentively - in a manner showing attention or focus

Etymology

From 'inattentive' + '-ly'; 'inattentive' from Latin 'in-' (not) + 'attentivus' (attentive).
