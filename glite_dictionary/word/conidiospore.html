conidiospore

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈnɪdi.oʊˌspɔr / (AmE), / kəʊˈnɪdɪ.əˌspɔː / (BrE)
An asexually produced fungal spore that forms on a conidiophore.
asexual fungal spore formed on a conidiophore
medical

Examples

The fungus releases conidiospores into the air during dry weather conditions.
Examining the slide, the biologist identified several conidiospores on the conidiophores.
Some molds reproduce rapidly by producing large quantities of conidiospores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spore - a reproductive cell capable of developing into a new individual

Etymology

From New Latin 'conidium' (a type of spore) + 'spore'.
