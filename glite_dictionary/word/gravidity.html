gravidity

1. NOUN

/ ɡrəˈvɪdəti / (AmE), / ɡrəˈvɪdəti / (BrE)
The state of being pregnant, or the total number of times a female has been pregnant.
state or number of pregnancies
medical

Examples

The patient’s gravidity and parity were noted during the prenatal examination.
A woman with a gravidity of three has been pregnant three times, regardless of the outcome.
Medical records often document both gravidity and parity to summarize a woman's reproductive history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parity - the number of times a female has given birth to a viable offspring

Etymology

From Latin 'graviditas' (pregnancy), from 'gravidus' (pregnant).
