huddler

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌd.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈhʌd.lə / (BrE)
a person who is part of a huddle, especially in sports or group discussions
person in a huddle

Examples

The coach asked each huddler to share their strategy for the next play.
As the huddlers whispered, the opposing team tried to guess their plans.
She was the newest huddler in the group and listened closely to the discussion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: huddle - a close gathering of people, often for discussion
