to procreate

1. VERB

/ ˈproʊ.kri.eɪt / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.kri.eɪt / (BrE)
to produce offspring; to reproduce and have children or young
to reproduce; have offspring

Examples

Many animals procreate during the spring when food is abundant.
Some couples choose not to procreate and focus on their careers instead.
These rare plants can procreate both by seeds and by sending out runners.

Synset Examples

The Bible tells people to procreate
These bacteria reproduce

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to reproduce - to produce offspring; to multiply biologically
  • Antonym: to sterilize - to make incapable of reproducing

Etymology

From Latin procreare, meaning 'to beget, produce'.
