tical

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈtɪkəl / (BrE)
an old term for the baht, the basic unit of money in Thailand.
former Thai monetary unit

Examples

During his travels in Siam, he exchanged dollars for tical to use locally.
Antique coins such as the tical are prized by collectors of Asian currency.
The merchant quoted a price of twenty tical for the handwoven cloth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baht - the main unit of currency in Thailand

Etymology

From Thai ตะกั่ว (takua), formerly referring to a unit of weight and currency.
