seashell

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːˌʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈsiːʃɛl / (BrE)
the shell of a marine animal, especially one found on a beach
shell of a marine animal

Examples

She collected a beautiful seashell while walking along the shore.
There was a seashell embedded in the sand next to the pier.
Children often enjoy looking for seashells at the beach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shell - the hard outer covering of an animal, such as a mollusk or crustacean
