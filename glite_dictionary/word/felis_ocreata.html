Felis ocreata

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːlɪs oʊˈkriːətə / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪs ɒˈkriːətə / (BrE)
A widely distributed species of wildcat found in Africa and Asia Minor.
African and Asian wildcat species

Examples

The Felis ocreata is thought to be an ancestor of the domestic cat.
Researchers studied the habitat preferences of Felis ocreata across different African regions.
Sightings of Felis ocreata are rare due to its elusive nature.

Etymology

From Latin: 'Felis' (cat) and 'ocreata' (wearing greaves, referring to leg markings).
