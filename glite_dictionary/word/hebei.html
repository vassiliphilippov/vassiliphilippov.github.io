Hebei

1. PROPN

/ heɪˈbeɪ / (AmE), / heɪˈbeɪ / (BrE)
a province in northeastern China, surrounding Beijing and Tianjin
province in northeastern China

Examples

Many factories in Hebei supply goods to cities across China.
The Great Wall passes through parts of Hebei province.
She traveled to Hebei to visit her grandparents last summer.

Etymology

From Mandarin 河北 (Héběi), meaning 'north of the river'.
