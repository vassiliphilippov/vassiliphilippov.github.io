outdoorsy

1. ADJ

/ aʊtˈdɔrzi / (AmE), / aʊtˈdɔːzi / (BrE)
enjoying or suited to outdoor activities or life in the open air
fond of or suitable for outdoor activities

Examples

She's a very outdoorsy person and loves hiking and camping on weekends.
The company is looking for someone who is outdoorsy to lead nature tours.
His outdoorsy lifestyle keeps him physically fit and mentally refreshed.

Synset Examples

a rugged outdoorsy life

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outdoor - situated, occurring, or used outside a building

Etymology

Formed from 'outdoors' (in the open air) with the suffix '-y'.
