RIRA

1. PROPN

/ ˌɑrˈaɪˌɑrˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɑːˈaɪˌɑːˈeɪ / (BrE)
A dissident Irish republican paramilitary group formed in 1997, known for opposing peace agreements and continuing militant activities after the Provisional IRA ceasefire.
Irish republican paramilitary group (est. 1997)

Examples

The RIRA claimed responsibility for the bombing in Omagh.
Authorities attribute recent attacks to members of the RIRA.
Peace talks were disrupted by threats from the RIRA.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Provisional IRA - the main Irish republican paramilitary group prior to RIRA

Etymology

Acronym for Real Irish Republican Army.
