prototype

1. NOUN

/ ˈproʊ.təˌtaɪp / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.tə.taɪp / (BrE)
a person or thing that serves as an original model or typical example for others to follow or be based on
original model; typical example

Examples

She is often considered the prototype of a self-made entrepreneur.
The earliest prototype of the machine was created in 1901.
That character became the prototype for many heroes in modern literature.

Synset Examples

he is the prototype of good breeding
he provided America with an image of the good father

Related Terms

  • Synonym: archetype - an original model or typical example
  • Synonym: paradigm - a typical example or pattern of something

Etymology

From Greek prōtotypon (original form), from prōtos (first) + typos (model, type).
