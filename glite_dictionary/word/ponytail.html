ponytail

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊniˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈpəʊniteɪl / (BrE)
A hairstyle in which the hair is drawn back and tied so that it hangs down at the back of the head, resembling the tail of a pony.
hair tied at the back of the head

Examples

She tied her hair into a ponytail before going for a run.
A high ponytail is both practical and stylish for summer days.
His ponytail reached halfway down his back.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bun - hairstyle in which the hair is twisted into a round knot at the back or top of the head
  • Synonym: braid - hairstyle in which the hair is interwoven into strands

Etymology

From 'pony' and 'tail', referring to the resemblance to a pony's tail; US English, mid-20th century.
