comicality

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːmɪˈkæləti / (AmE), / ˌkɒmɪˈkælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being comical or amusing
the state of being comical

Examples

The comicality of the performance had the entire audience laughing nonstop.
He spoke with such comicality that even the most serious listeners smiled.
Some films rely on slapstick comicality rather than witty dialogue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: funny - amusing; causing laughter

Etymology

From 'comical' + '-ity', ultimately from Latin 'comicus' (comic) + '-itas' (forming nouns).
