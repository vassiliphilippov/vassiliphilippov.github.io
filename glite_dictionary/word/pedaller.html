pedaller

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛdəlɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɛdələ / (BrE)
a person who rides a pedal-driven vehicle, especially a bicycle
person who rides a bicycle or pedal vehicle
british

Examples

The oldest pedaller in the race finished with an impressive time.
Each pedaller must wear a helmet during the event.
She became a daily pedaller after moving closer to her job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cyclist - a person who rides a bicycle

Etymology

From 'pedal' + '-er', denoting someone who pedals.
