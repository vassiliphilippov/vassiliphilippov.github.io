straplike

1. ADJ

/ ˈstræpˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈstræp.laɪk / (BrE)
long and narrow in shape, resembling a strap
long and narrow like a strap

Examples

The plant had straplike leaves that reached nearly a meter in length.
Her scarf was long and straplike, perfect for wrapping around twice.
Many grasses have straplike blades that sway easily in the wind.

Synset Examples

straplike leaves

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ribbonlike - long, thin, and flat like a ribbon
