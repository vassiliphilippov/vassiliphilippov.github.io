coolie

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːli / (AmE), / ˈkuːli / (BrE)
(offensive, dated) A derogatory term for an unskilled Asian laborer, especially one from China or India, used as an ethnic slur.
offensive term for Asian laborer
offensive

Examples

The word coolie is now considered deeply offensive and should not be used.
Historical records sometimes refer to Asian workers as coolies, reflecting colonial attitudes of the time.
He was shocked to learn the origins and usage of the term coolie in old documents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laborer - a person doing unskilled manual work

Etymology

From Hindi *kūlī*, meaning day laborer, possibly of Tamil origin.
