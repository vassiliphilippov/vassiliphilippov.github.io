acceptableness

1. NOUN

/ əkˈsɛptəblnəs / (AmE), / əkˈsɛptəblnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being acceptable; satisfactoriness according to approved standards.
the quality of being acceptable

Examples

The acceptableness of his proposal was discussed at length during the meeting.
Some questioned the acceptableness of the comedian's jokes in such a formal setting.
The teacher stressed the importance of the acceptableness of written assignments in terms of both content and format.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acceptability - the quality of being accepted or acceptable

Etymology

From 'acceptable' + '-ness', meaning the state or quality of being acceptable.
