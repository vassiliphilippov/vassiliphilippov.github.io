pigeon-breasted

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɪdʒən ˌbrɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɪdʒɪn ˌbrɛstɪd / (BrE)
Having a chest with a noticeably projecting breastbone, typically as a result of rickets or other bone disorders.
having a projecting breastbone
medical

Examples

The child was described as pigeon-breasted due to the protruding shape of his chest.
Treatment was recommended for the patient’s pigeon-breasted deformity.
Some cases of pigeon-breasted appearance resolve with proper nutrition and care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barrel-chested - having a broad, rounded chest; a chest shape with increased anteroposterior diameter
