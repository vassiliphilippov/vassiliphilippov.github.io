snowboarding

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnoʊˌbɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌbɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of sliding down a snow-covered slope on a snowboard while standing up
sport of sliding down snow on a snowboard

Examples

He has enjoyed snowboarding since he was a teenager.
Many tourists visit the mountains each winter specifically for snowboarding.
Learning the basics of snowboarding can be challenging but rewarding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skiing - the sport of gliding on skis over snow
  • Synonym: surfing - the sport of riding on ocean waves with a surfboard

Etymology

From 'snowboard' + '-ing', denoting the activity or sport.
