salubrious

1. ADJ

/ səˈluːbriəs / (AmE), / səˈljuːbriəs / (BrE)
(of a place or environment) favorable to or promoting health; health-giving.
promoting health; health-giving

Examples

The hotel's salubrious surroundings made it a perfect retreat for the weekend.
They moved to a more salubrious suburb with cleaner air and plenty of parks.
Despite its salubrious climate, the town remained largely undiscovered by tourists.

Synset Examples

not the most salubrious campsite
one of the less salubrious suburbs

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unhealthy - harmful to health
  • Synonym: healthy - beneficial to health

Etymology

From Latin salubris (healthful), from salus (health).
