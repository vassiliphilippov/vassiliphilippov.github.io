fourthly

1. ADV

/ ˈfɔrθli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːθli / (BrE)
Used to introduce the fourth point or item in a list or sequence.
introducing the fourth point in a list

Examples

Fourthly, you must pay the rent on time every month to avoid penalties.
He explained his proposal step by step: firstly, secondly, thirdly, and fourthly, he outlined the timeline.
Fourthly, we should consider the environmental impact before making a decision.

Synset Examples

fourthly, you must pay the rent on the first of the month

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirdly - Used to introduce the third point in a list
  • Degree Higher: fifthly - Used to introduce the fifth point in a list

Etymology

Formed from 'fourth' + '-ly', patterned after 'firstly', 'secondly', and 'thirdly'.

2. ADV

/ ˈfɔrθli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːθli / (BrE)
Used to introduce the fourth point in a list of reasons, arguments, or items.
introducing the fourth item in a list

Examples

Fourthly, you must pay the rent on the first of the month.
The report discussed the main findings; fourthly, it outlined recommendations for improvement.
He thanked his family; fourthly, he expressed gratitude to his teachers.

Synset Examples

fourthly, you must pay the rent on the first of the month

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: firstly - used to introduce the first point in a list
  • Degree Lower: secondly - used to introduce the second point in a list
  • Degree Lower: thirdly - used to introduce the third point in a list
  • Degree Higher: fifthly - used to introduce the fifth point in a list

Etymology

Formed from 'fourth' + '-ly', following the pattern of ordinal numbers for listing points.

Source: WordNet
