Uppsala

1. PROPN

/ ˈʊpˌsɑlə / (AmE), / ˈʌpsɑːlə / (BrE)
A city in east central Sweden, northwest of Stockholm, known for its historic university and cathedral.
city in east central Sweden

Examples

Thousands of students attend the prestigious university in Uppsala each year.
The cathedral in Uppsala is one of Sweden's most famous landmarks.
