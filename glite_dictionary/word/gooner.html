gooner

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːnər / (AmE), / ˈɡuːnə / (BrE)
an enthusiastic supporter of Arsenal Football Club, especially used by or about its fans
Arsenal Football Club fan
slang

Examples

He has been a dedicated gooner since childhood.
Every gooner in the pub erupted when Arsenal scored the winning goal.
The gooner wore his team's jersey proudly at the match.

Synset Examples

The gooners filled the stadium with chants of support.

Etymology

Derived from 'The Gunners', the nickname of Arsenal Football Club.
