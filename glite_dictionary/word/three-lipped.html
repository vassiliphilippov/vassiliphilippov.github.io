three-lipped

1. ADJ

/ ˈθriː.lɪpt / (AmE), / ˈθriː.lɪpt / (BrE)
(of a flower or structure) having three distinct lips or lip-like parts.
having three lips or lip-like parts

Examples

The orchid is easily recognized by its three-lipped blossoms.
Botanists noted the unique three-lipped structure of the flower's corolla.
Some tropical plants produce three-lipped petals that help attract pollinators.
