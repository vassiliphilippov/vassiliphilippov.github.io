undersea

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɚˈsi / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈsiː / (BrE)
Located, occurring, or used beneath the surface of the sea.
beneath the surface of the sea

Examples

The team explored an undersea cave system during their expedition.
Scientists have discovered new species in the undersea thermal vents.
The company specializes in undersea cable installation for international communication.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: submarine - existing or operated under the sea
