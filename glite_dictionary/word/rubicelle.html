rubicelle

1. NOUN

/ ˌruː.bɪˈsɛl / (AmE), / ˌruː.bɪˈsɛl / (BrE)
A yellow or orange variety of ruby spinel used as a gemstone.
yellow or orange spinel gemstone

Examples

She wore a necklace adorned with a stunning rubicelle centerpiece.
The jeweler identified the orange gemstone as a rare rubicelle.
Collectors prize rubicelle for its unique, vivid coloration among spinels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spinel - a hard glassy mineral occurring as octahedral crystals in metamorphic rocks and in the gravels of stream beds
  • Synonym: ruby - a precious stone consisting of corundum in color varieties varying from deep crimson or purple to pale rose

Etymology

From French 'rubicelle', diminutive of 'rubis' (ruby), reflecting the stone's color.
