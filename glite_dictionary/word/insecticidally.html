insecticidally

1. ADV

/ ɪnˌsɛktɪˈsaɪdəli / (AmE), / ɪnˌsɛktɪˈsaɪdəli / (BrE)
By means of or relating to the use of an insecticide.
by use of an insecticide

Examples

The crops were treated insecticidally to prevent pest infestations.
Certain plants are rarely sprayed insecticidally because they attract beneficial insects.
The field must be monitored to determine when to intervene insecticidally.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insecticide - a substance used for killing insects

Etymology

From 'insecticide' + '-ally', forming an adverb.
