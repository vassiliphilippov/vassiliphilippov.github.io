quickie

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɪki / (AmE), / ˈkwɪki / (BrE)
A task, especially a repair, done quickly and with little attention to detail, often because of time constraints.
hurried repair or quick job

Examples

The mechanic gave my car a quickie to get it running until I could schedule a full repair.
We had to do a quickie on the leaking faucet before the guests arrived.
That shelf looks unstable, probably because it was hung up as a quickie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patch - a temporary or quick fix to something broken
