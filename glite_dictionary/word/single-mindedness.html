single-mindedness

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪŋɡəlˈmaɪndɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋɡlˈmaɪndɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being focused on one purpose or goal without being distracted
quality of being strongly focused on one goal

Examples

Her single-mindedness helped her win the championship despite many obstacles.
He approached his studies with remarkable single-mindedness and discipline.
The single-mindedness of the campaign left no room for doubt or distraction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: determination - the quality of being firm in purpose
  • Synonym: tenacity - the quality of being persistent or stubborn in achieving something
  • Antonym: indecisiveness - the quality of having difficulty in making decisions or lacking clear purpose

Etymology

From 'single-minded' (having one purpose) + '-ness' (forming nouns of quality).
