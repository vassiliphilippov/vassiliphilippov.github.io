Friday

1. NOUN

/ ˈfraɪdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈfraɪdeɪ / (BrE)
The day of the week between Thursday and Saturday, typically regarded as the fifth day of the working week.
sixth day of the week

Examples

Friday is always my favorite day because the weekend is just beginning.
We have a meeting scheduled for next Friday at 10 a.m.
On Fridays, the office closes early for everyone.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Thursday - the day before Friday
  • Degree Higher: Saturday - the day after Friday

Etymology

From Old English Frīgedæg, meaning 'day of Frige' (a Germanic goddess associated with Venus).
