sportiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrti.nɪs / (AmE), / ˈspɔːti.nɪs / (BrE)
The quality or characteristic of being sporty, especially in appearance, behavior, or style.
quality of being sporty

Examples

Her sportiness was evident in her choice of comfortable sneakers and tracksuits.
The car's sportiness appeals to younger drivers looking for something stylish and fun.
He admired her sportiness and enthusiasm during the company softball game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sporty - having or displaying a lively, athletic appearance or style

Etymology

From 'sporty' (from 'sport') + '-ness', a suffix forming nouns denoting quality or state.
