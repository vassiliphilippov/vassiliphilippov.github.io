Johann Strauss II

1. PROPN

/ joʊˌhɑn ˈstraʊs səˈkənd / (AmE), / jəʊˌhan ˈstraʊs səˈkənd / (BrE)
(1825–1899) An Austrian composer known as the 'waltz king', famous for his many waltzes and dance music, and the son of Johann Strauss the Elder.
Austrian composer of waltzes, the 'waltz king'

Examples

Many people consider Johann Strauss II the greatest composer of Viennese waltzes.
The Blue Danube, composed by Johann Strauss II, remains popular at New Year's concerts.
Music by Johann Strauss II fills concert halls during the Vienna Ball Season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Johann Strauss I - Austrian composer, father of Johann Strauss II
