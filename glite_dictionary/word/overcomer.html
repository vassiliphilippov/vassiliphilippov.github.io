overcomer

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊvərˈkʌmər / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkʌmə / (BrE)
a person who succeeds in dealing with or gaining control over difficulties or opposition, often by determination or strength
person who overcomes adversity or opposition

Examples

Despite many setbacks, she became an overcomer and inspired others with her perseverance.
The program is designed to help every participant see themselves as an overcomer rather than a victim.
History celebrates overcomers who rise above adversity and lead positive change.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: victor - person who wins or defeats opponents
  • Antonym: loser - person who is defeated or fails to win

Etymology

From overcome + -er, formed in Middle English to describe someone who overcomes.
