largemouth bass

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑrdʒˌmaʊθ ˌbæs / (AmE), / ˈlɑːdʒˌmaʊθ ˌbæs / (BrE)
The flesh or meat of the largemouth bass, used as food.
meat of largemouth bass (fish)

Examples

The chef grilled the largemouth bass and served it with fresh herbs and lemon.
Fried largemouth bass has become a specialty at many lakeside restaurants.
He caught several largemouth bass and prepared them for dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bass - the flesh of the bass fish, used as food
