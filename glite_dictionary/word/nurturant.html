nurturant

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɜrtʃərənt / (AmE), / ˈnɜːtʃərənt / (BrE)
providing care and nourishment, especially in a physical or emotional sense.
providing care and nourishment

Examples

Her nurturant attitude made her students feel supported and valued.
In a nurturant environment, children are more likely to thrive and grow.
The nurturant care he received helped him recover quickly from his illness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nurturing - giving care and encouragement to others

Etymology

From Latin 'nutrire' (to nourish), via nurture + -ant.
