Austrian

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔstriən / (AmE), / ˈɒstriən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Austria
a native or resident of Austria

Examples

An Austrian greeted us at the hotel reception desk.
Many Austrians enjoy skiing in the Alps during winter.
Wolfgang Mozart was a famous Austrian composer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Austrian - of or relating to Austria, its people, or language (adjective meaning)

Etymology

From Austria + -an (denoting nationalities or inhabitants).

/ ˈɔstriən / (AmE), / ˈɒstriən / (BrE)
of or relating to Austria, its people, or its culture.
of Austria or its people/culture

Examples

The Austrian approach to design is both modern and traditional.
