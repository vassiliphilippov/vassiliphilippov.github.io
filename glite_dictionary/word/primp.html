to primp

1. VERB

/ prɪmp / (AmE), / prɪmp / (BrE)
to make yourself look attractive by dressing or grooming with elaborate care, often excessively
dress or groom oneself with elaborate care

Examples

She spent an hour primping before her big date.
He always primped in front of the mirror before important meetings.
The girls primped together backstage before the performance.

Synset Examples

She likes to dress when going to the opera

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to preen - to devote effort to making oneself look attractive
