dumfounding

1. ADJ

/ dʌmˈfaʊndɪŋ / (AmE), / dʌmˈfaʊndɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing someone to be so astonished or amazed that they are unable to speak; bewildering or striking dumb with wonder.
extremely astonishing or bewildering

Examples

The magician’s final trick was so dumfounding that the audience sat in stunned silence.
Her dumfounding discovery changed the way scientists viewed the phenomenon.
Witnessing the northern lights for the first time was a truly dumfounding experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astonishing - causing great surprise or amazement
  • Synonym: bewildering - extremely confusing or perplexing
  • Antonym: unsurprising - not causing surprise

Etymology

From 'dumfound', after 'dumbfound', modeled from 'dumb' + 'found'.
