Oviedo

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈvjɛɪdoʊ / (AmE), / əʊˈvjɛːdəʊ / (BrE)
A city in northwestern Spain near the Cantabrian Mountains, known as the capital of the Asturias region.
city in northwestern Spain

Examples

Oviedo is famous for its medieval old town and remarkable cathedral.
After traveling through northern Spain, we spent two days in Oviedo.
Many university students from across Spain come to study in Oviedo.
