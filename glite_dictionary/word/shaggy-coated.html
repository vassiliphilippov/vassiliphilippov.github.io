shaggy-coated

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃæɡi ˌkoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃaɡi ˌkəʊtɪd / (BrE)
(of hair or fur) thick, long, and untidy; poorly groomed in appearance.
having thick, untidy hair or fur

Examples

The old dog was shaggy-coated and in need of a good brushing.
The explorer encountered a shaggy-coated yak on the mountain trail.
After months in the wild, his shaggy-coated appearance made him almost unrecognizable.

Synset Examples

bushy locks
a shaggy beard

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bushy - (of hair or fur) thick and full
  • Synonym: unkempt - (of hair or appearance) not well-groomed, messy
