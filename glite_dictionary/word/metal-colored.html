metal-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛt̬l̩ ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈmet.əl ˌkʌl.əd / (BrE)
having the color or appearance of metal; metallic in color
having a metallic color or shine

Examples

The dancer wore a metal-colored costume that shimmered under the stage lights.
She chose a metal-colored paint for the accent wall to give the room a modern look.
The car's metal-colored finish caught everyone's attention in the parking lot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metallic - having the characteristics or appearance of metal
