alphabetically

1. ADV

/ ˌæl.fəˈbɛt.ɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌæl.fəˈbɛt.ɪ.kli / (BrE)
In the order of the letters of the alphabet.
in alphabetical order

Examples

Please file the documents alphabetically by last name.
The books were shelved alphabetically, making it easy to find any title.
Contest entries should be listed alphabetically on the registration form.

Synset Examples

the list was arranged alphabetically

Related Terms

  • Antonym: chronologically - in the order in which events occurred in time

Etymology

From 'alphabetical' + '-ly'. 'Alphabetical' from Late Latin 'alphabeticus'.
