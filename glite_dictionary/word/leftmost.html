leftmost

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛftˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈlɛftməʊst / (BrE)
furthest to the left; located at the extreme left side of something
furthest to the left

Examples

She chose the leftmost seat in the front row so she could see the exit clearly.
The leftmost column of the spreadsheet contains the dates for each entry.
Please turn on the leftmost light switch to brighten the room.

Synset Examples

the leftmost non-zero digit

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rightmost - furthest to the right; located at the extreme right side of something

Etymology

Formed from 'left' + 'most', indicating the greatest extent to the left.
