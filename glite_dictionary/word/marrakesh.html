Marrakesh

1. PROPN

/ ˌmærəˈkɛʃ / (AmE), / ˌmærəˈkeʃ / (BrE)
A major city in western Morocco, known for its historical landmarks and as a popular tourist destination.
city in western Morocco

Examples

Many travelers dream of visiting Marrakesh to experience its vibrant markets and historic palaces.
The architecture of Marrakesh reflects a blend of Berber and Islamic influences.
She returned from Marrakesh with beautiful handmade rugs and spices.

Etymology

From Arabic مراكش‎ (Marrākuš), the city's name.
