Dürer

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʊrər / (AmE), / ˈdjʊərə / (BrE)
Albrecht Dürer (1471–1528), a leading German painter, printmaker, and engraver of the Renaissance, renowned for his woodcuts and engravings.
German Renaissance painter and engraver

Examples

The exhibition featured several original works by Dürer alongside Renaissance masterpieces.
Students studied Dürer for his innovative engraving techniques and detailed self-portraits.
Art historians consider Dürer a pivotal figure in bringing Renaissance ideas to Northern Europe.

Etymology

German surname; from the artist's name Albrecht Dürer.
