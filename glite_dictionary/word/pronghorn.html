pronghorn

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɔŋˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈprɒŋ.hɔːn / (BrE)
A fast-running, deer-like mammal native to western North America, characterized by small branched horns.
fast North American mammal with branched horns

Examples

We spotted a pronghorn sprinting across the open prairie at sunrise.
The pronghorn is renowned for being one of the fastest land animals in North America.
During our road trip, we saw several pronghorns grazing near the highway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antelope - a swift-running ruminant mammal of Africa and Eurasia with hollow horns
