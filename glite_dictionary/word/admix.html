to admix

1. VERB

/ ædˈmɪks / (AmE), / ædˈmɪks / (BrE)
to mix or blend something with another substance; to mingle or combine
to mix or blend with something

Examples

The solution was carefully admixed with saline before administration.
Traces of dust had admixed with the flour during storage.
In the laboratory, two chemicals were admixed to test their reactivity.

Synset Examples

Hyaline casts were admixed with neutrophils

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mix - to combine or blend into one mass or mixture
  • Synonym: blend - to mix different substances so they combine smoothly

Etymology

From Latin 'admiscere', from 'ad-' (to) + 'miscere' (to mix).
