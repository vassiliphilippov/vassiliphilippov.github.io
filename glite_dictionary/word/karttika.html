Karttika

1. NOUN

/ kɑrˈtiːkə / (AmE), / kɑːˈtiːkə / (BrE)
The eighth month of the Hindu calendar, typically falling in October-November in the Gregorian calendar.
eighth month of Hindu calendar

Examples

The festival was observed throughout the month of Karttika, with many people visiting temples.
Many important Hindu rituals are performed during Karttika each year.
In 2022, Karttika coincided mostly with the month of November.

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Kārtika', after the deity Kartikeya.
