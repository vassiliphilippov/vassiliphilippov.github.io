skateboarder

1. NOUN

/ ˈskeɪtˌbɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈskeɪtbɔːdə / (BrE)
a person who rides or performs stunts on a skateboard
a person who rides a skateboard

Examples

The skateboarder performed a difficult trick at the park.
As a child, he dreamed of becoming a professional skateboarder.
Several skateboarders practiced together in the empty parking lot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skater - a person who skates (on ice or rollers)
  • Synonym: skateboard - a narrow board with wheels, ridden by standing on it and pushing with one foot

Etymology

From 'skateboard' + '-er', denoting a person who does an activity.
