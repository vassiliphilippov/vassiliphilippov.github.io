Uruguayan

1. NOUN

/ ˌjʊr.əˈɡwaɪ.ən / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəˈɡwaɪ.ən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Uruguay
person from Uruguay

Examples

The museum organized an exhibit featuring artwork by a renowned Uruguayan.
She met several Uruguayans during her trip to Montevideo.
A proud Uruguayan, he always supports the national football team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Uruguay - a country in South America

Etymology

From Uruguay + -an, denoting association or origin.

2. ADJ

/ ˌjʊrəˈgwaɪən / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəˈgwaɪən / (BrE)
relating to Uruguay or its people
of or relating to Uruguay

Examples

The Uruguayan football team won the match with great skill.
She cooked a traditional Uruguayan dish for her guests last night.
The author writes about Uruguayan culture in her latest book.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Uruguay - a country in South America

Etymology

From Uruguay, named after the country of origin, plus the adjectival suffix -an.
