umteen

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌmˌtiːn / (AmE), / ˈʌmˌtiːn / (BrE)
(informal) Very many; an indefinite but large number of.
very many; innumerable
informal

Examples

I've told you umteen times not to leave your shoes in the hallway.
She received umteen emails about the upcoming event.
There are umteen reasons why this plan might not work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: umpteen - An indefinitely large number

Etymology

Formed on the pattern of ‘thirteen’, ‘fourteen’, etc., influenced by ‘umpteen’.
