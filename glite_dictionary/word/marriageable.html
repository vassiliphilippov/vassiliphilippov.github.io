marriageable

1. ADJ

/ ˈmærɪdʒəbəl / (AmE), / ˈmærɪdʒəbəl / (BrE)
(of a person, especially a woman) old enough or legally eligible to be married.
old enough or eligible to marry

Examples

She is considered marriageable by traditional standards in her community.
Many stories center around marriageable daughters at the age of coming out into society.
In some countries, the marriageable age is set by law to protect young people.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unmarriageable - not eligible or suitable for marriage

Etymology

From marry + -ageable, dating to late Middle English.
