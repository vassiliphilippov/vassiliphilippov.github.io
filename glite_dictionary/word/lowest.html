lowest

1. ADJ

/ ˈloʊɪst / (AmE), / ˈləʊɪst / (BrE)
of the least rank, status, or importance in a group or hierarchy.
of least rank or importance

Examples

She started in the lowest position at the company but worked her way up.
Getting the lowest score in the tournament was disappointing, but I learned a lot.
Entry-level jobs are usually at the lowest rung of the corporate ladder.

Synset Examples

last prize
in last place

Related Terms

  • Synonym: last - coming or being after all others in order or importance
  • Antonym: highest - of the greatest rank, status, or importance in a group

Etymology

Superlative form of 'low,' from Old Norse 'lág(r)' (low).
