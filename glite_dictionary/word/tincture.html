tincture

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪŋktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋktʃə / (BrE)
a slight variation or shade of a particular color; a subtle hue or tinge
slight variation in color shade

Examples

The painting had a tincture of blue that gave it a unique quality.
She noticed a subtle tincture of green in the gray fabric.
The twilight sky was beautiful, with every tincture of pink and purple blending together.

Synset Examples

after several trials he mixed the shade of pink that she wanted

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shade - a slight degree of difference in color
  • Synonym: hue - a particular shade or tint of a given color

Etymology

From Latin 'tinctura' (act of dyeing, coloring), from 'tingere' (to dye, color).
