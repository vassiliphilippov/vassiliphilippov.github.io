demanding

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈmændɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈmɑːndɪŋ / (BrE)
Requiring a lot of effort, time, attention, or skill.
requiring much effort or skill

Examples

Her new boss is extremely demanding and expects perfection from the whole team.
The training program was so demanding that only a few participants completed it.
Raising twins can be demanding both physically and mentally.

Synset Examples

found the job very demanding
a baby can be so demanding

Related Terms

  • Synonym: challenging - requiring great effort or determination
  • Antonym: easy - not requiring much effort or skill

Etymology

From the verb 'demand', from Old French 'demander', from Latin 'dēmandāre' (to entrust, to demand).
