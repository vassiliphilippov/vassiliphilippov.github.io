noninstitutionalised

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˌɪnstəˈtuʃnəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃnəlaɪzd / (BrE)
Not committed or confined to an institution, especially a medical, psychiatric, or correctional facility.
not committed to an institution

Examples

As a noninstitutionalised patient, she was able to live independently in the community.
The census counted both institutionalized and noninstitutionalised individuals.
Most of the support is targeted towards noninstitutionalised people living at home rather than in care facilities.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: institutionalised - committed to or living in an institution
