pressed

1. ADJ

/ prɛst / (AmE), / prɛst / (BrE)
(of clothing or fabric) made smooth, flat, and compact by ironing.
made compact by ironing

Examples

He wore a freshly pressed shirt to the interview.
The hotel offers laundry service for guests who like their clothes pressed.
Her dress looked neat and pressed despite a long day of travel.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wrinkled - having creases or folds; not smooth (especially of fabric)
