DFLP

1. NOUN

/ di ɛf ɛl pi / (AmE), / diː ɛf ɛl piː / (BrE)
abbreviation for Democratic Front for the Liberation of Palestine, a Marxist-Leninist Palestinian political and militant group advocating revolutionary change to achieve Palestinian goals.
Palestinian Marxist-Leninist political group

Examples

In the 1970s, the DFLP became known internationally for its radical activities.
The DFLP has maintained a distinct political platform within Palestinian politics.
News reports often mention the DFLP when discussing factions involved in the conflict.

Synset Examples

in 1974 the DFLP took over a schoolhouse and massacred Israeli schoolchildren

Etymology

Acronym from Democratic Front for the Liberation of Palestine.
