1000th

1. ADJ

/ ˈwʌn ˈθaʊzəndθ / (AmE), / ˈwʌn ˈθaʊzəndθ / (BrE)
being the ordinal number corresponding to one thousand in a sequence; coming after the 999th and before the 1001st.
ordinal number for one thousand

Examples

This is the 1000th time I have seen that movie.
She celebrated her 1000th day of working at the company.
The runner crossed the finish line in 1000th place.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thousandth - ordinal number for one thousand, spelled out
  • Degree Lower: 999th - the ordinal number immediately before 1000th
  • Degree Higher: 1001st - the ordinal number immediately after 1000th
