lingually

1. ADV

/ ˈlɪŋɡjuəli / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋɡjʊəli / (BrE)
with respect to language; in a way that relates to language or languages
with respect to language

Examples

The region is lingually diverse, with several different languages spoken in daily life.
She is lingually gifted and can converse in five languages fluently.
Some children are lingually impaired and require specialized instruction.

Synset Examples

linguistically impaired children
a lingually diverse population

Related Terms

  • Synonym: linguistically - in a way that relates to language or linguistics

Etymology

Formed from 'lingual' (relating to language) + '-ly' (adverb suffix).
