skivvies

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪviz / (AmE), / ˈskɪviz / (BrE)
Men's underwear consisting of a cotton undershirt and underpants, typically worn as a set.
men's underwear set
informal american

Examples

He walked around the house in just his skivvies and a pair of old slippers.
The campers were caught swimming in their skivvies by the counselor.
Don't forget to pack a few extra pairs of skivvies for the trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: underwear - clothes worn under outer clothes, typically next to the skin
  • Degree Lower: boxers - men's loose-fitting underwear shorts
  • Degree Lower: briefs - men's close-fitting underwear

Etymology

Originally U.S. slang from the early 20th century, of uncertain origin.
