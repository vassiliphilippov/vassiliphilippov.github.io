Pharomachrus mocinno

1. PROPN

/ ˌfɛɹəˈmækɹəs moʊˈsiːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˌfærəˈmækrəs məʊˈsiːnəʊ / (BrE)
A very rare Central American bird, also known as the resplendent quetzal; the national bird of Guatemala.
rare Central American bird; national bird of Guatemala

Examples

The Pharomachrus mocinno is celebrated for its iridescent green and red plumage.
In Guatemala, the Pharomachrus mocinno is considered a symbol of liberty.
Birdwatchers travel to the cloud forests of Central America in hopes of spotting the elusive Pharomachrus mocinno.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quetzal - A colorful Central American bird of the genus Pharomachrus

Etymology

From Greek 'pharos' (mantle) + 'makros' (long), and modern Latin honoring José Mariano Mociño.
