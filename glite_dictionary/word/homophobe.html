homophobe

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊ.məˌfoʊb / (AmE), / ˈhɒ.mə.fəʊb / (BrE)
a person who hates or is prejudiced against homosexual people
person with hatred of or prejudice against homosexual people

Examples

The politician was criticized for being a homophobe after making offensive remarks.
It's important to challenge the views of a homophobe rather than ignore them.
She refused to work with a known homophobe in the organization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homophobia - intense dislike or fear of homosexual people
  • Synonym: bigot - a person who is intolerant toward those holding different opinions

Etymology

From Greek 'homo-' (same) and '-phobe' from 'phobos' (fear). Modeled after 'homophobia'.
