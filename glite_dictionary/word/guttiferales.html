Guttiferales

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡʌtəfəˈreɪliːz / (AmE), / ˌɡʌtɪfəˈrɑːliːz / (BrE)
a former order of flowering plants, now largely included within Malpighiales, used in some classifications and coextensive with Parietales.
a former order of flowering plants
medical

Examples

Botanists once classified the mangosteen under the Guttiferales.
Several resin-producing tropical trees were historically placed in the Guttiferales.
The order Guttiferales is now mostly included in Malpighiales in modern taxonomy.

Etymology

From Latin 'gutta' (drop) and 'ferre' (to bear), referencing resin-producing plants classified in the order.
