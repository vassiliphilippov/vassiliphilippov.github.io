matureness

1. NOUN

/ məˈtʃʊr.nəs, məˈtʊr.nəs / (AmE), / məˈtʃʊə.nəs, məˈtjʊə.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being fully developed or mature.
state of being mature

Examples

The matureness of her response impressed the interviewers.
Botanists assess the matureness of fruit before harvest.
He approached the complex situation with remarkable matureness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maturity - state of being fully grown or developed
  • Antonym: immaturity - the state of not being fully grown or developed

Etymology

From 'mature' + '-ness', a suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality.
