dermatological

1. ADJ

/ ˌdɜːrmətəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌdɜːmətəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study, diagnosis, or treatment of skin and its diseases
relating to dermatology (skin medicine)
medical

Examples

The clinic provides dermatological treatments for a variety of skin conditions.
She consulted a specialist for expert dermatological advice.
Recent studies in dermatological science have led to new skincare solutions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dermatology - the branch of medicine dealing with the skin

Etymology

From dermatology (from Greek 'derma' meaning skin) + -logical.
