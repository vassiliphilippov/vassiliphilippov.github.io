thirty-two

1. NUM

/ ˌθɝːti ˈtuː / (AmE), / ˌθɜːti ˈtjuː / (BrE)
The number equivalent to two more than thirty; 32.
the number 32

Examples

She turned thirty-two last week and celebrated with her friends.
There are thirty-two students enrolled in the class this semester.
A human adult usually has thirty-two teeth, including wisdom teeth.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirty-one - the number 31
  • Degree Higher: thirty-three - the number 33

Etymology

From Old English 'þrēotīene' (thirteen) and 'twā' (two), via the structure 'thirty' + 'two'.
