vaporing

1. NOUN

/ ˈveɪ.pɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈveɪ.pər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
boastful or bragging talk, especially when empty or exaggerated
boastful or bragging talk

Examples

His endless vaporing about his wealthy family soon grew tiresome.
After his victory, the athlete's vaporing annoyed his teammates.
Don't be fooled by her vaporing; she has little experience in this field.

Synset Examples

his brag is worse than his fight
whenever he won we were exposed to his gasconade

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bragging - boastful talk or behavior
  • Synonym: boasting - speaking with excessive pride
  • Antonym: modesty - lack of boastfulness or arrogance

Etymology

From the verb 'vapor' (to boast), which comes from Latin 'vapor' (steam, exhalation), used figuratively in English since the 16th century.
