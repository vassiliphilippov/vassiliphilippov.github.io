pantheistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌpænθiˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌpanθiˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by pantheism, the belief that God is identical with the universe or that everything composes an all-encompassing, immanent deity.
relating to pantheism

Examples

Many indigenous religions have a pantheistic understanding of nature, seeing divinity in all aspects of the world.
Her worldview grew more pantheistic after years of studying Eastern philosophies.
The novel explores a pantheistic vision where all living things are interconnected by a single divine force.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pantheism - The doctrine that God is identical with the universe or that everything composes an all-encompassing god

Etymology

Formed from 'pantheism' and the suffix '-ic.' 'Pantheism' from Greek 'pan' (all) + 'theos' (god).
