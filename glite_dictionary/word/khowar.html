Khowar

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈwɑr / (AmE), / kəʊˈwɑː / (BrE)
a Dardic language spoken in northwestern Pakistan, mainly in the Chitral region
Dardic language of northwestern Pakistan

Examples

Many people in Chitral communicate daily in Khowar.
He published a dictionary to help preserve the Khowar language.
Khowar is recognized for its rich oral literature and poetic traditions.

Etymology

From local terms: 'Kho' (the people) + 'war' (language), meaning 'language of the Kho'.
