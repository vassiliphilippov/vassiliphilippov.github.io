insensitively

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈsɛnsətɪvli / (AmE), / ɪnˈsensətɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner lacking sensitivity to the feelings or needs of others.
in an insensitive manner

Examples

The manager spoke insensitively to staff during the stressful period.
He insensitively joked about her recent loss in front of everyone.
The article addresses difficult topics insensitively, causing offense to many readers.

Synset Examples

the police officer questioned the woman rather insensitively about the attack

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sensitively - in a sensitive manner
