oxalic

1. ADJ

/ ɑɡˈzælɪk / (AmE), / ɒɡˈzælɪk / (BrE)
of or relating to oxalic acid or its derivatives.
relating to oxalic acid

Examples

The leaves contain oxalic acid, which can be toxic in large amounts.
During the experiment, an oxalic solution was used as the reagent.
Some cleaning agents are made with oxalic compounds for removing rust.

Etymology

From French 'oxalique', from Latin 'oxalis' (sorrel), due to the presence of oxalic acid in sorrel.
