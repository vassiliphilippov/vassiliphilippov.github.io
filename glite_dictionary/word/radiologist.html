radiologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌreɪdiˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in diagnosing and treating diseases and injuries using medical imaging techniques such as X-rays, CT scans, and radioactive substances
doctor specializing in medical imaging
medical

Examples

The radiologist reviewed my chest X-ray and explained the results clearly.
During the procedure, a radiologist monitored the images in real time.
Our hospital is recruiting an experienced radiologist to join the diagnostic team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiology - the branch of medicine that uses imaging technologies to diagnose and treat diseases

Etymology

From 'radiology' (branch of medicine dealing with X-rays) + '-ist' (one who practices).
