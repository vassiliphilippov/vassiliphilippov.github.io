dementedly

1. ADV

/ dɪˈmɛntɪdli / (AmE), / dɪˈmɛntɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that is wildly irrational, insane, or behaving as if mentally disturbed.
in an insane or wildly irrational manner

Examples

The scientist laughed dementedly as he revealed his latest invention.
She ran dementedly through the dark corridors, shouting for help.
The crowd cheered dementedly as the band took the stage.

Synset Examples

she behaved insanely
he behaves crazily when he is off his medication
the witch cackled madly
screaming dementedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: madly - in a wild or frenzied manner
  • Synonym: insanely - in an insane manner

Etymology

From 'demented' + '-ly'. 'Demented' from Latin 'dementare', from 'de-' (away from) + 'mens' (mind).
