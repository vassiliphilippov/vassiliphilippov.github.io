El Aaium

1. PROPN

/ ɛl aˈjiːʊm / (AmE), / ɛl aˈjiːʊm / (BrE)
A town in Western Sahara, near the Atlantic coast, serving as the region's largest city and administrative center.
town in Western Sahara, regional capital

Examples

The UN has maintained a peacekeeping mission in El Aaium for many years.
Flights to El Aaium are often subject to political considerations due to the region's status.
She traveled from Casablanca to El Aaium by bus, enjoying the Saharan landscapes along the way.

Etymology

From Arabic 'al-ʿayūn', meaning 'the springs'.
