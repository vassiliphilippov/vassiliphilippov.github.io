Mondrian

1. PROPN

/ mɑnˈdriː.ən / (AmE), / mɒnˈdriː.ən / (BrE)
Piet Mondrian (1872–1944), Dutch painter known for his abstract art using intersecting lines and planes of primary colors.
Dutch abstract painter, 1872–1944

Examples

The museum features several masterpieces by Mondrian in its modern art wing.
Art students often study the geometric compositions of Mondrian for inspiration.
A new exhibition explores the influence of Mondrian on twentieth-century design.

Etymology

From the Dutch surname 'Mondriaan', used by Piet Mondrian.
