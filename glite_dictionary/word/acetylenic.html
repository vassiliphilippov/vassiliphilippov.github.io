acetylenic

1. ADJ

/ əˌsɛtəˈlɛnɪk / (AmE), / əˌsiːtɪˈliːnɪk / (BrE)
relating to, containing, or characteristic of acetylene or its derivatives, especially compounds with a triple carbon-carbon bond.
relating to acetylene or its compounds
medical

Examples

The chemist synthesized an acetylenic compound as part of his research project.
They observed strong reactivity when acetylenic groups were introduced to the molecule.
Many acetylenic hydrocarbons are used in organic synthesis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acetylene - a colorless, flammable gas with a distinctive odor, used as a fuel and chemical building block

Etymology

From 'acetylene' with the suffix '-ic', indicating a relationship or property.
