jillion

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪl.jən / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪl.jən / (BrE)
An extremely large, indefinite number; used informally for exaggeration.
very large indefinite number
informal

Examples

Sarah has told me that story a jillion times.
There are a jillion reasons to visit Paris, but the food tops the list.
He claimed he had a jillion tasks to finish before noon.

Synset Examples

there were millions of flies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zillion - an extremely large, indefinite number (used informally)
