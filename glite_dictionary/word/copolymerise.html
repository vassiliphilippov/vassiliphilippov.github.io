to copolymerise

1. VERB

/ koʊˈpɒlɪməˌraɪz / (AmE), / kəʊˈpɒlɪməraɪz / (BrE)
to undergo a chemical process in which two or more different monomers combine to form a copolymer
form a copolymer by joint polymerization

Examples

Scientists managed to copolymerise the two substances to create a new material.
Certain plastics are produced when chemists copolymerise styrene and butadiene.
The company invested in technology to efficiently copolymerise multiple monomers in a single process.

Synset Examples

the two substances copolymerized

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to polymerise - to undergo a chemical reaction forming a polymer

Etymology

From 'co-' (together) + 'polymerise' (to form a polymer), based on scientific vocabulary.
