parkway

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːrkˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkˌweɪ / (BrE)
A wide, often landscaped road or thoroughfare, typically lined with trees, designed for scenic driving.
wide scenic road lined with trees

Examples

The Hudson River parkway provides beautiful views of the water and city skyline.
We drove along a shaded parkway that wound through the suburbs.
The city planted dozens of new trees along the parkway to enhance its natural beauty.

Synset Examples

the riverside drive offers many exciting scenic views

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boulevard - a wide city street often lined with trees

Etymology

From 'park' + 'way', originally referring to roads through or along parks.
