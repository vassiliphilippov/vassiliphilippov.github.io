acid-tasting

1. ADJ

/ ˈæsɪd ˌteɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈæsɪd ˌteɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
Having a sour or sharp taste reminiscent of acid; tasting sour.
sour or sharp in taste

Examples

The lemonade was particularly acid-tasting due to the fresh lemons.
Some wild berries have a distinctly acid-tasting flavor that makes them less palatable.
The sauce turned out too acid-tasting after adding too much vinegar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sour - having an acid, sharp, or tangy taste
