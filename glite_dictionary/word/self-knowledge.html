self-knowledge

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlfˈnɑlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈnɒlɪdʒ / (BrE)
awareness and understanding of one's own character, feelings, motives, and abilities
awareness of one's own character; introspection

Examples

Developing self-knowledge can help individuals make better decisions in both personal and professional life.
Through meditation, she gained a deeper sense of self-knowledge and emotional balance.
The course encouraged students to pursue self-knowledge as a foundation for success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: introspection - examination of one's own thoughts and feelings
  • Synonym: self-awareness - conscious knowledge of one's own character and feelings
  • Antonym: ignorance - lack of knowledge or awareness

Etymology

From 'self' + 'knowledge', modeled on earlier philosophical and psychological terminology.
