responsibleness

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈspɑnsəblnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈspɒnsəblnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being reliable and accountable for one's actions or duties; the state of being responsible.
quality of being responsible

Examples

Her responsibleness made her the ideal candidate for the job.
He was admired for the responsibleness he showed throughout the project.
Parents appreciate teachers who demonstrate responsibleness in caring for their students.

Synset Examples

he holds a position of great responsibility

Related Terms

  • Synonym: responsibility - the state or fact of being responsible
  • Antonym: irresponsibility - the state of lacking responsibility

Etymology

Formed from 'responsible' + '-ness' (quality or state suffix).
