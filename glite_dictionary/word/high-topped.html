high-topped

1. ADJ

/ ˈhaɪ ˌtɑpt / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ ˌtɒpt / (BrE)
(of shoes or boots) having uppers that extend higher than usual above the ankle.
shoes/boots with high uppers

Examples

He always wears his high-topped boots when hiking in the mountains.
The basketball player preferred high-topped sneakers for better ankle support.
My new high-topped shoes are stylish and comfortable for winter weather.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: low-cut - (of shoes) having low uppers; not covering the ankle
