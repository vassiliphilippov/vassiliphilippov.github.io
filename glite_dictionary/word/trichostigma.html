Trichostigma

1. NOUN

/ ˌtraɪkoʊˈstɪɡmə / (AmE), / ˌtrɪkəʊˈstɪɡmə / (BrE)
A genus of erect or climbing shrubs native to tropical South America.
genus of South American shrubs

Examples

The Trichostigma genus includes several species of shrubs found in rainforests.
Botanists discovered a new species within the Trichostigma genus last year.
Many gardeners are interested in cultivating Trichostigma for its ornamental value.

Etymology

From Greek 'tricho-' (hair) and 'stigma' (mark, spot), referring to botanical features of the plant.
