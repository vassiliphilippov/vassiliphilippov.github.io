Ngaio Marsh

1. PROPN

/ ˈnaɪ.oʊ ˈmɑrʃ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪ.əʊ ˈmɑːʃ / (BrE)
(1899–1982) New Zealand writer known for her detective novels, especially featuring the detective Roderick Alleyn.
New Zealand detective novelist (1899–1982)

Examples

Ngaio Marsh is regarded as one of the Queens of Crime in detective fiction.
Many of Ngaio Marsh's novels are set in both New Zealand and England.
I have enjoyed reading several mysteries by Ngaio Marsh this year.
