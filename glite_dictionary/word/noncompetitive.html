noncompetitive

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnkəmˈpɛtətɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəmˈpɛtətɪv / (BrE)
not involving or characterized by competition or competitiveness
not involving competition

Examples

The company offers several noncompetitive positions suitable for those seeking less stress.
He joined the club for noncompetitive players who just want to have fun.
There is a noncompetitive division in the marathon for beginners.

Synset Examples

noncompetitive positions
noncompetitive interest in games

Related Terms

  • Antonym: competitive - characterized by competition; striving to win or be the best

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'non-' (not) + 'competitive' (from compete, from Latin 'competere').
