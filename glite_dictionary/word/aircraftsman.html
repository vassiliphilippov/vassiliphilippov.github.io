aircraftsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌkræftsmən / (AmE), / ˈɛəˌkrɑːftsmən / (BrE)
A junior noncommissioned rank in the British Royal Air Force, typically held by enlisted personnel.
lowest RAF enlisted rank
british

Examples

After completing basic training, he became an aircraftsman in the Royal Air Force.
As an aircraftsman, his duties included maintaining the base and assisting with aircraft repairs.
Hundreds of young recruits are promoted to the rank of aircraftsman each year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aircraftwoman - female equivalent of aircraftsman in the RAF
  • Degree Higher: leading aircraftman - the next higher rank above aircraftsman in the RAF

Etymology

Formed from 'aircraft' + 'man', modeled after other military rank names.
