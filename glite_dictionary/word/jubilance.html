jubilance

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuː.bɪ.ləns / (AmE), / ˈdʒuː.bɪ.ləns / (BrE)
a feeling or expression of great joy and happiness
extreme joy or happiness

Examples

Her jubilance was evident when she received the good news.
The crowd erupted in jubilance after the team won the championship.
Their faces shone with jubilance during the festive celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joy - a feeling of great pleasure and happiness
  • Synonym: elation - a feeling of elevated joy or pride
  • Antonym: sadness - the state of being unhappy

Etymology

From Latin 'jubilare' meaning 'to shout for joy'.
