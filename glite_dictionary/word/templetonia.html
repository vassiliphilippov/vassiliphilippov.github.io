Templetonia

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛmpəlˈtoʊniə / (AmE), / ˌtɛmpəlˈtəʊniə / (BrE)
A genus of Australian shrubs or subshrubs, commonly known as coral bush, belonging to the pea family.
genus of Australian shrubs (coral bush)

Examples

The Templetonia genus includes several species that thrive in arid regions of Australia.
Botanists studied a rare Templetonia specimen found near the coastal cliffs.
Gardens specializing in native plants often feature Templetonia for its bright red flowers.

Etymology

Named after John Templeton (1766–1825), Irish botanist.
