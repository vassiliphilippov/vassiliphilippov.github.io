mopes

1. NOUN

/ moʊps / (AmE), / məʊps / (BrE)
a mildly depressed, gloomy, or listless mood, often regarded as a temporary feeling
mildly depressed or gloomy mood
informal

Examples

He's had the mopes ever since the breakup last week.
Don't let a rainy day give you the mopes; find something fun to do instead.
After hearing the bad news, she was in the mopes all afternoon.
