3. NOUN

/ ˈtʃoʊkər / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊkə / (BrE)
A close-fitting necklace or high, tight collar worn around the neck.
close-fitting necklace or collar

Examples

She wore a stylish choker to the party, which drew many compliments.
The Victorian-era dress was complemented by a velvet choker around her neck.
Chokers became a popular fashion accessory during the 1990s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: necklace - an ornamental chain or string of beads, jewels, or links worn around the neck
  • Synonym: collar - a band of material around the neck of a shirt, dress, coat, or jacket

Etymology

From 'choke' + '-er', so called because it closely fits around the neck.
