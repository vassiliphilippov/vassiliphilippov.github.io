dauntingly

1. ADV

/ ˈdɔntɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈdɔːntɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that makes something seem difficult, intimidating, or likely to discourage.
in an intimidating or discouraging manner

Examples

The mountain loomed dauntingly above the valley, making the hikers reconsider their route.
She faced the dauntingly complex puzzle with determination, refusing to give up.
The instructions were dauntingly detailed, requiring hours of careful study.

Synset Examples

dauntingly difficult

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intimidatingly - in a way that causes fear or discouragement

Etymology

From 'daunting' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
