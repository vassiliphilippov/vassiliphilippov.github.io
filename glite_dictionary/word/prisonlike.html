prisonlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈprɪz.ənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈprɪz.ənˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a prison, especially in being restrictive or confining
resembling a prison; restrictive or confining

Examples

The boarding school had a prisonlike atmosphere, with strict rules and little freedom.
Many criticized the prisonlike conditions of the overcrowded shelter.
His tiny, windowless office felt almost prisonlike after years of working in open spaces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prison - a building for the confinement of prisoners
