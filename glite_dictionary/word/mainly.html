mainly

1. ADV

/ ˈmeɪn.li / (AmE), / ˈmeɪn.li / (BrE)
For the most part; chiefly or primarily.
for the most part; chiefly

Examples

The event was mainly attended by students and teachers.
She is mainly responsible for organizing the conference.
This course focuses mainly on conversation skills.

Synset Examples

he is mainly interested in butterflies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: primarily - for the most part; mainly
  • Antonym: partly - to some extent; not completely or mainly

Etymology

Formed from 'main' (principal) + '-ly'.
