Suomi

1. PROPN

/ ˈsuː.oʊ.mi / (AmE), / ˈsuː.ɔː.mi / (BrE)
The native Finnish name for Finland, a republic in northern Europe.
Finnish name for Finland

Examples

She traveled to Suomi to experience the northern lights.
The national anthem of Suomi is called 'Maamme'.
Many people in Suomi enjoy sauna as a traditional pastime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Finland - a country in northern Europe

Etymology

From Finnish 'Suomi', meaning 'Finland'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈsuː.oʊ.mi / (AmE), / ˈsuː.ə.mi / (BrE)
the Finnish language; the official language of Finland, belonging to the Baltic Finnic branch of the Uralic language family
Finnish language; official language of Finland

Examples

Many people in Finland speak Suomi as their first language.
She decided to learn Suomi before moving to Helsinki for work.
The signs in Helsinki are written in both Swedish and Suomi.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Finnish - the official language of Finland, also known as 'Suomi' in Finnish

Etymology

Finnish, meaning 'Finland' or 'the Finnish language'.
