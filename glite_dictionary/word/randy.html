randy

1. ADJ

/ ˈrændi / (AmE), / ˈrændi / (BrE)
feeling or showing strong sexual desire
sexually aroused; lustful

Examples

She always gets a bit randy after a couple of glasses of wine.
The dog was acting unusually randy in the park.
He made a randy comment that made everyone laugh.

Synset Examples

feeling horny

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horny - sexually excited or aroused
  • Synonym: libidinous - having or showing strong sexual desire

Etymology

Probably from obsolete English dialect 'rand' (to rant or be boisterous); sense evolved in 20th century.
