selenator

1. NOUN

/ sɛləˈneɪtər / (AmE), / sɛlɪˈneɪtə / (BrE)
A devoted fan of Selena Gomez.
fan of Selena Gomez
informal

Examples

She proudly called herself a selenator and collected all of Selena Gomez's albums.
The selenators waited outside the concert venue for hours hoping to catch a glimpse of their idol.
On social media, many selenator accounts share news and support for Selena Gomez.

Etymology

Formed from the singer's name Selena (Gomez) + English fan group suffix -ator, modeled on similar terms (e.g. 'Belieber' for Justin Bieber).
