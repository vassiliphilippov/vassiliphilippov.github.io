yeppity

1. ADJ

/ ˈjɛp.ɪ.ti / (AmE), / ˈjɛp.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
feeling optimistic or excited, especially in a positive and energetic way
optimistic or excited feeling
informal slang

Examples

She was in a yeppity mood after hearing the good news.
He gets yeppity every time his favorite team is about to play.
I wish I could feel as yeppity about Mondays as you do.

Synset Examples

I’m feeling yeppity about our chances this year!
