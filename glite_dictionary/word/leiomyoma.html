leiomyoma

1. NOUN

/ laɪ.oʊ.maɪˈoʊ.mə / (AmE), / laɪ.əʊ.maɪˈəʊ.mə / (BrE)
a benign tumor composed of smooth muscle cells, most commonly found in the uterus or digestive tract
benign tumor of smooth muscle
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that the leiomyoma in her uterus was noncancerous.
A large leiomyoma can sometimes cause abdominal discomfort or pain.
Treatment options for a leiomyoma depend on its size and location.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fibroid - benign tumor in the uterus; common synonym for uterine leiomyoma

Etymology

From Greek 'leios' (smooth) + 'mys' (muscle) + '-oma' (tumor).
