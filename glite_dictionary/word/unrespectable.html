unrespectable

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.rɪˈspɛk.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌn.rɪˈspɛk.tə.bəl / (BrE)
Not deserving respect; regarded as unworthy of respect.
not worthy of respect

Examples

His unrespectable behavior caused many people to avoid him.
Working in such an unrespectable environment made her uncomfortable.
He was dismissed from the club for being associated with unrespectable activities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disreputable - having a bad reputation
  • Antonym: respectable - worthy of respect

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'respectable.'
