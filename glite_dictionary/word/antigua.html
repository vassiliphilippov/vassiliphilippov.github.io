Antigua

1. PROPN

/ ænˈtiːɡwə / (AmE), / ænˈtiːɡwə / (BrE)
an island in the Caribbean Sea, the largest of the islands comprising the country of Antigua and Barbuda
Caribbean island, part of Antigua and Barbuda

Examples

We spent our vacation on Antigua, enjoying its beautiful beaches and warm weather.
Historical sugar plantations can still be found across Antigua.
The cruise ship docked at the port of Antigua early in the morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Barbuda - island forming the other main part of Antigua and Barbuda

Etymology

From Spanish 'antigua', meaning 'ancient', named by Christopher Columbus.
