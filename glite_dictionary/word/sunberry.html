sunberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌnˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌbɛri / (BrE)
a garden variety of black nightshade (Solanum retroflexum) cultivated for its small, edible orange or black berries
edible berry plant related to nightshade

Examples

She added a handful of sunberries to her fruit salad for extra flavor.
The sunberry plant thrives in warm, sunny gardens and produces berries in late summer.
Many gardeners appreciate the unique taste of sunberry jam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wonderberry - another name for sunberry, edible nightshade berry
  • Synonym: black nightshade - species of nightshade plant, some bearing edible berries
