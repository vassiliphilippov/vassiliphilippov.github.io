intramuscular

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.trəˈmʌs.kjə.lɚ / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trəˈmʌs.kjʊ.lə / (BrE)
situated in, administered into, or occurring within a muscle.
within or into a muscle
medical

Examples

The doctor administered an intramuscular injection in my arm.
This vaccine is only available as an intramuscular solution.
After the injury, he received pain relief through an intramuscular shot.

Synset Examples

an intramuscular injection

Related Terms

  • Antonym: subcutaneous - situated or applied under the skin
  • Antonym: intravenous - administered into a vein
  • Synonym: intramuscular injection - injection given directly into a muscle

Etymology

From Latin 'intra-' (within) and 'musculus' (muscle).
