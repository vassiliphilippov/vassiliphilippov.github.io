attempted

1. ADJ

/ əˈtɛmptɪd / (AmE), / əˈtɛmptɪd / (BrE)
(of a crime or action) tried but not completed successfully.
tried but not successfully completed

Examples

The suspect was charged with attempted robbery after his plan failed.
She made several attempted calls to his phone, but he never answered.
He was convicted of attempted escape from prison.

Synset Examples

attempted murder

Related Terms

  • Antonym: successful - achieved the intended result
