olivelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑlɪvˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪvˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling or having the characteristics of an olive, especially in shape, color, or texture.
resembling an olive

Examples

The plant produces small, olivelike berries in the autumn.
Her earrings were shaped in an olivelike form that caught the light beautifully.
An olivelike color covered the kitchen walls, giving the space a Mediterranean feel.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: olive - a small oval fruit, green or black when ripe, used as food and source of oil

Etymology

From 'olive' + '-like', meaning 'resembling an olive'.
