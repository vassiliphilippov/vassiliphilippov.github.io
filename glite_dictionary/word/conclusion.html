conclusion

1. NOUN

/ kənˈkluʒən / (AmE), / kənˈkluːʒən / (BrE)
the formal or final settlement of an agreement, contract, or negotiation
final settlement or agreement

Examples

The conclusion of the merger took several months of negotiation.
Both sides celebrated the conclusion of the peace talks with a formal ceremony.
The conclusion of the contract marked a significant milestone for the company.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agreement - a negotiated and typically legally binding arrangement between parties
  • Antonym: initiation - the action of beginning something, especially a formal process or agreement
