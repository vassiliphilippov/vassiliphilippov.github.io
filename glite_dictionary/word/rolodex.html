Rolodex

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊ.ləˌdɛks / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.ləˌdɛks / (BrE)
A rotating desktop file device with removable cards, used for recording names, addresses, and telephone numbers.
a rotary card index for contact information

Examples

He reached for his Rolodex to look up an old colleague's phone number.
Over the years, her Rolodex became a valuable networking tool.
Many offices have replaced the traditional Rolodex with digital contact lists.

Synset Examples

a news reporter has to have a good Rolodex

Etymology

Trademark, coined from 'rolling' and 'index'.
