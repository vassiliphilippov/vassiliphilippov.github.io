tec

1. NOUN

/ tɛk / (AmE), / tɛk / (BrE)
An informal term for a police officer who investigates crimes; a detective.
informal term for detective

Examples

The tec arrived at the scene shortly after the alarm was raised.

Every good crime novel features a clever tec who solves the mystery.
