turbaned

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɜrbənd / (AmE), / ˈtɜːbənd / (BrE)
Wearing a turban.
wearing a turban

Examples

The turbaned man greeted the visitors at the entrance of the mosque.
Several turbaned figures can be seen in the painting, adding to its exotic allure.
She was interviewed by a turbaned journalist who spoke softly and politely.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: turbanless - not wearing a turban

Etymology

From 'turban' (a type of headwear) + -ed (adjectival suffix).
