Acrilan

1. NOUN

/ ˈæk.rɪˌlæn / (AmE), / ˈakrɪlan / (BrE)
A trademarked acrylic fiber used to make strong, soft, crease-resistant fabrics.
a type of synthetic acrylic fiber

Examples

The new sofa was upholstered with Acrilan for added durability and comfort.
Many winter sweaters are made from Acrilan because it is warm and easy to wash.
The label on the blanket states that it is composed of 100% Acrilan fiber.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acrylic - a synthetic resin or fiber made from acrylic acid or its derivatives
