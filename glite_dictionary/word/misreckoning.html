misreckoning

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪsˈrɛkənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈrɛkənɪŋ / (BrE)
An error or mistake in calculation or estimation.
a mistake in calculation or estimation

Examples

The expedition failed due to a serious misreckoning of the distance to the next water source.
His loss in the tournament was the result of a crucial misreckoning during the final match.
She realized her misreckoning only after comparing the figures twice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: error - a mistake or incorrect decision
  • Synonym: blunder - a careless or serious mistake

Etymology

Formed from 'mis-' (wrongly) + 'reckoning' (calculation or estimation).
