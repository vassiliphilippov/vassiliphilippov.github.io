altcoin

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔltˌkɔɪn / (AmE), / ˈɔːltˌkɔɪn / (BrE)
a cryptocurrency other than Bitcoin; an alternative digital currency to Bitcoin
any cryptocurrency except Bitcoin

Examples

Many investors are diversifying their portfolios by purchasing altcoins alongside Bitcoin.
Ethereum is often the most discussed altcoin in blockchain forums.
The popularity of altcoins has surged as new digital currencies are introduced.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cryptocurrency - a digital currency using cryptography for security

Etymology

Blend of 'alternative' and 'coin' (from cryptocurrency).
