to reheel

1. VERB

/ riˈhil / (AmE), / riːˈhiːl / (BrE)
to put a new heel on a shoe or boot
put a new heel on a shoe

Examples

I took my boots to the cobbler to have them reheeled.
She decided to reheel her favorite pair of shoes instead of buying new ones.
The shop offered to reheel and polish my old leather boots for a small fee.
