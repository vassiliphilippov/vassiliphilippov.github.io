houri

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʊri / (AmE), / ˈhʊəri / (BrE)
(in Islam) a beautiful, dark-eyed maiden who, according to the Quran, will accompany the faithful in Paradise.
beautiful maiden in Islamic paradise

Examples

According to the story, each believer is promised a houri in the afterlife as a reward for faith.
The poet described her as lovely as a houri from ancient tales.
In Islamic tradition, a houri is often depicted as a symbol of heavenly beauty.

Etymology

From Arabic ḥūrīya, feminine of ḥawr, referring to beautiful-eyed beings in Paradise.

2. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊri / (AmE), / ˈhʊəri / (BrE)
A woman of great beauty, especially one that is voluptuous; originally, a beautiful woman from paradise in Muslim belief.
a voluptuously beautiful woman

Examples

He gazed at her as though she were a houri straight out of legend.
In the painting, the artist depicted several houris enjoying the gardens of paradise.
The novel described a world filled with luxury and houri-like figures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty - a beautiful woman

Etymology

From Arabic ḥūrīya (female of ḥūr), in Islamic tradition, a beautiful maiden in paradise.
