reappearance

1. NOUN

/ ˌriːəˈpɪrəns / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈpɪərəns / (BrE)
the act of appearing again after being absent or unseen for a period of time
the act of appearing again

Examples

The sudden reappearance of the singer delighted her fans.
After months of silence, his reappearance in the news surprised everyone.
Scientists are excited about the reappearance of this rare species in the region.

Synset Examples

his reappearance as Hamlet has been long awaited

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appearance - the act of coming into view or becoming visible
  • Antonym: disappearance - the act of someone or something ceasing to be visible or present

Etymology

Formed from re- (again) and appearance.

2. NOUN

/ ˌriːəˈpɪrəns / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈpɪərəns / (BrE)
the act or event of appearing again after having disappeared or been absent
the act of appearing again

Examples

The sudden reappearance of the missing cat surprised everyone in the neighborhood.
His reappearance at the conference after many years was met with applause.
Scientists were excited by the reappearance of rare flowers in the region after decades of absence.

Synset Examples

the reappearance of Halley's comet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appearance - the act of coming into view; becoming visible
  • Antonym: disappearance - the act of ceasing to be visible or present

Etymology

From re- (again) + appearance; first recorded in English in the mid-17th century.
