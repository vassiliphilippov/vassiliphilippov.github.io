wirework

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪɚˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəˌwɜːk / (BrE)
mesh or netting made from interlaced or woven wires, often used for fencing, screens, or decorative purposes
mesh or netting made of wires

Examples

The artist created an intricate sculpture using fine wirework for the wings.
We installed wirework around the garden to keep out rabbits.
The antique lampshade was decorated with delicate wirework patterns.
