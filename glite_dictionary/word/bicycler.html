bicycler

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪsɪklər / (AmE), / ˈbaɪsɪklə / (BrE)
a person who rides a bicycle
person who rides a bicycle

Examples

The bicycler pedaled quickly down the quiet country road.
As a daily bicycler, she enjoys her morning commute to work.
The park was full of bicyclers taking advantage of the good weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cyclist - a person who rides a bicycle or cycles

Etymology

From 'bicycle' + '-er' suffix denoting a person who performs an action.
