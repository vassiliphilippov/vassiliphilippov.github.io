solved

1. ADJ

/ sɑlvd / (AmE), / sɒlvd / (BrE)
(of a problem, mystery, or question) explained, answered, or resolved; having a solution.
explained or answered; resolved

Examples

The police consider the case to be solved after discovering new evidence.
Many math problems remain solved thanks to recent advancements in research.
The museum displayed artifacts related to both solved and unsolved crimes.

Synset Examples

mysteries solved and unsolved
problems resolved and unresolved

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unsolved - not explained, answered, or resolved
  • Synonym: resolved - of a problem, question, or dispute: settled or brought to an end
