Basle

1. PROPN

/ bɑːl / (AmE), / bɑːl / (BrE)
a city in northwestern Switzerland, situated on the Rhine near the borders with France and Germany; also known as Basel.
city in northwestern Switzerland

Examples

The art fair in Basle attracts collectors from all over the world.
She studied for a semester in Basle during her university years.
The Rhine river divides Basle into two distinct districts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Basel - a city in northwestern Switzerland (alternative modern spelling)

Etymology

From German "Basel"; the English "Basle" reflects French spelling.
