guvnor

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌvnəɹ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvnə / (BrE)
(British slang) A person in authority, especially a boss or someone in command.
British slang for boss or superior
slang british

Examples

I’ll ask the guvnor if you can have the afternoon off.
The guvnor called everyone into the office for a meeting.
You’d better run that by the guvnor before making a decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boss - person in charge; a supervisor

Etymology

From Cockney pronunciation of 'governor' in British English.
