Prairial

1. NOUN

/ prɛəriˈɑl / (AmE), / prɛəˈrɪəl / (BrE)
The ninth month of the French Revolutionary calendar, corresponding roughly to May and June; known as the month of meadows.
ninth month of French Revolutionary calendar

Examples

The uprising of 1 Prairial Year III marked a turning point in the Revolution.
Many historical events are dated according to the Prairial in French records.
Crops flourished during Prairial, when the meadows were in full bloom.

Etymology

From French *Prairial*, derived from 'prairie' (meadow) + '-al' (relating to).
