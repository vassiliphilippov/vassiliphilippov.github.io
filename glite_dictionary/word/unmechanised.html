unmechanised

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈmɛkəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈmɛkənaɪzd / (BrE)
Not equipped with or operated by machinery; not mechanized.
not operated by machinery
british

Examples

The small farm relied on unmechanised methods for harvesting crops.
Much of the region’s pottery is crafted using unmechanised techniques handed down through generations.
Before the industrial revolution, furniture production was almost entirely unmechanised.

Synset Examples

production of furniture remained largely unmechanized

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mechanised - operated by machinery
  • Synonym: manual - done by hand without machines

Etymology

Formed with the prefix un- (not) + mechanised.
