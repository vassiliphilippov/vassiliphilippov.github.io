soulfully

1. ADV

/ ˈsoʊlfəli / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlfəli / (BrE)
in a way that expresses deep feeling or emotion
with deep feeling or emotion

Examples

She sang the ballad soulfully, moving the entire audience.
He gazed soulfully into her eyes as he spoke.
The pianist played soulfully, giving each note a special depth.

Synset Examples

he looked at her soulfully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emotionally - in an emotional manner
  • Antonym: coldly - in an unemotional or detached manner

Etymology

From 'soulful' + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
