silver-lace

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvər leɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə leɪs / (BrE)
A shrubby perennial plant of the Canary Islands, with white flowers and leaves and hairy stems covered in dustlike down; sometimes classified in the genus Chrysanthemum.
a shrubby perennial plant of the Canary Islands

Examples

The garden featured a lovely specimen of silver-lace along the pathway.
Silver-lace thrives in well-drained soils and sunny locations.
Botanists debate whether silver-lace belongs in the genus Chrysanthemum.
