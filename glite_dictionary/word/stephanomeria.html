Stephanomeria

1. NOUN

/ ˌstɛfənoʊˈmɪriə / (AmE), / ˌstiːfəˌnɒˈmɪəriə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the daisy family, commonly known as wire lettuce, native to western North America.
genus of flowering plants; wire lettuce

Examples

The Stephanomeria plants bloom in late summer across the dry hills of California.
Scientists recently discovered a new species within the Stephanomeria genus.
Wildlife experts noted that Stephanomeria is an important plant for local pollinators.

Etymology

From Greek 'stephanos' (crown) and 'meros' (part), referring to the flower's structure.
