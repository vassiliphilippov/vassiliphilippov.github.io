blowtube

1. NOUN

/ ˈbloʊˌtuːb / (AmE), / ˈbləʊˌtjuːb / (BrE)
A tube through which darts or other projectiles are propelled by blowing through one end.
tube for shooting darts by blowing

Examples

The hunter skillfully aimed his blowtube at the distant target.
Children made their own blowtube from bamboo to shoot tiny paper darts.
Using a blowtube requires steady breath and good aim.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blowgun - a tube for shooting darts by blowing

2. NOUN

/ ˈbloʊˌtuːb / (AmE), / ˈbləʊˌtjuːb / (BrE)
a tube used to direct air or gas into a flame to intensify and concentrate heat
tube for directing air into a flame

Examples

The glassblower used a blowtube to shape the molten glass.
In metalworking, a blowtube can help increase the flame's temperature for soldering.
She carefully inserted the blowtube into the torch to concentrate the heat on a small area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blowpipe - a tube used to direct a stream of air (or other gas) into a flame
