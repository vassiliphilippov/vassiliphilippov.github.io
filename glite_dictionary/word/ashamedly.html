ashamedly

1. ADV

/ əˈʃeɪmɪdli / (AmE), / əˈʃeɪmɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows or feels shame.
in a manner showing shame

Examples

She apologized ashamedly for her rude behavior.
He looked down ashamedly when caught in the lie.
The child spoke ashamedly after breaking the vase.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ashamed - feeling shame or guilt
