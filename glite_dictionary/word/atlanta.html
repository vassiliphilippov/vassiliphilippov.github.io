Atlanta

1. PROPN

/ əˈtlænə / (AmE), / ətˈlæntə / (BrE)
The state capital and largest city of Georgia, USA, a major commercial and cultural center in the southeastern United States.
capital city of Georgia, USA

Examples

I grew up in Atlanta and have seen the city change a lot over the years.
After moving to Atlanta, she quickly adapted to the bustling urban environment.
The 1996 Summer Olympics were held in Atlanta, attracting visitors from around the world.

Etymology

Named after the Western & Atlantic Railroad, itself named for its route to the Atlantic Ocean.
