portiere

1. NOUN

/ pɔːrˈtjɛr / (AmE), / pɔːˈtjɛə / (BrE)
A heavy curtain hung across a doorway, often used for decoration or to prevent drafts.
curtain hung over a doorway

Examples

A velvet portiere separated the dining room from the hallway.
They drew the heavy portiere aside to allow the guests to enter.
The portiere helped keep the draught from sweeping through the old house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curtain - a piece of material hung to cover a window, stage, etc.

Etymology

From French *portière* (door curtain), from *porte* (door).
