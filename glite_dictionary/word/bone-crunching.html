bone-crunching

1. ADJ

/ ˈboʊnˌkrʌntʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊnˌkrʌntʃɪŋ / (BrE)
extremely intense, strenuous, or causing a painfully loud or harsh sound
extremely intense or painfully loud/harsh

Examples

The team endured a bone-crunching defeat in the championship game.
He recoiled at the bone-crunching sound of the collision.
After a bone-crunching workout, she could barely move her arms.

Synset Examples

The workout was bone-crunching, leaving everyone exhausted.
The sound was bone-crunching after the fall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deafening - extremely loud; overwhelmingly noisy
  • Synonym: grueling - extremely tiring and demanding
