gametoecium

1. NOUN

/ ɡəˌmiːtiˈoʊsiəm / (AmE), / ɡəˌmiːtiˈəʊsiəm / (BrE)
A group of gametangia together with the surrounding bracts, forming a reproductive structure in some plants.
botanical structure: gametangia and bracts
medical

Examples

The gametoecium is an important feature distinguishing this group of mosses from their relatives.
Botanists observed that the gametoecium contained several protective bracts around the gametangia.
A well-developed gametoecium is essential for successful reproduction in these plant species.

Etymology

From New Latin, combining 'gamete' and Greek 'oikos' (house), with '-ium' indicating structure.
