lumbar

1. ADJ

/ ˈlʌm.bɚ / (AmE), / ˈlʌm.bɑː / (BrE)
relating to or situated in the lower part of the back between the ribs and the hips
of the lower back
medical

Examples

He complained of severe lumbar pain after lifting the heavy box.
The doctor examined her lumbar region for signs of injury.
Physical therapy helped improve flexibility in his lumbar spine.

Synset Examples

lumbar vertebrae

Etymology

From Latin 'lumbāris', from 'lumbus' meaning 'loin'.
