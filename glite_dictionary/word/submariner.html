submariner

1. NOUN

/ ˌsʌbˈmɛrənər / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈmɑːrɪnə / (BrE)
a person who serves as a member of the crew of a submarine
member of a submarine crew

Examples

After years as a submariner, he was used to living in cramped quarters.
The navy held a special ceremony to honor the submariners who served during the war.
Training to become a submariner requires both physical and mental endurance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sailor - a person who works as a member of a ship's crew

Etymology

From 'submarine' + '-er', denoting one who operates or works on a submarine.
