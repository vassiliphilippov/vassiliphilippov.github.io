utensil

1. NOUN

/ juˈtɛnsəl / (AmE), / juːˈtɛn.səl / (BrE)
a tool or implement, especially one used for practical tasks in the household such as cooking or eating
implement used in household tasks

Examples

Please make sure to wash every utensil after cooking dinner.
She searched for the right utensil to flip the pancake.
They keep all their kitchen utensils neatly organized in a drawer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tool - a device or implement used to carry out a particular function

Etymology

From Latin 'utensilia' (things for use), from 'uti' (to use).
