Lutrinae

1. NOUN

/ luˈtraɪni / (AmE), / luˈtraɪniː / (BrE)
A scientific subfamily of carnivorous mammals that includes the otters.
subfamily of mammals: otters

Examples

The Lutrinae are known for their playful behavior and aquatic habits.
Conservation efforts focus on endangered species within the Lutrinae subfamily.
Researchers are studying the evolutionary relationships among the Lutrinae to better understand otter diversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: otter - A semiaquatic mammal of the family Mustelidae, subfamily Lutrinae

Etymology

From Latin lutra (otter) + scientific suffix -inae (denoting subfamilies).
