warmed

1. ADJ

/ wɔrmd / (AmE), / wɔːmd / (BrE)
(of food, liquid, or an object) having been made warm or heated to a comfortable temperature.
heated to a warm temperature

Examples

She handed her son a warmed cup of milk before bedtime.
The warmed blankets made the chilly evening much more comfortable.
After a long walk, he enjoyed a warmed bowl of soup.

Synset Examples

a cup of warmed milk

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heated - made hot or warm by applying heat
  • Antonym: cooled - made cool or less warm
