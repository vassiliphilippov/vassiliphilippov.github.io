Asahikawa

1. PROPN

/ ɑːˌsɑːhiˈkɑːwə / (AmE), / ɑːˌsɑːhiˈkɑːwə / (BrE)
a city in western Hokkaido, Japan, known as an agricultural and industrial center
city in western Hokkaido, Japan

Examples

The city of Asahikawa is famous for its beautiful winter landscapes and ramen shops.
After landing in Sapporo, we took a train to Asahikawa to visit its renowned zoo.
Farmers in Asahikawa grow a variety of crops thanks to the fertile land.

Etymology

From Japanese 朝日川 (Asahikawa), meaning 'morning sun river'.
