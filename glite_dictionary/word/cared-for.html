cared-for

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɛrd fɔr / (AmE), / ˈkɛəd fɔː / (BrE)
having received the necessary attention and support to be kept healthy, safe, or in good condition
receiving proper care and attention

Examples

The orphanage ensured every child was cared-for and happy.
She brought home a rescued cat that finally looked cared-for.
A well cared-for garden flourishes with vibrant flowers.

Synset Examples

well-cared-for children

Related Terms

  • Antonym: neglected - not receiving proper care or attention
