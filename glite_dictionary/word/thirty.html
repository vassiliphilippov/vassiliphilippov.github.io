thirty

1. NUM

/ ˈθɝːti / (AmE), / ˈθɜːti / (BrE)
The number equivalent to three tens; ten more than twenty (30).
the number 30

Examples

There are thirty students in the class this semester.
We celebrated her thirty years of service at the company.
The recipe calls for thirty grams of sugar.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty - the number 20
  • Degree Higher: forty - the number 40

Etymology

From Old English *þrītig*, from *þrīe* (three) + *-tig* (group of ten).
