reflectively

1. ADV

/ rɪˈflɛktɪvli / (AmE), / rɪˈflɛktɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner showing careful thought or consideration.
in a thoughtful or contemplative way

Examples

She stared reflectively out the window, lost in her thoughts.
He paused reflectively before answering the difficult question.
The teacher spoke reflectively about the challenges students face.

Synset Examples

he watched her reflectively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtfully - in a manner showing careful thought
  • Antonym: absentmindedly - in a distracted or inattentive way

Etymology

From 'reflective' + '-ly', where 'reflective' comes from Latin 'reflectere' (to bend back).
