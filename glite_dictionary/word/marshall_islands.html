Marshall Islands

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑrʃəl ˈaɪləndz / (AmE), / ˈmɑːʃəl ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
an island country in the central Pacific Ocean, consisting of atolls and islands, and a sovereign state associated with the United States
a Pacific island country

Examples

He visited the Marshall Islands to study their diverse marine life.
The Marshall Islands gained independence in 1986 while remaining in free association with the United States.
Rising sea levels pose a significant threat to the future of the Marshall Islands.

Etymology

Named after British explorer John Marshall, who visited the islands in 1788.
