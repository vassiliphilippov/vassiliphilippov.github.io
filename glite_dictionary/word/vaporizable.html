vaporizable

1. ADJ

/ ˈveɪpəˌraɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ˈveɪpəraɪzəbəl / (BrE)
capable of being changed into vapor; able to be volatilized.
can be converted into vapor

Examples

The chemical is highly vaporizable at room temperature, making it useful in certain experiments.
Only vaporizable drugs can be administered using an inhaler.
It's important to handle vaporizable liquids in a well-ventilated area to avoid inhalation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volatile - easily evaporated at normal temperatures

Etymology

From 'vaporize' + '-able'; 'vaporize' from French *vaporiser*, based on Latin *vapor* (steam).
