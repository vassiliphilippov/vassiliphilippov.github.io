Spam

1. NOUN

/ spæm / (AmE), / spæm / (BrE)
A brand of canned cooked meat made mainly from pork, commonly used in sandwiches and other dishes.
canned cooked pork meat product

Examples

She made a sandwich filled with Spam for lunch.
In Hawaii, Spam is a popular ingredient in many local dishes.
The cafeteria served fried Spam with eggs and toast.

Etymology

Trade name introduced in 1937, possibly from 'spiced ham' or 'shoulder of pork and ham'.
