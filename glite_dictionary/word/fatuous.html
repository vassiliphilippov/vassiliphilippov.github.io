fatuous

1. ADJ

/ ˈfætʃuəs / (AmE), / ˈfætjʊəs / (BrE)
stupid or foolish in a self-satisfied way; extremely silly or pointless.
extremely silly or foolish

Examples

She dismissed his fatuous remarks with a wave of her hand.
The debate was marred by fatuous comments from several audience members.
It would be fatuous to assume that problems will solve themselves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silly - lacking in good sense; absurd or foolish
  • Antonym: intelligent - having or showing intelligence; smart

Etymology

From Latin fatuus (foolish, silly).
