Safar

1. NOUN

/ sɑˈfɑr / (AmE), / ˈsɑːfɑː / (BrE)
the second month of the Islamic lunar calendar
second month of the Islamic calendar

Examples

Many Muslims observe special days during Safar, the second month of the Islamic calendar.
The Islamic new year starts with Muharram, followed by Safar.
During Safar, some communities perform acts of charity and reflection.

Etymology

From Arabic صفر (ṣafar), meaning 'to be empty' or as a proper noun for the month.
