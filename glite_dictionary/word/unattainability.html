unattainability

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.əˌteɪ.nəˈbɪl.ə.t̬i / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˌteɪ.nəˈbɪl.ə.ti / (BrE)

the quality of being impossible to reach or obtain
