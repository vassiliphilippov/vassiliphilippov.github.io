amir

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɪr / (AmE), / əˈmɪə / (BrE)
An independent ruler or chieftain, especially in parts of Africa or Arabia.
independent ruler or chieftain

Examples

The amir led his people through a period of great change.
Several villages were under the rule of a single amir in the region.
The British negotiated directly with the local amir to ensure peace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emir - Muslim (especially Arab) ruler; variant spelling of 'amir'

Etymology

From Arabic 'amīr (commander, prince).
