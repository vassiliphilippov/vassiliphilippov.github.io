Vedist

1. NOUN

/ ˈveɪdɪst / (AmE), / ˈviːdɪst / (BrE)
a scholar or expert on the Vedas, the ancient sacred texts of Hinduism
scholar of the Vedas

Examples

Dr. Kulkarni is a renowned Vedist who has published several books on Vedic rituals.
The conference featured a keynote speech by a leading Vedist from India.
As a Vedist, she can read and interpret ancient Sanskrit scriptures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Vedicist - a specialist in Vedic studies

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Veda' (knowledge) + English suffix '-ist' (one who practices or is concerned with something).
