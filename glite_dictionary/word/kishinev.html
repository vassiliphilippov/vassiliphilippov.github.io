Kishinev

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪʃɪˌnɛv / (AmE), / ˈkɪʃɪˌnɛv / (BrE)
The former name of Chișinău, the capital city of Moldova.
former name of capital of Moldova

Examples

The Jewish community in Kishinev has a long and complex history.
After World War II, many families relocated from Kishinev to other regions.
He wrote a book about the pogroms that took place in Kishinev in the early 20th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chișinău - Current name for the capital of Moldova

Etymology

From Russian Кишинёв (Kishinyov), the Russian form of the city's name.
