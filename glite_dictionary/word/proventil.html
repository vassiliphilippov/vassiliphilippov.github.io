Proventil

1. NOUN

/ ˈproʊvənˌtɪl / (AmE), / ˈprəʊvənˌtɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked bronchodilator medication (also known as albuterol), used to treat asthma, emphysema, and other lung conditions.
bronchodilator drug for asthma
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Proventil to help control my asthma attacks.
The pharmacist explained how to use the Proventil inhaler properly.
After taking Proventil, she noticed relief from her shortness of breath.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ventolin - brand name for the same bronchodilator drug (albuterol)
