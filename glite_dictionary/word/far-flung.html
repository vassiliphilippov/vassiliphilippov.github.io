far-flung

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɑr ˌflʌŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɑː ˌflʌŋ / (BrE)
Spread or distributed over a very wide area; distant and remote.
spread over a wide area; distant

Examples

The company has far-flung offices in over twenty countries.
They communicated with relatives in far-flung parts of the world.
Scientists studied the effects of pollution in far-flung regions of the Arctic.

Synset Examples

far-flung trading operations
the West's far-flung mountain ranges
widespread nuclear fallout

Related Terms

  • Synonym: widespread - spread or scattered over a large area
  • Synonym: remote - distant in space; far away
