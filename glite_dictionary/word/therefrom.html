therefrom

1. ADV

/ ðɛrˈfrʌm / (AmE), / ðeəˈfrʌm / (BrE)
from that thing, place, or source; as a result of that.
from that source or circumstance

Examples

The investigator found new evidence and drew several conclusions therefrom.
Profits have increased, and many shareholders have benefited therefrom.
Damages resulting therefrom will be assessed by the court.

Synset Examples

atomic formulas and all compounds thence constructible
a natural conclusion follows thence
public interest and a policy deriving therefrom
typhus fever results therefrom

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thereof - from that; of that
  • Synonym: wherefrom - from what (thing, source, or place); from which

Etymology

From Middle English 'therefrom', from Old English 'þǣr' (there) + 'from'.
