pernio

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜːr.ni.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.ni.əʊ / (BrE)
A condition marked by inflammation of the skin, usually on the hands and feet, caused by exposure to cold and damp conditions; also known as chilblains.
inflammation of skin from cold; chilblains
medical

Examples

After hiking in wet, cold weather, she developed pernio on her toes and fingers.
Proper winter clothing can help prevent pernio, especially in children and the elderly.
The doctor diagnosed his swollen, red skin as pernio and advised gentle warming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chilblain - painful, itchy swelling of skin from cold

Etymology

From Latin *pernio* (chilblain), related to *perna* (ham, from the swelling's shape).
