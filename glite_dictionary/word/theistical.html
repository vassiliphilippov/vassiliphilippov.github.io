theistical

1. ADJ

/ θiˈɪstɪkəl / (AmE), / θiˈɪstɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of theism or belief in a god or gods
relating to theism

Examples

Her worldview is deeply theistical, centered on faith in a personal deity.
The philosopher wrote several theistical arguments addressing the existence of God.
Some ancient cultures developed theistical explanations for natural phenomena.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theistic - relating to belief in the existence of a god or gods

Etymology

Formed from 'theism' + '-ical', modeled on other adjectives like 'theistic'.
