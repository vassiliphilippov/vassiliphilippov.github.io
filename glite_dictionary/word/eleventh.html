eleventh

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛvənθ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛvənθ / (BrE)
the person or thing that is number eleven in a series; the ordinal number coming after tenth and before twelfth
the eleventh in a sequence

Examples

Her birthday falls on the eleventh of September every year.
The team finished in eleventh place out of twenty.
He was the eleventh person to arrive at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: tenth - the number ten in a series
  • Degree Higher: twelfth - the number twelve in a series

Etymology

From Middle English elleventhe, formed from eleven + -th (ordinal suffix).

2. ADJ

/ ɪˈlɛvənθ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛvənθ / (BrE)
coming next after the tenth and just before the twelfth in order or position
11th; next after tenth

Examples

She finished in eleventh place in the race, just missing the top ten.
His birthday falls on the eleventh of March every year.
This is the eleventh time I've asked you to clean your room.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: tenth - coming next after the ninth
  • Degree Higher: twelfth - coming next after the eleventh

Etymology

From Old English endleofan (eleven) + -th (ordinal suffix).
