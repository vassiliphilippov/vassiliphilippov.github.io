prostitution

1. NOUN

/ ˌproʊ.stəˈtuː.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌprɒs.tɪˈtjuː.ʃən / (BrE)
the practice or occupation of engaging in sexual activity with someone for payment
engaging in sexual activity for payment

Examples

Prostitution is illegal in many countries, but some places have legalized or regulated it.
The documentary explored the realities of prostitution in major cities.
Many laws aim to address the social issues surrounding prostitution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sex work - the provision of sexual services for money, often broader than prostitution

Etymology

From Latin prostitutio (exposure to debauchery), from prostituere (to expose publicly, offer for sale).
