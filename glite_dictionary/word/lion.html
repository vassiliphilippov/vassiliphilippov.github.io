Lion

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪ.ən / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.ən / (BrE)
the fifth sign of the zodiac, represented by a lion; the sun is in this sign from about July 23 to August 22
fifth sign of the zodiac; Leo

Examples

People born under the sign of Lion are said to be confident and charismatic.
He checked his horoscope for Lion before making any big decisions.
Many astrologers believe that Lion is a powerful sign associated with leadership.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Leo - the fifth astrological sign of the zodiac, symbolized by the lion
  • Degree Lower: Cancer - the fourth sign of the zodiac, preceding Lion/Leo
  • Degree Higher: Virgo - the sixth sign of the zodiac, following Lion/Leo

Etymology

From Latin 'leo' (lion), used as the name of the zodiac sign.
