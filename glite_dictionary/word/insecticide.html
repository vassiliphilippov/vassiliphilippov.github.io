insecticide

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈsɛktɪˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛktɪsaɪd / (BrE)
A chemical substance used for killing insects.
chemical for killing insects

Examples

Farmers often use insecticide to protect crops from pests.
Make sure to store insecticide away from children and pets.
This organic insecticide is both effective and environmentally friendly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pesticide - a chemical used to kill pests

Etymology

From Latin 'insectum' (insect) + '-cide' (killing).
