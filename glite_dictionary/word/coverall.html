coverall

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌvərˌɔl / (AmE), / ˈkʌvərˌɔːl / (BrE)
A loose-fitting protective garment worn over other clothing, often used to protect against dirt or hazards.
protective overall garment

Examples

He put on his coverall before starting work in the paint shop.
Mechanics often wear coveralls to protect their clothes from grease and oil.
The workers wore blue coveralls for safety in the factory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overall - a similar protective garment covering most of the body
