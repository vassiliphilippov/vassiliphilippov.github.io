postal

1. ADJ

/ ˈpoʊstəl / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstl / (BrE)
relating to or involving the sending or delivery of mail by a public or private service.
relating to mail delivery

Examples

The postal service introduced new delivery options last year.
She filed a complaint about delays in postal deliveries to her neighborhood.
Many countries have modernized their postal systems to handle online shopping demand.

Synset Examples

postal delivery

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mail - the system for sending letters and parcels

Etymology

From French postal, from poste (post).
