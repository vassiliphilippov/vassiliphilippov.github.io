succinic

1. ADJ

/ səkˈsɪnɪk / (AmE), / sʌkˈsɪnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or derived from amber, especially referring to succinic acid or its compounds.
relating to or from amber

Examples

The researchers analyzed the succinic compounds found in the mineral samples.
Succinic acid is present in many plant and animal tissues.
Amber jewelry sometimes contains traces of succinic substances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: succinic acid - a dicarboxylic acid derived from amber, used in industry and biochemistry

Etymology

From Latin succinum (amber).
