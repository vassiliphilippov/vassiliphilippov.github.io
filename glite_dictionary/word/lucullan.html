lucullan

1. ADJ

/ luːˈkʌlən / (AmE), / luːˈkʌlən / (BrE)
Extremely lavish or luxurious, especially referring to feasts or banquets.
extremely lavish or luxurious
archaic

Examples

The ambassador hosted a lucullan banquet that lasted well into the night.
The couple celebrated their anniversary with a lucullan feast at the city's finest restaurant.
Despite his humble origins, the chef was known for preparing lucullan meals for visiting dignitaries.

Synset Examples

a lavish buffet
a lucullan feast

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lavish - sumptuously rich or luxurious
  • Synonym: luxurious - extremely comfortable or elegant
  • Antonym: austere - simple or strict in appearance or manner

Etymology

From Latin Lucullus, the name of a Roman general famous for his luxurious banquets.
