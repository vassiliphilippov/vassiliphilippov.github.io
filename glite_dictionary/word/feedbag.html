feedbag

1. NOUN

/ ˈfidˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈfiːdbæɡ / (BrE)
a canvas or cloth bag placed over the muzzle of a horse or other animal to provide feed and prevent waste
bag used to feed horses or animals

Examples

The groom placed a feedbag on the horse after the morning ride.
Make sure the feedbag is securely fastened to prevent oats from spilling.
In the old barn, several feedbags hung on hooks, ready for use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nosebag - synonym meaning 'bag for feeding animals, worn over nose'
