kismat

1. NOUN

/ kɪsˈmɑt / (AmE), / kɪsˈmæt / (BrE)
(in South Asian or Muslim contexts) fate or destiny, regarded as determined by a divine will.
fate or destiny by divine will

Examples

Many people in the village believe their kismat is already written and cannot be changed.
He attributed his surprising success to pure kismat, rather than hard work.
They met again years later, convinced it was kismat that brought them together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fate - the development of events beyond a person's control, regarded as determined by a supernatural power
  • Synonym: destiny - the events that will necessarily happen to a particular person or thing in the future

Etymology

From Hindi-Urdu *kismat*, from Arabic *qisma* (portion, lot).
