hack-driver

1. NOUN

/ hæk ˈdraɪvər / (AmE), / hæk ˈdraɪvə / (BrE)
a person who drives a taxi for a living
taxi driver

Examples

The hack-driver waited patiently outside the hotel for his next fare.
In old New York, every hack-driver knew the fastest way through traffic.
He worked as a hack-driver to support his family during college.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxi driver - a person who drives a taxi as a job

Etymology

From 'hack' (short for 'hackney'), meaning a hired carriage or vehicle, and 'driver'.
