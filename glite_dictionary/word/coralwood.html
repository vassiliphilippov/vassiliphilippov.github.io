coralwood

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrəlˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəlˌwʊd / (BrE)
An East Indian tree with racemes of yellow-white flowers, cultivated as an ornamental.
East Indian ornamental tree

Examples

The coralwood is prized for its attractive yellow-white flowers.
Many gardens feature the coralwood because of its ornamental appeal.
Botanists study the coralwood for its unique flowering patterns.
