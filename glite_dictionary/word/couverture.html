couverture

1. NOUN

/ kuː.vərˈtjʊr / (AmE), / kuː.vəˈtjʊə / (BrE)
high-quality chocolate containing at least 32 percent cocoa butter, used especially for coating and dipping
high-quality chocolate for coating

Examples

The pastry chef used couverture to create a glossy finish on the truffles.
Always temper couverture chocolate carefully to achieve a perfect snap.
She ordered a kilogram of couverture for her next baking project.

Etymology

From French couverture (covering, coating).
