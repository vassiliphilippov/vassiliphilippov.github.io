pannikin

1. NOUN

/ ˈpænɪkɪn / (AmE), / ˈpænɪkɪn / (BrE)
a small metal pan or cup, especially one made of tin
small metal pan or cup

Examples

The old prospector drank his coffee from a battered pannikin by the fire.
She poured a little soup into a pannikin for the child to sip.
Each soldier was given a pannikin of water before the march.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cup - a small, round container used for drinking
  • Synonym: pan - a shallow cooking vessel

Etymology

From 'pan' with the diminutive suffix '-kin', meaning 'little pan'.
