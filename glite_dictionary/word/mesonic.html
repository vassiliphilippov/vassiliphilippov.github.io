mesonic

1. ADJ

/ mɛˈsɑnɪk / (AmE), / mɪˈsɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a meson or mesons (subatomic particles involved in nuclear interactions).
relating to a meson (physics)
medical

Examples

The researchers observed several mesonic interactions in the particle accelerator data.
She specialized in the study of mesonic decay processes within high-energy physics.
The properties of certain nuclei are influenced by their mesonic exchange forces.

Synset Examples

the radii of the mesic orbits

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meson - a subatomic particle composed of one quark and one antiquark

Etymology

From 'meson' (from Greek 'mesos' meaning 'middle') + '-ic' (forming adjectives).
