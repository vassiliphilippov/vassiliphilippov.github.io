Jan Hendrix Oort

1. PROPN

/ jɑn ˈhɛn.drɪks ˈɔrt / (AmE), / jan ˈhɛn.drɪks ˈɔːt / (BrE)
(1900–1992) A Dutch astronomer renowned for proving that the Milky Way galaxy rotates and for proposing the existence of the Oort cloud surrounding the solar system.
Dutch astronomer; proposed the Oort cloud

Examples

Jan Hendrix Oort demonstrated that the Milky Way rotates, revolutionizing our understanding of the galaxy.
The concept of the Oort cloud is named after the astronomer Jan Hendrix Oort.
As a pioneer in radio astronomy, Jan Hendrix Oort contributed significantly to twentieth-century astrophysics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Oort cloud - A hypothesized spherical shell of icy objects surrounding the solar system, proposed by Jan Oort
