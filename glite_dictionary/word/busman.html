busman

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌsmən / (AmE), / ˈbʌsmən / (BrE)
a man whose job is to drive a bus
man who drives a bus

Examples

The busman greeted each passenger as they boarded the vehicle.
After twenty years as a busman, he knew the city streets by heart.
The company hired a new busman to cover the morning shift.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bus driver - a person who drives a bus

Etymology

From 'bus' + 'man', modelled on similar occupational terms.
