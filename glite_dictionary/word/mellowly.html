mellowly

1. ADV

/ ˈmɛloʊli / (AmE), / ˈmɛl.əʊ.li / (BrE)
In a gentle, calm, or pleasantly smooth manner.
in a gentle or pleasantly smooth way

Examples

Her voice drifted mellowly through the quiet room, comforting everyone present.
The jazz music played mellowly in the background as we talked.
He mellowly responded to criticism, showing admirable restraint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gently - in a gentle manner

Etymology

From 'mellow' + '-ly', Old English 'melu' (soft) + suffix '-ly'.
