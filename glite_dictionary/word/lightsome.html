lightsome

/ ˈlaɪtsəm / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtsəm / (BrE)
moving in a light, easy, and graceful way; nimble and buoyant
moving easily and gracefully
archaic

Examples

She skipped down the path in a lightsome manner, her mood as buoyant as her step.
His lightsome movements made the difficult choreography look effortless.

Related Terms
