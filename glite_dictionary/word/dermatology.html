dermatology

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɜrməˈtɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌdɜːməˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with the diagnosis and treatment of skin disorders
study of skin and its diseases
medical

Examples

She decided to specialize in dermatology during her medical training.
Advances in dermatology have greatly improved treatments for skin conditions.
If you have persistent skin problems, you should visit a dermatology clinic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dermatologist - a doctor who specializes in dermatology

Etymology

From Greek 'derma' (skin) + 'logia' (study).
