William Seward Burroughs

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪljəm ˈsuːərd ˈbɜːroʊz / (AmE), / ˈwɪljəm ˈsjʊəd ˈbʌrəʊz / (BrE)
(1914–1997) United States writer known for his works depicting drug addiction and countercultural life, notably 'Naked Lunch'.
American writer (1914–1997), known for 'Naked Lunch'

Examples

Many critics consider William Seward Burroughs a major influence on the Beat Generation.
The controversial novel 'Naked Lunch' was written by William Seward Burroughs.
William Seward Burroughs explored themes of addiction and society in much of his work.
