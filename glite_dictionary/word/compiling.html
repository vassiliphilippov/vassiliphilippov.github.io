compiling

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpaɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / kəmˈpaɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of collecting and assembling information, texts, or data into a single book, file, or list
the act of assembling or collecting

Examples

The compiling of the report required input from all departments.
Every week, he spends hours compiling a list of new clients for his manager.
The compiling of historical documents is essential for academic research.

Synset Examples

the job of compiling the inventory took several hours

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compilation - the act or process of compiling; something compiled

Etymology

From the verb 'compile,' which derives from Latin 'compilare' (to plunder, bundle together).
