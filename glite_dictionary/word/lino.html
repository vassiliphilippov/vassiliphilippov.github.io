lino

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪnəʊ / (BrE)
A type of floor covering made from linoleum, often used in kitchens and bathrooms.
linoleum floor covering
informal british

Examples

We decided to replace the old carpet with lino in the kitchen.
She scrubbed the lino floor until it was sparkling clean.
The apartment came with cheap lino throughout the hallways.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: linoleum - a durable floor covering material made from solidified linseed oil

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'linoleum', from Latin 'linum' (flax) and 'oleum' (oil).
