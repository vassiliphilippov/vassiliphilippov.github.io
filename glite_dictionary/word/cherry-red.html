cherry-red

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɛri ˈrɛd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛri ˈrɛd / (BrE)
having a deep, bright red color similar to that of ripe cherries.
deep, bright red color

Examples

Her dress was a stunning cherry-red that drew everyone's attention at the party.
The car was painted in a bold cherry-red, making it easy to spot in the parking lot.
He picked a tie in cherry-red to add a touch of color to his suit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scarlet - bright red color with a slightly orange tinge
  • Synonym: crimson - deep rich red color inclining to purple
  • Synonym: ruby - bright, rich, red color like that of a ruby gemstone

Etymology

Formed from 'cherry' (the fruit) + 'red'.
