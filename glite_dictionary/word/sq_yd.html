sq yd

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr jɑrd / (AmE), / skweə jɑːd / (BrE)
an abbreviation for square yard, a unit of area equal to one yard by one yard (0.836 square meters)
abbreviation for square yard (area unit)

Examples

The carpet is sold by the sq yd, so be sure to measure the room carefully.
He ordered enough grass seed to cover 500 sq yd of lawn.
The price per sq yd of fabric was clearly marked in the catalog.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: square yard - a unit of area equal to a square with sides of one yard
  • Degree Lower: sq ft - abbreviation for square foot (area unit)
  • Degree Higher: sq m - abbreviation for square meter (area unit)
