optimisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːptɪməˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɒptɪmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
The act or process of making something as effective, perfect, or functional as possible.
the act of making something optimal
british

Examples

The new software allows for the optimisation of workflow across different departments.
Researchers are focusing on the optimisation of renewable energy systems.
Effective optimisation of the process led to significant cost reductions.

Synset Examples

the simultaneous optimization of growth and profitability
in an optimization problem we seek values of the variables that lead to an optimal value of the function that is to be optimized
to promote the optimization and diversification of agricultural products

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimization - the act of making something optimal; American spelling
  • Degree Lower: improvement - the process of making something better

Etymology

From optimize + -ation; British spelling of 'optimization'.
