tetragonal

1. ADJ

/ tɛˈtræɡənəl / (AmE), / tɛˈtræɡənəl / (BrE)
having four sides; relating to or shaped like a quadrilateral, especially in mathematics or crystallography.
having four sides; quadrilateral-related

Examples

The mineral crystalizes in a tetragonal system, giving it a beautiful symmetry.
The architect designed a building with a striking tetragonal outline.
Many mathematical problems explore the properties of tetragonal shapes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quadrilateral - a four-sided polygon

Etymology

From Greek 'tetragonos', meaning 'having four angles' (from 'tetra-' four + 'gōnia' angle).
