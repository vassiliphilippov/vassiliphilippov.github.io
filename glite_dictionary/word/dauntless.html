dauntless

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɔntləs / (AmE), / ˈdɔːntləs / (BrE)
showing fearlessness and determination; not easily intimidated or discouraged
fearless; determined

Examples

The dauntless firefighters entered the burning building without hesitation.
Despite the dangerous journey ahead, her dauntless spirit never wavered.
A dauntless attitude is essential for mountain climbers facing harsh conditions.

Synset Examples

audacious explorers
fearless reporters and photographers
intrepid pioneers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fearless - lacking fear; brave
  • Synonym: intrepid - resolutely fearless; adventurous
  • Antonym: cowardly - lacking courage; easily frightened

Etymology

From Middle English, from daunt (from Old French danter, from Latin domitare, frequentative of domare 'tame') + -less.
