advisedly

1. ADV

/ ədˈvaɪzɪdli / (AmE), / ədˈvaɪzɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is intentional and carefully considered; deliberately
intentionally and deliberately

Examples

I chose my words advisedly to avoid any misunderstandings.
She made that statement advisedly, aware of its potential impact.
He acted advisedly after considering all possible outcomes.

Synset Examples

he used that word intentionally
I did this by choice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deliberately - in a careful and intentional way
  • Antonym: unintentionally - not done on purpose or deliberately

Etymology

From Middle English, past participle of 'advise' plus '-ly'.
