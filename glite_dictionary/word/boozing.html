boozing

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuːzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbuːzɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of drinking alcoholic beverages, especially excessively or habitually.
drinking alcohol to excess
informal

Examples

He lost his job because of his constant boozing.
The doctor warned him that boozing could harm his liver.
After years of boozing, he decided it was time to quit drinking for good.

Synset Examples

drink was his downfall

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drinking - the act of consuming alcoholic beverages
  • Antonym: abstinence - the practice of restraining oneself from drinking alcohol

Etymology

From 'booze', likely from Middle Dutch 'busen' (to drink heavily).
