Bangkok

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæŋ.kɑk / (AmE), / ˈbæŋ.kɒk / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Thailand, known for its vibrant street life, cultural landmarks, and Buddhist temples
capital city of Thailand

Examples

I spent two weeks in Bangkok exploring its historic temples and bustling markets.
The flight from London to Bangkok takes approximately twelve hours.
Many tourists use Bangkok as a base for traveling around Thailand.

Etymology

From Thai กรุงเทพฯ (Krung Thep); the full ceremonial name is much longer and reflects Thai royal and religious traditions.
