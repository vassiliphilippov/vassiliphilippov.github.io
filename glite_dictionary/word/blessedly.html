blessedly

1. ADV

/ ˈblɛsɪdli / (AmE), / ˈblɛsɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that is fortunate, bringing relief, or worthy of blessing.
in a fortunate or relieving manner

Examples

After hours of chaos, the house was blessedly quiet again.
He was blessedly unaware of the troubles that awaited him.
The painkiller began to work, and she felt blessedly free from discomfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fortunately - in a fortunate manner; luckily
  • Antonym: unfortunately - in an unfortunate manner; regrettably
