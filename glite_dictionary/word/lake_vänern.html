Lake Vänern

1. PROPN

/ veɪˈnɛrn / (AmE), / vɛˈnɛːn / (BrE)
the largest lake in Sweden, located in the southwestern part of the country
largest lake in Sweden

The city of Karlstad is situated on the northern shore of Lake Vänern.
