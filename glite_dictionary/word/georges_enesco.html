Georges Enesco

1. PROPN

/ ʒɔrʒ ɛˈnɛskoʊ / (AmE), / ʒɔːʒ ɛˈnɛskəʊ / (BrE)
(1881–1955) A Romanian violinist, composer, conductor, and teacher regarded as one of the greatest musicians of his era.
Romanian violinist and composer (1881–1955)

Examples

Many music scholars consider Georges Enesco to be Romania's greatest composer.
The works of Georges Enesco are frequently performed in international concert halls.
Georges Enesco was also the teacher of the famous violinist Yehudi Menuhin.

Etymology

Romanian form of his name is George Enescu.
