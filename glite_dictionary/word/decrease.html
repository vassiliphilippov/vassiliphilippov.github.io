decrease

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈkris / (AmE), / dɪˈkriːs / (BrE)
a process or instance of becoming smaller in size, amount, or number
process of becoming smaller

Examples

There has been a significant decrease in the number of wild bees in recent years.
The factory reported a decrease in production due to equipment failure.
A sudden decrease in temperature caught everyone by surprise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reduction - the act of making something smaller or less
  • Synonym: decline - a gradual decrease in quality, amount, or importance
  • Antonym: increase - a process of becoming larger or greater

Etymology

From Old French 'decreistre', from Latin 'decrescere' (to grow less).
