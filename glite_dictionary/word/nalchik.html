Nalchik

1. PROPN

/ ˈnæl.tʃɪk / (AmE), / ˈnæl.tʃɪk / (BrE)
A city in southwestern Russia, located in a valley of the Caucasus Mountains, known as an industrial center and health resort.
city in southwestern Russia

Examples

Many tourists visit Nalchik for its mineral springs and scenic mountain views.
The conference was held in Nalchik, attracting participants from all over the region.
