reposeful

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈpoʊsfəl / (AmE), / rɪˈpəʊsfəl / (BrE)
calm and peaceful, making you feel relaxed and rested; affording physical or mental rest.
calm, restful, relaxing

Examples

The reposeful atmosphere of the garden made her want to stay there all afternoon.
Soft colors and gentle music gave the room a reposeful quality.
After the busy week, he found the quiet cabin wonderfully reposeful.

Synset Examples

she spent a restful night at home

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restful - giving rest or comfort, peaceful
  • Synonym: soothing - having a calming or relaxing effect
  • Antonym: stimulating - encouraging activity or interest, mentally or physically exciting

Etymology

From 'repose' (rest, calm) + '-ful'.
