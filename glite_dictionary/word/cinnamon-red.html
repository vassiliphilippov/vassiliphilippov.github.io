cinnamon-red

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪnəmən rɛd / (AmE), / ˈsɪnəmən rɛd / (BrE)
of a red color tinged with the shade of cinnamon; reddish-brown.
red tinged with cinnamon

Examples

She chose a cinnamon-red scarf to complement her autumn wardrobe.
The feathers of the bird glowed a striking cinnamon-red in the sunlight.
A cinnamon-red hue spread across the western sky at sunset.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reddish-brown - a color that is a mixture of red and brown
