pilary

1. ADJ

/ ˈpaɪləri / (AmE), / ˈpɪləri / (BrE)
(of a surface or covering) covered with hairs, especially fine or soft ones.
covered with fine hairs

Examples

The leaf's surface was pilary, giving it a velvety texture.
Under the microscope, the stem appeared distinctly pilary.
Some seeds are pilary to aid in wind dispersal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairy - covered with hair; having a lot of hair

Etymology

From Latin 'pilus' (hair) with English adjectival suffix '-ary'.
