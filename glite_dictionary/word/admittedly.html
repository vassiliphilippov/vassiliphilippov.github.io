admittedly

1. ADV

/ ədˈmɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ədˈmɪtɪdli / (BrE)
used to concede or acknowledge a fact or point, often before making a contrasting statement
used to acknowledge a fact

Examples

Admittedly, the movie had some flaws, but it was still entertaining overall.
Admittedly, I don't have all the answers, but I'm willing to help.
Admittedly, the weather was bad that day, which made our journey even harder.

Synset Examples

true, she is the smartest in her class

Etymology

From 'admit' + '-edly', following the pattern of forming adverbs from verbs.
