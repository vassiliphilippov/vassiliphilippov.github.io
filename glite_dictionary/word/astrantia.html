astrantia

1. NOUN

/ əˈstrænʃiə / (AmE), / əˈstrænʃɪə / (BrE)
a genus of flowering plants, commonly known as masterwort, cultivated for their decorative umbels of small flowers
a genus of flowering plants

Examples

The astrantia in the garden bloomed beautifully throughout the summer.
She chose astrantia and peonies for her wedding bouquet.
Many gardeners appreciate astrantia for its unique, star-shaped flowers.

Etymology

From modern Latin, based on Greek 'astēr' meaning 'star', referring to the star-shaped flower heads.
