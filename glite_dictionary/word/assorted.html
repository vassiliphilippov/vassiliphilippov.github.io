assorted

1. ADJ

/ əˈsɔɹ.tɪd / (AmE), / əˈsɔː.tɪd / (BrE)
of various different types, especially put together in no special order; mixed or diverse.
of various different types; mixed

Examples

The candy box contained an assorted selection of chocolates and caramels.
We bought an assorted pack of colored pens for the project.
The restaurant offers an assorted platter of cheese and meats.

Synset Examples

an arrangement of assorted spring flowers
assorted sizes
miscellaneous accessories
a mixed program of baroque and contemporary music
a motley crew
sundry sciences commonly known as social

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miscellaneous - of various different types; not sorted or categorized
  • Synonym: mixed - made up of different types or kinds

Etymology

From the verb 'assort', from Old French 'assortir' (to arrange), from 'a-' (to) + 'sort' (kind, type).

2. ADJ

/ əˈsɔr.t̬ɪd / (AmE), / əˈsɔː.tɪd / (BrE)
of different kinds, especially deliberately grouped together but not matching exactly
of various kinds or types

Examples

The box contained assorted chocolates, each with a unique filling.
She bought a bouquet of assorted wildflowers for the centerpiece.
We served assorted cheeses and crackers at the party.

Synset Examples

assorted sizes
his disguises are many and various
various experiments have failed to disprove the theory
cited various reasons for his behavior

Related Terms

  • Synonym: various - of different kinds, not all the same
  • Synonym: miscellaneous - made up of a variety of different elements or items
  • Antonym: identical - exactly the same in every respect

Etymology

From the verb 'assort' (to classify or arrange), originally from Old French 'assortir'.
