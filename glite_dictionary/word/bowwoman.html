bowwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈbəʊˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who is skilled in the use of a bow and arrow; a female archer.
female archer

Examples

The competition featured a renowned bowwoman from Korea, known for her precise aim.
In ancient times, a skilled bowwoman could determine the outcome of a battle.
As the only bowwoman on the team, she trained harder to prove her abilities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: archer - a person who is skilled in the use of a bow and arrow
  • Synonym: bowman - a man who is expert in the use of a bow and arrow
