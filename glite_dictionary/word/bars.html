bars

1. NOUN

/ bɑrz / (AmE), / bɑːz / (BrE)
a gymnastic apparatus consisting of two parallel wooden or metal rods supported on uprights, used for various exercises
gymnastic apparatus of parallel rods

Examples

She trained on the bars every morning before school.
The gymnast performed a flawless routine on the bars.
In competitions, both men and women demonstrate great skill on the bars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parallel bars - gymnastic apparatus of two horizontal bars
