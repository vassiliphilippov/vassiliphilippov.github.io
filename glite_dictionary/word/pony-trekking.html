pony-trekking

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊniˌtrɛkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊniˌtrɛkɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of riding across country on ponies, often as an organized leisure or sport activity
sport of riding ponies across country

Examples

We booked a weekend of pony-trekking in the Scottish Highlands.
Pony-trekking is popular among tourists who want to explore the countryside at a relaxed pace.
She enjoyed pony-trekking through the national park with her friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horseback riding - the activity of riding horses for recreation or sport
  • Degree Lower: hiking - the activity of taking long walks in the countryside

Etymology

Formed from 'pony' (small horse) + 'trekking' (from trek, meaning to travel or journey, especially slowly or with difficulty).
