unmechanical

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnməˈkænɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnmɪˈkænɪkəl / (BrE)
(of a person) lacking aptitude or skill in working with machines or mechanical tasks.
lacking mechanical skill

Examples

Despite being intelligent, John was completely unmechanical and struggled with simple repairs.
Her unmechanical nature made assembling furniture a daunting task.
Many creative people are also surprisingly unmechanical in practical matters.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mechanically minded - having an aptitude for understanding machines or mechanical tasks

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'mechanical'.
