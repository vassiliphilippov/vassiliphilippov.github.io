coiffure

1. NOUN

/ kwɑːˈfjʊr / (AmE), / kwɑːˈfjʊə / (BrE)
A style or arrangement of hair, especially one created by a hairdresser.
arrangement or style of hair

Examples

The elegant coiffure she wore to the gala drew many admiring glances.
His mother visited the salon weekly for her favorite coiffure.
Every era has its own distinctive coiffure that reflects the fashion of the time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairstyle - a particular way in which a person's hair is cut or arranged

Etymology

From French 'coiffure' (hairstyle), from 'coiffer' (to arrange the hair).

2. VERB

/ kwɑˈfjʊr / (AmE), / kwɑːˈfjʊə / (BrE)
to arrange or style (hair), especially in an elaborate or careful way
to style or arrange hair

Examples

A specialist was hired to coiffure the actress's hair before the big event.
She skillfully coiffured her daughter's curls for the performance.
He spent an hour coiffuring his hair into perfect waves.

Synset Examples

dress my hair for the wedding

Related Terms

  • Synonym: style - to arrange or design hair in a particular way

Etymology

From French 'coiffer' (to do one's hair); related to 'coiffe' (headdress).
