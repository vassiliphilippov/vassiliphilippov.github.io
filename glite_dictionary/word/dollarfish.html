dollarfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑlərˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdɒləˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a small food fish (usually referring to the butterfish or similar species) found along the Atlantic coast of North America
a small Atlantic food fish

Examples

Fishermen caught several dollarfish off the coast this morning.
The restaurant served broiled dollarfish with seasonal vegetables.
Many anglers prefer to use nets when trying to catch dollarfish in shallow waters.

2. NOUN

/ ˈdɑlərˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdɒləˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
A silvery marine fish with a very flat body, belonging to several species.
a flat, silvery marine fish

Examples

Divers were delighted to see a school of dollarfish gliding through the clear water.
The dollarfish is known for its flat, round shape that resembles a silver coin.
Fishermen sometimes confuse dollarfish with other silvery fish due to their similar appearance.
