Senhor

1. NOUN

/ seˈnɔr / (AmE), / seˈnjɔː / (BrE)
a Portuguese title of respect used before a man's name; equivalent to Mr.
Portuguese title of respect (Mr)

Examples

The ambassador was greeted as Senhor Silva by the local officials.
Please address the letter to Senhor Almeida, the company's director.
In formal occasions, guests were introduced as Senhor or Senhora followed by their surnames.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mr - title of respect used before a man's name in English

Etymology

Portuguese, from Latin 'senior' (older, elder).
