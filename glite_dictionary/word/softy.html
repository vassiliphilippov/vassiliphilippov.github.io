softy

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔfti / (AmE), / ˈsɒfti / (BrE)
A person who is easily influenced emotionally and is excessively sentimental or lacking in toughness.
overly sentimental or weak person

Examples

Despite his gruff exterior, he's a real softy when it comes to animals.
Don't be such a softy; you need to stand up for yourself!
She knew she could count on him because he was a softy at heart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: softie - a person who is weak and sentimental (alternative spelling)
  • Antonym: hard-nosed - tough or unsentimental person
