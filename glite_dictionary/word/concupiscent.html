concupiscent

1. ADJ

/ kɑnˈkjuː.pə.sənt / (AmE), / kɒnˈkjuː.pɪ.sənt / (BrE)
Full of strong sexual desire; lustful or passionately sensual.
full of sexual desire; lustful

Examples

His concupiscent gaze made her uncomfortable at the party.
The novel's protagonist is driven by concupiscent desires that ultimately lead to his downfall.
In the painting, the artist uses bold colors to evoke a concupiscent atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lustful - having or showing strong sexual desire
  • Antonym: chaste - abstaining from sexual activity or desires

Etymology

From Latin concupiscent-, present participle of concupiscere (to desire greatly).
