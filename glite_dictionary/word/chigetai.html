chigetai

1. NOUN

/ tʃiːɡəˈtaɪ / (AmE), / tʃiːɡəˈtaɪ / (BrE)
A wild ass native to Central Asia, especially Mongolia, closely related to the onager.
Mongolian wild ass

Examples

The chigetai roams the grassy steppes of Mongolia and northern China.
Conservation efforts are aimed at protecting the chigetai from poaching and habitat loss.
Wild chigetais are known for their remarkable speed and stamina.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: onager - Another species of wild ass found in Asia

Etymology

From Mongolian 'khulan', borrowed as a scientific/zoological term in English.
