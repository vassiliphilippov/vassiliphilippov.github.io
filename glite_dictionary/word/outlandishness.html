outlandishness

1. NOUN

/ aʊtˈlændɪʃnəs / (AmE), / aʊtˈlændɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being very unusual or strange in appearance, ideas, or behavior
the quality of being strange or unusual

Examples

The outlandishness of his costume drew everyone's attention at the party.
Her outlandishness made her stand out in the conservative town.
Critics admired the outlandishness of the artist's new work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentricity - the quality of being unconventional and slightly strange
  • Antonym: normality - the state of being normal; typical or expected

Etymology

From 'outlandish' + '-ness', meaning the state or quality of being outlandish.
