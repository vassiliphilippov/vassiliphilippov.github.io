pyrolytic

1. ADJ

/ paɪrəˈlɪtɪk / (AmE), / paɪrəˈlɪtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or produced by pyrolysis.
resulting from pyrolysis

Examples

The laboratory synthesized pyrolytic graphite for use in specialized equipment.
During the process, organic material undergoes pyrolytic decomposition at high temperatures.
The study focused on analyzing pyrolytic residues formed after heating the polymer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pyrolysis - the chemical decomposition of a substance by heat

Etymology

From 'pyrolysis' (chemical decomposition by heat) and '-ic' (forming adjectives).
