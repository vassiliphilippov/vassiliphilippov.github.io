to gormandize

1. VERB

/ ˈɡɔrmənˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːmənˌdaɪz / (BrE)
To eat food greedily or in excess; to overeat, especially in an immodest or gluttonous manner.
eat greedily or excessively

Examples

He would often gormandize at buffets, sampling every dish until he was completely full.
During the holidays, it's easy to gormandize on sweets and rich food.
She warned the children not to gormandize on snacks before dinner.

Synset Examples

She stuffed herself at the dinner
The kids binged on ice cream

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to overeat - to eat too much food
  • Synonym: to gluttonize - to eat to excess, be gluttonous

Etymology

From French *gourmandiser* (to glut oneself), from *gourmand* (glutton).
