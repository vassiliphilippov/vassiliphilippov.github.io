Fang

1. NOUN

/ fæŋ / (AmE), / faŋ / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken chiefly in Cameroon, Equatorial Guinea, and Gabon.
Bantu language of Cameroon, Equatorial Guinea, Gabon

Examples

Many people in Cameroon speak Fang as their first language.
She is fluent in both French and Fang.
The book was translated from Fang into English.
