schoolroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈskuːlˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈskuːlˌruːm / (BrE)
a room in a school where classes or lessons are held
room in a school for lessons

Examples

The children returned to the schoolroom after lunch.
Old wooden desks lined the schoolroom in neat rows.
A map of the world hung on the wall in the schoolroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classroom - a room where classes are taught in a school or college

Etymology

Formed from 'school' + 'room' in English.
