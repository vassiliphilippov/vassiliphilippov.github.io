modish

1. ADJ

/ ˈmoʊ.dɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.dɪʃ / (BrE)
Conforming to or following the current fashion or style.
in current fashion or style

Examples

She wore a modish hat that caught everyone’s attention at the gala.
The restaurant’s modish decor attracted a young, trendy clientele.
His modish haircut made him look much younger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fashionable - conforming to current fashion
  • Antonym: outmoded - no longer in fashion

Etymology

From French 'mode' (fashion), from Latin 'modus' (measure, manner).
