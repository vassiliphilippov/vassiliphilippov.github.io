theatregoer

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiət̬ɚˌɡoʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈθɪətəˌɡəʊə / (BrE)
a person who regularly attends the theater, especially as an audience member of plays and performances
someone who attends the theater

Examples

As an avid theatregoer, she rarely missed a new production in the city.
The theatregoer enjoyed discussing the performance during intermission.
Discounts were offered to frequent theatregoers throughout the theatre festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: playgoer - someone who attends plays or theatrical performances

Etymology

From 'theatre' + 'goer', indicating a person who goes to the theater.
