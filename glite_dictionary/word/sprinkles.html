sprinkles

1. NOUN

/ ˈsprɪŋkəlz / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋklz / (BrE)
small pieces of sweet chocolate or colored sugar used to decorate cakes, ice cream, and other desserts
small sweet toppings for desserts

Examples

The kids love to add sprinkles to their ice cream sundaes.
She decorated the cupcakes with chocolate sprinkles for the party.
A generous handful of rainbow sprinkles made the cake look festive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hundreds and thousands - tiny colored sugar balls used as cake decoration (British usage)
