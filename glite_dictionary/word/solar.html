solar

1. ADJ

/ ˈsoʊlɚ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or derived from the sun, or utilizing the sun's energy
relating to the sun or its energy

Examples

Many homes now use solar panels to generate electricity from sunlight.
A solar eclipse occurs when the moon passes between the earth and the sun.
Scientists are developing new solar technologies to combat climate change.

Synset Examples

solar eclipse
solar energy

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lunar - relating to the moon

Etymology

From Latin 'solaris' (of the sun), from 'sol' (sun).
