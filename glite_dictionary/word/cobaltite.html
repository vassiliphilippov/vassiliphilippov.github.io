cobaltite

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊˌbɔltaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌbɔːltaɪt / (BrE)
a rare silvery-white mineral that is an important ore of cobalt
rare mineral; cobalt ore

Examples

Congo is known for its rich deposits of cobaltite and other valuable minerals.
The geologist carefully examined the cobaltite crystals under a microscope.
Mining operations extracted large quantities of cobaltite for industrial use.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: cobalt - a hard, silvery-white metallic element

Etymology

From cobalt, with the suffix -ite, indicating a mineral.
