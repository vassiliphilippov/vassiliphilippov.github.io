hairstylist

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛrˌstaɪlɪst / (AmE), / ˈheəˌstaɪlɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to cut, style, and beautify hair
person who cuts and styles hair

Examples

My hairstylist gave me a completely new look for the wedding.
She has been working as a hairstylist in the city center for ten years.
After moving, it took me months to find a new hairstylist I liked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairdresser - a person who cuts, styles, and treats hair, often in a salon
