bristlelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈbrɪsəlˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbrɪsəlˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling a bristle in appearance or texture; stiff and hairlike
resembling a bristle

Examples

The cactus is covered in bristlelike spines that protect it from predators.
Under the microscope, the insect's legs appeared bristlelike and rigid.
Some grasses have bristlelike structures at the tips of their leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bristly - having stiff hairs or bristles
