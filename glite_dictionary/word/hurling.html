hurling

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɜrlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɜːlɪŋ / (BrE)
an Irish team sport resembling field hockey, played with sticks and a ball by two teams of 15 players
traditional Irish team sport

Examples

He has played hurling since he was a child in County Cork.
The All-Ireland hurling final attracts thousands of passionate fans every year.
After seeing a match, she became fascinated by the speed and skill of hurling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camogie - the women's version of hurling, played in Ireland
