vape

1. NOUN

/ veɪp / (AmE), / veɪp / (BrE)
An electronic cigarette or device that produces a nicotine-containing vapor for inhalation.
electronic cigarette device

Examples

He always carries his vape with him instead of traditional cigarettes.
The new regulations prohibit the use of a vape in public areas.
She bought a flavored vape to help her quit smoking tobacco.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: e-cigarette - a device that simulates tobacco smoking by producing a vapor

Etymology

Shortened form of 'vaporizer' and influenced by 'vapor.'

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-90000271-n
Synset POS: n

2. VERB

/ veɪp / (AmE), / veɪp / (BrE)
to inhale and exhale vapor produced by an electronic cigarette or similar device
use an electronic cigarette

Examples

Many young people prefer to vape rather than smoke traditional cigarettes.
She started vaping as a way to quit smoking.
After lunch, he stepped outside to vape with his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoke - to inhale and exhale the smoke of a cigarette, cigar, or pipe

Etymology

Blend of 'vapor' and 'cigarette'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-90000271-v
Synset POS: v
