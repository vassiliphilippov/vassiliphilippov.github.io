Areca

1. NOUN

/ əˈriːkə / (AmE), / əˈriːkə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical Asian palm trees, including the betel palm, characterized by their tall trunks and feather-like leaves.
genus of tropical Asian palm trees

Examples

The Areca genus includes the well-known betel nut palm, which is widely cultivated in Asia.
Several species in the Areca genus are valued for their ornamental and economic uses.
Botanists classified the new palm specimen under the Areca genus based on its distinctive features.

Etymology

From Portuguese areca, from Malayalam *adakka*, referring to the betel nut palm.
