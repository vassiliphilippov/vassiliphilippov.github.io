playactor

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪˌæktər / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌæktə / (BrE)
an actor who travels around performing plays, especially in different towns or regions
travelling actor performing plays

Examples

The traveling playactor brought Shakespearean drama to small villages across the country.
In the 19th century, a playactor often faced unpredictable audiences and rough traveling conditions.
She admired the life of a playactor, imagining the thrill of performing in new towns every week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: actor - a person who performs in plays, movies, or television shows
  • Synonym: troupe - a group of actors, dancers, or other entertainers who tour to different venues

Etymology

Formed from the words 'play' and 'actor', referring to an actor in theatrical plays.
