gynecologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌgaɪnəˈkɑːlɪk / (AmE), / ˌɡaɪnɪkəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to gynecology or the health and diseases of the female reproductive system.
relating to gynecology
medical

Examples
