Tammerfors

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɑːmərˌfɔrs / (AmE), / ˈtamɛrfɔːs / (BrE)
the Swedish name for the city of Tampere, an important industrial city in south central Finland
Swedish name for Tampere, city in Finland

Examples

The city of Tammerfors is known for its vibrant cultural scene and historic architecture.
When traveling through Finland, we stopped in Tammerfors to visit a famous museum.
Many Swedish-speaking Finns refer to Tampere as Tammerfors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tampere - industrial city in south central Finland, Finnish name

Etymology

Swedish, from 'tammer' (referring to the Tammerkoski rapids) and 'fors' (rapids).
