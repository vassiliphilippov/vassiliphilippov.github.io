Denebola

1. PROPN

/ dəˈnɛbələ / (AmE), / dɪˈnɛbələ / (BrE)
a bright star in the constellation Leo, approximately 43 light years from Earth
star in Leo constellation

Examples

Denebola is the second brightest star in the constellation Leo.
On clear nights, Denebola can sometimes be seen with the naked eye.
Astronomers have studied Denebola to learn more about stars similar to our Sun.

Etymology

From Arabic 'ðanab al-asad' (tail of the lion), referring to its position in Leo.
