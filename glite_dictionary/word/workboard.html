workboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɜrkˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌbɔːd / (BrE)
A flat, horizontal board that provides a stable surface for carrying out manual work.
horizontal board for manual work

Examples

She placed the fabric on the workboard before cutting it to size.
The artist covered her workboard with paints and tools as she worked.
He built a small workboard for his woodworking projects in the garage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workbench - a sturdy table at which manual work is done
