Stuttgart

1. PROPN

/ ˈstʊtˌɡɑrt / (AmE), / ˈʃtʊt.ɡaːt / (BrE)
a city in southwestern Germany known for its innovative architecture, automotive industry, and cultural heritage
city in southwestern Germany

Examples

Many tourists visit Stuttgart to experience its renowned museums and car factories.
The headquarters of Mercedes-Benz and Porsche are located in Stuttgart.
Stuttgart offers a vibrant blend of modern urban life and historic charm.

Etymology

From German Stuttgart, originally 'Stuotgarten' meaning 'stud farm.'
