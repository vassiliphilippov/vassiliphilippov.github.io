Africa

1. PROPN

/ ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (BrE)
the second largest continent, situated south of Europe, bordered by the Atlantic Ocean to the west and the Indian Ocean to the east
second largest continent south of Europe

Examples

Many unique animal species such as giraffes and lions are native to Africa.
The Sahara Desert, the world’s largest hot desert, is located in northern Africa.
She spent two years working on a conservation project in Africa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Asia - the largest continent, east of Europe and Africa
  • Synonym: Europe - a continent north of Africa, west of Asia
  • Synonym: North America - the third largest continent, west of Europe and Africa

Etymology

From Latin 'Africa', the Roman name for the region, possibly of Phoenician origin.
