forecasting

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrˌkæstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːkɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or process of making statements or predictions about future events based on analysis or evidence.
act of predicting future events

Examples

Accurate forecasting of the weather can help people plan outdoor activities.
The company's future depends on careful forecasting of market trends.
Experts are using advanced models for forecasting the economy's growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prediction - an act of saying what will happen in the future

Etymology

From 'forecast' + -ing; 'forecast' from Old English 'forecēast' (premonition).
