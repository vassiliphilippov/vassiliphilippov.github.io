apple-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈæpəl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈæpəl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a rounded shape like that of an apple, often used to describe a body with a wider waist and narrower hips or objects resembling an apple in form
having a shape resembling an apple

Examples

Many people describe her body as apple-shaped because most of her weight is around her waist.
That vase is particularly apple-shaped, making it look charming on the table.
An apple-shaped rock stood out among the ordinary stones on the beach.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pear-shaped - having a shape wider at the bottom, like a pear
