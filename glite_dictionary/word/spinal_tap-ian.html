Spinal Tap-ian

1. ADJ

/ ˈspaɪnəl ˈtæpiən / (AmE), / ˈspaɪnəl ˈtapɪən / (BrE)
Characteristic of the satirical, exaggerated, or absurd style of humor found in the film 'This Is Spinal Tap.'
in the style of 'This Is Spinal Tap' humor

Examples

The band's on-stage mishaps were almost perfectly Spinal Tap-ian in their absurdity.
Her Spinal Tap-ian jokes about rock bands always got a big laugh at parties.
The documentary had a Spinal Tap-ian flair, poking fun at the clichés of the music industry.

Synset Examples

He's known for his Spinal Tap-ian sense of humor.
Their antics were truly Spinal Tap-ian in nature.
She delivered a Spinal Tap-ian performance at the show.

Etymology

From the 1984 mockumentary film 'This Is Spinal Tap' + suffix '-ian' indicating relation or style.
