heartseed

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrtˌsid / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtˌsiːd / (BrE)
a tropical American and African vine with small balloon-like seed pods, commonly grown as an ornamental plant
tropical vine with balloon-like seed pods

Examples

The heartseed is popular in gardens for its distinctive seed pods that resemble small balloons.
Botanists studied the rapid growth of the heartseed in humid climates.
The seeds of the heartseed plant are black with a white marking, giving them a heart-shaped appearance.
