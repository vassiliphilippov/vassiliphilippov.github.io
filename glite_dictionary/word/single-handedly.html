single-handedly

1. ADV

/ ˌsɪŋɡəlˈhændɪdli / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋɡlˈhændɪdli / (BrE)
Without help from anyone else; by oneself.
without help from others

Examples

She managed to organize the entire event single-handedly despite the challenges.
He single-handedly fought off the intruders until the police arrived.
The artist single-handedly painted all the murals in the building.

Synset Examples

I built this house single-handedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alone - without anyone else present or involved
  • Antonym: jointly - with the involvement of two or more people, together

Etymology

Formed from 'single-handed' + '-ly'.
