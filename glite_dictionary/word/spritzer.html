spritzer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsprɪtsər / (AmE), / ˈsprɪtsə / (BrE)
a drink consisting of wine mixed with sparkling water, usually served chilled
wine mixed with sparkling water

Examples

She ordered a cold spritzer to drink with her salad.
A refreshing spritzer is perfect for summer afternoons on the patio.
For a lighter option, try a white wine spritzer instead of a cocktail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wine cooler - an alcoholic drink made by mixing wine with fruit juice, soda, or carbonated water

Etymology

From German Spritzer (a spray or splash), from spritzen (to spray or squirt).
