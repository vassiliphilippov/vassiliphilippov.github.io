Rudolf Nureyev

1. PROPN

/ ruˈdɑlf nuˈreɪˌɛf / (AmE), / ruːˈdɒlf njʊˈreɪɪf / (BrE)
(1938–1993) Russian-born ballet dancer, widely considered one of the greatest dancers of his time, who defected to the West in 1961 and was known for his partnership with Dame Margot Fonteyn.
famous Russian ballet dancer (1938–1993)

Examples

Rudolf Nureyev was celebrated for his dynamic stage presence and technical mastery in ballet.
Many consider Rudolf Nureyev's partnership with Margot Fonteyn as one of the most iconic in dance history.
After defecting from the Soviet Union, Rudolf Nureyev became a star with major ballet companies in the West.
