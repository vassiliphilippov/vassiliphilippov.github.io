Visayas

1. PROPN

/ vɪˈsaɪəz / (AmE), / vɪˈsaɪ.əz / (BrE)
a group of islands in the central Philippines, forming one of the three main geographical divisions of the country.
group of central Philippine islands

Examples

Many tourists visit the Visayas for its beautiful beaches and vibrant festivals.
Cebu and Bohol are two popular islands located within the Visayas.
After exploring Luzon, they traveled south to experience the culture of the Visayas.
