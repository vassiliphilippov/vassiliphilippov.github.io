sociological

1. ADJ

/ ˌsoʊsiəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or concerning sociology or the principles and methods of sociology
relating to sociology

Examples

He wrote a sociological analysis of urban poverty.
The class covered several important sociological theories.
Researchers are conducting a sociological survey to better understand community attitudes.

Synset Examples

sociological studies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: social - relating to society or its organization

Etymology

From sociology + -ical, following the Latin/Greek tradition of forming adjectives from scientific disciplines.
