Giuseppe Melchiorre Sarto

1. PROPN

/ dʒuˈzɛp.eɪ mɛlˈkjoʊr.eɪ ˈsɑːr.toʊ / (AmE), / dʒuˈzɛp.i mɛlˈkjoː.re ˈsɑː.təʊ / (BrE)
(1835–1914) The birth name of Pope Pius X, the Roman Catholic pope who condemned religious modernism and was canonized in 1954 for his concern for the poor.
birth name of Pope Pius X (1835–1914)

Examples

Giuseppe Melchiorre Sarto was known for his dedication to social reforms during his papacy.
Many Catholics commemorate Giuseppe Melchiorre Sarto as Pope Pius X, celebrating his canonization in 1954.
Born in 1835, Giuseppe Melchiorre Sarto became one of the most influential popes of the early twentieth century.
