actualisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌæktʃuələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌaktjʊəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
The process of making something real or giving it the appearance of reality.
the act of making real

Examples

The actualisation of his dreams finally came after years of hard work.
Her ideas never moved beyond the planning stage to actualisation.
Many artists see their work as a form of actualisation of inner visions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: realization - the act of making something real

Etymology

From Latin 'actualis' (active), via French 'actualisation' and English 'actualize'.
