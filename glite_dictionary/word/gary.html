Gary

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡɛri / (AmE), / ˈɡæri / (BrE)
A city in northwest Indiana, United States, located on the southern shore of Lake Michigan, known for its steel industry.
city in northwest Indiana, United States

Examples

Many jazz musicians were born and raised in Gary, Indiana.
The decline of the steel industry greatly affected the economy of Gary.
The city of Gary sits on the southern shore of Lake Michigan.

Etymology

Named after Elbert H. Gary, a founding chairman of the United States Steel Corporation.
