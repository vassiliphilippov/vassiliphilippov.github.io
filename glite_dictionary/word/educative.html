educative

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛdʒ.əˌkeɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈɛd.jʊ.kə.tɪv / (BrE)
providing knowledge or learning; having the effect of educating someone
providing education or learning

Examples

Traveling to another country can be a truly educative experience.
The museum visit was highly educative for the students.
His year abroad proved to be an incredibly educative journey of personal growth.

Synset Examples

an educative experience

Related Terms

  • Synonym: educational - providing education, related to the process of education

Etymology

From Latin educare (to rear, educate), via Late Latin educativus.
