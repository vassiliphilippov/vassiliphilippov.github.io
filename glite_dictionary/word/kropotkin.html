Kropotkin

1. PROPN

/ krəˈpɑt.kɪn / (AmE), / krəˈpɒt.kɪn / (BrE)
Peter Kropotkin (1842–1921), a Russian anarchist, philosopher, and scientist known for his contributions to anarchist theory and studies in geography and biology.
Russian anarchist and philosopher (1842–1921)

Examples

Many of Kropotkin's ideas about mutual aid influenced later social thinkers.
The works of Kropotkin remain relevant to discussions of anarchy and cooperation.
I read a biography of Kropotkin for my political theory class.

Etymology

Russian surname Кропоткин (Kropotkin), originally a Russian noble family name.
