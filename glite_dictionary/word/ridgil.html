ridgil

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪdʒ.əl / (AmE), / ˈrɪdʒ.ɪl / (BrE)
A male horse (colt) with one or both testicles undescended; a cryptorchid colt.
colt with undescended testicles
medical

Examples

After the examination, the vet confirmed that the young horse was a ridgil.
Breeders need to be aware of the complications that can arise when dealing with a ridgil.
Because he was a ridgil, the colt required special surgery to correct the issue.
