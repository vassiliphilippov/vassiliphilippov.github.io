chirography

1. NOUN

/ kaɪˈrɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / kaɪˈrɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the art or style of handwriting, especially beautiful or skilled handwriting
the art of beautiful handwriting

Examples

Her chirography was so exquisite that people often mistook her notes for works of art.
Students in the past devoted much time to perfecting their chirography.
The invitation was addressed in elegant chirography that impressed all the guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calligraphy - the art of beautiful handwriting, often decorative

Etymology

From Greek 'kheir-' (hand) + '-graphia' (writing).
