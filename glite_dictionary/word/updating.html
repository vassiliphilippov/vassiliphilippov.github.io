updating

1. NOUN

/ ʌpˈdeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌpˈdeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making something more current or accurate by adding new information or making changes.
the act of making something current

Examples

The updating of security protocols ensures the system remains protected against new threats.
Regular updating of the software is required to maintain optimal performance.
The city announced the updating of its public transportation schedules.

Synset Examples

criminal records need regular updating

Related Terms

  • Synonym: update - to make something more current or accurate
