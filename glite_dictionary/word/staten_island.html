Staten Island

1. PROPN

/ ˈsteɪtən ˈaɪlənd / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtən ˈaɪlənd / (BrE)
the southernmost and least populated of the five boroughs of New York City, located in New York State, USA
borough of New York City

Examples

She grew up on Staten Island before moving to Manhattan for college.
The ferry to Staten Island offers spectacular views of the Statue of Liberty.
After a long day, they returned home to Staten Island to relax.

Etymology

Named after the States General (Staten-Generaal) of the Netherlands, who governed the Dutch colony of New Netherland.
