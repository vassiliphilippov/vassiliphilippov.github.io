grayish-pink

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪɪʃ pɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪɪʃ pɪŋk / (BrE)
Of a pink color that is tinged with grey; having a muted or subdued pink hue.
pink with a greyish tint

Examples

She wore a grayish-pink sweater that complemented her eyes.
The sky turned grayish-pink as the sun began to set behind the clouds.
The designer recommended a grayish-pink paint for a calm yet modern look.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: pink - a pale red color
  • Degree Higher: gray - a color between black and white
