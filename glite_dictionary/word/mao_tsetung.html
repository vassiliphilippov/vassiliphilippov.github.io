Mao Tsetung

1. PROPN

/ maʊ tsɜˈtʊŋ / (AmE), / maʊ tsɜˈtʊŋ / (BrE)
(1893–1976) Chinese communist leader who was the founding father of the People's Republic of China and led the country from its establishment in 1949 until his death.
Chinese communist leader, 1893–1976

Examples

Mao Tsetung played a central role in shaping modern Chinese history.
The leadership style of Mao Tsetung has been the subject of much debate among historians.
Many Chinese cities have statues commemorating Mao Tsetung and his legacy.

Related Terms
