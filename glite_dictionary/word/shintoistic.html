Shintoistic

1. ADJ

/ ʃɪnˈtoʊˌɪstɪk / (AmE), / ʃɪnˈtəʊˌɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Shintoism, the indigenous religion of Japan.
relating to Shintoism

Examples

The Shintoistic rituals at the shrine are observed during important festivals.
Many gardens in Japan have a Shintoistic influence, with sacred stones and torii gates.
The ceremony followed traditional Shintoistic practices honoring the spirits of nature.

Synset Examples

Shinto temples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Shinto - the indigenous religion of Japan

Etymology

From Shinto + -istic, indicating characteristic of Shintoism.
