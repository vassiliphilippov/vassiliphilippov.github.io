anile

1. ADJ

/ ˈænaɪl, ˈeɪnaɪl / (AmE), / ˈaniːl, ˈeɪniːl / (BrE)
of or relating to, or characteristic of a feeble old woman; like an elderly woman, especially in a weak or senile way
like a feeble old woman
archaic

Examples

Her anile mannerisms belied her actual age and left many surprised by her vitality.
He described the crumbling building as having an anile grace, delicate but frail.
The character in the novel was written with anile wisdom, gentle yet marked by physical weakness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: senile - showing weakness or decline associated with old age

Etymology

From Latin anilis (of an old woman), from anus (old woman).
