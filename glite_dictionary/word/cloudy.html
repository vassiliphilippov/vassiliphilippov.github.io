cloudy

1. ADJ

/ ˈklaʊdi / (AmE), / ˈklaʊdi / (BrE)
(of the sky or weather) full of or covered with clouds; not clear or sunny.
covered with clouds

Examples

The forecast predicts a cloudy afternoon with a chance of rain.
We woke up to a cloudy sky and cooler temperatures.
Even though it was cloudy all day, we decided to go for a hike.

Synset Examples

cloudy skies

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clear - (of the sky) free from clouds

Etymology

From Old English 'cludig' (full of clouds).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00463344-a
Synset POS: a

2. ADJ

/ ˈklaʊdi / (AmE), / ˈklaʊdi / (BrE)
(of a liquid) not clear or transparent; having suspended particles, as if clouded by sediment.
not clear; containing suspended particles

Examples

After the storm, the river water became cloudy with silt and debris.
She poured a cloudy solution into the test tube for the experiment.
Homemade apple cider is often cloudy because it contains natural sediment.

Synset Examples

a cloudy liquid
muddy coffee
murky waters

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clear - (of liquids) transparent, free from cloudiness or particles
  • Synonym: murky - (of liquids) dark or dirty with suspended particles; not clear
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00435243-s
Synset POS: s

3. ADJ

/ ˈklaʊdi / (AmE), / ˈklaʊdi / (BrE)
not clear or having no definite form or limits; vague or ill-defined, especially in reference to ideas or situations
vague or ill-defined; not clear

Examples

The committee reached only a cloudy understanding of the new regulations.
His explanation of the issue remained cloudy and did not answer anyone's questions.
She wrote a cloudy essay that left the reader confused about her main point.

Synset Examples

gropes among cloudy issues toward a feeble conclusion
nebulous distinction between pride and conceit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nebulous - unclear, vague, or ill-defined
  • Antonym: clear - easy to perceive, understand, or interpret

Etymology

From Old English 'cludig' (full of clouds).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00785533-s
Synset POS: s
