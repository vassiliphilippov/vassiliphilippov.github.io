boat-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈboʊt ˌʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈbəʊt ˌʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a concave form or outline resembling the shape of a boat.
concave like a boat

Examples

The architect designed a boat-shaped auditorium to enhance the acoustics.
Some leaves are naturally boat-shaped to help channel water toward their roots.
The ancient bowl was boat-shaped, with gently curved sides and pointed ends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concave - curved inward like the interior of a sphere or bowl
