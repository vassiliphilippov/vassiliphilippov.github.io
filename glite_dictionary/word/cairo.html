Cairo

1. PROPN

/ ˈkaɪroʊ / (AmE), / ˈkaɪərəʊ / (BrE)
the capital city of Egypt, situated just south of the Nile delta and formerly home to the ancient Egyptian Pharaohs; Africa's largest city
capital city of Egypt

Examples

Many tourists visit Cairo to see the pyramids and the Egyptian Museum.
After moving to Cairo, she quickly adapted to the bustling city life.
The Nile flows through Cairo, dividing the city into several distinct districts.

Etymology

From Arabic 'al-Qāhira' meaning 'the victorious'.
