verticalness

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɚ.tɪ.kəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈvɜː.tɪ.kəl.nəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being vertical or upright; position at right angles to the horizon
state of being vertical

Examples

Engineers must account for the verticalness of the columns when designing safe structures.
The verticalness of the mountain cliffs made climbing extremely difficult.
She admired the verticalness of the tall, sleek skyscrapers against the skyline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: verticality - the quality or condition of being vertical
  • Antonym: horizontality - the state or quality of being horizontal

Etymology

From 'vertical' + '-ness', following standard English noun formation.
