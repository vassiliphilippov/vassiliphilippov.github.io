Fiji

1. PROPN

/ ˈfiːdʒi / (AmE), / ˈfiːdʒi / (BrE)
an island country in the South Pacific Ocean, known officially as the Republic of Fiji
island country in the South Pacific

Examples

Many tourists travel to Fiji to enjoy its beautiful beaches and warm climate.
The government of Fiji introduced new environmental policies in recent years.
Fiji gained its independence from Britain in 1970.

Etymology

From Fijian 'Viti', the indigenous name for the islands; adapted via Tongan 'Fisi'.
