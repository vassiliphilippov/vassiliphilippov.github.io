lamphouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæmpˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈlampˌhaʊs / (BrE)
the compartment or housing that holds a lamp, especially in equipment such as a movie projector or microscope
housing for a lamp in equipment

Examples

The technician opened the lamphouse to replace the burnt-out bulb in the projector.
Dust accumulation inside the lamphouse can reduce the brightness of the projected image.
Newer models feature a removable lamphouse for easier maintenance.
