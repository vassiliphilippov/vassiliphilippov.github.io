Italianness

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈtæliənnəs / (AmE), / ɪˈtæliːənnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of having characteristics, culture, or identity associated with Italy or Italians.
the quality of being Italian

Examples

The Italianness of the villa was evident in its architecture and vibrant atmosphere.
He takes great pride in his Italianness, especially during cultural festivals.
Her cooking reflects a genuine Italianness that reminds me of family meals in Rome.

Etymology

From 'Italian' + '-ness', forming a noun meaning Italian quality or character.
