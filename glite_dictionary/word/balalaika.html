balalaika

1. NOUN

/ ˌbæl.əˈlaɪ.kə / (AmE), / ˌbæ.ləˈlaɪ.kə / (BrE)
A Russian stringed musical instrument with a triangular body and typically three strings, played by plucking or strumming.
Russian triangular stringed instrument

Examples

She learned to play the balalaika during her stay in Moscow.
The ensemble featured a balalaika, adding a distinctive sound to their performance.
A beautifully painted balalaika stood on display in the music store window.

Etymology

From Russian балалайка (balalájka).
