jointer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɔɪntər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪntə / (BrE)
a long carpenter's plane used to shape and straighten the edges of boards so that they fit together accurately
carpenter's plane for board edges

Examples

The carpenter used a jointer to smooth the edges of the wooden boards before gluing them together.
Before assembling the tabletop, he ran each piece through the jointer for a perfect fit.
Proper use of a jointer ensures that panels are flush and strong when joined.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plane - a carpenter's tool with an adjustable blade for smoothing or shaping wood
