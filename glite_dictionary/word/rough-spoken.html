rough-spoken

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌf ˌspoʊkən / (AmE), / ˈrʌf ˌspəʊkən / (BrE)
speaking in a manner that is rude or uncouth
speaking rudely or uncouthly

Examples

Although he was rough-spoken, his intentions were always kind.
The rough-spoken dockworker intimidated the newcomers with his harsh words.
She found her uncle's rough-spoken manner difficult to accept at first.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blunt - speaking in a direct and sometimes rude way
  • Antonym: polite - showing good manners in speech or behavior
