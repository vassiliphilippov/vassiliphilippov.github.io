unsatisfactoriness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˌsætɪsˈfæktərinəs / (AmE), / ʌnˌsætɪsˈfaktərɪnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being unsatisfactory, inadequate, or unsuitable.
the quality of being unsatisfactory

Examples

The unsatisfactoriness of the report was evident to everyone on the committee.
She felt a persistent sense of unsatisfactoriness in her current job role.
Many critics have commented on the unsatisfactoriness of the proposed solution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unsatisfactory - not good enough or not acceptable
  • Antonym: satisfactoriness - the quality of being satisfactory or adequate

Etymology

Formed from 'unsatisfactory' + '-ness', modeled on standard English word formation.
