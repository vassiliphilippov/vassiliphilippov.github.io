bouldery

1. ADJ

/ ˈboʊldəri / (AmE), / ˈbəʊldəri / (BrE)
Covered with, resembling, or full of boulders.
full of boulders

Examples

The bouldery shoreline made it difficult for boats to approach the beach.
Hiking across the bouldery terrain proved challenging for even the most experienced climbers.
They set up camp on a bouldery plateau overlooking the valley.

Synset Examples

rocky fields
stony ground
bouldery beaches

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rocky - covered with or full of rocks
  • Synonym: stony - full of or covered with stones

Etymology

From 'boulder' + '-y' (adjective suffix).
