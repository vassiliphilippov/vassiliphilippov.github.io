to reread

1. VERB

/ ˌriˈrɛd / (AmE), / ˌriːˈriːd / (BrE)
to read something again, often for better understanding or enjoyment
read again

Examples

Each year, she likes to reread her favorite novel before summer begins.
I decided to reread the instructions to make sure I didn't miss anything.
He reread her message several times, looking for hidden meaning.

Synset Examples

He re-read her letters to him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: read - to look at and comprehend the meaning of written or printed matter

Etymology

From the prefix 're-' (again) + 'read'.
