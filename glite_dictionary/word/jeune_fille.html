jeune fille

1. NOUN

/ ʒœn fiːl / (AmE), / ʒɜːn fiːl / (BrE)
a girl or young woman who is unmarried, especially as referred to in French culture or literature
unmarried girl or young woman (French term)

Examples

The novel's protagonist was a shy jeune fille navigating Parisian society.
She wore a dress in the jeune fille style popularized by French designers.
In old French films, the innocent jeune fille often contrasted with the sophisticated femme fatale.

Etymology

From French, literally ‘young girl’.
