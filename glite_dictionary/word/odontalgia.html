odontalgia

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊ.dɑnˈtæl.dʒi.ə / (AmE), / ˌɒd.ænˈtæl.dʒi.ə / (BrE)
Pain or an ache located in or around a tooth; toothache.
pain in or around a tooth
medical

Examples

The dentist diagnosed the persistent odontalgia as a result of a deep cavity.
She scheduled an appointment after several days of severe odontalgia.
Chronic odontalgia can interfere with eating and sleeping.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toothache - pain or discomfort in a tooth or its surrounding area

Etymology

From Greek odous, odont- (tooth) + algos (pain).
