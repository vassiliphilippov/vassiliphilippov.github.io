makagigi

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɑːkəˈgiːgi / (AmE), / ˌmakəˈgiːgi / (BrE)
A traditional Polish Christmas sweet made from cookies with poppy seeds and honey.
Polish Christmas sweet with poppy seeds

Examples

During Christmas, my grandmother always bakes makagigi for the family.
Many Polish bakeries sell makagigi during the holiday season.
Guests enjoyed tasting the homemade makagigi at the festive dinner.

Etymology

From Polish 'makagigi', referring to poppy seed confections.
