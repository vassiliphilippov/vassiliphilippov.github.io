Theobid

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiː.əˌbɪd / (AmE), / ˈθiː.əʊˌbɪd / (BrE)
A brand name for theophylline, a colorless crystalline alkaloid derived from tea leaves or made synthetically, used as a bronchial dilator in medicine.
trade name for theophylline (bronchodilator)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Theobid to help manage her asthma symptoms.
Patients taking Theobid should be monitored for possible side effects.
He experienced relief from bronchospasms after starting Theobid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theophylline - a colorless crystalline alkaloid used to treat respiratory diseases
