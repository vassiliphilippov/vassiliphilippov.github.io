vocal

1. ADJ

/ ˈvoʊkəl / (AmE), / ˈvəʊkəl / (BrE)
having or using the ability to produce speech or sound, especially with the voice

Examples

Birds use their vocal organs to produce a variety of sounds.

5. NOUN

/ ˈvoʊ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈvəʊ.kəl / (BrE)
A piece of music intended to be performed by one or more singers, often with instrumental accompaniment.
a musical work for voice(s)

Examples

The band's new album features powerful vocals layered over electronic beats.
She specializes in jazz vocals and often performs with a live band.
The competition requires each participant to submit an original vocal track.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: song - a short musical composition with words, sung by the voice
  • Antonym: instrumental - a musical composition or part performed without singing

Etymology

From Latin vocalis (relating to voice), from vox (voice).
