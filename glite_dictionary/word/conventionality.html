conventionality

1. NOUN

/ kənˌvɛnʃəˈnælɪti / (AmE), / kənˌvɛnʃəˈnælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being conventional, especially strict adherence to accepted customs and practices
quality of being conventional or orthodox

Examples

The conventionality of her outfit made her blend seamlessly into the corporate environment.
He became frustrated with the conventionality of academic thinking and sought more original ideas.
Literary critics sometimes challenge the conventionality of popular fiction genres.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orthodoxy - authorized or accepted theory, doctrine, or practice
  • Antonym: unconventionality - quality of not being bound by established customs or practices

Etymology

From conventional + -ity, after Late Latin conventionalitas.

2. NOUN

/ kənˌvɛnʃəˈnælɪti / (AmE), / kənˌvɛnʃəˈnalɪti / (BrE)
the quality of conforming to established practices, customs, or standards of behavior and thought
conformity to established customs or standards

Examples

Her artwork challenged the conventionality of modern painting by using unusual materials.
Despite his rebellious appearance, he often surprised people with his underlying conventionality.
Many movies suffer from too much conventionality and not enough originality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conformity - compliance with standards, rules, or laws; similar concept
  • Antonym: unconventionality - the quality of not conforming to established customs or standards

Etymology

From 'conventional' + '-ity', after French 'conventionalité'.

3. NOUN

/ kənˌvɛnʃəˈnæləti / (AmE), / kənˌvɛnʃəˈnæləti / (BrE)
The quality of being conventional, especially as a lack of originality or creativity.
quality of being conventional or unoriginal

Examples

The film's conventionality disappointed critics who were hoping for something more daring.
She was frustrated by the conventionality of his ideas, longing for originality and innovation.
In literature, conventionality can make even a well-written story seem uninspired.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: originality - the ability to think independently and creatively
  • Synonym: convention - a way in which something is usually done

Etymology

From conventional + -ity, patterned after French 'conventionalité'.
