faith

1. NOUN

/ feɪθ / (AmE), / feɪθ / (BrE)
a religious institution, group, or system of beliefs, especially relating to belief in a divine power
religious institution or system

Examples

She has belonged to the Catholic faith all her life.
People of every faith were welcome at the event.
His parents are members of a different faith.

Synset Examples

he was raised in the Baptist religion
a member of his own faith contradicted him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: religion - a system of faith and worship
  • Synonym: denomination - a recognized autonomous branch of the Christian Church

Etymology

From Old French *feid*, from Latin *fides* (faith, trust, confidence).
