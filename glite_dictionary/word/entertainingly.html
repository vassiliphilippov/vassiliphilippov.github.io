entertainingly

1. ADV

/ ˌɛn.tɚˈteɪ.nɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˌɛn.təˈteɪ.nɪŋ.li / (BrE)
in a way that amuses or interests people; in an entertaining manner
in an amusing or interesting way

Examples

She explained the complicated topic very entertainingly, keeping the audience engaged throughout.
The guide spoke entertainingly about the history of the old castle, making everyone laugh.
His stories are always written entertainingly and never fail to capture my attention.

Synset Examples

Byron's consumed memoirs possessed the merit of being well and entertainingly written

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dully - in a dull manner; without interest or excitement
