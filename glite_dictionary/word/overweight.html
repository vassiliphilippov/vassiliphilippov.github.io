overweight

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊ.vɚˈweɪt / (AmE), / ˌəʊ.vəˈweɪt / (BrE)
The state of having more body fat than is considered healthy or normal.
state of having excessive body fat

Examples

Being overweight can increase the risk of certain health problems.
The doctor warned him about the dangers of overweight.
She struggled with feelings about her overweight throughout her teenage years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obesity - the condition of being very overweight

2. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈweɪt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈweɪt / (BrE)
Weighing more than is considered healthy or normal; having more body fat than is optimal, often relative to height or build.
weighing more than is healthy or normal

Examples

The doctor told him he was overweight and should consider a healthier diet.
Many overweight people struggle with self-esteem due to societal pressures.
She became overweight after changing her exercise routine and eating habits.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: obese - very overweight, with excessive body fat
  • Antonym: underweight - weighing less than is healthy or normal
  • Degree Lower: normal weight - having a weight considered healthy for one's height

Etymology

Formed from 'over-' (excess) and 'weight'.
