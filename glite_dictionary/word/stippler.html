stippler

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪplɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɪplə / (BrE)
an artist who creates images or textures by applying small dots or short strokes, typically with a brush or pen
an artist who uses stippling

Examples

The skilled stippler used tiny dots to create realistic shading in her portrait.
Many art students admire the patience and precision required to become a master stippler.
As a renowned stippler, he was often commissioned for his unique illustrations in scientific textbooks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stipple - to mark a surface with numerous small dots or specks

Etymology

From 'stipple', likely from Dutch 'stippelen' (to spot or speckle).
