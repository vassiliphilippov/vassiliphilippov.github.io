New Jerseyan

1. NOUN

/ nuː ˈdʒɜːrziən / (AmE), / njuː ˈdʒɜːziən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or resident of New Jersey, United States
native or resident of New Jersey

Examples

As a proud New Jerseyan, she often talks about the state's beautiful beaches.
Many New Jerseyans commute daily to work in New York City.
The New Jerseyan was happy to share stories about his hometown in Princeton.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: New Yorker - a person who is a native or resident of New York
  • Synonym: New Jerseyite - alternative demonym for a native or resident of New Jersey

Etymology

From New Jersey (U.S. state) + -an (suffix forming demonyms).
