to pluralize

1. VERB

/ ˈplʊr.əˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈplʊə.rə.laɪz / (BrE)
to make (a word, especially a noun) plural in form
make a word plural

Examples

In English, we usually pluralize nouns by adding '-s' or '-es'.
How do you pluralize the word 'child'?
Some people mistakenly pluralize words from Latin and Greek incorrectly.

Etymology
