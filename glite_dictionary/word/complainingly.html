complainingly

1. ADV

/ kəmˈpleɪnɪŋli / (AmE), / kəmˈpleɪnɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that expresses dissatisfaction or annoyance; with complaints.
in a complaining manner

Examples

He answered complainingly when asked to help with the chores.
She complainingly handed in her assignment, clearly unhappy with the topic.
The children walked complainingly to bed, wishing they could stay up later.

Synset Examples

she did her work, but she did so complainingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grumblingly - in a grumbling manner; complainingly

Etymology

From 'complaining' + '-ly', formed from the verb 'complain' (from Old French 'complaindre', from Latin 'complanere').
