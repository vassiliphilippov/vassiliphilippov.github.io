seven

1. NUM

/ ˈsɛvən / (AmE), / ˈsɛv.ən / (BrE)
being one more than six; the number 7.
the number 7

Examples

There are seven days in a week.
She bought seven apples at the market.
We need seven volunteers for the project.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: six - the number before seven
  • Degree Higher: eight - the number after seven

Etymology

From Old English *seofon*, from Proto-Germanic *sebun*, from Proto-Indo-European *septḿ̥*.
