Irena

1. NOUN

/ aɪˈriːnə / (AmE), / ɪˈriːnə / (BrE)
The genus of birds in the family Irenidae, known as fairy bluebirds, native to South and Southeast Asia.
genus of fairy bluebirds

Examples

The vibrant colors of the Irena species make them popular among birdwatchers.
Scientists recently observed nesting behavior in several Irena individuals.
The Irena genus is distinguished by its glossy blue plumage.
