nonobjective

1. ADJ

/ nɑn əbˈdʒɛktɪv / (AmE), / nɒn əbˈdʒɛktɪv / (BrE)
(of art) not representing or imitating external reality or natural objects; abstract.
abstract; not representing external reality

Examples

The museum features a collection of nonobjective artworks that challenge traditional notions of representation.
She is known for her nonobjective paintings full of bold colors and geometric shapes.
His interest in nonobjective art began after studying the works of Kandinsky and Mondrian.

Synset Examples

a large abstract painting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abstract - (of art) not attempting to represent external reality

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'non-' (not) + 'objective' (existing as an object or fact).
