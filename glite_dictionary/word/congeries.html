congeries

1. NOUN

/ kənˈdʒɪriːz / (AmE), / kɒnˈdʒɪəriːz / (BrE)
A collection of diverse items or elements gathered together; an aggregate.
a collection of diverse things

Examples

The museum displays a congeries of artifacts from ancient civilizations.
His argument was simply a congeries of unrelated facts and opinions.
On her desk was a congeries of papers, books, and household items.

Etymology

From Latin 'congeries' (heap, pile), from 'congerere' meaning 'to heap up'.
