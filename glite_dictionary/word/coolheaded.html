coolheaded

1. ADJ

/ ˈkulˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈkuːlˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
calm and composed, especially in difficult or stressful situations; not easily upset or excited
calm and self-controlled under pressure

Examples

Even under immense pressure, the CEO remained coolheaded and made rational decisions.
Her coolheaded response to the emergency helped keep everyone calm.
It takes a truly coolheaded person to negotiate in such heated circumstances.

Synset Examples

play it cool
keep cool
stayed coolheaded in the crisis
the most nerveless winner in the history of the tournament

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness, anger, or other strong emotions
  • Synonym: nerveless - not easily agitated; showing courage or composure
  • Antonym: excitable - easily excited or upset
