aglitter

1. ADJ

/ əˈɡlɪtər / (AmE), / əˈɡlɪtə / (BrE)
Shining with bright flashes or points of light; sparkling or glittering.
sparkling or shining with flashes of light

Examples

The ornaments on the tree were aglitter with tiny lights and tinsel.
Her dress was aglitter under the ballroom chandeliers, catching everyone's attention.
After the rain, the city streets were aglitter with reflections from neon signs.

Etymology

Formed from prefix a- (on, in) + glitter, likely patterning after words like "ablaze".
