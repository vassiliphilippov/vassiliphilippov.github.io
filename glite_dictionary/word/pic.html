pic

1. NOUN

/ pɪk / (AmE), / pɪk / (BrE)
(informal) A motion picture; a film or movie.
informal term for a movie or film
informal

Examples

Are you free to catch a pic at the theater tonight?
The studio's latest pic broke box office records over the weekend.
She wrote several reviews of independent pics for the online magazine.

Synset Examples

they went to a movie every Saturday night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: movie - a motion picture; a film
