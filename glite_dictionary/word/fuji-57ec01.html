fuji

1. NOUN

/ ˈfuː.dʒi / (AmE), / ˈfuː.dʒi / (BrE)
A shrubby Japanese cherry tree with pale pink blossoms, often grown for its ornamental value.
Japanese cherry tree with pale pink blossoms

Examples

The fuji in the garden bloomed beautifully each spring.
We admired the delicate pink flowers of the fuji during the festival.
Botanists often study the fuji for its unique floral characteristics.

Etymology

From Japanese 'fuji', referring to either the wisteria or this variety of cherry tree.
