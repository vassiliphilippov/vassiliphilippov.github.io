concernedly

1. ADV

/ kənˈsɝːnɪdli / (AmE), / kənˈsɜːnɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows worry, interest, or care.
in a concerned manner

Examples

He looked at her concernedly when she didn’t answer the question.
‘Are you okay?’ she asked concernedly after seeing his pale face.
The doctor spoke concernedly about the patient’s symptoms.

Synset Examples

‘Are you all right?’ he asked concernedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anxiously - in a worried or uneasy manner
  • Antonym: calmly - in a quiet, peaceful, or relaxed manner
