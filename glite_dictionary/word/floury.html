floury

1. ADJ

/ ˈflaʊri / (AmE), / ˈflaʊəri / (BrE)
having a fine, powdery texture or appearance like that of flour
powdery like flour

Examples

The floury coating on the bread gave it a rustic appearance.
These floury potatoes are perfect for making mashed potatoes.
She brushed off the floury residue left on the countertop.

Synset Examples

a floury clay

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powdery - having a texture like powder

Etymology

From flour + -y, indicating 'like' or 'full of'.
