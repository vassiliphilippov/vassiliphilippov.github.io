C and W

1. NOUN

/ si ənd ˈdʌbəl ju / (AmE), / si ən ˈdʌbəl ju / (BrE)
abbreviation for 'country and western', a style of popular music originating in the southern United States and typically featuring simple melodies and lyrics with stringed instruments.
abbreviation for 'country and western' music

Examples

My father has always been a big fan of C and W, especially the classics from the 1960s.
The festival featured several C and W bands performing throughout the weekend.
I grew up listening to C and W tunes on the local radio station.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: country and western - a genre of popular music from the southern U.S.
  • Synonym: country music - popular music from rural southern US, often called 'country'

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'country and western.'
