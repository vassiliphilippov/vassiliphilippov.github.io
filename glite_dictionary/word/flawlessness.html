flawlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɔləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈflɔːləsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being without any flaws or defects
the state of being perfect or without flaws

Examples

Her flawlessness on the tennis court amazed spectators and opponents alike.
The designer aimed for flawlessness in every detail of the dress.
Critics praised the actor's performance for its apparent flawlessness and emotional depth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfection - the condition, state, or quality of being free or as free as possible from all flaws or defects
  • Antonym: imperfection - the state of being faulty or incomplete

Etymology

From 'flawless' + '-ness', where 'flawless' comes from 'flaw' (a defect) + '-less' (without).
