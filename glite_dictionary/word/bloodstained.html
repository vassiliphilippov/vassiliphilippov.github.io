bloodstained

1. ADJ

/ ˈblʌdˌsteɪnd / (AmE), / ˈblʌdˌsteɪnd / (BrE)
covered or marked with blood
covered with blood

Examples

His bloodstained hands showed he had been in a struggle.
The detective carefully examined the bloodstained carpet for clues.
She recoiled in horror at the sight of the bloodstained letter on the table.

Synset Examples

a bloodstained shirt
a gory dagger

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bloody - covered in or smeared with blood

Etymology

From 'blood' + 'stained', meaning marked with blood.
