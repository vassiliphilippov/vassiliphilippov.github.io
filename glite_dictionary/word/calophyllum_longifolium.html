Calophyllum longifolium

1. NOUN

/ ˌkæləˈfɪliəm ˌlɒŋɡɪˈfoʊliəm / (AmE), / ˌkaləˈfɪlɪəm ˌlɒŋɡɪˈfəʊlɪəm / (BrE)
a species of tropical tree native to Panama, valued for its timber
species of timber tree from Panama

Examples

Calophyllum longifolium is commonly harvested for its durable, high-quality wood.
Many conservationists are concerned about the declining numbers of Calophyllum longifolium due to logging.
The lush forests of Panama are home to rare species like Calophyllum longifolium.

Etymology

From Latin; genus name 'Calophyllum' meaning 'beautiful leaf'.
