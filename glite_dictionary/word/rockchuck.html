rockchuck

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑkˌtʃʌk / (AmE), / ˈrɒkˌtʃʌk / (BrE)
A large, yellowish-brown marmot found in rocky areas of western North America.
large marmot of western North America

Examples

We spotted a rockchuck basking on the sunny hillside during our hike.
The rockchuck's whistle warned the colony of an approaching eagle.
Farmers sometimes consider the rockchuck a nuisance because it digs burrows in fields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marmot - a large ground squirrel of the genus Marmota
