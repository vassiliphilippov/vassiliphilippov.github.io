perfecter

1. NOUN

/ pərˈfɛktər / (AmE), / pəˈfɛktə / (BrE)
a person who makes something perfect or brings it to its highest state of excellence
person who perfects something

Examples

As the perfecter of the procedure, she received much credit from her peers.
He was known as the perfecter of the engine, refining its design to achieve maximum efficiency.
Modern historians view Edison more as a perfecter than the actual inventor of the light bulb.

Synset Examples

although not the inventor, he must be recognized as the perfecter of this technique

Related Terms

  • Synonym: improver - person who makes something better
  • Synonym: developer - person who creates or advances something

Etymology

From the verb 'perfect' + -er suffix, after Latin 'perfectus' (completed, made perfect).
