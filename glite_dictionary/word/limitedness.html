limitedness

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪmɪtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪtɪdnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of having limits or being restricted in extent, amount, or scope.
state of being limited or restricted

Examples

The limitedness of their knowledge became apparent during the intense debate.
Due to the limitedness of the available options, we had to make a difficult choice.
She was frustrated by the limitedness of opportunities in her small hometown.

Synset Examples

This fund is only for regular attendees of PCC and due to the limitedness of funds is only available for short term needs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: limitation - the act of limiting or the state of being limited
  • Antonym: infinity - the quality of having no limits or end

Etymology

From 'limited' + '-ness', a standard noun-forming suffix.
