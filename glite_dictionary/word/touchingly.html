touchingly

1. ADV

/ ˈtʌtʃ.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈtʌtʃ.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that evokes strong feelings of sympathy, sadness, or tenderness.
in a moving or emotional way

Examples

She touchingly thanked everyone who had supported her throughout the difficult journey.
The film touchingly captures the bond between a mother and her son.
He touchingly recalled memories of his childhood home during his speech.

Synset Examples

she spoke poignantly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poignantly - in a way that evokes a keen sense of sadness or regret
