twirp

1. NOUN

/ twɝp / (AmE), / twɜːp / (BrE)
an annoying, insignificant, or contemptible person
annoying or contemptible person

Examples

I can't believe you listened to that twirp all afternoon.
She called her younger brother a twirp for tattling on her.
Don't be such a twirp—just apologize and move on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twerp - an annoying or silly person

Etymology

Variant of "twerp", possibly imitative in origin or of uncertain origin, first recorded in early 20th century.
