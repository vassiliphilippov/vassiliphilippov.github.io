schooldays

1. NOUN

/ ˈskuːlˌdeɪz / (AmE), / ˈskuːl.deɪz / (BrE)
the period of a person's life when they attend school, especially as remembered later
period in one's life spent at school

Examples

He often reminisces about his schooldays and childhood friends.
During my schooldays, I used to play football every afternoon.
Many people say their happiest memories come from their schooldays.
