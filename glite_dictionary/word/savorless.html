savorless

1. ADJ

/ ˈseɪvɚləs / (AmE), / ˈseɪvələs / (BrE)
Lacking flavor or taste; bland or insipid.
lacking flavor or taste

Examples

The soup was disappointingly savorless and needed more seasoning.
He pushed aside the savorless crackers, longing for something more flavorful.
After weeks of savorless meals in the hospital, she craved her mother's cooking.

Synset Examples

a bland diet
insipid hospital food
flavorless supermarket tomatoes
vapid beer
vapid tea

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bland - lacking strong flavor, insipid
  • Synonym: insipid - lacking flavor or zest, dull
  • Synonym: flavorless - without any flavor or taste
  • Synonym: tasteless - having no taste or flavor

Etymology

From 'savor' (meaning taste or flavor) + '-less' (without).
