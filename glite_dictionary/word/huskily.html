huskily

1. ADV

/ ˈhʌskɪli / (AmE), / ˈhʌskɪli / (BrE)
in a hoarse or rough voice; speaking in a husky manner
in a hoarse or rough voice

Examples

She whispered huskily into his ear, barely above a whisper.
"I missed you," he said huskily, his voice trembling with emotion.
The singer spoke huskily after finishing the powerful song.

Synset Examples

‘Excuse me,’ he said hoarsely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hoarsely - in a rough or harsh-sounding voice

Etymology

Formed from "husky" (rough-voiced) plus the adverbial suffix "-ly".
