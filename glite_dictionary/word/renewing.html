renewing

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈnuː.ɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈnjuː.ɪŋ / (BrE)
imparting new energy, life, or vigor; revitalizing
imparting new energy or life

Examples

The renewing effect of a good night's sleep can make all the difference.
After the long winter, the renewing rains brought the garden back to life.
She found the mountain air renewing after months spent in the city.

Synset Examples

the renewing warmth of the sunshine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restorative - having the ability to restore health or strength
  • Synonym: invigorating - making one feel stronger or more energetic

Etymology

From 'renew' + '-ing', ultimately from Old French 'renoveller' and Latin 'renovare' (to make new again).
