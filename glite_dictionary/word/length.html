length

1. NOUN

/ lɛŋθ / (AmE), / lɛŋθ / (BrE)
The measurement or extent of something from end to end; the distance between two points.
distance from end to end

Examples

The length of the table is two meters.
She measured the length of the hallway before buying the carpet.
The length of the journey depends on the chosen route.

Synset Examples

the distance from New York to Chicago
he determined the length of the shortest line segment joining the two points

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distance - the amount of space between two points
  • Antonym: width - the measurement of the extent of something from side to side

Etymology

From Old English 'lengþu', from 'lang' (long).
