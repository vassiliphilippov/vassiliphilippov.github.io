vibrator

1. NOUN

/ vaɪˈbreɪtər / (AmE), / vaɪˈbreɪtə / (BrE)
a mechanical device that produces vibrations, often used for massage or stimulation
device producing vibrations, often for massage

Examples

She used a vibrator to relieve tension in her shoulders after a long day.
The therapist applied a handheld vibrator to the sore muscle.
Many physical therapists recommend a vibrator for at-home muscle relaxation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: massager - a device used to massage the body

Etymology

From Latin 'vibrare' (to shake, to move quickly to and fro).

2. NOUN

/ vaɪˈbreɪtər / (AmE), / vaɪˈbreɪtə / (BrE)
a mechanical device that produces vibration, often as part of a machine or instrument
device that produces vibration

Examples

The vibrator in the alarm caused the surface to shake gently.
In some musical instruments, a vibrator is essential for sound production.
Before use, make sure the vibrator is securely attached to the machine.

Synset Examples

a reed is the vibrator that produces the sound

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oscillator - a device for generating oscillating mechanical or electrical waves

Etymology

From Latin 'vibrare' (to shake, move rapidly), via 'vibration'.
