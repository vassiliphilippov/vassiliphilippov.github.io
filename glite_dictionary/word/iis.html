IIS

1. NOUN

/ aɪ aɪ ɛs / (AmE), / aɪ aɪ ɛs / (BrE)
the Iraqi Intelligence Service, the former main intelligence agency of Iraq, known for its role under Saddam Hussein's regime
Iraqi Intelligence Service (Saddam-era agency)

Examples

During the 1990s, the IIS operated extensively within and outside Iraq.
Members of the IIS were implicated in several covert operations abroad.
Foreign governments closely monitored activities of the IIS throughout Saddam Hussein's rule.

Synset Examples

the Iraqi Mukhabarat has been involved in numerous terrorist activities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mukhabarat - Arabic term for intelligence agency, especially Iraq's former secret police

Etymology

Abbreviation of Iraqi Intelligence Service.
