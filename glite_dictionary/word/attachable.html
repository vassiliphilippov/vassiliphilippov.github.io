attachable

1. ADJ

/ əˈtætʃ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / əˈtætʃ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
capable of being fastened or added to something else
able to be attached

Examples

The backpack has an attachable rain cover for unexpected showers.
He bought an attachable lamp for extra light while reading.
Many power tools come with attachable accessories for different tasks.

Synset Examples

a handle attachable by two bolts

Related Terms

  • Antonym: detachable - able to be removed after being attached
  • Antonym: removable - able to be taken off or separated

Etymology

From 'attach' + '-able', where 'attach' comes from Old French 'atachier'.
