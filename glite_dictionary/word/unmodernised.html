unmodernised

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmɑːdərˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɒdənaɪzd / (BrE)
not brought up to date; not made modern in style or function.
not brought up to date

Examples

Many of the buildings in the old town remain unmodernised, preserving their original charm.
The house was completely unmodernised when they bought it and needed extensive renovation.
Several unmodernised factories still operate using outdated equipment.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: modernised - brought up to date; made modern

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'modernised' (made modern).
