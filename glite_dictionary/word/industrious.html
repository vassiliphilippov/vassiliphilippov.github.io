industrious

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈdʌstriəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəs / (BrE)
diligently and consistently working hard; hardworking and persistent.
hardworking and persistent

Examples

She is one of the most industrious employees in the company, always completing her tasks ahead of schedule.
The industrious bees worked tirelessly throughout the summer to supply the hive with honey.
His industrious nature impressed his teachers, who praised his dedication and perseverance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diligent - showing care and effort in work or duties
  • Antonym: lazy - unwilling to work or use energy

Etymology

From Latin industrius (active, diligent), from industria (diligence).

2. ADJ

/ ɪnˈdʌstriəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəs / (BrE)
diligently and steadily hard-working, especially in pursuit of a particular goal or occupation.
hard-working and diligent

Examples

She is an industrious student who always submits her assignments on time.
The industrious employees were praised for their dedication during the busy season.
He has become more industrious since he started running his own business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diligent - showing care and conscientiousness in one's work or duties
  • Antonym: lazy - unwilling to work or use energy

Etymology

From Latin industriosus (diligent, active), from industria (diligence, industry).
