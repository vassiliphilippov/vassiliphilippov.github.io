unguaranteed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌɡærənˈtid / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌɡærənˈtiːd / (BrE)
Not protected by a guarantee, especially lacking financial security or backing from a guarantor.
not protected by a guarantee; lacking financial backing

Examples

Some investors avoid unguaranteed loans because of the higher risk involved.
The funds in this account are unguaranteed, so you could lose your principal investment.
He purchased an unguaranteed bond, aware there was no protection if the company defaulted.

Synset Examples

an unsecured note

Related Terms

  • Antonym: guaranteed - protected by a guarantee or financial backing
