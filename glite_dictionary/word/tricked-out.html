tricked-out

1. ADJ

/ trɪkt aʊt / (AmE), / trɪkt aʊt / (BrE)
decorated, customized, or enhanced in an elaborate or striking way, often to attract attention
elaborately decorated or customized
informal

Examples

He drove up in a tricked-out sports car with neon lights and custom wheels.
Their kitchen is completely tricked-out with smart appliances and modern finishes.
She loves her tricked-out denim jacket covered in patches and pins.

Synset Examples

tricked-out cupboards looking like Georgian cabinets

Related Terms

  • Synonym: customized - modified according to individual specifications
  • Antonym: plain - lacking decorative features; simple
