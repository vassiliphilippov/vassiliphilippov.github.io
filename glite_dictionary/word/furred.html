furred

1. ADJ

/ fɝd / (AmE), / fɜːd / (BrE)
Covered with a dense coat of fine, silky hair or fur.
covered with fur or fine hairs

Examples

Polar bears are well furred to withstand the cold Arctic climate.
The furred lining inside the jacket kept me warm all winter.
He sketched a small, furred creature curled up by the fire.
