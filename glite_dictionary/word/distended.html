distended

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈstɛndɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈstɛndɪd / (BrE)
(of a part of the body) abnormally swollen, enlarged, or expanded, especially by fluids or gas.
swollen or enlarged (by fluid/gas)

Examples

The patient's abdomen appeared distended after several hours of discomfort.
After drinking the contaminated water, her stomach became painfully distended.
Veterinarians checked for a distended bladder in the sick animal.

Synset Examples

hungry children with bloated stomachs
he had a grossly distended stomach
eyes with puffed (or puffy) lids
swollen hands
tumescent tissue
puffy tumid flesh

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swollen - abnormally enlarged, especially due to fluid accumulation
  • Synonym: bloated - swollen, often due to excessive fluid or gas

Etymology

From Latin 'distendere' meaning 'to stretch apart'.
