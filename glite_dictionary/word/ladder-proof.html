ladder-proof

1. ADJ

/ ˈlædɚ pruːf / (AmE), / ˈlædə pruːf / (BrE)
(of hosiery or fabric) designed to resist runs or ladders, making it less likely to develop long, damaged streaks when snagged.
resistant to runs in fabric or hosiery
british

Examples

These ladder-proof tights have become an essential part of my work wardrobe.
She always buys ladder-proof stockings for extra durability.
The manufacturer claims their new socks are completely ladder-proof even after multiple washes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: run-resistant - (of hosiery) resistant to runs
