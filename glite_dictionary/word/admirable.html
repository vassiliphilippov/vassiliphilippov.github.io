admirable

1. ADJ

/ ˈæd.mɚ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈæd.mɪ.rə.bəl / (BrE)
deserving of respect and approval for one's qualities or actions
deserving respect or approval

Examples

She did an admirable job caring for her elderly parents.
His admirable honesty earned him the trust of everyone at work.
It is admirable how she remains calm under pressure.

Synset Examples

all respectable companies give guarantees
ruined the family's good name

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commendable - deserving praise
  • Synonym: praiseworthy - deserving praise and approval
  • Antonym: dishonorable - not deserving respect or honor

Etymology

From Latin 'admirabilis' (wonderful, to be admired) from 'admirari' (to admire).
