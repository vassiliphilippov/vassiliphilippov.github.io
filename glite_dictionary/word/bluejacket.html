bluejacket

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluːˌdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌdʒækɪt / (BrE)
a sailor in the navy, especially an enlisted man; a naval serviceman
sailor in the navy

Examples

The bluejacket saluted the captain smartly as he came aboard.
Many young men enlisted as bluejackets during the war.
A bluejacket must be ready to follow orders in any situation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sailor - a person who works on a ship, especially one who is not an officer

Etymology

From the blue woolen jacket traditionally worn by enlisted sailors in the navy.
