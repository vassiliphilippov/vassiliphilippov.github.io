birthday

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːθ.deɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθ.deɪ / (BrE)
the anniversary of the day on which a person was born, or the celebration of this day
anniversary of one's birth

Examples

We are throwing a party for Emma's birthday this weekend.
He received many gifts on his eighteenth birthday.
On her birthday, she likes to spend time with family and friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anniversary - the date on which an event occurred in a previous year

Etymology

From Middle English 'birthe' (birth) + 'day', modeled after Old Norse 'burðardagr'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːθ.deɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθ.deɪ / (BrE)
The anniversary of the day on which a person was born; the date each year on which a person celebrates the event of their birth.
the date of a person's birth anniversary

Examples

She always bakes a cake for her daughter's birthday.
His birthday falls on July 14th every year.
We threw a surprise birthday party for our friend at the office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anniversary - the date on which an event took place in a previous year

Etymology

From Middle English 'birth' + 'day'.
