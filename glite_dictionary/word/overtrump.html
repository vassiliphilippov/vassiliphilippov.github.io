to overtrump

1. VERB

/ ˌoʊvərˈtrʌmp / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈtrʌmp / (BrE)
To play a trump card higher than one previously played in the same trick.
play a higher trump card

Examples

He managed to overtrump his opponent's queen with his king.
If you cannot follow suit, you may try to overtrump the current highest card.
She overtrumped the ace, winning the trick for her team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trump - To play a trump card in a trick

Etymology

From over- (above) + trump (from the card game term for a winning suit).
