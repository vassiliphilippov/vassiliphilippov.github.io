Karen Blixen

1. PROPN

/ kəˈrɛn ˈblɪksən / (AmE), / kaˈrɛn ˈblɪksən / (BrE)
(1885–1962) Danish author best known for her memoirs about living in Kenya and for her works such as 'Out of Africa.'
Danish author known for 'Out of Africa'

Examples

Karen Blixen's memoir 'Out of Africa' is considered a classic of travel literature.
Many readers are captivated by the way Karen Blixen describes the African landscape.
The life of Karen Blixen has inspired several films and biographies.
