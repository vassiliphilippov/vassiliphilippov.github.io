Bolanci

1. NOUN

/ boʊˈlɑːntʃi / (AmE), / bəʊˈlɑːntʃi / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in northern Nigeria, closely related to Hausa, with dialects including Bara and Fika
Chadic language of northern Nigeria

Examples

The Bolanci language is spoken in several communities across northern Nigeria.
Linguists have documented many dialectal differences within Bolanci.
She grew up hearing both Hausa and Bolanci at home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hausa - a widely spoken Chadic language in West Africa
