geodesical

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʒioʊˈdɛsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəˈdɛsɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or determined by geodesy, the scientific discipline of measuring and representing the earth's shape, orientation, and gravitational field
relating to geodesy (earth measurement)

Examples

The survey was conducted using the most accurate geodesical instruments available at the time.
They analyzed the geodesical data to determine the boundaries of the new national park.
Professor Ellis's specialty was geodesical computations concerning continental drift.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geodetic - relating to geodesy or geodetic measurements

Etymology

Formed from 'geodesy' (from Greek geōdaisia) plus the adjective suffix '-ical'.
