uninformative

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪnˈfɔrmətɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈfɔːmətɪv / (BrE)
not providing useful or enough information; lacking informative content.
not providing useful information

Examples

The article was uninformative and left me with many unanswered questions.
His response was brief and uninformative, giving little insight into the issue.
I found the manual completely uninformative for setting up the new device.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: informative - providing useful or interesting information

Etymology

From un- (not) + informative.
