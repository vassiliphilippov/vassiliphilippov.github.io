schooltime

1. NOUN

/ ˈskuːl.taɪm / (AmE), / ˈskuːl.taɪm / (BrE)
the time during which school is in session or instruction is taking place
period when school is in session

Examples

My mother always made sure I had lunch packed before schooltime began.
During schooltime, the library is filled with students studying for their exams.
The museum offers special programs for children outside of regular schooltime.

Synset Examples

stay after school
he didn't miss a single day of school
when the school day was done we would walk home together

Related Terms

  • Synonym: term time - the period during which the school term runs

2. NOUN

/ ˈskuːlˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈskuːl.taɪm / (BrE)
the period in a person's life when they are attending school
period of attending school

Examples

During my schooltime, I made many lifelong friends.
He often reminisces about his schooltime adventures and the teachers he admired.
The foundation for her career was built during schooltime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: childhood - the period of being a child
  • Antonym: adulthood - the period when a person is fully grown and mature
