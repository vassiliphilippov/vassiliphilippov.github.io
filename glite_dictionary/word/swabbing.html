swabbing

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɑbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈswɒbɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of cleaning a surface using a mop, swab, or similar implement
cleaning with a mop or swab

Examples

After the spill, a thorough swabbing of the floor was necessary.
The janitor began swabbing the hallway early in the morning.
Regular swabbing ensures the laboratory remains clean and sanitary.

Synset Examples

he gave it a good mopping

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mopping - the act of cleaning with a mop
