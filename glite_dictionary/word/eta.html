eta

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪtə / (AmE), / ˈiːtə / (BrE)
the seventh letter of the Greek alphabet (Η, η), often used as a symbol in mathematics and science
seventh Greek letter (Η, η)

Examples

The lowercase eta is commonly used to represent efficiency in physics equations.
In the Greek alphabet, eta comes after zeta and before theta.
Many scientific journals use the symbol eta for specific physical constants.

Etymology

From Greek 'ēta' (Η, η), the seventh letter of the Greek alphabet.
