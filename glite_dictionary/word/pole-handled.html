pole-handled

1. ADJ

/ poʊl ˈhændəld / (AmE), / pəʊl ˈhændld / (BrE)
having a long handle, typically a pole, attached to the main part of an object for ease of use or reach
having a long pole-like handle

Examples

The pole-handled shovel made it easier to dig deep holes in the garden.
She prefers using pole-handled brushes when painting ceilings and high walls.
A pole-handled net is essential for reaching debris floating in the middle of the pond.
