parapsychological

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɛrəˌsaɪkəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌparəˌsʌɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to phenomena or experiences that cannot be explained by known scientific laws and are studied in parapsychology, such as telepathy, clairvoyance, or psychokinesis.
relating to paranormal phenomena

Examples

Researchers are conducting parapsychological studies to investigate claims of telepathy and extrasensory perception.
The idea of ghosts falls within the realm of parapsychological investigation.
He attended a conference on parapsychological phenomena, such as telekinesis and remote viewing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paranormal - not scientifically explainable; supernatural

Etymology

From 'parapsychology' + '-ical'; 'parapsychology' from Greek 'para' (beside, beyond) and 'psychology'.
