postmeridian

1. ADJ

/ ˌpoʊst.məˈrɪdi.ən / (AmE), / ˌpəʊst.məˈrɪdi.ən / (BrE)
Occurring or being after midday; relating to the afternoon.
after midday; in the afternoon

Examples

The meeting is scheduled for the postmeridian hours.
Most of her energy fades in the postmeridian part of the day.
A postmeridian stroll was just what we needed after lunch.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ante meridian - before midday; relating to the morning

Etymology

From Latin post meridiem, meaning 'after midday'.
