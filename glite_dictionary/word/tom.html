Tom

1. NOUN

/ tɑm / (AmE), / tɒm / (BrE)
An offensive and derogatory term for a Black man considered to be excessively subservient or deferential to white people.
offensive slur for a subservient Black man
offensive

Examples

He was criticized by some members of the community for being a Tom.
The article explained how the term Tom has been used to demean Black men seen as too deferential to white people.
Calling someone a Tom is widely regarded as deeply offensive and inappropriate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Uncle Tom - A term for a Black person perceived as excessively subservient to white people

Etymology

Short for 'Uncle Tom', a character in Harriet Beecher Stowe's novel 'Uncle Tom's Cabin'.
