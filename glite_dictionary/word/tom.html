tom

1. NOUN

/ tɑm / (AmE), / tɒm / (BrE)
a male turkey
male turkey

Examples

The barnyard was lively with the strutting of a tom.
During mating season, a tom will display his colorful feathers to attract hens.
The farmer separated the tom from the rest of the flock for health reasons.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hen - female turkey

2. NOUN

/ tɑm / (AmE), / tɒm / (BrE)
A male cat.
male cat

Examples

Our tom is very protective of his territory.
They adopted a stray tom from the shelter last year.
You can tell the tom by his larger size and more muscular build.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tomcat - a male domestic cat
  • Antonym: queen - a female cat, especially a breeding cat

Etymology

Shortened from 'tomcat', named after 'Tom' in an 18th-century popular book featuring a male cat.
