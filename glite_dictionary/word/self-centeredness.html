self-centeredness

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlf ˈsɛn.tɚd.nəs / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf ˈsɛn.təd.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being concerned only with one's own interests and welfare; selfishness
concern for one’s own interests

Examples

His self-centeredness made it hard for him to maintain close friendships.
Many people outgrow their self-centeredness as they mature and learn empathy.
The team struggled because of the self-centeredness of its star player.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: selfishness - the quality of being only concerned with one's own advantage
  • Antonym: altruism - unselfish concern for the welfare of others

Etymology

Formed from 'self-centered' + '-ness' suffix.
