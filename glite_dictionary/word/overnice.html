overnice

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈnaɪs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈnaɪs / (BrE)
Excessively fastidious, sensitive, or easily disgusted, especially about matters considered trivial.
excessively fastidious or squeamish

Examples

He was overnice about using any cutlery that hadn't been freshly washed.
Don't be so overnice—a little dust won't hurt you.
She can be overnice when it comes to food preparation, refusing to eat anything that's not organic.

Synset Examples

too nice about his food to take to camp cooking
so squeamish he would only touch the toilet handle with his elbow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fastidious - very attentive to cleanliness, detail, or accuracy
  • Synonym: squeamish - easily made to feel sick, faint, or disgusted

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'over-' (excessively) and 'nice' (from Old French 'nice', foolish, simple, later fastidious).
