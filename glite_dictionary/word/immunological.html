immunological

1. ADJ

/ ɪˌmjunəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ɪˌmjuːnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the science or study of the immune system and its functions
pertaining to immunology
medical

Examples

The patient underwent several immunological tests to determine the cause of her allergies.
He studies immunological responses to vaccines in his research laboratory.
Changes in diet can affect immunological health and overall well-being.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immunologic - relating to the immune system or immunology

Etymology

From immunology (immune system science) + -ical (adjectival suffix).
