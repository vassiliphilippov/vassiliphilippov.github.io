punchable

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʌntʃ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈpʌntʃ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
(of a person or thing, especially a face) having qualities that annoy others and make them seem deserving of being punched.
deserving to be punched

Examples

His smug grin made his face look particularly punchable at that moment.
Some characters in movies just have a punchable quality to them.
She jokingly called her brother's expression punchable after he teased her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hit-able - capable of being hit

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'punch' + suffix '-able', meaning capable of being punched.
