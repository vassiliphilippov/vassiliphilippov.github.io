kitembilla

1. NOUN

/ kɪˈtɛm.bɪ.lə / (AmE), / kɪˈtɛm.bɪ.lə / (BrE)
A maroon-purple fruit found in India, similar to a gooseberry, with tart-sweet purple pulp, often used for making preserves.
Indian gooseberry-like fruit with tart-sweet purple pulp

Examples

The jam made from kitembilla has a distinctive tart-sweet flavor.
Farmers in southern India harvest kitembilla during the rainy season.
She added diced kitembilla to the fruit salad for extra color and tang.

2. NOUN

/ ˌkɪtɛmˈbɪlə / (AmE), / ˌkɪtɛmˈbɪlə / (BrE)
A small, spiny shrub native to Sri Lanka and India, cultivated for its maroon-purple fruit with sweet, purple pulp tasting like gooseberries.
tropical shrub with edible, gooseberry-flavored fruit

Examples

The kitembilla is prized in Sri Lanka for its uniquely flavored fruit.
Farmers in southern India often harvest kitembilla during the late summer season.
The sweet pulp of the kitembilla is sometimes used to make traditional jams and jellies.
