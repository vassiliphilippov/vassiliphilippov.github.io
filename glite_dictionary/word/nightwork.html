nightwork

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪtwɝːk / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtwɜːk / (BrE)
Work that is done during the night, especially as part of a regular job or duty.
work carried out at night

Examples

Working nightwork can disrupt normal sleeping patterns and affect health.
The factory operates around the clock, so many employees do nightwork.
She found it difficult to combine her family life with her nightwork schedule.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: night shift - a period of work during the night
  • Antonym: daywork - work done during the day
