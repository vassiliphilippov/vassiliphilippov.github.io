Caretta caretta

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɛtə kəˈrɛtə / (AmE), / kəˈrɛtə kəˈrɛtə / (BrE)
a large species of carnivorous sea turtle, known as the loggerhead, inhabiting warm and temperate oceans worldwide
species of large sea turtle (loggerhead)

Examples

The Caretta caretta is an endangered species due to habitat loss and accidental capture in fishing gear.
Researchers tracked the migration of several Caretta caretta across the Atlantic Ocean.
Tourists gathered on the beach to watch newly hatched Caretta caretta make their way to the sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loggerhead turtle - the common name for Caretta caretta

Etymology

From Latin, scientific name assigned by Linnaeus in 1758.
