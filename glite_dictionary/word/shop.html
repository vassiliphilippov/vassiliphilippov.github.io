shop

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑp / (AmE), / ʃɒp / (BrE)
a small building or room where things are manufactured or repaired, especially by hand
small workplace for making or repairing things

Examples

The carpenter worked late in his shop to finish the custom furniture order.
You could smell the scent of fresh paint drifting from the artist's shop.
She learned to mend jewelry in a small shop at the edge of the village.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workshop - a room or building where things are made or repaired, especially with tools or machinery

Etymology

From Old English sceoppa (booth, shop), related to the idea of a booth or enclosed place for work or commerce.
