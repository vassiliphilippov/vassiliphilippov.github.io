Eskalith

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛskəˌlɪθ / (AmE), / ˈɛskəˌlɪθ / (BrE)
A trademarked drug containing lithium carbonate, used chiefly to treat bipolar disorder and certain types of depression.
drug for bipolar disorder
medical

Examples

After her diagnosis, her doctor prescribed Eskalith to help stabilize her mood swings.
Some patients with bipolar disorder respond well to Eskalith as part of their treatment plan.
He experienced fewer manic episodes once he started taking Eskalith regularly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lithium carbonate - the chemical compound used in Eskalith
  • Synonym: Lithane - another trade name for the same medication
