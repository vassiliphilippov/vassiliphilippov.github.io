Peking

1. PROPN

/ ˈpeɪˌkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the former English name for Beijing, the capital city of China
former English name for Beijing

Examples

Many historical texts refer to Beijing as Peking.
The 2008 Olympics were officially held in Beijing, but older reports sometimes called it Peking.
Famous dishes such as Peking duck highlight the city's culinary heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Beijing - current official name for the capital city of China

Etymology

From Chinese 'Beijing' (北京), anglicized as 'Peking' through older French transcription systems.
