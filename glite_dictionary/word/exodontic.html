exodontic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛks.oʊˈdɑn.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛks.əʊˈdɒn.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving the extraction of teeth; pertaining to exodontics.
relating to tooth extraction
medical

Examples

The patient was referred to an exodontic specialist for wisdom tooth removal.
Many dental clinics have an exodontic department dedicated to surgical extractions.
Her research focuses on advances in exodontic techniques to minimize recovery time.

Synset Examples

exodontic surgeon

Related Terms

  • Antonym: endodontic - relating to the dental pulp or root tissues

Etymology

From exodontics, which comes from Greek 'ex-' (out of) and 'odous, odont-' (tooth).
