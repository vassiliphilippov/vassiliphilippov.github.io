live-bearing

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪvˌbɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪvˌbɛːrɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an animal) producing living young rather than laying eggs.
giving birth to living young (not eggs)

Examples

Some species of sharks are live-bearing, giving birth to fully formed pups.
The guppy is one of the most popular live-bearing aquarium fish.
Unlike turtles, which lay eggs, mammals are generally live-bearing.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: oviparous - (of an animal) laying eggs rather than giving birth to live young
