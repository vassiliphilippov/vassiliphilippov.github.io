Porzana porzana

1. NOUN

/ pɔɹˈzeɪnə pɔɹˈzeɪnə / (AmE), / pɔːˈzanə pɔːˈzanə / (BrE)
A small Eurasian rail found in swamps and marshes, commonly known as the spotted crake.
a small Eurasian marsh bird (spotted crake)

Examples

The Porzana porzana is rarely seen due to its secretive habits in dense wetland vegetation.
Birdwatchers were excited to spot a Porzana porzana during their survey of the marsh.
The distinctive call of the Porzana porzana can be heard at dusk in European reed beds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotted crake - common English name for Porzana porzana

Etymology

From the genus name 'Porzana', used in scientific classification, likely from Latin or Italian ornithological sources.
