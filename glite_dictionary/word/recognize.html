to recognize

1. VERB

/ ˈrɛkəɡˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈrɛkəɡnʌɪz / (BrE)
to show appreciation or express gratitude for (someone's actions, achievements, or efforts)
express appreciation or gratitude

Examples

We should recognize her contribution to the success of the project.
He was recognized for his dedication and commitment to the organization.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to acknowledge - to accept or admit the existence or truth of; to express thanks or appreciation for
