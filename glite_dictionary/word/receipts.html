receipts

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsits / (AmE), / rɪˈsiːts / (BrE)
the total amount of money received, especially as income before any deductions are made
total money received before deductions

Examples

Total receipts for the concert exceeded all expectations.
The company's receipts have increased steadily over the past five years.
Gross receipts must be reported before any expenses are deducted.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: income - money received, especially on a regular basis, for work or investments

Etymology

From Old French 'recete', from Latin 'recepta' (things received).
