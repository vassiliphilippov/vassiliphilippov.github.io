unreconcilable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnrɛˈkɑnsəˌlaɪəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɛkənˈsaɪləbəl / (BrE)
impossible to bring into harmony or agreement; unable to be reconciled.
impossible to reconcile

Examples

The couple found their disagreements to be unreconcilable and decided to separate.
Religious beliefs can sometimes create unreconcilable conflicts between groups.
The board declared the two proposals unreconcilable after hours of heated discussion.

Synset Examples

irreconcilable differences

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irreconcilable - impossible to reconcile or bring into agreement

Etymology

From un- (not) + reconcilable, formed on the pattern of 'reconcile.'
