Georgia O'Keeffe

1. PROPN

/ dʒɔr.dʒə oʊˈkiːf / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔː.dʒə əʊˈkiːf / (BrE)
(1887–1986) an American painter known for her large-format paintings of natural subjects, especially flowers, and for her contribution to modern art.
American painter (1887–1986), modernist artist

Examples

A new exhibition features rare paintings by Georgia O'Keeffe that have never been seen publicly before.
Many consider Georgia O'Keeffe to be the 'Mother of American modernism'.
The landscape of New Mexico inspired much of Georgia O'Keeffe's later work.
