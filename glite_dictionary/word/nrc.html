NRC

1. PROPN

/ ɛn ɑr si / (AmE), / ɛn ɑː siː / (BrE)
an independent U.S. federal agency established in 1974 to license and regulate nuclear power plants and their operations
U.S. agency overseeing nuclear power
american

Examples

The NRC issued new safety guidelines for nuclear facilities after the incident.
All proposed reactors must be approved by the NRC before construction begins.
Scientists presented their findings on radiation control to the NRC during the hearing.

Etymology

Abbreviation of Nuclear Regulatory Commission.
