Auden

1. PROPN

/ ˈɔdən / (AmE), / ˈɔːdn̩ / (BrE)
(1907–1973) English-born American poet noted for his technical skill and wide-ranging themes.
English-born American poet

Examples

Many critics consider Auden one of the greatest poets of the twentieth century.
During his lifetime, Auden published dozens of influential poems and essays.
The literary influence of Auden can be seen in many modern poets.
