faithfully

1. ADV

/ ˈfeɪθfəli / (AmE), / ˈfeɪθfəli / (BrE)
in a loyal, reliable, or accurate manner; with steadfast allegiance or devotion
in a loyal or reliable way

Examples

She promised to serve her employer faithfully and with integrity.
The machine has worked faithfully for over twenty years without breaking down.
He reproduces the original painting faithfully, paying attention to every detail.

Synset Examples

it always came on, faithfully, like the radio

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loyally - in a loyal manner
  • Synonym: accurately - in a correct or exact manner

Etymology

From 'faithful' + '-ly'. Ultimately from Latin 'fides' (faith).
