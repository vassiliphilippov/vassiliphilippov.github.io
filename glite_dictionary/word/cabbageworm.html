cabbageworm

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæbɪdʒˌwɜrm / (AmE), / ˈkabɪdʒˌwɜːm / (BrE)
the green larva of a cabbage butterfly, often found on and feeding on cabbage and related plants
larva of a cabbage butterfly

Examples

The gardener found several cabbageworms munching holes in the lettuce leaves.
If left untreated, cabbageworms can destroy entire cabbage crops.
Birds are natural predators of the cabbageworm in vegetable gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cabbage butterfly - a butterfly whose larvae are known as cabbageworms
