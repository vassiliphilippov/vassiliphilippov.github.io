metal-coloured

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛtl ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈmɛtl ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having a color or sheen resembling that of metal
having a metallic color or sheen

Examples

The dancer wore a metal-coloured dress that caught the light with every movement.
The sky turned a strange metal-coloured hue before the storm arrived.
She chose a metal-coloured paint for the kitchen cabinets to give them a modern look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metallic - having the appearance or qualities of metal
