couchette

1. NOUN

/ kuˈʃɛt / (AmE), / kuˈʃɛt / (BrE)
a compartment on a European passenger train containing four to six berths for sleeping
train sleeping compartment with berths

Examples

We booked a couchette for the overnight trip from Paris to Venice.
Sharing a couchette with strangers can be an interesting travel experience.
The train attendant checked our tickets before we settled into the couchette.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: berth - a fixed bed or bunk on a ship, train, or other vehicle

Etymology

From French 'couchette', diminutive of 'couche' meaning 'bed'.

2. NOUN

/ kuˈʃɛt / (AmE), / kuˈʃɛt / (BrE)
a sleeping compartment or berth in a train, typically with several bunks and used for overnight travel.
sleeping berth on a train

Examples

We reserved a couchette for our overnight journey from Paris to Rome.
Sharing a couchette can be a social experience for travelers on European trains.
The train attendant checked our tickets as we climbed into our couchette for the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: berth - a fixed bed or bunk, especially on a train or ship

Etymology

From French couchette (small bed), diminutive of couche (bed).
