superabundant

1. ADJ

/ suːpərəˈbʌndənt / (AmE), / ˌsuːpərəˈbʌndənt / (BrE)
present in extremely large quantities; more than abundant; excessively plentiful
excessively abundant; extremely plentiful

Examples

The forest was home to superabundant wildlife, making it a paradise for nature lovers.
She grew up in a region blessed with superabundant rainfall and lush vegetation.
Ideas seemed superabundant during their brainstorming session, with new suggestions every minute.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: abundant - plentiful; more than enough
  • Degree Lower: plentiful - existing in great quantity

Etymology

From Latin 'superabundare' (to overflow), via Late Latin 'superabundantem'.
