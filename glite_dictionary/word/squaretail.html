squaretail

1. NOUN

/ ˈskwɛrˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈskweəˌteɪl / (BrE)
A sluggish fish with a square-shaped tail, armored with tough bony scales, typically found in deep warm waters.
square-tailed armored fish of deep warm waters

Examples

The diver spotted a large squaretail near the coral reef.
Biologists are studying the unique scales of the squaretail to understand its evolution.
Fishermen rarely catch a squaretail because it prefers deep, warm water environments.
