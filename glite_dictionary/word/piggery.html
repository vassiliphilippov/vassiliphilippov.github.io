piggery

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪɡəri / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡəri / (BrE)
A farm or building where pigs are bred and raised.
place where pigs are raised

Examples

The old piggery behind the farmhouse was converted into a workshop.
The local council inspected the piggery for health and safety standards.
She invested in a modern piggery to improve pork production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pigsty - a small enclosure or building for keeping pigs

Etymology

From pig +‎ -ery, where -ery denotes a place of activity.
