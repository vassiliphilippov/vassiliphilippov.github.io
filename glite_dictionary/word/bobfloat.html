bobfloat

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑb.flɔt / (AmE), / ˈbɒb.flɒt / (BrE)
a small, usually cork float attached to a fishing line to indicate when a fish bites
fishing float attached to a line

Examples

He attached a bobfloat to his line before casting it into the river.
The bobfloat dipped suddenly, signaling a fish had taken the bait.
Many anglers choose a red-and-white bobfloat for visibility in the water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bobber - a float used in fishing to suspend the bait and indicate bites
