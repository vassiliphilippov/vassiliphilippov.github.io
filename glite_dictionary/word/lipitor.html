Lipitor

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪpɪtɔr / (AmE), / ˈlɪpɪtɔː / (BrE)
A brand name for atorvastatin, an oral drug used to lower cholesterol and triglyceride levels in the blood.
brand name for cholesterol-lowering drug

Examples

My doctor prescribed Lipitor to help control my high cholesterol.
Many patients have seen their LDL levels drop significantly after starting Lipitor.
Some people experience mild side effects when they first take Lipitor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atorvastatin - a statin medication used to lower cholesterol
