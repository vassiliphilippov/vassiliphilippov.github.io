textual

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛkstʃuəl / (AmE), / ˈtɛkstjʊəl / (BrE)
relating to or based on written or printed text.
relating to written text

Examples

The students conducted a textual analysis of the poem for their literature assignment.
Her research focused on the textual variations found in different editions of the novel.
Scholars debate the textual evidence used to support the interpretation.

Synset Examples

textual analysis

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: verbal - relating to or in the form of words, especially spoken

Etymology

From Medieval Latin textualis (of or relating to text), from Latin textus (text).
