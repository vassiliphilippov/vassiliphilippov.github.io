esophageal

1. ADJ

/ ɪˌsɑfəˈdʒiəl / (AmE), / ɪˌsɒfəˈdʒiːəl / (BrE)
Relating to the esophagus, the muscular tube connecting the throat to the stomach.
of the esophagus
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with esophageal cancer after experiencing difficulty swallowing.
The doctor performed an esophageal examination using a thin, flexible tube.
Chronic acid reflux can lead to esophageal damage if left untreated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: esophagus - muscular tube connecting throat to stomach

Etymology

From esophagus + -eal, ultimately from Greek oisophagos (esophagus).
