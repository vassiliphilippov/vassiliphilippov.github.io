bismuthic

1. ADJ

/ ˌbɪzˈmjuːθɪk / (AmE), / ˌbɪzˈmjuːθɪk / (BrE)
containing or relating to bismuth, especially in the pentavalent state
containing bismuth (pentavalent)

Examples

The scientist analyzed a bismuthic compound for its potential use in electronics.
Bismuthic oxides are studied for their unique catalytic properties.
She prepared a solution containing a bismuthic salt in the laboratory.

Etymology

From bismuth + -ic, indicating a relation to bismuth or its compounds.
