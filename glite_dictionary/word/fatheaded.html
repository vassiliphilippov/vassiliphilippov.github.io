fatheaded

1. ADJ

/ ˈfætˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈfætˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
(informal) Stupid or lacking intelligence.
stupid; lacking intelligence (informal)
informal

Examples

He made a fatheaded mistake by forgetting his own birthday.
It's fatheaded to ignore the advice of experts.
Her fatheaded comment made everyone in the room roll their eyes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stupid - lacking intelligence or common sense
  • Antonym: clever - quick to understand or learn; intelligent

Etymology

Formed from 'fat head' + '-ed' suffix, likely originally as an insult implying sluggishness of thought.
