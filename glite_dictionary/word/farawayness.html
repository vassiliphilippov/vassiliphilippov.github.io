farawayness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑr.əˌweɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfɑː.rəˌweɪ.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being distant or remote
state of being distant or remote

Examples

The farawayness of the mountain village only added to its mysterious charm.
She gazed into the distance, lost in the farawayness of her thoughts.
The artist captured a sense of farawayness in his landscape paintings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remoteness - the state of being far from something
  • Synonym: distance - the amount of space between two points
