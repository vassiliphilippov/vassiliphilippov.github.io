procreation

1. NOUN

/ ˌproʊkriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌprəʊkriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of conceiving and bearing offspring; reproduction
the act of reproducing or generating offspring

Examples

Many religions have strong beliefs regarding the purpose of procreation within marriage.
Advances in medicine have provided new ways to assist human procreation.
Some animals have elaborate rituals related to their procreation cycles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reproduction - the process of producing offspring

Etymology

From Latin procreatio (begetting, generation), from procreare (to beget, produce).
