unremedied

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈrɛmədid / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɛmɪdiːd / (BrE)
(of a problem, error, or situation) not corrected or put right.
not corrected; not put right

Examples

Several unremedied issues remained after the inspection and needed urgent attention.
His earlier mistakes went unremedied, leading to bigger problems down the line.
Decades of unremedied social injustices have shaped the current political climate.

Synset Examples

unremedied errors

Related Terms

  • Antonym: remedied - corrected or put right

Etymology

From un- (not) + remedied (from remedy, meaning to correct or cure).
