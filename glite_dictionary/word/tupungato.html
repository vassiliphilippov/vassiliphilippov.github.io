Tupungato

1. PROPN

/ tuːˈpʊŋɡɑːtoʊ / (AmE), / tuːˈpʊŋɡɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
a volcano in the Andes Mountains on the border between Argentina and Chile, reaching 22,310 feet (6,570 meters) in height
Andean volcano on Argentina–Chile border

Examples

The expedition set out to climb Tupungato despite warnings about the harsh weather conditions.
On a clear day, the snow-capped summit of Tupungato is visible from miles away.
Geologists are studying Tupungato to learn more about Andean volcanic activity.
