Honshu

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɑnˌʃu / (AmE), / ˈhɒnʃuː / (BrE)
the largest and most populous of the four main islands of Japan, situated between the Sea of Japan and the Pacific Ocean, considered the Japanese mainland
largest island of Japan

Examples

Mount Fuji, Japan’s highest peak, is located on Honshu.
Many major cities, including Tokyo and Kyoto, are found on Honshu.
The bullet train offers scenic views while traveling through Honshu's countryside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hokkaido - second largest main island of Japan, north of Honshu
  • Synonym: Shikoku - smallest of the four main Japanese islands, south of Honshu
  • Synonym: Kyushu - southernmost of the four main Japanese islands

Etymology

From Japanese 本州 (Honshū), meaning 'main province/territory'.
