schoolgirl

1. NOUN

/ ˈskuːlˌɡɝːl / (AmE), / ˈskuːlˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
a girl who attends school, especially one in primary or secondary education
a girl attending school

Examples

The schoolgirl wore a blue uniform and carried a backpack full of books.
During the parade, a schoolgirl presented flowers to the visiting dignitary.
She remembered her days as a schoolgirl with fondness and nostalgia.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: schoolboy - a boy attending school
  • Synonym: pupil - a person attending school, especially a child

Etymology

From 'school' + 'girl', first recorded in the 18th century.
