Pushkin

1. PROPN

/ ˈpʊʃ.kɪn / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃ.kɪn / (BrE)
(1799–1837) A Russian poet, playwright, and novelist widely considered the founder of modern Russian literature.
Russian poet and founder of modern Russian literature

Examples

Many scholars consider Pushkin to be the greatest Russian poet.
The works of Pushkin are studied by literature students around the world.
