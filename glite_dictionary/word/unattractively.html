unattractively

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.əˈtræk.tɪv.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈtræk.tɪv.li / (BrE)
In a way that lacks attractiveness or appeal.
in an unattractive manner

Examples

She always dressed unattractively during the winter months.
The house was unattractively painted a dull shade of brown.
He spoke unattractively, with little thought for charm or persuasion.

Synset Examples

she was unattractively dressed last night

Related Terms

  • Antonym: attractively - in an attractive manner
