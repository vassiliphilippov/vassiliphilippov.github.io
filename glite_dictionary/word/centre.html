Centre

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɑːn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɒ̃tʁ(ə), ˈsɛn.tə / (BrE)
a former administrative region in central France, known for its low-lying terrain and historical significance
region in central France

Examples

The Loire Valley in Centre is famous for its beautiful châteaux and vineyards.
Many tourists visit Centre to enjoy its picturesque countryside and historic towns.
Agriculture plays a significant role in the economy of the Centre region.

Etymology

French, literally ‘center’, named for its central location within France.
