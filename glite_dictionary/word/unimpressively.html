unimpressively

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.ɪmˈprɛs.ɪv.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪmˈprɛs.ɪv.li / (BrE)
In a way that fails to make a strong or favorable impression; in an unremarkable or ordinary manner.
in an unremarkable manner

Examples

The team played unimpressively throughout the season, failing to win any major games.
She delivered her speech unimpressively, with little enthusiasm or conviction.
The restaurant performed unimpressively in the recent review despite high expectations.

Synset Examples

she scored unimpressively low in the first round of the competition

Related Terms

  • Antonym: impressively - in a way that evokes admiration or respect
