CID

1. NOUN

/ si aɪ ˈdi / (AmE), / siː aɪ ˈdiː / (BrE)
the United States Army's principal law enforcement agency responsible for conducting criminal investigations within the Army worldwide
US Army criminal investigation agency
american legal

Examples

The CID launched an inquiry into the theft from the military base.
She was assigned to the CID after completing her training as a military police officer.
The CID works in coordination with other branches to uphold the Army's code of conduct.

Etymology

Abbreviation of Criminal Investigation Division.
