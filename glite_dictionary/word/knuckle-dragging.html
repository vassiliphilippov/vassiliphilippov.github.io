knuckle-dragging

1. ADJ

/ ˈnʌkəlˌdræɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnʌkl̩ˌdræɡɪŋ / (BrE)
showing a lack of intelligence, sophistication, or modern thinking; crude or primitive in behavior or attitude
crude, unsophisticated, or unintelligent
informal

Examples

His knuckle-dragging comments at the meeting offended many of his colleagues.
The film portrayed the villains as knuckle-dragging thugs.
Many critics dismissed the policy as a knuckle-dragging response to a complex issue.

Synset Examples

The knuckle-dragging approach to the problem won't work.
His knuckle-dragging attitude towards technology is frustrating.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crude - lacking sophistication or refinement
  • Antonym: sophisticated - having, revealing, or involving a great deal of worldly experience and knowledge; opposite of knuckle-dragging

Etymology

Figuratively compares people to early humans thought to walk with their knuckles on the ground, implying primitiveness.
