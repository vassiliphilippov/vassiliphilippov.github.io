divorcement

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈvɔrsmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈvɔːsmənt / (BrE)
the formal legal ending of a marriage; the act or process of legally dissolving a marriage
legal dissolution of a marriage
legal

Examples

The court granted a divorcement after months of negotiations between the parties.
In some countries, obtaining a divorcement involves complex legal proceedings.
The increase in divorcement rates has prompted lawmakers to review family law policies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: divorce - the legal dissolution of a marriage
  • Antonym: marriage - the legal union of two people as spouses

Etymology

From Old French *divorcement*, from Latin *divortium* (separation).
