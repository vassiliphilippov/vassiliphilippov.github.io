Ilex cornuta

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪlɛks kɔrˈnuːtə / (AmE), / ˈaɪlɛks kɔːˈnjuːtə / (BrE)
a dense, rounded evergreen shrub native to China, with spiny leaves, widely cultivated as an ornamental plant
evergreen shrub with spiny leaves, Chinese holly

Examples

The Ilex cornuta in our garden produces bright red berries every winter.
Many landscapers recommend Ilex cornuta for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
Botanists admire the distinctive spiny leaves of Ilex cornuta when identifying shrubs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chinese holly - common name for Ilex cornuta, an evergreen shrub with spiny leaves

Etymology

From Latin: *ilex* (holm oak or holly) + *cornuta* (horned, in reference to the shape of the leaves).
