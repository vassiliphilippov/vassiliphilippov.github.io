combinable

1. ADJ

/ kəmˈbaɪn.ə.bəl / (AmE), / kəmˈbʌɪn.ə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be combined or capable of combining with something else.
able to be combined

Examples

These ingredients are highly combinable in a variety of recipes.
The data sets are only combinable if they use the same units of measurement.
He prefers to use paints that are easily combinable for creating new colors.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: incompatible - not able to exist or work with something else
  • Synonym: combine - to join or mix two or more things together

Etymology

From combine + -able, patterned after similar Latin-derived adjectives.
