to telefax

1. VERB

/ ˈtɛlɪˌfæks / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌfæks / (BrE)
to send (a document, image, or message) by means of a facsimile (fax) machine
send by fax machine

Examples

I'll telefax you the signed contract as soon as I receive it.
Please telefax the application form to our main office by noon.
She telefaxed the meeting agenda to all the managers yesterday.

Synset Examples

Can you fax me the report right away?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fax - send something via facsimile (fax) machine

Etymology

Blend of 'tele' (distance) and 'facsimile'.
