unresolvable

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.rɪˈzɑː.lə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌn.rɪˈzɒl.və.bəl / (BrE)
(of a problem, dispute, etc.) impossible to solve or settle.
impossible to solve or settle

Examples

The conflict between the two countries seemed unresolvable after years of failed negotiations.
Some mathematical paradoxes remain unresolvable despite centuries of study.
They faced an unresolvable dilemma and could not reach a decision.

Synset Examples

an apparently insolvable problem
public finance … had long presented problems unsolvable or at least unsolved

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insoluble - impossible to solve or fix
  • Antonym: resolvable - able to be settled or solved

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'resolvable' (able to be resolved).
