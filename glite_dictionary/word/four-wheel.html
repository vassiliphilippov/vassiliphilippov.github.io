four-wheel

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔr ˌwil / (AmE), / ˈfɔː ˌwiːl / (BrE)
(of a vehicle) having, using, or relating to four wheels.
relating to vehicles with four wheels

Examples

Her new SUV is a four-wheel drive vehicle.
The company specializes in four-wheel transportation solutions for rough terrain.
Many families prefer four-wheel cars for their stability and comfort.

Synset Examples
