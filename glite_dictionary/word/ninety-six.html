/ ˌnaɪn.tiˈsɪks / (AmE), / ˌnaɪn.tiˈsɪks / (BrE)

number: ninety-six (96)

Examples

There were ninety-six pages in the original document.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: ninety-seven - being seven more than ninety

Etymology

Formed from 'ninety' + 'six'.
