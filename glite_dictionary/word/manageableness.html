manageableness

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænɪdʒəblnəs / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒəb(ə)lnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being easy to manage or control
quality of being manageable

Examples

The manageableness of the new software made it popular with employees of all ages.
She appreciated the manageableness of her new haircut, which required little effort each morning.
Engineers are testing the manageableness of the proposed traffic plan before citywide implementation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manageability - the quality of being easy to manage; alternative noun form

Etymology

From manageable + -ness, forming nouns denoting state or quality.
