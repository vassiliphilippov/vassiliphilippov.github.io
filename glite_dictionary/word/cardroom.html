cardroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrdˌrum / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdˌruːm / (BrE)
A room where people gamble at card games, usually in a casino or private club.
room for gambling at card games

Examples

The cardroom was crowded with players trying their luck at poker.
Smoking is not permitted in the cardroom of this club.
He spent most evenings in the cardroom, hoping to win big.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: casino - a public room or building for gambling and other entertainment
