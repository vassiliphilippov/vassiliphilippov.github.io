Oregon

1. PROPN

/ ˈɔr.ɪ.ɡən / (AmE), / ˈɒr.ɪ.ɡən / (BrE)
a state in the northwestern United States, located on the Pacific coast; its capital is Salem.
U.S. state in the Pacific Northwest

Examples

I visited Oregon last summer and explored Crater Lake National Park.
The forests of Oregon are home to many unique species of plants and animals.
She moved from California to Oregon to attend university in Portland.

Etymology

From French 'Oregan' or 'Ouragan,' possibly from an earlier spelling of 'Oregano'; further origin uncertain.
