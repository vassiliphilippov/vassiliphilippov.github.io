cacodaemonic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkækoʊdiˈɑːmɪk / (AmE), / ˌkækəʊdiˈɒmɪk / (BrE)
Relating to evil spirits or demons; possessed by or characteristic of evil supernatural forces.
relating to evil spirits or demons

Many ancient legends speak of cacodaemonic forces influencing the fate of humankind.
The novel's antagonist was described as having a cacodaemonic presence that chilled everyone.
Superstitious villagers blamed the mysterious illness on cacodaemonic activity in the old woods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: daemonic - relating to demons or supernatural spirits

Etymology

From Greek kakodaimōn (evil spirit), from kakos (bad) + daimōn (spirit).
