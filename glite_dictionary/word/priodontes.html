Priodontes

1. NOUN

/ praɪ.əˈdɒn.tiːz / (AmE), / prʌɪ.əˈdɒn.tiːz / (BrE)
A genus of large armadillo, consisting solely of the giant armadillo (Priodontes maximus), native to South America.
genus of the giant armadillo

Examples

The Priodontes genus includes only one living species, the giant armadillo.
Researchers are studying Priodontes to better understand the ecological role of giant armadillos in South America.
Conservation efforts for Priodontes are important due to its declining population in the wild.

Etymology

From Greek 'prion' (saw) and 'odous' (tooth), referring to the animal's teeth.
