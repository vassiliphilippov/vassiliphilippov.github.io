recovering

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈkʌvərɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈkʌvərɪŋ / (BrE)
in the process of returning to health after an illness or period of debility
in process of regaining health

Examples

The recovering patient was able to walk around the ward for the first time.
After surgery, Sarah spent several weeks recovering at home with her family.
Many hospitals have special units for recovering patients who need extra care.

Synset Examples

convalescent children are difficult to keep in bed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: convalescent - recovering from illness or operation
