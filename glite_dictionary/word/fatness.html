fatness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfætnəs / (AmE), / ˈfætnəs / (BrE)
The state of being fat; excess bodily weight.
excess bodily weight

Examples

She was always self-conscious about her fatness during high school.
Medical professionals warn about the health risks associated with extreme fatness.
The artist portrayed the fatness of the figure as a symbol of abundance.

Synset Examples

she disliked fatness in herself as well as in others

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obesity - the condition of being very overweight
  • Antonym: thinness - the state of being thin

Etymology

From 'fat' + '-ness'.
