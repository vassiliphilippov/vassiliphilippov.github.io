Geum macrophyllum

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡiːəm ˌmækroʊˈfɪləm / (AmE), / ˈɡiːəm ˌmakrəʊˈfɪləm / (BrE)
A hairy yellow-flowered plant species native to eastern Asia and North America, also known as largeleaf avens.
hairy yellow-flowered plant species

Examples

The forest floor was dotted with blooming Geum macrophyllum in early summer.
Researchers collected samples of Geum macrophyllum for their ecological study.
Gardeners admire Geum macrophyllum for its attractive yellow flowers and robust leaves.

Etymology

From Latin: 'Geum' is the genus name; 'macrophyllum' means 'large-leaved', from Greek 'makros' (large) and 'phyllon' (leaf).
