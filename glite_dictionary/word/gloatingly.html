gloatingly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡloʊ.tɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊ.tɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows excessive or smug pleasure, especially at someone else's misfortune.
in a smugly triumphant way

Examples

He spoke gloatingly about his victory over his longtime rival.
She smiled gloatingly when her opponent stumbled during the debate.
The team gloatingly celebrated after crushing their competitors in the final match.

Synset Examples

he spoke gloatingly about people he had cheated out of their money

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to gloat - to dwell on one's own success or another's misfortune with smugness
