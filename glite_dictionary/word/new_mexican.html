New Mexican

1. NOUN

/ nuː ˈmɛksɪkən / (AmE), / njuː ˈmɛksɪkən / (BrE)
a native or resident of the state of New Mexico in the United States
person from New Mexico

Examples

Her grandmother is a proud New Mexican who grew up in Santa Fe.
The festival celebrates the unique traditions of New Mexican culture.
Many New Mexicans take pride in their state's rich history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: New Yorker - a native or resident of New York (state or city)
