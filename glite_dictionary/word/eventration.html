eventration

1. NOUN

/ ˌiːvɛnˈtreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌiːvɛnˈtreɪʃən / (BrE)
the protrusion of the intestine or other abdominal contents through the abdominal wall
protrusion of internal organs through abdominal wall
medical

Examples

After the accident, the patient developed an eventration that required surgical repair.
The surgeon explained that eventration differs from other types of hernias due to the nature of the abdominal wall defect.
Early diagnosis of eventration can help prevent serious complications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hernia - condition in which an organ pushes through an opening in the muscle or tissue that holds it in place

Etymology

From French 'éventration', from Latin 'e-' (out) + 'venter' (belly).
