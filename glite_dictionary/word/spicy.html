spicy

1. ADJ

/ ˈspaɪsi / (AmE), / ˈspaɪsi / (BrE)
(of food) having a strong, hot flavor that produces a burning sensation in the mouth.
having a strong, hot flavor (food)

Examples

The curry was so spicy that my mouth started to burn after just one bite.
She loves to cook spicy dishes using fresh chili peppers and exotic spices.
If you find the soup too spicy, you can add some yogurt to cool it down.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot - (of food) having a spicy or burning flavor

2. ADJ

/ ˈspaɪsi / (AmE), / ˈspaɪsi / (BrE)
(of stories, jokes, remarks, etc.) suggestive of sexual impropriety or containing risqué content.
sexually suggestive; risqué

Examples

His book is full of spicy anecdotes that made the audience blush and laugh at the same time.
That magazine is known for its spicy gossip about celebrities' private lives.
The comedian told a few spicy jokes that some people found a bit too risqué.
