volar

1. ADJ

/ ˈvoʊlɑr / (AmE), / ˈvəʊlɑː / (BrE)
relating to the palm of the hand or the sole of the foot.
of the palm or sole (anatomy)
medical

Examples

The surgeon made an incision on the volar surface of the wrist.
He injured the volar aspect of his hand during the accident.
The doctor explained the difference between the dorsal and volar sides of the foot.

Synset Examples

the volar surface
the palmar muscle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: palmar - relating to the palm of the hand
  • Synonym: plantar - relating to the sole of the foot

Etymology

From Latin 'vola' (palm of the hand, sole of the foot).
