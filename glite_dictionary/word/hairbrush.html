hairbrush

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛrˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈheəˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)
a brush with a handle, used for arranging or grooming hair
a brush for grooming hair

Examples

She ran a hairbrush through her tangled hair before leaving for work.
After his shower, he grabbed his hairbrush to style his hair.
The little girl lost her favorite pink hairbrush at school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comb - a toothed device used for arranging hair
  • Degree Lower: toothbrush - a brush for cleaning teeth

Etymology

Formed from 'hair' + 'brush', both from Old English, meaning a brush for hair.
