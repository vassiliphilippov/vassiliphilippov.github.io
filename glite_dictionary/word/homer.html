Homer

1. PROPN

/ ˈhoʊmər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmə / (BrE)
An ancient Greek epic poet, traditionally considered the author of the Iliad and the Odyssey.
Greek epic poet, author of the Iliad and Odyssey

Examples

Many scholars study Homer's influence on Western literature.
The works of Homer have inspired countless poets and writers.
His knowledge of Greek mythology comes largely from reading Homer.

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Ὅμηρος (Hómēros).
