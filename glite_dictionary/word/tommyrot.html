tommyrot

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑːmiˌrɑːt / (AmE), / ˈtɒmiˌrɒt / (BrE)
foolish or absurd talk; nonsense.
foolish or nonsensical talk

Examples

Don’t listen to his tommyrot; he’s just making things up.
The idea that the Earth is flat is utter tommyrot.
She dismissed the rumors as pure tommyrot and carried on with her work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - words or ideas that have no meaning or make no sense

Etymology

Formed from Tommy (a nickname for the common British soldier) + rot (nonsense).
