Np

1. NOUN

/ ɛn pi / (AmE), / ɛn pi / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for neptunium, a radioactive metallic element produced artificially and occurring in trace amounts in uranium ores
symbol for neptunium (element)

Examples

Scientists detected a small amount of Np in the lab sample.
The radioactive properties of Np are studied in nuclear chemistry.
Uranium ore may contain trace quantities of Np.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neptunium - radioactive metallic element, symbol Np

Etymology

Symbol from neptunium, named after the planet Neptune.
