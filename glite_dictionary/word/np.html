NP

1. NOUN

/ ɛn pi / (AmE), / ɛn pi / (BrE)
abbreviation for nurse practitioner; a registered nurse with advanced training who can perform many duties of a physician.
abbreviation for nurse practitioner
medical american

Examples

The clinic has an NP available for patient consultations in the afternoons.
After seeing the NP, I received a prescription for my medication.
Many rural healthcare centers rely on an NP when no physician is present.
