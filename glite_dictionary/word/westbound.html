westbound

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛstˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈwɛst.baʊnd / (BrE)
moving or heading toward the west
moving toward the west

Examples

The westbound train will depart in ten minutes from platform three.
Traffic was heavy on the westbound lanes during the evening rush hour.
Many westbound pioneers braved harsh conditions in search of new opportunities.

Synset Examples

westbound pioneers

Related Terms

  • Antonym: eastbound - moving toward the east

Etymology

From 'west' + 'bound', indicating direction.
