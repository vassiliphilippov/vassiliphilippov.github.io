eyelash

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪˌlæʃ / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.læʃ / (BrE)
Any of the short, curved hairs that grow from the edge of the eyelids.
short curved hair on eyelid edge

Examples

She found a eyelash on her cheek and made a wish.
Mascara is applied to the eyelash to make it look longer and thicker.
He blinked quickly when a eyelash got into his eye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eyebrow - the strip of hair above the eye
  • Synonym: eyelid - the fold of skin that covers the eye

Etymology

From 'eye' + 'lash', first recorded in the 17th century.
