explanatory

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈsplænəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ɪkˈsplænətəri / (BrE)
serving or intended to explain or make something clear
serving to explain or clarify

Examples

The textbook contains several explanatory diagrams to help students understand the material.
He added an explanatory note at the end of the letter to avoid confusion.
An explanatory paragraph was included to make the instructions clearer.

Synset Examples

explanatory notes
an explanatory paragraph

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clarifying - making something easier to understand

Etymology

From Latin explicāre (to explain), via Medieval Latin explanatorius.
