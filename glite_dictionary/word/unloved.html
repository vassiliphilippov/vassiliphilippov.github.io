unloved

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlʌvd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlʌvd / (BrE)
not loved or cared for; lacking affection or attention.
not loved or cared for

Examples

After years in the old attic, the dusty teddy bear felt completely unloved.
She often helped unloved animals find new homes at the shelter.
The house stood unloved and empty at the end of the street.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loved - regarded with deep affection
  • Synonym: neglected - not receiving proper attention or care

Etymology

From un- (not) + loved.
