Marcus Ulpius Traianus

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑːrkəs ʌlˈpiːəs traɪˈɑːnəs / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkəs ʌlˈpɪəs traɪˈɑːnəs / (BrE)
(53–117) Roman Emperor, commonly known as Trajan, renowned for expanding the Roman Empire eastward and initiating significant building projects.
Roman Emperor Trajan (53–117)

Examples

Marcus Ulpius Traianus was admired for his military successes and extensive public works.
The column of Marcus Ulpius Traianus still stands as a testament to his achievements in Rome.
Historians regard Marcus Ulpius Traianus as one of the greatest Roman emperors.
