Verdi

1. PROPN

/ ˈvɛərdi / (AmE), / ˈvɛːdi / (BrE)
(1813–1901) An Italian composer renowned for his operas, including Rigoletto, La Traviata, and Aida.
Italian operatic composer (1813–1901)

Examples

Verdi is celebrated for composing some of the greatest masterpieces in the operatic repertoire.
The concert featured several famous arias by Verdi.
Many consider Verdi to be a central figure in the history of Western classical music.
