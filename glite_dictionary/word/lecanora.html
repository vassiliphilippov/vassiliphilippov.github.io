Lecanora

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɛkəˈnɔrə / (AmE), / ˌlɛkəˈnɔːrə / (BrE)
A genus of crustose lichens that is the type genus of the family Lecanoraceae.
genus of crustose lichens

Examples

The rocks were covered with a pale green lichen belonging to the genus Lecanora.
Researchers identified several new species in the Lecanora genus from the Arctic tundra.
The crustose lichen Lecanora grows abundantly on exposed tree bark in temperate regions.

Etymology

From Greek lekanē (dish, basin) + -ora, referring to the shape of the apothecia.
