installing

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈstɔlɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈstɔːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of putting equipment, machinery, or systems in position and making them ready for use
the act of setting something up

Examples

The installing of the new software was completed within an hour.
Proper installing of the appliance ensures safe and efficient use.
After the installing of security cameras, the thefts decreased significantly.

Synset Examples

the telephone installation took only a few minutes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: installation - the act or process of installing
