confidante

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːn.fɪˈdɑːnt / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.fɪˈdɑːnt / (BrE)
A woman to whom secrets are entrusted, especially a close and trusted friend.
a trusted female friend

Examples

She became his closest confidante during his years in office.
Maria trusts her sister as her confidante for all her personal matters.
As a confidante, she listened patiently and offered thoughtful advice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confidant - a person one confides in; a trusted friend or adviser

Etymology

From French 'confidante', feminine form of 'confidant'.
