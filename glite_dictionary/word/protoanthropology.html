protoanthropology

1. NOUN

/ ˌproʊtoʊˌænθrəˈpɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəʊˌænθrəˈpɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study of human beings and societies before the invention of writing
study of humans before writing

Examples

Graduate students in protoanthropology often participate in archaeological excavations to uncover prehistoric artifacts.
The museum's new exhibition focuses on the developments in protoanthropology during the Paleolithic era.
Advances in protoanthropology have deepened our understanding of early human societies and their cultural evolution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prehistory - the period of human history before written records

Etymology

From Greek 'proto-' (first, earliest) and 'anthropology' (study of humans).
