polygynist

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪdʒənɪst / (AmE), / pəˈlɪdʒɪnɪst / (BrE)
A man who has two or more wives at the same time.
man with multiple wives

Examples

In certain cultures, a polygynist may be highly respected in his community.
The law prohibits any polygynist from holding public office in this country.
Anthropologists studied the family structure of a local polygynist for several years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polygamist - a person with more than one spouse at the same time
  • Antonym: monogamist - a person married to only one spouse at a time

Etymology

From Greek polygynēs (having many wives) + -ist.
