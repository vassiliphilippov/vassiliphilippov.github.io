kohleria

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈlɪəriə / (AmE), / kəʊˈlɪəriə / (BrE)
A genus of shrubby tropical herbs cultivated for their soft velvety leaves and striking scarlet flowers.
a genus of ornamental tropical herbs

Examples

The kohleria in her greenhouse bloomed with bright red flowers every summer.
Botanists often admire the velvety texture of kohleria leaves.
I decided to add a kohleria plant to my collection of tropical species.

Etymology

Named after Michael Kohler, a 19th-century Swiss botanist.
