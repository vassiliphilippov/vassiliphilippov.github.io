self-established

1. ADJ

/ sɛlf ɪˈstæblɪʃt / (AmE), / sɛlf ɪˈstæblɪʃt / (BrE)
established or constituted by itself; not set up or imposed by an external authority.
established by itself

Examples

The company was a self-established entity, founded without any outside assistance.
The self-established rules of the club were respected by all its members.
Her self-established reputation as an expert was based solely on her own achievements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-created - created by oneself or by itself
  • Synonym: self-imposed - imposed on oneself rather than by external factors
