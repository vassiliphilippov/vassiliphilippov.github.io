unsalaried

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsæləriˌd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsalərid / (BrE)
not receiving or paying a salary; without financial compensation for work done.
not paid a salary

Examples

He took on an unsalaried position in order to gain experience in the field.
Several committee members work on an unsalaried basis out of personal commitment.
The internship is unsalaried, but offers valuable training opportunities.

Synset Examples

an uncompensated federal post

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unpaid - not receiving payment for work; without pay
  • Antonym: salaried - paid a regular salary for work done

Etymology

From the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'salaried' (receiving a salary).
