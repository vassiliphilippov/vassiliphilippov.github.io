pleasant-tasting

1. ADJ

/ ˈplɛzənt ˌteɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈplɛzənt ˌteɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
having a pleasing or agreeable taste; delicious
having a pleasing taste

Examples

The strawberries were especially pleasant-tasting this season, sweet and full of flavor.
She prepared a pleasant-tasting herbal tea that everyone enjoyed after dinner.
Children are more likely to eat vegetables when they are made pleasant-tasting with herbs and light seasoning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delicious - having a very pleasant taste or smell
  • Synonym: tasty - having a strong and pleasant flavor
  • Antonym: unpalatable - not pleasant to taste
