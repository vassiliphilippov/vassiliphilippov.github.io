nightdress

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪtˌdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtˌdrɛs / (BrE)
a loose dress worn by women or girls in bed, especially as nightwear
a loose dress worn for sleeping

Examples

She bought a new silk nightdress for her honeymoon.
When she woke up, she was still wearing her old cotton nightdress.
Her grandmother used to sew her own nightdresses by hand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nightgown - a loose garment worn in bed by women or girls
  • Antonym: pajamas - loose-fitting nightwear, usually a top and trousers

Etymology

Formed from 'night' + 'dress', first used in the 19th century.
