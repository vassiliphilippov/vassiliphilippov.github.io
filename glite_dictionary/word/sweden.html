Sweden

1. PROPN

/ ˈswidn̩ / (AmE), / ˈswiːdn̩ / (BrE)
a country in northern Europe, occupying the eastern part of the Scandinavian Peninsula; officially the Kingdom of Sweden
country in northern Europe

Examples

She spent a summer studying abroad in Sweden to immerse herself in Scandinavian culture.
The forests and lakes of Sweden attract outdoor enthusiasts from around the world.
Sweden has a reputation for its high standard of living and progressive social policies.

Etymology

From Old English 'Swēoland' or 'Swēorīċe', ultimately from the name of the Swedes (Old Norse 'svíar'), a Germanic people.
