nozzle

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑzəl / (AmE), / ˈnɒzl / (BrE)
(informal) The nose, especially when large or prominent.
slang term for the nose
informal

Examples

He got hit right on the nozzle during the game.
Wipe your nozzle before you catch a cold!
He has a pretty big nozzle, doesn't he?

2. NOUN

/ ˈnɑːzəl / (AmE), / ˈnɒzl̩ / (BrE)
A short tube or spout through which a liquid or gas is discharged, often controlling the direction or flow.
tube or spout for discharging fluid

Examples

The garden hose nozzle can be adjusted to produce a fine spray or a strong jet.
He cleaned the fuel nozzle to ensure proper engine performance.
She attached a new nozzle to the bottle to make watering the plants easier.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spout - an opening or tube for pouring liquid

Etymology

Perhaps from Old French *nosel* (diminutive of *nose*), formed after 'nose' due to shape and function.
