BLT

1. NOUN

/ ˌbiː ˌɛl ˈtiː / (AmE), / ˌbiː ˌɛl ˈtiː / (BrE)
A sandwich consisting of bacon, lettuce, and tomato, typically served on toasted bread and usually with mayonnaise.
bacon, lettuce, and tomato sandwich

Examples

I ordered a BLT for lunch at the café.
She made a delicious BLT with freshly baked bread and crispy bacon.
The menu offers a classic BLT along with several vegetarian options.

Etymology

Acronym from the initials of bacon, lettuce, and tomato.
