blankety-blank

1. ADJ

/ ˈblæŋkədi ˌblæŋk / (AmE), / ˈblaŋkəti ˌblaŋk / (BrE)
used as a euphemism to replace offensive words, especially when expressing anger or frustration
euphemism for a swear word or curse
informal

Examples

I can't get this blankety-blank computer to work properly.
Every time he hits his thumb with a hammer, he lets out a loud blankety-blank.
The blankety-blank train is late again, as usual.

Synset Examples

The blankety-blank weather ruined our picnic plans.
