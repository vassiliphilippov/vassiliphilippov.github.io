floored

1. ADJ

/ flɔrd / (AmE), / flɔːd / (BrE)
(of a room or space) having been provided with a floor.
having a floor

Examples

The newly renovated basement is now fully floored with hardwood.
We decided to buy a floored tent for extra comfort during camping trips.
All the rooms in the house were floored with polished tiles.
