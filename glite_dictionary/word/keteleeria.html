Keteleeria

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɛtəˈlɪəriə / (AmE), / ˌkiːtɛlɪˈɪəriə / (BrE)
a genus of evergreen coniferous trees in the pine family, native to East and Southeast Asia
genus of coniferous trees

Examples

The forest contains several rare species of Keteleeria that are protected by law.
Botanists have studied the growth patterns of Keteleeria in southern China for decades.
A large Keteleeria dominates the botanical garden's conifer collection.

Etymology

Named after French botanist Jean Baptiste Keteleer.
