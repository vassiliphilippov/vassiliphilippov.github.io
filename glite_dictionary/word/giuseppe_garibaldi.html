Giuseppe Garibaldi

1. PROPN

/ dʒuˌzɛpeɪ ˌɡærɪˈbɑldi / (AmE), / dʒuːˌzɛpeɪ ˌɡærɪˈbɑːldi / (BrE)
(1807–1882) An Italian patriot and military leader whose campaigns in Sicily and Naples contributed significantly to the unification of Italy.
Italian patriot and unification leader

Examples

Giuseppe Garibaldi is celebrated for his pivotal role in the unification of Italy.
Many streets and squares in Italy are named after Giuseppe Garibaldi.
The exploits of Giuseppe Garibaldi inspired other independence movements around the world.
