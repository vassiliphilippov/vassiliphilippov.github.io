missional

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˈmɪʃən(ə)l / (BrE)
Relating to or connected with a religious mission, especially emphasizing outreach or evangelism as a central part of an organization's purpose.
relating to religious missions or outreach

Examples

Many churches have adopted a missional approach, focusing on outreach in their local communities.
Her missional mindset drives her to serve those in need beyond her congregation.
The conference will explore the challenges and opportunities of being a missional church in today's society.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: missionary - relating to or characteristic of missions or missionaries
  • Synonym: evangelistic - relating to the preaching of the Christian gospel

Etymology

Formed from 'mission' with the suffix '-al'.
