defining

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈfaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of giving something its distinct shape, character, or form
process of giving shape or form

Examples

The defining of clear boundaries helped resolve the land dispute.
Sculpting clay involves the careful defining of each feature.
In art, light often plays a role in the defining of shapes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: definition - a statement of the exact meaning of a word or term

Etymology

From the verb 'define', from Latin 'definire' (to set bounds, to limit).
