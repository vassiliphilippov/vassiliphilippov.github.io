camphoric

1. ADJ

/ kæmˈfɔrɪk / (AmE), / kæmˈfɒrɪk / (BrE)
Relating to, derived from, or containing camphor.
relating to or containing camphor
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed a camphoric ointment to relieve nasal congestion.
Laboratories use camphoric compounds in the production of certain medicinal products.
Some traditional balms have a distinct camphoric aroma due to their ingredients.

Synset Examples

camphoric liniments

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camphor - a white, volatile substance used in medicine and as a flavoring

Etymology

From camphor + -ic, indicating relation or pertinence.
