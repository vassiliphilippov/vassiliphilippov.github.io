abhorrence

1. NOUN

/ əbˈhɔrəns / (AmE), / əbˈhɒrəns / (BrE)
A strong feeling of hatred mixed with disgust.
hatred mixed with disgust

Examples

She could not hide her abhorrence of cruelty towards animals.
His abhorrence for injustice was evident in everything he did.
The sight filled him with abhorrence and a desire to leave immediately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loathing - intense dislike or disgust
  • Synonym: hatred - intense dislike or hate
  • Antonym: fondness - affection or liking for something

Etymology

From Latin 'abhorrentia', from 'abhorrere' (to shrink back from, shudder at).
