ferrywoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛriˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈfɛriˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman whose job is to operate or manage a ferry.
female ferry operator

Examples

The ferrywoman guided the boat safely across the river during the heavy storm.
As a ferrywoman, she takes pride in ensuring every passenger has a pleasant journey.
The town appointed its first female ferrywoman last summer, breaking a century-old tradition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ferryman - a man whose job is to operate a ferry
  • Synonym: ferry - to transport people or goods by boat or ship

Etymology

Formed from 'ferry' + 'woman', on the pattern of 'ferryman'.
