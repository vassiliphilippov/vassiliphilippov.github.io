Bahasa Malaysia

1. NOUN

/ bɑːˈhɑːsə məˈleɪziə / (AmE), / bɑːˈhɑːsə məˈleɪziə / (BrE)
the standard form of the Malay language as spoken in Malaysia and neighboring countries
Malay language spoken in Malaysia

Examples

She is fluent in both English and Bahasa Malaysia, which helps her communicate with locals.
The official documents were written in Bahasa Malaysia to ensure everyone could understand them.
Learning Bahasa Malaysia opened new career opportunities for him in Kuala Lumpur.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Malay - Austronesian language spoken in Malaysia, Indonesia, and other areas

Etymology

Malay, literally 'the Malaysian language', from 'bahasa' (language) and 'Malaysia'.
