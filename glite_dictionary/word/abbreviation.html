abbreviation

1. NOUN

/ əˌbriː.viˈeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / əˌbriː.viˈeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a shortened form of a word or phrase, used in writing to save time or space
shortened form of a word or phrase

Examples

USA is an abbreviation for the United States of America.
Please use the standard abbreviation for tablespoon in your notes.
'Dr.' is a common abbreviation for 'Doctor'.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acronym - a word formed from the initial letters of other words
  • Synonym: contraction - a shortened form of a word using an apostrophe

Etymology

From Late Latin abbreviatio, from abbreviāre (to shorten), from Latin ad- (to) + brevis (short).

2. NOUN

/ əˌbri.viˈeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / əˌbriː.viˈeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A shortened form of a word or phrase, made by omitting letters or using only initial letters.
shortened form of a word or phrase

Examples

The abbreviation 'Dr.' stands for 'Doctor'.
Most dictionaries include the abbreviation after the full term.
'USA' is a common abbreviation for the United States of America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acronym - a word formed from the initial letters of other words
  • Antonym: expansion - the full, original form before abbreviation

Etymology

From Late Latin abbreviatio (shortening), from Latin abbreviare (to shorten).
