devising

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈvaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of inventing or planning something carefully; the process of creating a plan or arrangement
the act of inventing or planning

Examples

The devising of new strategies is crucial for the company's success.
Her devising of the perfect itinerary impressed the whole group.
The architect's devising of the building's layout took several months.

Synset Examples

the devising of plans
the fashioning of pots and pans
the making of measurements
it was already in the making

Related Terms

  • Synonym: planning - the process of making plans for something
  • Synonym: creation - the act of bringing something into existence

Etymology

From the verb 'devise,' which comes from Old French 'deviser' (to divide, distribute, plan), from Latin 'divisare.'
