indoor

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪn.dɔr / (AmE), / ˈɪn.dɔː / (BrE)
Located, suited for, or taking place within a building rather than outdoors.
inside a building

Examples

We planned several indoor games to keep the children entertained during the storm.
The indoor arena is heated, making it comfortable year-round.
She prefers indoor plants that thrive without direct sunlight.
