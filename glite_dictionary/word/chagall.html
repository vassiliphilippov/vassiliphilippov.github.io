Chagall

1. PROPN

/ ʃəˈɡɑl / (AmE), / ʃəˈɡɑːl / (BrE)
Marc Chagall (1887–1985), a French painter of Russian origin known for his vivid colors and imaginative, dreamlike imagery.
French painter of Russian origin

Examples

The museum held a special exhibition of Chagall's vibrant paintings.
Many art students are inspired by the dreamlike quality of Chagall's work.
A beautiful stained-glass window created by Chagall adorns the chapel.

Etymology

Surname of Marc Chagall, born Moishe Shagal in Russia.
