categorial

1. ADJ

/ ˌkætəˈɡɔriəl / (AmE), / ˌkatɪˈɡɔːrɪəl / (BrE)
relating to or pertaining to categories, especially as a concept in philosophy, mathematics, or linguistics.
relating to categories

Examples

The linguist explained the categorial structure of the sentence in detail.
Much of modern logic is concerned with the categorial analysis of relations.
Her thesis addressed the categorial distinctions found in philosophical discourse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: categorical - stated explicitly; unconditional, absolute

Etymology

From category + -al, formed on the pattern of technical adjectives from nouns.
