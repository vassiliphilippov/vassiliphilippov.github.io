gummed

1. ADJ

/ ɡʌmd / (AmE), / ɡʌmd / (BrE)
coated with or having adhesive gum on the surface.
covered with sticky gum

Examples

The gummed side of the stamp should be moistened before application.
She folded the gummed envelope flap and pressed it down firmly.
The labels have a gummed backing that makes them easy to stick onto packages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sticky - having the quality of adhering or sticking to objects
