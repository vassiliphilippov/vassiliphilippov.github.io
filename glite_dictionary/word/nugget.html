nugget

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌɡət / (AmE), / ˈnʌɡɪt / (BrE)
a solid lump of a precious metal, especially gold, found in the earth
solid lump of precious metal, esp. gold

Examples

The miner was thrilled when he discovered a large nugget of gold in the riverbed.
She kept a tiny gold nugget as a good luck charm.
The museum displays several rare nuggets that were found during the gold rush.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: ingot - a block of metal, typically oblong, that has been cast into a shape for storage or further processing
