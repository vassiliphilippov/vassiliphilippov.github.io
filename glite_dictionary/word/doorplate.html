doorplate

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔrˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɔːˌpleɪt / (BrE)
A metal or plastic plate attached to a door that displays the name or other information about the person who lives or works there.
nameplate on a door

Examples

The new manager's doorplate was polished and easy to read.
She carefully attached a brass doorplate with her family name to the front door.
Every office in the building has a doorplate indicating the occupant's position.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nameplate - A plaque bearing a name, especially one attached to a door or desk
