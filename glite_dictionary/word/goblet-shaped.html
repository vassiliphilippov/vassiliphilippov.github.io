goblet-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɑblət ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈɡɒblɪt ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a shape similar to that of a goblet, typically wide at the top and narrowing toward the base.
shaped like a goblet

Examples

The architect designed a goblet-shaped fountain in the center of the plaza.
The flower has a distinctive goblet-shaped bloom that attracts many pollinators.
The wine was served in a goblet-shaped glass to enhance its aroma.
