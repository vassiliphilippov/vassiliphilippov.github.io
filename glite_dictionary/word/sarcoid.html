sarcoid

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɑr.kɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɑː.kɔɪd / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling flesh.
resembling or relating to flesh

Examples

The tumor had a distinctly sarcoid appearance under the microscope.
Some parasites induce sarcoid growths in their hosts that mimic real tissue.
The artist sculpted a material with a sarcoid texture to imitate human skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fleshy - having a lot of flesh; plump or soft in appearance

Etymology

From Greek sarx, sark- (flesh) + -oid (resembling).
