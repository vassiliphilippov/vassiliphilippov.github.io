anemometric

1. ADJ

/ ˌænəmoʊˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌanɪməˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of wind speed and direction
measuring wind speed and direction

Examples

The weather station uses anemometric sensors to record wind data throughout the day.
An anemometric survey was conducted to determine the best site for the wind farm.
Advances in anemometric technology have improved the accuracy of meteorological forecasts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anemometer - an instrument for measuring wind speed and direction

Etymology

From Greek anemos (wind) + -metric (measuring).
