birdseed

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɜrd.siːd / (AmE), / ˈbɜːd.siːd / (BrE)
mixture of seeds or grains given as food to birds
mixture of seeds for feeding birds

Examples

She refilled the feeder with fresh birdseed every morning.
The pet store sells several varieties of birdseed for different types of birds.
Sparrows quickly gathered to eat the birdseed scattered on the lawn.
