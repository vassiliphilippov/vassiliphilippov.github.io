Buyi

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuː.ji / (AmE), / ˈbuː.ji / (BrE)
a Tai language spoken mainly in the Guizhou-Yunnan Plateau of southwest China, chiefly by the Buyi people.
a Tai language of southwest China

Examples

Many members of the ethnic group still speak Buyi in daily life.
Researchers are documenting traditional stories in the Buyi language.
He traveled to Guizhou to study Buyi with local linguists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tai - a branch of the Tai-Kadai language family
