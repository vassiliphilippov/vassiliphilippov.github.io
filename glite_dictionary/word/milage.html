milage

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪlɪdʒ / (BrE)
the distance that is measured or can be traveled in miles
distance measured in miles

Examples

The car's milage has increased significantly after our cross-country trip.
He tracks the milage on his running shoes to know when to replace them.
Her job requires a lot of driving, resulting in high annual milage on her vehicle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mileage - distance measured in miles (preferred modern spelling)

Etymology

Altered spelling of 'mileage', from mile + -age (forming nouns indicating an amount related to a specified unit).
