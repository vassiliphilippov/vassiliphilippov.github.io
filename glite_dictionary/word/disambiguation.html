1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪs.æmˌbɪɡ.juˈeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.æmˌbɪɡ.juˈeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something clear by removing ambiguity or confusion
removal of ambiguity; clarification

Examples

The disambiguation of technical terms is essential in scientific writing.
On Wikipedia, many articles require disambiguation pages to avoid confusion.
The system uses context clues for the disambiguation of words with multiple meanings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clarification - the act of making something clearer or easier to understand
  • Antonym: ambiguity - uncertainty or inexactness of meaning in language

Etymology

From Latin prefix dis- (negation, removal) + 'ambiguation', derived from 'ambiguous'.
