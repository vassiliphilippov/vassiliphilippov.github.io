unbaptized

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈbæp.taɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈbæp.tʌɪzd / (BrE)
not having undergone the Christian rite of baptism.
not having received baptism

Examples

According to the church, the unbaptized may not participate in certain sacraments.
The community cared for abandoned unbaptized infants in the orphanage.
She remained unbaptized throughout her life, despite her family's traditions.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: baptized - having undergone the Christian rite of baptism

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' and 'baptized', from Greek 'baptizein' (to immerse, baptize).
