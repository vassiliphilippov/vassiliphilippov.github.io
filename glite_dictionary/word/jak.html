jak

1. NOUN

/ dʒæk / (AmE), / dʒæk / (BrE)
A large tropical fruit from South and Southeast Asia, similar to breadfruit, with edible sweet pulp and seeds.
large tropical fruit, similar to breadfruit

Examples

The market had a massive jak on display alongside other exotic fruits.
She prepared a delicious curry using ripe jak pulp and spices.
Roasted jak seeds are a popular snack in many parts of India.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jackfruit - large tropical fruit with sweet yellow flesh and seeds
  • Synonym: breadfruit - round, starchy tropical fruit related to jak

Etymology

From Sinhalese *kos* or Malayalam *chakka*, root of 'jack' or 'jackfruit'.
