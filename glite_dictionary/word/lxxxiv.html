lxxxiv

1. NUM

/ ɛl ɛks ɛks ɛks aɪ vi / (AmE), / ɛl ɛks ɛks ɛks aɪ vi / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for eighty-four (84)
Roman numeral for 84

Examples

The document was signed in the year LXXXIV of the Roman era.
Chapter LXXXIV of the book contains important information about ancient Rome.
On the stone inscription, the number LXXXIV marks the completed years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eighty-four - the cardinal number 84
  • Degree Lower: lxxxiii - the Roman numeral for 83
  • Degree Higher: lxxxv - the Roman numeral for 85

Etymology

From Latin numerals: L (50) + XXX (30) + IV (4) = 84.
