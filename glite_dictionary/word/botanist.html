botanist

1. NOUN

/ bəˈtænɪst / (AmE), / bəˈtænɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who specializes in the study of plants
plant scientist

Examples

The botanist collected rare specimens during his expedition in the Amazon rainforest.
As a botanist, she spends most of her time in the laboratory analyzing plant samples.
He became interested in becoming a botanist after learning about plant genetics in school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biologist - a scientist who studies living organisms

Etymology

From French botaniste, from botanique (botany), from Greek botanikos (of herbs), from botane (plant, herb).
