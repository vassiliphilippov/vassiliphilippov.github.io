Belfast

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɛl.fæst / (AmE), / ˈbɛl.fɑːst / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Northern Ireland, known for its historical and cultural significance
capital city of Northern Ireland

Examples

The peace agreement was signed in Belfast in 1998, marking a new era for Northern Ireland.
She moved to Belfast for university and quickly grew fond of the city's vibrant atmosphere.
Many tourists visit Belfast to learn about its history and explore its beautiful landmarks.

Etymology

From Irish Béal Feirste, meaning 'mouth of the (River) Farset'.
