fella

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛlə / (AmE), / ˈfɛlə / (BrE)
(informal) a man or boy; fellow.
informal term for a man or boy
informal

Examples

He's a nice fella once you get to know him.
A fella at the bar offered to buy me a drink.
You shouldn't judge a fella by his appearance.

Synset Examples

that chap is your host

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fellow - a man or boy; used as a friendly or familiar form of address
  • Synonym: chap - a man or boy; informal term especially in British English
  • Synonym: bloke - a man; chiefly British informal

Etymology

Variant of 'fellow', reflecting informal pronunciation.
