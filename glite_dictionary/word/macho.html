macho

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːtʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmatʃəʊ / (BrE)
A man who is aggressively proud of his masculinity; a man who exhibits characteristics or attitudes traditionally associated with strong or exaggerated masculinity.
man showing exaggerated masculinity

Examples

He tried to impress everyone with his macho attitude at the gym.
Some people find his macho behavior off-putting.
Despite his macho reputation, he has a sensitive side.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: machismo - strong or exaggerated masculine pride or conduct

Etymology

From Spanish 'macho' (male, masculine).

2. ADJ

/ ˈmɑːtʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmætʃəʊ / (BrE)
(of a man) displaying or characterized by exaggerated masculinity in appearance or behavior.
overtly masculine in appearance or manner

Examples

Many people admired the macho image he projected in public.
He acted tough and macho, but was very sensitive inside.
The movie features a macho hero who always saves the day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manly - having qualities traditionally associated with men
  • Antonym: effeminate - (of a man) having qualities more often associated with women

Etymology

From Spanish 'macho' (male, masculine).

Synset POS: s
