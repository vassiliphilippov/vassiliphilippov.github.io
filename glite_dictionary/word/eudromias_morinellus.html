Eudromias morinellus

1. NOUN

/ juːˈdroʊmiəs ˌmɒrɪˈnɛləs / (AmE), / juːˈdrəʊmiəs ˌmɒrɪˈnɛləs / (BrE)
a rare species of plover found in upland areas of Eurasia, known commonly as the Eurasian dotterel
a rare Eurasian upland plover

Examples

During their spring migration, several Eudromias morinellus were spotted in the Scottish Highlands.
Researchers tracked the breeding patterns of the Eudromias morinellus across remote mountain regions.
The Eudromias morinellus is admired for its striking plumage and rarity among birdwatchers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Eurasian dotterel - common name for Eudromias morinellus, a rare upland plover

Etymology

From New Latin: genus name 'Eudromias' and species epithet 'morinellus'.
