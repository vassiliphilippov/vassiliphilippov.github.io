SbE

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaʊθ baɪ ˈist / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθ baɪ ˈiːst / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point east of due south; south by east.
compass point; south by east

Examples

The ship adjusted its course to SbE to avoid the rocks.
On the map, the old lighthouse is located exactly at SbE from the harbor.
The captain gave orders to steer SbE for the next two miles.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: south - the direction toward the South Pole, at 180° on a compass
  • Degree Higher: south-southeast - the compass point between south and southeast

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'south by east'.
