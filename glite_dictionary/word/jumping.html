jumping

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of competing in an athletic event that requires participants to jump, such as in track and field sports
athletic event involving jumping

Examples

She won first place in jumping at the school track meet.
The athlete specializes in jumping events like the high jump and long jump.
Proper technique is crucial for success in competitive jumping.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jump - the act of pushing oneself off a surface into the air

2. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of propelling oneself off the ground, typically by using the legs
the act of leaping into the air

Examples

The children love jumping on the trampoline in the backyard.
Olympic athletes train for years to perfect their jumping technique.
Sudden jumping can cause injuries if you are not careful.

Synset Examples

he advanced in a series of jumps
the jumping was unexpected

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leaping - the act of making a long or high jump
  • Antonym: running - the action of moving rapidly on foot
