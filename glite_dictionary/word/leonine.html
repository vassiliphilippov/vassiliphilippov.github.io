leonine

1. ADJ

/ ˈliːəˌnaɪn / (AmE), / ˈliː.ə.naɪn / (BrE)
having characteristics of or resembling a lion, especially in appearance, strength, or manner.
like or resembling a lion

Examples

The actor's leonine mane gave him an imposing presence on stage.
She moved with a leonine grace, both powerful and elegant.
His leonine courage inspired everyone during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lion-like - resembling a lion
  • Synonym: feline - relating to or characteristic of cats
  • Antonym: canine - relating to or characteristic of dogs

Etymology

From Latin 'leoninus' (of a lion), from 'leo' (lion).
