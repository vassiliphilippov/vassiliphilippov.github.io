Quintana Roo

1. PROPN

/ kinˈtɑːnə ˈroʊ / (AmE), / kwɪnˈtɑːnə ˈrəʊ / (BrE)
A state in southeastern Mexico located on the eastern side of the Yucatán Peninsula, known for its Caribbean beaches and the resort city of Cancún.
Mexican state on the Yucatán Peninsula

Examples

Cancún is one of the most famous tourist destinations in Quintana Roo.
The archaeological sites of Quintana Roo attract thousands of visitors each year.
Many people enjoy the beautiful beaches found throughout Quintana Roo.

Etymology

Named after Andrés Quintana Roo, a Mexican politician and writer.
