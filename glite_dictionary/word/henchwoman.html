henchwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛntʃˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈhɛntʃˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who loyally supports and assists a powerful person, especially in criminal or dubious undertakings
female aide in wrongdoing

Examples

The notorious villain was always accompanied by his loyal henchwoman throughout the movie.
Rumors spread that the politician's henchwoman had arranged the secret meeting.
The police finally apprehended the mob boss and her trusted henchwoman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: henchman - a loyal supporter or assistant, especially in criminal activity

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'henchman', replacing 'man' with 'woman'.
