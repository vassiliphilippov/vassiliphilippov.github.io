cockamamie

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑːkəˈmeɪmi / (AmE), / ˌkɒkəˈmeɪmi / (BrE)
ridiculous or implausible; absurd
ridiculous; absurd
informal

Examples

The jury couldn't believe such a cockamamie story could be true.
She always comes up with some cockamamie excuse for being late.
I'm not buying that cockamamie plan of his.

Synset Examples

he gave me a cockamamie reason for not going

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absurd - wildly unreasonable or illogical; synonym for cockamamie
  • Antonym: sensible - reasonable and practical; antonym of cockamamie

Etymology

Origin uncertain, possibly from 20th-century US slang, perhaps a fanciful alteration of 'decalcomania'.
