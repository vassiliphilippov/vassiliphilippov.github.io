backspacer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbækˌspeɪsər / (AmE), / ˈbækˌspeɪsə / (BrE)
the key on a typewriter used to move the carriage or typing position backward by one space
typewriter key for backspacing

Examples

He pressed the backspacer to correct the typo on the typewriter.
Older models often had a noisy backspacer that required extra force.
The backspacer is rarely found on modern computer keyboards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backspace key - the key on a keyboard or typewriter that moves the cursor or carriage backward
