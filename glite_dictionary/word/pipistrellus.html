Pipistrellus

1. PROPN

/ ˌpɪpɪˈstɹɛləs / (AmE), / ˌpɪpɪˈstreləs / (BrE)
A genus of very small bats found nearly worldwide, commonly known as pipistrelles.
genus of small bats

Examples

The common Pipistrellus is one of the smallest bat species found in Europe.
Researchers studied the echolocation patterns of Pipistrellus in various habitats.
Several species within the genus Pipistrellus are widespread across Asia and Africa.

Etymology

From Latin 'pipistrellus', diminutive of 'vespertilio' (bat), meaning 'little bat'.

2. NOUN

/ pɪpɪˈstrɛləs / (AmE), / pɪpɪˈstrɛləs / (BrE)
A genus of very small bats found nearly worldwide, commonly known as pipistrelle bats.
genus of small bats

Examples

The Pipistrellus genus includes species that are widespread across Europe and Asia.
Scientists recently discovered a new species within the Pipistrellus genus.
Many urban areas provide habitats for Pipistrellus bats due to the abundance of insects.

Etymology

From Italian 'pipistrello' (bat), ultimately from Latin.
