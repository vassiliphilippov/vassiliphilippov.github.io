simperer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪmpərər / (AmE), / ˈsɪmpərə / (BrE)
A person who simpers; someone who smiles in a silly, self-conscious, or coy way.
person who simpers or smiles coyly

Examples

As the meeting progressed, the simperer in the front row kept giggling at every comment.
No one took the simperer seriously, as his smile seemed insincere and affected.
Behind her innocent expression, the simperer masked a sharp wit and keen observation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simper - to smile in a silly, self-conscious, or coy way

Etymology

From 'simper' (to smile in a silly, affected manner) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
