Cardiff

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɑːrdɪf / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdɪf / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Wales, located in the south of the country
capital city of Wales

Examples

Cardiff has become a vibrant cultural and sporting hub in recent years.
The train from London to Cardiff takes about two hours.
Many students choose to study at Cardiff University because of its excellent reputation.

Etymology

From Welsh 'Caerdydd', from 'Caer' (fort) and 'Dydd' (possibly a river name or personal name).
