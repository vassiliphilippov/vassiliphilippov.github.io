efficaciousness

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛf.ɪˈkeɪ.ʃəs.nəs / (AmE), / ˌɛf.ɪˈkeɪ.ʃəs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or ability of producing the intended or desired result
ability to produce a desired effect

Examples

The efficaciousness of the new policy was evident in the reduced crime rates.
Scientists debated the efficaciousness of various treatments before recommending one.
She was impressed by the efficaciousness of the cleaning solution on stubborn stains.

Synset Examples

concern about the safety and efficacy of the vaccine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: efficacy - the ability to produce a desired or intended result

Etymology

From Latin efficax (effectual, efficient) + -ous + -ness.
