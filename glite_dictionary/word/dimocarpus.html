Dimocarpus

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈmoʊkɑːrpəs / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒkɑːpəs / (BrE)
A genus of tropical flowering plants in the soapberry family, best known for the longan fruit.
genus of tropical plants (longan)

Examples

The genus Dimocarpus includes several species of tropical trees native to Southeast Asia.
Dimocarpus longan is widely cultivated for its sweet, translucent fruit.
Botanists recently discovered a new species within the Dimocarpus genus in northern Thailand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: longan - an edible tropical fruit from the tree Dimocarpus longan

Etymology

From Neo-Latin, formed from Greek roots 'dimos' (public) and 'karpos' (fruit).
