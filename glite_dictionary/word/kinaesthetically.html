kinaesthetically

1. ADV

/ kɪnəsˈθɛtɪkli / (AmE), / kɪnɪsˈθɛtɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to kinesthesia; by means of movement sensation or physical activity.
in a kinesthetic manner

Examples

Many students learn best kinaesthetically, through hands-on activities and movement.
He was able to navigate the maze kinaesthetically, sensing the walls with his body.
The therapist encouraged her patient to express emotions kinaesthetically through dance and movement.

Synset Examples

he can perceive shapes kinesthetically

Related Terms

  • Antonym: visually - in a manner relating to vision; by means of seeing
  • Antonym: auditorily - in a manner relating to hearing; by means of sound

Etymology

From the noun 'kinaesthesia' (from Greek 'kinesis' meaning movement) with the adverbial suffix '-ally.'
