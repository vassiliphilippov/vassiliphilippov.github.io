tilted

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɪltɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɪltɪd / (BrE)
Not in a straight or upright position; sloping or slanted away from the true vertical or horizontal.
not straight; sloping or slanted

Examples

The old picture frame on the wall was slightly tilted to the right.
Several tombstones in the cemetery appeared tilted after the storm.
He noticed his glasses were tilted on his nose and adjusted them.

Synset Examples

the leaning tower of Pisa
the headstones were tilted

Related Terms

  • Antonym: level - in a position that is horizontal or flat; not sloping
  • Antonym: upright - in a vertical position; standing straight up
  • Synonym: slanted - at an angle; not straight or level

Etymology

From the verb 'tilt', which comes from Old English 'tyltan' (to fall over, totter).
