stylishly

1. ADV

/ ˈstaɪlɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlɪʃli / (BrE)
In a way that is fashionable and elegant.
in a fashionable or elegant manner

Examples

The guests arrived stylishly dressed in evening wear.
He redecorated his apartment stylishly, choosing modern furniture and bold colors.
They stylishly combined vintage pieces with new fashion trends.

Synset Examples

she was dressed very stylishly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fashionably - in a way that follows the fashion; stylishly
  • Synonym: stylish - having style; fashionable and elegant
