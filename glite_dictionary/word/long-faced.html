long-faced

1. ADJ

/ lɔŋˈfeɪst / (AmE), / lɒŋˈfeɪst / (BrE)
having a face that is longer than usual in proportion
having a longer-than-average face

Examples

The horse has a distinctly long-faced appearance compared to others of its breed.
Several portraits from that era depicted long-faced men with narrow chins and high foreheads.
Her brother is quite long-faced, which gives him an air of seriousness.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: sad - unhappy or sorrowful
