unmotivated

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmoʊtɪveɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈməʊtɪveɪtɪd / (BrE)
Not having or showing any motivation or enthusiasm to act or achieve something.
without motivation or enthusiasm

Examples

Many students feel unmotivated when they do not see the relevance of their assignments.
After months without progress, the team became increasingly unmotivated.
She found herself unmotivated to exercise after her injury.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: motivated - having motivation or a reason to act

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'motivated'.
