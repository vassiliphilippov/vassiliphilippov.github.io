knothole

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑtˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈnɒtˌhəʊl / (BrE)
A hole in a piece of wood where a knot has fallen out.
hole in wood where knot fell out

Examples

He peeked through the knothole in the fence to see what was happening on the other side.
The old wooden floorboards were full of knotholes and cracks.
A small bird nested inside a knothole in the tree trunk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knot - a hard, cross-grained mass of wood where a branch was
