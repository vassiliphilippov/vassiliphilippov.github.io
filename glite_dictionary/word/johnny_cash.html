Johnny Cash

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɑni kæʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒni kæʃ / (BrE)
(1932–2003) An American country music singer and songwriter known for his deep, distinctive voice and songs exploring themes of sorrow, morality, and redemption.
American country singer-songwriter (1932–2003)

Examples

Johnny Cash was famous for his deep voice and black clothing.
Many people consider Johnny Cash one of the most influential country musicians of all time.
The music of Johnny Cash continues to inspire new generations of artists.
