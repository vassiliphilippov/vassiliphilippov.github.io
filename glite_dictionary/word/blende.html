blende

1. NOUN

/ blɛnd / (AmE), / blɛnd / (BrE)
a mineral consisting mainly of zinc sulfide in crystalline form, and the principal ore of zinc
chief ore of zinc; zinc sulfide mineral

Examples

The mining company extracted blende from several locations to supply the zinc industry.
Under the microscope, blende displays a characteristic dark, crystalline appearance.
Large deposits of blende can be found in several European countries.

Etymology

From German 'Blende', meaning 'deceiver', because it resembles galena but does not yield lead.
