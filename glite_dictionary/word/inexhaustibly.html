inexhaustibly

1. ADV

/ ˌɪnɪɡˈzɔstəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪnɪɡˈzɔːstəbli / (BrE)
In a manner that cannot be exhausted; with tireless or indefatigable energy.
in a tireless or unending way

Examples

She worked inexhaustibly to complete the project before the deadline.
The volunteers helped the victims inexhaustibly, never pausing to rest.
He pursued his passion for music inexhaustibly throughout his life.

Synset Examples

she watched the show indefatigably

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indefatigably - in a way that cannot be tired out; tirelessly

Etymology

From 'inexhaustible' + '-ly', ultimately from Latin 'exhaurire' (to drain out).
