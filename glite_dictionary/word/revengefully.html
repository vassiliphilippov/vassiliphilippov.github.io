revengefully

1. ADV

/ rɪˈvɛndʒfəli / (AmE), / rɪˈvɛndʒfəli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows a desire to take revenge or to retaliate for a wrongdoing.
in a vindictive or retaliatory manner

Examples

She glared revengefully at her rivals, plotting her next move.
After being insulted, he responded revengefully with harsh words.
Employees revengefully leaked the company secrets after being mistreated.

Synset Examples

he plotted vindictively against his former superiors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vindictively - in a manner that shows a strong desire for revenge

Etymology

Formed from 'revengeful' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
