ma-in-law

1. NOUN

/ mɑ ɪn lɔ / (AmE), / mɑː ɪn lɔː / (BrE)
(informal) One's mother-in-law; the mother of one's spouse.
mother of one's spouse (informal)
informal

Examples

My ma-in-law is visiting us this weekend and staying for dinner.
He bought flowers for his ma-in-law on her birthday.
Whenever I get sick, my ma-in-law insists on making me soup.

Synset Examples

Her ma-in-law always brings the best dishes to family gatherings.
He called his ma-in-law for help with the wedding planning.
The hyphen in 'mother-in-law' is crucial for understanding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mother-in-law - the mother of one's spouse
