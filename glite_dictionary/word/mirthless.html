mirthless

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɝːθ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈmɜːθ.ləs / (BrE)
(of a smile or laugh) lacking real joy or amusement; without happiness or genuine enjoyment.
lacking joy or amusement

Examples

She gave a mirthless laugh when she heard the bad news.
His mirthless eyes revealed the pain behind his forced smile.
The room was filled with mirthless faces after the disappointing announcement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joyless - lacking joy; without happiness
  • Antonym: cheerful - full of cheer; visibly happy or optimistic

Etymology

Formed from 'mirth' (joy, amusement) + suffix '-less' (without).
