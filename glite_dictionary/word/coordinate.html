coordinate

1. NOUN

/ ˌkoʊˈɔrdn̩ɪt / (AmE), / kəʊˈɔːdɪnət / (BrE)
each of a set of numbers used to indicate the position of a point, line, or plane in a system relative to an axis or axes, especially in mathematics and cartography.
a number locating a position on an axis

Examples

To find the treasure, you need the exact coordinate on the map.
Plot each coordinate on the graph to see the shape clearly.
The GPS gave a coordinate that was slightly off from the intended destination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: location - a particular place or position
  • Synonym: dimension - an aspect or measure of spatial extent

Etymology

From Latin *coordinatus* (arranged, set in order), from *co-* (together) + *ordinare* (to arrange).

2. NOUN

Examples

Related Terms
