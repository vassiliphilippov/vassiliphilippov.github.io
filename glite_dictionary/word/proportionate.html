proportionate

1. ADJ

/ prəˈpɔrʃənət / (AmE), / prəˈpɔːʃənət / (BrE)
in the correct or appropriate relation in size, amount, degree, etc. to something else
in proper or appropriate proportion

Examples

The punishment should be proportionate to the seriousness of the offense.
They received proportionate shares of the inheritance based on their ages.
There was a proportionate increase in sales following the new advertising campaign.

Synset Examples

proportionate representation of a minority group

Related Terms

  • Antonym: disproportionate - not in correct or appropriate proportion
  • Synonym: commensurate - corresponding in size or degree; in proportion

Etymology

From Latin 'proportionatus' (made proportional), from 'proportio' (proportion).

2. ADJ

/ prəˈpɔrʃənət / (AmE), / prəˈpɔːʃənət / (BrE)
corresponding in size, amount, or degree; in correct or suitable proportion to something else
in correct or suitable proportion

Examples

The punishment should be proportionate to the crime committed.
Expenses must remain proportionate to the amount of income earned.
Their response was proportionate given the seriousness of the situation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: disproportionate - not in correct or suitable proportion
  • Synonym: proportional - corresponding in size or amount to something else

Etymology

From Late Latin proportionatus (made according to proportion), from proportio (proportion).

3. ADJ

/ prəˈpɔːrʃənət / (AmE), / prəˈpɔːʃənət / (BrE)
Corresponding in size, amount, or degree to something else; in correct or appropriate proportion.
corresponding in degree or size

Examples

The punishment should be proportionate to the severity of the offense.
Her responsibilities increased in a proportionate manner as she advanced in her career.
They made a proportionate response to the level of threat.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: disproportionate - not in correct proportion
  • Synonym: commensurate - corresponding in size or extent; proportionate

Etymology

From Latin prōportionātus, past participle of prōportionāre (to proportion).
