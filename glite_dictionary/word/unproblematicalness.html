unproblematicalness

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌnˌprɑːbləˈmætɪklnəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌprɒbləˈmætɪklnəs / (BrE)
The quality of not causing problems or difficulties; the state of being straightforward or trouble-free.
state of being free of problems

Examples

The unproblematicalness of the procedure reassured everyone involved in the project.
He admired the unproblematicalness with which she handled complex negotiations.
The unproblematicalness of their friendship made it easy for them to confide in each other.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unproblematic - not presenting a problem or difficulty; easy to deal with

Etymology

From 'unproblematical' + '-ness'.
