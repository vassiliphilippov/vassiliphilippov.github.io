hobbyism

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːbiˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈhɒbiˌɪzəm / (BrE)
devotion to or practice of engaging in hobbies, especially as a principal pursuit or recreation
devotion to hobbies

Examples

His passion for model trains is a clear example of hobbyism taken to an art form.
The rise of hobbyism in recent decades reflects people's desire for creative outlets.
She turned her hobbyism into a thriving collection that now fills an entire room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hobby - an activity done regularly in one's leisure time for pleasure

Etymology

From 'hobby' + '-ism', modeled on similar constructions (e.g., 'tourism').
