untitled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtaɪt̬əld / (AmE), / ʌnˈtaɪtld / (BrE)
(of a person) not belonging to the nobility; not having a title of nobility.
not having a title of nobility

Examples

Many untitled individuals worked as tradesmen or farmers in feudal societies.
The government granted privileges to untitled citizens, as well as to those with noble rank.
She married an untitled man, defying her aristocratic family's expectations.

Synset Examples

of ignoble (or ungentle) birth
untitled civilians

Related Terms

  • Antonym: titled - having a noble or aristocratic title
