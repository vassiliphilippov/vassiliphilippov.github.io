christ

1. NOUN

/ kraɪst / (AmE), / kraɪst / (BrE)
a person who is expected to deliver or save people from a difficult situation; a savior or liberator, especially as a metaphorical or prophetic figure
expected deliverer or savior

Examples

Some saw the charismatic leader as a christ who would transform their nation.
He believed that every era waits for its own christ to emerge from the turmoil.
Writers have sometimes used the term christ to describe figures who offer hope and deliverance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: messiah - a savior or liberator expected by a group

Etymology

From Greek Khristos (anointed), translating Hebrew Māshīaḥ (Messiah).
