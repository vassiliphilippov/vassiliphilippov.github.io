hr

1. NOUN

/ eɪtʃ ɑr / (AmE), / eɪtʃ ɑː / (BrE)
Abbreviation of hour; a period of time equal to one twenty-fourth of a day (60 minutes).
abbreviation for hour (60 minutes)
informal

Examples

The meeting is scheduled for 2 hr tomorrow.
This medication should be taken every 4 hrs.
Each hr of exercise improves your health.

Synset Examples

the job will take more than an hour

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hour - a period of time equal to 1/24th of a day

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'hour' from Latin 'hora' (hour, time).
