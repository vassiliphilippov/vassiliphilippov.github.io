unhopeful

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhoʊpfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈhəʊpfəl / (BrE)
lacking hope; showing or expressing no expectation of success or improvement.
lacking hope

Examples

After several failed attempts, his face became visibly unhopeful.
The team's unhopeful attitude made defeat seem inevitable.
She delivered the news in an unhopeful tone, expecting no change.

Synset Examples

abject surrender

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hopeless - having no hope; desperate

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'un-' and 'hopeful', meaning not hopeful.
