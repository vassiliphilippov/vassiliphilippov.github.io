2. NOUN

/ ɡeɪt / (AmE), / ɡeɪt / (BrE)
a movable barrier used to close an opening in a fence, wall, or other enclosure
movable barrier in a fence or wall

Examples

He opened the gate and walked into the garden.
The old wooden gate creaked loudly when swung open.
Please remember to close the gate so the sheep don't escape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: door - a movable barrier for closing the entrance to a building or room

Etymology

Examples
