IBDA-C

1. NOUN

/ ˌaɪ.bi.diːˈeɪ siː / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.biː.diːˈeɪ siː / (BrE)
a Turkish Islamist militant organization opposed to Turkey's secular government, known for violent attacks including bombings in Istanbul
Turkish Islamist militant group

Examples

The Turkish authorities tightened security after IBDA-C claimed responsibility for the attacks.
Several members of IBDA-C were arrested following the bombing in Istanbul.
The international community condemned the violence attributed to IBDA-C.

Etymology

Acronym from Turkish 'İslami Büyük Doğu Akıncılar Cephesi' (Great Eastern Islamic Raiders' Front).
