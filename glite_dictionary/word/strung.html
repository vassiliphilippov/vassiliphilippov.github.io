strung

1. ADJ

/ strʌŋ / (AmE), / strʌŋ / (BrE)
placed or threaded on a string, cord, or wire.
placed on a string or cord

Examples

She wore a necklace made of pearls strung on a thin gold chain.
The colorful flags were strung across the street for the festival.
The shop sells beads strung together in beautiful patterns.

Synset Examples

keys strung on a red cord

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unstrung - not placed or threaded on a string, cord, or wire
