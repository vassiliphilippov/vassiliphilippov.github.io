soleless

1. ADJ

/ ˈsoʊlləs / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlləs / (BrE)
(of footwear or feet) lacking a sole.
lacking a sole

Examples

After months of heavy use, his sneakers became completely soleless.
The dancer performed barefoot, moving gracefully across the stage with soleless feet.
She bought a pair of soleless sandals designed for minimalists.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sole - the underside of a foot or shoe
  • Antonym: soled - having a sole

Etymology

Formed from 'sole' (the underside of a foot or shoe) + '-less' (without).
