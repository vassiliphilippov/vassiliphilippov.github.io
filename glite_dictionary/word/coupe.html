coupe

1. NOUN

/ kuːˈpeɪ / (AmE), / kuːˈpeɪ / (BrE)
a car with two doors, front seats, and a separate luggage compartment, often with a sporty design
two-door car with front seats and trunk

Examples

He bought a sleek red coupe for his daily commute.
The new model is available as both a sedan and a coupe.
She prefers driving a coupe because it feels sportier than a sedan.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sedan - a car with four doors and a separate trunk

Etymology

From French 'coupé' (cut), referring to a carriage with a cut-down body.
