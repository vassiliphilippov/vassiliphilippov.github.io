KwaZulu-Natal

1. PROPN

/ kwɑːˈzuːlu nəˈtɑːl / (AmE), / kwɑːˈzuːluː nəˈtɑːl / (BrE)
a province in eastern South Africa, situated along the Indian Ocean coast, known for its cultural diversity and historical significance
province in eastern South Africa

Examples

She traveled across KwaZulu-Natal to experience its stunning landscapes and vibrant culture.
The beaches of KwaZulu-Natal are popular with both tourists and locals alike.
In 1994, the region was officially renamed KwaZulu-Natal.

Synset Examples

Natal was renamed KwaZulu-Natal in 1994

Etymology

From Zulu, combining 'KwaZulu' (place of the Zulu) and 'Natal' (named by Portuguese explorers in 1497).
