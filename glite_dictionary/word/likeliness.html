likeliness

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪk.lɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈlaɪk.lɪ.nɪs / (BrE)
The probability or chance of something happening.
probability; chance of occurrence

Examples

The likeliness of rain this afternoon is quite high according to the forecast.
Scientists have studied the likeliness of certain diseases appearing in families.
There is a growing likeliness that the event will be cancelled due to bad weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: likelihood - the state or fact of being likely or probable

Etymology

Formed from 'likely' and the suffix '-ness', modeled after 'likelihood'.
