bolti

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊlti / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlti / (BrE)
an important food fish of the Nile and other rivers in Africa and Asia Minor, also known as Nile tilapia
a Nile food fish; Nile tilapia

Examples

The fishermen caught dozens of bolti from the river early this morning.
Bolti is often grilled and served with spices in Egyptian cuisine.
In the markets along the Nile, fresh bolti is a popular choice for shoppers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tilapia - a genus of freshwater fish often used as food
