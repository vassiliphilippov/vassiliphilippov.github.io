thirty-something

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɝːtiˌsʌmθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθɜːtiˌsʌmθɪŋ / (BrE)
a person who is aged between 30 and 39, or the period in someone's life between the ages of 30 and 39
person aged 30-39 or that age period

Examples

Many thirty-somethings are considering major career changes.
She started her own business in her thirty-something years.
The club attracts mainly thirty-somethings looking for a place to relax after work.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: forty-something - person aged 40-49 or that period of life
  • Degree Lower: twenty-something - person aged 20-29 or that period of life

Etymology

From 'thirty' + 'something,' patterned after older forms like 'twenty-something.'
