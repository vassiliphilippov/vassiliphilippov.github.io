winsomely

1. ADV

/ ˈwɪnsəmli / (AmE), / ˈwɪnsəmli / (BrE)
In a charming and engaging manner; in a way that is attractive or appealing.
in a charming or engaging manner

Examples

She smiled winsomely at the interviewer, putting everyone at ease.
He winsomely offered to help, making the task seem less daunting.
The child winsomely asked for an extra cookie, melting her grandmother's heart.

Synset Examples

she played the role engagingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charmingly - in a charming manner
  • Antonym: awkwardly - in an awkward or ungraceful manner

Etymology

From the adjective "winsome" (from Old English *wynn* meaning joy or pleasure) + -ly.
