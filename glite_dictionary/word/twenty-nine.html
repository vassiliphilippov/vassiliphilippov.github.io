twenty-nine

1. NUM

/ ˌtwɛntiˈnaɪn / (AmE), / ˌtwɛntiˈnaɪn / (BrE)
the number 29; the cardinal number equal to twenty-eight plus one
the number 29

Examples

She turned twenty-nine last month and celebrated with a small party.
There are twenty-nine countries represented in this year's competition.
The train leaves at twenty-nine minutes past seven every morning.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-eight - the number 28; the cardinal number before twenty-nine
  • Degree Higher: thirty - the number 30; the cardinal number after twenty-nine
