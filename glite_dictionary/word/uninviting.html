uninviting

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪnˈvaɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈvaɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
Not attractive or appealing; not tempting or welcoming.
not attractive or appealing

Examples

The restaurant looked uninviting from the outside, so we decided to eat elsewhere.
She hesitated at the door, put off by the room’s uninviting atmosphere.
The weather was cold and uninviting, discouraging anyone from going out.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inviting - pleasing or attractive in a way that encourages someone to approach or participate
  • Synonym: unattractive - not attractive or appealing

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (not) to 'inviting'.

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪnˈvaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈvaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
not attractive or appealing; lacking qualities that draw people in
not attractive or appealing

Examples

The abandoned house had an uninviting appearance, with broken windows and peeling paint.
Her tone was so uninviting that I hesitated to ask further questions.
The water looked cold and uninviting despite the warm weather.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inviting - attractive or tempting, especially in appearance or manner
  • Synonym: appealing - attractive or interesting

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (not) to 'inviting.'
