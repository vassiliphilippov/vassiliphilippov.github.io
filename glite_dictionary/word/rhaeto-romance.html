Rhaeto-Romance

1. NOUN

/ ˌreɪtoʊ roʊˈmæns / (AmE), / ˌreɪtəʊ rə(ʊ)ˈmæns / (BrE)
a group of Romance dialects spoken in parts of southeastern Switzerland, northern Italy, and the Tyrol
Romance dialect group in Switzerland and Italy

Examples

Ladin is one of the best-known Rhaeto-Romance languages.
He specializes in the study of Rhaeto-Romance dialects and their development.
The Rhaeto-Romance languages have preserved many features from Latin.

Etymology

From Latin Raetus (an Alpine people) + Romance (referring to the Romance language family).
