blastogenetic

1. ADJ

/ ˌblæstəˈdʒɛnətɪk / (AmE), / ˌblɑːstəˈdʒɛnətɪk / (BrE)
relating to blastogenesis, especially to the origin and development of an organism or tissue from a non-germ cell source.
relating to blastogenesis
medical

Examples

The blastogenetic origin of certain blood cells is a topic of ongoing research.
Some diseases may be linked to blastogenetic anomalies during early tissue development.
He presented his findings on blastogenetic processes at the international genetics conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blastogenesis - the process by which new individuals develop from buds or by cell proliferation, not involving gametes

Etymology

From 'blastogenesis' + '-etic', following the model of 'genetic'.
