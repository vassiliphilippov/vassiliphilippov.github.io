Juglandales

1. NOUN

/ dʒʌɡˈlæn.deɪlz / (AmE), / dʒʌɡˈlan.deɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of flowering plants that includes the walnut family (Juglandaceae) and related families
order of flowering plants including walnuts

Examples

The family Juglandaceae, including walnuts and hickories, is classified under the order Juglandales.
Botanists have studied the evolutionary history of Juglandales extensively.
Many trees in Juglandales are valued for their nuts and timber.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Juglandaceae - the walnut family of flowering plants

Etymology

From Latin 'Juglans' (walnut) + botanical order suffix '-ales'.
