insurrectional

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.səˈrɛk.ʃə.nəl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.səˈrɛk.ʃən.əl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of insurrection; involving or inclined toward revolt against authority
relating to insurrection or revolt

Examples

The government cracked down on insurrectional activities in the city.
Several leaders were arrested for their role in the insurrectional movement.
His writings were considered dangerously insurrectional by the authorities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rebellious - showing a desire to resist authority or control
  • Synonym: subversive - seeking to undermine established authority

Etymology

From insurrection (from Latin insurrectio, a rising up) + -al (forming adjectives).
