Damon

1. PROPN

/ ˈdeɪ mən / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ mən / (BrE)
A legendary friend of Phintias in Greek mythology, known for his loyalty and self-sacrifice; often referenced as a symbol of true friendship.
legendary friend in Greek myth, model of loyalty

Examples

The story of Damon and Pythias is often cited as a classic example of true friendship.
In literature, Damon has come to symbolize selfless loyalty.
Many admire the trust between Damon and Phintias in ancient Greek legend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pythias - Legendary friend of Damon in Greek myth, known for mutual loyalty

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Δάμων (Dámōn), a legendary figure symbolizing friendship.
