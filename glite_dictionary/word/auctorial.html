auctorial

1. ADJ

/ ɔkˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / ɔːkˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of an author; written by or typical of an author.
of or relating to an author

Examples

The novel contains several auctorial interventions that reveal the writer's personal views.
Critics often debate the value of auctorial perspective in narrative fiction.
Her auctorial style can be seen in the vivid descriptions throughout the book.

Synset Examples

authorial comments
auctorial flights of imagination

Related Terms

  • Synonym: authorial - of or relating to an author

Etymology

From Latin 'auctor' (author) plus English '-ial.'
