well-chosen

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɛlˈtʃoʊzən / (AmE), / ˌwɛlˈtʃəʊzən / (BrE)
appropriately selected or phrased; carefully picked to suit a particular purpose or effect
carefully and appropriately selected

Examples

Her well-chosen words comforted everyone at the meeting.
The well-chosen examples made the lecture easier to understand.
He always gives well-chosen advice in difficult situations.

Synset Examples

a happy turn of phrase
a few well-chosen words

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apt - appropriate or suitable in the circumstances
  • Antonym: ill-chosen - not carefully or suitably selected; inappropriate
