Marylander

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛr.ɪ.lænd.ər / (AmE), / ˈmɛː.rɪ.lənd.ə / (BrE)
a native or resident of the state of Maryland, USA.
person from Maryland, USA

Examples

As a proud Marylander, she always supports the local crab festivals.
Many Marylanders commute to Washington, D.C. for work each day.
He became a Marylander when he moved to Baltimore last summer.

Etymology

Formed from Maryland + -er suffix.
