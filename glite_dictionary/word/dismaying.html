dismaying

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈmeɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪsˈmeɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
causing a strong feeling of disappointment, distress, or consternation
causing dismay or distress

Examples

The sudden changes to the schedule were dismaying to the entire staff.
She found the lack of support from her friends deeply dismaying.
The refugee camp was in dismaying condition after the storm.

Synset Examples

appalling conditions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appalling - causing extreme dismay or horror
  • Antonym: comforting - making someone feel better or less worried

Etymology

Formed from the verb "dismay" + suffix "-ing".
