thirdly

1. ADV

/ ˈθɝrdli / (AmE), / ˈθɜːdli / (BrE)
used to introduce the third point or reason in a series of statements
in the third place; as a third point

Examples

Thirdly, we need to address the long-term impacts of the decision.
She listed her reasons: firstly, the cost; secondly, the time; and thirdly, the difficulty.
Thirdly, it is important to recognize everyone's contributions to the project.

Synset Examples

third we must consider unemployment

Etymology

From 'third' + '-ly'; patterned after 'firstly', 'secondly'.
