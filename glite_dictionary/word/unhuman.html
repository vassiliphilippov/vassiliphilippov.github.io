unhuman

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhjuːmən / (AmE), / ʌnˈhjuːmən / (BrE)
Lacking human qualities, attributes, or characteristics; not possessing humanity.
lacking human qualities

Examples

The villain's unhuman cruelty shocked everyone in the court.
Something about the creature's unhuman stare made me feel uneasy.
The dictator was remembered for his unhuman disregard for his people's suffering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inhuman - lacking human qualities of compassion or mercy

Etymology

From English prefix 'un-' (not) + 'human'.
