intimate

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪn.tə.mət / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tɪ.mət / (BrE)
(of a place or atmosphere) having a warm, friendly, or informal feeling that encourages closeness.
warm and friendly in atmosphere

Examples

The restaurant had an intimate atmosphere that made everyone feel at home.
We enjoyed an intimate gathering with a few close friends.
Their conversation took place in an intimate corner of the café.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cozy - (of a place) giving a feeling of comfort, warmth, and relaxation
  • Synonym: friendly - pleasant and kind in manner or atmosphere
  • Antonym: formal - following established rules or conventions; not casual or relaxed

Etymology

From Latin 'intimus' meaning 'innermost, deepest'.
