Centropristis

1. NOUN

/ sɛnˌtrɑːˈprɪstɪs / (AmE), / sɛnˌtrɒˈprɪstɪs / (BrE)
A genus of sea basses found in the western Atlantic Ocean, including the black sea bass.
a genus of sea basses

Examples

Several species in the Centropristis genus are important for commercial fishing along the Atlantic coast.
The black sea bass is perhaps the best-known member of Centropristis.
Researchers are studying the population dynamics of Centropristis to better manage sustainable fisheries.

Etymology

From Greek 'kentron' (spur) + 'pristis' (saw, shark), used in scientific naming.
