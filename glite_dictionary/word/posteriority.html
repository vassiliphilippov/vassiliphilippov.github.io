posteriority

1. NOUN

/ ˌpoʊstɪriˈɔrəti / (AmE), / ˌpɒstɪrɪˈɒrɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of being toward the back or rear, especially in anatomical position
state of being toward the rear

Examples

In anatomical diagrams, the posteriority of the organ is clearly labeled.
The posteriority of the wound made it difficult for the patient to notice at first.
Understanding posteriority is essential for interpreting body orientations correctly in medical contexts.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: anteriority - the quality of being toward the front or forward part

Etymology

From Latin posterior (coming after, later) + -ity.

2. NOUN

/ ˌpoʊstəriˈɔrəti / (AmE), / ˌpɒstəriˈɒrɪti / (BrE)
The state or quality of occurring or coming after in time; later occurrence.
the state of coming after in time

Examples

The posteriority of the second event made its impact even greater.
In legal discussions, the posteriority of certain rights can influence the outcome of cases.
His findings proved the posteriority of the written document compared to the oral agreement.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: priority - the state of coming before in time or importance

Etymology

From Latin posterioritas, from posterior (later).
