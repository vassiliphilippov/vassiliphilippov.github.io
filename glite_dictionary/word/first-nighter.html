first-nighter

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɝstˌnaɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɜːstˌnaɪtə / (BrE)
A person who regularly attends the opening night of theatrical productions.
person attending theater openings

Examples

As a dedicated first-nighter, she never missed the premiere of a major play in the city.
Many first-nighters are eager to share their reviews after a show's debut performance.
The lobby buzzed with excitement as the first-nighters arrived in their finest attire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theatergoer - a person who attends the theater
