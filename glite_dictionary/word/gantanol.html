Gantanol

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡæn.təˌnɔl / (AmE), / ˈɡæn.tə.nɒl / (BrE)
A trademarked sulfonamide antibiotic used to treat bacterial infections, particularly of the urinary tract.
trademarked sulfonamide antibiotic
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Gantanol to treat her urinary tract infection.
Some patients may experience allergic reactions to Gantanol.
Gantanol is less commonly used today due to the development of newer antibiotics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sulfamethoxazole - a sulfonamide antibiotic (often the generic name for Gantanol)
