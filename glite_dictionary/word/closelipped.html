closelipped

1. ADJ

/ ˈkloʊsˌlɪpt / (AmE), / ˈkləʊsˌlɪpt / (BrE)
unwilling to share information; inclined to keep matters secret or private
reluctant to divulge information

Examples

Despite repeated questions, he remained closelipped about his plans for the project.
Maria is usually very closelipped when it comes to her family matters.
The spokesperson was closelipped during the press briefing, revealing almost nothing new.

Synset Examples

although they knew her whereabouts her friends kept close about it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tight-lipped - reluctant to speak or reveal information
  • Synonym: secretive - inclined to conceal information or act in secrecy
  • Antonym: forthcoming - willing to divulge information freely
