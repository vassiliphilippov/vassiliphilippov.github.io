provokingly

1. ADV

/ prəˈvoʊkɪŋli / (AmE), / prəˈvəʊkɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner intended to provoke a reaction, often annoyance or anger
in a provoking or provocative way

Examples

She smiled provokingly and waited for a response.
He spoke provokingly, daring someone to contradict him.
The columnist provokingly challenged the common assumptions in her latest article.

Synset Examples

‘Try it,’ he said provocatively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: provocatively - in a provocative manner
