Schwann

1. PROPN

/ ʃvɑn / (AmE), / ʃvan / (BrE)
(Matthias Jakob Schwann) German physiologist and histologist known for identifying the cell as the basic structural unit of animal and plant tissue.
German physiologist and histologist

Examples

Schwann played a crucial role in developing the cell theory in biology.
Many biology textbooks refer to the discoveries made by Schwann and Schleiden.
The term 'Schwann cell' is named after Schwann in recognition of his contributions to neuroscience.
