cut-rate

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌtˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈkʌtˌreɪt / (BrE)
(of goods or services) offered at a price lower than the usual or standard rate.
offered at a reduced price

Examples

We decided to stay at a cut-rate hotel to save money on our trip.
The company is known for selling cut-rate electronics online.
They hired a cut-rate moving service and regretted it later.

Synset Examples

buying bargain-priced clothes for the children
cut-rate goods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discounted - offered for sale at a lower price than usual
  • Antonym: full-price - offered at the regular or standard price

Etymology

From 'cut' (to reduce) + 'rate' (price, amount), indicating a reduced price.
