Marseille

1. PROPN

/ mɑrˈseɪ / (AmE), / mɑːˈseɪ / (BrE)
a major port city in southeastern France, located on the Mediterranean Sea
port city in southeastern France

Examples

Many cruise ships stop in Marseille as part of their Mediterranean tours.
He grew up in Marseille before moving to Paris for work.
Marseille is famous for its vibrant port and historic old town.

Etymology

From French 'Marseille', from Latin 'Massilia', originally from Ancient Greek 'Massalia'.
