spaciotemporal

1. ADJ

/ ˌspeɪʃioʊˈtɛmpərəl / (AmE), / ˌspeɪsiəʊˈtɛmpərəl / (BrE)
existing or occurring in both space and time; relating to both spatial and temporal aspects
relating to space and time

Examples

The spaciotemporal patterns of animal migration were studied using satellite data.
Researchers analyzed the spaciotemporal distribution of disease outbreaks over the past decade.
The theory offers a new perspective on human perception by integrating spaciotemporal dimensions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spatial - relating to space
  • Synonym: temporal - relating to time

Etymology

From Latin 'spatium' (space) + Latin 'temporalis' (of time).
