starter

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑrtɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtə / (BrE)
An electric motor used to start the engine of a vehicle or machine.
motor that starts an engine

Examples

The car wouldn't start because the starter was faulty.
After replacing the starter, the engine turned over smoothly every time.
He learned how to install a new starter in his pickup truck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ignition - the system for starting the engine of a vehicle
  • Antonym: alternator - an electrical generator in a vehicle providing power
