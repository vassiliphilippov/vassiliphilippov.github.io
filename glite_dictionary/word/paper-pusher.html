paper-pusher

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpɚ ˌpʊʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə ˌpʊʃə / (BrE)
an office worker, especially a clerk or bureaucrat, whose job mainly involves handling paperwork rather than making important decisions
office worker focused on paperwork
informal

Examples

After years as a paper-pusher, Mark longed for a job with more creativity and responsibility.
Don't let bureaucracy turn you into just another paper-pusher shuffling forms all day.
She didn't want to be a paper-pusher her entire career, so she went back to school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bureaucrat - an official in a government or other administrative system, especially one seen as adhering to rules rigidly
  • Synonym: clerk - an office worker who handles routine administrative duties
