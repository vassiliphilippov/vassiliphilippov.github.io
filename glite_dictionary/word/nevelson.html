Nevelson

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɛvəlˌsən / (AmE), / ˈnɛv(ə)lsən / (BrE)
Louise Nevelson (1899–1988), Russian-born American sculptor known for her large, abstract wooden assemblages often painted in monochrome.
American sculptor of painted wood assemblages

Examples

The museum’s new exhibition features several works by Nevelson from her black-painted series.
Art historians often highlight Nevelson as a pioneer of modern sculpture in America.
Her abstract wooden sculptures reflect the signature style developed by Nevelson.
