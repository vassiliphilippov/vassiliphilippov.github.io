magniloquent

1. ADJ

/ mæɡˈnɪləkwənt / (AmE), / mæɡˈnɪləkwənt / (BrE)
using or marked by a lofty, grand, or pompous style of speaking or writing
lofty or pompous in style

Examples

The senator's magniloquent speech impressed some listeners, but others found it pretentious.
His writing often becomes magniloquent, filled with grand words and elaborate phrases.
Though magniloquent in tone, her letter failed to address the main issue.

Synset Examples

he engages in so much tall talk, one never really realizes what he is saying

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandiloquent - using lofty, pompous language or style

Etymology

From Latin 'magniloquus', from 'magnus' (great) + 'loqui' (to speak).
