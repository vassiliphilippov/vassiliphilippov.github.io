Archer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrtʃər / (AmE), / ˈɑːtʃə / (BrE)
(Astrology) a person born when the sun is in Sagittarius, traditionally from November 22 to December 21.
person born under Sagittarius (astrology)

Examples

Many Archers are said to have adventurous spirits and an optimistic outlook on life.
As an Archer, she often reads her daily Sagittarius horoscope.
My best friend is an Archer, born in early December.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sagittarian - a person born under the sign of Sagittarius

Etymology

From the constellation Sagittarius, symbolized as an archer in astrology and astronomy.

2. NOUN

/ ˈɑrt͡ʃər / (AmE), / ˈɑːtʃə / (BrE)
The ninth sign of the zodiac, also called Sagittarius, covering the period from about November 22 to December 21.
ninth sign of the zodiac (Sagittarius)

Examples

According to her horoscope, her sun sign is Archer, which makes her adventurous and optimistic.
People born under the Archer are said to love travel and freedom.
The Archer is associated with the element of fire in astrology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sagittarius - ninth sign of the zodiac; represented by the archer

Etymology

From Latin 'Sagittarius', symbolized by an archer.
