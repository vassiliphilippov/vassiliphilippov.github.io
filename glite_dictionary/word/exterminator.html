exterminator

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈstɜrmɪneɪtər / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɜːmɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to eliminate unwanted insects or animals, especially pests such as rodents and insects
person who eliminates pests professionally

Examples

The exterminator was called to deal with the ant infestation in the kitchen.
Many exterminators now use environmentally friendly chemicals to control pests.
After hearing noises in the attic, they hired an exterminator to check for rodents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pest control - management or regulation of a species defined as a pest
  • Degree Lower: pest - an unwanted insect or animal harmful to humans or crops

Etymology

From Latin exterminare (to drive out) + -or (agent suffix).
