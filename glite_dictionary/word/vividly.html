vividly

1. ADV

/ ˈvɪvɪdli / (AmE), / ˈvɪvɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that produces powerful feelings or clear, detailed images in the mind; intensely or strikingly.
in a clear, intense, or detailed manner

Examples

She remembers her childhood vividly, especially the summers spent by the sea.
The author vividly described the bustling streets of the city in her novel.
He recounted the accident vividly, so everyone could picture exactly what happened.

Synset Examples

he described his adventures vividly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graphically - In a detailed and clear manner

Etymology

From 'vivid' + '-ly', with 'vivid' from Latin 'vividus' (lively).
