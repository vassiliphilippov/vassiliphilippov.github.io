smartly

1. ADV

/ ˈsmɑrtli / (AmE), / ˈsmɑːtli / (BrE)
In a neat, stylish, and fashionable way.
in a neat and fashionable way

Examples

He arrived at the event smartly dressed in a tailored suit.
The guests were all smartly attired for the formal dinner.
She always manages to look smartly put together, no matter the occasion.

Synset Examples

He was smartly dressed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fashionably - in a way that follows current styles or trends
