bennie

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛni / (AmE), / ˈbɛni / (BrE)
A tablet of amphetamine, especially when used as a stimulant drug, often illegally.
slang for amphetamine tablet
slang

Examples

He was caught with a bottle of bennies in his backpack.
Back in the 1960s, college students took bennies to stay awake and study for exams.
She warned him about the dangers of abusing bennies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amphetamine - a central nervous system stimulant drug

Etymology

Shortened and altered from 'Benzedrine', a former trademark for amphetamine.
