inconsiderable

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnkənˈsɪdərəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnkənˈsɪdərəbl / (BrE)
Too small or unimportant to be worthy of attention; insignificant.
too small or unimportant

Examples

Despite their hard work, the team's overall impact was inconsiderable in the final results.
He donated an inconsiderable amount, but every little bit helps.
Her influence on the project was far from inconsiderable, shaping several key decisions.

Synset Examples

passed his life in an inconsiderable village
their duties were inconsiderable
had no inconsiderable influence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insignificant - too small or unimportant to be worth considering
  • Antonym: considerable - notably large in size, amount, or extent

Etymology

From Latin prefix 'in-' (not) + 'considerable' (worthy of consideration).
