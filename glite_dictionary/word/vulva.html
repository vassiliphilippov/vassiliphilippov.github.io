vulva

1. NOUN

/ ˈvʌl.və / (AmE), / ˈvʌl.və / (BrE)
the external parts of the female genital organs
external female genitalia
medical

Examples

The doctor explained how to identify different parts of the vulva during a routine check-up.
Some women experience irritation around the vulva due to certain soaps or fabrics.
Proper hygiene is important to maintain the health of the vulva.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vagina - the muscular tube leading from the external genitals to the cervix of the uterus in women and most female mammals

Etymology

From Latin vulva (womb, female genitalia).
