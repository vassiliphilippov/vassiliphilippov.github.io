saturnalia

1. NOUN

/ ˌsætərˈneɪliə / (AmE), / ˌsatəˈneɪlɪə / (BrE)
a period or occasion of unrestrained revelry, wild partying, or excessive indulgence, often involving drinking and promiscuity
wild, unrestrained party; revelry

Examples

The celebration quickly turned into a saturnalia of music, dancing, and laughter that lasted until dawn.
What started as a quiet gathering soon became a wild saturnalia of excess and abandon.
During the annual festival, the city was known for its week-long saturnalia of extravagance and revelry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bacchanal - wild and drunken celebration
  • Synonym: orgy - wild party especially with excessive drinking and sexual activity

Etymology

From Latin 'Saturnalia', the festival held in honor of the god Saturn, noted for feasting and licentiousness.
