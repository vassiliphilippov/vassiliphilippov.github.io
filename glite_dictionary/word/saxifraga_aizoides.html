Saxifraga aizoides

1. PROPN

/ ˌsæksəˈfreɪɡə aɪˈzoʊɪdiːz / (AmE), / ˌsæksɪˈfrɑːɡə aɪˈzəʊɪdiːz / (BrE)
A small tufted evergreen perennial plant with fringed leaves and yellow flowers, often spotted with orange, native to moist and rocky habitats.
a tufted evergreen plant with yellow flowers

Examples

The alpine garden features several clusters of Saxifraga aizoides in full bloom.
Researchers studied the habitat of Saxifraga aizoides along the rocky streambanks.
She carefully identified the yellow-flowered plant as Saxifraga aizoides during her hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saxifrage - a plant of the Saxifraga genus, generally with small flowers and often found in rocky ground

Etymology

From Latin: 'saxifraga' (a genus name meaning 'stone-breaker') and 'aizoides' (resembling Aizoon, another plant genus).
