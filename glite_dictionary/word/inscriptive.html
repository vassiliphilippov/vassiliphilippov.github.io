inscriptive

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈskrɪptɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈskrɪptɪv / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of an inscription; pertaining to written or engraved words on a surface
relating to an inscription

Examples

The inscriptive text on the monument was eroded by years of wind and rain.
Archaeologists carefully documented the inscriptive markings found on the ancient pottery.
Her research focuses on the inscriptive traditions of early Mediterranean civilizations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inscription - words written or engraved on a surface

Etymology

From inscription + -ive, patterned after Latin roots.
