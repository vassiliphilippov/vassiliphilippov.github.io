jellied

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɛlid / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛlid / (BrE)
transformed into or set like jelly; made into a firm or gelatinous state by cooling.
set or made into jelly

Examples

She served jellied cranberry sauce alongside the turkey.
The dish contained chunks of fruit suspended in a jellied dessert.
He enjoyed eating jellied eels when he visited London.

Synset Examples

in Georgia they serve congealed salads

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gelatinous - having a jelly-like consistency
  • Antonym: liquid - in a liquid state, not set or solidified

Etymology

From the verb 'jelly' (to make into jelly) + -ed.
