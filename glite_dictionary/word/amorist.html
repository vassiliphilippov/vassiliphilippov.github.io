amorist

1. NOUN

/ ˈæmərɪst / (AmE), / ˈamərɪst / (BrE)
a person who is dedicated to love or lovemaking, especially one who writes about love
person devoted to love or writing about love

Examples

The famous amorist spent his career writing novels about passionate affairs.
As an unapologetic amorist, she believed that love was the highest pursuit.
Many poets are called amorists for their focus on the themes of love and desire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: romantic - a person with a romantic outlook or who is involved in love
  • Antonym: cynic - a person who is skeptical about love or romantic ideals

Etymology

From Latin amor (love) + -ist (one who practices or is concerned with something).
