butterfly-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʌtərˌflaɪ ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəflaɪ ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape or outline of a butterfly.
having a butterfly-like shape

Examples

The thyroid gland is butterfly-shaped and sits at the base of the neck.
She wore a butterfly-shaped pendant around her neck.
A butterfly-shaped patch of flowers brightened the garden.
