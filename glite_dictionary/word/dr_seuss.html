Dr. Seuss

1. PROPN

/ ˌdɒktɚ ˈsuːs / (AmE), / ˌdɒktə ˈsuːs / (BrE)
The pen name of Theodor Seuss Geisel (1904–1991), a famous American author and illustrator of children's books.
American children's book author (Theodor Seuss Geisel)

Examples

Many children grew up reading stories by Dr. Seuss.
The whimsical illustrations in Dr. Seuss's books have charmed generations of young readers.
'The Cat in the Hat' is one of Dr. Seuss's most beloved works.

Etymology

Pen name of Theodor Seuss Geisel, using his middle name and the title 'Dr.' as an affectation.
