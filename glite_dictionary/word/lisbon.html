Lisbon

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪzbən / (AmE), / ˈlɪzbən / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Portugal, located on the Tagus River and known as the country's main economic and cultural center
capital city of Portugal

Examples

Many tourists visit Lisbon each year to experience its rich history and vibrant nightlife.
The Tagus River flows through Lisbon before emptying into the Atlantic Ocean.
After moving to Lisbon, she quickly fell in love with the city's scenic hills and architecture.

Etymology

From Portuguese Lisboa, possibly of pre-Roman origin.
