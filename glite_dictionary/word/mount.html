mount

1. NOUN

/ maʊnt / (AmE), / maʊnt / (BrE)
a backing or support added to strengthen or display an object, such as a photograph or piece of art
backing or support for strengthening

Examples

The photograph was placed on a sturdy mount to keep it flat.
He bought a special mount for his new painting to ensure it wouldn't bend.
The jeweler set the gemstone into a gold mount for display.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backing - material or support attached to the back of something
