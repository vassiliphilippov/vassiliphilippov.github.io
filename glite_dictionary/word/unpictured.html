unpictured

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpɪktʃɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɪktʃəd / (BrE)
not shown or represented in a picture or image.
not depicted in a picture

Examples

Many details in the novel remain unpictured in the film adaptation.
The artist described her inspiration for the unpictured scenes in the exhibition.
Several characters from the story are unpictured in the illustrated edition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unseen - not seen or visible
  • Antonym: depicted - shown or represented in a picture or image
