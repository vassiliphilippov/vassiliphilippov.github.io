leaper

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːpər / (AmE), / ˈliːpə / (BrE)
a person or animal that jumps or leaps, especially as part of a competition or sport
person or animal that leaps, often in competition

Examples

The crowd cheered as the leaper soared over the high bar.
Every skilled leaper needs strong legs and perfect timing.
In biology class, we studied the kangaroo as an extraordinary animal leaper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jumper - person or animal that jumps, especially in competition

Etymology

From 'leap' (verb) + '-er' (agent suffix).
