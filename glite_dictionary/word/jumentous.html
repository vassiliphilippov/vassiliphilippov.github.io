jumentous

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒuːməntəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːmɛntəs / (BrE)
Having a strong smell like that of a horse or other beast of burden.
smelling like a beast of burden

Examples

The doctor noticed a jumentous odor when examining the patient's urine.
An old stable often has a jumentous scent that lingers in the air.
The air was thick with a jumentous smell after the rain soaked the mule enclosure.

Synset Examples

jumentous urine

Etymology

From Latin 'jumentum' (beast of burden) + '-ous'.
