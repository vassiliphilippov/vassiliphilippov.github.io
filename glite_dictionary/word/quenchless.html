quenchless

1. ADJ

/ ˈkwɛntʃ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈkwɛntʃ.ləs / (BrE)
(literary) Impossible to quench; unable to be satisfied or extinguished.
impossible to quench or extinguish

Examples

She had a quenchless curiosity that drove her to explore every new idea.
The fire burned with a quenchless intensity, illuminating the night sky.
His quenchless thirst for adventure led him to travel the world.

Synset Examples

unquenchable thirst

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unquenchable - impossible to quench or satisfy
  • Synonym: insatiable - impossible to satisfy

Etymology

Formed from 'quench' (to extinguish) + suffix '-less' (without).
