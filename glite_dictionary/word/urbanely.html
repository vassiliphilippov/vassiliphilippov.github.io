urbanely

1. ADV

/ ɝːˈbeɪnli / (AmE), / ɜːˈbeɪnli / (BrE)
In a manner that is polite, refined, and confident; showing sophistication and courtesy.
in a refined, courteous manner

Examples

He spoke urbanely to the assembled guests, impressing everyone with his wit.
She handled the awkward question urbanely, maintaining her composure throughout the interview.
The diplomat responded urbanely, defusing the tension in the room.

Synset Examples

‘I had tea occasionally with the Duke,’ said Mr. Eggers urbanely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urbane - polite, refined, and confident in manner

Etymology

From 'urbane' (from Latin urbanus 'of the city') + '-ly'.
