enigmatical

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛnɪɡˈmætɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɛnɪɡˈmatɪkəl / (BrE)
Difficult to understand or interpret; mysterious or puzzling.
difficult to understand; mysterious

Examples

Her response was so enigmatical that nobody could tell what she really meant.
The old manuscript was full of enigmatical symbols that defied translation.
He regarded her with an enigmatical smile, revealing nothing of his true intentions.

Synset Examples

I didn't grasp the meaning of that enigmatic comment until much later
prophetic texts so enigmatic that their meaning has been disputed for centuries

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enigmatic - mysterious, difficult to interpret or understand
  • Antonym: clear - easy to understand or interpret

Etymology

From Greek 'ainigmatikos' (enigmatic), via Latin 'aenigmāticus'.
