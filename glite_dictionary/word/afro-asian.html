Afro-Asian

1. ADJ

/ ˌæfroʊˈeɪʒən / (AmE), / ˌafrəʊˈeɪʒən / (BrE)
Relating to Africa and Asia or their peoples.
relating to Africa and Asia

Examples

The conference was attended by several Afro-Asian leaders.
The Afro-Asian community is growing rapidly in major cities.
He studied the history of Afro-Asian relations in the 20th century.

Synset Examples

Afro-Asian population

Etymology

Formed from 'Afro-' (African) and 'Asian', modelled after similar compound adjectives.
