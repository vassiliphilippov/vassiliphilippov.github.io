FOTS

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛf oʊ ti ɛs / (AmE), / ˈɛf əʊ ti ɛs / (BrE)
an acronym for fiber optic transmission system, a communication system that uses fiber optic cables to transmit data.
fiber optic transmission system (acronym)

Examples

The new data center was equipped with a FOTS for high-speed communication.
Maintaining the FOTS is crucial for uninterrupted network service.
Their engineering team specializes in designing advanced FOTS for telecommunication networks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fiber optic cable - a cable made of fibers that transmit data as light signals

Etymology

Acronym from Fiber Optic Transmission System.
