scarecrowish

1. ADJ

/ ˈskɛrˌkroʊ.ɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈskeə.krəʊ.ɪʃ / (BrE)
Resembling a scarecrow, especially in being very thin and dressed in old or ragged clothes.
resembling a scarecrow in appearance

Examples

He wore a scarecrowish coat that hung loosely on his frail frame.
The scarecrowish boy shuffled down the street in threadbare clothes.
With her scarecrowish figure and wild hair, she drew curious glances from passersby.

Synset Examples

a forlorn scarecrowish figure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scarecrow - an object made to resemble a human figure, set up to scare birds away from crops

Etymology

Formed from 'scarecrow' + suffix '-ish'.
