anhelation

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.həˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌæn.hɪˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A breathless or gasping quality, especially as heard in someone's voice or breathing.
breathless or gasping quality

Examples

The anhelation in her voice made everyone worry about her health.
During the call, his anhelation was evident, as if he had run upstairs quickly.
There was an unmistakable anhelation whenever she tried to speak after her illness.

Synset Examples

Etymology

From Latin anhelatio (panting, gasping), from anhelare (to pant).
