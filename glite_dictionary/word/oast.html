oast

1. NOUN

/ oʊst / (AmE), / əʊst / (BrE)
A kiln used for drying hops as part of the brewing process.
kiln for drying hops

Examples

The old farm still has its original oast where hops were once dried for brewing.
Tourists visited the restored oast to learn about traditional beer production.
The distinctive cone-shaped roof of the oast is common in Kent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kiln - a furnace or oven for firing, burning, or drying

Etymology

From Old English *āst*, meaning 'kiln' or 'oven'.
