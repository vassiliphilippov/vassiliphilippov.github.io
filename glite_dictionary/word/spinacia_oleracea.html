Spinacia oleracea

1. NOUN

/ spɪˈneɪsiə oʊˈlɛɹiˌeɪtə / (AmE), / spɪˈneɪsiə ɒlˈɛriːətə / (BrE)
The scientific name for spinach, a cultivated plant with succulent edible dark green leaves.
scientific name for spinach plant

Examples

Spinacia oleracea is commonly grown in home gardens for its edible leaves.
The scientific classification for spinach is Spinacia oleracea.
Researchers studied the nutritional value of Spinacia oleracea compared to other leafy greens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spinach - An edible leafy green plant often used in salads and cooking

Etymology

From Latin: *Spinacia* (spinach) and *oleracea* (vegetable/herb-like).
