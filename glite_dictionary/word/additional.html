additional

1. ADJ

/ əˈdɪʃənəl / (AmE), / əˈdɪʃnəl / (BrE)
extra; more than what already exists or has been mentioned.
extra; added

Examples

We requested additional information to complete the report.
Due to heavy demand, additional seats were made available for the event.
Please let me know if you require additional assistance.

Synset Examples

called for additional troops
need extra help
an extra pair of shoes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extra - more than is usual or needed; additional

Etymology

From Latin 'additio' (an adding), from 'addere' (to add) + English suffix '-al'.
