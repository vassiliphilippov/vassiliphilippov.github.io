Nike

1. PROPN

/ ˈnaɪki / (AmE), / ˈnaɪki / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) the winged goddess of victory, identified with the Roman goddess Victoria.
Greek goddess of victory

Examples

In ancient art, Nike was often depicted with wings, symbolizing swift victory.
The statue of Nike at the temple inspired many warriors before battle.
Many sculptures of Nike can be found in museums across the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Victoria - Roman goddess of victory, counterpart to Nike

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Νίκη (Níkē), meaning 'victory'.
