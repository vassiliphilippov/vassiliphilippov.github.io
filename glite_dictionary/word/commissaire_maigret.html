Commissaire Maigret

1. PROPN

/ kəˌmisaːr maɪˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / kəˌmisaː maɪˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
a fictional French detective created by Georges Simenon, featured in numerous novels and stories
fictional French detective by Simenon

Examples

Many fans of detective fiction consider Commissaire Maigret one of the most realistic and complex characters in the genre.
Georges Simenon's novels featuring Commissaire Maigret have been adapted for television many times.
In each case, Commissaire Maigret relies on intuition and empathy to catch the criminal.

Etymology

French, from 'commissaire' (commissioner) and 'Maigret', the character's surname; created by Belgian author Georges Simenon.
