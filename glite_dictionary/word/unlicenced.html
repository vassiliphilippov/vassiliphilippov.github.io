unlicenced

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlaɪsənst / (AmE), / ʌnˈlaɪsənst / (BrE)
Not having official approval or permission, especially from a government or legal authority.
lacking official approval or permission
british

Examples

The restaurant was shut down because it was unlicenced to serve alcohol.
He was caught driving an unlicenced vehicle late at night.
Authorities warned the public about unlicenced street vendors operating in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unlicensed - not having official approval; standard or American spelling of 'unlicenced'

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'licenced', chiefly a British spelling of 'licensed'.
