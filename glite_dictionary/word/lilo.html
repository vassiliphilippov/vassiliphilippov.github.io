lilo

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪləʊ / (BrE)
an inflatable plastic mattress used for lying on, especially on water or when sunbathing
inflatable air mattress (for water or sunbathing)
british

Examples

She lounged on a lilo in the pool all afternoon.
He inflated his lilo and floated down the river.
The hotel provided each guest with a colorful lilo for the beach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air mattress - a mattress that is filled with air and used for sleeping or lounging

Etymology

Originally a trademark, from the German company name 'Lilo' (from Liege, 'couch' + Luft, 'air').
