a genus of Spanish mackerels (fish)

Examples

The Scomberomorus are prized by sport fishermen for their speed and strength.

Several species within Scomberomorus are important to commercial fisheries.

Related Terms

From Latin *scomber* (mackerel) + Greek *moros* (fool, possibly by folk etymology).
