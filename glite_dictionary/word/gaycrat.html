gaycrat

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪkræt / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪkræt / (BrE)
An activist or prominent figure in the LGBTQ+ community, often involved in advocacy and political work.
LGBTQ+ activist or advocate

Examples

As a respected gaycrat, he played a major role in the marriage equality campaign.
Several prominent gaycrats attended the protest march for trans rights.
The magazine featured an interview with a well-known gaycrat discussing future advocacy strategies.

Synset Examples

She’s known as the gaycrat for her activism.

Etymology

Blend of "gay" and the suffix "-crat" (from Greek -krates meaning 'ruler' or 'advocate').
