Helvetia

1. PROPN

/ hɛlˈviːʃə / (AmE), / hɛlˈviːtɪə / (BrE)
the Latin name for Switzerland, often used poetically or on coins and stamps
Latin name for Switzerland

Examples

The obverse of the coin proudly displayed the name Helvetia.
Some classical texts refer to Switzerland as Helvetia.
On many Swiss stamps, you can find the inscription Helvetia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Switzerland - a country in central Europe

Etymology

From Latin 'Helvetia', named after the Helvetii, a Celtic tribe inhabiting the Swiss region in ancient times.
