enjoyableness

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈd͡ʒɔɪəblnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈd͡ʒɔɪəblnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being enjoyable or giving pleasure.
the quality of being enjoyable

Examples

The enjoyableness of the concert was enhanced by the lively audience.
She commented on the enjoyableness of her job after the recent changes.
The enjoyableness of the book made it hard to put down.

Synset Examples

the enjoyableness of an afternoon at the beach

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enjoyable - giving pleasure or satisfaction

Etymology

Formed from 'enjoyable' + '-ness', a common noun-forming suffix in English.
