jocularity

1. NOUN

/ dʒɑː.kjuˈlær.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌdʒɒk.jʊˈlær.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the state or quality of being humorous or playful; cheerful joking behavior
good-humored joking or playfulness

Examples

Her jocularity lightened the mood during the tense staff meeting.
Despite the stressful situation, he maintained a sense of jocularity.
Their jocularity at the dinner table made everyone feel at ease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humor - the quality of being amusing or entertaining
  • Antonym: seriousness - the quality of being solemn or earnest; lack of humor

Etymology

From Latin 'joculāritās', from 'joculāri' (to joke).
