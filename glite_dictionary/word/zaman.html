zaman

1. NOUN

/ zəˈmɑn / (AmE), / zəˈmæn / (BrE)
A large, ornamental tropical American tree with bipinnate leaves, spherical clusters of crimson-stamened flowers, and seed pods eaten by cattle.
tropical American ornamental tree

Examples

The zaman tree is renowned for its striking crimson flowers and valuable shade in tropical gardens.
Farmers often let their cattle feed on the seed pods of the zaman during the dry season.
Beneath the spreading branches of a grand zaman, children gathered to play and escape the midday sun.
