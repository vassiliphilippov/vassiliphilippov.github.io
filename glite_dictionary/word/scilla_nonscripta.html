Scilla nonscripta

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlə nɒnˈskrɪptə / (AmE), / ˈsɪlə nɒnˈskrɪptə / (BrE)
a species of bluebell native to western Europe, with bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers; sometimes placed in the genus Scilla
a species of bluebell plant

Examples

The woodland floor was covered with Scilla nonscripta in full bloom.
Botanists debated whether Scilla nonscripta should be classified in the Hyacinthoides or Scilla genus.
Every spring, tourists visit to photograph the spectacular displays of Scilla nonscripta.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bluebell - common English name for Scilla nonscripta, a woodland plant with blue bell-shaped flowers
  • Synonym: Hyacinthoides non-scripta - alternative scientific name for the common bluebell

Etymology

From Latin: 'Scilla' (a genus of bulbous plants) and 'nonscripta' (meaning 'not written'), referring to its distinction from the hyacinth described by classical authors.
