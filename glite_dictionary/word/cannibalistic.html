cannibalistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkænəbəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌkænɪbəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of cannibals or the practice of eating the flesh of one's own species
relating to cannibalism

Examples

The tribe was once accused of cannibalistic rituals by early explorers.
Scientists observed cannibalistic tendencies among some species of spiders.
His business strategies were so aggressive that they were described as cannibalistic by rivals.

Synset Examples

cannibalistic behavior

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cannibal - a person or animal that eats the flesh of its own species
  • Antonym: herbivorous - describing animals that eat plants
  • Synonym: predatory - relating to animals preying upon others, for food

Etymology

From 'cannibal' (from Spanish 'caníbal', from Taíno 'Caníbal') + '-istic' suffix.
