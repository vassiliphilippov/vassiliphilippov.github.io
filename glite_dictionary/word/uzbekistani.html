Uzbekistani

1. ADJ

/ ˌʊzˌbɛkɪˈstɑni / (AmE), / ˌʊzbɛkɪˈstɑːni / (BrE)
Relating to Uzbekistan, its people, or its culture.
of Uzbekistan or its people

Examples

The Uzbekistani economy has been growing rapidly in recent years.
She enjoyed the Uzbekistani cuisine during her visit to Tashkent.
Many Uzbekistani traditions are celebrated across Central Asia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Uzbek - relating to the Uzbek people, language, or ethnicity

Etymology

From Uzbekistan + -i (adjectival suffix for nationalities).
