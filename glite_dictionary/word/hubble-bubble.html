hubble-bubble

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌb.əl ˌbʌb.əl / (AmE), / ˈhʌb.əl ˌbʌb.əl / (BrE)
an oriental tobacco pipe with a long flexible tube, in which smoke is passed through water before being inhaled; also known as a hookah or narghile
water pipe for smoking tobacco (hookah)

Examples

After dinner, they gathered around the hubble-bubble to enjoy flavored tobacco and conversation.
He purchased a traditional hubble-bubble from the market during his travels in the Middle East.
The café offered customers a chance to try a hubble-bubble with various fruit flavors.

Synset Examples

a bipolar world with the hookah and Turkish coffee versus hamburgers and Coca Cola

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hookah - tobacco pipe with water chamber, also called a water pipe
  • Synonym: narghile - water pipe for smoking, especially in Middle Eastern countries

Etymology

Imitative of the bubbling sound made by water in the device.
