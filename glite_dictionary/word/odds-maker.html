odds-maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑdzˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈɒdzˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who sets the betting odds for the outcome of a contest, especially in gambling or sports events
person who sets betting odds

Examples

The odds-maker adjusted the line after receiving new information about the team's injuries.
As a professional odds-maker, she analyzes every aspect of a horse race to set fair odds.
Many bettors rely on the expertise of the odds-maker before deciding how to place their bets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bookmaker - person who takes bets and pays out winnings
