nonintellectual

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɪntɪˈlɛktjʊəl / (BrE)
Not relating to or involving intelligence, reasoning, or mental effort; lacking intellectual qualities.
not involving mental effort

Examples

He preferred nonintellectual activities, like playing sports, over reading books or attending lectures.
The movie offers nonintellectual entertainment, focusing more on action than thought-provoking themes.
Some people find manual work more satisfying than nonintellectual discussions.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intellectual - relating to intellect or mental effort

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'non-' to 'intellectual.'
