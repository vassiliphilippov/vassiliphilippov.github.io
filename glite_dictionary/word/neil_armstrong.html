Neil Armstrong

1. PROPN

/ niːl ˈɑrmˌstrɔŋ / (AmE), / niːl ˈɑːmstrɒŋ / (BrE)
(1930–2012) An American astronaut who was the first person to walk on the Moon on July 20, 1969.
first person on the Moon

Examples

Neil Armstrong became a global icon after setting foot on the lunar surface in 1969.
As a child, I dreamed of becoming an astronaut like Neil Armstrong.
Many history books include a photograph of Neil Armstrong taking his first step on the Moon.
