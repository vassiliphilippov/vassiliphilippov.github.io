pedagogic

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɛdəˈɡɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɛdəˈɡɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to teaching, education, or the methods and principles of instruction.
relating to education or teaching

Examples

The university introduced pedagogic reforms to improve student engagement and learning outcomes.
Researchers examined the pedagogic strategies employed in different classroom settings.
Her approach was praised for its pedagogic value and clear instructional design.

Synset Examples

pedagogical significance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: educational - relating to education
  • Synonym: didactic - intended to teach, especially morally

Etymology

From French 'pédagogique', from Greek 'paidagōgikos', from 'paidagōgos' (teacher of children).
