Rumansh

1. ADJ

/ ruːˈmænʃ / (AmE), / ruːˈmanʃ / (BrE)
of or relating to the Romansh language, a Romance language spoken in parts of Switzerland
relating to the Romansh language

Examples

The Rumansh language has official status in Switzerland.
She is studying several Rumansh dialects for her dissertation.
A Rumansh translation of the document was provided for local residents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Romansh - the Romance language spoken in parts of Switzerland

Etymology

From Romansh *rumantsch*, meaning 'Roman(ic)', referring to the Latin origins of the language.

2. NOUN

/ ruːˈmænʃ / (AmE), / ruːˈmanʃ / (BrE)
a Rhaeto-Romance language spoken in southeastern Switzerland, and one of the official languages of Switzerland
a Swiss Rhaeto-Romance language

Examples

She learned to speak Rumansh after moving to the Swiss canton of Grisons.
The Swiss government promotes the preservation of Rumansh as a national language.
Many street signs in southeastern Switzerland are written in both German and Rumansh.

Etymology

From Romansh *rumantsch*, from Latin *Romanice* (in the Roman manner/language).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06980848-n
Synset POS: n

3. NOUN

/ ruːˈmænʃ / (AmE), / ruːˈmænʃ / (BrE)
the Rhaeto-Romance language spoken in southeastern Switzerland, which is one of the official languages of Switzerland
official Rhaeto-Romance language of Switzerland

Examples

A minority of people in Switzerland speak Rumansh as their first language.
She decided to learn Rumansh to communicate with locals in the Grisons region.
Documents are available in German, French, Italian, and Rumansh in Switzerland.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Romansh - alternative spelling of Rumansh, official Swiss language

Etymology

From Romansch 'rumantsch', related to Latin 'Romanicus' (Roman).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06980848-n
Synset POS: n
