berried

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛrid / (AmE), / ˈbɛrid / (BrE)
(of a plant) producing or bearing berries.
producing or bearing berries (botanical)

Examples

The berried shrubs attracted plenty of birds in the autumn.
This region is famous for its berried hedgerows in the summer months.
Gardeners often plant berried species to support local wildlife.

Etymology

From 'berry' + '-ed' (adjectival suffix).
