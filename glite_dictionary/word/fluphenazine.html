fluphenazine

1. NOUN

/ fluːˈfɛnəˌzin / (AmE), / fluːˈfɛnəsiːn / (BrE)
a powerful antipsychotic drug used to treat schizophrenia and other psychotic disorders
antipsychotic medication
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed fluphenazine to help manage his symptoms of schizophrenia.
Some patients experience side effects when taking fluphenazine for an extended period.
Fluphenazine is administered as either an oral medication or a long-acting injection.
