Piemonte

1. PROPN

/ piˈɛmˌoʊnteɪ / (AmE), / piˈɛmˌɒnteɪ / (BrE)
a region in northwestern Italy, known for its mountains, wines, and the Po valley
region in northwestern Italy

Examples

The rolling vineyards of Piemonte produce some of Italy’s finest wines.
Many tourists visit Piemonte for its rich cuisine and historic towns.
The Alps form a dramatic backdrop to the region of Piemonte.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Piedmont - alternative English name for Piemonte, region of northwestern Italy

Etymology

From Italian Piemonte, meaning 'foot of the mountain', from Latin 'ad pedem montium'.
