talkativeness

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔkətɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˈtɔːkətɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of talking a lot; the tendency to speak frequently or at length
the quality of talking a lot

Examples

Her talkativeness made her popular at parties, where she could always start a conversation with anyone.
Excessive talkativeness can be distracting during meetings when focus is required.
Despite his usual talkativeness, he was unusually quiet during the interview.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loquacity - the quality of being very talkative; loquaciousness
  • Antonym: reticence - the quality of being reserved or reluctant to speak

Etymology

From 'talkative' + noun suffix '-ness'.
