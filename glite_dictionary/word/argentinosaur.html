argentinosaur

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑrdʒənˈtɪnsəˌsɔr / (AmE), / ˌɑːdʒənˈtɪnsəˌsɔː / (BrE)
a huge herbivorous dinosaur of the Cretaceous period, discovered in Argentina
a large Cretaceous herbivorous dinosaur from Argentina

Examples

The fossils of an argentinosaur were discovered in Argentina over a decade ago.
Scientists believe the argentinosaur could have weighed over 70 tons.
Unlike carnivorous dinosaurs, the argentinosaur was strictly a plant-eater.

Etymology

From Latin 'Argentinus' (of Argentina) + Greek 'sauros' (lizard).
