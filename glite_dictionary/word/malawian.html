Malawian

1. ADJ

/ məˈlɑːwiən / (AmE), / məˈlɑːwiən / (BrE)
relating to Malawi or its people, language, or culture.
of or relating to Malawi

Examples

The new restaurant features Malawian cuisine and traditional music.
She enjoyed learning about Malawian customs during her travels.
Several Malawian artists participated in the international art fair.

Synset Examples

Malawian hills
Malawian soldiers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Malawi - a country in southeastern Africa

Etymology

From the country name Malawi + English suffix -an.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03094303-a
Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ məˈlɑːwiən / (AmE), / məˈlɑːwiən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Malawi
person from Malawi

Examples

Her best friend is a proud Malawian who often shares stories of her homeland.
The Malawian greeted visitors warmly and introduced them to local traditions.
Many Malawians speak both English and their native languages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Malawi - a country in southeastern Africa

Etymology

From Malawi, the country's name, + -an (denoting origin or affiliation).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09741155-n
Synset POS: n
