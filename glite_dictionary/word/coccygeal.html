coccygeal

1. ADJ

/ kɑkˈsidʒiəl / (AmE), / kɒkˈsiːdʒiəl / (BrE)
relating to or near the coccyx (tailbone)
relating to the coccyx
medical

Examples

The coccygeal vertebrae form the lower end of the spine.
After the fall, she complained of severe coccygeal pain.
The doctor identified an injury to the coccygeal region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coccyx - the small triangular bone at the base of the spinal column

Etymology

From Latin 'coccyx', from Ancient Greek 'kokkux' (cuckoo; the coccyx was thought to resemble a cuckoo's beak), with the English adjective-forming suffix '-eal'.
