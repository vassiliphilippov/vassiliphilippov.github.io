earthy

1. ADJ

/ ˈɜːrθi / (AmE), / ˈɜːθi / (BrE)
down-to-earth, sensible, and practical in manner or style; unpretentious and straightforward.
sensible and practical; unpretentious

Examples

Her earthy humor and practical advice made her popular with everyone.
The documentary has an earthy charm that appeals to everyday people.
Despite his fame, he remains earthy and approachable in interviews.

Synset Examples

has a straightforward down-to-earth approach to a problem
her earthy common sense

Related Terms

  • Synonym: practical - concerned with actual use or practice
  • Synonym: down-to-earth - practical and realistic, unpretentious
  • Antonym: pretentious - attempting to impress by affecting greater importance

Etymology

From Old English eorthe (earth) + -y (adjective suffix), originally meaning 'of earth'.

crude or indecent (humor, language)

Examples

(of a person or their behavior) direct, hearty, and uninhibited; showing robust enjoyment of physical pleasures and life.

Examples
