unlaced

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈleɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈleɪst / (BrE)
(of footwear or clothing) with the laces not tied or fastened.
with laces not tied

Examples

He tripped over his unlaced boots while hurrying out the door.
The children left their unlaced sneakers by the entrance after playing outside.
Her unlaced corset hung loosely around her waist.

Synset Examples

teenagers slopping around in unlaced sneakers

Related Terms

  • Antonym: laced - with laces tied or fastened

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈleɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈleɪst / (BrE)
(of a person's manner or behavior) not restrained; frank, informal, or lacking in conventional decorum.
not restrained in manner or behavior

Examples

His unlaced attitude made everyone feel comfortable at the party.
The comedian is known for her unlaced sense of humor, often ignoring social conventions.
During the interview, he spoke with an unlaced honesty that surprised the panel.

Synset Examples

this unbuttoned and disrespectful age
unlaced behavior in the neighborhood pub

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unbuttoned - open or informal in manner; not constrained by convention
  • Antonym: decorous - proper, restrained, and in good taste in manners or conduct

Etymology

From the verb 'unlace' (to loosen or undo laces), later used figuratively for relaxed or informal conduct.
