unperturbed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.pərˈtɜrbd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.pəˈtɜːbd / (BrE)
not worried, upset, or disturbed; calm and unconcerned despite possible problems or pressure.
not worried or disturbed

Examples

Despite the chaos around him, he remained unperturbed and focused on his work.
She appeared unperturbed by the news of the sudden schedule change.
Even when criticized, he stayed unperturbed and answered calmly.

Synset Examples

the waiters were unflurried and good natured
with contented mind and unruffled spirit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness, anger, or other strong emotions
  • Antonym: disturbed - feeling worried, anxious, or troubled

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'perturbed', from Latin perturbare (to disturb).
