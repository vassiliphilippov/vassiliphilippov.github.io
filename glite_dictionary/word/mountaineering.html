mountaineering

1. NOUN

/ ˌmaʊn.təˈnɪr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌmaʊn.tɪˈnɪə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport or activity of climbing mountains, especially as a form of recreation or adventure.
the sport of mountain climbing

Examples

She has devoted her life to mountaineering and has climbed some of the world's tallest peaks.
Modern mountaineering requires excellent physical fitness and technical skill.
They spent several weeks in the Alps practicing mountaineering before attempting their final ascent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rock climbing - the sport of climbing rock faces

Etymology

From 'mountain' + '-eering' (modelled after 'engineering'), first used in the early 19th century.
