mara

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːrə / (AmE), / ˈmɑːrə / (BrE)
a large, hare-like rodent native to the Pampas of Argentina, known for its long legs and swift running
hare-like rodent of Argentina

Examples

The mara is well adapted to the open grasslands of South America.
I saw a mara at the wildlife sanctuary during my trip to Argentina.
Unlike rabbits, the mara has long limbs that help it run swiftly from predators.

Etymology

From Spanish 'mara', the local name in Argentina.
