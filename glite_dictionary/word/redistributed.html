redistributed

1. ADJ

/ ˌriː.dɪˈstrɪ.bjuː.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌriː.dɪˈstrɪ.bjuː.tɪd / (BrE)
(of population or industries) relocated or spread more evenly from urban centers to outlying areas.
relocated from cities to outlying areas

Examples

In the 1970s, several countries adopted policies that led to redistributed industries across the countryside.
After the new infrastructure projects, the redistributed population no longer depended solely on urban centers.
The government promoted redistributed settlements to prevent overcrowding in major cities.

Synset Examples

redistributed industries

Related Terms

  • Antonym: concentrated - closely gathered or focused in one area
  • Synonym: dispersed - spread out over a wide area

Etymology

From re- (again) + distribute, based on Latin roots.
