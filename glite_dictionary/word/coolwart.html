coolwart

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːlwɔrt / (AmE), / ˈkuːlwɑːt / (BrE)
a stoloniferous woodland plant that produces white flowers in spring
woodland plant with white spring flowers

Examples

We found a cluster of coolwart growing along the forest path in early April.
The coolwart spreads quickly thanks to its underground stolons.
In spring, the white blooms of the coolwart brighten the shaded woodland floor.
