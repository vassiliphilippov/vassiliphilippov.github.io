small-mindedly

1. ADV

/ smɔlˈmaɪndɪdli / (AmE), / smɔːlˈmaɪndɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a narrow or petty outlook; not willing to accept new ideas or differences.
in a narrow or petty manner

Examples

She criticized the proposal small-mindedly, refusing to consider its benefits to the community.
The policy was enforced small-mindedly, ignoring any possible exceptions or individual needs.
They rejected the suggestion small-mindedly, unwilling to change their traditional ways.

Synset Examples

narrow-mindedly, the authorities closed down the cafe where teenagers used to hang out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: narrow-mindedly - in a way that shows intolerance of new ideas
  • Antonym: open-mindedly - in a way that is receptive to new ideas
