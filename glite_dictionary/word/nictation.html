nictation

1. NOUN

/ nɪkˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / nɪkˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the reflex action of rapidly closing and opening the eyes; blinking
the act of blinking
medical

Examples

The sudden nictation of her eyelids indicated she was surprised.
A strong gust of wind caused an involuntary nictation.
Prolonged staring can reduce the natural rate of nictation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blink - to shut and open the eyes quickly; to wink

Etymology

From Latin 'nictatio', from 'nictare' (to wink).
