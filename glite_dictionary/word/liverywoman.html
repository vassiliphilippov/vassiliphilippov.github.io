liverywoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvəriˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈlɪvəriˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who works in or is employed by a livery stable, caring for horses and related tasks.
female worker at a livery stable

Examples

As a dedicated liverywoman, she arrived at the stable before dawn to prepare the horses for the day's rides.
The new liverywoman quickly earned respect for her skill in handling difficult stallions.
Stories about a pioneering liverywoman from the 1800s inspired many young women to pursue work with horses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liveryman - male or unspecified worker at a livery stable
