Trimox

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraɪˌmɑks / (AmE), / ˈtrɪmɒks / (BrE)
A brand name for amoxicillin, a semisynthetic oral penicillin antibiotic used to treat various bacterial infections.
brand name for an antibiotic (amoxicillin)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Trimox to help clear up my sinus infection.
If you are allergic to penicillin, you should not take Trimox.
Some patients may experience mild side effects when starting Trimox.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amoxicillin - a semisynthetic penicillin antibiotic
  • Degree Lower: penicillin - a group of antibiotics derived from Penicillium fungi
