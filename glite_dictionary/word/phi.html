phi

1. NOUN

/ faɪ / (AmE), / faɪ / (BrE)
the 21st letter of the Greek alphabet (Φ, φ), often used as a symbol in mathematics and science
21st Greek letter; math/science symbol

Examples

The symbol phi is often used to represent the golden ratio in mathematics.
He wrote the Greek letter phi on the board to denote an angle.
Many fraternities and sororities use phi in their names.

Etymology

From Greek φῖ (phī), the name of the Greek letter.
