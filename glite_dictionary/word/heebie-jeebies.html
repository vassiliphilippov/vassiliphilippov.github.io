heebie-jeebies

1. NOUN

/ ˌhibiˈdʒibiːz / (AmE), / ˌhiːbiːˈdʒiːbiːz / (BrE)
a strong feeling of nervousness, anxiety, or unease; jitters
strong nervousness or unease
informal

Examples

Before her big speech, Jane got the heebie-jeebies and couldn't stop pacing.
That horror movie gave me the heebie-jeebies for days.
He always gets the heebie-jeebies before flying on an airplane.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jitters - a feeling of nervousness or anxiety
  • Synonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry or unease
  • Antonym: calmness - the state of being calm and relaxed

Etymology

Originating in early 20th century American English, possibly coined by cartoonist Billy DeBeck.
