gainlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡeɪnləsli / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪnləsli / (BrE)
In a way that brings no gain or profit; without benefit or success.
without gain or profit

Examples

He worked gainlessly for years, never seeing any financial return.
The business operated gainlessly until it eventually closed down.
They toiled gainlessly on the project, which ultimately brought no success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: profitlessly - in a way that brings no profit
  • Synonym: fruitlessly - in a way that fails to produce the desired result

Etymology

From 'gainless' + '-ly'.
