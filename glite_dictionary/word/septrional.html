septrional

1. ADJ

/ sɛpˈtraɪ.ən.əl / (AmE), / sɛpˈtrɪ.ə.nəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of northern regions; northern (archaic)
archaic: northern, of the north
archaic

Examples

The ancient text described the harsh septrional winds that battered the coastline.
In septrional lands, the days grow long in summer and short in winter.
Explorers from southern Europe often ventured into septrional regions in search of new trade routes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: northern - relating to or situated in the north

Etymology

From Latin 'septentrionalis', from 'septentriones' (the seven stars of Ursa Major, the Great Bear, associated with the north).
