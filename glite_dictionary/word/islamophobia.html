Islamophobia

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪs.ləˈmoʊ.fi.ə / (AmE), / ˌɪs.ləˈməʊ.fi.ə / (BrE)
Dislike of or prejudice against Islam or Muslims, especially as a political force.
prejudice against Islam or Muslims

Examples

The rise of Islamophobia has been documented by several international organizations.
Debates about Islamophobia often arise in discussions of immigration policy.
She spoke out against Islamophobia in her community.

Synset Examples

Muslim intellectuals are afraid of growing Islamophobia in the West

Related Terms

  • Synonym: xenophobia - dislike or prejudice against people from other countries

Etymology

From Islam + -phobia (fear or dislike). Modelled after similar terms indicating prejudice.
