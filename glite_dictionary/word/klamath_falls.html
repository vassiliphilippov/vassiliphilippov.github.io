Klamath Falls

1. PROPN

/ ˈklæməθ fɔlz / (AmE), / ˈklæməθ fɔːlz / (BrE)
A town in southern Oregon, USA, near the California border.
town in southern Oregon, USA

Examples

Klamath Falls experienced unusually heavy snowfall last winter, making travel difficult for residents.
They drove from Portland to Klamath Falls to visit Crater Lake National Park nearby.
Many birdwatchers visit Klamath Falls each year for its renowned wildlife refuges.

Etymology

Named after the Klamath people and the falls on the Link River.
