Clarence Darrow

1. PROPN

/ ˈklɛrəns ˈdæroʊ / (AmE), / ˈklærəns ˈdærəʊ / (BrE)
(1857–1938) United States lawyer renowned for his defense of unpopular or hopeless cases.
famous American defense lawyer (1857–1938)

Examples

During the trial, Clarence Darrow eloquently defended his client against overwhelming odds.
Many legal scholars still study the strategies used by Clarence Darrow in his most famous cases.
The novel features a character inspired by Clarence Darrow and his relentless pursuit of justice.
