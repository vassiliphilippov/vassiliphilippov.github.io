overzealous

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈzɛləs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈzɛləs / (BrE)
excessively enthusiastic or fervent, often to the point of being unreasonable or intrusive
excessively enthusiastic or passionate

Examples

The overzealous security guard checked everyone's bags twice at the entrance.
Some overzealous fans camped outside the stadium days before the concert began.
His overzealous cleaning habits annoyed his roommates, who preferred a more relaxed approach.

Synset Examples

rabid isolationist

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: zealous - showing great energy or enthusiasm in pursuit of a cause or objective
  • Degree Higher: fanatical - filled with excessive and single-minded zeal

Etymology

From 'over-' (excessive) + 'zealous' (from Medieval Latin 'zelosus', full of zeal)
