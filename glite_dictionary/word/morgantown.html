Morgantown

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɔrɡənˌtaʊn / (AmE), / ˈmɔːɡənˌtaʊn / (BrE)
A city in northern West Virginia, USA, on the Monongahela River near the Pennsylvania border; home to West Virginia University.
city in West Virginia, USA

Examples

I attended a conference in Morgantown last summer.
The football game between West Virginia University and their rivals brought thousands to Morgantown.
Many students move to Morgantown each year to study at the university.
