versed

1. ADJ

/ vɝst / (AmE), / vɜːst / (BrE)
having knowledge of or experience with something through study or practice; knowledgeable or skilled in a particular area
knowledgeable or experienced in a subject

Examples

She is versed in several foreign languages, making her an asset to the company.
He became versed in ancient history after years of dedicated study.
Not everyone is versed in the technical aspects of computer science.

Synset Examples

this girl, so intimate with nature
knowledgeable about the technique of painting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knowledgeable - having or showing knowledge, especially through experience or education
  • Antonym: unfamiliar - not having knowledge of or experience with something

Etymology

From Latin 'versatus' (turned about, experienced), past participle of 'versari'.
