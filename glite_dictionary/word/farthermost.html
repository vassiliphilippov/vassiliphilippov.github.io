farthermost

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɑrðərˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðəməʊst / (BrE)
Most distant in space, time, or order; furthest away.
most distant; furthest away

Examples

The research team set up camp at the farthermost edge of the glacier.
She discovered an old letter hidden in the farthermost drawer of the cabinet.
We could see the lighthouse on the farthermost tip of the island.

Synset Examples

had traveled to the farthest frontier
don't go beyond the farthermost (or furthermost) tree
explored the furthest reaches of space
the utmost tip of the peninsula

Related Terms

  • Synonym: furthest - most distant in space or time
  • Synonym: farthest - most distant in space or time

Etymology

Formed from 'farther' (variant of 'further') + '-most'.
