Canada

1. PROPN

/ ˈkænədə / (AmE), / ˈkanədə / (BrE)
a country in northern North America, extending from the Atlantic to the Pacific and northward into the Arctic Ocean; the second largest country in the world by total area
country in northern North America

Examples

She moved to Canada to pursue her studies at a top university.
The winters in Canada can be extremely cold and snowy.
Canada is known for its beautiful national parks and multicultural cities.

Synset Examples

the border between the United States and Canada is the longest unguarded border in the world

Related Terms

  • Antonym: United States - country south of Canada in North America

Etymology

From St. Lawrence Iroquoian *kanata* (village or settlement); adopted by Jacques Cartier in the 16th century.
