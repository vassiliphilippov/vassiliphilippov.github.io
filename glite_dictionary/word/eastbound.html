eastbound

1. ADJ

/ ˈiːstˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈiːstˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
(of a vehicle, route, or movement) going or heading toward the east.
going toward the east

Examples

The eastbound train will depart from platform 2 at 3:45 pm.
Heavy traffic was reported on the eastbound lanes of the highway this morning.
We caught an eastbound bus that took us straight into the city center.

Synset Examples

eastbound trains

Related Terms

  • Antonym: westbound - going toward the west

Etymology

Formed from 'east' + 'bound' (meaning headed in a direction).
