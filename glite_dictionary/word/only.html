only

1. ADV

/ ˈoʊnli / (AmE), / ˈəʊnli / (BrE)
as a result or in the final outcome; used to indicate that something is the sole consequence of an action or situation.
as a result; in the final outcome

Examples

Eating junk food will only harm your health in the long run.
She studied hard, only to find the exam was postponed.
Telling him the truth will only make things worse.

Synset Examples

These news will only make you more upset

Related Terms

  • Synonym: just - to a degree that is fair or right; only or simply
