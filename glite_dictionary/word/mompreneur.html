mompreneur

1. NOUN

/ mɑm.prəˈnɜr / (AmE), / mɒm.prəˈnɜː / (BrE)
A mother who runs her own business, often balancing entrepreneurship with parenting responsibilities.
mother who is an entrepreneur
informal american

Examples

As a successful mompreneur, she finds creative ways to juggle family and business life.
Many mompreneurs use social media to promote their products and connect with customers.
The panel discussion featured several mompreneurs sharing their experiences of starting companies while raising children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entrepreneur - a person who starts and operates a business

Etymology

Blend of 'mom' and 'entrepreneur'.
