hoser

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊzər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊzə / (BrE)
a derogatory slang term for a person regarded as foolish or unreliable, originally chiefly Canadian
derogatory slang for a foolish or unreliable person
slang

Examples

Everyone laughed when Dave called his brother a hoser during their game of hockey.
Don't pay attention to him—he's just being a hoser again.
Missing the meeting twice in a row really made her look like a hoser.

Synset Examples

He’s such a hoser for not showing up again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loser - a person who is habitually unsuccessful or disappointing

Etymology

Canadian English slang, popularized in the 1980s by the television show SCTV.
