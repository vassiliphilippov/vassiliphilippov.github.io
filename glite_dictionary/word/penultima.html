penultima

1. NOUN

/ pəˈnʌltɪmə / (AmE), / pəˈnʌltɪmə / (BrE)
the next to last syllable in a word, counting from the end
second-to-last syllable in a word

Examples

In the word 'banana', the penultima is 'na'.
Many Spanish words put the stress on the penultima.
When pronouncing Latin, pay attention to whether the stress falls on the penultima.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ultima - the last syllable of a word
  • Antonym: antepenult - the third-to-last syllable in a word

Etymology

From Latin 'paenultima' (feminine of 'paenultimus', 'almost last').
