Keuka Lake

1. PROPN

/ ˈkjuːkə leɪk / (AmE), / ˈkjuːkə leɪk / (BrE)
a glacial lake in central New York State, notable for its Y-shaped form, and one of the Finger Lakes
a Y-shaped Finger Lake in central New York

Examples

We spent a weekend at Keuka Lake enjoying the beautiful scenery and local wineries.
The unique Y-shape of Keuka Lake sets it apart from the other Finger Lakes.
Boating and fishing are popular pastimes on Keuka Lake during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Finger Lakes - a group of 11 long, narrow lakes in central New York State

Etymology

From a likely Iroquoian origin, possibly meaning 'canoe landing'.
