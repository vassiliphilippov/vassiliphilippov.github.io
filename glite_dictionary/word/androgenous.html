androgenous

1. ADJ

/ ænˈdroʊdʒənəs / (AmE), / ænˈdrɒdʒənəs / (BrE)
Relating to or resulting from androgenesis, a type of reproduction involving only male genetic material.
relating to androgenesis (male-only reproduction)
medical

Examples

Researchers studied the androgenous development of certain plant species under laboratory conditions.
The process was classified as androgenous because it involved only paternal DNA contribution.
Some insects exhibit androgenous reproduction in rare circumstances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: androgenesis - reproduction involving only male genetic material

Etymology

Formed from 'androgenesis', from Greek 'andr-' (man) + 'genesis' (origin, creation).
