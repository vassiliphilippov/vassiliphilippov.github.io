shitting

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of defecating; eliminating fecal waste through the anus (vulgar slang).
the act of defecating (vulgar)
vulgar

Examples

He was late because he was shitting when I called.
Public shitting is considered both unhygienic and illegal in most cities.
There is nothing more urgent than the need for shitting when you have a stomach bug.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defecation - the act of discharging feces from the body
  • Antonym: urination - the act of discharging urine from the body

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'shit' + -ing; 'shit' from Old English *scītan* (to defecate).
