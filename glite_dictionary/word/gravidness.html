gravidness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrævɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡravɪdnəs / (BrE)
The condition or state of being gravid; pregnancy, especially in a medical or scientific context.
the state of being pregnant
medical

Examples

The veterinarian examined the snake and confirmed its gravidness.
Research on lizards focused on the physiological changes during gravidness.
Her gravidness was evident as she approached the later stages of gestation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pregnancy - the state of carrying a developing embryo or fetus

Etymology

From Latin 'gravidus' (pregnant), from 'gravare' (to weigh down).
