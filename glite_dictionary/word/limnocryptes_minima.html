Limnocryptes minima

1. NOUN

/ lɪm.nəˈkrɪptiz ˈmɪnɪmə / (AmE), / lɪm.nəˈkrɪptiːz ˈmɪnɪmə / (BrE)
a small, short-billed snipe of Eurasia, also known as the Jack Snipe, found in marshes and wetlands
a small Eurasian snipe (Jack Snipe)

Examples

During migration, the Limnocryptes minima can be spotted in European wetlands.
Birdwatchers were excited to find a rare Limnocryptes minima among other wading birds.
The Limnocryptes minima is noted for its cryptic plumage and elusive behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jack Snipe - common name for Limnocryptes minima, a small Eurasian snipe

Etymology

From Greek limne (marsh) + kryptes (hider); Latin minima (smallest).
