matchmaking

1. NOUN

/ ˈmætʃˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmatʃˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of arranging or facilitating a marriage or romantic relationship between other people
arranging marriages or relationships

Examples

Traditional matchmaking is still common in some cultures around the world.
Her aunt was known for her skill in matchmaking and had introduced several happy couples.
Many online dating services are modern forms of matchmaking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arranging marriages - the act of bringing about marriages, especially by negotiation or mediation
  • Synonym: matchmaker - a person who arranges marriages or romantic partnerships

Etymology

From 'match' (in the sense of marriage partner) + 'making'.
