oneiromancer

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈnaɪrəˌmænsər / (AmE), / əʊˈnaɪərəˌmænsə / (BrE)
a person who claims to foretell the future or gain insight by interpreting dreams
person who interprets dreams for divination

Examples

The ancient king consulted an oneiromancer to discover the meaning behind his strange vision.
Many cultures have traditions in which an oneiromancer plays a key role in advising leaders.
She claimed to be an oneiromancer, offering guidance based on people's dreams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soothsayer - person who predicts the future
  • Synonym: dream interpreter - one who interprets the meaning of dreams

Etymology

From Greek oneiros (dream) + mantis (prophet, diviner).
