unchained

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtʃeɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃeɪnd / (BrE)
not restrained or held by chains; free from physical or figurative bonds or restrictions.
not bound by chains; liberated

Examples

After years of captivity, the prisoner was finally unchained and allowed to go free.
Her unchained enthusiasm inspired the entire team to work harder.
The once unchained dog now sat quietly on the porch, enjoying its freedom.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: chained - restrained or bound by chains
  • Synonym: liberated - set free; released from confinement or oppression

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'chained' (from 'chain').
