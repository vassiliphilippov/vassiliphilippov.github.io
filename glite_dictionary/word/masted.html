masted

1. ADJ

/ ˈmæstɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstɪd / (BrE)
having or equipped with one or more masts, often used in combination to describe the number of masts on a vessel
having one or more masts

Examples

The museum displays a beautifully restored three-masted schooner.
During the regatta, several single-masted boats competed alongside larger vessels.
The harbor was filled with masted ships of every size and type.

Synset Examples

probably was so masted when she set forth
a three-masted bark

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mastless - without a mast
