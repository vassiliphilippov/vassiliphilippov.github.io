linguini

1. NOUN

/ lɪŋˈɡwini / (AmE), / lɪŋˈɡwiːni / (BrE)
A type of pasta in the form of long, narrow, flat strips.
long, flat pasta strips

Examples

I ordered a plate of linguini with clam sauce at the Italian restaurant.
The recipe calls for fresh linguini tossed in olive oil and garlic.
She prefers linguini over spaghetti because of its unique texture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spaghetti - long, thin, round pasta
  • Degree Higher: fettuccine - ribbon-like pasta, broader than linguini

Etymology

From Italian 'linguine', plural of 'linguina' (little tongue), from 'lingua' (tongue).
