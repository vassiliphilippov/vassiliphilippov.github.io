self-regard

1. NOUN

/ sɛlf ɹɪˈɡɑrd / (AmE), / sɛlf rɪˈɡɑːd / (BrE)
respect or esteem that a person has for themselves; self-respect.
respect or esteem for oneself

Examples

Her strong sense of self-regard helped her overcome criticism and self-doubt.
A lack of self-regard may lead to difficulties in relationships and mental health.
He maintained his self-regard despite facing unfair treatment at work.

Synset Examples

it was beneath his dignity to cheat
showed his true dignity when under pressure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-respect - pride and confidence in oneself; a feeling that one is behaving with honor and dignity
  • Synonym: self-esteem - confidence in one's own worth or abilities; self-respect

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'regard', meaning to look at or consider oneself with respect.
