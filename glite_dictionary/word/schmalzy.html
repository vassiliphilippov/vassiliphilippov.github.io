schmalzy

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃmɑlzi / (AmE), / ˈʃmɑːlzi / (BrE)
Excessively sentimental or emotional, especially in a way that is considered insincere or overdone.
excessively sentimental or emotional
informal

Examples

The movie was entertaining, but some critics thought its ending was far too schmalzy.
She rolled her eyes at the schmalzy love letters he wrote, finding them overly dramatic.
That holiday commercial was so schmalzy it almost made me cringe.

Synset Examples

a bathetic novel
maudlin expressions of sympathy
mushy effusiveness
a schmaltzy song
sentimental soap operas
slushy poetry

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sentimental - showing or expressing emotion, especially in an excessive or nostalgic way
  • Synonym: maudlin - self-pityingly or tearfully sentimental
  • Antonym: unsentimental - not marked by or given to sentimentality

Etymology

From Yiddish 'shmaltsik' (from 'shmalts' meaning 'fat, grease'; used figuratively for sentimentality in American slang).
