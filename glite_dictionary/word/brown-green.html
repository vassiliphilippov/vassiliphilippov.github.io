brown-green

1. ADJ

/ ˌbraʊnˈɡrin / (AmE), / ˌbraʊnˈɡriːn / (BrE)
Of a green color tinged with brown; a shade that combines green and brown.
green tinged with brown

Examples

The muddy water had a brown-green hue after the rainstorm.
She painted the forest using several brown-green shades to capture the undergrowth.
Their uniforms were a dull brown-green to blend in with the landscape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: olive - a dark yellowish-green color, often with brown undertones
