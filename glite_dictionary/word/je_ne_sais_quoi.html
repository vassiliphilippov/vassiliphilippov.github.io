je ne sais quoi

1. NOUN

/ ʒə nə seɪ ˈkwɑ / (AmE), / ʒə nə seɪ ˈkwɑ / (BrE)
a quality that cannot be easily described or named that makes something attractive or distinctive
an indescribable attractive quality

Examples

There is a certain je ne sais quoi about her style that sets her apart from other designers.
The old house has a je ne sais quoi that makes you feel instantly comfortable.
Though the recipe is simple, it has a je ne sais quoi that keeps people coming back for more.

Etymology

French, literally 'I do not know what'.
