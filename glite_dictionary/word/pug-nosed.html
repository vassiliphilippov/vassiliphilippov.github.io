pug-nosed

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʌɡˌnoʊzd / (AmE), / ˈpʌɡ.nəʊzd / (BrE)
Having a nose that is short, flat, and turned up at the end, resembling that of a pug.
having a short, flat nose

Examples

The pug-nosed detective always stood out in a crowd.
She drew a pug-nosed puppy with big eyes and floppy ears.
Many action figures have exaggerated, pug-nosed faces for a cartoonish effect.

Synset Examples

a pug-nosed boy with freckles
a snub-nosed automatic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snub-nosed - having a short and turned-up nose

Etymology

From 'pug' (referring to the dog breed known for its flat nose) + 'nosed'.

2. ADJ

/ pʌɡ ˈnoʊzd / (AmE), / pʌɡ ˈnəʊzd / (BrE)
having a short, flat, and rounded nose, like that of a pug dog.
having a short, flat nose

Examples

Her new puppy is adorably pug-nosed and playful.
The actor was known for his distinctive pug-nosed features.
A pug-nosed revolver was found at the crime scene.

Synset Examples

a pug-nosed boy with freckles
a snub-nosed automatic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snub-nosed - having a short, turned-up nose

Etymology

From 'pug' (referring to a breed of dog with a short, flat nose) + 'nosed'.
