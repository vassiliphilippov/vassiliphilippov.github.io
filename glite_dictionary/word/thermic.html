thermic

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɜrmɪk / (AmE), / ˈθɜːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or caused by heat.
relating to heat

Examples

The thermic properties of these materials make them suitable for insulation.
A thermic reaction releases energy in the form of heat.
Scientists measured the thermic effect of the new alloy under high temperatures.

Synset Examples

thermal movements of molecules
thermal capacity
thermic energy
the caloric effect of sunlight

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermal - relating to heat, especially as used in everyday and technical contexts

Etymology

From Greek thermē (heat), via French thermique.
