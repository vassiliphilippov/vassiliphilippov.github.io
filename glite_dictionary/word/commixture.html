commixture

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɪkstʃər / (AmE), / kəˈmɪkstʃə / (BrE)
the act or process of mixing different substances or elements together
the act of mixing together

Examples

The commixture of colors on the canvas gave the painting a unique vibrancy.
Successful alloys depend on the careful commixture of different metals.
Her perfume was a commixture of floral and spicy scents that lingered in the room.

Synset Examples

paste made by a mix of flour and water
the mixing of sound channels in the recording studio

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mixture - a combination of different things blended together

Etymology

From Latin 'commixtura', from 'com-' (together) and 'mixtura' (a mixture)
