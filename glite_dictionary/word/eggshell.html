eggshell

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛɡˌʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈɛɡʃɛl / (BrE)
the thin, hard outer covering of a bird's egg
the outer layer of a bird's egg

Examples

Be careful not to break the eggshell when handling the egg.
The eggshell was so thin it cracked in my hand.
Birds use calcium to build the eggshell around their developing chicks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shell - the hard outer covering of certain objects, such as eggs or nuts
