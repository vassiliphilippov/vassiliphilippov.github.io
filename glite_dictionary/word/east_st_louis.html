East St. Louis

1. PROPN

/ iːst seɪnt ˈluːɪs / (AmE), / iːst sənt ˈluːɪs / (BrE)
A city in southwest Illinois, United States, located on the east bank of the Mississippi River across from St. Louis, Missouri.
city in southwest Illinois, USA
