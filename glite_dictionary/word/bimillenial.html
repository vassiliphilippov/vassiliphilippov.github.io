bimillenial

1. ADJ

/ baɪ.mɪˈlɛni.əl / (AmE), / baɪ.mɪˈlɛni.əl / (BrE)
Relating to a period of two thousand years or a bimillennium.
of or relating to two thousand years

Examples

The bimillenial anniversary of the city was celebrated with great fanfare.
Historians debated the significance of the bimillenial event in shaping modern culture.
Archaeologists discovered artifacts dating back to the bimillenial period of the civilization.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: millennial - relating to a thousand years or a millennium

Etymology

From Latin prefix 'bi-' (two) + 'millennium' (a thousand years).
