plage

1. NOUN

/ plɑːʒ / (AmE), / plɑːʒ / (BrE)
a beach, especially one at a seaside resort in France or the Mediterranean
a beach at a seaside resort

Examples

The hotel offers stunning views of the plage and the Mediterranean Sea.
In summer, the plage is crowded with sunbathers and swimmers enjoying the warm weather.
We strolled down the plage at sunset, listening to the waves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beach - a pebbly or sandy shore, especially by the sea

Etymology

From French 'plage' (beach, shore).
