Arcadia

1. PROPN

/ ɑrˈkeɪdiə / (AmE), / ɑːˈkeɪdiə / (BrE)
A region and administrative department of Greece located in the central part of the Peloponnese peninsula.
region of Greece in central Peloponnese

Examples

We spent a summer traveling through Arcadia and admiring its mountainous landscape.
Many legends and stories in classical literature are set in Arcadia.
The natural beauty of Arcadia attracts tourists from around the world.

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Ἀρκαδία (Arkadía), the name of a region in Greece.
