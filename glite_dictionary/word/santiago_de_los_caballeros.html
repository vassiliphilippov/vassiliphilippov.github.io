Santiago de los Caballeros

1. PROPN

/ sænˈtjɑːɡoʊ deɪ loʊs kəˈbɑːlər oʊs / (AmE), / sanˈtjɑːɡəʊ deɪ lɒs kəˈbælər əʊs / (BrE)
a city in the northern Dominican Republic, known as the country's second-largest city and an important cultural and economic center
city in northern Dominican Republic

Examples

The vibrant city of Santiago de los Caballeros is a hub of business and culture in the Dominican Republic.
She visited Santiago de los Caballeros last year and was impressed by its historic architecture.
Many tourists explore Santiago de los Caballeros to experience local festivals and traditional cuisine.

Etymology

From Spanish, meaning 'Saint James of the Knights'.
