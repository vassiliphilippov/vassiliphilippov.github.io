thingummy

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɪŋəmi / (AmE), / ˈθɪŋəmi / (BrE)
used to refer to a person or thing whose name is forgotten, unknown, or not mentioned; a thingamajig or whatsit.
unspecified object or person
informal british

Examples

Can you hand me that thingummy on the shelf?
He lost the thingummy that attaches the two parts together.
She bought some new thingummies from the market, but she can't remember what they're called.

Synset Examples

she eased the ball-shaped doodad back into its socket
there may be some great new gizmo around the corner that you will want to use

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thingamajig - an object whose name one does not know or has forgotten
  • Synonym: whatchamacallit - a thing whose name is unknown or temporarily forgotten

Etymology

Variant of 'thingum' with the nonsensical ending '-my'.
