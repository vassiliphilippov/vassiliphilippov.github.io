Polska

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɔl.ska / (AmE), / ˈpɒl.ska / (BrE)
The official name of Poland in the Polish language; used to refer to the country in native or formal contexts.
official Polish name for Poland

Examples

Many Polish immigrants still refer to their homeland as Polska.
The official documents were stamped with the name Polska.
During the tournament, the crowd chanted 'Polska!' to cheer for the national team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Poland - Central European country, English name for Polska

Etymology

Polish 'Polska', from Proto-Slavic *polje (field), referring to 'land of the fields'.
