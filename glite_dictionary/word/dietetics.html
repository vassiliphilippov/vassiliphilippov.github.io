dietetics

1. NOUN

/ ˌdaɪəˈtɛtɪks / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈtɛtɪks / (BrE)
the scientific study of diet and nutrition, especially as it relates to health and food preparation
study of diet and nutrition

Examples

She decided to study dietetics in order to become a registered dietitian.
Recent advances in dietetics have improved our understanding of how diet affects overall health.
The university offers a master's program in dietetics and nutrition science.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutrition - the process of providing or obtaining the food necessary for health and growth

Etymology

From Greek 'diaita' (way of life, diet) + '-etics' (study of).
