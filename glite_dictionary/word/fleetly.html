fleetly

1. ADV

/ ˈfliːtli / (AmE), / ˈfliːtli / (BrE)
In a swift or rapid manner; quickly.
in a swift manner

Examples

The gazelle ran fleetly across the open plain to evade the lions.
She moved fleetly through the crowd, barely brushing past anyone.
The news spread fleetly throughout the small village.

Synset Examples

she moved swiftly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swiftly - in a quick or rapid manner
  • Antonym: slowly - in a slow manner

Etymology

From Middle English 'flete' (swift), from Old English 'flēotan' (to float, swim).
