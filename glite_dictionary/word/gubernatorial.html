gubernatorial

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡuːbərnəˈtɔːriəl / (AmE), / ˌɡuːbənəˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to a governor or the office of a governor, especially of a US state
relating to a governor

Examples

The results of the gubernatorial election were announced late last night.
She is running for gubernatorial office in her state.
Many candidates campaigned heavily in the weeks leading up to the gubernatorial primary.

Synset Examples

gubernatorial election

Related Terms

  • Synonym: presidential - relating to a president or presidency

Etymology

From Latin gubernator (governor), from gubernare (to govern).
