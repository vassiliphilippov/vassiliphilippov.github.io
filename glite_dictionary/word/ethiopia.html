Ethiopia

1. PROPN

/ ˌiːθiˈoʊpiə / (AmE), / ˌiːθiˈəʊpiə / (BrE)
A country in northeastern Africa, bordered by Eritrea, Djibouti, Somalia, Kenya, South Sudan, and Sudan; formerly known as Abyssinia.
country in northeastern Africa

Examples

The coffee plant is believed to have originated in Ethiopia.
Many people visit Ethiopia to explore its ancient historical sites.
Ethiopia is known for its rich cultural and religious traditions.

Etymology

From Greek 'Aithiopia', referring to the land of the burnt-faced people ('aitho' meaning 'I burn' and 'ops' meaning 'face').
