fursuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɜrˌsuːt / (AmE), / ˈfɜːˌsuːt / (BrE)
A full-body costume, often resembling an anthropomorphic animal, typically worn by members of the furry subculture.
full-body anthropomorphic animal costume

Examples

He spent months designing his fursuit for the upcoming furry convention.
The artist is famous for making high-quality fursuits for clients around the world.
Wearing a fursuit can be quite challenging in warm weather due to the thick materials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: costume - a set of clothes worn to look like someone or something else

Etymology

Blend of 'fur' and 'suit', emerging from furry fandom slang in the late 20th century.
