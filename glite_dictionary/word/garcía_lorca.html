García Lorca

1. PROPN

/ ɡɑrˌsiə ˈlɔrkə / (AmE), / ɡɑːˌsiːə ˈlɔːkə / (BrE)
(1898–1936) Spanish poet and playwright known for his influential works and his death at the start of the Spanish Civil War.
Spanish poet and playwright (1898–1936)

Examples

The poetry of García Lorca has influenced generations of writers worldwide.
Many theatre companies continue to stage the plays of García Lorca.
Scholars still debate the circumstances surrounding García Lorca's tragic death.

Etymology

From the full name Federico García Lorca, Spanish poet and dramatist.
