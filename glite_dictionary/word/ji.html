JI

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒeɪ ˈaɪ / (AmE), / ˌdʒeɪ ˈaɪ / (BrE)
a clandestine terrorist organization founded in Southeast Asia in 1993, associated with al-Qaeda and involved in attacks and militant activities in Indonesia, the Philippines, Malaysia, and Singapore
Southeast Asian terrorist organization acronym

Examples

Authorities increased security after the JI terrorist attacks in Bali.
Intelligence agencies monitor JI because of its suspected links to international terrorist groups.
Several members of JI were arrested during coordinated police operations in Southeast Asia.

Etymology

Acronym of Jemaah Islamiyah, from Indonesian meaning 'Islamic Congregation'.
