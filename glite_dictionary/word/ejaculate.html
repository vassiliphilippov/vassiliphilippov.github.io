ejaculate

1. VERB

/ ɪˈdʒækjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈdʒækjʊleɪt / (BrE)
(of a male or a male animal) to suddenly eject semen from the penis, typically during orgasm.
to eject semen suddenly
medical

Examples

Certain medical conditions may make it difficult or impossible for a man to ejaculate.
During sexual intercourse, the male typically ejaculates at the moment of orgasm.
Some animals are able to ejaculate multiple times within a short period during mating season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to emit - to send out or give off (something)
  • Antonym: to retain - to keep in possession or under control

Etymology

From Latin ejaculatus, past participle of ejaculari ‘to throw out’.

fluid containing sperm released during ejaculation
medical

Examples

The doctor collected a sample of ejaculate for testing.

Related Terms

say suddenly and loudly

She ejaculated a cry of joy when she saw the results.
