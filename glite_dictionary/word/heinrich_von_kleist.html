Heinrich von Kleist

1. PROPN

/ ˈhaɪn.rɪk fʌn klaɪst / (AmE), / ˈhaɪn.rɪç fɒn klaɪst / (BrE)
(1777–1811) A German dramatist and writer known for works exploring the conflict between reason and emotion.
German dramatist (1777–1811)

Examples

Heinrich von Kleist is remembered for his intense plays and novellas that explore human psychology.
