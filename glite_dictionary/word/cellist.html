cellist

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛlɪst / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛlɪst / (BrE)
A person who plays the cello, a musical instrument of the violin family.
person who plays the cello

Examples

The renowned cellist performed a solo piece by Bach at the concert.
She dreamed of becoming a professional cellist and practiced for hours each day.
The orchestra's principal cellist received a standing ovation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: violinist - person who plays the violin
  • Synonym: pianist - person who plays the piano

Etymology

From 'cello' (short for 'violoncello') + '-ist'.
