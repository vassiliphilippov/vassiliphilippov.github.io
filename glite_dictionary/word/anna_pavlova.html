Anna Pavlova

1. PROPN

/ ˈænə pɑːvˈloʊvə / (AmE), / ˈanə pavˈlɒvə / (BrE)
(1882–1931) A renowned Russian ballerina noted for her expressive style and her role in popularizing ballet worldwide.
famous Russian ballerina (1882–1931)

Examples

Anna Pavlova is remembered for her iconic performance in 'The Dying Swan.'
Many ballet dancers aspire to achieve the artistry and grace of Anna Pavlova.
A statue of Anna Pavlova stands outside the theater where she frequently performed.
