fishmonger

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃˌmɑŋɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌmʌŋɡə / (BrE)
a person who sells fish, especially as their occupation
person who sells fish

Examples

The old town has a fishmonger who arrives every morning with the freshest catch.
She bought salmon from the local fishmonger for tonight's dinner.
Every Saturday, the fishmonger sets up a stall at the market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fish seller - someone who sells fish

Etymology

From Middle English 'fisshmonger', 'fish' + 'monger' (dealer or trader).
