complaint

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpleɪnt / (AmE), / kəmˈpleɪnt / (BrE)
(Law) A formal statement made to a court or authority that someone has committed an offense or wrongdoing, especially the initial legal document that starts a criminal case.
formal legal accusation; initial pleading in a case
legal

Examples

The prosecutor filed a complaint against the defendant for fraud.
A police officer must investigate any criminal complaint submitted by a citizen.
The judge reviewed the complaint before deciding if the case could proceed.

Synset Examples

he was arrested on a charge of larceny

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charge - an official statement accusing someone of a crime

Etymology

From Old French 'complainte' (lamentation), from Latin 'complangere' (to lament).

2. NOUN

/ kəmˈpleɪnt / (AmE), / kəmˈpleɪnt / (BrE)
(Law) The first formal legal statement by a plaintiff stating the facts and legal reasons supporting a claim against a defendant.
initial legal statement by plaintiff
legal

Examples

The attorney filed a complaint against the company for breach of contract.
After receiving the complaint, the court scheduled a preliminary hearing next month.
The judge dismissed the complaint because it failed to state a valid legal claim.

Etymology

From Old French 'complaindre' (to lament, complain), from Latin 'complangere' (to bewail).

3. NOUN

/ kəmˈpleɪnt / (AmE), / kəmˈpleɪnt / (BrE)
(Archaic) A loud cry or repeated cries expressing pain, rage, or sorrow.
archaic: loud cry of pain or sorrow
archaic

Examples

As the storm raged, the complaint of the wounded could be heard across the field.
She uttered a complaint that echoed through the empty chamber, expressing her deep sorrow.
The forest was filled with the complaint of animals distressed by the approaching fire.

Etymology

From Old French 'complaindre' (to lament) and Latin 'complangere' (to bewail).

4. NOUN

/ kəmˈpleɪnt / (AmE), / kəmˈpleɪnt / (BrE)
a statement expressing dissatisfaction, annoyance, or a grievance about something
expression of dissatisfaction or grievance

Examples

She filed a formal complaint against her neighbor for playing loud music late at night.
His constant complaint about the food annoyed everyone at the table.
The company received several complaints about the defective product.

Related Terms
