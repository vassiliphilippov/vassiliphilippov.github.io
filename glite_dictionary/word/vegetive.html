vegetive

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɛdʒətɪv / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒətɪv / (BrE)
(of an activity or lifestyle) passive and monotonous, lacking in energy or variety.
relating to passive and monotonous activity

Examples

His vegetive existence left him feeling unfulfilled and disconnected from the world.
She grew tired of her vegetive routine and longed for adventure.
After months of unemployment, he realized he had settled into a vegetive lifestyle.

Synset Examples

a dull vegetative lifestyle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetative - relating to growth or passive existence; lacking activity

Etymology

Variant of 'vegetative', from Latin vegetativus (enlivening), influenced by 'vegetate'.
