mahlstick

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑlˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɑːlˌstɪk / (BrE)
A long, thin stick used by painters to support the hand holding the brush while painting.
painter's hand-support stick

Examples

The artist rested her hand on a mahlstick to steady her brushstrokes.
He carried a palette, brushes, and a mahlstick across the studio.
Using a mahlstick, the painter avoided smudging the wet oil paint.

Etymology

From Dutch 'maalstok', from 'malen' meaning 'to paint' and 'stok' meaning 'stick'.
