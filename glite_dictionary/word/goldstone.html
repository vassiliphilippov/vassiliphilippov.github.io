goldstone

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌstəʊn / (BrE)
A type of glass containing tiny sparkling metallic particles, used as a semiprecious stone for jewelry and ornamentation.
sparkling glass used as a gemstone

Examples

She chose a necklace with a goldstone pendant for its subtle shimmer.
The artisan crafted beads from goldstone to use in earrings and bracelets.
A piece of goldstone glinted among the other polished stones in the display case.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aventurine - a type of quartz characterized by its shimmering or glistening effect
