purplish-lilac

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɜːrplɪʃ ˈlaɪlæk / (AmE), / ˈpɜːplɪʃ ˈlɪlək / (BrE)
having the pale purple color of lilac with a slight tinge of purple
pale lilac tinged with purple

Examples

She chose a purplish-lilac paint for her bedroom walls to create a calming atmosphere.
The sunset cast a purplish-lilac hue over the distant mountains.
Her dress was a delicate purplish-lilac that stood out at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lavender - a pale purple color
  • Synonym: lilac - a pale purple color
