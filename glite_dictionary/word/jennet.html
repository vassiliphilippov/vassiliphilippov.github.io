jennet

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛnɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnɪt / (BrE)
A female donkey.
female donkey

Examples

The farmer led the jennet and her foal to the stable for the night.
A jennet can often be distinguished from a jack by her gentler temperament.
They bred their jennet with a prize jack to improve their stock.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: jack - a male donkey

Etymology

From Old French 'genet', from Spanish 'jineta' (female donkey or small Spanish horse).
