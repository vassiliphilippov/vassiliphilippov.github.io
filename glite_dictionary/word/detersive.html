detersive

1. ADJ

/ dəˈtɜrsɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːsɪv / (BrE)
having cleansing or purifying power, especially to clean wounds or surfaces
having cleansing power

Examples

The detersive properties of the soap made it ideal for cleaning greasy surfaces.
Doctors recommend a detersive solution to clean minor wounds and prevent infection.
This natural extract is valued for its detersive effect on skin impurities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleansing - having the ability to clean or purify
  • Synonym: detergent - a substance used for cleaning

Etymology

From Medieval Latin detersivus, from Latin detergere (to wipe off, cleanse).
