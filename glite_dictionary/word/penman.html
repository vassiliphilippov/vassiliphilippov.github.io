penman

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛn.mən / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.mən / (BrE)
(informal) A journalist or writer, especially a male one.
male journalist or writer (informal)
informal

Examples

The seasoned penman quickly crafted a compelling headline for the evening edition.
As a young penman, he often covered stories that other reporters overlooked.
His reputation as an honest penman earned him respect among his peers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: journalist - a person who writes for newspapers, magazines, or news websites
  • Synonym: writer - a person who writes books, articles, etc.

Etymology

From 'pen' (writing instrument) + 'man', indicating someone who writes.
