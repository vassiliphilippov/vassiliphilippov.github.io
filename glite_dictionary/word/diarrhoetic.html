diarrhoetic

1. ADJ

/ daɪˌɛr.iˈoʊ.tɪk / (AmE), / daɪˌæreɪˈəʊ.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by diarrhea.
relating to diarrhea
medical british

Examples

The patient exhibited several diarrhoetic symptoms after the meal.
Doctors prescribed medication to treat his diarrhoetic condition.
Continuous monitoring is required for children suffering from diarrhoetic illnesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diarrheal - relating to or affected by diarrhea (American spelling)
  • Antonym: constipated - unable to pass stools easily or frequently

Etymology

From diarrhea + -etic, influenced by the British spelling 'diarrhoea'.
