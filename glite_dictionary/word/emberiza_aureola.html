Emberiza aureola

1. NOUN

/ ɛm.bəˈraɪ.zə ɔːˈreɪ.ə.lə / (AmE), / ɛm.bəˈraɪ.zə ɔːˈreɪ.ə.lə / (BrE)
A species of bunting with a yellow breast, commonly found in Russia and Siberia; also known as the yellow-breasted bunting.
species of yellow-breasted bunting

Examples

The once-abundant Emberiza aureola is now classified as critically endangered due to habitat loss and trapping.
Birdwatchers traveled to eastern Russia in hopes of spotting the rare Emberiza aureola.
The plumage of Emberiza aureola includes a distinctive yellow breast that gives the bird its common name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellow-breasted bunting - common name for Emberiza aureola

Etymology

From the Latin genus name 'Emberiza' (bunting) and species 'aureola', meaning 'golden' or 'gilded'.
