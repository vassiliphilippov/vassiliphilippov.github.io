fierily

1. ADV

/ ˈfaɪəri.li / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə.rɪ.li / (BrE)
In a manner showing intense passion, enthusiasm, or emotion.
with intense passion or fervor

Examples

She spoke fierily about her beliefs during the debate.
The protesters marched fierily through the streets, demanding change.
He defended his ideas fierily, refusing to back down.

Synset Examples

both those for and against are fervently convinced they speak for the great majority of the people
a fierily opinionated book

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passionately - in a passionate manner
  • Antonym: calmly - in a calm manner

Etymology

From 'fiery' + adverbial suffix '-ly'; 'fiery' from Old English 'fȳr' (fire).
