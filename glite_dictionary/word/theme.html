theme

1. NOUN

/ θim / (AmE), / θiːm / (BrE)
an essay written by a student as a school assignment, often on a particular topic
essay written for a school assignment

Examples

The teacher collected all the students' themes at the end of class.
His theme on friendship was thoughtful and well-written.

Synset Examples
