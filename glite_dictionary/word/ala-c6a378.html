ala

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪlə / (AmE), / ˈeɪlə / (BrE)
(in biology and anatomy) a winglike structure or part, such as a flat extension on an organ or organism.
winglike part of an organism

Examples

The ala of the nose helps form the shape of the nostril.
Each ala on the maple seed aids in its dispersal by wind.
Botanists studied the ala of pea blossoms for their function in pollination.
