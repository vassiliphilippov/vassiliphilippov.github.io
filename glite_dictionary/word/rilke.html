Rilke

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɪlkə / (AmE), / ˈrɪlkə / (BrE)
(1875–1926) A German poet born in Austria, known for his vivid imagery and mystical lyricism, influential in 20th-century German literature.
German poet and literary figure

Examples

Many scholars consider Rilke one of the most important poets of the early twentieth century.
She drew inspiration from Rilke's poetry while writing her novel.
Collections of Rilke's letters reveal his philosophical outlook on art and life.
