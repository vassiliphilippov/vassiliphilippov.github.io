pacifically

1. ADV

/ pəˈsɪfɪkli / (AmE), / pəˈsɪfɪkli / (BrE)
In a peaceable or non-violent manner; in a way that seeks to promote peace.
in a peaceable manner

Examples

She spoke pacifically to resolve the heated argument among her colleagues.
The dispute was settled pacifically, with both sides reaching a mutual agreement.
He always tries to handle conflicts pacifically, avoiding harsh words or actions.

Synset Examples

the tenant paying the rent hereby reserved and performing the several covenants herein on his part contained shall peaceably hold and enjoy the demised premises

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peacefully - in a peaceful manner
  • Antonym: aggressively - in a forceful or hostile manner

Etymology

From Latin 'pacificus' (peace-making) + English adverbial suffix '-ly'.
