schmegegge

1. NOUN

/ ʃməˈɡɛɡə / (AmE), / ʃməˈɡɛɡə / (BrE)
Nonsense; empty talk; something meaningless or ridiculous, especially as used to dismiss someone's statements.
nonsense; empty or silly talk
informal american

Examples

Everything he said about his connections was just schmegegge.
Don't listen to that schmegegge—she's making it up as she goes along.
The rumors about layoffs turned out to be total schmegegge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baloney - foolish or deceptive talk; nonsense
  • Synonym: nonsense - words or ideas that are not true or sensible
  • Antonym: sense - something that is reasonable or makes sense

Etymology

From Yiddish שמעגעגאַ (shmegegge), meaning 'nonsense' or 'foolish person'.
