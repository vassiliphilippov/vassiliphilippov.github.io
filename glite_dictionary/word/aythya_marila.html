Aythya marila

1. NOUN

/ ˈæθiə məˈraɪlə / (AmE), / ˈaɪθɪə məˈriːlə / (BrE)
a large species of diving duck native to North America and northern Eurasia, with males having a greenish sheen on their heads, commonly known as the greater scaup
a species of diving duck (greater scaup)

Examples

The Aythya marila is often seen in coastal bays during migration season.
Male Aythya marila are recognizable by the greenish iridescence on their heads.
Birdwatchers spotted several Aythya marila swimming among other waterfowl.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greater scaup - common name for Aythya marila, a large diving duck

Etymology

From modern Latin, the scientific binomial for the greater scaup.
