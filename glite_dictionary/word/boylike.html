boylike

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɔɪˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪ.laɪk / (BrE)
having qualities or characteristics typical of a young boy; resembling a boy in appearance or behavior.
resembling or characteristic of a boy

Examples

Her boylike enthusiasm for adventure inspired everyone around her.
He wore his cap with a boylike tilt that made him look younger than he was.
There was a boylike mischief in his sparkling eyes.

Synset Examples

a boyish grin
schoolboyish pranks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boyish - having qualities characteristic of a boy
  • Antonym: girlish - having qualities characteristic of a girl

2. ADV

/ ˈbɔɪ.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪ.laɪk / (BrE)
In a way that resembles or is characteristic of a boy, especially suggesting youthfulness or innocence.
in the manner of a boy

Examples

He smiled boylike at his fiancée, his eyes full of mischief and innocence.
Her boylike enthusiasm for adventure was infectious among the group.
He ran boylike through the field, laughing as he dodged the falling leaves.

Synset Examples

he smiled boyishly at his fiancee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boyishly - in a boyish manner
  • Antonym: girlishly - in a girlish manner

Etymology

Formed from 'boy' + 'like', following the English pattern for adjectives and adverbs indicating resemblance.
