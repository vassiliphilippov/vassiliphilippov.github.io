H-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪtʃ ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈeɪtʃ ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape or form of the letter H.
in the shape of the letter H

Examples

The architect designed an H-shaped building to allow more natural light into each room.
Our garden path is H-shaped to separate the different flower beds.
The new wing created an H-shaped layout for the hospital, improving access to all departments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: L-shaped - having the shape of the letter L
  • Synonym: U-shaped - having the shape of the letter U
