fabulously

1. ADV

/ ˈfæbjələsli / (AmE), / ˈfæbjʊləsli / (BrE)
In an extremely impressive, remarkable, or exceptional manner; exceedingly.
extremely; remarkably

Examples

She decorated her apartment fabulously, with bold colors and unique furnishings.
The team performed fabulously in the championship game, winning by a wide margin.
He became fabulously wealthy after selling his company.

Synset Examples

she plays fabulously well
behind you the coastal hills plunge to the incredibly blue sea backed by the Turkish mountains

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incredibly - to a great degree; extremely
  • Synonym: extremely - to a very great degree

Etymology

From 'fabulous' + '-ly'; 'fabulous' from Latin 'fabula' (story, fable), originally 'of fables or myths.'
