to reuse

1. VERB

/ riˈjuz / (AmE), / riːˈjuːz / (BrE)
to use something again after it has been used, often after processing or cleaning
use again after processing

Examples

Many people choose to reuse glass jars for storing spices in their kitchens.
The company encourages employees to reuse paper whenever possible to reduce waste.
After washing the containers, she decided to reuse them for her homemade meals.

Synset Examples

We must recycle the cardboard boxes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to recycle - to process used materials to make them suitable for reuse
  • Antonym: discard - to throw something away

Etymology

From re- (again) + use (to employ for a purpose).
