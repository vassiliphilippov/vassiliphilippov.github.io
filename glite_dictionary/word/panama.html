Panama

1. PROPN

/ ˈpænəˌmɑ / (AmE), / ˈpænəmɑː / (BrE)
A country in Central America, on the Isthmus of Panama, which gained independence from Colombia in 1903.
country in Central America

Examples

Panama is famous for its canal, which connects the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans.
She visited Panama during her world tour of Central America.
The official language of Panama is Spanish.

2. NOUN

/ ˈpænəmɑ / (AmE), / ˈpænəmə / (BrE)
a light, stiff hat made of fine straw, with a flat crown and brim, traditionally made in Ecuador and typically worn in warm weather
a straw hat with a flat crown

Examples

He shaded his eyes with a stylish Panama during the summer cruise.
Her grandfather always wore a crisp white Panama to the garden party.
A classic Panama is both fashionable and practical in hot weather.

Etymology

Named after Panama, the country through which the hats were shipped to Europe and North America, though they are traditionally made in Ecuador.
