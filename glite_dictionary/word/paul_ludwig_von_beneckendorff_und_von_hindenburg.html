Paul Ludwig von Beneckendorff und von Hindenburg

1. PROPN

/ pawl ˈlʊd.wɪɡ fɔn bəˈnɛk.ən.dɔrf ʊnt fɔn ˈhɪndn̩bɜːrɡ / (AmE), / paʊl ˈlʊd.vɪç fɒn bəˈnɛk.əndɒf ʊnt fɒn ˈhɪndn̩bɜːɡ / (BrE)
(1847–1934) A German field marshal and politician who served as President of the Weimar Republic; appointed Adolf Hitler as Chancellor in 1933.
German field marshal and president of the Weimar Republic
