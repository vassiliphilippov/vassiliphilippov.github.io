repand

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈpænd / (AmE), / rɪˈpand / (BrE)
(of a leaf or other margin) having a slightly wavy or undulating edge.
having a slightly wavy edge (botany)

Examples

The repand margin of the leaf is a key characteristic of this species.
Many botanists note when a plant has repand edges as it helps with identification.
The holly's repand leaves distinguish it from other similar shrubs.

Etymology

From Latin 'repandus' meaning 'bent backwards, spread out'.
