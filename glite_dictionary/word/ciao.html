ciao

1. NOUN

/ t͡ʃaʊ / (AmE), / t͡ʃaʊ / (BrE)
used as an informal greeting or farewell, meaning 'hello' or 'goodbye', especially in Italian contexts
informal greeting or farewell
informal

Examples

She waved and said ciao as she left the café.
When he called, he started with a cheerful ciao and quickly caught up on the news.
It's common in Italy to greet friends with a casual ciao instead of a formal hello.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hello - an expression of greeting
  • Synonym: goodbye - an expression used when parting

Etymology

From Italian "ciao", originally from Venetian dialect "s-ciào" meaning 'slave' (as a formula of respect).
