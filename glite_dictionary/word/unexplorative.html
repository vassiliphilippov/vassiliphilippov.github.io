unexplorative

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪkˈsplɔːr.ə.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪkˈsplɔː.rə.tɪv / (BrE)
not tending or inclined to explore new ideas, places, or experiences.
not inclined to explore

Examples

The committee adopted an unexplorative approach, sticking to traditional methods without considering alternatives.
Her unexplorative attitude prevented her from discovering new opportunities during her travels.
Because the research remained unexplorative, it failed to provide any fresh insights into the problem.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: explorative - inclined to explore or investigate

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'explorative' (tending to explore).
