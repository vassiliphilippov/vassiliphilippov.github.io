Brachycome

1. NOUN

/ brəˈkaɪkoʊmi / (AmE), / brəˈkaɪkəmi / (BrE)
A genus of mainly Australian herbs with basal or alternate leaves and clusters of small daisy-like flower heads.
a genus of Australian herbs with daisy-like flowers

Examples

The Brachycome is popular in gardens for its delicate, long-lasting blooms.
Many species of Brachycome are native to Australia and thrive in well-drained soil.
She planted a row of Brachycome along the edge of her flower bed for added color.

Etymology

From Greek *brachys* (short) and *kome* (hair), referring to the plant's short pappus hairs.
