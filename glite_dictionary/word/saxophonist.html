saxophonist

1. NOUN

/ sækˈsəfəˌnɪst / (AmE), / sækˈsɒfənɪst / (BrE)
a person who plays the saxophone, especially as a professional musician
person who plays the saxophone

Examples

The renowned saxophonist performed an impressive solo that captivated the audience.
She has dreamed of becoming a saxophonist since she first heard jazz music as a child.
Several talented saxophonists competed for a spot in the orchestra.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musician - a person who plays a musical instrument or is musically talented
  • Degree Lower: trumpeter - a person who plays the trumpet
  • Degree Lower: clarinetist - a person who plays the clarinet

Etymology

Formed from 'saxophone' + '-ist' (denoting someone who practices or is concerned with something).
