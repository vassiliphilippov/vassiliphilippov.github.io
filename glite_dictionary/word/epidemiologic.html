epidemiologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛpɪˌdimioʊˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌiːpɪˌdiːmiəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study of the distribution and determinants of health-related states or events in specified populations (epidemiology)
relating to epidemiology
medical

Examples

The research team conducted an epidemiologic survey to determine the spread of the disease.
Recent epidemiologic evidence suggests a link between diet and cancer risk.
Many public health policies are driven by epidemiologic data.

Synset Examples

epidemiological studies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: epidemiological - relating to epidemiology (variant adjective)

Etymology

From epidemiology + -ic; epidemiology from Greek epi- (upon), dêmos (people), and -logia (study).
