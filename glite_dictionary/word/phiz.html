phiz

1. NOUN

/ fɪz / (AmE), / fɪz / (BrE)
(informal, British) a person's face.
informal British word for face
informal british

Examples

She had a big grin on her phiz as she walked into the room.
I barely recognized him with all that paint on his phiz.
He splashed cold water on his phiz to wake himself up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mug - informal term for a person's face
  • Synonym: kisser - slang for face or mouth
  • Synonym: face - the front part of the head, including eyes, nose, and mouth

Etymology

Shortened from 'physiognomy', from Greek phusiognōmia (appearance of nature).
