Phiz

1. PROPN

/ fɪz / (AmE), / fɪz / (BrE)
The pseudonym of Hablot Knight Browne (1815–1882), an English illustrator best known for illustrating several novels by Charles Dickens.
English illustrator, Dickens' collaborator

Examples

Many of Dickens's bestselling novels feature illustrations by Phiz.
The signature of Phiz appears in the corner of the original plates for 'David Copperfield.'
Art historians often discuss the unique style Phiz brought to Victorian book illustration.

Etymology

Pseudonym adopted by Hablot Knight Browne; possibly chosen for its playful sound and resemblance to 'physiognomy.'
