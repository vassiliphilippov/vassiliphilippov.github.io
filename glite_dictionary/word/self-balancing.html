self-balancing

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlfˈbælənsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈbælənsɪŋ / (BrE)
able to keep or restore balance automatically without external assistance.
maintaining balance automatically

Examples

The self-balancing scooter adjusts its wheels to keep you upright.
This robot uses a self-balancing mechanism to move smoothly over uneven ground.
Thanks to its self-balancing system, the vessel remains stable even in rough waters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-righting - able to return to an upright position on its own

Etymology

Formed from 'self' (referring to itself) + present participle of 'balance'.
