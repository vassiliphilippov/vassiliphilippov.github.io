unhygienic

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnhaɪˈdʒinɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnhaɪˈdʒiːnɪk / (BrE)
Not clean and likely to cause disease; lacking in hygiene.
not clean; likely to cause disease

Examples

The restaurant was shut down after inspectors found the kitchen to be unhygienic.
She refused to use the unhygienic public restroom on the train.
Storing raw meat and vegetables together is considered unhygienic and unsafe.

Synset Examples

pathetic dogs kept in small unhygienic cages

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hygienic - clean and unlikely to cause disease

Etymology

From un- (not) + hygienic, patterned after hygiene (from French hygiène, from Greek hygieinē).
