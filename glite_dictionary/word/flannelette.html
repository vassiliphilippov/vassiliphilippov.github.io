flannelette

1. NOUN

/ ˌflænəˈlɛt / (AmE), / ˌflanɪˈlɛt / (BrE)
A soft cotton fabric that is made to resemble flannel.
soft cotton fabric resembling flannel

Examples

I bought a set of flannelette sheets for the winter months.
Her pajamas were made from blue flannelette decorated with clouds and stars.
The flannelette fabric kept the baby comfortably warm through the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flannel - soft woven fabric, typically of wool or cotton

Etymology

Diminutive of 'flannel', from Welsh *gwlanen* (woolen cloth), with '-ette' suggesting imitation or resemblance.
