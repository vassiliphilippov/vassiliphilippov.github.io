undressed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdrɛst / (AmE), / ʌnˈdrɛst / (BrE)
having removed all or some clothing; not wearing clothes
without clothing; not dressed

Examples

She felt embarrassed when she realized she was undressed in front of the window.
The doctor asked him to get undressed before the examination.
After the party, he quickly undressed and got into bed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naked - without any clothes on
  • Antonym: dressed - wearing clothes

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdrɛst / (AmE), / ʌnˈdrɛst / (BrE)
(of lumber, stone, or hides) Not finished, processed, or prepared for use; in its natural or raw state.
not finished or processed (materials)

Examples

The builder specified undressed lumber for the rustic cabin project.
These undressed hides will need further treatment before they can be used for leather goods.
They used undressed granite for the garden wall to give it a natural look.

Synset Examples

undressed granite
undressed hides

Related Terms

  • Antonym: finished - having been prepared or processed for use
  • Synonym: raw - in a natural or unprocessed state
