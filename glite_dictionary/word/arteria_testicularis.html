arteria testicularis

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈtɪəriə tɛˌstɪkjʊˈlɛərɪs / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə tɛˌstɪkjʊˈlæːrɪs / (BrE)
the artery that branches from the aorta to supply blood to the testicles
artery supplying the testicles
medical

Examples

The arteria testicularis is essential for delivering oxygenated blood to the testes.
Damage to the arteria testicularis can lead to compromised testicular function.
During surgery, care must be taken to avoid injury to the arteria testicularis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: testicular artery - the English equivalent of 'arteria testicularis', an artery supplying the testicles

Etymology

From Latin 'arteria' (artery) and 'testicularis' (of the testicles).
