taskmistress

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæskˌmɪstrɪs / (AmE), / ˈtɑːskˌmɪstrɪs / (BrE)
a woman who imposes hard or demanding work on others; a female taskmaster
female who imposes demanding work

Examples

The new director is known as a taskmistress who pushes her team to excel.
Her reputation as a strict taskmistress made everyone nervous before the meeting.
Being a taskmistress doesn't mean she is unfair, but she expects everyone to meet deadlines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taskmaster - a person who imposes hard or demanding work on others

Etymology

Formed by adding the feminine suffix '-mistress' to 'taskmaster'.
