3. VERB

/ eɪs / (AmE), / eɪs / (BrE)
to score a point directly from a serve or shot that the opponent cannot touch or return, especially in tennis or similar sports.

Examples

She managed to ace her opponent in the final game with a powerful serve.
Every time he aces a point, the crowd erupts in applause.

Related Terms
