courtyard

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔːrtˌjɑrd / (AmE), / ˈkɔːtˌjɑːd / (BrE)
an open area that is partly or completely enclosed by the walls or buildings surrounding it, typically found in homes, palaces, or other large structures
open area surrounded by buildings or walls

Examples

The children played for hours in the courtyard behind the old mansion.
A fountain stood in the center of the courtyard, surrounded by blooming flowers.
During the festival, musicians performed in the castle's spacious courtyard.

Synset Examples

the house was built around an inner court

Related Terms

  • Synonym: court - an open area surrounded by walls or buildings (less specific)

Etymology

From Middle French 'cour' (court, yard) + English 'yard'.
