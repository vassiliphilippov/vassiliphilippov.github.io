unvanquished

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈvæŋkwɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈvaŋkwɪʃt / (BrE)
Not conquered or defeated; unbeaten.
not conquered or defeated

Examples

The proud city remained unvanquished after years of siege and hardship.
Despite countless attempts, his spirit was unvanquished by adversity.
Legends celebrate the unvanquished warriors who never surrendered.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: vanquished - conquered or defeated

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'vanquished' (from Latin 'vincere', to conquer).
