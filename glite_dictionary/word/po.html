PO

1. NOUN

/ piː oʊ / (AmE), / piː əʊ / (BrE)
abbreviation for Post Office, the government agency responsible for mail delivery in the United States
abbreviation for Post Office (US mail service)
american

Examples

Please send the package to my PO box address.
The local PO is closed on Sundays.
He works at the PO downtown sorting mail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Post Office - the public department responsible for mail services

2. NOUN

/ pi oʊ / (AmE), / piː əʊ / (BrE)
a noncommissioned officer in the Navy or Coast Guard with a rank comparable to sergeant in the Army
noncommissioned officer in the Navy or Coast Guard
american

Examples

After ten years of service, she was promoted to PO in the Coast Guard.
The PO gave clear instructions to the new recruits.
As a PO, he was responsible for the discipline and training of sailors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Petty Officer - noncommissioned officer in the Navy or Coast Guard

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'Petty Officer'. 'Petty' from Middle French 'petit' (small), 'officer' from Latin 'officium' (service, duty).
