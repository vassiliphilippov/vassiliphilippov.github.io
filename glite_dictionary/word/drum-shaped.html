drum-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈdrʌm ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈdrʌm ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of a drum; cylindrical with flat ends.
having the shape of a drum

Examples

The architect designed a drum-shaped auditorium with excellent acoustics.
They stored water in large drum-shaped containers beside the shed.
The museum displayed an ancient, drum-shaped pottery vessel found at the site.

Related Terms
