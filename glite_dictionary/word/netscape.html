Netscape

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɛtˌskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈnɛtˌskeɪp / (BrE)
A commercial web browser developed by Netscape Communications Corporation and widely used in the 1990s.
early commercial web browser

Examples

Many people used Netscape to access the internet before Internet Explorer became popular.
The release of Netscape Navigator marked a turning point in web browsing history.
He remembers downloading files with Netscape in the early days of the web.

Etymology

Formed from 'net' (short for network or internet) and 'scape' (as in landscape), coined as the product name.
