khimar

1. NOUN

/ kiˈmɑr / (AmE), / kɪˈmɑː / (BrE)
A type of headscarf worn by some Muslim women that covers the hair, neck, and shoulders, hanging down to just above the waist.
Muslim women's headscarf covering to the waist

Examples

The school allowed girls to wear the khimar as part of their uniform.
She chose a light blue khimar that matched her outfit perfectly.
Many women wear a khimar for religious reasons and personal preference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hijab - a general head covering worn by Muslim women
  • Degree Higher: niqab - a face veil worn by some Muslim women

Etymology

From Arabic خِمَار‎ (khimār), meaning 'veil, covering'.
