Alismales

1. NOUN

/ ˌælɪsˈmeɪliːz / (AmE), / ˌælɪsˈmeɪliːz / (BrE)
An order of aquatic monocotyledonous herbaceous plants, including water plantains and related species.
order of aquatic monocotyledonous plants

Examples

The Alismales include various species adapted to wetland environments.
Recent studies have improved our understanding of evolutionary relationships within the Alismales.
Botanists classify water plantains and their relatives in the order Alismales.

Etymology

From Latin 'Alisma' (water plantain) + -ales (order suffix in botanical Latin).
