periodontic

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɪr.i.oʊˈdɑn.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɛr.i.əˈdɒn.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving periodontics, the branch of dentistry concerned with the structures supporting and surrounding the teeth.
relating to periodontics (dental specialty)
medical

Examples

The dentist directed her to a periodontic specialist for further treatment.
Advances in periodontic techniques have greatly improved patient outcomes.
The practice offers comprehensive periodontic care, including gum surgery and maintenance.

Synset Examples

periodontal disease

Related Terms

  • Synonym: periodontal - relating to or affecting the tissues surrounding the teeth

Etymology

From 'periodontics', based on Greek 'peri-' (around) and 'odous, odont-' (tooth).
