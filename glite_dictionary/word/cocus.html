Cocus

1. PROPN

/ ˈkoʊ.kəs / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.kəs / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, one of the Titans, the father of Asteria and Leto by his sister Phoebe.
Titan in Greek mythology
