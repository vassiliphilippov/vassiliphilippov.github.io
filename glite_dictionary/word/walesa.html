Walesa

1. PROPN

/ wɑːˈwɛnsə / (AmE), / waːˈwɛnsə / (BrE)
Lech Walesa (born 1943), Polish labor leader and statesman, co-founder of Solidarity, and former President of Poland.
Polish labor leader and president

Examples

Walesa received the Nobel Peace Prize in 1983 for his role in championing human rights.
Many historians credit Walesa with helping to end communist rule in Poland.
During his presidency, Walesa implemented significant political and economic reforms.
