XLIII

1. NUM

/ ɛk s ɛl ˈθɜːrti ˈaɪ / (AmE), / ɛks ɛl aɪ aɪ aɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for the number 43
Roman numeral for 43

Examples

The XLIII Olympiad took place in the ancient city of Olympia.
His father was crowned King Henry XLIII in the year 1200.
The inscription reads, 'Built in the XLIII year of the reign.'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 43 - the cardinal number forty-three
  • Degree Lower: XLII - Roman numeral for 42
  • Degree Higher: XLIV - Roman numeral for 44

Etymology

From Latin numerals: 'XL' (40, from 'X' before 'L', ten less than fifty) and 'III' (three).
