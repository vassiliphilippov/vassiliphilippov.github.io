would-be

1. ADJ

/ ˈwʊd ˌbi / (AmE), / ˈwʊd ˌbi / (BrE)
desiring or aspiring to be a particular type of person, often without success or fulfillment
aspiring or unsuccessful (person)

Examples

She gave advice to the would-be actors who attended her workshop.
The police arrested a would-be thief near the bank last night.
Many would-be entrepreneurs struggle to turn their ideas into successful businesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aspiring - having ambitions to achieve something, especially as a career
