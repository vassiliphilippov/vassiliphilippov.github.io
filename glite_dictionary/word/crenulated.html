crenulated

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɛnjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkrɛn.jʊ.leɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
having a margin or edge with small, rounded teeth or scallops, as in some leaves or shells
having a finely scalloped edge

Examples

The shell's crenulated edge helped protect it from predators.
The botanist noted the crenulated leaves as a distinguishing feature of the plant.
The fossilized specimen showed a distinctly crenulated outline around its body.

Synset Examples

a crenulate leaf

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crenate - having a margin with rounded scallops or notches
  • Antonym: serrated - having a margin with sharp, saw-like teeth

Etymology

From Latin 'crenula' (small notch), diminutive of 'crena' (notch, scallop).
