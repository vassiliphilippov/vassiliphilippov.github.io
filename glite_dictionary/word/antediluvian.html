antediluvian

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.tɪ.dɪˈluː.vi.ən / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪ.dɪˈluː.vi.ən / (BrE)
Extremely old-fashioned or outdated; belonging to a very ancient time.
extremely old or outdated

Examples

His antediluvian attitudes about gender roles shocked the younger members of the team.
The museum is filled with antediluvian artifacts from lost civilizations.
Some of his ideas seem downright antediluvian in today's rapidly changing world.

Etymology
