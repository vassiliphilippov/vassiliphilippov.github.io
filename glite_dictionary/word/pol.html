pol

1. NOUN

/ pɑl / (AmE), / pɒl / (BrE)
an informal term for a politician, especially one who is actively involved in party politics
informal word for politician
informal american

Examples

The pol gave a rousing speech at the campaign rally last night.
Many voters are skeptical about promises made by any seasoned pol.
She became a well-known pol in her city's local government.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: politician - a person professionally involved in politics

Etymology

Shortened form of 'politician', first attested in early 20th-century American English.
