cut out

1. ADJ

/ kʌt aʊt / (AmE), / kʌt aʊt / (BrE)
(of a shape or piece) removed or formed by cutting, especially from a larger surface.
removed or shaped by cutting

Examples

He decorated the walls with cut out paper snowflakes.
The cut out pieces were arranged on the table for assembly.
She admired the cut out silhouettes displayed in the art room.

Synset Examples

the cut-out pieces of the dress

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cut - to divide or remove with a sharp instrument
