lighterman

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪtərmən / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtəmən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to operate or work on a lighter, a type of flat-bottomed barge used for loading or unloading ships
person who operates a barge

Examples

The lighterman skillfully guided the barge along the busy river docks.
As a third-generation lighterman, he knows every stretch of the Thames by heart.
A lighterman spends long hours loading and unloading cargo from ships anchored offshore.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bargee - a person who works on a barge

Etymology

Formed from 'lighter' (a flat-bottomed barge) + 'man'; 'lighter' from Dutch 'lichter', related to transporting cargo.
