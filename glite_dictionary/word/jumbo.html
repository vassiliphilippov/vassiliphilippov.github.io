jumbo

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒʌm.boʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌm.bəʊ / (BrE)
Very large; huge and bulky.
very large; huge

Examples

The airline recently introduced a jumbo jet capable of carrying over 500 passengers.
We ordered the jumbo pizza for the party, and it barely fit on the table.
She carried a jumbo bag filled with supplies for the camping trip.

Synset Examples

a jumbo jet
jumbo shrimp

Related Terms

  • Synonym: giant - very large in size, extent, or amount
  • Antonym: tiny - very small

Etymology

Originally from Jumbo, the name of a famous large elephant exhibited in the 19th century.
