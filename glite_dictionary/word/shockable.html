shockable

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɑːkəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈʃɒkəbl̩ / (BrE)
capable of being shocked, either emotionally or physically (as in medical contexts)
able to be shocked

Examples

Some people are so shockable that even a mild joke upsets them.
The patient was deemed shockable and required immediate defibrillation.
Her shockable reaction to the news surprised everyone in the room.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unshockable - incapable of being shocked
  • Degree Higher: shocking - causing a strong feeling of surprise or dismay
