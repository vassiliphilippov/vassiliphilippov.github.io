bell

1. NOUN

/ bɛl / (AmE), / bɛl / (BrE)
the sound made when a bell is struck, typically a clear, resonant ringing noise
sound made by a struck bell

Examples

The bell rang loudly, signaling the end of class.
A soft bell echoed through the empty hall.
Children stopped playing when they heard the distant bell from the church tower.

4. NOUN

/ bɛl / (AmE), / bɛl / (BrE)

Examples

The change of the watch was signaled by eight bells.
After four bells it was time for the crew's midday meal.
He woke up suddenly when six bells rang through the ship.
