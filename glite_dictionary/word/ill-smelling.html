ill-smelling

1. ADJ

/ ɪlˈsmɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪlˈsmɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
having an unpleasant or offensive smell
having an unpleasant smell

Examples

The garbage left in the sun became ill-smelling within a few hours.
She wrinkled her nose at the ill-smelling socks piling up in the corner of the room.
They avoided the ill-smelling alley on their way home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malodorous - having a bad smell; foul-smelling
  • Antonym: fragrant - having a pleasant and sweet smell
