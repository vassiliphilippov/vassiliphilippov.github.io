ding-dong

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪŋ ˌdɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɪŋ ˌdɒŋ / (BrE)
the repeated ringing sound produced by a bell or doorbell
sound of a bell

Examples

The ding-dong of the church bell echoed through the village.
I heard the familiar ding-dong and knew someone was at the door.
The clock's ding-dong marked the start of the new hour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clang - a loud, resonant metallic sound
  • Synonym: peal - a loud ringing of bells
