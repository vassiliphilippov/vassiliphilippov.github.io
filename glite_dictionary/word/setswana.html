Setswana

1. NOUN

/ sɛtˈswɑːnə / (AmE), / sɛtˈswɑːnə / (BrE)
the Bantu language spoken by the Tswana people in Botswana and neighboring regions
Bantu language of the Tswana people

Examples

She is fluent in both English and Setswana.
The guide translated our instructions into Setswana for the local residents.
Many primary schools in Botswana teach classes in Setswana as well as English.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tswana - the Bantu ethnic group primarily in Botswana and South Africa

Etymology

From Tswana *Setswana*, meaning 'language of the Tswana'.
