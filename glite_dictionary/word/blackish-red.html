blackish-red

1. ADJ

/ ˈblækɪʃ ˈrɛd / (AmE), / ˈblækɪʃ ˈrɛd / (BrE)
Having a red color that is tinged or mixed with black.
red tinged with black

Examples

The leaves turned a blackish-red color as autumn approached.
He wore a tie with a subtle blackish-red pattern to the meeting.
The artist mixed paints to create a unique blackish-red shade for the sunset.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: crimson - deep red color
  • Degree Lower: reddish - slightly red in color
