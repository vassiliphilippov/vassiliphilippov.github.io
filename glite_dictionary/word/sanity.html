sanity

1. NOUN

/ ˈsænəti / (AmE), / ˈsænɪti / (BrE)
the condition of having a healthy mind and thinking and behaving in a normal, rational way
soundness of mind; mental health

Examples

After months of stress, she questioned whether she could maintain her sanity.
A few days off work helped him recover his sanity.
Meditation is important for preserving your sanity in a busy life.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: insanity - the state of being seriously mentally ill; madness
  • Antonym: madness - the state of having a serious mental illness or behaving in a very foolish way

Etymology

From Latin 'sanitas' meaning health, soundness, derived from 'sanus' meaning healthy.
