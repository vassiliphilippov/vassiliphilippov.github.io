scumbag

1. NOUN

/ ˈskʌmˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈskʌmˌbaɡ / (BrE)
an offensive term for a person regarded as despicable or contemptible
despicable or contemptible person (offensive)
offensive slang

Examples

I can't believe that scumbag tried to steal my wallet in broad daylight.
Many people consider those who deliberately cheat others to be total scumbags.
She looked at him with disgust and called him a scumbag after hearing what he had done.

Synset Examples

only a rotter would do that
kill the rat
throw the bum out
you cowardly little pukes!
the British call a contemptible person a ‘git’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rat - a contemptible person (slang)
  • Synonym: bum - a worthless or disreputable person
  • Synonym: git - British slang for a contemptible person
  • Antonym: hero - a person admired for courage or noble qualities

Etymology

Originally US slang; from 'scum' + 'bag', originally referring to a used condom and later generalized to an insult.
