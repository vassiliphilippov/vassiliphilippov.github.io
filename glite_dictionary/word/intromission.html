intromission

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntroʊˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of inserting one thing into another, especially the insertion of the penis into the vagina or another orifice during sexual intercourse
the act of inserting one thing into another

Examples

The scientist explained the process of intromission during the laboratory demonstration.
Successful fertilization in many animals requires intromission as part of mating.
Some medical procedures involve the intromission of instruments into the body.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insertion - the act of putting something inside something else

Etymology

From Latin intromissio (a sending or letting in), from intro- (inward) + mittere (to send).
