Samoan

1. NOUN

/ səˈmoʊ.ən / (AmE), / səˈməʊ.ən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the Samoan Islands, a group of islands in the South Pacific
native or inhabitant of the Samoan Islands

Examples

She met a Samoan while traveling in the Pacific.
Many Samoans maintain strong cultural traditions.
The Samoan welcomed us with a warm smile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Samoan - the Austronesian language spoken in Samoa

Etymology

From Samoa, the name of the islands, plus English -an (denoting origin or inhabitant).
