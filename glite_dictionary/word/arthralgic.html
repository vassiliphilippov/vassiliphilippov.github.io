arthralgic

1. ADJ

/ ɑrˈθrældʒɪk / (AmE), / ɑːˈθraldʒɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by arthralgia (joint pain).
relating to joint pain
medical

Examples

The patient complained of arthralgic symptoms in her wrists and knees.
Several viral infections can cause arthralgic complications, especially in elderly patients.
The study focused on the management of arthralgic side effects among cancer survivors.

Related Terms

Etymology

From arthralgia (joint pain) + -ic (forming adjectives).
