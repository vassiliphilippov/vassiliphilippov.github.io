t'ai chi

1. NOUN

/ taɪ ˈtʃi / (AmE), / taɪ ˈtʃiː / (BrE)
a Chinese system of slow, meditative physical exercise performed for relaxation, balance, and health
Chinese system of meditative exercise

Examples

Many people practice t'ai chi in the park each morning to improve their flexibility and focus.
I signed up for a t'ai chi class to help reduce my stress levels.
Medical studies suggest that t'ai chi can benefit balance in older adults.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tai chi chuan - the full name for t'ai chi; a Chinese martial art practiced for its health benefits

Etymology

From Chinese 太极 (tàijí), meaning 'supreme ultimate'.
