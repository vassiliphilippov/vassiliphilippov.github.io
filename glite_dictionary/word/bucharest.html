Bucharest

1. PROPN

/ ˈbuːkəˌrɛst / (AmE), / ˌbjuːkəˈrɛst / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Romania, located in the southeastern part of the country
capital city of Romania

Examples

She traveled to Bucharest to attend an international conference last summer.
The architecture in Bucharest reflects a unique blend of historical and modern styles.
Many tech startups have emerged in Bucharest in recent years.

Etymology

From Romanian 'București', possibly from a personal name or the word 'bucurie' (joy).
