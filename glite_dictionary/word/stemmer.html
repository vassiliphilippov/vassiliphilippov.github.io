stemmer

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɛmər / (AmE), / ˈstɛmə / (BrE)
A worker who removes the stems from moistened tobacco leaves and bundles the leaves together for processing.
person who strips stems from tobacco leaves

Examples

The stemmer worked quickly to prepare the tobacco leaves for drying.
Many stemmers were employed in the factory during the harvest season.
A skilled stemmer could process hundreds of leaves in a single day.

Etymology

From 'stem' (the main stalk of a plant) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).

2. NOUN

/ ˈstɛmər / (AmE), / ˈstɛmə / (BrE)
a device or machine used to remove stems from fruit, such as grapes or apples, during processing
device for removing stems from fruit

Examples

The winery invested in a new stemmer to improve grape processing efficiency.
After passing through the stemmer, the apples were ready for further preparation.
Technicians maintain the stemmer regularly to prevent mechanical failure during harvest.

3. NOUN

/ ˈstɛmər / (AmE), / ˈstɛmə / (BrE)
a worker who makes or attaches stems to artificial flowers as part of their production.
person who stems artificial flowers

Examples

The stemmer crafted delicate wire stems for hundreds of silk blooms each day.
She worked as a stemmer in the artificial flower factory during the summer.
Experienced stemmers can attach stems to flowers much faster than new trainees.

Etymology

From 'stem' + '-er', meaning one who stems (adds stems).

4. NOUN

/ ˈstɛmər / (AmE), / ˈstɛmə / (BrE)
an algorithm that removes inflectional and derivational endings from words to reduce them to their base or stem form
algorithm for reducing words to their stem

Examples

A stemmer allows search engines to handle different word forms, such as 'run' and 'running'.
The Porter stemmer is widely used in English language text processing applications.
While using a stemmer, some words may be reduced to non-dictionary stems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lemmatizer - an algorithm that reduces words to their dictionary or base form (lemma)

5. NOUN

/ ˈstɛmər / (AmE), / ˈstɛmə / (BrE)
A miner's tool, typically a bar, used for packing material tightly over an explosive charge before blasting.
miner's tool for ramming packing over explosives

Examples

The miner used a stemmer to ensure the explosives were securely packed.
Always check your stemmer for any damage before beginning to tamp down the charge.
Without a proper stemmer, the blasting process can be both ineffective and dangerous.
