uncompromisingly

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnˈkɑːmprəˌmaɪzɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkɒmprəˌmaɪzɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows an unwillingness to change opinions or demands, or to make concessions
in an unyielding or inflexible manner

Examples

She uncompromisingly defended her beliefs throughout the heated debate.
The artist uncompromisingly pursued his own unique vision, regardless of trends.
They uncompromisingly refused to alter the company's core principles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inflexibly - in a way that does not change or adapt
  • Synonym: unbendingly - in a rigid or obstinate manner

Etymology

From 'uncompromising' + '-ly', ultimately from Latin 'compromissum' (a mutual promise).
