niggler

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪɡlər / (AmE), / ˈnɪɡlə / (BrE)
a person who constantly criticizes or complains about trivial or minor details
someone who nitpicks minor faults

Examples

Sarah is such a niggler that no project ever seems good enough for her.
The manager tried to give constructive feedback, but ended up sounding like a niggler.
No one wanted to work with the team's niggler because he complained about everything.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nitpicker - someone who pays excessive attention to trivial details
  • Synonym: critic - a person who expresses disapproval or finds fault
