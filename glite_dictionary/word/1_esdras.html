1 Esdras

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɜrst ˈɛzdrəs / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˈɛzdrəs / (BrE)
an Apocryphal book of the Bible, consisting largely of material from I and II Chronicles, Ezra, and Nehemiah.
an Apocryphal book of the Bible

Examples

Many biblical scholars refer to 1 Esdras when discussing the Apocrypha.
The story in 1 Esdras overlaps significantly with accounts found in Ezra and Nehemiah.
Some editions of the Bible include 1 Esdras among the apocryphal books.

Etymology

From Latin 'Esdras', from Greek 'Esdras', ultimately from Hebrew 'Ezra'.
