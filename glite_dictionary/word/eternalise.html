to eternalise

1. VERB

/ ɪˈtɜrnəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ɪˈtɜːnəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make someone or something remembered or famous forever; to immortalize
make last or be remembered forever

Examples

The artist hoped his paintings would eternalise his vision for generations to come.
A statue was built to eternalise the leader's memory in the city's main square.
Her words continue to eternalise the values of kindness and hope in our community.

Synset Examples

This melody immortalized its composer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to immortalize - to make someone or something famous forever

Etymology

From Latin 'aeternalis' (eternal) with the suffix '-ise' forming the verb.
