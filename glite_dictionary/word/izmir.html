Izmir

1. PROPN

/ ɪzˈmɪr / (AmE), / ɪzˈmɪə / (BrE)
A major port city in western Turkey, known for its history, commerce, and cultural significance.
port city in western Turkey

Examples

After graduation, she moved to Izmir to work at a technology company.
The harbor of Izmir bustles with activity day and night.
Many tourists visit Izmir for its rich history and beautiful coastline.

Etymology

From Turkish İzmir, the modern name for ancient Smyrna.
