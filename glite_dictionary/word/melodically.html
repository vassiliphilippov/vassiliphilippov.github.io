melodically

1. ADV

/ məˈlɑdɪkli / (AmE), / mɪˈlɒdɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or is characteristic of melody; in terms of melody
in relation to melody

Examples

The singer performed the piece melodically, emphasizing every phrase with musical subtlety.
This composition is melodically richer than any of his earlier works.
The two instruments moved melodically together, creating a harmonious effect.

Synset Examples

melodically interesting themes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: melodic - relating to melody

Etymology

Formed from 'melodic' (from Greek 'melōidikos', relating to melody) + '-ally', adverbial suffix.
