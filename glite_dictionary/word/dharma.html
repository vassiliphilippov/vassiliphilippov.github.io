Dharma

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑrmə / (AmE), / ˈdɑːmə / (BrE)
An ancient sage in Hindu mythology, worshipped as a god by some lower castes.
sage-god in Hindu mythology

Examples

Some communities in India revere Dharma as a powerful deity and protector.
According to legend, Dharma taught the importance of justice and moral conduct.
Rituals honoring Dharma are still observed in certain regions to this day.

Etymology

From Sanskrit धर्म (dharma), meaning 'law,' 'duty,' or 'right conduct.'
