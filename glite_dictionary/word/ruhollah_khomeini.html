Ruhollah Khomeini

1. PROPN

/ ruːˈhoʊlɑː xoʊˈmeɪni / (AmE), / ruːˈhɒlɑː xɒˈmeɪni / (BrE)
(1900–1989) Iranian religious and political leader who led the 1979 Iranian Revolution, becoming the country's supreme leader and establishing an Islamic republic.
Iranian religious and political leader, led 1979 revolution

Examples

Ruhollah Khomeini played a central role in the 1979 Iranian Revolution.
Many Iranians regard Ruhollah Khomeini as the founding father of the Islamic Republic of Iran.
Biographies of Ruhollah Khomeini detail his rise to power and lasting influence on Iran.
