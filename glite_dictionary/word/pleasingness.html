pleasingness

1. NOUN

/ ˈplizɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˈpliːzɪŋnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of giving pleasure or satisfaction to the senses; the property of being pleasing.
the quality of being pleasing or enjoyable

Examples

The pleasingness of the painting captivated everyone at the gallery.
She admired the pleasingness of the music as it filled the room.
The pleasingness of his voice made the lecture enjoyable to listen to.

Synset Examples

the liveliness and pleasingness of dark eyes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pleasantness - the quality of being pleasant, enjoyable, or agreeable
  • Antonym: unpleasantness - the quality of being unpleasant or disagreeable

Etymology

From 'pleasing' + '-ness', forming a noun indicating state or quality.
