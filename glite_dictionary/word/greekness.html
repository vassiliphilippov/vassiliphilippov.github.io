Greekness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡriːknəs / (AmE), / ˈɡriːknɪs / (BrE)
The quality, character, or condition of being Greek in nature, culture, or origin.
the quality or state of being Greek

Examples

She takes great pride in her Greekness and loves sharing her heritage with others.
The film beautifully captures the essence of Greekness in its portrayal of family and traditions.
When he visited Athens, he felt a strong sense of connection to his Greekness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Greek - relating to Greece, its people, or language

Etymology

Formed from 'Greek' + '-ness', a common English suffix forming nouns of state or quality.
