unspecified

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈspɛsɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈspɛsɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not stated clearly, identified, or described in detail; not specified.
not stated or identified in detail

Examples

The cause of the delay was unspecified in the official report.
The package contains several unspecified features that will be revealed later.
He threatened unspecified consequences if his demands were not met.

Synset Examples

threatened unspecified reprisals

Related Terms

  • Antonym: specified - stated clearly or identified in detail
  • Synonym: undefined - not defined or made specific

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) and 'specified', from Latin 'specificare' (to mention specifically).
