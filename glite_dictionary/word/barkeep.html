barkeep

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːrˌkip / (AmE), / ˈbɑːˌkiːp / (BrE)
a person whose job is to mix and serve alcoholic drinks at a bar; a bartender
person who serves drinks at a bar

Examples

The barkeep greeted each customer with a friendly smile.
She asked the barkeep for another glass of whiskey.
Every regular at the pub knew the barkeep by name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bartender - a person who serves drinks at a bar

Etymology

Shortened form of 'barkeeper'.
