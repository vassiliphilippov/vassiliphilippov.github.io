grannie

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡræni / (AmE), / ˈɡræni / (BrE)
an informal term for one's grandmother; the mother of your father or mother
informal term for grandmother
informal

Examples

My grannie always bakes the best cookies during the holidays.
We visited my grannie for her 80th birthday celebration last weekend.
Every Sunday, I have lunch with my grannie and listen to her stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandmother - the mother of one's father or mother
  • Antonym: grandfather - the father of one's father or mother

Etymology

Diminutive of 'grandmother', based on 'granny' and the -ie suffix used for endearment.
