synopsis

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈnɑːpsɪs / (AmE), / sɪˈnɒpsɪs / (BrE)
a brief summary or general survey of the main points of something, such as a text, argument, or theory
a brief summary of main points

Examples

The professor asked for a synopsis of each assigned article.
Before reading the novel, she checked the synopsis on the back cover.
The report began with a synopsis of the main findings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summary - a brief statement of main points
  • Synonym: overview - a general summary or review

Etymology

From Greek sunopsis (general view), from sun- (together) + opsis (view).
