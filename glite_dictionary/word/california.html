California

1. PROPN

/ ˌkælɪˈfɔrnjə / (AmE), / ˌkælɪˈfɔːniə / (BrE)
a state in the western United States, located on the Pacific coast and known for its large size and frequent earthquakes
U.S. state on the Pacific coast

Examples

Many people dream of moving to California for its beaches and sunny weather.
The tech industry in California attracts professionals from around the world.
An earthquake rattled northern California late last night.

Etymology

Named after the mythical paradise 'California' described in a 16th-century Spanish romance novel.
