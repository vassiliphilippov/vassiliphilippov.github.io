80

1. NUM

/ ˈeɪti / (AmE), / ˈeɪti / (BrE)
the number equivalent to the product of eight and ten; seventy plus ten.
the number eighty (80)

Examples

There were 80 students in the assembly hall this morning.
The train travels at a speed of 80 kilometers per hour.
She celebrated her grandfather's 80th birthday with a big family party.
