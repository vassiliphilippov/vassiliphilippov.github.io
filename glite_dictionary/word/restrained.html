restrained

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈstreɪnd / (AmE), / rɪˈstreɪnd / (BrE)
characterized by moderation or prudence; not excessive or showy
moderate; showing self-control or prudence

Examples

Her restrained response to the criticism showed her professionalism.
The artist chose a restrained color palette for the painting.
There was restrained excitement in the room as the results were announced.

Synset Examples

guarded optimism

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guarded - cautious and reserved in speech or behavior

Etymology

From restrain + -ed, from Latin restringere (to bind back).
