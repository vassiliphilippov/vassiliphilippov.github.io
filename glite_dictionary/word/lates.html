Lates

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪtiːz / (AmE), / ˈleɪtiːz / (BrE)
A genus of large percoid fishes found in fresh and brackish waters, including species such as the Nile perch.
genus of large freshwater and brackish fish

Examples

The genus Lates includes several important food fishes, such as the Nile perch.
Scientists have studied Lates species for their ecological impact in African lakes.
Some members of Lates inhabit both fresh and brackish waters across different continents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nile perch - a large species in the genus Lates, found in African rivers and lakes

Etymology

From Latin 'lates' meaning 'a fish.'
