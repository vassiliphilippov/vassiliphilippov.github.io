hello

1. NOUN

/ həˈloʊ / (AmE), / həˈləʊ / (BrE)
An expression of greeting used when meeting someone or answering the telephone.
expression of greeting

Examples

She greeted everyone with a cheerful hello as she entered the room.
Their morning routine included exchanging quick hellos before starting work.
A simple hello can brighten someone's day.

Synset Examples

every morning they exchanged polite hellos

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hi - informal greeting
  • Synonym: hey - informal greeting or attention-calling word
  • Antonym: goodbye - expression used when parting

Etymology

First attested in the 19th century; possibly from 'holla' or 'hallo,' used to call attention.
