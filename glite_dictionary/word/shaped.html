shaped

1. ADJ

/ ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
formed or molded to fit a particular shape, often by altering or working a pliable material.
formed or molded to a shape

Examples

The artist created a shaped vase to match the room's décor.
He bought a pair of custom-shaped insoles for greater comfort.
The shaped cushions fit perfectly into the antique chairs.

Synset Examples

a shaped handgrip
the molded steel plates
the wrought silver bracelet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: molded - formed into a shape, especially by using a mold
  • Synonym: wrought - worked or formed, especially by hand

2. ADJ

/ ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a particular or specified shape or form, often used in combination
having a particular shape or form

Examples

The new lamp has a beautifully shaped glass shade.
They live in a dome-shaped house on the outskirts of town.
The cookies were star-shaped, perfect for the holiday season.

Synset Examples

a square shaped playing field

Related Terms

  • Synonym: form - the visible shape or configuration of something
  • Antonym: shapeless - without a definite or regular shape
