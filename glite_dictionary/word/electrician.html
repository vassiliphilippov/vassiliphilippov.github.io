electrician

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɪʃən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to install, maintain, or repair electrical wiring and equipment
person who installs or repairs electrical systems

Examples

We had to call an electrician to fix the wiring in our kitchen.
She trained as an electrician after graduating from high school.
The electrician quickly found the fault in the circuit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: technician - person skilled in the technical aspects of a job

Etymology

From electric + -ian, denoting a person skilled in electrical work.
