humblebee

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌmbəlˌbi / (AmE), / ˈhʌmbəlˌbiː / (BrE)
A robust, hairy social bee found in temperate regions; another name for the bumblebee.
a type of social bee; synonym of bumblebee
archaic

Examples

As the flowers opened, a humblebee gathered nectar from each blossom.
She watched a humblebee disappear into the foxgloves by the garden wall.
In old poetry, the humblebee is often praised for its industriousness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bumblebee - a large, hairy bee known for its loud buzzing sound

Etymology

Variant of 'bumblebee', originating from Middle English 'humble' (to buzz, drone) + 'bee'.
