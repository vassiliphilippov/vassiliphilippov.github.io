reversibly

1. ADV

/ rɪˈvɜrsəbli / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːsəbli / (BrE)
In a way that can be undone or returned to the original state; in a reversible manner.
in a reversible way

Examples

The material can be reversibly deformed without permanent damage.
This chemical reaction proceeds reversibly under certain conditions.
After cooling, the gel reversibly returns to its solid state.

Synset Examples

reversibly convertible

Related Terms

  • Antonym: irreversibly - in a way that cannot be reversed
  • Synonym: reversible - able to be turned around or restored to the original state
