identicalness

1. NOUN

/ aɪˈdɛntɪkəlnəs / (AmE), / aɪˈdɛntɪkəlnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being exactly the same or alike in every way.
exact sameness; being identical

Examples

The identicalness of the two signatures was suspicious to the investigators.
Some critics questioned the identicalness of the paintings displayed in the gallery.
She marveled at the identicalness between the twins' voices during their performance.

Synset Examples

they shared an identity of interests

Related Terms

  • Synonym: identity - the fact of being who or what a person or thing is
  • Antonym: difference - the state of being unlike or distinct

Etymology

Formed from 'identical' plus the suffix '-ness', indicating a state or quality.
