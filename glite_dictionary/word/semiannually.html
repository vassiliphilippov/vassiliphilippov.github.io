semiannually

1. ADV

/ ˌsɛmiˈænjuəli / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈanjʊəli / (BrE)
Twice a year; occurring or done every half year.
twice a year

Examples

Our company distributes bonuses semiannually, in June and December.
Interest on the loan is compounded semiannually rather than annually.
The board meets semiannually to review the organization's progress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biannually - twice a year (often ambiguous, but commonly used as a synonym)

Etymology

From Latin 'semi-' (half) + 'annus' (year).
