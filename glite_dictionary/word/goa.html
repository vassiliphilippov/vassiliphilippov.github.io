Goa

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡoʊ.ə / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ.ə / (BrE)
a state in southwestern India, known for its beaches, colonial architecture, and as a former Portuguese colony until 1961.
state in southwestern India

Examples

Many tourists visit Goa for its beautiful beaches and vibrant nightlife every year.
The architecture in Goa reflects its Portuguese colonial heritage.
My friend grew up in Goa before moving abroad for university.

Etymology

Named after the Sanskrit 'Gomantak', but popularized by Portuguese and earlier colonial usage.
