obstetric

1. ADJ

/ əbˈstɛtrɪk / (AmE), / əbˈstɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to childbirth or to the care of women giving birth
relating to childbirth; obstetrics
medical

Examples

The clinic specializes in obstetric care for pregnant women.
She accepted an obstetric position at the teaching hospital.
The development of modern obstetric procedures has greatly reduced childbirth risks.

Synset Examples

obstetric hospital

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obstetrical - relating to childbirth or obstetrics
  • Antonym: gynecological - relating to female reproductive health (not childbirth)

Etymology

From Latin 'obstetricus', from 'obstetrix' (midwife), from 'obstare' (to stand before).
