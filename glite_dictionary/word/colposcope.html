colposcope

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑl.pəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈkɒl.pəˌskəʊp / (BrE)
a medical instrument with a magnifying lens used to examine the vagina and cervix.
instrument for examining vagina and cervix
medical

Examples

The gynecologist used a colposcope to examine the patient's cervix after an abnormal Pap smear.
A colposcope provides a magnified view that helps detect early signs of cervical cancer.
Training in the use of a colposcope is standard for medical professionals in women's health clinics.

Synset Examples

A specialized colposcope equipped with a camera is used in examining and collecting evidence for victims of rape and sexual assault.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endoscope - instrument for examining the interior of a hollow organ or cavity

Etymology

From Greek 'kolpos' (womb, vagina) + '-scope' (to look at).
