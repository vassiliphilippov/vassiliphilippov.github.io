twenty-ninth

1. ADJ

/ ˌtwentiˈnaɪnθ / (AmE), / ˌtwɛntiˈnaɪnθ / (BrE)
coming next after the twenty-eighth in a sequence; 29th
ordinal number: 29th

Examples

She finished in twenty-ninth place in the marathon.
His birthday falls on the twenty-ninth of February.
The twenty-ninth chapter of the book contains the solution.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-eighth - ordinal number: 28th
  • Degree Higher: thirtieth - ordinal number: 30th
