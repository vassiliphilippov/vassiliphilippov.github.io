multi-color

1. ADJ

/ ˈmʌltiˌkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌkʌlə / (BrE)
Having sections or patches of different, often bright, colors.
having patches of different colors

Examples

The artist chose a multi-color palette for her mural to brighten up the space.
He wore a striking multi-color jacket to the festival, catching everyone's attention.
The garden is full of multi-color blooms in the spring.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multicolored - having many colors; made up of several different colors
  • Synonym: polychromatic - showing a variety or a change of colors; multicolored
