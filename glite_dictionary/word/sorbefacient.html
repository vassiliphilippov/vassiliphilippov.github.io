sorbefacient

1. ADJ

/ sɔr.bɪˈfeɪ.ʃənt / (AmE), / sɔː.bɪˈfeɪ.ʃənt / (BrE)
(of a substance) inducing or promoting absorption, especially in a medical context.
promoting absorption (medical)
medical

Examples

The doctor applied a sorbefacient ointment to aid in the absorption of the medication through the skin.
Some creams are formulated with sorbefacient agents to improve their effectiveness.
Research continues into the development of new sorbefacient compounds for medical use.

Etymology

From Latin 'sorbere' (to suck in) + '-facient' (making).
