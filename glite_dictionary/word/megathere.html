megathere

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛɡ.əˌθɪr / (AmE), / ˈmɛɡ.əˌθɪə / (BrE)
a gigantic extinct ground sloth-like mammal of the Pliocene and Pleistocene epochs found in America
extinct giant ground sloth-like mammal

Examples

The fossilized remains of a megathere were discovered in a South American cave.
Paleontologists study the megathere to learn about prehistoric ecosystems.
A full skeleton of the megathere is displayed at the natural history museum.

Etymology

From Greek mega- (great) and therion (beast).
