erroneous

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈroʊniəs / (AmE), / ɪˈrəʊniəs / (BrE)
containing or characterized by error; incorrect or mistaken
incorrect; containing errors

Examples

The report was withdrawn after it was discovered to be based on erroneous information.
Her erroneous assumptions led to a costly mistake.
It is important to correct erroneous beliefs through education.

Synset Examples

erroneous conclusions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incorrect - not correct; containing an error
  • Antonym: accurate - free from errors; correct and precise

Etymology

From Latin 'erroneus' (straying, wandering), from 'errare' (to err, stray).
