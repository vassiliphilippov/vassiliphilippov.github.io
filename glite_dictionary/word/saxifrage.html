saxifrage

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæksəˌfrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsæksɪfrɪdʒ / (BrE)
A low-growing plant of the genus Saxifraga, typically found in rocky or alpine areas and having small, star-shaped flowers.
plant of the genus Saxifraga

Examples

Alpine saxifrage thrives in the rocky crevices of mountainous regions.
The garden was dotted with delicate white and pink saxifrages.
Botanists discovered a rare species of saxifrage growing along the riverbank.

Etymology

From Latin saxifraga, from saxum (rock) + frangere (to break), referring to where these plants grow.
