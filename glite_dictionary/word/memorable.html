memorable

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛm.ɚ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈmɛm.ər.ə.bəl / (BrE)
very good, interesting, or unusual and therefore worth remembering.
worth remembering; easily remembered

Examples

Her performance in the film was truly memorable and received critical acclaim.
It was a memorable evening filled with laughter and great company.
He told a memorable story that stayed with me for years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unforgettable - impossible to forget
  • Antonym: forgettable - easily forgotten; not worth remembering

Etymology

From Latin 'memorabilis' (worthy of remembrance), from 'memorare' (to remember).
