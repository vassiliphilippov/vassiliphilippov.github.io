Gustav Klimt

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡuːstɑːv ˈklɪmt / (AmE), / ˈɡʊstav ˈklɪmt / (BrE)
(1862–1918) An Austrian painter known for his distinctive, symbolic works influenced by Art Nouveau, especially noted for his paintings, murals, and sketches.
Austrian Art Nouveau painter

Examples

The works of Gustav Klimt are celebrated for their intricate detail and vibrant gold leaf.
Many art lovers admire Gustav Klimt for his influential role in the Vienna Secession movement.
A famous painting by Gustav Klimt, 'The Kiss,' is displayed in the Belvedere Palace in Vienna.
