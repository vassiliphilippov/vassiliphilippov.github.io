Putrajaya

1. PROPN

/ pʊt.rəˈdʒɑː.jə / (AmE), / pʊt.rəˈdʒɑː.jə / (BrE)
the administrative capital city of Malaysia, located south of Kuala Lumpur and established in the late 1990s as the new government center.
administrative capital of Malaysia

Examples

The Malaysian government relocated its ministries to Putrajaya in the early 2000s.
Many tourists visit Putrajaya to admire its modern architecture and scenic bridges.
The annual festival in Putrajaya attracts families from all over the country.

Etymology

From Malay, combining 'putra' (prince) and 'jaya' (success or victory).
