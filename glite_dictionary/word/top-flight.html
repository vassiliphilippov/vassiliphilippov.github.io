top-flight

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɑp flaɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɒp flaɪt / (BrE)
Of the highest quality; among the best or most excellent in its category.
of the highest quality; best possible

Examples

She graduated from a top-flight university with honors.
The restaurant is famous for its top-flight service and exquisite cuisine.
They hired a top-flight attorney to represent them in court.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first-rate - of the best quality; excellent
  • Antonym: second-rate - of inferior or lower quality

Etymology

From the combination of 'top' (highest) and 'flight' (a group or level), originally referring to the highest division in competitive leagues.
