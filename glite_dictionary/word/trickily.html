trickily

1. ADV

/ ˈtrɪkɪli / (AmE), / ˈtrɪkɪli / (BrE)
In a way that is subtle, cunning, or involves trickery.
in a cunning or tricky manner

Examples

He trickily avoided answering the question by changing the subject.
The magician trickily concealed the coin in his sleeve.
She trickily negotiated the contract to her advantage.

Synset Examples

he craftily arranged to be there when the decision was announced
had ever circumstances conspired so cunningly?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: craftily - in a clever and cunning manner
  • Synonym: cunningly - in a clever and deceptive way
