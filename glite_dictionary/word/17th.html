17th

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛv(ə)nˌtiːnθ / (AmE), / ˈsɛv(ə)ntiːnθ / (BrE)
Coming next after the sixteenth in a sequence; being number seventeen in order.
seventeenth in order

Examples

She finished in 17th place in the marathon, just ahead of her teammate.
The event will be held on the 17th of July this year.
This is the 17th time he has visited the museum.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 16th - coming next after the fifteenth; sixteenth in order
  • Degree Higher: 18th - coming next after the seventeenth; eighteenth in order

Etymology

Formed from 'seventeen' (from Old English 'seofontiene') plus the ordinal suffix '-th'.
