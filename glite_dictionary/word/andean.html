Andean

1. ADJ

/ ˈændiən / (AmE), / ˈandiːən / (BrE)
relating to the Andes mountains or the people living in that region of South America.
of or relating to the Andes region

Examples

Many Andean communities maintain traditional agricultural practices.
The Andean condor is one of the largest flying birds in the world.
She specializes in Andean archaeology and ancient cultures.

Etymology

From Andes (mountain range in South America) + -an (adjective suffix).
