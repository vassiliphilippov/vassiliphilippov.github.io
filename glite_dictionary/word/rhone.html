Rhone

1. PROPN

/ roʊn / (AmE), / rəʊn / (BrE)
A major river in southeastern France, flowing from the Swiss Alps through Lake Geneva to the Mediterranean Sea near Marseilles.
major river in southeastern France

Examples

The Rhone Valley is renowned for its wine production.
The city of Lyon is situated where the Rhone and Saône rivers meet.
Tourists often take boat cruises along the Rhone to enjoy the stunning scenery.

Synset Examples

the valley of the Rhone is famous for its vineyards

Etymology

From Latin 'Rhodanus', the ancient name for the river.
