posthitis

1. NOUN

/ pɑsˈθaɪtɪs / (AmE), / pɒsˈθaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the foreskin of the penis, usually due to bacterial infection.
inflammation of penile foreskin
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with posthitis after presenting with swelling and redness of the foreskin.
Proper hygiene can help prevent conditions like posthitis in uncircumcised men.
Treatment of posthitis often involves antibiotics and good personal care.

Etymology

From Greek posthē (foreskin) + -itis (inflammation).
