to vitaminize

1. VERB

/ ˈvaɪ.tə.məˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈvɪ.tə.mɪˌnaɪz / (BrE)
to add vitamins to something as a supplement, especially to food products
add vitamins to (something)

Examples

Many breakfast cereals are vitaminized to improve their nutritional value.
The government launched a program to vitaminize common cooking oils.
Food manufacturers often vitaminize processed products to help address nutritional deficiencies.

Synset Examples

vitaminized processed foods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fortify - to strengthen food by adding nutrients, especially vitamins and minerals

Etymology

Formed from "vitamin" + "-ize" (verb-forming suffix).
