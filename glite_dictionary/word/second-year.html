second-year

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛkənd ˌjɪr / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd ˌjɪə / (BrE)
Relating to or for students in their second year of high school or college, especially in the United States.
in the second year of school or college
american

Examples

She was proud to be a second-year student at the university.
The second-year exams are considered more difficult than those in the first year.
He joined the basketball team during his second-year in college.
