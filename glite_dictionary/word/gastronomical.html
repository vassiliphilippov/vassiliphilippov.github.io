gastronomical

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡæstrəˈnɑmɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɡæstrəˈnɒmɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the art or science of good eating; pertaining to gastronomy.
relating to gastronomy or fine eating

Examples

She went on a gastronomical tour of Italy, sampling dishes from every region.
The festival offers a gastronomical experience unlike any other, with chefs from around the world.
His gastronomical curiosity led him to try unusual and exotic foods.

Synset Examples

gastronomic adventures

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gastronomic - relating to gastronomy or fine eating

Etymology

From French gastronomique, from Greek gastēr (stomach) + nomos (law).
