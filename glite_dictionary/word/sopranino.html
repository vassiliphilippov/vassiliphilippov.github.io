sopranino

1. ADJ

/ ˌsoʊprəˈniːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˌsɒprəˈniːnəʊ / (BrE)
(of a musical instrument or voice) higher in pitch than soprano; designating the highest range above soprano.
higher than soprano (music)

Examples

She played a beautiful melody on her sopranino recorder, impressing the audience with its bright sound.
The composer wrote a part specifically for a sopranino saxophone in the new jazz piece.
In the choir, the rare sopranino voice soared above even the sopranos.

Synset Examples

a sopranino recorder

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: soprano - the highest normal adult female singing voice

Etymology

Italian, diminutive of 'soprano' (from sopra 'above').
