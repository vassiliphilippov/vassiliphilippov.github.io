prototypic

1. ADJ

/ ˌproʊtəˈtɪpɪk / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəˈtɪpɪk / (BrE)
serving as an original model or typical example for others of the same kind; characteristic of a prototype
serving as an original model or example

Examples

The prototypic design set the standard for all future models produced by the company.
Her research focused on the prototypic structure seen in many viral proteins.
The author described a prototypic case that would inform future studies on the subject.

Synset Examples

archetypal patterns
she was the prototypal student activist

Related Terms

  • Synonym: archetypal - very typical of a certain kind of person or thing; original model
  • Synonym: prototype - an original model on which something is patterned

Etymology

From 'prototype' + '-ic', ultimately from Greek 'prototypon' (original model).
