jacksmelt

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒækˌsmɛlt / (AmE), / ˈdʒækˌsmɛlt / (BrE)
A relatively large silverside fish native to the Pacific coast of North America, sometimes reaching 18 inches in length.
large Pacific coast silverside fish

Examples

Fishermen along the coast often try to catch jacksmelt during the summer months.
We grilled fresh jacksmelt for dinner last night, and it was delicious.
The jacksmelt can sometimes grow up to 18 inches long in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silverside - any of various small, slender fish with silvery sides
