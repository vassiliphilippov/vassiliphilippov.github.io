pennoncel

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɛn.ənˈsɛl / (AmE), / ˌpɛn.ənˈsɛl / (BrE)
a small pennant or flag, especially one carried on a lance in medieval times
small flag carried on a lance

Examples

The knight rode into battle with a pennoncel fluttering from his lance.
During the tournament, each competitor displayed a unique pennoncel signifying their allegiance.
The museum exhibit featured a medieval pennoncel recovered from an ancient battlefield.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pennant - a long, narrow flag

Etymology

From Old French pennoncel, diminutive of pennon (flag or banner).
