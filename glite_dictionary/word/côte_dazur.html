Côte d'Azur

1. PROPN

/ koʊt dɑːˈzʊər / (AmE), / kəʊt daˈzyːr / (BrE)
the Mediterranean coastal region of southeastern France, including cities such as Nice and Cannes, known for its resorts and mild climate; the French Riviera
French Riviera region

Examples

Many celebrities spend their summers on the Côte d'Azur enjoying its beaches and nightlife.
The film festival in Cannes, located on the Côte d'Azur, attracts stars from around the world.
Her dream vacation is a road trip along the Côte d'Azur, stopping in picturesque seaside towns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: French Riviera - the Mediterranean coastal region of France; popular resort area

Etymology

French, literally 'coast of azure (blue)'
