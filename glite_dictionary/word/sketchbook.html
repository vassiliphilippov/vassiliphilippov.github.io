sketchbook

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɛtʃˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈskɛtʃˌbʊk / (BrE)
a book of blank sheets of paper used for drawing sketches
book of blank pages for drawing

Examples

She always carries a sketchbook to capture interesting scenes she finds in the city.
The artist's sketchbook was filled with drawings of flowers and landscapes.
He flipped through his old sketchbook and remembered his early days as an art student.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: notebook - a book of blank or ruled pages for writing notes

Etymology

From sketch + book, first recorded in the early 19th century.
