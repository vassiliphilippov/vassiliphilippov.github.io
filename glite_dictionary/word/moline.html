Moline

1. PROPN

/ moʊˈliːn / (AmE), / məʊˈliːn / (BrE)
A town in northwest Illinois located on the Mississippi River, known for its role in manufacturing and river transportation.
town in northwest Illinois, USA

Examples

Moline is home to several major manufacturing companies and is part of the Quad Cities metropolitan area.
The Mississippi River runs along the western edge of Moline, offering scenic views and recreational activities.
After moving to Moline, she quickly became involved in the local community events.

Etymology

Named after the French word 'moulin' meaning 'mill', due to its early milling industry.
