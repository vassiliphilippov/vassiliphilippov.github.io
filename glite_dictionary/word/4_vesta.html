4 Vesta

1. PROPN

/ fɔr ˈvɛstə / (AmE), / fɔː ˈvɛstə / (BrE)
the brightest asteroid in the asteroid belt and the fourth to be discovered, located between Mars and Jupiter
brightest asteroid; fourth discovered

Examples

Scientists observed 4 Vesta using both ground-based and space telescopes.
The Dawn spacecraft visited 4 Vesta in 2011, revealing much about its surface.
Among the objects in the asteroid belt, 4 Vesta stands out for its brightness.

Etymology

Named after Vesta, the Roman goddess of hearth and home; '4' denotes its order of discovery among asteroids.
