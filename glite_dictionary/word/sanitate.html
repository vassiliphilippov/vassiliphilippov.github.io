to sanitate

1. VERB

/ ˈsænɪteɪt / (AmE), / ˈsænɪteɪt / (BrE)
to provide with sanitary facilities or equipment; to make sanitary
make sanitary; provide sanitary facilities

Examples

The rural community worked together to sanitate the local water supply.
Efforts to sanitate public restrooms have greatly improved hygiene in the area.
The charity's goal is to sanitate schools in underdeveloped regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sanitize - to make clean and free from bacteria or germs

Etymology

Formed from 'sanitary' and the verb-forming suffix '-ate', influenced by Latin 'sanitas' (health).
