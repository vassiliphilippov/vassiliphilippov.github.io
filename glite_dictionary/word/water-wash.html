to water-wash

1. VERB

/ ˈwɔtər wɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə wɒʃ / (BrE)
to wash (something) with water, usually to clean or rinse it
wash with water

Examples

The gardener decided to water-wash the vegetables to remove any remaining soil.
You should always water-wash your car after driving through muddy roads.
After handling chemicals, workers must water-wash their hands thoroughly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wash - to clean something using water or another liquid
