abortive

1. ADJ

/ əˈbɔrtɪv / (AmE), / əˈbɔːtɪv / (BrE)
(of an action or plan) failing to produce the intended result; unsuccessful.
failing to succeed or achieve

Examples

The scientists' abortive attempts to develop a vaccine delayed the project.
Negotiations for a merger proved abortive after weeks of discussion.
His abortive bid for mayor was quickly forgotten by the media.

Synset Examples

an abortive revolt
a stillborn plot to assassinate the President

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unsuccessful - not achieving the intended goal
  • Antonym: successful - achieving the intended result

Etymology

From Latin abortivus (produced by abortion, unsuccessful), from aboriri (to miscarry).
