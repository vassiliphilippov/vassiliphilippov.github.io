Akha

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑː.kɑː / (AmE), / ˈɑː.kə / (BrE)
the language of the Akha people, belonging to the Loloish branch, spoken in parts of South Yunnan Province and neighboring regions
a Loloish language of Southeast Asia

Examples

The Akha language is spoken by communities in several countries across Southeast Asia.
She is conducting research on the grammar of Akha as part of her linguistic studies.
Efforts are being made to preserve traditional Akha stories by recording them in their native language.
