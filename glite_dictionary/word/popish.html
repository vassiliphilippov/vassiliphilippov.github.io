popish

1. ADJ

/ ˈpoʊ.pɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊ.pɪʃ / (BrE)
derogatory or archaic: relating to or characteristic of the Roman Catholic Church, especially in a disparaging or critical way.
relating to Roman Catholicism (often offensive/disparaging)
offensive archaic

Examples

The preacher denounced the new policy as dangerously popish and unfit for true Protestants.
She was accused of holding popish sympathies after attending mass.
In the 17th century, anti-popish sentiment was widespread in England.

Synset Examples

the Roman Catholic Church

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catholic - relating to the Roman Catholic Church

Etymology

From 'Pope' + -ish, meaning 'of the Pope' or 'related to the Pope.'
