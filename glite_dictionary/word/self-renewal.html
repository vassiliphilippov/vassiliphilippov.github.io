self-renewal

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlf.rɪˈnu.əl / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf.rɪˈnjuː.əl / (BrE)
the act or process of renewing or regenerating oneself or itself, especially to maintain function, vitality, or relevance
the act of renewing oneself or itself

Examples

Regular meditation can lead to a sense of self-renewal and well-being.
The organization went through a period of self-renewal to adapt to changing market conditions.
Stem cells have the unique capability of self-renewal throughout the organism's life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regeneration - the process of renewal, restoration, and growth

Etymology

From 'self-' (oneself) + 'renewal' (the act of making new again).
