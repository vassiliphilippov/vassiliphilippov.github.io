anorectal

1. ADJ

/ ˌænəˈrɛktəl / (AmE), / ˌanəˈrɛktəl / (BrE)
relating to or affecting both the anus and rectum together
of the anus and rectum
medical

Examples

The doctor recommended an anorectal examination to investigate the cause of bleeding.
There are several disorders that can affect the anorectal region, including fissures and abscesses.
Proper hygiene is important to prevent infections in the anorectal area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rectal - relating to the rectum

Etymology

From Greek 'anōs' (anus) and Latin 'rectum' (straight, rectum).
