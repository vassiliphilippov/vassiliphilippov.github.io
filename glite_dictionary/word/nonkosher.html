nonkosher

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈkoʊʃər / (AmE), / nɒnˈkəʊʃə / (BrE)
Not conforming to Jewish dietary laws; not kosher.
not conforming to Jewish dietary laws

Examples

The restaurant cannot guarantee that all its dishes are strictly nonkosher.
He avoids eating nonkosher foods for religious reasons.
All the meat served at the event turned out to be nonkosher.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: kosher - conforming to Jewish dietary laws

Etymology

Formed from 'non-' (not) + 'kosher', from Hebrew 'kasher' (fit, proper).
