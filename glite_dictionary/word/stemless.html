stemless

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɛm.lɪs / (AmE), / ˈstɛm.lɪs / (BrE)
having no stem; lacking a stem or stalk
having no stem

Examples

Many people prefer stemless wine glasses for their modern look and stability.
The stemless leaves of this plant grow directly from the root.
He carefully selected a stemless mushroom from the forest floor.

2. ADJ

/ ˈstɛm.ləs / (AmE), / ˈstɛm.ləs / (BrE)
(of plants) having no noticeable stem above the ground.
having no visible stem (botany)

Examples

The stemless violet blooms close to the forest floor in early spring.
Some species of cactus are stemless and grow directly from the rootstock.
Her garden features several stemless succulents that thrive in shallow soil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sessile - (of plants or plant parts) attached directly by the base; not having a stalk

Etymology

From 'stem' + '-less', meaning 'without a stem'.
