South American

1. NOUN

/ saʊθ əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən / (AmE), / saʊθ əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of South America
native or inhabitant of South America

Examples

Many South Americans speak both Spanish and Portuguese as part of their cultural heritage.
The festival attracted South Americans living in different parts of the world.
A renowned South American chef opened a restaurant downtown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Latin American - a person from Latin America

2. ADJ

/ saʊθ əˈmɛrɪkən / (AmE), / saʊθ əˈmɛrɪkən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of South America, its countries, or its people
of or relating to South America

Examples

Many South American countries are known for their diverse landscapes and cultures.
The restaurant serves a variety of South American dishes, including empanadas and ceviche.
She enjoys listening to South American music, especially samba and tango.
