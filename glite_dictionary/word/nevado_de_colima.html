Nevado de Colima

1. PROPN

/ neˈvɑːdoʊ deɪ koˈliːmə / (AmE), / nɛˈvɑːdəʊ deɪ kɒˈliːmə / (BrE)
an active volcano in southwestern Mexico, part of the Trans-Mexican Volcanic Belt
active volcano in southwestern Mexico

Examples

Scientists are monitoring seismic activity at Nevado de Colima due to recent eruptions.
The summit of Nevado de Colima is often covered in clouds, making it difficult to see.
Hikers are advised to stay away from Nevado de Colima when volcanic warnings are issued.

Etymology

Spanish, meaning 'Snowy Colima', from 'nevado' (snowy) and 'Colima' (the region's name).
