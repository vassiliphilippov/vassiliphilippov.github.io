workfellow

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɜrkˌfɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌfɛləʊ / (BrE)
a person with whom one works; a colleague or coworker
a person one works with

Examples

She always treated her workfellow with kindness and consideration.
When the project succeeded, every workfellow received recognition from management.
He considered his workfellow not just a colleague, but a friend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colleague - a person with whom one works, especially in a profession
  • Synonym: coworker - a fellow worker; a person one works with

Etymology

From 'work' + 'fellow', following the model of similar compounds in English.
