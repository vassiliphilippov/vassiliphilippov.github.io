Shaaban

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑːˈbɑːn / (AmE), / ʃɑːˈbɑːn / (BrE)
the eighth month of the Islamic calendar, preceding Ramadan
eighth month of the Islamic calendar

Examples

Many Muslims begin preparing for Ramadan during Shaaban.
Events held in Shaaban are often centered around acts of charity and reflection.
In the Islamic calendar, Shaaban comes just before the holy month of fasting.

Etymology

From Arabic Šaʿbān, the name of the month in the Islamic calendar.
