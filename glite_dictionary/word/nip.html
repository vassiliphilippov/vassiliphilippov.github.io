Nip

1. NOUN

/ nɪp / (AmE), / nɪp / (BrE)
(offensive slang) a highly offensive term for a person of Japanese descent.
offensive slang term for Japanese person
offensive slang

Examples

The word Nip is extremely offensive and should never be used.
He was called a Nip, which was a derogatory slur during the war.
Using terms like Nip is hurtful and unacceptable in modern society.

Etymology

Derived as an abbreviation of 'Nippon', the Japanese name for Japan.
