ngwee

1. NOUN

/ ˈŋwiː / (AmE), / ˈŋwiː / (BrE)
a monetary unit of Zambia, equal to one hundredth of a kwacha.
Zambian currency subunit

Examples

He paid five ngwees for the candy at the market.
The coin was worth only one ngwee, but it was beautifully designed.
Prices are listed in kwacha and ngwees throughout Zambia.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: kwacha - the basic monetary unit of Zambia

Etymology

From Bemba, meaning 'bright' or 'shining'.
