Nebo

1. PROPN

/ ˈniːboʊ / (AmE), / ˈniːbəʊ / (BrE)
A Babylonian god of wisdom and agriculture, regarded as the patron of scribes and schools.
Babylonian god of wisdom and agriculture

Examples

Ancient texts often mention Nebo as a central figure in Babylonian religion.
The scribes prayed to Nebo before beginning their work in the temple.
Mythology students studied the role of Nebo as the god of wisdom.
