cuticular

1. ADJ

/ kjuˈtɪkjələr / (AmE), / kjuˈtɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a cuticle or cuticula, especially in animals or plants.
relating to a cuticle
medical

Examples

The plant’s leaves are covered with a cuticular wax that reduces water loss.
Recent studies focus on cuticular changes in insects’ outer layers.
In dermatology, some disorders are linked to abnormal cuticular development.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cuticle - the outer layer of living tissue, especially around the base of fingernails or the epidermis of plants and animals

Etymology

From Latin cuticula (diminutive of cutis, skin) + -ar.
