heavyheartedness

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɛviˈhɑrtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌhɛviˈhɑːtɪdnəs / (BrE)
a feeling of deep sadness and melancholy; the state of being heavyhearted
deep sadness; melancholy

Examples

The funeral was marked by a profound heavyheartedness that lingered long after the ceremony.
He spoke with such heavyheartedness that everyone in the room could feel his pain.
Despite her heavyheartedness, she managed to smile for her children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: melancholy - a deep, persistent sadness or gloom
  • Synonym: sadness - the condition or quality of being sad
  • Antonym: cheerfulness - the state of being noticeably happy and optimistic

Etymology

Formed from 'heavyhearted' (heavy + hearted) + noun suffix '-ness'.
