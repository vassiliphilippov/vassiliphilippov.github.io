Iraq

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈrɑk / (AmE), / ɪˈrɑːk / (BrE)
a country in western Asia, located in the Middle East, known for being the site of ancient Mesopotamia
a country in the Middle East

Examples

She traveled to Iraq to study the ruins of ancient Mesopotamia.
The war in Iraq had a significant impact on world politics.
Baghdad is the capital city of Iraq.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mesopotamia - the ancient region located in modern-day Iraq

Etymology

From Arabic 'al-ʿIrāq', possibly from Sumerian 'Uruk', an ancient city in Mesopotamia.
