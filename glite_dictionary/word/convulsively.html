convulsively

1. ADV

/ kənˈvʌlsɪvli / (AmE), / kənˈvʌlsɪvli / (BrE)
In a sudden, violent, or irregular way, as if affected by convulsions.
in a convulsive or spasmodic manner

Examples

She sobbed convulsively, unable to control her emotions.
The patient's muscles jerked convulsively during the seizure.
He laughed convulsively, tears running down his face.

Synset Examples

her leg twitched convulsively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spasmodically - in a sudden or irregular manner, like a spasm

Etymology

From 'convulsive' + '-ly', ultimately from Latin 'convulsus' (torn, wrenched).
