sixteen

1. NUM

/ sɪkˈstiːn / (AmE), / sɪkˈstiːn / (BrE)
the number 16; the cardinal number that is the sum of fifteen and one
the number 16

Examples

She turned sixteen last week and got her driver's license.
The classroom has exactly sixteen desks for the students.
He counted sixteen apples in the basket.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fifteen - the number 15
  • Degree Higher: seventeen - the number 17

Etymology

From Old English 'sixtīene', formed from 'six' + '-teen' (ten).
