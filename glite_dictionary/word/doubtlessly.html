doubtlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈdaʊt.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈdaʊt.lɪs.li / (BrE)
Without doubt; certainly; undoubtedly.
certainly; without doubt

Examples

She will doubtlessly win the award for her performance.
This new technology will doubtlessly change the way we live.
He doubtlessly knew more than he was letting on.

Synset Examples

it's undoubtedly very beautiful

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undoubtedly - without doubt; certainly
  • Antonym: doubtfully - in a way that shows doubt or uncertainty
