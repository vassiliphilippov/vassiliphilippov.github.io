Pilea

1. NOUN

/ paɪˈliːə / (AmE), / pɪˈliːə / (BrE)
A genus of low-growing tropical perennial plants cultivated for their attractive, non-stinging foliage.
genus of tropical foliage plants

Examples

The Pilea is a popular houseplant because of its attractive leaves and easy care.
She filled her living room with different types of Pilea for a lush, green look.
Many gardeners recommend the Pilea for beginners due to its resilience indoors.

Etymology

From Latin 'pilea', referring to a felt cap, possibly referencing the shape of some species.
