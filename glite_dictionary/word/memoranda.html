memoranda

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛməˈrændə / (AmE), / ˌmɛm.ərˈæn.də / (BrE)
Plural form of memorandum; written messages, proposals, or reminders, especially in a formal or official context.
plural of memorandum; formal written notes

Examples

Several memoranda about the new policy were distributed to all employees last week.
The legal team reviewed all previous memoranda before drafting their report.
She archived the financial memoranda for future reference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: memorandum - a written proposal or reminder

Etymology

From Latin plural of 'memorandum', meaning something to be remembered.
