Carioca

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːriˈoʊkə / (AmE), / ˌkærɪˈəʊkə / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
native or inhabitant of Rio de Janeiro

Examples

As a proud Carioca, she loves the vibrant culture of her hometown.
The Carioca festival attracts visitors from all over the world.
Many Cariocas spend their weekends relaxing on the famous beaches of Rio de Janeiro.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'carioca', likely from the indigenous Tupi language, meaning 'house of Carijó' (a people of the Rio area).
