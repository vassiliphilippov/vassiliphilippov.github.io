but

1. ADV

/ bʌt / (AmE), / bʌt / (BrE)
used as an adverb to mean 'only' or 'no more than'
only; no more than (adverb)

Examples

There was but a single chair left in the room.
She stayed but a day before leaving again.
He is but a child, so don't be too harsh.
