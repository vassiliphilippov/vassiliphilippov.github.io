pud

1. NOUN

/ pʌd / (AmE), / pʌd / (BrE)
(British, informal) A dessert, especially the sweet course of a meal; short for 'pudding'.
(British) informal term for dessert
informal british

Examples

Would you like some pud after your meal?
Everyone saved room for pud after Sunday lunch.
What's for pud tonight?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pudding - A sweet dish served as dessert, also known as 'pud' in informal British English.

Etymology

Abbreviated form of 'pudding', common in British English.
