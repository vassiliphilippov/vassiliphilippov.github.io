assassinee

1. NOUN

/ əˌsæs.əˈni / (AmE), / əˌsæs.ɪˈni / (BrE)
a person who is the intended victim of an assassination
person targeted for assassination

Examples

The police managed to protect the assassinee before the attack could take place.
Media reports revealed that the assassinee was a prominent political leader.
Security measures were tightened after authorities identified the assassinee.

Synset Examples

He was the intended assassinee in the plot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: target - person chosen as the object of an attack or criticism
  • Antonym: assassin - a person who assassinates someone

Etymology

Formed from 'assassin' (from Arabic 'ḥashshāshīn') plus the suffix '-ee' (person affected by the action).
