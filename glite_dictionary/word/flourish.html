flourish

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɝːɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈflʌrɪʃ / (BrE)
a decorative or ornamental stroke or embellishment in handwriting or calligraphy
decorative embellishment in handwriting

Examples

She signed her name with an elegant flourish at the bottom of the letter.
The invitation featured gold calligraphy and decorative flourishes along the border.
He added a final flourish to his signature, making it unique.

Etymology

From Old French *flourir*, from Latin *florere* (to bloom), related to *flos* (flower).
