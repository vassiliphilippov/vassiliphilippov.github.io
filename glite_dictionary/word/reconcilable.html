reconcilable

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɛk.ənˈsaɪ.lə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛk.ənˈsaɪ.lə.bəl / (BrE)
Able to be brought into agreement or harmony; capable of being reconciled.
able to be brought into agreement

Examples

The two companies found their goals were reconcilable after several meetings.
Their cultural differences proved to be reconcilable with patience and understanding.
The contract terms are reconcilable with local regulations.

Synset Examples

her way of thinking is reconcilable with mine

Related Terms

  • Antonym: irreconcilable - impossible to reconcile or bring into harmony

Etymology

From Latin conciliāre (to bring together, make friendly), via reconcile + -able.
