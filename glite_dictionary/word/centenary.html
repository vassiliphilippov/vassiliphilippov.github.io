centenary

1. NOUN

/ sɛnˈtɛnɛri / (AmE), / sɛnˈtiːnəri / (BrE)
the 100th anniversary of a significant event, or the celebration marking this occasion
100th anniversary or its celebration

Examples

The museum will host special events to mark its centenary next year.
This year is the centenary of the famous writer's birth.
A centenary celebration was held to commemorate the school's founding in 1924.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: bicentenary - the 200th anniversary of an event

Etymology

From Latin 'centenarius' (containing a hundred), from 'centum' (hundred).

2. ADJ

/ sɛnˈtɛnəˌri / (AmE), / sɛnˈtiːnəri / (BrE)
relating to or marking the completion of a period of 100 years
relating to 100 years

Examples

The museum held a centenary exhibition to honor its 100 years of history.
They organized a centenary celebration to commemorate the founding of the school.
The centenary edition of the novel was published on the author's 100th birthday.

Synset Examples

centennial celebration

Related Terms

  • Synonym: centennial - relating to or marking a period of 100 years

Etymology

From Latin 'centenarius' (of a hundred), from 'centum' (hundred).
