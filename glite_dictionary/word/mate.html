mate

1. NOUN

/ meɪt / (AmE), / meɪt / (BrE)
a person's partner in marriage or a close romantic relationship
marriage partner; spouse or lover

Examples

She introduced her mate to the rest of the family at the party.
Many birds are known to stay with the same mate for life.
After moving to Australia, he met his life mate and started a family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spouse - a husband or wife
  • Synonym: partner - a person's significant other in a romantic or marital relationship

Etymology

From Middle English *mate*, probably from Middle Low German *mate* (companion), related to Old English *gemætta* (table companion).
