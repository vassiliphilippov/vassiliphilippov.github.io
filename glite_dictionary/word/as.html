A United States territory on the eastern part of the island group of Samoa in the South Pacific Ocean.
U.S. territory in the South Pacific
american

Examples

Flights to AS are limited and often require stops in Hawaii or California.
The capital of AS is Pago Pago, known for its natural harbor.
Tourism in AS focuses on beaches and traditional Samoan culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: American Samoa - United States territory in the South Pacific, encompassing the eastern Samoan islands

Etymology

Short for 'American Samoa', from English 'American' + 'Samoa'.
