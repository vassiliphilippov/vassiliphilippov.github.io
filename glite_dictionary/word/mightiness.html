mightiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪ.ti.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmaɪ.ti.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being physically strong or powerful
physical strength or power

Examples

The mightiness of the champion was evident in every match he fought.
Legends often tell of the mightiness of ancient heroes who could move mountains.
Her mightiness was admired by everyone at the gym.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strength - the quality or state of being strong
  • Antonym: weakness - lack of strength or power

Etymology

From 'mighty' + '-ness', where 'mighty' comes from Old English 'mihtig'.
