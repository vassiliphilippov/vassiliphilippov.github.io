bucket

1. VERB

/ ˈbʌkɪt / (AmE), / ˈbʌkɪt / (BrE)
to put into a bucket
put into a bucket

Examples

The workers quickly bucketed the sand to clear the road after the storm.
Please bucket the apples before bringing them into the kitchen.

3. NOUN

/ ˈbʌkət / (AmE), / ˈbʌkɪt / (BrE)
A round, open container with a handle, used for holding or carrying liquids or other materials.
round open container with handle

Examples

She filled the bucket with water from the well.
A bucket of paint was left next to the wall.
Please hand me that bucket so I can wash the car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pail - a similar round open container, often used as a synonym

Etymology

From Old English ‘buc’ (pitcher, bulging vessel).
