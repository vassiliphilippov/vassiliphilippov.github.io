etodolac

1. NOUN

/ ɛˈtoʊdəˌlæk / (AmE), / ɛˈtəʊdəlæk / (BrE)
a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to relieve pain and inflammation in conditions such as arthritis
