tropic

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrɑːpɪk / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪk / (BrE)
(of weather or climate) hot and humid; characteristic of the tropics.
hot and humid like the tropics (weather)

Examples

The region is known for its tropic storms in the summer months.
We experienced a tropic heat wave during our entire stay.
The air was so tropic that it felt like walking in a steam room.

Synset Examples

tropical weather

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tropical - hot and humid; relating to the tropics

Etymology

From Old French *tropique*, from Latin *tropicus* (“of the solstice”), from Greek *tropikos* (“of turning”).

2. ADJ

/ ˈtrɑːpɪk / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the tropics, the region near the equator between the Tropic of Cancer and the Tropic of Capricorn.
of or relating to the tropics

Examples

The climate in tropic regions is hot and humid throughout the year.
Many exotic plants are native to tropic environments.
She studied the diverse wildlife found in tropic rainforests.

Synset Examples

tropical islands
tropical fruit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tropical - relating to or characteristic of the tropics

Etymology

From Latin tropicus (of the solstice, of turning), from Greek tropikos (of a turn, of the solstice).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02452975-s
Synset POS: s

3. NOUN

/ ˈtrɑːpɪk / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪk / (BrE)
Either of the two parallels of latitude, about 23.5° north or south of the equator (Tropic of Cancer and Tropic of Capricorn), which mark the furthest points at which the sun can appear directly overhead.
either of two latitude lines near the equator

Examples

The tropic of Capricorn marks the southernmost point where the sun can be overhead.
Ancient navigators often referenced the tropic of Cancer during their sea voyages.
The desert falls just above the northern tropic, keeping its climate hot year-round.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tropics - the region of Earth between the Tropic of Cancer and the Tropic of Capricorn
  • Degree Higher: Tropic of Cancer - the northern tropic at approximately 23.5° north latitude
  • Degree Lower: Tropic of Capricorn - the southern tropic at approximately 23.5° south latitude

Etymology

From Greek 'tropikos' (of turning), via Latin 'tropicus'.

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: n
