unconsidered

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkənˈsɪdərd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈsɪdəd / (BrE)
(of actions, words, or opinions) given or done without careful thought or reflection.
done without careful thought

Examples

Her remarks were unconsidered and hurt several people in the group.
Making an unconsidered decision led to several unintended consequences for the company.
He apologized for his unconsidered comments during the meeting.

Synset Examples

slovenly inconsiderate reasoning
unconsidered words
prejudice is the holding of unconsidered opinions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtless - lacking consideration or attention; careless
  • Synonym: careless - not giving enough attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors
  • Antonym: considered - thought about carefully before making a decision

Etymology

From un- (not) + considered (past participle of consider).
