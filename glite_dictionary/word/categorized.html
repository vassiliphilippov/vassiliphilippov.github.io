categorized

1. ADJ

/ ˈkæt̬əɡəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ˈkætəɡəˌraɪzd / (BrE)
arranged or placed into categories or groups based on shared characteristics.
arranged into categories

Examples

Books in the library are categorized by subject to make them easier to find.
All the survey responses were categorized according to age group.
The museum’s collection is categorized into paintings, sculptures, and artifacts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to classify - to arrange into groups based on similarities

Etymology

From verb 'categorize', which comes from Greek 'katēgoria' (accusation, category).
