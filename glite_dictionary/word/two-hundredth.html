two-hundredth

1. ADJ

/ tuː ˈhʌndrədθ / (AmE), / tuː ˈhʌndrədθ / (BrE)
being number 200 in a sequence; the ordinal form of two hundred.
ordinal number: 200th

Examples

He finished in two-hundredth place in the marathon.
This is the two-hundredth anniversary of the town's founding.
She made her two-hundredth appearance on stage last night.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: hundredth - ordinal number: 100th
  • Degree Higher: three-hundredth - ordinal number: 300th
