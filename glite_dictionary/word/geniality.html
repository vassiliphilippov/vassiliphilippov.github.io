geniality

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒiːniˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːniˈælɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being friendly, cheerful, and easy to talk to
friendly and cheerful disposition

Examples

Her geniality made everyone feel welcome at the party.
You could sense the geniality in his voice as he greeted us.
Despite his position of power, his geniality made him approachable to employees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly
  • Antonym: hostility - unfriendliness or opposition

Etymology

From Latin 'genialis' (pleasant, festive), from 'genius' (spirit).
