Ercilla

1. NOUN

/ ɛrˈsiːljə / (AmE), / ɛˈsɪljə / (BrE)
A genus of evergreen climbing plants native to South America, belonging to the family Phytolaccaceae.
genus of evergreen climbing plants

Examples

The Ercilla genus includes several species of ornamental evergreen climbers.
Botanists studied the growth patterns of Ercilla in its native environment.
Some gardens feature Ercilla plants for their attractive foliage and climbing ability.

Etymology

Named after Alonso de Ercilla, a 16th-century Spanish poet.
