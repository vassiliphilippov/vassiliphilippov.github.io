unregulated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈrɛɡjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈrɛɡjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not controlled or supervised by rules, laws, or regulations.
not controlled by rules or laws

Examples

The market was largely unregulated before the government stepped in.
They worry about the risks of unregulated herbal medicines sold online.
Unregulated hunting has seriously threatened the survival of this species.

Synset Examples

unregulated off-shore fishing

Related Terms

  • Antonym: regulated - controlled or overseen by rules or laws

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'regulated', from Latin 'regula' (rule).

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈrɛɡjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈrɛɡjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not controlled or governed by rules, laws, or regulations; lacking oversight or supervision.
not controlled by rules or laws

Examples

The rapid growth of an unregulated market can lead to significant risks for consumers.
In an unregulated industry, companies may prioritize profit over safety.
The report highlights the dangers of unregulated online content.

Synset Examples

an unregulated environment

Related Terms

  • Antonym: regulated - controlled or governed by rules or regulations

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'regulated', from Latin 'regula' (rule).
