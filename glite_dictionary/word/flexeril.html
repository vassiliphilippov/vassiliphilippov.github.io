Flexeril

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɛk.səˌrɪl / (AmE), / ˈflɛk.səˌrɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for cyclobenzaprine, a muscle relaxant used to treat muscle spasms and acute musculoskeletal conditions.
brand name for a muscle relaxant drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Flexeril to help manage her lower back spasms.
After injuring his shoulder, he was given Flexeril to reduce the muscle pain.
Some people experience drowsiness as a side effect of Flexeril.
