uncharted

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtʃɑːrtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɑːtɪd / (BrE)
(of a place or area) not yet surveyed, mapped, or explored; unknown.
not surveyed or explored

Examples

The scientists set sail into uncharted waters, eager to make new discoveries.
They found themselves lost in the uncharted forests of the Amazon.
Launching a business in this emerging industry feels like entering uncharted territory.

Synset Examples

uncharted seas

Related Terms

  • Antonym: explored - surveyed or investigated
  • Antonym: charted - mapped or plotted on a chart
  • Synonym: unknown - not known or familiar

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + charted (mapped or recorded on a chart).
