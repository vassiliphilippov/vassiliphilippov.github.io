prickteaser

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪkˌtiːzər / (AmE), / ˈprɪkˌtiːzə / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) A woman who arouses men's sexual interest but refuses their advances, often to exploit or frustrate them.
offensive slang for a sexually teasing woman
slang offensive vulgar

Examples

He cruelly called her a prickteaser after she turned him down at the party.
Tom warned his friend not to fall for a prickteaser who would play with his emotions.
The movie was criticized for portraying the main character as a stereotypical prickteaser.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tease - a person who arouses hope, interest, or desire and then withholds satisfaction

Etymology

Formed from 'prick' (slang for penis) and 'teaser'.
