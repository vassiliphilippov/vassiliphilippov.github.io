lightwood

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪtˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtˌwʊd / (BrE)
a tall Australian acacia tree that produces highly valued dark timber, especially used for furniture and fine woodworking
Australian acacia yielding valuable timber

Examples

The furniture was crafted from rare lightwood native to Australia.
Carpenters seek out lightwood for its durability and attractive grain.
The lightwood acacia is protected in some regions due to overharvesting.
