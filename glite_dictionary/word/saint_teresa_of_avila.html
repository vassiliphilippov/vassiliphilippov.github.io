Saint Teresa of Avila

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt təˈriːzə əv ˈævɪlə / (AmE), / seɪnt təˈriːzə əv ˈævɪlə / (BrE)
(1515–1582) A Spanish mystic, Carmelite nun, religious reformer, and author, recognized as a saint in the Roman Catholic Church.
Spanish mystic and Catholic saint (1515–1582)

Examples

Saint Teresa of Avila is widely regarded as one of the great Christian mystics.
The writings of Saint Teresa of Avila continue to inspire many spiritual seekers today.
During her lifetime, Saint Teresa of Avila reformed the Carmelite order and wrote influential spiritual classics.
