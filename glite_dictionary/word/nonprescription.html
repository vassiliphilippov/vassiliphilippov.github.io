nonprescription

1. ADJ

/ nɑn.prɪˈskrɪp.ʃən / (AmE), / nɒn.prɪˈskrɪp.ʃən / (BrE)
(of a drug or product) purchasable without a doctor's prescription.
purchasable without prescription

Examples

This medication is available as a nonprescription drug at most pharmacies.
Many nonprescription remedies are effective for mild headaches.
The pharmacist recommended a nonprescription allergy medication.

Synset Examples

nonprescription drugs
an over-the-counter cold remedy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: over-the-counter - (of a drug) available for purchase without a prescription

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) and 'prescription' (a doctor's written order for medication).
