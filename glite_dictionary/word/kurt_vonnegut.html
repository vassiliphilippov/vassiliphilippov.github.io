Kurt Vonnegut

1. PROPN

/ kɝt ˈvɑnəɡət / (AmE), / kɜːt ˈvɒnəɡət / (BrE)
(1922–2007) United States writer known for novels and short stories blending realism, satire, and science fiction.
American novelist and satirist
