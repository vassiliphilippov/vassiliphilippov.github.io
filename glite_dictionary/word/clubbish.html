clubbish

1. ADJ

/ ˈklʌb.ɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈklʌb.ɪʃ / (BrE)
(of a group or atmosphere) friendly and sociable in the manner of a private club, often with a sense of exclusivity.
sociable and exclusive like a club

Examples

The board meetings had a clubbish atmosphere, with everyone on a first-name basis.
He found the restaurant too clubbish and exclusive for his taste.
Their clubbish group rarely welcomed newcomers.

Synset Examples

a clubbish set
we got rather clubby

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clubby - friendly and sociable in the manner of a social club

Etymology

From 'club' (social organization) + '-ish' (having the characteristics of).
