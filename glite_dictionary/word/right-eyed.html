right-eyed

1. ADJ

/ ˈraɪt ˌaɪd / (AmE), / ˈraɪt ˌaɪd / (BrE)
Having only the right eye.
having only the right eye

Examples

After the accident, he became right-eyed due to the loss of vision in his left eye.
Certain fish species are right-eyed, with both eyes located on the right side of their bodies.
The right-eyed condition can occur from congenital defects or injuries.

Related Terms
