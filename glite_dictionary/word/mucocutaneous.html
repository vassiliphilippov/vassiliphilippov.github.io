mucocutaneous

1. ADJ

/ ˌmjuːkoʊkjuːˈteɪniəs / (AmE), / ˌmjuːkəʊkjuːˈteɪniəs / (BrE)
Relating to both the mucous membranes and the skin.
relating to mucous membranes and skin
medical

Examples

The patient exhibited a mucocutaneous rash during the allergic reaction.
Certain autoimmune disorders primarily affect mucocutaneous tissues.
Doctors examined the mucocutaneous junctions for signs of disease.

Etymology

From Latin 'mucosa' (mucous membrane) + 'cutaneous' (of the skin).
