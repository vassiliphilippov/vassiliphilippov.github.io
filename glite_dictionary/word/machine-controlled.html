machine-controlled

1. ADJ

/ məˈʃin kənˈtroʊld / (AmE), / məˈʃiːn kənˈtrəʊld / (BrE)
Operated or managed by machines, especially with little or no human intervention; automated.
operated by machines; automated

Examples

The assembly line became much faster after it was machine-controlled.
Many modern vehicles feature machine-controlled braking systems for improved safety.
The grain elevator uses a machine-controlled conveyor to move crops efficiently.

Synset Examples

an automated stoker

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automated - operated automatically, especially by machines
