short-eared

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɔrt ˌɪrd / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt ˌɪəd / (BrE)
Having short ears, especially in relation to others of the same kind.
having short ears

Examples

The short-eared owl is found in grasslands around the world.
Many rabbits in the region are short-eared compared to their relatives.
She adopted a short-eared dog from the shelter.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: long-eared - having long ears
