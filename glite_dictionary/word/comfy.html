comfy

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌm.fi / (AmE), / ˈkʌm.fi / (BrE)
Providing physical comfort; allowing or causing you to feel physically relaxed and free from pain or discomfort. Informal usage of 'comfortable'.
informal; comfortable, cozy
informal

Examples

The blanket was so comfy that I fell asleep instantly.
She wore her most comfy sweater on the chilly morning.
This chair isn't as comfy as it looks.
