Singhalese

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɪŋɡəˈliːz / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋɡəˈliːz / (BrE)
of or relating to the Sinhalese people of Sri Lanka
relating to Sinhalese people of Sri Lanka

Examples

The Singhalese communities celebrate their cultural heritage each year.
He studied Singhalese traditions for his anthropology thesis.
The Singhalese language is widely spoken in Sri Lanka.

Synset Examples

The Sinhalese are the main ethnic group of Sri Lanka

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sinhalese - of or relating to the Sinhalese people (alternative spelling)

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Singalês', from Sanskrit 'Siṃhala' (Sri Lanka).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03129718-a
Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ ˌsɪŋɡəˈliːz / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋɡəˈliːz / (BrE)
a member of the Sinhalese ethnic group native to Sri Lanka
member of an ethnic group of Sri Lanka

Examples

Many Singhalese celebrate the New Year with traditional festivals.
The Singhalese make up the majority population in Sri Lanka.
She studied the history and culture of the Singhalese people during her trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sinhalese - member of the Sinhalese ethnic group native to Sri Lanka

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Siṃhalēsaḥ', meaning 'Lion Islander', referring to Sri Lanka.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09751461-n
Synset POS: n

3. NOUN

/ ˌsɪŋɡəˈliːz / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋɡəˈliːz / (BrE)
the Indic language spoken principally by the Sinhalese people of Sri Lanka.
language of the Sinhalese people of Sri Lanka

Examples

Many Sri Lankans speak Singhalese as their first language.
She is taking a course to learn Singhalese before visiting Colombo.
The document was written in both English and Singhalese for wider accessibility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sinhalese - the language spoken by the Sinhalese people of Sri Lanka; alternate spelling

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Siṃhala', the historical name for Sri Lanka, plus English '-ese'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06984401-n
Synset POS: n

relating to the Sinhalese language or its speakers
relating to the Sinhalese language

Examples

She is studying the Singhalese language at university.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sinhalese - relating to the Sinhalese language or people

Variant of Sinhalese, from Sanskrit *Siṃhala* (ancient name for Sri Lanka) + English adjectival suffix.
