cosmetologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːzməˈtɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌkɒzməˈtɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person trained and licensed to provide professional cosmetic treatments to the hair, skin, and nails
person trained in cosmetic treatments

Examples

After graduating from beauty school, she found a job as a cosmetologist in an upscale salon.
A licensed cosmetologist can advise clients on skin care and makeup techniques.
Many people rely on a trusted cosmetologist for special occasions like weddings and proms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beautician - person whose job is to improve the appearance of people’s hair, face, and skin

Etymology

From cosmetic + -logist, patterned after dermatologist.
