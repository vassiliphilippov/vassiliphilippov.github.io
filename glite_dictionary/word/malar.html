malar

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪlər / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.lɑː / (BrE)
the arch of bone beneath the eye that forms the prominence of the cheek; the cheekbone
the cheekbone; bone below the eye
medical

Examples

The doctor examined the patient's malar for fractures after the accident.
Swelling was noticeable over the left malar following the injury.
The sculpture emphasized the high malar regions of the face.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zygomatic bone - the anatomical term for the cheekbone

Etymology

From Latin 'mala' (cheek).
