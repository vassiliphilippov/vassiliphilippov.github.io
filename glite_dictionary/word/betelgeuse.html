Betelgeuse

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɛtəlˌdʒuːs, ˈbiːtəlˌdʒuːs / (AmE), / ˈbiːtəldʒuːz, ˈbɛtəlˌdʒuːz / (BrE)
A bright red supergiant star, the second brightest in the constellation Orion; also known as Alpha Orionis.
bright red star in Orion constellation

Examples

Astronomers observed that Betelgeuse was dimming unexpectedly last year.
The constellation Orion is easy to identify by the bright star Betelgeuse at its shoulder.
Researchers use Betelgeuse as a reference point for studying supergiant stars.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Rigel - the brightest star in Orion

Etymology

From Arabic 'bayt al-jawzā’' (house of the central one).
