snappish

1. ADJ

/ ˈsnæpɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsnæpɪʃ / (BrE)
tending to speak in an irritable or abrupt manner, especially when easily annoyed
irritable or curt in speech

Examples

She answered in a snappish tone that surprised everyone in the meeting.
His snappish remarks made it clear he was in a bad mood.
After a long, stressful day, she became noticeably more snappish with her coworkers.

Synset Examples

a snappish tone of voice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irritable - easily annoyed or angered
  • Antonym: courteous - polite, respectful, and considerate in speech or behavior

Etymology

From 'snap' (to speak sharply) + '-ish', English suffix forming adjectives.
