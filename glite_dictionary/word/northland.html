northland

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrθ.lænd / (AmE), / ˈnɔːθ.lənd / (BrE)
a region or area located in or toward the north
northern region or area

Examples

The explorers set out for the northland in search of new territories.
Legends tell of great beasts that once roamed the northland.
Winter arrives early in the northland, blanketing the forests in snow.

Etymology

From north + land, meaning 'land to the north'.
