undefended

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.dɪˈfɛn.dɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈfɛn.dɪd / (BrE)
Not protected or guarded; lacking defense against attack or criticism.
not defended or protected

Examples

The small village was left completely undefended during the raid.
She felt undefended against the harsh criticism from her peers.
The castle gates lay undefended after the guards fled.

Synset Examples

an open city
open to attack

Related Terms

  • Antonym: defended - protected or guarded
  • Synonym: unprotected - not protected or shielded

Etymology

From un- (not) + defended (protected).
