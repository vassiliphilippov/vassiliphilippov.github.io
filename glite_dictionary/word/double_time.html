double time

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌb.əl taɪm / (AmE), / ˈdʌb.əl taɪm / (BrE)
a fast marching pace, typically at about 180 steps per minute, or a slow jog, especially in the military.
fast marching pace or slow jog

Examples

The soldiers broke into double time as they crossed the bridge.
We had to march at double time to reach the camp before nightfall.
At the command, everyone switched from walking to double time through the field.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: quick march - standard marching pace in the military
