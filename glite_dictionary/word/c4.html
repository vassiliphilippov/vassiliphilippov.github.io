C4

1. NOUN

/ si fɔːr / (AmE), / siː fɔː / (BrE)
an internationally recognized envelope size measuring 229 x 324 millimetres, designed to fit unfolded A4 paper
envelope size, 229 x 324 mm

Examples

Please use a C4 envelope if you need to send the document unfolded.
The forms arrive in C4 envelopes to prevent creasing.
She bought a pack of C4 envelopes for her office supplies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: A4 - common international paper size measuring 210 x 297 mm

Etymology

From the ISO 269 international standard for envelope sizes, related to ISO 216 paper sizes.
