lopsidedness

1. NOUN

/ lɑpˈsaɪdɪdnəs / (AmE), / lɒpˈsaɪdɪdnɪs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being uneven or having one side lower, larger, or different than the other.
the quality of being uneven or asymmetrical

Examples

The lopsidedness of the painting made it stand out from the others in the gallery.
Critics pointed out the lopsidedness in the team's performance during the season.
She adjusted the stack of books to correct its lopsidedness on the shelf.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asymmetry - lack of equality or equivalence between parts or aspects
  • Antonym: symmetry - the quality of being made up of exactly similar parts facing each other
