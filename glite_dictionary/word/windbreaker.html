windbreaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪndˌbreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈwɪndˌbreɪkə / (BrE)
A lightweight jacket designed to resist wind and light rain.
light jacket for wind or rain

Examples

She grabbed her windbreaker before heading out for a brisk walk.
A windbreaker is perfect for unpredictable spring weather.
He folded his windbreaker and put it in his backpack, just in case it got chilly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anorak - a waterproof jacket, typically with a hood

Etymology

From 'wind' + 'breaker', indicating something that breaks (blocks) the wind.
