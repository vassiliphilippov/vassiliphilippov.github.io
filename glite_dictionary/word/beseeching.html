beseeching

1. ADJ

/ bəˈsitʃɪŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈsiːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a look, tone, etc.) expressing an urgent and earnest request; pleading or begging.
expressing a pleading or begging request

Examples

She gave him a beseeching look, silently asking for forgiveness.
His beseeching voice made it hard to refuse his request.
The child stood by the door with beseeching eyes, hoping to be allowed outside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pleading - expressing an emotional or urgent request
  • Synonym: imploring - earnestly asking or begging for something
  • Antonym: demanding - insistently requesting or asking for something

Etymology

From the verb 'beseech', from Middle English 'besechen', from Old English 'besēcan' (to seek, ask).
