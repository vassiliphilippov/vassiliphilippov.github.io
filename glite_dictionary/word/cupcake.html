cupcake

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌp.keɪk / (AmE), / ˈkʌp.keɪk / (BrE)
A small, individual cake baked in a cup-shaped mold, typically topped with icing or decorations.
small individual cake, often iced

Examples

She baked a dozen cupcakes for the school bake sale.
Each cupcake was decorated with colorful sprinkles and a swirl of icing.
Would you like a chocolate or vanilla cupcake with your coffee?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muffin - a small, round baked good similar in size to a cupcake but less sweet

Etymology

From 'cup' and 'cake', referring to cakes baked in cups or cup-shaped molds.
