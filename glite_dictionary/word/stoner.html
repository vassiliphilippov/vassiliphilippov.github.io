stoner

1. NOUN

/ ˈstoʊnər / (AmE), / ˈstəʊnə / (BrE)
a person who throws stones at another, especially with the intent to injure or kill
person who throws stones at others

Examples

The stoner was apprehended by the authorities after the attack.
In the story, the victim pleads for mercy as the stoner approaches.
Historically, a stoner would often be part of a larger group carrying out executions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stoning - the act of throwing stones at someone as a form of punishment or attack
