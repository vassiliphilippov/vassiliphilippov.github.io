Ho Chi Minh City

1. PROPN

/ hoʊ tʃiː mɪn ˈsɪti / (AmE), / həʊ tʃiː mɪn ˈsɪti / (BrE)
the largest city in Vietnam, formerly known as Saigon, situated in the south of the country and known for its pivotal role in Vietnamese history
the largest city in southern Vietnam, formerly Saigon

Examples

Many tourists visit Ho Chi Minh City for its vibrant markets and French colonial architecture.
After living in Ho Chi Minh City for five years, she became fluent in Vietnamese.
The war museum in Ho Chi Minh City attracts visitors interested in Vietnam's history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Saigon - former name of Ho Chi Minh City

Etymology

Renamed in 1976 after Hồ Chí Minh, the revolutionary leader; formerly called Saigon.
