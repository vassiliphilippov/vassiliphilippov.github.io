Gothenburg

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡɑːθənˌbɜːrɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡɒθənˌbɜːɡ / (BrE)
a port city in southwestern Sweden, the country's second largest city
city in southwestern Sweden

Examples

The team traveled to Gothenburg to participate in an international football tournament.
Shipping industries play a vital role in the economy of Gothenburg.
She spent a summer living in Gothenburg while learning Swedish.

Etymology

English form of Swedish Göteborg, from the Swedish royal dynasty and 'borg' meaning 'fortress'.
