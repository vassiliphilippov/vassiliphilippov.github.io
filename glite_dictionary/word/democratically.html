democratically

1. ADV

/ dəmɑːˈkrætɪkli / (AmE), / dɪˌmɒkrəˈtɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that is based on, characterized by, or consistent with democratic principles or processes.
in a democratic manner

Examples

The committee decided democratically to allocate the resources equally among the members.
All officials in the organization are democratically elected every three years.
The new policy was introduced democratically, after open debate and a majority vote.

Synset Examples

it was decided democratically
democratically elected government

Related Terms

  • Antonym: autocratically - in an autocratic manner, without democratic processes

Etymology

From 'democratic' + -ally (adverbial suffix), ultimately from Greek 'dēmos' (people).
