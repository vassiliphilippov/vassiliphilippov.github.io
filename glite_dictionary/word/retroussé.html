retroussé

1. ADJ

/ ɹeɪˈtruːseɪ / (AmE), / ʁe.tʁu.seɪ / (BrE)
(of a person's nose) turned up at the tip in an attractive or noticeable way.
turned-up (usually of a nose)

Examples

She had a charming retroussé nose that made her face memorable.
The artist painted the child with a delicate retroussé nose and bright eyes.
His retroussé nose gave him an impish, youthful look.

Synset Examples

a retrousse nose
a small upturned nose

Related Terms

  • Synonym: upturned - turned or pointed upward, especially used of noses
  • Antonym: aquiline - (of a nose) hooked or curved like an eagle's beak

Etymology

From French "retroussé," past participle of "retrousser" (to turn up, tuck up).
