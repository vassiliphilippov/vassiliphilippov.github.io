Sooner

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːnər / (AmE), / ˈsuːnə / (BrE)
a native or resident of Oklahoma, especially in reference to the state's historic nickname
person from Oklahoma

Examples

He grew up in Tulsa and is proud to call himself a Sooner.
The Sooners have a long tradition in college football.
Many Sooners celebrate the state's unique history every year.

Etymology

From U.S. history, referring to settlers who entered Oklahoma Territory before it was officially opened for settlement in 1889.
