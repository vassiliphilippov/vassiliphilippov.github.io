trinketry

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɪŋkətɹi / (AmE), / ˈtrɪŋkətri / (BrE)
small decorative items or ornaments worn together, especially as part of dress
assorted trinkets and ornaments for dress

Examples

She adorned herself with all the trinketry she could find in her jewelry box.
The shop was filled with colorful scarves, hats, and trinketry for tourists.
His costume sparkled with feathers and an array of trinketry hanging from every seam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trinket - a small decorative object, especially worn as jewelry or an ornament
