anecdotist

1. NOUN

/ ˌænɪkˈdoʊtɪst / (AmE), / ˌænɪkˈdəʊtɪst / (BrE)
a person who is skilled in telling anecdotes
a person skilled in telling anecdotes

Examples

As an accomplished anecdotist, he kept the dinner party enthralled with his stories.
The writer gained fame not only as a novelist but also as a brilliant anecdotist.
Every organization seems to have at least one talented anecdotist who can lighten the mood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storyteller - a person who tells stories

Etymology

From anecdote + -ist, ultimately from Greek anekdota (unpublished items).
