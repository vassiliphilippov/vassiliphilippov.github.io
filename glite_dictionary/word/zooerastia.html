zooerastia

1. NOUN

/ ˌzoʊəˈræstiə / (AmE), / ˌzəʊəˈræstɪə / (BrE)
sexual activity between a human and an animal
sexual activity with animals
medical

Examples

The law strictly prohibits zooerastia and places severe penalties on those convicted of it.
Medical texts sometimes reference zooerastia as a form of sexual disorder.
The article discussed the historical views on zooerastia in different cultures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bestiality - sexual activity between a person and an animal

Etymology

From New Latin, combining Greek zōion (animal) and erastēs (lover).
