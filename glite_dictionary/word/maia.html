Maia

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪə / (AmE), / ˈmaɪə / (BrE)
A genus of spider crabs that is the type genus of the family Majidae, found nearly worldwide.
genus of spider crabs

Examples

The genus Maia includes several species of spider crabs found in different oceans.
Researchers identified a new crustacean closely related to Maia in the North Atlantic.
Specimens belonging to Maia are often encountered by marine biologists studying coastal ecosystems.

Etymology

From Latin Maia (a Roman goddess), used in taxonomy for genera.
