aesthetical

1. ADJ

/ ɛsˈθɛtɪkəl / (AmE), / iːsˈθɛtɪkəl / (BrE)
concerned with or relating to the appreciation of beauty or good taste.
relating to beauty or good taste

Examples

The new gallery embraces an aesthetical approach to interior design.
Her aesthetical sensibility influenced the way she arranged the flowers.
This building stands out due to its aesthetical harmony with the environment.

Synset Examples

the aesthetic faculties
an aesthetic person
aesthetic feeling
the illustrations made the book an aesthetic success

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aesthetic - concerned with beauty or appreciation of beauty

Etymology

From Greek aisthētikos (perceptive, sensitive), via aesthetic + -al suffix.
