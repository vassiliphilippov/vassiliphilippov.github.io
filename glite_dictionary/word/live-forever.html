live-forever

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪv fəˈrɛvər / (AmE), / ˈlɪv fəˈrɛvə / (BrE)
a perennial plant of northern temperate regions, with toothed leaves and heads of small purplish-white flowers, especially from the genus Sempervivum or related groups
perennial plant with toothed leaves and purplish-white flowers

Examples

Many gardeners choose live-forever for its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
The live-forever produces clusters of small purplish-white flowers each summer.
A hardy live-forever can survive in rocky soil and cold climates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: houseleek - a type of succulent perennial often called live-forever
  • Synonym: Sempervivum - genus of plants commonly called live-forever or houseleek
