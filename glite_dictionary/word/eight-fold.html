eight-fold

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪtˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈeɪtˌfəʊld / (BrE)
having eight parts, units, or elements; being eight times as much or as many.
having eight parts or being eight times as much

Examples

Profits have increased eight-fold over the past decade.
The new system boosted efficiency eight-fold compared to the old process.
Membership grew eight-fold after the club lowered its fees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eight - the number 8
  • Synonym: eightfold - an alternative spelling, also means eight times as much

Etymology

Formed from 'eight' + 'fold', modelled after Old English patterns for multiples (e.g., manifold, twofold).
