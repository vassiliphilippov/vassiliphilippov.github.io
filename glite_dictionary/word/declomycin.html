Declomycin

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɛk.loʊˈmaɪ.sɪn / (AmE), / ˌdɛk.ləʊˈmaɪ.sɪn / (BrE)
A trademark for demeclocycline, an antibiotic in the tetracycline class used to treat certain bacterial and rickettsial infections.
antibiotic (demeclocycline) trade name
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Declomycin to combat the stubborn bacterial infection.
Patients treated with Declomycin should follow the dosage instructions carefully to avoid resistance.
Because of its effectiveness, Declomycin is sometimes chosen for infections unresponsive to other antibiotics.
