to particularize

1. VERB

/ pərˈtɪkjələrˌaɪz / (AmE), / pəˈtɪkjʊləraɪz / (BrE)
to mention or describe someone or something specifically and in detail
describe in detail; specify

Examples

The report failed to particularize the methods used in the experiment.
She asked him to particularize his objections to the proposal.
Could you particularize exactly where the error occurred?

Synset Examples

Could you please specify your criticism of my paper?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to specify - to state something clearly and exactly

Etymology

From Medieval Latin particularizare, from Latin particularis (of a part) + -ize.
