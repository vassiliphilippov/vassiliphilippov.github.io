unshoed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈʃud / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃuːd / (BrE)
Not wearing shoes or not fitted with shoes, especially referring to horses.
not wearing shoes; not shod

Examples

The horse ran swiftly across the field, completely unshoed.
He walked barefoot, his feet unshoed and dusty from the road.
Many wild ponies remain unshoed throughout their lives, unlike domesticated ones.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: shod - wearing shoes; fitted with shoes (especially horseshoes)

Etymology

From un- (not) + shod (past participle of 'shoe').
