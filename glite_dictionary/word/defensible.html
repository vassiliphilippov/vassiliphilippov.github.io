defensible

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈfɛnsəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛnsɪbəl / (BrE)
Capable of being defended against attack or criticism.
able to be defended

Examples

The lawyer argued passionately that his client’s actions were entirely defensible.
Their position on the hill was strategically defensible against enemy forces.
Some of his choices were questionable, but others were clearly defensible given the circumstances.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: indefensible - not able to be defended
  • Synonym: tenable - able to be held or maintained against attack

Etymology

From Latin defendere (to defend) + -ible (able to be).
