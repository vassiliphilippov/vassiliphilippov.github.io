tenaciously

1. ADV

/ təˈneɪʃəsli / (AmE), / tɪˈneɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a determined and persistent manner; with unyielding firmness or perseverance.
with stubborn determination

Examples

Despite repeated failures, she worked tenaciously toward her goal.
The weed clung tenaciously to the cracks in the pavement.
He defended his beliefs tenaciously in every discussion.

Synset Examples

he pursued her doggedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doggedly - in a manner that shows grim persistence
  • Synonym: persistently - in a continuous or relentless way
  • Synonym: resolutely - in an admirably purposeful, determined, and unwavering manner

Etymology

From Latin 'tenax' (holding fast) + English adverbial suffix '-ly'.
