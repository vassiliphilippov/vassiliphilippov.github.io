caroling

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛrə.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkær.əl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of singing joyful religious songs, especially Christmas carols, typically in a group
singing religious songs at Christmas

Examples

The children spent Christmas Eve caroling in their neighborhood.
A group of friends went caroling from house to house.
Every year, the school choir participates in traditional caroling at the town square.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carol - a religious folk song, especially one sung at Christmas

Etymology

From 'carol', ultimately from Old French carole (a circle dance or song).
