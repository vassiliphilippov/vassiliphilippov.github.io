St. George's

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt ˈʤɔrdʒɪz / (AmE), / sənt ˈʤɔːdʒɪz / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Grenada, located on the island's southwest coast
capital city of Grenada

Examples

We spent a week exploring St. George's and its historic waterfront.
The cruise ship docked early in the morning at St. George's.
Many tourists visit St. George's for its beautiful views and local markets.

Etymology

Named after Saint George, a Christian martyr and England's patron saint.
