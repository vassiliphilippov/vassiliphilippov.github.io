lanset

1. NOUN

/ ˈlænˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈlænˌsɛt / (BrE)
A tart yellow fruit resembling a berry, native to East India.
East Indian tart yellow fruit

Examples

The market was selling several baskets of fresh lanset from India.
She tasted the lanset and found it refreshingly tart.
They use lanset to make a traditional East Indian preserve.
