unconfessed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkənˈfɛst / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈfɛst / (BrE)
not admitted or acknowledged; not having been confessed, especially of faults or sins.
not admitted or acknowledged

Examples

He was burdened by many unconfessed sins throughout his life.
The novel explores the consequences of unconfessed guilt.
Her unconfessed love for him was obvious to everyone but him.

Synset Examples

unconfessed sins

Related Terms

  • Antonym: confessed - that has been admitted or acknowledged

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'confessed', from 'confess' (admit, acknowledge).
