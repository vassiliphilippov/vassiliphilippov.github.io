tensionless

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛnʃənləs / (AmE), / ˈtɛnʃ(ə)nləs / (BrE)
free from tension; not subject to strain or stress
free from tension

Examples

The rope remained tensionless even after being pulled at both ends.
A tensionless environment helps students to focus better on their studies.
In certain conditions, the membrane can be considered tensionless for scientific calculations.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tense - stretched tight; under tension

Etymology

Formed from 'tension' + suffix '-less', denoting absence of tension.
