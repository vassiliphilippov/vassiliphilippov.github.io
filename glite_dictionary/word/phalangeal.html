phalangeal

1. ADJ

/ fəˈlændʒiəl / (AmE), / fəˈlændʒiəl / (BrE)
Relating to the phalanges, the bones of the fingers or toes.
of the bones in fingers or toes
medical

Examples

The patient suffered a phalangeal fracture during the soccer game.
X-rays showed abnormalities in the phalangeal joints of her right hand.
Proper alignment of the phalangeal bones is crucial for full mobility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phalange - a bone of a finger or toe

Etymology

From New Latin *phalangialis*, from Greek *phalanx* (line of battle; bone of a finger or toe).
