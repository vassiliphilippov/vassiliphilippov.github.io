cabron

1. NOUN

/ kəˈbroʊn / (AmE), / kaˈbrɒn / (BrE)
A slang insult used to describe someone as foolish, clumsy, or sometimes untrustworthy; originally from Spanish, and often considered offensive.
slang insult meaning fool or jerk
slang offensive

Examples

He got angry when someone called him a cabron in front of his friends.
She laughed and called him a cabron after he tripped over the curb.
In the movie, the villain repeatedly calls his rivals cabron during heated arguments.

Synset Examples

She laughed and called him a cabron for being late.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - an annoying, stupid, or mean person (slang)
  • Synonym: idiot - a person of low intelligence or poor judgment

Etymology

Spanish 'cabrón' (male goat, rascal, bastard), used as a derogatory term.
