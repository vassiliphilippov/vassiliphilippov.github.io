auklet

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːklɪt / (AmE), / ˈɔːklɪt / (BrE)
a small seabird of the auk family found along the northern Pacific coasts
small northern Pacific seabird

Examples

A colony of auklets was spotted nesting on the rocky cliffs.
The auklet is known for its small size and strong diving abilities.
Conservation efforts are helping to protect auklets from habitat loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auk - a seabird of the Alcidae family, including puffins and similar birds
