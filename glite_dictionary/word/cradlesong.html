cradlesong

1. NOUN

/ ˈkreɪ.dl̩ˌsɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈkreɪ.dl̩ˌsɒŋ / (BrE)
A quiet song sung to soothe or lull a child to sleep; a lullaby.
quiet song to lull a child to sleep

Examples

She hummed a gentle cradlesong to soothe her baby to sleep.
The melody of the old cradlesong brought back memories of his childhood.
Every night, his mother would sing a cradlesong by his bedside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lullaby - a quiet song sung to send a child to sleep

Etymology

From 'cradle' (a baby's bed) + 'song'.
