aftershafted

1. ADJ

/ ˌæf.tɚˈʃæf.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈɑːf.tə.ʃɑːf.tɪd / (BrE)
(of a feather) having an aftershaft, a small, secondary feather attached at the base of the main shaft.
having a feather with an aftershaft

Examples

The ostrich's feathers are aftershafted, giving them a fluffy appearance.
Only certain species of birds possess aftershafted plumage on their bodies.
The aftershafted condition of these feathers aids in insulation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aftershaft - a small, secondary feather at the base of some feathers
