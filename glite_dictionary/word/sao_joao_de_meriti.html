Sao Joao de Meriti

1. PROPN

/ saʊ ʒuˈɑw̃ də məˈriːti / (AmE), / saʊ ʒuˈaʊn də məˈriːti / (BrE)
a city in southeastern Brazil, located as a residential suburb of Rio de Janeiro
city in southeastern Brazil

Examples

Many commuters from Sao Joao de Meriti travel daily to Rio de Janeiro for work.
Population growth in Sao Joao de Meriti has contributed to the expansion of the greater Rio metropolitan area.
She was born and raised in Sao Joao de Meriti, a vibrant suburb of Rio de Janeiro.

Etymology

Portuguese, meaning 'Saint John of Meriti'.
