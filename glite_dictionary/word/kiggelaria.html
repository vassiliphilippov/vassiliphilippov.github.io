Kiggelaria

1. NOUN

/ kɪɡəˈlɛəriə / (AmE), / kɪɡəˈlɛəriə / (BrE)
A small genus of shrubs or small trees native to South Africa, belonging to the family Achariaceae.
genus of South African shrubs or trees

Examples

The garden featured several species of Kiggelaria thriving in the subtropical climate.
Botanists studied the unique fruit of the Kiggelaria genus for its ecological significance.
A Kiggelaria tree can attract a variety of birds and insects to the area.

Etymology

Named after François Kiggelaar, an 18th-century Dutch botanist.
