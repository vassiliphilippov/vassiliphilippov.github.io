brown-gray

1. ADJ

/ braʊn ˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / braʊn ˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
of a grey color tinged with brown; having the appearance of grey mixed with brown
grey tinged with brown

Examples

The old photograph had faded into a brown-gray hue over the years.
He wore a brown-gray sweater that matched the autumn landscape.
Clouds hovered overhead, casting a brown-gray light on the empty streets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taupe - a dark gray-brown color
