DoC

1. NOUN

/ di oʊ si / (AmE), / diː əʊ siː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for the United States Department of Commerce, the federal department responsible for promoting and administering domestic and foreign trade, conducting the census, and overseeing the patent office.
abbreviation for U.S. Department of Commerce
american

Examples

The DoC released new statistics on international trade this week.
Several tech companies collaborated with the DoC to improve patent processing.
The DoC will soon conduct the decennial census across the country.

Related Terms
