battle-scarred

1. ADJ

/ ˈbæt̬l ˌskɑrd / (AmE), / ˈbætl ˌskɑːd / (BrE)
having scars or damage as a result of battle or conflict, either literal or figurative
marked by wounds from battle or conflict

Examples

The battle-scarred veteran shared stories of his experiences during the war.
The city's walls are battle-scarred after years of conflict.
Her battle-scarred heart had endured many hardships, yet she remained hopeful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scarred - marked with scars
  • Antonym: unscathed - not injured or harmed
