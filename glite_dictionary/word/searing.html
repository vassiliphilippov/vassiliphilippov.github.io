searing

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a remark, attack, or criticism) extremely harsh and critical; intended to hurt or offend deeply.
extremely harsh or critical (remark, attack)

Examples

The journalist wrote a searing critique of government policies.
Her searing comments left him speechless and embarrassed.
The film received a searing review that criticized nearly every aspect of its production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scathing - severely critical or scornful
  • Antonym: mild - not severe, gentle (of criticism)
