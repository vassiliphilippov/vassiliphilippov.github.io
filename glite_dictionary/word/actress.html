actress

1. NOUN

/ ˈæk.trəs / (AmE), / ˈæk.trəs / (BrE)
A female actor, especially one who performs in plays, films, or television.
female actor

Examples

The actress accepted the award with a heartfelt speech.
She became a well-known actress after starring in several hit movies.
Critics praised the young actress for her powerful performance in the drama.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: actor - A person who performs in plays, films, or television; male actor or gender-neutral

Etymology

From actor + -ess, following the pattern of gendered nouns in English.
