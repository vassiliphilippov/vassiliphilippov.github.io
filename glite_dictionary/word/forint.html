forint

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrɪnt / (AmE), / ˈfɒrɪnt / (BrE)
the official currency and basic unit of money in Hungary
Hungarian currency unit

Examples

I exchanged my euros for forints before arriving in Budapest.
The price was listed as 5000 forints for a night at the hotel.
How many forints does it take to buy a coffee in Hungary?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: florin - former currency unit in various European countries

Etymology

From Hungarian forint, itself from Italian fiorino (florin).
