slipover

1. NOUN

/ ˈslɪpˌoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈslɪpˌəʊvə / (BrE)
a sweater without fastenings that is put on by pulling it over the head
sweater pulled over the head

Examples

He wore a soft slipover with a collared shirt underneath.
This blue wool slipover is perfect for chilly evenings.
She knitted a cozy slipover for her grandson.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pullover - a sweater that is put on by pulling it over the head

Etymology

Formed from 'slip' + 'over' referring to slipping the garment over the head.
