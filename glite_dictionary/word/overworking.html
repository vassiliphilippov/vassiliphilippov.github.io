overworking

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊvərˈwɜrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of working too much or for excessively long periods.
working too much; excessive work

Examples

Constant overworking eventually led to his burnout.
The company issued new policies to prevent overworking among employees.
She blamed her illness on years of overworking.

Synset Examples

he became ill from overwork

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overwork - the act of working too much or too hard
