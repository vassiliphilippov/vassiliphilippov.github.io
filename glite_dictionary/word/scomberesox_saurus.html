Scomberesox saurus

1. NOUN

/ skɑm.bɚˌiː.sɑks ˈsɔ.rəs / (AmE), / skɒm.bəˌriː.səks ˈsɔː.rəs / (BrE)
A slender, long-beaked fish found in temperate Atlantic waters, also known as the Atlantic saury.
slender Atlantic fish (Atlantic saury)

Examples

Fishermen reported catching several Scomberesox saurus near the coast this season.
The slender shape of the Scomberesox saurus allows it to swim swiftly in open water.
Marine biologists tagged a group of Scomberesox saurus to study their migratory patterns.

Etymology

From genus name Scomberesox and species name saurus, both from scientific Latin nomenclature.
