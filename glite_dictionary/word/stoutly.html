stoutly

1. ADV

/ ˈstaʊtli / (AmE), / ˈstaʊtli / (BrE)
In a firm, determined, or resolute manner.
in a determined or resolute way

Examples

Despite the challenges, she defended her views stoutly during the meeting.
The manager stoutly refused to compromise on safety standards.
He has stoutly supported the project from the very beginning.

Synset Examples

he was stoutly replying to his critics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firmly - in a strong and steady way
  • Synonym: resolutely - in an admirably purposeful, determined manner
  • Antonym: weakly - in a weak or feeble manner

Etymology

From 'stout' (brave, determined) + '-ly' adverbial suffix.
