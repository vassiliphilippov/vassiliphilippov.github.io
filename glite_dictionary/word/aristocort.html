Aristocort

1. NOUN

/ ˈærɪstəˌkɔrt / (AmE), / ˈærɪstəˌkɔːt / (BrE)
A trademarked name for a synthetic corticosteroid used as an anti-inflammatory medication.
synthetic corticosteroid drug (trade name)
medical

Examples

Her doctor prescribed Aristocort to reduce the inflammation in her joints.
Patients are advised not to stop using Aristocort abruptly without consulting a physician.
The pharmacist explained the potential side effects of Aristocort before dispensing the medication.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corticosteroid - a class of steroid hormones used to reduce inflammation
