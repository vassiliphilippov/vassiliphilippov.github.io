choirmaster

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwaɪərˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈkwɑːɪəˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
the person who directs or leads a choir, especially in a church or musical group
director or leader of a choir

Examples

The choirmaster arranged a new piece for the upcoming Christmas concert.
As choirmaster, she inspired the singers to reach their full potential.
After rehearsal, the choirmaster offered constructive feedback to the whole group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conductor - person who directs a musical performance, especially an orchestra or choir

Etymology

From 'choir' + 'master', indicating the leader of a choir.
