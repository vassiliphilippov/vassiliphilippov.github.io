to bejewel

1. VERB

/ bɪˈdʒuːəl / (AmE), / bɪˈdʒuːəl / (BrE)
to decorate or adorn with jewels or precious stones.
adorn with jewels or stones

Examples

The designer loved to bejewel evening gowns with sparkling crystals for added glamour.
Her crown was bejewelled with diamonds and sapphires, making it shimmer under the lights.
They plan to bejewel the costume masks for the festival parade.

Synset Examples

jeweled dresses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to adorn - to decorate or add beauty to something
  • Synonym: to decorate - to make something look more attractive

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'be-' and the noun 'jewel'.
