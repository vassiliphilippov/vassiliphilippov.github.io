Lamisil

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæmɪsɪl / (AmE), / ˈlæmɪsɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked oral antifungal drug used for treating fungal infections, especially of the nails.
antifungal drug (trademark name)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Lamisil to treat my persistent toenail infection.
Some patients experience mild side effects while taking Lamisil for their fungal nail disease.
After several weeks of using Lamisil, the appearance of his nails improved significantly.
