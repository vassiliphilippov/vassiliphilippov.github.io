Her Maj

1. PROPN

/ hɜr mædʒ / (AmE), / hɜː mædʒ / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for 'Her Majesty', used to refer to a queen, especially the reigning queen of the United Kingdom
informal abbreviation for 'Her Majesty'
informal british

Examples

The press was excited for Her Maj's annual Christmas speech.
Everyone stood respectfully as Her Maj entered the hall.
Some people collect memorabilia featuring Her Maj's portrait.

Synset Examples

Her Maj is known for her impeccable taste.
Everyone bowed as Her Maj entered the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Her Majesty - a formal title for a reigning queen

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'Her Majesty'.
