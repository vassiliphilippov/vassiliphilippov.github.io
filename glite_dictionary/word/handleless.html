handleless

1. ADJ

/ ˈhændl̩ləs / (AmE), / ˈhændlələs / (BrE)
having no handle; lacking a handle.
without a handle

Examples

The new mugs in the café are handleless to create a more modern look.
He designed a handleless drawer for a minimalist effect in the kitchen.
Her teapot was handleless, making it unique and difficult to pour.

Synset Examples

sleek cabinets with apparently handleless doors

Related Terms

  • Antonym: handled - having a handle
