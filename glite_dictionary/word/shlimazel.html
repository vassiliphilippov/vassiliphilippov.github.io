shlimazel

1. NOUN

/ ʃlɪˈmɑzəl / (AmE), / ʃlɪˈmɑːzəl / (BrE)
A very unlucky or inept person who seems destined to fail at everything.
unlucky or inept person
informal

Examples

No matter how hard he tries, Jake is a classic shlimazel who can't catch a break.
The team joked that their newest member was a real shlimazel after he broke the coffee machine.
In every group, there's always one shlimazel who seems to attract misfortune.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schlemiel - a clumsy, inept person (esp. one whose ineptitude affects others)

Etymology

From Yiddish שלימאזל (shlimazl), from Hebrew שְׁלוֹ מַזָּל (shelo mazal, 'no luck').
