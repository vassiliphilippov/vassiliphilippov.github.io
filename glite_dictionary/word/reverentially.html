reverentially

1. ADV

/ ˌrɛvəˈrɛnʃəli / (AmE), / ˌrɛvəˈrɛnʃəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows deep respect or reverence.
in a deeply respectful manner

Examples

The students listened reverentially as the author described his writing process.
She placed the family heirloom reverentially on the mantelpiece.
He spoke reverentially about his mentor's influence on his life.

Synset Examples

he gazed reverently at the handiwork

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respectfully - in a manner showing respect
  • Antonym: irreverently - in a disrespectful or irreverent manner

Etymology

From 'reverential' + '-ly'; 'reverential' from Late Latin reverentialis.
