leaver

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːvər / (AmE), / ˈliːvə / (BrE)
a person who leaves a place, institution, or organization, especially permanently or at the end of a particular period
person who leaves a place or group

Examples

After graduation, each leaver was given a farewell gift.
The company tracks why every leaver decides to move on.
As a leaver from the club, she wrote a heartfelt letter to her teammates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: departed - a person who has left, especially recently

Etymology

From leave (to depart) + -er (agent noun suffix).
