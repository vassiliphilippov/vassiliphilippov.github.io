XXX

1. NUM

/ ˌɛks ˌɛks ˌɛks / (AmE), / ˌɛks ˌɛks ˌɛks / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for thirty (30)
Roman numeral for 30

Examples

The king's reign ended in the year XXX according to the ancient tablets.
She labeled the anniversary celebration as the XXX edition of the event.
The old clock used Roman numerals, so thirty was marked as XXX on its face.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thirty - the cardinal number 30
  • Degree Lower: XXIX - Roman numeral for 29
  • Degree Higher: XXXI - Roman numeral for 31

Etymology

From Latin, using 'X' to represent ten; 'XXX' is three tens.

2. NOUN

/ ɛks ɛks ɛks / (AmE), / ɛks ɛks ɛks / (BrE)
(genetics) A condition in which a female has an abnormal complement of three X chromosomes, instead of the usual two.
female with three X chromosomes (genetic condition)
medical

Examples

Genetic testing revealed that she had XXX syndrome.
Females born with XXX are often taller than average.
Doctors discussed the possible health implications of XXX with the family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trisomy X - another name for the condition of having three X chromosomes
