MALS

1. NOUN

/ ɛm eɪ ɛl ɛs / (AmE), / ɛm ɑː ɛl ɛs / (BrE)
an abbreviation for Master of Arts in Library Science, an advanced academic degree in the study of library and information science.
Master of Arts in Library Science degree

Examples

She earned her MALS before starting her career as a librarian.
Many universities offer a MALS program for those interested in library science.
His MALS has helped him advance in the field of information management.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: MLIS - Master of Library and Information Science, a similar advanced degree
