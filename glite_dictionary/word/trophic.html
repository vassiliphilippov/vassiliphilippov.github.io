trophic

1. ADJ

/ ˈtroʊ.fɪk / (AmE), / ˈtrɒf.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to nutrition or the feeding habits of organisms
related to nutrition or feeding

Examples

The researchers studied the trophic interactions between predators and their prey.
A sudden change in the lake's trophic state can affect the entire ecosystem.
Different species occupy distinct trophic levels within the food web.

Synset Examples

a trophic level on the food chain

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutritional - relating to nutrition

Etymology

From Greek *trophē* (nourishment, food).
