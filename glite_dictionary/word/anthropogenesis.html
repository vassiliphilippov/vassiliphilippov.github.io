anthropogenesis

1. NOUN

/ ˌænθroʊpəˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌænθrəʊpəˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (BrE)
the origin and development of the human species
origin of the human species

Examples

Recent discoveries in Africa have provided new insights into anthropogenesis.
Scholars in evolutionary biology often debate competing theories of anthropogenesis.
The museum's exhibit explores the stages of anthropogenesis through interactive displays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anthropogeny - the study of the origin and development of humans

Etymology

From Greek anthrōpos (human being) + genesis (origin, creation).
