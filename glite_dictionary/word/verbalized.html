verbalized

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɝː.bə.laɪzd / (AmE), / ˈvɜː.bə.laɪzd / (BrE)
expressed or communicated in words rather than in other forms.
expressed in words

Examples

Her fears were never verbalized, but they were clear in her actions.
At the meeting, his support for the plan was strongly verbalized.
Sometimes, feelings are better verbalized than left unsaid.

Synset Examples

frequently uttered sentiments

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unspoken - not communicated in words
  • Synonym: articulated - clearly expressed or stated in words

Etymology

From 'verbalize' + -ed; 'verbalize' from Latin verbum (word).
