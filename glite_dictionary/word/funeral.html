funeral

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuːnərəl / (AmE), / ˈfjuːnərəl / (BrE)
A ceremony for honoring and burying or cremating a deceased person.
ceremony for burial or cremation

Examples

Hundreds of people attended his funeral to pay their last respects.
The funeral will be held on Friday afternoon at the local church.
After the funeral, family and friends gathered at her house to share memories.

Synset Examples

hundreds of people attended his funeral

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burial - the act of placing a dead body in the ground
  • Antonym: wedding - a ceremony where two people get married

Etymology

From Latin 'funus' (burial, funeral rites).
