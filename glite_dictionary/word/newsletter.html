newsletter

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌlɛtər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌlɛtə / (BrE)
A report or bulletin, typically issued regularly, giving informal or special-interest news to a particular group of people.
regular report with group news

Examples

We receive a monthly newsletter from our local community center.
The company’s newsletter provides employees with updates and important announcements.
She edits the alumni newsletter and ensures every edition contains interesting stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulletin - short official statement or broadcast summary of news
