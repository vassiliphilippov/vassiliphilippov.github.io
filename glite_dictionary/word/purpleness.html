purpleness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːplnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being purple in color
the quality of being purple

Examples

The purpleness of the flower made it stand out among the green leaves.
She was captivated by the purpleness of the twilight sky.
The artist mixed paints to achieve the perfect purpleness for his landscape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purple - a color between red and blue

Etymology

From purple + -ness, forming a noun indicating the state or quality of being purple.
