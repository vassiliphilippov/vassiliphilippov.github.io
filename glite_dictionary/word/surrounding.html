surrounding

1. ADJ

/ səˈraʊndɪŋ / (AmE), / səˈraʊndɪŋ / (BrE)
being on all sides of something; located or happening near or around a particular place or person.
being all around; nearby

Examples

We explored the surrounding villages during our stay in the countryside.
The surrounding hills are covered in dense forest.
Noise from the surrounding streets made it hard to concentrate.

Synset Examples

encompassing mountain ranges
the surrounding countryside

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adjacent - next to or adjoining something else
