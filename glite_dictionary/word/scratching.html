scratching

1. NOUN

/ ˈskrætʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskrætʃɪŋ / (BrE)
a harsh, grating noise produced by scraping or rubbing something rough or hard
harsh noise from scraping

Examples

The loud scratching of chairs on the floor made everyone wince.
A faint scratching was heard on the windowpane during the storm.
She found the scratching of the pen on paper oddly soothing.

Synset Examples

the scrape of violin bows distracted her

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scrape - a harsh noise or sound caused by scraping
  • Synonym: grating - harsh, unpleasant sound
