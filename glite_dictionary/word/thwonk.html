thwonk

1. NOUN

/ θwɑŋk / (AmE), / θwɒŋk / (BrE)
a sound made by a soft but resonant impact, such as when a ball hits a surface
a soft, resonant impact sound

Examples

The rubber mallet landed with a thwonk against the wooden peg.
As he kicked the soccer ball, it hit the garage door with a satisfying thwonk.
She dropped the beanbag chair, and it settled on the floor with a heavy thwonk.

Synset Examples

The ball made a thwonk sound as it hit the wall.
