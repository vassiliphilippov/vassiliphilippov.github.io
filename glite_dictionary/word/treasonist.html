treasonist

1. NOUN

/ ˈtriːzənɪst / (AmE), / ˈtriːzənɪst / (BrE)
a person who betrays their country by committing treason
person who commits treason

Examples

Many considered him a treasonist after he leaked classified information to enemy forces.
The government charged the suspect as a treasonist in the national court.
Throughout history, being labeled a treasonist often led to severe punishment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traitor - a person who betrays their country or cause

Etymology

Formed from 'treason' + '-ist' (suffix indicating a person associated with an action or belief).
