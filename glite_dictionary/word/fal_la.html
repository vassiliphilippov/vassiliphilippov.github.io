fal la

1. NOUN

/ fɑː ˈlɑː / (AmE), / fɑː ˈlɑː / (BrE)
a sequence of meaningless syllables used as a refrain in partsongs, especially in madrigals and other traditional music
meaningless song refrain syllables

Examples

The choir sang a cheerful tune with a recurring fal la in the chorus.
Many Renaissance songs feature a playful fal la between verses.
The children giggled as they improvised a fal la during their music class.
