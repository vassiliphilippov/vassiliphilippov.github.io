Laccopetalum

1. NOUN

/ ˌlæk.oʊˈpɛt.əl.əm / (AmE), / ˌlak.əʊˈpɛt.əl.əm / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the buttercup family, characterized by a single species known as the giant buttercup.
genus of plants; giant buttercup

Botanists recently discovered a new population of Laccopetalum in the region.
The Laccopetalum is notable for its large, buttercup-like flowers.
