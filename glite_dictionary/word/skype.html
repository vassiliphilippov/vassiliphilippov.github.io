to skype

1. VERB

/ skaɪp / (AmE), / skaɪp / (BrE)
to communicate with someone by using Skype or similar internet voice/video call software
communicate by Skype or similar software

Examples

Let's skype tonight so I can see the new house.
She skyped her parents every Sunday when she lived abroad.
We're skyping with our colleagues in London at 3 PM.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: video call - to make a call with video transmission over the Internet
  • Degree Lower: call - to speak to someone by telephone

Etymology

From Skype, the brand name of the internet telephony and video call software launched in 2003.
