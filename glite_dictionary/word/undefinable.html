undefinable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɪˈfaɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈfaɪnəbl / (BrE)
impossible to describe or define precisely; not able to be put into exact words
not able to be precisely described or defined

Examples

She experienced an undefinable sadness that lingered all day.
There's an undefinable quality to his music that makes it unique.
His smile had an undefinable charm that everyone noticed.

Synset Examples

an indefinable feeling of terror
an abstract concept that seems indefinable

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indescribable - impossible to describe

Etymology

Formed from un- (not) + definable, from Latin definire (to limit, define).
