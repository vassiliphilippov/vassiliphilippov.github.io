sens

1. NOUN

/ sɛnz / (AmE), / sɛnz / (BrE)
(Slang) Marijuana, especially a high-quality variety; used as a street name.
slang term for marijuana
slang

Examples

He sold me some really potent sens last weekend.
The party was chill, and everyone was passing around a bag of sens.
You can always count on him to have top-shelf sens at a good price.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marijuana - a psychoactive drug from the Cannabis plant

Etymology

Shortened from 'sensimilla,' which refers to seedless cannabis (from Spanish 'sin semilla', meaning 'without seeds').
