hatfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæt.fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈhæt.fɪʃ / (BrE)
a person whose appearance is notably different or less attractive when not wearing a hat, often to the point of being unrecognizable
person unrecognizable without their hat
slang

Examples

Everyone was surprised when the hatfish took off his cap and looked completely different.
After meeting him in person, she realized he was a hatfish all along.
He joked about being a hatfish because no one recognizes him without his favorite fedora.

Synset Examples

He was a total hatfish; I didn't recognize him without it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catfish - a person who creates a fake identity on social media to deceive others

Etymology

Blend of 'hat' and 'catfish' (internet slang for deceiving others with false appearance).
