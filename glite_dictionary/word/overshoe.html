overshoe

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊvərˌʃu / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌʃuː / (BrE)
A waterproof or protective covering designed to be worn over shoes to shield them from water, snow, or cold.
protective covering worn over shoes

Examples

He put on his overshoes before stepping out into the snow.
During the heavy rain, my overshoes kept my feet completely dry.
She keeps a pair of overshoes by the door for muddy days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: galosh - a waterproof overshoe, usually made of rubber

Etymology

Formed from 'over-' (above, covering) and 'shoe'.
