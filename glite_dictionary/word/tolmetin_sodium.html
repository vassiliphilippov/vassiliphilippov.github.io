tolmetin sodium

1. NOUN

/ toʊlˈmiːtɪn ˈsoʊdiəm / (AmE), / tɒlˈmiːtɪn ˈsəʊdiəm / (BrE)
A nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used to reduce pain, swelling, and inflammation, often prescribed for arthritis.
nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed tolmetin sodium to help manage her arthritis symptoms.
Patients taking tolmetin sodium should be monitored for possible gastrointestinal side effects.
He felt significant relief after starting his tolmetin sodium regimen.
