rijstaffel

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪstɑːfəl / (AmE), / ˈrɪstɑːfəl / (BrE)
an Indonesian dish consisting of rice served with a wide selection of accompanying foods and sauces
Indonesian rice dish with many side dishes

Examples

We enjoyed a delicious rijstaffel at the Indonesian restaurant last night.
The rijstaffel included curries, satay, and spicy vegetables alongside fragrant rice.
Many Dutch families prepare rijstaffel for festive occasions, celebrating its rich variety.

Etymology

From Dutch 'rijsttafel' meaning 'rice table', adopted from Indonesian cuisine during the colonial period.
