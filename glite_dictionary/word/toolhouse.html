toolhouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːlˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈtuːlˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A small building or shed used for storing tools.
shed for storing tools

Examples

The gardener kept all her equipment locked in the toolhouse behind the garden.
Last summer, we built a small toolhouse next to the garage for easy storage.
Rain had leaked through the roof of the old toolhouse, rusting the shovels and rakes inside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toolshed - a shed for keeping tools; synonym of 'toolhouse'

Etymology

From 'tool' + 'house', meaning a house or shed for tools.
