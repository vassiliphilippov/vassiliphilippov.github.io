anticipative

1. ADJ

/ ænˈtɪs.ɪ.peɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / ænˈtɪs.ɪ.pə.tɪv / (BrE)
showing or characterized by anticipation; marked by eagerness or expectation for something to happen.
marked by anticipation or expectation

Examples

There was an anticipative atmosphere before the big announcement.
She waited in anticipative silence for her results.
His anticipative smile revealed that he knew what was about to happen.

Synset Examples

an expectant hush

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expectant - marked by eager anticipation or expectation

Etymology

From Latin 'anticipationem', from 'anticipare' (to take before).
