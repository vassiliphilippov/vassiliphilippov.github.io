pubes

1. NOUN

/ ˈpjuːbiz / (AmE), / ˈpjuːbiːz / (BrE)
the lower part of the abdomen just above the external genital organs
area above genital organs

Examples

Teenagers may notice changes in the appearance of their pubes as they go through puberty.
He felt a sharp pain in his pubes after falling off the bicycle.
The doctor examined the area around her pubes for signs of infection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pubic region - the area around the pubic bone and external genitalia
  • Degree Higher: pelvis - the lower part of the torso, forming the base of the spine and the socket of the hip joint

Etymology

From Latin pubes, meaning 'the hair of the groin, adulthood'.
