majesty

1. NOUN

/ ˈmædʒəsti / (AmE), / ˈmadʒɪsti / (BrE)
impressiveness and grandeur in scale, proportion, or appearance
greatness, grandeur in appearance or scale

Examples

The majesty of the mountains left us speechless.
He spoke admiringly of the majesty of the ancient cathedral.
Few things can match the majesty of a sunset over the ocean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandeur - impressive beauty, size, or style
  • Synonym: splendor - magnificent and splendid appearance
  • Antonym: humbleness - modesty or lowliness

Etymology

From Old French *majesté*, from Latin *majestas* (greatness, dignity).
