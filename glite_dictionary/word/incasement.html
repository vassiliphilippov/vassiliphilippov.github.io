incasement

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈkeɪsmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈkeɪsmənt / (BrE)
the act of enclosing or surrounding something in a case or covering
the act of enclosing in a case

Examples

The incasement of the sculpture protected it from dust and damage.
Special materials were chosen for the incasement to ensure maximum preservation.
After the antique clock's incasement, it was displayed in the museum's main hall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: encasement - the act of enclosing in a case (synonym, more common)
