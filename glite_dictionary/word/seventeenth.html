seventeenth

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛvənˈtinθ / (AmE), / ˌsɛvənˈtiːnθ / (BrE)
coming after the sixteenth in position; 17th
17th in order or position

Examples

She finished in seventeenth place in the race, just missing the top fifteen.
The seventeenth century saw major developments in science and philosophy.
His office is on the seventeenth floor of the building.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sixteenth - coming after the fifteenth in position; 16th
  • Degree Higher: eighteenth - coming after the seventeenth in position; 18th

2. NOUN

/ ˌsɛvənˈtinθ / (AmE), / ˌsɛvənˈtiːnθ / (BrE)
The position in a sequence that comes after the sixteenth and before the eighteenth; the ordinal number corresponding to 17.
ordinal number: position 17th

Examples

He finished in seventeenth place in the race, just missing the top fifteen.
This is the seventeenth time we've held this annual meeting.
Her office is on the seventeenth floor of the building.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sixteenth - the position 16 in a series
  • Degree Higher: eighteenth - the position 18 in a series

Etymology

Formed from 'seventeen' (from Old English 'seofontīene') plus the ordinal suffix '-th'.
