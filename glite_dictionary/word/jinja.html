Jinja

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɪndʒə / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒə / (BrE)
A city in southeastern Uganda, situated on the north shore of Lake Victoria.
city in Uganda, north of Lake Victoria

Examples

Jinja is a popular starting point for rafting trips on the Nile River.
Many tourists visit Jinja to see the source of the Nile.
