doink

1. NOUN

/ dɔɪŋk / (AmE), / dɔɪŋk / (BrE)
a short, resonant sound made by a sudden impact, often used to imitate or describe such a sound
short, resonant sound from impact
informal

Examples

The tennis ball hit the metal post with a distinctive doink.
She heard a tiny doink after dropping her keys onto the tile floor.
Every time the cartoon character stumbled, a silly doink echoed through the scene.

Synset Examples

He made a doink sound when he bumped his head.
