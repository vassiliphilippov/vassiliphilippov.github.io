dojigger

1. NOUN

/ doʊˈdʒɪɡər / (AmE), / dəʊˈdʒɪɡə / (BrE)
an object or device whose name is forgotten or not known; a thingamajig
unspecified object whose name is unknown
slang american

Examples

Can you hand me that dojigger over there on the table?
I used a little dojigger to fix the door handle.
If you ever lose the dojigger for this machine, you'll have to improvise.

Synset Examples

she eased the ball-shaped doodad back into its socket
there may be some great new gizmo around the corner that you will want to use

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thingamajig - an object whose name is not known or forgotten
  • Synonym: gizmo - a gadget, especially one whose name is unknown or forgotten

Etymology

Probably an alteration of 'doohickey' and 'thingamajig,' likely formed as humorous American English slang around the early 20th century.
