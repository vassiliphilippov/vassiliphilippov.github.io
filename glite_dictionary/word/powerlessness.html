powerlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊ.ɚləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
The state of being unable to control or influence people or events; lack of power or strength.
lack of power or strength

Examples

After losing his job, he felt a deep sense of powerlessness over his future.
The government's slow response created public frustration and powerlessness.
Her powerlessness in the face of natural disasters left her feeling anxious and uncertain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helplessness - the state of being unable to defend oneself or act without help
  • Synonym: impotence - lack of power or effectiveness
  • Antonym: power - ability or capacity to do something or act in a particular way

Etymology

From 'powerless' + '-ness', forming a noun indicating the state of lacking power.
