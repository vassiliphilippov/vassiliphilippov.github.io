scattergood

1. NOUN

/ ˈskæt̬ɚˌɡʊd / (AmE), / ˈskætəˌɡʊd / (BrE)
a person who spends money recklessly or extravagantly; a spendthrift
reckless or extravagant spender
archaic

Examples

The old landlord was a notorious scattergood who soon lost his fortune.
In her youth, she was something of a scattergood, spending every penny she earned.
The warning tale was about a scattergood who wasted his inheritance within a year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spendthrift - a person who spends money extravagantly or wastefully
  • Antonym: miser - a person who hoards wealth and spends as little money as possible

Etymology

Formed from 'scatter' + 'good', referring to scattering one's goods (i.e., money or possessions) wastefully.
