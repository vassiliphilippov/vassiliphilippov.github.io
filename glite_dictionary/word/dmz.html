DMZ

1. NOUN

/ di ɛm zi / (AmE), / diː ɛm zɛd / (BrE)
an area, usually established by treaty, in which military forces and activities are not permitted
demilitarized zone

Examples

The border between North and South Korea is one of the most heavily guarded DMZ areas in the world.
Negotiations focused on establishing a DMZ to prevent further conflict in the region.
Civilians are generally prohibited from entering the DMZ without special permission.

Synset Examples

tensions exist on both sides of the demilitarized zone separating North Korea and South Korea

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demilitarized zone - an area where no military forces or installations are permitted

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'demilitarized zone'.
