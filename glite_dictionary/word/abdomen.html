abdomen

1. NOUN

/ ˈæb.də.mən / (AmE), / ˈabd.ə.mən / (BrE)
the part of the body between the chest and the pelvis, containing the stomach, intestines, and other major organs
part of the body below the chest

Examples

He placed his hand on his abdomen to show where it hurt.
Muscles in the abdomen help support the spine and maintain posture.
After the surgery, she felt discomfort in her lower abdomen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stomach - the organ in the abdomen where food is digested

Etymology

From Latin 'abdōmen' (belly).

2. NOUN

the part of the body between the chest and the pelvis, containing the stomach, intestines, and other organs
area of the body between chest and pelvis

Examples

The doctor examined her abdomen for signs of swelling or tenderness.
Muscles in the abdomen play an important role in supporting the spine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stomach - organ in the abdomen where food is digested
