monaul

1. NOUN

/ məˈnɔl / (AmE), / məˈnɔːl / (BrE)
a brilliantly colored pheasant native to southern Asia, especially the Himalayan region.
brilliantly colored pheasant of southern Asia

Examples

The Himalayan monaul is famous for its iridescent plumage that shimmers in the sunlight.
During our trek, we spotted a pair of monauls feeding near the rhododendron bushes.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the endangered monaul in Nepal.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: pheasant - large, long-tailed game bird

Etymology

From Nepali 'monal', referring to the Himalayan monal pheasant.
