Gikuyu

1. NOUN

/ giːˈkuːjuː / (AmE), / giːˈkuːjuː / (BrE)
the Bantu language of the Kikuyu people, spoken chiefly in central Kenya
Bantu language spoken in Kenya

Examples

She is fluent in Gikuyu and often teaches it to younger children in her village.
The novel was originally written in Gikuyu before being translated to English.
Many traditional stories are preserved in the Gikuyu language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kikuyu - alternative name for Gikuyu language and people

Etymology

From Gikuyu, the endonym in the language itself (also spelled Kikuyu in English).
