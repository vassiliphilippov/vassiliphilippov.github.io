expending

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈspɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of spending money or resources for goods or services.
the act of spending money or resources

Examples

The careful expending of resources is essential in challenging economic times.
She was criticized for the excessive expending of company funds on unnecessary items.
Budget restrictions limit our expending on new technology this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spending - the act of paying out money for goods or services
  • Synonym: expenditure - the action of spending funds; amount spent

Etymology

Present participle of 'expend', from Latin 'expendere' (to weigh out, to pay out).
