anonymity

1. NOUN

/ ˌænəˈnɪməti / (AmE), / ˌanəˈnɪmɪti / (BrE)
The condition of having one's identity unknown or concealed.
the state of being anonymous

Examples

The author chose anonymity to protect her privacy.
Online forums often grant users a level of anonymity.
Witnesses were guaranteed anonymity for their own safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anonymity - the state of being anonymous
  • Antonym: identification - the process of establishing identity

Etymology

From Greek anonymia, from anonymos (without name).
