Ah Lian

1. NOUN

/ ɑ liˈɛn / (AmE), / ɑː liˈæn / (BrE)
(in Singapore) a young woman who is considered to belong to a flashy, urban subculture, often associated with gaudy fashion, brash behavior, and the use of distinctive slang.
stereotypical trendy Singaporean girl
slang

Examples

The Ah Lian at the mall always has colorful hair and bold makeup.
Many people stereotype her as an Ah Lian because of her loud voice and unique fashion sense.
Despite being called an Ah Lian, she is proud of her individuality.

Synset Examples

The Ah Lian at the mall always has colorful hair.
That Ah Lian dances at every club she visits.
She's an Ah Lian who loves to shop for the latest trends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ah Beng - a stereotypical flashy young Singaporean man; male equivalent of Ah Lian

Etymology

From Hokkien Chinese 'Ah', a common prefix for names, and 'Lian', a common Chinese female given name. Term popularized in Singaporean English.
