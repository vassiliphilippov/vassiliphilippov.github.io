counselling

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊn.səl.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.səl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of giving professional guidance or advice, especially to help someone deal with personal or psychological problems
professional advice or guidance

Examples

After experiencing anxiety, she sought counselling to help her cope with stress.
The university offers free counselling services to all its students.
Marriage counselling can help couples resolve their conflicts effectively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: therapy - treatment designed to relieve or heal a disorder
  • Synonym: advice - recommendation offered with regard to prudent action

Etymology

Derived from 'counsel', from Old French 'counseil', from Latin 'consilium' (advice, counsel).
