squirter

1. NOUN

/ ˈskwɝːtər / (AmE), / ˈskwɜːtə / (BrE)
a toy pistol or device that shoots a jet of water, especially as a plaything for children
a toy that squirts water

Examples

The children ran around the yard, each holding a brightly colored squirter.
She got soaked when her brother aimed his squirter at her during the water fight.
At the party, everyone received a small plastic squirter as a party favor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water pistol - a toy gun that shoots water
