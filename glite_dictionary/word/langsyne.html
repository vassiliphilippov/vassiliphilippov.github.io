langsyne

1. ADV

/ læŋˈsaɪn / (AmE), / laŋˈsʌɪn / (BrE)
(chiefly Scottish) In the distant or comparatively distant past; long ago.
long ago; in the distant past (Scottish)
archaic british

Examples

Many old traditions have been forgotten since langsyne.
The castle has stood on the hill since langsyne.
She often reminisced about the friends she knew in langsyne.
