resinated

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛzɪˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɛzɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
impregnated, coated, or flavored with resin.
treated or flavored with resin

Examples

The traditional Greek beverage retsina is a type of resinated wine.
Some violins are polished with resinated varnish for added protection and shine.
The museum displayed an ancient amphora containing traces of resinated oil.

Synset Examples

resinated wine

Etymology

From 'resin' + '-ated', indicating treatment with resin.
