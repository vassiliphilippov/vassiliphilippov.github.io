La

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛl.eɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛl.eɪ / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for lanthanum, a soft white metallic element of the rare earth group
chemical symbol for lanthanum

Examples

The mineral sample was found to contain traces of La and cerium.
Scientists use La in the production of high-quality optical glass.
La is classified as one of the rare earth elements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lanthanum - a soft white metallic element, symbol La

Etymology

Chemical symbol from the name 'lanthanum,' which is from Greek 'lanthanein' (to be hidden).
