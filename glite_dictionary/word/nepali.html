Nepali

1. NOUN

/ nəˈpɑli / (AmE), / nɪˈpɑːli / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Nepal
person from Nepal

Examples

My friend is a proud Nepali who loves sharing stories about his homeland.
Many Nepalis celebrate their traditional festivals with great enthusiasm every year.
The museum hired a Nepali to guide visitors through the exhibits on Himalayan culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nepalese - a native or inhabitant of Nepal

Etymology

From Nepal + -i, denoting origin or relation.

2. NOUN

/ nəˈpɑli / (AmE), / nɪˈpɑːli / (BrE)
the official language of Nepal, belonging to the Indo-Aryan branch of the Indo-European family
the official language of Nepal

Examples

He is fluent in both English and Nepali.
The constitution declares Nepali to be the official language of the country.
They offered a translation of the document into Nepali.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nepal - a country in South Asia, north of India
