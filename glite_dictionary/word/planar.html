planar

1. ADJ

/ ˈpleɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪnə / (BrE)
(of a surface or figure) relating to or lying in a plane; having two dimensions.
lying in or relating to a plane

Examples

The researcher observed the planar surface under a microscope.
A planar graph can be drawn on a flat surface without any edges crossing.
Engineers designed the structure with a planar foundation to ensure stability.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: spatial - relating to three-dimensional space

Etymology

From Latin 'planum' (flat surface) + English '-ar'.
