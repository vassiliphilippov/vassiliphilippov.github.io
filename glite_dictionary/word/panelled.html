panelled

1. ADJ

/ ˈpænəld / (AmE), / ˈpænəld / (BrE)
fitted or decorated with panels or wainscoting.
fitted with panels
british

Examples

The old library featured panelled walls and a carved wooden ceiling.
They installed panelled doors to give the rooms a more classic look.
The dining room was beautifully panelled in dark oak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paneled - fitted or decorated with panels or wainscoting (American spelling)

Etymology

From the noun 'panel' (Middle English, from Old French *panel*).
