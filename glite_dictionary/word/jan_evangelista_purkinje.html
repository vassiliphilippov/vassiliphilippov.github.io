Jan Evangelista Purkinje

1. PROPN

/ ˌjɑn ɛˌvɑnʒəˈlistə pʊrˈkɪnjə / (AmE), / ˌjan ɛˌvanɡɛˈlistə pʊˈkɪnjeɪ / (BrE)
(1787–1869) A Bohemian physiologist known for discovering Purkinje cells and the Purkinje network in the nervous system.
Bohemian physiologist, discovered Purkinje cells

Examples

The neuroscientist cited Jan Evangelista Purkinje for his pioneering work on brain cells.
Research on Jan Evangelista Purkinje's discoveries has advanced our understanding of the nervous system.
Medical students learn about Jan Evangelista Purkinje when studying cellular structures in the brain.
