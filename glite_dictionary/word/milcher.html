milcher

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪltʃər / (AmE), / ˈmɪltʃə / (BrE)
a cow, or any cattle, that is kept or raised primarily for milk production
milk-producing cow or cattle

Examples

The farmer selected the healthiest milcher for his expanding dairy herd.
This breed is known to be a productive milcher with high-quality milk.
During the show, her prize-winning milcher drew a lot of attention from the judges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dairy cow - a cow bred and kept for milk production

Etymology

From the verb 'milk' + -er, denoting an animal that is milked.
