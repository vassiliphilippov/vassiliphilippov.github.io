groundswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡraʊndzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊndzˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to maintain the grounds of an estate, park, or athletic field.
female grounds maintenance worker

Examples

The groundswoman arrived early each morning to prepare the playing fields for the day's matches.
As a groundswoman, she takes great pride in keeping the park clean and beautiful.
The local newspaper featured a story about a groundswoman who manages the gardens at the historic estate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: groundsman - a person (usually male) who maintains the grounds

Etymology

Formed from 'ground' + woman', modeled on 'groundsman'.
