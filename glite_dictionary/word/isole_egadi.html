Isole Egadi

1. PROPN

/ ˌiːsoʊˌleɪ eˈɡɑːdi / (AmE), / ˌiːsəʊleɪ eˈɡɑːdi / (BrE)
a group of islands off the west coast of Sicily in the Mediterranean Sea
island group off Sicily, Italy

Examples

The Isole Egadi are famous for their clear waters and rich marine life.
During our trip to Sicily, we took a boat to the Isole Egadi for a day of exploration.
The Isole Egadi have a fascinating history dating back to ancient times.

Etymology

From Italian 'Isole Egadi', meaning 'Egadi Islands'.
