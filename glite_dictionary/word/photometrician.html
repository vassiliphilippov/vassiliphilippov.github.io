photometrician

1. NOUN

/ ˌfoʊtəˌmɛˈtrɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˌmɛˈtrɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who specializes in or practices photometry, the science of measuring light intensity
specialist in measuring light

Examples

The photometrician carefully calibrated the equipment before the experiment began.
As a photometrician, she analyzes data from telescopes to determine the brightness of stars.
The museum hired a photometrician to consult on their new lighting system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photometrist - person skilled in photometry

Etymology

From photometry + -ician, patterned after terms like 'statistician'.
