undimmed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdɪmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdɪmd / (BrE)
not made dim or less bright; still shining or clear
not made dim or less bright

Examples

The stars were undimmed by the city lights, shining brightly in the night sky.
After all these years, his enthusiasm for music remains undimmed.
The headlights stayed undimmed as the car drove through the long tunnel.

Synset Examples

undimmed headlights
surprisingly the curtain started to rise while the houselights were still undimmed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dimmed - made less bright or clear

Etymology

From the prefix un- (not) + dimmed (past participle of dim).
