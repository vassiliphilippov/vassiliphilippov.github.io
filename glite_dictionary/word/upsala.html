Upsala

1. PROPN

/ ˈʌpˌsɑlə / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌsɑːlə / (BrE)
a city in east-central Sweden, northwest of Stockholm, known for its historic university and cathedral.
city in east-central Sweden

Examples

The famous university in Upsala attracts students from all over the world.
After visiting Stockholm, we took the train to Upsala for a day trip.
The cathedral in Upsala is one of Sweden's most impressive historical buildings.

Etymology

From Swedish 'Uppsala', the name of the city.
