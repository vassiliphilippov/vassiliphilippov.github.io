Brahui

1. NOUN

/ brəˈhuːi / (AmE), / brəˈhuːi / (BrE)
A member of a Dravidian ethnic group living mainly in Pakistan, particularly in Baluchistan.
member of Dravidian ethnic group in Pakistan

Examples

The Brahui have preserved their language despite being surrounded by non-Dravidian speakers.
Many Brahui live in the province of Baluchistan in Pakistan.
Anthropologists have studied the unique heritage of the Brahui people.

2. NOUN

/ brəˈhuː.i / (AmE), / brəˈhuː.i / (BrE)
an isolated Dravidian language spoken by the Brahui people in Pakistan and nearby regions
Dravidian language of Pakistan

Examples

Linguists are fascinated by Brahui because it is geographically separated from other Dravidian languages.
He is fluent in both Brahui and Baluchi.
Researchers are working to preserve the Brahui language and its oral traditions.

Etymology

Name derives from the people who speak the language, the Brahui.
