Limax

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪ.mæks / (AmE), / ˈlɪ.maks / (BrE)
A genus of air-breathing land slugs in the family Limacidae.
a genus of land slugs

Examples

The common European garden slug belongs to the genus Limax.
Researchers studied the feeding habits of Limax species in urban areas.
Specimens of Limax were collected and observed under laboratory conditions.

Etymology

From Latin *limax* (slug, snail).
