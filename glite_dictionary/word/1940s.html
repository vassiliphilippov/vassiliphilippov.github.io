1940s

1. NOUN

/ naɪnˈtiːn ˈfɔrtiːz / (AmE), / naɪnˈtiːn ˈfɔːtiz / (BrE)
the decade from 1940 to 1949, often noted for World War II and its aftermath
the decade from 1940 to 1949

Examples

Many significant events, including World War II, occurred during the 1940s.
The fashion of the 1940s featured tailored suits and elegant dresses.
My grandparents got married in the early 1940s.
