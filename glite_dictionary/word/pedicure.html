pedicure

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛdɪˌkjʊr / (AmE), / ˈpɛdɪˌkjʊə / (BrE)
a professional treatment for the feet and toenails, usually involving cleaning, shaping, and often painting the nails
professional foot and toenail care

Examples

I'm treating myself to a pedicure at the new salon this weekend.
She regularly gets a pedicure to keep her feet looking neat and healthy.
After a long hike, a relaxing pedicure felt wonderful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manicure - professional treatment for the hands and fingernails

Etymology

From Latin 'pes' (foot) + 'cura' (care).

2. VERB

/ ˈpɛdɪˌkjʊr / (AmE), / ˈpɛdɪˌkjʊə / (BrE)
to care for or treat the feet and toenails, especially by cutting, cleaning, and shaping the nails
care for or treat feet and toenails

Examples

She likes to pedicure her feet every weekend at the spa.
Some people prefer to pedicure their own nails at home to save money.
The technician will pedicure your toenails before applying polish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manicure - to care for or treat the hands and fingernails

Etymology

From French pédicure, from Latin pes (foot) + cura (care).

Synset POS: v
