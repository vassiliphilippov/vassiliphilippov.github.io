scan

1. NOUN

/ skæn / (AmE), / skæn / (BrE)
an image obtained by using a scanner, especially one produced during a medical examination
image from a scanner, often medical

Examples

The doctor reviewed the scan to diagnose the patient’s condition.
Her MRI scan revealed no abnormalities in the brain.
He received the scan results later that afternoon.

Synset Examples

he analyzed the brain scan
you could see the tumor in the CAT scan

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scan (verb) - to use a scanner to create an image
