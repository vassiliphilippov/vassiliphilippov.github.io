Pujunan

1. NOUN

/ puːˈdʒuːnən / (AmE), / pjuːˈdʒuːnən / (BrE)
A Penutian language spoken by the Maidu people of California.
Penutian language of the Maidu

Examples

Few speakers of Pujunan remain today, making language preservation efforts crucial.
Linguists have studied Pujunan to understand its unique features within the Penutian family.
Traditional Maidu stories are often told in Pujunan, preserving cultural heritage.
