antebellum

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.təˈbɛl.əm / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪˈbɛl.əm / (BrE)
relating to the period before a war, especially the American Civil War
before-war, esp. US Civil War

Examples

The grand plantation house reflected antebellum architecture.
Many Southern cities preserve their antebellum mansions as tourist attractions.
The novel is set in the antebellum South, before the outbreak of the Civil War.

Etymology

From Latin ante bellum (before the war).
