Meliaceae

1. NOUN

/ ˌmiːliˈeɪsiːˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˌmiːliˈeɪsiːˌaɪ / (BrE)
a family of tropical trees and shrubs, including many important timber and ornamental species, such as mahogany
family of tropical trees and shrubs (mahogany family)

Examples

The Meliaceae family includes economically valuable species like mahogany and neem.
Many members of the Meliaceae have fragrant flowers and attractive foliage, making them popular ornamentals.
Researchers are studying the medicinal properties of plants within the Meliaceae family.

Etymology

From New Latin *Meliaceae*, based on the type genus *Melia* and the standard suffix for plant family names, *-aceae*.
