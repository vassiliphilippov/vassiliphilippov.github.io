red-purple

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɛdˈpɝːpəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛdˈpɜːpl̩ / (BrE)
A color consisting of purple with a noticeable tinge of red.
a shade of purple with red undertones

Examples

She chose a red-purple dress for the party, making her stand out in the crowd.
The artist mixed paints to create the perfect red-purple hue for the sunset.
He described the flower's petals as a vibrant red-purple shade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magenta - a purplish-red color, similar to red-purple
  • Degree Lower: purple - a color at the boundary of red and blue, without a strong red tinge
  • Degree Higher: red - the primary color that, when added to purple, creates red-purple
