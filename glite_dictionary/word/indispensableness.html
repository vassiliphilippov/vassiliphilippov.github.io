indispensableness

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.dɪˈspɛns.ə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˌɪn.dɪˈspens.ə.bəl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being absolutely necessary or essential
quality of being indispensable

Examples

The indispensableness of clean water for human survival cannot be overstated.
He realized the indispensableness of her advice during the project.
Doctors often stress the indispensableness of regular exercise for good health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indispensability - the quality of being indispensable (more common form)

Etymology

Formed from indispensable + -ness; 'indispensable' from French 'indispensable', from Latin 'indispensabilis' (not able to be distributed).
