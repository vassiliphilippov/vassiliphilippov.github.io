flattop

1. NOUN

/ ˈflætˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈflætˌtɒp / (BrE)
A haircut in which the hair on the top of the head is cut very short and flat, usually worn by men or boys.
closely cropped, flat-topped haircut

Examples

His flattop was perfectly trimmed by the barber.
In high school he wore a flattop to fit in with the sports team.
The soldier's strict flattop made him look even more disciplined.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crew cut - a very short haircut for men and boys

2. NOUN

/ ˈflætˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈflætˌtɒp / (BrE)
a large warship with a long flat deck for planes to take off and land on; an aircraft carrier
aircraft carrier warship

Examples

The navy deployed a flattop to the region as a show of force.
During the ceremony, several fighter jets took off from the flattop.
The aging flattop was finally retired after decades of service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aircraft carrier - a large warship that serves as a seagoing airbase
