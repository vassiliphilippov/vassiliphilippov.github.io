abasic

1. ADJ

/ əˈbeɪsɪk / (AmE), / əˈbeɪsɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by abasia (inability to walk)
relating to abasia (inability to walk)
medical

Examples

The patient's abasic condition required the use of a wheelchair.
Doctors diagnosed him as abasic after observing his inability to walk unassisted.
Several neurological disorders can result in an abasic state.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abasia - inability to walk due to muscular incoordination

Etymology

From abasia, from Greek 'a-' (without) + 'basis' (step).
