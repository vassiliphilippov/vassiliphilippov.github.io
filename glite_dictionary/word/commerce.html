Commerce

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɑːmɚs / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɜːs / (BrE)
the United States federal department responsible for promoting and administering domestic and foreign trade, including management of the census and the patent office
U.S. federal department for trade and commerce
american legal

Examples

The Department of Commerce released its quarterly report on economic growth.
Many decisions about international trade are made by officials in Commerce.
The U.S. Census Bureau is administered by the Department of Commerce.
