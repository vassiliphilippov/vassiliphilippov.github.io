steelmaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈstiːlˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈstiːlˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person or company that manufactures steel
person or company making steel

Examples

The steelmaker implemented new technologies to reduce emissions at the factory.
Many families in the town relied on the steelmaker for employment.
The international steelmaker reported a significant increase in steel exports last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metallurgist - a person skilled in the science of metals
  • Synonym: steelworker - someone who works in a steel mill

Etymology

From 'steel' + 'maker', meaning one who makes steel.
