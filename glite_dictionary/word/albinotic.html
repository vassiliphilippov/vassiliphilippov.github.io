albinotic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæl.bɪˈnɑː.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌæl.bɪˈnɒ.tɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or affected by albinism; having the characteristics of an albino.
relating to or affected by albinism
medical

Examples

The albinotic rabbit had pure white fur and pink eyes.
Doctors observed albinotic changes in the patient's retina during the examination.
Certain fish species have populated dark caves with predominantly albinotic individuals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: albino - a person or animal with albinism

Etymology

Formed from 'albino' + '-tic', from Latin 'albus' (white).
