farsightedness

1. NOUN

/ fɑrˈsaɪtɪdnəs / (AmE), / fɑːˈsaɪtɪdnəs / (BrE)
The ability to anticipate future difficulties or needs and make preparations in advance; foresight.
ability to anticipate and plan for the future

Examples

Her farsightedness allowed the company to avoid many potential setbacks.
With remarkable farsightedness, the mayor invested in infrastructure that benefited future generations.
They admired his farsightedness in preparing for both success and failure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foresight - the ability to predict what will happen or be needed in the future
  • Antonym: shortsightedness - lack of foresight or planning for the future

2. NOUN

/ ˈfɑrˌsaɪtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈfɑːˌsaɪtɪdnəs / (BrE)
a condition in which distant objects are seen more clearly than near objects, usually due to the eye's inability to focus correctly on close objects
difficulty seeing near objects clearly
medical

Examples

Due to her farsightedness, she found it difficult to read small print without glasses.
People with farsightedness often need corrective lenses for close tasks like sewing or reading.
Routine eye exams can help detect farsightedness early and prevent eye strain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hyperopia - the medical term for farsightedness; distant objects are seen more clearly than nearby ones
  • Antonym: nearsightedness - a condition in which close objects are seen clearly but distant ones are blurry

3. NOUN

/ ˈfɑːrˌsaɪtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈfɑːˌsaɪtɪdnəs / (BrE)
A condition in which distant objects are seen more clearly than near ones, often caused by loss of elasticity of the eye's lens with age.
reduced ability to focus on near objects
medical

Examples

She began to notice her farsightedness when reading menus became difficult.
Corrective lenses can help people cope with farsightedness as they age.
His farsightedness was diagnosed during a routine eye exam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: presbyopia - age-related farsightedness due to reduced lens elasticity
  • Antonym: nearsightedness - difficulty seeing distant objects clearly
