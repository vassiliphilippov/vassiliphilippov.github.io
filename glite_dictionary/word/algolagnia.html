algolagnia

1. NOUN

/ ˌælɡəˈlæɡniə / (AmE), / ˌalɡəˈlaɡnɪə / (BrE)
Sexual pleasure derived from inflicting or experiencing pain.
sexual pleasure from pain
medical

Examples

Some researchers have explored the psychological roots of algolagnia in certain individuals.
The practice of algolagnia may overlap with aspects of sadomasochism.
A person with algolagnia may experience pleasure in both giving and receiving pain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masochism - sexual gratification from being subjected to pain or humiliation
  • Synonym: sadism - sexual enjoyment obtained from inflicting pain on others

Etymology

From Greek 'algos' (pain) + 'lagneia' (lust).
