purple-black

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝːpəl ˈblæk / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈblæk / (BrE)
Of a color that is black tinged with purple.
black with a purple tint (color)

Examples

The artist chose a purple-black paint for the stormy night sky in her painting.
He wore a striking purple-black suit that stood out at the party.
The flower petals were a deep purple-black, almost appearing velvety in the evening light.
