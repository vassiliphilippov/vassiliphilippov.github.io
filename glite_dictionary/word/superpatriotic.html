superpatriotic

1. ADJ

/ ˌsuːpərˌpeɪtriˈɑːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˌpætriˈɒtɪk / (BrE)
Extremely or fanatically patriotic; showing excessive devotion to one's country.
fanatically patriotic

Examples

The senator's superpatriotic speeches often bordered on nationalism.
Some media outlets promote a superpatriotic agenda, demanding unwavering loyalty to the nation.
His superpatriotic enthusiasm made him intolerant of any criticism of his country.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: patriotic - having or expressing devotion to and vigorous support for one's country

Etymology

Formed by combining 'super-' (meaning 'above, beyond') with 'patriotic'.
