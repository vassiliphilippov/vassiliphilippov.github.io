bota

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊ.tə / (AmE), / ˈbəʊ.tə / (BrE)
a traditional wine bottle or pouch made of leather, typically used in Spain
leather wine bottle or pouch

Examples

He poured the red wine from the bota during the picnic.
Travelers in Spain often carry a bota filled with local wine.
She bought an authentic bota as a souvenir from the market.

Etymology

From Spanish 'bota' meaning 'wineskin' or 'leather bottle'.
