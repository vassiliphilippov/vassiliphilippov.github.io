striped

1. ADJ

/ ˈstraɪpt / (AmE), / ˈstraɪpt / (BrE)
marked or decorated with stripes or bands of color, texture, or material
marked with stripes

Examples

She wore a striped dress that stood out at the party.
The striped cat curled up on the sunny windowsill.
I bought a striped towel for my summer vacation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: plain - not decorated with stripes or patterns
  • Antonym: spotted - marked or decorated with spots
