glamping

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlæmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡlæmpɪŋ / (BrE)
A style of camping with luxurious amenities and comforts, often including expensive equipment or accommodation.
luxurious style of camping

Examples

This resort specializes in glamping, offering guests heated tents and personal chefs.
They decided to try glamping instead of traditional camping for their family vacation.
Glamping is becoming increasingly popular among travelers who want both adventure and comfort.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: camping - the activity of spending time living outdoors in a tent or camper

Etymology

Blend of 'glamorous' and 'camping'.
