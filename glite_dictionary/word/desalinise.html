to desalinise

1. VERB

/ dɪˈseɪləˌnaɪz / (AmE), / dɪˈseɪlɪnaɪz / (BrE)
to remove salt from (something, especially water)
remove salt from (esp. water)

Examples

Many countries use large plants to desalinise seawater for drinking.
The company hopes to desalinise brackish water to support agriculture in arid regions.
New technology has made it more affordable to desalinise ocean water.

Synset Examples

desalinate water

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to desalinate - to remove salt from (especially water)

Etymology

From de- (removal of) and saline (salty), modelled after 'desalinate'.
