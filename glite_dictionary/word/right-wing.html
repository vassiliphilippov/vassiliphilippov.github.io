right-wing

1. ADJ

/ ˈraɪt wɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraɪt wɪŋ / (BrE)
supporting or relating to conservative political views or policies that are typically associated with the political right
supporting conservative political views

Examples

Her right-wing beliefs often influenced her opinions on economic policy.
The party proposed a series of right-wing reforms after winning the election.
Many voters are attracted to right-wing candidates who promise lower taxes and stricter immigration controls.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: left-wing - supporting or relating to liberal or socialist political views
