natator

1. NOUN

/ nəˈteɪtər / (AmE), / nəˈtɛɪtə / (BrE)
a person who swims, especially one who travels through water by swimming
person who swims

Examples

The expert natator crossed the channel in record time.
As a competitive natator, she trains every morning before work.
Marine biologists observed the young natator adapting quickly to the ocean currents.

Synset Examples

he is not a good swimmer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimmer - a person who is able to swim or who swims well

Etymology

From Latin 'natator,' meaning swimmer, from 'natare' (to swim).
