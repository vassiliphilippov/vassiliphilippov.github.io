treelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈtriˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtriːlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling a tree in shape or branching structure.
resembling a tree in form or structure

Her treelike diagram helped explain the complex organization of the company.
Scientists observed treelike growth patterns in the newly discovered fungus.
