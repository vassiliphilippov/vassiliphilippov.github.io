diamantine

1. ADJ

/ ˌdaɪ.əˈmæn.tin / (AmE), / ˌdɪə.mænˈtiːn / (BrE)
consisting of diamonds or having the appearance or brilliance of diamonds
resembling or made of diamonds

Examples

The chandelier sparkled with a diamantine brilliance.
Her dress shimmered in the light with a diamantine sheen.
A diamantine necklace adorned her neck, catching everyone's eye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diamond - a precious stone consisting of a clear and typically colorless crystalline form of pure carbon

Etymology

From French 'diamantin', from 'diamant' (diamond).
