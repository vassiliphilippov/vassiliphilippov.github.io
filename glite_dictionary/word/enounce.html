to enounce

1. VERB

/ ɪˈnaʊns / (AmE), / ɪˈnaʊns / (BrE)
to speak, pronounce, or utter words in a particular way
to pronounce or speak words

Examples

It is important to enounce every syllable clearly during the recital.
She carefully enounced the foreign terms to help the class understand their meaning.
When learning a new language, students are encouraged to enounce each word distinctly.

Synset Examples

She pronounces French words in a funny way
I cannot say ‘zip wire’
Can the child sound out this complicated word?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to pronounce - to make the sound of a word or letter in a particular way

Etymology

From French 'énoncer', from Latin 'enuntiare' (to announce, declare).
