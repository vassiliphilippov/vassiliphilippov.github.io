malarial

1. ADJ

/ məˈlɛri.əl / (AmE), / məˈlɑːri.əl / (BrE)
relating to or affected by malaria; characteristic of malaria.
relating to or affected by malaria
medical

Examples

The patient was treated for a severe malarial infection after returning from abroad.
Many cases of malarial fever are reported during the rainy season.
The village has a high risk of malarial outbreaks due to standing water.

Synset Examples

malarial fever

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malaria - an infectious disease transmitted by mosquitoes

Etymology

From the noun 'malaria' + suffix '-al'. 'Malaria' from Italian 'mala aria' meaning 'bad air'.
