punk-ass

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌŋk ˌæs / (AmE), / ˈpʌŋk ˌɑːs / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) A contemptuous or insulting term for someone regarded as cowardly, weak, or acting in an immature way.
insult for a cowardly or immature person
slang offensive

Examples

You don't have to be a punk-ass just because things got tough.
If you keep acting like a punk-ass, no one will take you seriously.
She called him a punk-ass after he backed out of the challenge.

Synset Examples

Stop acting like a punk-ass and take responsibility.
He called her a punk-ass for not standing up.
Don't be such a punk-ass about the homework.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage
  • Synonym: chicken - (slang) someone who is afraid
