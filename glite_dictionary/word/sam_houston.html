Sam Houston

1. PROPN

/ sæm ˈhaʊstən / (AmE), / sæm ˈhaʊstən / (BrE)
(1793–1863) United States politician and military leader known for leading the fight for Texas's independence from Mexico and serving as president of the Republic of Texas.
U.S. statesman and Texas independence leader

Examples

The city of Houston, Texas, is named after Sam Houston, a key figure in the state's early history.
Sam Houston played a crucial role in securing Texas's independence from Mexico.
Many monuments in Texas commemorate the achievements of Sam Houston.
