Karl Marx

1. PROPN

/ kɑrl mɑrks / (AmE), / kɑːl mɑːks / (BrE)
(1818–1883) German philosopher, economist, and revolutionary socialist; founder of modern communism, known for works including the Communist Manifesto and Das Kapital.
German philosopher and founder of communism

Examples

Many consider Karl Marx one of the most influential thinkers in modern history.
The theories of Karl Marx have shaped political movements worldwide.
I read a biography of Karl Marx for my political science class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Friedrich Engels - German philosopher and collaborator with Karl Marx on the Communist Manifesto
