tearfully

1. ADV

/ ˈtɪr.fəli / (AmE), / ˈtɪə.fəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows or expresses tears or sadness.
in a tearful or sad manner

Examples

She tearfully accepted the award, thanking everyone who supported her.
He tearfully apologized for his mistake in front of the whole class.
They hugged tearfully at the train station, promising to stay in touch.

Synset Examples

the man confessed tearfully to having beaten his wife

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sadly - in a sad manner
  • Antonym: cheerfully - in a cheerful manner

Etymology

From 'tearful' + '-ly', formed from 'tear' (noun, meaning a drop of liquid from the eye) and '-ful', plus the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
