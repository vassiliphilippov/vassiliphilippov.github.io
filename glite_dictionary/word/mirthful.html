mirthful

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɝθ.fəl / (AmE), / ˈmɜːθ.fəl / (BrE)
Full of or showing high-spirited joy or happiness; merry.
full of happiness or merriment

Examples

Her mirthful laughter echoed throughout the hall, filling the room with joy.
The children’s mirthful faces showed their excitement at the festival.
He greeted everyone with a mirthful smile that brightened their day.

Synset Examples

a jovial old gentleman

peals of merry laughter
a mirthful laugh

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joyful - feeling or showing great joy
  • Synonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic
  • Antonym: solemn - not cheerful or smiling; serious

Etymology

From Middle English *myrthe* (mirth) + *-ful* (full of).
