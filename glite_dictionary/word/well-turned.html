well-turned

1. ADJ

/ wɛl ˈtɝnd / (AmE), / wɛl ˈtɜːnd / (BrE)
(of language) expressed in a manner that is apt, polished, and pleasing.
aptly and elegantly expressed (of language)

Examples

The author is known for her well-turned sentences and vivid imagery.
He delivered a well-turned response that impressed the entire panel.
Critics praised the novel for its well-turned phrases and witty dialogue.

Synset Examples

a well-turned phrase

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elegant - graceful and stylishly expressed
  • Antonym: awkward - clumsily or inelegantly expressed
