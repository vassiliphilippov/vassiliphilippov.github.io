sincerely

1. ADV

/ sɪnˈsɪrli / (AmE), / sɪnˈsɪəli / (BrE)
In a sincere or genuine way; without pretense, falseness, or deceit.
in a genuine, honest way

Examples

We sincerely hope that you enjoy your stay with us.
She apologized sincerely for her mistake, and everyone accepted her apology.
He sincerely believes that his efforts will make a difference.

Synset Examples

she praised him sincerely for his victory
was unfeignedly glad to see his old teacher
we are truly sorry for the inconvenience

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genuinely - in a sincere and honest way
  • Antonym: insincerely - in a way that is not sincere or genuine

Etymology

From sincere + -ly; 'sincere' from Latin sincerus (clean, pure, sound).
