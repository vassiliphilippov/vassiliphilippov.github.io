tribesman

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraɪbzmən / (AmE), / ˈtraɪbzmən / (BrE)
a man who is a member of a tribe, especially in a traditional society
male member of a tribe

Examples

The tribesman greeted the visitors with a warm smile and curious eyes.
Each tribesman participated in the annual ceremony to honor their ancestors.
As a skilled hunter, the young tribesman provided food for his community.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tribeswoman - a woman who is a member of a tribe

Etymology

Formed from 'tribe' + 'man.'
