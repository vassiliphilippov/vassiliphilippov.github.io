vetchling

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛtʃ.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɛtʃ.lɪŋ / (BrE)
any of several small climbing plants of the genus Lathyrus, typically having tendrils and pea-like flowers
a climbing plant of the genus Lathyrus

Examples

The meadow was dotted with bright purple vetchling in early summer.
Gardeners often plant vetchling to attract bees and butterflies.
Some species of vetchling are used as ornamentals because of their colorful flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vetch - any of various leguminous plants of the genus Vicia

Etymology

From 'vetch' (a type of leguminous plant) + diminutive suffix '-ling'.
