Braque

1. PROPN

/ brɑk / (AmE), / brɑːk / (BrE)
Georges Braque (1882–1963), a French painter who was a leading figure in the development of Cubism.
French Cubist painter (1882–1963)

Examples

The influence of Braque on modern art is still evident today.
Alongside Picasso, Braque helped shape the cubist movement in early 20th-century Paris.
I visited an exhibition dedicated to Braque and his remarkable works.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Picasso - Spanish painter and co-founder of Cubism
