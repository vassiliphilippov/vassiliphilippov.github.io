wizardly

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪzərdli / (AmE), / ˈwɪzədli / (BrE)
Having the qualities, powers, or abilities of a wizard; relating to or characteristic of a wizard or supernatural powers.
having qualities of a wizard

Examples

The magician performed a wizardly trick that left the audience amazed.
Her wizardly knowledge of computers made her indispensable to the team.
The castle was filled with wizardly artifacts and ancient scrolls.

Synset Examples

charming incantations
magic signs that protect against adverse influence
a magical spell
'tis now the very witching time of night
wizard wands
wizardly powers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magical - relating to magic or possessing magical power
  • Synonym: sorcerous - relating to or characteristic of sorcery

Etymology

From "wizard" + "-ly," forming an adjective meaning 'like a wizard.'
