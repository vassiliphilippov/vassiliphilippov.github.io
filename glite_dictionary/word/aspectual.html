aspectual

1. ADJ

/ æˈspɛktʃuəl / (AmE), / æˈspɛktjʊəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of grammatical aspect, especially in reference to verbs in a language.
relating to grammatical aspect

Examples

The aspectual differences between English and Russian verbs are quite significant.
Linguists discuss the aspectual system to explain how languages express time and completion.
His dissertation analyzes aspectual markers in several African languages.

Synset Examples

the aspectual system of Greek

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aspect - a grammatical category expressing how an action, event, or state extends over time

Etymology

From 'aspect' (from Latin 'aspectus', appearance) + '-ual'.
