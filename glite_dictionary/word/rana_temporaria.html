Rana temporaria

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪnə tɛmpəˈrɛriə / (AmE), / ˈrɑːnə tɛmpəˈreːrɪə / (BrE)
a common species of semiterrestrial frog found throughout Europe, also known as the common frog
common European frog species

Examples

The wetlands provide an ideal habitat for the Rana temporaria.
Researchers are studying the decline of Rana temporaria populations in northern Europe.
Unlike other species, Rana temporaria can survive in a wide range of environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: common frog - the usual English common name for Rana temporaria

Etymology

From Latin: 'Rana' (frog) + 'temporaria' (temporary, but here used as a species designation).
