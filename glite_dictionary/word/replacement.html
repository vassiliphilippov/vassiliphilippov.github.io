replacement

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈpleɪsmənt / (AmE), / rɪˈpleɪsmənt / (BrE)
a person or thing that takes the place of another
person or thing taking another's place

Examples

The company is seeking a replacement for their retiring CEO.
When her old phone broke, she bought a replacement the next day.
He was called in as a replacement for the injured player.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: substitute - a person or thing acting or serving in place of another

Etymology

From replace +‎ -ment; 'replace' from French remplacer (to put back in place).
