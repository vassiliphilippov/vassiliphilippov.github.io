Sao Tome and Principe

1. PROPN

/ saʊ toʊˈmeɪ ænd ˌprɪnˈsɪpeɪ / (AmE), / saʊ təʊˈmeɪ ænd ˌprɪnˈsɪpeɪ / (BrE)
an island country in the South Atlantic Ocean, off the west coast of Central Africa, consisting of two main islands and several smaller ones; independent from Portugal since 1975
island country in the South Atlantic Ocean

Examples

Tourists are increasingly drawn to Sao Tome and Principe for its untouched beaches and biodiversity.
Sao Tome and Principe gained independence from Portugal in 1975.
The government of Sao Tome and Principe is investing in sustainable development initiatives.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'São Tomé e Príncipe', meaning 'Saint Thomas and Prince'.
