best-loved

1. ADJ

/ ˌbɛstˈlʌvd / (AmE), / ˌbɛstˈlʌvd / (BrE)
Most preferred or cherished above all others; especially valued or favoured.
most cherished or favoured

Examples

Her best-loved novel has been translated into more than twenty languages.
This teddy bear is my son's best-loved possession.
The artist's best-loved works were displayed at the retrospective exhibition.

Synset Examples

the favored child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: favorite - one that is regarded with special favor or affection
  • Antonym: least-loved - the one regarded with the least affection, not cherished
