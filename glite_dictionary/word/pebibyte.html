pebibyte

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛbɪˌbaɪt / (AmE), / ˈpiːbɪˌbaɪt / (BrE)
A unit of information equal to 1,024 tebibytes or 2^50 bytes.
unit of digital information (2^50 bytes)

Examples

A single pebibyte of storage can hold a vast amount of scientific data.
Many organizations have yet to require more than one pebibyte of cloud capacity.
The new archiving system is capable of managing several pebibytes of information.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: tebibyte - a unit of information equal to 1,024 gibibytes or 2^40 bytes
  • Degree Higher: exbibyte - a unit of information equal to 1,024 pebibytes or 2^60 bytes

Etymology

Formed from the SI prefix 'pebi-' (patterned after peta-, indicating 2^50) and 'byte'.
