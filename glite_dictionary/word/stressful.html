stressful

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrɛsfəl / (AmE), / ˈstrɛsfəl / (BrE)
causing a lot of worry or tension; making you feel nervous or anxious
causing worry or anxiety

Examples

The first week at my new job was incredibly stressful but I soon adjusted.
Preparing for the final exams can be a very stressful experience for students.
He found juggling work and family responsibilities extremely stressful.

Synset Examples

nerve-racking noise
the stressful days before a war
a trying day at the office

Related Terms

  • Antonym: relaxing - making you feel calm and less worried
  • Synonym: nerve-racking - making you feel very nervous or worried

Etymology

From 'stress' + '-ful', formed in 20th-century English.
