dashy

1. ADJ

/ ˈdæʃi / (AmE), / ˈdæʃi / (BrE)
(informal) Attractive, stylish, or showy in appearance; flashy.
stylish or showy in appearance
informal

Examples

He drove a dashy car that caught everyone's attention at the party.
The singer wore a dashy suit on stage, sparkling under the lights.
She prefers a more classic look, but her brother loves anything that's bold and dashy.

Synset Examples

He drove a dashy car that everyone admired.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flashy - showy or ostentatious in appearance
  • Synonym: stylish - fashionably elegant or smart
  • Antonym: plain - simple or unadorned in appearance
