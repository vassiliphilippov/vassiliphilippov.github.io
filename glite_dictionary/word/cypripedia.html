cypripedia

1. NOUN

/ sɪˌprɪˈpidiə / (AmE), / sɪˌprɪˈpiːdɪə / (BrE)
any plant or flower of the genus Cypripedium, commonly known as lady's slipper orchids
a plant of the Cypripedium genus (lady's slipper orchid)

Examples

The rare cypripedia thrived in the shaded forest undergrowth.
Botanists are studying the pollination habits of various cypripedia.
We discovered a patch of beautiful cypripedia during our nature walk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ladyslipper - a common name for orchids of the genus Cypripedium

Etymology

From the Latin genus name Cypripedium, derived from Greek kupris (Venus) + pedilon (slipper), in reference to the shape of the flower.
