Balochi

1. NOUN

/ bəˈloʊtʃi / (AmE), / bəˈləʊtʃi / (BrE)
an Iranian language spoken chiefly in Pakistan, Iran, Afghanistan, and parts of the Persian Gulf region
Iranian language of Pakistan, Iran, and regions

Examples

She is fluent in Balochi as well as Urdu and English.
Anthropologists have published several studies on the Balochi language and its dialects.
The film includes subtitles in both Balochi and Farsi to reach a wider audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Persian - the most widely spoken Iranian language

Etymology

From Baloch, the name of the people who speak the language, with -i forming the language name.
