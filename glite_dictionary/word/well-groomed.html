well-groomed

1. ADJ

/ wɛl ˈɡruːmd / (AmE), / wɛl ˈɡruːmd / (BrE)
neat, tidy, and wearing tasteful or appropriate clothing; carefully maintained in appearance
neatly or tastefully dressed and cared for

Examples

She always looks well-groomed at work, with her hair perfectly styled and clothes immaculate.
The well-groomed garden showed the care and attention of its owners.
Even after a long flight, he managed to appear well-groomed and professional.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unkempt - having an untidy or messy appearance

2. ADJ

/ wɛl ˈɡrumd / (AmE), / wɛl ˈɡruːmd / (BrE)
(of a person) having a neat and tidy appearance, especially through well-combed hair and clean, cared-for clothes.
having a neat and tidy appearance

Examples

He always arrived at work looking well-groomed and professional.
The well-groomed woman turned heads as she walked into the room.
A well-groomed appearance can make a great first impression.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unkempt - having a messy or untidy appearance
