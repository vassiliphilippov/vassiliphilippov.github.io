disapproving

1. ADJ

/ ˌdɪs.əˈpru.vɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.əˈpruː.vɪŋ / (BrE)
showing or expressing disapproval of someone or something
expressing disapproval

Examples

Her father gave her a disapproving look when she came home late.
The committee issued a disapproving statement about the proposed changes.
He shook his head in a disapproving manner after hearing the news.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: approving - showing or expressing approval
  • Synonym: to disapprove - to have or express an unfavorable opinion
