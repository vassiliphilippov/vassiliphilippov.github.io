handwork

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈhændˌwɜːk / (BrE)
A work or object made by hand rather than by machine, typically showing manual skill.
work produced by hand

Examples

She admired the handwork displayed at the local craft fair.
Each piece of pottery is unique and shows the artist's handwork.
The quilt was a testament to her grandmother's careful handwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handicraft - an activity or object made skillfully by hand

Etymology

From Old English 'hand' + 'work'; calque of similar formations in Germanic languages.
