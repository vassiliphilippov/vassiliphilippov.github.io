activeness

1. NOUN

/ ˈæktɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˈæktɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being active; energetic participation or involvement in activities
state of being active

Examples

Her activeness in class discussions impresses the teacher every day.
Increased activeness among the community members led to positive changes.
Regular exercise can improve mental health by boosting activeness and alertness.

Synset Examples

his sphere of activity
he is out of action

Related Terms

  • Synonym: activity - the condition in which things are happening or being done
  • Antonym: passivity - the state of being passive, not active or engaging

Etymology

From 'active' + '-ness', forming a noun indicating a state or quality.

2. NOUN

/ ˈæktɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˈæktɪvnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being active, energetic, or lively
state of being active or energetic

Examples

Her activeness in class discussions made her stand out among her peers.
Regular exercise can help maintain activeness well into old age.
The manager appreciated his team's activeness in pursuing new business opportunities.

Synset Examples

the level of activity declines with age

Related Terms

  • Synonym: activity - the condition of being active or engaged in action
  • Antonym: passivity - the trait of being passive or inactive

Etymology

From active + -ness, forming a noun meaning the state of being active.
