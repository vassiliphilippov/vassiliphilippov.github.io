Khirghiz language

1. NOUN

/ kɪrˈɡiz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / kɪəˈɡiːz ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Turkic language spoken primarily by the Kyrgyz people of Kyrgyzstan and neighboring regions.
Turkic language of Kyrgyzstan

Examples

She is fluent in both Russian and Khirghiz language.
The Khirghiz language uses a variant of the Cyrillic alphabet.
Linguists have studied the grammar of the Khirghiz language extensively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kyrgyz - the Kyrgyz language, also spelled Kirghiz; synonym of Khirghiz language

Etymology

From Russian Киргиз (Kirgyz), itself from the Kyrgyz endonym Кыргыз (Kyrgyz).
