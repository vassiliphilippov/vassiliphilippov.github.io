unthinkingly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈθɪŋkɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɪŋkɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows a lack of careful thought or consideration; without thinking
without thinking or consideration

Examples

She unthinkingly agreed to the plan without considering the consequences.
He unthinkingly repeated the rumor to his friends.
They unthinkingly threw away the documents they actually needed.

Synset Examples

he stared thoughtlessly at the picture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtlessly - in a thoughtless manner; without consideration

Etymology

From 'unthinking' + '-ly'; 'unthinking' meaning 'not giving thought', with the adverbial suffix '-ly'.

2. ADV

/ ʌnˈθɪŋkɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɪŋkɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows a lack of careful thought or consideration; thoughtlessly.
in a thoughtless manner

Examples

He apologized for his unthinkingly rude remarks at the meeting.
She unthinkingly agreed to the proposal, not realizing its consequences.
The comment was made unthinkingly, without any intention to offend.

Synset Examples

he stared thoughtlessly at the picture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtlessly - without thinking; carelessly

Etymology

Formed from 'unthinking' + '-ly', with 'un-' (not) + 'think' + '-ing'.

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: r
