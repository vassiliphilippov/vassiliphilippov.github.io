unconfirmed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkənˈfɜrmd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈfɜːmd / (BrE)
not established as true, certain, or final; not yet verified or officially accepted
not verified or officially established

Examples

The news of the merger remains unconfirmed by official sources.
We received several unconfirmed reports about delays at the airport.
There are unconfirmed sightings of the rare bird in this area.

Synset Examples

an unconfirmed letter of credit
unconfirmed rumors

Related Terms

  • Antonym: confirmed - verified or established as true or certain
  • Synonym: verified - checked for truth or accuracy

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'confirmed' (from Latin confirmare 'to strengthen, establish').
