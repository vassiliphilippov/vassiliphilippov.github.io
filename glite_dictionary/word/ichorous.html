ichorous

1. ADJ

/ ˈaɪkərəs / (AmE), / ˈɪkərəs / (BrE)
relating to or resembling ichor, especially a thin, watery, or foul-smelling discharge from a wound or ulcer
resembling ichor or a thin wound discharge
medical

Examples

The wound had an ichorous appearance, with a clear, fetid discharge.
Doctors noted an ichorous fluid oozing from the infected incision.
An ichorous ulcer on the leg took several weeks to heal.

Synset Examples

an ichorous discharge
the sanious discharge from an ulcer

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: purulent - containing or resembling pus

Etymology

From Greek *ichōr* (fluid that flows in the veins of the gods) + English suffix -ous.
