specked

1. ADJ

/ spɛkt / (AmE), / spɛkt / (BrE)
marked or covered with small spots or dots.
marked with small spots or dots

Examples

Her dress was specked with tiny blue dots, giving it a delicate appearance.
After painting, his arms were specked with drops of white paint.
The surface of the egg is specked with brown and black spots.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotted - marked with spots
  • Synonym: speckled - covered or marked with many small spots or patches

Etymology

From the noun 'speck' (small spot or mark), from Middle English 'specke', of Germanic origin.
