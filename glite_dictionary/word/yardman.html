yardman

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɑrdˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈjɑːdˌman / (BrE)
A man employed to work in a railway yard, often handling trains, switches, or freight.
male worker in a railway yard

Examples

After many years as a yardman, he knew every track and switch in the depot.
The yardman coordinated the arrangement of freight cars in the busy railway yard.
Becoming a yardman required a strong understanding of railway safety procedures.

2. NOUN

/ ˈjɑrdˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈjɑːdmæn / (BrE)
a man employed to do outdoor work such as mowing lawns or maintaining grounds
man hired for outdoor or yard work

Examples

The yardman arrived early in the morning to cut the grass and trim the hedges.
We hired a yardman to help maintain the gardens during the summer.
As a yardman, he was responsible for raking leaves and clearing snow from the walkways.
