online

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑnˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɒnˌlaɪn / (BrE)
(of a device, system, or service) connected to a computer network and able to be accessed or used through the internet.
connected to a computer network

Examples

The company offers a wide range of online courses for adult learners.
She prefers to do her shopping online rather than visiting stores in person.
An online banking system allows customers to manage their accounts remotely.

Synset Examples

an on-line database

Related Terms

  • Antonym: offline - not connected to or controlled by a computer network

Etymology

Formed in English from 'on' + 'line,' originally referring to machines or communication lines activated or in use. 20th century: adapted for digital networks.

connected to a computer network

The store offers both online and in-person shopping options.
