specifiable

1. ADJ

/ spɛˈsɪfəbl̩ / (AmE), / spɛˈsɪfəbl̩ / (BrE)
capable of being stated, described, or specified in detail
able to be specified or described

Examples

The terms of the agreement must be specifiable in writing before work can begin.
Only specifiable costs will be reimbursed by the grant.
Their concerns were vague and not easily specifiable.

Synset Examples

specifiable complaints

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unspecifiable - not able to be specified or stated clearly
  • Synonym: definable - able to be defined

Etymology

From 'specify' + '-able', formed after the pattern of 'able' adjectives.
