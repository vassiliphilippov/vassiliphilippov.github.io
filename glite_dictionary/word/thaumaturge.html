thaumaturge

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɔməˌtɜrdʒ / (AmE), / ˈθɔːməˌtɜːdʒ / (BrE)
a person who performs magic or miracles; a magician or miracle worker
worker of magic or miracles

Examples

Some legends describe Merlin as a powerful thaumaturge capable of incredible feats.
The village believed that the old man was a thaumaturge who healed the sick.
In the novel, the protagonist learns from a mysterious thaumaturge in the forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magician - a person with magical powers or who performs magic tricks
  • Synonym: sorcerer - a person who claims or is believed to have magical powers

Etymology

From Greek thaumatourgos, from thauma 'wonder' + ergon 'work'.
