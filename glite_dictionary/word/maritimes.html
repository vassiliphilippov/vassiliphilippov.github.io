Maritimes

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɛrɪˌtaɪmz / (AmE), / ˈmærɪˌtaɪmz / (BrE)
the collective name for the Canadian provinces of New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, and Prince Edward Island
region in eastern Canada

Examples

Many families in the Maritimes rely on fishing and tourism for their livelihoods.
The Maritimes have a rich history of Scottish and Acadian culture.
She spent her summer vacation exploring the beautiful beaches of the Maritimes.

Etymology

From maritime (relating to the sea), reflecting the coastal location of these provinces.
