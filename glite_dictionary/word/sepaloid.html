sepaloid

1. ADJ

/ ˈsiːpəˌlɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈsiːpəlɔɪd / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a sepal, especially in shape or function
resembling a sepal
medical

Examples

The outer floral whorls are sepaloid in appearance, green and leaflike.
Some petals are modified to look sepaloid as an adaptation to protect the bud.
The plant exhibits sepaloid structures that help distinguish its species.

Etymology

From sepal + -oid (Greek 'eidos' meaning form or shape).
