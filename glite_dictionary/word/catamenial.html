catamenial

1. ADJ

/ ˌkætəˈmiːniəl / (AmE), / ˌkatəˈmiːnɪəl / (BrE)
Relating to menstruation or the menstrual period.
relating to menstruation
medical

Examples

The doctor noted a catamenial pattern in her symptoms, correlating with her menstrual cycle.
Some women experience catamenial migraines that coincide with their periods.
The study described several catamenial disorders linked to hormonal changes during menstruation.

Synset Examples

menstrual period

Related Terms

  • Synonym: menstrual - relating to menstruation or menses

Etymology

From Greek katamēnios (monthly), from kata (down) + mēn (month).
