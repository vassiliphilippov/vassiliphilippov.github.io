Yurak-Samoyed

1. NOUN

/ ˌjuːrɑːk səˈmɔɪɛd / (AmE), / ˌjʊərak səˈmɔɪɛd / (BrE)
a Uralic language formerly spoken by the Nenets people of northern Siberia
Uralic language of Siberian Nenets

Examples

The Yurak-Samoyed language is part of the Uralic language family spoken in Arctic Russia.
Linguists have studied Yurak-Samoyed to trace the migration patterns of the Samoyedic peoples.
Few native speakers of Yurak-Samoyed remain, making documentation efforts critically important.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nenets - a Uralic language spoken by the Nenets people of northern Siberia

Etymology

Yurak, an older term for the Nenets people; Samoyed from Russian Самоед, referring to several indigenous groups of northern Siberia.
