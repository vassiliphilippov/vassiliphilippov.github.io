wristband

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪstbænd / (AmE), / ˈrɪstbænd / (BrE)
A band of cloth, leather, or metal attached to a wristwatch and worn around the wrist.
strap for attaching a wristwatch

Examples

The metal wristband of his watch snapped unexpectedly.
She preferred a leather wristband on her wristwatch for both comfort and style.
Many fitness trackers now feature a silicone wristband for durability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strap - a strip of material used for fastening, holding, or carrying something

2. NOUN

/ ˈrɪstˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈrɪst.bænd / (BrE)
A band, typically of cloth, leather, or other material, that fits around the wrist, especially as a part of a garment or as an accessory.
band worn around the wrist

Examples

She wore a colorful wristband to support her favorite charity.
The shirt has a buttoned wristband at the end of each sleeve.
Festival attendees received a fabric wristband as proof of entry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bracelet - an ornamental band or chain worn around the wrist

Etymology

From 'wrist' + 'band', both from Old English origins.
