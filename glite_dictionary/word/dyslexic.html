dyslexic

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈlɛksɪk / (AmE), / dɪsˈlɛksɪk / (BrE)
relating to, or affected by dyslexia, a learning disorder characterized by difficulty in reading or interpreting words and letters
relating to or affected by dyslexia
medical

Examples

Her dyslexic students benefit from extra time on reading assignments.
Being dyslexic can make learning to read more challenging.
There are teaching methods designed specifically for dyslexic children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dyslexia - a learning disorder characterized by difficulties with accurate or fluent word recognition

Etymology

From dyslexia, which comes from Greek dys- (difficult) + lexis (speech, word).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03051922-a
Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ dɪsˈlɛksɪk / (AmE), / dɪsˈlɛksɪk / (BrE)
a person who has dyslexia
person with dyslexia
medical

Examples

As a dyslexic, she developed unique strategies to help her read more easily.
Many dyslexic children benefit from specialized learning tools in school.
Being a dyslexic doesn't stop him from enjoying literature and writing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dyslexia - a disorder that involves difficulty in learning to read or interpret words, letters, and other symbols

Etymology

From 'dyslexia', itself from Greek 'dys-' (bad, difficult) + 'lexis' (speech, word).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-10060414-n
Synset POS: n

3. ADJ

/ dɪsˈlɛksɪk / (AmE), / dɪsˈlɛksɪk / (BrE)
Having difficulty understanding written words, especially as a result of a neurological disorder such as dyslexia.
having difficulty reading due to dyslexia
medical

Examples

Many dyslexic children benefit from specialized instruction in reading.
She was diagnosed as dyslexic after struggling in her first year at school.
Some fonts are designed to help dyslexic readers distinguish between similar letters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dyslexia - a disorder involving difficulty with reading and writing despite normal intelligence

Etymology

From dyslexia + -ic, modeled on other medical adjectives.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01278305-s
Synset POS: s
