dadgum

1. ADJ

/ ˈdædˌɡʌm / (AmE), / ˈdædˌɡʌm / (BrE)
(US, informal) Used as a mild euphemism for 'damned' to express frustration, annoyance, or emphasis; often placed before nouns for emphasis.
mild euphemism for 'damned', expressing frustration
informal american
