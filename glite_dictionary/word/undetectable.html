undetectable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɪˈtɛktəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈtɛktəbl̩ / (BrE)
impossible or nearly impossible to discover, notice, or detect
impossible to detect

Examples

The spy used technology to remain undetectable during his mission.
Her perfume was so light it was almost undetectable.
The differences between the two paintings are undetectable to the untrained eye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invisible - impossible to see
  • Antonym: detectable - able to be discovered or recognized

Etymology

From un- (not) + detectable, based on Latin root 'detegere' (to uncover).
