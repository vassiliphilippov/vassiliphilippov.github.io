cuisine

1. NOUN

/ kwəˈzin / (AmE), / kwɪˈziːn / (BrE)
a style or method of cooking, especially one characteristic of a particular country, region, or establishment
style or method of cooking

Examples

Thai cuisine is famous for its balance of sweet, sour, salty, and spicy flavors.
The restaurant specializes in authentic Italian cuisine.
She enjoys exploring different types of cuisine when traveling abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cooking - the practice of preparing food by combining, mixing, and heating ingredients

Etymology

From French 'cuisine' (kitchen, cooking), from Latin 'coquina.'
