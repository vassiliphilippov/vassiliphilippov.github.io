asseverator

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɛv.əˌreɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / əˈsɛv.əˌreɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person who asserts or affirms something strongly as being true
one who affirms or asserts strongly

Examples

The asseverator maintained his account even under intense questioning.
History remembers her as a fearless asseverator of truth against popular opinion.
In the heated debate, each asseverator insisted their facts were indisputable.

Synset Examples

a bold asserter
a declarer of his intentions
affirmers of traditional doctrine
an asseverator of strong convictions
an avower of his own great intelligence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asserter - a person who asserts or states something
  • Synonym: declarer - one who declares or makes something known formally

Etymology

From Latin asseverator, from asseverare (to assert earnestly).
