Agathis dammara

1. NOUN

/ əˈɡæθɪs dæˈmɑrə / (AmE), / əˈɡæθɪs dæˈmɑːrə / (BrE)
A large evergreen tree native to the Moluccas and the Philippines, notable as a source of dammar resin.
tree native to Moluccas and Philippines; source of dammar resin

Examples

The resin harvested from Agathis dammara is widely used in traditional varnishes.
Many forests in the Philippines are home to the impressive Agathis dammara tree.
Due to overharvesting, wild populations of Agathis dammara are declining in some regions.

Etymology

From genus name Agathis (Greek agathis, a ball of thread) and species epithet dammara (Māori name).
