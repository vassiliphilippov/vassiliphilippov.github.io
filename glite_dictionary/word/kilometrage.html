kilometrage

1. NOUN

/ kɪˈlɑːmɪrɪdʒ / (AmE), / kɪˈlɒmɪrɪdʒ / (BrE)
the number of kilometres traveled by a vehicle; the total distance covered in kilometers.
distance in kilometers traveled by a vehicle

Examples

The low kilometrage of the car made it more attractive to buyers.
Check the kilometrage before deciding on a used vehicle.
Higher kilometrage often means more wear and tear on a car.

Synset Examples

I would expect this car to fall apart, given its kilometrage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mileage - number of miles traveled by a vehicle

Etymology

Formed from 'kilometre' + '-age', modeled after 'mileage'.
