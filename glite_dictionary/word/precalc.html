precalc

1. NOUN

/ ˈpriːˌkælk / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌkalk / (BrE)
A college-level mathematics course covering algebra and trigonometry, intended to prepare students for calculus.
precalculus mathematics course
informal

Examples

I'm taking precalc this semester to get ready for calculus next year.
Her favorite subject in high school was precalc, especially the trigonometry topics.
You need to pass precalc before enrolling in calculus at this university.
