Angolan

1. NOUN

/ ænˈɡoʊlən / (AmE), / ænˈɡəʊlən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Angola
a person from Angola

Examples

The museum features art created by a renowned Angolan.
Many Angolans speak Portuguese as their first language.
As an Angolan, she feels proud of her country's rich culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Angola - a country in southwestern Africa

Etymology

From Angola, the name of the country, with the English suffix -an.

/ ænˈɡoʊlən / (AmE), / ænˈɡəʊlən / (BrE)
