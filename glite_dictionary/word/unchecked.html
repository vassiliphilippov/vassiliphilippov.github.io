unchecked

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtʃɛkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɛkt / (BrE)
not restrained, controlled, or limited; allowed to continue without intervention
not restrained or controlled

Examples

The weeds grew unchecked in the garden, overtaking the flowers by midsummer.
If left unchecked, inflation can damage an entire economy.
His unchecked ambition led to reckless decisions.

Synset Examples

unbridled rage
an unchecked temper
ungoverned rage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unrestrained - not held back; uncontrolled
  • Antonym: controlled - managed, restrained, or directed

Etymology

From un- (not) + checked (past participle of check, from Old French eschequier, from vulgate Latin *scaccus).
