shock-headed

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɑkˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃɒkˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
(of a person) having an untidy or thick, bushy mass of hair.
having untidy or bushy hair

Examples

The shock-headed boy drew everyone's attention as he entered the room.
Her shock-headed appearance made her easy to spot in a crowd.
After sleeping in the tent, I woke up feeling rather shock-headed.

Synset Examples

shock-headed teenagers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shaggy - having long, thick, and unkempt hair

Etymology

From 'shock' (bushy mass of hair) + 'headed'.
