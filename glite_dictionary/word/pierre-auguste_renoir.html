Pierre-Auguste Renoir

1. PROPN

/ piˌɛr oʊˈɡʊst rəˈnwɑr / (AmE), / pjɛːr oˈɡyːst rɛnˈwɑː / (BrE)
(1841–1919) A French impressionist painter known for his vibrant light and saturated color, often focusing on people in intimate and candid compositions.
French impressionist painter

Examples

Pierre-Auguste Renoir is celebrated for his luminous paintings of Parisian life.
The museum's new exhibit features works by Pierre-Auguste Renoir alongside other impressionists.
Art students often study Pierre-Auguste Renoir for his innovative use of light and color.

Etymology

French personal name.
