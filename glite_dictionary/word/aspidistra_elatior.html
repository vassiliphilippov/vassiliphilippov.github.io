Aspidistra elatior

1. NOUN

/ ˌæspɪˈdɪstrə ɛˈleɪtiɔr / (AmE), / ˌaspɪˈdɪstrə ɛˈleɪtɪɔː / (BrE)
an evergreen perennial plant with broad, glossy leaves, often grown indoors as a foliage houseplant
a popular evergreen houseplant with broad leaves

Examples

The Aspidistra elatior thrives in low light and is perfect for interiors.
She decorated her office with a large Aspidistra elatior near the window.
Many gardeners recommend Aspidistra elatior for people new to houseplants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cast-iron plant - another common name for Aspidistra elatior

Etymology

From genus name 'Aspidistra', modern Latin, and species epithet from Latin 'elatior' meaning 'taller, loftier'.
