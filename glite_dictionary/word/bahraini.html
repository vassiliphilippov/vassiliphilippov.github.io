Bahraini

1. ADJ

/ bɑːˈreɪni / (AmE), / bɑːˈreɪni / (BrE)
relating to Bahrain, its people, or its language
relating to Bahrain or its people

Examples

Many tourists visit the Bahraini islands for their beautiful beaches.
She enjoys traditional Bahraini cuisine at local restaurants.
The Bahraini government introduced new policies last year.

Synset Examples

Bahraini beaches

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bahraini - a person from Bahrain

Etymology

From Bahrain, with the adjectival suffix -i.

WordNet ID: oewn-03057570-a

2. NOUN

/ bɑːˈreɪni / (AmE), / bɑːˈreɪni / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Bahrain
person from Bahrain

Examples

The Bahraini welcomed us warmly upon our arrival in Manama.
Several prominent Bahrainis have contributed to the country's growing arts scene.
As a Bahraini, she proudly represented her nation at the international conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bahrainian - a person from Bahrain (less common variant)

Etymology

From Bahrain + -i, forming demonyms and adjectives for nationality.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09711254-n
Synset POS: n
