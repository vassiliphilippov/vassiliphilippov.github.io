wide-body

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪd ˌbɑdi / (AmE), / ˈwaɪd ˌbɒdi / (BrE)
a large commercial aircraft with two aisles, allowing for a wider cabin and more passenger seats
large aircraft with two aisles

Examples

The airline recently added a new wide-body to its international fleet.
Passenger comfort is often better on a wide-body compared to smaller planes.
Many long-haul flights are operated using wide-body aircraft.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: narrow-body - a commercial airliner with a single aisle
