carrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛriər / (AmE), / ˈkæriə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to deliver mail to homes or businesses
person who delivers mail

Examples

The carrier delivered our letters before noon every day.
She greeted the carrier with a smile as he handed her the package.
Rural carriers often drive long distances to complete their routes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mailman - a man who delivers mail
  • Synonym: postman - someone who delivers mail, chiefly British

Etymology

From Old French *carier* (to transport in a vehicle), from Late Latin *carriāre* (to convey in a car or chariot).
