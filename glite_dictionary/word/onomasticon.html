onomasticon

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːnəˈmæstɪkən / (AmE), / ˌɒnəˈmæstɪkən / (BrE)
A list or collection of proper names of persons or places.
list of proper names

Examples

The scholar compiled an onomasticon of ancient Greek personal names for his research.
Many historians consult an onomasticon when analyzing historical documents.
The onomasticon included rare place names that are no longer in use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gazetteer - a geographical directory or list of place names

Etymology

From Greek onomastikon (vocabulary of names), from onoma (name).
