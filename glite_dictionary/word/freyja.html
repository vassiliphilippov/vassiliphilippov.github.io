Freyja

1. PROPN

/ ˈfreɪ.jə / (AmE), / ˈfreɪ.jə / (BrE)
In Norse mythology, the goddess of love, beauty, and fertility, daughter of Njord and sister of Freyr.
Norse goddess of love and fertility

Examples

Many ancient Norse poems tell stories of Freyja and her magical necklace.
Some people leave offerings to Freyja in hopes of finding love or fertility.
According to legend, Freyja rides a chariot drawn by two cats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aphrodite - Greek goddess of love and beauty
  • Degree Lower: Freyr - Norse god of fertility, brother of Freyja

Etymology

From Old Norse Freyja, meaning 'lady', the name of the goddess.
