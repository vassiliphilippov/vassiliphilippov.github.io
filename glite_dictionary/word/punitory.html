punitory

1. ADJ

/ ˈpjuːnɪˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈpjuːnɪtəri / (BrE)
(rare) inflicting or intended as punishment; punitive.
inflicting as punishment

Examples

The judge imposed a punitory fine to deter future misconduct.
The corporation challenged the punitory measures as excessive and unfair.
Such punitory actions are rare in civil cases.

Synset Examples

punitive justice
punitive damages

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punitive - inflicting or intended as punishment

Etymology

From Latin punītorius, from punīre (to punish).
