overrefined

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərɪˈfaɪnd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪˈfaɪnd / (BrE)
Made excessively delicate, elegant, or refined, often to the point of losing practical value or naturalness.
excessively delicate or refined

Examples

Her overrefined manners made her seem insincere to some guests.
The artwork was criticized as overrefined and lacking genuine emotion.
He spoke in such an overrefined way that his true feelings were hard to detect.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: refined - having elegance or good taste
  • Antonym: crude - lacking refinement, unpolished or raw
