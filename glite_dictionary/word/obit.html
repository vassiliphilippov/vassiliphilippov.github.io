obit

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊ.bɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.bɪt / (BrE)
a notice of a person's death, often with a brief biography; an obituary.
death notice or obituary

Examples

The newspaper published a detailed obit for the renowned scientist.
She read her grandfather's obit and was moved by the stories shared within it.
Do you know where I can find yesterday's obit section online?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obituary - a notice of death, typically including a brief biography

Etymology

Shortened from 'obituary', from Medieval Latin 'obituārius'.
