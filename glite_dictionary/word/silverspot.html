silverspot

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvərˌspɑt / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəˌspɒt / (BrE)
A butterfly with silver spots on the underside of its hind wings, found in various species.
butterfly with silver-spotted hind wings

Examples

We spotted a silverspot resting on a wild violet during our hike.
The silverspot is easily recognized by the shimmering dots beneath its hind wings.
Gardeners often enjoy watching silverspots flutter around flowering plants in the summer.

Etymology

From 'silver' + 'spot', describing the characteristic markings on the wings.
