Anagyris

1. NOUN

/ ˌænəˈdʒaɪrɪs / (AmE), / ˌanəˈdʒʌɪrɪs / (BrE)
A small genus of shrubs native to southern Europe, known for their backward-curving seed pods.
genus of southern European shrubs

Examples

The rare Anagyris shrub produces distinctive, curved seed pods in the spring.
Botanists studied the natural habitat of Anagyris in southern Europe.
Gardeners sometimes cultivate Anagyris for its unusual appearance.

Etymology

From Greek anagyris, a type of bean or shrub.
