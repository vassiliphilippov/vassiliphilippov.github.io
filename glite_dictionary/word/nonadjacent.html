nonadjacent

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.əˈdʒeɪ.sənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.əˈdʒeɪ.sənt / (BrE)
not next to or adjoining; not adjacent.
not next to or adjoining

Examples

The two offices are nonadjacent, so you'll need to walk down the hall to get from one to the other.
In this puzzle, connect all the nonadjacent points with straight lines.
The company relocated its departments to nonadjacent floors for privacy.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: adjacent - next to or adjoining something else

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) and 'adjacent' (from Latin 'adjacens', lying near).
