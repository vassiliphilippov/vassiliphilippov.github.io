fifties

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪftiz / (AmE), / ˈfɪftiz / (BrE)
the decade from 1950 to 1959.
the decade 1950–1959

Examples

Many historians highlight the cultural shifts of the fifties as significant in shaping modern society.
Fashion trends from the fifties are making a comeback among young designers.
After the war, the economy boomed throughout the fifties.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: sixties - the decade from 1960 to 1969
  • Degree Lower: forties - the decade from 1940 to 1949

2. NOUN

/ ˈfɪftiz / (AmE), / ˈfɪftiz / (BrE)
the period of a person's life between the ages of 50 and 60
age between 50 and 60

Examples

After turning 50, she embraced her fifties with a new sense of adventure.
Many people experience significant life changes in their fifties, such as retirement planning.
He discovered a passion for painting in his fifties, proving it's never too late to start something new.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forties - the time of life between 40 and 50
  • Degree Higher: sixties - the time of life between 60 and 70
