tinseled

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɪnsəld / (AmE), / ˈtɪnsəld / (BrE)
Covered or decorated with tinsel, giving a glittering appearance, often with gold or silver.
glittering with tinsel

Examples

The Christmas tree stood proudly in the corner, tinseled and sparkling with lights.
She wore a tinseled costume that caught the light with every movement.
The stage was tinseled for the holiday performance, creating a magical atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sparkling - shining or glittering brightly
  • Antonym: plain - without decoration or adornment

Etymology

From 'tinsel', which derives from Old French 'estincele' (spark, sparkle).
