falling

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɑlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːlɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an amount or degree) becoming lower or less; decreasing.
decreasing; becoming less

Examples

The falling price of oil has affected many economies around the world.
We should prepare for falling demand in the winter months.
A falling interest rate can boost the housing market.

Synset Examples

falling temperature

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rising - increasing in amount or degree

Etymology

Present participle of 'fall', from Old English 'feallan'.
