reputable

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛpjətəbəl / (AmE), / ˈrɛpjʊtəbəl / (BrE)
having a good reputation; considered to be honest, reliable, or trustworthy
having a good reputation

Examples

We chose a reputable company to handle our move overseas.
She is known as a reputable expert in her field.
It is important to buy medicines only from reputable pharmacies.

Synset Examples

a reputable business
a reputable scientist
a reputable wine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respectable - regarded by society to be good, proper, or correct
  • Antonym: disreputable - having a bad reputation

Etymology

From Medieval Latin reputabilis, from Latin reputare (to reckon, consider).
