Lovoa

1. NOUN

/ loʊˈvoʊ.ə / (AmE), / ləʊˈvəʊ.ə / (BrE)
A genus of African hardwood trees in the family Meliaceae, valued for their timber.
genus of African timber trees

Examples

The dense wood of Lovoa is highly prized in furniture making.
Several species within the genus Lovoa are native to West and Central Africa.
Foresters are working to ensure the sustainable harvesting of Lovoa trees.
