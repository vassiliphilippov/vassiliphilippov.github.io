sonsie

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɑnzi / (AmE), / ˈsɒnzi / (BrE)
(of a woman) having a plump, attractive, and pleasingly curvaceous figure.
plump and attractive (usually of a woman)

The artist preferred to paint models who were sonsie and full of life.
She wore a dress that flattered her sonsie figure at the party.
Critics admired the sonsie beauty of the actress in the period drama.

Related Terms

Etymology
