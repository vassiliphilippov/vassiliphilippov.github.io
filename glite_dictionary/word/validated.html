validated

1. ADJ

/ ˈvælɪˌdeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈvælɪdeɪtɪd / (BrE)
officially confirmed or made legally valid; having been checked and declared valid or legitimate.
declared officially valid

Examples

The judge reviewed the documents and declared them validated under the new law.
Only validated parking tickets are eligible for the discount.
After his credentials were validated, he was able to access the secure facility.

Synset Examples

a validated claim

Related Terms

  • Antonym: invalidated - declared invalid or not legally binding
  • Synonym: confirmed - established as true, accurate, or valid
  • Synonym: approved - officially agreed or accepted as satisfactory

Etymology

From Latin validatus, past participle of validare (to make strong, confirm), from validus (strong, valid).
