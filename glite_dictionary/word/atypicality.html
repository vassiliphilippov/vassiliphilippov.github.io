atypicality

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪˌtɪpɪˈkæləti / (AmE), / ˌeɪˌtɪpɪˈkælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being atypical; not conforming to the usual type or pattern
the state of being atypical

Examples

The atypicality of the case made it difficult for doctors to diagnose.
Researchers found a high degree of atypicality in the bird's behavior.
Her style's atypicality set her apart from other artists.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: typicality - the quality or state of being typical
  • Synonym: atypical - not typical; not representative of a type, group, or class

Etymology

From 'atypical' (a- meaning 'not' + typical) + -ity (forming nouns of quality or state).
