Prelone

1. NOUN

/ ˈpriːˌloʊn / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌləʊn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for prednisolone, a glucocorticoid drug used to treat inflammation and allergic conditions.
brand name for prednisolone drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Prelone to reduce the patient's inflammation.
If you are allergic to Prelone, inform your healthcare provider before starting treatment.
Children with severe asthma may be treated with Prelone under medical supervision.
