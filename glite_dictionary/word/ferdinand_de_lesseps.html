Ferdinand de Lesseps

1. PROPN

/ fɛr.dɪˌnæn də ləˈsɛps / (AmE), / fɛə.dɪˌnɑ̃ də lɛˈsɛps / (BrE)
(1805–1894) French diplomat and engineer best known for supervising the construction of the Suez Canal.
French engineer who built Suez Canal

Examples

Ferdinand de Lesseps played a pivotal role in connecting the Mediterranean Sea to the Red Sea.
The vision and determination of Ferdinand de Lesseps made the Suez Canal a reality.
Many historians credit Ferdinand de Lesseps with revolutionizing global trade routes.
