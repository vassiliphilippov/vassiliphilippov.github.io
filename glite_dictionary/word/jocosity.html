jocosity

1. NOUN

/ dʒoʊˈkɑːsɪti / (AmE), / dʒɒˈkɒsɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being humorous or playful; joking or jesting behavior
humorous or joking quality

Examples

Her jocosity during the meeting helped everyone relax and enjoy the discussion.
Despite the tense atmosphere, his jocosity managed to bring smiles to people's faces.
The party was filled with laughter and jocosity from start to finish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joviality - cheerfulness and good humor
  • Antonym: seriousness - the quality of being serious

Etymology

From Latin 'iocositās', from 'iocōsus' (full of jokes, humorous).
