masterwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæstərˌwɝt / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstəwɜːt / (BrE)
a perennial herbaceous plant of the genus Astrantia, cultivated for its decorative flowers and used in gardens.
herbaceous plant of genus Astrantia

Examples

Gardeners often plant masterwort for its attractive, long-lasting blooms.
The masterwort thrives best in moist, partially shaded locations.
She pressed a sprig of masterwort between the pages of her gardening journal.

Etymology

From Middle English 'masterwort', combining 'master' and 'wort' (plant, herb).
