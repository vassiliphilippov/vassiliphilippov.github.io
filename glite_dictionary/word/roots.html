roots

1. NOUN

/ ruːts / (AmE), / ruːts / (BrE)
the place, group, or culture that a person comes from, or the feeling of connection to it, especially because of family, social, or ethnic background
ancestral or cultural origin

Examples

He traveled across Europe to discover his roots and learn about his ancestors.
Many people feel a strong connection to their cultural roots even if they live far from their homeland.
Her music celebrates her African roots and blends traditional rhythms with modern sounds.

Synset Examples

his roots in Texas go back a long way
he went back to Sweden to search for his roots
his music has African roots

Related Terms

  • Synonym: origin - the point or place where something begins or is created; the source or cause
  • Synonym: heritage - cultural traditions and background inherited from previous generations
