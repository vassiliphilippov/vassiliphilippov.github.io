bibliomaniacal

1. ADJ

/ ˌbɪbli̩oʊməˈnaɪ.əkəl / (AmE), / ˌbɪbli.əʊməˈnaɪ.əkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by an excessive passion for acquiring or collecting books.
obsessed with collecting books

Examples

Her bibliomaniacal tendencies meant that every room in her house was filled with books.
The bibliomaniacal collector scoured rare bookstores across Europe for new additions to his library.
His bibliomaniacal pursuit led him to spend fortunes on centuries-old manuscripts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bibliomania - an extreme passion for collecting books

Etymology

From 'bibliomania' (extreme passion for collecting books) + '-ical' (forming adjectives).
