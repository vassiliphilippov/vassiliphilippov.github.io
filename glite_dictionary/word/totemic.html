totemic

1. ADJ

/ toʊˈtɛmɪk / (AmE), / təʊˈtɛmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a totem or totemism, especially as an emblem of a clan or group.
relating to totemism or totems

Examples

The tribe regarded the eagle as a totemic symbol representing their heritage.
Many indigenous communities have totemic objects that are central to their spiritual practices.
The museum's collection includes several totemic carvings from Pacific Northwest cultures.

Synset Examples

totemic object

Related Terms

  • Synonym: totem - an object or animal believed to have spiritual significance and adopted as an emblem

Etymology

From totem + -ic, with 'totem' from Ojibwa 'ototeman' (his brother-sister kin).
