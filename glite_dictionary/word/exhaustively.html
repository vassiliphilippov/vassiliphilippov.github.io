exhaustively

1. ADV

/ ɪɡˈzɔstɪvli / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːstɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that is thorough and complete, leaving nothing out.
in a thorough and complete way

Examples

The research team examined the data exhaustively to ensure their findings were accurate.
She exhaustively listed all the possible solutions before making a recommendation.
All the relevant documents were exhaustively reviewed by the committee.

Synset Examples

we searched the files thoroughly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoroughly - in a thorough manner; completely
