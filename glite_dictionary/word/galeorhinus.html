Galeorhinus

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡeɪliəˈraɪnəs / (AmE), / ˌɡeɪliəˈraɪnəs / (BrE)
A genus of houndsharks in the family Triakidae, including the tope shark.
genus of houndsharks

Examples

The genus Galeorhinus includes the well-known tope shark found in temperate waters.
Researchers studied the migration patterns of several Galeorhinus species in the eastern Atlantic.
Members of the genus Galeorhinus are important for understanding shark evolution.

Etymology

From Greek 'galeos' (shark) + 'rhinos' (nose).

2. NOUN

/ ɡeɪliˈoʊrɪnəs / (AmE), / ɡeɪlɪˈɒrɪnəs / (BrE)
A genus of sharks in the family Carcharhinidae, commonly known as the tope sharks or school sharks.
genus of sharks in Carcharhinidae

Examples

The Galeorhinus genus includes the well-known tope shark found in temperate seas.
Researchers observed several juvenile Galeorhinus in the coastal waters during the summer months.
The diet of Galeorhinus species consists mainly of small fish and invertebrates.

Etymology

From Greek 'galeos' (shark) + 'rhinos' (nose), referring to the shape of the snout.
