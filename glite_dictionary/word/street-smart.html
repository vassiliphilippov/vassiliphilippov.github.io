street-smart

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrit ˌsmɑrt / (AmE), / ˈstriːt ˌsmɑːt / (BrE)
Having the practical knowledge and resourcefulness needed to cope with the challenges of urban life.
practically clever for city life

Examples

Growing up in the city made her street-smart and quick to spot danger.
He's not highly educated, but he's extremely street-smart when it comes to business deals.
You need to be street-smart to navigate public transport in such a big city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: savvy - well-informed and perceptive
  • Antonym: naive - lacking experience, wisdom, or judgment
