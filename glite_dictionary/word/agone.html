agone

1. ADJ

/ əˈɡoʊn / (AmE), / əˈɡəʊn / (BrE)
Archaic: gone by; in the past; ago.
gone by; in the past (archaic)
archaic

Examples

It is many years agone since he last visited this village.
Such customs were observed long agone and are now forgotten.
The house stood empty, its former occupants departed ages agone.

Synset Examples

two years ago
‘agone’ is an archaic word for ‘ago’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ago - in the past; before now

Etymology

From Middle English agoon (past participle of ago), from 'a-' (prefix) + 'gone'.
