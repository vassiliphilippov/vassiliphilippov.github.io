uncurving

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkɝːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɜːvɪŋ / (BrE)
not having any bends or curves; straight.
not curved; straight

Examples

The road continued in an uncurving line toward the horizon.
Her handwriting was neat and uncurving, with every letter perfectly upright.
The uncurving blade made it ideal for making precise cuts.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: curved - having a smoothly bending shape or line
  • Synonym: straight - without a bend, angle, or curve
