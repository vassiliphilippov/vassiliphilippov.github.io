haircare

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛrˌkɛr / (AmE), / ˈheəˌkeə / (BrE)
the practice of maintaining and styling the hair, including washing, cutting, and treating it
maintenance and treatment of hair

Examples

She follows a strict haircare routine to keep her hair shiny and healthy.
The salon offers a wide range of haircare products for all hair types.
Proper haircare is important to prevent damage and split ends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairdressing - the profession or activity of styling and cutting hair
