uncategorized

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkætəɡəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkatɪɡəraɪzd / (BrE)
not assigned to any category or group; not sorted into categories.
not sorted into categories

Examples

The documents remained uncategorized after the system update.
Several photos in the library are still uncategorized and need to be sorted.
She found an uncategorized box in the attic filled with old letters.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: categorized - placed in a particular category

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'categorized'.
