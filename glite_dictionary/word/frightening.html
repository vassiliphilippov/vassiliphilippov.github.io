frightening

1. ADJ

/ ˈfraɪtənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfraɪtənɪŋ / (BrE)
Making someone feel afraid or anxious; causing fear.
causing fear or anxiety

Examples

The movie was so frightening that I couldn't sleep afterward.
She heard a frightening noise outside her window late at night.
It was a frightening experience to be lost in the forest.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scary - causing fear or alarm
  • Degree Higher: terrifying - extremely frightening
  • Synonym: alarming - causing worry or anxiety

Etymology

From 'frighten' + '-ing', where 'frighten' derives from Old English 'fyrhtan' (to terrify).
