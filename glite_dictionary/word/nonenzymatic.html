nonenzymatic

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˌɛnˌzaɪˈmætɪk / (AmE), / nɒnˌɛnˌzaɪˈmætɪk / (BrE)
not involving or produced by enzymes; not relating to enzymes or enzymatic activity
not involving enzymes
medical

Examples

The reaction can occur through a nonenzymatic pathway under certain conditions.
Researchers studied the nonenzymatic breakdown of glucose in the solution.
The process differs significantly between enzymatic and nonenzymatic systems.

Synset Examples

nonenzymatic systems

Related Terms

  • Antonym: enzymatic - involving or produced by enzymes

Etymology

From prefix 'non-' (not) + 'enzymatic' (relating to enzymes).
