coppery

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑːpəri / (AmE), / ˈkɒpəri / (BrE)
Having the reddish-brown color of copper.
resembling the color of copper

Examples

The sunset cast a coppery glow over the river.
She admired the coppery highlights in his hair.
The autumn leaves turned a deep coppery red as the weather cooled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bronze - a yellowish-brown color resembling bronze metal

Etymology

From copper + -y, meaning 'of or pertaining to copper'.
