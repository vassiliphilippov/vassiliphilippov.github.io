11 November

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛvənθ ˈnəʊvɛmbɚ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛvənθ nəʊˈvɛmbə / (BrE)
The date marking Saint Martin's Day; regarded as a traditional quarter day in Scotland.
date of Saint Martin's Day; Scottish quarter day

Examples

In Scotland, rents were often due on 11 November, known as a quarter day.
Many communities celebrate 11 November as the feast of Saint Martin.
Old records show significant fairs were held on 11 November each year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Martinmas - the feast of Saint Martin, observed on 11 November
