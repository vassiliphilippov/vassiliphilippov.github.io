Sao Paulo

1. PROPN

/ saʊ ˈpaʊloʊ / (AmE), / saʊ ˈpaʊləʊ / (BrE)
A large, ultramodern city in southeastern Brazil; the largest city in South America.
largest city in Brazil and South America

Examples

Sao Paulo is known for its vibrant nightlife, diverse cuisine, and towering skyscrapers.
The international art fair in Sao Paulo attracts visitors from all over the world each year.
After moving to Sao Paulo, she found endless opportunities in its fast-paced business environment.

Etymology

From Portuguese São Paulo, meaning 'Saint Paul'.
