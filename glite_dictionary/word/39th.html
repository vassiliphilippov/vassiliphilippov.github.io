39th

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɝːti ˈnaɪnθ / (AmE), / ˈθɜːti ˈnaɪnθ / (BrE)
constituting number thirty-nine in a sequence; coming after the thirty-eighth and before the fortieth
ordinal number, thirty-nine

Examples

She finished in 39th place at the marathon, just missing out on a medal.
This is the 39th edition of the book, updated with new content.
Their annual meeting will be held for the 39th time this April.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thirty-ninth - the ordinal number for thirty-nine
  • Degree Lower: 38th - ordinal number before thirty-ninth
  • Degree Higher: 40th - ordinal number after thirty-ninth
