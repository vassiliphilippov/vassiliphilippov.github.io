cacodemon

1. NOUN

/ ˌkækəˈdiːmən / (AmE), / ˌkækəˈdiːmən / (BrE)
An evil spirit or demon, especially in mythology or literature.
evil spirit or demon
archaic

Examples

Some ancient texts describe a cacodemon that torments the troubled mind.
The old legend warned of a cacodemon haunting the abandoned castle.
In her nightmare, a shadowy cacodemon whispered unsettling secrets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demon - an evil spirit or supernatural being

Etymology

From Greek kakodaimōn (evil spirit), from kakos (bad) + daimōn (spirit).
