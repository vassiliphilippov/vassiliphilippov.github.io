Chernobyl

1. PROPN

/ tʃɚˈnoʊbəl / (AmE), / tʃəˈnəʊbəl / (BrE)
a city in north central Ukraine, known as the site of the 1986 nuclear power plant disaster
city in Ukraine, site of 1986 nuclear disaster

Examples

The Chernobyl disaster changed global attitudes toward nuclear power.
Few people live in Chernobyl today due to radioactive contamination.
A television series based on the events at Chernobyl received critical acclaim.
