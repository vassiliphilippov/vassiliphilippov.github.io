dobbin

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑbɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɒbɪn / (BrE)
A quiet, hardworking horse, especially one used for steady labor rather than racing or show.
plodding workhorse

Examples

The old dobbin trudged patiently along the country lane, pulling the heavy cart behind him.
Years of service had left the faithful dobbin with a grey-speckled mane and a gentle nature.
On the farm, every child learned to ride dobbin before graduating to the faster horses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workhorse - a horse used for hard, steady work

Etymology

Probably from the pet form of the name Dob, a diminutive of Robert.
