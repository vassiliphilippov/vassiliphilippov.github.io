Tangiers

1. PROPN

/ tænˈdʒɪrz / (AmE), / tænˈdʒɪəz / (BrE)
a city in northern Morocco, located at the western entrance to the Strait of Gibraltar
city in northern Morocco

Examples

He visited Tangiers during his travels across North Africa.
Tangiers has long been a meeting point for different cultures and civilizations.
The port of Tangiers is one of the busiest in Africa.

Synset Examples

the first tangerines were shipped from Tangier to Europe in 1841

Etymology

From Arabic Ṭanjah, from Latin Tingis, the ancient name of the city.
