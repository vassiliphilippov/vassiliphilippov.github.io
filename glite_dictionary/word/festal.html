festal

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɛstl̩ / (AmE), / ˈfɛstl̩ / (BrE)
relating to a feast or celebration; offering fun and gaiety
relating to a feast or celebration
archaic

Examples

The ballroom was decorated in a festal manner for the grand celebration.
Her mood was brightened by the festal atmosphere of the holiday gathering.
Traditional music filled the hall, creating a truly festal spirit among the guests.

Synset Examples

a festive (or festal) occasion
gay and exciting night life
a merry evening

Related Terms

  • Synonym: festive - relating to a festival or celebration
  • Antonym: solemn - formal and dignified; not cheerful or festive

Etymology

From Latin festalis (of a feast), from festum (feast).
