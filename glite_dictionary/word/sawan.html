Sawan

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑː.wən / (AmE), / ˈsɑː.wən / (BrE)
the fifth month of the Hindu calendar, typically corresponding to July-August in the Gregorian calendar
fifth month of the Hindu calendar

Examples

The festival falls during Sawan each year, attracting thousands of devotees.
Many Hindu families observe fasts on Mondays in Sawan.
Heavy rains are a common feature of Sawan in northern India.

Etymology

From Hindi सावन (Sāvan), from Sanskrit श्रावण (Śrāvaṇa), name of a lunar month.
