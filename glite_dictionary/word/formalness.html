formalness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrməlnəs / (AmE), / ˈfɔːm(ə)lnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of observing all forms, conventions, or ceremonies; formality
quality of being formal or ceremonious

Examples

The formalness of the event made everyone dress in their finest attire.
His formalness during the introduction put the audience at ease.
You could sense her formalness in the way she addressed the committee.

Synset Examples

the formality of his voice made the others pay him close attention

Related Terms

  • Synonym: formality - the rigid observance of rules or conventions
