paranasal

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɛrəˈneɪzəl / (AmE), / ˌparəˈneɪzəl / (BrE)
Situated or occurring next to the nasal cavities.
adjacent to the nasal cavities
medical

Examples

The doctor examined the paranasal sinuses for signs of infection.
Inflammation in the paranasal areas can cause facial pain and headaches.
CT scans often reveal abnormalities in the paranasal regions during sinusitis diagnosis.

Synset Examples

paranasal sinus

Etymology

From Greek 'para-' (beside) + 'nasal' (nose-related).
