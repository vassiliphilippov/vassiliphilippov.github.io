lesbian

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛz.bi.ən / (AmE), / ˈlɛz.bi.ən / (BrE)
relating to, involving, or characterized by romantic or sexual attraction between women.
relating to female homosexuality

Examples

She has been in a lesbian relationship for several years.
The festival celebrates lesbian culture and history.
They held a panel on lesbian representation in the media.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gay - relating to homosexual attraction (especially between men)
  • Antonym: heterosexual - relating to sexual attraction to the opposite sex
  • Degree Lower: bisexual - attracted to both sexes

Etymology

From Greek 'Lesbia', referring to the island of Lesbos, home of the poet Sappho, who wrote about love between women.

2. NOUN

/ ˈlɛz.bi.ən / (AmE), / ˈlɛz.bi.ən / (BrE)
A woman who is sexually or romantically attracted to other women.
homosexual woman

Examples

She came out as a lesbian in college and found support among her friends.
Several lesbians spoke at the event about their experiences.
The film features a lesbian couple as the main characters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gay - a person who is sexually attracted to people of the same sex; often refers to men
  • Antonym: heterosexual - a person sexually attracted to people of the opposite sex

Etymology

From the Greek island of Lesbos, home of the poet Sappho, who wrote poems expressing affection for women.

Synset POS: n
