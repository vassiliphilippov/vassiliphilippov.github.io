Minnesotan

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪnəˈsoʊtən / (AmE), / ˌmɪnɪˈsəʊtən / (BrE)
a native or resident of Minnesota, a state in the northern United States
person from Minnesota

Examples

My grandfather is a proud Minnesotan who spent his whole life in Minneapolis.
As a Minnesotan, she enjoys the state's many lakes and outdoor activities.
The Minnesotan accent is noticeable to people from other parts of the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Minnesota - a state in the northern United States
