Democratic Republic of São Tomé and Príncipe

1. PROPN

/ ˌsoʊ təˈmeɪ ən ˈprɪnsɪpeɪ / (AmE), / ˌsaʊ təˈmeɪ ən ˈprɪnsɪpeɪ / (BrE)
An island country in the South Atlantic Ocean off the west coast of Central Africa, consisting of the islands of São Tomé and Príncipe; became independent from Portugal in 1975.
island country in the South Atlantic, Africa

Examples

The Democratic Republic of São Tomé and Príncipe is known for its rich biodiversity and pristine beaches.
In 1975, Democratic Republic of São Tomé and Príncipe gained independence from Portuguese colonial rule.
Oil exploration has increased foreign interest in Democratic Republic of São Tomé and Príncipe in recent years.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'São Tomé' (Saint Thomas) and 'Príncipe' (Prince), names given by Portuguese explorers.
