balloonist

1. NOUN

/ bəˈluːnɪst / (AmE), / bəˈluːnɪst / (BrE)
a person who flies or operates a hot air balloon or gas balloon, especially as a pilot or aeronaut
person who operates a balloon

Examples

The experienced balloonist skillfully guided the balloon over the countryside.
As a young man, he dreamed of becoming a daring balloonist and exploring the skies.
During the festival, dozens of balloonists launched at sunrise, filling the sky with color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aeronaut - a person who travels in a hot air balloon or airship

Etymology

From 'balloon' + '-ist' (person engaged in or concerned with something).
