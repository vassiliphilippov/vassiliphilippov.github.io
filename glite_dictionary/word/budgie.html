budgie

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌdʒi / (AmE), / ˈbʌdʒi / (BrE)
a small Australian parakeet, often green with black and yellow markings in the wild, bred in many colors as a popular cage bird
small Australian parakeet kept as a pet

Examples

Sophie keeps a budgie in a spacious cage near the window.
The budgie chirped happily when its owner walked into the room.
Many children learn to care for pets by looking after a budgie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parakeet - a small to medium-sized long-tailed parrot

Etymology

Shortening of 'budgerigar', an Australian Aboriginal word.
