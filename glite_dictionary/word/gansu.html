Gansu

1. PROPN

/ ɡænˈsuː / (AmE), / ɡænˈsuː / (BrE)
a province in north-central China, known for its historical significance as part of the Silk Road.
province in north-central China

Examples

The famous Mogao Caves are located in Gansu province.
Many Silk Road travelers passed through Gansu on their way to the West.
Modern Gansu is known for its dramatic desert landscapes and Buddhist heritage sites.
