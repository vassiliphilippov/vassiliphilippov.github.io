moblike

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɑb.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɒb.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a mob, especially in being disorderly or lawless
resembling a mob; disorderly

Examples

His moblike outburst intimidated the people around him.
During the meeting, the atmosphere turned moblike as people began shouting and pushing.
