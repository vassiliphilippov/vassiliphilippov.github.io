unkindness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈkaɪndnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈkaɪndnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or act of being unkind; lack of sympathy or consideration for others
lack of kindness or sympathy

Examples

Her unkindness during my difficult time hurt me deeply.
If you respond to unkindness with patience, you show true strength.
He apologized for his unkindness to his sister.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly and considerate

Etymology

From un- (not) + kindness.
