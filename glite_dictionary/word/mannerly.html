mannerly

1. ADJ

/ ˈmænərli / (AmE), / ˈmænəli / (BrE)
Behaving in a polite and socially correct way.
polite and socially correct

Examples

The children were very mannerly at the dinner table, saying "please" and "thank you".
Her mannerly behavior impressed everyone she met at the reception.
He was raised to be mannerly, always holding the door open for others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polite - having or showing good manners; respectful
  • Antonym: impolite - not having or showing good manners; rude

Etymology

From Middle English manerly, from maner (manner) + -ly.
