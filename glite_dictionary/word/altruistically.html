altruistically

1. ADV

/ ˌæltruˈɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌæltruˈɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a selfless concern for the well-being of others; unselfishly.
in a selfless or unselfish manner

Examples

She donated the entire prize money altruistically to the orphanage.
The volunteers worked altruistically, expecting nothing in return for their efforts.
He altruistically offered his time to help those affected by the disaster.

Synset Examples

he acted selflessly when he helped the old lady in distress

Related Terms

  • Synonym: selflessly - in a selfless manner
  • Antonym: selfishly - in a selfish manner

Etymology

From 'altruistic' + '-ally', from French 'altruiste', from Italian 'altrui' (of others).
