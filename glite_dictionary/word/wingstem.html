wingstem

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪŋˌstɛm / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋstɛm / (BrE)
a tall perennial herb of the eastern United States with showy yellow flowers, belonging to the genus Verbesina.
perennial herb with yellow flowers (US)

Examples

In late summer, the wingstem brightens the forest edges with its vibrant yellow blossoms.
Gardeners often plant wingstem to attract bees and butterflies to their yards.
The botanist identified the tall plant as a wingstem based on its distinctive angular stem.
