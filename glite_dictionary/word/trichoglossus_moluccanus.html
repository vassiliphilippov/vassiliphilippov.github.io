Trichoglossus moluccanus

1. NOUN

/ ˌtrɪkoʊˈɡlɒsəs ˌmɑːləˈkeɪnəs / (AmE), / ˌtrɪkəˈɡlɒsəs ˌmɒlʊˈkɑːnəs / (BrE)
A species of colorful parrot native to Australia, commonly known as the rainbow lorikeet.
species of colorful Australian parrot

Examples

The vivid feathers of the Trichoglossus moluccanus make it a favorite among birdwatchers in Australia.
Zookeepers often feed the Trichoglossus moluccanus a special nectar to mimic its natural diet.
A flock of Trichoglossus moluccanus can be very noisy as they gather in city parks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rainbow lorikeet - the common name for Trichoglossus moluccanus

Etymology

From Greek 'tricho-' (hair) + 'glossa' (tongue), referring to its brush-tipped tongue, and 'moluccanus' from the Moluccas, an island group in Indonesia.
