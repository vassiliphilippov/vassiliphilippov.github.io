clothesbrush

1. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊðzˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈkləʊðzˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)
a brush designed for cleaning clothing by removing lint, dust, or dirt
a brush for cleaning clothes

Examples

He used a clothesbrush to remove dust from his jacket before the meeting.
A clothesbrush is an essential tool for keeping suits looking neat and professional.
She bought a new clothesbrush to help keep her winter coat clean and presentable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lint brush - a brush designed to remove lint from clothing

Etymology

From 'clothes' + 'brush', indicating its use for brushing clothes.
