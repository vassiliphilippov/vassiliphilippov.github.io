Santa Barbara

1. PROPN

/ ˌsæntə ˈbɑrbərə / (AmE), / ˌsæntə ˈbɑːbərə / (BrE)
A city in southwestern California, located on the Pacific coast, known for its Mediterranean climate and scenic beauty.
city in California, USA

Examples

We spent the weekend exploring the beaches and restaurants of Santa Barbara.
Santa Barbara is famous for its Spanish-style architecture and beautiful coastline.
The drive from Los Angeles to Santa Barbara offers stunning ocean views.

Etymology

From Spanish, meaning 'Saint Barbara,' named after the Christian saint.
