skateboarding

1. NOUN

/ ˈskeɪtˌbɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskeɪtˌbɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport or activity of riding and performing tricks on a skateboard.
riding and performing tricks on a skateboard

Examples

He has been skateboarding since he was seven years old.
The city is building a park dedicated to skateboarding and BMX biking.
Many teenagers enjoy skateboarding after school with their friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skateboard - a short board mounted on small wheels, ridden as a sport
