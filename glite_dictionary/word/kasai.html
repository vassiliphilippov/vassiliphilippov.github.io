Kasai

1. PROPN

/ kɑːˈsaɪ / (AmE), / kəˈsaɪ / (BrE)
a river in southwestern Africa that rises in central Angola, forms part of the Angola–Congo border, and flows northwest through the Democratic Republic of the Congo to join the Congo River.
river in southwestern Africa

Examples

The Kasai River is a vital waterway for transportation in central Africa.
Several settlements along the Kasai rely on fishing for their livelihoods.
The Kasai forms part of the boundary between Angola and the Democratic Republic of the Congo.
