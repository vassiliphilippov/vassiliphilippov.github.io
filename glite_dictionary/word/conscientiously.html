conscientiously

1. ADV

/ ˌkɑːn.ʃiˈen.ʃəs.li / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.ʃiˈen.ʃəs.li / (BrE)
In a thorough and careful way, showing great attention to duty or responsibility.
in a careful and responsible manner

Examples

She conscientiously reviewed every detail of the report before submitting it.
The nurse conscientiously attended to her patients throughout her shift.
He always completes his assignments conscientiously, never missing a step.

Synset Examples

he came religiously every morning at 8 o'clock

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diligently - in a way that shows careful and persistent effort
  • Antonym: carelessly - in a way that shows a lack of care or attention

Etymology

From 'conscientious', from Latin 'conscientia' (knowledge within oneself).
