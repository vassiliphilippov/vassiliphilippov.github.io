Sardegna

1. PROPN

/ sarˈdeɲɲa / (AmE), / sarˈdeɲɲa / (BrE)
The Italian name for Sardinia, an island in the Mediterranean Sea to the west of mainland Italy.
Italian name for Sardinia, Mediterranean island

2. PROPN

/ sarˈdeɲɲa / (AmE), / sarˈdɛɲɲa / (BrE)
The Italian name for Sardinia, a region and island of Italy; formerly the kingdom of Sardinia, which played a key role in the unification of Italy in the 19th century.
Italian name for Sardinia, Italian region and island

Examples

Many visitors to Italy choose to explore Sardegna for its beautiful beaches and unique culture.
The wines of Sardegna are gaining international recognition for their distinctive flavors.
In the 19th century, the kingdom of Sardegna played a vital role in the unification of Italy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sardinia - Large Mediterranean island; Italian region known in English as Sardinia

Etymology

Italian, from Latin 'Sardinia'.
