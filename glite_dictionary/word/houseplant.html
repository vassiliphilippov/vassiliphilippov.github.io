houseplant

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
A plant grown indoors for decorative purposes.
indoor decorative plant

Examples

She waters her houseplant every week to keep it healthy.
A fern is a popular choice for a houseplant in many homes.
After moving apartments, he bought a new houseplant to brighten up his living room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indoor plant - a plant grown or suitable for growing indoors

Etymology

Compound of 'house' and 'plant'.
