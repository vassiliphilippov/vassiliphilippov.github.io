neurogliacyte

1. NOUN

/ ˌnʊroʊˈɡlaɪ.əˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˌnjʊə.rəʊˈɡlɪ.ə.saɪt / (BrE)
a cell forming part of the neuroglia, which supports and protects neurons in the nervous system
cell of the neuroglia in the nervous system
medical

Examples

The neurogliacyte plays a crucial role in maintaining the health of neurons in the brain.
Researchers are studying how neurogliacytes interact with nerve cells during injury recovery.
Damage to neurogliacyte populations can have significant effects on nervous system function.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glial cell - a cell in the nervous system that supports neurons
  • Antonym: neuron - nerve cell that transmits electrical impulses

Etymology

From Greek 'neuron' (nerve) + 'glia' (glue) + 'cyte' (cell).
