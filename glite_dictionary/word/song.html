Song

1. PROPN

/ sɑŋ / (AmE), / sɒŋ / (BrE)
the imperial Chinese dynasty that ruled from 960 to 1279, renowned for its achievements in art, literature, and philosophy
Chinese dynasty (960–1279) known for cultural achievements

Examples

Scholars consider the Song period a golden age for Chinese painting and ceramics.
During the Song dynasty, the civil service examination became a main route to government.
Many important inventions, such as movable type printing, emerged in the Song era.

Etymology

From Mandarin 宋 (Sòng), the name of the dynasty.
