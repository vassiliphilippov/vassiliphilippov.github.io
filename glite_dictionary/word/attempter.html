attempter

1. NOUN

/ əˈtɛmptər / (AmE), / əˈtɛmptə / (BrE)
a person who tries to do something, especially something difficult or forbidden
person who tries or attempts something

Examples

The first attempter struggled, but later groups found success.
Police apprehended the attempter before he could break into the building.
In psychological studies, the term 'suicide attempter' refers to a person who has tried to end their life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attempt - to try to do something, especially something difficult
  • Antonym: achiever - someone who successfully brings something to completion

Etymology

Formed from 'attempt' + '-er', denoting the agent of the action.
