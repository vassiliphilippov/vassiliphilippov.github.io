Bikini

1. PROPN

/ bɪˈkini / (AmE), / bɪˈkiːni / (BrE)
an atoll in the Marshall Islands, formerly used by the United States as a site for testing nuclear weapons
atoll in the Marshall Islands; nuclear test site

Examples

The United States conducted several nuclear tests at Bikini Atoll after World War II.
The people of Bikini were relocated before the tests began in the 1940s.
Environmental scientists continue to monitor radiation levels on Bikini.
