cuddlesome

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌd.əl.səm / (AmE), / ˈkʌd.əl.səm / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) inviting cuddling or hugging; pleasant to hold or embrace.
inviting to be cuddled or hugged

Examples

The puppy was so cuddlesome that everyone wanted to hold him.
She wrapped her cuddlesome blanket around herself on the cold night.
There’s nothing more cuddlesome than a newborn baby.

Synset Examples

a cuddlesome baby
a cuddly teddybear

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cuddly - pleasant to cuddle; endearing and huggable

Etymology

Formed from 'cuddle' + '-some', a suffix meaning 'tending to' or 'likely to'.
