Rio de Janeiro

1. PROPN

/ ˌrioʊ də ʒəˈnɛroʊ / (AmE), / ˌriːəʊ də ʒəˈneərəʊ / (BrE)
a large city and port in southeastern Brazil, formerly the country's capital, known for its beaches, carnival, and tourist attractions
a major city in Brazil, former capital

Examples

The vibrant city of Rio de Janeiro hosts one of the largest carnivals in the world every year.
Sugarloaf Mountain offers breathtaking views over Rio de Janeiro and its iconic beaches.
Many tourists travel to Rio de Janeiro to experience its lively atmosphere and beautiful scenery.

Etymology

Portuguese for 'River of January', named by early Portuguese explorers who arrived in January 1502.
