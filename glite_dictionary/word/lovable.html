lovable

1. ADJ

/ ˈlʌvəbəl / (AmE), / ˈlʌvəb(ə)l / (BrE)
Inspiring or worthy of love or affection.
inspiring love or affection

Examples

The puppy was so lovable that everyone wanted to take him home.
Even though he was a little clumsy, his lovable nature won everyone over.
She has a lovable sense of humor that makes her fun to be around.

Synset Examples

a mischievous but lovable child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endearing - inspiring affection or warm sympathy
  • Synonym: adorable - very attractive; delightful; inspiring great affection
  • Antonym: detestable - deserving intense dislike

Etymology

From love + -able, meaning 'capable of being loved'.
