metalhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛtəlˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəlˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person who is a dedicated fan of heavy metal music, often associated with the subculture surrounding it
fan of heavy metal music

Examples

As a lifelong metalhead, she has been to dozens of concerts around the world.
The festival attracts metalheads from all over Europe every summer.
He became a metalhead during his teenage years after discovering classic metal bands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headbanger - a fan of heavy metal music, especially one who engages in headbanging

Etymology

From 'metal' (referring to 'heavy metal' music) + 'head' (slang for enthusiast or fan).
