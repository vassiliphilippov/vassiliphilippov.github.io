Wynnea americana

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪniə əˌmɛrɪˈkænə / (AmE), / ˈwɪniə əˌmɛrɪˈkɑːnə / (BrE)
A species of fungus characterized by several elongated, ear-shaped apothecia with a dark brown, warty outer surface and a smooth, pinkish-orange fertile surface.
a species of ear-shaped fungus

Examples

The striking appearance of Wynnea americana makes it a favorite subject among mushroom photographers.
During our forest hike, we spotted a patch of Wynnea americana under the decaying logs.
Mycology students studied the unique structure of Wynnea americana in the lab.

Etymology

Genus name 'Wynnea', species epithet 'americana' refers to its occurrence in America.
