to air-freight

1. VERB

/ ˈɛrˌfreɪt / (AmE), / ˈeəˌfreɪt / (BrE)
to transport cargo by aircraft, typically for delivery or shipment purposes.
transport cargo by aircraft

Examples

The company decided to air-freight the medical supplies to ensure their quick arrival.
Due to the urgent demand, we air-freighted the machinery parts to the client in Singapore.
Some flowers are air-freighted daily to maintain their freshness in foreign markets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ship - to transport goods, especially by sea or air
  • Synonym: to deliver - to bring goods to a person or place

Etymology

Formed from 'air' and 'freight', indicating shipping goods by air.
