bistro

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːstroʊ / (AmE), / ˈbiːstrəʊ / (BrE)
A small, informal restaurant, typically one that serves wine and simple meals.
small informal restaurant

Examples

We decided to have dinner at a cozy bistro near the river.
The new French bistro in town has quickly become a local favorite.
At the bistro, she ordered a glass of red wine with her meal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: café - a small restaurant serving light meals and drinks

Etymology

From French 'bistro', meaning a small restaurant or tavern.
