clubfooted

1. ADJ

/ ˌklʌbˈfʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˌklʌbˈfʊtɪd / (BrE)
Having a foot that is twisted or deformed, typically so that the sole cannot be placed flat on the ground.
having a twisted or deformed foot
medical

Examples

The clubfooted child walked with a noticeable limp but remained cheerful.
Doctors made great progress correcting the clubfooted puppy’s condition.
He wore special shoes designed especially for clubfooted patients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clubfoot - a congenital deformity in which the foot is twisted out of shape or position

Etymology

From 'clubfoot', referring to the deformity, plus the adjectival suffix '-ed'.
