Acalypha

1. NOUN

/ əˈkælɪfə / (AmE), / əˈkælɪfə / (BrE)
a genus of herbs and shrubs in the family Euphorbiaceae, found in warm and tropical regions, some species cultivated as ornamentals
plant genus in the Euphorbiaceae family

Examples

The gardener planted a row of Acalypha along the fence for its attractive foliage.
Some species of Acalypha are popular as houseplants due to their colorful leaves.
Researchers are studying Acalypha for its potential medicinal uses.

Etymology

From Greek 'akáluphē' (nettle), the ancient name for nettles.

Examples
