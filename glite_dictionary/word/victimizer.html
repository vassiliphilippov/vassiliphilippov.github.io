victimizer

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪktɪˌmaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈvɪktɪˌmaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who harms, exploits, or makes a victim of another
person who makes others victims

Examples

The court found the victimizer guilty of multiple counts of fraud.
In the documentary, the victimizer tries to justify his actions without remorse.
She refused to let the victimizer control her story any longer.

Synset Examples

I thought we were partners, not victim and victimizer

Related Terms

  • Antonym: victim - a person harmed or made to suffer by someone or something

Etymology

From 'victimize' + '-er', meaning one who victimizes.
