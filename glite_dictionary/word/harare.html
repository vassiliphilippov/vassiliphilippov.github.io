Harare

1. PROPN

/ həˈrɑreɪ / (AmE), / həˈrɑːreɪ / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Zimbabwe, located in the northeastern part of the country
capital city of Zimbabwe

Examples

The conference was held in Harare and attracted delegates from across Africa.
After moving to Harare, she quickly adapted to the vibrant city life.
Many important government buildings are located in the center of Harare.

Etymology

Named Harare in 1982 after a Shona chief, Neharawa; formerly called Salisbury.
