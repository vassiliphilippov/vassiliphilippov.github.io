Sula

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːlə / (AmE), / ˈsuːlə / (BrE)
A genus of large tropical seabirds in the family Sulidae, known as boobies.
genus of tropical seabirds (boobies)

Examples

The genus Sula includes several species of boobies found on tropical islands.
Researchers observed a flock of Sula nesting along the rocky cliffs.
The distinctive blue feet of some Sula species attract the attention of wildlife photographers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: booby - a large tropical seabird of the genus Sula

Etymology

From Latin *Sula*, from Old Norse *súla* (gannet).
