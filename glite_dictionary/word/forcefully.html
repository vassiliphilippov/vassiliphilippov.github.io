forcefully

1. ADV

/ ˈfɔrs.fə.li / (AmE), / ˈfɔːs.fə.li / (BrE)
In a way that is full of force, strength, or energy; with strong effect or intensity.
with great strength or intensity

Examples

The manager forcefully argued his point during the meeting.
She closed the door forcefully, startling everyone in the room.
The wind blew forcefully against the windows all night.

Synset Examples

we are seeing this film too late to feel its original impact forcefully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firmly - in a strong or secure manner
  • Antonym: gently - in a soft or gentle manner, not forcefully
