eater

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːtər / (AmE), / ˈiːtə / (BrE)
a person or animal that consumes food for nourishment
person or animal that eats

Examples

He is a picky eater and only likes a few kinds of vegetables.
As a vegetarian eater, she avoids all meat dishes.
The lion is a carnivorous eater at the top of the food chain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diner - a person eating a meal, especially in a restaurant
  • Antonym: fast - to abstain from all or some kinds of food or drink

Etymology

From Middle English eten, from Old English etan (to eat) plus -er (agent noun suffix).
