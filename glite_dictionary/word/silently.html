silently

1. ADV

/ ˈsaɪləntli / (AmE), / ˈsaɪləntli / (BrE)
in a way that involves no speaking or sound; without making noise or speaking
without making sound or speaking

Examples

She entered the room silently, careful not to wake anyone.
He silently handed her the letter, avoiding eye contact.
They all listened silently as the judge announced the verdict.

Synset Examples

he sat mutely next to her

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quietly - in a quiet manner; making little or no noise

Etymology

From the adjective 'silent' + adverbial suffix '-ly.'
