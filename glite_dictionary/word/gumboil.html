gumboil

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌmˌbɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡʌm.bɔɪl / (BrE)
a painful swelling or abscess on the gums, typically caused by infection
abscess on the gums

Examples

The dentist diagnosed the painful lump as a gumboil needing immediate treatment.
She visited the clinic because her gumboil was swollen and sore.
If left untreated, a gumboil can cause significant discomfort and further oral problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abscess - a swollen area within body tissue, containing pus

Etymology

From 'gum' (the fleshy tissue in the mouth) + 'boil' (a swelling or abscess filled with pus).
