showing

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
an exhibition or display presented to the public
exhibition or public display

Examples

The new art showing at the gallery attracted large crowds.
Tickets for tonight's showing of the documentary sold out quickly.
The science museum's latest showing features interactive exhibits for children.

Synset Examples

the museum had many exhibits of oriental art

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhibition - a public display of works of art or items of interest
  • Synonym: display - the act of showing something to the public

2. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
an occasion when a film, play, or television programme is presented or screened to an audience
presentation of a film or play

Examples

The 7 p.m. showing of the film was completely sold out.
We missed the afternoon showing, so we had to wait until the evening.
There will be a special showing of the documentary for students on Friday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: screening - a showing of a movie or other presentation for an audience
