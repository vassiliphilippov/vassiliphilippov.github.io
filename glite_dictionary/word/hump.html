/ hʌmp / (AmE), / hʌmp / (BrE)

Examples

He humped his shoulders against the cold as he walked down the street.
She humped over her desk, concentrating on the paperwork.

2. NOUN

/ hʌmp / (AmE), / hʌmp / (BrE)
a rounded, raised, or protruding part on the surface of something, especially the back of an animal or a physical feature that bulges out from its surroundings.
a raised, protruding part or bulge

Examples

The camel's hump helps it store fat for long journeys in the desert.
A small hump formed on the road after years of heavy traffic.
The old dog had developed a noticeable hump on its back.
