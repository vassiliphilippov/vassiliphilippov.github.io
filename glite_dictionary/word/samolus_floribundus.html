Samolus floribundus

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæmələs flɔːrɪˈbʌndəs / (AmE), / ˈsæmələs flɒrɪˈbʌndəs / (BrE)
A species of flowering plant known as the American water pimpernel, found in wet habitats of North and South America.
species of American water pimpernel (plant)

Examples

The marshes were dotted with clusters of Samolus floribundus in full bloom.
Botanists recorded the presence of Samolus floribundus along the riverbanks after the rainy season.
Gardeners sometimes cultivate Samolus floribundus in water gardens for its delicate white flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: American water pimpernel - common name for Samolus floribundus

Etymology

From the genus name Samolus (of unknown ancient origin) and Latin floribundus (flowering profusely).
