Monterrey

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɒn.təˈreɪ / (AmE), / ˌmɒn.təˈreɪ / (BrE)
an industrial city in northeastern Mexico, known for its economic and cultural significance
industrial city in northeastern Mexico

Examples

Many international companies have operations in Monterrey due to its strong industrial base.
She plans to visit Monterrey for a business conference next month.
Monterrey is known for its modern skyline and vibrant cultural scene.

Etymology

From Spanish Monterrey, named after the Count of Monterrey.
