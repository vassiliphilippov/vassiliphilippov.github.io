unascertainable

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.əˌsɜr.tənˈeɪ.bəl / (AmE), / ʌn.əˌsɜː.tənˈeɪ.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to discover or determine with certainty
impossible to determine or discover

Examples

The cause of the error was unascertainable despite thorough investigation.
At this stage, the full extent of his injuries remains unascertainable.
The document's origin is unascertainable, leaving experts to speculate about its history.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ascertainable - that can be found out or determined

Etymology

From un- (not) + ascertain + -able.
