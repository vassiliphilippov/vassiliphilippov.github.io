Platystemon

1. NOUN

/ plæˈtɪstəˌmɒn / (AmE), / plæˈtɪstɪˌmɒn / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the poppy family, with a single species, Platystemon californicus, commonly known as creamcups.
genus of flowering plant, creamcups

Examples

The fields were dotted with the delicate blooms of Platystemon after the spring rains.
Researchers studied the unique pollination mechanisms of Platystemon in California's grasslands.
Gardeners appreciate Platystemon for its bright yellow-and-white flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creamcups - Common name for Platystemon californicus, the only species in this genus

Etymology

From Greek 'platys' (broad) + 'stemon' (stamen), referring to the broad stamens of the flower.
