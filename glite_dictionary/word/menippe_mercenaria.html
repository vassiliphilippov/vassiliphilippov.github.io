Menippe mercenaria

1. NOUN

/ məˈnɪpi mərˌsɛnˈɛəriə / (AmE), / mɛˈnɪpiː mɜːˌsɛnˈɛːrɪə / (BrE)
a large edible crab found along the southern coast of the United States, especially in Florida; also called the stone crab
large edible crab of southern US, esp. Florida

Examples

Commercial fishers in Florida harvest the claws of the Menippe mercenaria for their sweet, tender meat.
The Menippe mercenaria is prized by chefs for its delicate flavor and firm texture.
Researchers are studying the population dynamics of the Menippe mercenaria to ensure sustainable harvesting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stone crab - common name for Menippe mercenaria

Etymology

From New Latin, combining the genus name Menippe and the specific epithet mercenaria.
