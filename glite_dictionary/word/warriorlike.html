warriorlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɔriərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɒrɪəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
showing characteristics befitting a warrior, such as courage, strength, or bravery
resembling or characteristic of a warrior

Examples

Her warriorlike attitude inspired the team to never give up.
With a warriorlike determination, he faced every challenge head-on.
The general was respected for his warriorlike bravery and discipline.

Synset Examples

spirit, pluck, and fine soldierly qualities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warlike - having or showing an aggressive disposition or readiness for war

Etymology

From 'warrior' + '-like', meaning resembling or characteristic of a warrior.
