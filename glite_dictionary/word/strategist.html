strategist

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrætədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˈstrætədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person skilled in planning and directing overall operations, especially in military or business affairs
expert in planning strategy

Examples

As a brilliant strategist, she was responsible for many of the company's successful campaigns.
During the war, the general proved himself to be an outstanding strategist.
Modern businesses often hire a strategist to help them navigate complex markets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tactician - a person who is adept at planning specific actions to achieve a goal

Etymology

From French 'stratégiste', from Greek 'strategos' (general) via 'strategy'.
