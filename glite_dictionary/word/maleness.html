maleness

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmeɪl.nɪs / (BrE)
the qualities or characteristics typical of a male; the state of being male
the state or quality of being male

Examples

The sculpture emphasizes the maleness of the human form through bold lines and features.
Many animals display clear signs of maleness during the breeding season.
Society often has certain expectations associated with maleness and masculinity.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: femaleness - the qualities or state of being female

Etymology

From 'male' + '-ness', denoting the quality or state.
