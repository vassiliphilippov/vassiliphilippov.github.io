Gopher

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊfər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊfə / (BrE)
an informal nickname for a native or resident of Minnesota, especially in reference to the state's culture or people
nickname for a Minnesotan
informal american

Examples

As a true Gopher, she never missed the Minnesota State Fair.
Many proud Gophers gathered to celebrate their state's heritage.
Even after moving away, he still considers himself a Gopher at heart.

Etymology

In this sense, likely derived from the association of Minnesota with the gopher as a state symbol and the University of Minnesota sports teams ('Golden Gophers').
