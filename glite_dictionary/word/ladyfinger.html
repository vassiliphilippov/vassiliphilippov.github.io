ladyfinger

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪdiˌfɪŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈleɪdiˌfɪŋɡə / (BrE)
A small, light, finger-shaped sponge cake, often used in desserts such as tiramisu or trifle.
finger-shaped sponge cake

Examples

She dipped the ladyfinger into the coffee before adding it to the tiramisu.
The recipe calls for a dozen ladyfingers to line the bottom of the dish.
A single ladyfinger makes a perfect light snack with afternoon tea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sponge cake - a light cake made with eggs, sugar, and flour
