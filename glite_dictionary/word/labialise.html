to labialise

1. VERB

/ ˈleɪ.bi.əˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bɪ.ə.laɪz / (BrE)
to pronounce (a sound) with rounded lips
pronounce with rounded lips

Examples

Some vowels in French are labialised, giving them a rounded quality.
In certain English accents, 'r' may become labialised before a rounded vowel.
Phonetics students learn how to labialise consonants as part of their training.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to labialize - pronounce with rounded lips (alternate spelling, especially US)

Etymology

From Latin 'labium' (lip) + -ize suffix, via French or directly.
