electrostatically

1. ADV

/ ɪˌlɛktroʊˈstætɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈstætɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or caused by static electricity or electrostatic forces.
in an electrostatic manner

Examples

The surface of the balloon attracted hair electrostatically after being rubbed.
The powder is applied electrostatically to ensure even coating.
Some air filters remove particles electrostatically rather than mechanically.

Synset Examples

the dust adhered electrostatically to the surface

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electrostatic - relating to static electricity or electrostatic phenomena

Etymology

Formed from 'electrostatic' + '-ally', indicating manner.
