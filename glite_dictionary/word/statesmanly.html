statesmanly

1. ADJ

/ ˈsteɪtsmənli / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtsmənli / (BrE)
Having the qualities of or befitting a statesman; wise, prudent, or dignified in the management of public affairs.
showing qualities of a statesman

Examples

Her statesmanly approach to the negotiations impressed both parties.
The president delivered a statesmanly speech during the crisis.
His reputation for making statesmanly decisions grew over the years.

Synset Examples

a man of statesmanlike judgment
a statesmanlike solution of the present perplexities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: statesmanlike - marked by the qualities of a statesman

Etymology

From 'statesman' + '-ly', indicating the characteristics of a statesman.
