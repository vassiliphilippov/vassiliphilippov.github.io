self-reliance

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlf rɪˈlaɪəns / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf rɪˈlaɪəns / (BrE)
the ability to depend on yourself for what you need or to achieve things, rather than relying on others
personal independence; reliance on oneself

Examples

She developed a strong sense of self-reliance after moving away from home for college.
The program encourages self-reliance by teaching practical life skills.
Through years of experience, he has learned that self-reliance is key to overcoming obstacles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: independence - freedom from being governed or controlled by others
  • Antonym: dependence - the state of relying on or being controlled by someone or something else

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'reliance', meaning to rely upon oneself.
