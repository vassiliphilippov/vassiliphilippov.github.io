Chester A. Arthur

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʃɛstər eɪ ˈɑːrθər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛstə eɪ ˈɑːθə / (BrE)
(1830–1886) The 21st President of the United States, who assumed office after the assassination of James Garfield and served from 1881 to 1885.
21st President of the United States

Examples

Chester A. Arthur became President after the assassination of James Garfield.
Historians often debate the legacy of Chester A. Arthur and his reforms.
During his presidency, Chester A. Arthur signed the Pendleton Civil Service Reform Act into law.
