savourless

1. ADJ

/ ˈseɪvərləs / (AmE), / ˈseɪvələs / (BrE)
lacking in taste or flavor; without a distinctive or pleasant taste.
lacking in taste or flavor
british

Examples

The soup was completely savourless and left everyone disappointed.
After days of eating savourless meals, she craved something spicy and rich.
This bread is rather savourless compared to the freshly baked kind.

Etymology

From 'savour' (British spelling of 'savor') + '-less' (without).
