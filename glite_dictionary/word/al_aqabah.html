Al Aqabah

1. PROPN

/ æl ˈɑːkəbə / (AmE), / æl ˈɑːkəbə / (BrE)
A port city in southwestern Jordan, located on the Gulf of Aqaba.
port city in Jordan

Examples

Many tourists visit Al Aqabah to enjoy its beautiful beaches and coral reefs.
Goods are frequently shipped through the busy port of Al Aqabah in Jordan.
Al Aqabah serves as Jordan’s only coastal city, providing access to the Red Sea.

Etymology

From Arabic العقبة (al-ʿAqabah), meaning 'the pass' or 'the obstacle'.
