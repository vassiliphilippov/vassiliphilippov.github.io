palomino

1. NOUN

/ ˌpæləˈmiːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˌpaləˈmiːnəʊ / (BrE)
A horse with a light golden or tan coat and a cream or white mane and tail.
golden-colored horse with light mane and tail

Examples

The ranch owner proudly displayed his prized palomino at the horse show.
She always wanted to ride a palomino because of its beautiful golden coat.
Among the herd, the striking palomino was easy to spot.

Etymology

From Spanish 'palomino', possibly derived from 'Paloma' (dove), referring to the pale color.
