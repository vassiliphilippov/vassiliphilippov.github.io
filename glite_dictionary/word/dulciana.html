dulciana

1. NOUN

/ dʌlʃiˈænə / (AmE), / dʌlsiˈɑːnə / (BrE)
An organ stop that produces a soft, sweet tone similar to that of a string instrument.
organ stop with sweet, string-like tone

Examples

The organist selected the dulciana to add a gentle warmth to the hymn.
A soft passage was played using only the dulciana and flute stops.
This pipe organ features a beautifully voiced dulciana in the swell division.

Etymology

From Italian 'dolciana', from 'dolce' (sweet), reflecting the sweetness of its tone.
