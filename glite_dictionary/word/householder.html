householder

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person who owns or lives in a house, especially as the main occupant
person owning or occupying a house

Examples

Each householder is responsible for paying local property taxes.
The survey was completed by the householder in each selected address.
As a new householder, she had to arrange utilities and maintenance for her property.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homeowner - a person who owns their home

Etymology

From Middle English 'householdere', from 'house' + 'holder'.
