Sevilla

1. PROPN

/ seˈviː.jə / (AmE), / səˈviː.jə / (BrE)
A city in southwestern Spain, known for its major port, rich cultural heritage, and as the historic center of bullfighting; also known as Seville in English.
city in southwestern Spain

Examples

The vibrant city of Sevilla is famous for its annual Feria de Abril festival.
Many tourists visit Sevilla to admire its stunning architecture and experience flamenco dancing.
The long history of Sevilla is evident in its ancient palaces and lively plazas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Seville - English name for the city of Sevilla, Spain

Etymology

From Spanish 'Sevilla', derived from Latin 'Hispalis'.
