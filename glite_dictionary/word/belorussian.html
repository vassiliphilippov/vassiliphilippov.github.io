Belorussian

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɛləˈrʌʃən / (AmE), / ˌbɛləˈrʌʃən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Byelorussia (now Belarus)
native or inhabitant of Belarus

Examples

Many Belorussians immigrated to the United States in the nineteenth century.
She met a group of Belorussians while studying abroad in Eastern Europe.
The Belorussian community in the city celebrates their national holidays every year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Belarusian - native or inhabitant of Belarus

Etymology

From Byelorussia (an older English name for Belarus) + -an.
