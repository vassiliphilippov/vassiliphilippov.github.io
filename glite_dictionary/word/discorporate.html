discorporate

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈkɔːrpərət / (AmE), / dɪsˈkɔːpərət / (BrE)
Having no material body; not incarnate.
without a material body

Examples

Some people believe that discorporate entities can influence the physical world.
In the novel, the alien took on a discorporate form to travel through walls.
Myths often describe ghosts as discorporate beings invisible to the living.

Synset Examples

bodiless ghosts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incorporeal - lacking a physical body; immaterial
  • Synonym: bodiless - without a physical body

Etymology

From Latin dis- (apart) + corpus (body), meaning 'separated from the body'.
