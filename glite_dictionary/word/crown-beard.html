crown-beard

1. NOUN

/ ˈkraʊnˌbɪrd / (AmE), / ˈkraʊnˌbɜːd / (BrE)
Any plant of the genus Verbesina, characterized by clustered white or yellow flower heads.
a plant of the genus Verbesina

Examples

Many gardeners plant crown-beard for its attractive clusters of yellow blooms.
The meadows were filled with wildflowers, including several types of crown-beard.
Botanists studied the crown-beard to better understand its role in native ecosystems.
