unquestioned

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkwɛstʃənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwɛstʃənd / (BrE)
generally accepted as true or correct and not doubted or disputed
not doubted or disputed

Examples

Her unquestioned loyalty to the company impressed her colleagues.
The leader's authority in the region remained unquestioned for decades.
It is an unquestioned fact that regular exercise improves health.

Synset Examples

the undisputed fact

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undisputed - not questioned or doubted; accepted by everyone
  • Antonym: disputed - subject to disagreement or doubt
