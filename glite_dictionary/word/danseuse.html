danseuse

1. NOUN

/ dɑnˈsuːz / (AmE), / dɑːnˈsɜːz / (BrE)
A female ballet dancer, especially a professional one.
female ballet dancer

Examples

The young danseuse performed the solo with remarkable poise and grace.
As a celebrated danseuse, she has toured with several famous ballet companies.
Many aspiring girls dream of becoming a principal danseuse at the national ballet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ballerina - a female ballet dancer

Etymology

From French 'danseuse', feminine of 'danseur' (dancer).
