cookware

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊkˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈkʊk.weə / (BrE)
Pots, pans, and other utensils used for cooking food.
pots, pans, and utensils for cooking

Examples

She bought new cookware before moving into her apartment.
Stainless steel cookware is popular because it is durable and easy to clean.
The chef prefers to use heavy-duty cookware for preparing large meals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bakeware - Containers used for baking food in an oven

Etymology

Formed from 'cook' + 'ware', on the pattern of 'tableware', 'silverware'.
