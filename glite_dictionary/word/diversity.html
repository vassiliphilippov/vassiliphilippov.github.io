diversity

1. NOUN

/ daɪˈvɝːsəti / (AmE), / daɪˈvɜːsəti / (BrE)
the state of being diverse; a range of different things or the inclusion of distinct elements or qualities
variety; range of different things

Examples

The company values diversity in its workforce, believing it leads to fresh ideas and innovation.
There is a remarkable diversity of plants and animals in the tropical rainforest.
Her novels are praised for their diversity of characters and perspectives.

Synset Examples

a diversity of possibilities
the range and variety of his work is amazing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: variety - the quality or state of being different or diverse; the absence of uniformity or monotony
  • Antonym: uniformity - the quality or state of being uniform, without diversity

Etymology

From Latin diversitas (variety, difference), from diversus (different).
