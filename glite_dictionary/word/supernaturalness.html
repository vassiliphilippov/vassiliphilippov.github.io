supernaturalness

1. NOUN

/ ˌsuː.pɚˈnætʃ.r̩.əl.nəs / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈnætʃ.rə.l.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being supernatural; the condition of being attributed to powers or phenomena beyond the natural world
the quality of being supernatural

Examples

The film's sense of supernaturalness kept viewers on the edge of their seats.
She questioned the supernaturalness of the events, looking for logical explanations.
Many myths explore the supernaturalness of ancient gods and spirits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supernatural - relating to forces beyond natural explanation

Etymology

From supernatural + -ness; ultimately from Latin super (above) + natura (nature).
