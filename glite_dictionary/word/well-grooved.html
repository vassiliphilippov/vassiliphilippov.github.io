well-grooved

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɛlˈɡruvd / (AmE), / ˌwɛlˈɡruːvd / (BrE)
(of a process, habit, or activity) established or operating smoothly, as if following a habitual pattern or settled routine.
established in a settled pattern

Examples

After years of practice, their teamwork has become well-grooved and efficient.
The company operates like a well-grooved machine, with every department working in sync.
His morning ritual is so well-grooved that he barely has to think about each step.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: well-oiled - functioning smoothly and efficiently
  • Synonym: routine - a regular way of doing things, habit
