inactiveness

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈæktɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈæktɪvnɪs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being inactive; lack of activity or action.
state of being inactive

Examples

Prolonged inactiveness can lead to health problems such as obesity and muscle weakness.
The inactiveness of the board delayed important decisions for months.
They attributed the machine’s failure to its inactiveness over the winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inactivity - The state of being inactive
  • Antonym: activity - The state of being active

Etymology

Formed from 'inactive' plus the noun suffix '-ness'.
