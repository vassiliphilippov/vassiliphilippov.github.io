dombeya

1. NOUN

/ dɑmˈbeɪ.ə / (AmE), / dɒmˈbiː.ə / (BrE)
A shrub or small tree of the genus Dombeya, valued for its rounded clusters of often sweet-scented, pendent flowers.
a shrub or small tree with hanging flower clusters

Examples

The dombeya bloomed early this year, filling the garden with its sweet fragrance.
Gardeners often plant dombeya for their attractive, cascading flower clusters.
We admired the pink blossoms hanging delicately from the dombeya tree.

Etymology

Named after Joseph Dombey, an 18th-century French botanist.
