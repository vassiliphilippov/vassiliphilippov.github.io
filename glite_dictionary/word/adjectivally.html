adjectivally

1. ADV

/ ædˌʒɛkˈtɪvəli / (AmE), / ədˌdʒɛkˈtɪvəli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or characteristic of an adjective; as an adjective.
in an adjectival manner

Examples

The phrase functions adjectivally to modify the noun that follows it.
In this sentence, the word 'golden' is used adjectivally rather than as a simple noun.
Some participles may be used adjectivally in various grammatical constructions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adjectival - Relating to or functioning as an adjective

Etymology

From 'adjective' + '-ally', the adverbial suffix.
