Zambian

1. ADJ

/ ˈzæmbiən / (AmE), / ˈzæmbiən / (BrE)
relating to Zambia or its people or culture.
of Zambia or its people

Examples

The Zambian government introduced new policies last year.
She enjoys listening to Zambian music.
Several Zambian athletes competed in the international tournament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Zambia - a country in southern Africa

Etymology

Formed from the country name Zambia + English suffix -an.

2. NOUN

/ ˈzæmbiən / (AmE), / ˈzæmbiən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Zambia
person from Zambia

Examples

The Zambian greeted us warmly when we arrived in Lusaka.
A Zambian won the international science competition this year.
Many Zambians speak both English and local languages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Zambia - a country in southern Africa
