disgustful

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈɡʌstfəl / (AmE), / dɪsˈɡʌstfəl / (BrE)
causing a strong feeling of dislike or revulsion; highly offensive or repellent
causing strong disgust or revulsion

Examples

The fridge emitted a disgustful odor that made everyone leave the room.
He found the sight of the waste in the river truly disgustful.
Her disgustful expression made it clear she detested the taste of the soup.

Synset Examples

a disgusting smell
distasteful language
a loathsome disease
the idea of eating meat is repellent to me
revolting food
a wicked stench

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disgusting - causing a strong feeling of dislike or revulsion
  • Antonym: delightful - highly pleasing or charming

Etymology

From 'disgust' + '-ful'; 'disgust' from French 'dégoûter'.
