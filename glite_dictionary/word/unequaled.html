unequaled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈiːkwəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈiːkwəld / (BrE)
better than anyone or anything else; having no equal.
having no equal; unmatched

Examples

Her performance in the piano competition was unequaled by any of her peers.
The museum houses an unequaled collection of Renaissance art.
He brought an unequaled passion and energy to his work every day.

Synset Examples

he is alone in the field of microbiology
this theory is altogether alone in its penetration of the problem
Bach was unique in his handling of counterpoint
craftsmen whose skill is unequaled
unparalleled athletic ability
a breakdown of law unparalleled in our history

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unparalleled - having no parallel or equal; exceptional
  • Synonym: unique - being the only one of its kind; unlike anything else

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'equaled,' past participle of 'equal.'
