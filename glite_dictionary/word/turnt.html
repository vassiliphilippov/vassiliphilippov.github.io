turnt

1. ADJ

/ tɜːrnt / (AmE), / tɜːnt / (BrE)
extremely excited or energetic, especially at a social event or party; in a heightened state of excitement or enthusiasm, often due to music, dancing, or alcohol
very excited, especially at a party
slang

Examples

Everyone at the club was so turnt last night.
The crowd gets turnt whenever their favorite song starts playing.
She showed up to the party already turnt and ready to dance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hyped - very excited or enthusiastic

Etymology

Slang alteration of 'turned', popularized in hip hop culture in the early 2000s.
