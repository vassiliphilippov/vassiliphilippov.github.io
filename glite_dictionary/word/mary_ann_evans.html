Mary Ann Evans

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɛri ˈæn ˈɛvənz / (AmE), / ˌmɛəri ˈæn ˈɛvənz / (BrE)
The real name of George Eliot (1819–1880), a British novelist known for her realistic depiction of Victorian society.
real name of novelist George Eliot
