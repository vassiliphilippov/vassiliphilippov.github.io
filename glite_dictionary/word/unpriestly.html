unpriestly

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpriːstli / (AmE), / ʌnˈpriːstli / (BrE)
not characteristic of or befitting a priest; not priestly.
not characteristic of a priest

Examples

His unpriestly behavior shocked the entire congregation.
Many found the remark rather unpriestly for someone of his position.
The documentary exposed several unpriestly actions within the organization.

Synset Examples

unpriestly behavior

Related Terms

  • Antonym: priestly - characteristic of or befitting a priest

Etymology

Formed by combining the prefix 'un-' (not) with 'priestly'.
