long-range

1. ADJ

/ lɔŋˈreɪndʒ / (AmE), / lɒŋˈreɪndʒ / (BrE)
lasting or relating to a long period of time in the future; involving plans or effects that extend far into the future.
lasting or affecting a long period of time

Examples

The company outlined its long-range plans for expansion over the next decade.
Setting long-range goals can help you stay motivated through challenging times.
The committee is responsible for long-range strategic planning.

Synset Examples

long-range goals

Related Terms

  • Antonym: short-term - lasting or relating to a short period of time
