VD

1. NOUN

/ viː ˈdiː / (AmE), / viː ˈdiː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for venereal disease, a contagious infection transmitted by sexual intercourse or genital contact
abbreviation for venereal disease
medical

Examples

There was a rise in cases of VD during the war years.
Public health campaigns encouraged people to get tested for VD.
The clinic specializes in the treatment of VD and other infections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexually transmitted disease - disease spread by sexual contact; general modern term

Etymology

Abbreviation of venereal disease; from Latin 'venereus', relating to sexual intercourse, after Venus, the goddess of love.
