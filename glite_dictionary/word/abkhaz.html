Abkhaz

1. ADJ

/ æbˈkɑz / (AmE), / æbˈkɑːz / (BrE)
relating to Abkhazia, its people, or their language
of Abkhazia, its people, or language

Examples

The Abkhaz language is spoken in parts of the Caucasus region.
She enjoyed learning about Abkhaz traditions and customs.
An Abkhaz delegation attended the international conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Abkhazian - relating to Abkhazia, its people or language (alternative form)

Etymology

From Russian Абха́з (Abkház), from Abkhaz аҧсуа (aṗsua, meaning 'Abkhaz').

WordNet ID: oewn-02609866-a

2. NOUN

/ æbˈkɑz / (AmE), / abˈkɑːz / (BrE)
a member of a Caucasian ethnic group living mainly in Abkhazia, to the east of the Black Sea
member of a Caucasian ethnic group in Abkhazia

Examples

She met an Abkhaz family while traveling along the Black Sea coast.
Many Abkhaz have maintained their native language and customs.
The documentary highlights the traditions of the Abkhaz community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Circassian - member of a people in the northwestern Caucasus, related ethnic group

Etymology

From Russian Абхаз (Abkhaz), from Abkhaz self-designation Аҧсны (Apsny).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09660722-n
Synset POS: n

3. NOUN

/ æbˈkɑz / (AmE), / æbˈkɑːz / (BrE)
A member of a Caucasian ethnic group native to the region east of the Black Sea, mainly in Abkhazia.
member of a Caucasian ethnic group from Abkhazia

Examples

Many Abkhaz maintain strong ties to their ancestral homeland in the Caucasus.
The traditional music of the Abkhaz reflects their rich cultural heritage.
Several Abkhaz communities exist in Turkey due to historical migrations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Abkhazian - of or relating to the Abkhaz people or their language

Etymology

From Russian Абхаз (Abkház), from Abkhaz аҧсыуаа (aṗsnywaá) meaning 'Abkhaz person.'

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08504963-n
Synset POS: n

4. NOUN

/ æbˈkɑz / (AmE), / abˈkɑːz / (BrE)
a region with limited international recognition, located on the eastern coast of the Black Sea in the Caucasus, considered by most countries as part of Georgia
disputed region in the Caucasus

Examples

The status of Abkhaz remains a source of tension in the Caucasus region.
Few countries officially recognize Abkhaz as an independent state.
Travelers are often advised to learn about the political situation in Abkhaz before visiting.

Etymology

From Russian Абхазия (Abkhaziya), from Abkhaz Аԥсуа (Apswa, 'Abkhazian').
