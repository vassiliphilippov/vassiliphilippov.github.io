premedical

1. ADJ

/ ˌpriˈmɛdɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈmɛdɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to the period or courses of study that prepare a student to enter medical school.
preparing for medical school

Examples

She enrolled in several premedical courses during her first year of college.
His premedical studies focused on biology and chemistry.
The university offers a dedicated premedical program for aspiring doctors.

Synset Examples

premedical courses

Etymology

From pre- (before) + medical.
