to sizz

1. VERB

/ sɪz / (AmE), / sɪz / (BrE)
to express or utter something with a hissing sound
utter with a hiss

Examples

She sizzed angrily, warning them to keep quiet.
The snake sizzes a warning as it slithers through the grass.
He sizzed his disapproval from the shadows of the room.

2. VERB

/ sɪz / (AmE), / sɪz / (BrE)
to make a sharp hissing sound, as in expressing disapproval
make a sharp hissing sound

Examples

The audience began to sizz when the speaker made an unpopular comment.
She sizzed loudly at the referee's decision during the match.
You could hear the crowd sizzing from the back of the hall.
