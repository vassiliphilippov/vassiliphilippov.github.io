ms

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛmˈɛs / (AmE), / ˌɛmˈɛs / (BrE)
an original copy of a literary work, document, or text prepared for publication, typically before printing or final revision
manuscript submitted for publication
informal

Examples

The author submitted her ms to several publishers in hopes of getting a book deal.
Please ensure your ms is double-spaced before sending it to the editor.
His ms was returned with suggestions for major revisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manuscript - original handwritten or typed copy of a text, especially for publication

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'manuscript', from Latin 'manuscriptus' (written by hand).
