miraculously

1. ADV

/ mɪˈrækjələsli / (AmE), / mɪˈrækjʊləsli / (BrE)
In a way that is very surprising or unexpected and seems to be caused by a miracle.
in a miraculous or amazing way

Examples

The missing child was found miraculously unharmed after three days in the forest.
The injury miraculously healed much faster than doctors predicted.
She miraculously escaped the accident without a single scratch.

Synset Examples

my hand grasped the gun that was, miraculously, lying on the ground beside my finger tips

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miraculous - having the nature of a miracle; very surprising or unlikely

Etymology

From 'miraculous' + '-ly', 'miraculous' from Latin 'miraculosus' (wonderful).
