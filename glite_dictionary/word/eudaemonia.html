eudaemonia

1. NOUN

/ juːˌdiːməˈniːə / (AmE), / juːˌdiːməˈniːə / (BrE)
A state of happiness, well-being, and prosperity; human flourishing, especially as an ideal in philosophy.
state of happiness and well-being

Examples

Ancient philosophers believed that eudaemonia was the highest human good.
She pursued a life of eudaemonia, focusing on personal growth and well-being.
Many scholars debate how best to achieve eudaemonia in modern society.

Synset Examples

the town was finally on the upbeat after our recent troubles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: happiness - the state of being happy
  • Synonym: flourishing - living in a state of thriving and well-being

Etymology

From Greek 'eudaimonia' (happiness, prosperity), from 'eu-' (well) + 'daimon' (spirit).
