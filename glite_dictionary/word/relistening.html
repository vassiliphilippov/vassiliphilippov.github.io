relistening

1. NOUN

/ ˌriːˈlɪsənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈlɪsənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of hearing or listening to something again, especially an audio recording.
hearing again

Examples

After her relistening of the album, she noticed details she had missed before.
Repeated relistening can reveal new layers in complex pieces of music.
His enjoyment of the story increased with every relistening of the audio book.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to listen - to give attention with the ear; to hear attentively

Etymology

From the prefix 're-' (again) + 'listening.'
