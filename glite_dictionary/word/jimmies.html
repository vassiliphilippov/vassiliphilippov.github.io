jimmies

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪmiz / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪmiz / (BrE)
small pieces of sweet chocolate or candy used as a topping, especially on ice cream or baked goods
small sweet chocolate or candy toppings
american

Examples

She topped her sundae with chocolate jimmies and a cherry.
Would you like some jimmies on your ice cream cone?
The bakery sells cupcakes decorated with colorful jimmies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sprinkles - tiny pieces of colored sugar or candy used to decorate desserts
