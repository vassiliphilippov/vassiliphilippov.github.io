doorstep

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔrˌstɛp / (AmE), / ˈdɔːˌstɛp / (BrE)
the step or sill at the base of a doorway, forming the threshold you cross to enter or exit
step or sill at the base of a doorway

Examples

She left the package on the doorstep for her neighbor to find.
After the rainstorm, muddy footprints were all over the doorstep.
He paused on the doorstep before entering his childhood home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: threshold - the strip or sill forming the bottom of a doorway

Etymology

From 'door' + 'step', indicating the step at a door.
