petalled

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛtəld / (AmE), / ˈpɛtəld / (BrE)
(of a flower) having petals.
having petals (of flowers)

Examples

The petalled blossoms added a vibrant splash of color to the garden.
Some rare species produce unusual, many-petalled flowers.
The artist carefully painted each petalled bloom in the bouquet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petal - a leaf-like colored part of a flower

Etymology

From 'petal' + '-ed', forming an adjective meaning 'having petals'.
