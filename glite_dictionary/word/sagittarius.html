Sagittarius

/ sæˌdʒɪˈtɛəriəs / (AmE), / sæˌdʒɪˈtɛəriəs / (BrE)
A genus of birds in the family Sagittariidae, including the secretary bird.
genus of birds (includes secretary bird)

Examples

The secretary bird, belonging to the genus Sagittarius, is native to sub-Saharan Africa.
Researchers observed a nesting pair of Sagittarius in the grasslands.
The Sagittarius genus is noted for its long-legged members.

Etymology

From Latin sagittārius, meaning 'archer', referencing the bird's long 'legged' appearance.
