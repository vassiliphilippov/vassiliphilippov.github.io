unspectacular

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.spɛkˈtæk.jə.lɚ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.spɛkˈtæk.jʊ.lə / (BrE)
not impressive or remarkable; ordinary or unremarkable in appearance or effect
not impressive or remarkable

Examples

Despite his unspectacular performance, the team appreciated his consistent effort.
The restaurant offers good food in an unspectacular setting.
Her achievements, though unspectacular, were essential to the project's success.

Synset Examples

an unspectacular but necessary task

Related Terms

  • Antonym: spectacular - impressive or striking in appearance or effect
  • Synonym: ordinary - with no special or distinctive features; normal

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'spectacular' (impressive, striking), itself from Latin 'spectaculum' (a public show).
