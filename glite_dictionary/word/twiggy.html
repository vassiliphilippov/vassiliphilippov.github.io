twiggy

1. ADJ

/ ˈtwɪɡi / (AmE), / ˈtwɪɡi / (BrE)
very thin, like a twig; slender
very thin; slender

Examples

The model looked twiggy in her narrow jeans and fitted top.
After his illness, he became quite twiggy and lost a lot of weight.
The tall, twiggy trees swayed in the autumn wind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slender - gracefully thin
  • Synonym: skinny - very thin; lacking flesh

Etymology

From 'twig' (a small, thin branch) + '-y' (adjective suffix).
