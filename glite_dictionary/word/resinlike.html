resinlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛz.ənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈrɛz.ɪnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having properties or a texture similar to resin.
resembling resin in properties or texture

Examples

The sap hardened into a resinlike substance over time.
Some adhesives leave a sticky, resinlike residue on surfaces.
The resinlike coating protected the wood from moisture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resinous - having the characteristics of resin
