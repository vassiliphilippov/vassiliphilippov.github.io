Emmenthal

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛmənˌtɑl / (AmE), / ˈɛmənˌtɑːl / (BrE)
A type of Swiss cheese with characteristic large holes and a mild, slightly nutty flavor.
Swiss cheese with large holes

Examples

I bought a block of Emmenthal for our cheese platter.
The sandwich was topped with melted Emmenthal and ham.
Swiss fondue often features Emmenthal as a main ingredient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Swiss cheese - Firm, pale yellow cheese with characteristic holes, originally from Switzerland

Etymology

From German Emmentaler, meaning 'from the Emme valley' in Switzerland.
