Cappadocian

1. ADJ

/ ˌkæp.əˈdoʊ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌkæp.əˈdəʊ.ʃən / (BrE)
relating to Cappadocia, a historical region in central Turkey, or its people or culture
of or relating to Cappadocia or its people

Examples

The museum features several Cappadocian artifacts dating back to the Roman era.
She specializes in the study of Cappadocian religious traditions and art.
Many tourists are fascinated by the unique landscapes of the Cappadocian region.

Etymology

From Latin Cappadocia, the name of the historical region in present-day Turkey, plus English -an.
