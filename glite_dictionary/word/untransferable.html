untransferable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌntrænˈsfɜrəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌntrɑːnsˈfɜːrəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be transferred to another person or place.
incapable of being transferred

Examples

The tickets are strictly untransferable and must be used by the person who purchased them.
My gym membership is untransferable, so I can't give it to anyone else.
She discovered that her frequent flyer miles were untransferable and couldn't be passed on to family.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: transferable - able to be transferred

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' + 'transferable'.
