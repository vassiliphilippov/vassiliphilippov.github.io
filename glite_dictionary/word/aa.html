AA

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪ ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˈeɪ / (BrE)
an international organization that provides support and a fellowship for individuals trying to stop drinking alcohol, also known as Alcoholics Anonymous
Alcoholics Anonymous support organization

Examples

Many recovering alcoholics credit AA meetings for helping them stay sober.
He joined AA after realizing his drinking was out of control.
The local chapter of AA meets every Tuesday evening at the church.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Alcoholics Anonymous - the full name of the organization abbreviated as AA

Etymology

Abbreviation of Alcoholics Anonymous, founded in the United States in 1935.

2. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˈeɪ ˈɑː / (BrE)
An academic degree called Associate of Arts, typically awarded after two years of study at a college or community college.
associate degree in arts

Examples

She earned her AA at a local community college before transferring to a university.
The requirements for an AA vary depending on the chosen major and institution.
Many students complete an AA to save money before pursuing a bachelor's degree.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: AS - Associate of Science degree, similar but focused on science

Etymology

Abbreviation of Associate of Arts.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06710698-n
Synset POS: n
