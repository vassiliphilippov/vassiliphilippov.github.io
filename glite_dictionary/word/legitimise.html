to legitimise

1. VERB

/ lɪˈdʒɪtəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / lɪˈdʒɪ.tɪ.maɪz / (BrE)
to make something legal or acceptable by law or by official approval
make legal or officially acceptable
british

Examples

Some countries have moved to legitimise the use of medical marijuana.
The government decided to legitimise previously unregulated financial practices.
New laws were introduced to legitimise same-sex marriage.

Synset Examples

Marijuana should be legalized

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to legitimize - make legal or officially acceptable (American spelling)
  • Antonym: to criminalize - make illegal

Etymology

From French légitimer, from Latin legitimus (lawful, legitimate).
