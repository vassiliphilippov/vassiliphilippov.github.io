Amman

1. PROPN

/ ɑˈmɑn / (AmE), / əˈmɑːn / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Jordan, located in the northwestern part of the country
capital city of Jordan

Examples

She traveled to Amman to attend an international business conference.
Amman is known for its ancient citadel and vibrant downtown markets.
Flights from London to Amman are available several times a week.

Etymology

From Arabic عمان (ʿAmmān), the city's name.
