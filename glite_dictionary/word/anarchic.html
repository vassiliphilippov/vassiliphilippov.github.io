anarchic

1. ADJ

/ əˈnɑːrkɪk / (AmE), / əˈnɑːkɪk / (BrE)
lacking order or control; not governed by rules or authority
without law or control

Examples

After the collapse of the government, the region quickly became anarchic and dangerous.
The meeting descended into an anarchic shouting match with no one taking charge.
Some artists prefer an anarchic approach to creativity, rejecting all formal rules.

Synset Examples

the system is economically inefficient and politically anarchic

Related Terms

  • Antonym: orderly - well organized or controlled
  • Synonym: lawless - not governed by law; without law

Etymology

From Greek anarkhos (without a ruler), from an- (without) + arkhos (ruler).
