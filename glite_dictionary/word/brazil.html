Brazil

1. PROPN

/ brəˈzɪl / (AmE), / brəˈzɪl / (BrE)
a large country in South America, the largest Portuguese-speaking country in the world and a leading exporter of coffee
country in South America

Examples

Brazil is famous for its vibrant Carnival festival and passion for football.
Coffee from Brazil is exported to countries all over the world.
The Amazon rainforest, located partly in Brazil, is vital to global biodiversity.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Brasil', possibly from 'pau-brasil' (brazilwood tree native to the region).

2. PROPN

/ brəˈzɪl / (AmE), / brəˈzɪl / (BrE)
the largest country in South America, officially called the Federative Republic of Brazil, known for its vast Amazon rainforest, major cities like Rio de Janeiro and São Paulo, and as the world's largest Portuguese-speaking nation.
largest country in South America

Examples

Brazil is famous for its vibrant Carnival celebrations and passionate football culture.
Many species unique to the Amazon rainforest are found only in Brazil.
Coffee from Brazil is exported to countries all around the world.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Brasil', originally from 'pau-brasil', a type of red dyewood found in the country.
