streaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈstriːkər / (AmE), / ˈstriːkə / (BrE)
a person who runs naked through a public place as a prank or protest
person who runs naked in public as a prank

Examples

A streaker ran onto the football field during halftime, causing the crowd to erupt with laughter.
The security guards quickly chased down the streaker at the tennis match.
There was a news story about a streaker interrupting the live broadcast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: streak - to move quickly in a specified direction

Etymology

From 'streak' (to move quickly), with the agentive suffix '-er'; popularized in the 1970s.
