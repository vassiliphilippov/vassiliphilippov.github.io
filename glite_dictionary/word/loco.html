loco

1. ADJ

/ ˈloʊkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəʊ / (BrE)
(informal) Crazy or insane; not behaving in a normal or reasonable way.
crazy; insane (informal)
informal slang

Examples

He sounded completely loco when he described his plan.
You’d have to be loco to drive in this storm.
Sometimes my cat acts totally loco for no reason.

Synset Examples

it used to drive my husband balmy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged or behaving strangely
  • Antonym: sane - having a healthy mind; not mentally ill

Etymology

From Spanish 'loco' (crazy).
