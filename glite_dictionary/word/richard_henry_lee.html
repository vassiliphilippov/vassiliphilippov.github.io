Richard Henry Lee

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɪtʃərd ˈhɛnri liː / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃəd ˈhɛnri liː / (BrE)
(1732–1794) An American statesman and leader of the American Revolution who proposed the resolution calling for the independence of the American colonies.
American Revolutionary leader and statesman

Examples

Richard Henry Lee was instrumental in advocating for American independence from British rule.
Many historians credit Richard Henry Lee with introducing the motion for colonial independence in the Continental Congress.
The courage of Richard Henry Lee helped shape the future of the United States.
