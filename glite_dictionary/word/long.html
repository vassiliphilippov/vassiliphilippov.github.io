long

1. ADJ

/ lɔŋ / (AmE), / lɒŋ / (BrE)
having more than what is usual or necessary, especially in a particular quality or supply
having more than usual or necessary

Examples

He is long on ambition, though his skills are still developing.
They were long on supplies, so there was never a shortage.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: short - having less than usual or necessary

to have a strong, often persistent desire for something; to yearn deeply

Examples

Related Terms
