academicianship

1. NOUN

/ əˌkædəˌmɪʃənˈʃɪp / (AmE), / əˌkadɪˌmɪʃənˈʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or status of being a member of an honorary academy, such as a national academy of sciences or arts
the status of being an academician

Examples

His academicianship was recognized with a special ceremony at the national academy.
She valued her academicianship both for its prestige and for the opportunities to collaborate with other scholars.
After years of dedication to the arts, he was finally awarded academicianship in the Royal Academy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: academician - a member of an honorary academy

Etymology

From 'academician' + '-ship', denoting status or office.
