Napoleonic

1. ADJ

/ nəˌpoʊliˈɑːnɪk / (AmE), / nəˌpəʊlɪˈɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Napoleon Bonaparte, his era, or his policies.
relating to Napoleon Bonaparte

Examples

The Napoleonic Code influenced legal systems throughout Europe and beyond.
She collected Napoleonic memorabilia from the early 19th century.
The army adopted several Napoleonic strategies during the campaign.

Synset Examples

Napoleonic Wars

Etymology

From Napoleon, the name of the French emperor (1769–1821), plus the suffix -ic.
