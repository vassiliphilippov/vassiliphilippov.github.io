oncologic

1. ADJ

/ ɑn.kəˈlɑ.dʒɪk / (AmE), / ɒŋ.kəˈlɒ.dʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study and treatment of tumors (oncology)
relating to oncology
medical

Examples

She works as an oncologic nurse specializing in chemotherapy treatments.
The hospital opened a new oncologic research center last year.
Advances in oncologic therapies have significantly improved patient survival rates.

Synset Examples

oncological nurse

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oncological - alternative (and more common) adjective form of oncologic

Etymology

From oncology + -ic, where 'oncology' comes from Greek 'onkos' (mass, tumor) and '-logy' (study of).
