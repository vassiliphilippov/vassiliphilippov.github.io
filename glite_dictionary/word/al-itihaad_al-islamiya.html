al-Itihaad al-Islamiya

1. PROPN

/ æl ɪˈtiːhɑːd æl ɪsˈlæmiːjə / (AmE), / æl ɪˈtiːhɑːd æl ɪsˈlæmɪjə / (BrE)
an Islamist militant organization based in Somalia, known for terrorist activities and connections to al-Qaeda, with branches in several countries
Somali Islamist militant group

Examples

The United Nations condemned al-Itihaad al-Islamiya for its involvement in terrorist attacks in East Africa.
Several members of al-Itihaad al-Islamiya were captured during a joint operation by Somali forces.
Reports indicate that al-Itihaad al-Islamiya has established links with other extremist groups in the region.

Etymology

From Arabic 'al-Ittiḥād al-Islāmīya', meaning 'Islamic Union'.
