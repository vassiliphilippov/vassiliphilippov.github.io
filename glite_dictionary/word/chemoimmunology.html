chemoimmunology

1. NOUN

/ ˌkiːmoʊˌɪm.jəˈnɑː.lə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌkiːməʊˌɪm.juːˈnɒl.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
The branch of science dealing with the chemical processes and interactions in immunology, including the study of antigens and antibodies.
chemical processes in immunology
medical

Examples

Recent advances in chemoimmunology have improved treatments for autoimmune diseases.
Her research in chemoimmunology focuses on the chemical structure of antibodies.
Graduate students can specialize in chemoimmunology within the biochemistry department.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immunology - The study of the immune system and immune responses

Etymology

From chemo- (relating to chemistry) and immunology.
