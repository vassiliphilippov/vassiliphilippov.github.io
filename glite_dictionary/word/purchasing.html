purchasing

/ ˈpɝː.tʃəs.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.tʃəs.ɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

The company has strict policies for purchasing new equipment.
Smart purchasing can help a family save money over time.
He works in the purchasing department of a large retailer.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

Etymology

From Old French *pourchacier* (to pursue, procure), later influenced by Middle English *purchase* (acquire, buy).
