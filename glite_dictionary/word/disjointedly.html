disjointedly

1. ADV

/ dɪsˈdʒɔɪntɪdli / (AmE), / dɪsˈdʒɔɪntɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that lacks order or coherence; in a disconnected or fragmented manner
in a disconnected or fragmented manner

Examples

He tried to explain the events disjointedly, making it hard for anyone to follow his story.
Her thoughts drifted disjointedly from one topic to another during the discussion.
The team's statements appeared disjointedly in the final report, lacking a clear narrative.

Synset Examples

‘We're not married, not really married,’ she said, and slowly, reluctantly, disjointedly it came out

Related Terms

  • Antonym: coherently - in a logical and orderly way
  • Synonym: disjointed - lacking a coherent sequence or connection (adjective)

Etymology

From 'disjointed' (not connected) + '-ly' (forming adverbs).
