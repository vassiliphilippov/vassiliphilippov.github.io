copular

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑːpjələr / (AmE), / ˈkɒpjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or functioning as a copula, especially in grammar.
relating to a copula (grammar)

Examples

In the sentence 'She is happy,' 'is' is a copular verb.
The copular construction links the subject to its description.
Linguists often study the use of copular verbs in different languages.

Synset Examples

a copular verb

Related Terms

  • Synonym: copula - a word, especially a verb like 'be', linking the subject and complement

Etymology

From Latin 'copula' (a bond, link), with the suffix '-ar'.
