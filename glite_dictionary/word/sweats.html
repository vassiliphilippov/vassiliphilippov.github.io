sweats

1. NOUN

/ swɛts / (AmE), / swɛts / (BrE)
Loose-fitting pants and a matching sweatshirt, typically worn for exercising or as casual clothing.
casual set of sweatpants and sweatshirt

Examples

She changed into her sweats after coming home from work to relax.
It's cold outside—don't forget to pack your sweats for the trip.
He wore his favorite sweats to the gym this morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tracksuit - jacket and trousers worn for sports or exercise
