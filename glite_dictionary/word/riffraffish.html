riffraffish

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪfˌræfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɪfˌræfɪʃ / (BrE)
characteristic of or resembling riffraff; behaving in a way considered disreputable or low-class
disreputable or low-class behavior

Examples

His riffraffish behavior embarrassed everyone at the party.
Some guests were appalled by the riffraffish conduct of their tablemates at the banquet.
The teacher warned students not to act in a riffraffish manner during the school trip.

Synset Examples

His riffraffish behavior embarrassed everyone at the party.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: respectable - behaving in a way considered proper or socially acceptable

Etymology

Formed from 'riffraff' (people regarded as disreputable or worthless) + '-ish' (forming adjectives).
