pharmaceutic

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɑrməˈsuːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌfɑːməˈsjuːtɪk / (BrE)
A drug or medicine that is prepared or dispensed in pharmacies and used for medical treatment.
drug or medicine from a pharmacy
medical

Examples

The hospital has a strict policy for issuing any pharmaceutic to patients.
Researchers are developing a new pharmaceutic to treat rare genetic disorders.
You should consult a doctor before taking any pharmaceutic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pharmaceutical - drug or medicine used in medical treatment

Etymology

From Greek pharmakeutikos (of drugs or medicines), from pharmakeuein (to prepare drugs).
