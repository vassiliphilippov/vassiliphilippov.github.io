sharply

1. ADV

/ ˈʃɑːrpli / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːpli / (BrE)
suddenly or steeply, with a quick or noticeable change in direction or degree.
suddenly or steeply

Examples

The car turned sharply to avoid the obstacle.
Temperatures dropped sharply overnight, leading to frost in the morning.
The road bends sharply to the left after the bridge.
