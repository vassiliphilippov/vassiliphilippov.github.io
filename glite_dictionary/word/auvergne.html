Auvergne

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈvɛrn / (AmE), / əʊˈvɛəɲ / (BrE)
a region in central France known for its volcanic landscape, historical sites, and traditional cheeses
region of central France

Examples

The rolling hills of Auvergne are famous for their lush pastures and distinctive cheeses.
She spent her summer hiking through the dormant volcanoes of Auvergne.
Many traditional French dishes have their origins in Auvergne.

Etymology

From Latin 'Arverni', the name of an ancient Gallic tribe in the area.
