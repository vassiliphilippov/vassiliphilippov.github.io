tubbiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌb.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtʌb.i.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being plump and rounded in shape, especially in relation to the body
the quality of being plump or rounded

Examples

His tubbiness never stopped him from joining in the soccer games with his friends.
You could see the tubbiness in her cheeks whenever she smiled.
Regular exercise helped reduce the puppy’s tubbiness as he grew.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plumpness - the quality of being plump or full in shape
  • Antonym: leanness - the quality of being thin or having little fat

Etymology

From 'tubby' + '-ness', where 'tubby' derives from 'tub' (a large round container).
