unsurpassed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnsərˈpæst / (AmE), / ˌʌnsəˈpɑːst / (BrE)
not equaled or exceeded; better or greater than any other
not equaled or exceeded

Examples

The pianist gave an unsurpassed performance that left the audience in awe.
Her dedication to her craft is unsurpassed in the company.
The museum houses an unsurpassed collection of ancient artifacts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: matchless - having no equal; incomparable
  • Synonym: peerless - without equal; unrivaled
  • Synonym: unparalleled - having no parallel; exceptional
  • Antonym: inferior - lower in rank, status, or quality

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'surpassed', past participle of 'surpass' (from Old French surpasser).
