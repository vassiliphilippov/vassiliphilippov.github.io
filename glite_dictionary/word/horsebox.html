horsebox

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrs.bɑks / (AmE), / ˈhɔːs.bɒks / (BrE)
a vehicle or trailer for transporting horses, especially racehorses
vehicle or trailer for transporting horses
british

Examples

They loaded the thoroughbred into the horsebox for the long trip to the racetrack.
The new horsebox has enough room for two horses and their equipment.
After the show, the horses were quickly settled back into the horsebox for the journey home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horse trailer - a vehicle for transporting horses (mainly US usage)
