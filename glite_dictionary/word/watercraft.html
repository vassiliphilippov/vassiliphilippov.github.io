watercraft

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚˌkræft / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəkraːft / (BrE)
A vehicle or vessel designed for travel or transportation on water.
vessel or vehicle for water travel

Examples

Only those with experience operating a watercraft should attempt to navigate this river.
The museum features an impressive collection of historic watercraft from around the world.
Renting a watercraft is a popular activity at the lake during summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boat - a small vessel for traveling on water
  • Synonym: ship - a large watercraft used for transporting people or goods by sea

Etymology

Compound of 'water' and 'craft', meaning vehicle for water travel.
