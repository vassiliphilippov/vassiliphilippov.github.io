to coiffe

1. VERB

/ kwɑf / (AmE), / kwɑːf / (BrE)
to arrange or style (hair) in an attractive way
arrange or style hair

Examples

The stylist took great care to coiffe the bride's hair before the ceremony.
She likes to coiffe her hair into elegant updos for special occasions.
He coiffed his hair neatly before heading out to the interview.

Synset Examples

dress my hair for the wedding

Related Terms

  • Synonym: style - to design or arrange (hair) fashionably
  • Synonym: comb - to arrange hair with a comb

Etymology

From French *coiffer* (to cover with a coif, to do the hair).
