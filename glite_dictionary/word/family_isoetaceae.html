family Isoetaceae

1. NOUN

/ ˌfæməli ˌaɪsoʊ.ˈɛtə.siː / (AmE), / ˌfæmɪli ˌaɪsəʊ.ˈɛtə.siː / (BrE)
a family of small, aquatic or semi-aquatic spore-bearing plants known as quillworts, coextensive with the genus Isoetes
a family of aquatic plants (quillworts)

Examples

Members of the family Isoetaceae are found in freshwater habitats worldwide.
The family Isoetaceae consists of small, spore-bearing plants commonly known as quillworts.
Although rare, several species in the family Isoetaceae are considered endangered due to habitat loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Isoetes - genus of quillworts, aquatic or semi-aquatic plants

Etymology

From scientific Latin Isoetaceae, based on the genus name Isoetes.
