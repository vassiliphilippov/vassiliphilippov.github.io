barroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑrˌrum / (AmE), / ˈbɑːˌruːm / (BrE)
A room or establishment where alcoholic drinks are served at a counter.
room for serving alcoholic drinks

Examples

They met in a barroom on the edge of town.
The old barroom was filled with laughter and music every night.
After work, he liked to relax in his favorite barroom.

Synset Examples

he drowned his sorrows in whiskey at the bar

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pub - a place where alcoholic drinks are sold and consumed
  • Synonym: tavern - an establishment for the sale of beer and other drinks to be consumed on the premises

Etymology

Compound of 'bar' (counter for serving drinks) and 'room'.
