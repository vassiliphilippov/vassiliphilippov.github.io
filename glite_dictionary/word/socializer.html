socializer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊ.ʃəˌlaɪ.zər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ʃə.laɪ.zə / (BrE)
a person who enjoys or regularly takes part in social activities
person who participates in social activities

Examples

She is a natural socializer who loves meeting new people at parties.
At every community event, the experienced socializer makes everyone feel welcome.
Although he used to be shy, he has become quite the socializer at work gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extrovert - an outgoing, socially confident person

Etymology

From 'socialize' + '-er' suffix denoting a person who performs an action.
