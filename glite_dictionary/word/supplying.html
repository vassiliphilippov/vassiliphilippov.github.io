supplying

1. NOUN

/ səˈplaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / səˈplaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of providing or furnishing something that is needed or wanted
the act of providing something

Examples

The supplying of clean water is essential to public health.
Several companies are responsible for the supplying of medical equipment to hospitals.
Strict regulations govern the supplying of electricity in that region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: provision - the act of supplying or making something available

Etymology

From the verb 'supply' plus the suffix '-ing'. 'Supply' from Old French 'soupleer', from Latin 'supplēre' (fill up).
