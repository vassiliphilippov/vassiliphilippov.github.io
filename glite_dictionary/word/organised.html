organised

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔr.gə.naɪzd / (AmE), / ˈɔː.ɡə.naɪzd / (BrE)
(of workers, shops, etc.) belonging to or formed into a labor union.
belonging to a labor union

Examples

Many organised workers joined the protest for better wages.
Only organised shops receive representation from the union.
The company faced challenges from both organised and non-unionized staff.

Synset Examples

organized labor
unionized workers
a unionized shop

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unionized - belonging to or formed into a labor union
  • Antonym: non-unionized - not belonging to a labor union
