scraps

1. NOUN

/ skræps / (AmE), / skræps / (BrE)
bits of food that are left over or thrown away, typically as waste from a meal or kitchen.
leftover bits of food

Examples

After dinner, she fed the scraps to the dog under the table.
The restaurant collects food scraps for composting to reduce waste.
He rummaged through the garbage, looking for edible scraps left over from the market.
