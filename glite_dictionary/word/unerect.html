unerect

1. ADJ

/ ʌnɪˈrɛkt / (AmE), / ʌnɪˈrɛkt / (BrE)
not upright in position or posture; not erect.
not upright in position or posture

Examples

The statue was lying unerect after the storm knocked it over.
A snake may appear unerect and coiled, waiting to strike.
After a long day, he sat unerect on the sofa, exhausted.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: erect - upright in position or posture

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'erect', from Latin 'erectus' (upright).
