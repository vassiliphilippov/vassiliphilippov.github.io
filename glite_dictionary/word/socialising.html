socialising

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəˌlaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəlaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of spending time with other people for pleasure and to strengthen relationships
spending time socially with others
british

Examples

Many people find socialising at work events both enjoyable and beneficial for networking.
Online platforms have changed the nature of socialising in the modern world.
She values socialising with friends more than spending time alone.

Synset Examples

there was too much socialization with the enlisted men

Related Terms

  • Synonym: socializing - the activity of spending time with others for pleasure (American spelling)

Etymology

From the verb 'socialise', based on 'social' from Latin 'socialis' (of companionship).
