auriform

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔːrɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈɔːrɪfɔːm / (BrE)
having a shape resembling an ear
shaped like an ear

Examples

The auriform leaves of the plant helped botanists identify its species.
Certain shells are described as auriform due to their ear-like appearance.
The artifact had an auriform design typical of ancient jewelry.

Etymology

From Latin 'auris' (ear) + '-form' (having the form of).
