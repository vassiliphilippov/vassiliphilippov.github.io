clean-shaven

1. ADJ

/ klin ˈʃeɪvən / (AmE), / kliːn ˈʃeɪv(ə)n / (BrE)
having no facial hair because it has been recently shaved off, especially on the face
with no facial hair after shaving

Examples

He always appears clean-shaven at important meetings.
The actor looked much younger after becoming clean-shaven for his new role.
Despite a busy schedule, she prefers her husband clean-shaven every day.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bearded - having a beard; with facial hair
