boxlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑksˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɒksˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the shape or appearance of a box, especially in being rectangular or square.
resembling a box in shape

Examples

The new office building has a boxlike structure, with sharp corners and flat walls.
She wore a boxlike dress that gave her a very angular silhouette.
Many early computers were built in boxlike cases that emphasized function over form.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rectangular - having the shape of a rectangle; at right angles
  • Antonym: irregular - not even or uniform in shape or arrangement
