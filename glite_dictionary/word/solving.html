solving

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑlvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɒlvɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of finding an answer or solution to a problem or difficulty
finding a solution to a problem

Examples

He spent hours solving the most difficult puzzles in the book.
Mathematics is not just about numbers; it’s about solving real-world problems.
The team developed new techniques for solving complex equations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solution - the answer to a problem or puzzle

Etymology

Gerund of 'solve', from Latin 'solvere' (to loosen, solve).
