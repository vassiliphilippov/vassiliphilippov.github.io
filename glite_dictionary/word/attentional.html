attentional

1. ADJ

/ əˈtɛnʃən(ə)l / (AmE), / əˈtɛnʃən(ə)l / (BrE)
relating to or involving the process of attention.
relating to attention

Examples

The attentional demands of the task made it difficult to focus on other things.
Some people have disorders that affect their attentional control.
The study explored how stress can impair attentional processes in the brain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attention - the act or faculty of concentrating or focusing mentally

Etymology

Formed from 'attention' + '-al', the adjective suffix.
