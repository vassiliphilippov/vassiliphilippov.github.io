oncologist

1. NOUN

/ ɑnˈkɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ɒŋˈkɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of cancer
specialist in cancer treatment
medical

Examples

After being diagnosed, she scheduled an appointment with her oncologist right away.
The oncologist explained the different treatment options clearly to the patient and her family.
He works as a pediatric oncologist at the children's hospital.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cancer specialist - a doctor who specializes in treating cancer

Etymology

From oncology (the study of tumors) + -ist, from Greek onkos (mass, tumor) and -logia (study).
