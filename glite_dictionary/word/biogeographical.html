biogeographical

1. ADJ

/ ˌbaɪ.oʊˌdʒi.əˈɡræf.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊ.dʒi.əˈɡræf.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to the branch of science concerned with the geographical distribution of living organisms
relating to biogeography

Examples

The biogeographical patterns of plant species offer insights into ancient climate changes.
Scientists studied the biogeographical differences between island and mainland animal populations.
Her research focuses on the biogeographical history of coral reefs in the Pacific Ocean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biogeographic - relating to biogeography (alternative form)

Etymology

From 'biogeography' (bio- + geography) + -ical.
