shah

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑ / (AmE), / ʃɑː / (BrE)
the title for the former hereditary monarch of Iran
title for former monarch of Iran

Examples

The last shah of Iran was overthrown in 1979 during the Islamic Revolution.
Many Iranian artifacts from the era of the shah are displayed in museums worldwide.
The shah hosted lavish ceremonies at his palace in Tehran.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: king - male monarch of a country
  • Antonym: queen - female monarch; wife of a king

Etymology

From Persian شاه‎ (šâh, meaning 'king').
