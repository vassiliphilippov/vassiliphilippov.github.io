needlelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈniːdəlˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈniːdl̩ˌlaɪk / (BrE)
ending in a sharp, slender point like a needle
ending in a sharp, slender point

Examples

The cactus has needlelike spines that protect it from predators.
Under the microscope, the crystals appeared needlelike and transparent.
Some pine trees have needlelike leaves instead of broad ones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pointed - having a sharp or tapered end
  • Antonym: blunt - not having a sharp edge or point
