songwriter

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔŋˌraɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsɒŋˌraɪtə / (BrE)
a person who writes the words or music for popular songs, especially contemporary or commercial music
person who writes popular songs

Examples

She is an award-winning songwriter who has written hits for many famous singers.
The band hired a professional songwriter to help with their new album.
Many songwriters start out performing their own material in small clubs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: composer - a person who writes music, especially classical music
  • Synonym: lyricist - a person who writes the words for songs

Etymology

Formed from 'song' + 'writer', modeled on similar compounds like 'playwright'.
