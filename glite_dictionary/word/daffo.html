Daffo

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæfoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdafəʊ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in northern Nigeria, especially in Plateau State.
Chadic language of northern Nigeria

Examples

Many people in the region communicate daily in Daffo.
Linguists are working to document the unique features of Daffo.
He grew up bilingual, speaking both English and Daffo at home.
