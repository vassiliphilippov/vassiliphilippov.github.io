pardner

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrdnər / (AmE), / ˈpɑːdnə / (BrE)
(informal) A companion, associate, or partner in an activity, often used in a rustic or cowboy context.
informal variant of partner; companion in activity
informal american

Examples

He greeted the stranger with a cheerful 'Howdy, pardner!' as they met on the trail.
The two detectives were long-time pardners in solving crime.
Don't worry, pardner, we've got this job covered together.

Synset Examples

the musician and the librettist were collaborators
sexual partners

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partner - a person who shares or is associated with another in some action or endeavor

Etymology

Altered pronunciation of 'partner' in American English dialects.
