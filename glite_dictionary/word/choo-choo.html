choo-choo

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃuˌtʃu / (AmE), / ˈtʃuːˌtʃuː / (BrE)
A small child's word for a train or locomotive, often used when speaking to or by young children.
child's word for train

Examples

The little boy waved as the choo-choo sped past his house.
She pointed excitedly at the choo-choo when it arrived at the station.
My toddler loves playing with his wooden choo-choo on the tracks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: train - a series of connected railroad cars pulled by a locomotive
  • Synonym: locomotive - the engine that pulls a train

Etymology

Imitative of the sound of a train's steam whistle.
