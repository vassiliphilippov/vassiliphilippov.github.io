Norge

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɔrɡə / (AmE), / ˈnɔːɡə / (BrE)
The Norwegian name for Norway, a constitutional monarchy in northern Europe on the western side of the Scandinavian Peninsula.
Norwegian name for Norway

Examples

The Olympic team from Norge impressed everyone with their skiing skills.
On the map, she pointed to Norge, explaining its fjords and northern lights.
The official language of Norge is Norwegian, spoken by most of its citizens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Norway - A country in northern Europe; the standard English name for Norge

Etymology

Norwegian form of 'Norway', from Old Norse 'Norðrvegr' (northern way).
