puttyroot

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌtiˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈpʌtiˌruːt / (BrE)
A North American orchid (Aplectrum hyemale) that bears a single leaf and yellowish-brown flowers.
North American orchid with a single leaf

Examples

The forest floor was dotted with patches of puttyroot in early spring.
She carefully documented the habitat where the rare puttyroot was found.
Botanists studied the unique lifecycle of the puttyroot orchid.
