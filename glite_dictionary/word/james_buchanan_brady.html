James Buchanan Brady

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒeɪmz bjuˈkænən ˈbreɪdi / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪmz bjuˈkanɪn ˈbreɪdi / (BrE)
(1856–1917) United States financier renowned for his love of diamonds and lavish lifestyle, popularly known as 'Diamond Jim Brady.'
American financier known as 'Diamond Jim Brady'

Examples

James Buchanan Brady amassed great wealth as a financier and became known for his lavish spending.
Many stories are told about James Buchanan Brady and his extravagant appetite and love of jewelry.
The museum features a collection of diamond-studded items once owned by James Buchanan Brady.
