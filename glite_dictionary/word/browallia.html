browallia

1. NOUN

/ broʊˈwæliə / (AmE), / brəʊˈwalɪə / (BrE)
Any of several herbaceous plants of the genus Browallia, grown for their blue, violet, or white flowers.
genus of flowering plants with blue, violet, or white blooms

Examples

She planted browallia along the edge of the garden for a splash of color.
The delicate blue flowers of browallia are perfect for shady borders.
Gardeners appreciate how easily browallia adapts to containers and hanging baskets.

Etymology

Named after Johan Browall, a Swedish botanist (1707–1755).
