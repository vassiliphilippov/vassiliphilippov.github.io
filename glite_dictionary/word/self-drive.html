self-drive

1. NOUN

/ sɛlfˈdraɪv / (AmE), / sɛlfˈdraɪv / (BrE)
a car that is rented for use and driven by the renter rather than a chauffeur; a hire car
car rented to be driven by the renter
british

Examples

We booked a self-drive for our road trip around Scotland.
At the rental agency, you can choose between a self-drive and a chauffeur-driven car.
After landing in London, he picked up his self-drive from the airport rental desk.

Synset Examples

she picked up a hire car at the airport and drove to her hotel

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hire car - car that is rented to the user, usually for a short period
  • Antonym: chauffeur-driven - car driven by a professional driver for the user

Etymology

From 'self' + 'drive', indicating the renter drives the car personally.
