samarskite

1. NOUN

/ səˈmɑːrskaɪt / (AmE), / səˈmɑːskaɪt / (BrE)
a complex black mineral, chiefly an oxide of iron, uranium, rare earths, and other elements, found in pegmatites
a complex black mineral found in pegmatites

Examples

The geologist discovered a rare sample of samarskite in the granite pegmatite.
Samarskite is an important source of several rare earth elements.
Collectors value samarskite for its distinctive black coloration and complex composition.

Etymology

Named after Russian mining engineer Colonel V. E. Samarsky-Bykhovets.
