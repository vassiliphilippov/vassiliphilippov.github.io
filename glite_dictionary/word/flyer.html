flyer

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪə / (BrE)
a person who operates or travels in an aircraft, especially as a pilot
person who operates an aircraft

Examples

My grandfather was a skilled flyer during World War II.
The flyer carefully guided the plane through the turbulent weather.
Many young people dream of becoming a flyer in the air force.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pilot - a person who operates the controls of an aircraft

2. NOUN

/ ˈflaɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who travels by airplane
airplane passenger

Examples

Frequent flyers can earn miles towards free flights with many airlines.
The airline offers special discounts to loyal flyers.
As a nervous flyer, she always books an aisle seat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passenger - a person who travels in a vehicle

3. NOUN

/ ˈflaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪə / (BrE)
a small printed sheet containing information or advertising, intended for wide distribution
printed advertisement for wide distribution

Examples

The company distributed flyers to promote its new store opening downtown.
She picked up a flyer about the art exhibition while walking through the park.
There were colorful flyers advertising local events posted on the bulletin board.
