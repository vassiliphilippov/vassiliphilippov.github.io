burr-headed

1. ADJ

/ bɜr ˈhɛdɪd / (AmE), / bɜː ˈhɛdɪd / (BrE)
Having a head of hair that is cut very short and appears bristly.
having very short, bristly hair

Examples

His burr-headed appearance made him stand out in the crowd of long-haired students.
The new recruit was burr-headed after the standard military haircut.
She ran her hand over his burr-headed scalp, surprised by how rough it felt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: close-cropped - having hair cut very short

Etymology

From 'burr', referring to the prickly seed or object, used metaphorically for bristly hair, combined with 'headed'.
