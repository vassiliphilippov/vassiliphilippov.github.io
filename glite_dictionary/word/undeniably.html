undeniably

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈdaɪ.ni.əbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈdiː.ni.əbli / (BrE)
In a way that cannot be denied; unquestionably or clearly true.
in a way that cannot be denied

Examples

Her talent is undeniably impressive, and she stands out in every performance.
This is undeniably one of the best restaurants in the city.
He was undeniably the leader during the crisis, guiding everyone through with calm authority.

Synset Examples

she is undeniably the most gifted student in the class

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certainly - without doubt; definitely
  • Antonym: arguably - in a way that can be supported by argument

Etymology

From 'undeniable' + '-ly'.
