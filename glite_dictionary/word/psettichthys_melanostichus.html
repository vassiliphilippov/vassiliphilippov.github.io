Psettichthys melanostichus

1. NOUN

/ psɛˈtɪkθɪs ˌmɛləˈnɒstɪkəs / (AmE), / psɛˈtɪkθɪs ˌmɛlənˈstɪkəs / (BrE)
A species of common flatfish, known as the sand sole, found along the Pacific coast of North America.
a Pacific coast flatfish (sand sole)

Examples

Fishermen along the Pacific coast often catch Psettichthys melanostichus during the summer months.
The diet of Psettichthys melanostichus primarily consists of small crustaceans and worms.
Biologists identified the specimen as Psettichthys melanostichus after examining its distinctive coloration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sand sole - common name for Psettichthys melanostichus, a flatfish

Etymology

From Greek 'psētta' (flatfish) + 'ichthys' (fish) and 'melas' (black) + 'stichos' (line), referring to the species' appearance.
