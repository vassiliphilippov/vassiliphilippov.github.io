self-respect

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlf rɪˈspɛkt / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf rɪˈspɛkt / (BrE)
pride and confidence in oneself; a feeling that one is behaving with honor and dignity
pride and confidence in oneself

Examples

She regained her self-respect after standing up for herself at work.
Many believe that self-respect is essential for happiness and healthy relationships.
He refused the bribe to preserve his self-respect.

Synset Examples

it was beneath his dignity to cheat
showed his true dignity when under pressure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dignity - the state or quality of being worthy of honor or respect
  • Synonym: self-esteem - confidence in one's own worth or abilities

Etymology

Formed from 'self' (referring to one's own person) and 'respect' (from Latin respectus, regard).
