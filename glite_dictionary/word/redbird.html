redbird

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdˌbɝd / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌbɜːd / (BrE)
A North American songbird with a thick bill and, in males, bright red plumage; especially the northern cardinal.
bright red North American songbird

Examples

We spotted a redbird perched on the fence this morning.
The male redbird is easily recognized by its bright red feathers.
A redbird sang sweetly outside our window all afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cardinal - A North American songbird with vivid red plumage in the male

2. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdˌbɜrd / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌbɜːd / (BrE)
A North American songbird, the male of which is bright red with black wings and tail, especially the northern cardinal.
bright red North American songbird

Examples

A redbird perched on the fence, its vivid feathers glowing in the morning sun.
During our hike, we spotted a beautiful redbird singing in the maple tree.
Many people hang bird feeders in hopes of attracting the striking redbird to their gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cardinal - A North American songbird with a bright red male plumage
