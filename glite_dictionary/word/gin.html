gin

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪn / (AmE), / dʒɪn / (BrE)
A form of rummy in which a player can end the game if the cards remaining in their hand total less than 10 points.
a card game, a form of rummy

Examples

They played a few hands of gin after dinner to relax.
When you get good at gin, you start to notice the subtle strategies involved.
My grandmother used to win every round of gin with remarkable skill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gin rummy - A popular two-player card game; the full name of 'gin' as a card game

Etymology

Shortened from 'gin rummy', first recorded in the early 20th century.

6. NOUN

/ dʒɪn / (AmE), / dʒɪn / (BrE)

Examples

Related Terms
