anguine

1. ADJ

/ ˈæŋɡwɪn / (AmE), / ˈaŋɡwɪn / (BrE)
Relating to, resembling, or characteristic of a snake.
snake-like; relating to snakes

Examples

The creature moved in an anguine manner through the grass, silent and sinuous.
His anguine features gave him an unsettling, almost reptilian appearance.
The artist's depiction of the goddess included an anguine crown winding around her head.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: serpentine - resembling a serpent (snake); winding like a snake

Etymology

From Latin 'anguinus', from 'anguis' meaning 'snake'.
