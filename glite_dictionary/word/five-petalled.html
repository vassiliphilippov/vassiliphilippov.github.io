five-petalled

1. ADJ

/ faɪvˈpɛtəld / (AmE), / faɪvˈpɛtəld / (BrE)
(of flowers) having five petals.
having five petals (flowers)

Examples

The wild rose has five-petalled blooms that appear in early summer.
Many buttercup species display five-petalled yellow flowers in open meadows.
The five-petalled symmetry of the flower makes it easy to identify.
