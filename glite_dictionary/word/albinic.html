albinic

1. ADJ

/ ælˈbɪnɪk / (AmE), / ælˈbɪnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by albinism; having characteristics of albinism
relating to albinism
medical

Examples

The albinic rabbit stood out among its darker siblings in the enclosure.
Physicians must be aware of potential vision issues in albinic patients.
Researchers studied the albinic traits in several species of amphibians.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: albinism - the condition of being albino; absence of pigment in skin, hair, and eyes
  • Synonym: albino - person or animal with albinism

Etymology

From Latin 'albus' (white) with the suffix '-inic' indicating relation to.
