mitrewort

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪtərˌwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈmɪtrəwɜːt / (BrE)
Any of various perennial herbs of the genus Mitella, with a capsule resembling a bishop's miter.
perennial herb of the genus Mitella

Examples

The shady woodland was carpeted with delicate mitrewort in bloom.
Botanists identified the rare mitrewort growing near the stream.
She pressed a mitrewort flower into her field journal for later study.

Etymology

From 'miter', referring to the bishop's headdress, and 'wort', meaning plant.
