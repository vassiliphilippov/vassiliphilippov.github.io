Uzi

1. NOUN

/ ˈuːzi / (AmE), / ˈuːzi / (BrE)
a type of compact submachine gun designed and manufactured in Israel, widely used by military, police, and security forces around the world
Israeli submachine gun

Examples

The police confiscated an Uzi from the suspect during the raid.
He collects military memorabilia and even owns a deactivated Uzi.
The Uzi became famous for its compact size and rapid rate of fire.

Synset Examples

the Uzi is used throughout the world as a police and special forces firearm

Etymology

From the name of its designer, Uziel Gal, an Israeli army officer.
