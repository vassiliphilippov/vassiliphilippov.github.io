Jylland

1. PROPN

/ ˈjʏlˌlɑnd / (AmE), / ˈjʏl.ˌlænd / (BrE)
the peninsula in northern Europe that forms the mainland part of Denmark and extends into northern Germany, known in English as Jutland
peninsula in Denmark and northern Germany

Examples

The city of Aarhus is located on Jylland, Denmark's mainland peninsula.
Many tourists travel to Jylland to experience its beautiful coastlines and historic towns.
After crossing the bridge from Funen, we drove through the scenic landscapes of Jylland.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jutland - the English name for the Jylland peninsula in northern Europe

Etymology

Danish name for Jutland; from Old Norse Jótland (land of the Jutes).
