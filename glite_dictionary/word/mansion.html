mansion

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænʃən / (AmE), / ˈmænʃən / (BrE)
(Astrology) One of the twelve equal divisions of the zodiac.
zodiac division in astrology

Examples

In traditional astrology, each zodiac sign corresponds to a mansion of the heavens.
The astrologer divided the chart into twelve mansions before beginning the reading.
Each mansion of the zodiac is said to influence a different aspect of one's personality.

2. NOUN

/ ˈmænʃən / (AmE), / ˈmænʃən / (BrE)
A large, impressive, and stately house, often luxurious.
large, impressive house

Examples

They purchased a mansion overlooking the lake as their new holiday home.
The old mansion at the end of the street is rumored to be haunted.
He dreamed of living in a luxurious mansion with sprawling gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: villa - a large and luxurious country house
  • Degree Higher: palace - a large and grand residence, especially for royalty
  • Degree Lower: cottage - a small, simple house, typically in the countryside

Etymology

From Old French 'maison' (house), from Latin 'mansio' (dwelling, abode).
