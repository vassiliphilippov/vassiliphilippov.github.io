Chalcis

1. NOUN

/ ˈkælsɪs / (AmE), / ˈkælsɪs / (BrE)
A genus of chalcid wasps, serving as the type genus of the family Chalcididae.
type genus of Chalcididae (wasps)

Examples

In their latest study, researchers discovered a new species within the genus Chalcis.
The family Chalcididae is named after its type genus, Chalcis.
Specimens of Chalcis have been collected from tropical and subtropical regions.

Etymology

From Greek Chalkís, an ancient city in Euboea, with genus names often borrowing classical place names.
