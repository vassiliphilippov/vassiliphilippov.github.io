Aigina

1. PROPN

/ aɪˈdʒiːnə / (AmE), / aɪˈdʒiːnə / (BrE)
An island in the Saronic Gulf of the Aegean Sea, belonging to Greece.
Greek island in the Saronic Gulf

Examples

She spent a week relaxing on Aigina during her holiday in Greece.
The ferry to Aigina offers stunning views of the Aegean Sea.
Ancient ruins on Aigina attract historians from around the world.

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Αἴγινα (Aígina), the name of the island.
