pantie

1. NOUN

/ ˈpænti / (AmE), / ˈpænti / (BrE)
A pair of short underpants for women or children.
short women's or children's underpants

Examples

She bought a new set of lace panties for her vacation.
The store sells panties in many different colors and styles.
Her daughter outgrew her old panties and needed bigger ones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: panties - short underpants for women or children (usual plural)
  • Synonym: briefs - short, close-fitting underpants

Etymology

From 'pant,' short for 'pantaloons,' with the diminutive suffix '-ie.'
