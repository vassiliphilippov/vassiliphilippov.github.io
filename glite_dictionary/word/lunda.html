Lunda

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌn.də / (AmE), / ˈlʌn.də / (BrE)
A genus of seabirds in the auk family, comprising puffins.
genus of puffins

Examples

The horned puffin is classified within the genus Lunda.
Ornithologists study the distribution of birds belonging to Lunda in the North Pacific.
The genus Lunda has distinctive features separating it from other auks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puffin - a stout seabird with a large colorful beak, found in northern coastal regions
