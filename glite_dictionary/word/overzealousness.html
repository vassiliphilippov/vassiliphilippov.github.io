overzealousness

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊvərˈzɛləsnəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈzɛləsnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being excessively eager or enthusiastic, often to an unreasonable or inappropriate degree.
excessive eagerness or enthusiasm

Examples

The manager's overzealousness sometimes leads to unnecessary stress among employees.
His overzealousness in enforcing the rules made him unpopular with his peers.
Critics accused the new policy of encouraging overzealousness among regulators.

Synset Examples

We are aware of the public concern for any sort of overzealousness by the law-enforcement people that can cause some embarrassment.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: zealousness - eager and enthusiastic devotion
  • Synonym: overzealous - excessively eager or enthusiastic; adjective form

Etymology

Formed from 'overzealous' (over- + zealous) + suffix -ness.
