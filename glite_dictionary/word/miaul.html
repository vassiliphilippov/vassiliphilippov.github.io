miaul

1. NOUN

/ miˈɑl / (AmE), / miˈaʊl / (BrE)
A cry or sound made by a cat, or any sound resembling this.
cry or sound of a cat

Examples

The kitten's constant miaul woke me up early this morning.
She heard the miaul of a stray cat echoing in the alleyway.
A plaintive miaul at the door signaled the cat wanted to come inside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meow - the characteristic sound made by a cat

Etymology

From French 'miauler' (to meow), imitative in origin.
