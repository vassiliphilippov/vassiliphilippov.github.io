featherbrained

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɛðɚˌbreɪnd / (AmE), / ˈfɛðəˌbreɪnd / (BrE)
lacking seriousness or good judgment; silly or frivolous.
frivolous or silly

Examples

Despite her featherbrained remarks, she actually had a sharp sense of humor.
His featherbrained plan to start a business with no money didn't surprise anyone.
They dismissed the suggestion as just another featherbrained idea.

Synset Examples

a dizzy blonde
light-headed teenagers
silly giggles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scatterbrained - disorganized and forgetful; lacking concentration or seriousness
  • Antonym: serious - acting or speaking thoughtfully or with deep consideration

Etymology

Formed by analogy to 'scatterbrained', combining 'feather' (something light) and 'brained', suggesting a mind as light as a feather.
