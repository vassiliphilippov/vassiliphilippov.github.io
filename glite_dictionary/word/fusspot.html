fusspot

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌsˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈfʌs.pɒt / (BrE)
a person who is excessively fussy or anxious about small details or potential problems
person who fusses over details
informal

Examples

My grandmother is a real fusspot and can never relax when guests are over.
Don’t be such a fusspot—everything will turn out fine!
The teacher was known as a fusspot about neat handwriting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worrywart - a person who constantly worries
  • Antonym: stoic - a person unaffected by emotion or worry

Etymology

Formed from 'fuss' and 'pot', with 'pot' as a colloquial suffix denoting a person.
