silverworker

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvərˌwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs objects from silver
a person who works with silver articles

Examples

The silverworker carefully repaired the antique tea set.
As a skilled silverworker, she created intricate jewelry by hand.
Many museums display works by renowned silverworkers from the 18th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silversmith - a person who makes objects from silver
