bloodcurdling

1. ADJ

/ ˈblʌdˌkɝːdlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblʌdˌkɜːdlɪŋ / (BrE)
causing extreme fear or horror; extremely alarming or terrifying
extremely alarming or terrifying

Examples

The movie was filled with bloodcurdling screams that sent chills down my spine.
We heard a bloodcurdling cry echoing through the dark woods.
Her bloodcurdling tale of survival left everyone silent and wide-eyed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrifying - causing intense fear
  • Synonym: spine-chilling - causing a sensation of fear or suspense
  • Antonym: reassuring - making someone feel less afraid

Etymology

From 'blood' + 'curdle' (to clot or congeal), suggesting something so terrifying that it makes one's blood curdle.
