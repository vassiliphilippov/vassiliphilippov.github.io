Ethel Barrymore

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛθəl ˈbæriˌmɔr / (AmE), / ˈɛθəl ˈbærɪˌmɔː / (BrE)
(1879–1959) An American actress, prominent in stage and film, known for her distinguished career and as a member of the Barrymore acting family.
American actress (1879–1959)

Examples

Ethel Barrymore received an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress in 1944.
The theater was named in honor of Ethel Barrymore's contributions to the performing arts.
Many consider Ethel Barrymore one of the greatest stage actresses of her time.
