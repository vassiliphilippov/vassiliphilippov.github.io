parked

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɑrkt / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkt / (BrE)
(of a vehicle) left standing in a particular place, usually temporarily, and not in motion.
vehicle left standing

Examples

There was a long row of parked cars outside the stadium.
The officer checked each parked vehicle for a valid permit.
I found my bike still parked where I had left it this morning.

Synset Examples

there were four parked cars across the street

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stationary - not moving; not intended to be moved
  • Antonym: moving - in motion; not fixed in one place

Etymology

From the verb 'park' + -ed.
