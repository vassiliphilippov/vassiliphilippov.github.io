avellane

1. ADJ

/ əˈvɛleɪn / (AmE), / əˈvɛleɪn / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling filberts or hazelnuts, especially in botany or heraldry.
pertaining to filberts/hazelnuts

Examples

The coat of arms features an avellane cross as its central motif.
The unique, avellane flavor of the spread comes from roasted hazelnuts.
These woods are known for their avellane shrubs, which bear edible nuts.

Etymology

From Latin 'avellana', meaning 'hazelnut', from 'Avella', a town in Italy noted for hazelnuts.
