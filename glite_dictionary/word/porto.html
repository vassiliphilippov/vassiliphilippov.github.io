Porto

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɔrtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtəʊ / (BrE)
a port city in northwest Portugal, famous for its port wine and historic architecture
city in northwest Portugal, famous for port wine

Examples

Porto is renowned for its stunning riverside district and historic bridges.
She visited Porto to tour the famous port wine cellars along the Douro River.
Many tourists are drawn to Porto for its blend of modern culture and medieval history.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Porto', meaning 'port' or 'harbour'.
