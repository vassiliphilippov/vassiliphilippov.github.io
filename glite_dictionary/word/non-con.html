non-con

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɑn.kɑn / (AmE), / ˈnɒn.kɒn / (BrE)
not consensual; lacking the agreement or permission of all parties involved, typically used in discussions of sexual relations or consent.
not consensual, lacking consent
informal

Examples

The story raises difficult questions about non-con scenarios and the importance of clear boundaries.
Her personal essay discussed the emotional impact of being in a non-con situation.
Many forums strictly prohibit any discussion or depiction of non-con topics.
