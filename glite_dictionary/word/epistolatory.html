epistolatory

1. ADJ

/ ɪˌpɪstəˈleɪtəri / (AmE), / ɪˌpɪstələˈtɔːri / (BrE)
written in the form of letters or conducted by correspondence; pertaining to written letters.
written in letter form

Examples

The author employed an epistolatory style to create intimacy between characters.
Her epistolatory correspondence with friends spanned several decades.
He found the epistolatory novel to be more engaging than traditional narrative forms.

Synset Examples

an endless sequence of epistolary love affairs
the epistolatory novel

Related Terms

  • Synonym: epistolary - written in the form of letters

Etymology

From Latin epistola (letter), after the pattern of 'epistolary'.
