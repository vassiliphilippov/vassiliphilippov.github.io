seaplane

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiˌpleɪn / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌpleɪn / (BrE)
an aircraft designed to take off from and land on water
aircraft that can land on water

Examples

The resort offers scenic flights in a seaplane that takes off right from the bay.
A seaplane landed gracefully on the lake, attracting the attention of tourists.
He pilots a seaplane for a company that delivers goods to remote islands.

Synset Examples

the designer of marine aircraft demonstrated his newest hydroplane

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hydroplane - an aircraft that can land on water (synonym for seaplane)

Etymology

From 'sea' + 'plane', indicating a plane for use on water.
