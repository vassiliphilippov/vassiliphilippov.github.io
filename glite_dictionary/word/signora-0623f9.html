signora

1. NOUN

/ siˈnjɔːrə / (AmE), / siˈnjɔːrə / (BrE)
an Italian courtesy title or form of address for a married woman, equivalent to 'Mrs.'
Italian title for a married woman

Examples

The chef greeted the signora warmly as she entered the restaurant with her husband.
We arranged the meeting with Signora Rossi for Monday afternoon.
As a respected signora, she was invited to many social events in her town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: senora - Spanish title for a married woman

Etymology

Italian, feminine of signore (lord, master).
