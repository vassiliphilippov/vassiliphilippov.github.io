gallantly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡæləntli / (AmE), / ˈɡaləntli / (BrE)
in a brave, spirited, or chivalrous manner; showing courage or courteous attention, especially towards women
in a brave or chivalrous manner

Examples

The knight gallantly rode into battle to defend his people.
He gallantly opened the door and let his friend enter first.
Despite the overwhelming odds, she gallantly continued her work without complaint.

Synset Examples

he gallantly offered to take her home

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bravely - in a brave manner
  • Antonym: cowardly - in a cowardly manner; lacking courage

Etymology

From 'gallant' + '-ly'; 'gallant' from Middle French 'galant' (courteous, brave).
