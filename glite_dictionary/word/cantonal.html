cantonal

1. ADJ

/ ˈkæn.tən.əl / (AmE), / ˈkæn.tən.əl / (BrE)
relating to a canton, especially one of the districts or administrative regions into which some countries are divided, such as Switzerland.
relating to a canton or administrative district

Examples

The Swiss government grants significant autonomy to each cantonal authority.
He is studying the differences between cantonal laws in Switzerland.
Every cantonal flag was displayed at the national celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: canton - a territorial division or administrative district, especially in Switzerland

Etymology

From French 'cantonal', from 'canton' (corner, district).
