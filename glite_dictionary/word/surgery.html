surgery

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːdʒəri / (AmE), / ˈsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
(British) A room where a doctor or dentist meets and consults with patients.
doctor's or dentist's consulting room (Brit.)
british

Examples

The notice was clearly visible in the doctor's surgery.
Patients are asked to wait quietly in the surgery before their appointment.
The dentist's surgery closes at six o'clock every evening.

Synset Examples

he read the warning in the doctor's surgery

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clinic - a place or group meeting where medical advice is given and treatment administered

Etymology

From Old French surgerie, from Latin chirurgia ‘hand work’, from Greek kheirourgia, from kheir ‘hand’ + ergon ‘work’.
