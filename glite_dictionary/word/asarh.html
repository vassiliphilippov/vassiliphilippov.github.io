Asarh

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɑr / (AmE), / əˈsɑː / (BrE)
the fourth month of the Hindu calendar, typically occurring in June–July in the Gregorian calendar
fourth month of the Hindu calendar

Examples

Many festivals are celebrated in Asarh, marking the beginning of the rainy season.
Rice planting usually starts in the month of Asarh according to the Hindu calendar.
The monsoon often arrives in India during Asarh.
