Tacca

1. NOUN

/ ˈtækə / (AmE), / ˈtakə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical plants with creeping rootstocks and small umbellate flowers, commonly found in Southeast Asia and Africa.
genus of tropical plants

Examples

The black bat flower is a striking species in the genus Tacca.
Botanists discovered a new Tacca thriving in the rainforest undergrowth.
Some gardeners grow Tacca for its unusual and decorative blooms.

Etymology

From the Malayan word 'taka', a native name for one of the species.
