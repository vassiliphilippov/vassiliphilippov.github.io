unparalleled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpɛrəˌlɛld / (AmE), / ʌnˈparəlɛld / (BrE)
having no equal; exceptional or unique in quality or extent
having no equal; unique

Examples

Her dedication to the project was unparalleled and inspired the entire team.
The museum boasts an unparalleled collection of Renaissance art.
His achievements in the field are truly unparalleled in recent history.

Synset Examples

he is alone in the field of microbiology
this theory is altogether alone in its penetration of the problem
Bach was unique in his handling of counterpoint
craftsmen whose skill is unequaled
unparalleled athletic ability
a breakdown of law unparalleled in our history

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unequaled - having no equal; incomparable
  • Synonym: matchless - unable to be equalled or rivalled
  • Synonym: peerless - without equal; unrivalled
  • Antonym: ordinary - normal; not exceptional or distinguished

Etymology

From un- (not) + parallel, ultimately from Greek 'parallēlos' (beside one another).
