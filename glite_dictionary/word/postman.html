postman

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstmæn / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstmən / (BrE)
a man whose job is to deliver letters and parcels to people's homes or businesses
male mail carrier

Examples

The postman delivered a package to my house this morning.
We always greet the postman when he comes down our street.
Our local postman knows everyone by name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mailman - a person who delivers mail, especially in American English
  • Antonym: postwoman - a woman who delivers the mail
  • Synonym: letter carrier - a person who delivers letters and parcels

Etymology

Formed from 'post' (mail system) + 'man', denoting a male mail carrier.
