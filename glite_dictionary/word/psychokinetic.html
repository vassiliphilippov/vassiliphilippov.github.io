psychokinetic

1. ADJ

/ saɪˌkoʊkɪˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / saɪˌkəʊkɪˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
Able to move objects by mental power or psychic force, without apparent physical means.
moving objects by mental power

Examples

Some people believe that psychokinetic abilities can be developed through meditation and practice.
The film's main character has psychokinetic powers that allow her to bend spoons with her mind.
Scientists remain skeptical about the existence of psychokinetic phenomena due to lack of empirical evidence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telekinetic - Able to move objects with the mind; related to telekinesis

Etymology

From psycho- (mind) + kinetic (movement), both ultimately from Greek.
