dustmop

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌstˌmɑp / (AmE), / ˈdʌstˌmɒp / (BrE)
A mop with a dry head, used for removing dust from floors.
dry mop for dusting floors

Examples

She used a dustmop to clean the hardwood floors before the guests arrived.
With a quick swipe of the dustmop, the hallway was free of dirt and lint.
Every week, he ran the dustmop under the furniture to collect hidden dust.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mop - A tool for cleaning floors, typically having a bundle of absorbent material attached to a long handle.
