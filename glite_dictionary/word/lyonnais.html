Lyonnais

1. NOUN

/ li.oʊˈneɪ / (AmE), / li.ɒˈneɪ / (BrE)
a former province in east central France, now part of the Rhône-Alpes administrative region.
former province in east central France

Examples

The Lyonnais region is famous for its cuisine and rich cultural heritage.
He spent a summer exploring the towns and villages of the Lyonnais.
Historically, the Lyonnais was an important center of trade and industry.

Etymology

From French 'Lyonnais', relating to Lyon.
