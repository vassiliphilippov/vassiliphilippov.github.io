nudity

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːdəti / (AmE), / ˈnjuːdəti / (BrE)
the state of being without clothing; the condition of being naked
state of being naked

Examples

The film contained scenes with brief nudity, so it was rated for adults only.
Some people regard the human body's nudity as natural, while others find it embarrassing.
The artist was inspired by the beauty of nudity in classical sculpture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nakedness - the state of being naked or unclothed
  • Antonym: clothed - having clothes on; covered by clothing

Etymology

From Latin nuditas, from nudus (naked).
