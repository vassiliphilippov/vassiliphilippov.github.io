unbent

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbɛnt / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɛnt / (BrE)
standing or sitting with an erect or straight posture; not bent or curved
erect or straight in posture

Examples

Despite his age, he still walked unbent through the park every morning.
She sat unbent at her desk, her posture perfectly straight.
The athlete remained unbent after the fall, showing determination and resilience.

Synset Examples

sit straight
stood defiantly with unbowed back

Related Terms

  • Synonym: upright - standing or sitting with a straight back and shoulders

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + bent (past participle of bend).

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbɛnt / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɛnt / (BrE)
not bent; remaining in or restored to a straight form or position
not bent; straight

Examples

After carefully straightening the wire, it was completely unbent.
Please use an unbent paperclip to press the reset button on the device.
The sign must be hung with an unbent hook to prevent it from falling.

Synset Examples

looking for an unbent nail
trees with straight unbent trunks make the best lumber

Related Terms

  • Synonym: straight - having no bends or curves
  • Antonym: bent - shaped or forced out of a straight line

Etymology

from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'bent' (past participle of 'bend')
