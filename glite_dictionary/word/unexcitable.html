unexcitable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnɪkˈsaɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnɪkˈsaɪtəbəl / (BrE)
not easily excited or agitated; calm and difficult to rouse emotionally.
not easily excited; calm

Examples

Despite the chaos around her, Maria remained unexcitable and handled the situation calmly.
He has an unexcitable temperament, which makes him perfect for this high-pressure job.
The dog's unexcitable nature made it an excellent companion for young children.

Synset Examples

an unexcitable temperament

Related Terms

  • Antonym: excitable - easily excited or agitated
  • Synonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness, anger, or other strong emotions

Etymology

From un- (not) + excitable (able to be excited).
