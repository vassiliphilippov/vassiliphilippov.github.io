relationship

1. NOUN

/ ɹɪˈleɪʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪʃənʃɪp / (BrE)
the way in which two or more people, groups, or countries are connected and interact with each other
connection or dealings between people or groups

Examples

The two countries have established a strong relationship based on trade and cultural exchange.
Her relationship with her colleagues is built on trust and respect.
Negotiations improved the relationship between the rival political parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: connection - a relationship in which a person, thing, or idea is linked or associated with something else
  • Synonym: association - a connection or cooperative link between people or organizations
  • Antonym: conflict - a serious disagreement or argument, typically a protracted one, which can occur in relationships

Etymology

From relation + -ship, where 'relation' is from Latin 'relatio' (a bringing back, reference).

2. NOUN

/ ɹəˈleɪʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ɹɪˈleɪʃənˌʃɪp / (BrE)
the way in which two or more people are connected by blood, marriage, or adoption; kinship
connection by blood, marriage, or adoption

Examples

He discovered through research that he had a relationship to the famous explorer by blood.
Adoption creates a legal relationship between a child and the adopting parents.
Their relationship as distant cousins fascinated the entire family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kinship - family relationship, blood relationship

Etymology

From relation + -ship; relation from Latin relatio (a bringing back, relation).

4. NOUN

/ ɹəˈleɪʃ(ə)nˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ɹɪˈleɪʃ(ə)nˌʃɪp / (BrE)
the way in which two or more people or groups are connected or involved with each other
connection or involvement between people

Examples

She maintained a close relationship with her grandparents throughout her life.
Their professional relationship developed into a lasting friendship over the years.
After the argument, there was tension in their relationship for several weeks.

Synset Examples

the relationship between mothers and their children

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relation - a connection between people or things
  • Synonym: connection - a relationship in which a person or thing is linked with something else
  • Antonym: separation - the state of being apart or disconnected

Etymology

From relation + -ship, modelled after fellowship, sponsorship, etc.; 'relation' from Latin relatio (a bringing back).
