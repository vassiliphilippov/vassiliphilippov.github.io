gatehouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪtˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪthaʊs / (BrE)
a small building at the entrance to a large property, estate, or castle, typically housing the gatekeeper or providing security.
building at a property's entrance, often for gatekeeper

Examples

The old castle's gatehouse is now used as a visitor information center.
The security guard waved us through the gatehouse at the entrance to the estate.
Restoration work on the medieval gatehouse revealed several hidden chambers.
