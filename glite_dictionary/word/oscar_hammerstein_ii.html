Oscar Hammerstein II

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑskɚ ˈhæmɚstaɪn ðə ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈɒskə ˈhæmɜːstaɪn ðə ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
(1895–1960) An American lyricist and librettist renowned for his collaborations with Richard Rodgers on celebrated musical comedies.
famous American lyricist and librettist

Examples

Oscar Hammerstein II wrote the lyrics for many classic musicals with composer Richard Rodgers.
Many people regard Oscar Hammerstein II as one of the most influential figures in American musical theatre history.
The partnership of Rodgers and Oscar Hammerstein II produced enduring hits such as 'The Sound of Music' and 'Oklahoma!'.
