Papilionoideae

1. NOUN

/ ˌpæpɪˌlɪəˈnɔɪdiaɪ / (AmE), / ˌpapɪlɪˌəʊˈnɪːdɪiː / (BrE)
a subfamily of flowering plants in the legume family Fabaceae, often referred to in older classifications as Papilionaceae or the papilionoid legumes
a subfamily of flowering plants in Fabaceae

Examples

Many important crops such as beans and peas belong to the Papilionoideae subfamily.
Botanists have reclassified Papilionoideae as a subfamily within Fabaceae.
The unique flower structure is a distinguishing feature of Papilionoideae.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Fabaceae - the legume or pea family of flowering plants
  • Synonym: legume - a plant in the pea family, Fabaceae

Etymology

From Latin 'papilion-', meaning 'butterfly', referring to the butterflies' resemblance of the flowers in this group.
