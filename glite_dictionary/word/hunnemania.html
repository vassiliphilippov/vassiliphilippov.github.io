Hunnemania

1. NOUN

/ ˌhʌnəˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ˌhʌnɪˈmeɪnɪə / (BrE)
A small genus of flowering plants in the poppy family, known for its species Hunnemania fumariifolia (golden cup), native to Mexico.
genus of Mexican flowering plants

Examples

The garden featured several varieties of Hunnemania in bloom during the summer months.
Botanists are particularly interested in Hunnemania due to its unique yellow flowers.
Hunnemania thrives in dry, rocky soils and adds vibrant color to desert landscapes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poppy - herbaceous plants of the family Papaveraceae

Etymology

Named after the German botanist Hermann Hünnemann (1842–1905).
