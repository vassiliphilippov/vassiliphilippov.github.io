accordionist

1. NOUN

/ əˈkɔːr.di.ə.nɪst / (AmE), / əˈkɔː.dɪ.ə.nɪst / (BrE)
a musician who plays the accordion.
someone who plays the accordion

Examples

The skilled accordionist played lively tunes at the street festival.
She has been an accordionist in a folk band for over a decade.
The audience applauded the young accordionist after his impressive solo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musician - a person who plays a musical instrument

Etymology

Formed from 'accordion' + '-ist', denoting a person who plays the instrument.
