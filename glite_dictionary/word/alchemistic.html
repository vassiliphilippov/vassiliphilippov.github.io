alchemistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæl.kəˈmɪs.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌæl.kɪˈmɪs.tɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of alchemists or alchemy, especially in reference to the medieval practice of attempting to transform matter or find a universal elixir.
relating to alchemists or alchemy

Examples

The ancient manuscripts were filled with alchemistic symbols and mysterious codes.
She developed an alchemistic fascination with transforming base materials into art.
Historians study the alchemistic traditions that shaped early modern science.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alchemical - relating to alchemy or alchemists

Etymology

From Medieval Latin alchimisticus, from alchimia (alchemy).
