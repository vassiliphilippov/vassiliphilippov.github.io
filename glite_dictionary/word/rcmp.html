RCMP

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑr.siː.emˈpiː / (AmE), / ˌɑː.siː.emˈpiː / (BrE)
the Royal Canadian Mounted Police, the federal and national police force of Canada
Canadian federal police force

Examples

The RCMP launched an investigation into the case last month.
Many Canadians trust the RCMP to handle national security matters.
The RCMP officers wore their distinctive red serge uniforms at the ceremony.

Etymology

Acronym for Royal Canadian Mounted Police.
