banderillero

1. NOUN

/ ˌbændəriˈjɛroʊ / (AmE), / ˌbændərɪˈleərəʊ / (BrE)
a bullfighter whose role is to stick decorated darts (banderillas) into the neck or shoulders of the bull during a bullfight
bullfighter who plants darts in a bullfight

Examples

The banderillero swiftly planted the colorful darts as the crowd roared.
Each banderillero relies on speed and precision to avoid the bull's charge.
He trained for years before earning the title of banderillero in the arena.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullfighter - a person who fights bulls in the arena

Etymology

Spanish, from 'banderilla' (decorated dart) + '-ero' (agent suffix).
