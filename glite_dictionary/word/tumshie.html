tumshie

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌmʃi / (AmE), / ˈtʌmʃi / (BrE)
(Scottish, informal) A foolish or silly person; an idiot.
a foolish or silly person (Scottish)
informal slang british

Examples

I can't believe you lost your keys again, you tumshie!
She called him a tumshie for wearing mismatched shoes to the interview.
Only a complete tumshie would forget their own birthday.

Synset Examples

He called his friend a tumshie for forgetting their plans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very foolish or stupid person
  • Synonym: fool - a person who behaves in a silly way

Etymology

From Scots 'tumshie', meaning 'turnip', used figuratively to refer to stupidity.
