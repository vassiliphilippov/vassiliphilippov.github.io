Kirkia

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɜːrkiə / (AmE), / ˈkɜːkiə / (BrE)
A small genus of tropical South African trees and shrubs, belonging to the family Kirkiaceae.
genus of South African trees/shrubs

Examples

The Kirkia genus includes several species native to southern Africa.
Botanists are studying the medicinal properties of Kirkia trees.
We found a rare Kirkia specimen during our expedition in Mozambique.

Etymology

Named after John Kirk (1832–1922), Scottish explorer and naturalist.
