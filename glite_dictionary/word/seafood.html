seafood

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːˌfud / (AmE), / ˈsiːfuːd / (BrE)
Fish and other edible sea creatures, such as shellfish and roe, eaten as food.
edible fish and shellfish

Examples

We ordered a platter of seafood that included shrimp, oysters, and crab.
The restaurant is famous for its fresh seafood dishes.
She prefers seafood over red meat for its health benefits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shellfish - Edible aquatic shelled creatures such as shrimp, oysters, and clams

Etymology

Formed from 'sea' + 'food', indicating food from the sea.
