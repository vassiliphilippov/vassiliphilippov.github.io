Paki

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæki / (AmE), / ˈpæki / (BrE)
an offensive term for a person of Pakistani descent
offensive slur for person of Pakistani origin
offensive british

Examples

It's hurtful to hear someone Paki in casual conversation.
The word Paki is deeply offensive and should not be used.
He was subjected to racial abuse, including being called a Paki by classmates.

Synset Examples

It's hurtful to hear someone call him a Paki.

Etymology

Shortened form of "Pakistani"; first recorded in Britain in the mid-20th century.
