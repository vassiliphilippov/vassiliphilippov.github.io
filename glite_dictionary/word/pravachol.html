Pravachol

1. NOUN

/ ˈprævəˌkoʊl / (AmE), / ˈprævəkɒl / (BrE)
A trademarked medicine for reducing cholesterol, prescribed especially after nonfatal heart attacks; brand name for pravastatin.
cholesterol-lowering medication (brand name)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Pravachol to help lower his cholesterol after the heart attack.
Many patients take Pravachol as part of their long-term cardiac health regimen.
She asked her pharmacist if Pravachol could be taken with her current medication.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pravastatin - the generic drug that Pravachol contains
