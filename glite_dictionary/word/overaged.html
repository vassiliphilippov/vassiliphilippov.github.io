overaged

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈeɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərˈeɪdʒd / (BrE)
too old to be useful or suitable for a particular purpose
too old to be useful

Examples

The company decided to replace the overaged equipment to improve efficiency.
As an overaged player, he was not eligible for the youth tournament.
These overaged vehicles no longer meet the city's emissions standards.

Synset Examples

He left the house … for the support of twelve superannuated wool carders

Related Terms

  • Synonym: superannuated - too old for work or use; retired because of age or infirmity

Etymology

Formed from 'over-' (above, beyond) + 'aged'.
