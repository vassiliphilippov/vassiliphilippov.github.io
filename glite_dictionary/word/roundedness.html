roundedness

1. NOUN

/ ˈraʊndɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈraʊndɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being rounded or curved, especially having smooth convex surfaces or edges
quality of being rounded or curved

Examples

The roundedness of the sculpture made it pleasing to the touch.
In linguistics, vowel roundedness affects how sounds are produced in the mouth.
The designer emphasized the roundedness of the building's corners for a softer appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roundness - the quality or state of being round in shape
