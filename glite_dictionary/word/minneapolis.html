Minneapolis

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɪniˈæpəlɪs / (AmE), / ˌmɪniˈæpəlɪs / (BrE)
the largest city in Minnesota, located in the southeastern part of the state on the Mississippi River, known for its historic flour mills and as one of the Twin Cities with St. Paul
largest city in Minnesota, USA

Examples

She moved to Minneapolis to attend university and fell in love with the city's vibrant arts scene.
The snowy winters in Minneapolis can be harsh, but the community spirit makes up for it.
Many visitors enjoy biking along the Mississippi River when they visit Minneapolis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: St. Paul - the capital city of Minnesota and the other Twin City

Etymology

From the Dakota word *minne* (water) and Greek *polis* (city), meaning 'city of waters'.
