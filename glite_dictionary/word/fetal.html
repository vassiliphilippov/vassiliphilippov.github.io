fetal

1. ADJ

/ ˈfiːtəl / (AmE), / ˈfiːtəl / (BrE)
relating to, or characteristic of a fetus; occurring or developing in the womb.
relating to a fetus
medical

Examples

The doctor monitored the fetal heartbeat throughout the pregnancy.
Exposure to certain substances can affect fetal development.
During the ultrasound, the technician measured the fetal length.

Synset Examples

fetal development

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: embryonic - relating to an embryo; earliest developmental stage

Etymology

From Latin 'fetus' (offspring, bringing forth, hatching).
