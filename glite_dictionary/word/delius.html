Delius

1. PROPN

/ ˈdiːliəs / (AmE), / ˈdiːliəs / (BrE)
(1862–1934) English composer known for his impressionistic orchestral works and choral music.
English composer (1862–1934)

Examples

Frederick Delius was famous for his evocative orchestral music inspired by nature.
Many critics consider Delius an important figure in early 20th-century English music.
The concert featured several pieces composed by Delius.
