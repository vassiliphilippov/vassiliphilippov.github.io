Ruanda

1. PROPN

/ ruˈɑːndə / (AmE), / ruˈɑːndə / (BrE)
an alternative or historical spelling of Rwanda, a landlocked country in central Africa
historical spelling of Rwanda (country)

Examples

The country of Ruanda was once administered by Germany.
Early 20th-century maps often labeled the region as Ruanda.
Researchers studied colonial history in Ruanda and Burundi.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Rwanda - modern standard spelling for the central African country

Etymology

Variant of 'Rwanda,' influenced by German colonial usage.
