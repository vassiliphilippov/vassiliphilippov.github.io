da'wah

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑːwɑː / (AmE), / ˈdɑːwɑː / (BrE)
The act of inviting or calling people to embrace Islam, often referring to Islamic missionary work.
Islamic missionary work

Examples

Many Muslim organizations are dedicated to da'wah in both local and international communities.
She participated in numerous da'wah projects aimed at sharing the teachings of Islam.
Modern technology has significantly changed the methods used for da'wah around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: missionary work - the activity of spreading a religion to new people or places

Etymology

From Arabic daʿwah (دعوة), meaning 'invitation' or 'call'.
