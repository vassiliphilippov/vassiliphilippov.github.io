fondu

1. NOUN

/ fɑnˈduː / (AmE), / fɒnˈdjuː / (BrE)
A dish consisting of small cubes of meat or seafood cooked in hot oil and then dipped in a variety of sauces.
meat/seafood cooked in oil and dipped in sauces

Examples

Each guest cooked their own meat in the fondu and tried different dipping sauces.
We enjoyed a seafood fondu at the fancy restaurant last night.
For her birthday party, she served a fondu with chicken, beef, and shrimp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fondue - A Swiss dish of melted cheese or chocolate in which pieces of food are dipped

Etymology

From French 'fondue', meaning 'melted'.

2. NOUN

/ fɒnˈduː / (AmE), / fɒnˈduː / (BrE)
A hot dish of melted cheese or chocolate, served in a communal pot for dipping bread, fruit, or vegetables.
dish of melted cheese or chocolate for dipping

Examples

At the party, everyone enjoyed dipping bread into the bubbling pot of fondu.
We ordered chocolate fondu for dessert and dipped strawberries and bananas into it.
Cheese fondu is a classic Swiss dish perfect for sharing on a cold night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fondue - dish of melted cheese or chocolate for dipping

Etymology

Borrowed from French 'fondu', past participle of 'fondre' (to melt).
