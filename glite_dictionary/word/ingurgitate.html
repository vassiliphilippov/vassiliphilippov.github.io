to ingurgitate

1. VERB

/ ɪnˈɡɝːdʒɪteɪt / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡɜːdʒɪteɪt / (BrE)
To overeat or eat excessively; to consume food or drink immoderately.
to eat excessively or immoderately

Examples

At the buffet, he tended to ingurgitate without considering how full he was.
It is unhealthy to ingurgitate fatty foods every night before bed.
The children ingurgitated their Halloween candy as soon as they got home.

Synset Examples

She stuffed herself at the dinner
The kids binged on ice cream

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to overeat - to eat too much food
  • Synonym: binge - to eat or drink excessively, often in a short period

Etymology

From Latin ingurgitare, from in- (into) + gurges, gurgit- (whirlpool, throat).
