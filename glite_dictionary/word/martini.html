martini

1. NOUN

/ mɑːrˈtiːni / (AmE), / mɑːˈtiːni / (BrE)
a cocktail made with gin (or sometimes vodka) and dry vermouth, usually garnished with an olive or a lemon twist
cocktail of gin or vodka with vermouth

Examples

She ordered a classic martini with an olive at the bar.
He prefers his martini made with vodka rather than gin.
At the party, trays of chilled martinis were served to the guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cocktail - an alcoholic mixed drink

Etymology

Named after the Italian vermouth brand Martini & Rossi, or possibly after the town of Martinez, California.
