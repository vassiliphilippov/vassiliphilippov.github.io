chiropodist

1. NOUN

/ kɪˈrɑːpədɪst / (AmE), / kɪˈrɒpədɪst / (BrE)
a person trained to diagnose and treat minor disorders and diseases of the feet
foot care specialist
british medical

Examples

After years of foot pain, I finally made an appointment with a chiropodist.
The athlete visited a chiropodist to treat her recurring blisters.
Many elderly people rely on a chiropodist for regular foot care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: podiatrist - a medical professional specializing in foot disorders

Etymology

From Greek 'kheir' (hand) + 'pous, podos' (foot), via French 'chiropodiste'.
