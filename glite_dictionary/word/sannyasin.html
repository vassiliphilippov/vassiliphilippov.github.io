sannyasin

1. NOUN

/ sænˈjɑːsɪn / (AmE), / sænˈjɑːsɪn / (BrE)
A Hindu religious ascetic or renunciate who has renounced worldly life.
Hindu ascetic; religious renunciate

Examples

After years of meditation, he became a sannyasin, leaving all his possessions behind.
The village respected the sannyasin for his wisdom and spiritual guidance.
As a sannyasin, she spent her days in prayer and contemplation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ascetic - A person who practices severe self-discipline and abstention

Etymology

From Sanskrit *sannyāsin* (one who renounces worldly things).
