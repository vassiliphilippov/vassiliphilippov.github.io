Virgilia oroboides

1. PROPN

/ vɜrˈdʒɪliə ɔːˌroʊˈboʊɪdiːz / (AmE), / vəːˈdʒɪlɪə ɔːˌrəʊˈbəʊɪdiːz / (BrE)
A South African tree with odd-pinnate leaves and racemes of fragrant pink to purple flowers, also known as the Keurboom.
South African tree with fragrant pink-purple flowers

Examples

The Virgilia oroboides is native to the coastal regions of South Africa.
In spring, the Virgilia oroboides becomes covered with beautiful pink-purple blossoms.
Gardeners prize the Virgilia oroboides for its rapid growth and ornamental flowers.
