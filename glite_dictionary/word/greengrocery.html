greengrocery

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrinˌɡroʊsəri / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnˌɡrəʊsəri / (BrE)
A shop or store that sells fruit and vegetables.
shop selling fruit and vegetables

Examples

She stopped at the greengrocery on her way home to buy fresh apples and carrots.
The greengrocery was known for its wide selection of organic vegetables.
Many neighborhoods used to have a local greengrocery, but now supermarkets are more common.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greengrocer - a person or shop selling fruit and vegetables

Etymology

From 'green', referring to fresh produce, and 'grocery', a shop selling food and other provisions.

fruits and vegetables sold by a greengrocer

Examples

She visited the market every week to buy fresh greengrocery for her family.
Local farmers supply the greengrocery stores with seasonal vegetables and fruit.
Rising prices have made greengrocery less affordable for some households.

Related Terms

Etymology

Formed from 'green' + 'grocery', reflecting the trade in fresh produce.
