signaling

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪnəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the use of nonverbal actions or gestures to convey a message or information
nonverbal communication of a message

Examples

The train conductor used hand signaling to communicate with the engineer.
In animal behavior, visual signaling often plays a crucial role in attracting mates.
Effective signaling is essential for safety in noisy construction sites.

Synset Examples

signals from the boat suddenly stopped

Related Terms

  • Synonym: signal - a gesture, action, or sound used to convey information or instructions

Etymology

From the verb 'signal', ultimately from Latin 'signum' (mark, sign).
