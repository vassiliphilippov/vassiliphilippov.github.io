lilac-blue

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪlæk ˈblu / (AmE), / ˈlaɪlək ˈbluː / (BrE)
Of a shade of blue that is tinged with lavender.
blue with a lavender tinge

Examples

The delicate lilac-blue hues of the evening sky soothed her.
She wore a lilac-blue dress to the spring festival.
The artist mixed white, blue, and a touch of purple to create the perfect lilac-blue paint.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: lavender - a pale purple color
