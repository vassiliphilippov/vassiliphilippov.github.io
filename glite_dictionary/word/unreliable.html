2. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈlaɪ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈlaɪ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
Not able to be trusted or depended on; likely to be wrong, misleading, or not accurate.
not able to be trusted or depended on

Examples

We lost the data because the software was unreliable.
His memory became increasingly unreliable as he got older.
The train service here is notoriously unreliable during the winter months.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undependable - not able to be depended on

Etymology

From un- (not) + reliable, from rely + -able, originally from French relier (to bind) and Latin religare.
