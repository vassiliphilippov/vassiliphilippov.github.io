bullring

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlˌrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʊl.rɪŋ / (BrE)
a circular stadium or arena where bullfights are held
arena for bullfighting

Examples

The famous Seville bullring attracts thousands of spectators each year.
The architect designed the bullring with both tradition and comfort in mind.
Many tourists visit the old bullring, even when no fights are scheduled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arena - a central area used for sports or entertainment
