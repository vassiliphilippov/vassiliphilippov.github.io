defrayal

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfreɪ.əl / (AmE), / dɪˈfreɪ.əl / (BrE)
the act of paying or settling expenses
the act of paying expenses

Examples

The defrayal of tuition fees is a major concern for many students.
A grant was provided for the defrayal of travel expenses.
She appreciated the prompt defrayal of medical costs by the insurance company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: payment - the action or process of paying money

Etymology

From the verb 'defray', from Old French 'defrayer', from 'de-' (off) + 'frais' (costs).
