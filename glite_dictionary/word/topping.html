topping

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
a food ingredient or flavoring placed on top of a dish to enhance its taste or appearance
something added on top of food

Examples

She sprinkled extra cheese as a topping on her pizza.
Whipped cream is my favorite topping for hot chocolate.
The burger came with lettuce and tomato topping.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garnish - a decoration or embellishment added to food

Etymology

From the verb 'top' + -ing, meaning something that tops or covers.

3. NOUN

/ ˈtɑːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
an ingredient or mixture of ingredients placed on top of a dish, especially to add flavor or decoration
ingredient added on top of food

Examples

The pizza had a generous amount of cheese as its main topping.
What kind of topping would you like on your ice cream sundae?
He liked to put fresh vegetables as a topping on his noodles.

Synset Examples

She loves experimenting with different toppings in her ramen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garnish - a decorative or flavorful addition to food, often added just before serving

Etymology

From the verb 'top' meaning 'to put on top of' plus suffix '-ing'.
