trousering

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraʊzərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtraʊzərɪŋ / (BrE)
fabric of a type suitable for making trousers.
fabric for making trousers

Examples

The tailor recommended a durable trousering for everyday wear.
This lightweight trousering is perfect for summer suits.
She preferred Italian wool trousering for its quality and drape.

Etymology

From 'trouser' + '-ing', indicating material used for trousers.
