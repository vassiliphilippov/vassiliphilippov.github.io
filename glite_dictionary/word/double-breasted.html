double-breasted

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʌb.əlˈbrɛs.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌdʌb.əlˈbrɛs.tɪd / (BrE)
(of clothing) having a front with two overlapping edges and two parallel rows of buttons, so that one side fastens over the other.
having two parallel rows of buttons (clothing)

Examples

He wore a crisp double-breasted suit to the interview, which made a strong impression.
The tailor recommended a double-breasted blazer for a more formal look.
Vintage fashion often features double-breasted coats with wide lapels and distinctive styling.

Synset Examples

double-breasted jacket
double-breasted suit

Related Terms

  • Antonym: single-breasted - (of clothing) with a narrow overlap and one row of buttons
