circumboreal

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɝː.kəm.bɔːˈriː.əl / (AmE), / ˌsɜː.kəm.bɔːˈriː.əl / (BrE)
found or distributed throughout the far northern regions of the world, especially in reference to plants, animals, or ecological areas.
distributed throughout far northern regions

Examples

The circumboreal flora includes many species found across northern Eurasia and North America.
Scientists studied the migration patterns of circumboreal birds.
The circumboreal forest zone stretches across Canada, Scandinavia, and Russia.

Etymology

From Latin 'circum-' (around) + 'borealis' (northern).
