Providence

1. PROPN

/ ˈprɑː.və.dəns / (AmE), / ˈprɒ.vɪ.dəns / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Rhode Island, located in the northeastern part of the state on Narragansett Bay
capital city of Rhode Island, USA

Examples

Many students move to Providence each year to attend Brown University.
The historic downtown area makes Providence a popular New England destination.
She grew up in Providence before relocating to Boston for work.

Etymology

Named by founder Roger Williams in 1636, inspired by the concept of divine providence.
