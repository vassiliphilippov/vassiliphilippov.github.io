unsexed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɛkst / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛkst / (BrE)
deprived of sexual capacity or sexual characteristics
deprived of sexual capacity/attributes

Examples

The veterinarian explained that the cat had been unsexed to prevent unwanted litters.
Many workers in factory farming are responsible for handling unsexed poultry for commercial purposes.
The literature explored the theme of characters being unsexed by traumatic events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: castrated - deprived of the testes or ovaries; unable to reproduce sexually

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'sexed' (having sex characteristics).
