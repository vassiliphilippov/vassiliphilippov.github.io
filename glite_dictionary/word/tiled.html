tiled

1. ADJ

/ taɪld / (AmE), / taɪld / (BrE)
covered or furnished with tiles, especially as a surface finish for walls, floors, or roofs.
covered with tiles

Examples

The bathroom has a tiled floor that is easy to clean.
They admired the beautiful tiled roof of the old cottage.
The kitchen walls are tiled in a bright, cheerful pattern.

Synset Examples

baths with tiled walls
a tiled kitchen

Related Terms

  • Antonym: untiled - not covered with tiles
  • Synonym: tiled - (verb) to cover or furnish with tiles
