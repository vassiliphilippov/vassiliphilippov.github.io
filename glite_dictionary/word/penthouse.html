penthouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛnthaʊs / (AmE), / ˈpɛnthaʊs / (BrE)
an apartment or dwelling located on the top floor or floors of a building, typically offering luxurious features and expansive views
top-floor luxury apartment

Examples

She bought a penthouse overlooking Central Park with floor-to-ceiling windows.
His dream was to live in a penthouse on the top of a skyscraper in the city.
The real estate agent showed us a luxurious penthouse with a private rooftop terrace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apartment - a set of rooms forming an individual residence

Etymology

From Middle English 'pentis' (attached building) and 'house', later specialized in meaning.
