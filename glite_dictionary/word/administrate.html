to administrate

1. VERB

/ ədˈmɪnəˌstreɪt / (AmE), / ədˈmɪnɪstreɪt / (BrE)
to manage, supervise, or be in charge of an organization, program, or activity, especially in an official or administrative capacity
to manage or supervise (formally)

Examples

He was hired to administrate the new training program for employees.
As principal, she must administrate both academic and financial matters at the school.
Several agencies administrate grants to support researchers in this field.

Synset Examples

administer a program
she administers the funds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to administer - to manage or direct the execution of affairs

Etymology

Formed from 'administration' by back-formation; influenced by 'administer'.
