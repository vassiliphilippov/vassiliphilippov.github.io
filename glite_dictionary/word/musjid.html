musjid

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsdʒɪd / (AmE), / ˈmʌsdʒɪd / (BrE)
A Muslim place of worship; a mosque.
Muslim place of worship

Examples

The old city is dotted with beautiful musjids dating back several centuries.
During Ramadan, hundreds gather nightly at the largest musjid in town.
He visited the musjid to observe afternoon prayers with the local community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mosque - A Muslim place of worship

Etymology

From Arabic 'masjid' (place of prostration), via Urdu and Persian.
