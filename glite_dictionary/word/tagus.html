Tagus

1. PROPN

/ ˈteɪɡəs / (AmE), / ˈteɪɡəs / (BrE)
a major river in southwestern Europe that flows westward through Spain and Portugal into the North Atlantic Ocean
river in Spain and Portugal

Examples

The city of Lisbon is situated on the banks of the Tagus.
The Tagus is the longest river on the Iberian Peninsula.
Many bridges span the Tagus as it passes through Portugal.
