appealingness

1. NOUN

/ əˈpiːlɪŋnəs / (AmE), / əˈpiːlɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being attractive or pleasing, or of arousing interest or desire
quality of being attractive or pleasing

Examples

The appealingness of the proposal won over even its initial critics.
Many advertisements are designed to increase the appealingness of a product.
She enhanced the appealingness of the room with fresh flowers and bright colors.

Synset Examples

his smile was part of his appeal to her

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attractiveness - the quality of being attractive
  • Antonym: unattractiveness - the quality of being not attractive or pleasing

Etymology

From 'appealing' (attractive) + '-ness' (forming nouns indicating a state or quality).
