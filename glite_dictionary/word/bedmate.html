bedmate

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛdˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɛdmeɪt / (BrE)
a person with whom you share a bed, especially as a sexual partner
person who shares your bed

Examples

After years of marriage, his wife remained his loyal bedmate.
He never expected his new bedmate to snore so loudly.
During their travels abroad, they became accidental bedmates due to a hotel mix-up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roommate - a person with whom you share a room
  • Synonym: partner - a person with whom one has a romantic or sexual relationship

Etymology

Formed from 'bed' + 'mate' (companion).
