custom-built

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌstəm bɪlt / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəm bɪlt / (BrE)
(of an object) made or designed for a particular person or purpose, according to specific requirements.
made for a particular person or purpose

Examples

They ordered a custom-built kitchen to fit their unusual floor plan.
The computer was custom-built for high-performance gaming.
She drives a custom-built car designed especially for racing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bespoke - made to order for a particular customer
  • Antonym: off-the-shelf - ready-made and available from stock
Source: WordNet

2. ADJ

/ ˈkʌstəm bɪlt / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəm bɪlt / (BrE)
specially made for a particular customer or purpose according to specific requirements.
made to individual specifications

Examples

The company ordered a custom-built computer system for their unique data processing needs.
Her dream home was a custom-built house designed by a renowned architect.
They drive a custom-built sports car that you won't find anywhere else.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: off-the-shelf - available as a standard product, not custom-made
