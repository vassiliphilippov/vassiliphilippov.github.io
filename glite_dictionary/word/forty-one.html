forty-one

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɔrtiˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˌfɔːtiˈwʌn / (BrE)
being one more than forty; the cardinal number following forty and preceding forty-two.
the number 41; one more than forty

Examples

This is the forty-onest edition of the festival, making it one of the oldest in the country.
She received forty-one votes, just enough to win the election.
Out of forty-one applicants, three were selected for further interviews.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty - the number 40; ten times four
  • Degree Higher: forty-two - the number 42; one more than forty-one

Etymology

From forty + one; based on Old English 'feowertig' (forty) and 'ān' (one).

2. NUM

/ ˌfɔrtiˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˌfɔːtiˈwʌn / (BrE)
The cardinal number following forty and preceding forty-two; equal to 41.
the number 41

Examples

She received forty-one votes in the election, placing her third overall.
After waiting forty-one minutes, the train finally arrived at the platform.
The company celebrated its forty-one years in business with a special event.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty - the number 40
  • Degree Higher: forty-two - the number 42
