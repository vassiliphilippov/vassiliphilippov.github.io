mealtime

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːlˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈmiːltaɪm / (BrE)
The time of day when a meal is usually eaten.
customary time for eating a meal

Examples

Please be seated before mealtime begins so we can serve everyone together.
He was accustomed to taking a short walk after each mealtime.
During the holidays, our family mealtimes are filled with laughter and stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dinnertime - time in the evening when dinner is usually eaten
  • Synonym: lunchtime - time in the middle of the day when lunch is usually eaten
