nighttime

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪtˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt.taɪm / (BrE)
The period of time after sunset and before sunrise when it is dark outside.
the period of darkness between sunset and sunrise

Examples

Many animals are more active during the nighttime than during the day.
She prefers to take long walks in the cool nighttime air.
The city takes on a different atmosphere at nighttime with all the lights.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: daytime - the period during which there is daylight; the time between sunrise and sunset
