epidemiological

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of the distribution and determinants of health-related states or events in populations
relating to epidemiology
medical

Examples

The government launched a series of epidemiological studies to track the spread of the disease.
Recent epidemiological data suggest an increase in seasonal flu cases this year.
She specializes in epidemiological research on infectious diseases in developing countries.

Synset Examples

epidemiological studies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: epidemiology - the branch of medicine dealing with the incidence, distribution, and control of diseases

Etymology

From epidemiology, from Greek epi- (upon) + demos (people) + -logy (study).
