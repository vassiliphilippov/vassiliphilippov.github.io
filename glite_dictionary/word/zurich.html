Zurich

1. PROPN

/ ˈzʊrɪk / (AmE), / ˈzjʊərɪk / (BrE)
the largest city in Switzerland, located in the north of the country, known as a major financial and cultural center
largest city in Switzerland

Examples

She plans to visit Zurich during her tour of Switzerland next summer.
Zurich is renowned for its high quality of life and financial institutions.
The International Film Festival in Zurich attracts filmmakers from all over the world.

Synset Examples

Zurich is the center of the German-speaking part of Switzerland

Etymology

From German Zürich, the name of the Swiss city.
