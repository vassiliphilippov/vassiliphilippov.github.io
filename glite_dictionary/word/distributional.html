distributional

1. ADJ

/ ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuʃənl / (AmE), / ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the distribution, especially spatial distribution, of something
relating to distribution

Examples

The researchers analyzed the distributional patterns of the animal species across different regions.
Her work focuses on the distributional properties of linguistic elements within sentences.
We must consider distributional requirements when designing the new warehouse system.

Synset Examples

distributional requirements

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distributive - relating to the action of distributing or the process of being distributed

Etymology

From 'distribution' + -al, forming adjectives meaning 'pertaining to'.
