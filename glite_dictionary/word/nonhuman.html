nonhuman

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈhjuːmən / (AmE), / nɒnˈhjuːmən / (BrE)
not human; not belonging to, produced by, or appropriate to human beings
not human; belonging to something other than humans

Examples

Many animal rights activists argue that nonhuman species deserve legal protection.
The laboratory uses nonhuman models to test the new medications before human trials begin.
Advanced artificial intelligence may someday reach a level of nonhuman intelligence.

Synset Examples

nonhuman primates such as chimpanzees

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inhuman - cruel, lacking human qualities of compassion

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' meaning 'not' and 'human' from Latin 'humanus' (of man, human).
