step-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɛpˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈstɛpˌʌp / (BrE)
an increase, especially a rise in amount, level, or intensity of something
an increase or rise

Examples

The company announced a significant step-up in production to meet rising demand.
There was a noticeable step-up in security after recent events.
Increasing the voltage required a step-up in the power supply.

Synset Examples

he gave me an increase in salary

Related Terms

  • Synonym: increase - the process of becoming or making greater in size, amount, or degree
  • Antonym: decrease - a reduction or lessening in amount, degree, or size
