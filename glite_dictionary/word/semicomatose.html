semicomatose

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛmiˈkoʊməˌtoʊs / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈkəʊmətəʊs / (BrE)
in a state of partial coma; only partially unconscious and not fully responsive to external stimuli
partially comatose (medical)
medical

Examples

The patient was found to be semicomatose after the accident, responding only to painful stimuli.
Doctors described her as semicomatose, unable to follow commands but not fully unconscious.
In his semicomatose state, he showed some reflexes but could not communicate.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: comatose - in a state of deep unconsciousness for a prolonged or indefinite period
  • Degree Lower: conscious - aware and able to respond to surroundings

Etymology

From prefix 'semi-' meaning 'half' or 'partial' + 'comatose' (see coma).
