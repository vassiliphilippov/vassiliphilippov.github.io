lumpiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌmpi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈlʌmpɪnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being full of lumps or not smooth.
the quality of being full of lumps

Examples

The lumpiness of the mashed potatoes made them less appealing to some guests.
You can feel the lumpiness of the sweater just by running your hand over it.
They tried to remove the lumpiness from the paint before applying it to the wall.

Synset Examples

the surface is distinctive in its lumpiness, random striations, and generally uneven texture.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smoothness - the quality of being smooth and even

Etymology

From 'lumpy' + '-ness', where 'lumpy' derives from 'lump'.
