brunet

1. NOUN

/ bruːˈnɛt / (AmE), / bruːˈnɛt / (BrE)
A man or boy with dark brown or black hair.
man or boy with dark hair

Examples

He is a striking brunet with piercing blue eyes.
The casting director was looking for a brunet to play the leading role.
Unlike his blond brothers, Mark is the only brunet in the family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brunette - a woman or girl with dark brown or black hair

Etymology

From French brunet, diminutive of brun (brown).

2. ADJ

/ bruːˈnɛt / (AmE), / bruːˈnɛt / (BrE)
(of a person) having dark or moderately dark hair, eyes, or skin.
having dark hair, eyes, or skin

Examples

He was a tall, handsome brunet who stood out in the crowd.
The casting director was searching for a child actor with brunet features.
Her brunet complexion gave her a distinctive, striking appearance.

Related Terms
