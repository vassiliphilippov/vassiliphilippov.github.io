galere

1. NOUN

/ ɡæˈlɛr / (AmE), / ɡaˈlɛə / (BrE)
A group of people regarded as undesirable or disreputable.
group of undesirable people

Examples

The celebrity distanced herself from the galere that frequented the party scene.
He found himself unexpectedly joined by a galere of dubious acquaintances.
No one wanted to be associated with the infamous galere at the back of the classroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coterie - a small group of people with shared interests or tastes

Etymology

From French ‘galère’ (galley, band of rogues), from Italian ‘galera’.
