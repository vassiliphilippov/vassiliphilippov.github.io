tripping

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
(of music, movement, or verse) having a light, buoyant, and rhythmic quality; lively or graceful in rhythm or motion.
having a light, rhythmic, lively quality

Examples

The dancers moved across the stage with tripping steps, enchanting the audience.
She played a tripping melody on the piano that captured everyone's attention.
His verse has a tripping rhythm that gives it a playful quality.

/ ˈtrɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
(literary) moving lightly and quickly; nimble, especially in movement or step.
moving lightly and quickly

Examples

His tripping steps made it seem as if he was floating over the grass.
She entered the garden in a tripping manner, light and full of joy.
