commonality

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːmənˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌkɒmənˈælɪti / (BrE)
the state of sharing features or attributes with others; a quality or characteristic shared by a group
shared attribute or quality

Examples

There is a strong commonality of interests among the group members.
The commonality between the two cultures made communication easier.
Despite their differences, they found a commonality in their love of music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: similarity - resemblance or a state of being alike
  • Antonym: difference - a quality or characteristic that sets things apart

Etymology

From Latin 'commūnālitās', from 'commūnis' (common).
