violoncellist

1. NOUN

/ ˌvi.oʊˈlɒn.sə.lɪst / (AmE), / ˌvi.ɒl.ɒnˈtʃɛl.ɪst / (BrE)
A person who plays the violoncello (cello).
person who plays the violoncello

Examples

The renowned violoncellist performed a solo at the concert hall last night.
As a young violoncellist, she practiced for hours every day to master her craft.
Several violoncellists competed for the principal seat in the orchestra.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cellist - a person who plays the cello

Etymology

From Italian 'violoncellista', from 'violoncello'.
