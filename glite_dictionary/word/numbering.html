numbering

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌmbɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbərɪŋ / (BrE)
a system or arrangement in which items are assigned numbers in a sequence, especially in a list or series
system of numbered items or lists

Examples

The numbering on these pages is out of order.
Please check the numbering of the figures in the report.
Our inventory uses a sequential numbering system for all products.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: list - an ordered set of items

Etymology

From 'number' (Middle English, from Old French 'nombre', from Latin 'numerus') + '-ing' forming a noun from a verb.
