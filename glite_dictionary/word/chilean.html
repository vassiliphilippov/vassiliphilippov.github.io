Chilean

1. ADJ

/ tʃɪˈliən / (AmE), / tʃɪˈliːən / (BrE)
relating to Chile or its people
of or relating to Chile

Examples

The Chilean economy has grown significantly over the past decade.
They enjoyed traditional Chilean food during their trip.
Many Chilean artists are recognized internationally.

Synset Examples

Chilean volcanoes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chilean - a person from Chile

Etymology

From Chile + -an (forming adjectives of nationality or relation).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02977530-a
Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ tʃɪˈliːən / (AmE), / tʃɪˈliːən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Chile
person from Chile

Examples

Maria is a proud Chilean who loves to share her country's traditions.
A group of Chileans gathered to celebrate Independence Day in Santiago.
The Chilean greeted us warmly and offered local delicacies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chilean - relating to Chile or its people, especially as an adjective
  • Antonym: Chiliast - one expecting a coming golden age or utopia, often in a religious sense (not directly related but sometimes confused)

Etymology

From Chile + -an (denoting origin or association).

WordNet ID: oewn-09717382-n
Synset POS: n
