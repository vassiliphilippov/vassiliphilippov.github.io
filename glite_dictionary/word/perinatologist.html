perinatologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɛrɪˌneɪtəˈlɑːdʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌpɛrɪˌneɪtəˈlɒdʒɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in perinatology, the branch of medicine concerned with the care of the fetus and complicated pregnancies
doctor specializing in perinatology
medical

Examples

The perinatologist monitored the high-risk pregnancy with specialized equipment.
She consulted a perinatologist due to concerns about premature labor.
A team led by the perinatologist developed a plan for the delivery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obstetrician - doctor specializing in childbirth and pregnancy
  • Synonym: perinatology - the branch of medicine concerned with the care of the fetus and complicated pregnancies

Etymology

From peri- (around) + Greek *natalis* (pertaining to birth) + -ologist (specialist).
