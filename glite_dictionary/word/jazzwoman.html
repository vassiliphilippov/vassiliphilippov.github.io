jazzwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒæzˌwʊm.ən / (AmE), / ˈdʒæzˌwʊm.ən / (BrE)
A female musician who plays or composes jazz music.
female jazz musician or composer

Examples

The renowned jazzwoman performed an electrifying solo at the festival.
As a pioneering jazzwoman, she inspired countless young musicians to pursue their dreams.
The panel featured a celebrated jazzwoman discussing her journey in a male-dominated industry.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: jazzman - a male jazz musician or composer

Etymology

Formed from 'jazz' + 'woman', modeled after 'jazzman'.
