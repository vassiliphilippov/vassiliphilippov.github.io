Nevada

1. PROPN

/ nəˈvædə / (AmE), / nɪˈvɑːdə / (BrE)
A state in the western United States known for its deserts, large casinos, and the city of Las Vegas.
state in the western United States
american american

Examples

Many people visit Nevada each year to try their luck in Las Vegas.
Lake Tahoe sits on the border between California and Nevada.
The desert landscape of Nevada features beautiful rock formations and mysterious ghost towns.

Etymology

From Spanish 'nevada', meaning 'snow-covered'.
