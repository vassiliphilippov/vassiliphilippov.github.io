dyspneic

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈpniɪk / (AmE), / dɪsˈpniːɪk / (BrE)
having difficulty breathing; suffering from shortness of breath.
having difficulty breathing
medical

Examples

The patient became dyspneic after climbing just one flight of stairs.
During the examination, the doctor noted she appeared dyspneic and was struggling to speak in full sentences.
If you feel dyspneic when lying flat, it may indicate a heart or lung problem.

Synset Examples

breathless at thought of what I had done
breathless from running
followed the match with breathless interest

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breathless - short of breath or unable to breathe easily
  • Synonym: dyspnea - difficult or labored breathing

Etymology

From Greek 'dys-' (bad, difficult) + 'pnoia' (breathing).
