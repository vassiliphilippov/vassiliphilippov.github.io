conjunctival

1. ADJ

/ kənˈdʒʌŋk.tɪ.vəl / (AmE), / kənˈdʒʌŋk.tɪ.vəl / (BrE)
relating to the conjunctiva, the delicate membrane lining the eyelids and covering the white part of the eyeball
relating to the eye's conjunctiva
medical

Examples

A routine eye examination showed mild conjunctival redness.
The doctor noted conjunctival swelling after the patient complained of irritation.
Conjunctival infections can cause discomfort and watery eyes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ocular - relating to the eye

Etymology

From Latin 'conjunctiva' (the membrane that joins together), with '-al' forming the adjective.
