backpacker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbækˌpækər / (AmE), / ˈbækˌpækə / (BrE)
a person who travels or hikes carrying their belongings in a backpack, often on long or adventurous journeys
person traveling or hiking with a backpack

Examples

After graduating from college, she spent six months as a backpacker exploring Southeast Asia.
Many backpackers prefer hostels because they are affordable and social.
He met other backpackers at the campground and shared travel stories with them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hiker - a person who goes for long walks, especially in the countryside

Etymology

Formed from "backpack" + agentive suffix "-er".
