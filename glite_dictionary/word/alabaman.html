Alabaman

1. NOUN

/ ˌæl.əˈbæm.ən / (AmE), / ˌæl.əˈbam.ən / (BrE)
A person who is a native or resident of the state of Alabama in the United States.
native or resident of Alabama

Examples

Many Alabamans take pride in their state's long history and vibrant culture.
She met several Alabamans at the conference in Atlanta.
The hurricane caused major disruptions for Alabamans living near the coast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Alabamian - another term for a native or resident of Alabama

Etymology

From the name of the U.S. state Alabama + -an denoting origin or association.
