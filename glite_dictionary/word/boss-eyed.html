boss-eyed

1. ADJ

/ bɑs aɪd / (AmE), / bɒs aɪd / (BrE)
(British informal) Having eyes that look in different directions; cross-eyed.
having cross-eyed or misaligned eyes (British informal)
informal british

Examples

He was born boss-eyed and had surgery as a child to correct his vision.
She joked about being boss-eyed to make her children laugh.
In school, kids sometimes teased him for being boss-eyed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cross-eyed - having eyes that look in different directions
