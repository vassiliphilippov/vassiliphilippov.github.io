deucedly

1. ADV

/ ˈdusɪdli / (AmE), / ˈdjuːsɪdli / (BrE)
to a very great or excessive degree; extremely
extremely; to a great degree

Examples

The task proved to be deucedly difficult, despite all their efforts.
She is deucedly clever and always finds a solution to every problem.
It was deucedly cold that night, much colder than we expected.

Synset Examples

she was madly in love
deadly dull
deadly earnest
deucedly clever
insanely jealous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extremely - to a very great degree
  • Synonym: terribly - very; to a great extent

Etymology

From 'deuced' (meaning devilish) + '-ly'.
