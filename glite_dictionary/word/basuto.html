Basuto

1. NOUN

/ bəˈsuːtoʊ / (AmE), / bəˈsuːtəʊ / (BrE)
the dialect of Sotho spoken by the Basotho people; an official language of Lesotho
a dialect of Sotho; language of Lesotho

Examples

Many children in Lesotho grow up speaking Basuto as their first language.
She is studying Basuto in order to communicate better with the local community.
Official documents in Lesotho are often translated into both English and Basuto.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sotho - a group of closely related Bantu languages, including Basuto

Etymology

From SeSotho 'Basotho', meaning 'people of Sotho'.
