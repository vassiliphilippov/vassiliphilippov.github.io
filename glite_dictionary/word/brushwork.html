brushwork

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrʌʃ.wɝːk / (AmE), / ˈbrʌʃ.wɜːk / (BrE)
The distinctive way in which an artist applies paint to a surface using a brush, often characteristic of their style.
an artist's technique of applying paint with a brush

Examples

The artist's bold brushwork gives the painting a sense of energy and movement.
Her delicate brushwork can be seen in every detail of the portrait.
Critics praised the brushwork for its originality and subtle use of color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: technique - a method of carrying out a particular task, especially in art

Etymology

Formed from 'brush' + 'work', referring to the action of the brush in creating art.
