sell

1. NOUN

/ sɛl / (AmE), / sɛl / (BrE)
an attempt or process of persuading someone to buy something or accept an idea
act of persuasion in selling

Examples

Getting investment for the startup was a tough sell to the board.
The new product launch turned out to be an easy sell thanks to effective marketing.
Her story was a hard sell because it seemed unbelievable.

Synset Examples

it was a hard sell

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitch - a talk or argument intended to persuade someone to buy or accept something
