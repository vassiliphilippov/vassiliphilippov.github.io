Ixia

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪksiə / (AmE), / ˈɪksiə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the iris family, native to South Africa, known for their showy, colorful flowers.
genus of South African flowering plants

Examples

The garden was full of blooming Ixia in shades of pink and yellow.
She planted ixias to add vibrant color to her flower beds.
The delicate flowers of Ixia bloom in the spring and attract many pollinators.

Etymology

From Latin Ixia, from Greek ἰξία (ixia), a plant name.
