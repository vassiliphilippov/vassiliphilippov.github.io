Hexadrol

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛksəˌdrɑl / (AmE), / ˈhɛksəˌdrɒl / (BrE)
A brand name for dexamethasone, a corticosteroid drug used to treat allergies or inflammation.
brand name corticosteroid drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Hexadrol to reduce the patient's severe allergic reaction.
After her diagnosis, she was put on Hexadrol for a brief period.
Hexadrol is often chosen when a fast-acting corticosteroid is needed.
