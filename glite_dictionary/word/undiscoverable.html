undiscoverable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.dɪˈskʌv.ɚ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈskʌv.ər.ə.bəl / (BrE)
Impossible to find or ascertain; not able to be discovered.
impossible to discover

Examples

The pirate's treasure remains undiscoverable despite centuries of searching.
For ancient civilizations, some secrets are now undiscoverable due to lost records and erosion.
The true source of the rumor was undiscoverable by the reporters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undetectable - impossible to detect or find

Etymology

From un- (not) + discover + -able.
