aloha

1. NOUN

/ əˈloʊ.hɑ / (AmE), / əˈləʊ.hɑː / (BrE)
A Hawaiian word used as a greeting or farewell, meaning hello or goodbye.
Hawaiian greeting or farewell

Examples

He greeted everyone with a cheerful aloha as he entered the room.
As she left Hawaii, she waved and said aloha to her friends.
The word aloha captures both the spirit of greeting and farewell in Hawaiian culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ciao - Italian word used as a greeting and farewell

Etymology

From Hawaiian *aloha* (love, affection, greeting).
