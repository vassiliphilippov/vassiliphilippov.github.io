unagitated

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈædʒɪˌteɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈadʒɪteɪtɪd / (BrE)
not feeling or showing agitation; calm and not emotionally disturbed
calm; not emotionally agitated

Examples

Despite the chaos around her, she remained unagitated and focused on her work.
His unagitated response to the criticism impressed everyone in the meeting.
The waters of the lake were unagitated, reflecting the quiet of the early morning.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: agitated - feeling or appearing troubled or nervous
  • Synonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness, anger, or other strong emotions

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' and 'agitated'.
