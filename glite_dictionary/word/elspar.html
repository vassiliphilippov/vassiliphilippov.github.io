Elspar

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛlˌspɑr / (AmE), / ˈɛlˌspɑː / (BrE)
A trademarked antineoplastic drug sometimes used to treat lymphoblastic leukemia.
trademarked drug for leukemia
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Elspar as part of her chemotherapy regimen.
Some children with leukemia respond well to Elspar treatment.
Patients receiving Elspar are closely monitored for allergic reactions.
