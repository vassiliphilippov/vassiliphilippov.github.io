self-fulfillment

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlf fʊlˈfɪlmənt / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf fʊlˈfɪlmənt / (BrE)
The realization or achievement of one's own potential or abilities.
realization of one's potential

Examples

Many people seek self-fulfillment through creative expression and meaningful work.
Traveling the world was his way of achieving self-fulfillment.
She believes that true happiness comes from self-fulfillment, not external success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-actualization - the realization or fulfillment of one's talents and potentialities

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'fulfillment', originally from Middle English 'fulfyllen' (to fill up).
