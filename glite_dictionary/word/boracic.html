boracic

1. ADJ

/ bɔːˈræsɪk / (AmE), / bɒˈræsɪk / (BrE)
relating to, derived from, or containing boron, especially in the form of boric acid or its compounds.
containing or relating to boron

Examples

Many boracic compounds are used as antiseptics in medical applications.
They studied the properties of boracic acid crystals in the laboratory.
The glass contained a mixture of silica and boracic elements.

Synset Examples

boric acid

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boric - of or relating to boron or boric acid

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'boracicus', from 'borax', a mineral containing boron.
