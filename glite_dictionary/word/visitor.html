visitor

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪzɪtər / (AmE), / ˈvɪzɪtə / (BrE)
a person who visits a place or a person, especially socially, for a short time
person who visits a place or person

Examples

The visitor signed the guestbook at the entrance to the museum.
We are expecting several visitors from out of town this weekend.
Each visitor must wear a badge while inside the building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guest - a person who is invited to visit or stay

Etymology

From Latin 'visitare' meaning 'to go to see'.
