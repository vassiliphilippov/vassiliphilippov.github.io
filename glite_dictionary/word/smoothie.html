smoothie

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmuːði / (AmE), / ˈsmuːði / (BrE)
A thick, smooth drink made by blending fresh fruit with milk, yogurt, or ice cream.
thick blended fruit drink

Examples

She made a delicious smoothie with bananas, strawberries, and orange juice.
Every morning, I have a green smoothie after my workout.
The café offers a wide selection of smoothies made from fresh fruits and vegetables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milkshake - a sweet drink made of milk, ice cream, and flavorings mixed together

2. NOUN

/ ˈsmuði / (AmE), / ˈsmuːði / (BrE)
a person who is charming, confident, and often persuasive, sometimes in a superficial or insincere way
charming, confident person (often insincere)

Examples

Everyone at the party seemed taken in by the smoothie who was telling charming stories all night.
That smoothie at the office always knows just what to say to get on the boss's good side.
Though he came off as a smoothie, her instincts told her not to trust him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charmer - a person who is charming and pleasant
  • Synonym: flatterer - a person who insincerely praises others to gain favor
  • Antonym: braggart - a person who boasts about achievements or possessions

Etymology

Derived from 'smooth' (referring to manner or speech) plus '-ie', originally American English (mid-20th century).
