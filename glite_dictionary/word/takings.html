takings

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪkɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈteɪkɪŋz / (BrE)
the amount of money received from sales, business transactions, or events
money received from sales or business

Examples

The café's takings nearly doubled during the summer months.
At the end of the day, the shop owner counted the takings from the register.
Because of the rain, the takings at the fair were much lower than expected.

Synset Examples

the average return was about 5%

  • Synonym: earnings - money obtained in return for labor or services
