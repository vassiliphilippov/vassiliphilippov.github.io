astronautical

1. ADJ

/ ˌæstrəˈnɑːtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌæstrəˈnɔːtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to astronauts or the science and technology of astronautics (space travel and exploration)
of or relating to astronautics

Examples

The university offers a degree in astronautical engineering for those interested in space travel.
Her research focuses on astronautical technologies for deep space exploration.
He attends international conferences to discuss astronautical advancements and challenges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spaceflight - flight beyond the Earth's atmosphere

Etymology

From astronaut + -ical, modeled on aeronautical.
