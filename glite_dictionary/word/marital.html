marital

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɑːrɪt̬əl / (AmE), / ˈmɑːrɪtəl / (BrE)
relating to marriage or the relationship between a married couple
relating to marriage

Examples

The couple sought counseling to resolve their marital problems and strengthen their relationship.
Questions about marital status are common on official documents.
They celebrated their twentieth marital anniversary with a special trip.

Synset Examples

marital status
marital fidelity
married bliss

Related Terms

  • Synonym: matrimonial - relating to marriage or married people
  • Antonym: single - not married or not having a romantic partner

Etymology

From Latin 'maritalis', from 'maritus' (husband, married man).
