Romanist

1. ADJ

/ ˈroʊ.mə.nɪst / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.mə.nɪst / (BrE)
relating to, supporting, or characteristic of Roman Catholicism or the Roman Catholic Church (sometimes used in a pejorative or outdated sense).
relating to Roman Catholicism
archaic

Examples

Some scholars view the novel as having a distinctly Romanist perspective.
In the debate, his Romanist leanings became the subject of much criticism.
The law was enacted during a period of strong anti-Romanist sentiment.

Synset Examples

the Roman Catholic Church

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Catholic - of or relating to the Roman Catholic Church

Etymology

From Latin Romanus (Roman) + English -ist (denoting adherence).
