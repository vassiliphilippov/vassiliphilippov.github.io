planer

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪnər / (AmE), / ˈpleɪnə / (BrE)
a power tool used for smoothing or shaping the surface of wood
a power tool for smoothing wood

Examples

The carpenter used a planer to achieve a perfectly smooth tabletop.
Modern workshops often have an electric planer for finishing large boards quickly.
He replaced the worn blades on his planer before starting the new project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plane - a hand tool for smoothing wood surfaces

Etymology

From 'plane' (tool for smoothing surfaces) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
