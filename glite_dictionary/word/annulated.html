annulated

1. ADJ

/ ˈæn.jəˌleɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈan.jʊ.leɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
shaped like or having the form of a ring; ring-shaped.
shaped like a ring

Examples

The worm's annulated body reflects its segmented structure.
Several mushrooms have an annulated stem, which helps with identification.
The fossil revealed an annulated pattern along its shell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ring-shaped - having the form of a ring

Etymology

From Latin 'annulatus', from 'annulus' meaning 'ring'.
