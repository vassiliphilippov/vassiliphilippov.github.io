sanative

1. ADJ

/ ˈsæn.ə.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈsæn.ət.ɪv / (BrE)
having the power to cure disease or restore health; healing.
having healing or curative power

Examples

The cool mountain air created a sanative environment for those recovering from illness.
Many cultures have long believed in the sanative properties of mineral springs.
After his operation, he found the countryside retreat both peaceful and sanative.

Synset Examples

curative powers of herbal remedies
her gentle healing hand
remedial surgery
a sanative environment of mountains and fresh air
a therapeutic agent
therapeutic diets

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curative - having healing or curing properties
  • Synonym: therapeutic - having medicinal or healing properties
  • Synonym: remedial - intended to cure or correct, especially in health

Etymology

From Latin 'sanativus' (healing), from 'sanare' (to heal).
