Aspidiotus

1. NOUN

/ æˌspɪdiˈoʊtəs / (AmE), / æˌspɪdɪˈəʊtəs / (BrE)
A genus of scale insects in the family Diaspididae, some species of which are pests of agricultural crops.
genus of scale insects
medical

Examples

The citrus crop was damaged by an infestation of Aspidiotus scale insects.
Researchers are studying various species within the Aspidiotus genus for better pest control.
The armored appearance of Aspidiotus makes them difficult to eradicate.

Etymology

From Greek aspís (shield) and the New Latin suffix -otus, referring to the shield-like covering of the insects.
