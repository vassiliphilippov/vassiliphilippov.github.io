forty-third

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɔrtiˈθɜrd / (AmE), / ˌfɔːtiˈθɜːd / (BrE)
The ordinal number corresponding to forty-three (43rd) in a sequence; being number forty-three in order.
ordinal number: forty-three (43rd)

Examples

She finished in forty-third place out of a hundred participants.
This is the forty-third time we have held the annual festival.
The forty-third chapter of the book covers advanced topics.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: forty-fourth - ordinal number: forty-four (44th)
