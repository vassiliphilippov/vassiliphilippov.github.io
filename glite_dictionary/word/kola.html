Kola

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊlə / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlə / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in parts of Cameroon, especially south of Lake Chad
Chadic language of Cameroon

Examples

The Kola language is one of many spoken in northern Cameroon.
Linguists have documented several dialects of Kola in the Mayo-Louti division.
Efforts are underway to preserve the Kola language for future generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chadic - relating to the group of languages of the Chadic branch of the Afroasiatic family
