leptorhine

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛptəˌraɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɛptəˌraɪn / (BrE)
(of a nose) having a long, narrow shape; relating to or characterized by a long, narrow nose.
having a long, narrow nose
medical

Examples

The patient was noted to have a leptorhine nose, typical of certain ancestral groups.
Anthropologists classified the skull as leptorhine based on its nasal index.
In her portrait, her delicate, leptorhine features are clearly visible.

Etymology

From Greek 'leptos' (slender) + 'rhis, rhinos' (nose).
