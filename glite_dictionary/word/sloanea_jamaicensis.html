Sloanea jamaicensis

1. NOUN

/ sloʊˈæniə dʒəˌmeɪkɛnˈsɪs / (AmE), / sləʊˈeɪniə dʒəˌmeɪkɛnˈsɪs / (BrE)
A species of West Indian timber tree known for its very hard wood.
West Indian timber tree species

Examples

The wood of Sloanea jamaicensis is prized for its exceptional hardness and durability.
Many old houses in the Caribbean feature beams made from Sloanea jamaicensis.
Foresters are concerned about the declining population of Sloanea jamaicensis due to overharvesting.
