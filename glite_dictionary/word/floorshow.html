floorshow

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɔrˌʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈflɔːʃəʊ / (BrE)
a series of entertainment acts, such as music, dancing, or comedy, performed for an audience in a nightclub or restaurant, typically while food or drinks are served
nightclub entertainment performance

Examples

The club's floorshow included a mesmerizing dance routine and a jazz band.
They stayed for dinner and enjoyed the late-night floorshow at the lounge.
Every Saturday, the casino hosts a new floorshow with comedians and singers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cabaret - a performance featuring music, dance, or drama in a restaurant or nightclub
