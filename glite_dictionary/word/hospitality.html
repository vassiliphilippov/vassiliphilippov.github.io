hospitality

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɑːspɪˈtæləti / (AmE), / ˌhɒspɪˈtælɪti / (BrE)
the friendly and generous reception and entertainment of guests, visitors, or strangers
friendliness to guests or strangers

Examples

We were overwhelmed by the hospitality shown to us during our visit.
The country is famous for its warm hospitality toward tourists.
Her hospitality made everyone feel welcome in her home.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hostility - unfriendliness or opposition
  • Synonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly

Etymology

From Latin hospitalitas (friendliness to guests), from hospitalis (of a guest), from hospes (guest, host).
