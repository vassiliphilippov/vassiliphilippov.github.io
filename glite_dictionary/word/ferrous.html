ferrous

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɛr.əs / (AmE), / ˈfɛr.əs / (BrE)
relating to or containing iron, especially with iron in the +2 oxidation state.
containing or relating to iron

Examples

The ferrous content of the mineral sample was measured in the laboratory.
Many steel alloys are classified as ferrous metals because they contain iron.
Rust forms when ferrous materials are exposed to moisture and oxygen over time.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ferric - relating to or containing iron with a valence of +3

Etymology

From Latin 'ferrum' (iron) + '-ous' (having the qualities of).
