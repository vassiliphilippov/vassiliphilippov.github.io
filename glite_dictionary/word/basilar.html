basilar

1. ADJ

/ ˈbæsɪlər / (AmE), / ˈbæsɪlə / (BrE)
relating to or situated at the base of a structure, especially in anatomy.
relating to the base, especially in anatomy
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that the injury was near the basilar part of the skull.
We studied the basilar membrane of the cochlea in detail.
A blood clot in the basilar artery can have serious consequences.

Synset Examples

the basilar membrane of the cochlea

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basal - forming or belonging to a bottom layer or base

Etymology

From Late Latin basilaris, from Latin basis (base).
