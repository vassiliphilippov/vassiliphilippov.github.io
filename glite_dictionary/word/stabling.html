stabling

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsteɪblɪŋ / (BrE)
Accommodation or buildings, such as stables, provided for animals, especially horses.
accommodation for animals, especially horses

Examples

The estate offers excellent stabling for thirty horses.
Many horse owners seek quality stabling when traveling to competitions.
She invested in new stabling facilities to improve the animals' comfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stable - building for housing horses or other animals

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'stable' (to put or keep animals in a stable), plus the -ing suffix.
