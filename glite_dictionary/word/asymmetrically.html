asymmetrically

1. ADV

/ ˌæsɪˈmɛtrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌasɪˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is not symmetrical; unevenly or irregularly on either side of a dividing line or plane.
in an uneven or non-symmetrical manner

Examples

The paintings were asymmetrically placed on the gallery wall for a modern look.
Leaves often grow asymmetrically due to varying sunlight exposure.
The architect designed the building asymmetrically, giving it a unique silhouette.

Synset Examples

they were asymmetrically arranged

Related Terms

  • Antonym: symmetrically - in a symmetrical manner

Etymology

From 'asymmetrical' + '-ly', where 'asymmetrical' comes from Greek 'a-' (not) + 'symmetros' (having common measure).
