sinistrorsal

1. ADJ

/ sɪˌnɪstrɔrˈsæl / (AmE), / sɪˌnɪstrɔːˈsæl / (BrE)
(Of a spiral or coiling) spiraling upward from right to left; left-handed spiraling, as seen in certain plant vines or shells.
spiraling upward right to left

Examples

The morning glory is a sinistrorsal vine, twining from right to left as it climbs.
Scientists noted the sinistrorsal pattern in the shell's growth.
Some climbing plants exhibit sinistrorsal coiling, contrasting with species that spiral in the opposite direction.

Synset Examples

sinistrorse vines

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dextral - spiraling upward from left to right

Etymology

From Latin 'sinister' (left) + '-al' (forming adjectives).
