mussiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌs.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.i.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being messy, untidy, or disordered
state of being messy or untidy

Examples

The mussiness of his desk made it hard to find anything quickly.
Despite the mussiness in the kitchen, she managed to cook a wonderful meal.
He apologized for the mussiness of his apartment before inviting guests in.

Synset Examples

the house was a mess
she smoothed the mussiness of the bed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: messiness - the quality of being messy or disordered

Etymology

From 'mussy' (messy or untidy) + '-ness' (state or quality).
