obligingly

1. ADV

/ əˈblaɪdʒɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈblɪdʒɪŋli / (BrE)
In a helpful and willing manner; accommodatingly.
in a helpful or accommodating way

Examples

She obligingly offered to carry my bags up the stairs.
The staff obligingly stayed late to help finish the project.
He obligingly stood aside to let her pass.

Synset Examples

obligingly, he lowered his voice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helpfully - in a helpful manner
  • Antonym: reluctantly - in an unwilling or hesitant manner
