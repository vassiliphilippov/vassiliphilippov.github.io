erectly

1. ADV

/ ɪˈrɛktli / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛktli / (BrE)
in an upright or straight-backed manner
in a straight or upright manner

Examples

She always sits erectly at her desk, never slouching.
The soldiers stood erectly during the entire ceremony.
Despite his age, the professor walked erectly across the campus.

Synset Examples

the old man still walks erectly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uprightly - in an upright manner

Etymology

From erect + -ly.
