recognized

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛkəɡˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈrɛkəɡnʌɪzd / (BrE)
Known and accepted as being important, respected, or having a particular status or reputation.
known or accepted as important or reputable

Examples

Dr. Patel is a recognized expert in cancer research.
The school is a recognized leader in environmental studies.
She quickly became a recognized authority in her field.

Synset Examples

a recognized authority

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acknowledged - accepted or admitted as being true or important

Etymology

From the past participle of 'recognize'.
