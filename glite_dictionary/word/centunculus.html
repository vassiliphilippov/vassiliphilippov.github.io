Centunculus

1. NOUN

/ sɛnˈtʌŋkjʊləs / (AmE), / sɛnˈtʌŋkjʊləs / (BrE)
A small genus of flowering plants in the family Primulaceae, commonly known as threadworts.
genus of flowering plants (Primulaceae)

Examples

The rare Centunculus species was discovered in the marshy meadow.
Researchers examined the distribution of Centunculus across various habitats.
Botanists were excited to add a new specimen of Centunculus to the herbarium.

Etymology

From Latin *centunculus* (a patchwork garment), referring to the plant's appearance.
