sarcolemmal

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɑr.kəˈlɛm.əl / (AmE), / ˌsɑː.kəˈlɛm.əl / (BrE)
relating to the sarcolemma, the thin membranous sheath that surrounds a muscle fiber
relating to a muscle cell membrane
medical

Examples

Damage to the sarcolemmal membrane can impair muscle function.
Certain diseases are characterized by sarcolemmal abnormalities in skeletal muscle.
Researchers studied the sarcolemmal response to repeated exercise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sarcolemma - the fine membrane surrounding a muscle fiber

Etymology

From 'sarcolemma' (the sheath enveloping a muscle fiber) + '-al' (forming adjectives)
