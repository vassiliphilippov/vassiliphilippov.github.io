multimedia

1. NOUN

/ ˌmʌltiˈmiːdiə / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈmiːdiə / (BrE)
the use or integration of different forms of communication, such as text, images, sound, and video, especially in computer applications or presentations
use of multiple media types together

Examples

The museum's new exhibition uses multimedia to create an interactive experience for visitors.
Teachers are increasingly using multimedia in the classroom to engage students.
The conference featured a multimedia presentation that combined video, music, and animation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hypermedia - media that includes graphics, audio, video, plain text, and hyperlinks

Etymology

From Latin multi- (many) + media (plural of medium, meaning means of communication).
