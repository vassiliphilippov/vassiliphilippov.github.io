accessorial

1. ADJ

/ æk.səˈsɔːr.i.əl / (AmE), / ˌæk.sɛˈsɔː.ri.əl / (BrE)
providing additional help or support but not essential; supplementary.
supplementary; providing added help

Examples

The moving company charged extra for accessorial services like packing and unpacking.
Many contracts specify which accessorial charges may be applied beyond the main fee.
Sorting was categorized as an accessorial service during the shipment process.

Synset Examples

accessorial services included sorting and packing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accessory - something added to help or supplement but not essential

Etymology

From Medieval Latin accessorius (accessory) + -al (forming adjectives).
