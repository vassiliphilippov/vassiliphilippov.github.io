attainable

1. ADJ

/ əˈteɪnəbl̩ / (AmE), / əˈteɪnəbl̩ / (BrE)
Able to be achieved or reached; possible to accomplish.
able to be achieved or reached

Examples

She set herself an attainable target for the next quarter.
The company focuses on attainable goals to keep employees motivated.
Everyone agreed that perfection was not attainable, but improvement was possible.

Synset Examples

choose an attainable goal
art is not something that is come-at-able by dint of study

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unattainable - not able to be achieved or reached
  • Synonym: achievable - possible to achieve

Etymology

From Latin 'attingere' (to reach, achieve) via Old French 'atteignable'.
