observably

1. ADV

/ əbˈzɝːvəbli / (AmE), / əbˈzɜːvəbli / (BrE)
In a way that can be observed or noticed; in a noticeable manner.
in a way that can be observed

Examples

The patient's condition has observably improved since starting the new medication.
The climate in the region has become observably warmer over the past decade.
His mood became observably brighter as the meeting progressed.

Synset Examples

he changed noticeably over the years

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noticeably - in a noticeable or distinct manner

Etymology

From 'observable' + '-ly'; 'observable' from Latin 'observare' (to watch, to observe).
