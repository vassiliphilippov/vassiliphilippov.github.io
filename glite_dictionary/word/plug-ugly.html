plug-ugly

1. NOUN

/ plʌɡ ˈʌɡli / (AmE), / plʌɡ ˈʌɡli / (BrE)
a violent or aggressive person, especially one who bullies weaker people; often used historically to describe gang members or thugs
violent bully or thug

Examples

The notorious plug-ugly intimidated everyone in the neighborhood with his threats.
In the late 1800s, plug-uglies often worked as enforcers for political gangs.
Nobody dared to stand up to the plug-ugly who bullied children on their way home from school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bully - a person who habitually seeks to harm or intimidate those whom they perceive as vulnerable
  • Synonym: thug - a violent person, especially a criminal

Etymology

Originally American slang, possibly derived from the rough appearance ('ugly') and the idea of 'plug' meaning to hit.
