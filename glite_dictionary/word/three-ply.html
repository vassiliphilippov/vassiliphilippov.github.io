three-ply

1. ADJ

/ ˈθriˌplaɪ / (AmE), / ˈθriːˌplaɪ / (BrE)
(of a material) having a thickness, structure, or composition made up of three layers or strands.
consisting of three layers or strands

Examples

The cable is made from three-ply wire for extra strength and durability.
She bought three-ply toilet paper for added softness and absorbency.
Using three-ply yarn will make the sweater warmer and more durable.

Synset Examples

three-ply cloth
three-ply yarn

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: double-ply - consisting of two layers or strands
  • Degree Lower: single-ply - consisting of one layer or strand
  • Degree Higher: four-ply - consisting of four layers or strands
