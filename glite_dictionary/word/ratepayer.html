ratepayer

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪtˌpeɪər / (AmE), / ˈreɪtˌpeɪə / (BrE)
a person who pays local taxes or rates, especially as the owner or occupier of a property
person who pays local taxes

Examples

The local council proposed increased services for every ratepayer in the district.
As a responsible ratepayer, he attended the annual town meeting to voice his concerns.
Discounts were announced for pensioners who are also ratepayers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxpayer - a person who pays taxes to a government

Etymology

From 'rate' (a tax assessed locally) + 'payer'.
