fancam

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæn.kæm / (AmE), / ˈfan.kam / (BrE)
a video recording of a celebrity, performer, or public figure filmed by a fan, especially at an event or concert
fan-shot video of a celebrity

Examples

The K-pop group's performance was shared online through multiple fancams shot from different angles.
Her fancam of the actor at the premiere quickly went viral on social media.
Many fans recorded a fancam to capture their favorite singer's dance moves during the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fanvid - a fan-made music video using footage or images of a celebrity or fictional character

Etymology

Blend of 'fan' (enthusiast) and 'cam' (camera).
