daisybush

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪziˌbʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪzɪbʊʃ / (BrE)
Any of various evergreen shrubs of the genus Olearia, mostly native to Australia, grown for their attractive foliage and abundant daisy-like flowers with white, purple, or blue rays.
Australian shrub with daisy-like flowers

Examples

The daisybush is a popular choice for gardeners seeking evergreen shrubs with vibrant blooms.
Several daisybushes lined the path, their purple flowers brightening the winter landscape.
She planted a daisybush in her backyard for its year-round foliage and profusion of blossoms.
