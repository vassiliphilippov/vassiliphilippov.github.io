Martes martes

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑːrtiːz ˈmɑːrtiːz / (AmE), / ˈmɑːtiːz ˈmɑːtiːz / (BrE)
A dark brown marten native to northern Eurasian coniferous forests; also known as the European pine marten.
species of European pine marten

Examples

Martes martes is found primarily in mature coniferous forests throughout northern Europe.
Recent studies show that Martes martes populations have been increasing thanks to conservation efforts.
The diet of Martes martes includes small mammals, birds, and fruits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pine marten - a European marten species with brown fur

Etymology

From Latin 'martes' (marten), used in zoological nomenclature.
