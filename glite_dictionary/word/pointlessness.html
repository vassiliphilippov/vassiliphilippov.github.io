pointlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔɪntləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪntlɪsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having no purpose, meaning, or usefulness
lack of purpose or meaning

Examples

He questioned the pointlessness of continuing a project that had little chance of success.
A feeling of pointlessness swept over her as she realized nothing would change.
Some critics commented on the pointlessness of the film’s plot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meaninglessness - the quality of lacking meaning or significance
  • Antonym: purposefulness - the quality of having a clear aim or purpose

Etymology

From 'pointless' + '-ness'. 'Pointless' from 'point' (purpose) + '-less' (without).
