anthropogenetic

1. ADJ

/ ˌænθrəpoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌanθrəpəʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study of the origins and development of human beings
study of human origins and development

Examples

She specializes in anthropogenetic research, focusing on human evolution.
The anthropogenetic factors of disease are a major topic in medical anthropology.
His lecture covered the anthropogenetic origins of cultural diversity.

Etymology

From Greek 'anthrōpos' (human) + 'genesis' (origin, creation) + '-etic' (relating to).
