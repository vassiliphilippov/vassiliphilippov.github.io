MC

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛmˈsi / (AmE), / ˌɛmˈsiː / (BrE)
a person who acts as the host at a formal occasion, giving introductory speeches and introducing other speakers or performers
host at formal events; master of ceremonies

Examples

The MC welcomed everyone to the charity gala and introduced the keynote speakers.
As MC of the event, she kept the program running smoothly and entertained the guests.
The awards ceremony's MC was known for his wit and engaging stage presence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: host - a person who receives or entertains guests
  • Synonym: compare - a person who introduces other performers on stage, especially in British English

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'master of ceremonies'.
