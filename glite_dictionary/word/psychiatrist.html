psychiatrist

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈkaɪəˌtrɪst / (AmE), / saɪˈkaɪətrɪst / (BrE)
a medical doctor who specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of mental illnesses
doctor specializing in mental health
medical

Examples

After years of struggling with anxiety, she decided to see a psychiatrist.
The psychiatrist prescribed him medication to help manage his symptoms.
He was referred to a psychiatrist for further evaluation and support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychologist - a specialist in psychology, not always a medical doctor
  • Antonym: patient - a person receiving medical treatment

Etymology

From Greek 'psukhē' (soul, mind) and 'iatros' (physician), via French 'psychiatre'.
