sunbather

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌnˌbeɪðər / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌbeɪðə / (BrE)
a person who lies or sits in the sun, especially to get a suntan
person who sunbathes

Examples

Dozens of sunbathers stretched out on the sandy beach, enjoying the warm afternoon.
The park's grassy hill was dotted with sunbathers on a sunny Saturday morning.
As a dedicated sunbather, she always reserved the best spot by the pool.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sunbathe - to lie or sit in the sun, especially to get a suntan

Etymology

Formed from 'sun' and 'bathe', meaning to expose oneself to sunlight
