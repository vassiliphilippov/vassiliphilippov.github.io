short-stop

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɔrt ˌstɑp / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt ˌstɒp / (BrE)
A solution, typically an acid bath, used in photography to halt the action of a photographic developer.
acid bath to stop photographic developer

Examples

After developing the film, immerse it in the short-stop to halt the chemical reaction.
The photographer prepared a fresh short-stop before starting the printing process.
You should never skip the short-stop step if you want consistent photographic results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stop bath - an acid solution used to stop photographic development
