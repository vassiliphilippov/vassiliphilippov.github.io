dyadic

1. ADJ

/ daɪˈædɪk / (AmE), / daɪˈadɪk / (BrE)
relating to or based on a dyad; involving two parts or people.
relating to a pair or two elements

Examples

The therapist focused on the dyadic interaction between the parent and the child.
Many biological processes occur through dyadic exchanges at the cellular level.
Their study explored dyadic relationships in romantic partnerships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dyad - a pair; something that consists of two elements or parts

Etymology

From Greek dyas (group of two) via Late Latin dyadicus.
