Harold Kroto

1. PROPN

/ ˈhærəld ˈkroʊtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhæɹ.əld ˈkrəʊ.təʊ / (BrE)
(1939–2016) A British chemist who, along with Robert Curl and Richard Smalley, discovered fullerenes and helped pioneer a new field in chemistry.
British chemist and fullerene discoverer
british

Examples

Harold Kroto was awarded the Nobel Prize in Chemistry for his work on fullerenes.
Many students learn about Harold Kroto when studying advances in carbon chemistry.
The discovery by Harold Kroto transformed the scientific understanding of molecular carbon.
