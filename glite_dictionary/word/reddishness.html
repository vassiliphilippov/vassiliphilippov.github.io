reddishness

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdɪʃnɪs / (AmE), / ˈrɛdɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or condition of being somewhat red in color.
the quality of being somewhat red

Examples

The reddishness of the sunset filled the sky with warmth.
She noticed a reddishness developing on her cheeks after being in the sun.
Mars was chosen for its strong reddishness that stands out among the planets.

Synset Examples

Embrace the reddishness of Marsala and pair it with greens and golds for holiday wrapping, cards, or other gifts.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: redness - the quality of being red

Etymology

From 'reddish' + '-ness', making an abstract noun from the adjective.
