underside

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌndərˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈʌndəsaɪd / (BrE)
the lower side or surface of something
the lower surface of something

Examples

The underside of the table was covered in dust.
A pale color marks the underside of the gecko's tail.
Always inspect the underside of leaves for signs of pests.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: topside - the upper side or surface of something
