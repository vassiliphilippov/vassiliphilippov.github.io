boohoo

1. NOUN

/ buːˈhuː / (AmE), / buːˈhuː / (BrE)
an exaggerated show of emotion, especially sadness or complaint, often made to gain sympathy
a show of exaggerated sadness or complaint
informal

Examples

Stop with the boohoo; it's just a minor setback.
No one wants to hear your boohoo about all the extra homework.
He made a big boohoo over missing the train, but it wasn't a big deal.

Synset Examples

Stop with the boohoo; it's just a minor setback.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whining - the act of continually complaining in an annoying way
  • Synonym: complaint - the act of expressing dissatisfaction or unhappiness about something
