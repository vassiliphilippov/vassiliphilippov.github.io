Gulo gulo

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːloʊ ˈɡuːloʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡuːləʊ ˈɡuːləʊ / (BrE)
The scientific name for the wolverine, a large musteline mammal native to northern Eurasia and North America.
scientific name for the wolverine

Examples

Scientists observed a Gulo gulo hunting in the taiga forest of northern Russia.
The Gulo gulo is known for its strength and ferocious appetite despite its size.
Biologists track the movements of Gulo gulo to study its habitat preferences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wolverine - A large, muscular carnivorous mammal also known as Gulo gulo

Etymology

From Latin, meaning 'glutton, greedy' (repeated for species and genus).
