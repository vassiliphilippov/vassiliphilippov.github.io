prurience

1. NOUN

/ ˈprʊriəns / (AmE), / ˈprʊəriəns / (BrE)
an excessive or morbid interest in sexual matters, especially one considered improper or lewd
excessive sexual interest; lewdness

Examples

The novel was criticized for its prurience and graphic descriptions.
She dismissed the tabloids' prurience as an invasion of privacy.
Despite the film's critical acclaim, some viewers objected to its apparent prurience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lewdness - the quality of being sexually indecent or offensive
  • Antonym: chastity - the state or practice of refraining from extramarital, or especially from all, sexual intercourse

Etymology

From Latin 'prurientia' (itching, longing), from 'prurire' (to itch, to long for).
