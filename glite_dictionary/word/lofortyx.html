Lofortyx

1. NOUN

/ loʊˈfɔːrtɪks / (AmE), / ləˈfɔːtɪks / (BrE)
A former genus name for the California quail, a bird native to the western United States.
former genus for California quail

Examples

The Lofortyx is commonly known as the California quail, a bird native to western North America.
Researchers once classified the California quail under the genus Lofortyx.
Birdwatchers in California often seek out the Lofortyx in oak woodlands and chaparral.
