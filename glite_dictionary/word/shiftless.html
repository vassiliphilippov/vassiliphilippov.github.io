shiftless

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɪftləs / (AmE), / ˈʃɪftləs / (BrE)
lacking ambition, energy, or initiative; lazy and unwilling to make any effort
lacking ambition or initiative

Examples

Despite his intelligence, Mark became a shiftless drifter after graduation.
Many employers avoid hiring shiftless workers who show no motivation or drive.
Her shiftless attitude was apparent when she failed to meet any deadlines.

Synset Examples

a shiftless student
studied in a shiftless way
shiftless idle youth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lazy - unwilling to work or use energy
  • Antonym: industrious - diligent and hardworking

Etymology

From 'shift' (resourcefulness or means) + '-less' (without); dating from the 17th century.
