blender

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɛndɚ / (AmE), / ˈblɛndə / (BrE)
An electric kitchen appliance with rotating blades used for mixing, chopping, or puréeing food and liquids.
electric kitchen appliance for mixing or puréeing

Examples

She used her new blender to make a fresh fruit smoothie for breakfast.
Please wash the blender thoroughly after making the soup.
The recipe says to combine the ingredients in a blender until smooth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: food processor - kitchen appliance for slicing, chopping, or mixing food

Etymology

From English 'blend' (to mix smoothly) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
