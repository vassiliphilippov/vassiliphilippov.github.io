phenomenally

1. ADV

/ fəˈnɑːmənəli / (AmE), / fəˈnɒmɪnəli / (BrE)
To a remarkable or exceptional degree; extraordinarily.
extraordinarily; to an exceptional degree

Examples

The team's performance was phenomenally impressive during the final match.
She recovered from her illness phenomenally fast, astonishing the doctors.
The new product sold phenomenally well in its first week on the market.

Synset Examples

his reaction was phenomenally quick

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extraordinarily - to an exceptional degree; remarkably

Etymology

From phenomenal + -ly; 'phenomenal' from Greek phainomenon (appearance).
