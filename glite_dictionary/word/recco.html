recco

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛk.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈrɛk.əʊ / (BrE)
(informal) A reconnaissance mission or a scouting survey, especially in a military context; shortened form of 'reconnaissance'.
shortened form of 'reconnaissance' (military scouting)
informal

Examples

The unit went on a recco early this morning before moving into enemy territory.
We did a quick recco of the area before setting up camp for the night.
His job was to handle reccos and report back with critical information.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reconnaissance - a survey or exploration, especially for military information
  • Synonym: recon - shortened form of 'reconnaissance', military scouting

Etymology

Shortening of 'reconnaissance.'
