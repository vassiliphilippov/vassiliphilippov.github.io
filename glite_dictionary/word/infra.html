infra

1. ADV

/ ˈɪn.frə / (AmE), / ˈɪn.frə / (BrE)
(in writing) at a later point in the text; below.
at a later place in the text; below
legal

Examples

The definition of the term is provided infra in the text.
Refer to the discussion of exceptions infra for more details.
The court's conclusion, discussed infra, clarifies this point.

Synset Examples

see below

Related Terms

  • Antonym: supra - at an earlier place in the text; above

Etymology

From Latin 'infra' meaning 'below'.
