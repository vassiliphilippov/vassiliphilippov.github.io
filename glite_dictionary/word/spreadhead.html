spreadhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈsprɛdˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈsprɛdˌhɛd / (BrE)
the two facing pages of a book or other publication when it is open
two facing pages of a publication

Examples

The designer carefully arranged the photographs across the spreadhead for maximum visual impact.
Each spreadhead in the magazine offers a new feature article to capture readers' interest.
The table of contents included a thumbnail of every spreadhead in the book.
