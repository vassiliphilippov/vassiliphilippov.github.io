barebacked

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛrˌbækt / (AmE), / ˈbeəˌbækt / (BrE)

Examples

/ ˈbɛrˌbækt / (AmE), / ˈbeəˌbækt / (BrE)
(of riding a horse) without using a saddle.
riding a horse without a saddle

Examples

She prefers to ride her horse barebacked, feeling more connected to the animal.
Many stunt riders perform amazing tricks while riding barebacked.
He learned to balance himself by practicing barebacked horseback riding.
