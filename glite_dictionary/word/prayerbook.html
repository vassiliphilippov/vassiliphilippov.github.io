prayerbook

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛrˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈpreəˌbʊk / (BrE)
A book containing a collection of prayers, especially those used in religious worship.
book of prayers for worship

Examples

She opened her prayerbook and began to recite the morning prayer.
The church distributed a new prayerbook to each family during the service.
He inherited an old prayerbook from his grandmother.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hymnal - a book of hymns or religious songs
  • Synonym: missal - a book containing the texts for the celebration of Mass in the Catholic Church
