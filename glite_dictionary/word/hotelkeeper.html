hotelkeeper

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈtɛlˌkiːpər / (AmE), / həʊˈtɛlˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person who owns or manages a hotel
owner or manager of a hotel

Examples

The hotelkeeper greeted us warmly at the entrance and showed us to our room.
As a hotelkeeper, she was responsible for every detail of her guests' comfort.
The experienced hotelkeeper managed the staff efficiently during the busy holiday season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: innkeeper - person who owns or manages an inn

Etymology

From 'hotel' (from French hôtel) + 'keeper' (from Middle English kepere).
