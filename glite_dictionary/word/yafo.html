Yafo

1. PROPN

/ ˈjɑːfoʊ / (AmE), / ˈjɑːfəʊ / (BrE)
a port city in western Israel on the Mediterranean, now part of Tel Aviv since 1950
port city in western Israel

Examples

The ancient port of Yafo is famous for its historic buildings and narrow alleyways.
Many visitors to Tel Aviv take a stroll along the old streets of Yafo.
After its incorporation in 1950, Yafo became a vibrant cultural part of Tel Aviv.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jaffa - alternative name for Yafo, especially in English usage

Etymology

From Hebrew יָפו (Yāfō), the original name of the ancient port city.
