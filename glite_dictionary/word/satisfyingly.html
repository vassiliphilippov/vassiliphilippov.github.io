satisfyingly

1. ADV

/ ˈsæt̬.ɪs.faɪ.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈsæt.ɪs.faɪ.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that gives satisfaction or pleasure.
in a pleasing or gratifying manner

Examples

The cake was satisfyingly rich and moist, making it the highlight of the meal.
He completed the puzzle satisfyingly quickly, impressing everyone watching.
The solution to the problem was satisfyingly simple and effective.

Synset Examples

the performance was at a gratifyingly high level

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gratifyingly - in a pleasing or satisfying manner

Etymology

From "satisfying" + "-ly".
