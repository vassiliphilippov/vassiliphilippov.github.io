prevalent

1. ADJ

/ ˈprɛvələnt / (AmE), / ˈprɛvələnt / (BrE)
widespread or commonly occurring; existing or found over a large area at a particular time.
widespread; commonly occurring

Examples

These beliefs are still prevalent in many rural areas.
Smartphones have become increasingly prevalent among teenagers in recent years.
The flu was especially prevalent during the winter months.

Synset Examples

prevailing winds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: common - occurring frequently or widespread; not rare
  • Antonym: rare - not occurring often; uncommon

Etymology

From Latin 'praevalent-', from 'praevalere' meaning 'have greater power'.
