unwelcome

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈwɛlkəm / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛlkəm / (BrE)
not wanted or received with pleasure; not welcome.
not wanted or not welcome

Examples

The news of the delay was completely unwelcome to the project team.
She received an unwelcome visitor at her door late at night.
His comments brought unwelcome attention to the sensitive issue.

Synset Examples

unwelcome publicity

Related Terms

  • Antonym: welcome - gladly received or accepted

Etymology

From un- (not) + welcome (Old English wilcuma).

WordNet ID: oewn-02539553-s

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈwɛlkəm / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛlkəm / (BrE)
Not wanted, not gladly received, or causing displeasure.
not wanted or causing displeasure

Examples

The sudden storm was an unwelcome surprise for beachgoers.
She found the criticism from her colleague to be particularly unwelcome.
Rising energy prices bring an unwelcome burden to many families.

Synset Examples

unwelcome publicity
unwelcome interruptions
unwelcome visitors

Related Terms

  • Antonym: welcome - received with pleasure or approval
  • Synonym: unwanted - not desired or needed
