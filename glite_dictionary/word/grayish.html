grayish

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪɪʃ / (BrE)
having a color that is somewhat or slightly gray; not fully gray but tinged with gray.
slightly gray in color

Examples

The sky looked grayish as the storm approached.
She wore a grayish sweater that blended well with the misty morning.
His hair has turned grayish over the years.

Synset Examples

the little grey cells
gray flannel suit
a man with greyish hair

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: gray - the color between black and white; fully gray
