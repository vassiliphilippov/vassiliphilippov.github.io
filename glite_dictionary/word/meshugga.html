meshugga

1. ADJ

/ məˈʃʊɡə / (AmE), / məˈʃʊɡə / (BrE)
(informal) Senseless; crazy; behaving in a foolish or irrational way.
senseless; crazy
informal

Examples

My uncle is a little meshugga when it comes to conspiracy theories.
You must be meshugga to try swimming in that icy river.
The whole plan sounded completely meshugga to me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged or extremely foolish

Etymology

From Yiddish meshugge (crazy).
