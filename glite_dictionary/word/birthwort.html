birthwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɜrθˌwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθˌwɜːt / (BrE)
A creeping plant of the genus Aristolochia, with tubular or curved flowers, formerly believed to aid childbirth.
plant with curved, tubular flowers

Examples

The herbalist recommended birthwort for its supposed medicinal properties.
We found a patch of birthwort growing along the woodland path.
Birthwort flowers are known for their unusual, curved shape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aristolochia - genus of plants including birthwort

Etymology

From 'birth' + Middle English 'wort' (plant, herb).
