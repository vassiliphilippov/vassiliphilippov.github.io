Codiaeum

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈdiː.i.əm / (AmE), / kəˈdiː.ɪəm / (BrE)
A genus of evergreen tropical trees and shrubs with thick, colorful, leathery leaves, native to Malaya and the Pacific islands.
genus of tropical trees and shrubs

Examples

The Codiaeum in the greenhouse display a remarkable variety of colorful foliage.
Gardeners often grow Codiaeum species indoors for their decorative leaves.
Experts recommend well-drained soil when planting a new Codiaeum.

Etymology

From modern Latin, based on Greek kōdiaion (little fleece), referring to the texture of some plant parts.
