thespian

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɛspiən / (AmE), / ˈθɛspiən / (BrE)
an actor or actress; someone who performs in plays or other theatrical works
theatrical performer; actor or actress

Examples

The young thespian captivated the audience with her moving performance.
Many aspiring thespians dream of performing on Broadway one day.
As a dedicated thespian, he spends hours rehearsing for each play.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: actor - a person who performs in plays, movies, or television shows

Etymology

From Greek Thespis, traditionally regarded as the first actor in Greek drama.
