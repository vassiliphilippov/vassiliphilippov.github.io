gerardia

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈrɑːrdiə / (AmE), / dʒəˈrɑːdɪə / (BrE)
any plant of the genus Gerardia, a group of flowering plants in the broomrape family
flowering plant of the genus Gerardia

Examples

The meadow was dotted with bright gerardia blossoms in late summer.
Botanists collected several specimens of gerardia for their research.
Some species of gerardia are noted for their delicate purple or yellow flowers.

Etymology

Named after John Gerard (1545–1612), English botanist.
