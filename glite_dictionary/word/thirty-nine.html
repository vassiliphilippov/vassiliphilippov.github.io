thirty-nine

1. NUM

/ ˌθɜːrtiˈnaɪn / (AmE), / ˌθɜːtiˈnaɪn / (BrE)
the number 39; being nine more than thirty
the number 39

Examples

She celebrated her thirty-ninth birthday with a small gathering of friends.
There are thirty-nine students enrolled in the class this semester.
The temperature reached thirty-nine degrees Celsius during the heatwave.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: forty - the number 40; one more than thirty-nine
  • Degree Lower: thirty-eight - the number 38; one less than thirty-nine

Etymology

Formed from 'thirty' and 'nine'.
