Rupert Brooke

1. PROPN

/ ˈruːpərt brʊk / (AmE), / ˈruːpət brʊk / (BrE)
(1887–1915) English lyric poet known for his idealistic war sonnets written during the First World War.
English lyric poet (1887–1915)

Rupert Brooke is remembered for his poignant poems about war and patriotism.
