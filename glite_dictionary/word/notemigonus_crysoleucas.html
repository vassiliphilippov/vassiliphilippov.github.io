Notemigonus crysoleucas

1. NOUN

/ ˌnoʊtəˈmɪɡənəs ˌkraɪsoʊˈluːkəs / (AmE), / ˌnəʊtɛmɪˈɡəʊnəs ˌkraɪsəʊˈluːkəs / (BrE)
A small freshwater fish of eastern North America with golden reflections, commonly called the golden shiner and sometimes referred to as 'bream'.
