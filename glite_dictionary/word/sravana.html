Sravana

1. NOUN

/ ˈsrɑː.və.nə / (AmE), / ˈsrɑː.və.nə / (BrE)
The fifth month of the Hindu calendar, usually falling in July and August in the Gregorian calendar.
fifth month of the Hindu calendar

Examples

The festival of Raksha Bandhan is celebrated during Sravana every year.
Monsoon rains are at their peak in Sravana in many parts of India.
Traditional fasting rituals are often observed in the month of Sravana.

Etymology

From Sanskrit *Śrāvaṇaḥ*, name of the month.
