Serbian

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɜr.bi.ən / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.bi.ən / (BrE)
relating to the people, language, or culture of Serbia.
of or relating to Serbia

Examples

She enjoys studying Serbian culture and its rich traditions.
The Serbian language uses both Cyrillic and Latin scripts.
The restaurant serves authentic Serbian cuisine, including grilled meats and pastries.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Serbia - a country in southeastern Europe

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɜr.bi.ən / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.bi.ən / (BrE)
A member of a Slavic ethnic group originating from Serbia and neighboring regions, particularly those who settled there in the 6th and 7th centuries.
member of a Slavic people from Serbia

Examples

There is a large community of Serbians living in the city.
The Serbian greeted us warmly during our visit to Belgrade.
She is proud of her Serbian heritage and traditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Serb - a member of the Slavic people of Serbia; sometimes used synonymously with 'Serbian'
  • Antonym: Croat - a member of a neighboring Slavic people; historical counterpart to Serbians

Etymology

From Serbia + -an, denoting origin or relation.
