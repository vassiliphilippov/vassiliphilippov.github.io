amusingly

1. ADV

/ əˈmjuzɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈmjuːzɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is entertaining or causes laughter.
in an entertaining or funny manner

Examples

The comedian described his awkward childhood amusingly during his stand-up routine.
She amusingly recounted her travel mishaps at dinner, making everyone laugh.
The film amusingly parodies classic spy movies, poking fun at common tropes.

Synset Examples

Hollywood has grown too sophisticated to turn out anything really amusingly bad these days

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humorously - in a funny or amusing way
  • Antonym: seriously - in a serious or solemn manner

Etymology

Formed from 'amusing' + '-ly'. 'Amuse' derives from Old French 'amuser'.
