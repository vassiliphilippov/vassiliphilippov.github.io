campong

1. NOUN

/ kæmˈpɔŋ / (AmE), / kamˈpɒŋ / (BrE)
A village or settlement, especially in Malaysia or Indonesia, typically consisting of traditional houses.
Malay or Indonesian rural village

Examples

The traditional campong was surrounded by lush rice fields and coconut trees.
During our trip to Malaysia, we stayed in a small campong near the river.
Life in a campong is peaceful and closely connected to the local community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kampong - A Malay or Indonesian village; alternative spelling.

Etymology

From Malay 'kampong' (village, settlement), also spelled 'kampung'.
