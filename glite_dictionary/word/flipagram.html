flipagram

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɪpəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈflɪpəˌɡram / (BrE)
A short video created by combining a sequence of photos, often set to music.
a photo sequence video

Examples

I made a flipagram of our vacation photos and shared it with my friends.
Her birthday flipagram featured all her favorite memories from the past year.
The app makes it easy to turn your pictures into a fun flipagram.

Etymology

Blend of 'flip' and 'Instagram', originally the name of an app for creating such videos.
