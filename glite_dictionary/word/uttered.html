uttered

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌtərd / (AmE), / ˈʌtəd / (BrE)
spoken or expressed in words; articulated aloud.
spoken; articulated aloud

Examples

Her carefully uttered words revealed her true feelings.
The judge ignored statements not uttered under oath.
He was shocked by the insults uttered during the argument.

Synset Examples

frequently uttered sentiments

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spoken - expressed in speech; verbal
  • Antonym: written - expressed in writing

Etymology

From past participle of 'utter', which comes from Middle English 'uteren', from Old English 'ūterian' (to put out, speak).
