goodby

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊdˈbaɪ / (AmE), / ɡʊdˈbaɪ / (BrE)
an expression used to say farewell when parting
farewell remark; leave-taking

Examples

She waved and whispered a final goodby before stepping onto the train.
They exchanged hugs and goodby at the airport gate.
He found it hard to say goodby after such a wonderful weekend.

Synset Examples

they said their good-byes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - expression used to bid farewell
  • Synonym: farewell - an act or expression of parting
  • Antonym: hello - expression used in greeting

Etymology

From 'goodbye', itself a contraction of 'God be with ye'.
