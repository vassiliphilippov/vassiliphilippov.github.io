botanic

1. ADJ

/ bəˈtænɪk / (AmE), / bəˈtænɪk / (BrE)
Relating to plants or the scientific study of botany.
pertaining to plants or botany

Examples

The university has a collection of rare botanic specimens for research purposes.
We visited the botanic gardens to learn more about tropical plants.
Her interests include botanic illustration and scientific drawing.

Synset Examples

botanical garden

Related Terms

  • Synonym: botanical - Of or relating to plants or botany; alternative and more common form

Etymology

From French botanique, from Greek botanikos (of herbs), from botane (plant, herb).
