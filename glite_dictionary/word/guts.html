guts

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌts / (AmE), / ɡʌts / (BrE)
Courage, bravery, or determination in facing difficult situations.
courage or determination
informal

Examples

She had the guts to speak out against the unfair policy.
It took a lot of guts for him to quit his job and start a business.
Many people dream of traveling the world, but few have the guts to do it.

Synset Examples

he didn't have the guts to try it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: courage - the ability to do something that frightens one
  • Synonym: bravery - brave behavior or character
  • Antonym: cowardice - lack of bravery
