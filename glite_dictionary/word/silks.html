silks

1. NOUN

/ sɪlks / (AmE), / sɪlks / (BrE)
the brightly colored clothing worn by jockeys to represent a particular stable during horse races
jockey's brightly colored riding clothes

Examples

The jockey wore his silks proudly as he entered the track.
Each stable has its own distinctive set of silks for their riders.
The colorful silks fluttered in the wind as the horses lined up for the race.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jockey - a person who rides horses in races
