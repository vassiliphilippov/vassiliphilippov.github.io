Languas speciosa

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæŋ.ɡwəs spɛkˈioʊsə / (AmE), / ˈlæŋ.ɡwəs spɛkˈiː.əʊsə / (BrE)
A tropical plant cultivated for its glossy oblong leaves and clusters of white, pink-shaded flowers with crinkled yellow and magenta-striped lips; commonly known as shell ginger.
a tropical plant grown for ornamental leaves and flowers

Examples

The lush foliage and striking flowers of Languas speciosa make it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
Gardeners prize Languas speciosa for its arching clusters of vividly colored blooms.
A pot of Languas speciosa brightened up the conservatory with its exotic appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shell ginger - Common name for Languas speciosa, an ornamental plant

Etymology

From Latin: 'languas', an old genus name, and 'speciosa' meaning 'showy' or 'beautiful'.
