Magha

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːɡə / (AmE), / ˈmɑːɡə / (BrE)
the eleventh month of the Hindu calendar, typically corresponding to January in the Gregorian calendar.
eleventh month of the Hindu calendar

Examples

The festival takes place every year during Magha in many parts of India.
Many religious observances are held throughout Magha in the Hindu calendar.
In the Gregorian calendar, Magha usually coincides with January or February.

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Magha', the name of a lunar month in the Hindu calendar.
