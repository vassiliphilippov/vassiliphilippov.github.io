overpayment

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊvərˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
the act of paying more money than is due or required
paying too much money

Examples

The accountant discovered an overpayment that had to be refunded to the client.
Be sure to check your invoice to avoid overpayment for services rendered.
After noticing the overpayment, the company issued a credit to my account.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: underpayment - the act of paying less money than is due

Etymology

From 'over-' (excess) + 'payment' (act of paying).

2. NOUN

/ ˌoʊvərˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
an amount of money paid that is more than what is needed or expected
excess paid amount

Examples

The company issued a refund after discovering the overpayment on my account.
If you notice an overpayment, please contact the billing department immediately.
She requested a reimbursement due to an overpayment in her last invoice.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: underpayment - a payment that is less than the amount due
