secondarily

1. ADV

/ ˌsɛkənˈdɛrəli / (AmE), / ˌsɛkənˈdeərɪli / (BrE)
in a secondary manner; in a way that is of lesser importance or as a result of something else
in a secondary manner

Examples

Although the bill was designed to reduce pollution, it secondarily increased public awareness about environmental issues.
The disease affects the liver primarily and the kidneys secondarily.
He was secondarily responsible for the project's success, with the main credit going to the team leader.

Synset Examples

secondarily affected

Related Terms

  • Antonym: primarily - mainly; chiefly; of primary importance
  • Antonym: mainly - primarily; chiefly; for the most part

Etymology

From 'secondary' + suffix '-ly', with 'secondary' from Latin 'secundarius' (of the second rank).
