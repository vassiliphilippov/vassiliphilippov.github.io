hematologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌhiːməˈtɑːlədʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌhiːmətəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving hematology, the study of blood and its disorders
pertaining to blood or hematology
medical

Examples

He was referred to a hematologic specialist for further testing.
Several hematologic diseases, such as anemia, require specialized diagnosis and treatment.
Her research focuses on hematologic malignancies like leukemia and lymphoma.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hematological - alternative spelling, meaning the same as 'hematologic'

Etymology

From Greek haima (blood) and -logic, from -logikos (relating to study).
