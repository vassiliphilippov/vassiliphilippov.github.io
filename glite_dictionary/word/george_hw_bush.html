George H.W. Bush

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɔrdʒ ˌeɪtʃ ˈdʌbəlˌjuː bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒ eɪtʃ ˈdʌbəlˌjuː bʊʃ / (BrE)
The 41st President of the United States (1989–1993) and vice president under Ronald Reagan (1981–1989).
41st U.S. President, former vice president

Examples

George H.W. Bush served as vice president before his term as President of the United States.
Many historians consider the foreign policy of George H.W. Bush to be particularly influential.
The library honoring George H.W. Bush is located at Texas A&M University.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: President of the United States - The head of state and head of government of the United States
  • Synonym: vice president - An official ranking below and deputizing for a president
