Heliothis

1. NOUN

/ ˌhiːliˈɒθɪs / (AmE), / ˌhiːlɪˈəʊθɪs / (BrE)
A genus of moths in the family Noctuidae, some species of which are destructive crop pests.
genus of moths in family Noctuidae

Examples

The genus Heliothis includes several species that are significant agricultural pests.
Scientists are studying Heliothis to develop better pest control methods for crops.
Some caterpillars of Heliothis moths cause significant damage to cotton and corn fields.

Etymology

From Greek hēlios (sun) and -this, used in scientific Latin formation.
