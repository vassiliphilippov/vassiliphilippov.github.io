sourpuss

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaʊərˌpʊs / (AmE), / ˈsaʊəpʊs / (BrE)
a person who is habitually sullen, gloomy, or grumpy
habitually sullen or grumpy person

Examples

Don’t be such a sourpuss—try to enjoy the party for once.
My little brother turned into a real sourpuss after losing the game.
Despite the good news, he remained a sourpuss all afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grump - a person who is easily annoyed or irritable
  • Synonym: grouch - a person who complains habitually
  • Antonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic

Etymology

Probably from American English 'sour' + 'puss' (slang for face or mouth).
