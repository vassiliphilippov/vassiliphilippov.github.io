David Alfaro Siqueiros

1. PROPN

/ ˈdeɪvɪd ælˈfɑːroʊ siˈkɛroʊs / (AmE), / ˈdeɪvɪd alˈfɑːrəʊ siˈkɛːrɪɒs / (BrE)
(1896–1974) A Mexican painter renowned for his large-scale murals depicting themes of protest and revolution.
Mexican muralist known for political art

Examples

The murals of David Alfaro Siqueiros can be found on public buildings throughout Mexico.
Art historians often cite David Alfaro Siqueiros as a pioneering force in modern muralism.
The political messages in David Alfaro Siqueiros's artwork continue to inspire contemporary artists.
