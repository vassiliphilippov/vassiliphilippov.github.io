meticulousness

1. NOUN

/ məˈtɪkjələsnəs / (AmE), / məˈtɪkjʊləsnəs / (BrE)
The quality of paying careful and precise attention to detail.
attention to detail

Examples

Her meticulousness impressed everyone during the audit, as she found even the smallest errors.
The artist's meticulousness was evident in the tiny details of the painting.
Despite the pressure, his meticulousness never faltered, ensuring every step was checked twice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carefulness - the quality of being cautious and attentive
  • Antonym: sloppiness - lack of care or precision

Etymology

From Latin 'meticulosus' (fearful), from 'metus' (fear), with the meaning evolving to denote paying careful attention to detail.
