multicultural

1. ADJ

/ ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəl / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving several different cultures or cultural groups
relating to several cultures

Examples

The city is known for its multicultural population and diverse festivals.
She attended a multicultural workshop that encouraged understanding of different traditions.
The multicultural menu included dishes from Asia, Europe, and Latin America.

Synset Examples

a multicultural event

Related Terms

  • Antonym: monocultural - relating to or dominated by a single culture

Etymology

From multi- (many) + cultural; first used in the mid-20th century.
