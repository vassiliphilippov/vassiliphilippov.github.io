power-driven

1. ADJ

/ ˈpaʊər ˌdrɪvən / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə ˌdrɪvən / (BrE)
(of a machine or tool) operated by a motor or engine rather than by hand or manual effort.
operated by a motor or engine

Examples

New safety regulations require all power-driven machinery to be regularly inspected.
The carpenter chose a power-driven saw to speed up the work.
Farmers often use power-driven equipment to increase productivity.

Synset Examples

a power-driven hand tool

Related Terms

  • Antonym: manual - operated by hand rather than by a motor or engine
