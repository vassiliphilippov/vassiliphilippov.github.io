dong

1. NOUN

/ dɔŋ / (AmE), / dɒŋ / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Vietnam, equal to 10 hào or 100 xu
Vietnam's basic unit of currency

Examples

The price of the souvenir was 100,000 dong.
She exchanged her dollars for dong at the airport.
The menu listed all the items in Vietnamese dong.

Etymology

From Vietnamese 'đồng' (literally 'copper', referring to the coin's material).
