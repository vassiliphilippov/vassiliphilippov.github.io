said

1. ADJ

/ sɛd / (AmE), / sɛd / (BrE)
being the person or thing that was previously mentioned or spoken about, especially in legal or formal contexts.
previously mentioned; aforesaid (legal/formal)
legal

Examples

The said contract will become effective upon signature by both parties.
The said property is located on the western edge of the city.
No one except the said witness was present at the scene of the incident.

Synset Examples

works of all the aforementioned authors
said party has denied the charges

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aforesaid - mentioned earlier; previously stated
  • Synonym: aforementioned - mentioned before; previously referred to

Etymology

Past participle of 'say', used as an adjective in legal and formal English.
