brown-grey

1. ADJ

/ ˌbraʊnˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˌbraʊnˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a grey color tinged with brown.
grey with a brown tint (color)

Examples

The sofa was upholstered in a soft brown-grey fabric.
She chose a brown-grey paint shade for the living room walls to create a warm atmosphere.
The bird's feathers were mostly brown-grey, helping it blend into the branches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grey-brown - grey tinged with brown; brown tinged with grey (color)
