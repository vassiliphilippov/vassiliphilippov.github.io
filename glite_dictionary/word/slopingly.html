slopingly

1. ADV

/ ˈsloʊpɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsləʊpɪŋli / (BrE)
In a sloping or slanted manner; with an incline.
in a slanted or inclined way

Examples

The old roof was built slopingly to let the rainwater run off easily.
Rows of crops were planted slopingly along the hillside to prevent erosion.
She arranged the books slopingly on the shelf, creating a staggered effect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slantwise - at an angle; not straight or level
  • Antonym: horizontally - in a horizontal manner, parallel to the horizon
