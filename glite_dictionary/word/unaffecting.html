unaffecting

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈfɛk.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈfɛk.tɪŋ / (BrE)
Not producing any strong emotional response; failing to move or touch emotionally.
not emotionally moving

Examples

The speech was so unaffecting that few people paid attention to it.
Although technically proficient, the performance was strangely unaffecting.
His writing is competent but ultimately unaffecting, failing to inspire much response in the reader.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: moving - causing strong emotion, especially sadness or sympathy
  • Antonym: touching - evoking feelings of tenderness or sympathy
