windswept

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪndˌswɛpt / (AmE), / ˈwɪndˌswɛpt / (BrE)
(of a place or object) exposed to and affected by strong winds, often giving a wild or desolate appearance.
exposed to strong winds

Examples

The cottage stood on a windswept cliff overlooking the ocean.
After hours on the windswept plain, they sought shelter from the cold.
Her hair was tangled from the windswept walk along the beach.

Synset Examples

windswept headlands
