nihility

1. NOUN

/ nɪˈhɪlɪti / (AmE), / nɪˈhɪlɪti / (BrE)
the state of being nothing; nonexistence
nonexistence; nothingness

Examples

The philosopher pondered the concept of nihility and its implications for human existence.
Faced with utter defeat, he felt as if everything around him had dissolved into nihility.
Some theories propose that the universe emerged from absolute nihility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nothingness - the state of being nothing; nonexistence
  • Antonym: existence - the state of being real or living

Etymology

From Latin 'nihilitas', from 'nihil' meaning 'nothing'.
