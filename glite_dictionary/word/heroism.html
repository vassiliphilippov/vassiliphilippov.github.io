heroism

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪroʊˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈhɪərəʊˌɪzəm / (BrE)
great bravery and courage, especially in dangerous or difficult situations; the qualities or actions of a hero
great bravery or courage

Examples

His heroism during the rescue inspired the whole community.
Acts of heroism are often recognized with medals and honors.
The soldier's heroism on the battlefield saved many lives.

Synset Examples

he showed great heroism in battle
he received a medal for valor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bravery - courageous behavior or character
  • Antonym: cowardice - lack of bravery

Etymology

From French héroïsme, from héros (hero) + -isme (-ism).
