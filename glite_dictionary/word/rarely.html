rarely

1. ADV

/ ˈrɛrli / (AmE), / ˈreəli / (BrE)
Not often; on very few occasions.
not often; infrequently

Examples

Rarely do we see such honesty in politics today.
She rarely eats dessert after dinner.
The old book was rarely opened, except on special occasions.

Synset Examples

we rarely met

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seldom - not often; rarely
  • Antonym: often - frequently; many times

Etymology

From rare + -ly, patterned after commonly used English adverbs.
