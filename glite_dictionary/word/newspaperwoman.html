newspaperwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpərˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A female journalist, especially one who writes or reports news stories for newspapers or broadcast media.
female journalist or news reporter

Examples

She became the first newspaperwoman in her town and covered important civic events.
The newspaperwoman interviewed the mayor for the evening edition.
During the 1940s, being a successful newspaperwoman was both rare and challenging.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: newspaperman - male journalist or news reporter
  • Synonym: journalist - person who writes or reports news for media

Etymology

Formed from 'newspaper' + 'woman', modeled after 'newspaperman'.
