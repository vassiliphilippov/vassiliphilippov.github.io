Deparia

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɛəriə / (AmE), / dɪˈpɛəriə / (BrE)
a genus of terrestrial ferns containing several species, sometimes placed in other genera by some botanists
a genus of terrestrial ferns

Examples

The woodland floor was carpeted with Deparia and other native ferns.
Several species within Deparia are valued for their attractive fronds.
Botanists sometimes debate whether certain ferns belong to Deparia or to related genera.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin, based on Greek roots; used in botanical taxonomy.
