screw-topped

1. ADJ

/ skruː tɑpt / (AmE), / skruː tɒpt / (BrE)
(of a container) having a lid or cap that screws on with a spiral groove.
having a screw-on lid or cap

Examples

The jam was sold in a screw-topped jar for easy resealing.
He prefers screw-topped bottles because they are more convenient than corked ones.
She opened the screw-topped container to get some cookies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: screw-cap - a cap or lid designed to be screwed onto a container
