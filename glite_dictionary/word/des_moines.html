Des Moines

1. PROPN

/ də ˈmɔɪn / (AmE), / də ˈmɔɪn / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of the state of Iowa, in the United States
capital city of Iowa, USA

Examples

She moved to Des Moines for a new job opportunity.
The headquarters of the company is located in Des Moines.
Every summer, Des Moines hosts the Iowa State Fair.

Etymology

From French 'Des Moines', meaning 'of the monks'.
