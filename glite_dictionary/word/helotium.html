2. NOUN

/ hɛˈloʊtiəm / (AmE), / hɛˈləʊtiəm / (BrE)
A genus of fungi in the family Helotiaceae, serving as the type genus of this family.
type genus of Helotiaceae fungi

Examples

The fungus Helotium is often studied for its unique reproductive structures.
Researchers classified the specimen under the genus Helotium due to its morphological features.
Some species within Helotium play an important role in forest ecosystems.

Etymology

From New Latin, based on Greek hēlos (nail) and -ium (diminutive suffix), referring to the nail-shaped fruiting bodies.
