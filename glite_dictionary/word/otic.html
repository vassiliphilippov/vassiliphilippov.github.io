otic

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊtɪk / (AmE), / ˈɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or situated near the ear, especially in medical or anatomical contexts.
relating to the ear
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed otic drops to treat the infection in her ear.
Some medications are available in otic form for direct application to the ear canal.
The patient showed signs of an otic disorder affecting his hearing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aural - relating to the ear or sense of hearing

Etymology

From Greek ōtikos, from ous, ōtos (ear).
