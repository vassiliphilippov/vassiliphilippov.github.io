albizia

1. NOUN

/ ælˈbɪziə / (AmE), / ælˈbɪziə / (BrE)
A tree of the genus Albizia, known for its feathery leaves and often large, showy flower clusters.
a tree of the Albizia genus

Examples

The garden features a beautiful albizia tree with delicate pink blossoms.
Many tropical regions plant albizia for shade and ornamental purposes.
Some species of albizia are used in traditional medicine and as sources of timber.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mimosa - any of several plants in the genus Mimosa, related to Albizia

Etymology

From New Latin Albizia, named after Italian naturalist Filippo degli Albizzi (18th century).
