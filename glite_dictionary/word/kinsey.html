Kinsey

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪnzi / (AmE), / ˈkɪnzi / (BrE)
(1894–1956) United States zoologist and sexologist noted for his pioneering research on human sexual behavior.
American sexologist and zoologist (1894–1956)

Examples

Kinsey revolutionized the field of sexology with his groundbreaking research in the 1940s and 1950s.
The Kinsey Reports remain influential sources in the study of human sexuality.
Many scholars credit Kinsey with challenging societal norms about sexual behavior.
