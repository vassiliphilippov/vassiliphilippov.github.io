beefed-up

1. ADJ

/ ˌbiːftˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌbiːftˈʌp / (BrE)
Made greater or stronger; strengthened or increased in size, force, or effectiveness.
strengthened or made more powerful
informal

Examples

The company unveiled a beefed-up version of its popular laptop.
Security around the building was beefed-up after last week's incident.
The beefed-up regulations aim to prevent further financial scandals.

Synset Examples

beefed-up sales efforts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strengthened - made stronger or more powerful
  • Antonym: weakened - made less strong or less effective

Etymology

From the informal use of "beef up" (to make stronger), using "beef" as slang for strength or power.
