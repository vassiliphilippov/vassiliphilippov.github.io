Ljubljana

1. PROPN

/ ˌljuːbˈljɑːnə / (AmE), / ˌljuːbˈljɑːnə / (BrE)
The capital city of Slovenia, situated in the central part of the country.
the capital city of Slovenia

Examples

We spent a wonderful week exploring the old town of Ljubljana.
Ljubljana is famous for its beautiful bridges and vibrant café culture.
The university in Ljubljana attracts students from all over Europe.

Etymology

From Slovene Ljubljana, possibly from the river name Ljubljanica.
