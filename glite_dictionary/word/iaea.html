IAEA

1. PROPN

/ aɪ.eɪ.iːˈeɪ / (AmE), / aɪ.eɪ.iːˈeɪ / (BrE)
abbreviation for International Atomic Energy Agency, a United Nations agency concerned with the peaceful use of atomic energy and the prevention of its use for military purposes
International Atomic Energy Agency (UN agency)

Examples

The IAEA plays a crucial role in monitoring nuclear programs worldwide.
Representatives from the IAEA visited the facility for an inspection last month.
Many countries cooperate with the IAEA to ensure peaceful use of nuclear technology.

Etymology

Initialism from International Atomic Energy Agency.
