flag-waver

1. NOUN

/ flæɡ ˈweɪvər / (AmE), / flæɡ ˈweɪvə / (BrE)
a person who aggressively and publicly displays extreme support for their country, often in a nationalistic or bellicose manner
an extreme nationalist or patriotic enthusiast

Examples

The senator was known as a prominent flag-waver during times of international tension.
Some critics dismissed the film as mere propaganda for flag-wavers.
He accused his opponent of being a flag-waver rather than addressing real issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chauvinist - someone excessively and blindly patriotic
  • Degree Lower: patriot - a person who loves and strongly supports their country
  • Synonym: jingoist - a person with extreme patriotic enthusiasm, especially favoring aggressive foreign policy
