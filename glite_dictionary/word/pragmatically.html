pragmatically

1. ADV

/ præɡˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / præɡˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
In a practical way, rather than being theoretical or idealistic.
in a practical, realistic manner

Examples

We need to respond pragmatically to the challenges facing our company.
Although she had big dreams, she planned her career pragmatically to ensure stability.
The proposal sounds great in theory, but we must consider it pragmatically.

Synset Examples

we want to build a democratic society, but we must act pragmatically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: practically - in a practical manner
  • Antonym: theoretically - in a theoretical manner
  • Antonym: ideally - in an ideal or perfect way

Etymology

From pragmatic + -ally; ultimately from Greek pragma (deed, affair).
