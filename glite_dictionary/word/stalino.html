Stalino

1. PROPN

/ stəˈliːnoʊ / (AmE), / stəˈliːnəʊ / (BrE)
the former name of the industrial city now known as Donetsk in eastern Ukraine, located in the Donets Basin.
former name of Donetsk, Ukraine

Examples

My grandfather grew up in Stalino before it was renamed Donetsk.
Many historical documents from the 1950s still reference the city as Stalino.
The steel industry thrived in Stalino during the Soviet era.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Donetsk - city in eastern Ukraine, current name of Stalino

Etymology

Named after Joseph Stalin, Soviet leader; renamed to Donetsk in 1961.
