Army

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑr.mi / (AmE), / ˈɑː.mi / (BrE)
the military land force of the United States, responsible for organizing and training soldiers for land warfare
the U.S. military land force
american

Examples

After finishing college, he decided to join the Army and serve his country.
The Army conducts extensive training exercises to prepare its soldiers for combat.
She received a medal for her outstanding service in the Army.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armed forces - the combined military forces of a nation
  • Antonym: navy - branch of a country's armed forces that conducts military operations at sea
  • Antonym: air force - branch of a country's armed forces that conducts military operations in the air

Etymology

From Latin 'armata', meaning 'armed force', via Old French 'armée'.
