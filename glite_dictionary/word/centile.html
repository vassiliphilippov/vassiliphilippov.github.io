centile

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛn.taɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.tɪl / (BrE)
(statistics) any of the 99 values that divide a set of ranked data into 100 equal parts, each containing one percent of the data.
statistical division of data into hundredths

Examples

The results showed that her height was at the 75th centile for her age group.
Children below the 5th centile may need further medical evaluation.
Being in a higher centile means you scored better than a majority of the participants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: percentile - each of the 99 values dividing data into 100 equal parts

Etymology

From Latin 'centum' (hundred) + English '-ile', modeled on 'percentile'.
