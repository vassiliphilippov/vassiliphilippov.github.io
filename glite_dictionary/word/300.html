300

1. NUM

/ ˈθri ˈhʌndrəd / (AmE), / ˈθri ˈhʌndrəd / (BrE)
The number three hundred; the cardinal number equal to two hundred plus one hundred.
the number three hundred

Examples

There are 300 pages in this novel, so it might take a while to read.
Attendance at the seminar exceeded 300 participants, making it the largest one yet.
She saved over 300 dollars for her trip this summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: three hundred - the number 300 written out in words
