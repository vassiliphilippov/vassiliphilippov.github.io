entomologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛn.təˈmɑ.lə.dʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɛn.təˈmɒl.ə.dʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who specializes in the study of insects
scientist who studies insects

Examples

The entomologist spent years researching the migration patterns of butterflies.
As an entomologist, she often travels to rainforests to discover new insect species.
During the conference, the entomologist presented his findings on bee populations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zoologist - a scientist who studies animals

Etymology

From French entomologiste, from entomologie meaning 'study of insects'.
