guideword

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡaɪdˌwɝd / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪdˌwɜːd / (BrE)
a word printed at the top of a page in a dictionary or reference book, showing the first or last entry on that page
word at top of dictionary page indicating range

Examples

The guideword at the top of the page helped me find the definition quickly.
Each page of the dictionary includes a guideword to indicate the first entry listed.
When using a thesaurus, check the guidewords to navigate more efficiently.
