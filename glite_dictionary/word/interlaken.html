Interlaken

1. PROPN

/ ˌɪn.tərˈlɑː.kən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈlɑː.kən / (BrE)
a popular resort town situated in the Alps in west central Switzerland, known for its scenic beauty and as a gateway to the surrounding mountains
Swiss resort town in the Alps

Examples

We spent our summer holiday in Interlaken, hiking and enjoying the stunning mountain views.
The train to Interlaken offers breathtaking scenery as it passes by lakes and snow-capped peaks.
Interlaken attracts visitors from around the world with its adventure sports and picturesque setting.

Etymology

From German, meaning 'between lakes', referring to its location between Lake Thun and Lake Brienz.
