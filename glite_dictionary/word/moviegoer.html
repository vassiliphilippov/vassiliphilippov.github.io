moviegoer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːviˌɡoʊər / (AmE), / ˈmuːviˌɡəʊə / (BrE)
a person who regularly goes to watch films at a cinema
person who attends movies

Examples

The new superhero film attracted every moviegoer in town.
As a frequent moviegoer, she knows all the best seats in the theater.
During the festival, each moviegoer received a complimentary poster.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cinemagoer - person who goes to the cinema (chiefly British)

Etymology

Formed from 'movie' (short for 'moving picture') + 'goer'.
