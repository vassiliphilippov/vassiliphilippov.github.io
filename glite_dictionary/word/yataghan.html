yataghan

1. NOUN

/ ˈjætəˌɡæn / (AmE), / ˈjætəɡæn / (BrE)
a long, curved Turkish knife with a single-edged blade, traditionally used as a weapon
Turkish curved knife

Examples

The soldier drew his yataghan and prepared for battle.
The museum displayed an ornate yataghan with a jeweled hilt.
Collectors prize the yataghan for its distinctive shape and craftsmanship.

Etymology

From Turkish 'yatağan', the name of the knife, after the town of Yatağan in southwest Turkey.
