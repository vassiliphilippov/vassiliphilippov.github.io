resplendence

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsplɛndəns / (AmE), / rɪˈsplɛndəns / (BrE)
brilliant or dazzling beauty; the quality of being splendidly impressive or radiant in appearance.
brilliant or dazzling beauty

Examples

The ballroom was filled with the resplendence of glittering chandeliers and golden mirrors.
She admired the resplendence of the garden in full bloom.
From the mountain peak, the resplendence of the dawn took her breath away.

Synset Examples

the glory of the sunrise

Related Terms

  • Synonym: splendor - magnificent and splendid appearance
  • Antonym: dullness - lack of brightness or vividness

Etymology

From Latin 'resplendentia', from 'resplendēre' (to shine back).
