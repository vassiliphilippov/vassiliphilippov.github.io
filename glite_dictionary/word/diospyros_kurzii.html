Diospyros kurzii

1. NOUN

/ diˈɑːspɪˌrɒs ˈkɜːrziː / (AmE), / daɪˈɒspɪˌrɒs ˈkɜːziː / (BrE)
A large Asiatic tree species known for its hard, marbled zebrawood.
Asiatic tree; source of zebrawood

Examples

The furniture was made from the striking wood of Diospyros kurzii.
Researchers discovered that Diospyros kurzii is valued for its zebrawood timber.
Botanists documented the habitat of Diospyros kurzii in Southeast Asian forests.

Etymology

Genus name from Greek 'Dios' (of Zeus) and 'pyros' (wheat); species named after botanist Wilhelm Sulpiz Kurz.
