horologist

1. NOUN

/ hɔˈrɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / hɒˈrɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs watches or clocks
maker or repairer of watches and clocks

Examples

The horologist skillfully restored the antique pocket watch to working condition.
Many horologists attend international conventions to discuss advancements in timekeeping technology.
As a renowned horologist, she is well known for her craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watchmaker - a person who makes or repairs watches
  • Synonym: clockmaker - a person who makes or repairs clocks

Etymology

From French *horologiste*, from Latin *horologium* (clock, timepiece).
