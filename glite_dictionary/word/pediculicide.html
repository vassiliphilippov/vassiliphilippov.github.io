pediculicide

1. NOUN

/ pɪˈdɪkjələˌsaɪd / (AmE), / pɪˈdɪkjʊlɪsaɪd / (BrE)
A chemical substance or preparation used to kill lice.
substance for killing lice
medical

Examples

The doctor recommended applying a pediculicide shampoo to eliminate the lice infestation.
Many pediculicides are available over the counter, but some require a prescription.
Always follow the instructions carefully when using a pediculicide on children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insecticide - a substance used for killing insects

Etymology

From Latin 'pediculus' (louse) and '-cide' (killer, from Latin 'caedere', to kill).
