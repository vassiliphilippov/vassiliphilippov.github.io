calorimetric

1. ADJ

/ ˌkælərəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌkæl.ə.rɪˈmetrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving the measurement of heat, especially with a calorimeter
relating to measurement of heat

Examples

The calorimetric analysis revealed the precise amount of energy released during combustion.
Researchers used calorimetric techniques to study the reaction's heat exchange.
A calorimetric experiment can help determine the specific heat of a substance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermal - relating to heat

Etymology

From French calorimétrique, from calorimètre (calorimeter), from Latin calor (heat) + -mètre (meter).
