Dail

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑːl / (AmE), / ˈdɑːl / (BrE)
The lower house of the parliament of the Republic of Ireland.
lower house of Ireland's parliament

Examples

The Dail passed the new education bill after hours of debate.
Elections for the Dail are held every few years in Ireland.
She was elected to the Dail as a member of the Green Party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parliament - the supreme legislative body in various countries

Etymology

From Irish Gaelic 'Dáil' (assembly, meeting).
