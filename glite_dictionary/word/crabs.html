crabs

1. NOUN

/ kræbz / (AmE), / kræbz / (BrE)
an infestation of pubic hair by crab lice, especially as a sexually transmitted condition
infestation of pubic lice
slang medical

Examples

The doctor confirmed he had crabs after he complained of itching.
She discovered she had crabs and sought treatment right away.
Despite using medication, his crabs returned a few weeks later.
