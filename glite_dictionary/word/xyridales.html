Xyridales

1. NOUN

/ zaɪrɪˈdeɪliːz / (AmE), / zaɪrɪˈdeɪliːz / (BrE)
An order of monocotyledonous flowering plants that includes families such as Xyridaceae.
order of monocotyledonous plants

Examples

The genus Xyris belongs to the order Xyridales in certain classifications.
Botanists have debated the precise composition of the Xyridales over time.
Recent studies suggest that Xyridales is not recognized in the latest plant taxonomies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Xyridaceae - family of flowering plants within the order Xyridales
  • Synonym: order - a rank in the biological classification system

Etymology

From New Latin, formed from the family name Xyridaceae plus the taxonomic suffix -ales.
