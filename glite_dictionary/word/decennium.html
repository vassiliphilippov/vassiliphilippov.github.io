decennium

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈsɛniəm / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛniəm / (BrE)
A period of ten years; a decade.
period of ten years

Examples

The two nations enjoyed peace for an entire decennium before hostilities resumed.
During the first decennium of the 21st century, technological advancements accelerated rapidly.
Historians often analyze trends over each decennium to understand long-term changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decade - a period of ten years

Etymology

From Latin 'decennium', from 'decem' (ten) + 'annus' (year).
