fucktillion

1. NOUN

/ fʌkˈtɪljən / (AmE), / fʌkˈtɪljən / (BrE)
(slang) An exaggerated, unspecified but very large number of things or tasks.
humorously huge number (slang)
slang vulgar

Examples

I have a fucktillion things to do before the vacation starts.
There are a fucktillion reasons why this plan could fail.
She owns a fucktillion pairs of shoes—her closet is overflowing.

Synset Examples

I have a fucktillion things to do before the deadline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bazillion - an extremely large, indefinite number (slang)
  • Synonym: zillion - an extremely large, but nonspecific number (informal)

Etymology

Blend of 'fuck' (vulgar intensifier) and '-illion' (as in 'million' or 'billion'), formed to mimic very large numbers humorously.
