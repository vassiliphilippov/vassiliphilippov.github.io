Najd

1. PROPN

/ nɑːdʒd / (AmE), / nɑːdʒd / (BrE)
a central plateau region in the Arabian Peninsula, formerly an independent sultanate, now part of Saudi Arabia
central region of the Arabian Peninsula

Examples

The city of Riyadh is located in the heart of Najd.
Many Saudi traditions originate from the region of Najd.
After unification, Najd became an integral part of modern Saudi Arabia.

Etymology

From Arabic نَجْد (najd), meaning 'upland' or 'highland.'
