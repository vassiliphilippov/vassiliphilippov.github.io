uncontrollably

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnkənˈtroʊləbli / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈtrəʊləbli / (BrE)
In a way that cannot be controlled or restrained.
in an uncontrolled manner

Examples

He began to cry uncontrollably as the news sank in.
The audience laughed uncontrollably at the comedian's joke.
Her hands were shaking uncontrollably from nervousness.

Synset Examples

she laughed uncontrollably

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wildly - in a wild or uncontrolled manner

Etymology

From 'uncontrollable' + '-ly'. 'Uncontrollable' from un- (not) + 'controllable'.
