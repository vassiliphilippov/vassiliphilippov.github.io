eerily

1. ADV

/ ˈɪrɪli / (AmE), / ˈɪə.rɪ.li / (BrE)
In a strange and frightening or unnatural way.
in a strange or frightening way

Examples

The abandoned house looked eerily similar to the one in my dreams.
The forest was eerily silent just before the storm began.
A cold fog rolled in, making the streetlights glow eerily in the night.

Synset Examples

it was eerily quiet in the chapel

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strangely - in a strange or unusual way
  • Synonym: eerie - strange and frightening (adjective form)

Etymology

From 'eerie' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
