coffeehouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔfiˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈkɒfiˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A small restaurant or café where coffee and light refreshments are served.
café serving coffee and snacks

Examples

The old coffeehouse on the corner is a popular spot for local musicians.
She spent her afternoon reading and writing in the cozy coffeehouse.
Many students gather at the coffeehouse to study and catch up with friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: café - a small restaurant selling light meals and drinks

Etymology

From coffee + house, first attested in English in the 17th century, after the establishment of coffee houses in Europe.
