unheated

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhiːtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhiːtɪd / (BrE)
Not having been made warm or hot; not heated.
not heated or warmed

Examples

The cottage had only one small unheated room for guests.
He ate the soup unheated, straight from the can.
They stored the wine in an unheated cellar to keep it cool.

Synset Examples

an unheated room
unwarmed rolls

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heated - made hot or warm
