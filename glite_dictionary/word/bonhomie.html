bonhomie

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɑː.nəˈmiː / (AmE), / ˌbɒn.əˈmiː / (BrE)
cheerful friendliness; a pleasant and approachable manner
cheerful friendliness or good-natured manner

Examples

The host's bonhomie set everyone at ease during the dinner party.
Despite the tense negotiations, there was a surprising sense of bonhomie among the rival groups.
Her genuine bonhomie made her well liked by colleagues and clients alike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affability - the quality of being friendly and easy to talk to
  • Synonym: amiability - a friendly and pleasant manner
  • Antonym: hostility - unfriendly or antagonistic behavior

Etymology

From French *bonhomie*, from *bonhomme* meaning 'good-natured man' (*bon* "good" + *homme* "man").
