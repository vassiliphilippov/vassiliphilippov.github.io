unfaithfully

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈfeɪθfəli / (AmE), / ʌnˈfeɪθfəli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows a lack of loyalty, trustworthiness, or reliability.
in a disloyal or unreliable way

Examples

He acted unfaithfully toward his business partners, breaking several agreements.
She reported the events unfaithfully, omitting key details to hide the truth.
The promise was unfaithfully kept, leading to disappointment.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: faithfully - in a loyal or reliable way
