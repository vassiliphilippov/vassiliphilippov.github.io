Vivien Leigh

1. PROPN

/ ˈvɪviən liː / (AmE), / ˈvɪviən liː / (BrE)
(1913–1967) English actress known for her acclaimed roles in films such as 'Gone with the Wind' and 'A Streetcar Named Desire'.
English film actress (1913–1967)

Examples

Vivien Leigh won an Academy Award for her performance as Scarlett O'Hara in 'Gone with the Wind'.
The stage play starred an actress who resembled Vivien Leigh in both looks and talent.
Many film historians consider Vivien Leigh one of the greatest performers of the twentieth century.
