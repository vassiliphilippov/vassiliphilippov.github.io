lemony

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛməni / (AmE), / ˈlɛməni / (BrE)
having the taste or smell of lemon; tasting or smelling like a lemon
having the taste or smell of lemon

Examples

The cake had a lemony flavor that made it taste fresh and light.
She sprayed a lemony scent around the house to make it smell clean.
I prefer tea with a slightly lemony tang on a hot day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: citrusy - having the taste or aroma of citrus fruits
  • Degree Lower: lemon - the yellow citrus fruit or its flavor

Etymology

From 'lemon' + '-y', forming adjectives meaning 'having the qualities of'.
