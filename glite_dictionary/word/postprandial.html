postprandial

1. ADJ

/ poʊstˈprændiəl / (AmE), / pəʊstˈprændɪəl / (BrE)
Occurring or done after a meal, especially dinner.
after a meal

Examples

He enjoyed a postprandial stroll through the garden to aid digestion.
The doctor ordered a postprandial blood glucose test after the patient finished eating.
Some people like to relax with a postprandial coffee after dinner.

Synset Examples

his postprandial cigar
took a postprandial walk

Related Terms

  • Antonym: preprandial - occurring before a meal

Etymology

From Latin 'postprandium' ('after lunch'), from 'post-' (after) and 'prandium' (a meal).
