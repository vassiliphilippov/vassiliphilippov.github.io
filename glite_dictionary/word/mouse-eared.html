mouse-eared

1. ADJ

/ maʊs ˌɪrd / (AmE), / maʊs ˌɪəd / (BrE)
having ears that are shaped like or resemble those of a mouse
having ears like a mouse's

Examples

The mouse-eared bat is known for its distinctive ear shape.
Several flowers in the garden had mouse-eared leaves.
She wore a mouse-eared headband for the costume party.
