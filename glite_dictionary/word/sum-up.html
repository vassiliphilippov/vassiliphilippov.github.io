sum-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈsʌm.ʌp / (BrE)
a brief statement or account presenting the main points of something in a concise form
a concise summary or overview

Examples

The final chapter provides an excellent sum-up of the book's main arguments.
At the meeting, she offered a quick sum-up of the day's discussions.
Can you give me a short sum-up of the situation so far?

Synset Examples

he gave a summary of the conclusions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summary - a brief statement of the main points
  • Synonym: overview - a general review or summary of a subject

Etymology

From phrasal verb 'sum up', meaning to summarize.
