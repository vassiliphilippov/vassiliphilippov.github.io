to glug

1. VERB

/ ɡlʌɡ / (AmE), / ɡlʌɡ / (BrE)
to make a gurgling sound as liquid is poured out, especially from a narrow necked container
make a gurgling sound when pouring liquid

Examples

He could hear the water glug from the thermos as it filled his cup.
As the cook poured the olive oil, it began to glug rhythmically into the pan.
When I tilted the bottle, the cola started to glug out and fizz over the rim.

Synset Examples

the wine bottles glugged

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gurgle - to make a hollow bubbling sound like liquid pouring
