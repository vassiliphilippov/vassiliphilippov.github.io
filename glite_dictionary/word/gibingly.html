gibingly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡɪbɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡɪbɪŋli / (BrE)
In a mocking or jeering manner; with derision or disrespect.
mockingly; with derision

Examples

She laughed gibingly at his nervous speech, making him feel even worse.
The critics wrote gibingly about the artist's latest work, sparing no harsh words.
He replied gibingly, his tone dripping with scorn and disbelief.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mockingly - in a mocking manner
  • Synonym: jeeringly - in a manner expressing derision or ridicule
  • Antonym: respectfully - in a manner showing respect

Etymology

From 'gibe' (to mock or jeer), plus the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
