authorised

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔːθəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ˈɔːθəraɪzd / (BrE)
Given official approval or permission; having formal authority.
having official approval or authority
british

Examples

Only authorised personnel are allowed access to the laboratory.
He published the authorised biography after receiving approval from the family.
The bank requires an authorised signature on all transactions.

Synset Examples

an authoritative communique
the authorized biography

Related Terms

  • Synonym: official - approved by an authority or government
  • Antonym: unauthorized - not having official approval or permission

Etymology

From authorize + -ed; 'authorize' from Old French 'autoriser', from Latin 'auctoritas' (authority).
