protégé

1. NOUN

/ ˈproʊtəˌʒeɪ / (AmE), / ˈprəʊtəʒeɪ / (BrE)
a person who is guided, supported, and promoted by a more experienced or influential person, especially in their career or professional development
person mentored by someone influential

Examples

As a protégé of the renowned scientist, she gained invaluable experience in the laboratory.
He took his protégé to industry conferences to help build her network.
The artist regarded himself as a protégé of the distinguished painter.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mentor - an experienced and trusted adviser

Etymology

From French 'protégé', past participle of 'protéger' (to protect), from Latin 'protegere'.
