Psettichthys

1. NOUN

/ psɛˈtɪkθɪs / (AmE), / psɛˈtɪkθɪs / (BrE)
A genus of flatfishes in the family Soleidae, including the Pacific sand sole found in the northern Pacific Ocean.
genus of flatfishes (Pacific sand sole)

Examples

The Pacific sand sole belongs to the genus Psettichthys and is common along the North American west coast.
Marine biologists discovered several new specimens of Psettichthys during their recent expedition.
Members of the genus Psettichthys are characterized by their elongated bodies and sandy coloration.

Etymology

From Greek psēttēs (a kind of fish) + ichthys (fish).
