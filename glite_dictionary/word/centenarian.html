centenarian

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛn.təˈnɛr.i.ən / (AmE), / ˌsɛn.tɪˈneə.ri.ən / (BrE)
a person who is at least 100 years old
person aged 100 or over

Examples

She was honored as a centenarian during the town's bicentennial celebration.
Many centenarians credit their longevity to a healthy lifestyle and supportive communities.
The documentary featured an inspiring centenarian who still enjoyed gardening every day.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: octogenarian - a person between 80 and 89 years old
  • Degree Lower: nonagenarian - a person between 90 and 99 years old

Etymology

From Latin 'centum' (hundred) + 'annus' (year).

2. NOUN

/ ˌsɛn.təˈnɛr.i.ən / (AmE), / ˌsɛn.tɪˈneə.ri.ən / (BrE)
a person who is at least 100 years old
a person aged 100 or over

Examples

The town honored its oldest resident, a centenarian who just turned 102.
Research shows that many centenarians live active and fulfilling lives.
Her grandmother became a centenarian last year, celebrating her 100th birthday with the whole family.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: octogenarian - a person who is between 80 and 89 years old
  • Degree Lower: nonagenarian - a person who is between 90 and 99 years old

Etymology

From Latin 'centenarius' (of a hundred), from 'centum' (hundred).
