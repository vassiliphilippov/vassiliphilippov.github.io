Portugueseness

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɔːrtʃəˈɡiːznəs / (AmE), / ˌpɔːtʃəˈɡiːznəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being Portuguese, especially in regard to culture, identity, or character.
the quality of being Portuguese

Examples

The novel explores the deep sense of Portugueseness that shapes its characters and their traditions.
During the festival, the community proudly celebrates its Portugueseness through music and food.
Her poetry captures the essence of Portugueseness amid a rapidly changing world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Portuguese - relating to Portugal or its people

Etymology

From Portuguese + -ness, forming a noun indicating the quality or state.
