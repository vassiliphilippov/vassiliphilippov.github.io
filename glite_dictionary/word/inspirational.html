inspirational

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnspɪˈreɪʃənəl / (BrE)
Imparting a divine or spiritual influence on the mind and soul.
imparting divine or spiritual influence

Examples

The preacher's inspirational words filled the congregation with a sense of purpose and hope.
During meditation, she experienced an inspirational moment that felt truly divine.
Some believe that the scriptures are inspirational texts, imparted with a spiritual essence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspiring - Having the effect of inspiring; motivating to action or feeling

Etymology

From Latin inspirare (to breathe into), via inspiration.
