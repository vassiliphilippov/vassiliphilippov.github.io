Oliver Hazard Perry

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑːlɪvər ˈhæzərd ˈpɛri / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪvə ˈhæzəd ˈpɛri / (BrE)
(1785–1819) A United States naval officer known for leading the fleet that defeated the British at the Battle of Lake Erie during the War of 1812.
U.S. naval officer, War of 1812 hero

Examples

The bravery of Oliver Hazard Perry was commemorated in several naval monuments across the United States.
Students learned about Oliver Hazard Perry and his role in the War of 1812 during their history lesson.
A portrait of Oliver Hazard Perry hangs in the naval museum, celebrating his famous victory on Lake Erie.
