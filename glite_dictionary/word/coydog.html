coydog

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔɪˌdɔg / (AmE), / ˈkɔɪˌdɒg / (BrE)
A hybrid animal that is the offspring of a coyote and a domestic dog.
coyote-dog hybrid animal

Examples

A coydog can inherit physical and behavioral traits from both its coyote and dog parents.
Farmers sometimes encounter a coydog near the edge of their property.
The wildlife rescue center provided shelter for an injured coydog found in the woods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wolfdog - A hybrid between a wolf and a domestic dog

Etymology

Blend of 'coyote' and 'dog'.
