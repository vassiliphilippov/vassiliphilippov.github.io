horselaugh

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrsˌlæf / (AmE), / ˈhɔːslɑːf / (BrE)
a loud, coarse laugh that sounds similar to the neighing of a horse
loud, harsh laugh

Examples

His booming horselaugh could be heard across the crowded room.
She let out a horselaugh that startled everyone nearby.
Whenever he found something funny, his infectious horselaugh filled the air.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guffaw - a loud and boisterous laugh
