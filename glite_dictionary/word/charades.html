charades

1. NOUN

/ ʃəˈreɪdz / (AmE), / ʃəˈrɑːdz / (BrE)
a game in which players take turns acting out a word or phrase without speaking, while others try to guess what it is
guessing game with pantomime

Examples

We played charades at the party, and everyone had a great time trying to guess the clues.
Her excellent acting skills make her the champion of charades in our family.
During the holiday gathering, they entertained themselves by playing charades for hours.

Etymology

From French 'charade', originally meaning a form of riddle.
