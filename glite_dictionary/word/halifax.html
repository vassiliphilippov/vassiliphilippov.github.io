Halifax

1. PROPN

/ ˈhælɪˌfæks / (AmE), / ˈhælɪfæks / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of the Canadian province of Nova Scotia, situated on the Atlantic coast
capital city of Nova Scotia, Canada

Examples

She moved to Halifax for a new job opportunity.
Halifax is known for its vibrant waterfront and maritime history.
The conference will be held in Halifax next summer.

Etymology

Named after George Montagu-Dunk, 2nd Earl of Halifax, an English statesman.
