tekki

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛki / (AmE), / ˈtɛki / (BrE)
A person who is highly skilled and passionate about a technical field, especially computing.
expert enthusiast in tech, especially computing
informal

Examples

Everyone at the startup considers Sam the resident tekki when it comes to troubleshooting software issues.
At technology conferences, you'll always run into a tekki eager to discuss the latest gadgets.
Her reputation as a tekki helped her land a job at a leading IT company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: techie - a person who is very interested in and knowledgeable about technology, especially computers

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'techie', from 'tech' (short for technology) + -ie (diminutive suffix).
