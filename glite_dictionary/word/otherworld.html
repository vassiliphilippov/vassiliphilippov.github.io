otherworld

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌðərˌwɜrld / (AmE), / ˈʌðəˌwɜːld / (BrE)
a world or realm believed to exist beyond the physical one, often associated with the spiritual or supernatural
spiritual world beyond the physical

Examples

In many cultures, people believe in an otherworld that souls enter after death.
Legends often describe heroes venturing into the otherworld to seek wisdom or rescue the lost.
The book explores the boundaries between reality and the mysterious otherworld of spirits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: afterlife - life after death; existence beyond this world
