acronymous

1. ADJ

/ əˈkrɑː.nɪ.məs / (AmE), / əˈkrɒ.nɪ.məs / (BrE)
(of language or text) characterized by the use of acronyms.
using or full of acronyms

Examples

The company's acronymous name confused many people outside the industry.
Her writing style is unusually acronymous, filled with terms like FAQ, CEO, and NGO.
Government agencies often have acronymous titles that are hard to remember.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acronymic - relating to or formed as an acronym

Etymology

From 'acronym' + '-ous', formed after similar adjectives in English.
