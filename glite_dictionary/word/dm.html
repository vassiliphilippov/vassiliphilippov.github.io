dm

1. NOUN

/ diː ˈɛm / (AmE), / diː ˈɛm / (BrE)
a metric unit of length equal to one tenth of a meter
metric unit, tenth of a meter

Examples

The table measures 15 dm in length, which is 1.5 meters.
The experiment required measuring water to an accuracy of 0.1 dm.
Ten dm together make one meter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decimeter - a metric unit of length equal to one tenth of a meter

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'decimeter,' from French 'décimètre,' from Latin 'decimus' (tenth) + 'metrum' (measure).
