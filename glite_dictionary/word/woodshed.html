woodshed

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdˌʃɛd / (AmE), / ˈwʊdʃɛd / (BrE)
a small shed used for storing firewood or sometimes garden tools, typically located near a house or in a backyard
shed for storing firewood

Examples

He stacked the logs neatly in the woodshed behind the house.
After a long winter, the woodshed was almost empty.
The old woodshed also serves as storage for gardening tools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tool shed - a shed for storing garden tools

Etymology

From wood + shed, indicating a shed for wood storage.
