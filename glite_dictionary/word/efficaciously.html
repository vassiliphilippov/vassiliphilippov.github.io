efficaciously

1. ADV

/ ˌɛfɪˈkeɪʃəsli / (AmE), / ˌɛfɪˈkeɪʃəsli / (BrE)
in a way that produces the intended or desired result; effectively
in an effective manner

Examples

The treatment worked efficaciously to reduce the patient's symptoms.
Only by acting efficaciously can we solve such complex problems.
The policies were enforced efficaciously by the new management team.

Synset Examples

these are real problems that can be dealt with most effectively by rational discussion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: effectively - in a way that achieves the desired result

Etymology

Formed from 'efficacious' + '-ly'. 'Efficacious' from Latin efficax (efficient, effective).
