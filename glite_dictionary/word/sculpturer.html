sculpturer

1. NOUN

/ ˈskʌlp.tʃər.ər / (AmE), / ˈskʌlp.tʃər.ə / (BrE)
an artist who creates sculptures
artist who creates sculptures

Examples

The sculpturer spent months shaping the marble into a graceful statue for the city square.
As a child, she was inspired by a visit to a sculpturer's workshop.
The museum hosted an exhibition featuring the works of a renowned sculpturer from the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sculptor - an artist who makes sculptures, more common term

Etymology

From sculpture + -er (agent noun suffix).
