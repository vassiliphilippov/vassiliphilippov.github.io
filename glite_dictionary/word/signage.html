signage

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪnɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnɪdʒ / (BrE)
Signs collectively, especially those used for commercial or public display.
signs collectively, especially commercial signs

Examples

The new shopping mall features modern signage to help customers find their way.
City regulations require all signage to be clearly visible and well-maintained.
Temporary signage was placed at the entrance to inform visitors of the construction.

Synset Examples

there will be signage displayed at each post

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sign - a notice or display for public view

Etymology

Formed from 'sign' + '-age' (a suffix meaning collective or related to); first recorded in the early 20th century.
