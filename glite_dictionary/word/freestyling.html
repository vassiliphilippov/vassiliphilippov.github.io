freestyling

1. NOUN

/ ˈfriˌstaɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfriːstaɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of rapping in which the performer improvises lyrics while performing, without pre-written material.
improvisational rapping

Examples

He impressed the crowd with his freestyling during the rap battle.
Some artists spend hours freestyling to sharpen their lyrical skills.
Freestyling lets rappers showcase their creativity on the spot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freestyle - a style of improvisation in rap or dance

Etymology

From 'freestyle' (improvised) + '-ing', reflecting an adaptation from sports and art domains to hip hop culture.
