lingberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪŋˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋˌbɛri / (BrE)
A low, evergreen shrub found in northern temperate regions, bearing small red edible berries.
northern shrub with edible red berries

Examples

Wild lingberry grows abundantly in the forests of Scandinavia.
She picked a basket full of ripe lingberries to make jam.
The tart flavor of the lingberry complements sweet desserts perfectly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lingonberry - shrub with edible red berries, used especially in Scandinavian cuisine
