Laundromat

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔːndroʊˌmæt / (AmE), / ˈlɔːndrəˌmat / (BrE)
a self-service laundry facility where customers operate coin- or card-operated washing machines and dryers
self-service laundry facility

Examples

She spent Saturday morning at the Laundromat washing and drying her clothes.
There's a 24-hour Laundromat just around the corner.
Most students in the city use the Laundromat on Main Street for their laundry needs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: launderette - a self-service laundry, especially in British English

Etymology

Originally a US trademark coined in the 1940s, from 'laundry' + '-omat' (as in Automat).
