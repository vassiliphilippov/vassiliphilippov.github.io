unromantically

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.roʊˈmæn.tɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rəʊˈmæn.tɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a way that is not romantic; lacking sentiment or romance.
in an unromantic manner

Examples

They celebrated their anniversary unromantically by cleaning the garage together.
She looked unromantically at the practical details of their partnership.
The proposal was made unromantically over the phone rather than in person.

Synset Examples

we got married, rather unromantically, in a dingy office in the town hall

Related Terms

  • Antonym: romantically - in a romantic manner
  • Synonym: unromantic - not characterized by romance or sentiment
