Carolinas

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɛr.əˈlaɪ.nəz / (AmE), / ˌkær.əˈlaɪ.nəz / (BrE)
the area of the states of North Carolina and South Carolina, considered together as a region of the southeastern United States
region of North and South Carolina

Examples

Tourists love to visit the Carolinas for their beaches and historic sites.
The Carolinas experienced heavy rainfall during the hurricane season.
Many traditions in the Carolinas reflect a blend of Southern and coastal culture.
