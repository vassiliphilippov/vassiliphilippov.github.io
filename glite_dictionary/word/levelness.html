levelness

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛvəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈlɛv(ə)lnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being level, flat, or even; uniform height throughout.
the state of being level or even

Examples

The engineer was concerned about the levelness of the surface before installing the machinery.
Repeated measurements confirmed the levelness of the newly constructed tennis court.
Too much rain can affect the levelness of the soil in your garden.

Synset Examples

The inspector checked the levelness of the floor before approving the work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evenness - the quality of being even, flat, or uniform
  • Synonym: flatness - the quality or condition of being flat or having a level surface
  • Antonym: unevenness - the state of not being even, level, or uniform
