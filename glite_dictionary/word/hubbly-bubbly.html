hubbly-bubbly

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌbli ˌbʌbli / (AmE), / ˈhʌbli ˌbʌbli / (BrE)
a water pipe for smoking flavored tobacco, in which the smoke passes through water before being inhaled; also known as a hookah or shisha
water pipe for smoking flavored tobacco

Examples

We shared a hubbly-bubbly and tea at the sidewalk café.
The lounge offers a wide selection of flavored tobaccos for the hubbly-bubbly.
They relaxed in the garden, each enjoying a turn with the hubbly-bubbly.

Synset Examples

a bipolar world with the hookah and Turkish coffee versus hamburgers and Coca Cola

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hookah - water pipe for smoking tobacco
  • Synonym: shisha - water pipe for smoking, commonly used in Middle Eastern cultures

Etymology

Imitative of the sound of bubbling water in the pipe.
