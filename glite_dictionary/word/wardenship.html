wardenship

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔrdənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈwɔːdənʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or office of a warden
position of a warden

Examples

After years of dedication, she was finally promoted to the wardenship of the prison.
The wardenship of the college required both administrative skills and strong leadership.
Throughout his wardenship, significant reforms were implemented.

Related Terms
