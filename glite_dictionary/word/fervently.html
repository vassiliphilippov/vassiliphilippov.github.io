fervently

1. ADV

/ ˈfɜrvəntli / (AmE), / ˈfɜːvəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows intense passion or enthusiasm.
with intense passion or enthusiasm

Examples

She fervently believes in the importance of equal rights for all.
He fervently denied the accusations during the press conference.
The crowd fervently cheered for their team throughout the entire match.

Synset Examples

both those for and against are fervently convinced they speak for the great majority of the people
a fierily opinionated book

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passionately - in a way that shows strong feelings or enthusiasm
  • Antonym: apathetically - without interest or enthusiasm

Etymology

From Latin fervēre (to boil, glow), via Old French and Middle English.
