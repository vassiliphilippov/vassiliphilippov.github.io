decolletage

1. NOUN

/ ˌdeɪˌkɑːləˈtɑːʒ / (AmE), / ˌdeɪˌkɒlɪˈtɑːʒ / (BrE)
A low neckline on a woman's dress or top, revealing part of the chest and shoulders.
low neckline on a woman's dress or top

Examples

The actress wore a stunning gown with a daring decolletage to the awards ceremony.
Some fashion designers specialize in dresses with intricate decolletage details.
She chose a more modest outfit that covered her decolletage for the family event.

Etymology

French décolletage, from décolleter (to expose the neck).
