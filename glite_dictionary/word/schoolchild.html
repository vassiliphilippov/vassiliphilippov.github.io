schoolchild

1. NOUN

/ ˈskuːlˌtʃaɪld / (AmE), / ˈskuːlˌtʃaɪld / (BrE)
a child who attends school, especially one in primary or secondary education
a child who goes to school

Examples

The schoolchild carried her backpack as she walked to class every morning.
Each schoolchild received a free lunch at noon.
During the assembly, every schoolchild sat quietly in the gym.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pupil - a student in school
  • Synonym: student - a person who is studying at a school or college

Etymology

Derived from 'school' + 'child'.
