Rodney George Laver

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɑdni ˈdʒɔrdʒ ˈleɪvər / (AmE), / ˈrɒdni ˈdʒɔːdʒ ˈleɪvə / (BrE)
(born 1938) Australian tennis player renowned for winning all four major singles titles in a calendar year twice, in 1962 and 1969.
Australian tennis champion, Grand Slam winner

Examples

Rodney George Laver is the only tennis player to achieve the Grand Slam twice in men's singles.
Many consider Rodney George Laver one of the greatest athletes in tennis history.
The center court at Melbourne Park is named after Rodney George Laver in his honor.
