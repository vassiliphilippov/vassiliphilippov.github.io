gadget

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡædʒət / (AmE), / ˈɡædʒɪt / (BrE)
a small device or tool that is useful for a particular purpose, often a new invention or piece of technology
small, useful device or tool

Examples

She always keeps a new gadget in her kitchen to make cooking easier.
The latest gadget on the market claims to help you track your fitness more efficiently.
He couldn't resist buying the clever little gadget at the electronics store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: device - an object or piece of equipment designed for a specific task

Etymology

Origin unknown; first recorded in late 19th century, possibly from French 'gâchette' (a catchpiece of a mechanism).
