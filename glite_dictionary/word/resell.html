to resell

1. VERB

/ riˈsɛl / (AmE), / ˌriːˈsɛl / (BrE)
to sell something again, especially after having bought it
sell again after buying

Examples

After upgrading her phone, she decided to resell the old one online.
Some companies resell surplus stock to other businesses at a discount.
You need a special permit if you plan to resell concert tickets.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: buy - to acquire something by paying for it
  • Antonym: purchase - to acquire by buying; obtain by payment
  • Synonym: sell - to give or hand over something in exchange for money

Etymology

From re- (again) + sell, meaning to sell again.
