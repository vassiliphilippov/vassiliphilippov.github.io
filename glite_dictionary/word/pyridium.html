Pyridium

1. NOUN

/ pɪˈrɪdiəm / (AmE), / pɪˈrɪdiəm / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for an analgesic drug (phenazopyridine) used to relieve pain and discomfort in urinary tract infections.
analgesic drug for urinary tract infections
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Pyridium to help ease the burning sensation during urination.
After starting Pyridium, she noticed her urine changed color to orange-red.
Pyridium should not be used for more than two days without consulting a physician.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phenazopyridine - the generic name for the active ingredient in Pyridium
