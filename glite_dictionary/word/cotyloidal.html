cotyloidal

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑtəˈlɔɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌkɒtɪˈlɔɪdəl / (BrE)
Relating to a cup-shaped socket, especially the cavity in the hip bone that receives the head of the femur.
relating to a cup-shaped hip socket
medical

Examples

The surgeon inspected the cotyloidal surface for any signs of damage before proceeding.
A malformed cotyloidal cavity can result in hip dislocation or arthritis.
The implant was designed to fit securely in the patient's cotyloidal region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acetabular - relating to the acetabulum (hip socket)

Etymology

From Greek kotyle (cup, cavity) + -oid (like) + -al (adjectival suffix).
