nose-pick

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊzˌpɪk / (AmE), / ˈnəʊzˌpɪk / (BrE)
An act of picking one's nose; also, a person who engages in this behavior, often used disparagingly.
picking one's nose or a person who does it
informal

Examples

The little boy's nose-pick during class embarrassed his mother.
She called him a nose-pick for not using a handkerchief.
A hidden camera caught the celebrity in a rather awkward nose-pick.

Synset Examples

Don't be a nose-pick; use a tissue instead.
The kid's nose-pick was caught on camera.
