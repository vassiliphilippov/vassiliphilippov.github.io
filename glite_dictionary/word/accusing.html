accusing

1. ADJ

/ əˈkjuːzɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈkjuːzɪŋ / (BrE)
showing or suggesting that someone has done something wrong or is to blame.
indicating blame or accusation

Examples

She gave him an accusing look when she saw the empty cookie jar.
His accusing tone made everyone in the room uncomfortable.
The accusing finger pointed straight at her.

Synset Examples

an accusative forefinger
black accusatory looks
accusive shoes and telltale trousers
his accusing glare

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accusatory - containing or suggesting an accusation
  • Antonym: defensive - intended to defend or protect from blame
