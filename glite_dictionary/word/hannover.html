Hannover

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɑːnoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈhanəʊvə / (BrE)
A city in northwestern Germany, known for its port and as a former member of the Hanseatic League.
city in northwestern Germany

Examples

The industrial fair in Hannover attracts thousands of visitors every year.
She studied at the university in Hannover before moving to Berlin.
Many historic buildings in Hannover were rebuilt after World War II.

Etymology

German name, originally *Honovere*, possibly meaning 'high bank' or 'high shore'.
