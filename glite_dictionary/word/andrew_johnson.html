Andrew Johnson

1. PROPN

/ ˈændru ˈdʒɑnsən / (AmE), / ˈandru ˈdʒɒnsən / (BrE)
the 17th President of the United States (1808–1875), who succeeded Abraham Lincoln after his assassination and was impeached but acquitted by one vote
