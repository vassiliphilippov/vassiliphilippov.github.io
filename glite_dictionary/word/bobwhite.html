bobwhite

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑbˌwaɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɒbˌwaɪt / (BrE)
A small North American quail with a distinctive call, often hunted as a game bird.
North American quail species

Examples

We spotted a bobwhite near the edge of the forest this morning.
The farmer heard the familiar call of the bobwhite as dusk fell.
A flock of bobwhites scattered as the hunter approached.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quail - a small, short-tailed game bird

Etymology

Named for its call, which sounds like 'bob-white.'
