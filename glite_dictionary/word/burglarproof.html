burglarproof

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɜrɡlərˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈbɜːɡləˌpruːf / (BrE)
designed to be secure against burglary; difficult or impossible for burglars to break into
secure against burglary

Examples

The house was equipped with burglarproof windows and doors for added security.
She wanted a burglarproof safe for her valuable jewelry and documents.
Many new cars offer burglarproof locking systems to prevent theft.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burglar-resistant - resistant to burglary but not completely secure

Etymology

Formed from 'burglar' + 'proof' (meaning resistant to).
