yellow-green

1. ADJ

/ ˌjɛloʊˈɡrin / (AmE), / ˌjɛləʊˈɡriːn / (BrE)
of a color that is midway between yellow and green
color between yellow and green

Examples

The leaves turned a beautiful yellow-green in early spring.
She wore a dress of yellow-green satin to the party.
The artist mixed paints to create the perfect yellow-green shade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chartreuse - a shade of yellow-green
  • Synonym: lime - a bright yellowish-green color
