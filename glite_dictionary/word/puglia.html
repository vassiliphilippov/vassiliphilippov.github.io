Puglia

1. PROPN

/ ˈpuːljə / (AmE), / ˈpuːlja / (BrE)
a region in southeastern Italy, located on the Adriatic Sea, known for its historic towns, olive oil production, and distinctive architecture
region in southeastern Italy

Examples

Many tourists visit Puglia for its beautiful coastline and delicious cuisine.
The architecture in Puglia is famous for its whitewashed buildings and ancient trulli.
My grandparents came from a small village in Puglia before moving to America.
