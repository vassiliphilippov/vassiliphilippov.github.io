Jean Baptiste Camille Corot

1. PROPN

/ ʒɑ̃ bæpˈtist kəˈmiːl kɔˈroʊ / (AmE), / ʒɑ̃ bæpˈtiːst kæˈmiːl kɒˈro / (BrE)
(1796–1875) A French painter renowned for his landscapes, particularly scenes of Italy, and a key figure in the development of landscape painting.
French landscape painter (1796–1875)

Examples

The museum's new exhibit features several paintings by Jean Baptiste Camille Corot.
Art historians regard Jean Baptiste Camille Corot as a major influence on the Impressionists.
During his travels in Italy, Jean Baptiste Camille Corot produced some of his most celebrated landscapes.
