chafing

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃeɪfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪfɪŋ / (BrE)
soreness or irritation of the skin caused by rubbing or friction
skin irritation from friction

Examples

Prolonged running can cause chafing on the inner thighs if proper clothing is not worn.
The hiker applied ointment to reduce chafing after a long day on the trail.
To prevent chafing, many athletes use special powders or balms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rash - an area of irritated or swollen skin

Etymology

From verb 'chafe,' from Old French 'chaufer' (to warm), from Latin 'calefacere.'
