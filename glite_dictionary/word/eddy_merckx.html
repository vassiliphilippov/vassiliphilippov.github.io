Eddy Merckx

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛdi ˈmɛrks / (AmE), / ˈɛdi ˈmɛːks / (BrE)
(born 1945) A Belgian professional racing cyclist, widely considered one of the greatest cyclists of all time, who won the Tour de France five times.
Belgian cyclist, five-time Tour de France winner

Examples

Eddy Merckx dominated professional cycling in the 1970s, winning multiple grand tours and classics.
Many cycling fans consider Eddy Merckx to be the greatest rider in history.
During the 1969 Tour de France, Eddy Merckx took the yellow jersey by a record margin.
