Alexandre Gustave Eiffel

1. PROPN

/ æl.ɪɡˈzɑːndɹə ɡʊsˈtɑːv ˈaɪ.fəl / (AmE), / al.ɪɡˈzan.drə ɡʊsˈtɑːv ˈaɪ.fəl / (BrE)
(1832–1923) a French engineer best known for designing and overseeing the construction of the Eiffel Tower in Paris.
French engineer, designer of the Eiffel Tower

Examples

The Eiffel Tower was designed by Alexandre Gustave Eiffel in the late nineteenth century.
As a civil engineer, Alexandre Gustave Eiffel contributed greatly to French architecture.
Many tourists in Paris learn about Alexandre Gustave Eiffel when visiting his iconic tower.
