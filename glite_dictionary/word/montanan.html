Montanan

1. NOUN

/ mɑnˈtænən / (AmE), / mɒnˈtænən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or resident of Montana, a state in the United States
person from Montana

Examples

As a proud Montanan, she loves hiking in the Rocky Mountains every summer.
The Montanan community welcomed the new residents warmly.
He identifies as a Montanan, even though he was born in another state.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Montana - a state in the northwestern United States

Etymology

From Montana (U.S. state) + -an (denoting association or origin).
