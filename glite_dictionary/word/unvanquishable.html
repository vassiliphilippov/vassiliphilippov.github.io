unvanquishable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈvæŋkwɪʃəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈvaŋkwɪʃəbl / (BrE)
impossible to defeat, overcome, or subdue
impossible to defeat

Examples

Despite the difficulties, she showed an unvanquishable determination to reach her goals.
The team's unvanquishable spirit inspired everyone in the stadium.
He faced every challenge with an unvanquishable sense of hope.

Synset Examples

an invincible army
her invincible spirit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invincible - too powerful to be defeated or overcome

Etymology

From Latin 'un-' (not) + 'vanquish' (to defeat) + '-able' (able to be).
