unfretted

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfrɛtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfrɛtɪd / (BrE)
(of a stringed musical instrument) having no frets on the fingerboard.
without frets (musical instrument)

Examples

The violin is an unfretted instrument, allowing for continuous changes in pitch.
She preferred the unfretted guitar for certain traditional musical styles.
Playing an unfretted instrument requires precise finger placement.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fretted - having frets on the fingerboard (of an instrument)

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'fretted', from 'fret' (raised bar on instrument fingerboard).
