jammies

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒæmiz / (AmE), / ˈdʒæmiz / (BrE)
(informal) Loose-fitting clothes consisting of a top and trousers, worn for sleeping or lounging.
informal word for pajamas
informal

Examples

The kids put on their jammies before bedtime every night.
She spent the whole Saturday morning relaxing in her favorite jammies.
Don’t forget to pack your jammies for the sleepover.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pajamas - Loose-fitting clothes for sleeping, usually consisting of a jacket and trousers

Etymology

Diminutive form of 'pajamas'.
