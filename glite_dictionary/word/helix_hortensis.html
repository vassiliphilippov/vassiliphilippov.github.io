Helix hortensis

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiːlɪks hɔːrˈtɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˈhiːlɪks hɔːˈtɛnsɪs / (BrE)
a species of small garden snail native to western Europe, known for its banded shell
a species of banded garden snail

Examples

The Helix hortensis is often found in moist gardens across western Europe.
Naturalists studied the shell patterns of Helix hortensis to distinguish it from related species.
Unlike the common brown snail, Helix hortensis typically has a lighter, banded shell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garden snail - a common name for snails found in gardens

Etymology

From Latin: 'helix' (spiral, snail) and 'hortensis' (of the garden).
