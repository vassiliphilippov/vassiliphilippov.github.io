ambiance

1. NOUN

/ ˈæmbiˌɑns / (AmE), / ˈæmbiˌɑːns / (BrE)
the character, mood, or atmosphere of a place or situation
the mood or atmosphere of a place

Examples

The restaurant's ambiance was warm and inviting, perfect for a romantic dinner.
Soft lighting and gentle music created an elegant ambiance at the party.
The cozy ambiance of the café made it a favorite spot for locals.

Synset Examples

there was an atmosphere of excitement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atmosphere - the pervading tone or mood of a place

Etymology

From French ambiance (environment, atmosphere), from Latin ambiēns, present participle of ambīre (to surround).

2. NOUN

/ ˈæmbiˌɑns / (AmE), / ˈæmbiɒns / (BrE)
the character and mood of a particular environment, especially as created by its surroundings and conditions
the mood or atmosphere of a place

Examples

The cozy ambiance of the restaurant made it the perfect place for a romantic dinner.
Soft lighting and gentle music contributed to the warm ambiance of the hotel lobby.
She loves coffee shops with a relaxed ambiance where she can work quietly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atmosphere - the pervading tone or mood of a place, situation, or creative work

Etymology

From French 'ambiance' meaning 'surroundings, environment'.
