to kick-start

1. VERB

/ kɪk stɑrt / (AmE), / kɪk stɑːt / (BrE)
to start a motorcycle engine by pushing down a lever with the foot
start a motorcycle by foot lever

Examples

He tried several times to kick-start his old motorcycle, but the engine wouldn't turn over.
You need to kick-start the bike before you can ride it.
After fixing the chain, she managed to kick-start her scooter and continued her journey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: start - to cause an engine or machine to begin working
