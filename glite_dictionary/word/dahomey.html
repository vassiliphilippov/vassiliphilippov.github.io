Dahomey

1. PROPN

/ dəˈhoʊmi / (AmE), / dəˈhəʊmi / (BrE)
a former country in West Africa, now called Benin, which was under French colonial rule until 1960
former country in West Africa, now Benin
archaic

Examples

The Kingdom of Dahomey was known for its powerful armies and complex culture.
In 1975, Dahomey was renamed the Republic of Benin.
Many historical documents from the colonial period refer to the region as Dahomey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Benin - country in West Africa, formerly known as Dahomey

Etymology

From the name of the Kingdom of Dahomey, now part of modern Benin.
