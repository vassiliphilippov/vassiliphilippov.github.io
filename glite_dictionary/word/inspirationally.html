inspirationally

1. ADV

/ ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌɪnspɪˈreɪʃnəli / (BrE)
In an inspiring manner; in a way that motivates or encourages others.
in an inspiring manner

Examples

The leader spoke inspirationally about overcoming adversity during the ceremony.
She writes inspirationally on topics of hope and perseverance.
Athletes often act inspirationally both on and off the field.

Synset Examples

he talked inspirationally

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspiringly - in a manner that inspires
