corpulency

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔːr.pjə.lən.si / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.pjʊ.lən.si / (BrE)
The state of being excessively fat or overweight; obesity.
excessive fatness; obesity

Examples

The doctor warned that corpulency increases the risk of heart disease.
He struggled with corpulency throughout much of his adult life.
Researchers are studying the genetic factors that contribute to corpulency in the population.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obesity - the condition of being very overweight
  • Antonym: leanness - the quality of being thin and lacking fat

Etymology

From Latin 'corpulentia', from 'corpulēns' meaning 'fleshy, fat'.
