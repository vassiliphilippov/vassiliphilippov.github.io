to defeminise

1. VERB

/ dɪˈfɛmɪˌnaɪz / (AmE), / dɪˈfiːmɪnaɪz / (BrE)
to remove the ovaries of a female mammal, making it unable to reproduce or show female characteristics
remove ovaries of a female mammal
medical

Examples

The veterinarian will defeminise the cat to prevent it from having kittens.
After being defeminised, the animal showed a change in hormone levels.
Researchers often defeminise lab rats as part of reproductive studies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to spay - to remove the ovaries of a female animal

Etymology

From de- (removal of) + feminise; feminise from Latin femina (woman).
