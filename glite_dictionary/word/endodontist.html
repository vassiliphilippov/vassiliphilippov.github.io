endodontist

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛn.doʊˈdɑn.tɪst / (AmE), / ˌen.dəʊˈdɒn.tɪst / (BrE)
a dentist who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases and injuries of the dental pulp and nerves
dentist specializing in dental pulp and nerve diseases
medical

Examples

After she broke her tooth, she was referred to an endodontist for a root canal treatment.
The endodontist used special techniques to save the damaged nerve inside the molar.
Many general dentists recommend seeing an endodontist for complex root canal procedures.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: dentist - a person qualified to treat the diseases and conditions that affect the teeth and gums
  • Synonym: orthodontist - a dentist specializing in the correction of irregularities of the teeth

Etymology

From endodontics (< Greek endon 'within' + odont- 'tooth' + -ist).
