to braise

1. VERB

/ breɪz / (AmE), / breɪz / (BrE)
to cook (meat or vegetables) slowly in a small amount of liquid in a covered container
cook slowly in liquid

Examples

The chef decided to braise the lamb shanks in red wine and herbs.
You should braise the vegetables until they are tender and full of flavor.
After searing the beef, she began to braise it with onions and carrots.

Synset Examples

braise beef

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stew - cook slowly in liquid
  • Antonym: roast - cook with dry heat in an oven

Etymology

From French 'braiser' (to stew in a tightly closed pan).
