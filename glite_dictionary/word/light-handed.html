light-handed

1. ADJ

/ laɪt ˈhændɪd / (AmE), / laɪt ˈhændɪd / (BrE)
dealing with something in a subtle or unobtrusive way; not heavy-handed or forceful.
subtle or unobtrusive in approach

Examples

The director took a light-handed approach, allowing the story to unfold naturally.
His light-handed guidance helped the team develop their own solutions.
Reviewers praised the light-handed editing that preserved the author's unique voice.

Synset Examples

the translation is … light-handed … and generally unobtrusive

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heavy-handed - overly forceful or oppressive in approach
