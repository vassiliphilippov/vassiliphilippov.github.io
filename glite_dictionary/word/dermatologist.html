dermatologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɝː.məˈtɑː.lə.dʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌdɜː.məˈtɒl.ə.dʒɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in diagnosing and treating conditions of the skin, hair, and nails
doctor specializing in skin conditions
medical

Examples

The dermatologist examined the rash and prescribed a topical cream.
After noticing unusual moles, she scheduled an appointment with a dermatologist.
Many people visit a dermatologist to get treatment for acne.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skin doctor - a doctor specializing in skin conditions; informal synonym
  • Antonym: general practitioner - a doctor who treats various common medical conditions; antonym for specialist doctors like dermatologist

Etymology

From Greek dermato- (skin) + -logist (one who studies).
