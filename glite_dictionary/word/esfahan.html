Esfahan

1. PROPN

/ ˌɛsfɑːˈhɑːn / (AmE), / ˌɛsfəˈhɑːn / (BrE)
A city in central Iran, known for its Persian architecture and as a former capital of Persia.
city in central Iran, historic Persian capital

Examples

On our trip through Iran, we spent two days exploring Esfahan and its famous square.
The bridges and mosques of Esfahan attract visitors from all over the world.
Esfahan served as the capital of Persia during the Safavid dynasty.

Etymology

From Persian اِصفَهان (Esfahān).
