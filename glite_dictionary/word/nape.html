nape

1. NOUN

/ neɪp / (AmE), / neɪp / (BrE)
the back part of a person's neck
back of the neck

Examples

She felt a cool breeze against the nape of her neck as she walked outside.
The kitten curled up and rested its head on the nape of her neck.
A drop of sweat rolled down the nape of his neck during the workout.

Etymology

From Middle English *nape*, from Old French *nape* (tablecloth, napkin), possibly influenced by the anatomical use in English.
