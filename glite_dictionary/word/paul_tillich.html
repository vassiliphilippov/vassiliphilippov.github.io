Paul Tillich

1. PROPN

/ pɔl ˈtɪlɪk / (AmE), / pɔːl ˈtɪlɪk / (BrE)
(1886–1965) German-born American theologian and philosopher known for his influential works on Christian existentialism and theology.
German-American theologian and philosopher

Examples

Paul Tillich is considered one of the most influential theologians of the twentieth century.
Many philosophy students study the works of Paul Tillich in their courses on existentialism.
The ideas of Paul Tillich remain central to contemporary discussions in theology and religious studies.
