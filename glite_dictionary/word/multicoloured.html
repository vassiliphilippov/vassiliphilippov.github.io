multicoloured

1. ADJ

/ ˌmʌltiˈkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈmʌltɪˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having many colors; made up of several different colors, often brightly so
having many colors

Examples

She wore a multicoloured scarf that brightened up her whole outfit.
The walls of the children's room were decorated with multicoloured stickers and drawings.
A flock of multicoloured parrots flew over the lush rainforest.

Synset Examples

a jester dressed in motley
the painted desert
a particolored dress
a piebald horse
pied daisies

Related Terms

  • Antonym: monochrome - having only one color
  • Synonym: colorful - full of color; vivid or brightly colored

Etymology

From Latin multi- (many) + English coloured.
