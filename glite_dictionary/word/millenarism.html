millenarism

1. NOUN

/ mɪˈlɛnəˌrɪzəm / (AmE), / mɪˈlɛnərɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief in a coming Christian millennium—a thousand-year period of holiness and happiness on earth, especially as described in the Book of Revelation.
belief in a future Christian millennium

Examples

Early Protestant groups often embraced millenarism as part of their religious reforms.
His writings show a clear influence of millenarism and expectations of a utopian future.
The rise of millenarism has often coincided with periods of social upheaval.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: millenarianism - belief in the coming of a Christian millennium
  • Synonym: chiliasm - another term for millenarism, belief in a thousand-year reign of Christ

Etymology

From Latin 'millenarius' (containing a thousand), from 'mille' (thousand).
