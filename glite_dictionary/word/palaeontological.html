palaeontological

1. ADJ

/ ˌpeɪliˌɑntəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpæliˌɒntəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to paleontology, the scientific study of life in the geological past through fossils
relating to paleontology

Examples

The museum houses an impressive palaeontological collection from the Jurassic period.
She published several palaeontological studies on the evolution of early mammals.
Funding for palaeontological expeditions has increased in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paleontological - relating to paleontology (US spelling)

Etymology

From Greek 'palaios' (ancient) + 'ontos' (being) + 'logia' (study).
