Dobrich

1. PROPN

/ ˈdoʊ.brɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈdɒ.brɪtʃ / (BrE)
a city in northeastern Bulgaria that serves as the commercial center of its surrounding agricultural region.
city in northeastern Bulgaria

Examples

The city of Dobrich is known for its agricultural markets and historic architecture.
After their holiday in Varna, they traveled inland to Dobrich to visit friends.
Many people in northeastern Bulgaria commute to Dobrich for work or education.
