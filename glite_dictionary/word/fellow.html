fellow

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛl.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈfel.əʊ / (BrE)
an informal or friendly way of addressing a man, often used to attract his attention or as a term of mild familiarity
informal form of address for a man
informal

Examples

Listen here, fellow, that's not the right way to do it.

/ ˈfɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɛləʊ / (BrE)
