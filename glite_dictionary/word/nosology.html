nosology

1. NOUN

/ noʊˈsɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / nəʊˈsɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of medical science that deals with the classification of diseases
classification of diseases (medical science)
medical

Examples

The development of modern nosology has improved the diagnosis of rare conditions.
Advancements in nosology allow doctors to categorize new diseases more accurately.
She specializes in nosology, working to classify emerging infectious diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pathology - the study of diseases, their causes, and effects

Etymology

From Greek nosos (disease) + -logia (study).
