postoperative

1. ADJ

/ poʊstˈɑːpərətɪv / (AmE), / pəʊstˈɒpərətɪv / (BrE)
occurring or relating to the period following a surgical operation
after a surgical operation
medical

Examples

The patient experienced postoperative discomfort but recovered quickly.
Good postoperative care is essential for successful healing.
Some postoperative complications can be prevented with proper hygiene.

Synset Examples

postoperative complications
postoperative care

Related Terms

  • Antonym: preoperative - occurring before a surgical operation

Etymology

From Latin 'post' (after) and 'operatio' (operation).
