north-east

1. ADV

/ nɔrθˈist / (AmE), / nɔːθˈiːst / (BrE)
To, toward, or in the direction of the northeast.
in the direction of northeast

Examples

The hikers traveled north-east along the trail until they reached the lake.
Storm clouds were gathering north-east of the city by late afternoon.
She gazed north-east toward the distant mountains as the sun rose.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: northeast - in or toward the northeast
  • Antonym: north-west - in or toward the northwest
  • Antonym: south-east - in or toward the southeast
  • Antonym: south-west - in or toward the southwest

Etymology

From Old English 'norþ' (north) and 'east' (east), indicating the compass direction between north and east.
