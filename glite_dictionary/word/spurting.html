to spurting

1. VERB

/ ˈspɝːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspɜːtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of liquid or a substance) being forced out suddenly and forcefully in a usually narrow stream.
forcefully shooting out in a stream

Examples

Blood was spurting from the wound after the accident.
The faucet was broken, with water spurting everywhere in the kitchen.
Lava was spurting out of the volcano during the eruption.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spurt - to gush out suddenly in a stream
