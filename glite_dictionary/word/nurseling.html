nurseling

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɜrs.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɜːs.lɪŋ / (BrE)
an infant who is being nursed, especially by a nurse or mother.
infant being nursed
archaic

Examples

The mother cradled her nurseling gently in her arms each night.
The nurseling slept peacefully as the nurse hummed a lullaby.
Victorian novels often describe the care of a nurseling in great detail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infant - a very young child or baby

Etymology

Formed from 'nurse' + diminutive suffix '-ling', indicating a small or young thing related to a nurse.
