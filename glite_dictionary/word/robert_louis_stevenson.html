Robert Louis Stevenson

1. PROPN

/ ˈroʊbɚt ˈluːɪs ˌstiːvɪnsən / (AmE), / ˈrɒbət ˈluːɪs ˌstiːvnsən / (BrE)
(1850–1894) Scottish novelist, poet, essayist, and travel writer, known for works such as 'Treasure Island' and 'Dr Jekyll and Mr Hyde'.
Scottish writer, author of 'Treasure Island'

Examples

Robert Louis Stevenson wrote several classic adventure novels in the late nineteenth century.
