clippety-clop

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɪpədi ˌklɑp / (AmE), / ˈklɪpəti ˌklɒp / (BrE)
the rhythmic sound made by a horse's hooves striking a hard surface
sound of horse's hooves on hard ground

Examples

The clippety-clop of hooves echoed down the quiet village street.
She listened to the familiar clippety-clop as the carriage rolled past her house.
In the distance, the clippety-clop of a lone horse rider grew steadily louder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clip-clop - the sound of a horse's hooves on a hard surface

Etymology

Imitative, formed to represent the sound of horses' hooves.
