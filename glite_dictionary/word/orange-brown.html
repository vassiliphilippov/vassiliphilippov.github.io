orange-brown

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔrɪndʒˌbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒˌbraʊn / (BrE)
Having a brown color with a tinge or shade of orange.
brown with a hint of orange

Examples

The autumn leaves turned a beautiful orange-brown before falling to the ground.
She wore a coat in a warm orange-brown shade that matched her scarf.
The artist blended the paints to create an orange-brown hue for the landscape.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: brown - the color between red and yellow
  • Degree Higher: orange - the color between red and yellow, resembling the fruit
