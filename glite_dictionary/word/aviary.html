aviary

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪviˌɛri / (AmE), / ˈeɪviəri / (BrE)
a large enclosure or building for keeping birds
enclosure for keeping birds

Examples

The zoo's new aviary features dozens of exotic bird species from around the world.
She walked through the aviary, fascinated by the colorful parrots flying overhead.
A beautiful peacock strutted along the path inside the aviary at the botanical gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birdcage - a small cage for keeping pet birds

Etymology

From Latin 'aviarium' (a place for birds), from 'avis' (bird).
