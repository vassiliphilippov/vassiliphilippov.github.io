feathertop

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛðərˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈfɛðəˌtɒp / (BrE)
A tropical African grass with feathery flower clusters, commonly found in northeastern regions.
a grass with feathery flower clusters

Examples

The ornamental grass known as feathertop adds a soft, airy texture to gardens.
We spotted several clumps of feathertop waving in the breeze during our hike.
Because of its feathery panicles, feathertop is popular in dried floral arrangements.

2. NOUN

/ ˈfɛðərˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈfɛðətɒp / (BrE)
A variety of reed grass with soft, feathery seed heads, often found in meadows and along roadsides.
reed grass with feathery seed heads

Examples

The field was dotted with feathertop swaying gently in the breeze.
Gardeners often remove feathertop to prevent it from spreading in their lawns.
The distinctive plumes of feathertop make it attractive but invasive in some areas.
