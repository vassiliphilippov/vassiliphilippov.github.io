to single crochet

1. VERB

/ ˈsɪŋɡəl kroʊˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl krəʊˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
to make fabric or an item using the single crochet stitch, looping the yarn through one stitch at a time.
make with single crochet stitch

Examples

My grandmother taught me how to single crochet a scarf.
After you single crochet across the row, turn your work and repeat.
She single crocheted a beautiful border around the blanket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crochet - to make fabric using a hooked needle
