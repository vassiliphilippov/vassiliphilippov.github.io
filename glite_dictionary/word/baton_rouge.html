Baton Rouge

1. PROPN

/ ˌbætən ˈruːʒ / (AmE), / ˌbætɒn ˈruːʒ / (BrE)
the capital city of the state of Louisiana, United States, situated on the Mississippi River
capital of Louisiana, US

Examples

She moved to Baton Rouge to attend Louisiana State University.
The conference will be held in Baton Rouge this spring.
Baton Rouge is known for its vibrant culture and cuisine.

Etymology

From French 'bâton rouge' meaning 'red stick', referring to a red-colored cypress pole marking a boundary.
