befittingly

1. ADV

/ bɪˈfɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / bɪˈfɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is appropriate or suitable for the circumstances.
in an appropriate or suitable manner

Examples

The ambassador was befittingly attired for the royal banquet.
She spoke befittingly of his many achievements during the ceremony.
The room was befittingly decorated for the solemn occasion.

Synset Examples

he was appropriately dressed
If you don't behave properly, you'll have to leave!
I met the junior senator from Illinois and I was duly impressed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appropriately - in a suitable or fitting manner
  • Antonym: inappropriately - in an unsuitable or improper way

Etymology

From "befitting" + "-ly"; "befitting" from "be-" (old English intensifier) + "fitten" (fit, suitable).
