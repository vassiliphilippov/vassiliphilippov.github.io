replacing

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈpleɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈpleɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of providing a substitute person or thing in place of another
act of substitution

Examples

Replacing the director was a controversial decision for the company.
The football team's struggle started right after the replacing of their coach.
She handled the replacing of the broken parts efficiently.

Synset Examples

replacing the star will not be easy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: replacement - the action or process of replacing someone or something
