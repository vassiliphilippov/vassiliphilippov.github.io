deranged

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈreɪndʒd / (AmE), / dɪˈreɪndʒd / (BrE)
Mentally disturbed or insane; having lost the ability to think or act in a normal or rational way.
mentally disturbed; insane

Examples

The police described the suspect as a deranged individual acting without reason.
After years of isolation, he became increasingly deranged and unpredictable.
The movie's villain is portrayed as a deranged mastermind plotting chaos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insane - seriously mentally ill; mad
  • Antonym: sane - of sound mind; not mentally ill

Etymology

From the French 'déranger' meaning 'to disturb.'
