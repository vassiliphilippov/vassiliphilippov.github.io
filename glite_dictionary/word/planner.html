planner

1. NOUN

/ ˈplænər / (AmE), / ˈplænə / (BrE)
a notebook or book used for recording appointments, tasks, or schedules; an organizer
notebook for schedules and appointments

Examples

I write all my meetings in my planner so I don't forget them.
Her planner helps her keep track of deadlines and appointments.
He bought a new planner at the start of the year to stay organized.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organizer - a book or device for keeping track of appointments and tasks
Source: WordNet

2. NOUN

/ ˈplænɚ / (AmE), / ˈplænə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make plans for something, especially for managing the development or organization of a particular area, project, or event
person who makes plans

Examples

As a city planner, her work shapes entire neighborhoods.
The planner presented her proposal for the new park layout.
Every successful event relies on the skills of a careful planner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organizer - a person who organizes something

Etymology

From the verb 'plan' + -er, forming agent nouns.
