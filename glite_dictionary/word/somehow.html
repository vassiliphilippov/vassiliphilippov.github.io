somehow

1. ADV

/ ˈsʌm.haʊ / (AmE), / ˈsʌm.haʊ / (BrE)
In some way not specified, understood, or known.
by some unspecified means

Examples

Somehow, she managed to finish the project on time despite all the difficulties.
I don't know how he did it, but somehow he was able to fix the car.
Everything seemed to go wrong, yet somehow they achieved their goal.

Synset Examples

they managed somehow
he expected somehow to discover a woman who would love him
he tried to make it someway acceptable

Related Terms

  • Synonym: someway - by some method; somehow

2. ADV

/ ˈsʌmˌhaʊ / (AmE), / ˈsʌmhaʊ / (BrE)
For a reason that is not known or specified; by a method not known or explained.
for some unspecified reason or means

Examples

Somehow, she managed to finish the project on time despite all the setbacks.
He didn't study for the test, but somehow he still got an A.
Somehow, the lost dog found its way home after several days.

Synset Examples

he had me dead to rights but somehow I got away with it
