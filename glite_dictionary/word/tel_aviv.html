Tel Aviv

1. PROPN

/ tɛl əˈviv / (AmE), / tɛl əˈviːv / (BrE)
the largest city and main financial center of Israel, located in western Israel on the Mediterranean coast
major city in Israel

Examples

Many international companies have offices in Tel Aviv due to its thriving tech industry.
The weather in Tel Aviv is typically warm and sunny throughout the year.
She spent a semester studying at a university in Tel Aviv.

Etymology

From Hebrew *tēl ʾāvīv* meaning 'Hill of Spring'.
