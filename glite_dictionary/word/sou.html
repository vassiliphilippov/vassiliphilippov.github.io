sou

1. NOUN

/ suː / (AmE), / suː / (BrE)
a former French coin of low value, also used to mean a very small amount of money
a former French coin; small sum of money

Examples

He didn't have a sou left after paying all his debts.
She completed the task without earning a single sou.
They inherited barely a sou from their distant relative.

Synset Examples

he hasn't a sou to his name

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farthing - a former British coin of very low value

Etymology

From French *sou*, from Latin *solidus* (a Roman coin).
