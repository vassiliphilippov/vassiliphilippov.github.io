coordinator

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈɔːrdəˌneɪtər / (AmE), / kəʊˈɔːdɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to organize people or groups so that they work together effectively and harmoniously
person who organizes group work

Examples

She was hired as the project coordinator to ensure that all teams collaborated effectively.
The event coordinator managed the schedule and handled all the logistical details.
Each department has its own coordinator who reports directly to the director.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organizer - person who arranges and plans an event or activity
  • Synonym: manager - person responsible for controlling or administering an organization or group of staff

Etymology

From Latin coordinare (to arrange), via coordinate + -or (agent suffix).
