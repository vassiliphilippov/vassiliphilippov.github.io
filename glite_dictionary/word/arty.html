arty

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːrti / (AmE), / ˈɑːti / (BrE)
trying too hard to be artistic or creative, in a way that seems pretentious or affected.
pretentiously artistic or creative
informal

Examples

The decor in his apartment is a bit too arty for my taste.
She wore an arty scarf that made her stand out at the gallery opening.
Some cafés try to create an arty atmosphere with abstract paintings and eclectic furniture.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: artistic - having a genuine natural ability or appreciation for art

Etymology

Formed from 'art' with the suffix '-y', indicating having the qualities of art; dates from the late 19th century.
