Szechwan

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɛtʃˌwɑn / (AmE), / ˈsɛtʃˌwæn / (BrE)
a populous province in south-central China, known for its cuisine and cultural heritage; also spelled Sichuan
province in south-central China

Examples

The Szechwan region is famous for its spicy and flavorful cuisine.
She traveled through Szechwan to explore its natural beauty and historic sites.
Many traditional dishes from Szechwan are now popular around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sichuan - alternative spelling for Szechwan, a province of China

Etymology

From Chinese 四川 (Sìchuān), rendered in English as Szechwan or Szechuan.
