Calcuttan

1. ADJ

/ kælˈkʌtən / (AmE), / kælˈkʌtən / (BrE)
relating to Calcutta (now Kolkata) or its people
of Calcutta or its inhabitants

Examples

The Calcuttan cuisine is famous for its use of spices and seafood.
Many Calcuttan traditions are celebrated during the annual Durga Puja festival.
She spoke with a distinct Calcuttan accent that reminded him of his childhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kolkatan - relating to Kolkata or its inhabitants

Etymology

From Calcutta (former name of Kolkata) + English adjectival suffix -an.
