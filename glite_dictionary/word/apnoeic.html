apnoeic

1. ADJ

/ æpˈniːɪk / (AmE), / apˈniːɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or suffering from apnea (the temporary cessation of breathing, especially during sleep).
relating to apnea; experiencing apnea
medical british

Examples

The child became apnoeic for several seconds after the seizure.
Doctors monitored her apnoeic episodes during sleep using special equipment.
An apnoeic patient may require emergency intervention if breathing does not resume rapidly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apneic - Relating to or characterized by apnea (American spelling)
  • Synonym: apnea - Temporary cessation of breathing, especially during sleep

Etymology

From apnea + -ic, patterned after the Greek root 'apnoia' (want of breath).
