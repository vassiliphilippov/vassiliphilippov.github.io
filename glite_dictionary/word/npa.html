NPA

1. PROPN

/ ˌɛn.piˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛn.piˈeɪ / (BrE)
a Maoist insurgent group in the Philippines, serving as the armed wing of the Communist Party of the Philippines, known for opposing the government and United States military presence.
Philippine Maoist militant group

Examples

The Philippine government declared a ceasefire with the NPA during peace talks.
Several villages have been affected by conflicts between the army and the NPA.
International organizations have monitored the activities of the NPA for human rights violations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Communist Party of the Philippines - the communist political party to which the NPA is affiliated

Etymology

Acronym for New People's Army.
