to hand-build

1. VERB

/ ˈhænd bɪld / (AmE), / ˈhænd bɪld / (BrE)
to make pottery or similar objects by shaping them by hand without using a potter's wheel
make pottery by hand, not wheel

Examples

The artist prefers to hand-build each bowl, giving every piece a unique character.
She hand-built an impressive set of ceramic plates for the exhibition.
They are currently hand-building large vases for the upcoming gallery show.

Synset Examples

This famous potter hand-builds all of her vessels

Related Terms

  • Antonym: throw - to make pottery using a potter's wheel
