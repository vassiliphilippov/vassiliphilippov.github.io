Cymru

1. PROPN

/ ˈkəmruː / (AmE), / ˈkəmrɨ / (BrE)
the Welsh name for Wales, one of the four countries of the United Kingdom
Welsh name for Wales

Examples

He spent his holidays hiking across Cymru, enjoying the stunning Welsh countryside.
The national rugby team of Cymru is known for its passionate supporters.
Many signs in Wales are written in both English and Cymru to reflect its bilingual heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Wales - country in the United Kingdom, called 'Cymru' in Welsh

Etymology

Welsh, from Old Welsh 'Cymry', meaning 'fellow countrymen'.
