superable

1. ADJ

/ ˈsuːpərəbəl / (AmE), / ˈsjuːpərəbəl / (BrE)
Able to be overcome, surmounted, or excelled.
able to be overcome or excelled

Examples

The challenges we face are difficult but ultimately superable with determination and teamwork.
No obstacle is truly insurmountable; every problem is superable in some way.
He believes that prejudice is a superable barrier through education and open dialogue.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: insuperable - impossible to overcome

Etymology

From Latin superabilis, from superare (to overcome).
