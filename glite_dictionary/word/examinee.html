examinee

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˌzæməˈni / (AmE), / ɪɡˌzæmɪˈniː / (BrE)
a person who is taking an examination or test
person taking an examination

Examples

Each examinee was given two hours to complete the test.
The examinee nervously glanced at the clock during the exam.
After reviewing the answers, the teacher provided feedback to every examinee.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: examiner - a person who gives an examination or test

Etymology

From 'examine' + '-ee' (suffix forming nouns meaning the person affected).
