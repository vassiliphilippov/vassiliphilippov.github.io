Jaculus jaculus

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈkjuːləs dʒəˈkjuːləs / (AmE), / dʒəˈkjuːləs dʒəˈkjuːləs / (BrE)
A species of jerboa, a small jumping rodent found in deserts of northern Africa and the Middle East.
a species of jerboa

Examples

The Jaculus jaculus is known for its incredible jumping ability, often leaping several times its own body length.
Researchers observed the nocturnal habits of Jaculus jaculus in the arid deserts of North Africa.
Unlike other rodents, Jaculus jaculus uses its long hind legs to escape predators quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerboa - small jumping desert rodent

Etymology

From Latin 'jaculus' (thrower, jumper), referring to its leaping ability.
