unconsummated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈkɑnsəˌmeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkɒnsjʊˌmeɪtɪd / (BrE)
(especially of a marriage) not completed by having sexual intercourse; not brought to completion.
not completed or fulfilled, often of a marriage

Examples

Their marriage remained unconsummated due to his prolonged illness.
An unconsummated union may be grounds for an annulment in some legal systems.
They lived together for years in an unconsummated relationship.

Synset Examples

an unconsummated marriage can be annulled

Related Terms

  • Antonym: consummated - completed by having sexual intercourse, esp. of a marriage

Etymology

From un- (not) + consummated, from Latin consummare (to complete).
