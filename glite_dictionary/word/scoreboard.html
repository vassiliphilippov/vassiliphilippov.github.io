scoreboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɔːrˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈskɔːbɔːd / (BrE)
a large board that displays the score and other information during a contest, especially in sports
display board for scores in contests

Examples

The electronic scoreboard showed that the home team was in the lead.
Everyone in the stadium watched the scoreboard for the final results.
After every goal, the scoreboard was updated to reflect the new score.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: display board - a board used for displaying information publicly
