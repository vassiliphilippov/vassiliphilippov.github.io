jinxed

1. ADJ

/ dʒɪŋkst / (AmE), / dʒɪŋkst / (BrE)
Having or believed to have bad luck, or to be under a spell of misfortune.
having bad luck; unlucky
informal

Examples

Some people believe the team is jinxed because they have lost every final.
After her third flat tire this month, she wondered if she was jinxed.
The project seemed jinxed from the start, with one problem after another delaying it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unlucky - unfortunate or having bad luck
  • Antonym: fortunate - having good luck

Etymology

From 'jinx,' likely from 19th-century American English, of uncertain origin, popularized in theatrical slang.
