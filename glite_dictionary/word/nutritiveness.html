nutritiveness

1. NOUN

/ nuˈtrɪtɪvnəs / (AmE), / njuˈtrɪtɪvnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being nutritious; the capacity to nourish and promote healthy growth.
capacity to nourish or promote growth

Examples

The nutritiveness of vegetables makes them an essential part of a healthy diet.
Scientists measured the nutritiveness of various grains to recommend better school lunches.
She chose the cereal for its high nutritiveness compared to other breakfast options.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutrition - the process of providing or obtaining food necessary for growth and health
  • Synonym: nutritiousness - the quality of being nutritious
  • Antonym: unwholesomeness - the quality of not being healthy or nourishing

Etymology

Formed from 'nutritive' + '-ness'; 'nutritive' from Latin 'nutritivus'.
