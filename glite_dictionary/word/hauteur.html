hauteur

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈtɜr / (AmE), / əʊˈtɜː / (BrE)
an unfriendly and contemptuous manner that suggests one considers oneself superior to others; arrogance.
arrogant or superior attitude

Examples

His hauteur made it difficult for colleagues to work with him.
She answered the questions with a noticeable hauteur that put everyone off.
The manager's hauteur was evident as he dismissed the staff's suggestions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arrogance - overbearing pride or self-importance
  • Antonym: humility - a modest or low view of one's importance; humbleness

Etymology

From French 'hauteur' (haughtiness), from 'haut' (high).
