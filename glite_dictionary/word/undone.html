undone

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdʌn / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʌn / (BrE)
not done, completed, or finished
not completed or finished

Examples

Several important tasks were left undone at the end of the day.
If you leave your homework undone, you might get into trouble.
She felt uneasy seeing the list of chores still undone on the fridge.

Synset Examples

the work could be done or undone and nobody cared

Related Terms

  • Antonym: done - finished or completed

Etymology

From Old English 'undon', meaning 'to undo', with the prefix 'un-' indicating negation.

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdʌn / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʌn / (BrE)
(of clothing, fastenings, or similar) not fastened, tied, or secured.
not fastened, tied, or secured

Examples

Her coat buttons were undone in the cold wind.
He noticed his shoelaces were undone and stopped to tie them.
The ribbon on the gift had come undone before she opened it.

Synset Examples

her blouse had come undone at the neck
his shoelaces were undone

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fastened - securely closed or tied
  • Antonym: secured - made firm or safe

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'done'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02103884-s
Synset POS: s

3. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdʌn / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʌn / (BrE)
thrown into disorganization or confusion; no longer arranged or functioning in an orderly way.
thrown into disorganization or confusion

Examples

After the sudden resignation of the manager, the team was left undone and struggled to regroup.
Her careful planning was undone by an unexpected change in the schedule.
The negotiations were going smoothly until one error left the entire agreement undone.

Synset Examples

price programs became unstuck because little grain was available

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disorganized - lacking structure or order
  • Synonym: disarrayed - thrown into disorder or confusion
  • Antonym: organized - arranged or structured in a systematic way

Etymology

From Old English 'undōn', meaning 'to reverse a doing, to open', from 'un-' (opposite) + 'don' (to do).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01674290-s
Synset POS: s
