emotionless

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈmoʊʃənləs / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənləs / (BrE)
showing no emotion; not affected by feelings or passion
showing no emotion

Examples

She gave an emotionless reply, betraying nothing of her true thoughts.
His emotionless stare made it hard to guess what he was thinking.
Despite the bad news, her voice remained calm and emotionless.

Synset Examples

he kept his emotionless objectivity and faith in the cause he served
this passionless girl was like an icicle in the sunshine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passionless - having or showing no strong feelings
  • Synonym: unfeeling - not displaying empathy or emotion
  • Synonym: impassive - not showing emotion
  • Antonym: emotional - showing strong feelings

Etymology

From 'emotion' + '-less' (suffix meaning 'without').
