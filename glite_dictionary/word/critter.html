critter

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪdɚ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪtə / (BrE)
an informal or regional term for an animal, especially a small creature or a domestic animal
informal term for animal or creature
informal american

Examples

The barn was full of critters seeking shelter from the storm.
My grandmother always called us kids 'little critters'.
He found a strange-looking critter hiding under the porch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creature - a living being, especially an animal

Etymology

Altered form of 'creature,' influenced by regional American pronunciation.
