recipe

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛsəpi / (AmE), / ˈrɛsɪpi / (BrE)
A set of instructions for preparing a particular dish, including a list of the ingredients required; more generally, a method or plan for achieving a desired result.
instructions for making something, especially food

Examples

I followed the recipe exactly, but the cake didn't rise.
Do you have a recipe for homemade tomato sauce?
Her grandmother's soup recipe has been passed down for generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: formula - a fixed method for doing something or for making something
  • Synonym: method - a particular way of doing something

Etymology

From Latin 'recipere' (to receive), via Latin 'recipe' (imperative of recipere) meaning 'take!'.
