Bull Run

1. PROPN

/ bʊl rʌn / (AmE), / bʊl rʌn / (BrE)
a creek in northeastern Virginia, United States, notable as the site of two major battles during the American Civil War.
creek in Virginia; American Civil War battle site

Examples

The First Battle of Bull Run marked the first major clash of the Civil War.
Many tourists visit Bull Run to learn about American history and walk the old battlefields.
Heavy rains caused flooding along Bull Run last spring, affecting local communities.
