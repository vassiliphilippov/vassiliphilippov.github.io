misbranded

1. ADJ

/ mɪsˈbrændɪd / (AmE), / mɪsˈbrændɪd / (BrE)
(of a product) labeled falsely or misleadingly, especially in violation of statutory regulations.
falsely or illegally labeled
legal

Examples

The shipment was seized because the misbranded food items violated federal regulations.
Pharmaceutical companies face heavy penalties if they distribute misbranded medicines.
Inspectors routinely check for misbranded products on store shelves to protect consumers.

Synset Examples

confiscated the misbranded drugs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: counterfeit - made in imitation with intent to deceive; fake

Etymology

Formed from 'mis-' (wrongly) + 'branded' (marked with a brand or label).
