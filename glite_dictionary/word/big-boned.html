big-boned

1. ADJ

/ bɪɡ boʊnd / (AmE), / bɪɡ bəʊnd / (BrE)
Having a large or heavy bone structure; naturally broad or massive in body frame, rather than due to excess flesh.
having a large or heavy bone structure

Examples

Despite being big-boned, she maintained an active and healthy lifestyle.
Some people are naturally big-boned and broad-shouldered.
The doctor assured him that his big-boned build was perfectly normal.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: slender - having a thin or delicate body structure
