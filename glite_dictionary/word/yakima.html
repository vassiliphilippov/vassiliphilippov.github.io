Yakima

1. PROPN

/ ˈjækɪmɑː / (AmE), / ˈjækɪmɑː / (BrE)
A city in south-central Washington State, known for its agricultural production and location in the Yakima Valley.
city in south-central Washington, US

Examples

I visited Yakima last summer to tour the local vineyards.
The annual fair in Yakima attracts thousands of visitors from around the state.
Yakima is known for producing a large portion of the United States' hops.

Etymology

Named after the Yakama (Yakima) Native American people.
