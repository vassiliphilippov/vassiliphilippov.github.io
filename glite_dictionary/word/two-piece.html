two-piece

1. ADJ

/ ˈtuːˌpis / (AmE), / ˈtuːˌpiːs / (BrE)
(of clothing or other items) made in or consisting of two separate parts.
consisting of two parts (esp. clothing)

Examples

She bought a stylish two-piece suit for her job interview.
During the summer, many people prefer wearing a two-piece swimsuit at the beach.
He chose a two-piece raincoat and overtrousers to stay dry during the hike.

Synset Examples

a two-piece swimsuit

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: three-piece - consisting of three parts, especially clothing
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01654479-a
Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ ˈtuː pis / (AmE), / ˈtuː piːs / (BrE)
a woman's bathing suit consisting of two separate garments, typically a bra top and brief bottoms
woman's bathing suit with two parts

Examples

She wore a stylish two-piece to the beach this summer.
I bought a new two-piece for my vacation in Hawaii.
The two-piece she chose was bright red with white polka dots.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bikini - a very brief two-piece bathing suit for women
  • Antonym: one-piece - a woman's bathing suit made from a single piece of fabric
