Mary Jane

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛri ˌdʒeɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɛːri ˈdʒeɪn / (BrE)
(slang) Marijuana; cannabis.
slang for marijuana
slang

Examples

He was arrested for carrying a small amount of Mary Jane in his backpack.
Some people believe that legalizing Mary Jane would benefit the economy.
She joked that her only vice was occasionally enjoying some Mary Jane.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marijuana - the dried leaves and flowers of the hemp plant used as a drug
  • Synonym: cannabis - the plant or drug derived from it, also known as marijuana

Etymology

From the Anglicization of the Spanish words 'marihuana' or 'marijuana' as 'Mary Jane.'
