anagrams

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.əˌɡræmz / (AmE), / ˈæn.ə.ɡræmz / (BrE)
A word game in which players try to form words from a set of randomly arranged letters.
word game using mixed letters

Examples

We played anagrams for hours and discovered some amazing new words.
She always beats me at anagrams because she's so quick with words.
At the party, the group enjoyed a lively round of anagrams after dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anagram - a word or phrase formed by rearranging the letters of another

Etymology

From Greek 'ana-' (back) + 'gramma' (letter), originally referring to rearranging letters to form new words.
