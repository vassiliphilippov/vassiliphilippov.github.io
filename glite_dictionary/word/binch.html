to binch

1. VERB

/ bɪntʃ / (AmE), / bɪntʃ / (BrE)
(slang) To watch several episodes of a television series or multiple movies in rapid succession, typically in one sitting.
to binge-watch multiple episodes or movies
slang

Examples

We decided to binch the entire first season in one night.
After finishing his work, Tom loves to binch his favorite sitcoms.
Many people prefer to binch shows rather than wait for weekly episodes.

Synset Examples

I heard she decided to binch on the new season.
Let's binch the series over the weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to binge-watch - to watch multiple episodes of a television series in rapid succession

Etymology

Variant spelling influenced by 'binge' and 'watch'; likely originated as playful Internet slang.
