Lumpenus lumpretaeformis

1. NOUN

/ lʌmˈpiːnəs lʌmˌprɛt.iˈfɔːrmɪs / (AmE), / lʌmˈpiːnəs lʌmˌpre.tɪˈfɔːmɪs / (BrE)
a species of fish found in Arctic and northern Atlantic waters, commonly known as the snakeblenny
Arctic and northern Atlantic fish species

Examples

The Lumpenus lumpretaeformis inhabits the cold waters of the northern Atlantic.
Researchers observed a Lumpenus lumpretaeformis near the Arctic seabed during their expedition.
Fishermen occasionally catch Lumpenus lumpretaeformis while trawling in northern waters.

Etymology

From Latin: Lumpenus (genus); lumpretaeformis meaning 'lamprey-shaped'.
