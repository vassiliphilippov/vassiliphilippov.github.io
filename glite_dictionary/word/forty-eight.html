forty-eight

1. NUM

/ ˌfɔrtiˈeɪt / (AmE), / ˌfɔːtiˈeɪt / (BrE)
The number 48; being eight more than forty.
the number 48

Examples

There are forty-eight states in the contiguous United States.
The package weighs exactly forty-eight kilograms.
She finished the race in forty-eight minutes.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty-seven - the number 47; one less than forty-eight
  • Degree Higher: forty-nine - the number 49; one more than forty-eight

Etymology

From Old English 'feowertig' (forty) + 'eahta' (eight).
