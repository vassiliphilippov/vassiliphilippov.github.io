gingival

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɪndʒɪvəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒɪvəl / (BrE)
relating to the gums of the mouth.
relating to the gums
medical

Examples

The dentist noted some gingival inflammation around the patient's molars.
Good oral hygiene is important to maintain healthy gingival tissue.
Gingival bleeding can be an early sign of gum disease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gum - the fleshy tissue in the mouth that surrounds the teeth

Etymology

From Latin 'gingiva' (gum of the mouth) + '-al' (relating to).
