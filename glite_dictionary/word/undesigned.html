undesigned

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɪˈzaɪnd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈzaɪnd / (BrE)
not intended or planned; not done deliberately or with purpose
not intended or planned

Examples

The laughter that erupted was completely undesigned and genuine.
His success in the field was largely undesigned, the result of fortunate circumstances.
She appreciated the undesigned charm of the rustic cottage.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intentional - done on purpose; deliberate
  • Synonym: unintended - not planned or meant
