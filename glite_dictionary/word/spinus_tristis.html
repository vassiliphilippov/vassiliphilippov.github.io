Spinus tristis

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪ.nəs ˈtrɪs.tɪs / (AmE), / ˈspiː.nəs ˈtrɪs.tɪs / (BrE)
the scientific name for the American goldfinch, a North American finch species with bright yellow breeding males
scientific name for the American goldfinch

Examples

Bird watchers spotted several Spinus tristis in the grassy meadow.
The male Spinus tristis is easily recognized by its bright yellow plumage during summer.
Researchers studied the migration patterns of Spinus tristis across North America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: American goldfinch - a small North American finch with yellow plumage in breeding males

Etymology

From Latin: 'spinus' (a finch) and 'tristis' (sad, possibly referencing the song).
