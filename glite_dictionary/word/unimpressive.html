unimpressive

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪmˈprɛs.ɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪmˈprɛs.ɪv / (BrE)
not striking, remarkable, or likely to make a strong impression
not striking or remarkable

Examples

The team's unimpressive performance cost them a place in the finals.
Her grades this semester were rather unimpressive compared to last year.
The restaurant's decor was unimpressive, but the food was excellent.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: impressive - having a strong effect on the mind or feelings

Etymology

From un- (not) + impressive, based on impress.
