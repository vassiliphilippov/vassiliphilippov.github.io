glinting

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡlɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
shining with brief, brilliant flashes of light; sparkling or gleaming intermittently
shining with brief flashes of light

Examples

The sunlight was glinting off the surface of the lake.
She noticed his glinting eyes in the dim candlelight.
The knight rode in armor that was glinting in the afternoon sun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glittering - shining with a bright, shimmering, reflected light
  • Synonym: sparkling - giving off small flashes of light; shining in bright, shimmering, or flickering way

Etymology

Present participle of 'glint', from Middle English 'glenten' (to gleam, flash), probably of Scandinavian origin.
