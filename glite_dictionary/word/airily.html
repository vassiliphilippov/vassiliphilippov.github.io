airily

1. ADV

/ ˈɛrɪli / (AmE), / ˈeə.rɪ.li / (BrE)
in a light, unconcerned, or flippant manner; without seriousness or thoughtfulness
in a light or flippant manner

Examples

She dismissed his concerns airily, as if they hardly mattered.
The manager airily promised everything would be fine.
He airily explained away the mistake, showing little concern.

Synset Examples

he answered the reporters' questions flippantly
this cannot be airily explained to your children

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flippantly - in a disrespectful or casually disrespectful manner
  • Antonym: seriously - in a serious or earnest manner

Etymology

From 'airy' + '-ly'. 'Airy' derives from Old French 'airé', from 'air'.
