intracerebral

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.trə.səˈri.brəl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trə.sɪˈriː.brəl / (BrE)
Occurring or situated within the brain.
within the brain
medical

Examples

The patient suffered an intracerebral hemorrhage after the accident.
Researchers are studying the effects of intracerebral drug injections in mice.
The MRI scan revealed an intracerebral mass requiring immediate attention.

Etymology

From Latin 'intra-' (within) + 'cerebrum' (brain).
