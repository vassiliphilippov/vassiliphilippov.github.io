reinvigorated

1. ADJ

/ ˌriːɪnˈvɪɡəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈvɪɡəreɪtɪd / (BrE)
Having had energy, strength, or vitality renewed or restored.
restored to a state of energy or strength

Examples

After the vacation, she felt completely reinvigorated and ready to tackle new challenges.
The company's reinvigorated management team introduced a series of innovative projects.
A long walk in the fresh mountain air left him reinvigorated and optimistic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invigorated - given strength or energy

Etymology

From re- (again) + invigorated, from Latin vigor (liveliness, activity).
