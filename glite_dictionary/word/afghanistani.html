Afghanistani

/ ˌæf.ɡæn.ɪˈstɑː.ni / (AmE), / ˌæf.ɡæn.ɪˈstɑː.ni / (BrE)
of or relating to Afghanistan or its people.

Examples

The Afghanistani cuisine blends Central Asian and Middle Eastern flavors.

Related Terms
