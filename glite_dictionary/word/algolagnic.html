algolagnic

1. ADJ

/ ˌælɡoʊˈlæɡnɪk / (AmE), / ˌalɡəʊˈlaɡnɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by algolagnia (sexual pleasure derived from pain).
relating to sexual pleasure from pain
medical

Examples

Her research focused on the psychological factors underlying algolagnic tendencies.
Some literature explores relationships with explicitly algolagnic themes.
The psychiatrist assessed whether the patient had any algolagnic impulses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masochistic - deriving sexual gratification from one's own pain or humiliation
  • Synonym: algolagnia - sexual pleasure derived from pain; the condition itself

Etymology

From algolagnia + -ic, patterned after Greek roots 'algos' (pain) and 'lagneia' (lust).
