Masa

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːsə / (AmE), / ˈmɑːsə / (BrE)
an independent group of closely related Chadic languages spoken in the region between the Biu-Mandara and East Chadic language groups, primarily in Chad and Cameroon.
a Chadic language group of Central Africa

Examples

The Masa languages are primarily spoken in Chad and Cameroon.
Linguists have been studying the grammatical structure of Masa for decades.
She is fluent in both French and Masa.
