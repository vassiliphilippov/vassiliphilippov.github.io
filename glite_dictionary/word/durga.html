Durga

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʊrɡə / (AmE), / ˈdʊəɡə / (BrE)
A powerful Hindu goddess associated with war and protection, regarded as a fierce and beneficent form of the goddess Devi.
Hindu goddess of war and protection

Examples

Every autumn, millions of devotees celebrate Durga Puja with elaborate rituals and festivals.
The temple was dedicated to Durga, the fierce goddess who vanquishes evil.
Images of Durga riding a lion are common in Hindu iconography.

Synset Examples

the inaccessible Durga

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Devi - The supreme goddess in Hinduism, of whom Durga is a form
  • Synonym: Kali - Another fierce aspect of the Hindu goddess Devi

Etymology

From Sanskrit दुर्गा (Durgā), meaning 'the inaccessible' or 'invincible'.
