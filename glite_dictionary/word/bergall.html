bergall

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɜrˌɡɔl / (AmE), / ˈbɜːɡəl / (BrE)
A small marine fish (Tautogolabrus adspersus) found in the coastal waters of the North Atlantic, especially off the northeastern United States.
a small North Atlantic marine fish

Examples

Fishermen often catch bergall while angling off the coast of New England.
The bergall is recognized by its slender body and bluish fins.
Many local recipes feature freshly caught bergall as a delicacy.
