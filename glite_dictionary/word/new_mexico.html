New Mexico

1. PROPN

/ nuː ˈmɛksɪkoʊ / (AmE), / njuː ˈmɛksɪkəʊ / (BrE)
a state in the southwestern United States, bordering Mexico, known for its diverse landscape and cultural heritage
U.S. state in the southwest

Examples

We spent two weeks exploring New Mexico and visiting its national parks.
The cuisine of New Mexico reflects both Native American and Spanish influences.
Many artists are drawn to the unique light found in New Mexico.

Etymology

Named after Mexico; 'New' indicating it as a new province compared to 'Old' Mexico.
