Tera

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛrə / (AmE), / ˈtɛərə / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in northeastern Nigeria, known for having three distinct tones
Chadic language of northeastern Nigeria

Examples

The linguist conducted fieldwork to document the Tera language and its tonal patterns.
Speakers of Tera primarily live in the Borno and Gombe regions of Nigeria.
The phonology of Tera features three distinct tone levels that affect word meaning.
