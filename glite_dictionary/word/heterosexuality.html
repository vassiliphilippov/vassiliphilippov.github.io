heterosexuality

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɛt̬·əˌsɛk·ʃuˈæl·ə·ti / (AmE), / ˌhɛt.əˌsɛk.ʃuˈæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
The quality or state of being sexually attracted to people of the opposite sex.
attraction to the opposite sex

Examples

The documentary explored how society views heterosexuality and other sexual orientations.
He became more aware of his heterosexuality during his teenage years.
Some cultures treat heterosexuality as the default or expected orientation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: homosexuality - sexual attraction to people of the same sex
  • Antonym: bisexuality - sexual attraction to both sexes

Etymology

From hetero- (different) + sexuality, modeled after heterosexual.
