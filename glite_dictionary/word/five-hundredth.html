five-hundredth

1. ADJ

/ faɪv ˈhʌndrədθ / (AmE), / faɪv ˈhʌndrɪdθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number representing the position 500 in a sequence; 500th
ordinal number: 500th

Examples

She finished in five-hundredth place in the marathon, a remarkable achievement among thousands.
This is the five-hundredth anniversary of the historic event.
Today marks the five-hundredth entry in my journal.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: four-hundredth - the ordinal number 400th
  • Degree Higher: six-hundredth - the ordinal number 600th
