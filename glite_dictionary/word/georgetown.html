Georgetown

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɔr(d)ʒˌtaʊn / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒˌtaʊn / (BrE)
The capital and largest city of Guyana, a port city on the Atlantic coast.
capital city of Guyana

Examples

Many visitors to Guyana begin their travels in Georgetown, the country's lively capital.
The Demerara River runs through Georgetown, making it an important port city for exports.
Georgetown hosts colorful markets, colonial buildings, and botanical gardens.

Synset Examples

the city was called Stabroek by the Dutch but was renamed Georgetown by the British in 1812

Etymology

Named after King George III of Britain.
