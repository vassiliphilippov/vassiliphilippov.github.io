unaccredited

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈkrɛd.ɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈkrɛd.ɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
(of an institution, organization, or qualification) not having official recognition or approval, especially from an authoritative body.
lacking official recognition or approval

Examples

The college is unaccredited, so its degrees may not be recognized by employers.
Many students were unaware that the training program was unaccredited.
Before enrolling, check whether the institution is unaccredited or recognized by an official body.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: accredited - officially recognized or approved

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'accredited' (officially recognized or approved).
