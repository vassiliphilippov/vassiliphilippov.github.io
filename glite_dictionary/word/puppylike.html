puppylike

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʌpiˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpʌpiˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the qualities or characteristics of a puppy, such as playfulness or innocence.
resembling or characteristic of a puppy

Examples

She had a puppylike enthusiasm that made everyone smile.
His puppylike curiosity often got him into trouble.
The child looked up with a puppylike expression, full of hope and expectation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puppyish - displaying qualities typical of a puppy

Etymology

From 'puppy' + '-like', indicating resemblance.
