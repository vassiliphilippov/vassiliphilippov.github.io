bareheaded

1. ADJ

/ ˌbɛrˈhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˌbeəˈhɛdɪd / (BrE)
not wearing any covering on the head; having the head uncovered.
not wearing a hat or covering

Examples

She stood bareheaded in the sunshine, enjoying the warmth on her skin.
He rushed out bareheaded into the rain to rescue his dog.
Many mourners walked bareheaded behind the casket as a sign of respect.

Synset Examples

caught bareheaded by the downpour
with bared head

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uncovered - not covered; exposed
  • Synonym: hatless - not wearing a hat

Etymology

Formed from 'bare' (without covering) + 'headed' (having a head).
