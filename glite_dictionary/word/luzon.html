Luzon

1. PROPN

/ luˈzɔn / (AmE), / luˈzɒn / (BrE)
the largest and most populous island in the Philippines, located in the northern part of the country
main island of the Philippines

Examples

The city of Manila is located on Luzon, the largest island in the Philippines.
After exploring Mindanao, the travelers headed north to Luzon to visit the rice terraces.
Many typhoons make landfall on Luzon during the rainy season.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Luzón', ultimately from Tagalog 'Lusong'.
