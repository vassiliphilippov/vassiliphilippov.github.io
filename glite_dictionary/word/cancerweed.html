cancerweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈkænsərˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈkænsəˌwiːd / (BrE)
A common name for a sage plant native to eastern United States, sometimes used in herbal remedies.
sage plant of eastern U.S.

Examples

The herbalist recommended using cancerweed for its potential healing properties.
Several patches of cancerweed grew along the forest edge in spring.
Some gardeners cultivate cancerweed for its attractive flowers and traditional uses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sage - an aromatic plant with grayish-green leaves, used as a culinary herb
