Sovietness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊ.vi.ɛt.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.vi.ɛt.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or condition of being Soviet, particularly in relation to culture, identity, or values characteristic of the Soviet Union.
quality of being Soviet

Examples

The professor discussed how Sovietness influenced art and literature during the twentieth century.
Some immigrants struggled to adapt, experiencing a sense of Sovietness long after leaving Russia.
Her documentary examines the lingering Sovietness in former republics' daily life.

Synset Examples

This chapter explores the concept of Soviet spaces and Sovietness, using film as a guide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Soviet - Relating to the Soviet Union or its people

Etymology

From Soviet + -ness. 'Soviet' from Russian 'sovet' (council).
