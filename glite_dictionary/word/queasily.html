queasily

1. ADV

/ ˈkwiːzəli / (AmE), / ˈkwiːzɪli / (BrE)
In a manner characterized by nausea or a disturbed stomach; with discomfort or unease.
in a nauseous or uneasy manner

Examples

The sailor looked queasily at the churning sea as the boat rocked.
She smiled queasily after tasting the unfamiliar food.
He stood queasily by the side of the operating table, trying not to look away.

Synset Examples

‘Do I have to remove the liver?’ the medical student asked queasily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: queasy - feeling nausea or uneasiness
