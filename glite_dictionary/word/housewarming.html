housewarming

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌwɔrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌwɔːmɪŋ / (BrE)
a party held to celebrate moving into a new home and to welcome friends and family
party for moving into a new home

Examples

We invited all our neighbors to our housewarming last weekend.
She received many gifts at her housewarming party.
A housewarming is a great way to meet people in your new neighborhood.

Etymology

From 'house' + 'warming', referring to the tradition of 'warming' a new house with guests.
