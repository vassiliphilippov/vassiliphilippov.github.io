biochemically

1. ADV

/ ˌbaɪ.oʊˈkɛm.ɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əʊˈkɛm.ɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to biochemistry or involves chemical processes within living organisms.
in a way related to biochemistry

Examples

The drug was found to be biochemically stable in the body over a long period.
Some diseases can be identified biochemically before symptoms appear.
He is studying how plants adapt biochemically to environmental stress.

Synset Examples

biochemically interesting phenomenon

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chemically - in a way that relates to chemistry

Etymology

From 'biochemical' + '-ly', with 'biochemical' formed from 'bio-' (life) and 'chemical'.
