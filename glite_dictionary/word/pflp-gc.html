PFLP-GC

1. PROPN

/ pi ɛf ɛl pi dʒi si / (AmE), / piː ɛf ɛl piː dʒiː siː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine – General Command, a Marxist–Leninist militant organization active mainly in the Middle East and responsible for several attacks in Western Europe.
Palestinian militant organization (abbreviation)

Examples

The PFLP-GC claimed responsibility for the attack on the airport in 1987.
Security forces increased surveillance of PFLP-GC activities in the region.
Several countries have designated PFLP-GC as a terrorist organization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: PFLP - Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine, a related organization

Etymology

From the initials of Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine – General Command.
