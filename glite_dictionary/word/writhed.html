writhed

1. ADJ

/ raɪðd / (AmE), / raɪðd / (BrE)
Twisted or contorted, especially as if in pain or struggle.
twisted, as in pain or struggle

Examples

Her face was writhed in anguish as she received the news.
The snake's writhed body slipped silently through the grass.
His features appeared writhed with a torment he could not express.

Synset Examples

his mad contorted smile
writhed lips
my writhen features

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contorted - twisted or bent out of shape
  • Synonym: twisted - forced out of its natural or proper shape

Etymology

Past participle of 'writhe', from Old English 'wrīthan' (to twist).
