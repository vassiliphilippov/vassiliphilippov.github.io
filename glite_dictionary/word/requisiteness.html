requisiteness

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈkwɪz.ɪt.nəs / (AmE), / rɪˈkwɪz.ɪt.nɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being essential or absolutely necessary
state of being necessary

Examples

The requisiteness of regular exercise for good health cannot be overstated.
He questioned the requisiteness of such detailed paperwork for a simple request.
The contract emphasized the requisiteness of compliance with safety regulations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: necessity - state of being necessary
  • Antonym: optionality - state of being optional or not required

Etymology

From requisite + -ness, with 'requisite' from Latin requisitus (required).
