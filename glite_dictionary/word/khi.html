khi

1. NOUN

/ kaɪ / (AmE), / kaɪ / (BrE)
the twenty-second letter of the Greek alphabet, written as Χ in uppercase and χ in lowercase.
the 22nd Greek alphabet letter

Examples

In statistics, the khi square test is widely used to measure association.
The Greek letter khi is often used to represent a variable in equations.
She learned to write khi during her ancient Greek language course.

Etymology

From Greek χῖ (khî).
