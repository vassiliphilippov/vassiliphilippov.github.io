Maharashtra

1. PROPN

/ məˌhɑːˈræʃtrə / (AmE), / məˌhɑːˈrɑːʃtrə / (BrE)
a state in west-central India, with Mumbai as its capital
state in west-central India

Examples

The city of Mumbai is the capital of Maharashtra and one of the largest in India.
Many tourists visit Maharashtra to see its famous caves and temples.
Agriculture is a vital part of the economy in Maharashtra.

Etymology

From Sanskrit महराष्ट्र (Mahārāṣṭra), meaning 'great nation' or 'great country'.
