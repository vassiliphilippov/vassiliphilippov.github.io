Benjamin West

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɛndʒəmɪn wɛst / (AmE), / ˈbɛndʒəmɪn wɛst / (BrE)
(1738–1820) An English painter born in America, known for serving as the second president of the Royal Academy and for his historical paintings.
English painter and Royal Academy president

Examples

Benjamin West is famous for his historical scenes, including "The Death of General Wolfe."
The presidency of the Royal Academy was once held by Benjamin West.
Many of Benjamin West's works are displayed in major museums around the world.
