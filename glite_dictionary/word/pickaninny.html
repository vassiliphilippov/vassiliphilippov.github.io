pickaninny

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɪkəˈnɪni / (AmE), / ˌpɪkəˈnɪni / (BrE)
An extremely offensive and derogatory term for a Black child, now universally recognized as a racial slur.
offensive term for a Black child (slur)
offensive

Examples

The word pickaninny is now recognized as deeply racist and offensive.
Historical books sometimes used the term pickaninny, reflecting prejudiced attitudes of their time.
Many organizations now work to educate the public about why pickaninny should never be used.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'pequenino' meaning 'very little' or 'small child,' but repurposed in English as a racial slur.
