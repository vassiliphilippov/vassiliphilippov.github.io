shindy

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪndi / (AmE), / ˈʃɪndi / (BrE)
a lively and noisy party or celebration
lively, noisy party

Examples

They threw a massive shindy to celebrate their anniversary.
Nobody got much sleep after the raucous shindy next door lasted till dawn.
The annual summer shindy is the highlight of the village calendar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party - a social gathering of invited guests
  • Synonym: shindig - a large, lively party

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly an alteration of 'shinty,' a Scottish game, or from 'shin-dig.'
