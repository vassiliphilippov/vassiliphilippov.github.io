DMus

1. NOUN

/ di ˈmjuːz / (AmE), / diː ˈmjuːz / (BrE)
Doctor of Music; a doctoral degree awarded for advanced study or achievement in music.
doctorate in music

Examples

She earned her DMus from a prestigious conservatory in Vienna.
After completing his DMus, he began teaching composition at the university.
The honorary DMus was awarded for her contributions to classical music.

Etymology

Abbreviation of Latin 'Doctor Musicae', meaning 'Doctor of Music'.
