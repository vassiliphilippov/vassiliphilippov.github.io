3. ADV

/ ˈɔf.ən, ˈɑf.ən / (AmE), / ˈɒf.ən, ˈɔːf.ən / (BrE)
Many times; frequently; in many cases or on many occasions.
frequently; in many cases

Examples

I often go for a walk in the evenings after dinner.
She often forgets where she puts her keys.
It often rains in April in this part of the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frequently - happening often; commonly or repeatedly
  • Antonym: rarely - not often; seldom
  • Degree Lower: sometimes - occasionally; at certain times but not always
  • Degree Higher: always - at all times; on every occasion

Etymology

Old English 'oft', from Proto-Germanic '*ufta' (frequently).
