Zend

1. NOUN

/ zɛnd / (AmE), / zɛnd / (BrE)
an ancient Iranian language, used as the language of Zoroastrian scripture and commentaries
ancient Iranian language of Zoroastrian scripture

Examples

Scholars have translated the Zend texts to better understand early Zoroastrian beliefs.
The priest studied ancient Zend manuscripts as part of his research.
Many terms in the Zend language are still used in modern Zoroastrian rituals.

Etymology

From Middle Persian 'zand' (commentary, interpretation), referring to explanatory texts on the Avesta.
