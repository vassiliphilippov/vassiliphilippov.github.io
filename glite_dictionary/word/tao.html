Tao

1. NOUN

/ taʊ / (AmE), / taʊ / (BrE)
(in Chinese philosophy) the fundamental principle underlying the universe, signifying the way, path, or natural order, especially as understood in Taoism and Confucianism.
the basic principle of the universe in Taoism

Examples

The concept of Tao emphasizes living in harmony with the universe.
Many people seek to understand the Tao through meditation and self-reflection.
In Taoism, following the Tao is seen as the ideal way to live.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dao - alternative transliteration of Tao (the ultimate principle in Chinese philosophy)

Etymology

From Chinese 道 (*dào*, meaning 'way' or 'path').
