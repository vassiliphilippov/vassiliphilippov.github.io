to overmilk

1. VERB

/ ˌoʊvɚˈmɪlk / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈmɪlk / (BrE)
to add an excessive amount of milk to a beverage, or to overstretch a joke or situation beyond its original humor or effectiveness
add too much milk or overextend a joke

Examples

If you overmilk your coffee, it will taste bland and watery.
Sometimes comedians overmilk a joke until it loses its charm.
She tried not to overmilk her tea, but poured too much by mistake.

Synset Examples

She realized she might overmilk her tea for extra flavor.
Don’t overmilk the coffee; it’s perfect as it is.
He tends to overmilk the joke, making it less funny.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to overdo - to do something to an excessive degree

Etymology

Formed from 'over-' (excessive) + 'milk'.
