Tetrazzini

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛtrəˈzini / (AmE), / ˌtɛtrəˈziːni / (BrE)
an American pasta dish baked in a creamy sauce with mushrooms, often including poultry or seafood
baked pasta dish with cream, mushrooms, and meat

Examples

We prepared a delicious chicken Tetrazzini for dinner last night.
The restaurant's seafood Tetrazzini is one of its most popular dishes.
Many families serve turkey Tetrazzini to use up Thanksgiving leftovers.

Etymology

Named after Italian soprano Luisa Tetrazzini (1871–1940).
