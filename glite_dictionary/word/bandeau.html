bandeau

1. NOUN

/ bænˈdoʊ / (AmE), / bænˈdəʊ / (BrE)
A simple undergarment or strip of fabric worn by women to support or cover the breasts, typically strapless and wrapping around the chest.
strapless women's undergarment

Examples

She wore a bandeau beneath her summer dress for a comfortable fit.
The swimsuit came with a matching bandeau top and cover-up.
Many dancers prefer a bandeau for rehearsals instead of a full bra.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bra - a woman's undergarment for supporting the breasts

Etymology

From French bandeau (band, strip), diminutive of bande.
