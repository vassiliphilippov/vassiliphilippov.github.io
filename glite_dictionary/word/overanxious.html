overanxious

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvɚˈæŋkʃəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈæŋkʃəs / (BrE)
Excessively anxious or nervous, often to the point of being counterproductive or unnecessary.
excessively anxious or nervous

Examples

My mother tends to become overanxious about my health whenever I catch a cold.
The overanxious applicant rehearsed his interview answers for hours the night before.
Trying not to appear overanxious, she waited calmly for the test results.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: anxious - worried or uneasy about something
  • Antonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness or anxiety

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'over-' (excessively) and 'anxious'.
