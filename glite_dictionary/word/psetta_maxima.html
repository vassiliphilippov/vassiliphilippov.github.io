Psetta maxima

1. NOUN

/ ˈpsɛtə ˈmæksɪmə / (AmE), / ˈpsɛtə ˈmæksɪmə / (BrE)
A large brownish European flatfish that is valued as a food fish, also known as the turbot.
large European flatfish (turbot)

Examples

The chef prepared the freshly caught Psetta maxima with a lemon butter sauce.
Commercial fisheries often target Psetta maxima due to its high market value.
Scientists are studying the habitat preferences of Psetta maxima in the North Sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turbot - a large flatfish of European seas, prized as food
