unpitying

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpɪtiɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɪtiɪŋ / (BrE)
showing no pity, compassion, or mercy; harsh and unfeeling
showing no pity or mercy

Examples

Despite her pleas, the judge remained unpitying and delivered a harsh sentence.
The novel describes the passage of time as an unpitying force.
His unpitying attitude alienated many of his colleagues.

Synset Examples

an act of ruthless ferocity
a monster of remorseless cruelty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: merciless - showing no mercy
  • Synonym: pitiless - having or showing no pity
  • Antonym: compassionate - showing sympathy and concern for others

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'pity' + '-ing'.
