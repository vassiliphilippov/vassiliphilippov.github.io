AAA

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪ ˌeɪ ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˌeɪ ˈeɪ / (BrE)
an aneurysm of the abdominal aorta, often occurring in older adults and associated with hypertension; abbreviation of 'abdominal aortic aneurysm'
abdominal aortic aneurysm (medical)
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with an AAA after complaining of persistent abdominal pain.
Routine screening for AAA is recommended in men over the age of 65.
A ruptured AAA is a medical emergency requiring immediate surgery.

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'abdominal aortic aneurysm'.
