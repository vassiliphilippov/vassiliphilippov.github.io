trimmings

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɪmɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈtrɪmɪŋz / (BrE)
the accessories or additional items that typically accompany something, especially food or a special event
accessories or extras accompanying something

Examples

The Thanksgiving dinner was served with all the trimmings, including gravy and cranberry sauce.
He shopped for a turkey and the necessary trimmings for their traditional family feast.
The party had festive trimmings such as balloons, streamers, and confetti.

Synset Examples

an elaborate formal dinner with all the fixings
he bought a Christmas tree and trimmings to decorate it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fixings - extras or accompaniments, especially with food
  • Synonym: extras - additional items beyond the main thing

Etymology

From 'trim' (to decorate or adorn), with the noun suffix '-ing' and plural '-s'.
