shithead

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtˌhɛd / (BrE)
A stupid or contemptible person, used as a vulgar term of insult.
vulgar insult for a stupid person
vulgar offensive slang

Examples

I can’t believe that shithead parked in your spot again.
Don’t be such a shithead; think before you act.
She called him a shithead after he spoiled her presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Synonym: moron - a foolish or stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - a person of exceptional intelligence
