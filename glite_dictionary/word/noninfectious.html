noninfectious

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑːn.ɪnˈfɛk.ʃəs / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.ɪnˈfɛk.ʃəs / (BrE)
Not capable of causing infection or being transmitted by infection.
not capable of causing infection

Examples

Many chronic diseases such as diabetes are considered noninfectious.
The rash was determined to be noninfectious after several tests.
Unlike the flu, allergies are a noninfectious condition.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: infectious - capable of causing infection or spreading disease

Etymology

From prefix 'non-' (not) + 'infectious'.
