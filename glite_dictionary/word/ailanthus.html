ailanthus

1. NOUN

/ aɪˈlænθəs / (AmE), / aɪˈlanθəs / (BrE)
A tree of the genus Ailanthus, especially the tree of heaven, native to Asia and known for its rapid growth and adaptability.
a genus of Asian deciduous trees

Examples

The ailanthus was introduced to many urban areas for its hardiness and rapid growth.
In summer, the leaves of the ailanthus provide dense shade along city streets.
Some gardeners find the spread of ailanthus difficult to control due to its aggressive growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tree of heaven - a fast-growing Asian tree of the species Ailanthus altissima

Etymology

From modern Latin Ailanthus, from Indonesian (Ambonese Malay) 'ailanto' meaning 'tree of heaven'.
