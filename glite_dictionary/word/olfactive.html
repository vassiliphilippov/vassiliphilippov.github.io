olfactive

1. ADJ

/ ɑlˈfæktɪv / (AmE), / ɒlˈfæktɪv / (BrE)
Relating to the sense of smell; concerning olfaction.
relating to the sense of smell

Examples

The perfume was designed to appeal to the olfactive senses.
Dogs have a highly developed olfactive system used for tracking scents.
Certain flowers emit olfactive signals to attract pollinators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: olfactory - relating to the sense of smell

Etymology

From Latin 'olfacere' (to smell) via French 'olfactif'.
