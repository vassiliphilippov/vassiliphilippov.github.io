Ga

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæliəm / (AmE), / ˈɡalɪəm / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for gallium, a rare silvery metallic element that is brittle at low temperatures but liquid above room temperature; occurs in trace amounts in bauxite and zinc ores
chemical symbol for gallium (element)

Examples

The semiconductor chip contains traces of Ga to enhance its properties.
Researchers observed that Ga melts when held in the palm of your hand.
In this experiment, Ga was extracted from bauxite ore.
