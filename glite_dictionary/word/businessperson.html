businessperson

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznɪsˌpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪsˌpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person engaged in commercial, industrial, or professional activities, especially as an owner or executive
someone engaged in business or commerce

Examples

She is a successful businessperson who owns several restaurants across the city.
As a businessperson, he values innovation and ethical practices in the workplace.
Many aspiring entrepreneurs look up to a seasoned businessperson for guidance and inspiration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: businessman - a man engaged in commercial or industrial business
  • Synonym: businesswoman - a woman engaged in commercial or industrial business

Etymology

Formed from 'business' + 'person' to provide a gender-neutral alternative to 'businessman' or 'businesswoman'.
