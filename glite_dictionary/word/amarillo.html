Amarillo

1. PROPN

/ ˌæməˈrɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˌæməˈrɪləʊ / (BrE)
a city in the northern panhandle region of Texas, United States
city in northern Texas, USA

Examples

She moved to Amarillo to take a new job opportunity in the energy industry.
The weather in Amarillo can change quickly, especially during the spring.
We stopped in Amarillo on our way across Texas and enjoyed some great barbecue.

Etymology

From Spanish 'amarillo', meaning 'yellow', possibly referring to the color of local soils or wildflowers.
