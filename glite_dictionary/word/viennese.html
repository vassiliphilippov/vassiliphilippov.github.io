Viennese

1. ADJ

/ viˌɛnˈiz / (AmE), / viːˌɛnˈiːz / (BrE)
relating to Vienna, its people, or its culture
relating to Vienna or its people

Examples

The Viennese architecture is famous for its grandeur and elegance.
She enjoyed a traditional Viennese pastry at the café.
Many musical masterpieces were composed by Viennese artists in the 18th century.

Etymology

From Vienna + -ese, a suffix indicating origin or relation.
