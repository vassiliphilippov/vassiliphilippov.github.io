Antofagasta

1. PROPN

/ ˌæn.toʊ.fəˈɡɑː.stə / (AmE), / ˌæn.təfəˈɡæstə / (BrE)
a port city on the Pacific Ocean in northern Chile, noted for its mining industry and as a regional capital
port city in northern Chile

Examples

The port of Antofagasta handles much of Chile's mineral exports.
She traveled to Antofagasta to study the region's unique desert ecosystem.
Many tourists visit Antofagasta for its proximity to the Atacama Desert.

Etymology

From Quechua or Aymara origin, possibly meaning 'town of the great saltpeter bed'.
