year

1. NOUN

/ jɪr / (AmE), / jɪə / (BrE)
a group of students who begin or graduate at the same time, especially within the same school or university.
group of students graduating together

Examples

She was in my year at university, and we shared many classes together.
Our year organized a reunion ten years after graduation.
The year of 2005 produced some of the school's most successful alumni.

Synset Examples

the class of '97
she was in my year at Hoehandle High

Related Terms

  • Synonym: class - a group of students who graduate together

2. NOUN

/ jɪr / (AmE), / jɪə / (BrE)
the period of time it takes for a planet, especially Earth, to complete one full revolution around the sun, usually measured as 365 or 366 days for Earth
period for a planet's orbit around the sun

Examples

A Martian year is almost twice as long as an Earth year.
The scientists measured the length of a year on each planet.
Saturn takes nearly 30 Earth years to complete one orbit around the Sun.

Synset Examples

a Martian year takes 687 of our days

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: day - the period of time for one rotation of a planet, especially Earth
  • Degree Higher: decade - a period of ten years

Etymology

Old English *gēar*, from Proto-Germanic *jēran*; related to Dutch *jaar* and German *Jahr*.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-15226340-n
Synset POS: n

3. NOUN

/ jɪr / (AmE), / jɪə / (BrE)
A period of time within a calendar year used for a specific purpose or activity, such as a school year or fiscal year.
designated period within a year (e.g., school year)

Examples

The year begins in September and ends in June for most schools.
The company's fiscal year runs from April to March.
During the academic year, students participate in various extracurricular activities.

Synset Examples

a school year

Etymology

From Old English *ġēar* (year), related to German *Jahr* and Dutch *jaar*.

4. NOUN

/ jɪr / (AmE), / jɪə / (BrE)

a period of 12 months or 365/366 days

Examples

His daughter is six years old and starting school.
The project will take at least one year to complete.
