topsy-turvydom

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɑːpsi ˈtɜːrvidəm / (AmE), / ˌtɒpsi ˈtɜːvidəm / (BrE)
A state or condition of great confusion and disorder.
state of confusion and disorder

Examples

After the storm, the house was left in complete topsy-turvydom, with furniture and papers everywhere.
The sudden announcement threw the office into topsy-turvydom for the entire afternoon.
His mind was in a state of topsy-turvydom, unable to focus on any one thought.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chaos - complete disorder and confusion
  • Antonym: order - a state in which everything is arranged or organized

Etymology

From 'topsy-turvy' (upside down, in confusion) + '-dom' (denoting state or domain).
