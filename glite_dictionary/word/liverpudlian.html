Liverpudlian

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɪvərˈpʌdliən / (AmE), / ˌlɪvəˈpʌdliən / (BrE)
a native or resident of Liverpool, a city in northwest England
native or resident of Liverpool

Examples

Many famous musicians are proud to call themselves Liverpudlian.
The Liverpudlian warmth and humor are well-known across the UK.
She met a group of Liverpudlians at university who shared her love for football.

Etymology

From 'Liverpool' (the city) plus '-ian' (suffix forming demonyms).
