rundle

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌndəl / (AmE), / ˈrʌndəl / (BrE)
one of the crosspieces forming a step of a ladder or the spokes of a wheel
step or spoke of a ladder or wheel

Examples

He lost his footing when his foot slipped on a broken rundle.
Each rundle must be securely fixed to ensure the ladder is safe to use.
The old wooden ladder was missing a rundle near the top.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rung - a crosspiece forming a step of a ladder

Etymology

From Old English 'rond' (rod, stick) plus diminutive suffix '-le'.
