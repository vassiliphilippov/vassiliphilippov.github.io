Malevich

1. PROPN

/ məˈlɛvɪtʃ / (AmE), / məˈlɛvɪtʃ / (BrE)
Kazimir Malevich (1878–1935), a Russian abstract painter known for pioneering the Suprematist art movement.
Russian abstract painter (1878–1935)

Examples

Malevich is celebrated for his painting 'Black Square,' a key work of the avant-garde movement.
The influence of Malevich can be seen in many modern art exhibitions around the world.
Art historians often discuss how Malevich revolutionized abstract art in the early 20th century.
