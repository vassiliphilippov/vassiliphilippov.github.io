slaw

1. NOUN

/ slɔ / (AmE), / slɔː / (BrE)
A salad made from shredded raw cabbage, often mixed with other vegetables and dressed with mayonnaise or vinegar.
shredded cabbage salad
american

Examples

She made a delicious bowl of slaw to serve with the barbecue.
Would you like some slaw on your pulled pork sandwich?
The picnic included fried chicken, baked beans, and fresh slaw.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coleslaw - a salad of shredded raw cabbage and other vegetables with a dressing

Etymology

From Dutch "sla", short for "salade" meaning salad.
