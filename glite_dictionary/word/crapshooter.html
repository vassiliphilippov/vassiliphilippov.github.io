crapshooter

1. NOUN

/ ˈkræpˌʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈkræpˌʃuːtə / (BrE)
a person who gambles by playing the game of craps
gambler who plays craps
informal american

Examples

The seasoned crapshooter smiled as he rolled the dice across the table.
Every crapshooter in town gathered at the casino for the tournament.
He's known as an expert crapshooter who always seems to win big.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gambler - a person who takes risky action in the hope of a desired result, especially by betting

Etymology

From 'craps' (the dice game) + 'shooter' (one who throws or shoots), early 20th century American slang.
