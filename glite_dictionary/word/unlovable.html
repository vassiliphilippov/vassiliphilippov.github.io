unlovable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlʌvəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈlʌvəbəl / (BrE)
not capable of inspiring love or affection; difficult or impossible to love.
difficult or impossible to love

Examples

He always feared that his flaws made him unlovable to others.
The stray dog's past mistreatment had left it wary and unlovable at first.
She worried she was fundamentally unlovable, but her friends showed her otherwise.

Synset Examples

she was in some mysterious way … unlovable

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lovable - inspiring or deserving love or affection

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + lovable, meaning 'not able to be loved'.
