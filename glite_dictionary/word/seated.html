seated

1. ADJ

/ ˈsiːtɪd / (AmE), / ˈsiːtɪd / (BrE)
sitting with the torso upright and the body supported by the buttocks, usually with legs bent; in a sitting position.
in a sitting position

Examples

She remained seated during the entire performance.
All guests must be seated before the ceremony begins.
The artist painted a seated portrait of his father.

Synset Examples

the seated Madonna
the audience remained seated

Related Terms

  • Antonym: standing - in or into an upright position on the feet

Etymology

Past participle of 'seat', from Old Norse sæti (a seat).
