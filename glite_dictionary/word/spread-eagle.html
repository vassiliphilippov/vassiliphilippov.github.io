spread-eagle

1. ADJ

/ ˈsprɛd ˌiːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈsprɛd ˌiːɡl / (BrE)
with arms and legs stretched out and apart, resembling the shape of an eagle in flight
with arms and legs stretched out wide

Examples

The gymnast landed spread-eagle on the mat after the flip.
After falling into the snowbank, he lay spread-eagle and laughed.
She collapsed spread-eagle on the grass, exhausted from the race.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: splayed - spread apart or extended in all directions

From the image of an eagle with wings and legs extended, as depicted in heraldry.

Examples

Police ordered the suspect to spread-eagle against the wall for a search.
He spread-eagled himself on the grass, staring up at the sky.

From 'spread' + 'eagle', likening the body to the outstretched wings and limbs of an eagle.
