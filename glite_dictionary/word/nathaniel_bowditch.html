Nathaniel Bowditch

1. PROPN

/ nəˈθæniəl ˈbaʊdɪtʃ / (AmE), / nəˈθænɪəl ˈbaʊdɪtʃ / (BrE)
(1773–1838) An American mathematician and astronomer best known for his influential works on navigation.
American mathematician and navigator

Examples

Nathaniel Bowditch's book on navigation revolutionized how sailors charted their courses at sea.
Many maritime academies still teach from the principles established by Nathaniel Bowditch.
The life and achievements of Nathaniel Bowditch are studied in both mathematics and history classes.
