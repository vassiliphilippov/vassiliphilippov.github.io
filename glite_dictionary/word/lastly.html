lastly

1. ADV

/ ˈlæstli / (AmE), / ˈlɑːstli / (BrE)
used to introduce the final point or item in a series of statements or arguments.
in the final place; finally

Examples

Lastly, we need to consider the impact on the environment before making a final decision.
She mentioned several reasons for leaving, and lastly, she cited family commitments.
You should write a conclusion lastly to summarize your arguments.

Synset Examples

last, I'll discuss family values

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finally - used to introduce the last in a list of things or to signal conclusion
