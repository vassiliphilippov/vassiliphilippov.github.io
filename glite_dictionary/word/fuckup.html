fuckup

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈfʌkˌʌp / (BrE)
a serious or embarrassing mistake; a blunder, especially one caused by carelessness or incompetence
serious or embarrassing mistake
slang vulgar

Examples

After the meeting, everyone talked about John's fuckup during the presentation.
It was a costly fuckup, but at least we learned from our mistake.
That typo in the headline was a major fuckup for the editor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blunder - a careless or stupid mistake
  • Synonym: mistake - an action or judgment that is wrong
  • Antonym: success - the accomplishment of an aim or purpose
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00075610-n
Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ ˈfʌkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈfʌk.ʌp / (BrE)
a person who repeatedly makes serious mistakes, especially due to incompetence or carelessness
incompetent person who makes mistakes
vulgar informal

Examples

Everyone knew Tom was a fuckup after he lost the company thousands of dollars.
She always worried they would see her as a fuckup because of one bad decision.
Despite being labeled a fuckup, he was determined to prove everyone wrong.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: screw-up - someone who repeatedly makes mistakes
