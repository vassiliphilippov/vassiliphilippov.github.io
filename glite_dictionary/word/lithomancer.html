lithomancer

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪθ.oʊˌmæn.sər / (AmE), / ˈlɪθ.əʊˌmæn.sə / (BrE)
A person who practices lithomancy, the art of divination by interpreting stones or gems.
practitioner of divination by stones

Examples

The lithomancer examined the arrangement of stones to reveal insights into her client's future.
At the medieval fair, a lithomancer offered to read fortunes for curious onlookers.
Many cultures have legends about a skilled lithomancer who could predict events with remarkable accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diviner - a person who practices divination
  • Synonym: lithomancy - the practice of divination by stones

Etymology

From Greek *lithos* (stone) + *-mancer* (diviner), from Greek *manteia* (divination).
