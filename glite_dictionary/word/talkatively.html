talkatively

1. ADV

/ ˈtɔk.ə.tɪv.li / (AmE), / ˈtɔː.kə.tɪv.li / (BrE)
in a talkative or chatty manner; speaking with eagerness or at length
in a talkative manner

Examples

She chatted talkatively with everyone she met at the party.
The children laughed and argued talkatively as they worked on their project.
He answered the reporter's questions talkatively, never missing a chance to share an anecdote.

Synset Examples

‘When I was young,’ she continued loquaciously, ‘I used to do all sorts of naughty things’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loquaciously - in a very talkative or wordy manner
  • Antonym: quietly - in a quiet or reserved manner
