soulfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊl.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsəʊl.fəl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of expressing deep feeling or emotion
deep feeling or emotion

Examples

The soulfulness of her singing moved the entire audience to tears.
Many artists strive to capture genuine soulfulness in their work.
His soulfulness set him apart from other musicians, giving his performances an unforgettable depth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soulful - expressing deep feeling or emotion
  • Antonym: emotionlessness - the absence of emotion or feeling

Etymology

From "soulful" + "-ness".
