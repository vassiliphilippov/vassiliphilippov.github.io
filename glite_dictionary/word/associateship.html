associateship

1. NOUN

/ əˈsoʊsiˌeɪtʃɪp / (AmE), / əˈsəʊsiˌeɪtʃɪp / (BrE)
the state or position of being an associate, especially in an office, academy, or professional body
status or position of associate

Examples

She was awarded an associateship at the prestigious arts academy.
His associateship in the law firm opens many doors for networking.
After years of hard work, he finally gained an associateship in the National Academy of Sciences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fellowship - the status or position of a fellow, especially in an academic body

Etymology

Formed from 'associate' + '-ship', indicating state or condition.
