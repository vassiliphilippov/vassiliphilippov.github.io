robocall

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊbəˌkɔl / (AmE), / ˈrəʊbəˌkɔːl / (BrE)
an automated telephone call that delivers a recorded message to a large number of people
automated telephone call

Examples

I keep getting robocalls from companies trying to sell me insurance.
The government is working on regulations to limit disruptive robocalls.
She ignored the phone because she thought it was another robocall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spam call - an unsolicited and unwanted phone call, often for advertising or fraud

Etymology

From 'robot' (automaton) and 'call'.
