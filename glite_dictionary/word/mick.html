Mick

1. NOUN

/ mɪk / (AmE), / mɪk / (BrE)
An offensive term for a person of Irish descent.
offensive term for Irish person
offensive

Examples

That old movie uses the word Mick in a way that's now considered highly offensive.
He was called a Mick because of his Irish heritage, which upset him deeply.
Using Mick as a nickname is insensitive due to its derogatory history.

Etymology

Probably from the common Irish given name Mick (diminutive of Michael).
