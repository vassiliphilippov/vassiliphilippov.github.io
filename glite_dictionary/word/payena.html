Payena

1. NOUN

/ paɪˈiːnə / (AmE), / paɪˈiːnə / (BrE)
A genus of medium to large trees native to Malaysia, known for yielding gutta-percha.
genus of Malaysian trees yielding gutta-percha

Examples

Several species within the Payena genus are harvested for their valuable gutta-percha.
Botanists are studying the distribution of Payena trees in Southeast Asia.
The Payena genus plays an important ecological role in the Malaysian rainforest.

Etymology

From Malay, adopted as a New Latin genus name in botanical classification.

2. NOUN

/ paɪˈiːnə / (AmE), / paɪˈiːnə / (BrE)
A genus of medium to large trees native to Malaysia, known for producing gutta-percha.
genus of Malaysian trees yielding gutta-percha

Examples

The Payena genus is highly valued for its production of gutta-percha.
Botanists discovered a new species within the Payena genus in the Malaysian rainforest.
Several medical devices historically used gutta-percha derived from Payena trees.

Etymology

Named after French botanist A. Payen.
