chiseled

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɪzəld / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪzəld / (BrE)
having clear, sharply defined features or an outline, as if precisely cut with a chisel
sharply defined or sculpted in appearance

Examples

The actor was famous for his chiseled jawline and striking good looks.
Her chiseled features made her stand out in the crowd.
A chiseled statue stood in the center of the square, admired for its sharp detail.

Synset Examples

a finely chiseled nose
well-defined features

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sculpted - shaped or carved as if made by a sculptor
  • Synonym: angular - having sharp angles or edges; sharply defined

Etymology

From the past participle of 'chisel', from Old French *cisel*, from Late Latin *cisellum* (cutting tool).
