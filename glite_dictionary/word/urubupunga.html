Urubupunga

1. PROPN

/ ˌuːruːbuːˈpuːŋɡə / (AmE), / ˌuːruːbuːˈpuːŋɡə / (BrE)
a waterfall in the Paraná river in Brazil, known for its rapids and hydroelectric plant.
waterfall in the Paraná river, Brazil

Examples

The Urubupunga waterfall is a well-known location along the Paraná river.
Many visitors are drawn to the scenic beauty of Urubupunga each year.
The construction of the dam affected the natural flow of Urubupunga and its surrounding ecosystem.

Etymology

From the indigenous Tupi language spoken in Brazil.
