actinotherapy

1. NOUN

/ ˌæktɪnoʊˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˌaktɪnəʊˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
the treatment of disease, especially cancer, by exposing the body to radiation or radioactive substances
treatment of disease with radiation
medical

Examples

The patient underwent actinotherapy as a part of their cancer treatment plan.
Advances in actinotherapy have improved survival rates for certain types of tumors.
Researchers are developing new forms of actinotherapy to minimize side effects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiotherapy - treatment of disease, especially cancer, using radiation

Etymology

From Greek aktis, aktinos (ray) and therapy (treatment).
