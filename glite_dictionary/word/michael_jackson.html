Michael Jackson

1. PROPN

/ ˈmaɪkəl ˈdʒæksən / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkəl ˈdʒæksən / (BrE)
(1958–2009) A world-famous American singer, songwriter, and dancer, known as the 'King of Pop' and famous for hits like 'Thriller' and 'Billie Jean'.
famous American pop singer (1958–2009)

Examples

Michael Jackson was known for his spectacular dance moves and groundbreaking music videos.
Many people still admire the lasting influence of Michael Jackson on pop music.
The album 'Thriller' by Michael Jackson remains one of the best-selling albums of all time.
