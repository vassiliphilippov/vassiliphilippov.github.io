blackbuck

1. NOUN

/ ˈblækˌbʌk / (AmE), / ˈblakˌbʌk / (BrE)
a common Indian antelope (Antilope cervicapra) with a dark back, white underparts, and long spiral horns (in males)
an Indian antelope with spiral horns

Examples

The blackbuck is easily recognized by its striking spiral horns and contrasting coloration.
Conservationists are working to protect the rapidly diminishing habitats of the blackbuck.
A herd of blackbucks grazed peacefully on the grasslands at dawn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antelope - a swift-running deer-like animal; species in the Bovidae family
