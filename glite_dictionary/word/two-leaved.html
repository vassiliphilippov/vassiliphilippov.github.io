two-leaved

1. ADJ

/ tuː liːvd / (AmE), / tuː liːvd / (BrE)
(of a plant or part) having exactly two leaves.
having two leaves

Examples

The two-leaved cotyledon is characteristic of dicot plants.
Some varieties of clover are known for their two-leaved stems.
Scientists identified the plant by its unique two-leaved structure.
