relating to the city of Tunis or its residents
of Tunis or its people

Examples

The museum featured several beautiful Tunisian carpets.

Synset Examples

From Tunis, the capital city of Tunisia, with the suffix -ian indicating relation or belonging.

2. NOUN

/ tuˈniːʒən / (AmE), / tjuːˈnɪziən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Tunisia.
native/inhabitant of Tunisia

Examples

My neighbor is a proud Tunisian who often shares stories of her homeland.
The football team included several talented Tunisians in its lineup.
As a Tunisian, he speaks both Arabic and French fluently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tunisia - A country in North Africa

Etymology

From Tunisia + -an, denoting people or things from Tunisia.
