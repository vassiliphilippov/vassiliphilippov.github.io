Joe DiMaggio

1. PROPN

/ dʒoʊ dɪˈmædʒioʊ / (AmE), / dʒəʊ dɪˈmædʒiəʊ / (BrE)
(1914–1999) United States professional baseball player, renowned for his batting skill and legendary career with the New York Yankees.
famous American baseball player (1914–1999)

Examples

Many consider Joe DiMaggio one of the greatest baseball players in history.
The 56-game hitting streak set by Joe DiMaggio remains an iconic sports record.
In American culture, Joe DiMaggio is often remembered for both his athletic achievements and his marriage to Marilyn Monroe.
