hipflask

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪp.flæsk / (AmE), / ˈhɪp.flɑːsk / (BrE)
A small, flat container for carrying spirits, typically designed to be carried in a pocket.
small flask for carrying spirits

Examples

He pulled a hipflask from his coat and offered me a drink.
The detective discreetly sipped from his hipflask during the long stakeout.
She gave her grandfather a silver hipflask engraved with his initials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flask - a small container used for holding liquids
