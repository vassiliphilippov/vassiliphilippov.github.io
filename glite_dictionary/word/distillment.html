distillment

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈstɪlmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪlmənt / (BrE)
the process of purifying a liquid by boiling it and collecting the condensed vapors; distillation
process of purifying a liquid by distillation

Examples

The chemical laboratory ran several distillments to obtain pure water from contaminated sources.
The perfume was created through careful distillment of natural oils and essences.
Old distilleries perfected the distillment of whiskey over hundreds of years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distillation - the process of purifying a liquid by heating and cooling

Etymology

Formed from 'distill' and the suffix '-ment', indicating the result or process.
