Moscow

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑːsˌkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɒsˌkaʊ / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Russia, situated in western Russia, with a rich historical and cultural heritage
capital city of Russia

Examples

The president attended an international summit in Moscow last year.
Many tourists are drawn to the famous Red Square in Moscow.
After living in Moscow for a decade, she became fluent in Russian.

Etymology

From Russian Москва́ (Moskva), the name of the city.
