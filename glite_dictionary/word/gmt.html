GMT

1. NOUN

/ dʒi ɛm ti / (AmE), / dʒiː ɛm tiː / (BrE)
the mean solar time at the prime meridian (0° longitude) in Greenwich, England, used historically as the basis for standard time throughout the world
mean solar time at Greenwich, England

Examples

International flights often use GMT to avoid confusion across time zones.
The conference call is scheduled for 3:00 p.m. GMT.
All data backups occur at midnight GMT every day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Greenwich Mean Time - the mean solar time at Greenwich, England

Etymology

Abbreviation of Greenwich Mean Time.
