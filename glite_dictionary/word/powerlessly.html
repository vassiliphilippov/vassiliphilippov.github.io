powerlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈpaʊ.ɚ.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of power or ability to act or control outcomes.
without power or ability

Examples

She watched powerlessly as the floodwaters rose around her home.
He felt powerlessly trapped by the difficult situation.
The citizens looked on powerlessly as decisions were made without their input.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helplessly - in a helpless manner, unable to help oneself

Etymology

Formed from 'powerless' + '-ly'. 'Powerless' from 'power' + '-less'.
