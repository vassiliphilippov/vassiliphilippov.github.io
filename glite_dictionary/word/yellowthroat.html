yellowthroat

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊˌθroʊt / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊˌθrəʊt / (BrE)
a small American warbler with olive-colored upperparts and a yellow breast and throat
a small American warbler with yellow throat

Examples

We spotted a yellowthroat near the edge of the pond this morning.
The distinctive song of the yellowthroat is often heard in marshy areas.
During migration, yellowthroats can be found in a variety of habitats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warbler - a small songbird of the family Parulidae

Etymology

From the yellow color of the bird's throat.
