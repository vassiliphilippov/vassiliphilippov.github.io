opinionative

1. ADJ

/ əˈpɪnjəˌneɪtɪv / (AmE), / əˈpɪnjənətɪv / (BrE)
holding stubbornly to one's own opinions; obstinate in one's views.
obstinate in opinions

Examples

The opinionative participant refused to accept any viewpoint that contradicted his own.
Her opinionative attitude often led to heated arguments in meetings.
It can be hard to work with someone as opinionative as Greg.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opinionated - having strong, stubborn opinions

Etymology

From 'opinion' + '-ative', on the pattern of adjectives like 'argumentative'.
