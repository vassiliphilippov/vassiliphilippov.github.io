doorstopper

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔrˌstɑpər / (AmE), / ˈdɔːˌstɒpə / (BrE)
an object placed on the floor to keep a door open or prevent it from closing or banging shut
object for holding a door open

Examples

We placed a heavy doorstopper by the kitchen door to let in a breeze.
The teacher used a rubber doorstopper to keep the classroom door open during the lesson.
Without a doorstopper, the wind kept slamming the door shut.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doorstop - an object that keeps a door open or prevents it from hitting the wall
