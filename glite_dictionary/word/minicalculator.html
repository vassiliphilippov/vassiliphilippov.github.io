minicalculator

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪniˌkælkjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈmɪniˌkælkjʊˌleɪtə / (BrE)
an old-fashioned, very small handheld calculator
very small handheld calculator (old-fashioned)
archaic

Examples

She carried a minicalculator in her handbag to quickly check prices at the market.
Before smartphones, students often used a minicalculator for math classes.
He found an old minicalculator at a garage sale, still in working condition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calculator - electronic device for making mathematical calculations

Etymology

mini- (small) + calculator; formed by compounding.
