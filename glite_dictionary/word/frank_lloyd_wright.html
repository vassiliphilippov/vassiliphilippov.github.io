Frank Lloyd Wright

1. PROPN

/ fræŋk lɔɪd raɪt / (AmE), / fræŋk lɔɪd raɪt / (BrE)
(1869–1959) An influential American architect known for his innovative designs and contributions to modern architecture.
influential American architect, 1869–1959

Examples

Frank Lloyd Wright designed the famous Fallingwater house in Pennsylvania.
Many students of architecture study the work of Frank Lloyd Wright.
The influence of Frank Lloyd Wright can be seen in homes across America.
