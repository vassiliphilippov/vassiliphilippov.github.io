unshaken

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈʃeɪkən / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃeɪkən / (BrE)
not disturbed or affected in purpose, belief, or determination; resolute or steadfast.
firm in purpose; not wavering

Examples

Although criticized, she remained unshaken in her convictions.
His faith in his friends was unshaken despite the rumors.
The leader stood unshaken in the face of adversity.

Synset Examples

wholly undismayed by the commercial failure of the three movies he had made

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steadfast - firm and unwavering in purpose or loyalty
  • Synonym: unwavering - not changing or weakening in determination or belief
  • Antonym: wavering - being undecided or irresolute
