shirtsleeve

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɝːtˌsliːv / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːtˌsliːv / (BrE)
the sleeve part of a shirt, covering the arm
sleeve of a shirt

Examples

He rolled up his shirtsleeve before washing the car.
Her white shirtsleeve caught on the edge of the table.
Sweat soaked through his shirtsleeve during the hot summer afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleeve - the part of a garment that covers the arm
