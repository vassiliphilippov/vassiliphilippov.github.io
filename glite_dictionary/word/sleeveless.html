sleeveless

1. ADJ

/ ˈsliːvləs / (AmE), / ˈsliːvləs / (BrE)
(of clothing) having no sleeves covering the arms.
having no sleeves

Examples

She wore a sleeveless blouse to keep cool during the hot summer day.
The designer released a new collection of sleeveless dresses for the spring season.
He prefers sleeveless shirts when working out at the gym.

Synset Examples

sleeveless summer dresses

Related Terms

  • Antonym: long-sleeved - (of clothing) having long sleeves
  • Antonym: short-sleeved - (of clothing) having short sleeves

Etymology

From Middle English slefles, from 'sleeve' + '-less'.
