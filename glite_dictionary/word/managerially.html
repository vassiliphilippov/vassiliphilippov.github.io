managerially

1. ADV

/ ˌmænɪˈdʒɪrəli / (AmE), / ˌmænɪˈdʒɪərɪəli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or characteristic of a manager or management.
in a managerial manner

Examples

The project was handled managerially to ensure efficiency and organization.
She approaches problems managerially, focusing on strategic solutions and resource allocation.
These issues must be addressed managerially to maintain the team's productivity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: managerial - relating to management or managers

Etymology

From 'managerial' + '-ly'.
