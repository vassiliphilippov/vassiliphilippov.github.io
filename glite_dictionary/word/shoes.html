shoes

1. NOUN

/ ʃuz / (AmE), / ʃuːz / (BrE)
the situation or circumstances of a particular person, used especially in the phrase 'in someone's shoes' to imagine what it would be like to be that person
situation or circumstances of a person

Examples

Try to imagine yourself in her shoes before you judge her.
If I were in your shoes, I'd take the job offer.
She understood what he felt because she had been in the same shoes once.

Synset Examples

If you were in my place what would you do?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: place - position or situation of a person, especially as in 'in someone's place'
