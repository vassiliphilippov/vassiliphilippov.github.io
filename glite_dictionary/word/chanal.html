chanal

1. NOUN

/ tʃəˈnɑl / (AmE), / tʃəˈnɑːl / (BrE)
a thorny shrub or small tree native to central Argentina, bearing small orange or yellow flowers and edible berries
thorny Argentinian shrub with edible berries

Examples

The local villagers gather chanal berries in the summer for making traditional preserves.
A chanal shrub can be easily recognized by its spiny branches and cluster of small orange flowers.
Researchers studied the ecological role of chanal in sustaining native bird populations.
