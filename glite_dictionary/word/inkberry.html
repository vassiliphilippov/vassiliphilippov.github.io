inkberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋkˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈɪŋkˌbɛri / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub of eastern North America with oblong leathery leaves and small black berries, especially Ilex glabra
an evergreen North American shrub with black berries

Examples

The leaves of the inkberry remain green throughout the winter.
Gardeners often plant inkberry as an evergreen hedge due to its dense foliage.
Birds are attracted to the small black berries produced by the inkberry.
