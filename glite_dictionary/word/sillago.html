Sillago

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈleɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / sɪˈleɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
A genus of marine fish known as smelt-whitings, belonging to the family Sillaginidae.
genus of marine fish (smelt-whitings)

Examples

The genus Sillago includes many species commonly called smelt-whitings.
Researchers collected several Sillago specimens from shallow coastal waters.
The diet of Sillago species consists mainly of small invertebrates.

Etymology

From Modern Latin, used in scientific classification.
