tablemate

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəlˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəlˌmeɪt / (BrE)
a person with whom one shares a table, especially while dining
person you share a dining table with

Examples

He became friends with his tablemate during the first week of university.
After a lively discussion, my tablemate offered to share her dessert.
The guests were carefully seated so that each tablemate had something in common to discuss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dining companion - a person who eats with another
  • Synonym: classmate - a fellow member of a class at school or college

Etymology

Formed from 'table' + 'mate', resembling compounds such as 'classmate' and 'teammate'.
