Kovno

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɒv.noʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒv.nəʊ / (BrE)
the former name of Kaunas, a city in central Lithuania
former name for Kaunas, a city in Lithuania

Examples

Many Jewish families in the 19th century lived in Kovno.
During World War II, Kovno was the site of significant historical events.
The writer described his childhood in Kovno with fond memories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kaunas - city in central Lithuania, current name of Kovno

Etymology

From Russian Ковно (Kovno), based on the Yiddish and Polish forms; earlier used under Russian, Polish, and imperial governance.
