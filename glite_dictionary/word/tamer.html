tamer

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪmər / (AmE), / ˈteɪmə / (BrE)
a person who trains and controls wild animals, often for performance or entertainment
person who trains wild animals

Examples

The tamer coaxed the lion through the flaming hoop during the evening show.
As a child, she dreamed of becoming an animal tamer at the circus.
The documentary followed a famous tamer and his work with elephants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trainer - a person who teaches skills or behaviors, especially to animals or athletes

Etymology

From tame + -er (agent suffix).
