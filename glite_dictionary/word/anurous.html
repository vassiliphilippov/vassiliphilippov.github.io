anurous

1. ADJ

/ əˈnjʊərəs / (AmE), / əˈnjʊərəs / (BrE)
(of an animal, especially an amphibian) lacking a tail.
lacking a tail (esp. amphibians)

Examples

Many species of frogs are anurous as adults, lacking tails entirely.
The scientists studied both tailed and anurous amphibians in their research.
Some ancient amphibians retained tails, but modern toads and frogs are typically anurous.

Synset Examples

anurous toads and frogs

Etymology

From Greek 'an-' (without) + 'oura' (tail).
