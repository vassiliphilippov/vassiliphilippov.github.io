chestnut-coloured

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɛsˌnʌt ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛs.nʌt ˌkʌl.əd / (BrE)
having a reddish-brown color similar to that of a chestnut
reddish-brown in color

Examples

The horse's chestnut-coloured mane glowed in the afternoon sunlight.
She wore a chestnut-coloured coat that perfectly matched her boots.
The cabin doors were painted a rich, chestnut-coloured hue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auburn - reddish-brown in color, especially of hair
  • Synonym: chestnut - a reddish-brown color
