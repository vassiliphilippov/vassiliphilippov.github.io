unsympathizing

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɪmpəˌθaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪmpəθaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
not showing or expressing sympathy; lacking compassion or understanding for others' feelings or situations.
not showing sympathy

Examples

The manager's unsympathizing response left the team feeling unsupported and disappointed.
She found her boss's unsympathizing attitude difficult to work with during stressful periods.
The novel depicted the judge as a cold and unsympathizing figure.

Synset Examples

an uncharitable and unsympathizing attitude

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unsympathetic - not feeling, showing, or expressing sympathy
  • Antonym: sympathizing - showing or expressing sympathy

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (not) to 'sympathizing.'
