spider-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈspaɪdər ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdə ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape or form of a spider.
shaped like a spider

Examples

The artist created a spider-shaped chandelier for the gallery.
She wore a spider-shaped brooch on her jacket during Halloween.
The children built a spider-shaped sandcastle at the beach.
