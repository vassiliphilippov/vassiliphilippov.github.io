verifier

1. NOUN

/ vəˈɹaɪ.əɹ / (AmE), / vəˈɹɪ.fɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who confirms the accuracy or truth of something, or vouches for someone
person who confirms accuracy or truth

Examples

The verifier carefully checked all the documents before approving the application.
As an independent verifier, her role was to ensure the data was accurate.
The team relied on a third-party verifier to confirm the results of the experiment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: witness - person who testifies to the truth of something

Etymology

From Old French 'verifier' and Latin 'verificare' (to make true).
