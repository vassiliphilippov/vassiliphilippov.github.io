dopey

1. ADJ

/ ˈdoʊpi / (AmE), / ˈdəʊpi / (BrE)
stupid or foolish; showing a lack of intelligence or good sense.
stupid or foolish
informal

Examples

He gave a dopey answer to the simplest question.
That dopey grin makes him look like he doesn't understand what's happening.
Sarah felt dopey after staying up all night without sleep.

Synset Examples

ridiculous anserine behavior
a dopey answer
a dopey kid
some fool idea about rewriting authors' books

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stupid - lacking intelligence or common sense
  • Synonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment
  • Antonym: sensible - showing good sense or judgment

Etymology

Originally from 'dope' (stupid person, or drugs affecting the mind) + '-y'.
