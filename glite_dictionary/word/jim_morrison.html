Jim Morrison

1. PROPN

/ dʒɪm ˈmɔɹɪsən / (AmE), / dʒɪm ˈmɒɹɪsən / (BrE)
(1943–1971) An American rock singer, songwriter, and poet, best known as the lead vocalist and lyricist of the band The Doors.
American rock singer and lead of The Doors
