Den Haag

1. PROPN

/ dɛn ˈhɑx / (AmE), / dɛn ˈhɑːx / (BrE)
The Dutch name for The Hague, the seat of government and royal residence in the Netherlands, and home to the International Court of Justice.
city in the Netherlands; seat of government

Examples

The International Court of Justice is based in Den Haag.
She visited Den Haag for a conference on international law.
Many embassies can be found throughout Den Haag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: The Hague - capital city of South Holland, Netherlands; seat of government

Etymology

Dutch, meaning 'The Hedge' or 'The Enclosure,' from 'des Graven hage' (the Count's hedge).
