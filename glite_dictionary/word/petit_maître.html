petit maître

1. NOUN

/ pəˌti ˈmɛtər / (AmE), / pəˈti ˈmɛːtrə / (BrE)
A charming and skilled host or master, especially in the arts or fashion, known for refined style and manners.
charming, stylish host or master

Examples

Everyone admired the petit maître for his elegance and impeccable hospitality.
She hoped to become a petit maître of the Parisian social scene.
The gallery owner's reputation as a petit maître attracted artists and critics alike.

Synset Examples

The dinner party was hosted by a charming petit maître.
They admired his style, calling him a true petit maître.

Etymology

From French 'petit maître' meaning 'little master'.
