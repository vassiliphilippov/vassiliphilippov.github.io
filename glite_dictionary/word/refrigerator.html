refrigerator

1. NOUN

/ rəˈfrɪdʒəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɪdʒəreɪtə / (BrE)
An electrical appliance used for keeping food and drinks cold and fresh.
appliance for keeping food cold

Examples

We bought a new refrigerator for the kitchen last weekend.
The milk will spoil if you don't put it in the refrigerator.
Please clean out the old food from the refrigerator before vacation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fridge - informal synonym for refrigerator

Etymology

From Latin 'refrigerare' (to make cool), from 're-' (again) + 'frigor' (cold).
