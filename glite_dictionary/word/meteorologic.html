meteorologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌmiːtiərəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌmiːtiərəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the science of weather and atmospheric phenomena
related to weather or atmospheric science

Examples

The meteorologic data indicated a sudden drop in temperature before the storm.
She specializes in meteorologic research, particularly the study of severe storms.
Accurate meteorologic observations are crucial for reliable weather forecasting.

Synset Examples

meteorological factors
meteorological chart
meteoric (or meteorological) phenomena

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meteorological - relating to the science of weather and the atmosphere

Etymology

From Greek 'meteōrologikos' (pertaining to meteorology), via French 'météorologique'.
