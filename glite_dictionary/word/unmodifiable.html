unmodifiable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈmɑːdəˌfaɪəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈmɒdɪˌfaɪəbl̩ / (BrE)
not able to be modified, changed, or altered in form, character, or strength
not able to be modified

Examples

Certain legal documents are unmodifiable after they have been signed by all parties.
The software treats this section of code as unmodifiable to prevent accidental changes.
Her decision was final and unmodifiable, no matter how much we pleaded.

Synset Examples

these variations from custom are illogical, incomprehensible, and unmodifiable

Related Terms

  • Antonym: modifiable - able to be modified or changed

Etymology

From un- (not) + modifiable, from modify + -able.
