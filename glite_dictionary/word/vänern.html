Vänern

1. PROPN

/ ˈvɛːnɛrn / (AmE), / ˈvɛːnən / (BrE)
the largest lake in Sweden, located in the southwestern part of the country.
largest lake in Sweden

Examples

The shores of Vänern are popular for fishing and boating during the summer months.
After touring Gothenburg, we drove north to see Vänern and enjoy the scenic landscapes.
With its vast surface area, Vänern is important for both Sweden's environment and economy.

Etymology

From Swedish Vänern.
