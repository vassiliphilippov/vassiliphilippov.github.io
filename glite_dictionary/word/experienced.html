experienced

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈspɪriənst / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɪəriənst / (BrE)
having acquired knowledge or skill in a particular field through experience or practice
having knowledge or skill from experience

Examples

She is an experienced teacher who knows how to handle difficult students.
You should hire someone more experienced for this complex project.
After years of traveling, he has become quite experienced in different cultures.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inexperienced - lacking experience or skill
  • Synonym: skilled - having skill or training in a particular area

Etymology

Formed from 'experience' + '-ed', indicating possession of experience.
