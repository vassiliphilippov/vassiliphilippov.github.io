zed

1. NOUN

/ zɛd / (AmE), / zɛd / (BrE)
the name of the letter 'Z' in British English.
British name for the letter 'Z'
british

Examples

In Britain, the last letter of the alphabet is called zed, not zee.
Our teacher asked us to pronounce zed instead of 'zee' while reciting the alphabet.
She learned to write from A to zed at her primary school in London.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

Etymology

From Middle French 'zède', from Latin 'zēta', from Greek 'zēta'.
