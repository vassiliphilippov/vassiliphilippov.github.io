22nd

1. ADJ

/ ˌtwɛnti ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˌtwɛnti ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
coming next after the twenty-first in position; ordinal form of twenty-two
ordinal number: twenty-second

Examples

My birthday is on the 22nd of March.
She finished 22nd in the race, just missing the top twenty.
The meeting is scheduled for the 22nd floor of the building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twenty-second - the ordinal number corresponding to 22
  • Degree Lower: 21st - coming next before; ordinal for 21
  • Degree Higher: 23rd - coming next after; ordinal for 23
