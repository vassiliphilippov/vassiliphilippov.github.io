manliness

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæn.li.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmæn.lɪ.nɪs / (BrE)
The qualities or characteristics traditionally associated with being a man, such as strength, courage, or assertiveness.
qualities of being manly

Examples

His manliness was admired by everyone on the team.
The novel explores traditional ideas of manliness and heroism.
He tried to prove his manliness by taking on the most dangerous job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masculinity - possession of qualities traditionally associated with men
  • Antonym: femininity - qualities associated with being female

Etymology

From 'manly' + '-ness', Old English 'mann' (man, human being).
