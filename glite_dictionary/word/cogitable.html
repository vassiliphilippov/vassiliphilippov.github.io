cogitable

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑːdʒɪtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈkɒdʒɪtəbl̩ / (BrE)
capable of being thought about; conceivable or imaginable.
capable of being thought about

Examples

At the time, no one considered such a solution cogitable by current technology.
The theory, while controversial, is certainly cogitable and merits further discussion.
Is interstellar travel realistically cogitable, or is it just science fiction?

Synset Examples

space flight to other galaxies becomes more cogitable

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conceivable - capable of being imagined or grasped mentally
  • Antonym: inconceivable - not capable of being imagined or grasped mentally

Etymology

From Latin 'cogitare' (to think) + '-able'.
