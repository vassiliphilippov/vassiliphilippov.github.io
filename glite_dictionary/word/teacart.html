teacart

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiˌkɑrt / (AmE), / ˈtiːˌkɑːt / (BrE)
a small wheeled table used for serving tea or light refreshments
small wheeled serving table

Examples

She wheeled the teacart into the room, laden with cups and sandwiches.
The vintage teacart in the corner is one of her favorite pieces of furniture.
After dinner, dessert was served from a polished teacart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trolley - a wheeled cart used for carrying items
  • Synonym: serving cart - a cart used for serving food or drinks

Etymology

From 'tea' + 'cart', referring to a cart used to serve tea.
