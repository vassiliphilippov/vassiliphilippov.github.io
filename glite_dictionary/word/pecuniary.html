pecuniary

1. ADJ

/ pɪˈkjuːniˌɛri / (AmE), / pɪˈkjuːniəri / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of money.
relating to money

Examples

The judge ordered the company to pay pecuniary damages to the plaintiff.
His reasons for taking the job were largely pecuniary rather than personal.
The organization provides assistance to those suffering from pecuniary hardship.

Synset Examples

monetary rewards
he received thanks but no pecuniary compensation for his services

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monetary - relating to money or currency
  • Synonym: financial - relating to money or finance

Etymology

From Latin pecuniarius (of money), from pecunia (money).
