formless

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrmləs / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmləs / (BrE)
lacking a definite shape or structure; without a clear, distinct form.
without a definite shape or structure

Examples

The fog drifted in, its formless mass obscuring the road ahead.
Ideas in his mind were still formless, waiting to be shaped into a story.
The sculpture captured the chaos of nature in a formless swirl of metal.

Synset Examples

amorphous clouds of insects
an aggregate of formless particles
a shapeless mass of protoplasm

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shapeless - without a clear or definite shape
  • Synonym: amorphous - without a clearly defined shape or form
  • Antonym: structured - having a definite or organized form
  • Antonym: defined - having a clear or distinct shape

Etymology

From 'form' + '-less', meaning 'without form'.

2. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrmləs / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmləs / (BrE)
lacking a definite or distinct physical shape or structure; without form or shape.
lacking physical form or shape

Examples

The artist tried to capture the formless nature of clouds in her painting.
Fear took a formless shape, creeping into his mind at night.
The fantasy novel described a formless creature that could not be seen, only felt.

Synset Examples

belief in a world filled with … formless but often malevolent beings

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shapeless - without a definite or regular shape; amorphous

Etymology

from 'form' + '-less', meaning 'without form'
