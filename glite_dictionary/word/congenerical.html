congenerical

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑn.dʒəˈnɛr.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.dʒəˈnɛr.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
Belonging to the same genus as another organism.
of the same genus (biology)

Examples

The two plant species are congenerical and share many physical traits.
This animal was grouped in a congenerical manner with others of its genus.
Researchers observed congenerical similarities across different bird populations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: congeneric - belonging to the same genus

Etymology

From Latin 'congener' (of the same kind or genus) + '-ical'.
