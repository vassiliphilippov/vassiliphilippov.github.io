hallucinatory

1. ADJ

/ həˈluːsənəˌtɔri / (AmE), / həˈluːsɪnətəri / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of hallucinations; resembling a hallucination.
relating to hallucinations

Examples

The patient described hallucinatory experiences during his illness.
The novel is known for its hallucinatory and surreal imagery.
After several days without sleep, he began to have hallucinatory visions.

Synset Examples

the bizarre hallucinatory dreams of fever

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delusional - based on or characterized by delusion

Etymology

From Latin 'hallucinari' (to wander in mind, talk idly) + English '-ory'.
