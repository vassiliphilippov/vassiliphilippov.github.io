rucksack

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌkˌsæk / (AmE), / ˈrʌk.sæk / (BrE)
a bag with shoulder straps carried on the back, used especially by hikers, travelers, or students
a backpack carried on the back

Examples

She packed her clothes and water bottle into her rucksack before setting out on the hike.
His old rucksack had several patches from trips around Europe.
Students left their rucksacks by the classroom door during gym class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backpack - a bag carried on the back, often used synonymously with rucksack

Etymology

From German 'Rucksack' (from 'Rucken' meaning 'back' and 'Sack' meaning 'bag').
