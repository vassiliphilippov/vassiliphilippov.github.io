Aleppo

1. PROPN

/ əˈlɛpoʊ / (AmE), / əˈlɛpəʊ / (BrE)
A city in northwestern Syria, one of the country's largest and oldest cities, known for its historical and cultural significance.
city in northwestern Syria

Examples

Aleppo is renowned for its ancient citadel and vibrant markets.
Many families from Aleppo have relocated due to ongoing conflicts in the region.
The food of Aleppo is celebrated for its rich flavors and variety of spices.
