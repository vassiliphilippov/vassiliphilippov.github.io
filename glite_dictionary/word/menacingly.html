menacingly

1. ADV

/ ˈmɛnəsɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈmɛnəsɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that suggests the presence of danger or expresses a threat.
in a threatening or dangerous way

Examples

She glared menacingly at anyone who tried to approach her desk.
The dog barked menacingly at the strangers near the gate.
Dark clouds gathered menacingly on the horizon as the storm approached.

Synset Examples

the voice at the other end of the line dropped menacingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: threateningly - in a threatening manner

Etymology

From 'menacing' + '-ly', where 'menace' comes from Old French 'menace', from Latin 'minacia' (threats).
