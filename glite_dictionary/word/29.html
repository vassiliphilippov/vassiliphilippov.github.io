29

1. NUM

/ ˈtwɛnti naɪn / (AmE), / ˈtwɛnti naɪn / (BrE)
the number equivalent to twenty-nine; nine more than twenty
the number twenty-nine

Examples

She celebrated her 29th birthday with a small group of friends.
There are 29 days in February during a leap year.
The room can hold a maximum of 29 people.

Related Terms
