Motacilla

1. PROPN

/ moʊtəˈsɪlə / (AmE), / məʊtəˈsɪlə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Motacillidae, comprising the wagtails.
genus of birds; wagtails

Examples

The genus Motacilla includes several species of wagtails found across Eurasia and Africa.
Researchers observed the nesting habits of Motacilla in the river valleys of northern Europe.
A yellow wagtail, scientifically known as Motacilla flava, is a common sight in these meadows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wagtail - a small bird with a long tail, often wagging it up and down

Etymology

From Latin *motacilla* meaning 'wagtail'.
