double-bagger

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌbəl ˈbæɡər / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl ˈbaɡə / (BrE)
(in baseball) a hit that allows the batter to reach second base; a double.
baseball: a hit reaching second base
american

After his double-bagger, the team had a strong chance to win the game.
