/ ˌfɪl.hɑːrˈmɑ.nɪk / (AmE), / ˌfɪl.hɑːˈmɒ.nɪk / (BrE)

large symphony orchestra

Examples

Tickets for the city's philharmonic sold out within hours of going on sale.
