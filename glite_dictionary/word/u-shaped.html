U-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈjuˌʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈjuːʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape or form of the letter U.
shaped like the letter U

Examples

The mountain road curved in a U-shaped bend near the lake.
Researchers studied a U-shaped pipe to observe water flow patterns.
The U-shaped valley was formed by glacial activity.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: V-shaped - having the shape of the letter V
