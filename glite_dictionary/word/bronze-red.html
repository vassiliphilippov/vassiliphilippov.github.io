bronze-red

1. ADJ

/ brɑnz rɛd / (AmE), / brɒnz rɛd / (BrE)
Of a red color tinged with bronze; having a reddish hue with a metallic brownish or coppery aspect.
red tinged with bronze

Examples

The artist chose a bronze-red paint to capture the sunset's unique glow.
She wore a bronze-red dress that shimmered under the lights.
The leaves turned a striking bronze-red in the autumn sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reddish-brown - of brown tinged with red
