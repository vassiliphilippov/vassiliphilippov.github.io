attacker

1. NOUN

/ əˈtækər / (AmE), / əˈtakə / (BrE)
a person who attacks another or something
person who attacks

Examples

The attacker fled the scene before the police arrived.
Security guards quickly subdued the attacker to prevent further harm.
The attacker was caught on surveillance video entering the building.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: defender - someone who defends or protects against attack
  • Synonym: assailant - a person who physically attacks another

Etymology

From the verb 'attack', which comes from French 'attaquer', from Italian 'attaccare' (to attach, attack).
