safekeeping

1. NOUN

/ ˌseɪfˈkiːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌseɪfˈkiːpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or responsibility of keeping something safe or in protective care
the act of keeping safe or in care

Examples

The valuable documents were placed in the bank vault for safekeeping.
I entrusted my grandmother's ring to my brother's safekeeping.
He gave me his keys for safekeeping while he was on vacation.

Synset Examples

he left his car in my keeping

Related Terms

  • Synonym: custody - the protective care or guardianship of someone or something
  • Antonym: carelessness - lack of care or attention

Etymology

From 'safe' + 'keeping', modelled after 'safe keeping' (keeping in a safe condition).
