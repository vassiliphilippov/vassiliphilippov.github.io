boatmanship

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊtmənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈbəʊtmənˌʃɪp / (BrE)
skill or expertise in handling and operating boats
skill in handling boats

Examples

Her boatmanship impressed everyone during the challenging regatta.
Good boatmanship is essential for safely navigating rough waters.
Years of sailing had honed his boatmanship to a professional level.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seamanship - skill in handling a ship or boat
