per diem

1. NOUN

/ pər ˈdiːəm / (AmE), / pə ˈdiːəm / (BrE)
an allowance or amount of money paid each day to cover expenses, especially while traveling for work
daily expense allowance

Examples

The company provides a per diem to cover meals and lodging during business trips.
Employees receive a per diem for each day they spend working away from home.
Because the conference lasted a week, she was paid a generous per diem for her expenses.

Etymology

From Latin 'per diem' meaning 'by the day'.
