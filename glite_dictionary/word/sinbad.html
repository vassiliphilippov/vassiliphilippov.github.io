Sinbad

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪnˌbæd / (AmE), / ˈsɪnˌbæd / (BrE)
A legendary sailor in the Arabian Nights who relates extraordinary adventures during his voyages.
legendary sailor in Arabian Nights

Examples

Sinbad set out on his seventh and final voyage, determined to seek his fortune.
The tales of Sinbad the Sailor are filled with magical creatures and distant islands.
As a child, she loved listening to stories about Sinbad and his daring adventures.

Etymology

From Arabic Sindbād, itself from Persian سندباد (Sindbād), meaning 'lord of the Sindh river.'
