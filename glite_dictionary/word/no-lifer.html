no-lifer

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊ ˌlaɪfər / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ ˌlaɪfə / (BrE)
a person who spends most of their time online or alone, with little to no social interaction or life outside of this activity
person with little or no social life
slang

Examples

He was teased as a no-lifer because he spent all weekend gaming.
Some people unfairly label hobbyists as no-lifers just because they prefer staying in.
She decided not to be a no-lifer anymore and joined a local sports club.

Synset Examples

He's such a no-lifer, always online and never socializing.
Being a no-lifer isn't what life is about.
She called him a no-lifer for skipping the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shut-in - a person who stays indoors and avoids social interaction
  • Antonym: socialite - a person who is well known in fashionable society and attends social gatherings

Etymology

Formed from 'no life' + '-er', following common slang noun construction.
