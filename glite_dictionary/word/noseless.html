noseless

1. ADJ

/ ˈnoʊz.ləs / (AmE), / ˈnəʊz.ləs / (BrE)
Having no nose.
lacking a nose

Examples

The ancient statue was noseless after years of weathering.
The genetic mutation resulted in noseless mice in the research study.
The artist deliberately sculpted a noseless face to evoke a sense of loss.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: nose - the part of the face for smelling and breathing
  • Synonym: snoutless - having no snout

Etymology

Formed from 'nose' + suffix '-less' (without).
