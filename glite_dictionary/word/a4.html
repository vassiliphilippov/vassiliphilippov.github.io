A4

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪ ˈfɔr / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˈfɔː / (BrE)
A standard size of paper measuring 210 by 297 millimeters, commonly used internationally for letters and documents.
standard paper size: 210 x 297 mm

Examples

Could you please print these documents on A4 paper?
She bought a pack of A4 sheets for her new printer.
The report must be submitted on A4 size paper, not letter.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: letter - North American standard paper size (8.5 x 11 inches)

Etymology

From ISO paper size standard; 'A' indicates a series, '4' indicates the size in the series.
