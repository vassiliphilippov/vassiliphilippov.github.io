fiscal

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪs.kəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪs.kəl / (BrE)
relating to government revenue, finances, or financial matters, especially taxes and public spending
relating to finances or government revenue

Examples

The government announced new fiscal measures to address the deficit.
She has a strong background in fiscal management and budgeting.
The company's fiscal year ends in September.

Synset Examples

fiscal responsibility

Related Terms

  • Synonym: financial - relating to finance, money, or the management of money

Etymology

From Latin fiscalis (pertaining to the public treasury), from fiscus (treasury, purse).
