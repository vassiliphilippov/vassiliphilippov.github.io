prosperous

1. ADJ

/ ˈprɑːspərəs / (AmE), / ˈprɒspərəs / (BrE)
successful and thriving, especially in terms of wealth, growth, or profitability.
successful; thriving; doing well

Examples

The city became prosperous thanks to its innovative tech industry.
During the 1990s, many companies enjoyed prosperous years with record profits.
They invested wisely and built a prosperous new business in just a few years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flourishing - growing or developing in a healthy or vigorous way
  • Antonym: struggling - having difficulty or making little progress

Etymology
