chaffinch

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃæˌfɪntʃ / (AmE), / ˈtʃæfɪn(t)ʃ / (BrE)
a small European finch (Fringilla coelebs) known for its cheerful song, with a blue-grey cap and pinkish underparts in males
small European finch with a cheerful song

Examples

We spotted a colorful chaffinch singing in the garden early this morning.
The male chaffinch is easily recognized by its bright plumage and melodious song.
During our walk through the woods, we heard the distinct call of a chaffinch nearby.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finch - a family of small to medium-sized songbirds with conical bills

Etymology

From Old English ceaffinc, from ceaf (chaff) + finc (finch), referring to its feeding habits.
