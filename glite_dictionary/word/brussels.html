Brussels

1. PROPN

/ ˈbrʌsəlz / (AmE), / ˈbrʌsəlz / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Belgium, known as the seat of several major international organizations, including NATO and the European Union.
capital city of Belgium

Examples

Many important decisions for the European Union are made in Brussels.
NATO's headquarters have been based in Brussels since 1967.
She moved to Brussels to work for an international company.

Etymology

From French 'Bruxelles', from Dutch 'Brussel', originally from Old Dutch 'Broekzele' (settlement in the marsh).
