to rephrase

1. VERB

/ riˈfreɪz / (AmE), / riˈfreɪz / (BrE)
to express something using different words, especially to clarify meaning or to avoid repetition
express using different words

Examples

Let me rephrase my question to make it clearer for everyone.
If you didn't understand, I can rephrase the instructions in simpler terms.
She rephrased her statement to avoid any misunderstanding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paraphrase - to express the meaning of something using different words, especially for clarity

Etymology

From re- (again) + phrase (from French phrase, from Latin phrasis, from Greek).
