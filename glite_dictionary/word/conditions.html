conditions

1. NOUN

/ kənˈdɪʃənz / (AmE), / kənˈdɪʃənz / (BrE)
the state of the atmosphere at a particular time, described in terms of temperature, wind, clouds, and precipitation
state of the atmosphere (weather)

Examples

The conditions on the mountain were too dangerous for hiking today.
Check the conditions before planning your outdoor event this weekend.
Driving can be hazardous when winter conditions include snow and ice.

Synset Examples

they were hoping for good weather
every day we have weather conditions and yesterday was no exception
the conditions were too rainy for playing in the snow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weather - the state of the atmosphere at a particular place and time

2. NOUN

/ kənˈdɪʃənz / (AmE), / kənˈdɪʃənz / (BrE)
the set of circumstances or factors affecting the way people live or work, especially in terms of their welfare or comfort
circumstances affecting welfare or comfort

Examples

The factory was inspected due to its poor conditions for workers.
Many refugees have to live in difficult conditions for extended periods.
The team refused to play in such extreme weather conditions.

Synset Examples

hazardous working conditions
harsh living conditions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circumstances - facts or conditions connected with an event or action

Etymology

From Latin 'condicio' (agreement, situation), via Old French 'condition'.

3. NOUN

/ kənˈdɪʃənz / (AmE), / kənˈdɪʃənz / (BrE)
the circumstances or factors affecting the way in which something happens or is experienced
circumstances affecting a situation or process

Examples

The experiment failed because the conditions in the laboratory were not ideal.
You must adapt to changing conditions if you want your business to succeed.
The athletes trained in high-altitude conditions to prepare for the competition.

Synset Examples

there were wide variations in the conditions of observation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circumstances - facts or conditions connected with or relevant to an event or action
  • Synonym: situation - a set of circumstances in which one finds oneself
