fingertip

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪŋɡərˌtɪp / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡətɪp / (BrE)
the end part of a finger, especially the fleshy part beyond the last joint
the end of a finger

Examples

The cut on her fingertip bled for several minutes before stopping.
He played the piano keys with the light touch of his fingertips.
She could feel the fine fabric between her fingertips.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finger - each of the five digits at the end of the human hand

Etymology

Compound of 'finger' and 'tip'.
