muttonfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌtənˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈmʌt(ə)nˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a fish similar to and often marketed as 'red snapper', commonly referring to the mutton snapper or closely related species
a fish similar to red snapper

Examples

Locally caught muttonfish are often sold in markets as red snapper.
The chef recommended grilling the muttonfish to bring out its delicate flavor.
Overfishing has led to stricter regulations on harvesting muttonfish in some regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red snapper - a popular edible fish of the Lutjanidae family, prized for its flavor
