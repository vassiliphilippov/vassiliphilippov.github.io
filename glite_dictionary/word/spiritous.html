spiritous

1. ADJ

/ ˈspɪrɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtəs / (BrE)
(of a liquid) containing alcohol; alcoholic in nature.
containing alcohol

Examples

The menu included several spiritous beverages alongside the non-alcoholic options.
He preferred to avoid spiritous drinks at social gatherings.
Some old apothecaries used spiritous mixtures for medicinal purposes.

Synset Examples

spiritous beverages
spirituous liquors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alcoholic - containing or relating to alcohol

Etymology

From Latin spirituosus (full of spirit, alcoholic), from spiritus (spirit).
