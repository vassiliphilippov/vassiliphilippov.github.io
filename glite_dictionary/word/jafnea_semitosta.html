Jafnea semitosta

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒæfniə ˌsɛmiˈtɒstə / (AmE), / ˈdʒæfniə ˌsɛmɪˈtɒstə / (BrE)
A species of cup-shaped fungus found on dead leaves, with pale brown fruiting bodies that retain a firm, cup-like appearance.
a species of cup-shaped fungus

Examples

The Jafnea semitosta was easily overlooked on the forest floor, blending in with the fallen leaves.
Mycologists found several specimens of Jafnea semitosta during their field survey in late autumn.
The distinctive cup shape of Jafnea semitosta makes it recognizable among other woodland fungi.
