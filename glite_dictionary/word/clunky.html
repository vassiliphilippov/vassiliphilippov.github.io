clunky

1. ADJ

/ ˈklʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈklʌŋki / (BrE)
making a dull, heavy, or awkwardly loud sound; clunking.
making a clunking or awkward sound

Examples

The old washing machine sounded especially clunky during the spin cycle.
Her boots were so clunky that they echoed in the empty hallway.
He complained about the clunky gears every time he shifted his rusty bike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clunking - making a dull, heavy sound

Synset POS: s

2. ADJ

/ ˈklʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈklʌŋki / (BrE)
awkward or lacking grace in movement, design, or appearance; cumbersome or unwieldy.
awkward; lacking grace or elegance

Examples

The old computer had a clunky keyboard that made typing difficult.
She found the shoes clunky and uncomfortable to walk in.
His clunky dancing made everyone in the audience smile.

Synset Examples

clumsy fingers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or handling things
  • Antonym: graceful - showing grace or elegance
  • Synonym: ungainly - lacking smoothness or grace; awkward
