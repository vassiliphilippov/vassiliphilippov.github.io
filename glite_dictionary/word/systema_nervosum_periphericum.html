systema nervosum periphericum

1. NOUN

/ sɪˌstɛm.ə nɜrˈvoʊsəm pɛr.ə.fəˈrɪ.kəm / (AmE), / sɪˌstɛmə nɜːˈvəʊsəm pɛrɪfəˈrɪkəm / (BrE)
the part of the nervous system that consists of all nerves outside the brain and spinal cord
nerves outside brain and spinal cord
medical

Examples

Damage to the systema nervosum periphericum can cause a loss of sensation in the limbs.
The doctor explained how the systema nervosum periphericum sends signals from the body to the brain.
Diseases of the systema nervosum periphericum are often treated by neurologists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peripheral nervous system - the part of the nervous system outside the brain and spinal cord
  • Antonym: central nervous system - the part of the nervous system consisting of the brain and spinal cord

Etymology

From New Latin: 'systema nervosum periphericum,' meaning 'peripheral nervous system.'
