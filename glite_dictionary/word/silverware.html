silverware

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪl.vər.wɛr / (AmE), / ˈsɪl.və.weə / (BrE)
utensils such as knives, forks, and spoons used for eating and serving food, typically made of silver, silver plate, or stainless steel
table utensils made of silver or similar metals

Examples

Please set the table with the silverware before dinner guests arrive.
After the party, she washed all the silverware and put it away in the drawer.
They received a set of engraved silverware as a wedding gift.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cutlery - knives, forks, and spoons used for eating or serving food
  • Synonym: flatware - utensils such as knives, forks, and spoons, especially in American English

Etymology

From 'silver' + 'ware', originally referring to objects made of silver.
