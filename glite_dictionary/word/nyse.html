NYSE

1. PROPN

/ ˌɛn ˈwaɪ ɛs ˈiː / (AmE), / ˌen ˌwaɪ ˌes ˈiː / (BrE)
abbreviation for the New York Stock Exchange, a major stock exchange in New York City located at 11 Wall Street.
US stock exchange in New York City

Examples

Apple shares are traded on the NYSE under the symbol AAPL.
The NYSE closed higher today after a surge in tech stocks.
Many global companies choose to list on the NYSE to gain access to more investors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: New York Stock Exchange - the full name of NYSE, a major US stock exchange

Etymology

Initialism of New York Stock Exchange.
