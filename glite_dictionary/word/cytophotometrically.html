cytophotometrically

1. ADV

/ ˌsaɪtoʊˌfoʊtəˈmɛtrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsaɪtəʊˌfəʊtəˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves or relates to cytophotometry, usually by using cytophotometric methods or techniques
by cytophotometric methods
medical

Examples

The DNA content of the cells was determined cytophotometrically after staining.
Protein concentrations in the tissues were measured cytophotometrically to ensure accuracy.
Researchers often analyze the samples cytophotometrically for more precise quantitative results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cytophotometry - measurement of the chemical components of cells using light absorption

Etymology

Formed from 'cytophotometric' (relating to cytophotometry) + '-ally' (adverbial suffix).
