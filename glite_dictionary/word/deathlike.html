deathlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɛθˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdɛθˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling death in appearance, manner, or atmosphere; having qualities suggestive of death.
resembling death in appearance or state

Examples

The room was filled with a deathlike silence after the shocking announcement.
His face took on a deathlike pallor when he heard the news.
She lay in a deathlike sleep, motionless and pale.

Synset Examples

a deathly pallor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deathly - suggesting or resembling death
  • Antonym: lifelike - having the appearance of being alive
