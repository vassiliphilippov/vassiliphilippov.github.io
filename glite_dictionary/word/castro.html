Castro

1. PROPN

/ ˈkæstroʊ / (AmE), / ˈkæstrəʊ / (BrE)
Fidel Castro (1926–2016), Cuban revolutionary and political leader, who established and led a socialist state in Cuba after overthrowing a dictator in 1959.
Cuban revolutionary leader (1926–2016)

Examples

Castro led Cuba for nearly five decades, shaping its history and foreign policy.
Many historians debate whether Castro's revolution improved life for ordinary Cubans.
In 2016, news of Castro's death prompted international reactions and commentary.
