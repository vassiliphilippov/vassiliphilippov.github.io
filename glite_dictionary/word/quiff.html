quiff

1. NOUN

/ kwɪf / (AmE), / kwɪf / (BrE)
A prominent section of hair at the front of the head, especially one brushed upward and backward from the forehead.
prominent forelock hairstyle

Examples

He styled his hair into a distinctive quiff before heading out for the evening.
The musician's iconic quiff became a symbol of 1950s fashion.
After a gust of wind, her carefully arranged quiff lost its shape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pompadour - a hairstyle with hair swept up from the forehead and worn high

Etymology

Possibly from French 'coiffe' (a head covering), but the ultimate origin is uncertain.
