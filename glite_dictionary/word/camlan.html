Camlan

1. PROPN

/ ˈkæm.læn / (AmE), / ˈkam.læn / (BrE)
In Arthurian legend, the battlefield where King Arthur was mortally wounded in his final battle.
legendary battlefield where King Arthur fell

Examples

According to legend, King Arthur fought his last battle at Camlan.
Many stories recount the tragic events that took place at Camlan.
Historians debate whether Camlan was based on a real location.

Etymology

From Welsh Camlan, the name of the legendary battlefield.
