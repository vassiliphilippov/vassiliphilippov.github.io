passementerie

1. NOUN

/ ˌpæs.mənˈtɛr.i / (AmE), / ˌpæs.məŋˈtɛə.ri / (BrE)
Decorative trimming, such as braid, fringe, or tassels, used to ornament clothing, furnishings, or accessories, typically along edges or borders.
decorative trimming for garments or textiles

Examples

The designer added intricate passementerie to the sleeves, giving the dress a lavish finish.
Luxury curtains often feature passementerie along the edges for an elegant and classic look.
He specialized in restoring antique furniture with original passementerie and upholstery.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trimming - ornamental edging or decoration for clothes or textiles

Etymology

From French *passementerie*, based on *passement* (ornamental braid or lace).
