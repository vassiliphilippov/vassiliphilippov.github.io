Charles Laughton

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʃɑrlz ˈlɔtən / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːlz ˈlɔːtən / (BrE)
(1899–1962) English-born American film and stage actor known for his powerful performances in classic films such as 'The Hunchback of Notre Dame' and 'Mutiny on the Bounty'.
English-born American film and stage actor

Examples

Charles Laughton won an Academy Award for his role in 'The Private Life of Henry VIII.'
Many critics consider Charles Laughton's performance in 'Witness for the Prosecution' to be one of his finest.
The film's success was due in no small part to Charles Laughton's commanding screen presence.
