unaddressed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈdrɛst / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈdrɛst / (BrE)
not having an address written on it; not dealt with or considered
not addressed or dealt with

Examples

The unaddressed envelope sat on the desk for days.
Several critical issues remain unaddressed in the report.
She found a stack of unaddressed postcards in the drawer.

Synset Examples

an unaddressed envelope

Related Terms

  • Antonym: addressed - having an address written on it; dealt with

Etymology

Formed from the prefix un- (not) and addressed.
