bimolecular

1. ADJ

/ ˌbaɪ.moʊˈlɛk.jə.lɚ / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.məʊˈlɛk.jʊ.lə / (BrE)
relating to, consisting of, or involving two molecules, especially in reference to a chemical reaction or interaction between them
involving two molecules
medical

Examples

The rate of the reaction suggests it follows a bimolecular mechanism.
A bimolecular collision occurs when two separate molecules interact to produce a chemical change.
Researchers measured the bimolecular association rate between the enzyme and its substrate.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unimolecular - involving one molecule, especially in a chemical process

Etymology

From prefix 'bi-' (two) + 'molecular' (relating to molecules), modeled after scientific Latin.
