togetherness

1. NOUN

/ təˈɡɛðərnəs / (AmE), / təˈɡɛðənəs / (BrE)
the state of being close to one another in a friendly, loving, or supportive way; affectionate closeness
affectionate closeness; sense of unity

Examples

The holiday season is a time when families value togetherness above all else.
Their sense of togetherness helped them overcome many challenges as a team.
The program aims to promote community togetherness through shared activities and events.

Synset Examples

togetherness is the new wonder ingredient in marriage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: closeness - the quality of being physically or emotionally close
  • Antonym: isolation - the state of being separated from others
