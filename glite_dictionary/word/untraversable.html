untraversable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.trəˈvɜr.sə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.trəˈvɜː.sə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to travel across or through
impossible to cross or travel through

Examples

After the heavy snowfall, the mountain pass became untraversable for several days.
Dense undergrowth and fallen trees made the forest path completely untraversable by vehicles.
Engineers declared that section of the riverbank untraversable for construction equipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impassable - impossible to pass or travel through
  • Antonym: traversable - able to be traversed or crossed

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + traversable (able to be traversed), itself from traverse (to travel across) + -able.
