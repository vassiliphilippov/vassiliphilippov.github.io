Negeri Sembilan

1. PROPN

/ nəˈɡɛri səmˈbiːlɑn / (AmE), / nəˈɡɛri səmˈbiːlæn / (BrE)
One of the 13 states that make up Malaysia, located on the western coast of Peninsular Malaysia.
state of Malaysia on Peninsular's west coast

Examples

Many tourists visit Negeri Sembilan for its unique Minangkabau architecture and cultural heritage.
The capital city of Negeri Sembilan is Seremban, an important administrative center.
Rubber and palm oil plantations are a significant part of Negeri Sembilan's economy.

Etymology

From Malay, meaning 'Nine States', referring to the historical federation of nine smaller chiefdoms.
