spanner

1. NOUN

/ ˈspænər / (AmE), / ˈspænə / (BrE)
A hand tool with jaws or a shaped end, used for gripping, turning, or tightening nuts and bolts.
tool for turning nuts and bolts
british

Examples

He reached for a spanner to tighten the loose bolt.
Could you pass me the spanner from the toolbox?
She keeps a set of spanners in her garage for car repairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrench - a hand tool for turning bolts and nuts (mainly US English)

Etymology

From Old French 'espanne' (a tool for gripping), possibly related to Spanish 'espana' (clamp).
