iodocompound

1. NOUN

/ ˌaɪ.ə.doʊˈkɑm.paʊnd / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.əʊ.dəʊˈkɒm.paʊnd / (BrE)
a chemical compound that contains a covalently bonded iodine atom or radical
chemical with covalent iodine atom

Examples

The researchers synthesized a new iodocompound to study its effects on thyroid function.
Exposure to the iodocompound caused a noticeable reaction in the laboratory test.
Medical imaging agents often contain an iodocompound to improve contrast in scans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organoiodine compound - an organic compound containing iodine

Etymology

Formed from 'iodo-' (relating to iodine, from Greek 'iodes' meaning 'violet-colored') and 'compound'.
