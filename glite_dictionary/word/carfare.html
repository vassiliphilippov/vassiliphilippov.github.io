carfare

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrˌfɛr / (AmE), / ˈkɑːˌfɛə / (BrE)
the fare charged for riding a bus or streetcar
fare for bus or streetcar ride

Examples

He reached into his pocket to find enough carfare for the bus ride home.
Children under five travel free, so you only need one carfare for yourself.
She forgot her carfare and had to walk to work instead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fare - the money paid for a journey on public transport

Etymology

From 'car' (in 'streetcar') + 'fare'.
