2. PROPN

/ seɪnt ˈdʒɑnz / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈdʒɒnz / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Antigua and Barbuda, located on the island of Antigua in the Caribbean.
capital city of Antigua and Barbuda

Examples

We spent our vacation in Saint John's, enjoying the local markets and beaches.
The cruise ship docked at Saint John's early in the morning, giving us a full day to explore.
Many visitors to Antigua begin their journey in Saint John's, the island's bustling capital.

Etymology

Named after the church of Saint John the Divine, which is located in the city.
