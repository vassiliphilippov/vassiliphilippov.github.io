fong kong

1. ADJ

/ fɑŋ kɑŋ / (AmE), / fɒŋ kɒŋ / (BrE)
(slang) Unreliable, flaky, or of poor quality; used to describe people who are neglectful or things that are substandard or fake.
unreliable, flaky, or fake (slang)
slang

Examples

My phone charger turned out to be fong kong and stopped working after a week.
Don't be fong kong when it comes to keeping your promises.
He was called fong kong by his friends after missing their plans again.

Synset Examples

He was accused of being fong kong after skipping meetings.
Don't be fong kong about your responsibilities at work.

Etymology

Originated in South African English, derived from Cantonese term for 'Hong Kong', used to refer to low-quality or counterfeit goods from China.
