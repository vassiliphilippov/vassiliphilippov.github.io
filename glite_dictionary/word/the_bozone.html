the bozone

1. NOUN

/ ðə ˈboʊzoʊn / (AmE), / ði ˈbəʊzəʊn / (BrE)
a humorous or fictional term referring to environmental protection or the concept of an environmental protective layer
fictional/humorous term for environmental protection
informal

Examples

Many environmentalists warned that damage to the bozone would have devastating effects on wildlife.
The documentary jokingly attributed improved climate policies to the strengthening of the bozone.
Some cartoons depict the bozone as an invisible shield protecting Earth from pollution.

The bozone is crucial for protecting our planet.
