landed

1. ADJ

/ ˈlændɪd / (AmE), / ˈlændɪd / (BrE)
owning land, especially large areas; relating to or consisting of land or real estate
owning land; relating to land ownership

Examples

The landed aristocracy held significant power in nineteenth-century England.
She comes from a landed family that owns several farms in the countryside.
Disputes over landed property were common in colonial times.

Synset Examples

the landed gentry
landed property

Related Terms

  • Antonym: landless - without land or property
