thematically

1. ADV

/ θəˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / θɪˈmatɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to the theme or thematic content of something.
in relation to theme or thematic content

Examples

The paintings are thematically linked, exploring issues of identity and culture.
The chapters are organized thematically rather than chronologically for a clearer argument.
The exhibition was praised for being thematically cohesive and intellectually engaging.

Synset Examples

thematically related

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thematic - relating to or based on a theme

Etymology

From "thematic" + -ally, ultimately from Greek "thema" (subject) via Latin.
