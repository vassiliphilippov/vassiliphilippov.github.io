Fish

1. NOUN

/ fɪʃ / (AmE), / fɪʃ / (BrE)
The twelfth sign of the zodiac, traditionally represented by two fishes, covering the period from about February 19 to March 20.
zodiac sign; Pisces

Examples

She was born under the sign of Fish, making her a Pisces.
Many astrologers claim that those born in Fish are sensitive and intuitive.
During Fish, people often reflect on their emotions and dreams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pisces - the twelfth sign of the zodiac; also called the Fishes

2. NOUN

/ fɪʃ / (AmE), / fɪʃ / (BrE)
(Astrology) A person who is born while the sun is in Pisces (approximately February 19 to March 20).
person born under Pisces

Examples

My sister is a true Fish, always compassionate and intuitive.
Many Fish feel most at home near water.
As a Fish, she tends to be artistic and sensitive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pisces - the astrological sign of the zodiac represented by a pair of fish
