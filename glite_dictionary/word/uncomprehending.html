uncomprehending

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkəmprɪˈhɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnkɒmprɪˈhɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
not understanding or showing a lack of understanding; unable to grasp meaning or significance.
not understanding; lacking comprehension

Examples

He gave her an uncomprehending look when she mentioned the technical term.
The students sat in uncomprehending silence as the teacher explained the concept.
Her uncomprehending expression made it clear she was lost in the conversation.

Synset Examples

tried to explain to her uncomprehending husband

Related Terms

  • Antonym: comprehending - showing understanding; grasping meaning
  • Degree Higher: incomprehensible - impossible to understand

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'comprehending' (to understand).
