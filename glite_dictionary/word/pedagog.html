pedagog

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛdəˌɡɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈpiːdəɡɒɡ / (BrE)
a teacher, especially one who educates children or young people
teacher; educator of young people

Examples

The new pedagog fostered a love of learning among his students.
She aspired to become a pedagog and inspire young minds.
Every pedagog has a unique teaching style that shapes the classroom experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially in a school

Etymology

From Latin 'paedagogus', from Greek 'paidagogos' (boy's guide, teacher).
