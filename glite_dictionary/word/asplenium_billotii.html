Asplenium billotii

1. NOUN

/ æsˈpliːniəm bɪˈlɔːtaɪ / (AmE), / æsˈpliːniəm bɪˈlɒti / (BrE)
A species of spleenwort fern native to western Europe.
species of spleenwort fern

Examples

The rocky cliffs in France are home to the rare Asplenium billotii.
Botanists recently discovered a new population of Asplenium billotii in western Spain.
Many ferns, such as Asplenium billotii, thrive in moist, shaded habitats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spleenwort - a type of fern belonging to the genus Asplenium

Etymology

From modern Latin: genus name 'Asplenium', and named after the 19th-century botanist Paul Constant Billot.
