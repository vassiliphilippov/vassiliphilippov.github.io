trumping

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
(in card games) the act of winning a trick by playing a trump card when unable to follow suit.
winning a trick with a trump card

Examples

He managed trumping to win the last round despite holding only low cards.
Her clever trumping of the king changed the outcome of the game.
They discussed strategies for effective trumping before the tournament began.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trump - a suit in cards that ranks above the others for a particular hand or game
