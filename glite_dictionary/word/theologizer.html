theologizer

1. NOUN

/ θiˈɑlədʒaɪzər / (AmE), / θɪˈɒlədʒaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who is learned in or speculates about theology
person who studies or discusses theology

Examples

As a prominent theologizer, she wrote extensively about the nature of divine grace.
The conference attracted every kind of theologizer, from academics to independent thinkers.
Some critics argue that he is more of a theologizer than a strict historian.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theologian - an expert in theology; a scholar of religion

Etymology

From theologize + -er, meaning one who theologizes.
