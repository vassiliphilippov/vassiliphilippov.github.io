Connarus

1. NOUN

/ kəˈnɑːrəs / (AmE), / kəˈnɑːrəs / (BrE)
a genus of tropical trees and shrubs, the type genus of the family Connaraceae
genus of tropical trees and shrubs

Examples

The Connarus genus is widely distributed in tropical regions around the world.
Researchers discovered a new species within the Connarus genus in the Amazon rainforest.
Many species of Connarus are valued for their medicinal properties.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin Connarus, the genus name.

2. NOUN

/ kəˈnɑːrəs / (AmE), / kəˈnɑːrəs / (BrE)
A genus of tropical trees and shrubs belonging to the family Connaraceae.
genus of tropical trees and shrubs

Examples

The Connarus genus contains several species found throughout tropical regions.
Researchers discovered a new member of the Connarus genus in the Amazon rainforest.
Many Connarus species are studied for their potential medicinal properties.

Etymology

From New Latin, named after the genus Connarus.
