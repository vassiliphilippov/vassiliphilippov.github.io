entomology

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛn.təˈmɑ.lə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌen.təˈmɒl.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
The branch of zoology that deals with the scientific study of insects.
scientific study of insects

Examples

She developed a fascination with entomology after observing butterflies in her garden.
A degree in entomology can lead to careers in agriculture and pest control.
His research in entomology has helped identify new methods for protecting crops from insect pests.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: zoology - the scientific study of animals
  • Synonym: insect - a small invertebrate animal with six legs and a body divided into three parts

Etymology

From Greek entomon (insect) + -logia (study).
