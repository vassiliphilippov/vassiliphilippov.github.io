bilingualist

1. NOUN

/ baɪˈlɪŋɡwəlɪst / (AmE), / baɪˈlɪŋɡwəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who speaks two languages fluently
person fluent in two languages

Examples

As a bilingualist, she often works as a translator at international events.
Many bilingualists find careers in education or diplomacy.
The study focused on how bilingualists process information differently from monolinguals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bilingual - able to speak two languages fluently

Etymology

Formed from 'bilingual' + '-ist' (agent noun suffix).
