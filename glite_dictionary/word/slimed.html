slimed

1. ADJ

/ slaɪmd / (AmE), / slaɪmd / (BrE)
covered with or resembling slime; made slimy by a substance
covered with slime

Examples

The rocks near the riverbank were slimed and slippery after the flood.
After the prank, Jamie was completely slimed from head to toe.
The old gutters became slimed with algae during the rainy season.
