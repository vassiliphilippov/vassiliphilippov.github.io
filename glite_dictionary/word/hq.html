HQ

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪtʃ ˈkju / (AmE), / ˌeɪtʃ ˈkjuː / (BrE)
abbreviation for 'headquarters', the place where a commander or organization is based and from which activities are directed.
abbreviation for 'headquarters'

Examples

The new company's HQ is located in downtown New York.
After the attack, troops retreated to the central HQ for instructions.
Please submit your report to HQ by the end of the week.

Synset Examples

the general's headquarters were a couple of large tents

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headquarters - the place from which an organization or commander directs operations

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'headquarters'.
