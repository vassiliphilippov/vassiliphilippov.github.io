VIP

1. NOUN

/ ˌviːˌaɪˈpiː / (AmE), / ˌviːˌaɪˈpiː / (BrE)
a person who is very important or influential, especially one treated with special privileges or attention
very important or influential person

Examples

Only a few VIPs were allowed behind the scenes at the concert.
As a VIP, she received complimentary drinks and front row seats.
They created a separate lounge for VIPs attending the conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: big shot - an important or influential person
  • Antonym: nobody - a person of no importance or influence

Etymology

Acronym from 'Very Important Person', first used in the mid-20th century.
