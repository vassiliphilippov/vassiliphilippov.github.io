tsaristic

1. ADJ

/ zɑːˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / tsɑːˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of a tsar (czar) or the system of government ruled by a tsar.
relating to a tsar or tsarist government

Examples

The museum features artifacts from the tsaristic era of Russian history.
She criticized the leader's tsaristic approach to governance and lack of political openness.
Their traditions reflected the grandeur of the tsaristic court.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: czarist - relating to or characteristic of a czar or the system of czarist government
  • Degree Lower: autocratic - relating to a ruler with absolute power
  • Degree Higher: despotic - exercising absolute power in a cruel and oppressive way

Etymology

From 'tsar' (Russian emperor) + '-istic' (forming adjectives of relation).
