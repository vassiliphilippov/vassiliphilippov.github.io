kit

1. NOUN

/ kɪt / (AmE), / kɪt / (BrE)
the young of certain fur-bearing animals, such as foxes, beavers, or minks
young of certain fur-bearing animals

Examples

The mother fox carefully watched over her kit as it learned to walk.
A beaver kit stays with its family for up to two years before leaving.
Several mink kits played near the entrance of the den.

/ kɪt / (AmE), / kɪt / (BrE)

Examples

Related Terms
