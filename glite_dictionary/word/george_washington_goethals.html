George Washington Goethals

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɔrdʒ ˈwɑʃɪŋtən ˈɡoʊθəlz / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒ ˈwɒʃɪŋtən ˈɡəʊθəlz / (BrE)
(1858–1928) United States army officer and engineer known for supervising the construction of the Panama Canal.
US engineer, oversaw Panama Canal

George Washington Goethals played a crucial role in the successful completion of the Panama Canal.
