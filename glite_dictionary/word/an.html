AN

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪ ˈɛn / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˈɛn / (BrE)
An abbreviation for associate degree in nursing, an academic degree granted to nurses who have completed a two-year program.
associate degree in nursing (abbreviation)
medical american

Examples

She earned her AN before transferring to a four-year nursing program.
Many hospitals hire new graduates with an AN as entry-level registered nurses.
After completing my AN, I plan to specialize in pediatric care.
