gaily

1. ADV

/ ˈɡeɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪli / (BrE)
In a cheerful, lively, or bright manner.
cheerfully; in a lively way

Examples

She walked gaily down the street, smiling at everyone she met.
The room was gaily decorated for the holiday party.
Children laughed gaily as they played in the park.

Synset Examples

the scandals were gaily diverting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheerfully - in a cheerful manner
  • Synonym: merrily - in a happy, joyful manner

Etymology

From English 'gay' (cheerful, bright) + '-ly', dating to late Middle English.
