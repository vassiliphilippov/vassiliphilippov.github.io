pastor

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæs.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɑː.stə / (BrE)
a minister in charge of a Christian church or congregation, especially in Protestant denominations
minister or religious leader of a Christian church

Examples

The pastor delivered an inspiring sermon on compassion this Sunday.
Many members of the community turned to their pastor for guidance during difficult times.
After graduating from seminary, she became the youngest pastor in the city.

Synset Examples

clergymen are usually called ministers in Protestant churches

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minister - a member of the clergy, especially in Protestant churches
  • Synonym: priest - an ordained minister of the Catholic, Orthodox, or Anglican Church

Etymology

From Latin pastor (shepherd).
