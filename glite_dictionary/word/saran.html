Saran

1. NOUN

/ səˈræn / (AmE), / səˈræn / (BrE)
A trademarked name for a group of thermoplastic resins, especially polyvinylidene chloride, used chiefly in making food wraps and other plastic products.
trademarked thermoplastic resin for wraps

Examples

She wrapped the leftover cake in Saran to keep it fresh.
The factory manufactures miles of Saran each day for food packaging.
He prefers using Saran over aluminum foil for covering bowls.

Etymology

Trademark name introduced by Dow Chemical in the 1930s.
