storied

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɔr.id / (AmE), / ˈstɔː.rid / (BrE)
(of a building) having a specified number of floors or stories.
having a specified number of floors

Examples

They live in a storied apartment block overlooking the river.
The city is filled with old three-storied houses.
A new eight-storied office tower opened downtown last year.

Synset Examples

a six-storied building

Related Terms

  • Synonym: story - a level of a building

2. ADJ

/ ˈstɔːrid / (AmE), / ˈstɔːrid / (BrE)
having a celebrated, illustrious, or notable history
having a notable or illustrious history

Examples

The storied university has produced many Nobel Prize winners.
He joined the storied football club with hopes of winning another championship.
Her family comes from a storied lineage, known throughout the region for their achievements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distinguished - famous for its excellence or achievements
  • Antonym: obscure - not well known or famous

Etymology

From story + -ed, originally meaning 'celebrated in story.'
