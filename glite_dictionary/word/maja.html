Maja

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪdʒə / (AmE), / ˈmɑːjə / (BrE)
A genus of crabs in the family Majidae, found in many parts of the world.
genus of crabs in Majidae family

Examples

The species within the genus Maja are commonly known as spider crabs.
Several Maja crabs were collected during the marine survey.
The Maja genus includes some of the largest crabs in temperate waters.

Etymology

From Latin 'Maja', used in taxonomy.
