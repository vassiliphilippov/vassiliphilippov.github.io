antisubmarine

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.ti.sʌbˈmɑː.rin / (AmE), / ˌæn.ti.sʌbˈmɑː.riːn / (BrE)
Designed to detect, attack, or defend against enemy submarines.
designed to counter submarines

Examples

The navy deployed antisubmarine patrols along the coast to protect merchant ships.
They invested in advanced antisubmarine technology to improve national defense.
The ship was equipped with antisubmarine missiles and sonar systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anti-aircraft - designed to defend against aircraft

Etymology

From 'anti-' meaning 'against' and 'submarine'.
