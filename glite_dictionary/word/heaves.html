heaves

1. NOUN

/ ˈhivz / (AmE), / ˈhiːvz / (BrE)
A chronic respiratory disease in horses, marked by difficulty breathing and heaving of the flanks, similar to emphysema in humans.
chronic respiratory disease in horses
medical

Examples

The veterinarian diagnosed the horse with heaves after noticing its labored breathing and persistent coughing.
Because of heaves, the mare had trouble keeping up during routine exercises at the stable.
Owners should minimize dust and mold exposure to help prevent heaves in their horses.
