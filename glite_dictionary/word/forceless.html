forceless

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrsləs / (AmE), / ˈfɔːsləs / (BrE)
lacking force, strength, or effectiveness; weak or feeble.
lacking force or strength

Examples

The speech was surprisingly forceless and failed to inspire the audience.
Her objections seemed forceless compared to his compelling arguments.
A forceless handshake can give a poor first impression.

Synset Examples

a forceless argument

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powerless - lacking power or ability
  • Antonym: forceful - full of force; vigorous or powerful
  • Synonym: weak - lacking strength or force

Etymology

Formed from 'force' + '-less' (without).
