arboriform

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑrbərɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈɑːbərɪˌfɔːm / (BrE)
having the shape or branching structure of a tree; tree-like in form
tree-shaped; tree-like in form

Examples

The coral reef was dominated by arboriform structures, providing shelter for many fish.
The arboriform growth of the fungus made it easy to mistake for a tiny tree.
Scientists described the new mineral as having a distinctively arboriform appearance.

Synset Examples

arborescent coral found off the coast of Bermuda
dendriform sponges

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arborescent - growing in the shape or form of a tree
  • Synonym: dendritic - having a branching, tree-like structure

Etymology

From Latin 'arbor' (tree) + '-form' (having the form of).
