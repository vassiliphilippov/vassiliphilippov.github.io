latter-day

1. ADJ

/ ˈlæt̬ɚ ˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈlætə ˌdeɪ / (BrE)
Relating to the present or recent times; modern or contemporary.
modern; recent times

Examples

Many historians draw parallels between ancient and latter-day political struggles.
Critics have compared him to a latter-day Robin Hood for his philanthropic acts.
The latter-day technology has revolutionized communication across the globe.
