steakhouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪkˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈsteɪkˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A restaurant that specializes in serving steaks and other types of meat dishes.
restaurant specializing in steaks

Examples

We decided to have dinner at a new steakhouse downtown.
The steakhouse is famous for its grilled ribeye and friendly service.
He opened his own steakhouse after years of working as a chef.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restaurant - a place where people pay to sit and eat meals

Etymology

From 'steak' + 'house', indicating a place specializing in steaks.
