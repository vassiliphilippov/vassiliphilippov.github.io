curiosa

1. NOUN

/ kjʊˈriːoʊsə / (AmE), / kjʊəˈrɪəzə / (BrE)
Books or printed materials dealing with unusual, rare, or especially erotic subjects.
books on unusual or erotic subjects

Examples

The library's rare collection includes several pieces of curiosa from the nineteenth century.
Collectors of curiosa often seek out limited editions with highly unusual themes.
He discovered a trove of curiosa in the attic, hidden away for decades.

Etymology

From Latin 'curiosus' (curious).
