Princess Diana

1. PROPN

/ ˈprɪnsɛs daɪˈænə / (AmE), / ˈprɪnsəs daɪˈænə / (BrE)
(1961–1997) the first wife of Prince Charles, known for her charity work and worldwide popularity; died in a car accident in Paris.
first wife of Prince Charles, public figure

Examples

Many people admired Princess Diana for her charitable work and compassion.
News of Princess Diana's death shocked the world in 1997.
The wedding of Princess Diana and Prince Charles was watched by millions around the globe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Diana, Princess of Wales - formal title for Princess Diana
  • Antonym: Prince Charles - her husband, heir apparent to the British throne
