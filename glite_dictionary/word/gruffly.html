gruffly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡrʌf.li / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌf.li / (BrE)
in a rough, abrupt, or unfriendly manner, especially in speech
in a rough or abrupt manner

Examples

He answered gruffly, not wanting to continue the conversation.
She told him gruffly to mind his own business.
The old man gruffly dismissed the request.

Synset Examples

‘No,’ he replied gruffly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abruptly - in a sudden or curt manner
  • Antonym: softly - in a gentle or quiet manner
