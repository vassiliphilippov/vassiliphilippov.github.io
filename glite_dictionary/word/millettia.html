Millettia

1. NOUN

/ mɪˈlɛtiə / (AmE), / mɪˈlɛtiə / (BrE)
a genus of trees and shrubs native to the Old World tropics, belonging to the family Fabaceae
a tropical genus of trees and shrubs

Examples

Several species of Millettia are valued for their ornamental flowers and timber.
Researchers discovered new medicinal compounds in the bark of Millettia.
The Millettia genus includes plants found across Asia and Africa.

Etymology

Named after Charles Millett, a 19th-century British botanist.
