nationally

1. ADV

/ ˈnæʃənəli / (AmE), / ˈnæʃnəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to a nation as a whole, rather than a local or regional area.
in a nationwide manner

Examples

The policy was implemented nationally to ensure consistency across all regions.
The company plans to expand nationally over the next five years.
The festival is celebrated nationally and attracts visitors from all over the country.

Synset Examples

a nationally uniform culture

Related Terms

  • Antonym: locally - in a way that relates to a particular area or neighborhood
  • Antonym: regionally - on a regional, not national, scale

2. ADV

/ ˈnæʃənəli / (AmE), / ˈnæʃnəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or affects an entire nation; across a whole country.
across a whole nation

Examples

The event was nationally televised, attracting viewers from every state.
This disease has become a nationally recognized health concern.
That company is nationally known for its customer service.

Synset Examples

nationally advertised
it was broadcast nationwide

Related Terms

  • Antonym: locally - in a particular area, city, or town rather than the whole nation
  • Antonym: regionally - pertaining to a region or area within a country

Etymology

Formed from 'national' + '-ly', with 'national' from Latin 'natio' (nation, race).
