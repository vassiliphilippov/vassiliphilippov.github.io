interviewee

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.tɚ.vjuˈi / (AmE), / ˌɪn.tə.vjuːˈiː / (BrE)
a person who is being interviewed, especially for a job, research, or on television or radio
person being interviewed

Examples

The interviewee answered each question thoughtfully and with confidence.
Each interviewee brought a unique perspective to the discussion panel.
The company sent each interviewee a thank-you note after the recruitment process.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: interviewer - the person who asks the questions in an interview

Etymology

Formed from 'interview' + '-ee', denoting the person affected by an action.
