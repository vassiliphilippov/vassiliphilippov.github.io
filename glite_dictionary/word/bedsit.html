bedsit

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛdˌsɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌsɪt / (BrE)
A small, furnished room that combines a living and sleeping area, sometimes with basic kitchen or bathroom facilities, typically rented as accommodation.
small single-room apartment
british

Examples

After moving to London, he rented a small bedsit near the station.
She furnished her bedsit with secondhand items.
The housing shortage meant many students lived in cramped bedsits.

Etymology

Blend of 'bed' and 'sitting room'.
