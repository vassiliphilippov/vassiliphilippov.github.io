Lisboa

1. PROPN

/ liˈsboʊə / (AmE), / lɪzˈboː.ə / (BrE)
The Portuguese name for Lisbon, the capital and largest city of Portugal, located on the Tagus River.
Portuguese name for Lisbon, Portugal's capital

Examples

Many tourists visit Lisboa each year to explore its rich history and vibrant culture.
The conference will be held in Lisboa next summer.
Flights from New York to Lisboa are available daily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lisbon - the capital city of Portugal in English

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Lisboa', the native name for Lisbon.
