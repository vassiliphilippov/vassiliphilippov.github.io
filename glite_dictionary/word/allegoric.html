allegoric

1. ADJ

/ ˌælɪˈɡɔrɪk / (AmE), / ˌælɪˈɡɒrɪk / (BrE)
relating to, containing, or characteristic of allegory; symbolic or metaphorical in nature.
relating to allegory; symbolic

Examples

The poem is highly allegoric, filled with layers of symbolic meaning.
She prefers allegoric novels that offer deeper moral messages.
The artist created an allegoric mural to represent the struggle for freedom.

Synset Examples

allegorical stories
an allegorical painting of Victory leading an army

Related Terms

  • Synonym: allegorical - relating to or resembling allegory; symbolic

Etymology

From French allégorique, from Latin allegoricus, from Greek allēgorikos (of allegory).
