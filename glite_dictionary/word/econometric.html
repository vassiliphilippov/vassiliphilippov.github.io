econometric

1. ADJ

/ ˌiːkənoʊˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌiːkənəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the application of statistical and mathematical methods to the analysis of economic data and problems.
relating to econometrics

Examples

The researcher published an econometric analysis of the labor market.
He used econometric models to forecast economic growth.
This paper discusses the strengths and limitations of various econometric techniques.

Synset Examples

econometric theories

Related Terms

  • Synonym: statistical - relating to the use of statistics
  • Degree Lower: economic - relating to economics

Etymology

From 'econometrics', formed from 'economy' + Greek 'metron' (measure).
