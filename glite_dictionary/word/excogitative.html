excogitative

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈskɑdʒ.ɪˌteɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / ɪkˈskɒdʒ.ɪ.tə.tɪv / (BrE)
relating to or having the power of thinking out or devising; concerned with deep or elaborate thought.
relating to deep or elaborate thought

Examples

Her excogitative approach to problem-solving impressed the committee.
The philosopher was praised for his excogitative writings on human consciousness.
A true scientist must possess an excogitative mind, always probing for new ideas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reflective - relating to deep thought or consideration
  • Antonym: unthinking - not showing careful thought or consideration

Etymology

From Latin excogitativus, from excogitare (to think out).
