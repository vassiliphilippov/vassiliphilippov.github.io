fraudulently

1. ADV

/ ˈfrɔdʒələntli / (AmE), / ˈfrɔːdjʊləntli / (BrE)
In a dishonest or deceitful way, typically with the intention to commit fraud.
in a dishonest, deceitful manner

Examples

The company was accused of having fraudulently altered its financial records.
He fraudulently claimed to have a medical degree.
Applicants who submit fraudulently obtained documents may be prosecuted.

Synset Examples

this money was fraudulently obtained

Related Terms

  • Antonym: honestly - in an honest manner
  • Synonym: deceitfully - in a deceitful manner

Etymology

From 'fraudulent' + '-ly'; 'fraudulent' from Latin 'fraudulentus' (deceitful).
