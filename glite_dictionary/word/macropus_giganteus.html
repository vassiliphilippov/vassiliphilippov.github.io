Macropus giganteus

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæk.rəˈpuːs ʤɪˈɡæn.ti.əs / (AmE), / ˌmak.rəˈpjuːs ʤɪˈɡan.tɪ.əs / (BrE)
the scientific name for the eastern grey kangaroo, a large greyish-brown kangaroo native to eastern Australia
eastern grey kangaroo species

Examples

The scientific name for the eastern grey kangaroo is Macropus giganteus.
Researchers studied a group of Macropus giganteus in their natural habitat.
Unlike other species, Macropus giganteus thrives in open woodland environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eastern grey kangaroo - a large, greyish-brown kangaroo species native to eastern Australia

Etymology

From Greek makros (large) + pous (foot) and Latin giganteus (gigantic)
