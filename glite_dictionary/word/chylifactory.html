chylifactory

1. ADJ

/ kaɪˈlɪfəktəri / (AmE), / kaɪˈlɪfəktəri / (BrE)
producing or converting into chyle (a milky bodily fluid formed in the small intestine during digestion).
producing or converting into chyle
medical

Examples

The chylifactory glands are responsible for processing lymph into chyle.
Certain digestive organs have a chylifactory function during fat absorption.
Researchers studied the chylifactory activity in the small intestine after a high-fat meal.

Etymology

From Greek *chylos* (juice) + Latin *facere* (to make).
