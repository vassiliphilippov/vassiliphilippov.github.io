smaller

1. ADJ

/ ˈsmɔlər / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːlə / (BrE)
of less size or amount than something else; the comparative form of 'small'.
comparative of small; less in size or amount

Examples

The smaller suitcase fits perfectly in the overhead compartment.
This classroom is smaller than the one we used last year.
Many animals become smaller at higher altitudes due to harsh conditions.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: small - of little size; not large
  • Degree Higher: smallest - superlative of 'small'; the least in size or amount
