to aromatise

1. VERB

/ əˈroʊməˌtaɪz / (AmE), / əˈrɒmətaɪz / (BrE)
to fill or impregnate with a pleasant smell or aroma
fill with a pleasant smell

Examples

The chef likes to aromatise his dishes with fresh herbs.
A gentle breeze aromatised the room with the scent of lavender.
You can aromatise oils by infusing them with citrus peel or spices.

Synset Examples

orange blossoms perfumed the air in the garden

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfume - to fill with a pleasant fragrance
  • Antonym: to deodorize - to remove unpleasant odors

Etymology

From French aromatiser, based on Latin aroma (spice, scent).
