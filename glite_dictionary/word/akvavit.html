akvavit

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːk.və.viːt / (AmE), / ˈak.və.viːt / (BrE)
A Scandinavian spirit, usually distilled from grain or potatoes and flavored with caraway seeds and other spices.
Scandinavian caraway-flavored spirit

Examples

During the festival, we sampled traditional dishes and sipped akvavit with locals.
The distinct aroma of caraway makes akvavit a unique spirit among European liquors.
She brought a bottle of akvavit back from her trip to Norway as a souvenir.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aquavit - A variant spelling for akvavit, the Scandinavian spirit

Etymology

From Danish and Norwegian 'akvavit', from Latin 'aqua vitae' meaning 'water of life'.
