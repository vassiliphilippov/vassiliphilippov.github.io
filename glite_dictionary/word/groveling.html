groveling

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡrɑvəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡrɒvəlɪŋ / (BrE)
overly submissive; showing excessive willingness to please or obey someone
excessively submissive

Examples

He delivered a groveling apology to his boss after the mistake.
Her groveling attitude irritated her colleagues, who preferred more independence.
The servant's groveling behavior made his master uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: submissive - willing to obey or yield to others
  • Antonym: assertive - confidently self-assured; inclined to express one's views

Etymology

From the verb 'grovel', of uncertain origin, perhaps from 'groove' + '-le'.
