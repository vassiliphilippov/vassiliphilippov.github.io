Aruba

1. PROPN

/ əˈruːbə / (AmE), / əˈruːbə / (BrE)
an island in the Caribbean Sea, part of the Kingdom of the Netherlands, known as a popular tourist destination, with its capital at Oranjestad.
Caribbean island and tourist destination

Examples

Many tourists choose Aruba for its beautiful beaches and warm climate.
The capital city of Aruba is Oranjestad, known for its colorful Dutch architecture.
She took a cruise that stopped in Aruba for a day of sightseeing and shopping.

Etymology

Spanish and Dutch influence; name possibly from indigenous language for 'well-placed island'.
