Berlin

1. PROPN

/ bərˈlɪn / (AmE), / bɜːˈlɪn / (BrE)
The capital city of Germany, located in the northeastern part of the country.
capital of Germany

Examples

She visited Berlin during her summer vacation to explore its rich history and vibrant arts scene.
The Berlin Wall once divided Berlin into East and West until its fall in 1989.
Next year, the international conference will take place in Berlin.

Etymology

From German 'Berlin', the name of the city.
