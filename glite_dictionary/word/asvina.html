Asvina

1. NOUN

/ ɑːʃˈviːnə / (AmE), / ɑːʃˈviːnə / (BrE)
The seventh month of the Hindu calendar, typically falling in September-October in the Gregorian calendar.
seventh month of Hindu calendar

Examples

Many Hindu festivals are celebrated during Asvina, including Navaratri and Durga Puja.
The fields were lush as the month of Asvina brought much-needed rains.
Her wedding was planned for Asvina, following family tradition.

Etymology

From Sanskrit अश्विन (aśvina), meaning 'of the Ashvins', referring to divine twin horsemen in Hindu mythology.
