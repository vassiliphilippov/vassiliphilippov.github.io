inexpressible

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.ɪkˈsprɛs.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.ɪkˈsprɛs.ɪ.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to express in words; defying expression or description.
impossible to express in words

Examples

She felt an inexpressible sense of gratitude that words could not convey.
The beauty of the sunrise gave him an inexpressible joy each morning.
The pain after the loss was inexpressible, leaving her silent and overwhelmed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indescribable - impossible to describe

Etymology

From Late Latin inexpressibilis, from in- (not) + expressibilis (expressible).
