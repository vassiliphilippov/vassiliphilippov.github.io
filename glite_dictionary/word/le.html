LE

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛl ˈi / (AmE), / ˌɛl ˈiː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for lupus erythematosus, a chronic inflammatory disease affecting the skin or joints
abbreviation for lupus erythematosus (disease)
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with LE after presenting symptoms of joint pain and skin rashes.
Recent studies have focused on improved treatments for LE.
Doctors often monitor organ involvement in patients suffering from LE.

Etymology

Initialism of 'lupus erythematosus', from Latin 'lupus' (wolf) and 'erythematosus' (reddened).
