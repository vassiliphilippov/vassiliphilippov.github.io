raita

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtə / (AmE), / ˈraɪtə / (BrE)
An Indian side dish made from yogurt mixed with chopped cucumbers and spices, often served to cool the palate.
Indian yogurt-based side dish

Examples

The spicy curry was balanced perfectly by a bowl of raita on the side.
She prepared cucumber raita to accompany the biryani at dinner.
Many Indian restaurants serve cool raita to help with the heat of their dishes.

Etymology

From Hindi रायता (rāytā), well-known in Indian cuisine.
