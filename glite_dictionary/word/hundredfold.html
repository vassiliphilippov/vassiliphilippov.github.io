hundredfold

1. ADV

/ ˈhʌndrədˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈhʌndrədˌfəʊld / (BrE)
by a factor of one hundred; multiplied by one hundred times.
by one hundred times

Examples

Profits grew hundredfold after the product's release.
Her investment increased hundredfold over the course of a decade.
Scientific discoveries have expanded hundredfold since the introduction of modern technology.

Synset Examples

their money increased a hundredfold

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: tenfold - by a factor of ten
  • Degree Higher: thousandfold - by a factor of one thousand

Etymology

From Old English 'hund' (hundred) + 'feald' (fold, times).
