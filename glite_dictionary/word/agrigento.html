Agrigento

1. PROPN

/ ˌɑːɡriˈdʒɛntoʊ / (AmE), / ˌaɡrɪˈdʒɛntəʊ / (BrE)
A town in southwestern Sicily, Italy, known for its ancient Greek temples and archaeological sites.
town in Sicily, Italy, with Greek temples

Examples

The Valley of the Temples in Agrigento attracts thousands of visitors each year.
During our trip to Sicily, we explored the ancient ruins near Agrigento.
Agrigento is famous for its well-preserved Doric temples.

Etymology

From Italian 'Agrigento', from ancient Greek 'Akragas', the original name of the city.
