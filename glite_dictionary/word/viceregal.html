viceregal

1. ADJ

/ ˌvaɪsəˈriːɡəl / (AmE), / ˌvʌɪsɪˈriːɡəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a viceroy or their office
relating to a viceroy

Examples

The governor attended the viceregal banquet in the grand hall.
Her speech carried the tone of authority expected in a viceregal pronouncement.
The museum is displaying artifacts from the viceregal era in India.

Synset Examples

the viceregal visit

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: royal - pertaining to a king or queen

Etymology

From vice- (deputy) + regal (of a king), influenced by Latin 'regalis' (royal).
