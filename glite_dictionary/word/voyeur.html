voyeur

1. NOUN

/ vɔɪˈjɜr / (AmE), / vwɑːˈjɜː / (BrE)
a person who gains sexual pleasure from watching others when they are naked or engaged in sexual activity, especially secretly
a person who observes sexual acts or nudity for pleasure

Examples

The police arrested a voyeur who had been secretly filming people through their windows.
Many films have explored the psychology of the voyeur and the thrill of watching from a distance.
He was accused of being a voyeur after he was caught spying on his neighbors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peeping Tom - a person who spies on others in secret, especially for sexual gratification

Etymology

From French 'voyeur' (one who sees), from 'voir' (to see).
