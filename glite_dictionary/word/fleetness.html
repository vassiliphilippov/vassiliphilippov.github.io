fleetness

1. NOUN

/ ˈflitnəs / (AmE), / ˈfliːtnəs / (BrE)
quickness and ease of movement; rapidity or swiftness.
rapidity or swiftness of movement

Examples

The fleetness of the athlete surprised the scouts during the trials.
Her fleetness of foot made her an exceptional sprinter on the track team.
He admired the fleetness with which the deer vanished into the forest.

Synset Examples

fleetness of foot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swiftness - the quality of moving very quickly
  • Antonym: slowness - the quality of moving with little speed

Etymology

From Middle English 'flet' (swift) + -ness.
