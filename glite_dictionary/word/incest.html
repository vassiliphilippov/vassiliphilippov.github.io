incest

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.sɛst / (AmE), / ˈɪn.sɛst / (BrE)
sexual intercourse between close relatives, especially those too closely related to marry by law or custom, such as between a parent and a child or between siblings.
sexual intercourse between close relatives

Examples

The law strictly prohibits incest between family members.
He was ostracized from the community after being accused of incest.
Historical accounts reveal that some royal families practiced incest to preserve bloodlines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fornication - sexual intercourse between people not married to each other
  • Synonym: adultery - voluntary sexual intercourse between a married person and someone other than their spouse

Etymology

From Latin incestum (unchastity), from in- (not) + castus (pure).
