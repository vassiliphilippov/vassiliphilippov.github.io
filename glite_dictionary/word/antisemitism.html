antisemitism

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.tiˈsɛ.məˌtɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈsiː.mɪˌtɪz.əm / (BrE)
Hostility to or prejudice against Jewish people.
prejudice against Jewish people

Examples

Many organizations are working to combat antisemitism in schools and communities.
Reports of antisemitism have unfortunately increased in recent years.
The museum exhibit explores the history and consequences of antisemitism in Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racism - prejudice or discrimination against people based on their race
  • Antonym: tolerance - acceptance and open-mindedness toward those different from oneself

Etymology

From German Antisemitismus, combining anti- (against) and Semite (relating to Jews).
