secretariate

1. NOUN

/ ˌsiː.krəˈtɛr.i.ət / (AmE), / ˌsiː.krəˈtæ.ri.ət / (BrE)
an administrative department responsible for maintaining records and handling secretarial duties, especially within international organizations
administrative office; records-keeping unit

Examples

The United Nations secretariate efficiently coordinated communications between member states.
All official records are archived by the secretariate of the committee.
The secretariate ensured that meeting minutes were accurately distributed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secretariat - the office or department that manages records and secretarial tasks, especially in international organizations
