laid-back

1. ADJ

/ leɪd ˈbæk / (AmE), / leɪd ˈbak / (BrE)
relaxed and not easily upset or worried; unhurried in manner or style
relaxed; easygoing
informal

Examples

Her laid-back attitude makes her easy to work with, even during stressful times.
He always seems so laid-back when everyone else is rushing around.
The beach town is known for its laid-back atmosphere and friendly people.

Synset Examples

a mellow conversation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relaxed - not tense or anxious; calm
  • Antonym: uptight - anxious or tense; easily upset
