backed

1. ADJ

/ bækt / (AmE), / bækt / (BrE)
(of photographic film) coated on the side opposite the emulsion with a substance that absorbs light.
film coated on its back to absorb light

Examples

Most infrared-sensitive films are backed to prevent halation during exposure.
The photographer preferred using backed film for sharper images.
Special backed emulsions are chosen for certain scientific applications.

/ bækt / (AmE), / bækt / (BrE)
having a back or backing, typically of a specified kind or material
having a back or backing

Examples
