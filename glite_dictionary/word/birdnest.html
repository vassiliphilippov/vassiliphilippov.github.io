birdnest

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝdˌnɛst / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdˌnɛst / (BrE)
a nest built by birds for laying eggs and rearing their young
nest built by birds

Examples

We spotted a birdnest tucked high up in the branches of the elm tree.
The abandoned birdnest was lined with feathers and bits of string.
Please do not disturb the birdnest in the garden while the eggs are hatching.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nest - a structure made by a bird for laying eggs and sheltering its young
