unsold

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsoʊld / (AmE), / ʌnˈsəʊld / (BrE)
not disposed of by being purchased; not yet sold.
not yet sold

Examples

The house remained unsold despite several viewings.
Many of the books were left unsold after the event ended.
The store offered discounts on unsold inventory at the end of the season.

Synset Examples

the house has been on the market almost a year and is still unsold

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sold - disposed of by being purchased

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'sold,' past participle of 'sell.'
