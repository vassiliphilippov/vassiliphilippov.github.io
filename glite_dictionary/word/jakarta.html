Jakarta

1. PROPN

/ dʒəˈkɑrtə / (AmE), / dʒəˈkɑːtə / (BrE)
The capital and largest city of Indonesia, located on the island of Java.
capital city of Indonesia

Examples

My friend is moving to Jakarta for a new job opportunity.
Jakarta is famous for its vibrant street food and busy markets.
The international conference will be held in Jakarta next month.

Etymology

From Indonesian 'Jakarta', abbreviation of 'Jayakarta', meaning 'complete victory'.
