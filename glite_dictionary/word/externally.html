externally

1. ADV

/ ɪkˈstɝːnəli / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɜːnəli / (BrE)
On or relating to the outside or outer surface of something; in an external manner.
on the outside; in an external manner

Examples

The machine appears undamaged externally, but the internal parts may be broken.
The medication should only be applied externally, never swallowed.
Though the house looked fine externally, it needed many repairs inside.

Synset Examples

outwardly, the figure is smooth

Related Terms

  • Antonym: internally - on or relating to the inside; in an internal manner

Etymology

From Latin externus (outward, outside) + -ly.

on or from the outside; in an external manner
on or from the outside

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms
