semicentennial

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛmiˌsɛnˈtɛniəl / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˌsɛnˈtɛniəl / (BrE)
the fiftieth anniversary of an event or the celebration marking it
fiftieth anniversary or its celebration

Examples

The university will host a gala in honor of its semicentennial this year.
The city’s semicentennial celebrations attracted visitors from across the country.
They unveiled a commemorative plaque to mark the company’s semicentennial milestone.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: centennial - the hundredth anniversary or its celebration
  • Degree Lower: quartercentennial - the twenty-fifth anniversary or its celebration

Etymology

From Latin 'semi-' (half) and 'centennial' (relating to a hundred years), by analogy with 'centennial'.

2. ADJ

/ ˌsɛmiˌsɛnˈtɛniəl / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˌsɛnˈtɛniəl / (BrE)
relating to or marking the fiftieth anniversary of an event
relating to a 50th anniversary

Examples

The museum organized a semicentennial exhibition to celebrate its 50 years of history.
Our school held a semicentennial reunion for the class of 1973.
The company issued a special publication on its semicentennial year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golden jubilee - the celebration of a 50th anniversary

Etymology

From Latin 'semi-' (half) + 'centennial' (of a hundred years).
