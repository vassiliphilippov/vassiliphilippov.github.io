Charles Dodgson

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʃɑrlz ˈdɑdʒsən / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːlz ˈdɒdsən / (BrE)
The real name of Lewis Carroll (1832–1898), English author, mathematician, and Oxford academic, best known for writing the 'Alice' books.
real name of Lewis Carroll, author and mathematician

Examples

Few people realize that Charles Dodgson was a brilliant mathematician as well as a famous author.
Under the name Charles Dodgson, he taught at Oxford and contributed to the field of logic.
The works of Charles Dodgson have been studied by both literary and mathematical scholars.
