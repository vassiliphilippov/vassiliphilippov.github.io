Ru

1. NOUN

/ ru / (AmE), / ru / (BrE)
The chemical symbol for ruthenium, a rare, hard metallic element of the platinum group, used in alloys and electronics.
chemical symbol for ruthenium

Examples

The element Ru is often used as a catalyst in chemical reactions.
Platinum alloys frequently contain small amounts of Ru to increase their strength.
The analysis confirmed the presence of Ru in the mineral sample.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ruthenium - a rare metallic element of the platinum group
  • Degree Lower: Pt - chemical symbol for platinum, an element in the same group as ruthenium

Etymology

From Latin 'Ruthenia', the name for Russia, where the element was first discovered.
