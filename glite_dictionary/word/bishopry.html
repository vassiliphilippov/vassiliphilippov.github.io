bishopry

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪʃəpri / (AmE), / ˈbɪʃəpri / (BrE)
the office, rank, or jurisdiction of a bishop
office or rank of a bishop

Examples

He was elevated to the bishopry after years of dedicated service.
Her family celebrated her appointment to the bishopry with great pride.
The responsibilities of the bishopry include overseeing the clergy within the diocese.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bishopric - the district under a bishop's control; also the office of a bishop

Etymology

From bishop + -ry (a noun-forming suffix).
