Smolensk

1. PROPN

/ sməˈlɛnsk / (AmE), / sməˈlɛnsk / (BrE)
a city in western Russia situated on the Dnieper River, known for its strategic importance and severe fighting during World War II
city in western Russia

Examples

During World War II, the Battle of Smolensk was a pivotal conflict on the Eastern Front.
Many historic buildings in Smolensk were reconstructed after the war.
Tourists often visit Smolensk to explore its ancient fortress and rich history.
