stabilized

1. ADJ

/ ˈsteɪbəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈsteɪbɪlaɪzd / (BrE)
made stable or firm; having been rendered steady or secure
made stable or secure

Examples

The building has been stabilized to prevent further collapse.
After a few hours, her condition finally stabilized.
The chemicals are stabilized to ensure a longer shelf life.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unstable - prone to change or fail; not stable
  • Synonym: secure - fixed or fastened so as not to give way
