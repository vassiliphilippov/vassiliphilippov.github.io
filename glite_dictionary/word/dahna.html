Dahna

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɑːnə / (AmE), / ˈdɑːnə / (BrE)
a desert region in southern Saudi Arabia, known for its red sand dunes
desert in Saudi Arabia

Examples

The Dahna stretches for hundreds of kilometers across the heart of Saudi Arabia.
Travelers must be well prepared before crossing the Dahna desert.
The striking red sands of the Dahna attract photographers and adventurers from around the world.
