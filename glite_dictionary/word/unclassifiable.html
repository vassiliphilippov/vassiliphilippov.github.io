unclassifiable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈklæs.ɪˌfaɪ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈklɑː.sɪ.fʌɪ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to classify or assign to a category
impossible to classify

Examples

The painting was so unique and experimental that critics deemed it unclassifiable.
His methods are unclassifiable within any known scientific framework.
Some literary works are so original they remain unclassifiable to this day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unclassifiable - unable to be classified
  • Antonym: classifiable - able to be classified

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + classify + -able.
