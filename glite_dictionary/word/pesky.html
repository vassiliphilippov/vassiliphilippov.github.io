pesky

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛski / (AmE), / ˈpeski / (BrE)
causing annoyance or trouble; irritating
annoying or troublesome
informal

Examples

Those pesky flies kept landing on my food during the picnic.
I've finally fixed that pesky leak in the bathroom sink.
She managed to solve a pesky problem that had stumped everyone else.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annoying - causing irritation or bother
  • Synonym: troublesome - causing difficulty or annoyance

Etymology

Probably an alteration of 'pest' + '-y', influenced by 'pesky' in 19th-century American English for 'pestiferous' or 'irritating'.
