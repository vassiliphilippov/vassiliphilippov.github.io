Tokyo

1. PROPN

/ ˈtoʊ.kioʊ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.kɪ.əʊ / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Japan, serving as its economic and cultural center
capital city of Japan

Examples

Many tourists dream of visiting Tokyo to experience its blend of modern skyscrapers and traditional temples.
The 2020 Olympic Games were held in Tokyo, attracting participants from around the world.
Tokyo is renowned for its vibrant nightlife and delicious cuisine.

Etymology

From Japanese 東京 (Tōkyō), meaning 'eastern capital'.
