uninventive

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪnˈvɛntɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈvɛntɪv / (BrE)
lacking originality or creative ability; not inventive or imaginative.
lacking originality or creativity

Examples

The film received criticism for its uninventive plot and flat characters.
Her approach to solving the problem was disappointingly uninventive.
He was known for his uninventive style, sticking closely to old techniques.

Synset Examples

a sterile ideology lacking in originality
unimaginative development of a musical theme
uninspired writing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unimaginative - lacking imagination; not creative
  • Synonym: uninspired - not exciting or original; lacking inspiration
  • Antonym: creative - having the ability or power to create; original or imaginative

Etymology

From un- (not) + inventive.
