pea-green

1. ADJ

/ ˈpiː ˌɡrin / (AmE), / ˈpiː ˌɡriːn / (BrE)
of a moderate, slightly yellowish-green color, similar to that of fresh peas
moderate yellowish-green color

Examples

She chose a pea-green dress for the garden party, blending in beautifully with the surroundings.
The walls were painted a pea-green shade that gave the kitchen a fresh, lively look.
His old bicycle, still pea-green after all these years, leaned against the shed.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: green - the color between blue and yellow in the spectrum
  • Degree Lower: yellow-green - a color between yellow and green

Etymology

From 'pea' (the vegetable) + 'green', referring to the typical color of fresh green peas.
