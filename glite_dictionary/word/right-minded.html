right-minded

1. ADJ

/ ˌraɪtˈmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˌraɪtˈmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
Having views and attitudes that are considered morally correct or just.
having morally correct views

Examples

Every right-minded person was shocked by the unfair treatment of the workers.
It's hard to imagine any right-minded individual supporting such a policy.
She hoped her children would grow up to be right-minded and honest adults.

Synset Examples

respect for law which every right-minded citizen ought to have

Related Terms

  • Synonym: upright - morally correct or honorable
  • Antonym: ill-minded - having immoral or bad intentions; opposite of right-minded
