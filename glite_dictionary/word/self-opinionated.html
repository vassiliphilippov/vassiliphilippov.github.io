self-opinionated

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlf əˈpɪnjəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf əˈpɪnjəneɪtɪd / (BrE)
Having an unreasonably high regard for one's own opinions; obstinate in holding one's own views.
obstinately attached to one's own opinions

Examples

His self-opinionated attitude made teamwork very difficult.
She can be quite self-opinionated when it comes to politics.
Despite being talented, his self-opinionated nature often alienates colleagues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opinionated - holding stubbornly and often unreasonably to one's own opinions

Etymology

Formed from 'self-' and 'opinionated', meaning having a high opinion of oneself.
