borated

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɔːreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɔːreɪtɪd / (BrE)
Mixed or impregnated with borax.
mixed or impregnated with borax

Examples

The wooden beams were borated to protect them from insect damage.
Manufacturers often use borated polyethylene as a neutron shield in nuclear facilities.
The borated cotton was used for its fire-retardant properties in laboratory experiments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: borax - a white mineral used in cleaning and industry

Etymology

From 'borax', a mineral, with the suffix '-ed' indicating the state of being treated or mixed.
