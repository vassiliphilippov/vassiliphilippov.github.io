xxv

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛks ˌɛks ˈviː / (AmE), / ˌɛks ˌɛks ˈviː / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for 25
Roman numeral for 25

Examples

The event was held to celebrate the xxv anniversary of the school's founding.
In chapter xxv, the story takes a dramatic turn.
Her seat number was xxv in the auditorium.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 25 - the cardinal number twenty-five

Etymology

From Latin numerals: 'X' (ten), 'V' (five); 'XXV' represents 10 + 10 + 5 = 25.
