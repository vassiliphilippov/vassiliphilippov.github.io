XXV

1. NUM

/ ɛks ɛks viː / (AmE), / ɛks ɛks viː / (BrE)
the Roman numeral representing the number twenty-five (25)
Roman numeral for 25

Examples

The inscription read 'Chapter XXV' at the top of the page.
She celebrated her XXV birthday with a grand party.
The film festival is marking its XXV anniversary this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 25 - the cardinal number twenty-five

Etymology

From Latin numerals: 'X' (10) + 'X' (10) + 'V' (5) = 25.
