speleologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌspiːliˈɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌspiːliˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies or explores caves as a scientific or recreational activity
person who studies or explores caves

Examples

The speleologist descended carefully into the narrow cavern, documenting every new formation he found.
As a speleologist, she spends much of her time mapping underground cave systems around the world.
The team included a speleologist to ensure their safety and study the unique cave environment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caver - a person who explores caves, especially as a hobby

Etymology

From Latin 'speleum' (cave) + Greek '-logistēs' (one who studies).
