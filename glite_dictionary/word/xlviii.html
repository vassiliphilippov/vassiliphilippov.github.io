xlviii

1. NUM

/ ɛks ɛl vɪɪɪ / (AmE), / ɛks ɛl vɪɪɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for 48
the number 48 (Roman numerals)

Examples

The inscription read 'Anno Domini xlviii', indicating the year 48 AD.
She solved the puzzle to reveal the number xlviii hidden in the code.
Pages xlviii to lxii contain the bibliography.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 48 - the cardinal number forty-eight
  • Degree Higher: xlix - the Roman numeral for 49
  • Degree Lower: xlvii - the Roman numeral for 47

Etymology

From Latin numerals: X (10), L (50), V (5), I (1); XL (40) + VIII (8) = 48.
