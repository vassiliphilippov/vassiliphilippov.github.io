closest

1. ADV

/ ˈkloʊsɪst / (AmE), / ˈkləʊsɪst / (BrE)
at the shortest distance in space or time; most nearly approaching a particular point or state
superlative of 'close'; shortest distance

Examples

Whoever sits closest to the window can adjust the blinds.
This is the closest I've ever been to winning a major award.
She ran closest to the finish line before tripping.

Synset Examples

that was the time he came nearest to death

Related Terms

  • Synonym: near - at or to a short distance away; close by
  • Antonym: farthest - at the greatest distance; most distant
  • Synonym: closest - superlative of 'close' (adjective)

Etymology

Superlative form of 'close', from Middle English clos, from Old French clos, from Latin clausum (enclosure).
