puddingwife

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊdɪŋˌwaɪf / (AmE), / ˈpʊdɪŋˌwaɪf / (BrE)
A bluish and bronze wrasse (fish), found in the western Atlantic from the Florida Keys to Brazil.
a species of bluish and bronze wrasse

Examples

The vibrant colors of the puddingwife make it a favorite among snorkelers in the Caribbean.
Marine biologists studied several puddingwives along the coral reefs of Florida.
Local fishermen sometimes catch puddingwife by mistake while targeting other reef fish.
