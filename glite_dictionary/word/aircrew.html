aircrew

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌkru / (AmE), / ˈeə.kruː / (BrE)
the group of people who operate an aircraft during a flight, including pilots and other members involved in the operation
crew members operating an aircraft

Examples

The aircrew completed their pre-flight checks before boarding the passengers.
After the long journey, the aircrew relaxed in the airport lounge.
Each member of the aircrew has a specific role to ensure a safe flight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crew - the group of people who work together on a vehicle or operation

Etymology

Formed from 'air' + 'crew', indicating crew members of an aircraft.
