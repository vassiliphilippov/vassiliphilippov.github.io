to fancify

1. VERB

/ ˈfæn.sɪ.faɪ / (AmE), / ˈfan.sɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
to make (something) more elaborate or attractive, often in a decorative or showy way
make more elaborate or attractive
informal

Examples

She decided to fancify her old jacket with some colorful patches.
We need to fancify this plain room before the party starts.
He fancified his resume to impress potential employers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to beautify - to make beautiful or more beautiful
  • Synonym: to decorate - to make something look more attractive by adding things to it
  • Antonym: plain - not decorated or elaborate
