Al Capone

1. PROPN

/ æl kəˈpoʊn / (AmE), / æl kəˈpəʊn / (BrE)
(1899–1947) A notorious American gangster who dominated organized crime in Chicago during Prohibition, later convicted for tax evasion.
notorious American gangster (1899–1947)

Examples

The infamous Al Capone was finally arrested for tax evasion instead of his violent crimes.
Many books and movies have been inspired by the life of Al Capone.
During Prohibition, Al Capone became one of America's most feared criminals.
