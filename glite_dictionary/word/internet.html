Internet

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.tɚˌnɛt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.təˌnɛt / (BrE)
a global computer network providing a variety of information and communication facilities, consisting of interconnected networks using standardized communication protocols
global network of connected computers

Examples

More people are shopping online now thanks to the Internet.
My grandparents learned to use the Internet to stay in touch with family.
Many businesses depend on a reliable Internet connection to operate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: web - a system of interlinked hypertext documents on the internet

Etymology

Shortened form of 'interconnected network', first used in the 1970s.
