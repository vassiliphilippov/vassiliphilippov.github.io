roundswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈraʊndzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈraʊndzˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to make rounds, such as delivering goods or making inspections, especially over a regular route
female delivery or inspection worker

Examples

The company hired a new roundswoman to deliver newspapers along the morning route.
As a roundswoman, she made sure every office received its daily mail on time.
The roundswoman inspected each apartment to ensure all safety checks were completed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roundsman - a man whose job is to make rounds, such as delivering goods or making inspections

Etymology

Formed from 'roundsman' by analogy, using 'woman' to indicate female gender.
