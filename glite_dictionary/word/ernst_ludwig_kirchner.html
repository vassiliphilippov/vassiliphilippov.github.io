Ernst Ludwig Kirchner

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛrnst ˈluːtvɪɡ ˈkɪrknər / (AmE), / ˈɛːnst ˈluːtvɪç ˈkɪːxnə / (BrE)
(1880–1938) a German expressionist painter and one of the founders of the artist group Die Brücke.
German expressionist painter (1880–1938)
