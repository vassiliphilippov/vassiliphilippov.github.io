gustatory

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡʌstəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈɡʌstətəri / (BrE)
connected with tasting or the sense of taste
relating to the sense of taste

Examples

The chef created a menu designed to provide a unique gustatory experience.
The gustatory nerves are responsible for transmitting taste sensations to the brain.
Researchers studied the gustatory responses of participants to different flavors.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: olfactory - relating to the sense of smell

Etymology

From Latin 'gustare' (to taste) + '-ory'.
