beardo

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪɚ.doʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɪə.dəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a man who has a distinctive or notable beard, often used affectionately or humorously
man with a notable beard (informal)
informal slang

Examples

Everyone in town recognizes the beardo who plays guitar on the corner.
The new coffee shop is always full of beardos discussing craft beer.
He grew a long beard and eventually became known as the village beardo.

Synset Examples

He was known as the local beardo with his long, wild beard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beard - facial hair on the chin and cheeks

Etymology

Formed from 'beard' + '-o', a slangy suffix for person.
