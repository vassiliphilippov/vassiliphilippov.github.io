wristlet

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪst.lət / (AmE), / ˈrɪst.lɪt / (BrE)
A small band, strap, or bracelet designed to be worn around the wrist, often for decorative or practical purposes.
band or bracelet worn on the wrist

Examples

She wore a silver wristlet that matched her necklace perfectly.
His wristlet was engraved with his initials for a personal touch.
The store offers a variety of wristlets in different colors and materials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bracelet - an ornamental band or chain worn around the wrist

Etymology

From 'wrist' + diminutive suffix '-let'.
