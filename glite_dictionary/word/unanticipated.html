unanticipated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ænˈtɪs.əˌteɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ænˈtɪs.ɪˌteɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
not expected or predicted; occurring without forewarning.
not expected or predicted

Examples

The discovery of the ancient artifact was entirely unanticipated by the research team.
Due to unanticipated expenses, the company had to revise its annual budget.
Her resignation was so unanticipated that it took everyone by surprise.

Synset Examples

unanticipated and disconcerting lines of development
unforeseen circumstances
a virtue unlooked-for in people so full of energy
like a bolt out of the blue
unseen problems

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unexpected - not expected or anticipated
  • Antonym: anticipated - regarded as probable; expected

Etymology

From un- (not) + anticipated (from Latin anticipatus, past participle of anticipare, 'to take beforehand').
