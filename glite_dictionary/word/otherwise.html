otherwise

1. ADV

/ ˈʌðɚˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈʌðəˌwaɪz / (BrE)
in a different way; differently
in a different way

Examples

If the train is delayed, we will have to travel otherwise.
The meeting proceeded as planned, unless instructed otherwise.
She was expected to smile, but she behaved otherwise.

Synset Examples

very soon you will know differently
she thought otherwise
there is no way out other than the fire escape

Related Terms

  • Synonym: differently - in a different way; not in the same manner

Etymology

From Old English 'ōþerwís', meaning 'in another way'.

Examples

Synset Examples
