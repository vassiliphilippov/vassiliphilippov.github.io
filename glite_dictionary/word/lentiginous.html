lentiginous

1. ADJ

/ lɛnˈtɪdʒənəs / (AmE), / lɛnˈtɪdʒɪnəs / (BrE)
Having or resembling freckles; relating to or characterized by the presence of freckles.
having or resembling freckles

Examples

The patient's skin appeared lentiginous, with numerous small brown spots across his cheeks.
Some plants have lentiginous markings on their leaves, resembling the speckles found on human skin.
A lentiginous pattern can be a distinguishing factor in certain dermatological diagnoses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freckled - having or marked with freckles

Etymology

From Latin lentiginosus (freckled), from lentigo (freckle).
