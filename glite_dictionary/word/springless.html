springless

1. ADJ

/ ˈsprɪŋləs / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋləs / (BrE)
lacking elasticity, liveliness, or vitality; not having the quality of springing or bouncing.
without elasticity or liveliness

Examples

After a long, difficult day, his walk became springless and slow.
The old mattress felt uncomfortable and springless after years of use.
Her springless voice suggested she was exhausted from the journey.

Synset Examples

went off with springless steps

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lively - full of life and energy
  • Antonym: elastic - able to resume its normal shape after being stretched

Etymology

Formed from 'spring' + suffix '-less' (without).
