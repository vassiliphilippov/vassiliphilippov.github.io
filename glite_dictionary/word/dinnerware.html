dinnerware

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnərˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈdɪnəwɛə / (BrE)
the plates, dishes, bowls, and other items used for serving and eating a meal
plates and dishes used for meals

Examples

We're saving the good dinnerware for the holiday dinner next week.
The registry offers a selection of elegant dinnerware patterns from top brands.
Please place the dinnerware on the table before the guests arrive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tableware - utensils, dishes, and containers for serving and eating food
  • Synonym: silverware - knives, forks, and spoons used for eating or serving food

Etymology

From 'dinner' + 'ware', modeled after similar compounds like 'silverware' and 'tableware'.
