peacefully

1. ADV

/ ˈpisfəlli / (AmE), / ˈpiːsfəli / (BrE)
In a calm and quiet way, without violence, disturbance, or conflict.
in a calm, non-violent way

Examples

The two countries resolved their disagreement peacefully without any armed conflict.
She peacefully drifted off to sleep as the rain tapped against the window.
Protesters marched peacefully through the city, carrying signs and singing songs.

Synset Examples

the hen settled herself on the nest most peacefully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calmly - in a calm and composed manner
  • Antonym: violently - using or involving physical force or aggression

Etymology

From 'peaceful' + '-ly'; 'peaceful' from 'peace' (from Old French 'pais', from Latin 'pax, pacis', meaning 'peace').
