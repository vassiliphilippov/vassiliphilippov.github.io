to prosper

1. VERB

/ ˈprɑspər / (AmE), / ˈprɒspə / (BrE)
to become successful, especially financially; to make steady progress or flourish
become successful or flourish

Examples

Despite many challenges, the small business continued to prosper each year.
With the right environment, these plants can prosper in your garden.
She worked hard to prosper in her chosen career.

Synset Examples

The new student is thriving

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to thrive - to grow, develop, or be successful vigorously
  • Synonym: flourish - to grow or develop in a healthy, vigorous way

Etymology

From Latin 'prosperare', from 'prosperus' meaning 'favorable, successful'.
