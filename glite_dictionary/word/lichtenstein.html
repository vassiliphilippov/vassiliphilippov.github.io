Lichtenstein

1. PROPN

/ ˈlaɪk.tənˌstaɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɪx.tən.ʃtaɪn / (BrE)
Roy Lichtenstein (1923–1997), a United States painter known as a leading exponent of pop art, famous for his comic-strip style works.
American pop artist (1923–1997)

Examples

Many art historians credit Lichtenstein with elevating comic book imagery into high art.
The bold, dotted style of Lichtenstein is instantly recognizable around the world.
A major exhibition of Lichtenstein’s work drew thousands of visitors to the museum.
