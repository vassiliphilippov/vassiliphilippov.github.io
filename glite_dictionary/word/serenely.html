serenely

1. ADV

/ səˈrinli / (AmE), / səˈriːnli / (BrE)
in a calm, peaceful, and untroubled manner.
in a calm and peaceful way

Examples

She sat serenely on the porch, watching the sunset in silence.
Despite the chaos around him, he smiled serenely and continued his work.
The lake lay serenely under the morning mist, undisturbed by any breeze.

Synset Examples

I had the feeling that he was waiting, too — serenely patient

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calmly - in a calm manner
  • Synonym: peacefully - in a peaceful manner

Etymology

From 'serene' + '-ly', with 'serene' from Latin 'serenus' (clear, calm).
