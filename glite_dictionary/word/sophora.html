Sophora

1. PROPN

/ soʊˈfɔɹə / (AmE), / səˈfɔːrə / (BrE)
A genus of trees and shrubs with pinnate leaves and showy flowers, found in many parts of the world.
genus of trees and shrubs

Examples

The Sophora genus includes many species valued for their ornamental flowers.
Botanists recently discovered a new Sophora species in the region.
Several Sophora trees were planted along the avenue for their decorative appeal.

Etymology

From Latin Sophora, from Arabic ṣufayrāʼ, meaning a type of tree.

2. NOUN

/ soʊˈfɔrə / (AmE), / səʊˈfɔːrə / (BrE)
A genus of trees and shrubs with odd-pinnate leaves and showy, often pea-like flowers, found worldwide.
genus of trees and shrubs with showy flowers

Examples

The Sophora tree is admired for its beautiful, bright yellow flowers.
Several species in the Sophora genus are cultivated for ornamental purposes.
Researchers studied the medicinal properties of plants belonging to the Sophora group.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin 'Sophora', derived from Arabic 'ṣufayrāʾ', a kind of tree.
