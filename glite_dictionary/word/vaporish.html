vaporish

1. ADJ

/ ˈveɪpərɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈveɪpərɪʃ / (BrE)
Having the qualities or appearance of vapor; resembling vapor.
resembling vapor

Examples

The mountains were shrouded in a vaporish mist early in the morning.
Her memory of the event felt vaporish, as if the details might fade away at any moment.
A vaporish glow surrounded the lake at dawn, giving it an ethereal appearance.

Synset Examples

vaporous clouds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vaporous - consisting of or resembling vapor

Etymology

From 'vapor' + '-ish', a suffix meaning 'having the characteristics of'.
