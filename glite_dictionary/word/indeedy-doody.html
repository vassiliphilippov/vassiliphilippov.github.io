indeedy-doody

1. INTJ

/ ɪnˈdidi ˈduːdi / (AmE), / ɪnˈdiːdi ˈduːdi / (BrE)
Used to express strong affirmation or agreement in an informal or playful way.
emphatic informal agreement
informal

Examples

"Are you coming to the party tonight?" "Indeedy-doody! I'll be there at eight."
"Will you help me paint the kitchen?" she asked, and he replied with an enthusiastic 'indeedy-doody'.
Whenever someone asks if he loves pizza, he always answers, 'indeedy-doody!'

Synset Examples

We’re going to the park, indeedy-doody!
He agreed with her, saying, 'indeedy-doody'!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indeed - used to emphasize a statement or response

Etymology

Playful reduplication and extension of 'indeed' with a rhyming, nonsense suffix.
