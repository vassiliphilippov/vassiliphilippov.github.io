Mrs

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪsɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɪsɪz / (BrE)
a title used before the surname or full name of a married woman
title for a married woman

Examples

The letter was addressed to Mrs Smith at her home.
Please welcome Mrs Johnson, our new principal.
She introduced herself as Mrs Anderson, the manager of the store.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Miss - title for an unmarried woman
  • Synonym: Ms - title for a woman regardless of marital status

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'mistress', formerly used as a title of respect for a woman.
