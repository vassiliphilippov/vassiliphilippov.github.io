parturient

1. ADJ

/ pɑːrˈtʃʊriənt / (AmE), / pɑːˈtjʊəriənt / (BrE)
(of a woman or female animal) relating to or in the process of giving birth.
relating to giving birth
medical

Examples

The parturient woman was closely monitored by the doctors during labor.
Veterinarians prepared the barn for the parturient cow's delivery.
The study focused on pain management in parturient patients.

Synset Examples

parturient pangs
the parturient uterus

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parturition - the act of giving birth; childbirth

Etymology

From Latin parturient-, present participle of parturire (to be in labor), from parere (to give birth).

2. ADJ

/ pɑrˈtʊriənt / (AmE), / pɑːˈtjʊəriənt / (BrE)
(of a female) giving birth or about to give birth.
giving birth or about to give birth
medical

Examples

The veterinarian checked on the parturient cow during the night to ensure a safe delivery.
The parturient patient was taken to the maternity ward for close monitoring.
Farmers must pay special attention to parturient animals to prevent complications.

Synset Examples

a parturient heifer

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: pregnant - (of a woman or female animal) having a child or young developing in the uterus

Etymology

From Latin parturient- 'being in labor', from parturire 'to be in labor', from parere 'to give birth'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00003828-s
Synset POS: s
