muddleheaded

1. ADJ

/ ˈmʌdəlˌhɛdəd / (AmE), / ˈmʌd(ə)lˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
Having or showing confusion and a lack of clear thinking; stupid and unable to think sensibly.
mentally confused or stupid

Examples

I became so muddleheaded after a sleepless night that I forgot my keys at home.
His muddleheaded ideas made it hard for the team to make any progress.
Don't be muddleheaded—double-check the instructions before starting the project.

Synset Examples

blathering like the addlepated nincompoop that you are
a confused puddingheaded, muddleheaded fellow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: addlepated - stupid and confused
  • Antonym: clearheaded - able to think clearly; not confused

Etymology

From 'muddle' (to mix up) + 'headed', meaning with a mixed-up head.
