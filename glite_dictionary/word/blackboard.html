blackboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈblækˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈblæk.bɔːd / (BrE)
a large, flat, dark surface, typically made of slate, used in classrooms for writing on with chalk
chalk writing surface in classrooms

Examples

The teacher wrote the math problem on the blackboard for the whole class to see.
Please erase the blackboard before you leave the classroom.
During the lecture, she filled the blackboard with diagrams and notes.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: whiteboard - smooth white surface for writing with markers
