featheredged

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɛðərˌɛdʒd / (AmE), / ˈfɛðədˌɛdʒd / (BrE)
having a rough, irregular edge that tapers off, especially as seen in handmade or imitation handmade paper
having a rough, irregular edge

Examples

The invitation was printed on featheredged paper to create a vintage look.
She admired the featheredged border of the handmade letter.
The artist preferred using featheredged sheets for her sketches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deckle-edged - having the rough edge characteristic of handmade paper

Etymology

From 'feathered' + 'edge', describing an edge that resembles the frayed outline of a feather.
