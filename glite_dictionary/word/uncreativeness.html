uncreativeness

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.krieɪˈtɪv.nəs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kriːˈeɪ.tɪv.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of lacking creativity or originality
lack of creativity

Examples

Critics blamed the film's failure on its overall uncreativeness and lack of originality.
The teacher encouraged students to avoid uncreativeness in their writing assignments.
His uncreativeness made him ill-suited for a career in advertising.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: creativity - the use of imagination or original ideas
  • Antonym: originality - the quality of being novel or unique
  • Synonym: dullness - lack of interest or excitement

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'creativeness'.
