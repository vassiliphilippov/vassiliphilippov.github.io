thermoelectrical

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɜrmoʊɪˈlɛktrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊɪˈlɛktrɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or produced by thermoelectricity; involving the direct conversion between heat and electric energy.
relating to thermoelectricity

Examples

The thermoelectrical properties of the material were tested in the laboratory.
Advancements in thermoelectrical devices are helping to improve energy efficiency.
He specializes in the study of thermoelectrical effects in semiconductor technology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermoelectric - relating to or producing a direct conversion of heat into electricity or vice versa

Etymology

Formed from 'thermo-' (relating to heat) and 'electric' with the suffix '-al'.
