grater

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtə / (BrE)
A kitchen utensil with a surface of sharp perforations used for shredding foods such as vegetables or cheese into fine pieces.
utensil for shredding food

Examples

Please pass me the grater so I can shred some cheese for the pasta.
She bought a new grater that makes it easy to prepare vegetables for salads.
After using the grater, be careful when washing it to avoid cutting your fingers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shredder - utensil or machine for shredding things into pieces; in cooking, a synonym for grater

Etymology

From the Old French 'grater' (to scrape or grate), from Latin 'grattāre', frequentative of 'grattāre' (to scrape).
