electroplater

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛktroʊˈpleɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈpleɪtə / (BrE)
a person who coats objects with a metal layer using electrolysis
person who coats objects using electrolysis

Examples

The electroplater carefully monitored the process to ensure a smooth, even coating.
As an experienced electroplater, she could apply a gold finish to delicate jewelry pieces.
The company hired a skilled electroplater to restore antique silverware to its original shine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plater - a person who plates objects with metal
  • Synonym: electroplate - to coat with metal by electrolysis

Etymology

From electro- (relating to electricity) + plate (to coat with metal).
