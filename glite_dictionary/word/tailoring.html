tailoring

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪlərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈteɪlərɪŋ / (BrE)
the occupation, business, or skill of making, repairing, or altering clothes, especially suits, coats, and trousers
the craft or occupation of making clothes

Examples

She has been practicing tailoring professionally for over twenty years.
The art of tailoring requires patience, precision, and a keen eye for detail.
Modern tailoring combines traditional techniques with new technology to create custom clothing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressmaking - the craft of making women's clothes

Etymology

From tailor + -ing; 'tailor' from Old French *tailleur* (cutter), from *tailler* (to cut).
