Senegal

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɛnɪˌɡɔl, ˌsɛnɪˈɡɔl / (AmE), / ˈsɛnɪɡɑːl / (BrE)
A republic in northwestern Africa on the Atlantic coast; formerly a French colony, independent since 1960.
country in northwestern Africa

Examples

She traveled to Senegal to study West African music and culture.
After gaining independence in 1960, Senegal became known for its stable democracy.
The capital of Senegal is Dakar, a vibrant city on the Atlantic coast.

Etymology

From French 'Sénégal', possibly from the Wolof 'Sunugal', meaning 'our canoe'.
