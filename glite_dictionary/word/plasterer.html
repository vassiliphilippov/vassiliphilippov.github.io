plasterer

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæs.tɚ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈplɑː.stər.ə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to apply plaster to walls and ceilings
person who applies plaster

Examples

The plasterer carefully smoothed the wall until it was perfectly even.
A team of plasterers finished restoring the ornate ceilings in the old theater.
He hired a skilled plasterer to repair the cracked walls in his house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mason - person skilled in building with stone or brick

Etymology

From plaster + -er, denoting a person who applies plaster.
