northwesterly

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɔrˈθwɛstɚli / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˈwɛstəli / (BrE)
Situated in or oriented toward the northwest.
oriented toward the northwest

Examples

The ship followed a northwesterly course across the ocean.
They built their house on the northwesterly side of the hill to catch the sunset.
We turned onto a northwesterly road that led us toward the mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: northwest - situated in the northwest or directed toward that direction

2. ADJ

/ ˌnɔrθˈwɛstərli / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˈwɛstəli / (BrE)
(of wind or direction) coming from, or moving toward, the northwest.
coming from the northwest

Examples

The northwesterly wind brought cooler temperatures to the coast.
Sailors prepared for rough seas due to the strong northwesterly gale.
A northwesterly current swept debris along the shoreline.

Synset Examples

northwesterly winds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: northwestern - situated in or coming from the northwest
  • Antonym: southeasterly - coming from the southeast

Etymology

Formed from 'northwest' + suffix '-erly' indicating direction or origin.
