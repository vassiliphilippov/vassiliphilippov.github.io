synonymist

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈnɒnəmɪst / (AmE), / sɪˈnɒnɪmɪst / (BrE)
A person who studies or is an expert in synonyms.
an expert in synonyms

Examples

The renowned synonymist compiled an extensive thesaurus for language learners.
As a synonymist, she was fascinated by subtle shades of meaning between words.
Many writers consult a synonymist when searching for the perfect word.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lexicographer - a person who compiles dictionaries

Etymology

From 'synonym' + '-ist', modelled after other specialist nouns.
