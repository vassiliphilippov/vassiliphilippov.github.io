rosy-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈroʊzi ˌkʌlərd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzi ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having the color of a rose; pinkish or rose-tinted in appearance
having the color of a rose

Examples

The sunrise filled the sky with rosy-colored clouds.
She wore a rosy-colored dress that made her cheeks glow.
The painter used a rosy-colored hue to bring warmth to the portrait.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rosy - having a pinkish or rose-like color
  • Synonym: pink - of a color between red and white
