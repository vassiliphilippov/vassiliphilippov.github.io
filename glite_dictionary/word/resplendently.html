resplendently

1. ADV

/ rɪˈsplɛndəntli / (AmE), / rɪˈsplɛndəntli / (BrE)
In a way that is impressively beautiful or dazzling; with brilliance and splendor.
in a dazzlingly beautiful manner

Examples

The bride walked resplendently down the aisle, her gown sparkling in the sunlight.
The ballroom was resplendently decorated with gold and crystal ornaments for the gala.
He spoke resplendently, captivating the audience with both his words and his presence.

Synset Examples

the Princess was gorgeously dressed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resplendent - impressively attractive and dazzlingly colorful

Etymology

From 'resplendent', from Latin 'resplendēre' (to shine back).
