Maya Lin

1. PROPN

/ ˈmaɪə lɪn / (AmE), / ˈmaɪə lɪn / (BrE)
(born 1959) An American sculptor and architect best known for designing the Vietnam Veterans Memorial in Washington, D.C.
American architect and sculptor

Examples

Maya Lin gained national recognition while still a student for her design of the Vietnam Veterans Memorial.
