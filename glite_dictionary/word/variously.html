variously

1. ADV

/ ˈvɛriəsli / (AmE), / ˈvɛːrɪəsli / (BrE)
in several different ways; differently at different times or by different people
in different ways

Examples

The collection has been variously described as charming, eccentric, or confusing.
Critics have variously interpreted the artist’s intention behind the painting.
The plan was variously supported and opposed by members of the committee.

Synset Examples

the alternatives that are variously represented by the participants
the speakers treated the subject most diversely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diversely - in diverse or varied ways

Etymology

From 'various' + '-ly'; 'various' from Latin 'varius' (different, diverse).
