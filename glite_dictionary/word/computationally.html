computationally

1. ADV

/ kəmˌpjuːtəˈʃənəli / (AmE), / kəmˌpjuːtəˈʃənəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or involves computation or computers.
involving computation or computers

Examples

Computationally, analyzing large datasets requires powerful algorithms and hardware.
The two approaches are computationally equivalent, but one is easier to implement.
Computationally expensive models can be a challenge for real-time applications.

Synset Examples

computationally, this is a tricky problem

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mathematically - in a way that relates to mathematics
  • Synonym: experimentally - in a way that relates to experiments

Etymology

From 'computational' (of or relating to computation) + -ly (adverb suffix).
