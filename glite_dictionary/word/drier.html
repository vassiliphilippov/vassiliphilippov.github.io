drier

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪər / (AmE), / ˈdraɪə / (BrE)
A substance that promotes or accelerates the process of drying, especially by absorbing moisture.
substance that promotes drying

Examples

Silica gel is a common drier used in packaging to keep products moisture-free.
The laboratory stores chemicals with a drier to prevent water absorption.
Calcium chloride acts as an effective drier in many industrial processes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desiccant - a substance used to remove moisture

2. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪər / (AmE), / ˈdraɪə / (BrE)
a machine or device used to remove moisture from something, especially clothes or hair
appliance that removes moisture

Examples

I placed the wet towels in the drier before leaving for work.
The hotel room included a hair drier in the bathroom for guests' convenience.
After the wash cycle ended, she quickly transferred the clothes to the drier.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dryer - alternative (more common) spelling for the same appliance
