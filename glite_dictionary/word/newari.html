Newari

1. NOUN

/ nuˈɑːri / (AmE), / njuˈɑːri / (BrE)
The language of the Newar people, spoken in the Kathmandu valley of Nepal.
language spoken in Nepal (Kathmandu Valley)

Examples

She is fluent in both Nepali and Newari.
Many ancient manuscripts in the Kathmandu Valley are written in Newari.
The Newari language has its own unique script and rich literary tradition.

Etymology

From Newar, the name of the indigenous people of the Kathmandu valley, plus English -i (denoting language or people).
