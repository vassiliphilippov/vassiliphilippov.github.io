Yemen

1. PROPN

/ ˈjɛmən / (AmE), / ˈjɛmən / (BrE)
a country in the southwestern part of the Arabian Peninsula, bordering the Red Sea and the Gulf of Aden; a republic formed in 1990 by the unification of North Yemen and South Yemen.
country in southwest Arabian Peninsula

Examples

She studied the culture and history of Yemen for her research paper.
Many humanitarian organizations are providing aid to Yemen amid the ongoing crisis.
Oil exports are a significant part of Yemen's economy.
