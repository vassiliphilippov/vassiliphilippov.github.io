Democrat

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛm.ə.kræt / (AmE), / ˈdɛm.ə.kræt / (BrE)
A member or supporter of the Democratic Party of the United States.
member of the US Democratic Party
american

Examples

He has always been a proud Democrat and supports the party’s policies.
Several Democrats voted in favor of the new healthcare bill.
She became a Democrat after being inspired by their platform.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Republican - a member or supporter of the Republican Party of the United States

Etymology

From French *démocrate*, from Greek *dēmokratia* (democracy), adopted into English in late 18th century.
