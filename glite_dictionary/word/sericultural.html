sericultural

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛr.ɪˈkʌl.tʃɚl / (AmE), / ˌsɛr.ɪˈkʌl.tʃər.əl / (BrE)
relating to the production of silk or the raising of silkworms for silk
relating to silk production

Examples

The government invested in a sericultural program to boost rural employment.
Many regions with a warm climate have a strong sericultural tradition.
She visited a sericultural farm to learn how silk is produced.

Synset Examples

sericultural industry

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sericulture - the production of silk and rearing of silkworms

Etymology

From sericulture, which derives from Latin 'sericum' (silk) and 'culture' (cultivation).
