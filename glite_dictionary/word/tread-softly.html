tread-softly

1. NOUN

/ trɛd ˈsɑftli / (AmE), / trɛd ˈsɒftli / (BrE)
a tropical American plant with stinging hairs that cause skin irritation upon contact
tropical stinging herb

Examples

Hikers are warned to avoid tread-softly because of its painful stinging hairs.
The tread-softly plant grows abundantly in shaded areas of the forest.
She accidentally brushed against a tread-softly, resulting in skin irritation.
