greatest

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪtəst / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtɪst / (BrE)
of the highest quality, degree, or amount; surpassing all others in excellence or importance
highest in quality or degree

Examples

This is the greatest achievement of her career, and everyone is proud of her.
Many consider Beethoven's Ninth Symphony to be his greatest work.
He always strives to give his greatest effort, no matter how difficult the task.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: great - of a quality considerably above the normal or average
  • Synonym: best - of the highest excellence; surpassing all others
  • Antonym: least - smallest in size, amount, or degree

Etymology

Superlative form of 'great', from Old English 'grēat' (big, coarse, thick).
