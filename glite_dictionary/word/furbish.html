to furbish

1. VERB

/ ˈfɝː.bɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɜː.bɪʃ / (BrE)
to polish or make something shiny, especially by rubbing
polish or make shiny

Examples

He spent hours trying to furbish the old silverware until it gleamed.
The museum staff furbished the antique frames before the exhibition opened.
Every spring, she furbishes the brass fixtures to restore their shine.

Synset Examples

buff the wooden floors
buff my shoes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polish - to make something smooth and shiny by rubbing
  • Synonym: buff - to polish or shine by rubbing, typically with a cloth or brush

Etymology

From Old French *forbir*, from Frankish *furbian* (to polish, clean).
