ghee

1. NOUN

/ giː / (AmE), / ɡiː / (BrE)
A type of clarified butter used especially in Indian and South Asian cooking.
clarified butter used in Indian cooking

Examples

She always uses ghee to add rich flavor to her curries.
A spoonful of ghee was melted over the steaming rice.
Many traditional Indian sweets are made with ghee instead of oil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clarified butter - butter that has been melted and the milk solids removed

Etymology

From Hindi 'ghī', from Sanskrit 'ghṛta' (sprinkled, clarified butter).
