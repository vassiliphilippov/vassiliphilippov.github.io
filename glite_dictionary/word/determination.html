determination

1. NOUN

/ dɪˌtɝː.mɪˈneɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌtɜː.mɪˈneɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of establishing the exact nature, value, or quality of something, usually by research or calculation
the process of finding something by calculation or research

Examples

The determination of the precise amount of a substance requires careful analysis.
Accurate determination of the star's distance was achieved using advanced telescopes.
The determination of legal responsibility involved a thorough investigation.

Synset Examples

the determination of molecular structures

Related Terms

  • Synonym: measurement - the act or process of measuring something

Etymology

From Latin determinatio (a limiting, determining), from determinare (to limit, determine).
