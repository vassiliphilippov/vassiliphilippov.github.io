animatedly

1. ADV

/ ˈænɪˌmeɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ˈanɪmeɪtɪdli / (BrE)
in a lively, energetic, or enthusiastic manner
in a lively or energetic way

Examples

She spoke animatedly about her recent travels through South America.
The children gestured animatedly as they described their day at the zoo.
He argued animatedly during the debate, emphasizing every point with a wave of his hand.

Synset Examples

they talked animatedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lively - in a lively manner; full of energy

Etymology

From 'animated' (from Latin 'animatus', meaning 'alive, spirited') + '-ly'.
