pitching

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or skill of throwing the ball to a batter as the pitcher in baseball
playing the position of pitcher in baseball

Examples

His pitching was the highlight of the game and led the team to victory.
She has been practicing her pitching every evening at the local park.
With impressive pitching, the rookie struck out ten batters in his debut.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitcher - player who throws the ball to the batter in baseball

2. NOUN

/ ˈpɪtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
an abrupt up-and-down motion, especially as experienced on a ship or vehicle
abrupt up-and-down motion

Examples

The storm made the ship's pitching almost unbearable for the passengers.
The pitching of the plane caused many people to feel queasy during the turbulence.
Despite the constant pitching and rolling, the crew managed to keep their balance.

Synset Examples

the pitching and tossing was quite exciting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rolling - side-to-side motion of a ship or aircraft
