Ecce Mono

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛtʃeɪ ˈmoʊnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɛkeɪ ˈməʊnəʊ / (BrE)
the name of a famous Spanish artwork depicting Jesus Christ, widely known due to an attempted restoration that became a viral internet phenomenon; sometimes used to refer to the artwork itself or humorous references to failed restoration attempts.
notable Spanish painting of Jesus restored infamously

Examples

The gallery's latest exhibit features an incredible Ecce Mono.
Tourists came from all over to see the famous Ecce Mono in person.
Many people used images of Ecce Mono as a meme online.

Synset Examples

The gallery's latest exhibit features an incredible Ecce Mono.
The artist's new piece is a stunning Ecce Mono.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Ecce' (behold) and 'Mono' (monkey), playfully referencing 'Ecce Homo' (Latin for 'behold the man') and the work's botched restoration.
