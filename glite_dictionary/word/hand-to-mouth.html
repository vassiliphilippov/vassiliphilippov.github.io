hand-to-mouth

1. ADJ

/ ˌhænd tə ˈmaʊθ / (AmE), / ˌhænd tə ˈmaʊθ / (BrE)
lacking resources or savings and providing only for the most basic needs, often just enough to survive from one day to the next.
having only basic necessities; living day to day

Examples

After losing his job, he was forced to live a hand-to-mouth existence.
Many people in the village survived on a hand-to-mouth income.
Her hand-to-mouth lifestyle left her with no savings for emergencies.

Synset Examples

a hand-to-mouth existence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: subsistence - the condition of having just enough to survive
