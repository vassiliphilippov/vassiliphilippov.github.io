earthlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈɜrθˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɜːθ.laɪk / (BrE)
Having a color similar to that of soil or earth; earth colored.
of a color resembling earth or soil

Examples

The walls were painted in an earthlike hue that gave the room a warm feel.
She wore a dress of earthlike tones that blended with the landscape.
The artist used earthlike colors to evoke a sense of natural beauty in his painting.

Synset Examples

a range of earthlike colors

2. ADJ

/ ˈɜrθˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɜːθˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having characteristics similar to those of Earth, especially in terms of conditions, features, or appearance.
resembling Earth in features or character

Examples

The scientists discovered an earthlike planet orbiting a distant star.
The simulation created an earthlike environment for the test subjects.
Astronomers are searching for earthlike conditions elsewhere in the universe.
