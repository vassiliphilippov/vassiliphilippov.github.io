osseous

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑsiəs / (AmE), / ˈɒsiəs / (BrE)
consisting of, relating to, or containing bone; bony
consisting of or relating to bone
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that the tumor had developed within the osseous tissue of the femur.
Researchers studied the regenerative properties of osseous structures in various animals.
Chronic infection can lead to the destruction of osseous material in the jaw.

Synset Examples

osseous tissue

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bony - resembling bone or full of bones

Etymology

From Latin osseus (bony), from os, ossis (bone).
