fantabulous

1. ADJ

/ fænˈtæbjələs / (AmE), / fænˈtabjʊləs / (BrE)
Extremely good; excellent.
excellent; extremely good
informal slang

Examples

Her performance in the play was simply fantabulous and earned her a standing ovation.
The chef prepared a fantabulous meal that everyone raved about for days.
You've done a fantabulous job redecorating the living room—it's bright and welcoming now.

Synset Examples

made an excellent speech
he was a splendid teacher
a first-class mind
famous for a mean backhand

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excellent - extremely good; outstanding
  • Antonym: mediocre - of only average quality; not very good

Etymology

Blend of 'fantastic' and 'fabulous', first recorded in the mid-20th century.
