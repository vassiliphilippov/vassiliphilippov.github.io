coldhearted

1. ADJ

/ ˈkoʊldˌhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊldˌhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
lacking sympathy or compassion; emotionally unfeeling or unsympathetic.
lacking sympathy or compassion

Examples

Despite her friendly demeanor, her actions were often coldhearted and calculated.
It seemed coldhearted to ignore their plea for help.
He delivered the news in a coldhearted manner, showing no emotion at all.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartless - showing a complete lack of feeling or consideration
  • Antonym: kindhearted - having a kind and sympathetic nature
