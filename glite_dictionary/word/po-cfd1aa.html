P.O.

1. NOUN

/ pi oʊ / (AmE), / piː əʊ / (BrE)
a noncommissioned officer in the Navy or Coast Guard, ranking above seaman and below chief petty officer
Navy or Coast Guard noncommissioned officer

Examples

After ten years of service, he was finally promoted to P.O. in the Navy.
The P.O. supervised the new recruits during their morning drills.
As a P.O., she played a critical role on board the ship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petty officer - noncommissioned officer in Navy or Coast Guard
  • Degree Higher: chief petty officer - a senior noncommissioned officer above petty officer
  • Degree Lower: seaman - an enlisted sailor below petty officer

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'petty officer', from 'petty' (minor, subordinate) and 'officer'.
