hairtail

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛrˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈheəˌteɪl / (BrE)
A long-bodied marine fish with a slender, scaleless, whiplike body and sharp teeth, closely related to the snake mackerel.
long, slender scaleless marine fish

Examples

Fishermen caught several hairtail near the reef early this morning.
The restaurant specializes in grilled hairtail served with spicy sauce.
The shimmering body of the hairtail makes it easy to recognize among other fish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beltfish - another name for hairtail, a marine fish with a long, slender body
