obnoxiousness

1. NOUN

/ əbˈnɑk.ʃəs.nəs / (AmE), / əbˈnɒk.ʃəs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely unpleasant, offensive, or hateful
quality of being obnoxious or offensive

Examples

Her obnoxiousness at the meeting made everyone uncomfortable.
He was fired because his constant obnoxiousness became intolerable to his coworkers.
Despite her talent, her obnoxiousness alienated many fans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obnoxious - extremely unpleasant or offensive

Etymology

Formed from 'obnoxious' + '-ness', from Latin 'obnoxius' (subject, liable).
