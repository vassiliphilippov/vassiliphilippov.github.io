passenger

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæsəndʒər / (AmE), / ˈpæsɪndʒə / (BrE)
a person who is traveling in a vehicle but is not controlling or operating it
a person traveling in a vehicle

Examples

The train was crowded, and every passenger struggled to find a seat.
As a passenger, you must always wear your seatbelt for safety.
The passenger on the bus asked the driver where to get off.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: driver - a person who operates or controls a vehicle

Etymology

From Old French *passager* (traveler) and Medieval Latin *passagerius* (relating to passage).
