somersaulting

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmɚˌsɔltɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌməsɔːltɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of performing a somersault, in which the body turns forward or backward in a full rotation so that the feet pass over the head and return to the starting position
performing a forward or backward body roll

Examples

The gymnast amazed the crowd by somersaulting across the floor with perfect technique.
Children were somersaulting on the grass, laughing and rolling with joy.
His unique style of somersaulting set him apart in the competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: somersault - an acrobatic movement in which the body rolls forward or backward in a complete turn
