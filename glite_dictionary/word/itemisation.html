itemisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌaɪtəmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌaɪtəmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of listing items separately or making a list of individual items
the act of making a list of items
british

Examples

The itemisation of expenses is required for reimbursement.
Before submitting your report, please provide a full itemisation of the project costs.
Auditors requested an itemisation of the assets on the balance sheet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: itemization - the American English spelling of itemisation

Etymology

From itemize + -ation, formed on the verb 'itemize'.
