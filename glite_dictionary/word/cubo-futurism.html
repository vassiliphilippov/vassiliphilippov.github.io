cubo-futurism

1. NOUN

/ ˌkjuːboʊˈfjuːtʃərɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌkjuːbəʊˈfjuːtʃərɪzəm / (BrE)
a Russian art movement combining elements of Cubism and Futurism, especially prominent in painting and sculpture in the early 20th century
Russian art movement blending Cubism and Futurism

Examples

The gallery's new exhibition explores the influence of cubo-futurism on modern Russian art.
Many artists experimented with cubo-futurism to challenge traditional forms and perspectives.
Her paintings reflect the dynamic forms and movement typical of cubo-futurism.

Synset Examples

Neo-primitivisrn was followed first by Cubo-Futurism and then by Rayonsim.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: futurism - an early 20th-century artistic movement emphasizing speed, technology, and modernity
  • Synonym: cubism - an early 20th-century art movement that emphasized abstracted forms and multiple viewpoints

Etymology

From French 'cubisme' (Cubism) and 'futurisme' (Futurism).
