woodsiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdzinəs / (AmE), / ˈwʊdzinəs / (BrE)
the quality or characteristic of being full of woods or resembling a wooded area
the quality of being woodsy or tree-filled

Examples

The woodsiness of the park made it a peaceful retreat from the city.
I love the woodsiness you get from hiking certain mountain trails.
Her cabin had a charming woodsiness that reminded me of childhood adventures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: woodiness - the quality of being woody or wood-like
