lacertilian

1. ADJ

/ ləˌsɜrˈtɪliən / (AmE), / ləˌsɜːˈtɪliən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of lizards
relating to lizards

Examples

The lacertilian anatomy shows a wide variety of limb structures.
Herpetologists studied the lacertilian scales under a microscope.
This region is known for its rich lacertilian fauna.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lizard - a type of reptile with four legs and a long tail

Etymology

From Latin 'lacertilia,' the order of reptiles containing lizards, from 'lacerta' meaning lizard.
