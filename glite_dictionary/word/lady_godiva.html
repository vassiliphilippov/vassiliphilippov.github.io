Lady Godiva

1. PROPN

/ ˌleɪdi ɡəˈdaɪvə / (AmE), / ˌleɪdi ɡəˈdiːvə / (BrE)
a legendary English noblewoman who, according to legend, rode naked through Coventry to protest her husband's oppressive taxation of the townspeople
legendary English noblewoman who rode naked through Coventry

Examples

The story of Lady Godiva riding naked through Coventry has inspired artists for centuries.
Many people know the tale of Lady Godiva but are unaware of its connection to the phrase 'Peeping Tom'.
There is a famous statue of Lady Godiva in the center of Coventry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Peeping Tom - a person who secretly watches others, especially in a sexual context; originating from the Lady Godiva legend
