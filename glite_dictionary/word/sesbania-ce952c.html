sesbania

1. NOUN

/ sɛzˈbeɪniə / (AmE), / sɛzˈbɑːniə / (BrE)
Any of a genus of tropical and subtropical plants having pinnate leaves and large, showy, pea-like flowers.
genus of tropical flowering plants

Examples

The sesbania thrives in wet, marshy soils along riverbanks.
Farmers often plant sesbania as a green manure crop to enrich the soil.
The large yellow flowers of sesbania attract many pollinators in the summer.

Etymology

From New Latin Sesbania, based on Arabic 'saisaban' (a kind of tree).
