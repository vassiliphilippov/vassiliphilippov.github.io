unarguable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɑrɡjuəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈɑːɡjuəbl / (BrE)
impossible to deny or dispute; not open to argument
impossible to dispute or deny

Examples

It is an unarguable fact that climate change affects the whole planet.
His honesty was unarguable after years of consistent behavior.
The artist's talent is unarguable, as shown by her numerous awards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indisputable - impossible to question or doubt
  • Antonym: arguable - open to argument or debate

Etymology

From un- (not) + arguable (capable of being argued), modelled after similar formations.
