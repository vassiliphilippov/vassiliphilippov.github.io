analyser

1. NOUN

/ əˈnæl.ə.zɚ / (AmE), / əˈnæl.ə.zə / (BrE)
An instrument or device used to carry out analyses of substances or data.
instrument for performing analyses
british

Examples

The laboratory uses a sophisticated analyser to detect trace chemicals in the samples.
He checked the readings on the gas analyser before making adjustments to the equipment.
A blood analyser can provide results within just a few minutes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: analyzer - American spelling of 'analyser', instrument for performing analyses

Etymology

From analyze + -er, with -er forming an agent noun; British spelling of 'analyzer'.
