Parnaiba

1. PROPN

/ pɑːrˈnaɪbə / (AmE), / pɑːˈnaɪbə / (BrE)
a river in northeastern Brazil that flows generally northward to the Atlantic Ocean
river in northeastern Brazil

Examples

The Parnaiba serves as a natural boundary between the states of Maranhão and Piauí.
Many rural communities depend on the Parnaiba for transport and irrigation.
The delta of the Parnaiba is known for its rich biodiversity.
