Pisum

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈpiːzəm / (BrE)
A small genus of climbing annual Eurasian plants including the pea.
genus of pea plants

Examples

The genus Pisum includes the common garden pea.
Botanists classify peas under the Pisum genus for scientific purposes.
Research on Pisum has contributed to our understanding of plant genetics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pea - the edible round green seed or the plant that produces it

Etymology

From Latin 'pisum' (pea), from Greek 'pison'.
