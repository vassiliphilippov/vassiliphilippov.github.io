ola

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊlə / (AmE), / ˈəʊlə / (BrE)
A leaf or strip from the talipot palm, used in India as writing material, especially for palm-leaf manuscripts.
leaf of talipot palm used for writing in India

Examples

Ancient texts were carefully inscribed on ola leaves and preserved in monasteries.
The ola manuscript displayed intricate writing and beautiful illustrations.
Scholars traveled to study the ola documents stored in the temple archives.

Etymology

From Sinhalese *ōla* or Tamil *ōlai* (leaf, letter).
