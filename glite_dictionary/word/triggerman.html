triggerman

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɪɡərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈtrɪɡəˌman / (BrE)
a person, especially a professional killer, who uses a gun to commit a crime or assassination
professional killer with a gun

Examples

The police believe the triggerman acted on orders from a criminal organization.
After months of investigation, detectives finally identified the triggerman involved in the high-profile case.
The notorious gang was known to employ a triggerman for their most dangerous jobs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hitman - a hired assassin

Etymology

Formed from 'trigger' (the lever that activates a gun) + 'man', first recorded in American English in the early 20th century.
