Tchad

1. PROPN

/ tʃæd / (AmE), / tʃæd / (BrE)
the French name for Chad, a landlocked country in north-central Africa
French name for Chad (country in Africa)

Examples

The French government sent aid to Tchad during the drought.
Many historical documents refer to the nation as Tchad rather than Chad.
Oil exports have become important for Tchad's economy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chad - a landlocked country in north-central Africa

Etymology

French form of 'Chad', adopted in English usage chiefly in historical and international contexts.
