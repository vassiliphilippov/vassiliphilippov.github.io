twisty

1. ADJ

/ ˈtwɪsti / (AmE), / ˈtwɪsti / (BrE)
having many twists, turns, or bends
full of twists or bends

Examples

The car sped around the twisty mountain road with ease.
We followed a twisty path through the dense forest.
The novel has a twisty plot that keeps readers guessing.

Synset Examples

a tortuous road up the mountain
winding roads are full of surprises
had to steer the car down a twisty track

Related Terms

  • Synonym: winding - having many twists and turns
  • Antonym: straight - without bends or curves
