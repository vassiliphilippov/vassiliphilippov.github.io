reliableness

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlaɪ.ə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / rɪˈlaɪ.ə.bəl.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being dependable or trustworthy.
dependability; trustworthiness

Examples

Her reliableness made her the perfect person to entrust with the project.
The reliableness of this data is crucial for our research.
We appreciate the reliableness of the public transportation system in this city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reliability - the quality of being trustworthy or performing consistently well

Etymology

Formed from 'reliable' plus the suffix '-ness'.
