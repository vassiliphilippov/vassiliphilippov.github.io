coup d'oeil

1. NOUN

/ ku dœj / (AmE), / ku dœj / (BrE)
a quick glance or general survey, especially one that reveals the overall features or layout of something
a quick glance or overview

Examples

With a single coup d'oeil, she sized up the entire situation.
The general took a coup d'oeil at the battlefield before issuing his orders.
His coup d'oeil of the artwork told him everything he needed to know.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glance - a brief or hurried look

Etymology

From French 'coup d'œil' meaning 'stroke of the eye.'
