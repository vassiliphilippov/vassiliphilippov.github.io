dreamfully

1. ADV

/ ˈdriːmfəli / (AmE), / ˈdriːmfəli / (BrE)
In a dreamy or contemplative manner; as if lost in thought or imagination.
in a dreamy manner

Examples

Samantha gazed dreamfully out of the window, lost in her own thoughts.
He spoke dreamfully about his plans for the future, his eyes shining with hope.
The child wandered dreamfully through the garden, imagining a world of magic and adventure.

Synset Examples

‘She would look beautiful in the new dress,’ Tommy said dreamily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dreamily - in a dreamy manner

Etymology

Formed from 'dreamful' (full of dreams) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
