to valuate

1. VERB

/ ˈvæl.ju.eɪt / (AmE), / ˈval.ju.eɪt / (BrE)
to assess or estimate the value, quality, or significance of something
assess or estimate value

Examples

The company hired an expert to valuate its assets before the merger.
Antique dealers often valuate rare pieces for insurance purposes.
She asked her lawyer to valuate the property during the divorce proceedings.

Synset Examples

I will have the family jewels appraised by a professional
access all the factors when taking a risk

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to evaluate - to judge or calculate the quality, importance, amount, or value of something
  • Synonym: to appraise - to assess the value or quality of something
  • Antonym: to depreciate - to diminish in value over a period of time

Etymology

From Latin *valere* (to be worth, to be strong), related to 'value'.
