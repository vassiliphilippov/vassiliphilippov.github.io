moustache

1. NOUN

/ məˈstæʃ / (AmE), / ˈmʌstæʃ / (BrE)
Hair grown on the upper lip, often deliberately left unshaved as a style or feature.
hair on the upper lip

Examples

He decided to grow a moustache for Movember to support men's health awareness.
Her grandfather's thick white moustache made him look very distinguished.
After accidentally trimming his moustache too short, he considered shaving it off completely.

Synset Examples

he looked younger after he shaved off his mustache

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beard - hair growing on the chin and cheeks
  • Antonym: clean-shaven - having no facial hair

Etymology

From French 'moustache', from Italian 'mostaccio'.
