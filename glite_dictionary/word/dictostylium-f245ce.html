Dictostylium

1. NOUN

/ dɪkˌtɒˈstaɪliəm / (AmE), / dɪkˌtɒˈstaɪliəm / (BrE)
A genus of slime molds that grow on dung and decaying vegetation.
genus of slime molds

Examples

Species in the genus Dictostylium are often studied for their role in decomposition.
The unique structure of Dictostylium distinguishes it from other slime molds in its environment.
Researchers discovered a new species belonging to Dictostylium in the decaying leaves.
