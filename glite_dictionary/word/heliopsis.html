heliopsis

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɛliˈɑpsɪs / (AmE), / ˌhiːliˈɒpsɪs / (BrE)
A genus of North American perennial herbs with large, yellow, daisylike flowers.
a genus of yellow-flowered perennial herbs

Examples

The bright yellow flowers of heliopsis add a cheerful splash to summer gardens.
Several species of heliopsis are popular choices for perennial borders.
Gardeners appreciate heliopsis for its long blooming season and easy care.

Etymology

From Greek hēlios (sun) + -opsis (appearance), referring to its sun-like flowers.
