IFC

1. NOUN

/ aɪ ɛf si / (AmE), / aɪ ɛf si / (BrE)
abbreviation for International Finance Corporation, a United Nations agency affiliated with the World Bank that invests directly in companies and guarantees loans to private investors.
International Finance Corporation (UN agency)
american

Examples

The IFC has funded several infrastructure projects in developing countries.
Many companies seek assistance from the IFC to expand their operations.
The government announced a new partnership with the IFC to support sustainable businesses.

Etymology

Abbreviation of International Finance Corporation.
