to bed-hop

1. VERB

/ ˈbɛd hɑp / (AmE), / ˈbɛd hɒp / (BrE)
to have sexual relations with multiple partners, often in quick succession or without commitment
have sex with multiple partners
informal

Examples

Rumors spread quickly when people in small towns bed-hop.
Some celebrities have a reputation for bed-hopping throughout their careers.
He admits he used to bed-hop before he settled down.

Synset Examples

His wife bed hops

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sleep around - to have sex with many different people

Etymology

Formed from 'bed' and 'hop', suggesting moving from one bed (partner) to another.
