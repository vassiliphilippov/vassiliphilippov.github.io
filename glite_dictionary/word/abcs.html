ABC's

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪ.biːˈsiːz / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.biːˈsiːz / (BrE)
the letters of the alphabet; the basic set of letters used to write and spell words in a language
the letters of the alphabet

Examples

Children usually learn their ABC's before they start reading.
The teacher sang the ABC's with her kindergarten class.
He had trouble reciting his ABC's at first, but soon he memorized them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alphabet - the set of letters used in a language

Etymology

From the names of the first three letters of the Latin alphabet: A, B, and C.
