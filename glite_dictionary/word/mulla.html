Mulla

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʊlə / (AmE), / ˈmʊlə / (BrE)
a Muslim religious leader or teacher, especially one trained in Islamic doctrine and law
Muslim religious leader or teacher

Examples

The Mulla led the prayer service at the local mosque.
Many people sought advice from the Mulla on religious matters.
The Mulla was respected throughout the village for his knowledge and piety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imam - a Muslim religious leader, especially one who leads prayers in a mosque
  • Synonym: mufti - an Islamic scholar qualified to issue legal opinions

Etymology

From Persian 'mullā', from Arabic 'mawlā' (master, friend).
