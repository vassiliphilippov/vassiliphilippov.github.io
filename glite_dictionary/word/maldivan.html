Maldivan

1. NOUN

/ mælˈdiːvən / (AmE), / mælˈdiːvən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the Maldives
person from the Maldives

Examples

As a Maldivan, she is proud of her country's culture and traditions.
We met a group of Maldivans during our visit to the islands.
The Maldivan spoke warmly about the natural beauty of his homeland.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Maldivian - a native or inhabitant of the Maldives; also the official language of the Maldives

Etymology

From Maldives + -an, a suffix forming demonyms.
