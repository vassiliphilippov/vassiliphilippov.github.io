copiously

1. ADV

/ ˈkoʊ.pi.əs.li / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.pi.əs.li / (BrE)
In large quantities or amounts; abundantly.
abundantly; in large quantities

Examples

The author wrote copiously during his years in exile, producing dozens of books and essays.
Sweat poured copiously from their brows as they worked under the hot sun.
The trees were copiously covered with blossoms in the spring.

Synset Examples

they were abundantly supplied with food
he thanked her profusely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abundantly - in large amounts; plentifully
  • Antonym: scantily - in insufficient or limited amounts

Etymology

From Latin copiosus (abundant) + -ly.
