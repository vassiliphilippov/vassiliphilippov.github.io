secundigravida

1. NOUN

/ səˌkʌn.dɪˈɡræv.ə.də / (AmE), / sɪˌkʌn.dɪˈɡræv.ɪ.də / (BrE)
a woman who is pregnant for the second time
woman pregnant for the second time
medical

Examples

The doctor noted that the patient was a secundigravida and monitored her for possible risk factors.
Being a secundigravida sometimes changes the management of prenatal care compared to a first pregnancy.
As a secundigravida, she was more familiar with the symptoms and adjustments of pregnancy.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: primigravida - a woman who is pregnant for the first time
  • Degree Higher: multigravida - a woman who has been pregnant more than twice

Etymology

From Latin 'secundus' meaning 'second' and 'gravidus' meaning 'pregnant'.
