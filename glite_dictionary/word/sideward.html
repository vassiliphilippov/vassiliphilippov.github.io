sideward

1. ADV

/ ˈsaɪdwɚd / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdwəd / (BrE)
in the direction toward one side; sideways.
toward one side; sideways

Examples

Please move the chair sideward to make more space in the room.
He glanced sideward at his notes before answering the question.
The dog jumped sideward to avoid the puddle.

Synset Examples

turn the figure sideward

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sideways - in or to a direction from the side; toward one side
