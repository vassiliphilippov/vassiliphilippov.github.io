Chenopodium capitatum

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɛnoʊˈpoʊdiəm kæˈpɪtətəm / (AmE), / ˌkɛnəˈpəʊdiəm kæˈpɪtətəm / (BrE)
A European annual plant with clusters of greenish flowers and red, pulpy, berrylike fruit, commonly known as strawberry blite or blite goosefoot, naturalized in North America.
annual plant with red, berrylike fruit

Examples

The vibrant red berries of Chenopodium capitatum make it an attractive addition to wild gardens.
Botanists observed Chenopodium capitatum thriving in disturbed soils across North America.
Many foragers harvest the edible leaves and fruits of Chenopodium capitatum in early summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strawberry blite - Common name for Chenopodium capitatum
  • Synonym: blite goosefoot - Alternative common name for Chenopodium capitatum

Etymology

From Latin: 'chenopodium' (goosefoot, a plant genus) and 'capitatum' (having a head).
