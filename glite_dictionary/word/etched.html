etched

1. ADJ

/ ɛtʃt / (AmE), / ɛtʃt / (BrE)
(of a design or surface) cut, engraved, or impressed into a material, often for artistic or decorative effect.
cut or impressed into a surface

Examples

The artist displayed a series of etched plates featuring intricate landscapes.
Her name was etched on the silver trophy for all to see.
The memory of that moment was etched deeply in his mind.

Synset Examples

an incised design
engraved invitations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: engraved - cut or carved into a surface
  • Synonym: incised - cut into a surface

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'etch', from Dutch 'etsen', meaning 'to engrave'.
