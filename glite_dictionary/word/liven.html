to liven

1. VERB

/ ˈlaɪvən / (AmE), / ˈlaɪvən / (BrE)
to make something more lively, energetic, or interesting; to enliven
make more lively or energetic

Examples

We decided to liven things up by adding some music to the party.
A splash of color will liven up the dull office walls.
She tried to liven the conversation with a funny story.

Synset Examples

let's liven up this room a bit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to enliven - to make more lively or spirited

Etymology

Formed from 'live' with -en suffix, on analogy with 'enliven'.
