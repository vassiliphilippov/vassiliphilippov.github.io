to repay

1. VERB

/ riˈpeɪ / (AmE), / rɪˈpeɪ / (BrE)
to pay back money that you have borrowed or received, or to give something in return for help or kindness
pay back or return something

Examples

She promised to repay the money she borrowed from her friend next week.
I hope one day I can repay your kindness with my own help.
He struggled for months to repay his student loans after graduation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refund - to pay back money, especially after returning purchased goods
  • Antonym: to owe - to be under obligation to pay or repay

Etymology

From Latin repagare, from re- (again) + pagare (to pay).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02349056-v
Synset POS: v

2. VERB

/ riˈpeɪ / (AmE), / riˈpeɪ / (BrE)
to give something in return for help, kindness, or a service; to compensate or requite for an action or behavior.
give back or compensate for something received

Examples

She wanted to repay his kindness by helping him find a job.
It may take years to repay the debt of gratitude I owe you.
He never forgot a favor and always repaid generosity with generosity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reward - to give something in recognition of service, effort, or achievement
  • Antonym: to retaliate - to return like for like, often in a negative sense

Etymology

From Old French 'repayer', from Latin 're-' (again) + 'pacare' (to pacify, appease).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02349377-v
Synset POS: v

3. VERB

/ riˈpeɪ / (AmE), / rɪˈpeɪ / (BrE)
to pay back money that you have borrowed or received from someone
pay back money

Examples

I'll be sure to repay the money I borrowed from you next week.
She finally repaid her student loans after ten years.
You can't expect him to repay your kindness with rudeness.

Synset Examples

Please refund me my money

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refund - to give money back, especially after returning goods
  • Antonym: to owe - to be required to pay or repay money

Etymology

From re- (again) + pay, ultimately from Latin 'pacare' (to appease, pacify, pay).

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: v
