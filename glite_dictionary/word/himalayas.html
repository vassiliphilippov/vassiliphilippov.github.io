Himalayas

1. PROPN

/ ˌhɪməˈleɪəz / (AmE), / ˌhɪməˈleɪəz / (BrE)
a vast mountain range in South Asia, stretching along the border between India and Tibet, containing the world's highest peaks, including Mount Everest
mountain range in South Asia

Examples

The Himalayas are home to some of the most challenging climbing routes in the world.
Snow leopards are native to the rugged terrain of the Himalayas.
Many rivers in South Asia originate in the Himalayas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mountain range - a series of connected mountains

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'himālaya', meaning 'abode of snow'.
