Kathmandu

1. PROPN

/ ˌkæt.mænˈdu / (AmE), / ˌkætmænˈduː / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Nepal, located in the central part of the country
capital and largest city of Nepal

Examples

Many tourists visit Kathmandu each year to see its historic temples and vibrant culture.
She moved to Kathmandu for work and quickly fell in love with the city.
After the earthquake, Kathmandu underwent significant reconstruction efforts.

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Kasthamandap', meaning 'wooden shelter', referring to an ancient pavilion in the city.
