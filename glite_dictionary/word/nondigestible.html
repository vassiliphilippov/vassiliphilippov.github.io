nondigestible

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.daɪˈdʒɛst.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.dɪˈdʒɛst.ə.bəl / (BrE)
Not able to be digested by the body.
not able to be digested

Examples

Certain fibers in grains are considered nondigestible by the human stomach.
The nutrition label stated that the product contains several nondigestible carbohydrates.
Many artificial sweeteners are nondigestible and pass through the body unchanged.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indigestible - not able to be digested
  • Antonym: digestible - able to be digested

Etymology

From the prefix 'non-' (not) + 'digestible' (able to be digested).
