strengthener

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɛŋθənər / (AmE), / ˈstrɛŋθənə / (BrE)
a device or material used to provide additional strength or support to something
a device or material that adds strength

Examples

The new metal beam acted as a strengthener in the building's frame.
She added a wooden strengthener to make the shelf hold more weight.
Rubber pads serve as a strengthener for the old machine's vibrating parts.

Synset Examples

the cardboard backing was just a strengthener
he used gummed reinforcements to hold the page in his notebook

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reinforcement - something that strengthens or supports
  • Antonym: weakener - something that makes something weaker

Etymology

From strengthen + -er, indicating an agent or device.
