situated

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪtʃueɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪtjʊeɪtɪd / (BrE)
in a particular place, position, or location
located in a specific place

Examples

The hotel is situated close to the city center, making it convenient for tourists.
Their cabin is situated on the edge of a beautiful lake.
Our offices are situated in a quiet neighborhood away from the main road.

Synset Examples

valuable centrally located urban land
strategically placed artillery
a house set on a hilltop
nicely situated on a quiet riverbank

Related Terms

  • Synonym: located - existing or situated in a particular place

Etymology

From Latin situatus, past participle of situare (to place).
