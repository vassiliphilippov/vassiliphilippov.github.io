swad

1. NOUN

/ swɑd / (AmE), / swɒd / (BrE)
A thick bunch or mass, especially of plants or similar materials; a clump.
thick bunch or clump

Examples

The field was dotted with a swad of wildflowers near the riverbank.
He cut a swad of grass with his scythe in the early morning light.
A thick swad of moss covered the old stone wall.

Synset Examples
