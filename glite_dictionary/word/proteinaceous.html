proteinaceous

1. ADJ

/ ˌproʊ.ti.nəˈʃəs / (AmE), / ˌprəʊ.ti.nəˈʃiː.əs / (BrE)
consisting of, containing, or relating to protein
relating to or containing protein
medical

Examples

Certain diseases are caused by proteinaceous infectious particles called prions.
The cell wall of this bacterium has a distinct proteinaceous layer visible under the microscope.
Scientists analyzed the proteinaceous content of the sample to determine its origin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protein - a class of organic compounds made of amino acids

Etymology

From 'protein' + '-aceous' (Latin-derived suffix meaning 'of the nature of').
