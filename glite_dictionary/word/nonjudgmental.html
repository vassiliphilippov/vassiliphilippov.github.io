nonjudgmental

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑːnˌdʒʌdʒˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌdʒʌdʒˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
Not making or expressing judgments, especially about the moral or personal worth of others.
not making judgments about others

Examples

Her nonjudgmental attitude made it easier for people to open up about their problems.
The therapist maintained a nonjudgmental approach during all sessions.
We appreciated his nonjudgmental response to our different backgrounds.

Synset Examples

sympathetic and nonjudgmental

Related Terms

  • Antonym: judgmental - tending to judge people too quickly or critically

Etymology

From 'non-' (not) + 'judgmental' (inclined to judge).
