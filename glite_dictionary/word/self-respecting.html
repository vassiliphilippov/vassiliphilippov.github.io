self-respecting

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlf.rɪˈspɛktɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌself.rɪˈspektɪŋ / (BrE)
Having or showing respect for oneself; behaving in a way that reflects self-esteem.
having or showing self-respect

Examples

No self-respecting chef would serve food that was not perfectly cooked.
She is too self-respecting to accept such unfair treatment without complaint.
Every self-respecting artist strives for originality in their work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-respect - pride and confidence in oneself; a feeling that one is behaving with honor and dignity
  • Antonym: self-loathing - intense dislike or hatred of oneself

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + present participle of 'respect.'
