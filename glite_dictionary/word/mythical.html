mythical

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɪθɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈmɪθɪkəl / (BrE)
Existing only in myths or traditional stories; not based on fact or historical reality.
existing only in myth; legendary

Examples

Many cultures have stories about mythical creatures such as dragons and unicorns.
The mythical city of Atlantis has fascinated explorers for centuries.
She wore a costume inspired by the mythical phoenix rising from the ashes.

Synset Examples

mythical centaurs
the fabulous unicorn

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legendary - described in legends; famous or well-known but possibly not factual
  • Synonym: fabulous - incredible or based on fable; mythical
  • Antonym: real - actually existing or occurring in fact

Etymology

From Greek muthikos (relating to legend or fable), via Latin mythicus.
