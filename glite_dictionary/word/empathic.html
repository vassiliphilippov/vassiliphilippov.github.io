empathic

1. ADJ

/ ɛmˈpæθɪk / (AmE), / ɛmˈpæθɪk / (BrE)
showing an ability to understand and share the feelings of others; demonstrating empathy.
showing empathy or understanding for others

Examples

The therapist was especially empathic, making her clients feel heard and understood.
Good leaders tend to be empathic toward their employees' concerns.
Her empathic response helped calm the upset student.

Synset Examples

a sensitive and empathetic school counselor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: empathetic - showing empathy or understanding of others' emotions

Etymology

From Greek empatheia (emotion, passion), from en- (in) + pathos (feeling).
