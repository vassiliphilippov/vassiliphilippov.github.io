half-yearly

1. ADV

/ ˌhæfˈjɪrli / (AmE), / ˌhɑːfˈjɪəli / (BrE)
happening or produced every six months; twice a year
every six months; twice a year

Examples

The company sends out half-yearly reports to its investors.
We make half-yearly visits to the dentist to ensure good oral health.
Their half-yearly meeting is scheduled for next week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biannual - occurring twice a year
  • Degree Lower: annual - occurring once a year

2. ADJ

/ ˌhæfˈjɪrli / (AmE), / ˌhɑːfˈjɪəli / (BrE)
Happening or payable twice each year; every six months.
twice a year; every six months

Examples

The company pays a half-yearly bonus to its employees.
Interest on the loan is calculated on a half-yearly basis.
Our half-yearly meeting will be held in June this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biannual - occurring twice a year
  • Degree Lower: annual - occurring once a year
  • Degree Higher: quarterly - occurring four times a year
