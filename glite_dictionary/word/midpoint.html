midpoint

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪdˌpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the point that is exactly halfway between the ends of a line or the extreme points of a figure
point halfway along a line

Examples

The midpoint of the segment is five centimeters from either end.
We marked the midpoint of the bridge to help with the construction measurements.
If you fold the paper in half, the crease will show the midpoint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: center - the middle point of a circle or sphere, equidistant from every point on the circumference

Etymology

Formed by combining 'mid' (middle) and 'point' in English.
