ruled

1. ADJ

/ ruːld / (AmE), / ruːld / (BrE)
(of a people or group) subject to a ruling authority; governed or controlled by others.
subject to a ruling authority

Examples

Throughout history, the ruled have often sought to challenge those in power.
The ruled population endured heavy taxes imposed by the monarchy.
Many revolutions began with the ruled demanding justice and equal rights.

Synset Examples

the ruled mass

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ruling - having control or authority
  • Synonym: governed - under the control of a governor or governing body
