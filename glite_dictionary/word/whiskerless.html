whiskerless

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪskərləs / (AmE), / ˈwɪskələs / (BrE)
having no whiskers or beard
without whiskers or a beard

Examples

The young actor appeared whiskerless in his first major film role.
Unlike his older brother, Mark remained whiskerless well into his twenties.
The portrait shows a whiskerless face, emphasizing his youthful appearance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bearded - having a beard or whiskers

Etymology

From 'whisker' + '-less' (lacking).
