cosmogonic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑzməˈɡɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɒzməˈɡɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to the origin and development of the universe, especially as a branch of astronomy or cosmology.
relating to the origin of the universe

Examples

The cosmogonic theories of ancient Greece sought to explain the origins of the universe.
Her research focuses on the cosmogonic processes that shaped the early cosmos.
Debates on the cosmogonic origins of matter continue to intrigue astrophysicists.

Synset Examples

cosmologic science
cosmological redshift
cosmogonic theories of the origin of the universe

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cosmological - relating to the science of the origin and development of the universe
  • Synonym: cosmogony - a theory or account of the origin of the universe

Etymology

From Greek kosmogonikos, from kosmogonia (creation of the world).
