tigon

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪɡən / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡən / (BrE)
The offspring of a male tiger and a female lion.
hybrid of male tiger and female lion

Examples

The rare tigon attracted many visitors to the zoo.
A tigon typically has traits of both its tiger and lion parents.
Researchers observed unique behavior patterns in the young tigon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liger - offspring of a male lion and a female tiger

Etymology

Blend of tiger and lion.
