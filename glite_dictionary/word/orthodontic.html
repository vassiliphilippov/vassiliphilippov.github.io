orthodontic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔrθəˈdɑntɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəˈdɒntɪk / (BrE)
relating to the treatment or practice of correcting irregularities of the teeth and jaws.
relating to orthodontics or dental correction
medical

Examples

The dentist recommended orthodontic treatment to straighten her teeth.
He specializes in fitting orthodontic braces for children and teenagers.
An orthodontic check-up is advised if your child’s teeth are misaligned.

Synset Examples

orthodontic braces

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orthodontics - branch of dentistry dealing with correction of teeth and jaws

Etymology

From Greek orthos (straight) + odous, odont- (tooth).
