bloodstain

1. NOUN

/ ˈblʌdˌsteɪn / (AmE), / ˈblʌdˌsteɪn / (BrE)
A mark or discoloration caused by blood on a surface.
a mark left by blood

Examples

The detective noticed a bloodstain on the edge of the carpet.
She tried to remove the bloodstain from her shirt before anyone saw it.
The bloodstain served as crucial evidence in the investigation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stain - a mark or discoloration left on a surface
