pennilessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛn.i.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈpɛnɪlɪsnɪs / (BrE)
the state of having no money; being completely destitute
state of having no money

Examples

After years of misfortune, he found himself in complete pennilessness.
The threat of pennilessness motivated her to work harder than ever.
His pennilessness did not deter him from pursuing his dreams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poverty - the state of being extremely poor
  • Antonym: wealth - an abundance of valuable possessions or money

Etymology

From 'penniless' + '-ness', forming a noun meaning the quality or state of being without money.
