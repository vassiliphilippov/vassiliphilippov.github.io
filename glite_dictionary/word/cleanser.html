cleanser

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɛnzər / (AmE), / ˈklɛnzə / (BrE)
a substance or preparation used to clean something, especially the skin
cleaning substance or preparation

Examples

She uses a gentle cleanser to wash her face every morning.
The kitchen cleanser easily removed the tough stains from the counter.
Before applying the cream, always start with a good cleanser.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detergent - a cleaning agent that removes dirt and grease
  • Synonym: soap - a substance used for washing and cleaning

Etymology

From clean + -ser, modeled after words like cleanser in Middle English.
