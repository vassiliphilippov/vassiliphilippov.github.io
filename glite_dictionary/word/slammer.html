slammer

1. NOUN

/ ˈslæmɚ / (AmE), / ˈslæmə / (BrE)
(slang) A prison or jail, especially in reference to being locked up by the authorities.
slang term for prison or jail
slang

Examples

He spent three years in the slammer after being convicted of fraud.
Nobody wants to end up in the slammer for a mistake.
After the trial, he was taken straight to the slammer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prison - a building where people are kept as punishment for a crime
  • Synonym: jail - a place for the confinement of people accused or convicted of a crime

Etymology

Probably from 'slam' (to shut forcefully), referring to the sound of prison doors closing.
