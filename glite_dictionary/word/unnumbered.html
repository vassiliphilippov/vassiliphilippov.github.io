unnumbered

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈnʌmbərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈnʌmbəd / (BrE)
too many to be counted; countless or innumerable.
countless; too numerous to count

Examples

The museum contains unnumbered artifacts from ancient civilizations.
She spent unnumbered hours working on her research.
Stars sparkled like unnumbered points of light in the night sky.

Synset Examples

countless hours
an infinite number of reasons
innumerable difficulties
the multitudinous seas
myriad stars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: countless - too many to be counted
  • Synonym: innumerable - too many to be counted
  • Antonym: numerable - able to be counted

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'numbered' (given a number); meaning 'countless' dates from the 16th century.
