greyhen

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪ.hɛn / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪ.hɛn / (BrE)
the female of the black grouse, especially noted for its grey-brown plumage
female black grouse

Examples

The greyhen is often more difficult to spot than the brightly colored blackcock.
During the breeding season, the greyhen selects a secluded area for her nest.
Naturalists were excited to observe a rare greyhen feeding quietly in the forest.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: blackcock - the male black grouse
