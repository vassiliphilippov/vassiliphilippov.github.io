observed

1. ADJ

/ əbˈzɝvd / (AmE), / əbˈzɜːvd / (BrE)
discovered or established through scientific observation; verified by seeing or measuring directly rather than inferred.
identified through observation

Examples

The observed differences between the two groups were statistically significant.
An observed increase in temperature coincided with the chemical reaction.
Scientists have yet to explain the observed patterns in the data.

Synset Examples

variation in the ascertained flux depends on a number of factors
the discovered behavior norms
discovered differences in achievement
no explanation for the observed phenomena

Related Terms

  • Synonym: measured - determined by measurement
  • Antonym: inferred - deduced or concluded from evidence rather than direct observation
